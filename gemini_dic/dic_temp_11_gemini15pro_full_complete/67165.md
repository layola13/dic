expostulate:/ɪkˈspɒstjʊleɪt/ /ɛkˈspɑːstʃəleɪt/
verb
抗议；劝诫；告诫；反对
express strong disapproval or disagreement.
例句：
She expostulated with him about his rudeness. 她责备他粗鲁无礼。

He expostulated with the referee about the decision. 他向裁判抗议判罚。


近义词：remonstrate, protest, object, argue, reason, plead
反义词：agree, consent, approve
词性：v.
