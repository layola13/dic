nugacity:/njuːˈɡæsɪti/
n.
琐碎；无价值；轻浮
Trivialness, worthlessness, or frivolity.
Her nugacity surprised everyone in the serious meeting. 她在严肃的会议上表现出的轻浮让每个人都感到惊讶。
Synonyms: triviality, futility, insignificance, frippery
Antonyms: importance, significance, value, gravity
