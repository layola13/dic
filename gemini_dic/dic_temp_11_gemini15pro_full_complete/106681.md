kvetchy: /ˈkvɛtʃi/ | adj. | 爱抱怨的，吹毛求疵的 | Inclined to complain habitually; fretful; whining. |  He was being kvetchy about the food, even though it was delicious. 他一直在抱怨食物，即使它很美味。|  She's always so kvetchy in the morning. 她早上总是爱抱怨。| Near synonyms: complaining, carping, whining, griping, grousing | Antonym: content, satisfied | Adjective
