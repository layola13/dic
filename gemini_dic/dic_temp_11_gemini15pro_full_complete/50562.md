deputizes:/ˈdɛpjʊˌtaɪzɪz/
verb
代理；委任…为副手；授权
acts or speaks for another person in an official way, especially for a short time
The foreman deputized for the manager during his absence.  工头在经理不在的时候代理他的职务。
synonyms: represent, stand in for, act for, substitute for, cover for, fill in for, take the place of
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for deputize as a verb, consider antonyms for the noun "deputy" like "principal," "chief," or "leader")
词性: 动词
