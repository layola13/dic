downstream:/ˌdaʊnˈstriːm/
adjective|顺流的，下游的|situated or moving in the direction in which a stream flows.|例句：The downstream current carried the boat towards the sea. 顺流的水流把船带向大海。|近义词：downriver|反义词:upstream
adverb|顺流地，在下游|in the direction in which a stream flows.|例句：They traveled downstream on a raft. 他们乘木筏顺流而下。|近义词：downriver|反义词:upstream
noun|下游，顺流|the direction in which a stream flows.|例句：Pollution from the factory contaminated the downstream area. 工厂的污染污染了下游地区。|近义词：downriver|反义词:upstream
