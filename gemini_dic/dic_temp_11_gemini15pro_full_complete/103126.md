jerkinhead:/ˈdʒɜːrkɪnˌhed/ |n.|悬山顶，截尖山墙|A roof in which the gable end is partially hipped, resulting in a truncated gable end.|The old farmhouse had a distinctive jerkinhead roof.  这栋古老的农舍有一个独特的悬山顶。|Synonyms:  clipped gable, half-hipped roof, truncated gable|Antonyms: gable roof|Noun
