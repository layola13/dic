gloomy:/ˈɡluːmi/| adj.| 忧郁的，阴暗的，沮丧的 | Feeling sad and without hope; dark or poorly lit, especially in a way that is depressing or frightening. | The weather was gloomy and overcast. 天气阴沉沉的，乌云密布。|  The gloomy news made everyone feel depressed.  这令人沮丧的消息让每个人都感到沮丧。 | synonyms: depressing, dismal, bleak, somber, dark, overcast | antonyms: cheerful, bright, sunny, optimistic | adjective
