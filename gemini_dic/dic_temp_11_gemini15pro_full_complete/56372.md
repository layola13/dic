dokimastic:/ˌdɒkɪˈmæstɪk/| adj.|鉴定性的，化验的|Relating to the assaying or testing of metals or ores to determine their composition and purity.  Used especially in historical contexts.|The dokimastic art was highly valued in ancient civilizations for ensuring the purity of coinage.|近义词：assaying, testing, analytic|反义词:synthetic, unproven|词性:adjective
