microbicidal: /ˌmaɪkrəʊbɪˈsaɪdəl/ | adj. |杀菌的，灭菌的 |Destructive to microbes. | This disinfectant is microbicidal and effective against a wide range of bacteria.  这种消毒剂具有杀菌作用，对多种细菌有效。|  The microbicidal properties of the new cleaning solution were tested thoroughly. 新型清洁液的杀菌性能经过了彻底的测试。| synonyms:  antibacterial, antimicrobial, germicidal, disinfectant | antonyms:  microbistatic | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From microbe + -cidal (killing). "Microbe" comes from French, coined by Charles-Emmanuel Sédillot in 1878, from Greek mikrós "small" + bios "life". "-cidal" comes from Latin -cida, from caedere "to kill".
