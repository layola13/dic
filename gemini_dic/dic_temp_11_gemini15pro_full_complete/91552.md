hyper-inflation:/ˌhaɪpərɪnˈfleɪʃn/ | n. |恶性通货膨胀|A period of rapid and excessive price increases in an economy, typically at a rate exceeding 50% per month.|Example 1: The country experienced a period of hyper-inflation after the war, rendering its currency almost worthless.  这个国家在战后经历了一段恶性通货膨胀时期，使其货币几乎一文不值。

Example 2:  Hyper-inflation can wipe out savings and destabilize an entire economy. 恶性通货膨胀会摧毁储蓄，并破坏整个经济的稳定。|近义词: runaway inflation, galloping inflation |反义词: deflation, disinflation |词性:名词
