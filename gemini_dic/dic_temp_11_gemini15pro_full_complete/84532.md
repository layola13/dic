hamiform:/ˈhæmɪfɔːrm/
adjective
钩形的；钩状的
Resembling a hook; hooked.  Shaped like a hook.
The hamiform wings of the bird allowed it to cling to branches. 这只鸟钩状的翅膀使它能够抓住树枝。
近义词:hooked, hook-shaped, uncinate
反义词:straight
