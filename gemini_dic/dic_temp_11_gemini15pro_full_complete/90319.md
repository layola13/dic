housewives:/ˈhaʊsˌwaɪvz/
noun|家庭主妇（复数）|A married woman whose main occupation is caring for her family, especially her husband, children, and home.|Many housewives feel unfulfilled and isolated.|近义词：homemaker, stay-at-home mom|反义词：working woman, career woman|n.


housewife:/ˈhaʊsˌwaɪf/
noun|家庭主妇（单数）|A married woman whose main occupation is caring for her family, especially her husband, children, and home.|Being a housewife can be a demanding and often thankless job.|近义词：homemaker, stay-at-home mom|反义词：working woman, career woman|n.

词源:  From Middle English *huswif, from Old English hūsƿīf (“housewife”), equivalent to house +‎ wife.
