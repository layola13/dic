over-defensive:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈfɛnsɪv/
adjective
过分防御的；过分自卫的
Excessively protective of oneself, often to the point of appearing insecure or aggressive.  Characterized by responding to perceived criticism or threats with disproportionate force or emotion.
He became over-defensive when asked about his past. 当被问及他的过去时，他变得过分防御。
She's so over-defensive that she takes everything as a personal attack. 她太敏感了，把每件事都当成是针对她个人的攻击。
近义词:sensitive, insecure, touchy
反义词:confident, secure, open
词性:adjective

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "defensive" (adjective from "defense," from Old French "defense," ultimately from Latin "defendere" - to ward off)
