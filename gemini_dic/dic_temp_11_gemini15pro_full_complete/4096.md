aim-to:/eɪm tuː/ | phrasal verb | 旨在，目标是 | To have something as a goal or target. |  We aim to finish the project by next week. 我们旨在下周完成这个项目。

|  The company aims to increase profits by 10% this year. 公司的目标是今年将利润提高10%。

| 近义词: intend to, plan to, aspire to | 反义词: avoid, neglect | 词性: 动词短语
