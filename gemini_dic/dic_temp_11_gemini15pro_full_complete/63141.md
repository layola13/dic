ensoul:/ɪnˈsoʊl/ | vt. | 赋予灵魂；使有生气；激励 | To endow with a soul or spirit; to animate or inspire. |  He wrote poems that ensouled the landscape of his childhood. 他写的诗歌赋予了他童年景色的灵魂。

| The artist's passion ensouled her paintings. 艺术家的热情赋予了她的绘画生命。 | 近义词: animate, inspire, enliven, vitalize | 反义词: deaden, devitalize | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From en- (to cause to be in a certain condition) + soul (spirit, animating principle).  Ultimately from Latin *insolāre, from in- (in, into) + sol (sun).  The sense shifted from "putting sun into" to "putting soul into."
