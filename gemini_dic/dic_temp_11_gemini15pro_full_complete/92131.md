hyperscrupulousnesses:/ˌhaɪpərˈskruːpjələsnəsɪz/
noun
过度谨慎；过分小心；过于拘泥
Excessive carefulness and conscientiousness, often to an unnecessary degree.
His hyperscrupulousness about hygiene made him unpopular with his roommates. 他对卫生的过分谨慎让他和室友相处得不好。
近义词:meticulousness, fastidiousness, punctiliousness
反义词:carelessness, negligence, laxity
词性:n.


Etymology:
From hyper- (prefix meaning "over, excessive") + scrupulous (from Latin scrūpulus, meaning "a small sharp stone," hence "anxiety," "uneasiness," and finally "conscientious") + -ness (suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality).
