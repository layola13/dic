anthropomancy: /ˌænθrəpəˈmænsi/ | n. | 人祭占卜；通过检查人体（尤其是内脏）进行占卜 | Divination by the inspection of human entrails, especially the liver. |  Ancient Greeks sometimes practiced anthropomancy to predict the outcome of battles. 古希腊人有时会进行人祭占卜来预测战争的结果。 | Synonyms: necromancy, divination | Antonyms:  None (lack of a direct antonym for this specific type of divination) | noun
