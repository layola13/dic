but-for:/bʌt ˈfɔːr/ | preposition, conjunction | 除...之外；如果没有... |  Used to introduce the only thing that prevented something from happening.  |  But for your help, I would have failed. 如果没有你的帮助，我就会失败。 |  without, except for |  with, including | preposition, conjunction

but-for:/bʌt ˈfɔːr/ | preposition, conjunction | 除...之外；如果没有... |  Indicates a necessary condition.
