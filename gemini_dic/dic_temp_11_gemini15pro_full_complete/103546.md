joggly:/ˈdʒɒɡli/ (British) /ˈdʒɑːɡli/ (American)| adj.|摇晃的，不稳定的，颤动的|Unstable and shaky, tending to wobble or vibrate.|The table was joggly and the coffee spilled. 桌子摇摇晃晃的，咖啡洒了。|The jelly was still a bit joggly. 果冻还有点晃动。|近义词：wobbly, shaky, unsteady, unstable|反义词：stable, firm, steady|词性:形容词
词源:  Likely a variant of "jog" (meaning to move with a slight jerk or shake) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix forming adjectives).  The meaning shifted from describing the movement itself to describing something that allows or exhibits such movement.
