adumbrating:/ædˈʌmbreɪtɪŋ/
v.
预示;略述;遮蔽;使昏暗
to outline or sketch broadly; to foreshadow or prefigure; to overshadow or obscure
The artist adumbrated the scene with charcoal before painting.  这位艺术家在作画前用木炭勾勒了场景。
The clouds adumbrated the sun.  云遮蔽了太阳。
His early sketches adumbrated the final masterpiece. 他早期的草图预示了最后的杰作。
近义词:foreshadow, prefigure, intimate, suggest, outline, sketch
反义词:reveal, clarify, illuminate
词性:动词
