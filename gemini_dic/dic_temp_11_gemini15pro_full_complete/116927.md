mays:/meɪz/| v.|可能；可以；也许|Present tense, third-person singular form of may. Expresses possibility, permission, or a wish.|Mays I borrow your pen?  我可以借你的笔吗？|近义词：might, could, can|反义词:cannot, must not|动词

She mays play the piano beautifully. = 她可能弹钢琴弹得很好。

虚拟语气:
If he mays come, we will have a party. = 如果他可以来，我们就举行一个派对。 (Note: This is not standard English.  It is more natural to say "If he can come...")
May you have a wonderful time! = 祝你玩得开心！

被动语态:
Not applicable.  "Mays" cannot be used in the passive voice.

疑问句:
Mays I leave now? = 我现在可以离开吗？
Mays she join us for dinner? = 她可以和我们一起吃晚饭吗？

否定句:
He mays not leave yet. = 他可能还不能离开。(Note: This is not standard English. It is more natural to say "He may not leave yet.")
She mays not understand the instructions. = 她可能不明白指示。(Note: This is not standard English. It is more natural to say "She may not understand the instructions.")



PHRASE->
(Note: "Mays" doesn't form common phrases. It's the third-person singular present tense of "may".  Phrases typically use the base form "may".)
May I help you? = 我能帮您吗？
May God bless you. = 愿上帝保佑你。
May I ask a question? = 我可以问个问题吗？
May I have your attention, please? = 请大家注意一下。
May the best man win. = 愿最好的男人/人赢。


词源: Middle English *mai, from Old English mæg; akin to Old High German mag I can, Latin posse to be able, Sanskrit śaknoti he is able.
