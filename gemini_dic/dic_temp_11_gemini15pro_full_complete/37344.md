commendably:/kəˈmɛndəbli/| adv.|值得称赞地，令人钦佩地|In a manner deserving praise; laudably.|She handled the difficult situation commendably. 她令人钦佩地处理了这个棘手的局面。|Synonyms: admirably, creditably, excellently, laudably, meritoriously, praiseworthily|Antonyms: poorly, badly, inadequately, unsatisfactorily|词性: 副词

commend:/kəˈmɛnd/| vt.|称赞，表扬；推荐；委托|1. To praise, express approval of. 2. To present as worthy of attention, confidence, or notice. 3. To entrust for care or preservation.|I commend her for her bravery. 我称赞她的勇敢。|Synonyms: praise, applaud, compliment, recommend, entrust|Antonyms: criticize, condemn, censure, denounce|词性: 动词
