cartooning:/kɑːrˈtuːnɪŋ/
n.
漫画创作；漫画艺术
the art or skill of creating cartoons
He took a course in cartooning. 他参加了一个漫画创作课程。

近义词：caricaturing, animating
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps "realistic drawing")
词性：noun (名词)
