deceptious:/dɪˈsepʃəs/
adjective
欺骗性的，虚伪的
Intended to deceive; misleading.  Giving a false impression.
例句：
The advertisement was deceptious, promising results that were impossible to achieve.
这则广告具有欺骗性，承诺的结果根本无法实现。

例句：
His deceptious smile hid his true intentions.
他虚伪的笑容掩盖了他真实的意图。

近义词：misleading, deceitful, deceptive, fraudulent, dishonest
反义词：honest, truthful, sincere, genuine
词性：adjective

词源：Late Middle English: from Old French deceptif, -ive, from late Latin deceptivus, from decipere ‘deceive’.
