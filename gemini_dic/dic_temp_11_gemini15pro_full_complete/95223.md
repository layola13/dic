inbreds:/ˈɪnbrɛdz/
n.|近亲繁殖的动物（或植物）; 天生具有某种特性的人|People or animals that are inbred, especially over many generations.
|The study focused on the effects of isolation on small, inbred populations.|这项研究的重点是隔离对小型近亲繁殖种群的影响。
|近义词：purebreds, homozygotes|反义词:outbreds, hybrids|词性:名词(复数)


inbred:/ˈɪnbrɛd/
adj.|近亲繁殖的；天生的|Produced by inbreeding; innate.
|The problems of inbred communities.|近亲繁殖群落的问题。
|近义词：inherent, congenital, innate|反义词:acquired, learned|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From in + breed.  "In-" here means "within" or "into." "Breed" refers to reproduction.  So "inbred" literally means bred within a close group.
