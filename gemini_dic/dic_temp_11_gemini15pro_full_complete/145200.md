overbalance:/ˌoʊvərˈbæləns/| v. n.|失去平衡，过重，过分|To lose balance and fall or tip over; to be greater in weight, importance, or influence than something else.|The canoe overbalanced and they fell into the water. 独木舟失去平衡，他们掉进了水里。|Synonyms: outweigh, overweigh, preponderate, tip, capsize, upset|Antonyms: balance, stabilize|verb

overbalance:/ˌoʊvərˈbæləns/| n.|不平衡，优势|The state of being out of balance; an excessive amount or influence.|The overbalance of power in the region led to conflict. 该地区力量的失衡导致了冲突。|Synonyms: imbalance, preponderance, excess|Antonyms: balance, equilibrium|noun
