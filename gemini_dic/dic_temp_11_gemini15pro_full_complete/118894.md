metatheses:/məˈtæθɪsiːz/
n.
换位，置换，语音转换
The word "aks" for "ask" is an example of metathesis. “aks”代替“ask”是语音转换的一个例子。
Metathesis is a common phonological process in language evolution.  换位是语言演变中常见的语音过程。
synonyms: transposition, inversion, rearrangement
antonyms:  fixation, stabilization (in a linguistic context)
noun
