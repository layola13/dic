andalusia:/ˌændəˈluːʒə/ /ˌændəˈluːziə/ | n. |安达卢西亚|A region in southern Spain, known for its flamenco music and dance, Moorish architecture, and vibrant culture. |例句：Andalusia is a popular tourist destination. 安达卢西亚是一个受欢迎的旅游目的地。|例句：The climate in Andalusia is typically Mediterranean. 安达卢西亚的气候是典型的地中海气候。|近义词：Southern Spain, |反义词:None|词性:noun

Etymology: From Arabic  الأنْدَلُس (al-ʾAndalus), ultimately from Vandalicia, the Roman name for the Iberian Peninsula when it was under Vandal control.
