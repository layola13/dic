discloak:/dɪsˈkloʊk/
|v.|揭露，暴露，脱去（伪装）|To remove a cloak or disguise; to reveal or expose something previously hidden.|The magician discloaked the rabbit, revealing it was just a stuffed animal. 魔术师揭开了兔子的伪装，结果发现它只是一个毛绒玩具。|The investigation discloaked a network of corruption within the company. 调查揭露了公司内部的腐败网络。|reveal, expose, uncover|conceal, hide, mask|verb
