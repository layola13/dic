audit:/ˈɔːdɪt (UK) /ˈɑːdɪt (US)| n. vt.|审计，查账；旁听|An official inspection of an individual's or organization's accounts, typically by an independent body.|The company's accounts are audited annually. 这家公司的账目每年都会被审计。|Synonyms: inspection, examination, review|Antonyms: disregard, neglect|noun
audit:/ˈɔːdɪt (UK) /ˈɑːdɪt (US)| vt.|审计，查账；旁听|To officially inspect (an individual's or organization's accounts).|They audited the company's accounts. 他们审计了公司的账目。|Synonyms: inspect, examine, review|Antonyms: disregard, neglect|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *audītus*, past participle of *audīre* "to hear."  Originally referred to a hearing given to accounts.
