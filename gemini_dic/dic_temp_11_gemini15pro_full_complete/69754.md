fergusonite:/ˈfɜːrɡəsəˌnaɪt/| n. |弗格森石|A brownish-black mineral containing yttrium, cerium, niobium, tantalum, and titanium. It is a radioactive rare earth mineral and a source of several rare earth elements.|Fergusonite is a valuable ore of yttrium. 弗格森石是钇的宝贵矿石。|The geologist discovered a sample of fergusonite. 地质学家发现了一块弗格森石样本。|Synonyms: None (as it's a specific mineral name)|Antonyms: None (as it's a specific mineral name)|noun
