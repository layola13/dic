alluvium:/əˈluːviəm/
n.
冲积层，冲积土
a deposit of clay, silt, and sand left by flowing water in a river valley or delta, typically producing fertile soil.
The fertile alluvium of the Nile valley supports a large agricultural population. 尼罗河谷肥沃的冲积土养活了大量农业人口。
近义词: sediment, deposit, silt
反义词: bedrock


alluviums:/əˈluːviəmz/
n.
冲积层（复数）
plural form of alluvium.  Refers to multiple deposits of clay, silt, and sand left by flowing water.
The farmer's land was enriched by the alluviums deposited over centuries.  几个世纪以来沉积的冲积层使农民的土地更加肥沃。
近义词: sediments, deposits
反义词: N/A
