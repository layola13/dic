carpet-bombing:/ˈkɑːrpɪt ˌbɒmɪŋ/ /ˈkɑːrpɪt ˌbɑːmɪŋ/| n.|地毯式轰炸|The intense and indiscriminate bombing of a large area, typically a city or region.|The city was subjected to relentless carpet-bombing during the war.  这座城市在战争期间遭受了无情的地毯式轰炸。|Synonyms: saturation bombing, area bombing|Antonyms: precision bombing, surgical strike|noun

carpet-bomb:/ˈkɑːrpɪt ˌbɒm/ /ˈkɑːrpɪt ˌbɑːm/| vt.|地毯式轰炸|To bomb (an area) intensively and indiscriminately.|The air force carpet-bombed the enemy's supply lines. 空军地毯式轰炸了敌人的补给线。|Synonyms: saturation bomb, area bomb|Antonyms: precision bomb, surgically strike|verb
