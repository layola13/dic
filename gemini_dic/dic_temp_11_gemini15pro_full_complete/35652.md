coaxing:/ˈkoʊksɪŋ/| n. adj.|哄骗，劝诱； coax的现在分词形式|The act of persuading someone gently and patiently.|She finally got the baby to sleep by gentle coaxing. 她轻柔地哄着，终于让宝宝睡着了。|近义词：persuasion, cajoling, wheedling|反义词：forcing, demanding|n. adj.

coax:/ˈkoʊks/| vt.|哄骗，劝诱|To persuade someone gently and patiently to do something.|I coaxed the cat down from the tree with a saucer of milk. 我用一碟牛奶把猫从树上哄了下来。|近义词：persuade, cajole, wheedle|反义词：force, demand|v.
