overproducers:/ˈoʊvərprəˌdusərz/ /ˈəʊvəprəˌdjuːsəz/
noun
过度生产者；生产过剩者
Those who produce more of a good or service than is required or desired by the market.  Those who produce in excess.
Farmers are sometimes overproducers of certain crops, leading to lower prices.  农民有时会过度生产某些作物，导致价格下跌。
synonyms: oversuppliers
antonyms: undersuppliers
n.
