idiotism:/ˈɪdiətɪzəm/| n.|愚蠢；低能；白痴；（语言）不合语法、不合习惯的表达|An act, remark, or idea that is extremely stupid or foolish.  A language usage that is peculiar to a particular language, region, or group and considered incorrect in standard usage.|His suggestion was sheer idiotism. 他的建议纯粹是愚蠢。That idiotism he uttered made everyone cringe. 他说的那句蠢话让每个人都感到尴尬。|近义词：folly, stupidity, absurdity, nonsense|反义词：wisdom, intelligence, brilliance|词性:noun
idiotism:/ˈɪdiətɪzəm/| n.|白痴行为|An instance of very foolish or unintelligent behavior.|Driving on the wrong side of the road is sheer idiotism. 在路的错误一侧开车纯粹是白痴行为。|近义词：lunacy, madness, insanity|反义词：sanity, rationality|词性:noun
