chicer:/ˈʃiːkər/ | adj. | 更时尚的，更别致的 | More stylish and elegant; comparative form of "chic". | Her new dress is much chicer than her old one. 她的新裙子比旧裙子更时尚。|  She decorated her apartment in a chicer style. 她把她的公寓装修得更别致了。 | 近义词: more stylish, more fashionable, more elegant | 反义词: less stylish, less fashionable, dowdy, frumpy | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From French "chic" (stylish, elegant), ultimately of German origin.
