counteraggressions:/ˌkaʊntərəˈɡrɛʃənz/| n. |反击，对抗性攻击|Actions taken in response to perceived or actual aggression, often with the intention of neutralizing or deterring further aggression.|  Example:  Her counteraggressions were swift and decisive, effectively silencing her bully. 她的反击迅速而果断，有效地压制了欺负她的人。

Example: The therapist helped him understand the root causes of his counteraggressions.  治疗师帮助他理解了他反击行为的根源。|近义词：retaliation, reprisal, counterattack|反义词:submission, passivity|词性:名词
