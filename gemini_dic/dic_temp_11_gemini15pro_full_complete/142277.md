orrery:/ˈɔːrəri/ (US); /ˈɒrəri/ (UK)| n.| 太阳系仪，行星仪 |A mechanical model of the solar system, or of just the sun, earth, and moon, used to represent their relative positions and motions.|The orrery accurately depicted the planets' orbits around the sun.  这个太阳系仪精确地描绘了行星绕太阳运行的轨道。|Example: The museum displayed a beautiful orrery made of brass and clockwork.  博物馆展出了一个用黄铜和钟表装置制作的精美太阳系仪。|Synonym: planetarium, astronomical model|Antonym: None|Noun
