inswathe:/ɪnˈsweɪð/ /ɪnˈswɑːð/| vt.|包裹，襁褓|To wrap or enfold, as in bandages or swaddling clothes.|She inswathed the baby in a soft blanket. 她用柔软的毯子包裹婴儿。|The mummy was inswathed in layers of linen. 木乃伊被包裹在多层亚麻布里。|近义词：wrap, envelop, swaddle|反义词：unwrap, uncover|词性：动词

词源:
From Middle English *inswathen, from in- + swathen (“to wrap”).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *swathaną (“to bind, swathe”). Cognate with Dutch zwaden (“to swathe”), German swaden (“to swathe”), Swedish svada (“to swathe”).
