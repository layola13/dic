duuk:/duːk/ | n. |  躲避；闪避；低下身子 |  The act of lowering the body quickly, especially to avoid being hit or seen. |  He ducked his head to avoid the low-hanging branch. 他低下头躲避低垂的树枝。 | synonyms: stoop, crouch, bob, dodge | antonyms: rise, stand, confront | noun
