directest:/dɪˈrɛktɪst/| adj.|最直接的|Superlative form of direct: most direct.  Meaning the shortest route or most straightforward manner.|The directest route is often the quickest, but not always the most scenic. 最直接的路线通常是最快的，但不总是最风景优美的。|The general gave the directest order possible to his troops. 将军向他的部队发出了最直接的命令。|近义词：straightest, shortest, most immediate|反义词：indirectest, longest, most circuitous|词性:形容词
