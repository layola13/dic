lech:/lɛtʃ/| v. n.| 舔舐；渴望；垂涎 | To lick up with the tongue; To feel or express strong sexual desire for (someone). | The dog leched the spilled ice cream. 狗舔掉了洒出来的冰淇淋。 He was leching after the young women at the bar. 他对酒吧里的年轻女性垂涎三尺。 | 近义词：lick, crave, lust, yearn | 反义词：abhor, detest | 词性: 动词, 名词

词源: From Middle English *lechen*, from Old English *liccian* "to lick," of Germanic origin; related to Dutch *likken*, German *lecken*.  The sexual sense developed in the late 19th century.
