humanists:/ˈhjuːmənɪsts/| n.|人文主义者|People who believe in humanism, a philosophical stance that emphasizes human reason, ethics, and justice, while generally rejecting supernatural and religious dogma.  |例句：The humanists believed in the power of education to improve society. 人文主义者相信教育的力量可以改善社会。

例句：Many Renaissance artists and writers were humanists. 许多文艺复兴时期的艺术家和作家都是人文主义者。|近义词：humanitarians, freethinkers, rationalists|反义词:dogmatists, fanatics|词性:noun
