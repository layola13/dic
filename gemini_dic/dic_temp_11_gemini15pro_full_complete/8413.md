anti-teutonic:/ˌænti-tuːˈtɒnɪk/
anti-teutonic| adj.| 反日耳曼的，反德意志的|Opposed to or hostile towards Teutonic peoples, culture, or influence (Teutonic refers to Germanic peoples, especially in ancient times).|  The anti-teutonic sentiment was strong in France after the war. 战后，法国的反德情绪很强烈。| Synonyms: anti-German, anti-Germanic | Antonyms: pro-German, pro-Germanic| adjective

anti-teutonic:/ˌænti-tuːˈtɒnɪk/
anti-teutonic| n.| 反日耳曼主义者|A person who is opposed to or hostile towards Teutonic peoples, culture, or influence.| He was a known anti-teutonic, refusing to buy any German products. 他是一个著名的反德人士，拒绝购买任何德国产品。| Synonyms: opponent of Germanic culture | Antonyms: supporter of Germanic culture| noun
