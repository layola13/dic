mismatching:/ˌmɪsˈmætʃɪŋ/ | adj. | 不匹配的，不协调的 | Not matching; incongruous or unsuitable. |  The mismatching socks made her outfit look slightly off.  不匹配的袜子使她的服装看起来有点不协调。 | synonyms: clashing, conflicting, discordant, incompatible, unsuited | antonyms: matching, coordinating, harmonious, compatible, suited | adjective

mismatch:/ˌmɪsˈmætʃ/ | n. | 不匹配，不协调 | A failure to match or correspond; a bad or unsuitable combination. | There was a mismatch between the company's needs and the employee's skills. 公司的需求与员工的技能不匹配。 | synonyms: disparity, discrepancy, incompatibility, incongruity | antonyms: match, correspondence, compatibility, harmony | noun

mismatch:/ˌmɪsˈmætʃ/ | v. | 使不匹配，错误地搭配 | To match badly or incorrectly. | The colors of the curtains and the carpet were mismatched. 窗帘和地毯的颜色不匹配。| synonyms: pair wrongly, combine badly, misjoin | antonyms: match, coordinate, harmonize | verb
