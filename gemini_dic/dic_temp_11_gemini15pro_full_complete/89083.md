holpen:/ˈhoʊlpən/| v.|（古英语）帮助，援助|A past participle of "help," primarily archaic and now used mostly in religious contexts.  It signifies having been aided or assisted.|例句：They were holpen by a kind stranger. 他们得到了一个好心陌生人的帮助。|例句：He hath holpen his people Israel. 他帮助了他的子民以色列。|近义词：helped, aided, assisted|反义词：hindered, obstructed|词性:动词
