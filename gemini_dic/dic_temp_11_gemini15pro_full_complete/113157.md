lupines:/ˈluːpɪnz/ (US), /ˈljuːpaɪnz/ (UK)| n. |羽扇豆，鲁冰花|A plant of the pea family, with deeply divided leaves and tall, colorful spikes of flowers.  They are often grown as ornamentals and some species are used as green manure or for their edible seeds.|例句：The lupines in the garden were a vibrant mix of blues, pinks, and purples. 花园里的羽扇豆是蓝色、粉色和紫色的鲜艳混合。

例句：Lupines are known for their ability to improve soil fertility. 羽扇豆以其改良土壤肥力的能力而闻名。|近义词：bluebonnets (specifically for blue lupines), wildflowers|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
