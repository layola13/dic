outmastering:/ˌaʊtˈmæstərɪŋ/
|v.|胜过，超越|Surpassing or exceeding in skill or mastery; dominating completely.|He outmastered his opponent in the chess match. 他在国际象棋比赛中战胜了对手。|Synonyms: outdoing, surpassing, excelling, conquering, triumphing over|Antonyms:  being outdone, being surpassed, failing, losing to|verb

outmastering:/ˌaʊtˈmæstərɪŋ/
|adj.|（用于描述行为）技高一筹的，占绝对优势的|Characterized by superior skill or mastery; completely dominant. |His outmastering performance secured him the championship. 他技高一筹的表现为他赢得了冠军。|Synonyms: dominant, superior, masterful|Antonyms: inferior, subordinate, weak|adjective

词源：out- + mastering.  "Out-"  is a prefix meaning "surpassing" or "exceeding." "Mastering" comes from "master,"  meaning "having control or skill in something."  Therefore, "outmastering" means exceeding in mastery or control.
