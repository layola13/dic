excrescency:/ɪkˈskresənsi/ /ekˈskresənsi/
|n.|赘生物，瘤，异常突出物|An abnormal outgrowth or projection, often considered undesirable or unsightly.  Something that is superfluous and unwanted.|The mayor denounced the new skyscraper as a hideous excrescence on the city skyline. 市长谴责这座新的摩天大楼是城市天际线上丑陋的赘生物。|Synonyms: outgrowth, protuberance, lump, wart, blemish|Antonyms: indentation, concavity, hollowness|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *excrescentia, from Latin excrescēns, present participle of excrescō (“grow out”), from ex (“out”) + crescō (“grow”).
