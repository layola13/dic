azurine:/ˈæʒərɪn, əˈzʊərɪn/| n. adj.|天蓝色，蔚蓝色；天蓝色的染料|A bright greenish-blue colour, like the sky on a clear day. Also a dye that produces this colour.|The azurine sky was reflected in the calm lake.  蔚蓝的天空映照在平静的湖面上。|近义词：azure, cerulean, sky-blue|反义词:dark blue, black|词性:noun, adjective
词源: From French *azurin*, from Italian *azzurrino*, diminutive of *azzurro* ("blue"), from Arabic *lāzaward* (لازورد, "lapis lazuli").
