orthopinacoid:/ˌɔːrθoʊˈpɪnəkɔɪd/
n.
正交轴面；侧轴面
A crystal face parallel to the vertical and macro axes.  It intersects the brachy pinacoid at right angles.
The orthopinacoid is a prominent face in many orthorhombic minerals. 正交轴面在许多正交晶系矿物中是一个突出的面。
Crystals of barite often show well-developed orthopinacoids. 重晶石晶体通常显示出发育良好的正交轴面。
近义词: side pinacoid (sometimes used, but less precise)
反义词:  (No direct antonym; different pinacoids exist based on crystallographic axes)
词性: noun
