aliner:/ˈeɪlaɪnər/| n.|A-型拖车，A型房车|A towable camping trailer, shaped like a capital "A" when viewed from the front or rear, that folds down for towing and expands when parked.|We rented an aliner for our camping trip. 我们租了一辆A型拖车去露营。|近义词：travel trailer, camper, RV|反义词: house, apartment|noun
