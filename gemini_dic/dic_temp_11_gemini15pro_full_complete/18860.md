beseechment:/bɪˈsiːtʃmənt/
n.
恳求，乞求
The act of beseeching; an earnest or urgent request.
Her beseechment for mercy fell on deaf ears. 她恳求宽恕，但无人理会。
近义词：supplication, plea, entreaty, imploration
反义词：demand, command, order
词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *beseching, verbal noun from besechen (“to beseech”), from Old English besēcan (“to seek, beseech”), from be- + sēcan (“to seek”).  Cognate with Dutch bezoeken (“to visit”), German besuchen (“to visit”).  The -ment suffix denotes the result or product of an action.
