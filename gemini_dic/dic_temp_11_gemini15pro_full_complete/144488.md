outthunder:/ˌaʊtˈθʌndər/
verb
胜过雷鸣般的声音；发出比…更大的雷声
to be louder than (another sound of thunder)
The storm outthundered the previous one.  这场暴风雨的雷声比上次的更大。
Synonyms: drown out, overpower
Antonyms: whisper, murmur
词性: 动词

outthunder:/ˌaʊtˈθʌndər/
verb
以更强烈的言辞压倒；以更激烈的怒吼压倒
to surpass in loudness or vehemence of speech or denunciation
He outthundered his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中以更强烈的言辞压倒了对手。
Synonyms: outshout, overpower
Antonyms:  be quiet, yield
词性: 动词
