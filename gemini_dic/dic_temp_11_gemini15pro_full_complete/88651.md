hoarders:/ˈhɔːrdərz/ (US) /ˈhɔːdəz/ (UK)| n. |囤积者|People who excessively save items and have difficulty discarding them, often resulting in cluttered living spaces and distress.|例句：The hoarders' house was filled with piles of newspapers and old clothes. 囤积者的房子里堆满了报纸和旧衣服。

例句：The show features hoarders who struggle to let go of their possessions.  这个节目讲述了难以放弃自己物品的囤积者。|近义词：collectors, savers, accumulators|反义词：minimalists, spenders|Noun
