nectocalyx: /ˈnɛktəˌkeɪlɪks/ | n. | 泳钟 | The bell, or umbrella, of a jellyfish or other medusa. It is the main body of the jellyfish and is responsible for its pulsating movement. | The nectocalyx of the moon jelly is almost transparent.  海月水母的泳钟几乎是透明的。| The nectocalyx pulsed rhythmically, propelling the jellyfish through the water. 泳钟有节奏地搏动，推动水母在水中前进。 | 近义词: bell, umbrella | 反义词:  (None -  This is a specific anatomical term) | 词性: noun
