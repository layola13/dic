ephoral:/ˈɛfərəl/ | adj. |  执政官的; 监督官的; 短暂的 | Relating to an ephor (a magistrate or overseer in ancient Sparta), or more generally, short-lived or transient. |  The ephoral powers were significant in Spartan government. 斯巴达政府中执政官的权力很大。|  His influence was ephoral, lasting only a brief period. 他的影响力是短暂的，只持续了很短的一段时间。 | 近义词: transient, fleeting, ephemeral | 反义词: permanent, lasting, enduring | 词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *ephoroi* (overseers).
