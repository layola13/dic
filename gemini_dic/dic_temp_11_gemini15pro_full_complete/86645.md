hellbrewed:/ˈhɛlˌbrud/| adj.|地狱酿造的，恶魔般产生的|Something hellbrewed is conceived or created in a wicked or malicious way, as if originating from hell. It implies something sinister or evil in nature.|The villain unleashed a hellbrewed plan to destroy the city.  这个恶棍发动了一个毁灭城市的恶魔计划。|The rumors spreading about her were clearly hellbrewed lies.  关于她的谣言显然是地狱般的谎言。|近义词：infernal, demonic, fiendish|反义词:heavenly, divine|词性:形容词
词源:  A compound word from "hell" (Old English hel, meaning the underworld or a place of torment) and "brewed" (Old English breowan, meaning to make by infusion, steep, boil).
