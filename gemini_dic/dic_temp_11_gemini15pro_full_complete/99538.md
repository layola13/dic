intergossipped:/ˌɪntərˈɡɑːsɪpt/ | v. |互相八卦，穿插着闲聊 |To engage in gossip between or among other actions or conversations. | The women intergossipped while they folded laundry. 女人们一边叠衣服一边闲聊八卦。|Synonyms: chatted, gossiped, whispered|Antonyms: worked silently, remained quiet |verb

intergossipped:/ˌɪntərˈɡɑːsɪpt/ | adj. |穿插着闲聊的 |Characterized by intermittent gossip. | Their intergossipped conversation covered everything from local news to celebrity scandals.  她们穿插着闲聊，谈论的话题从当地新闻到名人丑闻无所不包。|Synonyms: gossipy, chatty|Antonyms: serious, focused |adjective
