caaba:/ˈkɑːbə/ /ˈkæbə/| n.|克尔白, 天房|A cube-shaped building in Mecca, Saudi Arabia, that is the central shrine of Islam, toward which Muslims turn five times daily in prayer.|Pilgrims circumambulate the Kaaba during the Hajj. 朝圣者在朝觐期间绕克尔白行走。|The Kaaba is considered the most sacred site in Islam. 克尔白被认为是伊斯兰教最神圣的地方。|近义词:none|反义词:none|名词
