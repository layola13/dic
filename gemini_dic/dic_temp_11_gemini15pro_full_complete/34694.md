cleromancy: /ˈklɛrəʊmænsi/ (UK) /ˈklɪrəˌmænsi/ (US)
|n.|占卜，求签|Divination by casting lots, or by interpreting random patterns or occurrences.|Ancient Greeks practiced cleromancy to seek guidance from the gods. 古希腊人通过求签来寻求神的指引。|Synonyms: divination, sortilege, augury|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, potentially "scientific analysis")|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
