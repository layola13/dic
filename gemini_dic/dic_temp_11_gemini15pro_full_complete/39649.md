conservationist:/ˌkɑːnsərˈveɪʃənɪst/ | n. | 保育人士，自然资源保护者 | A person who advocates or acts for the protection and preservation of the natural environment and wildlife. |  Example 1: The conservationist dedicated her life to protecting endangered species.  这位环保人士毕生致力于保护濒危物种。

Example 2:  The conservationist argued against the development of the protected forest. 这位环保人士反对在受保护的森林中进行开发。| Near Synonyms: environmentalist, preservationist, ecologist | Near Antonyms: developer, polluter | Noun
