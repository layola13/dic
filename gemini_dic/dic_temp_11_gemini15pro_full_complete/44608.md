crowded: /ˈkraʊdɪd/ (US) /ˈkraʊdɪd/ (UK)| adj.|拥挤的；挤满的|Full of people; packed.|The bus was crowded during rush hour. 公共汽车在高峰时段很拥挤。|The concert was so crowded we could barely move.  演唱会非常拥挤，我们几乎动弹不得。|近义词：packed, jammed, full, congested|反义词：empty, deserted, spacious, uncrowded|词性:形容词
Etymology: From crowd + -ed.  From Middle English *crowden, from Old English crūdan (“to press, urge, hasten”).
