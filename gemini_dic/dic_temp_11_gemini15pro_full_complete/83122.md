gustless:/ˈɡʌstləs/| adj.| 无味的；平淡的；缺乏热情的| Lacking flavor, zest, or spirit; bland. | The soup was disappointingly gustless. 这汤淡而无味，令人失望。| The performance was technically perfect but utterly gustless.  这场演出技术上很完美，但却完全缺乏激情。| 近义词：bland, insipid, tasteless, vapid, uninspired | 反义词：flavorful, tasty, zesty, spirited, inspiring | 词性: 形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From gust (a sudden burst of wind or feeling) + -less (suffix meaning "without").  The original meaning referred to something lacking a sudden burst of flavor.
