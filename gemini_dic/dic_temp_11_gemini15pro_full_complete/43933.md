crewmate:/ˈkruːmeɪt/| n.|船员；同事；伙伴；队友|A fellow member of a crew.  A person you work with, especially in a difficult or challenging situation.|例句：He was a loyal crewmate and a good friend. 他是一位忠诚的船员和好朋友。|例句：My crewmates and I worked tirelessly to finish the project. 我和我的同事们不知疲倦地工作以完成这个项目。|近义词：colleague, teammate, partner, associate|反义词:opponent, enemy|词性:noun
