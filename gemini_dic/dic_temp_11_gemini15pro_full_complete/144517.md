outtricks:/ˌaʊtˈtrɪks/
|v.| 以计谋胜过，智胜|to surpass or defeat by means of tricks or cleverness|He outtricked his opponents in the negotiation and secured a favorable deal. 他在谈判中智胜了对手，达成了有利的协议。|例句：The magician outtricked the audience with his illusions. 魔术师用他的幻觉智胜了观众。|近义词：outmaneuver, outsmart, outwit|反义词：be outwitted, be outsmarted|词性:动词

outtricks:/ˌaʊtˈtrɪks/
|n.|  (通常复数) 计谋，诡计|clever tricks or schemes used to deceive or outwit someone|His outtricks were legendary in the business world. 他的计谋在商界是传奇。|例句：The fox used its outtricks to catch the rabbit. 狐狸用诡计抓住了兔子。|近义词：tricks, ruses, stratagems|反义词：honesty, forthrightness|词性:名词
