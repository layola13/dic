binaural:/baɪˈnɔːrəl/ | adj. | 双耳的，两耳的 | Relating to both ears; involving or used with both ears. |  Example: Binaural hearing allows us to locate the source of sounds. 双耳听力使我们能够定位声源。

Example:  Binaural recording techniques create a more immersive listening experience. 双耳录音技术创造更具沉浸感的聆听体验。 |  Near synonyms: stereophonic, two-eared | Antonyms: monaural, single-eared | adjective
