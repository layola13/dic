noticeability:/ˈnoʊtɪsəˈbɪləti/| n.|显著性；明显度；引人注目|The noticeability of the change surprised everyone.  这种变化的显著性让每个人都感到惊讶。|English Explanation: The quality or state of being easily or readily noticed; conspicuousness.|Example: The noticeability of her new haircut was undeniable. 她新发型的显眼程度不容否认。

Example:  The noticeability of the stain on the carpet was embarrassing. 地毯上的污渍很明显，真让人尴尬。

|Synonyms: conspicuity, prominence, obviousness, salience, visibility|Antonyms: inconspicuousness, unnoticeableness, obscurity, subtlety|noun
