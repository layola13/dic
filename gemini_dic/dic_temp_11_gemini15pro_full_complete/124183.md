mosaiclike:/moʊˈzeɪɪklaɪk/
adjective
像马赛克的，拼凑式的
Resembling a mosaic; composed of diverse elements.
The painting had a mosaiclike quality, with small squares of color forming a larger image. 这幅画有一种马赛克般的特质，由小方块的颜色组成了一个更大的图像。
The city's skyline presented a mosaiclike pattern of lights and shadows. 城市的天际线呈现出光影交错的马赛克图案。
近义词: tessellated, fragmented, patchwork
反义词: uniform, homogeneous
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From mosaic + -like.  "Mosaic" comes from Late Latin *mosaicus, from Latin musivum (opus) "(work) of the Muses," perhaps because mosaics were often used to decorate shrines or places dedicated to the Muses.
