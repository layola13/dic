nonendorsements:/ˌnɒnɪnˈdɔːrsmənts/
n.
不认可；不背书；不赞同
Lack of official support or approval for something.
The candidate's statement was seen as a nonendorsement of the controversial policy.  这位候选人的声明被视为对这项有争议的政策的不认可。
The company's nonendorsement of the product led to its failure. 该公司对该产品的不支持导致了它的失败。

近义词:disapproval, rejection, opposition
反义词:endorsement, approval, support
词性:名词(plural)
