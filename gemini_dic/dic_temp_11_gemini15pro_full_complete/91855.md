hyperimmunity:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈmjuːnɪti/ | n. | 过度免疫，超免疫 | A condition of heightened or excessive immunity.  It refers to an enhanced immune response, often beyond what is considered normal or necessary. |  His hyperimmunity made him resistant to many common infections. 他的过度免疫使他对许多常见感染具有抵抗力。 | The patient's hyperimmunity after the bone marrow transplant was closely monitored.  病人在骨髓移植后的过度免疫状态受到了严密监测。 | 近义词: heightened immunity, excessive immunity | 反义词: immunodeficiency, immunosuppression | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From hyper- + immunity.  "Hyper-" is a Greek prefix meaning "over, above, beyond." "Immunity" comes from the Latin word "immunitas," meaning "exemption from a charge or duty."
