futuristic:/ˌfjuːtʃəˈrɪstɪk/ | adj. | 未来派的；未来主义的 | Relating to or resembling the future, especially in style or design; having or involving very modern technology or design. |  The building had a futuristic design with sleek curves and metallic finishes. 这座建筑采用未来主义设计，线条流畅，金属质感十足。

The movie depicted a futuristic society with flying cars and robots. 这部电影描绘了一个未来社会，有飞行汽车和机器人。 | 近义词: advanced, modern, innovative, space-age, avant-garde | 反义词: outdated, old-fashioned, traditional, antiquated, archaic | 词性: adjective
