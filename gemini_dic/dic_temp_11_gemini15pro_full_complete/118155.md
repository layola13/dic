mensurableness:/ˈmenʃərəblnəs/
n.
可测性，可测度
The quality of being measurable; measurability.
The mensurableness of the results ensures the validity of the experiment.  实验结果的可测性确保了实验的有效性。

近义词: measurability, quantifiability
反义词:  immeasurability, unquantifiability
词性: noun
词源:  From Late Latin *mensurabilis + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin mensura (“measure”) + -bilis (“-ble, capable of being”).
