grumblers: /ˈɡrʌmblərz/ (US), /ˈɡrʌmbləz/ (UK)
| n. | 抱怨者，爱发牢骚的人 | People who complain frequently or habitually. | The grumblers in the office were never happy with anything. 办公室里的抱怨者对任何事情都不满意。| Example: The restaurant was full of grumblers complaining about the service.  这家餐馆里满是抱怨服务差的顾客。 | Synonyms: complainers, whiners, malcontents | Antonyms: optimists, enthusiasts | Noun
