demonize:/ˈdiːmənaɪz/| v.|妖魔化，污名化|To portray someone or something as evil or wicked.|The propaganda campaign sought to demonize the enemy. 宣传活动试图妖魔化敌人。|近义词：vilify, denigrate, defame|反义词：sanctify, glorify|verb
