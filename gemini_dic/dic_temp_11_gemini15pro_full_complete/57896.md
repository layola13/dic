driftiest:/ˈdrɪftiɪst/
adjective
最漂流的；最随波逐流的
Most subject to drifting; most aimless or lacking in direction.
The driftiest boat in the harbor eventually ran aground. 港口里最漂流的船最终搁浅了。
Near synonyms: most adrift, most rootless, most wandering
Near antonyms: most anchored, most grounded, most stable
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From drift + -y + -est.  "Drift" comes from Old Norse *drifa, meaning "to drive, push."
