Hindenburg: /ˈhɪndənbɜːrɡ/ | n. |兴登堡|  Refers to Paul von Hindenburg, a German field marshal and statesman, and also to the LZ 129 Hindenburg, a large German commercial passenger-carrying rigid airship which was destroyed by fire on May 6, 1937, in Lakehurst, New Jersey. | The Hindenburg disaster marked the end of the airship era. 兴登堡空难标志着飞艇时代的结束。|  The name Hindenburg is synonymous with disaster. 兴登堡这个名字是灾难的代名词。 | synonym: zeppelin, dirigible | antonym: N/A | noun
