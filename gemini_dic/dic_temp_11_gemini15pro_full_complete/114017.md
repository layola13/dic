magmatist:/ˈmæɡmətɪst/| n. |岩浆学家|A scientist who studies magma, its formation, and its behavior.  A geologist specializing in the study of magmas.|例句：The magmatist analyzed the chemical composition of the volcanic rock. 岩浆学家分析了火山岩的化学成分。

例句：The magmatist presented her findings on magma chamber evolution at the conference.  岩浆学家在会议上展示了她关于岩浆房演化的研究结果。|近义词：volcanologist, petrologist, igneous petrologist|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
Etymology: From magma + -ist. "Magma" comes from the Greek μάγμα (magma), meaning "thick unguent."  "-ist" is a suffix forming agent nouns.
