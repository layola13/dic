cleat:/kliːt/| n.|防滑钉，夹板，系缆柱|A metal or plastic projection on the sole of a shoe, designed to prevent slipping, or a similar projection on a bicycle pedal.  A piece of wood or metal used to fasten ropes or cables. |The soccer player's cleats dug into the muddy field. 足球运动员的防滑钉陷入了泥泞的场地。|Synonyms: spike, stud, grip, fastener|Antonyms: smooth surface|noun


cleat:/kliːt/| v.|用夹板固定，系住|To fasten with a cleat. |He cleated the rope to the deck. 他把绳子系在甲板上。|Synonyms: secure, fasten, tie, bind|Antonyms: untie, loosen, release|verb
