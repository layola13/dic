frostbow:/ˈfrɔːstbəʊ/ (US) /ˈfrɒstbəʊ/ (UK)| n.|冻疮；霜弓|A condition that causes skin damage due to prolonged exposure to freezing temperatures; a type of magical bow that shoots ice arrows (fantasy context).|例句：
The explorer suffered from frostbow after his expedition to the Arctic.  探险家在北极探险后患上了冻疮。

He wielded a mythical frostbow, capable of freezing even the fiercest dragons.  他挥舞着一把神话般的霜弓，即使是最凶猛的龙也能冻住。


|近义词：frostbite, chilblains (for the medical condition)|反义词: None (no direct opposite for the medical sense; for the fantasy sense, perhaps "firebow")|词性: noun
