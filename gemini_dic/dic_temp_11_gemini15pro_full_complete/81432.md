gratelike:/ˈɡreɪtlaɪk/
adjective
像格栅的；类似格子的
Resembling a grate; having the appearance of a grid or lattice.
The building's facade had a gratelike pattern.  这栋建筑的正面有一个像格栅一样的图案。
The shadows cast a gratelike pattern on the floor.  阴影在地板上投射出格子状的图案。
近义词:gridlike, latticelike, meshlike
反义词:solid, continuous

词性: adjective
