heftinesses:/ˈhɛftɪnɪsɪz/
noun
沉重；份量；重要性
The quality or state of being hefty; weighty, bulky, or substantial.
The heftinesses of the packages surprised the mail carrier.  这些包裹的沉重让邮递员感到惊讶。
heft, weight, bulk, massiveness, ponderousness
lightness, slightness, insubstantiality
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From hefty + -ness. Hefty from Old Norse *hepti (“weight, importance”), from hefja (“to lift”).
