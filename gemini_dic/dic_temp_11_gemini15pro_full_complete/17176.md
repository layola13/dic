batture:/bæˈtʃʊər/ /ˈbætʃər/ | n. |  沙洲；河漫滩；淤积滩 | An alluvial land by the side of a river, especially in Louisiana.  A usually submerged embankment bordering a river. |  The batture was covered with driftwood after the flood.  洪水过后，沙洲上满是浮木。 | synonym:sandbar, shoal, alluvion | antonym:channel, deep water | noun
