monomyary: /ˌmɒnəˈmaɪəri/ | adj. | 单肌的; 单肌性的 | Having only one muscle; characterized by a single muscle. |  The monomyary structure of the organism limited its movement.  这个生物体的单肌结构限制了它的运动。| 近义词: uni-muscular (less common) | 反义词: polymyary (rare), multi-muscular | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
mono- (prefix): from Greek mónos, meaning "alone," "single," "only."
-my- (combining form): relating to muscle.
-ary (suffix): forming adjectives, meaning "pertaining to," "connected with."
