overexpansive: /ˌoʊvərɪkˈspænsɪv/ | adj. | 过度扩张的；过分膨胀的 | Inclined to expand or grow too much; excessively or inappropriately expansive.  Characterized by or tending toward excessive expansion or extension. | The company's overexpansive policies led to its downfall.  这家公司过度扩张的政策导致了它的垮台。| The overexpansive empire eventually crumbled under its own weight.  这个过度扩张的帝国最终在其自身重压下崩溃了。 | synonyms: inflated, excessive, immoderate, extravagant, overextended | antonyms: restrained, moderate, limited, conservative, contracted | adjective
