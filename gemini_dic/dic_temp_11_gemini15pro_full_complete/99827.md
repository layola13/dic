intermingling:/ˌɪntərˈmɪŋɡlɪŋ/
n.|交织，混合|The intermingling of different cultures creates a vibrant society. 不同文化的交织创造了一个充满活力的社会。|
v.|（使）混合，（使）交织|The two rivers intermingle at the confluence. 两条河流在汇合处交汇。|近义词：mixing, blending, merging, integrating|反义词：separating, dividing, segregating|词性: 名词, 动词

intermingle:/ˌɪntərˈmɪŋɡl/
v.|（使）混合，（使）交织|The scents of various flowers intermingled in the garden. 花园里各种花香交织在一起。|The dancers intermingled gracefully on the stage. 舞者们在舞台上优雅地交织在一起。|近义词：mix, blend, merge, integrate|反义词：separate, divide, segregate|词性: 动词

词源: From inter- + mingle, from Old English mengan "to mix."
