expiators: /ˈɛkspiˌeɪtərz/
n.
赎罪者，补偿者
Those who atone for a wrong or sin.
The expiators offered sacrifices to appease the gods.  赎罪者们献祭以安抚众神。
The priest acted as an expiator for the sins of the people.  牧师充当人民罪孽的赎罪者。
synonyms: atoners, redeemers, propitiators
antonyms: sinners, offenders, transgressors
noun
