backstories:/ˈbækˌstɔːriz/| n.|背景故事，幕后故事|A backstory is a history or background, especially one created for a fictional character in a movie, novel, or the like.|The movie provided a compelling backstory for the villain, explaining his motivations.|Synonyms: background, history, origin story|Antonyms: foreground, present|noun
