dendrite:/ˈdɛndraɪt/| n. |树突|A short branched extension of a nerve cell, along which impulses received from other cells at synapses are transmitted to the cell body.|The dendrites receive signals from other neurons.|近义词:nerve ending, axon terminal|反义词:axon|noun
