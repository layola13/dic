emerald: /ˈem(ə)rəld/ (US) /ˈemərəld/ (UK)| n. adj.|祖母绿；翠绿色|A precious stone of a rich green color.  Also used to describe things of this color.|例句：She wore an emerald necklace. 她戴着一条祖母绿项链。|近义词：jade, beryl|反义词: ruby, sapphire|词性: noun, adjective
emerald: /ˈem(ə)rəld/ (US) /ˈemərəld/ (UK)| adj.|翠绿色的|Having a rich green color.|例句：The hills were covered in emerald green grass.  山丘上覆盖着翠绿的草。|近义词：viridescent, jade green|反义词: crimson, scarlet|词性: adjective

词源:
From Old French *esmeraude (modern émeraude), from Latin smaragdus, from Greek σμάραγδος (smáragdos), from a Semitic source (compare Hebrew בָּרֶקֶת (bareqet) "emerald", related to Akkadian baraqtu "emerald", ultimately from a Proto-Semitic word meaning "shine, glitter, flash").
