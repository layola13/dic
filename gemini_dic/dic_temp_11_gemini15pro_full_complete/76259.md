fuzzbox:/ˈfʌzbɒks/ (UK) /ˈfʌzbɑːks/ (US)| n.|失真效果器；电子琴的一种|An electronic device, especially one used with an electric guitar, that produces a distorted sound.|He plugged his guitar into the fuzzbox and cranked up the volume. 他把吉他插到失真效果器上，调大了音量。|Synonyms: distortion pedal, overdrive pedal|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "clean signal" as a contrasting concept)|noun
