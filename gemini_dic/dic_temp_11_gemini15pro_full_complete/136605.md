nonsanguinenesses:/ˌnɒnsæŋˈɡwɪnɪsnɪsɪz/ | n. | 冷静，沉着，不乐观 | Lack of sanguineness; the state of being not sanguine; absence of cheerful confidence; pessimism. |  The general's nonsanguineness about the upcoming battle worried his troops. 将军对即将到来的战斗缺乏乐观情绪，这让他的部队感到担忧。| The nonsanguinenesses displayed by the negotiators made a breakthrough seem unlikely.  谈判者表现出的不乐观使得突破似乎不太可能。 | 近义词: pessimism, cynicism, gloom, despair, negativity | 反义词: sanguineness, optimism, hopefulness, cheerfulness, positivity | 词性: 名词

词源:  From non- (not) + sanguine (optimistic, cheerful) + -ness (suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality).  Ultimately from Latin sanguis (blood), referring to the medieval belief that a person's temperament was determined by the balance of humors in the body, with sanguine people considered to have an excess of blood.
