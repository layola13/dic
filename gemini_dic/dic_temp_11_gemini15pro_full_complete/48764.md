deflagrability:/ˌdiːˌflæɡrəˈbɪləti/| n. |可燃性，易燃性|The quality of being easily set on fire and burning rapidly.|The deflagrability of this material makes it unsuitable for use near open flames. 这种材料的可燃性使其不适合在明火附近使用。|Synonyms: flammability, combustibility|Antonyms: non-flammability, incombustibility|noun
