leadenness:/ˈlɛdənɪs/| n.|沉重，迟钝，阴郁|A state of heaviness, sluggishness, or gloom.|The leadenness of the sky threatened rain. 天空的阴沉预示着即将下雨。|Synonyms: heaviness, dullness, sluggishness, torpor, inertia, gloom, depression|Antonyms: lightness, brightness, liveliness, energy, activity, cheerfulness|noun

leaden:/ˈlɛdən/| adj.|铅制的，沉重的，迟钝的|Made of lead; heavy, dull, or sluggish.|His eyelids felt leaden with fatigue. 疲劳使他的眼皮沉重。|Synonyms: heavy, weighty, ponderous, dull, sluggish, slow|Antonyms: light, buoyant, lively, energetic, quick|adjective
