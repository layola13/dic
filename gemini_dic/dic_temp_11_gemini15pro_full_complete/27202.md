callosum:/kəˈləʊsəm/| n. |胼胝体|The corpus callosum is a large bundle of nerve fibers that connects the two hemispheres of the brain.|The corpus callosum allows for communication between the two hemispheres of the brain. 胼胝体允许大脑两个半球之间的交流。|近义词: commissure (解剖学上的连接)|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *callōsum* (accusative singular of *callōsus* "hard, thick-skinned"), from *callus* "hard skin".
