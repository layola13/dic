envato:/ɛnˈvɑːtoʊ/ | n. |  Envato | An Australian online marketplace for creative assets and talent.  It offers a suite of websites including ThemeForest (website themes), CodeCanyon (code scripts), GraphicRiver (graphics), VideoHive (video footage), AudioJungle (music and sound effects), PhotoDune (stock photos), and 3DOcean (3D models). |  Many web developers use Envato's ThemeForest to find templates for their projects. 许多网页开发者使用Envato的ThemeForest来寻找他们项目的模板。 | synonyms:  creative market, digital marketplace, stock asset platform | antonyms:  brick-and-mortar store, physical store | noun
