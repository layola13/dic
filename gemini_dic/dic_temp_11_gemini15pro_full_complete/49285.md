deliberation:/dɪˌlɪbəˈreɪʃn/
n.
深思熟虑；审议；商议
Careful consideration or discussion of a subject.
The jury reached its verdict after hours of deliberation. 陪审团经过数小时的审议后作出了裁决。
近义词：consideration, reflection, contemplation, discussion
反义词：impulse, recklessness, haste
词性: 名词

deliberate:/dɪˈlɪbəreɪt/
adj.
故意的；蓄意的；慎重的；从容的
Done consciously and intentionally; slow and careful.
His movements were slow and deliberate. 他的动作缓慢而从容。
近义词：intentional, purposeful, conscious, careful
反义词：accidental, unintentional, hasty, impulsive
词性: 形容词

deliberate:/dɪˈlɪbəreɪt/
v.
仔细考虑；商讨
Engage in long and careful consideration.
The committee deliberated for several hours before reaching a decision. 委员会商讨了几个小时才做出决定。
近义词：consider, ponder, contemplate, discuss
反义词：rush, hasten
词性: 动词

词源: From Late Latin *dēliberātiōn-*, from Latin dēlīberāre 'weigh up, consider, consult', from dē- 'thoroughly' + librāre 'weigh'.
