dispensatory:/dɪˈspɛnsətɔːri/ | n. | 药典；配方集 | A book that lists medicines, especially one officially recognized as a standard. |  例句：The pharmacist consulted the dispensatory to verify the correct dosage. 药剂师查阅药典以核实正确的剂量。

例句：The old dispensatory contained many now-obsolete remedies.  这本古老的药典包含许多现在已经过时的药物。| 近义词：pharmacopoeia, formulary | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

dispensary:/dɪˈspɛnsəri/ | n. | 药房；诊疗所 | A place where medicine or medical treatment is dispensed. | 例句：She went to the dispensary to get her prescription filled. 她去药房配药。

例句：The free dispensary offered basic medical care to the poor.  这个免费诊疗所为穷人提供基本的医疗服务。| 近义词：pharmacy, clinic | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dispensatorium, from Latin dispensāre (“to dispense”), from dis- (“apart, away”) + pendere (“to weigh, pay”).  The original sense was "a place where things are weighed out," specifically medicines.
