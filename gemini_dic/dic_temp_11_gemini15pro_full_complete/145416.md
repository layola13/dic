overcerebral: /ˌōvərˈsərəbrəl/ | adj. | 过于理性的；过分动脑筋的 | Excessively intellectual; given to abstract or analytical thought rather than to practical considerations. Characterized by and preoccupied with intellectual activity at the expense of emotional or physical activity.  |  He was too overcerebral to be a good salesman; he spent too much time analyzing and not enough time persuading. 他太过于理性，不适合做一个好销售；他花了太多时间分析，而没有足够的时间去说服。 |  例句：She dismissed his concerns as overcerebral and impractical. 她认为他的担忧过于理性而不切实际。 | 近义词：intellectual, analytical, abstract, theoretical, highbrow, eggheaded | 反义词：practical, pragmatic, instinctive, intuitive, down-to-earth | 词性: 形容词
