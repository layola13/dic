buzzardlike:/ˈbʌzərdlaɪk/| adj.|像秃鹰的，秃鹰似的|Resembling or characteristic of a buzzard, especially in being predatory or scavenging.  Having qualities (like keen eyesight or a scavenging habit) that are associated with buzzards.|His buzzardlike gaze swept over the battlefield, searching for the wounded.  他像秃鹰一样的目光扫过战场，寻找着伤员。|近义词：predatory, rapacious, scavenging, hawklike, vulturine|反义词:generous, benevolent|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From buzzard +‎ -like.
