dictating:/ˈdɪkteɪtɪŋ/ |v. |口述，命令，支配|The boss is dictating a letter to his secretary. 老板正在向他的秘书口述一封信。|近义词：ordering, commanding, imposing|反义词：obeying, complying, submitting|词性: 动名词 (gerund) or present participle of "dictate"
Etymology: From Latin dictare "to say often, prescribe," frequentative of dicere "to say, speak."
