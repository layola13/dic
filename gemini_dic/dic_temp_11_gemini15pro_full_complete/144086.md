outsailing:/ˌaʊtˈseɪlɪŋ/
verb
胜过航行，以优越的航海技术超越
to sail faster or more skillfully than another vessel
The newer yacht easily outsailing the older boats in the race.  这艘新游艇在比赛中轻松超越了旧船。
近义词：outmaneuver, surpass, beat
反义词：be outsailed by, lose to
词性：动词
