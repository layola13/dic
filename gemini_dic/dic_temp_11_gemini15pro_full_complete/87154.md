herbivore:/ˈhɜːrbɪvɔːr/ (US), /ˈhɜːbɪvɔː(r)/ (UK)| n.|食草动物|An animal that feeds on plants.|Cows are herbivores. 奶牛是食草动物。|Rabbits are herbivores and eat grass and vegetables. 兔子是食草动物，吃草和蔬菜。|近义词：vegetarian, plant-eater|反义词:carnivore, omnivore|noun
