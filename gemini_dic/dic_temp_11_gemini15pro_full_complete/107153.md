laevo:/ˈliːvoʊ/ /ˈleɪvoʊ/
adjective
左旋的, 左边的
Rotating plane-polarized light to the left, counterclockwise.  Relating to the left side or left-handed form of a molecule.
Example: Laevo-rotatory substances rotate light to the left.  左旋物质使光向左旋转。

Example:  The laevo isomer of the drug is more effective.  这种药物的左旋异构体更有效。
synonym: left-handed, levorotatory
antonym: dextro, right-handed
词性: adjective
