disuniformity:/ˌdɪsˌjuːnɪˈfɔːrmɪti/
n.
不均匀性，不一致性
The lack of uniformity; the state or quality of being disuniform.
The disuniformity of the paint job was obvious, with patches of lighter and darker shades. 油漆工作的参差不齐很明显，有深浅不一的色块。
The disuniformity in the size of the apples made them difficult to pack efficiently. 苹果大小不一，难以有效包装。
近义词：nonuniformity, heterogeneity, inconsistency, irregularity, unevenness, variability
反义词：uniformity, homogeneity, consistency, regularity, evenness
词性: 名词

词源: dis- (not) + uniformity (the state of being uniform)  From Latin dis- "not, apart" + uniformis "having the same form," from uni- "one" + forma "form, shape."
