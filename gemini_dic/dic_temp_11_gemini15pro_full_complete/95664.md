incompressibility:/ˌɪnkəmˌprɛsəˈbɪləti/| n.|不可压缩性|The quality or state of being incompressible; resistance to compression.|The incompressibility of water makes it a useful hydraulic fluid.  水的不可压缩性使其成为一种有用的液压流体。|Near synonyms: noncompressibility, impenetrability|Near antonyms: compressibility, squeezability|noun
