impulsions:/ɪmˈpʌlʃənz/
n.
冲动，推动，刺激
A sudden, strong and unreflective urge or desire to act.
He felt an impulsion to tell her everything. 他感到一股冲动，想把一切都告诉她。
impulses: /ɪmˈpʌlʃənz/
n.
冲动
Sudden, involuntary inclinations to act.
Her impulses often led her into trouble. 她的冲动常常使她陷入麻烦。
impulsions:/ɪmˈpʌlʃənz/
n.
推动，刺激
Driving forces or influences.
The new tax laws are expected to provide fresh impulses for investment. 新的税法有望为投资提供新的刺激。
impulsions:/ɪmˈpʌlʃənz/
近义词: urges, desires, impulses, drives, instincts
反义词: inhibitions, restraints, controls
