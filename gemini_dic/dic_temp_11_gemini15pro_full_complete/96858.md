inexcusably:/ˌɪnɪkˈskjuːzəbli/
adverb
不可原谅地， inexcusably
Without any excuse or justification.
He behaved inexcusably. 他表现得不可原谅。
近义词: unforgivably, unpardonably, indefensibly
反义词: justifiably, pardonably, excusably
词性: adverb

词源:
From in- (not) + excusable + -ly (adverb suffix).  Excusable itself comes from the Late Latin excusabilis, from excusare (to excuse), from ex- (out) + causa (cause, reason).
