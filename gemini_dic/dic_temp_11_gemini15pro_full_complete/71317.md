fizzing:/ˈfɪzɪŋ/| adj.  v. (present participle of fizz)|嘶嘶作响的，起泡的|Making a hissing sound like carbonated water.  The sound itself can also be referred to as fizzing.|The fizzing drink was refreshing on a hot day.  炎热的一天，嘶嘶作响的饮料令人神清气爽。|近义词：bubbling, effervescent, sparkling|反义词:flat, still|词性: adjective, verb (present participle)
fizz:/fɪz/| n. v.|嘶嘶声，起泡|A hissing sound, or to make such a sound.|The fizz of the champagne added to the celebratory atmosphere.  香槟的嘶嘶声增添了庆祝的气氛。|近义词：hiss, bubble, sparkle|反义词:none|词性: noun, verb
