anthemic:/ænˈθɛmɪk/
adjective
像赞美诗的；雄壮的；激昂的；适合做圣歌的
Resembling or suggestive of an anthem; rousing, uplifting, inspiring.  Characterized by qualities that make it suitable for use as an anthem.
The band played an anthemic song that had everyone singing along. 乐队演奏了一首雄壮的歌曲，每个人都跟着唱。
The crowd roared with excitement as the anthemic music filled the stadium. 当雄壮的音乐响彻体育场时，人群兴奋地欢呼起来。
近义词: uplifting, inspiring, rousing, majestic, grand
反义词: depressing, melancholic, somber, subdued
