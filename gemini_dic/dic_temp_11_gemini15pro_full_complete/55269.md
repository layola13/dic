dissipaters:/ˈdɪsɪpeɪtərz/
|n.| 散热器；消散者；挥霍者
|Something that dissipates, especially a device for dissipating heat.|
|The computer uses heat dissipaters to keep the CPU cool.| 这台电脑使用散热器来保持CPU冷却。
|synonyms: dispersers, scatterers, diffusers, radiators (for heat), spendthrifts (for money), squanderers (for money)|
|antonyms: concentrators, collectors, accumulators, savers (for money)|
|noun|


dissipate:/ˈdɪsɪpeɪt/
|v.| 消散；驱散；浪费
|To cause to disappear or disperse; to scatter; to squander or fritter away (money, energy, or resources).|
|The fog dissipated quickly in the morning sun.| 晨曦中，雾气迅速消散。
|He dissipated his inheritance on gambling.| 他把遗产都赌光了。
|synonyms: disperse, scatter, diffuse, vanish, evaporate, squander, waste|
|antonyms: gather, collect, accumulate, concentrate, save, conserve|
|verb|
