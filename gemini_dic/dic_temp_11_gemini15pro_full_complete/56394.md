dolefullest:/ˈdoulfʊləst/ | adj. | 最悲伤的，最凄凉的 |  Superlative form of doleful: expressing sorrow; mournful.  The dolefullest sound I ever heard was the lone howl of a wolf in the wilderness. | 例句：The dolefullest music filled the old, abandoned house.  最悲伤的音乐弥漫在这座古老而废弃的房子里。

例句：His dolefullest expression appeared when he heard the news. 当他听到这个消息时，他露出了最悲伤的表情。 | 近义词：saddest, most mournful, most gloomy | 反义词：happiest, most cheerful, most joyful | 词性：形容词

词源：From Middle English *dolefulest, from doleful + -est.  Doleful itself comes from dole "grief, sorrow" + -ful.  Ultimately from Old French duel, from Latin dolus "grief, pain, deceit."
