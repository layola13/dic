constabulary's:/kənˈstæbjʊlɛriz/
noun
警察部门的；警察队伍的
belonging to or associated with a constabulary (police force).
The constabulary's budget was cut this year. 今年警察部门的预算被削减了。
Synonyms: police force's, law enforcement's
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for possession.  Could consider "civilian's" in some contexts)
