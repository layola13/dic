hyperethically:/ˌhaɪpərˈɛθɪkli/
adv.
极其符合伦理地，超级伦理地
Acting in a way that goes beyond the usual ethical standards, often to an excessive degree.
She approached the dilemma hyperethically, considering not only the immediate consequences but also the potential long-term impact on all stakeholders.
近义词:over-scrupulously, excessively ethically
反义词:unethically, immorally
词性:副词

Etymology:
From "hyper-" (Greek, meaning "over, beyond") + "ethical" (from Greek "ethikos," meaning "relating to moral character").
