besiegers:/bɪˈsiːdʒərz/
noun
包围者，攻城者
Those who besiege a place; those who lay siege to a town, fortress, etc.
The besiegers bombarded the city walls day and night.  攻城者日夜轰炸城墙。
The besiegers finally breached the castle walls. 攻城者最终攻破了城堡的城墙。
synonyms: attackers, assailants, invaders
antonyms: defenders, protectors, besieged
n.
