climber:/ˈklaɪmər/ (US), /ˈklaɪmə/ (UK)| n.|攀登者；攀缘植物；向上爬的人或物|A person or plant that climbs. |The climber reached the summit of the mountain.  登山者到达了山顶。|Near Synonyms: mountaineer, alpinist, scaler, ascender|Antonyms: descender|Noun

climber:/ˈklaɪmər/ (US), /ˈklaɪmə/ (UK)| n.|攀缘植物；攀爬植物|A plant that climbs, typically using tendrils, suckers, or other specialized structures. |The climber covered the entire wall with its lush foliage.  这种攀缘植物用茂密的叶子覆盖了整面墙。|Near Synonyms: vine, creeper, rambler|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "groundcover")|Noun
