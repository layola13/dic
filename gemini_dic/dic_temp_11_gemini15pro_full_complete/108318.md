laurinol:/ˈlɔːrɪnɒl/ | n. |月桂醇, 十二烷醇 | A fatty alcohol found in bayberries and other plants. It is a white, crystalline solid at room temperature.  |  Laurinol is used in the production of surfactants and other chemicals. 月桂醇用于生产表面活性剂和其他化学物质。 | Laurinol is a common ingredient in cosmetics. 月桂醇是化妆品的常见成分。 | Synonyms: dodecanol, 1-dodecanol | Antonyms: N/A | noun
