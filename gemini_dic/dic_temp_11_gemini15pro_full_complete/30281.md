cementitious:/sɪˌmɛnˈtɪʃəs/
adjective
水泥的；胶结的
Relating to or resembling cement; having binding properties.
The cementitious material used in the construction was a special blend.  这种建筑中使用的水泥材料是一种特殊的混合物。
The mixture had cementitious properties, allowing it to harden and bind the bricks together. 这种混合物具有胶结性，使其能够硬化并将砖块粘合在一起。

synonym: binding, adhesive, cohesive
antonym: non-binding, loose, separating
词性: adjective
