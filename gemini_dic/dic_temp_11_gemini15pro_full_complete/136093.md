nonreconciliation:/nɒnˌrɛkənˌsɪliˈeɪʃən/
n.
不和解，不调和
lack of reconciliation; failure to reconcile.
The nonreconciliation between the two families lasted for generations.  两个家族之间的不和解持续了几代人。
The committee expressed concern over the continued nonreconciliation of the warring factions. 委员会对交战派别持续不和解表示担忧。

近义词:discord, disunity, conflict, estrangement
反义词:reconciliation, accord, harmony, agreement
词性:名词
