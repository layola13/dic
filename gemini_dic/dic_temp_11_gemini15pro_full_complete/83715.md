haglike:/ˈhæɡlaɪk/| adj.|像巫婆的，丑陋的|Resembling a hag, especially in being ugly or frightening.|The old woman had a haglike appearance, with a crooked nose and warts on her chin.  这个老妇人有着女巫般的外表，鹰钩鼻，下巴上还有疣。|Synonyms: witchlike, cronelike, hideous, repulsive|Antonyms: beautiful, attractive, lovely|adjective


Etymology->
From hag +‎ -like.
