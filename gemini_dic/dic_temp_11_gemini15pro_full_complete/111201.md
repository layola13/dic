lithomarge:/ˈlɪθəˌmɑːrdʒ/
n.
lithomarge; 軟黏土; 石脂土
A smooth, compact, earthy clay, usually white, pale yellow, or red. It is a hydrous silicate of alumina.
Lithomarge is often found in association with iron deposits. 石脂土常与铁矿伴生。
Synonym: clay, kaolin
Antonym: rock, sand
词性: noun
