cementless:/sɪˈmɛntləs/
adjective
无水泥的
Without cement.  Used especially in medical contexts to describe implants that are fixed in place without bone cement.
The surgeon opted for a cementless hip replacement.  外科医生选择了无水泥的髋关节置换手术。
cementless total knee arthroplasty (TKA)  无水泥全膝关节置换术
synonym: uncemented
antonym: cemented
