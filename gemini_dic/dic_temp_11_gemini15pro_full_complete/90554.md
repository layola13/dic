hugged:/hʌɡd/
verb
拥抱(过去式)
past tense of hug, meaning to hold someone close to your body with your arms, usually to show affection.
She hugged her son tightly before he left for college. 她紧紧拥抱了儿子，然后他才离开去上大学。
embraced, cuddled, squeezed
released, pushed away
