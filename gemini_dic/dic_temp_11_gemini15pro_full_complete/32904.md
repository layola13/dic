choo-choo:/ˈtʃuː tʃuː/
noun
火车(儿童用语)
the sound a train makes, often used by children
例句：The little boy loves playing with his choo-choo train. 小男孩喜欢玩他的火车玩具。

例句：My nephew points at the train and shouts "Choo-choo!" 我的侄子指着火车大喊“啾啾！”

近义词：train
反义词：(None -  choo-choo is a childish term and doesn't have a direct opposite)
词性:n.
