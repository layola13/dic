Etymology:  a- (prefix meaning "not, without") + sexual + -ity (suffix forming nouns of quality or state) From the prefix "a-" meaning "not" or "without," combined with "sexual" referring to sex or sexuality, and the suffix "-ity" which forms nouns indicating a state or quality.
