gelastic:/dʒɪˈlæstɪk/
adjective
笑的；由笑引起的；与笑有关的
Relating to or caused by laughter; characterized by laughter.
例句：He suffers from gelastic epilepsy, a rare form of epilepsy that causes seizures triggered by laughter. 他患有笑性癫痫，这是一种罕见的癫痫，由笑引发癫痫发作。

例句：The gelastic syncope is a rare condition characterized by a sudden loss of consciousness caused by laughter. 笑性晕厥是一种罕见的疾病，其特征是因大笑而突然失去知觉。

近义词：laughing, mirthful, humorous
反义词：serious, somber, grave
词性：adjective
