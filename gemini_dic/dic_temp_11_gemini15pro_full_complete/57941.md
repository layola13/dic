drinking:/ˈdrɪŋkɪŋ/| n. adj.| 饮酒；喝酒；饮料|The act of consuming liquids, especially alcoholic beverages. Also, something that can be drunk.|
He enjoys drinking a glass of wine with dinner. 他喜欢晚餐时喝一杯葡萄酒。

The drinking water here is safe.  这里的饮用水是安全的。
|近义词：imbibing, consumption (of beverages)|反义词：abstinence, sobriety|n. adj.
