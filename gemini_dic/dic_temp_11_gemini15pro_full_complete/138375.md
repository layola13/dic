notist:/ˈnəʊtɪst/ | n. |记录者；抄写员；公证人| A person who takes notes, especially one employed to make a formal record of proceedings. | The notist carefully recorded every word of the meeting.  记录员仔细地记录了会议的每一个字。 |Synonyms: recorder, scribe, clerk | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "forgetful person" or "inattentive listener" in a loose sense) | noun

/ˈnəʊtɪst/ | n. |记谱者；（尤指）速记员| A person who writes down musical notes, especially a shorthand writer. | The composer dictated the melody while the notist transcribed it onto sheet music. 作曲家口述旋律，而记谱员将其抄写到乐谱上。 | Synonyms: transcriber, musical scribe | Antonyms: (No direct antonym) | noun
