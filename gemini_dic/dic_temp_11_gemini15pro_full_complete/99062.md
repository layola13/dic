interassures:/ˌɪntərəˈʃʊrz/ | v. |相互保证，互相安慰| To reassure each other; to provide mutual comfort and support. |  "The two friends interassured each other that everything would be alright." 两个朋友互相安慰说一切都会好起来的。 | 近义词: reassure, comfort, support, encourage | 反义词: discourage, distress, worry | 词性:动词

词源:  "Inter-" (between) + "assure" (to give confidence to).  The word is a combination of the prefix "inter-" indicating reciprocity and the verb "assure."
