abjudicate:/əˈdʒuːdɪkeɪt/
verb
正式宣判；裁决；剥夺
To formally pronounce or decree judicially; to deprive by judicial decision.
The court abjudicated him of his rights. 法庭剥夺了他的权利。
近义词: adjudge, decree, rule, decide, determine
反义词:  acquit, award, grant, reinstate
词性: verb
