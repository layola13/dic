de-seeding:/dɪˈsiːdɪŋ/
noun
去籽；除籽
the process of removing seeds from a fruit or vegetable
例句：The de-seeding of the grapes is a time-consuming process.  葡萄去籽是一个耗时的过程。

例句：A machine is used for the de-seeding of pomegranates.  用机器给石榴去籽。
synonyms: seeding removal, seed extraction
antonyms: seeding, planting
n.
