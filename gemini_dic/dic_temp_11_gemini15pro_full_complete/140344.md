ohm:/oʊm/ | n. | 欧姆 |The SI unit of electrical resistance, transmitting a current of one ampere when subjected to a potential difference of one volt. | The resistance of this wire is about 10 ohms. 这根导线的电阻大约是10欧姆。|近义词: unit of resistance |反义词:  (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement) | noun
