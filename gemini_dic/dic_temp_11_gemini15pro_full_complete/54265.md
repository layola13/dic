degradation: /ˌdɛɡrəˈdeɪʃən/ | n. | 降级；退化；降解；堕落 |  The process of something being reduced to a lower quality, condition, or status.  | The environmental degradation caused by pollution is a serious concern.  环境污染造成的环境恶化是一个严重的问题。 | Synonyms: deterioration, decline, decay, degeneration | Antonyms: improvement, enhancement, elevation, amelioration | noun

degradation: /ˌdɛɡrəˈdeɪʃən/ | n. | 降解；分解 | The process of breaking down a substance into simpler components, often by chemical or biological means. |  The degradation of plastic in the environment takes hundreds of years. 塑料在环境中的降解需要数百年时间。 | Synonyms: decomposition, breakdown, disintegration | Antonyms: synthesis, formation, building up | noun
