gammoner:/ˈɡæmənə(r)/
n.
骗子，吹牛者
A person who tells lies or exaggerates; a con artist.
He's a real gammoner, always telling tall tales. 他真是个骗子，总是吹牛。
近义词:liar, fraud, imposter, cheat, swindler
反义词:honest person

词性:noun

词源:
From obsolete *gammon*, "nonsense, trickery," perhaps from Anglo-Norman *gamen*, "game, amusement, trick."
