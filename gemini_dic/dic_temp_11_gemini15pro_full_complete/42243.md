countdown:/ˈkaʊntdaʊn/| n.|倒计时，倒数|a period that leads up to an important event, marked by counting down days, hours, etc.|The countdown to the launch of the new product has begun. 新产品发布的倒计时已经开始了。|synonyms: count backward, reverse count|antonyms: count up|noun

countdown:/ˈkaʊntdaʊn/| v.|倒计时，倒数|to count backward to zero, usually to mark the time until an important event|We're counting down the days until our vacation. 我们正在倒数假期到来的日子。|synonyms: count backward, reverse count|antonyms: count up|verb
