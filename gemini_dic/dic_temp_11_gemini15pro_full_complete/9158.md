antinature:/ˌæntaɪˈneɪtʃər/ | n. |违反自然，悖逆自然|Something that is unnatural or violates the laws of nature.  A strong aversion or opposition to what is considered natural or normal.|例句：His treatment of animals was an antinature. 他对待动物的方式违反自然。|例句：Antinature is often explored in science fiction. 违反自然常在科幻小说中被探讨。|近义词：unnaturalness, perversion, abnormality|反义词：nature, naturalness, normality|词性:名词

词源：
anti- (prefix) : against, opposed to (源自希腊语 anti-)
nature (noun): the phenomena of the physical world collectively, including plants, animals, the landscape, and other features and products of the earth, as opposed to humans or human creations. (源自拉丁语 natura)
