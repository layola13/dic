counterrevolutionary:/ˌkaʊntərˌrɛvəˈluːʃənɛri/| adj. n.|反革命的，反革命分子|Opposed to or seeking to overthrow a revolution.|例句：The government labeled the protesters as counterrevolutionary. 政府将抗议者贴上反革命的标签。
