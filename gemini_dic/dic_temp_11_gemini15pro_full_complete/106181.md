knocked:/nɒkt/ (UK) /nɑːkt/ (US)| v.|敲，击，撞；打败，击败；批评，非难|To strike something, typically with the knuckles or a hard object; to defeat or surpass; to criticize.|例句：Someone knocked on the door. 有人敲门。

例句：The boxer knocked out his opponent in the first round.  拳击手在第一回合就击倒了对手。

近义词：hit, rap, tap, beat, strike, defeat, criticize|反义词:praise, commend|词性:动词

knocked:/nɒkt/ (UK) /nɑːkt/ (US)| adj.|(非正式) 疲惫的，筋疲力尽的|Exhausted, worn out (informal usage).|例句：I'm absolutely knocked after that run. 那次跑步后我累坏了。

例句：He felt knocked out after working a double shift.  上完两班倒后，他感到筋疲力尽。

近义词：tired, exhausted, worn out|反义词:energetic, refreshed|词性:形容词
