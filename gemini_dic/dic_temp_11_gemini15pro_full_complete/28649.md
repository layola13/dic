caricatural:/ˌkærɪkəˈtʃʊərəl/| adj.|漫画式的，夸张的|Characterized by or resembling caricature; distorted or exaggerated for comic effect.  It derives from the French word "caricature," ultimately from Italian "caricare" meaning "to load, exaggerate."|His caricatural depiction of the president drew both laughter and criticism.
