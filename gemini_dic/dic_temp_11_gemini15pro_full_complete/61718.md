emotionalism:/ɪˈmɒʊʃənəlɪzəm/ /ɪˈmoʊʃənəlɪzəm/| n.|感情主义，情绪化|An excessive expression or display of emotion.  An overemphasis on emotion. |例句：The film was criticized for its excessive emotionalism.  这部电影因其过度的感情主义而受到批评。

例句：Her emotionalism made it difficult to have a rational discussion. 她的情绪化使理性讨论变得困难。|近义词：sentimentality, effusion, excitability|反义词：stoicism, restraint, composure|词性:noun
