hungering:/ˈhʌŋɡərɪŋ/| adj.|渴望的，渴求的| Feeling a strong desire or craving for something, typically food. |例句：The refugees were hungering for news from their families. 难民们渴望得到家人的消息。

例句：He was hungering for knowledge and experience. 他渴望知识和经验。

|近义词：craving, yearning, longing, desiring|反义词：sated, satisfied, full|词性:形容词
