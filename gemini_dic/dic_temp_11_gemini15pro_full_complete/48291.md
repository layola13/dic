decreasingly:/dɪˈkriːsɪŋli/
adverb
递减地，越来越少地
in a way that becomes smaller, weaker, etc.
The population is decreasingly rural.  人口越来越少地居住在农村。
The company's profits have been decreasingly steadily over the past few years.  这家公司的利润在过去几年里一直在稳步下降。
近义词:progressively less, diminishingly
反义词:increasingly
词性:adverb
