noninterruptive:/ˌnɑːnɪntəˈrʌptɪv/
adjective
不间断的，非中断的
Not interrupting the flow or progress of something; allowing something to continue without interfering.
The software update was noninterruptive, allowing users to continue working.  这个软件更新是不间断的，允许用户继续工作。
The system performs noninterruptive backups. 系统执行不间断备份。
near synonyms:  continuous, seamless, uninterrupted, smooth
antonyms: disruptive, interrupting, interfering
词性: 形容词
