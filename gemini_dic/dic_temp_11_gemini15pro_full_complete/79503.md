globe:/ɡloʊb/ (US) /ɡləʊb/ (UK)| n.|地球仪；地球；球体|A globe is a spherical model of Earth, or of the celestial sphere.|She spun the globe and pointed to a small island. 她转动地球仪，指着一个小岛。|The Earth is not a perfect globe. 地球并非一个完美的球体。|近义词：sphere, earth, world|反义词:|noun
