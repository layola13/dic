bulking: /ˈbʌlkɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈbʌlkɪŋ/ (US)| adj. n.|增重的，增肌的；膨胀，增大|The process of gaining weight, typically muscle mass, by consuming a calorie surplus.  It can also refer to the appearance of being large or bulky.| He's been bulking up for his next bodybuilding competition. 他一直在为下一次健美比赛增重。|Synonyms:  building, enlarging, expanding, increasing, growing|Antonyms:  shrinking, reducing, slimming, cutting, dieting|adjective, noun
