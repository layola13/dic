lathwork: /ˈlæθwɜːrk/ (US) /ˈlɑːθwɜːrk/ (UK)
n.
板条工, 板条制品, 板条结构
Open framework of laths, used especially as a base for plaster or stucco.
The ceiling was made of intricate lathwork. 天花板由复杂的板条构成。
近义词: latticework, framework
反义词: solid construction
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From 'lath' (a thin, narrow strip of wood) + 'work' (the result of action or labor).  'Lath' comes from Old English 'lætt', meaning 'lath, bat'.
