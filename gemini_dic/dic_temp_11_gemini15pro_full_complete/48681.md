deferrers:/dɪˈfɜːrərz/| n.| 延期者，推迟者 | People who defer something, typically payment or action. |  Many deferrers are now facing penalties for late payment. 许多延期付款者现在面临滞纳金。| synonyms: postponers, procrastinators, delayers | antonyms: expediters, agilists | noun
Etymology: From the verb "defer" (from Old French differer, from Latin differre "to carry different ways, put off, postpone"), + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting a person or thing that performs a specified action).
