adenoidal:/ˌædɪˈnɔɪdəl/| adj.|腺样体的，鼻音重的|Relating to or resembling the adenoids; having a nasal quality of voice, typically as a result of enlarged adenoids.|Example:  He spoke in a flat, adenoidal voice. 他说话声音扁平，带有鼻音。

Example:  The child's adenoidal breathing was a cause for concern.  这孩子用腺样体呼吸令人担忧。|近义词：nasal, twangy|反义词: clear, resonant|词性:形容词
