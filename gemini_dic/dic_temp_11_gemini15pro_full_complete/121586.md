misoccupied:/ˌmɪsˈɒkjupaɪd/ | adj. | 心不在焉的，专注于错误事物的 | Preoccupied with something unimportant or inappropriate; distracted. | He seemed misoccupied with trivial matters, ignoring the urgent deadline. 他似乎专注于琐碎的事情，忽略了紧急的截止日期。|  He was so misoccupied with his phone that he walked into a lamppost. 他如此专注于他的手机，以至于撞到了路灯杆上。|Synonyms: distracted, preoccupied, inattentive, absorbed, engrossed | Antonyms: attentive, focused, concentrated | adjective
