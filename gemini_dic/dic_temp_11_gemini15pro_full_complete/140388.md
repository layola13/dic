oilnut:/ˈɔɪlnʌt/| n.|油桐树，油桐果|A tree (Aleurites fordii) native to China, cultivated for its seeds which yield tung oil.|例句：The oilnut tree is prized for its oil-rich seeds. 油桐树因其富含油脂的种子而备受珍视。

例句：Oilnut trees are commonly grown in warmer climates. 油桐树通常生长在温暖的气候中。|近义词：tung tree, tung oil tree|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
