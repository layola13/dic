divertible:/daɪˈvɜːrtəbl/ /dɪˈvɜːrtəbl/| adj.|可转移的，可改变用途的|Capable of being diverted or turned aside from a course or direction; capable of being used for a different purpose.|The river's water is divertible to irrigate the fields. 这条河的水可以用来灌溉田地。|The funds are divertible to other projects. 这些资金可以用于其他项目。|近义词：redirect-able, deflectable, shiftable|反义词：fixed, inflexible, unchangeable|词性:形容词

divert:/daɪˈvɜːrt/ /dɪˈvɜːrt/| vt.|转移，使转向，使分心|To turn aside from a course or direction; to draw the attention of someone from something.|Police diverted traffic around the accident scene. 警察将交通改道绕过事故现场。|The magician diverted the audience's attention while his assistant hid the rabbit. 魔术师转移了观众的注意力，同时他的助手藏起了兔子。|近义词：redirect, deflect, distract|反义词：maintain, focus, concentrate|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dīvertibilis, from Latin dīvertere "to turn aside," from dī- "aside" + vertere "to turn."
