overdye:/ˈoʊvərˌdaɪ/ (US), /ˌəʊvəˈdaɪ/ (UK)| v. n. adj.|过度染色，套染；套染的织物|To dye (fabric) again, typically a darker shade, after it has already been dyed.  As a noun, it refers to the fabric that has been overdyed. As an adjective, it describes something that has been overdyed.|
例句：
She overdyed the faded curtains a rich burgundy. 她把褪色的窗帘套染成了浓郁的酒红色。

The overdye process created a unique, vintage look. 套染工艺创造了独特的复古外观。


近义词：redye, tint|
反义词:bleach, fade|
词性:verb, noun, adjective|

overdye:/ˈoʊvərˌdaɪ/ (US), /ˌəʊvəˈdaɪ/ (UK)| adj.|套染的|Dyed over a previous color.|
例句：
The overdye fabric had a subtle two-toned effect. 套染的面料有一种微妙的双色效果。

She wore an overdye shirt with jeans. 她穿着一件套染衬衫和牛仔裤。

近义词：redyed|
反义词:N/A|
词性:adjective|

词源:  "Over-" (meaning "above" or "in addition") + "dye" (meaning "to color").  The term reflects the process of adding a second dye over an existing color.
