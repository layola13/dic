despotic:/dɪˈspɒtɪk/ (US) /dɛˈspɑːtɪk/| adj.|专横的，暴虐的，独裁的|Of, relating to, or characteristic of a despot; tyrannical.  Exercising absolute power, often in a cruel or oppressive way.|The despotic ruler governed with an iron fist, silencing any opposition.|近义词：tyrannical, autocratic, dictatorial, authoritarian|反义词：democratic, egalitarian, liberal|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *despoticus*, from Greek *despotikos*, from *despotēs* "master, lord, absolute ruler".
