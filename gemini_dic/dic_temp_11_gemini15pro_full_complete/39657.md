conservativeness:/kənˈsɜːrvətɪvnəs/| n.|保守性，保守主义|The quality or state of being conservative; avoidance of excess; moderation; caution.|Her conservativeness in financial matters is commendable. 她在财务方面的保守性值得称赞。|近义词：caution, prudence, traditionalism|反义词：radicalism, liberalism, progressiveness|词性:名词

Etymology: From conservative + -ness.  "Conservative" traces back to Late Latin *conservativus, from Latin conservare "to preserve," from com- "together" + servare "to keep."
