eventualities:/ɪˌvɛntʃuˈælɪtiz/| n. |可能性；可能发生的事情；不测事件|Possible outcomes or occurrences, especially those that are unpleasant or unwanted.  It refers to all the things that might happen in the future, especially things you have to be prepared for.|We must consider all eventualities. 我们必须考虑所有可能发生的情况。|Synonyms: possibilities, contingencies, outcomes, prospects|Antonyms: certainties, impossibilities|noun
