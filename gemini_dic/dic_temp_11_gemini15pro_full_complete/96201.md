indevotion: /ˌɪndɪˈvoʊʃən/
noun
缺乏奉献精神；不虔诚
lack of devotion; impiety
He showed indevotion to his work, often arriving late and leaving early.  他对工作缺乏奉献精神，经常迟到早退。
The priest condemned the king's indevotion to the church.  神父谴责国王对教会不虔诚。
Near synonyms:  irreverence, impiety, apathy, indifference
Antonyms: devotion, piety, zeal, fervor
词性: 名词
