areole/ˈæriəʊl/ | n. | 乳晕；仙人掌的刺座 | A small raised area, especially the ring of pigmented skin surrounding a nipple, or the area on a cactus from which spines grow. | The areole around her nipple was sensitive to the touch. 她的乳晕对触摸很敏感。| The cactus areole produced a cluster of sharp spines.  仙人掌的刺座长出了一簇尖刺。 | synonyms:  papilla, disk, mound | antonyms: N/A | noun

areolet/ˈæriəʊlɛt/ | n. | 小乳晕; 小刺座 | A small areole.  A diminutive of areole. |  The botanist examined the areolet under a microscope.  植物学家在显微镜下检查了小刺座。 | Tiny hairs grew from the areolet on the cactus. 小刺座上长出了细小的毛发。 | synonyms:  areola,  papilla (in some contexts) | antonyms: N/A | noun
