labid:/ˈlæbɪd/| adj.| Forceps-like; resembling forceps.| Having the form of pincers or forceps.  Relating to or resembling a labidophorous wasp. |The labid mandibles of the insect allowed it to firmly grasp its prey. 昆虫钳状的上颚使其能够牢牢抓住猎物。|近义词：forcipate, chelate, pincer-like|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *labis* (forceps, tongs) + *-id* (suffix forming adjectives).
