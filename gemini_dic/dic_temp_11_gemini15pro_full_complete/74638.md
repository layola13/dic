free-basing:/ˈfriːˌbeɪsɪŋ/
n.
游离碱化；(尤指)吸食可卡因游离碱
The process of converting cocaine into its freebase form for smoking.  This involves removing hydrochloride from cocaine powder, resulting in a more potent and smokable form of the drug.
He was arrested for free-basing cocaine. 他因吸食可卡因游离碱而被捕。
近义词:  crack smoking,  cocaine use
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "abstinence" or "sobriety")
词性: noun
