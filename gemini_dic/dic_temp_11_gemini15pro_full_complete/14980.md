babbitted:/ˈbæbɪtɪd/
adjective
巴氏合金处理的，衬有巴氏合金的
lined or coated with babbitt metal (a soft, white, non-ferrous alloy of tin, copper, and antimony used as a bearing surface).
The engine's babbitted bearings ensured smooth operation.  发动机的巴氏合金轴承确保了平稳运转。
The crankshaft journals were babbitted to reduce friction. 曲轴轴颈衬有巴氏合金以减少摩擦。
近义词: lined (with babbitt), bearinged
反义词: (None, since it describes a specific process)
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From the name of Isaac Babbitt (1799–1862), the American inventor who patented the alloy in 1839.  源自美国发明家伊萨克·巴比特（1799-1862）的名字，他在1839年获得了该合金的专利。
