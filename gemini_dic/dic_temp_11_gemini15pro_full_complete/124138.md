mortiferous:/ˈmɔːrtɪfərəs/| adj.|致命的，致死的|Causing or likely to cause death; deadly.|例句：The Black Death was a mortiferous plague that swept across Europe. 黑死病是一场席卷欧洲的致命瘟疫。

例句：The venom of that snake is mortiferous. 那条蛇的毒液是致命的。|近义词：lethal, deadly, fatal, mortal|反义词：life-giving, invigorating, salubrious|词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *mortifer, from Latin mors (genitive mortis) "death" + -fer "bearing."  Equivalent to mort(i)- + -ferous.
