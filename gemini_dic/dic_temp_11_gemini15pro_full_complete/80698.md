gorgonian:/ɡɔːrˈɡəʊniən/ | n. | 柳珊瑚；海扇 | A type of coral having a horny or flexible skeleton.  A sessile marine invertebrate of the order Gorgonacea, such as a sea fan or sea whip. |  例句：The divers marveled at the vibrant gorgonians swaying in the current. 潜水员对在水流中摇曳的鲜艳柳珊瑚感到惊叹。

例句：The reef was covered in a variety of gorgonians, creating a colorful underwater landscape.  珊瑚礁上覆盖着各种各样的柳珊瑚，形成了一个五彩缤纷的海底景观。 | 近义词：sea fan, sea whip, coral | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *Gorgoneus, from Gorgō (“Gorgon”), from Ancient Greek Γοργώ (Gorgṓ).  Named after the Gorgons of Greek mythology, whose hair was said to be made of snakes, resembling the branching structure of some gorgonians.
