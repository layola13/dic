ichthus: /ˈɪkθəs/ | n. | 鱼，鱼形符号（基督教象征） | A word from the Greek language meaning "fish," used as a secret Christian symbol. |  The ichthus was scratched on walls and carved on tombstones as a quiet sign of faith.  鱼形符号被刻在墙上和墓碑上，作为一种安静的信仰标志。 | synonyms: fish (literal),  Chi Rho, cross (symbolic) | antonyms: None (as a symbol, it lacks a direct opposite) | noun
