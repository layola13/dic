outpreach: /ˌaʊtˈpriːtʃ/
verb
胜过…的讲道；以更热烈的讲道胜过
to surpass in preaching; to preach more fervently than
He outpreached his rival and converted many to his cause. 他以更热烈的讲道胜过了他的对手，使许多人皈依了他的事业。
近义词: outdo, surpass, excel
反义词:  be outdone, be surpassed
词性: verb
