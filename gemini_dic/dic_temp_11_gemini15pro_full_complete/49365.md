deliquescence:/ˌdelɪˈkwɛsəns/ |n.| 潮解 |The process by which a substance absorbs moisture from the atmosphere until it dissolves in the absorbed water and forms a solution. |The deliquescence of the salt caused it to dissolve into a puddle.  盐的潮解导致它溶解成一滩水。|  hygroscopicity, liquefaction |  efflorescence, dehydration | noun

deliquescent:/ˌdelɪˈkwɛsənt/ |adj.| 潮解的 |Tending to absorb moisture from the air and dissolve in it. |Calcium chloride is a deliquescent substance. 氯化钙是一种潮解性物质。|hygroscopic | non-hygroscopic | adjective
