extoller:/ɪkˈstəʊlə(r)/ /ɪkˈstoʊlər/
noun|赞美者，颂扬者|A person who praises someone or something highly.|He was an enthusiastic extoller of the virtues of country life. 他热烈赞扬乡村生活的种种好处。
|近义词：praiser, lauder, eulogist|反义词：critic, detractor|词性:n.

extol:/ɪkˈstəʊl/ /ɪkˈstoʊl/
verb|赞美，颂扬|To praise someone or something highly.|The poem extols the beauty of nature. 这首诗赞美了大自然的美丽。
|近义词：praise, laud, eulogize|反义词：criticize, condemn|词性:v.
