noncontemptible:/ˌnɒnkənˈtɛmptɪbl/
adjective
不可轻视的，值得尊敬的
Not deserving contempt; worthy of respect or admiration.
Her efforts, though small, were noncontemptible. 虽然她的努力很小，但值得尊敬。
Synonyms: admirable, commendable, respectable, laudable, praiseworthy
Antonyms: contemptible, despicable, disdainful, scornful
词性: 形容词
