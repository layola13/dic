irretrievableness:/ˌɪrɪˈtriːvəblnəs/
n.
不可挽回，无法弥补
the state of being impossible to retrieve or recover
The irretrievableness of the lost data caused a major setback for the project.  丢失数据的不可挽回性导致了该项目的重大挫折。
近义词:irrevocability, irreparability, finality
反义词:recoverability, retrievability
词性:名词

irretrievable:/ˌɪrɪˈtriːvəbl/
adj.
不可挽回的，无法弥补的
impossible to retrieve or recover
The damage to the painting was irretrievable. 这幅画的损坏是不可挽回的。
近义词:irrevocable, irreparable, lost
反义词:retrievable, recoverable
词性:形容词
