digestibly:/daɪˈdʒɛstɪbli/| adv.|易消化地；易理解地|In a manner that is easy to digest, either physically or mentally.  This means something is presented in a way that is easy to understand or process.|The information was presented digestibly, even for beginners.|近义词：clearly, simply, understandably, plainly|反义词：incomprehensibly, confusingly|词性:副词
词源: From "digestible" + "-ly". "Digestible" comes from Late Middle English: from late Latin *digestibilis, from Latin digerere ‘distribute, arrange, digest’.
