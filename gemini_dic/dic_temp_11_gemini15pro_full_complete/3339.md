affirmative:/əˈfɜːrmətɪv/
noun|肯定，赞成|A statement of agreement or consent.|He responded with a simple affirmative. 他简单地表示了赞同。|近义词：agreement, consent, yes|反义词:negative, denial|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *affirmatif*, from Old French *affirmatif*, from Latin *affirmātīvus*, from *affirmō* (“I affirm”).
