aiguille:/eɪˈɡwiːj/| n.| 缝针;注射针;指针;尖峰;尖塔|A thin, sharp, pointed instrument, especially one used for sewing or for piercing tissues in medical procedures, or a pointed indicator on a dial or compass, or a sharp, needle-like peak of a mountain.|The surgeon used an aiguille to stitch the wound. 外科医生用缝针缝合伤口。|Synonyms: needle, spike, pinnacle|Antonyms: blunt, dull|noun
