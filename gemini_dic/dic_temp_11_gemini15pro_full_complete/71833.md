fleetingnesses:/ˈfliːtɪŋnəsɪz/
n.
短暂性；飞逝；转瞬即逝
The quality of being fleeting; transience.  The fleetingnesses of youth are often lamented in old age.
The fleetingnesses of fashion trends are well-documented.  时尚潮流的短暂性是有据可查的。
evanescence, transience, brevity, ephemerality
permanence, eternity, durability
noun
