deteriority:/dɪˌtɪəriəˈreɪʃn/ | n. |恶化，退化，变质|The state of becoming progressively worse. |The deterioration of the building was evident in the crumbling facade.  这栋建筑的恶化从破碎的正面就能明显看出。| Synonyms: decline, degradation, decay, worsening, degeneration | Antonyms: improvement, amelioration, enhancement, betterment | noun

deteriorate:/dɪˈtɪəriəreɪt/ | v. |恶化，退化，变质|To become progressively worse. |His health deteriorated rapidly after the diagnosis. 他的健康在诊断后迅速恶化。| Synonyms: worsen, decline, degenerate, decay | Antonyms: improve, recover, ameliorate, enhance | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *deterioratus, past participle of deteriorare "to make worse," from Latin deterior "worse," comparative of *deter (source of de- "down" in compounds), from PIE root *de- "down, off, away."
