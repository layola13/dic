lenin:/ˈlɛnɪn/| n. |列宁|Vladimir Ilyich Ulyanov, better known by his alias Lenin, was a Russian revolutionary, politician, and political theorist. He served as the first and founding head of government of Soviet Russia from 1917 to 1924 and of the Soviet Union from 1922 to 1924.|Lenin led the Bolsheviks to victory in the October Revolution.|近义词:leader, revolutionary|反义词:tsar, monarch|词性:名词
