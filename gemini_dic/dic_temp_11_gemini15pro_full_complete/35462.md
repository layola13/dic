coachable:/ˈkoʊtʃəbl/| adj.|可训练的；可教导的|Capable of being coached or trained; readily accepting instruction and guidance.|例句：He is a very coachable player and responds well to feedback. 他是一个非常容易受教的球员，对反馈反应良好。

例句：She has a coachable attitude and is always willing to learn new things. 她有可教的态度，并且总是愿意学习新事物。|近义词：trainable, receptive, teachable, malleable|反义词：uncoachable, intractable, stubborn, resistant|词性:adjective
