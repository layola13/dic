afterlives:/ˈɑːftərlaɪvz/| n. |来世，后世; 死后的生活|The concept of afterlives varies across different religions.  来世的概念在不同的宗教中各不相同。| The book explores the various afterlives imagined throughout history. 这本书探讨了历史上人们想象的各种来世。|近义词： hereafter, next world |反义词: current life, present life |词性:noun

Etymology: "afterlife" comes from Old English *æfterlif, a compound of æfter ("after") and līf ("life"). The plural form "afterlives" simply adds the regular English plural suffix "-s."
