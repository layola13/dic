gilts:/ɡɪlts/ | n. | 金边债券；英国政府债券；小母猪 | British government bonds or other high-grade bonds.  Also, a young female pig. | Example 1: Investors are buying up gilts as a safe haven during the market turmoil.  投资者在市场动荡期间争相购买金边债券作为避风港。

Example 2: The farmer sold several gilts at the auction.  农民在拍卖会上卖了几头小母猪。 | Synonym: (for bonds) government bonds, securities, treasuries; (for pig) sow, piglet | Antonym: (for bonds)  risky investments, junk bonds | noun
