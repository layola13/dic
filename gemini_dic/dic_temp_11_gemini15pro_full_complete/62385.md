endocarp:/ˈɛndəʊkɑːp/ /ˈɛndoʊkɑːrp/ | n. | 内果皮 | The innermost layer of the pericarp of a fruit, surrounding the seeds. | The hard endocarp of the peach protects the seed within.  桃子的坚硬内果皮保护着里面的种子。 | 近义词: putamen | 反义词: exocarp, mesocarp | noun
