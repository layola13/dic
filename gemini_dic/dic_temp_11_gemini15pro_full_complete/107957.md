lasciviousness:/ləˈsɪviəsnəs/| n.|好色，淫荡，猥亵|The state of being lascivious; lustfulness; lewdness.  A tendency to provoke or incite sexual desire.|His lasciviousness was evident in his suggestive remarks and lingering stares.|Synonyms: lust, lechery, lewdness, salaciousness, prurience|Antonyms: chastity, purity, modesty|noun


lascivious:/ləˈsɪviəs/| adj.|好色的，淫荡的，猥亵的|Characterized by or expressing lust or lewdness; inciting sexual desire.|She gave him a lascivious look.|Synonyms: lustful, lecherous, lewd, salacious, prurient|Antonyms: chaste, pure, modest|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *lasciviousus*, from Latin *lascivus* ("wanton, playful, sportive, frolicsome"), probably from *lascivia* ("wantonness, sportiveness").
