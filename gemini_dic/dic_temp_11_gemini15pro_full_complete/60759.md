electrographic:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
电流描记的;  电描记法的
Relating to or made by electrography.  A technique for creating images using electrical discharges.
Electrographic recordings of brain activity can help diagnose epilepsy.  脑电描记法记录的大脑活动可以帮助诊断癫痫。
近义词:electroencephalographic (EEG)
反义词: None
