data-warehousing:/ˈdeɪtə ˈwɛərˌhaʊzɪŋ/ | n. |数据仓库| The process of centralizing and consolidating large amounts of data from multiple sources into a single repository for reporting and analysis. | Example: Data-warehousing allows businesses to gain valuable insights into customer behavior. 数据仓库使企业能够获得对客户行为的宝贵见解。 Example:  The company invested heavily in data-warehousing to improve its decision-making process.  这家公司在数据仓库上投入巨资以改进其决策过程。| 近义词: data storage, information management | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
