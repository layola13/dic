khufu:/ˈkuːfuː/| n.|胡夫（古埃及法老）|Khufu, also known as Cheops, was an ancient Egyptian pharaoh who ruled during the Fourth Dynasty of the Old Kingdom.  He is generally credited with building the Great Pyramid of Giza, one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.|Khufu's pyramid is a testament to the power of the pharaohs. 胡夫的金字塔证明了法老的权力。|Synonym: Cheops|Antonym: N/A|Noun
