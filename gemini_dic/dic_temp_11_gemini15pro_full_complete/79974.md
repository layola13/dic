go-between:/ˈɡəʊ bɪˌtwiːn/ (UK) /ˈɡoʊ bɪˌtwiːn/ (US)| n.|中间人，媒人|A person who communicates between two others, especially those who are unfriendly or unwilling to communicate directly.|例句：She acted as a go-between for the two warring families. 她充当了两个敌对家庭之间的中间人。|近义词：intermediary, mediator, negotiator|反义词: antagonist, opponent|noun

go-between:/ˈɡəʊ bɪˌtwiːn/ (UK) /ˈɡoʊ bɪˌtwiːn/ (US)| n.|媒介物|Something that connects two things.|例句：The river acted as a go-between for the two communities. 这条河流充当了两个社区之间的连接纽带。|近义词：link, connection, bridge|反义词: barrier, separation|noun
