extinction:/ɪkˈstɪŋkʃ(ə)n/
n.|灭绝，消亡|The dinosaur's extinction occurred millions of years ago. 恐龙的灭绝发生在数百万年前。|
|The extinction of the passenger pigeon is a cautionary tale. 旅鸽的灭绝是一个警示性的故事。|
近义词：annihilation, extermination, eradication
反义词：survival, preservation, existence
词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *extinctionem*,  from Latin *extinctus*, past participle of *extinguere* "to extinguish, quench, put out, destroy," from *ex* "out" + *stinguere* "to quench."
