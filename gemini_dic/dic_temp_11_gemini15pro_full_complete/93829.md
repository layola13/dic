imminently: /ˈɪmɪnəntli/ | adv. |即将地，马上地| about to occur; very soon |例句：The storm is imminently approaching. 暴风雨即将来临。|例句：He was imminently expecting a phone call. 他正期待着马上会打来的电话。|近义词：soon, shortly, directly, immediately|反义词: distantly, remotely |词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *imminent- (stem of imminens), present participle of imminēre "to overhang, impend, threaten," from in- "in, on" + minnēre.
