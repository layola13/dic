genteelly:/dʒɛnˈtiːli/
adjective
文雅地；有教养地
in a refined or polite manner; like a gentleman or lady.
She genteelly sipped her tea, avoiding any slurping sounds. 她优雅地小口啜饮着茶，避免发出任何的啧啧声。
He genteelly offered his seat to the elderly woman on the bus. 他彬彬有礼地把座位让给了公交车上的老妇人。
近义词: politely, courteously, gracefully, elegantly
反义词: rudely, crudely, coarsely, vulgarly
词性: adjective

词源:
From gentle + -ly.  Ultimately from Latin *gentilis* "of the same clan or race," from *gens* "race, clan, family."
