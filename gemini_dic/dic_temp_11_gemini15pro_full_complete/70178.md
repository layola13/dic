fibril:/ˈfaɪbrɪl/ (US), /ˈfɪbrɪl/ (UK)| n. |细丝，纤丝，原纤维|A small or slender fiber or filament, especially one that is a component of a larger fiber or structure.  A fibril is a fine, thread-like structure that is often a component of a larger structure, such as muscle fibers or connective tissue.|Collagen fibrils provide structural support to the skin. 胶原纤维为皮肤提供结构支撑。

The muscle fibrils contracted, causing the muscle to shorten. 肌原纤维收缩，导致肌肉缩短。|近义词：fiber, filament, thread|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "mass" in some contexts)|词性:noun
