bismuthous: /ˈbɪzməθəs/
adjective
亚铋的；二价铋的
Containing or relating to bismuth, especially with a valence of three.  Relating to or containing bismuth with a lower valence than bismuthic.
Bismuthous compounds are less common than bismuthic compounds.  亚铋化合物不如铋化合物常见。
Obsolete term for bismuth(III) compounds.  亚铋化合物的过时术语。
Near Synonyms: bismuth(III)
Antonyms: bismuthic (in older terminology)
词性: adjective
