malfeasance:/ˌmælfɪˈzæns/ | n. | (尤指官员的)渎职；违法乱纪 | Wrongdoing, especially by a public official. | The mayor was accused of malfeasance and corruption. 市长被指控渎职和腐败. | 近义词: misconduct, wrongdoing, misbehavior, malpractice | 反义词: propriety, rectitude | 词性: noun
