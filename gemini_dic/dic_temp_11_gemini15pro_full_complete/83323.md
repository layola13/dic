gypseian: /dʒɪpˈsiːən/ | adj. n.|吉普赛的；吉普赛人|Of or relating to Gypsies or their way of life; a Gypsy.|例句：She had a gypseian spirit, always yearning to travel. 她有一种吉普赛人的精神，总是渴望旅行。

例句：He lived a gypseian lifestyle, moving from place to place. 他过着吉普赛式的生活，四处漂泊。|近义词：nomadic, Romany, Romani|反义词:settled, sedentary|词性:形容词, 名词

gypsy: /ˈdʒɪpsi/ | n. |吉普赛人|A member of a traveling people traditionally believed to have come from India, now living chiefly in Europe and North America and noted as itinerant traders and fortune tellers.|词源：From Middle English *Gypcian*, from Egyptian (because Gypsies were mistakenly thought to have originated in Egypt).|词性:名词
