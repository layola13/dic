counterculturists:/ˈkaʊntərˈkʌltʃərɪsts/
n.
反主流文化者，反文化者
People who actively reject the dominant values and norms of society.
The counterculturists of the 1960s challenged traditional societal norms.  20世纪60年代的反主流文化者挑战了传统的社会规范。

The festival attracted artists, musicians, and counterculturists from all over the world.  这个节日吸引了来自世界各地的艺术家、音乐家和反主流文化者。

nonconformists, bohemians, rebels, radicals
conformists, traditionalists
noun
