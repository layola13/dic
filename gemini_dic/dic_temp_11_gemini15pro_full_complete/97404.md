infrequently:/ɪnˈfriːkwəntli/
adverb
不经常地，很少地
not often; rarely
She visits her parents infrequently, maybe once or twice a year. 她很少去看望她的父母，也许一年只有一两次。
The bus runs infrequently on Sundays. 周日公共汽车很少运行。
Synonyms: rarely, seldom, occasionally, sporadically
Antonyms: frequently, often, regularly, commonly
词性: 副词
