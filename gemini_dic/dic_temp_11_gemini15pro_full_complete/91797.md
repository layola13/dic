hyperfastidiousnesses:/ˌhaɪpərˌfæˈstɪdiəsnəsɪz/ | n. | 过分讲究, 过分挑剔 |  The state of being excessively fastidious; extreme attention to detail and a tendency to find fault with minor imperfections.  It refers to an excessive concern with minor details and a propensity to criticize even the smallest flaws. |  Her hyperfastidiousnesses made it impossible to work with her on the project. 她过分挑剔，使得在这个项目上无法和她共事。

The designer's hyperfastidiousnesses were evident in the impeccable stitching and perfectly aligned seams of the garment.  这位设计师的过分讲究体现在服装无可挑剔的针脚和完美对齐的接缝上。 | 近义词: meticulousness, punctiliousness, overscrupulousness | 反义词: carelessness, negligence, sloppiness | 词性: noun
