loquaciously:/loʊˈkweɪʃəsli/| adv.|喋喋不休地，饶舌地|In a talkative and voluble manner; excessively wordy.|She spoke loquaciously about her trip to Europe. 她喋喋不休地谈论她的欧洲之旅。|He described the events loquaciously, leaving out no detail. 他絮絮叨叨地描述了事件的经过，没有遗漏任何细节。|近义词：talkatively, volubly, garrulously|反义词：taciturnly, concisely, succinctly|词性:副词

loquacious:/loʊˈkweɪʃəs/| adj.|健谈的，多话的|Tending to talk a great deal; talkative.|He was a loquacious and entertaining companion. 他是一个健谈且有趣的同伴。|She became more loquacious after a couple of glasses of wine.  喝了几杯酒后，她变得更加健谈了。|近义词：talkative, voluble, garrulous|反义词：taciturn, reticent, laconic|词性:形容词
