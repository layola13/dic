disgruntled:/dɪsˈɡrʌnt(ə)ld/
adjective
不满的，不高兴的
Feeling or showing dissatisfaction or resentment.
The disgruntled employees protested the new company policy.  不满的员工抗议公司的新政策。

The team was disgruntled after their loss. 球队输球后很不高兴。

近义词:discontented, dissatisfied, grumpy, annoyed, irritated, displeased
反义词:contented, satisfied, happy, pleased

词性:adjective
词源:From dis- (expressing negation) + gruntle (to grumble or complain).  源自dis-（表示否定）+ gruntle（抱怨或发牢骚）。
