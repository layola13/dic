nonconductor:/ˌnɒnkənˈdʌktər/ |n.| 非导体；绝缘体 |A substance that does not readily conduct heat, sound, or electricity.| Rubber is a good nonconductor of electricity. 橡胶是良好的电绝缘体。|Synonyms: insulator, dielectric|Antonyms: conductor|Noun
