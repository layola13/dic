grievers:/ˈɡriːvərz/
noun
悲痛者，哀悼者
People who are grieving; those who mourn a loss.
The grievers gathered at the funeral to pay their respects. 悲痛者聚集在葬礼上表示敬意。
The support group offered solace to the grievers.  互助小组为悲痛者提供了慰藉。
mourners, bereavers
rejoicers
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From grieve (v.) + -er (suffix forming nouns denoting a person who performs an action).  Grieve comes from Old French grever "to burden, oppress, harm, injure," and ultimately from Latin gravare "to burden."
