massiness:/ˈmæsɪnɪs/ | n. | 厚重感；庞大；笨重 | The massiness of the furniture made the room feel smaller.  家具的笨重感让房间显得更小了。| The massiness of the castle walls was impressive. 城堡城墙的厚重感令人印象深刻。 | 近义词: bulk, heaviness, massiveness, ponderousness | 反义词: lightness, delicacy, slightness | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From massy + -ness.  Massy itself comes from mass, ultimately from Latin massa.
