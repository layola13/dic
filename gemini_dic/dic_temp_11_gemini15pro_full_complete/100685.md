intra-subjective:/ˌɪntrə səbˈdʒɛktɪv/ | adj. | 主体内的，主体间的 | Existing or occurring within the subject's mind or self; relating to or involving the internal or private experiences of an individual. | Example: Intra-subjective feelings are deeply personal and unique to each individual.  主体内的感觉是非常私人的，并且对每个人来说都是独一无二的。

Example:  The artist explored intra-subjective realities through abstract paintings.  这位艺术家通过抽象绘画探索了主体内的现实。 | Synonym: personal, subjective, internal | Antonym: inter-subjective, objective, external | word type: adjective
