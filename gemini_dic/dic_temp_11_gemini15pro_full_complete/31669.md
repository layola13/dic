chatting:/ˈtʃætɪŋ/ | n. v.| 聊天，闲谈 | The act of engaging in informal conversation. |  We spent the evening chatting about old times. 我们晚上聊起了过去。| synonyms: conversing, talking, gossiping | antonyms:  being silent, listening, ignoring | verb/noun

chatting:/ˈtʃætɪŋ/ | adj.| 聊天的 |  Used to describe someone in the act of chatting. | They were chatting happily in the corner. 他们在角落里聊得很开心。| synonyms:  talkative, communicative | antonyms: quiet, reserved | adjective
