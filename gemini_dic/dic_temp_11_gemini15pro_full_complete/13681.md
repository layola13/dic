attrap:/ɑːˈtʁap/ (French pronunciation)| n.|陷阱，圈套|A device or trick designed to catch someone or something unawares.|The police set an attrap to catch the thief. 警察设下陷阱抓捕小偷。|Synonyms: trap, snare, ambush|Antonyms: release, liberation|noun
