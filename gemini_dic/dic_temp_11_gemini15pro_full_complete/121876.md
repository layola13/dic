misruled:/ˌmɪsˈruːld/
adjective
统治不善的，管理不当的
governed badly or unjustly.
The country was misruled for decades by a corrupt dictator.  这个国家被一个腐败的独裁者统治了几十年。
The misruled kingdom was plagued by poverty and unrest.  这个统治不善的王国饱受贫困和动乱的困扰。
近义词: misgoverned, ill-governed, badly ruled
反义词: well-governed, well-ruled
