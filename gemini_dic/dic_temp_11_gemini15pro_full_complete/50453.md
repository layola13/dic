depreciator:/dɪˈpriːʃieɪtər/
n.|贬值者，折旧者|Someone or something that depreciates or causes depreciation.  A person who belittles or undervalues something or someone.|The constant depreciator was never happy with anything.|Synonym: detractor, critic, disparager|Antonym:  appreciator, admirer|noun

depreciate:/dɪˈpriːʃieɪt/
v.|贬值，折旧，轻视|To diminish in value over time; to belittle or undervalue.|The car depreciated quickly after I bought it. He tends to depreciate his own achievements.|Synonym:  devalue, downgrade, underestimate|Antonym:  appreciate, enhance, value|verb
