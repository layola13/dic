monodelph:/ˈmɒnədɛlf/ /ˈmɑːnədɛlf/ | n. |单孔目动物|A mammal of the Monodelphia subclass, characterized by a single urogenital opening and a uterus with a single chamber.  These are also known as placental mammals.|例句：The kangaroo is not a monodelph, but a marsupial. 袋鼠不是单孔目动物，而是有袋动物。

例句：Monodelphs nourish their young through a placenta. 单孔目动物通过胎盘滋养幼崽。|近义词：placental mammal, eutherian|反义词:prototherian (e.g., monotreme), metatherian (e.g., marsupial)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *mónos* ("single") + *delphýs* ("womb").
