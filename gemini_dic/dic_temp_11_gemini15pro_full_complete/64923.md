establishment: /ɪˈstæblɪʃmənt/ | n. | 建立；机构；企业；体制 | the act of establishing something or the state of being established.  A business organization, public institution, or household. The dominant group or elite in a society and its customs, values, etc. |  The restaurant is a popular establishment. 这家餐馆是一家很受欢迎的场所。| synonyms: foundation, institution, organization, business, company | antonyms: abolition, destruction, ruin | noun


establishment: /ɪˈstæblɪʃmənt/ | n. | 体制；当权派 | the ruling class or inner circle of a society or institution. | The political establishment is resistant to change. 政治当权派抵制变革。| synonyms:  ruling class, elite, authority, powers that be | antonyms:  outsiders, rebels, counter-culture | noun
