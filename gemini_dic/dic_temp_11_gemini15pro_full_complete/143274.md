outdodged:/ˌaʊtˈdɒdʒd/
verb
以更灵巧的方式躲避; 比...更善于躲避
Evaded or avoided something more skillfully than another.
The spy outdodged his pursuers through the crowded market.  这个间谍在拥挤的市场中比追捕者更机敏地躲避了他们。
He outdodged the security guards and escaped the building. 他比保安更机智地躲开了，逃出了大楼。
outmaneuver, outwit, surpass
- (No clear antonym, perhaps "be outdodged by")
v.
