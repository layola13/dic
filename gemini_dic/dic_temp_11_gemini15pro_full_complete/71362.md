flag-waving:/ˈflæɡˌweɪvɪŋ/
adjective
挥舞旗帜的；爱国主义的（常贬义，指盲目爱国）
The act of enthusiastically displaying and supporting one's country's flag, often used to describe excessive or ostentatious patriotism, sometimes with a negative connotation of jingoism or nationalism.
His flag-waving patriotism embarrassed some of his friends. 他那煽动性的爱国主义令他的一些朋友感到尴尬。
Synonyms: patriotic, nationalistic, jingoistic
Antonyms: unpatriotic, cosmopolitan, internationalist
词性: adjective


flag-waving:/ˈflæɡˌweɪvɪŋ/
noun
挥舞旗帜；爱国主义的言行（常贬义）
The act of displaying or expressing patriotism, especially in an excessive or ostentatious way, often used with a negative connotation.
The politician's speech was full of flag-waving and empty promises. 这位政客的演讲充满了煽动性的爱国主义言论和空洞的承诺。
Synonyms: patriotism, nationalism, jingoism
Antonyms: unpatriotic, cosmopolitan, internationalist
词性: noun

词源:  "flag" + "waving" - both words have Old English origins. "Flag" comes from "fleogan" meaning to fly or float, and "waving" from "wafian" meaning to waver or fluctuate. The combined term became common in the late 19th/early 20th century.
