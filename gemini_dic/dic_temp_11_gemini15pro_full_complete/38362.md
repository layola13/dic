concentre:/ˈkɒnsəntreɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsəntreɪt/ (US)| v. |集中；浓缩|To focus all one's attention or efforts on a particular activity, subject, or problem; to bring or come together at one point; to increase the strength or proportion of a substance in a solution by removing some of the water or other solvent.|例句：She found it hard to concentrate on her work. 她发现很难集中精力工作。|近义词：focus, converge, condense|反义词:disperse, scatter|词性:动词

concentre:/ˈkɒnsəntreɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsəntreɪt/ (US)| n. |浓缩物|A substance made by concentrating something, especially a food or liquid.|例句：The orange concentrate is mixed with water to make juice.  橙汁浓缩物与水混合制成果汁。|近义词：extract, concentrate|反义词:dilution|词性:名词
