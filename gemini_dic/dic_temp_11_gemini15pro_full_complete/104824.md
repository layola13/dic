kedge:/kɛdʒ/
noun
a small anchor
A kedge is a small anchor used to keep a boat steady in a particular position, especially in a river or harbor, or to warp a ship.
The sailors dropped the kedge to prevent the boat from drifting.  水手们抛下小锚以防止船漂流。
anchor, grapnel
mooring
