decompartmentalize:/ˌdiːkəmˌpɑːrtˈmɛntəlaɪz/
verb
去隔间化；打破隔阂；消除界限
To break down the barriers between different parts of something, especially within an organization or system.  To abolish the divisions between different groups or aspects of something.
The company is trying to decompartmentalize its operations to improve efficiency.  这家公司正在尝试打破其运营部门之间的隔阂以提高效率。
The new software helps to decompartmentalize information, making it easier for everyone to access.  这款新软件有助于消除信息之间的隔阂，使每个人更容易获取信息。
synonyms: integrate, unify, merge, combine, consolidate
antonyms: compartmentalize, divide, separate, segregate, isolate
词性: 动词
