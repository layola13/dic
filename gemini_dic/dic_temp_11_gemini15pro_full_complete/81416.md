grassward:/ˈɡræs.wərd/| adv.|朝着草地的方向，向草地|Moving or extending in the direction of grassland. | The sheep drifted grassward, seeking greener pastures. 羊群朝着草地移动，寻找更绿的牧场。|  The gentle breeze carried the scent of wildflowers grassward.  微风把野花的香味带向草地。 | 近义词: toward the grass, towards the grassland | 反义词: away from the grass |词性: 副词
