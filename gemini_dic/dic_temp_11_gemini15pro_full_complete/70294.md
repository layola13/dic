fidgeted:/ˈfɪdʒɪtɪd/| v.|烦躁不安，坐立不安|To make small, restless movements, especially with the hands and feet, typically as a sign of nervousness or impatience.|She fidgeted nervously in her chair during the interview.  面试期间，她紧张地在椅子上坐立不安。|Synonyms: squirmed, wriggled, twitched, fussed|Antonyms: relaxed, calmed, still|verb

fidgeted:/ˈfɪdʒɪtɪd/| adj.|烦躁的，坐立不安的|Characterized by fidgeting; restless and uneasy.|The children were fidgeted and impatient to open their presents. 孩子们坐立不安，迫不及待地想打开礼物。|Synonyms: restless, uneasy, nervous, agitated|Antonyms: calm, composed, relaxed, tranquil|adjective
