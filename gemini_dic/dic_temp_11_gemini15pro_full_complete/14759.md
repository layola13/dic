awing:/ˈɔːɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人敬畏的，使人惊叹的 | Inspiring awe; impressive. | The awing beauty of the Grand Canyon left me speechless. 大峡谷令人敬畏的美丽让我 speechless。| 近义词：amazing, astounding, breathtaking, impressive, magnificent, stunning, wonderful | 反义词：unimpressive, underwhelming, ordinary | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  From awe + -ing.  Awe comes from Old Norse *agi, meaning "fear, terror," related to Old English ege "fear, dread."  The meaning shifted from "inspiring fear" to "inspiring reverence and wonder."
