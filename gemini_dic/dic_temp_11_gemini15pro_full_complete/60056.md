edulcorant: /ɪˈdʌlkərənt/ /iːˈdʌlkərənt/
n.
甜味剂;甜料
A substance that sweetens.

例句：
This yogurt contains no artificial edulcorants.
这种酸奶不含人工甜味剂。

Many processed foods use high-intensity edulcorants.
许多加工食品使用高强度甜味剂。


近义词：sweetener, sweetening agent
反义词：bittering agent


词性：noun


词源：From French *édulcorant*, present participle of *édulcorer* (“to sweeten”), from Latin *ēdūlcōrō* (“I sweeten”), from *dulcis* (“sweet”).
