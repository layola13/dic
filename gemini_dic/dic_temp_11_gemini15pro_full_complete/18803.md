berseem:/ˈbɜːrsiːm/| n.|埃及三叶草; 金花菜|A clover-like plant (Trifolium alexandrinum) native to the eastern Mediterranean region and widely cultivated as a forage crop.|Berseem is a valuable forage crop for livestock. 埃及三叶草是一种有价值的牲畜饲料作物。| Berseem is often used as green manure. 埃及三叶草常被用作绿肥。|Synonyms: Egyptian clover, Alexandrian clover|Antonyms: N/A|noun
