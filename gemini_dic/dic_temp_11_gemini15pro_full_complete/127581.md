nectarlike:/ˈnɛktərˌlaɪk/| adj.|像花蜜的，甘美的|Resembling or characteristic of nectar; sweet and delicious.|The drink had a nectarlike flavor, sweet and refreshing.  这种饮料有着花蜜般的味道，甘甜清爽。|例句：The nectarlike scent of the flowers filled the air. 花蜜般的香味弥漫在空气中。|近义词：sweet, honeyed, delicious, ambrosial|反义词：bitter, sour, acrid|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From nectar + -like.  Nectar comes from Greek *néktar, the drink of the gods, from *nek-, death + *tar, overcoming.
