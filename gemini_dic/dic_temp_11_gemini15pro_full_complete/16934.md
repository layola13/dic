bassa:/ˈbæsə/ | n. |巴萨，低音|A member of a West African people living chiefly in Liberia and Sierra Leone. Also: the language of this people. |例句：The Bassa people are known for their rich cultural heritage. 巴萨人以其丰富的文化遗产而闻名。

例句：The Bassa language is part of the Niger-Congo family. 巴萨语属于尼日尔-刚果语系。|近义词: (for the language) Kru, Grebo |反义词:  None (Languages don't typically have antonyms)|词性:noun


basso:/ˈbæsoʊ/ (US), /ˈbæsəʊ/ (UK) | n. adj. |低音, 低音的|A bass singer or instrument; having a low pitch.  |例句：The basso profondo added a rich depth to the choir. 男低音为唱诗班增添了丰富的深度。

例句：The basso continuo provided a harmonic foundation for the piece. 通奏低音为该作品提供了和声基础。|近义词: bass, deep |反义词: alto, soprano, treble, high |词性:noun, adjective
