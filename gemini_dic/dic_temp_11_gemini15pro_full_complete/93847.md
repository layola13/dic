immobilised:/ɪˈmoʊbəlaɪzd/ (US) /ɪˈməʊbəlaɪzd/ (UK)| adj.|固定的，不能动的|Made unable to move or function.|The injured player was immobilised by a neck brace. 受伤的运动员的脖子被颈托固定住了。|The car was immobilised by the flat tire. 因为轮胎瘪了，车子动不了了。|近义词：fixed, stationary, stuck, incapacitated|反义词：mobile, movable, free|词性:形容词

immobilize:/ɪˈmoʊbəlaɪz/ (US) /ɪˈməʊbəlaɪz/ (UK)| vt.|使固定，使不能动|To prevent something or someone from moving or operating.|The police immobilised the stolen vehicle with a wheel clamp. 警察用车轮夹锁锁住了被盗车辆。|The doctor immobilised the patient's broken leg with a cast. 医生用石膏固定了病人骨折的腿。|近义词：restrict, disable, restrain|反义词：mobilize, activate, free|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *immobiliser*, from *im-* (not) + *mobiliser* (to mobilize), from Latin *mobilis* (movable). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *meue- (to move).
