on-point:/ˌɑːn ˈpɔɪnt/ | adj. | 恰当的；合适的；切题的；中肯的 | Relevant, appropriate, and effective; hitting the mark precisely. | Her presentation was on-point and very persuasive. 她的演讲切中要害，很有说服力。 |  Her fashion sense is always on-point. 她的时尚感总是很到位。 | synonyms: relevant, apt, appropriate, fitting, suitable, pertinent, germane, apposite, apropos, to the point, spot-on, accurate, precise, exact, perfect, impeccable | antonyms: irrelevant, inappropriate, off-topic, inaccurate, imprecise | adjective
