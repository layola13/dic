eradicants:/ɪˈrædɪkənts/| n.| 根除剂；消灭剂 |Substances used to eradicate something, especially a disease or pest. |The farmer used powerful eradicants to eliminate the fungal infection affecting his crops. 农民使用强效的根除剂来消除影响他作物的真菌感染。|近义词：eliminators, exterminators, destroyers|反义词: promoters, encouragers|词性: 名词

eradicant:/ɪˈrædɪkənt/| adj.| 根除的；消灭的 |Having the power to eradicate something. |The eradicant properties of the new pesticide were highly effective. 新型杀虫剂的根除特性非常有效。|近义词：eliminative, destructive, exterminative|反义词:  promotive, encouraging|词性: 形容词


词源:  From the Latin word "eradicare," meaning "to root out" or "to pluck up by the roots."  由拉丁语单词 "eradicare" 演变而来，意思是“根除”或“连根拔起”。
