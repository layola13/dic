interlingual:/ˌɪntərˈlɪŋɡwəl/
| adj.|
|语际的，跨语言的|
|Relating to or existing between different languages; used to facilitate translation between languages.|
|Interlingual communication can be challenging due to differences in grammar and vocabulary. 语际交流可能会因为语法和词汇的差异而具有挑战性。|

|An interlingual error is a type of error made by language learners that is influenced by their native language. 语际错误是语言学习者犯的一种错误，受其母语的影响。|
|cross-linguistic, multilingual|
|monolingual|
|adjective|
