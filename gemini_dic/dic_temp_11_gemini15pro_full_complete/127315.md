naxalism:/ˈnæksəlɪzəm/
n.
纳萨尔主义，纳萨尔派
A militant communist movement in India.  It began in 1967, inspired by Mao Zedong's thoughts, advocating for peasant rights and land redistribution through armed struggle.
The Naxalism movement poses a significant threat to internal security in several Indian states. 纳萨尔派运动对印度几个邦的国内安全构成重大威胁。

The government is trying to address the root causes of Naxalism. 政府正在努力解决纳萨尔主义的根源问题。

近义词:Maoism, left-wing extremism
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "establishment" or "government forces" in a specific context)
词性:noun
