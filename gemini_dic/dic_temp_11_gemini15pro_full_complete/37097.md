combines:/kəmˈbaɪnz/| v.|结合，联合，合并|The company combines traditional craftsmanship with modern technology. 这家公司将传统工艺与现代科技相结合。|例句：He combines both talent and hard work. 他兼具天赋和勤奋。|近义词：mixes, blends, integrates, unites, merges|反义词:separates, divides|词性:动词
combines:/ˈkɒmbaɪnz/| n.|联合收割机|Farmers use combines to harvest their crops. 农民使用联合收割机收割庄稼。|例句：The combines are working in the fields. 联合收割机正在田里工作。|近义词：harvesters|反义词:N/A|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *combynen, from Old French combiner, from Latin combinare (“to unite, join together”), from com- (“together”) + bini (“two by two”).
