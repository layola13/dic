chamoising:/ˈʃæmwɑːzɪŋ/
n.|麂皮擦拭，油鞣|The act of cleaning or polishing something, typically leather, with a chamois. |He gave his shoes a good chamoising. 他用麂皮好好擦了擦他的鞋。|近义词:buffing, polishing, cleaning|反义词:soiling, dirtying|词性:名词
chamois:/ˈʃæmi/
n.|羚羊,麂皮|A type of European antelope or the soft leather made from its skin. |She used a chamois to dry the car. 她用麂皮擦干了汽车。|近义词:antelope, leather|反义词:None|词性:名词
