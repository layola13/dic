humblenesses:/ˈhʌmbəl.nəsɪz/
|n.|谦逊，谦卑|The quality of being humble; modesty.|She spoke with a disarming humbleness about her achievements. 她以令人放松的谦逊谈论她的成就.|Synonyms: modesty, humility, meekness, unpretentiousness|Antonyms: arrogance, pride, haughtiness, conceit|noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *humblenesse*, from humble +‎ -ness.
