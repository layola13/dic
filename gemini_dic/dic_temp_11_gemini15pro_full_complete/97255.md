inflictable:/ɪnˈflɪktəbl/| adj.|可施加的，可强加的|Capable of being inflicted; that can be imposed (as a punishment or suffering).|例句：The judge determined that the punishment was inflictable. 法官判定该惩罚可以施加。

例句：Emotional pain is sometimes more inflictable than physical. 有时候，精神上的痛苦比肉体上的痛苦更容易施加。|近义词：imposable, applicable|反义词：unimposable, preventable|词性:形容词
