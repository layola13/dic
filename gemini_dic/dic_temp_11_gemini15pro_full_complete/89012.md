hollower:/ˈhɒləʊər (UK) /ˈhɑːloʊər (US)| n. |挖空者，掏空者|Someone or something that makes something hollow or creates a hollow space. |The woodpeckers are the hollowers of these trees, creating nests inside. 啄木鸟是这些树的挖空者，在里面筑巢。|近义词：excavator, borer, driller|反义词:filler, packer|词性:名词

hollower:/ˈhɒləʊər (UK) /ˈhɑːloʊər (US)| adj. (比较级) |更空的，更凹陷的|More hollow than something else; having a larger or deeper hollow.|This bowl is hollower than the other one, making it perfect for serving soup. 这个碗比另一个碗更凹陷，非常适合盛汤。|近义词：emptier, concaver|反义词:fuller, more solid|词性:形容词


USAGE->

词源：From the verb "hollow" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Hollow" comes from Old English "holh," meaning "cave, hole."
