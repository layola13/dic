emender:/ɪˈmendər/| n.|校订者；修订者|A person who corrects or improves a text, especially a literary work.|The emender carefully reviewed the manuscript, correcting errors and suggesting improvements. 校订者仔细审阅了手稿，纠正了错误并提出了一些改进建议。|近义词:corrector, editor, reviser|反义词:corruptor, distorter|noun
