intrapericardial:/ˌɪntrəˌpɛrɪˈkɑːrdiəl/
adjective
心包内的
Located within the pericardium (the sac surrounding the heart).
The intrapericardial pressure increased significantly during the procedure.  手术过程中心包内压力显著升高。
The surgeon repaired the intrapericardial defect.  外科医生修复了心包内缺损。
近义词:  endocardial (although this refers specifically to the lining *within* the heart chambers, not the sac surrounding it)
反义词: extrapericardial
词性: adjective


intrapericardial:/ˌɪntrəˌpɛrɪˈkɑːrdiəl/
noun
(罕见用法，通常用作形容词) 心包内的事物/结构
Something located within the pericardium.
(This usage is less common.  "Intrapericardial structures" might be used in a highly specialized medical context.)
The intrapericardial showed signs of inflammation. 心包内结构显示出炎症迹象。(This usage is less common; usually phrased as "The intrapericardial space/area showed...")
The physician identified the intrapericardial as the source of the problem.  医生确定心包内的东西是问题的根源。(Again, this usage is less common;  a more typical phrasing would be "The physician identified a problem within the pericardium.")
