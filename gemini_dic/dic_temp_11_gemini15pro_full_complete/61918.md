empyrosis:/ˌɛmpaɪˈroʊsɪs/| n. |烧心，胃灼热|A burning sensation in the epigastric region, typically extending toward the throat, often associated with regurgitation of gastric acid.|She frequently experienced empyrosis after eating spicy food. 她吃完辛辣食物后经常感到烧心。|近义词：heartburn, pyrosis, acid indigestion|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider relief or absence of burning sensation)|noun
