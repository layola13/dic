bedstock:/ˈbɛdstɒk/| n.|床架，床身|The bedstock was made of solid oak. 床架是用实橡木做的。|例句：The antique bedstock was intricately carved. 这古董床架雕刻精美。

例句：The bedstock creaked every time I moved. 我每次动一下，床架都会发出吱吱嘎嘎的声音。

近义词：bedframe, bedstead|反义词: 无|词性:名词

词源:  From Middle English *beddestokk, equivalent to bed + stock (“trunk, stem, post”).  Essentially, the "trunk" or supporting structure of the bed.
