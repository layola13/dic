bemaze: /bɪˈmeɪz/
verb
使迷惑；使惊愕
to confuse or bewilder someone
例句：The sudden change of plans bemazed everyone.  计划的突然改变使每个人都感到迷惑。

例句：She was bemazed by the complexity of the situation. 她被情况的复杂性弄糊涂了。
近义词：confuse, bewilder, puzzle, mystify
反义词：clarify, enlighten
词性：v.
