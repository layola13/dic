bilateralism:/ˌbaɪˈlætrəlɪzəm/| n.|双边主义|The conduct of political, economic, or cultural relations between two sovereign states.|例句：The two countries have a long history of bilateralism. 这两个国家有着悠久的双边主义历史。

例句：Bilateralism is preferred over multilateralism in some situations. 在某些情况下，双边主义比多边主义更受欢迎。|近义词：two-sidedness, mutuality|反义词：multilateralism, unilateralism|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From bi- (two) + lateral (sided) + -ism (doctrine, belief, practice).  Ultimately from Latin *bi* (two) + *latus* (side).
