evangelical: /ˌiːvænˈdʒɛlɪk(ə)l/ | adj. n. | 福音派的；福音派信徒 | Of or relating to the Christian gospel, especially the four Gospels of the New Testament.  Characterized by a belief in the literal truth of the Bible and the importance of personal conversion.  |  例句：He is an evangelical Christian. 他是一位福音派基督徒。|  近义词：fundamentalist, orthodox, devout | 反义词：liberal, secular | 词性：形容词, 名词

evangelicals: /ˌiːvænˈdʒɛlɪk(ə)lz/ | n. | 福音派信徒 |  Plural form of "evangelical", referring to people who adhere to evangelical beliefs. | 例句：Evangelicals often emphasize the importance of missionary work. 福音派信徒通常强调传教工作的重要性。 | 近义词：fundamentalists | 反义词：liberals, secularists | 词性：名词
