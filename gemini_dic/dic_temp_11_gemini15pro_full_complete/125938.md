mushrooming:/ˈmʌʃrʊmɪŋ/ /ˈmʌʃrumɪŋ/| v. n. adj.|迅速增长，迅速发展；像蘑菇一样生长；采蘑菇|The town's population is mushrooming. 这个镇的人口正在迅速增长。|近义词：proliferating, booming, expanding, burgeoning|反义词:shrinking, dwindling, declining|词性:Verb (主要), Noun, Adjective

mushrooming:/ˈmʌʃrʊmɪŋ/ /ˈmʌʃrumɪŋ/| adj.|迅速增长的，迅速发展的|The mushrooming demand for housing is driving up prices. 对住房日益增长的需求正在推高价格。|近义词：proliferating, booming, expanding, burgeoning|反义词:shrinking, dwindling, declining|词性:Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From mushroom (n.) + -ing.  The literal sense of "growing like a mushroom" led to the figurative sense of "rapidly expanding."
