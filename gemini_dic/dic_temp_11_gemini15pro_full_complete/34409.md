ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *klateren, frequentative of *klāten (“to clap, strike”), from Proto-Germanic *klatjaną (“to resound”). Cognate with Dutch klateren (“to clatter, ripple, purl”), German klatern (“to climb, clamber”), English clotter (“to clot”).
