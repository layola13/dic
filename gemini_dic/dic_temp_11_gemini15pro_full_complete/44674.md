crucifies:/ˈkruːsɪfaɪz/| vt.| 钉死在十字架上；折磨；严厉批评；谴责 |To put (someone) to death by nailing or tying to a cross.  To criticize (someone) severely and unrelentingly.|The Romans crucified Jesus. 罗马人把耶稣钉死在十字架上。  He was crucified by the press for his comments. 他因自己的言论而遭到媒体的严厉批评。|Synonyms: execute, kill, torture, torment, excoriate, condemn, castigate|Antonyms: praise, commend, exonerate|Verb
