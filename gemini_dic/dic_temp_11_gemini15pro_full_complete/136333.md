nonreservations:/ˌnɒnˌrezəˈveɪʃənz/ |n.|无保留; 不预订|Absence of reservations; lack of restrictions or limitations.|They accepted the offer with nonreservations. 他们毫无保留地接受了这个提议。|近义词:openness, acceptance|反义词:hesitation, reluctance|词性:名词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
reservation (noun): from Late Latin reservationem (nominative reservatio) "a keeping back, reservation," noun of action from past-participle stem of reservare "reserve, keep back," from re- "back" (see re-) + servare "to keep, save, preserve" (from PIE root *ser- (1) "to protect").
