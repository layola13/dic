flyweight:/ˈflaɪˌweɪt/| n. adj.|蝇量级；轻量级；无足轻重的人或物|A person or thing of little importance or influence; in boxing, a weight division for boxers weighing no more than 112 pounds (51 kilograms).|The new employee was treated like a flyweight and his opinions were ignored.  这个新员工被当作无足轻重的人，他的意见被忽略了。

The company decided to discontinue the flyweight product line due to low sales.  由于销量低，公司决定停止生产轻量级产品线。|近义词：lightweight, insignificant, unimportant, trivial|反义词：heavyweight, significant, important|词性:名词，形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From fly + weight.  The term originated in boxing, referring to a weight class for very light boxers.  The metaphorical meaning developed later, referring to anything small or insignificant.
