awarded:/əˈwɔːrdɪd/
verb
已授予的，已奖励的
given as a prize or reward.
She was awarded a medal for her bravery. 她因其勇敢而被授予一枚奖章。
近义词：granted, bestowed, presented, conferred
反义词：denied, refused, revoked, withdrawn
词性：v. (past participle of "award")

Etymology:
From Middle English awarden, from Anglo-Norman awarder, from Old North French eswarder, from Frankish *wis-wardōn (“to guard, protect, keep safe, preserve”), from Proto-Germanic *wis (“wise, certain, true”) + *wardōną (“to watch, guard”).
