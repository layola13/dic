besetting:/bɪˈsɛtɪŋ/| adj.|困扰的，萦绕的，缠身的|Habitually or persistently troubling; constantly harassing.|Procrastination is his besetting sin. 拖延是他一贯的毛病。|近义词：persistent, chronic, inherent, ingrained|反义词：occasional, sporadic, intermittent|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From the verb "beset," from Old English "besettan" (to set upon, surround, attack), from "be-" (around, about) + "settan" (to set).
