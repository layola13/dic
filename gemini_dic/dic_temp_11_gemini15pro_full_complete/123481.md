monosyllabically:/ˌmɒnəsəˈlæbɪkli/ | adv. | 单音节地 | In a way that uses only single-syllable words or sounds. |  He answered monosyllabically, clearly annoyed by the interruption. 他单音节地回答，显然是被打断很不高兴。| synonym: tersely, briefly, curtly | antonym: polysyllabically, verbosely, lengthily | adverb

Etymology: From mono- (one) + syllable + -ically (adverbial suffix). Ultimately from Greek monos (single, alone) and syllabē (a taking together, syllable).
