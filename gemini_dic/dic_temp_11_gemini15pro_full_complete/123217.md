monoclinous: /məˈnɒklɪnəs/ | adj. |雌雄同花的，雌雄同株的 | Having both male and female reproductive organs in the same flower (perfect flower) or on the same plant (monoecious).  | Example: The monoclinous flowers of the hibiscus plant are large and showy. 芙蓉花的雌雄同株花朵又大又艳丽。

Example: Many common garden plants are monoclinous, simplifying pollination. 许多常见的园林植物都是雌雄同株的，简化了授粉过程。 | Near synonyms: hermaphroditic, perfect, bisexual, monoecious | Antonym: dioecious | Word type: Adjective
