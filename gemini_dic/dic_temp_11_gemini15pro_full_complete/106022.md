kneadability:/ˌniːdəˈbɪləti/
n.
可揉性；揉捏性
The quality of being kneadable; the extent to which something can be kneaded.
The dough has good kneadability.  这块面团的可揉性很好。
kneadableness
unyieldingness, stiffness
noun
