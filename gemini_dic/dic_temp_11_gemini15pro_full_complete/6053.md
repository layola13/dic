amendses: /əˈmɛndzɪz/
noun
赔偿；赔礼
compensation or reparation for a loss or injury, typically to one's honour or dignity.
The company offered amendses to customers affected by the data breach.  这家公司向受数据泄露影响的客户提供赔偿。
He made amendses for his rude behavior by sending a gift basket. 他送了一个礼品篮来弥补他的粗鲁行为。
synonyms: compensation, reparation, restitution, recompense, redress
antonyms: penalty, punishment
词性: 名词


词源:
From Anglo-French *amendes, plural of amende, from amender “to correct, amend,” from Latin emendare “to correct, free from fault,” from ex- “out” + mendum “fault.” The plural form with -es is now archaic; the usual plural is amends.
