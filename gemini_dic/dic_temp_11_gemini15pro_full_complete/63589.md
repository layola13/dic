envision:/ɪnˈvɪʒən/ /ɛnˈvɪʒən/| vt.|设想，展望，想象|To form a mental picture of something not yet present or visible.|She envisioned a future where everyone had access to clean water. 她设想了一个人人都有清洁用水的未来。|近义词：imagine, visualize, conceive, foresee, anticipate|反义词:doubt|verb

envision:/ɪnˈvɪʒən/ /ɛnˈvɪʒən/| n.|设想，展望|The act or process of envisioning.|His envision of a peaceful world inspired many. 他对和平世界的设想鼓舞了很多人。|近义词：vision, imagination, concept|反义词: - |noun  (This noun form is less common)
