endosteal:/ɛnˈdɒstiəl/ | adj. | 骨内膜的 | Relating to the endosteum, the thin vascular membrane lining the medullary cavity of a bone. |  The endosteal surface of the bone is where bone resorption occurs. 骨吸收发生在骨的骨内膜表面。

The endosteal layer is important for bone growth and repair. 骨内膜层对骨骼生长和修复很重要。| 近义词: medullary | 反义词: periosteal | 词性: 形容词
