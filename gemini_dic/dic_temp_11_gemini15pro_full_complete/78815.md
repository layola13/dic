gimlet: /ˈɡɪmlɪt/ | n. |  手钻;  鸡尾酒(由琴酒或伏特加、酸橙汁和糖调制而成) | A small tool with a pointed, spiral end for boring holes, especially in wood. Also, a cocktail made of gin or vodka, lime juice, and sugar. |  He used a gimlet to make holes for the screws. 他用手钻钻孔来上螺丝。  She ordered a gimlet at the bar. 她在酒吧点了一杯鸡尾酒。 | 近义词：(tool) awl, drill; (drink) cocktail | 反义词: 无 | 词性: 名词

gimlet-eyed: /ˈɡɪmlɪt aɪd/ | adj. | 目光锐利的；眼神犀利的 | Having sharp, penetrating vision, often suggesting suspicion or alertness. | The detective gave him a gimlet-eyed stare.  侦探用锐利的目光盯着他。 He has gimlet-eyed attention to detail. 他对细节有着敏锐的洞察力。 | 近义词: sharp-eyed, keen-eyed, observant | 反义词: unobservant, inattentive | 词性: 形容词
