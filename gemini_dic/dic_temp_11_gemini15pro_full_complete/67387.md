exterminable:/ɪkˈstɜːrmɪnəbl/
adj.
可根除的，可消灭的
Capable of being exterminated; possible to destroy completely.
The exterminable pests were finally eradicated from the house. 这些可以消灭的害虫最终从房子里被根除了。
The government is determined to make the disease exterminable. 政府决心使这种疾病可以被根除。
近义词: eradicable, destroyable, killable
反义词: ineradicable, indestructible, immortal
词性: 形容词


exterminate:/ɪkˈstɜːrmɪneɪt/
vt.
根除，消灭，灭绝
To destroy completely; to wipe out.
The settlers exterminated the native population. 殖民者灭绝了当地土著居民。
They used pesticides to exterminate the cockroaches. 他们用杀虫剂消灭蟑螂。
近义词: eradicate, destroy, annihilate
反义词: preserve, conserve, protect
词性: 动词


extermination:/ɪkˌstɜːrmɪˈneɪʃn/
n.
根除，消灭，灭绝
The act of exterminating; complete destruction.
The extermination of the dinosaurs occurred millions of years ago. 恐龙的灭绝发生在数百万年前。
The government ordered the extermination of the infected livestock. 政府下令扑杀受感染的牲畜。
近义词: eradication, destruction, annihilation
反义词: preservation, conservation, protection
词性: 名词
