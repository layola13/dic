go-slow:/ˈɡəʊ sləʊ/ (UK) /ˈɡoʊ sloʊ/ (US)| n. adj.|怠工；缓慢行驶|A form of protest in which work is deliberately slowed down, or a traffic jam.|例句：The workers staged a go-slow to protest the new working conditions. 工人们举行怠工抗议新的工作条件。

例句：The accident caused a go-slow on the highway. 事故导致高速公路上车辆行驶缓慢。|近义词：slowdown, work-to-rule, industrial action|反义词:speed-up, acceleration|词性:名词，形容词
