old-age:/ˈoʊld eɪdʒ/ | adj. | 老年的；高龄的 | Relating to or characteristic of old age. |  The old-age pension provides financial support for senior citizens. 老年退休金为老年人提供经济支持。

The increasing old-age population poses challenges to healthcare systems.  日益增长的老年人口对医疗保健系统构成挑战。 | 近义词: elderly, geriatric, senescent | 反义词: young, youthful | 词性: adjective

old-age:/ˈoʊld eɪdʒ/ | n. | 老年；高龄 | The period of life when one is old. |  He spent his old-age in a quiet village. 他在宁静的乡村度过了晚年。

She is enjoying her old-age, surrounded by her family. 她享受着晚年生活，儿孙绕膝。 | 近义词: seniority, elderliness | 反义词: youth, childhood | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *eald-yldo (attested only as ealdylded 'advanced in years'), from eald 'old' + yldo 'age'.  The hyphenated form "old-age" became standard in the 16th century.
