give-the-lie-to:/ˌɡɪv ðə ˈlaɪ tuː/
verb phrase
揭穿；证明…是假的；反驳
to show that something is not true
例句：Her calm demeanor gave the lie to her nervous inner state. 她镇定的举止掩盖了她内心的紧张。

例句：The facts of the case give the lie to his alibi.  案件的事实证明他的不在场证明是假的。

近义词：contradict, disprove, debunk, refute, expose
反义词：confirm, corroborate, substantiate, verify
词性：动词短语
