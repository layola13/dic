nonlubricating: /ˌnɒnˈluːbrɪkeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 非润滑的，不润滑的 | Not providing lubrication; not designed to reduce friction. |  This nonlubricating coating is ideal for high-temperature applications.  这种非润滑涂层非常适合高温应用。

The nonlubricating surface of the material caused excessive wear.  该材料的非润滑表面导致过度磨损。| 近义词: friction-increasing, non-oiled | 反义词: lubricating, oiled | 词性: 形容词


词源:
non- (prefix)  + lubricating (verb/adjective)

"non-" comes from Latin meaning "not".
"lubricating" comes from Latin "lubricare" meaning "to make smooth or slippery".
