balefulnesses:/ˈbeɪlfʊlnɪsɪz/
n.
险恶; 凶兆
The state of being baleful; menacing or harmful influence.
The balefulnesses of the dark forest filled her with dread.  黑暗森林的险恶气氛让她充满了恐惧。
Near synonyms: malevolence, malignity, sinisterness, ominousness
Near antonyms: benevolence, benignity, auspiciousness
noun


baleful:/ˈbeɪlfʊl/
adj.
险恶的; 凶兆的
Threatening harm; menacing; sinister.
He gave me a baleful look. 他恶狠狠地瞪了我一眼。
Near synonyms: menacing, sinister, ominous, threatening
Near antonyms: benevolent, benign, auspicious
adjective
