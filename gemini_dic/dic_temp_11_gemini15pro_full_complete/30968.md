chalc:/kælk/ | n. | chalk; limestone; calcite | A soft, white, powdery limestone, primarily composed of calcium carbonate (CaCO3), used for writing or drawing on blackboards or other surfaces.  It can also refer to limestone or calcite in general. |  He used colored chalc to draw a picture on the sidewalk. 他用彩色粉笔在人行道上画了一幅画。 The cliffs are made of chalc. 这些悬崖是由石灰岩构成的。 | 近义词: limestone, calcite, calcium carbonate | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps consider: granite, marble - as other types of rock) | 词性: noun

词源: From Old English *cealc, from Latin calx "limestone, lime," from Greek khalix "small stone, pebble, rubble."
