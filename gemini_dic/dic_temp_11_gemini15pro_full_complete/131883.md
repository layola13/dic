noncontemplativeness:/ˌnɒnkənˈtɛmplətɪvnəs/
n.
缺乏沉思；不爱思考
lack of contemplation or meditative thought; absence of reflective thinking
Her noncontemplativeness often led to impulsive decisions. 她不爱思考的性格经常导致她做出冲动的决定。
noncontemplativeness prevented her from fully appreciating the beauty of the sunset.  她缺乏沉思让她无法完全欣赏日落的美丽。
近义词: thoughtlessness, impulsiveness, rashness
反义词: contemplativeness, thoughtfulness, reflectiveness
词性: noun

Etymology:
From non- + contemplativeness.  Contemplativeness comes from contemplate (from Latin contemplatus, past participle of contemplari "to gaze attentively, observe, contemplate," originally "to mark out a space, survey," from com- "together" + templum "temple, place for observation").  The suffix -ness forms nouns from adjectives.
