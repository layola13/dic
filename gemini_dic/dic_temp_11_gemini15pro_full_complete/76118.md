furthest:/ˈfɜːrðɪst/ /ˈfʌrðɪst/| adj. adv.|最远的；最大程度地|situated at the greatest distance in space or time; most remote. |例句：That’s the furthest I’ve ever run. 那是我跑过的最远距离了。|近义词：farthest, remotest, utmost|反义词：nearest, closest|词性：形容词/副词

furthest:/ˈfɜːrðɪst/ /ˈfʌrðɪst/| adj. adv.|最大程度地|to the greatest degree or extent. |例句：He took the argument furthest. 他把这场争论推向了极致。|近义词：utmost, extreme|反义词：least, slightest|词性：形容词/副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English ferthest, furtherest, from Old English fyrrest, fierrest (“furthest”), superlative of feorr (“far”). Cognate with Dutch verrst (“farthest”), German fürst (“prince”, literally “first, foremost”). Double superlative -est added to comparative further.
