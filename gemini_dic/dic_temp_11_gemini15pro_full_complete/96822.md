inescapably:/ɪnɪˈskeɪpəbli/
adverb
不可避免地
in a way that is impossible to avoid
例句：The conclusion is inescapably that we are failing.  结论不可避免地是我们正在失败。

例句：He was inescapably drawn to her. 他不可避免地被她吸引。

近义词：unavoidably, inevitably, necessarily
反义词：avoidably, preventably
词性：adverb
