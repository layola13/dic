over-colors:/ˈoʊvərˌkʌlərz/| n. |套印色，叠印色|The process of printing one color on top of another, often used to create a richer or more nuanced color.  It's the ink printed over a base ink.| The artist used over-colors to create a vibrant green by layering yellow over blue. 艺术家通过在蓝色上叠加黄色来创造鲜艳的绿色。|近义词：overprints, overlays|反义词: base colors, undercolors|noun

over-colors:/ˈoʊvərˌkʌlərz/| v. |套印，叠印|To print one color on top of another.|He over-colored the image to give it a vintage look. 他对图像进行了套印，使其具有复古外观。|近义词：overprint, overlay|反义词: underprint, underlay|verb
