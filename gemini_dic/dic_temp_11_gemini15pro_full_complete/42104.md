cotta:/ˈkɒtə/ (UK), /ˈkɑːtə/ (US)| n.|陶土，赤陶；土锅|A type of earthenware clay, often unglazed, used for making pottery, tiles, and other objects. Also, a cooking pot made of this material.|The gardener used terracotta cottas for his plants.|近义词:terracotta, earthenware, clay|反义词:(No direct antonym, possibly porcelain or stoneware, depending on context)|noun
