map-out:/ˈmæp aʊt/| phrasal verb | 策划；制定；详细安排 |to plan something in detail, usually in a way that is systematic and thorough|  We need to map out a strategy for the next quarter. 我们需要制定下个季度的战略。

The coach mapped out the play for the team. 教练为球队详细制定了比赛方案。

近义词:plan, outline, devise, formulate, strategize| 反义词:improvise, wing it| 词性:动词短语
