nondeporteds:/ˌnɒndɪˈpɔːtɪdz/ | n. | 未被驱逐出境的人 | People who have not been deported or expelled from a country.  This word is not standard English and is likely formed by adding the prefix "non-" to "deported" and then pluralizing it. |  The government is facing criticism for its handling of the nondeporteds. 政府因其处理未被驱逐出境者的手段而面临批评。|  There is a growing population of nondeporteds in the country.  这个国家未被驱逐出境的人口正在增长。| 近义词:  undocumented immigrants, illegal aliens (these terms can be controversial) | 反义词: deportees, expellees | 词性: 名词
