indefeasible:/ˌɪndɪˈfiːzəbl/| adj.|不可废止的，不可剥夺的，不能取消的|Not capable of being annulled, voided, or forfeited.|例句：He has an indefeasible right to the property. 他对这处房产拥有不可剥夺的权利。|例句：The contract contained an indefeasible clause guaranteeing ownership.  合同中包含一项不可废止的条款，保证了所有权。|近义词：inalienable, absolute, unassailable|反义词：defeasible, revocable, contingent|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French *indefaisable (recorded only in the feminine indefaisable), or from medieval Latin indefeasibilis, from in- ‘not’ + defeasibilis (from deficere ‘to do away with, annul’).
