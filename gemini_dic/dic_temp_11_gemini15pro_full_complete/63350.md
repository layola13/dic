entombed:/ɪnˈtuːmd/ /ɛnˈtuːmd/ | v. |埋葬；深埋；使陷入 | To place in or as if in a tomb; bury.  To enclose or confine as if in a tomb. | The ancient pharaohs were entombed in elaborate pyramids. 古代法老被埋葬在精心建造的金字塔里。

The miners were entombed alive after the cave-in.  矿工们在山洞坍塌后被活埋了。 | 近义词：bury, inter, immure, encase, trap | 反义词：exhume, unearth, liberate, free | 词性：动词

词源：From en- + tomb (坟墓) + -ed (动词过去分词后缀).  The prefix "en-" means "in" or "into."
