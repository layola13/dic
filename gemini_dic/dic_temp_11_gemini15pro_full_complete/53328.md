disaccord:/ˌdɪsəˈkɔːrd/ | n. |不一致，分歧，不和 |lack of agreement or harmony; conflict. |例句：There is considerable disaccord between the two groups. 这两个团体之间存在相当大的分歧。

例句：The disaccord between the sisters saddened their parents.  姐妹之间的不和让她们的父母很伤心。

|近义词：disharmony, disagreement, discord, conflict, friction |反义词:accord, agreement, harmony, concord |词性:名词

disaccord:/ˌdɪsəˈkɔːrd/ | v. |不一致，不符 |to be out of harmony; clash. |例句：His behavior disaccords with his words. 他的行为与他的言辞不符。

例句：Her casual attire disaccorded with the formal event. 她的便装与正式场合不符。
