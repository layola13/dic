counter-letter:/ˈkaʊntər ˌlɛtər/| n. |回信；反函；抵销契约|A letter written in reply to another, especially one that opposes or contradicts the first.  A secret agreement designed to invalidate a public agreement. |例句：He produced a counter-letter proving the agreement was null and void. 他出示了一封反函，证明该协议无效。

例句：The counter-letter revealed the true intentions behind the public facade. 这封回信揭示了公众表面背后的真实意图。|近义词：reply, response, rebuttal, rejoinder|反义词:original letter, initial letter|词性:noun
