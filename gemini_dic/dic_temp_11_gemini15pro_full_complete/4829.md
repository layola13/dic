alibaba: /ˌælɪˈbɑːbə/ | n. | 阿里巴巴 | A large Chinese multinational technology company specializing in e-commerce, retail, Internet, and technology.  It is one of the world's largest retail and e-commerce companies.  The name comes from the folk tale "Ali Baba and the Forty Thieves." |  Alibaba is a dominant force in online shopping. 阿里巴巴是在线购物的主导力量。| synonyms: e-commerce giant, online retailer | antonyms: brick-and-mortar store, traditional retailer | noun
