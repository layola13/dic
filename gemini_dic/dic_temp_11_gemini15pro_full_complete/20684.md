bladdered:/ˈblæd.əd/ | adj. | 醉醺醺的；喝得烂醉的 | Very drunk; intoxicated. | He came home absolutely bladdered. 他回家时已经醉得一塌糊涂了。

He was so bladdered he could barely stand. 他醉得站都站不稳了。

近义词: drunk, intoxicated, inebriated, plastered, hammered, sloshed | 反义词: sober | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From bladder + -ed.  Relating to the filling of the bladder, hence, drunk to the point of needing to urinate frequently.
