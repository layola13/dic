ideologist:/ˌaɪdiˈɒlədʒɪst/ /ˌɪdiˈɑːlədʒɪst/
n.| 思想家，理论家|A person who strongly believes in a particular set of ideas, especially political or economic ones, and tries to persuade others to accept them.|He was a passionate ideologist who dedicated his life to promoting his beliefs. 他是一位充满激情的思想家，毕生致力于推广他的信仰。|synonyms: theorist, doctrinaire, proponent, advocate|antonyms: pragmatist, realist|noun

ideological:/ˌaɪdiəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ /ˌɪdiəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/
adj.| 思想上的，意识形态的|Relating to or concerned with ideology.|The two countries had deep ideological differences. 这两个国家有着深刻的意识形态差异。|synonyms: doctrinal, theoretical, philosophical|antonyms: practical, pragmatic|adjective

ideology:/ˌaɪdiˈɒlədʒi/ /ˌɪdiˈɑːlədʒi/
n.| 思想体系，意识形态|A system of ideas and ideals, especially one which forms the basis of economic or political theory and policy.|The party's ideology is based on socialist principles. 该党的意识形态基于社会主义原则。|synonyms: doctrine, philosophy, creed, belief system|antonyms: - |noun
