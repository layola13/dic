monochromatically:/ˌmɒnəkrəˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 单色地，单调地 |  In a way that uses only shades of one color or that involves only one thing or type of thing. | The image was rendered monochromatically in shades of gray.  图像以灰色单色呈现。| 近义词:monotonously, dully | 反义词: colorfully, vibrantly | 词性: 副词

monochrome:/ˈmɒnəkroʊm/ | adj. n. | 单色的，单色画 |  (of a painting, photograph, or video image) using only shades of grey, or using only one colour.  |  He prefers monochrome photography. 他更喜欢单色摄影。| 近义词: single-color, one-color | 反义词: polychrome, multicolor | 词性: 形容词，名词

词源: From Greek *monos* (single) + *khrōma* (color).
