fuage:/ˈfjuːɪdʒ/| n.|炉税，灶税|A tax formerly levied on each hearth or fireplace.|In medieval times, the fuage was a significant source of revenue for the crown. 在中世纪，炉税是王室的重要收入来源。|Example: The fuage was a tax levied per household.  每个家庭都要缴纳炉税。

Example:  The king imposed a heavy fuage on his subjects. 国王向他的臣民征收了沉重的炉税.|Synonyms: hearth tax, chimney tax|Antonyms: tax exemption, tax rebate|noun
