iridescent:/ˌɪrɪˈdɛsənt/ | adj. | 彩虹色的，闪光的 | Showing luminous colors that seem to change when seen from different angles. | The butterfly's wings were iridescent, shimmering in the sunlight. 蝴蝶的翅膀闪着彩虹色的光芒，在阳光下闪耀。|  The oil slick on the water created an iridescent sheen. 水面上的油污形成彩虹色的光泽。 | synonyms: shimmering, opalescent, pearly, lustrous | antonyms: dull, matte, monochrome | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *īridis* (genitive of *iris*, "rainbow") + *-escent* (suffix meaning "beginning to be" or "slightly").  Ultimately from Greek *ἶρις* (îris, "rainbow").
