mulatto:/məˈlætoʊ/ /mjuˈlɑːtəʊ/ | n. | 混血儿（尤指白人和黑人的混血儿） | A person of mixed white and Black ancestry, especially a person with one white and one Black parent.  This term is now widely considered offensive due to its origins in slavery and racial classification. |  例句：In the 19th century, the term "mulatto" was commonly used to categorize people of mixed race.  19世纪，“mulatto”一词通常用于对混血人种进行分类。

例句：The novel explores the complex identity of a young mulatto woman in antebellum Louisiana. 这部小说探讨了路易斯安那州战前一位年轻混血女性的复杂身份。 | 近义词：mixed-race, biracial (preferred), person of color | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
