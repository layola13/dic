assessorial:/əˈsesəriəl/ | adj. | 辅助的，附带的 | Relating to or of the nature of an accessory; supplementary; additional. | The assessorial costs of the property purchase included legal fees and stamp duty.  房产购买的附加费用包括律师费和印花税。 | Synonyms: supplementary, ancillary, auxiliary, additional, incidental | Antonyms: primary, main, principal | adjective

assessorial:/əˈsesəriəl/ | n. | 附加物，附件 | Something added, especially to something else considered principal or more important. |  The assessorials to the main contract included insurance and maintenance. 主合同的附件包括保险和维护。 | Synonyms:  supplement, addition, adjunct, appendage | Antonyms: core, base | noun
