bludgeoners:/ˈblʌdʒənərz/| n.|棍棒打手; 用棍棒殴打的人|People who use a bludgeon (a short, heavy club) as a weapon.  They are often associated with violence and brutality.|The bludgeoners attacked the peaceful protesters. 棍棒打手袭击了和平抗议者。|The museum guards subdued the bludgeoners before they could damage the artifacts.  博物馆警卫在他们损坏文物之前制服了这些暴徒。|synonyms: thugs, assailants, attackers|antonyms: protectors, defenders|noun

bludgeon:/ˈblʌdʒən/| n. vt.|棍棒; (用棍棒) 殴打|A short, heavy club used as a weapon; to hit someone repeatedly with a bludgeon.|The burglar carried a bludgeon. 窃贼携带了一根棍棒。|He was bludgeoned to death. 他被棍棒打死了。|synonyms: club, cudgel; beat, batter|antonyms: shield, protect; caress, soothe|noun, verb
