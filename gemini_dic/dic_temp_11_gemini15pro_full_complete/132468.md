nonderelicts: /ˌnɒndɪˈrɛlɪkts/ | n. | 非遗弃物；未被遗弃的人（或物）| People or things that have not been abandoned or neglected.  Those who maintain their responsibilities and commitments. | The city council praised the nonderelicts, the business owners who invested in and improved their properties despite the economic downturn. | 近义词:  maintainers, stalwarts, keepers | 反义词: derelicts, abandonees | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From "non-" (not) + "derelict" (abandoned). "Derelict" comes from the Latin word "derelictus," the past participle of "derelinquere" (to abandon).
