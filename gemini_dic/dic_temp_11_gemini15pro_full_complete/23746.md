bravadoes:/brəˈvɑːdoʊz/
noun
虚张声势，故作勇敢；虚张声势的行为（bravado的复数）
A swaggering show of courage intended to impress or intimidate.
His bravadoes did not fool anyone. 他的虚张声势骗不了任何人。
近义词: swagger, bluster, boasting, ostentation, pretension
反义词: timidity, fear, humility, modesty
词性: 名词(复数)

bravado:/brəˈvɑːdoʊ/
noun
虚张声势，故作勇敢
A swaggering show of courage intended to impress or intimidate.
Despite his bravado, I could tell he was nervous. 尽管他故作勇敢，但我看得出他很紧张。
近义词: swagger, bluster, boasting, ostentation, pretension
反义词: timidity, fear, humility, modesty
词性: 名词

词源: From Italian *bravata*, from *bravo* (“wild, fierce”), from Late Latin *bravus*. Doublet of brave.
