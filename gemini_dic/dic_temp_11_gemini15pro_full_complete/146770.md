overhyping: /ˌoʊvərˈhaɪpɪŋ/ | v. | 过度炒作，过分宣传 | Excessively promoting or publicizing something, often to the point of creating unrealistic expectations. | The company was accused of overhyping its new product.  这家公司被指责过度炒作其新产品。 |  overpromoting, exaggerating, overselling | understating, downplaying | verb

overhyped: /ˌoʊvərˈhaɪpt/ | adj. | 被过度炒作的，被过分宣传的 | Excessively promoted or publicized. | The movie was overhyped and ultimately disappointing.  这部电影被过度炒作，最终令人失望。 | exaggerated, overpromoted, overblown | understated, underrated | adjective
