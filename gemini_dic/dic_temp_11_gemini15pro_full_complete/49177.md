delacrymation:/diːˌlækrɪˈmeɪʃən/
noun
流泪，催泪
the secretion of tears, especially when abnormal or excessive.
Delacrimation is a common symptom of allergies. 过敏的常见症状是流泪。
近义词: tearing, epiphora, lachrymation
反义词: None (lack of tearing would be a symptom, not a direct antonym)
词性: noun

delacrymation:/diːˌlækrɪˈmeɪʃən/
verb (rarely used)
使流泪
to cause to secrete tears, especially when abnormal or excessive.
The onion delacrimated her eyes. 洋葱让她流泪。
近义词:  None (phrases like "make someone cry" are more common)
反义词: None
词性: verb (rarely used)


ETYMOLOGY->
From French délacrimation, from dé- (de-, meaning "down, from") + lacrimation (the secretion of tears), from Latin lacrima ("tear").
