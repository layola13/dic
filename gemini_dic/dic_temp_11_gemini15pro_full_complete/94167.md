impenitent:/ɪmˈpɛnɪtənt/| adj.|不悔悟的，顽固不化的|Not feeling or showing regret or remorse for wrongdoing; unrepentant.|He remained impenitent despite the evidence against him.  他尽管证据确凿，仍然拒不悔改。|近义词：unrepentant, remorseless, unashamed, hardened|反义词：repentant, contrite, remorseful|词性:形容词
词源：Late Middle English: from im- ‘not’ + penitent.
