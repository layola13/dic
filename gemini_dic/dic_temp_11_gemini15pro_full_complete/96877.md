inexorability:/ɪnˌɛksərəˈbɪləti/ |n.| 不可阻挡性；必然性；无情|The inexorability of time marched on, indifferent to human suffering. 时间无情地流逝，对人类的苦难漠不关心。|English Explanation: The quality of being impossible to stop or prevent.|例句：The inexorability of fate.  命运的不可抗拒性。

例句：The inexorability of the aging process.  衰老过程的不可避免性。|近义词：inevitability, relentlessness, implacability|反义词：avoidability, preventability, flexibility|词性: 名词
