enwall:/ɪnˈwɔːl/
verb
围住，圈起
to enclose or surround with or as if with a wall
The garden is enwalled by high hedges. 花园被高高的树篱围住。
近义词: enclose, surround, fence
反义词: open, expose
词性: v.
