disunion:/dɪsˈjuːnjən/
n.
分裂，不团结
lack of unity or agreement, especially between people or groups; the state of being disunited
The Civil War was a result of the disunion between the North and the South.  南北战争是北方和南方之间不团结的结果。
近义词：discord, division, separation, schism
反义词：unity, harmony, concord, agreement
词性: 名词

disunite:/ˌdɪsjuˈnaɪt/
v.
使分裂，使不和
cause to be no longer united
The issue of slavery disunited the nation. 奴隶制问题使国家分裂。
近义词：divide, separate, sever, disjoin
反义词：unite, join, connect, link
词性: 动词
