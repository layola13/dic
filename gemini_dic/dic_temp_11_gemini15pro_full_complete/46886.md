daringnesses:/ˈdɛərɪŋnɪsɪz/
n.
大胆；勇敢；冒险精神
The quality or state of being daring; audacity.
His daringnesses in battle earned him a medal. 他在战斗中的勇敢为他赢得了一枚奖章。

daringnesses:/ˈdɛərɪŋnɪsɪz/
n.
大胆的行为；冒险的举动
Instances of daring or adventurous acts.
Her youthful daringnesses included climbing the highest tree in the park. 她年轻时的大胆行为包括爬上公园里最高的树。

近义词:audacity, boldness, bravery, courage, nerve, recklessness
反义词:caution, cowardice, fear, timidity

词源：From daring + -ness.  Daring itself comes from the Old English *dearrang, from the verb *durran "to dare".
