commensurable:/kəˈmɛnsərəbəl/ |adj.|可公度的，相称的，相当的|Measurable by the same standard; having a common measure or divisor; proportionate.  Able to be judged by the same standards. |The two lengths are commensurable, both being multiples of the same unit. 这两段长度是可公度的，它们都是同一单位的倍数。

The value of a college education is not always commensurable with its cost. 大学教育的价值并不总是与其成本相称。 |近义词： proportionate, comparable, compatible, similar, equivalent|反义词：incommensurable, disparate, dissimilar, incompatible|词性: adjective

commensurate:/kəˈmɛnʃərət/ |adj.|相称的，相当的|Corresponding in size or degree; proportionate. |Her salary is commensurate with her experience. 她的薪水与她的经验相称。

The punishment should be commensurate with the crime. 惩罚应该与罪行相称。|近义词： proportionate, corresponding, equivalent, appropriate|反义词：incommensurate, disproportionate, inadequate|词性: adjective
