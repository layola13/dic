bordeaux:/bɔːrˈdoʊ/ (US), /bɔːrˈdəʊ/ (UK)| n. adj.|波尔多（法国西南部港市），波尔多葡萄酒，深紫红色|A city and port in southwestern France, known for its wine. Also refers to the dark red-purple color of Bordeaux wine.|例句：This Bordeaux is excellent.  这波尔多葡萄酒很棒。

例句：She wore a bordeaux dress to the gala. 她穿了一件波尔多红色的礼服参加晚会。|近义词：claret, burgundy (for the wine), maroon, deep red (for the color)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps a contrasting color like white or pale yellow)|词性:noun, adjective
