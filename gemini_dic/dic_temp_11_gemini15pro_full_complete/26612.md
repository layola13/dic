cachexic: /kəˈkɛksɪk/ | adj. | 恶病质的；极度消瘦的 | Relating to or exhibiting cachexia, a condition of physical wasting away due to illness such as advanced cancer or AIDS.  |  The patient appeared cachexic, with sunken eyes and protruding bones.  病人看起来很虚弱，眼窝深陷，骨瘦如柴。 |  The cachexic effects of the disease were alarming. 这种疾病造成的恶病质效应令人担忧。 | Synonyms:  emaciated, wasted, atrophied, gaunt | Antonyms:  healthy, robust, plump, well-nourished | adjective
