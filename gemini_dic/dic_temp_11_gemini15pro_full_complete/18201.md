belaying:/bɪˈleɪɪŋ/
|n. / v.|保护, 确保安全|The act of securing a climber with a rope.  The belayer controls the rope to prevent a long fall if the climber slips.|例句：The belaying kept the climber safe during his ascent. 保护措施确保了登山者在攀登过程中的安全。|近义词：securing, safeguarding, roping|反义词: endangering, jeopardizing|词性: 名词/动词
Etymology: From be- + lay (v.), originally meaning "to make fast a rope".
