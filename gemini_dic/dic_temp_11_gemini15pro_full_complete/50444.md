deprecatory:/ˈdɛprɪkətɔːri/ /ˈdɛprɪkeɪtɔːri/| adj.|表示反对的，不赞成的，轻视的|Expressing disapproval; disapproving; belittling. |He gave a deprecatory wave of his hand. 他轻蔑地挥了挥手。|Synonyms: disapproving, critical, disparaging, belittling|Antonyms: approving, complimentary, laudatory|Adjective


deprecate:/ˈdɛprɪkeɪt/| vt.|反对，不赞成，轻视|To express disapproval of; to belittle. |He deprecated the use of violence. 他反对使用暴力。|Synonyms: disapprove of, criticize, condemn, deplore|Antonyms: approve of, commend, praise, extol|Verb
