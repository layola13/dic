oversman:/ˈoʊvərzmən/| n. |仲裁人，裁判员|A person chosen to settle a dispute or disagreement, especially between management and labor.  An umpire or referee.|The oversman ruled in favor of the workers. 仲裁人裁定工人胜诉。|Near synonyms: arbitrator, umpire, referee, judge, arbiter|Antonyms: disputant, litigant|noun
