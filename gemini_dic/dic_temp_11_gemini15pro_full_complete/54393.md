disimagine:/dɪsɪˈmædʒɪn/
|v.|停止想象；不再相信；抹去…的形象|To cease imagining; to cause to be no longer imagined or believed; to unimagine.|She tried to disimagine the horrors she had witnessed. 她试图不再去想她所目睹的恐怖景象。| 近义词:  unimagine, forget, dismiss, reject|反义词: imagine, conceive, visualize|词性: 动词
