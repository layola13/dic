mod:/mɒd/ (British), /mɑːd/ (American)| adj. n.| 模块化的，改装的；改装爱好者 |A shortened form of "modification," referring to something altered or customized, particularly in subcultures like gaming, cars, or fashion.  Also refers to a person who makes such modifications. | He's a real mod, always tinkering with his computer. 他是个真正的改装爱好者，总是摆弄他的电脑。|  She downloaded a mod for the game that added new levels. 她下载了一个游戏模组，添加了新的关卡。 | 近义词: modified, customized, altered, tuner (n.)| 反义词: stock, standard, original|词性: adjective, noun
