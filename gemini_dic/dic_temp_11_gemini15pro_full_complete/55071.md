disregarding:/ˌdɪsrɪˈɡɑːrdɪŋ/
preposition|不理会，漠视，忽视|Ignoring; treating as unimportant.|例句：Disregarding the doctor's advice, he continued to smoke.  他不顾医生的劝告，继续吸烟。|近义词：ignoring, neglecting, overlooking|反义词：heeding, respecting, considering|词性: 介词

disregarding:/ˌdɪsrɪˈɡɑːrdɪŋ/
adjective|漠视的，不顾的|Showing a lack of regard or concern.|例句：He was disregarding of the consequences. 他漠视后果。|近义词：inattentive, indifferent, heedless|反义词：attentive, mindful, considerate|词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "disregard," which comes from the prefix "dis-" (meaning "not" or "opposite of") and the Old French word "regarder" (meaning "to look at" or "to consider").
