impassably:/ɪmˈpɑːsəbli/
adjective
不可通行的
impossible to pass or travel through or over
The road was impassably muddy after the heavy rain.  大雨过后，道路泥泞不堪，无法通行。
近义词:inaccessible, impenetrable
反义词:passable, accessible
词性:adjective

impassably:/ɪmˈpɑːsəbli/
adverb
不可通行地
in a way that is impossible to pass or travel through or over
The mountain trails were impassably blocked by snow.  山间小路被雪完全堵塞，无法通行。
近义词:inaccessibly
反义词:passably
词性:adverb
