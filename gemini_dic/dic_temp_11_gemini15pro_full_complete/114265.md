main's:/meɪnz/ | n. | 主要部分；要点；主旨；（尤指输电线路的）干线；（通常指海洋或河流的）大陆；体力；力气；（诗、文）大海，海洋 |  Something's main part or essential point.  Belonging to the main. | This is the main's event of the year. 这是今年的主要事件。| 近义词：principal, chief, primary, central, leading, key, core, heart, essence, gist, substance,  | 反义词：minor, secondary, subsidiary, peripheral, unimportant | 词性: 名词 (Noun - Possessive form)

词源: From Middle English *maine, *mayne, from Anglo-Norman *maine, *mayne and Old French maine (“hand; power; strength; company of horsemen”), from Latin manus (“hand”).
