glutinative:/ˈɡluːtɪnətɪv/ | adj. | 粘着的；胶着的；(尤指语言)粘着性的 | Tending to adhere; gluey. (especially of a language) characterized by agglutination.  |  Many indigenous languages of the Americas are glutinative.  美洲的许多土著语言都是粘着语。 | agglutinative, cohesive | isolating, analytic | adjective

glutinative:/ˈɡluːtɪnətɪv/ | adj. | 粘合的; 胶粘的 |  United or joined together by or as if by glue. |  The dough was too glutinative to work with.  这块面团太粘了，不好操作。 | sticky, adhesive | non-sticky, separate | adjective
