gloomiest:/ˈɡluːmiɪst/| adj.|最阴郁的，最沮丧的|Superlative form of gloomy: most gloomy.  It describes something that is extremely dark, depressing, or pessimistic.|The gloomiest room in the house was the basement. 房子里最阴暗的房间是地下室。|The economic forecast is the gloomiest in years. 经济预测是多年来最悲观的。|Near synonyms: darkest, bleakest, dreariest|Near antonyms: brightest, cheeriest, sunniest|adjective
