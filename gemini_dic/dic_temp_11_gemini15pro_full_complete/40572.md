contuses:/kənˈtuːziz/ | n. | 挫伤；青肿 | an injury in which the skin is not broken but there is discoloration and pain resulting from a blow | The boxer suffered several contuses to the face.  拳击手面部多处挫伤。 | Synonyms: bruise, contusion | Antonyms: healing, recovery | noun

contuses:/kənˈtuːziz/ | v. | 挫伤；使…出现青肿 | to injure (a part of the body) without breaking the skin so as to cause discoloration and pain |  The fall contused his knee. 摔倒使他的膝盖挫伤。 | Synonyms: bruise, injure | Antonyms: heal, cure | verb
