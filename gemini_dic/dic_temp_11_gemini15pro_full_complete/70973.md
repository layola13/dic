firepowers:/ˈfaɪərˌpaʊərz/
n.
火力，攻击力
The capacity to deliver sustained or concentrated fire or bombardment.  The destructive capability of guns, missiles, or a military force.
The country's firepowers are unmatched in the region. 这个国家的火力在该地区是无与伦比的。
Their firepowers were significantly reduced after the bombing.  轰炸后，他们的火力大大减弱了。
synonyms: firepower, armament, weaponry, arsenal
antonyms: weakness, vulnerability
