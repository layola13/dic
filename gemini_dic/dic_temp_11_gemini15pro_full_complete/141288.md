open-airishnesses:/ˌoʊpənˈɛəriʃnɪsɪz/
| n. |露天状态；户外氛围|The quality of being open-air or having multiple open-air qualities. It refers to the atmosphere and feeling of being outdoors, exposed to the elements.  It suggests a pervasiveness or abundance of open-air characteristics.|The festival had a delightful open-airishness, with stages set amidst the trees and food stalls scattered across the lawn.  节日充满着令人愉悦的户外氛围，舞台设置在树林间，小吃摊散落在草坪上。|Synonyms: outdoorsiness, airiness, al fresco quality|Antonyms: indoorsiness, confinement, claustrophobia
