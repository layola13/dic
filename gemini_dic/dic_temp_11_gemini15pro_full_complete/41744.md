corroval: /kəˈroʊvəl/
n.
确认；批准；认可
An official confirmation or approval.
The project finally received corroval from the board.  这个项目最终得到了董事会的批准。
The president gave his corroval to the plan. 总统批准了这项计划。

近义词: confirmation, approval, ratification, sanction, endorsement
反义词: disapproval, rejection, denial, veto

词性: noun

词源:  Likely a back-formation from "corroborate" (to confirm or support), influenced by words like "approval."  "Corroborate" ultimately comes from the Latin "corroborare," meaning "to strengthen."
