marksmen:/ˈmɑːrksmən/ | n. | 神枪手；射手 | A person skilled in shooting, especially with a rifle. |  The marksmen took aim at the target. 神枪手瞄准了目标。

The competition attracted marksmen from all over the country. 比赛吸引了来自全国各地的射手。| 近义词: sharpshooter, sniper, rifleman | 反义词: amateur, novice | Noun
