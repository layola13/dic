decoct:/dɪˈkɒkt/ (UK), /dɪˈkɑːkt/ (US)| vt.| 煎煮，熬制；提取（精华）|To extract the essence from something by boiling or steeping it.|The herbalist decocted a potent remedy from the roots.  草药师从这些根中煎出了一种强效的药剂。

The chef decocted a rich broth from the bones.  厨师用骨头熬制了一锅浓汤。|近义词：brew, infuse, extract, concentrate|反义词: dilute|v.
