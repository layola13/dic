outpaths:/ˈaʊtpɑːðz/
noun
输出路径; 外出路径
The routes or paths used for exiting or leaving a place or system.  It can refer to physical paths as well as abstract routes, such as data transmission.
The airport has multiple outpaths for passenger departures. 机场有多个旅客出发路径。
The program generates several outpaths for the processed data. 程序为处理后的数据生成多个输出路径。
synonyms: exits, egresses, outlets
antonyms: entrances, ingresses, inlets
n.
