acquittals:/əˈkwɪt(ə)lz/
|n.|无罪释放；宣告无罪；清白|An acquittal is a judgment that a person is not guilty of the crime with which they have been charged.  无罪释放是指判定一个人没有犯下他们被指控的罪行。|The jury delivered acquittals for all three defendants. 陪审团宣布所有三名被告无罪释放。|近义词：absolutions, exonerations|反义词：convictions|Noun
