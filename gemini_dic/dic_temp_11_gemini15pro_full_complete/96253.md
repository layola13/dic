indicatory:/ˈɪndɪkətɔːri/ (US), /ɪnˈdɪkətəri/ (UK)| adj.|指示的，标示的，表明的|Serving as a sign or indication of something; pointing out or revealing.|The high fever is indicatory of a serious infection. 高烧表明感染严重。|Synonyms: indicative, suggestive, symptomatic, revelatory|Antonyms: concealing, hiding, masking|adjective

indicatory:/ˈɪndɪkətɔːri/ (US), /ɪnˈdɪkətəri/ (UK)| n.|指示器，标志|Something that serves as a sign or indication.|The flashing light is an indicatory of danger. 闪烁的灯光是危险的标志。|Synonyms: indicator, sign, signal, marker|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, consider using words like "camouflage" or "disguise" depending on context)|noun
