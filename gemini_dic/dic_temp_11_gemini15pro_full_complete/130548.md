nonacoustics:/ˌnɒn əˈkuːstɪks/
noun

Relating to branches of physics other than acoustics; not involving or dependent on sound.
The research focused on the nonacoustics properties of the material.  这项研究集中在材料的非声学特性上。
The lab equipment measured nonacoustics vibrations.  实验室设备测量了非声学振动。
Near Antonyms: acoustics, sonic
Adjective (rarely used): nonacoustic
