empty-headed: /ˌɛmptiˈhɛdɪd/ | adj. |  愚蠢的，轻浮的，没头脑的 | Lacking intelligence or good judgment; frivolous. | She made an empty-headed remark about the serious situation. 她对这个严肃的局势发表了轻浮的评论。| He's so empty-headed, he forgot his own birthday. 他真是个没头脑的人，竟然连自己的生日都忘了。 | 近义词：silly, foolish, frivolous, ditzy, airheaded, scatterbrained | 反义词：intelligent, sensible, wise, thoughtful | 词性：形容词
词源：empty (空的) + headed (头的)
