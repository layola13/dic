countercriticism:/ˌkaʊntərˈkrɪtɪˌsɪzəm/
noun
反批评，反驳
An act of criticizing someone or something that has criticized you.
例句：Her countercriticism of the report was well-researched and convincing. 她对报告的反驳经过充分研究，令人信服。

例句：He offered a sharp countercriticism of her analysis of the situation. 他对她的情况分析提出了尖锐的反驳。
synonym:rebuttal, refutation, response
antonym:criticism, attack
n.
