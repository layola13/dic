etherealnesses:/ɪˈθɪəriəlnɪsɪz/| n. |空灵特质；飘渺之感|The quality of being ethereal; a delicate and otherworldly beauty.|Her paintings capture the etherealnesses of the morning mist. 她的画捕捉到了晨雾的空灵之美。|近义词：delicacy, otherworldliness, spirituality, airiness, refinement|反义词：earthiness, grossness, materiality, coarseness|词性: 名词

词源: From ethereal + -ness.  "Ethereal" comes from Late Latin aethereus, from Greek aitherios "of the upper air," from aithēr "upper air; bright, pure air; the sky."
