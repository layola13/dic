arriere:/əˈrɪər/| n. adj.|后面，背后，欠款；落后的|Something that is in the arriere is toward the back or rear.  Also can refer to being in arrears, meaning behind in payment or progress.|The officer inspected the arriere of the troops. 军官视察了部队的后方。 |The rent payments are in arriere. 租金支付拖欠了。|近义词：rear, back, behind, overdue, late|反义词:front, ahead, advanced, current|词性:名词，形容词
Etymology: From French *arrière*, from Old French *ariere*, ultimately from Latin *ad retro* ("to the back").
