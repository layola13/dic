adventurist:/ədˈvɛntʃ(ə)rɪst/ | n. |冒险主义者；投机分子|A person who engages in risky and exciting undertakings, often for personal gain or excitement, especially in business or politics.| He was denounced as a reckless adventurist. 他被谴责为鲁莽的冒险主义者。|Synonyms: speculator, gambler, opportunist, maverick| Antonyms:  conservative, cautious, prudent| noun

adventurous:/ədˈvɛntʃ(ə)rəs/ | adj. |爱冒险的；充满冒险的|Willing to take risks or try new methods, ideas, or experiences; involving or full of risk or excitement.|She's always been adventurous. 她一直都很有冒险精神。|Synonyms: daring, bold, audacious, enterprising| Antonyms: timid, cautious, fearful| adjective
