enwreathe: /ɪnˈriːð/ /ɛnˈriːθ/
verb
环绕；盘绕；用…环绕
To encircle or adorn with a wreath or something resembling a wreath.
The bride's hair was enwreathed with flowers.  新娘的头发上环绕着鲜花。
Smoke enwreathed the mountain peak. 烟雾缭绕着山峰。
近义词: garland, festoon, crown, adorn, decorate
反义词: strip, bare
词性: verb
