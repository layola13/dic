diadelphous:/ˌdaɪəˈdɛlfəs/| adj.|二体雄蕊的，指雄蕊的花丝合生成两束的|Having stamens united by their filaments so as to form two groups.  This arrangement is common in the pea family (Fabaceae).|Example 1: The diadelphous stamens of the sweet pea are a key characteristic of the species. 香豌豆的二体雄蕊是该物种的一个关键特征。

Example 2:  Many legumes have diadelphous flowers, with nine stamens fused and one free. 许多豆科植物都有二体雄蕊的花，其中九个雄蕊融合在一起，一个雄蕊是分离的。|近义词: (No direct synonym, but related terms include) monadelphous, polyadelphous|反义词: None|词性: adjective
