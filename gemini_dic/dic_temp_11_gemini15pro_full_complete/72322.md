flouters:/ˈflaʊtərz/| n. |蔑视者，嘲笑者，违反者|People who openly disregard a rule, law, or convention.  Those who treat something with contemptuous disregard.|The company flouters environmental regulations.  这家公司无视环境法规。|synonyms: scoffers, mockers, scorners, deriders,  violators, transgressors|antonyms: conformists, adherents, followers|noun
