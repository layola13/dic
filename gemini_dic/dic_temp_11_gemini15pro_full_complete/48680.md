deferrer:/dɪˈfɜːrər/
n.|延期者，推迟者|Someone who postpones or delays something.|The deferrer of the meeting caused much inconvenience.  会议的推迟者造成了很多不便。|procrastinator, postponer|expediter, advancer|noun

deferrer:/dɪˈfɜːrər/
n.|延期付款者|Someone who defers payment.|The deferrer of the loan payment will incur penalties. 延期偿还贷款的人将受到处罚。|debtor,  payee (in some contexts)|creditor, payer (in some contexts)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "defer" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Defer" comes from Old French "deferrer"  and ultimately from Latin "differre" (to carry apart, to postpone), from "dis-" (apart) + "ferre" (to carry).
