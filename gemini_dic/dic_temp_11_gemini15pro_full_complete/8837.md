antiexpressionist:/ˌæntiɪkˈspreʃənɪst/
| adj. | 反表现主义的 | Opposed to or rejecting expressionism, an artistic style that emphasizes the artist's subjective feelings and emotions rather than objective representation. |  Her antiexpressionist paintings focused on precise geometric forms and avoided any hint of emotional outburst. 她的反表现主义绘画侧重于精确的几何形式，避免任何情绪爆发的暗示。 | near antonyms: expressionistic, emotional, subjective | antonyms: objective, representational | adjective
