Characterized by menial or unskilled labor involving fetching and carrying things.|He had a fetch-and-carry job at the office. 他在办公室做跑腿的工作。|Synonyms: gofer, errand-boy, dogsbody|Antonyms: managerial, executive|adjective, noun
