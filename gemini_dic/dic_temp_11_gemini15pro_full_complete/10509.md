appointor:/əˈpɔɪntər/ | n. | 任命者；指定人 |  A person who appoints someone to a position or office. | The appointor chose a suitable candidate for the job. 任命者为这份工作选择了一位合适的候选人。 | Example: The king was the appointor of the new prime minister. 国王是新首相的任命者。 | Synonyms: nominator, designator, selector | Antonyms: appointee | Noun
