neurapophysis:/ˌnjʊərəˈpɒfɪsɪs/
noun
神经弓
The bony dorsal part of a vertebra, formed from two laminae that fuse in the midline and enclose the vertebral canal.
The neurapophysis of the cervical vertebra is typically small and bifid. 颈椎的神经弓通常小而分叉。
The fracture extended to the neurapophysis, compressing the spinal cord. 骨折延伸至神经弓，压迫脊髓。
Synonyms: neural arch
Antonyms: centrum, vertebral body
