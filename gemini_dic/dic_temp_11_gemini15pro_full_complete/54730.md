disparagement:/dɪˈspærɪdʒmənt/| n.|贬低，轻视，诋毁|The act of speaking about someone or something in a way that shows you do not respect or value them.|His constant disparagement of his colleagues created a hostile work environment. 他不断贬低同事，制造了敌对的工作环境。|近义词：belittlement, denigration, depreciation|反义词：praise, commendation, compliment|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *desparegement*, from desparagier "to marry unequally," from des- "down" + parage "rank, lineage," from Latin par "equal." The sense shifted from unequal marriage to lowering in rank or estimation generally.
