characterisable:/ˌkærəktəˈraɪzəbl/ | adj. | 可描述的，可刻画的 | Able to be described or characterized.  This means that something has distinct features that allow it to be identified or defined. | This bacteria is characterisable by its resistance to antibiotics. 这种细菌的特点是对抗生素有抵抗力。|  The distinct artistic style is easily characterisable. 这种独特的艺术风格很容易被描述。 | describable, definable, identifiable | uncharacterisable, indefinable | adjective
