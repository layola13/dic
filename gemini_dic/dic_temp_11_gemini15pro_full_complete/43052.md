cowl:/kaʊl/
noun
头巾，兜帽，通风罩
A large loose hood, especially one forming part of a monk's habit.  A metal covering for a chimney, engine, etc.
The monk lowered his cowl.  和尚拉低了他的兜帽。
The mechanic checked the engine cowl.  机械师检查了发动机罩。
