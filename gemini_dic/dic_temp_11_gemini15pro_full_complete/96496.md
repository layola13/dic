indo-african:/ˌɪndoʊˈæfrɪkən/
adjective
印非的；印度和非洲的
Relating to both India and Africa.  Of or pertaining to the hypothetical Indo-African language family.
The conference explored indo-african trade relations.  这次会议探讨了印非贸易关系。
Near Synonyms:  Indian and African, Indo-African
Antonyms:  N/A
