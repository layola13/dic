inkiest:/ˈɪŋkiɪst/| adj.|最墨黑的，最黑暗的|Superlative adjective of inky: covered in or stained with ink; black or very dark.  Characterized by deep darkness or gloom.|例句：The inkiest shadows clung to the corners of the room.  最墨黑的阴影笼罩着房间的角落。|近义词：darkest, blackest, murkiest|反义词:brightest, lightest|词性:形容词
词源: From ink + -y + -est.  Ultimately from Old French "enque" and Late Latin "encaustum."
