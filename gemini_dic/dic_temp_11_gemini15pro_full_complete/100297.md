intersexes:/ˌɪntərˈsɛksiːz/
|n.|间性人|People who are born with sex characteristics (including genitals, gonads and chromosome patterns) that do not fit typical binary notions of male or female bodies are intersex.|Intersex people face unique challenges in a society that often forces them into binary gender categories.|synonyms: hermaphrodites (outdated and often considered offensive), people with variations of sex characteristics (DSD)|antonyms:  male, female|noun
