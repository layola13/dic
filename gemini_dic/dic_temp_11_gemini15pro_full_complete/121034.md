misconceiver:/ˌmɪskənˈsiːvər/
n.
误解者；持有错误观念的人
One who misconceives; someone who has a mistaken notion or idea.
He was a misconceiver of the situation, thinking it was a joke. 他误解了情况，以为这是一个玩笑。
misinterpreter, misconstruer
correct interpreter, understanding individual
noun
