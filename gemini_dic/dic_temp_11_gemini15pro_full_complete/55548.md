districted:/ˈdɪstrɪktɪd/
adjective
划分成区的；分成学区的
Divided into districts, especially for administrative or educational purposes.
The city is districted into several different zones.  这座城市被划分成几个不同的区域。
Schools are districted based on location. 学校是根据位置划分的。
近义词: zoned, regionalized
反义词: unified, integrated
词性: adjective
词源: From district + -ed, meaning "having been made into districts".  来自district + -ed，意思是“已经被划分成区域”。
