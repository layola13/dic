discussive:/dɪˈskʌsɪv/
adj.
爱讨论的；喜欢讨论的
inclined to discuss or debate; characterized by or given to discussion.
The class had a discussive atmosphere, with everyone eager to share their ideas.  这个班级有爱讨论的氛围，每个人都渴望分享他们的想法。
They engaged in a discussive exchange of ideas about the project. 他们就这个项目进行了思想交流。
近义词:talkative, communicative, conversational
反义词:reserved, reticent, taciturn
词性:形容词
