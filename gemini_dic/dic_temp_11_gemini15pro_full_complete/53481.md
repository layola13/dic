disarranging: /dɪsəˈreɪndʒɪŋ/
verb
扰乱，弄乱
putting something into a state of disorder
The wind disarranged her carefully styled hair. 风吹乱了她精心打理的头发。
disordering, messing up, disrupting
arranging, organizing
v.
