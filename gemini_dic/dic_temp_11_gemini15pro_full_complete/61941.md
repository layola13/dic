emulsibility:/ɪˌmʌlsɪˈbɪləti/
n.
乳化性，乳化能力
The ability of a liquid to be emulsified; the capacity of a liquid to mix with another liquid that is normally immiscible (like oil and water) to form an emulsion.
The emulsibility of this oil is excellent, making it suitable for salad dressings.  这种油的乳化性非常好，适合做沙拉酱。
近义词：miscibility, blendability
反义词：immiscibility
