incident-to:/ˈɪnsɪdənt tuː/| adj.|附带的，伴随的；次要的|Relating to, dependent on, or occurring as a consequence of something else. Specifically used in medical billing to describe services provided by non-physician practitioners (NPPs) under the direct supervision of a physician.|The nurse's services were billed as incident-to the physician's visit.  护士的服务作为医生的随访费用记账。|Synonyms: ancillary, subsidiary, attendant, appurtenant|Antonyms: primary, principal, main|adjective
