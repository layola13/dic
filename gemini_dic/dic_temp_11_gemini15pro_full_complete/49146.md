dejectedly:/dɪˈdʒɛktɪdli/
adverb
沮丧地，垂头丧气地
In a manner expressing discouragement or sadness.
She walked dejectedly away after hearing the bad news. 她听到坏消息后垂头丧气地走开了。
He sighed dejectedly and slumped onto the sofa. 他沮丧地叹了口气，瘫倒在沙发上。
近义词: sadly, glumly, morosely, despondently
反义词: cheerfully, happily, joyfully
词性: adverb

词源:  From the adjective "dejected" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Dejected" comes from the Latin "deicere," meaning "to throw down," from "de-" (down) + "iacere" (to throw).
