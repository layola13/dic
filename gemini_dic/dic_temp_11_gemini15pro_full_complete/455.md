ablet:/ˈæblɪt/ | n. |小药片; 片剂|A small, flat, and often round piece of a solid medicine that you swallow. |例句：The doctor prescribed me an ablet for my headache. 医生给我开了一片药治头痛。

例句：She took two ablets after dinner. 晚饭后她服用了两片药。


近义词：pill, tablet, capsule |反义词: - |词性:noun
