cronish:/ˈkrɔnɪʃ/| adj.|康沃尔郡的；康沃尔郡人的；康沃尔语的|Relating to Cornwall, its people, or their language.|例句：She speaks with a cronish accent. 她说话带着康沃尔口音。|近义词：Cornish|反义词:N/A|词性:形容词


Etymology: From Middle English *Cornisch*, from Old English *Cornwālisc* (Cornish), from *Cornwēalas* (the Cornish people) + *-isc* (suffix forming adjectives). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *kurniz* (horn) referring to the shape of the peninsula, and *walhaz* (foreigner, Celt).
