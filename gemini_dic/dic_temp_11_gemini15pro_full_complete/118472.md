mesiad:/ˈmiːsiæd/| n. |史诗;  (尤指) 长篇叙事诗|A long narrative poem, typically one celebrating the deeds of a hero or heroine, especially one modeled on Homer's Iliad or Odyssey. |Milton's "Paradise Lost" is considered a great English mesiad. |弥尔顿的《失乐园》被认为是一部伟大的英语史诗。|近义词：epic, epopee|反义词: None|词性: noun

词源: From Greek *mesiē* (epic poetry)
