amazonic:/ˌæməˈzɒnɪk/ | adj. |亚马逊的；巨大的，强大的|Of or relating to the Amazon River or its basin; resembling the Amazon region, especially in being vast, lush, or wild. |例句：The amazonic rainforest is teeming with life.  亚马逊雨林充满了生机。

例句：He has amazonic strength. 他力大无穷。|近义词：huge, immense, vast, gigantic, colossal, massive |反义词：small, tiny, minuscule | 词性: 形容词
