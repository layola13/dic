drossless:/ˈdrɒslɪs/| adj.|无渣的，纯净的|Free from dross; pure.  Referring to something, usually a metal or other substance, that is free from impurities or waste products.  Can also be used metaphorically to describe something pure or uncorrupted.|The drossless gold shone brightly.  纯净的黄金闪闪发光。|近义词：pure, unadulterated, refined, immaculate|反义词：impure, adulterated, contaminated, sullied|词性:形容词
词源: From "dross" (waste matter) + "-less" (suffix indicating absence).  "Dross" comes from Old English *dros* meaning "dregs, refuse."
