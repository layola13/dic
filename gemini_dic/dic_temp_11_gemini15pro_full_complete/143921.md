outproduces:/ˌaʊtprəˈd(j)uːsɪz/
verb
产量超过，生产力高于
Produces more than something else.
China outproduces the US in steel.  中国钢产量超过美国。
Synonyms: surpasses, exceeds, overtakes
Antonyms: underperforms, lags behind
词性: 动词
