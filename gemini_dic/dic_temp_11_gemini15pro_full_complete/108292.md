laureated:/ˈlɔːriˌeɪtɪd/
adjective
获得荣誉的；戴桂冠的；享有殊荣的
crowned with laurel, as a mark of honor; honored or praised
例句：He was a laureated poet. 他是一位享有殊荣的诗人。
例句：The laureated scientist gave a moving speech.  这位获奖的科学家发表了感人的演讲。

近义词：honored, acclaimed, distinguished, celebrated
反义词：unhonored, undistinguished, unknown
