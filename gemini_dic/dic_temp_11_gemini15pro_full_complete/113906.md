madrigalists:/ˈmædrɪɡəlɪsts/
n.
作曲家；创作牧歌的人；牧歌演唱者
Composers or singers of madrigals.
The madrigalists of the Renaissance period created complex and beautiful vocal works. 文艺复兴时期的牧歌作曲家创作了复杂而优美的声乐作品。
The English madrigalists were particularly influential in the development of the form. 英国牧歌作曲家对这种形式的发展尤其具有影响力。
synonyms: composers, musicians, singers
antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider non-musicians, instrumentalists)
noun
