disgruntledly:/dɪsˈɡrʌnt(ə)ldli/
adverb
不满地，牢骚地
in a disgruntled or discontented manner.
He stomped off disgruntledly after losing the game.  他输了比赛后不满地跺着脚走了。
She muttered disgruntledly about the long wait. 她不满地抱怨等待时间太长。
近义词: sullenly, resentfully, moodily, peevishly
反义词: cheerfully, happily, contentedly, pleasantly
词性: adverb

词源: From "disgruntled" + "-ly."  "Disgruntled" comes from the prefix "dis-" (expressing negation) + "gruntle" (to put in good humor).  The word "gruntle" is now rarely used on its own.
