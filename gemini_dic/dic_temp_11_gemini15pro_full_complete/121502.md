mislearning:/ˌmɪsˈlɜːrnɪŋ/ | n. | 错误学习；学错 |The act or process of learning something incorrectly or developing a misunderstanding of a concept. |  He realized his mislearning of the historical facts after reading a new biography. 他在读了一本新的传记后，意识到了自己对历史事实的错误学习。

His mislearning about the country's culture led to some embarrassing situations. 他对该国文化的错误学习导致了一些尴尬的局面。


| 近义词: misconception, misunderstanding, fallacy | 反义词: learning, understanding |词性:名词
