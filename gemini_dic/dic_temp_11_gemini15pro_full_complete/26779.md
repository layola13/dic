caesura:/siˈzʊərə/ /səˈʒʊrə/
|n.|休止，停顿，中断|A break or pause in the rhythm of a line of poetry, marked in scansion by a double vertical line. It is often used to emphasize a particular word or phrase or to create a dramatic effect.|The poem uses caesura to create a sense of suspense. 这首诗使用停顿来营造悬念感。|Synonyms: pause, break, interruption, rest|Antonyms: continuation, flow|noun

caesura:/siˈzʊərə/ /səˈʒʊrə/
|n.| (音乐)休止|A pause or break in a musical phrase.|The composer used a caesura to create a sense of anticipation.  这位作曲家使用休止来营造一种期待感。|Synonyms: pause, break, interruption, rest|Antonyms: continuation, flow|noun
