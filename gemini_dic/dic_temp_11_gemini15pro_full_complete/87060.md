heptagynous:/hɛpˈtæɡɪnəs/
adjective
七雌蕊的
Having seven pistils or styles in the flower.
Example: The heptagynous flower attracted the attention of the botanist.  这朵七雌蕊的花吸引了植物学家的注意。
Example:  Heptagynous plants are relatively rare in this region.  七雌蕊植物在这个地区比较少见。
Near Synonyms:  seven-styled, seven-pistilled (These are descriptive phrases rather than single words.)
Antonyms: (No direct antonym exists.  One might use terms like "monogynous" (one pistil) or "digynous" (two pistils) to express the opposite concept, depending on the specific context.)
词性: 形容词
