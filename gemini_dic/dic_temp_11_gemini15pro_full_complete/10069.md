apocopated:/əˈpɒkəpeɪtɪd/| adj.|省略词尾的，缩略的|Shortened by the loss of one or more sounds or letters from the end of a word.  Derived from the Greek *apokoptein*, meaning "to cut off."|The word "bio" is an apocopated form of "biology."  “bio”是“biology”的缩略形式。|Example:  The poet used an apocopated form of the word "heaven" for metrical reasons. 这位诗人为了韵律的缘故使用了“heaven”的缩略形式。|Synonyms: abbreviated, shortened, truncated, clipped|Antonyms: lengthened, extended, elongated|adjective
