overfertilizations: /ˌoʊvərˈfɜːrtɪlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 过度施肥 | The act of applying too much fertilizer to soil, which can have negative impacts on plant growth and the environment. | Excessive overfertilizations can lead to nutrient runoff and water pollution. 过度施肥会导致营养物质流失和水污染。| Overfertilization can damage the delicate balance of soil ecosystems. 过度施肥会破坏土壤生态系统的微妙平衡。 | 近义词: overapplication, excessive fertilization | 反义词: underfertilization | noun
