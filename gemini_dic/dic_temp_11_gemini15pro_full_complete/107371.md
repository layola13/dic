lamenesses:/ˈleɪmnɪsɪz/
n.
跛行，残废，缺陷
the state of being lame; limitations or weaknesses
The horse's lameness was obvious as it struggled to walk. 这匹马的跛足很明显，因为它走路很费劲。
The lameness of the argument was apparent to everyone.  这个论点的缺陷对每个人来说都很明显。
synonym: disability, infirmity, weakness, defect, imperfection
antonym: strength, ability, health, perfection
