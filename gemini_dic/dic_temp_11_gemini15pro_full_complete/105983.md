knack:/næk/
n.
诀窍，技巧，技能
A special skill or ability to do something easily and well.
She has a knack for making people feel comfortable. 她有一种让人感到舒服的诀窍。
He has a real knack for languages. 他有真正的语言天赋。
近义词: talent, gift, aptitude, skill, ability, flair
反义词: inability, incompetence, ineptitude
词性: 名词

knack:/næk/
Etymology:  From Middle English *knacken (“to crack, snap”), of uncertain origin. Perhaps related to Middle Dutch knaken, knacken (“to crack, break”). More at crack.
词源：源于中古英语 *knacken（“破裂，折断”），起源不确定。可能与中古荷兰语 knaken、knacken（“破裂、断裂”）有关。更多信息请参见 crack。
