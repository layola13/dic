meretricious:/ˌmɛrɪˈtrɪʃəs/| adj.|俗艳的；华而不实的；虚有其表的| Appealing or attractive but having in reality no value or integrity.| Her prose style is meretricious, all glitter and no substance. 她的散文风格华而不实，徒有华丽的外表而缺乏实质内容。| 近义词：gaudy, flashy, tawdry, showy, ostentatious | 反义词：genuine, authentic, real, substantive |词性:形容词

meretricious:/ˌmɛrɪˈtrɪʃəs/| adj.| 俗艳的；华而不实的；虚有其表的|Seeming attractive but really false or of little value.|The meretricious glamour of the casino fascinated her. 赌场的虚假魅力使她着迷。| 近义词：specious, spurious, artificial | 反义词：genuine, unpretentious |词性:形容词
