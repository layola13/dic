appurtenant:/əˈpɜːrtɪnənt/
adjective
附属的；相关的
belonging to; pertinent
The garage is appurtenant to the house.  车库是房屋的附属建筑。
The appurtenant land came with the purchase of the house. 购买房子时附带了所属土地。
近义词:  belonging, related, attached, pertinent, accessory, incident
反义词:  unrelated, detached, separate, independent
词性: adjective

appurtenance:/əˈpɜːrtnəns/
noun
附属物；附加物
something that belongs to something else; an accessory
The shed is an appurtenance to the main building.  棚屋是主楼的附属建筑。
The legal document listed all the appurtenances of the property.  法律文件列出了该房产的所有附属物。
近义词: accessory, adjunct, attachment, appendage
反义词:  (No clear single antonym, but could use concepts like "core," "essential,"  "primary")
词性: noun

词源:
From Old French *appartenant (present participle of appartenir “to belong to”), from Latin appertinēre, from ad- (“to”) + pertinēre (“to pertain”).
