emulsion:/ɪˈmʌlʃən/ | n. | 乳状液；乳剂；感光乳剂 | A mixture of two or more liquids that are normally immiscible (unmixable or unblendable). One liquid is dispersed in the other in the form of small droplets. |  Photographic film is coated with a light-sensitive emulsion.  照相胶片上涂有一层感光乳剂。

The mayonnaise is an emulsion of oil and vinegar. 蛋黄酱是油和醋的乳浊液。 | synonyms: suspension, mixture, blend, colloid | antonyms: solution, compound | noun
