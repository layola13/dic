credulity:/krəˈduːləti/ /krɪˈduːləti/| n. |轻信，易信|a tendency to be ready to believe that something is real or true, especially something that is unlikely or strange|例句：His credulity made him an easy target for con men. 他的轻信使他很容易成为骗子的目标。

例句：She showed surprising credulity about the story. 她对这个故事表现出令人惊讶的轻信。|近义词：gullibility, naivety, simple-mindedness|反义词:skepticism, cynicism, disbelief|词性:noun
