haded:/ˈheɪdɪd/| adj.|荫蔽的，遮蔽的|shaded, sheltered from direct light|The haded patio was a welcome respite from the afternoon sun. 荫蔽的露台是下午阳光下的一个受欢迎的休憩场所。|近义词：shaded, shadowy, dim|反义词:sunny, bright|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From hade + -ed.  Hade itself is an archaic word meaning "shade" or "shadow," likely derived from Old English  *hǣd, related to Old High German heida "heath" (a place with little sun). The suffix "-ed" forms adjectives, indicating the presence of the quality described by the root word.
