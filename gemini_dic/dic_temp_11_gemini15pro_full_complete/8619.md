anticolonialisms: /ˌæntiˈkəˈloʊniəlɪzəmz/
n.|反殖民主义（复数）|Theories and practices opposed to colonialism, often emphasizing diverse experiences and perspectives.|
例句：Anticolonialisms emerged as a powerful force in the 20th century.  反殖民主义在20世纪成为一股强大的力量。

例句：Her research focuses on the various anticolonialisms that shaped African literature. 她的研究重点是非洲文学形成过程中出现的各种反殖民主义。
近义词：decolonization, anti-imperialism|
反义词：colonialism, imperialism|
词性：noun
