overlimited:/ˌoʊvərˈlɪmɪtɪd/
adj.
过度限制的
restricted excessively; subject to too many limitations.
The company's growth was overlimited by strict regulations.  这家公司的发展受到严格规定的过度限制。
His creativity was overlimited by the rigid curriculum. 他的创造力被僵化的课程过度限制了。
近义词: restricted, confined, constrained, hampered
反义词: unrestricted, unconstrained, free, liberated
词性: 形容词


overlimited:/ˌoʊvərˈlɪmɪtɪd/
v. (past tense and past participle overlimited)
过度限制
to restrict excessively; to subject to too many limitations.
The government overlimited the freedom of speech. 政府过度限制了言论自由。
Bureaucracy overlimited the company's ability to innovate. 官僚主义过度限制了公司的创新能力。
近义词: restrict, confine, constrain, hamper
反义词: liberate, free, unleash
词性: 动词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "limited" (past participle of the verb "limit," from Old French "limiter," ultimately from Latin "limes," meaning "boundary").
