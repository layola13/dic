optimists:/ˈɒptɪmɪsts/ (UK), /ˈɑːptɪmɪsts/ (US)| n. |乐观主义者|People who have a positive outlook on life and expect good things to happen.|例句：The optimists believe the economy will recover soon. 乐观主义者相信经济很快就会复苏。

例句：Even in difficult situations, optimists manage to find a silver lining. 即使在困境中，乐观主义者也能找到一线希望。

近义词：positive thinkers, hopeful people|反义词: pessimists|词性:Noun
