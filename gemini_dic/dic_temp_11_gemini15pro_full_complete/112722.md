loyally: /ˈlɔɪəli/ | adv. | 忠诚地，忠实地 | In a way that shows complete faithfulness and support to someone or something. | She served her country loyally for many years. 她忠诚地为国家服务多年。|  He loyally defended his friend against the accusations. 他忠诚地为朋友辩护，反驳那些指控。 | 近义词: faithfully, steadfastly, truly, devotedly | 反义词: disloyally, treacherously, faithlessly | 词性: 副词

词源:
From Middle English *lealliche*, from Old French *lealment*, from *leal* (“loyal”) + *-ment* (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *lēgālis* (“legal, lawful”).
