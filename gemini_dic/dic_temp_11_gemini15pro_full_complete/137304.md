nonsystemic:/ˌnɒn.sɪˈstɛm.ɪk/ /ˌnɑːn.sɪˈstɛm.ɪk/
adjective
非系统性的；局部性的
not systemic; not affecting the body generally
This cream is for nonsystemic use only, meaning it should only be applied to the affected area and not all over the body.  这种药膏仅供局部使用，这意味着它只能涂抹在患处，而不是全身。

The doctor prescribed a nonsystemic treatment for the rash, as it was localized to a small area. 医生为皮疹开了局部治疗的药，因为它仅限于一小块区域。

synonym: localized, topical
antonym: systemic, general, widespread
词性: adjective
