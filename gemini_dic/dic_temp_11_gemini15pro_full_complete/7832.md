antalkali:/ˌæntˈælkəlaɪ/ | n. | 抗碱剂 | A substance that neutralizes alkalis or reduces their alkalinity. |  The farmer used an antalkali to balance the pH of his soil.  农民使用抗碱剂来平衡土壤的pH值。| synonyms: acid, neutralizer | antonyms: alkali, base | noun

antalkali:/ˌæntˈælkəlaɪ/ | adj. | 抗碱的 | Having the properties of an antalkali; neutralizing alkalis. | The antalkali solution effectively neutralized the base. 抗碱溶液有效地中和了碱。| synonyms: acid-resisting, alkali-neutralizing | antonyms: alkaline, basic | adjective
