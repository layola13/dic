outbrawling:/ˌaʊtˈbrɔːlɪŋ/
verb
胜过争吵；在争吵中胜过
defeating someone by brawling; surpassing in brawling.
He outbrawled his opponent in the tavern. 他在酒馆里打败了他的对手。
近义词: outfighting, vanquishing, conquering
反义词: losing to, being defeated by
词性: Verb

outbrawl:/ˌaʊtˈbrɔːl/
verb
争吵；打架
to quarrel or fight noisily.
They outbrawled each other in the street. 他们在街上互相争吵。
近义词: brawl, fight, quarrel
反义词: concede, yield
词性: Verb


词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning surpassing or exceeding) + "brawl" (verb meaning to fight or quarrel noisily).  The word "brawl" originates from the Old French word "brailler" meaning to cry out or shout.
