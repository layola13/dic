establishers:/ɪˈstæblɪʃərz/
n.
建立者；创立者；创始人
People who establish something, such as an organization, business, or system.
The establishers of the new republic faced many challenges.  新共和国的建立者们面临着许多挑战。

The establishers of the company laid a solid foundation for future growth. 公司的创始人为未来的发展奠定了坚实的基础。

founders, creators, originators
destroyers, demolishers
noun
