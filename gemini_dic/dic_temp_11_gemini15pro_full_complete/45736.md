curvinerved: /ˌkɜːrvɪˈnɜːrvd/ | adj. | 曲脉的；弧脉的 | Having curved veins or nerves, as in a leaf. |  The botanist identified the plant by its curvinerved leaves. 植物学家通过其弧形的叶脉识别了这种植物。| Example: The curvinerved leaves of the hosta plant are one of its distinguishing features. 玉簪的弧形脉叶是其显著特征之一。| Synonym: arcuate,  campylodromous | Antonym: rectinerved | adjective
