词源:
From Middle English *dagged, past participle of daggen (“to cut into dags, jags”), from a Scandinavian source akin to Old Norse daggar (“dewlaps of animals”), Swedish dagg (“dew”), from Proto-Germanic *daggaz (“dew, point, drop”). More at dew.
