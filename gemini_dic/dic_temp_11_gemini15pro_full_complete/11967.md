arterial:/ɑːrˈtɪəriəl/ | adj. | 动脉的；干线的；像动脉的 | Relating to or resembling an artery; of or relating to main routes or channels. | The arterial blood supply to the brain is crucial.  大脑的动脉供血至关重要。 | The city's arterial roads are congested during rush hour. 城市的干线道路在高峰时段拥堵。 | synonyms: main, principal, central, key | antonyms: venous, capillary | adjective

arterial:/ɑːrˈtɪəriəl/ | n. |  干线公路；要道 | A major road or highway. |  The new arterial will ease traffic congestion. 这条新的干线公路将缓解交通拥堵。| The arterial is under construction. 这条要道正在建设中。 | synonyms: highway, freeway, expressway, thoroughfare | antonyms: back road, side street | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *arterialis, from Latin arteria (“windpipe, artery”) (ultimately from Ancient Greek ἀρτηρία (artēría, “windpipe, artery”)), + -alis.
