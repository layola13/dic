luck-draw: /ˈlʌk drɔː/ (US) /ˈlʌk drɔː/ (UK)| n.|抽奖，抽签|A method of randomly selecting a winner or winners from a group of participants, often involving drawing numbered tickets or names from a container. |The annual charity gala featured a luck-draw with some amazing prizes. 每年一度的慈善晚会都有抽奖活动，奖品非常丰厚。|The school held a luck-draw to raise money for new equipment. 学校举办抽奖活动为新设备筹集资金。|Synonyms: raffle, lottery, drawing, sweepstakes|Antonyms: selection, appointment|noun
