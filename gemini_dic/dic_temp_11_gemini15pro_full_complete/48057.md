declamation:/ˌdɛkləˈmeɪʃən/| n.|慷慨激昂的演说；雄辩；朗诵|A formal or theatrical speech delivered in a dramatic or impassioned style.|The actor's declamation of the famous soliloquy was breathtaking. 这位演员对著名独白的慷慨激昂的演说是惊人的。|近义词：oration, speech, address, recitation|反义词:understatement, murmur|词性:名词

declamation:/ˌdɛkləˈmeɪʃən/| n.|宣言；声明|A forceful or assertive statement or announcement.|The government issued a declamation condemning the violence. 政府发表声明谴责暴力。|近义词：pronouncement, proclamation, declaration|反义词:whisper, silence|词性:名词
