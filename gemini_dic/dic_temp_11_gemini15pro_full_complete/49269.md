deliberate:/dɪˈlɪbərət/ /dəˈlɪbəreɪt/ | adj. v. | 故意的；深思熟虑的；从容不迫的；审议；商讨 | done consciously and intentionally; slow and careful |  She made a deliberate attempt to provoke him. 她故意试图激怒他。 | synonyms: intentional, purposeful, conscious, planned, calculated | antonyms: accidental, unintentional, impulsive, reckless | adjective


deliberate:/dɪˈlɪbərət/ /dəˈlɪbəreɪt/ | v. | 仔细考虑；商议 | engage in long and careful consideration | The jury deliberated for five days before reaching a verdict. 陪审团商议了五天才作出裁决。 | synonyms: consider, contemplate, ponder, meditate, mull over | antonyms: disregard, ignore | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin  _dēlīberātus_, past participle of _dēlīberāre_  "to weigh well, consider," from _de- "down, thoroughly" + _lībra_ "balance, scales."
