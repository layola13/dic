hickwall:/ˈhɪkwɔːl/ | n. |乡巴佬; 土包子 |A derogatory term for someone from a rural area, often perceived as unsophisticated or simple-minded.  This word is considered offensive and should be avoided.|He called them a bunch of hickwalls, which angered the locals.|近义词:yokel, rube, hillbilly |反义词:cosmopolitan, sophisticate |词性:名词
