amiga:/əˈmiːɡə/ | n. | 女性朋友；闺蜜 (Spanish origin) | A female friend.  Used especially in Spanish-speaking contexts. |  She's my amiga from college. 她是我的大学闺蜜。 | My amiga and I went shopping yesterday. 我和我的闺蜜昨天去购物了。 | 近义词：friend, pal, mate, buddy (more general), compañera (Spanish - female companion) | 反义词：enemy, foe | 词性: noun
