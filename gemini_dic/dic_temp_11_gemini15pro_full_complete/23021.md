boulderer:/ˈboʊldərər/| n.|攀岩者；抱石攀登者|A person who engages in bouldering, a form of rock climbing performed on small rock formations or artificial climbing walls without the use of ropes or harnesses.|例句：The boulderer effortlessly scaled the challenging rock face.  这位攀岩者毫不费力地攀登了富有挑战性的岩壁。|例句：Experienced boulderers often develop incredible upper body strength.  经验丰富的攀岩者通常会发展出惊人的上肢力量。|近义词：climber, rock climber|反义词:|noun
