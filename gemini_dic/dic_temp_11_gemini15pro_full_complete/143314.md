outdwelled:/ˌaʊtˈdwɛld/
verb
居住得比...更久，比...活得更长
To live longer than; to outlive.  To survive or persist beyond (something).
The ancient oak tree outdwelled several generations of families who lived in its shade.  这棵古老的橡树比生活在它树荫下的几代家庭活得更久。
He outdwelled all his siblings and friends. 他比他所有的兄弟姐妹和朋友都活得长。
Synonyms: outlive, survive, outlast
Antonyms: predecease
词性: 动词
