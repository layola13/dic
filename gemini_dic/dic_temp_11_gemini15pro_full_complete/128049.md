nerite:/ˈneəraɪt/| n.|蝾螺；玛瑙螺；一种半化石|A small, brightly colored marine snail of warm seas, often with a patterned shell.|Nerites are common in tide pools. 蝾螺在潮池中很常见。|近义词：snail, periwinkle|反义词: (无明确的反义词)|noun
