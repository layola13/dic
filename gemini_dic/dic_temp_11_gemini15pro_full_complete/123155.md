monkey-wrencher:/ˈmʌŋkiˌrɛntʃər/ | n. | 扳手；障碍；破坏因素 | A tool with adjustable jaws for gripping and turning nuts and bolts; something that throws a plan or activity into confusion. | He used a monkey wrench to tighten the bolts. 他用扳手拧紧螺栓。|  The sudden rain threw a monkey wrench into our picnic plans. 突如其来的大雨破坏了我们的野餐计划。 | synonyms: wrench, spanner, obstacle, impediment | antonyms:  aid, assistance | noun
