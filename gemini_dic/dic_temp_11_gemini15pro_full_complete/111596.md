lobster:/ˈlɑːbstər/ (US), /ˈlɒbstər/ (UK)| n.|龙虾|A large marine crustacean with five pairs of legs, the first pair of which are modified into large claws, used for catching prey. It is highly valued as food.|例句：We had lobster for dinner last night. 昨晚我们晚餐吃了龙虾。

例句：The lobster was caught in a trap. 这只龙虾是在陷阱里捕获的。

近义词：crayfish, crawfish, langoustine|反义词:None (Animals don't typically have antonyms)|词性:Noun
