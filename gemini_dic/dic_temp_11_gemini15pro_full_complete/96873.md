inexistent:/ˌɪnɪɡˈzɪstənt/

不存在的；虚无的
Not existing; not real.

Example:  The rumored treasure was inexistent.  传说中的宝藏并不存在。

Synonym:  nonexistent, missing, absent, lacking
Antonym:  existent, existing, real, present
词性: adjective
