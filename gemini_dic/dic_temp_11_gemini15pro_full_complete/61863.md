emporetical:/ˌɛmpəˈrɛtɪkəl/| adj.|经验的，以经验为依据的|Based on observation or experience rather than theory or pure logic.|例句：Emporetical evidence suggests that regular exercise improves cardiovascular health. 经验表明，经常锻炼可以改善心血管健康。|例句：His emporetical approach to farming led to innovative techniques. 他务农的经验方法带来了一些创新技术。|近义词：experiential, observed, practical, pragmatic|反义词：theoretical, hypothetical, conjectural|词性:形容词

词源: From Greek empeirikos "experienced," from empeiria "experience," from en- "in" + peira "attempt, trial, experience."  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *per- "to try, risk."
