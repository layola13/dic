diaphanously:/daɪˈæfənəsli/
adj.
透明地；轻薄地；精致地
(especially of fabric) so light and fine that you can see through it.
Her diaphanous dress flowed in the breeze. 她的透明连衣裙在微风中飘动。

The curtains were diaphanous, allowing the sunlight to filter through. 窗帘很薄，阳光可以透过。

She wore a diaphanous veil that barely concealed her face. 她戴着一层薄纱，几乎遮不住她的脸。

近义词:transparent, sheer, translucent, gossamer
反义词:opaque, thick, dense
词性:adjective
词源: From Greek διαφανής (diaphanēs, “transparent”) +‎ -ly
