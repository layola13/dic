cqd:/siːkjuːˈdiː/ | n. |  Come quickly, distress. (旧式无线电求救信号，现已由SOS取代) |  An early distress signal used in wireless telegraphy, superseded by SOS.  It represents an abbreviation of the original text "All stations: distress."  While not technically an acronym, it is often interpreted as "Come Quick, Danger." |  The ship sent out a "CQD" before sinking.  这艘船在沉没前发出了“CQD”求救信号。 | 近义词: SOS, Mayday | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
