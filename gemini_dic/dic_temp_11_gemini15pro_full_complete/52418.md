didi:/ˈdiːdiː/| n.|滴滴（打车软件，也指打车服务）|A Chinese ride-hailing company, similar to Uber or Lyft.  It also refers to the act of using the Didi app to hail a ride.|例句：I took a didi to the airport. 我打滴滴去了机场。|例句：Let's call a didi, it's too far to walk.  我们叫个滴滴吧，走路太远了。|近义词：ride-hailing, taxi, cab|反义词:walk, cycle|noun
