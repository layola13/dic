delicately:/ˈdɛlɪkətli/| adv.|精细地，微妙地，谨慎地|In a delicate manner; with refinement, gentleness, or tact.  Requiring careful handling.|She delicately placed the antique vase on the shelf. 她小心翼翼地把古董花瓶放在架子上。|近义词：finely, subtly, gently, carefully, tactfully|反义词：roughly, clumsily, crudely, carelessly|词性:副词

词源: From the Late Latin *delicatus*, from Latin delicatus (“delightful, alluring, charming, dainty”), from Latin deliciae (“delight, pleasure”), from delicio (“allure, entice”), probably related to lacio (“entice, attract”).
