cleave /kliːv/ | v. | 劈开；粘住；坚持 | To split or divide by force; to adhere firmly; to remain faithful to. | The butcher cleaved the meat in two. 屠夫把肉劈成两半。 | 近义词：split, adhere, stick, cling | 反义词：separate, detach | 词性：动词

cleave /kliːv/ | v. | 粘住；坚持 | To adhere firmly; to remain faithful to. | He cleaved to his principles despite the pressure. 尽管压力很大，他仍然坚持自己的原则。 | 近义词：adhere, stick, cling, hold fast | 反义词：detach, abandon | 词性：动词

词源：Old English clēofan (to split, separate),  clīfan (to adhere); related to Old High German klioban (to split), klīban (to adhere).  The two meanings developed from the same root but diverged over time.
