infinitude:/ɪnˈfɪnɪtjuːd/ /ɪnˈfɪnɪtuːd/
n.
无限；无穷；无限大
The vastness of space suggests infinitude.  浩瀚的宇宙暗示着无限。
The infinitude of the universe is difficult to grasp. 宇宙的无限难以理解。
synonyms: immensity, boundlessness, eternity, vastness, limitlessness
antonyms: finitude, finiteness
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin infinitudo (“boundlessness”), from infinitus (“unbounded, infinite”), from in- (“not”) + finitus (“limited, bounded”).
