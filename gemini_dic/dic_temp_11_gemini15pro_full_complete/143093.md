outbreathed:/ˌaʊtˈbriːðd/
adjective
呼出的，吐出的
Exhaled, breathed out.  The outbreathed air was warm against the cold glass.  呼出的空气在冰冷的玻璃上感到温暖。
例句：His outbreathed words were barely audible. 他呼出的字几乎听不见。
例句：The dying man's outbreathed sigh was his last.  垂死之人呼出的叹息是他的最后一口气。
近义词：exhaled, expired
反义词：inhaled, inspired
词性：adjective


outbreathe:/ˌaʊtˈbriːð/
verb
呼出，吐出
To breathe out; exhale.  She outbreathed a sigh of relief. 她松了一口气。
例句：He outbreathed slowly, trying to calm his racing heart. 他慢慢地呼气，试图让狂跳的心平静下来。
例句：The dragon outbreathed a plume of smoke.  龙喷出一缕烟雾。
近义词：exhale, expire
反义词：inhale, inspire
词性：verb


词源：
From out- + breathe.  由out- + breathe构成。
