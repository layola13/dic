blagged:/blæɡd/
verb
骗取， 乞求；(英国非正式)成功做到；(英国非正式)胡扯；
persuade someone to give you something you want, typically by using clever and slightly dishonest methods. (British informal) manage to do or obtain something. (British informal) talk persuasively, especially at length and without substance.
I blagged a free ticket to the concert. 我骗取了一张免费的音乐会门票。
He blagged his way into the party. 他骗过了保安混进了派对。
Synonyms: wheedle, coax, cajole, wangle, finagle
Antonyms: earn, deserve, purchase
