micromanages:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈmænɪdʒɪz/
|v.|微观管理；事无巨细地管理|to control every part of a situation, including small details, often in a way that is annoying to other people|例句：He micromanages his team, which stifles their creativity. 他对团队进行微观管理，扼杀了他们的创造力。

例句：The new CEO is known for micromanaging, which has led to some employee dissatisfaction. 新任首席执行官以事无巨细的管理风格而闻名，这导致了一些员工的不满。
|近义词：oversee excessively, control tightly|反义词:delegate, empower|词性:动词
