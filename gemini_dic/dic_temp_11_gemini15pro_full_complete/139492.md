obstruct:/əbˈstrʌkt/
verb
阻碍; 妨碍; 阻塞
to block a road, passage, entrance, etc. so that nothing can go along it, or to prevent something from happening or making progress
They were charged with obstructing the police. 他们被指控妨碍警察执法。
近义词: block, hinder, impede, bar
反义词: aid, assist, facilitate, clear
词性: verb

词源: From Latin obstructus, past participle of obstruere "to build in front of, block up, stop up," from ob "against" (see ob-) + struere "to pile, build" (see structure (n.)).
