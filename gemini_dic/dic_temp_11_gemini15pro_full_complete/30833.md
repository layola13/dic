chab:/tʃæb/| n. |小子，小伙子（苏格兰俚语）|A young man, a lad, often used in a friendly or slightly condescending way. |例句：He's a good chab, always willing to help. 他是个好小伙，总是乐于助人。

例句：The chabs were playing football in the park. 小伙子们在公园里踢足球。|近义词：lad, fellow, guy, bloke (informal)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "old man" in a specific context) |词性: noun
