afterglows:/ˈæftərɡloʊz/
n.
余辉，晚霞；美好的回忆，幸福的余韵
a pleasant feeling produced after a good experience
The afterglow of the sunset was beautiful. 日落后的余晖很美。
The afterglow of their victory lasted for days. 胜利的喜悦持续了好几天。
近义词：aftermath, remnants, echoes
反义词：prelude, beginning, start
词性：名词
