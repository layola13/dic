nonemployments:/ˌnɒnɪmˈplɔɪmənts/
n.
失业，未就业人数
The state of being unemployed; the number or proportion of people who are unemployed.
The report highlighted the rising nonemployments among young people.  这份报告强调了年轻人失业率的上升。
Nonemployments figures are expected to increase next quarter. 预计下个季度失业人数将会增加。
近义词: unemployment, joblessness
反义词: employment
词性: 名词
