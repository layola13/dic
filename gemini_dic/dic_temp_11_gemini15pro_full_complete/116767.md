mausoleum:/ˌmɔːsəˈliːəm/ /ˌmɑːsəˈliːəm/| n. |陵墓；壮丽的坟墓|A large and impressive tomb, often a free-standing building. |例句：The king was buried in a grand mausoleum. 国王被安葬在一座宏伟的陵墓里。

例句：The Taj Mahal is perhaps the most famous mausoleum in the world. 泰姬陵或许是世界上最著名的陵墓。|近义词：tomb, sepulcher, crypt|反义词:None|词性:noun
