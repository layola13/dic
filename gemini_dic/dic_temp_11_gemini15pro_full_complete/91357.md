hydros:/ˈhaɪdrɒs/ (US) /ˈhaɪdrɒs/ (UK)| n.|水, 液体; 与水有关的 (前缀)|A prefix meaning "water," "liquid," or "hydrogen."  It appears in words like "hydroelectric" (water-powered electricity) and "hydrolysis" (chemical breakdown using water).|Hydroponics is a method of growing plants without soil, using mineral nutrient solutions in a water solvent. 水培法是一种无土栽培植物的方法，使用水溶液中的矿物质营养液。|近义词: aqua, hydr-|反义词: anhydro- (without water)|prefix
