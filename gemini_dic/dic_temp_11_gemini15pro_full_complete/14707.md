awardability:/əˌwɔːrdəˈbɪləti/
n.
可授予性；可奖励性
The quality of being eligible or suitable for an award.
The awardability of the contract was questioned due to irregularities in the bidding process.  由于招标过程中存在违规行为，该合同的可授予性受到质疑。
The committee assessed the awardability of the research grants based on the applicants' qualifications and the feasibility of their proposals. 委员会根据申请人的资格和提案的可行性评估了研究经费的可授予性。
Synonyms: eligibility, suitability, merit
Antonyms: ineligibility, unsuitability
