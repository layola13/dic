grandee:/ɡrænˈdiː/
n.
大人物，显贵，贵族
a person of high rank, especially in Spain or Portugal
The Spanish grandees were powerful landowners. 西班牙的显贵是强大的地主。

例句：
He lived like a grandee. 他过着像贵族一样的生活。

He considered himself a grandee among the town's citizens. 他认为自己是镇上的大人物。


近义词：
nobleman, aristocrat, lord, magnate

反义词：
commoner, peasant

词性
noun
