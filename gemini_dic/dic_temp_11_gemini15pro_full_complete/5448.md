almsgiving: /ˈɑːmzˌɡɪvɪŋ/ | n. |施舍，布施|The act of giving money or food to poor people. |例句：Almsgiving is a common practice in many religions.  布施在很多宗教中是一种常见的做法。

例句：The church encouraged almsgiving to the less fortunate. 教会鼓励向不幸的人施舍。

近义词：charity, donation, philanthropy|反义词：stinginess, miserliness|词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ælmessgyfu (unattested), from ælmesse (“alms”) + gyfu (“gift”).  The word "alms" itself comes from Old English ælmesse, from Late Latin eleemosyna, from Greek ἐλεημοσύνη (eleēmosynē, “pity, alms”), from ἐλεήμων (eleēmōn, “compassionate”).
