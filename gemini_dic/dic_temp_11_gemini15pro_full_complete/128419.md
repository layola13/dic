newfanglist: /ˈnjuːˌfæŋɡlɪst/ | n. | 新事物追捧者；赶时髦的人 | A person who is enthusiastic about new things, especially gadgets and technology. |  He's a real newfanglist, always buying the latest gadgets. 他是个真正的赶时髦的人，总是买最新的电子产品。 | synonyms: innovator, early adopter, trendsetter, faddist | antonyms: traditionalist, Luddite, conservative | noun

Etymology:
From "newfangled" + "-ist". "Newfangled" dates back to the late 16th century and means "newly fashioned or invented," often with a connotation of disapproval.  It's formed from "new" + "fangle," meaning "contrivance" or "gadget," which is now obsolete.  The suffix "-ist" denotes a person who practices or is devoted to something.
