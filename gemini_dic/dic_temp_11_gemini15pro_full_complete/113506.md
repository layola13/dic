m-commerce:/ˈɛmˌkɑːmɜːrs/ (US), /ˈɛmˌkɒmɜːs/ (UK)| n. |移动商务|Electronic commerce conducted on mobile phones or other wireless handheld devices.|Example: M-commerce is rapidly growing in popularity due to the increasing use of smartphones. 由于智能手机的日益普及，移动商务正在迅速发展。|Example: Many businesses are investing in m-commerce platforms to reach a wider audience. 许多企业正在投资移动商务平台，以接触更广泛的受众。|Synonym: mobile commerce, wireless commerce|Antonym: e-commerce (in a broader sense, as m-commerce is a subset of e-commerce)|Noun
