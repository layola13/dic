clearheaded:/ˌklɪrˈhɛdɪd/| adj.|头脑清醒的；冷静的；清醒的|Having or showing an ability to think clearly, especially in a difficult situation.|例句：She remained clearheaded throughout the crisis.  她在整个危机中保持头脑清醒。

例句：He made a series of clearheaded decisions. 他做了一系列头脑清醒的决定。|近义词：lucid, rational, coherent, levelheaded, composed|反义词：confused, muddled, bewildered, disoriented, befuddled|词性:形容词
