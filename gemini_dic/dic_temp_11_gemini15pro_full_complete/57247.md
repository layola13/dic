downlink:/ˈdaʊnlɪŋk/| n. |下行链路|A communications link used to transmit signals from a satellite or other space station to a ground station.  The transmission path from a satellite to an earth station.|The satellite experienced a downlink failure, cutting off communication. 卫星下行链路出现故障，通信中断。|Synonyms: down-link, down stream, down channel|Antonyms: uplink|noun


downlink:/ˈdaʊnlɪŋk/| v. |下行传输|To transmit (data) from a satellite or other space station to a ground station.|The team downlinked the latest images from the space telescope. 团队下行了太空望远镜的最新图像。|Synonyms: transmit, send, relay|Antonyms: uplink|verb
