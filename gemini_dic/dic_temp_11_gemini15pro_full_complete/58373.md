dualistic:/ˌduːəˈlɪstɪk/ |adj.|二元的，二元论的|Characterized by or consisting of two elements or aspects; of or relating to dualism.|例句：Many religions have a dualistic view of good and evil. 很多宗教都持有善恶二元论的观点。

例句：The novel presents a dualistic view of human nature, exploring both the capacity for great love and the potential for immense cruelty. 这部小说呈现了人性的二元观，探讨了既有伟大的爱的能力，也有巨大残忍的潜力。|近义词：binary, dichotomous|反义词：monistic, unified|词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dualisticus, from dualis ("dual") + -istic.  Dualis comes from Latin duo ("two").
