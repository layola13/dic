asker's:/ˈæskərz/| possessive pronoun|提问者的，提问人的|Belonging to or associated with the person who asks a question.  It is the possessive form of "asker".|This is the asker's right to know the answer. 这是提问者知道答案的权利。|The asker's curiosity was piqued. 提问者的好奇心被激起了。|近义词：inquirer's, questioner's|反义词: answerer's, respondent's|词性:pronoun


词源: From "asker" + "'s" (possessive suffix). "Asker" comes from the verb "ask," which has Old English origins.
