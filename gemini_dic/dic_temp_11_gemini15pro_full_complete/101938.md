islets:/ˈaɪləts/ /ˈaɪlɪts/| n.|小岛;胰岛|A small island.  Also, a cluster of specialized cells in the pancreas that produce hormones such as insulin. |例句：The islets were too small to be inhabited. 这些小岛太小了，不适合居住。

例句：The islets of Langerhans secrete insulin.  朗格汉斯胰岛分泌胰岛素。|近义词：islands, isles|反义词:continent, mainland|noun
