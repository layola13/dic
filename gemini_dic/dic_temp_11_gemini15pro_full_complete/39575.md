conscriptable:/kənˈskrɪptəbl/ | adj. | 可征召的，可被征入伍的 |  Able to be conscripted; liable or eligible for conscription.  This describes someone who meets the requirements for being drafted into military service. | Example 1:  During wartime, all able-bodied men between 18 and 35 were conscriptable.  (战争期间，所有18至35岁的体壮男子都可以被征召入伍。)

Example 2:  He knew he was conscriptable and began training in anticipation. (他知道自己可能被征召入伍，于是开始提前进行训练。) | Synonyms:  draftable, eligible, liable | Antonyms: exempt, ineligible | word type: adjective
