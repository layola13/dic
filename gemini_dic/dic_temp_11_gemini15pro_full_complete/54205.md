disfavorer:/dɪsˈfeɪvərər/
noun
不喜欢的人；反对者
One who disfavors someone or something; an opponent.
The disfavorer of the new policy voiced his concerns at the town hall meeting.  这个新政策的反对者在市政厅会议上表达了他的担忧。
The king’s disfavorer was banished from the kingdom. 国王的反对者被驱逐出王国。

近义词: opponent, antagonist, critic, detractor
反义词: supporter, proponent, advocate, champion
词性: n.
