nonforensic:/ˌnɒn.fəˈrɛnsɪk/ | adj. | 非司法鉴定/非取证的 | Not related to or used in legal proceedings or criminal investigations.  Relating to something that is not used for the purpose of legal evidence. | The committee conducted a nonforensic audit of the company's finances. 委员会对公司的财务进行了非司法审计。 | The psychologist provided a nonforensic evaluation of the child's development. 心理学家对孩子的发展进行了非司法鉴定评估。 |  Near synonyms: nonlegal, extrajudicial | Antonyms: forensic, legal | adjective
