annualized: /ˈænjuəlaɪzd/
adjective
年度化的；按年计算的
Calculated or expressed as a rate for a year.
The annualized return on this investment is 10%. 这项投资的年化回报率为 10%。
annual, yearly
N/A
词性: adjective

annualized: /ˈænjuəlaɪzd/
verb
按年计算
To calculate or adjust (something, such as an amount or rate) to reflect a yearly rate or total.
We can annualize the figures to get a clearer picture of the long-term trend. 我们可以将这些数字年化，以便更清楚地了解长期趋势。
calculate yearly, compute annually
