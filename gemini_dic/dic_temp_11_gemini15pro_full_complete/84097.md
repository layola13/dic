half-instinctively:/ˌhɑːf ɪnˈstɪŋktɪvli/

例句：She half-instinctively ducked when she heard the loud bang. 她听到巨响后本能地低下头。|近义词：automatically, reflexively, spontaneously|反义词：consciously, deliberately|词性:副词
