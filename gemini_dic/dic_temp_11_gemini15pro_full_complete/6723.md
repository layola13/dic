analogue:/ˈænəlɒɡ (UK), ˈænəlɔːɡ (US)| n. adj.|类似物；模拟的|Something that is similar to something else, especially in function or purpose.  Relating to or using signals or information represented by a continuously variable physical quantity such as spatial position or voltage.|例句：The clock face is an analogue of the Earth and sun. 时钟表面是地球和太阳的类似物。|近义词：equivalent, parallel, similar, counterpart|反义词：digital|noun, adjective

analogue:/ˈænəlɒɡ (UK), ˈænəlɔːɡ (US)| n. adj.|模拟的|Relating to a device or system that represents changing values as continuously variable physical quantities.|例句：This is an analogue recording, not digital. 这是一个模拟录音，而不是数字的。|近义词：non-digital|反义词：digital|noun, adjective
