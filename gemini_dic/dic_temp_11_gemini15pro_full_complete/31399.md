chargeful:/ˈtʃɑːrdʒfʊl/
adjective
充满的，饱含的
filled with a specified feeling or quality.
The atmosphere was chargeful with excitement.  气氛充满了兴奋。
The courtroom was chargeful with tension as the jury delivered its verdict. 当陪审团宣读判决时，法庭上充满了紧张气氛。
近义词：fraught, filled, laden, imbued
反义词：empty, devoid, lacking
词性: adjective

词源：
From "charge" (meaning "load, burden, fill") + "-ful" (a suffix meaning "full of").  The word "charge" ultimately comes from the Old French "chargier" (to load).
