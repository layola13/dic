overhastinesses: /ˌoʊvərˈheɪstɪnəsɪz/
|n.|
|过度仓促，轻率|
|The state of being excessively hasty; undue haste or rashness.  A plural form of overhastiness.|
|His overhastinesses in making decisions often led to mistakes. 他在做决定时的过度仓促经常导致错误。|
|The many overhastinesses of youth are often regretted in later life. 年轻时诸多轻率之举往往在日后令人后悔。|
|rashness, impetuosity, precipitateness, hurry, haste|
|caution, deliberation, carefulness, prudence|
|noun|
