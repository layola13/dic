overpotential:/ˌoʊvərˈpəˈtɛnʃəl/| n. |过电位，超电势|The additional voltage required beyond the thermodynamically determined value to cause a reaction to occur at a desired rate.  It represents the extra energy needed to overcome kinetic barriers in an electrochemical reaction.|The overpotential for hydrogen evolution is quite high on mercury electrodes.  汞电极上析氢的过电位相当高。|Synonyms: overvoltage|Antonyms: N/A|noun
