crossbreed:/ˈkrɒsbriːd/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsbriːd/ (US)| n. v. adj.|杂交品种，杂交，杂交的|A crossbreed is an animal or plant that has parents of different breeds or varieties.  To crossbreed is to produce a crossbreed by mating or hybridizing different breeds or varieties.  Crossbreed can also be used as an adjective to describe something that is a product of crossbreeding.|例句：The dog is a crossbreed between a Labrador and a German Shepherd. 这只狗是拉布拉多犬和德国牧羊犬的杂交品种。|近义词：hybrid, mongrel (for animals)|反义词:purebred, pedigree|词性:名词,动词,形容词

crossbreed:/ˈkrɒsbriːd/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsbriːd/ (US)| v. |杂交，使杂交|To produce (an animal or plant) by mating or hybridizing two different breeds or varieties.|例句：They crossbred different varieties of corn to develop a more disease-resistant strain.  他们将不同品种的玉米杂交，以培育出更具抗病性的品种。|近义词：hybridize, interbreed|反义词:purebreed|词性:动词

crossbreed:/ˈkrɒsbriːd/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsbriːd/ (US)| adj. |杂交的|Produced by crossbreeding.|例句：The farmer raises crossbreed cattle. 这位农民饲养杂交牛。|近义词：hybrid, mongrel (for animals)|反义词:purebred, pedigree|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From cross + breed.  "Cross" in this context refers to mixing different kinds or breeds. "Breed" refers to a particular stock of animals or plants within a species.
