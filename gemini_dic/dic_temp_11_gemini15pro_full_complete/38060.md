compositae:/kəmˈpɒzɪtiː/
n.
菊科
A large family of flowering plants, also called Asteraceae, characterized by flower heads composed of many small florets.
Sunflowers and daisies belong to the Compositae family.  向日葵和雏菊属于菊科。
synonyms: Asteraceae
antonyms: (No direct antonym for a taxonomic family)
