keratonyxis:/ˌkɛrəˈtɒnɪksɪs/
noun
角膜穿刺术
Surgical puncture of the cornea.
Keratonyxis is sometimes performed to drain fluid from the cornea. 角膜穿刺术有时用于排出角膜内的液体。
Keratonyxis may be used to treat certain corneal conditions. 角膜穿刺术可以用来治疗某些角膜疾病。
Synonym:corneal puncture
Antonym: None (This is a surgical procedure; a direct antonym doesn't exist.)
词性: noun
