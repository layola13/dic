echidna:/ɪˈkɪdnə/ | n. |针鼹，刺猬|A spiny insectivorous mammal native to Australia and New Guinea, with a long snout and claws for digging.  It lays eggs and has a pouch.|例句：The echidna uses its long, sticky tongue to catch ants and termites. 针鼹用它长而粘的舌头捕捉蚂蚁和白蚁。

例句：Echidnas are one of the few mammals that lay eggs. 针鼹是少数几种产卵的哺乳动物之一。|近义词：spiny anteater|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

echidna:/ɪˈkɪdnə/ | n. | (Greek Mythology)  A monster, half woman, half serpent.|  In Greek mythology, Echidna was a monstrous creature, half-woman and half-snake, who mothered many of the most famous monsters. | 例句：In Greek mythology, the Chimera was said to be the offspring of Echidna. 在希腊神话中，据说奇美拉是厄喀德那的后代。

例句：Echidna's lair was a dark cave deep within the earth. 厄喀德那的巢穴是大地深处的一个黑暗洞穴。 | 近义词：monster, she-dragon | 反义词: N/A | 词性:noun
