d'oh:/doʊ/ (US), /dəʊ/ (UK)| int. | 唉！; 咄！| An exclamation expressing frustration, annoyance, realization of a mistake, or similar. Originally used by the character Homer Simpson in the television series *The Simpsons*.| Example: D'oh! I forgot my keys! 唉！我忘了带钥匙了！| Example: He hit his thumb with the hammer and yelled, "D'oh!" 他用锤子砸到了自己的拇指，大叫一声“咄！”| 近义词: oops, damn, shoot, rats | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for an exclamation of frustration) | 词性: interjection
