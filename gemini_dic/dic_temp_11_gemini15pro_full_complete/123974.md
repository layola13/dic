morline: /ˈmɔːrlaɪn/ | n. |  锚泊线，系泊索 | A line used for mooring a vessel. | The ship was secured to the buoy with a strong morline.  这艘船用一根结实的锚泊线固定在浮标上。 | 近义词：mooring line, anchor line, hawser | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源:  "Morline" comes from the Dutch word "meerlijn," meaning "sea line."  It combines "meer" (sea) and "lijn" (line).
