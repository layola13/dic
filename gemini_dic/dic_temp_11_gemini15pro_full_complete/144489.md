outthundered:/ˌaʊtˈθʌndərd/
verb
以更大的雷声压倒；盖过…的雷声
to surpass in loudness of thunder; to drown out with a louder sound of thunder
The rock band outthundered the fireworks display.  摇滚乐队的音量盖过了烟火表演的声音。
Synonyms: outshout, drown out, overpower
Antonyms:  be drowned out, be subdued
词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + thunder + -ed.  "Out-" acts as a prefix indicating surpassing or exceeding, combined with "thunder" referring to the loud sound, and "-ed" indicating the past tense form of the verb.
