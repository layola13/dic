overhanging:/ˌoʊvərˈhæŋɪŋ/
adjective
悬垂的，突出的
extending or projecting beyond a supporting base or edge.
The overhanging cliff threatened to collapse. 悬崖峭壁岌岌可危，好像要崩塌。
The house has an overhanging roof. 这房子有一个悬垂的屋顶。
synonyms: projecting, protruding, jutting,  overarching
antonyms: recessed, sunken, indented
词性: 形容词

overhang:/ˈoʊvərhæŋ/
noun
悬垂物，突出物
something that overhangs; a projection.
The overhang of the roof provided shade. 屋檐提供了阴凉。
The rock overhang provided shelter from the rain. 突出的岩石提供了避雨的场所。
synonyms: projection, protrusion, overhang
antonyms: recess, indentation
词性: 名词

overhang:/ˌoʊvərˈhæŋ/
verb
悬垂，突出，逼近
project, protrude, or impend over.
Dark clouds overhang the city.  乌云笼罩着城市。
The threat of war overhangs the region. 战争的威胁笼罩着这个地区。
synonyms: project, protrude, loom, impend, menace
antonyms: recede, withdraw
词性: 动词
