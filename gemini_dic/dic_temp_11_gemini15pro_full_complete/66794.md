expand:/ɪkˈspænd/ /ɪkˈspænd/ | v. | 扩张，扩大，扩展；展开，打开；详述，详细阐明 | To increase in size, scope, or extent; to spread out; to elaborate or discuss in detail. | The company plans to expand its operations overseas.  这家公司计划将其业务扩展到海外。 | synonyms: enlarge, grow, develop, broaden, widen, stretch, unfold, elaborate, expatiate | antonyms: contract, shrink, reduce, condense, compress | verb

expand:/ɪkˈspænd/ /ɪkˈspænd/ | n. | 扩张，扩大 | An act of expanding or the state of being expanded. |  The rapid expansion of the city led to housing shortages. 城市的快速扩张导致住房短缺。| synonyms: enlargement, growth, development, broadening | antonyms: contraction, shrinkage, reduction, compression | noun
