economize:/ɪˈkɒnəmaɪz/ (UK), /iˈkɑːnəmaɪz/ (US)| v. | 节约，节省；经济地使用 | To reduce expenditure; to avoid waste or extravagance. |  例句：The company is economizing by reducing staff. 公司正在通过裁员来节省开支。| 近义词：save, conserve, cut back, retrench | 反义词：waste, squander, splurge | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French économiser, from économie ("economy"), from Ancient Greek οἰκονομία (oikonomía, "household management"), from οἶκος (oîkos, "house") + νέμω (némō, "I manage, distribute").
