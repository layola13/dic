drunkennesses:/ˈdrʌŋkənɪsɪz/| n. |醉酒，醉态|Instances of being drunk; habitual intoxication.|His frequent drunkennesses caused concern among his family. 他的频繁醉酒令家人担忧。|The city saw a surge in drunkennesses during the festival. 节日期间，该市的醉酒人数激增。|近义词：intoxication, inebriation|反义词：sobriety|词性:noun

Etymology: From Middle English *drunkennesse, from Old English *druncennes, from Proto-Germanic *drunkiznassus. Equivalent to drunken +‎ -ness.
