non-cooperate:/ˌnɒn kəʊˈɒpəreɪt/ | v. | 不合作 | To refuse to work together or help someone; to fail to act in a way that achieves a common goal. | The witness refused to non-cooperate with the police investigation. 该证人不配合警方的调查。|  The children were non-cooperating during bedtime. 孩子们在睡觉时间不合作。| Synonyms: obstruct, hinder, resist, oppose | Antonyms: cooperate, collaborate, assist, support | Verb

non-cooperating:/ˌnɒn kəʊˈɒpəreɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 不合作的 | Not willing to work together or help someone. | The non-cooperating witness made the investigation difficult.  不合作的证人使调查变得困难。|  He was being non-cooperating and refused to answer any questions. 他很不配合，拒绝回答任何问题。| Synonyms: uncooperative, obstructive, unhelpful, resistant | Antonyms: cooperative, helpful, collaborative, supportive | Adjective

non-cooperation:/ˌnɒn kəʊˌɒpəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 不合作 | The act of refusing to work together or help someone. | The government faced non-cooperation from the public. 政府面临公众的不合作。| The project failed due to the non-cooperation of several team members.  由于几个团队成员的不合作，该项目失败了。| Synonyms: resistance, obstruction, opposition, defiance | Antonyms: cooperation, collaboration, assistance, support | Noun
