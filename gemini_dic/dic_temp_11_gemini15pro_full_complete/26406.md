bye-byes:/ˈbaɪ baɪz/
n.|再见（儿语）, 告别（儿语）|An informal, often childish way of saying goodbye.|例句：The toddler waved bye-byes to his mother.  那个幼儿向他的妈妈挥挥手说再见。|例句：She gave her teddy bear a hug and said bye-byes. 她拥抱了她的泰迪熊，说了再见。|近义词：goodbye, farewell|反义词: hello, greetings|词性:名词

词源: Reduplication of bye-bye, itself a reduplication of bye, a shortening of goodbye. Ultimately from God be with ye.
