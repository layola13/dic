n.
驱逐学; 驱逐术
The study or practice of eviction.  This word refers to the methods and strategies used in the process of evicting someone from a property.
Example: The landlord studied evectics to ensure a legal and efficient removal of the tenant. 房东研究了驱逐术，以确保合法有效地驱逐房客。

Example: He became an expert in evectics after dealing with numerous difficult tenants.  在处理了众多难缠的房客后，他成为了驱逐方面的专家。

近义词: removal, expulsion, ousting
反义词: admittance, acceptance, welcoming
词性: noun
