construing:/kənˈstruːɪŋ/
v.
解释，理解，分析
interpreting or understanding the meaning of something, typically a statement or an action
He construed her silence as agreement. 他把她的沉默理解为同意。
interpret, understand, analyze, explain, decode, decipher
misinterpret, misunderstand, misconstrued
verb
