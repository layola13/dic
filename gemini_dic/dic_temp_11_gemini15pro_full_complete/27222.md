callused: /ˈkæləst/ (US) /ˈkæləsɪd/ (UK)| adj.|长满老茧的；冷淡无情的|Having calluses; emotionally hardened.|His callused hands showed years of hard labor.  他那长满老茧的双手表明他多年辛勤劳作。|The nurse had become callused to the suffering of her patients.  这位护士对病人的痛苦已经变得麻木不仁了。|近义词：hardened, toughened, insensitive, unfeeling|反义词：soft, tender, sensitive, compassionate|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *callōsus*, from callus ("hard skin").
