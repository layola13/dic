jacketing:/ˈdʒækɪtɪŋ/
n.
夹克材料;套管;护套;封套
The protective covering of a cable, pipe, etc.
例句：The jacketing on the pipes needs to be replaced.  这些管道上的护套需要更换。

例句：They offer a wide range of jacketing materials for different applications.  他们提供各种用于不同用途的护套材料。

近义词：covering, cladding, casing, sheathing
反义词：(No direct antonym, consider using terms like "core" or "interior" depending on the context)
词性: noun
