mimetite:/ˈmɪmɪtaɪt/
n.
砷铅矿, 铅砷矿, 拟晶矿
A mineral consisting of lead arsenate chloride, typically occurring as yellow, orange, brown, or white crystals.
Mimetite is a common secondary mineral in lead deposits. 砷铅矿是铅矿床中常见的次生矿物。
Mimetite can be mistaken for pyromorphite due to its similar crystal habit. 砷铅矿因其相似的晶体习性而可能被误认为是磷氯铅矿。
synonym:campylite
antonym:N/A
noun
