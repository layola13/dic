freeboots:/ˈfriːbuːts/
n.
自由海盗，掠夺者
A pirate, especially one who plundered Spanish ships and settlements in the West Indies during the 17th century.
The freeboots sailed the Caribbean, searching for Spanish galleons laden with gold.  自由海盗航行在加勒比海，寻找满载黄金的西班牙大帆船。
The freeboots plundered the coastal towns, taking anything of value. 自由海盗掠夺了沿海城镇，拿走了所有值钱的东西。
近义词：pirates, buccaneers, privateers
反义词：sailors, merchants
