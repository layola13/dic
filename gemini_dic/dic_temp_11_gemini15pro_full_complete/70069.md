feudalists:/ˈfjuːdəlɪsts/| n.|封建主；封建主义者|People who supported or benefited from feudalism, a social system in the Middle Ages where land ownership and power were hierarchical.|The feudalists controlled vast tracts of land and demanded loyalty from their vassals.|近义词：lords, nobles, aristocracy|反义词：peasants, serfs|Noun
