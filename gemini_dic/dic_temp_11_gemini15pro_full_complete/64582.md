erubescency:/ˌɛrʊˈbɛsənsɪ/ /ˌɪrʊˈbɛsənsɪ/
n.
脸红；羞愧
The act of blushing; a blush.
例句：Her erubescency betrayed her embarrassment. 她的脸红暴露了她的尴尬。

例句：He felt a wave of erubescency wash over him when he realized his mistake.  当他意识到自己的错误时，一股潮红涌上他的脸庞。

近义词：blush, reddening, flush
反义词：pallor, paleness
词性：名词
