neuronal:/nʊˈrəʊnəl/ /nʊˈroʊnəl/| adj.|神经元的，神经系统的|Relating to a neuron or neurons; of or relating to the nervous system.|例句：Neuronal activity increases during REM sleep.  快速眼动睡眠期间神经元活动增加。

例句：The researchers studied the neuronal pathways involved in pain perception. 研究人员研究了与疼痛感知相关的神经通路。|近义词：nerve, neural|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective

Etymology: From neuron + -al.  "Neuron" comes from the Greek "neuron" meaning "nerve."
