flattering:/ˈflætərɪŋ/| adj.|奉承的；讨好的；使人显得更漂亮的|Making someone appear or feel more attractive or successful than they really are. |That dress is very flattering on you. 那件衣服你穿很漂亮。|近义词：complimentary, praising, enhancing|反义词：unflattering, critical|adjective

flattering:/ˈflætərɪŋ/| adj.|（比率或统计数据）出乎意料地好；令人惊喜的|(of a ratio or statistic) surprisingly good; giving a misleadingly favorable impression. |The unemployment figures paint a flattering picture of the state of the economy. 失业率数据描绘了一幅过于乐观的经济状况图。|近义词：deceptive, misleading, specious|反义词：accurate, realistic, truthful|adjective
