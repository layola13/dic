charterage:/ˈtʃɑːrtərɪdʒ/| n.|租船费，包租费|The charterage for the oil tanker was exorbitant due to the high demand.  油轮的租船费由于需求旺盛而过高。|The company paid a hefty charterage to transport the goods overseas.  该公司支付了巨额租船费将货物运往海外。|Synonyms: freight, hire, rental|Antonyms: N/A|noun

Etymology->
From "charter" (meaning to hire or lease) + "-age" (a suffix forming nouns denoting a charge or fee).  Ultimately from Latin "charta" (paper, document).
