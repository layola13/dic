juts:/dʒʌts/| n. |突出物，伸出物|Something that juts out.|The jut of the cliff offered a breathtaking view. 悬崖的突出部分提供了令人叹为观止的景色。|projection, protrusion, overhang|recess, indentation|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *jutten, likely frequentative of jetten (“to throw, cast”), from Old North French jetter (“to throw”). Ultimately from Latin jactare (“to throw, hurl”).
