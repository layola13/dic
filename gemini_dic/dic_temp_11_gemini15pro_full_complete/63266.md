enthean:/ɛnˈθiːən/
adjective
受神灵启示的;狂热的;出神的
Inspired by a god; divinely inspired; frenzied or ecstatic.
The poet's enthean vision resulted in a powerful and moving work. 这位诗人受神灵启示的幻象成就了一部充满力量和感人的作品。
近义词:inspired, ecstatic, rapturous, divine, oracular
反义词:mundane, ordinary, prosaic
词性:adjective
词源: From Greek "entheos" (possessed by a god), from "en" (in) + "theos" (god).
