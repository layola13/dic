choragus: /kɔːˈreɪɡəs/ /kəˈreɪɡəs/ | n. | 合唱队领队；戏剧节赞助人 | A wealthy Athenian citizen who financed and supervised the training and costuming of a chorus for dramatic performances, especially at festivals. |  The choregus spared no expense in ensuring his chorus was the best dressed.  这位合唱队领队不惜一切代价确保他的合唱队着装最佳。

The choregus was responsible for selecting the chorus members. 合唱队领队负责挑选合唱队员。 | 近义词: sponsor, patron, backer | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek khorēgos, from khoros (chorus) + agein (to lead).
