caliduct:/ˈkælɪdʌkt/| n. |热导管，输热管|A pipe or channel for conveying heat, especially in a heating or cooling system. |The architect designed a caliduct system to distribute heat evenly throughout the building. 建筑师设计了一个热导管系统，以便将热量均匀地分布到整个建筑物中。|Example: The caliduct failed, causing the greenhouse to freeze in winter.  热导管失效了，导致温室在冬天结冰。|Synonyms: heat pipe, heat conduit, thermal conduit|Antonyms: coolant pipe, cooling duct|noun
