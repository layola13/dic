irremovability:/ˌɪrɪˈmuːvəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可移动性; 不可撤销性; 无法移除;
The quality or state of being irremovable; inability to be removed.
The irremovability of the judge is essential for a fair trial.  法官的不可撤销性对公平审判至关重要。
The irremovability of the stain frustrated her. 污渍无法去除让她很沮丧。

近义词: permanence, fixedness, immutability, indestructability
反义词: removability, movability, displaceability

词性: noun

词源:  From ir- (not) + removable + -ity (noun suffix forming abstract nouns).  Removable comes from remove (from Old French remover, from Latin removere, from re- (back, away) + movere (to move)).
