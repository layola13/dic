capuccio:/kəˈpuːtʃoʊ/ (US), /kæˈpuːtʃiːoʊ/ (UK)| n.|卡布奇诺|A coffee beverage consisting of espresso and steamed milk, often topped with foamed milk.|例句：I ordered a cappuccino with extra foam. 我点了一杯加奶泡的卡布奇诺。

例句：She enjoys a cappuccino every morning before work. 她每天早上上班前都喜欢喝一杯卡布奇诺。|近义词：latte, macchiato|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps espresso as a contrasting simpler beverage)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *cappuccino*, literally "little hood, Capuchin", from *cappuccio* ("hood"), diminutive of *cappa* ("cape, hooded cloak").  Named after the resemblance of the drink's color to the brown robes worn by Capuchin monks.
