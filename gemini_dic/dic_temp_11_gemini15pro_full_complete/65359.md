eulogized:/ˈjuːləˌdʒaɪzd/
verb
颂扬；赞美
praised highly in speech or writing, especially in a formal eulogy.
He eulogized the former president as a great leader. 他赞扬前任总统是一位伟大的领袖。
The professor was eulogized for his contributions to science.  这位教授因其对科学的贡献而受到赞扬。
praised, extolled, lauded, acclaimed
criticized, condemned, denigrated
verb
