overenthusiastic:/ˌoʊvərɪnˌθuːziˈæstɪk/ | adj. | 过分热情的；过于积极的 | Excessively enthusiastic; too eager or zealous. | She was a bit overenthusiastic in her welcome, hugging everyone even those she'd just met. 她表示欢迎的方式有点过分热情，拥抱了每一个人，甚至包括那些她刚认识的人。

The overenthusiastic salesman nearly scared the customer away. 过于热情的推销员几乎把顾客吓跑了。

近义词: exuberant, fanatical, zealous, passionate | 反义词: apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic | 词性: 形容词
