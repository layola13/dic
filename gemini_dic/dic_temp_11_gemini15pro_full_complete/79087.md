glaciate:/ˈɡleɪʃiˌeɪt/ /ˈɡleɪsiət/| adj. |冰川的；冰河时代的|Relating to or resembling a glacier; produced by glacial action; marked by the presence of glaciers.|The glaciated landscape is breathtaking. 冰川化的景色令人叹为观止。|近义词：glacial, icy, frozen|反义词:tropical, warm|词性:形容词
