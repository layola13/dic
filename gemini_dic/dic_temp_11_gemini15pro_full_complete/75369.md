frow:/fraʊ/
noun
皱眉；蹙额
A facial expression of displeasure or concentration characterized by furrowing one's brow.
She gave a slight frow of concentration as she tried to solve the puzzle. 她试图解开谜题时，眉头微微皱了一下。
近义词:scowl, glare, grimace
反义词:smile, grin
