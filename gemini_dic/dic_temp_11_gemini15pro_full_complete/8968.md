antihypochondriac:/ˌæntaɪˌhaɪpəˈkɒndriæk/| adj. n.|不患疑病症的，不疑病的（人）|Someone who is antihypochondriac does not worry excessively about their health, and may even neglect it. |例句：He's so antihypochondriac he never goes to the doctor. 他非常不担心自己的健康，从来不去看医生。

例句：She has an antihypochondriac attitude, often dismissing real symptoms. 她有一种不疑病的态度，经常忽视真正的症状。|近义词：carefree, unconcerned, stoic|反义词:hypochondriac, neurotic, anxious|词性:adjective, noun
