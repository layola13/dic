confucianist: /kənˈfjuːʃənɪst/ | n. adj. | 儒家学者；儒家的；| A follower of the teachings of Confucius; relating to Confucianism. |  Confucius was a great thinker and teacher, and many people today still consider themselves Confucianists. 孔子是一位伟大的思想家和教育家，今天仍然有许多人自认为是儒家学者。

Confucianist principles emphasize the importance of family and social harmony. 儒家原则强调家庭和社会和谐的重要性。 | 近义词: Confucian, follower of Confucius | 反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially opposing philosophies like Legalism or Taoism depending on context) | 词性: Noun, Adjective
