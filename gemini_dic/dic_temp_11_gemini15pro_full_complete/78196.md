geothermal:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈθɜːrməl/| adj.|地热的|Of, relating to, or utilizing the heat of the earth's interior.|例句：Geothermal energy is a renewable resource. 地热能是可再生资源。|例句：Iceland is known for its geothermal activity. 冰岛以其地热活动而闻名。|近义词：volcanic, subterranean|反义词：surface, frigid|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From geo- "earth" + thermal "heat".  "Geo" comes from the Greek word "gē" meaning "earth". "Thermal" comes from the Greek word "thermē" meaning "heat".
