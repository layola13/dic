britannicize:/brɪˈtænɪsaɪz/ |v. |使英国化|To make British in character; to imbue with British characteristics or customs.|The government tried to Britannicize the colonies by imposing British laws and language.|Synonyms: anglicize|Antonyms:  decolonize|verb
britannicization:/brɪˌtænɪsaɪˈzeɪʃn/ |n. |英国化|The act or process of making something British in character.|The Britannicization of Ireland led to much cultural conflict. |Synonyms: anglicization|Antonyms: decolonization|noun
