mystified: /ˈmɪstɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 困惑的，迷惑不解的 |  Confused or puzzled, especially because you cannot understand something. |  I was completely mystified by her sudden change in behavior. 我对她突然改变的行为感到完全困惑。 | Example: The detective was mystified by the lack of evidence.  侦探对缺乏证据感到困惑。| Synonyms: puzzled, bewildered, perplexed, baffled | Antonyms: enlightened, clear, understanding | adjective

mystify: /ˈmɪstɪfaɪ/ | verb | 使迷惑，使困惑 | To confuse or puzzle someone completely. |  The magician's tricks mystified the audience. 魔术师的戏法使观众迷惑不解。 | Example:  His explanation only served to mystify me further. 他的解释只会让我更加困惑。 | Synonyms:  bewilder, perplex, puzzle, baffle  | Antonyms: clarify, enlighten, explain | verb
