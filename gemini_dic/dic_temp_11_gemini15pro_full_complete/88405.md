hirsute:/ˈhɜːrsjuːt/ /ˈhɜːrsuːt/ | adj. | 多毛的；毛茸茸的 | Covered with hair; hairy. |  The hirsute man had a beard that reached his chest.  那个毛茸茸的男人留着长及胸部的胡子。

The dog was rather hirsute, more like a sheepdog than a terrier.  这只狗毛茸茸的，更像牧羊犬而不是梗犬。


| 近义词：hairy, shaggy, woolly | 反义词: hairless, smooth, bald | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *hirsūtus* (hairy), from *hirsūta*, feminine of *hirsūtus*, past participle of *hirsutire* (to make rough, shaggy, or bristly; to cover with bristles), of uncertain origin. Perhaps related to *horrēre* (to stand on end, bristle).
