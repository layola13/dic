extramurally:/ˌekstrəˈmjʊrəli/
| adv.|校外地，院外地|Outside the walls or boundaries of an institution, especially a college or university.  Relating to activities performed or situations existing outside the walls of a building or institution. |The research was conducted extramurally. 这项研究是在校外进行的。|Synonyms: externally, outside, beyond the walls|Antonyms: intramurally, internally, within|Adverb
