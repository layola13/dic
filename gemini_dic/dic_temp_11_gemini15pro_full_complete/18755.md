berkeleian:/bɜːrˈkliːən/| adj. n.|伯克利派的，伯克利派哲学家|Of or relating to the philosophy of George Berkeley, especially his subjective idealism.|Berkeleyian idealism denies the existence of matter independent of mind. 伯克利派的唯心主义否认物质独立于意识而存在。|例句：His berkeleian views were challenged by his peers. 他的伯克利派观点受到了同行的挑战。|例句：She is a renowned Berkeleian scholar. 她是一位著名的伯克利学派学者。|近义词：idealist, immaterialist|反义词：materialist, realist|词性:形容词,名词

词源:
From George Berkeley (1685-1753), Irish philosopher + -ian. 源自爱尔兰哲学家乔治·伯克利 (1685-1753) 的名字，加上后缀 -ian。
