gentianaceous: /ˌdʒɛnʃiəˈneɪʃəs/
adjective
龙胆科的；属于龙胆科的
Relating to or characteristic of the Gentianaceae family of plants, which includes gentians.
The gentianaceous flowers were a vibrant blue.  龙胆科的花朵是鲜艳的蓝色。
These small, gentianaceous plants prefer alpine meadows. 这些小型龙胆科植物喜欢高山草甸。
Synonyms: gentian-like
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, potentially names of other plant families, depending on context, e.g., rosaceous)
词性: adjective
