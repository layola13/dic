formidably:/ˈfɔːrmɪdəbli/ /fɔːrˈmɪdəbli/| adv.|令人敬畏地，可怕地，难对付地|Inspiring fear or respect through being impressively large, powerful, intense, or capable.  It describes something that is very difficult to deal with or overcome.|例句：She is a formidably intelligent woman. 她是一位非常聪明的女人。|例句：The team faces a formidably difficult challenge.  这个团队面临着极其艰巨的挑战。|近义词：impressively, powerfully, overwhelmingly, daunting|反义词:weakly, mildly, easily, insignificantly|词性:副词
词源: Late Middle English: from Old French formidable, from Latin formidabilis ‘causing fear’, from formidare (based on formido ‘fear’).
