mycetoid:/ˈmaɪsɪˌtɔɪd/| adj.|类真菌的，似真菌的|Resembling a fungus; funguslike.  Having the appearance or characteristics of a fungus, but not necessarily being a true fungus.|The biologist discovered a new mycetoid organism in the rainforest.  生物学家在雨林中发现了一种新的类真菌生物。|The lab sample revealed a mycetoid growth pattern.  实验室样本显示出一种类似真菌的生长模式。|Synonyms: fungoid, fungal|Antonyms: non-fungal|adjective

mycetoid:/maɪˈsiːtɔɪd/| n.|类真菌生物|An organism resembling a fungus.|The researchers were studying various mycetoids found in the soil samples. 研究人员正在研究土壤样本中发现的各种类真菌生物。|Further analysis revealed the mycetoid wasn't a true fungus, but a protist.  进一步分析显示，这种类真菌生物并非真正的真菌，而是一种原生生物。|Synonyms:  fungoid organism|Antonyms:  N/A|noun
