overdebated:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈbeɪtɪd/
adjective
过度辩论的，被过度讨论的
having been debated or discussed excessively
The issue of school uniforms is overdebated and often leads to no real solutions.  校服问题被过度讨论，而且常常没有真正的解决方案。
The merits of coffee versus tea are an overdebated topic. 咖啡与茶的优劣是一个被过度讨论的话题。
Synonyms: overdiscussed, overanalyzed, overworked
Antonyms: underdiscussed, underexamined
