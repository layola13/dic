guidebooky: /ˈɡaɪdˌbʊki/ | adj. | 像指南的；像旅行指南的 | Resembling or characteristic of a guidebook, especially in being overly detailed, informative, or prescriptive. |  The itinerary felt a bit guidebooky, lacking spontaneity.  这行程感觉有点像指南，缺乏自发性。 |  The tone of the article was rather guidebooky, listing all the "must-see" sights. 这篇文章的语气相当像旅游指南，列出了所有“必看”的景点。| 近义词: informative, prescriptive, didactic, instructive | 反义词: spontaneous, unplanned, improvisational | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "guidebooky" is derived from "guidebook" + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives). "Guidebook" itself comes from "guide" + "book".  "Guide" originates from the Old French word "guider," meaning "to guide, lead, conduct." "Book" comes from the Old English word "bōc," meaning "book, writing, document."
