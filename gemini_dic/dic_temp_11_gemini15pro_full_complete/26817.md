cagey:/ˈkeɪdʒi/| adj.|谨慎的，守口如瓶的|Reluctant to give information owing to caution or suspicion.|He was very cagey about his plans.|近义词：guarded, secretive, wary, noncommittal, reticent|反义词：open, frank, candid|词性:形容词
词源:From "cage" + "-y," suggesting the guardedness of a caged animal.
