congruous:/ˈkɒŋɡruəs/ /kɑːŋˈɡruːəs/| adj.|一致的，适合的，和谐的|Being in agreement, harmony, or correspondence; appropriate or fitting.|Her behavior was perfectly congruous with her values. 她的行为与她的价值观完全一致。|The modern furniture was not congruous with the old house.  现代家具与老房子不协调。|近义词：consistent, harmonious, appropriate, fitting, suitable, compatible|反义词：incongruous, incompatible, unsuitable, inappropriate, discordant|词性:形容词

词源:
Late Middle English: from Latin congruus, from congruere ‘agree’, from con- ‘together’ + ruere ‘fall, rush’.
