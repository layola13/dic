nonaccrued:/ˌnɒnəˈkruːd/
adjective
未累计的，未应计的
Not having accrued; not having accumulated over time, especially of interest or other income.
The nonaccrued interest will be paid at maturity. 未累计利息将在到期时支付。
The bond was sold at a discount because it was nonaccrued.  由于未累计利息，该债券以折扣价出售。
Synonyms: outstanding, unpaid, due, owing
Antonyms: accrued, accumulated, paid
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + accrued.  "Accrued" comes from the Old French "acru" (increased, grown), past participle of "accroistre" (to increase, grow), from Latin "accrescere" (to grow to, be added to).
