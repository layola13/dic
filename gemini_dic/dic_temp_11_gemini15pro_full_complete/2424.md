adjourns:/əˈdʒɜːrnz/
verb
休会，延期，推迟
to have a pause or rest during a meeting or trial
The judge adjourned the trial until the following Monday. 法官将审判延期至下周一。
近义词: postpone, suspend, recess
反义词: convene, commence, begin
词性:v.

adjourns:/əˈdʒɜːrnz/
词源: From Old French *ajourner, from a- "to" + journée "day". Originally meant "to appoint a day for (a meeting, etc.)". The sense shifted to "defer, postpone" in the 16th century.
用法说明:  Primarily used in formal contexts, especially in legal or official settings.  Can be used transitively (adjourn the meeting) or intransitively (the meeting adjourned).
