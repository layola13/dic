campylospermous:/ˌkæmpɪləˈspɜːrməs/| adj.|弯种子的，弯胚珠的|Having seeds with a curved embryo; Relating to or characteristic of the Campylospermae, a group of plants (now usually included in the Umbelliferae) having a curved seed.|The campylospermous nature of the seed is a key identifying feature. 这种植物弯曲的种子是其关键识别特征。|The botanist identified the plant as campylospermous due to the shape of its seeds.  植物学家根据其种子形状将该植物鉴定为弯种子的。|curved, arcuate|straight|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *kampylos* (curved) + *sperma* (seed)
