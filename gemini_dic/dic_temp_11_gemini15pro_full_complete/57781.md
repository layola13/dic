dreariness:/ˈdrɪərinɪs/
n.
沉闷；凄凉；枯燥
The dreariness of the landscape oppressed her.  这片荒凉的景色让她感到压抑。
例句：The endless rain added to the dreariness of the day.  没完没了的雨增加了这天的沉闷。
例句：She couldn't stand the dreariness of small-town life. 她无法忍受小镇生活的枯燥。
近义词：dullness, monotony, gloominess, bleakness
反义词：brightness, excitement, cheerfulness
词性: 名词

Etymology: From dreary + -ness.  Dreary comes from Old English *dreorig, meaning "sad, mournful, bloody."
