callow:/ˈkæloʊ/| adj.|幼稚的，不成熟的，缺乏经验的|Lacking experience of life; immature.|例句：The callow youth made a series of foolish mistakes.  这个幼稚的年轻人犯了一系列愚蠢的错误。|例句：He was just a callow boy when he left home. 他离家时还是个乳臭未干的小伙子。|近义词：immature, inexperienced, naive, green|反义词：mature, experienced, sophisticated, worldly|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *calu "bald," from Proto-Germanic *kalwaz "bare, bald."  The original sense referred to young birds without feathers, then extended to inexperienced young people.  最终引申为缺乏经验的年轻人。
