baseless:/ˈbeɪslɪs/| adj.|无根据的，无基础的|Without a basis in reason or fact; unfounded.|例句：The accusations against him were completely baseless. 对他的指控完全是无中生有。|近义词：groundless, unfounded, unsubstantiated, spurious|反义词：grounded, founded, substantiated, well-founded|词性:形容词
Etymology: From base (foundation) + -less (without).  Ultimately from Latin *bassus* (low).
