manakin: /ˈmænəkɪn/ (US), /ˈmænəkɪn/ (UK)| n. |侏儒鸟，纨绔子弟|A small, brightly colored tropical American bird of the family Pipridae, known for the elaborate courtship displays of the males.| The male manakin performed an elaborate dance to attract a female. 雄性侏儒鸟表演了精心设计的舞蹈来吸引雌性。| 近义词: bird, show-off (for the figurative meaning)| 反义词: - | 词性: noun

manakin: /ˈmænəkɪn/ (US), /ˈmænəkɪn/ (UK)| n. |纨绔子弟|A man who is very interested in his clothes and appearance; a dandy.| He dressed like a manakin, always concerned with the latest fashions.  他穿着像个纨绔子弟，总是关注最新的时尚。|近义词: dandy, fop, coxcomb| 反义词: - | 词性: noun
