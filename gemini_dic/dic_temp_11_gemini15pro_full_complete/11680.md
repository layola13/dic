armoric:/ɑːrˈmɒrɪk/ | adj. n.|布列塔尼的；布列塔尼语；布列塔尼人|Relating to Brittany, its people, or their language.  Also, the language itself, a Brythonic Celtic language.| He spoke fluent Armorican. 他讲一口流利的布列塔尼语。 |近义词：Breton, Brythonic|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun
ETYMOLOGY->From Old French *armorique, from Late Latin Armorica, from Gaulish *Aremorica (“coastal region, area by the sea”), from are- (“at, on, by”) + mori (“sea”).
