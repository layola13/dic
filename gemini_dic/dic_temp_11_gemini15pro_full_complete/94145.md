impeditive:/ɪmˈpedɪtɪv/
adjective
阻碍的，妨碍的
hindering or obstructing progress.
The impeditive regulations made it difficult for the company to expand.  阻碍性的规章制度使公司难以扩张。
Heavy traffic proved impeditive to our journey.  拥堵的交通阻碍了我们的旅程。
近义词: hindering, obstructive, hampering, restrictive
反义词: helpful, conducive, facilitative
词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin *impeditīvus, from impedīre (“to shackle, fetter, hinder, impede”), from in- (“in, on”) + ped-, pes (“foot”).
