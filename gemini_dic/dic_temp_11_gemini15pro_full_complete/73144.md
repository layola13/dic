forasmuch:/fɔːrəzˈmʌtʃ/
conj.
因为，鉴于
Since; seeing that.
Forasmuch as the weather is fine, we will go out for a picnic. 因为天气好，我们要去野餐。
近义词:because, since, as, whereas, inasmuch as
反义词:although, though, even though
词性:连词

Forasmuch:/fɔːrəzˈmʌtʃ/
adv.
因为，鉴于
Since; seeing that (used archaically and chiefly in legal documents).
Forasmuch as it has been proven, we can now proceed. 鉴于它已经被证明，我们现在可以继续进行了。
近义词:because, since, as, whereas, inasmuch as
反义词:although, though, even though
词性:副词

PHRASE->
 (Formal/Archaic usage primarily)

词源:  From Middle English *for as muche as*, equivalent to for + as + much + as.  Essentially means "to the extent that" or "seeing that".  Over time, it shortened to *forasmuch*.
