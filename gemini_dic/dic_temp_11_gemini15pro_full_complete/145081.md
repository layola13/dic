overannotated:/ˌoʊvərˈænəteɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过度注释的 | Provided with too many annotations; excessively commented upon.  | The textbook was so overannotated that it was difficult to follow the original text. 这本教科书注释过多，以至于很难理解原文。| The code was overannotated, making it harder to read than necessary.  这段代码注释过多，使其比必要的更难阅读。| 近义词: over-commented, excessively explained | 反义词: under-annotated, sparsely commented | 词性: adjective
