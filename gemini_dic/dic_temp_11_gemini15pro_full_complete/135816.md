nonpromiscuously:/ˌnɒnprəˈmɪskjuəsli/
adverb|非滥交地，不混杂地|In a way that is not promiscuous; without engaging in casual sexual encounters.|She chose her partners nonpromiscuously, valuing commitment and emotional connection.|近义词:selectively, chastely, discreetly|反义词:promiscuously, indiscriminately|词性:副词

词源:
From non- + promiscuous + -ly.  "Promiscuous" comes from the Latin "promiscuus," meaning "mixed, indiscriminate."  The prefix "non-" negates the meaning.
