gemer:/ˈdʒɛmər/ | n. | 宝石商，宝石切割工 | A person who cuts, polishes, or deals in gems. |  He apprenticed as a gemer for five years before opening his own shop.  他做了五年的宝石商学徒，然后开了自己的店。 | 近义词: jeweler, lapidary | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From Old French "gemere", from "geme" (gem). 最终源自拉丁语 "gemma"（宝石）。
