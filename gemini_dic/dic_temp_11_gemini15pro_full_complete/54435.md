disinflation:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfleɪʃən/
n.
通货膨胀率下降，通货减缓
A reduction in the rate of inflation.
例句：The central bank's policies are aimed at achieving disinflation. 中央银行的政策旨在实现通货膨胀率下降。

例句：Disinflation is different from deflation, which is a decrease in the general price level of goods and services.  通货膨胀率下降不同于通货紧缩，后者是商品和服务价格水平的普遍下降。

近义词：deceleration of inflation
反义词：inflation, hyperinflation
词性: noun
