counter-passant:/ˌkaʊntər ˈpɑːsɒnt/ (British), /ˌkaʊntər ˈpæsənt/ (American)| n.|过路兵|A special move in chess where a pawn can capture an opponent's pawn that has just moved two squares forward from its starting position, as if the captured pawn had only moved one square forward.|Example: Black played a counter-passant and captured white's pawn. 黑方走了过路兵，吃掉了白方的兵。|Example:  The counter-passant move is a rare but exciting tactic in chess. 过路兵走法在国际象棋中很少见，但却是一个令人兴奋的战术。|近义词: en passant (French term)|反义词: None|词性: Noun


counter-passant:/ˌkaʊntər ˈpɑːsɒnt/ (British), /ˌkaʊntər ˈpæsənt/ (American)| adj.|过路兵的|Relating to or involving the counter-passant move in chess.|Example:  A counter-passant capture is only possible immediately after the opposing pawn moves two squares. 过路兵的吃子只有在对方兵走两格后才能立即进行。|Example:  The counter-passant rule adds an interesting dynamic to the game of chess. 过路兵规则为国际象棋增添了有趣的活力。 |近义词: en passant (French term)|反义词: None|词性: Adjective
