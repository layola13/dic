inerts:/ɪˈnɜːrts/ | n. |惰性物质；惰性气体| Chemically inactive substances, especially noble gases. | Argon is one of the inert gases and is used in welding. 氩气是一种惰性气体，用于焊接。| synonyms: inactive substances, inert materials | antonyms: reactive substances, active agents | noun

inerts:/ɪˈnɜːrts/ | adj. |惰性的；不活动的| Lacking the ability or strength to move. | The inert patient lay in bed. 这位不活动的病人躺在床上。| synonyms: inactive, motionless, passive | antonyms: active, mobile, reactive | adjective
