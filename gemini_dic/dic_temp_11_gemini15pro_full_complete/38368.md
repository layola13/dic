concentricity:/ˌkɒnsɛnˈtrɪsɪti/ /ˌkɑːnsɛnˈtrɪsɪti/| n. |同心度，同轴度|The state of having a common center, especially of circles or spheres.|The machinist checked the concentricity of the gears.  机械师检查了齿轮的同心度。|Synonyms: coaxiality, alignment|Antonyms: eccentricity, off-center|noun

concentric:/kənˈsɛntrɪk/ /kənˈsɛntrɪk/| adj. |同心的，同轴的|Having a common center, especially of circles or spheres.|The target was made of concentric circles.  靶子是由同心圆组成的。|Synonyms: coaxial, circular|Antonyms: eccentric, off-center|adjective
