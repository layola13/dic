awed:/ɔːd/| adj.|充满敬畏的，感到惊叹的|Filled with awe; inspired by a feeling of reverence, wonder, and respect.|例句：I was awed by the beauty of the Grand Canyon. 我被大峡谷的美丽所震撼。|近义词：amazed, astonished, impressed, wonderstruck|反义词：unimpressed, indifferent|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From the noun "awe" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Awe" comes from Old Norse *agi, meaning "fear, terror." The meaning shifted over time from fear to a feeling of reverential respect and wonder.
