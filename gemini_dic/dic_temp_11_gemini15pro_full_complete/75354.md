frother:/ˈfrɒðə(r)/ (UK), /ˈfrɑːðər/ (US)| n.|起泡器；发泡剂；（非正式）狂热者|A device or substance that creates froth.  A person who is excessively enthusiastic about something.|例句：He used a milk frother to make his cappuccino. 他用牛奶起泡器制作卡布奇诺。

例句：She's a real frother for that new band. 她真是那个新乐队的狂热粉丝。|近义词：foamer, enthusiast, fanatic|反义词：critic, detractor|noun
