flotilla:/fləˈtɪlə/ /floʊˈtɪlə/| n. |小型舰队，船队|A small fleet of ships or boats, especially warships or fishing boats.|例句：A flotilla of small boats escorted the royal yacht. 一支小型舰队护送着皇家游艇。

例句：The fishing flotilla returned to harbor before the storm. 渔船队在暴风雨来临前返回了港口。|近义词：fleet, squadron, armada|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "single ship" or "lone vessel" in specific contexts) |词性:noun
