arachnidial:/ərækˈnɪdiəl/| adj.|蜘蛛类的，蛛形纲动物的|Relating to or characteristic of arachnids (spiders, scorpions, mites, etc.).|例句：The spider spun an intricate arachnidial web. 蜘蛛结了一张复杂的蛛网。|近义词：spider-like, arachnidan|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Modern Latin *arachnidialis*, from Latin arāchnidēs ‎(“spider”), from Ancient Greek ἀράχνη ‎(arákhnē, “spider”) + -id ‎(“descendant of; resembling”) + -al ‎(“relating to”).
