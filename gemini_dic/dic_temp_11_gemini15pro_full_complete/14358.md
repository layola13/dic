autophoby:/ˌɔːtəˈfoʊbiə/ | n. | 孤独恐惧症; 独自一人恐惧症 | An abnormal and persistent fear of being alone or isolated.  A person with autophobia feels anxious and uneasy when they are by themselves, even in familiar and safe environments. |  Example 1: Her autophobia prevented her from living alone. 她的孤独恐惧症使她无法独自生活。

Example 2: He experienced a surge of autophobia whenever he was left alone in the house. 每当他被独自留在家里时，他都会感到一阵孤独恐惧症的袭来。 | Synonym: monophobia, eremophobia, isolophobia | Antonym:  sociability, gregariousness | noun
