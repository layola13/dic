durians:/ˈdʊəriənz/ | n. |榴莲|A large, oval, spiny fruit containing a creamy, pungent pulp.  It is native to Southeast Asia.|例句：Durians are known for their strong smell. 榴莲以其强烈的气味而闻名。

例句：She loves the taste of durians, but I can't stand it. 她喜欢榴莲的味道，但我受不了。|近义词: (No direct synonym for the fruit itself, perhaps "tropical fruit") |反义词: (No antonym) |词性:noun
