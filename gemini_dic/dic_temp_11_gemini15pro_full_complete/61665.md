emitters:/ɪˈmɪtərz/| n. |发射器，散发器|Something that emits something else, like light, heat, sound, or gas.|The factory's smokestacks were the primary emitters of pollutants in the valley. 工厂的烟囱是山谷中污染物的主要排放源。|例句：The sun is a powerful emitter of light and heat. 太阳是光和热的强大发射器。|例句：The company is a major emitter of greenhouse gases. 这家公司是温室气体的主要排放者。|近义词：sources, radiators, dischargers|反义词: absorbers, receivers|词性:noun
