outdelivers:/ˌaʊtdɪˈlɪvərz/
verb
超越交付；表现优于
To perform better than someone or something, especially in delivering goods or services.
Our new supplier consistently outdelivers the previous one in terms of speed and reliability. 我们的新供应商在速度和可靠性方面一直优于之前的供应商。
surpass, outperform, exceed
underperform, lag behind
