dependant:/dɪˈpɛndənt/ (UK) /dɪˈpɛndənt/ (US)| n. adj.|受抚养者，依赖的|A person who relies on another, especially financially.  As an adjective, it means relying on someone or something else for support.|例句：She claimed her son as a dependant on her tax return. 她申报儿子为受抚养者以获得退税。|近义词：dependent, reliant, ward|反义词:independent, self-sufficient|词性:名词，形容词

dependant:/dɪˈpɛndənt/ (UK) /dɪˈpɛndənt/ (US)| adj.|依赖的|Relying on someone or something else for support.|例句：The project is dependant on funding from the government. 这个项目依赖于政府的资助。|近义词：dependent, reliant, conditional|反义词:independent, autonomous|词性:形容词
