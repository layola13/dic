inspeximus:/ɪnsˈpɛksɪməs/
noun
查验证, 查验副本
an official copy of a document, especially a charter, authenticated by having an impression of the seal of the body which issued it attached to it.
The king issued an inspeximus of the town's charter. 国王签发了该镇宪章的查验副本。
synonym: examination, authentication, verification, confirmation
antonym: fabrication, forgery, falsification
n.

Etymology:
From Late Latin *inspeximus*, first person perfect plural indicative active of *inspicere* (“to inspect, examine”), from *in-* + *specere* (“to look”).
