laser:/ˈleɪzər/ (US), /ˈleɪzə/ (UK)| n.|激光|A device that generates an intense beam of coherent monochromatic light (or other electromagnetic radiation) by stimulated emission of photons from excited atoms or molecules.|The surgeon used a laser to remove the tumor.|近义词: coherent light,  maser |反义词: incoherent light|noun
