nurtural: /ˈnɜːrtʃərəl/ | adj. | 养育的，培育的 | Relating to nurture; fostering growth and development. | The school provides a nurtural environment for students. 学校为学生提供了一个培育的环境。|  The teacher's nurtural approach helped the shy child blossom. 老师的培育方法帮助害羞的孩子茁壮成长。 | 近义词: nurturing, fostering, supportive, cherishing | 反义词: neglectful, unsupportive, detrimental | 词性: adjective

词源: From nurture (v.) + -al (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Old French *norture, from nourrir "to nourish," from Latin nutrire "to suckle, nourish."
