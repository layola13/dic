non-receivable:/ˌnɒn rɪˈsiːvəbl/ | adj. | 不可收的，无法收回的 | Not able to be received or collected, especially of debts or payments. |  The company wrote off the non-receivable debts.  该公司注销了无法收回的债务。 | Bad debt, uncollectible | Receivable, collectible | adjective

non-receivable:/ˌnɑːn rɪˈsiːvəbl/ | n. | 坏账，不可收回的款项 | A debt or amount owed that is unlikely to be paid and is therefore treated as a loss. | The non-receivables totaled over $1 million. 坏账总额超过了100万美元。| Bad debt, uncollectible | Asset, receivable | noun
