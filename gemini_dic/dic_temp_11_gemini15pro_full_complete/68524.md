ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French fantasie ("fantasy, imagination, invention, appearance, illusion"), from Late Latin phantasia, from Ancient Greek φαντασία (phantásia, “appearance, imagination”), from φαντάζομαι (phantázomai, “I appear, I make visible”), from φαίνω (phaínō, “I show, I bring to light”).
