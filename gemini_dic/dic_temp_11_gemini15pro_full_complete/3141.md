aesthetics:/ɛsˈθɛtɪks/ /iːsˈθɛtɪks/| n.|美学；审美观|A set of principles concerned with the nature and appreciation of beauty, especially in art.|The aesthetics of the building are very pleasing. 这栋建筑的美学很令人愉悦。|Synonyms: beauty, taste, artistry|Antonyms: ugliness, vulgarity|noun


aesthetics:/ɛsˈθɛtɪks/ /iːsˈθɛtɪks/| adj.|审美的；美学的|Concerned with beauty or the appreciation of beauty.|The painting is aesthetically pleasing. 这幅画在审美上令人愉悦。|Synonyms: artistic, beautiful, tasteful|Antonyms: ugly, displeasing, tasteless|adjective
