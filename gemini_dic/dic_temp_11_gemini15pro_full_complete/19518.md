bifaces:/ˈbaɪfeɪsiːz/
n.
双面器，两面打制石器
A prehistoric stone tool that has been flaked on both sides to produce a roughly symmetrical shape.
Archaeologists unearthed several bifaces at the site. 考古学家在遗址中挖掘出几件双面器。

近义词:hand axe, handaxe
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "uniface" for a single-faced tool)
词性:noun (plural)
