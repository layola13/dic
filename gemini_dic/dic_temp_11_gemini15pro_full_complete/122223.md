miswired:/ˌmɪsˈwaɪərd/
adjective
接错线的；线路错误的
incorrectly wired; wired wrongly
The robot malfunctioned because it was miswired.  这个机器人发生故障是因为接线错误。
The electrician apologized for the miswired lights.  电工为线路接错的灯道歉。
synonym: faulty, incorrectly connected, wrongly wired
antonym: correctly wired, properly wired
词性: 形容词
