monocellular:/ˌmɒnəʊˈsɛljʊlə(r)/ /ˌmɑːnəˈsɛljələr/| adj.|单细胞的|Composed of a single cell; unicellular.|例句：Amoebas are monocellular organisms. 变形虫是单细胞生物。|例句：The research focused on the behavior of monocellular algae.  这项研究的重点是单细胞藻类的行为。|近义词：unicellular|反义词：multicellular|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From mono- (one) + cellular (relating to cells).  "Mono-" comes from Greek monos "alone, single, solitary." "Cellular" ultimately derives from Latin cella "small room, storeroom, cell."
