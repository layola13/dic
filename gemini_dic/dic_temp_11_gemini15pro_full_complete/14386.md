autosaving:/ˌɔːtoʊˈseɪvɪŋ/ | n. | 自动保存 | The process of automatically saving data to a file or storage device, usually at regular intervals, to prevent data loss in case of a system crash or power failure. | My word processor has an autosaving feature, so I don't have to worry about losing my work. 我的文字处理器有自动保存功能，所以我不必担心丢失我的工作。 | Synonyms: automatic saving, background saving | Antonyms: manual saving | Noun
