hater:/ˈheɪtər/| n. |憎恨者；仇恨者；讨厌……的人|A person who greatly dislikes a specified person or thing.|例句：He became a hater of all things modern. 他变得厌恶一切现代事物。

例句：She has many haters online due to her controversial opinions. 由于她有争议的观点，她在网上有很多黑粉。|近义词：enemy, foe, opponent, adversary, critic, detractor|反义词：admirer, fan, supporter, lover, friend|词性:noun
