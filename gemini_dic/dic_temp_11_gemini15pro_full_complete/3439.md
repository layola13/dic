afrikaans:/ˌæfrɪˈkɑːns/
noun|南非荷兰语；南非白人|A West Germanic language descended from 17th century Dutch, spoken mainly in South Africa and Namibia.|She speaks Afrikaans fluently. 她能流利地说南非荷兰语。|
近义词:Cape Dutch (旧称)|反义词: (无直接反义词，可以列出其他语言，例如 English, Zulu)|词性:n.
