cucullated: /ˈkjuːkəleɪtɪd/ | adj. |  兜状的，帽状的，头巾状的 | Having a hood or hood-like structure; hooded.  Resembling a cowl or hood. | The monk wore a cucullated robe.  僧侣穿着带兜帽的长袍。| The flower has cucullated petals.  这朵花有兜帽状的花瓣。| 近义词: hooded, cowled | 反义词: hoodless | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *cucullātus*, past participle of *cucullāre* "to furnish with a hood," from *cucullus* "hood, cowl."
