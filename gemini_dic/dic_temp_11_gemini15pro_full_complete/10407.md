appertainment:/əˈpɜːrtənmənt/
|n.|附属物，附属建筑；（通常复数）休闲娱乐设施|Something added to a main building or property, especially something providing leisure or recreation.  It often refers to amenities or facilities. |The luxury apartment complex boasts a swimming pool, gym, and other appertainments.  这个豪华公寓大楼拥有游泳池、健身房和其他休闲娱乐设施。|Synonyms: amenity, facility, convenience, extra, adjunct|Antonyms: necessity, essential, requirement|noun
