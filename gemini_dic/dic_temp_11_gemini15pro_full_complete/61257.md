emancipative:/ɪˈmænsɪpeɪtɪv/
adjective
解放的，使人获得自由的
Relating to or promoting emancipation; setting free from legal, social, or political restrictions.
The emancipative power of education can transform lives.  教育的解放力量可以改变生活。
The new laws had an emancipative effect on the serfs.  新的法律对农奴有解放作用。
近义词:liberating, freeing, releasing, enfranchising
反义词:oppressive, restrictive, enslaving, confining
词性:adjective

词源: From Late Latin emancipativus, from emancipare "to emancipate," from ex- "out" + mancipium "ownership, slave," from manus "hand" + capere "to take."
