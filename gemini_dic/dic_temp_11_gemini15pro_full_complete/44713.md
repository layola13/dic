cruelly:/ˈkruːəli/ | adv. | 残酷地，狠心地 | In a cruel manner; causing pain or suffering intentionally. | He treated the animal cruelly. 他残酷地对待这只动物。|  She laughed cruelly at his misfortune. 她残酷地嘲笑他的不幸。 | 近义词: brutally, heartlessly, mercilessly, ruthlessly, savagely | 反义词: kindly, gently, compassionately, mercifully | 词性: 副词

词源: From Middle English *cruelliche, from Old French cruelment, from Latin crūdēliter, from crūdus (“raw, cruel”) + -liter (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *kreu- (“raw flesh; blood”).
