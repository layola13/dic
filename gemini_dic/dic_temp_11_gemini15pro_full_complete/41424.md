cornerless:/ˈkɔːrnərləs/| adj.|无角的，没有角落的|Lacking corners; without corners.  It describes something that doesn't have any corners or angles.|The artist created a cornerless sculpture, smooth and flowing in all directions. 艺术家创作了一个无角的雕塑，各个方向都光滑流畅。|Near synonyms: rounded, smooth, curved|Near antonyms: angular, sharp, pointed|adjective
