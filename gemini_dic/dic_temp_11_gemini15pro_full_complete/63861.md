epigraphical:/ˌɛpɪˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
题词的；碑文的
Of or relating to an epigraph; inscribed as an epigraph.  Relating to or consisting of epigraphs.
The book began with an epigraphical quotation from Shakespeare. 这本书以一句莎士比亚的题词作为开头。
The epigraphical evidence supports the theory. 碑文证据支持了这个理论。
Synonyms:  inscribed, dedicatory, commemorative
Antonyms:  unmarked, blank, undecorated
词性: adjective
