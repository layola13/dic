例句：The project was initially expected to cost $1 million, but the final cost was much higher.  该项目最初预计耗资100万美元，但最终成本要高得多。|近义词：at first, originally, in the beginning, firstly|反义词：finally, eventually, ultimately, lastly|词性:副词
