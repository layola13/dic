orphean:/ˈɔːrfiən/ | adj. | 奥菲斯的，悲伤优美的，动人的 | Relating to Orpheus, the Thracian poet and musician in Greek mythology, or his music; mournful and melodious; enchanting. |  The orphean music moved the audience to tears. 奥菲斯般的音乐使观众感动得流泪。| synonyms: melodious, lyrical, haunting, enchanting, moving | antonyms: jarring, discordant, cacophonous | adjective
