half-reactionary:/ˌhæf riˈækʃəˌnɛri/ | adj. | 半反动派的；部分反动的 | Someone who is half-reactionary holds some, but not all, reactionary views.  They may be resistant to some forms of change, but not entirely opposed to progress. |  He had a half-reactionary stance on social issues, embracing some modern ideas while clinging to traditional values. 他对社会问题的立场半反动，既拥护一些现代思想，又坚持传统价值观。|  He's not a full-blown reactionary, more of a half-reactionary, cautiously accepting some change.  他不是一个彻头彻尾的反动派，更像是一个半反动派，谨慎地接受一些改变。 | Near synonyms: moderately conservative, partially reactionary | Near antonyms: progressive, radical, revolutionary | adjective
