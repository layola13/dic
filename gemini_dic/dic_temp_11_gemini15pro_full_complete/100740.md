intransigent:/ɪnˈtrænsɪdʒənt/ | adj. n. | 不妥协的，固执的；不妥协的人 | Unwilling or refusing to change one's views or to agree about something. |  The intransigent attitude of both parties made a compromise impossible. 双方不妥协的态度使得和解不可能。|  The intransigents in the group refused to consider any changes to the plan.  小组中不妥协的人拒绝考虑对计划做任何修改。 | synonyms: uncompromising, inflexible, obstinate, stubborn | antonyms: compliant, flexible, yielding | adjective/noun

intransigents:/ɪnˈtrænsɪdʒənts/ | n. | 不妥协的人 | People who are unwilling or refusing to change their views or to agree about something. | The intransigents refused to budge on the issue. 这些不妥协的人在这个问题上拒绝让步。| The negotiators were frustrated by the intransigents on both sides of the table.  谈判代表对双方的不妥协者感到沮丧。 | synonyms: diehards, hardliners, stick-in-the-muds | antonyms: moderates, compromisers | noun
