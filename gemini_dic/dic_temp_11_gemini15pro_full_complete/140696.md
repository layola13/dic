omitting: /əˈmɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 省略; 遗漏; 删节; 未做; 忘记做 |  The author omits any mention of his own childhood.  作者略去了任何提及自己童年的内容。 | 近义词: excluding, leaving out, neglecting, ignoring | 反义词: including, adding, inserting | 词性: 动词 (present participle of omit)

词源:  From Latin *omittere, from omittere (“to let go, send forth, lay aside, leave undone, pass over in silence”), from ob (“away, down, before”) + mittere (“to send, let go”).
