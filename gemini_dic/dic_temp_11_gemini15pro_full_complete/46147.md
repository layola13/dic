cymose:/ˈsaɪmoʊs/| adj.|聚伞状的，聚伞花序的|Of, relating to, or resembling a cyme; having the form of a cyme.|The plant has a cymose inflorescence.  这种植物的花序是聚伞状的。|The elder tree displays a characteristic cymose arrangement of its flowers. 接骨木的花朵呈现出典型的聚伞状排列。|近义词：determinate, centrifugal|反义词: indeterminate, centripetal|词性: adjective
Etymology: From French *cymeux*, from *cyme* + *-eux*. Ultimately from Ancient Greek *κῦμα* (kûma, “anything swollen, wave, fetus”).
