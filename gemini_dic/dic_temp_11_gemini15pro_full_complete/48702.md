defibrillation: /diːˌfɪbrɪˈleɪʃən/ /diːˌfaɪbrɪˈleɪʃən/
noun
除颤；电击复律
The process of restoring the heart's normal rhythm by administering a controlled electric shock.
The patient underwent defibrillation to restore his normal heart rhythm. 病人接受了电击复律以恢复正常心律。
cardioversion, electric shock therapy
asystole
n.
