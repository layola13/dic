kith:/kɪθ/ | n. | 亲朋好友；故交；(尤指)同乡；亲戚朋友 | One's friends, acquaintances, and relations. | Example:  She rarely had time to visit her kith and kin. 她很少有时间拜访她的亲朋好友。

Example: He felt a strong connection to his kith and kin in the old country.  他对家乡的亲朋好友有着强烈的感情。 | Synonym: friends, relatives, family, acquaintances, kinsfolk, kin | Antonym: strangers, enemies | noun
