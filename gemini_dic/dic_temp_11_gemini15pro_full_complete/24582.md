brocken:/ˈbrɒkən/ (UK) /ˈbroʊkən/ (US)| adj.|破碎的，残缺的；被打断的；不完整的；（土地）未开垦的；（精神）崩溃的；（德语）布罗肯峰|Broken can describe something that is physically damaged and separated into pieces, or something that is interrupted or incomplete. It can also refer to uncultivated land or someone who is emotionally or mentally broken.  In German, it refers to the Brocken mountain.|The vase was broken after it fell off the table. 花瓶从桌子上掉下来后摔碎了。

The broken promise left her feeling betrayed.  未兑现的承诺让她感到被背叛了。

The farmer worked the broken ground.  农民耕种着未开垦的土地。

He was a broken man after the loss of his family.  失去家人后，他成了一个精神崩溃的人。

They went hiking in the Harz Mountains, hoping to see the Brocken spectre. 他们去哈尔茨山脉徒步旅行，希望能看到布罗肯峰的幽灵。|近义词：fractured, shattered, damaged, ruined, incomplete, interrupted, uncultivated, devastated|反义词：whole, intact, complete, repaired, cultivated, healthy|词性:形容词
