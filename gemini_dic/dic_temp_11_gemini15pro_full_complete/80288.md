goiter:/ˈɡɔɪtər/ (US), /ˈɡɔɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |甲状腺肿|An abnormal enlargement of the thyroid gland, visible as a swelling at the base of the neck.|She had a noticeable goiter due to an iodine deficiency. 她由于缺碘而患有明显的甲状腺肿。|
|近义词：thyroid enlargement, struma|反义词: (No direct antonym exists for a physical condition like goiter.  One might consider "normal thyroid" as a contrasting state.) | noun|

goitre:/ˈɡɔɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |甲状腺肿|An abnormal enlargement of the thyroid gland, visible as a swelling at the base of the neck.|He developed a goitre after moving to a region with low iodine levels in the water. 他搬到一个水中碘含量低的地区后患上了甲状腺肿。|
