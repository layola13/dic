dhole:/doʊl/ | n. | 亚洲野犬，豺 | An Asian wild dog, *Cuon alpinus*, reddish-brown in color, that hunts in packs. |  例句：The dhole is a highly social animal. 亚洲野犬是一种高度社会化的动物。

ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi ढोल (ḍhol), probably imitative of its howl. Ultimately from Sanskrit दुन्दुभि (dundubhi, “a kind of drum”).
