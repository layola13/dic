co-adventurer:/ˌkəʊədˈvɛntʃərər/ | n. | 共同冒险者；同行者 | A person who shares in an adventure with another; a fellow adventurer. |  例句：She was a loyal co-adventurer on many of his expeditions. 她在他多次探险中都是他忠诚的同行者。

例句：He found a willing co-adventurer in his brother. 他找到了一个乐于同行的冒险伙伴，那就是他的兄弟。| 近义词：companion, partner, associate, collaborator | 反义词：opponent, adversary, enemy | 词性：名词
