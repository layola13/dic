metals: /ˈmetlz/ | n. | 金属 | A solid material that is typically hard, shiny, malleable, fusible, and ductile, with good electrical and thermal conductivity. |  Many everyday objects are made of metals. 许多日常用品是由金属制成的。 |  Steel is one of the strongest metals. 钢铁是最坚固的金属之一。 | synonyms: minerals, elements | antonyms: nonmetals | noun
