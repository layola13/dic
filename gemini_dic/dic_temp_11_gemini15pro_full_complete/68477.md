familism: /ˈfæmɪlɪzəm/
noun
家庭主义
A social structure where the needs of the family are more important than the needs of any individual family members.
Strong familism is a defining characteristic of many cultures.  重视家庭是许多文化的显著特征。
Familism is often seen in traditional societies.  在传统社会中，家庭主义很常见。
Synonyms: family-centeredness, clannishness
Antonyms: individualism, self-reliance
