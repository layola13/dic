irritative:/ˈɪrɪtətɪv/ | adj. |刺激性的； раздражающий (Russian) |Causing or tending to cause irritation. |The dust is irritative to the eyes. 灰尘刺激眼睛。| The rough fabric was irritative to her skin. 粗糙的布料刺激了她的皮肤。 | 近义词: irritating, annoying, vexing, provoking | 反义词: soothing, calming, relaxing | 词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin irritativus, from Latin irritatus (past participle of irritare ‘excite, provoke’), from irritus ‘invalid, void’.
