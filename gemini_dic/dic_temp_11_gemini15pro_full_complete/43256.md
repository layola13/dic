cradle-snatcher:/ˈkreɪdlˌsnætʃər/| n. |老牛吃嫩草的人; 专找未成年人约会的人|A person, usually an older man, who has a romantic or sexual relationship with a much younger person, often a minor.|例句：He's a cradle-snatcher, dating someone half his age. 他是个老牛吃嫩草的人，和一个年龄只有他一半的人约会。

例句：She was accused of being a cradle-snatcher when she married a man twenty years her junior. 她嫁给了一个比她小二十岁的男人，因此被指责为老牛吃嫩草。|近义词：pedophile (if the relationship is illegal), cougar, sugar daddy|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
