mandolin:/ˈmændəlɪn/ (US), /ˈmændəlɪn/ (UK)| n. |曼陀林|A small, stringed musical instrument, similar to a lute, with a pear-shaped body and a fretted neck. Played with a plectrum.|He played a lively tune on his mandolin. 他用曼陀林演奏了一曲轻快的调子。|Synonym: lute, bandurria|Antonym: (No direct antonym for a musical instrument)|noun

mandolin:/ˈmændəlɪn/ (US), /ˈmændəlɪn/ (UK)| n. |曼陀林|A kitchen utensil used for slicing vegetables into thin strips.|She used a mandolin to make potato chips. 她用曼陀林做土豆片。|Synonym: slicer, cutter|Antonym: (No direct antonym for a kitchen utensil)|noun
