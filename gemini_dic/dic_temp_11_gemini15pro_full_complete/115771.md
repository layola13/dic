marined:/məˈriːnd/ | v. | 用海水浸泡；使充满海洋气息 | To permeate or imbue with the qualities of the sea; to expose to seawater. | The old sailor's clothes were marined with the salty spray. 老水手的衣服充满了海水的咸味。| 近义词: soaked, immersed, steeped | 反义词: dried, dehydrated | 词性: 动词

词源: Derived from "marine" (adj. & n.), ultimately from Latin "marinus" (of the sea), from "mare" (sea).
