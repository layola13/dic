allusiveness:/əˈluːsɪvnəs/ |n.|暗示性; 含蓄; |The poem is notable for its allusiveness and subtle irony. 这首诗以其暗示性和微妙的讽刺而著称。| 近义词:indirectness, suggestion, implication|反义词:explicitness, directness|词性:名词

Etymology: From allusive + -ness.  Allusive comes from Latin *alludere* "to play with," from *ad-* "to" + *ludere* "to play."
