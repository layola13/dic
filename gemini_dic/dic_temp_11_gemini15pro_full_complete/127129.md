nativities:/nəˈtɪvɪtiz/
n.
耶稣诞生;诞生;圣诞剧;圣诞节
The artistic depiction of the Nativity.
对耶稣诞生的艺术描绘。

The church puts on a nativity play every year.
教堂每年都会上演一出圣诞剧。

The season of peace, goodwill, and nativities.
和平、善意和圣诞节的季节。

We went to see the school nativity play.
我们去看学校的圣诞剧。


Etymology: From Late Latin *nativitas, from Latin natus ‘born’, past participle of nasci ‘be born’.
词源：源自晚期拉丁语 *nativitas，来自拉丁语 natus “出生”，是 nasci “出生”的过去分词。


Synonyms: birth, origin, genesis, beginning
近义词：出生，起源，起源，开始

Antonyms: death, end, demise, conclusion
反义词：死亡，结束，死亡，结论
