bort:/bɔːrt/|n.|劣质钻石，工业钻石|An impure, dark-colored variety of diamond, unfit as a gemstone but useful for industrial applications because of its hardness.|The drill bit was tipped with bort.|近义词:industrial diamond|反义词:gem-quality diamond|词性:名词

词源: From Swedish *bort*, from Middle Low German *bort*, meaning "something worthless".  Ultimately of uncertain origin, possibly related to a word meaning "lump" or "projection."
