intellectual:/ˌɪntəˈlektʃuəl/ | adj. n. | 智力的，知识的；知识分子 |  relating to the ability to think and understand ideas and information; a person who engages in serious study and thought |  She's very intellectual. 她很聪明。/ He's an intellectual. 他是一位知识分子。 | 近义词：intelligent, cerebral, academic, learned | 反义词：unintellectual, simple, ignorant | 词性: 形容词, 名词

intellectual:/ˌɪntəˈlektʃuəl/ | n. | 知识分子 | a person possessing a highly developed intellect |  He considers himself an intellectual. 他认为自己是个知识分子。 | 近义词：scholar, thinker, academic | 反义词：philistine, ignoramus | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin intellectualis (“of the intellect”), from Latin intellectus (“understanding, perception”), from intellegō (“I understand”), from inter (“between”) + legō (“choose, gather, read”).
