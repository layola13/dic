amphibole:/ˈæmfɪboʊl/ (US) /ˈæmfɪbɒl/ (UK)| n. |闪石|A group of inosilicate minerals, forming prism or needlelike crystals, containing iron, magnesium, calcium, sodium, and aluminum, and typically occurring in metamorphic and igneous rocks. |Amphibole is a common mineral in many types of rocks. 闪石是许多类型岩石中的常见矿物。| The geologist identified the rock as containing amphibole. 地质学家鉴定出这块岩石含有闪石。|Synonyms: inosilicate, silicate|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
