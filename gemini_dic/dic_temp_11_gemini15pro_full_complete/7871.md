antecessor:/ˌæn.tɪˈses.ər/ | n. |前辈，先驱，前任|A person who held a job or office before someone else; a thing that comes before another of the same kind in time or order.|例句：His antecessor in the role had a very different management style.  他的前任的管理风格截然不同。

例句：The museum contains artifacts from his antecessors.  博物馆里藏有他前辈们的文物。|近义词：predecessor, forerunner, precursor |反义词:successor, descendant |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *antecessor*, from *antecedere* "to go before," from *ante* "before" + *cedere* "to go."
