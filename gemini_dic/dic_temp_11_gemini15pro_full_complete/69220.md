fearless:/ˈfɪərlɪs/| adj.|无畏的，勇敢的|Without fear; brave.|She showed fearless determination in the face of danger.  她在面对危险时表现出无畏的决心。|近义词：brave, courageous, valiant, intrepid, audacious|反义词:fearful, timid, cowardly|词性:形容词
词源:  From Old English *fǣrlēas, from fǣr (“fear”) +‎ -lēas (“-less”). Cognate with Dutch veesloos (“fearless”), German furchtlos (“fearless”).
