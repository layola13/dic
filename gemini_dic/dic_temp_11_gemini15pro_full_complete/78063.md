geocentrically:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈsɛntrɪkli/ | adv. | 以地球为中心地 |  Considering the Earth as the center of the universe or solar system.  | 例句：Ancient astronomers often viewed the universe geocentrically. 古代天文学家通常以地球为中心来看待宇宙。

例句：The model presented the solar system geocentrically, with all planets revolving around Earth.  该模型以地球为中心展示了太阳系，所有行星都围绕地球旋转。

| 近义词：| 反义词：heliocentrically(以太阳为中心地) | 词性:副词

Etymology:
From "geo-" (earth) + "centric" (center) + "-ally" (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Greek "gē" (earth) and "kentron" (center).
