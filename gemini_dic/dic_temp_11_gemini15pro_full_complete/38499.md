conciergerie:/kɔ̃sjerʒəri/ | n. |看守人住所，（尤指）巴黎革命时期关押犯人的监狱，服务台|A building, especially in Paris during the French Revolution, used as a prison. Also, a service desk in a hotel or apartment building that provides assistance to guests or residents. |The Conciergerie in Paris held Marie Antoinette before her execution. 巴黎的看守人住所是玛丽·安托瓦内特被处决前的关押地。| The hotel's conciergerie can help you book tours and make restaurant reservations. 酒店的服务台可以帮助你预订旅游和餐厅。|Synonyms: jail, prison, service desk, reception desk |Antonyms: freedom, liberty | noun
