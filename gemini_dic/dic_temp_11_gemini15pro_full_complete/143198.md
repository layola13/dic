outcourted:/ˌaʊtˈkɔːrtɪd/
verb
胜过…的追求；比…更会追求
to surpass someone in courting; to woo or pursue someone more effectively than another
He outcourted all the other suitors and won her hand in marriage. 他胜过所有其他追求者，赢得了她的芳心。
outdo, outstrip, surpass
be outdone by, lose to

词性: verb
