boroughmongery:/ˈbʌrəˌmʌŋɡəri/
n.
操纵选区；市政腐败；(通过控制选区来谋取私利的)不法行为
The manipulation of borough elections for political advantage, especially by the wealthy or powerful.  This typically involved bribery, intimidation, and other corrupt practices.
The rampant boroughmongery of the 18th and 19th centuries led to widespread calls for electoral reform.  18和19世纪猖獗的操纵选区导致了广泛的选举改革呼声。
Synonyms: corruption, graft, patronage, manipulation, influence-peddling
Antonyms:  fairness, impartiality, integrity, honesty
