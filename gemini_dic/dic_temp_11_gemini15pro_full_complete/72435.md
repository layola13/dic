fluidlike:/ˈfluːɪdlaɪk/
adjective
像流体的；流体状的
Resembling a fluid in its behavior or appearance.
The molten lava flowed in a fluidlike manner down the side of the volcano.  熔化的岩浆像液体一样沿着火山侧面流淌。
The silk fabric draped in fluidlike folds.  丝绸织物像液体一样垂坠成褶皱。
近义词:fluid, liquid, runny, watery
反义词:solid, rigid, viscous
词性:adjective

Etymology:
fluid +‎ -like
