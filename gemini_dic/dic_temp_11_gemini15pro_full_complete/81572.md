grazingly:/ˈɡreɪzɪŋli/| adv.|轻擦地，掠过地，放牧地|Touching or passing lightly over a surface; in a manner suggestive of grazing. |The car grazed the wall grazingly as it sped down the narrow alley. 汽车飞速驶过狭窄的小巷，轻轻擦过墙壁。|The bullet grazed his arm grazingly, causing only a superficial wound. 子弹掠过他的手臂，只造成了一处表皮伤口。|近义词：lightly, superficially, glancingly|反义词: heavily, forcefully|词性:副词
词源: From graze + -ing + -ly.  "Graze" comes from Old English *græsian, from græs "grass."
