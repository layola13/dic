The word "mediately" does not exist in English.  You likely mean "immediately".

immediately: /ɪˈmiːdiətli/ | adv. | 立即，马上 | at once; without delay |  I need you to come here immediately. 我需要你马上过来。|  The medicine relieved the pain immediately. 这药立刻缓解了疼痛。 | 近义词: instantly, directly, promptly, forthwith | 反义词: later, eventually, subsequently | 词性: 副词

词源:  From Late Latin *immediatus, from Latin in- “not” + mediatus “in the middle”.
