countermovement:/ˈkaʊntərˌmuːvmənt/| n. |反向运动，对抗运动|An action taken in opposition to another action or movement.  It can be physical, like a specific type of exercise, or metaphorical, referring to a social or political trend.|

例句：
The boxer used a countermovement to dodge his opponent's punch.
拳击手用反向运动躲开了对手的拳击。

The government's new policy sparked a countermovement among the people.
政府的新政策引发了民众的对抗运动。


近义词：reaction, opposition, resistance|
反义词: support, agreement|
词性: noun|
