lodestone:/ˈloʊdˌstoʊn/| n.|天然磁石，吸引人的东西|A lodestone is a piece of magnetite that has magnetic properties and attracts iron or steel. PHRASE->
a lodestone for something = 对某事物有吸引力的人或物
The new shopping mall is a lodestone for teenagers.
这个新的购物中心对青少年很有吸引力。

the lodestone of =  …的魅力所在；…的吸引力
The lodestone of this city is its vibrant nightlife.
这座城市的魅力在于其充满活力的夜生活。

act as a lodestone =  充当吸引人的东西
The beautiful scenery acted as a lodestone for tourists.
美丽的景色吸引了游客。

a powerful lodestone =  强大的吸引力
The promise of high salaries is a powerful lodestone for many job seekers.
高薪的承诺对许多求职者来说是一个强大的吸引力。

drawn to something like a lodestone = 像被磁石吸引一样被…所吸引
He was drawn to the mystery of the ancient artifact like a lodestone.
他像被磁石吸引一样被古代文物的奥秘所吸引。


词源：From Old English *lād(stān), from lādan (“to lead, guide”) + stān (“stone”). Cognate with Middle Dutch lēdesteen (“lodestone”), German Leitstern (“lodestar, guiding star”).  The "lode" part originally referred to the way the stone leads or guides sailors.
