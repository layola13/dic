deduce:/dɪˈdjuːs/ /dɪˈduːs/
|vt.|推断，演绎|To reach a conclusion from something known or assumed; infer.|From the evidence, we can deduce that the thief was left-handed. 从证据来看，我们可以推断小偷是左撇子。|infer, conclude, reason, gather|suppose, guess, assume|verb

deduit:/dɪˈdʌkt/ /dɪˈduːkt/
|n.|扣除，扣除额|An amount that is or may be deducted from something, especially from taxable income or tax to be paid.|The company offers a health insurance plan with a monthly deductibles. 公司提供每月扣除额的医疗保险计划。|reduction, subtraction, abatement|addition, increase|noun
