miterwort:/ˈmaɪtərwɜːrt/| n.|主教冠|A plant of the saxifrage family, typically having star-shaped white flowers.  It is also known as bishop's cap.|例句：The delicate miterwort bloomed in the shady corner of the garden.  花园阴凉的角落里，娇嫩的主教冠开花了。

例句：I found a patch of miterwort growing by the stream. 我在溪流边发现了一片主教冠。|近义词：bishop's cap|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
