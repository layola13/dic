brownness:/ˈbraʊn.nɪs/
n.
棕色；褐色；褐色状态
The brownness of the toast indicated it had been overcooked.
烤面包的棕色表明它烤过头了。
The leaves took on a rich brownness in the autumn.
秋天的树叶呈现出浓郁的棕色。
近义词:brown, tan
反义词:whiteness, paleness
词性:noun

Etymology:  From brown + -ness.  "Brown" comes from Old English "brūn," of Germanic origin. The suffix "-ness" is used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
