bottomlessly:/ˈbɒtəmləsli/ (US) /ˈbɒtəmlɪsli/ (UK)
adverb
无底地；无限地；深不可测地
In an immeasurable or inexhaustible way.  It describes something that seems to have no limit or end.
She stared bottomlessly into his eyes. 她深情地凝视着他的眼睛。
He sighed bottomlessly. 他深深地叹了口气。
Synonym: infinitely, endlessly, limitlessly, immeasurably, profoundly
Antonym: finitely, limitedly, superficially
词性: 副词
