overlearnedness:/ˌoʊvərˈlɜːrndnɪs/ | n. | 过度学习 | The quality or state of having learned something so thoroughly that it becomes automatic and difficult to modify. It often refers to skills or knowledge that have been practiced extensively, sometimes to the point of hindering further learning or adaptation. |  His overlearnedness of the old system made it difficult for him to adapt to the new software. 他对旧系统的过度学习使他难以适应新软件。|  overlearning, automaticity | inflexibility, unadaptability | noun

overlearned:/ˌoʊvərˈlɜːrnd/ | adj. | 过度学习的 |  Having learned something so well that it is automatic and difficult to modify. | His overlearned typing skills made it hard for him to learn a new keyboard layout.  他过度学习的打字技能使他难以学习新的键盘布局。| ingrained, habituated | unlearned, nascent | adjective
