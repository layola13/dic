murdress: /ˈmɜːrdres/ (Often pronounced /ˈmɜːdrɪs/ in casual speech)| n. |女凶手; 女杀人犯 | A woman who commits murder. |  The murdress was apprehended shortly after the crime. 女凶手在犯罪后不久就被逮捕了。 |  The notorious murdress was finally brought to justice.  臭名昭著的女凶手终于被绳之以法。 | Synonyms: killer, assassin, murderess (alternative spelling), homicide perpetrator (formal) | Antonyms: protector, rescuer | Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *murdresse*, from Anglo-Norman *murd(e)resse*, from *murdrer* (to murder) + *-esse* (feminine suffix).  The *-esse* suffix eventually became *-ess* in Modern English.  The word is related to "murderer," which refers to a person of any gender who commits murder.
