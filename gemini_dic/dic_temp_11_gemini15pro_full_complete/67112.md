explosiveness:/ɪkˈsploʊsɪvnəs/| n.|爆炸性，爆发力，易爆炸性|The explosiveness of the situation was palpable. 局势的爆炸性是显而易见的。|例句：The explosiveness of the volcano was evident in the frequent eruptions. 火山的爆发力体现在频繁的喷发中。

例句：The coach praised the player's explosiveness on the court. 教练称赞了球员在球场上的爆发力。|近义词：volatility, instability, power, force|反义词：stability, calmness, inertness|词性:名词
词源: From explosive + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin explodere "to drive out by clapping, hiss off the stage".
