overballast:/ˈoʊvərˈbæləst/| v.|过载，装载过重|To overload with ballast; to put in too much ballast.|The ship was overballasted and sat low in the water. 这艘船装载过重，吃水很深。|Synonyms: overload, overburden|Antonyms: underload, lighten|verb

overballast:/ˈoʊvərˈbæləst/| n.|过载，过重的压舱物|Excessive ballast.|The overballast caused the ship to list dangerously. 过重的压舱物导致船只危险地倾斜。|Synonyms: overload, excess|Antonyms: underload, deficiency|noun
