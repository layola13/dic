over-fertilizes: /ˌōvərˈfɜːrtəlaɪzɪz/
verb
过度施肥
Applies too much fertilizer to (soil or a plant).
The farmer over-fertilizes his crops, leading to runoff that pollutes the nearby river.  农民过度施肥他的作物，导致径流污染附近的河流。
He over-fertilizes his lawn, causing it to burn. 他过度施肥他的草坪，导致它枯黄。
Synonyms: overfeed, saturate
Antonyms: under-fertilize, starve
词性: 动词
