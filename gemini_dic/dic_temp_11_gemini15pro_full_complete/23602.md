brambling:/ˈbræmblɪŋ/ | n. | 花鸡，山雀 | A small finch (Fringilla montifringilla) that breeds in northern Europe and Asia and winters in southern Europe and Asia. It has a brightly coloured breeding plumage. |  We saw a flock of bramblings feeding on the ground. 我们看到一群花鸡在地上觅食。| Synonyms: finch, chaffinch | Antonyms: N/A | noun

brambling:/ˈbræmblɪŋ/ | adj. | 冬天从北方迁徙到南方的 | Migrating from the north to the south in winter. (Less common usage) | The brambling birds arrived with the first snow. 这些迁徙的花鸡随着第一场雪到来。 | Synonyms: migratory, wintering | Antonyms: resident, non-migratory | adjective
