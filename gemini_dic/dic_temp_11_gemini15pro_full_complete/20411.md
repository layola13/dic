biternate:/baɪˈtɜːrneɪt/
adjective
二回三出的；三回羽状分裂的
Twice or thrice ternate; (of a leaf) pinnately divided three times.
The biternate leaves of the plant are a distinctive characteristic.  这种植物二回三出的叶子是一个显著特征。
The fern has delicate, biternate fronds.  这种蕨类植物有纤细的二回三出叶子。
近义词:triternate (有时可互换使用，但triternate更倾向于指三回三出)
反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑simple, entire, undivided表示叶形简单)
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *bi- (twice) + ternatus (arranged in threes), from terni (three each).
