anamorphism:/ˌænəˈmɔːrfɪzəm/| n.|变形，失真；变形图像|A distorted projection or drawing that appears normal when viewed from a particular point or with a suitable mirror or lens.  An example of anamorphism is a distorted image that can only be seen correctly when reflected in a curved mirror.|The artist created an intriguing anamorphism that revealed a hidden message when viewed through a cylindrical mirror.|近义词：distortion, deformation|反义词:  conformity, regularity|noun
