byliners:/ˈbaɪlaɪnərz/
n.
署名作者，专栏作家
Journalists or writers who contribute regularly to a publication and whose names appear with their work.
The magazine has a stable of talented byliners. 这本杂志拥有一批才华横溢的专栏作家。
synonyms: columnists, contributors, feature writers, journalists
antonyms: ghostwriters, anonymous authors
noun

byline:/ˈbaɪlaɪn/
n.
署名；（尤指报刊文章的）作者署名行
A line at the beginning of a news story or article giving the writer's name.
The article appeared with my byline. 这篇文章以我的署名发表。
synonyms: attribution, credit, signature
antonyms: anonymity
noun
