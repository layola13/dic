comfortment: /ˈkʌmfətmənt/ | n. | 安慰; 慰藉; 舒适 |  A state of being comfortable, physically or mentally; solace. |  The soft blanket provided comfortment on the cold night.  柔软的毯子在寒冷的夜晚提供了舒适感。 | 近义词: comfort, solace, ease, relief | 反义词: discomfort, distress, unease | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From comfort + -ment (suffix forming nouns of action or result).
