gareth:/ˈɡærəθ/| n. |加雷思 (男子名)|A Welsh masculine given name, meaning "gentle" or "blessed".  It is derived from the Welsh elements *gwar*, meaning "gentle, mild," and *-haeth*, a suffix of uncertain meaning, possibly related to the word for "strife" or "battle".  In Arthurian legend, Gareth was a knight of the Round Table, one of the sons of King Lot and Morgause.|Gareth is a popular name in Wales.|近义词: (As a name, no direct synonyms. Similar sounding names might be Garrett, Jared)|反义词: (Names do not typically have antonyms)|词性:noun
