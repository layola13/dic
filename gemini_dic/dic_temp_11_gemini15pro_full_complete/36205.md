cogitability:/ˌkɒdʒɪtəˈbɪləti/ /ˌkɑːdʒɪtəˈbɪləti/
n.
可思考性；可认知性
The cogitability of the universe's origins is a question that has plagued philosophers for centuries. 宇宙起源的可思考性是一个困扰哲学家几个世纪的问题.
The philosopher pondered the cogitability of abstract concepts like infinity. 这位哲学家思考了诸如无限之类的抽象概念的可认知性。
近义词：thinkability, conceivability
反义词：inconceivability, unthinkability
词性：名词

cogitate:/ˈkɒdʒɪteɪt/ /ˈkɑːdʒɪteɪt/
v.
思考；考虑
I need some time to cogitate on this problem. 我需要一些时间来思考这个问题.
She cogitated deeply before making her decision. 她在做出决定之前深思熟虑。
近义词：think, ponder, contemplate, consider, meditate, reflect, deliberate, muse, ruminate
反义词：ignore, neglect
词性：动词

cogitative:/ˈkɒdʒɪtətɪv/ /ˈkɑːdʒɪtətɪv/
adj.
思考的；认知的
His cogitative abilities were impressive. 他的认知能力令人印象深刻.
The article explored the cogitative processes involved in creative writing. 这篇文章探讨了创作写作中涉及的认知过程。
近义词：thinking, reasoning, intellectual, mental, cognitive
反义词：unthinking, irrational
词性：形容词
