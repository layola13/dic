motormouths:/ˈmoʊtərˌmaʊðz/| n. |喋喋不休的人，话匣子|A person who talks incessantly.|例句：The motormouths in the office make it hard to concentrate. 办公室里那些喋喋不休的人让人很难集中注意力。
