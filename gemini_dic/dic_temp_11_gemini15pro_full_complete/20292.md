biscutate:/bɪˈskʌteɪt/
adjective
饼干状的；两面凸起的
Having the form of a biscuit; twice-baked.
The botanist described the seed as small and biscutate.  植物学家将种子描述为小而饼干状的。

The fossil was small, hard, and biscutate. 这块化石很小、很硬，并且呈饼干状。

近义词: biscuit-shaped, lenticular
反义词: (No common direct antonym; consider describing the opposite shape, e.g., concave, linear)
词性: adjective
