half-awake:/ˌhæf əˈweɪk/
adjective
半睡半醒的
partially awake; not fully alert.
She stumbled into the kitchen, still half-awake. 她跌跌撞撞地走进厨房，仍然半睡半醒。
The half-awake child couldn't understand what was happening.  半睡半醒的孩子不明白发生了什么。
近义词：drowsy, sleepy, groggy
反义词：wide-awake, alert, vigilant
词性: adjective
词源: From "half" + "awake." "Half" comes from Old English "healf," meaning "one of two equal parts." "Awake" comes from Old English "awacan," meaning "to wake up."
