brachycephalic: /ˌbrækiːsəˈfælɪk/ | adj. | 短头型的 | Having a short wide head.  Relating to or characterized by brachycephaly. |  Example:  The Pug is a brachycephalic breed and can suffer from breathing problems. 八哥犬是一种短头型犬种，可能会出现呼吸问题。

Example: Brachycephalic dogs are prone to overheating. 短头型犬容易中暑。| 近义词: short-headed, short-nosed | 反义词: dolichocephalic (长头型的) | 词性: adjective
