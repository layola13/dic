araucaria:/ˌærɔːˈkɛəriə/ | n. |南洋杉|A genus of evergreen coniferous trees of the family Araucariaceae, native to South America and Australasia, having whorls of stiff, flattened, pointed leaves. |例句：The araucaria in the park is over 100 years old. 公园里的那棵南洋杉已经超过一百岁了。

|例句：Araucaria seeds are edible and are a traditional food source for some indigenous peoples. 南洋杉的种子可食用，是一些土著居民的传统食物来源。

|近义词：monkey puzzle tree (specifically for *Araucaria araucana*) |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
