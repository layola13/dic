hyperbolize:/haɪˈpɜːrbəlaɪz/ | v. | 夸张；夸大 | To represent something as much greater or less, better or worse, or more intense than it really is; to exaggerate. | He hyperbolized the dangers of the trip to deter his children from going. 他夸大了旅行的危险，以阻止他的孩子们去。

She tends to hyperbolize when she's telling a story. 她讲故事时往往会夸大其词。 | 近义词：exaggerate, overstate, amplify, magnify | 反义词：understate, downplay, minimize | 词性: 动词
