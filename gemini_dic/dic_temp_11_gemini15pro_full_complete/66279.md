excommunicate: /ˌɛks kəˈmjuːnɪˌkeɪt/ | vt. |逐出教会，开除教籍 | To officially exclude someone from participation in the sacraments and services of the Christian Church. | The bishop excommunicated the heretic. 主教把异教徒逐出了教会。|  Synonyms: banish, ostracize, expel | Antonyms: admit, include, welcome | Verb

excommunicate: /ˌɛks kəˈmjuːnɪkɪt/ | n. |被逐出教会的人 | A person who has been officially excluded from participation in the sacraments and services of the Christian Church. | He became an excommunicate after challenging the church's authority.  他挑战教会权威后被逐出了教会。 | Synonyms: outcast, pariah | Antonyms: member, believer | Noun
