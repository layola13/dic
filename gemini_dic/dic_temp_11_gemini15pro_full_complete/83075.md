gurring:/ˈɡɜːrɪŋ/ | n. |  咕噜声，猫发出的呜呜声 |The sound of a cat purring, a low, continuous vibrating sound. |  例句：The gurring of the cat showed she was content. 猫的咕噜声表明它很满足。| 近义词：purring, rumbling | 反义词: hissing, growling | 词性:名词

gurring:/ˈɡɜːrɪŋ/ | adj. |  发出咕噜声的 |Making a low, continuous vibrating sound, like a cat. |  例句：The gurring cat curled up on my lap.  发出咕噜声的猫蜷缩在我的腿上。| 近义词：purring | 反义词: hissing | 词性:形容词
