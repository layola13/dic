antalgic:/ænˈtælˌdʒɪk/
adjective
止痛的，缓解疼痛的
Relieving or lessening pain.  Relating to antalgic posture, which is a posture adopted to avoid or lessen pain.
The patient adopted an antalgic gait to minimize pressure on her injured knee. 病人采取了止痛步态，以尽量减少对她受伤膝盖的压力。
She was prescribed antalgic medication for her back pain. 她开了止痛药来治疗背痛。
Synonyms: analgesic, pain-relieving
Antonyms: pain-inducing, agonizing
词性: adjective
