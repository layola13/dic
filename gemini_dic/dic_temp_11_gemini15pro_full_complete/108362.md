lavishingly:/ˈlævɪʃɪŋli/
adverb
慷慨地， lavishly
in a very generous or extravagant way
She entertained lavishingly, with mountains of food and rivers of champagne. 她热情款待，提供大量的食物和香槟。
The king was dressed lavishingly in silks and jewels. 国王穿着奢华的丝绸和珠宝。
Synonyms: generously, profusely, extravagantly, sumptuously
Antonyms: sparingly, frugally, meagerly
