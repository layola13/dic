liquescency:/lɪkˈwɛsənsɪ/
n.
液化，溶解
The process of becoming liquid or the state of being liquid.
The earthquake caused soil liquefaction, swallowing entire buildings. 地震造成了土壤液化，吞噬了整栋建筑物。
melting, fluidity, dissolving
solidification, freezing
noun
