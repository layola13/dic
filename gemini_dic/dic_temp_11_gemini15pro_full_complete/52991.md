dinky:/ˈdɪŋki/| adj.|小的；袖珍的；不值钱的|small, unimportant, or not very good|例句：He drives a dinky little car. 他开着一辆很小的车。|例句：It was a dinky little toy, nothing special. 那是一个很小的玩具，没什么特别的。|近义词：small, tiny, miniature, insignificant, cheap|反义词：large, huge, significant, valuable|词性：形容词
dinky:/ˈdɪŋki/| n.|小型机车；小型汽车|a small locomotive or car|例句：He took the dinky up to the mine. 他乘坐小型机车去了矿山。|例句：She bought a dinky to get around town. 她买了一辆小型汽车代步。|近义词：runabout, compact car|反义词：limousine, truck|词性：名词
