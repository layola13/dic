antireducing:/ˌæntiˈrɪˈdjuːsɪŋ/ | adj. | 抗还原的，防止还原的 |  Preventing or inhibiting reduction (a chemical reaction that involves the gain of electrons).  This term describes a substance or process that hinders reduction. |  The antireducing agent protected the metal from corrosion.  抗还原剂保护金属免受腐蚀。 | synonyms: antioxidant, reducing inhibitor | antonyms: reducing, reductant | adjective
