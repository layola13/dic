assume:/əˈsuːm/ |v.| 假设，承担，呈现 | To suppose to be the case, without proof. To take or begin to have (power or responsibility). To begin to have a particular quality or appearance. | She assumed he was coming, so she made dinner. 她假设他会来，所以她做了晚饭. | synonyms: presume, suppose, posit | antonyms: disbelieve, doubt | verb

assumption:/əˈsʌmpʃən/ |n.| 假设，承担，担任 | A thing that is accepted as true or as certain to happen, without proof. The action of taking on power or responsibility. |  His assumption that he would be promoted was wrong. 他认为他会升职的假设是错误的。| synonyms: presumption, supposition, hypothesis | antonyms: fact, proof, reality | noun
