conversing:/kənˈvɜːsɪŋ/
v.
交谈，谈话
engaging in conversation.
They were conversing in hushed tones. 他们在低声交谈。
近义词：talking, chatting, discussing, speaking
反义词：silent, mute
词性：动词(present participle of converse)

converse:/kənˈvɜːs/
v.
交谈，谈话
to engage in conversation.
We conversed late into the night. 我们谈到深夜。
近义词：talk, chat, discuss, speak
反义词：be silent
词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French converser, from Latin conversari "to live with, keep company with, dwell, abide, stay, pass time," from com- "together" + versari "to turn, be busy, be engaged," frequentative of vertere "to turn."
