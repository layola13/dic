effluvia:/ɪˈfluːviə/ | n. |  臭气，恶臭，（尤指难闻的）气味;  散发物| An unpleasant or harmful odor, smell, or emanation, especially a foul odor. | The effluvia from the factory was unbearable. 工厂散发出的臭气令人难以忍受。|Synonyms: emanations, exhalations, fumes, miasma, stench | Antonyms: fragrance, perfume, aroma | Noun
