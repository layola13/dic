lubricants:/ˈluːbrɪkənts/| n.|润滑剂|Substances used to reduce friction between moving parts, thereby minimizing wear and tear and allowing smoother operation.|The engine requires high-quality lubricants. 这台发动机需要高质量的润滑剂。|synonyms: oils, greases, fluids|antonyms: abrasives, desiccants|noun
