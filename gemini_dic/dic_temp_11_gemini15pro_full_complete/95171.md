inattentions: /ˌɪnəˈtɛnʃənz/
| n. |疏忽，注意力不集中|The accident was caused by a series of inattentions on the driver's part.  这起事故是由驾驶员一系列的疏忽造成的。|
| n. |漫不经心|His inattentions during the meeting were noticed by the boss. 他在会议期间的漫不经心被老板注意到了。|
|近义词：negligence, carelessness, oversight, distraction|
|反义词：attention, diligence, care, focus|
|词性：名词|

Etymology:
From in- (not) + attention.  The suffix -s makes it plural.  Ultimately from Latin *attendere* (to stretch toward, pay attention to).
