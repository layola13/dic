galvanized:/ˈɡælvənaɪzd/| adj.|镀锌的；刺激的，兴奋的|Coated with zinc to protect from rust; stimulated to action.|The bridge was galvanized to prevent corrosion. 这座桥进行了镀锌处理以防止腐蚀。

The news galvanized the community into action. 这条新闻激励了社区采取行动。|近义词：stimulated, excited, roused, invigorated, energized, spurred, impelled, prompted, activated, mobilized|反义词:apathetic, indifferent, unmoved, lethargic, sluggish, passive, inactive|词性:形容词

词源: From French *galvaniser*, from Italian *galvanizzare*, from Luigi Galvani, an Italian physician and physicist who discovered animal electricity. The original sense was "to stimulate by galvanic electricity", later extended to the coating process using zinc by electrolysis, and figuratively to "stimulate into action".
