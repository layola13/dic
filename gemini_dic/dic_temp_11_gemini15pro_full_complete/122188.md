misunderstander:/ˌmɪsʌndərˈstændər/ | n. |误解者；不理解的人|Someone who misunderstands something or someone.  A person who habitually misinterprets the words or actions of others.| He's a terrible misunderstander; he always takes things the wrong way. 他是一个糟糕的误解者；他总是误解别人的意思。| Near Synonyms: misinterpreter | Antonyms: interpreter, understander | Noun
