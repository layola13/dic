half-sightedness:/ˌhɑːf ˈsaɪtɪdnəs/ | n. | 半盲;弱视 |The condition of having poor eyesight; partial blindness. |  She struggled with half-sightedness her whole life. 她一生都在与弱视作斗争。

| My grandmother's half-sightedness made it difficult for her to read.  我祖母的弱视使她难以阅读。

| 近义词: poor eyesight, partial blindness, impaired vision | 反义词: perfect vision, 20/20 vision | 词性: noun
