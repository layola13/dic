approachableness:/əˌproʊtʃəˈbɪlnəs/| n. |可接近性；易接近；平易近人|The quality of being easy to talk to or deal with.  It suggests a friendly and welcoming demeanor that encourages interaction.|Her approachableness made her a popular teacher. 她的平易近人使她成为受欢迎的老师。|近义词：accessibility, affability, friendliness, sociability|反义词：unapproachability, aloofness, coldness, hostility|词性:noun

approachable:/əˈproʊtʃəbl/| adj. |平易近人的；可接近的；亲切的|Friendly and easy to talk to; accessible.|Despite his high position, he remained approachable. 尽管地位很高，他仍然平易近人。|近义词：accessible, affable, friendly, amiable|反义词：unapproachable, aloof, distant, cold|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From approach (v.) + -able + -ness.  "Approach" comes from Old French "aprochier"  meaning "to come near," ultimately from Latin "appropiare" meaning "to draw near to."
