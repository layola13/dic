narkier:/ˈnɑːrkiər/
adj.
更爱告密的，更加爱打小报告的
more inclined to inform on others, especially to the police; more likely to report someone's wrongdoing
The new kid in class seemed narkier than the others, always telling the teacher about minor infractions.  这个班上的新孩子似乎比其他孩子更爱打小报告，总是把一些小违规行为告诉老师。
He's become much narkier since he got promoted. 自从升职后，他变得更加喜欢告密了。
Synonyms:  telltale, snitching, gossiping, informing
Antonyms:  loyal, discreet, trustworthy, tight-lipped
词性: 形容词
