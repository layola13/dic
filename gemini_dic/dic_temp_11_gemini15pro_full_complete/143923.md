outpromise:/ˌaʊtˈprɒmɪs/
v.
胜过…的承诺；比…承诺更多
to exceed someone in promising; to make a greater or more extravagant promise than another.
He outpromised his rival in the election campaign, pledging lower taxes and more jobs.  他在竞选活动中比对手承诺得更多，保证降低税收和创造更多就业机会。

近义词: outbid, outdo, surpass
反义词: underpromise
