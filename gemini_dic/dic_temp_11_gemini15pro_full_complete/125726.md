muniment:/ˈmjuːnɪmənt/ | n. |防御工事；保障；（尤指）契约、证书等|A document, especially a deed, charter, or record, that serves as evidence of legal rights or privileges.|The museum holds the city's ancient muniments. 这家博物馆保存着这座城市的古老契约。|近义词：document, record, deed, charter, title, evidence|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "counter-evidence" or "refutation") |词性: noun

词源: From Old French *muniment*  (recorded as *moinement*) "fortification, defense, protection, support,"  from Latin *munimentum* "defense, fortification, rampart," noun of action from *munire* "to fortify, protect, defend."
