dada:/ˈdɑːdɑː/| n. |达达主义；胡说；爸爸|A movement in art and literature, founded in Zurich during the First World War, that rejected logic and reason and favoured the expression of absurdity.|The artist produced a dadaist collage.  这位艺术家创作了一幅达达主义拼贴画。|近义词：nonsense, babble|反义词：sense, logic|noun

dada:/ˈdɑːdɑː/| n. |爸爸|An informal word for father, often used by young children.|Dada, can we go to the park? 爸爸，我们能去公园吗？|近义词：papa, daddy|反义词：-|noun
