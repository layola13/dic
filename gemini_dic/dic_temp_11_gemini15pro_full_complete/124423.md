motorcar:/ˈməʊtəkɑː(r)/ (UK) /ˈmoʊtərˌkɑːr/ (US)| n.|汽车|A four-wheeled road vehicle that is powered by an engine and is able to carry a small number of people.|例句：The motorcar has revolutionized personal transportation. 汽车彻底改变了个人交通。

例句：Antique motorcars are highly collectible. 古董汽车很有收藏价值。

近义词：automobile, car, vehicle, auto|反义词:|词性：noun
