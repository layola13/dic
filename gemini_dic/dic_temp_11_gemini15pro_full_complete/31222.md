channel:/ˈtʃæn(ə)l/ | n. | 频道；渠道；途径；海峡；航道；(尤指用无线电传送的)信息通道 | A channel is a passage for water or other liquids to flow along, or a band of radio waves used for sending out programmes. |  The ship sailed through the English Channel. 这艘船航行穿过英吉利海峡。 | synonym: route, passage, waterway, medium | antonym: barrier, blockage | noun
channel:/ˈtʃæn(ə)l/ | vt. |引导；开导；(将…)导向 | To channel something means to direct it along a particular route or path. | The government is channeling funds into education. 政府正将资金投入教育。 | synonym: direct, guide, convey | antonym: disperse, scatter | verb
