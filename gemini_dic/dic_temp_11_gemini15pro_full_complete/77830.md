generativity:/ˌdʒɛnərəˈtɪvɪti/
n.| 生产力，创造力，生殖力|The concern for establishing and guiding the next generation.  The ability to transcend personal interests and invest in the development of others and future generations.  It involves a sense of care and responsibility for those who will follow.|例句：His theory emphasizes the importance of generativity in adulthood. 他的理论强调了成年期创造力的重要性。

例句：The artist's generativity seems to have no bounds.  这位艺术家的创造力似乎没有界限。|近义词：productivity, creativity, fecundity, procreativity|反义词：stagnation, barrenness|词性:名词
