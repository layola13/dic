multidirectional:/ˌmʌltiˌdɪˈrekʃənəl/ | adj. | 多方向的，多向的 | Moving or operating in several directions. | The dancer's multidirectional movements captivated the audience.  舞者多方向的动作吸引了观众。| The company adopted a multidirectional marketing strategy to reach a wider audience. 公司采取了多方向的营销策略，以接触更广泛的受众。|  近义词: omnidirectional, polydirectional | 反义词: unidirectional, single-directional | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
multi- (prefix): from Latin multus "much, many"
directional (adjective): from direction + -al (suffix forming adjectives)
direction (noun): from Old French direccion, from Latin directionem "a straightening, a direction," from dirigere "to direct, guide, arrange," from dis- "apart" + regere "to guide, rule."
