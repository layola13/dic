geologically:/ˌdʒiːəˈlɒdʒɪkli/
adjective
地质学上地；地质地
Relating to the study of the earth's physical structure and substance, its history, and the processes that act on it.
The area is geologically unstable.  这个地区地质不稳定。
近义词:geographically, earth science-related
反义词:None
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From geology + -ically.  Geology comes from the Greek gē (earth) and -logia (study of).
