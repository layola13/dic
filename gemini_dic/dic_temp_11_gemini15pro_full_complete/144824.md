over-inflated:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈfleɪtɪd/
adjective
过度充气的；过分膨胀的
Inflated beyond a proper or normal level.
The tires on the car were over-inflated, making the ride bumpy. 汽车上的轮胎充气过量，导致行驶颠簸。
Over-inflated expectations led to disappointment. 过高的期望导致了失望。
synonyms: inflated, overblown, exaggerated, distended
antonyms: deflated, underinflated, shrunken
词性: 形容词
