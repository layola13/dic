imitableness:/ˈɪmɪtəblənəs/ |n.|可模仿性；模仿的可能性|The imitableness of a style is a factor in its popularity.  一种风格的可模仿性是其受欢迎程度的一个因素。|例句：The imitableness of his signature made forgery a real threat.  他的签名的可模仿性使得伪造成为一个真正的威胁。

例句：The imitableness of the bird's song is remarkable.  这只鸟的歌声的可模仿性非常显著。|近义词：copyability, reproducibility,  mimicry|反义词： originality, uniqueness, inimitability|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From imitable + -ness.  Imitable comes from Late Latin imitabilis, from Latin imitari "to imitate."
