genteel:/dʒɛnˈtiːl/
adjective
有教养的，文雅的，上流社会的
Of or relating to the gentry; aristocratic.  Polite, refined, or respectable, often in an affected or ostentatious way. Characterized by exaggerated or affected politeness, refinement, or respectability.
She spoke in a genteel accent. 她说话带着上流社会的口音。
The hotel has a genteel atmosphere. 这家酒店有一种高雅的氛围。

近义词:refined, cultured, polite, courteous, aristocratic, elegant
反义词:rude, vulgar, coarse, unrefined, uncouth, common, boorish
词性:adjective
