moonlights:/ˈmuːnˌlaɪts/ | v. (third-person singular simple present moonlights, present participle moonlighting, simple past and past participle moonlighted) |  兼职；从事第二职业 | To work a second job, typically secretly or at night. | He moonlights as a bartender to earn extra money. 他兼职做酒保来赚外快。 | synonyms: works a second job, has a side hustle, freelances | antonyms:  unemployed, jobless | verb

moonlight:/ˈmuːnlaɪt/ | n. | 月光 | The light from the moon. | The moonlight illuminated the path. 月光照亮了小路。| synonyms: lunar light, moonshine | antonyms: sunlight, daylight | noun

moonlight:/ˈmuːnlaɪt/ | adj. | 月光的；在月光下进行的 | Illuminated by the moon. | We took a moonlight stroll along the beach. 我们在月光下沿着海滩散步。| synonyms: moonlit, lunar | antonyms: sunlit, daylight | adjective
