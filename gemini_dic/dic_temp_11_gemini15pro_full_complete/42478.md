counterfiring:/ˌkaʊntərˈfaɪərɪŋ/
noun
反击炮火；对抗射击
The act of returning fire, especially in artillery or other ranged weaponry, to suppress or neutralize enemy fire.
The counterfiring from the enemy positions was intense. 敌方阵地的反击炮火非常猛烈。
counterattack, return fire, retaliatory fire
offensive fire

verb
反击炮火；对抗射击
To return fire, especially in artillery or other ranged weaponry, to suppress or neutralize enemy fire.
The artillery battery counterfired effectively, silencing the enemy guns.  炮兵连有效地进行了反击，压制了敌人的炮火。
counterattack, return fire, retaliate
attack


ETYMOLOGY->
counter- (prefix): against, opposite  来自拉丁语 contra
fire (noun/verb): the rapid oxidation of a material in the exothermic chemical process of combustion, releasing heat, light, and various reaction products  来自古英语 fyr
