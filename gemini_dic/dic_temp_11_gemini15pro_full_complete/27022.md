caldera:/kælˈdɛrə/ /kɔːlˈdɪərə/ | n. |火山口，破火山口 | A large, basin-shaped volcanic depression, formed by the collapse of a volcano's summit or by a massive eruption. |  The Yellowstone caldera is a massive supervolcano. 黄石火山口是一个巨大的超级火山。

The eruption created a caldera several miles wide.  火山爆发形成了一个几英里宽的破火山口。| synonym: crater, volcanic crater | antonym:  volcanic cone, peak | noun
