grahams:/ˈɡreɪəmz/ | n. | 格雷厄姆饼干；格雷厄姆粉 | A type of cracker made with whole wheat flour, often used for pie crusts or as a snack.  Graham flour is the whole wheat flour used to make these crackers. | I love eating grahams with milk. 我喜欢用牛奶泡格雷厄姆饼干吃。| The key to a good cheesecake crust is using grahams. 做出好吃的芝士蛋糕底的关键是用格雷厄姆饼干。| 近义词: graham crackers, whole wheat crackers | 反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider refined crackers as a contrast) | 词性: noun
