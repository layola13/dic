conjugals:/ˈkɒndʒʊɡlz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːndʒəɡlz/ (US)| adj. n.| 夫妻的；婚姻的；配偶的 |Relating to marriage or the relationship between husband and wife; of or relating to sexual intercourse between married people.  Sometimes used as a noun, particularly in the plural, to refer to conjugal visits (visits specifically permitted for sexual relations between a prisoner and their spouse). |例句：The couple enjoyed their conjugal rights. 这对夫妇享有他们的夫妻权利。

|例句：Conjugal visits are permitted in some prisons.  一些监狱允许夫妻探视。

|近义词：marital, matrimonial, spousal |反义词：non-marital, extramarital|词性:adjective, noun
