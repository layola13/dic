arcades:/ɑːrˈkeɪdz/ | n. |拱廊，拱廊街，游乐中心|A covered passageway with arches and columns, often with shops along one or both sides. Also refers to a place with many coin-operated video games.|We strolled through the elegant arcades of Milan. 我们漫步在米兰优雅的拱廊街上。

The children spent hours playing games at the arcade. 孩子们在游戏厅玩了好几个小时。|近义词：passages, galleries, malls, amusement centers|反义词:open street|词性:名词

arcades:/ɑːrˈkeɪdz/ | n. |拱廊|A series of arches supported by columns or piers.|The Roman aqueduct featured impressive arcades.  这座罗马渡槽以其令人印象深刻的拱廊为特色。|近义词：colonnades |反义词: single arch|词性:名词
