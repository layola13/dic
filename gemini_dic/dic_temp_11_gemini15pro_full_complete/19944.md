biodegraded:/ˌbaɪoʊdɪˈɡreɪdɪd/ | adj. | 生物降解的 | Broken down or decomposed by natural biological processes, especially by the action of microorganisms such as bacteria and fungi. | Example 1: The packaging is made from biodegraded materials, so it's environmentally friendly.  这种包装是由可生物降解的材料制成的，所以很环保。

Example 2:  The scientists are studying how quickly the plastic is biodegraded in different environments. 科学家们正在研究这种塑料在不同环境下的生物降解速度。 | 近义词: compostable, decomposable | 反义词: non-biodegradable, persistent | 词性:形容词

biodegrade:/ˌbaɪoʊdɪˈɡreɪd/ | v. | 生物降解 | To undergo biodegradation; to be decomposed by natural biological processes. | Example 1:  Certain types of plastic can biodegrade in a compost heap.  某些类型的塑料可以在堆肥堆中生物降解。

Example 2: The researchers are trying to find ways to accelerate how quickly the waste materials biodegrade. 研究人员正在努力寻找加速废料生物降解速度的方法。| 近义词: decompose, rot, decay | 反义词: persist, remain | 词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
bio- + degrade

bio-: from Greek bios "life"
degrade: from Latin degradare "to step down," from de- "down" + gradus "step"
