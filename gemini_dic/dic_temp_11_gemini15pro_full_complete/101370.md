ionizing:/ˈaɪəˌnaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 电离的，使离子化的 | Capable of converting (an atom or molecule) into an ion or ions, typically by removing one or more electrons. |  X-rays and gamma rays are examples of ionizing radiation.  X射线和伽马射线是电离辐射的例子。 | synonyms: charging, electrifying | antonyms: non-ionizing, neutralizing | adjective
