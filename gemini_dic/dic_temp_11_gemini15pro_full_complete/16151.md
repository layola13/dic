banistered:/ˈbænɪstərd/| adj.|有栏杆的，有扶手的|Furnished with a banister or banisters; having a banister. |The banistered staircase wound gracefully to the upper floors.  有栏杆的楼梯优雅地盘旋而上，通往楼上。|Synonyms: railed, balustraded|Antonyms: open, unfenced|adjective

banister:/ˈbænɪstər/| n.|栏杆，扶手|A railing at the side of a staircase or balcony to prevent people from falling. |She gripped the banister tightly as she descended the steep stairs. 她紧紧抓住扶手，走下陡峭的楼梯。|Synonyms: railing, balustrade, handrail|Antonyms: none (lack of a banister implies openness)|noun
