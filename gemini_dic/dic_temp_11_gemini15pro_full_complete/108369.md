lavolta:/ləˈvɔːltə/ /ləˈvoʊltə/ | n. | 拉沃尔塔舞，快速旋转 | A lively dance for two persons, consisting of short, hopping steps and turns, popular in France and Italy in the 16th and 17th centuries. It involves a lot of turning and quick steps. | The couple danced a graceful lavolta. 这对夫妇跳了一支优雅的拉沃尔塔舞。 |  The king and queen impressed the court with their skillful lavolta. 国王和王后以他们娴熟的拉沃尔塔舞给宫廷留下了深刻的印象。 | 近义词：volta, galliard | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps slow dance) | 词性:noun
