dubs:/dʌbz/ | n. |配音; 复制品; 胜利;  |A victory, success, or accomplishment, especially in sports or competition. Also refers to recordings, particularly in music production, and can mean duplicate or copy.  From Jamaican Patois, influenced by "double". Originally referred to dubplates in reggae music. | The team secured the dubs in the final match.  该队在决赛中取得了胜利。 |  The producer was working on the dubs for the new album. 制作人正在为新专辑制作副本。| synonyms: victories, wins, triumphs, copies, duplicates | antonyms: losses, defeats, failures | noun

dubs:/dʌbz/ | v. (dubs, dubbing, dubbed) | 配音; 称呼; 授予称号; 轻拍 | To provide (a film, video, etc.) with a soundtrack in a different language, especially by replacing the original speech with a translation. Also, to give a nickname or title to someone or something.  From "double", referring to making a copy. | The film was dubbed into several languages. 这部电影被译制成多种语言。 | They dubbed him "The King." 他们称他为“国王”。 | synonyms: nickname, entitle, translate, re-record | antonyms:  (For dubbing a film) - keep original soundtrack | verb
