nowal:/ˈnoʊəl/| adj.| 无嫁妆的 (尤指新娘);|  Archaic. Lacking a dowry (especially of a bride).  Derived from the Old French word "noual," meaning "knot" or "bond," which, in this context, referred to the marriage bond established through the dowry.|  "She was considered a nowal bride, having no fortune to offer her husband." 她被认为是一个没有嫁妆的新娘，没有任何财产可以提供给她的丈夫。 | 近义词: dowerless| 反义词: dowered| 词性: adjective
