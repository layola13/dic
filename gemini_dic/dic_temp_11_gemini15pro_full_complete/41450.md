cornice:/ˈkɔːrnɪs/ (US), /ˈkɔːnɪs/ (UK)| n.|檐口，飞檐，雪檐|A projecting molding along the top of a wall or building, often crowning a wall or dividing it from the roof.|The cornice of the old building was intricately carved. 这栋古老建筑的檐口雕刻精美。|The heavy snow formed a cornice along the ridge of the roof. 厚厚的积雪在屋脊上形成了一个雪檐。|Synonyms: molding, ledge, eave|Antonyms: base, foundation|Noun
