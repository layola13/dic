demur:/dɪˈmɜːr/ (US) /dɪˈmɜː(r)/ (UK)| v. n.|反对，提出异议；犹豫|To express doubt or objection, often politely; to hesitate.|例句：She demurred at the idea of working on a Sunday. 她反对星期天工作。|近义词：object, protest, hesitate, waver|反义词：agree, assent, accept, comply|词性：动词

demur:/dɪˈmɜːr/ (US) /dɪˈmɜː(r)/ (UK)| n.|反对，异议|An objection or hesitation.|例句：Her demurs about the project were overruled. 她对这个项目的反对意见被否决了。|近义词：objection, protest, hesitation|反义词：agreement, assent, acceptance|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *demorer "to linger, delay, tarry," from Latin demorari "to linger, delay, tarry," from de- "down" + morari "to delay."
