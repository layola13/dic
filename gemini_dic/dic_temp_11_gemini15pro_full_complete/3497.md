afterness:/ˈæftərnɪs/
n.
后果，后继性，后续影响
The afterness of the storm was devastating.  暴风雨的后果是毁灭性的。
The afterness of the decision lingered for years.  这个决定的后续影响持续了数年。
The painting evokes a sense of afterness, a feeling of something having ended. 这幅画唤起了一种后继感，一种事情已经结束的感觉。
近义词：aftermath, consequence, result, sequel, repercussions
反义词：prelude, antecedent, foregoing
词性：名词
