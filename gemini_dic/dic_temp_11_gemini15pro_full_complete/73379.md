forehand:/ˈfɔːrˌhænd/ /ˈfɔːr.hænd/| n. adj. adv.|正手；正手的；用正手的|A stroke made with the palm of the hand facing forward.  A forehand shot is one played on the same side of the body as the hand holding the racket. |He hit a powerful forehand down the line. 他沿着边线打出一个强有力的正手球。|近义词: front hand |反义词: backhand|noun, adjective, adverb
