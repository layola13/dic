connaturality:/kɒnˌnætʃəˈrælɪti/ /kɑːnˌnætʃəˈrælɪti/
|n.|同质性；天赋性；天然性；自然属性|The state of being connatural; a sharing of the same nature.  A natural affinity or connection.|His connaturality with the land was evident in his paintings. 他对这片土地的天然情感在他的画作中显而易见。|Synonyms: affinity, kinship, inherentness, innate quality|Antonyms: unnaturalness, artificiality|noun
