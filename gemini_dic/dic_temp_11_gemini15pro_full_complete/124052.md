morphing:/ˈmɔːrfɪŋ/ | n. | 变形，变体，演变 |  The process of smoothly transforming one image into another, often used in special effects. | The morphing of the caterpillar into a butterfly is a fascinating process. 毛毛虫蜕变成蝴蝶是一个迷人的过程。 | synonyms: transforming, transmuting, metamorphosing | antonyms: stagnating, fixing, solidifying | noun

morph:/mɔːrf/ | v. | 变形，变化 | To change smoothly from one image or shape to another. | The software can morph one face into another.  这个软件可以将一张脸变形为另一张脸。| synonyms: transform, transmute, metamorphose | antonyms: stagnate, fix, solidify | verb
