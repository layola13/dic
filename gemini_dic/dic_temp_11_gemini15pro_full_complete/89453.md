honestation:/ˌɒnɪˈsteɪʃən/ /ˌɑːnɪˈsteɪʃən/
n.
诚实,正直,坦率
The quality or fact of being honest; uprightness of character or action.
His honesty and hard work earned him the respect of his colleagues. 他的诚实和努力工作赢得了同事们的尊重。
integrity, probity, rectitude, uprightness
dishonesty, deceit, duplicity
noun
