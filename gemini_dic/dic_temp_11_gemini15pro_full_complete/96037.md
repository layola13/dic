indebtedness:/ɪnˈdɛtɪdnəs/| n. | 债务；受恩惠；负债状态 |The state of owing something (usually money). A feeling of gratitude for a favor or service. |  The company's indebtedness increased significantly after the acquisition.  这家公司在收购后债务大幅增加。

The artist felt a deep sense of indebtedness to his mentor.  这位艺术家对他的导师深感感激。|  Synonyms: debt, liability, obligation, gratitude | Antonyms: credit, asset, independence | noun
