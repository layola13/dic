noncirculatory:/ˌnɒnsɜːrkjʊˈleɪtəri/
adjective
非循环的
Not relating to or involving circulation, especially of the blood.
The plant uses a noncirculatory system to transport nutrients.  这种植物使用非循环系统来运输营养物质。
The sponge's noncirculatory method of nutrient distribution relies on diffusion. 海绵的非循环营养分配方法依赖于扩散。
