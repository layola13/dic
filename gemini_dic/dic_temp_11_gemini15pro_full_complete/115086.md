manfully:/ˈmæn.fəl.i/| adv.|勇敢地；雄赳赳地；有男子气概地|In a brave, strong, and determined way, like a man who is considered to be brave. |He faced the challenge manfully. 他勇敢地面对挑战。|近义词：bravely, courageously, valiantly, boldly, heroically|反义词：cowardly, timidly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From Middle English *manfulli, equivalent to manful +‎ -ly.  Manful itself comes from Old English mannful. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *manną (“man”) + *fullaz (“full”).
