insectivores:/ɪnˈsɛktɪvɔːrz/ | n. |食虫动物|Animals that feed primarily on insects. |例句：Hedgehogs are insectivores. 刺猬是食虫动物。

例句：Bats are nocturnal insectivores.  蝙蝠是夜行性食虫动物。|近义词：insect-eaters|反义词:herbivores, carnivores|词性:名词

Etymology: From Latin *īnsectīvorus*, from *īnsectum* (insect) + *-vorus* (eating), from *vorāre* (to devour).
