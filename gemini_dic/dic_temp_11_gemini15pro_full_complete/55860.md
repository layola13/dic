divining:/dɪˈvaɪnɪŋ/ | n. | 占卜；预言 | The practice of seeking to foretell future events or discover hidden knowledge by supernatural means. | The old woman practiced divining with tea leaves. 老妇人用茶叶占卜。 | synonyms: prophecy, soothsaying, augury, fortune-telling | antonyms:  scientific inquiry, empirical observation | word type: noun
