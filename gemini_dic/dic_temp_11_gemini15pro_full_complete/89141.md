homefield:/ˈhoʊmˌfiːld/| n.|主场；主场优势|A team's home field is the ground or stadium where they usually play their home games. It also refers to the advantage a team has when playing on their home field.|The team has a strong homefield advantage. 这支球队有很强的 homefield advantage|近义词：home ground, home stadium, home court, home turf|反义词: away field, visitor's field|noun
