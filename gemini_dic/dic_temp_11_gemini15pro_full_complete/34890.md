clipping:/ˈklɪpɪŋ/| n.|剪报；剪辑；修剪|A piece of something cut or clipped off, such as a piece of hair, a newspaper article, or a plant cutting.  The act of cutting something off. |例句：She kept a clipping of the article about her son. 她保留了关于她儿子的文章的剪报。

例句：The gardener demonstrated the proper clipping of rose bushes. 园丁演示了玫瑰丛的正确修剪方法。|近义词：snippet, cutting, trim, excerpt|反义词:extension, lengthening|noun


clipping:/ˈklɪpɪŋ/| adj.|快速的；敏捷的；简洁的|Moving quickly; brisk.  Terse and concise. |例句：He walked at a clipping pace. 他步伐轻快地走着。

例句：Her clipping tone suggested she was annoyed. 她简洁的语气暗示她很生气。|近义词：quick, brisk, rapid, swift, concise, terse|反义词:slow, leisurely, verbose|adjective
