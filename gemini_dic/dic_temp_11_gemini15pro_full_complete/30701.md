certified:/ˈsɜːrtɪfaɪd/ /ˈsɝːtɪfaɪd/| adj.|被证明的；被认证的；合格的|Officially recognized as possessing certain qualifications or meeting certain standards.|例句：She is a certified public accountant. 她是一位注册会计师。|例句：The product is certified organic. 该产品获得了有机认证。|近义词：accredited, approved, licensed, authorized|反义词：uncertified, unauthorized|词性:形容词

certify:/ˈsɜːrtɪfaɪ/ /ˈsɝːtɪfaɪ/| vt.|证明；认证；保证|To officially recognize someone or something as possessing certain qualifications or meeting certain standards.|例句：The document certifies his birth in this country.  这份文件证明他出生在这个国家。|例句：I can certify that the information is correct. 我可以保证这些信息是正确的。|近义词：confirm, verify, attest, guarantee|反义词：deny, refute|词性:动词

certificate:/sərˈtɪfɪkət/ /sɚˈtɪfɪkət/| n.|证明；证书；执照|An official document attesting to a fact.|例句：He received a certificate of completion for the course. 他获得了该课程的结业证书。|例句：She needed a birth certificate to apply for a passport. 她需要出生证明来申请护照。|近义词：document, diploma, license, permit|反义词： N/A|词性:名词

词源: From Latin *certus* (“settled, determined, resolved”) + -ficare.
