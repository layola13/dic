hydrargochloride:/ˌhaɪdrɑːrɡoʊˈklɔːraɪd/
noun
氯化亚汞; 甘汞
A white, odorless powder, Hg2Cl2, used as a fungicide, antiseptic, and cathartic.  Also called calomel.
Calomel (hydrargochloride) was formerly used as a purgative. 甘汞（氯化亚汞）以前用作泻药。
Synonyms: calomel, mercurous chloride
Antonyms: None (Chemical compound, doesn't have a direct opposite)
