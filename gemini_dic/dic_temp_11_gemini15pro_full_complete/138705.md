nullification:/ˌnʌlɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n.|废除，无效，取消|The act of nullifying; the state of being nullified.  The doctrine that a state can refuse to recognize or enforce a federal law passed by the United States Congress.|The southern states threatened nullification of the federal tariffs.|近义词：abrogation, repeal, revocation, invalidation|反义词：ratification, validation, confirmation|noun
