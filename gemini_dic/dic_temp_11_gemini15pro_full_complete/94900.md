in-force:/ɪn ˈfɔːrs/| adj.|有效的，生效的，现行的|Currently operating and legally binding.  Something that is in force is currently active and effective.|The new regulations are now in force. 新规章现已生效。|Synonyms: valid, effective, current, operational|Antonyms: invalid, expired, obsolete|adjective

in force:/ɪn ˈfɔːrs/| adv.|大规模地，大批地，全体出动地|In large numbers; in great strength.|The police were out in force at the demonstration.  警察在示威现场大批出动。|Synonyms: en masse, in large numbers, in great strength|Antonyms: sparsely, individually|adverb
