ETYMOLOGY->
From  "exclusion" + "-ist".  "Exclusion" comes from the Latin "exclusio," meaning "a shutting out," from "excludere" ("to shut out").  "-ist" is a suffix forming nouns and adjectives denoting a person who practices or adheres to a particular doctrine or system or is associated with a particular quality, thing, or place.
