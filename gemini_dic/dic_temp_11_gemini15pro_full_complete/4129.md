air-drop:/ˈeərdɹɒp/ (UK) /ˈeərdrɑːp/ (US)| n. v.|空投；空投物资；空降|An act of dropping supplies or people from the air, typically by parachute.  A delivery of something (usually digital) over a wireless network.|The army organized an air-drop of food and medical supplies to the refugees.  军队组织了向难民空投食物和医疗用品。 I received an air-drop of cryptocurrency to my digital wallet. 我收到了空投到我数字钱包的加密货币。|近义词：paradrop, airlift|反义词: pickup, ground delivery|词性: noun, verb
