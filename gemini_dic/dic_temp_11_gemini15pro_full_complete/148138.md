oversensible:/ˈoʊvərˈsɛnsəbəl/
adjective
过分敏感的；神经质的
Excessively sensitive; easily offended or upset.
She's oversensible and takes everything to heart. 她过于敏感，什么事都往心里去。
近义词:sensitive, touchy, thin-skinned
反义词:insensitive, thick-skinned
词性:adjective

oversensible:/ˈoʊvərˈsɛnsəbəl/
adjective
过分理智的；过于拘谨的
Excessively practical or restrained in one's feelings or behavior.
He was too oversensible to show any emotion. 他过于理智，不会流露出任何情感。
近义词:prudent, cautious, reserved
反义词:imprudent, reckless, impulsive
词性:adjective
