calamities:/kəˈlæmətiz/| n. |灾难，灾祸|A serious event causing great and often sudden damage or distress; a disaster.|The country has suffered a series of natural calamities, including floods and earthquakes. 这个国家遭受了一系列自然灾害，包括洪水和地震。|近义词：disasters, catastrophes, misfortunes, tragedies|反义词: blessings, fortunes|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin *calamitās, from Latin calamitās (“damage, loss, injury, misfortune”), from calamus (“reed, stalk of grain”). Doublet of haulm.
