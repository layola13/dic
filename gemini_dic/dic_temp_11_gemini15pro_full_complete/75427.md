fruitarianism:/fruːˈtɛəriənɪzəm/
n.
果食主义
The practice of eating only fruits, nuts, and seeds, considered by some to be a subset of veganism.
例句：Fruitarianism is a restrictive diet that can be difficult to maintain. 果食主义是一种限制性很强的饮食，很难长期坚持。

例句：She adopted fruitarianism for ethical and health reasons. 她出于伦理和健康的原因采用了果食主义。

近义词：veganism, vegetarianism
反义词：omnivorism, carnivorism
词性：noun

Etymology:  From fruit + -arian + -ism.  "Fruit" comes from Old French, ultimately from Latin "fructus." "-arian" denotes a person who practices or adheres to something, while "-ism" denotes a doctrine, belief, or practice.
