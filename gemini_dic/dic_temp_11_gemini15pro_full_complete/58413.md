duboisia:/djuːˈbɔɪziə/ | n. | 杜波依斯属；洋金花属 | A genus of small trees and shrubs in the family Solanaceae, native to Australia. Several species contain potent alkaloids and are used medicinally. | Duboisia hopwoodii is used by Aboriginal Australians.  澳洲原住民使用杜波依斯洋金花。 | Duboisia is a source of scopolamine. 杜波依斯属植物是东莨菪碱的来源。 |  synonym: corkwood tree (for some species) | antonym: N/A | noun

duboisia:/djuːˈbɔɪziə/ | n. | 洋金花属 | Referring to a plant of the genus Duboisia. 指洋金花属的植物。 |  The duboisia plant is highly toxic.  这种洋金花属植物有剧毒。| The leaves of the duboisia contain several alkaloids. 洋金花属植物的叶子含有多种生物碱。 | synonym: pituri (for *Duboisia hopwoodii*) | antonym: N/A | noun
