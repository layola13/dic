overelaborating: /ˌoʊvərɪˈlæbəreɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度阐述; 过分详细说明
explaining or describing something in excessive detail, often to the point of confusion or boredom.
She was overelaborating on the details of the trip, and I started to zone out. 她过度详细地描述了旅行的细节，我开始走神了。
The witness overelaborated his story, making the jury suspicious.  证人过度详细地讲述了他的故事，让陪审团产生了怀疑。
近义词: overexplaining, belaboring, dwelling on
反义词: summarizing, simplifying, condensing
词性: verb (present participle)

overelaborate: /ˌoʊvərɪˈlæbəreɪt/
verb (transitive & intransitive) | adjective
过度阐述; 过分详细说明 | 过于复杂的; 过于精细的
(verb) to explain or describe something in excessive detail. (adjective) excessively elaborate or detailed.
(v) He overelaborated the point, losing the audience's attention. 他过度阐述了这一点，失去了听众的注意力。
(v) Don't overelaborate; just tell me the basics.  不要过度阐述；只要告诉我基本情况就行了。
(adj) Her outfit was a bit overelaborate for a casual lunch. 她的服装对于一顿便餐来说有点过于复杂了。
(adj) The plot of the movie was overelaborate and confusing.  这部电影的情节过于复杂，令人困惑。
近义词: (v) overexplain, belabor, dwell on; (adj) complex, intricate, fussy
反义词: (v) simplify, summarize, condense; (adj) simple, basic, minimalist
词性: verb, adjective

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "elaborate" (from Latin elaboratus, past participle of elaborare "to work out, produce by labor," from e- "out" + laborare "to labor")
