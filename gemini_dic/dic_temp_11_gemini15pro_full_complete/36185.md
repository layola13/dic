cofinances:/ˌkoʊˈfaɪnænsɪz/ /ˌkəʊˈfaɪnænsɪz/
verb
共同 финансирует; 共同出资
provides funding for a project or transaction together with another lender or investor.
The World Bank cofinances several development projects in Africa.  世界银行与其他机构共同出资在非洲开展多个发展项目。

The project is cofinanced by the government and several private companies. 该项目由政府和几家私营公司共同出资。
synonyms: funds jointly, shares the cost of, contributes to, subsidizes
antonyms: solely funds, fully funds, exclusively funds
词性: verb
