impacted:/ɪmˈpæktɪd/| v.| 冲击，撞击，压紧，嵌入(impact的过去式和过去分词)|Past tense and past participle of "impact," meaning to hit forcefully or to pack tightly.|例句：The meteor impacted the Earth millions of years ago.  数百万年前，这颗流星撞击了地球。|近义词：hit, strike, collide with, press, pack|反义词：miss, separate, loosen|词性:动词
