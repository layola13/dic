detailed:/ˈdiːteɪld/ /dɪˈteɪld/ | adj. | 详细的，精细的 | Showing or including many small facts or pieces of information | The report provided a detailed analysis of the company's finances.  这份报告对公司的财务状况进行了详细的分析。| synonyms: comprehensive, thorough, in-depth, meticulous, exhaustive | antonyms: brief, concise, general, superficial, cursory | adjective

Etymology:
From Middle French *detailler ("to cut into pieces"), from de- + tailler ("to cut").  Ultimately from Latin *talea ("a cutting, rod, stick").  The original sense was to cut into pieces.
