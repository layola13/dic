corruptingly:/kəˈrʌptɪŋli/
adverb
腐败地；败坏地
in a way that corrupts or makes something worse morally
例句：The officials were accused of acting corruptingly. 官员们被指控腐败行事。

例句：Power corruptingly influences some people.  权力腐败地影响了一些人。


近义词：dishonestly, fraudulently, wickedly
反义词：honestly, ethically, virtuously
词性: adverb
