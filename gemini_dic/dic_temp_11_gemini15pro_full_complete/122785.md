molasses:/məˈlæsɪz/| n.| 糖蜜 |a thick, dark brown syrup obtained from raw sugar during the refining process, a version of which is used in baking.| Grandma's gingerbread cookies are always perfect, thanks to her secret ingredient: molasses. 奶奶的姜饼总是很完美，多亏了她的秘方：糖蜜。| synonyms: treacle, syrup | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a viscous liquid, but one could consider refined sugar as an opposite in terms of processing level.) | noun

molasseses:/məˈlæsɪz/| n.| 糖蜜（复数，罕用）| plural form of molasses, though often treated as a mass noun and therefore not pluralized |  Some old recipes call for molasseses, but molasses is the more common form. 一些古老的食谱会用到molasseses这个复数形式，但molasses是更常见的形式。 | synonyms: treacle, syrup | antonyms: (No direct antonym, similar to molasses) | noun
