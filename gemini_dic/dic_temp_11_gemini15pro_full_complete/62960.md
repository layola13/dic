ennoblement:/ɪˈnoʊblmənt/
n.
赋予贵族称号；使高贵
The act of ennobling or the state of being ennobled.
His ennoblement as a duke was a great honor. 他被封为公爵是一项极大的荣誉。
elevation, dignification, exaltation
degradation,abasement
noun
