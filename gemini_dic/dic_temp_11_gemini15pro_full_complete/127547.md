例句：The necrophore quickly buried the dead mouse. 埋葬虫迅速地埋葬了死老鼠。

necrophoresis: /ˌnɛkrəˈfɔːrɪsɪs/ | n. | 搬运死尸 | The practice of certain social insects, such as ants, of carrying away dead members of the colony. | 例句：Necrophoresis helps maintain hygiene within the ant colony. 搬运死尸有助于维持蚁群内的卫生。

例句：Researchers observed the necrophoresis behavior of the ants in detail. 研究人员详细观察了蚂蚁搬运死尸的行为。

近义词：(No direct synonym, perhaps "corpse removal") | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性：noun
