lists:/lɪsts/ | n. | 列表，清单 | A series of names, words, or other items written or printed together in a meaningful grouping or sequence. |  I made a list of things to buy at the grocery store. 我列了一个在杂货店要买的东西的清单。 | synonyms: inventory, register, catalog, roster | antonyms: disorder, disorganization | noun
