ammerman:/ˈæmərˌmæn/ | n. | 锤子匠，铁匠 | A person who makes or repairs hammers or other metal tools. |  Blacksmiths and ammermen were essential in the pre-industrial era.  铁匠和锤子匠在工业化前时代是必不可少的。| Synonym: blacksmith, smith, metalworker | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps idler, non-worker) | noun
