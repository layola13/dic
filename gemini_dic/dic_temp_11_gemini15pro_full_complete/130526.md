nonaccretive:/ˌnɒnəˈkriːtɪv/
adjective
非增生的
Not accretive; tending to decrease in size or quantity.  Specifically used in mathematics and finance to describe a function or process that doesn't increase a certain quantity or value.
The nonaccretive nature of the investment led to significant losses. 这项投资的非增值性导致了重大损失。
The model incorporates nonaccretive forces that counteract growth. 该模型包含了抵消增长的非增值力量。
近义词:diminishing, decreasing, reductive
反义词:accretive, increasing, augmentative
词性:adjective

词源:
Prefix "non-" (not) + "accretive" (growing by addition).  "Accretive" comes from Latin "accrēscere" (to grow, increase), from "ad-" (to) + "crēscere" (to grow).
