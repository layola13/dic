half-madness:/ˌhæf ˈmædnəs/ | n. | 半疯狂；精神错乱| A state of being partially insane; a condition marked by erratic behavior and irrational thoughts. | He seemed to be in a state of half-madness, muttering to himself and pacing back and forth. 他似乎处于半疯狂状态，自言自语，来回踱步。 | The artist's half-madness was reflected in his disturbingly beautiful paintings. 这位艺术家的半疯狂状态体现在他那些美得令人不安的画作中。 | 近义词: partial insanity, dementia, derangement | 反义词: sanity, lucidity | 词性: 名词


Etymology:
"half-" (Old English healf) + "madness" (Middle English madnesse, from mad + -ness)
