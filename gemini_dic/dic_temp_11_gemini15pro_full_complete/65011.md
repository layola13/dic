estrogens: /ˈɛstrədʒənz/ | n. | 雌激素 | Any of a group of steroid hormones that promote the development and maintenance of female characteristics of the body. These hormones are mainly produced by the ovaries in women. |  High levels of estrogens are essential for normal reproductive function in women. 高水平的雌激素对女性正常的生殖功能至关重要。| synonyms: female sex hormones | antonyms: androgens | noun
