footprints:/ˈfʊtˌprɪnts/| n. |足迹，脚印|Marks left by a foot or shoe, often used figuratively to refer to the impact or influence someone has.|例句：The children's footprints were all over the wet cement. 孩子们的脚印遍布潮湿的水泥地上。

|例句：His footprints in the sand were quickly washed away by the tide. 他在沙滩上的脚印很快就被潮水冲走了。|近义词：tracks, marks, impressions|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

footprints:/ˈfʊtˌprɪnts/| n. |影响，痕迹|The lasting effects or evidence of someone's actions or presence.|例句：The company's footprints can be seen in many countries around the world.  这家公司的影响力遍及世界许多国家。

|例句：She left her footprints on the world through her philanthropy and activism. 她通过慈善事业和行动主义在世界上留下了自己的印记。 |近义词：legacy, impact, influence|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
