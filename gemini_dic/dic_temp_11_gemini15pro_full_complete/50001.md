denlike:/ˈdɛnˌlaɪk/| adj.|类似兽穴的；隐蔽的|Resembling a den; secluded and cozy.|The room was dark and denlike, perfect for reading. 房间很暗，像兽穴一样，很适合阅读。|例句：The hermit lived in a denlike cave in the mountains.  隐士住在山里一个像兽穴一样的洞穴里。

例句：Her office was small and denlike, filled with books and papers. 她的办公室又小又像兽穴，里面堆满了书籍和文件。|近义词：cozy, snug, secluded, cavelike|反义词：spacious, open, exposed|词性:形容词
