magnetiferous:/ˌmæɡnɪˈtɪfərəs/
adj.
含铁的，有磁性的
Containing magnetic iron or other magnetic material; capable of becoming magnetic.
The magnetiferous rocks of the region have attracted the attention of geologists. 该地区的含铁岩石吸引了地质学家的注意。
近义词:magnetic, ferruginous
反义词:nonmagnetic
