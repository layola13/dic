domiciling: /ˈdɒmɪsaɪlɪŋ/ | n. | domiciling (通常用作 gerund)  定居;  (尤指为避税) 选择居住地 | The act of establishing a domicile, especially for tax purposes. |  Example:  Domiciling in a tax haven can have legal and ethical implications.  在避税天堂定居可能有法律和伦理方面的影响。

Example:  He considered domiciling abroad for a lower tax rate.  他考虑为了较低的税率而移居国外。 | Synonym: residing, living, settling, inhabiting | Antonym: emigrating, leaving, departing | gerund (动名词)

domicile: /ˈdɒmɪsaɪl/ | n. vt.|  住所，居所；定居 |  A person's fixed, permanent, and principal home for legal purposes.  | Example: He changed his domicile to avoid paying higher taxes. 他为了避免缴纳更高的税款而更改了住所。

Example:  The company's domicile is in Delaware.  这家公司的注册地是特拉华州。| Synonym: residence, abode, dwelling, home | Antonym:  N/A | noun, verb
