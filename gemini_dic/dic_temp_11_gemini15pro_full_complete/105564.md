kinaesodic:/ˌkɪnɪˈsɒdɪk/ | adj. | 运动觉的，动觉的 | Relating to kinaesthesia, the sense of movement and position of the body. |  She has a strong kinaesodic memory, easily recalling dance routines. 她拥有很强的动觉记忆，能轻松回忆起舞蹈动作。|  Kinaesodic learners benefit from hands-on activities. 动觉型学习者受益于动手活动。| 近义词: kinesthetic, proprioceptive | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider terms relating to lack of bodily awareness) | 词性: adjective
