leather-like:/ˈleðərˌlaɪk/| adj.|类似皮革的；像皮革的|Resembling leather in appearance or texture.|This sofa has a leather-like cover.  这个沙发有一个类似皮革的套子。|The material feels leather-like.  这种材料摸起来像皮革。|近义词：leathery, leatherish, faux leather|反义词: unlike leather |词性: adjective
词源:  Compound of "leather" + "-like" (suffix indicating resemblance). "Leather" comes from Old English "lether", of Germanic origin. "-like" comes from Old English "-līc", meaning "having the appearance or form of."
