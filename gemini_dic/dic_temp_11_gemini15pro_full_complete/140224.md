officiating: /əˈfɪʃieɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
主持；担任裁判；执行公务
acting as an official in charge of something, especially a sporting event or religious service
The referee was officiating at the World Cup final.  这位裁判在世界杯决赛中担任裁判。
He officiated at their wedding. 他主持了他们的婚礼。
Synonyms: refereeing, umpiring, presiding, adjudicating
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider phrases like "being a spectator," "not participating," "being absent")
词性: 动词 (present participle)
