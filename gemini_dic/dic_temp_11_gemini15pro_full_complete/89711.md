Simple Present:
I hope for good weather tomorrow. 我希望明天天气好。
He hopes to finish the project on time. 他希望按时完成这个项目。


Present Continuous:
I am hoping for a promotion. 我正期望着升职。
They are hoping that the rain will stop. 他们正期望着雨会停。


Present Perfect:
I have hoped for this day for a long time. 我很久以来一直盼望着这一天。
She has hoped to travel the world. 她一直希望环游世界。


Present Perfect Continuous:
I have been hoping for a change in my life. 我一直希望我的生活能有所改变。
They have been hoping for a miracle. 他们一直期待着奇迹发生。


Simple Past:
I hoped for a better outcome. 我曾希望有一个更好的结果。
He hoped to win the lottery. 他曾希望中彩票。


Past Continuous:
I was hoping you would call. 我那时正希望你会打电话来。
She was hoping for some good news. 她那时正期望着好消息。


Past Perfect:
I had hoped to finish the book before the weekend. 我曾希望在周末前看完这本书。
She had hoped to see him one last time. 她曾希望最后再见到他一次。


Past Perfect Continuous:
I had been hoping for a reconciliation. 我一直希望能够和好。
They had been hoping for a sign. 他们一直期待着一个信号。


Simple Future:
I will hope for the best. 我会抱最好的希望。
He will hope to see you there. 他会希望在那里见到你。


Future Continuous:
I will be hoping for a quick recovery. 我会期望着早日康复。
They will be hoping for good news. 他们会期望着好消息。


Future Perfect:
By next year, I will have hoped for a long time. 到明年，我就已经希望很久了。 (此句意义略显重复，更常见的用法是表达其他完成状态)
By then, he will have hoped to have achieved his goals. 到那时，他将希望已经实现了目标。


Future Perfect Continuous:
By next month, I will have been hoping for this opportunity for a year. 到下个月，我就已经希望得到这个机会一年了。
By the time she graduates, she will have been hoping to become a doctor for many years. 到她毕业的时候，她就已经希望成为一名医生很多年了。


Subjunctive Mood:
If I were to hope for anything, it would be world peace. 如果我要希望什么，那就是世界和平。
I wish I could hope for a better future. 我希望我能对未来抱有更好的希望。


Passive Voice:
It is hoped that the situation will improve. 人们希望情况会好转。
It was hoped that the meeting would be productive. 人们曾希望会议富有成效。


Interrogative Sentence:
What do you hope for? 你希望什么？
What were you hoping to achieve? 你希望达到什么目的？


Negative Sentence:
I don't hope for much. 我不抱太大希望。
She didn't hope to win. 她没希望赢。
