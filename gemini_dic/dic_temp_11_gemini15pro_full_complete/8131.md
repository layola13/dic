anti-bolsheviki: /ˌæntiˌbɒlʃəˈviːki/ /ˌæntiˌboʊlʃəˈviːki/
noun
反布尔什维克主义者；反布尔什维克的
A person or group opposed to Bolshevism.  Referring to actions, policies, or sentiments directed against the Bolsheviks.
The White Army was largely composed of anti-bolsheviki forces. 白军主要由反布尔什维克势力组成。
Anti-bolsheviki sentiment was widespread amongst the aristocracy. 反布尔什维克情绪在贵族阶层中很普遍。
近义词: counter-revolutionary, anti-communist
反义词: Bolshevik, communist, pro-Soviet
词性: n. adj.
