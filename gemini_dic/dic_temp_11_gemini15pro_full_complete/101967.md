isobars:/ˈaɪsəʊbɑːrz/
noun
等压线
A line on a map connecting points having the same atmospheric pressure at a given time or on average over a given period.
Isobars are shown on weather maps to connect points of equal atmospheric pressure. 等压线在地图上连接气压相同的点。
The isobars on the weather map are close together, indicating a steep pressure gradient and strong winds. 天气图上的等压线彼此靠近，表明气压梯度陡峭且风力强劲。
Synonyms: contour lines (of equal pressure)
Antonyms: None (Isobars represent a specific value; there's no direct opposite concept.)
