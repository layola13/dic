overobjectified:/ˌoʊvərəbˈdʒɛktɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 过度物化的，过度客体化的 |  Turned into an object, especially a sexual object, to an excessive degree.  This word emphasizes the objectification beyond a normal or acceptable level. |  The models in the advertisement were overobjectified, reducing them to mere props.  广告中的模特被过度物化，沦为纯粹的道具。 | Example: The actress felt overobjectified by the media's constant focus on her appearance.  这位女演员感到媒体不断关注她的外表，让她感到被过度物化。 | Synonyms: oversexualized, dehumanized, commodified | Antonyms: humanized, respected, individualized | Word type: Adjective
