marls:/mɑːrlz/
marl:/mɑːrl/ | n. | 泥灰；泥灰土；泥灰岩 | A crumbly mixture of clay, sand, and calcium carbonate. | The farmer enriched his fields with marl.  这位农民用泥灰改良了他的土地。 | synonyms: clay, loam, silt | antonyms: rock, stone | noun

marls:/mɑːrlz/ | n. | (通常指红色的)泥灰岩 | (often red) marly soil or rock. | The marls in this region are rich in fossils.  这个地区的泥灰岩富含化石。| synonyms: claystone, mudstone | antonyms: granite, basalt | noun

marls:/mɑːrlz/ | v. | 用泥灰施肥 | To fertilize with marl. | He marled his fields every few years.  他每隔几年就用泥灰给田地施肥。 | synonyms: fertilize, enrich, amend | antonyms: deplete, impoverish | verb
