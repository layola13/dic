expire:/ɪkˈspaɪər/ (UK), /ɛkˈspaɪər/ (US)| v. |到期，失效；死亡；呼气| to come to an end; to die; to breathe out| My passport expires next month. 我的护照下个月到期。|Synonyms: cease, terminate, lapse, run out, die, perish |Antonyms: renew, begin, start, live|verb

expire:/ɪkˈspaɪər/ (UK), /ɛkˈspaɪər/ (US)| n. |到期，终止| the date or time at which something ends or becomes no longer valid| The expiry date of the milk is tomorrow. 牛奶的有效期到明天。|Synonyms: termination, expiry, end|Antonyms: beginning, start, commencement|noun
