mummichog:/ˈmʌmɪˌtʃɒɡ/ (US), /ˈmʌmɪtʃɒɡ/ (UK)| n.|鳉鱼|A small killifish (Fundulus heteroclitus) found in salt marshes and brackish waters along the Atlantic coast of North America.|例句：The mummichog is a hardy fish that can tolerate a wide range of salinities. 鳉鱼是一种耐寒的鱼，可以忍受各种盐度。

例句：Researchers often use the mummichog in studies of environmental adaptation. 研究人员经常在环境适应性研究中使用鳉鱼。|近义词：killifish|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
