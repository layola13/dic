bleak:/blik/
adjective
荒凉的；阴冷的；凄凉的；暗淡的
lacking vegetation and exposed to the elements; cold and miserable; depressing and cheerless; without hope or encouragement.
例句：The future looked bleak for the company. 这家公司的前景看起来很暗淡。
例句：The landscape was bleak and desolate. 景色荒凉凄凉。
近义词：desolate, barren, grim, dreary, depressing
反义词：cheerful, bright, promising, hopeful
词性: adjective

bleak:/blik/
adjective
(of the weather) cold and miserable.
(指天气)阴冷的
例句：The weather was bleak and windy. 天气阴冷多风。
例句：A bleak midwinter's day. 一个寒冷凄凉的冬日。
近义词：cold, raw, chilly, harsh
反义词：warm, mild, pleasant
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bleik, from Old Norse *bleikr (“pale, wan”), from Proto-Germanic *blaikaz (“pale, shining, white”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰleg- (“to shine, flash, burn”). Cognate with Icelandic bleikur (“pale”), Swedish blek (“pale”), Dutch bleek (“pale”), German bleich (“pale”).
