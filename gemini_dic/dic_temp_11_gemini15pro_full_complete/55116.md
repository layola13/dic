disrupters:/dɪsˈrʌptərz/| n.|干扰者，破坏者，颠覆者|People or things that cause disruption or upheaval in an established system or industry.  A person or thing that interrupts an event, activity, or process.|例句：Technological disruptors are changing the face of the music industry.  科技颠覆者正在改变音乐产业的面貌。
