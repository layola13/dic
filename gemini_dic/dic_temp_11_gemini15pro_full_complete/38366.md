concentricate:/ˈkɒnsəntreɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsəntreɪt/ (US)| v. |集中；聚集；浓缩|to focus all one's attention on something; to bring or draw to a common center or point; to make (a solution or mixture) less dilute by removing some of the solvent|例句：I need to concentrate on my work. 我需要集中精力工作。|近义词：focus, center, condense|反义词: disperse, scatter|词性:动词

concentricate:/ˈkɒnsəntreɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsəntreɪt/ (US)| n. |浓缩物|a concentrated form of something|例句：The orange concentrate is mixed with water to make juice.  橙汁浓缩物与水混合制成果汁。|近义词：extract, essence|反义词: dilution|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin concentratus, past participle of concentrare "to join in a center," from com- "together" + centrum "center."
