outsallied:/ˌaʊtˈsælid/
verb
突然袭击;出击;突围而出
To sally forth more effectively or successfully than another.  To surpass in making a sortie.
The besieged troops outsallied the enemy and routed them. 被围困的部队突袭敌人并击溃了他们。
近义词: outflank, overcome, defeat, conquer
反义词: surrender, yield, retreat
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
