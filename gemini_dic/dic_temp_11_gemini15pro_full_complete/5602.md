alterer:/ˈɔːltərər/ (US) /ˈɔːltərə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 修改者，变更者|A person who alters something; someone who makes changes or adjustments.|The alterer of the document was never identified. 修改文件的人始终未被确认。|Example: The tailor was a skilled alterer of clothes.  裁缝是一位技艺精湛的衣服修改者。

|Synonyms: modifier, changer, adjuster, editor, reviser|Antonyms: preserver, keeper, maintainer|Noun
