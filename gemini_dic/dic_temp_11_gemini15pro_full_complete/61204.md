elvers:/ˈɛlvərz/
n.
幼鳗，鳗线
Young eels, especially those migrating upstream from the sea.
Elvers are a delicacy in some parts of the world.  幼鳗在世界某些地方被视为美味佳肴。
Fishermen catch elvers as they migrate upstream. 渔民在幼鳗洄游上游时捕捞它们。
近义词:young eels, glass eels
反义词:adult eels
词性:名词
