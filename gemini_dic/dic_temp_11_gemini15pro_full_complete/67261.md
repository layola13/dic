expurgatorial:/ˌekspərˈɡeɪtɔːriəl/
adjective
净化（的）；删节（的）
Relating to or involving the removal of objectionable material from a book, film, etc.
The editor made several expurgatorial changes to the manuscript before publication. 编辑在出版前对手稿进行了几处净化修改。

The expurgatorial version of the novel omitted several sexually explicit scenes.  这本小说的删节版省略了几个露骨的性爱场景。


Near Synonyms:  censoring, bowdlerizing, sanitizing, purifying, cleansing
Near Antonyms:  unabridged, unexpurgated, uncensored, complete, integral
Word Family: expurgate (verb), expurgation (noun)
