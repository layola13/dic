inoffensively: /ˌɪnəˈfɛnsɪvli/ | adv. | 无冒犯地；不讨厌地 | Not causing any offence or annoyance. | She dressed inoffensively in a simple grey suit. 她穿着朴素的灰色套装，显得不引人注目。| He made an inoffensive remark about the weather. 他就天气说了句无关痛痒的话。 | 近义词: harmlessly, innocuously, discreetly | 反义词: offensively, rudely, aggressively | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From in- (not) + offensive + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Offensive" comes from Old French "offensif," from Late Latin "offensivus," from Latin "offendere" (to strike against, offend).
