blubberers: /ˈblʌbərərz/
n.
爱哭鬼，哭诉者
People who blubber; those who cry noisily and excessively.
The blubberers were escorted out of the courtroom.  那些哭诉者被护送出法庭。
近义词: crybabies, weepers, wailers
反义词: stoics
词性: 名词

blubber: /ˈblʌbər/
v.
大声哭，哭诉
To weep loudly and uncontrollably.
He blubbered like a child when he heard the bad news.  他听到坏消息后像个孩子一样大哭起来。
近义词: weep, cry, sob, wail
反义词: laugh, rejoice
词性: 动词

blubber: /ˈblʌbər/
n.
鲸脂
The fat of sea mammals, especially whales.
Whales use blubber for insulation. 鲸鱼用鲸脂保暖。
近义词: fat, lipid
反义词: (No direct antonym for this sense)
词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle Dutch *blubberen "to bubble," probably of imitative origin.  The noun meaning "whale fat" comes from the resemblance of the melted fat to bubbles.
