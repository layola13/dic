offspring: /ˈɔːfsprɪŋ/ (US) /ˈɒfsprɪŋ/ (UK)
n.
子孙，后代；产物
A person's child or children.  Something that is produced as a result of a process or activity.
例句：The farm animals produce healthy offspring. 农场里的动物产下了健康的后代。
例句：This book is the offspring of many years of research. 这本书是多年研究的成果。
近义词：children, progeny, descendants, issue, result, product
反义词：parent, ancestor
词性：名词

词源：
