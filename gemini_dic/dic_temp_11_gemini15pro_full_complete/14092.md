autarchies:/ˈɔːtɑːrkiːz/
n.
专制统治；自给自足的国家或地区
A system of government in which one person has absolute power; a country or region that is economically self-sufficient.
The small island nation declared itself an autarky, refusing all foreign aid.  这个小岛国宣布自给自足，拒绝一切外国援助。
Many dictatorships strive for economic autarchies, seeking to minimize dependence on other nations. 许多独裁国家都力求经济自给自足，以最大限度地减少对其他国家的依赖。
近义词:dictatorships, totalitarian states, closed economies
反义词:democracies, open economies, interdependent states
词性:名词 (plural)

autarchy:/ˈɔːtɑːrki/
n.
专制统治；自给自足
A system of government in which one person has absolute power; a country or region that is economically self-sufficient.
The country descended into autarchy after the coup. 政变后，这个国家沦为专制统治。
The leader envisioned an autarchy where the nation would be completely self-reliant. 这位领导人设想了一个国家完全自力更生的自给自足状态。
近义词:absolutism, despotism, self-sufficiency
反义词:democracy, dependence, interdependence
词性:名词 (singular)


词源: From Greek αὐτάρκεια (autarkeia), from αὐτάρκης (autarkēs, “self-sufficient”), from αὐτός (autos, “self”) + ἀρκέω (arkeō, “to suffice”).
