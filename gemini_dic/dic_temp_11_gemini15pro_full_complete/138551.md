nozzles:/ˈnɑːzəlz/ (US), /ˈnɒzəlz/ (UK)| n.|喷嘴; 管嘴;|A projecting spout, as of a hose or pipe, by which a flow of liquid or gas may be directed and controlled.|The garden hose has adjustable nozzles for different watering needs.  花园水管有可调节的喷嘴，以满足不同的浇水需求。|synonyms: spout, jet, tip, aperture|antonyms: inlet, intake|noun
