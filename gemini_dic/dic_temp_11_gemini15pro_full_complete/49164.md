dekagram:/ˈdekəɡræm/ /ˈdɛkəɡræm/| n. |十克|A unit of mass equal to 10 grams.|The recipe calls for one dekagram of saffron.  这个食谱需要十克藏红花。|近义词：decagram, 10 grams|反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement)|noun
Etymology: From French *décagramme*, from *déca-* (ten) + *-gramme* (gram). Ultimately from Greek *δέκα* (deka, “ten”) + *γράμμα* (gramma, “small weight”).
