interroad:/ˈɪntərˌroʊd/| n.|小路，岔路; (尤指)林间小路|A small road, especially one within a forest or other wooded area. |We took a scenic walk along the interroad, enjoying the dappled sunlight filtering through the leaves. 我们沿着林间小路漫步，欣赏着透过树叶洒落的斑驳阳光。|The interroad led us to a hidden clearing where we set up camp. 这条小路把我们带到了一片隐蔽的空地，我们就在那里扎营了。|path, track, trail, byway|highway, main road|noun
