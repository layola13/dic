coinable:/ˈkɔɪnəbl/| adj.|可铸造的，可创造的（新词等）|Able to be coined (made into coins); (of a word) able to be created and used.|例句：The gold is coinable.  这黄金可铸造。|例句：Many new technical terms are highly coinable. 许多新的技术术语很容易创造。|近义词：mintable, creatable, neologizable|反义词:uncoined|词性:形容词
