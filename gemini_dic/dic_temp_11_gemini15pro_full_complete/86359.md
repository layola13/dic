hectolitre:/ˈhɛktəˌliːtər/ (US) /ˈhɛktəʊˌliːtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |百升；一百公升|A unit of volume equal to 100 liters.|The farmer's silo can hold thousands of hectolitres of grain. 这位农民的筒仓可以容纳数千百升的谷物。|Synonyms:  100 liters, 26.4 US gallons, 22 Imperial gallons|Antonyms: milliliter, microliter|noun

hectoliters:/ˈhɛktəˌliːtərz/ (US) /ˈhɛktəʊˌliːtəz/ (UK)| n. |百升（复数）|Plural form of hectolitre.|The winery produced thousands of hectoliters of wine this year.  这家酒厂今年生产了数千百升葡萄酒。|Synonyms: hundreds of liters|Antonyms: milliliters, microliters|noun
