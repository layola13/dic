hooty:/ˈhuːti/| adj.|猫头鹰叫声般的, 忽忽叫的; (非正式)愚蠢的, 疯狂的|Resembling or characteristic of the sound of an owl; (informal) foolish, crazy.|例句：The hooty owl kept us awake all night.  那只忽忽叫的猫头鹰让我们整夜没睡。

例句：That's a hooty idea! 那真是个疯狂的想法！|近义词：owl-like, foolish, silly, daft|反义词:sensible, wise, intelligent|词性:adjective
词源:  Imitative of the sound of an owl.
