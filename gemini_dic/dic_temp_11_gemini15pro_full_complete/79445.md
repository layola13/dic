glittered:/ˈɡlɪtərd/| v.|闪烁，闪耀|shone with a sparkling light; sparkled.|The diamonds glittered under the lights. 钻石在灯光下闪闪发光。|近义词：sparkled, shimmered, flashed|反义词:dulled, dimmed|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *gliteren, frequentative of *glīten, from Old English glit(t)nian (“to shine, glitter, sparkle”). Cognate with Old High German glizan (“to shine, gleam”).
