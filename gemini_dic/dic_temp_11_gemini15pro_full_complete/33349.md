chummily:/ˈtʃʌmɪli/
adverb
亲密地，友好地
in a chumly manner; in a friendly and intimate way.
They chatted chummily about their childhoods. 他们亲密地聊起了他们的童年。
近义词:friendlily, amiably, intimately, warmly
反义词:coldly, distantly, unfriendlily
词性:adverb

chummy:/ˈtʃʌmi/
adjective
亲密的，友好的
(of people) on friendly and intimate terms.
They're very chummy with their neighbors. 他们和邻居相处得非常融洽。
近义词:friendly, close, intimate, amicable
反义词:unfriendly, distant, hostile
词性:adjective

词源: From chum + -y.  "Chum" originates from the 17th century, likely a shortened form of "chamber-fellow," referring to a roommate, especially at university.
