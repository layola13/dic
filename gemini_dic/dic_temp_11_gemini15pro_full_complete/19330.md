bibliographer:/ˌbɪbliˈɑːɡrəfər/ | n. | 书目学家，文献目录学家 | A person who compiles bibliographies or studies bibliography.  A scholar who specializes in the history, description, and identification of books. |  Example 1: The bibliographer meticulously cataloged every edition of the author's works.  中文：这位书目学家一丝不苟地将这位作者的每一版作品都编入了目录。

Example 2:  The rare book dealer consulted a bibliographer to authenticate the first edition. 中文：这位珍本书商咨询了一位书目学家以鉴定这本初版书的真伪。 | Synonym:  cataloger, indexer | Antonym: N/A | Noun
