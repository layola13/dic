alcoholization:/ˌælkəhəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
noun
酒精化；醇化
The process of saturating something with alcohol or the state of being saturated with alcohol.  The alcoholization of the tissues can cause serious health problems.
The excessive alcoholization of the fruit made it inedible. 水果过度酒精化使其无法食用。
saturation, impregnation
drying, dehydration
n.
