hitting-up:/ˈhɪtɪŋ ʌp/ | phrasal verb |联系，拜访，索要| To contact someone, often informally, typically to ask for something or to make plans. It often implies a casual or informal request. | Example: I'm hitting up my friend for some money. 我要找我的朋友要点钱。

Example:  I'm hitting up the mall later.  我晚点要去逛商场。| Synonyms: contacting, asking, visiting, requesting | Antonyms: avoiding, ignoring | verb phrase
