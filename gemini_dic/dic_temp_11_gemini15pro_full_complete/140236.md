offish:/ˈɒfɪʃ/ (UK), /ˈɑːfɪʃ/ (US)| adj.|冷淡的，疏远的，不友好的|Not friendly or forthcoming; aloof and reserved.|例句：He was rather offish with me when I first met him. 我第一次见到他时，他对我相当冷淡。

例句：She gave me an offish look and turned away. 她冷冷地看了我一眼，然后转身走开了。|近义词：aloof, distant, reserved, standoffish, unapproachable, unfriendly|反义词：friendly, approachable, warm, sociable, outgoing|词性:形容词

词源:
From *off* (adverb or preposition) + *-ish* (suffix forming adjectives).  The original sense was 'inclined to keep aloof, stand off'.  First recorded in the late 18th century.
