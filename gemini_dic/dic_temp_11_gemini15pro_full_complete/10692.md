appurtenance:/əˈpɜːrtənəns/
n.
附属物，附件
Something that belongs to something else; an accessory or adjunct.
The shed is an appurtenance to the main house.  小屋是主屋的附属建筑。
近义词：accessory, adjunct, attachment, appendage
反义词：N/A
词性：noun

appurtenance:/əˈpɜːrtənəns/
n.
(通常复数)装备，设备
(usually appurtenances) Equipment or accessories.
The knight's appurtenances included his sword, shield, and helmet. 骑士的装备包括他的剑、盾和头盔。
近义词：equipment, gear, paraphernalia
反义词：N/A
词性：noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French apartenance, from apartenir "to appertain," from Latin ad "to" + pertinere "to belong."
