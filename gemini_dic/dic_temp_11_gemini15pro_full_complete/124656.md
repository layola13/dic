mouthy:/ˈmaʊði/ (US), /ˈmaʊθi/ (UK)| adj.|多嘴的，爱顶嘴的，说话粗鲁的|Talkative, especially in a rude or disrespectful way. Often used to describe someone who talks back or complains excessively.|例句：That kid is so mouthy, he never listens to his parents. 那个孩子太爱顶嘴了，从来不听父母的话。

例句：She got a detention for being mouthy to the teacher. 她因为对老师说话粗鲁而被留校察看。|近义词：talkative, garrulous, loquacious, impertinent, impudent, insolent|反义词：quiet, reserved, respectful, polite|词性:形容词
