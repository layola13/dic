forward-looking:/ˈfɔːrwərd ˈlʊkɪŋ/ (US) /ˈfɔːwəd ˈlʊkɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|有远见的；前瞻性的；着眼未来的|Concerned with or relating to the future; planning for the future.|例句：The company has a forward-looking approach to investment. 这家公司在投资方面很有远见。|近义词：progressive, visionary, farsighted|反义词：shortsighted, myopic, backward-looking|词性:形容词

词源:  "forward" (向前) + "looking" (看的).  "Forward" comes from Old English "foreweard," meaning "directed towards the front."  "Looking" is the present participle of the verb "look," from Old English "locian," meaning "to see, behold."
