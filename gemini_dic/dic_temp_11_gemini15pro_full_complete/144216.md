outskating:/ˌaʊtˈskeɪtɪŋ/
verb
(以滑冰速度)超过，胜过
The act of skating faster or more skillfully than another person.
He easily outsakted his opponents. 他轻松地超越了对手。
Synonyms: outmaneuver, surpass, outstrip
Antonyms: lag behind, trail
verb

outskating:/ˌaʊtˈskeɪtɪŋ/
gerund
(以滑冰速度)超过，胜过的行为
The act of skating faster or more skillfully than another person.
His outskating of the competition secured him the gold medal.  他超越所有对手，赢得了金牌。
Synonyms: outmaneuvering, surpassing, outstripping
Antonyms: lagging behind, trailing
noun
