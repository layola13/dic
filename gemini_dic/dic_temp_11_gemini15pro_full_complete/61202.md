elven:/ˈelvən/| adj.|精灵的，小精灵似的|Of or relating to elves; elflike.  Having a magical, otherworldly quality, suggestive of elves.|The child had an elven grace as she danced through the forest.  那孩子在森林中跳舞时有一种精灵般的优雅。|近义词：magical, fairy-like, ethereal|反义词：mundane, ordinary|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *elven, *elvish, from Old English *elfisc, from elf + -isc. Cognate with Old High German elbisch, Old Norse alfsk. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *albiz.
