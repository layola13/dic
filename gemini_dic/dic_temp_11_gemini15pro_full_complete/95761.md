inconstancy: /ɪnˈkɑːnstənsi/ | n. |反复无常；易变；不稳定|The state of being inconstant; fickleness; changeableness.  Lack of firmness or steadiness; instability.|Her inconstancy made it difficult to maintain a serious relationship. 她的反复无常使得维持一段认真的感情变得很困难。|Synonyms: fickleness, volatility, capriciousness, mutability, variability|Antonyms: constancy, stability, steadfastness, firmness, fidelity|noun

inconstant: /ɪnˈkɑːnstənt/ | adj. |反复无常的；易变的；不稳定的|Not constant; frequently changing; variable; fickle.|The inconstant weather made it difficult to plan a picnic.  变化无常的天气使得计划野餐变得很困难。|Synonyms: fickle, volatile, capricious, erratic, mutable|Antonyms: constant, stable, steadfast, firm, faithful|adjective
