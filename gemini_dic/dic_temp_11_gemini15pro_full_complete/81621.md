grecianizing:/ˌɡriːʃənaɪzɪŋ/
v.
使希腊化
making something Greek in character or style.
The Romans grecianized many of their gods' names. 罗马人将许多神的名字希腊化了。
hellenizing, classicizing
romanizing, barbarizing
