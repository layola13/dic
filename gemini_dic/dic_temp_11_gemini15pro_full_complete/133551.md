nonfinite:/nɒnˈfaɪnaɪt/ /nɑːnˈfaɪnaɪt/ | adj. | 非限定的，非限定词的 |  A nonfinite verb form is not marked for tense or person and cannot stand alone as the main verb of a sentence.  Nonfinite verbs include infinitives, participles, and gerunds. |  "Reading is fun" contains the nonfinite verb "reading" (gerund).  阅读很有趣。| 近义词: infinite, unlimited | 反义词: finite, limited | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *nonfinit, from Latin *nōn fīnītus, equivalent to *nōn (not) + fīnītus (limited, finite).
