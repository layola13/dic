keratin: /ˈkɛrətɪn/ | n. | 角蛋白 | any of various sulfur-containing fibrous proteins that form the chemical basis of epidermal tissues (such as hair, nails, and horns) |  Keratin helps to strengthen hair and nails. 角蛋白有助于强化头发和指甲。|  Wool is primarily composed of keratin. 羊毛主要由角蛋白组成。| synonyms: protein, scleroprotein | antonyms: None | noun
