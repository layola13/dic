lovelike: /lʌvˈlaɪk/
adjective
像爱情一样的；充满爱的
Resembling or characteristic of love; filled with love.
Her lovelike gaze made him blush. 她充满爱意的注视让他脸红了。
Synonyms: loving, affectionate, tender, devoted, adoring
Antonyms: hateful, loveless, cold, indifferent
词性: 形容词
