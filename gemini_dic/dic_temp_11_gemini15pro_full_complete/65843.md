evisceration:/ɪˌvɪsərˈeɪʃən/
n.
取出内脏；除去精华
the process of removing the internal organs of a body
例句：The evisceration of the fish was the first step in preparing it for cooking.  取出鱼的内脏是烹饪的第一步。

例句：The censor's evisceration of the film left it meaningless.  审查员对这部电影的删减使其毫无意义。
近义词：disembowelment, gutting
反义词:filling, stuffing
词性:noun


eviscerate:/ɪˈvɪsərˌeɪt/
vt.
取出内脏；除去精华；严厉批评
to remove the internal organs of a body; to deprive something of its essential part
例句：The surgeon eviscerated the tumor.  外科医生切除了肿瘤。

例句：The critic's review eviscerated the young author's first novel.  评论家的评论严厉批评了这位年轻作家的第一部小说。
近义词：disembowel, gut, excoriate
反义词: praise, commend
词性:verb
