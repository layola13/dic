olympianly:/oʊˈlɪmpiənlɪ/
adverb|像奥林匹克运动员一样地；庄严地；超然地|In a manner resembling the gods of Olympus; majestically; aloofly.|He surveyed the scene olympianly, as if he were a god judging mortals. 他以超然的态度审视着整个场面，仿佛他是一位评判凡人的神。|例句：She smiled olympianly, seemingly unaffected by the chaos around her. 她庄严地微笑着，似乎没有受到周围混乱的影响。|近义词：majestically, grandly, loftily, serenely|反义词：humbly, modestly, submissively|词性: 副词

词源: From Olympian + -ly.  Olympian refers to the gods of Mount Olympus in Greek mythology, known for their power and serenity.
