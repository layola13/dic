highjacked:/ˌhaɪdʒækt/ | v. | 劫持，强占 | To seize or commandeer (a vehicle, aircraft, or ship) in transit by force or threat of force; to take over or control something without authority. | The terrorists highjacked the plane and forced it to land in a different country. 恐怖分子劫持了飞机，并迫使其在另一个国家降落。


The company's project was highjacked by a rival firm. 该公司的项目被竞争对手抢走了。| 近义词：seize, commandeer, hijack, appropriate | 反义词：release, surrender, relinquish | 词性: 动词 (past participle)
