detruncation:/diːtrʌŋˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 去截断，去尾，解除截断 | The process of restoring a truncated sequence to its original length or a close approximation.  It involves estimating or reconstructing the missing part of the data. |  Detruncation methods are crucial in analyzing censored data in survival analysis. 去尾方法在分析生存分析中的删失数据至关重要。

Detruncation allowed the researchers to get a more accurate picture of the distribution. 去尾使得研究人员能够更准确地了解分布情况。 | 近义词: restoration, reconstruction, completion | 反义词: truncation | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "de-" (prefix meaning "removal, reversal") + "truncation" (from Latin "truncare," meaning "to shorten by cutting").
