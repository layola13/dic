malm:/mɑːm/ | n. |  软白垩土；一种由白垩土制成的泥灰岩 | A soft, white, chalky rock; a marl consisting of chalk and clay. | The cliffs were composed of malm, easily eroded by the sea.  这些悬崖由软白垩土构成，很容易被海水侵蚀。 | Synonym: chalk, marl | Antonym: granite, basalt | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Swedish *malm* "ore, metal," related to Old Norse *malmr*.  Originally referring to various kinds of soft rock used for building, the word later narrowed in meaning to specify this particular type of chalky rock.
