non-practice:/ˌnɒnˈpræktɪs/ /ˌnɑːnˈpræktɪs/| adj. n.| 非实践的，非执业的；非实践，不执业|  Not related to or based on practice; not actively engaged in a profession or activity. |This theory is non-practice and difficult to apply in real-world situations. 这个理论是非实践的，难以应用于实际情况。| The surgeon's long non-practice made him hesitant during the operation.  这位外科医生长期不执业，使他在手术过程中犹豫不决。 | 近义词: theoretical, unpractical, inactive | 反义词: practical, applied, active | 词性: adjective, noun

non-practice:/ˌnɒnˈpræktɪs/ /ˌnɑːnˈpræktɪs/| n.| 非实践，不执业| The state of not engaging in a profession or activity. |His long non-practice led to a decline in his skills. 他长期不执业导致他的技能下降。|  The musician's non-practice was evident in his rusty performance.  这位音乐家不练习的状况在他的生疏的演奏中很明显。 | 近义词: inactivity, idleness | 反义词: practice, activity | 词性: noun
