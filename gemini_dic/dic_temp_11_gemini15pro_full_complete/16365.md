barbellulate: /bɑːrˈbɛljʊleɪt/ | adj. | 具小倒钩的；具倒钩毛的 | Having minute barbs or barbules. |  The barbellulate antennae of the moth helped it detect subtle changes in air currents.  飞蛾具小倒钩的触角帮助它探测到气流的细微变化。 | barbed, denticulate | smooth | adjective

Etymology:  From Latin *barbellulatus, diminutive of barbellatus ("furnished with little barbs"), from barbula, diminutive of barba ("beard").  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰar- ("point, bristle").
