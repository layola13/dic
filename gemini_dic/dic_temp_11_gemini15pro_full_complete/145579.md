overcompressed:/ˌoʊvər kəmˈprɛst/ | adj. | 过度压缩的 | Compressed beyond a desirable or useful point.  This often results in a loss of quality, especially in audio or video files. | The overcompressed image was pixelated and blurry. 过度压缩的图像像素化且模糊。| The audio file was so overcompressed that it sounded tinny. 音频文件过度压缩，听起来很微弱。|  synonyms: over-compressed, excessively compressed | antonyms: uncompressed, decompressed | adjective
