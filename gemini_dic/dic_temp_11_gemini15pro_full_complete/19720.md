bilirubins:/ˌbɪlɪˈruːbɪnz/| n.|胆红素|An orange-yellow pigment formed in the liver by the breakdown of hemoglobin and excreted in bile.  It is responsible for the yellow color of bruises and the brown color of feces.|Elevated bilirubins in the blood can indicate liver problems.|近义词:bile pigment|反义词: None|Noun
