genera:/ˈdʒɛnərə/
n.
复数 (plural)
属 (genus)
The biological classification system categorizes living organisms into kingdoms, phyla, classes, orders, families, genera, and species.  生物分类系统将生物体分类为界、门、纲、目、科、属和种。
There are many genera of butterflies in the Amazon rainforest. 亚马逊雨林中有许多蝴蝶属。
Synonyms: types, kinds, categories, classes
Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a taxonomic rank)
