exhibitor:/ɪɡˈzɪbɪtər/| n. |参展者，展出者|A person or company that displays their products or services at an exhibition.|例句：The exhibitor showcased their latest innovations at the trade fair.  这位参展商在交易会上展示了他们的最新创新。

例句：Many exhibitors were present at the annual conference. 许多参展商出席了年会。|近义词：displayer, presenter, participant|反义词: visitor, attendee|词性: noun

exhibit:/ɪɡˈzɪbɪt/| v. |展览，展出|To show something publicly.|例句：The museum exhibits a fascinating collection of ancient artifacts.  博物馆展出了引人入胜的古代文物收藏。

例句：He exhibited his paintings at a local gallery. 他在当地一家画廊展出了他的画作。|近义词：display, show, present|反义词: conceal, hide|词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *exhibitor, from Latin exhibitus, past participle of exhibere (“to hold out, present, show, furnish, provide”), from ex (“out”) + habere (“to hold, have”).
