fluctisonous:/ˌflʌktɪˈsoʊnəs/
adjective
声音起伏的; 波动的
Characterized by fluctuating or rising and falling sounds.
The fluctisonous nature of the ocean waves crashing against the shore was mesmerizing. 海浪拍打海岸的声音起伏不定，令人着迷。
近义词:  undulating, wavering, fluctuating, resonant, vibrant
反义词: monotonous, steady, constant
词性: adjective


Etymology:  From Latin *fluctisonus*  (not attested in Classical Latin), from *fluctus* ("wave") + *sonus* ("sound").  Meaning "sounding like waves" or "having a wave-like sound".
