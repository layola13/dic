dustpan:/ˈdʌstˌpæn/ (US), /ˈdʌstpən/ (UK)| n.|簸箕|A flat handheld receptacle with a short handle, used for collecting dust and dirt swept up with a brush.|She swept the broken glass into the dustpan.|Synonyms: dust shovel, pan, scoop|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps broom as its complementary tool)|noun
