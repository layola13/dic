bleeper:/ˈbliːpər/| n. |寻呼机；哔哔作响的东西|A small electronic device that makes a beeping sound, used to receive messages or to locate someone or something.  It is often used as a euphemism for a swear word. |  "He swore under his breath, muttering a string of bleepers."  他低声咒骂着，嘴里咕哝着一连串的哔哔声。|Synonym: pager, beeper, sensor | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "silence") | Noun
