appealing:/əˈpiːlɪŋ/ | adj. | 吸引人的，有感染力的 | Attractive or interesting; able to arouse a sympathetic response. |  This cake looks very appealing.  这个蛋糕看起来很诱人。

The idea of working from home is very appealing to me. 在家工作的想法对我很有吸引力。

近义词: attractive, engaging, alluring, charming, enticing, tempting | 反义词: unappealing, unattractive, repulsive, off-putting | 词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English *appelen*, from Anglo-Norman *apeler*, from Latin *appellāre* (“to address, call upon, appeal to”), from *ad* (“to”) + *pellāre* (“to speak, call”).
