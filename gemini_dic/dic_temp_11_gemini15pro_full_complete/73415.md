foreland:/ˈfɔːrlənd/ | n. | 前沿地带，海角，岬 | A piece of land that extends out into water; a promontory.  An area of relatively low-lying land along an advancing shoreline. | The lighthouse stood on a rocky foreland, warning ships away from the coast. 灯塔矗立在一个岩石岬角上，警告船只远离海岸。

The coastal foreland is slowly eroding due to rising sea levels. 沿海的前沿地带由于海平面上升正在慢慢地被侵蚀。 | 近义词: headland, promontory, cape, peninsula | 反义词: hinterland | 词性: 名词

foreland:/ˈfɔːrlənd/ | n. | 前沿，最前线，领先地位 | The foremost part or position of something; the leading edge of an advance or development. | The company is at the foreland of technological innovation. 这家公司处于技术创新的前沿。

They were at the foreland of the movement for social reform. 他们在社会改革运动中处于领先地位。 | 近义词: vanguard, forefront, cutting edge, leading edge | 反义词: rear, backwater | 词性: 名词
