darkly:/ˈdɑːrkli/ | adv. | 昏暗地，阴沉地 | in a dark manner; gloomily; ominously | The room was darkly lit, creating a sense of mystery. 房间光线昏暗，营造出一种神秘感。|  The future looked darkly for the struggling company.  这家苦苦挣扎的公司的未来看起来很黯淡。 | 近义词：gloomily, dimly, somberly | 反义词：brightly, cheerfully | 词性: 副词

词源: From Middle English *derkliche, equivalent to dark +‎ -ly.
