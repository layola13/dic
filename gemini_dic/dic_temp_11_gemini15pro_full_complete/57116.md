doused:/daʊst/| v. |浇灭，浸湿| to extinguish something by soaking it in liquid; to drench something or someone with liquid. | The campfire was doused with water.  篝火被水浇灭了。|synonyms: drenched, soaked, extinguished, saturated| antonyms: ignited, kindled, dried|verb
doused:/daʊst/| adj. |浸湿的| thoroughly wet |  He was doused in sweat after the marathon. 马拉松后他浑身是汗。 |synonyms: soaked, drenched, saturated| antonyms: dry, arid|adjective
