almonry: /ˈɑːlmənri/ | n. |施舍处，救济所| A place where alms are distributed; a room or building used for the distribution of alms. | The monastery had an almonry where the poor could receive food and clothing. 修道院有一个施舍处，穷人可以在那里领取食物和衣服。| The king's almonry provided for the needy in the kingdom. 国王的施舍处为王国中的穷人提供帮助。| Synonym: almshouse, charitable institution | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps a place of profit or greed) | noun
