coeternity:/ˌkoʊɪˈtɜːrnɪti/ /ˌkəʊɪˈtɜːnɪti/
|n.|共同永恒；永远共存|The state of existing with something else for all time.|The doctrine of the coeternity of the Son with the Father. 圣子和圣父永远共存的教义。|
|Synonyms: eternity, perpetuity, sempiternity|
|Antonyms: temporality, transience|
|noun|


coeternal:/ˌkoʊɪˈtɜːrnl/ /ˌkəʊɪˈtɜːnəl/
|adj.|永远共存的；同永恒的|Existing with something else for all time.|The coeternal existence of good and evil. 善与恶的永远共存。|
|Synonyms: eternal, everlasting, perpetual|
|Antonyms: temporary, transient|
|adjective|
