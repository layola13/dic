moxies:/ˈmɒksiz/ (US) /ˈmɒksiːz/ (UK)| n.|勇气，胆量，魄力| Moxie refers to courage, nerve, determination, or spiritedness. It suggests a combination of boldness, perseverance, and resourcefulness, especially in challenging situations.| She showed a lot of moxie standing up to the bully. 她对抗恶霸展现出了很大的勇气。| synonyms: nerve, guts, grit, pluck, spirit, determination, spunk, backbone, chutzpah | antonyms: cowardice, timidity, fear, hesitation | noun
