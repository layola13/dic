jenite:/ˈdʒɛnaɪt/| n.|钙铁链硅酸盐，褐帘石|A sorosilicate mineral of calcium and iron, Ca2Fe2Si2O7(OH)2·H2O.  It typically occurs as prismatic or fibrous crystals, often in metamorphic rocks.|Jenite is often found in association with other minerals such as garnet and magnetite.  褐帘石经常与其他矿物如石榴石和磁铁矿伴生。|Synonyms: None specific to the mineral composition|Antonyms: None|Noun
