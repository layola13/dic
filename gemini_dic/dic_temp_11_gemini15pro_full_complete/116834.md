maxim's:/ˈmæksɪmz/
noun
马克西姆的（餐厅、夜总会等）
Refers to Maxim's de Paris, a famous and luxurious restaurant in Paris, France, often used to refer to establishments of similar style or named after it.
He took her to Maxim's for their anniversary.  他带她去马克西姆餐厅庆祝他们的周年纪念日。
synonyms: restaurant, nightclub, bistro
antonyms: diner, cafe (informal establishments)
n.

maxim:/ˈmæksɪm/
noun
格言，箴言
A short, pithy statement expressing a general truth or rule of conduct.
"Early to bed, early to rise" is a well-known maxim. “早睡早起”是一句众所周知的格言。
synonyms: adage, proverb, saying, aphorism
antonyms: nonsense, fallacy
n.
