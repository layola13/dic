colloquialism: /kəˈloʊkwiəˌlɪzəm/ | n. | 口语；俗语；白话 | A colloquialism is a word or phrase that is used in informal conversation, but not in formal writing or speech.  It's characteristic of or appropriate to ordinary or familiar conversation rather than formal speech or writing. | Example: "Wanna" is a colloquialism for "want to."  “要”是“想要”的口语说法。| Example:  Using "ain't" is considered a colloquialism.  使用“ain’t”被认为是一种口语用法。 | Synonyms: slang, idiom, vernacular | Antonyms: formalism, literary | noun
