lozenge:/ˈlɒzɪndʒ/ (UK), /ˈlɑːzɪndʒ/ (US)| n.|菱形, 润喉糖|A rhombus or diamond shape. A medicated tablet, usually dissolved in the mouth to soothe a sore throat.|She sucked on a lozenge to relieve her cough. 她含着一颗润喉糖来缓解咳嗽。|Synonyms: diamond, rhombus, tablet, pastille|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

lozenge:/ˈlɒzɪndʒ/ (UK), /ˈlɑːzɪndʒ/ (US)| n.|窗格玻璃, 徽章|A pane of glass, especially a diamond-shaped one. A heraldic bearing in the shape of a diamond.|The stained-glass window was composed of many small lozenges. 彩色玻璃窗由许多小菱形组成。|Synonyms: pane, diamond, rhombus|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
