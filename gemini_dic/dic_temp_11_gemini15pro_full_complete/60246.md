effulgent:/ɪˈfʌldʒənt/| adj.|光辉灿烂的；光芒四射的|Shining brightly; radiant.|The effulgent sunset painted the sky in vibrant hues. 光辉灿烂的日落将天空染上了鲜艳的色彩。|近义词：radiant, luminous, brilliant, dazzling|反义词：dull, dim, dark|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from Latin effulgent- ‘shining forth’, from the verb effulgere, from ex- ‘out’ + fulgere ‘to shine’.
