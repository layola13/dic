odontograph:/ˌoʊdɑːnˈtəɡræf/ | n. | 牙形图; 牙位记录器 | An instrument for recording the characteristics of the teeth for identification purposes, used especially in forensic dentistry.  It produces a graphical representation of the teeth's shapes and positions. | The odontograph was crucial in identifying the victim. 牙形图在识别受害者方面至关重要。 |  The police used an odontograph to match the dental records. 警方使用牙形图来匹配牙科记录。 | synonyms: dental chart, dental record | antonyms: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From *odonto-* (Greek 'tooth') + *-graph* (Greek 'writing' or 'drawing').
