half-discriminatingly:/ˌhæf dɪˈskrɪmɪneɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 半区别地，半有辨别力地 |  In a manner that shows some, but not complete, ability to distinguish or differentiate.  It suggests a partial or imperfect discernment. |  He listened half-discriminatingly to the lecture, catching some points but missing others. 他半心半意地听着讲座，抓住了一些要点，但也漏掉了一些。|  partially discerningly, imperfectly selectively | indiscriminately, uncritically | adverb
