PHRASE->
An intransitive verb does not take a direct object. 不及物动词不能带直接宾语。
The verb "arrive" is intransitive. “到达”这个动词是不及物动词。
Some verbs can be both transitive and intransitive. 有些动词既可以是及物动词，也可以是不及物动词。
Intransitive verbs cannot be used in the passive voice. 不及物动词不能用于被动语态。
Many verbs of motion are intransitive. 许多表示运动的动词是不及物动词。
