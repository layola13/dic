atlantis:/ətˈlæntɪs/ | n. | 亚特兰蒂斯 | A fictional island civilization that is mentioned in historical works by Plato. Atlantis is thought to have mastered various forms of technology that were unheard of at the time. After attempting to conquer Athens, Atlantis is believed to have been sunk to the bottom of the Atlantic Ocean. |  Many writers have speculated about the location of the lost city of Atlantis. 许多作家都推测过失落的亚特兰蒂斯城的位置。

Atlantis is often portrayed as a technologically advanced utopia. 亚特兰蒂斯通常被描绘成一个技术先进的乌托邦。 | 近义词: utopia, lost city | 反义词: reality | 词性: noun
