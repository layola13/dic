legumes:/ˈleɡjuːmz/ (US), /ˈlɛɡjuːmz/ (UK)| n. |豆类；豆科植物|Plants of the pea family, cultivated for their seeds, which are eaten as vegetables.|例句：Legumes, such as beans and lentils, are a good source of protein. 豆类，例如豆子和扁豆，是良好的蛋白质来源。

例句：Farmers rotate legumes with other crops to improve soil fertility. 农民将豆类与其他作物轮作以提高土壤肥力。|近义词：pulses, beans, peas|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
