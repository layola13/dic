kanban:/ˈkɑːnbɑːn/| n.|看板；看板管理|A Japanese manufacturing system in which the supply of components is regulated through the use of an instruction card sent along the production line.|The team uses kanban to manage their workflow.|近义词: visual control, lean manufacturing, agile development|反义词: push system|名词
