cochineal:/ˌkɒtʃɪˈniːl/ /ˌkoʊtʃɪˈniːl/| n. |胭脂红；胭脂虫|A red dye made from the dried bodies of cochineal insects. 由胭脂虫的干燥尸体制成的红色染料。|The artist used cochineal to create a vibrant red in his painting.  这位艺术家使用胭脂红在他的画作中创造出鲜艳的红色。|近义词：carmine, crimson|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

cochineal:/ˌkɒtʃɪˈniːl/ /ˌkoʊtʃɪˈniːl/| adj. |胭脂红的|Of a vivid crimson or scarlet color. 鲜艳的深红色或猩红色。|She wore a cochineal dress to the party. 她穿着一件胭脂红的连衣裙去参加聚会。|近义词：carmine, crimson|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish "cochinilla," diminutive of "cochina" meaning "sow, female pig," because the insect's shape resembles a sow.  源自西班牙语 "cochinilla"，是 "cochina"（意思是“母猪”）的缩小词，因为这种昆虫的形状类似于母猪。 Later, the word also came to refer to the dye produced from the insect. 后来，这个词也指从这种昆虫身上提取的染料。
