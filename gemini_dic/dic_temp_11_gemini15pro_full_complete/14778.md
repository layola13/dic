awned:/ɔːnd/
adjective
有遮篷的；带篷的
furnished with an awning; covered with an awning
The cafe had awned tables outside, perfect for enjoying a coffee in the shade.  这家咖啡馆外面有带遮篷的桌子，非常适合在阴凉处享用咖啡。
The market stalls were awned, protecting the goods from the sun and rain.  市场摊位都有遮阳篷，保护货物免受日晒雨淋。
Synonyms: shaded, covered, canopied
Antonyms: uncovered, exposed
