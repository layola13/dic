malfunction:/mælˈfʌŋkʃən/
n.|故障，失灵|A malfunction is a failure to function or work properly.|The printer has developed a malfunction. 打印机发生了故障。|近义词：failure, breakdown, glitch, defect|反义词：function, operation|词性:名词

malfunction:/mælˈfʌŋkʃən/
vi.|发生故障，失灵|To fail to function or work properly.|The engine malfunctioned in mid-flight. 引擎在飞行途中发生故障。|近义词：fail, break down|反义词：function, operate, work|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From mal- + function.  "Mal-" is a prefix meaning "bad" or "badly."  "Function" comes from the Latin "functio," meaning "performance" or "execution."
