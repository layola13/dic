lie-down:/ˈlaɪ daʊn/| n.|躺下; 休息|A short period of rest, typically in bed.|After lunch, I always have a lie-down. 午饭后，我总是要躺一会儿。|近义词：rest, nap|反义词:get up, rise|noun

lie-down:/laɪ daʊn/|v.|(使)躺下|(of a person or animal) be in a horizontal or resting position on a supporting surface.|The dog lay down in the shade. 狗躺在阴凉处。|近义词：recline, rest|反义词:stand up, sit up|verb
