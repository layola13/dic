nondistributional:/ˌnɒndɪstrɪˈbjuːʃənl/
adjective
非分布式的
Not pertaining to or characterized by distribution.  Relating to linguistic analysis that does not focus on the distribution of linguistic elements.
This paper argues for a nondistributional approach to semantics. 这篇论文主张采用一种非分布式语义学方法。
The nondistributional properties of the word are difficult to analyze.  这个词的非分布式特性很难分析。
近义词: non-compositional
反义词: distributional
词性: adjective
