diastase:/ˈdaɪəsteɪs/ /ˈdaɪəsteɪz/| n. |淀粉酶; 糖化酶|An enzyme, found especially in germinating seeds, that catalyzes the hydrolysis of starch to maltose and dextrin.  一种酶，尤其存在于发芽的种子中，催化淀粉水解成麦芽糖和糊精。|Diastase is important in the brewing process. 淀粉酶在酿造过程中很重要。|Synonyms: amylase, enzyme|Antonyms: inhibitor|noun
