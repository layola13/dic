loud: /laʊd/ | adj. adv.| 大声的，响亮的；喧闹的；（颜色）艳丽的；招摇的 | producing or capable of producing much noise; easily audible; noisy; strongly emphasized | The music was too loud.  音乐太吵了。|  loudly, noisy, boisterous | quiet, soft, gentle | adjective, adverb

loud: /laʊd/ | adv.| 大声地，响亮地 | in a loud manner; loudly |  He spoke loud and clear. 他说得响亮而清晰。 | loudly, clearly | softly, quietly | adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *loud, lowd, from Old English hlūd (“loud, noisy, clamorous, famous, notorious”), from Proto-Germanic *hlūdaz (“loud”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱlew- (“to hear”). Cognate with West Frisian lûd (“loud”), Dutch luid (“loud”), German laut (“loud”), Swedish ljud (“sound”), Icelandic hljóð (“sound”).
