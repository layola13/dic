consternation:/ˌkɒnstəˈneɪʃn/ /ˌkɑːnstərˈneɪʃn/| n. |惊愕；惊恐；丧气| a feeling of anxiety or dismay, typically at something unexpected |例句：The announcement of the layoffs was met with consternation. 裁员公告一出，大家都惊愕不已。

例句：Her sudden appearance filled him with consternation. 她突然出现，使他大为惊恐。

近义词：dismay, alarm, shock, panic, distress, anxiety, perturbation, trepidation|反义词:calm, composure, tranquility|词性:名词
