mantelpieces: /ˈmæntlˌpiːsɪz/ | n. | 壁炉架，壁炉台 | A shelf above a fireplace, usually part of a frame surrounding it, often used for displaying ornaments. |  The mantelpiece was crowded with family photographs and porcelain figurines. 壁炉架上摆满了家庭照片和瓷器小雕像。

The antique mantelpiece was the focal point of the living room. 古董壁炉架是客厅的焦点。 | 近义词: mantel, fire surround | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps hearth - the floor area in front of the fireplace) | 词性: noun
