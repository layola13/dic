longships:/ˈlɔːŋʃɪps/
n.
Longships, Viking warship
A longship was a type of ship invented and used by the Vikings for trade, exploration, and warfare.
The Vikings sailed their longships to new lands.  维京人驾驶他们的长船去往新的土地。
Near synonyms:  warship,  vessel, galley
Antonyms: (no direct antonym for a specific type of ship)
