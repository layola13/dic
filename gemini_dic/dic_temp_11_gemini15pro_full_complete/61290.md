embank:/ɪmˈbæŋk/ | vt. | 筑堤；围堵；保护 | To enclose or protect with a bank, especially an embankment. | The river was embanked to prevent flooding.  这条河筑了堤坝以防止洪水泛滥。| 近义词:dike, levee, dam | 反义词:excavate | verb

embankment:/ɪmˈbæŋkmənt/ | n. | 堤防，路堤；筑堤 | A wall or bank of earth or stone built to prevent a river flooding an area. | The embankment protects the town from flooding.  堤坝保护着这个城镇免受洪水侵袭。| 近义词:dike, levee, dam | 反义词:ditch, trench | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *embanquer, from en- (“in”) + banquer (“to bench”), from banc (“bench”).
