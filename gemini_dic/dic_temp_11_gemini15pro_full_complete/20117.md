biotypic:/ˌbaɪəˈtɪpɪk/ | adj. | 生物型的，生物类型的 | Relating to a biotype, which is a group of organisms within a species that share the same genetic constitution or genotype.  A biotype can also refer to a group of organisms sharing the same hereditary characteristics. |  The researchers studied the biotypic variations within the population of butterflies.  研究人员研究了蝴蝶种群中的生物型变异。 |  The biotypic differences made the two groups of plants resistant to different diseases.  生物型的差异使得两组植物对不同的疾病具有抗性。 | Synonyms:  genotypic, constitutional | Antonyms: phenotypic | adjective
