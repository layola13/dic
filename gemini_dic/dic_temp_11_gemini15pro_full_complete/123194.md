monocentric:/ˌmɒnəˈsɛntrɪk/ /ˌmɑːnəˈsɛntrɪk/
adjective
单中心的；以一个中心为核心的
Having a single center; concentrated about one center.
例句：The monocentric city model assumes that all employment is concentrated in the central business district.  单中心城市模型假设所有就业都集中在中心商业区。

例句：The traditional monocentric urban model is no longer adequate to explain the complex spatial structure of modern cities.  传统的单中心城市模型已不再足以解释现代城市的复杂空间结构。
近义词：unicentric, single-centered
反义词：polycentric, multicentered
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From mono- (one) + centric (related to a center).  "Mono-" comes from Greek "monos" (single, alone), and "centric" comes from Latin "centrum" (center) and ultimately from Greek "kentron" (point, center).
