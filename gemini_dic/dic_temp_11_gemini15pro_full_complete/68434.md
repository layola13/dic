falter:/ˈfɔːltər/ (US) /ˈfɔːltə(r)/ (UK)| v. | 犹豫，迟疑；蹒跚；支吾；衰弱 | To hesitate or stumble; to speak hesitantly; to lose strength or momentum. |  His voice faltered as he spoke about the accident.  他谈到事故时，声音颤抖了。

The economy faltered after the stock market crash. 股市崩盘后，经济衰退了。| 近义词：hesitate, stumble, waver, stammer | 反义词：continue, persevere, persist | 词性: 动词
