leninist:/ˈlɛnɪnɪst/| adj. n.|列宁主义的；列宁主义者|Relating to the political theories of Vladimir Lenin, especially the principles of democratic centralism and the dictatorship of the proletariat.  A follower of the political theories of Vladimir Lenin.|例句：The Leninist ideology shaped the Soviet Union. 列宁主义思想塑造了苏联。|近义词：Marxist, communist, Bolshevik|反义词：capitalist, anti-communist|adjective, noun


leninist:/ˈlɛnɪnɪst/| n.|列宁主义者|A follower of the political theories of Vladimir Lenin.|例句：He considered himself a devout Leninist. 他自认为是一个虔诚的列宁主义者。|近义词：Marxist, communist, Bolshevik|反义词：capitalist, anti-communist|noun
