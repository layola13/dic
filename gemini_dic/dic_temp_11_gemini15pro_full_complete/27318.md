calypsonian:/kəˌlɪpˈsoʊniən/
noun
卡里普索歌曲演唱者; 作卡里普索歌曲的人
A singer or composer of calypso music.
Lord Kitchener was a famous calypsonian.  基奇纳爵士是一位著名的卡里普索歌曲演唱者。
singer, musician, performer
composer, songwriter
