overmerry:/ˌoʊvərˈmɛri/| adj.| 过于快乐的，过分高兴的| Excessively merry; too jovial.  It suggests a level of merriment that is inappropriate or excessive, perhaps bordering on boisterous or even slightly manic.| He became overmerry after a few drinks and started singing loudly. 他几杯酒下肚后变得过于兴奋，开始大声唱歌。|The party guests were quite overmerry, dancing on the tables and singing at the top of their lungs. 参加派对的客人们都非常兴奋，在桌子上跳舞，引吭高歌。| 近义词：exuberant, boisterous, jovial, excessively cheerful| 反义词：somber, gloomy, melancholy| 词性: adjective
