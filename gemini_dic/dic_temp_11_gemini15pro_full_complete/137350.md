nontaxers:/ˌnɑːnˈtæksərz/
noun
非纳税人
People who do not pay taxes.
The government is trying to find ways to reduce the number of nontaxers. 政府正在努力寻找减少非纳税人数的方法。
The impact of the new tax law on nontaxers is still unclear. 新税法对非纳税人的影响尚不清楚。
Synonym: tax evaders, tax dodgers
Antonym: taxpayers
