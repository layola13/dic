compatible:/kəmˈpætəbl/ | adj. | 兼容的；相容的；可共存的 |  (of two things) able to exist or occur together without conflict.  Capable of being used with a particular computer or other electronic equipment without modification.  (of two people) well suited to each other. |  This software is compatible with all versions of Windows.  这个软件与所有版本的Windows兼容。

The new printer is compatible with Macs. 新打印机与苹果电脑兼容。

They're a very compatible couple.  他们是一对很合得来的夫妇。| synonyms: consistent, congruous, consonant, agreeable, harmonious, adaptable | antonyms: incompatible, inconsistent, incongruous, discordant, conflicting | word type: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English: from late Latin compatibilis, from compati ‘suffer with,’ from com- ‘together’ + pati ‘suffer.’
