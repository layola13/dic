irrecoverably:/ˌɪrɪˈkʌvərəbli/
adverb
不能挽回地，不可恢复地
in a way that cannot be recovered or reversed
The data was irrecoverably lost after the hard drive crashed.  硬盘崩溃后，数据无法挽回地丢失了。
He was irrecoverably in love with her. 他无可救药地爱上了她。
Synonyms: permanently, irretrievably, hopelessly, beyond repair
Antonyms: retrievably, recoverably, repairably
词性: 副词
