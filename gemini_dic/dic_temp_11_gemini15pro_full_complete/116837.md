maximalism:/ˈmæksɪməlɪzəm/
n.|极繁主义；最大化主义|A philosophy or style that favors excess and extravagance, especially in art, literature, or design, and rejects minimalism.  It often involves using bright colors, rich textures, and ornate details.|Her apartment is a testament to maximalism, with every surface covered in vibrant patterns and objects.|近义词:excess, extravagance, flamboyance|反义词:minimalism, simplicity, understatement|词性:noun

maximalist:/ˈmæksɪməlɪst/
n.|极繁主义者；最大化主义者|A person who favors maximalism.|As a self-proclaimed maximalist, she embraced bold colors and patterns in her wardrobe.|近义词:extremist|反义词:minimalist|词性:noun

maximal:/ˈmæksɪməl/
adj.|最大的；最高的；极大的|Of the greatest possible size, amount, or degree.|The company sought maximal profits with minimal investment.|近义词:maximum, greatest, utmost|反义词:minimal, least, smallest|词性:adjective
