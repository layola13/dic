drudged:/drʌdʒd/
verb
苦干，做苦工
worked hard and monotonously; labored tirelessly at an unpleasant task.
He drudged away at his homework all weekend.  他整个周末都在辛苦地做作业。
toil, slave, grind
relax, play
v.
