gorgeous:/ˈɡɔːrdʒəs/ (US) /ˈɡɔːdʒəs/ (UK)| adj.|华丽的，灿烂的；极好的，令人愉悦的|Very beautiful and attractive; giving pleasure and delight|例句：She looked gorgeous in that dress. 她穿那件衣服看起来真漂亮。|近义词：beautiful, stunning, splendid, magnificent, lovely, attractive, delightful|反义词：ugly, hideous, unattractive, plain|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle French *gorgias* "elegant, fashionable," from Old French *gorgias* "showy, elegant in dress," perhaps of Germanic origin.
