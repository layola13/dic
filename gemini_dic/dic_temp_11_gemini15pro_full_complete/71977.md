flimsily:/ˈflɪmzɪli/| adv.|脆弱地；易损地；不牢固地|in a way that is likely to break or be easily damaged|The stage set was flimsily constructed and looked as if it might collapse at any moment.  舞台布景搭建得很脆弱，看起来随时都可能倒塌。|The tent was flimsily secured and blew away in the storm.  帐篷固定得不太牢固，在暴风雨中被吹走了。|近义词：weakly, insecurely, precariously|反义词：strongly, securely, firmly|词性:副词

flimsy:/ˈflɪmzi/| adj.|脆弱的；易损的；不牢固的|easily damaged or broken; not strongly made|The box was made of flimsy cardboard. 这个盒子是用不结实的纸板做的。|She wore a flimsy dress. 她穿着一件轻薄的连衣裙。|近义词：fragile, weak, thin|反义词：strong, sturdy, robust|词性:形容词
