at-the-wheel:/æt ðə wiːl/
adjective|掌舵的，负责的，控制的|in control of a vehicle or organization|John was at the wheel when the accident happened. 约翰开车时发生了事故。|Synonyms: in charge, in control, responsible|Antonyms: powerless, irresponsible|property: adjective

at-the-wheel:/æt ðə wiːl/
adverbial phrase|开车, 掌舵|driving a vehicle; in control of a situation|With Mary at the wheel, we're sure to arrive safely. 由玛丽开车，我们一定能安全到达。|Synonyms: driving, steering, controlling|Antonyms: passenger, subordinate|property: adverb
