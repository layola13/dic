jerfalcon:/ˈdʒɜːrfɔːlkən/ |ˈdʒɜːrˌfɔːlkən/| n. |矛隼；猎鹰|A large, swift falcon (Falco rusticolus) that is the largest falcon in the world, inhabiting arctic regions.|The jerfalcon plummeted from the sky, seizing its prey in its talons. 矛隼从天而降，用爪子抓住了猎物。|The jerfalcon is a powerful and skilled hunter. 矛隼是一个强大而熟练的猎人。|Synonyms: gyrfalcon, white gyrfalcon|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

Etymology:
From Old French *gerfaucon, from Old High German girfalko, from gîr "vulture" + falko "falcon."
