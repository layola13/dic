moth:/mɒθ/ (UK) /mɑːθ/ (US)| n. |蛾|A nocturnal insect with large wings, similar to a butterfly but typically duller in colour and less active during the day.  It is attracted to light.|The moth fluttered around the porch light.  这只飞蛾在门廊的灯周围飞舞。|Synonyms:  miller|Antonyms: butterfly|noun
