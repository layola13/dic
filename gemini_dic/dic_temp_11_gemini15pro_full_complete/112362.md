lory:/ˈlɔːri/ (US), /ˈlɔːri/ (UK)| n.|吸蜜鹦鹉；(尤指澳大利亚和新西兰产的)鹦鹉|A type of small, brightly coloured parrot, found especially in Australia and New Zealand, having a tongue with a brushlike tip for feeding on nectar and pollen. |We saw several lorys flitting among the trees. 我们看到几只吸蜜鹦鹉在树丛间飞来飞去。|Synonym: parrot, parakeet|Antonym: N/A|noun

lory:/ˈlɔːri/ (US), /ˈlɔːri/ (UK)| n.|卡车；货车|A large, heavy motor vehicle for transporting goods or troops; a truck.|(Chiefly British) The lory was loaded with crates of fruit. 这辆卡车装满了水果箱。|Synonym: truck, lorry, articulated lorry|Antonym: N/A|noun
