boxkeeper:/ˈbɒksˌkiːpər/ (US) /ˈbɒksˌkiːpə(r)/ (UK)| n.|售票员；票房管理员；（剧院等的）包厢管理员|A person who sells tickets or manages the box office in a theater, cinema, or other entertainment venue.  Also, someone who manages a private box in a theater.|The boxkeeper handed me my tickets. 售票员把票递给了我。|synonyms: ticket seller, cashier, usher|antonyms: audience member, patron|noun
