nidra:/ˈnɪdrə/| n.|睡眠，瑜伽休息术|A state of conscious deep rest, similar to yogic sleep, often induced by guided meditation.  It is a practice of pratyahara (withdrawal of the senses).|She practiced yoga nidra for an hour every evening to relieve stress. 她每晚练习一个小时的瑜伽休息术来缓解压力。|Synonyms: sleep, slumber, repose, rest|Antonyms: wakefulness, insomnia|noun
