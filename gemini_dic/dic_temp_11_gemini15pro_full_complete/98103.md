"inrees" 不是一个标准的英语单词。 你可能是拼写错误了，或者它是一个专业术语、俚语或新造词。  请再次确认拼写。

如果你是想了解单词 "what" 的用法，我可以提供以下信息：

what: /wɒt/ (UK), /wʌt/ (US) | pronoun, determiner, adverb | 什么, 多么 |  used to ask for information about something | What is your name? 你叫什么名字？ | What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！| synonyms: which, how | antonyms:  N/A | pronoun, determiner, adverb

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? = 你觉得怎么样？
What does she want for her birthday? = 她生日想要什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you doing right now? = 你现在正在做什么？
What is he cooking in the kitchen? = 他在厨房里煮什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? = 她用这笔钱做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading all afternoon? = 他整个下午都在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 开会的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left? = 你离开之前做了什么？
What had they discussed before the decision was made? = 在做决定之前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I arrived? = 我来之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 她转专业之前一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? = 你下周末要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们在会议上将要讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have accomplished by the end of the year? = 到年底你将会完成什么？
What will they have built by next summer? = 到明年夏天他们将会建成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业的时候她将已经学习了什么多久？


PHRASE->

What the hell? = 搞什么鬼！
What if...? = 如果...会怎么样？
What for? = 为了什么？
So what? = 那又怎样？
