belluard:/ˈbɛlwərd/ | n. | 城墙上的外侧防御工事，外堡 | A fortified outpost, especially one projecting beyond the main defenses of a castle or town. |  The defenders retreated to the belluard as the enemy breached the main gate.  守军在敌人攻破城门后撤退到外堡。

 The belluard offered a commanding view of the surrounding countryside.  外堡提供了周围乡村的绝佳视野。| synonym: bulwark, bastion, rampart | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps 'interior' or 'inner defenses') | noun
