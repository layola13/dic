loopholed: /ˈluːpˌhəʊld/ | adj. | 漏洞百出的；有漏洞的 | Having loopholes; full of loopholes.  Characterized by or containing loopholes. | The contract was so loopholed that it was practically worthless.  这份合同漏洞百出，几乎毫无价值。 |  Synonyms: flawed, defective, leaky, porous | Antonyms: airtight, sound, impeccable | adjective

loophole: /ˈluːpˌhəʊl/ | n. | 漏洞；空子 | An ambiguity or inadequacy in the law or a set of rules. |  They found a loophole in the tax law. 他们在税法中找到了一个漏洞。 | Synonyms:  escape clause, technicality, workaround,  | Antonyms:  closure, blockage | noun
