interentangled:/ˌɪntərɪnˈtæŋɡld/
adj.
相互缠绕的，纠缠不清的
twisted together or intimately connected in a complicated way.
The climbers' ropes were interentangled, making it difficult to separate them. 攀登者的绳子相互缠绕，很难分开。
Their lives had become so interentangled that it was impossible to imagine one without the other.  他们的生活变得如此纠缠不清，以至于无法想象没有彼此的生活。
近义词: intertwined, interwoven, entangled
反义词: separate, disentangled, detached
词性:形容词

词源:
From inter- + entangled.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between," "among," or "reciprocally." "Entangled" comes from the Old French word "entaillier," meaning "to involve," ultimately from the Latin "in-" (in, into) and "talea" (a cutting, twig).
