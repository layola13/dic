coquettishness:/ˌkoʊˈkɛtɪʃnəs/ /ˌkɒˈkɛtɪʃnəs/| n. | 卖弄风情，娇媚，轻浮 | The quality of being coquettish; flirtatious behavior intended to attract admiration or attention. | She played the piano with an exaggerated coquettishness, tossing her hair and fluttering her eyelashes. 她弹钢琴时故作娇媚，甩动头发，眨动睫毛。| 近义词：flirtatiousness, archness, teasing, flirtation | 反义词：seriousness, modesty, demureness | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *coquetterie*, from *coquet* ("flirtatious"), from Middle French *coquet*, from *coqueter* ("to strut like a rooster"). Ultimately of imitative origin.
