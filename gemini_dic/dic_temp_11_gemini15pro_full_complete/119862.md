milestone: /ˈmaɪlstəʊn/ (UK) /ˈmaɪlstoʊn/ (US)
n.|里程碑，重大事件，转折点|A significant stage or event in the development of something.|例句：Graduating from university was a major milestone in her life.  大学毕业是她人生中一个重要的里程碑。

例句：The company reached a milestone in its development when it launched its new product.  公司推出新产品，在其发展过程中达到了一个里程碑。
近义词：landmark, turning point, achievement
反义词: setback, failure
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "mile" + "stone." Originally referred to stones placed at intervals of one mile along a road.
