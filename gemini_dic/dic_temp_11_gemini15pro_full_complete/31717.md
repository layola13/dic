chazy:/ˈtʃeɪzi/| adj.|混乱的，无秩序的；湍急的|Characterized by turbulent, unpredictable motion or behavior, often implying disorder or irregularity.  Originating from Lake Champlain, referring to its sometimes unpredictable and choppy waters.|The chazy waters of the river made kayaking difficult. 这条河混乱的水流使皮划艇很难行驶。|近义词：turbulent, chaotic, choppy, rough|反义词：calm, smooth, orderly, peaceful|词性:形容词
