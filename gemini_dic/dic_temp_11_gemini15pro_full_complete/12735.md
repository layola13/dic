asseverate:/əˈsɛvəˌreɪt/
verb
郑重声明；断言
To declare or state positively or emphatically; to affirm earnestly or solemnly.
He asseverated that he was innocent of the crime. 他郑重声明自己没有犯罪。
The witness asseverated the truth of her statement.  证人郑重声明她的陈述属实。

synonym
affirm, assert, avow, declare, maintain, pronounce, state
antonym
deny, gainsay, refute, contradict
词性
verb
