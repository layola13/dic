carbonation:/ˌkɑːrbəˈneɪʃn/ | n. |碳化，碳酸化作用|The process of dissolving carbon dioxide in a liquid, especially to make a fizzy drink. |The carbonation in the soda gives it a refreshing fizz. 苏打水中的碳酸化作用使其具有清爽的气泡。|例句：The slow carbonation of the beverage resulted in a subtle fizz. 饮料缓慢的碳酸化作用产生了微妙的气泡。

例句：Excessive carbonation can cause bloating. 过多的碳酸化会引起腹胀。|近义词：fizz, effervescence|反义词: flatness|词性: noun
