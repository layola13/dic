incubations: /ˌɪŋkjuˈbeɪʃənz/ | n. | 孵化；潜伏期；酝酿期 | the process of keeping something at a suitable temperature so that it can develop | The project is still in its early incubations.  这个项目仍处于早期孵化阶段。| synonyms: developments, gestations | antonyms: decline, stagnation | noun

incubations: /ˌɪŋkjuˈbeɪʃənz/ | n. | 孵化（incubation 的复数） |  the act of incubating; sitting on eggs to hatch them, or the period of time during which this is done | The incubations of the eagle eggs lasted for about 35 days. 这些鹰蛋的孵化期持续了大约35天。| synonyms: hatchings, broodings | antonyms:  abandonment | noun
