infranchise:/ɪnˈfræntʃaɪz/ | vt. | 给予…公民权；赋予…选举权；特许经营 | To grant the rights of citizenship, especially the right to vote; to grant a franchise to. |  The Nineteenth Amendment enfranchised women in the United States.  美国宪法第十九修正案赋予了美国妇女选举权。

The company decided to enfranchise several local businesses to sell their products.  该公司决定授权几家当地企业销售他们的产品。 | 近义词: empower, entitle, authorize | 反义词: disenfranchise, disempower | 词性: verb

enfranchise someone with something: 赋予某人某种权利/资格  The new law enfranchises citizens with the right to free healthcare. 新法律赋予公民免费医疗的权利。
