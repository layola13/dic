bedeviling:/bɪˈdevəlɪŋ/
verb
中文翻译：困扰，折磨
英文解析：causing continual trouble or difficulty
例句：Technical problems bedeviled the project from the start.  项目从一开始就受到技术问题的困扰。

例句：He was bedeviled by insecurities throughout his life. 他一生都被不安全感所困扰。
近义词：plaguing, tormenting, harassing, troubling
反义词：aiding, assisting, helping
词性：v.
