denoising:/diːˈnɔɪzɪŋ/ | n. |去噪，降噪|The process of removing noise from a signal, such as audio or images.|例句：Denoising algorithms are used to improve the quality of old recordings.  旧录音的质量可以通过降噪算法来提升。

例句：Image denoising is crucial for medical imaging.  图像去噪对于医学成像至关重要。

|近义词：noise reduction, noise suppression|反义词：noise addition, noise amplification|词性：名词

denoising:/diːˈnɔɪzɪŋ/ | adj. |去噪的，降噪的|Relating to or used for denoising.|例句：Denoising software can significantly improve sound quality.  去噪软件可以显著提高声音质量。

例句：He applied a denoising filter to the image. 他对图像应用了去噪滤镜。|近义词：noise-reducing|反义词：noise-amplifying|词性：形容词


词源:  "de-" (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + "noise" + "-ing" (suffix forming present participle and gerund)
