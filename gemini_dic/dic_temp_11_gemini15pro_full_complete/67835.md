eyre:/ɛər/ | n. |  继承人；流浪者；巡回的法官 | An archaic term for an heir, especially a travelling judge on circuit. |  He was the rightful eyre to the vast estate. 他是这 vast estate 的合法继承人。 |  The eyre traveled from town to town dispensing justice.  巡回法官从一个镇到另一个镇执行审判。 | synonym: heir, inheritor, successor | antonym:  predecessor | noun
