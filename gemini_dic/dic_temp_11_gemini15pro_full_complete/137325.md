nontangible:/ˌnɒnˈtændʒəbl/ /ˌnɑːnˈtændʒəbl/ | adj. n.|无形的，非物质的|Something that is nontangible cannot be touched.  It often refers to things like ideas, emotions, or concepts.|例句：Goodwill is a nontangible asset for any company. 商誉对任何公司来说都是一项无形资产。|近义词：intangible, immaterial, abstract|反义词:tangible, concrete, material|词性:形容词

nontangible:/ˌnɒnˈtændʒəbl/ /ˌnɑːnˈtændʒəbl/ | n.|无形资产|A nontangible asset is something that has value but cannot be physically touched. |例句：Patents and copyrights are valuable nontangibles. 专利权和版权是有价值的无形资产。|近义词：intangible asset, immaterial asset|反义词:tangible asset, physical asset|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + tangible.  "Tangible" comes from Late Latin "tangibilis" meaning "that can be touched," from Latin "tangere" meaning "to touch."
