mapped:/mæpt/ | v. | 映射，绘制地图 | past participle of "map" -  to make a map of an area; to show the features of an area on a map; to represent something in a diagrammatic or symbolic way. | They mapped the human genome. 他们绘制了人类基因组图谱。| charted, plotted, surveyed | unmapped, leave unexplored | verb
