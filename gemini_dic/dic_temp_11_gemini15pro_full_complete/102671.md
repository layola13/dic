jamborees:/ˈdʒæmbəriːz/
noun
大型集会，露营大会
A large celebration or party, especially one involving young people such as scouts.
The scout troop went to the national jamboree. 童子军部队参加了全国露营大会。
synonyms:gathering, rally, festival, fiesta
antonyms:separation, dispersal
