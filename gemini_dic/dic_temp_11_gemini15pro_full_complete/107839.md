lard-ass:/ˈlɑːrdæs/
lard-ass| n.|肥猪; 懒汉; 屁股肥大的人|A derogatory term for someone considered lazy, obese, or both.  This term is highly offensive and should be avoided.| "That lard-ass just sits on the couch all day." 那个懒汉整天就坐在沙发上。|近义词：fatso, slob, couch potato|反义词: athlete, fitness enthusiast|名词

lard-ass:/ˈlɑːrdæs/
lard-ass| adj.|肥胖的; 懒惰的|Used informally and offensively to describe someone as fat and lazy.  Again, this word is highly offensive and should be avoided.|"He's such a lard-ass, he never helps with anything." 他真是个懒胖子，从来不帮忙做任何事。|近义词：obese, lazy, indolent|反义词: active, energetic, diligent|形容词
