assever:/əˈsɜːv/ | v. | 断言，郑重声明 | To declare or state positively; to affirm. |  He asseverated his innocence. 他郑重声明自己是无辜的。

She asseverated that she had seen the whole thing. 她断言自己目睹了整个事件。| 近义词：affirm, assert, declare, maintain, avow | 反义词：deny, contradict, gainsay | 词性：动词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *asseverare*, from Latin ad- (“to, towards”) + severus (“serious, earnest”).  The notion is of speaking with seriousness and gravity.
