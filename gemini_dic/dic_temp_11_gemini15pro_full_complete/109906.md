liassic:/liˈæsɪk/
adjective
里阿斯统的；里阿斯纪的
Relating to or denoting the earliest part of the Jurassic period, between about 201 and 174 million years ago.
The Liassic period saw the emergence of many new dinosaur species.  里阿斯纪见证了许多新恐龙物种的出现。
The museum has a fine collection of Liassic fossils.  博物馆收藏了大量里阿斯纪的化石。
近义词: Early Jurassic
反义词: (None -  It's a specific geological period)
词性: adjective
