freckling:/ˈfrɛklɪŋ/
n.|雀斑，长雀斑|The act of developing freckles or the appearance of freckles on the skin.  The sun exposure resulted in some freckling on her nose.|例句：She noticed some freckling on her arms after spending the day at the beach.  她在海滩上待了一天后，注意到手臂上长了一些雀斑。

例句：The freckling on his face added to his boyish charm. 他脸上的雀斑增添了他的男孩气概。|近义词：speckling, spotting, dappling|反义词: clearing (of skin)|词性:名词


freckle:/ˈfrɛkl/
n.|雀斑|A small, light-brown spot on the skin, especially on the face, caused by exposure to sunlight.|例句：She has a scattering of freckles across her nose. 她的鼻子上散布着一些雀斑。

例句：He always gets more freckles in the summer. 他夏天总是会长出更多雀斑。|近义词：spot, speck, lentigo|反义词: N/A|词性:名词


freckle:/ˈfrɛkl/
v.|长雀斑|To cause or develop freckles on (skin).|例句：The sun freckled her face.  阳光使她脸上长了雀斑。

例句：His skin freckles easily. 他的皮肤很容易长雀斑。|近义词：speckle, spot, dapple|反义词: N/A|词性:动词


词源: From *freken, a diminutive of Old English *frecel, a speck, related to Old Norse freknur. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *frak-, a speck.
