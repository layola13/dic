alpines:/ˈælpaɪnz/ | adj. n. | 高山的，高山植物；高山居民 | Relating to high mountains; plants or people that live in high mountains. |  The alpines are blooming beautifully this year. 今年高山植物开得很漂亮。| 近义词：mountainous, highland | 反义词: lowland | 词性: 形容词, 名词

alpines:/ˈælpaɪnz/ |  n. | 高山植物；高山居民 | Plants or people that live in high mountains. |  The alpines have adapted to the harsh conditions. 高山植物已经适应了恶劣的环境。| 近义词：mountain dwellers, highlanders | 反义词: lowlanders | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *alpin*, from Latin *alpīnus*, from *Alpēs* ("the Alps"). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *albʰós* (“white”).
