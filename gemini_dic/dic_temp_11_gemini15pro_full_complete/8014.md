anthracite: /ˈænθrəsaɪt/ | n. | 无烟煤 | A hard, compact variety of coal that has a high luster. It has the highest carbon content, the fewest impurities, and the highest calorific content of all types of coal. | The blacksmith used anthracite in his forge because of its high heat output. 铁匠在他的锻造炉里使用无烟煤，因为它产生的热量很高。|  Anthracite burns with little smoke. 无烟煤燃烧时几乎没有烟雾。 | Synonyms: hard coal, black diamond, culm | Antonyms: lignite, peat | noun
