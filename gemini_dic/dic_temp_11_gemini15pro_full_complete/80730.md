gorse:/ɡɔːrs/| n.|金雀花，荆豆|A spiny, evergreen shrub with bright yellow flowers, common in Western Europe and often found on heathland and coastal areas.  It's known for its tough, prickly nature and vibrant blooms.|The hills were covered in gorse, a blaze of yellow against the blue sky.  山丘上覆盖着金雀花，在蓝天下像一片燃烧的黄色。|The gorse bushes provided shelter for the nesting birds. 金雀花丛为筑巢的鸟类提供了庇护所。|Synonyms: furze, whin|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
