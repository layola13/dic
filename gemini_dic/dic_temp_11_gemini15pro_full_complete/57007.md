double-parker:/ˈdʌbl ˌpɑːrkər/| n.|并排停车者；双排停车者|A person who parks their vehicle alongside another that is already parked at the curb, thus blocking it in.|Example 1: The double-parker blocked me in for twenty minutes!  那个双排停车的人把我堵了二十分钟！

Example 2:  Double-parkers are a nuisance in busy city streets.  在繁忙的城市街道上，双排停车的人很讨厌。|Near synonyms: inconsiderate parker, selfish parker|Antonyms: considerate parker|noun
