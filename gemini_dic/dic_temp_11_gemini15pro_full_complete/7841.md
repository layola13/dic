antarchist: /ˈæntɑːrkɪst/ | n. | 反统治者；反对任何形式统治的人 |  Someone who is opposed to all forms of government and ruling authority. |  He declared himself an antarchist, refusing to acknowledge any government's legitimacy. 他宣称自己是一个反统治者，拒绝承认任何政府的合法性。 |  Synonyms: anarchist, nihilist, rebel, insurgent | Antonyms: conformist, loyalist, authoritarian | Noun


Etymology:  From Ancient Greek ἀντί (antí, “against”) +‎ ἄρχων (árkhōn, “ruler”).
