maskable:/ˈmɑːskəbəl/ | adj. | 可屏蔽的，可掩蔽的 | Able to be masked or hidden; typically refers to an interrupt in computer systems that can be disabled or delayed. | The interrupt request (IRQ) is maskable, allowing the CPU to prioritize other tasks.  中断请求 (IRQ) 是可屏蔽的，允许 CPU 优先处理其他任务。| Synonyms: suppressible, inhibitable, concealable | Antonyms: unmaskable, non-maskable, unconcealable | adjective
