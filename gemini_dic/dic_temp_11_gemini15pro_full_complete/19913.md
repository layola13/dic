bio-gases:/ˈbaɪoʊˌɡæsɪz/
|n.|沼气，生物气体|A mixture of gases, primarily methane and carbon dioxide, produced by the anaerobic decomposition of organic matter such as manure or sewage.  It can be used as a fuel.|Bio-gases from the landfill site are collected and used to generate electricity. 垃圾填埋场的沼气被收集起来用于发电。|Synonyms: biogas, methane, landfill gas|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but could consider "fossil fuels" as a contrasting energy source)|Noun
