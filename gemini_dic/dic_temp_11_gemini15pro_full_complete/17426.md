beanbag:/ˈbiːnˌbæɡ/| n. |豆袋椅，豆袋|A soft, informal seat consisting of a fabric bag filled with dried beans or similar material.|She flopped down on the beanbag chair. 她猛地倒在豆袋椅上。|The children were throwing beanbags at the target. 孩子们正把豆袋扔向目标。|Synonyms: beanbag chair, floor cushion|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "rigid chair")|noun
