co-host:/koʊˈhoʊst/| n. v. |共同主持人，联合举办|A person who hosts something jointly with another or others. |例句：She co-hosted the event with her colleague. 她和她的同事共同主持了这次活动。

例句：They will co-host the Olympics next year. 他们明年将共同主办奥运会。|近义词：joint host, co-presenter|反义词: sole host|词性:名词,动词

co-host:/koʊˈhoʊst/| v. |共同主持，联合举办|To host something jointly with another or others. |例句：He co-hosted the radio show for five years. 他与别人共同主持了这档广播节目五年。

例句：We are co-hosting a party for our friends. 我们正在为朋友们合办一个聚会。|近义词：jointly host, co-present|反义词: host alone|词性:动词
