beyondnesses:/biˈjɑːndnəsɪz/
n.
超越性，超越的境界；超越的複数状态
The states or quality of being beyond; exceeding or surpassing limitations or boundaries in multiple ways or instances.
His art explores the beyondnesses of human experience, delving into realms of consciousness rarely accessed.  他的艺术探索了人类经验的超越性，深入到很少有人能接触到的意识领域。
The philosopher contemplated the beyondnesses of time and space. 这位哲学家思考着时间和空间的超越性。
Synonyms: transcendencies, immensities, sublimities
Antonyms: limitations, confinements, restrictions
