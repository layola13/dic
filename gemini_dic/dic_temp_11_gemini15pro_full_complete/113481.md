lyricism:/ˈlɪrɪsɪzəm/| n.|抒情性；抒情调；歌词|The quality of being lyrical, expressing deep personal emotion or observations in an imaginative and musical way.  It can refer to the expression of emotion in any art form, particularly in literature and music.|Her poetry is full of lyricism. 她的诗歌充满了抒情性。|近义词：poeticness, melodiousness, expressiveness|反义词：prosaicness, dullness|词性:noun
