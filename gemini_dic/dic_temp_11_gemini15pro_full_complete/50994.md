desiring:/dɪˈzaɪərɪŋ/
adj. |渴望的，想得到的|Having or expressing a strong want or wish for something.|She had a desiring look in her eyes as she gazed at the diamond necklace. 她凝视着钻石项链，眼里充满了渴望。|近义词：wanting, yearning, craving, coveting|反义词：apathetic, indifferent|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *desirynge*, present participle of *desiren*, from Anglo-Norman *desirer*, from Old French *desirer*, from Latin *dēsīderāre* (“to long for, desire, demand, expect, require, miss, regret”), from *dē-*, from *de* (“down, from”) + *sīderāre* (“to look at the stars”), from *sīdus* (“star, constellation”).
