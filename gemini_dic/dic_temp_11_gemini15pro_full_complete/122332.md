mixableness:/ˌmɪksəbəlnəs/
n.
可混合性, 可混性
The quality of being able to be mixed.
The mixableness of the paint allows for a wide range of custom colors.  这种油漆的可混合性使其能够调出各种各样的定制颜色。
mixability, blendability
immiscibility, unmixability
noun
