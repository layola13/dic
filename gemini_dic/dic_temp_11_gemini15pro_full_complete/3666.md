agglutinant:/əˈɡluːtɪnənt/

noun|粘合剂|A substance that causes adhesion; glue.|The carpenter used a strong agglutinant to join the pieces of wood. 木匠用一种强力粘合剂把木块粘在一起。|adhesive, glue, binder|solvent, separator
词性|adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *agglūtinant- (stem of agglūtināns), present participle of agglūtināre  "to glue to," from ad- "to" + glūtināre "to glue," from glūten "glue."
