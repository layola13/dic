buffeter:/ˈbʌfɪtər/ (US), /ˈbʌfɪtə/ (UK)| n.| 暴风雨袭击的人；自助餐常客；经常受打击的人|Someone who is exposed to and battered by strong winds or storms; someone who frequently eats at buffets; someone who endures hardship or repeated adversity.

例句：
The buffeter struggled against the gale-force winds.
暴风雨袭击的人在狂风中挣扎。

He's a real buffeter, always at the all-you-can-eat buffet.
他真是个自助餐常客，总是在无限量自助餐那里。

近义词：victim, sufferer, regular, habitué|
反义词: sheltered person, protected individual |
词性: noun
