intercloud:/ˈɪntərklaʊd/
n.
云间互联，云际互联
The connection and interaction between different cloud computing environments.
Data can be seamlessly transferred between different interclouds.  数据可以在不同的云间互联环境中无缝传输。
The company specializes in developing intercloud solutions for businesses.  这家公司专门为企业开发云间互联解决方案。
近义词: cloud federation, multi-cloud
反义词: single cloud
词性: 名词
