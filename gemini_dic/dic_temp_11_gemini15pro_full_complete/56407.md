dolichocephalic:/ˌdɒlɪkoʊsəˈfælɪk/ | adj. | 长头型的 | Having a relatively long head, with a cephalic index of under 75.  A skull is considered dolichocephalic when the ratio of its width to its length is less than 75%. |  Example 1:  The archaeologist discovered a dolichocephalic skull in the ancient burial site.  考古学家在古代墓地发现了一个长头型的头骨。

Example 2:  Dolichocephalic breeds of dogs, like Greyhounds, are prone to certain health issues. 像灰狗这样的长头型犬种容易患某些健康问题。 | 近义词: long-headed | 反义词: brachycephalic | 词性: adjective
