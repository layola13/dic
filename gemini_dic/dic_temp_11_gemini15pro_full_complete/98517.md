instate:/ˌɪnˈsteɪt/ | adj. | 州内的；本州的 | Existing or occurring within a state. |  Instate tuition is significantly cheaper than out-of-state tuition. 州内学费比州外学费便宜得多。|  Example: She qualified for instate tuition because she had lived in the state for over a year. 她有资格获得州内学费，因为她在这个州居住了一年以上。 | Synonyms: domestic, local | Antonyms: out-of-state, foreign | adjective
