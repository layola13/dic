nonimitative:/ˌnɒnˈɪmɪtətɪv/
adjective
非模仿的
Not imitative; original.  Not derived from or based on imitation.
Her style is fresh and nonimitative. 她的风格清新，不落俗套。

近义词:original, innovative, creative, novel
反义词:imitative, copied, derivative, unoriginal
词性:adjective
