envelope:/ˈenvələʊp/ (UK) /ˈɑːnvəloʊp/ (US)| n. |信封；包裹；封皮；包层|A flat paper container used for sending letters by post, or something that covers or surrounds something else.|例句：I sealed the letter in the envelope. 我把信封封好。|例句：The atmosphere is an envelope of gases around the Earth. 大气是围绕地球的气体层。|近义词：wrapper, covering, sheath|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly contents if referring to the contents inside) |词性:noun
