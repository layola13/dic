footboy:/ˈfʊtbɔɪ/| n. |男仆；侍童|A boy or young man employed as a servant, especially to run errands and attend to a person's needs. |例句：The footboy opened the carriage door for the lady. 男仆为女士打开了马车门。

例句：The wealthy merchant had several footboys attending to his every whim.  这位富商有几个侍童照顾他的所有突发奇想。|近义词：page, servant, attendant, valet |反义词:master, employer|noun
