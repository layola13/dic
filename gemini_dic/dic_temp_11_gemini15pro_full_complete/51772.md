devotionalist:/dɪˈvoʊʃənəlɪst/
n.
虔诚的信徒；献身者
A person who is extremely devoted to a religion or cause.
She is a devotionalist and attends church every Sunday. 她是一位虔诚的信徒，每个星期天都去教堂。
The devotionalists gathered for their annual pilgrimage. 这些虔诚的信徒聚集在一起进行他们的年度朝圣。
devotee, believer, adherent
skeptic, doubter
noun
