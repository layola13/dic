ETYMOLOGY->

From anti- + eavesdropping.  "Anti-" is a prefix of Greek origin meaning "against," "opposite of," or "opposed to." "Eavesdropping" comes from "eavesdrip," referring to water dripping from the eaves of a house.  Originally, an eavesdropper was someone who stood under the eaves of a house to listen to conversations inside.
