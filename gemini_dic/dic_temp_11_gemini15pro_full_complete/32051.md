cherry: /ˈtʃeri/ (US), /ˈtʃɛri/ (UK)| n. |樱桃；樱桃树；樱桃色；处女膜|A small, round, red or black fruit with a single hard stone in the middle, or the tree on which the fruit grows.  A bright red colour.|

例句：The cherry tree is in full bloom. 樱桃树盛开。

例句：She wore a cherry-red dress. 她穿了一件樱桃红色的连衣裙。


近义词：fruit, berry|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


cherry: /ˈtʃeri/ (US), /ˈtʃɛri/ (UK)| adj. |樱桃色的；鲜红色的|Bright red in colour.|

例句：Her lips were cherry red. 她的嘴唇是樱桃红的。

例句：He drove a cherry-red sports car. 他开着一辆樱桃红的跑车。


近义词：crimson, scarlet|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective
