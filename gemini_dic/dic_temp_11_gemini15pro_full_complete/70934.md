firecloud:/ˈfaɪərklaʊd/| n.|火烧云|A cloud that appears fiery red, typically at sunset or sunrise, due to the scattering of sunlight by atmospheric particles.|The firecloud painted the sky in vibrant hues of orange and red. 火烧云将天空染成了鲜艳的橙色和红色。|The sunset produced a breathtaking firecloud. 日落产生了一片令人叹为观止的火烧云。|近义词：sunset glow, afterglow|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider  grey sky, overcast sky)|noun
