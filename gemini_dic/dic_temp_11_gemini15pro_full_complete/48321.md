decretorial:/ˌdɛkrɪˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
法令的；教令的；依据教令的
Relating to, or having the nature of, a decree or decretal.
The decretorial was issued by the Pope.  教皇颁布了教令。
The king issued a decretorial banning public gatherings. 国王颁布法令禁止公众集会。
Synonyms: authoritative, canonical, ordained
Antonyms: unofficial, unsanctioned
词性: adjective
