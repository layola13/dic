baptization:/bæpˈtɪzəm/ | n. | 洗礼，浸礼 |  The religious rite of admission into the Christian Church, symbolized by the immersion of a person in water or the sprinkling of water on the head. |  His baptism took place in a small church. 他的洗礼仪式在一个小教堂举行。| synonyms: christening, initiation, immersion | antonyms: excommunication, apostasy | noun

baptize:/bæpˈtaɪz/ | v. | 为…施洗；(以…)命名 | To administer the rite of baptism to; to give a name to, especially at baptism. |  They baptized their child John. 他们给他们的孩子施洗，取名为约翰。| synonyms: christen, dedicate, name | antonyms:  None (The act of "unbaptizing" doesn't exist.) | verb
