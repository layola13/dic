leach:/liːtʃ/| v. n.|过滤，沥滤；(养分)流失，溶滤；(化学物质)渗漏，渗滤|To remove (nutrients or salts) from soil or rock by percolation.  (noun) A substance used to percolate or filter.|The rain leached the nutrients out of the soil. 雨水把土壤中的养分都冲走了。|The coffee grounds are steeped in hot water and then leached to make a brew. 咖啡渣浸泡在热水中，然后过滤以制成咖啡。|近义词：filter, percolate, seep, drain|反义词：accumulate, deposit|verb, noun

leach:/liːtʃ/| n.|沥滤液；沥滤灰；灰堆|The material removed by percolation or filtration.|The leach from the industrial waste contaminated the groundwater. 工业废料的渗滤液污染了地下水。|近义词：filtrate, effluent|反义词：deposit, sediment|noun
