gitano: /xiˈtano/ (Spanish), /dʒɪˈtɑːnoʊ/ (English)| adj. n.| 吉普赛人，吉普赛的 | A member of a traditionally nomadic people originating in northern India, now living chiefly in south and west Asia, Europe, and North America; Romani. Also used as an adjective to describe things related to Romani people or culture. |  例句：The gitano family traveled across Europe in their caravan.  吉普赛一家开着大篷车穿越欧洲。

例句：She was fascinated by the gitano music and dance. 她被吉普赛音乐和舞蹈深深吸引。

近义词：Roma, Romani, Gypsy (often considered pejorative) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym.  Could consider "settled" as an antonymous adjective related to lifestyle.) | 词性: adjective, noun
