auxiliary: /ɔːɡˈzɪliəri/ (US) /ɔːɡˈzɪljəri/ (UK)| adj. n.|辅助的；辅助者|Providing supplementary or additional help and support.  A person or thing that gives such help.|例句：The auxiliary engine started when the main engine failed. 辅助引擎在主引擎失效时启动了。|近义词：supplementary, additional, supporting, reserve, backup|反义词：primary, main, principal|词性: adjective, noun

auxiliary: /ɔːɡˈzɪliəri/ (US) /ɔːɡˈzɪljəri/ (UK)| n.|助动词|A verb (such as *have*, *be*, or *do*) that is used with another verb to show tense, person, etc.  |例句：She has finished her homework. 她已经完成了作业。( *has* is an auxiliary verb) |近义词：helping verb|反义词: (No direct antonym for a grammatical term)|词性: noun
