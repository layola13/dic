interspace:/ˈɪntərˌspeɪs/| n.|空隙，间隔，中间的空间|An interspace is a space between two things.|例句：There was a small interspace between the two buildings. 两栋建筑物之间有一小块空地。

例句：Plants were growing in the interspaces of the paving stones. 植物在铺路石的缝隙中生长。|近义词：gap, interval, space, opening|反义词:closure, connection, junction|noun
