cuttystool:/ˈkʌtistuːl/
n.
矮凳; 短凳; 
A low stool.
She sat on a cuttystool by the fire. 她坐在火炉旁的一张矮凳上。

近义词:stool, footstool, bench
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑高脚凳 high stool)
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Scots *cutty stool*,  from *cutty*  ("short") + *stool*.  The cutty-stool was a stool used for public penance, especially by those accused of fornication or adultery, in Scottish Presbyterian churches.
