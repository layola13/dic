antitechnologists:/ˈæntiˌtɛkˈnɑːlədʒɪsts/
n.
技术反对者，反技术者
People who oppose or resist the development and use of technology.
Antitechnologists argue that technological advancements often come at the expense of the environment. 技术反对者认为，技术进步往往是以牺牲环境为代价的。
Antitechnologists sometimes prefer simpler, more traditional ways of life. 技术反对者有时更喜欢更简单、更传统的生活方式。
Synonyms: Luddites, technophobes
Antonyms: technophiles, technology enthusiasts
