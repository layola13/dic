nummary 不是一个标准的英文单词。 你可能是想问 **summary**。 

summary: /ˈsʌməri/ | n. | 总结，概要，摘要 | a brief statement or account of the main points of something. | Example:  The teacher gave a summary of the lesson at the end of class. 老师在课结束时对课程进行了总结。

Example:  Could you give me a summary of the news? 你能给我概括一下新闻吗？ | Synonyms: synopsis, abstract, digest, outline,  recap | Antonyms: expansion, elaboration, detail  | noun
