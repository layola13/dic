例句：She is a laborious student, always studying late into the night. 她是一个勤奋的学生，总是学习到深夜。

近义词：arduous, strenuous, difficult, demanding, taxing, tiring, exhausting, grueling, backbreaking|反义词：easy, simple, effortless, facile|词性:形容词
