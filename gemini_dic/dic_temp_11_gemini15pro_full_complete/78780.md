gillers:/ˈɡɪlərz/| n.| 鳃, 呼吸器官 (通常指鱼类的),  鱼贩,卖鱼的人|A giller is a respiratory organ of aquatic animals, especially fish, that breathe dissolved oxygen in the water. It can also refer to a person who sells fish. |The fish's gillers were bright red. 这条鱼的鳃鲜红。|The gillers at the market had a wide selection of fresh fish. 市场上的鱼贩有各种各样的新鲜鱼。|近义词：fishmonger (for a person), gills|反义词: lung (as a respiratory organ)|词性: noun
