invulnerably:/ɪnˈvʌlnərəbli/
adverb
刀枪不入地，无懈可击地
in a way that cannot be harmed or defeated
例句：He spoke invulnerably about his past mistakes. 他毫不避讳地谈论他过去的错误。

例句：She seemed invulnerably confident. 她似乎有着无比的自信。

近义词：impregnable, invincible, unassailable
反义词：vulnerable, weak, susceptible
词性：adverb
