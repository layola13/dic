overfancifulnesses: /ˌoʊvərˈfæn(t)sɪfʊlnəsɪz/
noun
过度想象力；过分花哨；过分奇特
The state or quality of being excessively fanciful or imaginative, often to the point of being unrealistic or impractical.
His prose is marred by overfancifulnesses and a tendency toward purple prose. 他的散文因过度想象和倾向于华丽的辞藻而受损。
The overfancifulnesses of the plot made it difficult to follow.  情节的过度奇特使得它难以理解。

synonym:  extravagance, elaborateness,  ornateness, flamboyance
antonym:  simplicity, plainness, austerity,  understatement
