decretion:/dɪˈkriːʃən/ | n. |减少；分泌物；排泄物 |The process of decreasing or the product of such a process, especially a decrease by secretion or excretion.  A substance separated or discharged by the process of decretion. |The decretion of calcium from bone can lead to osteoporosis. 骨骼中钙的减少会导致骨质疏松。|近义词：secretion, excretion, discharge, reduction, diminution |反义词:augmentation, increase, accretion |词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *decretiōn*, from Latin dēcrētus, past participle of dēcernere “to decide, decree, separate,” from dē- “down, away from” + cernere “to sift, separate, distinguish.”
