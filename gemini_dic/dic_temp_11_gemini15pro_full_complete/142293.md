ortho:/ˈɔːrθoʊ/ (US), /ˈɔːθəʊ/ (UK)| prefix|正，直，原，邻|A prefix meaning "straight," "upright," "right," "true," "correct," or "in a normal condition," often used in technical and scientific terms.|Orthopedic surgery deals with the correction of deformities of the bones or muscles. 骨科手术处理骨骼或肌肉畸形的矫正。|Near synonyms: right, correct, normal|Antonyms: skewed, abnormal, irregular|prefix
