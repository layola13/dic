hexagrammoids:/ˈhɛksəɡræmɔɪdz/
|n.|六面体，六角形物体|A six-sided polyhedron or a figure resembling a six-sided polyhedron.  Specifically, it often refers to a member of a class of polyhedra related to the truncated tetrahedron.|
|例句：The mathematician studied the properties of hexagrammoids.  数学家研究了六面体的性质。|
|例句：The crystal structure formed a complex series of interconnected hexagrammoids. 晶体结构形成了一个复杂的互连六面体系列。|
|近义词：polyhedron, hexahedron|
|反义词：(No direct antonym, could be something like 'sphere' if contrasting shapes)|
|词性：noun|
