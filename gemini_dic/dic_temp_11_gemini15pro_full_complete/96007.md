Relating to or involving invasion or aggressive attack.|Her handwriting is elegant and cursive. 她的字迹优美流畅。|Synonyms: cursive, flowing, running, rapid, aggressive, invasive|Antonyms: print, block, non-cursive, defensive, receding|adjective
