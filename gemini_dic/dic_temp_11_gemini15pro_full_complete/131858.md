nonconsular:/ˌnɒnˈkɒnsjʊlə(r)/
adjective
非领事的
Not relating to or performed by a consul.
A nonconsular passport is issued to individuals who are not diplomats or consular officers.  非领事护照发放给非外交官或领事官员的个人。
The nonconsular staff at the embassy handles administrative tasks.  大使馆的非领事工作人员处理行政任务。
Synonyms: nondiplomatic, civilian
Antonyms: consular, diplomatic
词性: adjective
