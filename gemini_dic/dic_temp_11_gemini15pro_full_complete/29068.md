carve-up: /ˈkɑːv ʌp/ (British), /ˈkɑːrv ʌp/ (American) | n. |瓜分，分割；（尤指不公平的）划分| An act of dividing something into parts, often in a way that is seen as unfair or self-serving. |The carve-up of the company's assets left many small shareholders with nothing. 公司资产的瓜分让许多小股东一无所有。|Synonyms: division, partition, split, share-out | Antonyms: unification, merger, combination | Noun
