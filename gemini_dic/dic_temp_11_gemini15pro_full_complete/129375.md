nomadization:/ˌnoʊmədaɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.|游牧化，游牧生活|The process of becoming nomadic or the state of being nomadic; the adoption of a nomadic lifestyle.|The nomadization of the tribe was a gradual process, driven by changes in climate and available resources. 部落的游牧化是一个渐进的过程，是由气候和可用资源的变化驱动的。|近义词：migration, wandering, pastoralism|反义词:settlement, sedentarization|词性:名词

Etymology:  From nomad + -ization.  Ultimately from Greek *νομάς (nomas, “roaming, wandering”)
