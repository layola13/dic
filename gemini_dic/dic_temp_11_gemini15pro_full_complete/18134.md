PHRASE->

it behooves one to = 一个人有必要
it ill behooves sb to do sth = 某人不应该做某事 (ill behooves -  acts against what is proper or fitting)
it behooves us all to = 我们所有人都有必要
as behooves = 正如...应该的那样
