nonfilterable:/ˌnɒnˈfɪltərəbl/ /ˌnɑːnˈfɪltərəbl/| adj.|不可过滤的|Not able to be filtered, usually referring to substances too small to be removed by a filter.|The virus is nonfilterable, making it difficult to remove from water supplies. 这种病毒不可过滤，因此很难从供水中去除。|Synonyms: unfilterable|Antonyms: filterable|Adjective
