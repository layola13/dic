ninepins:/ˈnaɪnˌpɪnz/ | n. | 九柱戏；九柱戏木瓶 | A bowling game played with nine wooden pins set up in a diamond formation. |  We played ninepins in the park yesterday.  我们昨天在公园玩了九柱戏。

The ninepins were scattered across the alley. 九柱戏的木瓶散落在球道上。


|近义词：bowling, skittles |反义词: None |词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *nine pynnes*, from Old English *nigon pinnas* (nine pins).  The word "pin" comes from Old French *pin*, ultimately from Vulgar Latin *pinnus*.
