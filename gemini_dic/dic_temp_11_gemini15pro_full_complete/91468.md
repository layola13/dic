hyloism:/ˈhaɪloʊɪzəm/
n.
物质活力论，泛心论
The philosophical doctrine that matter is imbued with life.
Hyloism proposes that all matter, even seemingly inanimate objects, possesses a kind of life or soul.  物质活力论认为，所有物质，即使是看似无生命的物体，也拥有一种生命或灵魂。
The philosopher argued for hyloism, suggesting even rocks have a rudimentary form of consciousness. 这位哲学家主张泛心论，认为即使是岩石也有一种基本的意识形式。
近义词:panpsychism, animism
反义词:materialism, mechanism
词性:noun
