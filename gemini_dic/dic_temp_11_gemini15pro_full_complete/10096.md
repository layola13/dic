apodes:/ˈæpoʊˌdiːz/ /ˈæpəʊˌdiːz/
n.
无足类动物; 无足目动物
legless vertebrates; an order of amphibians, also known as caecilians.
Apodes are burrowing amphibians that lack limbs. 无足类动物是缺乏四肢的穴居两栖动物。
Caecilians, members of the order Apodes, are often mistaken for worms or snakes.蚓螈，无足目的成员，经常被误认为是蠕虫或蛇。
近义词:caecilians, gymnophiona
反义词: (No direct antonym, as it is a specific biological order.  One could consider animals *with* legs as a broad contrast, but this isn't a true antonym.)
词性: 名词
