obsolete:/ˈɒbsəliːt/ (UK) /ˌɑːbsəˈliːt/ (US)| adj.|过时的，废弃的，淘汰的|No longer produced or used; out of date.|Example: Floppy disks are now obsolete.  软盘现在已经过时了。|Example:  The old factory uses obsolete machinery.  这家老工厂使用的是过时的机器。|Synonyms: outdated, outmoded, archaic, antiquated, superseded|Antonyms: modern, current, contemporary, up-to-date|Adjective
