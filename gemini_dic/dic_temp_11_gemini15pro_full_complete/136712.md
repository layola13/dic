nonsemantic:/ˌnɒn.sɪˈmæn.tɪk/ | adj. | 非语义的；无意义的 |  Relating to the non-semantic aspects of language, such as phonetics or syntax, rather than meaning.  Not concerned with meaning; having no meaning. | The nonsemantic aspects of the poem, such as rhyme and meter, were more interesting than its actual message. 这首诗的非语义方面，如韵律和格律，比其实际信息更有趣。| Synonyms: meaningless, nonsensical, insignificant | Antonyms: semantic, meaningful, significant | adjective
