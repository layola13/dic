lurer's:/ˈlʊrərz/
noun
引诱者, 欺骗者的 (所有格)
possessive form of lurer, meaning "belonging to or associated with a lurer."  A lurer is someone who tempts or tricks someone into a particular course of action.
The lurer's tricks were eventually discovered. 骗子的伎俩最终被发现了。
Synonyms: deceiver's, trickster's, con artist's, scammer's
Antonyms: helper's, assistant's, guide's
