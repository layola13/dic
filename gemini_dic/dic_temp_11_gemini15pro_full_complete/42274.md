counter-criticism:/ˌkaʊntərˈkrɪtɪsɪzəm/
noun
反批评，反驳
An argument made in opposition to another argument, especially one that criticizes something previously said or written.
The politician offered a counter-criticism of the journalist's claims.  这位政治家对记者的说法提出了反驳。
The author’s counter-criticism effectively addressed the points raised by the reviewers.  作者的反驳有效地回应了评论员提出的观点。
Synonym: rebuttal, refutation, response
Antonym: criticism, attack
n.
