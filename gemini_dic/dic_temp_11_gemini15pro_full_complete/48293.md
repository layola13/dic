decree: /dɪˈkriː/
n.|法令，命令；判决|An official order that has the force of law. |The king issued a decree banning public gatherings. 国王颁布法令禁止公众集会。|edict, order, proclamation, judgment, ruling|repeal, countermand|noun
decree: /dɪˈkriː/
vt.|颁布，下令；判决|To issue a decree; to order something by decree. |The court decreed that the company must pay damages. 法院判决该公司必须支付赔偿金。|order, command, pronounce, rule|rescind, revoke|verb
