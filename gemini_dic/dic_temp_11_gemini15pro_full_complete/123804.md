moping:/ˈməʊpɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈmoʊpɪŋ/ (US)| v. n.|拖地；闷闷不乐|Cleaning the floor with a mop. Feeling depressed and listless.|He was moping around the house all day after he lost his job. 他失业后整天在家闷闷不乐。|近义词：sulking, brooding, cleaning, wiping|反义词：celebrating, rejoicing|词性: 动词/名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *moppen*, perhaps of imitative origin.  Related to Dutch *moppen* ("to pout, sulk"). The sense of cleaning with a mop developed later.
