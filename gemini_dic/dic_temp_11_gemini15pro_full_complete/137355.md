词源: "nonteachabilities" is derived from "teach" + "-able" + "non-" + "-ity" + "-ies" (plural).  The root word "teach" comes from Old English *tǣcan.  "-able" is a suffix indicating capability or suitability. "non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "-ity" is a suffix forming nouns expressing state or condition.  "-ies" is the plural suffix.
