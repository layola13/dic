galvani:/ɡælˈvæni/ | n. | 伽伐尼 | A unit of electromotive force. (Obsolete) |  The term "galvani" is rarely used as a unit of measurement now.  “伽伐尼”这个词现在很少用作计量单位。 | 近义词: volt | 反义词: None | noun
