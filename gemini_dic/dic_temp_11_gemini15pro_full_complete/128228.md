neurodermatosis:/ˌnʊroʊˌdɜːrməˈtoʊsɪs/ /ˌnjʊərəʊˌdɜːməˈtəʊsɪs/| n. |神经性皮炎|A chronic skin condition characterized by itching and scratching, often leading to lichenification (thickening and hardening of the skin).  It is often associated with psychological factors such as stress and anxiety.|She suffers from neurodermatosis, which is exacerbated by stress.|Synonym: lichen simplex chronicus, atopic dermatitis (sometimes)|Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a disease.  One could consider "healthy skin" a contrasting concept.)|noun
