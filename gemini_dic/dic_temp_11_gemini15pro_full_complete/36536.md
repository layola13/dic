collaborative:/kəˈlæbərətɪv/| adj.|合作的，协作的|Produced or conducted by two or more parties working together. |例句：The project was a collaborative effort between several departments. 这个项目是几个部门共同努力的结果。

例句：We adopted a collaborative approach to problem-solving. 我们采取了一种合作的方式来解决问题。|近义词：cooperative, joint, combined, shared|反义词：independent, solo, individual|词性:adjective
词源:Late 19th century: from collaborate + -ive.
