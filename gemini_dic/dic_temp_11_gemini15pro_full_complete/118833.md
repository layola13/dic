metaphonic:/ˌmɛtəˈfɒnɪk/ /ˌmɛtəˈfɑːnɪk/| adj.|变音的，元音变换的|Relating to or characterized by metaphony, a vowel change in which an unstressed vowel in a word influences a stressed vowel.  Specifically, a type of sound change in which a vowel is influenced by a following vowel or semivowel.|The metaphonic changes in Old English are complex and fascinating.|近义词:umlauted (though not perfectly synonymous), vowel-shifted|反义词: (No direct antonym exists. Consider "unchanged" in specific contexts.)|词性:adjective
