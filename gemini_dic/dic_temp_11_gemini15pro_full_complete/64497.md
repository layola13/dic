erodes:/ɪˈroʊdz/ (American) /ɪˈrəʊdz/ (British)| v. |侵蚀，腐蚀|gradually wears away or destroys something, often by natural forces like wind or water, but also by chemical action or other processes.  |例句：The coastline is slowly being eroded by the sea. 海岸线正慢慢地被海水侵蚀。

例句：Acid rain erodes stone buildings. 酸雨腐蚀石材建筑。|近义词：wear away, corrode, abrade, disintegrate|反义词：build up, strengthen, deposit|词性:动词
