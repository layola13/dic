overapprehensive:/ˌoʊvərˌæprɪˈhɛnsɪv/| adj.|过度忧虑的，过分担心的|Excessively apprehensive; unduly or excessively worried or fearful about something that might happen.|She was overapprehensive about the upcoming exam, even though she had studied diligently.|近义词：anxious, worried, apprehensive, fearful, solicitous|反义词：calm, relaxed, composed, confident, carefree|词性:形容词
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "apprehensive" (from Latin "apprehendere" - to seize, grasp, understand).  The sense evolved from grasping something mentally (understanding) to anticipating something, often with fear.
