equaled: /ˈiːkwəld/ (US), /ˈiːkwəld/ (UK)
verb
等于；比得上
made equal to something else; amounted to the same as something else.
Her test scores equaled his. 她的考试分数和他一样。
synonyms: matched, amounted to, corresponded to
antonyms: differed from, surpassed, fell short of
词性: 动词

equaled: /ˈiːkwəld/ (US), /ˈiːkwəld/ (UK)
adjective
相等的，平等的 (less common as an adjective)
Being the same or equivalent in value, quantity, or measure.
Their contributions were equaled by their dedication. 他们的贡献与他们的奉献精神相当。
synonyms: equivalent, identical, uniform
antonyms: unequal, different, dissimilar
词性: 形容词


词源:  From the Old French word *egaler*, derived from Latin *aequalis* meaning "equal," ultimately from *aequus*  "level, even, fair."
