intone:/ɪnˈtoʊn/ | v. |吟诵，咏唱，用某种语调说话|to utter in musical or prolonged tones; chant.  He intoned the sacred verses. 他吟诵了神圣的经文。| 近义词：chant, recite, sing, utter | 反义词：whisper, murmur | 词性: 动词

词源：From Late Latin *intonare, from Latin in- + tonare “to thunder”.
