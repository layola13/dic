mintier:/ˈmɪntiər/ | adj. | 更虚伪的，更矫情的 (非正式用法) | More affectedly or pretentiously proper or refined; more sanctimonious (informal usage). | Her mintier-than-thou attitude annoyed everyone. 她那副比谁都高尚的态度惹恼了大家。| synonyms: more sanctimonious, more hypocritical, holier-than-thou | antonyms: genuine, sincere, down-to-earth | adjective
