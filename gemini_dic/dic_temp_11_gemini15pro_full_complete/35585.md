coannex:/ˌkoʊəˈnɛks/| vt.|合并，兼并；附加|To join or combine (something) with something else, especially as a subordinate part. |The two companies were coannexed to form a larger corporation.  两家公司合并成了一家更大的公司。|The smaller library was coannexed to the main branch.  较小的图书馆被并入了总馆。|Synonyms: merge, combine, unite, amalgamate, consolidate|Antonyms: separate, divide, detach, disconnect|verb
