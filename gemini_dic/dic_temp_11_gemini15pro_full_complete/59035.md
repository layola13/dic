dwarfisms: /ˈdwɔːrfɪzəmz/ | n. |侏儒症|  A group of genetic or medical conditions characterized by unusually short height.  It refers to a range of conditions, not just one specific type. |  Achondroplasia is one of the most common forms of dwarfisms. 软骨发育不全是最常见的侏儒症之一。|  There are many different types of dwarfisms, each with its own specific characteristics.  有很多不同类型的侏儒症，每一种都有其特定的特征。| synonyms: short stature, restricted growth | antonyms: gigantism | noun
