breathableness:/ˈbriːðəbəlnəs/
n.
透气性，呼吸能力
The quality of being breathable; allowing air to pass through.
This fabric has excellent breathability, making it perfect for summer wear.  这种面料透气性极佳，非常适合夏季穿着。
permeability, porosity, ventilation
impermeability, airtightness
noun
