curd:/kɜːrd/| n. |凝乳，豆腐|A soft, white substance formed when milk sours, used as the basis for making cheese.  Also, any substance resembling this.|例句：She added lemon juice to the milk to make curds. 她往牛奶里加柠檬汁做凝乳。|近义词：coagulum, clabber|反义词: whey (乳清, 指的是凝乳分离后的液体部分, 与凝乳互补)|名词
