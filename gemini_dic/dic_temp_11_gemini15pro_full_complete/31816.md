checksum: /ˈtʃɛksʌm/ | n. |校验和，检验码 |A number produced by a calculation performed on a block of data to check whether it has been transmitted or stored correctly.  |Example: The checksum verified that the file had not been corrupted during download. 校验和验证了文件在下载过程中没有损坏。|Example:  Before burning the ISO image, always check the checksum to ensure data integrity. 在刻录ISO镜像之前，务必检查校验和以确保数据完整性。 |Synonym: hash, digest, CRC (Cyclic Redundancy Check) |Antonym: N/A |noun
