epistemically:/ˌɛpɪˈstɛmɪkəli/ | adv. | 认识论地 |  In a way that relates to the theory of knowledge, especially with regard to its methods, validity, and scope. |  Epistemically, his argument is weak because it relies on anecdotal evidence. 认识论上，他的论点很薄弱，因为它依赖于轶事证据。 |  Cognitively, intellectually |  Empirically, practically | adverb
