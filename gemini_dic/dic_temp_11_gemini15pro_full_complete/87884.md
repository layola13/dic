hied:/haɪd/
verb
躲藏，隐匿
went into hiding; hid
例句：The fox hied in its den. 狐狸躲藏在它的洞穴里。

例句：He hied himself away in the forest. 他把自己藏在森林里。
近义词：concealed, sheltered, secreted
反义词：revealed, exposed
词性：v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English hiden, from Old English hȳdan, from Proto-Germanic *hūdijaną (“to hide, conceal”), from Proto-Indo-European *keudh- (“to hide, conceal”). Cognate with Dutch huiden (“to hide”), German hüten (“to guard, protect, keep”), Old Norse hylja (“to cover, conceal”).
