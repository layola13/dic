decrees:/dɪˈkriːz/| n. |法令，命令；判决|An official order that has the force of law; a judicial decision. |The king issued a decree banning public gatherings. 国王颁布了一项禁止公众集会的法令。

decrees:/dɪˈkriːz/| v. |颁布，下令；判决|To issue a decree; to order something officially; to decide judicially. |The court decreed that the company must pay damages. 法院判决该公司必须支付赔偿金。

Etymology: From Old French *decreer* (Modern French *décréter*), from Latin *decernere* "to decide, decree," from *de-* "down, completely" + *cernere* "to distinguish, separate, perceive, decide."

Synonyms:(n.) order, edict, proclamation, judgment, ruling, mandate
Synonyms:(v.) ordain, command, pronounce, adjudge

Antonyms:(n.) repeal, revocation, countermand
Antonyms:(v.) rescind, revoke, countermand
