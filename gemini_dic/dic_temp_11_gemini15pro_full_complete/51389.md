determining:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪnɪŋ/
| adj. | 决定性的；果断的 | Having the power to determine an outcome; resolute. | Her determining factor in choosing a college was its proximity to home. 她选择大学的决定性因素是离家近。 | Example:  The judge's ruling was the determining factor in the case. 法官的裁决是该案的决定性因素。| Synonyms: decisive, conclusive, crucial, pivotal | Antonyms: insignificant, unimportant, minor, trivial | word property:adjective


determining:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪnɪŋ/
| n. | 测定；决定 | The act of finding something out or establishing exactly, typically as a result of research or calculation. | The determining of the ship's position was crucial. 对船舶位置的测定至关重要。| Example: The precise determining of the boundary line was essential. 精确测定边界线至关重要。 | Synonyms:  ascertaining, establishing, calculating, finding | Antonyms: guessing, estimating, supposing, conjecturing | word property:noun
