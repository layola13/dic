investor:/ɪnˈvɛstər/ | n. | 投资者 |  Someone who puts money into something, such as a business or property, with the expectation of making a profit. |  Example 1: The investor bought a large stake in the company.  这位投资者购买了该公司的大量股份。

Example 2:  Foreign investors are showing increasing interest in the local market. 外国投资者对当地市场越来越感兴趣。 | Synonyms: financier, stakeholder, backer | Antonyms: borrower, debtor | Noun
