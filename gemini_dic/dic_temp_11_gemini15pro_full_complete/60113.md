effaced:/ɪˈfeɪst/| adj.|抹掉的，擦掉的，消除的|Erased, wiped out, obliterated; made indistinct.  The inscription on the tombstone had been effaced by the weather.|例句：The wind and rain had effaced the inscription on the old headstone. 风雨抹去了旧墓碑上的铭文。

例句：His expression was effaced, making it hard to read his emotions. 他的表情消失了，让人难以读懂他的情绪。|近义词：erased, obliterated, expunged, deleted|反义词：preserved, enhanced, emphasized|词性:形容词
词源->
From French *effacer*, from *ex-* (out) + *face* (face). Ultimately from Latin *facies* (face, form, appearance).
