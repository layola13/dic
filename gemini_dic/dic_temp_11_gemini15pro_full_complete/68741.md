farmhands: /ˈfɑːrmhændz/
n.
农场工人，农业工人
A person employed to do manual labor on a farm.
The farmhands were busy harvesting the wheat. 农场工人们正忙着收割小麦。

The farmhands started their day before sunrise.  农场工人们日出之前就开始工作了。


近义词: farmworker, agricultural laborer, ranch hand, field hand
反义词: owner, manager, supervisor
词性: 名词 (plural)
