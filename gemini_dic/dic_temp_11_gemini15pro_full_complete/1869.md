acropolises: /əˈkrɒpəlɪsiːz/
n.
卫城（复数）
A citadel or fortified part of an ancient Greek city, typically built on a high rock.
The acropolises of Athens and Corinth are popular tourist destinations.  雅典和科林斯的卫城是热门旅游目的地。

近义词: citadels, fortresses
反义词: (无明确反义词)
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀκρόπολις (akrópolis, “upper city, citadel”), from ἄκρος (ákros, “highest, topmost”) + πόλις (pólis, “city”).
