half-ashamed:/ˌhɑːf əˈʃeɪmd/ | adj. | 半惭愧的，略带羞愧的 | Feeling slightly ashamed or embarrassed; showing some shame or guilt. |  He gave a half-ashamed grin. 他咧嘴一笑，略带羞愧。 | 近义词: sheepish, embarrassed, guilty, apologetic | 反义词: proud, shameless, unashamed | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "half-" (Old English *half, from Proto-Germanic *halbaz) + "ashamed" (from Old English ofsceamian "to feel shame," from sceamu "shame").
