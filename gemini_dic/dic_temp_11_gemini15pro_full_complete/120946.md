miscensuring: /ˌmɪsˈsɛnʃərɪŋ/
| adj. | 错误地责备的；不公正地批评的 |  Wrongly or unjustly censuring; blaming or criticizing unfairly.  | He felt he was being miscensured for the mistakes of others. 他觉得自己因他人的错误而受到不公正的批评。| Near synonyms: misjudging, misconstruing, wrongly blaming. | Antonyms:  praising, commending, justly criticizing. |词性: 形容词
