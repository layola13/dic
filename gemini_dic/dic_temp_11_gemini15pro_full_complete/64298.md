equivalve:/ˌiːkwɪˈvælv/ | adj. | 等瓣的；双壳相等的 | Having two valves that are equal in size and shape. | The equivalve shell of the clam was perfectly symmetrical.  这只蛤蜊的等瓣壳完全对称。 | 近义词: equivalved | 反义词: inequivalve | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *aequivalvis*, from aequus (“equal”) + valva (“leaf of a folding door, valve”).
