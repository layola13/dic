baulked:/bɔːkt/ /bɑːlkt/ | v. | 阻碍，阻止，畏缩不前 | to refuse to proceed or to allow something to proceed | The horse baulked at the jump. 马在跳跃前畏缩不前。| synonyms: hesitate, refuse, balk, recoil, shy | antonyms:  proceed, continue, accept | verb

baulk:/bɔːk/ /bɑːlk/ | n. | 障碍，阻碍物 | a hindrance or obstacle |  His refusal to cooperate was a major baulk to the project. 他拒绝合作是这个项目的主要障碍。| synonyms: obstacle, hindrance, impediment, obstruction | antonyms:  aid, assistance, help | noun
