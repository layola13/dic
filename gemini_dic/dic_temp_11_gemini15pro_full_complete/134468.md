nonjuring:/ˌnɒnˈdʒʊərɪŋ/
adjective
不愿宣誓的；拒绝对新政权效忠的
Refusing to take an oath of allegiance, especially to the new monarchs William and Mary after the Glorious Revolution of 1688.
The nonjuring bishops were deprived of their sees.  不愿宣誓效忠的主教们被剥夺了主教职位。
Synonyms:  recusant, dissident, objecting
Antonyms:  loyal, compliant, conforming
词性: adjective
