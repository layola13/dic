breasthook: /ˈbrestˌhʊk/ | n. | 胸梁，胸钩，胸板 | A horizontal timber or metal strap connecting and strengthening two opposite rafters or principals, especially in a roof.  A curved timber or metal fitting across the bows of a boat or ship, connecting and strengthening them. | The breasthook in the attic was old and needed replacing.  阁楼里的胸梁很旧了，需要更换。


The breasthook on the small sailboat helped to maintain the shape of the hull. 这艘小帆船上的胸板有助于保持船体的形状。 | synonym: breast beam, collar beam, tie beam (in certain contexts) | antonym: N/A | noun
