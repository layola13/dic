gibaro: /ɡiˈβaɾo/ | n. | 吉巴罗人 | A member of a small group of culturally related indigenous peoples inhabiting the mountainous regions of Puerto Rico. They were known for their independent spirit and agricultural skills. |  The gibaros played a significant role in shaping Puerto Rican culture. 吉巴罗人在塑造波多黎各文化方面发挥了重要作用。|  The museum exhibit showcased traditional gibaro crafts. 博物馆的展览展示了传统的吉巴罗工艺品。 | synonyms:  Taino,  indigenous people,  mountain people | antonyms:  mainlander, foreigner | noun
