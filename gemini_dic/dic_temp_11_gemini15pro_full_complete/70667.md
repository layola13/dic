fin:/fɪn/
n.
鳍; 鱼翅; 蹼; 鳍状物; 鱼翅汤
a thin, flat part that sticks out from the body of a fish, used for swimming
The fish uses its fins to propel itself through the water.  鱼用鳍在水中推动自己前进。

fin:/fɪn/
n.
(汽车、飞机、炸弹等的)尾翼，鳍状平衡器
a part that sticks out from the body of a vehicle, aircraft, or bomb, used for stability or control
The plane's tail fin helps to keep it stable in the air. 飞机的尾翼有助于它在空中保持稳定。


近义词:flipper, wing
反义词:(无明显的反义词)
词性:名词
