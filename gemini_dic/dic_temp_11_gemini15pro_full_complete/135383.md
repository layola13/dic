nonperiodically:/ˌnɒnˌpɪəriˈɒdɪkli/ /ˌnɑːnˌpɪriˈɑːdɪkli/| adv.|非周期地，不定期地|Not occurring at regular intervals; not periodic.|The comet appears nonperiodically, making its trajectory difficult to predict. 这颗彗星不定期出现，使其轨迹难以预测。|The geyser erupts nonperiodically, sometimes weeks or even months apart.  这个间歇泉不定期喷发，有时相隔数周甚至数月。|近义词：aperiodically, irregularly, sporadically|反义词: periodically, regularly|词性:副词

Etymology:
non- + periodically.  "Non-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "not." "Periodically" comes from "periodic" + "-ally." "Periodic" derives from the Late Latin "periodicus," and ultimately from the Greek "periodikos," meaning "recurring at regular intervals."
