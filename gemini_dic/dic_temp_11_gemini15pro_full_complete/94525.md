imposers:/ɪmˈpoʊzərz/| n. |强加者；施加者；冒名顶替者|People who impose something on others, such as rules, beliefs, or their own presence.  Those who falsely claim a particular identity or skill.|The new management team were seen as imposers by the existing staff. 新的管理团队被现有员工视为强加者。|Synonyms: dictator, authoritarian, usurper, pretender, intruder |Antonyms:  subordinate, follower, conformist|noun
