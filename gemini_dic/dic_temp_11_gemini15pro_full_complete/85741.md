hawkings:/ˈhɔːkɪŋz/
n.
霍金的 (所有格)；霍金的作品/理论/著作等
Referring to something related to, owned by, or characteristic of Stephen Hawking, the renowned physicist.
例句：
Hawking's theories on black holes are revolutionary.
霍金关于黑洞的理论是革命性的。

Hawking's books have popularized complex scientific concepts.
霍金的书籍普及了复杂的科学概念。

近义词: (No direct synonyms, as it's a proper noun.  Could use "related to cosmology", "related to theoretical physics" depending on context)
反义词: (None. Proper nouns generally don't have antonyms)
词性: Noun (proper noun, possessive form)
