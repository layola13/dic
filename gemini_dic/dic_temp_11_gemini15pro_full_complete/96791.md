inergetically:/ˌɪnərˈdʒɛtɪkli/
adjective
惰性地; 不积极地
lacking energy or enthusiasm; sluggishly.
The team played inergetically, showing little enthusiasm for the game.  该队比赛懒散，对比赛缺乏热情。
Near synonyms: lethargically, listlessly, apathetically, passively
Antonyms: energetically, enthusiastically, actively, vigorously
词性: adjective
