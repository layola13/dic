lymphatic:/lɪmˈfætɪk/
adjective|淋巴的；关于淋巴的|Relating to lymph or the lymphatic system.|The lymphatic system plays a vital role in immunity. 淋巴系统在免疫中起着至关重要的作用。|
lymphatic:/lɪmˈfætɪk/
noun|淋巴管|A vessel of the lymphatic system.|Lymph flows through the lymphatics. 淋巴液流经淋巴管。|近义词:lymph vessel|反义词: (No direct antonym exists for a physical structure like a lymphatic vessel)
Etymology: From Late Latin lymphaticus (“distracted, frantic”), from Latin lympha (“water, clear liquid”), from Greek νύμφη (númphē, “water nymph”).  The sense shifted to "lymph" and related terms due to the clear watery appearance of the fluid.
