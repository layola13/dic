childproof:/ˈtʃaɪldpruːf/
adjective
防儿童的; 防止儿童开启的
Designed to prevent children from opening or using something potentially dangerous.
例句：
The cabinet has a childproof lock.  这个橱柜有一个防儿童锁。

Make sure all medications are stored in childproof containers. 确保所有药物都存放在防儿童开启的容器中。


近义词：child-resistant, tamper-proof, safety
反义词：accessible to children

词性: adjective
