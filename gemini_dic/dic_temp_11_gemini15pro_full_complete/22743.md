borosilicate:/ˌbɔːroʊˈsɪlɪkeɪt/
adjective|硼硅酸盐的|A type of glass that contains boron trioxide and silica, making it resistant to thermal shock.|Laboratory glassware is often made of borosilicate glass because it can withstand high temperatures. 实验室玻璃器皿通常由硼硅酸盐玻璃制成，因为它可以承受高温。|
近义词:heat-resistant glass, Pyrex (trademark)|反义词:soda-lime glass|词性:adjective

borosilicate:/ˌbɔːroʊˈsɪlɪkeɪt/
noun|硼硅酸盐玻璃|A type of glass made with boron trioxide and silica.|Borosilicate is used in cookware due to its durability. 硼硅酸盐玻璃因其耐用性而用于炊具。|
近义词:heat-resistant glass, Pyrex (trademark)|反义词:soda-lime glass|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From *boron* + *silicate*.  *Boron* is named after *borax*, a mineral containing boron. *Silicate* comes from *silica*, ultimately from Latin *silex*, meaning "flint."
