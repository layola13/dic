disunity:/dɪsˈjuːnɪti/
n.
不团结，分裂
lack of unity or agreement
The disunity within the party cost them the election.  党内不团结让他们输掉了选举。

近义词:discord, division, disharmony, friction, strife
反义词:unity, harmony, concord, agreement, solidarity
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (not) + unity (oneness). Ultimately from Latin unus (one).
