maleficial:/ˌmælɪˈfɪʃəl/
adjective
有害的；恶毒的
Producing or intended to produce harm or evil.
Maleficial magic was used to curse the king. 恶毒的魔法被用来诅咒国王。
Maleficial effects of pollution are evident. 污染的有害影响是显而易见的。
近义词: harmful, detrimental, injurious, pernicious, malign
反义词: beneficial, benign, salutary
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *maleficus* (“evil-doing”), from *male* (“badly, ill”) + *facere* (“to do”).
