kindest:/ˈkaɪndɪst/| adj.|最善良的，最亲切的|Superlative form of "kind."  It means having or showing the greatest degree of sympathy, gentleness, and concern for others.|She gave me the kindest smile I'd ever seen. 她给了我我见过的最亲切的微笑。|nicest, friendliest, most generous, warmest, most compassionate|cruelest, meanest, unkindest, harshest, most ruthless|adjective
