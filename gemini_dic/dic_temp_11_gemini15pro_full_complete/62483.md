endospore:/ˈendəˌspɔːr/ (US) /ˈendəˌspɔː(r)/ (UK)| n.| 内生孢子 |A resistant asexual spore that develops inside some bacteria cells.  It is a dormant, tough, non-reproductive structure produced by certain bacteria from the Firmicute phylum. |Bacterial endospores allow the bacteria to survive unfavorable environmental conditions. 细菌的内生孢子使其能够在不利的环境条件下生存。| The discovery of endospores was a significant advancement in microbiology. 内生孢子的发现是微生物学上的一个重大进步。|synonym: spore, cystospore| antonym: vegetative cell| noun
