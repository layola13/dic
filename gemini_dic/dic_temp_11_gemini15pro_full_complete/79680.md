gloves: /ɡlʌvz/ | n. | 手套 | A covering for the hand worn for protection against cold or dirt and typically having separate parts for each finger and the thumb. |  I wear gloves when it's cold. 我在冷的时候戴手套。

She wore gloves to protect her hands while gardening.  她戴着手套在园艺时保护她的双手。


| 近义词：mittens, gauntlets | 反义词: bare hands | 词性: noun
