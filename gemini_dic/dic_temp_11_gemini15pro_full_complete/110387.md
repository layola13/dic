lignous:/ˈlɪɡnəs/
adjective
木质的，木本的
of or resembling wood; woody.
The lignous tissue of the tree was strong and resilient. 这棵树的木质组织坚韧而有弹性。
The ancient artifact was made of a lignous material. 这件古代文物是用一种木质材料制成的。

近义词:woody, ligneous, xyloid
反义词:herbaceous

词性:adjective
