astone:/əˈstoʊn/| adj.| astonished, surprised; stunned.| Very surprised or shocked; amazed.| Example: I was astone at her beauty. 我被她的美貌惊呆了。| Example: He was astone by the news. 他被这个消息惊呆了。| 近义词: astonished, amazed, surprised, stunned, shocked, dumbfounded, flabbergasted | 反义词: unsurprised, expected, indifferent | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Old French *estoner "to stun, daze," from Vulgar Latin *extonare, from Latin ex- "out" + tonare "to thunder."  The original sense was thus "thunderstruck."
