nontraitorously:/ˌnɒnˈtreɪtərəsli/
Adverb
非叛逆地；无叛国行为地
In a way that is not traitorous; loyally.
He served his country nontraitorously during the war.  他在战争期间忠诚地为国家服务。
Synonyms: loyally, faithfully, patriotically, devotedly
Antonyms: traitorously, treacherously, disloyally, perfidiously
词性: 副词
