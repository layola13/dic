deject:/dɪˈdʒɛkt/
verb
使沮丧，使灰心
To make someone sad and dispirited.
The news of her friend's illness dejected her.  她朋友生病的消息让她很沮丧。
He was dejected after failing the exam. 他考试不及格后非常沮丧。
synonyms:dishearten, dispirit, depress, sadden
antonyms:encourage, hearten, cheer, uplift
词性:v.
