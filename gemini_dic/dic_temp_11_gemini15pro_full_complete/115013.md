mandazi:/mɑːnˈdɑːzi/ | n. |曼达奇，曼达兹，油炸面包圈（东非一种油炸甜面包）|A kind of fried dough bread popular in East Africa, often sweetened.  It is similar to a doughnut. |例句：I bought some mandazi from a street vendor in Mombasa. 我在蒙巴萨的一个街头小贩那里买了一些曼达奇。

例句：Mandazi are a common breakfast food in Kenya. 曼达奇是肯尼亚常见的早餐食品。|近义词：doughnut, fritter, beignet |反义词: None (no direct opposite for a food item) |词性: Noun


Etymology->

From Swahili *mandazi*, ultimately from Arabic *manda*, plural of *mandā*, meaning "circular bread".
