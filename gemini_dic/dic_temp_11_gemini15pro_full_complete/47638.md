debit:/ˈdɛbɪt/ (US) /ˈdɛbɪt/ (UK)| n. vt.|借方；借记；借项|An entry recording a sum owed, listed on the left-hand side or column of an account.|例句：The debit side of my bank statement showed a large withdrawal. 我的银行对账单的借方显示一大笔取款。|近义词：charge, expense|反义词：credit|词性：名词/动词

debit:/ˈdɛbɪt/ (US) /ˈdɛbɪt/ (UK)| vt.|借记；记入借方|To enter a debit against (an account).|例句：My account was debited for the purchase. 我的账户被借记了这笔购买款项。|近义词：charge|反义词：credit|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *debte, from Latin debitum "something owed," neuter past participle of debere "to owe," originally "to keep away from oneself," from de- "away" + habere "to have."
