crushably:/ˈkrʌʃəbli/
adjective
易碎地; 可压碎地
Easily crushed or capable of being crushed.
The cookies were crushably delicate.  这些饼干易碎且精致。
The styrofoam cup was crushably soft. 这个聚苯乙烯泡沫杯子软得一捏就碎。
近义词:fragile, brittle, breakable
反义词: sturdy, robust, unbreakable
词性:adjective


Etymology:
From "crush" (verb) + "-able" (suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being") + "-ly" (suffix forming adverbs from adjectives).  "Crush" ultimately comes from Old French "croisir," meaning "to break, crack."
