cider:/ˈsaɪdər/| n.|苹果酒，苹果汁|An alcoholic drink made from fermented apple juice.  It can also refer to unfermented apple juice in American English.|例句：I prefer cider to beer. 我更喜欢苹果酒而不是啤酒。

例句：She brought a jug of cider to the picnic. 她带了一壶苹果酒去野餐。|近义词：apple juice (especially in American English), perry|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
