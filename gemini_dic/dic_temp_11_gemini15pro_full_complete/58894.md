duressor:/dʊˈrɛsər/ /djʊˈrɛsər/
noun
施压者，胁迫者
Someone who forces another person to do something against their will.
He claimed he signed the confession under duress from the duressor. 他声称是在施压者的胁迫下签署了供词。
Synonyms: oppressor, coercer, intimidator, bully
Antonyms: protector, defender, supporter
词性: 名词
