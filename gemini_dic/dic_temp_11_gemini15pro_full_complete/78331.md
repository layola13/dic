gersner:/ˈɡɜːsnər/ | n. | 盖斯纳波 | A type of trochoidal wave, often used in computer graphics to simulate realistic ocean waves.  It is named after Gerstner, who first described them mathematically. | Example: The game uses Gerstner waves to create a dynamic and visually appealing ocean surface. 游戏使用盖斯纳波来创建动态且视觉上吸引人的海洋表面。 | Example:  The Gerstner wave implementation allows for adjustable wave height and frequency. 盖斯纳波的实现允许调整波浪高度和频率。| 近义词: trochoidal wave, ocean wave,  | 反义词: N/A | noun
