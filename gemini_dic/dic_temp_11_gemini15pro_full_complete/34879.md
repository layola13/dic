clip-clop:/klɪpˈklɑːp/ | n. & v. |（马蹄）得得声，发出得得声|The sound of a horse's hooves striking a hard surface. |The horse clip-clopped down the cobbled street. 马沿着鹅卵石街道发出得得的响声。 |synonyms: clatter, clomp | antonyms: (For the sound itself, there isn't a direct antonym.  One might consider silence or a muffled sound.) | noun & verb

clip-clop:/klɪpˈklɑːp/ | v. |发出得得声|To make the sound of a horse's hooves striking a hard surface. |The horse clip-clopped across the bridge. 马儿发出得得的响声穿过桥。 |synonyms: clatter, clomp | antonyms: (For the sound itself, there isn't a direct antonym. ETYMOLOGY->
Onomatopoeic, imitating the sound of hooves.
