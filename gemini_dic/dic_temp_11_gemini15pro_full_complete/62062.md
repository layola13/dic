encashed:/ɪnˈkæʃt/
verb
兑现，变现
To convert (something, such as a check, bond, or policy) into cash.
He encashed his savings bonds to pay for the trip. 他兑现了他的储蓄债券来支付旅行费用。
synonyms: cash, redeem, liquidate
antonyms: invest, save, deposit
