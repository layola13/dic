microfab:/ˈmaɪkroʊfæb/| n.|微加工，微制造|A facility or process used for microfabrication, the fabrication of miniature structures with dimensions on the micrometer scale.|The new microfab will allow us to produce smaller and more efficient chips. 新的微加工厂将使我们能够生产更小、更高效的芯片。|近义词：micromanufacturing, micromachining|反义词:|noun
