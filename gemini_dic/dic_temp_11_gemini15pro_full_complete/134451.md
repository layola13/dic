nonitemized:/nɒnˈaɪtəmaɪzd/ | adj. | 未逐项列出的，总括的 | Not listed item by item; given as a total. | The bill was nonitemized, showing only the total amount due.  账单未逐项列出，只显示了总金额。| The invoice provided a nonitemized sum. 发票提供了一个总额。|  detailed, itemized | non-detailed, summarized | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + itemized (listed by item).  "Itemize" comes from the Latin "item," meaning "likewise, also."
