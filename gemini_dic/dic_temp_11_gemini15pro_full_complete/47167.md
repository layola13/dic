dawn on:/dɔːn ɒn/ | phrasal verb | 渐渐明白；开始理解 |  To become gradually apparent to someone; to begin to be understood by someone. | It finally dawned on me that I had left my keys at home. 我终于意识到我把钥匙落在家里了。

It dawned on him that he had been tricked. 他渐渐明白自己被骗了。

近义词: realize, understand, comprehend, grasp, become aware, sink in, click
反义词:  ignore, overlook, miss, misunderstand
词性: 动词短语
