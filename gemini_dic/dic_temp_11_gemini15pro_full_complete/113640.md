machine:/məˈʃiːn/ (US) /məˈʃiːn/ (UK)| n.|机器，机械|A device that performs a particular task, having a system of separate parts.|例句：The sewing machine is broken. 缝纫机坏了。|近义词：apparatus, device, mechanism, appliance|反义词: - |noun

machine:/məˈʃiːn/ (US) /məˈʃiːn/ (UK)| v.|用机器加工|To make or operate on something using a machine.|例句：The parts were machined to precise tolerances. 这些零件是用机器加工到精确的公差的。|近义词：manufacture, process, fabricate|反义词: - |verb
