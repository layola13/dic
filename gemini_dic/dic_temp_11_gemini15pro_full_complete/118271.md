merchantly:/ˈmɜːrtʃəntlɪ/
adjective
商人般地，像商人的
In a manner characteristic of a merchant; commercially.
He dealt with the problem merchantly, focusing on profit and loss.  他以商人般的方式处理这个问题，专注于盈利和亏损。

反义词: charitably, altruistically
词性: adjective
