heptyl:/ˈhɛptɪl/ | adj. n.| 庚基；七烷基 |Relating to or containing seven carbon atoms.  It is used in organic chemistry to describe a univalent alkyl radical derived from heptane. It can also be a noun referring to this radical. | Heptyl alcohol is a colorless liquid. 庚醇是一种无色液体。| The heptyl chain in this molecule contributes to its hydrophobic properties.  该分子中的庚基链有助于其疏水性。 |近义词: C7H15 (chemical formula) |反义词: N/A |词性: adjective, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *heptá* (seven) + *-yl* (suffix denoting a univalent radical).
