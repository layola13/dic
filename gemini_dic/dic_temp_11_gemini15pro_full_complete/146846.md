overindexings:/ˌoʊvərˈɪndɛksɪŋz/ | n. | 过度索引；过度加权 | The act of overindexing, which means giving too much weight or emphasis to something.  This often happens in investment portfolios, marketing strategies, or data analysis, where certain factors are disproportionately represented or prioritized. |  Their marketing strategy suffered from overindexings on social media influencers and neglected traditional advertising. 他们的营销策略过度依赖社交媒体影响者，而忽略了传统广告。

The fund's overindexings in tech stocks led to significant losses during the market correction.  该基金对科技股的过度加权导致了市场调整期间的重大损失。 | 近义词: overemphasis, overweighting | 反义词: underindexing, underweighting | 词性: 名词 (plural)
