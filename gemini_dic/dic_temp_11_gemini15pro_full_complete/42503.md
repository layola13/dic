counterinfluencing:/ˌkaʊntərˈɪnfluənsɪŋ/
| n. |反影响，反作用|The act of influencing someone in the opposite direction of another influence.|The government launched a counterinfluencing campaign to combat misinformation spread by foreign actors. 政府发起了一场反影响运动，以打击外国势力散布的虚假信息。|近义词：counteracting, opposing, resisting|反义词：influencing, promoting, supporting|词性:名词 (derived from verb)

counterinfluence:/ˌkaʊntərˈɪnfluəns/
| v. |反影响，反作用|To exert influence against an opposing influence.|They tried to counterinfluence the negative publicity with a positive PR campaign. 他们试图通过积极的公关活动来抵消负面宣传的影响。|近义词：counteract, oppose, resist|反义词：influence, promote, support|词性:动词
