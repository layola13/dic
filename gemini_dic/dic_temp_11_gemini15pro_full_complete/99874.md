intermuseum:/ˌɪntərˈmjuːziəm/ | adj. | 博物馆间的，跨博物馆的 | Relating to or involving cooperation between museums. | The intermuseum loan program allows visitors to see artifacts from different collections.  博物馆间的借贷项目允许参观者看到来自不同收藏的文物。|  interinstitutional, collaborative |  independent, separate | adjective

intermuseum:/ˌɪntərˈmjuːziəm/ | n. | 博物馆间的合作 | Cooperation between museums. |  The intermuseum project resulted in a joint exhibition showcasing the history of aviation. 这个博物馆间的项目促成了一个展示航空历史的联合展览。 | collaboration, partnership |  isolation, division | noun
