maniac:/ˈmeɪniæk/ (US), /ˈmeɪniæk/ (UK)| adj.|狂热的，着迷的| Exhibiting excessive enthusiasm or obsession.|He's a maniac about cleanliness. 他有洁癖。|近义词：obsessive, fanatical, zealous|反义词:moderate, calm, indifferent|词性:形容词
