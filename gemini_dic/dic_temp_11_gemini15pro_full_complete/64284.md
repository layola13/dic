equitancy:/ˈɛkwɪtənsi/
n.
共同继承权; 平等继承权;  共同使用权
The state or right of possessing something equally with another; joint ownership or occupancy.
She held the property in equitancy with her sister. 她和她妹妹共同拥有这处房产。
Synonyms: co-ownership, joint tenancy, joint possession
Antonyms: sole ownership, individual ownership
noun


equitable:/ˈɛkwɪtəbl/
adj.
公平的; 公正的;  合理的
Fair and impartial.
An equitable distribution of resources. 资源的公平分配。
Synonyms: fair, just, impartial, even
Antonyms: unfair, unjust, biased, prejudiced
adjective


equitably:/ˈɛkwɪtəbli/
adv.
公平地; 公正地;  合理地
In a fair and impartial manner.
The resources were distributed equitably among the community members. 资源在社区成员之间公平分配。
Synonyms: fairly, justly, impartially
Antonyms: unfairly, unjustly, biasedly
adverb
