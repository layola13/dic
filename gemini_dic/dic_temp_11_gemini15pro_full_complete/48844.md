deforciation:/ˌdiːfɔːrˈseɪʃən/ | n. | 削价；货币贬值；森林滥伐|  The act of reducing the value of something, particularly currency or natural resources like forests. This word is not commonly used and may be considered archaic or non-standard.  A more common term for currency devaluation would be "devaluation," and for forest depletion, "deforestation." |  The deforciation of the currency led to economic instability. 货币贬值导致了经济不稳定。 | Near synonyms: devaluation (for currency), deforestation (for forests), depletion, degradation | Antonyms: appreciation, enhancement,  reforestation, afforestation | noun
