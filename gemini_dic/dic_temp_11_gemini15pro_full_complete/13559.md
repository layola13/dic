attend-on:/əˈtɛnd ɒn/ | v. |侍候，照顾 |To wait upon or serve (someone), especially in a medical or domestic capacity. |例句：Nurses attend on patients in the hospital. 护士在医院照顾病人。

例句：Several servants attend on the royal family.  几位仆人侍候皇室家族。


近义词：wait on, serve, minister to |反义词: neglect |词性:动词

attend-on:/əˈtɛnd ɒn/ | n. |侍从，随从 |A person who attends on someone, such as a servant or an aide. (This usage is less common than the verb form.) |例句：The king's attend-ons were always ready to fulfill his requests. 国王的侍从总是随时准备满足他的要求。

例句：She had several attend-ons who helped her with her daily tasks. 她有几个侍从帮助她处理日常事务。


近义词：attendant, servant, aide |反义词: master |词性:名词
