clarichord:/ˈklærɪkɔːrd/ | n. | 古钢琴，翼琴 |A rectangular harpsichord with a single keyboard and strings running parallel to the keyboard. It produces a delicate, ethereal sound, quieter than a harpsichord.|  The musician played a mournful melody on the clarichord.  音乐家在古钢琴上演奏了一首悲伤的旋律。| synonym: clavichord, virginal | antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps modern piano or grand piano due to contrasting sound and mechanics) | noun
