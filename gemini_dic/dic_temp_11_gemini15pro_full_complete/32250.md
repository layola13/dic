chickpea:/ˈtʃɪkˌpiː/ (US) /ˈtʃɪkˌpiː/ (UK)|n.|鹰嘴豆；埃及豆|A legume with small, round, pale-brown seeds, used widely in South Asian and Middle Eastern cuisine.|例句：I love adding chickpeas to my salads. 我喜欢在沙拉里加鹰嘴豆。

例句：Hummus is made from chickpeas.  鹰嘴豆泥是用鹰嘴豆做的。|近义词：garbanzo bean|反义词:|noun
