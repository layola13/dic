dambonite:/ˈdæmbəˌnaɪt/| n. |达姆波糖; 达姆酮糖|A white, crystalline, sweet, water-soluble carbohydrate, C8H14O6, found in Gabon dammar and other rubber-producing plants.|Dambonite is extracted from the sap of certain trees.  达姆波糖是从某些树的汁液中提取的。|Synonym: dimethyl ether of inositol|Antonym: N/A|noun
