entendres:/ˌɑ̃ntɑ̃ˈdreɪz/ | n. | 含沙射影；双关语；引申义 | An indirect or subtle, usually derogatory implication in an expression; double meaning. |  The comedian's act was filled with sexual entendres that went over the children's heads.  这位喜剧演员的表演充满了孩子们无法理解的性暗示。| synonyms: innuendo, double entendre, suggestion, hint | antonyms: explicitness, directness | noun
