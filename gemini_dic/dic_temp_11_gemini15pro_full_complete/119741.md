midwestern:/ˌmɪdˈwɛstərn/| adj.|中西部的，美国中西部的|Of or relating to the American Midwest.|She has a typical Midwestern accent. 她带有典型的中西部口音。|近义词：Middle West, central|反义词:eastern, western, southern|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
mid- + western.  "Mid-" denotes middle or central, and "western" refers to the western part of a particular region.  In this case, it refers to the central western part of the United States.
