freebooted:/ˈfriːbuːtɪd/
verb
掠夺的，以海盗方式获得的
obtained by freebooting; plundered.
The freebooted treasure was hidden on a remote island.  掠夺来的财宝藏在一个偏僻的岛屿上。
The company freebooted the design from a smaller competitor. 这家公司从一家较小的竞争对手那里盗用了设计。
近义词:pirated, plundered, looted
反义词:earned, legitimate, rightfully obtained
词性:v.

freebooting:/ˈfriːbuːtɪŋ/
noun
海盗行为，掠夺
the act of freebooting; piracy.
Freebooting was rampant in the Caribbean during the 17th century. 17世纪，加勒比海地区海盗猖獗。
The authorities are trying to crack down on online freebooting.  当局正试图打击网络盗版。
近义词:piracy, plundering, looting
反义词:lawful trade, legitimate business
词性:n.
