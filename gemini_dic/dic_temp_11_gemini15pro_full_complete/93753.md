immeability:/ɪˌmiːəˈbɪləti/
n.
不能渗透；不透性
The impermeability of the membrane prevents water from passing through.
这层膜的不透水性阻止了水的通过。
The rock's impermeability makes it ideal for building dams.
这种岩石的不透水性使其成为建造水坝的理想材料。
impenetrableness, imperviousness
permeability, penetrability
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *immediabilitas, from Latin immediabilis (unable to be traversed), from in- (not) + mediabilis (passable, able to be traversed), from medius (middle) + -abilis (able, capable of).
