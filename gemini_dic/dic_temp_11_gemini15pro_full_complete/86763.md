helplessness:/ˈhɛlplɪsnəs/
| n. |无助，无力，无能为力|The state of being helpless; inability to help oneself or others.|  She felt a deep helplessness as she watched the fire consume her home.  看着大火吞噬她的家，她感到深深的无助。|近义词：powerlessness, inability, incapacity, vulnerability|反义词: capability, competence, power, strength|词性:名词


词源:  From helpless + -ness.  Helpless itself comes from help + -less. Ultimately from Old English helpan "to help."
