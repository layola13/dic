jingoish:/ˈdʒɪŋɡoʊɪʃ/
adjective
侵略好战的；沙文主义的；极端爱国的
Characterized by extreme patriotism, especially in the form of aggressive or warlike foreign policy.
Jingoistic politicians are eager for war.  好战的政客渴望战争。
The crowd became increasingly jingoistic, chanting nationalist slogans.  人群越来越沙文主义，高呼民族主义口号。
近义词:chauvinistic, nationalistic, patriotic, bellicose, hawkish
反义词:pacifist, dovish, internationalist
词性:adjective
词源:From "Jingo," a figure in British music halls who sang patriotic songs during the Russo-Turkish War (1877-78), expressing a desire for Britain to intervene on the Turkish side.
