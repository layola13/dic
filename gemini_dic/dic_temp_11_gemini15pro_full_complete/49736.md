demogorgon:/ˌdeməˈɡɔːrɡən/ | n. |  恶魔，妖怪 | A powerful, terrifying demon or infernal deity.  Originally a mysterious underworld deity in Greek mythology, later popularized as a monstrous antagonist in the Dungeons & Dragons game and the Netflix series "Stranger Things". |  The demogorgon emerged from the Upside Down to terrorize the town.  恶魔从颠倒世界出现，恐吓着小镇。 | Synonyms: monster, fiend, devil, behemoth | Antonyms: angel, deity, god | Noun
