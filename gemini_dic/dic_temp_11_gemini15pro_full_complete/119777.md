/maɪt/ | modal verb | 可能；也许；可以 | Used to express possibility, permission, or a polite request. |  Might I borrow your pen? 我可以借你的笔吗？ | 近义词: may, could, perhaps | 反义词: must, will | 词性: modal verb

注意事项:  "Might" doesn't have a distinct past participle form; "might have" is used for perfect tenses.  It's more common in British English than American English, where "may" is often used instead.
