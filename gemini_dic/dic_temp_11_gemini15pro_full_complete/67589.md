extraterritorial:/ˌekstrətɛrɪˈtɔːriəl/ | adj. | 治外法权的；域外的 |  Existing or occurring beyond the limits of a particular jurisdiction or territory. | The embassy has extraterritorial status. 大使馆享有治外法权。|  The company's extraterritorial operations are subject to local laws. 该公司的域外业务受当地法律约束。|  近义词:  exterritorial, transnational | 反义词: domestic, territorial | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From extra- (meaning "outside" or "beyond") + territorial (relating to territory).  The word "territorial" ultimately derives from the Latin word "terra" meaning "earth" or "land."
