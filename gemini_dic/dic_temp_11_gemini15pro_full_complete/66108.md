excelled:/ɪkˈsɛld/| v.| 擅长；胜过；突出 |She excelled in mathematics. 她擅长数学。|近义词：surpassed, outperformed, triumphed, shone, exceeded|反义词:failed, underperformed|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *excellen*, from Latin excellere (“to surpass, excel, be eminent”), present active infinitive of excello, from ex (“out of, from”) + cello (“to rise, tower”).
