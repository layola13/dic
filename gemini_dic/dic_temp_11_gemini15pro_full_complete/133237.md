nonexculpations: /ˌnɒnɛksˌkʌlˈpeɪʃənz/
n.
中文翻译：无罪辩护的缺乏；无法开脱；不能免责
英文解析：The absence of exculpations; the state of not being exculpated or cleared of blame.  Plural form of nonexculpation.
例句：The nonexculpations in the report pointed towards the CEO's guilt. 报告中缺乏免责声明指向了CEO的罪行。

例句：His repeated nonexculpations made him appear even more suspicious. 他反复无法为自己开脱，让他看起来更加可疑。

近义词：incriminations, accusations, condemnations
反义词：exculpations, exonerations, acquittals
词性：名词


词源：
Prefix "non-" (not) + "exculpation" (from Latin "exculpare," meaning "to clear from blame")
