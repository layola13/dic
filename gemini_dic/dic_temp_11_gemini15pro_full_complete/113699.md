macrame:/ˈmækrəmeɪ/ (US), /ˈmækrəmiː/ (UK)| n.|花边，绳结工艺；用绳结做成的饰品|The art of knotting cord or string in patterns to make decorative articles.  A piece of work made in this way.|She made a beautiful macrame wall hanging. 她制作了一个漂亮的绳结壁挂。|Synonyms: knotting, lacework|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "plain weave" in the context of textiles)|noun
