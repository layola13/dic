ascribe: /əˈskraɪb/ | v. | 归因于，归咎于 | To attribute something to a cause, source, or origin.  To regard a quality as belonging to. | He ascribed his success to hard work and dedication. 他将他的成功归因于努力工作和奉献精神。 The painting was ascribed to Rembrandt. 这幅画被认为是伦勃朗的作品。 | synonyms: attribute, impute, credit, assign | antonyms: deny, refute, disavow | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ascribere, from Latin ad- + scribere "to write".  Originally meant "to add in writing," hence "to attribute in writing."
