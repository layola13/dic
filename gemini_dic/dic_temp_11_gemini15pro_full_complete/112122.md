longueurs:/lɔ̃ˈɡœʀ/ (French, often pluralized in English usage)| n. |冗长乏味；沉闷|A period of boredom or inactivity.  It often refers to a tedious or monotonous stretch of time or an excessively lengthy passage in a book, film, or speech.  Derived from the French word "longueur" meaning "length."|

例句：
The play suffered from several longueurs in the second act.
第二幕的戏有几处冗长乏味的地方。

例句：
She found the book full of longueurs and struggled to finish it.
她觉得这本书很冗长，很难读完。

近义词：tedium, monotony, dullness, boredom
反义词：excitement, interest, liveliness
词性: noun
