anti-radar:/ˌæntiˈreɪdɑːr/ | adj. | 反雷达的，抗雷达的 | Designed to avoid or reduce detection by radar.  |  The plane was coated with anti-radar paint. 这架飞机涂上了反雷达涂料。 | Synonyms: radar-absorbing, radar-resistant, stealth | Antonyms: radar-detectable | adjective

anti-radar:/ˌæntiˈreɪdɑːr/ | n. | 反雷达技术或装置 | Technology or equipment designed to counteract radar detection. | The new anti-radar system is highly effective. 新的反雷达系统非常有效。 | Synonyms: radar countermeasures, electronic countermeasures (ECM) | Antonyms: radar detection | noun
