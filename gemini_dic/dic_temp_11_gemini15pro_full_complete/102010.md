isodrome:/ˈaɪsəˌdroʊm/| n. |等时曲线，等时线|A line on a map connecting points having the same average travel time to a given center.|例句：Isodromes are useful in urban planning to determine areas accessible within a certain timeframe. 等时线在城市规划中很有用，可以确定在特定时间范围内可到达的区域。

例句：The isodromes around a major city show how commuting times increase with distance.  围绕一个主要城市的等时线显示了通勤时间是如何随着距离的增加而增加的。|近义词：contour line, isoline|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *isos* (equal) + *dromos* (running, course)
