amoebic:/əˈmiːbɪk/ | adj. | 变形虫状的；阿米巴性的；无固定形状的 | Relating to or resembling an amoeba; shapeless and indefinite |  The growth had an amoebic shape, spreading irregularly.  这种增生物呈变形虫状，形状不规则地扩散。|  amorphous, protean, undefined | fixed, definite | adjective


amoeboid:/əˈmiːbɔɪd/ | adj. | 变形虫状的；阿米巴样的 | Resembling an amoeba, especially in having pseudopodia and being able to change shape. | The amoeboid robot could squeeze through tight spaces.  这个变形虫状的机器人可以挤过狭窄的空间。 |  amoebic, amorphous, formless | fixed, rigid | adjective
