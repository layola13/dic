nonincarnate:/ˌnɒnɪnˈkɑːneɪt/
adjective
非肉身的，无实体的
Not having or taking on physical form; not embodied in flesh.
The novel explores the concept of nonincarnate beings existing in a different dimension. 这部小说探讨了存在于不同维度的非肉身存在的概念。
Near synonyms: disembodied, incorporeal, spiritual, ethereal
Antonyms: incarnate, embodied, physical, corporeal
词性: adjective
