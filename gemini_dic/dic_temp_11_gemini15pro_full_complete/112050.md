longbowman:/ˈlɒŋˌbəʊmən/ (UK) /ˈlɔːŋˌboʊmən/ (US)| n. |长弓手，弓箭手|A person skilled in using a longbow, a type of powerful medieval bow.|例句：The longbowman took aim and released the arrow. 长弓手瞄准并射出了箭。

例句：Longbowmen were crucial in English armies during the Middle Ages.  在中世纪，长弓手在英国军队中至关重要。|近义词：archer, bowman|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From *longbow* + *-man*.  *Longbow* refers to a particular type of bow known for its length and power.  *-man* is a suffix denoting a person who performs a certain action or has a certain skill or occupation.
