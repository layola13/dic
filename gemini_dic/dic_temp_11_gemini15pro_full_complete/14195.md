autobiographical:/ˌɔːtəˌbaɪəˈɡræfɪk(ə)l/
adjective
自传的；自传体的
Relating to a story of a person's life written by that person.
She wrote an autobiographical novel loosely based on her childhood experiences. 她写了一部自传体小说，大致基于她童年的经历。
This is his most autobiographical work to date. 这是他迄今为止最具自传性质的作品。

Synonym:  personal, memoiristic, self-written
Antonym:  fictional, biographical
