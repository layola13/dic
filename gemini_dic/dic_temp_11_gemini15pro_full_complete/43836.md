crepane:/krɪˈpeɪn/ | adj. |破旧的，摇摇欲坠的；（织物）薄而透明的 | (of fabric) thin and transparent; flimsy.  Also used figuratively to describe something weak or insubstantial.  Derives from Italian "crepare" (to crack, burst, die). |  The crepane silk scarf felt delicate against her skin. 这条薄绸围巾贴在她皮肤上感觉很精致。 | Synonyms: flimsy, delicate, sheer, diaphanous, gossamer, fragile | Antonyms: thick, sturdy, robust, durable | Adjective
