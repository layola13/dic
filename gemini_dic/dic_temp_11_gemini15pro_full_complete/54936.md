dispossess:/ˌdɪspəˈzɛs/ | vt. |剥夺，使丧失, 驱逐|To deprive someone of land, property, or other possessions. |The landlord dispossessed the tenant for not paying rent. 房东因租户未付房租而将其驱逐。| The government dispossessed the farmers of their land. 政府剥夺了农民的土地。|Synonyms: oust, evict, deprive, strip, divest |Antonyms: provide, give, endow, grant |verb
