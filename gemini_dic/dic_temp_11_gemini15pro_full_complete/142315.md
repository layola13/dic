正统的，拘泥于正统的

例句：His orthodoxastical views on marriage caused conflict with his more liberal children. 他在婚姻问题上正统的观点与他思想更开放的子女产生了冲突。

例句：The orthodoxastical sect refused to accept any changes to their ancient rituals.  这个正统的教派拒绝接受对其古老仪式的任何改变。

近义词：dogmatic, doctrinaire, rigid, conventional, traditional
反义词：unorthodox, heterodox, liberal, progressive, radical
词性：adjective
