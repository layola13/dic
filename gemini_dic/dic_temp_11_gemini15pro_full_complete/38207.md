compunction:/kəmˈpʌŋkʃən/| n.|内疚，悔恨，良心不安|A feeling of guilt or moral scruple that follows the doing of something bad.|He felt no compunction about his crimes. 他对自己的罪行毫无悔意。|近义词：remorse, guilt, contrition, penitence|反义词： impenitence, remorselessness|noun
