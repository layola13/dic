moreland:/ˈmɔːrlənd/ | n. | 荒野；未开垦地；边陲地带 | A tract of open or uncultivated upland; a moor. |  He roamed the morelands, breathing in the fresh air. 他在荒野上漫步，呼吸着新鲜空气。 |  The sheep grazed peacefully on the moreland. 羊群在荒野上平静地吃草。 | synonyms: moor, heath, wasteland, wilderness | antonyms: cultivated land, farmland | noun
