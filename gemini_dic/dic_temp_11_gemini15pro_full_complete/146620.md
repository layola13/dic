overhandicapped:/ˌoʊvərˈhændɪkæpt/ | adj. | 过度照顾的，过分补偿残疾的 |  Given excessive advantages or assistance intended for people with disabilities, often to the point of hindering their development or independence.  | Example: The overhandicapped child was never allowed to try things for himself, which stunted his growth.  这个过度照顾的的孩子从来不被允许自己尝试事情，这阻碍了他的成长。

Example:  Some argue that overhandicapping athletes with minor disabilities creates an unfair advantage in competitions. 有人认为，对轻度残疾的运动员过度照顾会在比赛中造成不公平的优势。 | 近义词: overprotected, coddled | 反义词: neglected, underserved | 词性: adjective
