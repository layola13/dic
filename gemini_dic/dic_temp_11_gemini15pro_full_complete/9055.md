antimephitic:/ˌænˌtɪmɛˈfɪtɪk/| adj.|抗瘴气的；预防瘴气的|Preventing or counteracting malaria or other swamp fevers.|Quinine is an antimephitic drug. 奎宁是一种抗疟疾的药物。|Synonyms: antimalarial, antipyretic|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, could consider words like "malaria-causing" or "fever-inducing")|adjective

antimephitic:/ˌænˌtɪmɛˈfɪtɪk/| n.|抗瘴气的药物|A medicine that prevents or counteracts malaria or other swamp fevers.|The doctor prescribed an antimephitic. 医生开了一种抗瘴气的药。|Synonyms: antimalarial, antipyretic|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms)|noun
