antheral:/ˈænθərəl/
adjective
花的；雄蕊的；像花药的
Relating to anthers or pollen; flowery.
The antheral fragrance filled the air.  花的香味弥漫在空气中。
The botanist studied the antheral structures of the flower.  植物学家研究了这朵花的雄蕊结构。
synonyms: floral, flowery, staminate
antonyms: pistillate
Etymology: From Late Latin *antheralis, from Latin anthera "anther," from Greek anthera "flower, bloom," literally "that which blossoms," from anthos "flower, blossom."
word type: adjective
