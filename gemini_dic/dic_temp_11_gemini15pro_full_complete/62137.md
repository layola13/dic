encircling: /ɪnˈsɜːrkəlɪŋ/ /ɛnˈsɝːkəlɪŋ/
verb
包围，环绕
forming a circle around; surrounding.
The troops were encircling the city. 部队正包围着这座城市。

The vines were encircling the old oak tree. 藤蔓缠绕着古老的橡树。

surrounding, enclosing, encompassing
releasing, freeing, liberating
v.
