metagnathous:/ˌmɛtəɡˈneɪθəs/
adjective
后颚的，下颚突出的
Having the lower jaw projecting beyond the upper jaw.
The metagnathous skull structure is a characteristic feature of certain primate species.  后颚的颅骨结构是某些灵长类物种的特征。
The anthropologist identified the skull as belonging to a metagnathous hominid.  这位人类学家确定这个头骨属于一个下颚突出的原始人类。
近义词: prognathous
反义词: orthognathous
词性: adjective

词源:
From meta- + Greek gnathos “jaw”
