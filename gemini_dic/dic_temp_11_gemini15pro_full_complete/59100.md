dying-away:/ˈdaɪɪŋ əˈweɪ/
adjective
逐渐消失的; 渐弱的
gradually fading or diminishing in intensity, especially of a sound
The dying-away strains of the music filled the air.  逐渐消失的音乐声弥漫在空气中。
The dying-away echo of the shout could still be heard.  喊叫声逐渐消失的回声仍然可以听到。
近义词: fading, diminishing, weakening, subsiding
反义词: increasing, strengthening, intensifying
词性: adjective

dying-away:/ˈdaɪɪŋ əˈweɪ/
adverb
逐渐减弱地
in a way that gradually fades or diminishes in intensity
The sound died away slowly. 声音慢慢消失了。
The light died away as the sun set.  随着太阳落山，光线逐渐减弱。
近义词: gradually, slowly, softly
反义词: suddenly, abruptly, sharply
词性: adverb


Etymology:
"dying" comes from Old English "diegan" (to die).
"away" comes from Old English "aweg" (away).
