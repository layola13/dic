friendly:/ˈfrendli/ | adj. | 友好的；親切的；溫和的；容易的；互相支持的 | Showing kindness and goodwill; easy to understand or use. |  She gave me a friendly smile. 她给了我一个友好的微笑。|  The instructions were user-friendly and easy to follow.  说明书易于理解，便于遵循。 | 近义词: amiable, amicable, cordial, kind, pleasant | 反义词: unfriendly, hostile, unkind | 词性:形容词
词源: Old English *frēondlic (unattested), from frēond 'friend' + -lic '-ly'.
