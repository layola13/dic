overinsistences:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈsɪstənsɪz/
|n.|过度坚持；过分强调|The speaker's overinsistences on minor details made the presentation tedious. 演讲者对细枝末节的过分强调使得报告冗长乏味。|Her overinsistences on her innocence began to sound suspicious. 她过分强调自己的清白反而开始让人怀疑。|近义词：overemphasis, insistence, persistence|反义词：understatement, concession|词性:名词

Etymology:
"over-" (prefix): from Old English "ofer," meaning "above, beyond, excessively"
"insist": from Latin "insistere," meaning "to stand upon, persist, dwell upon"
"-ence" (suffix): forming nouns expressing action, state, or quality
