lawyerly:/ˈlɔɪərli/| adj.|律师般的；精明而谨慎的；像律师的|Characteristic of a lawyer; especially, clever and careful, like a lawyer.| He gave a lawyerly response, carefully avoiding any direct admission of guilt. 他给出了一个律师式的回答，小心翼翼地避免直接承认任何罪行。|Synonyms: legalistic, attorney-like, professional, meticulous, shrewd, cautious|Antonyms: careless, reckless, unprofessional, artless|adjective
