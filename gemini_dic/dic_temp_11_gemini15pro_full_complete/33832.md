circumlocution:/ˌsɜːrkəmloʊˈkjuːʃən/ | /ˌsɜːkəmləˈkjuːʃən/ | n. | 迂回说法，冗长表达 | An indirect way of expressing something, especially something unpleasant or embarrassing.  The act of talking around a subject instead of addressing it directly. | He used a lot of circumlocution to avoid answering the question directly. 他用了很多迂回的说法来避免直接回答问题。| synonyms: periphrasis, roundabout expression, indirectness, verbosity, wordiness | antonyms: directness, conciseness, brevity, succinctness | noun
