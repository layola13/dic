impartation:/ˌɪmpɑːrˈteɪʃn/ | n. |传授，赋予，给予|The impartation of knowledge is the purpose of education. 教育的目的是传授知识。|例句：The impartation of his wisdom to his followers was invaluable.  他将自己的智慧传授给追随者，这是无价的。

例句：The ceremony involved the impartation of a special blessing.  这个仪式包括赋予一种特殊的祝福。|近义词：communication, transmission, bestowal, conveyance|反义词：withholding, concealment|词性: 名词

impart:/ɪmˈpɑːrt/ | vt. |传授，赋予，告知|He imparted his knowledge to his students. 他把他的知识传授给学生们。|例句：She imparted the secret to her best friend. 她把秘密告诉了她最好的朋友。

例句：The spice imparts a rich flavor to the dish.  这种香料赋予了这道菜浓郁的风味。|近义词：communicate, convey, transmit, disclose, reveal|反义词：conceal, withhold|词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *impartacioun, from Late Latin impartātiō (“communication”), from Latin impartō (“I share, communicate”), from in- (“into”) + pars (“part, share”).
