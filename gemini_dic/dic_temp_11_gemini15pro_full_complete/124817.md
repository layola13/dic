mucin: /ˈmjuːsɪn/| n. |黏蛋白；粘素|A glycoprotein constituent of mucus, secreted by mucous membranes. |The goblet cells secrete mucin which forms a protective layer on the epithelial surface. 杯状细胞分泌黏蛋白，在表皮表面形成保护层。|Synonyms: glycoprotein, mucinous substance|Antonyms: None|Noun

mucin: /ˈmjuːsɪn/| n. |黏蛋白|A slimy substance, typically secreted by mucous membranes. |Mucin helps to lubricate and protect the lining of the digestive system. 黏蛋白有助于润滑和保护消化系统的内壁。|Synonyms: mucus, slime|Antonyms: None|Noun
