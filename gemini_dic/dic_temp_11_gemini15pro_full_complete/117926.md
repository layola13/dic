meltwater:/ˈmeltwɔːtər/ (US) /ˈmeltwɔːtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|融水|Water derived from the melting of snow or ice.|The glacier calved, creating a surge of meltwater into the fjord. 冰川崩解，大量的融水涌入峡湾。|近义词：thaw water, glacial runoff|反义词:ice, snow|noun
