draining:/ˈdreɪnɪŋ/ | adj. v. | 排水的，使人筋疲力尽的 | causing the gradual loss of something; exhausting | The draining work left him feeling weak.  这项耗费精力的工作让他感到虚弱。| Synonyms: exhausting, tiring, depleting, sapping | Antonyms: energizing, invigorating, revitalizing | adjective
draining:/ˈdreɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 排水，流干 | to cause liquid to flow away from something; to become empty of liquid; to exhaust | The farmer drained the field. 农民把田里的水排干了。| Synonyms: emptying, depleting, voiding | Antonyms: filling, flooding | verb

Etymology: From Old English *drēahnian, from Proto-Germanic *draugijaną, from *dreugą ("dry"). Related to dry.
