ambulances:/ˈæmbjələnsɪz/
n.
救护车
A vehicle equipped to transport injured or sick people, especially in emergencies.
Several ambulances arrived at the scene of the accident. 几辆救护车到达了事故现场。

The ambulance rushed the patient to the hospital. 救护车把病人迅速送往医院。

近义词:emergency vehicle, medic unit, paramedic unit
反义词:N/A
词性:Noun
