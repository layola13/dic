airheaded:/ˈerˌhedɪd/| adj.|轻浮的，愚蠢的，傻乎乎的|Lacking seriousness or good judgment; silly or frivolous.  Often implies being scatterbrained or ditzy.|She made an airheaded comment about the importance of the meeting, completely missing the seriousness of the situation. 她对会议的重要性发表了轻率的评论，完全没有意识到情况的严重性。|Synonyms: flighty, dizzy, scatterbrained, foolish, silly|Antonyms: serious, sensible, intelligent, wise|adjective
