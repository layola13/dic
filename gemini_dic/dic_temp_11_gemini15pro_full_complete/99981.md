interpale:/ˌɪntərˈpeɪl/ | adj. | 浅色的，淡色的 | Situated or occurring between pales or pale-colored areas.  Often used in heraldry to describe a charge placed between pales. | The interpale area of the shield was a vibrant blue. 盾牌浅色区域之间是鲜艳的蓝色。 | The butterfly's wings had interpale stripes of yellow and white. 蝴蝶的翅膀上有浅黄色和白色的条纹。 | 近义词: light, pale, pastel | 反义词: dark, deep, vibrant | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From "inter-" (between) + "pale" (a broad vertical stripe in heraldry, often light in color).
