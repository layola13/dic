mauveine:/moʊˈviːn/ /mɔːˈviːn/| n. |苯胺紫，淡紫色|A synthetic organic dye, the first commercially successful synthetic dye, producing a vibrant purple color.|The discovery of mauveine revolutionized the dye industry. 苯胺紫的发现彻底改变了染料工业。|The dress was a beautiful shade of mauveine. 这件衣服是美丽的淡紫色。|Synonyms: aniline purple, Perkin's mauve|Antonyms: N/A|noun

mauve:/moʊv/ /mɔːv/| n. adj.|淡紫色，淡紫色的|A pale purple color, named after the mauveine dye.|She wore a mauve dress to the wedding. 她穿着一件淡紫色的连衣裙去参加婚礼。|The walls were painted a delicate mauve. 墙壁被漆成了淡雅的淡紫色。|Synonyms: lavender, lilac|Antonyms: N/A|noun, adjective
