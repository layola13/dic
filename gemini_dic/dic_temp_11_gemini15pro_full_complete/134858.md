nonmomentariness:/ˌnɒnˌməʊmənˈtɛrɪnɪs/ | n. | 非瞬时性，持续性 | The nonmomentariness of the pain made it unbearable.  这种疼痛的持续性使它难以忍受。 |  The nonmomentariness of the experience allowed for deeper reflection. 这种经历的持续性允许更深入的反思。 | duration, continuity, persistence | momentariness, transience | noun

Etymology:
From non- + momentary + -ness.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," "momentary" refers to something lasting only a moment, and "-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns from adjectives.
