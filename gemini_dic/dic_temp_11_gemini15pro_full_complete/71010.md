firkin:/ˈfɜːrkɪn/| n. |小桶，弗金（容量单位）|A firkin is a small cask, typically used for liquids like beer or ale, and also a unit of volume equal to a quarter of a barrel. |例句：The pub served its best ale in firkins. 这家酒吧用小桶供应最好的麦芽酒。

例句：He ordered a firkin of ale for the party. 他为聚会订购了一小桶麦芽酒。|近义词：cask, keg, barrel|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
