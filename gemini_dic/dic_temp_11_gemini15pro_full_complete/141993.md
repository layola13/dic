organisation:/ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃən/| n.|组织，机构，团体|A group of people who work together in a structured way to achieve a specific goal.|The World Health Organisation is a specialized agency of the United Nations responsible for international public health. 世界卫生组织是联合国负责国际公共卫生的专门机构。

The organisation of the event was impeccable.  这次活动的组织工作无可挑剔。

近义词:group, association, institution, body|反义词:disorder, chaos|词性:noun


Etymology: From Old French *organizacion, from Late Latin organizatio, from Latin organum ("instrument, tool") + -izatio.
