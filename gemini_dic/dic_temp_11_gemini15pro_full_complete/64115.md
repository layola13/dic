eps:/iːpiːˈɛs/ | n. | 每股收益 (Earnings Per Share) | Earnings per share is the portion of a company's profit allocated to each outstanding share of common stock. |  The company reported EPS of $2.50 for the quarter. 该公司报告本季度每股收益为 2.50 美元。| synonyms: earnings per share, profit per share | antonyms: N/A | noun
