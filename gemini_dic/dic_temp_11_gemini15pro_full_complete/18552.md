benefiters:/ˈbɛnɪfɪtərz/
noun
受益人，受惠者
Those who receive an advantage or profit from something.
The new tax law will greatly benefit small business owners, making them the primary benefiters of the legislation.  新的税法将极大地惠及小企业主，使他们成为该立法的首要受益者。

The charity event raised millions of dollars, with the benefiters being homeless families in the city.  慈善活动筹集了数百万美元，受益者是该市的无家可归家庭。

synonyms: recipients, beneficiaries, gainers, profiteers
antonyms: losers, sufferers, victims
