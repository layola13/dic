draftee:/ˌdræfˈtiː/ /ˌdrɑːfˈtiː/
noun
被征召者；应征入伍者
A person who has been conscripted into military service.
The young draftee was nervous about leaving home for basic training.  这个年轻的被征召者对离开家去接受基本训练感到紧张。
conscript, recruit
volunteer, civilian
