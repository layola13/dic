nonassertive:/ˌnɒnəˈsɜːtɪv/ | adj. | 非自信的；不果断的 | Not assertive; lacking confidence and the ability or willingness to express one's views or enforce one's rights. | He has a nonassertive personality. 他性格不自信。| Near Synonyms: timid, submissive, passive, meek, unassertive | Antonyms: assertive, confident, forceful, bold | 词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + assertive (from assert, from Latin asserere "to claim, declare").
