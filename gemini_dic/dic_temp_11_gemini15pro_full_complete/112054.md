longed-for:/ˈlɒŋd fɔːr/ (US) /lɒŋd fɔː(r)/ (UK)| adj.|渴望的，梦寐以求的|Something that is longed-for is something that someone has wanted very much for a long time.|例句：She finally achieved her longed-for ambition of becoming a doctor. 她终于实现了成为一名医生的夙愿。|近义词：desired, wished-for, coveted, yearned-for|反义词：undesired, unwanted|词性:形容词

longed-for:| | |From long (v.) + for (prep.).  The verb "long" meaning "to have a strong desire or craving" comes from Old English langian, from Proto-Germanic *langōną. The preposition "for" indicates the object of the desire.| | | |
