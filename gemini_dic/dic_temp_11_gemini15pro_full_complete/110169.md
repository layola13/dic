lienable:/ˈliːnəbl/
adjective
可留置的，可扣押的
Subject to a lien; that may be legally seized or attached.
The contractor placed a lien on the property because the owner failed to pay for the renovations, demonstrating that the property was lienable.  承包商对该房产进行了留置，因为业主未支付翻新费用，这表明该房产是可以留置的。
The bank confirmed that the equipment was lienable and could be seized if the loan wasn't repaid. 银行确认该设备可留置，如果不偿还贷款，可以扣押该设备。

近义词:attachable, securable
反义词:unattachable, unsecurable
词性:adjective

词源:
From lien + -able.  来自 lien (留置权) + -able (可...的)
