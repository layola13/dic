admissions:/ədˈmɪʃənz/
n.
录取，准许进入；入场费；承认；坦白
The process of allowing someone to enter a place or institution, especially a hospital, school, or university; the amount of money charged for entry to a place; a statement acknowledging that something is true or that you have done something wrong.
The university received thousands of admissions applications this year. 今年这所大学收到了数千份入学申请。
Synonym: entry, acceptance, admittance, confession
Antonym: rejection, denial, refusal
词性: 名词

admit:/ədˈmɪt/
v.
承认；准许…进入；可容纳
Confess to be true or to be the case; allow someone to enter a place; have room for.
He admitted his mistake. 他承认了自己的错误。
Synonym: acknowledge, confess, concede, permit
Antonym: deny, refuse, reject
词性: 动词
