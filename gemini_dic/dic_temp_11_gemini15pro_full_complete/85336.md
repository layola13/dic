harps:/hɑːrps/| n. |竖琴（harp的复数形式）|A stringed musical instrument, consisting of a resonating sound box, a curved neck, and strings of graded length that are plucked with the fingers.|The angels played their harps in heaven. 天使们在天堂弹奏着竖琴。|Near synonyms: lyres, zithers|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a musical instrument. Could consider "silence" conceptually.)|Noun

harps:/hɑːrps/| v. |反复谈论，喋喋不休（harp的第三人称单数形式）|To talk or write persistently and tediously on a particular topic.|He keeps harping on about his ex-girlfriend. 他不停地唠叨着他的前女友。|Near synonyms: dwells, perseverates, reiterates|Antonyms: ignores, drops, forgets|Verb
