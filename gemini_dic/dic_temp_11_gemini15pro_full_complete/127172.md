naturalists:/ˈnætʃrəlɪsts/ | n. |博物学家；自然主义者|A person who studies natural history, especially plants and animals. |例句：The naturalists spent months in the rainforest, cataloging new species. 博物学家们在雨林中待了几个月，记录新物种。

例句：Charles Darwin was a famous naturalist. 查尔斯·达尔文是一位著名的博物学家。|近义词：biologist, ecologist, scientist |反义词: - |词性:noun
