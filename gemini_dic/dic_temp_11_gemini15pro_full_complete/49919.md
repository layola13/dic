denationalising:/diːˌnæʃnəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使非国有化；取消国有化
The act of transferring an industry or enterprise from government ownership to private ownership.
The government is denationalising the railway system. 政府正在将铁路系统私有化。

The government is denationalising several key industries. 政府正在将几个关键产业私有化。

Synonyms: privatizing, deregulating
Antonyms: nationalizing, state-controlled
