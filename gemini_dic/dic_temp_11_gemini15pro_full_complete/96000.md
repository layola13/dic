incurred:/ɪnˈkɜːrd/ | v. |招致，引发，遭受|  To experience something unpleasant or unwelcome as a result of one's actions or behavior. |  The company incurred heavy losses due to the economic downturn.  这家公司由于经济衰退而遭受了严重的损失。 |Synonyms: suffer, experience, sustain, obtain, acquire| Antonyms: avoid, prevent, avert, escape, evade | Verb

incur:/ɪnˈkɜːr/ | v. |招致，引发，遭受| To become subject to something unpleasant or unwelcome as a result of one's actions or behavior. | He incurred the wrath of his boss by being late. 他因迟到而招致了老板的愤怒。|Synonyms:  bring upon oneself, contract, provoke, arouse| Antonyms: avoid, prevent, avert, escape, evade | Verb
