countywide:/ˈkaʊntiwaɪd/
adjective
全县的；遍及全县的
Extending throughout a county.
The sheriff's department launched a countywide search for the missing child.  警长部门在全县范围内搜寻失踪儿童。
Synonyms: county-spanning, county-covering
Antonyms: local, regional
词性:形容词
