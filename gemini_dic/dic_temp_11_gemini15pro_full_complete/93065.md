idolism: /ˈaɪdəlɪzəm/ | n. |偶像崇拜；盲目崇拜|The excessive admiration or worship of an idol.  It can refer to the religious worship of idols or, more broadly, to extreme devotion to any person, thing, or idea.|例句：The band's idolism among teenagers was overwhelming.  这个乐队在青少年中的偶像崇拜令人难以置信。

例句：His idolism of his favorite athlete bordered on obsession. 他对他最喜欢的运动员的崇拜近乎痴迷。|近义词：worship, adoration, veneration, hero-worship|反义词：disdain, contempt, iconoclasm|词性: noun

Etymology: From French *idolism*, from *idole* (“idol”) + *-isme* (“-ism”).  *Idole* ultimately derives from Greek *εἴδωλον* (eídōlon, “image, phantom, idol”).
