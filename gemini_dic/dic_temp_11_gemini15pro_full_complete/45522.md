curette: /kjʊˈrɛt/ | v. |刮除 | to scrape or clean a surface, especially a wound or body cavity, with a curette. | The dentist curetted the patient's teeth to remove plaque. 牙医刮除了病人的牙齿以去除牙菌斑。| 近义词: scrape, debride | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 动词

词源:  From French *curette*, diminutive of *cure*, from Latin *cura* ("care, attention, diligence").  The verb is a back-formation from the noun.
