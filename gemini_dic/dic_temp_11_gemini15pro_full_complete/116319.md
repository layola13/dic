masterer:/ˈmæstərər/| n.| 母带工程师，母带制作人 |A person who masters recordings, preparing them for final release.| The masterer did a great job on the album, making it sound polished and professional. 母带工程师在这张专辑上做得很好，使它听起来很优美很专业。|近义词：audio engineer, sound engineer|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

Etymology: From "master" (v.) + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Master," in the audio context, means to prepare a recording for final release, often involving adjustments to equalization, compression, and limiting.
