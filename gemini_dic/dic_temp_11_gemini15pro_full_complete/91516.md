hyoglossus:/ˌhaɪoʊˈɡlɒsəs/ | n. |舌骨舌肌|A thin, quadrilateral muscle extending from the greater horn and body of the hyoid bone to the side of the tongue.|The hyoglossus depresses and retracts the tongue. 舌骨舌肌使舌头下降和回缩。|Synonyms:  lingual muscle (more general) |Antonyms:  genioglossus (has opposite action, protruding the tongue)|noun
