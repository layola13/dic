normalises:/ˈnɔːməlaɪzɪz/
verb
使正常化，标准化
to make something conform to a standard or norm.
The software normalises the volume of different audio tracks.  该软件使不同音轨的音量标准化。
New regulations are being introduced to normalise the industry.  新的法规正在出台以规范该行业。
standardizes, regulates
deviates, distorts
v.
