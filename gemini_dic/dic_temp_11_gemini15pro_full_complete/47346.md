de-stratifies:/diːˈstrætɪfaɪz/
verb
消除分层，使均匀
to eliminate or reduce the stratification of (something); to make less stratified
The mixing of the water in the lake destratifies the temperature layers.  湖水混合消除了温度分层。
近义词: homogenize, mix, blend, integrate
反义词: stratify, layer, separate, segregate
词性: verb

词源:
de- (prefix): meaning "down, away, off, thoroughly, reversal"
stratify (verb): from Late Latin stratificare "to arrange in layers," from Latin stratum "covering, pavement, layer, blanket" + -ficare "-fy"
