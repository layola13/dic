deploredness:/dɪˈplɔːrɪdnəs/
n.
悲惨；凄惨；可悲
The state of being deplored; wretchedness.
The deploredness of the situation moved many to tears.  这场灾难的悲惨景象让很多人流下了眼泪。
近义词: wretchedness, misery, piteousness, sadness, sorrowfulness
反义词: blessedness, happiness, joyfulness
词性: 名词

deplore:/dɪˈplɔːr/
v.
公开表示强烈反对；谴责；对…感到悲痛；对…深感遗憾
1. To feel or express strong disapproval of (something).
2. To regret deeply; lament.
He deplored the violence and bloodshed. 他谴责暴力和流血事件。
She deplored the loss of her beloved pet. 她对失去心爱的宠物深感悲痛。
近义词: lament, bewail, bemoan, condemn, denounce
反义词: approve, commend, praise, celebrate
词性: 动词

词源:  From Old French *deplorer*, from Latin deplōrāre (“to bewail, lament”), from de- (“down, completely”) + plōrāre (“to weep, cry, wail”).
