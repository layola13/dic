appease: /əˈpiːz/ | v. | 平息，安抚，缓和 | to make someone less angry or upset by giving them what they want |  The government tried to appease the protesters by promising to consider their demands. 政府承诺考虑抗议者的要求，试图安抚他们。

The company appeased its investors by increasing dividends. 公司通过增加股息来安抚投资者。

近义词: placate, conciliate, mollify, soothe | 反义词: provoke, aggravate, incite, antagonize | 词性: 动词

词源: From Old French *apaisier "pacify, appease," from a- "to" (see ad-) + pais "peace," from Latin pacem (nominative pax) "peace," related to pangere "to fix, fasten"
