misorganized:/ˌmɪsˈɔːrɡənaɪzd/ | adj. | 混乱的，组织不好的 | lacking organization; not properly planned or arranged |  His desk was completely disorganized, covered in piles of papers. 他的桌子完全杂乱无章，堆满了文件。

The disorganized state of the company led to its eventual downfall.  公司混乱的状态导致了它最终的垮台。| 近义词: messy, cluttered, chaotic, disordered | 反义词: organized, tidy, orderly | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning "badly" or "wrongly") + "organized" (from "organize," from French "organiser," from Late Latin "organizare," from Latin "organum" meaning "instrument, tool")
