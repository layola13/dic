artotyrite: /ɑːrˈtɒtɪˌraɪt/ | n. | 阿托泰利特派教徒；artotyrite面包 | A member of a Christian sect in Galatia in the 2nd century who celebrated the Eucharist with bread and cheese, believing that Christ offered the same elements at the Last Supper.|  The artotyrites were considered heretical by the early Church.  阿托泰利特派教徒被早期教会视为异端。| Synonym: heretic | Antonym: orthodox | noun
