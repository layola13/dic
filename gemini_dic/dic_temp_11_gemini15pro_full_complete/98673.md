insulation:/ˌɪnsəˈleɪʃn/ | n. | 隔热；绝缘；隔离；隔音 | The house has good insulation, so it stays warm in winter. 这房子隔热性能好，所以冬天很暖和。 |  Material used to prevent the passage of heat, electricity, or sound.  用于防止热量、电流或声音通过的材料。|  The insulation in the attic needs to be replaced.  阁楼上的隔热材料需要更换。| synonyms: padding, lagging, isolation, protection | antonyms: conduction, connection | noun

insulate:/ˈɪnsjʊleɪt/ | v. | 隔离；使绝缘；使隔热；隔音 |  They insulated the attic to reduce energy costs.  他们给阁楼做了隔热处理以降低能源成本。|  To protect something by covering it with a material that prevents the passage of heat, electricity, or sound.  用一种能防止热量、电流或声音通过的材料覆盖某物以保护它。 |  Double-glazed windows help to insulate the house against noise.  双层玻璃窗有助于隔绝房屋噪音。| synonyms: protect, shield, isolate, cushion | antonyms: expose, connect | verb
