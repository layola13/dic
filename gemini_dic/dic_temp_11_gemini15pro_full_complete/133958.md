nonhepatic:/ˌnɒn.hɪˈpæt.ɪk/
adjective
非肝脏的；肝外
Not relating to or originating in the liver.
例句：Nonhepatic causes of jaundice include hemolytic anemia and biliary obstruction.  非肝脏引起的黄疸包括溶血性贫血和胆道梗阻。

例句：The doctor ruled out nonhepatic sources of the problem. 医生排除了问题出在肝脏以外的可能性。


近义词：extrahepatic
反义词：hepatic
词性：adjective
