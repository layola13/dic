baluster: /ˈbæləstər/ | n. | 栏杆小柱，支柱 | A short, vertical post or pillar, typically one of a series supporting a rail or coping. |  The ornate balusters of the staircase added to the grandeur of the house.  这楼梯上华丽的栏杆小柱增添了房屋的宏伟。| Synonyms: spindle, banister, balustrade | Antonyms: none | Noun


balustrade: /ˌbæləˈstreɪd/ | n. | 栏杆 | A railing supported by balusters, especially an ornamental parapet on a balcony, bridge, or terrace. | The balustrade overlooking the ocean provided a stunning view.  俯瞰大海的栏杆提供了令人惊叹的景色。| Synonyms: railing, banister | Antonyms: none | Noun
