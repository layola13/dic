asperifoliate:/ˌæspərɪˈfoʊliət/
adj.
粗糙叶的，具有粗糙叶子的
Having rough leaves, often covered with short, stiff hairs.
The plant is asperifoliate, with leaves that feel like sandpaper.  这种植物是粗糙叶的，叶子摸起来像砂纸。
近义词:hispid, scabrous
反义词:smooth, glabrous
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *asperifolius*  (rough-leaved), from *asper* (rough) + *folium* (leaf).
