embedment:/ɪmˈbedmənt/| n.|嵌入，埋置，包埋|The process of embedding something, or the state of being embedded.  The act of fixing something firmly and deeply in a surrounding mass.|The archaeologist found an embedment of ancient coins in the rock. 考古学家在岩石中发现了一处古代硬币的埋藏。|近义词：embedding, insertion, incorporation, implant|反义词:extraction, removal|noun
