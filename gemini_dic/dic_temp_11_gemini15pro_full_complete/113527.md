maat: /mɑːt/
noun
真理;正义;秩序;古埃及神话中的真理和正义女神
The ancient Egyptian concept of maat encompassed truth, justice, balance, and cosmic order.
古埃及玛阿特概念包含真理、正义、平衡和宇宙秩序。

Maat was personified as a goddess in ancient Egyptian religion.
玛阿特在古埃及宗教中被拟人化成一位女神。

The pharaoh was responsible for upholding maat in the kingdom.
法老有责任维护王国的玛阿特。


近义词: justice, righteousness, truth, balance, order, harmony
反义词: injustice, chaos, disorder, falsehood

词性: n.
