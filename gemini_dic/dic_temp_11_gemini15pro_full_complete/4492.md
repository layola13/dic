albinisms:/ˈælbɪˌnɪzəmz/
n.
白化病（复数）
A group of inherited disorders characterized by little or no melanin production.  It affects the color of the skin, hair, and eyes.
Albinisms can affect people of all races. 白化病可以影响所有种族的人。
Oculocutaneous albinism is the most common type of albinism. 眼皮肤白化病是最常见的白化病类型。
synonym: achromia, achromasia
antonym: pigmentation
noun
