malignly:/məˈlaɪnli/ | adv. | 恶毒地，恶意地 |  In a manner that shows ill will or hatred; with the intention to harm or damage someone's reputation.  | He spoke malignly about his former employer.  他恶毒地谈论他以前的雇主。|  She glared malignly at him. 她恶狠狠地瞪着他。 | synonym: spitefully, viciously, malevolently | antonym: kindly, benevolently, charitably | 词性: 副词

malignant:/məˈlɪɡnənt/ | adj. | 恶性的，恶意的 |  (of a disease) very virulent or infectious.  | The doctor discovered a malignant tumor. 医生发现了一个恶性肿瘤。| His malignant gossip spread quickly. 他恶意的流言蜚语传播得很快。 | synonym: cancerous, harmful, malevolent | antonym: benign, harmless, benevolent | 词性: 形容词

词源：
