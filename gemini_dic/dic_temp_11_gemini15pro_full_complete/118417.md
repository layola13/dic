merry-go-round:/ˈmeri ɡoʊ raʊnd/ (US) /ˈmeri ɡəʊ raʊnd/ (UK)| n.|旋转木马|A revolving circular platform with seats, often in the shape of animals, on which people, especially children, ride for amusement.|The children were delighted with the merry-go-round at the fair. 孩子们对游乐场里的旋转木马感到很高兴。|synonyms: carousel, roundabout|antonyms: None|noun

merry-go-round:/ˈmeri ɡoʊ raʊnd/ (US) /ˈmeri ɡəʊ raʊnd/ (UK)| adj. (informal)|繁忙的，令人晕头转向的|Characterized by rapid and confusing change or activity.|It was a merry-go-round of meetings and phone calls all day. 一整天都是令人晕头转向的会议和电话。|synonyms: hectic, busy, chaotic|antonyms: calm, peaceful|adjective
