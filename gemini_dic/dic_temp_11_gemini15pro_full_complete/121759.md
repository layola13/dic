misqualifies:/ˌmɪskˈwɑːlɪfaɪz/
verb
使…不合格，使…丧失资格
To make someone or something ineligible or unsuitable for something.
His past criminal record misqualifies him from holding public office. 他的过去犯罪记录使他没有资格担任公职。
The doping scandal misqualified her from competing in the Olympics.  兴奋剂丑闻使她没有资格参加奥运会。
disqualifies, invalidates
qualifies, entitles
