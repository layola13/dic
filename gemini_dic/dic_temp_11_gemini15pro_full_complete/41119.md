coprolite: /ˈkɒprəlaɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːprəlaɪt/ (US)| n. |粪化石；石化的粪便|Fossilized feces. It provides valuable information about the diet and lifestyle of extinct animals.| Paleontologists found a coprolite containing fish bones, indicating the creature's diet.  古生物学家发现了一块含有鱼骨的粪化石，这表明了该生物的饮食。|Synonyms: fossilized feces, petrified dung|Antonyms: None|Noun
