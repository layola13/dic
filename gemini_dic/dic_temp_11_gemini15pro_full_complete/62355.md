endgames:/ˈɛndɡeɪmz/| n.|残局，最终阶段，最后阶段|The endgame of the negotiation was tense. 谈判的最后阶段气氛紧张。|例句：The company is in the endgame of its restructuring process.  这家公司正处于重组过程的最后阶段。

例句：The political endgame is approaching, and tensions are high. 政治角逐的最后阶段即将到来，气氛十分紧张。

近义词：conclusion, final stage, last phase, denouement|反义词:beginning, start, commencement|词性:名词

endgame:/ˈɛndɡeɪmz/| n.|最终目的，最终目标|His endgame is to become CEO. 他的最终目标是成为首席执行官。|例句：What's his endgame in all this? 他这一切的最终目的是什么？

例句：Her endgame was clear: to win the championship. 她的最终目标很明确：赢得冠军。

近义词：goal, objective, aim, purpose|反义词: - |词性:名词
