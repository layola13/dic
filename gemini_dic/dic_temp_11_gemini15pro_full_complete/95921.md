incrimination:/ɪnˌkrɪmɪˈneɪʃən/| n.|控告；归罪；牵连|The act of accusing someone of a crime or wrongdoing; the state of being accused. |His incrimination in the robbery led to his arrest. 他因被指控参与抢劫而被捕。|Synonyms: accusation, indictment, implication, charge|Antonyms: exoneration, acquittal, vindication|noun


incriminate:/ɪnˈkrɪmɪneɪt/| vt.|使负罪；牵连；控告|To make someone appear guilty of a crime or wrongdoing; to involve someone in a crime. |The evidence seemed to incriminate him.  证据似乎表明他有罪。|Synonyms: implicate, accuse, indict, charge|Antonyms: exonerate, acquit, vindicate|verb
