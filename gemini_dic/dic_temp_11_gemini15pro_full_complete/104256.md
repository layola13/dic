jus:/dʒʌs/ | n. | 汁; 液; 肉汁; 重要内容;  |The essential point; the gist. Derived from Latin "jus" meaning "law, right, broth, juice". Originally referring to law or right, it evolved to also mean the liquid extracted from meat, and later figuratively to the essence or core of something. | The jus of the argument is that we need to invest more in education.  这番论证的核心是，我们需要在教育上加大投资。 |  近义词: essence, gist, substance, gravy | 反义词:  exterior, surface | 词性: 名词
