impervious:/ɪmˈpɜːrviəs/| adj.|不能渗透的；不为所动的；不受影响的|Not allowing fluid to pass through.  Unable to be affected by. |The material is impervious to water. 这种材料不透水。

The President remained impervious to criticism. 总统对批评无动于衷。|近义词：impenetrable, resistant, immune|反义词：permeable, porous, susceptible|词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Late Latin *impervius*, from in- ‘not’ + pervius ‘passable’.
