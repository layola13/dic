braggart: /ˈbræɡərt/ (US), /ˈbræɡɑːt/ (UK)| n.| 自夸者，吹牛大王 |A person who boasts about achievements or possessions.| He's a braggart who can't stop talking about himself. 他是个吹牛大王，总是停不下来地谈论自己。|近义词：boaster, show-off, blowhard|反义词：humble person, modest person|词性: noun

braggart: /ˈbræɡərt/ (US), /ˈbræɡɑːt/ (UK)| adj.| 吹牛的，自夸的 |Boastful or arrogant.| He made braggart claims about his wealth. 他吹嘘自己的财富。|近义词：boastful, arrogant, vainglorious|反义词：humble, modest|词性: adjective
