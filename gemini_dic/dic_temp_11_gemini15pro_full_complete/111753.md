lodemanage:/ˈloʊdmænɪdʒ/ | n. |  领航费，引水费 | The fee paid to a pilot for guiding a ship. | The ship's owner paid a hefty lodemanage for navigating the treacherous strait. 船主为引航通过危险的海峡支付了高额的领航费。 | 近义词: pilotage, pilot's fee | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Middle French *lodemanage*, from *lodeman* "pilot" (from Middle Dutch *loetman*, from *loet* "lead" + *man* "man") + *-age*.
