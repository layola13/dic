aliquot:/ˈælɪkwɑːt/ | n. | 等分，等份，部分 | A portion of a larger whole, especially a sample taken for chemical analysis or other treatment.  |  The chemist took several aliquots of the solution for testing. 化学家取了几份溶液进行测试。 | synonyms: portion, sample, share, fraction | antonyms: whole, entirety | noun

aliquot:/ˈælɪkwɑːt/ | adj. | 等分的 | Consisting of, or being a known fraction of a whole. | Aliquot portions of the sample were analyzed. 样品的等分部分被分析了。| synonyms: proportional, fractional | antonyms: whole, entire | adjective
