nonprotections:/ˌnɒnprəˈtɛkʃənz/
noun
缺乏保护；未受保护的状态
The lack of protections for whistleblowers discourages reporting of misconduct. 缺乏对举报人的保护措施阻碍了对不当行为的举报。
The report highlighted the nonprotections afforded to migrant workers. 报告强调了移民工人缺乏保护。
Synonyms: vulnerabilities, exposures, defenselessness
Antonyms: safeguards, protections, securities
词性: 名词


Etymology:
non- (prefix):  not; absence of. From Latin *non.
protection (noun): the action of protecting or the state of being protected. From Late Middle English: from Old French proteccion, from late Latin protectio(n-), from the verb protegere (see protect).
