eloge:/ˈɛlədʒi/ | n. |颂词，赞词，悼词|A formal expression of praise; a eulogy. |例句：The poet wrote a moving eloge for his deceased friend. 这位诗人为他已故的朋友写了一篇感人的颂词。

例句：His eloge for the fallen soldiers was both heartfelt and inspiring. 他对阵亡士兵的颂词既感人又鼓舞人心。|近义词：eulogy, tribute, panegyric, encomium, accolade|反义词：criticism, condemnation, denunciation|词性: noun

词源: From French *éloge*, from Ancient Greek *εὐλογία* (*eulogía*)  meaning "praise", from *εὖ* (*eu*) "well" + *λόγος* (*lógos*) "word, speech".
