aforethought:/əˈfɔːrθɔːt/| adj.|预谋的，事先想好的|Planned or intended beforehand; premeditated.  Characterized by deliberate planning.|He was charged with murder with malice aforethought. 他被指控犯有预谋杀人罪。|Synonyms: premeditation, deliberation, intent, design, purpose|Antonyms: impulsiveness, spontaneity|adjective

aforethought:/əˈfɔːrθɔːt/| n.|预谋，预先考虑|The planning or intention to commit a crime, especially murder, beforehand.|The prosecution had to prove malice aforethought. 控方必须证明恶意预谋。|Synonyms: premeditation, deliberation, forethought|Antonyms: impulsiveness, spontaneity|noun
