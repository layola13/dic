multistemmed:/ˌmʌltiˈstemd/ | adj. | 多茎的，多杆的 | Having multiple stems arising from the base or root system. | The multistemmed tree provided ample shade.  这棵多茎的树提供了充足的阴凉。| The gardener pruned the multistemmed bush. 园丁修剪了多茎的灌木。 |  multi-trunked, many-stemmed | single-stemmed | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From "multi-" (meaning "many") + "stemmed" (having stems).  "Multi-" comes from Latin "multus" (many, much).  "Stemmed" comes from Old English "stefn" and "stemn" (stem, trunk, prow of a ship).
