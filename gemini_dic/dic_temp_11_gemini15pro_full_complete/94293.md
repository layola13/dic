impertinentness:/ɪmˈpɜːrtɪnəntnəs/| n. |无礼，鲁莽，不恰当|Lack of respect; rudeness; inappropriateness.  The quality or state of being impertinent.|Her impertinentness to the judge almost landed her in jail. 她对法官的无礼几乎让她进了监狱。|Synonyms: rudeness, insolence, impudence, audacity, cheek, disrespect, incivility, presumption, forwardness|Antonyms: politeness, respect, courtesy, deference, humility|noun
