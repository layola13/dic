appealer:/əˈpiːlər/| n.|上诉人，申诉人，引起兴趣的人(或事物)|A person who appeals a decision to a higher court or authority; something that evokes interest or sympathy.|The appealer argued that the original verdict was flawed. 上诉人辩称原判决有缺陷。|Synonyms: applicant, petitioner, claimant, appellant|Antonyms: respondent, defendant|noun

appealing:/əˈpiːlɪŋ/| adj.|吸引人的，有感染力的，令人感兴趣的|Attractive or interesting; evoking sympathy or approval.|The idea of a vacation in the tropics is very appealing.  在热带地区度假的主意非常吸引人。|Synonyms: attractive, charming, engaging, alluring|Antonyms: unappealing, repulsive, off-putting|adjective
