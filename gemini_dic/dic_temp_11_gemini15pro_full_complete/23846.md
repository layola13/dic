breadfruit:/ˈbredˌfruːt/| n. |面包果|A large, starchy fruit of a tropical tree, resembling a bread roll in shape and texture when cooked.|The islanders relied heavily on breadfruit as a staple food. 岛民主要依靠面包果作为主食。|Near synonyms:  jackfruit, durian|Antonym: None (It's a specific fruit, not a concept with an opposite.)|noun

breadfruits:/ˈbredˌfruːts/| n. |面包果（复数）|Plural form of breadfruit, referring to multiple of these fruits.|She brought home a basket of breadfruits from the market. 她从市场带回一篮子面包果。|Near synonyms: jackfruits, durians|Antonym: None|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bread + fruit.  The name refers to the texture and taste of the cooked fruit, which resembles bread.
