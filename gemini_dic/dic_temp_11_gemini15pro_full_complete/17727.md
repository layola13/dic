beddy-byes:/ˈbɛdi baɪz/
noun
睡觉时间；上床睡觉
A child's term for bedtime.
例句：It's time for beddy-byes, little one. 小家伙，该睡觉了。

例句：Come on, sleepyhead, let's get you to beddy-byes.  来吧，瞌睡虫，我们去睡觉吧。

近义词：bedtime, sleep time
反义词: waking time, get-up time
词性:noun
