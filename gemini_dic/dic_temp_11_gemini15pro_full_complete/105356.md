kickee: /ˈkɪkiː/
noun
婴儿连体衣;  (尤指)柔软舒适的婴儿连体衣
A one-piece garment for babies, especially a soft, comfortable one.
例句：The baby slept soundly in his soft kickee.  婴儿穿着柔软的连体衣睡得很香。

例句：She bought several cute kickees for her newborn nephew. 她给她刚出生的侄子买了几个可爱的婴儿连体衣。

近义词：onesie, romper, sleeper
反义词:  (无明确的反义词，可以考虑用adult clothing 成人服装)
词性: n.


Etymology:  "Kickee" is a relatively recent term, likely originating in the late 20th or early 21st century, possibly as a brand name or a descriptive term evoking the kicking movements of babies. Its etymology is not definitively established.
