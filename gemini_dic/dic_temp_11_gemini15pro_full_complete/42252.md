countenancing:/ˈkaʊntənənsɪŋ/ | v. | 赞成；支持；认可；容忍 |  Admitting as acceptable or possible. | He would never countenance such behavior. 他绝不会容忍这种行为。| 近义词：tolerating, permitting, condoning, accepting, approving | 反义词：opposing, condemning, disapproving, rejecting | 词性: 动词

countenance: /ˈkaʊntənəns/ | n. | 面容，表情；赞同，支持 | A person's face or facial expression. Support or approval. | His countenance was grim. 他的表情很严肃。/  She gave her countenance to the project. 她对这个项目表示支持。| 近义词：(n.) face, expression, mien; (v.) support, approval | 反义词：(v.) disapproval, opposition | 词性: 名词, 动词
