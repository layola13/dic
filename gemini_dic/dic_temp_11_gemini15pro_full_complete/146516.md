overgenialities:/ˌoʊvərˌdʒiːniˈælɪtiz/| n. | 过分热情，过分友好 | Excessive geniality; instances of being too friendly or amiable. | His overgenialities made some people uncomfortable. 他的过分热情让一些人感到不舒服。| The overgenialities of the host were a bit much for the introverted guests.  主人过分的热情对于内向的客人来说有点太多了。 | 近义词：effusiveness, exuberance | 反义词： coldness, aloofness | 词性: 名词 (plural noun)

overgenial:/ˌoʊvərˈdʒiːniəl/| adj. | 过分热情的，过分友好的 | Excessively or inappropriately friendly or amiable. | He gave an overgenial welcome that felt insincere. 他给予了一个感觉不真诚的过分热情的欢迎。 |  Her overgenial laughter seemed forced. 她过分热情的笑声显得勉强。| 近义词：effusive, exuberant | 反义词：cold, aloof | 词性: 形容词

genial:/ˈdʒiːniəl/| adj. | 友好的，亲切的，和蔼的 | Friendly and cheerful. | He had a genial smile and a warm personality. 他面带和蔼的微笑，性格热情。 | The genial host made everyone feel welcome.  和蔼可亲的主人让每个人都感到宾至如归。 | 近义词：amiable, affable, cordial | 反义词：unfriendly, hostile | 词性: 形容词
