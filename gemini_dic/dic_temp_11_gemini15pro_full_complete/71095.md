fish-out-of-water:/ˌfɪʃ aʊt əv ˈwɔːtər/ | idiom |  格格不入的人；在陌生环境不得其所的人 | Someone who is in a situation they are not used to and feels uncomfortable |  Moving from a small rural town to a bustling metropolis, she felt like a fish out of water. 从一个乡村小镇搬到一个熙熙攘攘的大都市，她感到格格不入。|  out of place, uncomfortable, awkward | comfortable, at ease | noun phrase


fish-out-of-water:/ˌfɪʃ aʊt əv ˈwɔːtər/ | adjective |  格格不入的；在陌生环境不得其所的 | Feeling uncomfortable or awkward in unfamiliar surroundings |  He had a fish-out-of-water expression on his face as he tried to navigate the formal dinner party.  当他试图驾驭正式的晚宴时，他的脸上带着一种格格不入的表情。|  out of place, uncomfortable, awkward | comfortable, at ease | adjective
