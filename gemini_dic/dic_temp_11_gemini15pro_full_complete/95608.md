incommunicated:/ˌɪnkəˈmjuːnɪkeɪtɪd/
adjective
未传达的，未沟通的，未交流的
Not communicated; not disclosed or revealed.
Her feelings of frustration remained incommunicated. 她沮丧的心情一直没有表达出来。
The true reason for his departure was incommunicated. 他离开的真正原因没有透露。
Synonyms: undisclosed, unrevealed, unsaid, unexpressed, withheld, hidden, concealed, private, secret
Antonyms: communicated, disclosed, revealed, expressed, public, open
词性: 形容词
