corninesses:/ˈkɔːrnɪnɪsɪz/
noun
陈腐；老套；庸俗
the quality of being corny or overly sentimental
Her speech was full of corninesses that made the audience cringe. 她的演讲充满了陈词滥调，让观众感到尴尬。

近义词:banalities, platitudes, clichés, triteness
反义词: originality, freshness, sophistication

词性:noun (plural)
