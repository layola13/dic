cyperus:/saɪˈpɪərəs/
n.
莎草,莎草属植物
A large genus of grass-like or rush-like plants of the sedge family, growing in wet places. Many species have medicinal or aromatic properties.
The ancient Egyptians used cyperus papyrus to make paper. 古埃及人用纸莎草制作纸张。

近义词:sedge, papyrus
反义词:None
词性:noun
