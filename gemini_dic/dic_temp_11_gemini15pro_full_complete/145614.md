overconservativeness:/ˌoʊvərˈkənˈsɜːrvətɪvnəs/
|n.|过度保守，过分保守|The overconservativeness of the bank's lending policies stifled economic growth.  这家银行过于保守的贷款政策扼杀了经济增长。|
|overconservativeness is a noun derived from the adjective "conservative" with the prefix "over-" indicating an excessive degree.  It refers to the quality or state of being excessively cautious and resistant to change.|
|Their overconservativeness prevented them from taking advantage of new market opportunities. 他们的过度保守使他们无法利用新的市场机会。|
|近义词:excessive caution, undue caution, timidity|
|反义词:rashness, recklessness, boldness|
|词性:noun|
