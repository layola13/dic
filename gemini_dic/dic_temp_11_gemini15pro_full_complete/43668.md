crebrous:/ˈkrebrəs/| adj.|频繁的，密集的，稠密的|Frequent, close together, dense.  Characterized by frequent recurrence or numerous occurrences within a short span.  Often used to describe things that are clustered or closely packed together.|The crebrous undergrowth made it difficult to navigate. 稠密的灌木丛使得难以通行。|The doctor noted the patient's crebrous coughing fits. 医生注意到病人频繁的咳嗽发作。|近义词：frequent, numerous, dense, thick, copious|反义词：sparse, infrequent, scarce, rare|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *creber* (frequent, thick, numerous).
