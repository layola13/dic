outyelled: /ˌaʊtˈjɛld/
verb
大声喊过，盖过…的喊叫声
To shout louder than someone else.
He outyelled the other fans at the concert. 他在音乐会上盖过了其他歌迷的喊叫声。
Synonyms: outshout, drown out
Antonyms: be drowned out, be outshouted
词性: 动词
