de-democratizes:/diː.dɪˈmɒkrəˌtaɪzɪz/ | v. | 去民主化；削弱民主 |  To remove democratic elements or processes from something, making it less democratic. | The new laws effectively de-democratize the election process by limiting voter access. 新法律通过限制选民投票，有效地削弱了选举进程的民主性。| The military coup de-democratized the country, stripping citizens of their basic rights. 军事政变使该国去民主化，剥夺了公民的基本权利。 | 近义词: undermine democracy, dismantle democracy, authoritarianize | 反义词: democratize, empower, enfranchise | 词性: 动词

词源:  The prefix "de-" indicates reversal or removal, combined with "democratize" (to make democratic), resulting in "de-democratize," meaning to undo or reverse the process of democratization.
