dismissable:/dɪsˈmɪsəbəl/| adj.|可解雇的；可驳回的；可消除的|Able to be dismissed, rejected, or put aside.|The case was dismissable due to lack of evidence.  这案子由于缺乏证据而被驳回。|The notification is dismissable by clicking the "X" button.  点击“X”按钮即可消除通知。|近义词:rejectable, dispensable, negligible|反义词:essential, crucial, indispensable|词性:形容词

词源: From "dismiss" + "-able". "Dismiss" comes from Old French "desmettre"  (to put out of commission, discharge), from "des-" (apart, away) + "mettre" (to put, place).  "-able" is a suffix meaning "capable of being".
