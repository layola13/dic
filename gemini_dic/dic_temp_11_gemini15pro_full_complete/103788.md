journos:/ˈdʒɜːrnoʊz/
Noun
记者（复数）
Informal term for journalists.
The journos were all scrambling to get the scoop on the latest scandal. 记者们都在争先恐后地报道最新丑闻。
Synonyms: reporters, journalists, correspondents, press, media
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps consider "censors," "propagandists")
词性: noun (plural)
