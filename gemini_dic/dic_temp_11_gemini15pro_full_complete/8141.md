anti-catholics:/ˌæntiˈkæθəlɪks/
|n.|反天主教徒，反对天主教的人|People who oppose or are prejudiced against the Catholic Church and its followers are called anti-catholics.  那些反对或对天主教会及其追随者抱有偏见的人被称为反天主教徒。|During the Reformation, tensions between Protestants and anti-catholics were high.  在宗教改革期间，新教徒和反天主教徒之间的关系非常紧张.|Synonyms: Catholic-haters, opponents of Catholicism|Antonyms: Catholics, supporters of Catholicism|noun
