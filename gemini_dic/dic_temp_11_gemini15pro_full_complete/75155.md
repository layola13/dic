fritter:/ˈfrɪtər/ (US) /ˈfrɪtə(r)/ (UK)| v. n.|浪费， fritter (something) away; 油炸馅饼|to waste time, money, or effort on something small or unimportant; a small quantity of fried batter (= a mixture of flour, egg, and milk or water) containing fruit, vegetables, or meat|例句：She frittered away her inheritance on expensive clothes. 她把遗产浪费在昂贵的衣服上。|例句：He frittered away the whole afternoon watching TV. 他浪费了整个下午看电视。|近义词：squander, waste, dissipate|反义词：save, conserve, preserve|词性:动词
