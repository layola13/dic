hyomandibular:/haɪˌoʊmænˈdɪbjʊlər/| adj. n.|舌颌骨的；舌颌骨|A bone forming part of the jaw suspension in most gnathostome fishes, being lost or reduced in tetrapods.|The hyomandibular bone plays a crucial role in the jaw mechanics of many fishes.  舌颌骨在许多鱼类的颌部力学中起着至关重要的作用。|Synonyms:  hyoid,  jawbone (in a broader sense)|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific bone)|adjective, noun
