overclogged:/ˌoʊvərˈklɑːɡd/
adjective
过度堵塞的
Excessively clogged; filled to excess and blocked.
The drain was overclogged with hair and soap.  排水沟被头发和肥皂堵塞了。
The internet was overclogged with traffic during the peak hours.  高峰时段互联网流量过度拥堵。
Synonyms: blocked, congested, jammed, obstructed
Antonyms: clear, free-flowing, unobstructed
词性: adjective
