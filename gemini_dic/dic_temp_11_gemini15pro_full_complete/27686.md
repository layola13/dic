candlenut:/ˈkændəlˌnʌt/ | n. |  桐树果，石栗 | The candlenut tree produces a nut that is rich in oil.  桐树产一种富含油脂的坚果。|例句：Candlenuts are used in Indonesian cuisine. 石栗用于印尼菜肴。|例句：The oil from candlenuts can be used as a biofuel.  石栗油可以用作生物燃料。|近义词：kukui nut | 反义词: (无常用反义词) | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From candle + nut. So named because the nuts are so oily they can be strung together and burned like candles.
