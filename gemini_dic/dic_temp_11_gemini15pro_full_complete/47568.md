debark:/dɪˈbɑːrk/| v. |下船;下飞机;登陆|To leave a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle, especially at the end of a journey.|The passengers debarked at Southampton. 乘客们在南安普敦下船。|近义词：disembark, land, go ashore|反义词:embark, board|词性:动词
debark:/dɪˈbɑːrk/| v. |(从树皮上)剥皮|To remove the bark from (a tree or log).|The lumberjacks debarked the trees before processing them. 伐木工人在加工树木之前先剥去了树皮。|近义词：peel, strip|反义词:N/A|词性:动词

词源：
