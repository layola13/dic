antiseptic:/ˌæntiˈsɛptɪk/ | adj. n. |抗菌的，防腐的；防腐剂，抗菌剂 |A substance that prevents the growth of disease-causing microorganisms. |
例句：The doctor cleaned the wound with an antiseptic solution. 医生用防腐溶液清洗了伤口。

例句：Antiseptic wipes are essential for any first-aid kit.  抗菌湿巾是任何急救箱的必需品。

近义词：disinfectant, germicidal, antimicrobial |
反义词：septic |
词性：adjective, noun


antiseptics:/ˌæntiˈsɛptɪks/ | n. |防腐剂，抗菌剂 |Substances that prevent the growth of disease-causing microorganisms. |
例句：Many different antiseptics are available over the counter. 许多不同的防腐剂都可以非处方购买。


例句：The use of antiseptics is crucial in preventing infection. 使用防腐剂对于预防感染至关重要。

近义词：disinfectants, germicides, antimicrobials |
反义词：N/A |
词性：noun


词源:  From Ancient Greek ἀντί (anti, “against”) + σηπτικός (sēptikos, “putrefactive”).
