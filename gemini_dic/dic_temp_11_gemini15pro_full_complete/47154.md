dawdled:/ˈdɔːdld/ (US) /ˈdɔːd(ə)ld/ (UK)| v. |闲逛，磨蹭，拖延|To waste time; to spend time idly; to linger or loiter.|例句：He dawdled on his way to school and missed the bus. 他在上学的路上磨蹭，错过了公交车。

例句：She dawdled over her breakfast, savoring every bite. 她慢悠悠地吃着早餐，细细品味每一口。|近义词：linger, loiter, delay, procrastinate, tarry|反义词：hurry, rush, hasten, expedite|词性：动词
