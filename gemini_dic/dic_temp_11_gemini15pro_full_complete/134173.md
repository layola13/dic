nonincestuous:/ˌnɒnɪnˈsɛstʃuəs/
adjective
非乱伦的
Not involving or characterized by incest; not related by blood closely enough to make sexual relations illegal or taboo.
A nonincestuous marriage is considered the norm in most societies.  在大多数社会中，非乱伦婚姻被认为是常态。
The study focused on nonincestuous relationships between adults.  这项研究的重点是成年人之间的非乱伦关系。
近义词: non-incestuous (variant spelling), exogamous
反义词: incestuous
词性: adjective
