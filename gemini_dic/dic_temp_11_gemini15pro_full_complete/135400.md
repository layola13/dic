nonpermissibly:/ˌnɒnpərˈmɪsɪbli/
adv.
不允许地，非法地
Not in a way that is permitted; illegally or without authorization.
The funds were nonpermissibly used for personal expenses. 资金被非法用于个人开支。
The document was nonpermissibly accessed by unauthorized personnel.  未经授权的人员非法访问了该文件。
近义词:illegally, unlawfully, wrongly, improperly
反义词:legally, permissibly, lawfully, properly
词性:adverb

Etymology:
From non- (not) + permissible + -ly (adverb suffix).  "Permissible" comes from  "permit" which traces back to Latin "permittere" (to let pass through).
