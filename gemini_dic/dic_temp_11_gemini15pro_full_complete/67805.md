eyesore:/ˈaɪˌsɔːr/| n. |眼中钉，碍眼的事物|Something unpleasant to look at; an ugly thing that spoils the appearance of a place.|例句：That abandoned factory is a real eyesore. 那家废弃的工厂真是个碍眼的东西。

例句：The graffiti on the wall is an eyesore. 墙上的涂鸦很不雅观。|近义词：blight, blemish, monstrosity|反义词:beauty, attraction|词性:noun
