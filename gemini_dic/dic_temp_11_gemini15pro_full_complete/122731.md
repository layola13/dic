moiety:/ˈmɔɪəti/ | n. | 一部分，一半；份额 |  One of two (approximately) equal parts. |  Each moiety of the molecule exhibits a distinct function.  这个分子的每一部分都表现出不同的功能。 | synonyms: half, portion, share, part, section | antonyms: whole, entirety | noun

moiety:/ˈmɔɪəti/ | n. | （尤指部落或土地的）一半 | (especially in anthropology or law) each of two parts into which a thing is or can be divided. |  The tribe was split into two moieties.  这个部落被分成两部分。 | synonyms: half, division, section, subsection | antonyms: whole, entirety | noun
