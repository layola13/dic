ciders:/ˈsaɪdərz/| n.|苹果酒（尤指不甜的，通常含酒精）|An alcoholic (typically strong) drink made from fermented apple juice. |例句：We enjoyed a refreshing glass of ciders on a hot summer day. 我们在炎热的夏日享用了一杯清爽的苹果酒。

例句：The local pub offers a wide variety of ciders. 当地的酒吧提供各种各样的苹果酒。|近义词：apple wine, hard cider|反义词:N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *cidre, *sidre, from Old French cidre, from Late Latin sicera, from Ancient Greek σίκερα (síkera, “strong drink”), borrowed from some Semitic language (compare Hebrew שֵׁכָר (šēḵār)).
