calamitousness:/kəˌlæmɪtəsnəs/
noun
灾难性，悲惨性
The state or quality of being calamitous; disastrousness.
The calamitousness of the earthquake shocked the world.  这场地震的灾难性震惊了世界。
calamity, disastrousness, tragedy, catastrophe
