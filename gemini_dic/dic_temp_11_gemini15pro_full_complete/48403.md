dedicating:/ˈdɛdɪkeɪtɪŋ/
v.
奉献，致力于
devoting oneself or one's efforts to a particular purpose or undertaking.
He is dedicating his life to helping the poor. 他正致力于帮助穷人。
devote, commit, consecrate
abandon, forsake
verb
