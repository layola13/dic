lavoltateer: /ləˌvɒl.təˈtɪər/
noun
旋转舞者; 参加旋转舞的人
A person who participates in or performs the lavolta, a lively dance popular in the 16th and 17th centuries involving high leaps and turns.
The lavoltateers twirled and leaped across the dance floor.  旋转舞者在舞池中旋转跳跃。
Near Synonyms: dancer, performer
Antonyms: spectator, observer
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *lavolta*, from *voltare* "to turn," from Latin *volvere* "to roll, turn."  The suffix "-eer" denotes a person who engages in a particular activity.
