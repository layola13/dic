darwinius: /dɑːrˈwɪniəs/
n.|达尔文猴|An extinct primate genus from the Eocene epoch, considered a significant find in the study of primate evolution.  It's known from a remarkably complete fossil nicknamed "Ida".|The discovery of Darwinius masillae provided valuable insights into early primate evolution.  达尔文猴的发现为早期灵长类动物的进化提供了宝贵的见解。|近义词:adapid (broadly), primate ancestor|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
