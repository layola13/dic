incongruous:/ɪnˈkɒŋɡruəs/ (US) /ˌɪnkənˈɡruːəs/| adj.|不协调的，不一致的，不相称的|Not in harmony or keeping with the surroundings or other aspects of something.|Her bright pink dress looked incongruous against the somber background of the funeral.|Synonyms: inappropriate, unsuitable, out of place, incompatible, inconsistent, jarring, clashing, discordant, dissonant|Antonyms: appropriate, suitable, fitting, harmonious, consistent, compatible|adjective
