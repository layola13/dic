biomagnetic:/ˌbaɪoʊmæɡˈnɛtɪk/| adj.|生物磁的|Relating to the magnetic fields produced by living organisms or the study of such fields.|Magnetoencephalography (MEG) is a biomagnetic technique for mapping brain activity. 脑磁图 (MEG) 是一种用于绘制大脑活动图的生物磁技术。

Biomagnetic fields are extremely weak. 生物磁场极其微弱。|近义词：geomagnetic|反义词: Non-magnetic|词性: adjective
