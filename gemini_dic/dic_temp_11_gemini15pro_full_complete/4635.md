aldis:/ˈɔːldiːz/
proper noun
奥乐齐
A global discount supermarket chain originally from Germany.
例句：I bought some groceries at Aldi's yesterday. 我昨天在奥乐齐买了一些食品杂货。

例句：Aldi's offers a wide range of affordable products. 奥乐齐提供各种价格实惠的产品。

近义词：Lidl, Trader Joe's (in some aspects)
反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific store, perhaps) High-end supermarkets, e.g., Whole Foods Market
词性: proper noun
