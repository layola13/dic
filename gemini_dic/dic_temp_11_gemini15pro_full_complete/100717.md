intramarginal:/ɪntrəˈmɑːrdʒɪnəl/ | adj. | 内边际的，边际内的 |  Existing or operating within a margin; specifically relating to units of a commodity, factor of production, etc., whose cost is less than the market price. | Intramarginal firms are those that would still be profitable even if the price of their product fell slightly.  内边际企业是指即使其产品价格略有下降，仍然能够盈利的企业。| Intramarginal land is land that is so productive that it would still be farmed even if crop prices were lower. 内边际土地是指即使作物价格下降，仍然可以耕种的高产土地。| 近义词: inframarginal | 反义词: extramarginal, supramarginal | 词性: adjective

词源: intra- (within) + marginal (relating to a margin)
