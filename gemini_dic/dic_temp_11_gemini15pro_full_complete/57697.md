drawplate:/ˈdrɔːpleɪt/ | n. |拉丝板，拔丝模 |A steel plate with a series of graduated holes through which metal is drawn to reduce its diameter.| The wire was thinned by pulling it through a drawplate. 这根金属丝是通过拉丝板拉细的。| synonyms: die, drawing plate | antonyms: N/A | noun
