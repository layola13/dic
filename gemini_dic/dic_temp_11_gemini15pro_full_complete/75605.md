fufu:/ˈfuːfuː/| n.| 芙芙，非洲的一种淀粉类食物|Fufu is a dough-like food made from boiled starchy vegetables, such as cassava, yams, or plantains, that are pounded together. It is a staple food in many West African and Central African countries.|She served fufu with a flavorful peanut soup. 她用美味的花生汤配芙芙。|Near synonyms: pounded yam, cassava fufu, plantain fufu|Antonyms: None (fufu is a specific food, not a concept with an opposite)|noun
