nonusuriousness:/ˌnɑːnjuˈʒʊəriəsnəs/
|n.| 不放高利贷;公平利率 |The nonusuriousness of the loan terms was confirmed by the bank.  银行确认了贷款条款的公平性。| The contract guaranteed the nonusuriousness of the interest rate.  合同保证了利率的合理性。 | fairness, reasonableness, legality | usury, extortion | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + usurious + -ness.  "Non-" prefix meaning "not," "usurious" meaning "practicing usury (the illegal action or practice of lending money at unreasonably high rates of interest)," and "-ness" suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality.
