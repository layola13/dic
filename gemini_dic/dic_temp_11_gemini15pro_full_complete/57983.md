drive-wall: /ˈdraɪv wɔːl/ | n. |挡土墙；支护墙| A retaining wall that resists the lateral pressure of soil or other materials. |  Example: The drive-wall prevented the hillside from collapsing onto the road. 挡土墙防止山坡坍塌到道路上。

Example:  They reinforced the drive-wall with steel beams. 他们用钢梁加固了挡土墙。 | Synonym: retaining wall, breast wall,revetment | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps 'excavation' in certain contexts if the drive-wall is preventing one) | Noun
