kiddy's:/ˈkɪdiz/| n.|小孩的（东西）; 儿童的（场所）|kiddy's refers to things or places intended for young children. It's a less formal and sometimes slightly condescending term.|例句：This is the kiddy's section of the pool. 这是泳池的儿童区。|近义词：children's, toddler's, infant's|反义词:adult's|词性:名词(所有格)

kiddy:/ˈkɪdi/| n.|小孩|A child, usually a young one.  Often used informally or even condescendingly.|例句：The kiddies were enjoying the playground. 孩子们在操场上玩得很开心。|近义词：child, toddler, youngster, tot|反义词:adult, grown-up|词性:名词
