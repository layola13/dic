liter:/ˈliːtər/ (US), /ˈliːtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|升|A metric unit of capacity, formerly defined as the volume of one kilogram of water under standard conditions, now equal to 1,000 cubic centimeters (about 1.75 pints).|I bought a two-liter bottle of soda. 我买了一瓶两升的汽水。|近义词:litre (British spelling)|反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement)|noun
