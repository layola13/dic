ghazal:/ˈɡɑːzəl/ | n. | غزل (ghazal) | A lyric poem with mystical, religious, or amorous themes, consisting of five or more couplets that share a single end rhyme and refrain. Each couplet can stand alone in meaning. |  She recited a beautiful ghazal about the pain of separation. 她朗诵了一首关于离别之痛的优美的加扎勒诗。| synonym: lyric, poem, ode | antonym: prose, novel | noun
