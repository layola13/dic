inferobranchiata:/ˌɪnfɛroʊbræŋˈkiːɑːtə/
n.
inferobranchs, a group of sea slugs
A taxonomic group of sea slugs characterized by gills located on the underside of the mantle.
Inferobranchs are a group of sea slugs found in marine environments.
Synonyms: inferobranchs
No commonly used antonyms.
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "inferus" (below, beneath) + Greek "brankhia" (gill)
