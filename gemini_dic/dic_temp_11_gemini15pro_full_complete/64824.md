espaliers:/ɪˈspæljərz/ | n. |  果树墙；棚架；墙树 | A framework typically made of wood or wire, used to train fruit trees, shrubs, or other plants to grow flat against a wall or other support. | The gardener carefully pruned the branches of the espaliers against the sunny wall. 园丁仔细修剪了靠在阳光充足的墙上的果树墙的树枝。

The espaliers were laden with ripe peaches. 这些棚架上挂满了成熟的桃子。| 近义词:trellis, lattice | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "free-standing") | 词性: noun


espalier: /ɪˈspæljər/ | vt. | 将…绑在棚架上；使…展开 | To train (a plant) to grow flat against a wall or other support, typically by pruning and tying.  |  He espaliered the pear trees along the south wall of the house. 他把梨树绑在房子南墙的棚架上。

She carefully espaliered the climbing roses to create a beautiful display. 她小心翼翼地将攀缘玫瑰绑在架子上，营造出美丽的景象。 | 近义词: train, support | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: verb
