delphinium:/dɛlˈfɪniəm/| n. |飞燕草；翠雀|A tall garden plant with spikes of often blue flowers.  It belongs to the buttercup family. |例句：The delphiniums in her garden were a vibrant shade of blue. 她花园里的飞燕草是鲜艳的蓝色。

例句：Delphiniums thrive in full sun and well-drained soil. 飞燕草在阳光充足、排水良好的土壤中生长茂盛。|近义词：larkspur|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *delphinium, from Ancient Greek δελφίνιον (delphínion), diminutive of δελφίς (delphís, “dolphin”), because of the resemblance of the unopened flower bud to the shape of a dolphin's nose.
