onomastics:/ˌɒnəˈmæstɪks/ | n. |命名学；专名研究|The study of proper names, including their origins, meanings, and usage across cultures.  It encompasses personal names, place names, and other proper nouns.|Onomastics is a fascinating field for those interested in the history and evolution of language. 命名学对于那些对语言的历史和演变感兴趣的人来说是一个迷人的领域。|Synonyms: name study, nomenclature|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym) | noun
