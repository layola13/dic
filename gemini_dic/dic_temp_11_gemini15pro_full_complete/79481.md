globalises:/ˈɡloʊbəlaɪzɪz/ /ˈɡləʊbəlaɪzɪz/| v. |使全球化|To make something worldwide in scope or application.|例句：The company is globalising its operations.  这家公司正在使其运营全球化。

例句：Technology has globalised communication. 科技使通讯全球化。

近义词：internationalizes, universalizes|反义词: localizes|词性:动词
