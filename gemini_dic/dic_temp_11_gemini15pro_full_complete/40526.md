controllership:/kənˈtroʊlərʃɪp/
n.
主计长职位；主计长职权；财务总监的职位
The position and duties of a financial controller.
The company is seeking a candidate with experience in controllership.  这家公司正在寻找一位有主计长经验的候选人。
Controllership involves overseeing all financial aspects of a company. 主计长工作涉及监督公司的所有财务方面。
synonyms:  comptrollership, financial control, financial management
antonyms:  lack of control, mismanagement

ETYMOLOGY->
From controller + -ship.  Controller ultimately derives from the Medieval Latin *contrarotulator, from contra- "against" + rotulator "one who keeps a roll," from rota "wheel."
