goofballs:/ˈɡuːfbɔːlz/
noun
笨蛋，傻瓜
A silly or foolish person.
Those goofballs locked their keys in the car again. 那些笨蛋又把钥匙锁在车里了。
近义词：idiots, fools, clowns, nitwits
反义词：geniuses, intellectuals
词性：n.


goofball:/ˈɡuːfbɔːl/
noun
笨蛋，傻瓜
A silly or foolish person.
He's such a goofball, always making us laugh. 他真是个傻瓜，总是让我们笑。
近义词：idiot, fool, clown, nitwit
反义词：genius, intellectual
词性：n.


词源：
From goof (meaning "a fool") + ball (meaning "a person").  The term likely originated in American slang in the mid-20th century.
