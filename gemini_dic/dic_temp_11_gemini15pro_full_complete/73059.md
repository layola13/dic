footnotes:/ˈfʊtnəʊts/ (UK) /ˈfʊtnoʊts/ (US)| n. |脚注；注释|A note at the bottom of a page that gives more detailed information about something mentioned in the main text.|The book contains numerous footnotes explaining the historical context. 这本书包含许多解释历史背景的脚注。|synonyms: annotation, note, comment, reference, explanation|antonyms: main text, body (of text)|noun
