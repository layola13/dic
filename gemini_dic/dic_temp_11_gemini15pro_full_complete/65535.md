euthanasia:/ˌjuːθəˈneɪʒə/ /ˌjuːθəˈneɪʒiə/
|n.|安乐死|The act or practice of painlessly putting to death persons suffering from incurable and painful diseases or in an irreversible coma. |The debate about euthanasia continues. 关于安乐死的争论仍在继续。|
|近义词：mercy killing|反义词: resuscitation|词性:名词|


euthanasias:/ˌjuːθəˈneɪʒəz/ /ˌjuːθəˈneɪʒiəz/
|n.|安乐死（复数）|Plural form of euthanasia, referring to multiple instances of the practice. |The euthanasias performed at the clinic were controversial.  这家诊所实施的安乐死备受争议。|
|近义词：mercy killings|反义词: resuscitations|词性:名词|


词源: From Greek, eu- (good) + thanatos (death).
