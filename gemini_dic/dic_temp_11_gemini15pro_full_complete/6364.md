amphiblastic:/æm.fɪˈblæs.tɪk/
adjective
两胚层的，兼有植物极和动物极的卵裂方式的
Relating to or denoting a type of egg cleavage in which there is complete division of the egg in the vegetal hemisphere and incomplete division in the animal hemisphere.
Amphiblastic cleavage is characteristic of amphibians. 两胚层卵裂是两栖动物的特征。
Near Synonyms: holoblastic, meroblastic (depending on the specific context, these can be considered somewhat similar in relating to cleavage types, though amphiblastic is a distinct category)
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, as it's a specific type of cleavage)
词性: adjective
