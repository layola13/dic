cornerman:/ˈkɔːnəmæn/ (US), /ˈkɔːnəmæn/ (UK)| n. |拳击教练，场边助理|A person who assists a boxer between rounds.  Someone who supports or advises another, especially in a difficult situation. |例句：The cornerman gave his fighter some water and advice between rounds. 拳击教练在回合间给他的拳击手一些水和建议。

例句：He was a loyal cornerman, always there to offer support and guidance. 他是一个忠诚的场边助理，总是在那里提供支持和指导。|近义词：second, assistant, supporter, advisor|反义词: opponent, adversary|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "corner" + "man".  Refers to the corner of the boxing ring where the assistant attends to the boxer.
