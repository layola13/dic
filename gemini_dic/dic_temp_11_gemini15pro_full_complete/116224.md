masoola: /məˈsuːlə/ | n. |彩礼；嫁妆（尤指在印度次大陆）| A payment made by the bride's family to the groom's family as part of the marriage customs, especially in the Indian subcontinent. |  Example 1: The masoola was a substantial sum, reflecting the bride's family's wealth. 彩礼数额巨大，反映了新娘家的财富。

Example 2:  Negotiations over the masoola continued for weeks before the wedding. 婚礼前几周，双方一直在就彩礼问题进行协商。| 近义词: dowry, bridal price | 反义词:  N/A (no direct opposite) | 词性: noun


Etymology: From Urdu and Hindi मसूला (masūlā), meaning 'collected, levied,' ultimately from Arabic مسؤل  (mas'ūl), meaning 'responsible.'
