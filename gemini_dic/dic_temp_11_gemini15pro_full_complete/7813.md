antagonising:/ænˈtæɡənaɪzɪŋ/
verb
对抗；引起…的敌意；激怒
acting in a way that makes someone angry or hostile
His comments were clearly antagonising her. 他的评论显然激怒了她。
irritating, provoking, alienating
conciliating, appeasing, placating
v.
