etchings: /ˈetʃɪŋz/
|n.|蚀刻版画；蚀刻术|A print made from an etched plate. The process of creating designs by etching. |The museum had a beautiful collection of Rembrandt's etchings.  博物馆收藏了伦勃朗的精美蚀刻版画。|Synonyms: engravings, prints, inscriptions|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "smooth surfaces," "paintings" in a contrasting sense)|noun
