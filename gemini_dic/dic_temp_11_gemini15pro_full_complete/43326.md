cramming:/ˈkræmɪŋ/| n.| 填鸭式学习，临时抱佛脚 |The act of trying to learn a lot of information quickly, especially before an exam.  |He's been cramming for his finals all week. 他整个星期都在为期末考试临时抱佛脚。|Synonyms: swotting, grinding, memorizing |Antonyms:  reviewing steadily,  learning gradually |noun
