dyspepsy:/dɪsˈpɛpsiə/ | n. | 消化不良，胃病 | Indigestion or upset stomach. |  I ate too much and now I have dyspepsy. 我吃太多了，现在消化不良。

She suffers from dyspepsy after eating rich foods.  她吃完油腻的食物后就会消化不良。 | 近义词：indigestion, upset stomach | 反义词: eupepsia (good digestion - 很少使用) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dyspepsia, from Ancient Greek δυσπεψία (duspepsía, “indigestion”), from δυσ- (dus-, “bad, difficult”) + πέψις (pépsis, “digestion”).
