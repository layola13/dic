immutable:/ɪˈmjuːtəbl/| adj.|不可改变的，不变的|Not capable of or susceptible to change.|例句：The laws of physics are immutable. 物理定律是不可改变的。|例句：His decision was immutable. 他的决定是不可改变的。|近义词：unchangeable, fixed, permanent, constant|反义词：mutable, changeable, variable|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *immutabilis, from Latin in- (“not”) + mutabilis (“changeable”), from mutare (“to change”). Equivalent to im- + mutable.
