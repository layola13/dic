amniote:/ˈæmniˌoʊt/| n. adj.|羊膜动物|An animal whose embryo develops inside an amnion.  A vertebrate animal of a group that includes mammals, birds, and reptiles.|例句：The amniote egg allowed reptiles to colonize dry land. 羊膜卵使得爬行动物能够在陆地上栖息。

例句：Birds, reptiles, and mammals are all amniotes. 鸟类、爬行动物和哺乳动物都是羊膜动物。|近义词：None (This is a specific biological classification)|反义词:anamniote|词性: noun, adjective
