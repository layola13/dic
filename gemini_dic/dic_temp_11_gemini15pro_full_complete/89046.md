holocrystalline:/ˌhɒləʊˈkrɪstəlɪn/| adj.|全晶质的|Composed entirely of crystals; having a completely crystalline structure.  It refers to rocks that are made up entirely of interlocking crystals, with no glassy or amorphous material.|The granite sample was determined to be holocrystalline.  这块花岗岩样品被确定为全晶质的。|The geologist noted the holocrystalline texture of the igneous rock. 地质学家注意到火成岩的全晶质结构。|crystalline, phaneritic|glassy, amorphous|adjective
