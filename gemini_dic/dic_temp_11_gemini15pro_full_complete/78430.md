get-through:/ɡet θruː/| phrasal verb | 完成；度过（难关）；接通电话；使理解 | to succeed in finishing a difficult or unpleasant task or experience; to manage to contact someone by telephone; to succeed in making someone understand something | I finally managed to get through all my work. 我终于完成了所有的工作。| synonyms: complete, finish, survive, endure, contact, reach, communicate | antonyms: fail, abandon, give up | verb

get-through:/ɡet θruː/| phrasal verb | 通过；批准 | to cause (something, such as a bill) to be accepted or approved | The bill got through both houses of Congress. 这项法案在国会两院都获得了通过。 | synonyms: pass, approve, ratify | antonyms: reject, defeat | verb
