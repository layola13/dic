calve:/kɑːv/ /kælv/| v. |产犊；(冰川、冰山)崩解|To give birth to a calf (of a cow, moose, etc.); (of a glacier or iceberg) to break away and shed a mass of ice.|The cow calved this morning. 这头母牛今天早上产犊了。|The glacier calved a massive iceberg. 冰川崩解出一座巨大的冰山。|近义词：give birth (for animals), break off (for glaciers)|反义词: N/A|词性:动词

calve:/kɑːv/ /kælv/| n. |(鲸鱼、海豹等的)幼崽|A young whale, seal, etc.|The mother whale stayed close to her calve. 母鲸鱼紧紧地靠近它的幼崽。|近义词：calf, young, offspring|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
