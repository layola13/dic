antagonistic:/ænˌtæɡəˈnɪstɪk/
adjective
对抗性的，敌对的
Showing or feeling active opposition or hostility toward someone or something.
He took an antagonistic stance against the new policy.  他采取了反对新政策的敌对立场。

The two groups have had an antagonistic relationship for decades. 这两个团体几十年来一直关系敌对。

近义词: hostile, opposed, adversarial, inimical
反义词: friendly, supportive, cooperative, amicable
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *antagonisticus, from Ancient Greek ἀνταγωνιστής (antagōnistḗs, “rival, competitor, opponent”), from ἀντί (antí, “against”) + ἀγωνίζομαι (agōnízomai, “I contend for a prize”).
