knickerbocker:/ˈnɪkərˌbɑːkər/| n. |灯笼裤；纽约人（尤指老式荷兰裔）|Loose-fitting breeches gathered at or below the knee. Also, a descendant of the early Dutch settlers of New York.  A term sometimes used to refer to New Yorkers in general.|He wore a pair of old-fashioned knickerbockers. 他穿了一条老式的灯笼裤。|

The Knickerbocker Club is a prestigious social club in New York City.  纽约尼克博克俱乐部是一家享有盛誉的社交俱乐部。|synonyms: breeches, plus fours, New Yorker|antonyms: (No direct antonym for the clothing; for the person, perhaps "outsider," "non-resident")|noun
