adiabatic:/ˌeɪdiəˈbætɪk/ /ˌædɪəˈbætɪk/ | adj. | 绝热的 | Preventing heat from entering or leaving.  Relating to or denoting a process or condition in which no heat enters or leaves the system concerned. | The process is adiabatic, meaning no heat exchange occurs. 这个过程是绝热的，意味着没有热交换发生。 |  A thermos flask is designed to create an adiabatic environment for its contents.  保温瓶的设计是为了为其内部创造一个绝热环境。 | 近义词：insulated, non-conducting | 反义词：diathermic, conducting | 词性: 形容词
adiathermic:/ˌeɪdaɪəˈθɜːrmɪk/ /ˌædɪəˈθɜːmɪk/ | adj. | 不透热的 | Impervious to radiant heat; not allowing heat to pass through. |  Adiathermic materials are used in applications where heat transfer needs to be minimized. 不透热材料用于需要最小化热传递的应用。 | The walls of the chamber were adiathermic, preventing external temperature fluctuations from affecting the experiment. 房间的墙壁是不透热的，防止外部温度波动影响实验。 | 近义词：heat-resistant, insulating | 反义词：diathermic, heat-conducting | 词性: 形容词


词源:
From Greek *a-* (not) + *dia-* (through) + *thermē* (heat).
