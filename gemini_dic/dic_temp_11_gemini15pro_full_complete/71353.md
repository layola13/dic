flacked:/flækt/
verb
遭到批评；被抨击；被指责
subjected to criticism, especially from the media.
The movie was flacked by critics for its poor plot and acting. 这部电影因其糟糕的情节和演技而受到评论家的抨击。

The politician was flacked for his controversial comments.  这位政客因其有争议的言论而受到抨击。


criticized, attacked, slammed, panned, denounced
praised, lauded, acclaimed
