anachoretical:/ˌænəkəˈrɛtɪkəl/
adjective
隐居的，遁世的，远离尘世的
characteristic of an anchorite or hermit; solitary and withdrawn from the world.
例句：He lived an anachoretical existence in the remote mountains. 他在偏远的山区过着隐居的生活。

例句：Her anachoretical lifestyle was a stark contrast to her previous life in the city. 她隐居的生活方式与她以前在城市里的生活形成鲜明对比。
近义词：hermitic, reclusive, ascetic, secluded, solitary
反义词：gregarious, sociable, outgoing
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *anachorēticus, from Greek anakhōrētēs (“hermit, recluse”), from anakhōreō (“I retire, withdraw”), from ana- (“back, up”) + khōreō (“I give way, withdraw”).
