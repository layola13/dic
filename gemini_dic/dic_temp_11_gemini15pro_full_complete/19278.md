bias:/ˈbaɪəs/| n. vt.|偏见，偏爱；斜线|A particular tendency, trend, inclination, feeling, or opinion, especially one that is preconceived or unreasoned.|The judge was accused of bias in favor of the defendant.|Synonyms: prejudice, partiality, inclination, predisposition|Antonyms: impartiality, fairness, objectivity|noun
bias:/ˈbaɪəs/| vt.|使有偏见|To influence in an unfair way.|The media's coverage of the event was biased against the protesters.|Synonyms: prejudice, influence, sway|Antonyms: be objective|verb
