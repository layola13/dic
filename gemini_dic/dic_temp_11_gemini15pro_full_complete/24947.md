brush-like:/ˈbrʌʃˌlaɪk/| adj.|像刷子一样的，刷状的|Resembling a brush, especially in shape or texture.|The plant had brush-like flowers. 这种植物的花像刷子一样。|近义词：bristly, brushy|反义词:smooth, sleek|词性:形容词
词源:  From 'brush' + '-like'.  'Brush' comes from Old French 'broisse', ultimately from Vulgar Latin 'bruscia', of unknown origin.  '-like' is a common suffix meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of."
