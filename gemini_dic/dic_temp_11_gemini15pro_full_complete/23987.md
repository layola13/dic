breathe:/briːð/
verb
呼吸
to take air into and out of your lungs
例句：I can't breathe. 我无法呼吸。

例句：He breathed deeply before diving into the water. 他深吸一口气，然后跳入水中。
近义词：inhale, respire
反义词:exhale, suffocate
词性:v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *brēðan (attested only in derived forms such as brēð "breath," ābrēðan "to take away one's breath"), from Proto-Germanic *brēþaną (source also of Old Saxon brēthan, Old Frisian bretha "to smell, breathe," Old High German brādam, German braten "to roast, broil," Old Norse bræða "to smell, exhale"), from Proto-Indo-European *gʷʰes- "to blow, breathe."
