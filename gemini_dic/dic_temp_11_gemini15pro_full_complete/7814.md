antagonism: /ænˈtæɡənɪzəm/ | n. |敌意，对抗；对立，敌对|Active opposition or hostility.|There's a long history of antagonism between the two families. 这两个家族之间长期存在敌对关系。|Synonyms: hostility, animosity, friction, antipathy, enmity|Antonyms: friendship, amity, goodwill, harmony|noun

antagonism: /ænˈtæɡənɪzəm/ | n. |对抗（作用）|opposition in physiological action; counteraction.|The antagonism between the two drugs can cause serious side effects.  这两种药物之间的对抗作用会导致严重的副作用。|Synonyms: counteraction, opposition, neutralization|Antonyms: synergism, cooperation|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *antagonisma, from Ancient Greek ἀνταγωνισμός (antagonismós, “rivalry”), from ἀνταγωνίζομαι (antagōnízomai, “I struggle against”), from ἀντί (antí, “against”) + ἀγωνίζομαι (agōnízomai, “I contend for a prize”).
