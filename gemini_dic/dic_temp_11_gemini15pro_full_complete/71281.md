fixed:/fɪkst/| adj.|固定的，不变的；修理好的；准备好的；确定的；(价格)固定的；(比赛等)操纵的；(目光)凝视的；(主意)坚定的|A fixed price is one that cannot be changed.  固定价格是不能更改的。|例句：The meeting is fixed for 9 am tomorrow. 会议定于明天上午9点举行。

例句：He gave me a fixed stare. 他目不转睛地盯着我。|近义词：stable, permanent, repaired, set, determined, established, intent|反义词：broken, variable, flexible, uncertain, changeable|词性:形容词


fixed:/fɪkst/| v.|修理；固定；安装；确定；操纵；凝视；(使)稳定下来；准备(饭菜)；(用化学方法)使(颜色)固定；|I got the mechanic to fix my car. 我让机修工修理我的汽车。|例句：They fixed the date for the wedding. 他们确定了婚礼的日期。

例句：She fixed her gaze on the horizon. 她凝视着地平线。|近义词：repair, mend, fasten, secure, settle, arrange, rig|反义词：break, damage, loosen, detach|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fixen*, from Old French *fixer*, from Latin *fīxāre*, present active infinitive of *fīgō* (“to fix, fasten, attach”), from Proto-Italic *figō*, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰeyǵʰ- (“to stick, attach”).
