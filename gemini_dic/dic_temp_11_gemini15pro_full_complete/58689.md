dumper's:/ˈdʌmpərz/
noun
倾倒者(的),丢弃者(的)
belonging to or associated with someone who dumps something.  The possessive form of "dumper."  Refers to something belonging to, or originating from, a person or thing that dumps.
The dumper's remorse was short-lived. 倾倒者的懊悔是短暂的。
近义词:disposer's, jettisoner's
反义词:keeper's, hoarder's
词性:n. (noun, 名词)
