dissolvingly:/dɪˈzɒlvɪŋli/
adv. |消融地，溶解地|In a manner that suggests dissolving or fading away.  This describes something disappearing gradually, as if it were dissolving into something else.|The scene dissolvedly shifted from the bustling city to a serene countryside.  场景从繁华的都市消融般地转换到宁静的乡村。|The music faded dissolvingly into silence.  音乐渐渐消融在寂静之中。|Synonym: fadingly, gradually, imperceptibly|Antonym: abruptly, suddenly|词性:副词
