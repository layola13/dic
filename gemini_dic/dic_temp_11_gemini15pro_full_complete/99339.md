interdebated:/ˌɪntər dɪˈbeɪtɪd/
adjective
互相争论的，内部辩论的
Having been debated or discussed among a group of people, especially internally within an organization or community.
The issue was interdebated among the members of the committee for weeks before a decision was reached.  委员会成员就这个问题进行了数周的内部辩论，才最终做出决定。

The interdebated topic caused a rift in the community.  这个内部辩论的话题在社区造成了裂痕。
近义词: discussed, debated, argued, contested
反义词: agreed upon, undisputed, uncontroversial
词性: adjective
