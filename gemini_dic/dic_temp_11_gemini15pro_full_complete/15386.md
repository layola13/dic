backwardness:/ˈbækwərdnəs/ /ˈbækərdnəs/| n. |落后，迟钝，愚昧|The state of being backward or underdeveloped; lack of progress or development.|例句：The country's economic backwardness is a major concern.  这个国家的经济落后是一个主要问题。

例句：His social backwardness made it difficult for him to make friends. 他的社交迟钝让他难以交友。|近义词：underdevelopment, retardation, primitiveness|反义词：progress, advancement, sophistication|词性:名词
