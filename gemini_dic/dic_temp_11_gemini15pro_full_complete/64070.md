epitrochoid:/ˈɛpɪˌtroʊkɔɪd/ | n. | 外旋轮线；外摆线 | A curve traced by a point attached to a circle rolling around the outside of a fixed circle. | The spirograph toy creates epitrochoids.  这台万花尺玩具可以画出外旋轮线。|  The planet's orbit is an epitrochoid. 这颗行星的轨道是一条外旋轮线。 | synonym: roulette, cycloid | antonym: hypotrochoid | noun
