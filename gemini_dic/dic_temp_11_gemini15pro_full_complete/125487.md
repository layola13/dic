multipropellant:/ˌmʌltiprəˈpɛlənt/ | adj. | 多推进剂的 | Using or involving more than one propellant. |  The multipropellant rocket was more efficient than its single-propellant counterpart. 多推进剂火箭比单推进剂火箭更高效。| Synonyms: mixed-propellant | Antonyms: single-propellant, monopropellant | adjective

Etymology:
"multi-" (prefix meaning "many, multiple") + "propellant" (noun meaning "a substance that propels something")
