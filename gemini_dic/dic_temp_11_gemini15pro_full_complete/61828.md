empiricists:/ɪmˈpɪrɪsɪsts/
|n.|经验主义者|People who believe that knowledge comes primarily from sensory experience and observation, rather than from innate ideas or intuition.|Empiricists argue that all knowledge is ultimately derived from experience. 经验主义者认为所有知识最终都来源于经验。|
|
