after-the-fact:/ˌæftər ðə ˈfækt/
adjective
事后的；既成事实后的
happening or done after an event, and usually as a reaction to it
例句：After-the-fact analysis is often less effective than proactive measures. 事后分析通常不如主动措施有效。

例句：It's easy to criticize after the fact. 事后批评很容易。
近义词：retrospective, ex post facto, post hoc
反义词：prior, beforehand, anticipatory
词性：adjective
