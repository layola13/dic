fennessy:/ˈfɛnɪsi/| n. |芬尼西, 芬尼西环尾狐猴|A surname of Irish origin, also the name of a species of lemur, the Fennessy's sifaka (Propithecus verreauxi coquereli), named after the primatologist Kenneth Fennessy.  It can also less commonly refer to anyone bearing the surname. |例句：The Fennessy's sifaka is a critically endangered lemur found in Madagascar. 芬尼西环尾狐猴是一种极度濒危的狐猴，发现于马达加斯加。

例句：Dr. Fennessy dedicated his life to the study and conservation of primates. 芬尼西博士毕生致力于灵长类动物的研究和保护。|近义词：(For the lemur) sifaka, Coquerel's sifaka |反义词: N/A |词性:Noun
