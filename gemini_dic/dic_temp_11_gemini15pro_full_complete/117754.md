melanterite:/məˈlæntəˌraɪt/
noun
水绿矾
A hydrated iron sulfate mineral, FeSO4·7H2O, occurring in green to white masses.  It is a secondary mineral formed by the weathering of pyrite or marcasite.
Melanterite is often found in mine workings. 水绿矾常在矿井中发现。
Synonyms:copperas, green vitriol
Antonyms: N/A
词性: noun
