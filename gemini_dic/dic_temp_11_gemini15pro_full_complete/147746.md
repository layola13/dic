overproduction:/ˌoʊvərprəˈdʌkʃən/ | n. | 过度生产；生产过剩 | The act of producing more of a product, commodity, or substance than is needed or can be sold. |  The overproduction of oil led to a drop in prices. 石油生产过剩导致价格下跌。| oversupply, excess production | underproduction, shortage | noun
