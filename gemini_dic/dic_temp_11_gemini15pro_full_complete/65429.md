euphoria:/juːˈfɔːriə/
noun
欣快症；狂喜；异常兴奋
A feeling or state of intense excitement and happiness.
例句：She was in a state of euphoria after winning the lottery. 她中了彩票后欣喜若狂。

例句：The drug induced a state of euphoria. 这种药物引起了一种欣快感。

近义词：elation, joy, ecstasy, bliss, rapture
反义词：despair, misery, depression, sadness
词性:n.


euphoric:/juːˈfɒrɪk/
adjective
欣快的；狂喜的；异常兴奋的
Characterized by or feeling intense excitement and happiness.
例句：I felt euphoric after finishing the marathon. 跑完马拉松后我感到欣喜若狂。

例句：The team was euphoric after their victory. 团队在胜利后欣喜若狂。

近义词：elated, joyful, ecstatic, blissful, rapturous
反义词：despondent, miserable, depressed, sad
词性:adj.
