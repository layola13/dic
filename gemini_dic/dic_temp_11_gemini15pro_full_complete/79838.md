glyphograph:/ˈɡlɪfəɡræf/
n.
雕版印刷；雕版；电刻版画
A printing plate or print made by an electrotype process, the design being produced in intaglio.
The glyphograph was an early form of photomechanical printing. 这是一种早期的照相制版印刷术。

glyphograph:/ˈɡlɪfəɡræf/
n.
雕版印刷；雕版；电刻版画
A design engraved or etched, especially one made for printing.
This rare glyphograph depicts a scene from ancient Greece.  这幅罕见的雕版画描绘了古希腊的一个场景。


Etymology:  From Greek *glyphē* (雕刻) + *-graphos* (书写,记录)

Synonym:  engraving, etching, intaglio, block print

Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "digital print" in a modern context)
