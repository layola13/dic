fancier's:/ˈfænsiərz/| n.|爱好者，鉴赏家(尤指动物)|A fancier is someone who has a special interest in a particular type of animal, especially one that they breed or show.|例句：He is a pigeon fancier. 他是一位鸽子爱好者。|例句：The dog fancier's prized poodle won first place. 那位爱犬者的贵宾犬获得了第一名。|近义词：enthusiast, connoisseur, aficionado|反义词:critic, detractor|词性:名词

词源:  From "fancy" (v.) + "-er" (agent suffix) + "-'s" (possessive).  "Fancy" itself comes from Old French "fantaisie."
