词源:
From Middle English *blunt, blont, blount, from Old Norse blundra (“to doze, slumber”), from Proto-Germanic *blundaną (“to close one's eyes”). Cognate with Icelandic blunda (“to shut the eyes”), Faroese blunda (“to blink, doze”), Norwegian blunda, Danish blunde (“to blink, doze”). Related to blend.  More at blend. (Wiktionary)
