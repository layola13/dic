orgiastic:/ɔːrˈdʒiːæstɪk/
adjective
狂欢的；放荡的
Characterized by or resembling an orgy; wild, unrestrained revelry.  Marked by excessive or uncontrolled indulgence, especially in emotional expression.
The festival culminated in an orgiastic celebration of music and dance.  节日在音乐和舞蹈的狂欢庆祝活动中达到高潮。
The band's performance was an orgiastic display of raw energy. 乐队的表演是原始能量的狂欢展示。
近义词: frenzied, wild, unrestrained, bacchanalian, debauched
反义词: restrained, controlled, subdued, inhibited
词性: adjective
词源: From Greek *orgia*, plural of *orgon* ("secret rite, sacred action").
