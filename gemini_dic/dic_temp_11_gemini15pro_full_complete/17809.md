bedrug:/bɪˈdrʌɡ/
n.
床毯，地毯
A thick rug laid beside a bed.
例句：She stepped out of bed onto the soft bedrug. 她下床踩在柔软的床边毯上。

例句：The cat curled up on the bedrug for a nap. 猫蜷缩在床边毯上打盹。

近义词：rug, carpet, mat
反义词：(No direct antonym, potentially bare floor)
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bed + rug.  "Bed" originating from Old English "bedd" and "rug" from  probably Scandinavian origin, possibly from Old Norse "rogg" (shaggy tuft).
