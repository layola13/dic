It is the county seat of Fresno County.|例句：Fresno is the fifth most populous city in California. 弗雷斯诺是加州人口第五多的城市。|例句：Fresno is known as the Raisin Capital of the World. 弗雷斯诺被称为世界葡萄干之都。|近义词：city, municipality|反义词:countryside, rural area|词性:noun
