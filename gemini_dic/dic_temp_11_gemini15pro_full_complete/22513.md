boomerang:/ˈbuːməræŋ/ /ˈbuːməræŋ/ | n. | 回飞棒；自食其果的行为；意想不到的恶果|A curved wooden missile that can be thrown so as to return to the thrower.  Also, something that returns with harmful consequences to the originator. | He threw the boomerang and it came back to him.  他扔出回飞棒，它又飞回来了。| synonyms: comeuppance, backlash, recoil | antonyms: gift, blessing | noun
