menstrue: /ˈmɛnstruː/ (US), /ˈmɛnstruː/ (UK)| v. |月经来潮；行经|To have a menstrual period.  The biological process of discharging blood and other material from the lining of the uterus at intervals of about one lunar month from puberty until menopause, except during pregnancy.| She began to menstruate at age 13. 她13岁开始月经来潮。| Synonyms:  have one's period, bleed, flow | Antonyms:  N/A (A physiological process doesn't have a direct antonym)| Verb
