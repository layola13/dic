apses:/ˈæpsiːz/ (US), /ˈæpsiːz/ (UK)| n.|后殿（教堂半圆形突出部分）|A recess, usually semicircular, in the wall of a building, typically at the eastern end of a church. |The church's apses were beautifully decorated with mosaics.|近义词:alcove, niche, recess|反义词:nave (教堂的正厅)|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "apsis," from Greek "hapsis" (arch, loop, vault).  Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European root "*ap-" (to seize, reach).
