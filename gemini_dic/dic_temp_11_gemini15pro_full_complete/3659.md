agglomerate:/əˈɡlɒməreɪt/ (UK) /əˈɡlɑːməreɪt/ (US)| n. v. adj.|聚集体，凝聚；使聚集，使凝聚；聚集的，凝聚的|A mass or collection of things that have gathered together, or the process of gathering into a mass.|The snowflakes agglomerated into large balls of slush. 雪花聚集成大雪球。|Synonyms: accumulate, amass, congregate, cluster|Antonyms: disperse, separate, scatter|n.

agglomerate:/əˈɡlɒməreɪt/ (UK) /əˈɡlɑːməreɪt/ (US)| v. |聚集，凝聚|To gather into a mass or ball.|Dust particles agglomerate to form larger particles. 灰尘颗粒聚集形成更大的颗粒。|Synonyms: coalesce, consolidate, merge|Antonyms: disperse, separate, scatter|v.

agglomerate:/əˈɡlɒməreɪt/ (UK) /əˈɡlɑːməreɪt/ (US)| adj.|聚集的，凝聚的|Gathered together into a mass.|Agglomerate rock is formed from fragments of volcanic material. 集块岩是由火山物质碎片形成的。|Synonyms: clustered, compacted, concentrated|Antonyms: dispersed, scattered, separate|adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin agglomeratus, past participle of agglomerare "to wind into a ball," from ad- "to" + glomus "ball."
