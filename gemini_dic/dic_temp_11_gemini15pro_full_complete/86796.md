hemaphaein:/ˌhiːməˈfeɪɪn/| n. |嗜血; 食血|The act of consuming blood.  It derives from the Greek words "haima" (blood) and "phagein" (to eat).|Bats exhibit hemaphaein.  蝙蝠表现出嗜血行为。|近义词：hematophagy, blood-feeding|反义词: None (lack of a direct antonym)|noun
