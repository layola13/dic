freethinker: /ˈfriːˌθɪŋkər/ | n. | 自由思想家 | A person who forms their own opinions and beliefs, especially on religious or political matters, rather than accepting those of other people or of tradition. |  Galileo was condemned as a heretic for his freethinking ideas. 伽利略因其自由思想的观点而被谴责为异端。

She considers herself a freethinker and questions everything she's told. 她认为自己是一个自由思想家，并质疑她被告知的一切。 | 近义词：nonconformist, independent thinker, heretic, iconoclast, skeptic, doubter | 反义词：conformist, traditionalist, dogmatist | 词性: noun
