multisonorousness:/ˌmʌltiˈsɒnərəsnəs/ |/ˌmʌltiˈsɑːnərəsnəs/| n. | 多音性；响亮洪亮 |The quality of having many sounds, especially deep, resonant sounds.  It suggests a richness and complexity of sound. | The multisonorousness of the organ filled the cathedral.  教堂里管风琴的多音性回荡不已。 | 近义词：resonance, sonority, richness | 反义词：dullness, flatness | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From multi- (many) + sonorous (producing a deep or full sound) + -ness (forming nouns denoting a state or quality).  "Sonorous" ultimately comes from Latin "sonorus" (sounding).
