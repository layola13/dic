combustibility:/kəmˌbʌstəˈbɪləti/| n. |可燃性，燃烧性|The quality of being combustible; the capacity for catching fire and burning.|The combustibility of dry leaves makes them a fire hazard. 干树叶的可燃性使它们成为火灾隐患。|近义词：flammability, ignitability|反义词：incombustibility, nonflammability|词性:名词
Etymology: From Late Latin *combustibilitas, from Latin combustus (past participle of comburere "to burn up"), from com- "together" + burere "to burn".
