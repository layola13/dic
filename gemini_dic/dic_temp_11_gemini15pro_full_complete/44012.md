discriminatory:/dɪˈskrɪmɪnətɔːri/| adj.|歧视性的，差别待遇的|Treating a person or group differently based on prejudice, usually involving unfair treatment.|例句：The company has been accused of discriminatory hiring practices.  该公司被指控 hiring practices 歧视性的招聘做法。|例句：Discriminatory laws against minorities are unjust and harmful. 针对少数族裔的歧视性法律是不公正且有害的。|近义词：prejudiced, biased, unfair, unjust|反义词：fair, just, impartial, equitable|词性:形容词

词源: Late 16th century (in the sense ‘making or constituting a distinction’): from late Latin discriminātōrius, from discriminare (see discriminate). The current sense dates from the mid 19th century.
