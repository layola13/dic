overleather:/ˈoʊvərˌleðər/ | n. | 套鞋，鞋罩 | An outer covering for a shoe, often made of leather or rubber, worn for protection against wet or muddy conditions. |  He pulled his overleathers over his shoes before venturing out into the snow. 他在冒险出去到雪地之前把套鞋套在了鞋子上。| Synonyms:  overshoes, galoshes, rubbers | Antonyms: None | Noun
