centrifugate: /ˌsɛnˈtrɪfjuːɡeɪt/ | n. v. | 离心分离物，离心分离 |  (n.) The material separated from a solution by means of a centrifuge. (v.) To rotate something at high speed in order to separate substances of different densities, like separating blood cells from plasma. |  (n.) The centrifugate was then analyzed for protein content. 离心分离物随后被分析蛋白质含量。 (v.)  We will centrifugate the sample to isolate the DNA. 我们将离心样品以分离DNA。 | 近义词：(n.) sediment, precipitate, deposit (v.) spin down, separate | 反义词: (v.) mix, combine | 词性: noun, verb

centrifuge: /ˈsɛntrɪfjuːdʒ/ | n. v. | 离心机，离心分离 | (n.) A machine with a rapidly rotating container that applies centrifugal force to its contents, typically to separate fluids of different densities or liquids from solids. (v.) To subject to the action of a centrifuge. | (n.) The lab technician placed the blood samples in the centrifuge. 实验室技术员将血液样本放入离心机中。 (v.)  They centrifuged the mixture to separate the components.  他们对混合物进行离心以分离各成分。| 近义词: (n.) separator, spinner (v.) spin, whirl | 反义词: None | 词性: noun, verb
