disconducive:/ˌdɪskənˈdjuːsɪv/
adj.
不利的，不 conducive 的，妨碍的
Not conducive; tending to hinder or obstruct.
A noisy environment is disconducive to study. 吵闹的环境不利于学习。
近义词:unfavorable, adverse, detrimental, unhelpful, counterproductive
反义词:conducive, helpful, beneficial, advantageous, favorable
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From dis- (expressing negation) + conducive (from Latin conducere, "to lead together").
