chiliast: /ˈkɪliæst/ | n. | 千年至福论者；相信千年至福说的人 | A person who believes in chiliasm, the doctrine of the millennium. |  Many early Christians were chiliasts, believing Christ would return to reign on Earth for a thousand years. 许多早期的基督徒都是千年至福论者，相信基督会回到地球统治一千年。 |  The chiliast eagerly awaited the dawn of the new millennium.  这位千年至福论者热切地等待着新千年的到来。 | synonyms: millennialist, premillennialist | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider "skeptic" or "non-believer" in a broader sense) | noun
