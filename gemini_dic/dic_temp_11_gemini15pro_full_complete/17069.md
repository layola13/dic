bathysphere:/ˈbæθɪsfɪr/ | n. |深海球，潜水球 |A manned spherical chamber for deep-sea observation. |例句：The bathysphere descended into the abyss. 深海球下降到深渊。

例句：William Beebe used a bathysphere to explore the deep ocean. 威廉·毕比使用深海球探索深海。|近义词：submersible, diving bell |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
Etymology:  From Greek *báthos* (depth) + *sphaîra* (sphere, ball).
