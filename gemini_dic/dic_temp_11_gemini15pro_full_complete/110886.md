lions:/ˈlaɪənz/| n.|狮子|Plural form of lion, referring to more than one lion.  A large, powerfully built cat-like animal, native to Africa and India, having a tawny coat, a tufted tail, and, in the male, a heavy mane around the head and neck.|例句：The lions roared loudly in the savanna. 狮子在热带草原上大声咆哮。|例句：We saw a pride of lions resting under a tree. 我们看到一群狮子在树下休息。|近义词：big cats, felines|反义词:|词性:noun
