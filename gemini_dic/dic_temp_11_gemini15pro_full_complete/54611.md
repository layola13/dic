dismayfully:/dɪsˈmeɪfəli/
adverb
沮丧地，灰心地
In a manner that shows or expresses dismay.
She looked at the broken vase dismayfully. 她沮丧地看着破碎的花瓶。
He shook his head dismayfully at the news. 他听到这个消息沮丧地摇了摇头。

近义词: dejectedly, despondently, glumly, sadly
反义词: cheerfully, happily, joyfully

词性: adverb
词源: From dismay + -fully. Dismay comes from the Old French *desmayer, from des- (expressing reversal) + mayer (“to be able to”).
