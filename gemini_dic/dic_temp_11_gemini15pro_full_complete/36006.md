codicil: /ˈkɒdɪsɪl/ (US) /ˈkəʊdɪsɪl/ (UK)| n. |遗嘱附录，补遗|A supplement or addition to a will, especially one that explains, modifies, or revokes a previous will.|Example: He added a codicil to his will, leaving his collection of rare stamps to his nephew.|近义词:amendment, appendix, supplement|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly original will)|noun
