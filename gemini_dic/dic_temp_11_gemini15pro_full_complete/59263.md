eacher: (This word is likely a typo for "teacher") /ˈtiːtʃər/
teacher: n. |教师，老师|A person who teaches, especially in a school. |例句：My favorite teacher is Mrs. Smith. 我最喜欢的老师是史密斯夫人。|例句：He is a dedicated teacher who inspires his students. 他是一位尽职尽责的老师，激励着他的学生。|近义词：instructor, educator, tutor|反义词：student, pupil|词性: noun
Etymology: From Middle English techere, from Old English tēahtere, agent noun from tēahhian (“to teach, show, point out”), from Proto-Germanic *taikijaną (“to show, teach”), from Proto-Indo-European *deḱ- (“to show, teach”).
