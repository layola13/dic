damara:/dəˈmɑːrə/ |n.|达马拉人；达马拉语；达马拉牛|A member of a people living mainly in Namibia. Also: the Bantu language of this people. Also: a breed of reddish-brown domestic cattle.|The Damara people have a rich cultural heritage. 达马拉人拥有丰富的文化遗产。|The Damara language is related to other Bantu languages. 达马拉语与其他班图语系语言有关。|synonyms:  (for the people) Herero (related group),  (for the language) OtjiHerero (related language) |antonyms: None (for people or language).  There is no direct opposite for an ethnic group or language. |noun

damara:/dəˈmɑːrə/ |n.|达马拉牛|A breed of reddish-brown domestic cattle, originally from Namibia.|Damara cattle are known for their hardiness and adaptability. 达马拉牛以其耐寒性和适应性而闻名。|The Damara cattle are well-suited to arid climates. 达马拉牛非常适合干旱气候。|synonyms: Nguni (related breed) |antonyms: None (for cattle breed). There is no direct opposite for a cattle breed. |noun
