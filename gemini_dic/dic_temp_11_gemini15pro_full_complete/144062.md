outroars:/ˈaʊtrɔːrz/
n.
喧嚣，吵闹，强烈抗议
a loud and sustained expression of disapproval or protest, often from a large number of people
The outroars from the crowd forced the speaker to stop.  人群的喧嚣迫使演讲者停了下来。
clamor, uproar, outcry, commotion
silence, quiet
noun
