anticommercialism:/ˌænti kəˈmɜːrʃəlɪzəm/
n.|反商业主义|Opposition to commercialism or excessive emphasis on profit-making in social or cultural activities.|The artist's work was a statement of anticommercialism, rejecting the mass-produced art of the time. 这位艺术家的作品是对反商业主义的声明，拒绝了当时的大规模生产的艺术。|近义词:anticonsumerism, antimaterialism|反义词:commercialism, materialism|词性:名词

anticommercial:/ˌænti kəˈmɜːrʃəl/
adj.|反商业的|Opposed to or hostile towards commercial principles or practices.|His anticommercial stance made him unpopular with the advertising executives. 他的反商业立场使他不受广告主管的欢迎。|近义词:noncommercial|反义词:commercial, pro-business|词性:形容词
