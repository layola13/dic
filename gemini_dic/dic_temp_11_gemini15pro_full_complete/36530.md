collaborates:/kəˈlæbəreɪts/
verb
合作，协作
works jointly with others or together especially in an intellectual endeavor
例句：The two companies collaborated on a new project.  两家公司合作开展了一个新项目。

例句：She collaborates with other scientists on her research. 她与其他科学家合作进行研究。

近义词：cooperates, partners, works together, joins forces
反义词：competes, works alone

词性
v.
