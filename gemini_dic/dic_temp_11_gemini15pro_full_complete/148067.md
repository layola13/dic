oversatieties:/ˌoʊvərˈseɪʃieɪtiz/ /ˌəʊvəˈseɪʃɪeɪtɪz/
| v. | 使过度饱足，使生腻 | to satisfy (a desire or an appetite) to excess | He oversatieties himself with rich food. 他吃油腻的食物吃腻了。| 近义词：glut, gorge, surfeit | 反义词: deprive, starve | 词性:动词

oversatiety:/ˌoʊvərˈseɪʃɪəti/ /ˌəʊvəˈseɪʃɪəti/
| n. | 过度饱足，过饱 | the state of being oversatiated |  Oversatiety can lead to indigestion.  暴饮暴食会导致消化不良。| 近义词：satiation, repletion, fullness | 反义词: hunger, starvation | 词性:名词

词源:
From over- + satiety, from Late Latin satietatem (nominative satietas), from Latin satis "enough."  来自over- + satiety，源自晚期拉丁语satietatem（主格satietas），来自拉丁语satis“足够”。
