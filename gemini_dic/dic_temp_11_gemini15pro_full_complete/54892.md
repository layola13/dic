disponee:/ˌdɪspəˈniː/ | n. | 受让人，接受处置人 |  A person who receives something, especially property or a right, from another.  Someone who benefits from a disposition (a legal transfer of property or rights). | The disponee of the estate will inherit a considerable fortune.  这位遗产的受让人将继承一大笔财产。

The lawyer ensured the disponee understood their responsibilities. 律师确保受让人理解他们的责任。| 近义词：recipient, beneficiary, heir, legatee | 反义词: disponer, grantor, donor | noun
