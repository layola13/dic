anticruelty:/ˌæntiˈkruːəlti/
| adj.| 反虐待的，防止虐待的 | Opposing or designed to prevent cruelty, especially cruelty to animals. |  The anticruelty society works to protect animals from abuse.  反虐待动物协会致力于保护动物免受虐待。 | 近义词: humane, compassionate, kind | 反义词: cruel, inhumane, barbaric | 词性: 形容词
Etymology: anti- (against) + cruelty (the quality of being cruel). From the Latin "crudelis" meaning "hard-hearted, unfeeling, rough, harsh, severe."
