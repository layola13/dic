anti-royalist:/ˌæntiˈrɔɪəlɪst/
noun
反君主主义者；反对君主制的人
A person who opposes monarchy or royal rule.
The anti-royalist movement gained traction during the revolution.  反君主主义运动在革命期间获得了关注。
The writer was a known anti-royalist and often criticized the monarchy in his works.  这位作家是一位著名的反君主主义者，经常在他的作品中批评君主制。
近义词:republican, democrat
反义词:royalist, monarchist
词性:n.

anti-royalist:/ˌæntiˈrɔɪəlɪst/
adjective
反君主制的；反对君主制的
Opposed to monarchy or royal rule.
He held strong anti-royalist views. 他持有强烈的反君主制观点。
The anti-royalist sentiment was widespread throughout the country. 反君主制情绪在全国蔓延。
近义词:republican, democratic
反义词:royalist, monarchistic
词性:adj.
