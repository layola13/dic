frequents:/ˈfriːkwənts/ | v. | 常去，经常光顾 |  To visit a place or person often.  | She frequents the local library. 她经常去当地图书馆。 | He frequents that cafe. 他经常光顾那家咖啡馆。 | 近义词: visits often, patronizes, haunts | 反义词: avoids, shuns | 词性: 动词


词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French frequenter, from Latin frequentare ‘crowd, visit often’, from frequens, frequent- ‘crowded, numerous’.
