foundershaft:/ˈfaʊndərˌʃæft/
n.
创始人股份，创始人股权
The portion of a company's equity held by its founders.
Example: The foundershaft was divided equally among the three original partners. 创始人股份由三个最初的合伙人平分。

Example:  Negotiating the terms of the foundershaft was a crucial step in launching the startup.  协商创始人股份的条款是启动这家初创公司的关键一步。

Synonym: founder's equity, founder's stock
Antonym: N/A (No direct antonym.  One could consider "employee stock options" a contrasting concept, but not a true opposite.)
词性: 名词
