lipogram:/ˈlɪpəɡræm/| n.|离合诗，缺字诗|A composition from which the writer systematically omits a particular letter of the alphabet.|The author wrote a lipogram omitting the letter 'e'. 作者写了一首缺字母“e”的离合诗。|Synonyms:  constrained writing, word puzzle|Antonyms:  pangram (uses all letters)|noun
