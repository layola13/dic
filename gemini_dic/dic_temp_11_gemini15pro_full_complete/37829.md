compest:/ˈkɒmpɛst/ |n.|堆肥，混合肥料 |A mixture of various decaying organic substances, as dead leaves or manure, used for fertilizing soil.| The gardener enriched the soil with compest. 园丁用堆肥改良土壤。| 近义词: compost, fertilizer, manure | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

compest:/kəmˈpɛst/ |v. |施肥，用堆肥覆盖| To fertilize with or cover with compost.| He compested the garden every spring. 他每年春天都给花园施肥。| 近义词: fertilize, manure | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 动词

词源: From Middle English *compesten*, from Old French *composter* (to compose, arrange), from Latin *componere* (to put together).  The sense of "manure" likely developed from the idea of composing or mixing various organic materials.
