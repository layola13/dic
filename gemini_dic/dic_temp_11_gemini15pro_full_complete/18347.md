bello:/ˈbɛloʊ/ (US), /ˈbɛləʊ/ (UK)| adj.|美丽的，美好的；英俊的|A term of endearment or affectionate address, especially used for males.  It is derived from Italian and means "beautiful" or "handsome."|例句：He's such a bello boy! 他真是个漂亮的小伙子！|近义词：beautiful, handsome, gorgeous, lovely, attractive|反义词:ugly, unattractive, hideous|词性:形容词
