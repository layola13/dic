furlough:/ˈfɜːrloʊ/ (US) /ˈfɜːləʊ/ (UK)| n. v.|休假，暂时解雇|A period of time when an employee is laid off from work, typically without pay, but sometimes with reduced pay or no pay at all.  The employee is expected to return to work when the furlough period is over.|Many companies were forced to furlough employees during the pandemic. 许多公司在大流行期间被迫让员工休假。|The factory workers were furloughed for three months due to a shortage of materials. 由于材料短缺，工厂工人休假了三个月。|synonyms: layoff, leave of absence, temporary dismissal|antonyms: hiring, recall, reinstatement|noun, verb
