nannies:/ˈnæniz/
noun
保姆（nanny的复数形式）
A person, typically a woman, employed to care for a child or children in their own home.
Mary Poppins is one of the most famous fictional nannies. 玛丽·波平斯是最著名的虚构保姆之一。

The family hired two nannies to care for their triplets. 这家人雇了两个保姆来照顾他们的三胞胎。

Synonyms:  childminders, babysitters, caregivers, au pairs
Antonyms: parents, guardians

词性: noun
