blotlike:/ˈblɑːtlaɪk/
adjective
像污点的；像墨渍的
Resembling a blot; blotchy.  Something that looks like a stain or a mark.
The painting had a blotlike stain in the corner. 这幅画的角落里有一个像污渍的斑点。
The bruise on his arm was blotlike in shape. 他手臂上的瘀伤形状像墨渍。
近义词: blotchy, stained, spotty
反义词: clean, spotless, immaculate
词性: adjective

Etymology:  "blot" (from Old Norse *blatta "to strike") + "-like" (Old English -lic, meaning "having the form or appearance of")
