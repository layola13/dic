diaphragmatically:/ˌdaɪəfræɡˈmætɪkli/
| adv. |横膈膜地；用横膈膜|Using the diaphragm; by means of the diaphragm.|She breathed diaphragmatically to control her anxiety. 她用横膈膜呼吸来控制焦虑。|
|例句：He sang diaphragmatically, producing a rich, resonant tone. 他用横膈膜唱歌，发出浑厚洪亮的音调。|
|例句：The singer was trained to breathe diaphragmatically for better vocal control.  这位歌手被训练用横膈膜呼吸以更好地控制声音。|
|近义词：abdominally|反义词：(No clear antonym)|词性：副词


Etymology:
From diaphragm + -atically.  Ultimately from Greek *diáphragma*, from *diá* ("across") + *phrágma* ("fence, partition").
