diuretic: /ˌdaɪjʊˈrɛtɪk/ | adj. n. |利尿的；利尿剂 | Increasing the volume of urine excreted.  | Example (adj.):  Diuretic drugs can help control high blood pressure. 利尿药可以帮助控制高血压。

Example (n.): The doctor prescribed a diuretic to reduce fluid retention. 医生开了利尿剂来减少液体潴留。 | Synonym: (adj.)  natriuretic, hydragogue (n.) water pill | Antonym:  antidiuretic |  adjective, noun
