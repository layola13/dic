chirographist: /kaɪˈrɒɡrəfɪst/
n.
书法家，写字漂亮的人
A person skilled in handwriting, especially calligraphy.
The chirographist meticulously penned the invitation in elegant script.  书法家一丝不苟地用优美的字体写下了请柬。
He was renowned as a master chirographist. 他以书法大师而闻名。
近义词: calligrapher, penman, scribe
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps someone with poor handwriting)
词性: noun
