giggled:/ˈɡɪɡəld/ | v. | 咯咯地笑；傻笑 | Laughed lightly in a silly or nervous way. | The children giggled when the clown made a funny face. 孩子们看到小丑做鬼脸时咯咯地笑了。|  She giggled nervously as he complimented her dress. 他称赞她的裙子时，她紧张地咯咯笑了。 | 近义词：chuckled, tittered, snickered | 反义词：cried, sobbed, wailed | 词性: 动词

词源:  From Middle English *gigelen, frequentative of gigen "to giggle," of imitative origin.
