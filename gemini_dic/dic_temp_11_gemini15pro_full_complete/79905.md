gnawing:/ˈnɔːɪŋ/ (US) /ˈnɔːɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. | 咬的；啮；折磨人的；令人烦恼的 | persistently and painfully affecting the mind or body;  causing continual distress or anxiety. | The gnawing pain in his stomach wouldn't go away. 他胃里隐隐作痛，一直不好。| Synonyms: nagging, biting, persistent,  tormenting, agonizing | Antonyms: soothing, comforting, relieving | adjective


gnawing:/ˈnɔːɪŋ/ (US) /ˈnɔːɪŋ/ (UK)| n. | 咬；啮；啃 | the act of biting or chewing something persistently. | The gnawing of the mice kept him awake all night. 老鼠啃咬的声音让他整夜无法入睡。| Synonyms: biting, chewing, nibbling | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the noun form) | noun
