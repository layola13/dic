caas:/kɑːz/ | n. | Communications as a Service |  A cloud-based model where a provider hosts all the necessary hardware and software for businesses to deliver their communications services, such as voice, video, and messaging. | Example: The company decided to switch to CaaS to reduce their IT infrastructure costs.  该公司决定改用 CaaS 以降低其 IT 基础设施成本。| Example:  Using CaaS allowed the startup to scale their communication capabilities quickly and efficiently. 使用 CaaS 使初创公司能够快速有效地扩展其通信能力。 | Synonym:  Unified Communications as a Service (UCaaS), cloud communications | Antonym: On-premises communications | noun
