duramen:/djʊˈreɪmən/, /dʒəˈreɪmən/
|n.|心材|The duramen, also known as heartwood, is the central, non-living, and usually darker part of the trunk of a tree.|The furniture was made from the duramen of the oak tree. 这件家具是由橡树的心材制成的。|Synonyms: heartwood|Antonyms: sapwood|noun
