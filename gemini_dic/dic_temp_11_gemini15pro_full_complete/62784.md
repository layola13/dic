engravings: /ɪnˈɡreɪvɪŋz/
n.
雕刻品；版画
Pictures or designs cut into a hard surface, or prints made from such surfaces.
The museum has a collection of 18th-century engravings.  这家博物馆收藏了18世纪的版画。
The artist's engravings are highly valued by collectors.  这位艺术家的版画受到收藏家的高度评价。

近义词: carvings, etchings, prints
反义词:  None (Engravings refer to a specific process; there isn't a direct opposite process)
词性: noun (plural)

engraving: /ɪnˈɡreɪvɪŋ/
n.
雕刻；雕刻术
The process or art of cutting or carving a design on a hard surface.
The engraving on the watch was intricate and beautiful. 手表上的雕刻精美绝伦。
He studied engraving under a master craftsman. 他在一位工艺大师的指导下学习雕刻。

近义词: carving, etching, inscription
反义词: None
词性: noun (singular)
