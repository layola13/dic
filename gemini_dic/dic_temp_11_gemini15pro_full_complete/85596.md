hatter:/ˈhætər/ (US), /ˈhætə(r)/ (UK)| n.|帽商；制帽匠；<非正式>疯子，古怪的人|A person who makes or sells hats.  Informally, it can also refer to someone eccentric or mad. |例句：The hatter crafted a beautiful felt hat. 制帽匠制作了一顶漂亮的毡帽。

例句：He's a bit of a hatter, always wearing mismatched socks. 他有点古怪，总是穿着不配套的袜子。|近义词：milliner (for women's hats), eccentric, madman|反义词:conformist, sane|词性:noun

词源:From Middle English hattere, from Old English hætere, meaning "hat maker."  Related to "hat."
