mauvaniline:/ˌmɔːvˈænɪlɪn/| n.|貌苯胺|A synthetic purple dye, chemically related to rosaniline and pararosaniline. It is an intermediate in the synthesis of other dyes.|Mauvaniline was one of the first synthetic dyes. 貌苯胺是最早的合成染料之一。|Mauvaniline is used in the production of certain pigments. 貌苯胺用于生产某些颜料。|Synonyms: aniline violet, Perkin's violet|Antonyms: None|noun
