frustrately:/ˈfrʌstreɪtɪdli/| adv.| 懊恼地，沮丧地|In a frustrated manner; with feelings of annoyance and disappointment because one's efforts are being prevented from succeeding.|He sighed frustratedly and slammed the door. 他懊恼地叹了口气，砰地关上了门。|She tapped her foot frustratedly, waiting for the delayed train.  她懊恼地跺着脚，等待着晚点的火车。|近义词：angrily, irritably, impatiently|反义词：patiently, calmly, cheerfully|词性:副词
词源: From the adjective "frustrated" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Frustrated" ultimately comes from the Latin "frustrare" (to deceive, disappoint, frustrate).
