mushroomer:/ˈmʌʃˌrumər/ |n.| 采蘑菇的人；迅速发展的人或事物 |A person who gathers mushrooms, especially for food; something that grows or expands rapidly. |例句：He's a keen mushroomer and knows all the best spots in the forest. 他是一个热衷于采蘑菇的人，知道森林里所有最好的地点。

例句：The town became a mushroomer during the gold rush. 在淘金热期间，这个小镇迅速发展起来。|近义词：collector (for gathering mushrooms), grower, expander (for rapid growth) |反义词:shrinker, decliner (for rapid growth)|词性:名词
