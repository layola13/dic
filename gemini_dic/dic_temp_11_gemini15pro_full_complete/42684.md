countersuit: /ˈkaʊntərˌsuːt/
n.
反诉；反控；对抗性诉讼
A lawsuit filed in response to an initial lawsuit.
The company filed a countersuit against their former employee.  该公司对他们的前雇员提起了反诉。

The defendant filed a countersuit alleging breach of contract.  被告提起反诉，声称对方违反合同。

近义词: counterclaim, cross-action, cross-suit
反义词: lawsuit, suit, claim
词性: noun
