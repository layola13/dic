halfbeak:/ˈhæfˌbiːk/| n.|半喙鱼，鱵鱼|A fish with a long lower jaw and a shorter upper jaw, found in warm seas.  It resembles a garfish.|例句：The halfbeak skimmed the surface of the water. 半喙鱼掠过水面。

例句：Halfbeak populations are declining in some areas due to overfishing.  由于过度捕捞，一些地区的半喙鱼数量正在下降。|近义词：garfish, needlefish|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
