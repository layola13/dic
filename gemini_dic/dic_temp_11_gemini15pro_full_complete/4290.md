airworthinesses:/ˈɛərˌwɜːrðiːnɪsɪz/
n.
适航性（复数）
The state of an aircraft being fit and safe to fly.
All airworthinesses of the fleet were checked before departure. 机队的所有适航性在出发前都进行了检查。
The various airworthinesses of the different models were compared.  不同型号的各种适航性进行了比较。
近义词:fitness, suitability
反义词:unairworthiness
词性:名词
