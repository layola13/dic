acclaim:/əˈkleɪm/
n.|赞扬，称赞；欢迎|enthusiastic and public praise.|The film met with considerable critical acclaim. 这部电影受到了评论界的大力赞扬。|
近义词:praise, applause, commendation, approval, recognition
反义词:criticism, condemnation, disapproval, censure
词性:noun

acclaims:/əˈkleɪmz/
n.|称赞，赞扬（acclaim的复数形式）|plural form of acclaim, meaning enthusiastic and public praise.|His work has received many acclaims from critics. 他的作品受到了评论家的诸多赞扬。|
近义词:praises, applause, commendations
反义词:criticisms, condemnations
词性:noun


词源:
From Middle French *aclamer, from Latin acclamare (“to shout at, applaud”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + clamare (“to shout, cry out”).
