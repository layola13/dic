bare-knuckle:/ˌberˈnʌk(ə)l/
adjective
赤手空拳的；不戴拳击手套的；残酷激烈的
fought without boxing gloves; involving unrestrained conflict
The two men engaged in a bare-knuckle brawl in the street.  这两个男人在街上赤手空拳地打了起来。
The company is involved in a bare-knuckle fight for market share.  这家公司正进行着一场残酷的市场份额争夺战。
Synonyms:  unarmed, brutal, fierce, no-holds-barred
Antonyms:  gloved, civilized, restrained, gentle
词性: 形容词


bare-knuckle:/ˌberˈnʌk(ə)l/
noun
赤手空拳拳击；不戴拳击手套的打斗
boxing without gloves
Bare-knuckle boxing is a dangerous sport. 赤手空拳拳击是一项危险的运动。
The bare-knuckle fight left both men bruised and battered. 这场赤手空拳的打斗让两人都伤痕累累。
Synonyms:  fistfight, brawl
Antonyms:  gloved boxing
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From bare (adj.) + knuckle (n.).  Referring to fighting with bare hands, without the protection of gloves.
