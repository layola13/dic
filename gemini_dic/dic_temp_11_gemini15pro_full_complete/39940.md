constructibilities: /kənˌstrʌktɪˈbɪlɪtiz/
|n.|可构造性；可建性|The property of being constructible, often referring to mathematical or geometrical objects that can be created using specific tools or rules.  指能够使用特定工具或规则创建的数学或几何对象的属性。|The constructibility of the polygon depends on the given angles.  这个多边形的可构造性取决于给定的角度。|constructability|impossibility|noun
constructibility: /kənˌstrʌktɪˈbɪlɪti/
|n.|可建造性；可施工性|The feasibility of constructing something, typically a building or other physical structure. 指建造某物，通常是建筑物或其他物理结构的可行性。|The engineers questioned the constructibility of the bridge due to the challenging terrain.  由于地形复杂，工程师们对桥梁的可建造性提出了质疑。|feasibility, practicality|infeasibility, impracticality|noun
