capitalisms:/ˈkæpɪtəlɪzəmz/
n.
资本主义（复数）
Various forms of capitalisms exist around the world.
世界上存在各种形式的资本主义。
Capitalisms prioritize profit and private ownership.
资本主义优先考虑利润和私有制。
Synonyms: free market economies, private enterprise systems
Antonyms: socialisms, communisms
noun


Etymology: From "capitalism" + "-s" (plural suffix). "Capitalism" derives from "capital," ultimately from Latin "caput" (head).
