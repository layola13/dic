carnalisms: /ˈkɑːrnlˌɪzəmz/ | n. | 肉慾,  淫蕩行為 |  Excessive indulgence in physical pleasures, especially sexual ones.  Acts or expressions of sensuality or lewdness.  Focuses on the base or crude aspects of physical desires. |  His writings were filled with carnalisms, offensive to many readers.  他的作品充满了肉慾描写，冒犯了许多读者。

The preacher condemned the carnalisms of modern society.  牧师谴责了现代社会的淫荡行为。 | 近义词: lewdness, lasciviousness, debauchery, licentiousness | 反义词: chastity, abstinence, purity, virtue | 词性: 名词

词源: From Late Latin carnalismus, from Latin carnalis "of the flesh," from caro "flesh."
