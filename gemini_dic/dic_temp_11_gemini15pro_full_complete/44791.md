crunchinesses:/ˈkrʌntʃinəsiz/
n.
酥脆，松脆（复数）
The quality of being crunchy; crispness.  This refers to multiple instances of crunchiness.
The snack boasts delightful crunchinesses, each bite offering a satisfying snap. 这种零食有着令人愉悦的松脆口感，每一口都嘎嘣脆。
The various crunchinesses of the toppings added textural interest to the salad. 配料的各种酥脆口感为沙拉增添了质感趣味。
crispness, crunch
softness, chewiness
noun
