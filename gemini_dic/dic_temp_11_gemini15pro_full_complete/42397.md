counterchanged:/ˌkaʊntərˈtʃeɪndʒd/
| adj. | 对换的，互换色的 | (Heraldry) Having the tinctures of a field and a charge reversed. | The shield was counterchanged, with argent lions on a field of gules, and gules lions on a field of argent. | 近义词: permuted, interchanged, transposed | 反义词:  unchanged, unaltered | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Middle English *contrechaungen, from Anglo-Norman contrechanger, from contre- (“against, opposite”) + changer (“to change”).
