cadenza:/kəˈdɛntsə/ /kəˈdɛnzə/
n.
华彩乐段; 华彩段落
An elaborate, improvised, or written-out ornamental passage played or sung by a soloist or soloists, usually in a "free" rhythmic style and often allowing for virtuosic display.
The soprano delivered a breathtaking cadenza in the aria. 女高音在咏叹调中演唱了一段令人叹为观止的华彩乐段。
近义词:flourish, embellishment, ornamentation
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "simplicity" in a musical context)
词性:noun

cadenza:/kəˈdɛntsə/ /kəˈdɛnzə/
n.
(比喻) 华丽的辞藻; 炫技
A brilliant or virtuosic display, especially in speaking or writing.
His closing argument was a cadenza of legal rhetoric. 他的结束辩论是一段华丽的法律辞藻的炫技。
近义词:flourish, display, show
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "understatement")
词性:noun
