erodable:/ɪˈroʊdəbl/
adjective
可侵蚀的，易受腐蚀的
Susceptible to erosion.  Something that can be worn away or destroyed by natural forces such as wind, water, or ice.
The soft sandstone cliffs are highly erodable.  这些柔软的砂岩悬崖很容易被侵蚀。
The coastline is erodable and constantly changing.  海岸线易受侵蚀，不断变化。
synonyms: corrodible, degradable, vulnerable
antonyms:  impervious, resistant, durable
词性: 形容词
