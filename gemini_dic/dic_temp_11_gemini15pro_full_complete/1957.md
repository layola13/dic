actionist:/ˈækʃənɪst/
noun
行动主义者；激进主义者
a person who participates in direct and often confrontational action in support of a political or social cause
例句：The actionists staged a protest outside the government building. 行动主义者在政府大楼外举行了抗议。

例句：The actionist chained himself to the gate in a desperate attempt to be heard.  这名激进分子把自己锁在门口，拼命想让别人听到他的声音。

近义词：activist, protester, demonstrator, militant
反义词：passivist, conformist
词性：n.


Etymology:  From action + -ist.  "Action" ultimately derives from Latin "agere" (to do, to drive, to lead).
