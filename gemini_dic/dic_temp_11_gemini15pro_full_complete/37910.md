complementary-angles:/ˌkɒmplɪˈmentəri ˈæŋɡlz/ |n.| 互补角 |Two angles are complementary if their sum is 90 degrees.  两个角的和如果是 90 度，则它们是互补角。| The complementary angles formed a right angle.  这些互补角形成了一个直角。| supplementary angles | opposite angles, adjacent angles | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *complementarius, from Latin complementum (“that which fills up or completes”), from complēre (“to fill up, complete”), from com- (“together”) + plēre (“to fill”).  Angle from Latin angulus (“corner, angle”).
