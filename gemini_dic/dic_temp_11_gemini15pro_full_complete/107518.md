landscape: /ˈlændskeɪp/ | n. |风景，景色；山水画；地形；（事件发生的）背景，局面 | An extensive view of natural scenery; the aspect of the land characteristic of a particular region; a painting representing such scenery. | The landscape was breathtaking, with rolling hills and lush green valleys.  这片风景美得令人窒息，连绵起伏的丘陵和郁郁葱葱的山谷。| synonym: scenery, view, vista, panorama | antonym: cityscape, interior | noun

landscape: /ˈlændskeɪp/ | vt. |对…进行景观美化；美化…的景观 | To improve the appearance of an area of land by planting trees, shrubs, etc. | They landscaped the garden with a variety of flowers and trees. 他们在花园里种植了各种各样的花草树木，使其景观美化。 | synonym: beautify, embellish, adorn | antonym: uglify, spoil, damage | verb
