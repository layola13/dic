hardinesses:/ˈhɑːrdinəsiz/
n.
耐寒性，强壮，坚韧
the ability of a plant to survive adverse conditions, especially cold weather.
The hardinesses of these plants allow them to thrive in harsh climates. 这些植物的耐寒性使它们能够在恶劣的气候中茁壮成长。
近义词: resilience, toughness, robustness, durability
反义词: fragility, weakness, vulnerability
词性: 名词 (plural of hardiness)


Etymology:
From hardy + -ness.  "Hardy" traces back to Old Norse *harðr "hard," related to Old English heard.  The "-ness" suffix forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
