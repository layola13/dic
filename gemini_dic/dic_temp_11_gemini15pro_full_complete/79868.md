gnarled:/nɑːrld/| adj.| 粗糙的，扭曲的，多瘤的|Covered with gnarls; twisted and knotty.  Characterized by being rough, twisted, and knotted, often due to age or harsh conditions.|The old oak tree had a gnarled trunk and branches.|近义词：knotted, twisted, gnarly, misshapen, contorted|反义词:smooth, straight, unblemished
