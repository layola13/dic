disemployment:/ˌdɪsɪmˈplɔɪmənt/
|n.|失业；失业状态|The state of being without paid work, typically due to factors such as technological advancements, economic downturns, or company restructuring.|例句：The factory closure led to widespread disemployment in the town. 工厂关闭导致该镇普遍失业。|
|
|近义词：unemployment, joblessness|
|
|反义词：employment|
|
|词性：名词|


ETYMOLOGY->

From dis- (expressing negation, reversal) + employment.  First recorded in the late 19th century.
