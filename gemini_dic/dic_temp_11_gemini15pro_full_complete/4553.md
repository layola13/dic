alchemically:/ˌælkɪˈmɪkli/| adv.|炼金术地，神奇地|In a way that relates to or suggests alchemy.  Processed in a way reminiscent of alchemy; often used figuratively to describe transformative or seemingly magical processes.|The base metals were alchemically transformed into gold. 这些贱金属被神奇地变成了黄金。|Synonym: magically, mysteriously, supernaturally|Antonym: naturally, ordinarily, scientifically|词性:副词
