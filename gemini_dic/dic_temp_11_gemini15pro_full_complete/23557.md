brainstormed:/ˈbreɪnstɔːrmd/
verb
头脑风暴，集体讨论，集思广益
past tense and past participle of brainstorm: engaged in a discussion to produce ideas or solve a problem.
The team brainstormed new marketing strategies.  团队集体讨论了新的营销策略。
We brainstormed for hours but couldn't come up with a solution. 我们集思广益了几个小时，但还是想不出解决办法。
Synonyms: ideated, generated ideas, collaborated, discussed
Antonyms: procrastinated, avoided, ignored
