anchovy: /ˈænˌtʃoʊvi/ (US), /ˈænˌtʃəvi/ (UK)| n. |凤尾鱼；鯷鱼|A small, common saltwater fish, often used as bait or preserved in oil and eaten as a snack or ingredient in various dishes.|例句：I love anchovies on my pizza. 我喜欢披萨上的凤尾鱼。

例句：Anchovies are a key ingredient in Caesar salad dressing. 凤尾鱼是凯撒沙拉酱的关键成分。

近义词：sprat, sardine|反义词: None|词性:noun
