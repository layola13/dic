coleopteral:/ˌkoʊliˈɑːptərəl/
adjective
鞘翅目的；甲虫类的
Relating to or characteristic of beetles, an order of insects (Coleoptera) characterized by hard, shell-like front wings.
Coleopteral larvae are often found in decaying wood. 鞘翅目幼虫经常在腐烂的木头中发现。
Coleopteral diversity is highest in tropical rainforests. 鞘翅目的多样性在热带雨林中最高。
近义词: beetle, insect
反义词:  (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑其他昆虫类别，例如 lepidopteran 鳞翅目的)
词性: adjective

词源:
From Coleoptera, the scientific name for the beetle order, from Greek koleos "sheath" + pteron "wing."
