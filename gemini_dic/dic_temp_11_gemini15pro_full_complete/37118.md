combustibles:/kəmˈbʌstəblz/
| n. |易燃物，可燃物|Materials that can catch fire and burn easily. |Highly combustibles should be stored safely away from heat sources. 高度易燃物应安全存放在远离热源的地方。| flammable materials, inflammables |non-combustibles, fire-resistant materials|noun

combustible:/kəmˈbʌstəbl/
| adj. |易燃的，可燃烧的|Capable of catching fire and burning. |This material is highly combustible and should be handled with care. 这种材料非常易燃，应小心处理。|flammable, inflammable|non-combustible, fire-resistant|adjective


Etymology:
From Late Latin *combustibilis, from Latin combustus, past participle of comburere "to burn up," from com- "completely" + burere "to burn."
