deride:/dɪˈraɪd/| v. |嘲笑，讥讽|To laugh at or make fun of someone or something in a way that shows contempt or scorn. |例句：They derided his efforts as childish. 他们嘲笑他的努力幼稚。

例句：Critics derided the movie as predictable and sentimental.  评论家嘲笑这部电影老套又煽情。


|近义词：mock, ridicule, scorn, taunt, jeer|反义词: praise, admire, commend, respect|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dērīdēre*, from *dē-* (down, away) + *rīdēre* (to laugh).  Essentially meaning "to laugh down" or "to laugh away".
