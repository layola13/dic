dedesignated:/ˌdiːˈdɛzɪɡneɪtɪd/
verb
撤销指定；取消称号
remove the designation of; no longer assign a particular role or function to
The area was dedesignated as a national park.  该地区被取消了国家公园的称号。
The judge was dedesignated from the case. 法官被取消了此案的资格。
Synonyms:  removed, revoked, withdrawn, rescinded, annulled
Antonyms: designated, appointed, assigned
词性: 动词
