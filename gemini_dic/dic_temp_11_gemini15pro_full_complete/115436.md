mantra:/ˈmæntrə/ | n. | 咒语，真言，口头禅 | A word or sound repeated to aid concentration in meditation.  A statement or slogan repeated frequently. |  She repeated her mantra, "I will succeed." 她重复着她的咒语：“我会成功的。” |  creed, slogan, motto, catchphrase |  antonym:  (No direct antonym, but consider:  silence, expletive) | noun
