legitimising: /lɪˈdʒɪtɪmaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使合法化；使正当化
The government is trying to legitimise its actions. 政府正试图使其行动合法化。
Present participle of *legitimise*.  是动词legitimise的现在分词形式。
例句：
The report attempts to legitimize the use of force.  这份报告试图将使用武力合法化。
They are legitimising the illegal occupation of land. 他们正在使非法占地合法化。
近义词: validating, legalizing, justifying, authorizing
反义词: invalidating, criminalizing, condemning
词性: v. (verb)


legitimise: /lɪˈdʒɪtɪmaɪz/
verb
使合法化；使正当化
The government tried to legitimise its actions. 政府试图使其行动合法化。
From French légitimiser, from légitime ‘legitimate’. 源于法语 légitimiser，来自 légitime “合法的”。
例句：
The regime is seeking to legitimize its power. 该政权正在寻求使其权力合法化。
His success legitimized the business model. 他的成功使这种商业模式合法化。
近义词: validate, legalize, justify, authorize
反义词: invalidate, criminalize, condemn
词性: v. (verb)
