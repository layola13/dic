mahwa:/ˈmɑːhwɑː/ | n. | 木槿花; 木槿; | A flowering plant, Hibiscus syriacus, native to Asia, with large showy flowers. Also refers to its edible flowers and leaves. |  The mahwa trees lined the street, their blossoms a vibrant pink. 木槿花排列在街道两旁，花朵鲜艳粉红。

The mahwa flowers are used in traditional Korean teas. 木槿花用于韩国传统茶饮。 | 近义词: Rose of Sharon, hibiscus | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
