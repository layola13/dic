condolences:/kənˈdoʊlənsɪz/
|n.|哀悼，慰问|An expression of sympathy, especially on the occasion of a death.|Please accept my sincere condolences on the passing of your father.  请接受我对您父亲去世的诚挚哀悼。|Synonyms: sympathy, commiseration, solace|Antonyms: congratulations, felicitations|noun
