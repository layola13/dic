associativenesses:/əˌsoʊʃiəˈtɪvnɪsɪz/| n.|结合性；结合律|The property of an operation whereby the result of applying the operation to three or more operands does not depend on the way in which the operands are grouped.|Matrix multiplication exhibits associativeness. 矩阵乘法具有结合性。|The associativenesses of the various operators need to be considered. 需要考虑各种运算符的结合性。|synonyms: associativity|antonyms: non-associativity|noun
