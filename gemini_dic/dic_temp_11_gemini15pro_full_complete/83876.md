half-admittedly:/ˌhæf ədˈmɪtɪdli/ | adv. | 半承认地，不完全承认地 |  Indicates a statement or feeling is only partially or reluctantly acknowledged. It combines "half" and "admittedly," suggesting a degree of reservation or hesitation in the admission. |  He half-admittedly enjoyed the attention. 他半推半就地享受着关注。/ Half-admittedly, I was a little scared.  我得承认，我有点害怕。 | Near Synonyms: partly, partially, somewhat, to some extent, almost, kind of, sort of | Antonyms: fully, completely, wholly, entirely |词性:副词
