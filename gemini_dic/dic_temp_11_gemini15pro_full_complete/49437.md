delphic:/ˈdɛlfɪk/ /ˈdɛl.fɪk/| adj.|神谕的；谜一般的；晦涩难懂的|Of or relating to the ancient Greek oracle at Delphi; ambiguous and difficult to understand. | The priestess gave a delphic answer to the king's question. 女祭司对国王的问题给出了一个谜一般的回答。 |  The writer's delphic prose made it hard to grasp the meaning.  这位作家的晦涩难懂的散文使人难以理解其含义。|近义词：ambiguous, cryptic, enigmatic, obscure, oracular |反义词：clear, explicit, straightforward|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *Delphicus, from Latin Delphicī (plural), from Greek Delphoí (Δελφοί), the name of the ancient Greek city of Delphi, site of the famous oracle of Apollo.
