ayin:/ˈaɪɪn/ /ˈʕain/ | n. | 第十八个希伯来字母;  عين (阿拉伯字母)|The eighteenth letter of the Hebrew alphabet; the sixth letter of the Arabic alphabet, representing a voiced pharyngeal fricative or, in some dialects, a glottal stop. |  The word "ayin" itself comes from the Semitic word for "eye." |  No direct English equivalent exists, as the sound represented by ayin doesn't occur in English.  | 近义词: (No direct equivalent) | 反义词: (No direct equivalent) | 词性:noun
