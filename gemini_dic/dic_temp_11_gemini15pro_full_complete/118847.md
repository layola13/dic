ETYMOLOGY->
From meta- (meaning "change") + phrase (meaning "way of speaking or writing"). The prefix "meta-" comes from Greek, meaning "after," "beyond," or "change." "Phrase" also derives from Greek, "phrazein," meaning "to tell."
