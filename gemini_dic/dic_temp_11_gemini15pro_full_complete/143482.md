outgaze:/ˌaʊtˈɡeɪz/
n.|凝视，注视|The act of looking steadily and intently, especially at something distant or at someone.|She met his outgaze across the crowded room. 她与他在拥挤的房间里对视。|近义词：stare, gaze|反义词：glance|词性:名词
