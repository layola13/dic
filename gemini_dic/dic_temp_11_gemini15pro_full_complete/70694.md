finalizes:/ˈfaɪnəlaɪzɪz/
verb
完成；最终确定
puts the finishing touches to something; completes the last part of something
例句：The committee finalized the report. 委员会最终完成了报告。

例句：They finalized the deal after weeks of negotiation.  经过数周的谈判，他们最终敲定了这笔交易。

近义词：complete, conclude, finish, settle
反义词：begin, start, commence, initiate
词性：v.
