oversoftened: /ˌoʊvərˈsɔːfənd/ | adj. | 过度软化的；过度柔化的 |  Made too soft, often referring to water or fabrics.  It implies a negative connotation, suggesting that the softening has gone beyond what is desirable or useful. | The oversoftened water left a filmy residue on the dishes.  过度软化的水在盘子上留下了一层薄膜。

The oversoftened fabric pilled easily. 过度软化的织物很容易起球。 | 近义词: excessively soft, over-tenderized | 反义词: hard, firm, tough | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "soften" (verb meaning "to make soft") + "-ed" (past participle suffix)
