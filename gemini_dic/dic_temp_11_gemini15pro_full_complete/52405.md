diddle:/ˈdɪdəl/| v. | 欺骗，骗取（尤指小钱）；拨弄，玩弄 | To cheat or swindle someone, typically out of a small amount of money; to fiddle or play with something. | He diddled the company out of a few hundred dollars. 他骗了公司几百美元。

He was diddling with the radio knob. 他一直在拨弄收音机旋钮。 | 近义词：cheat, swindle, defraud, fiddle, toy | 反义词: be honest, compensate | 动词

diddle:/ˈdɪdəl/| n. | 欺骗，骗局 | An act of cheating or swindling. |  The whole investment scheme was a diddle. 整个投资计划就是一个骗局。 | 近义词：cheat, swindle, fraud | 反义词: honesty, legitimacy | 名词
