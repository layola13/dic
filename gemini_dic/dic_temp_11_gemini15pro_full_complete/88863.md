hoise:/hɔɪs/| vt.| 吊起，升起; 拉起|To lift or pull something heavy up to a higher position.|They hoised the flag to the top of the pole. 他们把旗子升到旗杆顶端。|The sailors hoised the sails. 水手们升起了帆。|近义词：lift, raise, elevate, heave|反义词:lower, drop|verb
