expands:/ɪkˈspændz/| v.| 扩张，扩大，扩展，膨胀|The business expands into new markets. 该企业拓展到新的市场。|例句：The company expands its operations overseas.  公司将其业务扩展到海外。

例句：Heating metal expands it. 加热金属会使其膨胀。|近义词：increases, grows, enlarges, stretches, widens, develops, amplifies, augments|反义词:contracts, shrinks, reduces, compresses, diminishes|词性:动词

Etymology: From Middle English *expanden, from Old French espandre, expandre, from Latin expandere, from ex- (“out”) + pandere (“to spread”).
