convention:/kənˈvɛnʃən/ | n. | 惯例，习俗；会议，大会 | A convention is a way of behaving that is considered to be usual or correct by most people. |  Shaking hands is a common convention in many cultures.  握手在许多文化中是一种常见的习俗。 | custom, tradition, practice | unconventionality | noun


convention:/kənˈvɛnʃən/ | n. | 会议，大会 | A convention is a large meeting of people who come together to discuss something or to make decisions. | The political party held its annual convention. 该政党举行了年度大会。 | conference, meeting, assembly |  | noun


convention:/kənˈvɛnʃən/ | n. | 公约，协定 | A convention is a formal agreement between countries. | The Geneva Conventions protect the rights of prisoners of war.  《日内瓦公约》保护战俘的权利。 | agreement, treaty, pact | disagreement | noun


Etymology: From Old French *convention, from Late Latin conventio (“a coming together, agreement”), from Latin convenio (“come together, assemble”).
