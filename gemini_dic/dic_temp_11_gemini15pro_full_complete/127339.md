nazifications:/ˌnɑːtsɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/
n.
纳粹化（复数）
The process of imposing Nazi ideology and policies on a country or organization.
The nazifications of several European countries during World War II led to widespread suffering and oppression. 二战期间几个欧洲国家的纳粹化导致了广泛的苦难和压迫。
