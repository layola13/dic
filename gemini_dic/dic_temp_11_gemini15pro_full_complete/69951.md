feste:/ˈfɛstə/ | n. | 节日; 盛宴; 庆祝; | A festive occasion, especially a public celebration. |  例句：The town held a feste to celebrate the harvest.  小镇举行节日庆祝丰收。| 近义词：festival, celebration, feast, holiday | 反义词：mourning, wake | 词性：名词

feste:/ˈfɛsteɪ/ | adj. | 节日的; 欢乐的; | Relating to or characteristic of a festival. | 例句：The hall was decorated in a festive manner. 大厅装饰得很有节日气氛。 | 近义词：festive, celebratory, merry, joyous | 反义词：somber, gloomy | 词性：形容词


词源：From Old French *feste*, from Latin *festa* "holiday, feast day", plural of *festum*.
