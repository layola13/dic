muskoxes:/ˈmʌskɒksɪz/ | n. | 麝牛（复数） | A long-haired Arctic ox with a musky odor, having a shaggy coat, short legs, and curving horns. |  Muskoxes are well adapted to the harsh Arctic climate. 麝牛非常适应严酷的北极气候。|  Muskoxes form a defensive circle to protect their young from predators. 麝牛会形成一个防御圈来保护它们的幼崽免受捕食者的侵害。 | 近义词: ovibovine (更正式的科学名称) | 反义词:  无 | 词性: 名词

muskox:/ˈmʌskɒks/ | n. | 麝牛（单数） | A long-haired Arctic ox with a musky odor, having a shaggy coat, short legs, and curving horns. | The muskox is a herbivore that feeds on grasses, sedges, and willows. 麝牛是一种食草动物，以草、莎草和柳树为食。 |  A lone muskox is vulnerable to wolf attacks.  一只单独的麝牛很容易受到狼的攻击。 | 近义词: ovibovine (更正式的科学名称) | 反义词: 无 | 词性: 名词
