flouter:/ˈflaʊtər/| v. |藐视；嘲笑；轻视；愚弄|To show contempt for; scorn: to treat with disdain or derision. |He flouted the school rules by wearing a hat indoors. 他戴着帽子进教室，藐视了校规。|近义词：scorn, disdain, mock, defy, disregard|反义词：respect, obey, honor, observe|词性：动词
flouter:/ˈflaʊtər/| n. |藐视者；嘲笑者|One who flouts; a scorner.  |He was a flouter of conventions. 他是一个藐视传统的人。 |近义词：scorner, mocker, rebel|反义词：conformist, follower|词性：名词
