mariput:/ˈmærɪpʊt/ | n. |香猫; 椰子猫|A small, arboreal, nocturnal mammal (Paradoxurus hermaphroditus) of southern Asia, related to the civets, having a long prehensile tail and valued for its musklike scent.|The mariput is known for its role in producing kopi luwak, a type of coffee made from partially digested coffee cherries.| 近义词:common palm civet, toddy cat | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

词源: From French *maripette*. Ultimately from Dravidian (cf. Tamil *maṛai*, *maṛa*, *maraṅki*, Malayalam *maram*, Kannada *mara* ("tree") + *puṇuku* ("cat")).
