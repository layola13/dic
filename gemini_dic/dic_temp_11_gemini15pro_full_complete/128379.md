nevertheless:/ˌnɛvərðəˈlɛs/ | adv. | 然而，仍然，不过 | despite something that you have just mentioned |  例句：He was feeling ill; nevertheless, he went to work. 他感觉不舒服；然而，他还是去上班了。

例句：The weather was bad; nevertheless, we enjoyed ourselves. 天气很糟糕；不过，我们玩得很开心。


近义词：however, nonetheless, yet, still, in spite of that, despite that | 反义词: therefore, consequently, accordingly | 词性: 副词
