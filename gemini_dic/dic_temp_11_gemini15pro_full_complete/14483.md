avast:/əˈvɑːst/ | int. |停！住手！|A nautical command meaning "stop" or "cease". It is derived from the Dutch phrase "houd vast" meaning "hold fast".  It's now primarily used in a humorous or archaic way. |Avast, ye scurvy dogs! 停！你们这些坏蛋！|Example 2: "Avast there, matey!"  停下来，伙计！|Synonym: stop, cease, halt |Antonym: continue, proceed |interjection
