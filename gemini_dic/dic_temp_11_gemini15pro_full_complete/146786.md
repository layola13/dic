overidleness:/ˌoʊvərˈaɪdəlnəs/| n. |过度闲散，过度懒惰|A state of excessive idleness or inactivity.|He succumbed to overidleness during his unemployment and struggled to regain his motivation. 他在失业期间陷入了过度闲散的状态，难以重新找回动力。|Example: The overidleness of the workforce led to a decline in productivity.  劳动力过度闲散导致生产力下降。|Synonyms: sloth, inactivity, indolence, laziness|Antonyms: industry, diligence, activity, busyness|noun
