algonquian: /ælˈɡɒŋkiən/ (US), /ælˈɡɒŋkwɪən/ (UK)| adj. n.| 阿尔冈昆语（族）的；阿尔冈昆语（族）|Relating to or denoting a family of North American Indian languages formerly spoken across a vast area from the Atlantic seaboard to the Rocky Mountains.  Also: a member of any of the peoples speaking these languages.  |The Algonquian languages were once spoken across a vast territory.|近义词: Algonquin|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun
