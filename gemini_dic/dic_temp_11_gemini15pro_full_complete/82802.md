guipure:/ɡɪˈpjʊər/| n.|吉普蕾花边，缉花网眼花边|A heavy lace of linen, silk, or cotton thread, made in elaborate patterns, often with large open spaces connected by bars or stitches.  A bobbin lace resembling Venetian point but coarser.|The bride wore a beautiful guipure lace veil.|近义词：lace, Venetian point, bobbin lace|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly plain fabric)|词性:noun
