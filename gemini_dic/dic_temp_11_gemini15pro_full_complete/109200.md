legumin:/ˈlɛɡjʊmɪn/ | n. | 豆球蛋白; 植物白蛋白 | A globulin protein found in the seeds of many plants, especially legumes, such as peas and beans. It is a major source of protein in these plants and is important for human and animal nutrition. |  Legumin is an important source of protein in vegetarian diets. 豆球蛋白是素食中重要的蛋白质来源。|  Peas and beans are rich in legumin. 豌豆和豆类富含豆球蛋白。 | Synonyms: vegetable protein, globulin | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific protein) | noun
