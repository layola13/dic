glad-hander:/ˈɡlædˌhændər/
noun
热情握手者；虚情假意的人
A person who is overly friendly and enthusiastic, often in an insincere way, especially someone who does this to gain an advantage.
He's such a glad-hander, always shaking everyone's hand and slapping them on the back, but you can tell it's all fake. 他真是个虚情假意的人，总是跟每个人握手拍背，但你能看出这一切都是假的。
