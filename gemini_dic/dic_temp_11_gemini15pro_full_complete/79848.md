glyptography: /ɡlɪpˈtɒɡrəfi/ | n. |雕刻术，雕刻学|The art and process of carving, engraving, or sculpting, especially on gemstones or other hard materials.  It refers to the practice of carving inscriptions or designs onto surfaces, particularly gemstones. |Ancient Egyptian glyptography is renowned for its intricate detail. 古埃及雕刻以其复杂的细节而闻名。| The museum's collection boasts impressive examples of Greek glyptography. 该博物馆的收藏品中有一些令人印象深刻的希腊雕刻作品。| 近义词: engraving, carving, sculpture | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider 'erasure' or 'smoothing' in certain contexts) | 词性: noun
