axisymmetry:/ˌæksiˈsɪmətri/ | n. | 轴对称 |The property of a body or system being symmetric about an axis.  A shape is axisymmetric if it looks the same after being rotated around a central axis.| The cylindrical pipe exhibits axisymmetry. 圆柱形管道呈现轴对称性。 | synonyms: rotational symmetry, radial symmetry | antonyms: asymmetry, non-axisymmetry | noun
