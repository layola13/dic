clutchier:/ˈklʌtʃiər/
adjective
更重要的，更关键的，更要紧的
More decisive or critical, especially at a crucial moment.  More tenacious or grasping.
He made a clutchier performance in the final game. 他在决赛中表现得更关键。
The team needs a clutchier player in the final minutes.  球队在最后时刻需要一个更关键的球员。
近义词:  more crucial, more critical, more decisive, tighter
反义词: less crucial, less critical, less decisive, looser
词性: adjective


clutchier:/ˈklʌtʃiər/
adjective
更贪婪的，更吝啬的
More tenacious or grasping.
She's become clutchier with her money as she's gotten older. 她年纪越大越对金钱斤斤计较。
He had a clutchier grip on power than his predecessor. 他比他的前任更贪恋权力。
近义词: greedier, stingier, more possessive
反义词: more generous, more liberal
词性: adjective


词源:
From clutch (v.) + -y + -er (comparative suffix).  The verb "clutch" originates from Old English *clyccan, meaning "to bend, hook."
