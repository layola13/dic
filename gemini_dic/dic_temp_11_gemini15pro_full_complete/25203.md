buddleias:/ˈbʌdliəz/
noun
醉鱼草属植物;醉鱼草
Deciduous or semi-evergreen shrubs with dense panicles of fragrant flowers.
The buddleias are in full bloom, attracting butterflies and bees. 醉鱼草正盛开着，吸引着蝴蝶和蜜蜂。
Synonyms: butterfly bush, summer lilac
Antonyms: N/A
