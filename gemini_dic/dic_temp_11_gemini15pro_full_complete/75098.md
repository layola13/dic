fringed:/frɪndʒd/
adjective
有边的，有流苏的
bordered with or having a fringe.
The fringed tablecloth added a touch of elegance to the room.  有流苏的桌布为房间增添了一丝优雅。
The plant has fringed leaves. 这种植物的叶子有边。
近义词: bordered, edged, trimmed
反义词: plain, unadorned
词性: adjective


fringe:/frɪndʒ/
noun
边缘，流苏，穗
a decorative border of hanging threads, cords, or strips, often attached to a curtain, garment, or rug.  Also refers to the outer, less important, or marginal part of something.
The fringe of the curtain was tangled.  窗帘的流苏缠结在一起了。
He operates on the fringes of the music industry. 他在音乐行业的边缘地带活动。
近义词: border, edge, rim, periphery, margin
反义词: center, core, middle
词性: noun

fringe:/frɪndʒ/
verb
加流苏，形成…的边缘
to decorate with a fringe; to form a border or edge around.
She fringed the scarf with silk threads. 她用丝线给围巾加了流苏。
Tall trees fringed the clearing. 高大的树木环绕着空地。
近义词: border, edge, trim
反义词: uncover, expose
词性: verb

词源: From Middle English *fringen, from Old French frenge, from Frankish *frimbja, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *frimbijǭ (“fringe”). Cognate with Old High German frimbja (“fiber, fringe”).
