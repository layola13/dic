biliousness:/ˈbɪliəsnəs/
n.
恶心, 胆汁质, 坏脾气
A state of nausea and indigestion, often accompanied by a bad temper, traditionally attributed to an excess of bile.
She complained of biliousness and a headache. 她抱怨恶心和头痛。
近义词:nausea, indigestion, dyspepsia, queasiness, irritability, grumpiness, ill humor
反义词:good humor, cheerfulness, amiability
