aventail:/ˈævənteɪl/| n.|面甲，护面帘|A piece of chain mail or plate armor hanging from a helmet to protect the neck and shoulders.|The knight lowered his aventail before entering the fray.|近义词：visor, beaver|反义词: None|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Old French *aventaile*, from *avant* "before" + *taille* "cut".  Originally referred to the part of the helmet that was in front of the face opening, later extended to mean the whole movable face guard and eventually the neck protection.
