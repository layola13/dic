ecumenical:/ˌɛkjuˈmɛnɪkəl/ |adj.| 世界范围的；普遍的；基督教各派联合的|Promoting worldwide Christian unity or cooperation.|  The Pope made an ecumenical gesture by inviting leaders of other faiths to the Vatican.  教皇邀请其他宗教领袖到梵蒂冈，做出了一个基督教各派联合的姿态。|Synonyms: universal, global, worldwide, general, catholic|Antonyms: parochial, sectarian, denominational|adjective
