词源: From French chaperon, originally denoting a hood or hooded cape.  The sense of a protective escort developed later, perhaps from the image of the chaperon shielding a young woman.  来自法语 chaperon，最初表示兜帽或带兜帽的斗篷。保护性护送的含义是后来发展起来的，也许是来自陪伴人保护年轻女性的形象。
