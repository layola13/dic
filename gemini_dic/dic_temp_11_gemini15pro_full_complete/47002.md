dashel:/ˈdæʃəl/| n. |破折号，连接号|A horizontal stroke in writing or printing to mark a pause or break in sense or to represent omitted letters or words. |例句：Use a dashel to separate the two clauses.  用破折号分开这两个从句。

例句：Her name was Mary–Anne. 她的名字是玛丽-安妮。|近义词：hyphen, dash |反义词: (No direct antonym for a punctuation mark)|词性:noun
