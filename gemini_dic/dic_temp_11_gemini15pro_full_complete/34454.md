clavicles: /ˈklævɪkəlz/
n.
锁骨
The collarbones, technically known as clavicles, connect the sternum to the scapulae.
例句：
She fractured both her clavicles in the accident.  她在事故中摔断了双侧锁骨。

The clavicles are essential for shoulder movement. 锁骨对肩部运动至关重要。

近义词：collarbones
反义词：（无）
词性：名词
