isotherm:/ˈaɪsəθɜːrm/ /ˈaɪsoʊθɜːrm/ | n. |等温线|A line on a map connecting points having the same temperature at a given time or on average over a given period.|The isotherms on the map showed a steady increase in temperature as you moved south.|Synonyms: contour line (of temperature)|Antonyms: N/A|noun

isotherms:/ˈaɪsəθɜːrms/ /ˈaɪsoʊθɜːrms/ | n. |等温线（复数）|Plural form of isotherm, referring to multiple lines connecting points of equal temperature.|The meteorologist studied the isotherms to understand the movement of the warm front.|Synonyms: contour lines (of temperature)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
