befogs:/bɪˈfɒɡz/
verb
使模糊，使困惑
Obscures or confuses something, as if with fog.
The heavy rain befogs the windshield, making it difficult to see.  暴雨使挡风玻璃模糊，难以看清。
Dense fog befogged the harbor, delaying all shipping. 浓雾笼罩着港口，所有船只都延误了。
近义词: obscures, confuses, clouds, muddles
反义词: clarifies, elucidates

词性:v.

词源: From be- + fog, meaning "to cover with fog."  由be- + fog组成，意思是“用雾覆盖”。
