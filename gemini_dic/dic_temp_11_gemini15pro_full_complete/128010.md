nephrolithic:/ˌnɛfrəˈlɪθɪk/
adjective
肾结石的；与肾结石有关的
Relating to or characterized by kidney stones.
He experienced nephrolithic pain. 他经历了肾结石的疼痛。
The doctor diagnosed a nephrolithic condition. 医生诊断出肾结石病症。
近义词:renal, calculous
反义词:(None - describes a specific medical condition)
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From nephro- (Greek nephros, "kidney") + -lithic (Greek lithos, "stone").
