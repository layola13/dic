divertedly:/daɪˈvɜːrtɪdli/
|adv.|
|心不在焉地，转向地|
|in a way that shows that someone's attention is not fully on what they are doing|
|She listened to him divertedly, her mind elsewhere. 她心不在焉地听着他说话，心思飘到了别处。|
|absentmindedly, inattentively, distractedly|
|attentively, carefully|
