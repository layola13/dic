building: /ˈbɪldɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbɪldɪŋ/ (UK)| n.| 建筑物；房屋；大楼|A structure with walls and a roof, such as a house or factory.|The building is very tall. 这栋楼很高。|synonyms: structure, edifice, construction|antonyms: demolition, destruction|noun

building: /ˈbɪldɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbɪldɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.| 建筑的；建设的| Relating to the construction of buildings.|The building industry is booming. 建筑业正在蓬勃发展。|synonyms: construction, architectural|antonyms: destructive, demolition|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English "buildan" (to build, construct) and "-ing" (suffix forming nouns from verbs).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*bildaną" (to build).
