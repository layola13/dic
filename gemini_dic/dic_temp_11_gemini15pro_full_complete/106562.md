krummhorn:/ˈkrʌmˌhɔːrn/ | n. | 克鲁姆管; 一种古双簧木管乐器 | A capped double-reed woodwind instrument of the Renaissance, with a cylindrical bore and a curving, upturned end.  The krummhorn has a distinctive, somewhat reedy tone. | The krummhorn player joined the medieval music ensemble.  克鲁姆管演奏者加入了中世纪音乐合奏团。

| The krummhorn's sound is often described as buzzing. 克鲁姆管的声音通常被描述为嗡嗡声。 | 近义词:crumhorn,  cornamuse (similar instrument) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
