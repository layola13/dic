misguidednesses:/ˌmɪsˈɡaɪdɪdnɪsɪz/
|n.|误导，错误引导（复数）
|The state of being misguided; a misguided action or belief.  指被误导的状态；一个被误导的行为或信念。
|His misguidednesses led him down the wrong path. 他的错误引导使他误入歧途。
|近义词：errors, fallacies, misconceptions
|反义词：wisdom, prudence, guidance
|词性: noun

misguided:/ˌmɪsˈɡaɪdɪd/
|adj.|被误导的，错误的
|Incorrectly guided or directed; based on wrong information or bad judgment. 指导或引导错误的；基于错误信息或判断的。
|She had a misguided belief that she could fly. 她错误地认为自己能飞。
|近义词：erroneous, mistaken, wrong
|反义词：correct, right, sound
|词性: adjective

guide:/ɡaɪd/
|n. v.|指导，引导；向导
|A person who leads or directs other people on a journey or tour.  带领或指导他人旅行或参观的人。
|The guide led us through the forest. 向导带领我们穿过森林。
|近义词：lead, direct, instruct
|反义词：mislead, misdirect
|词性: noun, verb
