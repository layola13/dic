USAGE->
Simple Present:
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? = 他晚饭在做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? = 她在商店买了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么事？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? = 她参加聚会穿了什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you came here? = 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had they decided before the meeting? = 会议前他们决定了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I called? = 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about? = 他们一直在争论什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do this weekend? = 你这周末要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have finished by Friday? = 你到星期五会完成什么？
What will they have achieved by the end of the year? = 他们到年底会取得什么成就？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？


Subjunctive Mood:
If I knew what to do, I would tell you. = 如果我知道该怎么做，我会告诉你的。
I wish I knew what she was thinking. = 我希望我知道她在想什么。

Passive Voice:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在对这个问题采取什么措施？

Interrogative Sentences:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the summer? = 你暑假的计划是什么？

Negative Sentences:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what I'm saying. = 她不明白我在说什么。
