lakh:/læk/ |n.|十万; 拉克(印度、巴基斯坦等国的货币单位)|A lakh is a unit in the Indian numbering system equal to one hundred thousand.  It is often used to denote a sum of money. |The car cost five lakhs of rupees.|Synonym: one hundred thousand|Antonym: N/A|noun
