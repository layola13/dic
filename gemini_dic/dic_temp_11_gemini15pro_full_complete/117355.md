median: /ˈmiːdiən/ (US) /ˈmiːdɪən/ (UK) | n. adj. | 中位数，中间的 |The middle value in a list of numbers when they are arranged in order. Also, relating to or situated in the middle.| The median house price in this area is $500,000.  这个地区房屋价格的中位数是50万美元。| The median nerve runs down the arm. 正中神经沿着手臂向下延伸。 | synonyms: average, middle, central | antonyms: extreme, outer, edge | noun, adjective
