athletically:/æθˈlɛtɪkli/
adverb
运动地；强健地
in a way that relates to athletes or athletics; in a physically fit and strong way
He moves athletically. 他行动敏捷。
近义词:physically, actively, energetically, nimbly
反义词:clumsily, awkwardly, weakly
词性:adverb

词源: From athlete + -ic + -al + -ly. Ultimately from the Greek athletes "contestant in games," from athlos "contest," from athlein "to contend for a prize."
