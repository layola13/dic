macrocosmically:/ˌmækroʊˈkɒzmɪkli/
| adv. |宏观地，宇宙地|From a macrocosmic perspective, encompassing the whole universe or system.  Considering something in relation to the entire universe or a large system.|例句：Macrocosmically speaking, human beings are but a tiny speck in the vast expanse of the universe. 从宏观的角度来看，人类只是浩瀚宇宙中的一个小点。

例句：He viewed the problem macrocosmically, considering its implications for the entire global ecosystem. 他从宏观角度看待这个问题，考虑了它对整个全球生态系统的影响。|近义词：universally, globally, cosmically|反义词: microscopically, locally, narrowly|词性:副词
