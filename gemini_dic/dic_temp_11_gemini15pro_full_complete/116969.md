mbes:/ˈɛmˌbiːz/ | n. | 移动式海底测深系统 (Multibeam echosounder systems) | Multibeam echosounder systems (MBES) are a type of sonar that is used to map the seabed. They emit a fan-shaped pulse of sound waves and then measure the time it takes for the sound waves to return to the sonar. This information is then used to create a detailed map of the seabed. | The research vessel used MBES to map the ocean floor. 研究船使用多波束测深系统绘制海底地图。|  近义词: multibeam sonar, bathymetric sonar | 反义词: single beam echosounder | 词性: 名词
