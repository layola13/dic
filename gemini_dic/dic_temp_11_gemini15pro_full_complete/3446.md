afrikaners:/ˌæfrɪˈkɑːnərz/
n.
南非白人，布尔人
A person of Dutch or Huguenot descent born, raised, and living in South Africa; an Afrikaans-speaking South African of European ancestry.
The Afrikaners have a rich cultural heritage. 南非白人有着丰富的文化遗产。
Afrikaners are known for their resilience and strong sense of community.  南非白人以其韧性和强烈的社区意识而闻名。
synonyms: Boers
antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider non-Afrikaner South Africans)
