ceaselessly:/ˈsisləsli/ | adv. | 不停地，不断地 | Without stopping or pausing; constantly. | The river flows ceaselessly to the sea.  这条河流不停地流向大海。|  The machine worked ceaselessly throughout the night. 这台机器整夜不停地工作。 | 近义词: constantly, incessantly, perpetually, continually | 反义词: intermittently, occasionally, sporadically | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Middle English *ceselesli*, from Old French *cesselement*, from *cesser* (“to cease”) (from Latin *cessāre*), + *-ment* (from Latin *-mentum*).
