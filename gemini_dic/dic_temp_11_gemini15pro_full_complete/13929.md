aumbry:/ˈɑːmbri/ (US), /ˈɔːmbri/ (UK)| n. |圣餐柜，壁橱，碗橱|A cupboard or recess in a church wall, usually near the altar, used to store sacred vessels and vestments. Also, a general term for a cupboard, closet, or small room.|The priest retrieved the chalice from the aumbry.  牧师从圣餐柜里取出圣杯。|The family kept their valuable china in an aumbry in the dining room.  这家人把他们珍贵的瓷器放在餐厅的一个碗橱里。|Synonyms: cupboard, closet, locker, cabinet|Antonyms: open space|noun
