dematerialization:/ˌdiːməˌtɪəriələˈzeɪʃən/ |n.| 去物质化，非物质化 |The dematerialization of money has led to an increase in online transactions.  货币的去物质化导致在线交易的增加。| The company is exploring the dematerialization of share certificates.  该公司正在探索股票证书的非物质化。| 近义词：intangibility, immaterialization | 反义词：materialization, embodiment | 词性: 名词

dematerialize:/ˌdiːməˈtɪəriəlaɪz/ |v.| 去物质化，非物质化，消失 |The magician made the rabbit dematerialize. 魔术师让兔子消失了。|  Many physical processes, like burning, appear to dematerialize objects, but the matter is simply transformed. 许多物理过程，例如燃烧，似乎使物体去物质化，但物质只是被转化了。| 近义词：vanish, disappear, dissolve | 反义词：materialize, appear | 词性: 动词

词源:  From de- + materialize, from Late Latin materialis "material," from Latin materia "matter."  来自 de- + materialize，源于晚期拉丁语 materialis “物质的”，来自拉丁语 materia “物质”。
