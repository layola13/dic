aisleless:/ˈaɪl.ləs/| adj.|无通道的，没有过道的|Lacking aisles, as in a large open space or a building without internal dividing walls.|The aisleless warehouse allowed for flexible storage arrangements.  这个没有通道的仓库可以灵活安排存储。|Synonyms: open-plan, undivided|Antonyms: sectioned, partitioned|adjective
