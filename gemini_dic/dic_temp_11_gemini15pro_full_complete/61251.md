emancipate: /ɪˈmænsɪpeɪt/ | vt. | 解放，使摆脱 | To free someone from slavery, control, or the power of another person. |  Lincoln emancipated the slaves. 林肯解放了奴隶。

The new CEO emancipated the company from outdated traditions. 新任首席执行官使公司摆脱了过时的传统。

近义词: liberate, free, release, discharge

反义词: enslave, imprison, confine, restrict

词性: verb (动词)

词源: Late Middle English: emancipaten, from Latin emancipare ‘transfer ownership of’, from e- (variant of ex-) ‘out, from’ + mancipare ‘transfer ownership’, from manus ‘hand’ + capere ‘take’.
