cochampion:/ˌkoʊˈtʃæmpiən/
n.|共同冠军|A person or team that shares a championship with another.  Sharing the same title or honor.|例句：The two teams were declared co-champions after the final match ended in a tie.  两支队伍在决赛打成平手后被宣布为共同冠军。

例句：They were co-champions of the league last year. 去年他们是联赛的共同冠军。
|近义词：joint champion, co-winner|反义词:runner-up, loser|词性:名词
