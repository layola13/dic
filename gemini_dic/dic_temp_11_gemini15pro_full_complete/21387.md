blottesque: /blɒˈtɛsk/ | adj. | 墨迹般的，污点状的，(绘画风格)粗犷的，涂抹状的 |  Resembling a blot or stain; characterized by blotchy or smeared marks, especially in a painting style; crudely or sketchily done.  Derived from "blot" with the French suffix "-esque" meaning "in the style of." | The painting's blottesque style gave it a raw, unfinished feel. 这幅画的涂抹风格给它一种原始、未完成的感觉。 | The artist's blottesque brushstrokes evoked a sense of urgency and chaos. 艺术家墨迹般的笔触唤起了一种紧迫感和混乱感。 | synonyms: blotchy, smeared, smudged, sketchy, impressionistic | antonyms: precise, detailed, meticulous, refined, polished | adjective
