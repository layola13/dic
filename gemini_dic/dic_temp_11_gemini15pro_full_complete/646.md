abridger:/əˈbrɪdʒər/| n.|  缩写者；删节者| Someone who shortens a written work. |  The abridger condensed the 1000-page novel into a 300-page version.  这位缩写者将这本1000页的小说压缩成了300页的版本。|  condenser, summarizer | expander, elaborator | noun

abridge:/əˈbrɪdʒ/| vt.| 缩短；删节；节略| To shorten a written work without losing the sense. | They abridged the dictionary for use in schools. 他们为了学校使用而删节了这本词典。 | shorten, condense, curtail | expand, lengthen, elaborate | verb
