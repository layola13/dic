hri:/ˈeɪtʃɑːrˈaɪ/ |abbr.| human-robot interaction |人机交互|Human-robot interaction (HRI) is the study of interactions between humans and robots. It is often referred to as HRI by researchers. HRI is a multidisciplinary field with contributions from human-computer interaction, artificial intelligence, robotics, natural language understanding, design, and social sciences.| HRI research is crucial for developing robots that can seamlessly integrate into human environments.|近义词: human-machine interaction|反义词:  None (HRI describes a specific field; there isn't a direct opposite.)|noun (abbreviation)
