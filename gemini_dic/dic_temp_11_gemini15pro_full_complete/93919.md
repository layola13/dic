immune:/ɪˈmjuːn/ | adj. |免疫的，免除的|Protected from or resistant to a particular disease or infection; exempt or free from something unpleasant.|例句：He seems to be immune to colds. 他似乎对感冒免疫。|例句：The company is not immune to the effects of the recession. 这家公司也不能免受经济衰退的影响。|近义词：resistant, exempt, insusceptible|反义词：susceptible, vulnerable|词性:形容词

immune:/ɪˈmjuːn/ | n. |免疫者|A person who is immune to a particular disease.|例句：Immunes were rare during the outbreak. 在疫情爆发期间，免疫者很少。|例句：Researchers are studying the immunes to understand the disease better. 研究人员正在研究免疫者以更好地了解这种疾病。|近义词：resistant individual|反义词：susceptible individual|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *immunis* "exempt, free, not paying a share," from *in-* "not" (see in- (1)) + *munis* "performing services, obliged," related to *munus* "duty, service, gift, present; public spectacle, gladiatorial show."
