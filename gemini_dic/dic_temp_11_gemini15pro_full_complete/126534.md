nabobishly:/ˈnɑːbɑːbɪʃli/
adv.|像富豪一样地；奢侈地|In a manner characteristic of a nabob; luxuriously or ostentatiously.|She redecorated her apartment nabobishly, with imported silks and antique furniture. 她像富豪一样重新装修了她的公寓，用了进口丝绸和古董家具。|Synonym: lavishly, extravagantly, ostentatiously|Antonym: frugally, modestly, simply
