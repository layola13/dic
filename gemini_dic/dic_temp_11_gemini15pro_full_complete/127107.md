nationalize: /ˈnæʃənəlaɪz/ (UK), /ˈnæʃnəˌlaɪz/ (US) | vt.| 国有化 |To transfer (a major branch of industry or commerce) from private to state ownership or control. | The government nationalized the railways in 1948. 政府在1948年将铁路国有化。| The government decided to nationalize the failing bank to prevent a financial crisis. 政府决定将这家濒临倒闭的银行国有化，以防止金融危机。 | synonyms: socialize, expropriate,  | antonyms: privatize, denationalize | verb
