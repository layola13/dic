oligarchal: /ˌɒlɪˈɡɑːkɪkəl/ /ˌɑːlɪˈɡɑːrkɪkəl/ | adj. | 寡头政治的；由少数人统治的 | Relating to or characteristic of an oligarchy; governed by a small group of people. |  The country's oligarchal system concentrated power in the hands of a few wealthy families.  这个国家的寡头政治体制将权力集中在少数富裕家庭手中。

The oligarchal regime was overthrown by a popular uprising. 这个寡头政权被人民起义推翻了。 | 近义词：autocratic, plutocratic, aristocratic | 反义词：democratic, egalitarian | 词性: adjective

oligarchy: /ˈɒlɪɡɑːki/ /ˈɑːlɪɡɑːrki/ | n. | 寡头政治；寡头统治集团 | A small group of people having control of a country or organization. | The oligarchy controlled the country's resources and wealth.  寡头统治集团控制着国家的资源和财富。

Russia was accused of being ruled by a powerful oligarchy.  俄罗斯被指责由一个强大的寡头统治集团统治。| 近义词：clique, junta | 反义词：democracy | 词性: noun


词源: From Ancient Greek ὀλιγαρχία (oligarkhía, “rule by a few”), from ὀλίγος (olígos, “few”) + ἄρχω (árkhō, “to rule, command”).
