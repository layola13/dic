fanfiction:/ˌfænˈfɪkʃ(ə)n/
n.
同人小说
Fiction written by fans of a particular work (such as a movie, television series, or video game), using existing characters and settings.
She wrote a Harry Potter fanfiction about Hermione and Draco. 她写了一篇关于赫敏和德拉科的哈利波特同人小说。
fan fiction, fanwork
original fiction
noun
