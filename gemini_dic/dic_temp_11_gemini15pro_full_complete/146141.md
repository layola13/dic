overemulating:/ˌoʊvərˈɛmjʊleɪtɪŋ/
verb
过度模仿
Engaging in emulation to an excessive degree, often resulting in a negative outcome or a loss of originality.
He was overemulating his idol, copying every mannerism and even his hairstyle, which made him seem less authentic.  他过度模仿他的偶像，模仿每一个举止甚至发型，这让他看起来不真实。
By overemulating successful companies, startups can lose sight of their own unique strengths and market opportunities.  通过过度模仿成功的公司，初创企业可能会忽略自身独特的优势和市场机会。
近义词: overimitating, mimicking excessively, copying too much
反义词: innovating, originating, being original
词性: verb (present participle)
