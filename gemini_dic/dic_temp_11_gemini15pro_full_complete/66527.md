exhaustibility:/ɪɡˌzɔːstɪˈbɪləti/
n.|可耗尽性; 可穷尽性|The exhaustibility of natural resources is a serious concern. 自然资源的可耗尽性是一个严重的问题。|例句：The exhaustibility of fossil fuels is a major driver for renewable energy research. 化石燃料的可耗尽性是可再生能源研究的主要驱动力。|近义词：depletability, finiteness|反义词：inexhaustibility,  renewability|词性:名词
Etymology: From exhaust + -ibility.  "Exhaust" comes from Latin *exhaustus*, past participle of exhaurire "to draw out completely," from ex- "out" + haurire "to draw, drain."
