glyptodon:/ˈɡlɪptədɒn/ (US) /ˈɡlɪptədɒn/ (UK)| n. |雕齿兽|An extinct, heavily armored mammal, related to armadillos, that lived in North and South America during the Pleistocene epoch.|The glyptodon was a massive creature, some species reaching the size of a small car.|近义词:none (extinct animal, closest living relative is the armadillo)|反义词:none|词性:noun

etymology->
From Greek *gluptós* (γλυπτός) "carved, sculpted" + *odṓn* (ὀδών) "tooth".  Referring to the sculpted appearance of its teeth.
