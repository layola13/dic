benzonaphtol:/ˌbɛnzoʊˈnæfθɒl/ |n.|苯并萘酚; 萘酚苯甲酸酯|A crystalline organic compound, specifically 2-naphthyl benzoate. It is used as an antiseptic and in the manufacture of perfumes and lacquers. |Benzonaphtol was once used as an intestinal antiseptic. 苯并萘酚曾被用作肠道防腐剂。
