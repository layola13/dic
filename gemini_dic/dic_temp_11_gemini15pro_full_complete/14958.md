b-movie:/ˈbiː ˈmuːvi/| n. |B级片，低成本电影|A low-budget, often genre film, originally shown in addition to a main feature in a double bill.  These films often exploit sensational or topical subjects and are characterized by fast pacing, simple plots, and formulaic characters. |例句：We went to a drive-in and watched a double feature, including a classic b-movie about giant spiders. 我们去了一个汽车影院，看了一场双片连映，其中包括一部关于巨型蜘蛛的经典B级片。

例句：B-movies are often appreciated for their campy aesthetic and unintentional humor.  B级片通常因其夸张的审美和无意的幽默而受到赞赏。|近义词：exploitation film, low-budget film, genre film|反义词: A-movie, blockbuster|词性:noun
