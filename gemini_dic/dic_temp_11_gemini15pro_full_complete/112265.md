lopes:/ləʊps/ | n. |  (通常指动物的)大步慢跑，轻快地奔跑 | a long bounding stride. | The wolf's lopes carried it quickly across the field. 狼的大步慢跑使它迅速穿过田野。| 近义词：strides, bounds, runs | 反义词：crawls, creeps | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lopen (“to leap, run”), of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old Norse hlaupa (“to run, leap”).
