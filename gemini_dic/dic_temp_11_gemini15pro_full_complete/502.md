abodes:/əˈboʊdz/
noun
住所，居所
A place of residence; a house or home.
They made their abodes in caves. 他们以洞穴为家。
synonyms: dwellings, residences, homes, houses, domiciles
antonyms:  none (lack of a place to live could be homelessness, displacement)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English abode, abood, from Anglo-Norman abode, from aboder (“to reside, dwell”), from Vulgar Latin *abitare, from Latin habitāre (“to dwell, inhabit”), frequentative of habēre (“to have, hold, possess”).
