excipient:/ɛkˈsɪpiənt/ | n. |赋形剂；辅料|An inactive substance that serves as the vehicle or medium for a drug or other active substance.|The tablets contain the drug and various excipients.|Synonyms: additive, vehicle, base, medium|Antonyms: active ingredient, drug|noun
