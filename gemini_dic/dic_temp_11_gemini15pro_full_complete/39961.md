construe:/kənˈstruː/ /ˈkɑːnstruː/| vt.|解释，理解，分析|To interpret or understand the meaning of something, especially in a particular way. |She construed his silence as a sign of agreement. 她把他的沉默理解为同意。|
The lawyer construed the contract in favor of his client. 律师对合同作出了有利于其客户的解释。|
interpret, understand, analyze, decipher, explain|misinterpret, misunderstand, misconstrued|verb
