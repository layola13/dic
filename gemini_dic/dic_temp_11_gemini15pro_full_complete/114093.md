magnified:/ˈmæɡnɪfaɪd/
adj.
放大的，夸大的
made to appear larger than it actually is, either by a lens or metaphorically.
The image was magnified ten times. 图像被放大了十倍。
近义词:enlarged, amplified, exaggerated, heightened
反义词:reduced, diminished, minimized
词性:形容词

magnify:/ˈmæɡnɪfaɪ/
v.
放大，扩大，夸大
to make something appear larger than it is, especially with a lens or microscope; to make something seem more important or serious than it really is.
The microscope magnified the bacteria so we could see them. 显微镜放大了细菌，所以我们能看到它们。
近义词:enlarge, amplify, exaggerate, heighten
反义词:reduce, diminish, minimize
词性:动词

词源:
From Late Latin magnificāre, from Latin magnus (“great”) + -ficāre,  related to facere (“to make”).  Literally "to make great".
