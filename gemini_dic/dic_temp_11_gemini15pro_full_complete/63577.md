environmentalistic: /ɪnˌvaɪrənˌmɛntəlˈɪstɪk/
adjective
环境保护主义的
Relating to or characteristic of environmentalism; concerned with the protection of the environment.
例句：
She has a very environmentalistic approach to gardening, avoiding pesticides and using only organic methods.
她有一种非常环保的园艺方法，避免使用杀虫剂，只使用有机方法。

Her environmentalistic views led her to a career in conservation.
她的环保主义观点使她从事了自然保护事业。

近义词：green, eco-conscious, conservationist, eco-friendly
反义词：anti-environmental, unsustainable
