comanager:/ˌkoʊˈmænɪdʒər/ | n. |共同经理；联合经理|A person who shares the responsibility of managing something with another person or people.| Example: She and her husband are comanagers of the restaurant. 她和她丈夫是这家餐厅的共同经理。| Example:  The project has two comanagers to ensure its smooth execution.  这个项目有两名共同经理以确保其顺利执行。 |Synonyms: co-director, joint manager| Antonyms: subordinate, assistant | noun
