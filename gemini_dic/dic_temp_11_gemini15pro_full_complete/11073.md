archaeology:/ˌɑːrkiˈɒlədʒi/ (US) /ˌɑːrkiˈɑːlədʒi/| n. |考古学|The study of human history and prehistory through the excavation of sites and the analysis of artifacts and other physical remains.|例句：Archaeology can reveal a lot about past civilizations. 考古学可以揭示很多关于过去文明的信息。

例句：She studied archaeology at university. 她在大学学习考古学。

近义词：paleontology, anthropology|反义词: - |词性: noun

archaeological:/ˌɑːrkiəˈlɒdʒɪkl/ (US) /ˌɑːrkiəˈlɑːdʒɪkl/| adj. |考古学的|Relating to the study of human history and prehistory through the excavation of sites and the analysis of artifacts and other physical remains.|例句：The archaeological dig unearthed several Roman artifacts. 考古发掘出土了几件罗马文物。

例句：They made an important archaeological discovery. 他们有了一项重要的考古发现。

近义词：paleontological, anthropological|反义词: - |词性: adjective
