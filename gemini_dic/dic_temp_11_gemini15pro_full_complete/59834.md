ectolecithal:/ˌɛktoʊˈlɛsɪθəl/
adjective
外卵黄的
Having the yolk concentrated at the surface of the egg, as in some arthropods.  The yolk is external to the embryo.
例句：Ectolecithal eggs are characteristic of certain arthropods.  某些节肢动物的卵是外卵黄的。
例句：The ectolecithal arrangement of the yolk allows for a specific type of development.  卵黄的外卵黄排列允许特定类型的发育。
近义词：centrolecithal (somewhat)
反义词：endolecithal
词性：adjective
