nonextinctions:/ˌnɒnɪkˈstɪŋkʃənz/ | n. | 非灭绝，未灭绝 | The study of nonextinctions focuses on species that have persisted despite challenges.  这项研究的重点是那些尽管面临挑战依然存在的物种。 |  The conservation efforts contributed to the nonextinctions of several endangered bird species. 保护工作促成了几种濒危鸟类的非灭绝。| 近义词: survival, persistence | 反义词: extinction, annihilation | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "extinction" (noun derived from Latin "extinctus," past participle of "extinguere," meaning "to extinguish").
