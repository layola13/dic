cooperativeness:/kəʊˈɒpərətɪvnəs/| n. |合作性，协作性|The quality of being willing to work together with others.|Her cooperativeness made the project a success. 她的合作精神使项目取得了成功。|Synonyms: collaboration, teamwork, willingness, helpfulness|Antonyms: uncooperativeness, reluctance, resistance|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From cooperative + -ness.  "Cooperative" comes from the Latin "co-" (together) and "operari" (to work).
