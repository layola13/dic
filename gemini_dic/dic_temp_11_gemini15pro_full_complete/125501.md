multirecreational:/ˌmʌltiˌrɛkriˈeɪʃənəl/
adjective
多娱乐的，多种休闲的
Combining multiple forms of recreation or entertainment.  Designed or used for multiple recreational activities.
This multirecreational facility offers swimming, bowling, and a movie theater all under one roof. 这个多功能娱乐设施在一个屋檐下提供游泳、保龄球和电影院。
The park is a multirecreational area with trails for hiking and biking, a lake for swimming and boating, and fields for various sports.  这个公园是一个多功能休闲区，有徒步旅行和骑自行车的步道，一个游泳和划船的湖，以及用于各种运动的场地。
近义词:versatile, multipurpose, diverse
反义词:specialized, single-purpose, limited
