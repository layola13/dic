judoists:/ˈdʒuːdoʊɪsts/

The judoists bowed to each other before the match.  柔道运动员在比赛前互相鞠躬。
The judoists trained diligently for the upcoming tournament.  柔道运动员们为即将到来的比赛刻苦训练。
