half-silk:/ˌhæf ˈsɪlk/ | adj. | 半丝绸的；仿丝的 | Made of a mixture of silk and another fiber, typically cotton or rayon.  A fabric resembling silk but made from less expensive materials. |  She wore a beautiful half-silk dress to the party. 她穿着一件漂亮的半丝绸连衣裙去参加派对。 | The curtains were made of a shimmering half-silk material. 窗帘是用一种闪闪发光的仿丝材料做的。 | Near synonyms: imitation silk, artificial silk | Antonym: pure silk | Adjective
