emancipators:/ɪˈmænsɪpeɪtərz/
|n.|解放者|People who free others from slavery or oppression.  Those who advocate for liberation.|Abraham Lincoln is considered one of the greatest emancipators in American history. 亚伯拉罕·林肯被认为是美国历史上最伟大的解放者之一。|liberators, deliverers|oppressors, enslavers|noun

emancipate:/ɪˈmænsɪpeɪt/
|v.|解放|To free someone from slavery or oppression. To liberate.|The Emancipation Proclamation emancipated enslaved people in the Confederate states.  《解放黑奴宣言》解放了邦联的奴隶。|free, liberate, release|enslave, oppress, restrict|verb
