hemostat:/ˈhiːməˌstæt/ (US), /ˈhiːməstæt/ (UK)| n.|止血钳，止血器|A surgical instrument used to clamp blood vessels to stop bleeding.|The surgeon used a hemostat to control the bleeding during the operation. 外科医生在手术过程中使用止血钳来控制出血。|

hemostatic:/ˌhiːməˈstætɪk/ (US), /ˌhiːməˈstætɪk/ (UK)| adj.|止血的|Having the property of stopping bleeding.|The hemostatic properties of the drug helped to control the bleeding. 这种药物的止血特性有助于控制出血。|
