manurable:/məˈnʊərəbl/ |adj.|可施肥的；可耕种的|Able to be fertilized or cultivated; capable of being worked or tilled (as land).|The farmer found the soil to be surprisingly manurable after the rains. 雨后，农民发现土壤出奇地适宜耕种。|The land is rich and manurable. 这片土地肥沃，适宜耕种。|近义词: arable, cultivable, tillable|反义词: barren, infertile, unproductive|词性:形容词

词源:  From  "manure" (肥料) + "-able" (可...的) ultimately from Latin "manu operare" (to work by hand).
