dissepiment:/dɪˈsɛpɪmənt/ | n. | 隔膜，隔板 | A dividing wall or partition, especially in a plant or animal structure. | The dissepiment between the two chambers of the heart is crucial for proper blood flow. 心脏两个腔室之间的隔膜对于正常的血液流动至关重要。

The dissepiments in a coral help to strengthen its structure. 珊瑚中的隔膜有助于加强其结构。 | 近义词: partition, septum, divider | 反义词: connection, opening | noun
