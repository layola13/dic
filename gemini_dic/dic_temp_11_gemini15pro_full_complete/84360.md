halicarnassus:/ˌhælɪkɑːrˈnæsəs/
proper noun
哈利卡纳苏斯
An ancient Greek city in southwestern Asia Minor (modern Bodrum, Turkey), famous as the site of the Mausoleum, one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.  It was also the birthplace of the Greek historians Herodotus and Dionysius.
Herodotus was born in Halicarnassus.  希罗多德出生在哈利卡纳苏斯。
The Mausoleum at Halicarnassus was a magnificent tomb. 哈利卡纳苏斯的摩索拉斯王陵墓是一座宏伟的陵墓。
synonym:Bodrum (modern name)
antonym:N/A
noun
