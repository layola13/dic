abietic:/ˌeɪbiˈɛtɪk/| adj.|松香的，枞酸的|Relating to, derived from, or resembling resin, especially the resin of conifers like fir trees.  Characterized by or containing abietic acid.| Abietic acid is a major component of rosin, a substance extracted from pine trees.  松香酸是松香的主要成分，松香是从松树中提取的物质。|Synonyms: resinous, resiny|Antonyms: non-resinous|adjective
