nonundergraduate:/ˌnɒnˌʌndəˈɡrædʒuət/
noun
非本科生
A student who is not pursuing an undergraduate degree, typically a graduate student.
Nonundergraduate students are not allowed to register for undergraduate courses. 非本科生不允许注册本科课程。
The library has a separate section for nonundergraduate research materials.  图书馆有一个单独的区域用于非本科生研究资料。
Synonyms: graduate student, postgraduate student
Antonyms: undergraduate, undergraduate student
词性: 名词
