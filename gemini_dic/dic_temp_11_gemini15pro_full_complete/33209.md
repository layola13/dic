chronically: /ˈkrɒnɪkli/ (US) /ˈkrɑːnɪkli/ (UK)| adv.|慢性地，长期地|In a chronic manner; persistently; habitually.  Characterized by long duration or frequent recurrence, especially of illness.|He is chronically late for work. 他上班总是迟到。|近义词: constantly, persistently, habitually, invariably|反义词: acutely, briefly, temporarily|词性:副词
Etymology: From Late Latin chronicus, from Greek khronikos "of time," from khronos "time."
