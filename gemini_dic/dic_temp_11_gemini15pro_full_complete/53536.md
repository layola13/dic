disavowal:/ˌdɪsəˈvaʊəl/| n.|否认，否定，拒绝承认|The suspect issued a strong disavowal of the charges against him. 嫌疑人强烈否认对他的指控。|Example: His disavowal of the debt angered his creditors.  他否认债务激怒了他的债权人。

Example: The government issued a disavowal of any involvement in the scandal. 政府发表声明否认卷入这桩丑闻。

|Synonyms: denial, repudiation, rejection, disclaimer|Antonyms: acknowledgement, acceptance, admission, affirmation|noun
