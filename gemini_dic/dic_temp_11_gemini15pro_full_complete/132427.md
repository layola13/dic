nondependencies:/ˌnɒndɪˈpɛndənsiz/| n.|非依赖性，独立性|The system boasts nondependencies, meaning it can operate without external support.|该系统以其非依赖性而自豪，这意味着它可以在没有外部支持的情况下运行。|例句：The modular design ensures nondependencies between different components.  模块化设计确保了不同组件之间的非依赖性。

例句：Achieving financial nondependencies is a key goal for many young adults. 实现经济独立是许多年轻人的主要目标。|近义词：independence, autonomy, self-sufficiency|反义词：dependence, reliance|词性:名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "dependencies" (plural of "dependency").  "Dependency" comes from the Late Latin "dependentia,"  meaning "a hanging down," from "dependere" ("to hang down").  Over time, the meaning shifted to reliance or being contingent on something else.
