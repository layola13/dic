absorption:/əbˈzɔːrpʃən/ |n.|吸收; 吸收过程; 全神贯注; 专心|The absorption of water by the soil. 土壤对水的吸收。|例句：The artist's complete absorption in his work. 艺术家对他的作品全神贯注。|例句：Vitamin D is necessary to aid the absorption of calcium from food. 维生素D有助于人体从食物中吸收钙。|近义词：intake, uptake, assimilation, incorporation|反义词：release, emission, excretion|词性：名词

词源: From Late Latin absorptionem, accusative of absorptio (“a swallowing up”), from absorbere (“to swallow up”), from ab- (“from, away from”) + sorbere (“to suck in, absorb”).
