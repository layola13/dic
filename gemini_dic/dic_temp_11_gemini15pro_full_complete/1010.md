acaulose: /eɪˈkɔːloʊs/ | adj. | 无茎的，无柄的 | Lacking a stem or stalk.  Specifically referring to parts of plants, such as leaves or flowers, that grow directly from the root or other main part of the plant, without a supporting stalk.  | The acaulose violets bloomed directly from the ground.  这些无茎紫罗兰直接从地面开花。 | The dandelion produces an acaulose flower head. 蒲公英开出无茎的花头。| sessile, stalkless | stemmed, stalked | adjective
