muzzinesses:/ˈmʌzinɪsɪz/ | n. | 模糊，迷糊，昏沉 | The state of being muzzy; a lack of clarity or sharpness. |  After the surgery, she experienced periods of muzzinesses and disorientation. 手术后，她经历了一段时间的迷糊和方向感丧失。| Synonyms: fuzziness, haziness, blurriness, cloudiness, indistinctness | Antonyms: clarity, sharpness, distinctness, lucidity | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From muzzy + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  Muzzy itself comes from the earlier dialectal mozzy, perhaps related to Old English *mosig, "boggy, marshy," suggesting a sense of mental fogginess.
