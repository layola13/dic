imaginative:/ɪˈmædʒɪnətɪv/ (US) /ɪˈmædʒɪnətɪv/ (UK)| adj.|富有想象力的；爱想象的|Having or showing a good imagination.|例句：She's a very imaginative child, always making up stories. 她是一个充满想象力的孩子，总是编故事。|近义词：creative, inventive, visionary|反义词：unimaginative, dull, prosaic|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *imaginativus, from Latin imaginari ("to imagine, conceive, picture to oneself") + -ivus.
