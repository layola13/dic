fluffy:/ˈflʌfi/| adj.|毛茸茸的，蓬松的|Covered with soft fur or feathers; light and airy.|The kitten was small and fluffy. 小猫又小又毛茸茸的。|近义词：downy, furry, soft, feathery|反义词:smooth, flat, hard|词性:形容词
Etymology: From fluff + -y.  Fluff itself is likely imitative in origin, referring to the sound or appearance of light, airy material.
