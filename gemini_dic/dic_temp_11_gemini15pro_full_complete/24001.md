breathings:/ˈbriːðɪŋz/| n. |呼吸;  呼吸的动作或方式|The patient's breathings were shallow and rapid. 病人的呼吸浅而急促。|Synonyms: respiration, inhalation, exhalation|Antonyms: suffocation, asphyxiation|noun

Etymology: From Middle English *brething, from Old English brēþung (“breathing, exhalation”), from brēþan (“to breathe”).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *brēþaną (“to blow, smell”).
