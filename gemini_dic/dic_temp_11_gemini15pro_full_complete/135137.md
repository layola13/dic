nonoffer:/ˌnɒnˈɒfər/ |n.| 非报价；未提出要约|An expression indicating something is not a formal offer, often used in legal or business contexts to avoid creating a binding agreement.| We made a nonoffer, just a suggestion.  我们只是提了个建议，并非正式报价。|Synonyms: suggestion, proposal (informal), indication |Antonyms: offer, bid, proposition|noun
