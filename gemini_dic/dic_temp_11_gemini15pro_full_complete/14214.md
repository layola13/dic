autochthonism: /ɔːˈtɒkθənɪzəm/ (UK) /ˌɔːˈtɑːkθənɪzəm/ (US) | n. |本土性；土著性；原住民性|The state of being autochthonous; the quality of being indigenous rather than descended from migrants or colonists.  The belief in autochthonism can be a powerful force in nationalist movements. |  The museum exhibit highlighted the autochthonism of the region's artistic traditions.  这个博物馆的展览突出了该地区艺术传统的本土性。 |  indigeneity, nativeness, aboriginality | exoticism, foreignness | noun
