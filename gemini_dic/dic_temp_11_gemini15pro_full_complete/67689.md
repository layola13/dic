exudation:/ˌɛksjʊˈdeɪʃən/ | n. |渗出, 渗出物|The process of exuding; the oozing of a fluid, such as pus or serum, through pores or incisions, or from a diseased structure.|The exudation of sap from the tree was a sign of disease. 树液的渗出是疾病的迹象。|近义词：discharge, secretion, oozing, seepage|反义词: absorption, retention|词性: noun

exudate:/ˈɛksjʊdeɪt/ | n. |渗出物|A substance that has oozed forth, as from cuts or sores.|The wound was covered in a yellowish exudate. 伤口上覆盖着淡黄色的渗出物。|近义词：discharge, secretion|反义词: None|词性: noun

exude:/ɪɡˈz(j)uːd/ | v. |渗出, 流露|To discharge or emit (a liquid or gas) slowly through pores or incisions; to display (a quality or feeling) strongly and openly.|The pine tree exuded sticky resin. 松树渗出粘稠的树脂。  He exuded confidence. 他流露出自信。|近义词：ooze, seep, emanate, radiate|反义词: absorb|词性: verb
