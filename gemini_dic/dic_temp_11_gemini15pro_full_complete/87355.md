heroicomical:/ˌhɛroʊɪˈkɒmɪkəl/| adj.|英雄喜剧的，滑稽英勇的|A heroicomical story or character combines elements of heroism and comedy, often by presenting a heroic figure in a humorous or satirical way. It can also describe a situation that is both brave and ridiculous.|The play was a heroicomical portrayal of a bumbling knight who somehow managed to save the kingdom.|近义词：mock-heroic, burlesque, satirical|反义词：serious, tragic|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From "heroico-" (combining form of "heroic") + "comical."  "Heroic" ultimately derives from the Greek "hērōs" (ἥρως), meaning "hero," while "comical" comes from the Latin "comicus," related to "cōmoedia" (comedy) and ultimately the Greek "kōmōidia" (κωμῳδία).
