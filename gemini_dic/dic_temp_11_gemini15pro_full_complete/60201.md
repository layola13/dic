efflagitate: /ˌɛfləˈdʒɪteɪt/
verb
恳求；迫切请求
To demand or request earnestly or persistently.
He efflagitated the king for a pardon. 他恳求国王赦免他。
Synonyms: beseech, implore, importune, supplicate
Antonyms: demand, command, order
词性: 动词
