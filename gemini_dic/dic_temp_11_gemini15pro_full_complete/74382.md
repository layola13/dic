frame-ups:/ˈfreɪmˌʌps/
n.|陷害，诬陷|The act of framing someone; a false accusation or setup designed to make someone appear guilty of a crime they did not commit.|He claimed the charges were a frame-up. 他声称这些指控是诬陷。|setup, conspiracy, fabrication|exoneration, acquittal|noun

frame-up:/ˈfreɪmˌʌps/
n.|（电影、电视等的）画面，帧|A single image in a film or video.|The frame-up showed the suspect entering the building. 画面显示嫌疑人进入了大楼。|shot, scene, still|N/A|noun
