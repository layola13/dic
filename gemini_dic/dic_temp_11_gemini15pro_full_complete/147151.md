overlords:/ˈoʊvərlɔːrdz/
n.
霸主，最高统治者
A ruler, especially one who has or claims supreme power.
The overlords of the galactic empire demanded absolute obedience.  银河帝国的霸主要求绝对服从。

Overlords exploited the local population for their own gain. 霸主们为了自己的利益剥削当地居民。

近义词:rulers, masters, dictators, tyrants
反义词:subjects, servants, subordinates
词性:名词

overlord:/ˈoʊvərlɔːrd/
n.
霸主，最高统治者
A ruler, especially one who has or claims supreme power.
The overlord demanded tribute from the conquered territories. 霸主要求被征服的领土进贡。

He acted like an overlord, dictating every aspect of our lives. 他像个霸主一样，支配着我们生活的方方面面。

近义词:ruler, master, dictator, tyrant
反义词:subject, servant, subordinate
词性:名词

词源: From Old English *oferhlaford (unattested), from ofer (“over”) + hlaford (“lord”). Cognate with Old Norse yfirherr (“overlord”). Literally "over-lord".
