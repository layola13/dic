necropsy: /ˈnekrɑpsi/ (US), /ˈnekrɒpsi/ (UK) | n. | 尸检，尸体解剖 | An examination of a dead body to determine the cause of death. |  The necropsy revealed that the animal died from heart failure. 尸检显示这只动物死于心力衰竭。

The necropsy confirmed the presence of a brain tumor. 尸检证实了脑瘤的存在。
