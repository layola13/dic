hydraulical:/haɪˈdrɔːlɪkəl/ | adj. | 水力的，液压的 | Operated by, moved by, or employing liquid (water or oil) under pressure. |  例句：Hydraulical engineering is a challenging field. 水利工程是一个具有挑战性的领域。

例句：The hydraulical system in the car failed. 汽车的液压系统失灵了。 | 近义词：hydraulic | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *hydraulicus, from Greek hydraulikos (“of or for a water organ”), from hydr-, the stem of hydōr (“water”) + aulos (“pipe, tube”).
