depulped:/diːˈpʌlpt/
adjective
去果肉的，去果皮的
Having had the pulp removed.
The depulped coffee beans were spread out to dry. 去果肉的咖啡豆被摊开晾干。
近义词: husked, shelled, pitted
反义词: unpulped
