maneuverable:/məˈnuːvərəbl/ /məˈnuːvrəbl/
adjective
易操纵的；灵活的
Easy to steer or control; readily handled.
The car is small and maneuverable, ideal for city driving. 这辆车小巧灵活，非常适合在城市里驾驶。

The new fighter jet is highly maneuverable.  这架新型战斗机非常灵活。


近义词:agile, nimble, controllable, steerable
反义词:clumsy, awkward, unwieldy, unmanageable
词性:adjective
