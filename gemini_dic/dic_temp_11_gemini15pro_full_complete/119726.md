midsummers:/ˈmɪdˌsʌmərz/ | n. |仲夏, 仲夏节|Midsummer refers to the period of time centered upon the summer solstice. |The midsummers festival is celebrated in many European countries. 仲夏节在许多欧洲国家都有庆祝活动。| Midsummer's Eve is a magical night. 仲夏夜是一个神奇的夜晚。 | synonyms: summer solstice, midsummer's day | antonyms: midwinter | noun
