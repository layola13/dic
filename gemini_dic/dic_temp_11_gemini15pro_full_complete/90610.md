humanate: /ˈhjuːməneɪt/ | n. | 人酸盐；腐植酸盐 | A salt of humic acid.  | Example: The gardener used a humanate soil amendment to improve drainage. 园丁使用腐植酸盐土壤改良剂来改善排水。 | Example: Humanate is often used in agriculture to improve soil health. 腐植酸盐常用于农业以改善土壤健康。 | 近义词: humate (often used interchangeably) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, potentially a non-humanate soil amendment) | 词性: noun

词源:  Derived from "humic acid" + "-ate" (suffix indicating a salt).  Ultimately from Latin "humus" (earth, soil).
