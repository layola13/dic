fibrousness:/ˈfaɪbrəsnəs/| n.|纤维性；纤维化；多纤维|The state or quality of being fibrous; containing or resembling fibers.|The fibrousness of the plant makes it difficult to digest. 这种植物的纤维性使它难以消化。|近义词：stringiness, toughness, texture|反义词: smoothness, softness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From fibrous + -ness.  Fibrous comes from Late Middle English, from Latin fibrōsus “fibrous,” from fibra “fiber” + -ōsus.
