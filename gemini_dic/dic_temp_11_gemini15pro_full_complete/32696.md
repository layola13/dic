chloranil: /ˈklɔːrənɪl/ | n. | 四氯对苯醌; 四氯苯醌 | a bright-yellow crystalline compound used as a fungicide, vulcanizing agent, and dehydrogenating agent. | Chloranil is used in the production of certain dyes. 四氯对苯醌用于生产某些染料。| Chloranil can be a skin irritant. 四氯对苯醌可能刺激皮肤。 |Synonyms: tetrachloro-p-benzoquinone, tetrachloroquinone | Antonyms: None | noun
