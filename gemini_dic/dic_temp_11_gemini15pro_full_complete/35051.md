closehauled:/kləʊsˈhɔːld/ | adj. | 逆风航行，迎风航行 |  Sailing as close to the wind as possible. A vessel is close-hauled when sailing as close to the wind as possible with the sails trimmed in tight. | The yacht was close-hauled, struggling against the strong headwind. 帆船逆风航行，与强劲的逆风搏斗。| The captain ordered the crew to close-haul the sails. 船长命令船员迎风航行。 | 近义词: by the wind, close-winded | 反义词: running, downwind | 词性: 形容词
