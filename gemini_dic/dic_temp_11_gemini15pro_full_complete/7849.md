Antarctica: /ænˈtɑːrktɪkə/ /ænˈtɑːrkdə/ | n. |南极洲|A continent surrounding the South Pole, almost entirely covered by ice.|例句：Scientists are studying the effects of climate change in Antarctica. 科学家们正在研究气候变化对南极洲的影响。

例句：Penguins are native to Antarctica.  企鹅原产于南极洲。


近义词：South Pole (although this refers specifically to the geographical point, not the continent)|反义词:Arctic|词性:noun
