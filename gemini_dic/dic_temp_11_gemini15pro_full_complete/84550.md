hammer-out:/ˈhæmər aʊt/
verb
锤炼出；敲定；商定；详细讨论后达成(协议)
To discuss something thoroughly, usually something difficult, until everyone agrees or a solution is reached.
The two sides finally hammered out a peace agreement after weeks of negotiations.  双方经过数周的谈判，最终敲定了一项和平协议。
After hours of debate, they hammered out a compromise.  经过数小时的辩论，他们终于达成妥协。
近义词:  work out, thrash out, iron out, negotiate, forge
反义词:  abandon, give up, drop
