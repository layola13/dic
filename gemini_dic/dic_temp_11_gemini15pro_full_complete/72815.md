foldings:/ˈfoʊldɪŋz/
n.
折叠；褶皱；折叠物
the act of folding something, or the way in which it is folded; something that is folded
The intricate foldings of the origami crane were impressive.  这只纸鹤复杂的折叠令人印象深刻。

近义词:creases, pleats, wrinkles
反义词:unfolding, straightening

词性: noun
