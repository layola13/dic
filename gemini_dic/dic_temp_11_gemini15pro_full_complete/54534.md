disloyal:/dɪsˈlɔɪəl/
adj.
不忠的，不忠诚的
Not loyal; faithless.
He was accused of being disloyal to the company. 他被指控对公司不忠。
近义词：treacherous, faithless, unfaithful, perfidious
反义词：loyal, faithful, true
词性：形容词

Etymology:
From dis- "not" + loyal.  The word "loyal" comes from Old French "loial," ultimately from Latin "lex" (law).
