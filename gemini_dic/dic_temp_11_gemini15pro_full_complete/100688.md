intracapsular:/ɪntrəˈkæpsjʊlər/
adjective
囊内的；关节囊内的
Located or occurring within a capsule, especially within the articular capsule of a joint.
An intracapsular fracture of the femoral neck.  股骨颈囊内骨折。
近义词: endosteal, subperiosteal
反义词: extracapsular
词性: adjective
