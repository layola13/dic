bogy:/ˈboʊɡi/ (US), /ˈbəʊɡi/ (UK)| n.| 鬼怪，妖怪，吓人的东西|A bogy is an imaginary evil spirit or being, often used to frighten children.  It can also refer to a source of fear or anxiety.|例句：The children were scared of the bogy man in the closet. 孩子们害怕壁橱里的妖怪。

例句：High taxes are a bogy for small businesses.  高税收是小企业的梦魇。|近义词：ghost, goblin, monster, phantom, specter, apparition, bugbear|反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider: comfort, reassurance)|词性: noun
