nondesisting:/ˌnɒndɪˈsɪstɪŋ/ | adj. | 不停的，持续的 |  Continuously or persistently occurring without ceasing; unceasing.  It describes something that continues without stopping.  The prefix "non-" negates the verb "desist," which means "to stop." | The nondesisting rain caused the river to overflow. 不停的雨导致河水泛滥。|  continuous, incessant, persistent, unrelenting, unremitting | ceasing, intermittent, sporadic | adjective
