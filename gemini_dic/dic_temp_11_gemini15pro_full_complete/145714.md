overcovetousness:/ˌoʊvərˈkʌvɪtəsnəs/| n.|过度贪婪，贪得无厌|An excessive or inordinate desire for wealth or possessions; greed.|His overcovetousness led him to cheat his own family.|近义词：greed, avarice, cupidity|反义词：generosity, contentment|词性:名词

词源: From "over" (excessive) + "covetousness" (desire for possessions).  "Covetousness" comes from the Old French "coveitise," ultimately derived from the Latin "cupiditas," meaning "desire" or "passion."
