overimitativeness:/ˌoʊvərɪmɪˈteɪtɪvnɪs/
n.
过度模仿
The excessive imitation of another person's actions, especially by children.  Children often display overimitativeness, copying even unnecessary actions.
例句：The child's overimitativeness led him to repeat every gesture the adult made, even though some were clearly irrelevant to the task.  这个孩子的过度模仿让他重复了大人做的每一个动作，即使有些动作显然与任务无关。

例句：Researchers are studying the role of overimitativeness in children's social and cognitive development.  研究人员正在研究过度模仿在儿童社会和认知发展中的作用。

近义词：mimicry, imitation, emulation
反义词：independence, originality
词性：noun
