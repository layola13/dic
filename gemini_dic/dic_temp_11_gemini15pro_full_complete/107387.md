lame:/leɪm/| adj.|跛的，瘸的；站不住脚的，无说服力的|Physically weak, especially in the foot or leg; unconvincing or unsatisfactory.|例句：The horse went lame after the race. 赛后那匹马瘸了。

例句：He gave a lame excuse for being late. 他为迟到找了个蹩脚的借口。|近义词：crippled, weak, unconvincing, feeble|反义词：strong, convincing, sound, robust|词性:形容词

lames:/leɪmz/| n.|跛子；瘸子|(plural of lame) People or animals that are lame.|例句：The shepherd separated the lames from the flock. 牧羊人把瘸腿的羊从羊群中分了出来。

例句：The hospital ward was full of lames recovering from injuries. 医院病房里住满了正在康复的瘸子。|近义词：cripples, invalids|反义词：healthy individuals|词性:名词
