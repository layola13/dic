glyconic:/ɡlaɪˈkɒnɪk/
adjective
糖酸的; 葡萄糖酸的
Relating to or derived from any of the glyconic acids.  Formed by oxidation of an aldose at C-1 to yield a carboxylic acid.
Gluconic acid is a mild organic acid derived from glucose.  葡萄糖酸是一种由葡萄糖衍生的温和有机酸。
Glyconic acid is used as a food additive. 葡萄糖酸被用作食品添加剂。
Synonyms:  (none direct, but related to sugar acids) aldonic
Antonyms:  (none direct)
