hysteretic:/ˌhɪstəˈrɛtɪk/ | adj. | 磁滞的，迟滞的 | Exhibiting hysteresis; lagging in response.  Relating to or characterized by hysteresis. | The material exhibited hysteretic behavior under stress. 这种材料在压力下表现出磁滞行为。 | Examples:
The hysteretic properties of the material make it suitable for use in dampeners.  这种材料的迟滞特性使其适用于减震器。

The system displayed hysteretic damping, which helped to reduce vibrations. 该系统显示出迟滞阻尼，有助于减少振动。

| Synonyms: lagging, delayed, slow, retarding | Antonyms: immediate, instantaneous, prompt | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hysterēsis* "shortcoming, deficiency, a coming too late,"  from *hysteros* "later"  + *-sis* noun suffix.  Originally used in physics to describe the lagging of a magnetic effect behind its cause.
