elderflowers:/ˈɛldərˌflaʊərz/
noun
接骨木花
The flowers of the elder tree, used in making cordials, wines, and teas.
Elderflowers have a delicate, sweet scent and are often used in summer drinks. 接骨木花有着精致的甜香味，常用于夏季饮品中。
Elderflower cordial is a refreshing summer drink.  接骨木花露水是一种清爽的夏日饮品。
Synonyms: elder blossoms
