iguanas:/ɪˈɡwɑːnəz/
n.
鬣蜥
Large lizards native to tropical areas of Central and South America and the Caribbean.
The iguanas basked in the sun on the rocks. 鬣蜥在岩石上晒太阳。
近义词:lizards
反义词:(No direct antonym for a specific animal)
词性:noun
