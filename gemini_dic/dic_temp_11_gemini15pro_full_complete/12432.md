askew:/əˈskjuː/| adj.|歪斜的， کج的|Not straight; at an angle.|Her hat was askew on her head. 她的帽子戴歪了。|The picture hung askew on the wall. 画歪挂在墙上。|近义词:awry, crooked, lopsided, tilted|反义词:straight, level, even|词性:形容词
词源:From a- + skew (Old North French eskiu "oblique").  Ultimately from a Germanic source related to "shy, shun".  Originally meant "to shy away from, avoid."
