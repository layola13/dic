nonrefracting:/ˌnɒnriˈfræktɪŋ/
adjective
非折射的
Not refracting; not causing refraction of light, sound, or other waves.
The nonrefracting material allowed the light to pass straight through.  这种非折射材料允许光直接穿过。
The scientist studied the properties of the nonrefracting lens. 科学家研究了非折射透镜的特性。
近义词: non-refractive
反义词: refracting, refractive
词性: adjective
