irrepentance:/ˌɪrɪˈpɛntəns/| n. |不悔改，顽固，执迷不悟|Lack of repentance; absence of regret or remorse for wrongdoing.|His irrepentance for his crimes shocked the jury. 他对罪行毫无悔意，震惊了陪审团。|近义词：impenitence, remorselessness, obduracy|反义词: repentance, remorse, penitence|词性:名词

词源:
From ir- (not) + repentance (from Old French repentence, from repentir "to repent").
