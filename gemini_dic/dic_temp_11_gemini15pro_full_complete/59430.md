earthnut:/ˈɜːθnʌt/ | n. |花生；块茎植物|A plant with an edible tuber or nutlike root, especially a peanut or a groundnut.|例句：The earthnut is a valuable source of protein. 花生是宝贵的蛋白质来源。

例句：Earthnuts are grown in warm climates. 花生生长在温暖的气候中。|近义词：peanut, groundnut, goober|反义词:  (没有直接的反义词)|词性: 名词


词源:  From earth + nut.  Referring to the nuts that grow underground.
