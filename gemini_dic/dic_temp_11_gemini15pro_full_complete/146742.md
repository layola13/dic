overhugenesses:/ˌoʊvərˈhjuːdʒnɪsɪz/
|n.|巨大；庞大；极大的规模|The sheer overhugeness of the universe is difficult to comprehend. 宇宙的浩瀚难以理解。|
|例句：The overhugeness of the task before them was daunting. 面对如此巨大的任务，他们感到畏惧。|
|例句：She was overwhelmed by the overhugeness of the ocean.  浩瀚的海洋让她不知所措。|
|近义词：immensity, vastness, enormity|
|反义词：smallness, tininess, minuteness|
|词性：名词|


Etymology:
over- + huge + -ness

"over-"  is a prefix meaning "excessively," "too much."
"huge" is an adjective meaning "extremely large in size or amount."
"-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
