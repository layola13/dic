lionized:/ˈlaɪənaɪzd/
verb
崇拜；把…视为名人；将…捧为名流
treated someone as a famous person
The returning astronauts were lionized everywhere they went.  返回的宇航员所到之处都受到人们的追捧。
The band was lionized by the media after their first album went platinum.  这支乐队在他们的第一张专辑获得白金销量后被媒体捧为明星。
近义词: celebrated, acclaimed, exalted, glorified
反义词: vilified, condemned, criticized, ignored
词性:v.
