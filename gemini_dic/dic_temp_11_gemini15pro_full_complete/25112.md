bucentaur:/bjuːˈsɛntɔːr/
n.
半人半牛海怪，人首牛身海怪
A mythical creature, half man and half ox (or bull), with the head and torso of a man and the body of an ox, often depicted as a sea creature.
The bucentaur was a mythical creature said to inhabit the waters near Venice. 半人半牛海怪据说栖息在威尼斯附近的水域。
The Doge of Venice would ceremonially wed the sea each year aboard a ship called the Bucentaur. 威尼斯总督每年都会在一艘名为“布森托罗”的船上举行与海洋的婚礼仪式。
Synonyms: chimera, monster, mythical creature
Antonyms: reality, human, animal
Noun
