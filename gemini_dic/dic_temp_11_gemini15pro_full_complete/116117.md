marvelment:/ˈmɑːrvəlmənt/ | n. |惊叹，惊奇 |A feeling of wonder and astonishment. |例句：She gazed at the Grand Canyon in marvelment. 她惊叹地凝视着大峡谷。

例句：The children watched the fireworks display with marvelment. 孩子们惊奇地看着烟火表演。|近义词：wonder, awe, amazement, astonishment |反义词：indifference, apathy |词性: 名词

词源:  Late Middle English: from marvel + -ment.
