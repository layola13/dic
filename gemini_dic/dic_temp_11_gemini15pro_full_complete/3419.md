afoul:/əˈfaʊl/
adverb|与…冲突，与…相抵触|into conflict or difficulty with.|The company ran afoul of environmental regulations. 这家公司违反了环境法规。|近义词:amiss, astray|反义词:agreeably, accordingly|词性:副词
词源:Middle English *afoulen, from a- (on, in) + foulen (to tread on, trample, defile). Ultimately from Old French fouler (to trample).
