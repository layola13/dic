mindblowing:/ˈmaɪndˌbloʊɪŋ/| adj.|令人兴奋的；给人印象极深刻的；使人感到震撼的|Overwhelmingly impressive, exciting, or shocking.|The special effects in the movie were mindblowing.  这部电影的特效令人叹为观止。|近义词：amazing, astonishing, incredible, phenomenal, stunning|反义词：boring, dull, mundane, unimpressive|词性:形容词
词源：mind (心智) + blowing (吹) -> 字面意思是“把你的心智吹走”，引申为极其震撼，令人印象深刻。
