cutthroat:/ˈkʌtθroʊt/| adj. n.|残酷的，激烈的；凶手，刺客|Relentlessly competitive; ruthless. A murderer. |The music industry is known for its cutthroat competition.  音乐行业以其残酷的竞争而闻名。| He was known as a cutthroat businessman who would stop at nothing to succeed. 他被称为一个冷酷无情的商人，为了成功不择手段。|近义词：ruthless, merciless, fierce, competitive, killer, assassin|反义词：merciful, compassionate, gentle, cooperative|词性:形容词,名词

Etymology:  From the literal meaning of cutting someone's throat, originating in the 16th century.  It evolved to describe ruthless or murderous individuals, and later to describe fiercely competitive situations.
