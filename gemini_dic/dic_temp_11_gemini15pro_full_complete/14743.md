awesome:/ˈɔːsəm/ (US) /ˈɔːs(ə)m/ (UK)| adj.|令人惊叹的；极好的；很棒的|Inspiring an overwhelming feeling of reverence, admiration, or fear. Originally used with reference to God or deities, now also (informally) used to describe things considered to be impressive or excellent.|例句：That new movie is awesome!  那部新电影太棒了！

例句：The view from the mountaintop was awesome. 从山顶望下去的景色令人叹为观止。|近义词：amazing, incredible, fantastic, wonderful, excellent, terrific|反义词：terrible, awful, dreadful|词性:形容词
