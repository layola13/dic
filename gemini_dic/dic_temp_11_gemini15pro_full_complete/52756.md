dilatational:/ˌdaɪleɪˈteɪʃənəl/ /ˌdɪləˈteɪʃənəl/
adjective
膨胀的，扩张的
Relating to or causing dilation; expanding.
The dilatational wave, also known as the P-wave, is a type of seismic wave that travels through the Earth by compressing and expanding the material it passes through.  膨胀波，也称为P波，是一种地震波，它通过压缩和膨胀其穿过的物质在地球上传播。

The dilatational properties of the material allowed it to stretch significantly without breaking.  这种材料的膨胀特性使其能够在不断裂的情况下显著拉伸。

近义词: expansive, expanding, distending
反义词: contractile, compressive
词性: adjective
