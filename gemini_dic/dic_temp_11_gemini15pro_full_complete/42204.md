councillorships:/ˈkaʊnsələʃɪps/
n.
市议员职位；市政委员的职位
the position of a councillor
He held two councillorships simultaneously. 他同时担任两个市议员的职位。

近义词:council seats, council positions
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider positions outside of local government)


词源:
From councillor + -ship.  Councillor derives from council, ultimately from Latin *consilium "council, deliberation," from consulere "to consult, take counsel," related to con- "together" + salire "to leap, spring."  The suffix -ship denotes "state, condition, office, dignity, skill."
