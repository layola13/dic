mercenarinesses:/ˌmɜːrsənˈɛrɪnɪsɪz/
n.
唯利是图；贪财
The state of being motivated solely by a desire for monetary or material gain.
His mercenarinesses were evident in his willingness to betray his friends for money.  他的唯利是图表现在他为了钱愿意背叛朋友。
The lawyer's mercenarinesses led him to take on cases he didn't believe in. 这位律师的唯利是图导致他接手了一些他并不相信的案件。

近义词:cupidity, avarice, greed, rapacity
反义词:altruism, generosity, selflessness
词性: Noun (plural)


词源: From mercenary + -ness + -es (plural suffix). Ultimately from Latin *mercēnārius, from mercēs (“wages, price”).
