arboret:/ˈɑːrbəret/| n. |树木园，植物园|A place where many different trees, shrubs, and other plants are cultivated for scientific, educational, or aesthetic purposes. |例句：The university maintains a beautiful arboret for research and public enjoyment. 大学维护着一个美丽的树木园，用于研究和公众欣赏。

例句：We visited the local arboret and saw a wide variety of exotic plants. 我们参观了当地的树木园，看到了各种各样的外来植物。|近义词：botanical garden, arboretum|反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性: noun
