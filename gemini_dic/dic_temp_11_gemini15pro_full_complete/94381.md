impleach:/ɪmˈplitʃ/ | v. | 控告，弹劾 | To charge (a public official) with misconduct in office before a competent tribunal.  | The House of Representatives voted to impleach the president. 众议院投票弹劾总统。 | Example:  The governor was impleached for corruption. 州长因腐败被弹劾。 | Synonyms: accuse, indict, charge | Antonyms: exonerate, acquit | verb
