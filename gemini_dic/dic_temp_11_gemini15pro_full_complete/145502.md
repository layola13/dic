overcloy:/ˌoʊvərˈklɔɪ/ | v. |过分装饰；使生腻；使过饱|To supply or fill to excess; surfeit; satiate.  To weary by excess or sameness.|The rich, overly sweet dessert overcloyed the palate.  这道浓郁过甜的甜点使人感到腻味。|近义词:satiate, glut, surfeit|反义词:starve, deprive|词性:动词
