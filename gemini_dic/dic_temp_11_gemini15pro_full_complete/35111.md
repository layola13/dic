cloud:/klaʊd/| n. v.|云，阴影，忧郁，一大群|A visible mass of condensed water vapor floating in the atmosphere, typically high above the ground.  Also used figuratively to represent something that obscures or casts a shadow.|The clouds drifted lazily across the sky. 云朵懒洋洋地飘过天空。|He clouded the issue with irrelevant details. 他用无关紧要的细节混淆了问题。|近义词：mist, haze, fog, swarm, flock|反义词:sunshine, clarity|名词/动词
