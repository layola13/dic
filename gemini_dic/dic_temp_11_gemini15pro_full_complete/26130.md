bustier:/ˈbʌsti.eɪ/ (US), /ˈbʌstɪeɪ/ (UK)| n. |紧身胸衣，塑身衣|A close-fitting strapless garment worn on the upper body, resembling a corset but generally less constricting.|She wore a bustier under her sheer blouse. 她穿了一件紧身胸衣在透明衬衫里面。|近义词：corset, bodice|反义词: loose garment|词性:noun
