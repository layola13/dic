luminescent:/ˌluːmɪˈnesənt/ /ˌluːməˈnesnt/
| adj. | 发光的，冷光的 | Emitting light not caused by heat, as in fluorescence or phosphorescence. | The jellyfish emitted a luminescent glow in the dark water. 水母在黑暗的水中发出冷光。| synonyms: glowing, shining, radiant, brilliant | antonyms: dull, dark, dim, opaque | adjective

luminescence:/ˌluːmɪˈnesəns/ /ˌluːməˈnesns/
| n. | 发光，冷光 | The emission of light not caused by heat. | The luminescence of the fireflies danced in the night. 萤火虫的冷光在夜间飞舞。| synonyms: glow, radiance, shine, brilliance | antonyms: darkness, dullness, dimness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *lūmin- (stem of lūmen "light") + -escent (suffix meaning "beginning to be" or "becoming").
