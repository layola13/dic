agronomy:/əˈɡrɑːnəmi/ /əˈɡrɒnəmi/| n. |农学；农艺学|The science of soil management and crop production.|She's studying agronomy at the university. 她正在大学学习农学。|近义词：agriculture, farming, husbandry|反义词: N/A|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *agronomie*, from Ancient Greek ἀγρός (*agrós*) meaning "field" + νόμος (*nómos*)  meaning "law" or "custom".  Literally, "land management".
