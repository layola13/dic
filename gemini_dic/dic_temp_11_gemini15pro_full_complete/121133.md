misdefining:/ˌmɪsdɪˈfaɪnɪŋ/
v.
错误地定义；定义不当
Giving an incorrect or inadequate definition of something.
They are misdefining the problem as a simple lack of resources. 他们错误地将这个问题定义为仅仅是缺乏资源。
misinterpreting, misconstruing, distorting
correctly defining, accurately defining
verb
