abhorrent:/əbˈhɔːrənt/ /əbˈhɑːrənt/
adjective
令人憎恶的；可恶的
Inspiring disgust and loathing; repugnant.
He found the idea of eating insects abhorrent. 他觉得吃昆虫的想法令人作呕。
The dictator's abhorrent treatment of his people led to widespread rebellion.  独裁者对其人民的憎恶行径导致了大范围的反抗。
近义词: detestable, loathsome, repugnant, repulsive
反义词: admirable, delightful, pleasant, appealing
词性: adjective


abhorrible:/əbˈhɔːrəbl/ /əbˈhɑːrəbl/
adjective
可怕的；极坏的；糟糕的
Causing or deserving strong dislike or hatred.
The food at the hotel was absolutely abhorrible. 这家旅馆的食物糟透了。
They lived in abhorrible conditions. 他们生活在恶劣的环境中。
近义词: terrible, awful, dreadful, horrible
反义词: wonderful, excellent, delightful, pleasant
词性: adjective
