high-concept:/ˌhaɪ ˈkɒnsept/ (US) /ˌhaɪ ˈkɑːnsept/ (UK)| adj.|高概念的|A high-concept film, book, etc. is one that can be easily described in a few words, and is therefore thought likely to be commercially successful.|The studio is looking for a high-concept romantic comedy. 这家工作室正在寻找一部高概念的浪漫喜剧电影。|近义词： marketable, commercial, easily understood|反义词： complex, intricate, nuanced|词性:形容词
