e-zine:/ˈiːziːn/
n.
electronic magazine; online magazine
电子杂志；网络杂志
She publishes an e-zine about sustainable living. 她出版了一份关于可持续生活的电子杂志。
Synonyms: online magazine, digital magazine, webzine
Antonyms: print magazine
noun
