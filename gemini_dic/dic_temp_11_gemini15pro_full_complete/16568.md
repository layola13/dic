barnes:/bɑːrnz/| n.|巴恩斯（姓氏）; (尤指旧时或乡村的)谷仓, 畜棚|A surname, primarily of English origin, meaning "son of Barnaby". Also, a plural form of "barn", a building for storing farm produce, especially grain.|例句：
Mr. Barnes is our new neighbor. 巴恩斯先生是我们的新邻居。

The farmer stored hay in the barnes. 农民把干草储存在谷仓里。

|近义词：(for barn) shed, outbuilding, stable|反义词: (for barn) N/A|词性:noun
