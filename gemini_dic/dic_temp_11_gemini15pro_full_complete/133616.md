nonflyable:/ˌnɒnˈflaɪəbəl/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈflaɪəbəl/ (UK)
adjective
不能飞的，不宜飞行的
Not capable of flying; unsuitable for flying.
The aircraft was declared nonflyable after the bird strike. 这架飞机在撞上鸟后被宣布无法飞行。
The weather conditions made the helicopter nonflyable. 天气状况使直升机无法飞行。
近义词:unflyable, unairable
反义词:flyable, airworthy
词性:adjective
