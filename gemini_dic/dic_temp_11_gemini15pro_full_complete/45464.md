curator:/kjʊˈreɪtər/ | n. | 馆长；监护人；管理者 | A person who is in charge of a museum, art collection, etc., and who decides what should be included in it. | The museum curator is responsible for acquiring new pieces for the collection.  博物馆馆长负责为收藏品购置新的藏品。|  The curator gave a tour of the exhibit. 馆长带领参观了展览。| synonyms: administrator, manager, superintendent, director, custodian | antonyms:  None (The concept of a curator doesn't have a direct opposite.) | noun
