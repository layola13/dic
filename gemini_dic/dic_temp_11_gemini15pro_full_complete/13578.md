attentat:/ˌætɑ̃ˈtɑː/ /əˈtɛntæt/ | n. |袭击，攻击，暗杀|An attempt to kill an important person, usually for political reasons. An attack on something that people value.|例句：The president survived the attentat. 总统在袭击中幸存下来。

例句：The museum was the target of an attentat. 博物馆是袭击的目标。|近义词：attack, assault, attempt, onslaught|反义词:defense, protection|词性:noun

词源: From French *attentat*, from *attenter* "to attempt," from Latin *attentare* "to try, attempt," from *ad-* "to" + *tentare* "to handle, touch, try."
