omni-antenna:/ˈɒmni ænˈtɛnə/ | n. | 全向天线 | An antenna that radiates radio waves equally in all directions horizontally, typically used in broadcasting and mobile communications.  | Example 1: The omni-antenna on the roof provides a strong signal to all nearby devices. 屋顶上的全向天线为所有附近的设备提供强信号。

Example 2: Omni-antennas are commonly used in Wi-Fi routers.  全向天线通常用于Wi-Fi路由器中。 | Synonym: isotropic antenna | Antonym: directional antenna | noun
