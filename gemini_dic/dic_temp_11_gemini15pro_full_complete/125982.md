musketo:/mʌˈskiːtoʊ/ (US) /məˈskiːtəʊ/ (UK)| n. |蚊子|A small flying insect that bites people and animals and sucks their blood. Some types of mosquito spread serious diseases like malaria. |例句：The mosquitos were buzzing around my head all night. 蚊子整夜都在我头上嗡嗡叫。

例句：I got bitten by a mosquito and now the bite is itching. 我被蚊子叮了一口，现在叮咬处很痒。|近义词：gnat, midge|反义词: N/A (没有蚊子的反义词)|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *mosquito* (small fly), diminutive of *mosca* (fly), from Latin *musca* (fly).
