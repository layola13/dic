hinderest:/ˈhɪndərest/
adjective
最后方的；最后部的；最偏僻的
furthest behind; most remote
例句：The hinderest part of the procession was out of sight. 行列最后方的人群已经看不见了。

例句：He lived in the hinderest cabin in the woods. 他住在森林中最偏僻的小屋里。
近义词：rearmost, farthest, last
反义词：foremost, first, nearest
词性：adjective

词源：From Middle English hinderest, superlative of *hinder (“situated behind; posterior”), from Old English hind (“behind, after, following”), from Proto-Germanic *hindaną (“behind”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₁indʰ- (“behind, after”). Cognate with Dutch hinder (“hinder, rear”), German hinter (“behind”), Swedish hinder (“hinder, rear”).  The superlative suffix -est is a common Germanic superlative marker.
