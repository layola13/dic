nonemotively:/ˌnɑːnɪˈmoʊtɪvli/ /ˌnɒnɪˈməʊtɪvli/| adv.|不动感情地，无感情地|Without emotion; in a nonemotional manner.  It describes actions or expressions that lack emotional content.|He reported the news nonemotively, as if reading from a script.|近义词: impassively, apathetically, indifferently|反义词: emotionally, expressively|词性:副词
词源: non- (not) + emotive (relating to emotion) + -ly (adverbial suffix)
