apparent:/əˈpærənt/ /əˈpɛərənt/| adj.|明显的，显而易见的；表面上的|Clearly visible or understood; seeming real or true, especially when this is not actually the case.|例句：It was apparent that he was lying. 显然他在撒谎。|例句：His apparent lack of concern worried her. 他表面上的漠不关心让她担心。|近义词：obvious, evident, clear, visible, seeming|反义词：hidden, obscure, unclear, real, genuine|词性:形容词
