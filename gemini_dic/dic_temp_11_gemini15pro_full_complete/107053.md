lacturamic: /læktjʊˈræmɪk/
adjective
催乳的，促进泌乳的
Relating to or promoting the secretion of milk.
Lactation is the secretion of milk from the mammary glands and the period of time that a mother lactates to feed her young.  Lactation describes the process of milk production in the breasts to feed a baby. The word "lacturamic" is a less common term used to describe something related to or promoting this process.
例句：The doctor prescribed a lacturamic drug to help the new mother with milk production. 医生给这位新妈妈开了一种催乳药，以帮助她产奶。

例句：Lactation consultants provide support and guidance for lacturamic challenges. 哺乳顾问为哺乳挑战提供支持和指导。

近义词：galactagogue, lactogenic, milk-producing
反义词: anti-galactagogue, lactation-suppressing
