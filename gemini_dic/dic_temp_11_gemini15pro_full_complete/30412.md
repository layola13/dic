cento:/ˈtʃɛntoʊ/ | n. | 诗歌集锦; 集锦诗; | A poem or other literary work composed of lines or passages from other authors, often rearranged in a new order to create a different meaning. |  Example 1: The poet created a beautiful cento using lines from Shakespeare's sonnets. 诗人用莎士比亚十四行诗中的诗句创作了一首优美的集锦诗。

Example 2: Her latest work is a cento of famous love poems. 她最新的作品是一部著名的爱情诗集锦。 | synonym: patchwork, anthology, compilation | antonym: original work, new composition | noun
