lie-about:/ˈlaɪ əˌbaʊt/ | n. | 闲荡,无所事事,懒散|A person who spends their time idly or in aimless activity; a loafer.  A period of inactivity or idleness.|He was known as a bit of a lie-about, never taking anything seriously.  他被认为是个游手好闲的人，从来不把任何事当回事。|Synonyms: loafer, idler, slacker, good-for-nothing |Antonyms: hard worker, diligent person| noun

lie-about:/ˈlaɪ əˌbaʊt/ | n. | （尤指令人不快的）谎言，假话|A lie or fabrication, especially a blatant or unpleasant one.|The whole story was a complete lie-about.  整个故事完全是胡编乱造。|Synonyms: fabrication, falsehood, untruth, lie |Antonyms: truth, fact, reality| noun
