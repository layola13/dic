ecphoneme:/ˈɛkfəˌniːm/ | n. |感叹词；呼语；感叹句 | An exclamation or exclamatory phrase.  It's a rhetorical device used to express strong emotion. |  Example: "Alas!" is an ecphoneme. “唉！”是一个感叹词。 | Synonym: exclamation, interjection, outcry | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but consider understatement) | noun

词源:
From Late Latin *ecphōnēma,  from Ancient Greek ἐκφώνημα (ekphṓnēma) "exclamation, utterance," from ἐκφωνέω (ekphōneō) "to cry out, exclaim," from ἐκ- (ek-) "out" + φωνέω (phōneō) "to speak, utter a sound."
