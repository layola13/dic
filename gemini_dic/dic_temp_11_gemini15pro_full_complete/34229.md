clankingness:/ˈklæŋkɪŋnəs/
n.
发出叮当声的性质; 叮当声不断的状态
the quality of making a loud, repeated metallic sound
The clankingness of the old machinery was deafening. 老机器的叮当声震耳欲聋。
近义词: clangor, rattle, jangle
反义词: silence, quietness
词性:名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *clanken*, of imitative origin + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state, condition, quality, or degree).
