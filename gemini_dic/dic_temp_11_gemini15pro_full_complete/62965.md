ennobling:/ɪˈnoʊblɪŋ/
adjective
使人高尚的；崇高的
making someone feel or seem to be a better person; giving someone greater dignity
Reading about the lives of great people can be an ennobling experience. 阅读伟人的生平可以是一次令人高尚的体验。
Synonyms: uplifting, elevating, inspiring, dignifying
Antonyms: degrading, debasing, corrupting
Word Origin:  From ennoble + -ing. Ennoble comes from the Old French ennoblir, from en- "in" + noble "noble."
