craterless:/ˈkreɪtərlɪs/
adj.
无陨石坑的，没有坑的
Lacking craters; having a surface without craters.
The newly discovered planet appeared craterless, a surprising find for the astronomers.  这颗新发现的行星看起来没有陨石坑，这对天文学家来说是一个惊人的发现。
The pristine, craterless surface of the moon Europa intrigues scientists.  木卫二欧罗巴原始而无陨石坑的表面激起了科学家的兴趣。
近义词:smooth, unblemished, unmarked
反义词:cratered, pitted, pocked
词性:adjective
crater: /ˈkreɪtər/ | n. | 陨石坑；火山口；弹坑 | A bowl-shaped cavity in the ground or on the surface of a planet or moon, typically caused by an explosion or the impact of a meteorite or other celestial body. |
-less: /lɪs/ | suffix | 无...的；没有...的 |  A suffix forming adjectives and meaning “without” or “lacking”. |
