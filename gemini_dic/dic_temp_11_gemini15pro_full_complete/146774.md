overidealistic: /ˌoʊvərˌaɪdiːəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
过于理想化的
excessively idealistic; unrealistically optimistic.  Characterized by having ideals that are too far removed from reality.
He had overidealistic views on marriage. 他对婚姻持有过于理想化的看法。
The young activist's overidealistic plans were quickly met with the harsh realities of political maneuvering.  这位年轻激进分子的过于理想化的计划很快遭到了政治运作的残酷现实的打击。


synonyms: utopian, quixotic, romantic, starry-eyed, impractical
antonyms: realistic, pragmatic, cynical, pessimistic, hard-headed
词性: adjective
