kip:/kɪp/| n. v.|小睡，千磅|A short sleep, especially during the day.  A unit of weight equal to 1,000 pounds.|例句：I had a quick kip after lunch. 我午饭后小睡了一会儿。

The bridge can support a kip. 这座桥可以支撑一千磅的重量。|近义词：(sleep) nap, doze, snooze; (weight) kilopound, half ton|反义词:(sleep) stay awake|词性:名词，动词

kip:/kɪp/| v.|跳过，略过|To skip or pass over (something).|例句：I kipped the introduction and went straight to chapter two. 我跳过了引言，直接看了第二章。


I kipped over the boring parts of the book.  我跳过了书中无聊的部分。|近义词：skip, omit, bypass|反义词:include, read, peruse|词性:动词

From Middle English *kippen*, of uncertain origin.  The weight sense derives from *kilopound*.
