flashy:/ˈflæʃi/ (US), /ˈflæʃi/ (UK)| adj.|俗丽的；炫耀的；显眼的|Attracting attention by being bright, expensive, or showy.|He drives a flashy sports car. 他开着一辆炫耀的跑车。|Synonyms: showy, ostentatious, gaudy, flamboyant, glitzy|Antonyms: understated, subtle, subdued, modest, plain|adjective

flashy:/ˈflæʃi/ (US), /ˈflæʃi/ (UK)| n.|炫耀；卖弄；一瞬间|A brief, intense burst of light or display.|The fireworks made a flashy display.  烟火表演很炫目。|Synonyms: display, burst, blaze, flash|Antonyms:  - |noun (rarely used)
