fanboy:/ˈfænˌbɔɪ/| n.|狂热粉丝，迷弟|A fanboy is a male fan who is excessively enthusiastic and devoted, sometimes to the point of obsession, about a particular hobby, franchise, celebrity, or other cultural phenomenon.  |例句：He's such a fanboy for that band, he knows all their lyrics by heart. 他是那个乐队的超级粉丝，所有的歌词都能倒背如流。

例句：The fanboys went crazy when the lead singer walked on stage. 当主唱歌手走上舞台时，粉丝们都疯狂了。|近义词：enthusiast, devotee, aficionado|反义词：critic, detractor|词性:noun
