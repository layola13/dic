basketing:/ˈbɑːskɪtɪŋ/
n.|装篮；装入篮子|The basketing of the apples was done carefully.  苹果被小心地装进了篮子。|例句：The basketing of the produce took several hours.  农产品的装篮工作花了几个小时。|近义词：packing, crating|反义词: unpacking, unloading|词性:名词
Etymology: From basket + -ing (present participle suffix, forming a noun from a verb).  Ultimately from the Middle English *basket*, likely borrowed from Old North French *baskete* or *baschette*, diminutive of *basc*, from Frankish *baska* "basket."
