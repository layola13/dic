dummiest:/ˈdʌmiɪst/| adj.|最愚蠢的|The superlative form of "dummy," meaning most unintelligent or foolish.|That was the dummiest thing I've ever heard. 那是
我听过的最愚蠢的事情。|近义词：stupidest, most foolish, most idiotic|反义词：smartest, brightest, wisest|词性:形容词

Etymology: From "dummy" + "-est." "Dummy" originates from the Middle Dutch "dom," meaning "mute" or "stupid." Over time, it evolved to refer to a person lacking intelligence. The superlative suffix "-est" is added to indicate the highest degree of the adjective.
