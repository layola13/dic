over-seeded:/ˌoʊvərˈsiːdɪd/ | adj. | 过度播种的；重复播种的 |  Covered with too much seed, or seeded again after a previous seeding. This is often done with lawns to improve density or introduce a different grass variety. |  The lawn was over-seeded in the spring to fill in bare patches. 春天的时候，草坪被过度播种以填补光秃秃的地方。| Synonyms: overplanted, thickly sown | Antonyms: sparsely seeded, underseeded | adjective

over-seed:/ˌoʊvərˈsiːd/ | vt. | 过度播种；重复播种 | To sow (seed) too densely or to sow again after a previous seeding. | He over-seeded the lawn and now the grass is too thick. 他在草坪上播种太多了，现在草太密了。| Synonyms: oversow | Antonyms: underseed | verb
