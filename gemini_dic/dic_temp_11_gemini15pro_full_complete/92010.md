hyperorthodoxy:/ˌhaɪpərˈɔːrθədɒksi/ | n. |极端正统；极端正统观念|Extreme or excessive adherence to orthodox religious beliefs or practices.  It often involves a strict and literal interpretation of religious texts and traditions, sometimes leading to rigid and exclusionary views.|His hyperorthodoxy alienated many members of the congregation. 他的极端正统疏远了会众中的许多成员。|The community was known for its hyperorthodoxy and resistance to change. 这个社区以其极端正统和抵制变革而闻名。|近义词: fundamentalism, ultraorthodoxy |反义词: liberalism, heterodoxy |词性:名词

Etymology:
From hyper- + orthodoxy.  "Hyper-" is a prefix of Greek origin meaning "over, above, beyond," indicating excess or exaggeration. "Orthodoxy" comes from the Greek "orthos" (right, correct) and "doxa" (opinion, belief).
