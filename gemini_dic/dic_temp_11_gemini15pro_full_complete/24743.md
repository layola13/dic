broodiness:/ˈbruːdinəs/ | n. | 孵卵期；沉思默想；渴望生孩子 | The state of being broody, especially characterized by a hen sitting on eggs to hatch them or a woman's strong desire to have a baby. |  She felt a sudden pang of broodiness when she saw her friend's newborn. 她看到朋友的新生儿时，突然感到一阵想要孩子的渴望。| synonyms:  brooding, thoughtfulness, pensiveness, contemplativeness (for the general sense of deep thought); maternal instinct, baby fever (for the desire to have children) | antonyms:  cheerfulness, lightheartedness, indifference (for the general sense); aversion to children (for the desire to have children) | noun
