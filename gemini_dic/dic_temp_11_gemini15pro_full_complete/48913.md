defrocked:/diːˈfrɒkt/
adjective
被剥夺圣职的，被革除教籍的
Deprived of ecclesiastical status, especially by removal of holy orders.
The defrocked priest was no longer allowed to perform religious ceremonies.  这位被革除教籍的牧师不再被允许举行宗教仪式。
synonyms: unfrocked, dismissed, expelled, ousted
antonyms: ordained, consecrated
word type: adjective
