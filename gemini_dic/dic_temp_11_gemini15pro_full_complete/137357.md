nonteachable:/ˌnɒnˈtiːtʃəbl/
adjective
不可教的；无法教的
Not able to be taught or educated.
This concept is so complex, it's practically nonteachable to beginners.  这个概念太复杂了，对于初学者来说几乎是无法教授的。
His stubbornness makes him nonteachable. 他的固执使他不可教。
近义词:uneducable, intractable, unmanageable
反义词:teachable, docile, educable
词性:adjective
