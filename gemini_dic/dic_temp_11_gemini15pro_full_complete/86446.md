PHRASE->
heft of something = the weight of something 某物的重量
heft something up = lift or raise something heavy  举起或抬起重物
feel the heft of something = feel the weight of something  感觉到某物的重量
have some heft = be heavy  很重
have some heft (figurative) = be important or influential  (比喻) 重要或有影响力
