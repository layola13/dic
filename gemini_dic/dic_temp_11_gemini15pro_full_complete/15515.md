baffle:/ˈbæfl/ (US) /ˈbæfəl/ (UK)| v. n.|使困惑，难倒；挡板，隔板|To confuse or frustrate someone; a device used to control the flow of air, light, or sound.|The complex instructions completely baffled me. 复杂的说明彻底把我搞糊涂了。|The magician baffled the audience with his tricks. 魔术师用他的戏法迷惑了观众。|近义词：confuse, perplex, bewilder, frustrate, mystify, stump|反义词：clarify, enlighten, explain|词性: verb, noun
