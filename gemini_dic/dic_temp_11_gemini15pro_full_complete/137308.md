nontactic: /nɒnˈtæktɪk/ | adj. | 非战术的，非策略的 | Not relating to or characterized by tactics or strategy.  |  The artist's nontactic approach to composition resulted in a surprisingly harmonious piece. 艺术家非策略性的构图方法出人意料地创作出了一幅和谐的作品。 |  The general preferred a more direct, nontactic approach, simply overwhelming the enemy with superior numbers. 将军更喜欢更直接、非战术的方法，只是用优势兵力压倒敌人。 | 近义词: nonstrategic, unplanned, random | 反义词: tactical, strategic, planned | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From non- + tactic.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "tactic" refers to a method or plan for achieving a goal.
