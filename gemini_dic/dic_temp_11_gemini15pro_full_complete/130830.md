nonapplicabilities:/ˌnɒnəˌplɪkəˈbɪlətiz/
n.|不适用性；不适用之处|The nonapplicabilities of this rule in certain situations are obvious.  这条规则在某些情况下不适用是显而易见的。|This section details the nonapplicabilities of the new regulations. 本节详细说明了新规定的不适用之处。|近义词:exceptions, exclusions, limitations|反义词:applicabilities, relevance|词性:名词

词源:  "non-" (not) + "applicable" (capable of being applied) + "-ity" (noun suffix indicating state or quality) + "-ies" (plural suffix)
