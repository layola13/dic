deambulatory:/ˌdiæm.bjʊ.lə.tɔːr.i/ /dɪˈæmbjə.lə.tɔːr.i/
adjective
可走动的；步行的
Relating to walking or able to walk.
The hospital has a special deambulatory care unit for patients who can walk.  这家医院有一个专门的步行护理单元，供能走路的病人使用。
The patient was deambulatory after surgery. 手术后病人能够走动。
ambulatory, mobile, peripatetic
bedridden, immobile, sedentary
adjective


deambulate:/diˈæmbjʊ.leɪt/
verb
行走；漫步
To walk or move about.
The patient was encouraged to deambulate after surgery to prevent blood clots.  鼓励病人在手术后走动以防止血栓。
He deambulated around the park, enjoying the fresh air. 他在公园里漫步，享受着新鲜空气。
walk, stroll, amble
sit, stay, lie down
