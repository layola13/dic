carpool: /ˈkɑːrpuːl/ (US) /ˈkɑːpuːl/ (UK)| n. v.|拼车；合伙用车；|An arrangement between people to make a regular journey together in a single car, especially to work or school, typically sharing the costs involved.| We decided to carpool to work to save money. 我们决定拼车上班以省钱。|近义词：rideshare, lift-sharing|反义词: drive alone|词性: noun, verb

carpool: /ˈkɑːrpuːl/ (US) /ˈkɑːpuːl/ (UK)| v.|拼车；合伙用车；|To travel to work or school regularly with other people in a single car, especially sharing the costs involved.|They carpool to school every day. 他们每天拼车上学。|近义词：rideshare, lift-share|反义词: drive alone|词性: verb

词源:  A blend of *car* and *pool*, meaning a group sharing resources.  First recorded in the 1940s.
