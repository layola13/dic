bibliophilists:/ˌbɪbliəˈfɪlɪsts/
n.
藏书家，爱书者
A person who collects or has a great love of books.
Example:  My uncle is a true bibliophilist, his house is overflowing with books. 我叔叔是一个真正的藏书家，他的房子里堆满了书。

Example:  Bibliophilists often spend hours browsing in secondhand bookstores. 藏书家经常花几个小时在二手书店里浏览。

Synonym:  bookworm, book lover, bibliophile
Antonym:  illiterate, philistine
词性: 名词
