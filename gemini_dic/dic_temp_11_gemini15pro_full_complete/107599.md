landslided:/ˈlændslaɪdɪd/ | v. | 发生山体滑坡；以压倒性优势获胜 | The area landslided after heavy rain.  暴雨过后，该地区发生了山体滑坡。| The party landslided to victory in the election. 该党在选举中以压倒性优势获胜。 | 近义词：collapsed, avalanched (for physical landslides), routed, trounced (for electoral landslides) | 反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "lost narrowly" for elections) | 词性: 动词

landslide: /ˈlændslaɪd/ | n. | 山体滑坡；压倒性胜利 | The landslide destroyed several homes. 山体滑坡摧毁了几座房屋。| The election resulted in a landslide for the incumbent. 选举结果是现任者获得压倒性胜利。| 近义词：avalanche, rockslide (for physical landslides), rout, blowout (for electoral landslides) | 反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "close contest" for elections) | 词性: 名词


词源:  "land" + "slide," literally meaning a slide of land.  "Land" comes from Old English, and "slide" comes from Old English "slīdan."  The figurative meaning of an overwhelming victory emerged in the 19th century.
