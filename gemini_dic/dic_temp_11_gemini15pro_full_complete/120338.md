ETYMOLOGY->
From "minimal" + "-ist".  "Minimal" comes from the Late Latin "minimalis," meaning "smallest," from "minimus," the superlative of "parvus" meaning "small." The suffix "-ist" forms nouns that refer to a person who practices or is associated with something.
