muli:/ˈmuːli/| n.|骡子|A hybrid offspring of a male donkey (jack) and a female horse (mare).|例句：The muli carried heavy loads up the mountain. 骡子驮着重物上山。

例句：Mulis are known for their strength and surefootedness. 骡子以其力量和稳健的步伐而闻名。|近义词：hinny (the offspring of a male horse and a female donkey), hybrid|反义词: horse, donkey |词性:noun
