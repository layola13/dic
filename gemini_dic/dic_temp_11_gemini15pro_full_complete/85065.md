hard-edged:/ˌhɑːrdˈɛdʒd/
|adj.|轮廓鲜明的；线条分明的；冷酷无情的；直截了当的|Having clear, sharp outlines or a uncompromising, blunt manner.|The photograph had a hard-edged clarity. 这张照片轮廓清晰分明。|近义词：sharp, crisp, clear-cut, blunt, uncompromising|反义词：soft, blurry, vague, compassionate, compromising|词性: 形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From "hard" + "edged." "Hard" meaning firm, unyielding, and "edged" referring to having a distinct edge or boundary.  This combination creates the sense of something sharply defined or uncompromising.
