consanguineal:/ˌkɒnsæŋˈɡwɪnɪəl/ /ˌkɑːnsæŋˈɡwɪniəl/| adj.|同血缘的，血亲的|Related by blood; descended from the same ancestor.|例句：They have a consanguineal marriage. 他们有血缘婚姻。|例句：Consanguineal relationships are often valued in some cultures. 在某些文化中，血缘关系通常被重视。|近义词：kindred, related|反义词：unrelated, non-consanguineous|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin consanguineus ‘of the same blood’, from con- ‘together’ + sanguis ‘blood’.
