albuminimeter:/ˌælbjʊmɪˈnɪmɪtər/
n.
蛋白定量器，尿蛋白计
An instrument used to measure the amount of albumin in a liquid, particularly in urine.
The doctor used an albuminimeter to check the patient's urine for protein. 医生用蛋白定量器检查病人的尿液中的蛋白质含量。
近义词:  proteinuria meter,  albumin test
反义词:  None (No direct antonym for a measuring device)
词性: noun

Etymology:
albumin + -i- + -meter
albumin (from Latin albumen, "egg white") refers to a class of water-soluble proteins found in blood plasma and egg white.
-i- is a connecting vowel.
-meter (from Greek metron, "measure") denotes a measuring instrument.
