mailings:/ˈmeɪlɪŋz/
n.
邮件, 邮寄, 邮递, (宣传册等的) 邮寄活动
the act of sending something by mail; something sent by mail, especially advertising material.
The company sent out mailings to potential customers.  这家公司向潜在客户寄出了邮件。
We received several mailings about the new product. 我们收到了几封关于新产品的邮件。
dispatch, shipment, posting
receipts
noun
