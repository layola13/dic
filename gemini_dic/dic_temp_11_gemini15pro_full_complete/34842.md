clinician:/klɪˈnɪʃən/| n.|临床医生；临床工作者|A healthcare professional who works directly with patients, rather than in a laboratory or as a researcher.  A clinician may be a physician, nurse, therapist, or other healthcare provider.|The clinician carefully examined the patient's symptoms. 临床医生仔细检查了病人的症状。|The clinician recommended a course of physical therapy. 临床医生建议进行物理治疗。|Synonyms: practitioner, doctor, physician, therapist, medic, healer|Antonyms: researcher, scientist|noun
