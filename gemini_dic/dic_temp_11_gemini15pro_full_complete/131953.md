noncontributors:/ˌnɒn kənˈtrɪbjətəz/ (US) /ˌnɑːn kənˈtrɪbjətərz/
noncontributor|n.|非贡献者，未捐献者|A person who does not contribute, especially to a fund or cause.|The fundraising drive identified a significant number of noncontributors.|Synonyms: non-donor, freeloader, bystander|Antonyms: contributor, donor, supporter|Noun
noncontributors|n.|未投稿者，未参与者|A person who does not contribute, especially to a project or discussion.|The project suffered from a lack of input from noncontributors.|Synonyms: non-participant, outsider|Antonyms: participant, contributor, member|Noun
