fantoccini:/ˌfæntɒˈtʃiːni/| n.|木偶，牵线木偶|Puppets, especially marionettes, manipulated by strings or wires.  They are often used in theatrical performances.|The puppeteer skillfully manipulated the fantoccini, bringing the characters to life.  木偶操纵者熟练地操纵着牵线木偶，使角色栩栩如生。|Synonyms: marionettes, puppets|Antonyms: None|Noun
