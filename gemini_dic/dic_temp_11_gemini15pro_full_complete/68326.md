fall-down:/ˈfɔːl daʊn/| adj.(非正式，较少用) |  破旧的；摇摇欲坠的 | Describing something dilapidated or in a state of disrepair (informal, less common). |  The old barn was a real fall-down.  那个旧谷仓真是摇摇欲坠。 | 近义词：ramshackle, dilapidated | 反义词: sturdy, sound  | 词性:形容词
