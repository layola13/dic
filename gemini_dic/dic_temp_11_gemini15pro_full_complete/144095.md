outsallying: /ˌaʊtˈsæliɪŋ/
outsallying: n.|突围，出击|A sudden attack made from a defensive position.|The troops made a daring outsallying against the enemy siege lines.  部队对敌人的包围线进行了大胆的突击。|近义词：sortie, attack, assault, sally|反义词：retreat, withdrawal|词性: 名词 (gerund/present participle of the verb "sally")
outsallying: /ˌaʊtˈsæliɪŋ/
outsallying: adj.|突围的，出击的|Characterized by sudden attacks from a defensive position.|The outsallying force surprised the enemy. 突围部队使敌人感到惊讶。|近义词：attacking, assaulting, sallying|反义词：retreating, withdrawing|词性: 形容词 (present participle of the verb "sally")

词源: From the verb "sally," from Old French "saillir" (to leap, rush out), ultimately from Latin "salire" (to leap, jump).
