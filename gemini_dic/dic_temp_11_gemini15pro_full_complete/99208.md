interclouds:/ˌɪntərˈklaʊdz/ | n. | 云间；云层之间 | The airplane flew through the interclouds, briefly emerging into sunlight. 飞机飞过云层之间，短暂地出现在阳光下。| The interclouds were filled with swirling mist. 云层之间充满了旋转的薄雾。| 近义词: between the clouds | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially clear sky) | 词性: noun

Etymology:
"inter-" (Latin) meaning "between, among" + "clouds" (Old English)
