freelancer:/ˈfriːlænsər/| n.|自由职业者；自由撰稿人|A person who works independently for different companies rather than being employed by one company.|She works as a freelancer, writing articles for various magazines. 她作为一名自由职业者，为各种杂志撰写文章。|近义词：independent contractor, self-employed person, consultant|反义词：employee, full-time worker|词性:noun
freelance:/ˈfriːlæns/| v.  adj. |自由职业；自由职业的|To work as a freelancer; working independently.|He freelanced for several years before finding a permanent job.  他在找到一份固定工作前做了几年的自由职业。|近义词：work independently, contract, consult|反义词：work full-time, be employed|词性:verb, adjective

词源:  From "free lance," referring to medieval mercenary soldiers who would fight for whoever paid them.  源自“free lance”，指的是中世纪的雇佣兵，他们会为任何付钱给他们的人作战。
