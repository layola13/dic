allegro:/əˈlɛɡroʊ/ /əˈleɡroʊ/| n. adj. adv.|快板，活泼的，快速地|A musical term indicating a brisk, lively tempo. Also used as an adjective or adverb to describe something fast or lively. |The symphony's final movement was an exciting allegro. 交响曲的最后乐章是一段激动人心的快板。|近义词：brisk, lively, fast, quick|反义词:slow, adagio, lento|词性:名词,形容词,副词
词源: From Italian *allegro*, literally "cheerful," from Latin *alacris* "cheerful, brisk, lively."
