enfeoff: /ɪnˈfiːf/ /ɛnˈfif/ | vt. | 封邑；授予…封地 | To grant a fief (estate) to someone; to invest with a fief.  | The king enfeoffed his loyal knight with a large estate. 国王将一大片领地封给了他忠诚的骑士。 |  The duke enfeoffed him with vast tracts of land. 公爵授予他大片土地。 | 近义词: invest, endow, grant | 反义词: dispossess, divest | 词性: verb

词源: From Old French *enfeffer, from en- + fief (“fief”), from Frankish *fehu (“cattle, property”).
