cabbaged:/ˈkæbɪdʒd/| adj.| 切碎的（像卷心菜一样）; <罕见> 头脑糊涂的 | shredded (like cabbage); <rare> muddle-headed | The document was cabbaged after he accidentally put it through the shredder. 他不小心把文件放进了碎纸机，文件被切碎了。| 近义词：shredded, chopped | 反义词：whole, intact |词性:形容词
