impassioned:/ɪmˈpæʃənd/| adj.|充满激情的，热烈的|Filled with or showing great emotion.|Her impassioned plea for help moved everyone in the room. 她充满激情的求助打动了房间里的每一个人。|Synonyms: fervent, ardent, passionate, fiery|Antonyms: apathetic, indifferent, unemotional|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *impassionatus*, from Latin impassionare (“to fill with passion”), from in- + passion-, passio (“passion, suffering”).  Ultimately from Latin patior (“to suffer, endure”).
