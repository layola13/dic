grainednesses:/ˈɡreɪndnəsnɪsɪz/
n.
粒度，颗粒性
The level of detail or texture, especially relating to the size of grains in a substance or the distinctness of individual elements in a system.
The photographer admired the grainednesses of the old black and white film. 摄影师欣赏旧黑白胶片的颗粒感。
granularity, texture, coarseness
smoothness, fineness
