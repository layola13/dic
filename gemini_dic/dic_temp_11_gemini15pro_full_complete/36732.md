colloid:/ˈkɒlɔɪd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːlɔɪd/ (US)| n. |胶体|A substance microscopically dispersed evenly throughout another substance.  The dispersed-phase particles have a diameter between about 5 and 1000 nanometers.|Milk is an example of a colloid. 牛奶是胶体的一个例子。|
colloid:/ˈkɒlɔɪd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːlɔɪd/ (US)| adj. |胶体的|Relating to or of the nature of a colloid.|The colloid solution scattered light.  这种胶体溶液散射光线。|
colloidal:/kəˈlɔɪdəl/ (UK) /kəˈlɔɪdl/ (US)| adj. |胶体的，胶态的|Relating to or of the nature of a colloid.|The colloidal particles are suspended in the liquid. 胶体颗粒悬浮在液体中。|
