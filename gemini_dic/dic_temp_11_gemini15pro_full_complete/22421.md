bookable:/ˈbʊkəbl/| adj.|可预订的，可预约的|Able to be booked or reserved, especially of a service, facility, or event.|例句：The hotel rooms are bookable online.  酒店房间可以在线预订。|例句：Tickets are bookable through the website.  门票可通过网站预订。|近义词：reservable, available|反义词：unavailable, unbookable|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From book (v.) + -able.  "Book" comes from Old English "bōc", meaning "written document, book," from Proto-Germanic "*bōkō," related to "beech," the tree whose bark was used for writing.  "-able" is a suffix of Latin origin, meaning "capable of being."
