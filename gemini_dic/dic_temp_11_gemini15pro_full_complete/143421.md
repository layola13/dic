outfled:/ˌaʊtˈfled/ | v. | 逃离，飞出 |  To have fled or escaped from, especially by flying.  This word implies a swift and often desperate escape. | The birds outfled the approaching storm. 鸟儿们逃离了即将来临的风暴。 | The family outfled the war-torn country. 这家人逃离了饱受战争蹂躏的国家。| 近义词: escaped, fled, flew away | 反义词: returned, stayed | 动词
