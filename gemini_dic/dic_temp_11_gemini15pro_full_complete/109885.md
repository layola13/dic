liad:/ˈlaɪæd/| n.|捆, 负载, 诗歌集|A bundle; a load; a collection of poems, especially ballads.|He carried a heavy liad of firewood. 他背着一大捆柴火。|近义词：bundle, load, collection|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *liade, from Old English *hlād (“load, cargo”), from Proto-Germanic *hlaþaz (“load, cargo”), from Proto-Indo-European *kʰléh₂dʰos (“load, burden”). Cognate with Scots laid (“load, burden”), West Frisian lêst (“load, burden”), Dutch lading (“cargo”), German Ladung (“cargo, load, charge”), Danish lad (“cargo”), Swedish last (“load, cargo, burden”), Icelandic hlað (“load, cargo, pile”).
