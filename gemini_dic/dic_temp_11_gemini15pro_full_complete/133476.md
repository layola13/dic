nonfeelingly:/nɒnˈfiːlɪŋli/
adv.
无情地，冷漠地
Without feeling or emotion; insensibly.
He spoke nonfeelingly about the tragedy, showing no empathy. 他冷漠地谈论着这场悲剧，没有表现出丝毫的同情心。
She nonfeelingly dismissed his concerns. 她无情地驳回了他的担忧。
近义词: callously, heartlessly, indifferently, apathetically
反义词: compassionately, empathetically, sympathetically, feelingly
词性: 副词
