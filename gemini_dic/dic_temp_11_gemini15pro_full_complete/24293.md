bright:/braɪt/| adj.|明亮的，聪明的，鲜艳的|Shining with light; intelligent; vivid.|例句：The sun is bright today. 今天阳光明媚。|近义词：shining, brilliant, intelligent, smart, vivid, radiant|反义词：dim, dull, dark, stupid|词性：形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *beorht (attested only in compounds like beorhtlic "brightly"), from Proto-Germanic *berhtaz, from Proto-Indo-European *bhereg- "to shine," source also of Greek phlegein "to burn, scorch."
