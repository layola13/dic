come to one's senses:/kʌm tə wʌnz ˈsɛnsɪz/ |verb phrase|恢复理智；醒悟；清醒过来|To begin thinking clearly and rationally again after a period of confusion or irrationality.  It implies regaining control over one's thoughts and actions.|He finally came to his senses and realized the foolishness of his actions. 他终于醒悟过来，意识到了自己行为的愚蠢。| regain consciousness, become aware, see reason, see the light |persist in folly, be deluded, lose one's mind|phrase
