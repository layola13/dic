hyena:/haɪˈiːnə/ | n. |鬣狗|A doglike carnivorous mammal of Africa and southern Asia, having coarse fur, a sloping back, and large teeth adapted for crushing bones.|The hyena laughed maniacally as it devoured its prey. 鬣狗疯狂地笑着吞食猎物。|Synonyms: scavenger, predator | Antonyms: prey | Noun
