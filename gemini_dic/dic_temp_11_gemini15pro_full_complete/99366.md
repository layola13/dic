interdepending:/ˌɪntər dɪˈpɛndɪŋ/ | adj. |相互依存的，互相依赖的 | Existing in a state of mutual dependence.  | Example: The interdepending economies of the two countries made a trade war disastrous for both.  两个国家相互依存的经济使得贸易战对双方都是灾难性的。 | Example: The interdepending parts of the ecosystem are delicate and easily disrupted. 生态系统中相互依存的部分非常脆弱，容易受到破坏。 | Synonym: interconnected, interdependent, interrelated | Antonym: independent, separate, autonomous | 词性:形容词
