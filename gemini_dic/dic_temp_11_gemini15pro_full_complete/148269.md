oversmoked:/ˌōvərˈsmōkt/
adjective
熏制过度的；烟熏味太浓的
Having been smoked for too long, resulting in a strong, often unpleasant flavor.
The bacon was oversmoked and tasted bitter. 培根熏制过度，味道发苦。
oversmoked:/ˌəʊvəˈsməʊkt/
adjective
熏制过度的；烟熏味太浓的
Having been smoked for too long, resulting in a strong, often unpleasant flavor.
The salmon was oversmoked, which masked its delicate flavor.  这块鲑鱼烟熏过度，掩盖了它本身的鲜美。
Synonym: overcured
Antonym: undersmoked
词性: adjective
