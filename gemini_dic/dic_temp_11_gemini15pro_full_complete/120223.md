minesweeper:/ˈmaɪnˌswiːpər/ | n. |扫雷舰，扫雷艇；扫雷器；（电脑游戏）扫雷 |A ship or device used to clear mines from an area, or a popular computer game where the objective is to clear a grid without detonating hidden mines. |例句：He played minesweeper for hours. 他玩了几个小时的扫雷。|例句：The minesweeper carefully navigated the mined waters. 扫雷舰小心翼翼地在布雷水域航行。|近义词：minehunter, minesweeping vessel (for the ship/device) |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
