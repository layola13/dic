diviser:/dɪˈvaɪzər/ (UK), /dəˈvaɪzər/ (US)|n.|除数，因子|A number that divides another number exactly.|The divisor in 10 ÷ 2 = 5 is 2. 10 ÷ 2 = 5 中的除数是 2。|Synonyms: factor, denominator|Antonyms: dividend, numerator|Noun
