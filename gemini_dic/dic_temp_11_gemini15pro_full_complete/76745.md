game-changing:/ˈɡeɪmˌtʃeɪndʒɪŋ/
adjective
变革性的，颠覆性的
Having a significant and disruptive impact on the existing situation or market.
例句：The development of the internet was a game-changing innovation. 互联网的发展是一项颠覆性的创新。

例句：The new technology proved to be game-changing for the industry. 这项新技术被证明对该行业具有变革性的意义。

近义词：revolutionary, transformative, groundbreaking, disruptive, innovative
反义词：insignificant, minor, trivial
词性：adjective
