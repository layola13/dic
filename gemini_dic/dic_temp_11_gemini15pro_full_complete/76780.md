gamic:/ˈɡeɪmɪk/| adj.|婚姻的；配偶的；性爱的|Of or relating to marriage or sexual relations.  Pertaining to gametes or reproduction.|例句：The biologist studied the gamic behavior of the fruit fly.  生物学家研究了果蝇的交配行为。

例句：The gamic rituals of the birds were fascinating to observe. 鸟类的交配仪式令人着迷。|近义词：conjugal, marital, sexual, reproductive|反义词：asexual, non-reproductive|词性:adjective

词源: From Greek *γαμικός (gamikos) "nuptial, of marriage," from *γάμος (gamos) "marriage, wedding."
