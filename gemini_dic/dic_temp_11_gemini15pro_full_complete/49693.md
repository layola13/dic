demiwolf:/ˈdɛmiwʊlf/| n.|半狼（虚构生物）|A fictional creature that is half wolf and half human, or possessing characteristics of both.  It is not a recognized term in zoology or mythology.|The book described a powerful demiwolf, able to shift between human and wolf form. 这本书描述了一个强大的半狼，能够在人类和狼的形态之间转换。|近义词：werewolf, lycanthrope|反义词:human|词性:noun
