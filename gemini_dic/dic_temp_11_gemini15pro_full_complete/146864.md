overindustrializing: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌstriəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
|v.|过度工业化|The process of developing too much industry in a region or country, often leading to negative consequences such as pollution, resource depletion, and social problems.|The city is suffering from the effects of overindustrializing, with polluted air and water.|近义词：overdeveloping|反义词: underdeveloping
|verb|
