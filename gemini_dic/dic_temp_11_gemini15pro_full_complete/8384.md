anti-riot:/ˌæntiˈraɪət/
adjective
防暴的
Designed to control or suppress riots.
The police used anti-riot measures to disperse the crowd. 警察采取了防暴措施来驱散人群。
近义词: riot-control
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "peaceful," "conciliatory")
词性: adjective

anti-riot:/ˌæntiˈraɪət/
noun
防暴装备（通常指复数）
Equipment designed to control or suppress riots.
The police were equipped with anti-riot gear, including shields and helmets. 警察配备了防暴装备，包括盾牌和头盔。
近义词: riot gear
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun
