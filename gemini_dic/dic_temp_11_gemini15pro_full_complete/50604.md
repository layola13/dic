derecho:/dəˈreɪtʃoʊ/ (US), /dɛˈrɛtʃoʊ/ (UK)| n.| 直线风暴，德雷乔|A widespread, long-lived, straight-line windstorm that is associated with a fast-moving group of severe thunderstorms.  It is characterized by damaging winds over a wide area.|The derecho raced across the Midwest, flattening crops and knocking out power. 德雷乔横扫中西部，夷平了庄稼，切断了电力供应。|Synonyms: windstorm, squall line, gale|Antonyms: calm, breeze|noun
