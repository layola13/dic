believeth:/bɪˈliːvɛθ/
verb
相信
Archaic third-person singular present indicative form of believe.  This form is no longer used in modern English.
He believeth that the earth is flat. 他相信地球是平的。（古英语用法，现代英语已不再使用）
近义词:trusts, has faith in
反义词:doubts, disbelieves
词性:verb
