affix-to:/əˈfɪks tuː/| v.|附加到，贴到|To attach or fasten something to something else. |She affixed a stamp to the envelope. 她把邮票贴到信封上。|Synonyms: attach, fasten, stick, adhere |Antonyms: detach, remove, unfasten|verb
Etymology: From Latin affixus, past participle of affigere "to fasten to," from ad- "to" + figere "to fix."
