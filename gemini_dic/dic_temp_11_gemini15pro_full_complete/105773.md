kirkyard:/ˈkɜːkjɑːrd/ (US), /ˈkɜːkjɑːd/ (UK)| n.|教堂墓地；墓地|A burial ground adjoining or surrounding a church.  It is an older, more poetic term than "churchyard," often evoking a sense of history and solemnity.|The crumbling headstones in the kirkyard whispered tales of generations past. 教堂墓地里破败的墓碑低声诉说着过去几代人的故事。|synonym: churchyard, cemetery, graveyard, burial ground|antonym:  (No direct antonym, but consider "living quarters," "city," or "town")|noun
