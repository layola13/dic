hyperanarchy:/ˌhaɪpərˈænərki/
n.
超级无政府状态; 极端无政府状态
A state of extreme anarchy or disorder, characterized by the absence of any overarching authority or structure, often leading to chaos and violence.
The collapse of the central government resulted in hyperanarchy, with various factions vying for control. 中央政府的崩溃导致了超级无政府状态，各派系争夺控制权。
The internet, some argue, fosters a sense of hyperanarchy, where information spreads unchecked and traditional hierarchies are challenged.  有些人认为，互联网助长了一种超级无政府状态，信息不受控制地传播，传统等级制度受到挑战。
Synonyms: chaos, lawlessness, disorder, pandemonium
Antonyms: order, control, government, stability
