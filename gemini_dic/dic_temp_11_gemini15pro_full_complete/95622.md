incompassionate:/ˌɪnkəmˈpæʃənət/| adj.|无同情心的，冷酷的|Lacking compassion; unfeeling; hardhearted.  Not sympathetic to the pain or suffering of others.|He gave an incompassionate shrug when he heard about the tragedy.  他对这场悲剧无动于衷地耸了耸肩。|Synonyms: unsympathetic, callous, heartless, cold, uncaring|Antonyms: compassionate, sympathetic, caring, kind, empathetic|adjective
