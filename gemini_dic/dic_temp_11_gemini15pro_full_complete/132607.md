nondiplomatic:/ˌnɒndɪpləˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 非外交的；不圆滑的；不老练的 | Lacking tact and sensitivity in dealing with others; blunt or insensitive. |  His nondiplomatic approach offended many people. 他的不圆滑的处事方式冒犯了很多人。| Synonyms: tactless, undiplomatic, blunt, insensitive, rude, indelicate | Antonyms: diplomatic, tactful, sensitive, polite, courteous | adjective

Etymology:
From non- + diplomatic. "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of." "Diplomatic" comes from the French word "diplomatique" and ultimately from the Greek word "diploma," meaning "a folded document."
