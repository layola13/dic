man-boy:/ˈmænˌbɔɪ/| n.|男人男孩; 半大男孩; 小伙子|A male of an age somewhere between boyhood and manhood, typically a teenager or young adult. |He's still a man-boy, not quite ready for the responsibilities of adulthood. 他还是个半大男孩，还没有准备好承担成年人的责任。|

He was awkward and gangly, a real man-boy. 他笨手笨脚，又高又瘦，真是个小伙子。|Synonyms: adolescent, youth, teenager, young man|Antonyms: adult, man, grown-up|Noun
