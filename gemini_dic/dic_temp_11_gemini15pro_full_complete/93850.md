immobilities:/ɪˌmoʊˈbɪlɪtiz/
n.
不动产; 固定性; 不动性; 无法移动的状态
The state of being immobile or incapable of movement.  The lack of ability to move or be moved.
The patient's immobilities after the stroke were a major concern.  中风后病人的无法移动是一个主要问题。

The immobilities of large investments can make them risky.  大额投资的固定性可能会使它们变得有风险。

近义词:inactivity, stillness, paralysis, rigidity
反义词:mobility, movement, flexibility, fluidity
词性:名词(plural)
