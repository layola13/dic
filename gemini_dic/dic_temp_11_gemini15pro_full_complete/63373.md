entomostraca:/ˌɛntəˈmɒstrəkə/ | n. | 甲壳纲(Entomostraca)|A large and diverse subclass of crustaceans, mostly of small size, including water fleas, barnacles, copepods, etc., formerly considered a distinct taxonomic group but now known to be paraphyletic. |  Many entomostraca are important components of the zooplankton. 许多甲壳纲动物是浮游动物的重要组成部分。 | synonyms:  crustacean, zooplankton (in part) | antonyms:  (None, as this is a broad biological group with no clear opposite.) | noun
