orbitolites:/ɔːrˈbɪtəlaɪtiːz/| n. |盘Orbitolites; 圆盘虫;  |A genus of large, disc-shaped foraminifera, common in shallow tropical waters.  A type of marine protozoan belonging to the foraminifera family, characterized by its disc-like or lenticular test (shell).|The orbitolites shells are abundant in the fossil record. 圆盘虫壳在化石记录中很丰富。|Orbitolites are important indicators of past environments. 圆盘虫是过去环境的重要指标。|Synonyms: foraminiferan, nummulite|Antonyms: N/A|noun
