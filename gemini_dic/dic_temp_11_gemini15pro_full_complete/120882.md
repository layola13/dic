misbrewed:/ˌmɪsˈbruːd/
adjective
酿造失败的；调制失败的
incorrectly brewed; badly made (of a drink, especially beer or coffee).
The misbrewed coffee tasted bitter. 这杯酿坏的咖啡尝起来很苦。
Near synonyms: badly brewed, poorly brewed, badly made
Antonyms: well-brewed, perfectly brewed
词性: 形容词
