co-payment:/ˈkoʊˌpeɪmənt/
n.|共同支付，分摊费用|A fixed amount that an insured person must pay for covered healthcare services, in addition to what the insurer pays.|例句：My co-payment for a doctor's visit is $25. 我每次看医生的共同支付费用是25美元。|例句：The co-payment for this prescription is $10.  这张处方的共同支付费用是10美元。|近义词：cost-sharing, out-of-pocket expense|反义词:|词性:noun
