decompoundable:/ˌdiːkəmˈpaʊndəbl/
adj.
可分解的，可化解的
Capable of being decomposed or broken down into simpler constituent parts.
This complex chemical is decompoundable into several simpler elements.  这种复杂的化合物可以分解成几种更简单的元素。
The problem, though complex, is decompoundable into smaller, manageable parts. 虽然这个问题很复杂，但可以分解成更小、更容易处理的部分。
近义词: separable, divisible, analyzable
反义词: indivisible, inseparable, irreducible
词性: adjective
词源: From de- (meaning "reverse" or "remove") + compound (meaning "to combine") + -able (suffix meaning "capable of being").
