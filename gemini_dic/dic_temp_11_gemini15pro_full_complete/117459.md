The restaurant's menu was mediterraneanized with the addition of fresh seafood and grilled vegetables.  这家餐厅的菜单添加了新鲜的海鲜和烤蔬菜，使其更具地中海特色。 | 近义词: adapt, modify, transform | 反义词:  None | 词性: verb
