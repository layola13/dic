conjuring:/ˈkʌndʒərɪŋ/ | n. | 召唤，变戏法，魔术 | The act of performing magic tricks or summoning spirits. | He amazed the audience with his conjuring tricks. 他用他的戏法使观众惊叹。 | synonyms: magic, sorcery, witchcraft, invocation | antonyms: dispelling, banishing | noun

conjure:/ˈkʌndʒər/ | v. | 变戏法，召唤，想象 | To perform magic tricks, summon something as if by magic, or evoke a feeling, memory, or image. |  She seemed to conjure the delicious meal out of thin air. 她像变戏法一样变出了一顿美味的佳肴。  He conjured up images of his childhood. 他想起了童年的景象。 | synonyms: evoke, summon, materialize, materialize | antonyms: banish, dispel, dismiss | verb
