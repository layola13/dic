oppositipetalous: /ˌɒpəzɪtˈpɛtələs/ | adj. | 对瓣的；对生花瓣的 | Situated or arising opposite the petals, as stamens.  Referring to stamens that are attached to the receptacle directly across from the petals, rather than alternating with them. |  The flower has oppositipetalous stamens.  这朵花有对生花瓣的雄蕊。 |  antipetalous | alternate, alternipetalous | adjective
