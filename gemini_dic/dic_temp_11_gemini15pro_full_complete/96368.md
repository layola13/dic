indiscernibly:/ˌɪndɪˈsɜːrnɪbli/ /ˌɪndɪˈsɝːnɪbli/
adverb
难以察觉地；不易察觉地
in a way that is impossible to see, hear, or notice clearly.
The lines on the map were almost indiscernibly faint. 地图上的线条几乎淡得看不出来。
Near synonyms: imperceptibly, unnoticeably, subtly
Antonyms: noticeably, perceptibly, obviously, clearly
词性: 副词
