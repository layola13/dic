bicentennials:/ˌbaɪsenˈtɛniəlz/
noun
两百周年纪念；二百周年纪念活动
A bicentennial is the two-hundredth anniversary of a significant event.

The United States celebrated its bicentennial in 1976.
美国于1976年庆祝了其二百周年纪念。

Many towns across the country held bicentennials in 2000.
2000年，全国许多城镇都举行了两百周年纪念活动。

Synonym: bicentenaries
Antonym: (No direct antonym, consider using words like "anniversary" with a different number)
词性: 名词
