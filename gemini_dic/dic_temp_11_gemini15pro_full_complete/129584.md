non-collection:/ˌnɒn kəˈlɛkʃən/ | n. |未收款项；未收账款| The non-collection of debts can severely impact a company's financial stability. 未收账款会严重影响公司的财务稳定性。 | The report highlighted the issue of non-collection of taxes. 报告强调了税收未收的问题。 | 近义词：non-payment, arrears, outstanding debts | 反义词：collection, payment | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "collection" (from Latin "colligere," meaning "to gather").  The word signifies the absence or lack of collection.
