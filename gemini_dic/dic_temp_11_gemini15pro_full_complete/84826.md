hang-around:/ˈhæŋ əˈraʊnd/ | n. | 闲荡的人；狐朋狗友；（某处的）常客 | A person who spends a lot of time in a particular place or with a particular group of people, often without a clear purpose. | He's one of the hang-arounds at the local bar.  他是当地酒吧的常客之一。|  loafer, idler, regular |  busybody, worker | noun


hang-around:/ˈhæŋ əˈraʊnd/ | v. | 闲逛；逗留；徘徊 | To spend time in a place or with someone, usually without a specific purpose. |  I hung around the mall for a few hours. 我在商场闲逛了几个小时。| loiter, linger, stay | leave, depart | verb
