aquamarine:/ˌækwəməˈriːn/ | n. adj. |海蓝宝石；海蓝色的 |A transparent blue-green variety of beryl, used as a gemstone. Also used to describe this color. |例句：She wore a necklace with a large aquamarine pendant. 她戴着一条镶有大海蓝宝石吊坠的项链。

例句：The water was a beautiful aquamarine color. 水呈现出美丽的碧绿色。|近义词：beryl, teal, turquoise, cyan |反义词: (No direct antonym for a color.  Could consider contrasting colors like red, orange, yellow) |词性: noun, adjective

aquamarines:/ˌækwəməˈriːnz/ | n. |海蓝宝石（复数） |Plural form of aquamarine.  Refers to multiple aquamarine gemstones. |例句：The jeweler displayed a collection of sparkling aquamarines.  珠宝商展示了一系列闪耀的海蓝宝石。

例句：She inherited a set of aquamarines from her grandmother. 她从祖母那里继承了一套海蓝宝石。|近义词：beryls (in a general sense) |反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性: noun
