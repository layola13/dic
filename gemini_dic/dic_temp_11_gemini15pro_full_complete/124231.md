Mossad: /moʊˈsɑːd/ | n. | 摩萨德（以色列情报及特别行动局）| The Institute for Intelligence and Special Operations, commonly known by its Hebrew acronym Mossad, is the national intelligence agency of Israel. | The Mossad is known for its covert operations. 摩萨德以其秘密行动而闻名。| The Mossad played a key role in the capture of Adolf Eichmann. 摩萨德在抓捕阿道夫·艾希曼的行动中扮演了关键角色。 | 近义词: intelligence agency, spy agency | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑"public relations agency"等表示公开机构的词) | 词性: noun
