fightback:/ˈfaɪtbæk/| n.|反击，抵抗，反攻|An organized attempt to resist or overcome something.|The team launched a determined fightback in the second half.  该队在下半场发动了坚决的反击。|Example:  After being down by three goals, the team staged a dramatic fightback and won the game.  在落后三球的情况下，这支队伍进行了戏剧性的反击并赢得了比赛。|Synonyms: counterattack, resistance, comeback|Antonyms: surrender, submission|noun


fight back:/faɪt bæk/| v.|反击，抵抗，反攻|To resist or oppose something strongly, especially an attack or unwelcome change.|The residents fought back against the proposed development. 居民们奋起反抗拟议中的开发项目。|Example:  He fought back the tears as he listened to the sad news.  听到这个悲伤的消息，他强忍着不让眼泪流下来。|Synonyms: resist, oppose, defend|Antonyms: surrender, submit|verb
