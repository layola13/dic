ephemeralism: /ɪˈfɛmərəlɪzəm/ | n. | 短暂性；朝生暮死 | The belief in or acceptance of the ephemeral nature of things, especially that beauty is fleeting. | The artist's work explored the ephemeralism of youth and beauty.  这位艺术家的作品探讨了青春和美丽的短暂性。 | Synonyms: transience, brevity, impermanence | Antonyms: permanence, eternity, immortality | Noun

ephemeral: /ɪˈfɛmərəl/ | adj. | 短暂的；朝生暮死的 | Lasting for a very short time. |  The beauty of the cherry blossoms is ephemeral.  樱花的美丽是短暂的。 | Synonyms: fleeting, transient, short-lived | Antonyms: permanent, lasting, enduring | Adjective
