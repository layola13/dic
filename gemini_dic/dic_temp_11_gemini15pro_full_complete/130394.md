non-vintage:/ˌnɒn ˈvɪntɪdʒ/ /ˌnɑːn ˈvɪntɪdʒ/| adj.|非年份的，非特定年份的|Not from a specific vintage year, especially of wine. Refers to a blend of wines from different years.|This is a non-vintage champagne, ideal for everyday celebrations. 这是一款非年份香槟，适合日常庆祝。|Synonyms: multi-vintage, blended|Antonyms: vintage|adjective
