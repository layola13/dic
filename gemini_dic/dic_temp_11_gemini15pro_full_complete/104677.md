karstenite:/ˈkɑːrstəˌnaɪt/| n. |硬石膏|Anhydrous calcium sulfate, CaSO4, occurring as a mineral, often in massive granular or earthy form.  It is a common constituent of evaporite deposits.|Karstenite was found in the cave.  硬石膏在洞穴中被发现。|Synonyms: anhydrite, anhydrous gypsum|Antonyms: gypsum (hydrated form)|noun
