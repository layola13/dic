nonsimplicities:/ˌnɒnsɪmˈplɪsɪtiz/| n. |非简单性；复杂性；|The nonsimplicities of the legal system can be overwhelming.  法律制度的复杂性可能让人难以承受。|英文解析: The plural form of nonsimplicity, referring to the state or quality of not being simple; complexities.  非简单性的复数形式，指的是不简单的状态或性质；复杂性。|例句：The nonsimplicities of the situation made it difficult to find a solution. 情况的复杂性使得找到解决方案变得困难。|近义词：complexities, intricacies, complications|反义词：simplicities, easiness|词性: 名词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + simplicity + -ities (plural suffix).  non-（表示“不”的前缀）+ simplicity + -ities（复数后缀）。
