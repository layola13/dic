nonminerals:/nɒnˈmɪnərəlz/ | n. | 非矿物质 | Substances that are not minerals, typically organic materials.  Derived from "non-" (not) + "minerals." |  Compost and peat moss are good examples of nonminerals for your garden.  堆肥和泥炭藓是花园中非矿物质的好例子。 | synonyms: organic matter, non-inorganic matter | antonyms: minerals, inorganic matter | noun
