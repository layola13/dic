interjectionary:/ˌɪntərˈdʒɛkʃənəri/
adjective
感叹的；插入感叹词的
Throwing in an interjectionary "Wow!" showed her surprise.  插入一句感叹词“哇！”表明了她很惊讶。
Having the quality of an interjection; uttered as an interjection.  具有感叹词的性质；像感叹词一样发出。
"Well, I never!" she said interjectionarily. “好吧，我从来没有！”她感叹道。
近义词:exclamatory, ejaculatory
反义词:unemotional, impassive
词性:adjective

词源: Late 19th century: from interjection + -ary.
