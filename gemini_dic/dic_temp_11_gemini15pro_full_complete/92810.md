iconoclast:/aɪˈkɒnəklæst/ (US) /aɪˈkɑːnəklæst/| n.|破坏偶像者；打破传统观念者|A person who attacks cherished beliefs or institutions.|例句：The professor, a renowned iconoclast, challenged the conventional wisdom about the causes of the war.  这位教授是一位著名的打破传统观念者，他挑战了关于战争起因的传统观点。

|例句：She was an iconoclast in the fashion world, rejecting established trends and creating her own unique style.  她是时尚界的反传统者，拒绝既定趋势，创造自己独特的风格。

|近义词：rebel, maverick, dissenter, nonconformist, heretic|反义词：conformist, traditionalist|词性：noun
