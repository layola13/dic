uncertainty:/ʌnˈsɜːrtnti/ | n. |不确定性，不确定，无把握 |The state of being uncertain; doubt.  Lack of certainty or confidence. |The uncertainty of the future makes me anxious. 未来的不确定性让我焦虑。| synonyms: doubt, ambiguity, vagueness | antonyms: certainty, assurance, confidence | noun

Etymology: From Late Middle English:  *incerteinte*, from Old French *incerteinte*, based on Latin *incertus* 'unsettled, not sure'.
