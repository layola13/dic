colorfast:/ˈkʌlərˌfæst/| adj.|不褪色的，耐洗的|Resistant to fading or running of colors, especially in washing.|This shirt is colorfast, so you don't have to worry about the color bleeding. 这件衬衫不褪色，所以你不必担心掉色。|The curtains are colorfast and won't fade in the sun.  这些窗帘不褪色，在阳光下也不会褪色。|近义词：non-fading, dye-fast|反义词：non-colorfast, fugitive|词性:形容词

Etymology->
color + fast. "fast" here means "firmly fixed," from Old English fæst "firmly fixed, constant; strong, fortified,"
