enclitic:/ɪnˈklɪtɪk/ | n. adj.| 附着词，附着的 |A word or part of a word that is unstressed and attached phonologically to a preceding word.  It functions as part of that word. | The word "n't" in "isn't" is an enclitic. | 近义词: clitic | 反义词: proclitic | 词性: noun, adjective

enclitic:/ɪnˈklɪtɪk/ | adj.| 附着的 | Pronounced with very little emphasis and attached to the preceding word. | He often uses enclitic forms in his speech. | 近义词: dependent, attached | 反义词: independent, detached | 词性: adjective
