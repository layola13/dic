homesteaded:/ˈhoʊmstedɪd/| v.|取得宅基地；在宅基地上居住；|The family homesteaded 160 acres in Oklahoma. 这家人在俄克拉荷马州获得了160英亩的宅基地。|近义词：settled, colonized|反义词:|verb

Etymology:
From homestead (n.) + -ed.  The noun "homestead" comes from Old English "hamstede," meaning a home or dwelling place.
