discourager:/dɪˈskʌrɪdʒər/| n.|阻止者；劝阻者；令人沮丧的人或事物|A person or thing that discourages someone. |The rain was a real discourager for the picnic. 这场雨真是野餐的扫兴鬼。|Synonyms: deterrent, obstacle, impediment |Antonyms: encouragement, incentive|noun


discourage:/dɪˈskʌrɪdʒ/| vt.|阻止；劝阻；使灰心；使气馁|To cause (someone) to lose confidence or enthusiasm; to try to prevent something |The high cost of college discouraged him from applying.  高昂的大学费用使他打消了申请的念头。|Synonyms: deter, dissuade, hinder |Antonyms: encourage, inspire, motivate|verb
