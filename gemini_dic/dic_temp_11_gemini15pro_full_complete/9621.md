antistate:/ˈæntiˈsteɪt/
adjective
反国家的；反对国家存在的
Opposed to the existence or authority of a state or government.
The antistate militia group plotted to overthrow the government.  反国家的民兵组织密谋推翻政府。
Synonyms: anarchist, rebellious, revolutionary, insurgent, seditious
Antonyms: pro-government, statist, loyalist
adjective

antistate:/ˈæntiˈsteɪt/
noun
反国家主义者；反对国家存在的人
A person who is opposed to the existence or authority of a state or government.
He was known as an antistate activist and frequently clashed with authorities. 他被称为反国家活动家，经常与当局发生冲突。
Synonyms: anarchist, rebel, revolutionary, insurgent
Antonyms: statist, loyalist
