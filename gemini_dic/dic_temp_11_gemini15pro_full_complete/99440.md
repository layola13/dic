interest-free:/ˈɪntrɪst friː/
adjective
无息的，免息的
Not charging or incurring interest.
This is an interest-free loan.  这是一笔无息贷款。
The company offers an interest-free credit period of 30 days.  该公司提供30天的无息信用期。
近义词: no-interest, zero-interest
反义词: interest-bearing
词性: adjective


interest:/ˈɪntrɪst/
noun
兴趣，利息
The feeling of wanting to know or learn about something or someone.  A sum paid or charged for the use of money or for borrowing money
She has a keen interest in music. 她对音乐很感兴趣。
The bank charged 5% interest on the loan. 银行对这笔贷款收取了5%的利息。
近义词: curiosity, concern, stake, share
反义词: apathy, indifference
词性: noun


free:/friː/
adjective, adverb, verb
自由的，免费的，释放
Not under the control or in the power of another; costing nothing, or not needing to be paid for; release from captivity or a difficult situation.
Birds are free to fly wherever they want. 鸟儿可以自由地飞到任何想去的地方。
The museum offers free admission on Sundays.  博物馆在周日免费开放。
He freed the bird from its cage. 他把鸟从笼子里放了出来。
近义词: liberated, independent, complimentary, gratis, release
反义词: captive, restricted, paid, confine
词性: adjective, adverb, verb
