overplausibleness:/ˌoʊvərˌplɔːzəˈbɪlnəs/| n. | 过于似是而非 | The quality of seeming too reasonable or probable, thus arousing suspicion.  It suggests an excessive or artificial appearance of truthfulness. |  His overplausibleness made me doubt his story. 他的过于似是而非的说法让我怀疑他的故事。| credibility, believability | implausibility, incredibility | noun
