nectarine:/ˈnɛktərɪn/ /ˈnɛktəriːn/| n. |油桃; |A smooth-skinned peach. A fruit that looks like a peach but has smooth skin.| I prefer nectarines to peaches because I dislike fuzzy skin. 我更喜欢油桃而不是桃子，因为我不喜欢毛茸茸的表皮。|Synonyms: smooth-skinned peach |Antonyms: peach (in the sense of having fuzzy skin)| noun
