air-drying:/ˈer ˌdraɪɪŋ/ | adj. |风干的;  空气干燥的|The process of drying something by exposing it to the air, rather than using artificial heat. | I prefer air-drying my clothes to using a tumble dryer. 我更喜欢把衣服晾干而不是用烘干机。|  The herbs were air-drying in the kitchen.  这些草药正在厨房里风干。| synonyms:  line-drying, drip-drying, sun-drying | antonyms:  oven-drying, kiln-drying, dehydrating | adjective

air-drying:/ˈer ˌdraɪɪŋ/ | n. |风干; 空气干燥|The process of drying something by exposing it to the air. | Air-drying is a more energy-efficient way to dry clothes.  风干是一种更节能的干衣方式。| The air-drying of the paint took several hours. 油漆风干花了几个小时。| synonyms:  line-drying, drip-drying | antonyms:  oven-drying, kiln-drying, dehydration | noun
