martello:/mɑːrˈtɛloʊ/| n.| 铁锤；马泰洛塔；（音乐）强音|A heavy hammer; a type of fortified tower built by the British in Corsica and elsewhere in the 19th century; (music) a forceful, accented note, especially on a harpsichord.|The British built martello towers along the coast to defend against invasion. 英国人在沿海建造了马泰洛塔以抵御入侵。|近义词：hammer, tower, fortress, accent|反义词:|noun
