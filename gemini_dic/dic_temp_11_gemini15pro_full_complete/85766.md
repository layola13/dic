haybird:/ˈheɪbɜːrd/| n. |乡巴佬，土包子|A derogatory term for a rustic or unsophisticated person, often used in a condescending way.|例句：He called them haybirds, but they were just simple country folk. 他称他们为乡巴佬，但他们只是普通的乡下人。|例句：Don't be a haybird, learn some manners! 别像个土包子，学点礼貌！|近义词：yokel, hick, bumpkin|反义词:sophisticate, cosmopolitan|词性:名词
