megastores:/ˈmɛɡəˌstɔːrz/| n. |大型商店，特大商店|A very large store, typically one of a chain.|  例句：We bought all our furniture at one of the megastores on the edge of town. 我们在城郊的一家大型商店买了所有家具。

例句：Megastores offer a wide variety of products. 大型商店提供各种各样的产品。

|近义词：superstores, hypermarkets, big-box stores|反义词:small shops, boutiques|词性:名词
