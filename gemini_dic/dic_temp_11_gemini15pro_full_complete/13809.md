auditability:/ˌɔːdɪtəˈbɪləti/
n.|可审计性|The quality of being able to be audited.  This refers to the ability to trace and verify the history of transactions or activities.|The company improved the auditability of its financial records. 这家公司提高了其财务记录的可审计性。|traceability, verifiability|inauditability|noun


auditabilities:/ˌɔːdɪtəˈbɪlətiz/
n.|可审计性（复数）|Plural form of auditability, referring to multiple instances or aspects of being auditable.|The auditabilities of different departments were assessed separately.  不同部门的可审计性被分别评估。|traceabilities, verifiabilities|inauditabilities|noun
