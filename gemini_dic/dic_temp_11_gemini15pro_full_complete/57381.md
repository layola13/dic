doze-away:/doʊz əˈweɪ/
v.
打瞌睡度过（时间）；迷迷糊糊地消磨（时间）
spend (time) sleeping lightly or half-sleeping; to while away (time) by dozing
He dozed away the afternoon in his armchair. 他在扶手椅上打瞌睡度过了下午。
近义词: sleep away, while away, laze away
反义词: stay awake, be alert
