hypermutabilities:/ˌhaɪpərˌmjuːtəˈbɪlɪtiz/ | n. | 高突变率；高变异性 | The tendency of certain organisms or cells to mutate at a much higher rate than normal.  This can be due to defects in DNA repair mechanisms or other factors. |  Research is ongoing into the hypermutabilities observed in some cancer cells.  一些癌细胞中观察到的高突变率正在进行研究。 | hypervariability, instability | stability | noun
