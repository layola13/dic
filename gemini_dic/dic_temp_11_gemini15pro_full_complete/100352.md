intersticed:/ˌɪntərˈstɪst/
adjective
散布的，点缀的
spaced with something at intervals
例句：The fields were intersticed with groves of trees. 田野上散布着树林。

例句：Her speech was intersticed with witty remarks. 她的演讲中穿插着机智的评论。

近义词：interspaced, interspersed, dotted, scattered
反义词：solid, continuous, unbroken
词性：adjective


词源:
From Latin *interstitium (“interval”), from inter (“between”) + sistō (“stand”).
