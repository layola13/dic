gordier:/ˈɡɔːrdiər/| adj.|更复杂的，更棘手的|More intricate or difficult to solve; more knotted. |The plot of the novel became gordier with each chapter. 这部小说的情节每一章都变得更加复杂。|近义词：more complex, more intricate, knottier, trickier|反义词: simpler, easier, less complicated|词性:形容词
词源: From "gordian," ultimately from the legend of the Gordian Knot, a complex knot tied by Gordius, king of Phrygia, which was said to be impossible to untie. Alexander the Great supposedly cut it with his sword.
