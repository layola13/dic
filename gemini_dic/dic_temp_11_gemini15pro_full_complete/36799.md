colon: /ˈkoʊlən/ (US) /ˈkəʊlɒn/ (UK) | n. | 冒号 | A punctuation mark (:) used to precede a list of items, a quotation, or an expansion or explanation. |  例句：
The recipe called for the following ingredients: flour, sugar, eggs, and milk.  食谱要求以下配料：面粉、糖、鸡蛋和牛奶。

He began his speech with a famous quote: "Ask not what your country can do for you..." 他以一句名言开始了他的演讲：“不要问你的国家能为你做什么……”


近义词:  (No direct synonym for the punctuation mark itself, but in the sense of "large intestine", a synonym would be large bowel) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
