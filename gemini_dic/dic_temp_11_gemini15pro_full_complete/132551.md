nondexterousness:/ˌnɒndekˈstɛrəsnəs/ | n. |笨拙，不灵巧 |Lack of dexterity; clumsiness.  The state of being not skillful with one's hands. | His nondexterousness made it difficult for him to tie his shoes. 他的笨拙使他难以系鞋带。| The surgeon's nondexterousness during the operation was alarming.  外科医生在手术过程中的笨拙令人担忧。 | 近义词: clumsiness, awkwardness, ineptness, gaucheness | 反义词: dexterity, skill, adroitness, deftness | 词性: noun
