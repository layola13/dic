excuseless:/ɪkˈskjuːslɪs/
adjective
无借口的，不可原谅的
Without excuse or justification; inexcusable.
His behavior was utterly excuseless. 他的行为完全不可原谅。
近义词: inexcusable, unjustifiable, unforgivable
反义词: excusable, justifiable, forgivable
词性: adjective
词源:  From excuse + -less (suffix meaning "without").
