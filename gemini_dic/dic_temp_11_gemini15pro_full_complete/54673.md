disopinion:/ˌdɪsəˈpɪnjən/
noun
不同意见，异议
Lack of agreement or concord; difference in opinion.
He voiced his disopinion about the new policy. 他表达了对新政策的不同意见。
近义词: disagreement, dissent, discord, variance
反义词: agreement, concord, harmony, consensus
词性:n.
