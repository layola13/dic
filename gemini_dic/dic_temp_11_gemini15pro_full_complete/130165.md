non-retroactive:/ˌnɒnˌrɛtrəʊˈæktɪv/ | adj. | 不溯及既往的 | Not having retroactive effect; not applying to past events or situations. | The new law is non-retroactive, so it won't affect past cases. 新法律不溯及既往，因此不会影响过去的案件。|  The policy change is non-retroactive and applies only to future purchases. 这项政策变更不溯及既往，仅适用于未来的购买。|Synonyms: prospective,  non-applicable to the past | Antonyms: retroactive, retrospective | adjective
