brighter:/ˈbraɪtər/| adj.|更明亮的，更鲜艳的，更聪明的，更快乐的|Comparative form of bright. Having more light or shine. More vivid in color. More intelligent or clever. More cheerful or hopeful.|The sun is brighter today than yesterday. 今天太阳比昨天更明亮。|The flowers in the garden are brighter this year. 今年花园里的花更鲜艳。|synonyms: shinier, lighter, more vivid, smarter, happier|antonyms: dimmer, darker, duller, less intelligent, sadder|adjective
