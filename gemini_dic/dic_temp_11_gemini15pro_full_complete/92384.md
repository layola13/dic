hypocrite:/ˈhɪpəkrɪt/| n.|伪君子，伪善者|A person who pretends to have virtues, moral or religious beliefs, principles, etc., that he or she does not actually possess, especially a person whose actions belie stated beliefs.|例句：He's a hypocrite – he says he cares about the environment, but he drives a gas-guzzling SUV. 他是个伪君子——他说他关心环境，却开着一辆耗油量大的SUV。

例句：She's such a hypocrite! She criticizes other people for gossiping, but she does it all the time herself. 她真是个伪君子！她批评别人说闲话，但她自己却一直在说。

|近义词：phony, fraud, charlatan, impostor, deceiver|反义词：sincere person, honest person|词性: Noun
