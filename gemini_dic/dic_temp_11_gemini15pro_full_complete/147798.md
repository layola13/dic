overprotects: /ˌoʊvərprəˈtekts/
verb
过度保护
Shields someone excessively from harm or difficulty, often hindering their development of independence and resilience.
He overprotects his children, never letting them experience any hardship. 他过度保护他的孩子，从不让他们经历任何困难。
overprotect, cosset, shield, shelter
neglect, endanger, expose
v.
