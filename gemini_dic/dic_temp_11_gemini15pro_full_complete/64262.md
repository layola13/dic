equiponderous:/ˌiːkwɪˈpɒndərəs/ /ˌiːkwɪˈpɑːndərəs/
adjective
等重的；同等重要的
equal in weight, importance, or power.

The two sides in the debate presented equiponderous arguments.  辩论双方提出了同等重要的论点。

The two boxes were equiponderous.  这两个盒子一样重。

synonym: equal, equivalent, commensurate, comparable
antonym: unequal, disparate, disproportionate
词性: adjective
