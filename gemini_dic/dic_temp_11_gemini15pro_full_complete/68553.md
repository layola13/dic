fanfictions:/ˈfænˌfɪkʃənz/| n. |同人小说|Stories written by fans of a particular work (such as a movie, TV show, or book), using existing characters and settings.|She writes Harry Potter fanfictions in her free time. 她空闲时间写哈利波特同人小说。|近义词：fanfiction, fan works|反义词:original fiction|词性:名词

fanfiction:/ˈfænˌfɪkʃən/| n. |同人小说|Stories written by fans of a particular work (such as a movie, TV show, or book), using existing characters and settings.|He posted his new fanfiction online. 他把他的新同人小说发布到网上了。|近义词：fanfictions, fan works|反义词:original fiction|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
"Fan" (short for "fanatic") + "fiction".  "Fan" traces back to the Latin "fanaticus," meaning "inspired by a deity, frenzied."  "Fiction" comes from the Latin "fictio," meaning "a shaping, a feigning."
