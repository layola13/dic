duddy:/ˈdʌdi/| n. |破旧的衣服；衣衫褴褛的人；无用之物|Old or ragged clothing; a person dressed in ragged clothes; something worthless or insignificant.|He was wearing a duddy old coat. 他穿着一件破旧的大衣。|近义词：rags, tatters, shabby clothes, worthless, insignificant|反义词:finery, best clothes, valuable, significant|词性:noun

词源:
From the Hindi word *dūdhī* meaning "bottle gourd," referring to the white, pulpy, and somewhat useless nature of the vegetable.  Over time, the meaning shifted to encompass anything worthless or insignificant, and eventually came to refer to ragged clothing.
