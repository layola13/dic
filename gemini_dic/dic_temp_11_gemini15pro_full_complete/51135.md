despoiled:/dɪˈspɔɪld/
verb
掠夺，抢劫，破坏
To deprive of something valuable by force; rob; plunder.  Also, to deprive of value, beauty, or effectiveness; ruin; mar.
The invaders despoiled the city of its treasures.  侵略者掠夺了这座城市的宝藏。
The oil spill despoiled the coastline. 石油泄漏破坏了海岸线。
synonyms: plundered, pillaged, ravaged, looted, stripped, robbed
antonyms: preserved, protected, restored, enhanced
词性: 动词
