littoral:/ˈlɪtərəl/ (US), /lɪˈtɒrəl/ (UK)| adj. n.|海岸的，沿海的；海岸，沿岸地区|Relating to or situated on the shore of the sea or a lake, especially between the high and low water marks.  Also, the region lying along a shore.|The hotel was built in the littoral zone.  这家酒店建在沿海地带。|synonyms: coastal, shoreline, seaside, beachfront|antonyms: inland, interior|adjective, noun

littoral:/ˈlɪtərəl/ (US), /lɪˈtɒrəl/ (UK)| n.|海岸，沿岸地区|The region lying along a shore.|The littoral of the island is rocky and difficult to access.  这个岛的沿岸地区岩石密布，难以进入。|synonyms: coast, shore, shoreline, beachfront|antonyms: inland, interior|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *litoralis*, from *lītus* ("shore").
