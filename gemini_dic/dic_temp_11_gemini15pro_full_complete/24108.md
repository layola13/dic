brew-up:/ˈbruː ʌp/| n. |沏茶，煮咖啡；(非正式)调制饮料|A hot beverage, typically tea or coffee.  The act of making such a beverage.|例句：I'll brew up a pot of tea. 我来沏一壶茶。

例句：Fancy a brew-up? 想喝杯茶/咖啡吗？|近义词：make (tea/coffee), fix (tea/coffee), prepare (tea/coffee)|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑) go without a drink|词性: noun, (less commonly used as a verb)


brew-up:/ˈbruː ʌp/| v. |沏茶，煮咖啡；(非正式)调制饮料|To make a hot beverage, typically tea or coffee.|例句：She brewed up some coffee for us. 她给我们煮了一些咖啡。

例句：Can you brew up a cuppa? 你能泡杯茶吗？|近义词：make (tea/coffee), fix (tea/coffee), prepare (tea/coffee)|反义词:  (没有直接的反义词)|词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "brew" (meaning to infuse hot water with e.g. tea leaves or coffee grounds) + "up" (adverb indicating completion or thoroughness).  The "up" emphasizes the action of making the drink ready.
