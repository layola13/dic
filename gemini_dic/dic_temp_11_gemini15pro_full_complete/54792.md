dispensative:/dɪˈspɛnsətɪv/ | adj. | 分发的，分配的 | Relating to dispensing or distributing; having the power or function of dispensing. | The government has a dispensative role in providing social welfare. 政府在提供社会福利方面起着分配作用。| The charity has a dispensative program for food and clothing to the needy.  这个慈善机构有一个向贫困者分发食物和衣服的计划。 | 近义词: distributive, allocative | 反义词: retentive, withholding | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  From Late Latin *dispensativus, from Latin dispensatus, past participle of dispensare "to dispense," from dis- "apart" + pensare "to weigh."
