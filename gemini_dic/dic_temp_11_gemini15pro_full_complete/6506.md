amusable:/əˈmjuːzəbl/| adj.|有趣的，好笑的|Capable of being amused; entertaining.|例句：The children found the clown highly amusable. 孩子们觉得小丑非常有趣。|例句：She told some highly amusable stories about her travels. 她讲述了一些关于她旅行的非常有趣的故事。|近义词：entertaining, funny, humorous, diverting|反义词：boring, dull, tedious|词性:形容词
词源: From French amuser "to amuse," from Old French amus- "entertain, divert, distract," from a- "out, away" + muser "to muse, loiter, stare fixedly."
