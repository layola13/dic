confiner:/kənˈfaɪnər/
noun
限制者；禁闭者
Someone or something that confines or restricts.
The jailer is the confiner of the prisoners.  狱卒是囚犯的限制者。
The mountains acted as a natural confiner, preventing the spread of the fire. 群山充当了天然的屏障，阻止了火势蔓延。

近义词:restricter, limiter, restrainer
反义词:liberator, emancipator
词性:n.

confiner:/kənˈfaɪnər/
verb (rarely used)
限制；禁闭
To confine or restrict.
This illness confiners him to his bed.  这种疾病把他限制在床上。
The fence confiners the animals to a small area.  栅栏把动物限制在一个小区域里。
近义词:restrict, limit, restrain, imprison
反义词:liberate, free, release
词性:v.
