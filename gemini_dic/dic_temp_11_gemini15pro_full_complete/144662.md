outwriggled: /ˌaʊtˈrɪɡəld/
verb
胜过，比…更灵活，通过扭动胜过
To surpass or defeat someone by wriggling or maneuvering more effectively.
The smaller fish outwriggled the larger predator and escaped. 小鱼比更大的捕食者更灵活，逃脱了。
The gymnast outwriggled her opponent in the floor exercise.  这个体操运动员在地板操中比她的对手更灵活。
outmaneuver, outwit, surpass
be outmaneuvered by, be defeated by
v.
