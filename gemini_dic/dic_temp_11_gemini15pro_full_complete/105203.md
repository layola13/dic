keying:/ˈkiːɪŋ/| n. v.|键入，输入；键控；对准（频率）；楔入|The act of pressing keys on a keyboard or similar device to input data or commands; the process of adjusting the frequency of a radio transmitter to a specific channel; securing something in place with a key or wedge.|He spent hours keying in data. 他花了几个小时输入数据。|The engineer was keying the transmitter to the correct frequency. 工程师正在将发射机的频率调到正确的频率上。|近义词: typing, inputting, entering, adjusting, wedging|反义词: extracting, removing|词性: noun, verb
