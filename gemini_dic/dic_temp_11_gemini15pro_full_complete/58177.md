drowning:/ˈdraʊnɪŋ/| n. |溺水，淹死|The act of dying by suffocation in liquid. |The lifeguard saved the child from drowning. 救生员把孩子从溺水中救了出来。|近义词：suffocation, asphyxiation|反义词:survival, rescue|noun
drowning:/ˈdraʊnɪŋ/| adj. |溺水的，快要淹死的|Experiencing or about to experience drowning. |He heard the drowning man's cries for help. 他听到了溺水男子呼救的声音。|近义词：sinking, submerging|反义词:floating, surviving|adjective
