ectoderm:/ˈektəʊdɜːm/ (UK) /ˈektədɜːrm/ (US)| n.|外胚层|The outermost of the three primary germ layers of an embryo, from which the epidermis, nervous system, and sense organs develop.|The ectoderm gives rise to the skin and nervous system. 外胚层会发育形成皮肤和神经系统。|Synonyms: epiblast|Antonyms: endoderm, mesoderm|noun
