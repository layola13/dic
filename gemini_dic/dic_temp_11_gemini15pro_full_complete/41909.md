cosmically:/ˈkɑːzmɪkli/| adv.|宇宙地；巨大地；无限地|Relating to the cosmos; vast and awe-inspiring.|例句：The event was cosmically significant.  这个事件具有宇宙性的意义。|例句：The two events were cosmically linked. 这两件事在宇宙层面上是相互关联的。|近义词：universally, immensely, vastly, enormously|反义词: trivially, insignificantly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From "cosmic" + "-ly."  "Cosmic" comes from the Late Latin "cosmicus,"  from the Greek "kosmikos," meaning "of the universe," from "kosmos" meaning "order, universe."
