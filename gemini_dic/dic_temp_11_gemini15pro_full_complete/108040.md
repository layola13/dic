latch-key:/ˈlætʃˌkiː/
adjective|钥匙儿童的；挂钥匙儿童的|A latch-key child is a child who returns from school to an empty house because their parents are out at work.|例句：He was a latch-key kid, letting himself into the empty house after school. 他是个挂钥匙的孩子，放学后自己走进空荡荡的房子。|近义词：self-care, unsupervised|反义词:supervised, cared-for
noun|挂锁钥匙|A key, especially one for a latch. |例句：She fumbled with the latch-key, trying to open the door. 她笨手笨脚地摆弄着门闩钥匙，试图打开门。|近义词：key|反义词: N/A
