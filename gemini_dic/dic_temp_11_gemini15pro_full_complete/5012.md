all-but:/ɔːl ˈbʌt/ | adv. | 几乎；差不多 | Almost; very nearly. |  The game was all but over by half-time. | 近义词: almost, nearly, practically, virtually | 反义词: completely, totally | 词性: 副词

词源:  From "all but one," meaning all except one, which then broadened to mean almost entirely.
