demster:/ˈdɛmstər/ | n. |法官；裁判员 (Scottish, historical)| A judge in Scotland, especially in the past. | The demster pronounced the sentence. 法官宣判了判决。| synonym: judge, magistrate, arbiter | antonym: defendant, accused | noun

demster:/ˈdɛmstər/ | n. |宣判员；唱名员 (Isle of Man, historical)| An official on the Isle of Man who formerly proclaimed the laws. | The demster read out the new laws to the assembled people. 唱名员向聚集的人们宣读了新的法律。 | synonym: announcer, proclaimer, herald | antonym: listener, audience | noun
