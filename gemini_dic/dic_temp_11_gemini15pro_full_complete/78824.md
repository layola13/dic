gimmick:/ˈɡɪmɪk/| n.| 花招；噱头；诡计；小玩意儿 | a trick or device intended to attract attention, publicity, or business | The free toy was just a marketing gimmick to get people into the store.  这个免费玩具只是一个吸引人们进店的营销噱头。 | synonyms: trick, ploy, stunt, scheme, device, gadget | antonyms: authenticity, genuineness | noun

gimmicky:/ˈɡɪmɪki/| adj.| 花哨的；噱头的；诡计多端的 | using or characterized by gimmicks | The new product had a gimmicky design, but lacked any real functionality.  新产品的设计很花哨，但缺乏真正的功能性。 | synonyms: tricky, flashy, showy, contrived | antonyms: genuine, authentic, substantial | adjective
