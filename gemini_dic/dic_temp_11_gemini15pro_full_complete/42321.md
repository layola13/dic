counter-revolutionary:/ˌkaʊntərˌrɛvəˈluːʃənɛri/
noun|反革命分子，反革命的|A person who opposes or tries to overthrow a revolution, or something that opposes a revolution.|The government labeled the protesters as counter-revolutionaries.|Synonyms: reactionary, rebel, insurgent|Antonyms: revolutionary, radical|n.

counter-revolutionary:/ˌkaʊntərˌrɛvəˈluːʃənɛri/
adjective|反革命的|Opposing or seeking to overthrow a revolution.|The counter-revolutionary forces attempted a coup.|Synonyms: reactionary, subversive|Antonyms: revolutionary, progressive|adj.
