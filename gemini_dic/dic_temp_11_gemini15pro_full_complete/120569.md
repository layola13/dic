mirid:/ˈmaɪrɪd/ | n. | 盲蝽,盲蝽象 | A mirid is a type of insect belonging to the family Miridae, which is part of the order Hemiptera (true bugs). They are often plant feeders and can be agricultural pests. |  Mirids are common pests in cotton fields. 盲蝽是棉田里的常见害虫。 | synonyms: plant bug, leaf bug | antonyms: N/A | noun
