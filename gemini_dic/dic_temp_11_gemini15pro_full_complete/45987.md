cyanosite: /saɪˈænoʊsaɪt/ | n. |蓝铜矿|A dark blue mineral consisting of hydrated copper sulfate, occurring as prismatic crystals or massive. |例句：Cyanosite is a relatively common secondary copper mineral. 蓝铜矿是一种相对常见的次生铜矿物。

例句：The cyanosite crystals were a beautiful deep blue color. 蓝铜矿晶体呈现美丽的深蓝色。|近义词：chalcanthite|反义词: N/A |词性: noun

词源: From Ancient Greek κύανος (kúanos, “dark blue”) + -site.
