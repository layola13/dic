frau:/fraʊ/
n.|夫人，太太，妻子|A married woman; a wife.  A respectful form of address for a woman.|例句：Frau Schmidt is a very kind woman.  施密特夫人是一位非常和蔼的女士。|例句：Please welcome Frau Dr. Meier to the stage.  请欢迎梅尔博士夫人上台。|近义词：wife, Mrs.|反义词：Mr., Herr|词性:noun
词源:From Middle High German vrou, from Old High German frouwa, from Proto-Germanic *frōwijō, meaning "lady, mistress". Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *per- "forward, through".  Cognate with Dutch vrouw, Afrikaans vrou, English "lady".
