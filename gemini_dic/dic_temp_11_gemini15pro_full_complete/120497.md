minuets:/ˌmɪnjuˈɛts/ | n. |小步舞曲|A slow, stately dance in triple time, popular in the 17th and 18th centuries.  It is also the music for this dance.|The dancers moved gracefully through the minuets. 舞蹈演员们优雅地跳着小步舞曲。|近义词：dance, waltz|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From French *menuet*, diminutive of *menu* (“small, slender”), from Old French.
