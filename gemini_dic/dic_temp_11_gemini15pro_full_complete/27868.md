canoodling:/kəˈnuːd(ə)lɪŋ/
n.|搂抱爱抚，卿卿我我|The act of kissing, caressing, and cuddling amorously.|They were spotted canoodling in the park.  他们被发现正在公园里搂抱爱抚。|Synonyms: cuddling, snuggling, petting, necking|Antonyms: fighting, arguing|noun

canoodle:/kəˈnuːd(ə)l/
v.|搂抱爱抚，卿卿我我|To kiss, caress, and cuddle amorously.|The young couple canoodled on the park bench. 这对年轻情侣在公园长椅上卿卿我我。|Synonyms: cuddle, snuggle, pet, neck|Antonyms: fight, argue|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain, possibly from dialectal *knuddle 'to cuddle', or from Scottish canoodle 'to whisper, chat', or a blend of both. First recorded in the mid-19th century.
