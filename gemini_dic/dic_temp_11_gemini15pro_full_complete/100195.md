v.|相互抵抗，相互抵制|The two groups continued to interresist each other's efforts.|两组继续相互抵制对方的努力。|
近义词:oppose, resist, counteract

词源: "inter-" (between) + "resist" (to withstand the action or effect of).  The prefix "inter-" suggests a reciprocal action between two or more entities.  "Resist" comes from the Latin "resistere" (to stand back, oppose).
