colonizer:/ˈkɒlənaɪzə(r)/ (UK) /ˈkɑːlənaɪzər/ (US)| n.|殖民者；殖民地开拓者|A person who settles in a new region or colony.|The early colonizers faced many hardships. 早期的殖民者面临许多困难。|Synonyms: settler, pioneer, immigrant|Antonyms: native, indigenous|noun

colonizer:/ˈkɒlənaɪzə(r)/ (UK) /ˈkɑːlənaɪzər/ (US)| n.|（生物）定殖者|An organism that establishes itself in a new environment.|Certain bacteria are effective colonizers of the gut. 某些细菌是肠道的有效定殖者。|Synonyms: inhabitant, resident|Antonyms: emigrant|noun
