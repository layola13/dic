numbless:/ˈnʌmlɪs/| adj.|麻木的，失去感觉的|Lacking sensation, especially the ability to feel pain or touch.|My fingers were numbless from the cold. 我的手指冻得麻木了。|Synonyms: insensible, numb, unfeeling, anesthetic, deadened|Antonyms: sensitive, feeling, responsive|adjective

numbless:/ˈnʌmlɪs/| adj.|无情的，冷漠的|Lacking emotional response or sympathy.|She seemed numbless to the suffering around her. 她似乎对周围的苦难无动于衷。|Synonyms: apathetic, indifferent, uncaring, callous, heartless|Antonyms: compassionate, empathetic, sensitive, caring|adjective
