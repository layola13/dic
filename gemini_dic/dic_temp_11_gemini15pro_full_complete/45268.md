cultivates:/ˈkʌltɪveɪts/ |v.|耕作，培养，陶冶|develops or improves something, such as a skill, quality, or relationship|She cultivates a wide circle of friends.|近义词:develops, fosters, nurtures, promotes, refines|反义词:neglects, ignores, abandons|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cultivare*, from Latin *cultus* "care, cultivation," past participle of *colere* "to till, cultivate, inhabit, protect, worship."
