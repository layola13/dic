lichenous:/ˈlaɪkənəs/| adj.|青苔状的；地衣状的|Covered with or resembling lichens; having the characteristics of a lichen. |The lichenous rocks provided a perfect camouflage for the small lizards.  这些长满青苔的岩石为小蜥蜴提供了完美的伪装。|The old wall was lichenous and crumbling.  古老的墙壁长满了青苔，正在 crumbling。|近义词：mossy, covered with lichen|反义词: smooth, bare|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *līchēnōsus, from līchēn (“lichen”) + -ōsus (suffix forming adjectives of abundance). Ultimately from Ancient Greek λειχήν (leikhḗn, “lichen”).
