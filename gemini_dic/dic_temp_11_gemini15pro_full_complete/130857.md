nonaquatic:/ˌnɒn əˈkwætɪk/ /ˌnɑːn əˈkwɑːtɪk/
adjective
非水生的；陆生的
Not living or growing in water; terrestrial.
例句：Nonaquatic plants thrive in dry soil. 非水生植物在干燥的土壤中茁壮成长。

例句：Cacti are nonaquatic plants adapted to desert conditions. 仙人掌是非水生植物，适应沙漠环境。

近义词：terrestrial, land-based
反义词：aquatic, marine
词性：adjective
