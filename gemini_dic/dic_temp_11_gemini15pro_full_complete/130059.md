non-polar:/ˌnɒnˈpəʊlə(r)/ /ˌnɑːnˈpoʊlər/| adj.|非极性的|A non-polar molecule has no separation of charge, so no positive or negative poles are formed.|Oil and water don't mix because water is polar and oil is non-polar. 油和水不能混合，因为水是极性的，而油是非极性的。|
non-polar:/ˌnɒnˈpəʊlə(r)/ /ˌnɑːnˈpoʊlər/| adj.|非极性的|Non-polar substances are not soluble in polar solvents. 非极性物质不溶于极性溶剂。|例句：Methane is a non-polar molecule. 甲烷是一个非极性分子。|近义词：hydrophobic, lipophilic|反义词:polar|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
non-: prefix meaning "not" from Latin *non
polar: from Latin *polaris "relating to the poles," from polus "pole, end of an axis" from Greek polos "pivot, axis, pole"
