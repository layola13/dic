decollectivizing: /ˌdiːkəˈlɛktɪvaɪzɪŋ/
verb
非集体化，取消集体化
The process of dismantling collective farms and returning land to individual ownership.
The government began decollectivizing agriculture in the 1990s. 政府在20世纪90年代开始农业非集体化。
decollectivisation, privatization
collectivization, nationalization
