laver:/ˈleɪvər/ (US), /ˈlɑːvər/ (UK)| n.| 洗脸盆，水盆；食用紫菜|A basin or large bowl for washing, especially one used in a religious ceremony; an edible seaweed.|The priest washed his hands in the laver before the ceremony. 神父在仪式前在水盆里洗手。|She added dried laver to the soup. 她往汤里加了干紫菜。|Synonyms: basin, bowl (for washing); seaweed, nori (for edible seaweed)|Antonyms: None|noun

laver:/ˈleɪvər/ (US), /ˈlɑːvər/ (UK)| n.| (经常用复数) 海藻类植物|Various kinds of edible seaweed.|Lavers are a good source of vitamins and minerals.  紫菜富含维生素和矿物质。|Synonyms: seaweed, algae|Antonyms: None|noun
