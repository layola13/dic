hypervirulent:/ˌhaɪpərˈvɪrʊlənt/| adj.|高毒力的，极度危险的|Having or showing an unusually high degree of virulence.|The study identified a hypervirulent strain of the fungus responsible for the outbreak.  该研究确定了一种导致疫情爆发的真菌的高毒力菌株。|Synonyms: highly infective, highly pathogenic|Antonyms: weakly virulent|adjective
