expounds:/ɪkˈspaʊndz/ /ɪkˈspaʊndz/| v. |解释；阐述；详细说明|To explain or interpret something in detail; to clarify or elaborate on a subject.|The professor expounds on the theories of relativity.  教授详细阐述了相对论的理论。

The article expounds the benefits of a vegetarian diet. 这篇文章详细阐述了素食的好处。|近义词：explain, interpret, clarify, elaborate, elucidate|反义词：obfuscate, confuse, obscure|词性:动词
