jonesian:/ˈdʒoʊnzɪən/
adjective
琼斯式的；渴望得到（尤指物质上的）的；模仿琼斯的
Relating to or characteristic of keeping up with the Joneses; striving to own or achieve as much as one's neighbors or peers, especially in material possessions.
She had a jonesian desire for a bigger house. 她有一种琼斯式的渴望，想要一栋更大的房子。
synonyms: acquisitive, covetous, envious, materialistic, status-seeking
antonyms:  content, satisfied, unambitious, modest, humble
词性: 形容词
