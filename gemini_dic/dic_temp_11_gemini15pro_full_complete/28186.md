capra:/ˈkæprə/| n. | 山羊；(尤指)母山羊 |A female goat, especially a domesticated one. |例句：The capra grazed peacefully in the meadow.  山羊在草地上平静地吃草。

例句：The farmer milked his capra every morning.  农夫每天早上都挤他那只母山羊的奶。|近义词：goat, nanny goat |反义词: billy goat (公山羊) | 词性:noun
