ginkgo:/ˈɡɪŋkoʊ/ (US), /ˈɡɪŋkɡəʊ/ (UK)| n.|银杏（树）|A large Chinese tree with fan-shaped leaves and yellow fruit.  Its extract is sometimes used in herbal medicine.|例句：The ginkgo tree in my yard turns a beautiful golden color in the fall. 我院子里的银杏树在秋天变成了美丽的金色。

例句：Ginkgo biloba extract is believed to improve memory.  银杏叶提取物被认为可以提高记忆力。

近义词：maidenhair tree|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
