gauss:/ɡaʊs/ | n. | 高斯; 磁感应强度单位 | A unit of magnetic induction, equal to one maxwell per square centimeter.  | The Earth's magnetic field strength is typically measured in gauss.  地球磁场强度通常以高斯为单位测量。 | synonyms: maxwell per square centimeter | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the German physicist Carl Friedrich Gauss (1777-1855).
