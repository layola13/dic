multidiscipline:/ˌmʌltiˈdɪsəplɪn/ | adj. n.|多学科的，多专业的；多学科，跨学科|Involving several disciplines or branches of learning.|This research project is multidiscipline, drawing on expertise in biology, chemistry, and physics. 这个研究项目是多学科的，利用了生物学、化学和物理学方面的专业知识。|interdisciplinary, cross-disciplinary|single-discipline|adjective, noun
