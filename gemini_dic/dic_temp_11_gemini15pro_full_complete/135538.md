nonpliantnesses:/nɒnˈplaɪəntnəsɪz/| n. |不顺从，顽固，不服从|The state or quality of being nonpliant; inflexibility, unyieldingness, or stubbornness.|His nonpliantnesses made negotiation difficult. 他的不顺从使得谈判变得困难。|近义词：inflexibility, rigidity, stubbornness, obstinacy, recalcitrance|反义词：flexibility, pliability, compliance, obedience, docility|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + pliant (flexible) + -ness (suffix forming nouns of state or quality).  "Pliant" comes from Old French "pliant" (bending), present participle of "plier" (to bend), from Latin "plicare" (to fold).
