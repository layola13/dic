cornucopia:/ˌkɔːrnjəˈkoʊpiə/ /ˌkɔːnjuˈkəʊpiə/| n. |丰饶之角，大量，丰富|A horn overflowing with fruit, flowers, and grain, symbolizing abundance; an abundant supply of good things of a specified kind.|The Thanksgiving table held a cornucopia of delicious foods. 感恩节的餐桌上摆满了丰盛的美食。|近义词：abundance, profusion, plethora, bounty|反义词：scarcity, dearth, lack, paucity|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Latin *cornu copiae* "horn of plenty", from *cornu* "horn" + *copiae* (genitive) "plenty".
