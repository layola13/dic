impregnability:/ɪmˌpreɡnəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可征服性；攻不破；难以渗透
The state or quality of being impregnable; incapable of being taken by assault; unconquerable.
The impregnability of the fortress made it a safe haven.  这个堡垒的坚不可摧使它成为一个安全的避风港。
invincibility, invulnerability, impenetrability
vulnerability, weakness, penetrability
noun
