fusilier:/ˌfjuːzɪˈlɪər/ | n. | 火枪手；燧发枪兵 | A soldier armed with a fusil (early type of firearm). | One fusilier was wounded in the skirmish. 一名火枪手在小规模冲突中受伤。 | synonyms: infantryman, rifleman, musketeer | antonyms: civilian | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *fusilier*, from *fusil* "musket," from Old French *focile* "steel for striking fire," ultimately from Latin *focus* "hearth, fireplace."
