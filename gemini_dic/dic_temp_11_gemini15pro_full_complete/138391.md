nototherium:/ˌnoʊtəˈθɪəriəm/
noun
南蹄兽
An extinct genus of hoofed mammals belonging to the order Notoungulata, native to South America.
Nototherium fossils have been found in Argentina.  在阿根廷发现了南蹄兽化石。
synonyms:Toxodon, Homalodotherium
antonyms:N/A
