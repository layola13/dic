by-product:/ˈbaɪˌprɑːdʌkt/ | n. | 副产品； побочный продукт | something produced in the making of something else; a secondary result |  The company's main product is leather, but glue is a valuable by-product. 该公司的主要产品是皮革，但胶水是一种有价值的副产品。

The by-product of this chemical process is highly toxic. 这种化学过程的副产品毒性很强。

| 近义词：spin-off, side effect, consequence, result | 反义词：main product, primary product | 词性: noun
