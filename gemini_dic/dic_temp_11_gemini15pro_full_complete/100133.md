interprofessional:/ˌɪntərprəˈfɛʃənəl/| adj.|跨专业的，跨职业的|Involving or relating to members of more than one profession.|例句：Interprofessional collaboration is crucial for providing comprehensive patient care. 跨专业合作对于提供全面的病人护理至关重要。

例句：The conference promoted interprofessional dialogue between doctors, nurses, and social workers.  这次会议促进了医生、护士和社会工作者之间的跨专业对话。|近义词：multidisciplinary, collaborative, joint, shared|反义词：unidisciplinary, single-profession|词性:形容词
