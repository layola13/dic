kale-seed:/ˈkeɪlˌsiːd/
noun
羽衣甘蓝种子
The seed of the kale plant, used for growing new kale plants.
Scatter the kale seed thinly across the prepared soil.  把羽衣甘蓝种子薄薄地撒在准备好的土壤上。
Sow the kale seed in early spring for a summer harvest.  在早春播种羽衣甘蓝种子，以便在夏季收获。
Synonyms: kale seed, borecole seed
Antonyms: N/A
