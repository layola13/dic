kishi: /ˈkiːʃiː/ | n. | Kishi | A title, often translated as "knight," used in several East Asian cultures, particularly in Japan. It originally referred to a high-ranking warrior. |  The legendary figure Minamoto no Yoshitsune was a revered kishi. 源义经是一位受人尊敬的武士。 | synonym: warrior, knight, samurai | antonym: peasant, commoner | noun
