chambered:/ˈtʃeɪmbərd/| adj.|有腔室的，分隔的|Divided into chambers or compartments; having a chamber or chambers. |The pistol was a .45, chambered for .45 ACP.  这把手枪是.45口径的，可装.45 ACP子弹。|The nautilus shell is beautifully chambered. 鹦鹉螺的壳有着美丽的腔室。|compartmentalized, partitioned|open, undivided|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From chamber + -ed.  "Chamber" comes from Old French "chambre" from Latin "camera" meaning "vault, arched roof."
