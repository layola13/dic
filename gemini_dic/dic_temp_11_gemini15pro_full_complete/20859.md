blatherer:/ˈblæðərər/ (US), /ˈblæðərə(r)/ (UK)| n. |空谈者；喋喋不休的人|A person who talks foolishly and at length about unimportant matters.|He's such a blatherer; he talks for hours but never says anything of substance. 他真是个空谈家，说了好几个小时，却从来没说过什么有实质内容的话。|Synonyms: chatterbox, windbag, gasbag|Antonyms: reticent person, quiet person|noun

blather:/ˈblæðər/ (US), /ˈblæðə(r)/ (UK)| v. n. |喋喋不休；胡说八道|To talk foolishly and at length about unimportant matters.|He blathered on about the weather for ten minutes. 他絮絮叨叨地谈论了十分钟的天气。|Synonyms: chatter, prate, babble|Antonyms: be silent, be quiet|verb, noun
