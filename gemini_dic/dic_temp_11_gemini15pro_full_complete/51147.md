despondent:/dɪˈspɒndənt/ /dɪˈspɑːndənt/| adj.|沮丧的，消沉的，失望的|Feeling or showing profound hopelessness and discouragement.|例句：She felt despondent about the future. 她对未来感到很失望。|近义词：gloomy, glum, downcast|反义词：joyful, elated, buoyant|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin despondent- (stem of despondēns), present participle of despondēre ‘lose heart, give up’, from de- ‘down, away’ + spondere ‘to promise, engage oneself’.
