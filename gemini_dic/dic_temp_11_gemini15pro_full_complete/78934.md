girardian:/ʒɪˈrɑːrdiən/
adjective|吉拉尔式的，与勒内·吉拉尔相关的|Relating to or characteristic of René Girard, a French historian, literary critic, and philosopher of social science, particularly his theories of mimetic desire and scapegoating.|His work offers a Girardian interpretation of Shakespeare's tragedies.|近义词:mimetic, imitative|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective
Etymology: Derived from the surname of René Girard (1923–2015).
