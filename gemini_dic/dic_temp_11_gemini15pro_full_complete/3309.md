afflicts:/əˈflɪkts/| v. |使痛苦，折磨，困扰|To cause pain or suffering to; to trouble or distress.|例句：The disease afflicts millions of people worldwide. 这种疾病困扰着全世界数百万人。|近义词：troubles, bothers, distresses, plagues|反义词：comforts, soothes, relieves|词性:动词
afflict:/əˈflɪkt/| v. |使痛苦，折磨，困扰|From Latin *affligere, from ad- "to" + fligere "to strike".  Meaning "to dash to the ground, overthrow" in classical Latin, it evolved to "weaken" and then "distress mentally".|例句：He was afflicted with a rare skin disease. 他患有一种罕见的皮肤病。|近义词：trouble, bother, distress, plague|反义词：comfort, soothe, relieve|词性:动词
