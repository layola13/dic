nonheinous:/nɒnˈheɪnəs/
adjective
非十恶不赦的；非罪大恶极的
Not heinous; not grossly wicked or reprehensible.
This was a nonheinous crime, more a prank than anything truly harmful. 这并非十恶不赦的罪行，与其说是真正的伤害，不如说更像个恶作剧。
The judge determined that the offense was nonheinous and warranted a lighter sentence.  法官认定该罪行并非罪大恶极，因此判处较轻的刑罚。
Synonyms: minor, venial, slight, trifling, petty
Antonyms: heinous, atrocious, monstrous, egregious, abhorrent
词性: 形容词
