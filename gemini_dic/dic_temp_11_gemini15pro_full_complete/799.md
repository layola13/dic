absorptional:/əbˈzɔːrpʃənəl/
adjective
吸收性的
Relating to or characterized by absorption.  The process of absorbing or of being absorbed.
The absorptional properties of the sponge were impressive.  海绵的吸收性令人印象深刻。
The scientist studied the absorptional rate of the new material.  科学家研究了这种新材料的吸收率。
近义词: absorbent, absorptive
反义词: nonabsorbent, impermeable
词性: adjective

词源: From absorption + -al.  Absorption comes from Late Latin absorptionem, from absorbere "to swallow," from ab- "away" + sorbere "to suck up."
