artfully:/ˈɑːrtfəli/ | adv. | 巧妙地；狡猾地；精巧地 |  In a clever and skillful way, often in a way that is intended to deceive. From "art" (skill) + "-ful" (full of) + "-ly" (adverb suffix).  | She artfully dodged the question. 她巧妙地回避了这个问题。 | skillfully, cleverly, cunningly, craftily | clumsily, awkwardly, ineptly | adverb
