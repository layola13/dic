词源:
From Old English *crācettan, frequentative of crācian (“to croak”), of imitative origin. Cognate with Middle Dutch crāken (“to creak, crack, croak”), German krachen (“to crack, crash”).
来自古英语 *crācettan，是 crācian（“呱呱叫”）的重复形式，是拟声词。与中古荷兰语 crāken（“嘎吱作响、破裂、呱呱叫”）、德语 krachen（“破裂、碰撞”）同源。
