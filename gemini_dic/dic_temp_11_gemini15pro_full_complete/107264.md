lakes:/leɪks/
n.
湖泊；湖水
A large body of water surrounded by land.
例句：The Lake District is famous for its beautiful lakes.  湖区以其美丽的湖泊而闻名。

例句：We went boating on the lakes. 我们去湖里划船了。

近义词：ponds, reservoirs
反义词: rivers, seas
词性:名词
