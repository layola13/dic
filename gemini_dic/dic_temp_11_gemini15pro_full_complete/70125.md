fez's:/ˈfɛzɪz/
noun
土耳其毡帽（fez）的复数形式
Plural form of "fez," referring to more than one of these brimless, cylindrical hats, typically red with a black tassel.
The fez's were stacked neatly on the shelf.  这些土耳其毡帽整齐地堆放在架子上。
Near synonyms:  hats, caps
Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific type of hat)
词性: 名词


Etymology:
From Turkish *fes*, ultimately from Arabic *fas* "Morocco," because the hats were originally made there.
