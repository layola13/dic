fortifier:/ˈfɔːrtɪfaɪər/| n.|强化剂；设防者|Something that fortifies or strengthens. |The vitamin is added to the milk as a fortifier.  这种维生素作为强化剂添加到牛奶中。|Synonyms: strengthener, reinforcer,  |Antonyms: weakener, debilitator|noun


fortify:/ˈfɔːrtɪfaɪ/| vt.|加强；增强；设防；(尤指用酒精)强化(饮料)|To make something stronger, especially in order to protect it. |They fortified the city against attack. 他们加强了城市的防御以抵御攻击。 The wine is fortified with brandy. 这种葡萄酒用白兰地强化过。|Synonyms: strengthen, reinforce, bolster |Antonyms: weaken, undermine|verb



ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *fortifier, from Late Latin fortificare “to make strong,” from Latin fortis “strong” + facere “to make.”
