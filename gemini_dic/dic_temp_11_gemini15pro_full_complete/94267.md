imperseverant:/ˌɪmpɜːrsɪˈvɪərənt/| adj.|不坚持的，不锲而不舍的|Not persevering; lacking persistence or tenacity.  Characterized by a lack of determination or the inability to continue working toward a goal, especially when faced with difficulties or obstacles.|He was imperseverant in his studies and frequently changed majors. 他学习不坚持，经常换专业。

She was so imperseverant with her diet that she gave up after only a week.  她的节食计划一点也不坚持，才一个星期就放弃了。|近义词：inconstant, fickle, erratic, unstable, unsteady, wavering, vacillating|反义词：persevering, persistent, tenacious, resolute, steadfast, determined|词性:adjective
