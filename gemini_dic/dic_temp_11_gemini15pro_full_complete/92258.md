hypervascularities:/ˌhaɪpərˌvæskjʊˈlærɪtiz/
n.
血管增生; 多血管性
The presence of an excessive number of blood vessels in a tissue or organ.  This can be a normal physiological response, as seen in wound healing, or it can be associated with pathological conditions like tumors.
Hypervascularities were observed in the tumor, suggesting a rapid growth rate.  在肿瘤中观察到血管增生，表明其生长速度很快。
hypervascularity, neovascularization, angiogenesis
hypovascularity, avascularity
noun
