doffer:/ˈdɒfər/ (UK) /ˈdɑːfər/ (US)| n.|落纱工；(纺织机上)落纱装置|A worker who removes full bobbins, spools, or pirns from spinning frames and replaces them with empty ones, or the mechanism itself that performs this function. |The doffer quickly replaced the full bobbins with empty ones. 落纱工迅速地把满筒子换成了空筒子。

The doffer on the spinning machine malfunctioned, causing a delay in production. 纺纱机上的落纱装置发生故障，导致生产延误。|近义词：remover, taker-off|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑winder - 卷绕工，因为是相反的操作)|词性: noun
