divisively:/dɪˈvaɪsɪvli/| adv.|分裂地，引起分歧地|In a way that causes disagreement or division.|The issue of immigration has been debated divisively for years.  移民问题多年来一直在引起分歧地争论着。|The policy was divisively implemented, causing friction within the community. 这项政策的实施引起了分歧，导致了社区内部的摩擦。|近义词: controversially, contentiously, disruptively|反义词: unifyingly, harmoniously|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From divide (v.) + -ive (suffix forming adjectives) + -ly (suffix forming adverbs).  Ultimately from Latin dividere "to force apart, distribute, separate."
