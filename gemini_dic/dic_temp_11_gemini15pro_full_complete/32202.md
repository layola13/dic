chicalote:/tʃiːkəˈloʊteɪ/| n.|罂粟;罂粟籽;罂粟植物(尤指墨西哥罂粟);|A plant, *Argemone mexicana*, of the poppy family, with prickly leaves and large yellow flowers, common as a weed in the southwestern US and Mexico. Its seeds are used as a laxative and as a source of oil.|The chicalote grew wild along the roadside.|Synonyms: Mexican poppy, prickly poppy|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
