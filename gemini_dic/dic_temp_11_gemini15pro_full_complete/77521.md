gazunder:/ɡəˈzʌndə(r)/
n.
压低房价；临时降价
The act of reducing a previously agreed offer price for a property just before the exchange of contracts.
The seller was forced to accept a gazunder of £5,000. 卖家被迫接受了5000英镑的降价。
近义词: price reduction, down valuation, last-minute discount
反义词: gazump
词性: 名词
