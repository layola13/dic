intravarietal:/ˌɪntrəvəˈraɪɪtəl/
adjective
品种内的
occurring within a single variety or cultivar.
Intravarietal cross-pollination can lead to genetic variation. 品种内的异花授粉会导致遗传变异。
近义词: within-variety
反义词: intervarietal
词性: adjective
