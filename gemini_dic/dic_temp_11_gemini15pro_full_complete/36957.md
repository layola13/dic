colourize:/ˈkʌləraɪz/ (UK) /ˈkʌləraɪz/ (US)| v. |给...着色，彩色化|To add colour to a black and white film or photograph, usually artificially.|The old black and white movie was colourized for television. 这部黑白老电影为了电视播放而被彩色化了.|近义词: colorize, tint, color, dye|反义词: bleach, decolorize, desaturate|词性: 动词
Etymology: From "colour" + "-ize" (suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or become").  "Colour" ultimately comes from the Latin "color".
