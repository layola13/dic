cowiest:/ˈkaʊɪɪst/ | adj. | 最像母牛的; 最胆怯的; 最懦弱的 |  Resembling a cow the most, especially in a timid or cowardly way. This is a superlative form of "cowy," meaning resembling or suggestive of a cow. It implies an exaggerated sense of timidity, docility, or awkwardness. |  He gave the cowiest moo I've ever heard. 他发出了我听过的最像母牛的哞哞声。| 近义词: timidest, meekest, mousy, fainthearted | 反义词: bravest, boldest, courageous | 词性: 形容词

词源: From "cow" + "-y" (adjective-forming suffix) + "-est" (superlative suffix).
