ostriches:/ˈɒstrɪtʃɪz/ (UK), /ˈɑːstrɪtʃɪz/ (US)| n.|鸵鸟|Large, flightless birds native to Africa, known for their long necks and legs.  They are the largest living bird species.|例句：Ostriches can run very fast. 鸵鸟跑得很快。|近义词：ratite (泛指走禽)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps small birds like hummingbirds for contrast)|Noun
