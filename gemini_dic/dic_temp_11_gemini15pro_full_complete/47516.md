dearworth:/dɪərwɜːrθ/ | adj. | 珍贵的；可爱的；高贵的 | Precious; beloved; of high worth or value. |  This ring is my most dearworth possession. 这枚戒指是我最珍贵的财产。 |  She is a dearworth friend to me. 她是我珍贵的朋友。 | 近义词: precious, valuable, cherished, beloved | 反义词: worthless, cheap, common | 词性:形容词

词源: "Dearworth" is a combination of "dear" and "worth." "Dear" comes from Old English "dēore," meaning "precious, valuable, costly, beloved." "Worth" comes from Old English "weorð," meaning "value, price, respect."  Therefore, "dearworth" emphasizes something's value and the affection held for it.
