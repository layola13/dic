infraocular:/ˌɪnfrəˈɒkjʊlə(r)/ (US) /ˌɪnfrəˈɑːkjələr/ (UK)| adj.|眼下的，位于眼下的|Situated beneath the eye.|The surgeon made an infraocular incision.  外科医生做了眼下切口。|The doctor administered an infraocular injection. 医生进行了眼内注射。|近义词:subocular|反义词:supraocular|词性:adjective

Etymology:
infra- (Latin, preposition) meaning "below, beneath" + ocular (Latin, adjective, from oculus "eye") meaning "of or relating to the eye".
