frustration:/frʌˈstreɪʃən/
n.|挫败，沮丧，失意，令人沮丧的事物|The feeling of being upset or annoyed, especially because of inability to change or achieve something.|His constant frustration at work led him to quit. 他在工作中不断受挫，导致他辞职。|近义词：disappointment, annoyance, exasperation|反义词：satisfaction, contentment
词性:名词
