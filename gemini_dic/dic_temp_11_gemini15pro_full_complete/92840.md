icosandrous:/ˌaɪkəˈsændrəs/
| adj. |二十雄蕊的；具有二十枚或更多雄蕊的，着生于花萼上的|Having twenty or more stamens attached to the calyx.|The flower is icosandrous, meaning it has numerous stamens attached to its calyx.|近义词:polyandrous|反义词:oligandrous|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Greek *eikosi* "twenty" + *aner*, *andros* "man" (in botany, "stamen").
