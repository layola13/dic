disclamation:/dɪsˈkleɪmər/
n.|免责声明；否认；放弃|A statement that denies something, especially responsibility.|The advertisement included a disclaimer that the product might cause drowsiness.|近义词:denial, waiver, rejection|反义词:claim, acceptance, affirmation|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation) + claim (to assert ownership of; to demand as a right).  Ultimately from Latin dis- (not) + clamare (to cry out, shout).
