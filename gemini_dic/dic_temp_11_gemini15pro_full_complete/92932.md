identified: /aɪˈdɛntɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 已识别的，被确认的 |  Recognized or established as being a particular person or thing.  Having the identity known or established. | The police identified the suspect from security camera footage. 警方通过监控录像确认了嫌疑人的身份。

The identified patient was immediately quarantined. 被确诊的病人立刻被隔离了。

| 近义词: recognized, distinguished, pinpointed, determined, ascertained | 反义词: unknown, unidentified, anonymous | 词性: adjective

词源:  Late Middle English: past participle of identify, from late Latin identificare, from ident- ‘same’ + Latin facere ‘make’.
