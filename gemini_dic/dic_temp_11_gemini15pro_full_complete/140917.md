oncologists: /ɑːŋˈkɑːlədʒɪsts/ /ɒŋˈkɒlədʒɪsts/
noun
肿瘤学家；肿瘤科医生
A medical doctor who specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of cancer.
The oncologists discussed the patient's treatment plan. 肿瘤科医生们讨论了病人的治疗方案。

The oncologist recommended chemotherapy. 肿瘤学家建议化疗。
Synonyms: cancer specialist, cancer doctor
Antonyms: None (There isn't a direct antonym for a medical specialization.)
词性: noun
