anecdotal: /ˌænɪkˈdəʊtl/ (US) /ˌænɪkˈdɒtl/ (UK)| adj.|轶事的；趣闻的；传闻的|Based on personal accounts rather than facts or research; informal.  Not necessarily true or reliable, because based on personal accounts rather than facts or research.|例句：Anecdotal evidence suggests that the medicine is effective.  传闻证据表明这种药是有效的。

例句：The book is full of anecdotal stories about famous scientists. 这本书里充满了关于著名科学家的轶事。|近义词：informal, casual, unscientific, hearsay|反义词：scientific, empirical, factual, statistical|词性：adjective
