flue:/fluː/
noun
烟道，管道
a duct for smoke and waste gases produced by a fire, a gas heater, etc., leading to the outdoors.
The chimney flue needs cleaning.  烟囱的烟道需要清理。

近义词: vent, chimney, duct
反义词: None


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *flue (unattested), from Vulgar Latin *flucta (“flowing”), feminine past participle of Latin fluere (“to flow”).
