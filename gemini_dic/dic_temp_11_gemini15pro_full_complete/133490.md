nonfermentability:/ˌnɒnfɜːrmɛntəˈbɪləti/ |n.| 不可发酵性；非发酵性 |The nonfermentability of certain sugars can be a key identifying characteristic for bacteria.  某些糖的不可发酵性可以作为细菌鉴定的关键特征。|例句：The nonfermentability of the substance was confirmed by the lab results.  实验室结果证实了该物质的不可发酵性。

例句：The bacteria's nonfermentability makes it difficult to culture in standard media. 这种细菌的不可发酵性使其难以在标准培养基中培养。


|近义词：non-fermentation, inability to ferment|反义词:fermentability|词性:noun
