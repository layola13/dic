dropa:/ˈdrɒpə/ (UK) /ˈdrɑːpə/ (US)| n.|投递员；投弹手；滴管；滴注器|A person who drops something, especially bombs from an aircraft, or a device used to administer liquid by drops. |The bomber dropa released their payload over the target.  轰炸机投弹手向目标投放了有效载荷。| The chemist used a dropa to add the reagent to the solution. 化学家用滴管将试剂添加到溶液中。|Synonyms: dropper, dispenser, bomber (for bomb dropper)|Antonyms: collector, gatherer|noun
