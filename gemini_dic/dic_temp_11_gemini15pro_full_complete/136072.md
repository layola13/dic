nonrecipient:/ˌnɒn rɪˈsɪpiənt/ /ˌnɑːn rɪˈsɪpiənt/
noun|非接受者；非受领人|a person or organization that does not receive something|例句：The nonrecipients of the award were understandably disappointed.  未获得奖项的人可以理解地感到失望。|近义词：non-beneficiary, excluded party|反义词：recipient, beneficiary|词性:n.


nonrecipient:/ˌnɒn rɪˈsɪpiənt/ /ˌnɑːn rɪˈsɪpiənt/
adjective|非接受的；非受领的|not having received something|例句：The nonrecipient group served as a control in the study. 非接受组在研究中充当对照组。|近义词：unexcluded, non-receiving|反义词：recipient, receiving|词性:adj.
