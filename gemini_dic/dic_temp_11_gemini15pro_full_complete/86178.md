heartwood:/ˈhɑːrtˌwʊd/| n. |心材|The dense inner part of a tree trunk, yielding the hardest timber.|The carpenter prefers heartwood for making furniture because it is more durable. 木匠更喜欢用心材制作家具，因为它更耐用。|Synonyms: duramen|Antonyms: sapwood|noun
