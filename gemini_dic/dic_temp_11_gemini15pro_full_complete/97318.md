informatics:/ˌɪnfərˈmætɪks/ | n. |信息学，信息科学|The study and application of information technology to various fields, including the acquisition, storage, retrieval, processing, and transmission of information.  It often involves the use of computers and computational methods to manage and analyze large datasets.| Example: Bioinformatics is a branch of informatics that deals with biological data. 生物信息学是处理生物数据的信息学的一个分支。| Example: He's studying health informatics to improve healthcare systems. 他正在学习健康信息学以改进医疗保健系统。|Synonym: information science, information technology, computing, computer science|Antonym: N/A|Noun
