half-jokingly:/ˌhɑːf ˈdʒoʊkɪŋli/
adverb
半开玩笑地
in a way that is partly serious and partly not serious
"I half-jokingly suggested we should just give up."  “我半开玩笑地建议我们干脆放弃。”
Synonyms: playfully, teasingly, lightly
Antonyms: seriously, earnestly
词性: 副词
