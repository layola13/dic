christianlike:/ˈkrɪstʃənlaɪk/
adjective
像基督徒的；基督徒似的
Resembling or characteristic of a Christian, especially in behavior; charitable, forgiving.
She displayed a christianlike compassion for the homeless. 她对无家可归者表现出基督徒般的同情。
近义词:charitable, compassionate, forgiving, pious, saintly
反义词:uncharitable, unforgiving, cruel, merciless
词性:adjective
