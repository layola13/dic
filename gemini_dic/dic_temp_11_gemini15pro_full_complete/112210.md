loopie:/ˈluːpi/| n.|疯子；古怪的人；精神错乱者|A person who is considered crazy, eccentric, or mentally unstable.|He's a bit of a loopie, always talking to himself. 他有点疯疯癫癫的，总是自言自语。|近义词：crazy person, lunatic, eccentric, oddball|反义词:sane person, normal person|词性:名词

词源:  Likely a slang term derived from "loop," referring to something going around in circles, perhaps alluding to the perceived circular or illogical thinking of someone considered crazy.  There is no definitive etymology.
