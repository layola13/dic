frivolism: /ˈfrɪvələzəm/ | n. | 轻浮；无聊；琐碎 | Lack of seriousness; behavior or activities that are silly or wasteful. | She dismissed his suggestion as pure frivolism. 她认为他的建议纯属无聊。| synonyms: levity, flippancy, triviality, superficiality | antonyms: seriousness, gravity, solemnity, importance | noun

frivolous: /ˈfrɪvələs/ | adj. | 轻浮的；无聊的；琐碎的 | Not having any serious purpose or value. |  He made a frivolous remark and we all laughed. 他说了一句轻佻的话，我们都笑了。| synonyms: silly, foolish, trifling, superficial | antonyms: serious, important, significant, profound | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *frivole* (14c.), from Latin *frivolus* "silly, trifling, worthless," of unknown origin.
