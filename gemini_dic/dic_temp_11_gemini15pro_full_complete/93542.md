imaginate:/ɪˈmædʒɪneɪt/ | v. | 想象，设想 | To form a mental image or concept of.  It is often used informally, especially in some dialects, and is considered non-standard English.  Standard English uses "imagine". | I imaginate a world without war. 我想象一个没有战争的世界。|  I can imaginate him winning the lottery. 我能想象他中了彩票的样子。 | 近义词: imagine, envision, conceive, visualize | 反义词: N/A | 词性: Verb

词源: From late Middle English *imaginaten, from Latin imaginari (“to form an image, imagine, picture to oneself”), from imago (“image, likeness”).  The "-ate" ending is likely influenced by other verbs ending in "-ate".
