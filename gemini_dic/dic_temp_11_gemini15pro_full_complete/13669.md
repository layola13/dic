attraction:/əˈtrækʃən/| n.|吸引力，吸引人的事物；景点|Something that attracts or is intended to attract people.|例句：The Eiffel Tower is a popular tourist attraction. 埃菲尔铁塔是一个很受欢迎的旅游景点。|例句：The attraction between the two magnets is strong. 两块磁铁之间的吸引力很强。|近义词：appeal, charm, allure, magnetism|反义词：repulsion, aversion|词性：名词

词源:
From Late Latin *attractionem (nominative attractio), noun of action from past-participle stem of attrahere "to draw toward," from ad- "to" + trahere "to draw, pull".
