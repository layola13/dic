batfowling:/ˈbætˌfaʊlɪŋ/
noun
蝙蝠捕猎;一种夜间捕鸟的活动
The act of catching birds at night, typically using nets and lights.
例句：Batfowling was a popular pastime in the 18th century.  18世纪，蝙蝠捕猎是一种流行的消遣活动。

例句：They went batfowling in the woods after dark. 天黑后，他们到树林里去捕鸟。

近义词：bird-catching, fowling
反义词:bird-releasing
