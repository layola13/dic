downwind:/ˌdaʊnˈwɪnd/| adj. adv.|顺风的，在下风的；顺风地，在下风地|In a direction downwind; with the wind. |The yacht drifted downwind.  游艇顺风漂流。|近义词：leeward|反义词: upwind, windward|词性:形容词, 副词


downwind:/ˌdaʊnˈwɪnd/| n.|顺风处，下风处|The direction from which the wind is blowing. |We were standing downwind of the factory and the fumes were awful. 我们站在工厂的下风处，那股烟很糟糕。|近义词：leeward|反义词: upwind, windward|词性:名词


词源: From "down" (meaning in a descending direction) + "wind" (referring to the movement of air).  由“down”（意为向下方向）和“wind”（指空气的运动）组成。
