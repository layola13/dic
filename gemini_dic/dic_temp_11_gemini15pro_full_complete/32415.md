chilognath: /ˈkaɪləɡnæθ/| n. |千足虫，倍足纲动物|An arthropod of the class Chilopoda, having a segmented body with one pair of legs per segment.  It is a carnivorous invertebrate with a flattened body and a pair of venomous claws just behind the head. |例句：The chilognath scurried across the forest floor. 千足虫飞快地穿过森林地面。

例句：She found a chilognath under a rock in her garden. 她在花园的岩石下发现了一只千足虫。|近义词：centipede, myriapod|反义词: None (No direct opposite for an animal classification)|词性: Noun
