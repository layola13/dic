load:/loʊd/ (US) /ləʊd/ (UK)| n. v.|负载，装载；负担；大量；一批|A large quantity of something carried.  The process of putting a large quantity of something onto or into something else.|The truck carried a heavy load. 卡车载着沉重的货物。|He loaded the truck with boxes. 他把箱子装上卡车。|近义词:burden, cargo, freight, weight|反义词:unload, empty|noun, verb
