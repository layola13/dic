double-faulting:/ˈdʌbəl ˈfɔːltɪŋ/ | n. | 双误; 两次发球失误 |  In tennis, a double fault occurs when a player fails to legally land their serve in the service box on two consecutive attempts. |  He lost the match due to his persistent double-faulting.  他因为持续的双误输掉了比赛。 | Synonym: serving fault (though not perfectly synonymous as it can refer to a single fault), error | Antonym: ace, successful serve | noun

double-fault:/ˈdʌbəl ˈfɔːlt/ | v. | 双误; 两次发球失误 | To commit two serving faults in a row in tennis. | He double-faulted on match point. 他在赛点双误了。 | Synonym: serve two faults | Antonym: serve an ace | verb
