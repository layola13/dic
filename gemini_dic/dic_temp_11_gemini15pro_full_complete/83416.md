habib:/hæˈbiːb/ | n. | 朋友，亲爱的，宝贝（男性） | A term of endearment, primarily used in Arabic-speaking cultures, meaning "friend," "beloved," or "dear" (masculine). | Example: He greeted his old friend, "Habib, it's so good to see you!" 他向他的老朋友打招呼：“哈比卜，很高兴见到你！” | Example: She called her son, "Habib, come here please." 她叫她的儿子：“亲爱的，请过来。” | Synonym: friend, beloved, dear, darling | Antonym: enemy, foe | Noun
