dhow:/daʊ/| n.|单桅帆船，阿拉伯帆船|A lateen-rigged sailing vessel used in the Indian Ocean.|The dhow sailed gracefully across the turquoise water. 单桅帆船优雅地驶过碧绿的海水。|Near synonyms: sailboat, sailing vessel, felucca|Antonyms: steamship, motorboat|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Swahili *dau*, from Arabic *dāw*, ultimately from Sanskrit *dāru* (wood).
