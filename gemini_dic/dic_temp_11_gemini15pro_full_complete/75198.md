frock-coat:/ˈfrɒk kəʊt/| n.|双排扣长礼服，男士常礼服|A man's long coat, double-breasted and usually worn for formal occasions.|He wore a frock-coat to the wedding. 他穿着一件双排扣长礼服参加婚礼。|Synonyms:dress coat, morning coat|Antonyms:casual wear|noun

frock-coat:/ˈfrɑːk koʊt/| n.|双排扣长礼服，男士常礼服|A man's long coat, double-breasted and usually worn for formal occasions.|He donned a frock-coat for the opera. 他穿着一件双排扣长礼服去听歌剧。|Synonyms:tailcoat, formal coat|Antonyms:informal wear, casual wear|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From frock (a monk's habit) + coat.  Originally referred to a loose, outer garment.  Over time, it evolved to specifically denote the style of formal coat described above.
