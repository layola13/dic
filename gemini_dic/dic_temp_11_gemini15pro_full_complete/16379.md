barbicanage:/bɑːrˈbɪkənɪdʒ/
n.
城楼,  防御工事,  堡垒
A system of fortifications or strong defenses, especially those of a city.  The term is often associated with the Barbican Centre in London, though not directly related etymologically to its specific architecture.
The city's barbicanage proved impenetrable to the invaders. 这座城市的防御工事使入侵者无法攻破。
Synonyms: fortification, bulwark, rampart, bastion
Antonyms: weakness, vulnerability

词性: 名词
