flexicostate:/ˌflɛksiˈkɒsteɪt/
adjective
灵活成本的; 可变成本的
Relating to or characterized by flexible costs, especially in relation to labor.  Costs that can be adjusted relatively quickly, often in response to changes in demand or output.
The company adopted a flexicostate model, allowing them to quickly adjust labor costs during economic downturns.  这家公司采用了灵活成本模式，使其能够在经济低迷时期快速调整劳动力成本。
Synonyms: variable cost, adjustable cost, adaptable cost
Antonyms: fixed cost, overhead cost
词性: adjective
