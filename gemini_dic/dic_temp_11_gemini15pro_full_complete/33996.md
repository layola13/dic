cited:/ˈsaɪtɪd/| v. |引用，引证，提及|The author cited several sources in his research paper. 作者在他的研究论文中引用了几个来源。|例句：The lawyer cited a previous case to support his argument. 律师引用了以前的案例来支持他的论点。|例句：She cited several reasons for leaving her job. 她列举了几个离开工作的理由。|近义词：quoted, mentioned, referred to|反义词：omitted, ignored|词性：动词

词源:
From Latin *citātus, past participle of citāre (“to summon, urge, rouse, call, name, quote”), frequentative of ciēre (“to set in motion, stir, rouse, call”).
