bight:/baɪt/
n.
弯曲,海湾,绳圈
A curve or bend in a shoreline, river, or other geographical feature, forming a bay; a loop of rope.
The ship anchored in the bight of the bay. 这艘船停泊在海湾的弯曲处。
The sailor made a bight in the rope. 水手在绳子上打了一个圈。
curve, bend, bay, loop, coil
straightness
