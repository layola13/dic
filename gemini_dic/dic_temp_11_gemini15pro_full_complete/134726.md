nonmeasurability:/ˌnɒnˈmeʒərəˈbɪləti/ /ˌnɑːnˈmeʒərəˈbɪləti/| n. |不可测性，不可衡量性|The quality or state of being impossible to measure. |The nonmeasurability of human emotions makes studying them difficult. 人类情感的不可测性使得研究它们变得困难。|近义词：immeasurability, unquantifiability|反义词: measurability, quantifiability|词性:名词

nonmeasurable:/ˌnɒnˈmeʒərəbl/ /ˌnɑːnˈmeʒərəbl/| adj. |不可测量的，无法衡量的|Not capable of being measured. |Some aspects of quality are nonmeasurable. 某些质量方面是不可测量的。|近义词：immeasurable, unquantifiable|反义词: measurable, quantifiable|词性:形容词
