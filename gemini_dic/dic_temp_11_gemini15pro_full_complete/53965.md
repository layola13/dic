discreet:/dɪˈskriːt/| adj.|谨慎的，小心的，不引人注意的|Careful and prudent in one's speech or actions, especially in order to keep something confidential or to avoid embarrassment.|She was always very discreet about her personal life. 她对自己的私生活总是非常谨慎。|近义词:prudent, circumspect, tactful, careful, cautious|反义词:indiscreet, careless, imprudent, tactless|adjective
