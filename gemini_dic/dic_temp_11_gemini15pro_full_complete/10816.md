aquiform:/ˈækwɪfɔːrm/
adjective
水状的，水形的
having the form or nature of water; watery.
The substance exhibited an aquiform appearance, shimmering and flowing like a liquid. 这种物质呈现出水状外观，像液体一样闪闪发光，流动自如。
近义词:watery, aqueous, liquid
反义词:solid, dry
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *aqua* (water) + *-formis* (having the form of).
