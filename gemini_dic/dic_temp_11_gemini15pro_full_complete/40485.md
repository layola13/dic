contributors:/kənˈtrɪbjətərz/ | n. | 贡献者；投稿人 | People or organizations who contribute something, especially money, goods, or time, to a cause or project.  | The contributors to the charity event were very generous.  慈善活动的捐助者非常慷慨。 | donors, supporters, participants | opponents, detractors | noun

Etymology:  From the verb "contribute,"  from Latin "contributus," past participle of "contribuere"  ("to bring together, contribute"), from "com-" (together) + "tribuere" (to grant, bestow).
