centripetalism:/ˌsɛnˈtrɪpɪtəlɪzəm/ | n. | 向心力；向心主义 | The tendency to unify a country or group by increasing the power of the central government.  The policy of a state promoting the interests of unity. |  The government's policies encouraged centripetalism, aiming to strengthen national identity. 政府的政策鼓励向心主义，旨在加强民族认同。

The new educational curriculum emphasizes centripetalism by focusing on shared history and values. 新的教育课程通过强调共同的历史和价值观来强调向心主义。 | 近义词：centralization, unification, integration | 反义词： centrifugalism, separatism, fragmentation | 词性：noun

centripetal:/ˌsɛnˈtrɪpɪtl/ | adj. | 向心的 | Moving or tending to move towards a centre. | The centripetal force keeps the planets in orbit around the sun. 向心力使行星绕太阳运行。

The design of the new park incorporates centripetal pathways leading to a central fountain. 新公园的设计包含通往中央喷泉的向心路径。 | 近义词：inward, converging | 反义词：centrifugal, diverging | 词性：adjective
