manans: /ˈmænænz/ | n. | 甘露糖 | Manans are a type of polysaccharide found in some plants, particularly in the tubers of certain species. They are used as thickeners and stabilizers in food products. | Konjac glucomannan is a popular supplement for weight loss. 魔芋葡甘露聚糖是一种流行的减肥补充剂。 | The manans in the plant help it retain water. 这种植物中的甘露糖有助于其保水。 | synonyms: polysaccharide, hemicellulose | antonyms: monosaccharide | noun
