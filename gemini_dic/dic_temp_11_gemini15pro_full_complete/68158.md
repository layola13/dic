fails:/feɪlz/| v.|失败，不及格，未能做到，失灵，辜负，衰退|A third attempt to break the record failed.  第三次打破纪录的尝试失败了。|近义词：loses, neglects, disappoints, declines, weakens|反义词:succeeds, passes, thrives|verb (third-person singular simple present fails, present participle failing, simple past failed, past participle failed)

Etymology: From Middle English *failen, *failȝen, from Old French faillir (“to miss, make a mistake, be lacking, default, fail”), from Vulgar Latin *fallire, from Latin fallere (“to deceive, trick, disappoint, escape the notice of”).
