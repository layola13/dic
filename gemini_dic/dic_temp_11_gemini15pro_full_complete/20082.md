bios:/ˈbaɪɒs/ (UK), /ˈbaɪoʊs/ (US)| n. | 基本输入/输出系统 | Basic Input/Output System.  It's firmware used to perform hardware initialization during the booting process, and to provide runtime services for operating systems and programs. | The BIOS settings allow you to change the boot order.  BIOS设置允许您更改启动顺序。

The computer's BIOS was corrupted, preventing it from starting.  计算机的BIOS已损坏，导致无法启动。|近义词:firmware|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
