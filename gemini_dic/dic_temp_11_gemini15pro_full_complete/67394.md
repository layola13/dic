exterminator:/ɪkˈstɜːrmɪneɪtər/ /ɪkˈstɝːmɪneɪtər/| n. |灭虫者，除害者|A person whose job is to kill unwanted insects or animals, especially rodents.|例句：We called an exterminator to get rid of the rats in the attic. 我们打电话叫灭虫员来清除阁楼里的老鼠。

例句：The exterminator sprayed the house for termites. 灭虫员喷洒房屋以防白蚁。

近义词：pest controller, fumigator|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑protector 保护者) |词性:noun


exterminate:/ɪkˈstɜːrmɪneɪt/ /ɪkˈstɝːmɪneɪt/| vt. |消灭，根除|To kill all of a particular group of people or animals.|例句：The settlers exterminated the native population.  殖民者灭绝了当地居民。

例句：They're trying to exterminate the rats in the building. 他们在设法消灭大楼里的老鼠。

近义词：eradicate, wipe out, destroy|反义词: cultivate, preserve |词性:verb
