intractable:/ɪnˈtræktəbl/ | adj. |难处理的，棘手的，倔强的 | Not easily managed, controlled, or solved; stubborn. |  例句：The intractable problem of poverty continues to plague the city. 城市贫困这一棘手问题持续困扰着这座城市。

例句：He has an intractable personality and refuses to compromise. 他性格倔强，拒绝妥协。

近义词：stubborn, obstinate, unmanageable, unruly, difficult, recalcitrant | 反义词：tractable, manageable, docile, compliant, amenable | 词性:形容词
