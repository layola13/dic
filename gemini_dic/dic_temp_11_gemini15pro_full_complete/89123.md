home-in-on:/hoʊm ˈɪn ɑːn/ /həʊm ˈɪn ɒn/ | phrasal verb | 瞄准；对准；集中注意力于；越来越接近（目标）| To direct attention or focus onto something specific, often gradually and precisely.  It can also mean to move closer to a target. | The missile homed in on the target. 导弹瞄准了目标。

|近义词：focus on, zero in on, concentrate on, target, pinpoint |反义词: ignore, disregard, neglect |词性: phrasal verb
