nummulite: /ˈnʌmjʊˌlaɪt/ | n. |货币石，钱币石 | A lenticular fossil shell of a large, extinct marine protozoan of the genus Nummulites, common in the Eocene.  These fossils resemble coins. |  Nummulites are common in Eocene rocks.  货币石在始新世岩石中很常见。 |  Synonyms: foraminifer, fossil | Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a specific fossil type) | Noun
