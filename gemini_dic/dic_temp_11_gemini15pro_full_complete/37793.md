compellation:/ˌkɒmpəˈleɪʃn/ | n. |称呼，称谓；强迫，强制|The formal compellation for a judge is "Your Honor". 对法官的正式称呼是“法官大人”。|A style of address, especially a formal one; the act of compelling or the state of being compelled.|例句：He addressed her by the compellation "Madam". 他用“夫人”这个称呼来称呼她。

例句：The king issued a compellation for all able-bodied men to join the army. 国王发布命令，强迫所有身体健全的男子参军。|近义词：address, title, appellation, coercion, constraint|反义词:request, persuasion|词性:名词
