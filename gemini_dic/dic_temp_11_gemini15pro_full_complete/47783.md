decanters:/dɪˈkæntərz/
n.
醒酒器；倾析器
A decanter is a vessel, usually an ornamental glass bottle, into which wine or other alcoholic beverages are decanted.
The waiter brought a decanter of red wine to the table. 服务员拿来一瓶红葡萄酒到餐桌上。

近义词:carafe, bottle
反义词:(无明显的反义词)
词性:名词(plural)
