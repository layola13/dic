nowhere:/ˈnəʊwɛə(r)/ (UK) /ˈnoʊwɛr/ (US)|n.|无名之地; 死胡同; 困境|a place where nothing of interest happens or exists; a situation in which no progress can be made|例句：My career is going nowhere. 我的事业毫无进展。|近义词：dead end, backwater|反义词:prominence, success|词性:名词

Etymology: From Old English *nāhwār, from nā "no" + hwār "where".
