fresher:/ˈfreʃər/ (UK) /ˈfrɛʃər/ (US)| n.|新生；新手；刚毕业的学生|A student in their first year at university or college.  A person who is new to a particular job or activity.|例句：The university welcomed the new freshers with an orientation week. 大学用一个迎新周来欢迎新生。

例句：He's a fresher in the company and still learning the ropes. 他是公司的新人，还在学习业务。|近义词：newcomer, novice, beginner, rookie|反义词：veteran, expert, senior|词性:noun
