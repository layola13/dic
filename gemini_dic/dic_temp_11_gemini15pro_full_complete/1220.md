accommodatory:/əˈkɒmədeɪtəri/
adjective
 accommodating; obliging
willing to fit in with someone's wishes or needs.
Example: The hotel staff were very accommodatory, providing us with everything we needed.  酒店工作人员非常乐于助人，为我们提供了一切所需。

Example:  She has an accommodatory nature and is always willing to help. 她性格随和，总是乐于助人。
Synonym: obliging, accommodating, cooperative, helpful, compliant
Antonym: unaccommodating, unhelpful, inflexible, rigid
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin accommodatorius, from accommodare  "to adapt," from ad- "to" + commodare "to make fit," from commodus "suitable, convenient."
