outface:/ˌaʊtˈfeɪs/| vt.| 面对；战胜；比…脸皮厚；在气势上压倒|To stare down or intimidate someone by maintaining a steady gaze.|He outfaced his accusers and denied all the charges. 他直视着原告，否认了所有指控。|Synonyms: stare down, intimidate, defy, browbeat|Antonyms: cower, submit, yield|verb
outface:/ˌaʊtˈfeɪs/| vt.| 对抗；正视|To oppose someone boldly and confidently.|She outfaced her critics with unwavering determination. 她以坚定的决心对抗批评她的人。|Synonyms: confront, oppose, challenge, resist|Antonyms: avoid, evade, surrender, concede|verb
