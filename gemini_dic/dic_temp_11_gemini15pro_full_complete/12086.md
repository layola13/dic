artiodactyla:/ˌɑːrtiəˈdæktɪlə/ | n. | 偶蹄目 | An order of hoofed mammals in which the weight of the body is borne equally by the third and fourth toes; even-toed ungulates. |  Cows, sheep, and deer are artiodactyla. 牛、羊和鹿都是偶蹄动物。| synonyms: even-toed ungulates | antonyms: perissodactyla (odd-toed ungulates) | noun
