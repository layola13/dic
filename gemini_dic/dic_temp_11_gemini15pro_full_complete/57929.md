drink-driving:/ˈdrɪŋk ˈdraɪvɪŋ/ | adj.(less common, usually attributive)|酒后驾驶的 | Relating to the act of driving after consuming alcohol. | The drink-driving incident resulted in serious injuries.  这起酒后驾驶事件导致了严重的伤害。 | intoxicated driving|sober driving|adjective
