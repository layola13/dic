forelay:/fɔːrˈleɪ/
verb
预先安排；预先布置；抢先占领
to arrange or do something before it is needed or expected, especially in order to prevent something
The explorers forelaid a store of provisions in case of emergency. 探险家们预先储备了粮食，以防万一。
foresee, anticipate, prearrange
hinder, delay
v.
