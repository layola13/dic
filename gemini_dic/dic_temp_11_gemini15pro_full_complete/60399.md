eiderdown:/ˈaɪ.dər.daʊn/
noun
羽绒被
A quilt filled with the soft, fine feathers of eider ducks.
She snuggled under the warm eiderdown. 她依偎在温暖的羽绒被下。
Synonyms: duvet, comforter, quilt
Antonyms: sheet, blanket (though these are not exact opposites, they provide contrast in terms of warmth and thickness)
n.
