mistral:/ˈmɪstrəl, miˈstrɑːl/| n.|密史脱拉风；西北风|A strong, cold, dry, northwesterly wind that blows from the French Alps towards the Mediterranean coast, especially in winter.|The mistral was whipping the waves into a frenzy. 密史脱拉风把海浪吹得狂怒。|近义词：northwesterly, northerly|反义词: southerly|noun
