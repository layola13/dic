blingest:/ˈblɪŋɪst/ | adj. | 最闪亮的，最炫的 | Superlative form of "blingy," meaning covered in or displaying expensive jewelry or other flashy ornamentation. |  She wore the blingest dress at the party, covered in sequins and diamonds. 她穿着派对上最闪亮的裙子，上面缀满了亮片和钻石。

| Example: He drove up in the blingest car I've ever seen. 他开着我见过的最炫的汽车来了。 | Synonym: flashiest, gaudiest, most ostentatious | Antonym: plainest, simplest, most understated | adjective
