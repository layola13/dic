antimechanism:/ˌæntiˈmɛkənɪzəm/ | n. | 反机制，对抗机制 | A process, structure, or behavior that counteracts or opposes a mechanism or system.  This can refer to biological processes, social structures, or psychological strategies. | The body's antimechanisms for fighting infection are incredibly complex.  人体对抗感染的反机制非常复杂。

The government introduced antimechanisms to prevent monopolies. 政府引入了反垄断机制。 | synonyms: countermeasure, counter-strategy, defense mechanism | antonyms: mechanism, process, system | noun
