dislocation's:/ˌdɪsləˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. |脱臼，错位，混乱|The possessive form of "dislocation," referring to something belonging to or associated with a dislocation.  It signifies the state of being out of place or the act of putting something out of its usual place. It can also refer to a disruption or disturbance.|The doctor diagnosed the runner's knee pain as a dislocation's effect on the surrounding ligaments. 医生诊断跑步者的膝盖疼痛是脱臼对周围韧带的影响。|Synonym: displacement, disturbance, disruption|Antonym: order, arrangement, alignment|noun
