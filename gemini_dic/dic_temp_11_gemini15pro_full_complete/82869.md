gullied:/ˈɡʌlid/| adj.|被冲成沟壑的；沟壑遍布的|eroded by gullies; having many gullies |The heavy rains left the hillside gullied and scarred.  暴雨使山坡沟壑纵横，伤痕累累。|The landscape was barren and gullied. 这片土地贫瘠，沟壑纵横。|近义词：furrowed, channeled, grooved|反义词:smooth, flat|词性:形容词
词源: From gully + -ed.  来自名词gully（沟壑）加上形容词后缀-ed。
