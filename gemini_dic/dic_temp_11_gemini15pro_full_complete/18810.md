berserks:/bərˈsɜːrks/ | n. | 狂暴战士；疯子 |  A wild, frenzied warrior or someone behaving in a similar way. | The berserks attacked the village with savage fury. 狂暴战士以野蛮的狂怒袭击了村庄。 | The berserks fought with a reckless abandon. 狂暴战士们不顾一切地战斗。| 近义词: maniacs, fanatics, wildmen | 反义词: cowards, pacifists | 词性: 名词 (plural of berserk)

berserker:/bərˈsɜːrkər/ | n. | 狂暴战士；疯子 |  A wild, frenzied warrior or someone behaving in a similar way. |  The berserker charged into battle without fear.  狂暴战士无所畏惧地冲入战斗。| He went completely berserker when he found his car had been stolen. 当他发现他的车被偷了时，他完全疯了。| 近义词: maniac, fanatic, wildman | 反义词: coward, pacifist | 词性: 名词

berserk:/bərˈsɜːrk/ | adj. | 狂暴的；疯狂的 |  Frenzied, wild, out of control. | He went berserk when he heard the news. 他听到这个消息后变得狂暴。 | The crowd went berserk when their team scored the winning goal. 当他们的球队攻入制胜球时，人群变得疯狂。| 近义词: frenzied, frantic, raging | 反义词: calm, peaceful, controlled | 词性: 形容词


Etymology: From Old Norse *berserkr, meaning "bear-shirt" or "bear-skin," referring to warriors who wore animal skins and fought with intense ferocity.
