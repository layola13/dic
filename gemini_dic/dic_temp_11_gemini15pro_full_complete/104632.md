karakoram:/ˌkærəˈkɔːræm/ | n. | 喀喇昆仑山脉 |A large mountain range spanning the borders of Pakistan, India, and China. It is the second highest mountain range in the world, after the Himalayas. |  We're planning a trekking expedition to the Karakoram next summer. 我们计划明年夏天去喀喇昆仑山脉徒步旅行。| The Karakoram is home to K2, the second highest peak in the world. 喀喇昆仑山脉是世界第二高峰K2的所在地。 | synonyms: mountain range, massif | antonyms: valley, plain | noun
