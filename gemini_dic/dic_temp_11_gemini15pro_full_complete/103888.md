judahite:/ˈdʒuːdəˌhaɪt/| n. adj.|犹大人，犹太人的|A person from the ancient kingdom of Judah.  A descendant of Judah, one of Jacob's sons. Also used as an adjective to describe things relating to Judah.|例句：The Judahite king ruled wisely.  这位犹大国王统治英明。|例句：The Judahite army was strong.  犹大军队很强大。|近义词：Jew, Israelite|反义词:Gentile|词性:noun, adjective
