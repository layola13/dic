intermazes:/ˌɪntərˈmeɪzɪz/
verb
使…成为迷宫; 在…中穿插迷宫

The author intermazes the plot with intricate subplots and red herrings.  作者用复杂的支线剧情和障眼法使情节错综复杂。
The garden was intermazed with tall hedges.  花园里高高的树篱构成了迷宫。
Synonyms:  entangle, complicate, confuse
Antonyms: simplify, clarify, untangle
词性: 动词
