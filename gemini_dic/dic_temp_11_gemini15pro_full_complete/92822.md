iconography:/ˌaɪkəˈnɒɡrəfi/ (US) /ˌaɪkəˈnɑːɡrəfi/ (UK)
n.
图像学;肖像画法;肖像;图解
The study or interpretation of the symbolic meaning of images (icons) and their attributes, and the study of such images in works of art.
The iconography of the painting suggests a biblical scene.  这幅画的图像学表明它描绘的是一个圣经场景。
近义词: symbolism, imagery
反义词:  None (No direct antonym for a field of study)
词性: Noun


iconography:/ˌaɪkəˈnɒɡrəfi/ (US) /ˌaɪkəˈnɑːɡrəfi/ (UK)
n.
肖像;肖像画法;图像学;图解
A set of conventional images or symbols associated with a subject or theme.
The artist employed traditional Christian iconography in his frescoes.  这位艺术家在他的壁画中使用了传统的基督教肖像画法。
近义词: symbolism, imagery
反义词:  None (No direct antonym for a field of study)
词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *iconographie*, from Greek *eikon* ("image") + *-graphia* ("writing, description").
