brutehood:/ˈbruːthʊd/| n. |野蛮状态，兽性，残暴|The state or quality of being a brute; brutality. |His descent into brutehood was swift and shocking. 他堕落成野蛮人的速度之快令人震惊。| The dictator ruled with unrestrained brutehood.  独裁者实行着无节制的暴政。| 近义词：brutality, savagery, barbarity |反义词: civility, humanity, kindness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From brute + -hood.  "Brute" comes from the Latin word "brutus," meaning "dull, senseless, irrational."  "-hood" is an Old English suffix denoting a state, condition, or quality.
