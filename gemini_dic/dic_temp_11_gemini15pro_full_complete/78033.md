genu:/ˈdʒiːnuː/
n.
knee
The part of the leg between the thigh and the lower leg, where the femur and tibia meet.
He injured his genu playing football.  他踢足球时伤了膝盖。
synonyms: knee, kneecap, patella
antonyms: N/A (Body parts generally don't have direct antonyms)
noun
