francomania:/ˌfræŋkoʊˈmeɪniə/ | n. |对法国的狂热；对法国文化的喜爱|An excessive enthusiasm for French customs, manners, or culture.|  During the 18th century, francomania swept through the Russian aristocracy, leading to the adoption of French language and fashion. 18世纪，崇法狂潮席卷了俄罗斯贵族，导致了法语和时尚的采用。|Synonyms: Gallomania, francophilia | Antonyms: francophobia | noun
