guano:/ˈɡwɑːnoʊ/| n. |鸟粪，海鸟粪|A natural substance consisting of the excrement of seabirds, often used as fertilizer. |Bats produce guano, which is an excellent fertilizer.  蝙蝠产生鸟粪，这是一种极好的肥料。|
The farmers used guano to enrich the soil. 农民用鸟粪来肥沃土壤。|Synonyms: fertilizer, manure|Antonyms: N/A|noun
