carpal:/ˈkɑːrpl/ (US), /ˈkɑːp(ə)l/ (UK)| adj. n.|腕骨的；腕骨|Relating to the bones of the wrist (carpus).  A bone of the carpus. |Example (adj.): Carpal tunnel syndrome is a common problem. 腕管综合征是一个常见的问题。

Example (n.):  The eight carpal bones form the wrist joint. 八块腕骨构成了腕关节。|Synonym (adj.): wrist |Antonym (adj.):  (No direct antonym for anatomical location) | word type: adjective, noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *carpālis, from Latin carpus ‘wrist’, from Greek karpós.
