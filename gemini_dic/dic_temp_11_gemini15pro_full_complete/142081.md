orichalceous:/ˌɔːrɪˈkælsiəs/
adjective
黄铜色的，山铜制的
Made of or resembling orichalc, a gold-colored metal mentioned in ancient writings.
The orichalceous glow of the sunset illuminated the city. 黄铜色的夕阳照亮了城市。
The statue had an orichalceous sheen, like polished brass. 这座雕像有一种黄铜色的光泽，就像抛光的黄铜一样。
近义词:brassy, golden, bronze, copper
反义词:silver, steel, iron
词性:adjective

词源:From Latin *orichalceus, from Ancient Greek ὀρείχαλκος (oreíkhalkos, “mountain copper”), from ὄρος (óros, “mountain”) + χαλκός (khalkós, “copper”).
