capitalistic:/ˌkæpɪtəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
资本主义的
Relating to or based on the principles of capitalism.
Example 1: The United States has a capitalistic economy. 美国实行资本主义经济。
Example 2:  He criticized the capitalistic system's focus on profit. 他批评了资本主义制度对利润的关注。

synonym:  free-market, private-enterprise
antonym:  socialist, communist
词性: adjective
