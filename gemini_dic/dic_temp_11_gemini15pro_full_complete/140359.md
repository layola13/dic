oiks:/ɔɪks/
interj.|哎呀；哎哟|An exclamation expressing surprise, dismay, or disgust.|Oiks! I spilled my coffee. 哎呀！我洒了咖啡。|近义词:oops, yikes|反义词:None|词性:感叹词
Etymology:  Likely a variant of "oy vey," a Yiddish exclamation with similar meaning, ultimately from German "weh."
