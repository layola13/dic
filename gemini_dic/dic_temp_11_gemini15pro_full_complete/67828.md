eying:/ˈaɪɪŋ/| v. | 注视，打量，觊觎 |  Looking at something or someone with interest, often with the intention of obtaining or achieving it. | She was eyeing the last piece of cake. 她正盯着最后一块蛋糕。| He's been eyeing that promotion for months. 他觊觎那个晋升机会已经好几个月了。 | 近义词: watching, observing, considering, coveting | 反义词: ignoring, overlooking | 词性: 动词

词源:  Present participle of the verb "eye," which comes from Old English *ēage, from Proto-Germanic *augō, from Proto-Indo-European *ōkʷ-.
