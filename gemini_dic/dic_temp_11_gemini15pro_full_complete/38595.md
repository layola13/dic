concorporate:/kənˈkɔːrpəreɪt/
verb
合并，结合
to unite or combine into one body or mass.
The company will concorporate several smaller firms into its operations.  这家公司将把几家小公司合并到其业务中。
The new law concorporates several previous statutes. 新法律合并了几项以前的法律。
近义词:amalgamate, merge, combine, consolidate, unite
反义词:separate, divide, disintegrate
词性:v.

concorporate:/kənˈkɔːrpəreɪt/
adjective
