cerussite:/ˈsɛrəsaɪt/ /ˈsɪərəˌsaɪt/
n.|白铅矿|A mineral consisting of lead carbonate (PbCO3), usually colourless or white, and occurring in orthorhombic crystalline masses or granular aggregates.|Cerussite is an important ore of lead. 白铅矿是重要的铅矿石。|Synonyms: lead carbonate, white lead ore|Antonyms: None|Noun

cerussite:/ˈsɛrəsaɪt/ /ˈsɪərəˌsaɪt/
n.|白铅矿|A naturally occurring mineral composed of lead carbonate (PbCO3), often found in oxidized zones of lead deposits.  It typically appears as colorless or white crystals and serves as a significant ore of lead.|The miners discovered a rich vein of cerussite. 矿工们发现了一条富含白铅矿的矿脉。|Synonyms: lead carbonate, white lead ore|Antonyms: None|Noun
