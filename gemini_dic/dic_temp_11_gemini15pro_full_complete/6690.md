analemma: /ˌænəˈlɛmə/ | n. | 日行迹 | A curve representing the Sun's apparent position in the sky at the same mean solar time, as viewed from a fixed location on Earth, throughout the course of a year.  It typically has a figure-eight shape. | The photographer captured a stunning image of the analemma over the course of a year.  这位摄影师在一年中拍摄了一张令人惊叹的日行迹图像。|  The analemma's shape is caused by the tilt of Earth's axis and the elliptical shape of its orbit. 日行迹的形状是由地轴的倾斜和地球轨道的椭圆形状造成的。|  synonym: solar path, sun path | antonym: N/A | noun
