laureating:/ˈlɔːrieɪtɪŋ/
n.|授予桂冠；授予荣誉；表彰|The laureating of the Nobel Prize winners is a grand ceremony.  诺贝尔奖获得者的颁奖典礼是一场盛大的仪式。|
v.|授予桂冠；授予荣誉；表彰|The university laureated him for his contributions to science.  大学授予他荣誉，以表彰他对科学的贡献。|
Etymology: From Latin *laureatus*, past participle of *laureare* "to crown with laurel," from *laurea* "laurel wreath."
近义词: honoring, crowning, recognizing, awarding
反义词: condemning, criticizing, denouncing
词性: Noun, Verb (present participle)
