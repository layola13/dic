insanable:/ɪnˈseɪnəbəl/ | adj. | 不可治愈的，无法满足的 | incapable of being satisfied; unquenchable | Her insatiable curiosity led her to explore every corner of the library. 她永不满足的好奇心驱使她探索图书馆的每一个角落。 |  His insatiable appetite for power ultimately led to his downfall. 他对权力永不满足的渴望最终导致了他的垮台。 | 近义词: unquenchable, ravenous, voracious | 反义词: satiable, satisfiable | 词性: 形容词

insanable (French) < insatiable (Latin) < in- (not) + satiabilis (capable of being satisfied) < satiare (to satisfy)
