out-and-away:/ˌaʊt ən əˈweɪ/
adverb
远远地；无疑地；绝对地
By far; without question; decisively.
She is out-and-away the best player on the team. 她无疑是队里最好的球员。
Synonym: by far, definitely, absolutely, easily
Antonym: slightly, marginally, barely, hardly
词性: 副词
