oligistic:/ˌɑlɪɡɪˈstɪk/ | adj. | 寡头的；少数人控制的 | Relating to or characterized by oligarchy; controlled by a small group of people. | The oligistic structure of the diamond industry keeps prices artificially high. 钻石行业的寡头结构使得价格被人为地抬高。 |  The company operates in an oligistic market, dominated by a few large players.  该公司在一个寡头垄断的市场中运营，由少数几个大公司主导。 | 近义词: monopolistic, cartelized | 反义词: competitive, democratic | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Greek *oligistikos*, from *oligos* (few) + *-istikos* (suffix forming adjectives).
