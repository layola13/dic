lesseeship:/ˌlesiːʃɪp/| n. |承租人身份，租赁权|The state of being a lessee; the period or term for which one holds a property by lease.|After years of lesseeship, they finally decided to buy the property. 多年的租赁之后，他们最终决定购买这处房产。|Synonyms: tenancy, leasehold|Antonyms: ownership, freehold|noun
