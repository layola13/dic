overillustration:/ˌoʊvərɪləˈstreɪʃən/
n.
过度图解; 过度阐释
The act of using excessive or redundant illustrations, often to the point of detracting from the message.
The presentation suffered from overillustration, making it difficult to focus on the key takeaways.  这个演示文稿过度图解，让人难以集中注意要点。
Overillustration can sometimes confuse the reader. 过度图解有时会让读者感到困惑。
synonyms: overexplanation, redundancy, excessive detail
antonyms:  understatement, minimalism, brevity
noun
