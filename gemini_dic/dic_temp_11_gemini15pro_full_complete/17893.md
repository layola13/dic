beenin: /ˈbɪnɪn/ | phrasal verb | 在…待过；到过…；去过… |  Been in is a colloquial contraction of "been in," which itself is a contraction of "have been in."  It suggests that someone has visited or stayed in a particular place. | I've beenin that restaurant before.  我以前去过那家餐厅。| been to, visited | haven't been to/in, never been to/in | verb phrase

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Rarely used in simple present.  Simple present implies a habitual action, and "been in" refers to a completed action.

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Not applicable. "Been" is a form of "be" that is used in perfect tenses, not continuous tenses.

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have been in this town for two weeks. 我在这个镇上待了两周了。
She has been in several movies. 她出演过几部电影。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
Not typically used in this tense, as the focus is on duration, whereas "been in" implies a completed visit.

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I was in Paris last year. (More common than using "been in" in the past tense) 我去年在巴黎。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was staying in a hotel when the fire alarm went off. (More common and natural than using "been in" in past continuous) 火灾警报响起时我正住在一家酒店里。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had been in London before I moved to Paris. 在我搬到巴黎之前，我曾在伦敦待过。
She had been in that situation many times before. 她以前多次遇到过那种情况。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
Not typically used in this tense.

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will be in London next week.  我下周会在伦敦。 (More common than "will have been in")

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be staying in a hotel next weekend. 我下周末将住在一家酒店里。(More common and natural than using "been in" in future continuous)

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next year, I will have been in this country for five years. 到明年，我将在这个国家待五年了。
She will have been in many plays by the time she graduates. 到她毕业的时候，她将已经出演过很多戏剧了。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
Not typically used in this tense.

虚拟语气:
If I had been in your shoes, I would have done the same thing. 如果我处在你的位置，我也会做同样的事情。

被动语态:
Not applicable. "Been in" cannot be used in the passive voice.

疑问句:
Have you been in this city long? 你在这个城市待了很久了吗？

否定句:
I haven't been in that restaurant before. 我以前没去过那家餐厅。


PHRASE->
Not typically used in set phrases.  "Been in" functions more as a standalone verb phrase.



注意事项:

"Been in" is colloquial and informal.  In formal writing, "been to" or "visited" are generally preferred.
