aharon:/ˈeɪərən/| n.|亚伦|A Hebrew name, meaning "high mountain," "exalted," or "enlightened." It is most famously associated with Aaron, the brother of Moses and the first high priest of the Israelites in the Bible.|Aaron led the Israelites in worship and served as a bridge between God and the people. | 近义词: (As a name, no direct synonyms, but similar biblical figures might include Moses, Miriam)| 反义词: None| noun
