forestalled:/fɔːrˈstɔːld/ | v. | 预先阻止，先发制人 | to prevent something from happening by acting first |  The company forestalled its competitors by releasing a new product earlier than expected.  这家公司通过比预期更早地发布新产品来先发制人，阻止了竞争对手。| prevent, anticipate, obviate | allow, permit | verb


forestalled:/fɔːrˈstɔːld/ | adj. | 被预先阻止的 | prevented from happening by advance action | Their plans were forestalled by the sudden change in weather. 他们的计划被突如其来的天气变化阻止了。 | thwarted, prevented, obstructed |  aided, facilitated | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *forstallen, from Old English forsteallan (“to intercept, anticipate, hinder, prevent”), from for- (“before, against”) + stellan (“to put, place, stand”).
