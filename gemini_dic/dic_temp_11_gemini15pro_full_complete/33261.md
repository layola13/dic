chrysaloid: /ˈkrɪsəlɔɪd/ | adj. n. | 蛹状的；处于过渡期的；蛹壳 |  Relating to or resembling a chrysalis; being in a transitional phase of development. | The company is in a chrysaloid state, about to emerge as a major player in the market.  这家公司正处于蛹状阶段，即将成为市场上的主要参与者。 |  The butterfly emerged from its chrysaloid shell. 蝴蝶从它的蛹壳中出来。 | 近义词: transitional, metamorphic, nascent, emergent | 反义词: mature, developed, static | 词性: adjective, noun

chrysaloid: /ˈkrɪsəlɔɪd/ | n. | 蛹壳 | The protective covering of a chrysalis. | The shed chrysaloid lay empty on the leaf.  蜕下的蛹壳空荡荡地躺在叶子上。| 近义词:  chrysalis, pupa, cocoon | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "adult" or "imago" in the context of insect development) | 词性: noun
