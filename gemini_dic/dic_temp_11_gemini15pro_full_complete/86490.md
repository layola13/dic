hein:/haɪn/ /heɪn/ | int. | 嗯？; 什么？; 对吧？| An interrogative utterance used to express a question, request for repetition, or seek confirmation. | Example: "Hein? I didn't hear you."  嗯？我没听到你说什么。 | Example: "It's a beautiful day, hein?" 天气真好，对吧？ | 近义词: eh, huh, pardon, what | 反义词:  (Interjections don't typically have antonyms) | interjection

Not commonly used in set phrases in English.  This interjection is more common in French and other languages.


注意事项:
"Hein" is not a common English word. It's more frequently used in Canadian French and some other languages. In English, using "eh?", "huh?", "pardon?", or "what?" would be more common.
