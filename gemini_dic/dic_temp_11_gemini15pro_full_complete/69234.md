feasibility:/ˌfiːzəˈbɪləti/| n.|可行性，可能性|The feasibility of the project is still under discussion.  该项目的可行性仍在讨论中。|例句：We are studying the feasibility of building a new factory. 我们正在研究建立新工厂的可行性。|近义词：practicability, viability, possibility|反义词：infeasibility, impossibility|词性:名词
词源: Late Middle English: from Old French faisable, from faire ‘to do’, from Latin facere (same as fact1). The English formation was influenced by feasible.
