optimality:/ˌɒptɪˈmælɪti/ /ˌɑːptɪˈmælɪti/| n.| 最优性，最佳性|The state or quality of being optimal; the best possible result or solution.|The algorithm searches for optimality in terms of speed and memory usage.  该算法致力于在速度和内存使用方面达到最佳状态。|
optimality:/ˌɒptɪˈmælɪti/ /ˌɑːptɪˈmælɪti/| n.| 最优性，最佳性|The best possible outcome given certain constraints.|We haven't reached optimality yet, but we're close. 我们还没有达到最佳状态，但我们快了。|近义词: ideal, perfection, peak performance|反义词: suboptimality, inferiority, deficiency|词性:noun
