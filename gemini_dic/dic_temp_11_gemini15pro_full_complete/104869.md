keeners:/ˈkiːnərz/| n.|用功的学生；热心者|A person, typically a student, who is extremely eager, enthusiastic, and hardworking, often to the point of being perceived as overly zealous or nerdy.  They are often seen as trying too hard to impress teachers or superiors. |例句：He was such a keener, always the first to raise his hand in class. 他真是个用功的学生，总是在课堂上第一个举手。

例句：The keeners were already studying for the exam weeks in advance.  这些热心者已经在几周前就开始准备考试了。|近义词：go-getter, eager beaver, high achiever, enthusiast|反义词：slacker, loafer, underachiever|词性: Noun (plural)
