outflashing:/ˌaʊtˈflæʃɪŋ/
verb
胜过，闪耀过
surpassing in flashing; emitting a more brilliant flash or light.
The fireworks were outflashing each other in the night sky. 焰火在夜空中交相辉映，胜过彼此。
The new lighthouse was outflashing the old one. 新灯塔的光芒胜过旧灯塔。
outshining, outshining, excelling
dulling, dimming
v.
