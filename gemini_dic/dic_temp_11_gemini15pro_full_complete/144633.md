outwitting:/ˌaʊtˈwɪtɪŋ/
|v.|以智取胜，智胜|defeating someone by being more clever or cunning than they are|He outwitted his opponents by a clever strategy. 他以巧妙的策略智胜了对手。|近义词：outsmarting, outmaneuvering, deceiving|反义词：being outwitted, being outsmarted|词性：动词现在分词

词源：
From outwit + -ing.  "Out-" prefix indicating surpassing or exceeding, combined with "wit" meaning intelligence or cleverness.
