cuneate:/ˈkjuːnieɪt/ /ˈkjuːnɪ.eɪt/| adj.|楔形的；楔状的|Shaped like a wedge; triangular with the narrow end at the base.|The cuneate leaves of the plant were a distinctive feature. 这种植物的楔形叶子是一个显著特征。|Synonyms: wedge-shaped, triangular, pointed|Antonyms: rounded, obtuse|adjective

cuneate:/ˈkjuːnieɪt/ /ˈkjuːnɪ.eɪt/| n.|楔骨|A wedge-shaped bone, specifically the medial cuneiform bone of the foot.|The doctor pointed to the cuneate on the x-ray of the foot. 医生指着脚部X光片上的楔骨。|Synonyms: medial cuneiform, (loosely) wedge-shaped bone|Antonyms: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cuneatus*, from *cuneus* "wedge".
