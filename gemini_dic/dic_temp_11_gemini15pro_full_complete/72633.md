flyness:/ˈflaɪnɪs/
adjective
时尚; 酷炫;  (尤指黑人文化中) 真实和酷的品质
The quality of being fly, especially in a way that reflects Black culture; a combination of stylishness, coolness, authenticity, and charisma.
Her outfit showcased effortless flyness. 她的穿搭展现了毫不费力的酷炫。
近义词:coolness, stylishness, swag, charisma
反义词:uncoolness, lameness, dullness
词性:adjective

flyness:/ˈflaɪnɪs/
noun
时尚; 酷炫;  (尤指黑人文化中) 真实和酷的名词形式
The state or quality of being fly, particularly within Black culture, encompassing style, coolness, authenticity, and a unique personal flair.
He exuded an aura of flyness that captivated everyone in the room. 他散发出一种迷人的酷炫气息，吸引了房间里所有人的目光。
近义词:cool, style, swag, charisma
反义词:uncoolness, lameness, dullness
词性:noun


词源:
Derived from the adjective "fly," meaning "stylish" or "cool," with the suffix "-ness" added to form a noun denoting the quality or state of being fly.  The term gained prominence in African American Vernacular English (AAVE).
