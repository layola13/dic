mitzvah:/ˈmɪtsvə/ (US), /ˈmɪtsva/ (UK)| n.|诫命，善行|A precept or commandment of the Jewish law; a meritorious deed performed in fulfillment of a divine commandment.  A good deed. |例句：

Performing acts of charity is considered a mitzvah.
行善被认为是一种诫命。

It's a mitzvah to visit the sick.
探望病人是一种善行。

|近义词：good deed, precept, commandment|反义词：sin, transgression|词性:noun
