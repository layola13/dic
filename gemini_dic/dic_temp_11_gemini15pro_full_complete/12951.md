asteriscus: /ˌæstəˈrɪskəs/| n. |星号，星形符|A small star-like symbol (*) used in printing or writing to indicate a footnote, omission, etc.|Example 1: The asteriscus next to the word indicated a further explanation in the footnote. 星号在单词旁边表示脚注中有进一步的解释。

Example 2: He used an asteriscus to mark the sections he wanted to edit later. 他用星号标记了他想稍后编辑的部分。|Synonym: star, asterisk|Antonym: N/A|Noun
