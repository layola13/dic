agbada:/ɑːɡˈbɑːdə/
n.
a flowing wide-sleeved robe worn by men in much of West Africa.
阿格巴达，一种西非男子穿着的宽袖长袍。
The elder wore a brightly colored agbada to the ceremony.  长老穿着鲜艳的阿格巴达参加了仪式。
The agbada flowed behind him as he walked.  他走动时，阿格巴达在他身后飘动。
近义词: robe, gown
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "western suit" as a contrasting garment)
词性: noun
