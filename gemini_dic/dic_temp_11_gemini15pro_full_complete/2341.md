adhesive:/ədˈhiːsɪv/ /ædˈhiːsɪv/| adj. n.|粘合的，粘着的；粘合剂|A substance that sticks things together.|The adhesive on the envelope wasn't strong enough. 信封上的粘合剂不够牢固。|近义词：sticky, gluey, glutinous, adherent|反义词：non-adhesive, slippery|词性：adjective, noun
adhesive:/ədˈhiːsɪv/ /ædˈhiːsɪv/| n.|粘合剂|A substance used for sticking things together.|We need a strong adhesive to fix this broken vase. 我们需要一种强力粘合剂来修补这个破花瓶。|近义词：glue, paste, cement, gum|反义词：solvent, remover|词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adhaesīvus*, from adhaerēre (“to adhere”), from ad- (“to”) + haerēre (“to stick”).
