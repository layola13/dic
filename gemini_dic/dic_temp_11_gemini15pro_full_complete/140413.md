ojo:/ˈoʊhoʊ/ (US), /ˈɒhɒ/ (UK)| n.|眼睛 (eye); 注视; 注意力; 洞察力|A watchful eye; attention; notice; observation.  It comes from the Spanish word "ojo," which directly translates to "eye."|He kept an ojo on the suspect. 他密切注意着嫌疑人。|近义词: eye, gaze, watch, attention, notice, observation|反义词: inattention, neglect|词性:名词
