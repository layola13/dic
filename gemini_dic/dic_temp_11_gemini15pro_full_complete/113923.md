maestoso:/maɪˈstosoʊ/ /mɑːeˈstəʊsoʊ/| adj. |雄伟的，庄严的|Marked by majestic stateliness, grandeur, and dignity.  It is a musical direction indicating a stately and dignified pace.|The symphony's opening movement was marked maestoso, creating a sense of awe.  交响曲的开场乐章被标记为“雄伟的”，营造出一种敬畏感。|Synonyms: majestic, grand, stately, dignified, noble|Antonyms: humble, simple, modest, unassuming|adjective
