fifed:/faɪft/| v.|吹奏横笛；以横笛声伴奏| played on a fife; accompanied by a fife |The tune was fifed merrily by a young boy. 一个小男孩欢快地吹奏着横笛。|He fifed a jig for the dancers. 他用横笛为舞者们吹奏了一首快步舞曲。|近义词：played, piped, whistled|反义词：(无直接反义词，可考虑silenced, muted)|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fifen, from Old French fifre, from Frankish *pīpa (“pipe”), from Vulgar Latin *pipa, from Latin pīpāre (“to peep, chirp”). Cognate with Dutch pijp (“pipe”), German Pfeife (“pipe, whistle”).
