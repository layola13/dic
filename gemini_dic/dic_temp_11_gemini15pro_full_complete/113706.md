macro:/ˈmækroʊ/ (US) /ˈmækrəʊ/ (UK)| n. adj.|宏, 宏指令，宏观|A single instruction that expands automatically into a set of instructions to perform a particular task.  A prefix meaning “large” or “long.”|例句：The macro automated a series of complex commands.  这个宏指令自动执行了一系列复杂命令。|例句：He studies macro economics. 他学习宏观经济学。|近义词：overall, large-scale, global|反义词：micro, small-scale, local|词性:名词, 形容词
