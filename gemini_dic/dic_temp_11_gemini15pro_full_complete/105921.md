what:/wɒt/ (UK), /hwʌt/, /wʌt/ (US)|pron. det. int. adv.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information about something. 用于询问有关某事的信息。|What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|近义词:which|反义词:无|代词/限定词/疑问词/副词

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you usually eat for breakfast? 你早餐通常吃什么？
What does she do for a living?  她是做什么工作的？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? 他晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done to deserve this award? 她做了什么才赢得这个奖项？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading this week? 他这周一直在读什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last night? 你昨晚做了什么？
What happened at the meeting? 会议上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the lecture? 讲座期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you came here? 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had they discussed before the decision was made? 做决定之前他们讨论了什么？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I interrupted you?  在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? 他们一直在争论什么这么久？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后打算做什么？
What will happen if we don't take action? 如果我们不采取行动会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? 他们在下次会议上会讨论什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by the time we get back? 等我们回来的时候会有什么变化？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时她将已经学习什么多久了？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
I wish I knew what to do in this situation. 我希望我知道在这种情况下该怎么办。


被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？


疑问句:
What is the capital of France? 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? 你周末有什么计划？


否定句:
I don't know what to say. 我不知道该说什么。
She doesn't understand what you mean. 她不明白你的意思。
