decrement:/ˈdekrəmənt/ (US), /ˈdɛkrɪmənt/ (UK)| n. v.| 减少，减量；递减|A decrement is a reduction or decrease in a quantity or value.  As a verb, it means to reduce something by a fixed amount.|

例句：
The decrement in sales was a cause for concern.
销售额的下降令人担忧。

The program will decrement the counter by one each time the button is pressed.
每次按下按钮，程序都会将计数器减一。


近义词：decrease, reduction, decline, diminution|
反义词: increment, increase, augmentation|
词性: noun, verb|
