exhibitionist:/ˌɛksɪˈbɪʃənɪst/
n.
暴露癖者；喜欢引起别人注意的人
A person who compels attention to himself or herself, especially by public exposure or exaggerated behavior.
He was an exhibitionist, always trying to be the center of attention. 他是个爱出风头的人，总是想成为关注的焦点。
She's not shy; she's more of an exhibitionist. 她不害羞；她更像是一个爱表现自己的人。
show-off, attention seeker
introvert, reserved person
noun
