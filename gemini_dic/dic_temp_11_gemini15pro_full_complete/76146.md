fusel:/ˈfjuːzl/ (US), /ˈfjuːsəl/ (UK)| n.|杂醇油；杂醇|A colorless, oily, and toxic liquid consisting of a mixture of higher alcohols, formed as a byproduct of alcoholic fermentation.|The cheap whiskey had a strong fusel smell.|Synonyms: bad liquor, rotgut|Antonyms: pure alcohol|noun
