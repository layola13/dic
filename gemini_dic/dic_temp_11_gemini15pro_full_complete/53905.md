discountenancer:/dɪsˈkʌntənənsər/
n.|阻止者；劝阻者；使人泄气的人或事|Someone or something that discourages or hinders. |His constant criticism acted as a discountenancer to her artistic endeavors. 他不断的批评阻碍了她的艺术追求。|Synonyms: deterrent, inhibitor, impediment|Antonyms: encouragement, incentive|noun

discountenance:/dɪsˈkʌntənəns/
vt.|使气馁；使沮丧；阻止；反对|To discourage, dishearten, or express disapproval of.|The teacher discountenanced any whispering during the exam. 老师不准考试期间有任何交头接耳。|Synonyms: deter, dissuade, disapprove|Antonyms: encourage, support|verb
