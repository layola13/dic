competition:/ˌkɒmpəˈtɪʃn/ (British), /ˌkɑːmpəˈtɪʃn/ (American)| n.| 竞争，比赛|A situation in which someone is trying to win something or be more successful than someone else.|The competition for the job was fierce.  这份工作的竞争非常激烈。|synonyms: rivalry, contest, match, race|antonyms: cooperation, collaboration|noun

competition:/ˌkɒmpəˈtɪʃn/ (British), /ˌkɑːmpəˈtɪʃn/ (American)| n.| 竞争者|A person, team, company, etc. that is competing against others.|Our main competition is another large company in the same industry. 我们的主要竞争对手是同行业中的另一家大公司。|synonyms: rival, opponent, contender, contestant|antonyms: ally, partner|noun
