confrontationalist:/ˌkɒnfrʌnˈteɪʃənəlɪst/ | n. | 对抗主义者；好争论的人 | Someone who seeks out or enjoys confrontation. | He's a bit of a confrontationalist, always picking fights. 他有点好斗，总是挑起争端。 |  She's such a confrontationalist, always stirring up trouble. 她真是个好争论的人，总是惹事生非。 | synonyms: aggressor, antagonist,  provocateur,  rabble-rouser | antonyms: peacemaker, mediator, conciliator | noun

confrontational:/ˌkɒnfrʌnˈteɪʃənl/ | adj. | 对抗性的；挑衅的 | Relating to or involving confrontation. | He has a very confrontational style of management. 他的管理风格非常具有对抗性。 | Using a confrontational approach can sometimes escalate the situation. 使用对抗性的方法有时会使情况升级。 | synonyms: aggressive, antagonistic, hostile, provocative | antonyms: conciliatory, cooperative, peaceful | adjective


Etymology:
From confrontation + -al + -ist.  Confrontation itself comes from the French word "confronter," meaning "to confront," from the Latin "confrontare," meaning "to compare face to face."
