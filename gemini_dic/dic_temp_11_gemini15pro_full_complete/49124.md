deir/dɛər/ (Scottish), /dɪər/ (Irish)| n.|鹿|A hoofed grazing or browsing animal, with branched bony antlers that are shed annually and typically borne only by the male.|The deer grazed peacefully in the meadow. 鹿群在草地上平静地吃草。|近义词:hart, stag (male), hind, doe (female)|反义词:predator|词性:名词

deir/dɛər/ (Scottish), /dɪər/ (Irish) |adj.|亲爱的，珍贵的|Beloved or held in high regard.|My deir friend, I missed you so much. 我亲爱的朋友，我非常想念你。|近义词:beloved, precious, dear|反义词:hateful, despised|词性:形容词
