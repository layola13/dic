canoodlers:/kəˈnuːdlərz/| n. |爱抚者，搂抱者，亲昵者|People who canoodle; those who engage in affectionate cuddling, kissing, and embracing. |The park bench was occupied by a pair of canoodlers, oblivious to the world around them. 公园的长椅上坐着两个搂搂抱抱的情侣，对周围的世界漠不关心。|Synonyms: lovers, sweethearts, snugglers|Antonyms:  arguers, fighters|Noun

canoodle:/kəˈnuːdl/| v. |爱抚，搂抱，亲昵|To engage in affectionate cuddling, kissing, and embracing. |They canoodled on the sofa while watching a movie. 他们一边看电影一边在沙发上搂搂抱抱。|Synonyms: cuddle, snuggle, caress, neck|Antonyms:  fight, argue|Verb
