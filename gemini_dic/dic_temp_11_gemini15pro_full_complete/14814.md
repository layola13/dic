axial:/ˈæksiəl/| adj.|轴的，轴向的，沿轴的|Relating to or situated on an axis; extending along an axis.|The Earth's axial tilt is responsible for the seasons. 地球的自转轴倾斜造成了四季变化。|The axial skeleton includes the skull, vertebral column, and rib cage.  中轴骨骼包括头骨、脊柱和肋骨。|Synonyms: longitudinal, central|Antonyms: peripheral, radial|adjective
