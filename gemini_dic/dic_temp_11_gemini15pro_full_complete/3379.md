affrontiveness:/əˈfrʌntɪvnəs/
n.
冒犯; 失礼; 无礼
The state or quality of being offensive or disrespectful.
His affrontiveness shocked the guests. 他的无礼震惊了客人。
近义词:rudeness, insolence, impudence, offensiveness
反义词:politeness, courtesy, respectfulness
词性:名词

Etymology:
From affront + -ive + -ness.  Affront comes from Old French afronter "to strike on the forehead, insult," from a- "to" + front "forehead."
