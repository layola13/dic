contriver:/kənˈtraɪvər/
n.
发明者，设计者，策划者
Someone who contrives; an inventor or designer, especially of machines or elaborate schemes.
He was the contriver of a new type of engine. 他发明了一种新型引擎。
inventor, designer, deviser, schemer, planner, architect, engineer, builder, maker, creator, originator, author
opponent, adversary, foe
