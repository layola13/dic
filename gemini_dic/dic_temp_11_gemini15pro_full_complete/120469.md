minnow:/ˈmɪnoʊ/ (US), /ˈmɪnəʊ/ (UK)| n. |米诺鱼，小鱼，无足轻重的人|A small freshwater fish of the carp family. Also used figuratively to refer to an insignificant person or thing. |例句：The fisherman caught a minnow. 渔夫钓到了一条米诺鱼。

例句：He felt like a minnow in the vast ocean of the corporate world. 在浩瀚的企业界，他觉得自己像一条小鱼。|近义词：tidder, small fry|反义词：whale, giant|词性:noun
