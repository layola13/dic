duckweed:/ˈdʌkwiːd/| n. |浮萍；青萍|A small, free-floating aquatic plant that often forms a dense mat on the surface of still water.|The pond was covered in duckweed.|Synonyms: water lentil, Wolffia|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps submerged aquatic plants)|noun
