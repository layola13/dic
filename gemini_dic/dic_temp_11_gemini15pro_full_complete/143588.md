outhunting:/ˌaʊtˈhʌntɪŋ/
verb
胜过狩猎，在狩猎中超过
Surpassing or exceeding in hunting; hunting more successfully than.
The young hunter was outhunting his father, bringing back more game each day.  年轻的猎人胜过他的父亲，每天带回更多的猎物。
近义词: outdoing, surpassing, excelling
反义词: failing, underperforming
词性: verb
