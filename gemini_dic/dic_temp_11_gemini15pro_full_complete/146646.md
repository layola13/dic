overharsh:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrʃ/ | adj. | 过于严厉的，过分苛刻的 | Excessively harsh; too severe. |  The teacher's criticism was overharsh, considering the student's effort. 老师的批评过于严厉，考虑到学生的努力。 | Synonyms: excessively severe, unduly harsh, too strict, overcritical | Antonyms: lenient, gentle, mild, forgiving | Word type: Adjective

overharsh:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrʃ/ | adj. | 过于刺耳的，过于尖锐的 (referring to sound) | Excessively sharp or grating to the ear. | The overharsh sound of the feedback made everyone wince.  刺耳的反馈声让每个人都畏缩了一下。| Synonyms: grating, strident, piercing, shrill | Antonyms: mellow, soft, gentle, soothing | Word type: Adjective
