celebratory: /ˈsɛləbrətɔːri/ (US), /ˈsɛləbreɪtəri/ (UK)| adj.| 庆祝的，庆贺的 |Marking or relating to a celebration. |例句：They held a celebratory dinner after the successful launch.  他们成功发射后举行了一场庆祝晚宴。

例句：The atmosphere was celebratory. 气氛很喜庆。

近义词：festive, joyous, commemorating | 反义词：mournful, somber | 词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *celebrātōrius, from Latin celebrātus (past participle of celebrāre ‘to frequent, celebrate’), from celeber ‘frequented, populous’.
