geff:/ɡɛf/ | n. | 有效质量 | Effective mass.  A parameter that represents the inertial properties of an electron or hole in a crystal lattice, taking into account the influence of the periodic potential of the lattice. | The geff of the electrons in this material is significantly larger than the free electron mass.  这种材料中电子的有效质量明显大于自由电子的质量。|  synonyms: effective mass | antonyms: N/A | noun
