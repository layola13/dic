moutan:/ˈmoʊtɑːn/| n. |牡丹|A shrubby plant, Paeonia suffruticosa, of the peony family, native to China, widely cultivated for its large, showy, usually double, variously colored flowers.|The moutan is a symbol of prosperity in Chinese culture. 牡丹在中国文化中是繁荣的象征。|Synonyms: tree peony|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
