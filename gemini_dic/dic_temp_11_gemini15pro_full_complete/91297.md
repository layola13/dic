hydromagnesite:/ˌhaɪdrəʊˈmæɡnəsaɪt/| n.|水菱镁矿|A hydrated magnesium carbonate mineral, typically occurring as white, chalky masses.  It is formed by the alteration of magnesium-rich rocks in a near-surface environment.|Hydromagnesite deposits were discovered in the caves. 在洞穴里发现了水菱镁矿矿床。|Synonyms:  Hydrated magnesium carbonate,  |Antonyms: None|noun
