magnetician:/ˌmæɡnəˈtɪʃən/ | n. | 磁学家；磁性学家 | A scientist who studies magnetism and magnetic materials. |  The magnetician explained the principles of magnetic resonance imaging.  磁学家解释了磁共振成像的原理。|  近义词: physicist, scientist | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From magnet + -ician.  From Latin *magneta, from Magnesia (a region in ancient Thessaly, Greece, where magnetic lodestones were found).
