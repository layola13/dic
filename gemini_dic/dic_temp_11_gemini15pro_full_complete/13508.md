attaghan:/ˈætəɡən/| n.|袭击者，攻击者; 爪牙|A person who attacks another person or place.  Someone who supports a particular person, group, or plan, especially in a forceful or aggressive way.|The rebels launched an attaghan on the government forces. 叛军对政府军发动了袭击。|Synonyms: assailant, attacker, aggressor, raider, assaulter|Antonyms: defender, protector|noun
