envelope:/ˈenvələʊp/ (UK) /ˈɑːnvəloʊp/ (US)| n. |信封; 覆盖物; 包层; 封皮|A flat paper container, usually rectangular and gummed, for sending a letter or other document in.|I sealed the letter in the envelope. 我把信封封好。|Synonyms: wrapper, covering, sheath|Antonyms: contents|noun

envelope:/ɪnˈvɛləp/ (US) /ɪnˈvɛləʊp/ (UK)| vt. |包住; 覆盖; 笼罩; 遮蔽|To wrap up, cover, or surround completely.|Fog enveloped the city. 雾笼罩着城市。|Synonyms: surround, encase, enfold|Antonyms: uncover, expose|verb
