mercurialization:/mɜːkjʊəriəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.| 汞化；汞中毒；水银中毒|The process of combining or treating something with mercury, or the condition of being poisoned by mercury. |The mercurialization of felt hats was a common practice in the 19th century, leading to widespread mercury poisoning among hatters.| 近义词: mercury poisoning | 反义词: detoxification | 词性: 名词
