counteracts:/ˌkaʊntərˈækts/| v. |抵消，中和，对抗| acts against something in order to reduce its force or neutralize it |  Regular exercise counteracts the effects of a sedentary lifestyle.  规律的运动可以抵消久坐不动的生活方式带来的影响。

The medication counteracts the poison. 这种药物可以中和毒物。 | synonyms: neutralizes, offsets, combats, opposes | antonyms: reinforces, strengthens, enhances, exacerbates | verb
