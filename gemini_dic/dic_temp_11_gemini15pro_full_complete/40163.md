contendingly:/kənˈtɛndɪŋli/
adverb
争辩地，竞争地
in a contending manner; characterized by contention or dispute.
The two teams battled contendingly for the championship.  两支队伍为了冠军而激烈竞争。
He argued contendingly that his proposal was the best. 他争辩说他的提议是最好的。
近义词: argumentatively, competitively, disputatiously
反义词: agreeably, harmoniously, cooperatively
词性: adverb

contend:/kənˈtɛnd/
verb
竞争，争夺；声称，主张
1. (v.) To strive in rivalry; to vie; to fight.
2. (v.) To maintain; to assert; to argue.
Etymology: From Latin "contendere" (to stretch out, strive, contend), from "com-" (together) + "tendere" (to stretch).
The athletes contended for the gold medal.  运动员们争夺金牌。
He contends that the evidence is flawed. 他声称证据有缺陷。
近义词: compete, vie, argue, assert
反义词: yield, surrender, concede
词性: verb
