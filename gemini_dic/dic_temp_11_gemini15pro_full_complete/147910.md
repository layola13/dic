overreflectivenesses:/ˌoʊvərˌriːflɛkˈtɪvnəsɪz/
n.
过度反思
The tendency to excessively analyze oneself, one's thoughts, feelings, and motivations, often leading to paralysis and inaction.
Her overreflectivenesses prevented her from making a decision. 她过度反思让她无法做出决定。
Overthinking, rumination, introspection
Impulsiveness, spontaneity
noun
