existed:/ɪɡˈzɪstɪd/
verb
存在过，曾经存在
The dinosaurs existed millions of years ago. 恐龙存在于数百万年前。
Evidence suggests that the civilization existed for centuries. 证据表明，该文明存在了几个世纪。
近义词: lived, was, was present, subsisted
反义词: perished, vanished, died out
词性: verb
