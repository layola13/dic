adware:/ˈædˌweər/| n.|广告软件|Software that automatically displays or downloads advertising material (often unwanted) when a user is online.|Example 1: My computer is running slowly because it's infected with adware. 我的电脑运行很慢，因为它感染了广告软件。

Example 2:  The free software I downloaded came bundled with adware, which is annoying. 我下载的免费软件捆绑了广告软件，真烦人。|近义词：malware, spyware|反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "anti-adware" or "ad-blocking software")|词性: noun
