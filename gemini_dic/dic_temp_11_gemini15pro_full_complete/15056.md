babygirl:/ˈbeɪbi ɡɜːrl/ /ˈbeɪbi ɡɝːl/| n.|宝贝女孩，小女孩，(尤指对女性的爱称)|A term of endearment for a female, often used romantically or between close friends.|例句：He calls his girlfriend "babygirl." 他称他的女朋友为“宝贝女孩”。

例句：She's my babygirl, even though she's 20 years old. 她虽然20岁了，但仍然是我的宝贝女孩。

近义词：sweetheart, darling, honey, babe|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
