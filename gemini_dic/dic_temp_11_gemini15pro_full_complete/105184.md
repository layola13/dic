keyboarded:/ˈkiːbɔːrdɪd/| adj.|键盘输入的，用键盘打的|Typed using a keyboard.  This term describes something that has been entered or created using a keyboard.  It is often used in the context of digital text, music, or data. |例句：The keyboarded document was easier to edit than the handwritten one. 用键盘输入的文档比手写的文档更容易编辑。

例句：She keyboarded the entire novel in just three months.  她仅用了三个月就用键盘打完了整部小说。|近义词：typed, input, entered|反义词:handwritten, analog|词性:形容词
