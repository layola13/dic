hyperconstitutionalisms:/ˌhaɪpərˌkɑːnstɪˈtuːʃənəlɪzəmz/
|n.|超级宪政主义|The belief in or advocacy of strict adherence to a constitution, often to the point of elevating it above other considerations.  It emphasizes the supremacy of the constitution and its principles in all aspects of governance and legal interpretation.|The party's platform embraced hyperconstitutionalisms, arguing that any deviation from the original document was a betrayal of the nation's founding principles.|近义词: strict constitutionalism, originalism|反义词: judicial activism, living constitutionalism|词性: Noun
