intermediates:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdiəts/ | n. | 中间体；中间物；媒介物 |A substance formed during a chemical process before the desired product is obtained.  A person who has reached an intermediate level of skill, proficiency, or knowledge. |The reaction produces several unstable intermediates. 这个反应会产生几种不稳定的中间体。| synonyms: intermediary, mediator, go-between, agent, broker, middleman, negotiator | antonyms: extremes, endpoints | noun

intermediate:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdiət/ | adj. | 中间的；中级的 |Lying or occurring between two extremes or in a middle position or state.  Being at a level of development or learning between basic and advanced. |She enrolled in an intermediate French class. 她报名参加了一个中级法语班。| synonyms: middle, medium, mid, midway, halfway, central, mean | antonyms: beginning, elementary, advanced, extreme | adjective
