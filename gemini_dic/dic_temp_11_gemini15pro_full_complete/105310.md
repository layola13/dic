kibishi:/kiˈbiːʃiː/ | adj. | 严格的，严厉的，苛刻的 |  Derived from the Japanese word "厳しい" (kibishī), meaning "strict," "severe," or "harsh."  It describes someone who demands high standards and is inflexible. |  The kibishi teacher expected perfect attendance.  这位严厉的老师要求全勤。|  strict, severe, demanding, rigorous, harsh | lenient, easygoing, relaxed | adjective
