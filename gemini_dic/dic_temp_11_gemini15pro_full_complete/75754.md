fulminate:/ˈfʊlmɪneɪt/| v. |强烈谴责；爆炸；引爆|to criticize harshly and vehemently; to explode suddenly and violently|The senator fulminated against the proposed tax increases. 参议员强烈谴责拟议中的增税方案。|近义词：denounce, condemn, explode, detonate|反义词：praise, commend, defuse|词性:动词
fulminate:/ˈfʊlmɪneɪt/| n. |雷酸盐；雷汞|a salt or ester of fulminic acid|Mercury fulminate is a primary explosive. 雷汞是一种起爆药。|近义词：explosive|反义词：-|词性:名词
