dumpling:/ˈdʌmplɪŋ/ (US), /ˈdʌmplɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|饺子；汤圆；团子|A small ball of dough (= flour and water mixed together) cooked and eaten, often with meat or vegetables inside.|例句：My favorite food is dumplings. 我最喜欢的食物是饺子。

例句：She made delicious pork dumplings for dinner. 她晚饭做了美味的猪肉饺子。|近义词：potsticker, ravioli, pierogi, wonton|反义词: (No direct antonym for a food item)|词性:noun
