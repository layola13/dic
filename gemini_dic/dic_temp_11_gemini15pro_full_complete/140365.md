oilbird:/ˈɔɪlbɜːrd/ | n. | 油鸱 | A nocturnal fruit-eating bird (Steatornis caripensis) of northern South America, the only nightjar to navigate by echolocation. It nests in colonies in caves and is a source of oil used by local people. |  Oilbirds use echolocation to navigate in dark caves. 油鸱利用回声定位在黑暗的洞穴中导航。 | synonyms: guacharo, cave bird | antonyms: N/A | noun

oilbird:/ˈɔɪlbɜːrd/ | n. | 油鸟 |  A nocturnal, fruit-eating bird found in South America, known for its echolocation abilities and the oil produced by its young. | The oilbird is the only nightjar species that uses echolocation. 油鸟是唯一使用回声定位的夜鹰物种。 | synonyms: guacharo | antonyms: N/A | noun
