benefit:/ˈbenɪfɪt/| n. vt. vi.|利益，好处；受益；有益于|Something that is advantageous or good; an advantage.|例句：The benefit of regular exercise is improved health.  规律锻炼的好处是改善健康。|近义词：advantage, profit, gain|反义词：disadvantage, detriment, harm|词性:n.

benefit:/ˈbenɪfɪt/| vt. vi.|使受益；得益；|To receive an advantage; profit.|例句：The charity benefited from the generous donation.  这个慈善机构受益于慷慨的捐赠。|近义词：help, aid, assist|反义词：harm, hurt, injure|词性:v.
