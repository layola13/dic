nonadvocacy:/nɑːnˈædvəkəsi/
n.
不提倡，不拥护，不辩护
lack of advocacy; absence of pleading for a cause or position.
The organization's nonadvocacy stance prevents it from endorsing any political candidate.  这个组织的不拥护立场使其不能支持任何政治候选人。
non-endorsement, neutrality, impartiality
advocacy, support, promotion
noun
