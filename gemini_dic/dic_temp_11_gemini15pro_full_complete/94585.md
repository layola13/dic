impracticableness:/ɪmˌpræktɪkəbəlnəs/
n.
不可行性；不切实际；行不通
The impracticableness of the plan was evident from the start.
这个计划不可行性从一开始就很明显。
The committee acknowledged the impracticableness of implementing the proposal within the given timeframe.
委员会承认在规定的时间内实施该提案是不可行的。
Near Synonyms: infeasibility, impossibility, unworkability, impracticality
Antonyms: feasibility, practicality, possibility, viability
ETYMOLOGY->
From impracticable + -ness.  Impracticable from in- "not" + practicable "able to be put into practice," from practic "of action, practical" (from Old French practique "practical," from Medieval Latin practicus "active, practical," from Greek praktikos "fit for action," from prassein "to do, act, perform") + -able.
