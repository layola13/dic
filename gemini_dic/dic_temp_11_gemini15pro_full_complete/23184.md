bowdlerize: /ˈboʊdləˌraɪz/ (US) /ˈbaʊdləraɪz/ (UK)| vt.| 删节，净化，修订（通常指删除被认为不道德或冒犯的内容）| To remove material that is considered improper or offensive from a text or account, especially by omitting or modifying words. | The publisher bowdlerized the novel before releasing it to a younger audience. 出版商在将小说发行给年轻读者之前删节了其中的内容。|  The play was bowdlerized to remove the profanity. 这部戏剧被删改以去除其中的脏话。| 近义词: censor, expurgate, sanitize | 反义词: unexpurgated | 词性: 动词
