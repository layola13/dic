handless:/ˈhændlɪs/| adj.|无手的，没有把手的|Lacking hands; having no hands.  Also, lacking a handle. |The handless statue was a poignant reminder of the war's casualties. 这座无手的雕像令人心酸地想起战争中的伤亡。

The handless mug was difficult to hold.  这个没有把手的杯子很难拿。|Near synonyms:  armless, manacled (when bound), |Antonyms:  dexterous, skillful|adjective
