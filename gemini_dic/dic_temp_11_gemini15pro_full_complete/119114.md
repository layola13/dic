metronymic:/ˌmɛtrəˈnɪmɪk/
n.
母姓，取自母亲的名字
A name derived from the name of a person's mother or a female ancestor.
Her last name is a metronymic, passed down through generations of women. 她的姓氏是母姓，代代相传。
近义词: matronymic
反义词: patronymic
词性: noun
