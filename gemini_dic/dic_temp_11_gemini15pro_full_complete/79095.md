glacis:/ˈɡleɪsɪs/ /ˈɡlɑːsiː/| n.|斜堤，缓坡；冰川|A gently sloping bank, especially one that slopes down from a fortification.  A smooth expanse of ice; a glacier.|The soldiers charged across the glacis.|近义词：slope, incline, ramp, bank, gradient, escarpment (when referring to fortifications)|反义词:cliff, precipice|noun
