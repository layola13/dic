captaining:/ˈkæptɪnɪŋ/
verb
担任队长，指挥
acting as a captain
例句：He is captaining the team this year.  他今年担任队长。

例句：She's been captaining the debate team for three years. 她担任辩论队队长已经三年了。
近义词：leading, heading, commanding
反义词：following, obeying
词性：v.
