individuality:/ˌɪndɪˌvɪdʒuˈælɪti/| n.|个性，特性，独特|The quality or character of a particular person or thing that distinguishes them from others of the same kind.|She expressed her individuality through her unique style of dress. 她通过独特的穿着风格展现了个性。|synonyms: uniqueness, distinctiveness, originality, singularity, peculiarity|antonyms: conformity, similarity, sameness|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English: from medieval Latin individualitas, from individuus (see individual).
