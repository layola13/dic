nocturn:/ˈnɒktɜːn/ (UK) /ˈnɑːktɜːrn/ (US)| n.|夜曲；夜祷|A piece of music, especially for the piano, that is expressive of the night.|Chopin's nocturnes are some of his most beautiful works. 肖邦的夜曲是他最优美的作品中的一部分。|近义词:serenade|反义词:aubade|词性:名词

nocturnal:/nɒkˈtɜːnl/ (UK) /nɑːkˈtɜːrnl/ (US)| adj.|夜间的；夜间活动的|Done, occurring, or active at night.|Bats are nocturnal creatures.  蝙蝠是夜行动物。|近义词:nightly, nighttime|反义词:diurnal|词性:形容词
