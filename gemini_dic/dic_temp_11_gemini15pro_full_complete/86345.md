hectarage:/ˈhɛktərɪdʒ/ | n. | 面积（以公顷计）; 公顷数 |  The farm has a hectarage of 100.  这个农场面积为100公顷。 | area, acreage | extent | noun
Etymology:  From hectare + -age.  Hectare comes from French,  from Greek *hekatón 'hundred' + are 'a unit of area'.  The -age suffix forms nouns denoting an aggregate, collection, or quantity.
