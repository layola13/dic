flame-proof:/ˈfleɪmˌpruːf/
adjective
防火的，阻燃的
Treated or made of a material that will not burn easily.
例句：The firefighters wore flame-proof clothing. 消防队员穿着防火服。

例句：Flame-proof curtains are essential in a kitchen. 厨房里防火窗帘是必不可少的。

近义词：fire-resistant, fireproof, non-flammable
反义词：flammable, combustible
词性：adjective


flame-proof:/ˈfleɪmˌpruːf/
noun
防火材料
A material that will not burn easily.
例句：The new building materials include a type of flame-proof. 新的建筑材料包括一种防火材料。

例句：They used flame-proof to protect the important documents. 他们用防火材料来保护重要文件。

近义词：fireproofing, fire retardant
反义词: accelerant
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"flame" (Old French "flambe") + "proof" (Old English "prōf" - a test, trial)  Combined to form "flame-proof," meaning resistant to catching fire.
