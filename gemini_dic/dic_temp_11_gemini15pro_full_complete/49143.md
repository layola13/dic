deject:/dɪˈdʒɛkt/
verb
使沮丧，使灰心
To make someone sad and dispirited; to lower someone's spirits.
The news of her friend's illness dejected her. 她朋友生病的消息让她很沮丧。
He was dejected after failing the exam. 他考试不及格后很沮丧。
synonyms:discourage, dispirit, depress, sadden
antonyms:encourage, cheer, uplift
词性:v.
