what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US) |pron. det. adv.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information about something.| What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|Which, who |Nothing|pronoun/determiner/adverb

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? = 你怎么想？
What does he do for a living? = 他做什么工作？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is she doing this weekend? = 她这周末做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned recently? = 你最近学了什么？
What has he been up to? = 他最近在忙什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on? = 你一直在忙什么？
What has she been reading lately? = 她最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 聚会上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? = 她参加聚会穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had they eaten for dinner? = 他们晚饭吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the phone rang? = 电话响之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about? = 他们一直在争论什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting? = 他们将在会议上讨论什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将取得什么成就？
What will have changed by then? = 到那时会有什么变化？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been studying by the time you graduate? = 到你毕业的时候你将学习什么多久了？
What will they have been doing for the past ten years by 2030? = 到2030年，他们在过去的十年里将会做什么？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
What if I told you the truth? = 如果我告诉你真相会怎么样？

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在对这个问题采取什么措施？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what you're saying. = 她不明白你在说什么。
