heroism:/ˈhɛrəʊɪzəm/ (US) /ˈhɪərəʊɪzəm/ (UK)| n. |英雄主义；英勇行为|Great courage or bravery, especially in battle or other dangerous situations.  The quality of being a hero.|例句：The firefighters showed incredible heroism in rescuing the trapped family. 消防队员在营救被困家庭时展现了令人难以置信的英雄主义。

例句：Her heroism during the war earned her several medals. 她在战争中的英勇行为为她赢得了数枚奖章。|近义词：bravery, courage, valor, gallantry|反义词:cowardice, fear, timidity|词性:名词
