biweekly: /ˌbaɪˈwiːkli/ | adj. adv.|  两周一次的，每两周一次 | Happening every two weeks or twice a week.  |  Example: I receive a biweekly newsletter. 我每两周收到一份简报。| Synonym: semi-monthly, fortnightly | Antonym: weekly, monthly | word type: adjective/adverb

biweekly: /ˌbaɪˈwiːkli/ | n.|  双周刊 | A publication issued every two weeks. | Example:  I subscribe to a biweekly magazine. 我订阅了一份双周刊杂志。 | Synonym: semi-monthly publication | Antonym: weekly publication, monthly publication | word type: noun
