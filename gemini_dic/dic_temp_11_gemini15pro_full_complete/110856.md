linux:/ˈlɪnəks/ | n. | Linux（操作系统） |  A freely distributable open-source operating system that runs on a wide variety of computer hardware. It is based on the Unix operating system and is developed by a global community of programmers. | I installed Linux on my old laptop. 我在我的旧笔记本电脑上安装了Linux。 |  Linux is a powerful and versatile operating system. Linux是一个强大且多功能的操作系统。 | 近义词: Unix, GNU/Linux | 反义词: Windows, macOS | 词性: noun
