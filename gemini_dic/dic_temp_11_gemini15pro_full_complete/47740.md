decahydronaphthalene:/ˌdekəˌhaɪdroʊˈnæfθəˌlin/ | n. |十氢化萘|A saturated hydrocarbon derived from naphthalene by the addition of ten hydrogen atoms. It exists as cis and trans isomers.|Decahydronaphthalene is used as a solvent in some industrial processes.|Synonyms: decalin, bicyclo[4.4.0]decane|Antonyms: naphthalene (in a sense, as it is the unsaturated parent compound)|noun
