higgler:/ˈhɪɡlər/| n.|小贩；流动商贩；（尤指）在市场上或挨家挨户兜售农产品的小贩|A person, typically a woman, who travels from place to place selling small items, especially food, door to door or at markets.|例句：The higgler sold fresh vegetables from her cart.  那个小贩在她的手推车上卖新鲜蔬菜。

例句：Many higglers in Jamaica sell their produce at the local market.  在牙买加，许多小贩在当地市场出售他们的农产品。|近义词：vendor, peddler, hawker, trader, merchant|反义词:buyer, customer|词性:noun
