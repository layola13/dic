bedesman:/ˈbiːdzmən/| n.|施舍受领者，祈祷者|A person who is supported by a charity and required to pray for the benefactor.  A person, often old or disabled, who prays for others, especially one who was formerly supported by charity to do so.|The aged bedesman knelt in the chapel, praying for the king's health.|Synonym: almsman, pauper, supplicant|Antonym: benefactor, philanthropist|noun
