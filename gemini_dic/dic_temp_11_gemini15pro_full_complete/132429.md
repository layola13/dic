nondependence:/ˌnɒndɪˈpɛndəns/ |n.| 非依赖性；独立性 |The state of not being dependent on something or someone.  |Example: The country achieved nondependence in food production.  这个国家实现了粮食生产的自给自足。

Example:  Her financial nondependence allowed her to pursue her artistic passions. 她的经济独立使她能够追求自己的艺术热情。 |Synonyms: independence, self-sufficiency, autonomy, self-reliance |Antonyms: dependence, reliance |noun
