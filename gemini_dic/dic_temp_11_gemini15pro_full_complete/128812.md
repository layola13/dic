nightlier:/ˈnaɪt.li.ər/
adjective
更夜的；更频繁的夜间发生的
happening more frequently at night.
例句：The owls' calls became nightlier as the breeding season approached. 繁殖季节临近，猫头鹰的叫声在夜间更加频繁。

例句：Nightlier patrols were implemented to deter crime.  为了阻止犯罪，夜间巡逻更加频繁。
近义词：more nocturnal, more frequent at night
反义词：less nocturnal, less frequent at night
词性: adjective

词源:  "nightly" + "-er" (comparative suffix). "Nightly" comes from "night" (Old English *niht) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  The "-er" suffix is used to form the comparative degree of adjectives.
