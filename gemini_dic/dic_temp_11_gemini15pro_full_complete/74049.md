fosset:/ˈfɑːsɪt/ (US) /ˈfɒsɪt/ (UK)| n.|水龙头，（尤指旧式）龙头|A device that controls the flow of liquid, especially from a pipe.  Specifically, an older term for a faucet/tap.|The old fosset in the garden leaked constantly.|近义词：faucet, tap, spigot|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "plug" or "stopper" in the context of stopping flow)|noun
