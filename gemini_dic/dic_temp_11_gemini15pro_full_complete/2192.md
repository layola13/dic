addition:/əˈdɪʃn/ | n. | 添加；增加；加法；附加物 |  The addition of a new wing to the building provided much-needed space.  给大楼增加了一个新侧翼，提供了急需的空间。 |  例句：The addition of spices improved the flavor of the dish. 添加香料改善了菜肴的味道。

The new car is a welcome addition to the family. 这辆新车是这个家庭的一个受欢迎的补充。 | 近义词：increase, supplement, addendum | 反义词：subtraction, deduction, removal | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *addicioun*, from Old French adicion, from Latin additiō (“addition”), from additus, past participle of addō (“add”).  源于中古英语 *addicioun*，来自古法语 adicion，来自拉丁语 additiō（“加法”），来自 additus，addō（“添加”）的过去分词。
