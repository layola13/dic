insolublenesses:/ɪnˈsɒljʊblnəsɪz/
n.
不可溶性；不溶解性
the state or quality of being insoluble.
The insolublenesses of certain compounds make them ideal for specific applications.  某些化合物的不可溶性使它们成为特定应用的理想选择。
Near synonyms: insolubility, undissolvedness
Antonyms: solubility, dissolvability
noun
