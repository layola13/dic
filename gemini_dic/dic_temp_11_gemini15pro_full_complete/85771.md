hayed:/heɪd/ | v. | 储存干草;  | to store hay; to cut and dry grass for hay |  The farmer hayed the field last week.  农民上周把田里的草晒干储存起来了。|  The ranchers hayed their alfalfa for winter feed.  农场主们把他们的苜蓿晒干储存起来作为冬季饲料。| 近义词: store, harvest | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hēgian (attested only in gehēgian "to hedge in"), from hēg "hedge".  Related to hedge.  The original sense was to enclose hay in a hedge or haystack.  The meaning shifted to the process of making hay.
