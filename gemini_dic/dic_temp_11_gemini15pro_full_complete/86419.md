heedfulnesses:/ˈhiːdfʊlnɪsɪz/
n.
注意，留心，谨慎
The state of being heedful; attentiveness.
He demonstrated great heedfulnesses in his preparation for the exam. 他在考试准备中表现出了极大的谨慎。
近义词：attentiveness, mindfulness, alertness, vigilance
反义词：negligence, carelessness, inattention, disregard
词性: 名词

词源:  From heedful + -ness. Heedful comes from Middle English *hedful, from heed + -ful. Heed comes from Old English hēdan "to take heed, observe, attend to," from Proto-Germanic *hauzijan "to hear, listen, perceive."
