hyperpigmented:/ˌhaɪpərˈpɪɡməntɪd/
adjective
过度色素沉着的，色素沉积的
Characterized by hyperpigmentation; abnormally darkened skin.
Example: The doctor examined the hyperpigmented patches on her skin. 医生检查了她皮肤上色素沉积的斑块。
Example:  Sun exposure can cause hyperpigmented areas on the face.  阳光照射会在脸上造成色素沉着区域。
Synonym:  discolored, darkened, pigmented
Antonym: hypopigmented, depigmented
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- (prefix meaning 'over, excessive') + pigmented (from pigment, meaning 'coloring matter').  Ultimately from Latin *pigmentum*, from *pingere* 'to paint'.
