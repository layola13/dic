overrates:/ˌoʊvərˈreɪts/
verb
高估
To have too high an opinion of.
The critic overrated the mediocre movie.  这位评论家高估了这部平庸的电影。
overestimate, overvalue
underrate, undervalue
词性:动词

overrated:/ˌoʊvərˈreɪtɪd/
adjective
被高估的
Regarded too highly.
The restaurant was overrated and overpriced. 这家餐馆被高估了，价格也过高。
overvalued, overestimated
underrated, undervalued
词性:形容词
