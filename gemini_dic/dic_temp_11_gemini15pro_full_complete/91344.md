hydroplane:/ˈhaɪdrəpleɪn/ (US) /ˈhaɪdrəpleɪn/ (UK)| n. vi.|水上飞机；滑水板；|A hydroplane is an airplane designed to take off from and land on water.  A hydroplane is also a board used for riding on the surface of water when towed behind a motorboat.|The hydroplane skimmed across the lake. 水上飞机掠过湖面。|近义词：seaplane, floatplane|反义词:|noun, intransitive verb

hydroplane:/ˈhaɪdrəpleɪn/ (US) /ˈhaɪdrəpleɪn/ (UK)| vi.|滑水；(车轮)打滑|When a vehicle hydroplanes, its tires lose contact with the road surface due to a layer of water.|The car hydroplaned on the wet road and spun out. 汽车在湿滑的路面上打滑并旋转了起来。|近义词：aquaplane, skid|反义词:|intransitive verb
