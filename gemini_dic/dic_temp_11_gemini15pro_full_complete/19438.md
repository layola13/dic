biconcave:/baɪˈkɒnkeɪv/ /baɪˈkɑːnkeɪv/| adj.|双凹的|Having two concave surfaces, curving inwards.|Red blood cells have a biconcave shape. 红细胞呈双凹形。|Example: The lens of the eye is biconcave. 眼睛的晶状体是双凹的。|Synonym:concavo-concave|Antonym:biconvex|adjective

biconcave:/baɪˈkɒnkeɪv/ /baɪˈkɑːnkeɪv/| n.|双凹面|A shape or surface that is concave on both sides.|The biconcave shape of red blood cells increases their surface area. 红细胞的双凹形状增加了它们的表面积。|Example: The biconcave disc is commonly used in optics. 双凹面圆盘常用于光学。|Synonym:concavo-concave disc|Antonym:biconvex disc|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bi- "two" + concave.  "Concave" comes from Latin *concavus, from com- "together" + cavus "hollow".
