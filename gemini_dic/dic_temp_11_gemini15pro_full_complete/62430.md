endophyllous:/ˌɛndoʊˈfɪləs/| adj.|内生的，叶内的|Growing or living within a leaf or leaves.  Specifically, referring to fungi or other organisms that live inside leaf tissue without causing immediate harm.|Example: The endophyllous fungi colonize the leaf tissue. 这些内生真菌在叶组织中繁殖。|Example:  Researchers are studying the role of endophyllous bacteria in plant growth. 研究人员正在研究内生细菌在植物生长中的作用。|近义词:intrafolial|反义词:epiphyllous|词性:形容词
