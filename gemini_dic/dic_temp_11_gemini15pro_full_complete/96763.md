ineptness:/ɪˈnɛptnɪs/
n.
无能；笨拙；不称职
lack of skill or ability; awkwardness.
His ineptness as a leader is obvious. 他作为领导者的无能显而易见。
ineptitude, incompetence, clumsiness, awkwardness
skill, ability, competence, dexterity
noun
