nondisclosure: /ˌnɒn.dɪˈskləʊ.ʒər/ (UK) /ˌnɑːn.dɪˈskloʊ.ʒər/ (US)| n.|保密；不公开；不泄露|The act of not disclosing something; a legal agreement that prevents someone from disclosing information.|例句：The company required all employees to sign a nondisclosure agreement.  这家公司要求所有员工签署保密协议。

例句：The nondisclosure of her medical history was crucial to protect her privacy. 对她病史的保密对于保护她的隐私至关重要。


近义词：secrecy, confidentiality, silence|反义词: disclosure, revelation|词性:noun
