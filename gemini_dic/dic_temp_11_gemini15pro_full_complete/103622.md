jointweed:/ˈdʒɔɪntwiːd/
n.
节节草，木贼
A common name for plants of the genus *Polygonum*, especially knotweed (*P. aviculare*).  Also used for other plants with jointed stems, such as horsetail (*Equisetum* species).
The farmer struggled to eradicate the persistent jointweed from his fields.  农民努力从他的田地里根除顽固的节节草。
Synonyms: knotweed, knotgrass, doorweed, wireweed, swinegrass, birdweed
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a plant name, could list desirable plants)
