kib:/kɪb/| n.|基布;千比特|A kibibyte (KiB) is a unit of information or computer storage equal to 1024 bytes (2^10).  A kib is a less formal term often used for kibibyte.  It's sometimes mistakenly used interchangeably with kilobyte (KB), which is 1000 bytes (10^3).|He downloaded a 10 kib file.|近义词：kibibyte, kilobyte (informally)|反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement)|noun
