diplograph:/ˈdɪpləˌɡræf/| n.|双写字母，双字母|A group of two letters representing a single sound.  It can also refer to an instrument for writing double.|The "ph" in "phone" is a diplograph. "phone"中的"ph"是一个双写字母。|Synonym: digraph|Antonym: N/A|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *di- "two, double" + -graph "writing"
