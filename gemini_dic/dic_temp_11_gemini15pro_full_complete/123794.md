mope:/moʊp/| vi. n.|闷闷不乐，郁郁寡欢；闲荡，游荡|To be in a low or dejected state of mind; to wander about listlessly.|He's been moping around the house ever since he lost his job.  他失业后一直闷闷不乐地在家闲逛。|近义词：brood, sulk, languish|反义词：rejoice, celebrate|词性:动词(vi.), 名词(n.)
mope:/moʊp/| n.|闷闷不乐的人；游荡|A person who is listlessly and dejected; the act of moping.|He was such a mope after his girlfriend left him.  他女朋友离开他之后，他变得非常消沉。|近义词：sad sack, killjoy|反义词：optimist, cheerleader|词性:名词(n.)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *māpen "to gape, to be dazed," probably of imitative origin.
