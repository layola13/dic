flummoxer:/ˈflʌməksər/ | n. | 使困惑的人或事；难题 | Something or someone that confuses or perplexes. | The crossword clue was a real flummoxer.  这个纵横字谜的线索真是个难题。| Synonyms: puzzle, enigma, conundrum, baffler | Antonyms: clarification, explanation | noun

flummox:/ˈflʌməks/ | vt. | 使困惑；难住 | To bewilder or perplex. | The complex instructions completely flummoxed me. 这些复杂的说明完全把我搞糊涂了。| Synonyms: bewilder, confuse, perplex, confound | Antonyms: clarify, enlighten | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From the dialectal flummox "to bewilder, confound," of uncertain origin, possibly related to flamask "to flicker."  First recorded in the mid-19th century.
