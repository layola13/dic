elaeoptene:/ɪˈliːɒptiːn/ | n. |香精油;液态萜烯|The elaeoptene is the liquid portion of an essential oil, as distinguished from the stearoptene. 香精油的液态部分，与硬脂烯不同。| Peppermint oil's elaeoptene contributes to its characteristic cooling sensation. 薄荷油中的香精油使其具有独特的清凉感。|Synonyms:  liquid terpene, essential oil fraction |Antonyms: stearoptene |noun
