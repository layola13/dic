fended:/fɛndɪd/
|v.|抵挡，防御，挡开，照顾自己|Past tense of "fend". |He fended off the blows with his arms. 他用手臂挡开了打击。|近义词：warded off, parried, deflected, repelled, resisted|反义词：succumbed, yielded|verb

fended:/fɛndɪd/
|adj.|设防的，有防御的 (rare usage)|Protected or defended. (This usage is less common.)|The fended city held strong against the siege.  设防的城市在围攻中屹立不倒。|近义词：fortified, defended, protected|反义词：vulnerable, exposed, undefended|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fenden*, from Old English *fendan* "to defend, protect, provide, support," from Proto-Germanic *fandijaną* "to try, test, tempt," from Proto-Indo-European *\*bʰendʰ- "to bind, tie."  The sense shifted from "provide for" to "defend."
