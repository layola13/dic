hellenic:/hɛˈlɛnɪk/
adjective
希腊的；希腊文化的
Relating to Greece, its people, or its language.
The Hellenic Association of Cultural Heritage promotes Greek culture.  希腊文化遗产协会致力于推广希腊文化。
The museum houses a remarkable collection of Hellenic art.  这家博物馆收藏了大量珍贵的希腊艺术品。
synonyms: Greek, Grecian
antonyms: Roman, Latin
词性: adjective


hellenic:/hɛˈlɛnɪk/
noun
希腊语
The Greek language. (Often used in a historical or academic context.)
He studied Hellenic literature at university. 他在大学学习希腊文学。
The inscription was written in ancient Hellenic. 这段铭文是用古希腊语写的。
synonyms: Greek
antonyms: (No direct antonym, but could contrast with other languages, e.g., Latin)
词性: noun
