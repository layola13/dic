fearlessly:/ˈfɪrləsli/| adv.|无畏地，勇敢地|Without fear; bravely.  Showing no fear; bold.  In a manner that displays courage and a lack of fear.|She fearlessly spoke out against the injustice. 她无畏地公开反对不公正。|近义词：bravely, courageously, boldly, daringly, intrepidly|反义词: fearfully, timidly, cowardly|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From fear + -less + -ly.  "Fear" comes from Old English "fǣr" (sudden calamity, danger, sudden attack), from Proto-Germanic "*feraz" (danger, fear).  "-less" is a suffix meaning "without," from Old English "-lēas."  "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs from adjectives.
