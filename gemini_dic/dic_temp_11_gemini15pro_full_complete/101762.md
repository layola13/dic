irresponsibly:/ˌɪrɪˈspɒnsɪbli/ (US) /ˌɪrɪˈspɒnsəbli/ (UK)| adv.|不负责任地，轻率地|In a manner that avoids or neglects responsibility; without due care or consideration.|He behaved irresponsibly by leaving his young children alone at home. 他把年幼的孩子独自留在家中，行为很不负责任。|近义词: carelessly, recklessly, negligently|反义词: responsibly, carefully, conscientiously|词性:副词

词源: ir- (not) + responsible + -ly (adverb suffix).  From Latin *responsibilis*, from *respondere* "to respond".
