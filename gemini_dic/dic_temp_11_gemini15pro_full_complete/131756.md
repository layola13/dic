noncongratulatory:/ˌnɒnkənˌɡrætʃʊˈleɪtəri/ | adj. | 非祝贺的，不表示祝贺的 | Not expressing or offering congratulations.  Lacking in congratulatory remarks or sentiments. |  His response to my promotion was surprisingly noncongratulatory.  他对我的晋升的反应令人惊讶地没有表示祝贺。|  He gave a noncongratulatory nod. 他面无表情地点了一下头。 |  unenthusiastic, apathetic, indifferent |  congratulatory, celebratory | adjective
