fading:/ˈfeɪdɪŋ/| adj. | 衰落的，褪色的，逐渐消失的 | losing freshness, strength, or vitality; becoming weaker; disappearing gradually | The photograph is fading with age.  这张照片因年代久远而褪色了。|Synonyms: weakening, declining, diminishing, disappearing, withering | Antonyms: strengthening, intensifying, brightening, flourishing | adjective

fading:/ˈfeɪdɪŋ/| n. | 衰落，褪色 | the process of becoming weaker or less intense | The fading of the summer flowers made her feel melancholic. 夏日花朵的凋零让她感到忧郁。 | Synonyms: decline, decay, deterioration, weakening | Antonyms: growth, strengthening, intensification | noun
