fug:/fʌɡ/
n.| 闷热，浑浊的空气|a warm, stuffy or smoky atmosphere. |The air in the room was thick with fug. 房间里的空气充满了浑浊的烟雾。|近义词:stuffiness, closeness, mustiness, oppressiveness|反义词:freshness, coolness|词性:名词

fug:/fʌɡ/
v.|(使)空气污浊，(使)变得闷热|make (a place) stuffy with fumes or stale air. |The room was fugged up with cigarette smoke.  房间里弥漫着香烟的烟雾，变得很闷。|近义词:pollute, contaminate, stifle|反义词:freshen, ventilate|词性:动词

词源：
