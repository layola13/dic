mucopurulent:/ˌmjuːkoʊˈpjʊərələnt/ | adj. | 粘液脓性的 | Containing or consisting of both mucus and pus. | The patient presented with a mucopurulent discharge from the nose. 病人鼻腔流出粘液脓性分泌物。|  purulent, suppurative |  non-purulent | adjective
Etymology->
From mucos + purulent.  "Mucos" derives from Latin "mucus," meaning "slime," "mucus."  "Purulent" comes from Latin "purulentus," meaning "full of pus," from "pus," meaning "pus."
