osseter:/ˈɒsɪtər/ |n.| 鲟鱼 |A large, primitive, bony fish with five rows of bony plates along its body. It is valued for its caviar and flesh.|  Example: The osseter is a source of highly prized caviar. 鲟鱼是珍贵鱼子酱的来源。| Synonym: sturgeon | Antonym: N/A | Noun


Etymology:
From Middle English *oster, from Old French esturgeon, from Frankish *sturjo-, from Proto-Germanic *sturjōnaz.
