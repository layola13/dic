disaffiliating:/dɪsəˈfɪlɪeɪtɪŋ/
verb
脱离关系，断绝联系
The act of officially ending a connection or association with a larger group or organization.
The church is disaffiliating from the national organization.  教会正在脱离国家组织。
The local chapter is considering disaffiliating from the national association due to disagreements over policy.  由于在政策上的分歧，地方分会正在考虑脱离国家协会。
synonyms:  separating, disconnecting, withdrawing, severing ties, breaking away
antonyms:  affiliating, joining, uniting, associating, merging
verb
