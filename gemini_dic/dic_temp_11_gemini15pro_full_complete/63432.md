entreatance:/ɪnˈtriːtəns/
n.
恳求, 乞求
The act of entreating; earnest request or petition.
He gained admittance by entreatance. 他恳求入内。
近义词：supplication, plea, petition, appeal
反义词：demand, command
词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *entretance, from Old French entretantoe, from entreater (“to treat, negotiate, entertain, receive as a guest, beg, pray”), from en- (“in, into”) + traiter (“to treat”), from Latin tractare (“to handle, manage, discuss”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *tragh- (“to draw, drag, pull”).
