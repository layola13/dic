cochampion:/koʊˈtʃæmpiən/ | n. | 共同冠军，并列冠军 | A person or team that shares a championship with another.  |  Example:  They were cochampions of the league last year. 他们去年是联赛的共同冠军。

Example:  The two teams finished with identical records, making them cochampions.  这两个队以相同的记录结束比赛，使他们成为并列冠军。| 近义词: joint champion, co-winner | 反义词: runner-up, loser | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From co- + champion.  "Co-" is a prefix meaning "jointly," "mutually," or "together." "Champion" comes from the Old French "champion," meaning "fighter, combatant," ultimately from the Late Latin "campio," meaning "fighter in the campus (field)."
