lithotripsy: /lɪˈθɒtrɪpsi/ (UK) /lɪˈθɑːtrɪpsi/ (US)
noun
碎石术，体外震波碎石术
The medical procedure of breaking up stones in the kidney, bladder, or gallbladder using shock waves.
Example:  The patient underwent lithotripsy to remove kidney stones. 病人接受了碎石术来去除肾结石。

Example: Lithotripsy is a less invasive alternative to surgery for removing gallstones. 碎石术是去除胆结石的一种比手术创伤更小的方法。
Synonym:  lithoclasty, extracorporeal shock wave lithotripsy (ESWL)
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but open surgery could be considered a contrasting procedure)
词性: 名词
