ethiopianism: /ˌiːθiˈoʊpiənɪzəm/ | n. | 埃塞俄比亚主义；泛非主义（尤指黑人解放运动）| A political and cultural movement emphasizing the unity and empowerment of people of African descent worldwide, often specifically focused on the liberation of black people from oppression.  It draws inspiration from the history of Ethiopia, one of the few African countries to remain independent during the era of European colonization. |  Marcus Garvey was a prominent advocate of Ethiopianism. 马库斯·加维是埃塞俄比亚主义的杰出倡导者。|  The rise of Ethiopianism coincided with the growth of Pan-Africanism. 埃塞俄比亚主义的兴起与泛非主义的发展同步。 | synonyms: Pan-Africanism, Black Nationalism, Afrocentrism | antonyms: colonialism, imperialism, white supremacy | noun
