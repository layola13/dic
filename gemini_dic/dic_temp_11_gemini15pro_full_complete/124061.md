USAGE->
Simple Present:
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？

What does he do for a living? = 他靠什么谋生？


Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？

What is she cooking for dinner? = 她晚饭在做什么？


Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？

What has he done with the money? = 他用这笔钱做了什么？


Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在做什么？

What has she been reading this week? = 她这周一直在读什么？


Simple Past:
What did you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃了什么？

What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？


Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？

What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间她在想什么？


Past Perfect:
What had you done before you went to bed? = 你睡觉前做了什么？

What had they discussed before the decision was made? = 做出决定之前他们讨论了什么？


Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I arrived? = 我来之前你一直在做什么？

What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 她换专业之前一直在学什么？


Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？

What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？


Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？

What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们下次会议会讨论什么？


Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你会完成什么？

What will have changed by the time we get back? = 等我们回来的时候会有什么变化？


Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？

What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？


Subjunctive Mood:
If I knew what to say, I would tell you. = 如果我知道该说什么，我会告诉你。

I wish I knew what he was thinking. = 我希望我知道他在想什么。


Passive Voice:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？

What is being done about the problem? = 这个问题正在采取什么措施？


Interrogative Sentences:
What time is the flight? = 航班是什么时候？

What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？


Negative Sentences:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。

She didn't say what she wanted. = 她没说她想要什么。
