has got: /həz ˈɡɒt/ (British), /hæz ˈɡɑːt/ (American) | v. | 有 |  Possesses, owns, or is required to do something. | He has got a new car. 他有一辆新车。| 近义词: have, own, possess | 反义词: lack, need | verb


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
She has got a beautiful garden. 她有一个漂亮的花园。
He has got a lot of friends. 他有很多朋友。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
This tense is not typically used with "has got".  "Have got" implies a state of possession, not an ongoing action.  Use "is getting" instead for actions in progress.  He is getting a new car. 他正在买一辆新车。
She is getting her hair cut. 她正在理发。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
He has got used to living in the city. 他已经习惯了住在城市里。 (Note: This is a specific usage related to becoming accustomed to something.)
She has got all the ingredients for the cake. 她已经拿到了所有做蛋糕的配料。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
This tense is not typically used with "has got".

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He had got a new job last year.  去年他得到了一份新工作。
She had got a promotion before she resigned.  在她辞职前，她得到了晋升。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
This tense is not typically used with "had got".

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
He had already got the tickets when I called. 我打电话时他已经拿到了票。
She had got lost before she found the main road. 在找到主路之前，她迷路了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
This tense is not typically used with "had got".

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
He will get a new phone next week.  (More common than "will have got") 他下周会买一部新手机。
They will get married in the spring. (More common than "will have got") 他们将在春天结婚。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
This tense is rarely used with "will have got".

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next week, he will have got his driver's license. 到下周，他将拿到驾照。(More common than using "get" with future perfect)
By next month, she will have got used to her new job. 到下个月，她将适应她的新工作。(More common than using "get" with future perfect)

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
This tense is not typically used with "get".

虚拟语气:
If I had got the job, I would have moved to London. 如果我得到了那份工作，我就搬到伦敦了。
I wish I had got more sleep last night. 我希望我昨晚多睡一会儿。

被动语态:
"Get" can be used in the passive voice, but "have got" cannot.  A new car was gotten. (Unusual, "A new car was obtained" is more natural.)
The work was gotten done.  (Unusual, "The work was completed" is more natural.)

否定句:
