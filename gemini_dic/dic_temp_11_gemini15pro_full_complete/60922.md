注意事项:

* "Eles" is primarily used in Brazilian Portuguese. It's considered informal. The formal equivalent is "eles/elas."
*  While "eles" technically can be used for mixed genders, it's increasingly common to see "eles/elas" or "elu/s" (gender neutral) to be more inclusive.  This entry focuses on the traditional usage.
* There's no direct equivalent phrase usage with "eles" like the examples with "what" provided in the prompt.  The provided phrases are common ways "eles" is used in sentences.
