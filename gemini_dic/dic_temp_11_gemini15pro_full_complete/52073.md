dialoguist:/ˈdaɪəlɔɡɪst/| n. |对话家；对话作者；对话专家|A person skilled in writing dialogue, especially for plays or films. |例句：The renowned dialoguist crafted witty and engaging conversations for the characters.  这位著名的对话家为角色们精心设计了诙谐而引人入胜的对话。

例句：The film's success was largely due to the skilled dialoguist who brought the characters to life. 这部电影的成功很大程度上归功于这位技艺精湛的对话家，他赋予了角色们生命。|近义词：scriptwriter, screenwriter, playwright|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "monologuist")|词性:noun
