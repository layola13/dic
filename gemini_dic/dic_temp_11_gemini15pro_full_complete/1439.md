ace-up-one's-sleeve:/ˌeɪs ʌp wʌnz ˈsliːv/ | idiom | 藏有锦囊妙计; 秘而不宣的优势; 杀手锏 |  Having a secret advantage or resource that can be used when needed.  It refers to a hidden card, particularly an ace, that can ensure victory in a card game.  Figuratively, it represents any undisclosed plan or advantage. | He had an ace up his sleeve going into the negotiations. 他在谈判中藏有锦囊妙计。 | synonyms: secret weapon, hidden advantage, trump card | antonyms: disadvantage, weakness | idiom
