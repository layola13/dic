overimmunize: /ˌoʊvərˈɪmjʊnaɪz/
verb
过度免疫
To immunize excessively or unnecessarily.  Giving too many vaccines or giving them too frequently.
The pediatrician cautioned against overimmunizing children, as it could potentially weaken their immune systems.  儿科医生告诫不要过度免疫儿童，因为这可能会削弱他们的免疫系统。
Overimmunization is a rare but potential risk. 过度免疫是一种罕见但潜在的风险。
近义词: overvaccinate
反义词: underimmunize
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
over- + immunize
"over-"  Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubar "over, above, beyond"
"immunize" from immune + -ize.  "immune" from Latin immunis "exempt, free, not paying a share,"
