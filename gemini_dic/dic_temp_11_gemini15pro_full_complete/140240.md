offloaded:/ˌɒfˈləʊdɪd/ /ˌɔːfˈloʊdɪd/
verb
卸载，卸货，转嫁（负担）
To remove a load from something, especially a vehicle or ship.  To transfer (something, such as a burden or responsibility) to another party.
The ship offloaded its cargo at the port.  这艘船在港口卸货。
He offloaded his work onto his assistant. 他把他的工作转嫁给了他的助手。
近义词: unloaded, discharged, transferred, dumped
反义词: loaded, charged, assumed
词性: verb


offloaded:/ˌɒfˈləʊdɪd/ /ˌɔːfˈloʊdɪd/
adjective
已卸载的
Having had a load removed.
The offloaded truck was now empty and ready for its next assignment.  已卸载的卡车现在空了，准备执行下一个任务。
近义词: unloaded, empty
反义词: loaded, full
词性: adjective
