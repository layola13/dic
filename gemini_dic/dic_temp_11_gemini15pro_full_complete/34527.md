cleanlinesses:/ˈklɛnlɪnɪsɪz/
n.
清洁度；干净整洁（cleanliness 的复数）
The high cleanlinesses of the operating rooms are crucial for patient safety.  手术室的高度清洁对于患者安全至关重要。
The report praised the cleanlinesses of the city's public spaces. 报告赞扬了城市公共场所的清洁度。
近义词: tidiness, neatness, purity
反义词: dirtiness, filth, messiness
词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English clenenesse, from Old English clǣnnes (“cleanness, purity”), equivalent to clean +‎ -ness.
