newcomer:/ˈnjuːkʌmər/ (US), /ˈnjuːkʌmə(r)/ (UK)| n. |新来者，新手|A person who has recently arrived in a place or joined a group.|The newcomers were welcomed to the community with open arms. 新来者受到社区的热烈欢迎。|Synonyms: beginner, novice, initiate, freshman|Antonyms: veteran, old-timer, expert|Noun
