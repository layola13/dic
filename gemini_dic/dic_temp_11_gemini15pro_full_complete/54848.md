displaces:/dɪsˈpleɪsɪz/
verb
取代，移置，迫使…离开家园
forces something or someone out of its usual or original position
The flood displaced thousands of people. 洪水使数千人流离失所。
近义词:replace, substitute, remove, relocate, oust
反义词:place, situate, install, establish
