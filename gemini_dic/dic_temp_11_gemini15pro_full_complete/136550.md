nonsacerdotal:/ˌnɒnsækərˈdoʊtl/
adjective
非神职的，世俗的
Not relating to priests or the priesthood; secular.
The nonsacerdotal duties of a minister might include visiting the sick or organizing community events.  一位牧师的非神职职责可能包括探望病人或组织社区活动。
The king held both sacerdotal and nonsacerdotal power. 国王同时拥有神职和非神职的权力。
synonyms:lay, secular, temporal
antonyms:sacerdotal, priestly, clerical
词性: adjective
