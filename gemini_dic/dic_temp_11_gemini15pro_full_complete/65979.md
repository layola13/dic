exalbuminous:/ˌɛksælˈbjuːmɪnəs/
adjective
无胚乳的
Lacking a nutritive tissue called endosperm around the embryo in the seed.  The food reserves are stored in the cotyledons (seed leaves).
Orchids produce exalbuminous seeds. | 兰花产生无胚乳的种子。
Synonyms:  non-endospermic,  cotyledonous
Antonyms: albuminous, endospermic
adjective
