forgathered:/fɔːrˈɡæðərd/
verb
聚集，集合
assembled or met together, especially by chance.
We forgathered at the local pub. 我们在当地酒吧聚会。
The old friends forgathered for a reunion. 老朋友们聚在一起叙旧。
Synonyms: assemble, congregate, gather, meet
Antonyms: disperse, scatter
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From for- + gather.  "For-" in this context is an archaic intensifier.
