annunciable:/əˈnaʊnsəbl/
adjective
可公布的；可宣布的
capable of being announced; fit to be announced.
The engagement is now annunciable.  订婚现在可以公布了。
近义词:declarable, pronounceable, publishable
反义词:unannunciable, secret, confidential
词性:adjective

词源: From Late Latin annuntiabilis, from Latin annuntiare (“to announce”).  源自晚期拉丁语 annuntiabilis，来自拉丁语 annuntiare（“宣布”）。
