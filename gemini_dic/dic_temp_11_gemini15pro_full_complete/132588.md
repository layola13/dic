不能消化;难消化

Dietary fiber contributes to good health due to its nondigestibleness. 膳食纤维由于其不可消化性而有助于身体健康。
The nondigestibleness of some mushroom varieties can cause stomach upset.  某些蘑菇品种的不可消化性会导致胃部不适。
