half-consummated:/ˌhæf kənˈsʌmɪteɪtɪd/
| adj. |
| 半完成的；未完全实现的 |
| Partially completed or not fully realized, especially referring to a marriage or sexual act. It describes something that has begun but has not been finished or fully achieved. |
| The marriage was half-consummated due to unforeseen circumstances.  由于不可预见的情况，这桩婚姻只是半完成的。

| Their plans for a weekend getaway were half-consummated, ruined by a sudden storm.  他们周末度假的计划只完成了一半，被突如其来的暴风雨破坏了。 |
| incomplete, unfinished, partial, unfulfilled |
| complete, finished, full, fulfilled, consummated |
| adjective |
