nongrieving:/nɒnˈɡriːvɪŋ/
adj.
没有悲伤的，不悲伤的
Not experiencing or expressing grief.
She appeared remarkably nongrieving after the loss of her pet.  她失去宠物后看起来非常平静，没有悲伤。
He maintained a nongrieving facade despite his inner turmoil. 尽管内心很乱，他还是保持着不悲伤的表情。
近义词: unemotional, impassive, stoic
反义词: grieving, sorrowful, mournful
词性: 形容词
