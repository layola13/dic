flashcard:/ˈflæʃˌkɑːrd/ | n. | 抽认卡 | A card bearing words, numbers, or pictures that is briefly displayed as an aid to memorization. |  I use flashcards to learn new vocabulary. 我用抽认卡学习新词汇。| synonyms: study card, index card, note card | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "textbook" as a broader learning method) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "flash" (a quick or brief display) + "card"
