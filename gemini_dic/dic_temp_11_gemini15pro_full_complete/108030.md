lastings:/ˈlɑːstɪŋz/
n.
持久物; 耐用品
things that are made to last a long time.
These shoes are made of good quality leather, they're real lastings.  这些鞋子是用优质皮革制成的，它们真的很耐穿。
近义词: durables, perennials
反义词: ephemerals, disposables
词性: 名词

词源:  From the verb "to last" (meaning to endure or continue for a long time),  with the suffix "-ings" forming a plural noun referring to things that possess this quality of lasting.  "Last" comes from Old English "læstan".
