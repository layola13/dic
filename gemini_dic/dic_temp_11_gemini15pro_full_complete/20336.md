bisk:/bɪsk/ | n. |浓汤，浓菜汤 | A thick, rich soup, typically containing cream or pureed vegetables. |  She made a delicious bisk for dinner. 她晚餐做了一道美味的浓汤。| synonyms: soup, potage, chowder | antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "thin broth") | noun

Etymology->
From French *bisque*, related to *bis*, "twice cooked" (referring to the shellfish being first sautéed, then simmered in wine and aromatics).
