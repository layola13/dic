egalitarian: /ɪˌɡælɪˈtɛəriən/ | adj. n. | 平等主义的；平等主义者 | Believing in the principle that all people are equal and deserve equal rights and opportunities. |  例句：She has always held egalitarian views. 她一直持有平等主义的观点。| 近义词：democratic, equitable, just, fair | 反义词：elitist, hierarchical, discriminatory | 词性: adjective, noun

egalitarian: /ɪˌɡælɪˈtɛəriən/ | n. | 平等主义者 | A person who advocates or supports egalitarian principles. | 例句：He considers himself an egalitarian. 他自认为是一个平等主义者。| 近义词：leveler | 反义词：elitist | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *égalitaire*, from *égal* (“equal”), from Latin *aequalis*.
