misstart:/ˌmɪsˈstɑːrt/
noun|错误的开始，不顺利的开端|A bad or unsuccessful beginning to something.|The team's misstart to the season put them in a difficult position.|近义词：false start, bad start, stumble, setback|反义词:good start, strong start|词性:n.
verb|错误地开始，不顺利地开始|To begin something badly or unsuccessfully.|He misstarted the engine several times before it finally caught.|近义词：flub, bungle, botch|反义词:start well, begin successfully|词性:v.
