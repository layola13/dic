musquashes:/ˈmʌskræʃɪz/ | n. |麝鼠（复数）|A large semiaquatic North American rodent (Ondatra zibethicus) with a musky odor, valued for its fur.  It is the only extant species in the genus Ondatra and tribe Ondatrini. |Musquashes build elaborate lodges in marshes. 麝鼠在沼泽地建造精致的巢穴。|
