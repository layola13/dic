biogenetic:/ˌbaɪoʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk/ | adj. | 生物发生的，生物起源的 | Relating to biogenesis, the theory that living organisms arise only from other living organisms. |  The biogenetic law states that ontogeny recapitulates phylogeny. 生物发生律指出个体发育重演系统发育。|  Biogenetic processes are fundamental to understanding life. 生物发生过程对于理解生命至关重要。| 近义词:  biogenic | 反义词: abiogenetic | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From bio- (life) + genetic (relating to origin or genes).  Ultimately from Greek bios (life) and genesis (origin).
