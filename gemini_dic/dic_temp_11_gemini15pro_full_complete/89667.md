hooper:/ˈhuːpər/| n.|箍匠，制桶工人；从事篮球运动的人|A person who makes or repairs hoops, or a person who plays basketball. |The old hooper still remembered how to craft a barrel.  那位老箍匠仍然记得如何制作桶。

The hooper showed off his impressive skills on the court. 这位篮球运动员在球场上展示了他令人印象深刻的技巧。|近义词：barrel maker, cooper (for barrel making); basketball player (for basketball) |反义词: None|noun
