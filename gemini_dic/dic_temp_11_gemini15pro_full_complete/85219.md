harkness:/ˈhɑːrknɪs/| n.|哈克尼斯教学法；圆桌讨论；|A teaching method involving students seated in a circle, facilitating discussion and collaborative learning.  A Harkness table is a large, oval table used in this method.|The students thrived in the Harkness classroom, actively engaging in discussions. 学生们在哈克尼斯课堂上茁壮成长，积极参与讨论。 The Harkness method fosters critical thinking and collaboration. 哈克尼斯教学法培养批判性思维和协作能力。|近义词：discussion, seminar, roundtable|反义词:lecture, monologue|noun
