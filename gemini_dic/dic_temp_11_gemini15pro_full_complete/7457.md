anisophyllous:/ˌænˌaɪsoʊˈfɪləs/
adj.
不等叶的；异形叶的
Having leaves of two or more sizes and shapes on the same plant.
例句：This anisophyllous plant exhibits a striking difference in leaf morphology.  这种不等叶植物的叶子形态差异显著。

例句：Anisophyllous foliage can be an adaptation to varying light conditions. 不等叶的叶子可以适应不同的光照条件。
近义词：heterophyllous
反义词：isophyllous
词性：adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ánisos* (unequal) + *phýllon* (leaf).
