blowhard:/ˈbloʊhɑːrd/| n.|吹牛大王，自夸者|A person who boasts loudly and excessively about their own achievements or qualities. |He's a real blowhard, always bragging about his supposed accomplishments. 他是个十足的吹牛大王，总是吹嘘他所谓的成就。|He was known as a blowhard, constantly exaggerating his own importance. 他以爱吹牛而闻名，总是夸大自己的重要性。|近义词：braggart, boaster, show-off|反义词：humble person, modest person|词性: 名词

词源:
From blow + hard.  Originally (1890s) referred to a whale expelling air from its blowhole.  The figurative sense of a person who expels a lot of “hot air” (empty talk) developed soon after.
