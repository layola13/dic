goose:/ɡuːs/ /ɡus/| n.|鹅|A large waterbird with a long neck, short legs, webbed feet, and a short broad bill.  A female goose is sometimes called a dame.|例句：The goose hissed at the dog.  鹅对着狗嘶嘶叫。
