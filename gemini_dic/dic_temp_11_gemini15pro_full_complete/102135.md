isps:/ˈaɪɛsˈpiːz/| n. |互联网服务提供商 (Internet Service Providers)|Companies that provide internet access to individuals and organizations. They offer various services like broadband, dial-up, and fiber optic connections.|Example: Many ISPs offer bundled packages that include internet, phone, and TV services. 许多互联网服务提供商提供包含互联网、电话和电视服务的捆绑套餐。

Example:  Choosing the right ISP can significantly impact your internet speed and reliability. 选择合适的互联网服务提供商会显著影响你的网速和可靠性。|近义词: internet providers, internet service companies|反义词: None|词性:noun (plural)
