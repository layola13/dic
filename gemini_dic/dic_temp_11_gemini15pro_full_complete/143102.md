outbrings:/ˈaʊtbrɪŋz/ | v. | 产生，带来 |  Produces or brings as a result. |  The new policy outbrings significant changes to the education system. 新政策给教育系统带来了重大变化。| 近义词：produces, yields, generates, brings forth, results in | 反义词：suppresses, inhibits, prevents | 词性：动词

词源:  "out-" (out) + "bring" (bring).  "Out-" acts as an intensifier, suggesting a complete or thorough bringing forth.
