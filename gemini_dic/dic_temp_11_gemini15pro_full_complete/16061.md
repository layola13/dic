bandinesses:/ˈbændɪnɪsɪz/
noun
弯曲度，曲折度
The quality or state of being bandy; crookedness or curvature, especially of the legs.
His bandinesses made it difficult for him to walk straight. 他的双腿弯曲使他难以直行。
