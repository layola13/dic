expounders:/ɪkˈspaʊndərz/
| n. |  阐释者；讲解者；详细说明者 | People who explain or interpret something in detail; those who provide a comprehensive explanation. | The expounders of the new theory presented their findings at the conference.  这些新理论的阐释者在会议上展示了他们的发现。| Synonyms: interpreters, explainers, commentators, elucidators | Antonyms: obfuscators, confounders | Noun

expound:/ɪkˈspaʊnd/
| v. |  阐述；详细解释；说明 | To explain or interpret something in detail; to provide a comprehensive explanation. | The professor expounded on the complexities of quantum physics. 教授详细阐述了量子物理学的复杂性。| Synonyms: explain, interpret, elucidate, clarify | Antonyms: obfuscate, confuse, obscure | Verb
