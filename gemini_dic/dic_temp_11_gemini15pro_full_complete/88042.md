"highe" 不是一个标准的现代英语单词。它看起来像中古英语或早期现代英语中的单词，拼写与现代英语 "high" 相似。因此，我将以 "high" 为基础进行解析。

high: /haɪ/
| adj. adv. n.|高的，高度的；高级的；高音的；(价格)高的；(程度)大的|High above the city, a lone eagle soared. 在城市高空，一只孤鹰翱翔。|近义词：tall, elevated, lofty, expensive|反义词:low, short, inexpensive|词性:形容词，副词，名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *hih*, *heih*, from Old English *hēah*, from Proto-West Germanic *hauχ*, from Proto-Germanic *hauhaz* (“high”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)ḱewh₂- (“to shine,” “to swell”). Cognate with West Frisian *heech*, Dutch *hoog*, German *hoch*, Danish *høj*, Swedish *hög*, Icelandic *hár*.
