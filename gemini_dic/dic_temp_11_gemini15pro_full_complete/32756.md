Simple Present:
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What does he do for a living? = 他做什么工作？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? = 她正在做什么晚餐？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned so far? = 到目前为止你学到了什么？
What has he done to deserve this? = 他做了什么值得拥有这个？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has she been reading lately? = 她最近一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? = 我看到她的时候她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you eaten before you got sick? = 你生病前吃了什么？
What had happened before the police arrived? = 警察到达之前发生了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 在她换专业之前她一直在学什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting tomorrow? = 他们明天会在会议上讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到今年年底你将取得什么成就？
What will have changed by the time we get back? = 等我们回来的时候会有什么变化？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业的时候她将已经学习什么多久了？
