nonagon:/ˈnɒnəɡɒn/ (US) /ˈnɑːnəɡɑːn/| n.|九边形；九角形|A polygon with nine sides and nine angles. |A nonagon has nine sides.  一个九边形有九条边。

The interior angles of a regular nonagon add up to 1260 degrees.  一个正九边形的内角和为1260度。|近义词:enneagon|反义词: (No direct opposite, but could consider shapes with fewer or more sides, like triangle or decagon)|词性:noun
