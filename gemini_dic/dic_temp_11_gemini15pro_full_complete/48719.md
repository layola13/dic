defies:/dɪˈfaɪz/
verb
反抗；违抗；使不可能；无法描述；经受住；
resists or challenges (an authority, convention, or rule); is impossible to explain or understand; successfully resists or withstands.
His strength defies explanation. 他的力气大得无法解释。
The beauty of the scenery defies description.  这景色的美丽无法用语言形容。
近义词:resists, challenges, opposes, disobeys, confronts
反义词:obeys, complies, submits, surrenders, yields
词性:v.

defies:/dɪˈfaɪz/
verb
(third person singular simple present defies, present participle defying, simple past defied, past participle defied)
反抗；违抗；使不可能；无法描述；经受住；
resists or challenges (an authority, convention, or rule); is impossible to explain or understand; successfully resists or withstands.
The climber defied death and reached the summit. 这位登山者战胜了死亡，到达了顶峰。
This problem defies all logic. 这个问题完全不合逻辑。
近义词:resists, challenges, opposes, disobeys, confronts
反义词:obeys, complies, submits, surrenders, yields
词性:v.

词源：From Old French *desafiier, from des- (expressing reversal) + afiier (“to trust, confide in”), from Latin adfidare (“to pledge faith to”), from ad- (“to”) + fidus (“faithful”).
