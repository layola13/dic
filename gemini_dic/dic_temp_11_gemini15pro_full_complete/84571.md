hammier:/ˈhæmiər/ | adj. | 更做作的，更矫揉造作的 | More affected or overly dramatic in behavior or speech.  A comparative form of "hammy." |  His acting was even hammier than usual. 他的表演比平常更加做作。 | 近义词：more theatrical, more affected, more dramatic, more exaggerated | 反义词：more natural, more understated, more subtle | 词性：形容词

词源:
From "ham" (overactor) + "-ier" (comparative suffix). "Ham," originally referring to a poor actor, is thought to derive from a minstrel song called "The Ham-Fat Man" in the 19th century.  The greasy makeup used by these performers was likened to ham fat.
