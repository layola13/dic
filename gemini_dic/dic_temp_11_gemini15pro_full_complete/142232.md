orogenically:/ˌɔːrəˈdʒɛnɪkli/
adjective
造山地，由造山运动形成的
Relating to orogeny; resulting from mountain-building processes.
The Himalayas were orogenically formed by the collision of the Indian and Eurasian plates. 喜马拉雅山脉是由印度板块和欧亚板块碰撞而形成的。
The region’s complex geology is a result of orogenically driven uplift and erosion. 该地区复杂的地质是由造山运动驱动的隆起和侵蚀造成的。
近义词: tectonically
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider terms like "erosionally" or "denudationally" for contrasting processes)
词性: adjective
