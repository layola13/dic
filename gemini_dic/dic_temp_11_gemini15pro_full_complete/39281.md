congruency:/kənˈɡruːənsi/| n.|一致性，全等，适合|The congruency of the two triangles was easily proven. 这两个三角形的全等很容易证明。|Synonyms: agreement, conformity, correspondence, harmony, consistency|Antonyms: disagreement, incongruity, disharmony, inconsistency|noun

congruent:/ˈkɑːŋɡruːənt/| adj.|一致的，全等的，适合的|Her actions are congruent with her values. 她的行为与她的价值观一致。|Synonyms: agreeing, conforming, corresponding, harmonious, consistent|Antonyms: disagreeing, incongruous, disharmonious, inconsistent|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *congruentia, from Latin congruēns, present participle of congruere "to come together, agree," from com- "together" + -gruere, related to ruere "to rush."
