installs:/ɪnˈstɔːlz/
verb
安装，设置
The technician installs the new software on the computer.  技术人员在电脑上安装新软件。
They are installing new security cameras around the building. 他们正在大楼周围安装新的安全摄像头。
Synonym: sets up, implements, puts in
Antonym: uninstalls, removes
词性: verb
