dhurna:/ˈdʊrnɑː/ (US), /ˈdɜːrnə/ (UK)
|n.|静坐示威，静坐抗议|A form of non-violent protest in which people sit and refuse to move until their demands are met. It originates from India.|
Example: The workers staged a dhurna outside the factory gates. 工人们在工厂门口举行静坐示威。

Example:  The farmers' dhurna lasted for several days, disrupting traffic. 农民们的静坐抗议持续了几天，扰乱了交通。
|Synonym: sit-in, protest, demonstration|
|Antonym: cooperation, compliance|
|Noun|
