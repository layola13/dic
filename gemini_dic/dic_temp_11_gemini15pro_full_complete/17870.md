beefwood:/ˈbiːfwʊd/| n.|牛肉木，澳洲木麻黄|A type of tree native to Australia, known for its reddish wood resembling beef in color and its tough, durable properties.  It's scientifically known as *Casuarina equisetifolia*.|The beefwood tree is highly resistant to salt spray, making it ideal for coastal planting. 牛肉木非常耐盐雾，很适合在沿海种植。

The beefwood timber is popular for making fence posts and flooring. 牛肉木木材常用于制作栅栏柱和地板。|近义词：ironwood, sheoak|反义词:|n.
