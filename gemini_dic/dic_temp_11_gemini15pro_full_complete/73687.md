forgivable:/fərˈɡɪvəbl/| adj.|可原谅的，可宽恕的| readily excused or forgiven; pardonable | Example: His mistake was forgivable under the circumstances.  在那种情况下，他的错误是可以原谅的。| Example: It's a forgivable offense, considering his age. 考虑到他的年龄，这是一个可以原谅的过错。 |Synonyms: excusable, pardonable, venial| Antonyms: unforgivable, inexcusable, unpardonable |词性:形容词

词源:  From "forgive" + "-able".  "Forgive" comes from Old English "forgiefan," meaning "to give up, remit, grant," from "for-" (meaning "away, off") + "giefan" (meaning "to give"). "-able" is a suffix meaning "capable of being."
