outworded: /ˌaʊtˈwɜːrdɪd/
verb
胜过言语；使难以表达
surpassed in eloquence or effectiveness of expression; left speechless
He was completely outworded by her eloquent speech. 他被她雄辩的演讲说得哑口无言。
synonyms: silenced, speechless, dumbfounded, at a loss for words
antonyms: eloquent, articulate, expressive

Etymology:  "out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding," "surpassing") + "word" (verb meaning "to express in words")
