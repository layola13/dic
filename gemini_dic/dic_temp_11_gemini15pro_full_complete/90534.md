huffily:/ˈhʌfɪli/| adv.|生气地，傲慢地|In a way that shows you are annoyed or offended.|She turned and walked huffily out of the room. 她生气地转身走出了房间。|例句：He huffily rejected their offer. 他怒气冲冲地拒绝了他们的提议。|近义词：angrily, indignantly, sullenly, resentfully|反义词：cheerfully, pleasantly, happily|词性:副词

词源:
From huff (n.) + -ily (adverbial suffix).  The noun "huff" originates from imitative origin, suggesting the sound of forced exhalation expressing annoyance.
