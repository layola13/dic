misuses:/ˌmɪsˈjuːsɪz/ | n. | 误用；滥用 | The report documented numerous misuses of public funds.  该报告记录了对公共资金的大量滥用。| Examples of misuse include taking too much of a medication or using it for the wrong purpose. 误用的例子包括服用过量的药物或将其用于错误的目的。 | 近义词: abuses, misapplications | 反义词: proper uses, correct applications | noun
