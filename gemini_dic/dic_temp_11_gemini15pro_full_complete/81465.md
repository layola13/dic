gratuity:/ɡrəˈtuːɪti/ /ɡrəˈtjuːɪti/| n.|小费，赏钱，赠金|A gratuity is a tip given to someone for providing a service.|The waiter received a generous gratuity. 服务员收到了一笔丰厚的小费。|
The hotel added a 10% gratuity to the bill. 酒店在账单上加了10%的小费。|
Synonyms: tip, gift, bonus, reward|
Antonyms: deduction, penalty|
