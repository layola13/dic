bicyclism: /ˈbaɪsɪklɪzəm/
n.
骑自行车运动;自行车主义;自行车骑行
the use of bicycles for transportation, sport, or recreation.
Bicyclism is a popular form of exercise. 骑自行车是一项很流行的运动方式。
The growth of bicyclism has led to an increase in bike lanes. 骑自行车运动的发展导致自行车道的增加。
近义词：cycling, biking
反义词：driving, walking
词性：名词

词源:
From bicycle + -ism.  "Bicycle" itself comes from the French "bicyclette," a combination of "bi-" (two) and "cycle" (wheel, circle).  "-ism" is a suffix forming nouns signifying action, practice, or doctrine.
