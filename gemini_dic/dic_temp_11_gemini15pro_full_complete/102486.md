jacky:/ˈdʒæki/| n.|杰基（人名）;  морячок (俄语，水手，海员)|A diminutive of Jack, often used as a given name.  It can also be a slang term for "sailor" (from Russian "морячок").|例句：Jacky is a common nickname. 杰基是个常见的昵称。

例句：He called the sailor "Jacky". 他称呼那个水手为“杰基”。|近义词：Jack, Johnny (as diminutives)|反义词: N/A|词性:Noun


jacky:/ˈdʒæki/| adj.|俗气的；花哨的；低劣的|Showy, gaudy, or cheap in nature, often implying poor quality or taste.  Primarily used in British English.|例句：She wore a jacky-looking dress. 她穿了一件俗气的裙子。

例句：The decorations were a bit jacky.  这些装饰有点低劣。|近义词：tacky, gaudy, cheap, flashy|反义词: elegant, sophisticated, refined|词性:Adjective
