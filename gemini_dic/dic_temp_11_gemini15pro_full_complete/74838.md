frenzies:/ˈfrɛnziːz/
n.
疯狂；狂暴；狂热
A state of wild excitement or uncontrolled activity.
The shoppers worked themselves into a frenzy trying to grab the limited-edition items.
购物者为了抢购限量版商品而陷入疯狂。
The fans were in a frenzy after their team won the championship.
球队赢得冠军后，球迷们欣喜若狂。
synonyms:  madness, hysteria, delirium, wildness, turmoil
antonyms: calm, peace, tranquility, composure
noun

frenzy:/ˈfrɛnziː/
n.
疯狂；狂暴；狂热
A state of wild excitement or uncontrolled activity.
He was in a frenzy of activity as he prepared for the upcoming deadline.
他为即将到来的截止日期做准备而忙得不可开交。
In a buying frenzy, she maxed out her credit card.
在疯狂购物中，她刷爆了信用卡。
synonyms:  madness, hysteria, delirium, wildness, turmoil
antonyms: calm, peace, tranquility, composure
noun
