noun
精细的网状结构; 花边状饰物;  蕾丝状物品（lacy的名词复数）
The delicate lacinesses of the spider's web glistened in the morning dew.  蜘蛛网精致的蕾丝状结构在晨露中闪闪发光。
The dress was adorned with intricate lacinesses. 这件衣服装饰着复杂的蕾丝。
Synonyms: intricacies, filigrees, delicacies, openwork, tracery
Antonyms: solidity, denseness, coarseness


Etymology:
From lacy + -ness + -es.  Lacy itself comes from lace (from Old French las, "noose, snare," likely of Germanic origin) + -y (suffix forming adjectives).
