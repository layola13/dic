daffily: /ˈdæfɪli/ /ˈdæfəli/ | adv. | 傻傻地，愚蠢地 | In a lighthearted, silly, or foolish manner. Characterized by carefree silliness or foolishness. | She danced daffily around the room, singing at the top of her lungs. 她傻乎乎地在房间里跳舞，放声高歌。

He daffily wore mismatched socks to the important meeting.  他傻乎乎地穿着不配套的袜子去参加重要的会议。

| 近义词：foolishly, giddily, ridiculously, absurdly, sillily | 反义词：sensibly, seriously, rationally | 词性：副词


ETYMOLOGY->

From *daffy* + *-ly*. *Daffy* is of uncertain origin, possibly a variant of *daft*.  Ultimately from Old English *gedæfte* meaning "mild, meek, humble".  The suffix *-ly* is used to form adverbs.
