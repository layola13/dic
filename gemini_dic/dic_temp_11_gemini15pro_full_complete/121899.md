misscript:/ˈmɪsˌskrɪpt/
noun
错误的笔迹; 写错的字; 抄写错误
an error in writing, especially in copying something
The monk apologized for the misscript in the sacred text.  僧侣为圣典中的抄写错误道歉。
近义词:miswriting, error, slip of the pen
反义词:correct script
