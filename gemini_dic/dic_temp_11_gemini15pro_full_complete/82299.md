grouplet: /ˈɡruːplɪt/ | n. | 小组，小团体 | A small group; a cluster.  |  Example:  A grouplet of students gathered to discuss the assignment. 一小群学生聚集在一起讨论作业。

Example: Several grouplets of tourists explored the museum. 几小群游客在博物馆里参观。


| 近义词: group, cluster, clique, band | 反义词: individual | 词性: 名词 |
