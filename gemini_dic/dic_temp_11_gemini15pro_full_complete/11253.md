archs:/ɑːrtʃɪz/ | n. | 拱门，拱形结构 |A curved structure that spans an opening and supports the weight above it.  A series of arches. | The Roman aqueduct was supported by a series of arches.  罗马渡槽由一系列拱门支撑。| synonyms: vaults, spans, curves | antonyms: beams, lintels | noun

archs:/ɑːrtʃɪz/ | v. | 拱起，成弓形 | Form or build (something) into an arch. To curve or bend into the shape of an arch. | The cat arched its back when it saw the dog. 猫看到狗时拱起了背。| synonyms: curve, bend, arc | antonyms: straighten, flatten | verb
