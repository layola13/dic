Kamtschadales: /kæmtʃəˈdɑːlz/ | n. |堪察加人|A member of an indigenous people of the Kamchatka Peninsula, Russia. |The Kamtschadales traditionally relied on fishing and hunting for their livelihood. 堪察加人传统上依靠捕鱼和狩猎为生。| Kamchadals| None | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Russian Камчадалы (Kamchadaly), from Kamchadal, the name of a Koryak man encountered by Russian explorers, ultimately probably of Even origin.
