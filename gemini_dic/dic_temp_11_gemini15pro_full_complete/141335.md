PHRASE->
open up:  畅所欲言；打开
The therapist encouraged her to open up about her feelings.  治疗师鼓励她畅所欲言。

open to:  愿意接受；对…开放
I'm open to suggestions. 我愿意接受建议。

open fire:  开火；开枪
The soldiers opened fire on the enemy. 士兵们向敌人开火。

open house:  开放参观日
The school is having an open house next weekend. 学校下周末有开放参观日。

open the door to:  为…创造机会
This new technology opens the door to many exciting possibilities. 这项新技术为许多令人兴奋的可能性创造了机会。
