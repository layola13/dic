loathest:/ˈloʊðɪst/| adj.|最讨厌的；最不喜欢的|Feeling intense dislike or disgust for something or someone.  It is the superlative form of "loathe".|He is the person I loathe the most in the world. 他是我在这个世界上最讨厌的人。|近义词：detest, abhor, despise|反义词：adore, love, cherish|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *lothest, superlative of lothen (“to loathe”), from Old English lāð (“hateful, hostile, loathsome; evil, wicked; unpleasant, painful”), from Proto-Germanic *laiþaz (“hateful, loathsome”). Cognate with Scots laid (“loath, reluctant”), West Frisian leed (“ugly, nasty”), Dutch leed (“sorrow, grief”), German Leid (“sorrow, grief, suffering”).
