courtship:/ˈkɔːrtʃɪp/ (US), /ˈkɔːtʃɪp/ (UK)| n.|求爱，追求；求偶|The period of time during which a couple develop a romantic relationship before getting married.  The process of trying to win the love of someone with the intention of marrying them. |例句：The peacock's elaborate courtship display is designed to attract a mate.  孔雀精心设计的求偶炫耀是为了吸引配偶。

例句：Their courtship was brief, and they were married within a few months. 他们的求爱很短暂，几个月内就结婚了。|近义词：wooing, romance, courting, pursuit|反义词: rejection, estrangement|词性:noun
