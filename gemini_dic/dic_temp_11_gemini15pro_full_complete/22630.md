bopping:/ˈbɒpɪŋ/ /ˈbɑːpɪŋ/| adj.  vi. | 跳跃的，有节奏地跳动的；轻快地跳舞 |  Moving rhythmically to music; dancing in a lively way |  They were bopping to the music all night. 他们整晚都在随着音乐跳动。|近义词：dancing, jiving, grooving, bouncing|反义词：still, motionless|词性:形容词，动词

bopping:/ˈbɒpɪŋ/ /ˈbɑːpɪŋ/| n. | (尤指轻快的) 舞蹈 |  A lively dance | The bopping was infectious and soon everyone joined in.  这种轻快的舞蹈很有感染力，很快大家都加入进来了。|近义词：dance, jig, jive|反义词：-|词性:名词


词源:  "Bop" originally referred to a type of jazz music in the 1940s and 1950s, and "bopping" emerged as a term to describe the style of dancing associated with this music.
