fulminator:/ˈfʊlmɪneɪtər/| n. |严厉谴责者；激烈批评者|A person who criticizes harshly and vehemently.|He was known as a fulminator against injustice. 他以猛烈抨击不公正而闻名。|Synonyms: critic, denouncer, attacker|Antonyms: supporter, defender|noun
