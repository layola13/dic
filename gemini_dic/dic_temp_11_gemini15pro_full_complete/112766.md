lubbe:/ˈlʌbə/ | n. | 笨蛋，傻瓜 | A clumsy or stupid person. |  He's such a lubbe, he tripped over his own feet. 他真是个笨蛋，居然被自己的脚绊倒了。| Synonym: oaf, klutz, dolt | Antonym: genius, mastermind | Noun

lubbe:/ˈlʌbə/ | n. | 乡巴佬 |  A rustic, unsophisticated person. |  He acted like a real lubbe when he visited the city.  他到城里的时候表现得像个十足的乡巴佬。 | Synonym: yokel, bumpkin | Antonym: sophisticate, cosmopolitan | Noun
