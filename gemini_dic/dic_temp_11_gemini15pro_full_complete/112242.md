loot:/luːt/| n. v. |战利品，赃物；掠夺|Goods, especially private property, taken from an enemy in war or by robbers. |The soldiers looted the city after it fell. 士兵们在城市沦陷后洗劫了这座城市。|近义词：spoils, plunder, booty, pillage|反义词: restitution|n.
loot:/luːt/| v. |掠夺，抢劫|To rob a place or person, especially during a war or riot. |Rioters looted shops and set fire to cars. 暴徒洗劫了商店并放火焚烧汽车。|近义词：ransack, plunder, pillage|反义词: protect, defend|v.
