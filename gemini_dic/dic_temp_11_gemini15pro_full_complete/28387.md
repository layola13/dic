carbohydrate:/ˌkɑːrboʊˈhaɪdreɪt/ /ˌkɑːbəʊˈhaɪdreɪt/| n. |碳水化合物；糖类|A carbohydrate is an organic compound occurring in foods and living tissues and including sugars, starch, and cellulose. They contain hydrogen and oxygen in the same ratio as water (2:1) and typically can be broken down to release energy in the animal body.|
Rice is a good source of carbohydrates. 米饭是碳水化合物的一个很好的来源。

Carbohydrates are essential for providing energy. 碳水化合物对提供能量至关重要。


近义词:saccharide, sugar|反义词: protein, fat|词性:noun
