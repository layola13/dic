lob:/lɑːb/ (US) /lɒb/ (UK)| n. v.|高抛球，吊高球；笨拙地扔；缓慢地移动|A shot in tennis or other racket sports that is hit high and slow, typically over the head of an opponent who is close to the net. To throw something slowly and with a high arc.|
He lobbed the ball over the net. 他把球高高地抛过网。
She lobbed a grenade over the wall. 她把手榴弹从墙上扔了过去。

近义词：toss, throw, fling, pitch, chuck (informal)|
反义词：smash, volley (in racket sports)|
词性：noun, verb|


lob:/lɑːb/ (US) /lɒb/ (UK)| v.|游说|To try to influence a politician or the government to support a particular law or plan.|
They are lobbying for stricter gun control laws. 他们在游说更严格的枪支管制法。
The company is lobbying Congress to reduce taxes. 这家公司正在游说国会减税。

近义词：persuade, influence, sway|
反义词：-|
词性：verb|
