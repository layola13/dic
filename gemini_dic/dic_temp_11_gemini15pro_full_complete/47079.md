dats:/dæts/ |pron. (informal) |that's的非正式写法，意思是“那是”|It is a contraction of "that is".  It's very informal and often used in spoken language or casual writing.|Dats a good idea. 那是个好主意。|近义词:that's, that is|反义词: N/A |contraction


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Dats my book. = 那(是)我的书。
Dats how it works. = 那就是它的工作原理。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Dats happening right now. = 那就是现在正在发生的事情。
Dats what she's doing. = 那就是她正在做的事情。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Dats what I've been saying. = 那就是我一直以来在说的。
Dats how it has always been. = 那就是它一直以来的样子。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
Dats what I've been working on. = 那就是我一直以来在做的事情。
Dats what they've been talking about. = 那就是他们一直在谈论的事情。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
Dats what he said. = 那就是他说的。
Dats how it happened. = 那就是事情发生的经过。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
Dats what I was thinking. = 那就是我当时在想的。
Dats what they were doing. = 那就是他们当时在做的。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
Dats what had happened. = 那就是之前发生的事情。
Dats what we had discussed. = 那就是我们之前讨论过的事情。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
Dats what I had been doing. = 那就是我之前一直在做的事情。
Dats what they had been planning. = 那就是他们之前一直在计划的事情。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
Dats what I'll do. = 那就是我将要做的事情。
Dats what will happen. = 那就是将要发生的事情。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
Dats what I'll be doing tomorrow. = 那就是我明天将要做的事情。
Dats what they'll be discussing. = 那就是他们将要讨论的事情。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
Dats what I'll have done by then. = 那就是我到那时将已经完成的事情。
Dats what will have changed. = 那就是到那时将已经改变的事情。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
Dats what I'll have been working on. = 那就是我到那时将已经一直在做的事情。
Dats what they'll have been studying. = 那就是他们到那时将已经一直在学习的事情。


PHRASE->
Dats what I thought. = 我也是这么想的。
Dats all. = 就这样。
Dats it! = 就是这样!/对了!
Dats right. = 对的。
Dats OK. = 没关系。

注意事项:  "dats" is extremely informal and should be avoided in formal writing or speech.  Using it in formal contexts can be perceived as uneducated or unprofessional.  Stick to "that's" or "that is" in these situations.
