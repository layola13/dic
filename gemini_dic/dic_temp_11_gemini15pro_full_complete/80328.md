goldennesses:/ˈɡoʊldənɪsnɪsɪz/
noun
金色；金色的状态；黄金般的光辉
The state or quality of being golden.
The goldennesses of the sunset filled the sky. 夕阳的金光洒满了天空。
Synonyms: goldiness, aureateness
Antonyms: dullness, drabness
