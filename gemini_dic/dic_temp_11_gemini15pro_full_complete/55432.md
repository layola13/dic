distinctest:/dɪˈstɪŋktɪst/
adjective
最清晰的，最明显的
Most easily distinguishable; clearest.
Her voice was the distinctest of all the singers. 她的声音是所有歌手中最清晰的。
近义词: clearest, sharpest, plainest, most obvious
反义词: faintest, vaguest, indistinctest, blurred
词性: adjective
