oblationer:/ˌɒbleɪˈʃənər/ | n. |奉献者；祭品提供者|A person who makes an oblation, or offering, especially to a religious institution or deity. |例句：The oblationer presented the fruits of his harvest to the temple. 奉献者将他的收获果实献给了寺庙。

例句：In ancient times, oblationers would bring animals for sacrifice. 在古代，奉献者会带来动物进行祭祀。|近义词：donor, contributor, offerer|反义词:receiver, recipient|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *oblacioner, *oblatour, from Anglo-Norman oblatiunere, oblatour, from Medieval Latin oblātiōnārius (“one who makes an oblation”), from Latin oblātiō (“an offering”).
