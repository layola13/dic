conscientiousness:/ˌkɒnʃiˈenʃəsnəs/ /ˌkɑːnʃiˈenʃəsnəs/| n. |认真；尽责；一丝不苟|The quality of wishing to do one's work or duty well and thoroughly.|She is known for her conscientiousness and attention to detail. 她以认真负责和注重细节而闻名。|近义词：diligence, meticulousness, carefulness|反义词：negligence, carelessness|词性:名词

Etymology:  From Late Latin *conscientiōsus*, from Latin conscientia (“conscience”), from consciēns, present participle of conscire ("to be conscious of, aware of"), from con- (“together”) + scire ("to know").
