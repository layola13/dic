defamatory:/dɪˈfæməˌtɔːri/ /dɪˈfæməˌtəri/
adjective
诽谤的；中伤的
(of remarks, writing, etc.) damaging the good reputation of someone; slanderous or libelous.
The newspaper published a defamatory article about the politician. 这家报纸发表了一篇关于这位政治家的诽谤性文章。
Synonyms: libelous, slanderous, calumnious, denigratory, disparaging, malicious
Antonyms: complimentary, flattering, laudatory
词性: adjective
