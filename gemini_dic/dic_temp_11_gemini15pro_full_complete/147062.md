overlegislates:/ˌoʊvərˈlɛdʒɪsleɪts/ | v. | 立法过度，过度立法 | to create too many laws, especially on minor issues, which can be seen as excessive or unnecessary regulation. | The city council overlegislates, creating numerous ordinances that restrict businesses and residents.|  Synonyms: overregulate, overgovern | Antonyms: deregulate, underregulate | verb
