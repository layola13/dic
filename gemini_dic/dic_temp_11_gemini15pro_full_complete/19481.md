bidders:/ˈbɪdərz/| n. |投标人，竞标者|People or organizations who offer a price for something, especially at an auction.|例句：The bidders competed fiercely for the antique vase. 投标人为古董花瓶激烈竞争。

例句：Several bidders expressed interest in the property.  几位竞标者对这处房产表示了兴趣。

|近义词：tenderers, offerers, applicants|反义词:sellers, vendors|词性:Noun (plural)


bidder:/ˈbɪdər/| n. |投标人，竞标者|A person or organization who makes a formal offer for something, especially at an auction.|例句：The highest bidder won the auction.  出价最高的投标人赢得了拍卖。

例句：The bidder offered $1 million for the painting.  这位投标人出价100万美元购买这幅画。


|近义词：tenderer, offerer, applicant|反义词:seller, vendor|词性:Noun (singular)
