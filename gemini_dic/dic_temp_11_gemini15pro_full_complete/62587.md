infect:/ɪnˈfɛkt/ | vt. | (感情、思想等)感染，影响 | To affect another with one's enthusiasm, interest, or the like; to imbue. | Her enthusiasm infected the whole team. 她的热情感染了整个团队。 | influence, affect, inspire | discourage, dispirit | verb
