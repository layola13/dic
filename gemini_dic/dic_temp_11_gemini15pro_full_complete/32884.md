chondritis:/kɒnˈdraɪtɪs/ | n. |软骨炎|Inflammation of cartilage.  Cartilage is a strong, flexible tissue that covers the ends of bones at joints. It also gives shape and support to other parts of the body, such as the ears, nose, and windpipe. |The doctor diagnosed him with costochondritis, an inflammation of the cartilage that connects the ribs to the breastbone. 医生诊断他患有肋软骨炎，这是一种连接肋骨和胸骨的软骨发炎。| Costochondritis can cause chest pain that mimics a heart attack. 肋软骨炎会导致胸痛，类似于心脏病发作。 |Synonyms: cartilage inflammation |Antonyms: None | Noun
