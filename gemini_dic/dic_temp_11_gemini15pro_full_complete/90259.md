housecoats:/ˈhaʊskoʊts/| n. |家居服，晨衣（尤指妇女的）|A loose, informal robe worn over nightclothes, typically by women, around the house.|She slipped on her housecoat before going downstairs for breakfast. 她下楼吃早餐前穿上了她的晨衣。|近义词：dressing gown, robe, negligee|反义词: (No direct opposite, possibly street clothes, formal wear) |noun
