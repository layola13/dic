nurture:/ˈnɜːrtʃər/ (US) /ˈnɜːtʃə(r)/ (UK)| v. n.|养育，培育；培养，滋养|To care for and encourage the growth or development of. |例句：She nurtured her plants with great care. 她悉心照料她的植物。|近义词：cultivate, foster, cherish|反义词:neglect, abandon|词性:动词/名词

nurtured:/ˈnɜːrtʃərd/ (US) /ˈnɜːtʃəd/ (UK)| adj.|受过良好教养的；精心培育的|Having been cared for and encouraged to develop. |例句：He was nurtured in a loving home. 他在一个充满爱的家庭中长大。|近义词：cherished, fostered, cultivated|反义词:neglected, abandoned|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *norturen*, from Old French *norturer* "to nourish, educate," from Latin *nutrire* "to suckle, nourish."
