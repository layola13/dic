ethe:/ˈiːθeɪ/ | n. | 以太，乙醚 | A colorless, volatile, highly flammable liquid, C₂H₅OC₂H₅, derived from ethyl alcohol and used as an anesthetic and as a solvent. |  Example: The patient was anesthetized with ether. 病人用乙醚麻醉。| Synonym: diethyl ether | Antonym: N/A | noun

ethe:/ˈiːθər/ | n. | 以太，乙醚，苍穹，天空 | the upper regions of air beyond the clouds. | Example: The rocket soared into the ether. 火箭飞入太空。| Synonym: sky, heavens | Antonym: earth, ground | noun
