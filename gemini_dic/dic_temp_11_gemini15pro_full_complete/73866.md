formulize:/ˈfɔːrmjəlaɪz/ /ˈfɔːrmjʊlaɪz/| v. |使公式化，使系统化，正式化|To express something precisely or systematically, often in a formula or set of rules. |例句：They are trying to formulized their research methodology.  他们正在努力使他们的研究方法系统化。

例句：The company formulized its hiring process to reduce bias. 公司将其招聘流程正式化，以减少偏见。|近义词：systematize, codify, formalize, organize, structure|反义词：disorganize, randomize|词性:动词
