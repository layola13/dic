cast-down:/ˌkæst ˈdaʊn/ | adj. | 沮丧的，垂头丧气的 | Feeling sad and discouraged. |  He felt cast-down after failing the exam. 他考试不及格后感到沮丧。| 近义词: dejected, depressed, downcast, discouraged | 反义词: cheerful, happy, elated, upbeat | 词性:形容词

cast-down:/ˌkæst ˈdaʊn/ | v. | 使沮丧，使低落 | To make someone feel sad and discouraged. | The news of his friend's death cast him down.  他朋友去世的消息使他很沮丧。| 近义词: depress, sadden, dishearten | 反义词: cheer up, encourage, uplift | 词性:动词


词源:  From the Middle English *casten doun*, meaning "to throw down," from Old Norse *kasta niður*.  The figurative sense of "to depress or deject" developed later.
