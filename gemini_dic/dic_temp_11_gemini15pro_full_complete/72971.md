fool-away:/fuːl əˈweɪ/
|phrasal verb|挥霍，浪费|To spend time or money unwisely or frivolously. |He fooled away his inheritance on gambling. 他把遗产都浪费在赌博上了。|例句：She fooled away her entire summer vacation watching TV. 她把整个暑假都浪费在看电视上了。|近义词：squander, waste, fritter away|反义词：save, conserve, invest|词性:动词短语

fool:/fuːl/
|n.|傻瓜，愚人|A person who lacks good judgment or acts unwisely.|例句：He was a fool to trust her.  他真是个傻瓜，竟然相信她。|近义词：idiot, moron, simpleton|反义词：genius, sage, wise person|词性:名词

away:/əˈweɪ/
|adv.|离开，远离|At a distance in space or time.|例句：He went away for the weekend. 他周末出去了。|近义词：off, gone, absent|反义词：here, present, near|词性:副词
