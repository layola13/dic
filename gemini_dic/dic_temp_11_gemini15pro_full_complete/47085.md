daturine:/ˈdeɪtʃərɪn/ | n. |颠茄碱，曼陀罗碱| A poisonous alkaloid occurring in some plants of the nightshade family, such as belladonna. It is a potent anticholinergic and has mydriatic (pupil-dilating) properties. | Daturine is extracted from plants like deadly nightshade. 颠茄碱是从像颠茄这样的植物中提取的。| Daturine can be used to dilate pupils during eye examinations. 颠茄碱可用于眼科检查时扩张瞳孔。|Synonyms: atropine, hyoscyamine | Antonyms: None | Noun
