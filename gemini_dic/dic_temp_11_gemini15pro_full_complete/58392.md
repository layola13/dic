dubbers:/ˈdʌbərz/| n.|配音演员；译制人员|People who dub films or other audiovisual material into another language. |The dubbers did an excellent job of matching the lip movements to the translated dialogue. 配音演员们出色地完成了将唇形与翻译后的对话相匹配的工作。|近义词：voice actors, voice artists|反义词:|noun


dubbers:/ˈdʌbərz/| n.|复制者；翻录者|People who make copies of recordings, especially illegally. |The dubbers were selling pirated copies of the latest movies. 复制者正在出售最新电影的盗版。|近义词：copiers, bootleggers|反义词:|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "dub," which originated from the Old French word "douber" meaning "to equip with," later evolving to mean "to give a name or title to" and "to make a copy of."  The "-ers" suffix indicates a person who performs the action of dubbing.
