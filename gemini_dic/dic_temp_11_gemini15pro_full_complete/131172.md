nonbreeder:/ˈnɒnˌbriːdər/ (US) /ˈnɒnˌbriːdə(r)/ (UK)| n. |非繁殖者，不育动物|An animal that does not breed, especially during a particular breeding season.  A nonbreeder might be too young, too old, or experiencing environmental stress.|Many young birds are nonbreeders in their first year.|Synonyms: non-reproductive, barren (for females)|Antonyms: breeder, reproductive|Noun
