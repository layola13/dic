convulsively:/kənˈvʌlsɪvli/ | adv. | 抽搐地，痉挛地 |  In a way that is affected by or produces convulsions (sudden, violent, irregular movements of the body, caused by involuntary contraction of muscles). |  He grasped the railing convulsively.  他痉挛地抓住栏杆。 | synonyms: spasmodically, jerkily, fitfully, violently | antonyms: smoothly, calmly, steadily | word type: adverb

convulsively:/kənˈvʌlsɪvli/ | adv. |  抽搐地，痉挛地 |  Characterized by or causing convulsions;  happening in a sudden, jerky way.  | She sobbed convulsively. 她抽搐着哭泣。 | synonyms:  spasmodically,  erratically,  irregularly | antonyms: smoothly, calmly, evenly | word type: adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *convulsivus, from Latin convulsus (past participle of convellere “to tear loose, pull violently”), from con- (“together, completely”) + vellere (“to pluck, tear”).
