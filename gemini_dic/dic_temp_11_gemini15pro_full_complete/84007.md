half-discriminating:/ˌhæf dɪˈskrɪmɪneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 半歧视的；有部分歧视的 | Showing or involving partial discrimination; discriminating to some extent but not completely. |  The study found evidence of half-discriminating practices in hiring. 这项研究发现了招聘过程中存在部分歧视行为的证据。 |  The policy was criticized for being half-discriminating and not going far enough to ensure equality. 这项政策因其部分歧视的性质而受到批评，因为它在确保平等方面做得还不够。| 近义词: partially discriminatory, selectively biased | 反义词: non-discriminatory, unbiased, impartial | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
"half-" (Old English healf) + "discriminating" (from Latin discriminare, "to distinguish between")
