dragonnade:/ˌdræɡəˈneɪd/
noun
龙骑兵的迫害；强迫改宗
The persecution of French Protestants by quartering dragoons in their homes to force conversions to Catholicism.
The king used dragonades to force Huguenots to convert. 国王用龙骑兵迫害来强迫胡格诺派教徒皈依。
persecution, oppression, coercion
tolerance, acceptance
