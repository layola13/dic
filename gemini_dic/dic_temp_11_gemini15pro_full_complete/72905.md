foment:/foʊˈmɛnt/ | v. |煽动，激起；促进，助长 | To instigate or stir up (an undesirable or violent sentiment or course of action); to promote the growth or development of.|  The rebels are accused of fomenting violence. 叛乱分子被指控煽动暴力。|  The politician was accused of fomenting unrest among the people.  这位政客被指控煽动民众的不安。| 近义词: incite, instigate, provoke, abet, stimulate, encourage | 反义词: quell, suppress, allay, soothe, discourage | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From Late Middle English *fomenten*, from Old French *fomenter*, from Latin *fomentāre* "to cherish, nurse, promote," from *fōmentum* "poultice, lotion."
