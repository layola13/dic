kaisership:/ˈkaɪzərʃɪp/
n.
皇帝的职位；皇帝的统治
the office or reign of a kaiser
The kaisership of Germany ended with the abdication of Wilhelm II in 1918. 德意志帝国的皇帝职位随着威廉二世在1918年的退位而结束。
近义词:  empire, reign, sovereignty
反义词: N/A
词性: noun

词源:
From German *Kaisertum*, from *Kaiser* (“emperor”) + *-tum* (suffix forming abstract nouns).  *Kaiser* itself comes from Latin *Caesar*.
