hal:/hæl/ | n. | 大厅，门厅 | A large entrance hall or reception area. |  Example: The guests gathered in the grand hal for the reception. 客人们聚集在宏伟的大厅里参加招待会。

Example:  The king addressed his subjects from the castle hal. 国王在城堡大厅里向他的臣民讲话。 | 近义词: hall, lobby, foyer, entrance, vestibule | 反义词: room, chamber, closet | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Middle English *hale*, from Old English *heal*, *heall*, from Proto-Germanic *hallō*, *halluz* ("covered place, hall"), from Proto-Indo-European *kel-*, *kol-*. Cognate with Dutch *hal*, German *Halle*, Icelandic *höll* ("hall").
