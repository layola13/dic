disparagingly:/dɪˈspærɪdʒɪŋli/
|adv.|轻蔑地，贬低地|In a way that expresses a low opinion of someone or something; critically.|He spoke disparagingly of his colleagues. 他轻蔑地谈论他的同事们。|Synonyms: contemptuously, scornfully, disdainfully|Antonyms: respectfully, admiringly, approvingly|词性: 副词

disparage:/dɪˈspærɪdʒ/
|v.|贬低，轻视|To regard or represent as being of little worth.|He never missed an opportunity to disparage his competitors. 他从不放过任何一个贬低竞争对手的机会。|Synonyms: belittle, denigrate, deprecate|Antonyms: praise, commend, extol|词性: 动词
