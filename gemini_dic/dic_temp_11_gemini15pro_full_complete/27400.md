cameoed:/ˈkæmioʊd/
v.
客串；短暂露面
made a brief appearance (especially as oneself) in a film, play, or other work.
例句：The famous author cameoed in the movie adaptation of his book.  这位著名作家在他的书改编的电影中客串了一下。

例句：She cameoed as a waitress in the background of the scene.  她在场景的背景中客串了一个服务员的角色。

近义词：appeared, guested, featured
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could be considered the opposite of "starred")
词性：verb (past tense of "cameo")
