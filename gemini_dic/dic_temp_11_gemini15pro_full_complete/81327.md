grapheme:/ˈɡræfiːm/| n.|字素|A grapheme is the smallest meaningful contrastive unit in a writing system. It is a written symbol that represents a phoneme or a combination of phonemes.  |The grapheme "gh" can represent different sounds, as in "tough" and "ghost". 字素“gh”可以表示不同的声音，例如在“tough”和“ghost”中。| synonym: letter, character | antonym: phoneme | noun
