decennium:/dɪˈsɛniəm/
n.
十年，十年期
a period of ten years
The first decennium of the 21st century witnessed significant technological advancements.  21世纪的第一个十年见证了重大的技术进步。

The past decennium has been a period of rapid growth for the company. 过去十年是公司快速发展的时期。

decade
biennium (two years), triennium (three years)
noun
