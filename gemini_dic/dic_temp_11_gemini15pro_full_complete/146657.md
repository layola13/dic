overhastening:/ˌoʊvərˈheɪstənɪŋ/| n. | 过于匆忙，操之过急 | The act of hastening something excessively or prematurely.  It implies a negative consequence due to rushing. | His overhastening in making the decision led to costly mistakes. 他过于匆忙地做出决定，导致了代价高昂的错误。| Overhastening led to a flawed product launch. 过于仓促导致产品发布失败。|近义词： rushing, hurrying, precipitateness |反义词: deliberation, carefulness, patience |词性:noun
词源: From "over-" (excessively) + "hasten" (to speed up), from Middle English "hasten," from Old Norse "heista."
