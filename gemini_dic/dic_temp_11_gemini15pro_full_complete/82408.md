grudger:/ˈɡrʌdʒər/ | n. | 吝啬鬼，心怀不满的人 | A person who is unwilling to forgive or forget; someone who bears a grudge. |  He's such a grudger; he still hasn't forgiven me for borrowing his pen last year. 他真是个小气鬼，我去年借了他的钢笔，他到现在还没原谅我。

The landlord was a grudger who refused to make any repairs to the building.  房东是个吝啬鬼，他拒绝对大楼进行任何维修。| 近义词: malcontent, resentful person, sore loser | 反义词: forgiving person, magnanimous person | 词性: 名词
