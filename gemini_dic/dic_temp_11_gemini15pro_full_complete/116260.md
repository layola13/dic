massacrers:/ˈmæsəkərərz/
n.|屠杀者；大屠杀的实行者|People who commit massacres are called massacrers.  实行大屠杀的人被称为屠杀者。|The massacrers showed no mercy. 屠杀者没有表现出任何怜悯。|近义词：killers, murderers, slaughterers|反义词：saviors, protectors|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *massacreur*, from *massacrer* (to massacre), from Old French *maçacre*, of uncertain origin. Possibly from Vulgar Latin *macecre* or *mazacre*, from *mattiāre* (to kill), a variant of *mactāre* (to slaughter, sacrifice), from Proto-Italic *maktā-,* from Proto-Indo-European *mégh₂- (“to slaughter, offer sacrifice”).
