location:/loʊˈkeɪʃən/ (US), /ləʊˈkeɪʃn/ (UK)| n.|位置，地点；场所；外景拍摄地|A location is a particular place or position.|例句：The restaurant is in a beautiful location. 这家餐厅位于一个美丽的地方。|近义词：place, position, site, spot, venue|反义词: nowhere|名词

location:/loʊˈkeɪʃən/ (US), /ləʊˈkeɪʃn/ (UK)| n.|定位；测位|The act of locating something.|例句：The location of the missing ship took several days.  搜寻失踪船只的位置花了几天时间。|近义词：pinpointing, finding, situating|反义词: losing|名词
