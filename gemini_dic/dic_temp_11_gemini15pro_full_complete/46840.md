dapperling:/ˈdæpərlɪŋ/
n.
衣着讲究的年轻人；花花公子
a dapper young man; a man who is always dressed very smartly.
The dapperling strolled into the cafe, attracting admiring glances.  这个衣着讲究的年轻人漫步走进咖啡馆，吸引了赞赏的目光。
近义词: dandy, fop, swell
反义词: slob, ragamuffin
词性: 名词

Etymology:  From dapper + -ling (a diminutive suffix).  Dapper itself comes from Middle Dutch dapper, meaning "brave, valiant."  The sense shifted to "neat and trim" in the 17th century.
