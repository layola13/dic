breadfruit:/ˈbredˌfruːt/| n.|面包果|A large, starchy fruit of a tropical tree, resembling a bread roll in shape and texture when cooked.|例句：Breadfruit is a staple food in many Pacific Island cultures.  面包果是许多太平洋岛国文化中的主食。

例句：We roasted the breadfruit over an open fire. 我们在篝火上烤面包果。|近义词：cassava, taro, plantain|反义词:None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From bread + fruit.  So named because when cooked, the starchy fruit resembles freshly baked bread.
