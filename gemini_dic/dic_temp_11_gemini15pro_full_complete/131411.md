nonclassroom:/ˌnɑːnˈklæsˌruːm/ /ˌnɒnˈklɑːsˌruːm/
adj.|非课堂的，课外的|Taking place outside of a traditional classroom setting. nonclassroom:/ˌnɑːnˈklæsˌruːm/ /ˌnɒnˈklɑːsˌruːm/
n.|非课堂环境|A setting or environment outside of the traditional classroom where learning can take place.|例句：Museums can be a valuable nonclassroom learning environment.  博物馆可以成为一个有价值的非课堂学习环境。|近义词：out-of-classroom setting, informal learning space|反义词：classroom|词性:名词
