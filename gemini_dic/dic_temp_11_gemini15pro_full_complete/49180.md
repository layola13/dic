delamination:/diːˌlæmɪˈneɪʃn/
n.|分层，剥离|The separation of layers in a material, such as plywood or composite materials.|The delamination of the aircraft's wing was a serious concern. 飞机机翼的分层是一个严重的问题。|separation, splitting, peeling| adhesion, bonding
词性: 名词

delaminate:/diːˈlæmɪneɪt/
v.|分层，剥离|To separate into layers.|The poster began to delaminate after being exposed to rain.  海报被雨淋后开始分层。|separate, split, peel|bond, adhere
词性: 动词
