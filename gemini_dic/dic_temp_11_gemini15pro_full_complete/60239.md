effronteries: /ɪˈfrʌntəriːz/
noun
厚颜无耻，放肆
extreme boldness; presumptuousness.
Her effronteries to the judge resulted in her being held in contempt of court. 她对法官的无礼导致她被判藐视法庭。
近义词: audacity, impudence, insolence, temerity
反义词: humility, modesty, diffidence, respect
词性: noun (plural)

effrontery: /ɪˈfrʌntəri/
noun
厚颜无耻，放肆
shameless boldness; impudence.
He had the effrontery to ask for a raise after only a week on the job. 他竟然敢在工作才一周后就要求加薪。
近义词: audacity, impudence, insolence, temerity
反义词: humility, modesty, diffidence, respect
词性: noun
