insolubly:/ɪnˈsɒljʊbli/| adv.|不可溶地，无法解决地|In a way that cannot be solved or explained; in a way that cannot be dissolved in a liquid.|例句：The two countries' interests are insolubly linked. 这两个国家的利益密不可分。|近义词：permanently, inextricably, hopelessly|反义词: solubly, resolvably|词性:副词
Etymology: From Late Latin *insolubilis*, from in- "not" + solubilis "soluble," from solvere "to loosen, dissolve."
