archiver:/ˈɑːrkaɪvər/| n.|存档器；归档程序；档案管理员|A person or thing that archives something, especially computer files or software.|The archiver automatically backs up my files every night.  这个存档器每晚自动备份我的文件。|近义词:archivist, recorder, custodian|反义词: N/A|noun


archiver:/ˈɑːrkaɪvər/| n.|档案馆；档案室|A place where archives are stored.|The university archiver contains a wealth of historical documents. 大学档案馆收藏了大量的历史文献。|近义词:archive, repository, storehouse|反义词: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From archive + -er.  "Archive" comes from the Old French "archif," from Medieval Latin "archivum," ultimately from Greek "arkheion" meaning "public building, town hall," from "arkhē" meaning "government."
