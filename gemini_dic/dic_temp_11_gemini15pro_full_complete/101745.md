irresoluteness:/ɪˌrɛzəˈluːtnəs/
n.
优柔寡断，犹豫不决
The state of being unable to decide what to do; lack of determination.
His irresoluteness cost him the election. 他的优柔寡断让他输掉了选举。
近义词:indecision, vacillation, hesitation
反义词:resoluteness, decisiveness, determination
词性:名词

irresolute:/ɪˈrɛzəluːt/
adj.
优柔寡断的，犹豫不决的
Showing or feeling hesitancy; uncertain.
She was irresolute about what to do next. 她对接下来该做什么犹豫不决。
近义词:indecisive, hesitant, vacillating
反义词:resolute, decisive, determined
词性:形容词
