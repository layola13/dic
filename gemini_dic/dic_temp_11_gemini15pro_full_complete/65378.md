euonymin: /juːˈɒnɪmɪn/ | n. | 卫矛苷，卫矛提取物 | A bitter, crystalline glucoside, C31H50O17, extracted from the bark of several species of euonymus, especially the European spindle tree (Euonymus europaeus), and formerly used as a heart stimulant, diuretic, and expectorant. | Euonymin is a powerful purgative and should be used with caution. 卫矛苷是一种强力泻药，应谨慎使用。| Euonymin is extracted from the bark of the euonymus tree. 卫矛苷是从卫矛树的树皮中提取的。| Synonyms: evonymite | Antonyms: None | Noun
