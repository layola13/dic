outrebounding:/ˌaʊtrɪˈbaʊndɪŋ/
v. |超过…的篮板球数|Grabbing more rebounds than the opposing team.  超过对方球队的篮板球数量。|The Lakers outrebounded the Celtics by a significant margin. 湖人队的篮板球数量远远超过了凯尔特人队。|近义词: out-rebound, dominate the boards|反义词: be outrebounded|词性:动词

outrebounded:/ˌaʊtrɪˈbaʊndɪd/
v. |篮板球被超过|Having fewer rebounds than the opposing team. 篮板球数量少于对方球队。|The Celtics were outrebounded by the Lakers in the last game. 在上一场比赛中，凯尔特人队的篮板球数量被湖人队超过。|近义词: lose the battle of the boards|反义词: outrebound|词性:动词
