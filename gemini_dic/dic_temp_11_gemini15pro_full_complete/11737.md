aromatic:/ˌærəˈmætɪk/ | n. | 芳香植物；芳香剂 | A plant, herb, or spice with a pleasant smell. |  Many aromatics are used in cooking. 许多芳香植物用于烹饪。| fragrance, perfume, spice | stench, stink | noun
