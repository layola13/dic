microblogging: /ˈmaɪkrəʊblɒɡɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈmaɪkroʊblɑːɡɪŋ/ (US) | n. |微博; 微型博客; |The act of posting short, frequent updates to a microblog (like Twitter).  This typically involves sharing text, images, links, and videos with a network of followers.| She's been busy microblogging about her trip to Japan. 她一直忙着在微博上记录她的日本之旅。 |近义词：tweeting, social networking |反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps traditional blogging) | 词性: noun
