beluga:/bəˈluːɡə/ /bɪˈluːɡə/| n.|白鲸|A species of Arctic whale (Delphinapterus leucas) that is white when adult and is related to the narwhal.|例句：The beluga whale is known for its distinctive white color. 白鲸以其独特的白色而闻名。

例句：Belugas are highly social animals that communicate through a variety of clicks and whistles.  白鲸是高度社会化的动物，通过各种咔哒声和哨声进行交流。

|近义词：white whale|反义词:|词性:noun


beluga:/bəˈluːɡə/ /bɪˈluːɡə/| adj.|白鲸的|Relating to or characteristic of the beluga whale.|例句：Beluga caviar is considered a delicacy. 白鲸鱼子酱被认为是一种美味佳肴。

例句：The beluga whale's diet consists mainly of fish and crustaceans. 白鲸的饮食主要由鱼类和甲壳类动物组成。
