collectivistically:/kəˌlɛktɪˈvɪstɪkli/
|adv.|集体主义地；集体地|In a way that prioritizes the needs and goals of a group over the needs and goals of individuals.  强调群体需求和目标高于个人需求和目标的方式。|例句：They work collectivistically towards a common goal.  他们为了一个共同目标集体工作。

|例句：The community operated collectivistically, sharing resources and making decisions together. 这个社区集体运作，共享资源并共同做出决定。|近义词：communally, cooperatively, jointly|反义词：individualistically, independently, selfishly|词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From collective + -ist + -ic + -al + -ly.  源自 collective（集体的） + -ist（…主义者） + -ic（…的） + -al（…的） + -ly（…地）。
