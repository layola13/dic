momentally:/ˈmoʊmənˌtɛrəli/ /məʊˈment(ə)li/| adv.|片刻地，暂时地；立即，马上|For a moment; briefly.  In a moment; soon.|例句：The pain was intense, but only momentarily. 疼痛很剧烈，但只是暂时的。

例句：I'll be with you momentarily. 我马上就来。|近义词：briefly, shortly, soon, in a moment, presently|反义词：permanently, indefinitely, for a long time|词性:副词

词源: From moment + -ally.  The two senses developed independently. The sense "for a moment" is older.
