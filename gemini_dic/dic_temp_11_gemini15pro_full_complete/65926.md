exacerbate:/ɪɡˈzæsərbeɪt/ /ɛkˈsæsərˌbeɪt/| v. |加剧，恶化|to make something worse, especially a problem or a disease|The humid weather exacerbated her asthma. 潮湿的天气加剧了她的哮喘。|近义词：aggravate, worsen, intensify|反义词: alleviate, improve, mitigate|词性:动词
