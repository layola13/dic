laminary:/ˈlæmɪnəri/ | adj.| 层流的，薄片状的 | Relating to, or consisting of, thin plates or layers; arranged in layers. | The laminary flow of the air around the wing was crucial for efficient flight. 机翼周围空气的层流对于高效飞行至关重要。

The artist created a laminary sculpture using thin sheets of metal.  这位艺术家用薄金属片创作了一个层状雕塑。 | 近义词：layered, stratified, foliated | 反义词: turbulent, chaotic | 词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *laminarius, from Latin lamina "thin plate, leaf," from PIE root *lembh- "to hang loosely, limp."
