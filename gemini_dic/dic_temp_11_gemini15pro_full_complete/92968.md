idil: /ˈɪdɪl/ | n. | 田园诗; 牧歌; | A short poem descriptive of rural or pastoral life. |  Example: The poet's idyll celebrated the simple joys of country life. 诗人的田园诗歌颂了乡村生活的简单快乐。

Example:  Their honeymoon was a romantic idyll in a secluded cabin. 他们的蜜月是在一个僻静的小屋里度过的浪漫田园诗。| 近义词: pastoral, eclogue, romance | 反义词: reality, actuality | 词性: noun
