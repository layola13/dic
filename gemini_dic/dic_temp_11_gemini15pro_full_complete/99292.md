intercorpuscular:/ˌɪntərˈkɔːrpəskjʊlər/
adjective
微粒间的，粒子间的
existing or occurring between corpuscles or small particles.
The intercorpuscular forces are weak in gases. 气体中的微粒间作用力很弱。
近义词:intermolecular, interatomic
反义词:intramolecular, intra-atomic
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From inter- + corpuscular.  "Inter-" is a Latin prefix meaning "between" or "among." "Corpuscular" derives from the Latin word "corpusculum," meaning "a small body or particle."
