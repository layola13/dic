crocheter:/kroʊˈʃeɪ.ər/ (US), /krəʊˈʃeɪ.ər/ (UK)| n.|钩编者；钩针编织者|A person who crochets.  A crocheter is someone skilled in the art of creating fabric by interlocking loops of yarn or thread with a crochet hook.| My grandmother is a skilled crocheter, and she makes beautiful blankets. 我祖母是一位技艺精湛的钩编者，她制作的毯子很漂亮。|Synonym: knitter, needleworker|Antonym: None|Noun

crocheting:/kroʊˈʃeɪ.ɪŋ/ (US), /krəʊˈʃeɪ.ɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|钩边；钩针编织；钩针织物|The action or process of creating fabric by interlocking loops of yarn or thread with a crochet hook.
