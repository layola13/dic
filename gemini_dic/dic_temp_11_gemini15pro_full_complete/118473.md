mesial:/ˈmiːziəl/| adj.|近中的，中间的|Situated in the middle, especially of the dental arch, closest to the midline between the central incisors.|The mesial surface of the canine tooth is adjacent to the lateral incisor. 犬齿的近中面与侧切牙相邻。|Synonyms: medial, central|Antonyms: distal, lateral|adjective
