exec:/ɪɡˈzɛk/ (US) /ˈɛksɪk/ (UK)| n. vt.|执行，执行程序，执行指令，(尤指)公司主管|A program or command that is executed; (especially) a senior manager in a business organization.|The exec decided to restructure the company.  这位主管决定重组公司。|execute, run, implement|manager, director, administrator|verb, noun
ETYMOLOGY->
Shortened from *executive*. From Latin *exsequi* "to follow out, pursue, enforce, execute," from *ex* "out" + *sequi* "to follow".
