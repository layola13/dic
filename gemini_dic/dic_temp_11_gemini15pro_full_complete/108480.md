lay-to:/ˈleɪ tuː/
verb phrase
停航，使船顶风停航
To bring a sailing vessel into the wind and hold it almost stationary, either by arranging the sails or by using the engine.
The captain decided to lay-to until the storm passed. 船长决定停航，直到暴风雨过去。
The small yacht lay-to, drifting gently with the current. 小游艇停航了，随着水流轻轻漂流。
近义词: heave to
反义词: get under way, set sail
词性: 动词短语
