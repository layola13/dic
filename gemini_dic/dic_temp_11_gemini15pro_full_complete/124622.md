mousse:/muːs/| n.|慕斯，泡沫，摩丝|A light, airy dessert or topping made with whipped cream, egg whites, or both, often flavored with chocolate, fruit, or other ingredients. Also a styling foam for hair.|The chocolate mousse was rich and decadent. 巧克力慕斯浓郁而奢华。|近义词:foam, froth|反义词:(no direct opposite, perhaps "solid")|noun

mousse:/muːs/| n.|摩丝(头发定型)|A light foam used as a styling product for hair.|She used mousse to give her hair volume. 她用摩丝给头发增加蓬松感。|近义词:hair styling foam|反义词:(no direct opposite)|noun
