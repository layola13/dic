nonappropriative:/ˌnɒnəˈproʊprieɪtɪv/
adjective
非占有的，不占有的
Not relating to or involving the appropriation of something, especially of water rights.  Relating to or designating a water right acquired other than by the usual means specified by statute, as by adverse possession.
The state recognizes both appropriative and nonappropriative water rights.  该州承认占有性和非占有性水权。
Nonappropriative rights are often based on historical use. 非占有性权利通常基于历史使用。
Synonyms: non-exclusive, shared
Antonyms: appropriative, exclusive
词性: adjective
