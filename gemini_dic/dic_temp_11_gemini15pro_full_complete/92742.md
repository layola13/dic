ichthyocoprolite:/ˌɪkθiɒˈkɒprəlaɪt/
n.
鱼粪石，鱼类粪便化石
fossilized fish excrement.  It provides valuable insights into the diet and environment of ancient fish.
Example: The ichthyocoprolite contained remnants of crustaceans, indicating the fish's diet. 鱼粪石中含有甲壳类动物的残骸，表明了这种鱼的饮食。

Example: Researchers analyzed the ichthyocoprolites to understand the prehistoric ecosystem. 研究人员分析了鱼粪石以了解史前生态系统。
近义词: coprolite (更广义的粪便化石)
反义词: 无
词性: noun
