croquette:/kroʊˈkɛt/| n. |炸丸子，炸肉饼|A small roll of minced meat, fish, or vegetables, coated in breadcrumbs and deep-fried.|例句：She served the croquettes with a side salad. 她配着沙拉上了炸丸子。

例句：These salmon croquettes are absolutely delicious. 这些鲑鱼炸丸子真是太美味了。|近义词：patty, fritter, rissole|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "soup" or "stew" as contrasting dishes) |词性:noun
