/hwɑːt/ |pron. det. int. adv.| 什么，多么 |Used to ask for information about something or someone.| What are you doing? 你在做什么？| which, who | N/A | pronoun, determiner, interjection, adverb


Etymology: From Old English _hwæt_, neuter of _hwā_ ("who"). Cognate with Old Frisian _hwat_, Old Saxon _hwat_, Old High German _waz_, Gothic _ƕa_ (hwa), Old Norse _hvat_, Latin _quod_.

USAGE->
Simple Present:
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does he do for a living? 他靠什么谋生？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? 她晚饭在做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned recently? 你最近学到了什么？
What has he done to help? 他做了些什么来帮忙？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on? 你一直在忙什么？
What has she been reading lately? 她最近一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? 派对上发生了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about? 她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you left? 你走之前做了什么？
What had happened before the police arrived? 警察到达之前发生了什么事？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I called? 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major?  她转专业之前一直在学什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will happen next? 接下来会发生什么？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time tomorrow? 明天这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting? 他们会在会议上讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将取得什么成就？
What will they have finished by then? 到那时他们将完成什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业的时候她将已经学习了什么多久了？
