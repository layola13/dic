half-jewish:/ˌhæf ˈdʒuːɪʃ/ | adj. | 半犹太人的 |  Someone who has one parent who is Jewish and one who is not.  It's important to note that Jewish identity is complex and views on who is Jewish vary. Some Jewish communities only consider someone Jewish if their mother is Jewish. | My friend is half-Jewish; his father is Jewish, and his mother is Catholic. 我的朋友是半犹太人；他的父亲是犹太人，他的母亲是天主教徒。|  half-blooded, partially Jewish | fully Jewish, non-Jewish | adjective
