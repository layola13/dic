fricassee:/ˈfrɪkəˌsiː/| n. vt.|炖肉,  fricasséed, fricasseeing|A dish of meat (especially chicken) cut into small pieces, stewed in stock, and served in a thick white sauce.|例句：She prepared a delicious chicken fricassee for dinner. 她晚餐准备了一道美味的鸡肉炖菜。|近义词：stew, ragout|反义词:|noun, verb
