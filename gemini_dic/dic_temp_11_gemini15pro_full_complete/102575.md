jagua:/ˈhɑːɡwə/ | n. |  哈瓜,  黑素果,  染料果 | A tropical American tree (Genipa americana) of the madder family, bearing a large, edible fruit. Also, a black dye prepared from the fruit, used for body painting and tattoos.  |  The indigenous people used jagua to create intricate designs on their skin.  土著人用哈瓜在他们的皮肤上绘制复杂的图案。 | synonym: Genipa americana, black dye | antonym:  N/A | noun
