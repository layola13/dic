interments:/ɪnˈtɜːrmənts/
n.
埋葬；安葬
The act of burying a dead person.
The interments took place in the church cemetery. 这些安葬仪式在教堂墓地举行。

The museum holds many ancient Egyptian interments.  这个博物馆收藏了许多古埃及的陪葬品。


近义词:burials, funerals
反义词:exhumations


词性:名词(plural)


词源:From Old French enterrement, from enterrer "to bury," from en- "in" + terre "earth."
