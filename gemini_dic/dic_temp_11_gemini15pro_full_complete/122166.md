mistruth:/ˌmɪsˈtruːθ/
n.
虚假;不真实;谎言
a statement that is not true
例句：She uttered a deliberate mistruth. 她故意说了一句谎话。

例句：The politician spread mistruths about his opponent.  这个政客散布关于他对手的不实之词。

近义词：lie, falsehood, fib, untruth, fabrication
反义词：truth, verity, fact
