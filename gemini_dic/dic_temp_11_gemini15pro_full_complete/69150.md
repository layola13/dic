favouritism:/ˈfeɪvərɪtɪzəm/ /ˈfeɪvərɪˌtɪzəm/| n.|偏袒；偏爱；徇私|The act of unfairly treating one person better than others because you like them better.|He was accused of favouritism towards his son. 他被指控偏袒自己的儿子。|Synonyms: bias, partiality, preferential treatment|Antonyms: impartiality, fairness, equity|noun

favouritism:/ˈfeɪvərɪtɪzəm/ /ˈfeɪvərɪˌtɪzəm/| n.|偏袒；偏爱；徇私|Showing unfair favor to one person or group at the expense of others.|She resigned amid accusations of favouritism. 她因被指控徇私而辞职。|Synonyms: nepotism, cronyism|Antonyms:  impartiality, objectivity|noun
