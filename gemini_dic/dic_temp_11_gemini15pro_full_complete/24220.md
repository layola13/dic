bridgeless:/ˈbrɪdʒləs/| adj.|无桥的，没有桥梁的|Lacking a bridge or bridges; not connected by a bridge.|The river was bridgeless, forcing them to find another way across.|近义词：unbridged|反义词:bridged|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From "bridge" + "-less" (suffix indicating absence). "Bridge" comes from Old English "brycg," from Proto-Germanic "*brugjo-," meaning "bridge, beam."  "-less" comes from Old English "-lēas."
