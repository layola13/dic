metatitanic: /ˌmɛtətɑɪˈtænɪk/
adjective
超泰坦尼克号的；极其巨大的
Relating to or resembling something of immense size or power, often used hyperbolically in reference to the Titanic disaster.  Evoking a sense of scale or disaster even greater than the sinking of the Titanic.
The metatitanic struggle between the two corporations left the entire industry in ruins.  这两个公司之间超泰坦尼克号的斗争使整个行业陷入瘫痪。
The film portrayed a metatitanic battle between good and evil.  这部电影描绘了一场善恶之间极其巨大的战斗。
近义词: colossal, gigantic, immense, mammoth, gargantuan
反义词: tiny, minuscule, insignificant, small
词性: adjective
词源:  "meta-" (beyond, transcending) + "Titanic" (referring to the RMS Titanic).
