oscine:/ˈɑːsaɪn/ /ˈɒsaɪn/| adj. n.|鸣禽，鸣禽的|Of or relating to the suborder Oscines, the songbirds; a songbird.|例句：The oscine birds are known for their complex songs. 鸣禽以其复杂的歌声而闻名。

例句：The robin is an oscine bird common in gardens. 知更鸟是一种常见于花园的鸣禽。|近义词：songbird, passerine|反义词:non-oscine|词性：adjective, noun

Etymology:
From Latin *oscinis, from oscen, meaning "singing bird, bird whose song was regarded as ominous". Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ōs- "sing".
