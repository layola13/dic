dispark:/dɪsˈpɑːrk/
verb
剥夺…的公园资格；取消…作为公园的资格
To deprive (a place) of its status or privileges as a park.
The city council voted to dispark the area and build a shopping mall. 市议会投票决定取消该地区的公园资格并建造一个购物中心。
synonyms:  depark, divest of park status
antonyms:  designate as a park, establish as a park
词性: 动词
