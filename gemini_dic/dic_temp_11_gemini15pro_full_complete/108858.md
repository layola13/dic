lecherous: /ˈlɛtʃərəs/ | adj. | 好色的；淫荡的 | Having or showing excessive or offensive sexual desire. | He gave her a lecherous look. 他用好色的眼神看着她。|  The old man leered at the young women with a lecherous grin.  那个老男人带着淫荡的笑容对着年轻女子 leer。 | 近义词: lustful, lascivious, libidinous, lewd, salacious | 反义词: chaste, virtuous, pure | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Middle English *lecherous, from Old French lecheros, from lecheor (“lecher”), from lechier (“to lick”), from Latin lingere (“to lick”).
