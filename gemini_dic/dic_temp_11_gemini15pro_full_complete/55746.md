diverge:/daɪˈvɜːrdʒ/ /dɪˈvɜːrdʒ/
verb
分叉；偏离；分歧
to separate and go in different directions; to become different
The paths diverged in the woods.  这两条小路在树林里分岔了。
separate, deviate, branch off
converge, meet, unite
verb

diverged:/daɪˈvɜːrdʒd/ /dɪˈvɜːrdʒd/
verb (past tense)
分叉；偏离；分歧(过去式)
past tense of diverge
