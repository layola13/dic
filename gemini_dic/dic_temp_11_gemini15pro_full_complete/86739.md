helotism:/ˈhɛləˌtɪzəm/ | n. | 奴役状态；奴隶制度 | A form of symbiosis in which one species is enslaved by another.  One organism is forced to perform tasks for another, typically involving providing food or shelter.|  Ant colonies sometimes practice helotism, enslaving other ant species. 蚂蚁群落有时会实行奴役制度，奴役其他种类的蚂蚁。|  slavery, servitude, bondage | freedom, liberty | noun
