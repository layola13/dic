appending:/əˈpɛndɪŋ/ | v. | 附加，添加 | Adding something to the end of something else, usually a document. |  例句：He is appending a signature to the contract. 他正在给合同附加签名。

例句：She is appending a postscript to the letter.  她在信的末尾添加了一段附言。

近义词：adding, attaching, affixing | 反义词: removing, detaching, deleting | 词性: 动词(Present Participle of append)

append:/əˈpɛnd/ | vt. | 附加，添加 | To add something to the end of something else, usually a document. | 例句：Please append your signature to the document. 请在文件上添加您的签名。

例句：The lawyer appended a codicil to the will. 律师在遗嘱上附加了一份附录。


近义词：add, attach, affix | 反义词: remove, detach, delete | 词性: 动词
