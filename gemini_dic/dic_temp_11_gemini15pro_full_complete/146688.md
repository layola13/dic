overhearing:/ˌoʊvərˈhɪrɪŋ/| n. |偷听；无意中听到|The act of hearing something unintentionally, usually a conversation. | I apologized for overhearing their private conversation. 我为无意中听到他们的私人谈话而道歉。|近义词：eavesdropping, listening in|反义词: ignoring, disregarding|词性:Noun (Gerund)
overhear:/ˌoʊvərˈhɪr/| v. |偷听；无意中听到|To hear (something) without the speaker's intention or knowledge. | I overheard them talking about the surprise party. 我无意中听到他们在谈论惊喜派对。|近义词：eavesdrop, listen in|反义词: ignore, disregard|词性:Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *overheren*, from Old English *oferhīeran*, from *ofer* ("over") + *hīeran* ("to hear").
