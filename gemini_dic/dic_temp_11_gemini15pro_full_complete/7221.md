anglicans:/ˈæŋɡlɪkənz/
noun
圣公会教徒
Members of the Church of England or other churches in communion with it.
Many Anglicans attend church regularly. 许多圣公会教徒定期去教堂。
synonyms: Episcopalians, Church of England members
antonyms: Catholics, Baptists, Methodists
