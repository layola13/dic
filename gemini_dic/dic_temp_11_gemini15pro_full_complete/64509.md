erosion:/ɪˈroʊʒən/ (US), /ɪˈrəʊʒən/ (UK)| n.|腐蚀，侵蚀|The gradual wearing away of something, especially of land or rock, by the action of wind, water, or other natural agents.  The erosion of the coastline is a serious problem.|The constant wind and rain caused significant erosion of the cliffs.  持续的风雨造成了悬崖的严重侵蚀。|近义词：corrosion, wearing away, abrasion, attrition|反义词:accretion, deposition|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *erosionem (nominative erosio), from erodere "to gnaw away," from ex- "out" + rodere "to gnaw."
