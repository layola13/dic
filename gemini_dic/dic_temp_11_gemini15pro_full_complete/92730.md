ichnology: /ɪkˈnɒlədʒi/ | n. | 生痕学；遗迹学 | The study of trace fossils, such as tracks, burrows, and other evidence of animal activity. | Example 1: Ichnology provides valuable insights into the behavior of extinct animals. 生痕学为了解已灭绝动物的行为提供了宝贵的见解。

Example 2: The ichnology of the site revealed a diverse community of dinosaurs.  该遗址的生痕学揭示了一个多样化的恐龙群落。 | Near synonyms:  paleoichnology, trace fossil study | Antonyms:  None | Noun
