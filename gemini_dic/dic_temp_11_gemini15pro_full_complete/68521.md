fancier:/ˈfænsiər/| adj. n.|更喜欢的；更昂贵的；更精致的；爱好者；饲养者|Someone who has a particular interest in or liking for something/more elaborate, sophisticated, or expensive than usual. |She's a real fancier of antique furniture. 她是一位真正的古董家具爱好者。|近义词：connoisseur, enthusiast, admirer, devotee |反义词:critic, detractor|adjective, noun


fancier:/ˈfænsiər/| n.|爱好者；饲养者|Someone who breeds or keeps a particular animal, especially a horse, dog, or other pet.|He is a fancier of pedigree dogs. 他是纯种狗的饲养者。|近义词：breeder, keeper |反义词: N/A|noun
