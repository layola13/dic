marriageable:/ˈmærɪdʒəbəl/
adjective
适婚的，可以结婚的
Suitable for marriage; of a suitable age for marriage.
She considered herself marriageable once she turned eighteen. 她认为自己十八岁就可以结婚了。
近义词:nubile, eligible
反义词:unmarriageable, ineligible
词性:adjective
