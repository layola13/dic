nonsalutations:/ˌnɒnsæljuˈteɪʃənz/
n.
非问候语；不打招呼
Absence of greetings or salutations.
He walked past his neighbor with a nonsalutation, a clear sign of their ongoing feud. 他走过邻居身边，没有打招呼，这清楚地表明了他们之间持续的争执。
近义词: no greeting, lack of acknowledgement
反义词: greetings, salutations
词性: noun


Etymology:
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "salutation" (from Latin "salutatio," meaning "greeting")
