jelly-like:/ˈdʒɛliˌlaɪk/
adjective
果冻状的；胶状的
Having a consistency similar to that of jelly; gelatinous.
The substance had a strange, jelly-like texture. 这种物质有一种奇怪的、果冻状的质地。
The alien's skin felt cold and jelly-like. 外星人的皮肤摸起来又冷又像果冻。
近义词:gelatinous, viscous, gummy
反义词:firm, solid, hard
词性:adjective

jelly:/ˈdʒɛli/ | n. | 果冻 | A sweet, clear food made from fruit juice, sugar, and gelatin. |  例句：I like strawberry jelly. 我喜欢草莓果冻。| 近义词：gelatin | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially "solid food")

Etymology:
"Jelly" comes from the Old French "gelee," meaning "frost, jelly," ultimately from Latin "gelare" meaning "to freeze."  "Like" comes from Old English "lic" and "gelic," meaning "having the same form, similar."  The suffix "-like" forms adjectives meaning "resembling, characteristic of."
