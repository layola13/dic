coyish:/ˈkɔɪɪʃ/ | adj. |害羞的，腼腆的，忸怩作态的 | Affectedly or teasingly shy or reserved. | She gave him a coyish smile. 她给了他一个害羞的微笑。 |  近义词: bashful, shy, demure, timid, reserved | 反义词: bold, forward, brazen, immodest | 词性:形容词

Etymology:
From coy + -ish.  Coy itself comes from Middle English *coi, from Old French coi (“quiet, still”), from Latin quietus.
