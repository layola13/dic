hamletize:/ˈhæmlɪtaɪz/| v. |使优柔寡断，使犹豫不决|To make or become indecisive or to overthink a situation, like Hamlet.  To behave in a way reminiscent of Hamlet, especially in being melancholic and indecisive.|He has a tendency to hamletize even the simplest decisions, like choosing what to have for lunch. 他即使在做最简单的决定，比如选择午餐吃什么时，也往往会犹豫不决。|Synonyms: dither, vacillate, waver, hesitate|Antonyms: decide, resolve, determine|verb

hamletizing:/ˈhæmlɪtaɪzɪŋ/| n. |优柔寡断，犹豫不决|The act of being indecisive or overthinking, like Hamlet.|His constant hamletizing drove his friends crazy. 他不断的犹豫不决让他的朋友们抓狂。|Synonyms: indecision, vacillation, hesitation|Antonyms: decision, resolution, determination|noun
