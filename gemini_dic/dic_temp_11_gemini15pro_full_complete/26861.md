cajole:/kəˈdʒoʊl/ /kəˈdʒəʊl/| v. |哄骗；劝诱|To persuade someone to do something by sustained coaxing or flattery.| He cajoled her into going to the party with him. 他哄骗她和他一起去参加派对。|Synonyms: coax, wheedle, persuade, entice|Antonyms: dissuade, deter, discourage|verb

cajole:/kəˈdʒoʊl/ /kəˈdʒəʊl/| v. |劝诱；诱骗|She cajoled him into buying her a new dress. 她劝诱他给她买了一件新裙子。|Synonyms:  wheedle, blandish, inveigle|Antonyms:  dissuade, deter |verb
