deceivingly:/dɪˈsiːvɪŋli/| adv.|欺骗性地，貌似地，虚假地| appearing to be one thing but actually being another| The cake looked deceivingly simple to make, but it actually required a lot of skill.  蛋糕看起来很容易做，但实际上需要很多技巧。|  The path was deceivingly long. 这条路看起来不长，实际上很长。|近义词：misleadingly, deceptively, falsely|反义词: genuinely, truly, honestly|词性:副词

词源:
From deceive (v.) + -ing + -ly.  Deceive comes from Old French decevoir, from Latin de- "down, away" + capere "to take."
