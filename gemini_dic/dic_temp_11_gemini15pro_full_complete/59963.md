edificatory:/ˈɛdɪfɪkətɔːri/
adjective
有教益的，启发的
Intended to instruct and improve morally or intellectually.
His sermons were always edificatory, leaving the congregation feeling inspired. 他的布道总是很有启发性，让会众感到鼓舞。
近义词:instructive, enlightening, educational, improving, uplifting
反义词:unhelpful, frivolous, demoralizing
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin aedificātōrius (“building up”), from Latin aedificō (“I build”), from aedēs (“building, temple”) + faciō (“I do, make”).
