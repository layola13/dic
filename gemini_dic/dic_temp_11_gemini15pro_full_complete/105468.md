killdeer:/ˈkɪlˌdɪr/ (US) /ˈkɪlˌdɪə(r)/ (UK)| n.|美洲啼鸻|A North American plover (Charadrius vociferus) known for its loud, piercing cries, often interpreted as "kill-deer," and its distinctive broken-wing display to distract predators from its nest.|例句：The killdeer feigned injury to lure us away from its chicks. 美洲啼鸻装作受伤的样子把我们从它的雏鸟身边引开。

例句：We heard the distinctive call of the killdeer echoing across the field. 我们听到美洲啼鸻独特的叫声在田野里回荡。|近义词：plover|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
