hardness:/ˈhɑːrdnəs/ | n. |硬度，坚硬，难度，艰苦，严厉，冷酷无情 |The state or quality of being hard. |The hardness of the diamond makes it ideal for cutting glass. 钻石的硬度使其成为切割玻璃的理想材料。|近义词：firmness, solidity, rigidity, toughness, difficulty |反义词：softness, flexibility, easiness |词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *heardnes, from heard "hard" + -nes, a suffix forming abstract nouns from adjectives.  The word "hard" traces back to Proto-Germanic *harduz, and ultimately to Proto-Indo-European *kratus, meaning "strong" or "powerful."
