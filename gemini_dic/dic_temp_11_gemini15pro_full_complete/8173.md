anti-darwinism:/ˌæntiˈdɑːrwɪnɪzəm/
n.
反达尔文主义
Opposition to Charles Darwin's theory of evolution by natural selection.
The resurgence of anti-Darwinism in some communities is concerning to scientists.  一些社区中反达尔文主义的复兴令科学家们担忧。
Creationism is a common form of anti-Darwinism.  神创论是反达尔文主义的一种常见形式。
近义词:creationism, intelligent design
反义词:Darwinism, evolutionism
词性:noun
