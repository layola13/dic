fulfilling:/fʊlˈfɪlɪŋ/ | adj. | 完成的，实现的 | carrying out or achieving something desired or intended | He played a fulfilling role in the project. 他在这个项目中扮演了一个重要的角色。| achieving, completing, realizing | failing, incomplete | adjective
