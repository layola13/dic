microphoning:/ˈmaɪkrəˌfoʊnɪŋ/| n.|话筒效应，啸叫|The amplified sound from a loudspeaker is picked up by a microphone and re-amplified, creating a loud, high-pitched squeal.  扩音器发出的声音被麦克风拾取并重新放大，产生响亮的高音啸叫。|例句：The band had to stop playing because of the terrible microphoning. 乐队不得不停止演奏，因为出现了严重的啸叫。

例句：The sound engineer quickly adjusted the levels to eliminate the microphoning. 音响工程师快速调整了音量以消除啸叫。|近义词：feedback, howl, squeal|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "silence") |词性:noun
