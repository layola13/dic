infraposition:/ˌɪnfrəpəˈzɪʃən/| n. |下置，下移，低位|The process or result of placing something in a lower position, or of a lower element displacing a higher one, especially in linguistics.  It refers to a specific type of metathesis where a phoneme or morpheme moves to a later position in a word.|Infraposition of the nasal consonant is a common feature of this dialect.  这个方言的鼻辅音下置是一个常见特征。|近义词：metathesis, transposition|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially "preposition" or "supraposition" depending on context) |词性:noun
