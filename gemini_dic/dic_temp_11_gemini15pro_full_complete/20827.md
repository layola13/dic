blastier:/ˈblæstiər/ | adj. | 更猛烈的，更爆炸性的 | More blasty; characterized by greater intensity of blasting. | The new fireworks were blastier than last year's. 今年的新烟花比去年的更猛烈。|  近义词: more explosive, more intense, stronger | 反义词: weaker, less intense, milder | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "blastier" is the comparative form of "blasty," which is derived from "blast" + "-y." "Blast" comes from Old Norse "blastr" meaning "blowing, blast."  "-y" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "full of."
