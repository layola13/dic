hothead:/ˈhɒthed/ (UK) /ˈhɑːthed/ (US)| n.| 鲁莽的人；性急的人；暴躁的人|A person who gets angry easily and acts rashly or without thinking.|例句：He's such a hothead; he always gets into fights. 他真是个暴脾气，总是跟人打架。

例句：Don't be a hothead and rush into this decision. 不要冲动，不要急于做出这个决定。

近义词：firebrand, spitfire, hotspur|反义词：calm person, patient person|词性: noun
