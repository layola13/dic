nodder:/ˈnɒdə(r) (British), ˈnɑːdər (American)| n.|点头者；应声虫|A person who nods, especially one who habitually nods in agreement or as a sign of drowsiness.|The speaker looked around the room, seeking confirmation from the nodders in the audience. 演讲者环顾房间，寻找听众中点头表示同意的人。|近义词：yes-man, sycophant|反义词：dissenter, objector|noun


nodder:/ˈnɒdə(r) (British), ˈnɑːdər (American)| n.|点头的人；瞌睡虫|A person who nods, especially habitually, either in agreement or from drowsiness.|He was such a nodder in meetings, always seeming to agree even when he didn't understand. 他在会议上总是点头，即使不明白也好像总是同意。|近义词：acquiescer, conformist|反义词：rebel, nonconformist|noun
