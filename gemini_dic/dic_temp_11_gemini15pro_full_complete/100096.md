interposing:/ˌɪntərˈpoʊzɪŋ/| v. adj.|插入，介入，干涉；居间的，插入的|placing or inserting between; intervening|He interposed himself between the two arguing men. 他介入到两个争吵的男人之间。|近义词：intervening, mediating, inserting|反义词: withdrawing, removing|词性:动词现在分词/形容词
Etymology: From Latin "interponere" (to place between), from "inter-" (between) + "ponere" (to put, place).
