noncommunications:/ˌnɑːnkəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃənz/
n.
lack of communication; failure to communicate
The project's failure was largely due to noncommunications between the departments. 该项目的失败很大程度上是由于部门之间缺乏沟通。
Misunderstandings can arise from noncommunications. 误解可能源于缺乏沟通。
近义词:silence, radio silence, lack of communication, no communication
反义词:communication, dialogue, exchange, interaction
词性:名词
