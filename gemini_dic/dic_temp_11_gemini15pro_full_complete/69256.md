feather:/ˈfeðər/ (US) /ˈfeðə(r)/ (UK)| n. |羽毛|One of the light, soft, fringed structures forming the external covering of the body of birds.|The bird preened its feathers.  这只鸟梳理它的羽毛。|Synonyms: plume, plumage|Antonyms: scale, fur|noun

feather:/ˈfeðər/ (US) /ˈfeðə(r)/ (UK)| v. |用羽毛装饰；长羽毛；使（桨）与水面平行|To cover or adorn with feathers; to turn (an oar) edgewise while lifting it from the water.|The dancer's costume was feathered with bright plumes.  舞者的服装饰有鲜艳的羽毛。|Synonyms: adorn, decorate, deck|Antonyms: strip, bare|verb
