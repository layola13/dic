congealments:/kənˈdʒiːlmənts/
n.
凝固物；冻结物
The process of a liquid changing to a solid or semi-solid state.  The result of something congealing.
The congealments of fat in the gravy were unappetizing.  肉汁里的脂肪凝块令人倒胃口。
近义词：coagulations, clots, solidifications
反义词：liquefactions, meltings, dissolutions
词性：名词
