discourse:/ˈdɪskɔːrs/ (US) /ˈdɪskɔːs/ (UK)| n. vi.| 谈话，演讲；论述；论文|A formal discussion of a subject in speech or writing.|The professor gave a discourse on the history of philosophy. 教授做了一个关于哲学史的演讲。|近义词：speech, lecture, discussion, conversation, treatise|反义词：silence, taciturnity|词性：名词/动词

discourse:/ˈdɪskɔːrs/ (US) /ˈdɪskɔːs/ (UK)| vi.| 谈论；讲述|To speak or write formally and at length.|He discoursed at length on the merits of organic farming. 他详细地讲述了有机农业的好处。|近义词：speak, talk, discuss, converse|反义词：be silent|词性：动词


词源:
From Middle French discours, from Late Latin discursus, past participle of discurrere (“to run about, run to and fro, digress”), from Latin dis- (“apart, in different directions”) + currere (“to run”).
