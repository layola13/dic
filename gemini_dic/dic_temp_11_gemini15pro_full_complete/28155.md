caplike:/ˈkæplaɪk/
adjective
帽状的；像帽子的
Resembling a cap in shape or form.
The fungus had a caplike structure. 这种真菌有一个帽状结构。
The cloud formation was distinctly caplike. 云的形状很明显像帽子。
Near synonyms: cap-shaped, hooded, pileate
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but consider) shapeless, amorphous
Word origin: cap + -like (suffix meaning "resembling")
