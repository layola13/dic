brick-walled:/ˈbrɪk wɔːld/
adj.
用砖墙围起来的；坚决反对的；毫无同情心的
Made of or enclosed by a brick wall; unyielding; unresponsive.
The garden was brick-walled.  这个花园用砖墙围了起来。
He met with a brick-walled refusal. 他遭到了断然的拒绝。
近义词:unyielding, inflexible, impassive, stony
反义词:yielding, flexible, sympathetic
