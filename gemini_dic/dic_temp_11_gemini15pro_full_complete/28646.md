caribou:/ˈkærɪbuː/ (US), /ˈkærɪbuː/ (UK)| n.|驯鹿，北美驯鹿|A large North American deer (Rangifer tarandus), related to the reindeer of Europe and Asia, found in arctic and subarctic regions.|例句：The caribou migrated south for the winter. 驯鹿冬季向南迁徙。

例句：Caribou are an important food source for many indigenous peoples. 驯鹿是许多原住民的重要食物来源。|近义词：reindeer|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


caribous:/ˈkærɪbuːz/ (US), /ˈkærɪbuːz/ (UK)| n.|驯鹿（复数）|Plural form of caribou, referring to multiple North American deer (Rangifer tarandus).|例句：We saw a herd of caribous grazing in the meadow. 我们看到一群驯鹿在草地上吃草。

例句：The caribous shed their antlers each year. 驯鹿每年都会脱落鹿角。|近义词：reindeer|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
