man-of-his-word:/ˌmæn əv hɪz ˈwɜːrd/ | n. | 言而有信的人；信守诺言的人 | A man who keeps his promises; a reliable and trustworthy person. | He's a man of his word; if he said he'd do it, he will. 他是个言而有信的人；如果他说他会做，他就一定会做。| 近义词：trustworthy person, reliable person, dependable person | 反义词：liar, dishonest person, unreliable person | 词性: 名词短语
