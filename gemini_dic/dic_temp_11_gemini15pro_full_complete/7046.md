annelids:/ˈænəlɪdz/
n.
环节动物
Segmented worms, characterized by their ringed bodies.
Earthworms and leeches are examples of annelids. 蚯蚓和水蛭是环节动物的例子。
近义词:segmented worms
反义词:None
词性:名词

annelid:/ˈænəlɪd/
n.
环节动物
A worm of the phylum Annelida, having a long segmented body.
The earthworm is a common annelid. 蚯蚓是一种常见的环节动物。
近义词:segmented worm
反义词:None
词性:名词
