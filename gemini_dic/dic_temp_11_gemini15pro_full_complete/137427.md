nontheistic:/ˌnɒnθiˈɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非有神论的 | Not believing in the existence of a god or gods. |  Many modern philosophers hold nontheistic worldviews. 许多现代哲学家持有非有神论的世界观。 | Some people describe themselves as nontheistic, preferring not to align with any particular religious label. 一些人将自己描述为非有神论者，不愿与任何特定的宗教标签保持一致。 | 近义词: atheistic, secular, unbelieving | 反义词: theistic, religious | 词性: adjective

Etymology: non- (not) + theistic (relating to theism)
