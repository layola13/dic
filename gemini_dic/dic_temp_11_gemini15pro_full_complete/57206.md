downbear:/ˈdaʊnˌbɛər/| v.|压制；压倒；击败；使沮丧|To overcome by superior force; overpower. To depress or dispirit.|The team downbore their opponents in the final match.  这支队伍在决赛中击败了他们的对手。 The constant criticism began to downbear her spirits.  不断的批评开始使她沮丧。|近义词：overwhelm, subdue, crush, defeat, depress, deject|反义词：uplift, encourage, support|词性:动词

词源:  "Downbear" is a compound verb formed from "down" (indicating downward direction or reduction) and "bear" (meaning to carry or endure).  It literally suggests bearing something down or suppressing it.
