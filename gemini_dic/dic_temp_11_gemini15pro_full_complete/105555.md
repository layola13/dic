kimono:/kɪˈmoʊnoʊ/
n.
和服
A long, loose robe with wide sleeves and tied with a sash, originally worn as a formal garment in Japan.
She wore a beautiful silk kimono to the tea ceremony. 她穿了一件漂亮的丝绸和服参加茶道仪式。
近义词: robe, garment
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps Western clothing)
