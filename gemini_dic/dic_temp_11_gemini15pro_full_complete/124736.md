mpc:/ɛmˌpiːˈsiː/ | n. | 媒体播放器中心; 多用途电脑; 模型预测控制 | Media Player Classic (a free and open-source media player); Multi-Purpose Computer; Model Predictive Control | Example: I use MPC to watch my movies. 我用MPC来看电影。 | Synonym: media player, computer, control system | Antonym: None | Noun

mpc:/ɛmˌpiːˈsiː/ | abbr. | 边际消费倾向 | Marginal Propensity to Consume (the proportion of an aggregate raise in pay that a consumer spends on the consumption of goods and services, as opposed to saving it) | Example: The MPC is an important factor in Keynesian economics. 边际消费倾向是凯恩斯主义经济学中的一个重要因素。 | Synonym: None | Antonym: MPS (marginal propensity to save) | Abbreviation
