antipoaching:/ˌæntiˈpoʊtʃɪŋ/| adj. n.|反盗猎的，反偷猎的；反盗猎|Relating to or directed against poaching, especially the illegal hunting of protected animals.|The antipoaching unit works tirelessly to protect endangered species. 反盗猎小组不知疲倦地工作以保护濒危物种。|近义词：anticrime, conservationist|反义词:pro-poaching (this term is rarely used, the opposite action is usually described more specifically, e.g., "supporting illegal hunting")|词性: 主要作形容词，也可作名词

antipoaching:/ˌæntiˈpoʊtʃɪŋ/| n.|反盗猎，反偷猎|The activity of preventing poaching, especially the illegal hunting of protected animals.|Antipoaching efforts have increased in recent years. 近年来反盗猎力度有所加大。|近义词：conservation, wildlife protection|反义词:poaching|词性:名词

Etymology:
"anti-" (prefix of Greek origin) meaning "against, opposite" + "poaching" (from Old French "pochier" meaning "to push, poke," later evolving to mean "to trespass for game")
