brooch:/broʊtʃ/ (US), /brəʊtʃ/ (UK)| n.|胸针，饰针|A decorative pin worn on clothing.|She wore a beautiful diamond brooch on her lapel. 她在外衣翻领上佩戴了一枚漂亮的钻石胸针。|近义词：pin, ornament, badge|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与其功能相反的物品，例如 zipper 拉链)|noun

brooch:/broʊtʃ/ (US), /brəʊtʃ/ (UK)| v.|用胸针别住|To fasten or decorate with a brooch.|She brooched the flower to her dress. 她用胸针把花别在衣服上。|近义词：pin, fasten|反义词: unpin, unfasten|verb
