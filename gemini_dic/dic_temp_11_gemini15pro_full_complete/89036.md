holmium:/ˈhəʊlmiəm/ | n. |钬|A chemical element, atomic number 67, a silvery-white metal of the lanthanide series. It is used in some lasers and magnets.|Holmium is used in certain types of lasers for medical and industrial applications. 钬被用于某些类型的激光器，用于医疗和工业应用。

Holmium oxide gives glass a pink color. 氧化钬使玻璃呈现粉红色。|近义词: (As an element, it doesn't have direct synonyms, but other lanthanides like dysprosium and erbium have similar properties.) |反义词: (None for a chemical element.)|词性:noun
