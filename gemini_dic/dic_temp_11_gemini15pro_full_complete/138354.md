noticing:/ˈnoʊtɪsɪŋ/| gerund, present participle|注意到，察觉到|The act of observing or becoming aware of something.|I'm noticing a change in his behavior. 我注意到他的行为有所改变。|近义词：observing, perceiving, seeing, realizing|反义词：ignoring, overlooking|v.
