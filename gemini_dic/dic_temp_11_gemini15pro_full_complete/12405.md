asides:/əˈsaɪdz/| adv. n.|旁白，顺便说一句，在旁边|Apart from the main subject; incidentally.  As a separate remark or aside. On or to one side.|例句：Asides from his work, he enjoys playing golf. 除了工作，他还喜欢打高尔夫球。|近义词：apart, besides, separately|反义词: included, together|词性:副词, 名词

asides:/əˈsaɪdz/| n.|（戏剧中的）旁白|A remark or passage by a character in a play that is intended to be heard by the audience but unheard by the other characters in the play.|例句：The actor delivered his asides with perfect timing. 这位演员在恰当的时机说出了他的旁白。|近义词：whispers, murmurs|反义词: dialogue, speech |词性:名词
