carpogenic:/ˌkɑːrpəˈdʒɛnɪk/
adjective
产果实的;结果实的
Relating to or involved in the production of fruit.
The carpogenic hyphae of the fungus are responsible for the development of the fruiting bodies.  这种真菌的产果菌丝负责子实体的发育。
Synonyms: fruiting, fructiferous
Antonyms: barren, sterile
词性: adjective
