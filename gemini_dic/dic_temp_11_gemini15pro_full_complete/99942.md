internship:/ˈɪntɜːrnʃɪp/ (US), /ˈɪntɜːnʃɪp/ (UK)| n. |实习; 实习期|A period of work experience offered by an organization for a limited period of time, often to students or recent graduates.|She had a summer internship at a law firm. 她暑期在一家律师事务所实习。|

|My internship at the museum was a valuable learning experience. 我在博物馆的实习是一次宝贵的学习经历。|
|Synonym: placement, traineeship, apprenticeship|
|Antonym: permanent employment|
|Noun|
