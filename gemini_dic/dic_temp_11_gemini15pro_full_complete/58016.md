drizzlier:/ˈdrɪzliər/ | adj. | 更毛毛雨的 | Comparative form of drizzly: characterized by more numerous or heavier droplets of drizzle. | It's much drizzlier today than yesterday. 今天比昨天毛毛雨更多了。| The drizzlier weather made the football game unpleasant to watch. 毛毛雨天气让足球比赛看起来很不舒服。| 近义词: rainier, wetter | 反义词: drier, sunnier | 词性: 形容词

词源: From drizzle + -ier (comparative suffix). Ultimately from Old English *dreosan "to fall."
