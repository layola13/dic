alloying:/əˈlɔɪɪŋ/
n.|合金化，熔合|The process of mixing two or more metals, or a metal and another element, to create a new material with enhanced properties.|例句：Alloying copper with tin creates bronze. 铜与锡熔合形成青铜。|近义词：blending, mixing, combining|反义词:separating, purifying
词性:名词
