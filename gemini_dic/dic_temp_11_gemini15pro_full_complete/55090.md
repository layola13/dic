disrespecter:/ˌdɪsrɪˈspɛktər/
n.
不尊重者，失礼的人
Someone who is disrespectful; a person who shows a lack of respect.
He was labeled a disrespecter of tradition by the elders.  他被长辈们称为不尊重传统的人。
The disrespecter was asked to leave the ceremony.  那个失礼的人被要求离开仪式。
近义词:insulter, offender, scoffer
反义词: admirer, respecter
