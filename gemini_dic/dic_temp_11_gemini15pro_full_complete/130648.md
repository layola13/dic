The nonadorners in the group opted for practical clothing rather than fashionable attire.  小组里不加装饰的人选择了实用性服装而不是时尚的服装。 | 近义词: minimalists, purists, ascetics | 反义词: decorators, embellishers, maximalists | 词性: noun (plural)
