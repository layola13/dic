marvellous:/ˈmɑːrvələs/ (US) /ˈmɑːvələs/ (UK)| adj.|绝妙的，不可思议的，非凡的|Causing wonder; extraordinary.  Inspiring great admiration, astonishment, or wonder.|The sunset last night was marvellous. 昨晚的日落美极了。|Synonyms: wonderful, amazing, fantastic, incredible, superb, splendid, excellent, outstanding|Antonyms: awful, terrible, dreadful, horrible, unpleasant|adjective
