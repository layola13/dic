overcompress:/ˌoʊvər kəmˈprɛs/| v.|过度压缩|To compress something excessively, beyond what is optimal or desirable.  It often results in a loss of quality, especially in audio and image files.|The photographer overcompressed the image, resulting in a noticeable loss of detail.|Synonyms: overcondense, squeeze, compact excessively|Antonyms: expand, decompress|verb
