Etymology->
Derived from the initialism HTML, which stands for HyperText Markup Language. The 's is the possessive suffix in English.  Hypertext refers to text displayed on a computer that has links to other text that can be accessed immediately by a user.  Markup Language refers to a system for annotating a document in a way distinguishable from the text.
