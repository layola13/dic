off-air:/ˌɔːf ˈɛər/ /ˌɑːf ˈer/| adj.|停播的；未播出的；私下的|Not being broadcast on television or radio; happening or existing away from the microphones or cameras, and therefore not being broadcast or recorded.|The interview was conducted off-air.  这次采访是在私下进行的。|The president made some off-air remarks that were later leaked to the press. 总统私下发表了一些言论，后来被泄露给了媒体。|近义词：unbroadcast, private, unofficial|反义词：on-air, broadcast, public|词性:形容词

词源:  "off" (meaning away from or not operating) + "air" (referring to broadcasting).  The term originated in the context of radio broadcasting.
