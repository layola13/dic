antischolastic:/ˌænti-skəˈlæstɪk/
adjective
反学校的；反对学校教育的；非学院派的
opposed to or unsuitable for school or scholars; not scholarly.
His antischolastic attitude made him drop out of college. 他反学校的态度使他从大学辍学。
近义词:unacademic, nonscholarly, anti-intellectual
反义词:scholarly, academic, intellectual
词性:adjective


antischolastic:/ˌænti-skəˈlæstɪk/
noun
反学校教育者
a person who is opposed to school or scholarly pursuits.
He was labeled an antischolastic for his critical views on the education system.  他因对教育体系的批判性观点而被贴上了反学校教育者的标签。
近义词:anti-intellectual
反义词:scholar, academic
词性:noun
