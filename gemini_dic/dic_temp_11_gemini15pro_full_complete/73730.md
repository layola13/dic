forkers:/ˈfɔːrkərz/
n.
分叉者，使用分叉工具的人, (尤指)餐叉使用者, (开源软件项目的)分支开发者
People who use forks, especially for eating; developers who create a branch of an open-source software project.
The forkers enjoyed their salad with gusto.  分叉者津津有味地享用着他们的沙拉。
The forkers created a new branch of the Linux kernel. 分支开发者创建了Linux内核的一个新分支。
近义词: users, developers, contributors
反义词: (无明确的反义词)
词性: 名词
