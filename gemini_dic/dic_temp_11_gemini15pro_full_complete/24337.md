brimfulness:/ˈbrɪmfʊlnəs/
n.
充满；盈满

Her heart was filled with brimfulness at the sight of her newborn baby.  看到她刚出生的婴儿，她的心中充满了喜悦。
Near synonyms: fullness, abundance, overflowing, repletion
Near antonyms: emptiness, lack, deficiency, scarcity
