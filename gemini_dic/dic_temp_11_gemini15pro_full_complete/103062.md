jeniquen: /ˈdʒɛnɪkən/
n.
jeniquen fiber, hard fiber
A hard, strong leaf fiber obtained from the henequen plant (Agave fourcroydes), used for rope, twine, and other products.
Jeniquen is primarily used for making rope due to its strength and durability.  剑麻主要用于制造绳索，因为它强度高且耐用。
The ship's ropes were made of jeniquen.  这艘船的绳索是用剑麻制成的。
近义词: sisal, hemp
反义词: (No direct antonym for a plant fiber. Could consider materials with opposite qualities like silk or cotton for softness.)
词性: noun
