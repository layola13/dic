oboval:/ˈoʊboʊvəl/ | adj. | 倒卵形的；卵圆形的 | Having a shape resembling an egg with the broader end at the apex. | The leaf of the plant is oboval.  这种植物的叶子是倒卵形的。| The petals of the flower are oboval in shape.  这朵花的花瓣呈倒卵形。| 近义词: ovate, elliptical | 反义词: - | 词性: adjective

oboval:/ˈoʊboʊvəl/ | n. | 倒卵形 | An oboval shape. | The artist used obovals in his abstract painting.  这位艺术家在他的抽象画中使用了倒卵形。 | The cross-section of the fruit is an oboval.  这种水果的横截面是倒卵形的。 | 近义词: oval, ellipse | 反义词: - | 词性: noun
