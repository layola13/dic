blemish:/ˈblɛmɪʃ/ | n. | 瑕疵，缺点，污点 | A small mark or flaw that spoils the appearance of something. | The painting had a small blemish in the corner. 这幅画的角落里有一个小瑕疵。| synonyms: flaw, defect, imperfection, mark, spot, stain | antonyms: perfection, flawlessness | noun

blemish:/ˈblɛmɪʃ/ | vt. | 损害，玷污 | To spoil the appearance of something. | The scandal blemished his reputation.  这桩丑闻玷污了他的名誉。| synonyms: tarnish, mar, spoil, damage, impair | antonyms: enhance, improve, perfect | verb
