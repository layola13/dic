hole-in-corner:/ˌhəʊl ɪn ˈkɔːnə(r)/ /ˌhoʊl ɪn ˈkɔːrnər/
noun
秘密的地方；隐蔽的角落
A secret or secluded place.
They met in a hole-in-corner somewhere in the city. 他们在城市里的某个隐蔽角落碰面。
synonyms: hideaway, hideout, retreat, refuge, sanctuary
antonyms: public place, open area
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the literal sense of a small, out-of-the-way corner, perhaps one with a hole in the wall for observation or escape.
