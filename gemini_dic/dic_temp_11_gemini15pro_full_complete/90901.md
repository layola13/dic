hunting:/ˈhʌntɪŋ/| n. & adj.|狩猎，打猎；寻找|The act of chasing and killing wild animals for food or sport; the act of searching for something.|

例句：
He enjoys hunting deer in the fall.  他喜欢在秋天猎鹿。

They went hunting for mushrooms in the woods. 他们在树林里寻找蘑菇。


近义词：shooting, stalking, pursuing, searching, seeking|
反义词：preserving, protecting, conserving|
词性：noun, adjective|


hunting:/ˈhʌntɪŋ/| adj.|狩猎的|Used for or relating to hunting.|

例句：
He wore his hunting boots. 他穿着他的猎靴。

She has a hunting license. 她有狩猎许可证。

近义词：venatic|
反义词：N/A|
词性：adjective|


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *huntian, from hunta (“hunter”), of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to Old Norse hund (“dog”).
