intermenstrual:/ˌɪntəˈmɛnstruəl/ | adj. |月经间的，两次月经之间的|Occurring between menstrual periods.  Relating to the time between two consecutive menstrual periods.|例句：She experienced some intermenstrual bleeding. 她经历了一些经期之间的出血。

例句：Intermenstrual pain can be a symptom of various conditions. 经期间疼痛可能是各种疾病的症状。|近义词：intermenstruum |反义词: N/A |词性: adjective


Etymology:
From inter- + menstrual.  "Inter-" is a Latin prefix meaning "between," and "menstrual" relates to menstruation.
