forte:/ˈfɔːrteɪ/ (UK) /ˈfɔːrteɪ/ (US)| n. |特长，强项|Something at which someone excels; a strong point; a speciality.|Singing is her forte. 唱歌是她的强项。|近义词：strength, talent, skill, aptitude, gift|反义词：weakness, foible|noun

forte:/ˈfɔːrteɪ/ (UK) /ˈfɔːrˌteɪ/ (US)| adj. |(音乐)强音的，响亮的|(of a musical passage) to be performed loudly.|The piece ends with a forte chord. 这首曲子以一个强音和弦结束。|近义词：loud, strong|反义词：piano, soft|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *forte* "strong, loud," from Latin *fortis*. The musical sense is a shortening of Italian *forte* "loudly."
