invisibly:/ɪnˈvɪzəbli/| adv.|无形地，看不见地|In a way that cannot be seen.|The magician invisibly switched the cards. 魔术师偷偷地把牌换了。|近义词：unseenly, imperceptibly|反义词：visibly, noticeably|词性:副词
Etymology: From in- (not) + visible (able to be seen) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *invisibilis*.
