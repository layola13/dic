agencies:/ˈeɪdʒənsiz/| n.|代理机构，机构，部门|A business or organization that provides a particular service, typically one that involves organizing transactions between two other parties.|The travel agency booked our flights and hotels. 旅行社预订了我们的航班和酒店。|synonyms: bureaus, offices, organizations, departments|antonyms: individuals, freelancers|noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *agenţia, from Latin agens, present participle of agere "to do, drive, act, perform,"
