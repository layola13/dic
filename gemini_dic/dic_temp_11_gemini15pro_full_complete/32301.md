chiff:/ʃɪf/ | n. | 薄纱，雪纺绸 | A sheer, lightweight fabric, typically made of silk or nylon. | The dress was made of a delicate chiff. 这件连衣裙是用精致的雪纺绸制成的。 | 近义词: gauze, voile, georgette | 反义词: (No direct antonym, consider thick fabrics like: velvet, corduroy) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French *chiffon*, meaning "rag, cloth," itself from the verb *chiffer*, meaning "to crumple, to rumple."
