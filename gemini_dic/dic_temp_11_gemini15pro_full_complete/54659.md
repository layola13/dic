disobeyer:/ˌdɪsəˈbeɪər/
noun
不服从者，违抗者
A person who disobeys rules or commands.
The disobeyer was punished for breaking the law.  这个不服从者因违法而受到惩罚。
The disobeyer refused to follow the teacher's instructions.  这个不服从者拒绝听从老师的指示。
Synonyms:  rebel, nonconformist, insurgent, deviant, offender
Antonyms:  conformist, follower, obedient person
