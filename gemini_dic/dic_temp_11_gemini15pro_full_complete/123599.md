montealban:/ˌmɒnteɪˈælbɑːn/ | n. | 蒙特阿尔班 |  A large pre-Columbian archaeological site in the Santa Cruz Xoxocotlán Municipality in the southern Mexican state of Oaxaca. It is located on a low mountainous range rising above the plain in the central sections of the Valley of Oaxaca where the latter is joined by the smaller valleys of Etla and Tlacolula. | We visited the ruins of Monte Albán during our trip to Oaxaca. 我们在 Oaxaca 旅行期间参观了蒙特阿尔班遗址。| Near Antonyms: Modern City, Contemporary Settlement | Far Antonyms:  Empty Field, Uninhabited Area | Noun
