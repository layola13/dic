nonlaminating:/ˌnɒnˈlæmɪneɪtɪŋ/
| adj. |非层压的，非叠层的 |Not forming or tending to form laminae or layers.  It describes something that doesn't separate or peel into thin layers.|This paint has a nonlaminating formula, so it won't peel off over time. 这种油漆具有非层压配方，因此不会随着时间的推移而剥落。|Synonym: nonlayered, monolithic |Antonym: laminated, layered
|adjective|
