humorize:/ˈhjuːməraɪz/ (US) /ˈhjuːməraɪz/ (UK)| v. |迎合；迁就；顺应|To comply with the whims or caprices of (someone) to keep them happy or contented.|例句：I had to humorize my boss by pretending to agree with his ridiculous plan. 我不得不迎合我的老板，假装同意他那荒谬的计划。

例句：She humorized her little brother by playing his silly game. 她陪她弟弟玩他那愚蠢的游戏来迁就他。


近义词：indulge, pander to, appease, accommodate|反义词：oppose, contradict, confront|词性：动词
