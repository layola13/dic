acceptably:/əkˈsɛptəbli/
adverb|可接受地，尚可地|in a way that is satisfactory and able to be agreed to or approved of.|Her work was deemed acceptably complete.|近义词：satisfactorily, adequately, tolerably|反义词：unacceptably, poorly, inadequately|词性: 副词
Etymology: From 'acceptable' + '-ly'. 'Acceptable' comes from Late Middle English: from late Latin acceptabilis, from acceptare ‘accept’, from ad- ‘to’ + capere ‘take’.
