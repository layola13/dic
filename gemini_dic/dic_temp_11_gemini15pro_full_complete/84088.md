half-ingeniousness:/ˌhæf ˌɪnˈdʒiːniəsnəs/
|n.|
|半灵巧；略微的机智|
|A state of being somewhat ingenious, possessing a moderate degree of cleverness or originality.|
|His solution showed a certain half-ingeniousness, though it ultimately didn't work.|他的解决方案显示出一定的半灵巧，虽然最终没有奏效。|
|partial ingenuity, slight cleverness, limited inventiveness|
|stupidity, dullness, lack of imagination|
|noun|
