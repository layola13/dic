lusk:/lʌsk/
noun
潜伏；躲藏；闲逛
A state of inactivity or idleness, often in a hidden or secluded place.
The leopard lurked in the lusk, waiting to ambush its prey. 豹子潜伏在灌木丛中，等待伏击猎物。
近义词：skulk, loiter, idle, loaf
反义词：work, toil, labor
词性:n.

lusk:/lʌsk/
verb
潜伏；躲藏；闲逛
To lie hidden or concealed, typically with a sinister or malicious purpose; to be idle or inactive.
He lusked in the shadows, hoping to avoid detection. 他躲在阴影里，希望能避开侦查。
近义词：skulk, loiter, idle, loaf
反义词：work, toil, labor
词性:v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lusken*, probably from a Scandinavian source (compare Norwegian *luska* “to listen secretly,” Swedish *lursk* “sly, cunning”).  Ultimately of uncertain origin.
