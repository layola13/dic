bescumber:/bɪˈskʌmbər/
verb
阻碍，妨碍；拖累
To encumber or weigh down; to burden or overload.
The hiker was bescumbered by a heavy backpack.  这个徒步旅行者被一个沉重的背包拖累了。
He was bescumbered with debts and responsibilities. 他被债务和责任压得喘不过气来。
近义词: encumber, burden, overload, hamper
反义词: unburden, relieve, assist
词性: verb
