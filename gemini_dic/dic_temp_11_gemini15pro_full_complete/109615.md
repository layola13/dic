letterbox:/ˈletərˌbɒks/ (UK), /ˈletərˌbɑːksɪz/ (US)| n.|邮箱（单数）|A receptacle, often attached to a door or wall, for the delivery of mail.|I checked my letterbox for any mail.|Synonyms: mailbox, postbox|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "letter" + "box."  "Letter" referring to written correspondence, and "box" referring to a container.  The compound word simply denotes a box for letters.
