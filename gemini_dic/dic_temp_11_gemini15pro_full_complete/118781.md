metallophone:/ˈmɛtəl.ə.foʊn/ (US), /məˈtæl.ə.fəʊn/ (UK)| n.|金属乐器，金属板琴|A musical instrument with metal bars of different lengths that are struck with hammers to produce different notes.|The children enjoyed playing the metallophone in music class. 孩子们喜欢在音乐课上演奏金属板琴。|Synonym: glockenspiel, xylophone|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "silent instrument")|Noun
