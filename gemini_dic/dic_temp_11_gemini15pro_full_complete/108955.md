leeriness:/ˈlɪərinəs/ | n. | 警惕；谨慎；怀疑；迟疑 | a feeling of distrust or suspicion; a reluctance to do something |  He regarded the new proposal with leeriness. 他对新提议持谨慎态度。 | 近义词: suspicion, distrust, wariness, caution, skepticism | 反义词: trust, confidence, eagerness | 词性: 名词

词源: From *leery* (suspicious, wary) + *-ness* (suffix forming nouns of quality or state).  *Leery* likely comes from a dialectal variant of *lary* (wary, cautious), ultimately of uncertain origin.
