cauker:/ˈkɔːkər/| n. |补鞋匠，修鞋匠|A person who mends or makes shoes.|The old cauker sat in his shop, surrounded by leather and tools. 老补鞋匠坐在他的店里，周围都是皮革和工具。|例句：He took his worn boots to the local cauker. 他把破旧的靴子拿到当地的补鞋匠那里。
The cauker repaired the sole of my shoe expertly. 补鞋匠熟练地修补了我鞋底。|近义词：cobbler, shoemaker, shoe repairer|反义词:|n.
