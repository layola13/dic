lugubriousness:/luːˈɡuːbriəsnəs/ | n. | 悲哀；忧郁 | The state of being mournful or gloomy. |  The lugubriousness of the funeral was palpable. 葬礼上的悲哀是显而易见的。| synonyms: gloominess, melancholy, sadness, sorrow | antonyms: cheerfulness, happiness, joy, lightheartedness | noun

lugubrious:/luːˈɡuːbriəs/ | adj. | 悲哀的；忧郁的 | Looking or sounding sad and dismal. | He had a lugubrious expression on his face. 他脸上带着忧郁的表情。| synonyms: mournful, gloomy, melancholic, sorrowful | antonyms: cheerful, happy, joyful, lighthearted | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *lūgubris* ("mournful, doleful"), from *lūgēre* ("to mourn").
