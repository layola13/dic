overhumanizing:/ˌoʊvərˈhjuːmənaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度人性化 | Attributing human characteristics, emotions, or motivations to non-human entities or concepts, often to an excessive or inappropriate degree.  | Example: Overhumanizing pets can lead to unrealistic expectations of their behavior.  过度人性化宠物会导致对它们行为的不切实际的期望。 | Example:  He was overhumanizing the AI, believing it had genuine feelings. 他过度人性化了人工智能，认为它有真正的感情。 |  Near Synonyms: anthropomorphizing, personifying | Antonyms: objectifying, dehumanizing | Word Type: Adjective, derived from humanize


overhumanize:/ˌoʊvərˈhjuːmənaɪz/ | v. | 过度人性化 | To attribute human characteristics, emotions, or motivations to non-human entities or concepts, often to an excessive or inappropriate degree. | Example:  Some people overhumanize their cars, giving them names and talking to them. 有些人过度人性化他们的汽车，给它们起名字并与它们交谈。 | Example:  Be careful not to overhumanize artificial intelligence; it's just a tool. 注意不要过度人性化人工智能；它只是一个工具。 | Near Synonyms: anthropomorphize, personify | Antonyms: objectify, dehumanize | Word Type: Verb, derived from humanize
