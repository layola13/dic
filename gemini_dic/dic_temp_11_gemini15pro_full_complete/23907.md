breakers:/ˈbreɪkərz/| n.|破碎者，断路器，浪花|a person or thing that breaks something. waves breaking on the shore.|The breakers crashed against the rocks. 海浪拍打着岩石。|synonyms: waves, surf, rollers|antonyms: calm, stillness|noun

breakers:/ˈbreɪkərz/| n.|断路器|a switch that automatically interrupts an electric circuit to prevent excessive current flow.|The circuit breakers tripped and the power went out.  断路器跳闸，电源断电了。|synonyms: fuse, interrupter|antonyms: conductor|noun
