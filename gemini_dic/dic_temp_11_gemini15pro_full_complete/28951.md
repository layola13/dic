carry-away:/ˈkæri əˈweɪ/ | adj.| 忘乎所以的，被…冲昏头脑的；过于兴奋的|  Overcome by emotion; transported with excitement or enthusiasm. |  The music carried her away.  音乐让她忘乎所以。| 近义词:  ecstatic, enraptured, overwhelmed | 反义词:  calm, composed, restrained | 词性:形容词

carry away:/ˈkæri əˈweɪ/ | v.|带走，拿走；使失去自制力| To remove something or someone from a place; to cause someone to lose control of their emotions.| The flood carried away the bridge. 洪水冲走了桥梁。 The crowd was carried away by his speech.  群众被他的演讲所感动，变得忘乎所以。| 近义词:  transport, sweep away, enrapture | 反义词:  calm, subdue | 词性:动词


词源: From the literal sense of "to carry something away" (from its original location), the phrase developed a figurative meaning of being "carried away" by strong emotions.
