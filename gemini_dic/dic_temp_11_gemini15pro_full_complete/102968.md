jealousy: /ˈdʒeləsi/ | n. | 妒忌，嫉妒 | A feeling of resentment against someone because of that person's rivalry, success, or advantages. | She was consumed by jealousy when she saw her friend's new car. 她看到朋友的新车后充满了嫉妒。 | 近义词: envy, resentment, covetousness | 反义词: admiration, goodwill, generosity | 词性: 名词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French jalousie, from jaloux ‘jealous’, from jalouser ‘be jealous of’, based on Greek zelos ‘emulation, zeal’.
