osteoporoses: /ˌɒstiəʊpəˈroʊsiːz/ | n. | 骨质疏松症 | A medical condition in which the bones become brittle and fragile from loss of tissue, typically as a result of hormonal changes, or deficiency of calcium or vitamin D. |  Osteoporosis is a serious health concern, especially for older women. 骨质疏松症是一个严重的健康问题，尤其是对老年妇女而言。| osteoporosis is often called the "silent disease" because bone loss occurs without symptoms. 骨质疏松症常被称为“沉默的疾病”，因为骨质流失发生时没有症状。 | 近义词: bone thinning, osteopenia | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but healthy bone density can be considered) | 词性: noun
