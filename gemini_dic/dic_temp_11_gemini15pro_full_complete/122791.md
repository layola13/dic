moldable:/ˈmoʊldəbl/| adj.|可塑的，易成型的|Able to be molded or shaped.  Something that is pliable and can be easily formed into a different shape.|The clay was soft and moldable, perfect for the pottery class.|近义词: pliable, ductile, malleable, plastic, shapeable|反义词: rigid, inflexible, stiff, unyielding|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From mold (v.) + -able.  "Mold" comes from Old French "molde," ultimately from Latin "modulus" (measure, standard).
