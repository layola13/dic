cleaner:/ˈkliːnər/ (US) /ˈkliːnə(r)/ (UK)| n. adj.|清洁工，清洁剂，清洁器|A person or thing that cleans something.  Something used for cleaning, such as a chemical or a device.|The office hired a new cleaner. 办公室雇佣了一名新的清洁工。|Synonyms: cleanser, detergent, purifier, janitor, custodian|Antonyms: polluter, contaminant|noun

cleaner:/ˈkliːnər/ (US) /ˈkliːnə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更干净的，更清洁的|Comparative form of "clean."|This room is cleaner than the last one. 这个房间比上一个房间更干净。|Synonyms: tidier, purer|Antonyms: dirtier, messier|adjective
