no-dice:/ˌnoʊ ˈdaɪs/| idiom |不行；没门；拒绝| An informal expression indicating refusal or failure. It originates from gambling, where "dice" are used, and "no dice" signifies an unsuccessful roll.| "I asked for an extension on the deadline, but it was no dice." 我要求延长截止日期，但被拒绝了。 | 近义词: no way, no go, not a chance, out of the question| 反义词: okay, sure, certainly | idiom
