distillment:/dɪˈstɪlmənt/ | n. | 蒸馏；蒸馏物；精华；提炼 | The process of distilling a liquid; something obtained by distilling.  The distillment of wisdom from experience. |  The distillment of whiskey takes time and precision.  威士忌的蒸馏需要时间和精确度。  The book is a distillment of years of research. 这本书是多年研究的结晶。 | synonyms: distillation, essence, extract, concentrate | antonyms:  dilution, admixture | noun
