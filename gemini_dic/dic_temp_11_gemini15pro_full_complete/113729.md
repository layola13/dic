macrodiagonal:/ˌmækroʊdaɪˈæɡənəl/ | n. | 主对角线; 长对角线 |The main diagonal of a matrix, extending from the top left to the bottom right. In a multidimensional array, it refers to the longest diagonal. | The macrodiagonal of this 3x3 matrix contains the values 1, 5, and 9.  这个 3x3 矩阵的主对角线包含值 1、5 和 9。| synonym: main diagonal, principal diagonal | antonym: antidiagonal, counter-diagonal | noun

macrodiagonal:/ˌmækroʊdaɪˈæɡənəl/ | adj. | 主对角线的; 长对角线的 | Relating to or located on the main diagonal of a matrix or the longest diagonal of a multidimensional array. |  The macrodiagonal elements of the tensor are of particular interest.  该张量的长对角线元素特别令人感兴趣。 | synonym: main diagonal, principal diagonal | antonym: antidiagonal, counter-diagonal | adjective
