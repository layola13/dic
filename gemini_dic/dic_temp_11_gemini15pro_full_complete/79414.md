glinting:/ˈɡlɪntɪŋ/| adj.|闪烁的，闪光的|Giving off or reflecting brief flashes of light; sparkling.|The sun was glinting off the water. 太阳在水面上闪闪发光。|Synonyms: sparkling, gleaming, flashing, shimmering, twinkling|Antonyms: dull, matte|adjective
