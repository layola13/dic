decentralizer:/diːˈsɛntrəlaɪzər/ | n. | 去中心化者，分散化者 |  A person or thing that decentralizes something.  A person or thing that distributes power or control away from a central authority. | The company acted as a decentralizer, empowering regional offices.  该公司充当了权力下放的角色，赋予了区域办事处权力。 | Synonyms: distributor, disperser, deconcentrator | Antonyms: centralizer, concentrator | noun


decentralize:/diːˈsɛntrəlaɪz/ | v. | 去中心化，分散 | To distribute the administrative powers or functions of (a central authority) over a less concentrated area. To reorganize (a government, industry, etc) so that activities and the people taking part in them are spread over a wider area. | The company decided to decentralize its operations to improve efficiency.  公司决定分散运营以提高效率。  They decentralized the government by giving more power to local authorities.  他们通过赋予地方政府更多权力来分散政府权力。 | Synonyms: distribute, disperse, deconcentrate | Antonyms: centralize, concentrate | verb
