enantiopathic:/ɪˌnæntiəˈpæθɪk/
adjective
对映疗法的；对抗疗法的
Treating disease with drugs that produce symptoms opposite to those of the disease.  Based on the idea that opposites cure opposites.
Enantiopathic medicine focuses on treating symptoms. 对映疗法注重治疗症状。
Homeopathy is considered the opposite of enantiopathic medicine. 顺势疗法被认为是与对映疗法相反的医学。
近义词: allopathic, conventional
反义词: homeopathic
词性: adjective
