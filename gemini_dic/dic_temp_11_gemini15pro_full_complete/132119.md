noncrusading:/ˌnɑːnkrʊˈseɪdɪŋ/
adjective
非改革的，不热衷改革的
Not engaged in or advocating a crusade; not zealous or reformist.
He took a noncrusading approach to the issue, preferring quiet diplomacy. 他对这个问题采取了非改革的方式，更喜欢安静的外交。
non-interventionist, moderate, pacifist
crusading, zealous, militant
adjective
