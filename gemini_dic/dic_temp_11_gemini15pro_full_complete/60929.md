elevate: /ˈɛləveɪt/ (US) /ˈɛlɪveɪt/ (UK)| v. |提升；举起；提高；振奋|To raise to a higher position, rank, or level; to improve morally or intellectually.|The platform elevated the speaker above the crowd.  平台使演讲者高出人群。|The music elevated her spirits. 音乐振奋了她的精神。|近义词：raise, lift, boost, uplift, enhance|反义词: lower, depress, degrade|词性:动词

词源: From Latin *elevatus*, past participle of elevare (“to lift up”), from e- (“out, up”) + levare (“to lift”).
