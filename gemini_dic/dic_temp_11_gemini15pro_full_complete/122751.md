moisture:/ˈmɔɪstʃər/ (US), /ˈmɔɪstʃə(r)/ (UK)| n.|水分，湿气|Water or other liquid diffused in a small quantity as vapor, within a solid, or condensed on a surface.|The air was thick with moisture. 空气中充满了湿气。|Synonyms: dampness, humidity, wetness|Antonyms: dryness, aridity|Noun
