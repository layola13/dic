abbasids:/əˈbæsɪdz/
n.
阿拔斯王朝
The Abbasids were a dynasty of caliphs who ruled a vast empire in the Middle East and North Africa.
阿拔斯王朝是统治中东和北非广大帝国的哈里发王朝。
The Abbasid caliphate marked a golden age of Islamic scholarship.
阿拔斯哈里发国标志着伊斯兰学术的黄金时代。
近义词:Abbasid dynasty
