nonextinguished:/ˌnɒnɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃt/
adjective
未熄灭的，未扑灭的
Not extinguished; still burning or alight.
The nonextinguished embers still glowed in the fireplace. 壁炉里未熄灭的余烬仍在发光。
The firefighters worked tirelessly to put out the nonextinguished fire. 消防员不知疲倦地扑灭着未熄灭的大火。
Synonyms: burning, alight, unextinguished, smoldering
Antonyms: extinguished, out, quenched
词性: adjective
