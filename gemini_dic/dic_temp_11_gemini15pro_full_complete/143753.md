outmanoeuvre:/ˌaʊtməˈnuːvər/ /ˌaʊtməˈnuːvər/
verb
智胜，以策略取胜
to defeat someone by being more clever or skillful in your plans and methods
例句：The company outmanoeuvred its competitors by launching a new product line first.  这家公司通过率先推出新的产品线，智胜了竞争对手。

例句：In the negotiations, she skillfully outmanoeuvred her opponents. 在谈判中，她巧妙地战胜了对手。
近义词：outwit, outsmart, circumvent, surpass
反义词：be outwitted, be outsmarted
词性：v.
