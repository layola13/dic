overimpressionably:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈprɛʃənəbli/
adverb
过度敏感地，易受影响地
easily impressed; excessively susceptible to impressions.
He reacted overimpressionably to the criticism, bursting into tears. 他对批评反应过度敏感，突然哭了起来。
Near synonyms: sensitively, impressionably, susceptibly
Near antonyms: impassively, stoically, unfeelingly
词性: 副词
