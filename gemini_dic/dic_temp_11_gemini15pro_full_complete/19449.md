bicrural: /baɪˈkruːrəl/
adjective
两足动物的；用两条腿的
Relating to or involving two legs; having two legs.
Bipedal locomotion is characteristic of bicrural animals.  两足运动是两足动物的特征。
The bicrural posture of humans distinguishes them from most other mammals. 人类的两足站立姿势将他们与大多数其他哺乳动物区分开来。
近义词: bipedal, two-legged
反义词: quadrupedal, four-legged
词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin *bicrūrālis, from bi- (“two”) + crūs (“leg”) + -ālis (adjectival suffix).
