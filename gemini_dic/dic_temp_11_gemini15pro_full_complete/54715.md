disownment:/dɪsˈoʊnmənt/
n.|断绝关系，否认，抛弃|The act of refusing to acknowledge or accept someone or something as one's own.| He announced the disownment of his son.  他宣布与儿子断绝关系。|Synonyms: rejection, repudiation, renunciation, denial, abandonment|Antonyms: acceptance, acknowledgment, embrace, recognition|noun

disownment:/dɪsˈoʊnmənt/
n.|否认，不承认|The act of denying responsibility for or connection with something.|The government's disownment of the report caused a scandal. 政府否认该报告引起了轩然大波。|Synonyms: denial, disclaimer, repudiation|Antonyms: acceptance, acknowledgment, admission|noun
