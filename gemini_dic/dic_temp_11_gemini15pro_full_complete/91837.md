hypergrammatical:/ˌhaɪpərɡrəˈmætɪkl/| adj.|过度语法化的，过度规则化的|Excessively concerned with grammatical rules, sometimes to the point of producing unnatural or incorrect forms.  Characterized by an excessive focus on grammar rules, often leading to unnatural or incorrect language use.|例句：His writing style is a bit hypergrammatical, making it sound stilted. 他的写作风格有点过度语法化，听起来很生硬。

例句：Children sometimes go through a hypergrammatical phase, applying rules even when they don't apply. 孩子们有时会经历一个过度语法化的阶段，即使规则不适用也会应用它们。|近义词：overly grammatical, pedantic, prescriptive|反义词：ungrammatical, colloquial, informal|词性:形容词
