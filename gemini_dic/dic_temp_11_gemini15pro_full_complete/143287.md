outdragged:/ˌaʊtˈdræɡd/
verb
胜过，超过（在拖拽或拉力比赛中）
To have dragged something farther or with more force than someone else.
The team outdragged their opponents in the tug-of-war.  这个队在拔河比赛中胜过了他们的对手。
The fishermen outdragged the heavy net onto the shore. 渔民们把沉重的渔网拖上了岸。
Synonyms: outpulled, outmatched, surpassed, bested
Antonyms: lost to, defeated by
词性: 动词
