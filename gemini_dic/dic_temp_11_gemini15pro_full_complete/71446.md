flamboyantly:/flæmˈbɔɪəntli/
adv.
华丽地，炫耀地，引人注目地
in a flamboyant manner; in a way that attracts attention because of unusual and exciting qualities.
She dressed flamboyantly in a bright pink dress and feather boa. 她穿着一件亮粉色的连衣裙和羽毛围巾，打扮得花枝招展。
The peacock strutted flamboyantly, displaying its magnificent tail feathers.  孔雀炫耀地走来走去，展示着它华丽的尾羽。
近义词:ostentatiously, showily, dramatically, theatrically
反义词:subtly, modestly, plainly, simply
词性:adverb
词源:From French flamboyant, from Old French flamboiant ‘flaming,’ present participle of flamboier ‘to blaze,’ from flambe ‘flame.’
