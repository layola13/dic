deliberate:/dɪˈlɪbərət/ /dɪˈlɪbəreɪt/ | adj. v. | 故意的，深思熟虑的；仔细考虑 | done consciously and intentionally; engage in long and careful consideration |  He made a deliberate attempt to provoke a fight. 他故意挑起争端。 |  近义词：intentional, planned, conscious, purposeful, careful, thoughtful | 反义词：accidental, unintentional, impulsive, careless, rash | 词性:形容词

deliberate:/dɪˈlɪbərət/ /dɪˈlɪbəreɪt/ | v. | 仔细考虑，商议 | engage in long and careful consideration | The jury deliberated for five hours before reaching a verdict. 陪审团商议了五个小时才做出裁决。 | 近义词：consider, ponder, contemplate, discuss, debate | 反义词：ignore, disregard, neglect, impulse | 词性:动词


词源:  From Latin  _dēlīberātus_, past participle of _dēlīberāre_  "to consider, weigh, consult, deliberate," from _de-_ "thoroughly" + _librare_ "to weigh," related to _libra_ "balance, scales."
