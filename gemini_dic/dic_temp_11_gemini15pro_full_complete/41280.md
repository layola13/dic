cordially:/ˈkɔːrdʒəli/ /ˈkɔːrdiəli/| adv.|热情地，诚挚地，友善地|In a warm and friendly way; politely.|例句：You are cordially invited to attend our wedding. 你被诚挚地邀请参加我们的婚礼。|例句：She greeted me cordially. 她热情地跟我打招呼。|近义词：warmly, sincerely, politely, affably|反义词：coldly, rudely, curtly|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *cordialement*, from Old French *cordial*, from Late Latin *cordiālis* ("hearty, of the heart"), from Latin *cor* ("heart").
