anesthetists: /əˈnɛsθətɪsts/ (US) /əˈniːsθətɪsts/ (UK)| n.|麻醉师|A medical doctor trained to administer anesthetics (drugs that cause temporary loss of sensation, including pain) and manage patients' conditions during surgical operations.|The anesthetists monitored the patient's vital signs throughout the surgery. 麻醉师在整个手术过程中监测病人的生命体征。|The anesthetists explained the risks and benefits of different types of anesthesia. 麻醉师解释了不同类型麻醉的风险和益处。|Synonyms: anesthesiologist (US), anaesthetist (UK)|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
