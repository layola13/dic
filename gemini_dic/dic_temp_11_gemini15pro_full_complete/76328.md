gables:/ˈɡeɪblz/
n.
三角墙，山墙
The triangular upper part of a wall at the end of a ridged roof.
The house has charming gables and a thatched roof. 这座房子有迷人的山墙和茅草屋顶。
roofs, eaves
bases, foundations
noun
