exhaustion:/ɪɡˈzɔːstʃən/ |n.| 疲惫，筋疲力尽 |The state of being extremely tired, either physically or mentally.|She collapsed from exhaustion after running the marathon. 她跑完马拉松后因精疲力竭而倒下了。|fatigue, tiredness, weariness|energy, vigor|noun


etymology->
From Late Latin *exhaustio, from Latin exhaurire "to draw out completely," from ex- "out" + haurire "to draw, drain."
