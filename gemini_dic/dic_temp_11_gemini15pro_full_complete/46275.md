czarism:/ˈzɑːrɪzəm/ |n.| 沙皇统治；专制统治 |A system of government by a czar; autocratic rule. |例句：The Russian Revolution brought an end to czarism. 俄国革命结束了沙皇统治。

例句：Many criticized the czarism for its oppression of the people. 许多人批评沙皇统治对人民的压迫。|近义词：absolutism, autocracy, despotism, tyranny|反义词：democracy, republic|词性：名词

词源: From Russian царь (carʹ), from Old Church Slavonic цѣсарь (cěsarĭ), from Latin Caesar. Ultimately from the name of Julius Caesar.
