drench:/drentʃ/
verb
使湿透；淋透；浸透
to make someone or something extremely wet
例句：The sudden downpour drenched the hikers. 突如其来的倾盆大雨把徒步旅行者淋透了。

例句：The lawn was drenched with dew. 草坪上沾满了露水。

近义词：soak, saturate, deluge
反义词：dry
词性：v.
