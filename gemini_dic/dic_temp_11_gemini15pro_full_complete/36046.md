codpiece: /ˈkɑdˌpis/ | n. | 遮阴布，下体遮盖 | A pouch or flap attached to the front of men's breeches or close-fitting hose in the 15th and 16th centuries, covering the genitals. |  He strutted about, showing off his elaborate codpiece.  他趾高气扬地走来走去，炫耀着他精心制作的遮阴布。| synonym: fly (in modern usage) | antonym: N/A | noun
