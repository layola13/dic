novelism: /ˈnɒvəlɪzəm/ (US) /ˈnɒvəlɪzəm/ (UK)
n.
新奇事物；新奇性；小说手法；小说笔法
The quality of being novel or unusual;  a novel practice, custom, or idea.  The use of techniques or styles characteristic of novels.
His paintings were striking for their novelism. 他的绘画以其新颖性而引人注目。

The novelism of the idea attracted investors. 这个想法的新颖性吸引了投资者。


近义词: innovation, novelty, originality, freshness
反义词: banality, staleness, triteness, conventionality
词性: noun
