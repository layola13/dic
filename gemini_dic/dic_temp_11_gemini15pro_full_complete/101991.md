isochronism:/aɪˈsɒkrənɪzəm/ /aɪˈsoʊkrənɪzəm/
n.
等时性，同步性
The quality or state of being isochronous; the performance of oscillations or vibrations in equal times.
The pendulum's isochronism ensures accurate timekeeping. 钟摆的等时性确保了计时的准确性。
isochrony, synchronism
anisochronism
noun
