hydrometrical:/ˌhaɪdrəˈmɛtrɪk(ə)l/
adjective
水文的; 水力测量的
relating to the measurement of water, especially the flow and level of rivers and streams.
Hydrometrical measurements are crucial for flood forecasting. 水文测量对于洪水预报至关重要。
The hydrometrical data revealed a significant increase in river flow. 水文数据显示河流流量显著增加。
近义词: hydraulical, hydrologic
反义词:  (No common direct antonym)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From hydro- + -metrical.  Hydro- comes from Greek *hydōr* meaning "water."  -metrical relates to measurement.
