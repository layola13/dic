obstreperousness:/əbˈstrɛpərəsnəs/
n.|喧闹，吵闹，任性|The state of being noisy and difficult to control.  The quality of being obstreperous.|His obstreperousness disrupted the entire meeting. 他的吵闹扰乱了整个会议。|Synonyms: boisterousness, clamor, rowdiness|Antonyms: quietness, calmness, docility|noun

Etymology:
From obstreperous + -ness.  Obstreperous comes from Latin *obstreperus* "noisy, clamorous," from obstrepere "to make a noise against," from ob- "against" + strepere "to make a noise."
