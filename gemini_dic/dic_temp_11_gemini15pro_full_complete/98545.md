instillatory:/ɪnˈstɪlətɔːri/
adjective
灌输的；滴注的
Relating to instillation, the act of gradually introducing something into the mind or feelings.
The teacher's instillatory methods helped the students develop a love for learning. 老师的灌输式教学方法帮助学生培养了对学习的热爱。
instilling, inculcating
removing, eradicating
adjective
