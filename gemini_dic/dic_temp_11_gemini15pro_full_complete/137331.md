nontariff: /ˌnɒnˈtærɪf/ (British) /ˌnɑːnˈtɛrɪf/ (American)| adj.| 非关税的| Relating to trade barriers that are not in the form of tariffs, such as quotas, embargoes, sanctions, levies and other restrictions.| The government imposed nontariff barriers to protect domestic industries. 政府实施了非关税壁垒来保护国内产业。|Synonyms: protectionist, restrictive|Antonyms: free trade, open market|adjective

nontariff barrier: /ˌnɒnˈtærɪf ˈbæriər/ (British) /ˌnɑːnˈtɛrɪf ˈbɛriər/ (American)| n.| 非关税壁垒| A restriction on trade that doesn't involve a direct tax or tariff, such as quotas, embargoes, sanctions, levies and other restrictions.|  Nontariff barriers can significantly impact international trade. 非关税壁垒会严重影响国际贸易。|Synonyms: trade restriction, trade barrier|Antonyms: tariff reduction, free trade|noun
