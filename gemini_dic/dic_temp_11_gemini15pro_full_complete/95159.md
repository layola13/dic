inarticulately:/ɪnɑːrˈtɪkjələtli/
| adv. |表达不清地，不善言辞地|Not able to express oneself clearly or fluently.|He mumbled inarticulately, his words barely audible. 他含糊不清地嘟囔着，几乎听不见他的话。|She tried to explain her feelings, but she could only express herself inarticulately. 她试图解释她的感受，但她只能表达不清。| 近义词：incoherently, unclearly, vaguely, haltingly| 反义词：articulately, eloquently, clearly, fluently|词性: 副词

inarticulate:/ɪnɑːrˈtɪkjələt/
| adj. |说不出的，表达不清的|Unable to express oneself clearly or fluently.|He was inarticulate with rage. 他气得说不出话来。|The child, still in shock, was inarticulate. 受到惊吓的孩子说不出话来。| 近义词：speechless, tongue-tied, dumb, mute| 反义词：articulate, eloquent, expressive, fluent|词性: 形容词

词源:
From Latin *inarticulatus, from in- (“not”) + articulatus (“jointed, distinct”).  The root "articulate" ultimately comes from Latin "articulus" meaning "small joint," referring to the distinct parts of speech.
