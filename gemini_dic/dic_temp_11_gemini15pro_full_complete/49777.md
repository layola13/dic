demonianism:/ˌdiːˈmoʊniəˌnɪzəm/
n.
恶魔崇拜；极端残忍
The worship of demons; extreme cruelty or wickedness.
His demonianism manifested in his cruel treatment of animals. 他对动物的残忍行径表现出他的恶魔崇拜。
The dictator's reign was marked by demonianism and violence.  独裁者的统治以恶魔崇拜和暴力为标志。
近义词:devil worship, satanism, barbarity, cruelty
反义词:humanity, kindness, compassion
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *daemōniānismus, from Ancient Greek δαιμονιακός (daimoniakos, “possessed by a demon”) + -ismus. Ultimately from Ancient Greek δαίμων (daímōn, “deity, spirit”).
