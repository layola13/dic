meliorate:/ˈmiːliəreɪt/| v. |改善，改进；减轻|To make or become better; to improve. |The new mayor promised to meliorate the city's traffic problems. 新市长承诺要改善城市的交通问题。

The doctor gave him medicine to meliorate the pain. 医生给他开了药以减轻疼痛。

|近义词：improve, enhance, better, amend, ameliorate|反义词：worsen, aggravate, exacerbate|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *melioratus, past participle of meliorare "to improve," from Latin melior "better."
