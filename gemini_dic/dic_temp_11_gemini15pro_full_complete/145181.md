overattenuated: /ˌoʊvərəˈtɛnjuˌeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过度衰减的 | Having been reduced in strength or intensity to an excessive degree, especially of a signal.  The signal has been weakened too much. | The overattenuated signal was barely detectable by the receiver. 信号过度衰减，接收器几乎无法检测到。 | The overattenuated audio was difficult to understand.  过度衰减的音频很难理解。 | 近义词：weakened, diminished, reduced, dampened | 反义词：amplified, boosted, strengthened, enhanced | 词性: adjective

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "attenuate" (from Latin "attenuare," meaning "to make thin" or "to weaken").
