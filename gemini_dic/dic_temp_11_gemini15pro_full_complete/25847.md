burgundy:/ˈbɜːrɡəndi/ (US), /ˈbɜːɡəndi/ (UK)| n. adj.|勃艮第；勃艮第酒；紫红色|A region of east-central France, or the red wine made there. Also, a dark purplish-red color.|例句：She wore a burgundy dress to the party. 她穿了一件紫红色的连衣裙去参加聚会。|近义词：maroon, wine, ruby|反义词:cyan, teal|词性:名词, 形容词
burgundy:/ˈbɜːrɡəndi/ (US), /ˈbɜːɡəndi/ (UK)|adj.|紫红色的|Having a dark purplish-red color.|例句：The walls were painted a deep burgundy. 墙壁被漆成了深紫红色。|近义词：maroon, wine, ruby|反义词:cyan, teal|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *Bourgogne, from Late Latin Burgundia, from the Germanic tribe Burgundians.  Referencing both the region and the wine produced there, the color term emerged later, inspired by the color of the wine.
