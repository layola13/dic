heave: /hiːv/ | v. n. | 举起；拉起；投掷；起伏；呕吐 | To lift or haul (something heavy) with great effort.  To rise and fall rhythmically; undulate. To retch or vomit. |  He heaved the anchor up. 他拉起了锚。 The ship heaved on the waves. 船在波浪中起伏。 He heaved with laughter. 他大笑不止。| 近义词: lift, raise, hoist, throw, toss, vomit, retch | 反义词: lower, drop | 词性: 动词, 名词

heave: /hiːv/ | n. | 举起；拉起；投掷；起伏；呕吐（的动作）| The act of heaving or lifting something heavy. A strong outward breath.  A rhythmic rise and fall. |  With a mighty heave, he lifted the rock. 他用力一举，抬起了石头。  The heave of her chest showed she was struggling for breath. 她胸部的起伏表明她呼吸困难。| 近义词: lift, throw, toss, surge, swell | 反义词: drop | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Old English *hēafian, from Proto-Germanic *hafjaną (“to lift, raise”), from Proto-Indo-European *keh₂p- (“to grasp, take”).
