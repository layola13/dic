militarising:/ˈmɪlɪtəˌraɪzɪŋ/
verb
军事化
The act of equipping or organizing something for military purposes.
The government is militarising the police force. 政府正在将警察部队军事化。
The island nation began militarising in response to the threat.  这个岛国开始军事化以应对威胁。
arming, fortifying, equipping
demilitarising, civilianising
v.
