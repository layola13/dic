narrow-gage:/ˈnæroʊ ɡeɪdʒ/| adj.|窄轨距的|Having a narrower distance between rails than the standard gauge (which is typically 4 feet 8 1/2 inches).|The narrow-gage railway wound its way through the mountains.  窄轨铁路蜿蜒穿过群山。|The old mining town had a narrow-gage railroad. 这个古老的矿业小镇有一条窄轨铁路。|近义词：small-gauge|反义词：broad-gauge, standard-gauge|词性:形容词

narrow-gauge:/ˈnæroʊ ɡeɪdʒ/| n.|窄轨距|A railroad track or system with a narrower distance between rails than the standard gauge.|The narrow gauge made the train ride more precarious. 窄轨距使得火车行驶更加不稳定。|They used a narrow gauge for the mountain railway. 他们为山区铁路使用了窄轨距。|近义词：small gauge|反义词：broad gauge, standard gauge|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
"narrow" + "gauge"

"narrow" comes from Old English "nearu," meaning "close, constricted."

"gauge" comes from Old North French "gaugie," meaning "measuring rod."
