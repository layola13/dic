eringo:/ɪˈrɪŋɡoʊ/
n.
刺芹
A tall Mediterranean plant (Eryngium campestre) of the parsley family, with thistlelike flower heads.  Also: the root of this plant, formerly candied and eaten.
The eringo root was once a popular sweet treat. 刺芹根曾经是一种受欢迎的甜食。
synonyms: sea holly, eryngo
antonyms: N/A
