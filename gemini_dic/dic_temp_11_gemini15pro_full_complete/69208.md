fear-monger:/ˈfɪərˌmʌŋɡər/ | n. | 恐惧贩子；散布恐慌的人 | A person who deliberately tries to create fear in others, especially for political purposes. |  The politician was accused of being a fear-monger, using scare tactics to gain votes.  这个政客被指责是一个恐惧贩子，使用恐吓策略来赢得选票。 | synonyms: alarmist, scaremonger, panic-monger | antonyms: comforter, reassurer | noun

fear-mongering:/ˈfɪərˌmʌŋɡərɪŋ/ | n. | 散布恐慌；制造恐慌的行为 | The act of deliberately creating fear in others. | The media's fear-mongering about the virus caused widespread panic. 媒体对病毒的恐慌宣传引起了广泛的恐慌。 | synonyms: alarmism, scaremongering | antonyms: reassurance, comfort | noun
