belying: /bɪˈlaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 掩饰；与…不符；证明…虚假 |  misrepresenting; contradicting; proving false | Her calm demeanor belied her inner turmoil. 她平静的举止掩饰了她内心的混乱。 | synonyms: contradicting, masking, disguising | antonyms: revealing, exposing, showing | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *belien*, from Old English *belēogan* ("to deceive, betray, belie"), from *be-* (prefix meaning "around, about, over") + *lēogan* ("to lie, tell a falsehood").  Cognate with Dutch *belogen* ("to belie"), German *belügen* ("to belie").
