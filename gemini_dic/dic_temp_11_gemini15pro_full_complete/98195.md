insecta:/ɪnˈsɛktə/
n.
昆虫纲；虫类
A class of arthropods with three body sections (head, thorax, and abdomen), six legs, one pair of antennae, and usually one or two pairs of wings.
Insects play a vital role in the ecosystem. 昆虫在生态系统中扮演着至关重要的角色。
近义词：bug, creepy-crawly
反义词: 无
词性: 名词
