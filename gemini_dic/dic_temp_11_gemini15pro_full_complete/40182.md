contentiousness:/kənˈtɛnʃəsnəs/
n.
争论性，好争辩
The tendency to be argumentative or quarrelsome.
His contentiousness often led to heated debates. 他的好争辩经常引发激烈的争论。
近义词：argumentativeness, quarrelsomeness, disputatiousness
反义词：agreeableness, amiability, peacefulness
词性：名词

contentious:/kənˈtɛnʃəs/
adj.
有争议的，引起争论的
Likely to cause disagreement or argument.
The contentious issue of immigration reform has divided the country.  移民改革这个有争议的问题已经分裂了国家。
近义词：controversial, disputed, debatable
反义词：uncontroversial, undisputed, agreeable
词性：形容词


词源：Late Middle English: from Old French contencieux, -ieuse, from Latin contentiosus ‘obstinate in a suit’, from contendere ‘strive’.
