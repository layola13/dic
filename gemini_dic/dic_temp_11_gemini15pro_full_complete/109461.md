lepidoptera:/ˌlɛpɪˈdɒptərə/ (US) /ˌlɛpɪˈdɒptərə/ (UK)| n. |鳞翅目昆虫（蝴蝶和飞蛾）|An order of insects that includes butterflies and moths, characterized by their large, often brightly colored wings covered in scales. |Butterflies and moths belong to the order Lepidoptera. 蝴蝶和飞蛾属于鳞翅目。| synonym:  Rhopalocera (butterflies), Heterocera (moths) | antonym: N/A | noun
