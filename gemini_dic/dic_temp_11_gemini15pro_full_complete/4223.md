airlifted:/ˈɛərˌlɪftɪd/| v. |空运，空投|transported by aircraft, especially in an emergency.|The injured climbers were airlifted to safety. 受伤的登山者被空运到安全的地方。|例句：The supplies were airlifted to the flood-stricken area.  这些物资被空运到遭受洪灾的地区。

例句：The refugees were airlifted out of the war zone.  难民被空运出战区。|近义词：flown, transported by air|反义词: transported by land/sea |词性:动词
