lacuna:/ləˈkjuːnə/ (ləˈkuːnə)/| n.|空隙，空白，缺失|a gap or missing part in something, especially in a system or sequence.|The historian noted a lacuna in the available records of the period.|Synonyms: gap, void, omission, blank, hiatus|Antonyms: fullness, completeness|noun

lacunae:/ləˈkjuːniː/ (ləˈkuːnaɪ)/| n.|空隙，空白，缺失（lacuna的复数形式）|plural form of lacuna, referring to multiple gaps or missing parts.|The researchers identified several lacunae in their understanding of the disease.|Synonyms: gaps, voids, omissions, blanks, hiatuses|Antonyms:  fullnesses, completenesses|noun
