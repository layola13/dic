marketability:/ˌmɑːrkɪtəˈbɪləti/
|n.|适销性；市场性|The quality of being easy to sell or being in demand.|The marketability of this product is high due to its unique features.  由于其独特的特性，该产品的市场性很高。|Synonym: salability, merchantability|Antonym: unsalability, unmarketability|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From market + -able + -ity.  "Market" ultimately comes from Latin "mercatus" (market, trade). "-able" is a suffix meaning "capable of being," and "-ity" is a suffix forming nouns of quality or state.
