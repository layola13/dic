establisher:/ɪˈstæblɪʃər/
n.
建立者，创立者
A person or entity that establishes something, such as an organization, institution, or custom.
King Alfred is considered the establisher of the English navy.  阿尔弗雷德大帝被认为是英国海军的建立者。
The company was the establisher of new standards in the industry. 这家公司是行业新标准的制定者。
founder, creator, originator
destroyer, demolisher
noun
