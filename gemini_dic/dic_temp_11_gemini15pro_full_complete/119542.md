microseismometer:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈsaɪzməˌmɪːtər/ | n. |微震仪|An instrument that measures and records microseisms (very faint earth tremors).|The microseismometer detected subtle vibrations in the earth's crust. 微震仪探测到地壳的细微震动。

microseismometer:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈsaɪzməˌmɪːtər/ | n. |微震仪|A highly sensitive seismometer designed to detect microseisms.|Scientists used the microseismometer to monitor volcanic activity. 科学家们使用微震仪监测火山活动。

microseismometer:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈsaɪzməˌmɪːtər/ | n. |微震仪|A device used in seismology for measuring microseisms.|The data from the microseismometer helped predict the earthquake. 来自微震仪的数据有助于预测地震。


microseismometer:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈsaɪzməˌmɪːtər/| n. |微震仪|A specialized instrument used for detecting and recording micro-earthquakes.|The microseismometer registered a series of small tremors before the main quake. 微震仪在主震前记录到了一系列小震颤。

microseismometer:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈsaɪzməˌmɪːtər/| n. |微震仪|An instrument designed to measure minute ground motions.|The microseismometer is capable of detecting vibrations imperceptible to humans.  微震仪能够探测到人类无法察觉的震动。


近义词: seismometer, seismograph
反义词: None

词源: micro- (small) + seism (earthquake) + -meter (measuring instrument)
