incorporeally:/ˌɪnkɔːrˈpɔːriəli/
adverb
无实体地；非物质地；精神上地
Without a body; in a disembodied or spiritual form.
The ghost appeared incorporeally in the mist.  幽灵无形地出现在雾中。
He visited her incorporeally in her dreams. 他在她的梦中以灵魂的形式出现。
近义词:spiritually, intangibly, immaterially
反义词:physically, bodily, corporeally
词性:adverb

Etymology:
From Late Latin *incorporeālis, from in- (“not”) + corporeus (“bodily, corporeal”), from corpus (“body”).
