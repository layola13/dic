desaturation:/diːˌsætʃəˈreɪʃn/ |n.|去饱和，脱饱和；减色|The desaturation of the image made it look faded.  图像的去饱和使其看起来褪色。|例句：The artist used desaturation to create a vintage feel. 艺术家使用去饱和来营造复古感。

例句：Desaturation of the blood can be a serious medical condition. 血液脱饱和可能是一种严重的疾病。|近义词：fading, dulling, bleaching, paling|反义词：saturation, intensification, brightening|词性: 名词

词源:  From de- (prefix meaning 'removal' or 'reduction') + saturation (from saturate, from Latin saturatus, past participle of saturare 'to fill').
