derisions:/dɪˈrɪʒənz/
|n.|嘲笑，嘲弄，奚落|The constant derisions from his classmates made him feel small and insignificant.  他同学不断的嘲笑让他感到自己渺小而微不足道。|
|n.|The comedian endured the derisions of the hecklers.  喜剧演员忍受着起哄者的嘲笑。|
|Synonyms:|mockery, ridicule, scorn, contempt, disdain|
|Antonyms:|praise, admiration, respect, approval|
|Etymology:|From Old French *derision*, from Late Latin *derisio*, from Latin *deridere* "to deride," from *de-* "down" + *ridere* "to laugh."|
