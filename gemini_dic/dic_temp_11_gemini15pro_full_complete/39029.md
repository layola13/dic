conflates:/kənˈfleɪts/| v.|合并，混合，混淆|combines two or more things into one, often in a way that is confusing or misleading|The report conflates several distinct issues.  这份报告混淆了几个截然不同的问题。|The author conflates fact and fiction in his latest novel. 作者在他最新的长篇小说中糅合了事实与虚构。|synonyms: merge, combine, blend, fuse, amalgamate|antonyms: separate, distinguish, differentiate|verb
