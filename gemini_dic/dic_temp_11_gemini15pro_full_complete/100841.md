intromittent:/ˌɪntrəˈmɪtənt/ | adj. | 间歇的，断断续续的 | Occurring at irregular intervals; not continuous or steady. | The patient experienced intromittent pain in his abdomen. 病人的腹部一阵一阵地疼。 | 近义词: sporadic, intermittent, occasional | 反义词: continuous, constant, persistent | 词性: adjective

intromit:/ˌɪntrəˈmɪt/ | vt. | 插入，干涉，介入 | Insert or introduce (something) into something else. (Also used figuratively to mean interfering in something.) | It is unwise to intromit in other people's affairs. 干涉别人的事情是不明智的。 | 近义词: insert, introduce, interfere, meddle | 反义词: abstain, withdraw | 词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin intromittere "to send or let in," from Latin intro- "inward" + mittere "to send."
