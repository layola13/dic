overlarding: /ˌoʊvərˈlɑːrdɪŋ/ | n. | 过度修饰；过分渲染；加厚肥肉 | The act of embellishing something excessively, often to the point of being ostentatious or excessive; also, the act of adding layers of fat to meat. |  His writing suffered from overlarding with technical jargon. 他的写作因充斥着专业术语而显得晦涩难懂。 | 近义词：embellishment, exaggeration, ornamentation, gilding, overdoing | 反义词：understatement, simplification | 词性: noun
