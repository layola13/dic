attributively:/əˈtrɪbjʊtɪvli/| adv.|定语地，属性地|Used to describe how an adjective, noun, or phrase functions as an attribute, modifying or describing a noun.|She used the word "red" attributively, describing the "red car." 她用“红色”作为定语，描述了那辆“红色的车”。|近义词：descriptively, qualifyingly|反义词: predicatively|词性:副词
