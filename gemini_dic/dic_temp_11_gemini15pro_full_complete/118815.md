metamorphoser:/ˌmɛtəˈmɔːrfəzər/| n.|变形者，使变形的人|A metamorphoser is someone or something that transforms or changes something else drastically.|The alchemist was a metamorphoser, turning base metals into gold.|近义词：transformer, transmuter|反义词: preserver, maintainer|词性:noun

metamorphose:/ˌmɛtəˈmɔːrfoʊz/| v.|变形，使变形|To metamorphose is to transform or change drastically, often in form or nature.|The caterpillar will metamorphose into a butterfly.|近义词：transform, convert, transmute|反义词: stagnate, fix, remain|词性:verb
