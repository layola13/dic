caped:/keɪpt/| adj.|披斗篷的，披肩的|Wearing a cape; covered with or as if with a cape. |The superhero wore a caped costume.  超级英雄穿着披风服装。|例句：The mysterious figure was caped and masked.  那个神秘的人物披着斗篷，戴着面具。|近义词：cloaked, hooded|反义词：uncaped, bare|词性:形容词

词源: From cape + -ed.  来源于名词 cape（斗篷）加上形容词后缀 -ed。
