bruiting:/ˈbruːɪtɪŋ/
| n.| 散布；谣传|The bruiting of rumors can damage a person's reputation.  谣言的散播会损害一个人的名誉。|The constant bruiting of his supposed wealth irritated him.  不断有人散播他所谓的财富，这让他很恼火。|近义词：spreading, circulating, disseminating, publicizing, rumor-mongering|反义词：concealing, suppressing, hiding|noun
