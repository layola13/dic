boofhead:/ˈbuːfhed/| n. |笨蛋，傻瓜|A derogatory term for a stupid or foolish person.  It implies a lack of intelligence or common sense.|He's such a boofhead, he locked his keys in the car again. 他真是个笨蛋，他又把钥匙锁在车里了。|近义词：idiot, fool, moron, nitwit, dimwit, airhead, dope|反义词:genius, intellectual, bright spark|noun
