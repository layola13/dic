japanned:/dʒəˈpænd/ | adj. | 漆过的，涂漆的，日式漆器的 | Coated with a hard, brilliant varnish, originally in imitation of Japanese lacquerware. | The japanned box gleamed in the sunlight. 漆盒在阳光下闪闪发光。| 近义词: lacquered, varnished, glazed | 反义词: unvarnished, bare, matte | 词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From japan (v.), "to cover with japan," from Japan, where such lacquerware originated.
