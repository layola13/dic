overfacility:/ˌoʊvər fəˈsɪləti/| n.| 过于轻易, 过于熟练, 过于流畅| An overfacility in writing can sometimes lead to a lack of depth and meaning. 写作过于流畅有时会导致缺乏深度和意义。|例句：His overfacility with language sometimes obscured the deeper meaning. 他过于流利的语言有时会掩盖更深层的含义。

例句：Her overfacility in playing the piano made her performance seem mechanical. 她弹钢琴过于熟练，使她的演奏显得机械化。|近义词：fluency, facility, slickness|反义词：awkwardness, clumsiness, difficulty|词性:名词
