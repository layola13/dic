bell-curve:/ˈbel kɜːrv/| n. |钟形曲线|A statistical distribution with a characteristic bell shape, where most values cluster around the mean and fewer values occur towards the extremes.|The distribution of IQ scores follows a bell-curve. 智商分数的分布呈钟形曲线。|synonyms: normal distribution, Gaussian distribution||noun
bell-curves:/ˈbel kɜːrvz/| n. |钟形曲线（复数）|Plural form of "bell-curve," referring to multiple bell-shaped statistical distributions.|Several bell-curves were observed in the data analysis. 在数据分析中观察到几个钟形曲线。|synonyms: normal distributions, Gaussian distributions||noun
