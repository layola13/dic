lent:/lent/ | adj. | 借出的；（基督教）大斋期的；严肃的；克制的 | Past participle of lend. Given or allowed to be used temporarily.  | Example:  I lent him my car. 我把我的车借给了他。| Synonym: loaned, advanced, granted | Antonym: borrowed, received | Past Participle

lent:/lent/ | n. | (基督教) 四旬斋 | The period of forty days from Ash Wednesday to Holy Saturday, observed in many Christian churches as a time of fasting, penitence, and spiritual discipline. | Example:  Christians often give up something for Lent. 基督徒在四旬斋期间经常放弃一些东西。 | Synonym:  Quadragesima | Antonym: N/A | Noun
