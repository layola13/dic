enchanter:/ɪnˈtʃæntər/ | n. | 魔法师；施魔法的人；迷人的人或物 | Someone who enchants; a magician or sorcerer.  A person or thing that delights or captivates. | The enchanter cast a spell on the princess. 魔法师对公主施了咒语。|Synonyms: magician, sorcerer, wizard, charmer | Antonyms: bore | noun

enchant:/ɪnˈtʃænt/ | v. | 使着魔；施魔法于；使陶醉；使喜悦 | To cast a spell on; to charm or delight greatly. |  The beautiful music enchanted the audience. 美妙的音乐使观众陶醉。|Synonyms: charm, captivate, fascinate, bewitch | Antonyms: repel, disgust | verb
