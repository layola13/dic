multi-axially:/ˌmʌltiˈæksiəli/ | adv. | 多轴地，多方向地 |  Acting along or around multiple axes; in multiple directions or dimensions.  This term often refers to forces, stresses, or movements. | The composite material is multi-axially reinforced for increased strength. 这种复合材料是多轴加固的，以提高强度。 |The robot arm can move multi-axially, allowing it to perform complex tasks.  这个机械臂可以多轴移动，使其能够执行复杂的任务。| 近义词: multi-directional, multidimensional | 反义词: uniaxial, single-axis | 词性: 副词

multi-axial:/ˌmʌltiˈæksiəl/ | adj. | 多轴的，多方向的 | Having or relating to multiple axes; capable of moving or operating along multiple axes. |  The bridge is designed to withstand multi-axial loading. 这座桥梁的设计能够承受多轴荷载。 |  A multi-axial joint allows for a wider range of motion. 多轴关节允许更大范围的运动。 | 近义词: multi-directional, multidimensional | 反义词: uniaxial, single-axis | 词性: 形容词
