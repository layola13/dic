kippernut:/ˈkɪpərˌnʌt/| n.|小鲱鱼；鲱鱼干；（俚语）小个子；矮子|A kippernut is a small or immature herring, often smoked or dried.  It can also be used slang to refer to a small person, particularly a child.|He ate kippers for breakfast. 他早餐吃了鲱鱼。|The cheeky kippernut hid behind his mother's skirt.  那个厚脸皮的小家伙躲在他妈妈的裙子后面。|近义词：herring, sprat, tiddler (slang)|反义词:|名词
