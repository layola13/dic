boose: /buːz/
noun
酒精饮料，酒
Alcoholic drink; liquor.
He drank too much boose last night and got sick. 他昨晚喝太多酒了，病了。
近义词: alcohol, liquor, booze (itself), spirits, hooch
反义词: sobriety, abstinence
词性: noun

Etymology:
From obsolete Dutch *būze "strong alcoholic drink," from Middle Dutch būse "cask, barrel."  The connection likely arises from the storage of alcoholic beverages in casks.  First recorded in English in the early 19th century.
