felicities:/fɪˈlɪsɪtiz/
|n.|幸福，快乐；祝贺；巧妙的措辞| intense feelings of joy and happiness; expressions of good wishes; aptly chosen and effective words|She accepted their congratulations with felicties. 她巧妙地表达了谢意，接受了他们的祝贺。|Synonyms: joys, blessings, happiness, delights, compliments, congratulations|Antonyms: miseries, sorrows, misfortunes, tragedies|noun

felicity:/fɪˈlɪsɪti/
|n.|幸福，快乐；祝贺；巧妙的措辞|the state of being happy, especially in a high degree; an instance of this; a source of happiness; the ability to find appropriate expression for one's thoughts; an appropriate and pleasing manner or style (especially literary style)|He spoke with great felicity. 他说话很得体。|Synonyms: bliss, happiness, joy, aptness, appropriateness|Antonyms: misery, unhappiness, sorrow, inappropriateness|noun
