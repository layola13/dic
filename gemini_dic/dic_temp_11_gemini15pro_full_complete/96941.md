inextricabilities:/ɪnˌɛkstrɪkəˈbɪlɪtiz/
noun
错综复杂; 难解难分
the state of being inextricable; things that are very complicated and difficult to understand.
The novel explored the inextricabilities of human relationships. 这部小说探讨了人际关系的错综复杂。
Synonyms: complexities, complications, intricacies, entanglements
Antonyms: simplicities, clarities, easiness
n.

inextricability:/ɪnˌɛkstrɪkəˈbɪlɪti/
noun
错综复杂; 难解难分
the state of being inextricable; the quality of being impossible to disentangle or separate.
The inextricability of the problem frustrated the researchers.  这个问题的错综复杂让研究人员感到沮丧。
Synonyms: complexity, complication, intricacy, entanglement
Antonyms: simplicity, clarity, easiness
n.

inextricable:/ɪnˈɛkstrɪkəbl/
adjective
无法解脱的; 难解难分的
impossible to disentangle or separate.
The two families were linked by an inextricable bond. 这两个家族有着千丝万缕的联系。
Synonyms: inseparable, tangled, intertwined, complex
Antonyms: separable, simple, clear, straightforward
adj.
