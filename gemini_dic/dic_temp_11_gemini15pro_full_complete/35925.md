cocultivation:/ˌkoʊˌkʌltɪˈveɪʃən/
n.|共同栽培，共培养|The cocultivation of different cell types can reveal complex interactions. 不同细胞类型的共培养可以揭示复杂的相互作用。|例句：Cocultivation of bacteria and fungi can lead to the discovery of new antibiotics.  细菌和真菌的共培养可以帮助发现新的抗生素。

例句：The cocultivation of these two species of plants yielded interesting results. 这两种植物的共培养产生了有趣的结果。

近义词：coculture, joint cultivation|
反义词:|
词性:noun
