mermaids:/ˈmɜːrmeɪdz/ | n. | 美人鱼 |A mythical sea creature with the head and upper body of a woman and the tail of a fish. |例句：Sailors used to tell tales of beautiful mermaids. 水手们过去常讲美丽美人鱼的故事。

例句：In many cultures, mermaids are symbols of beauty and mystery. 在许多文化中，美人鱼是美丽和神秘的象征。|近义词：siren, sea nymph |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "land creature") | Noun
