decalcify:/diːˈkælsɪfaɪ/
verb
脱钙；去除钙化物
To remove calcium salts from something, especially bone.
The bones were decalcified before being sectioned for microscopic study.  这些骨头在被切片用于显微镜研究之前先进行了脱钙。
The acid rain decalcified the statue. 酸雨使雕像脱钙了。
synonyms: delime, descale
antonyms: calcify
词性:v.
