begonias:/bɪˈɡoʊnjəz/ | n. | 秋海棠（复数） | A genus of plants in the family Begoniaceae, native to moist subtropical and tropical climates.  They are popular as ornamental houseplants and garden plants for their colorful flowers and foliage. |  She has a beautiful collection of begonias in her garden. 她在花园里种了一系列漂亮的秋海棠。 | synonyms: begonia (singular), wax begonia, tuberous begonia | antonyms: N/A | noun
