concern:/kənˈsɜːrn/ (US) /kənˈsɜːn/ (UK)| n. vt.|关心, 关注; 涉及; 使担心|Something that interests you because it is important or affects you.  To be about something. To worry someone.|例句：The rising crime rate is a matter of great public concern.  犯罪率上升是一个公众非常关心的问题。|近义词：worry, anxiety, interest, matter, business|反义词：indifference, apathy|noun

concern:/kənˈsɜːrn/ (US) /kənˈsɜːn/ (UK)| vt.|涉及; 使担心; 关系到|To be about something. To worry someone.|例句：The news concerns your brother. 这消息与你兄弟有关。|近义词：involve, affect, worry, bother|反义词：disregard, ignore|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *concerner, from Latin concernere (“to mix together, sift, examine”), from con- (“together”) + cernere (“to separate, distinguish, decide”).
