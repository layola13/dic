finfish:/ˈfɪnˌfɪʃ/| n. |鱼类（尤指有鳍的鱼，区别于贝类、甲壳类等）|A fish of a class distinguished by having fins, especially one that is caught for food. |例句：The restaurant specializes in finfish dishes. 这家餐厅专门经营鱼类菜肴。

例句：The decline in finfish populations is a serious concern. 鱼类数量的下降是一个严重的问题。|近义词：fish, bony fish|反义词:shellfish, crustacean|词性:noun
