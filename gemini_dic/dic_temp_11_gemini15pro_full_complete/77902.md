genies: /ˈdʒiːniːz/ | n. |精灵，妖怪|A supernatural creature, often depicted as inhabiting a lamp or bottle, that can grant wishes. |The genies granted him three wishes.| 近义词:spirits, djinn |反义词: N/A |Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *génie*, from Latin *genius* "guardian deity or spirit which watches over each person from birth; a person's natural inclination or talent." Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\*ǵenh₁-* "to give birth, beget, produce."
