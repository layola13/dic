delignate:/diːˈlɪɡneɪt/ | vt. |  (正式)选定；指定；标明 | To mark out; designate.  Specifically, to designate (wood) for use as paper pulp by removing lignin. | The forest area was delignated for paper production.  这片森林区域被指定用于造纸。 | The researchers delignated the specific genes responsible for the disease. 研究人员确定了导致这种疾病的特定基因。 |  近义词: designate, specify, earmark | 反义词: generalize | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From de- + lign- (from Latin lignum, "wood") + -ate (verb-forming suffix).  The word literally means "to remove wood" or, more specifically, "to remove lignin from wood."
