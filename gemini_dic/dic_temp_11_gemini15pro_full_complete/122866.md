mollifier:/ˈmɒlɪfaɪər/ (UK) /ˈmɑːlɪfaɪər/ (US)| n. |缓和剂；调解人；润肤剂|Something that softens or soothes; a person who appeases or pacifies; an emollient.|The negotiator acted as a mollifier in the heated dispute.  这位谈判代表在激烈的争论中充当了调解人。|Synonyms: appeaser, mediator, conciliator, emollient, softener, soother|Antonyms: irritant, aggravator, provocateur|noun

mollify:/ˈmɒlɪfaɪ/ (UK) /ˈmɑːlɪfaɪ/ (US)| vt. |缓和；抚慰；使平静|To appease the anger or anxiety of someone; to make less severe or violent.|His kind words mollified her anger. 他的友善之词平息了她的怒火。|Synonyms: appease, placate, pacify, soothe, calm, allay|Antonyms: aggravate, exacerbate, provoke, irritate|verb
