aluat:/aˈluat/| n.|dough, paste|A mixture of flour, water, and sometimes other ingredients, used for making bread, pastry, etc.|例句：She kneaded the aluat until it was smooth and elastic. 她揉面团直到它变得光滑有弹性。

例句：The aluat is ready to be shaped into loaves.  面团可以做成面包了。|近义词：dough, paste, batter|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
