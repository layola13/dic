ill-fated:/ˌɪlˈfeɪtɪd/
adjective
注定要失败的;倒霉的;不幸的
destined to disaster; unlucky
The ill-fated expedition ended in tragedy.  这支不幸的探险队以悲剧告终。
synonyms: doomed, unfortunate, unlucky, cursed, jinxed
antonyms: fortunate, lucky, blessed, successful
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From ill + fated.  Ill meaning "bad" and fated meaning "destined".  The word implies a predetermined negative outcome.
