harness:/ˈhɑːrnɪs/ (US) /ˈhɑːnɪs/ (UK)| n. vt.|马具，挽具；安全带；利用，控制|A piece of equipment with straps and belts, used to control or hold in place a person, animal, or object.  To put a harness on (a person or animal); to control and make use of (natural resources, especially to produce energy).|
例句：The farmer put a harness on the horse. 农民给马套上挽具。

例句：They are harnessing the power of the wind to generate electricity. 他们正在利用风力发电。
|近义词：tackle, gear, equipment, exploit, utilize|反义词：release, liberate|词性:名词,动词
