droplets:/ˈdrɑːplɪts/| n. |小滴，微滴|Tiny drops of liquid.|The rain fell in tiny droplets.|近义词：drops, beads, specks|反义词:flood, deluge|词性:名词
Etymology: From Middle English *droppelette*, diminutive of *droppe*, from Old English *dropa*. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *drupon*.
