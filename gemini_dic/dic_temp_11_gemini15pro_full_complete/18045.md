begrave:/bɪˈɡreɪv/
verb
埋葬；使沉浸于；雕刻
To bury (a corpse) in a grave. To cause to be weighed down, as with sorrow; oppress. To carve or cut (letters, figures, etc.) into a surface.
He chose to begrave his pet in the garden. 他选择把他的宠物埋在花园里。
Grief begraved his features.  悲痛使他满脸愁容。
The inscription was begraved on the tombstone.  铭文被刻在了墓碑上。
近义词: bury, inter, entomb, immerse
反义词: exhume, unearth
词性: verb
