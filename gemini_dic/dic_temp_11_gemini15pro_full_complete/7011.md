andromed:/ænˈdrɒmɪd/ | adj. | 仙女座的;  | Relating to the Andromeda constellation or galaxy. |  例句：The Andromeda galaxy is the closest major galaxy to our own. 仙女座星系是我们最近的主要星系。

例句：He studied the Andromeda constellation through his telescope. 他用望远镜研究了仙女座星座。 | 近义词：Andromedan | 反义词： N/A | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Andromeda, a princess in Greek mythology who was chained to a rock as a sacrifice to a sea monster, and later rescued by Perseus.  The constellation Andromeda is named after her.
