coagulative:/koʊˈæɡjʊlətɪv/
adjective
凝固的；凝结的
Relating to or causing coagulation; tending to coagulate.
The doctor explained that the patient's condition was due to a coagulative process in the blood vessels. 医生解释说，病人的病情是由于血管内的凝血过程造成的。

The coagulative properties of this substance make it useful in wound healing.  这种物质的凝血特性使其在伤口愈合方面很有用。

synonym: clotting, thickening
antonym: thinning, dissolving
词性: adjective
