masterly:/ˈmɑːstərli/ /ˈmæstərli/| adj.|精湛的，熟练的，高超的|Showing great skill or technique, like that of a master.  Demonstrating a thorough understanding and expert execution.|He gave a masterly performance on the piano. 他在钢琴上进行了精湛的演奏。|近义词：skillful, expert, adept, proficient, accomplished|反义词：clumsy, inept, amateurish|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From master + -ly.  Meaning "like a master" is from the late 14th century.
