hesitation:/ˌhezɪˈteɪʃn/| n.|犹豫，踌躇|a pause or indecision before doing something|She spoke without hesitation. 她毫不犹豫地说了出来。| synonyms: pause, indecision, wavering, vacillation| antonyms: decisiveness, certainty, confidence| noun

hesitations:/ˌhezɪˈteɪʃnz/| n.|犹豫，踌躇（复数）|pauses or indecisions before doing something|After a few hesitations, he agreed to go.  犹豫片刻之后，他同意去了。| synonyms: pauses, indecisions, waverings, vacillations| antonyms: decisiveness, certainty, confidence| noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: hesitacioun, from Old French: hesitation, from Latin: haesitātiō, from haesitāre  "to stick fast, hesitate," from haerēre "to stick, adhere."
