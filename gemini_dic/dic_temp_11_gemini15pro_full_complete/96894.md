inexpensively:/ˌɪnɪkˈspɛnsɪvli/
adverb
便宜地，廉价地
In a way that does not cost a lot of money.
We ate inexpensively at a local cafe. 我们在当地一家咖啡馆吃得很便宜。
They furnished their apartment quite inexpensively. 他们用很便宜的家具布置了他们的公寓。
近义词:cheaply, affordably, economically
反义词:expensively, lavishly, dear
词性:副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + expensive + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Expensive" comes from Old French "expensif" meaning costly, derived from Latin "expendere" (to weigh out, pay out).
