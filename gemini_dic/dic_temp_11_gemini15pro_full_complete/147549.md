overpeer:/ˌəʊvərˈpɪər/ | v. |  超越；胜过；比…看得更远 | To look over or beyond something, especially something high, or to be taller than something.  Also, to surpass or excel someone or something. |  He overpeered the wall and saw the enemy approaching. 他越过墙看到了敌人正在靠近。 The new skyscraper overpeers all the other buildings in the city. 这座新的摩天大楼比城市里所有其他建筑都高。 She overpeered her rivals in the competition. 她在比赛中胜过她的对手。 | 近义词: overlook, surpass, excel, outdo, transcend | 反义词:  be inferior to, be overshadowed by | 词性: 动词
