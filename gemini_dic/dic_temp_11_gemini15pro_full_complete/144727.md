ovens:/ˈʌvənz/
n.
烤箱，烤炉
Enclosed compartments for baking, roasting, or heating food.
The baker put the bread dough into the hot ovens.  面包师把面包面团放进热烤箱里。
The ovens are preheating to 400 degrees. 烤箱正在预热到400度。
近义词:kilns, furnaces
反义词:(none specific - perhaps "refrigerators" in some contexts)
词性:noun
