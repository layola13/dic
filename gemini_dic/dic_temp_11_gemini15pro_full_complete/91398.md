hydroxanthic:/ˌhaɪdrəˈzænθɪk/ | adj. |氢硫醇的，黄原酸的 | Relating to or containing hydroxanthates (xanthic acids). | This compound exhibits hydroxanthic properties.  这种化合物表现出氢硫醇的性质。| The hydroxanthic acid is a key intermediate in this reaction.  黄原酸是这个反应的关键中间体。| 近义词: xanthogenic | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists) | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From hydro- (water, hydrogen) + xanthic (yellow, relating to xanthates).  Xanthic itself comes from the Greek xanthos (yellow) because many xanthates are yellow in color.
