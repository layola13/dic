blers:/ˈblɜːrz/
noun
胡说八道；废话；无稽之谈
Nonsense; meaningless talk; gibberish.
He was talking a load of blers, I didn't understand a word. 他一直在胡说八道，我一个字也听不懂。
近义词:nonsense, gibberish, balderdash, rubbish
反义词:sense, truth
词性:名词
