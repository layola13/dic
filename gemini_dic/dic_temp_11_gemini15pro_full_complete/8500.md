antiboycott:/ˌæntiˈbɔɪkɒt/ /ˌæntiˈbɔɪkɑːt/| adj. n.|反抵制的，反抵制运动|Opposing or intended to counteract a boycott.  Relating to laws or actions that oppose boycotts.|The state passed antiboycott legislation.  该州通过了反抵制立法。|近义词：counter-boycott|反义词:pro-boycott|词性:adjective, noun

antiboycott:/ˌæntiˈbɔɪkɒt/ /ˌæntiˈbɔɪkɑːt/| n.|反抵制运动|A movement or action opposing a boycott.|The antiboycott gathered momentum as more businesses spoke out. 反抵制运动随着越来越多的企业公开表态而势头增强。|近义词：counter-boycott|反义词:boycott|词性:noun
