overlooked:/ˌoʊvərˈlʊkt/| adj.|被忽视的；俯瞰的|Failing to notice or consider something; having a view from above.|例句：The potential risks of the investment were overlooked. 这项投资的潜在风险被忽视了。|近义词：ignored, neglected, missed, disregarded|反义词：noticed, observed, considered, heeded|词性:形容词
overlook:/ˌoʊvərˈlʊk/| vt.|忽视；俯瞰；宽恕|Fail to notice or consider something; have a view from above; forgive or condone (an offense).|例句：I think there is one key fact that you have overlooked. 我认为你忽略了一个关键的事实。|近义词：ignore, neglect, miss, disregard, condone, excuse|反义词：notice, observe, consider, heed, condemn|词性:动词

词源: From over + look.  The literal sense "look over" developed the figurative sense of "not notice, fail to see" in the 15th century. The sense of "have a view from above" is from the late 16th century.
