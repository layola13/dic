monsoon:/ˌmɒnˈsuːn/ (UK) /ˌmɑːnˈsuːn/ (US)| n.|季风|A seasonal prevailing wind in the region of South and Southeast Asia, blowing from the southwest between May and September and bringing rain (the wet monsoon ), or from the northeast between October and April (the dry monsoon).|The monsoon rains are crucial for agriculture in India.|近义词:rainy season, deluge|反义词:dry season|noun
