misbehaving:/ˌmɪsbɪˈheɪvɪŋ/
adjective
行为不端的，调皮捣蛋的
Behaving badly or improperly.
The misbehaving child was sent to his room.  行为不端的孩子被送回了房间。
The teacher scolded the misbehaving students. 老师责骂了那些调皮捣蛋的学生。

近义词:naughty, disobedient, unruly, acting up
反义词:well-behaved, obedient, good

词性:adjective
词源:mis- (prefix meaning "wrong" or "bad") + behaving (present participle of "behave")
