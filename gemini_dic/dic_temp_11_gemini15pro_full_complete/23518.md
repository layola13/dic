brain-damaged: /ˈbreɪn ˌdæmɪdʒd/
adjective
脑损伤的
Having lasting damage to the brain, which causes problems with thinking, learning, or physical abilities.
The brain-damaged patient struggled with basic motor skills.
这个脑损伤的病人连基本的运动技能都很困难。
He suffered brain damage in the accident.
他在事故中遭受了脑损伤。
近义词: brain-injured, impaired
反义词: healthy, unimpaired
词性: adjective


brain-damaged: /ˈbreɪn ˌdæmɪdʒd/
noun
脑损伤患者
A person who has suffered brain damage.
The hospital has a special unit for the brain-damaged.
这家医院有一个专门收治脑损伤患者的单元。
Support groups can be beneficial for the brain-damaged and their families.
互助小组对脑损伤患者及其家人有益。
近义词: brain-injured person
反义词: healthy person
词性: noun


Etymology:
