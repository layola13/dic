lackadaisical:/ˌlækəˈdeɪzɪkəl/| adj.| 懒散的，无精打采的|Characterized by a lack of enthusiasm and effort; listless. |例句：He had a lackadaisical approach to his studies, rarely attending classes and never completing assignments.  他学习态度懒散，很少上课，从不完成作业。

例句：The team's lackadaisical performance cost them the game. 球队懒散的表现让他们输掉了比赛。|近义词：listless, apathetic, lethargic, indolent|反义词：energetic, enthusiastic, motivated|词性:形容词

lackadaisy:/ˌlækəˈdeɪzi/| n.| 懒散，无精打采|A lack of enthusiasm and effort; listlessness. |例句：A general air of lackadaisy permeated the office on Friday afternoon.  周五下午，办公室里弥漫着一种懒散的气氛。

例句：Her lackadaisy made it difficult for her to complete any tasks efficiently. 她的懒散使她难以有效地完成任何任务。|近义词：listlessness, apathy, lethargy, indolence|反义词：energy, enthusiasm, motivation|词性:名词

词源: From lack + daisy, influenced by lackaday (expressing mild dismay or regret).  由 lack 和 daisy 组成，受 lackaday（表达轻微沮丧或遗憾）的影响。
