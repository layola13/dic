daub:/dɔːb/| n. vt.|涂抹，涂料，拙劣的画|A sticky substance used for coating a surface.  To coat or smear (a surface) with a sticky substance. A crude or inartistic painting.|He daubed some mud on the wall to cover the graffiti. 他在墙上涂了些泥巴来盖住涂鸦。|近义词：smear, smudge, plaster, coat|反义词:clean, wipe|词性:noun, verb

daubs:/dɔːbz/| n.|涂抹，涂料，拙劣的画（复数）|Smears, coatings, or crude paintings (plural).|The artist's early works were just daubs of color.  这位艺术家早期的作品只是一些颜色的涂抹。|近义词：smears, smudges, plasters, coatings|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

词源:
From Old French *dauber, from a Germanic source related to Old High German tūban "to dip, plunge."
