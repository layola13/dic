carpels:/ˈkɑːpəlz/ (US) /ˈkɑːpəlz/ (UK)| n. |心皮|The female reproductive organs of a flower, comprising the stigma, style, and ovary.|The flower has five carpels.|Synonyms:  gynoecium (collective term), pistil (sometimes used synonymously but can also refer to a single unit when carpels are fused) |Antonyms: stamen (male reproductive organ)|noun
