monkeys:/ˈmʌŋkiz/ | n. |猴子|Plural form of monkey, referring to more than one monkey.  A small to medium-sized primate that typically has a long tail, a relatively flat face, and hands and feet adapted for grasping.|The monkeys swung through the trees. 猴子在树上荡来荡去。|近义词：primates, apes|反义词: None|Noun
