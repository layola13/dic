misology:/mɪˈsɒlədʒi/ /mɪˈsɑːlədʒi/
noun
厌恶推理；厌恶辩论；仇恨理性
hatred of reasoning; aversion to argument or discussion; hatred of reason
His misology prevented him from engaging in any meaningful debate. 他的厌恶推理使他无法参与任何有意义的辩论。
Synonyms: anti-intellectualism, irrationality
Antonyms:  rationality, reason, logic
n.
