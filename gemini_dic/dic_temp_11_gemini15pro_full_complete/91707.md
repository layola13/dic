hyperconservative:/ˌhaɪpər.kənˈsɜːrvətɪv/ | adj. |极端保守的；极端守旧的|Extremely conservative;  holding extremely right-wing political views.|例句：His hyperconservative views alienated many moderate voters. 他的极端保守观点疏远了许多温和派选民。|例句：The hyperconservative faction within the party blocked any attempts at reform. 党内极端保守派阻止了一切改革的尝试。|近义词：reactionary, ultraconservative, right-wing|反义词：liberal, progressive, radical|词性:形容词

Etymology:
hyper- + conservative
"hyper-"  Greek *huper "over, above"
"conservative"  Latin *conservare "to keep, preserve," from com- "together" + servare "to keep safe, watch over"
