hyperparoxysm:/ˌhaɪpərˈpærəkˌsɪzəm/ | n. | 极度发作，亢进 | A sudden attack or intensification of a paroxysm (a sudden recurrence or intensification of symptoms, such as a spasm or seizure).  It implies an extreme or excessive degree of the paroxysm. | The hyperparoxysm of coughing left him breathless.  极度咳嗽让他喘不过气来。|  The child suffered a hyperparoxysm of rage. 这孩子勃然大怒。 | 近义词: spasm, fit, seizure, convulsion | 反义词: remission, abatement | 词性: noun
