beguilement:/bɪˈɡaɪlmənt/| n. |欺骗；诱惑；魅力|The act of beguiling; the state of being beguiled.  A charming or enchanting quality.|Her beguilement was evident in her captivating smile. 她的魅力在她迷人的微笑中显露无疑。|Synonyms: charm, enchantment, allure, fascination, seduction|Antonyms: repulsion, aversion, distaste|noun


beguile:/bɪˈɡaɪl/| vt. |欺骗；诱惑；使着迷|To charm or enchant (someone), sometimes in a deceptive way. To trick or deceive.|He beguiled her with promises of riches. 他用财富的承诺诱惑了她。|Synonyms: charm, enchant, allure, captivate, deceive, trick|Antonyms: repel, disgust, disenchant|verb
