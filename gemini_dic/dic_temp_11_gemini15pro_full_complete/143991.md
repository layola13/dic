outrate:/ˌaʊtˈreɪt/
verb
超过，胜过
To surpass or exceed in rate or degree.
例句：The new model outrates the old one in fuel efficiency.  新款车型在燃油效率上超过了旧款。

例句：Our sales team outrated all the others this quarter. 我们的销售团队在本季度超过了所有其他团队。

近义词：surpass, exceed, outstrip, outdo, outperform
反义词：lag behind, fall behind
词性：verb
