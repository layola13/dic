languid:/ˈlæŋɡwɪd/| adj.|倦怠的，懒洋洋的，没精打采的|Displaying or having a disinclination for physical exertion or effort; slow and relaxed.|She gave him a languid smile. 她懒洋洋地冲他笑了笑。|Synonyms: listless, lethargic, sluggish, inert, apathetic, phlegmatic, indolent, idle, lazy, dreamy, lackadaisical |Antonyms: energetic, vigorous, lively|adjective
