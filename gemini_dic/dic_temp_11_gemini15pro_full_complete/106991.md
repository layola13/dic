lacquered:/ˈlækəd/ (US), /ˈlækəd/ (UK)| adj.|漆过的，涂漆的；光滑的|Coated with lacquer; having a smooth, shiny surface.|例句：The lacquered table gleamed in the sunlight.  阳光下，漆桌闪闪发光。|例句：She admired the lacquered boxes from Japan. 她很欣赏那些来自日本的漆盒。|近义词：polished, varnished, glazed, glossy|反义词：unpolished, dull, matte|词性:形容词

lacquer:/ˈlækə(r)/ (US), /ˈlækə(r)/ (UK)| n. vt.|漆，漆器；涂漆|A hard, glossy, protective coating; to coat with lacquer.|例句：He applied a coat of lacquer to the wood. 他在木头上涂了一层漆。|例句：The lacquer on the antique chest was chipped. 古董箱上的漆剥落了。|近义词：varnish, shellac, enamel|反义词：N/A|词性:名词,动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *lacre*, from Portuguese *lacre* or Spanish *laca*, ultimately from Persian *lāk*, meaning "lac," a resinous substance secreted by certain insects.
