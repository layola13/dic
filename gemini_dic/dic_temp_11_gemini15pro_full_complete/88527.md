hit-bottom:/hɪt ˈbɑːtəm/ | n. | 最低点 | The lowest point, especially of a price, value, or level of personal well-being.  | The economy finally seems to have reached hit bottom. 经济似乎终于触底了。 | synonyms: rock bottom, bottom, nadir | antonyms: peak, apex, summit | noun
