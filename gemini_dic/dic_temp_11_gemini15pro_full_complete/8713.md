anticultural:/ˌæntiˈkʌltʃərəl/
adj.
反文化的，对抗文化的
Opposing or hostile to the established culture.
His anticultural stance made him unpopular with the mainstream art community. 他反文化的立场使他不受主流艺术界的欢迎。

近义词:countercultural, nonconformist, unconventional, rebellious, iconoclastic
反义词:cultural, conventional, traditional, conformist, mainstream
词性:adjective
