hopelessnesses:/ˈhəʊplɪsnəsɪz/ /ˈhoʊplɪsnəsɪz/
noun
绝望，无望
The state of being without hope; a feeling of despair or futility.

Near Synonyms: despair, despondency, desperation, hopelessness
Antonyms: hope, optimism, hopefulness

noun
绝望，无望
The state of being without hope; a feeling of despair or futility.

词源: From hopeless + -ness.  Hopeless comes from Middle English *hopeles*, from Old English *hopelos*, from hope + -less.
