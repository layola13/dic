negotiation:/nɪˌɡoʊʃiˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 谈判，协商，磋商 | the process of discussing something with someone in order to reach an agreement |  The negotiation between the two companies lasted for months. 两家公司之间的谈判持续了几个月。 | synonyms: bargaining, discussion, deliberation | antonyms:  dictation, imposition | noun
