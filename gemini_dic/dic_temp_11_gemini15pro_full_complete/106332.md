kogechow: /ˈkoʊɡəˌtʃaʊ/ | int. |  好啊; 真棒; 太好了 |  A slang term expressing excitement, approval, or congratulations, often used in online gaming communities, particularly associated with Twitch streamer xQc.  It's a combination of "Kreygasm" (another Twitch emote expressing excitement) and pogchamp (another popular Twitch emote) blended with xQc's pronunciation. |  Kogechow! We won the match!  好啊！我们赢了比赛！|  Synonyms: Poggers, PogChamp, Kreygasm, awesome, great, excellent | Antonyms:  Boo, awful, terrible | interjection
