hypersuspiciousness:/ˌhaɪpər səˈspɪʃəsnəs/| n.|过度怀疑，极度猜疑|Excessive suspiciousness; a tendency to distrust others or interpret their actions as hostile or harmful.|His hypersuspiciousness made it difficult for him to form close relationships. 他极度多疑，难以建立亲密关系。|近义词：paranoia, distrust, mistrust|反义词：trust, confidence|词性:名词

词源:  "hyper-" (Greek: ὑπέρ, hupér, 'over, beyond') + "suspicious" (from Latin suspiciosus 'suspecting, mistrustful') + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality).
