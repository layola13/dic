khaudarak: /kʰɔːdəˈrɑːk/ | adj. | 自私的，贪婪的 | Selfish, greedy; having a strong desire to possess more than one needs or deserves.  Derived from Hindi/Urdu "khudgarz" (خودغرض). | Example: He was so khaudarak that he hoarded all the food for himself. 他是如此的自私，把所有的食物都据为己有。| Example: Her khaudarak nature made it difficult for her to share anything. 她贪婪的本性使她很难分享任何东西。 | Synonym: selfish, greedy, avaricious, covetous | Antonym: generous, altruistic, selfless | Word Type: Adjective
