marrowbone: /ˈmærəʊˌbəʊn/ (UK) /ˈmærˌoʊˌboʊn/ (US) | n. | 骨髓；精髓；活力；管乐器(常复数) |  The hard bone that contains marrow, especially a large bone from the leg of an animal.  Figuratively, it can also refer to strength, vigor, or essential elements. In some contexts, it refers to a wind instrument, often used in the plural. |  They made a rich broth from marrowbones.  他们用骨髓熬制了浓汤。 | synonyms: bone, core, essence, pith, vigor, stamina, wind instrument | antonyms: weakness, lethargy | noun
