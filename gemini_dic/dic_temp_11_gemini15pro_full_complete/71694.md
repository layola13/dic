flavian:/ˈfleɪviən/| adj. n.|弗拉维王朝的；弗拉维王朝的人|Of or relating to the Roman emperors Vespasian, Titus, and Domitian or their dynasty (AD 69–96).  Relating to the architectural style prevalent during the Flavian dynasty, characterized by grandeur and elaborate ornamentation.|The Colosseum is a prime example of Flavian architecture.  罗马斗兽场是弗拉维王朝建筑的典型代表。| The Flavian Amphitheatre was completed during the reign of Titus. 弗拉维圆形剧场在提图斯统治时期竣工。|近义词：Roman, imperial|反义词：-|adjective, noun
