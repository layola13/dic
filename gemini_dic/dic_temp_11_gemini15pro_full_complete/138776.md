numerous:/ˈnjuːmərəs/| adj.|许多的，众多的|A large number; many.|例句：Numerous studies have shown the link between smoking and cancer. 许多研究表明吸烟和癌症之间存在联系。|例句：There are numerous opportunities for career advancement within the company. 公司内部有许多职业发展的机会。|近义词：many, several, plentiful, abundant, copious|反义词：few, scarce, limited|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle French *nombreus*, from Latin *numerosus* "very numerous, rhythmical," from *numerus* "number."
