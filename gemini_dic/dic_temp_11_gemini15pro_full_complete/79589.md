gloppy:/ˈɡlɑːpi/| adj.|黏糊糊的；湿软的|Resembling glue in texture; sticky and viscous.  Covered with or containing a sticky or viscous substance.|The oatmeal was too gloppy to eat. 燕麦粥太黏糊糊了，没法吃。|近义词：gooey, sticky, viscous, slimy|反义词：dry, firm|词性:形容词
Etymology:  Likely a blend of "glob" (a lump of a viscous substance) and "-y" (a suffix forming adjectives).  "Glob" itself is of uncertain origin, possibly imitative.
