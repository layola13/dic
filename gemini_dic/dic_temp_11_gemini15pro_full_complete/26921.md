calamistrum:/kəˈlæmɪstrəm/| n. |梳毛器；栉状器|A row of curved bristles on the metatarsus of the fourth leg of some spiders, used to comb out the silk from the spinnerets.|The spider used its calamistrum to create a fluffy web.|近义词: hackle |反义词: None |noun
