heinous:/ˈheɪnəs/| adj.|可憎的，十恶不赦的|Utterly odious or wicked.|The judge called the crime heinous and sentenced the perpetrator to life in prison. 法官称这起犯罪令人发指，判处犯罪者无期徒刑。|近义词：abominable, atrocious, monstrous, wicked, evil, odious|反义词：praiseworthy, commendable, laudable|词性:形容词
词源:From Old French *haineus* "hateful," from *haine* "hatred," from Frankish *haini*, from Proto-Germanic *hatiz*.
