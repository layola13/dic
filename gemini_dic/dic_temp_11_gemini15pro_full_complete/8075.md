anthropomorphized: /ˌænθrəpəˈmɔːrfaɪzd/ | adj. | 拟人化的 | Given human characteristics or behavior.  Attributing human traits, emotions, or intentions to non-human entities (like animals, objects, or concepts). | The fox in the fable was anthropomorphized, speaking and acting like a human. 寓言中的狐狸被拟人化了，像人一样说话和行动。 |  The company anthropomorphized its product in its advertising campaign, giving it a friendly face and a catchy name. 这家公司在广告宣传中将产品拟人化，赋予它一张友好的面孔和一个朗朗上口的名字。 | personified, humanized | - | adjective
