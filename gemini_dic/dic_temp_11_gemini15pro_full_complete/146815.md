overimport:/ˌoʊvərˈɪmpɔːrt/ | n. | 过度进口 | The act of importing more goods than are needed or can be readily absorbed by the domestic market. | The overimport of cheap electronics led to the collapse of several local businesses. 过度进口廉价电子产品导致几家本地企业倒闭。 | 近义词：excess import, surplus import | 反义词: underimport | 词性: 名词

overimport:/ˌoʊvərˈɪmpɔːrt/ | v. | 过度进口 | To import more goods than are needed or can be readily absorbed by the domestic market. |  The country overimported luxury cars last year. 该国去年过度进口了豪华轿车。 | 近义词：import excessively | 反义词: underimport | 词性: 动词
