brittle:/ˈbrɪt(ə)l/| adj.|易碎的，脆弱的|Easily broken or cracked; fragile.  Having little elasticity; likely to break or snap under stress or strain, rather than bend or deform.|The old bone was brittle and snapped easily. 这块老骨头很脆，很容易折断。|Synonyms: fragile, frail, breakable, crisp, crumbly|Antonyms: flexible, resilient, tough, pliable|adjective

brittled:/ˈbrɪt(ə)ld/| adj. |变脆的|Made brittle; having been made hard and fragile.|The extreme cold brittled the metal pipes. 极度的寒冷使金属管道变脆了。|Synonyms: hardened, fragile, inflexible|Antonyms: softened, flexible, resilient|adjective
