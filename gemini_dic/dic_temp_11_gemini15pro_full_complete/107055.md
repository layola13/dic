lacuna:/ləˈkjuːnə/ (US), /ləˈkuːnə/ (UK)| n.|空白；空隙；缺失；漏洞|an empty space or missing part; a gap or deficiency|The historian noted a lacuna in the historical record concerning the king's early life. 这位历史学家注意到国王早期生活的历史记录中存在空白。|There's a lacuna in her knowledge of modern history. 她对现代历史的知识存在缺失。|synonyms: gap, void, blank, omission, hiatus|antonyms: completeness, fullness, entirety|noun
