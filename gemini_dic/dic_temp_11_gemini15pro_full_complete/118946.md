meteoroid:/ˈmiːtiərɔɪd/| n.|流星体|A small body moving in the solar system that would become a meteor if it entered the Earth's atmosphere.|A meteoroid entered the Earth's atmosphere and became a fiery meteor. 一颗流星体进入地球大气层，变成了一颗火流星。|Synonyms: meteor, shooting star|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps planet, asteroid as larger celestial bodies)|Noun
