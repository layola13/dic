monopolise:/məˈnɒpəlaɪz/ (UK) /məˈnɑːpəlaɪz/ (US)| v. |垄断，独占|To obtain exclusive possession or control of.| The company has monopolised the market for this type of software.  这家公司垄断了这种软件的市场。|Synonyms: dominate, control, corner|Antonyms: share, distribute|verb

monopolization:/məˌnɒpəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ (UK) /məˌnɑːpəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ (US) | n. |垄断，独占|The act of monopolising something.| The government is investigating the company for monopolization of the market. 政府正在调查该公司垄断市场的情况。|Synonyms: domination, control|Antonyms: sharing, distribution|noun
