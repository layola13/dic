decimator:/ˈdesɪmeɪtər/ | n. | 大量毁灭者；屠杀者；每十人杀一人者 |  A person or thing that decimates something, reducing its size or strength greatly. | The disease was a decimator of the population. 这种疾病是人口的大量毁灭者。| The locusts were the decimators of the crops. 蝗虫是农作物的大量毁灭者。 | synonyms: destroyer, killer, exterminator | antonyms: preserver, savior, protector | noun

decimate:/ˈdesɪmeɪt/ | v. | 大量毁灭；杀死（尤指群体中的十分之一）；大大削弱 | To destroy or kill a large part of (something). Originally, to kill every tenth person. | The population was decimated by the plague. 鼠疫毁灭了大量人口。| The hurricane decimated the coastal towns. 飓风摧毁了沿海城镇。 | synonyms: destroy, devastate, ruin | antonyms: build, create, restore | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *decimatus*, past participle of *decimare* "to take the tenth, tithe, select by lot and kill every tenth man," from *decimus* "tenth," from *decem* "ten."
