一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think about this book? = 你觉得这本书怎么样？
What does she usually do on weekends? = 她周末通常做什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing right now? = 他现在在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she been up to lately? = 她最近在忙些什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all this time? = 你这段时间一直在做什么？
What has he been reading recently? = 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):

What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):

What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 开会的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

What had they discussed before the decision was made? = 做决定之前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by then? = 到那时会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
