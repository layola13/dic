moonscapes:/ˈmuːnskeɪps/ | n. |月球表面；月球景色|A desolate or barren landscape resembling the surface of the moon.|The astronauts were awestruck by the otherworldly moonscapes. 宇航员们被地球之外的月球景色所震撼。|例句：

The photographs captured the stark beauty of the lunar moonscapes.
这些照片捕捉到了月球荒凉的美景。


The volcanic eruption transformed the area into a desolate moonscape.
火山爆发把这个地区变成了荒凉的月球表面。

|近义词：lunar landscapes, lunar surface |反义词: lush landscapes, fertile terrains|词性:名词
