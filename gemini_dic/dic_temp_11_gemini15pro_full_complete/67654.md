extroversively:/ˌɛkstrəˈvɜːsɪvli/
adverb|外向地；性格外向地|In a manner characterized by extroversion; outwardly or expansively.|She interacted with the guests extroversively, making everyone feel welcome.|近义词:outgoingly, gregariously, sociably|反义词:introvertedly, shyly, reservedly|词性:副词
词源: From extrovert + -ive + -ly. Extrovert comes from Latin extra- (outside) and vertere (to turn).
