currentless:/ˈkʌrəntləs/
adj.
无电流的，无水流的
Having no electric current; lacking a flow of water.
The wire was currentless, so the bulb didn't light. 这根电线没有电流，所以灯泡不亮。
The desert streambed was currentless after the long drought.  长时间的干旱后，沙漠河床干涸无水。
近义词:inactive, stagnant, dead
反义词:live, active, energized
词性:形容词

Etymology:
From "current" + "-less" (suffix indicating absence). "Current" comes from Middle English *coraunt*, from Old French *corant*, present participle of *corre*, "to run," from Latin *currere*.  The suffix "-less" is a native English suffix.
