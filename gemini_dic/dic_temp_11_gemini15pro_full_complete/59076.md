dwine:/dwaɪn/| v.| 衰落，衰败；凋谢；消亡 |To lessen gradually in strength, intensity, or power; fade away; decline. | The empire dwinled in power after the death of the emperor.  帝国在皇帝死后逐渐衰落。| 近义词: decline, diminish, fade, wane | 反义词: grow, increase, flourish, thrive | 词性: 动词

词源: From Middle English *dwinen, from Old English dwīnan (“to vanish, disappear, perish, waste away”), from Proto-Germanic *dwīnąną (“to fade, vanish”), from Proto-Indo-European *dweyh₁- (“to disappear, vanish”).
