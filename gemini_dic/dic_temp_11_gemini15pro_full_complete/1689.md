acondylous:/eɪˈkɒndɪləs/| adj.|无髁的，没有髁的|Lacking condyles; without knobs or projections, especially at the articular ends of a bone.|The fossil showed evidence of an acondylous mandible. 这块化石显示出下颌骨无髁的证据。|Synonyms: knobless, projectionless|Antonyms: condylar, condyloid|adjective
