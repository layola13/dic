fragrantly:/ˈfreɪɡrəntli/| adv.|芬芳地，香喷喷地|In a fragrant manner; sweetly smelling. |The flowers bloomed fragrantly in the garden. 花园里的花朵散发着芬芳。|The kitchen smelled fragrantly of baking bread. 厨房里弥漫着烤面包的香味。|近义词：sweetly, aromatically, redolently|反义词: acridly, foully, unpleasantly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *fragrauntly*, from Old French *fragrantement*, from Latin *fragrantem* (nominative *fragrans*), present participle of *fragrare* (“to emit a sweet smell”), from *fragrāre*, present active infinitive of *fragrō* (“to smell sweet, exhale fragrance”).
