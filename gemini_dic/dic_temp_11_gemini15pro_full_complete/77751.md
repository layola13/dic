genderless:/ˈdʒɛndələs/| adj.|无性别的，不分性别的|Not having or appearing to have a gender; androgynous.  Relating to a person who does not identify as having a particular gender.|The fashion show featured genderless clothing designs that could be worn by anyone.|Synonyms: androgynous, unisex, nongendered, agender|Antonyms: gendered, masculine, feminine|adjective
