butyraceous:/ˌbjuːtɪˈreɪʃəs/| adj.|奶油状的，似奶油的，含奶油的|Resembling or containing butter; buttery.  Derived from the Latin word "butyrum" meaning "butter."|The cheese had a smooth, butyraceous texture.  这种奶酪质地光滑，像奶油一样。|The sauce had a rich, butyraceous flavor.  酱汁味道浓郁，有奶油味。|近义词：buttery, creamy, rich|反义词：dry, thin, watery|词性:adjective
