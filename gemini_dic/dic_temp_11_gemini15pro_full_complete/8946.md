antihomosexual:/ˌæntiˌhoʊmoʊˈsɛkʃuəl/
adj.|反同性恋的，歧视同性恋的|Opposed to or prejudiced against homosexuality or homosexuals.|His antihomosexual views made him unpopular. 他的反同性恋观点让他不受欢迎。|近义词：homophobic, bigoted, prejudiced|反义词：pro-gay, gay-friendly|词性:形容词

antihomosexual:/ˌæntiˌhoʊmoʊˈsɛkʃuəl/
n.|反同性恋者|A person who is opposed to or prejudiced against homosexuality or homosexuals.|The antihomosexual protested the pride parade.  那个反同性恋者抗议骄傲游行。|近义词：homophobe, bigot|反义词：gay rights advocate|词性:名词

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek "anti")
homosexual (adjective/noun): relating to or characterized by sexual attraction to people of one's own sex (from Greek "homos" meaning "same" and Latin "sexualis")
