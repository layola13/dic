double-thinker:/ˈdʌbəlˌθɪŋkər/ | n. | 双重思想者，两面派 | A person who can hold two contradictory beliefs simultaneously and accept both of them without recognizing the contradiction. |  例句：In Orwell's 1984, the protagonist Winston struggles against the Party's manipulation of doublethinkers. 在奥威尔的《1984》中，主人公温斯顿与党操纵双重思想者的行为作斗争。

|例句：He's a real doublethinker, saying he supports free speech while simultaneously advocating for censorship. 他是一个真正的双重思想者，一方面说支持言论自由，一方面又鼓吹审查制度。 | 近义词：hypocrite, liar, deceiver | 反义词：truth-teller, honest person | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From George Orwell's novel *Nineteen Eighty-Four* (1949), combining *double* and *thinker*.
