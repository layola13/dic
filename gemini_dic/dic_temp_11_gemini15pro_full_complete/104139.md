juncture:/ˈdʒʌŋktʃər/| n.| 连接点；接合点；时机；关头|A critical juncture in the negotiations. 谈判的关键时刻。|Synonyms: joint, connection, seam, link, intersection, confluence, crossroads|Antonyms: division, separation, disconnection|noun
juncture:/ˈdʒʌŋktʃər/| n.| 特定的时刻，(尤指重要的)时刻|At this juncture, we must be very careful. 在这个关键时刻，我们必须非常小心。|Synonyms: moment, time, point, stage, occasion, instant|Antonyms: eternity|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin junctura (“a joining”), from Latin jungere (“to join”).
