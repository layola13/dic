geographers:/dʒiˈɒɡrəfəz/
n.
地理学家
Experts in geography, those who study the Earth's physical features, climates, populations, and other related phenomena.
Geographers study the earth and its land, features, and inhabitants.  地理学家研究地球及其土地、特征和居民。
The geographers mapped the unexplored territory.  地理学家绘制了未勘探的领土的地图。
近义词:cartographers, earth scientists
反义词:None
词性:Noun
