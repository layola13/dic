caveats:/ˈkæviæt/ (US), /ˈkæviæt/ (UK)| n.|警告，告诫；注意事项|A warning or proviso of specific stipulations, conditions, or limitations.|例句：He added several caveats to the agreement.  他在协议中添加了几项警告。

例句：There are a number of caveats which concern the validity of the assessment procedures. 关于评估程序的有效性，有一些需要注意的事项。

近义词：warnings, cautions, provisos, stipulations, conditions, limitations|反义词:guarantees, assurances|词性:noun
