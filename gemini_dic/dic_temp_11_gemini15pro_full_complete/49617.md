demibastion:/ˌdɛmiˈbæstʃən/| n. |半堡, 半棱堡|A projecting part of a fortification, smaller than a bastion.  A work resembling a half bastion.  A small bastion.| The demibastion offered limited protection from enemy fire.  半棱堡提供的保护有限，抵御不了敌人的炮火。

The fort's demibastion was vulnerable to attack.  堡垒的半棱堡容易受到攻击。|Synonym: half-bastion|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps full bastion or complete fortification)|noun
