eeny:/ˈini/| determiner, pronoun (used before nouns)|用于计数的词，类似于“点”|Used in children's counting rhymes, typically as part of the phrase "eeny, meeny, miny, moe".  It helps to choose someone or something at random. |Eeny, meeny, miny, moe, catch a tiger by the toe. If he hollers let him go, eeny, meeny, miny, moe. 点点豆豆，抓把老虎抓到手，如果它叫就放手，点点豆豆。|近义词: (No direct equivalent, but serves a similar function as other counting-out rhymes)|反义词: None|词性:  Determiner, Pronoun
