miscibility:/ˌmɪsɪˈbɪləti/| n.|混溶性，互溶性|The miscibility of two liquids depends on their molecular structures. 两种液体的混溶性取决于它们的分子结构。|例句：The miscibility of oil and water is very low. 油和水的混溶性很低。  The miscibility of ethanol and water is high. 乙醇和水的混溶性很高。|近义词：compatibility, blendability|反义词：immiscibility|词性:noun

Etymology->
From Late Latin *miscibilitās, from Latin miscibilis (“mixable”) + -itās (“-ity”). Equivalent to miscible +‎ -ity.
