acquitters: /əˈkwɪtərz/
| n. |  宣判无罪者；赦免者 | People who have been acquitted of a crime. Those declared not guilty by a court of law. | The jury of his peers were the acquitters in his case.  他的同辈陪审团宣判他无罪。| 近义词：exonerees, vindicated | 反义词： convicts, condemned | 词性: 名词

acquitters: /əˈkwɪtərz/
| n. | 无罪开释者；释放者 |  Individuals who pronounce someone not guilty of a charge.  | The acquitters in the trial celebrated the verdict.  审判中的无罪开释者庆祝了这一判决。| 近义词：liberators, dischargers | 反义词： accusers, prosecutors | 词性: 名词
