boringly:/ˈbɔːrɪŋli/ | adv. | 乏味地，无聊地 | In a boring manner; tediously. | The lecture was presented boringly.  讲座进行得乏味无聊。| 近义词：tediously, monotonously, dully | 反义词：interestingly, excitingly, engagingly | 词性：副词

词源: From "boring" + "-ly". "Boring" comes from the Old English "borian" meaning "to pierce, to bore."
