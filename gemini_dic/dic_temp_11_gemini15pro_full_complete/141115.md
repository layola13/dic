ontogeny:/ɒnˈtɒdʒəni/ /ɑːnˈtɑːdʒəni/| n. |个体发育;个体发生|The development of an individual organism or anatomical or behavioral feature from the earliest stage to maturity. |例句：The ontogeny of the human brain is a complex process. 人类大脑的个体发育是一个复杂的过程。

例句：Scientists study the ontogeny of birds to understand their evolution. 科学家研究鸟类的个体发育以了解它们的进化。|近义词：development, maturation, growth|反义词:phylogeny|词性:noun
