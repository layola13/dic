bigfooted:/ˌbɪɡˈfʊtɪd/| adj.|大脚的；大足的|Having large feet.  Referring to a creature, real or imaginary, with unusually large feet.  Sometimes used figuratively to describe someone clumsy or awkward.|The legendary bigfooted creature is said to roam the forests of the Pacific Northwest.  传说中的大脚怪据说在太平洋西北部的森林里游荡。|近义词：large-footed|反义词:small-footed|词性:形容词
Etymology:  Compound of "big" + "footed".  "Big" from Old English *bigge (large, strong); "foot" from Old English fōt.
