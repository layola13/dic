hypersensitive:/ˌhaɪpərˈsɛnsɪtɪv/ | adj. | 过敏的，过度敏感的 | Excessively or abnormally sensitive.  |  例句：She is hypersensitive to criticism. 她对批评过度敏感。 | 近义词：oversensitive, touchy, susceptible | 反义词：insensitive, impassive | 词性: 形容词

hypersensitive:/ˌhaɪpərˈsɛnsɪtɪv/ | n. | 过敏体质的人 | A person who is excessively or abnormally sensitive. | 例句：Hypersensitives often struggle with loud noises. 过敏体质的人经常难以忍受噪音。 | 近义词：oversensitive person | 反义词：insensitive person | 词性: 名词
