love-matches:/ˈlʌv mætʃɪz/
noun
爱情婚姻；恋爱结婚；因相爱而结婚
a marriage between people who love each other, rather than for other reasons (such as money or social status)
They had a true love-match and were very happy together. 他们拥有真正的爱情婚姻，并且在一起非常幸福。
Their parents disapproved of their love-match.  他们的父母不赞成他们的恋爱婚姻。
synonyms: love marriage
antonyms: arranged marriage
