deleter:/dɪˈliːtər/| n.|删除者，删除程序|A person or thing that deletes something. |The deleter removed all traces of the file. 删除程序删除了该文件的所有痕迹。|近义词：remover, eraser, expunger|反义词：creator, adder, writer|词性:noun

词源: From the verb "delete" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Delete" comes from Latin "dēlēre" meaning "to destroy, wipe out, blot out."
