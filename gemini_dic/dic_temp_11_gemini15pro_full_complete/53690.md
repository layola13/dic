discipliner:/ˈdɪsəplɪnər/| n.|训练者，执行纪律者|A person who enforces discipline.|The coach was a strict discipliner, ensuring all team members followed the rules. 教练是一个严格的训练者，确保所有队员都遵守规则。|Synonyms: enforcer, regulator, instructor|Antonyms: lenient person, pushover|noun

discipline:/ˈdɪsəplɪn/| n. vt.|纪律，学科；训练，惩罚|Training to improve strength or self-control; controlled behavior resulting from such training; a branch of knowledge, typically one studied in higher education; punish or rebuke formally for an offense.|He lacked the discipline to study regularly. 他缺乏规律学习的纪律性。  The teacher disciplined the student for talking in class. 老师因为学生上课讲话而惩罚了他。|Synonyms: order, training, control, punishment, subject, field|Antonyms: chaos, disorder, freedom, reward|noun, verb
