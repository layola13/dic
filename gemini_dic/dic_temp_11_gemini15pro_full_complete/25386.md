bulbous:/ˈbʌlbəs/| adj.|球根状的；膨胀的；圆形的|Resembling a bulb, especially in being swollen or rounded.|The plant has a bulbous root.  这种植物有球茎状的根。|The man had a rather bulbous nose.  这个男人有一个相当大的圆鼻子。|swollen, rounded, bulging, protuberant|slender, thin, narrow|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin bulbosus, from Latin bulbus (“bulb, onion”) + -osus (suffix forming adjectives meaning "full of, abounding in").  Ultimately from Ancient Greek βολβός (bolbós, “bulb, onion”).
