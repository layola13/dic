eeriness:/ˈɪərɪnɪs/
n.
怪异；阴森；神秘
The quality of being eerie; strangeness or unnaturalness that causes a vague feeling of fear.
The eeriness of the abandoned house made the children afraid to enter.  废弃房屋的阴森气息让孩子们不敢进去。

近义词: spookiness, creepiness, uncanniness, weirdness
反义词: normalcy, ordinariness, familiarity

词性: noun

词源: From eerie + -ness.  Eerie itself comes from Old English earg, meaning "timid, cowardly." The meaning shifted to "supernatural, inspiring fear" over time.
