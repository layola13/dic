aeneous:/ˈiːniəs/| adj.|青铜色的，铜色的|Made of or resembling bronze; bronze-colored.|The statue had an aeneous sheen, gleaming in the sunlight. 这座雕像有着青铜色的光泽，在阳光下闪闪发光。|近义词：bronze, brazen, coppery|反义词：silver, golden|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *aeneus*, from *aes* (“bronze, copper”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂éyos* ("metal, bronze, copper").
