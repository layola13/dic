anaplasty:/ˈænəˌplæsti/ | n. | 整形术，成形术 |Surgical restoration or repair of deformed or congenitally absent parts of the body, especially of the face.  |  Example: He underwent anaoplasty after the accident to reconstruct his nose. 他在事故后接受了鼻部重建整形手术。

Example:  Anaplasty can significantly improve the quality of life for individuals with birth defects.  整形手术可以显著提高出生缺陷者的生活质量。 | Synonym:  reconstructive surgery, plastic surgery | Antonym: N/A | noun
