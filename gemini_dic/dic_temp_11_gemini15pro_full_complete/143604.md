outintrigue: /ˌaʊtɪnˈtriːɡ/
verb
以智取胜，比…更会耍阴谋诡计
to surpass or defeat in intrigue; to be more intriguing than
She outintrigued her rivals and won the CEO position. 她智胜对手，赢得了CEO的职位。
He outintrigued the other bidders and secured the antique. 他比其他竞标者更会耍手腕，最终获得了这件古董。
近义词: outmaneuver, outsmart, outwit
反义词: be outwitted, be outsmarted
