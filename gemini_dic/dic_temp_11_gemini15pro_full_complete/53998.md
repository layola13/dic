discriminates:/dɪˈskrɪmɪneɪts/| v.| 区别对待；歧视；区分；辨别 |This test discriminates between those with the disease and those without.  这项测试区分了患有这种疾病的人和没有患病的人。|例句：The law discriminates against women. 这项法律歧视女性。|近义词：differentiates, distinguishes, biases, prejudices|反义词： equates, confuses, includes|词性:动词

discriminating:/dɪˈskrɪmɪneɪtɪŋ/| adj.| 有识别力的；有鉴赏力的；敏锐的|She has a discriminating palate. 她有敏锐的味觉。|例句：He is a discriminating collector of antique books. 他是一位有鉴赏力的古籍收藏家。|近义词： discerning, selective, judicious|反义词： undiscriminating, indiscriminate, uncritical|词性:形容词

discriminatory:/dɪˈskrɪmɪnəˌtɔːri/| adj.| 歧视性的；差别待遇的|Discriminatory practices in hiring are illegal.  招聘中的歧视性做法是非法的。|例句：The government has promised to abolish all discriminatory laws. 政府已承诺废除所有歧视性法律。|近义词： biased, prejudiced, unfair|反义词： fair, impartial, unbiased|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin discriminare "to divide, separate, distinguish," from discrimen "interval, difference," from discernere "to distinguish between."
