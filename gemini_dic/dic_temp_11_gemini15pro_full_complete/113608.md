macerators:/ˈmæsəˌreɪtərz/
n.
浸渍器; 软化剂; 沤制器
A machine that macerates substances, especially organic matter.  A substance used for macerating.
The macerators ground the food waste into a slurry. 这些浸渍器将食物残渣研磨成浆状物。
The hospital uses macerators to dispose of contaminated waste. 医院使用浸渍器处理污染废物。
synonyms: grinders, pulverizers, crushers, softeners
antonyms: hardeners, solidifiers
noun
