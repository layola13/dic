monobasic:/ˌmɒnəʊˈbeɪsɪk/ | adj. | 一元的；单碱的 | Having only one hydrogen atom replaceable by a metal or positive radical to form a salt. | Phosphoric acid is tribasic, while nitric acid is monobasic. 磷酸是三元的，而硝酸是一元的。 | Synonyms: univalent, monoprotic | Antonyms: polybasic, dibasic, tribasic | word type: adjective
