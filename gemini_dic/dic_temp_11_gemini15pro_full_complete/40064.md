contraction:/kənˈtrækʃn/ | n. |收缩，缩小；缩写；宫缩|  The contraction of the muscles allows movement. 肌肉的收缩使运动成为可能。| abbreviation, shortening, reduction | expansion, enlargement | noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *contraccioun, from Late Latin contractionem, accusative singular of contractio (“a drawing together”), from Latin contractus, past participle of contrahere (“to draw together, contract”).
