hipshot:/ˈhɪpʃɒt/ (US) /ˈhɪpʃɑːt/| adj.| 仓促的，草率的，未经深思熟虑的 |Done quickly and without careful thought; impulsive.|He made a hipshot decision to sell the company. 他草率地决定出售公司。|She gave a hipshot answer without thinking about the consequences. 她未经思考就草率地回答了问题。|近义词: hasty, impulsive, rash, reckless|反义词: careful, considered, deliberate, thoughtful|词性:形容词

词源:  From *hip* (n.) + *shot* (adj.). Originally referring to shooting from the hip without aiming carefully.  源自 *hip*（臀部）+ *shot*（射击）。最初指的是从臀部射击而不仔细瞄准。
