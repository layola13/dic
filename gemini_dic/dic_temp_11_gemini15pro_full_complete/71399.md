flagration:/fləˈɡreɪʃn/
n.
大火，烈火；爆发，突发
a large destructive fire; a sudden outburst
The forest fire was a flagration that destroyed thousands of acres. 这场森林大火是一场毁灭了数千英亩土地的熊熊烈火。
The sudden flagration of violence shocked the city. 突如其来的暴力爆发震惊了整个城市。
近义词：inferno, blaze, conflagration
反义词：deluge (flood)
