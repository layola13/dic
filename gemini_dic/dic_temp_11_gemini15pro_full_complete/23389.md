brachycephalism: /ˌbrækiːˈsɛfəlɪzəm/ | n. | 短头畸形 | A condition characterized by a disproportionately short head, broad face, and flattened nose. |  Brachycephalism is a common trait in certain dog breeds like Bulldogs and Pugs. 短头畸形是某些犬种（如斗牛犬和哈巴狗）的常见特征。

Brachycephalism can sometimes cause breathing difficulties. 短头畸形有时会导致呼吸困难。

| Synonyms: short-headedness | Antonyms: dolichocephalism (长头畸形) | noun
