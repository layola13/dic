noninformativeness:/ˌnɒnɪnˈfɔːrmətɪvnəs/ | n. | 无信息性，不提供信息 | Lack of providing useful or relevant information.  The noninformativeness of the data made it impossible to draw any conclusions. 数据缺乏信息，无法得出任何结论。 | 近义词: uninformativeness, meaninglessness, inanity | 反义词: informativeness, significance, meaningfulness | 词性: noun

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + informative + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality)
