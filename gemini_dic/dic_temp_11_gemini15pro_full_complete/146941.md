overinterestedness:/ˌoʊvərˈɪntrəstɪdnəs/ | n. | 过分感兴趣，过度关注 |  The state of being excessively interested in something, often to an inappropriate or intrusive degree.  This can manifest as nosiness or being overly involved in the affairs of others. | Her overinterestedness in her neighbor's life made them uncomfortable. 她对她邻居生活的过度关注让邻居们感到不舒服。| 近义词: nosiness, inquisitiveness, intrusiveness | 反义词: apathy, indifference, detachment | 词性: 名词

词源:
"over-" (prefix) + "interested" (adjective) + "-ness" (suffix)
"over-" 表示 "过度"、"过分"
"interested" 源自拉丁语 "interesse"，意为 "to be of importance, to concern"
"-ness" 后缀用于构成名词，表示状态或性质
