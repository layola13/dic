falling: /ˈfɔːlɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈfɑːlɪŋ/ (US)
adjective
下降的；落下的；掉落的
decreasing; descending; dropping
例句：
The falling leaves are a sign of autumn.
落叶是秋天的迹象。

Falling prices are good for consumers.
价格下跌对消费者有利。

近义词: descending, dropping, plummeting, sinking, tumbling
反义词: rising, ascending, climbing, soaring


falling: /ˈfɔːlɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈfɑːlɪŋ/ (US)
noun
落下；下降；陷落
the act of falling; a decrease
例句：
The falling of the Berlin Wall was a historic event.
柏林墙的倒塌是一个历史性事件。

The heavy rain caused a falling of rocks from the cliff.
暴雨导致悬崖上的岩石坠落。

近义词: descent, drop, decline, fall
反义词: rise, ascent, climb


falling: /ˈfɔːlɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈfɑːlɪŋ/ (US)
verb
落下；下降；掉落 (present participle of fall)
descending; dropping; decreasing
例句：
The snow is falling heavily.
雪下得很大。

The temperature is falling rapidly.
温度正在迅速下降。

近义词: descending, dropping, plummeting, sinking, tumbling
反义词: rising, ascending, climbing, soaring

词源: From Middle English *fallinge*, from Old English *fallende* (present participle of *fallan* (“to fall”)), from Proto-Germanic *fallaną* (“to fall”), from Proto-Indo-European *phol- (“to fall, lie”). Cognate with Dutch vallend, German fallend, Swedish fallande.
