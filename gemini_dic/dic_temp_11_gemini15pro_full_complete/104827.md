kedgeree:/ˈkɛdʒəri/ (US), /ˈkɛdʒəri/ (UK)| n.|咖喱鱼饭，鱼肉烩饭|A dish of cooked fish (especially smoked haddock), rice, boiled eggs, butter, cream, and spices.|例句：We had kedgeree for breakfast. 我们早餐吃了咖喱鱼饭。|例句：The kedgeree was flavorful and filling. 这咖喱鱼饭味道鲜美，令人饱腹。|近义词：fish and rice dish, rice pilaf|反义词: None|词性:noun

Etymology: From the Hindi word *khichṛī*, meaning a dish of rice and lentils.
