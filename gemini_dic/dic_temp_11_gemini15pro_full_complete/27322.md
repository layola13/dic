calyptriform: /kəˈlɪptrɪfɔːrm/
adjective
帽状的，似帽状体的
Resembling a calyptra; shaped like a hood or cap.  Having the form or appearance of a calyptra.
The moss capsule is calyptriform.  这个苔藓的孢蒴是帽状的。
The flower has calyptriform petals. 这朵花有帽状的花瓣。
Near Synonyms: hooded, capped, mitrate
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider describing the opposite shape, e.g., flat, open, spreading)
Etymology: From Latin *calyptra* (from Greek *καλύπτρα* (kalúptra) meaning 'covering, veil') + *-formis* (meaning 'having the form of').
