nondiaphanousness:/nɒndaɪəˈfænəsnəs/| n. |不透明性，不透光性|The quality or state of being nondiaphanous; opacity.|The nondiaphanousness of the frosted glass provided privacy. 磨砂玻璃的不透明性提供了隐私。|近义词：opacity, opaqueness|反义词：transparency, translucence|词性: noun

Etymology:
non- (prefix): not
diaphanous (adjective): allowing light to pass through; transparent
-ness (suffix): forming nouns expressing state or quality
