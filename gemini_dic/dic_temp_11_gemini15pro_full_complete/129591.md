non-committalism:/ˌnɒn kəˈmɪtəlɪzəm/ (US) /ˌnɒn kəˈmɪtəlɪzəm/ (UK)| n.|不表态，不承诺|The state of being non-committal; unwillingness to commit oneself to a particular opinion or course of action.|His non-committalism on the issue frustrated everyone.|近义词：indecision, reluctance, hesitancy, ambiguity, evasiveness|反义词：commitment, decisiveness, resoluteness|词性:名词

non-committal:/ˌnɒn kəˈmɪtəl/ (US) /ˌnɒn kəˈmɪtəl/ (UK)| adj.|不表态的，不承诺的|Not expressing or revealing commitment to a definite opinion or course of action.|She gave a non-committal answer to the journalist's question.|近义词：evasive, ambiguous, guarded, reserved, uninformative|反义词：committed, decisive, resolute, definite, forthcoming|词性:形容词
