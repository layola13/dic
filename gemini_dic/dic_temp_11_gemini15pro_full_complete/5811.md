amassed:/əˈmæst/| v. |积累，积聚|to gather together or accumulate (something, especially a large amount) over a period of time.|She has amassed a fortune in real estate. 她积累了大量的房地产财富。|Synonyms: accumulate, gather, collect, garner, compile|Antonyms: disperse, scatter, dissipate, squander, spend|verb

amassed:/əˈmæst/| adj. |积累的，积聚的 (This usage is less common but can be found, especially in older texts.)|gathered or accumulated, especially in large quantities.|His amassed wealth was legendary. 他积累的财富是传奇的。|Synonyms: accumulated, gathered, collected|Antonyms: dispersed, scattered, dissipated|adjective
