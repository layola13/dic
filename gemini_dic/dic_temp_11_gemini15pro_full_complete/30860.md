chadors:/ˈtʃɑːdɔːrz/| n.|伊朗式长袍，罩袍|A full-length garment worn by some Muslim women, especially in Iran, which covers the body from head to toe.| Many women in Iran wear chadors in public.  许多伊朗女性在公共场合穿着罩袍。|  The women donned their chadors before leaving the house.  这些女人在出门前穿上了她们的罩袍。|近义词：veil, hijab, burka|反义词:(No direct antonym, possibly Western-style clothing)|名词
