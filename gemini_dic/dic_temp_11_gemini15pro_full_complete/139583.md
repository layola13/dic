obviation:/ˌɒbviˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 消除，避免，排除 | The invention of the internet obviated the need for sending letters by post. 互联网的发明消除了通过邮寄信件的需要。| The new medicine obviates the need for surgery. 这种新药避免了手术的需要。|  近义词：prevention, avoidance, elimination, preclusion | 反义词: causation, necessity | 词性: 名词

obviate:/ˈɒbvieɪt/ | vt. | 消除，避免，排除 |  Regular exercise can obviate the need for medication in some cases.  在某些情况下，定期锻炼可以避免药物治疗的需要。|  The new security measures obviated the risk of theft. 新的安全措施排除了盗窃的风险。| 近义词：prevent, avoid, eliminate, preclude | 反义词: cause, necessitate | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *obviātiōn- (stem of obviātiō), from Latin obviātus, past participle of obviāre (“to meet or encounter; to go towards; to prevent, obviate”). Equivalent to obviate + -ion.
