neutrophil: /ˈnjuːtrəfɪl/ (US), /ˈnjuːtrəfɪl/ (UK) | n. | 中性粒细胞 | A type of white blood cell that is an important part of the immune system and helps the body fight infection by ingesting microorganisms and releasing enzymes that kill them. |  Neutrophils are the most abundant type of white blood cell in the human body. 中性粒细胞是人体内数量最多的一种白细胞。 |  A low neutrophil count can increase your risk of infection. 中性粒细胞计数低会增加感染的风险。 | Synonyms: polymorphonuclear leukocyte, PMN, granulocyte | Antonyms: N/A | noun
