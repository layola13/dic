acerous:/ˈæsərəs/| adj.|针状的；似针的|Resembling a needle; needle-shaped.  Thin and pointed, like a needle.|The plant had long, acerous leaves.  这种植物长着细长的针状叶子。|近义词：needle-like, acicular, setiform|反义词: broad, wide|词性: adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *acerōsus, from acer "sharp, pointed, keen" + -ōsus "-ous."
