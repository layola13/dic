disorb:/dɪˈsɔːrb/| v. |解吸，脱附|The process of a substance releasing from a surface.  A substance disorbs from a surface. |The pollutants disorbed from the soil into the groundwater. 污染物从土壤中解吸到地下水中。|近义词：desorb, release, detach|反义词：adsorb, absorb, sorb|词性:动词
Etymology: dis- (apart, away) + -sorb (to suck in)  From Latin sorbere "to suck in." The prefix "dis-" indicates reversal or separation.
