archosaurian: /ˌɑːrkəˈsɔːriən/ | adj. n. |  主龙类的；主龙类动物 | Relating to or denoting the Archosauria, a group of diapsid reptiles that includes crocodiles, birds, and extinct dinosaurs. |  Birds are archosaurian descendants of theropod dinosaurs. 鸟类是兽脚亚目恐龙的主龙类后代。|  The archosaurian lineage includes some of the most iconic extinct animals, like dinosaurs.  主龙类谱系包括一些最具标志性的灭绝动物，例如恐龙。 | synonym: reptilian, saurian | antonym: mammalian | adjective, noun
