mittened:/ˈmɪtənd/ | adj. | 戴着手套的；像手套的 | Having mittens; resembling a mitten. |  She wore a thick, mittened hand to shield herself from the cold. 她戴着一只厚厚的连指手套来御寒。| 近义词: gloved | 反义词: barehanded, ungloved | 词性: 形容词

词源: From mitten + -ed.  From Middle English *mitayn, from Old French mitaine, perhaps ultimately of Germanic origin.
