epeira:/ɪˈpaɪrə/
noun
圆蛛，蜘蛛
A genus of orb-weaving spiders.  Specifically, the European garden spider, *Araneus diadematus*, was formerly classified under this genus.
The epeira spins an intricate web.  圆蛛结出一个复杂的网。
Synonyms: spider, orb-weaver, araneid
Antonyms: None (No direct antonym for a genus of spider)
词性: Noun
