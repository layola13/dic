cisterns:/ˈsɪstərnz/| n.|蓄水池；水箱|A cistern is a tank for storing water, especially rainwater collected from the roof of a building.|The old house had a cistern in the basement for collecting rainwater. 这栋老房子地下室里有一个蓄水池用来收集雨水。|Example: The cisterns were full after the heavy rain.  暴雨过后，蓄水池都满了。|Synonyms: tank, reservoir, water tank|Antonyms: drain, outlet|Noun
