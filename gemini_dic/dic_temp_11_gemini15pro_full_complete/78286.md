germicide: /ˈdʒɜːrmɪsaɪd/ /ˈdʒɜːrməsaɪd/
noun.
杀菌剂；消毒剂
A substance that kills germs (bacteria, viruses, etc.).
A germicide was used to sterilize the surgical instruments.  杀菌剂被用来给手术器械消毒。

germicide: /ˈdʒɜːrmɪsaɪd/ /ˈdʒɜːrməsaɪd/
adjective.
杀菌的
Capable of killing germs.
The germicidal properties of bleach make it a powerful cleaning agent.  漂白剂的杀菌特性使其成为一种强力清洁剂。


近义词: disinfectant, antiseptic, bactericide
反义词: None

词性: noun, adjective


词源:
From Latin *germen-* ("seed, sprout, bud") +‎ *-cida* ("killer," from caedere, "to kill"). Coined by William Williams in 1854.
