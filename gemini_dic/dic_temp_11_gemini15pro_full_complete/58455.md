ductible:/ˈdʌktəl/ (US), /ˈdʌktaɪl/ (UK)| adj.|可延展的，韧性的，易延展的|Able to be drawn out into a thin wire or hammered into thin sheets without breaking.  Relating to a material's ability to deform under tensile stress; pliable.|The copper wire is highly ductile.  这根铜线延展性很好。|malleable, pliable, flexible, plastic|brittle, rigid, inflexible, stiff|adjective

ductility:/ˌdʌkˈtɪləti/ (US), /ˌdʌkˈtɪlɪti/ (UK)| n.|延展性，韧性|The property of a material that allows it to be drawn out into a thin wire or hammered into thin sheets without breaking.|The ductility of gold makes it ideal for jewelry making.  黄金的延展性使它成为制作珠宝的理想材料。|malleability, plasticity, flexibility|brittleness, rigidity, stiffness|noun
