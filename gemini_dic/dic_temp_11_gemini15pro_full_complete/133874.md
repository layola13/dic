nongravity:/nɒnˈɡrævɪti/
adjective
非重力
Not subject to or involving gravity; weightless.
The astronauts experienced nongravity during their spacewalk. 宇航员在太空行走期间体验了失重状态。
近义词:weightlessness, zero gravity
反义词:gravity
