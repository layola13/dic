anticlassicists:/ˌæntiˈklæsɪsɪsts/ | n. |反古典主义者|People who oppose or reject the principles of classicism, especially in art, literature, and architecture. |The anticlassicists of the Romantic era championed emotion and individualism over reason and order. 浪漫主义时期的反古典主义者拥护情感和个人主义，而不是理性与秩序。|  近义词:modernists, iconoclasts | 反义词:classicists | noun

anticlassicist:/ˌæntiˈklæsɪsɪst/ | adj. |反古典主义的|Opposed to or rejecting the principles of classicism. |The anticlassicist movement gained momentum in the late 18th century. 反古典主义运动在18世纪后期获得了发展势头。|近义词:modernistic, iconoclastic| 反义词:classical | adjective
