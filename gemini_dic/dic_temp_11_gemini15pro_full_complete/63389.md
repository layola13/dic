entosternum:/ɛntoʊˈstɜːrnəm/
n.
内胸骨，腹中线
The entosternum is an internal skeletal plate in some arthropods, located on the ventral side of the thorax.
某些节肢动物的内胸骨是位于胸部腹侧的内部骨板。
The dragonfly's entosternum provides attachment points for its flight muscles.
蜻蜓的内胸骨为其飞行肌肉提供了附着点。
近义词:  sternite, ventral plate
反义词:  tergum, dorsal plate
词性: noun
