austere:/ɔːˈstɪər/ (US) /ɒˈstɪər/ (UK)| adj.| 朴素的，简朴的；严厉的，严肃的；苦行的|Severely simple and unadorned; having no comforts or luxuries; strict or severe in manner, attitude, or appearance.| The monk lived an austere life, devoid of any luxury.  这位僧侣过着简朴的生活，没有任何奢侈品。|  The courtroom had an austere atmosphere. 法庭的气氛很严肃。| synonyms: stark, simple, plain, harsh, severe, strict, ascetic | antonyms: luxurious, opulent, lavish, ornate, lenient, indulgent | adjective
