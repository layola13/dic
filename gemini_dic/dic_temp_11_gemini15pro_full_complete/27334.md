camarasaurus:/ˌkæmərəˈsɔːrəs/| n. |卡玛拉龙|A large herbivorous dinosaur of the late Jurassic period, with a long neck and tail.|The Camarasaurus was a massive quadruped. 卡玛拉龙是一种大型四足动物。|Synonym: sauropod|Antonym: (No direct antonym for a specific dinosaur)|noun
Etymology->
From Greek *kamara* (“vaulted chamber”) + *sauros* (“lizard”)
