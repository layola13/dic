abanaki:/əˈbænəki/| n. |阿贝纳基人|A member of an Algonquian people originally inhabiting Maine, New Hampshire, and adjacent parts of Canada, many of whom now live in Quebec.|The Abanaki have a rich history and culture. 阿贝纳基人有着丰富的历史和文化。|Synonym:  Algonquian, Wabanaki|Antonym: N/A|Noun
Etymology: From their own name for themselves, *wəbanakiyak*, meaning "People of the Dawnland" or "Easterners".|
