outgallop:/ˌaʊtˈɡæləp/
verb
超越, 以奔驰取胜
to gallop faster than (another horse or rider).
The young horse easily outgalloped the older one.  这匹年轻的马轻易地超过了那匹老马。
The knight outgalloped his pursuers. 骑士飞驰，甩掉了追赶他的人。
outpace, outrun, outstrip
lag behind
v.
