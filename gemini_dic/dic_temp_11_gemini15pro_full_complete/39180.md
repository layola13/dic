congee:/ˈkɒndʒiː/ (UK) /ˈkɑːndʒiː/ (US)|n.|粥，稀饭|A type of rice porridge or gruel boiled until thick and smooth, often eaten for breakfast in Asian countries.|例句：I had a bowl of congee for breakfast this morning. 我今天早上喝了一碗粥。

例句：Congee is a comforting food when you're sick. 当你生病时，粥是一种令人舒适的食物。

|近义词：porridge, gruel|反义词:|词性: noun
