over-impose:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈpoʊz/| v. |过度强加；过分施加|To impose something to an excessive degree or on something already burdened.|The new regulations were over-imposed on the already struggling businesses. 新的规章制度过度强加于已经苦苦挣扎的企业。|近义词：overload, overburden|反义词: understate, minimize|词性:动词
over-imposition:/ˌoʊvərɪmpəˈzɪʃən/| n. |过度强加；过分施加|The act of imposing something excessively.|The over-imposition of taxes led to widespread unrest.  税收的过度征收导致了 widespread 的动荡。|近义词：surfeit, excess|反义词: lack, deficiency|词性:名词
