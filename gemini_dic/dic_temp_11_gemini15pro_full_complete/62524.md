endurances:/ɪnˈdjʊərənsɪz/
n.
耐力，忍耐力，持久力
The ability to withstand hardship, adversity, or stress for a prolonged period of time.
Marathon runners need a lot of endurance. 马拉松运动员需要很大的耐力。
stamina, resilience, fortitude
fragility, weakness
noun
