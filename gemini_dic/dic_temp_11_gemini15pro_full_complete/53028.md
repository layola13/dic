diocletian:/ˌdaɪəˈkliːʃən/ |n.|戴克里先|Roman emperor (reigned AD 284-305); reorganized the later Roman Empire|Diocletian's reforms helped prolong the life of the empire. 戴克里先的改革有助于延长帝国的寿命。|Synonyms: emperor, ruler, Caesar|Antonyms: subject, citizen|noun

diocletian:/ˌdaɪəˈkliːʃən/ |adj.|戴克里先的|Relating to or characteristic of the Roman emperor Diocletian|The Diocletian period saw significant changes in the Roman Empire. 戴克里先时期，罗马帝国发生了重大变化。|Synonyms: imperial, Roman|Antonyms: modern, contemporary|adjective
