counterargued:/ˌkaʊntərˈɑːrɡjud/ | v. | 反驳，提出相反的论点 | To argue against something that someone else has said. |  He counterargued that the evidence was flawed. 他反驳说证据有缺陷。

The defense lawyer skillfully counterargued the prosecutor's claims. 辩护律师巧妙地反驳了检察官的主张。

| 近义词: rebutted, refuted, disputed, challenged | 反义词: conceded, agreed, accepted | 词性: 动词
