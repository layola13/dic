field-tests:/ˈfiːld tɛsts/
n.|现场测试，实地试验|A test conducted in the real-world environment where the product or system will be used, rather than in a laboratory or controlled setting.|The new software underwent rigorous field-tests before its official release. 新软件在正式发布前经过了严格的现场测试。|field trials, pilot studies|lab tests, simulations
词性:名词

field-test:/ˈfiːld tɛst/
v.|现场测试，实地试验|To test (something) in the real-world environment where it will be used.|The company field-tested the new drone in various weather conditions.  该公司在各种天气条件下对新型无人机进行了现场测试。|field trial, pilot test|simulate, model
词性:动词
