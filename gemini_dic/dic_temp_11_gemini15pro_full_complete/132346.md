nondegradation:/ˌnɒnˌdɛɡrəˈdeɪʃən/
n.
不退化，不降级
The principle of nondegradation ensures that environmental quality does not deteriorate.
The Environmental Protection Agency enforces regulations to prevent the nondegradation of air and water quality.
non-degradation is the principle of maintaining existing environmental quality.
例句：The nondegradation policy aims to prevent further deterioration of air quality.  不退化政策旨在防止空气质量进一步恶化。

The city council passed a nondegradation ordinance to protect the local river. 市议会通过了一项不退化条例来保护当地的河流。

近义词：preservation, conservation, protection
反义词：degradation, deterioration, decline
词性: 名词
