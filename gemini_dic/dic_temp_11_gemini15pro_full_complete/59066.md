dwey:/duːi/| adjective|应有的; 欠的; 应支付的; 到期的|Owed or owing. Due can refer to something owed or owing, such as a debt, payment, or obligation. It can also indicate something that is expected or scheduled to happen at a specific time.|例句1：The rent is due at the end of the month.  房租月底到期。

例句2：He is due a large sum of money. 他应得一大笔钱。|近义词：owed, payable, outstanding|反义词：paid, received|词性:adjective
