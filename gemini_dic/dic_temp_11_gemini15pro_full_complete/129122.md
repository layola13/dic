nizam:/nɪˈzɑːm/| n.| نظام; 统治; 组织; 安排; |A system of administration or organization, especially a political or social system.  Specifically, the former ruler of Hyderabad in India.|The Nizam of Hyderabad was fabulously wealthy. 海得拉巴的尼扎姆非常富有。|synonyms: ruler, sovereign, monarch, potentate, prince, king, emperor, sultan, emir, shah, raja, maharaja|antonyms: subject, commoner|noun
