misunderstandable:/ˌmɪsʌndərˈstændəbl/ | adj. | 可被误解的 | Likely to be misunderstood; capable of being misinterpreted. |  His handwriting is so messy that it's misunderstandable. 他的字迹太潦草了，很容易被误解。

His instructions were unclear and misunderstandable. 他的指示不清楚，容易引起误解。

Synonyms: confusing, ambiguous, unclear, equivocal, obscure
Antonyms: clear, unambiguous, perspicuous, lucid, plain
词性: 形容词
