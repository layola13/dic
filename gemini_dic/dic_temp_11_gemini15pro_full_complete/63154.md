ensuing:/ɪnˈsuːɪŋ/
adjective
随后的，接着发生的
happening after something and because of it
例句：In the ensuing days, more information came to light.  接下来的几天里，更多的信息被曝光。

例句：The ensuing chaos caused widespread panic. 随后的混乱造成了广泛的恐慌。

近义词：following, subsequent, resulting
反义词：preceding, previous, prior
词性：adjective

词源：From Middle English ensuen, from Old French ensuir, from Latin insequī (“to follow closely”), from in- (“in, on, upon”) + sequī (“to follow”).
