disconnectable:/ˌdɪskəˈnɛktəbəl/
adjective
可断开的，可拆卸的
able to be disconnected.
The battery pack is disconnectable for easy charging.  电池组可拆卸，方便充电。
The modular design allows for disconnectable components.  模块化设计允许组件可拆卸。
synonyms: detachable, removable, separable, unpluggable
antonyms: fixed, permanent, attached, non-detachable
词性: adjective
