brontosaurus:/ˌbrɒntəˈsɔːrəs/ (US) /ˌbrɒntəˈsɔːrəs/ (UK)| n. |雷龙|A large quadrupedal herbivorous dinosaur that lived in the Late Jurassic period.  It has a long neck and tail.
