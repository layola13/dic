ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + diarrhoeal, from diarrhoea ("diarrhea") + -al (suffix forming adjectives).  Diarrhoea is ultimately from Greek διαρροία (diarrhoía, “diarrhea”), from διαρρέω (diarrhéō, “I flow through”), from διά (diá, “through”) + ῥέω (rhéō, “I flow”).
