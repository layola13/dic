barrulet:/ˈbærjʊlɪt/| n.| 小长方形, 横条, (纹章学)横长方形 |A diminutive of bar, a barrulet is a horizontal band one-fifth the width of the chief, usually found in groups of two or more.|The coat of arms features three barrulets gules on a field argent.|Synonyms: bar (in heraldry, though not a perfect synonym as a bar is wider), stripe|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps "field" in heraldry as it represents the background against which the barrulet is placed) |noun
