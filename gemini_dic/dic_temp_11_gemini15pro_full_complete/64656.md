es:/ɪz/, /əz/ | v. | is 的第三人称单数现在式 | is the third-person singular present tense form of be | He es very tall. 他很高。| 近义词: exists (exists emphasizes existence itself, while "is" simply states a fact) | 反义词: isn't | 词性: 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
He es a doctor. = 他是一名医生。
She es a teacher. = 她是一名教师。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
He es playing basketball. = 他正在打篮球。
She es reading a book. = 她正在读书。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
He has never been to China.  (Doesn't directly use "es") = 他从未去过中国。
The weather has been nice today. (Doesn't directly use "es") = 今天天气很好。


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
He has been playing the piano for hours. (Doesn't directly use "es") = 他一直弹钢琴好几个小时了。
She has been working on this project for weeks. (Doesn't directly use "es") = 她一直致力于这个项目好几个星期了。

虚拟语气:
If he were taller, he could reach the shelf. (were, not es, is used in the subjunctive) = 如果他更高一点，他就能够到架子。
I wish it were summer. (were, not es, is used in the subjunctive) = 我希望现在是夏天。


被动语态:
The car es driven by him. = 车是他开的。（被动语态下使用is，而不是es）
The food es cooked by her. = 食物是她做的。（被动语态下使用is，而不是es）

疑问句:
Where es he? = 他在哪里？
How old es she? = 她多大了？


否定句:
He es not a student. = 他不是学生。
She es not happy. = 她不高兴。

PHRASE-> (Most phrases don't directly use "es" as a standalone word.  These use "is":)

What is up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What is the problem? = 问题是什么？
What is going on? = 发生了什么事？
What is new? = 有什么新鲜事？
Who is that? = 那个人是谁？


词源:  "es" comes from Middle English "is," from Old English "is," which is related to other Germanic forms of the verb "to be."  The "-s" ending marks the third-person singular present tense.
