hosted:/ˈhoʊstɪd/ (US) /ˈhəʊstɪd/ (UK)| adj. |被托管的，被主办的|Provided with or using computer servers or other computing resources from a hosting provider.|例句：The website is hosted on a secure server. 该网站托管在一个安全的服务器上。|例句：They used a hosted virtual desktop environment. 他们使用了一个托管的虚拟桌面环境。|近义词：managed, accommodated, facilitated|反义词:local, on-premises|词性:形容词

hosted:/ˈhoʊstɪd/ (US) /ˈhəʊstɪd/ (UK)| v. |主持，主办，招待|Past tense and past participle of "host".  Act as host at (an event).|例句：She hosted a dinner party for her friends. 她为朋友们举办了一个晚宴。|例句：The city hosted the Olympic Games.  这座城市主办了奥运会。|近义词：organized, presented, held|反义词:attended, participated|词性:动词
