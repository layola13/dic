chatspeak:/ˈtʃætˌspiːk/ | n. |网络语言，聊天语言| A form of slang or abbreviated language used primarily in online chat and other forms of electronic communication. | Example:  Using "LOL" and "BRB" is common chatspeak. 使用“LOL”和“BRB”是常见的网络语言。| Synonym:  netspeak, cyberslang, online slang, text speak | Antonym: formal language | noun
