blackball:/ˈblækˌbɔːl/| v. n.|投反对票；阻止；排斥；黑球|To reject or ostracize someone, often by a secret vote using a black ball.|He was blackballed from the club for his controversial views. 他因为有争议的观点而被俱乐部拒之门外。|近义词：ostracize, exclude, reject, shun, snub|反义词：welcome, accept, admit|词性：动词 & 名词

blackball:/ˈblækˌbɔːl/| n.|黑球；反对票|A black ball used in voting to indicate opposition or rejection.|His application was met with a blackball. 他的申请遭到反对。|近义词：rejection, veto|反义词：acceptance, approval|词性：名词
