fumiferous:/fjuːˈmɪfərəs/
adjective
产烟的；冒烟的
producing or emitting smoke or fumes.
The fumiferous factory polluted the air. 这家冒烟的工厂污染了空气。
The volcano became increasingly fumiferous. 这座火山冒出的烟越来越多了。
synonyms: smoky, fuming, smoldering
antonyms: clear, clean, unpolluted
word origin: from Latin *fūmifer*, from *fūmus* 'smoke' + *-fer* '-bearing'.
