impatiently: /ɪmˈpeɪʃəntli/ | adv. | 不耐烦地 | In a manner that shows a lack of patience; restlessly or annoyedly because of delays or difficulties. | He tapped his foot impatiently, waiting for the bus. 他不耐烦地敲着脚，等着公共汽车。|  The children were waiting impatiently for the movie to start. 孩子们不耐烦地等着电影开始。 | 近义词: restlessly, anxiously, eagerly (when context implies frustration with delay) | 反义词: patiently, calmly, placidly | 词性: 副词

impatient: /ɪmˈpeɪʃənt/ | adj. | 不耐烦的 |  Having or showing a tendency to be quickly irritated or provoked. | He was impatient with the slow service at the restaurant.  他对餐馆里缓慢的服务很不耐烦。| She became impatient when her computer froze. 当她的电脑死机时，她变得不耐烦。 | 近义词: restless, fidgety, restive | 反义词: patient, tolerant, forbearing | 词性: 形容词

patience: /ˈpeɪʃəns/ | n. | 耐心 | The capacity to accept or tolerate delay, trouble, or suffering without getting angry or upset. |  She showed great patience with the difficult child. 她对这个难缠的孩子表现出了极大的耐心。 |  Learning a new language requires patience and persistence. 学习一门新的语言需要耐心和毅力。| 近义词: tolerance, forbearance, endurance | 反义词: impatience, intolerance, irritability | 词性: 名词
