jet lag: /ˈdʒɛt læɡ/ (US) /ˈdʒɛt læɡ/ (UK)| n.|时差反应；时差综合症|A disruption of circadian rhythms due to crossing multiple time zones rapidly, typically by airplane. It can manifest as fatigue, insomnia, and other physical and mental symptoms.|I'm suffering from jet lag after my trip to Tokyo. 我东京之行后一直受时差反应的困扰。|Synonyms:  time-zone syndrome, circadian dysrhythmia|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, one might consider "acclimatization")|Noun
