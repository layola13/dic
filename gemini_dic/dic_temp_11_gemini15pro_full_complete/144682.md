outyielded:/ˌaʊtˈjiːldɪd/
verb
产量超过，胜过
produced a greater yield than
Example: This year, the new strain of wheat outyielded the old one by 15%. 今年，新品种小麦的产量比旧品种高出 15%。

Example:  The company's new product outyielded all its competitors in the first quarter. 该公司的新产品在第一季度的产量超过了所有竞争对手。
Synonym: surpassed, exceeded, outproduced, outstripped
Antonym: underperformed, lagged behind
词性: 动词(verb)
