contradictory:/ˌkɒntrəˈdɪktəri/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈdɪktəri/| adj.|矛盾的，对立的，不一致的|Containing or characterized by contradiction; mutually opposed or inconsistent.|The witness gave contradictory statements to the police. 证人向警方提供了相互矛盾的陈述。|Synonyms: conflicting, opposing, inconsistent, incompatible, contrary|Antonyms: consistent, compatible, harmonious, agreeing, concordant|adjective
contradictory:/ˌkɒntrəˈdɪktəri/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈdɪktəri/| n.|矛盾因素，对立物|A proposition, statement, or phrase that asserts or implies both the truth and falsity of something.|His behavior was a contradictory to his words. 他的行为与他的言语相矛盾。|Synonyms: paradox, inconsistency, contradiction|Antonyms: consistency, agreement, harmony|noun
