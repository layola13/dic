elevational:/ˌɛləˈveɪʃənəl/
adjective
高度的; 海拔的;  与海拔有关的;
Relating to elevation, especially above sea level.  It describes something related to height or altitude.
The elevational gradient of species richness is a key topic in ecology.  物种丰富度的海拔梯度是生态学中的一个关键主题。
The study examined elevational changes in vegetation.  这项研究考察了植被的海拔变化。
近义词: altitudinal, hypsometric
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "lowland" as a contrasting concept)
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From elevation + -al.  Elevation comes from the Late Latin ēlevātiōn- (stem of ēlevātiō ), from the past participle stem of ēlevāre  "to lift up,"  from e- "out, up" + levare "to raise."
