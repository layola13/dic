destressing:/diːˈstrɛsɪŋ/
adjective|减压的，缓解压力的|Reducing or relieving stress; relaxing.|例句：I find gardening a very destressing activity. 我发现园艺是一项非常减压的活动。|近义词：relaxing, calming, soothing, therapeutic|反义词：stressful, anxiety-inducing|词性: adjective
destress:/diːˈstrɛs/
verb|缓解压力，减压|To reduce or relieve stress; to relax.|例句：I need to find a way to destress after a long day at work.  我需要找到一种方法来缓解工作一天后的压力。|近义词：relax, unwind, de-stress, chill out|反义词：stress, tense up|词性: verb
ETYMOLOGY->
From de- + stress.  "De-" is a prefix meaning "remove" or "reduce". "Stress" refers to a state of mental or emotional strain or tension.
