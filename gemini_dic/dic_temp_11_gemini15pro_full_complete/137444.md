nontherapeutical:/ˌnɒnθɛrəˈpjuːtɪkəl/
adjective
非治疗性的
Not designed to treat an illness or medical condition.  Relating to something that does not involve therapy or treatment.
This cream has nontherapeutical benefits for the skin, making it softer and smoother.  这款面霜对皮肤有非治疗性的益处，使其更柔软光滑。
The study explored the nontherapeutical uses of certain herbs. 这项研究探索了某些草药的非治疗用途。
Synonyms: non-therapeutic, non-medicinal
Antonyms: therapeutic, medicinal
词性: 形容词
