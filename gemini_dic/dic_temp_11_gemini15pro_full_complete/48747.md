definitely:/ˈdefɪnɪtli/ (US), /ˈdefɪnətli/ (UK)| adv.|肯定地，明确地|Without any doubt; certainly.|I will definitely be there. 我一定会在那里的。

例句：
She definitely improved her English skills.
她英语水平确实提高了。

例句：
I definitely want to go to the party.
我肯定想去参加派对。


近义词：certainly, absolutely, undoubtedly, for sure, without a doubt
反义词：possibly, maybe, perhaps, probably
词性：副词


词源:
From late Middle English *difinitifliche, from Old French definitiv and Latin dēfīnītīvus (“defining, limiting, decisive”). Equivalent to definite +‎ -ly.
