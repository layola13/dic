cast-on:/ˈkɑːst ɒn/ (US) /ˈkɑːst ɒn/ (UK)| n. v.| 起针，套针；(名词)起针法；(动词)起针，套针|The act of creating the initial row of stitches in knitting or crocheting.  The term also refers to the specific method used to create these stitches. |例句：She used the long-tail cast-on method for her project. 她用长尾起针法开始了她的编织项目。

例句：I need to cast on 100 stitches for this scarf. 我需要为这条围巾起100针。|近义词：beginning, commencement (for the act);  cable cast-on, knitted cast-on (for the method) |反义词: cast off, bind off |词性: noun, verb
