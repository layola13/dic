invasions:/ɪnˈveɪʒənz/| n. |入侵，侵略|An act of invading, especially the entrance of a hostile army into a country for conquest or plunder. The spread of something undesirable or harmful.|例句：The invasions of the barbarians brought down the Roman Empire. 野蛮人的入侵导致了罗马帝国的覆灭。

例句：The invasion of privacy by the paparazzi is a serious issue. 狗仔队对隐私的侵犯是一个严重的问题。|近义词：attacks, assaults, incursions, offenses|反义词:retreats, withdrawals|词性:名词
