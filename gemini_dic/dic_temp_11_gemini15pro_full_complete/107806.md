lapsing:/ˈlæpsɪŋ/| adj.|失效的，流逝的，逐渐消失的|ceasing to be in effect; becoming invalid because not renewed; passing away gradually|例句：The lapsing of the treaty created a power vacuum. 条约的失效造成了权力真空。|例句：The photographer captured the lapsing light of the setting sun. 摄影师捕捉到了落日逐渐消失的光线。|近义词：expiring, ceasing, waning, declining|反义词：renewing, continuing, increasing|词性:形容词

词源: From the Latin word "lapsus," the past participle of "labi" meaning "to slip" or "to fall."
