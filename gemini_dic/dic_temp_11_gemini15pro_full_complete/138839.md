nuphar:/ˈnjuːfɑːr/ (US), /ˈnjuːfɑː(r)/ (UK)| n. |睡莲；萍蓬草属植物|A genus of aquatic plants with floating leaves and yellow cup-shaped flowers, commonly known as water lilies or spatterdocks. |Example: The nuphar adorned the pond with its vibrant yellow flowers. 睡莲以其鲜艳的黄色花朵装饰着池塘。

Example:  Nuphar lutea is a common species of spatterdock. 黄睡莲是一种常见的萍蓬草。|近义词：water lily, spatterdock, pond-lily|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
