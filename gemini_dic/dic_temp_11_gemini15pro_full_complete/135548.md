nonpoets:/ˈnɒnˌpəʊɪts/ /ˈnɑːnˌpoʊɪts/
noun
非诗人
people who are not poets; those who do not write poetry
Nonpoets often struggle to understand the nuances of poetic language. 非诗人往往难以理解诗歌语言的细微之处。
The workshop was open to poets and nonpoets alike.  这个研讨会对诗人和非诗人开放。
Synonyms: laymen, amateurs
Antonyms: poets, versifiers
n.
