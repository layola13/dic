lavishing:/ˈlævɪʃɪŋ/
verb (present participle of lavish)
慷慨地给予；挥霍；浪费
Giving or bestowing something in generous or extravagant quantities.
She is lavishing attention on her new puppy. 她正对她的小狗倾注大量的关爱。
He's lavishing money on his new girlfriend. 他正为他的新女友挥金如土。
Synonyms: showering, bestowing, heaping, spending freely, squandering
Antonyms: withholding, stinting, skimping
part of speech: verb (present participle)


lavish: /ˈlævɪʃ/
adjective
奢华的；丰富的；慷慨的
Characterized by extravagance and abundance; giving or given in great amounts.
The hotel was lavishly decorated.  这家酒店装饰奢华。
They gave a lavish party. 他们举办了一场盛大的派对。
Synonyms: luxurious, opulent, extravagant, profuse, generous
Antonyms: meager, sparse, economical, frugal
part of speech: adjective
