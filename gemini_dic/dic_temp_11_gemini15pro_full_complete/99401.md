interdisciplinarities: /ˌɪntərˌdɪsəplɪˈnɛrɪtiz/ | n. | 交叉学科性，跨学科性 | The quality or state of involving or drawing on two or more branches of learning.  It refers to the integration of knowledge and methods from different disciplines to address complex problems or create new fields of study. |  The program fosters interdisciplinarities, encouraging collaboration between science and art departments.  这个项目促进了跨学科性，鼓励科学和艺术部门之间的合作。 |  The strength of the research project lies in its interdisciplinarities, bringing together experts from sociology, psychology, and economics. 这项研究项目的优势在于其跨学科性，汇集了来自社会学、心理学和经济学的专家。 |  interdisciplinarity, cross-disciplinarity, multidisciplinarity |  disciplinarity | noun
