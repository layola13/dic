coalsack: /ˈkoʊlˌsæk/ | n. |煤袋；暗星云|A dark nebula in the Southern Hemisphere, near the Southern Cross.  A dark area in the Milky Way, resembling a black sack, caused by obscuring interstellar dust.|The Coalsack Nebula is easily visible to the naked eye on a clear night.  煤袋星云在晴朗的夜晚肉眼很容易看到。|The explorers lost their way in the darkness, feeling like they were trapped in a cosmic coalsack. 探险者在黑暗中迷失了方向，感觉就像被困在一个宇宙煤袋里。|synonyms: dark nebula, dust cloud|antonyms: bright nebula|noun
