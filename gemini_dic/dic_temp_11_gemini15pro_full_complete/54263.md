disgracious:/dɪsˈɡreɪʃəs/
adjective
丢脸的，不光彩的；失礼的，粗鲁的
Lacking grace; rude and embarrassing.
例句：
He made a disgraceful remark about her weight.
他对她体重说了无礼的话。

It was disgraceful that he was fired after 20 years of service.
他在服务了 20 年后被解雇，真是丢脸。

近义词：rude, impolite, uncivil, ungracious, disrespectful, shameful, dishonorable
反义词：gracious, polite, courteous, respectful, honorable

词性: adjective
