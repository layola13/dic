collectivist:/kəˈlɛktɪvɪst/| adj. n.|集体主义的；集体主义者|Relating to or supporting collectivism; a person who believes in collectivism.|例句：Collectivist ideologies prioritize the needs of the group over the individual. 集体主义意识形态优先考虑集体需要而非个人需要。

例句：She's a staunch collectivist and believes in shared resources and responsibilities. 她是一位坚定的集体主义者，相信资源共享和共同承担责任。|近义词：communal, cooperative, socialist|反义词：individualist, capitalist|词性：形容词，名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *collectiviste*, from *collectif* "collective," from Latin *collectus*, past participle of *colligere* "to gather," from *com-* "together" + *legere* "to gather."
