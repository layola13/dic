gentrifying: /ˈdʒɛntrɪfaɪɪŋ/
verb
仕绅化；中产阶级化
The process of renovating and improving a house or district so that it conforms to middle-class taste.
Example: The neighborhood is rapidly gentrifying, with new coffee shops and boutiques opening up. 这个街区正在快速地仕绅化，新的咖啡店和精品店纷纷开张。

Example:  Gentrifying older neighborhoods often displaces lower-income residents.  老社区的仕绅化经常会迫使低收入居民搬迁。


近义词:  upgrading, improving, renovating, revitalizing
反义词:  deteriorating, declining, decaying
词性:  verb (present participle)
