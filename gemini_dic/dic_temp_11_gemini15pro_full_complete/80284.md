goings:/ˈɡəʊɪŋz/
noun
行为，活动；事件，发生的事情
events or activities that are happening or being done
There were some strange goings-on at the party last night. 昨晚的派对上发生了一些奇怪的事情。
近义词: happenings, proceedings, events, activities
反义词: inactivity, stillness
词性: noun


goings:/ˈɡəʊɪŋz/
noun
(go 的现在分词)去；走
present participle of "go"
The comings and goings of the neighbours kept me awake. 邻居们来来往往的声音让我睡不着。
近义词: departures, exits, leavings
反义词: arrivals, entrances, comings
词性: noun
