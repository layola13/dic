myosis:/maɪˈoʊsɪs/
n.
瞳孔缩小；缩瞳
Constriction of the pupil of the eye.
Example: The doctor observed myosis in the patient's eyes after administering the medication.  医生给病人用药后观察到病人瞳孔缩小。

Example:  Myosis can be a symptom of certain neurological conditions. 缩瞳可能是某些神经系统疾病的症状。

近义词: miosis, pupillary constriction
反义词: mydriasis, pupillary dilation
词性: noun

Etymology: From Ancient Greek μύειν (múein, “to close the eyes”) +‎ -osis.
