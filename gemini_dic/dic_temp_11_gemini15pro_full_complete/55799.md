diverting:/daɪˈvɜːrtɪŋ/ | adj. | 有趣的，娱乐的；转移注意力的 |  Causing amusement or entertainment; distracting.  | The comedian's jokes were quite diverting.  喜剧演员的笑话非常有趣。 | synonyms: amusing, entertaining, engaging, diverting, distracting | antonyms: boring, tedious, dull, monotonous, uninteresting | adjective

diverting:/daɪˈvɜːrtɪŋ/ | adj. | 转移的 | Turning from a course or concern. | The diverting road led us away from the main highway. 这条岔路使我们离开了主干道。| synonyms: distracting, detouring, deviating, deflecting | antonyms: direct, straight, undeviating | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *diverten*, from Old French *divertir*, from Latin *dīvertere* ("to turn aside, turn away, divert"), from *dis-* ("apart") + *vertere* ("to turn").
