authorize: /ˈɔːθəraɪz/ (US) /ˈɔːθərʌɪz/ (UK) | vt. | 授权，批准 | To give official permission or approval for someone to do something. |例句：The manager authorized the purchase of new equipment. 经理批准购买新设备。


例句：Only authorized personnel can access this area. 只有获得授权的人员才能进入该区域。


近义词：permit, allow, sanction, empower | 反义词：forbid, prohibit, deny | 词性：动词
