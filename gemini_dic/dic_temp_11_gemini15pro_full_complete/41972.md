cosseting:/ˈkɒsətɪŋ/ (US) /ˈkɑːsətɪŋ/
adjective
娇惯的，溺爱的，过分呵护的
Treating someone with excessive indulgence and attention.
The cosseting atmosphere of the spa made her feel completely relaxed.  水疗中心娇惯的氛围让她感到完全放松。
Synonyms: pampering, indulging, spoiling, coddling
Antonyms: neglecting, ignoring, disregarding
词性: adjective


cosset:/ˈkɒsɪt/ (US) /ˈkɑːsɪt/
verb
娇惯，溺爱，过分呵护
To treat someone with excessive indulgence and attention.
She cossets her dog with expensive treats and toys. 她用昂贵的零食和玩具娇惯她的狗。
Synonyms: pamper, indulge, spoil, coddle
Antonyms: neglect, ignore, disregard
词性: verb


cosset:/ˈkɒsɪt/ (US) /ˈkɑːsɪt/
noun
受宠的人，被娇惯的人
A person who is treated with excessive indulgence and attention.
He was a cosset, used to getting everything he wanted. 他是一个被娇惯的人，习惯于得到他想要的一切。
Synonyms: pet, favorite, darling
Antonyms: outcast, pariah
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *cosseten*, probably from Old Norse *kot-setja* (“cot-sitter,” someone who stays at home by the fire), from *kot* (“cottage”) + *setja* (“to set, place”).
