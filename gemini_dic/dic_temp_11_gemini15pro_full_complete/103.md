aback:/əˈbæk/| adv.|吃惊地，措手不及地|Taken aback means surprised and disconcerted.  It often implies being thrown off balance or momentarily speechless due to an unexpected event or remark.|I was taken aback by his sudden anger. 我对他的突然生气感到吃惊。|近义词：surprised, astonished, shocked, startled|反义词：unfazed, unemotional|词性:副词
