eutrophies:/juːˈtrɒfiz/ /juˈtroʊfiz/| n. |富营养化|  An overabundance of nutrients in a body of water, frequently due to runoff from the land, which causes a dense growth of plant life and death of animal life from lack of oxygen.| The eutrophies of the lake led to a massive fish kill.  湖泊的富营养化导致了大量的鱼类死亡。|Synonyms:  hypertrophication, overfertilization|Antonyms:  oligotrophication|noun
