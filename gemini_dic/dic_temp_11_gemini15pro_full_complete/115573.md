maranatha:/ˌmærəˈnæθə/| interj. |主来吧；愿主来|An Aramaic expression, found in the New Testament, used as a prayer that Christ may return soon.|Maranatha! Come quickly, Lord Jesus! 主来吧！快来，主耶稣！|近义词:Come, Lord Jesus|反义词: None|感叹词

maranatha:/ˌmærəˈnæθə/| n. |(基督教用语)主来吧|An Aramaic expression used in early Christianity as a salutation or watchword.|The early Christians used "maranatha" as a reminder of Christ's promised return. 早期基督徒使用“马拉拿他”来提醒基督应许的再来。|近义词:The Lord's coming|反义词: None|名词
