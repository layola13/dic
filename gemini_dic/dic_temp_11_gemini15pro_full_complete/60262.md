EFTA:/ˈɛftə/
noun
欧洲自由贸易联盟 (European Free Trade Association)
A trade organization founded in 1960 by Austria, Denmark, Norway, Portugal, Sweden, Switzerland, and the United Kingdom.  Its aim was to promote free trade and economic cooperation among its members.
Iceland joined in 1970, Finland in 1986, and Liechtenstein in 1991.  The UK, Denmark, and Portugal left in 1973 to join the European Economic Community (now the European Union).
例句：EFTA and the EU have a close trading relationship. 欧洲自由贸易联盟和欧盟有着密切的贸易关系。

例句：Switzerland is a member of EFTA but not the EU. 瑞士是欧洲自由贸易联盟的成员，但不是欧盟的成员。
近义词：trade bloc, free trade area
反义词：(No direct antonym, but consider) customs union, common market
词性:n.
