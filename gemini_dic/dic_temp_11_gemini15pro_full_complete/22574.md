booths:/buːðz/
|n.|售货亭，摊位；（电话）亭；小隔间|A small enclosed space, such as one for selling goods, making telephone calls, or voting.|
例句：We rented a booth at the craft fair to sell our handmade jewelry. 我们在工艺品展销会上租了一个摊位来出售我们手工制作的珠宝。

例句：He stepped into the phone booth to make a quick call. 他走进电话亭打了个电话。
|近义词：stall, kiosk, cubicle, compartment|反义词:open space|词性:名词
