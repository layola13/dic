faithlessly:/ˈfeɪθləsli/
adverb|不忠实地，背信弃义地|In a manner that lacks faith or loyalty; disloyally; treacherously. |He faithlessly abandoned his wife and children. 他背信弃义地抛弃了他的妻子和孩子。| 近义词: disloyally, treacherously, perfidiously, unfaithfully | 反义词: faithfully, loyally | 词性: 副词

词源：

注意事项:
faithlessly 更常用于描述行动和行为，而不是人本身。虽然可以用来形容人“不忠的”，但 disloyal 或 unfaithful 更常用。
