leftwardness:/ˈlɛftwərdnɪs/
n.
左倾；左撇子倾向；向左性
The tendency to move or extend toward the left.
Her leftwardness was evident in her writing. 她的左倾在她写作中很明显。
The artist's leftwardness is reflected in the composition of his paintings. 这位艺术家的左撇子倾向反映在他的绘画构图中。
tendency, inclination, leaning, bias
rightwardness, dextrality
noun
