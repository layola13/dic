furry:/ˈfɜːri/ (US), /ˈfʌri/ (UK)| adj. n.|毛茸茸的；毛皮的；furry（爱好者）|Covered with fur; resembling fur.  (adj.) A person interested in anthropomorphic animal characters. (n.)|The kitten was soft and furry. 小猫柔软而毛茸茸的。|He's a furry and enjoys attending conventions. 他是一个furry爱好者，喜欢参加相关集会。|近义词：hairy, downy, fluffy (adj.);  fan, enthusiast (n.)|反义词:bald, smooth, hairless (adj.)|词性:adjective, noun

Etymology: From Old English *furrig, from fur. The noun sense related to fandom originated in the late 20th century.
