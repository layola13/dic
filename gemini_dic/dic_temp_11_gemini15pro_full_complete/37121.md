combustion:/kəmˈbʌstʃən/ | n. |燃烧，氧化|The process of burning, especially the rapid chemical combination of a substance with oxygen, involving the production of heat and light. |The combustion of fossil fuels releases harmful gases into the atmosphere. 燃烧化石燃料会向大气中释放有害气体。|Synonyms: burning, ignition, oxidation |Antonyms: extinction, quenching |noun
