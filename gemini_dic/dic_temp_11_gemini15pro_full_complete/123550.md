monroes:/ˈmʌnroʊz/| n.|梦露; 梦露们（指像玛丽莲·梦露一样的女性）| Refers to Marilyn Monroe or women who resemble her in style or attributes.  The name can also be pluralized to refer to multiple such women. This usage is often informal and can be considered somewhat objectifying.  The surname Monroe can also refer to James Monroe, the fifth president of the United States, but this context is less likely when pluralized as "Monroes."|Marilyn's image lives on, inspiring countless "Monroes" throughout the decades. 玛丽莲的形象依然存在，几十年来激励了无数个“梦露们”。
