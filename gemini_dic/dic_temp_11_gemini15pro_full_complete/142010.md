organizabilities:/ˌɔːrɡənɪˌzeɪbɪlətiz/
n.|组织能力，可组织性|The organizabilities of the team are impressive.  这个团队的组织能力令人印象深刻。|The company's success depends on the organizabilities of its employees. 公司的成功取决于其员工的组织能力。|近义词: organization skills, management skills, planning abilities|反义词: disorganization, chaos, lack of structure|词性:名词

Etymology:
From organize (v.) + -able (suffix) + -ity (suffix) + -es (plural suffix).  "Organize" comes from Old French "organiser" and ultimately from Latin "organum" meaning "instrument" or "tool."  "-able" forms adjectives meaning "capable of being," "-ity" forms nouns from adjectives, and "-es" marks the plural form.
