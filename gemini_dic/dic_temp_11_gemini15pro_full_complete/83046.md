gurge:/ɡɜːrdʒ/| n. | 涌流，漩涡，吞没 | A large, powerful, swirling flow of water.  A sudden influx or outpouring.| The gurge of the river carried the boat downstream. 河流的漩涡把船冲向下游。 | synonyms: surge, swirl, eddy, vortex, deluge, flood | antonyms: trickle, drip, dribble | noun

gurge:/ɡɜːrdʒ/| v. |  涌出，喷涌，吞没 | To flow in a large, powerful, swirling manner. To swallow up or engulf. |  Lava gurged from the volcano.  熔岩从火山中喷涌而出。 | synonyms: surge, gush, spout, spew, engulf, devour | antonyms: trickle, drip, dribble | verb
