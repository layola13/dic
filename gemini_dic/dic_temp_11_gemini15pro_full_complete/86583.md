heliocentricities:/ˌhiːliːoʊsɛnˈtrɪsɪtiz/ | n. | 日心性；日心说| The state or quality of being heliocentric.  The theory that the sun is the center of the solar system, with the planets revolving around it. |  The growing acceptance of heliocentricities revolutionized astronomy. 日心说的日益接受彻底改变了天文学。| synonyms: heliocentricism | antonyms: geocentrism | noun
