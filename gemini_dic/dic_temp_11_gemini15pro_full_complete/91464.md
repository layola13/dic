hylicist: /ˈhaɪlɪsɪst/
n.
森林学家; 树木学家
A specialist in the study of forests or trees.
The hylicist studied the effects of drought on the ancient redwood trees.  这位森林学家研究了干旱对古老红杉的影响。
The hylicist recommended planting a variety of tree species to create a more resilient forest.  这位树木学家建议种植各种树种，以创建一个更具韧性的森林。
近义词: forester, arborist, dendrologist
反义词: N/A
词性: 名词

Etymology: From Greek 'hylē' (wood, forest) + '-ist' (one who specializes in).
