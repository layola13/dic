nondescriptively:/ˌnɒndɪˈskrɪptɪvli/ | adv. | 平淡地，毫无特色地 |  In a way that is lacking in distinctive or interesting features; uninterestingly. | The witness nondescriptively recounted the events of that night. 目击者平淡地叙述了那天晚上的事情。 | Synonym: uninterestingly, blandly, dully | Antonym: vividly, strikingly, expressively | Adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + descriptive + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Descriptive" comes from the Latin "describere" meaning "to write down" or "to copy."  The suffix "-ly" turns the adjective "nondescriptive" into an adverb.
