multigrain:/ˈmʌltiɡreɪn/| adj.|多谷物的，杂粮的|Made with several different types of grain.|例句：I enjoy eating multigrain bread for breakfast. 我喜欢早餐吃杂粮面包。

例句：She chose a multigrain cereal for its health benefits. 她选择了一种杂粮麦片，因为它对健康有益。|近义词：wholegrain, whole wheat|反义词: refined, white (flour/bread)|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From multi- (meaning "many") + grain.  "Multi-" comes from Latin multus "much, many." "Grain" comes from Old French grain, ultimately from Latin granum "seed, grain."
