bulbaceous:/bʌlˈbeɪʃəs/
adjective
球根状的；球茎状的
Resembling a bulb, especially in roundness or fullness.  Having a bulb.
The plant has a bulbous root.  这种植物有球根状的根。
The onions were large and bulbous.  这些洋葱又大又圆。
近义词:bulbous, rounded, swollen
反义词:slender, thin, narrow
词性:adjective
