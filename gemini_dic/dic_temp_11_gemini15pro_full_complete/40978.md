coordinate:/koʊˈɔːrdɪnət/ (US), /kəʊˈɔːdɪnət/ (UK)| adj. n. v. |同等的，并列的；坐标；协调，配合|A coordinate is a number that identifies a position relative to a fixed point or axis.  Coordinates are often used in pairs or sets to pinpoint locations on maps or graphs. As an adjective, it describes things of equal importance or rank. As a verb, it means to bring different elements into a harmonious or efficient relationship.|例句：The coordinates of the treasure are 30°N, 120°E. 宝藏的坐标是北纬30度，东经120度。|近义词：align, organize, harmonize, match, corresponding, equivalent|反义词:discordant, clashing, mismatched|词性:adjective, noun, verb

coordinate:/koʊˈɔːrdɪneɪt/ (US), /kəʊˈɔːdɪneɪt/ (UK)| v. |协调，配合|To coordinate means to organize or manage different activities or elements so that they work together effectively.|例句：She coordinated the marketing campaign with the product launch. 她将营销活动与产品发布协调起来。|近义词：organize, manage, harmonize, synchronize, integrate|反义词:disorganize, disrupt|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *coōrdinātus*, past participle of coōrdināre (“to arrange together”), from com- (“together”) + ōrdināre (“to arrange”), from ōrdō (“order, rank”).
