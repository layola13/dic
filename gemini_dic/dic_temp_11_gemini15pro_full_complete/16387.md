barbizon: /ˈbɑːrbɪzən/ | n. | 巴比松，巴比松画派 |  Barbizon refers to a village in France, southeast of Paris, or the school of landscape painting founded there in the 1830s. |  The Barbizon painters were inspired by the natural beauty of the area. 巴比松画派的画家受到该地区自然美景的启发。

The Barbizon School had a profound impact on Impressionism. 巴比松画派对印象主义产生了深远的影响。 | 近义词: Barbizon school, landscape painting | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: 名词
