doles:/doʊlz/| n. |施舍物; (尤指政府发放的) 救济金; 少量; |A share or portion, especially of something given out sparingly. Originally meaning a portion of land, it evolved to signify a charitable gift, and later, government relief. |The government doles out unemployment benefits. 政府发放失业救济金。|Synonyms: handouts, allotments, allowances|Antonyms: earnings, income|noun

doles:/doʊlz/| v. (doles, doling, doled) |(少量地)发放，分发; |To distribute or give out sparingly or in small portions. |He doled out the candy to the children. 他把糖果分发给孩子们。|Synonyms: distribute, dispense, apportion|Antonyms: hoard, withhold|verb
