flisk: /flɪsk/
n. |轻弹;轻拂; flick| A quick light blow or stroke. |She flisked the dust off her jacket with a flick of her wrist. 她用手腕轻拂了一下，把夹克上的灰尘掸掉。|近义词：flick, flip, whisk |反义词: pat, stroke| 词性:名词

flisk: /flɪsk/
v. |轻弹;轻拂; flick| To hit or move something with a quick light blow or stroke. |He flisked the fly away with his hand. 他用手轻拂了一下，把苍蝇赶走了。|近义词：flick, flip, whisk |反义词: pat, stroke| 词性:动词

词源:  Likely a variant of "flick," possibly influenced by words like "whisk."  No definitive etymology is readily available.
