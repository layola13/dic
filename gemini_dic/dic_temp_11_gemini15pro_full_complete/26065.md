bushmaster:/ˈbʊʃˌmæstər/ | n. | 巨蝮蛇；灌木丛主宰者 | A large venomous pit viper snake found in forests from Panama to Brazil.  A person who has mastery over a bush or forest area. | Example 1: The bushmaster is one of the most dangerous snakes in South America. 巨蝮蛇是南美洲最危险的蛇类之一。

Example 2: He was a true bushmaster, knowing every trail and plant in the rainforest. 他是一个真正的丛林主宰者，了解雨林中的每一条小径和每一株植物。 | 近义词：pit viper, fer-de-lance (for the snake meaning) , expert, master (for the person meaning)| 反义词：novice, beginner (for the person meaning) | 词性: 名词
