evacuating: /ɪˈvækjueɪtɪŋ/
verb
撤离，疏散
the process of moving people from a dangerous place to somewhere safe
例句：They are evacuating the building because of a fire. 他们正在因为火灾疏散大楼。

例句：The city is evacuating residents due to the approaching hurricane.  由于飓风即将到来，该市正在疏散居民。

近义词：removing, clearing, withdrawing
反义词：entering, populating, occupying
词性：verb (present participle)

Etymology:
From Latin evacuatus, past participle of evacuare "to empty out," from ex- "out" + vacuus "empty."
