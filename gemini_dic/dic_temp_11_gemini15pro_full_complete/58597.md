dull-wittedness:/ˌdʌlˈwɪtɪdnəs/| n.|迟钝，愚笨|The dull-wittedness of the character was portrayed through his slow reactions and poor decision-making.  这个角色的迟钝通过他缓慢的反应和糟糕的决策来刻画。|近义词：stupidity, obtuseness, slow-wittedness|反义词：intelligence, cleverness, sharpness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From dull + wit + -ed + -ness.  "Dull" meaning lacking sharpness or vibrancy. "Wit" referring to mental sharpness and intelligence. "-ed" forming the adjective "dull-witted," and "-ness" transforming the adjective into the noun "dull-wittedness."  由dull（迟钝的）+ wit（智慧）+ -ed + -ness组成。“Dull”表示缺乏敏锐或活力。“Wit”指的是精神敏锐和智力。“-ed”构成形容词“dull-witted”，“-ness”将形容词转化为名词“dull-wittedness”。
