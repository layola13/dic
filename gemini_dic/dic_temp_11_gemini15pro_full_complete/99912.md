internationalized:/ˌɪntərˈnæʃənəlaɪzd/ | /ˌɪntərˌnæʃnəlaɪzd/
adj.|国际化的|Adapted or altered to suit the needs or tastes of different countries or cultures.|例句：The company has internationalized its website to cater to a global audience.  该公司已将其网站国际化，以迎合全球受众。|近义词：globalized, worldwide, cosmopolitan|反义词:localized, regional, domestic|词性:形容词
词源:From international + -ize + -ed.  源自 international（国际的） + -ize（使…化）+ -ed（过去分词后缀）
