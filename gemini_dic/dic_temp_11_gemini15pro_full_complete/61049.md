elk:/ɛlk/ | n. |麋鹿|A large deer with big antlers, native to North America and eastern Asia. |We saw a herd of elk grazing in the meadow. 我们看到一群麋鹿在草地上吃草。|例句：The elk bugled loudly during the mating season.  交配季节，麋鹿大声鸣叫。|近义词：wapiti, moose (in British English, "elk" refers to the moose), red deer|反义词: N/A|noun
