assure:/əˈʃʊr/ | vt. |使确信；向…保证；使放心|To tell someone something confidently to dispel any doubts they may have.|I assure you that everything will be alright. 我向你保证一切都会好起来的。|近义词: guarantee, convince, promise |反义词: alarm, worry |动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French assurer "to make safe, secure, guarantee," from Vulgar Latin *assecurare "make safe," from Latin ad- "to" + securus "secure, safe, free from care."
