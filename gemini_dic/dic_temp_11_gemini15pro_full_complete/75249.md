from-rags-to-riches:/frəm ræɡz tə ˈrɪtʃɪz/
|adj.|白手起家，从赤贫到巨富|From a state of poverty to a state of wealth.  Describes someone who started with nothing and became very successful and wealthy.|He went from rags to riches, starting as a street vendor and ending up a millionaire. 他白手起家，从一个街头小贩变成了百万富翁。|近义词：self-made, prosperous|反义词: impoverished, destitute|词性:adjective

from-rags-to-riches:/frəm ræɡz tə ˈrɪtʃɪz/
|n.|白手起家的故事，暴富|The story of someone who started with nothing and became very successful and wealthy.|Her life was a true rags-to-riches story. 她的人生是一个真正的白手起家的故事。|近义词：success story, Cinderella story|反义词: downfall, ruin|词性:noun
