brittle:/ˈbrɪtl/| adj.|易碎的；脆弱的；尖刻的；冷淡的|Easily broken or cracked; not flexible; likely to snap under pressure; also, sharp and unkind in speech or manner.|The antique vase was so brittle that I was afraid to touch it.  这个古董花瓶太脆了，我都不敢碰它。

The dry leaves crumbled to a brittle powder. 干枯的叶子碎成了易碎的粉末。

Her brittle laughter revealed her nervousness. 她那尖刻的笑声暴露了她的紧张。

He responded with a brittle tone. 他以冷淡的语气回应。|近义词：fragile, frail, crisp, sharp, curt|反义词：flexible, resilient, pliable, tough, warm|词性:adjective


Etymology: From Old English *brittol, from Proto-Germanic *brutilaz, from Proto-Indo-European *bhreu- (“to break, cut”). Cognate with Old Frisian brittil (“brittle”), Middle Dutch brokel, brokkelig (“brittle”), German brüchig (“brittle”), Old Norse brjóta (“to break”), Gothic brikan (“to break”).
