gruff:/ɡrʌf/| adj.|粗暴的，生硬的，（声音）粗哑的|Having a rough or surly voice or manner.|He answered in a gruff voice. 他用粗哑的声音回答。|近义词：surly, curt, brusque, abrupt, rough, harsh|反义词：gentle, kind, polite, courteous|词性:形容词

词源:  From Middle English *gruffen, *groffen (“to snort, grunt”), apparently of imitative origin. Cognate with Scots grufe (“to snort, grunt”), West Frisian grof (“coarse”), Dutch grof (“coarse, rough, rude”), German grob (“coarse, rough, rude”). More at groff.
