overplaying:/ˌoʊvərˈpleɪɪŋ/
verb
过分强调；夸大；表演过火
Exaggerating the importance or effect of something; performing with excessive or exaggerated emotion or action.
He was overplaying his injury to get sympathy. 他为了博取同情而夸大他的伤势。

The actor was criticized for overplaying the role.  这个演员因表演过火而受到批评。

近义词: exaggerating, overdramatizing, overacting, overemphasizing
反义词: underplaying, downplaying
词性: verb (present participle)
