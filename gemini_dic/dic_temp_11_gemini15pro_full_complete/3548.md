agalaxy:/ˈæɡələksi/| n. |星系；银河系；一群出色的人或事物|A system of millions or billions of stars, together with gas and dust, held together by gravitational attraction.  A large group of impressive people or things.|例句：Our galaxy is called the Milky Way. 我们的星系被称为银河系。

例句：The event attracted a galaxy of artists and writers.  这次活动吸引了一大批艺术家和作家。|近义词：cosmos, universe, world|反义词: (no direct antonym, could consider void, nothingness for contrasting concepts)|noun
