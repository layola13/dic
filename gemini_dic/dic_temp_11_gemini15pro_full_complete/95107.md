inanest:/ɪnˈeɪnɪst/ | adj. | 无生命的，无感觉的 | Not alive; inanimate.  Lacking sensation or awareness. | The statue was inanest, yet somehow seemed to watch me. 这座雕像是无生命的，但不知何故似乎在看着我。|  The inanest rock face stared back at the climbers.  无生命感的岩壁凝视着攀登者。 | 近义词: lifeless, inanimate, insensate | 反义词: animate, living, sentient | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From in- (not) + animate (having life). Ultimately from Latin *inanimatus*, from in- (not) + animatus (having breath, alive).
