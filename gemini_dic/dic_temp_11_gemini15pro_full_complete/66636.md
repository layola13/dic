existentialism:/ˌɛɡzɪˈstɛnʃəlɪzəm/ /ˌɛksɪˈstɛnʃəlɪzəm/| n. |存在主义|A philosophical theory or approach that emphasizes the existence of the individual person as a free and responsible agent determining their own development through acts of will.|

例句：Existentialism emphasizes individual freedom and responsibility. 存在主义强调个体自由和责任。

例句：He explored themes of existentialism in his novels. 他在他的小说中探讨了存在主义的主题。

近义词：absurdism, nihilism|反义词:essentialism, determinism|词性:noun
