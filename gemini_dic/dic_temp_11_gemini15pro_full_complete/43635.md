creationist:/kriˈeɪʃənɪst/
n.|神创论者；创造论者|A person who believes that the universe and living organisms originate from specific acts of divine creation, as in the biblical account, rather than by natural processes such as evolution.|例句：The creationist argued against the theory of evolution.  神创论者反对进化论。|近义词：anti-evolutionist|反义词：evolutionist|词性:noun

Etymology:
From creation + -ist.  Creation comes from the Old French creacion and ultimately from the Latin creatio, meaning "a creating, founding, establishing." The suffix -ist denotes a person who adheres to a particular doctrine or system of belief.
