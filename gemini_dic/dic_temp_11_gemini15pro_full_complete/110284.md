ligates:/ˈlaɪɡeɪts/
verb |连接，结合，结扎| To bind or tie off, especially blood vessels or ducts during surgery.  |The surgeon ligates the artery to stop the bleeding. 外科医生结扎动脉以止血。|近义词：bind, tie, connect|反义词:separate, disconnect|词性:v.

ligates:/ˈlaɪɡeɪts/
noun |连接物|Something that binds or connects. (Less common usage) |The enzyme acts as a ligates, joining the two DNA strands. 这种酶充当连接物，连接两条 DNA 链。|近义词：link, connector|反义词:separator, divider|词性:n.
