mabby:/ˈmæbi/| adj. adv.| 也许，可能 |Perhaps; possibly.  Used especially in informal speech. |"Maybe I'll go to the party later." 也许我晚点会去参加派对。|近义词：perhaps, possibly, perchance|反义词: definitely, certainly, absolutely|词性:形容词, 副词
