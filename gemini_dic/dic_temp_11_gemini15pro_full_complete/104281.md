justiceship:/ˈdʒʌstɪsʃɪp/
n.
法官的职务；法官职位；审判员职位
The office or position of a judge or magistrate.
He was appointed to the justiceship of the Supreme Court. 他被任命为最高法院法官。
synonyms: judgeship, magistracy, office of judge
antonyms: N/A
