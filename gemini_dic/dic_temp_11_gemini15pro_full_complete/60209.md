efflorescent:/ˌɛfləˈrɛsənt/| adj.|开花的；风化的；（比喻）繁荣的；成熟的|Losing moisture and crumbling to a powder, as a crystalline substance. Figuratively, reaching a peak of development or perfection.|The efflorescent crystals on the cave walls shimmered in the lamplight. 洞穴墙壁上风化的晶体在灯光下闪闪发光。|近义词：blooming, flourishing, thriving, blossoming|反义词：withered, faded, declining, decaying|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin efflorescere "to blossom, bloom," from ex- "out" + florescere "to begin to blossom," from flos "flower."
