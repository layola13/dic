heavy-set:/ˈhevi set/ /ˈhɛvi sɛt/| adj.|体格健壮的，敦实的；肥胖的|Someone who is heavy-set has a large, solid, and strong body.  Often implies being overweight but can also describe someone muscular and stocky.|例句：The heavy-set man struggled to fit into the small airplane seat. 那个体格健壮的男人费力地挤进飞机的小座位。

例句：He was a heavy-set fellow with a booming voice. 他是个身材魁梧、声音洪亮的人。|近义词：stocky, stout, portly, hefty, thickset|反义词：slim, slender, thin, slight|词性:形容词

heavy-set:/ˈhevi set/ /ˈhɛvi sɛt/| n.|体格健壮的人，肥胖的人 (较少用作名词)|A person who is heavy-set.|例句：A heavy-set blocked the doorway. 一个体格健壮的人挡住了门口。

例句：Several heavy-sets were seated in the front row.  几个身材肥胖的人坐在前排。|近义词：N/A (名词用法较少，通常用形容词描述)|反义词：N/A|词性:名词 (较少使用)


词源: Compound of "heavy" and "set." "Heavy" referring to weight and "set" referring to build or physique.
