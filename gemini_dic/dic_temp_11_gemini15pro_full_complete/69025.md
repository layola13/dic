fattinesses:/ˈfætɪnəsɪz/
n.
油腻；肥胖
The state or quality of being fatty; excessive fat content.
The fattinesses of the food made him feel sick.  食物的油腻让他感到恶心。
近义词:oiliness, greasiness, richness, adiposity
反义词:leanness, thinness
词性:名词

词源:
From fatty + -ness.  来自形容词 fatty 加名词后缀 -ness.
