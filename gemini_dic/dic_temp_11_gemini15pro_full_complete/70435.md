figulate:/ˈfɪɡjʊleɪt/
verb
塑造，使成形
to shape or mold into a particular form
The potter figulated the clay into a beautiful vase. 陶艺家把黏土塑造成一个漂亮的花瓶。
近义词：shape, mold, form, fashion
反义词：deform, distort
词性: v.

figulate:/ˈfɪɡjʊleɪt/
adjective
(rare)  成形的；有形的
having a definite shape
The figulate mass began to resemble a human form.  这团成形的物质开始像人形。
近义词：shaped, formed, molded
反义词：shapeless, formless
词性: adj.


词源：From Latin *figulatus, past participle of *figulare (“to shape, mold”), from figulus (“potter”), from fingere (“to shape, mold, fashion”).
