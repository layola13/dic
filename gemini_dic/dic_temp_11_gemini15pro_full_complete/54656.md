disobeisant:/ˌdɪsəˈbeɪsɑːnt/ /ˌdɪsəˈbeɪsənt/
adjective
不服从的，不顺从的
Not obedient; refusing to obey.
The disobeisant child refused to go to bed.  不服从的孩子拒绝上床睡觉。
The disobeisant soldier was court-martialed. 不服从命令的士兵被送上了军事法庭。
近义词: disobedient, rebellious, defiant, insubordinate, unruly, wayward
反义词: obedient, compliant, submissive, docile
词性: adjective
词源: From Old French *desobeissant, present participle of desobeir “to disobey,” from des- “dis-” + obeir “to obey.”
