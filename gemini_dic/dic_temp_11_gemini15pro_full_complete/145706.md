overcourteousness:/ˌoʊvərˈkɜːrtiəsnəs/| n.| 过分礼貌，过分客气| The overcourteousness of the staff made her feel uncomfortable.  员工过分的礼貌让她感到不舒服。|近义词：excessive politeness, obsequiousness|反义词：rudeness, impoliteness|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + courteousness.  "Over-"  means "excessive" or "too much". "Courteousness" is the noun form of "courteous," meaning polite and respectful.  Ultimately from Latin "cōrs" (court).
