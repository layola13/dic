augustinianism:/ɔːˌɡʌstɪˈniːənɪzəm/
n.
奥古斯丁主义
The theological system of St. Augustine of Hippo, emphasizing the total depravity of humankind, the necessity of divine grace for salvation, and predestination.
Augustinianism played a significant role in the Reformation. 奥古斯丁主义在宗教改革中扮演了重要的角色。
近义词:Augustinism
反义词:Pelagianism
词性:noun
