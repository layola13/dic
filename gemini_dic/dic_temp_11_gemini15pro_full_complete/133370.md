nonextractions:/ˌnəʊnɪkˈstrækʃənz/ | n. | 无提取物；无萃取物 | The product boasts nonextractions, meaning it's made without using any extracts.  这种产品标榜无提取物，意味着它是在不使用任何提取物的情况下制造的。 | 例句：This juice is made with nonextractions, only fresh fruit.  这种果汁是用无提取物制成的，只有新鲜水果。

例句：We prefer using ingredients with nonextractions for a purer taste. 我们更喜欢使用无提取物成分，以获得更纯正的味道。


近义词：pure, unadulterated, natural | 反义词：extract, concentrate | 词性：名词

词源： "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "extraction" (noun derived from the verb "extract," ultimately from Latin "extrahere," meaning "to draw out").
