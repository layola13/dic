bunyip:/ˈbʌnɪp/| n.| 邦尼普（澳大利亚传说中的水怪）|A mythical Australian creature said to inhabit swamps and billabongs.|The bunyip is a prominent figure in Aboriginal Australian folklore. 邦尼普是澳大利亚原住民民间传说中的一个突出人物。|The old timers used to tell stories about the bunyip lurking in the deep waterholes. 老人们过去常常讲关于邦尼普潜伏在深水潭里的故事。|近义词：monster, cryptid|反义词:|n.
