nigerias:/naɪˈdʒɪəriəz/ | n. | 尼日利亚（的）|  A plural form sometimes encountered, though "Nigeria" is typically used as the country name even when referring to multiple aspects of it.  It's likely a non-standard usage.  "Nigeria" itself comes from the Niger River, the country's prominent geographical feature.  The river's name is believed to originate from a local term, possibly meaning "great river". | Example: He visited several nigerias. (Non-standard usage -  He visited several parts of Nigeria would be more appropriate).  The nigerias he saw were diverse. (Non-standard usage - The different parts of Nigeria he saw were diverse would be more appropriate). | Synonym: (As a singular) Nigeria | Antonym: N/A | Noun
