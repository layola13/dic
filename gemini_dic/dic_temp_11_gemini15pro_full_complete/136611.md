nonsatire:/ˌnɑːnˈsætaɪər/ | n. | 非讽刺; 真实情况 |  Something that is genuine and not intended to be satirical. |  The documentary presented a nonsatire portrayal of life in the slums.  这部纪录片真实地描绘了贫民窟的生活。| 近义词: reality, truth, fact | 反义词: satire, irony, parody | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (not) + "satire" (a literary work holding up human vices and follies to ridicule or scorn)
