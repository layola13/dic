cavorting:/kəˈvɔːrtɪŋ/
verb
嬉戏; 欢跃
jumping or dancing around excitedly
The lambs were cavorting in the field. 羊羔在田野里嬉戏。
近义词: frolicking, gamboling, romping, prancing
反义词: moping, slouching, still, quiet
词性: verb (现在分词)

cavort:/kəˈvɔːrt/
verb
嬉戏; 欢跃
to jump or dance around excitedly
The children cavorted in the playground. 孩子们在操场上嬉戏。
近义词: frolic, gambol, romp, prance
反义词: mope, slouch
词性: verb


词源: From the French *colverter*, from Italian *colvertire* "to turn over, somersault"  ultimately from Latin *convertere*  "to turn around".
