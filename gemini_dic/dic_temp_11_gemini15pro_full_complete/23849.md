breadlessness:/ˈbrɛdləsnəs/
n.
lack of bread; state of being without bread
The breadlessness of the Great Depression forced many families to go hungry. 大萧条时期的缺粮迫使许多家庭挨饿。
The prolonged drought resulted in widespread breadlessness throughout the region. 长期干旱导致整个地区普遍缺粮。
Synonyms: starvation, famine, hunger, destitution, impoverishment
Antonyms: abundance, plenty, prosperity, affluence
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bread + -less + -ness.  "Bread" comes from Old English "bread."  "-less" is a suffix meaning "without" or "lacking." "-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns from adjectives.
