audibly:/ˈɔːdəbli/| adv.|听得见地，可闻地|in a way that can be heard clearly|例句：She whispered audibly. 她低声说着，声音听得见。|例句：He sighed audibly. 他清晰地叹了口气。|近义词：clearly, distinctly, perceptibly|反义词：inaudibly, silently, imperceptibly|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *audibilis, from Latin audire "to hear".
