disgospel:/dɪsˈɡɒspəl/
verb
剥夺福音;使不信福音
To deprive of the Gospel or of its benefits; to turn away from the Gospel.
The constant barrage of negative news seemed to disgospel even the most devout believers.  不断的负面新闻轰炸似乎甚至让最虔诚的信徒都放弃了福音。
The cult leader sought to disgospel his followers from their previous religious beliefs.  邪教头目试图让他的追随者放弃他们以前的宗教信仰。
synonym:  alienate, subvert, corrupt
antonym:  evangelize, proselytize, convert
