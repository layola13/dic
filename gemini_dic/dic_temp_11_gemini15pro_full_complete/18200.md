belayers:/bɪˈleɪərz/| n. |保护者，确保者|A person who belays another climber.  The belayer controls the climbing rope, providing safety and taking in slack as the climber ascends.|The belayers were attentive and kept the climbers safe.  保护者们非常专注，确保了攀登者的安全。|近义词：secures, safeguards, protectors|反义词: endangers|词性:Noun
