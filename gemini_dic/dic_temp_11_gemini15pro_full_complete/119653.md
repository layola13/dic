middleman:/ˈmɪdəlmæn/| n.|中间人，经纪人|A person who buys goods from producers and sells them to retailers or consumers, or who acts as an intermediary between two parties in a business transaction.|The farmer sold his produce through a middleman.  这个农民通过中间人出售他的农产品。|Synonyms: intermediary, agent, broker, go-between, negotiator|Antonyms: producer, consumer, direct seller|noun


middlemen:/ˈmɪdəlmɛn/| n.|中间人（复数）|Plural form of middleman.|The middlemen took a large cut of the profits.  中间商拿走了很大一部分利润。|Synonyms: intermediaries, agents, brokers, go-betweens, negotiators|Antonyms: producers, consumers, direct sellers|noun
