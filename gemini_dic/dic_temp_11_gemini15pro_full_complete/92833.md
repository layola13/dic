iconomy:/aɪˈkɒnəmi/ (UK) /aɪˈkɑːnəmi/ (US)| n. |图像经济；图像学；肖像画法| The study of images, symbols, or icons, especially in art or religion.  It also refers to the modern economy driven by visual content and imagery. |例句：The iconomy of the Byzantine Empire was highly developed. 拜占庭帝国的图像学非常发达。

例句：Social media plays a significant role in the modern iconomy. 社交媒体在现代图像经济中扮演着重要的角色。|近义词：iconography, symbolism, imagery|反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially "aniconism" which refers to the avoidance of images) |词性: Noun
