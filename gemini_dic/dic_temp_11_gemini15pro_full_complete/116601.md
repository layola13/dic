matrilineal:/ˌmæt.rɪˈlɪn.i.əl/ | adj. | 母系的 | Inherited or determined through the female line; relating to descent through the mother's side of the family. |  The Minangkabau people of Indonesia have a matrilineal society where inheritance and family name are passed down through the mother.  印度尼西亚的米南佳保人拥有母系社会，遗产和姓氏是通过母亲传下来的。 |  matriarchal, uterine | patrilineal, agnatic | adjective
