interreflecting:/ˌɪntərɪˈflektɪŋ/
| adj. |相互反射的；互相影响的|Reflecting back and forth or influencing each other mutually.|The interreflecting surfaces of the mirrors created a dazzling display of light. 镜子相互反射的表面创造出令人眼花缭乱的光影。|The two cultures were interreflecting, each influencing and changing the other. 这两种文化相互影响，互相改变。|近义词：interacting, reciprocal, mutually influencing|反义词：independent, separate|词性:形容词

词源:
inter- (prefix): between, among, mutually  来自拉丁语inter
reflecting (present participle of reflect): throwing back light, heat, sound, etc.; mirroring; expressing a thought or feeling. 来自拉丁语reflectere, re- (back) + flectere (to bend)
