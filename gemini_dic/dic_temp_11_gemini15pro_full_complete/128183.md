neuralgia:/nʊˈræl.dʒə/ /nʊˈræl.dʒi.ə/| n.|神经痛|Intense, sharp, stabbing pain along the course of a nerve, not caused by stimulation of the nerve's receptor.  A disorder characterized by paroxysms of pain along the course of one or more nerves.|She suffered from severe neuralgia in her face. 她面部神经痛很严重.|Synonyms: nerve pain|Antonyms: analgesia|noun
