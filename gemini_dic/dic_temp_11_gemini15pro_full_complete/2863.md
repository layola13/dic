adversative:/ədˈvɜːsətɪv/
adjective
表示反义的，表示相反的
Introducing a word or phrase expressing opposition or contrast.
He was an adversative person, always disagreeing. 他是一个爱反驳的人，总是不同意别人的意见。
synonyms: contrasting, opposing, contradictory
antonyms: supportive, agreeing, harmonious
词性: 形容词

adversative:/ədˈvɜːsətɪv/
noun
反义词，反义连接词
A word or phrase that expresses opposition or contrast.
"But" is a common adversative. “但是”是一个常见的反义连接词。
synonyms: conjunction, connector
antonyms: N/A
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *adversātīvus, from Latin adversārī (“to oppose”)
