bodhisattva:/ˌboʊdiːˈsʌtvə/ | n. |菩萨|A person who is able to reach nirvana but delays doing so out of compassion in order to save suffering beings.|The Dalai Lama is considered a bodhisattva. 达赖喇嘛被认为是菩萨。|Synonyms: enlightened being, compassionate one |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "unenlightened being") |noun
