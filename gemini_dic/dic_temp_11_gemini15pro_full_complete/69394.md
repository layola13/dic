feeding:/ˈfiːdɪŋ/| n. |喂养; 饲养; 食物; 进食|The act of giving food to a person or an animal.|The mother bird was busy feeding her young. 母鸟正忙着喂养它的幼鸟。|近义词：nourishment, sustenance, provision|反义词:starvation, deprivation|noun

feeding:/ˈfiːdɪŋ/| adj. |摄食的; 给食的; 供给…的|Relating to the act of giving food.|The feeding habits of the animals were carefully observed.  这些动物的摄食习惯得到了仔细的观察。|近义词：nutritive, nourishing|反义词:fasting, starving|adjective
