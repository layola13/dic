lurchers:/ˈlɜːrtʃərz/ (UK), /ˈlɜːrtʃərz/ (US)| n.|猎犬（尤指杂交品种）; 流浪汉，游手好闲的人|A lurcher is a cross-bred sighthound, most commonly a cross between a Greyhound and a Collie or Terrier.  Lurchers are also sometimes used to refer to people who lurk or loiter.|例句：The farmer used lurchers to hunt rabbits. 农民用猎犬猎兔子。

例句：He was known as a lurcher, always hanging around the market. 他被称为流浪汉，总是徘徊在市场周围。|近义词：mongrel, crossbreed (for dogs), vagrant, loiterer (for people)|反义词:pedigree (for dogs)|词性:名词
