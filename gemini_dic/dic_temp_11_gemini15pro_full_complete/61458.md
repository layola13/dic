embrace: /ɪmˈbreɪs/ (US) /ɪmˈbreɪs/ (UK)| v. n. |拥抱；信奉；欣然接受|To hold someone closely in one's arms, typically as a sign of affection; to accept or support (a belief, theory, or change) willingly and enthusiastically. |She embraced her son warmly. 她热情地拥抱了她的儿子。|
He embraced the opportunity to study abroad. 他欣然接受了出国留学的机会。|
Synonyms: hug, cuddle, accept, adopt, espouse|
Antonyms: reject, spurn, repel, push away|
verb, noun
