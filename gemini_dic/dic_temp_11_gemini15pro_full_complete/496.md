abundance: /əˈbʌndəns/ | n. | 丰富，充裕 |  A very large quantity of something. |  There is an abundance of fresh fruit at the market. 市场上的新鲜水果很丰富。 | synonyms: plenty, profusion, wealth | antonyms: scarcity, lack, dearth | noun

Etymology: From Middle French *abundance*, from Latin *abundantia*, from *abundans* ("overflowing, abundant"), present participle of *abundare* ("to overflow"), from *ab* ("from") + *undare* ("to surge, overflow"), related to *unda* ("wave").
