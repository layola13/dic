decoction:/dɪˈkɒkʃən/
n.
煎煮液；药汁；煮出的东西
the act or process of extracting the essence of something by boiling, or the liquid produced by this process.
The doctor prescribed a decoction of herbs to soothe her cough. 医生给她开了一种草药煎剂来止咳。
extract, infusion, brew
solution, dilution
noun
