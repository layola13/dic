impressionists:/ɪmˈprɛʃənɪsts/
n.
印象派艺术家；印象主义者
A group of artists who worked in the Impressionist style, characterized by short, visible brushstrokes and an emphasis on capturing the fleeting effects of light and color.
The Impressionists revolutionized painting in the late 19th century. 印象派画家在19世纪晚期彻底改变了绘画。
Monet and Renoir are among the most famous Impressionists. 莫奈和雷诺阿是最著名的印象派画家之一。
synonyms: Impressionist painters, Impressionist artists
antonyms: Realists, Classicists
noun
