noneuphonious:/ˌnɒnjuːˈfəʊniəs/ | adj. | 不悦耳的，不和谐的 | Not pleasing to the ear; dissonant or harsh-sounding.  Lacking euphony. |  The noneuphonious sound of nails on a chalkboard made everyone cringe.  指甲刮黑板的刺耳声音让每个人都感到畏缩。 | Synonyms: cacophonous, discordant, inharmonious, jarring, raucous | Antonyms: euphonious, harmonious, melodious, pleasant, sweet | word property: adjective

Etymology:

From "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "euphonious" (from Greek "euphōnos" meaning "well-sounding," from "eu-" meaning "good" + "phōnē" meaning "sound, voice").
