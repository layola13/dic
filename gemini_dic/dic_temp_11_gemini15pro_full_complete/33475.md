chylification:/ˌkaɪlɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |乳糜化|The process of converting food into chyle in the small intestine.|例句：Chylification is a crucial part of digestion. 乳糜化是消化过程的关键部分。

例句：Problems with chylification can lead to malabsorption of nutrients. 乳糜化问题会导致营养吸收不良。|近义词：emulsification, digestion |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

Etymology: From Medieval Latin *chylificatio, from chylificare "to make chyle,"  from Ancient Greek χυλός (khulós, “juice”) + Latin facere "to make."
