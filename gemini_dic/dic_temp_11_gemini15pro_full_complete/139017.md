O-levels:/ˈoʊ lɛvəlz/ | n. | 普通中等教育证书 | Examinations taken in various subjects, usually at the age of 16, in the UK and other countries formerly part of the British Empire.  These exams are a prerequisite for A-levels. | He passed his O-levels with flying colors. 他以优异的成绩通过了普通中等教育证书考试。|Synonym: GCSE (in the UK), secondary school leaving exams | Antonym:  N/A (no direct opposite, but perhaps university entrance exams) | noun
