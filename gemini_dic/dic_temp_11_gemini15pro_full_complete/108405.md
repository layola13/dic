lawmakers:/ˈlɔːˌmeɪkərz/
n.
立法者；造法者
People who make laws, such as members of a legislature.
The lawmakers are debating a new bill.  立法者正在辩论一项新的法案。
Congress is a body of lawmakers. 国会是一个立法机构。
legislators, lawgivers
lawbreakers
noun
