churnability:/ˌtʃɜːrnəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可搅拌性，易搅动性 | The churnability of butter is affected by temperature. | The ease with which something can be churned, especially butter.  This refers to how easily a substance, typically a semi-solid or viscous liquid, can be agitated or stirred into a different state or consistency. For example, the churnability of cream is important in butter making. |  例句：The churnability of the cream improved as it warmed. 奶油随着温度升高更容易搅拌。

例句：The low churnability of the mixture made it difficult to produce a smooth consistency. 混合物的低搅动性使其难以产生光滑的稠度。| 近义词: stirability, mixability, workability | 反义词: viscosity, thickness, firmness | 词性: noun
