changes:/ˈtʃeɪndʒɪz/
n.|变化，改变|Alterations or modifications in a thing or situation.|The changes in the weather are unpredictable. 天气的变化是不可预测的.|synonyms: alterations, modifications, variations, transformations|antonyms: constants, consistencies|noun

changes:/ˈtʃeɪndʒɪz/
v.|改变，更改|To make or become different.|She changed her mind about going to the party. 她改变了去参加派对的主意.|synonyms: alter, modify, vary, transform|antonyms: maintain, preserve|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "changier" (to change), from Late Latin "cambiare" (to exchange), from Latin "cambire" (to barter).
