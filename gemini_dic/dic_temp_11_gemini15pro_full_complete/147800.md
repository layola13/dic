overprove:/ˌoʊvərˈpruːv/| v. |过度发酵|To allow dough to rise for too long, resulting in a coarse texture and a slightly sour taste.| The baker overproved the dough, and the bread came out flat.  面包师把面团发酵过度了，导致面包烤出来是扁的。| The yeast was too active, and I accidentally overproved the dough for the cinnamon rolls. 酵母太活跃了，我不小心把肉桂卷的面团发酵过度了。|近义词: overrise, overferment|反义词: underprove|词性:动词

overproof:/ˈoʊvərˌpruːf/| adj. |(酒精)过度的|Containing more alcohol than proof spirit.|  Overproof rum is popular in some Caribbean cocktails.  高浓度朗姆酒在一些加勒比鸡尾酒中很受欢迎。|  This whisky is overproof, so be careful how much you drink.  这种威士忌酒精度很高，所以要注意饮用量。|近义词: strong, potent|反义词: weak, mild|词性:形容词
