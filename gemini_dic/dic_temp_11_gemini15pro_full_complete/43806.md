crenellated:/ˈkrɛnəleɪtɪd/
adjective
城垛状的；有锯齿形的
having battlements; notched or indented, especially in a regular or ornamental way.
The castle walls were crenellated, providing defensive positions for archers. 城墙呈锯齿状，为弓箭手提供了防御阵地。
crenelated, embattled, castellated
smooth, straight
adjective
