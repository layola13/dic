disappearances:/ˌdɪsəˈpɪərənsɪz/
n.
消失，失踪（disappearance 的复数形式）
The disappearances of several young women have alarmed the community.  几个年轻女子的失踪惊动了社区。
例句：The police are investigating the mysterious disappearances of valuable paintings from the museum. 警方正在调查博物馆珍贵画作神秘失踪的案件。

例句：There have been a number of unexplained disappearances in the area recently. 最近该地区发生了一些原因不明的失踪事件。

近义词：vanishings, absences
反义词：appearances, arrivals
词性：名词


disappearance:/ˌdɪsəˈpɪərəns/
