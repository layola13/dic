logistical:/ləˈdʒɪstɪk(ə)l/
adjective
逻辑的；后勤的
relating to the process of planning and organizing the supply of materials, goods, and people needed for a particular activity.
The logistical problems of mounting an expedition to the Antarctic are formidable.  组织南极探险的后勤问题极其艰巨。
synonyms: organizational, planning, managerial, administrative, executive, operational
antonyms: unplanned

logistically:/ləˈdʒɪstɪkli/
adverb
逻辑上地；后勤上地
in a way that relates to the process of planning and organizing the supply of materials, goods, and people needed for a particular activity.
Logistically, the project was a nightmare.  从后勤角度来看，这个项目是一场噩梦。
synonyms: organizationally, managerially, administratively
antonyms: haphazardly
