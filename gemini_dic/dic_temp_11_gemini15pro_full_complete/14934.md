azoturia:/ˌeɪzəˈtʊəriə/ /ˌæzəˈtʊəriə/| n. |氮尿症，肌红蛋白尿症|A condition characterized by the presence of excessive amounts of nitrogenous substances, especially urea, in the urine.  It's often associated with muscle damage and the release of myoglobin into the bloodstream.|The horse suffered from azoturia after a strenuous workout. 这匹马在剧烈运动后患上了氮尿症。|近义词：myoglobinuria|反义词:|名词
