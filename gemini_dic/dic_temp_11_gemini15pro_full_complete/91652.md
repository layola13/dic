hypercarbureted:/ˌhaɪpərˈkɑːrbjəreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过度增碳的，过度渗碳的 | Excessively carburized or carbonated; containing an excessive amount of carbon.  Relates to a fuel mixture that is overly rich, having more fuel than is needed for complete combustion.  While technically applicable to any substance, it's most commonly used in the context of internal combustion engines. | The hypercarbureted engine sputtered and backfired.  过度增碳的引擎发出噼啪声并回火。

The hypercarbureted mixture caused the engine to run poorly.  过度增碳的混合物导致发动机运行不良。 | 近义词: over-rich, oversaturated | 反义词: lean (mixture) | 词性: 形容词

词源: hyper- (prefix, meaning "over, excessive") + carbureted (past participle of carburet, meaning "to combine or impregnate with carbon")
