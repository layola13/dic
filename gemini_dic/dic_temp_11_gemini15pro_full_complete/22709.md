borer:/ˈbɔːrər/ (US), /ˈbɔːrə(r)/ (UK)| n.|钻孔机，钻孔者，蛀虫|A tool or insect that makes holes, especially in wood.  A person or thing that bores.|The carpenter used a power borer to make holes for the dowels. 木匠用电钻钻孔装销钉。|近义词:drill, auger, worm, grub|反义词:filler|noun
