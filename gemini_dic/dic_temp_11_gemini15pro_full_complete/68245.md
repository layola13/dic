fairys:/ˈfeəriːz/
n.
仙女们 (plural of fairy)
A mythical being of folk tales, typically having magical powers, often depicted as a small winged figure with a human form.  The plural form indicates multiple fairies.
Fairies danced in the moonlight. 仙女们在月光下跳舞。
The fairys bestowed a gift upon the newborn princess.  仙女们赐予了新生公主一份礼物。
近义词:sprites, pixies, elves
反义词:monsters, ogres, demons
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fairie*, from Old French *faerie* (meaning "enchantment, fairyland"), from *fae* (meaning "fairy"), from Vulgar Latin *fata* (meaning "fate, destiny; a goddess of fate"), from Latin *fatum* (meaning "fate").
