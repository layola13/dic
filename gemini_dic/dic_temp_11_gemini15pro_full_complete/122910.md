moltenly:/ˈmoʊltənli/| adv.|熔融地，像熔化了一样|In a manner resembling molten material; like something melted.  It describes something that appears or behaves as if it were melted, usually referring to light, color, or a smooth, flowing quality.|The sun beat down moltenly on the desert sand.  太阳熔化般地照射在沙漠的沙子上。|The lava flowed moltenly down the volcano's side.  熔岩像熔化了一样流下火山的一侧。|近义词：fluidly, liquidly, smoothly|反义词:solidly, rigidly|词性:副词

词源:
From "molten" + "-ly."  "Molten" comes from the past participle of "melt," which has Proto-Germanic roots.
