objectifies: /əbˈdʒɛktɪfaɪz/
verb
使…客观化；使…物化；将…视为物体
treats (someone) as an object rather than a person.
That advertisement objectifies women. 那则广告物化了女性。
synonyms: dehumanize, depersonalize, reify
antonyms: personalize, humanize
词性: 动词
