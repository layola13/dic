equate:/ɪˈkweɪt/| v.|使相等；等同|To consider one thing to be the same as or equivalent to another thing.|例句：He equates wealth with happiness. 他把财富等同于幸福。|近义词：equalize, compare, liken, associate|反义词:differentiate, distinguish|verb
equated:/ɪˈkweɪtɪd/ |v. |(equate的过去式和过去分词) 使相等；等同|Past tense and past participle of "equate".  Indicates that something was considered the same as or equivalent to another thing.|例句：The study equated experience with competence. 这项研究将经验等同于能力。|近义词：equalized, compared, likened, associated|反义词:differentiated, distinguished|verb
