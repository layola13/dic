oaring:/ˈɔːrɪŋ/ /ˈɔːrɪŋ/| adj.|喧嚣的，轰鸣的，(生意)兴隆的|Characterized by a loud, deep sound, like the roar of a lion or the booming of waves; also, thriving or prosperous.|The roaring twenties were a time of great economic prosperity. 喧嚣的二十年代是经济繁荣的时期。|The engine made a roaring sound. 引擎发出轰鸣的声音。|synonyms: booming, thunderous, resounding, flourishing, thriving|antonyms: quiet, silent, hushed, still, failing|adjective
