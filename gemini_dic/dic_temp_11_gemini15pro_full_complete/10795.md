aquatically:/əˈkwætɪkli/
adverb
水生地；水生性地
in a way relating to water; in or on water
例句：Some plants grow aquatically.  一些植物水生生长。

例句：Many insects live aquatically. 许多昆虫水生生活。

近义词：underwater, submerged, marine, aquatic
反义词：terrestrially, on land

词性: adverb
