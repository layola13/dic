amphigenous:/æmˈfɪdʒɪnəs/
adjective
两面生的；环生的
Growing all around; on both sides of a leaf or other lateral organ.
Example: The amphigenous rust is a type of fungus that grows on both sides of a leaf.  这种两面生的锈菌是一种生长在叶子两面的真菌。

Example: Amphigenous growth was observed on the seaweed.  在海藻上观察到了环生生长。
synonym: surrounding, encircling
antonym: unilateral, localized
adjective
