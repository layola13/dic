odorous:/ˈoʊdərəs/
|adj.|有气味的; 有臭味的（尤指难闻的）|The odorous fumes from the factory polluted the air. 工厂散发出的臭气污染了空气。|近义词: smelly, fragrant (if pleasant), stinky, foul (if unpleasant)|反义词: fragrant, perfumed (pleasant smells), odorless|词性:形容词


词源:
From Latin *ōdorōsus*, from ōdor (“smell, scent”).
