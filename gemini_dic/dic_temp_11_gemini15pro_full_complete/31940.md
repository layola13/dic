chelicera:/kɪˈlɪsərə/ | n. | 螯肢 |Either of the first pair of appendages of arachnids and some other arthropods, usually modified as pincerlike claws. | Example 1: The spider used its chelicera to inject venom into its prey. 蜘蛛用它的螯肢将毒液注入猎物体内。

Example 2:  The scorpion's chelicera are small, but its pedipalps are large and claw-like. 蝎子的螯肢很小，但它的须肢很大，像爪子一样。 | Synonym:  fang, claw (in some contexts) | Antonym:  N/A | noun


chelicerae:/kɪˈlɪsəriː/ | n. | 螯肢（复数） | The plural form of chelicera. | Example 1: The spider uses its chelicerae to grasp and crush its prey. 蜘蛛用它的螯肢抓住并压碎猎物。
