euphemism:/ˈjuːfəmɪzəm/| n. |委婉语|A mild or indirect word or expression substituted for one considered to be too harsh or blunt when referring to something unpleasant or embarrassing.|The term "passed away" is a euphemism for "died". “去世”是“死亡”的委婉说法。|近义词：circumlocution, understatement|反义词：dysphemism|词性: noun
