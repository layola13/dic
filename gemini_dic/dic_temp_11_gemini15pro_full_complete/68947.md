father:/ˈfɑːðər (US), ˈfɑːðə(r) (UK)| n. |父亲; 创始人; 神父; (尤指天主教的)长老|A male parent.|例句：He is a loving father. 他是一位慈爱的父亲。|例句：My father taught me how to ride a bike. 我的父亲教我如何骑自行车。|近义词：dad, papa, parent|反义词：son, daughter|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *fæder, from Proto-Germanic *fader, from Proto-Indo-European *ph₂tḗr. Cognates include Dutch vader, German Vater, Latin pater, Greek πατήρ (patḗr), Sanskrit पितृ (pitṛ́), and Persian پدر (pedar).
