deambulate: /dɪˈæmbjʊleɪt/ | vi. | 漫步，闲逛 | To walk or move around, especially in a leisurely or aimless way. |  He deambulated around the park, enjoying the fresh air. 他在公园里漫步，享受着新鲜空气。

The tourists deambulated through the ancient ruins. 游客们在古老的废墟中漫步。
