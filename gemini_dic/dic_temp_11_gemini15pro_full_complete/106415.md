kops:/kɒps/ (UK), /kɑːps/ (US) | n. | 警察 |  An informal, often derogatory term for police officers.  Derived from the Yiddish word "kop," meaning "head." | Example:  The kops showed up at the party. 警察出现在派对上。| Example:  Watch out, the kops are coming! 小心，警察来了！| Synonym: police, cops, officers | Antonym:  N/A | Noun
