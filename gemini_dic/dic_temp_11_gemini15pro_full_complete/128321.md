neurotoxins:/ˈnjʊərəʊˌtɒksɪnz/ (US) /ˈnʊəroʊˌtɑːksɪnz/| n. |神经毒素|Poisons that act on the nervous system, disrupting or destroying nerve function.|Example:  Some snakes produce potent neurotoxins that can paralyze their prey. 一些蛇会产生强效的神经毒素，可以麻痹它们的猎物。

Example: Botulinum toxin, one of the most potent neurotoxins known, is used in small doses for cosmetic purposes (Botox). 肉毒杆菌毒素是已知的最强效的神经毒素之一，少量用于美容目的（肉毒杆菌）。|近义词:nerve poisons, neuropoisons|反义词:neurotrophic factors (promote nerve growth)|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From neuro- + toxin.  "Neuro-" comes from Greek "neuron" meaning "nerve." "Toxin" comes from Greek "toxikon (pharmakon)" meaning "poison (for arrows)."
