arquebus:/ˈɑːrkɪbʌs/ | n. | 火绳枪 | An early type of portable gun, supported on a tripod or other rest when fired, and ignited by a match applied to a touch-hole.  It was a predecessor to the musket. | The arquebus was a revolutionary weapon in the 16th century.  火绳枪在16世纪是一种革命性的武器。

The soldiers were armed with arquebuses and pikes. 士兵们装备了火绳枪和长矛。| 近义词: handgun, firearm, musket | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "bow and arrow" or "sword" as representing earlier weaponry) | 词性: noun
