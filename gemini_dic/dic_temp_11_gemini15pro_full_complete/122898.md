molon:/ˈmoʊlɒn/| interj.|来拿吧；过来拿|Come and get them (a defiant challenge).  A classical Greek phrase quoted by Plutarch, supposedly said by King Leonidas of Sparta during the Battle of Thermopylae.|例句：Molon Labe is a favorite motto among soldiers.  “来拿吧”是士兵们最喜欢的座右铭。|近义词: Come and get them,  defy, challenge|反义词: surrender, yield|词性:感叹词
