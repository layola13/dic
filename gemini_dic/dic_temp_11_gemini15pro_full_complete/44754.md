crumbling:/ˈkrʌmblɪŋ/| adj.|崩溃的，破碎的，衰落的|Breaking or falling apart into small fragments; deteriorating.  |The crumbling empire was once a global superpower.  这个崩溃的帝国曾经是一个全球超级大国。|Synonyms:  disintegrating, decaying, deteriorating, collapsing|Antonyms:  stable, strong, solid, developing|adjective

crumbling:/ˈkrʌmblɪŋ/| n.|崩溃，破碎，衰败|The process of breaking or falling apart into small fragments. |The crumbling of the building was caused by years of neglect. 这座建筑的倒塌是多年失修造成的。|Synonyms: disintegration, decay, deterioration, collapse|Antonyms:  growth, development, strengthening, construction|noun
