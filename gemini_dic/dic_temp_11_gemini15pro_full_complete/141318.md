openair:/ˈoʊpənˌɛr/| adj.|露天的，户外的|Exposed to the air; outdoors. |例句：We enjoyed an openair concert in the park. 我们在公园里欣赏了一场露天音乐会。

例句：The openair market was full of fresh produce. 露天市场里摆满了新鲜的农产品。|近义词：outdoor, alfresco|反义词：indoor, inside|词性:形容词

词源:  From open + air.  Relatively modern, first attested in the late 19th century.
