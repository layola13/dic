demurrer:/dɪˈmɜːrər/| n.|异议，反对，抗辩|An objection that an opponent's point is irrelevant or invalid, especially in law.  在法律上，尤指对对方观点无关紧要或无效的反对。|例句：The lawyer filed a demurrer, arguing that the plaintiff's complaint was insufficient.  律师提出了异议，认为原告的控诉不足。

例句：She expressed a demurrer to the proposed plan, citing potential risks. 她对提议的计划表示反对，理由是存在潜在风险。|近义词：objection, challenge, protest, exception|反义词：acceptance, agreement, approval|noun
