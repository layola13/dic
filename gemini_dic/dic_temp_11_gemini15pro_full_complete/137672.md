nonunitarians:/ˌnɒnˌjuːnɪˈtɛəriənz/ | n. |非一神论者；不信三位一体论者 |People who do not believe in the doctrine of the Trinity, which states that God is one being in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.  |In the 19th century, many nonunitarians were active in social reform movements. 19世纪，许多非一神论者积极参与社会改革运动。|  The church welcomed both Unitarians and nonunitarians.  这个教会欢迎一神论者和非一神论者。| Synonyms: antitrinitarians, dissenters | Antonyms: Trinitarians | noun
