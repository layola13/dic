chivalrously:/ˈʃɪvəlrəsli/
adverb
骑士般地；彬彬有礼地
In a manner resembling a knight; courteously and bravely, especially toward women.
He chivalrously opened the door for her. 他骑士般地为她开了门。
Synonyms: gallantly, courteously, politely, graciously
Antonyms: rudely, discourteously, ungallantly, boorishly
词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From chivalry + -ous + -ly.  Chivalry comes from Old French chevalerie "knighthood, cavalry," from chevalier "knight," from Late Latin caballarius "horseman," from Latin caballus "horse."
