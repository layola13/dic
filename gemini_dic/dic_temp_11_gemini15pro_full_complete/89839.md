hornito:/ɔːrˈniːtoʊ/ (US), /hɔːrˈniːtəʊ/ (UK)| n.|熔岩丘，熔岩塔|A small, cone-shaped structure built up by lava spattering or being ejected from a vent in a solidified lava flow or crust.|
The geologists observed several hornitos forming on the newly solidified lava field.|地质学家观察到新凝固的熔岩地上形成了几个熔岩塔。|
The eruption created a field of hornitos, each spouting lava like miniature volcanoes.|这次喷发形成了熔岩塔群，每个熔岩塔都像微型火山一样喷出熔岩。|
