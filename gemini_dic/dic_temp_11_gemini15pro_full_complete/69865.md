ferrous:/ˈferəs/| adj.|含铁的；亚铁的|Containing or consisting of iron, especially in the divalent state.|例句：Ferrous sulfate is commonly used as a source of iron in fertilizers. 硫酸亚铁常被用作肥料中的铁源。

例句：The ferrous metals are magnetic.  亚铁金属具有磁性。|近义词：iron-containing|反义词：ferric|词性：形容词
