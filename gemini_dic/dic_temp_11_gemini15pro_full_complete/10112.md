apogeic:/ˌæpəˈdʒiːɪk/
adjective
最高点的; 远地点的
Relating to or denoting the highest point in the development of something; an apogee.  At its most extreme; culminating.
例句：The apogeic moment of her career was winning the Nobel Prize. 她职业生涯的巅峰时刻是获得诺贝尔奖。

例句：The empire reached its apogeic phase during the reign of Emperor Xuanzong.  这个帝国在玄宗皇帝统治时期达到了鼎盛阶段。

近义词：culminating, climactic, peak, highest, maximum
反义词：nadir, lowest, perigean
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *apogéique*, from apogée (“apogee”) + -ique (“-ic”), from Ancient Greek ἀπόγειος (apógeios) (“away from earth”), from ἀπό (apó, “from, away from”) + γῆ (gê, “earth”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *dʰéǵʰōm (“earth”).
