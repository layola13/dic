crawly:/ˈkrɔːli/ | adj. | 爬行的；令人毛骨悚然的；起鸡皮疙瘩的 |  Causing a sensation as of things crawling on the skin; creepy.  |  The dark, empty house gave me a crawly feeling.  那栋黑暗空荡的房子让我感到毛骨悚然。 |  creepy, chilling, scary, unnerving | comfortable, pleasant, reassuring | adjective

Etymology->
From crawl + -y.  Ultimately from Old Norse *krafla ("to claw, scrape").
