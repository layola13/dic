overabundant:/ˌoʊvərəˈbʌndənt/| adj.|过剩的，过多的|Existing or available in excess; more than sufficient.|The garden produced an overabundant supply of tomatoes this year. 今年花园里生产的西红柿过剩。|近义词：excessive, superfluous, plentiful, copious|反义词：scarce, meager, insufficient|词性:形容词
词源: From over- + abundant.  "Over-" signifies "excessively" or "too much," while "abundant" comes from the Latin "abundans," present participle of "abundare," meaning "to overflow" or "to abound."
