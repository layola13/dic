misstyling:/mɪsˈstaɪlɪŋ/
n.
错误的样式；不合适的款式
the act of styling something incorrectly or inappropriately
Her misstyling of the dress made her look older than she was. 她对这条裙子的错误搭配让她看起来比实际年龄要大。
近义词: misfashioning, bad styling
反义词: good styling, appropriate styling
词性: noun

misstyle:/ˌmɪsˈstaɪl/
v.
错误地设计样式；搭配不当
to style something incorrectly or inappropriately
He misstyled his hair and it looked ridiculous.  他把头发弄得很难看，看起来很滑稽。
近义词: misfashion
反义词: style well
词性: verb


词源:
From mis- + style.  "Mis-" is a prefix indicating wrong or bad. "Style" refers to a particular way in which something is done, created, or performed.
