latherer:/ˈlæðərər/ | n. |起泡的人/物;涂抹肥皂的人/物 |A person or thing that lathers something.  |The latherer scrubbed the horse's coat vigorously.  涂抹肥皂的人用力擦洗马的皮毛。|Synonyms: soap applier, foam producer |Antonyms: cleaner, rinser | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From lather + -er. Lather from Middle English *latheren, from Old English *lēaþrian, from Proto-Germanic *lauþrōną (“to lather”), from *lauþrą (“lather”).  -er is a suffix forming nouns designating persons from the action of the verb.
