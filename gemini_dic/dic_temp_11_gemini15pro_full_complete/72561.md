flutterless:/ˈflʌtərlɪs/
adjective
无颤动的，稳定的
Without fluttering; steady.
The flag hung flutterless in the still air.  旗帜在静止的空气中一动不动。
Synonyms: steady, still, motionless, unmoving
Antonyms: fluttering, flapping, waving, trembling
词性: 形容词
