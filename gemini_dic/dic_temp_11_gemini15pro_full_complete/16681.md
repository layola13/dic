barrenest:/ˈbærənɪst/
adj.
最贫瘠的，最荒芜的
Most unproductive; lacking vegetation or life.
The barrenest landscape I've ever seen was the Atacama Desert. 我见过的最荒芜的景象是阿塔卡马沙漠。
The farmer struggled to grow crops in the barrenest part of his land.  农民在他最贫瘠的土地上努力种植庄稼。

近义词: desolate, infertile, sterile, unproductive
反义词: fertile, productive, lush, fruitful
词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English *barenest*, superlative of *barren*, from Old French *baraigne* (sterile, barren), of uncertain origin.
