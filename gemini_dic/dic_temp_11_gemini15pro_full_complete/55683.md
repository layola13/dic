dittology:/dɪˈtɒlədʒi/ | n. | 二联剧，双部曲 | A work consisting of two parts which are usually related. | The Odyssey and The Iliad form a dittology.  《奥德赛》和《伊利亚特》构成了一部双部曲。 | Example:  His latest work is a dittology about the lives of two sisters. 他最新的作品是一部关于两姐妹生活的双部曲。| Synonym: diptych, duology | Antonym: N/A | noun
