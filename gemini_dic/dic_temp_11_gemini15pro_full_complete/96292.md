indifuscin:/ɪndɪˈfʌsɪn/
noun
不溶褐素
A brownish pigment found in certain aging cells, particularly heart muscle cells, and thought to be formed from the oxidation and polymerization of lipids and phospholipids.
Indifuscin accumulates in the heart muscle with age.  不溶褐素随着年龄的增长在心肌中积累。
