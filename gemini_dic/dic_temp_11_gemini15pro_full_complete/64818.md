ETYMOLOGY->
From French *espadon*, from Old Provençal *espadon*, from Late Latin *spadonem*, accusative of *spado* (“sword”), from Ancient Greek *σπάθη* (*spathē*, “any broad blade, spatula, oar-blade”). Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European *sph₂-ē-dh-*.
