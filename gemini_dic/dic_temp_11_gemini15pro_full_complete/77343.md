gaud:/ɡɔːd/| n. |俗丽的装饰；炫耀；（尤指廉价而俗气的）小玩意儿；|Something showy and bright, but cheap and of poor quality. |例句：She loved cheap gauds and trinkets. 她喜欢廉价俗丽的小玩意儿。

例句：The fair was full of gaudy gauds.  集市上到处都是俗丽的装饰品。|近义词：trinket, bauble, ornament, finery |反义词：simplicity, understatement|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *gaude, from Old North French gaude (“joy, ornament, finery”), from Late Latin *gaudia, plural of gaudium (“joy”), from Latin gaudēre (“to rejoice”). Doublet of joy.
