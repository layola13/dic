orthogonal:/ɔːrˈθɒɡənəl/ (US) /ɔːˈθɑːɡənəl/ (UK)| adj.|正交的，垂直的，无关的|Relating to or involving right angles; statistically independent.|例句：The two lines are orthogonal. 这两条线是正交的。

例句：The vectors are orthogonal to each other. 这些向量彼此正交。

近义词：perpendicular, unrelated, independent|反义词：parallel, related, dependent|词性：形容词

orthogonality:/ɔːrˌθɒɡəˈnælɪti/ (US) /ɔːˌθɑːɡəˈnælɪti/ (UK)| n.|正交性，垂直性，无关性|The state of being orthogonal.|例句：The orthogonality of the axes is crucial. 轴的正交性至关重要。

例句：The orthogonality of the data ensures unbiased results.  数据的正交性确保结果无偏差。

近义词：perpendicularity, independence|反义词：dependence, parallelism|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *orthogonal*, from Greek *ὀρθός* (orthós, “straight, right”) + *γωνία* (gōnía, “angle”).
