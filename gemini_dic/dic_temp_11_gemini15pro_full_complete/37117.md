combustiblenesses:/kəmˈbʌstɪblənəsɪz/
n.
可燃性；易燃性
The quality of being combustible; flammability.
The combustiblenesses of various materials were tested in the lab.  各种材料的可燃性都在实验室进行了测试。
combustibility, flammability, inflammability
incombustibility, nonflammability
