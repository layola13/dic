gotten-through:/ˈɡɒtn θruː/ /ˈɡɑːtn θruː/
verb phrase
完成；度过；用完；接通电话
Successfully dealt with a difficult period or situation; used up a supply of something; managed to contact someone by phone.
I've finally gotten through all the paperwork. 我终于完成了所有的文书工作。

近义词:completed, finished, survived, overcome, used up, consumed, contacted, reached
反义词:failed, abandoned, saved, preserved

词性:Verb Phrase (动词短语)
词源: "Gotten" is the past participle of "get," which comes from Old Norse "geta" (to get, obtain). "Through" comes from Old English "þurh" (through, by means of).
