epicenisms:/ɪˈpɪsɪnɪzəmz/
|n.|通性词;  无性词;  两性词|A word that has only one form for both male and female, such as "cousin", "pupil", "child".  |例句：English relies heavily on epicenisms, using words like "teacher" for both men and women.  英语大量使用通性词，例如“teacher”一词既可以指男性，也可以指女性。

例句：The use of epicenisms can help avoid gender bias in language. 使用通性词有助于避免语言中的性别偏见。|近义词：gender-neutral terms, dual-gender terms|反义词:|词性:noun
