context's:/ˈkɒnˌteksts/| n. |上下文；语境；背景|Relating to or depending on the circumstances that form the setting for an event, statement, or idea.|The context's unclear, so I'm not sure what they mean. |语境不清楚，所以我不知道他们的意思。|近义词：circumstances, background, setting, situation|反义词: irrelevance, isolation|词性:名词

词源: From Latin contextus "a joining together," past participle of contexere "to weave or knit together," from com "with, together" + texere "to weave."
