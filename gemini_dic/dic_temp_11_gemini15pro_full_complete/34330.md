clasher:/ˈklæʃər/| n. |冲突者，发生冲突的人或物|A person or thing that clashes or is in conflict with another. |The two armies were clashers in a bloody battle.  这两支军队在一场血腥的战斗中冲突。|Synonyms: antagonist, opponent, combatant|Antonyms: ally, supporter, friend|noun

clasher:/ˈklæʃər/| n. |不协调者，不和谐的事物|Something that clashes, especially in terms of style or taste. |The bright pink curtains were a real clasher with the rest of the room's decor.  亮粉色的窗帘与房间的其他装饰很不协调。|Synonyms: mismatch, incongruity, discord|Antonyms: harmony, match, concord|noun
