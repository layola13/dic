half-Italian:/ˌhæf ɪˈtæliən/
adjective
半意大利的；有一半意大利血统的
Someone who is half-Italian has one Italian parent and one parent of a different nationality.
My mother is Irish and my father is Italian, so I'm half-Italian. 我的母亲是爱尔兰人，我的父亲是意大利人，所以我是半个意大利人。
She makes delicious half-Italian, half-French cuisine. 她做美味的半意大利半法国菜。
近义词: part-Italian
反义词: full-blooded Italian, fully Italian
词性: adjective
