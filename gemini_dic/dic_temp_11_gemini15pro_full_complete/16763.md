baryta:/bəˈraɪtə/ | n. | 重晶石 | A dense white mineral consisting of barium sulfate. It is used as a pigment and in making glass and barium chemicals. | Baryta is commonly used in the production of high-quality paper. 重晶石通常用于生产高质量的纸张。 | Baryta is a key ingredient in some types of drilling mud. 重晶石是某些类型钻井泥浆的关键成分。 | synonyms: barite | antonyms: N/A | noun
