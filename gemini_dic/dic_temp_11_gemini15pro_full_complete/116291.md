massing:/ˈmæsɪŋ/| n.| 集中; 群集; 聚集; (尤指部队或建筑物的) 集结|The massing of troops along the border caused concern. 边境上的军队集结引起了人们的担忧。|近义词：accumulation, aggregation, concentration, amassing|反义词: dispersal, scattering|noun

Etymology: From Middle English *massinge*, from Old French *masse*, from Latin *massa* (“lump, mass”).
