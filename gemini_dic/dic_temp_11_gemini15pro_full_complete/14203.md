autocarpous:/ˌɔːtəˈkɑːrpəs/ | adj. | 自生的，自花授粉的 | (of a plant) fertilized by its own pollen; self-pollinating.  | Example: Autocarpous plants are capable of self-fertilization. 自花授粉植物能够进行自花授粉。| Example:  Many varieties of tomatoes are autocarpous, allowing them to produce fruit even in isolation. 许多番茄品种都是自花授粉的，即使在隔离环境下也能结果。 | Synonym: self-fertilizing, self-pollinating, autogamous | Antonym: allogamous, cross-fertilizing | adjective
