interreflected:/ˌɪntərɪˈflektɪd/
adjective
相互反射的
reflecting each other; reflected repeatedly.
The interreflected light created a warm glow in the room. 相互反射的光线在房间里营造出温暖的光芒。
The dancers' movements were interreflected in the mirrored walls. 舞蹈演员的动作在镜面墙上相互反射。
synonym:interreflective
antonym:non-reflective
adjective
