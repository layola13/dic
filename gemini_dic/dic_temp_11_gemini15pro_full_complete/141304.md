open-mouthedness:/ˌoʊpənˈmaʊðɪdnəs/ |n.|目瞪口呆，瞠目结舌|The state of having one's mouth open, especially in surprise or astonishment.|He stared at the spectacle with open-mouthedness.|近义词: astonishment, amazement, awe, wonder, incredulity|反义词: indifference, apathy, composure|词性:名词

open-mouthed:/ˌoʊpənˈmaʊðd/ |adj.|张大嘴巴的，目瞪口呆的|Having one's mouth open, typically in surprise or astonishment.|She stood open-mouthed, staring at the unexpected sight.|近义词: agape, gaping, stunned, amazed|反义词: closed-mouthed, tight-lipped|词性:形容词
