desiccated:/ˈdɛsɪkeɪtɪd/
adjective
干燥的，脱水的
thoroughly dried; deprived of moisture
The desiccated landscape offered little in the way of food or water.  这片干旱的土地几乎没有什么食物和水。
The desiccated remains of the plant crumbled in my hand.  这株植物干枯的残骸在我手中碎裂了。
近义词: dried, dehydrated, parched, arid
反义词: moist, hydrated, damp, wet
词性: adjective

desiccate:/ˈdɛsɪkeɪt/
verb
使干燥，使脱水
to remove the moisture from (something); to dry (something) thoroughly
The hot desert sun quickly desiccates any exposed food.  炎热的沙漠阳光很快就会使任何暴露在外的食物脱水。
They desiccate the herbs to preserve their flavor.  他们将草药干燥以保存其风味。
近义词: dry, dehydrate, parch
反义词: moisten, hydrate, dampen
词性: verb
