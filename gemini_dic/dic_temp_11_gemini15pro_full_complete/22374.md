boobyish:/ˈbuːbiiʃ/| adj.|愚蠢的，傻气的|Resembling or characteristic of a booby; silly or foolish.|He made a boobyish mistake and forgot his passport.|近义词：foolish, silly, stupid, idiotic, inane, asinine|反义词：intelligent, clever, smart, wise, sensible|词性:形容词
Etymology: From booby + -ish.  "Booby" originally referred to a type of tropical seabird known for its apparent clumsiness and ease of capture, hence its extended meaning of "a foolish person." The suffix "-ish" adds the meaning "having the qualities of."
