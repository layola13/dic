calamitous:/kəˈlæmɪtəs/
adjective
灾难性的；悲惨的
Involving calamity; disastrous or catastrophic.
The calamitous earthquake devastated the city.  灾难性的地震摧毁了这座城市。
The ship sank in a calamitous storm.  这艘船在一场灾难性的风暴中沉没了。
近义词: disastrous, catastrophic, devastating, ruinous
反义词: fortunate, lucky, beneficial, auspicious
词性: adjective

calamity:/kəˈlæmɪti/
noun
灾难；灾祸
An event causing great and often sudden damage or distress; a disaster.
The flood was a calamity for the region. 洪水对该地区来说是一场灾难。
He described the fire as a calamity. 他把这场火灾描述为一场灾难。
近义词: disaster, catastrophe, tragedy, misfortune
反义词: fortune, luck, blessing, boon
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin calamitosus, from Latin calamitas (“misfortune, damage, loss, injury”),  equivalent to calam- +‎ -osus.  Calamitas is from calamus (“reed, stalk”), perhaps referring to damage to crops.
