disanalogy:/ˌdɪsəˈnælədʒi/
n.|不同, 差异, 相反|Lack of analogy; difference or dissimilarity.|There's a disanalogy between learning a language and learning to ride a bike. 学习语言和学习骑自行车之间存在差异。|Near Antonyms: analogy, similarity, resemblance|Antonyms: likeness|noun
