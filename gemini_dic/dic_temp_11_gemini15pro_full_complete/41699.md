corrival:/kəˈraɪvəl/ | n. | 竞争对手，竞争者 | A corrival is a fellow competitor or rival.  A person who is competing for the same object or goal as another. |  He saw his corrivals as a threat to his success. 他将他的竞争对手视为对其成功的威胁。

Her main corrivals for the championship were injured, improving her chances.  她主要的冠军争夺对手都受伤了，这增加了她的机会。 | 近义词: rival, competitor, opponent | 反义词: ally, partner, teammate | 词性: 名词

词源:  Late 16th century from co- + rival.
