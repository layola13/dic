keplerian:/kɛpˈlɪəriən/
adjective
开普勒的; 行星运动遵循开普勒定律的
Relating to or obeying Kepler's laws of planetary motion.
例句：The probe entered a Keplerian orbit around the asteroid. 探测器进入了绕小行星运行的开普勒轨道。

例句：Keplerian elements are used to describe the orbit of a celestial body. 开普勒元素用于描述天体的轨道。
近义词：orbital, planetary
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider non-Keplerian for contexts involving deviations from Kepler's laws)
词性: adjective
