chaffiest:/ˈtʃæfiɪst/
adjective
最轻浮的，最无价值的
full of or resembling chaff; worthless
The chaffiest of jokes fell flat with the serious audience.  那些最轻浮的笑话在这个严肃的观众面前毫无效果。
近义词:flimsiest, worthless, trivial, insubstantial
反义词:substantial, valuable, meaningful, weighty
词性:adjective
