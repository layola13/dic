hawser:/ˈhɔːzər/ | n. | 缆绳；粗绳 | A thick rope or cable used for mooring or towing a ship. | The ship was secured to the dock with a heavy hawser. 这艘船用一根粗缆绳系在码头上。 |  The tugboat strained at its hawser, pulling the massive cargo ship into port. 拖船拉紧缆绳，将巨大的货船拖进港口。 | synonyms: cable, rope, line | antonyms: N/A | noun
