collaud:/kɒˈlɔːd/ | v. | 检验，鉴定；认可，批准 | to officially approve or authorize something, especially after testing or inspecting it | The bridge was collauded and opened to traffic. 这座桥经过检验，正式通车。 | 近义词：approve, authorize, certify, endorse, ratify, validate | 反义词：reject, disapprove, condemn | 词性: 动词

词源: From Italian *collaudare*, from Latin *cum laudare* "to praise with", from *cum* "with" + *laudare* "to praise".
