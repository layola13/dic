endoscopists:/ɪnˈdɒskəpɪsts/ | n. | 内镜医师；内窥镜检查者 | A physician specializing in endoscopy, the use of an endoscope to examine the interior of a body cavity or hollow organ. | The endoscopists performed a colonoscopy to screen for colon cancer. 内镜医师进行了结肠镜检查以筛查结肠癌。 | 近义词:endoscope specialist, gastroenterologist (in some cases) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: Noun

Etymology:
From endoscope + -ist.
"endoscope" from endo- (within) + -scope (instrument for viewing).  From Greek *éndon "within" + skopeîn "to look at."
"-ist" a suffix forming agent nouns, denoting a person who practices or is concerned with something, or holds certain principles.
