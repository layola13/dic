nonpresentations:/ˌnɒnˌprɛzɛnˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 未出席，缺席；未提交 |  The act of not presenting something, or the instance of not being present. This word often refers to the failure to attend scheduled meetings or appointments, or the failure to submit required documents or materials. |  Numerous nonpresentations at the board meetings led to a lack of quorum. 董事会会议上多次缺席导致人数不足。

The professor penalized students for nonpresentations of their term papers. 教授因学生未提交学期论文而对他们进行了处罚。 |  absences, defaults, omissions, failures to appear, nonattendances | attendances, presentations, appearances, submissions | noun
