emulsive:/ɪˈmʌlsɪv/| adj.|乳化的；使乳化的；易乳化的|Having the tendency to form an emulsion; relating to or promoting emulsification. |This emulsifying agent helps to create a stable mixture of oil and water. 这种乳化剂有助于形成稳定的油水混合物。|The soap acts as an emulsifying agent. 这块肥皂起到了乳化剂的作用。|近义词：emulsifying, emulsion-forming|反义词：coalescing, demulsifying|词性:形容词
emulsive:/ɪˈmʌlsɪv/| n.|乳化剂|A substance that stabilizes an emulsion.|Lecithin is a common emulsifier used in food production. 卵磷脂是食品生产中常用的乳化剂。|Egg yolk acts as an emulsifier in mayonnaise. 蛋黄在蛋黄酱中充当乳化剂。|近义词：emulsifying agent, stabilizer|反义词：demulsifier|词性:名词
