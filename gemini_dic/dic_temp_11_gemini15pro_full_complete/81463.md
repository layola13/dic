gratuitousness:/ˌɡrætʃuˈɪtəsnəs/
n.
无偿；免费；无故；多余
the state of being gratuitous; unwarranted or unnecessary.
His speech was filled with gratuitous violence. 他的演讲充满了无端的暴力。
Synonyms:unwarrantedness, needlessness, uncalled-for-ness
Antonyms:necessity, needfulness
