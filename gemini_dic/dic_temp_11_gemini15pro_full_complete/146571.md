overgratefully:/ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪtfəli/
adverb
过度感激地，过分感激地
Expressing gratitude to an excessive degree.
She thanked him overgratefully for his small act of kindness. 她对他小小的善举表示了过分的感谢。
近义词: excessively gratefully, too gratefully
反义词: ungratefully, thanklessly
词性: adverb

Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "grateful" (adjective meaning "feeling or showing an appreciation of kindness; thankful") + "-ly" (adverb suffix)
