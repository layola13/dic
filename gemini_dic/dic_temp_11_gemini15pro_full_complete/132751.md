nondomestic:/ˌnɒndəˈmɛstɪk/ /ˌnɑːndəˈmɛstɪk/| adj.|非国内的，非家庭的，野生的|Not related to the home or household; not native to a particular country; foreign.|例句：Nondomestic sales increased by 15% this quarter. 本季度非国内销售额增长了15%。|近义词：foreign, international, overseas, wild|反义词：domestic, native, local|词性:形容词

nondomestic:/ˌnɒndəˈmɛstɪk/ /ˌnɑːndəˈmɛstɪk/| n.|外来物种|A species not native to a particular region.|例句：The introduction of nondomestic species can have devastating effects on local ecosystems. 引进外来物种会对当地生态系统造成破坏性影响。|近义词：introduced species, exotic species, alien species|反义词：native species, indigenous species|词性:名词
