laevorotatory:/ˌliːvəˈrəʊtətɔːri/ /ˌlevəˈroʊtətɔːri/ | adj. |左旋的 | Rotating the plane of polarized light to the left.  A substance that rotates plane-polarized light to the left (counter-clockwise). | This chemical compound is laevorotatory.  这种化合物是左旋的。

| The laevorotatory form of the sugar is less common.  这种糖的左旋形式不太常见。 | levorotatory, left-handed (in terms of optical activity) | dextrorotatory | adjective
