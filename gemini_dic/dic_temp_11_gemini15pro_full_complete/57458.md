draftable:/ˈdræftəbl/| adj.|可起草的，可征召的|Eligible to be drafted; capable of being drafted.  Suitable for being made into a draft or plan.|The new law makes all 18-year-olds draftable.  新法律规定所有18岁的人都可被征召入伍。|synonyms: eligible, qualified, available|antonyms: ineligible, unqualified, unavailable|adjective
draft:/drɑːft/| n. vt.|草稿，汇票；起草，征兵|A preliminary version of a piece of writing; a written order to pay a sum of money; to select someone compulsorily, especially for military service.|I'm working on the first draft of my novel. 我正在写我的小说的初稿。|synonyms: sketch, outline, version; conscription, recruitment|antonyms: final version; discharge, release|noun, verb
