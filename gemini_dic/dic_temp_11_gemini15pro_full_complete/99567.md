interimistical:/ˌɪntərɪmˈɪstɪkəl/ | adj. | 临时的，过渡的 |  Of or relating to an interim; temporary; provisional.  It describes something that is meant to last only for a short time, until a permanent arrangement can be made. | The interimistical government held power until the elections could be held. 过渡政府掌权，直到选举能够举行。| The interimistical solution was to patch the roof until a full repair could be scheduled. 临时解决方案是修补屋顶，直到可以安排全面维修。|  Synonyms: temporary, provisional, interim, transitional | Antonyms: permanent, lasting, enduring | word property: adjective
