bargecourse:/ˈbɑːrdʒkɔːrs/| n. |驳船航道，驳船水道|A navigable waterway, especially a canal, suitable for barges.|The bargecourse was too shallow for the heavily loaded vessel. 这条驳船航道对于这艘满载的船来说太浅了。|近义词：canal, waterway, channel|反义词: None|词性: 名词

Etymology:
"barge" (from Old French "barge," a type of boat) + "course" (from Old French "cors," meaning "run," "running," or "path")
