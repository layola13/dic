factorising:/ˈfæktəraɪzɪŋ/ | n. | 因式分解，因子分解 | The process of finding the factors of a mathematical expression.  |  Example: Factorising the expression x² + 5x + 6 gives (x+2)(x+3).  因式分解表达式 x² + 5x + 6 得到 (x+2)(x+3). | Synonyms: factoring | Antonyms: expanding, multiplying | noun

factorise:/ˈfæktəraɪz/ | v. | 因式分解，因子分解 | To express (a mathematical expression) as a product of two or more factors. | Example: Factorise the quadratic equation.  将二次方程式因式分解。 | Synonyms: factor | Antonyms: expand, multiply | verb
