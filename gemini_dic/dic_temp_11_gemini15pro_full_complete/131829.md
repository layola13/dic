nonconsignment:/ˌnɒnkənˈsaɪnmənt/
noun
非寄售，非委托
A system of selling goods where the retailer pays for the goods upfront, rather than selling them on behalf of the supplier and paying only after they are sold.
The store operates on a nonconsignment basis, meaning they purchase all inventory outright. 这家商店以非寄售的方式经营，这意味着他们直接购买所有库存。
