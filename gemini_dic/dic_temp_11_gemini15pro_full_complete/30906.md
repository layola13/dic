chai:/tʃaɪ/| n.|印度奶茶，香料茶|A spiced tea beverage originating from India. It typically contains black tea infused with aromatic spices like cardamom, cinnamon, ginger, cloves, and black pepper.  Milk and sweetener are also commonly added.|I love a warm cup of chai on a cold day. 我喜欢在寒冷的日子里喝一杯热腾腾的印度奶茶。|She ordered a chai latte from the coffee shop.  她从咖啡店点了一杯印度奶茶拿铁。|近义词：masala chai, spiced tea|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps coffee or plain tea)|词性:noun
