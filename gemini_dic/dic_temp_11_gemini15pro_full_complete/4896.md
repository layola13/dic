alikenesses:/əˈlaɪknɪsɪz/
|n.|相似之处，相似点|Resemblances or similarities between people or things.|There are striking alikenesses between the two sisters. 这两姐妹之间有惊人的相似之处。|近义词: similarities, resemblances, parallels, affinities|反义词: differences, dissimilarities, contrasts|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *alikenes*, equivalent to alike +‎ -ness.
