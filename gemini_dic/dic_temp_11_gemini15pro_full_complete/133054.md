nonepiscopalian:/ˌnɒnɪpɪˈskəʊpəlɪən/
adjective
非圣公会的；非英国国教的
Not belonging to or connected with the Episcopal Church or any church of the Anglican Communion.
The chapel served a diverse congregation, including nonepiscopalian Christians. 这座小教堂服务于不同的会众，包括非圣公会的基督徒。
Near synonyms: non-Anglican, nonconformist
Antonyms: Episcopalian, Anglican
词性: 形容词
