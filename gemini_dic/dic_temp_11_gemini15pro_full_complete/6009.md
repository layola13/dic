ameba:/əˈmiːbə/ (US), /əˈmiːbə/ (UK)| n. |变形虫，阿米巴|A single-celled animal that catches food and moves about by extending fingerlike projections of protoplasm.  A microscopic living organism, usually single-celled, that can change its shape.|
Amebas are found in freshwater and soil. 变形虫在淡水和土壤中被发现。

Amebas can cause dysentery.  变形虫能引起痢疾。|
Synonyms: amoeba, protozoan|
Antonyms: None (as it's a specific type of organism)|
Noun

amebas:/əˈmiːbəz/ (US), /əˈmiːbəz/ (UK)| n. |变形虫（复数）|Plural form of ameba.  Multiple single-celled animals that catch food and move about by extending fingerlike projections of protoplasm.|
Amebas are simple organisms. 变形虫是简单的有机体。

Many different types of amebas exist in nature. 自然界中存在许多不同种类的变形虫。|
Synonyms: amoebas, protozoa|
Antonyms: None (as it's a specific type of organism)|
