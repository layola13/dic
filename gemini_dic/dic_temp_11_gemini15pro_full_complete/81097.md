graminaceous:/ˌɡræmɪˈneɪʃəs/
adjective
禾本科的；似禾草的；草本的
Relating to or resembling grasses; belonging to the family Gramineae (Poaceae).
The prairie is dominated by graminaceous plants.  草原上以禾本科植物为主。
Synonyms: grassy, gramineous, herbaceous
Antonyms: woody, arboreal
词性: adjective
