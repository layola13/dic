my:/maɪ/
词性:pron.
中文翻译:我的
英文解析: possessive pronoun.  Used to show that something belongs to or is connected to the speaker.
例句：This is my book. 这是我的书。
例句：My car is red. 我的车是红色的。
近义词:mine (possessive pronoun, absolute form)
反义词:your, his, her, its, our, their
词性:pronoun
