cacher:/kæˈʃeɪ/ (US), /ˈkæʃeɪ/ (UK)| v. |隐藏，贮藏|To hide or store something in a concealed place. |例句：He cached the treasure under a loose floorboard. 他把宝藏藏在松动的木板下。

例句：Squirrels cache nuts for the winter. 松鼠贮藏坚果过冬。|近义词：conceal, hide, stash, store|反义词：reveal, expose, uncover|词性:动词

cacher (noun): /kæˈʃeɪ/ (US), /ˈkæʃeɪ/ (UK)| n. |隐藏处，贮藏处|A hiding place or storage place. |例句：The police found the cacher where the thief had hidden the stolen goods. 警察找到了小偷藏赃物的地方。

例句：The old tree served as a cacher for the children's toys.  这棵老树成了孩子们玩具的藏身之处。|近义词：hideout, hiding place, storage|反义词：N/A|词性:名词


词源: French, from cacher "to hide," from Vulgar Latin *coacticare, frequentative of Latin coactare "to force, compel," from cogere "to gather together, compel."
