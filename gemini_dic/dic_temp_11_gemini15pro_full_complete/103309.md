jiffy's:/ˈdʒɪfiz/| n.| 极短的时间，瞬间 |A jiffy is a very short period of time.|'I'll be back in a jiffy.' 我马上就回来。|Synonyms: moment, second, instant|Antonyms: eternity, age|noun
