nonintellectualnesses:/ˌnɑːnˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəlnəsɪz/
noun
缺乏智力；非智力性
lack of intellectual capacity or interest; the state or quality of not being intellectual
His nonintellectualnesses led him to choose a career in manual labor. 他的缺乏智力使他选择从事体力劳动。
Near Synonyms: unintelligence, simplemindedness, mindlessness
Antonyms: intelligence, intellect, brilliance
词性: 名词
