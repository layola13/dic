baggiest: /ˈbæɡiɪst/ | adj. | 最宽松的，最袋状的 | Superlative form of baggy: most baggy.  It means something is extremely loose-fitting, typically clothing. |  His baggiest pair of jeans were his favorites. 他最宽松的牛仔裤是他最喜欢的。|  The clown wore the baggiest pants I'd ever seen.  那个小丑穿着我见过的最宽松的裤子。| 近义词：loosest, roomiest, most oversized | 反义词：tightest, snuggest, most fitted | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From baggy + -est (superlative suffix). "Baggy" itself comes from "bag" (referring to its loose, sack-like appearance).  The "-est" suffix is used to form the superlative degree of adjectives.
