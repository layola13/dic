conglomerative:/kənˈɡlɒmərətɪv/ /kənˈɡlɑːmərətɪv/
adjective
聚集的，成团的，砾岩的
Relating to, or consisting of a conglomerate; gathered into a rounded mass or ball.
The conglomerate rock was made up of many smaller stones. 这块砾岩由许多小石头组成。

The company is a massive conglomerate with holdings in various industries.  这家公司是一个庞大的企业集团，在各行各业都有控股。
近义词: aggregate, combined, composite
反义词: discrete, separate, individual
词性: adjective

conglomerate:/kənˈɡlɒmərət/ /kənˈɡlɑːmərət/
noun
聚集体，砾岩，企业集团
A thing consisting of a number of different and distinct parts or items that are grouped together; a rock consisting of rounded fragments cemented together; a large corporation formed by merging several smaller companies.
The conglomerate controls a number of subsidiary companies.  这个企业集团控制着许多子公司。

The geologist examined the conglomerate, noting the various types of rock within it. 地质学家检查了这块砾岩，注意到其中各种类型的岩石。
近义词: aggregation, collection, cluster, corporation, combine
反义词: individual, element
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin conglomeratus, past participle of conglomerare "to roll together, heap up," from Latin com- "together" + glomerare "to wind into a ball," from glomus "ball."
