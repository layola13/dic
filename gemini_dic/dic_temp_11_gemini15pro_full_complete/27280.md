calumnied:/kəˈlʌmniːd/
|v.|被诽谤的|having been falsely and maliciously accused|He felt calumnied by the newspaper article. 他觉得被那篇报纸文章诽谤了。|近义词：defamed, slandered, libeled, vilified|反义词：praised, lauded, extolled|Verb (past participle)

calumny:/ˈkæləmni/
|n.|诽谤|a false and malicious statement designed to injure the reputation of someone or something|The politician spread calumny about his opponent.  这位政客散布关于他对手的诽谤。|近义词：slander, defamation, libel|反义词：praise, commendation|Noun
