hypertypical:/ˌhaɪpərˈtɪpɪkl/ | adj. | 超典型的，极具代表性的 |  Exhibiting features or characteristics that are exaggerated or intensified compared to the typical or average example of a category.  It goes beyond simply being typical; it represents an intensified or exaggerated version of the typical qualities. |  Her performance was hypertypical of the genre, showcasing all the key elements with exceptional skill. 她的表演是该类型的超典型代表，以非凡的技巧展示了所有关键元素。 |  The child's behavior was hypertypical of autism, displaying intense focus on specific interests and repetitive actions.  这个孩子的行为是自闭症的超典型表现，表现出对特定兴趣的强烈关注和重复性动作。 | 近义词: quintessential, exemplary, prototypical, archetypal | 反义词: atypical, unusual, aberrant | 词性: 形容词

词源:
"hyper-" (Greek): over, above, beyond
"-typical": relating to or constituting a type
