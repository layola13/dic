nonendemic:/ˌnɒnɛnˈdɛmɪk/ (US) /ˌnɒnɛnˈdɛmɪk/ (UK)| adj.|非地方性的，非流行的|Not native or restricted to a certain region or population; not endemic.|The virus, while nonendemic to this region, has quickly spread due to international travel. 这种病毒虽然并非该地区的特有病毒，但由于国际旅行已迅速传播。|  The rise in cases suggests the disease is becoming nonendemic and spreading to new areas. 病例的增加表明该疾病正在变得非地方性，并蔓延到新的地区。|Synonyms: non-native, foreign, introduced, exotic|Antonyms: endemic, native, indigenous|adjective
