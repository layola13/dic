crease:/kriːs/| n. |折痕，皱纹|A line or ridge produced on paper or cloth by folding or crushing it.|The paper had a crease down the middle. 这张纸中间有一条折痕。|近义词：wrinkle, fold, furrow|反义词:smoothness, flatness|noun
crease:/kriːs/| v. |使起皱褶，弄皱|To make a line or ridge in (something, typically paper or cloth) by folding or crushing.|He carefully creased the paper to make a card. 他小心地把纸折起来做成一张卡片。|近义词：wrinkle, crinkle, rumple|反义词:smooth, iron, flatten|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *krýsa "to wrinkle," related to Old English crysan "to ripple."
