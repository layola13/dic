amorphousness:/əˈmɔːrfəsnəs/| n. |无定形；非晶形；无组织|The state of being amorphous; lacking definite form or shape.  |The amorphousness of the clay made it difficult to mold.  这种黏土的无定形使它难以塑形。|Synonyms: formlessness, shapelessness, indefiniteness, structurelessness |Antonyms: definiteness, structure, form, crystallinity|noun
