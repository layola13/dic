debitable:/ˈdɛbɪtəbl/
adjective
可借记的，可扣除的
Capable of being debited; chargeable.  Something that can be entered as a debit in an account.
The expense is debitable to your account.  这笔费用可记入您的账户。
The amount is debitable against this year's budget. 这笔款项可以从今年的预算中扣除。
Synonyms: chargeable, accountable
Antonyms: creditable
词性: adjective
