malleableness:/ˌmæliəbəl.nəs/ |n.| 延展性；可塑性；顺从；温顺|The quality of being malleable; capable of being shaped or formed, often by hammering or pressure. Also refers to a flexible or compliant nature. |The malleableness of gold makes it ideal for jewelry.  黄金的延展性使它成为制作珠宝的理想材料。

The artist admired the clay's malleableness.  艺术家欣赏黏土的可塑性。

His malleableness made him easy to manipulate. 他的顺从使他很容易被操纵。

Her malleableness allowed her to adapt to new situations easily. 她的温顺使她能够轻松适应新环境。 |近义词: plasticity, flexibility, ductility, docility, compliance |反义词: rigidity, inflexibility, stubbornness, resistance| 词性: noun
