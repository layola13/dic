itinerate:/aɪˈtɪnəreɪt/ | v. | 旅行；巡回；巡游 | to travel from place to place, especially on a fixed route or for a specific purpose | The preacher itinerated throughout the countryside, spreading his message.  传教士在乡下巡回，传播他的信息。 |  travel, journey, tour, roam, wander | settle, stay, remain | verb

itinerant:/aɪˈtɪnərənt/ | adj. | 巡回的；流动的； itinerant preacher 巡回传教士 | traveling from place to place, especially to work | Itinerant workers moved from farm to farm, harvesting crops. 流动工人从一个农场搬到另一个农场收割庄稼。 |  traveling, nomadic, wandering, roaming, peripatetic | settled, stationary, resident | adjective

itinerary:/aɪˈtɪnərəri/ | n. | 旅行计划；行程；路线 | a planned route or journey |  The travel agent prepared a detailed itinerary for their trip to Europe. 旅行社为他们的欧洲之旅准备了详细的行程。 | schedule, program, route, plan |  deviation | noun
