infeasibleness:/ɪnˌfiːzəˈbɪlnəs/| n.|不可行性|The infeasibleness of the project became apparent after a thorough analysis. |The infeasibleness of building a bridge across the Atlantic is obvious. |近义词：impracticability, impossibility, unworkability|反义词: feasibility, possibility, practicability|词性:noun
infeasible:/ɪnˈfiːzəbl/| adj.|不可行的|Due to the limited resources, the plan was deemed infeasible. |It's infeasible to expect everyone to agree.|近义词：impractical, impossible, unworkable|反义词: feasible, possible, workable|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + feasible (from Old French faisable, from faire "to do," from Latin facere "to make, do") + -ness (suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality).
