annulation:/ˌænjuˈleɪʃn/ | n. | 取消，废除 |The annulment of the contract relieved both parties of their obligations.  合同的取消解除了双方的义务。| Example: The annulment of their marriage was a long and difficult process. 他们的婚姻废除是一个漫长而艰难的过程。| Synonyms: cancellation, revocation, repeal, abrogation | Antonyms: ratification, confirmation, validation | noun

Etymology:  From Late Middle English *annullacioun, from Old French annulation, from Late Latin annullātiō, from Latin annullō (“I abolish, cancel”), from ad (“to”) + nullus (“none, not any”).
