chamsin:/ˌkɑːmˈsiːn/| n.|哈姆辛风（来自阿拉伯语，指来自埃及沙漠的热风）|A hot, dry, dust-laden southerly wind blowing in North Africa and the Middle East, especially in Egypt, Israel, Palestine, Jordan, Syria and Lebanon.|The chamsin blew relentlessly for three days, coating everything in a fine layer of dust.|近义词：sirocco, simoom, khamsin|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑表达凉爽风的词，例如breeze)|词性: 名词
