amenity:/əˈmiːnəti/ /əˈmenəti/| n.| 便利设施；舒适；礼仪；愉快；娱乐 |Something that adds to one's comfort or convenience.  Something intended to make life more pleasant or easier. |The hotel offers a wide range of amenities, including a swimming pool, spa, and fitness center.  这家酒店提供各种各样的便利设施，包括游泳池、水疗中心和健身中心。|  近义词: facility, convenience, comfort, service | 反义词: inconvenience, discomfort | 词性: 名词

amenity:/əˈmiːnəti/ /əˈmenəti/| n.|  舒适；礼仪；愉快|Pleasantness; agreeable ways or manners. |The staff were known for their amenity and helpfulness.  工作人员以其和蔼可亲和乐于助人而闻名。| 近义词: civility, courtesy, pleasantness| 反义词: rudeness, impoliteness| 词性: 名词
