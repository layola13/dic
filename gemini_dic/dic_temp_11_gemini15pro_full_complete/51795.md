devout:/dɪˈvaʊt/| adj.|虔诚的；衷心的|Deeply religious or sincere; earnest.|She is a devout Catholic. 她是一位虔诚的天主教徒。|例句：He has always been a devout supporter of the arts. 他一直是艺术的忠实支持者。|近义词：pious, religious, dedicated, committed|反义词：impious, irreligious, faithless|词性:形容词
词源: From Old French *devot "devoted," past participle of devorer "devote, dedicate," from Latin devovere "dedicate by vow," from de- "down, completely" + vovere "to vow."
