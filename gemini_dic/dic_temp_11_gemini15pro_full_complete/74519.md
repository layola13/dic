fraternalism: /frəˈtɜːrnəlɪzəm/ | n. | 兄弟情谊，博爱精神 | The spirit or feeling of brotherhood; brotherly or fraternal regard.  A social organization based on the principles of brotherhood. |  Example 1: The fraternalism of the firefighters was evident as they supported each other through the difficult rescue. 消防员之间的兄弟情谊在他们互相支持完成艰难的救援行动时显而易见。

Example 2:  The fraternity promoted fraternalism and community service among its members.  这个兄弟会倡导其成员之间的兄弟情谊和社区服务。 | Synonyms: brotherhood, fellowship, camaraderie, solidarity | Antonyms: animosity, hostility, antagonism | noun

fraternal: /frəˈtɜːrnl/ | adj. | 兄弟般的，友爱的 | Of or relating to brothers; brotherly. | Example 1:  They shared a fraternal bond, closer than many biological brothers.  他们有着兄弟般的纽带，比许多亲兄弟还要亲密。

Example 2: The fraternal organization raised money for local charities. 这个兄弟般的组织为当地慈善机构筹款。 | Synonyms: brotherly, amicable | Antonyms: hostile, unfriendly | adjective
