changeably:/ˈtʃeɪndʒəbli/
adjective
易变地，可变地
in a manner that is likely to change frequently
The weather here is changeably, so be prepared for anything.  这里的天气变化无常，所以要做好各种准备。
Her moods are changeably; she can be happy one minute and sad the next. 她的情绪多变；她可能前一分钟快乐，下一分钟就悲伤了。
Synonyms: variably, unpredictably, erratically, inconstantly
Antonyms: constantly, consistently, steadily, invariably
词性: 形容词
