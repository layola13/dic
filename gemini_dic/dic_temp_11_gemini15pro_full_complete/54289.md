disgusted:/dɪsˈɡʌstɪd/
adjective
厌恶的，憎恶的
feeling extreme dislike or disapproval of something.
例句：I was disgusted by his behavior. 我对他的行为感到厌恶。

例句：She felt disgusted with herself for lying. 她对自己撒谎感到恶心。


近义词：revolted, nauseated, repelled, sickened
反义词：pleased, delighted, impressed
词性: adjective
