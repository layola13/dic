at-the-mercy-of:/æt ðə ˈmɜːrsi ɒv/ /æt ðə ˈmɜːrsi əv/
prepositional phrase
受...支配；任凭...摆布
Completely in the power of someone or something, and unable to protect yourself from them.
The refugees were at the mercy of the weather. 难民们任凭天气摆布。
synonyms: powerless against, vulnerable to, exposed to, helpless against, defenseless against, under the thumb of, at the whim of, subject to the whims of
antonyms: in control of, protected from
