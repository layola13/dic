goes:/ɡoʊz/ (US) /ɡəʊz/ (UK)| v.|去，进行，进展，运转|Third-person singular simple present indicative form of go|She goes to school every day. 她每天都去上学。| proceed, move, travel, function|stays, remains|verb
Etymology: From Middle English *gōþ, *gāþ, from Old English gæþ (“goes, goes away, departs, passes away, dies, happens, is committed, comes to pass, comes, elapses”), third-person singular present indicative of gān (“to go”).
