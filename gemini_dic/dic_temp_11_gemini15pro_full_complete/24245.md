brie:/briː/
n.
布里干酪，白霉奶酪
A soft, mild, creamy cheese with a white rind, originally from France.
例句：The brie was perfectly ripe and spread easily on the crackers. 布里奶酪熟得恰到好处，很容易涂在饼干上。

例句：We had a selection of cheeses, including brie, cheddar, and gouda.  我们有各种各样的奶酪，包括布里奶酪、切达奶酪和高达奶酪。


近义词：Camembert, triple crème cheese
