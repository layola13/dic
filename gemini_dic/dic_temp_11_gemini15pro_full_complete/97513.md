ingloriously:/ɪnˈɡlɔːriəsli/
adverb
不光彩地，可耻地
in a way that is not glorious; shamefully or dishonorably.
He died ingloriously in a drunken brawl. 他在一次醉酒斗殴中不光彩地死去。

The team performed ingloriously, losing every match.  这个队伍表现糟糕，每场比赛都输了。

近义词:shamefully, dishonorably, disgracefully, ignominiously
反义词:gloriously, honorably, triumphantly

词性:adverb
