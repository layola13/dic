comprobate:/ˈkɒmprəʊbeɪt/ (US) /ˈkɑːmprəbeɪt/| n.|遗嘱认证, 认证书| An official copy of a will and a grant of administration, officially authorizing the executor to administer the estate. |例句：The executor presented the comprobate to the bank to access the deceased's funds.  执行人向银行出示了遗嘱认证书，以便提取死者的资金。

例句：Obtaining the comprobate was the first step in settling the estate. 获得遗嘱认证是处理遗产的第一步。|近义词：probate, letters testamentary, letters of administration|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
