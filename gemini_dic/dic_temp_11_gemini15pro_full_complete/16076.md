bandmaster:/ˈbændˌmæstər/ (US), /ˈbændˌmɑːstə(r)/ (UK)| n.|乐队指挥，军乐队队长|A person who directs a band, especially a military or brass band.|The bandmaster raised his baton and the music began. 乐队指挥举起指挥棒，音乐开始了。|The bandmaster led the parade through the town. 乐队指挥带领游行队伍穿过城镇。|synonyms: conductor, director, leader|antonyms: musician, player|noun
