decreed:/dɪˈkriːd/
verb
颁布；裁定；注定
officially ordered something to happen
The court decreed that the company be liquidated. 法院裁定该公司清算。
synonym: ordered, commanded, ruled, dictated
antonym: rescinded, revoked, countermanded
词性: 动词

decree:/dɪˈkriː/
noun
法令；判决；裁定
an official order that has the force of law
The president issued a decree banning smoking in public places. 总统颁布了一项法令，禁止在公共场所吸烟。
synonym: order, command, ruling, edict
antonym: repeal, revocation
词性: 名词

词源: From Old French *decreer*, from Latin *decernere* "to decide, determine," from *de-* "down, completely" + *cernere* "to separate, distinguish, perceive."
