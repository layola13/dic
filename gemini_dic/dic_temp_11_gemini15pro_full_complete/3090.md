aerophobia:/ˌɛəroʊˈfoʊbiə/ /ˌeərəˈfəʊbiə/| n. |恐飞症，飞行恐惧症|An extreme or irrational fear of flying.|例句：Her aerophobia prevented her from traveling abroad. 她的恐飞症使她无法出国旅行。

例句：He is trying to overcome his aerophobia through therapy. 他正在尝试通过治疗克服他的恐飞症。|近义词：aviophobia, fear of flying|反义词: None. |词性: noun
