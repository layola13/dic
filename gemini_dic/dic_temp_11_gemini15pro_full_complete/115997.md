marshaller:/ˈmɑːrʃələr/ | n. |编组员，调度员，整理员| A person who arranges or organizes something, especially people or things that are going to move from one place to another.  | The marshaller directed the planes to their designated parking spots.  调度员将飞机引导到指定的停机位。 |  coordinator, organizer, director | disorganizer | noun

marshaller:/ˈmɑːrʃələr/ | n. | (computing)编组程序，数据序列化程序 | A piece of software that converts data into a format suitable for transmission or storage, and vice versa. | The marshaller converts the complex data structures into a simple stream of bytes for transmission over the network.  编组程序将复杂的数据结构转换为简单的字节流，以便通过网络传输。 | serializer, encoder, decoder |  - | noun
