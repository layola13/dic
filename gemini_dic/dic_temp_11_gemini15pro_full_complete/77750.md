gendering:/ˈdʒɛndərɪŋ/
verb
赋予性别，性别化
The process of ascribing or attributing a gender to someone or something.
The author avoids gendering the protagonist in the novel, leaving their sex ambiguous. 作者在小说中避免给主人公赋予性别，让他们的性别模棱两可。
The act of constructing or representing someone or something as having a particular gender.
The advertisement's gendering of toys was criticized for reinforcing stereotypes.  这则广告对玩具的性别化设计因强化刻板印象而受到批评。
synonyms:  categorizing by gender, assigning gender, sexualizing, typifying
antonyms:  neutralizing, degendering
