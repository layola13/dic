overroasted:/ˌoʊvərˈroʊstɪd/| adj.|烤过头的，烤焦了的|Roasted for too long, resulting in dryness or burning.|The chicken was overroasted and dry. 这鸡肉烤过头了，很干。|近义词：burnt, scorched, overcooked|反义词: undercooked, rare|词性:形容词
Etymology: over- + roasted. "Over-" is an Old English prefix meaning "excessively." "Roasted" comes from the Old French "rostir," meaning "to roast."
