assubjugate: /əˈsʌbdʒʊɡeɪt/ | v. | 征服， subjugate，使屈服，压制 | To bring under control by force; conquer.  | The Roman empire assubjugated many countries. 罗马帝国征服了许多国家。 |  The king assubjugated the rebellious province. 国王征服了反叛的省份。 | 近义词: conquer, subdue, vanquish, overcome | 反义词: liberate, free, emancipate | 词性: 动词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *assubjugāre*, from Latin *subjugāre* (“to subdue, subjugate”), from *sub* (“under”) + *jugum* (“yoke”).  The prefix *as-* is an assimilated form of *ad-*.
