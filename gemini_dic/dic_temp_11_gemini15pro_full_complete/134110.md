nonimmunities:/ˌnɒnɪˈmjuːnɪtiz/
noun
缺乏免疫力；无豁免权
The state of not possessing immunity or exemption from something.
The refugees' nonimmunities to local diseases caused a rapid spread of illness.  难民对当地疾病缺乏免疫力导致疾病迅速蔓延。
Their nonimmunities to prosecution led to several arrests. 他们没有豁免被起诉的权利，导致多人被捕。
Near Antonyms: immunities, exemptions
Antonyms: vulnerabilities, susceptibilities
