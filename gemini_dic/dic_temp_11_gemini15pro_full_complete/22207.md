bona fide:/ˌbōnə ˈfīdē/ /ˌbɒnə ˈfaɪdiː/
adjective
真诚的；真实的；合法的
genuine; real; undertaken in good faith
例句：He made a bona fide offer for the house.  他真的出价买这栋房子。

例句：Only bona fide members of the club are allowed to use the facilities. 只有真正的俱乐部成员才可以使用这些设施。

近义词：genuine, authentic, real, legitimate, valid
反义词：fake, counterfeit, spurious, sham, fraudulent
词性：adjective
