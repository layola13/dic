labelled:/ˈleɪb(ə)ld/ | v. | 贴标签于，标记 | To attach a label to. |  They labelled the samples with the date and time. 他们给样品贴上了日期和时间的标签。|  The technician labelled all the wires for easy identification. 技术人员给所有的电线都贴上了标签以便于识别。| synonyms: tag, mark, identify | antonyms: unlabel, remove label | verb (past participle of label)
