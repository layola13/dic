convener:/kənˈviːnər/
n.|召集人，召集者|A person who organizes a meeting or conference. |The convener of the meeting ensured everyone had a chance to speak. 会议的召集者确保每个人都有机会发言。|organizer, coordinator, chair| attendee, participant |noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "convene" which comes from Old French "convenir"  meaning "to agree, assemble, be suitable", ultimately from Latin "convenīre" "to come together, assemble, agree".
