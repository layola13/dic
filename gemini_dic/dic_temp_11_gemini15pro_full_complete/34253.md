clape:/klæp/
n.
拍手声，掌声
The sound of clapping hands.
The audience erupted in claps after the performance.  演出结束后，观众爆发出热烈的掌声。
近义词：applause, ovation
反义词:silence, hush
词性:名词


clape:/klæp/
v.
拍手，鼓掌
To strike the palms of one's hands together.
The children clapped their hands in excitement. 孩子们兴奋地拍手。
近义词：applaud
反义词:boo
词性:动词

词源:
Likely from a variant of "clap," influenced by words like "scrape" or "grape."  Its origin is uncertain and considered obscure or dialectal.  It likely emerged through the same imitative process as "clap," mimicking the sound of hands striking together.
