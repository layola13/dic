electing:/ɪˈlɛktɪŋ/
verb
选举；选择
present participle of elect
例句：
They are electing a new president.
他们正在选举一位新总统。

The people are electing their representatives.
人民正在选举他们的代表。

近义词：choosing, appointing, selecting, voting for
反义词：rejecting, dismissing

词性: verb (动词)
词源: From Middle English *electen, from Old French elire (“to choose, elect”), from Latin ēligere (“to pick out, choose, select, elect”).
