haematocrya:/ˌhiːmətəˈkraɪə/ | n. | 冷血动物 | Animals whose blood temperature varies with the surrounding environment; cold-blooded animals. |  Lizards, snakes, and frogs are all examples of haematocrya. 蜥蜴、蛇和青蛙都是冷血动物的例子。| synonyms: poikilotherm, ectotherm | antonyms: homeotherm, endotherm | noun
haematocryal:/ˌhiːmətəˈkraɪəl/ | adj. | 冷血的 | Having blood whose temperature varies with the surrounding environment; cold-blooded. | Haematocryal animals are less active in cold weather. 冷血动物在寒冷的天气里活动较少。| synonyms: poikilothermic, ectothermic | antonyms: homeothermic, endothermic | adjective
