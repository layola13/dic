accident-prone:/ˈæksɪdəntˌproʊn/| adj.|易出事故的，容易发生意外的|Likely to have accidents; clumsy.|例句：He's so accident-prone, he's always tripping over things. 他很容易出事，总是被东西绊倒。

例句：She's an accident-prone driver and has had several minor collisions. 她是一位容易出事故的司机，已经发生过几次小碰撞了。|近义词：clumsy, awkward, mishap-prone, disaster-prone|反义词:careful, cautious, safe|词性:形容词
