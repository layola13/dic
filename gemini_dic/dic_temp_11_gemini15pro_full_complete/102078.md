isopiestic:/ˌaɪsoʊˈpiːstɪk/ | adj. | 等压的 | Having equal or constant osmotic pressure.  Relating to solutions that have the same or equal osmotic pressure. |  The isopiestic method is used to determine the water activity of food. 等压法用于测定食物的水分活度。 | The two solutions are isopiestic. 这两种溶液是等压的。 |  isotonic, isosmotic | anisopiestic | adjective
