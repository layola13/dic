nonprovidential:/ˌnɒnprɒvɪˈdɛnʃəl/
adjective
非天意的，非神助的，偶然的
Not attributed to or characterized by divine providence; due to chance or human agency.
The nonprovidential nature of the accident suggests it was simply a tragic coincidence. 这起事故的非天意性质表明它只是一场悲剧性的巧合。
The scientist argued for a nonprovidential explanation for the origin of life.  这位科学家主张用非神创论来解释生命的起源。
近义词: accidental, chance, fortuitous, random, unintentional
反义词: providential, destined, fated, preordained
词性: adjective

nonprovidential: /ˌnɒnprɒvɪˈdɛnʃəl/
词源:  From non- + providential.  "Providential" comes from Late Latin *providentialis, from providentia "foresight, providence," from providens, present participle of providere "to foresee, provide," from pro- "before" + videre "to see."
