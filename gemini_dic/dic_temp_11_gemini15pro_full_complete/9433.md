antirealisms:/ˌæntiˈriːəlɪzəmz/| n.|反现实主义|The doctrines that deny any independent reality to the objects of our judgments, or that deny we can know a mind-independent reality.|Philosophical antirealisms encompasses a variety of ideas, including the belief that the external world is not independent of our minds.|近义词：idealism, subjectivism|反义词:realism|词性:noun
