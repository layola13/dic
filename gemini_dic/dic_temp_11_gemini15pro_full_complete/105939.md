klicket:/ˈklɪkɪt/| n.|点击声，咔嗒声; (尤指旧式电话的)拨号盘|A short, sharp sound, such as that made by a switch or a ratchet.  Specifically, the sound of a rotary dial telephone.|例句：I heard a distinct klicket as the lock engaged. 我听到锁扣上时发出清晰的咔嗒声。

例句：The old telephone made a satisfying klicket with each number dialed.  老式电话每拨一个号码都会发出令人满意的咔嗒声。|近义词：click, clack, snap|反义词: (没有明确的反义词)|词性:名词


词源:
Likely imitative of the sound.  Similar to "click" and other onomatopoeic words.  Potentially influenced by German "klicken" (to click).
