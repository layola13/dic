centrolecithal: /ˌsɛntrəˈlɛsɪθəl/
| adj. | 中黄的 | Having the yolk concentrated in the center of the ovum, as in insects and many other arthropods. |  The centrolecithal eggs of insects undergo superficial cleavage. 昆虫的中黄卵进行表面卵裂。 |  近义词:  telolecithal (in some contexts, referring to yolk concentration) | 反义词: isolecithal | 词性: adjective

centro-: /ˈsɛntrəʊ/ | prefix | 中心- |  From Greek *kéntron* meaning "center" or "point."  Forms the beginning of words relating to a center or central point.  | 中心- |  近义词: mid- | 反义词: peri- | 词性: prefix

-lecithal: /ˈlɛsɪθəl/ | suffix |  -黄的 |  From Greek *lékithos* meaning "yolk of an egg."  Relates to the yolk of an egg. |  -黄的 |  词性: suffix
