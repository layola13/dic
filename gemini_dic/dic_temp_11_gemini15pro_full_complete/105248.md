khalifs:/ˈkælɪfs/ | n. | 哈里发（伊斯兰教国家元首） | A title used by Muslim rulers, especially the successors of Muhammad as temporal and spiritual leaders of the Islamic community. |  The Abbasid khalifs ruled a vast empire. 阿巴斯王朝的哈里发统治着一个庞大的帝国。

The khalifs of the Ottoman Empire held considerable power. 奥斯曼帝国的哈里发掌握着相当大的权力。 | synonyms: caliphs, sultans, emirs | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "subjects" or "citizens" in a relative sense) | noun
