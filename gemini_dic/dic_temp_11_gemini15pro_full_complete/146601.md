overgrieving:/ˌoʊvərˈɡriːvɪŋ/
verb
过度悲伤，过分悲痛
Experiencing or expressing grief excessively or for a prolonged period.
She was overgrieving the loss of her pet hamster, neglecting her work and social life. 她过度悲伤于失去她的宠物仓鼠，忽略了她的工作和社交生活。
He was so overgrieving about his failed exam that he couldn't concentrate on anything else.  他过度悲伤于考试不及格以至于无法集中精力做任何其他事情。
近义词: excessive grieving, prolonged grieving, wallowing in grief
反义词: accepting loss, moving on, recovering
词性: verb (动词)

词源:
"Over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "grieving" (present participle of "grieve")
