mobility:/moʊˈbɪləti/ | n. | 流动性；移动性；机动性 | The ability to move or be moved freely and easily. |  Electric cars are increasing mobility for people in cities. 电动汽车正在提高城市居民的出行能力。

The new job offered her greater mobility and opportunities for advancement. 这份新工作为她提供了更大的灵活性以及晋升机会。


近义词: flexibility, maneuverability, portability, versatility
反义词: immobility, inflexibility, rigidity
词性: noun
