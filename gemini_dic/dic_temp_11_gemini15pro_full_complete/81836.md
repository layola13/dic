greyish:/ˈɡreɪɪʃ/| adj.|浅灰色的，略带灰色的|Somewhat grey; having a tinge of grey.|The sky was a greyish white. 天空是灰白色的。|近义词：gray, silver, ashen|反义词:black, white, colorful|词性:形容词

词源: From grey + -ish.  The suffix "-ish" indicates a degree or slight resemblance to the root word.
