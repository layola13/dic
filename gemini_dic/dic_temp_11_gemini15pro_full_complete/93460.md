illusionistic:/ɪˌluːʒəˈnɪstɪk/
adjective
幻觉的，产生幻觉的
Creating or tending to create an illusion; deceptive.
The painting was so illusionistic that it looked like a real window. 这幅画非常逼真，看起来像一扇真窗户。
The magician's illusionistic tricks amazed the audience. 魔术师的幻术技巧让观众惊叹不已。
近义词: deceptive, illusory, misleading, trompe-l'oeil
反义词: realistic, genuine, authentic
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From illusion + -istic.  "Illusion" comes from the Latin "illusio," meaning "mockery" or "deception," derived from the verb "illudere" (to mock, play with). The suffix "-istic" forms adjectives meaning "relating to" or "characteristic of."
