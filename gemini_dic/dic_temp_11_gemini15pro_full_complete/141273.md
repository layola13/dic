opaque:/əʊˈpeɪk/ (UK) /oʊˈpeɪk/ (US)| adj. n.|不透明的，不透光的；难理解的，晦涩的|not allowing light to pass through; difficult to understand|The windows were opaque with steam. 窗户上满是蒸汽，变得不透明了。|Synonyms: nontransparent, cloudy, obscure, unclear|Antonyms: transparent, clear, translucent|adjective

opaque:/əʊˈpeɪk/ (UK) /oʊˈpeɪk/ (US)| n.|不透明物；遮光涂料|something that is opaque; a substance used to make something opaque|She used an opaque to cover the stain on the wall. 她用不透明涂料盖住了墙上的污渍。|Synonyms: coating, covering|Antonyms: transparent material|noun
