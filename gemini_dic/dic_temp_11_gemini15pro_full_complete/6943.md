andabatism:/ænˈdæbətɪzəm/
n.
失明行走能力; 蝙蝠式回声定位
The ability to walk or navigate without the use of sight, often by using echolocation, similar to a bat.
Bats use andabatism to fly in the dark.  蝙蝠在黑暗中飞行运用回声定位。
Andabatism allows certain cave-dwelling creatures to navigate their surroundings.  回声定位使某些穴居生物能够在周围环境中导航。
