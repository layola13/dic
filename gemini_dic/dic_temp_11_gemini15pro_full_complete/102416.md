词源:  The term "jack-in-a-box" likely originated from the use of "jack" as a generic term for a common man or fellow, and the surprise element of the toy popping out of the box.  Some sources speculate a connection to medieval "Jack-in-the-Green" festivities, but a definitive link is unclear.
