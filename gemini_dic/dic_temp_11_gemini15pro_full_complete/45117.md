cucullate:/ˈkjuːkjʊleɪt/| adj.|兜帽状的，头巾状的；僧侣袍状的|Having a hood or cowl; hooded.  Resembling a cowl or hood. |The cucullate flower of the monkshood plant is distinctive. 僧侣兜花的兜帽状花朵很特别。|The bird has cucullate feathers on its head. 这只鸟的头上长着兜帽状的羽毛。|近义词：hooded, cowled|反义词:unhooded|词性:adjective

词源: From Latin cucullatus, past participle of cucullare (“to furnish with a hood”), from cucullus (“hood, cowl”).
