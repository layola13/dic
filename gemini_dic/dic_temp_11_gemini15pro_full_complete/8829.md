antievolutionist:/ˌæntiˌɛvəˈluːʃənɪst/ | n. | 反进化论者 |  A person who opposes the theory of evolution, especially biological evolution by natural selection. |  The antievolutionist argued against the fossil record.  反进化论者反对化石记录。| synonyms: creationist, intelligent design proponent | antonyms: evolutionist, Darwinist | noun

antievolutionist:/ˌæntiˌɛvəˈluːʃənɪst/ | adj. | 反进化论的 |  Opposing or relating to the opposition of the theory of evolution. | The antievolutionist movement gained traction in the early 20th century. 反进化论运动在20世纪初获得了关注。 | synonyms: creationist, anti-Darwinian | antonyms: evolutionary, pro-Darwinian | adjective
