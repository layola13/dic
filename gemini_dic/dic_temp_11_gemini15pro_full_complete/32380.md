chiliarch:/ˈkɪliɑːrk/ | n. | 千夫长，指挥千人的军官 | A chiliarch was a commander of a thousand soldiers in ancient Greece and other armies. |  The chiliarch led his troops into battle. 千夫长率领他的部队投入战斗。

The chiliarch was responsible for the training and discipline of his men. 千夫长负责训练和纪律他的士兵。| 近义词：commander, captain | 反义词: soldier, private | 词性: 名词
Etymology: From Greek *χιλίαρχος (khiliarkhos) from χίλιοι (khilioi, “thousand”) + ἄρχω (arkhō, “to rule, command”).
