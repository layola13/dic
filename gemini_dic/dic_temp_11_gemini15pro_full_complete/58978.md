duteous:/ˈdjuːtiəs/ | adj. | 尽职的，忠诚的 | Performing one's duties faithfully and obediently; marked by a sense of duty. |  She was a duteous daughter, always caring for her aging parents. 她是一位孝顺的女儿，总是照顾她年迈的父母。

| Example: The duteous soldier followed every order without question.  尽职尽责的士兵毫无疑问地执行了每一条命令。 | Synonym: obedient, respectful, responsible, devoted | Antonym: disobedient, rebellious, irresponsible, negligent | word type: adjective
