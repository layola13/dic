laced:/leɪst/
adjective
饰以花边的；系带的
decorated with lace or laces; fastened with laces
Her dress was laced with delicate embroidery. 她的连衣裙饰有精致的刺绣。
The boots were laced tightly. 靴子系得很紧。

synonym:tied, fastened,trimmed
antonym:unlaced, untied, plain

Etymology: From Middle English *lasen*, from Old French *lacer*, from Late Latin *laqueare* "to ensnare, entangle," from Latin *laqueus* "noose, snare."
