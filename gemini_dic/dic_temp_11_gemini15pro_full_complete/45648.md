curst:/kɜːrst/ /kɝːst/| adj.|被诅咒的；可憎的；讨厌的|Suffering from a curse; under a curse; damnable.  Also used as a general term of abuse.|例句：The curst disease ravaged the land.  这该死的疾病蹂躏了这片土地。

例句：He muttered a curst oath under his breath. 他低声咒骂了一句。|近义词：cursed, damned, accursed, blighted, execrable|反义词：blessed, hallowed, sacred|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *cursed, *cursid, from Old English cursian (“to curse”), from Proto-Germanic *karzōną (“to lament, complain”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱers- (“to cry, scream”). Cognate with Dutch kerzen (“to lament”), German kerzen (“to groan, lament”), Old Norse korzna (“to groan, lament”). More at curse.
