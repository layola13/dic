distinct:/dɪˈstɪŋkt/
adjective|独特的，明显的，清晰的|Clearly different or separate.|The two flavors are distinct. 这两种味道截然不同。|近义词:different, separate, clear, obvious|反义词:similar, indistinct, vague|词性:adjective
distinct:/dɪˈstɪŋkt/
verb|区别，辨别|Recognize or treat (someone or something) as different.|He was able to distinctly recall the events of that day. 他能够清楚地回忆起那天发生的事情。|近义词:differentiate, distinguish|反义词:confuse|词性:verb
