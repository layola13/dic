heavens:/ˈhɛvənz/| n.|天，天空；天堂；神|Used to express surprise, annoyance, or other strong emotions, or as a euphemism for God.  It's often used in exclamations.|Good heavens, what a mess! 天哪，真是一团糟！|Synonyms: sky, paradise, firmament|Antonyms: hell, underworld|Noun

heavens:/ˈhɛvənz/| n.|天命，天意|Used to refer to fate or destiny.|The heavens have decreed it. 天意如此。|Synonyms: fate, destiny, providence|Antonyms: -|Noun
