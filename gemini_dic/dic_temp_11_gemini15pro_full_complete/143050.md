outblushed:/ˌaʊtˈblʌʃt/
verb
使（某人）羞愧得脸更红；胜过（某人）的羞愧
surpass someone in blushing; cause (someone) to blush more deeply than oneself or another.
She outblushed the shyest girl in the room when she was called up to accept the award. 当她被叫上台领奖时，她比房间里最害羞的女孩脸还要红。
近义词: shame, embarrass, humiliate
反义词: embolden, reassure
