limbed:/lɪmd/ | adj. | 有…肢的；有…臂的；有…腿的 | Having limbs of a specified kind or number. |  The four-limbed animal climbed the tree. 这只四肢动物爬上了树。|  The long-limbed dancer moved gracefully across the stage.  这位肢体修长的舞者优雅地穿过舞台。 | 近义词: legged, armed | 反义词: limbless | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Old English *limud, from lim "limb" + -ed.
