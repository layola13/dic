counter-arch:/ˈkaʊntərˌɑːrtʃ/ | n. |反拱，逆拱，对拱 |An arch built against another arch for support or reinforcement, or an inverted arch used as a relieving arch or in foundations.| The bridge employed a series of counter-arches to distribute the weight. 这座桥采用了一系列反拱来分散重量。|  The counter-arch prevented the wall from collapsing. 反拱防止了墙倒塌。| synonym: relieving arch, inverted arch | antonym: arch, vault | noun
