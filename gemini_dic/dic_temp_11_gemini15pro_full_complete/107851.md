lardiest:/ˈlɑːrdiɪst/| adj.|最肥胖的；最油腻的|superlative adjective of "lardy," meaning containing or resembling lard; excessively fat or fatty.|He was the lardiest pig in the entire pen. 他是整个猪圈里最肥的猪。|The lardiest gravy I've ever seen. 我见过最油腻的肉汁。| 近义词：fattest, plumpest, chubbiest, heaviest, obese, corpulent|反义词：thinnest, leanest, skinniest, slimmest|词性:形容词
