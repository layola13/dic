interknowledge: /ˌɪntərˈnɒlɪdʒ/ | n. |相互了解，共同认知| The state of shared understanding or awareness between individuals or groups.  It emphasizes the reciprocal nature of knowledge, where each party is aware of the other's understanding. |  Example 1:  Interknowledge between team members is crucial for effective collaboration. 团队成员之间的相互了解对于有效合作至关重要。

Example 2:  Developing interknowledge among different departments can improve organizational efficiency.  在不同部门之间发展共同认知可以提高组织效率。 | 近义词: mutual understanding, shared knowledge, common ground | 反义词:  misunderstanding, ignorance, lack of communication | 词性: 名词


词源: "Inter-" (prefix meaning "between, among, mutually") + "knowledge"
