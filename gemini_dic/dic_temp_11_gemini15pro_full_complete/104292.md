justifiably:/ˈdʒʌstɪfaɪəbli/
adverb
有正当理由地，无可非议地
in a way that can be justified; rightfully
He was justifiably proud of his achievements. 他有理由为自己的成就感到自豪。
Synonyms: rightfully, legitimately, validly, reasonably, properly
Antonyms: unjustly, unfairly, wrongly, unreasonably, improperly
词性: 副词
