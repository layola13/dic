outbulks:/ˌaʊtˈbʌlk/
verb
超过…的体积；在体积上胜过
to exceed in bulk; to be larger in volume than something else.
Example: The new warehouse outbulks the old one by a significant margin.  新仓库的体积比旧仓库大得多。

Example:  The cruise ship outbulks all the other vessels in the harbor. 这艘游轮比港口里的所有其他船只都要大。
Synonym: outweigh, dwarf, surpass
Antonym:  be smaller than, be dwarfed by
词性:v.
