arbitrators:/ˈɑːrbɪtreɪtərz/| n.|仲裁人，裁决人|A person or group chosen to judge and settle a dispute between other people or groups. |The arbitrators heard both sides of the story before making their decision. 仲裁员在做出决定之前听取了双方的陈述。|Example: The two companies chose a panel of arbitrators to resolve their contract dispute.  两家公司选择了一个仲裁小组来解决他们的合同纠纷。|Synonyms: judge, mediator, umpire, referee|Antonyms: litigant, disputant|Noun
