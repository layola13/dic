legroom: /ˈleɡruːm/ | n. | 腿部空间 | The amount of space available for your legs, especially when seated in a vehicle or aircraft. |  I booked an exit row seat for the extra legroom. 我订了一个靠近紧急出口的座位，这样腿部空间更大一些。

The car has plenty of legroom in the back. 这辆车的后座腿部空间很宽敞。


近义词：space, room
反义词: crampedness, tightness
词性: 名词
