ascriptitious:/ˌæskrɪpˈtɪʃəs/
adjective
归属的，归因于…的
of, relating to, or characterized by ascription :  attributed, assigned
例句：His success was ascriptitious to hard work and dedication. 他的成功归因于努力工作和奉献精神。

例句：The ascriptitious qualities of a leader include charisma and vision.  领导者的归属特质包括魅力和远见。
synonym: attributable, assigned, credited
antonym: inherent, intrinsic
词性: adjective
