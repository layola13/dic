following:/ˈfɒləʊɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈfɑːloʊɪŋ/ (US)| adj. n. prep.|接下来的，随后的；下列的；追随者；信徒|The following is a list of the participants. 下面是参与者名单。|近义词：subsequent, ensuing, next, succeeding|反义词：preceding, previous|adjective

following:/ˈfɒləʊɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈfɑːloʊɪŋ/ (US)| n.|追随者；信徒|He has a large following on social media. 他在社交媒体上有很多粉丝。|近义词：supporters, adherents, fans|反义词：opponents, detractors|noun

following:/ˈfɒləʊɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈfɑːloʊɪŋ/ (US)| prep.|在...之后，接着|Following the meeting, we went for lunch. 会议结束后，我们去吃午饭了。|近义词：after, subsequent to|反义词：before, prior to|preposition

词源: From Middle English *folewend, from Old English folgend, present participle of folgian (“to follow”).
