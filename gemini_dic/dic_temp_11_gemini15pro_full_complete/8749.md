antidilutive:/ˌænti daɪˈluːtɪv/
adjective
反稀释的
Reducing or counteracting dilution, especially of earnings per share.  Antidilutive securities are those that would increase earnings per share if exercised or converted.
The company chose not to issue the convertible bonds because they were deemed antidilutive in the current market conditions. 公司选择不发行可转换债券，因为它们在当前市场条件下被认为是反稀释的。
The warrants are currently antidilutive and therefore not included in the diluted EPS calculation. 这些认股权证目前是反稀释的，因此不包括在稀释每股收益计算中。
近义词: accretive
反义词: dilutive
词性: adjective
