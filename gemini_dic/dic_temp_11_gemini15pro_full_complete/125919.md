museum:/mjuːˈziːəm/ | n. | 博物馆 | A building in which objects of historical, scientific, artistic, or cultural interest are stored and exhibited. |  I visited the natural history museum last weekend. 我上周末参观了自然历史博物馆。

The museum has a fascinating collection of ancient artifacts.  这家博物馆收藏了令人着迷的古代文物。


| 近义词: gallery, exhibition, collection | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "private collection") | 词性: noun
