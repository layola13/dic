grogginesses:/ˈɡrɒɡɪnəsɪz/ | n. | 昏沉，醉态，迷糊 | The state of being groggy; drowsiness or dizziness, often caused by alcohol or lack of sleep. |  After the long flight, I was suffering from severe grogginesses. 长途飞行后，我感到非常昏沉。| Near synonyms: drowsiness, dizziness, sleepiness, lethargy, stupor | Antonyms: alertness, wakefulness, vigilance | noun

groggy:/ˈɡrɒɡi/ | adj. | 昏沉的，醉态的，迷糊的 | Dazed, weak, or unsteady, especially from lack of sleep or drunkenness. | I felt groggy after the sleeping pills. 我吃了安眠药后感到昏昏沉沉。| Near synonyms: dazed, sleepy, drowsy, unsteady, wobbly | Antonyms: alert, awake, clear-headed | adjective
