coproprietor:/ˌkoʊprəˈpraɪətər/ | n. |共同所有人，共同业主|A coproprietor is a person who owns something jointly with one or more other people.| Mary and John are coproprietors of the bakery. 玛丽和约翰是这家面包店的共同所有人。|
Example:  The coproprietors decided to sell the building. 共同业主决定出售这栋楼。|
Example: As coproprietors, they share the profits and responsibilities equally.  作为共同所有人，他们平等地分担利润和责任。|
Synonyms: joint owner, co-owner | Antonyms: sole proprietor, sole owner | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From co- + proprietor.  "Co-" is a prefix meaning "jointly," "together," or "mutually." "Proprietor" comes from the Latin word "proprietarius," meaning "owner."
