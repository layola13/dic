enjoying:/ɪnˈdʒɔɪɪŋ/ | v. | 享受; 喜爱; 享有 | Present participle of "enjoy".  Experiencing pleasure or satisfaction from something. |  I am enjoying the sunshine. 我正在享受阳光。|  She is enjoying her new job. 她很享受她的新工作。 | synonyms: relishing, savoring, appreciating, liking | antonyms: disliking, hating, loathing | verb
