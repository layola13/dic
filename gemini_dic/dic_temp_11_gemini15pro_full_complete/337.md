abhorrences:/əˈbɔːrənsɪz/
|n.|憎恶，厌恶之物|Things that are abhorred; feelings of strong dislike or disgust.|He spoke of his abhorrence of violence. 他表达了他对暴力的憎恶。|近义词：hatred, loathing, detestation, aversion|反义词：love, affection, admiration|词性:名词
词源: Late Middle English: from Old French abhorrence, from Latin abhorrentia, from abhorrere ‘shrink from’.
