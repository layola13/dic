gummite:/ˈɡʌmaɪt/| n. | 水胶铀矿 | An amorphous, brownish-yellow to orange-red alteration product of uraninite, a uranium oxide mineral. It is usually found in association with uraninite and other uranium minerals. | 例句：The gummite formed a coating on the uraninite. 水胶铀矿在晶质铀矿上形成了一层涂层。

例句：The geologist identified the yellow mineral as gummite. 地质学家将这种黄色矿物鉴定为水胶铀矿。| 近义词：uranophane, uraninite (in a broad sense, as it's a derivative) | 反义词: N/A (lacks a direct opposite) | 词性: noun
