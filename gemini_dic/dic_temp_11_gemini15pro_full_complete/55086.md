disrespect:/ˌdɪsrɪˈspɛkt/ | n. vt. |不尊重，失礼； بی‌احترامی; |Lack of respect; rudeness. |例句：She showed disrespect to her elders. 她对长辈表示不尊重。|近义词：disregard, contempt, scorn, insolence, impertinence |反义词: respect, reverence, honor, esteem |词性:noun, verb

disrespect:/ˌdɪsrɪˈspɛkt/ | vt. |不尊重， بی‌احترامی کردن; |To treat without respect. |例句：Don't disrespect your teachers. 不要不尊重你的老师。|近义词：insult, slight, offend, dishonor |反义词: respect, honor, esteem |词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation) + respect.  Ultimately from Latin respectus "regard, consideration," past participle of respicere "look back at, regard," from re- "back" + specere "to look."
