adjacency:/əˈdʒeɪsənsi/| n.|邻接，毗邻|The adjacency of the two countries has led to close cultural exchange. 这两个国家的邻接导致了密切的文化交流。|例句：The adjacency of the two properties made it convenient to combine them.  这两个地产的毗邻使得合并它们变得很方便。

例句：The software analyzes the adjacency of network nodes. 这个软件分析网络节点的邻接关系。


近义词：proximity, closeness, nearness|反义词:distance, remoteness|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *adjacent-ia, from Latin adjacent-, present participle stem of adjacēre "to lie near," from ad- "to" + jacēre "to lie."
