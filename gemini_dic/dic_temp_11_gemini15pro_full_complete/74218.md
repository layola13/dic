fourths:/fɔːrθs/| n.|四分之几；四分之几的人（或物）|A fraction representing three out of four equal parts. It can also refer to a group of things divided into four equal parts, and you are talking about more than one of these parts. |She ate three fourths of the pizza. 她吃了四分之三个披萨。|Near Synonyms:quarters|Antonyms: wholes|noun
