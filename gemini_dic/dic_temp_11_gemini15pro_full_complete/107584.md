landreeve: /ˈlændriːv/ | n. | 地主, 土地管家 | A person who acted as a steward of a manor or estate. | The landreeve collected rents and managed the farmlands. 土地管家收取租金并管理农田。| The landreeve oversaw the daily operations of the estate. 土地管家监督庄园的日常运作。 | Synonyms: steward, bailiff, overseer | Antonyms: tenant, serf | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *landrēfa, from land "land" + rēfa "reeve". A reeve was a high-ranking official in Anglo-Saxon England.
