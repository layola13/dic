nominees:/ˌnɑːmɪˈniːz/| n. |被提名者；候选人|A person who is nominated for an award, office, or position. |例句：The nominees for the Oscar awards were announced. 奥斯卡奖的提名者已经公布。

例句：All the nominees for best actress wore stunning dresses. 所有最佳女主角的提名人都穿着惊艳的礼服。|近义词：candidates, contenders, applicants|反义词：non-nominees, losers|词性:noun
