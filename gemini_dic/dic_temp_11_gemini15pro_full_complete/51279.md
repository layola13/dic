desyncs:/ˌdiːˈsɪŋks/ | n. |不同步，失步|A state where two or more things are no longer synchronized or in agreement, often in the context of computing, gaming, or video. It refers to a loss of synchronization. | The desyncs in the online game made it unplayable.  网络游戏中的不同步使其无法进行。| Near synonyms: discrepancies, inconsistencies, mismatches, asynchronies | Antonyms: synchronizations, harmonies, agreements | Noun

desync:/ˌdiːˈsɪŋk/ | v. |失去同步，不同步|To become out of synchronization; to lose synchronization. | The audio and video desynced during the livestream. 直播期间音频和视频不同步了。| Near synonyms:  fall out of sync, lose synchronization | Antonyms: synchronize, harmonize | Verb
