cameoing:/ˈkæmioʊɪŋ/
v.
客串演出;  短暂露面
Making a brief appearance, especially as a character in a film or play.
Brad Pitt cameoed in the latest Deadpool movie. 布拉德·皮特在最新的死侍电影中客串演出。
He is cameoing as a police officer in the new TV series. 他在新电视剧中客串出演一名警察。
appear, guest star
disappear, vanish
verb
