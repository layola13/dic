dysuria:/dɪsˈjʊəriə/
n.
排尿困难，尿痛
Painful or difficult urination.
Example: She experienced dysuria and frequent urination, indicating a possible urinary tract infection. 她感到排尿困难和尿频，表明可能患有尿路感染。

Example: The patient reported dysuria as his primary complaint. 病人自述排尿困难是他的主要症状。

近义词:strangury,  painful urination
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "normal urination" or "asymptomatic urination")
