overdubbed:/ˌoʊvərˈdʌbd/| adj.| 译配的，配音的|To record new audio over existing audio, typically in a film or video.|The dialogue in the foreign film was overdubbed in English. 这部外国电影的对话被译配成了英语。|Synonyms: dubbed, re-recorded|Antonyms: original soundtrack, live sound|Adjective


overdub:/ˌoʊvərˈdʌb/| v.| 译配，配音|To record new audio over existing audio, typically in a film or video.|They overdubbed the actor's lines because the original recording was poor. 他们重新配音了演员的台词，因为原来的录音质量不好。|Synonyms: dub, re-record|Antonyms: record live, original recording|Verb
