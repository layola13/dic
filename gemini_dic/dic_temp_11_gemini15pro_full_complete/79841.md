glyphosate: /ˈɡlaɪfəˌseɪt/ | n. | 草甘膦 | a broad-spectrum systemic herbicide and crop desiccant. It is an organophosphorus compound, specifically a phosphonate, which acts by inhibiting the plant enzyme 5-enolpyruvylshikimate-3-phosphate synthase. | Glyphosate is the most widely used herbicide in the world.  草甘膦是世界上使用最广泛的除草剂。 | Synonyms: Roundup (brand name), N-(phosphonomethyl)glycine | Antonyms:  None (as a specific chemical compound)  | Noun
