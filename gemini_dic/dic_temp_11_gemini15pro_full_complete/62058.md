encasement:/ɪnˈkeɪsmənt/| n. |包装，封装，外壳|The encasement of the artifact protected it from damage.  文物的包装保护它免受损坏。|The diamond was held in a gold encasement. 钻石被镶嵌在一个金色的外壳里。|近义词：casing, covering, enclosure, sheath, jacket|反义词：exposure, uncovering|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From encase + -ment.  Encase comes from en- (meaning "in" or "into") + case (meaning "a container").  The suffix -ment forms nouns from verbs, indicating the result or product of an action.
