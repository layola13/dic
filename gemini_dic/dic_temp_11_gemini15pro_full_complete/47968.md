decigram: /ˈdesɪɡræm/ | n. |分克，十分之一克 | A unit of mass equal to one tenth of a gram. |  She measured out a decigram of the powder for the experiment. 她称量了十分之一克的粉末用于实验。

| Example: The chemist carefully measured one decigram of the substance.  化学家小心地测量了该物质的十分之一克。
|Synonym: 0.1 gram | Antonym: dekagram (10 grams) | Noun
