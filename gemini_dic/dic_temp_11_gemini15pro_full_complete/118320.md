merest:/ˈmɪərɪst/| adj.|最小的，丝毫的，纯粹的|Smallest or slightest; barest.|The merest hint of a smile crossed her face. 她脸上掠过一丝微笑。|近义词：slightest, smallest, barest, least|反义词:greatest, largest, most|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *merest, superlative of mere (“pure, unmixed; sheer, utter; absolute, unqualified”), from Old French mer (“pure, unadulterated; sheer, simple”), from Latin merus (“pure, unmixed, undiluted”).
