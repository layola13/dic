blearinesses: /ˈblɪərinɪsɪz/
n.
模糊不清, 模糊状态(复数)
The state of being bleary or blurred.  A condition of blurred vision.
The blearinesses of the old photographs made it difficult to identify the people in them.  这些老照片的模糊不清让人很难辨认出其中的人物。
近义词: blurriness, haziness, dimness, indistinctness
反义词: clarity, sharpness, distinctness
词性: 名词

词源:
From bleary + -ness + -es (plural).  Bleary itself comes from Middle English *blerie, from bleren "to make bleary," probably of imitative origin.
