anti-poetical:/ˌænti-pəˈetɪkəl/
adjective
反诗意的；缺乏诗意的
lacking poetic beauty or qualities; unpoetic.
His clunky prose was remarkably anti-poetical. 他的笨拙的散文明显缺乏诗意。
The stark realism of the scene was almost anti-poetical.  这场景的赤裸裸的现实主义几乎是反诗意的。
近义词:prosaic, unpoetic, mundane, unimaginative
反义词:poetic, lyrical, romantic, imaginative
词性:adjective
