flintwood:/ˈflɪntˌwʊd/| n. |燧石木，坚硬的木头|A hard, dense wood, figuratively suggesting strength and resilience.  It's not a common word in modern usage and is often found in literary or poetic contexts.|The old warrior had a heart of flintwood, unyielding to fear.  这位老战士有一颗燧石般的心，不屈于恐惧。|近义词：ironwood, hardwood|反义词:softwood|词性:noun
Etymology:  Compound of "flint" (a hard stone) and "wood."  The word evokes the hardness and durability of flint applied to wood.
