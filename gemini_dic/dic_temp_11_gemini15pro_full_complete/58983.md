dutifully:/ˈdjuːtɪfəli/
adverb
尽职地，忠实地
In a conscientious and obedient manner, fulfilling one's obligations.
She dutifully performed her chores every day. 她每天尽职地做家务。
近义词:obediently, respectfully, conscientiously, diligently, faithfully
反义词:negligently, irresponsibly, carelessly, rebelliously
词性:adverb

dutifully:/ˈdjuːtɪfəli/
词源:From duty + -fully.  "Duty" comes from Old French "deu, duit" meaning "what is due, owed," ultimately from Latin "debitum" "something owed." "-fully" is a suffix meaning "in a specified manner," from Old English "-fullice."
用法说明:Dutifully describes actions performed out of a sense of obligation or responsibility, often with a connotation of respect or obedience. It implies a careful and thorough approach to fulfilling one's duties.
