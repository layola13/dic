ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bi time, from Old English *bī tīman (“by time, at the right time”), equivalent to by +‎ time. Cognate with Scots betime (“betimes, early”), West Frisian bitiid (“in time”), Dutch bijtijds (“in time, early”), German beizeiten (“in good time, early”).
