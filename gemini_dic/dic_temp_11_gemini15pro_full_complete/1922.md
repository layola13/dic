actinogram: /ˈæktɪnəɡræm/
noun
光化记录; 光能记录图
A record produced by an actinograph, showing the intensity of sunlight or other radiation received.
The actinogram showed a significant increase in solar radiation during the summer months. 光化记录图显示夏季太阳辐射显著增加。

Etymology:  From actino- + -gram.  Actino- comes from Greek *aktīn-, *aktînos "ray." -gram comes from Greek *gramma "something written or drawn."

Synonyms:  actinograph record,  solar radiation record
Antonyms:  N/A
词性: noun
