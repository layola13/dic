delimiter:/dɪˈlɪmɪtər/| n.|分隔符，定界符|A character or string of characters used to separate or mark the beginning or end of a unit of data, such as a word, field, or record.|The comma is a common delimiter in CSV files. 逗号是CSV文件中常用的分隔符。|separator, divider|connector, uniter|noun
