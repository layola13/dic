headlet:/ˈhɛdlɪt/ | n. | 小标题; 短标题; 副标题 | A small or subordinate heading. | The article used several headlets to break up the text.  这篇文章使用了几个小标题来划分文本。 | Example: The headlets made the document easier to navigate.  小标题使文档更容易浏览。 | synonym: subheading, subtitle, caption | antonym: main heading, title | noun
