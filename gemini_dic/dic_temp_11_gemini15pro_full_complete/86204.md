heathenishly:/ˈhiːðənɪʃli/
adv.
异教地；野蛮地；粗鲁地
In a heathenish manner; like a heathen.  Acting in a way considered uncivilized or irreligious.
He swore heathenishly at the broken machine. 他对着坏掉的机器破口大骂，像个野蛮人。
Synonyms: barbarously, savagely, crudely, uncivilizedly, irreligiously
Antonyms: piously, devoutly, reverently, respectfully

ETYMOLOGY->
From heathen + -ishly.  Heathen (n.) comes from Old English *hæðen "not Christian or Jewish," literally "dwelling on the heath," from hæð "heath."
