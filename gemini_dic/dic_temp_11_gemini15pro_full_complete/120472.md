minsky:/ˈmɪnski/| n.|明斯基 (人名，通常指 Hyman Minsky)|Hyman Minsky, an American economist, is best known for his work on financial instability.|Hyman Minsky was an influential economist. 海曼·明斯基是一位有影响力的经济学家.|Synonym: economist, scholar|Antonym: N/A|Noun

minsky moment:/ˈmɪnski ˈmoʊmənt/| n.|明斯基时刻|A Minsky moment is a sudden major collapse of asset values which is part of the credit cycle or business cycle.  It is based on the hypothesis that periods of high speculation and leveraged asset purchases end in a dramatic price reversal.|The subprime mortgage crisis of 2008 was considered by some to be a Minsky moment. 2008年的次贷危机被一些人认为是明斯基时刻.|Synonym: crash, collapse, crisis|Antonym: boom, recovery|Noun
