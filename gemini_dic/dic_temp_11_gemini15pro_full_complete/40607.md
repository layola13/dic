convenable:/kənˈviːnəbl/ /kənˈvenəbl/| adj.|合适的，方便的，适宜的|Suitable, convenient, or agreeable; fitting.|The meeting was held at a convenable time for everyone. 会议在一个对每个人都合适的时间举行。|近义词：suitable, appropriate, fitting, convenient, apt, opportune|反义词：inconvenient, inappropriate, unsuitable, unfit|词性:形容词
词源: From Old French convenable, from convenir “to agree, be suitable,” from Latin convenīre “to come together, assemble, agree," from com- “together” + venīre “to come.”
