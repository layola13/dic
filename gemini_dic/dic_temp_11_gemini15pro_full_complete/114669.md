maliciously: /məˈlɪʃəsli/ | adv. | 恶意地，怀有恶意地 |  in a way that shows a desire to harm somebody or cause them suffering |  He was maliciously accused of stealing. 他被恶意指控偷窃。 |  spitefully, viciously, malevolently | kindly, benevolently | adverb

Etymology->
From Middle English *maliciusli, from Old French malicieusement, from malicieus + -ment. Equivalent to malicious +‎ -ly.
