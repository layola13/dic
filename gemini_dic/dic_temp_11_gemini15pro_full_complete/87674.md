hexagrams:/ˈhɛksəˌɡræmz/
n.
六爻卦，卦象
A figure formed of six lines, some whole and some broken, used in divination based on the I Ching.
The I Ching uses 64 hexagrams to offer wisdom and guidance.  易经用64个卦象来提供智慧和指导。

The ancient book explained the meaning of all 64 hexagrams. 这本古书解释了所有64个卦象的含义。

Near Synonyms:  symbols, figures, emblems, signs, diagrams
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but one could consider "non-symbols" or "meaningless figures")
noun
