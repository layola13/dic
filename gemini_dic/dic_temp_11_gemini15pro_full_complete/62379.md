endoblast:/ˈɛndəˌblæst/| n.|内胚层，内胚叶|The endoblast is the innermost layer of the germ layers of an embryo. 内胚层是胚胎胚层的内层。|The endoblast develops into the digestive tract and associated organs. 内胚层发育成消化道和相关器官。|近义词：hypoblast, entoderm|反义词: ectoderm (外胚层), mesoderm (中胚层)|noun
