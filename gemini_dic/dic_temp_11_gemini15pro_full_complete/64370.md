erect:/ɪˈrɛkt/| adj. v. | 竖立的，直立的；建造，建立 |  Upright in posture or position.  Construct (something) by putting parts or material together. | The soldiers erected a barricade. 士兵们筑起了一道路障。 | 近义词: build, construct, raise, establish | 反义词: demolish, dismantle, destroy |词性:形容词, 动词

erected:/ɪˈrɛktɪd/| adj.| 竖立的，建造的 | Having been built or established.  |  The newly erected building gleamed in the sunlight.  新建的大楼在阳光下闪闪发光。 | 近义词: built, constructed, established | 反义词: demolished, dismantled, destroyed | 词性:形容词


词源: From Latin *ērigere, from ē- (out) + regere (to direct, rule).
