brothers:/ˈbrʌðərz/| n.|兄弟；同胞；教友|Male siblings; people who share a common bond, especially in a religious or social context.|My two brothers are both older than me. 我的两个兄弟都比我大。|synonyms: siblings, brethren, comrades, fellows|antonyms:  sisters, enemies|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *brōþor, from Proto-Germanic *brōþēr, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰréh₂tēr.  Cognates include Dutch broeder, German Bruder, Latin frater, and Sanskrit bhrātā.
