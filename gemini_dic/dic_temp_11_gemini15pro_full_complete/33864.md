circumscribe:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈskraɪb/ | v. |限制，约束；画…的外接圆|To draw a line around; to encircle; to restrict something within limits.|The king's power was circumscribed by the constitution. 国王的权力受宪法限制。|The circle circumscribes the square.  这个圆是这个正方形的外接圆。|Synonyms: restrict, confine, limit, restrain, bound, delimit|Antonyms: expand, broaden, enlarge, extend|verb

circumscribed:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈskraɪbd/ | adj. |受限制的，被约束的|Restricted within limits.|His life was circumscribed by poverty and illness. 他的生活受到贫困和疾病的限制。|Her social circle was very circumscribed. 她的社交圈非常狭窄。|Synonyms: limited, restricted, confined, narrow|Antonyms: unlimited, unrestricted, broad, wide|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin  "circum-" (around) + "scribere" (to write or draw).
