intercede:/ˌɪntərˈsiːd/| v. |调解，斡旋，求情|To plead on behalf of someone else; to act as a mediator.|例句：She interceded with the teacher to get her son a better grade. 她向老师求情，希望儿子能得到更好的成绩。

例句：The lawyer interceded for his client. 律师为他的客户求情。

近义词：mediate, intervene, plead, negotiate|反义词:ignore, disregard|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *intercedere*, from *inter-* (between) + *cedere* (to go).  Meaning "to go between" or "to intervene."
