overflutter:/ˌoʊvərˈflʌtər/
verb
过度飘动，过度振动
To flutter excessively or too much.
The butterfly's wings overfluttered in the strong wind.  蝴蝶的翅膀在强风中过度飘动。
The flag overfluttered, making a loud snapping sound. 旗帜过度飘动，发出很大的啪啪声。
近义词: flutter rapidly, flap excessively
反义词: be still, lie flat
词性: verb
