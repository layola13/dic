driftage:/ˈdrɪftɪdʒ/
n.
漂流物，漂流距离，漂移量，(尤指船舶的)漂移
The driftage was mostly plastic bottles and fishing nets. 漂流物主要是塑料瓶和渔网。
The driftage of the boat was about 10 miles. 船的漂移距离大约是10英里。
The storm caused a large amount of driftage along the coast.  暴风雨导致沿岸出现大量漂流物。

近义词: drift, drifting, floatage
反义词: anchorage, mooring

词性: noun
