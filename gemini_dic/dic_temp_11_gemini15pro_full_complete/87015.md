词源：
From Middle English *hennes, plural of *henne, from Old English henn (“hen”), from Proto-Germanic *hannō (“hen”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kʷen-  (“female bird, hen”). Cognate with Dutch hen (“hen”), German Henne (“hen”), Danish høne (“hen”). Related also to Latin canō (“sing”).
