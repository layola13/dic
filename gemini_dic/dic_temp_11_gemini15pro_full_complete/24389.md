brinjaree:/ˌbrɪndʒəˈriː/| n.|驮运货物; 驮运货物的牛队; (旧时印度的)武装商队|A train of pack animals, especially oxen, used for transporting goods; a company of merchants traveling with such a train, especially in former times in India.|The brinjaree traveled slowly across the plains, their oxen laden with goods.|近义词：caravan, convoy, train|反义词: None|词性:noun

词源: From Hindi binjāri, from Sanskrit vanij- 'merchant'.
