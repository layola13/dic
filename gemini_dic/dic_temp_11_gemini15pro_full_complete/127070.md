natatores:/nəˈteɪtɔːriːz/
| n. |游禽；游泳鸟类|A group of birds characterized by their webbed feet and adaptations for swimming and diving, including ducks, geese, swans, and other waterfowl.|Ducks, geese, and swans are all members of the order Natatores. 鸭子、鹅和天鹅都是游禽目.|近义词：waterfowl, swimming birds|反义词:|n.
