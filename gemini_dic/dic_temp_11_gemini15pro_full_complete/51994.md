diagnoses:/ˌdaɪəɡˈnoʊsiːz/ | n. | 诊断（复数） | The plural form of "diagnosis," referring to multiple instances of identifying a disease or condition. | The doctor reviewed the patient's test results and offered three possible diagnoses. 医生查看了病人的检测结果，并提出了三种可能的诊断。|  The diagnoses ranged from a simple infection to a more serious autoimmune disorder. 诊断范围从简单的感染到更严重的自身免疫性疾病。 | synonyms: assessments, evaluations, determinations | antonyms: uncertainties, ambiguities | noun
