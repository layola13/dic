cowing:/ˈkaʊɪŋ/| adj. |恐吓的，使人畏缩的|Causing someone to feel intimidated or frightened.|The cowing presence of the dictator silenced the crowd. 独裁者令人畏惧的出现使人群鸦雀无声。|近义词：intimidating, frightening, terrifying, daunting, menacing|反义词：emboldening, encouraging, reassuring|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From the verb "cow," meaning to intimidate or frighten.  The verb "cow" comes from Old Norse *kúga* "to oppress, subdue," related to *kví* "pen for animals," from Proto-Germanic *kwi-janan* "to torment, pinch, squeeze," from PIE root *gweyə-* "to live."
