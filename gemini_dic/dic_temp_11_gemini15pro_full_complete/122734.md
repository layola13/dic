moineau:/mwano/ (French)| n. |麻雀|A small Old World bird related to the finch, typically with brown and grey plumage.| J'ai vu un moineau sur le balcon. (I saw a sparrow on the balcony.) | 近义词: sparrow | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From Old French *moinel, diminutive of moine (“monk”), because of the bird's brownish plumage resembling a monk's habit.
