blackshirts:/ˈblækʃɜːrts/| n.| 黑衫党（意大利法西斯党员），黑衫队员|A member of the Italian Fascist party, so called because of their uniform.|Mussolini's blackshirts marched on Rome.  墨索里尼的黑衫军向罗马进军。|Synonyms: fascists, Nazis|Antonyms: democrats, liberals|Noun
blackshirts:/ˈblækʃɜːrts/| n.| 黑衫（指制服）|A black shirt, especially as part of a uniform.|The blackshirts were a symbol of fascism. 黑衫是法西斯主义的象征。|Synonyms: uniform, attire|Antonyms: civilian clothes|Noun
