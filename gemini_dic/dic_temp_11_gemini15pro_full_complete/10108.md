apogeal:/ˌæpəˈdʒiːəl/
adjective
最高点的；顶峰的；极盛的
Relating to or denoting the highest point in the development of something; culminating.
The apogeal moment of her career was winning the Olympic gold medal. 她职业生涯的巅峰时刻是赢得奥运金牌。
The empire reached its apogeal phase during the 18th century.  这个帝国在18世纪达到了鼎盛时期。

近义词:culminating, climactic, peak, highest, topmost, zenithal
反义词:nadiral, lowest, bottommost

词性:adjective

词源:From French *apogé*, from Greek *apogaion*, neuter of *apogaios* ‘away from earth’, from *apo* ‘from’ + *gē* ‘earth’.
