gigos:/ˈɡiːɡɒz/ /ˈɡiːɡoʊz/ |n.|垃圾进，垃圾出 (Garbage In, Garbage Out)|GIGO is a concept in computer science that emphasizes the importance of input quality.  垃圾进，垃圾出是计算机科学中的一个概念，强调输入质量的重要性。|The results of the simulation were nonsensical, a clear case of gigos.  模拟的结果毫无意义，这是一个明显的垃圾进，垃圾出的例子。|Synonyms:  rubbish in, rubbish out; bad data in, bad data out|Antonyms: quality in, quality out|noun
