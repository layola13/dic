excoriable:/ɛksˈkɔːriəbl/
adjective
可剥落的，可擦伤的
Capable of being excoriated; susceptible to excoriation.  This means that the skin or surface can be easily abraded, scratched, or worn off.
The patient presented with excoriable skin lesions. 病人的皮肤损伤可剥落。

The baby's delicate skin is excoriable. 婴儿娇嫩的皮肤容易擦伤。

Synonym:abrasive, susceptible, delicate, tender, sensitive
Antonym:resilient, tough, durable, resistant
