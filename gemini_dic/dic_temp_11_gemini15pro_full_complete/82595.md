guardians:/ˈɡɑːrdiənz/| n.|监护人，守护者|A person who guards or protects something.  Those responsible for the care of a person, especially a minor or someone incapable of managing their own affairs. |The child's guardians consented to the medical treatment.  孩子的监护人同意进行医疗。|Synonyms: protector, custodian, keeper, warden|Antonyms: ward, dependent|noun
