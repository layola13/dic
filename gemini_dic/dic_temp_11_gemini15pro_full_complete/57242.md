downiness:/ˈdaʊninəs/| n.|绒毛；柔软；轻柔|The downiness of the feathers kept the bird warm.  羽毛的绒毛使鸟儿保持温暖。|例句：The peach had a delightful downiness to its skin.  这个桃子的表皮有着令人愉悦的绒毛感。

The blanket's downiness made it incredibly cozy. 这条毯子的柔软使其非常舒适。|近义词：softness, fluffiness, fuzziness|反义词: roughness, hardness|词性:名词

Etymology: From *downy* + *-ness*. *Downy* comes from *down* (the soft underfeathers of birds) + *-y*.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *dūną* (down).
