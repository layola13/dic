archus:/ˈɑːrkəs/| n. |首领，统治者|A person who rules or leads; a chief or ruler.  It's often used in biological classifications, particularly in entomology (the study of insects).| The archus of the ant colony directed the workers.  蚂蚁群的首领指挥着工蚁。|近义词：leader, ruler, chief, master, sovereign|反义词:follower, subordinate|noun
