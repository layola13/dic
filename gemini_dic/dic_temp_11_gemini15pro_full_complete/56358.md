doil:/dɔɪl/| n.|一小块；少量；少许|(Scottish) A small portion or amount of something.|He gave me a doil of bread.|近义词：bit, morsel, scrap, smidgen|反义词:lot, abundance|词性:名词

词源：
Likely a variant of "dole," from Old English *dāl, meaning "portion," related to German "Teil" and Dutch "deel."
