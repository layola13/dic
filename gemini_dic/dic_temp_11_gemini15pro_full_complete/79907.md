gnawingly:/ˈnɔːɪŋli/| adv.|持续地、折磨人地、令人不安地|In a way that causes persistent worry or anxiety.  It suggests a feeling that eats away at you slowly and relentlessly.|The thought of failing gnawed at him gnawingly.  这种失败的想法一直折磨着他。|Synonyms: persistently, incessantly, relentlessly, constantly, naggingly|Antonyms: soothingly, calmingly, reassuringly|词性:副词

gnawingly:/ˈnɔːɪŋli/| adv.|持续地、折磨人地、令人不安地|In a way that suggests a persistent, irritating feeling or thought that is difficult to ignore.|A gnawingly familiar feeling of anxiety crept over her. 一种挥之不去的熟悉焦虑感蔓延了她全身。|Synonyms: naggingly, persistently, troublingly,  tormentingly|Antonyms: comfortingly, reassuringly, pleasantly|词性:副词
