narcotism: /ˈnɑːrkətɪzəm/ | n. | 麻醉状态; 毒瘾; | A state of stupor, drowsiness, or unconsciousness produced by a narcotic drug.  The condition of being addicted to narcotic drugs. | He was in a state of narcotism after the surgery.  他手术后处于麻醉状态。|  She struggled with narcotism for many years. 她与毒瘾斗争了很多年。 | 近义词: drug addiction, stupor, narcosis | 反义词: sobriety, alertness | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French *narcotisme*, from Greek *narkōtikos* (narcotic) + *-ismos* (-ism).
