morian: /ˈmɔːriən/ | n. | 摩尔人，深色皮肤的人 | A dark-skinned person, especially of North African descent, historically associated with the Moors. |  In medieval romances, the morian was often depicted as a noble warrior. 在中世纪的浪漫故事中，摩尔人经常被描绘成高贵的战士。

| Example: The morian knight rode a magnificent black steed.  摩尔骑士骑着一匹壮丽的黑马。| Synonym: Moor, dark-skinned person, person of color | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, potentially "fair-skinned person," but this can be problematic) | Noun
