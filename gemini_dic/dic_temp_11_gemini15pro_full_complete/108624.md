leadership: /ˈliːdəʃɪp/ (US) /ˈliːdəʃɪp/ (UK)| n.|领导力，领导才能；领导阶层|The quality of being a leader; the ability to guide, direct, or influence people or organizations. |例句：Her leadership skills are highly valued in the company. 她的领导才能在公司受到高度重视。

例句：The company thrived under his strong leadership. 在他强有力的领导下，公司蓬勃发展。

近义词：guidance, direction, management, authority|反义词:subservience, followership|词性:noun
