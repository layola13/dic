liefest:/ˈliːfɪst/
adjective
最亲爱的，最心爱的
Dearest; most beloved.
She sent the letter to her liefest friend. 她把信寄给了她最亲爱的朋友。
近义词: dearest, beloved, cherished, precious
反义词: hated, detested, despised
词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English *leveste, *leofeste, from Old English lēofost, superlative of lēof (“dear, beloved”). Cognate with Dutch liefst, German liebst.
