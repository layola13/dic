devoted:/dɪˈvoʊtɪd/| adj.|挚爱的；忠诚的；专心的|Dedicated to a cause, activity, or person; characterized by loyalty and dedication. |She is a devoted wife and mother. 她是一位贤妻良母。|He is devoted to his work. 他对他的工作很投入。|近义词：dedicated, committed, loyal, faithful|反义词：unfaithful, disloyal, indifferent|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: past participle of devote, from Old French devoter, from Latin devovere ‘vow, dedicate,’ from de- ‘down, away’ + vovere ‘vow.’
