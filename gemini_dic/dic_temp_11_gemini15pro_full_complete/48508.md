deerhound:/ˈdɪərhaʊnd/ | n. |猎鹿犬|A tall, rough-coated breed of sighthound, similar in shape to a greyhound but larger and more powerfully built, originally bred for hunting deer.|例句：The deerhound gracefully pursued the stag across the open moor.  猎鹿犬优雅地在开阔的荒野上追逐雄鹿。

例句：Her deerhound, with its shaggy coat, was a gentle giant.  她的猎鹿犬有着蓬松的毛，是个温柔的巨人。|近义词：Scottish deerhound, staghound |反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific breed, perhaps "lapdog" for a contrasting size/purpose) |词性:noun
