gratis: /ˈɡrætɪs/ /ˈɡreɪtɪs/
adjective
免费的
without charge; free.
The drinks were gratis. 饮料是免费的。
Food and lodging were provided gratis. 食宿是免费提供的。

近义词: free, complimentary, gratuitous
反义词: paid, charged

词性: adjective
词源: From Latin grātīs (“for thanks, gratuitously, freely”).
