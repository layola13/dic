blurrier:/ˈblɜːriər/ (US), /ˈblɜːrɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更模糊的，更不清楚的|Comparative form of blurry: more blurry.  Something that is blurrier is less clear or distinct than something else that is already blurry.|The photo became blurrier the more I zoomed in. 照片我越放大越模糊。|近义词：hazier, fuzzier, less distinct, less clear|反义词：sharper, clearer, more distinct|词性: adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From blurry + -er (comparative suffix).  Blurry itself comes from blur (verb) + -y (adjective suffix).  Blur ultimately derives from the Middle English word *blure*, probably of imitative origin.
