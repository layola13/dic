kerite:/ˈkɪəraɪt/| n.|凯利特（一种绝缘材料）|A rubber-like insulating material formerly used for coating electrical wires and cables, made from oxidized vegetable oils, natural rubber, and sulfur.|The old telegraph cables were insulated with kerite.|近义词:gutta-percha, insulation|反义词:conductor|noun
