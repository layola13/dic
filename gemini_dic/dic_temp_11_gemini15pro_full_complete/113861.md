madge:/mædʒ/
noun
女人，唠叨的女人 (informal, often derogatory)
A woman, especially a gossipy or talkative one.
例句：The old madge next door is always peering out of her window. 隔壁那个老女人总是从窗户往外偷看。

例句：Don't be such a madge, mind your own business.  别这么八卦，管好你自己的事。
近义词：gossip, busybody, chatterbox
反义词:quiet person, introvert
词性:n.
