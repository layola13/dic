hypobole:/haɪˈpɜːrbəli/ /haɪˈpɜːrbəʊli/ | n. |夸张；夸张法|An extravagant statement or figure of speech not intended to be taken literally, as “to wait an eternity.” |例句：Her claim that she was the smartest person in the world was pure hyperbole. 她声称自己是世界上最聪明的人，这纯粹是夸张。

例句：He used hyperbole to emphasize the importance of his work. 他用夸张的语气强调了他工作的重要性。|近义词：exaggeration, overstatement |反义词: understatement, meiosis |词性: noun
