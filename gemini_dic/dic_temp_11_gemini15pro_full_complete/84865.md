hangers:/ˈhæŋərz/| n. |衣架|A device, typically a shaped piece of wood, plastic, or metal, with a hook at the top, from which clothes may be hung in order to keep them in shape and free of wrinkles.|I need to buy some new hangers for my closet. 我需要买一些新的衣架放在我的衣柜里。|She carefully placed the dress on the padded hanger. 她小心翼翼地把连衣裙放在软垫衣架上。|Synonyms: coat hanger, clothes hanger|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
