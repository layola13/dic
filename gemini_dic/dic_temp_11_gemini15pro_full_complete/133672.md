nonfragilities:/ˌnɒnfrəˈdʒɪlɪtiz/
n.|非脆弱性；抗脆弱性|The quality of a system or entity that not only withstands stress and volatility but also benefits from or thrives in such conditions.  It is the opposite of fragility, where something breaks under stress.  |The CEO emphasized building nonfragilities into the company's supply chain to ensure resilience against future disruptions. 首席执行官强调要在公司的供应链中建立抗脆弱性，以确保未来面对干扰时的弹性。|Synonym: resilience, robustness, antifragility|Antonym: fragility, vulnerability|Noun
