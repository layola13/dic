eruption:/ɪˈrʌpʃən/ | n. | 爆发，喷发 | A sudden outbreak, often violent, as of a volcano, disease, or war.  |  The volcanic eruption destroyed the entire city. 火山爆发摧毁了整座城市。 | synonyms: outburst, explosion, burst, discharge | antonyms: suppression, containment, quiescence | noun
