outquarters:/ˈaʊtˌkwɔːrtərz/
n.
外部住所，外部营房
A place of lodging away from one's usual residence or military quarters.
The CEO maintained luxurious outquarters in the mountains for corporate retreats. 首席执行官在山上保留了豪华的外部住所，用于公司休养。
synonyms:  lodgings, accommodations, quarters, billet
antonyms: home, residence
