chastisers:/ˈtʃæstaɪzərz/| n.|惩罚者；责罚者|People who inflict punishment or reprimand someone for wrongdoing.  Those who chastise others are called chastisers.|The chastisers were relentless in their pursuit of justice. 惩罚者不懈地追求正义。|The school chastisers punished the bullies severely. 学校的惩罚者严厉地惩罚了恃强凌弱者。|synonyms: punishers, disciplinarians, reprovers, censors|antonyms: rewarders, praisers, commenders|noun
chastise:/ˈtʃæstaɪz/| v.|惩罚；责罚|To reprimand or punish severely.  To inflict punishment on (someone).|The teacher chastised the student for being late. 老师责备学生迟到。|He was chastised for his dishonesty. 他因为不诚实而受到惩罚。|synonyms: punish, discipline, reprimand, scold, rebuke, censure|antonyms: praise, commend, reward|verb
