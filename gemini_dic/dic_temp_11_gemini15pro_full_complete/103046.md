jellyfishes:/ˈdʒɛliˌfɪʃɪz/
n.
水母
Free-swimming marine coelenterates with a gelatinous bell- or saucer-shaped body that is typically transparent and has stinging tentacles around the edge.  Derived from "jelly" and "fish," though they are not true fish.
Many jellyfish species are bioluminescent, meaning they can produce their own light. 许多种类的水母都会生物发光，这意味着它们可以自己产生光。

The beach was closed due to a large number of jellyfish in the water.  由于水中有很多水母，海滩关闭了。
近义词：medusa, sea jelly
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a living creature)
词性: Noun
