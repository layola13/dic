dull:/dʌl/| adj.|迟钝的，无趣的，暗淡的| lacking interest or excitement; not bright or shiny; slow to understand|The movie was so dull that I fell asleep. 这部电影太无聊了，我都睡着了。|Synonyms: boring, tedious, uninteresting, gloomy, dim|Antonyms: interesting, exciting, bright, sharp, intelligent|adjective

dull:/dʌl/| v.|使迟钝，使无趣，使暗淡|to make something less sharp, bright, or interesting|The constant rain dulled the colors of the flowers.  持续的雨水使花朵的颜色暗淡了。|Synonyms: blunt, dampen, dim, weaken|Antonyms: sharpen, brighten, enhance, strengthen|verb
