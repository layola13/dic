muscles:/ˈmʌsəlz/ | n. | 肌肉 |  A band or bundle of fibrous tissue in a human or animal body that has the ability to contract, producing movement in or maintaining the position of parts of the body. |  The athlete developed strong muscles through years of training.  这位运动员经过多年的训练，练就了强健的肌肉。 | synonyms: sinews, brawn, tissues | antonyms:  fat, flab | noun
