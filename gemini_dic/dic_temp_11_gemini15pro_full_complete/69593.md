felty:/ˈfɛlti/| adj.|毛毡状的；像毛毡的；覆盖着毛毡的|Made of, resembling, or covered with felt. |例句：The old teddy bear had a felty texture.  那只旧泰迪熊摸起来像毛毡。|近义词：woolen, fuzzy, nappy|反义词:smooth, sleek|词性:形容词
Etymology: From felt + -y.  Felt itself comes from Old English *felt, from Proto-Germanic *feltaz, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *pel-, meaning "to cover, hide, wrap."
