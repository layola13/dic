matelasse:/ˌmæt.ləˈseɪ/ | n. | 提花织物；凸纹织物 | A woven fabric, or a knitted fabric made to look woven, with a raised or puffed design. |  I bought a beautiful matelasse bedspread. 我买了一条漂亮的提花床罩。 | The sofa was upholstered in a luxurious green matelasse. 沙发用奢华的绿色提花织物装饰。 | 近义词: brocade, damask | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "plain fabric") | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *matelas* (mattress), from Arabic *matrah* (something thrown down, cushion).  The fabric originally resembled the stitching used on mattresses.
