mufti:/ˈmʌfti/ (UK), /ˈmʌfti/ (US)| n.|便服，便衣；伊斯兰教法典的讲解员|Civilian clothes, as opposed to a military uniform; a Muslim legal expert who is empowered to give rulings on religious matters.|The soldiers were allowed to go out in mufti. 士兵们获准穿便服外出。|Synonyms: civilian clothes, plain clothes, civvies (informal), everyday clothes|Antonyms: uniform, military attire|noun
