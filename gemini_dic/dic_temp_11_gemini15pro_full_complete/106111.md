knighthoods:/ˈnaɪthʊdz/
n.
骑士身份；骑士精神；骑士阶层
the rank, status, or dignity of a knight; the qualities traditionally associated with knights, such as chivalry, courage, and honor;  the group or body of knights
He received a knighthood for his services to the country. 他因对国家的贡献而被授予骑士爵位。
The ideals of knighthood are still admired today.  骑士精神的理想至今仍受人敬仰。
近义词:chivalry, nobility
反义词:dishonor, cowardice
词性:noun
