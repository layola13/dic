drosometer:/drɒˈsɒmɪtər/ | n. |露量计| An instrument used to measure the amount of dew formed. | The drosometer showed a significant amount of dew formation overnight. 露量计显示夜间形成了大量的露水。|  The farmer used a drosometer to assess the moisture levels in his fields. 农民用露量计来评估他田地里的湿度水平。|Synonyms: dew gauge | Antonyms: N/A | noun
