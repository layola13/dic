bolection:/boʊˈlɛkʃən/
n.
凸线，凸起线脚，凸线装饰
A molding that projects beyond the surface it decorates.
The fireplace was adorned with a simple bolection molding. 壁炉装饰着简单的凸线。

bolection:/boʊˈlɛkʃən/
n.
镶板，嵌板
A type of raised paneling, often used on furniture or doors.
The antique cabinet featured intricate bolection paneling. 古董橱柜的特点是复杂的镶板。


Etymology:
From French *bouléction*, from *bouler* "to swell," from Old French *bole* "bowl," ultimately from Late Latin *bolla*.

Synonyms:
molding, trim, frame, paneling, raised panel

Antonyms:
recess, indentation, groove

Noun
