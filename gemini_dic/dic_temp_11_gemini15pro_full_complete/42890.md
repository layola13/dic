courtyard:/ˈkɔːrtjɑːrd/ (US) /ˈkɔːtjɑːd/ (UK)| n. | 庭院，院子 | An enclosed area, often paved, adjoining a building or house. |  The hotel has a beautiful courtyard where guests can relax.  这家酒店有一个美丽的庭院，客人可以在那里放松身心。

The children were playing in the courtyard. 孩子们在院子里玩耍。

|近义词：yard, patio, court, plaza |反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly open field) | 词性: 名词
