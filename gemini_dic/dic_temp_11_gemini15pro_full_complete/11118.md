archdeaconships: /ˌɑːrtʃˈdiːkənʃɪps/ | n. | 副主教的职位或辖区 | The office or jurisdiction of an archdeacon.  It refers to the territorial area of responsibility of an archdeacon. |  The bishop reorganized the archdeaconships within the diocese. 主教重新组织了教区内的副主教辖区。|  He was appointed to one of the archdeaconships in the province. 他被任命为该省的副主教之一。| synonyms: archdeaconry, jurisdiction, province, district | antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "parish" which is a smaller unit) | noun
