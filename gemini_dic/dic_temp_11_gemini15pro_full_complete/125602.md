multivalvular:/ˌmʌltiˈvælvjʊlər/
adjective
多瓣膜的
Affecting or involving more than one heart valve.
The patient presented with multivalvular heart disease.  这位病人患有多瓣膜心脏病。
Multivalvular involvement was detected during the echocardiogram.  超声心动图检查发现多瓣膜受累。
Near Synonyms: polyvalvular
Antonyms: univalvular, single-valve

Etymology:
multi- (prefix): many, multiple (from Latin multus "much, many")
valvular (adjective): relating to or having valves (from Latin valva "leaf of a folding door," hence "valve")
