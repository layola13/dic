hallowed:/ˈhæləʊd/ (UK) /ˈhæloʊd/ (US)| adj.|神圣的，受崇敬的|Made holy; consecrated; highly respected and revered.|例句：The hallowed halls of the university.  大学神圣的殿堂。|例句：Hallowed be thy name. 愿人都尊你的名为圣。(出自主祷文)|近义词：sacred, holy, consecrated, revered|反义词：profane, unholy, desecrated|词性:形容词
词源: From Old English *hālgod, past participle of hālgian (“to hallow”), from hālig (“holy”). Cognate with Scots hallowit (“hallowed”), West Frisian helge (“holy”), Dutch geheiligd (“hallowed, sanctified”), German geheiligt (“hallowed, sanctified”), Swedish helgad (“hallowed”).
