deductible:/dɪˈdʌktəbl/ | adj. n. | 可扣除的；扣除额 |  An amount that you must pay yourself before your insurance company pays a claim. |  My car insurance has a $500 deductible. 我的汽车保险有 500 美元的免赔额。| synonyms:subtractable, exempted | antonyms:nondeductible, inclusive | adjective

deductible:/dɪˈdʌktəbl/ | n. | 免赔额；可扣除金额 | The amount you have to pay out-of-pocket before your insurance coverage kicks in. | The deductible on my health insurance is quite high. 我的健康保险的免赔额相当高。| synonyms:excess, co-pay  | antonyms:premium | noun
