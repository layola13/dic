erodent:/ɪˈrəʊdənt/ (UK) /ɪˈroʊdənt/ (US)| adj. n.|腐蚀的；侵蚀的；腐蚀剂；侵蚀剂|Causing erosion; a substance that erodes.|

例句：
The erodent effect of the wind and rain had worn away the inscription on the tombstone. 风和雨的侵蚀作用磨损了墓碑上的铭文。

The acid rain is a powerful erodent to the stone buildings. 酸雨对石制建筑物有很强的腐蚀作用。

近义词：corrosive, abrasive, caustic|
反义词：protective, preservative|
词性：adjective, noun|

词源：From Latin ērōdere "to gnaw out, consume," from ex "out" + rōdere "to gnaw."|
