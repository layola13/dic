A bearing is also a machine part that allows rotation. It can also refer to the way one carries or conducts oneself. |She lost her bearings in the dense fog.  她在浓雾中迷失了方向。

The engine bearings need to be replaced.  发动机的轴承需要更换。

His arrogant bearings alienated his colleagues. 他傲慢的举止疏远了他的同事。 |近义词：direction, location, position, demeanor, attitude,  bearing (singular for machine part)|反义词: disorientation, confusion (for location sense)|词性:名词
