ohps:/oʊps/ | interj. | 哎哟；糟糕 | An exclamation expressing mild apology, surprise, or dismay. | Oops, I spilled my coffee! 哎哟，我咖啡洒了！ |  Oops, I almost forgot your birthday. 哎哟，我差点忘了你的生日。 | 近义词: whoops, uh-oh | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for an interjection) | 词性:感叹词

词源:  Likely a shortening of "whoops," which itself is imitative of a sound expressing surprise or dismay.
