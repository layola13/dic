disassembler:/ˌdɪsəˈsɛmblə(r)/ | n. | 反汇编程序 | A computer program that translates machine language (code that the computer can execute directly) into assembly language (a more human-readable representation of the machine code). This is often used in reverse engineering or debugging. | The disassembler helped me understand how the malware worked.  这个反汇编程序帮助我理解了恶意软件是如何工作的。 | The disassembler showed the branching logic of the program. 反汇编程序显示了程序的分支逻辑。 | Synonym: decompiler (for higher-level languages) | Antonym: assembler | noun
