encased:/ɪnˈkeɪst/ /ɛnˈkeɪst/| adj.|被包住的，被封入的|Enclosed tightly within something; covered or protected completely. |The radioactive waste was encased in concrete. 放射性废料被封在混凝土里。

The sword was encased in a beautiful scabbard.  这把剑被装在一个漂亮的剑鞘里。|近义词：enclosed, enveloped, surrounded, covered, protected|反义词：exposed, uncovered, open|词性:形容词

词源: From en- (meaning "in" or "into") + case (meaning "a container").
