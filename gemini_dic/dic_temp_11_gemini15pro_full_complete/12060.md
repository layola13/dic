artifact:/ˈɑːrtɪfækt/ | n. | 人工制品，手工艺品；(尤指具有历史或文化价值的)史前古器物，文物 | An object made by a human being, typically an item of cultural or historical interest. | The museum displays many artifacts from ancient Egypt.  博物馆展出许多来自古埃及的文物。 | synonym: relic, object, antiquity | antonym: natural object | noun


artifact:/ˈɑːrtɪfækt/ | n. | (尤指科技产品产生的) 瑕疵，失真 | something observed in a scientific investigation or experiment that is not naturally present but occurs as a result of the preparative or investigative procedure.  |  The strange sound on the recording was just an artifact of the recording equipment.  录音里的奇怪声音只是录音设备产生的失真。 | synonym: glitch, flaw, distortion | antonym: genuine feature | noun
