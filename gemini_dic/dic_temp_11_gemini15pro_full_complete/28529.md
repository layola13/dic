cardigans: /ˈkɑːrdɪɡənz/ | n. | 羊毛衫，开襟羊毛衫 | A knitted jacket or sweater that opens down the front, typically made of wool. | She wore a cozy cardigan over her dress. 她穿了一件舒适的开襟羊毛衫套在连衣裙外面。 |  Example: My grandmother always knits beautiful cardigans for the family. 我祖母总是为家人织漂亮的羊毛衫。 | synonyms: sweater, jumper, jacket | antonyms: N/A | noun
