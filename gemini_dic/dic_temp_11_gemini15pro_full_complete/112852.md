词源:
From luckiness + -'s (possessive). Luckiness is derived from lucky + -ness. Lucky comes from Middle English lukky, from Old English *lucciġ (unattested), probably a derivative of luck, from Middle Dutch luc, gheluc "luck, fortune, happiness," of unknown origin.
