nonmineral:/ˌnɒnˈmɪnərəl/
adjective

Not of or derived from mineral substances; organic.
例句：Plants obtain nonmineral nutrients from the soil.  植物从土壤中获取非矿物质营养。

例句：Nonmineral oils are often used in lubricants. 非矿物油常用于润滑剂。
近义词：organic, non-inorganic
反义词：mineral, inorganic
词性：adjective
