heterogenetic:/ˌhɛtərəʊdʒɪˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
异基因的，异源的
Originating outside an organism or system; derived from a different species.  Produced from dissimilar sources.
The infection was caused by heterogenetic antibodies.  这种感染是由异基因抗体引起的。
heterologous, allogeneic
homogeneous, autologous
词性: adjective
