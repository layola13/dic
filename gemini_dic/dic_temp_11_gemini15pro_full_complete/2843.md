adventurists:/ədˈvɛntʃərɪsts/| n. |冒险家；冒险主义者|People who seek out and enjoy adventures, often involving some degree of risk or danger. |例句：The adventurists set off on their expedition to the uncharted jungle. 冒险家们开始了他们前往未知丛林的探险之旅。

例句：Many adventurists are drawn to the Himalayas. 许多冒险家都被喜马拉雅山所吸引。|近义词：daredevils, thrill-seekers, explorers, adventurers|反义词：cautious people, conservatives|词性：名词

词源: From adventure + -ist.  Ultimately from the Latin adventus "arrival."
