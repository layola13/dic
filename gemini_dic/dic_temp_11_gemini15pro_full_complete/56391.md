doled:/doʊld/| v. (past tense of dole)|施舍，发放（少量钱或物）|The government doled out meager unemployment benefits.|政府发放了微薄的失业救济金。|近义词：distributed, dispensed, apportioned, allocated|反义词：hoarded, withheld|词性:动词
Etymology: From Old English *dǣlan (to divide, share, distribute), from Proto-Germanic *dailjaną (to divide, share), from Proto-Indo-European *dail- (to divide).  Related to German teilen (to divide).  The original sense was to distribute a portion, which narrowed to distributing small portions, often of charity.
