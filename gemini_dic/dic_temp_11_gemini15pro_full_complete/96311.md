indigestedness:/ˌɪndɪˈdʒɛstɪdnəs/ | n. | 消化不良；不消化 |The state of being indigested; inability to digest food properly.  This can refer to both physical indigestion and the inability to mentally process information or experiences. |  He suffered from indigestion and its accompanying indigestedness. 他患有消化不良及其伴随的不消化。| Synonyms: dyspepsia, indigestion | Antonyms: digestion, assimilation | noun

indigestedness:/ˌɪndɪˈdʒɛstɪdnəs/ | n. | 未消化的；未吸收的（信息、知识等）|The state of information, ideas, or experiences not being fully understood or absorbed. | The indigestedness of the complex theories made it difficult for the students to grasp the main concepts. 这些复杂理论的未被消化使得学生难以掌握主要概念。| Synonyms: unassimilated, unprocessed | Antonyms: digested, assimilated, understood | noun
