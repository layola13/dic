ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin anaesthēsia, from Ancient Greek ἀναισθησία (anaisthēsía, “lack of sensation”), from ἀν- (an-, “without”) + αἴσθησις (aísthēsis, “sensation, feeling”).
