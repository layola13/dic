noninflected:/ˌnɒnɪnˈflektɪd/ /ˌnɑːnɪnˈflektɪd/ | adj. | 非屈折的，不变形的 | Not inflected; unchanging in form.  A noninflected word does not change its spelling or pronunciation to show grammatical function or tense. |  Example: Chinese is a largely noninflected language.  中文在很大程度上是一种非屈折语。 | Synonym: uninflected, invariable | Antonym: inflected, variable |词性: 形容词
