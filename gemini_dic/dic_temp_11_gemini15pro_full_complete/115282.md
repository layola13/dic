manlessly:/ˈmæn.ləs.li/
adv.|无男子气概地，缺乏男子气概地|Without manly qualities; in a way that lacks manliness. |He behaved manlessly, refusing to take responsibility for his actions. 他表现得毫无男子气概，拒绝为自己的行为承担责任。|Example: He walked manlessly across the stage, his shoulders slumped and his eyes downcast. 他无精打采地走过舞台，肩膀低垂，目光低垂。|Synonyms: effeminately, unmanly, weakly|Antonyms: manfully, courageously, bravely|词性: 副词
