fishling:/ˈfɪʃlɪŋ/
n.
小鱼，鱼苗
A young or small fish.
The fishling darted through the reeds.  小鱼 darted through the reeds。
近义词：fry, fingerling
反义词：(None -  opposite would be a large/adult fish, not a single word)
词性：noun
