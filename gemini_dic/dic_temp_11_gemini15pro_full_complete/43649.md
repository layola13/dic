creatorships:/kriˈeɪtərˌʃɪps/
n.
创造者身份；创造力；创作者的地位
The state or quality of being a creator; the works produced by a creator.  The collective body of creators.
The rise of digital platforms has empowered individuals to embrace their creatorships and share their work with the world. 数字平台的兴起使个人能够拥抱他们的创造者身份，并与世界分享他们的作品。
The museum's exhibit showcased the creatorships of several local artists.  博物馆的展览展示了几位当地艺术家的创作。
synonyms: authorship, origination, innovation, artistry
antonyms: imitation, copying, derivation
