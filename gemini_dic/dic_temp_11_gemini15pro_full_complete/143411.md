outflanks:/ˌaʊtˈflæŋks/ | v. | 包抄，迂回；智胜，胜过 | To go around the side of an enemy or opponent, especially in order to attack them from behind; to gain an advantage over someone by being cleverer or more skillful. | The army outflanked the enemy and forced them to retreat.  军队包抄了敌人，迫使他们撤退。

The company outflanked its competitors by launching a new product line.  这家公司通过推出新的产品线胜过了竞争对手。 | 近义词: bypass, circumvent, outmaneuver, surround | 反义词: be outflanked, be outmaneuvered | 词性: 动词
