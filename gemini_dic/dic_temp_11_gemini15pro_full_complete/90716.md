humidify:/hjuːˈmɪdɪfaɪ/ |v. |加湿，使湿润|To increase the humidity of the air or a space.  |例句：A humidifier can humidify a dry room.  加湿器可以给干燥的房间加湿。|近义词：moisten, dampen|反义词：dehumidify, dry|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Late Latin humidificare, from Latin humidus "moist" + -ficare "-fy".
