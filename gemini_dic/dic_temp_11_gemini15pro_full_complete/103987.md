juggler:/ˈdʒʌɡlər/| n.|杂耍演员，玩杂耍的人，同时应付多项工作的人|A juggler can keep several objects in the air at the same time. 一个杂耍演员可以同时让几个物体在空中飞舞。|The CEO is a master juggler, balancing the needs of employees, customers, and investors.  这位CEO是一位高手，能够平衡员工、客户和投资者的需求。|近义词: acrobat, performer, multitasker|反义词: specialist|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *jogleor, from Late Latin joculator "jester, minstrel," from Latin joculari "to jest," from jocus "jest, joke."  The meaning shifted from "jester" to "one who performs tricks of manual dexterity."
