ecotype:/ˈiːkəʊtaɪp/ | n. |生态型|A distinct form or race of a plant or animal species occupying a particular habitat. |例句：The researchers studied the different ecotypes of the plant species found across the mountain range. 研究人员研究了山脉中发现的植物物种的不同生态型。

例句：The coastal ecotype of this species is more tolerant to salt spray than the inland ecotype.  这个物种的海岸生态型比内陆生态型更耐盐雾。|近义词：subspecies, race, variety, form |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
