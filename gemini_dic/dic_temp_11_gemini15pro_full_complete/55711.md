div:/dɪv/| n.| (HTML) division; divisor|A section in an HTML document; the number by which another number is divided.|例句：The div element is used to structure web pages. div元素用于构建网页结构。

例句：In 10 / 2 = 5, 2 is the div. 在 10 / 2 = 5 中，2 是除数。|近义词：section, segment (for HTML); divisor, factor (for math)|反义词:  (no direct antonym for div in either sense) |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From HTML, short for "division"; from Latin *dīvīdere*, "to divide".  As a mathematical term, shortened from "divisor".
