fusionists:/ˈfjuːʒənɪsts/
noun
融合主义者；融合论者
People who advocate or support fusion, especially the merging of different styles, ideas, or cultures.
The fusionists in the art world sought to blend traditional techniques with modern aesthetics.  融合主义艺术家试图将传统技法与现代美学融合在一起。
The fusionists believe that combining the best aspects of both systems will yield superior results. 融合主义者认为，将两个系统的优点结合起来将会产生更好的结果。
synonyms: integrationists, synthesizers, combiners
antonyms: separatists, purists, segregationists
