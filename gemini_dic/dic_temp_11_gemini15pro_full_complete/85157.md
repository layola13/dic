hardtops:/ˈhɑːrdtɒps/ (US) /ˈhɑːrdtɒps/ (UK)| n. |硬顶汽车；硬顶|A car with a rigid roof that is not detachable.  A rigid roof itself. |例句：He traded in his convertible for a hardtop. 他把敞篷车换成了一辆硬顶车。

例句：The hardtop provided better insulation than the soft top. 硬顶比软顶隔热效果更好。|近义词：coupe, sedan, fixed-roof|反义词：convertible, soft-top|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "hard" + "top".  "Hard" meaning rigid or firm, and "top" referring to the roof of a car.  The term came into common usage in the mid-20th century with the rise in popularity of cars with permanent, non-detachable roofs.
