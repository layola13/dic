iridaceous:/ˌɪrɪˈdeɪʃəs/| adj.|彩虹色的，虹彩的|Having colors like those of the rainbow, shimmering or exhibiting iridescence.|The hummingbird's feathers were iridescent, flashing different colors in the sunlight.  蜂鸟的羽毛是彩虹色的，在阳光下闪烁着不同的颜色。|Synonyms: shimmering, opalescent, pearly, nacreous|Antonyms: dull, matte, monochrome|adjective

iridaceous:/ˌɪrɪˈdeɪʃəs/| adj.|彩虹色的，虹彩的|Displaying a play of lustrous colors like those of the rainbow.|The soap bubble floated, an iridescent sphere against the blue sky.  肥皂泡漂浮着，在蓝天映衬下，像一个彩虹色的球体。|Synonyms: prismatic, glittering, sparkling|Antonyms: lackluster, drab|adjective
