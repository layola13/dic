comprehensivization:/ˌkɒmprɪˌhensɪvaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 理解化；全面化；综合化 | The process of making something comprehensive or more comprehensive.  The act of including all or nearly all elements or aspects of something. | The comprehensivization of the healthcare system is a complex process.  医疗保健系统的全面化是一个复杂的过程。|  The comprehensivization of the study included data from multiple sources. 这项研究的综合化包括来自多个来源的数据。 | synonyms:  integration, synthesis, consolidation | antonyms:  fragmentation, simplification, reduction | noun
