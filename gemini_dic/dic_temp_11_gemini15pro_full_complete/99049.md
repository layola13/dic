interannular:/ˌɪntərˈæn.jʊ.lər/
adjective
环间的，环状的
Situated between rings or within the space between two concentric rings.
The interannular space on the piston was damaged. 活塞上的环间隙损坏了。

interannular:/ˌɪntərˈæn.jʊ.lər/
adjective
环状的
Having the form of a ring.
The interannular design provided extra strength. 环状设计提供了额外的强度。

interannular:/ˌɪntərˈæn.jʊ.lər/
adjective
位于年轮之间的
Situated between annual rings (in trees, for example).
Interannular growth patterns can reveal information about past climates. 年轮间的生长模式可以揭示过去气候的信息。


近义词:annular, ring-shaped, cyclic
反义词:non-cyclic, linear
