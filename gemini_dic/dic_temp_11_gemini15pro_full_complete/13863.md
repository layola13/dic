augmentatives:/ɔːɡˈmɛntətɪvz/ | n. | 增大词，放大词 | Words formed by adding a suffix or other element to a base word to indicate greater size, importance, or intensity. |  The word "gigantic" is an augmentative of "giant."  “gigantic”是“giant”的增大词。 | synonyms: intensifiers, magnifiers | antonyms: diminutives | noun
