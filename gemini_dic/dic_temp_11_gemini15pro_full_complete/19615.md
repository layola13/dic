bigot:/ˈbɪɡət/| n.| 偏执者，顽固者；抱偏见的人|A person who is intolerant towards those holding different opinions.|He was known as a religious bigot, intolerant of those with different beliefs. 他因宗教偏执而闻名，不能容忍信仰不同的人。|Synonyms: fanatic, extremist, dogmatist, partisan|Antonyms: tolerant, open-minded, liberal|Noun

bigoted:/ˈbɪɡətɪd/| adj.| 偏执的，顽固的；抱偏见的|Obstinately or unreasonably attached to a belief, opinion, or faction, especially one that is prejudiced against or antagonistic towards a person or people on the basis of their membership of a particular group.|She expressed bigoted views about immigrants. 她表达了对移民的偏见。|Synonyms: prejudiced, biased, intolerant, narrow-minded|Antonyms: tolerant, open-minded, unbiased, impartial|Adjective
