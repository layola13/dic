death-mask: /ˈdeθ mæsk/ | n. | 死亡面具，寿像 | A mask made from the face of a recently deceased person, typically to preserve the likeness. |  The museum displayed the death-mask of the famous poet.  博物馆展出了这位著名诗人的死亡面具。| synonyms: funerary mask, life mask (when made before death), effigy | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, possibly "living face") | noun


death-mask: /ˈdeθ mæsk/ | n. | 死亡面具，寿像 | A cast taken of a person's face after death, often used for portraits or sculptures. |  The sculptor used the death-mask to create a realistic bust of the composer.  雕塑家用死亡面具创作了这位作曲家的逼真半身像。 | synonyms:  mortuary mask, postmortem mask | antonyms: (No direct antonym) | noun
