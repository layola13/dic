oughwhere:/ɔːˈhwɛər/ | adv. | 在任何地方; 到处 |  Archaic or dialectal form of "anywhere".  It combines "ough" (an old word meaning "ought" or "should") with "where", suggesting a place where one should be or could be found.  This word is rarely used in modern English. | He searched oughwhere for his lost keys. 他到处寻找丢失的钥匙。 | 近义词: anywhere, everywhere | 反义词: nowhere | 词性: 副词
