factory: /ˈfæktəri/ (US), /ˈfæktri/ (UK) | n. | 工厂 | A building or group of buildings where goods are manufactured or assembled. |  The factory produces cars. 这家工厂生产汽车。

The new factory employs over 500 people.  这家新工厂雇佣了500多人。


近义词: plant, mill, works | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "home" or "farm" in certain contexts) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *factorie ("trading post, agency"), from Late Latin factoria ("place of business"), from Latin factor ("maker, doer"), from facere ("to do, to make").
