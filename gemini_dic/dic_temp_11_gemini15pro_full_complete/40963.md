cooperativization:/koʊˌɑːpərətɪvɪˈzeɪʃən/ /kəʊˈɒpərətɪvaɪˈzeɪʃn/
|n.|合作化，集体化|The process of forming a cooperative or converting an existing business into a cooperative.  The transformation of private businesses or farms into cooperatives, often involving joint ownership and democratic control.|The cooperativization of agriculture in the 1950s drastically changed the rural landscape.  20世纪50年代农业合作化极大地改变了农村面貌。|collectivization, mutualization|privatization|noun
