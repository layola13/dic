islandish:/ˈaɪləndɪʃ/ | adj. | 岛屿的；偏狭的；孤陋寡闻的 | Characteristic of an island or islanders, especially in being narrow-minded or isolated. | The islanders had a rather islandish view of the world, unaware of the changes happening elsewhere. 岛民们对世界的看法相当狭隘，不了解其他地方正在发生的变化。|  例句：Their islandish attitudes made it difficult for them to adapt to city life. 他们的岛民心态使他们难以适应城市生活。

例句：The small town had an islandish feel, as if time had stood still. 这个小镇有一种岛屿般的感觉，仿佛时间静止了。 | 近义词：insular, parochial, narrow-minded, provincial | 反义词：cosmopolitan, worldly, broad-minded | 词性：形容词
