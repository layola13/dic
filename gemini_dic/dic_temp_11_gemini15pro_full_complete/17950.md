befit:/bɪˈfɪt/| vt.|适合，相称|To be suitable for or appropriate to; to be worthy of.|例句：Such a noble cause befits a generous donation.  如此崇高的事业值得慷慨解囊。

例句：A crown befits a king.  王冠与国王相称。|近义词：suit, become, behoove|反义词：misfit, unbecoming|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + fit (adj.).  "Be-" is a prefix meaning "completely, thoroughly," or indicating "action upon." "Fit" comes from Old English "fett" meaning "well-nourished, plump; proper, suitable," ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*fetaz" (fat).  The sense of "suitable" developed later.

PHRASE->

It ill befits you to... = 你不应该...

注意事项: 注意befit主要用于正式场合，口语中较少使用。虽然它可以表示“装备”或“提供”，但这种用法不如“suit”或“become”常见，并且通常出现在更文学化的语境中.
