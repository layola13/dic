/əˈrɪðməs/ | adj. | 无节奏的，不规律的 | Lacking rhythm or regularity, especially of the heartbeat. |  His arrhythmous breathing worried the doctors. 他的呼吸没有规律，让医生很担心。 |  The pianist's arrhythmous playing made the piece almost unbearable.  这位钢琴家演奏的毫无节奏感，几乎让人无法忍受。 | irregular, uneven, erratic, unsteady | rhythmic, regular, steady | adjective
