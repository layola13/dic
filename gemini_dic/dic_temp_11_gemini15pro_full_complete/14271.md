autogenic:/ˌɔːtəˈdʒɛnɪk/
adjective
自生的；自体发生的；自发的
Self-generating; produced from within or by the organism itself.
Autogenic training involves a series of exercises that help the practitioner control bodily functions like heart rate and temperature.  自体训练包括一系列练习，帮助练习者控制心率和体温等身体机能。
Autogenic relaxation techniques can help manage stress. 自生放松技巧可以帮助控制压力。
Synonyms: endogenous, self-generated, spontaneous
Antonyms: exogenous, external
词性: adjective
