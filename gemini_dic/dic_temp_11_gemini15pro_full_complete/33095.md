christendoms:/ˈkrɪs(ə)ndəmz/
n.
基督教世界，基督教国家
the whole body of Christians or the areas in which Christianity prevails
The crusades were fought to reclaim the holy lands for Christendom.  十字军东征是为了夺回基督教世界的圣地。

近义词:Christian world, Christian countries
反义词:N/A
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English cristendom "Christianity, Christian world," from cristen "Christian" + -dom (suffix forming abstract nouns of state, condition, or domain).
