cds:/ˌsiːˈdiːˈɛs/ | n. |  certificates of deposit | Certificates of deposit are savings accounts that hold a fixed amount of money for a fixed period of time, and they pay a fixed rate of interest. | Example:  I invested some money in cds for a better return. 我投资了一些钱在定期存单上以获得更好的回报。

Example:  CDs offer a higher interest rate than regular savings accounts.  定期存单提供的利率比普通储蓄账户高。 | Synonym: time deposit, fixed deposit | Antonym: checking account, demand deposit | noun
