dooryard:/ˈdɔːrjɑːrd/ | n. | 庭院，院子 | The dooryard was filled with colorful flowers. 院子里开满了五颜六色的花。|  A small area in front of or around the entrance to a house, often paved or planted with grass or other vegetation. It is essentially the same as a yard, but often implies a smaller, more intimate space directly associated with the house's entrance. | The children were playing in the dooryard. 孩子们在院子里玩耍。| 近义词：yard, courtyard, front yard, garden | 反义词:  (No direct opposite, perhaps "indoors") | 词性: 名词
