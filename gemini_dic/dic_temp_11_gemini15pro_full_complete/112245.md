looter:/ˈluːtər/| n.|掠夺者，抢劫者|A person who steals goods from shops or homes during a riot or after a disaster, etc.|例句：Looters smashed shop windows and grabbed whatever they could. 抢劫者砸碎商店橱窗，抓起所有能拿到的东西。

例句：The looters took advantage of the chaos after the hurricane. 抢劫者利用飓风后的混乱局面。|近义词：thief, robber, plunderer, pillager|反义词:protector, guardian|noun
