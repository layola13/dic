forebodingly:/fɔrˈboʊdɪŋli/
adverb
预兆不祥地，不吉利地
in a way that gives the feeling that something bad is going to happen
The wind howled forebodingly. 风不祥地呼啸着。
ominously, threateningly, menacingly
auspiciously, hopefully, encouragingly
词性: 副词

词源:
From foreboding + -ly.  Foreboding comes from the Old English word *forebōdian, from fore- "before" + bōdian "to announce, proclaim, foretell."
