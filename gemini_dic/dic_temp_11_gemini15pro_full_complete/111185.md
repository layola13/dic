lithographical:/ˌlɪθəˈɡræfɪk(ə)l/
adjective
平版印刷的
Relating to lithography (printing from a flat stone or metal plate).
The lithographic process is used to print high-quality images. 平版印刷工艺用于印刷高质量图像。

The museum has a collection of lithographic prints.  博物馆收藏了一些平版印刷品。

synonym: planographic
antonym:  (No direct antonym, but could consider "intaglio" or "relief" printing as contrasting methods)
词性: adjective
