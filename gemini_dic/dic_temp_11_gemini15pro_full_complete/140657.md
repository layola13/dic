ombudsman: /ˈɒmbʊdzmən/ (UK) /ˈɑːmbədzmən/ (US)| n.| 申诉专员；监察员；巡视员| An official appointed to investigate individuals' complaints against maladministration, especially that of public authorities.|  The ombudsman received numerous complaints about the new policy. 申诉专员收到了许多关于新政策的投诉。

The university appointed an ombudsman to handle student grievances. 大学任命了一名监察员来处理学生的申诉。| synonym: mediator, arbitrator, advocate, inspector| antonym: defendant, accuser | noun
