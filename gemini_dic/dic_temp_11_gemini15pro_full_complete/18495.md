bendable:/ˈbendəbl/| adj.|可弯曲的，柔韧的|Something that is bendable can be bent easily without breaking.  Able to be bent or flexed.|例句：The wire is thin and bendable. 这根金属丝又细又容易弯曲。|例句：The toy is made of bendable plastic. 这个玩具是用可弯曲的塑料制成的。|近义词：flexible, pliable, supple|反义词：rigid, stiff, inflexible|词性:adjective
bend:/bend/| v. n.|弯曲，屈服|From Old English *bendan "to curve, bind," of Germanic origin.|例句：He bent the wire into a circle. 他把金属丝弯成一个圆圈。|例句：The road bends sharply to the left. 这条路向左急转弯。|近义词：curve, flex, bow|反义词：straighten, unbend|词性:verb, noun
