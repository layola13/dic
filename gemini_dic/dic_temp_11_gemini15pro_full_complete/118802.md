metameric:/ˌmɛtəˈmɛrɪk/ | adj. | 分节的，体节的，同分异构的 | Divided into segments; relating to metamerism (the repetition of body parts). Also, relating to metamers (isomers having the same elements in the same proportion but differing in structure). | Example 1: The earthworm's body is metameric, composed of repeating segments. 蚯蚓的身体是分节的，由重复的节段组成。

Example 2: Glucose and fructose are metameric, having the same chemical formula but different structures. 葡萄糖和果糖是同分异构体，具有相同的化学式但结构不同。 | Near Synonyms: segmented, isomeric | Antonyms: unsegmented, non-isomeric | word property:adjective


metamere: /ˈmɛtəmɪər/ | n. | 体节，分节 | A body segment, especially one of a series of similar segments. | Example 1: Each metamere of the insect's body has a pair of legs. 昆虫身体的每个体节都有一对腿。

Example 2: The metameres of the tapeworm are clearly visible under a microscope. 在显微镜下，绦虫的体节清晰可见。 | Near Synonyms: segment, somite | Antonyms: whole, entire | word property:noun
