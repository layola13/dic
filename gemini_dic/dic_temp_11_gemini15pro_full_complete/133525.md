nonfictitiously: /ˌnɑːnfɪkˈtɪʃəsli/ /ˌnɒnfɪkˈtɪʃəsli/
adverb
非虚构地；真实地
in a way that is based on facts and not made up; truthfully
He wrote nonfictitiously about his experiences in the war. 他如实地写下了他在战争中的经历。
She recounted the events nonfictitiously, sticking to the verifiable facts. 她真实地叙述了这些事件，坚持可验证的事实。
近义词: factually, truthfully, accurately, veridically
反义词: fictitiously, falsely, untruthfully
词性: adverb

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + fictitious (adjective meaning "imaginary" or "fabricated") + -ly (adverb suffix)
