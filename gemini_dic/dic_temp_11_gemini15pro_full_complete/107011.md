lactate:/ˈlækteɪt/ (US), /lækˈteɪt/ (UK)| n. vi. |乳酸盐；乳酸酯；[生化] 分泌乳汁|A salt or ester of lactic acid.  To produce lactic acid, as in muscles during strenuous exercise.|The athlete's muscles began to lactate after the intense sprint.| 近义词: lactic acid salt| 反义词: (No direct antonym for a chemical compound)|n., vi.
