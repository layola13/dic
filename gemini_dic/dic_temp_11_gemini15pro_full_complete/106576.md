kshatriya:/ˈkʃʌtrijə/| n.|刹帝利，武士， क्षत्रिय (kṣatriya)|A member of the second of the four Hindu castes, traditionally the military or ruling caste.|The king, a kshatriya, led his army into battle. 国王，一位刹帝利，带领他的军队参加战斗。|Synonyms: warrior, nobleman, ruler|Antonyms: commoner, servant|Noun
