exuberant:/ɪɡˈz(j)uːbərənt/| adj.|繁茂的，丰富的，兴高采烈的|full of energy, excitement, and cheerfulness|例句：The children were exuberant as they opened their presents. 孩子们打开礼物时兴高采烈。|近义词：enthusiastic, lively, energetic, cheerful|反义词：depressed, subdued, apathetic|词性:形容词
Etymology: Late Middle English (in the sense ‘luxuriant’): from Latin exuberant- ‘overflowing’, from the verb exuberare, from ex- ‘out’ + uber ‘fertile, copious’.
