gentlesse:/ˈdʒɛntlnəs/ | n. | 温和，优雅；高贵，礼貌 | The queen treated everyone with utmost gentleness. 女王以极度的温和对待每个人。|  Refinement, courtesy, grace, politeness, civility | Harshness, rudeness, cruelty, coarseness | noun
Etymology: From Old French *gentilesse*, from gentil (“noble, of good family”) + -esse (“-ness”). Ultimately from Latin gentilis.
