dapifer:/ˈdæpɪfər/| n.|管家；膳食管理员|A dapifer was a steward or seneschal in a medieval household, responsible for managing the feasts and serving food to the lord and his guests. |The dapifer oversaw the preparation and serving of the banquet. 管家监督了宴会的准备和上菜。|Synonyms: steward, seneschal, major-domo|Antonyms: servant, underling|noun

dapifer:/ˈdæpɪfər/| n.|膳食管理员|In some historical contexts, the dapifer specifically referred to the officer in charge of the king's or nobleman's table and provisions. |The dapifer presented the first dish to the king. 膳食管理员向国王呈上了第一道菜。|Synonyms: caterer, butler|Antonyms: guest, diner|noun
