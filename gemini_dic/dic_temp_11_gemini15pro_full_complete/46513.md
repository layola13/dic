daisy-like:/ˈdeɪziˌlaɪk/
adjective
像雏菊的；菊科植物似的
Resembling a daisy in form or appearance.
The small, daisy-like flowers covered the meadow.  小巧的、像雏菊一样的花朵覆盖着草地。
The plant has daisy-like white petals.  这种植物有像雏菊一样的白色花瓣。
近义词: daisy-shaped, chamomile-like
反义词: (No clear antonym, could describe contrasting shapes or flower types, e.g., orchid-like, rose-like)
词性: adjective
