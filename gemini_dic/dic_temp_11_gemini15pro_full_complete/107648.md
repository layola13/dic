langues:/lɑ̃ɡ/| n.|语言（法语复数形式）|This word is the French plural form of "langue" which means "tongue" or "language".  It's rarely used in English, and when it is, it's usually in a specialized context like linguistics.  It's better to use "languages" in English.|He studies the Romance langues.|近义词：(in French) langues, (in English) languages|反义词: (No direct opposite.  Perhaps silence or muteness in a figurative sense.)|noun
