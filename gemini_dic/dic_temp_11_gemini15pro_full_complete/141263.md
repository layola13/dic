opaline:/ˈoʊpəlaɪn/ | adj. n. |乳白色的，乳白玻璃的；乳白色玻璃 |Resembling or having the milky iridescence of an opal; made of or resembling opal glass. |The vase had an opaline sheen.  花瓶有一种乳白色的光泽。|The opaline sky hinted at rain. 乳白色的天空预示着要下雨。|近义词：milky, iridescent, pearly |反义词：transparent, clear |词性: 形容词，名词

Etymology: From French *opalin*, from *opale* (“opal”), from Latin *opalus*, from Greek *ὀπάλλιος* (*opallios*).
