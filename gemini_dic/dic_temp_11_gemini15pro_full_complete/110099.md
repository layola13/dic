lick:/lɪk/ | v. n. |舔，少量，轻拍，击败 | to pass the tongue over something; a small amount; a quick stroke; a defeat | The cat licked its paw. 猫舔了舔爪子。 He took a lick of the ice cream. 他舔了一口冰淇淋。  The flames gave a lick at the dry wood. 火苗轻舔着干木。  Our team took a lick in the final game. 我们队在决赛中被打败了。 |近义词：lap, sip, taste, beat, defeat |反义词：none (verb), none (noun) |词性:动词, 名词

lick:/lɪk/
Etymology: From Old English *liccian, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch likken and German lecken.
