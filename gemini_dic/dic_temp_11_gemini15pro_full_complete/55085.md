disputes:/dɪˈspjuːts/ | n. | 争论，纠纷 | A disagreement, argument, or debate. |  The two countries have been engaged in a series of border disputes.  两国之间发生了一系列的边境争端。| synonyms: arguments, quarrels, disagreements, conflicts, controversies | antonyms: agreements, accords, harmonies, resolutions, settlements | noun

disputes:/dɪˈspjuːts/ | v. | 质疑，否认，争论 | To argue about or question the truth or validity of something. | He disputed the judge's decision. 他质疑法官的判决。| synonyms: challenge, question, contest, oppose, contradict | antonyms: accept, agree, concede, confirm, endorse | verb
