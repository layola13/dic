ammeters:/ˈæmiːtərz/
n.
电流表
An instrument for measuring electric current in amperes.
The ammeter showed a reading of 2 amps. 电流表显示读数为2安培。
近义词:amperemeter
反义词: (No direct antonym for a measuring instrument.  Perhaps conceptually: insulator)
词性:noun
