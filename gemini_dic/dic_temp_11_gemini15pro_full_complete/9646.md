antisubsidies:/ˈæntiˈsʌbsɪdiz/
noun
反补贴
measures opposing the practice of government subsidies
The WTO has rules against antisubsidies.  世界贸易组织有反补贴规定。
近义词: countervailing duties, anti-dumping measures
反义词: subsidies, government support
