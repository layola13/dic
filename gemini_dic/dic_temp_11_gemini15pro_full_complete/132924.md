nonelucidating:/ˌnɒnɪˈluːsɪdeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
未能阐明的，没有解释清楚的
Not making something clear; failing to explain something.
The professor's lecture was nonelucidating, leaving the students more confused than before.  教授的讲座没能阐明问题，让学生们比以前更困惑了。
His response was nonelucidating and unhelpful. 他的回答没有解释清楚，也没有帮助。
Synonyms: unclear, obscure, confusing, opaque, ambiguous, vague, nebulous
Antonyms: clear, lucid, illuminating, enlightening, explanatory, explicit
词性: 形容词
