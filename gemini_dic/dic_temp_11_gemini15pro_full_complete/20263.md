birthless:/ˈbɜːrθləs/| adj.|无生的，未出生的，非由生育产生的|Not born; having no birth; not produced by birth.  It often describes something abstract or divine, existing eternally.|The myth spoke of a birthless god who created the universe.|近义词：eternal, uncreated, ageless, timeless|反义词:born, created, mortal|词性:形容词
词源: From "birth" + "-less" (suffix indicating absence).  "Birth" comes from Old English "birþ," related to "beran" (to bear, to give birth).
