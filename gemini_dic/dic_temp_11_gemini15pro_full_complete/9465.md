antireligiously:/ˌænti rɪˈlɪdʒəsli/
adverb
反宗教地，非宗教地
In an irreligious manner; against religion.
He spoke antireligiously about the church's practices. 他用反宗教的口吻谈论教会的习俗。
Synonyms: irreligiously, profanely, impiously
Antonyms: religiously, piously, devoutly
词性: 副词
