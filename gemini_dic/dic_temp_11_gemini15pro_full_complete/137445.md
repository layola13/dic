nontherapeutically:/ˌnɑːnˌθɛrəˈpjuːtɪkli/
adv.
非治疗地，不以治疗为目的
Not for therapeutic purposes; in a way that does not aim to cure or treat a medical condition.
The drug was administered nontherapeutically to the control group.  该药物非治疗性地给予对照组。
The substance was used nontherapeutically in the experiment.  该物质在实验中被非治疗性地使用。
Near Antonyms: therapeutically
Antonyms: medicinally, curatively
