grippin:/ˈɡrɪpɪn/| adj. | 吸引人的；非常棒的；令人印象深刻的 | Extremely attractive, impressive, or cool.  Derived from "gripping," meaning firmly holding the attention. Often used in African American Vernacular English (AAVE). | That new car is grippin'. 那辆新车真棒。|  近义词：amazing, awesome, fantastic, cool, impressive | 反义词：boring, dull, unimpressive | 词性:形容词
