nonclarifications:/ˌnɒnˌklærɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 未澄清的内容；未说明的内容 |  The lack of nonclarifications in the contract led to disputes. 合同中缺少未澄清内容的说明导致了纠纷。 |  Statements or situations that do not provide clarification or further explanation.  | Example: The witness's nonclarifications only confused the jury. 证人的未澄清内容只会让陪审团感到困惑.  | Synonyms: ambiguities, obscurities, uncertainties | Antonyms: clarifications, explanations, elucidations | noun

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "clarification" (from "clarify," from Latin "clarificare," meaning "to make clear").
