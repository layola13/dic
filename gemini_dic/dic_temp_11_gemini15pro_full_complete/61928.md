emulative:/ˈɛmjʊleɪtɪv/| adj.|竞争的，仿效的，好胜的|Inclined or tending to emulate; characterized by emulation.  Seeking to equal or excel, especially through imitation.|He had an emulative spirit and always strived to be the best in his class. 他有一种好胜心，总是力求成为班上最好的。|近义词：competitive, imitative, ambitious, aspiring|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, unambitious|词性: adjective
Etymology: Late Middle English: from Latin aemulativus, from aemulari ‘rival, imitate’.
