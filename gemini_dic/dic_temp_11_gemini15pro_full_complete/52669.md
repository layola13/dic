digitorium:/ˌdɪdʒɪˈtɔːriəm/| n. |指套; 指骨|A sheath-like covering for a finger or toe, especially one used for protection or support. It can also refer to the part of a glove or mitten that covers a finger.|She wore a digitorium on her injured finger to help it heal. 她戴着指套来帮助受伤的手指愈合。|Synonyms: finger stall, finger cot, finger cover|Antonyms: N/A|Noun


digitorium:/ˌdɪdʒɪˈtɔːriəm/| n. |指骨;趾骨|In anatomy, a digitorium can refer to a finger or toe bone (phalanx).|The digitorium of his little finger was fractured. 他小指的指骨骨折了。|Synonyms: phalanx|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
