cosmography:/kɒzˈmɒɡrəfi/ | n. |宇宙志，宇宙学|The description of the universe, especially in its structure and relation of its parts.|例句：Early cosmography often mixed scientific observation with mythology. 早期的宇宙志常常将科学观测与神话混杂在一起。

例句：His book offered a detailed cosmography of the known world at that time. 他的书详细描述了当时已知世界的宇宙结构。|近义词：cosmology, uranography |反义词: N/A |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cosmographia*, from Ancient Greek  κοσμογραφία (kosmographía, “description of the universe”), from κόσμος (kósmos, “world, universe; order, ornament”) + -γραφία (-graphía, “writing, description”), from γράφω (gráphō, “I write”).
