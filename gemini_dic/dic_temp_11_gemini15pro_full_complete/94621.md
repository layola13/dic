imprejudicate:/ɪmˈprɛdʒʊdɪkɪt/
adjective
无偏见的；公平的；不带成见的
Not having or showing prejudice; impartial.
The jury must be impredjudicate and consider only the evidence presented in court.  陪审团必须保持公正，只考虑法庭上提出的证据。

近义词:unbiased, impartial, neutral, objective, fair
反义词:prejudiced, biased, partial, subjective, unfair
词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *impraeiudicatus, from in- 'not' + praejudicatus 'prejudged'.
