echelon:/ˈeʃəlɒn/ (UK), /ˈeʃəlɑːn/ (US)| n.|梯队；等级；阶层|A level or rank in an organization, a profession, or society.|The upper echelons of the company are very secretive about their plans. 公司的高层对他们的计划非常保密。|Synonyms: level, rank, tier, grade, stratum|Antonyms: none|Noun

echelon:/ˈeʃəlɒn/ (UK), /ˈeʃəlɑːn/ (US)| adj.|梯形的；阶梯式的|Arranged in a series of levels or steps, like an echelon formation.|The troops advanced in echelon formation. 部队以梯队队形前进。|Synonyms: tiered, stepped, staggered|Antonyms: none|Adjective
