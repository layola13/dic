droll:/droʊl/| adj. n.|滑稽古怪的，小丑|Something droll is amusing or funny, often in a strange or unusual way. |The play was full of droll characters and witty dialogue. 这部戏充满了滑稽古怪的角色和妙语连珠的对白。|近义词：amusing, comical, whimsical, quirky|反义词：serious, solemn, dull|词性:形容词, 名词

drolls:/droʊlz/| n.|滑稽剧；滑稽人物|Plural form of "droll," referring to short comic sketches or farces, or the actors who perform them.|The traveling players performed a series of drolls to the delight of the villagers.  巡回演出者表演了一系列滑稽剧，让村民们乐开了花。|近义词：farces, skits, comedians|反义词：tragedies, dramas|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *drolle*, "funny, comical,"  possibly from Middle Dutch *drolle*,  "imp, goblin, mischievous creature."
