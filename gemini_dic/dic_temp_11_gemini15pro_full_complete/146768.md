ETYMOLOGY->
From *over-* + *hype* + *-ed*.  *Over-* signifies "excessive" or "too much". *Hype* originated in the mid-20th century as slang, likely short for "hypodermic needle," referring to injecting something artificially, like excitement or publicity. *-ed* is a suffix used to form adjectives from verbs.
