blunderful:/ˈblʌndərfəl/| adj.|极好的；绝妙的（带有讽刺意味，指因愚蠢的错误而造成的糟糕局面）|A humorous blend of "blunder" and "wonderful," used ironically to describe something so bad it's almost comical.|例句：That was a blunderful attempt at parking, you managed to hit three cars at once! 那次停车真是“精彩绝伦”，你竟然一下子撞了三辆车！


例句：He made a blunderful decision to invest all his money in that failing company. 他做了“英明”的决定，把所有的钱都投资在那家倒闭的公司里。


近义词：disastrous, catastrophic, calamitous, ill-fated|反义词: fortunate, successful, brilliant|词性:形容词
