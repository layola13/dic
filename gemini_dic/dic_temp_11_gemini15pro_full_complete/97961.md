innumeracies:/ɪˈnjuːmərəsiːz/
n.
缺乏计算能力; 数学盲
lack of ability to understand and use numbers; illiteracy with numbers
Many adults suffer from innumeracy, finding it difficult to interpret statistics or manage their finances. 许多成年人缺乏计算能力，难以理解统计数据或管理财务。
Near synonyms: innumeracy, mathematical illiteracy
Antonyms: numeracy, mathematical proficiency
词性: 名词
