acceleration:/əkˌseləˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 加速，加快|The acceleration of the car was impressive. 这辆车的加速令人印象深刻。| An increase in speed or rate.  The rate at which something increases in velocity. |例句：The acceleration of the car was impressive. 这辆车的加速令人印象深刻。

例句：The new policy led to an acceleration of economic growth. 新政策促进了经济的快速增长。


近义词：speedup, quickening, hastening |反义词: deceleration, slowing, retardation |词性:noun
