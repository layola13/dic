nonefficiencies:/ˌnɒnɪˈfɪʃənsiz/
|n.|低效, 无效率|The report highlighted the nonefficiencies in the current system. 报告强调了当前系统中的低效之处。|English Explanation: The lack of efficiency; the state of being inefficient.  It refers to situations where resources are not being used effectively, leading to wasted time, effort, or money.|例句：The nonefficiencies in the production process led to significant losses. 生产过程中的低效导致了重大损失。

|例句：Addressing the nonefficiencies in the system is crucial for improving productivity.  解决系统中的低效对于提高生产力至关重要。
|近义词：inefficiencies, inadequacies, shortcomings|反义词：efficiencies, effectiveness|词性: 名词
