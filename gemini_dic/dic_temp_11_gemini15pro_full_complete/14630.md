avoidances:/əˈvɔɪdənsɪz/
noun
避免,回避,规避
The act of avoiding something or someone.
例句：His avoidance of eye contact made her suspicious. 他避免眼神接触让她起了疑心。

例句：Tax avoidance is a serious issue. 逃税是一个严重的问题。

近义词：evasion, shunning, dodging
反义词：confrontation, acceptance
词性:n.
