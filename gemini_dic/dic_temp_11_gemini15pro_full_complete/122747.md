moistless:/ˈmɔɪstləs/
adjective
无水分的，干燥的
lacking moisture; dry.
The desert air was hot and moistless. 沙漠的空气炎热干燥。
近义词: arid, dry, parched, desiccated
反义词: moist, damp, humid, wet
词性: adjective

词源: moist + -less.  "Moist" comes from Old French "moiste" (moist, damp), ultimately from Latin "musteus" (new, fresh, young).  "-less" is a suffix meaning "without" or "lacking," from Old English "-lēas."
