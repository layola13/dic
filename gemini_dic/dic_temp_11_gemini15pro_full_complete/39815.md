conspectus:/kənˈspɛktəs/| n.|概要，纲要，一览|a summary or outline of a subject, especially a short, systematic survey of a topic|The professor provided a conspectus of the course at the beginning of the semester. 教授在学期开始时提供了一份课程概要。|synonym: summary, overview, synopsis, abstract|antonym: detail, elaboration|noun

conspectus:/kənˈspɛktəs/| n.|概观，综述|a general view or survey of something|The report provided a conspectus of the current economic situation. 这份报告概述了当前的经济形势。|synonym: overview, survey, panorama|antonym: detail, minutiae|noun
