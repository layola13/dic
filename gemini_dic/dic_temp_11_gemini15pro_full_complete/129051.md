nitrified:/ˈnaɪtrɪfaɪd/
adjective
硝化的
converted into nitrates; treated with nitrates.
例句：The nitrified soil is rich in nutrients for plants.  硝化土壤富含植物所需的营养。

例句：The wastewater treatment plant nitrified the sewage before releasing it into the river. 污水处理厂在将污水排入河流之前对其进行了硝化处理。

近义词: oxidized, converted
反义词: denitrified, reduced
词性: adjective
