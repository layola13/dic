disillusionize:/ˌdɪsɪˈluːʒənaɪz/
verb
使幻想破灭；使清醒
To free from illusion or naive belief; disenchant.
The harsh realities of the business world soon disillusionized the young entrepreneur.  残酷的商业现实很快使这位年轻的企业家幻想破灭。
