nonparliamentary:/ˌnɒnpɑːrləˈment(ə)ri/ /ˌnɑːnpɑːrləˈment(ə)ri/
adjective
非议会的，违反议会规则的，不适合议会程序的
Not in accordance with the rules or customs of a parliament or other legislative assembly; unsuitable for parliamentary proceedings.
His nonparliamentary language resulted in his removal from the chamber.  他使用了非议会语言，结果被逐出会场。
The speaker ruled the remark nonparliamentary.  议长裁定该言论不符合议会规则。
Synonyms: unparliamentary, improper, unsuitable, indecorous, unseemly
Antonyms: parliamentary, proper, suitable, decorous, seemly
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + parliamentary. "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Parliamentary" comes from the Old French word "parlement," meaning "speaking, talk, conference," ultimately from the verb "parler," meaning "to speak."
