nystagmus:/nɪˈstæɡməs/| n. |眼球震颤|Involuntary rapid eye movements, often from side to side, up and down, or in a circular motion.|The doctor observed nystagmus in the patient's eyes after the head injury. 医生观察到头部受伤后病人的眼睛出现了眼球震颤。|Synonyms:  oscillopsia, eye flutter, eye tremor|Antonyms:  ophthalmostasis (eye fixation)|noun
