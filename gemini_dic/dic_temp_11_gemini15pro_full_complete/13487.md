attaboy:/ˌætəˈbɔɪ/ | int. | 好小子！/好样的！/干得好！| An exclamation of encouragement or approval, typically addressed to a male.  It combines "at" and "boy". | Attaboy, John! You won the race! 好小子，约翰！你赢得了比赛！| Attaboy, son, you hit that ball a mile! 好样的，儿子，你把球打得老远！| Near Synonyms: good job, well done, way to go | Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for expressions of praise) | interjection
