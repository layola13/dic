nurture: /ˈnɜːrtʃər/ (British) /ˈnɜːrtʃər/ (American)| n.|养育，培育；营养物|The process of caring for and encouraging the growth or development of someone or something.|例句：The nurture she received as a child shaped her personality. 她童年受到的养育塑造了她的性格。|近义词：upbringing, training, sustenance|反义词：neglect|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *norturen*, from Old French *norrir, nurrir* "to nourish, educate, bring up," from Latin *nutrire* "to suckle, nourish," related to *nutrix* "nurse."
