ayond: /əˈjɑːnd/
adjective
在...的那一边；超过；越过
situated further away than; on the far side of
例句：The village lay ayond the river. 村庄在河的那一边。

例句：His understanding is ayond mine. 他的理解力超过我的。

近义词：beyond, past, further than
反义词：near, close, within
词性：adjective
