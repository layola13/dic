casterless:/ˈkæstərləs/
adjective
无脚轮的，没有脚轮的
Lacking casters or wheels.
The casterless chair was difficult to move across the room.  无脚轮的椅子很难在房间里移动。
The heavy, casterless table had to be dragged across the floor.  这张笨重的无脚轮桌子不得不在地板上拖来拖去。
Near Synonyms: fixed, immobile, stationary
Antonyms: wheeled, mobile, rolling
词性: 形容词
