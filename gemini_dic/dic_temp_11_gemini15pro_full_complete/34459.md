clavier:/ˈklævɪə(r)/ /ˈkleɪviər/ | n. |键盘（尤指钢琴、管风琴等）;  |A keyboard, especially that of a piano, organ, or harpsichord.|  She ran her fingers lightly over the clavier. 她轻轻地用手指划过键盘。| 近义词: keyboard, keys | 反义词:  (无直接反义词) | 词性: 名词

词源:  From French *clavier*, from Old French *clavier* (“key, lock, keystone”), from Late Latin *clāvis* (“key”), from Latin *clāvis* (“key”).
