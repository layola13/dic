antitraditionalist:/ˌæntitrəˈdɪʃənəlɪst/| n. adj.|反传统主义者，反传统的|A person who opposes or rejects traditional beliefs, customs, or practices.  Someone who challenges established norms and advocates for change.|例句：The antitraditionalist artist challenged the conventions of classical painting.  这位反传统主义艺术家挑战了古典绘画的传统。

例句：She was considered an antitraditionalist in her community for her unconventional lifestyle.  由于她不传统的生活方式，她在社区里被认为是一个反传统主义者。|近义词：iconoclast, rebel, nonconformist, maverick|反义词：traditionalist, conformist|词性:名词，形容词
