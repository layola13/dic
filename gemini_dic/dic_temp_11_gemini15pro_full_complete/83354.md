gyrators:/ˈdʒaɪreɪtərz/
n.
旋转器，回转器，陀螺仪
A gyrator is a passive, linear, lossless two-port electrical network element proposed in 1948 by Bernard D. H. Tellegen as a hypothetical fifth linear element after the resistor, capacitor, inductor, and ideal transformer.
Gyrator circuits are used extensively in active filter design.
gyrators are used in electronic circuits. 旋转器用于电子电路中。
gyrators are essential components in some filters. 旋转器是某些滤波器中的重要组成部分。

近义词:rotators, twisters
反义词:None
词性:noun
