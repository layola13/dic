hootenanny:/ˈhuːtənæni/ | n. | 民间音乐聚会，热闹的社交聚会 | An informal gathering with folk music and sometimes dancing. | We're having a hootenanny at the community center this weekend.  这个周末我们将在社区中心举办一场民间音乐聚会。 |  例句：The annual hootenanny is always a lot of fun. 年度民间音乐聚会总是充满乐趣。 | 近义词：party, gathering, celebration, get-together, social | 反义词: wake, funeral | 词性: 名词

hootenanny:/ˈhuːtənæni/ | n. |  喧闹，吵闹 |  A noisy or boisterous activity or situation. |  What a hootenanny they're having next door! 隔壁真吵闹！| 例句：The construction site was a real hootenanny all day long. 建筑工地整天都很吵。 | 近义词：commotion, racket, uproar, din, hubbub | 反义词: silence, quiet | 词性: 名词
