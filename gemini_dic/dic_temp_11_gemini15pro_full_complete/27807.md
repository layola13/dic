canonical:/kəˈnɒnɪkəl/| adj.|规范的，经典的，权威的|Following a set of rules or a fixed standard; accepted as being accurate and authoritative; relating to the set of books, plays, poems, etc., that are traditionally considered to be very important.|The canonical gospels are Matthew, Mark, Luke, and John.|近义词：standard, accepted, authoritative, orthodox, established|反义词：unorthodox, unconventional, apocryphal|词性:形容词
