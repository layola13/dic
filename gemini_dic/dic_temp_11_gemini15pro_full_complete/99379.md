The two warring factions interdevoured each other, leaving their kingdom in ruins.  这两个交战派系互相吞噬，使他们的王国化为废墟。

近义词:annihilate each other, destroy each other, consume each other
反义词:coexist, cooperate, support each other
词性:v.

词源: "inter-" (Latin, meaning "between, among, mutually") + "devour" (from Old French "devorer," meaning "to swallow up, consume").
