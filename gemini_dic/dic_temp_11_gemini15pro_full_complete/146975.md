overjoyfully: /ˌoʊvərˈdʒɔɪfəli/ | adv. | 非常高兴地，欣喜若狂地 |  In a manner expressing extreme joy; with overwhelming happiness. | She laughed overjoyfully at the good news. 她听到好消息欣喜若狂地大笑起来。|  joyously, ecstatically, blissfully | sadly, sorrowfully | adverb

Etymology:
From "over-" (excessively) + "joyful" (full of joy) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Joy" ultimately derives from the Old French "joie," from Latin "gaudia," plural of "gaudium" (joy).
