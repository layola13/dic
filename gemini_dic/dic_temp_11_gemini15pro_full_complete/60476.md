ejectable:/ɪˈdʒɛktəbəl/
adjective
可弹出的；可射出的
Capable of being ejected or thrown out.
The pilot activated the ejectable seat. 飞行员启动了弹射座椅。
The plane is equipped with an ejectable canopy. 这架飞机配备了可弹出的座舱盖。
synonyms:removable, detachable, releasable
antonyms:fixed, permanent, non-detachable
word type:adjective
