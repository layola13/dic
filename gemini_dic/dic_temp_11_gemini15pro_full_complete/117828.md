mellifluent:/məˈlɪfluənt/| adj.|流畅的，甜美的，悦耳的|Flowing smoothly and sweetly, like honey; smooth and persuasive.|Her mellifluent voice soothed the crying baby. 她甜美的声音安抚了哭闹的婴儿。|近义词：euphonious, dulcet, smooth, harmonious|反义词：harsh, grating, discordant|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *mellifluus*, from *melli-* (from *mel*, "honey") + *-fluus* (from *fluere*, "to flow").  Meaning "flowing like honey."
