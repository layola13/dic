moans:/moʊnz/ | n. | 呻吟声，抱怨声 | A low, mournful sound expressing pain or suffering.  A complaint. | The moans of the injured filled the air.  受伤者的呻吟声弥漫在空气中。| synonyms: groans, sighs, complaints, whimpers | antonyms: cheers, laughter, applause | noun
moans:/moʊnz/ | v. | 呻吟，抱怨 | To make a low, mournful sound expressing pain or suffering. To complain. | He moaned in pain as he tried to sit up. 他试图坐起来时痛苦地呻吟着。| synonyms: groans, sighs, complains, whimpers | antonyms: cheers, laughs, applauds | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *monen*, from Old English *mānian* ("to moan, lament, complain"), from Proto-Germanic *mainaną* ("to complain, mean"), from Proto-Indo-European *moy-/*mey- ("to moan").
