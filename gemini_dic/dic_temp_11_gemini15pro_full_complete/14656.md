avowed:/əˈvaʊd/| adj.|公开承认的；公然的|openly acknowledged; declared|He is an avowed supporter of the president. 他是总统的公开支持者。|例句：She is an avowed vegetarian. 她是一位公开的素食主义者。|近义词：declared, admitted, acknowledged, professed, sworn|反义词：secret, unacknowledged, hidden, undeclared|词性:形容词
词源: From the Old French *avouer* (to avow, confess, acknowledge), from Latin *advocare* (to summon, call to one's aid), from *ad-* (to) + *vocare* (to call).
