accept:/əkˈsept/ | vt. | 接受，同意 | To receive or take (something offered).  |  She accepted the job offer. 她接受了工作邀请。

| 近义词: receive, admit, approve, acknowledge | 反义词: reject, refuse, decline | 词性: 动词

accept:/əkˈsept/ | vt. | 承认，认可 | To regard as true, proper, or inevitable. | He accepted the blame for the accident. 他承担了事故的责任。

| 近义词: acknowledge, recognize, admit | 反义词: deny, dispute | 词性: 动词
