irrevocabilities:/ˌɪrɪˈvɒkəˈbɪlɪtiz/
n.
不可撤销性；不可改变性
The state or quality of being irrevocable; the fact that something cannot be changed or reversed.
The irrevocability of the decision caused much distress.  这个决定的不可撤销性造成了很大的痛苦。
The lawyer stressed the irrevocability of the contract. 律师强调了合同的不可撤销性。
近义词: finality, conclusiveness, unchangeableness
反义词: reversibility, changeability
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

词源:
From irrevocable + -ity.  Irrevocable comes from in- (not) + revocabilis (capable of being revoked), from revocare (to recall, revoke).  Ultimately from Latin.
