nondiscernments:/ˌnɒndɪˈsɜːnmənts/ | n. | 非识别; 不辨别; 无法区分 | Lack of discernment or the inability to distinguish between things. |  His nondiscernments in art led him to buy a fake masterpiece.  他不懂艺术，结果买了一幅假名画。 | 近义词: indiscrimination, lack of discrimination,  undiscriminatingness,  failure to distinguish | 反义词: discernment, discrimination,  perception,  insight | 词性: 名词

词源:  "Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "discernment" (from Old French "decernement," ultimately from Latin "discernere" - "to separate, distinguish").
