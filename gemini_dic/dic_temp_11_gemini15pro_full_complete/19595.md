bigha:/ˈbiːɡə/ | n. | 比加 (一种南亚土地面积单位) | A unit of land area used in parts of South Asia, varying in size from region to region but generally equivalent to about one-third of an acre. | Example 1:  The farmer owned five bighas of land.  这个农民拥有五比加的土地。

Example 2:  He bought another two bighas to expand his farm. 他又买了两个比加来扩大他的农场。 | Near synonyms: acre, hectare | Antonyms: N/A (unit of measurement, no direct opposite) | Noun
