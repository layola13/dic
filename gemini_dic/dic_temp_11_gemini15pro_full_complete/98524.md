insteep:/ɪnˈstiːp/
verb
浸泡，浸透
to soak something in liquid
The tea bags were insteeped in hot water. 茶包浸泡在热水中。
近义词: soak, immerse, submerge
反义词: dry, dehydrate
词性: verb

词源: From "in" + "steep" (Old English *stēpan, to soak)
