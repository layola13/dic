embogue:/ɛmˈboʊɡ/ | v. | 流出，注入；排放 | To discharge or empty (as a stream) into a larger body of water or an open space. | The river embogues into the sea. 这条河流注入大海。 | 近义词: discharge, empty, flow, debouch | 反义词: absorb, receive | verb

embogue:/ɛmˈboʊɡ/ | n. | 河口，出海口 | The mouth of a river or stream. | The embogue of the river was wide and sandy. 这条河的河口又宽又沙。| 近义词: mouth, outlet, estuary | 反义词: source, headwater | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *embouguer*, from *em-* (in, into) + *bouguer* (to bulge, to stir), of uncertain origin.
