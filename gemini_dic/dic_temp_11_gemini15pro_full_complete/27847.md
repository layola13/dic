canonises:/ˈkænənaɪzɪz/
verb
正式宣布（死者）为圣徒；高度赞扬
officially declare (a dead person) a saint; regard with the greatest respect and admiration
The church canonised him in the 15th century.  教会在15世纪将他封为圣徒。
glorifies, exalts, sanctifies
condemns, vilifies
v.
