narragansetts: /ˌnærəˈɡænsɪts/ | n. | 纳拉甘西特人；纳拉甘西特语 |  A member of an Algonquian people formerly inhabiting Rhode Island and adjacent parts of Massachusetts and Connecticut. Also: the Algonquian language of this people. | The Narragansetts were skilled farmers and fishermen. 纳拉甘西特人都是熟练的农民和渔民。|  The Narragansett language is now extinct. 纳拉甘西特语现在已经灭绝了。 | synonyms:  Narragansett Indian, Narraganset | antonyms: N/A | noun
