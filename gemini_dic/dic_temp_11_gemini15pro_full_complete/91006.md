husbanders:/ˈhʌzbəndərz/
noun
节俭者；精打细算的人；善于经营者
One who husbands resources; a thrifty or economical manager.
The husbanders of the community's resources ensured its survival through the tough winter.  这个社区资源的精打细算者确保了它在严冬中的生存。
Near Synonyms: economizers, savers, conservers
Antonyms: spendthrifts, wasters
