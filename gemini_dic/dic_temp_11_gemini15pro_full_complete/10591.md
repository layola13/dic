apprentices:/əˈprɛntɪsɪz/| n.|学徒，徒弟|A person who is learning a trade or profession, especially by working for someone in that trade or profession for a fixed period of time.|The apprentices were eager to learn from the master craftsman.|近义词：trainee, learner, novice, beginner|反义词：expert, master, professional|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *aprentiz* (modern French *apprenti*), from *aprendre* "to learn," from Latin *apprehendere* "to grasp, seize, understand."
