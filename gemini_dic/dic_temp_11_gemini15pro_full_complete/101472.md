iridal:/ˈɪrɪdəl/ | adj. | 虹膜的; 彩虹色的 | Relating to the iris of the eye; having the colors of the rainbow. | The iridal pigments give the eye its color.  虹膜色素赋予眼睛颜色。 | The hummingbird's feathers shimmered with iridal hues. 蜂鸟的羽毛闪烁着彩虹般的色调。 | 近义词: iridescent, rainbow-like | 反义词: dull, colorless | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  From Latin *iridalis, from iris "rainbow" + -al. Ultimately from Greek ἶρις (îris, “rainbow”).
