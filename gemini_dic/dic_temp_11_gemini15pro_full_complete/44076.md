crinoidea:/kraɪˈnɔɪdiə/ | n. | 海百合纲 |A class of marine invertebrates, in the phylum Echinodermata, characterized by a cup-shaped body and feathery arms. They are typically attached to the seabed by a stalk.|  Crinoids are ancient animals, with fossils dating back millions of years. 海百合是古老的动物，化石可以追溯到数百万年前。

Crinoids come in a variety of vibrant colors. 海百合有多种鲜艳的颜色。| 近义词: sea lilies (common name) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
