fidgeter:/ˈfɪdʒɪtər/ |n.| 坐立不安的人；爱烦躁的人 |A person who fidgets; someone who is restless and uneasy. |例句：He's a terrible fidgeter in meetings; he just can't sit still. 他在开会时总是坐立不安，根本无法安静地坐着。

例句：My son is a fidgeter and has trouble concentrating in class. 我儿子好动，上课难以集中注意力。| 近义词：restless person, squirmer |反义词：calm person, composed individual |词性：名词

fidget:/ˈfɪdʒɪt/ |v.| 烦躁不安；坐立不安 |To make small movements, especially of the hands and feet, through nervousness or impatience. | 例句：She fidgeted nervously with her pen during the interview.  面试期间，她紧张地摆弄着她的笔。

例句：Stop fidgeting and pay attention! 别烦躁了，注意听！|近义词：squirm, twitch |反义词：relax, be still |词性：动词

fidgety:/ˈfɪdʒɪti/ |adj.| 烦躁的；坐立不安的 |Restless and uneasy. |例句：The children were getting fidgety waiting for their dinner. 孩子们等着吃饭，变得烦躁不安。

例句：I always get fidgety during long flights. 长途飞行中我总是坐立不安。|近义词：restless, antsy |反义词：calm, patient |词性：形容词
