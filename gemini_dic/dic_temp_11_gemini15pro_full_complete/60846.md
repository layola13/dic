electrotonous:/ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒtənəs/
adjective
单调的，沉闷的
lacking in variation in pitch or tone; monotonous.
The professor's electrotonous lecture nearly put the students to sleep.  教授单调的讲课几乎让学生们睡着了。
The electrotonous drone of the machinery made it difficult to concentrate.  机器单调的嗡嗡声使人难以集中注意力。
近义词:monotonous, tedious, dull, boring
反义词:varied, interesting, engaging, stimulating
词性:adjective
