nonreligiousness:/ˌnɒnrɪˈlɪdʒəsnəs/ | n. |非宗教性，世俗性|The state of not being religious; lack of religious belief or practice. |  Many millennials cite nonreligiousness as a defining characteristic. 许多千禧一代将非宗教性视为一个重要的特征。

| Example:  Her nonreligiousness doesn't prevent her from having a strong moral compass. 她的非宗教性并不妨碍她拥有坚定的道德指南针。


| Synonym: secularism, irreligion, atheism, agnosticism | Antonym: religiosity, piety, devoutness | Noun
