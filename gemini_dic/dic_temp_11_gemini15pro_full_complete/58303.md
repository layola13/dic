dry-cleans:/ˈdraɪ klinz/
verb
干洗
cleans (something) using a chemical solvent rather than water.
My suit dry-cleans well. 我的西装很适合干洗。
近义词: chemically cleans
反义词: washes, launders
词性:v.


dry-cleaned:/ˈdraɪ kliːnd/
adjective
干洗过的
cleaned with chemical solvents rather than with water
This garment is dry-cleaned only. 这件衣服只能干洗。
近义词: chemically cleaned
反义词: washed, laundered
词性:adj.


dry-cleaning:/ˌdraɪˈkliːnɪŋ/
noun
干洗
the process of cleaning clothes using a chemical solvent rather than water.
I took my coat to the dry-cleaning. 我把我的外套送去干洗了。
近义词: chemical cleaning
反义词: washing, laundering
词性:n.

词源:  "dry-clean" emerged in the early 20th century, combining "dry" with "clean" to describe a cleaning process without water.  The use of solvents for cleaning textiles predates this term, but the specific term "dry-cleaning" solidified with the development and popularization of modern dry-cleaning methods.
