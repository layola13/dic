insatiately:/ɪnˈseɪʃəbli/ | adv. |贪得无厌地，不知足地 | In a way that is impossible to satisfy. | She read insatiately, devouring book after book. 她如饥似渴地阅读，一本接一本地读。| He insatiately pursued knowledge and experience. 他不知疲倦地追求知识和经验。| 近义词：voraciously, ravenously, greedily | 反义词：moderately, temperately | 词性: 副词

词源：From Late Latin insatiabilis, from in- ‘not’ + satiabilis (from satiare ‘satisfy’).
