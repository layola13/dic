non-homogeneity:/ˌnɒnˌhɒmədʒəˈniːɪti/ (US) /ˌnɒnˌhɒmədʒəˈneɪɪti/ (UK)| n.|非均匀性，不均一性|The lack of uniformity or consistency in a substance or system.  The state of not being homogeneous. |The non-homogeneity of the soil caused problems for the construction project. 土壤的不均匀性给建设项目带来了问题。|Synonyms: heterogeneity,  disparity,  variance,  inconsistency,  irregularity|Antonyms: homogeneity, uniformity, consistency|noun


non-homogeneous:/ˌnɒnˌhɒməˈdʒiːniəs/ (US) /ˌnɒnˌhɒməˈdʒiːniəs/ (UK)| adj.|非均匀的，不均一的|Not of uniform structure and composition throughout. Consisting of different or dissimilar elements or parts.|The mixture was non-homogeneous, with clumps of undissolved sugar. 混合物是非均匀的，有一些未溶解的糖块。|Synonyms: heterogeneous,  dissimilar,  varied,  inconsistent,  irregular|Antonyms: homogeneous, uniform, consistent|adjective


Etymology:
From non- + homogeneity.  Homogeneity comes from the Greek "homogenes", meaning "of the same kind".
