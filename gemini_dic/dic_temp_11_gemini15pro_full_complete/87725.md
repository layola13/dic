hexers:/ˈhɛksərz/| n.|施法者，使用十六进制的人|People who practice witchcraft or magic, especially those who use hexadecimal notation in computer programming or other technical fields.  This term blends "hex" (short for hexadecimal) with "-er" (a suffix denoting a person who performs an action).|The hexers gathered around the bonfire. 施法者们聚集在篝火旁。|The hexers were skilled at manipulating hexadecimal code. 这些使用十六进制的人能熟练地操作十六进制代码。|近义词：witches, sorcerers, programmers, coders|反义词:none|词性:noun
