coranach:/ˈkɒrənɑːx/ /kəˈrɑːnəx/| n. |挽歌；哀歌；悲歌|A mournful dirge or lament for the dead, especially one with a slow, wailing melody.|Example 1: The haunting coranach echoed through the glen as the villagers mourned their lost chieftain. 村民们为失去的首领哀悼，凄凉的挽歌回荡在峡谷中。

Example 2:  A lone piper played a coranach at the graveside. 一位孤独的吹笛者在墓旁吹奏挽歌。|近义词：dirge, lament, elegy, requiem|反义词: celebration, paean, hymn|词性:名词
