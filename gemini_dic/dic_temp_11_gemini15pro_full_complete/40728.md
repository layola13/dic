convexities:/kənˈvɛksɪtiz/
|n.|凸面，凸度|The plural form of convexity, referring to multiple outward curves or bulges.|The lens has several slight convexities.  这个透镜有几个轻微的凸起。|Synonyms: bulges, protuberances, prominences|Antonyms: concavities, depressions, hollows|noun


convexities:/kənˈvɛksɪtiz/
|n.|（金融）凸性|In finance, it refers to the non-linear relationship between bond prices and interest rates.|The portfolio manager considered the convexities of the bonds before making the investment.  投资组合经理在进行投资前考虑了债券的凸性。|Synonyms: curvature, second derivative (in a financial context)|Antonyms: concavity (in a financial context)|noun
