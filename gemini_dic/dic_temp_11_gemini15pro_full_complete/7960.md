antheriferous:/ænθəˈrɪfərəs/
adjective
生花药的；有花药的
Bearing anthers; producing anthers.
The antheriferous part of the stamen is essential for plant reproduction.  雄蕊的生花药部分对植物繁殖至关重要。
The botanist identified the antheriferous structures under the microscope.  植物学家在显微镜下识别了生花药的结构。

近义词: staminate, pollen-bearing
反义词:  pistillate
