microcephalic: /ˌmaɪkroʊsəˈfælɪk/| adj. |小头的，患小头症的|Having an abnormally small head and underdeveloped brain.|The child was diagnosed as microcephalic soon after birth.  这个孩子出生后不久就被诊断为小头症。|Synonyms: small-headed|Antonyms: macrocephalic|adjective

microcephaly: /ˌmaɪkroʊˈsɛfəli/| n. |小头症|A condition in which the head is abnormally small, usually as a result of abnormal brain development.|Microcephaly can be caused by genetic factors or infections during pregnancy. 小头症可能是由遗传因素或怀孕期间的感染引起的。|Synonyms: small-headedness|Antonyms: macrocephaly|noun

**ETYMOLOGY**

From micro- (small) + -cephalic (relating to the head).  The prefix "micro-" comes from the Greek "mikros" meaning "small," and "-cephalic" comes from the Greek "kephalē" meaning "head."
