gaber:/ˈɡeɪbər/| v. |闲聊，瞎扯|To talk idly or foolishly; chatter. |例句：They spent the afternoon gabbering about nothing in particular. 他们一下午都在闲聊些无关紧要的事情。

例句：The children were gabbering excitedly about the upcoming holiday. 孩子们兴奋地聊着即将到来的假期。|近义词：chatter, prattle, babble, jabber|反义词: be quiet, be silent |词性: verb
