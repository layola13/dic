anaesthetist:/əˈniːsθətɪst/
n.
麻醉师
A medical professional trained to administer anesthetics.
The anaesthetist carefully monitored the patient's vital signs during the surgery. 麻醉师在手术过程中仔细监测病人的生命体征。
Before the operation, the anaesthetist explained the procedure to me. 手术前，麻醉师向我解释了手术过程。
近义词:anesthesiologist
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek "anaisthesia," meaning "lack of sensation," coined from "an-" (without) + "aisthesis" (sensation).
