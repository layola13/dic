antares:/ænˈtɛəriːz/ | n. |心宿二| A red supergiant star in the constellation Scorpius, the brightest star in that constellation and the 15th brightest star in the night sky. | Antares is a prominent star in the summer sky. 心宿二是夏季夜空中一颗很显眼的星星。| Antares is located near the center of the Milky Way galaxy. 心宿二位于银河系中心附近。| synonyms: Alpha Scorpii, Cor Scorpii | antonyms: N/A | noun
