heaven-sent:/ˈhɛvnˌsɛnt/
adjective
天赐的；恰到好处的；及时的
A welcome or needed thing that seems to have been provided by God or by chance.
例句：
The rain was heaven-sent for the farmers.
这场雨对农民来说真是及时雨。

例句：
His arrival was heaven-sent; we desperately needed help.
他的到来真是天赐良机，我们非常需要帮助。

近义词：providential, fortunate, opportune, timely, God-given, blessed
反义词：unfortunate, unlucky, ill-timed, inappropriate
