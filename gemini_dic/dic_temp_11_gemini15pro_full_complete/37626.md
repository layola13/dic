communistically:/ˌkɒmjʊˈnɪstɪkli/ /ˌkɑːmjʊˈnɪstɪkli/
adverb|共产主义地；共产式地|In a manner characteristic of or consistent with the principles of communism.|The country was run communistically, with all property belonging to the state. 这个国家以共产主义方式运行，所有财产都属于国家。|Synonyms: collectivistically, marxistically|Antonyms: capitalistically, individualistically|词性: 副词

communistically:/ˌkɒmjʊˈnɪstɪkli/ /ˌkɑːmjʊˈnɪstɪkli/
adverb|共产主义地；共产式地|Acting or thinking like a communist, advocating for a communist society.|They organized the farm communistically, sharing all profits and responsibilities equally. 他们以共产主义的方式组织农场，平等地分享所有利润和责任。|Synonyms: collectively, socialistly|Antonyms: privately, freely|词性: 副词
