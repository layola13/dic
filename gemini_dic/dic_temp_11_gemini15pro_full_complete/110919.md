lipogrammatic:/ˌlɪpəˈɡræmætɪk/
adjective
缺字体的，缺字母的
A lipogrammatic text is one that deliberately avoids using a particular letter or letters of the alphabet.
The author wrote a lipogrammatic novel, omitting the letter 'e'. 作者写了一部缺字小说，省略了字母“e”。
近义词:constrained, restricted
反义词:unconstrained, unrestricted
词性:adjective

lipogram:/ˈlɪpəɡrɑːm/
noun
缺字体，缺字母文
A lipogram is a piece of writing such as a poem or novel that excludes a particular letter or letters of the alphabet.
He challenged himself to write a lipogram excluding the letter 'a'. 他挑战自己写一篇不包含字母“a”的缺字体文章。
近义词:word puzzle
反义词:N/A
词性:noun
