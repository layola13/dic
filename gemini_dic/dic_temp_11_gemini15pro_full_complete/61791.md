emphasises:/ˈɛmfəsaɪzɪz/| v.|强调，着重|Gives special importance or prominence to (something).|The report emphasises the need for improved safety measures.  这份报告强调了加强安全措施的必要性。|
emphasizes:/ˈɛmfəˌsaɪzɪz/| v.|强调，着重|Gives special importance or prominence to (something).|She emphasized the importance of regular exercise. 她强调了定期锻炼的重要性。|
近义词:stress, highlight, underscore, point up|反义词:downplay, de-emphasize, understate|词性:verb

词源:
From Late Middle English: from Old French emphasizer, based on Greek emphasis ‘a showing forth’.
