afterward:/ˈæftərwərd/ | adv. | 之后，后来 | Subsequently, at a later time; following something else in time. |  The party was great, and afterward, we went for a walk. 派对很棒，之后我们去散步了。

Afterward, she regretted her hasty decision. 后来，她后悔自己仓促的决定。


近义词: subsequently, later, thereafter | 反义词: beforehand, previously | 词性: 副词
