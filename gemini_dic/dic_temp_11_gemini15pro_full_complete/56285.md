doggerel:/ˈdɒɡərəl/ (US) /ˈdɑːɡərəl/| n. |打油诗，蹩脚的诗|Trivial, poorly constructed verse; crude, simplistic poetry. |The poem he wrote for her birthday was pure doggerel, but she loved it anyway. 他为她生日写的诗纯粹是打油诗，但她还是很喜欢。|Synonyms: verse, jingle, rhyme, limerick|Antonyms: epic, masterpiece, classic|noun
doggerels:/ˈdɒɡərəlz/ (US) /ˈdɑːɡərəlz/| n. |打油诗，蹩脚的诗（复数形式，不常用）|Trivial, poorly constructed verses; crude, simplistic poetry (plural form, less common). |He entertained the children with silly doggerels he made up on the spot. 他用他当场编的愚蠢的打油诗逗孩子们开心。|Synonyms: verses, jingles, rhymes, limericks|Antonyms: epics, masterpieces, classics|noun
