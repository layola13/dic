circumscriptive:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈskrɪptɪv/| adj.|限制性的，限定的|Serving to circumscribe or restrict; limiting.  Defining something precisely, often by listing its characteristics.|The circumscriptive nature of the contract left little room for negotiation.  合同的限制性条款几乎没有留下任何谈判的余地。|近义词：restrictive, limiting, confining, defining|反义词：expansive, broad, unlimited, general|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin "circumscriptus," past participle of "circumscribere" (to draw a line around, encircle, confine), from "circum" (around) + "scribere" (to write).
