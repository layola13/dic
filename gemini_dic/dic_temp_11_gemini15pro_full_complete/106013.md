knaves:/neɪvz/| n. |恶棍，无赖；流氓（knave的复数）|A dishonest or unscrupulous man.|The knaves cheated the villagers out of their land. 这些恶棍骗走了村民们的土地。|Synonyms: rogue, scoundrel, villain, rascal |Antonyms: hero, gentleman, saint |Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *cnafa* (boy, servant), later acquiring a sense of "rogue, villain."  Cognate with German *Knabe* (boy).
