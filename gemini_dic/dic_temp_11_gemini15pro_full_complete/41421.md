cornered:/ˈkɔːnərd/ (US) /ˈkɔːnəd/ (UK)| adj.|被逼到角落的；陷入困境的；走投无路的|Forced into a difficult or inescapable position; trapped.|The police cornered the suspect in an alley. 警方在一个小巷里将嫌疑人逼入绝境。|近义词：trapped, ambushed, stuck, besieged|反义词：free, liberated|词性:形容词

cornered:/ˈkɔːnərd/ (US) /ˈkɔːnəd/ (UK)| v.|把…逼入困境；将…逼到角落|To force (someone or something) into a difficult or inescapable position.|The dog cornered the rat against the wall. 狗把老鼠逼到墙角。|近义词：trap, ambush|反义词：release, free|词性:动词
