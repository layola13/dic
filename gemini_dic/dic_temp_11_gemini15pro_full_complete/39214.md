conglobulate:/kənˈɡlɒbjʊleɪt/ /kənˈɡlɑːbjʊleɪt/| v. adj.|使凝结成球；成球状的|to gather or form into a rounded mass; formed into a rounded mass|The small particles conglobulated into larger lumps. 小颗粒凝结成更大的块状物。|Synonyms: agglomerate, coalesce, aggregate|Antonyms: disperse, separate, disintegrate|verb

conglobulate:/kənˈɡlɒbjʊleɪt/ /kənˈɡlɑːbjʊleɪt/| adj.|球状的|formed into a rounded mass|The artist created a conglobulate sculpture from recycled materials.  这位艺术家用回收材料创作了一个球状的雕塑。|Synonyms: spherical, globular, round|Antonyms: irregular, uneven, fragmented|adjective

Etymology: From Latin *conglobulatus*, past participle of *conglobulare* "to gather into a ball," from *con-* "together" + *globulus* "small ball," diminutive of *globus* "sphere, ball."
