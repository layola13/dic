feedback:/ˈfiːdbæk/| n. |反馈，意见|Information about reactions to a product, a person's performance of a task, etc. which is used as a basis for improvement.|The teacher gave me some feedback on my essay. 老师就我的论文给我提了一些意见。|Synonyms: response, reaction, input, comment, criticism|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "lack of response," "silence")|Noun

feedback:/ˈfiːdbæk/| v. |反馈，提供反馈|To provide feedback.|I'll feedback my thoughts to the team. 我会把我的想法反馈给团队。|Synonyms: report, respond, reply|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "withhold information," "suppress")|Verb
