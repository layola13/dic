legitimated:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmeɪtɪd/

legitimated:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmeɪtɪd/
|adj.|被合法化的；被授予合法地位的；被证明为合法的|Having been made legal or lawful; having been given legal status; having been shown to be lawful.|The legitimated use of medical marijuana is still controversial.  药用大麻的合法使用仍然存在争议。|legal, lawful, valid|illegal, unlawful, invalid|adjective
