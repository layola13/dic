astronomers:/əˈstrɒnəməz/
n.
天文学家
Scientists who study celestial objects, space, and the physical universe as a whole.
Astronomers have discovered a new planet. 天文学家们发现了一颗新的行星。
Astronomers spend years studying the universe. 天文学家花费数年研究宇宙。
近义词:astrophysicists, cosmologists
反义词: (无直接反义词)
词性:noun (plural)
