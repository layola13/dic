latinx: /ləˈtiːnɛks/  /læˈtiːnɛks/ | adj. n. | 拉丁美洲的，拉丁裔的；拉丁裔人士 |  A gender-neutral or nonbinary term referring to a person of Latin American origin or descent.  It is used as an alternative to Latino or Latina to be more inclusive of people who do not identify within the gender binary. | Example: Many Latinx students are the first in their families to attend college. 许多拉丁裔学生是家里第一个上大学的人。

Example: The Latinx community is diverse and vibrant. 拉丁裔群体多元而充满活力。

| 近义词: Hispanic, Latino/a/x, Latin American | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it refers to an ethnicity) | 词性: adjective, noun
