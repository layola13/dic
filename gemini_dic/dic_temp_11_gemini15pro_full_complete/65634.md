evangelize:/ɪˈvændʒəlaɪz/| v. |传福音，宣扬福音；使皈依；使热衷|To preach the gospel to; to convert to Christianity; to promote something with evangelical zeal.|Missionaries went to Africa to evangelize. 传教士去非洲传福音。|The company's CEO evangelized the new product to potential investors. 公司CEO向潜在投资者大力宣传新产品。|近义词：preach, proselytize, promote, advocate|反义词：suppress, discourage|词性:动词
