diabetes:/ˌdaɪəˈbiːtiːz/ /ˌdaɪəˈbiːtis/
n.|糖尿病|A metabolic disease in which the body's inability to produce any or enough insulin causes elevated levels of glucose in the blood.|My grandmother has diabetes and has to monitor her blood sugar levels. 我的祖母患有糖尿病，必须监测她的血糖水平。|hyperglycemia, high blood sugar|hypoglycemia, low blood sugar|noun
