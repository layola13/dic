benzosol:/ˈben.zoʊ.sɒl/ | n. |苯佐柳酯;愈创木酚苯甲酸酯|A white, crystalline powder used as an antiseptic and expectorant, especially in the treatment of tuberculosis.  It is the benzoic acid ester of guaiacol. |Example: Benzosol was historically prescribed for respiratory ailments. 苯佐柳酯过去曾被用于治疗呼吸系统疾病。|Synonym: guaiacol benzoate|Antonym: N/A|noun
