horsenail:/ˈhɔːrsneɪl/ | n. |马钉；蹄钉|A nail used to shoe a horse.  A nail driven through a horseshoe and into a horse's hoof to hold the shoe in place. |例句：The farrier carefully hammered the horsenail into the hoof.  钉蹄匠小心地把马钉敲进马蹄。

例句：The lost horsenail caused the rider to fall.  丢失的马钉导致骑手摔倒。|近义词：hoof nail|反义词: None|词性:noun
