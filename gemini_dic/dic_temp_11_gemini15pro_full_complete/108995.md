left-winger:/ˌleft ˈwɪŋər/| n.|左翼分子，左派人士|A person with left-wing political views, typically advocating social equality or reform.|Example: The left-winger argued for higher taxes on the wealthy.  左翼分子主张对富人征收更高的税。
