mutuality:/ˌmjuːtʃuˈælɪti/ |n.|相互性，互惠性|A feeling or relationship of shared trust, support, or understanding.  The quality or state of being mutual. |例句：There is a mutuality of interests between the two organizations. 这两个组织之间存在着共同的利益。

例句：The success of the project depends on the mutuality of the team members.  这个项目的成功取决于团队成员之间的相互配合。|近义词：reciprocity, interdependence, interrelation, correlation|反义词：independence, detachment, disconnection|词性: 名词
