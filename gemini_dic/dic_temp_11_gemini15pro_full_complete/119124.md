metropolitanship:/ˌmɛtrəˈpɒlɪtənʃɪp/
n.
大都市的身份或地位；大主教的职位
the state of being a metropolitan, especially the office of a metropolitan bishop
He held the metropolitanship of York for many years. 他多年来一直担任约克大主教的职位。

近义词: archbishopric, primacy
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps rural leadership or provincial leadership)
词性: noun
