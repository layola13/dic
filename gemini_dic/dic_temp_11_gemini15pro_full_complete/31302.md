chaplainships:/ˈtʃæplɪnʃɪps/
n.
牧师职位；随军牧师职位；牧师的任期
The position or office of a chaplain.  The term of office of a chaplain.
The university offers several chaplaincies. 该大学提供几个牧师职位。
He held the chaplaincy of the regiment for five years. 他担任该团的随军牧师五年。
近义词: chaplaincy, pastorate
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 secular leadership 世俗领导)
词性: noun
