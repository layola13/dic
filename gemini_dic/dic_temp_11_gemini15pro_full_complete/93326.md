ill-timed:/ˌɪlˈtaɪmd/ | adj. | 不合时宜的，不凑巧的 | Happening or done at a bad or inappropriate time. |  His ill-timed joke fell flat. 他不合时宜的笑话很尴尬。 | synonyms: inappropriate, inopportune, unfortunate, untimely | antonyms: timely, opportune, appropriate, well-timed | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From ill- + timed.  "Ill" meaning bad or unfavorable, and "timed" referring to the timing of something.
