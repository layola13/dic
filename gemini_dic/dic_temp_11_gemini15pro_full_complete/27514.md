camphoraceous: /ˌkæmfəˈreɪʃəs/ | adj. | 樟脑的，有樟脑气味的 | Smelling of or resembling camphor. |  The ointment had a strong, camphoraceous scent.  这种药膏有很浓的樟脑味。 | The air was thick with a camphoraceous aroma. 空气中弥漫着浓烈的樟脑香气。 | 近义词: camphoric | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "odorless," "fragrance-free") | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From camphor + -aceous (suffix meaning "of the nature of, resembling").  Camphor itself comes from Medieval Latin camphora, from Arabic kāfūr, ultimately from Sanskrit karpūra.
