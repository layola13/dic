inaugurals:/ɪˈnɔːɡjərəlz/ | n. | 就职典礼，开幕式 |  Ceremonies or speeches that mark the beginning of something, especially the beginning of a president's term of office. | The president's inaugurals were attended by millions. 总统的就职典礼有数百万人参加。|  inauguration ceremonies, opening ceremonies, commencement exercises | closing ceremonies | noun

inaugural:/ɪˈnɔːɡjərəl/ | adj. | 就职的，开幕的 | Marking the beginning of an institution, activity, or period of office. |  The inaugural address was inspiring.  就职演说是鼓舞人心的。| first, initial, opening | final, closing | adjective
