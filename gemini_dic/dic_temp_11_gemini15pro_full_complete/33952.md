cirrostratus:/ˌsɪroʊˈstrætəs/ /ˌsɪərəˈstreɪtəs/ | n. |卷层云|A high-altitude, thin, sheetlike cloud composed of ice crystals, often producing a halo around the sun or moon. It often precedes a warm front and may indicate approaching precipitation.|  The cirrostratus clouds veiled the sun, creating a halo effect. 卷层云遮蔽了太阳，形成了光晕。

The appearance of cirrostratus often signals an approaching storm. 卷层云的出现通常预示着暴风雨即将来临。 | 近义词:high cloud, veil cloud | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific cloud type) | 词性:noun
