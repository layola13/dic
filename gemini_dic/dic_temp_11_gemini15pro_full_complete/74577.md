fraxinus:/ˈfræksɪnəs/| n. |梣属；梣树|A genus of trees of the olive family (Oleaceae), typically having ash-gray bark and pinnate leaves.  The common ash (Fraxinus excelsior) is the most important timber tree of northern Europe.|The fraxinus trees lined the avenue, their leaves rustling in the breeze. 梣树排列在林荫道上，树叶在微风中沙沙作响。|Synonym: ash tree|Antonym: N/A|noun
