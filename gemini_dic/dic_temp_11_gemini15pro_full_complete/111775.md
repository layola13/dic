loellingite:/ˈloʊlɪŋaɪt/| n. |硫砷铁矿|A steel-gray mineral, FeAs₂, that is a source of arsenic.  It is an iron arsenide mineral belonging to the marcasite group.|Loellingite is often found in hydrothermal veins. 硫砷铁矿常在热液矿脉中发现。|The loellingite sample showed signs of weathering.  这块硫砷铁矿样本显示出风化的迹象。|Synonyms: arsenopyrite, safflorite|Antonyms: N/A|noun
