fellowlike:/ˈfɛləʊlaɪk/ | adj. | 像伙伴的；友好的；合群的 | Resembling or characteristic of a fellow; companionable; sociable. | He had a fellowlike friendliness that made him easy to talk to. 他有一种像伙伴一样的友善，使他很容易与人交谈。 |  He greeted me with a fellowlike slap on the back. 他用像伙伴一样的拍背来问候我。 | 近义词：companionable, sociable, friendly, affable, amiable | 反义词：unsociable, unfriendly, aloof, distant | 词性: 形容词

词源：From "fellow" + "-like".  "Fellow" comes from Old Norse *felagi, meaning "partner, shareholder," and "-like" is a native English suffix meaning "resembling, characteristic of."
