outgalloped:/ˌaʊtˈɡæləpt/
verb
超过…的速度；跑得比…快
To ride faster than another rider or horse, leaving them behind.
Secretariat outgalloped the other horses in the Kentucky Derby.  秘书处在肯塔基德比中超过了其他马匹。
The experienced jockey outgalloped his rivals to win the race.  经验丰富的骑师超过了他的对手，赢得了比赛。
outrun, outstrip, overtake
lag behind, trail

ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + gallop.  Out- is a prefix meaning "beyond, exceeding, surpassing." Gallop refers to a horse's fastest gait.
