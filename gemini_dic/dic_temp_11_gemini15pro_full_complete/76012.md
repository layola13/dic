furcular:/ˈfɜːrkjʊlər/
adjective
叉骨的；叉状的
relating to or resembling a furcula; forked.
The furcular bone in birds is formed by the fusion of the clavicles. 鸟类的叉骨是由锁骨融合形成的。
furcular:/ˈfɜːrkjʊlər/
noun
叉骨；叉状物
a forked or Y-shaped structure, especially the furcula of a bird.
The wishbone, also known as the furcula, is a forked bone found in birds.  叉骨，也被称为叉状骨，是在鸟类中发现的一种分叉的骨头。
synonym: forked, wishbone (as noun)
antonym: straight, unbranched
adjective, noun
