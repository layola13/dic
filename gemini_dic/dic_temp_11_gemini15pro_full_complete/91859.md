hyperimmunized:/ˌhaɪpərˈɪmjʊnaɪzd/| adj.|高度免疫的，超免疫的|Having been subjected to repeated and intensive immunization to produce a high level of antibodies.|The hyperimmunized horses are used to produce antivenom.  这些高度免疫的马被用来生产抗蛇毒血清。|Example 2:  Hyperimmunized plasma can be used as a treatment for certain infections. 高度免疫的血浆可以用来治疗某些感染。|Synonyms: highly immunized, superimmunized|Antonyms: non-immunized, immunodeficient|adjective
