im·pale:/ɪmˈpeɪl/| vt.|刺穿，钉住，使陷入困境|To pierce with a sharp stake or point; to fix on a sharp point.|The defeated rebels were impaled on stakes as a warning to others.  被击败的叛军被钉在木桩上以儆效尤。|Synonyms: pierce, spear, transfix|Antonyms: extricate, free|verb


im·pale:/ɪmˈpeɪl/| vt.|使陷入困境，使固定|To fix in an inescapable or helpless position.|He felt impaled by her accusing stare. 他感到被她责备的目光钉住了，动弹不得。|Synonyms: trap, fix, stick|Antonyms: release, free|verb
