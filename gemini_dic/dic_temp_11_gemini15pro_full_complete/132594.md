nondigestive:/ˌnɒndaɪˈdʒɛstɪv/ /ˌnɑːndaɪˈdʒɛstɪv/
adjective
非消化的，不消化的
Not digestible; unable to be digested.
Example:  Nondigestive food components, such as fiber, are still important for gut health.  非消化的食物成分，比如纤维，对肠道健康仍然很重要。

Example:  He experienced discomfort after eating a large amount of nondigestive material. 他吃了大量的难消化食物后感到不适。
近义词:indigestible
反义词:digestible
词性:adjective
