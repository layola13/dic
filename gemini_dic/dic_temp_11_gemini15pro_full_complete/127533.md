necromantically:/ˌnɛkrəʊˈmæntɪkli/| adv.|以通灵术的方式；通过通灵术|Relating to or involving necromancy; in a necromantic manner.  |She necromantically summoned the spirit of her deceased grandfather. 她以通灵术的方式召唤了她已故祖父的灵魂。|近义词：magically, supernaturally|反义词：naturally, scientifically|词性:副词

necromancy: /ˈnɛkrəʊmænsi/ | n. |通灵术；死灵法术|The supposed practice of communicating with the dead, especially in order to predict the future. |The old witch practiced necromancy in her dark tower. 老巫婆在她的黑暗塔楼里练习通灵术。|近义词：sorcery, witchcraft|反义词：- |词性:名词

词源:  From Late Latin *necromantia, from Ancient Greek νεκρομαντεία (nekromanteía), from νεκρός (nekrós, “dead body”) + μαντεία (manteía, “divination”).
