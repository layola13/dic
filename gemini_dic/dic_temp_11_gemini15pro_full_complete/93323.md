ill-starred:/ˌɪlˈstɑːrd/
adjective
不幸的，倒霉的，注定要失败的
Unlucky; doomed to failure.  Marked by or accompanied by bad fortune.  Often used to describe ventures or enterprises that are ultimately unsuccessful.
The ill-starred expedition ended in tragedy.  这次不幸的探险以悲剧告终。
The ill-starred romance was doomed from the start. 这段不幸的恋情从一开始就注定要失败。
近义词:unlucky, unfortunate, doomed, jinxed
反义词:lucky, fortunate, blessed
词性:adjective

ill-starred:/ˌɪlˈstɑːrd/
词源: From "ill" (bad, evil) + "starred" (influenced by the stars).  The belief that the stars influenced human destiny led to the idea that being "ill-starred" meant having a bad or unlucky destiny determined by the stars.
词性:adjective
