hematologist:/ˌhiːməˈtɑːlədʒɪst/ /ˌheməˈtɑːlədʒɪst/
n.| 血液学家；血液病专家 | A medical doctor who specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of blood disorders. | The hematologist examined the patient's blood smear. 血液学家检查了病人的血涂片。|  synonyms: blood specialist, blood doctor | antonyms: None | noun
