knittable:/ˈnɪtəbl/| adj.|可编织的|Suitable for being knitted.  Made of a material that can be knitted.|This yarn is very soft and knittable.  这种纱线很柔软，可以用来编织。|近义词:weavable|反义词:non-knittable|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From knit + -able.  "Knit" comes from Old English "cnyttan," meaning "to tie, knot."  "-able" is a suffix indicating capability or suitability.
