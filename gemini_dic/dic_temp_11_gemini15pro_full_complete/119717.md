midsized:/ˌmɪdˈsaɪzd/| adj.|中型的，中等大小的|Of medium size; neither large nor small.  Relatively average in size, especially of companies or businesses. |例句：They bought a midsized car. 他们买了一辆中型汽车。|例句：It's a midsized company with about 500 employees. 这是一家大约有500名员工的中型公司。|近义词：medium-sized, average-sized|反义词: large, small, oversized, undersized|词性:形容词
