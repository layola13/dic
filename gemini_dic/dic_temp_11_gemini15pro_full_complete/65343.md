eulerian:/ɔɪˈlɪəriən/ | adj.| 欧拉的；与欧拉有关的；欧拉式的 | Relating to or characteristic of the Swiss mathematician Leonhard Euler or his work. |  Eulerian path is a path in a graph that visits every edge exactly once. 欧拉路径是图中访问每条边恰好一次的路径。| Eulerian cycle is a Eulerian path that starts and ends on the same vertex.  欧拉回路是一条起始顶点和结束顶点相同的欧拉路径。 |  Hamiltonian, | non-Eulerian | adjective
