inaidable:/ɪnˈeɪdəbl/ | adj. | 不可避免的，无法逃避的 | Unable to be avoided, evaded, or escaped. |  例句：Their defeat was inaidable. 他们的失败是不可避免的。 | 例句：The inaidable conclusion is that we need to change our strategy.  不可避免的结论是我们需要改变我们的策略。 | 近义词：inevitable, unavoidable, inescapable | 反义词：avoidable, preventable, escapable | 词性:形容词

词源:  From in- (not) + aidable (able to be aided or helped), thus meaning "not able to be aided or avoided."  The root "aid" comes from the Old French "aider," meaning "to help."
