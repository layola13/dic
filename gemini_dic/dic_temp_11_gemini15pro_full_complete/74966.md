fricandeau:/ˌfriːkænˈdoʊ/| n.|红烧肉，炖肉|A dish of braised veal, larded and served with a rich brown sauce.  It is a classic dish of French cuisine.|We had a delicious fricandeau for Sunday dinner. 我们星期天晚餐吃了美味的红烧肉。|近义词：braise, stew, pot roast|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用raw meat 生肉)|noun
