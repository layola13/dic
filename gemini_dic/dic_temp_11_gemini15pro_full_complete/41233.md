corallite:/ˈkɒrəˌlaɪt/ (US) /ˈkɔːrəˌlaɪt/ (UK)| n. |珊瑚虫体；个体珊瑚|The cup-shaped skeleton secreted by a single coral polyp. |The Great Barrier Reef is built up of millions of corallite skeletons. 大堡礁是由数百万个珊瑚虫体骨骼构成的。|近义词：polyp, calicle|反义词: N/A|名词

词源:

From Modern Latin *corallium + -ite, from Latin coralium “coral” + -ite.  Ultimately from Ancient Greek κοράλλιον (korállion).
