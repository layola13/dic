outrigger:/ˈaʊtrɪɡər/| n.|舷外支架，突出物，平衡装置|A framework, float, or spar extending from the side of a boat for increasing stability or as a support for a platform or an oarlock.|The outrigger canoe skimmed across the water. 舷外支架独木舟掠过水面。|Synonyms: stabilizer, support, float|Antonyms: hull, center, interior|noun

outrigger:/ˈaʊtrɪɡər/| n.|支腿，平衡器|A projecting support to steady or strengthen a structure.|The crane's outriggers provided extra stability. 起重机的支腿提供了额外的稳定性。|Synonyms: brace, prop, support|Antonyms: core, center, interior|noun

outrigger:/ˈaʊtrɪɡər/| n.|吊杆，伸杆|A projecting beam or spar, as on a crane.|The outrigger extended the reach of the crane. 吊杆扩展了起重机的范围。|Synonyms: boom, jib, arm|Antonyms: base, foundation, interior|noun
