decidedness:/dɪˈsaɪdɪdnəs/
n.
果断；决断；坚定
The quality of being decisive; resoluteness.
Her decidedness in the face of crisis was admirable.  她在危机面前的果断令人钦佩。
近义词：resoluteness, determination, firmness, decisiveness
反义词：indecision, hesitation, vacillation
词性: 名词

词源:  From decide + -ed + -ness.  来自decide (决定) + -ed (过去分词后缀) + -ness (名词后缀，表示状态或性质).
