disinfective:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛktɪv/
adjective
消毒的，杀菌的
Capable of destroying or inhibiting the growth of disease-causing microorganisms.
The disinfective properties of bleach make it a useful cleaning agent. 漂白剂的消毒特性使其成为一种有用的清洁剂。
Synonyms: antiseptic, germicidal, bactericidal, antimicrobial
Antonyms: septic, contaminated, infected
词性: adjective


disinfectant:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛktənt/
noun
消毒剂
A chemical agent used to destroy or inhibit the growth of disease-causing microorganisms.
Bleach is a common household disinfectant. 漂白剂是一种常见的家用消毒剂。
Synonyms: antiseptic, germicidal, bactericidal, antimicrobial
Antonyms: contaminant, pollutant
词性: noun


Etymology:
From dis- (expressing negation, reversal) + infect + -ive.  Ultimately from Latin in- (in, into, on, upon, against) + facere (to do, make).
