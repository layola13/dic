neutrons:/ˈnjuːtrɒnz/ (UK), /ˈnuːtrɑːnz/ (US)| n. |中子|A subatomic particle of about the same mass as a proton but without an electric charge, present in all atomic nuclei except those of ordinary hydrogen.|例句：Neutrons and protons make up the nucleus of an atom. 中子和质子构成了原子的原子核。

例句：The neutrons bombarded the uranium atoms, causing fission. 中子轰击铀原子，导致裂变。

近义词: nucleon (a general term for protons and neutrons)|反义词:  (No direct antonym, but contrasting particle would be proton)|词性: Noun
