cantic: /ˈkæntɪk/
n.
颂歌；圣歌
A hymn or chant, especially one from a religious service.
The congregation joined in singing the cantic.  会众一起唱起了圣歌。

近义词: hymn, chant, psalm, anthem
反义词: dirge, elegy


词性: noun

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French cantique, via ecclesiastical Latin from biblical Greek kantikos ‘sung’, from kanō ‘sing’.
