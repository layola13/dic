cheetah:/ˈtʃiːtə/ | n. |猎豹|A large, slender, spotted cat found in Africa and parts of Asia, known for being the fastest land animal.|例句：The cheetah chased the gazelle across the savanna. 猎豹追着瞪羚穿过大草原。

例句：A cheetah can run faster than any other land animal. 猎豹比任何其他陆地动物跑得都快。|近义词：leopard, jaguar (though not as fast) |反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "slow animal" like tortoise or sloth) |词性:noun
