co-operated:/koʊˈɑːpəreɪtɪd/
verb
合作，协作
Worked together towards a common goal or purpose.
The two companies co-operated on the project.  两家公司合作开展了这个项目。
Several countries co-operated in the rescue effort.  几个国家合作参与了救援工作。
collaborated, joined forces, worked together
opposed, resisted, hindered
