brewis:/ˈbruːɪs/| n.|浓汤，肉汤泡面包|A dish of broth poured over bread.|He enjoyed a hearty brewis for breakfast. 他早餐享用了一顿丰盛的肉汤泡面包。|近义词：broth, soup, stew|反义词:|名词


Etymology:
From Middle English *brewys, *brewes, from Old English brīw, from Proto-Germanic *bruwaz (“broth, brew”). Cognate with Old High German briuwan (“to brew”), Old Norse brugg (“brew”).  The sense development is from "broth" to "bread soaked in broth."
