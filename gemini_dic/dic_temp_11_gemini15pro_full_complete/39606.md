consension:/kənˈsɛnʃən/| n. |一致同意，共识|Agreement or accord; harmony of opinion. |The consensus among scientists is that climate change is real. 科学家们的共识是气候变化是真实存在的。|The two parties reached a consension on the terms of the contract. 双方就合同条款达成了一致。|近义词：agreement, accord, consensus, unanimity|反义词：disagreement, discord, dissent|词性:名词

Etymology:  From Late Latin *consēnsiō, from Latin cōnsēnsus (“agreement”),  from cōnsentiō (“I agree”), from com- (“together”) + sentiō (“I feel, think”).
