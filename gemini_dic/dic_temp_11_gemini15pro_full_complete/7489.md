ankylosis:/ˌæŋkɪˈloʊsɪs/ /ˌæŋkɪˈləʊsɪs/| n. |关节强直，骨融合|Stiffness or fixation of a joint as the result of injury, disease, or surgery.|例句：The patient experienced ankylosis of the knee after the accident.  病人事故后膝盖僵硬。

例句：Ankylosis of the spine can severely limit mobility. 脊柱强直会严重限制活动能力。|近义词：stiffness, rigidity, immobility|反义词:mobility, flexibility|词性:noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *ankylosis*, from Greek *ankylosis*  "bending, stiffening,"  from *ankyloun*  "to bend, to make crooked,"  from *ankulos*  "bent, crooked."
