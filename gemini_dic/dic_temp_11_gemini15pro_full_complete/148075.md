oversauce:/ˌoʊvərˈsɔːs/| vt.|加太多酱汁，过分修饰|To add excessive sauce or embellishment to something, often to the point of ruining it.  It can also refer to being overly dramatic or sentimental.|例句：The chef oversauced the pasta, making it too salty. 厨师在意大利面上放了太多酱汁，使它太咸了。

例句：She oversauced the story with unnecessary details. 她给故事添加了不必要的细节，显得过于修饰。|近义词：overdo, exaggerate, embellish|反义词:understate, minimize|词性:动词
