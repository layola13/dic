cytogenesis:/ˌsaɪtoʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
n.
细胞生成;细胞发生
The formation of cells, especially by division.
例句：Cytogenesis is a crucial process in the development of an organism. 细胞生成是一个有机体发育的关键过程。

例句：The study of cytogenesis helps us understand how cells multiply and differentiate. 对细胞生成的研究有助于我们理解细胞如何增殖和分化。

近义词：cell formation, cell development
反义词：cell death, apoptosis
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
cyto- (from Greek κύτος kutos, "hollow vessel," later "cell") + -genesis (from Greek γένεσις genesis, "origin," "creation," "generation")
