hypercard:/ˈhaɪpərkɑːrd/
n.
超卡; 超卡片;
A software application for Apple Macintosh computers that allowed users to create and use interactive multimedia information stacks.  It combined database functionality with a graphical user interface.
HyperCard was a popular tool for creating presentations, prototypes, and educational materials. 超卡是苹果Macintosh电脑上的一种软件应用程序，允许用户创建和使用交互式多媒体信息栈。它结合了数据库功能和图形用户界面。超卡是创建演示文稿、原型和教育材料的流行工具。
例句：
HyperCard was revolutionary for its time. 超卡在当时具有革命性意义。

HyperCard stacks were easy to share. 超卡堆栈很容易共享。
