aldous:/ˈɔːldəs/| n. |奥尔德斯（男子名）|A male given name, most famously associated with Aldous Huxley, author of Brave New World.|Aldous has always been a bookworm. 奥尔德斯一直是个书虫。|近义词: (No direct synonyms for a name, could consider similar sounding names like:  Wallace,  Marcus)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


Etymology:
From the Old English personal name *Ealdweald, composed of the elements eald "old" and weald "power, ruler".  It has seen renewed usage in the 20th century, largely due to the fame of Aldous Huxley.
