nonsystematically:/ˌnɒnˌsɪstəˈmætɪkli/ /ˌnɑːnˌsɪstəˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 非系统地，无系统地 |  Not done or acting according to a system or regular plan; in a disorganized or random way. |  He approached the problem nonsystematically, jumping from one idea to another. 他没有系统地处理这个问题，从一个想法跳到另一个想法。 | Example: She packed her suitcase nonsystematically, throwing in clothes at random. 她胡乱地收拾行李箱，随意地把衣服扔进去。 | Synonyms: randomly, haphazardly, irregularly, unsystematically | Antonyms: systematically, methodically, orderly | 词性: 副词
