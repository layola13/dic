forsaken:/fərˈseɪkən/ | adj. | 被遗弃的，被抛弃的；荒凉的 |Abandoned or deserted; lonely and desolate | The forsaken town was eerily silent.  被遗弃的小镇寂静得可怕。|  The child felt forsaken by his parents.  这个孩子感到被父母抛弃了。| synonyms: abandoned, deserted, desolate, lonely, neglected | antonyms: cherished, protected, loved, inhabited | adjective

forsake:/fərˈseɪk/ | vt. | 抛弃，遗弃；放弃 | To abandon or desert someone or something; to renounce or give up something | He forsook his family for a life of adventure. 他为了冒险的生活抛弃了他的家人。 | She forsook her career to raise her children.  她为了抚养孩子放弃了事业。| synonyms: abandon, desert, renounce, relinquish | antonyms: keep, cherish, maintain, support | verb
