hookah:/ˈhʊkə/ (US), /ˈhʊkɑː/ (UK)| n. |水烟袋，水烟壶|A water pipe with a long flexible tube attached to a container where tobacco is burned and the smoke is drawn through water.|例句：We relaxed at the cafe, smoking a hookah. 我们在咖啡馆放松，抽着水烟。

例句：The hookah bar was dimly lit and filled with the scent of sweet tobacco. 水烟吧灯光昏暗，弥漫着甜烟草的香味。|近义词：water pipe, narghile, shisha|反义词: None (no direct antonym for a physical object)|词性: noun
