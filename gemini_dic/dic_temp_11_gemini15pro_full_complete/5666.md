alto:/ˈæltəʊ/ (UK), /ˈæltoʊ/ (US)| n. adj.|女低音；中音的|The highest adult male voice or the lowest female voice, or a singer with this voice; a part written for this voice; (adj.) of or for such a voice or part. |She has a lovely alto voice. 她有一副优美的女低音嗓音。|The alto part is very important in this piece.  这首曲子中的中音声部非常重要。|近义词：contralto, mezzo-soprano (n.); contralto, mezzo-soprano (adj.)|反义词：soprano, tenor (n.); soprano, tenor (adj.)|词性:名词，形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian alto "high,"  (used musically for the highest male voice and, by analogy, the lowest female voice), from Latin altus "high, deep, noble."
