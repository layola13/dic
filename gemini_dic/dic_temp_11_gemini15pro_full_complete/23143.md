boustrophedon:/ˌbuːstrəˈfiːdən/
noun
回转书写，牛耕式书写
A way of writing in which alternate lines are written in opposite directions (from right to left and then from left to right, as in the manner in which an ox plows a field).
The ancient inscription was written in boustrophedon. 这段古代铭文是用回转书写法写成的。

The farmer plowed his field in a boustrophedon pattern. 农民以回转书写的方式犁地。

近义词:alternate writing
反义词:conventional writing
词性:n.

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek βουστροφηδόν (boustrophēdón), from βοῦς (boûs, “ox”) + στροφή (strophḗ, “a turning”) + -δόν (-dón, adverbial suffix). Literally "ox-turning-wise".
