nonassertivenesses:/ˌnɒnəˈsɜːtɪvnɪsɪz/
noun
不坚定；不自信；缺乏自信的表现
Lack of assertiveness; a failure to assert oneself.  The plural form of nonassertiveness.
His nonassertivenesses led to him being overlooked for promotion.  他因缺乏自信而被忽视晋升。
synonym:passivity, timidity, submissiveness, meekness
antonym:assertiveness, confidence, boldness, self-assurance
词性:名词
