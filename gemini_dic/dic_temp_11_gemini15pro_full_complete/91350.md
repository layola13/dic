hydroponics:/ˌhaɪdrəˈpɒnɪks/| n. |水培，无土栽培|The process of growing plants in sand, gravel, or liquid, with added nutrients but without soil.|例句：Hydroponics allows you to grow plants in areas with poor soil. 水培可以让你在土壤贫瘠的地区种植植物。

例句：The hydroponics system in my greenhouse is very efficient.  我温室里的水培系统非常高效。|近义词：soilless culture, aquaculture (in some contexts)|反义词: geoponics, soil-based agriculture|词性:noun


hydroponicses:  This is not a standard English word.  "Hydroponics" is an uncountable noun and therefore does not have a plural form.  You would simply use "hydroponics" regardless of the quantity.
