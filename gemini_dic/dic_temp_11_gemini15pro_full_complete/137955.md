nonwinged:/ˌnɒnˈwɪŋd/ /ˌnɑːnˈwɪŋd/
adjective
无翅的;没有翅膀的
Not having wings.
例句：Nonwinged insects, such as ants, are often found in colonies. 无翅昆虫，例如蚂蚁，经常以群落的形式被发现。

例句：The nonwinged bird hopped along the ground. 这只没有翅膀的鸟在地上跳来跳去。

近义词：wingless, flightless
反义词：winged, alate
词性：adjective
