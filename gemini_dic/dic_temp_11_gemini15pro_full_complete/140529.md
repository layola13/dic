olein:/ˈoʊliɪn/ | n. |油酸甘油酯; 油精 | A colorless to pale yellow, oily liquid, C18H34O2, the glyceryl ester of oleic acid, occurring naturally in animal and vegetable fats and oils and used in soaps, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals. |  Olein is a major component of olive oil. 油酸甘油酯是橄榄油的主要成分。| synonyms: triolein, glyceryl trioleate | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps saturated fats could be considered a functional opposite) | noun
