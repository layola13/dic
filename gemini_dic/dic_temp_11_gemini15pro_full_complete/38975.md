例句：The confineless expanse of the ocean inspired a sense of awe.  浩瀚无垠的海洋激发了一种敬畏感。

近义词：boundless, limitless, infinite, unlimited, vast, immense
反义词：confined, limited, restricted, finite, bounded
词性：adjective
