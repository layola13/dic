edifier:/ˈɛdɪfaɪər/| n.|教化者，启迪者，熏陶者|Someone or something that edifies, that is, instructs or improves someone morally or intellectually. |The preacher was a true edifier, inspiring his congregation to live better lives.  这位传教士是一位真正的启迪者，激励他的会众过上更好的生活。|近义词：instructor, teacher, mentor, guide, enlightener|反义词：corrupter, misleader|词性:noun

edify:/ˈɛdɪfaɪ/| v.|教诲，启迪，熏陶|To instruct or improve someone morally or intellectually. |The purpose of the workshop is to edify participants about the latest research.  研讨会的目的是向参与者介绍最新的研究成果。|近义词：instruct, teach, enlighten, uplift, improve|反义词：corrupt, mislead|词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *edifien*, from Old French *edifier*, from Latin *aedificāre* (“to build”), from *aedēs* (“house, temple”) + *facere* (“to make, do”).  The meaning shifted from "to build" to "to build up morally or intellectually."
