flounce:/flaʊns/| v. n.| 猛然离去；突然站起；荷叶边；装饰褶边 | To move impatiently or abruptly with exaggerated movements, especially in a way that shows displeasure. Also, a wide ornamental strip of gathered or pleated material attached to a garment, typically around the hemline. | She flounced out of the room in a huff. 她怒气冲冲地走出了房间。 | synonyms:  storm, swagger, toss, sashay, ruffle | antonyms: glide, slink, tiptoe | verb
