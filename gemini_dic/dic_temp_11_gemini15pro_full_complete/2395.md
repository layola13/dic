adipsy: /əˈdɪpsi/ | adj. |  渴的，口渴的 |  Characterized by thirst; thirsty. | He felt adipsy after running the marathon. 他跑完马拉松后感到口渴。 | 近义词: thirsty, parched | 反义词: quenched, satiated | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Latin *adipsōsus, from adipsī "I was thirsty," perfect active indicative of adipīscī "to become thirsty," inchoative of ad- "to" + dipsīre "to be thirsty," related to dipsa "thirst."
