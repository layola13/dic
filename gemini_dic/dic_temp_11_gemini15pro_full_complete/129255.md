nodal:/ˈnoʊdl/ (US) /ˈnəʊdl/ (UK)| adj.|节点的；结的|Relating to or characterized by a node or nodes; a point at which lines or pathways intersect or branch; a central or connecting point.|The nodal points in the network are crucial for communication.|近义词：central, junction, intersection, connecting|反义词：peripheral, terminal|词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *nodalis*, from Latin nodus (“knot”).
