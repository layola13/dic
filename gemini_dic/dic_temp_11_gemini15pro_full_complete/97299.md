ETYMOLOGY->

From in- + fold + -ment.  "In-" denotes inwardness or inclusion. "Fold" refers to the act of bending something over on itself. "-ment" is a suffix forming nouns that denote the result of an action. Thus, "infoldment" literally means the act or result of folding inward.
