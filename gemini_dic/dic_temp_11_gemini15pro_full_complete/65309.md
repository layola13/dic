eudiometrical:/ˌjuːdiəˈmɛtrɪk(ə)l/
adjective
测气法的；用于气体分析的
Relating to or involving eudiometry (the measurement of the volume changes of gases during chemical reactions).
The eudiometrical analysis showed a high concentration of oxygen. 测气分析显示氧气浓度很高。
近义词:gasometric
反义词:None
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Greek *eudios* "clear (air)" + *metron* "measure" + *-ikos* "-ic" + *-al*.
