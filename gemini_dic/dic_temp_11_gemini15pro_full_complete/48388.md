dedecorate: /diːˈdekəreɪt/
verb
去除装饰
To remove decorations from.
The crew dedecorated the Christmas tree after the holidays.  节假日过后，工作人员拆除了圣诞树上的装饰。
近义词: strip, undecorate, dismantle
反义词: decorate, adorn, embellish
词性: v.

Etymology: de- (prefix meaning "removal or reversal") + decorate (from Latin decorare "to adorn")
