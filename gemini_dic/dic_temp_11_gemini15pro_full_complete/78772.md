gilgamesh:/ˈɡɪlɡəˌmɛʃ/| n.|吉尔伽美什|A legendary Sumerian king and the protagonist of the Epic of Gilgamesh, an ancient Mesopotamian poem considered one of the earliest surviving works of great literature.  He is two-thirds god and one-third human.|Example: Gilgamesh and Enkidu journeyed to the Cedar Forest. 吉尔伽美什和恩奇都前往雪松森林。

Example: The story of Gilgamesh is a powerful exploration of mortality. 吉尔伽美什的故事是对死亡的有力探索。|近义词:hero, legend|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
