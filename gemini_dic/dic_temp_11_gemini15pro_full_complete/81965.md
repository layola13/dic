grinch:/ɡrɪntʃ/| n.|脾气暴躁的人; 讨厌圣诞节的人; 扫兴的人|A grumpy person who spoils the pleasure of others; especially one who dislikes Christmas. |The Grinch stole Christmas! 格林奇偷走了圣诞节！|近义词:curmudgeon, killjoy, spoilsport|反义词:Santa Claus, cheerful person|noun


grinch:/ɡrɪntʃ/| v. |偷走圣诞节; 扫兴; 破坏乐趣|To steal Christmas or to spoil someone's fun. |He grinched all the presents from under the tree. 他把树下的所有礼物都偷走了。|近义词:spoil, ruin, destroy|反义词:celebrate, enhance|verb
