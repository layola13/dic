homebuyers:/ˈhoʊmˌbaɪərz/ (US) /ˈhəʊmˌbaɪəz/ (UK)
noun
购房者，买房的人
People who purchase homes.
例句：Homebuyers are facing rising interest rates. 购房者正面临不断上升的利率。
例句：The new development is attracting many homebuyers.  这个新的开发项目吸引了许多购房者。
近义词：home purchaser, house buyer
反义词:home seller, home vendor
词性:n.
