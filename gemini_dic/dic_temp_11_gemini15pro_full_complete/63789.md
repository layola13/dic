epicycloid:/ˌɛpɪˈsaɪklɔɪd/ | n. | 外摆线 | A curve traced by a point on the circumference of a circle as it rolls around the outside of a fixed circle. |  An epicycloid is formed when a circle rolls around another circle. 当一个圆绕着另一个圆滚动时，就会形成外摆线。

The spirograph toy creates beautiful epicycloid patterns.  万花尺玩具可以创造出漂亮的外摆线图案。| 近义词: roulette (更广义的轮转线) | 反义词: hypocycloid (内摆线) | 词性: noun


epicycle:/ˈɛpɪˌsaɪk(ə)l/ | n. | 本轮 | A small circle whose center moves around the circumference of a larger circle.  In ancient astronomy, epicycles were used to explain the apparent retrograde motion of planets. |  Ptolemy's model of the universe used epicycles to explain planetary motion. 托勒密的宇宙模型用本轮来解释行星运动。

The concept of epicycles was central to the geocentric model of the universe. 本轮的概念是地心说宇宙模型的核心。| 近义词: deferent (均轮) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
