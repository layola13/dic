lode:/loʊd/| n.|矿脉；大量；丰富的来源|A vein of metal ore; a rich source or supply.|例句：The miners discovered a rich lode of gold. 矿工们发现了一条丰富的金矿脉。

例句：He inherited a lode of money from his grandfather. 他从祖父那里继承了一大笔钱。|近义词：vein, deposit, abundance, wealth, store|反义词：lack, scarcity, dearth|noun
