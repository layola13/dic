infausting:/ɪnˈfɔːstɪŋ/
adjective
不祥的，不幸的
Unlucky, unfavorable, ill-omened; suggesting misfortune.  Relating to something that is not conducive to success or happiness.
The infausting storm clouds gathered overhead.  不祥的风暴云聚集在头顶。
The black cat crossing their path was seen as an infausting sign.  黑猫穿过他们的道路被视为不祥之兆。
Synonyms: ominous, unfavorable, unlucky, ill-omened, portentous
Antonyms: auspicious, favorable, lucky, fortunate, propitious
