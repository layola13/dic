bench:/bɛntʃ/ | n. vt.|长凳，工作台，法官席；使坐板凳席|A long seat for several people, typically made of wood or stone.  A long table for working on, especially in a laboratory or workshop. bench:/bɛntʃ/ | vt.|使坐板凳席，把…列入后备队员名单|To remove a player from a game and have them sit on the bench. To keep someone out of action or a competition. |The coach benched him for the rest of the game. 教练让他在剩下的比赛中坐冷板凳。|synonyms: sideline, withdraw, remove|antonyms:  play, start, field|verb
