fascia:/ˈfæʃ(i)ə/ /ˈfeɪʃə/ | n. | 筋膜；饰带；(汽车仪表盘的)镶板 | A thin casing of connective tissue that surrounds and holds every organ, blood vessel, bone, nerve fiber and muscle in place. | The surgeon cut through the fascia to reach the damaged organ.  外科医生切开筋膜以到达受损的器官。| synonyms: covering, membrane, band, strip | antonyms: core, center | noun

fascia:/ˈfæʃ(i)ə/ /ˈfeɪʃə/ | n. | (尤指商店、房屋的)面板，饰带 | A board or other flat piece of material covering a surface or forming a part of a facade. | The fascia of the shop front was painted bright red. 店面的面板被漆成了鲜红色。 | synonyms: facing, facade, cladding | antonyms: interior, inside | noun
