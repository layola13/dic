astarte:/əˈstɑːrti/ | n. | 阿斯塔特; 阿斯塔蒂;  | Astarte is the Hellenized name of Ishtar, the Great Goddess of the Mesopotamian pantheon.  She represents love, beauty, sex, war, and justice. |  Astarte was worshipped throughout the ancient Near East.  阿斯塔特在整个古代近东地区都受到崇拜。

Astarte is a major goddess in Phoenician mythology. 阿斯塔特是腓尼基神话中的一个主要女神。| 近义词: Ishtar, Inanna, Aphrodite | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun
