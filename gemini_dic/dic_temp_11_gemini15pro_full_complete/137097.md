nonsubconsciousness:/nɒnˌsʌbˈkɒnʃəsnəs/ /nɑːnˌsʌbˈkɑːnʃəsnəs/ | n. | 非潜意识; 非下意识 | The state or quality of not being subconscious; a state of awareness.  This word emphasizes the active and present awareness, distinguishing it from thoughts or feelings repressed in the subconscious. |  His nonsubconsciousness of the social cues made him appear rude. 他对社交暗示的非下意识让他显得粗鲁。 | 近义词: awareness, consciousness | 反义词: subconsciousness, unconsciousness | 词性: noun
