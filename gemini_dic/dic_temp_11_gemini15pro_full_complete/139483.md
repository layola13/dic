obstreperousness:/əbˈstrɛpərəsnəs/| n. |喧闹，吵闹，任性|The state of being noisy and difficult to control.|His obstreperousness disrupted the entire class. 他的吵闹扰乱了整个课堂。|The children's obstreperousness made it difficult to enjoy the meal. 孩子们的吵闹让人难以享受这顿饭。|近义词：boisterousness, clamor, rowdiness|反义词：quietness, calmness, docility|词性:noun

obstreperous:/əbˈstrɛpərəs/| adj. |喧闹的，吵闹的，任性的|Noisy and difficult to control.|The obstreperous child was finally sent to the principal's office.  这个吵闹的孩子最终被送到了校长办公室。|The obstreperous fans cheered wildly after their team scored. 喧闹的球迷在他们的球队得分后疯狂欢呼。|近义词：unruly, boisterous, clamorous|反义词：quiet, calm, docile|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *obstreperus*  "noisy, clamorous," from *obstrepere*  "to make a noise against," from *ob-* "against" + *strepere* "to make a noise."
