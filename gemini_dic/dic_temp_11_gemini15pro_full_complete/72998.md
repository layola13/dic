foosball:/ˈfuːzbɔːl/| n. | 桌上足球 | A table game resembling soccer, in which players turn rods attached to miniature figures of players to kick a small ball. |  We played foosball all afternoon. 我们玩了一下午的桌上足球。|  table soccer, table football |  (No common antonym) | noun
