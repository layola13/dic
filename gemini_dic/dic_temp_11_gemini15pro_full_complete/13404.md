atomist:/ˈætəmɪst/| n.|原子论者; 原子学家|A person who believes in or studies atomism (the theory that all matter is composed of tiny indivisible particles called atoms).|例句：The atomist argued that the universe was made up of tiny, indivisible particles. 原子论者认为宇宙是由微小、不可分割的粒子组成的。

例句：Democritus was a prominent atomist in ancient Greece. 德谟克利特是古希腊著名的原子论者。|近义词：physicist, scientist, |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "holist")|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Greek word "atomos" (indivisible) + "-ist" (a suffix denoting a person who adheres to a particular doctrine or theory).
