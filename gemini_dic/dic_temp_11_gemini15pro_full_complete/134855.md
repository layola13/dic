nonmoderns:/ˌnɒnˈmɒdənz/ |n.|非现代人；非现代主义者|People or things not belonging to the modern era.  Those who reject or are outside of the values, styles, or ideas typically associated with modernity.|The museum's exhibit focused on the art of the nonmoderns, showcasing pre-20th century works.|Synonym:traditionalists, premoderns|Antonym:moderns|noun

nonmodern:/ˌnɒnˈmɒdən/ |adj.|非现代的|Not modern; belonging to or characteristic of a period before the modern era.|Her nonmodern sensibilities were reflected in her preference for classical music.|Synonym:traditional, premodern|Antonym:modern|adjective
