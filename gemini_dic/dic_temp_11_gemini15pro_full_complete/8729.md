antidegradation:/ˌæntiˌdɛɡrəˈdeɪʃən/ | n. |反退化，防止恶化|The principle of antidegradation ensures that existing water quality is maintained and protected.| The state has adopted an antidegradation policy for its rivers and streams. 该州对其河流和小溪采取了反退化政策。 | synonyms: preservation, conservation, protection | antonyms: degradation, deterioration, decline | noun

Etymology:  "anti-" (against) + "degradation" (the act or process of degrading).  "Degradation" comes from Late Latin "degradationem," from the past participle stem of "degradare"  (to reduce in rank), from "de-" (down) + "gradus" (step, rank).
