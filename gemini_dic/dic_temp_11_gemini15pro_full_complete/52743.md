dilaceration:/dɪˌlæsəˈreɪʃn/
n.
撕裂；裂伤；牙根弯曲
The abnormal bending or curving of the root of a tooth.  A tearing or laceration.
The dentist diagnosed a dilaceration of the canine root. 牙医诊断出犬齿根部弯曲。
The accident caused a severe dilaceration of his leg. 事故导致他的腿严重撕裂。
近义词: laceration, tear, rupture, avulsion
反义词: repair, healing
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dīlacerātiō, from dīlacerāre (“to tear apart”), from di- (“apart”) + lacerāre (“to tear, rend”).
