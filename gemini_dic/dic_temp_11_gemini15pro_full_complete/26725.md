cadger:/ˈkædʒər/| n.|乞丐；骗子；不劳而获的人|A person who tries to get something from someone else without paying or working for it; someone who sponges off others.|He's such a cadger, always borrowing money and never paying it back. 他真是个爱占便宜的人，总是借钱不还。|Synonyms: sponger, scrounger, freeloader, beggar|Antonyms: giver, philanthropist|noun
