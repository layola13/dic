dangling:/ˈdæŋɡlɪŋ/| adj.|悬挂的，摇摆的，不完整的|hanging loosely; incomplete or unresolved|例句：The dangling wires were a safety hazard.  悬挂的电线是一个安全隐患。

例句：He left the sentence dangling, creating suspense. 他故意留下一个悬而未决的句子，制造悬念。|近义词：hanging, suspended, swinging, unfinished, incomplete|反义词：fixed, secure, complete, resolved|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *danglen, frequentative of dangen (“to hang”), from Old Norse dingla (“to swing, dangle”). Related to Old English denġan (“to strike, knock”). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *dangōną (“to hang”).
