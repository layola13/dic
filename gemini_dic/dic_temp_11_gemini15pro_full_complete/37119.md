combustibly:/kəmˈbʌstɪbli/ | adv. | 可燃地，易燃地 | In a way that is capable of catching fire and burning. | The material is stored combustibly, posing a significant fire hazard.  这种材料储存方式易燃，构成了重大的火灾隐患。 | Example:  Dry leaves and twigs are highly combustible materials. 干树叶和小树枝是高度易燃的材料. | Synonyms: inflammably, ignitably | Antonyms: noncombustible, fireproof | Word Type: Adverb

Etymology:
From "combustible" + "-ly".  "Combustible" comes from Late Latin "combustibilis", from "comburere" (to burn up), from "com-" (together) + "burere" (to burn).
