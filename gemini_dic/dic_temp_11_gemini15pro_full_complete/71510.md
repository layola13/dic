flanks:/flæŋks/ | n. | 侧翼，侧面 | The sides of something, especially an army or ship. | The general positioned troops on both flanks of the city. 将军在城市的两个侧翼都部署了军队。| The enemy attacked our left flank. 敌人攻击了我们的左翼。 | synonyms: sides, wings, flanks | antonyms: front, center | noun

flanks:/flæŋks/ | v. |  位于…的侧面，守卫…的侧翼 | To be situated on each side of something or someone. | The fireplace is flanked by two armchairs. 壁炉两侧各放着一张扶手椅。|  Guards flanked the president as he walked through the crowd.  当总统穿过人群时，警卫守卫在他的两侧。| synonyms: border, edge, flank | antonyms: face, confront | verb
