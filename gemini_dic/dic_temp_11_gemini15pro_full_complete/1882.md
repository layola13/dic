acroter:/ˈækrətər/ | n. |  acroterion的单数形式，（古希腊罗马建筑中）山墙或山墙边缘的装饰，通常为雕像或花瓶 | An acroter is an architectural ornament placed on the apex or at the lower corners of a pediment.  |  The temple's acroters were intricately carved statues of mythical creatures. 这座寺庙的山墙装饰是雕刻精美的神话生物雕像。| Synonym: finial, ornament, decoration | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps plainness, bareness) | noun

acroteria:/ˌækrəˈtɪəriə/ | n. | acroter的复数形式，（古希腊罗马建筑中）山墙或山墙边缘的装饰，通常为雕像或花瓶 | Acroteria are architectural ornaments placed on the apex or at the lower corners of a pediment. | The acroteria of the Parthenon were badly damaged by pollution.  帕台农神庙的山墙装饰被污染严重破坏。| Synonym: finials, ornaments, decorations | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps plainness, bareness) | noun
