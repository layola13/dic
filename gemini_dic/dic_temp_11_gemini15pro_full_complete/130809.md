nonapologetical:/ˌnɒnəˌpɒləˈdʒetɪkəl/ | adj. | 不道歉的，不后悔的，理直气壮的 |  Not feeling or showing remorse or regret; unapologetic.  It describes someone who is unrepentant and makes no apologies for their actions, beliefs, or statements. | She was nonapologetical about her decision to leave the company. 她对离开公司的决定毫不后悔。|  unrepentant, unapologetic, remorseless, unashamed, defiant | apologetic, remorseful, repentant, ashamed, contrite | adjective
