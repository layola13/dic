essentially:/ɪˈsɛnʃəli/| adv.|本质上；根本上；基本上|Used to emphasize the basic, fundamental, or intrinsic nature of something.  It means "in essence" or "fundamentally".|The book is essentially a love story. 这本书本质上是一个爱情故事。|basically, fundamentally, primarily, chiefly, mainly|superficially, peripherally|adverb
