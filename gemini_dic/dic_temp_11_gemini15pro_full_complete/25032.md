bryological:/ˌbraɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
苔藓学的
Relating to bryology; the scientific study of mosses and liverworts.
Bryological research is crucial for understanding ecosystem dynamics.  苔藓学研究对于理解生态系统动态至关重要。
Mosses collected for bryological analysis. 为苔藓学分析收集的苔藓。
近义词:muscological
反义词:None
词性:adjective

bryology:/braɪˈɒlədʒi/
noun
苔藓学
The scientific study of mosses and liverworts.
She specialized in bryology during her postgraduate studies. 她在研究生学习期间专攻苔藓学。
Bryology helps us understand the role of these plants in various habitats. 苔藓学帮助我们了解这些植物在不同生境中的作用。
近义词:muscology
反义词:None
词性:noun
