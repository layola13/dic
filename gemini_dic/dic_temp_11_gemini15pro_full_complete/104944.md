kelpie:/ˈkɛlpi/ (UK) /ˈkɛlpi/ (US)| n.|水鬼，凯尔派（苏格兰和爱尔兰神话中一种栖息在河流湖泊里的水怪，通常以马的形态出现）|A kelpie is a mythological shape-shifting water spirit inhabiting the lochs and pools of Scotland and Ireland. It is usually described as appearing as a horse.|例句：Legend has it that the kelpie lures unsuspecting travelers to their deaths. 传说凯尔派引诱毫无戒心的旅行者走向死亡。

例句：The kelpie is said to be able to change its shape at will. 据说凯尔派能够随意改变形状。|近义词：water horse, water spirit, each-uisge|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
