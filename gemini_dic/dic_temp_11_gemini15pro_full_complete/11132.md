archean:/ɑːrˈkiːən/ | adj. n.| 太古代的；太古代 | Relating to or denoting the earliest eon in the history of the Earth, between 4,000 million and 2,500 million years ago.  Also, the eon itself.  | The Archean rocks contain some of the oldest fossils.  这些太古代岩石中包含一些最古老的化石。 |  Ancient, primordial | Modern, recent | adjective, noun
