douse:/daʊs/| vt. n.|浇灭，熄灭；弄湿，浸泡|to extinguish (a fire, flames, etc.) by pouring or throwing a liquid over; to plunge into water; drench.|例句：Firefighters doused the flames with water. 消防队员用水浇灭了火焰。

例句：He doused himself with cold water after his workout. 他锻炼后用冷水冲洗自己。|近义词：extinguish, quench, soak, drench|反义词：ignite, kindle|词性：动词, 名词

douse:/daʊs/| n.|浇灭，浸泡|An act of dousing; a soaking.|例句：The douse of cold water woke him up. 一盆冷水把他泼醒了。

例句：The plants received a thorough douse of fertilizer.  这些植物得到了充分的肥料浇灌。|近义词：soaking, wetting|反义词：drying|词性：名词
