misestimate:/ˌmɪsˈɛstɪmeɪt/ | v.  n.| 错误估计；估错 | To estimate incorrectly; to make a wrong judgment or calculation about something. | I misestimated the amount of time it would take to complete the project. 我错误估计了完成项目所需的时间。 | synonyms: underestimate, overestimate, miscalculate, misjudge | antonyms: estimate correctly, judge accurately | verb

misestimate:/ˌmɪsˈɛstɪmeɪt/ | n.| 错误估计；估错 | An incorrect estimation. |  My misestimate of the cost led to budget problems. 我对成本的错误估计导致了预算问题。| synonyms: underestimation, overestimation, miscalculation | antonyms: accurate estimation, correct judgment | noun
