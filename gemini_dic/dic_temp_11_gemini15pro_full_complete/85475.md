hassel:/ˈhæsəl/ (US), /ˈhæsl/ (UK)| n. v.|麻烦，困难; 烦扰，纠缠|A situation that is annoying because it involves doing something difficult or complicated; to annoy someone by repeatedly asking them something.|例句：It's such a hassel getting the kids ready for school in the morning. 早上让孩子们准备好上学真是麻烦。|近义词：trouble, difficulty, bother, annoyance, inconvenience, nuisance|反义词:ease, convenience, simplicity|词性:名词, 动词

hassel:/ˈhæsəl/ (US), /ˈhæsl/ (UK)| v.|烦扰，纠缠|To annoy someone by repeatedly asking them something.|例句：Stop hassling me! I'll do it when I'm ready. 别再烦我了！我准备好了就会做的。|近义词：pester, bother, annoy, harass|反义词:leave alone, ignore|词性:动词
