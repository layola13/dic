mineralogists:/ˌmɪnərˈælədʒɪsts/ | n. | 矿物学家 | A scientist who studies minerals, their composition, structure, properties, and occurrence. |例句：The mineralogists analyzed the rock samples to identify the minerals present. 矿物学家分析了岩石样本以识别存在的矿物质。

例句：Mineralogists play a crucial role in understanding the Earth's composition and history. 矿物学家在理解地球的组成和历史方面起着至关重要的作用。| 近义词: geologists, geochemists | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | Noun
