ETYMOLOGY->
From the Middle English *curtesie*, a shortening of *curteisye* "courtesy," which ultimately comes from Old French *corteisie* "courtesy, courtliness." The word originally referred to the manners of the court, and the curtsy was a gesture associated with courtly behavior.
