conciliate: /kənˈsɪlieɪt/ | vt. | 安抚，调解，调停 | To stop someone from being angry or discontented; placate; to reconcile, pacify. | The company tried to conciliate the angry customers by offering them a full refund.  公司试图通过提供全额退款来安抚愤怒的顾客。

The mediator worked to conciliate the two warring factions. 调解员努力调解这两个交战派别。 | 近义词: appease, placate, pacify, reconcile, mediate | 反义词: antagonize, provoke, aggravate, estrange | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin conciliatus, past participle of conciliare ("to bring together, unite, win over, recommend"), from concilium ("council, meeting, gathering"), from com- ("together") + calare ("to call, summon").
