avocational:/ˌævəˈkeɪʃənəl/ | adj. |  副业的；嗜好的；业余爱好的 | Relating to a hobby or minor occupation pursued for pleasure rather than financial gain. |  She pursued photography as an avocational interest. 她把摄影作为一种业余爱好。| synonyms:  amateur, hobbyist, leisure, recreational, spare-time | antonyms: professional, vocational, occupational | 词性: adjective

avocation:/ˌævəˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 副业；嗜好；业余爱好 | A hobby or minor occupation pursued for pleasure rather than financial gain. |  His avocation is collecting stamps. 他的业余爱好是集邮。| synonyms: hobby, pastime, leisure pursuit, diversion | antonyms:  profession, vocation, occupation, job | 词性: noun
