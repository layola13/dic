moulding:/ˈmoʊldɪŋ/ (US) /ˈməʊldɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|  造型，塑造；装饰线条；铸件|A decorative strip of shaped wood, plastic, or plaster fixed along the top or bottom of a wall.  |The ornate moulding around the ceiling added a touch of elegance to the room.  房间天花板周围华丽的装饰线条增添了一丝优雅。|Synonyms: trim, cornice, molding (US spelling)|Antonyms: N/A|noun

moulding:/ˈmoʊldɪŋ/ (US) /ˈməʊldɪŋ/ (UK)| n.| 模具；铸造|The process of shaping something in a mould. |The moulding of plastic toys is a highly automated process. 塑料玩具的模制是一个高度自动化的过程。|Synonyms: casting, forming, shaping|Antonyms: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *moldinge*, from Old French *moldeure*, from *moldre* (“to mould”), from Latin *molere* (“to grind, mill”).
