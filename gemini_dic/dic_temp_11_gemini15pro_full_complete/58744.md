dunged:/dʌndʒd/
adjective
施过粪的；粪肥的；幽暗的，阴森森的
Manured; filthy; dark and gloomy.
The dungeon was dark and dunged.  地牢又黑又脏。
近义词:manured, fertilized, filthy, squalid, dark, gloomy
反义词:clean, bright
词性:adjective

dunged:/dʌndʒd/
verb (past participle of *dung*)
施肥，用粪便施肥
Manured with dung.
The fields had been recently dunged. 田地最近施过肥了。
近义词:manured, fertilized
反义词:unfertilized
词性:verb (past participle)


词源: From *dung* + *-ed*.  The word *dung* comes from Old English *dung*, and ultimately from Proto-Germanic *dunga-*.
