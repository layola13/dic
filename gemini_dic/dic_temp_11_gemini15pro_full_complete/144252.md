outsnatched:/ˌaʊtˈsnætʃt/
verb
抢夺; 攫取
past tense and past participle of *outsnatch*:  to snatch something away from someone else, especially in a quick or unexpected way.
He outsnatched the ball from his opponent's grasp. 他从对手手中抢走了球。
近义词:grab, seize, wrest, snatch
反义词:give, offer, relinquish
词性:verb
