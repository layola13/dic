bellylike:/ˈbɛliˌlaɪk/
adjective|像肚子的；大肚子的|Resembling a belly; having a large belly.|The Buddha statue had a bellylike appearance.  佛像有个像肚子的外观。|The overfed cat had a bellylike shape.  这只被过度喂养的猫体型像个大肚子。|近义词：potbellied, paunchy, rotund|反义词：slim, thin|词性:adjective
词源: From "belly" + "-like." "Belly" comes from Old English "belg," meaning "bag, pouch, belly." "-like" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling, characteristic of."
