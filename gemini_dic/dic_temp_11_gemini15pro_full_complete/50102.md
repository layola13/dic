dentition:/dɛnˈtɪʃən/
n.
齿列；牙列；牙系
The arrangement or condition of the teeth in a particular species or individual.
The horse's dentition showed that it was very young. 这匹马的齿列表明它非常年轻。

dentition:/dɛnˈtɪʃən/
