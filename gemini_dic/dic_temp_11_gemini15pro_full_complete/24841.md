brownfield:/ˈbraʊnfiːld/| adj. n.|棕地，荒地|Previously developed land that is not currently in use, typically contaminated by industrial or commercial waste.  指以前开发过、目前未使用、通常被工业或商业废料污染的土地。|The government is encouraging investment in brownfield sites. 政府正在鼓励对棕地的投资。|Brownfield sites offer opportunities for urban regeneration. 棕地为城市更新提供了机会。|近义词：derelict, abandoned, contaminated|反义词:greenfield|词性:形容词, 名词
