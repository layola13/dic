ditrichotomous: /daɪˌtraɪˈkɒtəməs/ | adj. | 三分的，分成三部分的 | Divided into three parts, categories, or classes. | The ditrichotomous classification system divided the plants into three distinct groups.  这种三分的分类系统将植物分成三个不同的组。 | The survey presented a ditrichotomous choice: agree, disagree, or neutral.  该调查提出了一个三分的选择：同意、不同意或中立。 |  近义词: tripartite, threefold | 反义词:  undivided, whole | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Greek di- "two" + tri- "three" + -chotomos "cutting," from -tomia "a cutting," related to temnein "to cut."  来自希腊语 di- “二” + tri- “三” + -chotomos “切割”，来自 -tomia “切割”，与 temnein “切割”相关。
