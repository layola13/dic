nonbrowsing:/nɒnˈbraʊzɪŋ/
adjective
非浏览的，不浏览的
Not browsing; not looking through or perusing casually.
The library offers nonbrowsing access to certain digital archives.  图书馆提供对某些数字档案的非浏览访问。
Students were given nonbrowsing access to specific online journals for the research project.  学生被给予对特定在线期刊的非浏览访问权限，用于研究项目。
Near synonyms: restricted, limited, direct
Antonyms: browsing, perusing, exploring
word type: adjective
