exacerbation:/ɪɡˌzæsərˈbeɪʃən/ | n. |加重，恶化|an increase in the severity of a disease or its signs and symptoms|Her asthma was exacerbated by the cold weather. 她的哮喘因寒冷天气而加重。|近义词：aggravation, worsening, intensification|反义词: improvement, amelioration|noun

exacerbation:/ɪɡˌzæsərˈbeɪʃən/ | n. |激怒|an act of making something worse|The government's actions only exacerbated the situation. 政府的行动只会使情况恶化。|近义词：inflammation, irritation|反义词: appeasement, mitigation|noun
