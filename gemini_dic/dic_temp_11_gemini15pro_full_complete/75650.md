fujan:/ˈfuːdʒæn/| n.|腐女|A term originating in Japan, referring to women who are passionate about Boy's Love (BL), a genre focusing on romantic relationships between male characters.  It often carries a negative connotation, implying obsessive or inappropriate interest. |Example:  She's such a fujan, she spends all her money on BL manga. 她是个腐女，她把所有的钱都花在BL漫画上了。|Example: The fujan community is very active online. 腐女群体在网上非常活跃。|近义词: BL fan, yaoi fan |反义词: None (lack of a direct antonym) |词性: noun
