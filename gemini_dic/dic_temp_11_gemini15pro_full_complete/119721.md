midst's:/mɪdstz/| n.|中心，当中|The possessive form of "midst," indicating something belonging to or associated with the middle of something.|In the midst's of chaos, he remained calm. 在混乱之中，他保持冷静。|近义词：center's, middle's|反义词：edge's, periphery's|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *middes, from Old English middes, genitive of midd (“middle”). Cognate with Dutch middens, German mitten.
