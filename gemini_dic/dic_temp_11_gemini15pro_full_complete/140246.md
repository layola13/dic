offprinted:/ˌɒfˈprɪntɪd/ | adj. |  重印的；抽印的 |  Printed separately from a larger publication, such as a book or journal.  |  This article was offprinted from the journal "Nature." 这篇文章是从《自然》杂志上抽印出来的。 | reprint, separate | original | adjective

offprint:/ˈɒfˌprɪnt/ | n. | 抽印本；重印本 | A separately printed copy of an article, chapter, or other excerpt from a larger publication. |  I requested an offprint of the professor's latest research paper. 我索要了一份教授最新研究论文的抽印本。 | reprint, separate | original | noun
