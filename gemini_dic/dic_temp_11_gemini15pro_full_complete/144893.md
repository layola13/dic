over-served:/ˌoʊvərˈsɜːrvd/
adjective
过度招待的；被灌醉的
Having been served too much alcohol, typically to the point of intoxication.
The restaurant was liable for the accident because they had over-served the customer.  这家餐馆对这起事故负有责任，因为他们过度招待了那位顾客。
He was clearly over-served at the party and became quite boisterous. 他在聚会上显然被灌醉了，变得非常喧闹。
近义词: intoxicated, drunk, tipsy
反义词: sober, under-served
