interested: /ˈɪntrəstɪd/ (US) /ˈɪntrestɪd/ (UK)| adj. | 感兴趣的；关心的 | Having a feeling of interest; wanting to learn more about something or to be involved in something. | She was very interested in the history of art. 她对艺术史非常感兴趣。| I'm interested in learning more about this opportunity. 我有兴趣了解更多关于这个机会的信息。| 近义词: curious, intrigued, fascinated, keen | 反义词: uninterested, apathetic, indifferent, bored | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Middle English *interessed, from Anglo-Norman enteresser, from Old French interess-, stem of enteresser (“to concern, touch, affect, interest”), from Late Latin interessāre, from Latin inter (“between”) + esse (“to be”).
