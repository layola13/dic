ducts:/dʌkts/ | n. | 管道，导管 | Tubes or passages that carry liquids, gases, etc. | The air conditioning system uses a network of ducts to distribute cool air throughout the building.  空调系统使用管道网络将冷气输送到整个建筑物。| synonyms: channels, tubes, pipes, conduits | antonyms: blockages, obstructions | noun

ducts:/dʌkts/ | v. |输送，引导 | To convey or direct something through a duct.  | The company ducts water from the river to irrigate the fields.  该公司将河水引入管道来灌溉田地。| synonyms: channel, convey, pipe, transmit | antonyms: obstruct, block, impede | verb
