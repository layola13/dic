ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *deorcnian, from deorc "dark".  The "-ing" suffix transforms the adjective "dark" into a verb (the act of becoming dark) or a noun (the process of becoming dark).  It can also function as an adjective describing something in the process of becoming dark.
