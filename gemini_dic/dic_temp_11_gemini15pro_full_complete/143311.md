outduels:/ˌaʊtˈduːəlz/
verb
胜过，击败（在决斗中）
defeats someone in a duel or contest of skill.
He outdueled his opponent in the championship match. 他在冠军赛中击败了对手。
Synonyms: outfights, outmaneuvers, overcomes, defeats, bests
Antonyms: loses to, is defeated by
词性: 动词


outdueling:/ˌaʊtˈduːəlɪŋ/
noun
决斗中胜过，击败
the act of defeating someone in a duel or contest of skill
His outdueling of his rival cemented his reputation as the best swordsman. 他战胜对手的举动巩固了他作为最佳剑客的声誉。
Synonyms: outfighting, outmaneuvering, overcoming, defeating, besting
Antonyms: losing, being defeated
词性: 名词 (动名词)
