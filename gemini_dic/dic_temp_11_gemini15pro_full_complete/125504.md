multiregional:/ˌmʌltiˈriːdʒənəl/
adjective|多区域的，多地区的|Characterized by or involving several regions.|The multiregional hypothesis of human evolution suggests that modern humans evolved in different parts of the world simultaneously.|近义词:polycentric, global, international, worldwide|反义词:local, regional, limited|词性:adjective

Etymology:
"multi-" (Latin, "many") + "regional" (from "region," ultimately from Latin "regio").
