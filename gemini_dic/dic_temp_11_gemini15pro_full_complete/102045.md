isolectic:/ˌaɪsoʊˈlɛktɪk/ | adj. | 全同立构的 |  Relating to or denoting a polymer in which all the repeating units have the same stereochemical configuration. | The properties of isolectic polypropylene differ significantly from those of atactic polypropylene. 全同立构聚丙烯的性质与无规立构聚丙烯的性质显著不同。|  isotactic | atactic | adjective
