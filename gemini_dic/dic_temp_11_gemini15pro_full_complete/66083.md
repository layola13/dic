excavate: /ˈekskəveɪt/ (US) /ˈɛkskəveɪt/ (UK)
verb
挖掘；开凿；发掘
to dig a hole or channel in the ground, especially with a machine; to remove earth carefully from (an area) in order to find buried objects
例句：Archaeologists are excavating the site of an ancient city.  考古学家正在挖掘一座古城的遗址。

例句：The workers excavated a tunnel through the mountain. 工人们在山中开凿了一条隧道。

近义词：dig, unearth, uncover, disinter
反义词：bury, fill, cover
词性：v.
