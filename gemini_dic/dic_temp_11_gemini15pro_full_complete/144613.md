outwhirls:/ˌaʊtˈhwɜːrlz/ | v. | 超越旋转，胜过旋转 | Surpasses in whirling; whirls faster or more powerfully than. | The dust devil outwhirls the leaves in a frantic dance.  尘卷风疯狂地旋转着，胜过了树叶的旋转。 |  The helicopter's blades outwhirled the wind itself. 直升机的螺旋桨旋转速度超过了风本身。 | synonyms: outspin, outrotate, surpass | antonyms: lag, trail | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From 'out-' (prefix meaning "exceeding, surpassing") + 'whirl' (verb meaning "to turn rapidly").
