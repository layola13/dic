chomper:/ˈtʃɒmpə(r)/ (US) /ˈtʃɑːmpər/ (UK)| n.|大吃大喝的人；贪吃的人；（动物的）牙齿，嚼东西|A person or animal that chomps or bites something vigorously.  Relating to teeth or the act of chewing.|例句：That kid's a real chomper; he ate three hot dogs in five minutes. 那孩子真是个大胃王，五分钟就吃了三个热狗。

例句：The lion's chompers looked sharp and dangerous.  狮子的牙齿看起来又尖又危险。|近义词：eater, muncher, chewer, glutton, gourmand|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider someone with a delicate appetite)|词性: noun
