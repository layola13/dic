anticulturally:/ˌæntiˈkʌltʃərəli/
adj.
反文化的
opposed to or hostile toward the dominant culture or its values
His anticultural stance alienated him from mainstream society. 他的反文化立场使他与主流社会格格不入。

The artist's work was seen as anticultural and controversial. 这位艺术家的作品被视为反文化且具有争议性。

countercultural, nonconformist, rebellious
pro-establishment, conventional, conformist
adjective
