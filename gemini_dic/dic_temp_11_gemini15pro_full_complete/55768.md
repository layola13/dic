diversifies:/daɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪz/
verb
使多样化；使变化；分散（投资）
To make or become more diverse or varied; to expand into a range of different activities or investments.
The company has diversified its product line to include clothing and accessories.  该公司将其产品线多元化，增加了服装和配饰。
They diversified their investments to reduce risk. 他们分散投资以降低风险。
synonyms: vary, expand, branch out, broaden, spread
antonyms: specialize, focus, concentrate, narrow, limit
词性: 动词
