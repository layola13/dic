flagginess:/ˈflæɡɪnɪs/
noun
衰弱；疲惫；萎靡不振
The state of being flagging; lack of energy or enthusiasm.
His recent flagginess was concerning his friends.  他最近的萎靡不振让他的朋友们很担心。
lassitude, weariness, fatigue, weakness, exhaustion, enervation
energy, vigor, vitality, strength
