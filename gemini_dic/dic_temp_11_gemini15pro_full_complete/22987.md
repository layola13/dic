boud:/baʊd/
| adj. |Destined; fated.  (苏格兰语/古英语)| Bound by fate to meet again. 注定要再次相遇。|He felt boud to help her. 他觉得命中注定要帮助她。| 近义词：destined, fated, predetermined | 反义词: free, unconstrained | 词性: 形容词
词源：From Middle English *bōwen, from Old Norse *búinn (“prepared, ready, finished”), past participle of *búa (“to prepare, reside, dwell”). Cognate with Danish and Norwegian bo (“to reside”), Icelandic búa (“to prepare, reside”), Swedish bo (“to reside”). More at bow (verb). Related to bound (past participle).

注意事项： “boud”主要用于苏格兰语和一些古英语方言中，在现代英语中不常用。  它的意思是“注定”或“受约束”。  在现代英语中，“bound”更常用。
