nonflexibility:/ˌnɑːnˌflɛksəˈbɪləti/
noun
不灵活性，僵化
Lack of flexibility; inability or unwillingness to bend or change.
The nonflexibility of the company's policies made it difficult to adapt to changing market conditions.  公司的政策僵化，使其难以适应不断变化的市场环境。
近义词: rigidity, inflexibility, stiffness
反义词: flexibility, adaptability, pliability
词性: noun
