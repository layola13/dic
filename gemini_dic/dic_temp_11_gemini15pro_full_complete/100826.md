introduce:/ˌɪntrəˈd(j)uːs/ /ˌɪntrəˈduːs/| vt.|介绍，引进，提出|To make (someone) known to another by name and often other personal details; to bring (something, such as a product, measure, or concept) into use or operation for the first time.|例句：I'd like to introduce my friend, John. 我想介绍一下我的朋友，约翰。

例句：The company introduced a new product line last year.  这家公司去年推出了一条新的产品线。|近义词：present, familiarize, launch|反义词:remove, withdraw|词性:verb
