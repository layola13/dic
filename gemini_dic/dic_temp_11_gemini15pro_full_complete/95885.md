increasingly:/ɪnˈkriːsɪŋli/
adverb
越来越；日益；逐渐地
becoming greater in amount, intensity, or degree.
The world is becoming increasingly interconnected. 世界正变得越来越相互关联。
The situation is increasingly difficult. 局势越来越困难。
近义词: progressively, more and more, gradually
反义词: decreasingly, less and less
词性: adverb
词源: From increase + -ing + -ly.  Ultimately from Latin *increscere, from in- "in" + crescere "to grow."
