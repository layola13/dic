monotony:/məˈnɒtəni/ /məˈnɑːtəni/| n.|单调，乏味|Lack of variation and interest; tedious repetition and routine.|The monotony of the desert landscape was oppressive. 沙漠景观的单调令人压抑。|近义词：tedium, dullness, boredom, sameness|反义词:variety, diversity, excitement, change|词性:noun

monotonous:/məˈnɒtənəs/ /məˈnɑːtənəs/| adj.|单调的，乏味的|Dull, tedious, and repetitious; lacking in variety and interest.|The monotonous drone of the engine made me sleepy.  发动机单调的嗡嗡声让我昏昏欲睡。|近义词：tedious, dull, boring, repetitive|反义词:varied, diverse, exciting, stimulating|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *monotonie*, from Late Latin *monotonia*, from Greek *monotōnia*  "uniformity of sound,"  from *monos* "single, alone" + *tonos* "tone, pitch."
