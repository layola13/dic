epipharyngeal:/ˌɛpɪfəˈrɪndʒiəl/ /ˌɛpɪfəˈrɪndʒɪəl/
adjective|咽上部的，鼻咽部的|Relating to the epipharynx, the upper part of the pharynx, located behind the nasal cavity.|The epipharyngeal lymph tissues are prone to infection. 咽上部淋巴组织容易感染。|
近义词:nasopharyngeal|反义词:|词性:adjective

epipharynx:/ˌɛpɪˈfærɪŋks/ /ˌɛpɪˈfærɪŋks/
noun|会厌，咽上部|A cone-shaped flap of cartilage located at the base of the tongue, which prevents food from entering the trachea (windpipe) during swallowing. Also refers to the upper part of the pharynx.|The epiglottis is a vital part of the epipharynx. 会厌是咽上部的重要组成部分。|近义词:|反义词:|词性:noun
