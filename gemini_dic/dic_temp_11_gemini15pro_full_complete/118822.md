metanephridium:/ˌmɛtəˈnɛfrɪdɪəm/ | n. | 后肾管 | A type of excretory gland found in many invertebrates, such as annelids and mollusks. It typically consists of a ciliated funnel opening into the body cavity and a duct that leads to an excretory pore. | Earthworms have metanephridia to filter waste from their body fluids. 蚯蚓有后肾管来过滤体液中的废物。 | nephridium, excretory organ |  None | noun
