apocarpous:/ˌæpoʊˈkɑːrpəs/| adj.|离生的，离心皮的|Having carpels that are free from one another.  A flower in which the carpels are separate is called apocarpous.|The magnolia flower is an example of an apocarpous flower.  木兰花是离心皮花的例子。|synonymous: choripetalous|antonymous: syncarpous|adjective
