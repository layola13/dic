belay:/bɪˈleɪ/| v. n.|停止，固定，保护(攀岩运动中)，证实，确认|To secure a climber by attaching a rope to a fixed point.  To stop or cease.|The climber shouted "Belay on!" to his partner. 攀岩者对他的同伴大喊“固定！” |The captain belayed the order to abandon ship. 船长取消了弃船的命令。|Synonyms: secure, fasten, stop, cancel, confirm|Antonyms: release, loosen, continue, deny|verb, noun

belay:/bɪˈleɪ/| n.|绳索的固定点|A secure point to which a rope is attached, especially in climbing.|The belay was strong and secure.  固定点很牢固。|Synonyms: anchor, fastening|Antonyms: N/A|noun
