hike:/haɪk/| n. vi. vt.|远足，徒步旅行；增加；提高|A long walk especially in the country or wilderness.|We went on a hike in the mountains. 我们去山上远足了。|近义词：walk, trek, tramp, ramble, excursion|反义词:rest, stay|noun, verb

hike:/haɪk/| n. vi. vt.|大幅提高，猛增|A sharp increase, especially in price.|The government hiked taxes. 政府大幅提高了税收。|近义词：increase, raise, boost, escalate|反义词:decrease, reduce, lower|noun, verb

hikes (plural of hike):/haɪks/| n. |远足，徒步旅行；增加；提高（复数）|Multiple instances of long walks or sharp increases.|We went on several hikes last summer. 去年夏天我们进行了几次远足。|近义词：walks, treks, excursions|反义词:rests, stays|noun
