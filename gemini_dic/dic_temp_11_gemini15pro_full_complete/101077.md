inventorially:/ɪnˌvɛnˈtɔːriəli/
adjective
清单地；存货地
In a way that relates to inventory (the goods held by a business).  Relating to listing and evaluating assets.
The company tracks its assets inventorially.  公司以清单的方式追踪其资产。
The museum has begun to catalog its holdings inventorially.  博物馆已开始以清单的方式对其馆藏进行编目。
Synonym:  as part of inventory, as stock
Antonym:  non-inventoried, off-book
