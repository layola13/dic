avoiders:/əˈvɔɪdərz/ | n. | 逃避者；回避者 | People who tend to avoid certain situations, activities, or people. |  Example: Avoiders often struggle with social anxiety. 逃避者经常与社交焦虑作斗争。| Synonym: shirkers, evaders | Antonym: confronters, embracers | noun

avoiders:/əˈvɔɪdərz/ | n. | 回避者；躲避者 | Individuals who systematically try to escape unpleasant or challenging experiences. | Example: He is one of those avoiders who never deal with their problems directly. 他是那种从不直接处理问题的人。| Synonym: escapists, dodgers | Antonym: pursuers, tacklers | noun
