clung:/klʌŋ/
verb
粘住，紧握，依附，坚持
Past participle of cling.  It means to hold on tightly to something.
He clung to the rope with all his might. 他用尽全力抓着绳子。
近义词: adhered, grasped, gripped, held
反义词: released, let go
词性: verb

clung:/klʌŋ/
verb
(对信仰、希望、想法) 紧抱，固守，坚持；墨守；抱定
Past participle of cling.  It describes maintaining a belief, hope, or idea strongly.
She clung to the hope that he was still alive. 她紧紧抱着他还活着的希望。
近义词:  maintained, retained, preserved
反义词: abandoned, relinquished
词性: verb
