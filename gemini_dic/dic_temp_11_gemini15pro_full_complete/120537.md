mir:/mɪr/ | n. | 世界; 和平; 公社; 村社 | A Russian word meaning "world," "peace," or "village community."  Historically, it referred to a self-governing peasant community in Russia. |  The mir system played a significant role in Russian history.  Мир制度在俄罗斯历史上扮演了重要的角色。| synonyms: community, commune, world | antonyms: discord, conflict | noun
