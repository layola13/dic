blessedest:/ˈblesɪdɪst/| adj.|最受祝福的，最幸福的|Superlative form of blessed, meaning most fortunate or holy.|例句：The blessedest of all men are those who love God. 所有的人中最受祝福的是那些爱上帝的人。|例句：She considered herself the blessedest woman alive. 她认为自己是活着的最幸福的女人。|近义词：holiest, most fortunate, luckiest|反义词：cursed, damned, most unfortunate|词性:形容词

词源: From Old English *blēdsiendest, superlative of blēdsian "to bless," from blōd "blood" (originally in a sacrificial sense).  The superlative ending -est is a common Germanic form.
