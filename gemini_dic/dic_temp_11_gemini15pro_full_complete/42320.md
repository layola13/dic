counter-revolution:/ˌkaʊntər rɛvəˈluːʃən/| n.|反革命，反动|A counter-revolution is a movement or action that opposes a previous revolution and attempts to reverse its effects. |The counter-revolution aimed to restore the monarchy. 反革命旨在恢复君主制。

The government brutally suppressed the counter-revolution. 政府残酷地镇压了反革命。|近义词：reaction, revolt, uprising|反义词：revolution|词性:名词
