bloater:/ˈbloʊtər/ (US), /ˈbləʊtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |膨胀物；过度肥胖的人；（软件）代码膨胀；熏鲱鱼|A bloater is something that has become swollen or excessively large.  In software development, it refers to code that has become unnecessarily large and complex. It can also refer to a type of smoked herring.|例句：The project became a bloater after years of adding features without refactoring.  这个项目在多年来添加功能而没有重构之后变得臃肿不堪。

例句：He looked like a bloater after the holidays. 他假期过后看起来像个胖子。|近义词：swollen, inflated, obese, bloated (adj.)|反义词：slim, thin, lean, compact (software)|词性: 名词
