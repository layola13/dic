finickiest:/ˈfɪnɪkiɪst/| adj.|最挑剔的，最吹毛求疵的|superlative adjective of finicky; excessively particular, demanding, or fussy, especially about details.| She's the finickiest eater I know; she only eats organic, locally sourced food. 她是我认识的最挑剔的食客；她只吃有机、本地采购的食物。|He was so finickiest about the placement of the furniture that it took hours to arrange the room. 他对家具的摆放非常挑剔，以至于花了几个小时才把房间布置好。|近义词：fussy, picky, particular, fastidious, choosy, demanding|反义词：undemanding, easygoing, tolerant, adaptable|词性:形容词

词源:
From finick + -y + -est.  Finick (now obsolete or dialectal) meant "to be finical or fussy," perhaps related to Low German fiken "to fidget, twitch."
