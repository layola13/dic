insubordination:/ˌɪnsəˌbɔːrdɪˈneɪʃn/
n.
不服从，违抗命令
Defiance of authority; refusal to obey orders.
His insubordination will cost him his job. 他的不服从将让他丢掉工作。

近义词: disobedience, defiance, rebellion, mutiny
反义词: obedience, compliance, submission
词性: 名词

insubordinate:/ˌɪnsəˈbɔːrdɪnət/
adj.
不服从的，违抗的
Not submitting to authority; disobedient.
The insubordinate soldier was court-martialed.  这名不服从命令的士兵被送上了军事法庭。

近义词: disobedient, defiant, rebellious, mutinous
反义词: obedient, compliant, submissive
词性: 形容词


词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin insubordinatio(n-), from in- ‘not’ + subordinare (see subordinate).
