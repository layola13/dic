buttercream:/ˈbʌtərkriːm/| n.|奶油霜，黄油乳脂|A sweet, creamy mixture of butter, sugar, and often other ingredients, used for filling and frosting cakes.|例句：The cake was decorated with a rich chocolate buttercream.  这个蛋糕用浓郁的巧克力奶油霜装饰。

例句：She piped delicate swirls of vanilla buttercream onto the cupcakes. 她用香草奶油霜在纸杯蛋糕上挤出精致的漩涡。|近义词：frosting, icing|反义词:|noun
