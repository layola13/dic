mirza:/ˈmɪərzə/ | n. | 王子；贵族头衔；先生 (敬称) | A title of respect for a prince or nobleman, especially in Persia and India.  Derived from the Persian "Amīrzā," meaning "son of the emir."  Often used as a courtesy title similar to "Mr." | Example 1: Mirza Ghalib was a renowned Urdu poet.  米尔扎·加利卜是一位著名的乌尔都语诗人。

Example 2: He addressed him as Mirza Sahib. 他称他为米尔扎先生。 | Near Synonyms: prince, nobleman, lord, sir, mister | Antonyms: commoner, peasant | noun
