kabir:/kəˈbɪər/| n.|卡比尔，卡比尔诗歌|A mystic poet and saint of India (c. 1440-1518), whose beliefs, a unique synthesis of Hinduism and Islam, are still influential in India today. Kabir's poetry is characterized by its directness and its rejection of ritualistic religion.|Kabir's poems are still sung today. 卡比尔的诗歌至今仍在传唱。|近义词：poet, mystic, saint|反义词:None|词性:noun
