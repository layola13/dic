flash-on:/ˈflæʃ ɒn/ (UK) /ˈflæʃ ɑːn/ (US)| n.| 闪光 |A sudden burst of light. |The flash-on blinded me momentarily. 闪光使我暂时失明。|近义词: flash, burst |反义词: darkness |词性:名词
