membranes:/ˈmɛmbreɪnz/ | n. | 膜，隔膜 | Thin layers of tissue that separate different parts of the body or enclose structures. | Cell membranes control what enters and leaves the cell. 细胞膜控制物质进出细胞。| synonyms: films, sheets, linings | antonyms: openings, gaps | noun

membranes:/ˈmɛmbreɪnz/ | n. | 膜片，振动膜 | A thin, flexible sheet that vibrates to produce sound. | The eardrum is a membrane that vibrates in response to sound waves. 鼓膜是一种响应声波而振动的膜。| synonyms: diaphragms | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider rigid structures) | noun
