on-your-uppers:/ɒn jɔːr ˈʌpəz/ | adj. | 穷困潦倒的，身无分文的 | Being in a state of extreme poverty; having very little or no money. |  Example: They were on their uppers after both losing their jobs.  他们俩都失业后，穷困潦倒。| Example:  He's been on his uppers for months, relying on food banks to survive.  他已经几个月身无分文了，靠食品银行生存。 | Synonym: impoverished, destitute, penniless | Antonym: wealthy, affluent | adjective
