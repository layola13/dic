dowryless: /ˈdaʊri.ləs/ | adj. | 没有嫁妆的 | Without a dowry; lacking a dowry.  A woman who is dowryless enters marriage without bringing any property or financial assets with her. |  She was a dowryless bride, but her family was known for their kindness and generosity. 她是一位没有嫁妆的新娘，但她的家人以善良和慷慨而闻名。

Her dowryless status made her a less desirable match in the eyes of some families.  她没有嫁妆的地位使她在一些家庭眼中成为不太理想的结婚对象。 | 近义词： portionless, dowerless | 反义词： dowered | 词性: 形容词
