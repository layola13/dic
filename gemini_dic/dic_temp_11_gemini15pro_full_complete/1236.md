accomplice: /əˈkʌmplɪs/ | n. | 共犯，帮凶 | A person who helps another person commit a crime or do something wrong. |  He was arrested as an accomplice to the robbery. 他因参与抢劫而被捕。

The getaway driver was an accomplice to the bank heist. 逃跑的司机是银行抢劫案的同伙。 |  Synonyms:  partner in crime, accessory, collaborator, confederate | Antonyms: opponent, adversary, enemy | noun


Etymology:
From Middle French *complice, from Late Latin complex, complic-, "partner, confederate," from Latin complicāre "to fold together," from com- "together" + plicāre "to fold."
