ahistoricism:/ˌeɪhɪˈstɔːrɪsɪzəm/
n.
非历史性；非历史主义；脱离历史
A lack of awareness of or interest in history; an approach to understanding something without considering its historical context.
The author’s ahistoricism led him to misinterpret the events entirely.  作者的非历史主义导致他完全曲解了这些事件。

Her analysis suffers from ahistoricism, ignoring the social context of the time. 她的分析存在非历史主义的缺陷，忽略了当时的社会背景。

近义词:  historical ignorance, disregard for history
反义词:  historicism, historical consciousness
词性: noun
