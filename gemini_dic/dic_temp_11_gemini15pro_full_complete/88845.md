hogshead: /ˈhɒɡzhɛd/ (UK) /ˈhɑːɡzhɛd/ (US) | n. |大桶；桶（液量单位）|A large cask, especially one of a capacity varying from 63 to 140 gallons (about 238 to 530 liters), formerly used for liquids such as wine or beer. |例句：The hogshead of ale was rolled into the tavern.  那桶麦芽酒被滚进了酒馆。

例句：The merchant ordered three hogsheads of wine from France.  这个商人从法国订购了三大桶葡萄酒。


|近义词：cask, barrel, butt|反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of volume)|词性: noun
