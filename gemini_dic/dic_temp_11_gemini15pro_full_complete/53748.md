discolouring:/dɪsˈkʌlərɪŋ/
adjective
变色的，褪色的
causing loss of colour; staining
The sun is discolouring the curtains.  太阳晒得窗帘褪色了。
近义词:fading, bleaching, staining
反义词:colouring, brightening
词性:adjective


discolour:/dɪsˈkʌlər/
verb
使变色，使褪色
to (cause something to) change from its original colour to a less attractive one
Exposure to sunlight can discolour fabrics. 暴露在阳光下会导致织物褪色。
近义词:stain, fade, bleach
反义词:colour, brighten
词性:verb
