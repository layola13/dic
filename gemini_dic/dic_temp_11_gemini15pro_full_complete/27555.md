canai:/ˈtʃɑːnaɪ/ /ˈtʃɑːni/| n.|印度煎饼，一种南印度和东南亚流行的扁平面包|A type of flaky flatbread, popular in South India and Southeast Asia, often served with dhal or curry.|例句：I had a delicious canai for breakfast this morning. 我今天早上吃了一份美味的煎饼。

例句：The canai was crispy and fluffy, and the curry was perfectly spiced.  煎饼酥脆蓬松，咖喱的香料也恰到好处。


近义词：roti, paratha, flatbread|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "thick bread")|词性:noun
