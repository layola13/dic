asterisk: /ˈæstərɪsk/ | n. | 星号，星形符号 | A small star-like symbol (*) used in printing or writing to indicate a footnote, omission, etc. |  Example: The asterisk refers to a footnote at the bottom of the page. 星号指的是页面底部的脚注。

Example:  He used an asterisk to mark the important sentences. 他用星号标记重要的句子。


|近义词：star, |反义词:  (No direct antonym for a symbol) | 词性: noun
