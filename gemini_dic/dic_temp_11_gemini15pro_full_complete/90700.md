humectant:/hjuːˈmektənt/ | n. |保湿剂；润湿剂 |A substance that absorbs or helps another substance retain moisture. | This lotion contains several humectants to keep your skin hydrated. 这款乳液含有多种保湿剂，可保持肌肤水润。| Glycerin is a common humectant used in skincare products. 甘油是一种常用的护肤品保湿剂。 | synonyms: moisturizer, emollient | antonyms: desiccant, drying agent | noun
