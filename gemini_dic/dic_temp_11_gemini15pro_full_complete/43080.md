cowries:/ˈkaʊriz/ (US), /ˈkaʊriːz/ (UK)| n.|贝壳；(尤指)宝贝；用作货币的贝壳|Small shells, especially those of certain mollusks used as money in parts of Africa and Asia. |Cowries were once used as currency in many parts of the world. 宝贝曾在世界许多地方用作货币。

Cowries are often used in jewelry and crafts. 宝贝经常用于珠宝和工艺品。|Synonyms: shells, currency (in historical context)|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, possibly modern currency)|Noun
