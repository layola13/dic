malevolence:/məˈlɛvələns/| n. |恶意，狠毒|The villain's malevolence was evident in his cruel actions. 这个恶棍的恶意在他的残忍行为中显而易见。|例句：He felt no malevolence toward his rivals. 他对他的对手没有恶意。

She glared at him with pure malevolence. 她带着十足的恶意瞪着他。|近义词：malice, spite, ill will, hostility, venom|反义词：benevolence, goodwill, kindness, compassion|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin malevolentia, from Latin malevolent- ‘wishing ill’, from male ‘badly’ + volent- ‘wishing’ (from the verb velle).
