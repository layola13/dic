cowage: /ˈkaʊɪdʒ/ /ˈkaʊɪtʃ/ | n. | 刺毛豆，绒毛豆 | A tropical American plant (Mucuna pruriens) having hairy pods that cause intense itching.  The stinging hairs from the pods are sometimes used medicinally. |  The cowage plant is notorious for its irritating hairs. 刺毛豆植物因其刺激性的毛发而臭名昭著。

Cowage is sometimes used in traditional medicine to treat intestinal worms.  刺毛豆有时在传统医学中用于治疗肠道蠕虫。 | 近义词: cowitch,  velvet bean | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a plant. Could consider antonyms related to its effect, like soothing, calming) | 词性: noun
