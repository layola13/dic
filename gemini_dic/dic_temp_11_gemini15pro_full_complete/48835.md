defoliating:/ˌdiːˈfoʊliˌeɪtɪŋ/
adjective|落叶的; 使落叶的; 脱叶的|causing leaves to fall off|例句：The defoliating effects of the herbicide were quickly apparent. 除草剂的落叶效果很快显现出来。|近义词：leaf-shedding, deciduous|反义词: evergreen, persistent|词性: adjective
defoliate:/ˌdiːˈfoʊliˌeɪt/
verb|使落叶; 脱叶;|to cause the leaves of a plant to fall off|例句：The trees were defoliated by the caterpillars.  这些树的叶子被毛毛虫吃光了。|近义词：strip, denude|反义词:  refoliate, leaf|词性: verb
defoliation:/ˌdiːˌfoʊliˈeɪʃən/
noun|落叶; 脱叶; defoliation|the act of causing leaves to fall off|例句：Defoliation of the trees can be caused by several factors. 树木落叶可能是由多种因素造成的。|近义词：leaf fall, shedding|反义词: foliation|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dēfoliāre, from dē- (“from, down, away, off”) + folium (“leaf”).
