capitalism: /ˈkæpɪtəlɪzəm/ | n. | 资本主义 | An economic and political system in which a country's trade and industry are controlled by private owners for profit, rather than by the state. |  Many believe that capitalism is the most efficient economic system. 很多人认为资本主义是最有效的经济制度。

| Example: The United States is often described as a capitalist nation. 美国常被描述为一个资本主义国家。 | Synonyms: free enterprise, private enterprise | Antonyms: socialism, communism | noun
