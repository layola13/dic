fordone:/fɔːrˈdʌn/
adjective
筋疲力尽的；注定失败的；完蛋的
Exhausted, worn out; ruined, doomed.
The hikers were fordone after their three-day trek through the mountains. 这些徒步旅行者在穿越山区的三天跋涉之后筋疲力尽。
His reputation was fordone after the scandal.  丑闻之后，他的名声毁了。
近义词:exhausted, worn out, spent, ruined, doomed
反义词:energetic, refreshed, thriving
词性:adjective
