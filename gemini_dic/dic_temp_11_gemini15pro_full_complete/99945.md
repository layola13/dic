internucleotide:/ˌɪntərˈnjuːkliːətaɪd/ | adj. | 核苷酸间的 | Situated between nucleotides; connecting nucleotides. | The internucleotide bond is formed through a phosphodiester linkage. 核苷酸间的键是通过磷酸二酯键形成的。| The internucleotide distance varies depending on the DNA structure. 核苷酸间的距离取决于DNA结构。 |  近义词: inter-nucleotide | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could potentially use terms like "intranucleotide" if referring to within a single nucleotide, though not a standard term) | 词性: adjective
