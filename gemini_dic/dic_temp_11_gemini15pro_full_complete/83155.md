guttering:/ˈɡʌtərɪŋ/ | n. | 水槽；檐槽；排水沟；烛泪；(感情的)流露 | A channel or pipe fixed to the edge of a roof to carry away rainwater. | The guttering was overflowing after the heavy rain. 大雨过后，檐槽都溢出来了。 |  The guttering is blocked with leaves. 排水沟被树叶堵塞了。 | synonyms: eavestrough, drainpipe, downspout, channel | antonyms: ridge, peak | noun


guttering:/ˈɡʌtərɪŋ/ | adj. |  摇曳的，忽明忽暗的；(感情)强烈的 |  (of a flame or candle) burning unsteadily with a sputtering sound; (of an emotion) experienced intensely. |  The guttering candle flame cast strange shadows on the wall. 摇曳的烛光在墙上投下奇异的阴影。 | A guttering sense of dread filled him. 一种强烈的恐惧感充满了他的内心。 | synonyms: flickering, wavering, sputtering, intense, powerful | antonyms: steady, constant, weak, mild | adjective
