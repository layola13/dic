alleging:/əˈledʒɪŋ/
verb
声称，断言，指控
present participle of allege, meaning to claim or assert that someone has done something illegal or wrong, typically without proof.
The newspaper is alleging that the mayor accepted bribes. 这家报纸声称市长收受贿赂。
He alleged that he had been assaulted. 他声称自己遭到了袭击。
synonyms: claiming, asserting, declaring, maintaining, stating
antonyms: denying, refuting, contradicting
