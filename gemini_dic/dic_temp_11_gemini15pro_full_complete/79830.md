glyn: /ɡlɪn/
n.
a wooded valley; a glen
(尤指威尔士的)树木繁茂的山谷；峡谷
The river flowed through the glyn. 这条河从峡谷中流过.
近义词: glen, valley, dale
反义词: mountain, hill
词性: 名词

词源: From Welsh *glinn, from Proto-Celtic *glennos. Cognate with Breton glen, Cornish glyn, Irish gleann, Manx glion, Scottish Gaelic gleann.
