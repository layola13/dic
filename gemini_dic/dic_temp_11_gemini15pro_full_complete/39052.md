n.|一致性，适合性，顺从|The conformableness of the new policy to the company's overall strategy ensured its smooth implementation. 新政策与公司整体战略的一致性确保了其顺利实施。|
conformableness:/kənˌfɔːrməblənəs/
Etymology:  From conformable + -ness. Conformable comes from Old French conformable, from Late Latin conformābilis, from conformāre "to conform," from Latin com- "together" + formāre "to form."
conformableness:/kənˌfɔːrməblənəs/
Synonyms: compatibility, suitability, compliance, obedience, conformity, agreement
conformableness:/kənˌfɔːrməblənəs/
Antonyms: incompatibility, unsuitability, noncompliance, disobedience, nonconformity, disagreement
