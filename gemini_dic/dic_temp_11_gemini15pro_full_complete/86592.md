heliographers:/ˌhiːliˈɒɡrəfəz/
n.
日光仪操作员；日光通信员
A person who operates a heliograph, a device used for signaling using sunlight.
The heliographers signaled the approaching ships. 日光仪操作员向驶近的船只发出信号。
近义词:signalmen, communicators
反义词:None
词性:Noun

heliograph:/ˈhiːliəɡrɑːf/
n.
日光仪；日光反射信号器
A device used for signaling using sunlight reflected by a mirror.
The army used a heliograph to communicate across long distances. 军队使用日光仪进行远距离通信。
近义词:semaphore, signal lamp
反义词:None
词性:Noun

heliography:/ˌhiːliˈɒɡrəfi/
n.
日光照相术；日光通信术
The art or practice of signaling using a heliograph or photographing by sunlight.
Heliography was an essential means of communication in the 19th century. 日光通信术是19世纪重要的通信手段。
近义词:photography, signaling
反义词:None
词性:Noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek hēlios "sun" + -graphos "writer," from graphein "to write."
