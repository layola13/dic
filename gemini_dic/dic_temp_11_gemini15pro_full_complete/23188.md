bowdlerizes:/ˈboʊdləˌraɪzɪz/
|v.|
|删节；净化；修订（使其适合儿童或道德标准）|
|To remove or change parts considered vulgar or immoral from (a book, play, film, etc.)|
|The publisher bowdlerized the novel before releasing it to a younger audience. 出版商在将小说发行给年轻读者之前对其进行了删节。|
|censors, expurgates, sanitizes|
|unabridged, unexpurgated|
|verb|
