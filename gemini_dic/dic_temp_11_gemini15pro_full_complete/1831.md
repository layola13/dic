acrocarpous:/ˌækrəˈkɑːrpəs/| adj.|顶果的，茎顶结实的|Having the fruiting bodies terminal on the branches or stems, as in certain mosses.|The acrocarpous moss formed a dense cushion on the rock. 顶果藓在岩石上形成了一个密集的垫子。|近义词: cladocarpous |反义词: pleurocarpous|词性: adjective
Etymology: From Greek *ákros* ("highest, outermost") + *karpós* ("fruit").
