accomplisher:/əˈkɑːmplɪʃər/
n.
完成者；成就者；成功者
A person who achieves or completes something successfully.
She is a remarkable accomplisher, having achieved great success in her field. 她是一位杰出的成就者，在她的领域取得了巨大的成功。
The company is looking for accomplishers who can deliver results. 公司正在寻找能够交付成果的成功人士。
Synonyms: achiever, winner, victor, conqueror
Antonyms: failure, loser, underachiever
Noun
