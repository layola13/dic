oratorship: /ˈɔːrətərʃɪp/ (US) /ˈɒrətərʃɪp/ (UK)
n.
雄辩术；演说技巧；演说才能
The art or practice of public speaking, especially in a formal and eloquent manner.
He demonstrated impressive oratorship during his commencement speech.  他在毕业典礼上展现了令人印象深刻的演讲才能。
His oratorship captivated the audience. 他的演讲才能吸引了观众。
近义词：eloquence, rhetoric, public speaking
反义词：inarticulacy
词性：名词
