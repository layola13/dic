magnate:/ˈmæɡneɪt/ | n. |（尤指行业）巨擘，大腕，权威 |  A person of great influence, authority, or importance in a particular field. |  She's a magnate in the fashion world. 她是时尚界的巨头。| synonyms: luminary, leader, expert, authority | antonyms: nobody, unknown | noun
