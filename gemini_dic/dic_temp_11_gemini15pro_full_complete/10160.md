aponeurotic:/ˌæpənjʊˈrɑːtɪk/ /ˌæpənjʊˈrɒtɪk/
adjective
腱膜的
Relating to, or characteristic of, an aponeurosis.
The aponeurotic expansion of the muscle provides a broad area of attachment.  这块肌肉的腱膜扩展提供了一个广阔的附着区域。
The surgeon carefully repaired the aponeurotic tear.  外科医生仔细地修复了腱膜撕裂。
近义词: tendinous, sinewy
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) fleshy, muscular
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *aponevrotique*, from Late Latin *aponeuroticus*, from Greek *ἀπονεύρωσις* (aponeurōsis, “change of a tendon into a membrane”), from *ἀπό* (apo, “from”) + *νεῦρον* (neuron, “sinew, tendon”) + *-osis* (suffix forming nouns of action or process).
