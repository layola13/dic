elegant: /ˈelɪɡənt/ | adj. | 优雅的；优美的；精致的 | Graceful and stylish in appearance or manner; pleasingly ingenious and simple. | She wore an elegant dress to the party. 她穿着一件优雅的连衣裙去参加聚会。 | The solution to the problem was elegant and efficient. 这个问题的解决方案既优雅又高效。 | 近义词：refined, sophisticated, graceful, stylish | 反义词：clumsy, awkward, crude, inelegant | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Old French *elegant (modern French élégant) and directly from Latin ēlēgantem (nominative ēlēgāns) "choice, tasteful, elegant, fastidious," present participle of ēlēgere "to pick out, select," from ex "out" (see ex-) + legere "to choose, gather, read" (from PIE root *leg- (1) "to collect, gather").
