cassava:/kəˈsɑːvə/ | n. | 木薯，树薯 | A tropical plant with thick roots that are eaten as a vegetable. | Cassava is a staple food in many parts of Africa. 木薯是非洲许多地方的主食。| synonyms: yuca, manioc | antonyms: None | noun

cassava:/kəˈsɑːvə/ | n. | 木薯粉，树薯粉 | The starchy flour made from the cassava root. | Cassava flour is used to make tapioca. 木薯粉用来制作木薯淀粉。| synonyms: tapioca flour, manioc flour | antonyms: None | noun
