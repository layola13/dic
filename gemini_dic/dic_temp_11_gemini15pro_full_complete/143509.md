outgnaws:/ˌaʊtˈnɔːz/
verb
啮穿，咬穿; 比...更持久地啃咬
to gnaw something more persistently or effectively than something else.  It implies a slow, persistent erosion or destruction, often by something less powerful or expected.
The relentless tide outgnaws the cliffs.  无情的潮水侵蚀着悬崖。
Erosion outgnaws even the hardest rock over time.  随着时间的推移，侵蚀甚至会腐蚀最坚硬的岩石。
Synonyms: erode, corrode, wear away, undermine
Antonyms: build, strengthen, reinforce
词性: 动词
