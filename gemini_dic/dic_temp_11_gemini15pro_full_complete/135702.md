nonpressures:/ˈnɒnˌprɛʃəz/ (US) /ˈnɒnˌprɛʃəz/ (UK)| n.| 无压力；非压力；轻松自在的环境|A state or situation characterized by the absence of pressure, stress, or urgency.  Relatively informal and less common than "lack of pressure" or "stress-free environment".|例句：The team enjoyed a period of nonpressures after winning the championship. 团队在赢得冠军后享受了一段没有压力的时期。

例句：She sought a career path with more nonpressures and less intense competition. 她寻求一条压力更小、竞争更不激烈的职业道路。


|近义词：ease, relaxation, calm, tranquility, peace, quiet, serenity|反义词: pressure, stress, tension, strain, anxiety|词性: noun
