dome-like:/ˈdoʊmˌlaɪk/
adjective
穹顶状的；圆屋顶形的
Resembling a dome in shape.
The yurt has a dome-like roof.  这个蒙古包有一个穹顶状的屋顶。
The beetle has a smooth, dome-like shell.  这只甲虫有一个光滑的，穹顶状的外壳。
近义词: domed, vaulted, arched
反义词: flat, concave
