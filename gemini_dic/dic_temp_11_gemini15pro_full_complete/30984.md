chalder:/ˈtʃɔːldər/ | n. | 一种干量单位（通常用于煤炭、石灰等）;大量;一大堆 | A dry measure of capacity (typically for coal, lime, etc.), equal to 36 bushels. Also, a large quantity; a great deal. |  The farmer ordered a chalder of lime for the fields.  农民订购了一查尔德的石灰用于田地。

| Example: They brought a whole chalder of firewood for the winter. 他们为冬天带来了一大堆柴火。| Synonyms: heap, load, mass, pile | Antonyms:  bit, little, drop | noun
