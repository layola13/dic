extremist:/ɪkˈstriːmɪst/
n.|极端主义者；极端分子|A person who holds extreme political or religious views, especially one who resorts to or advocates extreme action.|The extremist group claimed responsibility for the bombing. 极端组织声称对爆炸事件负责。|近义词：fanatic, radical, zealot|反义词：moderate, centrist|词性：名词

extremist:/ɪkˈstriːmɪst/
adj.|极端的；过激的|Holding extreme political or religious views, especially those advocating extreme action.|His extremist views alienated many of his former supporters. 他的极端观点疏远了他的许多前支持者。|近义词：radical, extreme, fanatical|反义词：moderate, temperate|词性：形容词

Etymology:
From French extrémiste, from extrême ‘extreme’.
