happening:/ˈhæpənɪŋ/| n. adj.|发生的事情，事件；发生的，进行中的|Something that happens, an event.  Also used as an adjective to describe something that is current and exciting.|例句：Tell me everything that happened. 告诉我发生的一切。|近义词：event, occurrence, incident, affair, proceeding; current, trendy, fashionable|反义词:non-event, uneventful; outdated, old-fashioned|词性:noun, adjective

Etymology: From the verb "happen," which comes from the Middle English "happen," from the Old Norse "happa" (to chance, befall).
