baris:/ˈbɑːrɪs/| n.|排；列；行|A row, line, or rank of people or things.|The soldiers stood in baris, waiting for their orders. 士兵们排成一排，等待命令。|Synonyms: row, line, rank, file|Antonyms: disorder, chaos|noun
