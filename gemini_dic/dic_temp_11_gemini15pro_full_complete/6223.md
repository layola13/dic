amnesias:/æmˈniːʒəz/
n.
失忆症（复数）
Partial amnesias are more common than total amnesias. 部分失忆症比完全失忆症更常见。
例句：He suffered from recurring amnesias after the accident. 事故发生后，他反复出现失忆症。

例句：The patient experienced several amnesias throughout her life.  这位病人在她的一生中经历了几次失忆。

近义词：memory loss, forgetfulness
反义词：remembrance, recollection
词性：名词

amnesia:/æmˈniːʒə/
n.
失忆症
Amnesia can be caused by a variety of factors, including trauma and illness. 失忆症可以由多种因素引起，包括创伤和疾病。
例句：The blow to his head resulted in amnesia.  他头部受到的打击导致了失忆。

例句：She suffered from amnesia after the traumatic event.  她在经历了创伤性事件后患上了失忆症。

近义词：memory loss, forgetfulness
反义词：remembrance, recollection
词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek ἀμνησία (amnēsia)  "forgetfulness," from ἀ- (a-) "not" + μνήμη (mnēmē) "memory" + -ια (-ia) noun suffix.
