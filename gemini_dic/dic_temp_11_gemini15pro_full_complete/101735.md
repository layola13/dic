irresistible:/ˌɪrɪˈzɪstəbl/ | adj. | 不可抗拒的，无法抵挡的 | Too tempting to be resisted. | Her smile was irresistible. 她的笑容令人无法抗拒。 |  The chocolate cake was irresistible.  巧克力蛋糕太诱人了，我忍不住要吃。| 近义词: tempting, alluring, compelling, captivating | 反义词: resistible, avoidable | 词性: 形容词

词源: ir- (not) + resist (to withstand) + -ible (capable of being)  来自拉丁语resistere，意思是“抵抗，反对”。
