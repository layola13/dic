knowingest:/ˈnoʊɪŋɪst/
adjective
最有见识的，最博学的
most knowledgeable or shrewd, especially in a sly or cunning way.
He's the knowingest guy I've ever met when it comes to classic cars.  他是我见过的对老爷车最有见识的人。
Near Synonyms:  wise, clever, astute, sharp, canny, shrewd, insightful, perceptive, perspicacious
Antonyms:  naive, ignorant, foolish, gullible
词性: adjective
