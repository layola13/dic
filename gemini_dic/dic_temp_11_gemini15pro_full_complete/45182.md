cuirassed:/kwɪˈræst/ /kjʊˈræst/
adjective
穿着胸甲的，装甲的
Protected by a cuirass (a piece of armor covering the chest).
The cuirassed knight charged into battle.  穿着胸甲的骑士冲入战斗。
The tank was heavily cuirassed.  这辆坦克装甲很厚。
近义词:armored, protected
反义词:unprotected, vulnerable
词性:adjective

词源:
From French *cuirassé*, past participle of *cuirasser* "to equip with a cuirass," from *cuirasse* "cuirass," from Italian *corazza* "breastplate," from *cuore* "heart," from Latin *cor*.
