monogyny: /məˈnɒdʒɪni/ /məˈnɑːdʒɪni/
n.
一妻制
The practice or condition of having only one wife at a time.
Monogyny is the most common form of marriage in Western cultures.  一妻制是西方文化中最常见的婚姻形式。
The anthropologist's study focused on societies that practiced monogyny.  这位人类学家的研究重点是实行一妻制习俗的社会。
近义词: monogamy
反义词: polygyny, polygamy
词性: noun

monogamous: /məˈnɒɡəməs/ /məˈnɑːɡəməs/
adj.
一夫一妻制的
Having only one spouse at a time.
Swans are known for their monogamous mating habits. 天鹅以其一夫一妻的交配习性而闻名。
They have a monogamous relationship. 他们有着一夫一妻的关系。
近义词: single-mate
反义词: polygamous
词性: adjective
