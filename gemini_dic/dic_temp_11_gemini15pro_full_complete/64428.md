ergotism:/ˈɜːrɡətɪzəm/ | n. |麦角中毒|A disease caused by eating grains contaminated with ergot fungus.  It can cause hallucinations, convulsions, and gangrene.| The medieval outbreak of ergotism was known as St. Anthony's Fire. 中世纪爆发的麦角中毒被称为圣安东尼之火。|Synonyms: Saint Anthony's fire|Antonyms: health, wellness|noun
