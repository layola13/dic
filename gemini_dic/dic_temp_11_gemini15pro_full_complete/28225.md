capsizable:/ˈkæpsaɪzəbl/
adj.
易倾覆的，易翻的
able or likely to capsize (overturn in the water).
The small boat was capsizable in the rough seas. 这艘小船在汹涌的大海中很容易翻覆。
近义词: unstable, tippy, top-heavy
反义词: stable, steady
词性: adjective


capsize:/ˈkæpsaɪz/
v.
（使船）翻覆，倾覆
(of a boat) overturn in the water.
The strong wind capsized the sailboat.  强风使帆船翻了。
近义词: overturn, upend, keel over
反义词: right
词性: verb
