ascigerous:/æsɪˈdʒɪrəs/
adjective
产生子囊的；有子囊的
Containing asci (the sacs in which sexual spores are formed in ascomycete fungi).
This fungus is ascigerous, meaning it produces asci. 这种真菌会产生子囊，也就是说它是子囊菌。
The ascigerous stage of the fungus is critical for its reproduction.  这种真菌的子囊阶段对其繁殖至关重要。
近义词: ascus-bearing
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly asexual if referring to reproduction)
词性: adjective
