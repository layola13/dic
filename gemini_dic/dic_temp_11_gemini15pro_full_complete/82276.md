groundsel:/ˈɡraʊndsl/ | n. | 千里光，一种常见的杂草 | A common weed with small yellow flowers, often found in disturbed ground. | The garden was overrun with groundsel.  花园里长满了千里光。| synonyms: ragwort, senecio | antonyms: cultivated plant | noun

groundsels:/ˈɡraʊndslz/ | n. | 千里光（复数） | Plural form of groundsel. |  Several groundsels were growing through the cracks in the pavement.  人行道缝隙里长出几株千里光。| synonyms: ragworts, senecios | antonyms: cultivated plants | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *grundeswylige, from grund "ground" + swilian "to swallow," perhaps in reference to its low-growing habit.
