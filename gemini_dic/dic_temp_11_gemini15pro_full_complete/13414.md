atomizing:/ˈætəˌmaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 雾化；喷雾；使…分裂 | converting a liquid into a fine spray; breaking something down into very small parts | The perfume bottle atomizes the scent into a fine mist.  香水瓶将香水雾化成细小的喷雾。| synonyms: spraying, vaporizing, nebulizing | antonyms: condensing, solidifying | verb

atomizing:/ˈætəˌmaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 雾化的；喷雾的 | relating to or causing atomization |  An atomizing nozzle is used in spray painting.  喷漆中使用雾化喷嘴。| synonyms: spraying, vaporizing, nebulizing | antonyms: condensing, solidifying | adjective
