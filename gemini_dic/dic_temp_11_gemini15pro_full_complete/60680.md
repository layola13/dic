electricalness:/ɪˈlɛktrɪkəlnəs/
n.
電氣性，電氣狀態
The state or quality of being electrical; the degree to which something is electrical.
The electricalness of the atmosphere increased before the storm.  暴风雨前，大气的电气性增强了。
electricity, electric charge
non-electricalness (This is a rarely used, if ever, antonym.  A more natural opposite would be expressed with a phrase like "lack of electrical charge" or "absence of electricity.")
noun
