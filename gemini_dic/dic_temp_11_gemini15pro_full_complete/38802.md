conductress:/kənˈdʌktrɪs/
n.
女售票员；女指挥
a woman who collects fares on a bus or train, or who leads an orchestra or choir.
The conductress checked our tickets. 女售票员检查了我们的车票。
The orchestra played beautifully under the baton of the young conductress.  年轻的女指挥棒下，乐队演奏得非常优美。
