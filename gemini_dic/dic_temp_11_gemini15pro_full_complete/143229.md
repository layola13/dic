outdances:/ˌaʊtˈdænsɪz/
verb
比…跳得更好
to dance better than someone else
例句：He outdanced everyone at the party. 他在派对上比所有人都跳得好。

例句：She easily outdanced her competitors in the dance competition.  她在舞蹈比赛中轻松击败了她的对手。

近义词：surpasses, outperforms, excels
反义词：is outdanced by, is surpassed by
词性：动词
