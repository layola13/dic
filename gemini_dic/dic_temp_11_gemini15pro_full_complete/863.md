abstractors:/æbˈstræktərz/
n.
摘要者，提取者
People who make abstracts, summaries, or extracts of information from a larger body of work.
The abstractors summarized the key findings of hundreds of research papers. 摘要者总结了数百篇研究论文的主要发现。
The abstractors prepared concise summaries of legal documents. 摘要者准备了法律文件的简明摘要。
synonyms: summarizers, epitomizers, condensers
antonyms: elaborators, expanders
noun
