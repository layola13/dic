outcant:/ˌaʊtˈkænt/
verb
胜过；以咒语压倒
To surpass or defeat in chanting or magic.
The wizard outcanted his rival and banished the demon.  巫师用咒语压倒了他的对手，并驱逐了恶魔。
近义词:outdo, surpass, overcome
反义词: lose to, be defeated by
词性:v.

outcant:/ˌaʊtˈkænt/
verb
(方言)大声喊叫；大声说话
(dialectal) To shout or speak loudly.
He outcanted the other vendors in the market, trying to attract customers. 他在市场上比其他商贩喊得更大声，试图吸引顾客。
近义词:outcry, outshout, yell
反义词:whisper, murmur
词性:v.
