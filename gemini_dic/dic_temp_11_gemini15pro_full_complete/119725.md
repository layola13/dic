midsummer:/ˈmɪdˌsʌmər/ | n. | 仲夏; 夏至; |The period of time around the summer solstice (June 20 or 21 in the Northern Hemisphere). | Midsummer is a magical time of year. 仲夏是一年中神奇的时刻。| Midsummer celebrations are common in many cultures. 许多文化中都有庆祝仲夏的习俗。 | 近义词: solstice | 反义词: midwinter | 词性: 名词
