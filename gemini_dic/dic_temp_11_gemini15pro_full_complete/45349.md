cumulose:/ˈkjuːmjʊləs/| adj.|积云状的，堆积的|Characterized by the presence or formation of cumulus clouds; accumulated; heaped up.|例句：The cumulose clouds hinted at an approaching storm. 积云暗示暴风雨即将来临。

例句：The researcher presented a cumulose amount of data to support her hypothesis. 研究人员提供了大量的累积数据来支持她的假设。|近义词：accumulated, amassed, heaped, piled|反义词：scattered, dispersed|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Latin *cumulōsus*, from cumulō (“I heap up”), from cumulus (“heap”).
