low-cut:/ˈloʊ kʌt/ (US) /ˈləʊ kʌt/ (UK)| adj.|低胸的，低领口的|A garment, especially a dress or top, that is cut low at the neckline, exposing a significant amount of the chest and/or back.|She wore a low-cut dress to the party. 她穿了一件低胸的连衣裙去参加派对。|近义词：revealing, plunging, low-necked|反义词：high-necked, modest, conservative|词性:形容词

low-cut:/ˈloʊ kʌt/ (US) /ˈləʊ kʌt/ (UK)| n.|低胸的衣服|A garment that is low-cut.|The low-cut was a bit too much for the office. 这件低胸装对于办公室来说有点太过了。|近义词：revealing dress, plunging neckline|反义词：high-necked top, modest attire|词性:名词
