exemplifiable:/ɪɡˈzɛmplɪfaɪəbl/
adjective
可示例的；可例证的
Capable of being exemplified;  serving as a good example.
This principle is exemplifiable by several case studies.  这个原则可以通过几个案例研究来例证。

This theory is easily exemplifiable. 这个理论很容易举例说明。


近义词:illustrative, demonstrable, typifiable
反义词:unexemplifiable, abstract


词性: adjective
