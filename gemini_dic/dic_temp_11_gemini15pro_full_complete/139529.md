 The citizens are expected to obtemper the laws of the land. 公民们应该遵守国家的法律。 | 近义词: obey, comply, submit, yield, accede | 反义词: disobey, defy, resist, oppose | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *obtemperāre* ("to comply with, obey"), from *ob-* ("towards, facing") + *temperāre* ("to moderate, regulate, control").  The sense shifted from "to control oneself" to "obey an external control."
