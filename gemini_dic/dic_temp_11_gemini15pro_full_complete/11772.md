arpeggio:/ɑːrˈpɛdʒioʊ/ (US), /ɑːˈpɛdʒiːəʊ/ (UK)| n.|琶音；分解和弦|A musical chord in which the notes are played in sequence, one after the other, rather than simultaneously. |The guitarist played a rapid arpeggio. 吉他手演奏了一段快速的琶音。|Synonyms: broken chord|Antonyms: chord|noun

arpeggi:/ɑːrˈpɛdʒiːz/ (plural)| n.|琶音（复数）|The plural form of "arpeggio," referring to multiple instances of playing a chord's notes sequentially. |The composer used a series of arpeggi to create a cascading effect. 作曲家使用了一系列琶音来营造瀑布般的声音效果。|Synonyms: broken chords|Antonyms: chords|noun
