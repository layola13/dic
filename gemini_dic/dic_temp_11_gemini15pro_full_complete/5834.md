amatorian:/ˌæməˈtɔːriən/ | adj. n.| 业余的；业余爱好者 |  Of or relating to an amateur; not professional.  A person who engages in a pursuit, especially a sport, on an unpaid rather than a professional basis. |  He's an amatorian golfer. 他是一位业余高尔夫球手。|  amateur, nonprofessional, layperson | professional, expert | adjective, noun
