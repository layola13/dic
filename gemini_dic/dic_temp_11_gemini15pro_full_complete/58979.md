dutiable:/ˈdjuːtiəbl/
adjective
应纳税的；应征税的
Subject to import duty; taxable.
例句：Dutiable goods are subject to customs tariffs. 应纳税的商品需缴纳关税。

例句：He brought back several dutiable items from his trip abroad. 他从国外旅行带回几件应纳税的物品。

近义词：taxable, chargeable
反义词：duty-free, exempt
词性：adjective
