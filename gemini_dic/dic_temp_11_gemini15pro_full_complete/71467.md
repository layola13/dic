flamer:/ˈfleɪmər/| n.|喷火器；煽动者；发出火焰的东西|A person who expresses or shows great anger, passion, or enthusiasm, or who makes extremely critical or abusive remarks, especially online.  A device that produces a stream of burning fuel.|He's a real flamer on social media, always starting arguments. 他在社交媒体上是个十足的煽动者，总是挑起争端。

The firefighters used a flamer to burn away the dry brush. 消防员用喷火器烧掉了干灌木丛。|近义词：firebrand, agitator, fanatic, hothead, incendiary|反义词：pacifist, moderate|词性:noun


flamer:/ˈfleɪmər/| v.|燃烧；发怒；变得激动|To burn brightly or intensely; to suddenly become angry or excited.|The logs flamed in the fireplace. 木柴在壁炉里熊熊燃烧。

His face flamed with anger. 他的脸因愤怒而涨红。|近义词：blaze, flare, erupt, ignite|反义词：extinguish, cool down|词性:verb
