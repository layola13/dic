fallaciousnesses:/ˌfæləˈʃəsnəsɪz/
|n.|谬误, 谬论|The fallaciousnesses in his argument were quickly exposed by the expert.  专家很快就揭露了他论点中的谬误。| The sheer number of fallaciousnesses in the document made it worthless. 文件中大量的谬误使其毫无价值。 |Synonyms: errors, fallacies, misconceptions, untruths, falsehoods|Antonyms: truths, realities, facts, accuracies, validities|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From fallaciousness + -es (plural suffix).  Fallaciousness comes from fallacious (containing a fallacy; misleading) + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  Ultimately from Latin fallax (deceitful, misleading).
