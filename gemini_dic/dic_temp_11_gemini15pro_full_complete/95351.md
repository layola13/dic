incentivizing: /ɪnˈsɛntɪvaɪzɪŋ/
verb
激励；刺激
The government is incentivizing companies to invest in renewable energy. 政府正在激励企业投资可再生能源。
Encouraging or motivating someone to do something, typically by offering some sort of reward.
例句：
The company is incentivizing its employees to work harder by offering bonuses.  公司通过提供奖金来激励员工更加努力地工作。

The program incentivizes farmers to adopt sustainable practices. 该计划激励农民采用可持续的耕作方式。


近义词: motivating, encouraging, stimulating, prompting
反义词: discouraging, deterring, hindering
词性: verb (present participle)

Etymology: From incentive + -ize + -ing. Incentive comes from Late Middle English: from medieval Latin incentivum ‘something inciting’, neuter of incentivus, from incinere ‘set on fire’, from in- ‘into’ + candere ‘be white-hot’.
