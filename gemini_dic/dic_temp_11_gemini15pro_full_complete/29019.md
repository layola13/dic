cartographies:/kɑːrˈtɒɡrəfiz/
|n.|地图绘制;地图集;地图学|The study and practice of making maps.  It also refers to the collection of maps of a particular area, period, or subject.|The exhibition showcased historical cartographies of the Silk Road.  这次展览展示了丝绸之路的历史地图。|近义词:mapmaking, mapping, chartography|反义词: N/A (Cartography doesn't have a direct opposite.)
