blaeberry:/ˈbleɪˌbɛri/ | n. | 欧越橘，欧洲越橘 | A small, dark blue, edible berry, typically found in northern Europe.  It is the fruit of a low-growing shrub related to the blueberry. | The hills were covered in blaeberries, ripe for picking.  山上长满了欧越橘，可以采摘了。| synonym: bilberry, whortleberry | antonym:  N/A (no direct opposite for a type of berry) | noun
