astrolithology: /ˌæstrəʊlɪˈθɒlədʒi/
n.
星石学
The study of rocks from space, specifically meteorites and lunar samples.
Astrolithology helps us understand the formation and evolution of the solar system. 星石学帮助我们了解太阳系的形成和演化。
Astrolithology provides crucial insights into the composition of other celestial bodies. 星石学为了解其他天体的组成提供了重要的见解。
近义词: meteoritics, planetary geology
反义词:  (No direct antonym exists)
词性: noun

Etymology:
astro- (from Greek astron, "star") + litho- (from Greek lithos, "stone") + -logy (from Greek -logia, "study of")
