nonsubmergences:/nɒn.səbˈmɜː.dʒən.sɪz/
|n.| 非淹没，未淹没状态 |The nonsubmergences of the islands during high tide were surprising.  涨潮时这些岛屿没有被淹没，真是令人惊讶。| The study tracked the nonsubmergences of coastal areas over a decade. 这项研究追踪了十年来沿海地区的未淹没状态。 | near-surface appearances, emergences | submergences, inundations | noun

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non < PIE *ne "not"
submergence (noun): from submerge + -ence. Submerge from sub- "under" + merge "to dip, plunge."
