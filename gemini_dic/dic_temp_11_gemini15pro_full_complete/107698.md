lanolins:/ˈlænəlɪnz/
n.
羊毛脂（复数）
a greasy yellow substance secreted by the sebaceous glands of sheep, used in ointments and cosmetics.
Lanolins are widely used in skin care products. 羊毛脂广泛用于护肤产品中。
Lanolins can help to soothe dry and chapped skin. 羊毛脂可以帮助舒缓干燥和皲裂的皮肤。
synonyms: wool fat, wool wax, adeps lanae
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps synthetic emollients)
noun
