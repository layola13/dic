arbitrage:/ˈɑːrbɪtrɑːʒ/| n.|套利，套汇，仲裁|The simultaneous buying and selling of securities, currency, or commodities in different markets or in derivative forms in order to take advantage of differing prices for the same asset.|Example 1: He made a fortune through arbitrage in the currency markets. 他通过在货币市场套利发了大财。

Example 2:  Arbitrage opportunities are often short-lived. 套利机会通常是短暂的。|近义词：speculation, trading|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
