nutlike:/ˈnʌtlaɪk/
adjective
坚果状的；像坚果的
Resembling a nut in shape, size, or texture.
The candy had a nutlike flavor.  这颗糖果有一种坚果的味道。
The seeds are small and nutlike. 这些种子很小，像坚果一样。
