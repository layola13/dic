digraphs:/ˈdaɪɡræfs/| n.|双字母，二合字母|A digraph is a combination of two letters representing one sound, such as 'sh' in 'ship' or 'ea' in 'head'.|The word "phone" contains two digraphs: "ph" and "ne".  这个单词“phone”包含两个双字母：“ph”和“ne”.|synonyms:  ligature, double letter|antonyms:  monograph|noun
