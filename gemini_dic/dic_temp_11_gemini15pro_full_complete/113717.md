macrocephalous:/ˌmækroʊˈsɛfələs/ | adj. |大头的，头大的|Having an abnormally large head.| The doctor diagnosed the child as macrocephalous. 医生诊断这个孩子患有大头症。|Synonyms: megacephalic, large-headed | Antonyms: microcephalic, small-headed | adjective

macrocephaly:/ˌmækroʊˈsɛfəli/ | n. |大头症|The condition of having an abnormally large head.|Macrocephaly can be a symptom of various underlying conditions. 大头症可能是各种潜在疾病的症状。|Synonyms: megacephaly, large-headedness | Antonyms: microcephaly, small-headedness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek *makros* (large) + *kephalē* (head)
