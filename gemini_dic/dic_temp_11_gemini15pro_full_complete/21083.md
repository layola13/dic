bling-blinging:/ˈblɪŋ blɪŋɪŋ/
adjective
闪亮的，炫耀的
Covered in sparkly, expensive jewelry or other flashy accessories.  Characterized by a showy display of wealth.
She was bling-blinging in her diamond-encrusted watch and rings. 她戴着镶满钻石的手表和戒指，闪闪发光。
The rapper arrived at the club bling-blinging with gold chains.  这位说唱歌手到达俱乐部时，浑身上下都是金链子，闪瞎众人。
近义词:flashy, ostentatious, glittering, sparkling
反义词:understated, simple, modest
词性:adjective

bling-bling:/ˈblɪŋ blɪŋ/
noun
闪亮的首饰，炫耀的财物
Expensive, ostentatious jewelry or other accessories.
He loves to wear bling-bling. 他喜欢佩戴闪亮的饰品。
She showed off her new bling-bling to her friends. 她向朋友们炫耀她的新首饰。
近义词:jewelry, ice, flash
反义词:plainness, simplicity
词性:noun

词源:
The term "bling-bling" originated in the 1990s hip-hop culture, popularized by rappers and their songs. It is believed to be onomatopoeic, imitating the sound of light reflecting off shiny jewelry.
