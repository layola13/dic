elided:/ɪˈlaɪdɪd/ | adj. | 省略的，删节的 |  Omitted, suppressed, or passed over in silence, often a vowel or syllable in pronunciation. Derived from the Latin "elidere," meaning "to strike out." | Example: The poet elided the final vowel for the sake of meter. 诗人为了韵律而省略了最后一个元音。 | Example: Several lines were elided from the play to shorten its running time.  为了缩短演出时间，该剧删去了几行。| Synonym: omitted, deleted, suppressed, excised | Antonym: included, added, inserted | word type: adjective
