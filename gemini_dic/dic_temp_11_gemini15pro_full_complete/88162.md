hilling:/ˈhɪlɪŋ/| n.|培土, 起垄, 小山|The process of creating small hills or mounds of earth around the base of plants, usually to support them or improve drainage. Also, a small hill or mound.|The farmer was hilling the potatoes to protect them from frost. 农民正在给土豆培土以防霜冻。|近义词：mounding, ridging|反义词: leveling, flattening|Noun
