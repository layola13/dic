clifted:/ˈklɪftɪd/| adj.|峭壁的，悬崖的|Characterized by cliffs; having cliffs.|The clifted coastline was breathtaking. 峭壁林立的海岸线令人叹为观止。|近义词：precipitous, steep, sheer|反义词:flat, level|词性:形容词

词源:  From cliff + -ed.  "Cliff" comes from Old English *clif, related to Middle Dutch clif "cliff, rock," probably from a Scandinavian source akin to Old Norse klif "cliff, crag."  The "-ed" suffix forms adjectives from nouns, meaning "having, characterized by."
