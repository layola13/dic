noncontributing:/ˌnɑːnkənˈtrɪbjətiŋ/
adjective
不贡献的；无贡献的
Not making a contribution; not contributing.
The report identified several noncontributing members of the team.  报告指出了团队中几个没有贡献的成员。
Inactive accounts are considered noncontributing to the company's revenue.  不活跃的账户被认为对公司收入没有贡献。
Synonyms: unproductive, idle, inactive, inert, passive
Antonyms: contributing, productive, active, helpful, beneficial
词性: 形容词
