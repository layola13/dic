expolish:/ɪkˈspɒlɪʃ/ |v. | 磨光，擦亮；润色（文章等）| To make something smooth and shiny by rubbing it; to improve or refine something, especially a piece of writing. | He expolished the silver until it gleamed. 他把银器擦得闪闪发光。

She expolished her essay until it was perfect. 她反复润色文章直至完美。 | 近义词: polish, refine, burnish | 反义词: dull, tarnish |词性: 动词
