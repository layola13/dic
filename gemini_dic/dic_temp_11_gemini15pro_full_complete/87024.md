hepatic:/hɪˈpætɪk/
adjective
肝的；肝脏的
Relating to the liver.
The hepatic artery supplies blood to the liver. 肝动脉向肝脏供血。
The patient presented with hepatic dysfunction. 病人表现出肝功能障碍。
近义词:liver, biliary
反义词:None
词性:adjective
