half-bleached:/ˌhæfˈbliːtʃt/
adjective
半漂白的
Partially bleached; bleached to a lighter shade but not completely white.
The half-bleached jeans were trendy in the 90s.  九十年代，半漂白的牛仔裤很流行。
The designer used a half-bleached effect on the fabric. 设计师在面料上使用了半漂白的效果。
Near synonyms: partially bleached, lightened, faded
Opposite: fully bleached, unbleached
word class: adjective
