mumble:/ˈmʌmbəl/ (US), /ˈmʌmbl/ (UK)| v. n.|含糊地说，咕哝；咕哝，含糊的话|To speak in a low, indistinct voice; a low, indistinct utterance.|He mumbled something about being tired. 他咕哝着说他累了。|近义词：mutter, murmur, whisper|反义词：shout, yell, articulate|词性:动词/名词
