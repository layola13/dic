oppositionary:/ˌɒpəˈzɪʃənəri/ | adj. | 反对的，对抗的 | Of or relating to opposition; opposing. Characterized by or exhibiting opposition. |  The oppositionary party voted against the new bill.  反对党投票反对这项新法案。|  The writer's oppositionary stance on the issue was clear. 作家在这个问题上的反对立场很明确。 | synonyms: opposing, antagonistic, contrary, dissident, resistant | antonyms: supporting, agreeing, compliant, acquiescent | adjective
