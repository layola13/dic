lungie:/ˈlʌŋi/| n. |沙滩裤；男式围裙（主要指苏格兰和爱尔兰)|A piece of clothing resembling a skirt, worn by men in parts of Scotland and Ireland.  It's a type of kilt, often made of tartan. |He wore his lungi to the Highland games. 他穿着他的lungi去参加高地运动会。|Synonyms: kilt, sarong|Antonyms: trousers, pants|noun

lungi:/ˈlʌŋi/| n. |(澳大利亚俚语) 长裤；休闲裤| (Australian slang) Trousers; casual pants|He threw on a pair of lungies and headed to the beach. 他随便穿了条长裤就去了海滩。|Synonyms: trousers, pants, slacks|Antonyms: shorts, skirt|noun
