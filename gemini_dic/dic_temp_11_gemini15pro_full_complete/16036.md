bandala:/bænˈdɑːlə/ | n. |  强征；勒索；掠夺(尤指战时)；赃物 |  The forcible seizure of goods, especially in wartime; loot. |  The soldiers lived off the bandala they collected from the villagers.  士兵们靠从村民那里收集的赃物为生。| synonyms: loot, plunder, spoils, booty | antonyms: restitution, return | noun

bandala:/bænˈdɑːlə/ | n. |  (菲律宾)一种轻便的敞口男士衬衫，通常由菠萝纤维制成| A lightweight open-necked men's shirt, typically made of piña fiber (from the pineapple plant), worn in the Philippines. | He wore a crisp white bandala for the fiesta. 他穿着一件崭新的白色班达拉衬衫参加节日庆典。| synonyms: Barong Tagalog (more formal term), shirt | antonyms: N/A | noun
