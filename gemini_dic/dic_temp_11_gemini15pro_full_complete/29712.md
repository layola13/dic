catholicizes:/kəˈθɑːləˌsaɪzɪz/
verb
使皈依天主教;使普遍化
To convert to Catholicism; to make more general or universal.
The missionary catholicizes the indigenous people.  传教士使土著居民皈依天主教。
The new CEO tried to catholicize the company's appeal to reach a broader market.  新任首席执行官试图将公司的吸引力普遍化，以触及更广阔的市场。
synonyms: universalize, generalize, broaden, expand
antonyms: restrict, limit, narrow, specialize
词性: 动词
