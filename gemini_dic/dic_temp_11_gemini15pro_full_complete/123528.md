monotower:/ˈmɒnəʊtaʊər/ (US) /ˈmɑːnətaʊər/| n. |单管塔;单柱塔|A tower supporting antennas for mobile phone and other wireless communication services, characterized by a single pole or columnar structure.  A monotower blends in better with the surrounding environment than a traditional lattice tower.|The city council approved the construction of a new monotower to improve cell phone reception. 市议会批准建造一座新的单管塔以改善手机信号。

The monotower is designed to be less visually intrusive than a traditional cell tower.  这个单管塔的设计是为了减少视觉上的干扰，比传统的信号塔更美观。|近义词:cell tower, mobile phone mast, radio tower, telecommunications tower|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
