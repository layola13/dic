jambeux:/ˈdʒæmbʊz/ /ʒɑ̃bo/| n. |护胫甲，腿甲|Armor for the legs, especially plate armor covering the lower leg from knee to ankle.|The knight's jambeux gleamed in the sunlight. 骑士的护胫甲在阳光下闪闪发光。|The jambeux protected the knight's legs from sword blows. 护胫甲保护骑士的腿免受剑击。|近义词：greaves, leg armor|反义词:|名词
