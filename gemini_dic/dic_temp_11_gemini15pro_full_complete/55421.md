distiller:/dɪˈstɪlər/ | n. | 蒸馏器；酿酒商 | A person or company that distils spirits. | The distiller produces a range of single malt whiskies. 这家酿酒厂生产一系列单一麦芽威士忌。 | synonyms: refiner, purifier | antonyms: N/A | noun

distil:/dɪˈstɪl/ | v. | 蒸馏；提取；渗出 | Purify (a liquid) by heating it so that it vaporizes, then cooling and condensing the vapor and collecting the resulting liquid. | They distil the whisky in copper stills. 他们在铜制蒸馏器中蒸馏威士忌。 | synonyms: extract, refine, concentrate | antonyms: combine, mix, dilute | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *distillen*, from Old French *distiller*, from Latin *distillāre* (“to drip or trickle down”), from *de-* (“down”) + *stillāre* (“to drip”).
