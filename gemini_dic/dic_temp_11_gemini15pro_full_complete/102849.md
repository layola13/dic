jasperated:/ˈdʒæspəˌreɪtɪd/
adjective
恼怒的，绝望的
Feeling or expressing extreme irritation or despair.
She was jasperated by the constant interruptions. 她被不断的打扰弄得恼怒不已。
The long wait left him feeling utterly jasperated. 漫长的等待让他感到彻底绝望。
近义词: exasperated, frustrated, annoyed, irritated, vexed, infuriated
反义词: calm, content, pleased, satisfied
词性: adjective

词源:
From the obsolete verb "jasper" (to vex, annoy), possibly of imitative origin.
