|n.|截肢者，被截肢者|A person who has had a limb or limbs surgically removed.|The amputee was fitted with a prosthetic leg.|Synonyms:  (No single perfect synonym, context-dependent) person with amputation, limbless individual (if all limbs are missing)|Antonyms: N/A (no direct opposite)|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *amputatus, past participle of amputare (“to cut off, prune, lop off”), from Latin ambi- (“around, about”) + putare (“to prune, to think”).
