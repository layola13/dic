efflate:/ɪˈfleɪt/
verb
放出气体；泄气；使缩小
To deflate or release air or gas from something; to cause something to shrink or collapse.
The balloon slowly efflated after the party. / 派对结束后，气球慢慢地瘪了下去。
The doctor efflated the patient's lung to perform surgery. / 医生为了进行手术，放掉了病人的肺部气体。
近义词: deflate, collapse, shrink, empty
反义词: inflate, expand, enlarge
词性:v.
