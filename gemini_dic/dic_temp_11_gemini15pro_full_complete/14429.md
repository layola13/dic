autumny: /ˈɔːtəmni/ /ˈɑːtəmni/ | adj. | 秋天的；似秋的 | Characteristic of autumn; resembling autumn in color, mood, or imagery. | The autumny leaves fell gently to the ground. 秋天的树叶轻轻地落在地上。 |  The park had an autumny feel with the leaves turning brown and orange. 公园里弥漫着秋天的气息，树叶变成了棕色和橙色。| 近义词: fall-like, autumnal | 反义词: springlike, summery | 词性: 形容词

词源: From autumn + -y.
