downlinked:/ˌdaʊnˈlɪŋkt/| adj. v. (past participle)|下行链路的，下传的|Transferred or transmitted from a spacecraft or satellite to a ground station.  It can also be used more generally to describe data transfer from a higher point in a hierarchy to a lower one.|The data was downlinked from the satellite to the ground station.|Synonyms: downloaded, transmitted, relayed|Antonyms: uploaded, uplinked|Verb (past participle), Adjective
