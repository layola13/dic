cooperationists:/koʊˌɑːpəˈreɪʃənɪsts/
|n.|合作主义者，通敌分子|People who cooperate with an enemy force occupying their country.|The cooperationists were seen as traitors by many of their countrymen. 这些通敌者被许多同胞视为叛徒。|collaborators, sympathizers|resisters, dissidents|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From cooperation + -ist.  Cooperation comes from co- (together) + operation (working).  The suffix -ist denotes a person who adheres to a particular doctrine or system.
