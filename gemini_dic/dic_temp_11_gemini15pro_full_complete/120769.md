misappraisals:/ˌmɪsəˈpreɪzəlz/
|n.|错误评估，估价过低，误判|An act of misappraising something; a wrong or unfair judgment or estimate.|The company's misappraisals of the market led to significant losses. 公司对市场的错误评估导致了重大损失。|Misjudgments, miscalculations, misestimations|Accurate assessments, correct valuations|noun
