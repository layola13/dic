mythologers:/mɪˈθɒlədʒərz/
n.
神话学家，神话研究者
Experts who study myths and legends, interpreting their meaning and cultural significance.
Mythologers have debated the meaning of the Prometheus myth for centuries.  神话学家们几个世纪以来一直在争论普罗米修斯神话的意义。

The mythologers traced the origins of the story back to ancient Sumeria.  神话学家们将这个故事的起源追溯到古代苏美尔。

近义词:mythologists, folklorists
反义词:N/A
词性:noun

Etymology:
From "mythology" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Mythology" comes from the Greek "mythologia"  (telling of stories, myths), from "mythos" (story, myth) + "logia" (speaking, study).
