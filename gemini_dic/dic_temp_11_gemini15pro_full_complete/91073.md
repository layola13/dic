hutchinsonian:/hʌtʃɪnˈsoʊniən/
adjective
哈钦森学派的；哈钦森主义的
Relating to or characteristic of John Hutchinson (1674-1737), a theologian and natural philosopher, or his doctrines.
John Hutchinson rejected Newtonian physics and believed in a literal interpretation of the Bible, including its scientific pronouncements.  His followers are known as Hutchinsonians.
The hutchinsonian interpretation of Genesis was controversial.  哈钦森对创世纪的解释是有争议的。
Hutchinson developed a hutchinsonian system of interpreting scripture.  哈钦森发展了一套哈钦森式的解释圣经的体系。
近义词: fundamentalist, literalist
反义词: modernist, liberal
词性: adjective
