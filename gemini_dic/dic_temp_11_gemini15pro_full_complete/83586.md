haematodynamometer:/ˌhiːmətəˌdaɪnəˈmɒmɪtər/ | n. | 血压计 | An instrument for measuring blood pressure. | Example 1: The doctor used a haematodynamometer to check the patient's blood pressure. 医生用血压计检查病人的血压。

Example 2:  The haematodynamometer showed a dangerously high reading. 血压计显示读数危险地高。 | Synonym: sphygmomanometer, blood pressure monitor, blood pressure gauge | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for an instrument.  Perhaps "hypotensive drug" as it *lowers* blood pressure) | noun


haemato-/hemato- (prefix): /ˌhiːmətə(ʊ)-, ˌhɛmətə(ʊ)-/  relating to blood (from Greek *haima*, *haimatos* blood)

-dynamo- (combining form): /ˈdaɪnəməʊ/ power, force (from Greek *dynamis* power)

-meter (suffix): /ˈmiːtər/ instrument for measuring (from Greek *metron* measure)
