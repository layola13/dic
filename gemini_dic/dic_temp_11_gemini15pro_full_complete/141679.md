optometer:/ɒpˈtɒmɪtər/ | n. | 视力计，验光仪 | An instrument for measuring refractive error of the eye; an optometer measures the eye's ability to focus. | The optometrist used an optometer to determine her prescription.  验光师用视力计确定她的处方。| Synonyms: refractor, phoropter | Antonyms: None | Noun
