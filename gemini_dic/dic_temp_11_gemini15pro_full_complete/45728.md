curviest:/ˈkɜːrviɪst/
adjective
最弯曲的；曲线最多的
having the most curves; most shapely.
She wore a dress that accentuated her curviest features. 她穿着一件能凸显她优美曲线的连衣裙。
近义词: shapely, curvy, voluptuous
反义词: straight, flat, linear
词性: adjective

Etymology:  From 'curve' + '-y' (forming adjectives meaning 'having the quality of')  + '-est' (superlative suffix).  Ultimately from Latin 'curvus' (bent, curved).
