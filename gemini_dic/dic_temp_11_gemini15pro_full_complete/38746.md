condolence:/kənˈdoʊləns/
| n. |慰问，吊唁（单数）|An expression of sympathy, especially on the occasion of a death.|She offered her condolences to the grieving family. 她向悲痛的家人表示慰问。|
|近义词：sympathy, commiseration|反义词:congratulation|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *condolence*, from Old French *condoulance*, from Latin *condolēns*, present participle of *condolēre* (“to suffer together, grieve with”), from *con-* (“together”) + *dolēre* (“to grieve, suffer”).
