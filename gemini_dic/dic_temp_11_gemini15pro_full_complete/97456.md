infusive:/ɪnˈfjuːsɪv/| adj.|注入的；灌输的；充满的；浸渍的|Having the quality of infusing or being infused; tending to infuse.  Imparting a particular quality. |The tea had an infusive aroma of bergamot. 这茶带有佛手柑的香气。|近义词：pervasive, imbued, steeped, saturating|反义词：bland, dilute|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *infūsus, past participle of Latin infundō (“to pour in, infuse”), from in (“in, into”) + fundō (“to pour”).
