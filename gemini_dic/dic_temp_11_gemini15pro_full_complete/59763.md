economising:/ɪˈkɒnəmaɪzɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
节省; 节约
reducing expenditure or avoiding waste.
例句：She is economising on household expenses. 她正在节省家庭开支。

例句：The company is economising by reducing staff. 公司正在通过裁员来节省开支。

近义词：saving, budgeting, conserving, retrenching
反义词：spending, wasting, squandering
词性: v.


economise:/ɪˈkɒnəmaɪz/
verb
节省; 节约
spend less; reduce one's expenses.
例句：I have to economise on food this month.  这个月我必须节省食物开支。

例句：They are economising for a new car. 他们正在为买新车而存钱。

近义词：save, budget, conserve, retrench
反义词：spend, waste, squander
词性: v.


economy:/ɪˈkɒnəmi/
noun
经济; 节约
the wealth and resources of a country or region, especially in terms of the production and consumption of goods and services.  / careful management of available resources.
词源：From French économie, from Latin oeconomia, from Greek oikonomia ‘household management’, from oikos ‘house’ + nemein ‘manage’.
例句：The global economy is facing a downturn. 全球经济正面临衰退。

例句：The car has good fuel economy. 这辆车很省油。

近义词：thrift, frugality, saving
反义词：extravagance, waste
词性: n.
