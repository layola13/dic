deifies:/ˈdiːɪfaɪz/
verb
神化，奉若神明
treats someone or something as a god or goddess.
The ancient Egyptians deified their pharaohs.  古埃及人神化他们的法老。
They deified the leader of their movement. 他们把运动的领导者神化了。
exalt, glorify, worship
humanize, belittle
