bookmonger:/ˈbʊkˌmʌŋɡər/| n.|书商；书痴；爱书的人|A person who buys and sells books, especially rare or secondhand ones; a person who is very interested in books.|He was a well-known bookmonger with a shop on Charing Cross Road. 他是查令十字街上一家著名的书店的老板，也是个有名的书商。|近义词：bibliophile, book lover, book dealer, bookseller|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑illiterate 不识字的人，或者 philistine 庸俗的人，但语义上并非完全相反)|noun
