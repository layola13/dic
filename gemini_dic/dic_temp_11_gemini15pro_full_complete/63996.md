epistolatory: /ɪˌpɪstəˈlɔːri/ /ɪˌpɪstəˈlətɔːri/
adjective
书信体的；通信的
Relating to or suitable for letters; contained in or carried on by letters.
Example:  The novel is written in an epistolatory style. 这部小说是以书信体写成的。

Example: Her epistolatory skills were quite impressive. 她的书信写作技巧令人印象深刻。

Synonym: letter-writing, postal, written, correspondence-based
Antonym: oral, spoken, conversational

Etymology:  From Late Latin *epistolarius, from Latin epistola (“letter, epistle”), from Ancient Greek ἐπιστολή (epistolḗ, “message, letter, command”).
