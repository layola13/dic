gbo:/ˈɡboʊ/ | n. | 格博, 利比里亚货币单位 | The official currency of Liberia is the Liberian dollar, often referred to as the "gbo". 利比里亚的官方货币是利比里亚元，通常被称为“格博”。 | 例句1：He paid 100 gbo for the mango. 他花了100格博买了芒果。

例句2：The price of rice has increased by 5 gbo. 大米的价格上涨了5格博。| 近义词：Liberian dollar | 反义词:  (无，因为是货币单位) | 词性: 名词

词源:  "Gbo" is a colloquial term for the Liberian dollar, derived from the Liberian English pronunciation of "dollar".  "Gbo"源于利比里亚英语对“dollar”（美元）的发音。
