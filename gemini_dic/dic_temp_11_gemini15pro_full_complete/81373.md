grapsoid:/ˈɡræpsɔɪd/| adj.|类似螃蟹的，蟹形的|Resembling a crab; crablike.  Relating to or belonging to the Grapsoidea, a superfamily of crabs.|The grapsoid crabs are known for their square bodies.  这些蟹形蟹以其方形的身体而闻名。|Near Synonyms:  crablike, cancroid, brachyuran|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, could consider non-crablike or names of dissimilar creatures)|adjective
