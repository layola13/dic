muslined:/ˈmʌzlɪnd/ | adj. |  用薄纱覆盖的; 薄纱似的 | Covered or resembling muslin; gauzy. | The windows were muslined to diffuse the bright sunlight. 窗户上覆盖着薄纱，以散射明亮的阳光。 | The bride wore a muslined gown.  新娘穿着一件薄纱礼服。 | 近义词: gauzy, sheer, diaphanous | 反义词: opaque, thick | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From muslin + -ed.  源自名词 "muslin"（薄纱）加上形容词后缀 "-ed"。
