newslessnesses: /ˈnjuːzləsnɪsɪz/ | n. | 无新闻状态；缺乏新闻的情况 |  The state or quality of being without news or information.  It refers to a situation where there is a dearth of news or a lack of noteworthy events. | The prolonged newslessnesses of the isolated community led to widespread anxiety.  与世隔绝的社区长期缺乏新闻导致了广泛的焦虑。|  The newslessnesses were broken by a sudden announcement from the government. 政府突然发布的公告打破了无新闻状态。 | near synonyms:  information vacuum, lack of news, dearth of news, news drought, silence, quiet | antonyms: barrage of news, news deluge, abundance of news, information overload| noun
