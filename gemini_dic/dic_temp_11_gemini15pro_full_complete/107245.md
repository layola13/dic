laism:/ˈleɪɪzəm/ | n. | 世俗主义; 非宗教主义 | The belief that religion should not interfere with or be integrated into the affairs of the state or public education. |  France is known for its strong tradition of laism.  法国以其强大的世俗主义传统而闻名。| synonyms: secularism, non-clericalism | antonyms: clericalism, theocracy | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *laïsme*, from *lai* "lay, secular" (from Latin *laicus*, from Greek *laikos* "of the people," from *laos* "people") + *-isme* (from Greek *-ismos*).
