exhortatory:/ɪɡˈzɔːrtətɔːri/
adjective
劝告的，激励的
intended to urge or encourage
The coach gave an exhortatory speech to his team before the final game. 教练在决赛前对他的队员们发表了鼓舞人心的讲话。
Synonyms: encouraging, urging, inspiring, motivating
Antonyms: discouraging, dissuasive, deterrent
词性: 形容词
