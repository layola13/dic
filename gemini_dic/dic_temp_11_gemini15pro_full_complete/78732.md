giggliness:/ˈɡɪɡəlɪnəs/| n. |咯咯笑；傻笑|The state of being prone to giggling.  A tendency to giggle frequently or easily. |Her giggliness was infectious and soon everyone was laughing. 她的咯咯笑很有感染力，很快大家都笑了。|The children's giggliness filled the room. 孩子们的咯咯笑充满了整个房间。|近义词：merriment, mirth, levity|反义词：seriousness, solemnity|词性：noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From giggle (v.) + -ness. Giggle is of imitative origin.
