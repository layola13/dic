blub:/blʌb/| n. vi.|哭诉; 啜泣; 哽咽; 肥胖臃肿的人（或物）|A blub is a loud cry caused by sadness or pain.  (n.) / To blub is to cry noisily. (v.)|The little boy blubbed when he lost his toy. 小男孩丢了玩具后哭了。|近义词：sob, weep, cry, wail|反义词:laugh, giggle|词性:noun, intransitive verb

blubs:/blʌbz/| n. vi.|哭诉; 啜泣; 哽咽; 肥胖臃肿的人（或物）(plural)|Plural form of blub. Refers to multiple instances of crying or multiple plump individuals or things.|The children were all blubs after their favorite pet died. 孩子们最喜欢的宠物死后都哭了。|近义词：sobs, weeps, cries, wails|反义词:laughs, giggles|词性:noun, intransitive verb
