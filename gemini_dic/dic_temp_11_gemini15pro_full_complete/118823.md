metanephritic:/ˌmɛtəˈnɛfrɪtɪk/ | adj. | 后肾的，后肾起源的 | Relating to or denoting the metanephros, the most posterior of the three pairs of vertebrate kidneys developing in an embryo.  | The metanephritic kidney is the functional kidney in adult amniotes. 后肾是成年羊膜动物的功能性肾脏。 | The metanephritic duct develops into the ureter. 后肾管发育成输尿管。 |  mesonephritic, pronephritic |  - | adjective
