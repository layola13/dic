co-feoffee:/ˌkoʊfiːˈfiː/ | n. |共同受封人；共同受赠人 | A person who holds land jointly with another under the same title or feoffment. | Example 1:  John and Mary were co-feoffees of the estate, inheriting it equally from their parents.  约翰和玛丽是这片地产的共同受封人，从他们父母那里平等地继承了它。

Example 2: The co-feoffees disagreed on how to manage the property, leading to a legal dispute. 共同受封人在如何管理房产上意见不一，导致了法律纠纷。 | Near synonyms: joint tenant, co-owner, co-heir | Antonyms: sole owner, sole proprietor | Noun
