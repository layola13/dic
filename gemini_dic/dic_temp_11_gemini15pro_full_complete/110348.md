lightman:/ˈlaɪtmən/ | n. | 光之使者；照明者；灯塔管理员；姓氏 | A person associated with light, either literally or metaphorically.  It can refer to someone who works with light (like a lighthouse keeper), or someone who brings enlightenment or understanding. | He was a true lightman, guiding lost ships through the stormy night. 他是一位真正的光之使者，引导迷失的船只度过暴风雨之夜。|近义词: illuminator, beacon, guide |反义词:obscurer |词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "light" (Old English "lēoht," meaning brightness) + "man" (Old English "mann," meaning person).  The combination simply means a "person of light."
