landowners:/ˈlændˌoʊnərz/
n.
土地所有者，地主
People who own land.
The landowners refused to sell their property to the developers. 土地所有者拒绝将他们的财产卖给开发商。
The landowners in this area are wealthy.  这个地区的土地所有者都很富有。

近义词:landholders, proprietors
反义词:tenants, renters
词性:noun
