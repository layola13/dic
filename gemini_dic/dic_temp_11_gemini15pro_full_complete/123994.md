morning-glory:/ˈmɔːrnɪŋ ˌɡlɔːri/ (US) /ˈmɔːnɪŋ ˌɡlɔːri/ (UK)| n.|牵牛花；昙花一现的人或物|A climbing plant with trumpet-shaped flowers that open in the morning and close later in the day. Also, something beautiful or successful that lasts only a short time.|The morning-glories climbed up the trellis. 牵牛花爬上了棚架。|近义词：bindweed, convolvulus|反义词:|noun
