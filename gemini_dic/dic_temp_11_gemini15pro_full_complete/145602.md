overconscientious: /ˌoʊvərˌkɑːnʃiˈenʃəs/ | adj. | 过分认真的，过度尽责的 | Excessively conscientious; too careful and meticulous. | She was so overconscientious that she worried about every little detail. 她过度认真，以至于每一个小细节都让她担心。| He's an overconscientious student, always striving for perfection. 他是一个过于认真的学生，总是力求完美。 | 近义词: meticulous, scrupulous, painstaking | 反义词: careless, negligent, remiss | 词性: adjective

词源:  From over- + conscientious, from Latin conscientia "knowledge within oneself," from com- "with" + scientia "knowledge"
