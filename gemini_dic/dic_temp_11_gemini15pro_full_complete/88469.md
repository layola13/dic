histolysis:/hɪˈstɒlɪsɪs/ (US) /hɪˈstɒlɪsɪs/ (UK)| n. |组织溶解；组织分解|The destruction or dissolution of tissues.  The breakdown of tissues, often by enzymatic action.| During metamorphosis, the tadpole's tail undergoes histolysis. 在变态发育过程中，蝌蚪的尾巴会发生组织溶解。|Synonyms: tissue breakdown, disintegration, catabolism|Antonyms: histogenesis, tissue growth|noun
