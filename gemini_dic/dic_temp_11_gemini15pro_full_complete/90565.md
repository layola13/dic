huguenot:/ˈhjuːɡənɒt/ /ˈhjuːɡəˌnɑːt/| n. |胡格诺派教徒|A French Protestant of the 16th and 17th centuries, especially one of those who suffered persecution for their faith.|Many Huguenots fled France after the revocation of the Edict of Nantes. 许多胡格诺派教徒在南特敕令被废除后逃离了法国。|Synonyms: Protestant, Calvinist|Antonyms: Catholic|noun
