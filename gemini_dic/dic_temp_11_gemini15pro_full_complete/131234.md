noncapitals: /nɒnˈkæpɪtəlz/ | n. | 非大写字母；小写字母 |  Referring to letters of the alphabet that are not capitalized (uppercase).  It usually refers to lowercase letters. |  The manuscript was written entirely in noncapitals.  这份手稿完全是用小写字母写的。| synonyms: lowercase, minuscule | antonyms: capitals, uppercase | noun

Etymology:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "capitals" (from Latin "capitalis," meaning "of the head, chief, principal").
