folly:/ˈfɒli/ (UK), /ˈfɑːli/ (US)| n.|愚蠢；愚蠢的行为；愚蠢的事；轻率；荒唐事|Lack of good sense; a foolish action, idea, practice, etc.|例句：It was sheer folly to build the hotel so close to the airport.  把旅馆建得离机场这么近纯粹是愚蠢。

例句：His folly cost him his job. 他的愚蠢行为让他丢了工作。

近义词：stupidity, foolishness, absurdity, recklessness, imprudence|反义词：wisdom, prudence, sense, sagacity|词性：名词
