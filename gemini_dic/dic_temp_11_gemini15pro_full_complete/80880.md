governably:/ˈɡʌvərnəbli/
adjective
可统治地；可控制地
capable of being governed; manageable.
The country is now governably democratic.  这个国家现在是可以治理的民主国家。
The once unruly classroom became governably quiet after the new teacher arrived.  新老师来了以后，曾经难以管教的教室变得安静可控。
近义词: manageably, controllably
反义词: ungovernably, unmanageably
词性: adjective
词源: From "governable" + "-ly". "Governable" comes from the verb "govern," ultimately from the Latin "gubernare" (to steer, guide, govern).
