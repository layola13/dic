fireproof:/ˈfaɪərˌpruːf/| adj.|防火的；耐火的|Resistant to fire; not easily burned. |例句：Fireproof materials are used in building construction. 建筑施工中使用防火材料。

例句：The safe is fireproof.  这个保险箱是防火的。|近义词：fire-resistant, non-flammable|反义词:flammable, combustible|词性:形容词
