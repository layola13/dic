njai:/ˈnjaɪ/
noun
女佣；保姆；（尤指东南亚的）家庭女佣
A female domestic servant, especially in Southeast Asia.
She worked as a njai in a wealthy household.  她在一个富裕的家庭里做女佣。
The njai took care of the children.  这个女佣照顾孩子们。
synonyms: maid, servant, domestic helper, housekeeper
antonyms: employer, master, mistress
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Malay *ŋɟai*, ultimately from Sanskrit *nagarikā* (नागरिका) meaning "citizen (female)".  The term evolved in Malay to refer specifically to female domestic servants, often of Chinese or native descent, in colonial households.
