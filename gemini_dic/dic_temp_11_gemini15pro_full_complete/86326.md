hecatomb: /ˈhɛkətuːm/ /ˈhɛkətɑːm/ | n. | 大屠杀，百牲祭 | A great public sacrifice, originally of a hundred oxen; hence, a large-scale slaughter or destruction. |  The conquering army wrought a hecatomb upon the defeated city, leaving few survivors. 征服的军队对战败的城市进行了大屠杀，几乎没有幸存者。

The dictator ordered a hecatomb of his political opponents. 独裁者下令屠杀他的政敌。 | 近义词：slaughter, massacre, carnage, holocaust | 反义词: salvation, preservation | 词性: noun
