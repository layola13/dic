omniscients:/ɒmˈnɪsiənts/ | n. |无所不知者；全知者| Beings or entities that are all-knowing. |  God is often considered omniscient. 上帝通常被认为是无所不知的。

The narrator in the story is omniscient. 故事中的叙述者是无所不知的。| 近义词: all-knowing, all-seeing | 反义词: ignorant, unknowing | 词性: 名词

omniscient:/ɒmˈnɪsiənt/ | adj. |无所不知的；全知的| Having complete or unlimited knowledge, awareness, or understanding. | An omniscient narrator knows the thoughts and feelings of all the characters.  一个无所不知的叙述者了解所有角色的想法和感受。

The omniscient point of view allows the author to reveal information that the characters themselves do not know.  无所不知的视角允许作者揭示角色本身不知道的信息。| 近义词: all-knowing, all-seeing | 反义词: ignorant, unknowing | 词性: 形容词
