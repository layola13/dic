meeses:/ˈmiːsiz/ | n. | 老鼠（非正式，复数）| A humorous, non-standard plural of "mouse."  It is often used in children's stories or to create a playful effect. | The cat chased the meeses out of the kitchen.  猫把老鼠们赶出了厨房。 | 近义词: mice | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a plural noun) | 词性: noun

PHRASE->
(Since "meeses" is informal and childish, it's rarely used in set phrases.  Here are some phrases using the standard "mice":)

Look what the cat dragged in!  瞧猫把什么东西拖进来了！（表示惊讶或不赞成）
When the cat's away, the mice will play.  猫儿不在，老鼠翻天。（指当权威不在时，下属就会为所欲为。）
Quiet as a mouse.  像老鼠一样安静。
Play cat and mouse.  玩猫捉老鼠的游戏。（比喻捉弄或戏耍某人）
As poor as a church mouse.  一贫如洗。
