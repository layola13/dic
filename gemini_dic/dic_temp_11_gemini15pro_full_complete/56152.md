docusoaps:/ˈdɒkjuːˌsəʊps/ | n. |纪实肥皂剧；真人秀| A television genre that combines documentary techniques with the melodramatic conventions of soap operas, focusing on the everyday lives of ordinary people. | Example: The docusoap followed the lives of a group of fishermen in Alaska. 这部纪实肥皂剧讲述了阿拉斯加一群渔民的生活。

Example:  Docusoaps often focus on the dramatic aspects of everyday life.  纪实肥皂剧通常关注日常生活的戏剧性方面。| 近义词: reality TV, reality show | 反义词:  scripted television, drama | 词性: noun
