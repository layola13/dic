farl:/fɑːrl/ | n. | 烤饼，燕麦饼 | A quarter section of a bannock or cake of oat bread, especially as eaten in parts of Scotland and Northern Ireland. |  She offered him a farl of oatcake with butter. 她给了他一块涂了黄油的燕麦饼。| Synonyms: bannock, oatcake, scone | Antonyms: N/A | noun

farl:/fɑːrl/ | v. |  (苏格兰、北爱尔兰) 将 (面包) 切成四等份 | To divide (bread) into four quarters. | The baker farled the bannock into four equal pieces. 面包师把大面包切成了四等份。| Synonyms: quarter, divide | Antonyms: combine, join | verb
