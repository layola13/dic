nontraditional:/ˌnɒntrəˈdɪʃənəl/ /ˌnɑːntrəˈdɪʃənəl/| adj.|非传统的，不传统的|Not following established traditions or customs.|例句：They took a nontraditional approach to education. 他们采取了一种非传统的教育方法。|近义词：unconventional, unorthodox, innovative, modern|反义词：traditional, conventional, orthodox, customary|词性:形容词

nontraditional:/ˌnɒntrəˈdɪʃənəl/ /ˌnɑːntrəˈdɪʃənəl/| n.|非传统的事物|Something that deviates from established traditions or customs. (This usage is less common than the adjective form.)|例句：The nontraditional was embraced by the younger generation. 年轻一代欣然接受了非传统的事物。|近义词：innovation, novelty|反义词：tradition, custom|词性:名词
