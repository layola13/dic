inferrers:/ɪnˈfɜːrərz/
|n.|推理者，推断者|People who make inferences; those who draw conclusions from evidence or premises.|The inferrers concluded that the suspect was guilty based on the available evidence. 推理者根据现有证据得出结论，嫌疑人有罪。|synonyms: interpreters, interpreters, deducing, concluding, surmising|antonyms: misunderstanding, misinterpreting|noun
