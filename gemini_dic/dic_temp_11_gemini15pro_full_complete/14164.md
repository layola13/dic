autism: /ˈɔːtɪzəm/ (UK) /ˈɑːtɪzəm/ (US)| n.|自闭症|A developmental disorder of variable severity that is characterized by difficulty in social interaction and communication and by restricted or repetitive patterns of thought and behavior. |My nephew was diagnosed with autism at the age of three. 我的侄子三岁时被诊断出患有自闭症。

Autism can make it difficult for individuals to understand social cues. 自闭症会使人难以理解社交暗示。|近义词:autistic spectrum disorder (ASD)|反义词:neurotypical|词性:noun
