cauldron:/ˈkɔːldrən/ (US) /ˈkɔːldrən/ (UK)| n. |大锅， cauldron; |A large metal pot with a lid and handle, used for cooking over an open fire.|The witch stirred a bubbling potion in her cauldron. 女巫在她的大锅里搅拌着冒泡的药水。|synonyms: pot, kettle, vat|antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "bowl" for a smaller container)|noun
