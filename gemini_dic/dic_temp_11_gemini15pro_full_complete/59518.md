easy-care:/ˈiːzi kɛər/| adj.|易于打理的，方便护理的|Easy-care refers to something that requires minimal effort or attention to maintain, especially in regards to clothing or fabrics, or sometimes plants or pets.|This shirt is easy-care; just wash and wear. 这件衬衫易于打理，洗完就能穿。|近义词：low-maintenance, convenient, simple|反义词:high-maintenance, demanding, laborious|词性:adjective

easy-care:/ˈiːzi kɛər/| n.|易于打理的物品（通常指衣物或植物）|Easy-care as a noun refers to items, usually clothing or plants, that require minimal care.|The nursery specializes in easy-care houseplants. 这家苗圃专门种植易于打理的室内植物。|近义词：convenience items|反义词:demanding items|词性:noun
