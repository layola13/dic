alimentally:/ˌælɪˈmentəli/
| adv. |通过消化道地，营养地|Concerning nutrition; by way of the alimentary canal (the digestive tract).|例句：The patient was alimentally challenged due to a digestive disorder.  这个病人由于消化系统疾病，在营养摄入方面受到了挑战。

例句：Nutrients are supplied alimentally. 营养物质是通过消化道供给的。|近义词：nutritionally, digestively |反义词: parenterally (非经肠道)|词性：副词
