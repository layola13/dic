frislet:/ˈfrɪzlɪt/| n.|卷发，小卷，（尤指）额前短卷发|A small, tight curl or ringlet of hair, especially one over the forehead. |Her frislets bounced as she skipped down the street. 她额前的卷发随着她蹦蹦跳跳地走下街道而弹跳。|近义词：curl, ringlet, tendril|反义词:straight hair|词性:noun

词源:
From the French word "friser" (to curl).  "Friser" itself likely comes from Frankish *fris, related to the Old High German "friso," meaning "curl."
