jumpiness:/ˈdʒʌmpɪnɪs/
noun
神经质，紧张不安
The state of being easily startled or nervous.
She exhibited a certain jumpiness after the incident.  事故发生后，她表现出某种紧张不安。
He couldn't shake the feeling of jumpiness. 他无法摆脱这种紧张不安的感觉。
nervousness, anxiety, edginess, skittishness
calmness, composure, tranquility
