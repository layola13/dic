overgarnish:/ˌoʊvərˈɡɑːrnɪʃ/ | v. | 过度装饰，装饰过火 | To garnish excessively; to add too many garnishes to a dish, making it look cluttered or overwhelming. | The chef overgarnished the plate, making it look less appealing.  厨师在盘子里装饰得太多了，使它看起来不那么吸引人。|  The birthday cake was so overgarnished that it was difficult to see the actual cake underneath. 生日蛋糕装饰得太多了，很难看到下面的蛋糕。 | overdecorate, overdo | undergarnish | verb
