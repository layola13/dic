accenting:/ækˈsɛntɪŋ/ /əkˈsɛntɪŋ/ |v.|强调; 着重; 突出; |Giving particular emphasis to something.|She spoke slowly, accenting each syllable. 她慢慢地说着，每个音节都加重了语气。|emphasizing, highlighting, stressing|downplaying, de-emphasizing|verb
Etymology: From accent (n.) + -ing.  Ultimately from Latin accentus "accent, tone," from ad- "to" + cantus "song."
