calamitousness:/kəˌlæmɪtəsnəs/
|n.|灾难性，灾祸连连|The calamitousness of the situation was overwhelming.  这种情况的灾难性是压倒性的。|The calamitousness of the flood left the town in ruins. 洪水的灾难性使该镇成为废墟。|近义词：disastrousness, catastrophism|反义词: fortunate, blessed|词性:noun
calamitous:/kəˈlæmɪtəs/
|adj.|灾难性的，不幸的|The calamitous fire destroyed the entire building. 这场灾难性的大火烧毁了整栋楼。|The earthquake had a calamitous effect on the region. 地震对该地区造成了灾难性的影响。|近义词：disastrous, catastrophic, tragic|反义词: fortunate, beneficial|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *calamitosus, from Latin calamitas (“disaster, calamity”), from calamus (“reed, stalk of grain”), perhaps originally referring to damage to crops.
