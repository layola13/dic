dependancy:/dɪˈpɛndənsi/| n.|依赖，依靠；瘾|A state of relying on or being controlled by someone or something else.  This word is often considered a misspelling of "dependency".|His dependancy on alcohol ruined his career. 他对酒精的依赖毁了他的事业。|近义词：dependence, reliance, addiction|反义词: independence, self-reliance|noun


dependency:/dɪˈpɛndənsi/| n.|依赖，依靠；瘾|A state of relying on or being controlled by someone or something else.|Her dependency on her parents is hindering her personal growth. 她对父母的依赖阻碍了她的个人成长。|近义词：reliance, addiction, habit|反义词: independence, self-sufficiency|noun
