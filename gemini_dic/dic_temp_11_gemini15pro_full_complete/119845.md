mildish:/ˈmaɪldɪʃ/| adj.|稍微温和的，略微轻微的|Somewhat mild; moderately mild.  It describes something that has a mild quality but not to a great extent.|The weather was mildish, not too hot and not too cold. 天气稍微温和，不太热也不太冷。|The curry was mildish, perfect for those who don't like it too spicy. 这咖喱稍微有点辣，适合那些不喜欢太辣的人。|近义词:moderately mild, slightly mild, somewhat mild|反义词:strong, harsh, severe|词性:形容词
