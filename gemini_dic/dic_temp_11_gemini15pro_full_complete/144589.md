outwaves:/ˈaʊtweɪvz/
| v. | 超越，胜过，发出更强的波 | To surpass or overcome something, often by emitting stronger waves (literal or metaphorical). | The new technology outwaves the old one in terms of efficiency. 这项新技术在效率方面胜过旧技术。 |  The signal from the lighthouse outwaves the interference from the storm. 灯塔的信号胜过了风暴的干扰。 | surpass, exceed, outdo, outperform | lag behind, fall short of | verb

词源:  "Out-" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "wave" (referring to a wave of energy, influence, or trend).  "Out-" 表示超过或胜过，"wave" 指的是能量、影响或趋势的浪潮。 因此，"outwave" 的意思是发出更强的波来超越或克服。  虽然这个词不常见，但它是由常见的词缀和词根构成的，因此其含义是可以理解的。
