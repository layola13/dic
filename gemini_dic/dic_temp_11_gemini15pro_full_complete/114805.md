mam:/mæm/ | n. | 妈妈；女士；夫人 | A child's word for mother; a polite term of address for a woman. | Example: My little brother still calls me "Mam."  我的弟弟仍然叫我“妈妈”。

| Example: Excuse me, Mam, could you tell me where the post office is?  打扰一下，女士，你能告诉我邮局在哪里吗？ | Synonym: mom, mother, ma, lady, madam | Antonym: sir (when used as a polite address) | noun
