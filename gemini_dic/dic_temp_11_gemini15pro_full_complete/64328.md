eradiate:/ɪˈreɪdieɪt/ | v. | 消除，根除；放射，辐射 | The government is trying to eradicate corruption. 政府正在努力根除腐败。| 例句：The sun radiates heat and light. 太阳辐射热和光。

例句：The organization works to eradicate hunger in developing countries. 该组织致力于消除发展中国家的饥饿问题。| 近义词：eliminate, exterminate, remove, abolish, obliterate; emit, radiate | 反义词：establish, create, introduce | 词性: 动词

词源:  From Latin eradicatus, past participle of eradicare "to root out, pluck up by the roots," from ex- "out" (see ex-) + radix (genitive radicis) "root" (from PIE root *wrad- "root"). The sense of "destroy completely" is first recorded in English 1520s.
