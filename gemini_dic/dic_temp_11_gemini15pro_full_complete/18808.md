berserkness:/bərˈsɜːrknəs/
n.
狂暴，暴怒
A state of violent or destructive rage.
He flew into a berserkness and began smashing furniture. 他勃然大怒，开始砸家具。
frenzy, rage, fury, madness
calmness, sanity, composure
noun
