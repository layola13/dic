flushingly:/ˈflʌʃɪŋli/
adverb|脸红地，羞愧地|In a manner that causes or shows blushing; with a flush.  Characterized by or causing a rush of blood to the face, typically as a result of embarrassment, shame, or excitement.|She smiled flushingly at the compliment. 她因赞美而羞红了脸笑了。|近义词: blushingly, shyly, self-consciously|反义词:  calmly, coolly, composedly|词性: 副词
