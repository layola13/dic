confuting: /kənˈfjuːtɪŋ/ | adj. | 驳倒的，使困惑的 | Proving wrong by argument or evidence; causing confusion. |  The lawyer presented confuting evidence that challenged the witness's testimony. 律师提出了驳斥证据，质疑证人的证词。| Synonyms: disproving, refuting, perplexing, bewildering | Antonyms: confirming, supporting, clarifying | adjective

confute: /kənˈfjuːt/ | vt. | 驳倒，反驳 | Prove (a person or an assertion) to be wrong. | The scientist confuted the theory with new experimental data.  科学家用新的实验数据驳斥了这个理论。| Synonyms: disprove, refute, rebut | Antonyms: confirm, support, corroborate | verb
