hornwork:/ˈhɔːrnwɜːrk/| n. |角堡，角形防御工事|A hornwork is an outwork in fortification consisting of two demi-bastions joined by a curtain and connected to the main fortification by two long sides.|The hornwork provided a strong defense against attacks from that direction.|近义词:outwork, fortification, bastion|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用“vulnerability”表示防御薄弱的概念)|noun
