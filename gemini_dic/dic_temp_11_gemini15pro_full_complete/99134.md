intercepting:/ˌɪntərˈseptɪŋ/
adjective
拦截的，截取的
taking, stopping, or seizing something on its way from one place to another.
The police were intercepting phone calls. 警察正在监听电话。
近义词：catching, seizing, grabbing, stopping
反义词：releasing, sending, transmitting
词性: adjective

intercept:/ˌɪntərˈsept/
verb
拦截，截取
to stop and take something that is being passed or sent from one person or place to another.
The police intercepted a shipment of drugs. 警方截获了一批毒品。
近义词：catch, seize, grab, stop
反义词：release, send, transmit
词性: verb

interception:/ˌɪntərˈsepʃn/
noun
拦截，截取
the act of intercepting someone or something.
The interception of the missile prevented a disaster.  拦截导弹防止了一场灾难。
近义词：capture, seizure, arrest
反义词：release, transmission
词性: noun
