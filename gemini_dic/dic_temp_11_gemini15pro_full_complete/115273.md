manitrunk: /ˈmænɪˌtrʌŋk/
noun
动物躯干; 主体
The main part of an animal's body, not including the head, limbs, or tail.
The manitrunk of the spider was swollen with eggs. 蜘蛛的躯干部充满了卵。
body, torso, trunk
head, limbs, tail
