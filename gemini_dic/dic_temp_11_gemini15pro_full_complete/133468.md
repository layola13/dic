nonfederated:/ˌnɒnˈfɛdəreɪtɪd/
adjective
非联邦的，未加入联邦的
Not united under a federal government; not belonging to a federation.
Example:  Nonfederated states often face challenges in coordinating policies.  非联邦制国家经常在协调政策方面面临挑战。

Example: The nonfederated territories maintained their own individual laws. 这些未加入联邦的领土保持着自己的法律。

Synonym: independent, autonomous, separate, decentralized
Antonym: federated, united, centralized
