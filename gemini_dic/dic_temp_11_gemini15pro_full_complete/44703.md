crudities:/ˈkruːdɪtiz/ /ˈkrʊdətiz/| n. |粗糙，粗俗，未加工状态|something in a raw or unrefined state; something crude or vulgar.|The play was full of crudities and lacked sophistication.  这出戏充满了粗俗之处，缺乏成熟感。| Examples: The museum exhibit showcased the crudities of early metalworking. 博物馆的展览展示了早期金属加工的粗糙之处。 His jokes were filled with crudities that offended many people.  他的笑话充满了冒犯很多人的粗俗之处。|近义词：vulgarities, improprieties, coarseness, rawness|反义词: refinements, subtleties, sophistication, elegance|词性:noun (plural)


crude:/kruːd/ /krʊd/| adj. |粗糙的，粗俗的，天然的，未加工的|in a natural or raw state; not yet processed or refined; lacking in tact or refinement.|The crude oil needs to be refined before it can be used. 原油需要精炼之后才能使用。| Examples:  He made a crude remark about her appearance. 他对她的外貌作出了粗鲁的评论。 The early settlers lived in crude cabins. 早期的定居者住在简陋的小屋里。 |近义词：raw, unrefined, unprocessed, vulgar, coarse|反义词: refined, sophisticated, polished, elegant|词性:adjective
