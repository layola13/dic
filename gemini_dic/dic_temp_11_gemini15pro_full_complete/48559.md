defaming:/dɪˈfeɪmɪŋ/
verb
诽谤，中伤
The act of damaging someone's reputation by making false and malicious statements.
He is suing the newspaper for defaming him. 他正在起诉这家报纸诽谤他。
slandering, libeling, maligning, vilifying, disparaging
praising, commending, lauding, extolling
v.
