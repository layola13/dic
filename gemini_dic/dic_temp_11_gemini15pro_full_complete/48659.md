defensiblenesses:/dɪˌfɛnsɪblənəsɪz/ | n. |防御性，守势，敏感|The state or quality of being easily offended or prone to defensiveness.  A tendency to protect oneself from criticism or attack, often by justifying one's actions or attitudes. | Her defensiveness made it difficult to have an open conversation. 她的防御心态使得很难进行坦诚的交流。 |Synonyms: sensitivity, touchiness, vulnerability, guardedness | Antonyms: openness, receptiveness, confidence, assurance | noun
