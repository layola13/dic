helium:/ˈhiːliəm/ | n. |氦|A chemical element, a colorless, odorless, tasteless, non-toxic, inert, monatomic gas, the first member of the noble gas group in the periodic table. Atomic number 2. |Helium is used in balloons. 氦气用于气球。| Helium is lighter than air. 氦气比空气轻。|Synonyms: None (as it's a specific element)|Antonyms: None (as it's a specific element)|noun
