devolvement:/ˌdiːˈvɒlvmənt/ |/ˌdiːˈvɑːlvmənt/| n. |移交，下放，转让|The devolution of power from central government to local authorities. 中央政府向地方当局的权力下放。|例句：The devolution of power to Scotland has led to significant changes. 向苏格兰下放权力导致了重大变化。|近义词：transfer, delegation, decentralization|反义词:centralization|词性:名词
