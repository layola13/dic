burning:/ˈbɜːrnɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbɜːnɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. n.|燃烧的，灼热的；强烈的，迫切的|Something that is burning is on fire.  The word can also describe strong emotions or urgent needs. |The burning building collapsed. 燃烧的建筑物倒塌了。|近义词：blazing, fiery, intense, urgent|反义词：extinguished, cold, apathetic|词性:形容词

burning:/ˈbɜːrnɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbɜːnɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|燃烧|The act of something being on fire.|The burning of fossil fuels contributes to climate change. 燃烧化石燃料导致气候变化。|近义词：combustion, incineration|反义词：extinguishing|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *byrnan, from Proto-Germanic *brunnaną (“to burn”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰrew- (“to boil, bubble, effervesce, burn”).
