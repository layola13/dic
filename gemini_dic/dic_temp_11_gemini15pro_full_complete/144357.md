outstays:/ˌaʊtˈsteɪz/
verb
停留过久，逗留过长
to stay longer than the permitted or expected time.
He outstayed his visa by three months. 他签证逾期三个月。
近义词: overstay
反义词: leave, depart
词性: verb

Etymology:  From out- + stay.  "Out-" acts as an intensifier, meaning to stay beyond or longer than.
