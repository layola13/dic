bergo:/ˈbɜːrɡoʊ/| n.|伯戈帽，一种宽檐帽|A bergo is a wide-brimmed hat, often made of straw or other lightweight material, designed to protect the wearer from the sun.  It is similar to a sun hat but often has a more structured shape and may include decorative elements.|She wore a stylish bergo to shield her face from the intense sun. 她戴着一顶时尚的伯戈帽，以遮挡强烈的阳光。|The bergo provided excellent protection from the sun during her gardening.  这顶伯戈帽在她园艺时提供了极好的防晒保护。|近义词：sun hat, wide-brimmed hat|反义词:|名词
