mazy:/ˈmeɪzi/| adj.| 错综复杂的，迷宫似的 |Resembling a maze; intricate and confusing.|The old city has mazy streets that are easy to get lost in.  这座老城的街道错综复杂，很容易迷路。|例句：The corridors of the ancient castle were mazy and dimly lit. 古堡的走廊错综复杂，光线昏暗。|近义词：labyrinthine, convoluted, intricate, complex, winding|反义词：straightforward, simple, direct|词性:形容词
Etymology: From maze + -y.  Ultimately from Old English *masian "to confuse," related to amaze.
