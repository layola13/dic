equiaxed:/ˌiːkwiˈækst/ | adj. | 等轴的，等粒状的 | Having axes of equal length; composed of grains of similar size in all dimensions.  | The metal solidified into an equiaxed structure.  金属凝固成等轴结构。|  isotropic, uniform | anisotropic, unequal | adjective


equiaxe:/ˌiːkwiˈæks/ | n. | 等轴晶体 | A crystal having axes of equal length. | The sample contained numerous equiaxes.  样品中包含许多等轴晶体。| isotropic crystal, uniform crystal | anisotropic crystal, unequal crystal | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From equi- (equal) + -axed (having axes).  The "equi-" prefix comes from Latin "aequus" meaning "equal", and "axis" comes from Latin "axis" meaning "axle" or "pivot."
