antirealist:/ˌæntiˈriːəlɪst/ | n. |反实在论者| Someone who opposes realism, especially in philosophy.  An antirealist believes that reality is dependent on our minds or perceptions, rather than existing independently of them.| Many antirealists argue that moral truths are subjective, not objective. 许多反实在论者认为道德真理是主观的，而非客观的。| synonyms:  idealist, subjectivist, constructivist | antonyms: realist, objectivist | noun

antirealists:/ˌæntiˈriːəlɪsts/ | n. |反实在论者（复数）| Plural form of antirealist.  Refers to multiple individuals who oppose realism. | The antirealists presented a compelling argument against the existence of an objective reality. 反实在论者提出了一个令人信服的论点，反对客观现实的存在。| synonyms: idealists, subjectivists, constructivists | antonyms: realists, objectivists | noun
