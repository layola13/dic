binny:/ˈbɪni/| n.|箱子，容器;  (苏格兰) 煤仓; (英格兰北部) 啤酒桶|A binny is a large container, typically used for storage, especially for coal in Scotland or beer in northern England.|The coal was kept in the binny outside the back door. 煤被存放在后门外面的煤仓里。|The pub had several binnies of ale. 酒馆里有几桶麦芽酒。|Synonyms: container, receptacle, bin, vat, cask|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "emptiness" in a conceptual sense)|noun
