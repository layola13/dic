begild:/bɪˈɡɪld/| vt.|镀金，使辉煌，使迷惑|To cover with or as if with gold; to give a deceptively attractive appearance to.|The setting sun begilded the clouds with orange and gold. 落日给云朵镀上了一层橙色和金色。|Synonyms: adorn, embellish, enhance, glorify, gild|Antonyms: tarnish, dull, dim, mar|verb

begild:/bɪˈɡɪld/| vt.|欺骗，诱惑|To deceive or mislead by presenting a false appearance of something.|The con man begilded his victims with promises of easy riches. 骗子用轻松致富的承诺欺骗了他的受害者。|Synonyms: deceive, mislead, delude, beguile|Antonyms: enlighten, expose, reveal|verb
