icteric: /ɪkˈtɛrɪk/ /aɪˈtɛrɪk/
adjective
黄疸的；患黄疸病的
Affected by jaundice, which causes the skin and whites of the eyes to turn yellow.

The patient presented with icteric sclera, indicating liver dysfunction.  病人巩膜黄染，提示肝功能障碍。

His skin had an icteric tinge. 他的皮肤略带黄疸。


Synonym: jaundiced
Antonym: healthy, non-jaundiced
词性: adjective
