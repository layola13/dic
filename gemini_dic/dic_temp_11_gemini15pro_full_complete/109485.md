leprously:/ˈlɛprəsli/| adv.|麻风病地；像麻风病人一样地|In a manner resembling leprosy; as if affected with leprosy.  Characterized by moral corruption or decay.|He looked at her leprously, as if she were contaminated. 他像看着被污染了似的，厌恶地盯着她。|近义词：corruptly, decayedly, foully, vilely|反义词: purely, cleanly, healthily|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *leprusly*, from Old French *leprus*, *lepreus* (“leprous”), from Late Latin *leprōsus*, from Latin *lepra* (“leprosy”), from Ancient Greek *λέπρα* (*lépra*, “leprosy”), from *λέπω* (*lépō*, “to peel, scale”).
