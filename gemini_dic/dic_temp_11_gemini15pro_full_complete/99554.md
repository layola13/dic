interhemal: /ˌɪntərˈhiːməl/
adjective
位于血管间的；血管间的
Situated between blood vessels.
The interhemal spaces in the gills of fish are crucial for gas exchange. 鱼鳃中的血管间隙对于气体交换至关重要。
Interhemal injections are sometimes used in veterinary medicine.  血管间注射有时用于兽医学。
近义词: intervascular
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider terms like "intravascular" - within the blood vessel)
词性: adjective


词源:
From inter- + haemal (British spelling of hemal), from Greek haima "blood."
