droughts:/draʊts/ |n.|干旱，旱灾|A prolonged period of abnormally low rainfall, leading to a shortage of water.|The severe droughts devastated the crops. 严重干旱摧毁了农作物。|近义词:aridness, dryness, scarcity|反义词:flood, deluge|名词
