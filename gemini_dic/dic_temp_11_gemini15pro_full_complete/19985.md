biogeography:/ˌbaɪoʊdʒiˈɑːɡrəfi/ | n. | 生物地理学 | The study of the distribution of species and ecosystems in geographic space and through geological time. | Example 1: Biogeography helps us understand why certain animals are found in specific regions. 生物地理学帮助我们理解为什么某些动物出现在特定的区域。

Example 2:  Island biogeography is a fascinating subfield of this discipline. 岛屿生物地理学是这个学科中一个引人入胜的子领域。 | Near Synonyms:  phytogeography, zoogeography | Antonym: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bio- + geography.  "Bio" comes from the Greek "bios" meaning "life". "Geography" comes from the Greek "geographia" meaning "earth description," from "ge" (earth) + "graphein" (to write).
