merismatic:/ˌmɛrɪsˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 分生性的，具有分生组织的 | Relating to or having meristems, plant tissue capable of cell division and differentiation. |  The merismatic tissue is responsible for the plant's growth. 分生组织负责植物的生长。|  Apical meristems are located at the tips of roots and shoots. 顶端分生组织位于根和茎的尖端。 | 近义词: dividing, growing, proliferating | 反义词: differentiated, mature | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *meristos* "divided," from *merizein* "to divide."  Related to "meristem," the plant tissue where growth occurs.
