mediation:/ˌmiːdiˈeɪʃən/| n.|调解，斡旋，调停|Intervention in a dispute in order to resolve it; arbitration.|The mediation between the two warring factions finally led to a ceasefire.  这两个交战派别之间的调解最终导致了停火。|近义词：arbitration, conciliation, negotiation, intervention|反义词：conflict, dispute, disagreement|noun

词源:
