haunter:/ˈhɔːntər/ | n. | 鬼魂，常出没的人或物 | A person or thing that haunts a place or person, often a ghost or memory. | The haunter of the graveyard was said to be a headless horseman.  据说墓地的鬼魂是一个无头骑士。|近义词: ghost, specter, apparition |反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly "inhabitant" in certain contexts) | 词性: Noun (singular)

词源: From the verb "haunt," which comes from Old French "hanter" meaning "to frequent." Ultimately from Frankish "heim" meaning "home."
