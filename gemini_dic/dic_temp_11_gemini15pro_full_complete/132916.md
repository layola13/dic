nonelites:/ˌnɒnɪˈliːts/
noun
非精英人士；非精英阶层
People who do not belong to the elite group; the ordinary people.
The government's policies often ignore the needs of the nonelites. 政府的政策常常忽视非精英阶层的需求。
commoners, masses, ordinary people
