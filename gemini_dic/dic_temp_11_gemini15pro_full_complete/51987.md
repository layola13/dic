diageotropic:/ˌdaɪəˌdʒiːəˈtrɒpɪk/ | adj. | 横向生长的，背地的 | Growing horizontally or at right angles to the force of gravity; plagiotropic.  | Example: The diageotropic roots of the mangrove tree help to stabilize it in the soft mud.  红树林的横向生长的根有助于使其在软泥中稳定。

Example:  Diageotropic growth is common in rhizomes. 横向生长在根茎中很常见。 | Synonym: plagiotropic, horizontal | Antonym: orthotropic, vertical | adjective
