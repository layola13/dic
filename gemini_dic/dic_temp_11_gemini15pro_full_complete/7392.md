animate:/ˈænɪmeɪt/ | v. |使有生气；赋予生命；激励；使活泼 |To give life or motion to; to make lively or spirited. |例句：The artist animated the cartoon character with vibrant colors.  艺术家用鲜艳的色彩使卡通人物栩栩如生。

例句：Her speech animated the audience and inspired them to action. 她的演讲激励了听众，并鼓舞他们采取行动。|近义词：enliven, invigorate, inspire, stimulate |反义词：dampen, deaden, stifle, suppress|词性:动词

animate:/ˈænɪmət/ | adj. |有生命的；活的；有生气的 |Living; possessing life or spirit. |例句：Rocks and minerals are not animate objects.  岩石和矿物不是有生命的物体。

例句：She has a very animate and engaging personality. 她性格活泼迷人。 |近义词：living, alive, lively, spirited |反义词：inanimate, lifeless, dead |词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin animatus, past participle of animare (“to give life to, enliven”), from anima (“breath, soul”).
