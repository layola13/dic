downturning:/ˌdaʊnˈtɜːrnɪŋ/
adjective
下降的，衰退的
Characterized by a decline or downturn.
The downturning economy caused many businesses to close. 衰退的经济导致许多企业倒闭。
declining, weakening, worsening
improving, rising, strengthening
词性:形容词

downturning:/ˌdaʊnˈtɜːrnɪŋ/
noun
下降，衰退
A decline or downturn.
The downturning of the market was unexpected. 市场的衰退出乎意料。
decline, slump, recession
increase, rise, upturn
词性:名词
