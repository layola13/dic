inter-runs:/ˈɪntərˌrʌnz/
| n. |中间跑，两轮跑之间的休息|The inter-runs allowed the athletes to catch their breath and hydrate.  两轮跑之间的休息让运动员们得以喘口气并补充水分。|The coach scheduled short inter-runs during the sprint practice. 教练在短跑训练中安排了短暂的中间跑。|近义词：intervals, rest periods|反义词：continuous running|词性:名词


词源: "inter-" (prefix) from Latin, meaning "between, among" + "runs" (noun) from Old English "rinnan," meaning "to flow, to run."  "Inter-" 前缀来源于拉丁语，意为“在...之间，在...之中”； "runs" 名词来源于古英语 "rinnan"，意为“流动，奔跑”。
