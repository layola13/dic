earthmad:/ˈɜːθmæd/| adj.|疯狂的，狂热的（尤指对环境保护）|Excessively enthusiastic or passionate, especially about environmental protection.  Origin: A blend of "earth" and "mad."  It suggests a passionate, almost obsessive concern for the environment.|例句：She's completely earthmad, spending all her free time protesting against deforestation. 她完全是个环保狂热分子，把所有的空闲时间都花在抗议砍伐森林上。

例句：He's so earthmad he only buys clothes made from recycled materials. 他太热衷环保了，只买用回收材料做的衣服。|近义词：eco-conscious, environmentally aware, green, passionate about the environment|反义词:apathetic to the environment, indifferent to the environment|词性:形容词
