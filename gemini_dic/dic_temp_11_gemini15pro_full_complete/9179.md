antinomianism:/ˌæntɪˈnəʊmiənɪzəm/| n. |反律法主义，唯信仰论|The belief that Christians are not bound by moral law. It emphasizes faith and God's grace as the sole means of salvation, sometimes leading to the rejection of any moral constraints.| Antinomianism is a controversial doctrine that has been condemned by many mainstream Christian denominations. 反律法主义是一个有争议的教义，已被许多主流基督教派谴责。 | Near Synonyms: lawlessness, libertinism, moral relativism | Antonyms: legalism, moral absolutism | Noun
