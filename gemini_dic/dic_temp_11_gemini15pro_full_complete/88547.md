hit-the-wall:/hɪt ðə wɔːl/| idiom|撞墙期；达到极限|To reach a point of exhaustion, either physically or mentally, where further progress seems impossible.| After running for 20 miles, I hit the wall and had to slow down.  跑了20英里后，我到了极限，不得不放慢速度。|Synonyms:  reach a plateau,  burn out,  stall,  peak,  flag|Antonyms:  improve, progress,  advance|idiom

hit-the-wall:/hɪt ðə wɔːl/| idiom|精疲力尽；黔驴技穷|To be unable to continue or make further progress due to exhaustion or having used up all available resources.|The project hit the wall when the funding ran out.  当资金用完时，项目陷入了僵局。|Synonyms:  come to a standstill,  reach a dead end,  be stuck|Antonyms:  make headway,  flourish, thrive|idiom
