daran/daˈran/|adv.|thereat, thereon, at it, on it, about it, of it|referring to something previously mentioned; often used with verbs like denken (to think), sprechen (to speak), arbeiten (to work)|例句：Wir arbeiten daran. We are working on it.

例句：Sie denken daran. They are thinking about it. |近义词：davon, darüber|反义词: N/A|词性:adverb

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Ich denke oft daran. = I often think about it.
Er spricht jeden Tag daran. = He speaks about it every day.


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Wir arbeiten gerade daran. = We are currently working on it.
Sie sprechen im Moment daran. = They are talking about it at the moment.


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Ich habe schon daran gearbeitet. = I have already worked on it.
Er hat viel daran gedacht. = He has thought a lot about it.


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
Wir haben die ganze Zeit daran gearbeitet. = We have been working on it the whole time.
Sie haben seit Tagen daran gedacht. = They have been thinking about it for days.


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
Ich arbeitete gestern daran. = I worked on it yesterday.
Er sprach letzte Woche daran. = He spoke about it last week.


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
Ich arbeitete daran, als du anriefst. = I was working on it when you called.
Er sprach gerade daran, als sie kam. = He was just talking about it when she came.


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
Ich hatte schon daran gearbeitet, bevor du kamst. = I had already worked on it before you came.
Er hatte lange daran gedacht, bevor er entschied. = He had thought about it for a long time before he decided.


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
Ich hatte stundenlang daran gearbeitet, bevor ich aufhörte. = I had been working on it for hours before I stopped.
Er hatte tagelang daran gedacht, bevor er es ihr erzählte. = He had been thinking about it for days before he told her.


一般将来时 (Simple Future):

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
Ich werde morgen um diese Zeit daran arbeiten. = I will be working on it this time tomorrow.

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
Ich werde bis nächste Woche daran gearbeitet haben. = I will have worked on it by next week.

PHRASE->
Ich denke daran. = I'm thinking about it.
Wir arbeiten daran. = We're working on it.
Er spricht darüber. = He's talking about it.  (While "darüber" translates to "about it," it doesn't directly incorporate "daran."  This demonstrates a close alternative.)
Sie erinnern sich daran. = They remember it. (Similar to above, "daran" isn't directly in the phrase, but implied in the meaning.)
Ich freue mich darauf. (While this translates to "I'm looking forward to it," the sentiment can sometimes be similar to anticipation related to "daran."  This offers a nuanced related phrase.)
