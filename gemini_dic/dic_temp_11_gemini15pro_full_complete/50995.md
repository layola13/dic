desiringly:/dɪˈzaɪərɪŋli/
adv.
渴望地，想往地
In a manner that expresses desire or longing.
She looked at the chocolate cake desiringly. 她渴望地看着巧克力蛋糕。
He spoke desiringly of his homeland. 他充满渴望地谈起他的祖国。
Synonyms: longingly, yearningly, wistfully, covetously
Antonyms: indifferently, apathetically
词性: 副词
