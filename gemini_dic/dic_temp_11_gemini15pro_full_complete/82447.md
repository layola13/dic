grumbliest: /ˈɡrʌmbliɪst/ | adj. | 最爱抱怨的，脾气最坏的 |  Superlative form of grumpy: bad-tempered and complaining.  This word describes someone who complains more than anyone else. | He was the grumbliest old man in the village, always finding something to moan about. 他是村里最爱抱怨的老人，总是能找到一些事情来抱怨。 |  Example 2: Of all the siblings, she was by far the grumbliest. 在所有兄弟姐妹中，她是最爱抱怨的。 | Synonyms:  most complaining, most bad-tempered, most irritable, sourest | Antonyms:  most cheerful, most good-natured, happiest, most content | Word type: Adjective
