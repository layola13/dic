noncola:/ˌnɑːnˈkoʊlə/
noun
非可乐饮料
A carbonated soft drink that is not cola.  Specifically, any soft drink that is not flavored with kola nut extract.
The store had a wide selection of noncolas, including lemon-lime and orange.  这家商店有各种各样的非可乐饮料，包括柠檬酸橙和橙子口味的。
synonyms: soft drink, soda, pop, carbonated beverage
antonyms: cola
n.
