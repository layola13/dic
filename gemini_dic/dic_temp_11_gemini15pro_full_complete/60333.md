egoists:/ˈiːɡəʊɪsts/ (UK) /ˈiːɡoʊɪsts/ (US)| n. |利己主义者；自私者|A person who is excessively self-centered and concerned primarily with their own interests, often at the expense of others. |例句：Egoists rarely consider the needs of others. 利己主义者很少考虑别人的需要。

例句：The egoists only cared about their own profits. 这些利己主义者只关心他们自己的利润。

|近义词：narcissist, self-seeker, egotist|反义词: altruist, philanthropist|词性:noun
