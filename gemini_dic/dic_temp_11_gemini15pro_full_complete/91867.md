hyperingenuities:/ˌhaɪpərˌɪndʒɪˈnjuːɪtiz/
n.
超级精巧的设计；极度的机巧；过度的巧妙设计
excessive cleverness or ingenuity, especially in devising plans or schemes.
His plan was full of hyperingenuities, making it too complex to execute. 他的计划充满了过度的巧妙设计，使其过于复杂而无法执行。
The author's hyperingenuities in plotting the novel sometimes felt contrived. 作者在小说情节设计上的过度巧妙有时让人觉得不自然。
近义词: overingenuities, complexities, intricacies
反义词: simplicities, straightforwardness
词性: noun (plural)
