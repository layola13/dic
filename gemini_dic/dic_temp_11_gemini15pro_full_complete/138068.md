normalising:/ˈnɔːməlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 归一化，标准化，常态化 |The process of bringing or returning something to a normal condition or state.  Making something standard or regular.  |例句：They are normalising relations with neighbouring countries. 他们正在与邻国关系正常化。

|例句：The software normalises the volume levels across all tracks.  这个软件将所有音轨的音量级别归一化。|近义词：standardizing, regularizing, adjusting, adapting |反义词：distorting, exaggerating, abnormalizing |词性: 动词 (Verb)
