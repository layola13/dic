chroma:/ˈkroʊmə/ (US) /ˈkrəʊmə/ (UK)| n. |色度，饱和度|The intensity of a color, its saturation or purity. It distinguishes a strong color from a weak one.  It's separate from hue and brightness/lightness.|The chroma of this red paint is very high, making it vibrant and eye-catching.  这种红色颜料的色度很高，使其鲜艳夺目。|intensity, saturation, vividness|dullness, paleness|noun
