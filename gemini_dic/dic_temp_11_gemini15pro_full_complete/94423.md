impliedly:/ɪmˈplaɪɪdli/| adv.|隐含地，暗中地|In a way that is suggested but not directly expressed.  It means something is understood or communicated indirectly, without being explicitly stated.|His silence impliedly agreed to the terms. 他的沉默暗示着同意了这些条款。|The contract impliedly forbids such behavior. 合同中隐含地禁止了这种行为。|implicitly, tacitly|explicitly|adverb
