alliteral:/əˈlɪtərəl/| adj.|押头韵的|Relating to or characterized by alliteration; containing alliteration.  Using the same consonant at the beginning of each stressed syllable in a line of verse.|The poet used alliteral phrases to create a sense of rhythm. 这位诗人使用押头韵的短语来营造节奏感。|近义词：alliterative|反义词:|adjective
