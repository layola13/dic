hierotheca:/ˌhaɪəroʊˈθiːkə/ | n. | 圣器收藏室，圣物库 | A place where sacred objects are kept, especially in a church or temple. | The hierotheca of the cathedral housed a collection of priceless relics.  大教堂的圣器收藏室收藏了一批无价的圣物。 | Example: The museum's hierotheca contained ancient scrolls and religious artifacts. 博物馆的圣物库里收藏着古代卷轴和宗教文物。 | synonyms: reliquary, treasury, sacristy | antonyms: N/A | noun
