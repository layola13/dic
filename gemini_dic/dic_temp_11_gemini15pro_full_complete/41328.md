coresidential:/ˌkɔːrɛzɪˈdɛnʃəl/
adjective
共同居住的，同住的
Sharing a residence with others, especially of different groups or backgrounds.
Example: The coresidential program allows students from different cultures to live together.  这个共同居住项目允许来自不同文化的学生一起生活。

Example:  The university offers coresidential housing for international and domestic students. 这所大学为国际和国内学生提供共同居住的住房。
Synonym: cohabiting, shared
Antonym: separate, individual
词性: adjective
