digitized:/ˈdɪdʒɪtaɪzd/| adj.|数字化的，数码化的|converted into digital form, suitable for use by a computer.|例句：The library has digitized all its old manuscripts. 图书馆已将其所有旧手稿数字化。|近义词：digital, electronic, computerized|反义词:analog, non-digital|词性:形容词
Etymology: From digit + -ize + -ed.  Ultimately from Latin *digitus* "finger, toe".
