king's:/kɪŋz/| determiner, possessive pronoun | 国王的；国王的（东西）|  The possessive form of "king," indicating ownership or belonging. It's used before a noun to show that the king possesses something.  |例句：The king's crown was made of gold. 国王的王冠是金子做的。

|例句：This is the king's decree. 这是国王的法令。|近义词：royal, regal |反义词: N/A |词性: determiner, possessive pronoun


king's:/kɪŋz/| noun | 国王的（东西）；国王的权力| Used as a noun, usually in a more abstract sense referring to things associated with the king or his authority. Though less common than the determiner usage. |例句：The king's reached far and wide. 国王的权力远及各地。

|例句：Challenging the king's was dangerous.  挑战国王的权威是危险的。|近义词：royalty, kingdom, reign |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From Old English *cyninges*, genitive case of cyning (“king”), from Proto-Germanic *kuningaz (“king”), from *kuningaz (“clan, family, tribe”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵénh₁- (“give birth, beget”). Cognate with Scots kingis, West Frisian kening, Dutch koning, German König.
