overdepressiveness:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈprɛsɪvnɪs/| n.|过度抑郁，极度沮丧|A state of being excessively depressed, beyond what might be considered typical or normal depression.  It suggests an intensity or duration of depressive symptoms that is significantly pronounced.|Her overdepressiveness made it difficult for her to function in daily life. 她极度的抑郁使她难以进行日常生活。|近义词：extreme depression, severe melancholy, profound sadness|反义词：elation, euphoria, joy|词性:noun
Etymology: "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above") + "depressive" (from "depress," from Latin depressus, past participle of deprimere "to press down, sink") + "-ness" (Old English -nes(s), noun-forming suffix denoting state, quality, or condition).
