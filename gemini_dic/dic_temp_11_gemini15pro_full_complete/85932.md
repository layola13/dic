headlining:/ˈhedˌlaɪnɪŋ/| v. adj.|担任主角；领衔主演；成为头条新闻|Being featured as the main performer or act; being the most important or prominent news story.|例句：The band is headlining the festival this year.  今年这个乐队将领衔音乐节。|近义词：starring, featuring, topping|反义词：supporting, minor|词性：动词/形容词

headlining:/ˈhedˌlaɪnɪŋ/| adj.|主要的；重要的；头条的|Main; important; the most prominent.|例句：The headlining story in the newspaper was about the election. 报纸的头条新闻是关于选举的。|近义词：main, principal, leading|反义词：secondary, minor|词性：形容词
