cavaliers:/ˌkævəˈlɪrz/| n.|骑士，骑兵；(17 世纪英国)保皇党|A cavalier is a supporter of King Charles I in the English Civil War.|The cavaliers charged across the battlefield. 骑士们冲过战场。|近义词：knight, horseman, royalist|反义词:roundhead|noun

cavalier:/ˌkævəˈlɪr/| adj.|漫不经心的，傲慢的|If you describe someone as cavalier, you disapprove of them because you think they are not careful or thorough enough about something important.|He was cavalier about the risks involved. 他对涉及的风险漫不经心。|近义词：dismissive, offhand, arrogant|反义词:careful, considerate|adjective
