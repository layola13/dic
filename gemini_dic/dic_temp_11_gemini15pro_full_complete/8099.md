anthroposcopy:/ˌænθrəˈpɒskəpi/ | n. |人体观察；人体测量学|The study of the human body, especially its external features and proportions, typically for purposes of identification or classification.  It involves visual inspection and sometimes measurement of various body parts. |Forensic anthroposcopy was used to identify the remains.  法医人体测量学被用来识别遗骸。| 近义词: anthropometry, somatoscopy | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, potentially a concept like "microscopic examination") |词性: noun
