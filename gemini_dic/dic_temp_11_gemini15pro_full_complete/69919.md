fervency:/ˈfɜːvənsi/ /ˈfɜːrvənsi/| n. |热情，热烈，炽热 |The state of being fervent; ardor; intensity of feeling; warmth of devotion; zeal.|She spoke with a fervency that convinced everyone in the room. 她说话的热情感染了房间里的每一个人。|近义词：ardor, passion, zeal, enthusiasm, intensity|反义词：apathy, indifference, coldness, lukewarmness|词性:名词

词源:
From Late Latin *ferventia, from Latin fervens, present participle of fervēre “to boil, glow.”
