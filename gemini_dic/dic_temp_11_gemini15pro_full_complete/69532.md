feller:/ˈfelər/ (US), /ˈfɛlə/ (UK)| n.|家伙，伙计；伐木工人|A man or boy, often used informally or colloquially.  Sometimes also refers to someone who cuts down trees.|例句：That feller over there looks like he's lost.  那边那个家伙看起来像是迷路了。|例句：He's a strong feller and can handle the heavy lifting. 他是个强壮的家伙，可以搬重物。|近义词：guy, chap, bloke, fellow, lumberjack (when referring to a tree-cutter)|反义词: (No direct opposite, depends on the specific meaning)|词性:名词
