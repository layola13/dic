intraaxillary:/ˌɪntrəˈæksɪləri/
adjective
腋内的；位于腋窝内的
Located within the armpit or axilla.
The surgeon made an intraaxillary incision to access the lymph nodes.  外科医生在腋窝做了一个切口以进入淋巴结。
The rash was localized to the intraaxillary region. 皮疹位于腋窝区域。
Near synonyms: axillary, subaxillary
Antonyms: extraaxillary
word type: adjective
