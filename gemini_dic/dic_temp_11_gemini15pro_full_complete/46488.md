daintiness:/ˈdeɪntinəs/
n.
精致;优雅;美味
The quality of being dainty; delicacy, exquisiteness.
Her daintiness was evident in her delicate movements and graceful mannerisms. 她的优雅体现在她精致的动作和优雅的举止上。
delicacy, exquisiteness, elegance, grace, refinement
clumsiness, awkwardness, gaucheness
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From dainty (adjective) + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  Dainty itself comes from Old French *daintie, variant of deintié "delight, pleasure, value," from Latin dignitatem "worthiness, dignity."
