opisthotonos:/ˌɒpɪsˈθɒtənɒs/ /ˌɑːpɪsˈθɑːtənɑːs/ | n. | 角弓反张 |A severe spasm of the muscles of the back, neck, and extremities, in which the body is held rigidly arched.  This is a characteristic symptom of tetanus (lockjaw) and some other conditions.| The patient experiencing opisthotonos arched their back dramatically. 病人出现了角弓反张，背部剧烈地拱起。| Opisthotonos is a classic sign of tetanus infection. 角弓反张是破伤风感染的典型症状。|  Synonyms: arc de cercle, tetanic spasm | Antonyms: None | Noun
