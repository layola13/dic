compluvium:/kɒmˈpluːviəm/ | n. | 房顶开口，天井 | An opening in the roof of a Roman house, letting in light and rain into the atrium below. | The compluvium allowed rainwater to collect in the impluvium below. 天井让雨水汇集到下面的蓄水池里。 |  The compluvium was essential for ventilation and light in Roman houses. 罗马房屋的天井对于通风和采光至关重要。 | synonym: opening, skylight | antonym: roof, ceiling | noun

compluvium:/kɒmˈpluːviəm/ | n. | 蓄水池 | A shallow pool or basin in the atrium of a Roman house, designed to collect rainwater from the compluvium. | The impluvium filled quickly during the heavy rain, thanks to the compluvium above.  由于上面的天井，蓄水池在大雨期间迅速装满。| The compluvium directed rainwater into the impluvium, providing a source of fresh water. 天井将雨水引到蓄水池中，提供了一个淡水来源。| synonym: basin, cistern, pool | antonym:  none | noun
