indispensable:/ˌɪndɪˈspɛnsəbl/ | adj. | 不可或缺的；绝对必要的 | Something that is indispensable is so important or useful that it is impossible to manage without it. |  A good dictionary is indispensable for learning a new language. 一本好字典对于学习一门新语言是必不可少的。

Water is indispensable to life. 水是生命所必需的。


| 近义词: essential, crucial, vital, necessary | 反义词: dispensable, unnecessary, nonessential | 词性: adjective
