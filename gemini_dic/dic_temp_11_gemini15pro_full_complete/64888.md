essayistical:/ˌɛseɪˈɪstɪkəl/
adjective
散文式的；随笔体的
characteristic of or resembling an essay, especially in being informal or discursive.
His writing style is very essayistical, full of personal anecdotes and reflections. 他的写作风格非常散文式，充满了个人轶事和反思。
Synonyms: discursive, rambling, digressive, informal, free-flowing
Antonyms: concise, succinct, brief, structured, formal
词性: 形容词
