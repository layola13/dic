microlight:/ˈmaɪkrəʊlaɪt/ (UK) /ˈmaɪkroʊlaɪt/ (US)| n.|微型飞机，超轻型飞机|A very small, light aircraft with a flexible wing.|例句：He took flying lessons and bought a microlight. 他上了飞行课，买了一架微型飞机。|例句：Microlights are becoming increasingly popular for recreational flying. 微型飞机越来越受欢迎，用于休闲飞行。|近义词：ultralight aircraft, hang glider (somewhat)|反义词:heavy aircraft, airliner|词性:noun

microlights:/ˈmaɪkrəʊlaɪts/ (UK) /ˈmaɪkroʊlaɪts/ (US)| n. pl.|微型飞机（复数）|Plural form of microlight, referring to more than one microlight aircraft.|例句：Several microlights were circling the airfield. 几架微型飞机在机场上空盘旋。|例句：The microlights took off one after another into the clear blue sky. 微型飞机一个接一个地飞向晴朗的蓝天。|近义词：ultralight aircraft (plural), hang gliders (plural, somewhat)|反义词:heavy aircraft, airliners|词性:noun
