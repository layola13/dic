crushability:/ˌkrʌʃəˈbɪləti/
n.
可压碎性；易碎性
The quality of being crushable; the extent to which something can be crushed.
The crushability of the material makes it suitable for packaging.  这种材料的可压碎性使其适合用于包装。
synonyms: fragility, frangibility, breakability
antonyms: strength, durability, resilience
noun
