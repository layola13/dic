gce:/dʒiː siː iː/ | n. | General Certificate of Education | A British examination taken by students at the age of 16 (GCSE) or 18 (GCE A Level).  It is a qualification that shows a certain level of achievement in secondary education. | Example:  She passed her GCEs with flying colors. 她以优异的成绩通过了普通教育证书考试。 | Example: He's studying hard for his GCE A Levels. 他正在努力学习，准备参加普通教育高级证书考试。 | Synonym: exam, qualification, certificate | Antonym: N/A | abbreviation

注意事项: GCE is mostly used in the plural form (GCEs) when referring to the exams themselves. When talking about the qualification, it can be used in the singular.  While GCE O Levels were replaced by GCSEs, the term "GCE" is still used when referring to A Levels (GCE A Levels).
