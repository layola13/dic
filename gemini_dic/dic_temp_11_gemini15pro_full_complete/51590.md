devalued:/diːˈvæljuːd/
verb, adjective
贬值的，被贬低的
reduced in value, worth, or status.
The currency was devalued by 10%.  该货币贬值了10%。
The company's stock was devalued after the scandal.  丑闻之后，这家公司的股票贬值了。
synonyms: depreciated, reduced, lowered, cheapened, downgraded, underestimated, underrated
antonyms: appreciated, increased, enhanced, raised, upgraded, overestimated, overrated
