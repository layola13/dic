outclerk: /ˌaʊtˈklɜːrk/
verb
胜过…的办事员；比…的办事员更有效率
to surpass in clerical skills or efficiency.
Our team outclerked their team and finished the project a week early. 我们的团队比他们的团队办事员更有效率，提前一周完成了项目。
The new hire quickly outclerked everyone in the department.  新员工很快就比部门里所有人的办事员都更胜一筹。
近义词: outperform, outdo, surpass
反义词: underperform
