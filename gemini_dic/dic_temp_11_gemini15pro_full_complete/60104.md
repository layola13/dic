eerinesses:/ˈɪərɪnɪsɪz/
noun
怪异；阴森；诡异
The quality of being eerie; strangeness or spookiness that causes uneasiness or fear.
The eerinesses of the abandoned house sent shivers down my spine.  废弃房屋的怪异气氛让我脊背发凉。
Near Synonyms: creepiness, spookiness, uneasiness, weirdness, strangeness
Opposite: normalcy, ordinariness, familiarity, comfort
词性: 名词 (plural of eeriness)

eerie:/ˈɪəri/
adjective
怪异的；阴森的；诡异的
Strange and frightening.
The eerie silence of the graveyard was broken only by the rustling leaves.  墓地里怪异的寂静只有树叶的沙沙声才能打破。
Near Synonyms: creepy, spooky, unsettling, weird, strange
Opposite: normal, ordinary, familiar, comforting
词性: 形容词
