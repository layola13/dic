lynchpin:/ˈlɪntʃpɪn/| n. |关键人物，核心人物，关键要素|A person or thing vital to an organization or operation.  Something that holds the various elements of something together.|例句：She is the lynchpin of the whole project.  她是整个项目的关键人物。

例句：The central contract is the lynchpin of the agreement. 这份核心合同是整个协议的关键。|近义词：keystone, cornerstone, linchpin, pivotal, essential, crucial |反义词:unimportant, insignificant, minor, peripheral|词性:noun
