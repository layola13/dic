circummure:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈmjʊər/ /ˌsɝːkəmˈmjʊr/| v.|用墙围住，禁锢|To enclose within walls, especially to imprison.|The king circummured the rebel leader in a lonely tower. 国王将叛军首领囚禁在一座孤塔里。|

例句：
The high walls circummured the ancient city.  高墙环绕着这座古城。

The prisoners were circummured within the castle walls. 囚犯们被关押在城堡的围墙内。


近义词：enclose, wall in, imprison, confine, immure|
反义词：liberate, free, release|
词性：verb|

词源:
From Latin circummūrāre (to wall around), from circum- (around) + mūrāre (to wall), from mūrus (wall).
