contributive:/kənˈtrɪbjʊtɪv/ | adj. |贡献的，起作用的；（语法）定语的 | serving to contribute to something; (Grammar) relating to or functioning as a modifier |  Her contributive efforts to the project were invaluable. 她对这个项目的贡献 invaluable. | 近义词: contributing, contributory, constructive | 反义词: detrimental, harmful | 词性: adjective

contributive:/kənˈtrɪbjʊtɪv/ | n. |(语法)定语 | (Grammar) a word or phrase that modifies another word or phrase, typically a noun |  "Red" is a contributive in the phrase "red car."  在“红色汽车”这个短语中，“红色”是定语。| 近义词: modifier, adjective, attributive | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *contributif, from Latin contributivus (“tending to contribute”), from contribuere (“to contribute”), from com- (“together”) + tribuere (“to grant, bestow, pay tribute”).
