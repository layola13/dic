The ladies enjoyed their weekly kaffeeklatsches, gossiping about the neighborhood. 女士们喜欢她们每周的咖啡聚会，聊聊邻里间的八卦。| 近义词：get-together, social gathering, chat, informal meeting | 反义词: formal meeting, conference | 词性: 名词
