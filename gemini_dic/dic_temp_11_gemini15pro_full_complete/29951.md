caveman:/ˈkeɪvmæn/ (US), /ˈkeɪvmən/ (UK)| n. |穴居人；野人|A person who lived in caves, especially during the Stone Age.|例句：The caveman hunted woolly mammoths. 穴居人猎杀猛犸象。

例句：Cavemen used simple tools made of stone and bone. 穴居人使用由石头和骨头制成的简单工具。|近义词：troglodyte, Neanderthal|反义词:modern man|词性:noun
