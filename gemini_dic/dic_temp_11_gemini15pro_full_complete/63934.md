epipolic:/ˌɛpɪˈpɒlɪk/ |adj.| 表面上的，表观的；（尤指）水面上的|Relating to or denoting a thin layer of slightly different density from the surrounding water, occurring especially at the surface of the sea or a lake and affecting the propagation of light or sound.| The epipolic layer of the ocean scatters sunlight, creating a shimmering effect. 海洋的表观层散射阳光，产生闪烁的效果。| superficial, surface | deep, subsurface | adjective
