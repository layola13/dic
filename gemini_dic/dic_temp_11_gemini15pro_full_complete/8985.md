antiliberalist:/ˌæntiˈlɪbərəlɪst/
n.|反自由主义者|Someone who opposes liberalism.  A person who is against the principles of liberalism, which typically include individual rights, limited government, and free markets.|The antiliberalists staged a protest against the proposed free trade agreement.|近义词:conservative, reactionary, traditionalist|反义词:liberal, progressive|词性:名词

antiliberalist:/ˌæntiˈlɪbərəlɪst/
adj.|反自由主义的|Opposed to liberalism. Characterized by opposition to the principles of liberalism.|The antiliberalist rhetoric in his speech was alarming.|近义词:conservative, reactionary, illiberal|反义词:liberal, progressive|词性:形容词
