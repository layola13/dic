desertification:/dɪˌzɜːtɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ /dɪˌzɜːrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/
n.|沙漠化|The process by which fertile land becomes desert, typically as a result of drought, deforestation, or inappropriate agriculture.|例句：Desertification is a serious environmental problem. 沙漠化是一个严重的环境问题。|近义词：aridification, degradation|反义词:reforestation, reclamation|词性:名词

desertification:/dɪˌzɜːtɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ /dɪˌzɜːrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/
