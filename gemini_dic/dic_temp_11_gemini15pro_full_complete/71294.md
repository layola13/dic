fixidity:/fɪkˈsɪdɪti/
n.|固定性；不变性；僵化|The fixidity of his ideas prevented him from adapting to new situations.  他的思想僵化使他无法适应新形势。|English explanation: The state or quality of being fixed or unchanging; inability to adapt or change.|例句：The fixidity of the exchange rate caused problems for exporters.  固定汇率给出口商带来了问题。

例句：The company's fixidity in its marketing strategy led to declining sales. 该公司在营销策略上的僵化导致销售额下降。|近义词：rigidity, inflexibility, stiffness|反义词：flexibility, fluidity, adaptability|词性:noun
