isotopism:/ˈaɪsəˌtoʊpɪzəm/
n.
同位素性，同位素现象
The phenomenon of having isotopes; the state of being isotopic.
Uranium exhibits isotopism, with U-235 and U-238 being its most common isotopes. 铀表现出同位素性，U-235和U-238是其最常见的同位素。

Isotopism plays a crucial role in radiometric dating. 同位素性在放射性测年中起着至关重要的作用。

近义词: isotopy
反义词: (No direct antonym)
