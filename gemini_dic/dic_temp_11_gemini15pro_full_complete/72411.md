fluework:/ˈfluːwɜːrk/
n.
烟道工程，烟道系统
The system of flues in a building.
The fluework needs to be inspected regularly to ensure safe operation.  烟道系统需要定期检查以确保安全运行。
The old house had intricate fluework that was a marvel of engineering.  这栋老房子有着复杂的烟道工程，堪称工程奇迹。
synonyms: flue system, chimney system, venting system
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "ventilation blockage")
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From flue + work.  Flue (from Old French *fluer "to flow") refers to a duct for smoke and waste gases.
