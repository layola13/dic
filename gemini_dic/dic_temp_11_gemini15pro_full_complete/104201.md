jupartie: /ʒuːˈpɑːrti/ | n. |  聚会; 欢乐的聚会 | A festive gathering, often involving music, dancing, and socializing. |  We're having a jupartie at the beach this weekend.  我们这周末在海滩举行一个聚会。 | synonyms: party, celebration, festivity, fete, bash | antonyms: wake, funeral | noun


Etymology:
From French "jour" (day) + "partie" (part, portion, party).  Originally referred to a portion of the day set aside for enjoyment.
