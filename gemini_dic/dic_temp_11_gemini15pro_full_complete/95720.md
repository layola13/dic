inconsequence:/ɪnˈkɒnsɪkwəns/ /ɪnˈkɑːnsɪkwəns/| n.|不重要，无关紧要；不合逻辑|Lack of logical connection or importance.|His speech was full of inconsequence, jumping from one topic to another without any clear connection. 他的演讲充满了不连贯性，在各个主题之间跳来跳去，没有任何清晰的联系。|The inconsequence of her remark made everyone in the room uncomfortable. 她漫不经心的话让房间里的每个人都感到不舒服。|近义词：insignificance, triviality, irrelevance, illogicality|反义词：significance, importance, relevance, logic|词性:名词

inconsequent:/ɪnˈkɒnsɪkwənt/ /ɪnˈkɑːnsɪkwənt/| adj.|不重要的，无关紧要的；不合逻辑的；不一致的|Not important or significant; illogical; inconsistent.|His argument was inconsequent and failed to persuade anyone. 他的论点不合逻辑，没能说服任何人。|Her actions were inconsequent with her words, leading to confusion. 她的行为与她的话不一致，导致了混乱。|近义词：insignificant, trivial, irrelevant, illogical, inconsistent|反义词：significant, important, relevant, logical, consistent|词性:形容词
