executer:/ˈɛksɪˌkjutər/| n.|执行者，遗嘱执行人|A person who carries out a plan, order, or course of action.  The executor of the will distributed the assets according to the deceased's wishes.| The executor of the estate managed the sale of the property. 这位遗产执行人管理了房产的出售。|Synonyms: administrator, enforcer, implementer|Antonyms: obstructor, hinderer|noun
executrix:/ɪɡˈzɛkjətrɪks/| n.|女遗嘱执行人|A female executor of a will.|She was named the executrix of her mother's will. 她被指定为她母亲遗嘱的执行人。|Synonyms: administratrix|Antonyms: N/A|noun
