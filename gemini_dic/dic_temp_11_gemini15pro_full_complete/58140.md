drosophilas:/droʊˈsɒfɪləz/| n. |果蝇（复数）|Small flies belonging to the family Drosophilidae, commonly used in genetic research due to their short lifespan and ease of breeding.|例句：Drosophilas are important model organisms in genetics. 果蝇是遗传学中重要的模式生物。

例句：Scientists use drosophilas to study the effects of mutations. 科学家利用果蝇来研究突变的影响。|近义词：fruit flies|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin *Drosophila*, from Greek δρόσος (drosos, “dew”) + φίλος (philos, “loving”).  Therefore, "dew-loving".
