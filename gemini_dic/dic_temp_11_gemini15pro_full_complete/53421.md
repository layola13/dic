disappear:/ˌdɪsəˈpɪər/ /ˌdɪsəˈpɪr/| vi.|消失，不见|To cease to be visible; vanish.|The magician made the rabbit disappear. 魔术师让兔子消失了。|近义词：vanish, fade, evaporate|反义词:appear, emerge, materialize|verb

disappear:/ˌdɪsəˈpɪər/ /ˌdɪsəˈpɪr/| vi.|消亡，灭绝|To cease to exist.|Many species are disappearing due to habitat loss. 许多物种由于栖息地丧失而正在消失。|近义词：die out, become extinct, perish|反义词:arise, originate, develop|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French desaparier, from des- (expressing reversal) + aparoir (to appear), from Latin apparēre.
