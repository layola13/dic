nymphic: /ˈnɪmfɪk/ | adj. | 仙女的；似仙女的；优雅的；娇美的 | Relating to or characteristic of a nymph; resembling or suggestive of a nymph, especially in grace and beauty. |  The painting captured the girl's nymphic beauty. 这幅画捕捉到了女孩仙女般的美丽。

The dress gave her a nymphic appearance. 这件衣服让她看起来像仙女一样。 | 近义词: ethereal, graceful, delicate, fairylike | 反义词: coarse, ungainly, awkward | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Late Latin *nymphĭcus, from Latin nympha (“nymph, bride, young woman”) + -icus.  Ultimately from Ancient Greek νύμφη (númphē).
