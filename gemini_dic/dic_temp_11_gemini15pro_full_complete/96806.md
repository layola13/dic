inertia:/ɪˈnɜːʃə/ /ɪˈnɝːʃə/| n.|惯性，惰性|A property of matter by which it continues in its existing state of rest or uniform motion in a straight line, unless that state is changed by an external force.  A tendency to do nothing or to remain unchanged.|例句：The car rolled down the hill due to inertia. 由于惯性，汽车从山上滚了下来。

例句：He showed considerable mental inertia. 他表现出相当的精神惰性。|近义词：inactivity, idleness, apathy, resistance, momentum|反义词：activity, energy, change, motion|Noun
