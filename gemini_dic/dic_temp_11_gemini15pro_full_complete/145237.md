overbite:/ˈoʊvərˌbaɪt/| n. |覆颌，龅牙|A condition in which the upper teeth protrude significantly over the lower teeth when the jaws are closed.|She has a noticeable overbite.|Synonyms:  malocclusion, buck teeth|Antonyms: underbite|Noun

overbites:/ˈoʊvərˌbaɪts/| n. |覆颌，龅牙（复数）|Multiple instances of the condition where the upper teeth protrude over the lower teeth.|Overbites are common dental issues.|Synonyms: malocclusions |Antonyms: underbites|Noun
