keratinocytes:/ˈkɛrətɪnəˌsaɪts/ /ˈkɛrətɪnoʊˌsaɪts/ | n. |角质形成细胞|An epidermal cell which produces keratin.|Keratinocytes are the predominant cell type in the epidermis. 角质形成细胞是表皮中的主要细胞类型。|Keratinocytes synthesize keratin, a fibrous protein that gives skin its protective properties. 角质形成细胞合成角蛋白，一种赋予皮肤保护特性的纤维蛋白。|Synonyms: epidermal cell |Antonyms: None |Noun
