carved:/kɑːrvd/ | adj. | 雕刻的；雕琢的 | shaped or decorated by cutting into a hard material, such as wood or stone |  The table was carved from a single piece of oak. 这张桌子是用一块橡木雕刻而成的。|  sculpted, engraved, etched | plain, smooth | adjective

carved:/kɑːrvd/ | v. | 雕刻；雕琢（carve的过去式和过去分词）| past tense and past participle of carve | He carved his initials into the tree. 他把他的姓名首字母刻在了树上。| sculpted, engraved, etched | erase, smooth | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ceorfan (attested only in the past participle corfen), from Proto-Germanic *kerfaną (source also of Old Frisian kerva, Middle Dutch kerven, Dutch kerven, Old High German kerban, German kerben "to notch, carve"), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kerb- (source also of Latin curvus "curved, bent", Old Church Slavonic skrbati "to cut").
