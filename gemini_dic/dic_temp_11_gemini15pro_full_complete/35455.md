coacervate:/koʊˈæsərveɪt/
n.|凝聚层;凝聚体|A collection of colloidal droplets held together by electrostatic forces.|Coacervates are thought to have played a role in the origin of life. 凝聚层被认为在生命起源中扮演了重要角色。|
coacervate:/koʊˈæsərveɪt/
v.|凝聚;形成凝聚层|To form into a coacervate.|The molecules coacervated in the solution.  这些分子在溶液中凝聚。|
coacervate:/koʊˈæsərveɪt/
adj.|凝聚的|Formed into a coacervate.|The coacervate droplets were visible under the microscope. 在显微镜下可以看到凝聚的液滴。|
coacervate: /koʊˈæsərveɪt/ | Etymology:  From Latin coacervare "to heap up," from co- "together" + acervus "heap." |
