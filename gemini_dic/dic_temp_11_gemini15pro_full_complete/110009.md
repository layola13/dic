librarianships: /laɪˈbrɛəriənʃɪps/
n.
图书馆学；图书馆员的职位
the profession of a librarian.  The study or practice of organizing and managing libraries.
She completed a Master's degree in Librarianships. 她获得了图书馆学硕士学位。
The university offers several Librarianships for students interested in working in academic libraries. 这所大学为有兴趣在学术图书馆工作的学生提供几个图书馆员职位。
近义词: library science, library management
反义词:  (No direct antonym. Could consider concepts like "information chaos" or "lack of organization" as opposing ideas.)
词性: noun
