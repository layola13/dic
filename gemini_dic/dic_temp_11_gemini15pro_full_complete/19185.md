bewitched:/bɪˈwɪtʃt/
adjective
着迷的，被迷住的
Deeply affected by witchcraft; enchanted, fascinated.
She was utterly bewitched by his charm. 她完全被他的人格魅力迷住了。

近义词: enchanted, captivated, charmed, fascinated, spellbound
反义词: disenchanted, uninterested, bored, repulsed

词性: adjective
词源: From be- + witch + -ed.  "Be-" is a prefix meaning "thoroughly," and "witch" refers to the use of magic or charms. "-ed" is a suffix forming the past participle.
