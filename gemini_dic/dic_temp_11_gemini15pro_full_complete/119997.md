milligrams:/ˈmɪlɪɡræmz/
noun
毫克
A unit of mass equal to one thousandth of a gram.
The doctor prescribed 500 milligrams of the medication. 医生开了500毫克的药。
synonyms: mg
antonyms: kilograms, grams
