meagerness:/ˈmiːɡərnəs/| n. |贫乏，不足，少量|The meagerness of the harvest caused widespread famine.  收成歉收导致了大范围的饥荒。|例句：The meagerness of his salary barely allowed him to pay rent. 他微薄的薪水几乎不够付房租。|近义词：scarcity, paucity, insufficiency, lack, dearth|反义词：abundance, plenty, profusion, wealth|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *megreness, from Old French maigresse, from maigre (“lean, thin”) (from Latin macer (“lean, thin”)), + -ness.
