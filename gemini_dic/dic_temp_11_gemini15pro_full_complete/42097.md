cotransported:/ˌkoʊˈtrænsˈpɔːr.tɪd/
adjective | 共同转运的，协同转运的 |  Transported together across a membrane.  This often refers to the simultaneous transport of two or more different molecules by a single transporter protein. | Glucose is cotransported with sodium ions in the intestine. 葡萄糖在肠道中与钠离子共同转运。| synonyms: symported, coupled transport | antonyms: antiported |词性: adjective

cotransport:/ˌkoʊˈtrænsˈpɔːrt/
verb | 共同转运，协同转运 |  To transport two or more different molecules simultaneously across a membrane. | The protein cotransports sodium and glucose.  这种蛋白质共同转运钠和葡萄糖。| synonyms: symport, couple transport | antonyms: antiport |词性: verb

cotransporter:/ˌkoʊˈtrænsˈpɔːr.tər/
noun | 共同转运体，协同转运体 | A protein that transports two or more different molecules simultaneously across a membrane. | The sodium-glucose cotransporter is essential for glucose absorption. 钠-葡萄糖协同转运体对于葡萄糖吸收至关重要。| synonyms: symporter, coupled transporter | antonyms: antiporter |词性: noun
