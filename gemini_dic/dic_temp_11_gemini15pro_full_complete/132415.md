nondepartmental:/ˌnɒndɪˌpɑːtˈmɛntəl/
adjective
非部门的; 不属于任何部门的
Not belonging to or connected with any particular government department.
The report was prepared by a nondepartmental public body.  这份报告是由一个非政府部门的公共机构准备的。
The government set up a nondepartmental body to oversee the project. 政府设立了一个非部门机构来监督这个项目。

近义词: independent, autonomous, extra-departmental
反义词: departmental

词性: adjective
