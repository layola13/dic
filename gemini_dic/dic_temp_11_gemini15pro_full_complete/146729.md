overhonoring:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːnərɪŋ/
|v.| 过分尊敬；给予过多的荣誉|The company was accused of overhonoring its CEO by giving him an exorbitant bonus.|该公司被指责给予其首席执行官过高的奖金，对他表示过分的尊敬。|
|v.| 过分尊重|She felt they were overhonoring her with such lavish praise.|她觉得他们对她如此 lavish 的赞扬，让她感到过分尊重。|
|近义词:|overpraising, overestimating, idolizing|
|反义词:|disrespecting, slighting, undervaluing|
|词性:|动词 (verb)|
