detonatable:/ˈdɛtəneɪtəbl/
adj.
可引爆的
Capable of being detonated; explosive.
The bomb squad carefully handled the detonatable materials.  拆弹小组小心地处理了可引爆的材料。
The warehouse stored several tons of detonatable chemicals. 仓库里储存了几吨可引爆的化学品。

近义词:explosive, ignitable, flammable
反义词:non-explosive, inert, stable
词性:adjective
词源:From detonate + -able. Detonate comes from Latin detonare "to thunder down," from de- "down" + tonare "to thunder."
