foregainst:/fɔːrəˈɡɛnst/ | prep. | 在...前面，面对; 预防; 反对 | Before; in preparation for; against. | The ship sailed foregainst the wind.  这艘船逆风航行。| 近义词: before, against, in front of | 反义词: behind, after | 词性: 介词

Etymology:  A combination of "fore" (before) and "against."  "Fore" comes from Old English "fore," meaning "before, in front." "Against" comes from Old English "ongegn," meaning "opposite, toward."
