inextinguishably:/ˌɪnɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbli/
adverb
不可磨灭地，永不熄灭地
in a way that cannot be extinguished; permanently.
Her name is inextinguishably etched in the annals of history. 她的名字被不可磨灭地刻在了历史的史册上。
The memory of that day is inextinguishably burned into my mind. 那一天的记忆永远铭刻在我的脑海里。
近义词:permanently, indelibly, eternally
反义词:temporarily, fleetingly
词性:adverb

词源:
From in- (not) + extinguishable + -ly (adverbial suffix). Extinguishable comes from Latin extinguere (to extinguish).
