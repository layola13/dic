maltobiose:/ˌmɔːltəˈbaɪoʊs/ /ˌmɔːltəˈbaɪəʊs/| n. |麦芽二糖|A reducing disaccharide formed by the action of amylase on starch, consisting of two glucose units linked by an α-1,4 glycosidic bond.  It is an intermediate product of starch hydrolysis.|Maltobiose is less sweet than sucrose.|近义词:disaccharide|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific chemical compound)|noun
