crawfish:/ˈkrɔːfɪʃ/ (US) /ˈkrɔːfɪʃ/ (UK)| n.|小龙虾；淡水螯虾|A small freshwater crustacean similar to a lobster, with a hard shell and large claws.|例句：We caught crawfish in the creek. 我们在小溪里捉小龙虾。|例句：Crawfish boils are a popular summertime tradition in Louisiana.  在路易斯安那州，煮小龙虾是夏季的流行传统。|近义词：crayfish, crawdad, mudbug|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
