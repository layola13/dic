coveters:/ˈkʌvətərz/| n. |贪婪者，垂涎者|People who have a strong desire to possess something that belongs to someone else.  Those who covet. |The coveters eyed his new sports car with envy. 那些贪婪者以羡慕的眼光看着他的新跑车。|近义词：enviers, desirers, wanters|反义词:  philanthropists, altruists|词性: 名词

词源: From the verb "covet" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Covet" comes from Old French "coveiter" which ultimately derives from Latin "cupiditare," meaning "to desire earnestly," from "cupidus," meaning "eagerly desirous."
