connoted:/kəˈnoʊtɪd/
verb
意味着; 暗示;  包含
implied as a meaning or quality in addition to the denotation.
The word "home" often connotes warmth and security. “家”这个词常常意味着温暖和安全。

connoted:/kəˈnoʊtɪd/
verb
内涵; 隐含意义
to suggest or imply in addition to the precise, literal meaning
The raised eyebrow connoted disapproval. 扬起的眉毛暗示着不赞成。


Synonyms: suggest, imply, intimate, signify, indicate
Antonyms: denote, specify, declare, state explicitly

词性: verb (动词)
