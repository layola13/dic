insulary:/ˈɪnsjʊləri/ /ˈɪnsjəleri/| adj.|岛屿的；偏狭的；孤立的|Relating to or characteristic of an island; also, narrow-minded or illiberal; isolated.|The islanders maintained an insulary culture, resistant to outside influences. 岛民保持着一种孤立的文化，抵制外来影响。|The politician's insulary views prevented him from seeing the bigger picture. 这位政治家的狭隘观点使他无法看到全局。|近义词：narrow-minded, parochial, provincial, isolated, detached|反义词：cosmopolitan, broad-minded, open-minded, inclusive|词性: adjective

insularity:/ˌɪnsjʊˈlærəti/ /ˌɪnsjəˈlærəti/| n.|岛屿性；偏狭；孤立|The state of being insular, especially the narrowness of viewpoint or the condition of being isolated.|The insularity of the community made it difficult for newcomers to integrate.  这个社区的封闭性使新来者难以融入。|Their insularity led to a lack of understanding of other cultures. 他们的狭隘导致了对其他文化的缺乏了解。|近义词：narrow-mindedness, parochialism, provincialism, isolation, detachment|反义词：cosmopolitanism, broad-mindedness, open-mindedness, inclusiveness|词性: noun
