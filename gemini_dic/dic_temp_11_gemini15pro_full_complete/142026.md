organless:/ˈɔːrɡənlɪs/| adj.|无器官的，无组织的|Lacking organs; without organized physical structure. |The virus, being organless, cannot survive outside a host cell. 这种病毒没有器官，在宿主细胞外无法存活。|Example 2:  Scientists are studying the behavior of organless life forms. 科学家正在研究无器官生命体的行为。|近义词：inorganic, structureless|反义词: organic, structured|词性:形容词

词源: organ + -less

organ (n.) 来自拉丁语 organum “instrument, tool; bodily organ,” 源自希腊语 organon “instrument, tool, organ of the body,” 源自 PIE *wergon-,  词根 *werg- “to work, do.”

-less (suffix) 来自古英语 -leas,  表示 “without,”  来自 PIE *loisos,  词根 *leis- “to track, furrow.”
