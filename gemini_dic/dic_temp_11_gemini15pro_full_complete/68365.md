fallibility:/ˌfæləˈbɪləti/| n.|易错性，出错性|The fallibility of human memory is well documented. 人类记忆的易错性已被充分证明。|例句：The scientist acknowledged the fallibility of his theory.  这位科学家承认他的理论可能出错。

例句：The project's failure was due to the fallibility of the initial plan. 该项目的失败是由于最初计划的易错性造成的。|近义词：frailty, imperfection, weakness|反义词:infallibility, impeccability|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *fallibilitas, from Latin fallibilis ("liable to err"), from fallere ("to deceive, err, fail") + -ibilis (suffix forming adjectives meaning "able to be").
