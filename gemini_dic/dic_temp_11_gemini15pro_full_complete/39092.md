confraternity: /ˌkɒnfrəˈtɜːnɪti/ (US) /ˌkɑːnfrəˈtɜːrnəti/
n.
宗教兄弟会; 联谊会; 同业公会; 协会
a brotherhood or association, especially a religious one; a society of people with shared interests.
The local confraternity of carpenters met once a month. 当地的木匠联谊会每月聚会一次。
synonyms: brotherhood, fellowship, association, society, guild, league, union
antonyms:  isolation, division, separation
noun
