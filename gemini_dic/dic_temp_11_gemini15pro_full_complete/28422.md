carbonization:/ˌkɑːrbənaɪˈzeɪʃn/ |n.|碳化，炭化|The process of converting an organic substance into carbon or a carbon-containing residue through pyrolysis or destructive distillation, excluding calcining and incineration. |The carbonization of wood produces charcoal.| 近义词: pyrolysis, destructive distillation |反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "combustion" for a contrasting process) | noun
