non-resisting:/ˌnɒn rɪˈzɪstɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːn rɪˈzɪstɪŋ/| adj.|不抵抗的，顺从的，不反抗的|Not offering resistance; passive.  This describes someone or something that does not fight back or oppose force.|The non-resisting crowd was easily dispersed by the police.  不抵抗的群众很容易就被警察驱散了。|Synonyms: passive, submissive, yielding, compliant|Antonyms: resisting, defiant, opposing|adjective
