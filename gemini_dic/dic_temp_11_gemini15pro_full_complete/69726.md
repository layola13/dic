feoffee:/fɪˈfiː/
n.
受封者；封臣
A person who is granted a fief or fee.
The king granted lands to his loyal feoffees. 国王将土地赐予他忠诚的封臣。
近义词:vassal, tenant
反义词:lord, grantor
词性:noun


Etymology:
From Old French feffé, past participle of feffer (“to enfeoff”), from Medieval Latin feoffare, from Frankish *fehon (“property, cattle”).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *fehu (“cattle, wealth”). Cognate with fee.
