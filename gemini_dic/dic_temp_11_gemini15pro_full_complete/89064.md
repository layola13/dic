holophrasis:/ˌhɑːləˈfreɪsɪs/ /ˈhɒləfreɪsɪs/
noun
单词句;单词句表达;独词句
A holophrase is a single word or phrase that expresses a complete thought or meaning, typically used by young children in the early stages of language development.
The child's utterance of "milk" could be a holophrase meaning "I want milk."  孩子说“牛奶”可能是一个单字句，意思是“我想要牛奶”。
Holophrastic speech is common in children around one year old.  单词句表达在1岁左右的儿童中很常见。
synonyms: one-word sentence, one-word utterance
antonyms: sentence, multi-word utterance
n.

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
A holophrase often represents a simple present tense statement.  单字句通常代表一个一般现在时的陈述。
"Up" can mean "I want to be picked up." “上”可以表示“我想要被抱起来”。


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
A holophrase can also imply a present continuous action.  单字句也可以暗示一个现在进行时的动作。
"Car" might mean "The car is going." “车”可能表示“车正在行驶”。


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
While less common, a holophrase could suggest a recently completed action. 单字句虽然不太常见，但也可能暗示一个最近完成的动作。
"Milk" could also mean "I finished my milk." “牛奶”也可以表示“我喝完了我的牛奶”。


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
It's unlikely a holophrase would convey this complex tense.  单字句不太可能表达这种复杂的时态。

疑问句:
A rising intonation can make a holophrase a question.  升调可以使单字句成为一个问句。
"Mama?" could mean "Where is Mama?" “妈妈？”可以表示“妈妈在哪里？”


否定句:
While less common, context and intonation can imply negation. 虽然不太常见，但上下文和语调可以暗示否定。
"No" accompanied by a shake of the head can mean "I don't want that."  摇头并说“不”可以表示“我不想要那个”。
