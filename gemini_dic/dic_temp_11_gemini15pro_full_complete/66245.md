exclamatory:/ɪksˈklæmətɔːri/
adjective
感叹的；惊叹的
Expressing strong emotion, typically surprise, excitement, or anger.
"An exclamatory sentence."
She let out an exclamatory "Wow!" when she saw the gift. 她看到礼物时发出一声惊叹的“哇！”。
exclamatory, excited, emphatic
neutral, calm
adjective
