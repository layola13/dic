literary-editorship:/ˈlɪtərəri ˈɛdɪtərʃɪp/
n.
文学编辑职位；文学编辑工作
The position and duties of a literary editor.  The role involves overseeing the editorial content of literary works, often for a publishing house or journal.
She held the literary-editorship of a prestigious journal for many years. 她多年来一直担任一家著名期刊的文学编辑。
synonyms: editorship, literary editor position
antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider roles unrelated to editing, e.g., authorship, writing)
noun
