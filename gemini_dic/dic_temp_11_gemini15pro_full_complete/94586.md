impracticableness: /ɪmˌpræktɪkəˈbɪlnəs/ | n. | 不可行性；不切实际性 |  The impracticableness of his suggestion was immediately obvious. | 例句：The impracticableness of his suggestion was immediately obvious. 他的建议的不可行性显而易见。 | 近义词：impossibility, infeasibility | 反义词：feasibility, possibility | 词性: 名词


词源:  From impracticable + -ness.  Impracticable comes from in- (not) + practicable (able to be put into practice).  Ultimately from Latin practicus (fit for action).
