customhouse:/ˈkʌstəmhaʊs/ (US), /ˈkʌstəmzhaʊs/ (UK)| n. |海关；关税局|A government building where customs duties are collected and where vessels are entered and cleared.|The goods were held at the customhouse until the duty was paid. 货物被扣在海关，直到关税付清。|Synonyms: customs, port of entry|Antonyms: N/A|noun
