anti-populisms:/ˌæntiˈpɒpjʊlɪzəmz/
n.
反民粹主义，反民粹主义思潮
opposition to populism; ideologies or movements that counter populist sentiments
The rise of anti-populisms in several European countries reflects a growing weariness of nationalist rhetoric.  几个欧洲国家反民粹主义的兴起反映了人们日益厌倦民族主义言论。
The conference explored various forms of anti-populisms and their effectiveness.  会议探讨了各种形式的反民粹主义及其有效性。
近义词: anti-demagoguery, elitism
反义词: populism, popularism
词性: noun
