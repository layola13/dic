androphore:/ˈændrəfɔːr/
n.
雄蕊柄，花丝基部连合而成的柱状体
The part of a flower that supports the stamens.
The androphore of this hibiscus flower is particularly long and prominent.  这种芙蓉花的雄蕊柄特别长而突出。
