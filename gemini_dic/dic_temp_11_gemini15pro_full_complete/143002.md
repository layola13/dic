outbelch: /ˌaʊtˈbɛltʃ/
verb
喷出;  咆哮而出
to emit or discharge violently, as if belching.  To utter loudly and forcefully.
The volcano outbelched lava and ash. 火山喷发出熔岩和火山灰。
The dragon outbelched a stream of fire.  龙喷出一股火焰。
近义词: erupt, spew,  expel,  discharge
反义词: absorb, inhale, ingest
词性: verb

词源：out- + belch.  "Out-"  作为前缀，表示“向外”，“完全地”。 "Belch" 指的是打嗝，或像打嗝一样喷出。因此，"outbelch"  强调喷出的强烈和彻底。
