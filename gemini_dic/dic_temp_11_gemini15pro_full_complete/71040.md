/fɜːrst/ (US) /fɜːst/ (UK)| adj. adv. n.| 第一的；首先；最初| Coming before all others in time, order, or importance; firstly; initially.|

例句：
He came first in the race.
他赢得了比赛的第一名。

She always puts her family first.
她总是把家人放在第一位。


近义词：initial, leading, primary, foremost, premier
反义词：last, final, ultimate
词性：adjective, adverb, noun

/fɜːrst/ (US) /fɜːst/ (UK)| n.| 第一；首位；（汽车、自行车等的）一挡| The person or thing that comes before all others in time, order, or importance; the lowest forward gear or speed of a motor vehicle or bicycle.|

例句：
She achieved a first in her exams.
她在考试中得了第一名。

Put the car into first.
把车挂上一挡。


近义词：beginning, start, onset
反义词：last, end
词性：noun
