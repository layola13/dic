one-stop:/wʌnˈstɒp/ (US) /wʌnˈstɒp/ (UK)| adj.|一站式的；全方位的|Providing or offering a comprehensive range of products or services at a single location.|Example 1:  We offer a one-stop shop for all your travel needs. 我们提供一站式旅行服务。

Example 2:  This website is a one-stop resource for learning English. 这个网站是一个学习英语的一站式资源。|近义词：comprehensive, all-in-one, full-service|反义词: limited, specialized|词性:形容词
