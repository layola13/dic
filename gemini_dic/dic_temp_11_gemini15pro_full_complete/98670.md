insulated:/ˈɪnsjəleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 绝缘的，隔热的，隔离的 | Protected from heat, cold, or noise by a material that prevents the passage of heat, sound, or electricity. |  The house is well insulated, so it stays warm in the winter and cool in the summer.  这房子隔热良好，冬天保暖，夏天凉爽。 | synonyms: protected, shielded, isolated | antonyms: exposed, vulnerable, connected | adjective

insulate:/ˈɪnsjəleɪt/ | v. | 隔离，使绝缘，使隔热 | To cover or surround something with a material that prevents the passage of heat, sound, or electricity. | They insulated the attic to reduce energy costs. 他们给阁楼做了隔热处理以降低能源成本。 | synonyms: protect, shield, isolate | antonyms: expose | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *insulatus, past participle of insulare "to make into an island, isolate," from Latin insula "island."
