impactful:/ɪmˈpæktfəl/| adj.|有影响力的，有冲击力的|Having a major impact or effect.|Her presentation was impactful and persuaded many people to support the project. 她的演讲很有影响力，说服了许多人支持这个项目。|例句：The impactful images from the war zone left a lasting impression.  战争地区带来的震撼影像留下了深刻的印象。

例句：His impactful speech motivated the team to work harder. 他振奋人心的演讲激励团队更加努力地工作。|近义词：influential, effective, powerful, significant|反义词：insignificant, ineffective, weak|词性:形容词

词源: impact + ful.  Impact 源自拉丁语 impāctus,  意为"a striking against," 来自 impingere "to strike, push against,"  由 in- "in, into" + pangere "to fix, fasten" 组成。 -ful 是一个形容词后缀，表示“充满...的”。
