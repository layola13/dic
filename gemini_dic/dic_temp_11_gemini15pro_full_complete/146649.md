overharshnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrʃnəsɪz/
noun
过度严厉；过度苛刻
The overharshnesses of the punishment sparked public outrage.  这种惩罚的过度严厉引发了公众的愤怒。
The report detailed the overharshnesses of the prison regime.  报告详细描述了监狱制度的过度苛刻。
Synonym:severity, strictness, rigidity, austerity
Antonym:leniency, mildness, gentleness, kindness
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
"overharshnesses" is derived from "overharshness," which itself is formed from "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much") and "harshness" (noun meaning "severity" or "roughness"). "Harshness" ultimately comes from the Old Norse word "harskr" meaning "sharp" or "rough."
