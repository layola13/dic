coati-mundis:/koʊˌɑːtiˈmʌndiz/| n. |南美浣熊，白鼻浣熊|A raccoon-like mammal with a long, ringed tail found in Central and South America. It is known for its long snout and foraging habits.|Coati-mundis are highly social animals, often seen foraging in groups. 南美浣熊是高度社会化的动物，经常成群觅食。| Coati-mundis are adept climbers, often seen in trees. 南美浣熊是熟练的攀爬者，经常在树上看到。|Synonyms: coatimundi, Brazilian aardvark|Antonyms: N/A|noun
