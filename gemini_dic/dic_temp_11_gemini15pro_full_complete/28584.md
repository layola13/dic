careerwomen: /kəˈrɪər ˌwɪmɪn/
n.
职业女性
Women who are focused on their professional careers.
Many young careerwomen prioritize their work over starting a family. 许多年轻的职业女性将工作置于组建家庭之上。
She is a successful careerwoman in the finance industry.  她在金融行业是一位成功的职业女性。
近义词: professional women, businesswomen
反义词: homemaker, housewife
词性: noun


Etymology:
"career" (from Old French "carriere" meaning "racecourse, road") + "women" (plural of "woman")
