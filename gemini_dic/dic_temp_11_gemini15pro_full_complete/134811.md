nonmilitantly:/ˌnɒnˈmɪlɪtəntli/
adverb
非好战地；不激进地
in a nonmilitant manner; without militancy.
The protesters demonstrated nonmilitantly.  抗议者以非好战的方式示威。
The union negotiated nonmilitantly for better wages. 工会以非激进的方式就提高工资问题进行谈判。
Synonyms: peacefully, passively, nonaggressively
Antonyms: militantly, aggressively, belligerently
词性: 副词
