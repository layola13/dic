nonreceivable:/ˌnɑːn rɪˈsiːvəbl/
adjective
不可收的；无法收回的
Not able to be received or collected, especially of debts or payments.
The company wrote off $1 million in nonreceivable debts.  这家公司注销了100万美元的无法收回的债务。
Invoices more than 90 days overdue are classified as nonreceivable.  超过90天逾期的发票被归类为不可收回的。
近义词:uncollectible, irrecoverable, bad
反义词:receivable, collectible
词性:adjective

nonreceivable:/ˌnɑːn rɪˈsiːvəbl/
noun
坏账；无法收回的款项
A debt or amount of money that is unlikely to be paid.
The company's nonreceivables increased by 20% this year.  这家公司的坏账今年增加了20%。
The bank had to write off a large number of nonreceivables.  银行不得不注销大量的坏账。
近义词:bad debt, uncollectible account
反义词:receivable, asset
词性:noun
