blockchain:/ˈblɒkˌtʃeɪn/ (UK) /ˈblɑːkˌtʃeɪn/ (US)| n.| 区块链 | A digital ledger in which transactions made in bitcoin or another cryptocurrency are recorded chronologically and publicly. | Bitcoin uses blockchain technology to record transactions. 比特币使用区块链技术来记录交易。 | 近义词: distributed ledger, digital ledger | 反义词: centralized database | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  A compound of "block" and "chain."  "Block" refers to a group of transactions bundled together. "Chain" refers to the chronological linking of these blocks.
