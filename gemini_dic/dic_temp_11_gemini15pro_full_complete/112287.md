loquacious: /loʊˈkweɪʃəs/ | adj. | 健谈的，多话的 | Tending to talk a great deal; talkative. |  She's a loquacious woman who can talk for hours about anything. 她是个健谈的女人，可以滔滔不绝地谈论任何事情。|  He was naturally loquacious, which made him a great storyteller. 他天生健谈，这使他成为一个很棒的说书人。 | 近义词: talkative, garrulous, voluble | 反义词: taciturn, reticent, laconic | 词性: adjective

词源: From Latin loquax, loquac- ‘talkative’, from loqui ‘to speak’.
