bernacle:/ˈbɑːrnəkəl/ (US), /ˈbɑːnəkl/ (UK)| n.|藤壶；（喻）难以摆脱的人或事物|A barnacle is a small crustacean that attaches itself to rocks, ships' hulls, and other submerged surfaces. Figuratively, it refers to a person or thing that clings persistently and is difficult to get rid of.|The hull of the ship was covered in barnacles. 船体上覆盖着藤壶。

He's become a barnacle at the club, always hanging around. 他成了俱乐部的常客，总是闲逛在那里。|近义词：limpet, freeloader (figurative)|反义词: None|词性: noun
