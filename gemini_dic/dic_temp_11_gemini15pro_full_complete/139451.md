obsoletes: /ˈɒbsəliːts/ (US) /ˈɒbsəliːts/ (UK)| v. | 使过时，淘汰 | To make something outdated or no longer in use. |  Example: New technology quickly obsoletes older models.  新技术很快淘汰了旧型号。

Example:  The rise of digital photography has largely obsoleted film cameras. 数码摄影的兴起很大程度上淘汰了胶卷相机。

| 近义词: replaces, supersedes, displaces, retires | 反义词:  modernizes, updates, renews | 词性: verb
