inventorize: /ˈɪnvəntəˌraɪz/ | v. | 盘点，列出清单 | To make a detailed list of things, especially assets or goods in stock. |  The museum decided to inventorize its entire collection of artifacts.  博物馆决定对其所有文物进行盘点。

The company inventorizes its warehouse stock every quarter. 该公司每个季度对其仓库库存进行盘点。| 近义词: catalogue, list, inventory, itemize | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: verb

Etymology: From inventory + -ize.  "Inventory" comes from the Old French "inventoire" meaning "a list of goods, a finding list," from the Late Latin "inventorium" meaning a list of things found.
