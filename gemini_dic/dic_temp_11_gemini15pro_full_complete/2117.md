adaptably:/əˈdæptəbli/
adverb
适应地，灵活地
in a way that shows the ability to change or adjust easily to different conditions
The company has adapted ably to the changing market.  这家公司已经灵活地适应了变化的市场。
Synonyms: flexibly, resiliently, resourcefully
Antonyms: inflexibly, rigidly, unyieldingly
词性: 副词
