heteropathic:/ˌhɛtərəˈpæθɪk/ | adj. |异种病的，由异种物质引起的|Caused by a different agent or originating outside the body; relating to or characterized by heteropathy.| Example: The doctor suspected a heteropathic reaction to the new medication. 医生怀疑是对新药的异种病反应。| Example: Heteropathic diseases are often difficult to diagnose. 异种病通常难以诊断。| 近义词: allopathic, allogenic | 反义词: homeopathic, idiopathic | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From hetero- (different, other) + -pathic (relating to suffering or disease).  Ultimately from Greek *héteros* (other, different) + *páthos* (suffering, disease).
