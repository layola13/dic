nonprovocativeness:/ˌnɑːnprəˌvɑːkətɪvnəs/| n.|无挑衅性；不刺激性|The nonprovocativeness of his speech helped to calm the angry crowd. 他的讲话没有煽动性，有助于平息愤怒的人群。|The diplomat's nonprovocativeness was key to the successful negotiations.  这位外交官的不挑衅态度是谈判成功的关键。|近义词:inoffensiveness, neutrality, peacefulness|反义词:provocativeness, aggressiveness, belligerence|词性:名词

词源:  From non- (not) + provocative (tending or serving to provoke) + -ness (forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  Ultimately from Latin provocare (to call forth, challenge).
