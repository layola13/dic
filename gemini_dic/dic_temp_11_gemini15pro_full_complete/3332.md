afford:/əˈfɔːrd/ | v. |提供; 给予; 买得起|to be able to pay for something; to provide something or allow something to happen | I can't afford a new car. 我买不起新车。|  This job affords me the opportunity to travel. 这份工作使我有机会旅行。|近义词：provide, supply, furnish, offer, give, manage, allow, permit|反义词：refuse, deny|词性:动词

词源:  From Old English *geforðian (unattested), from forð "forward, further, forth".  Originally meant "to carry out, accomplish, achieve," later "to supply, furnish," and finally "to have the means to buy."
