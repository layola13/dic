outspend:/ˌaʊtˈspend/| v. | 比...花费更多；在支出上超过 | to spend more than (someone or something) | The company outspent its rivals on advertising.  这家公司在广告上的花费超过了竞争对手。|  例句：The government outspent its budget this year. 政府今年的支出超过了预算。 | 近义词：outlay, surpass, exceed | 反义词：underspend | 词性：动词
