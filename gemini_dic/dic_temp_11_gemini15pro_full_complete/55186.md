dissenter:/dɪˈsɛntər/ | n. |持异议者，反对者|A person who disagrees with a particular opinion, especially one that is generally accepted.|例句：The dissenters were imprisoned for their beliefs. 这些持异议者因其信仰而被监禁。

例句：She was a vocal dissenter of the government's policies. 她是一位直言不讳地反对政府政策的人。|近义词：objector, protester, nonconformist, rebel, iconoclast|反义词：conformist, supporter, adherent|词性: noun
