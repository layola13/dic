corymb: /ˈkɔːrɪm/ (US), /ˈkɒrɪm/ (UK) | n. |伞房花序 | A flat-topped or convex inflorescence in which the individual flower stalks arise at different points on the main stem, but reach approximately the same level. | The elder bush displays a beautiful corymb of white flowers. 接骨木丛展示着美丽的白色伞房花序。|  The yarrow has a corymb of tiny white flowers.  西洋蓍草有一簇白色的小花伞房花序。 | synonyms: raceme, panicle | antonyms: single flower, spike | noun
