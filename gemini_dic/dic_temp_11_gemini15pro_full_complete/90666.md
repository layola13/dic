humbled:/ˈhʌmbəld/| adj.|谦逊的，虚心的|Feeling or showing a modest or low estimate of one's own importance.|Her award humbled her.  她的获奖让她感到谦卑。|
近义词:modest, unassuming, meek|反义词:proud, arrogant, haughty|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *humblen, from Old French humbler, from Latin humilis (“low, lowly, near the earth”), from humus (“ground, earth”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *(dʰ)ǵʰem- (“earth”).
