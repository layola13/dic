levy:/ˈlevi/ (US), /ˈlɛvi/ (UK)| n. vt.|征收，征税；税款；征兵|An act of collecting an amount of money, or the amount collected.|The government levied a new tax on gasoline. 政府对汽油征收了新的税。|synonyms: tax, impose, charge|antonyms: refund, remit|noun

levy:/ˈlevi/ (US), /ˈlɛvi/ (UK)| vt.|征收，征税；征兵|To impose or collect by legal authority.|The king levied troops for the war. 国王为了战争而征兵。|synonyms:  collect, exact, raise|antonyms: disburse, distribute|verb
