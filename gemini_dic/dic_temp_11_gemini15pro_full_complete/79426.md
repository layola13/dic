glistening:/ˈɡlɪsənɪŋ/ /ˈɡlɪsnɪŋ/| adj.|闪闪发光的，闪亮的|Shining with reflected light; sparkling.|The glistening dew on the grass. 草地上闪闪发光的露珠。|近义词：sparkling, shimmering, glittering, gleaming|反义词：dull, matte|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *glisninge, *glysnynge, present participle of glisnen, glisnien (“to glisten, shine”), from Old English glisnian (“to shine, glitter, glisten”), from Proto-Germanic *glisnōną (“to shine, glitter”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵleys- (“to shine”).  Cognate with Scots glissen (“to glisten”), West Frisian glinsterje (“to glitter”), Dutch glinsteren (“to glitter, sparkle”), German glimmen (“to glimmer, glow”), Danish glimte (“to glitter, gleam, flash”), Swedish glimma (“to glitter, gleam, shine”).
