neophyte:/ˈniːəˌfaɪt/ | n. |新手，初学者，新皈依者|A person who is new to a subject, skill, or belief. |例句：The neophytes were eager to learn the ropes.  这些新手渴望学习窍门。

例句：As a neophyte to the world of coding, I found the jargon confusing.  作为一个编程世界的新手，我发现这些术语很令人困惑。|近义词：beginner, novice, tyro, rookie, greenhorn |反义词：expert, veteran, professional, master |词性: noun
