confidingly:/kənˈfaɪdɪŋli/ | adv. | 信任地；亲密地；私下地 | In a way that shows trust or intimacy; privately. | She confidingly told him her secret. 她私下里把她的秘密告诉了他。|  He smiled confidingly at her. 他对她露出了信任的微笑。 | 近义词：intimately, privately, secretly, trustingly | 反义词：publicly, openly, suspiciously | 词性: 副词

词源:
From "confiding" + "-ly."  "Confiding" comes from the verb "confide," which originates from the Latin word "confīdere," meaning "to trust entirely."  "-ly" is a suffix that forms adverbs.
