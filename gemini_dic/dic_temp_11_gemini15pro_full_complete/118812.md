metamorphize:/ˌmɛtəˈmɔːrfaɪz/| v. |变形，变质，使变成|To change or cause to change completely in form or nature; transform.|The caterpillar will metamorphize into a butterfly. 毛毛虫会变成蝴蝶。|
The company has metamorphized from a small startup to a global corporation. 这家公司已经从一家小型初创企业转变为一家全球性公司。|近义词：transform, convert, transmute|反义词: remain, stay, stagnate|词性:动词

metamorphosis:/ˌmɛtəˈmɔːrfəsɪs/| n. |变形，变质|A change of the form or nature of a thing or person into a completely different one, by natural or supernatural means.|The metamorphosis of the tadpole into a frog is a fascinating process. 蝌蚪变成青蛙是一个迷人的过程。|
She underwent a complete metamorphosis, both physically and emotionally. 她经历了彻底的蜕变，包括身体上和情感上。|近义词：transformation, conversion, transmutation|反义词: stagnation, fixity|词性:名词


词源: From Greek metamórphōsis, from metamorphoun 'to transform,' from meta- 'change' + morphē 'form.'
