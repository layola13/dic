embezzle: /ɪmˈbezəl/ | v. | 盗用, 挪用 | To illegally take money that you are responsible for, usually from a company or organization. |  The accountant embezzled thousands of dollars from the company. 会计从公司盗用了数千美元。| synonyms: misappropriate, pilfer, defraud | antonyms: donate, contribute | verb

embezzle: /ɪmˈbezəl/ | v. | 盗用, 挪用 | To steal money that you have been trusted with. | The former CEO was charged with embezzling millions from the pension fund. 前首席执行官被控从养老基金中挪用数百万美元。| synonyms: steal, thieve, purloin | antonyms: reimburse, repay | verb
