altostrati: /ˌæltoʊˈstrɑːtiː/ /ˌaltoʊˈstreɪtiː/
| n. | 高层云 | A principal cloud type, occurring as a gray or bluish sheet or layer of uniform appearance, sometimes with a fibrous or striated aspect, and composed of water droplets, ice crystals, or both. It is thicker than cirrostratus but thinner than nimbostratus. | The altostrati clouds obscured the sun. 高层云遮住了太阳。| Synonym: cloud cover | Antonym: clear sky | Noun
