flotage:/ˈfloʊtɪdʒ/| n.|漂浮物，浮力，漂浮能力|The flotage of the logs allowed them to be transported down the river. 原木的浮力使它们能够沿着河流运输。|例句：

The flotage of the raft was barely enough to support the weight of the passengers.  木筏的浮力几乎不足以支撑乘客的重量。

The recent storm brought a lot of flotage to the shore. 最近的风暴给岸边带来了很多漂浮物。|近义词：buoyancy, floatability|反义词: sinkage, submersion|词性: noun

Etymology: From the verb "float" + "-age" (suffix forming nouns of action or result).  Ultimately from Old French "floter" and Latin "flutare," frequentative of "fluere" (to flow).
