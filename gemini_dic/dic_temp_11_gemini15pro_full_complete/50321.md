deplorate:/dɪˈplɔːr/ /dɪˈplorɪt/ | v. | 哀叹；悲悼；强烈反对 | To feel or express strong disapproval of (something). |  The president deplored the violence and promised to bring those responsible to justice. 总统强烈谴责了暴力行为，并承诺将肇事者绳之以法。

They deplored the loss of life. 他们对生命的逝去表示哀悼。 | 近义词：lament, bewail, condemn, decry | 反义词：approve, commend, praise | 词性: 动词

deplorable:/dɪˈplɔːrəbl/ /dɪˈplorəbəl/ | adj. | 令人深感遗憾的； deplorable | Deserving strong condemnation; completely unacceptable. | The working conditions in the factory were deplorable. 工厂里的工作条件非常糟糕。

The state of the roads is deplorable. 道路状况很差。| 近义词：appalling, dreadful, terrible, disgraceful | 反义词：admirable, commendable, excellent | 词性: 形容词
