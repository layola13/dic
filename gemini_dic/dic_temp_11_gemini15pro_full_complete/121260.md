misevaluated:/ˌmɪsɪˈvæljuˌeɪtɪd/
|adj.|被错误评估的，被低估的|Incorrectly judged or assessed the value or worth of something or someone.|The company's potential was misevaluated by investors, leading to a lower stock price. 该公司的潜力被投资者错误评估，导致股价下跌。|The teacher misevaluated the student's abilities. 老师错误地评估了学生的能力。|undervalued, underestimated, misjudged|overvalued, overestimated|adjective
