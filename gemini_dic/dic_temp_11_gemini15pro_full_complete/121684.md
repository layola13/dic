mispointing:/ˌmɪsˈpɔɪntɪŋ/
verb
错误地指向；指错方向
incorrectly directing or aiming something.
The compass was mispointing, leading them astray. 指南针指错了方向，使他们迷路了。
misdirecting, misguiding
correctly pointing, guiding
