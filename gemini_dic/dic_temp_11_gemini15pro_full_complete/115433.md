manto:/ˈmæntoʊ/ (US), /ˈmæntəʊ/ (UK)| n. |斗篷，披风；覆盖物；昆虫的鞘翅|A cloak or mantle, especially a loose sleeveless garment worn over other clothes. Also, the hard outer covering of some insects.|She wore a vibrant red manto over her dress. 她穿着一件鲜艳的红色斗篷套在连衣裙外面。 The beetle's manto shimmered in the sunlight. 甲虫的鞘翅在阳光下闪闪发光。|近义词：cloak, cape, mantle, shawl|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "undergarment" in some contexts)|noun
