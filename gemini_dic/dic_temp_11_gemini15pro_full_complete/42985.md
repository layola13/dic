covetous: /ˈkʌvɪtəs/ | adj. | 贪婪的，垂涎的 | Excessively desirous of wealth or possessions belonging to another; greedy.  Marked by or showing an inordinate desire for wealth or possessions or for another's possessions. |  He gave her a covetous glance. 他贪婪地看了她一眼。| synonyms: greedy, avaricious, grasping, acquisitive, rapacious, envious, jealous | antonyms: generous, charitable, altruistic, benevolent | adjective

covetousness: /ˈkʌvɪtəsnəs/ | n. | 贪婪，贪心 | The state of being covetous; inordinate desire for wealth or possessions, or for another's possessions. | Covetousness is one of the seven deadly sins. 贪婪是七宗罪之一。 | synonyms: greed, avarice, acquisitiveness, rapacity, envy, jealousy | antonyms: generosity, charity, altruism, benevolence | noun
