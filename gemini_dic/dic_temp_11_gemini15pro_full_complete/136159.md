nonregression:/ˌnɒn rɪˈɡrɛʃən/ /ˌnɑːn rɪˈɡrɛʃən/
noun
无退化，不退步
The absence of regression; maintenance of a previous level of performance or quality.
Software testing is crucial for nonregression to ensure new features don't break existing functionality. 软件测试对于无退化至关重要，以确保新功能不会破坏现有功能。
Regular exercise is important for nonregression of muscle strength after an injury.  受伤后定期锻炼对于肌肉力量的无退化很重要。
近义词: stability, maintenance,  continuation
反义词: regression, deterioration, decline
词性: n.
