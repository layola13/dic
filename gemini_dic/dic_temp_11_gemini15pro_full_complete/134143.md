nonimporting:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpɔːtɪŋ/ | adj. | 非进口的 | Not importing; that does not import.  Relating to a country, company, or industry that does not import goods. |  The country's nonimporting policy aimed to boost domestic production.  这个国家的非进口政策旨在促进国内生产。 |  domestic, local | importing, foreign | adjective
Etymology:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "importing" (present participle of "import," from Latin "importare" - to bring in).
