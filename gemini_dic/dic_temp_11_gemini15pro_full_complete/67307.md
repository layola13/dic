exsufflation:/ˌɛksʌˈfleɪʃən/
noun
呼气；吹出；吹气法
The act of blowing out air from the lungs, or the removal of something by blowing.
Exsufflation is an important part of playing wind instruments. 吹气是演奏管乐器的一个重要部分。
The archaeologist used a small brush and exsufflation to remove dust from the artifact. 考古学家用小刷子和吹气法清除文物上的灰尘。
synonyms:expiration, exhalation, blowing
antonyms:inspiration, inhalation, sucking
