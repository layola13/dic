jibed:/dʒaɪbd/| v.|嘲讽，与…不一致，转向|To make a sarcastic or mocking remark; to change direction abruptly, especially of a sailing vessel. |The comedian jibed at the politician's hypocrisy.  这位喜剧演员嘲讽了这位政客的虚伪。

The boat jibed suddenly, causing the boom to swing across the deck. 船突然转向，导致吊杆在甲板上摆动。|近义词：mocked, taunted, scoffed, veered, shifted|反义词:praised, complimented, steadied|词性:动词

PHRASE->

jibe at (嘲笑): He jibed at her nervousness. 他嘲笑她的紧张。
a jibe (嘲讽): He made a jibe about her weight. 他嘲笑了她的体重。

The boat jibed around.  船转向了。
