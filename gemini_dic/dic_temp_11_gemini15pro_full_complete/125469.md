multiplier:/ˈmʌltɪplaɪər/| n.|乘数；倍增器|A quantity by which a given number (the multiplicand) is multiplied.|The multiplier in the equation 2 x 4 = 8 is 4.|近义词：factor, coefficient|反义词:divisor, divider|noun
multiplier:/ˈmʌltɪplaɪər/| n.|乘数；倍增器|Something that increases something else by a large amount.|Tourism has acted as a multiplier, boosting the local economy.|近义词：amplifier, enhancer|反义词:reducer, diminisher|noun
