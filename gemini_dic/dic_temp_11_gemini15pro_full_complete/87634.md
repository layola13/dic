hewers:/ˈhjuːərz/| n.|伐木工；矿工；(比喻)从事艰苦体力劳动的人|People who cut wood or mine, or figuratively, those engaged in arduous physical labor. |The hewers of coal worked long hours in dangerous conditions. 煤矿工人在危险的条件下长时间工作。|近义词：laborers, workers, miners, woodcutters|反义词:managers, supervisors|noun
