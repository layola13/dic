acentric:/eɪˈsɛntrɪk/ | adj. |无中心的，离心的，古怪的|Lacking a center; not centered.  Also, deviating from the normal or conventional; eccentric.|The acentric fragment of the chromosome was lost during cell division.|近义词: eccentric, unconventional, irregular, off-center|反义词: centric, central, conventional, normal|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From a- (not, without) + centric (relating to a center). Ultimately from Greek kentron (center).
