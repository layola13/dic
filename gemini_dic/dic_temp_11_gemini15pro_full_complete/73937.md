forth:/fɔːrθ/ | adv. |向前，向外| forward in place or time; onward; out into view |例句：From that day forth, he never touched alcohol again. 从那天起，他再也没有碰过酒。|近义词：forward, onward, ahead, out |反义词：back, backward |词性:副词
forth:/fɔːrθ/ | adj. |向前的，外面的 (古语，罕用) | going out or leaving | 例句：The ship set forth on its maiden voyage. 这艘船开始了它的处女航。| 近义词：outgoing | 反义词：incoming, returning | 词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *forth, from Proto-Germanic *furtha- "forward," from PIE *pr-to- "forward, toward," from root *per- (1) "forward," hence "in front of, before."
