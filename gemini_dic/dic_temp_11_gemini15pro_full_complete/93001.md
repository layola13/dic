idiopathic:/ˌɪdiəˈpæθɪk/ | adj. | 特发性的，原因不明的 |  Relating to or denoting any disease or condition that arises spontaneously or for which the cause is unknown. |  The doctor diagnosed the patient with idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis. 医生诊断该病人患有特发性肺纤维化。|  Example: His idiopathic epilepsy started when he was a child. 他的特发性癫痫始于童年时期。 | Synonyms: essential, primary, spontaneous, cryptogenic | Antonyms: secondary, acquired | Word type: adjective
