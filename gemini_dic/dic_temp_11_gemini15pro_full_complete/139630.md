occlude:/əˈkluːd/ (US), /ɒˈkluːd/ (UK)| v.| 阻塞，闭塞；遮住，挡住 |To stop, close, or obstruct (an opening, orifice, or passage). To cover or shut off from view.|The thrombus occluded the artery. 血栓阻塞了动脉。|近义词：block, obstruct, close, cover, conceal|反义词:open, uncover, reveal|verb

occluded:/əˈkluːdɪd/ (US), /ɒˈkluːdɪd/ (UK)| adj.| 被阻塞的，闭塞的；被遮蔽的 |Closed, blocked, or obstructed. Covered or shut off from view.|The occluded artery caused a heart attack. 阻塞的动脉导致了心脏病发作。|近义词：blocked, obstructed, closed, covered, concealed|反义词:open, uncovered, revealed|adjective

occlusion:/əˈkluːʒən/ (US), /ɒˈkluːʒən/ (UK)| n.| 阻塞，闭塞；咬合；遮挡 |The act of occluding, or the state of being occluded. The act of closing or of being closed. The way the upper and lower teeth fit together when the jaws are closed.|The dentist checked the patient's occlusion. 牙医检查了病人的咬合。|近义词：blockage, obstruction, closure|反义词:opening|noun
