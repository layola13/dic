debriefer:/diːˈbriːfər/| n.|汇报人，事后分析员|A person who conducts a debriefing.  A debriefer helps individuals or groups process and understand their experiences after a significant event, often stressful or traumatic. |例句：The debriefer helped the soldiers process the trauma they experienced during combat.  汇报员帮助士兵们处理他们在战斗中经历的创伤。

例句：The debriefer asked the pilot a series of questions about the near-miss incident.  汇报员就此次险些发生的意外事故向飞行员提了一系列问题。|近义词：interpreter, analyst, facilitator|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
