cohosted:/koʊˈhoʊstɪd/| v.|共同主持|acted as a host with someone else; hosted jointly|例句：They cohosted a popular radio show. 他们共同主持了一个很受欢迎的广播节目。

例句：The event was cohosted by two local charities.  这次活动由两个当地慈善机构共同主办。|近义词：jointly hosted, presented together|反义词:solo hosted, hosted alone|词性:动词

词源: co- (prefix) + hosted (past participle of "host")
"co-" 源自拉丁语 "com-"，意为"一起，共同"。
"host" 源自古法语 "hoste"，最终源自拉丁语 "hospes"，意为"客人"或"主人"。
