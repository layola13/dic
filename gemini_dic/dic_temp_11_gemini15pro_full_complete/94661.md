impressment:/ɪmˈprɛsmənt/| n. |强征；强迫入伍；(尤指)强迫服役|The act of forcing someone into service, especially into the navy.|The impressment of sailors was a common practice in the 18th and 19th centuries.  18和19世纪，强征水手是一种常见的做法。|Synonyms: conscription, forced enlistment, draft|Antonyms: voluntary enlistment, discharge|noun
