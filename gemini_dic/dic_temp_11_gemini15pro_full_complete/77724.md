gemmiparous:/dʒɛˈmɪpərəs/
adjective
出芽繁殖的
Reproducing by budding; (of certain algae or fungi) putting forth buds.
Example:  The gemmiparous fungus quickly colonized the petri dish. 出芽繁殖的真菌迅速在培养皿中繁殖。

Example:  Gemmiparous reproduction is common in yeasts. 出芽繁殖在酵母菌中很常见。
Synonym: budding
Antonym: fissiparous (分裂生殖的)
word class: adjective
