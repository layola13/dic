buttes:/bjuːts/| n.|孤峰，孤山，平顶山 |An isolated hill with steep sides and a flat top, similar to but smaller than a mesa.|We drove past several impressive buttes on our road trip through Arizona. 我们在穿越亚利桑那州的公路旅行中经过了几座令人印象深刻的孤峰。|近义词：mesas, plateaus, hills|反义词: valleys, canyons|名词

词源: From French *butte*, from Old French *bote* (“mound, hillock”), from a Gaulish source akin to Middle Irish *bót* (“hut, tent”), from Proto-Celtic *budī- (“dwelling, hut”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰuH- (“to dwell, exist”).
