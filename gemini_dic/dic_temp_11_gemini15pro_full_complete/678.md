abscesses:/ˈæbsɪsiz/
noun
脓肿（abscess的复数形式）
A localized collection of pus surrounded by inflamed tissue.
The doctor drained the abscesses on his back. 医生排出他背上的脓肿。
近义词：boils, infections, sores, ulcers
反义词：health, wellness
