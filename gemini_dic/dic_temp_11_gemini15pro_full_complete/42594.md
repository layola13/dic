counterpressures:/ˈkaʊntərˌprɛʃərz/
noun
反压力；反制压力
Pressures exerted in opposition to other pressures.
The government faced counterpressures from the public to lower taxes. 政府面临来自公众要求降低税收的反压力。
Negotiations often involve a series of counterpressures and compromises.  谈判通常涉及一系列的反压力和妥协。
Synonyms: opposing pressures, countervailing forces, resistance, pushback
Antonyms: supporting pressures, assisting forces, cooperation
