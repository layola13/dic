kalmia: /ˈkælmɪə/ | n. | 山月桂，美洲月桂 | A genus of North American evergreen shrubs of the heath family, with clusters of showy pink, white, or purple flowers.  These plants are poisonous to livestock. |  The kalmia bushes were in full bloom, covered in delicate pink flowers.  山月桂盛开着，布满了娇嫩的粉红色花朵。 | synonyms: mountain laurel, lambkill, sheep laurel | antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a plant genus) | noun
