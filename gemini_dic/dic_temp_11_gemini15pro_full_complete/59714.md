eclecticist: /ɪˈklɛktɪsɪst/
n.
折衷主义者；博采众长者
Someone who selects or uses what seems best from various doctrines, methods, or styles.
She is an eclecticist in her musical tastes, enjoying everything from classical to hip-hop. 她对音乐的品味不拘一格，从古典到嘻哈，什么都喜欢。

Synonym: selector, synthesizer, syncretist
Antonym: purist, dogmatist
词性: 名词


eclectic: /ɪˈklɛktɪk/
adj.
折衷的；兼收并蓄的
Deriving ideas, style, or taste from a broad and diverse range of sources.
His house is filled with an eclectic mix of furniture and art. 他的房子里摆满了各种各样的家具和艺术品，兼收并蓄。

Synonym: diverse, varied, broad, wide-ranging
Antonym: narrow, limited, restricted, homogeneous
词性: 形容词


eclecticism: /ɪˈklɛktɪsɪzəm/
n.
折衷主义
The practice of deriving ideas, style, or taste from a broad and diverse range of sources.
Eclecticism in architecture can lead to interesting and unique buildings. 建筑中的折衷主义可以创造出有趣而独特的建筑物。

Synonym:  synthesis, syncretism, fusion
Antonym:  purism, orthodoxy
词性: 名词
