overpassionateness:/ˌoʊvərˈpæʃənɪtnəs/

overpassionate:/ˌoʊvərˈpæʃənɪt/
| adj. | 过于热情的，过于激情的 | Excessively passionate; too ardent or fervent. | Her overpassionate response surprised everyone. 她过于激动的反应让每个人都感到惊讶。 |  fervent, ardent, zealous | apathetic, indifferent | adjective


Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above, beyond") + "passionate" (from Old French passionat, from Late Latin passionatus "having suffered," from Latin passio "suffering, enduring," from past participle stem of pati "to suffer, endure") + "-ness" (Old English -nes(s), forming abstract nouns of quality or state).
