museo:/muˈzeɪoʊ/ (US), /mjuːˈzeɪəʊ/ (UK)| n. |博物馆|A building or institution dedicated to the acquisition, conservation, study, exhibition, and educational interpretation of objects having scientific, historical, or artistic value.| We visited the natural history museo. 我们参观了自然历史博物馆。|Example: The museo is hosting a special exhibit on ancient Egypt.  这家博物馆正在举办一个关于古埃及的特别展览。|Synonyms: museum, gallery|Antonyms: None|Noun
