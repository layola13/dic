glitch:/ɡlɪtʃ/| n.|小故障，失灵，小差错|A sudden, usually temporary malfunction or irregularity in a system, especially in electronics or computer programs.  A minor problem or setback.|The computer experienced a glitch and shut down. 电脑出现了故障，关机了。|Synonyms: bug, error, fault, malfunction, snag|Antonyms:  perfection, smooth operation|noun

glitch:/ɡlɪtʃ/| v.|发生小故障，出现小差错|(Of a machine or system) suffer a sudden, usually temporary malfunction or irregularity.|The system glitched and lost all the data. 系统出现故障，丢失了所有数据。|Synonyms: malfunction, fail, falter|Antonyms: function, work, operate|verb
