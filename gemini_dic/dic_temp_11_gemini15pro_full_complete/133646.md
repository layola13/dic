nonformative:/nɒnˈfɔːmətɪv/ | adj. | 非形成性的，无意义的 | Not contributing to the formation of words or other meaningful units; having no semantic content. |  The prefix "un-" in "unhappy" is formative, while the "h" in "hour" is nonformative. “unhappy”中的前缀“un-”是形成性的，而“hour”中的“h”是非形成性的。 | Synonyms: meaningless, insignificant, unproductive | Antonyms: formative, meaningful, productive | adjective
