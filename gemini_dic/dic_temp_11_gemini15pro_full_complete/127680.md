negated:/nɪˈɡeɪtɪd/
adjective
已否定的，被否定的
nullified or made ineffective
The impact of the new law was negated by a loophole. 新法律的影响因为一个漏洞而被否定了。
近义词:contradicted, denied, nullified, invalidated
反义词:affirmed, confirmed, validated
