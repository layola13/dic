lambasting:/læmˈbæstɪŋ/
verb
严厉批评，痛斥
criticizing harshly; censuring severely or angrily
例句：
The coach was lambasting the team for their poor performance. 教练严厉批评了球队的糟糕表现。

He was lambasted by the media for his controversial remarks. 他因其有争议的言论而遭到媒体的抨击。

近义词：
criticizing, censuring, berating, denouncing, reprimanding, castigating, excoriating
反义词：
praising, commending, lauding, applauding
词性
v.
