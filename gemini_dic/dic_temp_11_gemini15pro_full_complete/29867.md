causers:/ˈkɔːzərz/
noun
起因者；肇事者；造成…的人（或事物）
Those who pollute the environment are the causers of climate change. 那些污染环境的人是气候变化的起因。
The causers of the accident were identified by the police. 事故的肇事者被警方确认。
Synonyms: creators, instigators, origins, sources
Antonyms: results, effects, consequences
n.
