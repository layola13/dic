onion-like:/ˈʌnjənˌlaɪk/| adj.|像洋葱的，层层叠叠的|Resembling an onion in having many layers or in causing tears. |Cutting the onion-like plant made my eyes water. 切割这种类似洋葱的植物让我的眼睛流泪了。|multilayered, complex|simple, uniform|adjective
