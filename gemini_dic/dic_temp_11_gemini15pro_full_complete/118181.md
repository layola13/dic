mentioned:/ˈmenʃənd/ | adj. | 提到的，提及的 | Something that is mentioned is referred to briefly or casually. |  Example: The report mentioned several factors contributing to the decline.  报告提到了导致下降的几个因素。

Example:  As mentioned earlier, the project is due next week.  如前所述，该项目将于下周到期。| synonyms: cited, referred to, noted, specified, alluded to | antonyms: omitted, ignored, unmentioned | adjective
