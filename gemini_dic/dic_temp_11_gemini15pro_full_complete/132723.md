nondiversified:/ˌnɒndaɪˈvɜːsɪfaɪd/
adjective
未多元化的，不分散的
Not diversified; lacking variety or diversity, especially in investments.
Their portfolio was heavily weighted in real estate, a rather nondiversified approach. 他们的投资组合 heavily weighted in real estate，这是一个相当不分散的做法。
non-diversified:/ˌnɒndaɪˈvɜːsɪfaɪd/
adjective
未多元化的，不分散的
Not having investments spread among a variety of securities or asset classes.
The nondiversified nature of their business made them vulnerable to market fluctuations.  他们业务的不分散性质使他们容易受到市场波动的影响。
近义词:undiversified, concentrated, specialized
反义词:diversified, varied, broad
