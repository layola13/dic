indigently:/ɪnˈdɪdʒəntli/| adv.|贫困地，贫穷地|In a poor or needy manner; lacking the necessities of life.|He lived indigently, relying on charity for food and shelter. 他生活贫困，依靠慈善机构提供食物和住所。|例句：The family lived indigently after the father lost his job.  这个家庭在父亲失业后生活贫困。

例句：She dressed indigently, wearing worn-out clothes. 她衣着破旧，穿着破烂的衣服。|近义词：poorly, needily, destitution|反义词:richly, affluently|词性:副词
