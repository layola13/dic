niellist:/ˈniːlɪst/
n.|虚无主义者|A person who believes that life is meaningless and rejects all religious and moral principles.|例句：The nihilist argued that there was no inherent purpose to existence.  这个虚无主义者认为存在没有内在的目的。|例句：He became a nihilist after witnessing the horrors of war.  在目睹了战争的恐怖之后，他变成了一个虚无主义者。|近义词：pessimist, cynic|反义词:optimist, believer
词性: 名词
