dissolve:/dɪˈzɒlv/ (UK) /dɪˈzɑːlv/ (US)| v. n.|溶解，解散，消失|To cause a solid to become incorporated into a liquid so as to form a solution; to bring to an end; to fade away.|例句：The sugar dissolved quickly in the hot water. 糖在热水中很快溶解了。

例句：The parliament was dissolved after the scandal. 丑闻之后议会解散了。

近义词：melt, liquefy, disintegrate, disperse, break up|反义词： solidify, congeal, assemble, gather|词性：verb

dissolve:/dɪˈzɒlv/ (UK) /dɪˈzɑːlv/ (US)| n.|溶解，溶解的过程|The process of dissolving; the act of bringing something to an end.|例句：The dissolve of the company took several months.  公司解散花了几个月的时间。

例句：The slow dissolve of the image created a dreamlike effect.  图像的缓慢溶解营造了一种梦幻般的效果。

近义词：disintegration, dissolution|反义词： solidification, coagulation|词性：noun
