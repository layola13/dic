fish-out:/ˈfɪʃ aʊt/ | phrasal verb | 捞出；找出；掏出 | To remove something from a place where it is hidden or difficult to reach.  It can also mean to find something after searching for it. | The police fished the body out of the river. 警察把尸体从河里捞了出来。

The librarian fished out an old book from the dusty shelf. 图书管理员从满是灰尘的书架上找出一本旧书。 | 近义词: retrieve, extract, pull out, recover, locate, find | 反义词: insert, put in, hide | verb phrase

fish-out:/ˈfɪʃ aʊt/ | noun | 捞出物；意外的发现 | Something that is fished out, often unexpectedly. | The fish-out from the old trunk turned out to be a valuable antique. 从旧箱子里捞出来的东西竟然是一件珍贵的古董。 | 近义词: find, discovery | 反义词: N/A | noun
