amanitin:/əˈmænɪtɪn/ | n. |鹅膏蕈碱|A cyclic peptide that is a potent inhibitor of RNA polymerase II, found in several poisonous mushrooms, especially of the genus *Amanita*.  It is responsible for the majority of fatal mushroom poisonings. |Example 1:  Amanitin is a deadly toxin found in death cap mushrooms.  鹅膏蕈碱是一种致命的毒素，存在于死帽蕈中。

Example 2:  The symptoms of amanitin poisoning can be delayed for several hours.  鹅膏蕈碱中毒的症状可能会延迟几个小时出现。|Synonym: amatoxin (a broader category which includes amanitin)|Antonym: None (There isn't a direct opposite for a specific toxin)|Noun
