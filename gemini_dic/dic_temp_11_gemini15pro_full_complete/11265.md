arciform:/ˈɑːrsɪˌfɔːrm/
adjective
弓形的，拱形的
Having the shape of a bow or arch.
The bridge had elegant, arciform supports. 这座桥有优雅的弓形支撑。
近义词: arched, bow-shaped, curved
反义词: straight, linear
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *arciformis*, from arcus ("arch, bow") + -formis ("-form").
