ETYMOLOGY->
From distinct + -ness.  Distinct traces back to Latin *distinctus, past participle of distinguere "to separate, distinguish," from dis- "apart" + -stinguere "to prick, extinguish."  The suffix -ness forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
