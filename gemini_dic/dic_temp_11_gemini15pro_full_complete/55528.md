distributees:/ˌdɪstrɪbjuːˈtiːz/
n.
受益人，分配对象
Persons who receive something that is distributed.
The distributees of the estate will receive their share of the inheritance next month.  遗产的受益人下个月将收到他们的继承份额。
synonyms: recipients, beneficiaries, heirs
antonyms: distributors, donors, grantors
noun
