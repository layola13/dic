deviceless:/dɪˈvaɪsləs/
adjective
无设备的；无需设备的
Without the need for a physical device; typically referring to accessing services or performing tasks through the cloud or other network-based solutions.
Many companies are now offering deviceless access to their services, allowing users to work from anywhere. 许多公司现在提供无需设备的服务访问，允许用户在任何地方工作。

Deviceless authentication is becoming increasingly popular. 无设备身份验证正变得越来越流行。

Near Synonyms: device-free, cloud-based, virtual, wireless
Antonyms: device-dependent, hardware-based, physical
