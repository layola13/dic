gabbier:/ˈɡæbiər/| adj.|更健谈的，更爱说话的|Comparative form of "gabby," meaning more talkative or loquacious.  It describes someone who talks more than someone else or more than they usually do.|She's much gabbier after a couple of glasses of wine. 她喝了几杯葡萄酒后变得健谈多了。|He was gabbier than usual at the party.  他在聚会上比平时更健谈。|近义词：more talkative, more loquacious, chattier|反义词：quieter, less talkative, more reserved|词性:形容词

gabby:/ˈɡæbi/| adj.|健谈的，爱说话的|Informal adjective meaning talkative or loquacious. It describes someone who talks a lot, often about unimportant things.|My aunt is so gabby, she can talk for hours. 我的阿姨很健谈，她可以连续说上几个小时。|He's a gabby fellow, always ready with a story. 他是个健谈的人，总是准备好讲故事。|近义词：talkative, loquacious, chatty|反义词：quiet, reserved, taciturn|词性:形容词
