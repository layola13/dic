bride:/braɪd/
n.
新娘
A woman who is about to be married or has recently been married.
The bride looked radiant in her white gown.  新娘穿着白色礼服容光焕发。
The bride and groom exchanged vows. 新郎新娘交换了誓言。
近义词:newlywed, wife
反义词:groom
词性:名词
