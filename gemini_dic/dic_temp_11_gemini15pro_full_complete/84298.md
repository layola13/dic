half-vexed:/ˌhæf ˈvekst/
adjective
略感恼怒的，有点生气的
Slightly annoyed; irritated.  A blend of "half" and "vexed."
She gave him a half-vexed, half-amused look. 她给了他一个略带恼怒又略带好笑的眼神。
He felt half-vexed at being interrupted. 他被打断感到有点恼火。
近义词: slightly annoyed, irritated, a little upset
反义词: pleased, happy, delighted
词性: adjective
