adhering:/ədˈhɪərɪŋ/
verb (present participle of adhere)|粘附，坚持，遵守|sticking firmly to something; following a particular rule, belief, or idea|例句：The tiles weren't adhering to the wall properly.  瓷砖没有好好地粘在墙上。|近义词：sticking, clinging, complying, conforming|反义词：detaching, separating, disobeying, violating|v.

adhering:/ədˈhɪərɪŋ/
adjective|粘附的，坚持的|sticking firmly to something|例句：The adhering label was difficult to remove.  粘附的标签很难去除。|近义词：sticky, clinging, attached|反义词：detached, separate|adj.
