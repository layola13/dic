cantankerousness:/ˌkæntæŋˈkərəsnəs/| n.|暴躁，坏脾气|Bad-tempered or argumentative; quarrelsome.|The old man's cantankerousness was legendary in the village.  这个老人的坏脾气在这个村子里是出了名的。|Synonyms: irritability, grumpiness, irascibility, crossness|Antonyms: good humor, amiability, pleasantness|noun

cantankerous:/kænˈtæŋkərəs/| adj.|脾气坏的，爱争吵的|Bad-tempered, argumentative, and uncooperative.|He was a cantankerous old man who yelled at children for walking on his lawn. 他是一个脾气暴躁的老人，会对着在他草坪上行走的的孩子大喊大叫。|Synonyms: bad-tempered, grumpy, irascible, cross|Antonyms: good-natured, amiable, pleasant|adjective
