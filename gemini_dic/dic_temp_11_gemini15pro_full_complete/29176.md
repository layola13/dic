cashed:/kæʃt/| v.|兑现（支票）；支付；变卖| To have exchanged a check or other negotiable instrument for cash.  To have received payment. To have sold or disposed of something, often for quick profit.| I cashed my paycheck at the bank. 我在银行兑现了我的薪水支票。| Synonyms: redeemed, encashed, liquidated, sold | Antonyms: deposited, invested, saved | verb

cashed:/kæʃt/| adj. |(非正式)身无分文的；破产的| Having no money. | I'm completely cashed after that trip. 那次旅行之后我身无分文了。| Synonyms: broke, penniless, insolvent | Antonyms: wealthy, rich, affluent | adjective
