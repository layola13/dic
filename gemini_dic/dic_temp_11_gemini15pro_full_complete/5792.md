amaranth:/ˈæmərænθ/ (US), /ˈæmərænθ/ (UK)| n. adj.|苋菜，不凋花，紫红色|A flowering plant of the genus Amaranthus, which has dense plumes of tiny flowers and brightly colored foliage. Also, a purplish-red dye made from the flowers of amaranth plants, or a color resembling this dye.|The ancient Greeks believed that amaranth had special properties and used it in wreaths to decorate images of their gods.  古希腊人认为苋菜具有特殊的属性，并用它做成花环来装饰他们神明的形象。

Amaranth is a nutritious grain that is gaining popularity. 苋菜是一种营养丰富的谷物，越来越受欢迎。|近义词：pigweed (for the plant), magenta, fuchsia (for the color)|反义词:(No direct antonym for the plant. For the color, perhaps green or pale.)|词性:noun, adjective
