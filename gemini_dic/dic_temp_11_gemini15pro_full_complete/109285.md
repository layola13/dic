lemons:/ˈlɛmənz/ | n. | 柠檬 |Oval yellow citrus fruit with acidic juice and pulpy flesh, used in cooking and drinks. |  She added the juice of two lemons to the cake batter. 她往蛋糕糊里加了两个柠檬的汁。

Lemons are rich in vitamin C. 柠檬富含维生素C。| 近义词: citrus fruit | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific fruit) | 词性: noun
