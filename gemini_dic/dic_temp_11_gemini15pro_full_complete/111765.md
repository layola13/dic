lodger:/ˈlɒdʒər/ (UK), /ˈlɑːdʒər/ (US)| n. |房客；寄宿者|A person who rents a room in another person's house.|例句：The lodger paid his rent weekly.  房客每周支付租金。

例句：She had a lodger staying in her spare room. 她有一个房客住在她的空房间里。

近义词：tenant, boarder, roomer|反义词:landlord, landlady|词性:noun
