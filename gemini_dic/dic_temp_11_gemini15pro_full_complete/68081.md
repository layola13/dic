fade-away:/ˈfeɪd əˈweɪ/| n. adj.|后仰投篮，逐渐消失的|A jump shot in basketball where the shooter jumps backward, away from the defender; something that gradually disappears or diminishes.|例句：His fade-away jumper is unstoppable. 他的后仰跳投无人能挡。|近义词：fade, disappear, diminish|反义词:intensify, strengthen|noun, adjective

fade-away:/ˈfeɪd əˈweɪ/| n. adj.|逐渐消失, 衰退|The act of gradually disappearing or diminishing; something that is fading.|例句：The memory of that summer was a slow fade-away.  那个夏天的记忆慢慢消退了。|近义词：decline, wane, ebb|反义词:increase, growth|noun, adjective
