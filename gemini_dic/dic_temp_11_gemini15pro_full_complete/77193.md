gassed:/ɡæst/ | adj. | 兴奋的；激动的；喝醉的；被毒气毒死的 |  Filled with enthusiasm or excitement; intoxicated; killed by poison gas. |  例句1：I'm absolutely gassed about the concert tonight! 我对今晚的音乐会非常兴奋！ | 例句2：He was so gassed he could barely stand. 他醉得几乎站不住了。| 近义词：excited, thrilled, elated, intoxicated, drunk | 反义词：bored, unenthusiastic, sober | 词性:形容词

gassed:/ɡæst/ | adj. |  被毒气毒死的 | Killed by poison gas.  | 例句1：Thousands were gassed during the war.  战争期间成千上万的人被毒气毒死。| 例句2：They found gassed canaries in the mine. 他们在矿井里发现了被毒气毒死的金丝雀。| 近义词：poisoned | 反义词： (无直接反义词) | 词性:形容词


词源: From *gas* + *-ed*. Originally referring to being killed by poison gas, the meaning extended to being intoxicated and then, more recently, to being excited or thrilled.
