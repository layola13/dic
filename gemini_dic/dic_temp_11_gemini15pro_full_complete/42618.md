counterreformer:/ˌkaʊntər rɪˈfɔːrmər/
n.
反改革者，反对改革的人
A person who opposes reform, especially religious reform.
The Council of Trent was a gathering of Catholic counterreformers.  特伦特会议是天主教反改革者的集会。
synonyms:  reactionary, conservative, traditionalist
antonyms: reformer, progressive, radical
noun
