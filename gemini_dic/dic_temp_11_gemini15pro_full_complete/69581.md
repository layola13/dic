felspar:/ˈfɛlspɑːr/ (US) /ˈfɛlspɑː/ (UK)| n. |长石|A group of crystalline minerals that consist of aluminosilicates of potassium, sodium, and calcium and that constitute the most abundant group of minerals in the earth's crust.|Feldspar is a common component of many rocks. 长石是许多岩石的常见成分。|Synonyms: albite, anorthite, orthoclase|Antonyms: None|Noun
