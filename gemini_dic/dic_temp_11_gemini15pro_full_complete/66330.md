excruciate: /ɪkˈskruːʃieɪt/ /ɛkˈskruːʃiˌeɪt/ | vt. | 使极度痛苦，折磨 | To inflict severe pain or agony upon, especially mental or emotional; to torture. | The loss of his child excruciated him. 失去孩子使他痛苦万分。|  The constant noise excruciated me.  持续不断的噪音折磨着我。 | synonyms: torment, torture, agonize | antonyms: soothe, comfort | verb
