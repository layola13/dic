deallocating:/diːˈæləkeɪtɪŋ/
verb
释放，解除分配
The system is deallocating memory that is no longer needed. 系统正在释放不再需要的内存。
The program is deallocating the resources. 程序正在释放资源。
present participle of deallocate
近义词: releasing, freeing
反义词: allocating, assigning
v.
