overshare:/ˌoʊvərˈʃɛr/ | v. | 过度分享 | To share too much personal information, especially on social media. |  She tends to overshare on social media, posting details about her relationships and health. 她倾向于在社交媒体上过度分享，发布关于她的人际关系和健康的细节。

| Example: He overshared about his medical problems with his coworkers. 他向他的同事过度分享了他的医疗问题。| Synonym: divulge, reveal excessively | Antonym: withhold, conceal | Verb

overshare:/ˈoʊvərʃɛr/ | n. | 过度分享 | The act of sharing too much personal information. |  Her constant overshare on Facebook is starting to annoy her friends.  她在脸书上不断的过度分享开始惹恼她的朋友们。

| Example:  Social media can encourage overshare. 社交媒体可能会鼓励过度分享。| Synonym: excessive disclosure, TMI (too much information) | Antonym: discretion, privacy | Noun
