displeasure:/dɪsˈplɛʒə(r)/
n.
不悦，不快，生气
A feeling of annoyance and disapproval.
She expressed her displeasure at the decision. 她对这个决定表示不满。
annoyance, dissatisfaction, disapproval, resentment
pleasure, satisfaction, delight, happiness
noun
