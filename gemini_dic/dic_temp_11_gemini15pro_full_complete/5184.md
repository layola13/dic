all fours:/ɔːl ˈfɔːrz/
|n.| 四肢着地；爬行；（牌戏中的）全套四张同点牌|on all fours: on hands and knees, like an animal.  The baby was crawling on all fours. 婴儿四肢着地爬行。/  (in card games) all four cards of the same rank. He won the game with all fours. 他用全套四张同点牌赢了比赛。|The toddler was crawling on all fours across the living room floor.  这个小孩四肢着地爬过客厅地板。|crawling, creeping|standing, upright|noun
