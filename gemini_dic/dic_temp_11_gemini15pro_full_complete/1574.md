achlamydeous:/ˌeɪkləˈmɪdiəs/
adjective
无被的；裸露的；无花被的
Lacking a perianth; having neither calyx nor corolla.  Referring to a flower that lacks both sepals and petals.
The achlamydeous flowers of the willow tree are inconspicuous. 柳树无被的花朵不显眼。
The plant's achlamydeous flowers make it difficult to classify. 这种植物无花被的花朵使其难以分类。

近义词:naked, aperianthous
反义词:chlamydeous, petaloid
词性:adjective
