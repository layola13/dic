indorsor:/ɪnˈdɔːrsər/ | n. | 背书人；签署人 |  A person who indorses a check or other negotiable instrument.  The indorser signs the back of the check, thereby transferring ownership and guaranteeing payment. | Example: The indorsor of the check was the original payee. 支票的背书人是最初的收款人。

Example:  The bank required the indorsor's signature to be verified. 银行要求核实背书人的签名。 | 近义词: signer, endorser | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From Anglo-French *endoser, from Old French endosser "to put on one's back," from en- "on" + dos "back." The spelling with i- reflects the influence of Latin in-.
