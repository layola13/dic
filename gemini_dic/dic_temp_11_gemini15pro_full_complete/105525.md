kiloton:/ˈkɪlətʌn/| n. |千吨；(尤指核武器爆炸力相当于)一千吨TNT炸药|A kiloton is a unit of explosive power equivalent to 1,000 tons of TNT.|The bomb had a yield of 50 kilotons. 这颗炸弹的爆炸当量为5万吨。|近义词：megaton |反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑millionton)|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From kilo- + ton.  "Kilo-" is a combining form representing "thousand" and derived from the Greek khilioi. "Ton" refers to a unit of weight equal to 2,000 pounds.
