degreasers:/diːˈɡriːzərz/
noun
除油剂; 脱脂剂
A substance used to remove grease and oil.
The mechanic used a powerful degreaser to clean the engine. 机械师用强力除油剂清洁发动机。
synonyms: cleaner, solvent, detergent
antonyms: lubricant, oil
n.
