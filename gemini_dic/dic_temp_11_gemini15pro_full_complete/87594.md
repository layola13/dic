heterostylism: /ˌhetərəˈstaɪlɪzəm/
noun
异型花柱
The condition of having flowers of different forms, especially ones having styles of different lengths relative to the stamens, to promote outcrossing.
Primroses exhibit heterostylism, with some plants having long styles and short stamens, and others having short styles and long stamens.  报春花表现出异型花柱，一些植物花柱长而雄蕊短，而另一些植物花柱短而雄蕊长。
Heterostylism is a common mechanism to prevent self-pollination. 异型花柱是防止自花授粉的常见机制。
synonym: heterostyly
antonym: homostyly
n.
