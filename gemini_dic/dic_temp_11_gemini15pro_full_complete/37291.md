commandery:/kəˈmɑːndəri/
n.
司令部；指挥区；骑士团管区；（尤指中国古代的）军区
a military unit or region under the control of a commander, especially in ancient China; a local administrative district of the Knights Hospitaller or Knights Templar.
The Knights Templar established commanderies throughout Europe and the Holy Land. 圣殿骑士团在欧洲和圣地建立了指挥区。
The ancient Chinese commandery system played a crucial role in maintaining imperial control. 中国古代的军区制度在维护皇权方面发挥了至关重要的作用。
synonym: headquarters, district, prefecture, province
antonym:  (No direct antonym, but consider terms like "branch," "outpost," or "dependency," which imply subordinate status.)
词性: noun
