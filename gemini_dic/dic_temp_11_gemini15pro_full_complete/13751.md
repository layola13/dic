auctioneers:/ˌɔːkʃəˈnɪərz/
noun
拍卖师
A person who conducts auctions.
The auctioneers hammered down the final bid. 拍卖师一锤定音，最终出价成交。
The auctioneers described the item in detail. 拍卖师详细描述了这件物品。

Synonyms:  seller, broker, agent
Antonyms: buyer, bidder, purchaser
n.
