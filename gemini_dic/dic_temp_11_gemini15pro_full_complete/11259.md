archway:/ˈɑːrtʃweɪ/
n.
拱门，拱道
a passage or entrance with an arched roof
We walked through the stone archway into the courtyard. 我们穿过石拱门进入庭院。

近义词: arch, vault, passage
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun
