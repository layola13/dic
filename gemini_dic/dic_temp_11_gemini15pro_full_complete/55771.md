diversifying:/daɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪɪŋ/
|v.|多样化，使多样化|The company is diversifying its investments to reduce risk.  这家公司正在使其投资多样化以降低风险。|例句：They are diversifying their product range to attract new customers.  他们正在使产品范围多样化以吸引新客户。|近义词：varying, expanding, broadening, branching out|反义词：specializing, focusing, narrowing, concentrating|词性: 动词(present participle of diversify)

diversify:/daɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪ/
|v.|多样化，使多样化|Farmers are being encouraged to diversify their crops. 鼓励农民使其作物多样化。|例句：The museum is trying to diversify its exhibitions to appeal to a wider audience.  博物馆正试图使其展览多样化，以吸引更广泛的观众。|近义词：vary, expand, broaden, branch out|反义词：specialize, focus, narrow, concentrate|词性: 动词

Etymology: From Late Latin *diversificare, from Latin diversus (“diverse”) + -ficare (from facere (“to make”)).
