firkin:/ˈfɜːrkɪn/| n.|小桶，弗金（旧的容量单位）|A firkin is a small cask, typically used for liquids like beer or ale, and historically represented a specific unit of volume. |例句：The pub offered a special on a firkin of their best bitter.  这家酒吧特价出售一小桶他们最好的苦啤酒。

例句：He inherited a firkin of aged brandy from his grandfather. 他从祖父那里继承了一小桶陈年白兰地。 |近义词：cask, keg, barrel|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps tank for a very large container) |词性: noun
