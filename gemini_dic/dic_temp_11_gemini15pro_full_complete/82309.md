grouselike:/ˈɡraʊslaɪk/| adj.|类似松鸡的，松鸡似的|Resembling or characteristic of a grouse, a type of game bird.|The bird had a grouselike appearance with its mottled brown feathers.  这只鸟有着斑驳的棕色羽毛，看起来像松鸡。|Synonyms:  grouse-like, like a grouse|Antonyms: unlike a grouse|adjective
