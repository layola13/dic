orthospermous:/ˌɔːrθəˈspɜːrməs/
adjective
直立种子的
Having straight seeds; applied to ovules which are erect within the ovary.
The orthospermous arrangement of the seeds is a key characteristic of this plant family.  这种植物家族的一个关键特征是种子的直立排列。
The botanist identified the plant as orthospermous due to its upright ovules.  植物学家根据其直立的胚珠将该植物鉴定为直立种子的。
近义词:atropous, erect
反义词:anatropous
词性:adjective
Etymology: From Greek *orthos* (straight, upright) + *sperma* (seed).
