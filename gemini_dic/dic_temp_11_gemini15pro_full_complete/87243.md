hereto:/ˌhɪərˈtuː/| adv.| hitherto; up to this time; until now | up to this time; until the present| The details heretofore unavailable are now accessible online.  以前无法获得的详细信息现在可以在线访问。| 近义词：hitherto, thus far, previously, formerly| 反义词：hereafter, subsequently, henceforth| 词性: 副词

英文解析：Hereto is an adverb meaning "up to this point in time" or "until now." It is often used in formal or legal contexts.

例句：
The information hereto appended is confidential. 附加在此的信息是机密的。
The parties hereto agree to the following terms.  双方同意以下条款。
