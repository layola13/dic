kibe:/ˈkiːbeɪ/
noun
烤肉串（通常指巴西烤肉）; 碎肉馅饼
A kibe is a popular Brazilian snack food, consisting of seasoned ground meat (often beef or lamb), bulgur wheat, and various spices, often shaped into a torpedo-like form and fried or baked.
Example: I ordered a delicious kibe from the Brazilian restaurant. 我从巴西餐厅点了一份美味的烤肉串。

Example:  She made homemade kibes for the party. 她为聚会做了自制的碎肉馅饼。
Synonym: kibbeh, quibe
Antonym: (No direct antonym for a food item)
词性: 名词
