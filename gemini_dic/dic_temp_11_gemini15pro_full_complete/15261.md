backheel:/ˈbækˌhiːl/| n. v.|后脚跟，用后脚跟着球|A backheel is the part of the human foot where the heel is located.  As a verb, it refers to kicking a ball using the heel.|He scored with a brilliant backheel. 他用一个精彩的后脚跟射门得分。|Synonyms: heel flick, flick|Antonyms: (For the noun) toe, forefoot; (For the verb, no direct antonym, perhaps 'front kick')|noun, verb
