milometer:/maɪˈlɒmɪtər/ /maɪˈlɑːmɪtər/
noun
里程表
An instrument in a vehicle that measures the distance travelled in miles.
The milometer showed that we had travelled over 500 miles. 里程表显示我们已经行驶了超过500英里。
The car's milometer read 10,000 miles. 这辆车的里程表读数为10,000英里。
