cambro:/ˈkæmbroʊ/
noun
Cambro
A brand name for a type of durable, food-safe plastic storage containers, often used in commercial kitchens and catering.
Cambro containers are used to store ingredients in the refrigerator.  Cambro容器用于在冰箱中储存食材。

近义词: food storage container, plastic container
反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "disposable container")
词性: n.
