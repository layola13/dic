greedinesses: /ˈɡriːdinɪsɪz/
|n.|贪婪，贪心；贪吃|The greedinesses of the corporate executives led to the downfall of the company. 公司高管们的贪婪导致了公司的垮台。|Extreme desire for more than one needs or deserves, excessive acquisitiveness.|His greedinesses knew no bounds. 他的贪婪无止境。|近义词: avarice, cupidity, rapacity|反义词: generosity, altruism|词性: 名词(plural of greediness)
词源: From greediness + -es (plural suffix).  Greediness itself comes from greedy (Old English grǣdig "greedy, hungry, gluttonous") + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).
