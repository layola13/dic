cornea:/ˈkɔːrniə/ (US) /ˈkɔːniə/ (UK)| n.|角膜|The transparent front part of the eye that covers the iris, pupil, and anterior chamber. It refracts light and helps the eye to focus.|Example 1:  The doctor examined his cornea for scratches. 医生检查了他的角膜是否有划痕。

Example 2:  A corneal transplant can restore vision in some cases. 角膜移植在某些情况下可以恢复视力。|Synonym:  No exact synonym, but related terms include "lens" and "ocular surface".|Antonym: No antonym.|Noun
