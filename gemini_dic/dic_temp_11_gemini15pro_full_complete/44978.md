crystallise:/ˈkrɪstəˌlaɪz/ /ˈkrɪstəˌlaɪz/| v. | 结晶；使明确；使具体化 |  to cause something to form crystals or become definite | The events of that day crystallized his determination to change careers. 那天的事件使他下定决心改变职业。| 近义词: solidify, concretize, materialize | 反义词: dissolve, melt | 词性: 动词

crystallised:/ˈkrɪstəˌlaɪzd/ /ˈkrɪstəˌlaɪzd/| adj. | 结晶的；明确的；具体的 |  having become fixed and definite in form |  His vague ideas eventually crystallized into a concrete plan. 他模糊的想法最终形成了一个具体的计划。 | 近义词: definite, concrete, fixed | 反义词: vague, amorphous, fluid | 词性: 形容词

词源: From French *cristalliser*, from cristal ("crystal") + -iser. Ultimately from Ancient Greek κρύσταλλος (krústallos).
