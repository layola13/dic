lose:/luːz/ (US) /luːz/ (UK)| v.| 丢失，失去，输掉 | To be deprived of or cease to have or retain (something). | I lost my keys yesterday. 我昨天丢了钥匙。| near synonyms: misplace, forfeit, be defeated by | antonyms: find, gain, win | verb

lose:/luːz/ (US) /luːz/ (UK)| n.| 损失，失败 | The act or an instance of losing. (Less common as a noun) |  His team suffered a big lose. 他的队伍遭遇了一场大败。(不太常用) | near synonyms: defeat, failure | antonyms: victory, win, gain | noun
