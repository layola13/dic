belittle:/bɪˈlɪtl/| vt.|轻视，贬低|to make someone or something seem unimportant|He belittled her achievements, making her feel insignificant. 他贬低她的成就，让她觉得自己微不足道。|近义词：demean, disparage, denigrate|反义词：praise, commend, extol|verb
