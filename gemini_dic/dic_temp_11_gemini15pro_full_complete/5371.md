alluvium:/əˈluːviəm/ | n. | 冲积层；冲积土 |  Deposits of clay, silt, sand, and gravel left by flowing streams in a river valley or delta, typically producing fertile soil. | The fertile alluvium of the Nile delta has supported agriculture for millennia. 尼罗河三角洲肥沃的冲积土养育了数千年的农业。| 近义词: sediment, deposit, silt | 反义词: bedrock | 词性: 名词

alluvia:/əˈluːviə/ | n. | 冲积层；冲积土 | The plural form of alluvium.  | The farmer's fields were enriched by the alluvia deposited by the river.  这片农田因河流沉积的冲积土而肥沃。 | 近义词: sediments, deposits | 反义词: bedrock | 词性: 名词
