koolslaa:/ˈkuːl slɑː/ | n. | 凉鞋; 拖鞋 | A type of footwear, typically open-toed and backless, designed for casual wear, often in warm weather. | Example: She wore her koolslaas to the beach. 她穿着凉鞋去了海滩。| Synonyms: sandals, flip-flops, thongs | Antonyms: boots, shoes | noun

Etymology:  "Koolslaas" appears to be a misspelling or a variation of the Afrikaans word "koelskoene," which literally translates to "cool shoes."  "Koel" means cool, and "skoene" means shoes.  It likely entered South African English as a colloquial term for sandals or flip-flops.
