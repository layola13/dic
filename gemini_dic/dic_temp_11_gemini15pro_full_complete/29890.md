cauterant:/ˈkɔːtərənt/| n. adj.|烧灼剂，腐蚀剂；烧灼的，腐蚀性的|A substance that burns or sears tissue, typically to stop bleeding or prevent infection.  It can also be used as an adjective to describe something that has this effect.|The doctor used a cauterant to stop the bleeding. 医生用烧灼剂来止血。|近义词：caustic, escharotic|反义词: demulcent, emollient|词性: noun, adjective
cauterize:/ˈkɔːtəraɪz/| vt.|烧灼，腐蚀|To burn or sear tissue with a cauterant.|The wound was cauterized to prevent infection.  伤口被烧灼以防止感染。|近义词：sear, burn|反义词: heal, soothe|词性: verb
