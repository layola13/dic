banalized:/bəˈnəlaɪzd/ |adj.| 平庸的，陈腐的，老套的 |Made commonplace or trite; lacking originality or freshness.  | The plot of the movie was so banalized, I knew exactly how it would end.  这部电影的情节太老套了，我完全知道它将如何结束。| The once shocking images of violence have become banalized by overuse in the media.  曾经令人震惊的暴力画面因媒体的过度使用而变得平庸。 | Synonyms: trite, clichéd, hackneyed, commonplace, stale, stereotyped | Antonyms: original, fresh, innovative, novel, unique | Word Family: banal (adj.), banality (n.), banalize (v.)
