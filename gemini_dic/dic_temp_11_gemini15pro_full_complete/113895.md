madreporiform:/ˌmædrɪˈpɔːrɪfɔːrm/
| adj.| madreporic; 筛板状的，似 madrepora 的|Resembling a madrepore; having a structure similar to a madrepore, especially perforated with numerous small holes.|The starfish's madreporiform plate is a crucial part of its water vascular system.|Synonym: perforated, porous|Antonym: smooth, solid|adjective


madreporite:/ˌmædrɪˈpɔːraɪt/
| n.| madreporic plate; 筛板|A perforated plate, part of the water vascular system of echinoderms, through which water is drawn.|The madreporite is located on the aboral surface of most starfish.|Synonym: sieve plate|Antonym: N/A|noun


词源:

From French *madrépore*, from Italian *madrepora* "coral", literally "mother-pore", from Latin *mater* "mother" + *porus* "pore, passage".  The "-form" suffix comes from Latin *-formis*, meaning "having the form of".
