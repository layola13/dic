logarithmic: /ˌlɒɡəˈrɪðmɪk/ (US) /ˌlɒɡəˈrɪðmɪk/ (UK)| adj.| 对数的; 对数尺度的|Of, relating to, or expressed in terms of logarithms.  Characterized by or involving the use of logarithms.|The Richter scale is logarithmic, meaning an earthquake measuring 7 is ten times more powerful than one measuring 6.  里氏震级是对数的，这意味着7级地震的强度是6级地震的十倍。|近义词: exponential (in the opposite sense) |反义词: linear|词性: 形容词

logarithmically: /ˌlɒɡəˈrɪðmɪkli/ (US) /ˌlɒɡəˈrɪðmɪkli/ (UK)| adv.| 对数地; 以对数方式|In a logarithmic manner.|The graph displays the data logarithmically to show the wide range of values.  该图表以对数方式显示数据，以显示值的广泛范围。|近义词: exponentially (in the opposite sense) |反义词: linearly|词性: 副词
