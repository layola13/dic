dodgery:/ˈdɒdʒəri/ (UK) /ˈdɑːdʒəri/ (US)| adj.|狡猾的，躲闪的，可疑的|Evasive, shifty, and possibly dishonest; characterized by trickery or deception.|He gave me a dodgery look when I asked him about the money.  他当我问他钱的事情时，给了我一个躲闪的眼神。|Synonyms: shifty, shady, suspicious, dubious, tricky, cunning, deceitful, underhand, sly|Antonyms: honest, straightforward, trustworthy, aboveboard|adjective

dodger:/ˈdɒdʒə(r)/ (UK) /ˈdɑːdʒər/ (US)| n.|躲避者，逃避者，骗子|A person who dodges or evades something, especially a responsibility or a debt.|He's a tax dodger. 他是一个逃税者。|Synonyms: evader, shirker, cheat, swindler|Antonyms: conformist|noun
