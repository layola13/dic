kingdoms:/ˈkɪŋdəmz/
n.
王国；领域；界
A country or territory ruled by a king or queen.  Also, a broad category of living organisms.
The animal kingdom is incredibly diverse. 动物王国极其多样化。
Medieval kingdoms were often at war with each other. 中世纪的王国经常互相交战。
realms, empires, domains
republics, democracies
