cork:/kɔːrk/ (UK) /kɔːrk/ (US)| n. v.|软木塞；软木；（尤指葡萄酒瓶的）瓶塞；用软木塞塞住|A light, buoyant, elastic outer bark of a Mediterranean oak (Quercus suber). Also used figuratively to mean to stop up or suppress something.|He pulled the cork out of the wine bottle. 他把酒瓶的软木塞拔了出来。|近义词：stopper, plug|反义词: (For the verb) uncork, open|noun, verb

cork:/kɔːrk/ (UK) /kɔːrk/ (US)| v.|用软木塞塞住|To seal or stop up with a cork.|She corked the bottle tightly. 她紧紧地塞住了瓶子。|近义词：plug, stop, seal|反义词:uncork, open|verb
