hypnotic:/hɪpˈnɑːtɪk/ /hɪpˈnɑːdɪk/ | adj. | 催眠的；有催眠作用的；使人着迷的 | Relating to or inducing hypnosis; mesmerizing. |  The rhythmic sound of the waves had a hypnotic effect. 海浪有节奏的声音有催眠作用。

The dancer's movements were hypnotic.  舞者的动作令人着迷。

| 近义词：mesmerizing, captivating, entrancing, spellbinding | 反义词：stimulating, invigorating, exciting | 词性: adjective


hypnotic:/hɪpˈnɑːtɪk/ /hɪpˈnɑːdɪk/ | n. | 催眠药 | A drug that induces sleep. | The doctor prescribed a hypnotic to help him sleep. 医生给他开了一种安眠药来帮助他入睡。

She took a hypnotic to combat her insomnia. 她服用安眠药来对抗失眠。


| 近义词：soporific, sedative | 反义词：stimulant | 词性: noun
