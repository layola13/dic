inefficacious:/ˌɪnɪfəˈkeɪʃəs/
adjective
无效的，无用的
not producing the desired effect; ineffective.
The medicine proved inefficacious in treating the infection.  这种药治疗感染无效。
The inefficacious policy did little to improve the economy. 这项无效的政策对改善经济几乎没有作用。

近义词: ineffective, useless, futile, unproductive, impotent
反义词: effective, potent, useful, productive

词性: adjective

词源:  From Late Latin inefficax, from in- 'not' + efficax 'effective' (from efficere 'to work out, accomplish', from ex- 'out' + facere 'to do, make').
