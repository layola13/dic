cuteness:/ˈkjutnəs/| n.|可爱，讨人喜欢|The cuteness of the puppies made everyone smile. 小狗的可爱让每个人都笑了。|近义词：adorableness, charm, loveliness|反义词：ugliness, hideousness|词性:名词
Etymology: From cute + -ness.  "Cute" originated in the 18th century as a shortening of "acute," meaning sharp or clever.  Over time, its meaning shifted to charming or pretty, and eventually to its current meaning of endearingly sweet or attractive.
