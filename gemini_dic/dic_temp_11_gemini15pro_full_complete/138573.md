nth:/ɛnθ/ | adj. | 第n个；未知的；不确定的 |  Representing an unspecified ordinal number.  Used to denote an arbitrary member of a sequence or set. |  This is the nth time I've told you this. 这是我第n次告诉你这件事了。| synonyms: umpteenth, indefinite | antonyms: specific, definite | adjective

nth:/ɛnθ/ | n. | 不确定的事物；不确定的数量 | An unspecified thing or amount. |  I'm tired of this nth degree of difficulty. 我厌倦了这种不确定的难度。| synonyms: unknown, indeterminate | antonyms: known, determinate | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the mathematical usage of 'n' to represent an arbitrary number, plus the ordinal suffix '-th'.
