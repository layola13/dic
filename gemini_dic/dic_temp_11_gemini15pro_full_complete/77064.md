garnishee: /ˌɡɑːrnɪˈʃiː/ | n. vt.| 扣押债权，扣押令；被扣押的财产；受传讯的第三债务人 |  A legal process by which a creditor can collect a debt by seizing money or property owed to their debtor by a third party.  A garnishee can also refer to the third party who is subject to the garnishment order or the seized property itself. | The court issued a garnishee order on the debtor's wages. 法院对债务人的工资发出了扣押令。 | The bank was the garnishee in the case, holding funds owed to the debtor. 在这个案件中，银行是被扣押债权的第三方，持有欠债务人的资金。 | synonyms: attach, seize, levy | antonyms: release, disburse | noun, verb

garnishee: /ˌɡɑːrnɪˈʃiː/ | vt.|  To seize or attach (money or property) by legal process. | The creditor garnisheed the debtor's bank account. 债权人扣押了债务人的银行账户。 | They are seeking to garnishee his wages to pay off the debt. 他们正试图扣押他的工资来偿还债务。| synonyms: attach, seize, levy | antonyms: release, disburse | verb
