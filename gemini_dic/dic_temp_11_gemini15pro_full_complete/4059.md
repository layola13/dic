AIDS:/eɪdz/ |n.| 艾滋病 | Acquired Immune Deficiency Syndrome, a disease in which there is a severe loss of the body's cellular immunity, greatly lowering the resistance to infection and malignancy. | AIDS is a serious global health issue. 艾滋病是一个严重的全球健康问题。 |  synonyms: acquired immunodeficiency syndrome | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a disease.  One could consider "health" or "immunity" as contrasting concepts.) | noun
