nonperjuries:/ˌnɒnpɜːrˈdʒʊəriːz/
n.
没有伪证；真实陈述
The defendant's nonperjuries were corroborated by several witnesses.
被告的真实陈述得到了几位证人的证实。
Absence of perjury; truthful statements.
The judge commended the witness for his nonperjuries.  法官称赞证人说了实话。
近义词:truths, honest statements
反义词:perjuries, lies, falsehoods
词性:名词 (plural noun)


Etymology:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non "not."
perjury (noun): From Old French parjurie, from Latin perjurium "false oath," from perjurare "to swear falsely," from per- "falsely" + jurare "to swear."
-ies (suffix): Plural form of nouns ending in -y.
