chokers:/ˈtʃoʊkərz/
n.
窒息物；噎住的东西；项圈；
A tight-fitting necklace. A device that restricts breathing. Something that causes choking.
She wore a beautiful velvet choker. 她戴着一条漂亮的丝绒项圈。
The smoke was a choker and made it difficult to breathe. 烟雾令人窒息，难以呼吸。
