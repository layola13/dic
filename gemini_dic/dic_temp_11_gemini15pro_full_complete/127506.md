necklines: /ˈnekˌlaɪnz/ | n. | 领口； neckline (单数) /ˈnekˌlaɪn/ |
The dress has a very flattering neckline. 这条裙子的领口很漂亮。
She wore a dress with a plunging neckline. 她穿着一件领口很低的连衣裙。
Necklines can range from high and conservative to low and revealing. 领口可以从高而保守到低而暴露。
The bride chose a gown with a sweetheart neckline.  新娘选择了一件心形领口的礼服。

近义词: neck openings, collars (when referring to the part of a garment around the neck)
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "bottom hem" as an opposite part of a garment)
词性: noun (plural)
词源:  From 'neck' + 'line'.  'Neck' comes from Old English 'hēafod-necca' (head-neck), later shortened to 'necca'. 'Line' comes from Old French 'ligne', ultimately from Latin 'linea' (linen thread, line).
