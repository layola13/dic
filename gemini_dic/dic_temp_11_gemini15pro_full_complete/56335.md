dogs:/dɒɡz/ (UK) /dɔːɡz/ (US)| n.|狗（复数）|Plural form of "dog," referring to more than one canine animal.|例句：The dogs are barking at the mailman. 狗们对着邮递员吠叫。

例句：She walks her dogs every morning. 她每天早上遛狗。

近义词：canines, hounds (more specific types of dogs)|反义词:cats|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English "docga," of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to a Proto-Germanic word meaning "powerful" or "bite."
