abrook:/əˈbrʊk/ | adj. |忍受，容忍|To tolerate or allow something.  From Middle English *abrok*, from Old English *ābrecan* "to break up, frustrate, abolish,"  which also had the sense "to endure, bear."|He would not abrook any criticism of his work. 他不容忍任何对他工作的批评。|近义词: tolerate, endure, bear, permit, allow|反义词: oppose, resist, refuse, forbid|词性:形容词
