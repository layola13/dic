fusiform:/ˈfjuːzɪfɔːrm/ /ˈfjuːsɪˌfɔrm/ | adj. |梭形的，纺锤形的 | Tapering at both ends; spindle-shaped. | The fusiform body of the dolphin allows it to move swiftly through the water. 海豚的梭形身体使它能够在水中快速移动。

The muscle was fusiform in shape. 这块肌肉呈纺锤形。| 近义词：spindle-shaped, tapering | 反义词：bulbous, rounded | 词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fūsīformis*, from fūsus (“spindle”) + -formis (“-shaped”).
