interadditive:/ˌɪntərˈædɪtɪv/ | adj. | 相互加性的，互相叠加的 |  Relating to or involving the addition of separate effects, typically in a cumulative or synergistic way.  Meaning the combined effect is greater than the sum of the individual effects. | The interadditive effects of the two drugs resulted in a much stronger pain relief than either drug alone. 这两种药物的相互加性效应导致的疼痛缓解比单独使用任何一种药物都要强得多。|  The new alloy's strength is due to the interadditive properties of its constituent metals.  这种新合金的强度源于其组成金属的相互加性特性。 | synergistic, cumulative | antagonistic, subtractive | adjective
