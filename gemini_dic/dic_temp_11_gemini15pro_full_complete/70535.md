filibusterism:/ˈfɪlɪbʌstərɪzəm/ | n. | 阻挠议事，冗长辩论 | The act of obstructing progress in a legislative assembly by using tactics such as prolonged speeches.  | The senator's filibusterism prevented the bill from coming to a vote.  这位参议员的冗长辩论阻止了该法案进行投票。 | synonyms: obstructionism, delaying tactics | antonyms: cooperation, expediency | noun
