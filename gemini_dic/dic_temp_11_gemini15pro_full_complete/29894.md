cauterism: /ˈkɔːtərɪzəm/ | n. | 灼烧；烧灼术 | The application of a hot iron or caustic substance to destroy tissue, as to stop bleeding or prevent infection. | The doctor used cauterism to stop the bleeding from the wound. 医生用烧灼术来止血。| Synonyms: cauterization, searing, branding | Antonyms: healing, soothing | noun

cauterize: /ˈkɔːtəraɪz/ | vt. | 烧灼；灼烧 | To burn or sear (tissue) with a hot iron or caustic substance, especially to stop bleeding or prevent infection. | The surgeon cauterized the wound to prevent infection.  外科医生烧灼伤口以防止感染。| Synonyms: sear, burn, brand | Antonyms: heal, soothe | verb
