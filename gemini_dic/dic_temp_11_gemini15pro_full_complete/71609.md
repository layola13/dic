flatbeds:/ˈflætˌbɛdz/| n.|平板卡车，平板拖车|A truck or trailer with a flat bed, used for carrying large or awkward loads.|The flatbeds were loaded with construction materials. 平板卡车装载着建筑材料。|近义词：platform trucks, trailers|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly enclosed trailers)|Noun

flatbed:/ˈflætˌbɛd/| adj.|平板的|Having a flat bed or surface.|They used a flatbed truck to transport the large machinery. 他们用一辆平板卡车来运输大型机械。|近义词：level, even|反义词: sloped, inclined|Adjective
