catafalque: /ˈkætəfɔːlk/ | n. | 棺架，灵柩台 | A decorated platform, often on wheels, used to support a coffin during a funeral or while lying in state. | The king's body lay in state on a magnificent catafalque in the cathedral. 国王的遗体安放在大教堂里一个华丽的棺架上。

The catafalque was draped in black velvet and adorned with white lilies.  灵柩台覆盖着黑色天鹅绒，装饰着白色的百合花。

| 近义词: bier, hearse | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
