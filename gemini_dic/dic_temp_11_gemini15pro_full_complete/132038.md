noncorrosivenesses:/ˌnɒnkəˈrəʊsɪvnəsɪz/
n.
无腐蚀性；不腐蚀性
The lack of corrosive properties; the quality of not causing corrosion.
The noncorrosivenesses of the new alloy make it ideal for use in marine environments. 这种新合金的无腐蚀性使其非常适合用于海洋环境。
Stainless steel is known for its noncorrosiveness. 不锈钢以其耐腐蚀性而闻名。
inertness, resistance to corrosion
corrosiveness, corrosivity
noun
