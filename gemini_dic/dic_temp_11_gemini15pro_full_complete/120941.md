miscellanist:/ˌmɪsəˈleɪnɪst/
n.
杂集作家；杂文家；博学家
A writer of miscellanies; a writer on various subjects.
Ralph Waldo Emerson was a famous American essayist, lecturer, philosopher, and poet who is often referred to as a miscellanist due to the breadth of his work. 拉尔夫·沃尔多·爱默生是一位著名的美国散文家、演说家、哲学家和诗人，由于其作品涉及广泛，常被称为杂集作家。
He considered himself a miscellanist, exploring various fields of knowledge in his writing. 他认为自己是一位杂集作家，在他的写作中探索各个领域的知识。

近义词: polymath, scholar, eclectic, writer
反义词: specialist
