bewails:/bɪˈweɪlz/| v.| 恸哭；哀悼；悲叹 |expresses great sadness or disappointment about something. | She bewailed the loss of her beloved pet. 她为失去心爱的宠物而悲痛不已。| The community bewailed the closure of the local library. 社区居民为当地图书馆的关闭而哀叹。| lament, mourn, grieve | rejoice, celebrate | verb

He constantly *bewails* his... = 他不断哀叹他的...
She publicly *bewails* the... = 她公开哀叹...


词源: From Middle English *biwailen*, from Old English *biwǣlan* "to lament," from *bi-* "by" + *wǣlan* "to wail."
