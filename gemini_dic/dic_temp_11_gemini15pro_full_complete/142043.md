organometallic:/ˌɔːrɡənəʊməˈtælɪk/
adjective|有机金属的|Relating to or denoting compounds containing metal-carbon bonds.|Organometallic compounds are used as catalysts in many industrial processes.|近义词:metallo-organic|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "inorganic")|词性:adjective

organometallic:/ˌɔːrɡənəʊməˈtælɪk/
noun|有机金属化合物|A compound containing metal-carbon bonds.|Grignard reagents are common examples of organometallic compounds.|近义词:metallo-organic compound|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "inorganic compound")|词性:noun
