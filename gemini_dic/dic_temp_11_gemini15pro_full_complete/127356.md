what: /wɑːt/ (US) /wɒt/ (UK) | pron. det. adv. int.| 什么，多么 |

**英文解析:**

"What" can function as a pronoun, determiner, adverb, and interjection.  It is used to ask questions about things, actions, or qualities. It can also express surprise or emphasis.

**例句:**

* What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
* What did you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃了什么？

**近义词:** which, how

**反义词:**  (What doesn't have a direct antonym)

**词性:** pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection
