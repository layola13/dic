lilylike: /ˈlɪliˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 像百合花的;  百合花似的 | Resembling a lily in appearance or fragrance. | Her dress was a delicate, lilylike white. 她的裙子是精致的百合花般的白色。

The scent was delicate and lilylike.  这香味淡雅，像百合花一样。| 近义词：lily-white, floral, delicate | 反义词: coarse, rough | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "lily" + "-like."  "Lily" comes from Old English *lilie, from Proto-Germanic *lilijǭ, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *wleik-. "-like" is a common suffix denoting resemblance.
