myrtlewood:/ˈmɜːrtlˌwʊd/| n.|桃金娘木|The hard, close-grained wood of the myrtle tree, often used for furniture and decorative objects.|例句：The craftsman carved a beautiful bowl from myrtlewood.  工匠用桃金娘木雕刻了一个漂亮的碗。

例句：Myrtlewood is prized for its beautiful grain and rich color. 桃金娘木因其美丽的纹理和丰富的色彩而备受珍视。|近义词：baywood, laurelwood|反义词:|noun
