aliene: /əˈliːn/ | adj. | 陌生的；格格不入的；相异的 | Feeling isolated and alienated from society.  感到孤立，与社会格格不入。 |  She felt alienated from her family. 她感到与家人疏远了。 | 近义词: estranged, isolated, foreign | 反义词: familiar, close, connected | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *alienus, from alius (“other”).
