ampullary:/æmˈpʌləri/
adjective
壶腹的；安瓿的
Relating to or resembling an ampulla.  An ampulla is a dilated sac or pouch, such as the dilations in semicircular canals of the ear or the duodenum.
The patient presented with ampullary carcinoma.  病人表现出壶腹癌。
The surgeon identified the ampullary portion of the fallopian tube.  外科医生识别出输卵管的壶腹部。
Near synonyms:  dilated, sac-like, pouch-like
Antonyms: constricted, narrow
词性: adjective
