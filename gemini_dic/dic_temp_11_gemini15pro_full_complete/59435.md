earthquake: /ˈɜːθkwɪɪk/ (British) /ˈɜːrθˌkweɪk/ (American) | n. |地震|A sudden violent shaking of the ground, typically causing great destruction, as a result of movements within the earth's crust or volcanic action.|例句：The earthquake caused widespread damage. 地震造成了广泛的破坏。|例句：The city was devastated by a massive earthquake.  这座城市被一场大地震摧毁了。|近义词：tremor, temblor, quake|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用stability（稳定）或calm（平静）表示地震的反面状态) |词性: noun
