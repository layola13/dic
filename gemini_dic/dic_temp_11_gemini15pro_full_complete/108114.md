laticiferous: /ˌlætɪˈsɪfərəs/
adjective
产乳汁的; 含有乳汁的
Containing or conveying latex.
例句：The laticiferous vessels of the rubber tree yield a milky liquid known as latex.  橡胶树的乳汁管会产生一种乳白色的液体，称为乳胶。

例句：Dandelions and milkweed are examples of laticiferous plants. 蒲公英和马利筋是产乳汁植物的例子。

近义词：latex-bearing, latex-producing, milky
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps non-laticiferous)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *laticifer "latex-bearing," from latex (genitive laticis) "fluid, juice" + -fer "bearing," from ferre "to carry, bear."
