chakra:/ˈtʃʌkrə/ (US), /ˈtʃækrə/ (UK)| n. |脉轮，穴位|One of the centers of spiritual power in the human body, according to yoga philosophy.  |She believes that meditation can help to balance her chakras. 她相信冥想可以帮助平衡她的脉轮。|Synonyms: energy center, focal point|Antonyms: None|Noun

chakra:/ˈtʃʌkrə/ (US), /ˈtʃækrə/ (UK)| n. |轮，圆盘 (less common)|A disc or wheel, especially one used as a symbol in Hinduism and Buddhism.  |The king's chakra was a symbol of his power. 国王的轮盘是他力量的象征。|Synonyms: disc, wheel|Antonyms: None|Noun
