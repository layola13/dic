holdership:/ˈhoʊldərʃɪp/
n.
持有权，所有权
The state or fact of being a holder of something, especially property or a position.
The holdership of the land was transferred to the new owner.  这块土地的所有权转移给了新的所有者。
The company's holdership of the patent gives them a competitive advantage.  该公司持有该专利，使其具有竞争优势。
possession, ownership, tenure
