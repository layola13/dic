assayable:/əˈseɪəbl/
adjective
可化验的，可分析的
able to be assayed, or tested for the presence and amount of a particular substance.
The gold ore was assayable and found to be of high quality. 这金矿石可以化验，并且发现质量很高。
Synonyms: testable, analyzable, verifiable
Antonyms: untestable, unanalyzable

词性: adjective
