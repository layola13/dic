drearies:/ˈdrɪəriz/| n.| 沉闷，乏味，令人沮丧的事物| things that are dreary; dull or depressing things | The endless drearies of winter got her down. 冬日无休止的沉闷让她沮丧。| 近义词：doldrums, monotony, tedium | 反义词: excitements, thrills | 词性: 名词


词源:  From dreary + -ies (plural suffix).  Ultimately from Old English *drēorig, from *drēor "gore".
