haptic:/ˈhæptɪk/ (US), /ˈhætɪk/ (UK)| adj.|触觉的；触觉学的|Relating to the sense of touch; tactile.|例句：Haptic feedback is becoming increasingly common in modern smartphones.  触觉反馈在现代智能手机中越来越普遍。

例句：The haptic interface allows users to feel virtual objects.  触觉界面允许用户感受虚拟物体。

|近义词：tactile, kinesthetic, sensory|反义词：non-tactile|词性: adjective
