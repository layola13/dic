expatiates:/ɪkˈspeɪʃieɪts/
verb
详细阐述；细说；详谈
to speak or write at length or in detail
The professor expatiated on the history of the Roman Empire. 教授详细阐述了罗马帝国的历史。
近义词:elaborates, expands, dwells
反义词:summarizes, condenses, abbreviates
词性:v.
