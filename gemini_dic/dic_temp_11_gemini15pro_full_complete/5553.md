alpinist:/ˈælpɪnɪst/ | n. | 登山家；攀登者 | A person who climbs mountains, especially as a sport. |  The alpinist reached the summit after days of arduous climbing.  这位登山家经过数天的艰苦攀登，终于到达了顶峰。|  climber, mountaineer | antonym: N/A (lack of a direct opposite) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *alpiniste*, from *alpin* (“alpine”), from Latin *alpīnus* (“of the Alps”), from *Alpēs* (“the Alps”).
