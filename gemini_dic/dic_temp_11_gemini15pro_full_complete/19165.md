bewaring:/bɪˈwɛərɪŋ/ | v. | 提防，警惕，注意 | being cautious or wary of something or someone |  He was bewaring of strangers. 他对陌生人很警惕。| 近义词: wary, cautious, careful, watchful, vigilant | 反义词: careless, reckless, heedless | 词性: 动词(现在分词)

词源:  From Middle English *bewarynge, present participle of bewarien (“to beware”), equivalent to be- + ware + -ing.  The verb "beware" comes from Old English bewarian, meaning "to guard, watch out for."
