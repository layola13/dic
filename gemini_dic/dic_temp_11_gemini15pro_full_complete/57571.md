| adj. |可戏剧化的，可改编成戏剧的|Capable of being dramatized; suitable for conversion into dramatic form. |Her life story is highly dramatizable and would make a great movie.  她的生平故事非常具有戏剧性，可以拍成一部很棒的电影。|  This novel is very dramatizable. 这部小说很适合改编成戏剧。|近义词：adaptable, stageable|反义词：undramatizable, mundane|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From dramatize + -able.  Dramatize comes from drama + -ize. Drama ultimately comes from the Greek word "δρᾶμα" (drama), meaning "action, deed, play."
