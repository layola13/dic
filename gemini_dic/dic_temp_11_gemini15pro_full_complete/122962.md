monachal:/məˈnɑːkəl/
adjective
修道院的；僧侣的；禁欲主义的
Relating to monks or monasteries; characteristic of monks or monastic life, especially in its contemplative or austere aspects.
He led a monachal life dedicated to prayer and study. 他过着一种致力于祈祷和学习的僧侣生活。

The monachal atmosphere of the library was conducive to quiet contemplation. 图书馆的修道院氛围有利于安静的沉思。

synonym: ascetic, cloistered, monastic, contemplative, austere
antonym: secular, worldly, hedonistic
词性: 形容词
