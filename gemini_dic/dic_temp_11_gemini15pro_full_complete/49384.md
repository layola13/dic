deliriousness:/dɪˈlɪriəsnəs/
n.
神志不清，谵妄
A state of mental confusion, often accompanied by hallucinations and disordered speech.
The patient's deliriousness was caused by a high fever. 病人的谵妄是由高烧引起的。
delirium, confusion, incoherence
lucidity, clarity, coherence
noun

delirium:/dɪˈlɪriəm/
n.
谵妄，精神错乱
A state of mental confusion, often accompanied by hallucinations and disordered speech.
He experienced delirium from the high fever. 他因高烧而出现谵妄。
madness, frenzy, insanity
sanity, lucidity, rationality
noun
