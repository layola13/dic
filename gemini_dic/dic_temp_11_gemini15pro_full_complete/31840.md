cheekless:/ˈtʃiːklɪs/
adjective
无耻的，厚颜无耻的；没有脸颊的
lacking shame or modesty; having no cheeks
He gave a cheekless grin. 他厚颜无耻地咧嘴一笑。
The starving child had a gaunt, cheekless face.  那个饥饿的孩子面容憔悴，瘦得没有脸颊。
近义词: shameless, impudent, brazen, audacious
反义词: modest, shy, bashful
词性: adjective
