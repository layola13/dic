half-Asian:/ˌhæf ˈeɪʒən/ | n. | 半亚裔人士 | A person of mixed Asian and non-Asian descent. | Example:  My friend identifies as half-Asian, with a Korean mother and a British father. 我的朋友认为自己是半个亚裔，母亲是韩国人，父亲是英国人。

Example: There's a growing community of half-Asians sharing their experiences online.  越来越多的半亚裔群体在网上分享他们的经历。

| 近义词: mixed-race, biracial | 反义词:  (No direct antonym - could consider full-blooded, but depends on context and may be offensive) | 词性: noun
