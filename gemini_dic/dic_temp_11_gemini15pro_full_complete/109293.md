lemurine: /ˈliːmjʊˌraɪn/ | adj. n. |狐猴的；狐猴|Relating to or resembling a lemur; a lemur.|例句：The lemurine eyes stared back at me. 狐猴的眼睛盯着我。|例句：The zoo has a diverse collection of lemurine species.  这家动物园有各种各样的狐猴物种。 |近义词：prosimian (more general), lorisoid (related group)|反义词: anthropoid (higher primates like monkeys, apes, and humans) |词性: adjective, noun

词源: From French *lémurin*, from Latin *lemures* ("ghosts, spirits of the dead") + *-inus* (suffix forming adjectives).  Lemurs were given this name due to their nocturnal habits and large reflective eyes.
