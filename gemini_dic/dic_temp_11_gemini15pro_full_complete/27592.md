canberra:/ˈkænbərə/ /ˈkænbrə/| n.|堪培拉|The capital city of Australia.  It is located in the Australian Capital Territory (ACT), which is a separate territory within New South Wales. The name "Canberra" is thought to be derived from the word "Kamberra" or "Canberry," an Aboriginal word thought to mean "meeting place."|例句：Canberra is known for its planned layout and national institutions. 堪培拉以其规划的布局和国家机构而闻名。
