dwan:/dɔːn/| n.|黎明，曙光，破晓|The dwan is the first appearance of light in the sky before sunrise. 黎明是日出之前天空中的第一缕光。|例句：The birds began to sing at dwan. 鸟儿们在黎明时分开始歌唱。

例句：We woke up at dwan to watch the sunrise. 我们在黎明时分醒来观看日出。|近义词：dawn, daybreak, sunrise|反义词：dusk, sunset, twilight|词性:名词

词源：From Middle English *dawen, *dawignen, from Old English dagian (“to become day, dawn”), from Proto-Germanic *dagōną (“to become day”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰegʷʰ- (“to become light, bright, shine”).
