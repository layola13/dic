bedcover: /ˈbedˌkʌvər/ (US), /ˈbedˌkʌvə(r)/ (UK) | n. | 床罩 | A decorative outer covering for a bed, often made of fabric and used for warmth or decoration. |  I bought a new bedcover for the guest room. 我为客房买了一个新的床罩。| Synonyms: bedspread, comforter, quilt, duvet | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "sheet" in the sense of a minimal covering) | noun
