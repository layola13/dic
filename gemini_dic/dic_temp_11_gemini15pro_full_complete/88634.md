ETYMOLOGY->
HMRC is an acronym for Her Majesty's Revenue and Customs.  It was formed in 2005 by the merger of Inland Revenue and HM Customs and Excise. HMRC 是 Her Majesty's Revenue and Customs 的首字母缩写。它是由 Inland Revenue 和 HM Customs and Excise 于 2005 年合并而成。
