nonsanely:/nɒnˈseɪnli/ | adv. | 不理智地，疯狂地 | In a manner that lacks sanity or reason; insanely.  Derived from "non-" (not) + "sanely" (in a sane manner). | He acted nonsanely, driven by grief and rage. 他被悲痛和愤怒驱使，行为疯狂。|  She nonsanely threw her phone across the room. 她疯狂地把手机扔到房间的另一边。 | 近义词: insanely, madly, foolishly, irrationally | 反义词: sanely, rationally, reasonably | 词性: 副词
