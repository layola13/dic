abroad:/əˈbrɔːd/ | adv. | 在国外，到国外 | in or to a foreign country or countries |  She traveled abroad for a year after graduation. 她毕业后出国旅行了一年。| synonym: overseas, internationally | antonym: domestic, locally | adverb

abroad:/əˈbrɔːd/ | adj. |  国外的 | existing or occurring in foreign countries | There is a growing demand for abroad products. 国外产品的需求正在增长。| synonym: foreign, international | antonym: domestic, local | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *abrode, from Old English on brēade (“on breadth, broadly, abroad”), equivalent to a- (Old English prefix meaning "on, in, at, to") + broad.
