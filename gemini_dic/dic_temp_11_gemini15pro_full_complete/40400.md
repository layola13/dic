contraindicant:/ˌkɒntrəˈɪndɪkənt/ | n. |禁忌症，禁忌证|A contraindication is a specific situation in which a drug, procedure, or surgery should not be used because it may be harmful to the person.| The patient's history of blood clots is a contraindicant to this medication.  该病人的血栓病史是服用这种药物的禁忌症。|Synonyms: contraindication, no-no, proscription |Antonyms: indication |noun
