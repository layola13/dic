outreproduce:/ˌaʊtriːprəˈdjuːs/
verb
大量繁殖，过度繁殖
to reproduce in greater numbers than
例句：Some insects outreproduce their predators. 一些昆虫比它们的捕食者繁殖得更多。

例句：The rabbits outreproduced the foxes, leading to an overpopulation problem. 兔子的繁殖速度超过了狐狸，导致了种群过剩的问题。

近义词：outnumber, exceed, surpass
反义词：underproduce
词性：v.
