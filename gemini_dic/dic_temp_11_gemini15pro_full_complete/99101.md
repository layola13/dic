intercampus:/ˌɪntərˈkæmpəs/| adj.| 校际的，校园间的|Existing or occurring between two or more campuses of a university or college.| The intercampus bus runs every hour. 校际巴士每小时一班。|近义词：intercollegiate, between campuses|反义词：intracampus|词性：形容词

词源:  "inter-" (between) + "campus" (grounds of a college or university)
