oligosepalous:/ˌɒlɪɡəˈsɛpələs/ | adj. | 少萼片的；萼片数少于正常数的 | Having fewer sepals than usual for the particular type of flower. |  The oligosepalous flower had only three sepals. 这朵少萼片的花只有三个萼片。 |  Few-sepaled | Polysepalous | adjective
