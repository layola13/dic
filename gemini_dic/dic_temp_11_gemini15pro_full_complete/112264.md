loper:/ˈloʊpər/ | n. | 闲逛者；游荡者；潜行者 | someone who lopes; a person who runs at a steady easy gait | The lone loper disappeared into the dense forest.  孤独的游荡者消失在茂密的森林里。| synonyms: wanderer, roamer, stroller, prowler | antonyms: resident, settler | noun

loper:/ˈloʊpər/ | n. | 骗子；行骗者 | a person who deceives others, especially to gain money | The lopers tricked the elderly couple out of their savings. 骗子骗走了老夫妇的积蓄。 | synonyms: swindler, con artist, fraudster, cheat | antonyms: honest person, philanthropist | noun
