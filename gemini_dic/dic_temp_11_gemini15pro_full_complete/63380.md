entophyte:/ˈɛndəˌfaɪt/| n. |内生菌；内生植物|An endophyte is an organism, often a bacterium or fungus, that lives within a plant for at least part of its life cycle without causing apparent disease. |Example 1: Endophytes can help plants resist disease and environmental stress. 内生菌可以帮助植物抵抗疾病和环境压力。

Example 2:  The researchers isolated several endophytes from the roots of the tree. 研究人员从树根中分离出几种内生菌。|近义词：endobiont|反义词: pathogen|词性:noun
