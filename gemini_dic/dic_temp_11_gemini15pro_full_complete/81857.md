gridiron: /ˈɡrɪdˌaɪərn/ (US), /ˈɡrɪdˌaɪən/ (UK)| n.|烤架；美式橄榄球场|A framework of metal bars used for cooking over a fire; a football field. |例句：The steak sizzled on the gridiron. 牛排在烤架上发出咝咝声。

例句：The gridiron was packed with cheering fans.  橄榄球场挤满了欢呼的球迷。|近义词：grill, (for cooking); football field, field (for sports)|反义词: None (no direct opposite for either meaning)|词性: noun


gridiron: /ˈɡrɪdˌaɪərn/ (US), /ˈɡrɪdˌaɪən/ (UK)| adj.|像烤架的|Resembling a gridiron. This usage is less common. |例句：The city's gridiron street plan made navigation easy. 城市像烤架一样的街道规划使得导航很容易。

例句：The architect designed a gridiron pattern for the building's facade.  建筑师为建筑的外墙设计了一个像烤架一样的图案。|近义词：grid-like|反义词: None|词性: adjective (less common)
