hash:/hæʃ/ | n. | 哈希值，散列值，#号 | A hash is a fixed-size string of characters generated from arbitrary data using a mathematical function. |  Example: The password was stored as a hash.  密码被存储为哈希值。 | synonym: hash value, checksum, digital fingerprint | antonym: N/A | noun

hash:/hæʃ/ | vt. |  把…弄乱，切碎，搞糟 | To chop into small pieces. | Example:  He hashed the potatoes for breakfast. 他把土豆切碎做早餐。 | synonym: chop, mince, mangle | antonym: organize, arrange | verb
