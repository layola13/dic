minnow:/ˈmɪnoʊ/ (US), /ˈmɪnəʊ/ (UK)| n. |米诺鱼，小鱼，无足轻重的人|A small freshwater fish of the carp family.  Figuratively, it can also refer to an unimportant person or organization.|The fisherman caught a tiny minnow. 这位渔夫钓到了一条小米诺鱼。|The company is just a minnow in the industry. 这家公司在业内只是一条小鱼。|近义词：tidler, small fry|反义词：whale, giant|词性: 名词
