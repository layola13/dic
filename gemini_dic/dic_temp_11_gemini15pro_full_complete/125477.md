multipole:/ˈmʌltɪpoʊl/ | n. | 多极; 多极子 | A configuration of multiple poles, used in physics to describe the distribution of electric charge or magnetic moments.  | The antenna is a multipole, radiating energy in several directions.  这个天线是一个多极子，向多个方向辐射能量。| synonyms: multipole moment, multipole expansion | antonyms: monopole, dipole | noun

multipole:/ˈmʌltɪˌpəʊl/ | adj. | 多极的 | Having or relating to multiple poles. |  A multipole magnetic field is more complex than a dipole field. 多极磁场比偶极磁场更复杂。| synonyms: multipolar | antonyms: monopolar, dipolar | adjective

From multi- (many) + -pole (pole)
