aquitaid:/ˈækwɪˌeɪd/| n.|止泻药，口服补水液|A commercial solution containing glucose and electrolytes, taken orally to restore lost fluids and salts following dehydration, particularly caused by diarrhea.|The doctor recommended aquitaid for the child suffering from diarrhea. 医生建议给腹泻的孩子服用口服补水液。|近义词：ORS (Oral Rehydration Salts), electrolyte solution|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps dehydrating agent)|noun
