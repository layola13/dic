diphtheric:/dɪfˈθɛrɪk/ |adj.|白喉的；患白喉的|Relating to or suffering from diphtheria, a serious bacterial infection of the nose and throat.|The child had a diphtheric cough.  这孩子有白喉引起的咳嗽。| The doctor diagnosed a diphtheric infection. 医生诊断出白喉感染。|Synonyms: diphtheritic, diphtheroid|Antonyms: healthy, well|adjective

diphtheria:/dɪfˈθɪəriə/ |n.|白喉|A serious infectious bacterial disease affecting the mucous membranes of the throat and nose, characterized by the formation of a grayish membrane in the throat that can obstruct breathing.|Diphtheria is a highly contagious disease. 白喉是一种高度传染的疾病。|  Vaccination has greatly reduced the incidence of diphtheria. 疫苗接种大大降低了白喉的发病率。|Synonyms: None (specific disease)|Antonyms: None (specific disease)|noun
