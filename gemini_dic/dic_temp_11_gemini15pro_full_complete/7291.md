angstier:/ˈæŋstiər/| adj.|更焦虑的，更担忧的|A comparative form of "angsty," meaning experiencing more anxiety or angst than before or than something else.|例句：He seemed angstier than usual today. 他今天似乎比平时更焦虑。|近义词：more anxious, more worried, more apprehensive|反义词：calmer, more relaxed|词性:形容词

词源:  "Angstier" comes from "angsty," which is derived from "angst," a German word meaning "fear" or "anxiety." It gained popularity in English through existentialist philosophy.
