comanaging:/ˌkoʊˈmænɪdʒɪŋ/
verb
共同管理
The act of managing something together with one or more other people.
The parents are comanaging their son's college fund. 父母共同管理着儿子的大学基金。
We are comanaging the project with another firm. 我们正在和另一家公司共同管理这个项目。
collaborating, co-managing, joint managing
sole managing, independent managing
