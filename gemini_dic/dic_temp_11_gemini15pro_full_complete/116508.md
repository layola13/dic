maternity:/məˈtɜːrnɪti/ /məˈtɜːrnəti/
n.
母性，孕妇，产妇，产假
The period during which a woman is pregnant or shortly after she has given birth.  
She's on maternity leave. 她正在休产假。
近义词: motherhood, pregnancy, childbirth
反义词: paternity
词性: 名词
