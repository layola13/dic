agonistically:/ˌæɡəˈnɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 争斗地，对抗地 | In a manner characterized by conflict, struggle, or opposition.  | The two rams butted heads agonistically. 两只公羊争斗地顶着头。| synonym: combatively, contentiously, antagonistically | antonym: cooperatively, harmoniously, peacefully | adverb

agonistic:/ˌæɡəˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 争斗的，好斗的 | Relating to or characterized by conflict, struggle, or opposition. |  The agonistic behavior of the chimpanzees was fascinating to observe. 黑猩猩好斗的行为令人着迷。| synonym: combative, aggressive, belligerent | antonym: peaceful, cooperative, harmonious | adjective

Etymology: From Greek *agonistikos*, from *agonistes* "competitor," from *agon* "contest, struggle."
