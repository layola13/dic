overgratifies: /ˌoʊvərˈɡrætɪfaɪz/
verb
过度满足，过分纵容
to gratify to an excessive degree; to indulge (someone) to excess.
He overgratifies his children with toys and gadgets. 他用玩具和小玩意儿过度满足他的孩子们。
Synonyms: overindulge, spoil, pamper, coddle
Antonyms: deprive, neglect, restrict
词性: 动词
