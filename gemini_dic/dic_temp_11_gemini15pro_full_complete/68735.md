farmerly: /ˈfɑːrmərli/ | adj. | 农民般的；朴实的；乡土气的 |  Characteristic of or resembling a farmer; rustic; rural.  | He had a farmerly appearance, with his weathered hands and sunburnt face. 他有着农民般的外表，双手粗糙，脸晒得黝黑。| Synonyms: rustic, rural, agrarian, agricultural, countrified | Antonyms: urban, sophisticated, refined | adjective

Etymology: From "farmer" + "-ly" (suffix forming adjectives). "Farmer" comes from Old French "fermier" (from Medieval Latin "firmarius," meaning "leaseholder"), ultimately from Latin "firmus" (meaning "firm," "strong," "stable").
