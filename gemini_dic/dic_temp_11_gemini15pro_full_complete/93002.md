idiopathy:/ˌɪdiˈɒpəθi/ | n. | 特发症，原因不明的疾病 | A disease or condition that arises spontaneously or for which the cause is unknown. |  Idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis is a serious lung disease with an unknown cause.  特发性肺纤维化是一种原因不明的严重肺部疾病。| synonyms: essential disease, primary disease, cryptogenic disease | antonyms: secondary disease, acquired disease | noun
