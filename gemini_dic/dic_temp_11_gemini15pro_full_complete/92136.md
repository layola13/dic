hypersensibility:/ˌhaɪpərˌsɛnsɪˈbɪləti/| n. |过敏性，过度敏感|Excessive sensitivity, especially of the body to a particular substance (an allergen) or stimulus.|She has a hypersensitivity to certain foods. 她对某些食物过敏。|Near Synonyms: allergy, intolerance, reactivity|Antonyms: hyposensitivity, insensitivity|noun


hypersensitive:/ˌhaɪpərˈsɛnsɪtɪv/| adj. |过度敏感的|Excessively sensitive; easily offended or emotionally upset.|He's hypersensitive to criticism. 他对批评过度敏感。|Near Synonyms: oversensitive, touchy, thin-skinned|Antonyms: insensitive, thick-skinned|adjective
