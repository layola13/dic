categorematic:/ˌkætɪɡɔːrɪˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 范畴的，明确的 |  A categorematic word has independent meaning, like "tree" or "run," as opposed to syncategorematic words like "the" or "and" which only have meaning in relation to other words. |  "Red" is a categorematic term, signifying a particular color. 红色是一个范畴词，表示一种特定的颜色。 |  categorical, definite, explicit |  syncategorematic, ambiguous, implicit | adjective

categorematically:/ˌkætɪɡɔːrɪˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 范畴地，明确地 | In a categorematically stated sentence, the meaning is clear and unambiguous. | The rules were stated categorematically, leaving no room for interpretation. 规则被明确地陈述，没有留下任何解释的空间。 | categorically, definitely, explicitly | syncategorematically, ambiguously, implicitly | adverb
