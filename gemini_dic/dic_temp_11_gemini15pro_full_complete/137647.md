nonunanimousnesses:/ˌnɑnˌjuːˈnænɪməsnəsɪz/
n.
lack of unanimity; disagreements; instances of not being unanimous
The committee's report highlighted the nonunanimousnesses on several key issues. 委员会的报告强调了在几个关键问题上缺乏一致意见。
The nonunanimousnesses within the jury led to a hung jury. 陪审团内部的意见分歧导致陪审团无法达成一致裁决。
Synonyms: disagreements, discords, dissents
Antonyms: agreements, consensuses, unanimity
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + unanimous (fully in agreement) + -ness (state of being).  Ultimately from Latin *unus (one) + animus (mind).
