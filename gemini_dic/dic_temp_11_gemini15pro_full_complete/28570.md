careenage:/ˌkærɪˈniːdʒ/ | n. | 船坞，修船厂；（尤指西印度群岛的）港湾，内港 | A place where ships are careened (hauled out of the water for cleaning or repair), or a harbor or waterway specifically designed for this purpose, especially in the West Indies. |例句：The careenage was bustling with activity as the fishermen prepared their boats for the upcoming season.  修船厂里一片繁忙，渔民们正在为即将到来的捕鱼季准备船只。

例句：The historic careenage in Bridgetown, Barbados, is a popular tourist destination.  巴巴多斯布里奇顿的历史悠久的港湾是一个热门的旅游目的地。| 近义词：harbor, dock, shipyard, marina | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *carénage*, from *caréner* "to careen," from Spanish *carena* "keel," from Latin *carina*.
