brainchildren:/ˈbreɪnˌtʃɪldrən/
n.
智力产物；主意；点子
An idea or invention which is the result of someone's creative thinking.
These innovative products are the brainchildren of our talented research team. 这些创新产品是我们优秀研发团队的智力结晶。
The project was the brainchild of a local businessman. 这个项目是一位当地商人的主意。
