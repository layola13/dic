hyperpigmentations:/ˌhaɪpərˌpɪɡmənˈteɪʃənz/
|n.|色素沉着过度；色素沉着过多|The condition of having patches of skin that are darker than the surrounding skin, due to increased melanin production.|She suffered from hyperpigmentations after her pregnancy. 她怀孕后出现了色素沉着过度。|Synonyms: darkening, discoloration, melasma, chloasma|Antonyms: hypopigmentation|Noun
