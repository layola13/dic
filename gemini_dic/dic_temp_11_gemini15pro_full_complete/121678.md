misplayed:/ˌmɪsˈpleɪd/ | adj. v. | 失误的；玩得不好的；处理不当的 | Played badly or incorrectly; handled poorly. | The quarterback misplayed the snap and fumbled the ball.  四分卫玩砸了开球，弄丢了球。 | Synonyms: bungled, botched, flubbed, muffed | Antonyms:  well-played, expertly handled | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (wrongly) + play.  The prefix "mis-" comes from Old English "mis-" meaning "wrongly, badly, amiss." "Play" comes from Old English "plegan" meaning "to exercise oneself, move briskly, occupy oneself; contend, fight."
