outsoars:/ˌaʊtˈsɔːrz/
verb
超越，胜过
to fly higher or further than; to surpass or excel
The new plane outsoars all previous models in speed and fuel efficiency. 这款新飞机在速度和燃油效率方面都超过了以往所有机型。
synonyms: surpasses, exceeds, transcends, outstrips
antonyms: lags behind, falls short of
词性: 动词
