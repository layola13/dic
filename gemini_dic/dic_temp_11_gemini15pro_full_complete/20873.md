blay:/bleɪ/
verb
剥皮; 撕下; 剥掉
to strip off the skin or surface of something.
He blayed the bark off the tree. 他剥掉了树皮。
近义词: peel, skin, flay
反义词: cover

blay:/bleɪ/
noun
剥皮; 撕下; 剥掉
the act of stripping off the skin or surface of something.
The blay of the animal was quick and efficient. 对动物的剥皮既迅速又有效。
近义词: peeling, skinning, flaying
反义词: covering
