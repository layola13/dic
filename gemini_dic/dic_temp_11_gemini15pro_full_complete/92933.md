identifier:/aɪˈdɛntɪfaɪər/ | n. | 标识符，识别符 | A name that identifies a particular variable, function, class, module, or other entity in a computer program. | Example: In the code `int x = 5;`, `x` is the identifier. 在代码`int x = 5;`中，`x`是标识符。

| Example:  A unique identifier is assigned to each student.  每个学生都被分配了一个唯一的标识符。 | Synonyms: name, label, tag, designation | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
