backed:/bækt/
adjective
有靠背的；得到支持的
supported; having a back
例句：
The chair is backed with soft cushions. 这把椅子有柔软的靠垫支撑着。

He had a backed project by a large corporation. 他有一个大公司支持的项目。


近义词：supported, reinforced, subsidized
反义词：unsupported, opposed

词性
adjective


词源
From the verb "back" + "-ed" suffix, indicating the state of having a back or being supported.  "Back" itself comes from Old English "bæc," ultimately from Proto-Germanic *bakam.
