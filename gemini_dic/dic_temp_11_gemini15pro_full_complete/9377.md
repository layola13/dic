antiquark:/ˈæntiˌkwɔrk/ | n. | 反夸克 | A subatomic particle with the same mass and spin as a quark, but with opposite charge and other properties. It is the antimatter counterpart of a quark. |  Scientists believe that every quark has a corresponding antiquark. 科学家认为每个夸克都有一个对应的反夸克。

Antiquarks are essential components of some subatomic particles. 反夸克是某些亚原子粒子的重要组成部分。 | 近义词: (No direct synonym, but related terms include) antiparticle, antimatter | 反义词: quark | 词性: noun
