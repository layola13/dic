membraniferous:/ˌmɛmbrəˈnɪfərəs/
adjective
膜状的; 有膜的; 具有膜的
Having or bearing a membrane or membranes.
The membraniferous wings of the bat allow it to fly. 蝙蝠的膜状翅膀使它能够飞行。
membranous, filmy
impermeable
