antisyndicalism:/ˌæntiˌsɪndɪkəlɪzəm/
n.
反工团主义，反工会主义
Opposition to trade unionism; the belief that trade unions are harmful.
The government's antisyndicalism led to violent clashes with workers. 政府的反工会主义导致与工人的暴力冲突。
The dictator's regime was marked by strong antisyndicalism. 独裁者的政权以强烈的反工会主义为标志。
近义词: anti-unionism
反义词: syndicalism, pro-unionism
词性: noun
