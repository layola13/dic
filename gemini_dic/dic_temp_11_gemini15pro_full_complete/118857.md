metaphysis:/məˈtæfɪsɪs/
noun
干骺端；骨干骺端
The wider part of a long bone adjacent to the epiphyseal plate.  This is the region where bone growth occurs.
The fracture occurred in the metaphysis of the femur.  股骨干骺端发生骨折。
epiphyseal plate, growth plate
diaphysis (骨干)
n.
