disapprovals:/ˌdɪsəˈpruːvəlz/
n.
不赞成，反对；非难；不批准
expressions of disapproval.
表示不赞成。
The president expressed his disapproval of the plan. 总统表示不赞成这项计划。

近义词: objections, criticisms, censures, rejections
反义词: approvals, endorsements, acceptances
词性: 名词(plural)
