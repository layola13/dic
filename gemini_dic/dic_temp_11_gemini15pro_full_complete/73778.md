formality:/fɔːrˈmæləti/ | n. |礼节；仪式；拘谨；正式手续|Something done simply for form or ceremony. A required or conventional procedure.  The rigid observance of rules of convention or etiquette.|例句：The meeting was opened with the usual formalities. 会议以通常的礼节开始。

例句：We had to go through all the formalities of getting a visa. 我们不得不完成所有获得签证的正式手续。|近义词：ceremony, ritual, convention, etiquette, protocol, procedure |反义词：informality, casualness |词性: noun


formality:/fɔːrˈmæləti/ | n. |正式 |The quality or condition of being formal.|例句：The formality of the occasion made everyone feel a little awkward.  这个场合的正式性让每个人都感到有点尴尬。

例句：She appreciated the formality of the written invitation. 她很欣赏书面邀请的正式性。|近义词： stiffness, aloofness|反义词：informality, warmth|词性: noun
