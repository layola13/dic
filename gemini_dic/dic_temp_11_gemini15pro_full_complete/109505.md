lernaea:/lɜːrˈniːə/ | n. | 锚头蚤，锚头鳋 | A parasitic copepod crustacean of the genus Lernaea, which attaches itself to fish.  These parasites are known for their anchor-like shape and can cause significant harm to their host. | The lernaea infested the fish farm, causing widespread mortality. 锚头蚤感染了养鱼场，造成大面积死亡。 | synonyms: anchor worm, fish louse | antonyms: N/A | noun

lernaean:/lɜːrˈniːən/ | adj. |  像锚头蚤的; 难以摆脱的，纠缠不休的 | Resembling or characteristic of a lernaea; difficult to get rid of, persistent. | The lernaean infestation proved to be a lernaean problem for the fish farmer.  锚头蚤的感染对养鱼户来说是一个难以摆脱的问题。 | synonyms: tenacious, persistent, clinging, unshakeable | antonyms: fleeting, transient, temporary | adjective
