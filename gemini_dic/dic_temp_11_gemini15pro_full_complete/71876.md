fleshquake:/ˈflɛʃ.kweɪk/| n. |肉体震颤，强烈的情感冲击|A sudden, intense emotional or physical sensation, often described as a wave or tremor through the body. It can be triggered by powerful experiences, such as love, fear, or grief.| The song sent a fleshquake through her, bringing back memories of her first love. 这首歌在她身上引起一阵肉体震颤，让她回想起初恋的回忆。|近义词：thrill, shudder, tremor, shiver|反义词: numbness, apathy|词性:名词

词源:  A neologism formed by combining "flesh" and "quake."  "Flesh" refers to the physical body, and "quake" signifies a tremor or shaking.  The word evokes the physical manifestation of intense emotional experience.
