outvoicing:/ˈaʊtˌvɔɪsɪŋ/
n.
外呼；电话销售；电话推销
The act or process of making calls to potential customers, typically to solicit sales or gather information.
Example: Outvoicing can be a very effective marketing strategy. 外呼可以是非常有效的营销策略。

Example: The company relies heavily on outvoicing to generate new leads. 这家公司严重依赖外呼来产生新的潜在客户。

近义词:telemarketing, cold calling, outbound calling
反义词:inbound calling
