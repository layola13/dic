iMessage:/ˈaɪˌmɛsɪdʒ/| n.|iMessage (苹果公司即时通信软件)|An instant messaging service developed by Apple Inc. that allows users to send text messages, photos, videos, and other media over Wi-Fi or cellular data networks to other Apple devices.|例句：I sent him an iMessage, but he hasn't replied yet. 我给他发了一条iMessage，但他还没有回复。|近义词：text message, instant message, SMS|反义词:None|词性:noun

iMessage:/ˈaɪˌmɛsɪdʒ/| n.|苹果消息|A proprietary instant messaging service developed by Apple Inc. that allows users to send text, images, videos, documents, contact information, location, and other types of data to other Apple devices over Wi-Fi or cellular data networks.|例句：Can you send me the details via iMessage? 你能通过iMessage把细节发给我吗？|近义词：text, SMS, message|反义词:None|词性:noun
