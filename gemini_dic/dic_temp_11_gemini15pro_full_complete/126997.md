nasalizable:/ˈneɪzələɪzəbl/ | adj. |可鼻化的|Able to be nasalized (pronounced with air passing through the nose).  A sound is nasalizable if it can be made with the velum lowered, allowing air to escape through the nose. |The vowels in French are nasalizable before nasal consonants. 法语中的元音在鼻辅音前可以鼻化。| nasal, pronounceable | non-nasalizable | adjective
