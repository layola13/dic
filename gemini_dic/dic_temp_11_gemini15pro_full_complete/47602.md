debauched:/dɪˈbɔːtʃt/
adjective
放荡的，堕落的
Indulging in excessive pleasure, especially sexual pleasure; corrupt.
He led a debauched life filled with wild parties and illicit affairs. 他过着放荡的生活，充满了狂野的派对和不正当的私情。
Synonyms: dissipated, dissolute, profligate, decadent, corrupt
Antonyms: chaste, virtuous, moral, upright, ascetic
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *débaucher*, "to corrupt," from *dé-* (indicating reversal) + *bauche* "workshop," implying leading someone away from productive work.
