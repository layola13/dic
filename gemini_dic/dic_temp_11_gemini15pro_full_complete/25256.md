bufferhead:/ˈbʌfərˌhɛd/ | n. | 小潜鸭; 小秋沙鸭 | A small North American diving duck (Bucephala albeola) having a large head and a short bill. |  Example:  We saw a flock of buffleheads diving for fish. 我们看到一群小潜鸭潜入水中捕鱼。

Example: The male bufflehead is easily recognized by its large white head patch. 雄性小潜鸭很容易通过其头部的大块白色斑点辨认出来。 | synonym:  butterball, spirit duck | antonym: N/A | noun
