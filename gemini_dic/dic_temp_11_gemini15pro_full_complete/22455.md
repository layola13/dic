bookkeeper:/ˈbʊkkiːpər/| n. |簿记员，记账员|A person whose job is to keep records of the financial affairs of a business. |例句：The bookkeeper meticulously recorded every transaction. 簿记员一丝不苟地记录每一笔交易。

例句：She worked as a bookkeeper for a small firm. 她在一家小公司担任簿记员。|近义词：accountant, clerk |反义词: N/A |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From book +‎ keeper, ultimately from Middle English bok +‎ keper (“one who keeps, maintains, or guards”).
