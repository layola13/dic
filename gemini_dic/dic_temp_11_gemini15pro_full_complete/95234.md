inca:/ˈɪŋkə/| n. |印加人；印加帝国|A member of a South American Indian people living in the central Andes before the Spanish conquest.|The Inca built Machu Picchu high in the Andes Mountains. 印加人在安第斯山脉的高处建造了马丘比丘。|近义词：Peruvian 秘鲁人|反义词: Spanish 西班牙人|词性:noun

词源: From Quechua *Inka ‘ruler, king’.
