exonerator:/ɪɡˈzɑːnəreɪtər/ | n. | 免罪者，证明无罪者 |  A person who exonerates someone, clearing them of blame or guilt. | The lawyer acted as his client's exonerator, presenting evidence that proved his innocence.  律师充当了他委托人的辩护者，提供了证明他无罪的证据。|  近义词: defender, advocate, vindicator | 反义词: accuser, prosecutor | noun

exonerate:/ɪɡˈzɑːnəreɪt/ | v. | 免除…的罪责；证明…的清白 | To clear someone of blame or guilt. | The evidence exonerated him from all charges. 证据使他免于所有指控。| 近义词: absolve, acquit, vindicate | 反义词: incriminate, condemn | verb
