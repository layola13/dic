dowsing:/ˈdaʊzɪŋ/| n.|探测（地下水源等）|the practice of searching for underground water, minerals, or other hidden objects using a forked stick, a pendulum, or similar tools. |例句：He tried dowsing for water on his property. 他尝试在他的土地上探测水源。

例句：Dowsing is considered by many to be pseudoscience. 许多人认为探测术是伪科学。|近义词：divining, water witching|反义词:|词性:noun
