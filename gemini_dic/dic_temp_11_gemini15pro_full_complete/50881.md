desert-boots:/ˈdɛzət buːts/
noun
沙漠靴
A type of ankle-high suede boot with crepe rubber soles, originally worn by British officers in the Western Desert Campaign of World War II.
He wore his desert boots and khakis. 他穿着他的沙漠靴和卡其裤。
近义词:chukka boots, ankle boots
反义词:None
词性:n.
