fictionalization:/ˌfɪkʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.|小说化；虚构化|The fictionalization of his life story made him a legend.  他的人生故事被小说化后，使他成为了一个传奇人物。|The movie is a fictionalization of events that actually happened. 这部电影是对真实事件的虚构化改编。|novelization, adaptation, dramatization|factualization, documentation|noun
Etymology->
From fictionalize + -ation.  From fictional + -ize. From fiction + -al. From Latin fictiō (“a feigning, fashioning, fiction”), from fingō (“I shape, fashion, feign”).
