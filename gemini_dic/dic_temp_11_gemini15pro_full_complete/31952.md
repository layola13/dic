chelura:/kəˈlʊərə/ | n. | 船蛆，凿木水虱 | A small crustacean that bores into submerged wood, causing damage to wooden structures. |  Chelura terebrans is a species of wood-boring crustacean. 船蛆（凿木水虱）是一种钻木甲壳类动物。| synonym: woodborer, gribble | antonym: None | noun

Etymology: From Modern Latin *Chelura*, likely a variant of *Chelyura*, perhaps influenced by Greek χηλή (khēlē, “claw, pincer”) + οὐρά (oura, “tail”).
