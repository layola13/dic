muffledly:/ˈmʌfldli/ | adv. | 闷声地，低沉地 | In a muffled way; with a sound that is not clear or loud because it has been blocked in some way. | She spoke muffledly into the pillow. 她闷声对着枕头说话。| Near synonyms: indistinctly, quietly, faintly, softly, dully | Near antonyms: clearly, loudly, distinctly | adverb

muffled:/ˈmʌfld/ | adj. | 闷的，低沉的 | (of a sound) quiet and unclear because it has been blocked in some way. | A muffled cough came from the next room. 从隔壁房间传来一阵低沉的咳嗽声。| Near synonyms: indistinct, quiet, faint, soft, dull | Near antonyms: clear, loud, distinct | adjective
