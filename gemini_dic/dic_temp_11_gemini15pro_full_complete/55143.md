dissecting:/daɪˈsɛktɪŋ/ /dɪˈsɛktɪŋ/
verb
解剖,仔细分析
Methodically cutting up (a plant or animal) in order to study its internal parts. Also, analyzing something in minute detail.
The students were dissecting a frog in biology class. 学生们在生物课上解剖青蛙。

The lawyer dissecting the witness's testimony. 律师仔细分析证人的证词。

近义词: analyzing, examining, scrutinizing, probing
反义词: synthesizing, constructing, combining

词性: verb (present participle of dissect)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin dissecare "to cut apart," from dis- "apart" + secare "to cut."
