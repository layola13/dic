footwork:/ˈfʊtwɜːk/| n. |步法，足技|Skill and agility in using one's feet, especially in sports or dancing. |例句：His footwork on the court was impressive. 他在球场上的步法令人印象深刻。

例句：The boxer's footwork allowed him to dodge his opponent's punches.  这位拳击手的步法使他能够躲避对手的拳击。|近义词：stepping, footwork, steps |反义词:clumsiness |noun
