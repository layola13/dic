brachiate: /ˈbreɪkiˌeɪt/ /ˈbrækɪˌeɪt/ | v. | 用手臂移动；臂跃； | To move by using the arms to swing from branch to branch, as some primates do. |  Monkeys brachiate through the trees. 猴子在树上臂跃。|近义词：swing, clamber |反义词: walk, crawl |动词

词源: From Latin *brachiātus, past participle of brachiāre ‘to swing the arms’, from brachium ‘arm’.
