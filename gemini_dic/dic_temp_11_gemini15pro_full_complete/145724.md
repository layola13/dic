overcredulous: /ˌoʊvərˈkrɛdʒələs/ | adj. | 过于轻信的，易受骗的 | Too ready to believe things, easily deceived. | Example: He was overcredulous and easily taken in by scams. 他过于轻信，很容易被骗局所欺骗。| Example:  Overcredulous investors lost their savings in the Ponzi scheme. 轻信的投资者在庞氏骗局中损失了他们的积蓄。 | Synonyms: gullible, naive, trusting | Antonyms: skeptical, cynical, suspicious | adjective
