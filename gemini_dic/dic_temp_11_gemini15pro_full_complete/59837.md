ectomorph:/ˈɛktəˌmɔrf/| n.| 瘦型体质者|A person with a lean and delicate body build characterized by long limbs and a fast metabolism. They tend to have difficulty gaining weight.|例句：Ectomorphs often struggle to build muscle mass. 瘦型体质的人通常难以增加肌肉质量。

例句：He's a classic ectomorph – tall, thin, and wiry. 他是典型的瘦型体质——高个子、瘦弱、结实。|近义词：skinny, slender, lean, thin|反义词:endomorph, mesomorph|词性:noun
