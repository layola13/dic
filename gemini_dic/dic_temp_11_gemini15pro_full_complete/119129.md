metroscope: /ˈmɛtrəˌskoʊp/ | n. | 万花筒；城市风光观察镜；潜望镜 | An optical instrument, somewhat resembling a kaleidoscope, for viewing enlarged images of scenes, buildings, etc., as from a considerable distance or from below the street level. | Example 1: Through the metroscope, the city's vibrant nightlife unfolded before her eyes. 通过万花筒，这座城市充满活力的夜生活在她眼前展开。

Example 2:  The old metroscope offered a unique perspective of the bustling street below.  这个古老的城市风光观察镜提供了一个独特的视角来观察下面繁忙的街道。| 近义词: kaleidoscope, periscope | 反义词:  None (No direct antonym exists for this specialized instrument) | 词性: noun
