dextrously:/ˈdɛkstrəsli/| adv.|灵巧地，熟练地|In a skillful and clever way, especially in handling things.|She dextrously maneuvered the car through the narrow streets. 她熟练地驾驶着汽车穿过狭窄的街道。|近义词：skillfully, adroitly, nimbly, deftly|反义词：clumsily, awkwardly|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dexter* (right, right-handed, skillful) -> Old French *destre* -> Middle English *dexterous* -> dextrously
