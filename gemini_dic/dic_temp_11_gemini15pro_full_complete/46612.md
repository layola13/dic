damiana:/ˌdæmiˈɑːnə/ | n. | 达米阿那,特纳草 | An aromatic shrub (Turnera diffusa or Turnera aphrodisiaca) native to Mexico and the southern US, the leaves of which are used to make a tea and other beverages believed to have aphrodisiac and stimulant properties.  |  Damiana tea is popular in some cultures for its purported health benefits.  达米阿那茶因其所谓的健康益处在一些文化中很受欢迎。 | synonyms:  turnera, aphrodisiac, stimulant | antonyms:  depressant, sedative | noun

damiana:/ˌdæmiˈɑːnə/ | n. | 达米阿那酒 | A liqueur flavored with damiana. |  He ordered a damiana cocktail. 他点了一杯达米阿那鸡尾酒。 | synonyms:  liqueur, cordial | antonyms:  None | noun
