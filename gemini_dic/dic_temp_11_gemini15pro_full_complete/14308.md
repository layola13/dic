automate:/ˈɔːtəmeɪt/ (US) /ˈɔːtəmæt/ (UK)| v. |使自动化，自动操作|To convert (a process or facility) to largely automatic operation.|The factory is now fully automated.  这家工厂现在已经完全自动化了。|近义词：mechanize, computerize|反义词: manualize|verb

automate:/ˈɔːtəmeɪt/ (US) /ˈɔːtəmæt/ (UK)| adj. |自动化的|Operated largely automatically.|An automated teller machine. 自动取款机。|近义词：automatic, mechanized|反义词: manual|adjective

automation:/ˌɔːtəˈmeɪʃn/ (US) /ˌɔːtəˈmeɪʃən/ (UK)| n. |自动化|The technique, method, or system of operating or controlling a process by highly automatic means, as by electronic devices, reducing human intervention to a minimum.|Automation has led to job losses in some industries. 自动化导致了一些行业的失业。|近义词：mechanization, computerization|反义词: manual operation|noun
