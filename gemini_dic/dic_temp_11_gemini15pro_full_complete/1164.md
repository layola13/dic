acclaiming:/əˈkleɪmɪŋ/
verb
称赞；赞扬；给予高度评价
praising enthusiastically and publicly.
The critics are acclaiming her performance as the best of the year. 评论家们称赞她的表演是年度最佳。
近义词: praising, applauding, cheering, hailing, lauding
反义词: criticizing, condemning, denouncing
词性: present participle of acclaim

acclaim:/əˈkleɪm/
verb, noun
(v.)称赞；赞扬；(n.) 称赞; 赞扬
(v.) praise enthusiastically and publicly; (n.) enthusiastic and public praise.
(v.) The novel was acclaimed as a masterpiece. 这部小说被誉为杰作。
(n.) She received great acclaim for her performance. 她的表演获得了极大的赞誉。
近义词: (v.) praise, applaud, cheer, hail, laud; (n.) praise, applause, ovation
反义词: (v.) criticize, condemn, denounce; (n.) criticism, condemnation
词性: verb, noun

词源: From Latin acclamare "to shout at, applaud," from ad- "to" + clamare "to cry out."
