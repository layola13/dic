baksheesh:/bækˈʃiːʃ/| n.| 小费，赏钱|A tip or gratuity (often in countries where such payments are customary).|He offered the waiter a baksheesh. 他给了服务员小费。|Baksheesh is expected in some cultures. 在某些文化中，小费是意料之中的事。|近义词: tip, gratuity, reward|反义词: fine, penalty|词性:名词

词源: From Urdu bakhshīsh, from Persian بخشش (bakhshesh, “gift, forgiveness”), from بخشیدن (bakhshidan, “to give, to forgive”).
