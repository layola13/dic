eulogizing: /ˈjuːləˌdʒaɪzɪŋ/
verb
赞颂，歌颂
praising someone or something very highly, usually in speech or writing, especially someone who has just died
He delivered a moving eulogy, eulogizing her courage and determination. 他发表了感人的悼词，赞扬了她的勇气和决心。
Synonyms: praising, extolling, lauding, commending
Antonyms: criticizing, condemning, denouncing
词性: 动词 (gerund or present participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek eulogia "praise,"  from eu- "well" + -logia "speaking," from logos "word, speech."
