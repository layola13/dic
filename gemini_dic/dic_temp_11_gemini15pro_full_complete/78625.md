gibed:/dʒaɪbd/ | v. | 嘲讽，讥讽 | To make mocking or sarcastic remarks; to taunt. |  He gibed at his friend's new haircut. 他嘲笑了朋友的新发型。

| The comedian gibed the politician's policies.  喜剧演员讽刺了政治家的政策。

| 近义词: mocked, taunted, ridiculed, jeered, sneered | 反义词: praised, complimented, flattered | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *geben*, perhaps of imitative origin, related to *gabben* "to mock," possibly influenced by Old Norse *geipa* "to gape, open wide," referring to opening the mouth in mockery.
