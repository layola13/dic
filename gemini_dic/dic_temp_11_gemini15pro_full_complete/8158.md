|n.| 反创造论的（观点、立场等）|The possessive form of "anti-creationism," referring to something belonging to or associated with the opposition to creationism.  It signifies the stance, arguments, or movement against the belief that the universe and living organisms originate from specific acts of divine creation, as in the biblical account, rather than by natural processes such as evolution.| Anti-creationism's core argument rests on scientific evidence. 反创造论的核心论点基于科学证据。|近义词:evolutionary theory's, scientific materialism's|反义词:creationism's, intelligent design's|词性:名词
