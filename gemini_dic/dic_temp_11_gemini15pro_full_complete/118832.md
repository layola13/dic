metaphil:/ˈmɛtəfɪl/
n.|超凡爱好者；追求卓越者|A lover of learning and wisdom, especially one who pursues knowledge beyond the ordinary. |He was a true metaphil, constantly seeking deeper understanding of the world around him. 他是一个真正的超凡爱好者，不断地寻求对周围世界的更深层次的理解。|近义词: polymath, intellectual, philosopher |反义词: philistine, ignoramus|词性: 名词


Etymology:
From Greek *μετά (meta, “beyond, after, with, among”) + *φίλος (phílos, “dear, beloved, loving”). Coined in the late 19th or early 20th century. 词源：源自希腊语 μετά（meta，“超越、之后、与、在…之中”）+ φίλος（phílos，“亲爱的、心爱的、爱…的”）。创造于19世纪末或20世纪初。
