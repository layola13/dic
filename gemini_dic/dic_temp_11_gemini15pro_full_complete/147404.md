overnoise:/ˌoʊvərˈnɔɪz/| v. |淹没；压过（噪音）|to drown out or overwhelm with noise|The loud music overnoised our conversation.  喧闹的音乐淹没了我们的谈话。|近义词：drown out, overwhelm, blast out|反义词: be quiet, be silent|动词

overnoise:/ˌoʊvərˈnɔɪz/| n. |(压倒性的)噪音|overpowering or excessive noise|The overnoise of the traffic made it hard to hear. 交通的噪音让人难以听到。|近义词：din, racket, clamor|反义词: silence, quiet|名词
