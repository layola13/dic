overdomesticate: /ˌoʊvərdoʊˈmɛstɪkeɪt/ | v. | 过度驯化 | To domesticate (an animal or plant) to an excessive degree, resulting in loss of original traits and reduced ability to thrive in natural environments. |  Humans have overdomesticated some dog breeds to the point where they suffer from numerous health problems. 人类过度驯化了一些犬种，以至于它们患有许多健康问题。

|  Overdomesticating crops can make them more susceptible to pests and diseases. 过度驯化农作物会使它们更容易受到病虫害的侵害。| 近义词: overbreed | 反义词: wild, undomesticated | 词性: 动词
