kowtow:/ˌkaʊˈtaʊ/ /ˈkaʊˌtaʊ/ | vi. n.|叩头，磕头；卑躬屈膝，唯命是从|To kneel and touch the ground with the forehead in worship or submission, or to act in an excessively subservient manner.|He kowtowed to the emperor. 他向皇帝叩头。| 近义词：grovel, fawn, prostrate oneself|反义词：defy, resist|verb
