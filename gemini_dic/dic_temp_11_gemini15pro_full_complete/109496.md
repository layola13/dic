leptons:/ˈlɛptɒnz/ (US) /ˈlɛptɒnz/ (UK)| n.|轻子|A fundamental particle, such as an electron, muon, or tau, that does not participate in the strong interaction.|Electrons are the most common type of leptons. 电子是最常见的轻子类型。|Leptons are involved in weak interactions, such as beta decay. 轻子参与弱相互作用，例如β衰变。|synonyms: fundamental particles, elementary particles|antonyms: baryons, hadrons|noun
