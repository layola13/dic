gobble:/ˈɡɑːbl/ (US) /ˈɡɒbl/ (UK)| v. n.|狼吞虎咽，吞噬；火鸡叫声|To eat something quickly and noisily.|He gobbled down his dinner in five minutes. 他五分钟内就把晚饭狼吞虎咽地吃完了.|近义词：devour, gulp, guzzle|反义词：nibble, savor|词性：动词、名词

gobble:/ˈɡɑːbl/ (US) /ˈɡɒbl/ (UK)| n.|火鸡叫声|The characteristic cry of a turkey.|The gobble of the wild turkey echoed through the forest. 野生火鸡的叫声在森林中回荡。|近义词：cluck, cry|反义词：-|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *gobelen, *gobelyn, of imitative origin.
