all-pervading:/ɔːl-pərˈveɪdɪŋ/
adjective
普遍存在的；弥漫的；无所不在的
present everywhere; permeating everything
The all-pervading smell of woodsmoke filled the air.  空气中弥漫着木烟的味道。

The all-pervading sense of doom hung heavy over the town. 笼罩在小镇上空挥之不去的厄运感让人感到沉重。

近义词: pervasive, ubiquitous, omnipresent, widespread, rife
反义词: localized, limited, contained, restricted
词性: adjective
