altiloquent:/ˌæltɪˈloʊkwənt/| adj.|高谈阔论的；辞藻华丽的|Speaking in or characterized by a high-flown, pompous, or bombastic style; inflated or pretentious in language.|His altiloquent speech impressed no one; it was merely verbose and empty.|近义词：bombastic, grandiloquent, pompous|反义词：simple, plain, unpretentious|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *altus* (high) + *loqui* (to speak).
