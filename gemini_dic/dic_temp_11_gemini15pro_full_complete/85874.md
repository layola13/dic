headbanger:/ˈhedˌbæŋər/| n.|甩头族；疯狂摇滚乐迷|A person who enjoys heavy metal music and often shakes their head vigorously to the music.|The concert was full of headbangers enjoying the loud music. 音乐会挤满了享受大声音乐的甩头族。|Synonyms: metalhead, mosher|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps someone who dislikes loud music)|noun

headbanger:/ˈhedˌbæŋər/| n.|撞头的人|A person who repeatedly bangs their head, often due to frustration, anger, or a medical condition.|The child, in a fit of frustration, became a headbanger against the wall.  孩子在沮丧中，开始用头撞墙。|Synonyms: (No direct synonym for this sense)|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for this sense)|noun
