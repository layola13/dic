duck:/dʌk/| n. v.|鸭子; 俯身; 躲避;|A waterbird with a broad blunt bill, short legs, webbed feet, and a waddling gait.|例句：The duck quacked loudly. 鸭子大声地叫。|近义词：waterfowl, bird|反义词: (无明确反义词)|noun
duck:/dʌk/| v.|快速低头或弯腰; 躲避; 推卸责任;|To lower the head or body suddenly; to avoid or evade.|例句：He ducked to avoid being hit by the ball. 他低头躲避被打到。|近义词：dodge, avoid, evade|反义词: confront, face|verb
