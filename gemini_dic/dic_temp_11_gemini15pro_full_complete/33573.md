ciliata:/ˌsɪliˈɑːtə/ /ˌsɪliˈeɪtə/ | n. |纤毛虫 |A ciliate is a single-celled animal that has tiny hair-like structures called cilia, which are used for movement and feeding.| Paramecium is a common ciliate found in freshwater. 草履虫是一种常见的淡水纤毛虫。|Ciliates play an important role in aquatic ecosystems. 纤毛虫在水生生态系统中发挥着重要作用。|近义词: protozoan |反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, as it is a specific biological classification.)| noun
