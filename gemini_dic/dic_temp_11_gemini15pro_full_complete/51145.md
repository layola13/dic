despondence:/dɪˈspɒndəns/ /dɪˈspɑːndəns/| n. |沮丧，绝望|A state of low spirits caused by loss of hope or courage.|例句：He sank into deep despondence after failing the exam. 他考试不及格后陷入了深深的沮丧之中。

例句：Her despondence was evident in her slumped posture and tear-stained face.  她的沮丧从她颓丧的姿势和泪痕斑斑的脸上显而易见。

近义词：despair, hopelessness, discouragement, gloom, melancholy|反义词:hope, optimism, cheerfulness, joy|词性:名词
