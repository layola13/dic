nucleolus:/n(j)uːˈkliːələs/, /n(j)uːˈkliːoʊləs/ | n. | 核仁 | A small, round structure found inside the nucleus of a cell. It is involved in the production of ribosomes. | The nucleolus is clearly visible under a microscope.  核仁在显微镜下清晰可见。

The nucleolus plays a crucial role in protein synthesis. 核仁在蛋白质合成中起着至关重要的作用。 |  Near synonyms:  nucleolar organizer | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
