midwifing:/ˈmɪdˌwaɪfɪŋ/
n.|助产；接生；协助；促成|The act of assisting in childbirth or the development of something.|She studied midwifing in college. 她在大学学习助产。|近义词：assisting, supporting, facilitating|反义词: hindering, obstructing|noun

midwife:/ˈmɪdˌwaɪf/
n. vt.|助产士；接生员；促成…的形成|A person, typically a woman, who is trained to assist women in childbirth.  To assist in the birth or development of something.|The midwife helped deliver the baby. 助产士帮助接生了婴儿。  She midwifed the development of the new program. 她促成了新项目的开发。|近义词：obstetrician, birth attendant (for the person), assist, support, facilitate (for the verb)|反义词: hinder, obstruct (for the verb)|noun, verb
