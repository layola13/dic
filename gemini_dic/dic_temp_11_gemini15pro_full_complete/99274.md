interconnecting:/ˌɪntərˈkəˈnɛktɪŋ/
| adj. | 互相连接的，互相联系的 | Connecting with each other, joining together. | The rooms are interconnected by a series of passageways. 这些房间通过一系列通道相互连接。| The various systems are interconnected and interdependent. 各种系统相互连接，相互依存。 | linked, joined, connected, networked | separate, isolated, detached | adjective

interconnect:/ˌɪntərˈkəˈnɛkt/
| v. |  (使)互相连接，(使)互相联系 | To connect with one another. | The two highways interconnect just north of the city. 两条高速公路就在城市的北边相连。 | The computer systems are interconnected to share data. 计算机系统相互连接以共享数据。 | connect, link, join, network | separate, disconnect, isolate | verb
