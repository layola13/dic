moby:/ˈmoʊbi/| adj. | 巨大的，庞大的 |  Of, relating to, or resembling a whale, especially in size; huge; massive.  Derived from Herman Melville's novel "Moby Dick," about a giant white whale. |  The new aircraft carrier is a moby piece of engineering.  这艘新的航空母舰是一个巨大的工程奇迹。 |  enormous, colossal, gigantic, immense, massive | small, tiny, minuscule | adjective

moby:/ˈmoʊbi/| n. |  A white whale; something huge or massive. | Referring to the white whale in Herman Melville's novel "Moby Dick." Can be used informally to refer to something large and imposing. | That SUV is a real moby!  那辆SUV真大！| behemoth, leviathan, giant | dwarf, miniature | noun
