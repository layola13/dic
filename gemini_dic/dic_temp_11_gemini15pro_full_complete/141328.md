openings:/ˈoʊpənɪŋz/| n.|开口; 空缺; 开端; 机会|An opening is a hole or gap in something, or an unoccupied job or position, or the beginning of something, or an opportunity.|The wall had several openings.  墙上有几个开口。|synonyms:  apertures, gaps, vacancies, positions, beginnings, opportunities, chances|antonyms: closings, endings, blockages|noun

openings:/ˈoʊpənɪŋz/| n.|开幕; 开放; 落成典礼|An opening can refer to the start of an official event, such as an exhibition or a new building.|The opening of the new museum was a grand affair. 新博物馆的开幕式非常隆重。|synonyms:  launches, inaugurations, premieres|antonyms: closings, finales|noun
