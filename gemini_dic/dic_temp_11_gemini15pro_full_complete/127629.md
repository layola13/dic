needlepoint:/ˈniːdlˌpɔɪnt/| n.| 刺绣；针尖；绣花绷架；[植]针尖|A form of embroidery worked with a needle and thread on canvas, typically with a design covering the entire surface.  A sharp point, like that of a needle. | She spent hours creating intricate designs in needlepoint. 她花了几个小时用针绣创作复杂的图案。 The needlepoint of the compass pointed north. 指南针的针尖指向北方。|Synonyms: embroidery, stitchery, needlework, tapestry |Antonyms: (For the craft sense) None. (For the point sense) bluntness, dullness| noun

needlepoint:/ˈniːdlˌpɔɪnt/| adj.| 尖锐的；精确的|Extremely precise or sharp. |He made a needlepoint turn in his car. 他开车来了个急转弯。 His arguments were needlepoint sharp. 他的论点非常尖锐。|Synonyms: sharp, precise, acute, keen |Antonyms: blunt, dull, vague, imprecise| adjective
