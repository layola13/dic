nodular:/ˈnɒdjʊlə(r)/ (US) /ˈnɑːdʒələr/| adj.|结节状的；有结节的|Characterized by or having nodules (small, rounded lumps or swellings).|The doctor discovered a nodular growth on the patient's thyroid. 医生在病人的甲状腺上发现了一个结节状的增生物。|
|Synonyms: lumpy, bumpy, knobby|Antonyms: smooth, even|adjective|

nodule:/ˈnɒdjuːl/ (US) /ˈnɑːdʒuːl/| n.|结节；小结|A small, rounded lump or swelling, especially one on a plant or in the body.|The biopsy revealed that the nodule was benign. 活检显示结节是良性的。|
|Synonyms: lump, bump, knob|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "absence of nodules")|noun|
