nonperseverance:/ˌnɒnpɜːsəˈvɪərəns/
n.|缺乏毅力，不能坚持|The lack of ability to continue in a course of action even in the face of difficulties or with the required diligence.|His nonperseverance led to the failure of the project. 他的缺乏毅力导致了项目的失败。|Synonyms:  instability, fickleness, vacillation|Antonyms: perseverance, persistence, tenacity|noun
