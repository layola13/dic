megascans:/ˈmɛɡəskænz/| n.| 超级扫描，百万扫描 (通常指 Quixel Megascans 高精度扫描的纹理和模型库)|A vast library of high-quality, PBR scanned assets, including textures, models, and sounds, used for creating realistic digital environments and objects.|Example 1: The artist used Megascans assets to create a photorealistic forest scene. 艺术家用百万扫描资源库创建了一个逼真的森林场景。

Example 2:  Megascans has revolutionized the way environments are created in video games and films. 百万扫描彻底改变了电子游戏和电影中环境的创建方式。|近义词:photogrammetry, 3D scanning, asset library|反义词:hand-painted textures, procedural generation|词性:名词
