maki-zushi:/ˈmɑːki ˈzuːʃi/ /ˈmæki ˈsuːʃi/ | n. | 巻き寿司 (Makizushi), rolled sushi | A type of sushi made by wrapping vinegared rice and various fillings in nori (dried seaweed) and rolling it into a cylindrical shape using a bamboo mat called a makisu.  It's then sliced into bite-sized pieces. | Example: My favorite maki-zushi is the California roll. 我最喜欢的寿司是加州卷。

Example:  We ordered a platter of assorted maki-zushi for the party.  我们为聚会点了一盘什锦寿司。 | 近义词: sushi roll, norimaki (海苔巻き) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
