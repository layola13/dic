incubative:/ˈɪŋkjuːbeɪtɪv/
adjective
孵化的；潜伏的；酝酿的
Relating to incubation; the process of keeping something at a suitable temperature and humidity so that it develops.  Also, in a figurative sense, referring to a period of development before something happens.
The virus has a long incubative period, making it difficult to track. 这种病毒潜伏期很长，因此难以追踪。
synonyms: developmental, formative, nascent, latent, dormant
antonyms: active, manifest, apparent
词性: 形容词
