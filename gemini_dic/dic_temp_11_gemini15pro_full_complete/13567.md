attended:/əˈtɛndɪd/| v.|出席，参加，照料，护理|She attended the meeting yesterday. 她昨天参加了会议。|近义词：present, participate, go to, be present at, care for, look after|反义词:absent, miss, ignore, neglect|verb

Etymology->
From Old French *atendre, from Latin attendere "to give heed to, pay attention to," from ad- "to" + tendere "to stretch, extend".
