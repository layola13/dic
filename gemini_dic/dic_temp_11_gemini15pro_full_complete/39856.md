constancy:/ˈkɒnstənsi/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnstənsi/ (US)| n. | 恒久不变；坚定；忠诚；持久性 |The quality of being unchanging or unwavering, especially in love, loyalty, or purpose. |She showed great constancy in her devotion to her husband. 她对丈夫的忠诚始终如一。|近义词：fidelity, loyalty, steadfastness, perseverance, dedication|反义词：inconstancy, fickleness, variability, instability|词性:名词

词源:
From Late Middle English constance, from Old French constance, from Latin constantia (“firmness, steadfastness”), from constans, present participle of constare (“to stand firm”).
