economize: /ɪˈkɑːnəˌmaɪz/ /iˈkɒnəmaɪz/
|v.|节省，节约，有效利用|To reduce spending or use resources carefully.|例句：They had to economize on fuel because of the rising prices. 由于价格上涨，他们不得不节省燃料。|近义词：save, conserve, cut back, retrench|反义词：waste, squander, splurge|verb

economized: /ɪˈkɑːnəˌmaɪzd/ /iˈkɒnəmaɪzd/
|v.|（过去式和过去分词）节省，节约，有效利用|Past tense and past participle of "economize".|例句：We economized on heating by wearing sweaters indoors. 我们通过在室内穿毛衣来节省暖气。|近义词：saved, conserved, cut back on, retrenched|反义词：wasted, squandered, splurged|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French économiser, from économie "economy," from Greek oikonomia "household management," from oikos "house" + nemein "to manage."
