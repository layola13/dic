indiscriminating:/ˌɪndɪˈskrɪmɪneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
不加选择的，不加鉴别的，不分皂白的
Showing a lack of care or judgment in making choices.
例句：She has indiscriminate taste in music. 她对音乐的品味不加选择。

例句：He's indiscriminate in his choice of friends. 他在选择朋友方面不加鉴别。

近义词：unselective, undiscerning, uncritical, random, haphazard
反义词：selective, discerning, discriminating, critical, careful, judicious
词性 adjective


indiscriminate:/ˌɪndɪˈskrɪmɪnət/
adjective
不加选择的，不加鉴别的，不分皂白的
Done at random or without careful judgment.
例句：Indiscriminate attacks on civilians are unacceptable.  不分皂白地攻击平民是不可接受的。

例句：The indiscriminate use of pesticides can harm the environment.  滥用杀虫剂会破坏环境。

近义词：unselective, undiscerning, uncritical, random, haphazard
反义词：selective, discerning, discriminating, critical, careful, judicious
词性 adjective
