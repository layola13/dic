maladministrator:/ˌmælədˈmɪnɪstreɪtər/
|n.|管理不善者；滥用职权者|A person who manages affairs badly or improperly, especially a public official.|The maladministrator was fired after the audit revealed his misuse of funds. 滥用职权者在审计揭露他滥用资金后被解雇了。|Synonyms: mismanager, incompetent, bungler|Antonyms: competent administrator, efficient manager|Noun

maladministration:/ˌmælədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən/
|n.|管理不善；滥用职权|The act of managing affairs badly or improperly, especially by a public official.|The report exposed the maladministration of the city's finances.  报告揭露了该市财政管理不善的情况。|Synonyms: mismanagement, incompetence, bungling|Antonyms: good governance, efficient management|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From mal- (bad, ill) + administration.  "Mal-" is from French, ultimately from Latin "malus" (bad). "Administration" is from Old French, from Latin "administratio," from "administrare" (to manage).
