harkener's:/ˈhɑːrkənərz/| n.|倾听者(的),  听众(的)|harkener's refers to something belonging to or associated with a harkener, someone who listens attentively.|The harkener's rapt attention flattered the speaker.  听众的全神贯注让演讲者感到受宠若惊。|近义词：listener's, auditor's|反义词:speaker's, talker's|词性:名词所有格

Etymology:
From harkener + -'s.  Harkener itself comes from the verb "hark," meaning "to listen attentively," which has Old English origins.
