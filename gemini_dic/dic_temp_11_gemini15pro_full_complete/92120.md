hypersceptical:/ˌhaɪpərˈskeptɪkl/ |adj.|极度怀疑的，非常怀疑的|Excessively or extremely skeptical; having or showing an extreme lack of belief.|例句：He was hypersceptical about the government's claims. 他对政府的说法极度怀疑。|例句：She gave me a hypersceptical look when I told her I'd won the lottery. 当我告诉她我中了彩票时，她给了我一个极度怀疑的眼神。|近义词：extremely skeptical, incredulous, distrustful, doubting|反义词：credulous, gullible, trusting|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From hyper- + skeptical.  "Hyper-" is a prefix meaning "over, above, beyond"  derived from Greek "hyper" (ὑπέρ). "Skeptical" comes from Greek "skeptikos" (σκεπτικός), meaning "inquiring, thoughtful," ultimately from "skeptesthai" (σκέπτεσθαι), "to look, consider."
