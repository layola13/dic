harmlessly:/ˈhɑːmlɪsli/| adv.|无害地，安全地|Without causing harm or damage.  It describes the manner in which something is done in a way that doesn't cause harm.|The spider, though large, is harmlessly. 虽然这只蜘蛛很大，但它是无害的。|近义词：safely, innocuously, inoffensively|反义词：dangerously, harmfully|词性: 副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From harm + -less + -ly.  "Harm" comes from Old English "hearm," meaning "injury, grief, pain." The suffix "-less" indicates a lack of something, and "-ly" transforms the adjective "harmless" into an adverb.
