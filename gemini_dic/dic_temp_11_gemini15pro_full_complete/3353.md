affricate:/ˈæfrɪkət/ | n. | 塞擦音 | A consonant sound that begins as a stop and releases as a fricative, such as the sounds /tʃ/ and /dʒ/. |  Example: The sounds "ch" in "chair" and "j" in "judge" are affricates.  椅子中的“ch”和judge中的“j”是塞擦音。| 近义词: None (as it's a specific phonetic term) | 反义词: None (as it's a specific phonetic term) | 词性: Noun
