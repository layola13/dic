nonderivable:/ˌnɒndɪˈraɪvəbl/ | adj. | 不可推导的，不可导出的 | Not able to be derived or deduced; not derivable. |  This conclusion is nonderivable from the given premises.  这个结论无法从给定的前提中推导出来。

The theorem states that certain properties are nonderivable in this system.  该定理指出某些属性在这个系统中是不可推导的。 | 近义词：indeducible, unconcludable | 反义词：derivable, deducible | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (not) + derivable (able to be derived)  来自拉丁语 derivare,  意为 "to draw off, divert,"  字面意思是 "to draw (water) from a stream."
