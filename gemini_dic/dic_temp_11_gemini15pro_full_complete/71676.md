flatworm:/ˈflætˌwɜːrm/ (US), /ˈflætˌwɜːm/ (UK)| n. |扁形虫，扁虫|A worm of the phylum Platyhelminthes, characterized by a flattened body and bilateral symmetry.  They include planarians, flukes, and tapeworms.|Example: The flatworm regenerated after being cut in half. 这条扁虫被切成两半后再生了。|Synonym: platyhelminth|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps a roundworm as a contrasting example)|noun

flatworm:/ˈflætˌwɜːrm/ (US), /ˈflætˌwɜːm/ (UK)| adj. |扁形的|Having a flattened body shape, like a flatworm.|Example: The creature had a flatworm-like appearance.  这个生物有着像扁虫一样的扁平外形。|Synonym: flattened, planar|Antonym: rounded, cylindrical|adjective
