obese:/oʊˈbiːs/| adj.|肥胖的，过胖的|Grossly fat or overweight. Characterized by excessive accumulation of body fat.|例句：He was morbidly obese. 他患有病态肥胖。

例句：Childhood obesity is a growing problem. 儿童肥胖是一个日益严重的问题。|近义词：overweight, fat, corpulent, portly|反义词：thin, slim, slender, lean|词性:形容词
obesity:/oʊˈbiːsəti/| n.|肥胖，肥胖症|The condition of being grossly fat or overweight.|例句：Obesity is a major health concern. 肥胖是一个主要的健康问题。

例句：The prevalence of obesity has increased dramatically in recent years. 近年来肥胖症的患病率急剧上升。|近义词：adiposity, corpulence|反义词：thinness, slenderness|词性:名词
