freeholders:/ˈfriːhoʊldərz/
n.
自由持有人；自耕农
A freeholder is someone who owns land or property freehold.
例句：The freeholders of the town met to discuss the new development. 城镇的自由持有人开会讨论新的发展项目。

例句：Many freeholders were concerned about the impact of the new road on their property values. 许多自由持有人担心新道路对其房产价值的影响。

近义词：landowner, property owner
反义词：tenant, lessee
词性：名词
