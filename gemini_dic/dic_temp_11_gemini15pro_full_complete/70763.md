finespun:/ˈfaɪnspʌn/| adj.|精细的；细密的；微妙的；过于精巧的；牵强的|Made of very fine threads; delicately woven. Also used figuratively to describe something subtle, intricate, or excessively refined. |例句：She wore a finespun silk scarf. 她戴着一条精细的丝绸围巾。

例句：His arguments were too finespun for the average person to understand. 他的论点过于精巧，一般人难以理解。|近义词：delicate, subtle, intricate, refined, gossamer, diaphanous|反义词：coarse, crude, simple, obvious|词性:形容词
