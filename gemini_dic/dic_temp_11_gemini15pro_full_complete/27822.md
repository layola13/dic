canoe:/kəˈnuː/| n. v.|独木舟，划独木舟|A narrow, lightweight boat pointed at both ends, propelled by paddles.|We rented a canoe and paddled across the lake. 我们租了一艘独木舟，划过湖面。|近义词：kayak, boat, raft|反义词:|n., v.

canoe:/kəˈnuː/| v. |划独木舟|To travel in or paddle a canoe.|They canoed down the river for three days. 他们划着独木舟顺流而下三天。|近义词：kayak, paddle, boat|反义词:|v.
