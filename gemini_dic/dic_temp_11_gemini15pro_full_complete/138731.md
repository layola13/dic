numberless:/ˈnʌmbərləs/
adjective
无数的；数不清的
Too numerous to be counted.  Incalculably large.
例句：The stars in the sky are numberless.  天上的星星数不胜数。

例句：He faced numberless difficulties in his research. 他在研究中面临着无数的困难。

近义词：countless, innumerable, myriad, infinite
反义词：few, countable, limited, finite
词性：adjective
