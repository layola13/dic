maral:/ˈmɑːrəl/| n.|马鹿（主要指中亚地区的雄性红鹿）|A large deer (Cervus elaphus maral) of Central Asia, the male of which has large, branched antlers.|The maral is prized for its antlers, which are used in traditional medicine.  马鹿因其鹿茸而被珍视，鹿茸用于传统医学。

The maral population has declined in recent years.  近年来，马鹿的数量有所下降。|近义词：red deer, elk, wapiti|反义词:|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Russian марал (maral), from a Turkic language (compare Kyrgyz маралы (maraly)).
