landed:/ˈlændɪd/ (US), /ˈlæn.dɪd/ (UK)| adj. v.|着陆的，登陆的；拥有土地的；（非正式）获得，得到|Landed is the past participle of the verb "land." As an adjective, it describes something that has landed or possesses land. Informally, it can also mean to have acquired or obtained something, often something desirable.|

例句：
The plane landed safely. = 飞机安全着陆。

The landed gentry were very powerful. = 地主阶级非常有势力。


He landed a great job. = 他找到了一份很棒的工作。


近义词：grounded, arrived, obtained, secured|反义词: airborne, departed, lost|词性: adjective, verb (past participle)


landed:/ˈlændɪd/ (US), /ˈlæn.dɪd/ (UK)| v.|降落, 登陆, 获得|Landed is the past tense and past participle of the verb "land," meaning to come down from the air or to arrive on land or shore. It can also mean to acquire or obtain something, particularly a job or opportunity.|

例句：
The bird landed on the branch. = 鸟落在树枝上。

She landed a leading role in the play. = 她获得了该剧的主角。


近义词：arrive, alight, touch down, acquire, obtain|反义词: depart, take off, lose|词性: verb (past tense, past participle)
