exploitationally:/ˌɛksplɔɪˈteɪʃənəli/ | adv. | 利用地，剥削地 | In a way that involves exploitation; in a manner that takes unfair advantage of someone or something for personal gain. | The company was accused of treating its workers exploitationally by paying them low wages and forcing them to work long hours.  该公司被指控剥削工人，支付低工资并强迫他们长时间工作。 |  近义词: abusively, unfairly, selfishly | 反义词: fairly, ethically, responsibly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From "exploitation" + "-al" + "-ly". "Exploitation" comes from the French "exploitation," from "exploiter" (to exploit), ultimately from Latin "explicitare" (to unfold, explain).
