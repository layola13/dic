monoecious:/məˈniːʃəs/ | adj. | 雌雄同株的 | Having both male and female reproductive organs in the same individual; hermaphroditic. | Corn is a monoecious plant, meaning that both male and female flowers are found on the same plant. 玉米是雌雄同株的植物，这意味着雄花和雌花都在同一株植物上。

Monoecious plants can self-pollinate, but they often rely on wind or insects for cross-pollination.  雌雄同株的植物可以自花授粉，但它们通常依靠风或昆虫进行异花授粉。| 近义词: hermaphroditic, bisexual | 反义词: dioecious | 词性: 形容词
