dentilabial:/ˌdɛntiˈleɪbiəl/| adj.|齿唇音的|A sound produced by placing the lower lip against the upper teeth, such as the sounds /f/ and /v/.|The sounds /f/ and /v/ are dentilabial fricatives. /f/和/v/是齿唇摩擦音。|近义词:labiodental (sometimes used interchangeably, though technically distinct)|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider velar, glottal, etc. depending on the context)|词性:adjective
