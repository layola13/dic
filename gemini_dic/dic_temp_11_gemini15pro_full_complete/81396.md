grasscycle:/ˈɡræsˌsaɪkəl/| v. |草屑堆肥；草循环|To leave grass clippings on the lawn after mowing, as a natural fertilizer.|Example: I decided to grasscycle this summer to improve my lawn's health.  我决定今年夏天进行草屑堆肥来改善我草坪的健康。|Example: Grasscycling not only saves time but also benefits the environment. 草屑堆肥不仅节省时间，而且有益于环境。|近义词：mulch, compost|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "bagging clippings")|词性:动词
