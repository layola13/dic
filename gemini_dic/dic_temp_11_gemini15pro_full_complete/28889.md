carpophyll: /ˈkɑːrpəfɪl/ | n. |  果叶，孢子叶 | A leaf that bears megasporangia (female spores) or microsporangia (male spores), often modified structurally compared to foliage leaves. | Example 1: The fern's carpophylls are distinctly different from its photosynthetic leaves.  蕨类的孢子叶与其光合作用的叶子明显不同。

Example 2:  The carpophylls of some plants are clustered together to form a cone.  一些植物的孢子叶聚集在一起形成球果。 | Synonym: sporophyll | Antonym: foliage leaf | noun
