detrimentally:/ˌdɛtrɪˈmɛntəli/
adverb
有害地
in a way that causes harm or damage.
Smoking affects your health detrimentally.  吸烟有害你的健康。
Her health has been detrimentally affected by the stress of her job.  她的健康受到了工作压力的不利影响。
近义词: harmfully, damagingly, negatively, adversely
反义词: beneficially, advantageously, positively
词性: adverb
词源: From detriment + -al + -ly. Ultimately from the Latin dētrīmentum.
