christs:/kraɪsts/ | n. | 基督徒们; 基督信徒们 | Plural form of Christ, referring to multiple individuals believed to be embodiments or representations of Christ.  While grammatically correct, its theological implications can be complex and depend on specific religious interpretations. |  In some mystical traditions, the idea of multiple "christs" might refer to individuals embodying Christ-like qualities. | 近义词: believers, followers, disciples | 反义词: nonbelievers, atheists | 词性: 名词
