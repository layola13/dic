nonhomogeneousnesses:/ˌnɒnˌhɒmədʒɪˈniːɪtɪnɪsɪz/ /ˌnɑːnˌhoʊmoʊdʒɪˈniːɪtɪnɪsɪz/
noun
非均匀性；不均一性
The state or quality of being nonhomogeneous; lack of homogeneity.  This refers to the variability or diversity within a system or substance.
The nonhomogeneousnesses of the soil sample made it difficult to analyze. 土壤样本的不均匀性使其难以分析。
The researcher noted the nonhomogeneousnesses in the population distribution. 研究人员注意到人口分布的不均匀性。
近义词: heterogeneity, diversity, variability, unevenness, disparity
反义词: homogeneity, uniformity, consistency, evenness
词性: noun
