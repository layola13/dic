epignathous:/ˌepɪɡˈneɪθəs/ | adj. | 上颚突出的，上颚过长的 | Having a projecting upper jaw; characterized by epignathy.  This term is used primarily in a biological or medical context to describe a skeletal feature. |  The skull exhibited epignathous characteristics, with the upper jaw extending significantly past the lower. 这个头骨呈现出上颚突出的特征，上颚明显地超过了下颚。

The epignathous jaw structure of the fish allows it to efficiently capture its prey.  这种鱼的上颚突出结构使其能够有效地捕获猎物。 | Near Synonyms: prognathous, overshot | Antonym: retrognathous | adjective
