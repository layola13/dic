depravers:/dɪˈpreɪvərz/| n.|堕落者；败坏者|People who corrupt or make others morally bad.  Those who cause the degradation of something.|The depravers of youth were brought to justice.  这些败坏青年的人被绳之以法。|Synonyms: corrupters, debauchers, perverters|Antonyms: reformers, improvers|noun

deprave:/dɪˈpreɪv/| v.|使堕落；败坏|To make someone morally bad; to corrupt. To reduce the quality or value of something.|Exposure to violence can deprave young minds.  接触暴力会败坏年轻人的思想。|Synonyms: corrupt, debauch, pervert|Antonyms: improve, elevate, reform|verb
