diphthonged:/ˈdɪfθɒŋd/ (UK) /ˈdɪfθɔːŋd/ (US)| adj.|双元音的，包含双元音的|Containing a diphthong (a sound formed by the combination of two vowels in a single syllable, in which the sound begins as one vowel and moves towards another).|The word "loud" is diphthonged, moving from /aʊ/.|近义词:None (highly specific term)|反义词:monophthongal|词性:adjective
