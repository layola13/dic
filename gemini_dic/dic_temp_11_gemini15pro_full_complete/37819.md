compensations:/ˌkɒmpenˈseɪʃənz/ | /ˌkɑːmpenˈseɪʃənz/ | n. | 补偿；赔偿金；赔偿物；报酬；薪酬 | something, typically money, awarded to someone as a recompense for loss, injury, or suffering.  |  The company offered compensations for the faulty products. 公司为缺陷产品提供了赔偿。 |  The court ordered the company to pay compensations to the victims. 法院责令该公司向受害者支付赔偿金. | synonyms: recompense, restitution, indemnity, remuneration, payment | antonyms: penalty, forfeiture, loss | noun
