hulkamania:/ˌhʌlkəˈmeɪniə/| n. |疯狂粉丝行为;狂热;赫克狂|Intense enthusiasm for professional wrestler Hulk Hogan; a craze.|Example: Hulkamania ran wild in the 1980s.  20世纪80年代，赫克狂潮席卷全球。|Example: The crowd erupted in hulkamania when Hogan entered the ring. 当霍根进入拳击场时，人群爆发出赫克狂潮。|Synonym: craze, mania, frenzy, obsession|Antonym: apathy, indifference|noun
