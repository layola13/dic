incorruptible:/ˌɪnkəˈrʌptɪbl/
adjective
廉洁的，不腐败的，正直的
Not susceptible to corruption, especially bribery; upright.
例句：He was known as an incorruptible judge. 他以廉洁的法官而闻名。

例句：She has an incorruptible spirit. 她有着正直的精神。

近义词：honest, upright, virtuous, unbribable, impeccable, probity
反义词：corrupt, dishonest, venal, bribable
词性: adjective


incorruptibility:/ˌɪnkəˌrʌptɪˈbɪləti/
noun
廉洁性，不可腐败性
The quality of being incorruptible.
例句：The incorruptibility of the judge was never questioned.  这位法官的廉洁从未受到质疑。

例句：The organization prides itself on its incorruptibility.  该组织以其廉洁而自豪。

近义词：honesty, integrity, probity, righteousness
反义词：corruption, dishonesty, venality
词性: noun


词源:
Late Middle English: from late Latin incorruptibilis, from in- ‘not’ + corrumpere ‘corrupt’.
