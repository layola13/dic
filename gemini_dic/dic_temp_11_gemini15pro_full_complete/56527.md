domicile: /ˈdɒmɪsaɪl/ (UK) /ˈdɑːməˌsaɪl/ (US)| n. vt.|住所，居所；定居|A person's fixed, permanent, and principal home for legal purposes.  It is the place where a person has their true, fixed, permanent home and principal establishment, and to which, whenever they are absent, they have the intention of returning. |She established her domicile in France. 她把住所定在了法国。|Synonyms: residence, abode, dwelling, home|Antonyms:  N/A|noun

domicile: /ˈdɒmɪsaɪl/ (UK) /ˈdɑːməˌsaɪl/ (US)| vt.|使定居，安置|To establish someone in a domicile. |The refugees were domiciled in temporary housing.  难民被安置在临时住所。|Synonyms:  settle, establish, reside|Antonyms:  N/A|verb
