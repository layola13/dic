a discourse on (关于...的论述):  a discourse on the history of art 关于艺术史的论述
engage in discourse (参与讨论): The two scholars engaged in a lively discourse. 两位学者进行了热烈的讨论。
political discourse (政治论述):  The current political discourse is highly polarized. 当前的政治论述高度两极分化。
academic discourse (学术论述):  Academic discourse often uses specialized vocabulary. 学术论述通常使用专门的词汇。
public discourse (公共讨论): This issue should be part of the public discourse. 这个问题应该成为公共讨论的一部分。
