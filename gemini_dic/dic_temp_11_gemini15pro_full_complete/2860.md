adversarinesses:/ˌædvərˈsɛrɪnɪsɪz/| n. |敌对状态，对抗性|The state of being hostile or opposed.|The political climate was marked by increasing adversarinesses between the two parties. 政治气氛的特点是双方对抗性日益增强。|近义词：hostilities, antagonisms, conflicts|反义词：friendliness, cooperation, amity|noun

Etymology:
From adversariness + -es (plural suffix).  Adversariness is from adversary + -ness. Adversary comes from Old French adversaire, from Latin adversarius "opponent, enemy," from adversus "turned toward, facing, opposite," past participle of advertere "to turn toward," from ad- "to" + vertere "to turn."
