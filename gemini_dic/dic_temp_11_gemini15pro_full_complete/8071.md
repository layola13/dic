anthropomorphite:/ˌænθrəpəˈmɔːrfaɪt/ | n. |拟人化者；赋予人性的事物|A being to which human form or attributes are ascribed, especially a deity.  A person who attributes human characteristics to non-human entities. |例句：Ancient Egyptians were anthropomorphites, believing their gods possessed human-like qualities. 古埃及人是拟人论者，相信他们的神拥有类似人类的特质。

例句：The author's portrayal of the talking animals was highly anthropomorphic. 作者对会说话的动物的刻画非常拟人化。| 近义词：humanizer, personifier | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
