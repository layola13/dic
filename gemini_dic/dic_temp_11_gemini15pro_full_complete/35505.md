coadjutrix:/ˌkoʊəˈdʒuːtrɪks/
noun
女助手，女帮手，女性协理主教
A female assistant or helper, especially a female assistant bishop.
She served as the bishop's coadjutrix for many years. 她多年来一直担任主教的助理。
The queen appointed her niece as her coadjutrix. 女王任命她的侄女为她的助手。
Synonyms: assistant, helper, aide, collaborator
Antonyms: opponent, adversary
n.


coadjutrix:/ˌkoʊəˈdʒuːtrɪks/
Etymology: From Late Latin *coadjutrix, feminine form of coadjutor, from co- "together" + adjutor "helper," from adjuvare "to help," from ad- "to" + juvare "to help."
