nonstrike:/ˈnɑːnstraɪk/ | adj. | 非罢工的，不罢工的 | Not involving or engaging in a strike; designed or intended to continue functioning during a strike.  | Example: Nonstrike clauses are often included in contracts to ensure continued service.  非罢工条款通常包含在合同中以确保服务的持续性。

| Example: The company hired nonstrike workers during the labor dispute.  该公司在劳资纠纷期间雇用了非罢工工人。 | Near Synonyms:  non-striking, anti-strike | Antonyms: striking | Word class: Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + strike.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "strike" refers to a work stoppage organized by workers.
