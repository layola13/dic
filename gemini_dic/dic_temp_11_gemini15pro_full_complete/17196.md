baulkiness:/ˈbɔːkɪnɪs/
n.
固执，倔强，不愿合作
The baulkiness of the mule made it difficult to control.  骡子的倔强使它难以控制。
The project was delayed due to the baulkiness of one of the partners.  由于其中一个合作伙伴的不配合，项目被推迟了。
Synonyms: obstinacy, stubbornness, reluctance, unwillingness, resistance
Antonyms: compliance, cooperation, willingness, amenability
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From baulky + -ness.  Baulky originates from balk (meaning "to stop short and refuse to proceed"), which likely comes from a Scandinavian source, related to Old Norse *balkr ("ridge, partition").
