expatiative: /ɪkˈspeɪʃiətɪv/ /ekˈspeɪʃiətɪv/
adjective
赎罪的; 补偿的
Serving to atone for something; making amends.
例句：He offered an expatiative gesture after bumping into her. 他在撞到她之后做了一个表示歉意的姿势。

例句：Her expatiative donation to the charity was substantial. 她为了赎罪向慈善机构捐了一大笔钱。

近义词：atonement, compensatory,  propitiatory,  redemptive
反义词：unrepentant, unforgiving


词性
adjective


词源
From Late Latin *expatiatus, past participle of expatiari (“to wander”), from ex (“out of”) + spatiari (“to walk”), from spatium (“space”).  The sense development is from "wandering from the right path" to "making amends for having wandered".
