nephridial:/nɪˈfrɪdiəl/
adjective|肾管的，排泄的|Relating to or resembling a nephridium, a tubular excretory organ found in many invertebrates.|Nephridial tubules filter waste products from the body cavity. 肾管过滤体腔中的废物。|
近义词:excretory, renal
反义词:(None -  lack of a direct antonym for a specific anatomical structure)
词性:adjective

nephridium:/nɪˈfrɪdiəm/
noun|肾管|A tubule open to the exterior that acts as an organ of excretion or osmoregulation in many invertebrates.|Earthworms have nephridia that filter waste from their blood. 蚯蚓有肾管过滤血液中的废物。|
近义词:protonephridium, metanephridium
反义词:(None -  lack of a direct antonym for a specific anatomical structure)
词性:noun
