encarpus:/ɛnˈkɑːrpəs/| adj.|内果皮的，果皮内的|Located or occurring within the pericarp or fruit wall.|The encarpus of this fruit is particularly thick.  这种水果的内果皮特别厚。|近义词: endocarpal|反义词: exocarpal|词性: adjective
Etymology: From Greek "en-" (in) + "karpos" (fruit).
