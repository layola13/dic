| Example:  Seeing everyone wearing jeans, she decided to join the majority and bought a pair for herself. 看到每个人都穿着牛仔裤，她也决定随大流给自己买了一条。 | Synonym: conform, follow the crowd, go with the flow | Antonym: dissent, disagree, stand out, be unique | verb
