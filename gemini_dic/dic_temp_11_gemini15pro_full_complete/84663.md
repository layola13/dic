handbrake:/ˈhændbreɪk/ (US) /ˈhændbreɪk/ (UK)| n.|手刹; 手闸|A lever used to apply the brakes on the rear wheels of a vehicle, typically used when parked or in an emergency.|Example: Remember to put on the handbrake when parking on a hill. 记住在山上停车时要拉上手刹。|Synonyms: parking brake, emergency brake|Antonyms: accelerator, gas pedal|noun


handbrake:/ˈhændbreɪk/ (US) /ˈhændbreɪk/ (UK)| v.|拉手刹|To apply the handbrake.|Example: He handbraked sharply to avoid hitting the dog. 他猛拉手刹以避免撞到狗。|Synonyms: apply the handbrake, engage the handbrake|Antonyms: release the handbrake, disengage the handbrake|verb
