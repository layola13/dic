adjective

Not marrying; unmarried, especially by choice.
She identified as nonmarrying and had no intention of ever getting married. 她认为自己是不婚主义者，并且从未打算结婚。
She is a successful, nonmarrying businesswoman. 她是一位成功的未婚女商人。
近义词: single, unmarried, unwed
反义词: married, wedded
词性: adjective

词源: non- + marrying (present participle of marry)
