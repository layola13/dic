magistratically:/ˌmædʒɪˈstreɪtɪkli/| adv.|专横地；威严地；有权威地|In a manner befitting a magistrate; authoritatively.  Acting or speaking in a way that suggests authority and importance, like a judge or other high official.|He dismissed the objection magistratically. 他专横地驳回了反对意见。|Synonyms: authoritatively, imperiously, dictatorially, pompously, domineeringly|Antonyms: humbly, meekly, submissively, modestly|词性: 副词
