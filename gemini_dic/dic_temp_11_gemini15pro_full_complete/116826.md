maxillary:/ˈmæksɪləri/ (US), /mækˈsɪləri/ (UK)| adj. n.|上颌的，上颌骨|Relating to the jaw or jawbone, specifically the upper jaw.  The maxillary bones form the upper jaw and part of the hard palate, eye sockets, and nasal cavity.|The maxillary sinus is located above the upper teeth.|近义词:jawbone (n.), upper jaw (n.)|反义词:mandibular|词性:adjective, noun


maxilla:/mækˈsɪlə/ (US), /mækˈsɪlə/ (UK)| n.|上颌骨|The bone that forms the upper jaw in vertebrates.|The maxilla is a crucial bone for chewing and speaking.|近义词:jawbone, upper jaw|反义词:mandible|词性:noun
