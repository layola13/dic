boswellian:/ˌbɒzˈwɛliən/ | adj. n.| 博斯韦尔式的，像博斯韦尔一样的；博斯韦尔式的人|Relating to or characteristic of James Boswell, especially in his devotion to Samuel Johnson; a biographer who diligently records the details of a subject's life.  A devoted admirer and recorder, especially of a famous person.|He kept a boswellian journal of his travels. 他像博斯韦尔一样详细记录了他的旅行日记。|Synonyms: admiring, devoted, reverent, worshipful, idolatrous |Antonyms: critical, disapproving, irreverent, disrespectful|adjective

boswellian:/ˌbɒzˈwɛliən/ | n.| 博斯韦尔式的人物；像博斯韦尔一样的传记作家|A biographer who diligently records the details of a subject's life, like James Boswell.|He was a true boswellian, always taking notes and observing his subject. 他是一个真正的博斯韦尔式人物，总是做笔记和观察他的研究对象。|Synonyms: biographer, chronicler, recorder|Antonyms: critic, detractor|noun
