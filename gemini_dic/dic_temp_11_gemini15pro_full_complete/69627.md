feminisms: /ˈfɛmɪnɪzəmz/
n.
女性主义（复数）
Theories and movements advocating for women's rights and equality.  Multiple perspectives on the fight for gender equality.
例句：
Third-wave feminisms emphasize intersectionality.  第三波女性主义强调交叉性。

Feminisms around the world have different focuses. 全球各地的女性主义有不同的侧重点。

近义词：
women's liberation movements, gender equality movements
反义词:
patriarchy, sexism, misogyny
词性
noun
