ceased:/siːst/| adj.(古旧用法，罕见)|停止的，终止的|no longer active or continuing|His ceased heartbeat caused great alarm. 他停止的心跳引起了极大的恐慌。|Synonyms: stopped, ended, finished, halted, terminated|Antonyms: started, began, commenced, initiated|adjective
