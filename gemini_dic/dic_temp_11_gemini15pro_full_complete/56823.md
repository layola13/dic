dormer:/ˈdɔːmə(r)/ (US) /ˈdɔːmə/ (UK)| n.|老虎窗，屋顶窗|A vertical window in a sloping roof, usually with its own small roof.|The dormer window provided a lovely view of the garden. 老虎窗提供了花园的美丽景色。|Synonym:roof window, garret window|Antonym: N/A|noun

dormer:/ˈdɔːmə(r)/ (US) /ˈdɔːmə/ (UK)| adj.|屋顶窗的|Relating to or resembling a dormer.|The dormer bedroom was small but cozy. 屋顶窗卧室虽小但很舒适。|Synonym: N/A|Antonym: N/A|adjective
