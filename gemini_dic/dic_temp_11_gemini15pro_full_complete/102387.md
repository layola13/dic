jabbering:/ˈdʒæbərɪŋ/ | n. | 喋喋不休，快速而不清楚的说话 | rapid, unclear, and often foolish talk  |  The monkeys were jabbering excitedly in the trees. 猴子在树上兴奋地吱吱喳喳叫个不停。| synonyms: chattering, babbling, prattling | antonyms: silence, quiet | noun

jabbering:/ˈdʒæbərɪŋ/ | adj. | 喋喋不休的，含糊不清地说个不停的 | talking rapidly and unclearly | He was jabbering nervously to himself. 他紧张地自言自语，含糊不清。 | synonyms: chattering, babbling, prattling | antonyms: silent, quiet | adjective
