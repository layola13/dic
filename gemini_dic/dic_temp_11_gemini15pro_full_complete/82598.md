guarding:/ˈɡɑːrdɪŋ/ | n. | 保护；守卫；看守（行为）| the act of protecting or defending something |  The guarding of the castle was entrusted to the knights. 城堡的守卫工作被委托给了骑士们。| protection, defense | neglect, abandonment | noun

Etymology: From Middle English *garden*, from Old North French *garder*, from Frankish *wardōn*, from Proto-Germanic *wardōną* (“to watch, guard”).  Ultimately related to the English word "ward".
