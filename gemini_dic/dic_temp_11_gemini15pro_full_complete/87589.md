heterosomati: /ˌhɛtərəˈsoʊmətˌaɪ/ /ˌhɛtərəˈsɒmətˌaɪ/ | n. |异体; 异形体; (尤指)比目鱼| Organisms, especially flatfishes, having asymmetry of the body, particularly of the head.  This asymmetry often involves the migration of one eye to the other side of the head during development. | Example: Heterosomati, such as flounders, are well-adapted to life on the seafloor. 比目鱼等异形体非常适应海底生活。

Example: The study of heterosomati provides insights into the evolution of asymmetry in vertebrates. 对异形体的研究为了解脊椎动物的不对称进化提供了见解。| 近义词: flatfish, Pleuronectiformes | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "symmetrical organisms") | 词性: Noun
