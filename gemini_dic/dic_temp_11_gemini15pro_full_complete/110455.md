lil:/lɪl/| adj.|小的，微小的|Shortened form of "little".  Used informally.|例句：He's got a lil' dog. 他有一只小狗。|近义词：small, tiny, little|反义词:big, large, huge|词性:形容词

lil:/lɪl/| n.| 小东西，小孩|Informal term for something small or a young child.|例句：Look at that lil' fella! 看看那个小家伙！|近义词：kid, child, tot|反义词:adult, grown-up|词性:名词
