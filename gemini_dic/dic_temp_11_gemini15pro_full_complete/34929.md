cloakedly:/ˈkloʊkɪdli/
|adv.|隐秘地，偷偷摸摸地|in a cloaked manner; secretly, covertly.  The information was passed cloakedly to the agent.  这个信息被秘密地传递给了特工。|Synonyms: secretly, covertly, stealthily, surreptitiously|Antonyms: openly, publicly, plainly, visibly|词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From cloak (n.) + -ed (forming past participles and adjectives) + -ly (forming adverbs). Cloak itself comes from Old North French cloke, ultimately from Frankish *klokko.
