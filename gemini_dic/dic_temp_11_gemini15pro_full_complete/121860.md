misrepresents:/ˌmɪsrɛprɪˈzɛnts/
verb
歪曲；不如实地叙述；误传
gives a false or misleading account of.
He misrepresented the facts to the court. 他向法庭歪曲了事实。
synonyms: distort, falsify, twist, misstate, pervert, misreport
antonyms: represent accurately, portray truthfully
词性: 动词
