epiglottitis:/ˌɛpɪɡlɒˈtaɪtɪs/ | n. |会厌炎|Inflammation of the epiglottis, a flap of cartilage at the root of the tongue, which can be life-threatening if it swells and blocks the airway. | The child was diagnosed with epiglottitis and rushed to the hospital. 这个孩子被诊断出患有会厌炎，并被紧急送往医院。

Epiglottitis can cause severe breathing difficulties. 会厌炎会导致严重的呼吸困难。|近义词: supraglottitis (less common) |反义词: None |词性: noun


Etymology:
From epi- (Greek: upon, on) + glottis (Greek: the aperture of the larynx) + -itis (Greek: inflammation).
