hotkey:/ˈhɒtkiː/ (UK) /ˈhɑːtkiː/ (US)| n.|快捷键|A key or combination of keys that provides a shortcut to a particular command or function.|Example:  I set up a hotkey to open my email program. 我设置了一个快捷键来打开我的电子邮件程序。|Example: Using hotkeys can significantly speed up your workflow. 使用快捷键可以显著加快你的工作流程。|Synonym: shortcut, keyboard shortcut|Antonym: N/A|noun

hotkeys:/ˈhɒtkiːz/ (UK) /ˈhɑːtkiːz/ (US)| n.|快捷键（复数）|Plural form of "hotkey."  Refers to multiple keyboard shortcuts.|Example:  I customized the hotkeys to fit my preferences. 我自定义了快捷键以符合我的偏好。|Example:  Learning the hotkeys will save you a lot of time. 学习快捷键将为你节省大量时间。|Synonym: shortcuts, keyboard shortcuts|Antonym: N/A|noun
