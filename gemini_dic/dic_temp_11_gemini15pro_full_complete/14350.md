autonomous:/ɔːˈtɒnəməs/ /ɔːˈtɑːnəməs/| adj.|自主的，自治的|Having the freedom to govern itself or control its own affairs.|例句：The university is autonomous, making its own decisions about curriculum and admissions. 这所大学是自主的，可以自己决定课程和招生事宜。

例句：Many teenagers yearn to be autonomous from their parents. 许多青少年渴望独立于父母。|近义词：independent, self-governing, self-reliant|反义词：dependent, subordinate, controlled|词性:形容词
