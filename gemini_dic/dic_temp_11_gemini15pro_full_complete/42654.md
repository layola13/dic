counterstated:/ˌkaʊntərˈsteɪtɪd/ | adj. | 反驳的，相反的 |  Having been opposed or contradicted.  | The government's claims were counterstated by the opposition party. 政府的说法遭到了反对党的反驳。 | Synonyms: contradicted, opposed, rebutted, gainsaid | Antonyms: supported, confirmed, corroborated | Adjective

counterstate:/ˌkaʊntərˈsteɪt/ | v. | 反驳，提出相反的意见 | To state the opposite of; to contradict. | He counterstated the allegations made against him. 他反驳了对他的指控。| Synonyms: contradict, oppose, rebut, gainsay | Antonyms: support, confirm, corroborate | Verb
