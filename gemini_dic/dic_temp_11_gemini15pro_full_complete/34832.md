clingingly:/ˈklɪŋɪŋli/| adv.|依恋地，紧紧地|In a way that clings or holds on tightly, either physically or emotionally.  Showing a strong emotional dependence or attachment, sometimes to an excessive degree.|The child clung clingingly to his mother's leg.  孩子紧紧地抱着妈妈的腿。|近义词：tenaciously, persistently, desperately|反义词：loosely, independently, detachedly|词性:副词
Etymology: From cling + -ing + -ly.  Cling comes from Old English clingan "to stick, adhere," of Germanic origin.
