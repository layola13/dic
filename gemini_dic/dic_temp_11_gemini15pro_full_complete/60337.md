egomania:/ˌiːɡəʊˈmeɪniə/ /ˌiːɡoʊˈmeɪniə/| n. |极端利己主义；极端自负；自恋狂|An obsessive preoccupation with oneself and one's own needs.|例句：His egomania alienated all his friends. 他的极端利己主义疏远了他的所有朋友。|例句：Her egomania prevents her from seeing the needs of others. 她的极端自负让她看不到别人的需求。|近义词：narcissism, self-obsession, vanity|反义词:humility, modesty|词性:noun

Etymology: From Greek ego (I) + mania (madness, obsession).
