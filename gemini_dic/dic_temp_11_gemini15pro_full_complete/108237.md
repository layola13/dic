laughing-stock: /ˈlɑːfɪŋ stɒk/ (UK), /ˈlæfɪŋ stɑːk/ (US)
n.|笑柄，嘲笑对象|A person subjected to general mockery or ridicule.|He became the laughing-stock of the whole school after he tripped and fell on stage. 他在舞台上绊倒后成了全校的笑柄。|synonyms: butt of jokes, object of ridicule, mockery|antonyms:  respected figure, role model|noun
