no-shows:/ˈnoʊ ʃoʊz/| n.| 缺席者；未到场的人|People who have made an appointment or reservation but fail to arrive without giving notice.  那些预约或预订后却没有通知就未出现的人。|例句：The restaurant had a lot of no-shows last night. 昨晚这家餐厅有很多预订的客人没来。|例句：The airline overbooked the flight, anticipating some no-shows. 航空公司超额预订了航班，预计会有一些人不会出现。|近义词：absentees, defaulters|反义词:attendees, presents|词性:noun
