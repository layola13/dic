bilging:/ˈbɪldʒɪŋ/
n.|进水，(船)破损|The act of a ship's hull being broken or fractured, allowing water to enter.  船体破裂或断裂，导致水进入的行为。|The bilging of the ship caused it to sink rapidly. 船的进水导致它迅速下沉。|flooding, leaking|sealing, patching|noun


bilge:/bɪldʒ/
n.|舱底，污水|The lowest internal part of a ship's hull, where water collects.  船体最低的内部部分，水在那里聚集。|The bilge was full of dirty water. 舱底满是脏水。|bottom, sump|top, deck|noun

bilge:/bɪldʒ/
v.|（船）破损进水|(Of a ship) to be broken or fractured in the hull, causing it to take in water.  （指船）船体破裂或断裂，导致进水。|The ship bilged on the rocks.  这艘船在岩石上撞破了。|founder, sink|float, sail|verb
