inexpressible:/ˌɪnɪkˈspre səbl/
adjective
无法表达的，难以形容的
too great, extreme, or delicate to be expressed or described in words.
The beauty of the sunset was inexpressible. 日落的美丽难以言表。
inexpressible joy  难以言喻的快乐
synonyms:unutterable, indescribable, ineffable
antonyms:expressible, describable, utterable
词性:形容词
