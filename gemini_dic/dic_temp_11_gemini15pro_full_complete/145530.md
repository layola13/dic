overcomes:/ˌoʊvərˈkʌmz/| v.|克服，战胜|To successfully deal with a problem or difficulty.|She overcame her shyness and gave a great presentation. 她克服了害羞，做了一个很棒的演讲。|近义词：conquers, defeats, surmounts, triumphs over|反义词: succumbs to, yields to|词性:动词(第三人称单数)
词源: From Middle English *overcomen*, from Old English *ofercuman* (“to overcome, conquer, subdue, surpass, overwhelm, overtake”), from *ofer* (“over”) + *cuman* (“to come”). Cognate with Dutch *overkomen* (“to happen, befall”), German *überkommen* (“to receive, seize”), Swedish *överkomma* (“to overcome”).
