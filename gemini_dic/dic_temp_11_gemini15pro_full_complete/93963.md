immutablenesses:/ɪˌmjuːtəˈbɪlnəsɪz/
|n.|不变性，永恒性|The quality or state of being immutable; inability to be changed.|The immutablenesses of the laws of physics fascinated her. 她对物理定律的不变性着迷。|近义词：invariability, permanence, constancy|反义词：mutability, changeability|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From immutable + -ness.  Immutable comes from Late Latin immutabilis, from in- "not" + mutabilis "changeable," from mutare "to change."
