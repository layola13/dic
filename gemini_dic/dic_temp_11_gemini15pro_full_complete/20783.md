blankly:/ˈblæŋkli/| adv.|茫然地；空洞地；直率地|In a way that shows no feeling, understanding, or interest.  Showing a lack of comprehension or reaction.|She stared blankly at the wall. 她茫然地盯着墙壁。|Synonyms: expressionlessly, vacantly, emptily|Antonyms: expressively, meaningfully, intently|adverb
