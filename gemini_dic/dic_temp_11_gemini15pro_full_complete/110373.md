ligniform:/ˈlɪɡnɪfɔːrm/
adjective
木质的，像木头的
Resembling wood; woody.
The fungus had a ligniform appearance. 这种真菌外观像木头。
近义词:woody, ligneous, xyloid
反义词: herbaceous, fleshy
