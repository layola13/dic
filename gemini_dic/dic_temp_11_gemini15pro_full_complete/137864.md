His nonvirility made him feel inadequate. 他的缺乏男子气概使他感到自卑。
近义词：impotence, weakness, effeminacy

词性：名词

词源：
From non- + virility.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "virility" comes from the Latin "virilis," meaning "manly."
