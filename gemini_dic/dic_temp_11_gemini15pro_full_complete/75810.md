fumet:/fjuːˈmeɪ/ | n. |浓烈的鱼汤；浓鱼汁 |A rich stock made from fish bones, often with added wine, vegetables, and herbs. |例句：The chef used a fumet as the base for his bouillabaisse.  厨师用浓鱼汁做浓鱼汤的底料。

例句：The fumet added a deep, savory flavor to the sauce. 这浓鱼汁给酱汁增添了浓郁的香味。 |近义词：stock, broth, court bouillon |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "water" in a culinary context if contrasting a flavorful base with a bland one) |词性:noun
