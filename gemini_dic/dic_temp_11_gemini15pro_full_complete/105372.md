kickstart:/ˈkɪkˌstɑːrt/
verb|启动，开始；促进，推动|To start or revitalize something, often suddenly or rapidly.  It can refer to both literal engines and metaphorical projects or processes.|The government announced a new program to kickstart the economy. 政府宣布了一项启动经济的新计划。|近义词：begin, start, launch, initiate, boost, stimulate|反义词：halt, stop, end, terminate|词性:v.
kickstart:/ˈkɪkˌstɑːrt/
noun|启动，开始；助力，刺激|The act of kickstarting something; something that kickstarts something else.|The tax cuts were intended as a kickstart for the housing market. 减税政策旨在刺激房地产市场。|近义词：boost, impetus, stimulus|反义词：hindrance, obstacle|词性:n.
