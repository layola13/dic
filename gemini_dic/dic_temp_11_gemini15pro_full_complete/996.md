acarine: /ˈækəraɪn/ | n. |螨虫；蜱螨 | A tiny, often parasitic arachnid belonging to the order Acarina. |例句：The acarine burrowed into the skin, causing intense itching.  螨虫钻进皮肤里，引起剧烈瘙痒。|例句：Acarines are responsible for transmitting a number of diseases. 螨虫是传播多种疾病的罪魁祸首。| 近义词：mite, tick | 反义词:  (无明确的反义词) | 词性: noun


Etymology:  From New Latin *Acārīna, neuter plural of *acārinus, from Greek ἀκαρής (akarēs, “indivisible, infinitely small”), from ἀ- (a-, “not”) + κεραίνω, variant of κείρω (keirō, “to shear, cut”).
