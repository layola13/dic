driftwood:/ˈdrɪftˌwʊd/| n. |浮木|Wood that has been washed ashore or is floating in the water.|例句：The beach was littered with driftwood after the storm.  暴风雨过后，海滩上到处都是浮木。

例句：The artist used driftwood to create unique sculptures.  这位艺术家用浮木创作独特的雕塑。|近义词：wreckage, debris, flotsam|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "livewood" in a poetic sense) |词性: noun
