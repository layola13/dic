emenagogue:/ɪˈmenəɡɒɡ/ | n. | 通经药，催经剂 | A substance that stimulates or increases menstrual flow. |  Example:  The doctor prescribed an emenagogue to help regulate her menstrual cycle. 医生给她开了一种通经药来帮助调节她的月经周期。

Example:  In traditional medicine, certain herbs were used as emenagogues.  在传统医学中，某些草药被用作通经药。 | 近义词: emmenagogue | 反义词: emmenostatic (止经药) | 词性: noun
