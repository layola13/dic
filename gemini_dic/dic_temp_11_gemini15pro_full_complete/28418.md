carbonises:/ˈkɑːrbənaɪzɪz/ | v. | 炭化，烧焦 | To heat (organic matter) in the absence of air so that it decomposes to carbon.  The intense heat carbonised the wood.  高温使木头炭化。 | 近义词: chars, scorches | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 decompose 分解) | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From French *carboniser*, from *carbone* ("carbon"). Ultimately from Latin *carbō* ("coal, charcoal").
