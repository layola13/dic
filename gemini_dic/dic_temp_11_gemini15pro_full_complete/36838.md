colonizable:/ˈkɒlənaɪzəbl/ (US) /ˈkɒlənʌɪzəbl/ (UK)| adj.|可殖民的，可开拓的|Able to be colonized; suitable for colonization.  This refers to a place or region that can be settled and established by a group of people from another place.|例句：The newly discovered island was deemed colonizable due to its fertile land and abundant resources.  这个新发现的岛屿由于其肥沃的土地和丰富的资源被认为是适宜殖民的。

例句：Some scientists believe Mars might be colonizable in the future. 一些科学家认为火星在未来可能是可以殖民的。|近义词：habitable, settleable, exploitable|反义词：uninhabitable, unsuitable|词性:形容词
