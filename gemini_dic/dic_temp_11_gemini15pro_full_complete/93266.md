ileocaecal:/ˌɪliːoʊˈsiːkəl/| adj.|回盲部的；回肠盲肠的|Relating to the ileum and the cecum, the parts of the small and large intestines that join.|The ileocaecal valve prevents backflow of material from the colon into the small intestine. 回盲瓣防止物质从结肠回流到小肠。|近义词:ileocecal|反义词:None|词性:adjective

词源:
From ileo- + caecal.  "Ileo-" comes from the Latin "ileum" referring to the last part of the small intestine. "Caecal" comes from the Latin "caecum" meaning blind, referring to the pouch-like cecum.
