divergence:/daɪˈvɜːrdʒəns/ /dɪˈvɜːrdʒəns/| n. |分歧，差异，背离|The state of diverging; separating or branching off from a single point or path. A difference or conflict in opinions, interests, etc.|The divergence between the two species became more pronounced over time. 这两个物种之间的差异随着时间的推移变得更加明显。|近义词：difference, disagreement, deviation, disparity, separation|反义词：convergence, agreement, similarity, unity|词性:名词

diverge:/daɪˈvɜːrdʒ/ /dɪˈvɜːrdʒ/| v. |分歧，偏离，相异|To separate and go in different directions; to differ in opinion or character.|Their paths diverged after graduation. 毕业后，他们的道路就分开了。|近义词：separate, deviate, differ, branch off|反义词：converge, unite, agree|词性:动词

divergent:/daɪˈvɜːrdʒənt/ /dɪˈvɜːrdʒənt/| adj. |分歧的，相异的，背道而驰的|Tending to diverge; differing; varying.|They hold divergent views on the issue. 他们在这个问题上持有不同的观点。|近义词：different, varying, dissimilar, conflicting|反义词：convergent, similar, uniform, agreeing|词性:形容词
