aniseed:/ˈænɪsiːd/ | n. |茴香籽；茴香味|The seeds of the anise plant, used as a flavoring. |I love the licorice-like flavor of aniseed in cookies. 我喜欢饼干里茴香籽的甘草味。|Anise seeds are often used in baking and to flavor liqueurs. 茴香籽常用于烘焙和调味利口酒。|近义词：anise, fennel|反义词: N/A|名词
