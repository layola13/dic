annulus:/ˈænjʊləs/ (US), /ˈænjʊləs/ (UK)| n. |环状物，环形空间|A ring-shaped object, area, or structure. |The area between two concentric circles is an annulus.|Synonyms: ring, band, circle, loop|Antonyms: core, center|noun

annuli:/ˈænjʊlaɪ/ (US), /ˈænjʊliː/ (UK)| n. |环状物（annulus的复数形式）|The plural form of annulus.|Several annuli were visible on the target.|Synonyms: rings, bands, circles, loops|Antonyms: cores, centers|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *anulus* "ring," diminutive of *anus* "ring, circle."
