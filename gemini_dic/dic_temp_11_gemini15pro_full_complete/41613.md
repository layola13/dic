corradiate:/kəˈreɪdieɪt/
verb
共同辐射，从同一点发出
To emit rays from the same point; to converge with other rays at a common point.
The spotlights corradiated on the stage, creating a dramatic effect. 聚光灯共同照射在舞台上，营造出戏剧性的效果。
The lasers corradiated on the target. 激光共同照射在目标上。
converge, focus
diverge, scatter
