growths:/ɡroʊθs/| n. |增长；生长；生长物；肿瘤|The growths on the tree were unusual. 树上的生长物很不寻常。|例句：The company experienced significant growths in sales last year.  这家公司去年销售额显著增长。

例句：The doctor examined the growths on her skin. 医生检查了她皮肤上的增生物。|近义词：increases, expansions, developments, tumors (in a medical context)|反义词:declines, reductions, decreases|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *growthe*, from Old English *grōwþ*, from *grōwan* "to grow".
