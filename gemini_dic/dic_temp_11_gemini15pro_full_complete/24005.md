breathlessnesses:/ˈbrɛθləsnəsɪz/
noun
气喘吁吁；呼吸困难（复数）
The state of being breathless, especially in repeated instances.  It refers to multiple occurrences of shortness of breath.
After running the marathon, she experienced several breathlessnesses.  跑完马拉松后，她多次感到呼吸困难。
近义词:shortness of breath, dyspnea, gaspings
反义词:normal breathing, eupnea


breathlessness:/ˈbrɛθləsnəs/
noun
气喘吁吁；呼吸困难
The state of being breathless; shortness of breath.
The climb left him in a state of breathlessness.  攀登使他气喘吁吁。
近义词:shortness of breath, dyspnea, gasping
反义词:normal breathing, eupnea


breathless:/ˈbrɛθləs/
adjective
气喘吁吁的；屏息的；激动的；令人激动的
1. Gasping for breath, out of breath.
2. Speechless or amazed, especially because of excitement.
3. (of an activity) So exciting or fast-paced as to leave one breathless.
She was breathless after running up the stairs. 她跑上楼梯后气喘吁吁。
The breathless pace of modern life.  令人喘不过气的现代生活节奏。
近义词:out of breath, gasping, panting, winded, amazed, astonished, awestruck
反义词:calm, composed, relaxed


ETYMOLOGY->
From breath + -less + -ness.  Ultimately from Old English bræð.
