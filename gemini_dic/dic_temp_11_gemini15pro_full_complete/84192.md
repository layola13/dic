half-rebellious:/ˌhɑːf rɪˈbɛliəs/
adj.
半叛逆的，略带叛逆的
Partly rebellious; exhibiting some, but not complete, rebellion.
Her half-rebellious attitude intrigued him. 她略带叛逆的态度激起了他的兴趣。
Near synonyms: slightly rebellious, somewhat rebellious, partially rebellious
Antonyms: obedient, compliant, conforming
词性: 形容词


half:/hæf/
n. adj. adv.
一半，半个；（尤指比赛的）半场
One of two equal or nearly equal parts of a divisible whole.
He ate half the cake. 他吃了半个蛋糕。
Near synonyms: moiety, one-half, part
Antonyms: whole, total, double
词性: 名词, 形容词, 副词


rebellious:/rɪˈbɛliəs/
adj.
叛逆的；反抗的；难控制的
Showing a desire to resist authority, control, or convention.
The rebellious teenagers refused to follow the rules. 叛逆的青少年拒绝遵守规则。
Near synonyms: defiant, disobedient, insubordinate
Antonyms: obedient, compliant, submissive
词性: 形容词
