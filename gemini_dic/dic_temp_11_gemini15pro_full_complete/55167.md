dissemblingly:/dɪˈsɛmblɪŋli/
adverb
假装地，掩饰地
in a dissembling manner; so as to conceal one's true motives or feelings.  Pretending; deceptively.
He smiled dissemblingly, hiding his true anger. 他假笑着，掩饰着他真正的愤怒。
She dissemblingly agreed to the plan, while secretly plotting against it. 她假意同意了这个计划，却暗中密谋反对它。
近义词: deceptively, falsely, hypocritically, disingenuously
反义词: honestly, sincerely, truthfully, candidly
词性: adverb
词源: From dissemble + -ing + -ly.  Dissemble comes from Old French dissembler "to disguise, feign," from dis- "apart, away" + sembler "to seem, appear."
