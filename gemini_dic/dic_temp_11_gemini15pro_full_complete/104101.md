jump-starting:/ˈdʒʌmpˌstɑːtɪŋ/ | n. v. (present participle)| 启动，发动; 刺激，推动 |The act of starting a vehicle's engine by connecting its battery to the battery of another vehicle using jumper cables. Also, figuratively, giving a boost or impetus to something.| I had to jump-start my car this morning because the battery was dead. 我今天早上不得不启动我的车，因为电池没电了。|  He jump-started the economy with his innovative policies. 他用他的创新政策刺激了经济。| synonyms: boosting, restarting, reviving | antonyms: stalling, hindering, obstructing | verb (present participle)
