nonsubjected:/ˌnɒnsʌbˈdʒɛktɪd/
adjective
未受…支配的; 未服从的;
Not subjected or brought under domination or control.
The indigenous population remained nonsubjected to the colonial power.  土著居民仍然没有屈服于殖民势力。
The artist's vision remained nonsubjected to the prevailing trends.  这位艺术家的视野仍然不受主流趋势的影响。
近义词:independent, autonomous, free, sovereign
反义词:subjected, subordinate, controlled, dependent
词性:adjective
词源: non- (not) + subjected (past participle of subject, from Latin subjectus, meaning "brought under," from subicere "to place under")
