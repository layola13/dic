arum:/ˈɛərəm/ | n. | 芋头，天南星科植物 | A perennial herbaceous plant of the arum family, which comprises a group of flowering plants that frequently feature arrowhead-shaped leaves and characteristic inflorescences. | The arum lily is a popular garden plant. 芋百合是一种很受欢迎的园艺植物。 |  The wild arum grows in damp woodlands. 野生芋生长在潮湿的林地。 | synonyms: aroid, cuckoopint, wake-robin, lords-and-ladies | antonyms: N/A | noun
