models:/ˈmɑːdlz/ (US) /ˈmɒdlz/ (UK)| n.|模型；模特；模式；典型|A simplified representation of something, often used for testing or demonstrating how something works; a person employed to display clothes or other merchandise; a particular type or version of a product.|The architect built a model of the proposed skyscraper. 建筑师建造了拟建摩天大楼的模型。|synonyms: prototypes, dummies, mannequins, examples, patterns, paradigms|antonyms: originals, realities|noun

models:/ˈmɑːdlz/ (US) /ˈmɒdlz/ (UK)| v.|模仿；塑造；模拟|To create a representation of something; to shape or form something; to base one's behavior or actions on someone else.|He modeled his career on his father's. 他以父亲的职业生涯为榜样。|synonyms: imitate, emulate, simulate, shape, mold|antonyms: distort, misrepresent|verb
