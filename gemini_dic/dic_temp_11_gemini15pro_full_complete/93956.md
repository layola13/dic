immurement:/ɪˈmjʊərmənt/
|n.|监禁，禁闭；围住，封闭|The act of immuring or the state of being immured; confinement, imprisonment, enclosure.|The king ordered the immurement of his enemies in the dungeon. 国王下令将他的敌人们囚禁在地牢里。|
|近义词: confinement, imprisonment, incarceration|
|反义词: release, liberation, freedom|
|词性: noun|


词源:
From Late Latin immūrāmentum, from immūrāre (“to wall in, enclose”), from in- (“in, into”) + mūrus (“wall”).
