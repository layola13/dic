collaterally:/kəˈlætərəli/
adv.| 附带地，作为抵押|In addition to the main subject or indirectly; as security.
He mentioned the issue collaterally. 他顺便提到了这个问题。
The house was collaterally secured by his other properties. 这栋房子由他的其他房产作抵押。
近义词:incidentally, indirectly, secondarily
反义词:directly, primarily
词性:副词


collateral:/kəˈlætərəl/
n.| 抵押品，担保品|Something pledged as security for repayment of a loan, to be forfeited in the event of a default.
The bank required collateral for the loan. 银行要求贷款要有抵押品。
He used his car as collateral for the loan. 他用他的汽车作为贷款的抵押品。
近义词:security, guarantee, pledge
反义词:debt
词性:名词


collateral:/kəˈlætərəl/
adj.| 附带的，旁系的|Descended from the same ancestor but through different lines.
Collateral damage was extensive. 附带损害是广泛的。
He is my collateral relative. 他是我的旁系亲属。
近义词:indirect, secondary, incidental
反义词:direct, primary
词性:形容词


词源: From Late Latin *collateralis, from com- "together" + lateralis "of the side."
