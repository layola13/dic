flobert:/ˈfloʊbərt/ | n. |弗洛贝尔步枪，弗洛贝尔弹|A type of small-caliber rimfire cartridge, and by extension, the rifles chambered for it.  Originally designed for indoor target practice.|He used a Flobert rifle for target shooting in his basement. 他在地下室用弗洛贝尔步枪进行射击练习。|Near synonyms:  rimfire, parlor rifle, salon rifle|Antonyms: centerfire, high-powered rifle|noun
