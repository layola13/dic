nonreinforcement:/ˌnɒnriːɪnˈfɔːsmənt/ | /ˌnɑːnriːɪnˈfɔːrsmənt/ | n. | 不强化，无强化 | Absence of reinforcement, which, in operant conditioning, weakens a conditioned response by not following it with a reinforcer.  |  The nonreinforcement of the behavior led to its extinction.  这种行为由于没有强化而最终消失。| synonyms: extinction, nonreward | antonyms: reinforcement, reward | noun
