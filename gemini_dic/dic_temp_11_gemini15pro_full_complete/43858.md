crescent:/ˈkresənt/| n. adj.|新月，月牙形，新月形的|A curved shape like the moon when it is less than half full.  It can also refer to anything of this shape. |The crescent moon hung low in the sky.  新月低垂在天空中。|近义词:moon, sickle, arc|反义词:full moon, gibbous|名词, 形容词

crescent:/ˈkresənt/| adj.|逐渐增长的，渐强的|Increasing or growing.|The city's crescent population is putting a strain on resources.  该市不断增长的人口给资源带来了压力。|近义词:growing, increasing, expanding|反义词:decreasing, diminishing, shrinking|形容词
