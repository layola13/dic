mistutoring:/ˌmɪsˈtjuːtərɪŋ/
noun
错误指导，不当教育
The student's poor performance was a result of years of mistutoring.  这个学生的糟糕表现是多年错误指导的结果。
The mistutoring he received as a child hampered his academic progress. 他小时候受到的错误教育阻碍了他的学业进步。
近义词: misteaching, miseducating
反义词: tutoring, educating, instructing
词性:n.

词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + "tutoring" (from "tutor," ultimately from Latin "tueri" meaning "to watch over, protect, guard")
