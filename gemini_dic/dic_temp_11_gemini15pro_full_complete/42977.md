covetable: /ˈkʌvɪtəbl/
adjective
令人垂涎的；梦寐以求的
Highly desirable and sought-after.
Her covetable handbag was the envy of all her friends. 她那令人垂涎的手袋是她所有朋友羡慕的对象。
The vintage car is highly covetable. 这辆老爷车非常令人向往。
desirable, enviable, in demand, sought-after, attractive
undesirable, unattractive, unwanted, repulsive
词性: 形容词
