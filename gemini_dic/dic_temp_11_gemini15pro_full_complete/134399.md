nonintrovertednesses:/nɒnˌɪntrəˈvɜːtɪdnɪsɪz/ | n. | 非内向性；外向性 | The state or quality of not being introverted; extroversion. |  Her nonintrovertednesses made her a natural leader. 她的外向性格使她成为天生的领导者。|  They admired her nonintrovertednesses and ability to connect with people. 他们钦佩她的外向和与人交往的能力。 | 近义词:extroversion, sociability | 反义词:introversion, shyness | 词性:名词

词源: "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "introverted" (adjective) + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality).  "Introverted" comes from "intro-" (Latin, meaning "inward") + "vertere" (Latin, meaning "to turn").
