acmes:/ˈækmiːz/| n.|顶点，极点，巅峰|The acme of his career was when he won the Nobel Prize. 他事业的顶峰是获得诺贝尔奖的时候。|例句：Reaching the summit of Mount Everest was the acme of his climbing career. 登顶珠穆朗玛峰是他攀登事业的顶峰。

例句：The company reached the acme of its success in the 1990s.  这家公司在20世纪90年代达到了成功的顶峰。

|近义词：peak, summit, pinnacle, apex, zenith|反义词:nadir, bottom, base|词性:名词
