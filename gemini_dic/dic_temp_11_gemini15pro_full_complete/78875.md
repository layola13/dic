ginglymoidal:/ˌdʒɪŋɡlɪˈmɔɪdəl/
adjective
滑车关节的, 铰链状的
Relating to, or resembling, a ginglymus; hinge-like.
The ginglymoidal joint allows for flexion and extension. 滑车关节允许屈伸。
The elbow is a ginglymoidal joint. 肘部是一个滑车关节。
Near Synonyms: hinge-like, trochlear
Antonyms: spheroidal, pivot
词性: adjective
