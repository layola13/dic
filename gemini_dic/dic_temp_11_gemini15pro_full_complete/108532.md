lazarly:/ˈleɪzərli/| adv.|像拉撒路一样；复活般地；（尤指病后）虚弱地|Resembling or suggestive of Lazarus, especially in terms of being raised from the dead or appearing weak and ill after an illness.|He rose lazarly from his sickbed after a week of fever. 他在发烧一周后，像拉撒路一样从病床上虚弱地站了起来。|Near synonyms: weakly, feebly, faintly, palely|Near antonyms: strongly, robustly, healthily|Adverb
