glutinous:/ˈɡluːtɪnəs/| adj.|粘性的；胶状的|sticky or gluey; viscous. Resembling glue in consistency; adhesive.|The glutinous rice made the dessert chewy.|近义词：sticky, gummy, adhesive, viscous|反义词：non-sticky, dry, runny|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *glūtinōsus, from glūtin-, stem of glūten (“glue”).
