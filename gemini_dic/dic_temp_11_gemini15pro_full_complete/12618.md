assassin: /əˈsæsɪn/ | n. | 刺客，暗杀者 | A person who murders someone important or famous, typically for political or religious reasons. |  He hired an assassin to eliminate his rival.  他雇佣了一名刺客来消灭他的对手。| synonyms: murderer, killer, hitman, executioner | antonyms: protector, guardian, bodyguard | noun

assassinate: /əˈsæsɪneɪt/ | vt. | 暗杀，行刺 | Murder (an important person) in a surprise attack for political or religious reasons. |  The president was assassinated by a lone gunman.  总统被一名枪手暗杀了。| synonyms: murder, kill, execute, liquidate | antonyms: protect, guard, save | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *assassin, from Arabic ḥashīshīn 'hashish-users', plural of ḥashīsh 'hashish', from the notion that members of a Muslim sect engaged in terrorism under the influence of hashish.
