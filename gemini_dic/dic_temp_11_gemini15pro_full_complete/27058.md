calendula:/kəˈlɛndʒʊlə/ | n. |金盏花，金盏菊 |A plant with orange or yellow flowers, used in herbal medicine.  A Mediterranean plant (Calendula officinalis) of the composite family, widely cultivated for its showy, many-rayed orange or yellow flower heads. The dried petals are used medicinally and as a dyestuff. Also called pot marigold.|Calendula cream is often used to treat minor burns. 金盏花膏常用于治疗轻微烧伤。|Synonyms: pot marigold, marigold|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
