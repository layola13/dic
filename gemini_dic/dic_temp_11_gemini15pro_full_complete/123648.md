monumental:/ˌmɒnjʊˈmentl/ | adj. | 不朽的，重大的，纪念碑的 | Of or relating to a monument; resembling a monument; massive or imposing; of outstanding significance or importance. |  例句：The signing of the peace treaty was a monumental event. 签署和平条约是一个具有里程碑意义的事件。

例句：They erected a monumental statue in his honor. 他们为他竖立了一座巨大的雕像。


近义词：historic, significant, important, grand, immense, colossal, massive | 反义词：insignificant, trivial, minor, small | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *monumentalis, from Latin monumentum (“monument, remembrance, record, tomb, memorial”), from monēre (“to remind, warn, advise, teach”).
