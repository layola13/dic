abseiler: /ˈæbseɪlər/ (UK) /æbˈseɪlər/ (US)| n. |绳降者，绕绳下降者|A person who descends a rock face or other steep surface by using a doubled rope coiled round the body and fixed at a higher point.|The abseiler skillfully navigated the cliff face. 绕绳下降者熟练地沿着悬崖面下降。|近义词：rappeller|反义词：climber (in some contexts)|词性：名词
