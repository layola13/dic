overluxuriantly: /ˌoʊvərˌlʌɡˈʒʊəriəntli/
adverb
过度奢华地，过于繁茂地
in an excessively luxurious or abundant way
The garden was overluxuriantly planted, with flowers spilling out of every corner.  花园里种植得过于繁茂，每个角落都开满了鲜花。
The banquet was decorated overluxuriantly, almost to the point of gaudiness.  宴会装饰得过于奢华，几乎到了俗气的程度。
近义词: excessively, profusely, immoderately
反义词: sparsely, modestly, plainly
词性: adverb

词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessive") + luxuriantly (adverb form of luxuriant, from Latin luxurians, present participle of luxuriare "to be luxuriant," from luxus "luxury")
