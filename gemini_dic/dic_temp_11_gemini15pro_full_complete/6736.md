analyses:/əˈnæləsiːz/ | v. |分析;  分解| Examine (something) methodically and in detail, typically in order to explain and interpret it. |  He analyzed the data to find trends. 他分析了数据以找出趋势。 | synonyms: examine, study, investigate | antonyms: synthesize, combine | verb
