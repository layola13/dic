canniness:/ˈkænɪnɪs/
n.|精明；狡猾；机敏|The quality of being canny, shrewdness and cleverness, especially in business or politics.|He showed considerable canniness in his handling of the situation. 他在处理这种情况时表现出相当的精明。|
近义词: shrewdness, astuteness, acumen, sagacity, wisdom,  perceptiveness
反义词: naivety, foolishness, gullibility, ingenuousness
词性:名词

Etymology:
From canny + -ness.  Canny itself is of uncertain origin, possibly from Scots cannie "careful, prudent," perhaps from or related to Middle English can "know, be able to," from Old English cunnan.
