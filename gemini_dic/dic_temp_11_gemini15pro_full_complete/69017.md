fatter:/ˈfætər/| adj.|更胖的，更肥的|Comparative form of "fat," meaning having more body fat than someone or something else.|The fatter cat struggled to jump onto the counter.  更胖的猫努力地跳上柜台。|近义词：larger, heavier, plumper, chubbier|反义词:thinner, slimmer, leaner|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old English *fættra, comparative of *fæt (fat). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *faitaz.
