overpowered: /ˌoʊvərˈpaʊərd/ | adj. |压倒性的，过于强大的，无法抗拒的 | Excessively powerful; too strong to be defeated or resisted. | The boss's personality was so overpowered that no one dared to disagree with him. 老板的个性太强势了，以至于没有人敢反对他。|  The overpowered smell of perfume gave me a headache.  浓烈的香水味让我头疼。 | 近义词: dominant, overwhelming, invincible, superior | 反义词: weak, powerless, inferior | 词性: 形容词

overpower: /ˌoʊvərˈpaʊər/ | vt. | 制服，压倒，使无法抵抗 | To defeat or subdue by superior force. | The police overpowered the suspect and arrested him. 警察制服了嫌疑犯并逮捕了他。 |  The army overpowered the enemy forces.  军队击败了敌军。 | 近义词: conquer, subdue, defeat, vanquish | 反义词: surrender, yield, succumb | 词性: 动词
