laid-backnesses:/ˌleɪdˈbæknəsəz/| n. |悠闲，懒散，放松|The state of being laid-back; relaxed and easy-going manner.|His laid-backness is sometimes misinterpreted as laziness. 他的悠闲有时被误解为懒惰。|The vacation was a time for complete laid-backness and relaxation. 假期是一段完全悠闲放松的时光。|近义词：relaxation, calmness, ease, tranquility|反义词:tension, anxiety, stress, uptightness|词性:名词

词源:  From laid-back + -ness. Laid-back itself originated in the mid-20th century, likely from the image of someone leaning back in a relaxed posture.
