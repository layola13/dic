morrice:/ˈmɒrɪs/ (UK), /ˈmɔːrɪs/ (US)| n. |莫里斯舞；一种古代的英国民间舞蹈|A traditional English folk dance involving bells, sticks, and handkerchiefs, often performed around May Day. |例句：The villagers performed a lively morrice on the green. 村民们在草地上表演了生动的莫里斯舞。

|例句：The morrice dancers wore bells on their legs. 莫里斯舞的舞者腿上系着铃铛。
|近义词：folk dance, sword dance|反义词: - |noun
