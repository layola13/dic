incoagulable:/ˌɪnkəˈæɡjələbəl/
adj.
不能凝固的，不凝结的
Not capable of being coagulated; unable to clot.
The patient's blood was found to be incoagulable due to a rare genetic disorder.  由于一种罕见的遗传疾病，该病人的血液无法凝固。
The incoagulable nature of the liquid made it difficult to work with.  这种液体不凝结的特性使得它很难处理。
近义词:noncoagulable, unclottable
反义词:coagulable, clottable
词性:adjective
词源:in- (not) + coagulable (capable of being coagulated) from Latin "coagulāre" (to curdle)
