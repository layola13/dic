hoodooisms:/ˈhuːduːɪzəmz/
n.
迷信; 巫术;  hoodoo 的仪式或实践
Practices or beliefs associated with hoodoo, a form of folk magic and superstition prevalent in the Southern United States.
He was known for his hoodooisms and strange rituals. 他以其巫术和奇怪的仪式而闻名。
近义词: superstitions, rituals, conjurations
反义词: rationality, logic
