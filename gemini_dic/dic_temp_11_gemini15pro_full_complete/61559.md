emendable:/ɪˈmɛndəbl/
adjective
可修正的，可修改的
capable of being emended; correctable
The text is emendable, but it will take a skilled editor to do it properly.  这篇文章可以修改，但这需要一位熟练的编辑来妥善处理。
This manuscript is so full of errors that it's barely emendable.  这份手稿错误太多，几乎无法修改。
近义词:correctable, revisable, amendable
反义词:incorrigible, uncorrectable, irreparable
词性:adjective
词源:From Late Latin *ēmendābilis, from Latin ēmendō (“I correct, free from faults”) + -ābilis.
