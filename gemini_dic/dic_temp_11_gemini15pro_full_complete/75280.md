frontal:/ˈfrʌntəl/| adj. n.|前部的，额的；正面|Relating to the forehead or front part of something.  A frontal attack. |The frontal lobes of the brain are responsible for higher cognitive functions.|Synonyms: anterior, forward, front, leading|Antonyms: rear, back, posterior|adjective

frontal:/ˈfrʌntəl/| n.|额叶|The frontal bone of the skull or the frontal region of the head.|Damage to the frontal can result in personality changes.|Synonyms: forehead|Antonyms: occiput|noun
