myotic:/maɪˈɑːtɪk/
adjective
缩瞳的；瞳孔缩小的
Relating to, characterized by, or causing contraction of the pupil of the eye.
例句：The drug caused a myotic reaction in the patient's eyes.  这种药物引起了病人眼睛的缩瞳反应。

例句：Myotic eye drops are sometimes used to treat glaucoma. 缩瞳眼药水有时用于治疗青光眼。
近义词：miosis, miotic
反义词：mydriatic
词性: adjective
