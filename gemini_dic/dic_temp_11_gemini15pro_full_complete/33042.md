chou:/ʃuː/ | n. | 臭味，恶臭 | A strong, unpleasant smell. |  The chou from the garbage was overwhelming. 垃圾的臭味让人难以忍受。 | synonyms: stench, stink, reek | antonyms: fragrance, perfume | noun

Etymology: From French "chou," meaning cabbage, later acquiring the sense of "rotten cabbage" and thus "bad smell."
