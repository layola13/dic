hypervascular:/ˌhaɪpərˈvæskjʊlər/
adjective
血管丰富的，血管增生的
Characterized by excessive formation of blood vessels.
The tumor was found to be hypervascular.  这个肿瘤被发现血管丰富。
The hypervascular nature of the tissue made surgery difficult.  该组织血管丰富的特性使手术变得困难。
近义词: angiogenic, vascularized
反义词: avascular, hypovascular
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From hyper- + vascular.  "Hyper-" is a prefix meaning "over, above, excessive," from Greek *hyper (over, beyond).  "Vascular" refers to blood vessels, from Latin vasculum (small vessel).
