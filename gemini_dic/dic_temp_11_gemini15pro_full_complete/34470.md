clawer:/ˈklɔːər/ (US), /ˈklɔːə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 爪子；爬行者；抓取工具 |A device or tool designed for gripping, grabbing, or pulling, often resembling a claw or hook.  A creature that crawls, especially using claws.  A claw itself.| The robotic clawer carefully picked up the delicate component. 机器人爪小心地拿起精致的部件。

The crab used its clawer to crack open the shell.  螃蟹用它的爪子敲开了贝壳。

The eagle's powerful clawers secured its prey.  老鹰强有力的爪子抓住了它的猎物。| 近义词：grabber, gripper, claw, crawler | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "claw" + "-er" (suffix forming agent nouns).  "Claw" ultimately comes from Proto-Germanic *klawō.
