communicatees: /kəˌmjuːnɪˈkiːz/
n.
被沟通者，信息接收者
The communicatees were informed about the changes in the policy. 被沟通者被告知了政策的变化。
The message was delivered to all the intended communicatees.  该信息已传递给所有预定的信息接收者。
近义词: recipients, audience, listeners
反义词: communicators, senders
词性: 名词

词源:  From communicate + -ee (a suffix forming nouns denoting one who is the object or beneficiary of the action of the verb).  "Communicate" comes from Latin communicatus, past participle of communicare "to share, divide out; communicate, impart, inform," from communis "common."
