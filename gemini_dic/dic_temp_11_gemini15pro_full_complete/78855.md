gingering:/ˈdʒɪndʒərɪŋ/| v.|给…加姜；使活跃；使兴奋；刺激；使有活力|Adding ginger to a dish or drink.  Figuratively, invigorating or stimulating something.|例句：She was gingering up the sales team with a motivational speech. 她用激励人心的演讲来激励销售团队。|近义词：invigorating, stimulating, energizing, enlivening, pep up, spice up|反义词：dulling, subduing, suppressing|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From ginger + -ing.  Ginger itself comes from Old English gingifer, from Medieval Latin gingiber, ultimately from Sanskrit śṛṅgavera.
