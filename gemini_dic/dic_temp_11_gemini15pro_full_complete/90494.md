huckster:/ˈhʌkstər/ | n. |叫卖小贩；推销员；骗子|A person who sells small items, either door-to-door or from a stall, often using aggressive sales tactics.  A person who promotes or sells something in a dishonest or aggressive way.| The huckster tried to sell me a fake Rolex.  那个小贩试图卖给我一块假劳力士。|Synonyms: peddler, hawker, vendor, salesman, charlatan, fraud|Antonyms: customer, buyer|noun
