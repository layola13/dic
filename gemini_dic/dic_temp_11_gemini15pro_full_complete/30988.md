chalet:/ˈʃæleɪ/ (US), /ˈʃæleɪ/ (UK)| n.|小木屋，瑞士山间房屋|A wooden house or cottage with overhanging eaves, typically found in the Swiss Alps.|We rented a cozy chalet in the mountains for our ski trip. 我们在山上租了一间舒适的小木屋滑雪度假。|例句：The chalet offered stunning views of the valley. 小木屋提供了山谷的壮丽景色。|近义词：cabin, cottage, lodge|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑) apartment, city house|词性: noun
