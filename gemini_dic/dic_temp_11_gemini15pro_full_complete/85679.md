have-a-screw-loose:/hæv ə skruː luːs/ | idiom | 脑子有点不正常；有点疯；行为古怪 | To be slightly crazy or eccentric; to have a mental instability. | He's always talking to himself – I think he has a screw loose. 他总是自言自语——我觉得他脑子有点不正常。 |  She's been acting strangely lately, like she has a screw loose. 她最近行为怪异，好像有点疯疯癫癫的。 | 近义词: crazy, insane, nuts, eccentric, off one's rocker, not all there | 反义词: sane, normal, rational | idiom
