igloos:/ˈɪɡluːz/
n.
冰屋，雪屋
A dome-shaped Inuit house, typically built from blocks of solid snow.
例句：
The Inuit people traditionally build igloos for shelter during the winter.
因纽特人传统上在冬天建造冰屋作为住所。

The igloo kept them warm despite the freezing temperatures.
尽管气温冰冻，冰屋仍然让他们保持温暖。

近义词：snow house, snow hut
反义词:house, building
词性:noun
