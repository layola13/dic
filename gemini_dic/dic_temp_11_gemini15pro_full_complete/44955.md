cryptovolcanic:/ˌkrɪptoʊvɒlˈkænɪk/
adjective
隐火山作用的；潜火山作用的
Relating to or denoting volcanic activity that does not result in the eruption of lava at the surface, but produces other effects such as the formation of maars or tuff rings.
The cryptovolcanic structure was identified by its circular shape and the presence of breccia.  这个隐火山构造因其圆形形状和角砾岩的存在而被识别出来。

The formation of the maar is attributed to cryptovolcanic processes.  火山口的形成归因于潜火山作用过程。

synonym:subvolcanic
antonym:extrusive, surface volcanic

ETYMOLOGY->
From crypto- (hidden, concealed) + volcanic.  来自 crypto-（隐藏的，隐蔽的）+ volcanic（火山的）。
