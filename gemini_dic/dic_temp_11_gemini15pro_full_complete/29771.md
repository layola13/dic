cattiest:/ˈkætiɪst/
adjective
最刻薄的，最恶毒的
Superlative adjective of "catty," meaning deliberately hurtful in one's remarks, especially in a sly or indirect way.
She made the cattiest remark about her rival's dress. 她对竞争对手的着装做了最刻薄的评价。
近义词:bitchiest, meanest, spiteful, malicious
反义词:kindest, nicest, most generous
词性:adjective

catty:/ˈkæti/
adjective
刻薄的，恶毒的
Deliberately hurtful in one's remarks, especially in a sly or indirect way.
Her catty comments made everyone uncomfortable. 她刻薄的评论让每个人都不舒服。
近义词:bitchy, mean, spiteful, malicious
反义词:kind, nice, generous
词性:adjective
