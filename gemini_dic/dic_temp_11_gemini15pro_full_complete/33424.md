churchwardens:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃˌwɔːrdnz/ | n. | 教堂管理员；教会委员 |  An elected lay officer in the Church of England, who assists the rector or vicar in the administration of the parish. | The churchwardens were responsible for the upkeep of the church building. 教堂管理员负责维护教堂建筑。| The churchwardens organized a fundraising event for the new roof. 教堂管理员为新屋顶组织了一次筹款活动。 | synonyms: vestrymen, church officers | antonyms: N/A | noun
