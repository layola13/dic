mucker:/ˈmʌkər/| n.|伙伴，朋友；勤杂工；捣乱者|A person who works with or assists another, especially in difficult or unpleasant work; a close friend or companion; someone who makes a mess or causes trouble.|例句：He's been my mucker since childhood.  他从小就是我的好朋友。

例句：The muckers cleared the debris from the construction site.  清洁工清理了建筑工地的碎石。|近义词：pal, friend, mate, comrade, partner, laborer, cleaner|反义词：enemy, foe, opponent|词性:名词

mucker:/ˈmʌkər/| v.|清理，清除（尤指马厩）；捣乱，弄脏|To clean, especially a stable; to make a mess of; to bungle or mismanage.|例句：She spent the morning mucking out the stables. 她早上都在清理马厩。

例句：He really mucked up the presentation with his lack of preparation. 他的缺乏准备真的把演讲搞砸了。|近义词：clean, tidy, mess up, spoil, bungle|反义词：organize, arrange, neaten|词性:动词
