nonpatented: /ˌnɒnˈpeɪtəntɪd/ /ˌnɑːnˈpeɪtəntɪd/
adjective
未申请专利的；无专利的
not patented; not protected by a patent.
The company decided to sell the nonpatented technology to its competitors.  这家公司决定将未申请专利的技术卖给竞争对手。
Generic drugs are often based on nonpatented formulas.  非专利药通常基于无专利配方。
Synonyms: unpatented, proprietary (in some contexts)
Antonyms: patented, protected
词性: 形容词
