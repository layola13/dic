chromas: /ˈkroʊməz/ | n. | 色度，色彩，饱和度 | The intensity of a color, its degree of saturation; a color or shade. | The chromas of the sunset were vibrant and varied. 夕阳的色彩鲜艳而多变。 | synonyms: hues, tints, shades, tones, colors | antonyms: achromatic (lacking color) | noun

chromas: /ˈkroʊməz/ | n. | （复数）色品图上的坐标 | Coordinates on a chromaticity diagram representing a color. | The chromas of the LED light fell within the specified range.  LED灯的色品坐标在规定范围内。| synonyms: color coordinates | antonyms: N/A | noun
