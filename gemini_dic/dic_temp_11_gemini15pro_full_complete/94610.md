impregnant:/ɪmˈpreɡnənt/ | adj. | 怀孕的；充满的；饱和的 | Filled with or permeated by something; saturated. Also, pregnant (especially of an animal). | The air was impregnant with the smell of pine needles. 空气中弥漫着松针的气味。 | The cloth was impregnant with dye. 布料浸透了染料。 | Synonyms: saturated, permeated, imbued, steeped, charged | Antonyms: barren, empty, devoid, unsaturated | adjective


impregnant:/ɪmˈpreɡnənt/ | v. | 使怀孕；使充满；灌输 | To make pregnant; to fill or saturate something with something else.  |  He impregnated his wife. 他使他的妻子怀孕了。 |  The artist impregnated the canvas with vibrant colors. 艺术家在画布上注入了鲜艳的色彩。 | Synonyms: saturate, permeate, imbue, infuse, impregnate | Antonyms: deplete, empty, drain | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *impraegnare, from in- "in" + praegnans "pregnant."
