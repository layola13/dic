haloesque:/ˌheɪloʊˈɛsk/
adjective
像光环的，神圣的
Resembling or evocative of a halo; saintly or angelic in appearance or quality.
The painting had a haloesque quality, with light seemingly emanating from the figure's head. 这幅画有一种光环般的感觉，光线似乎从人物的头部散发出来。
halo-like, saintly, angelic, divine, ethereal
mundane, earthly, secular, profane
词性:形容词
