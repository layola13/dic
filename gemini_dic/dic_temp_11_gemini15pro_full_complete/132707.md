nondistortive:/ˌnɒndɪˈstɔːtɪv/ | adj. |非扭曲性的，不引起扭曲的 | Not causing distortion; preserving the original form or meaning. |  A nondistortive tax policy would not significantly alter consumer behavior.  非扭曲性税收政策不会显著改变消费者行为。

The report aims to provide a nondistortive view of the current situation. 该报告旨在提供对当前形势的非扭曲性观点。 | 近义词: neutral, unbiased, non-interfering | 反义词: distortive, biasing, influencing | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + distortive (causing distortion), from distort (to twist out of shape) + -ive (forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Latin distortus, past participle of distorquere (to twist different ways), from dis- (apart, asunder) + torquere (to twist).
