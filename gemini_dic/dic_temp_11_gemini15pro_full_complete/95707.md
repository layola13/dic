incongruence:/ɪnkənˈɡruːəns/ /ɪnkɑːŋˈɡruːəns/| n.|不一致，不协调，不和谐|lack of congruence or agreement; incompatibility.  The state or quality of being incongruous.|Her flamboyant style is an incongruence with the somber atmosphere of the office. 她华丽的风格与办公室沉闷的气氛格格不入。|Synonyms:  discrepancy, disparity, incompatibility, inconsistency, dissonance, clash, conflict|Antonyms: congruence, agreement, harmony, consistency, compatibility|noun
