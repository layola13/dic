all-time:/ˈɔːlˌtaɪm/ | adj. | 空前的；创纪录的；历来的 | Of or relating to the whole of the past; the best, highest, or lowest of its kind recorded. |  This is her all-time best performance. 这是她迄今为止最好的表演。| 近义词: record-breaking, record-setting, unprecedented, unparalleled | 反义词: average, mediocre, ordinary | 词性: 形容词

all-time:/ˈɔːlˌtaɪm/ | n. |  (通常不用于复数) 历史，所有时间 |  A period of time encompassing the past, present, and future. | This event marked a turning point for all time. 这个事件标志着一个历史性的转折点. (这个用法相对少见) | 近义词: history, eternity | 反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑 moment, instant) | 词性: 名词
