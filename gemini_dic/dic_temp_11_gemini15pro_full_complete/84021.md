half-drunken:/ˌhɑːf ˈdrʌŋkən/ | adj. | 半醉的，微醉的 | Slightly drunk; intoxicated to a moderate degree. | He stumbled into the room, half-drunken and mumbling. 他跌跌撞撞地走进房间，半醉着，嘴里嘟囔着。|  She gave a half-drunken giggle. 她半醉地咯咯笑着。| 近义词: tipsy, slightly intoxicated | 反义词: sober, completely drunk | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "half-" (Old English healf) + "drunken" (past participle of "drink," Old English drincan).  The word signifies being partially affected by alcohol.
