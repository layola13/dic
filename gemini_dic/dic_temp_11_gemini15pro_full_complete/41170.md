copycats:/ˈkɒpiˌkæts/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpiˌkæts/ (US)| n.|模仿者，抄袭者|A person who copies another's behavior, work, etc.|例句：He's such a copycat, always wearing the same clothes as his older brother. 他真是个爱模仿的人，总是穿和他哥哥一样的衣服。

例句：Many new businesses are just copycats, offering nothing original. 很多新企业只是模仿者，没有提供任何原创的东西。|近义词：imitator, mimic, emulator|反义词：innovator, originator|词性：名词
