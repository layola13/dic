adjudication:/əˌdʒuːdɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 裁决；判决；宣判 |The formal judgment of a court or other tribunal; the act of adjudicating. |The adjudication of the bankruptcy case took several months. 破产案件的裁决花了几个月的时间。| Synonyms: judgment, ruling, decision, determination, decree, verdict, settlement | Antonyms: indecision,  hesitation | noun

adjudicate:/əˈdʒuːdɪkeɪt/ | v. | 裁决；判决；宣判 | To make a formal judgment or decision about a dispute or problem. | The judge adjudicated the case in favor of the plaintiff. 法官裁决原告胜诉。| Synonyms: judge, rule, decide, determine, settle, arbitrate | Antonyms: defer, postpone, waive | verb
