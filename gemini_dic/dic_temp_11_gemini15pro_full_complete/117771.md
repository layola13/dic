meld:/mɛld/| v. n.| 混合，合并；融合 |To blend or merge; to combine or unite. |例句：The two companies meld their resources to create a new product.  两家公司合并资源以创造新产品。|近义词：blend, merge, combine, fuse, amalgamate|反义词：separate, divide, disintegrate|词性:动词, 名词

meld:/mɛld/| n.| 混合物；融合 |A mixture; a combination of different things. |例句：The final product was a meld of different styles and influences. 最终产品是不同风格和影响的融合。|近义词：mixture, blend, combination, fusion|反义词：separation, division|词性:名词
