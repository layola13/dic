monodentate:/ˌmɒnəʊˈdɛnteɪt/ | adj. | 单齿的，单配位的 | A monodentate ligand is a ligand that bonds to a central metal atom through only one donor atom.  |  Example 1: Ammonia is a monodentate ligand. 氨是单齿配体。

Example 2:  Water acts as a monodentate ligand in many complexes.  水在许多络合物中充当单齿配体。 | 近义词: unidentate | 反义词: multidentate, polydentate | 词性: adjective


词源:

From mono- (one) + dentate (having teeth, from Latin dentatus).
