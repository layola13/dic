fellifluous:/fɛˈlɪfluːəs/
adjective
流畅的，流利的
flowing smoothly and copiously.
Her fellifluous prose delighted readers. 她流畅的散文使读者感到愉悦。
近义词:fluent, eloquent, articulate
反义词:halting, inarticulate, incoherent
词性:adjective
