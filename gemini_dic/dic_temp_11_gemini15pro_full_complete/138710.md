nullify:/ˈnʌlɪfaɪ/| vt.|使无效，取消，废除|To make legally null and void; invalidate.  To deprive of value or effectiveness; make futile.|The contract was nullified due to a breach of terms.  由于违反条款，合同被废除。|Synonyms: invalidate, annul, void, repeal, abolish, cancel|Antonyms: validate, confirm, ratify, uphold|verb
