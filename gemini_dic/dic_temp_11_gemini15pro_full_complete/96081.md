indefatigably:/ˌɪndɪˈfætɪɡəbli/
adverb
不知疲倦地
Persistently and tirelessly; not easily made tired.
She worked indefatigably on the project for months. 她不知疲倦地在这个项目上工作了几个月。
He pursued his goals indefatigably. 他不知疲倦地追求着自己的目标。
tirelessly, relentlessly, persistently, unflaggingly
lazily, listlessly, apathetically
adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + Latin defatigare (to tire out) + -ably (in a manner that).
