cede:/siːd/
verb
割让；放弃；交出
to give something such as land, power, or control to someone else, especially when you are forced to do so
例句：
He was forced to cede control of the company.  他被迫放弃了对公司的控制权。

Hong Kong was ceded to Britain after the Opium War. 香港在鸦片战争后被割让给英国。

近义词：relinquish, surrender, yield
反义词：retain, acquire, seize
词性：v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin cedere "to go, withdraw, yield," from PIE root *ked- "to go, yield."
