brachystochrone:/ˌbrækɪˈstɒkrəʊn/ (US) /ˌbrækɪˈstəʊkroʊn/| n. |最速降线|A curve between two points along which a body descending solely by gravity travels from the higher to the lower point in the shortest time.|The brachistochrone problem challenged mathematicians for centuries. 最速降线问题困扰了数学家几个世纪。|Synonyms: cycloid (in a uniform gravitational field), curve of fastest descent|Antonyms: N/A|noun

Etymology: From Greek *brachistos* (shortest) + *chronos* (time).
