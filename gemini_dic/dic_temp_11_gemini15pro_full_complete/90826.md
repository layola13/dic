hunchback: /ˈhʌntʃbæk/ (US), /ˈhʌntʃbæk/ (UK) | n. | 驼背；驼背的人 |  A rounded hump on the back, typically caused by a curvature of the spine. Also refers to a person with such a hump. |  The hunchback of Notre Dame is a famous literary character.  巴黎圣母院的驼背是一个著名的文学人物。 |  kyphosis, humpback |  straight back | noun

hunchbacked: /ˈhʌntʃbækt/ (US), /ˈhʌntʃbækt/ (UK) | adj. | 驼背的；有驼背的 | Having a hunchback. | He was a small, hunchbacked man. 他是一个矮小的驼背男人。 |  kyphosic, humpbacked | straight-backed | adjective


Etymology:
From "hunch" (meaning a lump or hump) + "back".  "Hunch" is likely of Scandinavian origin, related to Norwegian "hunkr" (mass, lump).
