abnormity:/æbnɔːrˈmɪti/| n. |异常，畸形，反常|Something that is abnormal; a malformation or deformity. abnormity:/æbnɔːrˈmɪti/| n. |不正常，变态|A quality or state of being abnormal; deviation from the average or norm, especially in behavior or character.  A deviation from what is accepted as normal or right.|His obsession with cleanliness bordered on abnormity. 他对清洁的痴迷近乎变态。|Synonyms:  deviancy, perversion, eccentricity|Antonyms: normality, normalcy, conformity|noun
