maos:/maʊz/| n.| 毛泽东思想（通常缩写为 Maoism）|  A body of political, military, and economic theories derived from the writings of Mao Zedong (Mao Tse-tung), adapted from Marxism-Leninism to the conditions of pre-revolutionary China.  It emphasizes the importance of the peasantry, of small-scale industry, and of guerilla warfare.|  The Cultural Revolution was a direct result of maos. 文化大革命是毛泽东思想的直接结果。| synonym: Maoism, Mao Zedong Thought | antonym:  (No direct antonym, but opposing ideologies could include capitalism, liberalism, etc.) | noun
