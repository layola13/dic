bosomier:/ˈbuːzəmiər/| adj.|更丰满的，更亲密的|More intimate or confidential; affecting one's deepest feelings.|She shared her bosomier secrets with her closest friend. 她和她最亲密的朋友分享了她最深处的秘密。|近义词：closer, intimate, confidential|反义词：distant, aloof|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From bosom + -ier (comparative suffix). Bosom itself comes from Old English *bōsm, meaning "breast, lap, womb".  The figurative sense of "close, intimate" developed from the idea of holding someone close to one's breast.
