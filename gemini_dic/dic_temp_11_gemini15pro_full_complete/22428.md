bookcrossing:/ˈbʊkˌkrɒsɪŋ/| n.|图书漂流，图书环游|The practice of leaving a book in a public place for someone else to pick up and read, and then releasing it back into the wild.|例句：She discovered bookcrossing while on vacation and has been an avid participant ever since. 她度假时发现了图书漂流，从此成为了一名狂热的参与者。

例句：Bookcrossing is a fun way to share your love of reading with others. 图书漂流是一个与他人分享阅读乐趣的有趣方式。|近义词：book swapping, book sharing|反义词: book hoarding|词性:noun
