difficulties:/ˈdɪfɪkəltiz/
n.|困难，难处，难题|Things that are hard to do, understand, or deal with.|We encountered several difficulties during the project. 我们在项目中遇到了一些困难。|近义词：problems, challenges, obstacles, hurdles, setbacks|反义词:easiness, simplicities|词性:名词

Etymology: From Middle French *difficulté*, from Latin *difficultas*, from *difficilis* ("difficult").
