didrachma:/dɪˈdrækmə/| n.|双德拉克马（古希腊货币单位，等于两个德拉克马）|A didrachma was an ancient Greek silver coin equivalent to two drachmas. |The temple tax was half a shekel, which was equivalent to a didrachma. 圣殿税是半舍客勒，相当于一个双德拉克马。|Synonyms: stater (sometimes), double drachma|Antonyms: N/A (lack of a single direct antonym for a unit of currency)|noun
