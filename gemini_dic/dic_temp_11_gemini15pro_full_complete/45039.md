cub:/kʌb/
n.
幼崽; (熊、狮、狼等的)幼兽
a young animal of certain mammals, such as bears, lions, or wolves
The lioness protected her cubs fiercely. 母狮凶猛地保护着她的幼崽。

young person, especially an inexperienced one
年轻人，尤指没有经验的人
He's just a cub reporter, straight out of college. 他只是个刚从大学毕业的初出茅庐的记者。

近义词:youngster, offspring, whelp (especially for dogs)
反义词:adult, mature (animal)

词性:noun
