overapprehensions: /ˌoʊvərˌæprɪˈhɛnʃənz/
noun
过分忧虑，过分担心
Excessive anxieties or fears, often about things that are unlikely to happen.
Her overapprehensions about the trip were unfounded; everything went smoothly. 她对这次旅行的过分担心是没有根据的；一切都很顺利。
Near Synonyms: anxieties, worries, fears, concerns, misgivings
Antonyms: calmness, composure, serenity, tranquility
n.
