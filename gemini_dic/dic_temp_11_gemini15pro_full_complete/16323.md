barbacanage:/ˌbɑːrbækənɪdʒ/ |n.|城垛，城墙上的垛口；（尤指）射击孔|A defensive fortification or architectural feature, specifically the apertures or embrasures in a parapet wall, through which arrows or other missiles could be discharged.|The castle's barbacanage provided excellent cover for the archers. 城堡的射击孔为弓箭手提供了绝佳的掩护.|Synonyms: embrasures, loopholes, crenellations|Antonyms: opening, gap|Noun
