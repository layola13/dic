oenophile:/ˈiːnəfaɪl/ | n. |  葡萄酒爱好者；品酒行家 | A connoisseur of wines; a person who enjoys wine. | He's a true oenophile, with a cellar full of vintage wines. 他是一位真正的葡萄酒爱好者，酒窖里收藏了大量的年份葡萄酒。 | 近义词: wine lover, wine connoisseur, vintner | 反义词: teetotaler, abstainer | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *oinos* (wine) + *philos* (loving).
