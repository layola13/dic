overinflated:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈfleɪtɪd/
adjective
过分膨胀的；过分夸大的
inflated beyond normal or desirable limits; exaggerated.
The housing market is overinflated and due for a correction.  房地产市场过热，即将迎来调整。
The CEO's ego was overinflated by years of constant praise.  多年的持续赞扬让这位CEO的自我膨胀得过分。
Synonyms: inflated, bloated, exaggerated, overblown, hyped up
Antonyms: deflated, understated, realistic, modest
词性: 形容词
