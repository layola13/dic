de-powers:/diːˈpaʊəz/
|v.|使失去权力，剥夺权力|To deprive of power or authority; to reduce the power or influence of someone or something.|The new law de-powers local governments. 新法律剥夺了地方政府的权力。|Synonyms: weaken, disable, disempower|Antonyms: empower, strengthen|verb


de-powers:/diːˈpaʊəz/
|n.|剥夺权力|The act of depriving of power or authority.|The de-powers of the king led to a revolution. 国王的权力被剥夺导致了革命。|Synonyms: weakening, disabling, disempowering|Antonyms: empowerment, strengthening|noun
