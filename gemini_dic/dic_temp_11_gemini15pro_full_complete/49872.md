demotivational:/ˌdiːˌmoʊtɪˈveɪʃənl/
adjective
消极的；使人泄气的
Reducing or destroying motivation.  Causing someone to lose enthusiasm or interest.
The constant criticism had a demotivational effect on the team.  不断的批评对团队产生了消极的影响。
synonyms: discouraging, disheartening, dispiriting
antonyms: motivating, inspiring, encouraging
词性: adjective


demotivation:/ˌdiːˌmoʊtɪˈveɪʃən/
noun
消极性；使人泄气的事物
The act or process of demotivating someone, or the state of being demotivated.
The team's demotivation was evident in their poor performance. 该团队的消极情绪在他们糟糕的表现中显而易见。
synonyms: discouragement, disillusionment
antonyms: motivation, inspiration, encouragement
词性: noun
