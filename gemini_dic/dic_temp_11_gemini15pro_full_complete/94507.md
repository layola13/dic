importunate:/ɪmˈpɔːrtʃənət/ | adj. | 持续烦扰的；纠缠不休的；再三恳求的 | Persistent, especially to the point of annoyance or intrusion; making repeated or insistent requests. |  The child's importunate demands for a new toy finally wore down his parents.  这个孩子不断要求买新玩具，最终他的父母妥协了。

| Synonyms: persistent, insistent, demanding, relentless, pertinacious | Antonyms: reserved, reticent, unassuming, modest, diffident | adjective |


importunately:/ɪmˈpɔːrtʃənətli/ | adv. | 持续烦扰地；纠缠不休地；再三恳求地 |  In a persistently annoying or intrusive manner; with repeated or insistent requests. | He importunately knocked on the door until someone answered. 他不停地敲门，直到有人来应门。

| Synonyms: persistently, insistently, pressingly | Antonyms: reservedly, reticently, modestly | adverb |


importunateness:/ɪmˈpɔːrtʃənətnəs/ | n. | 持续烦扰；纠缠不休；再三恳求 | The quality of being persistent to the point of annoyance or intrusion; the act of making repeated or insistent requests. | The importunateness of the salesman made her leave the store.  售货员的纠缠不休让她离开了商店。

| Synonyms: persistence, insistence, demandingness | Antonyms: reserve, reticence, modesty | noun |
