overboastfulness: /ˌoʊvərboʊstfəlnəs/ | n. | 过度自夸，夸大其词 | The state of boasting excessively, characterized by arrogant and exaggerated claims about oneself or one's achievements. | His overboastfulness alienated his friends. 他的过度自夸疏远了他的朋友。|  Synonyms: arrogance, bragging, conceit, vainglory, pomposity | Antonyms: humility, modesty, understatement | noun


Etymology: "over" (OE. ofer) + "boast" (ME. bosten, from OF. boster) + "ful" (OE. full) + "ness" (OE. -nes(s))
