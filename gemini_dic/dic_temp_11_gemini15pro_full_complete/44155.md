criticisable:/ˈkrɪtɪsaɪzəbl/ (UK) /ˈkrɪtɪsaɪzəbəl/ (US)| adj.|可批评的，可指责的|Open to or deserving of criticism.  Susceptible to being criticized; worthy of criticism.|Her decision to leave the company was highly criticisable.|Synonyms:  censurable, objectionable, reprehensible, blameworthy, faulty, flawed|Antonyms:  praiseworthy, commendable, laudable, impeccable, faultless|adjective
