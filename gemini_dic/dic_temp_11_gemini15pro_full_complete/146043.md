overdriven:/ˌoʊvərˈdrɪvən/| adj.|过度驱动的；过载的；失真的|Driven or worked to exhaustion or beyond normal capacity; also, of an amplifier or electronic circuit: distorted by excessive input.|The guitarist's overdriven sound filled the room. 吉他手过度驱动的音效充满了整个房间.|近义词：overworked, overloaded, strained, distorted|反义词:underdriven, relaxed, moderate|词性:形容词
overdrive:/ˌoʊvərˈdraɪv/| n. v.|超速档；过度驱动；加班加点|A gear in a motor vehicle providing a higher speed with reduced engine revolutions; also, the act of working or driving something too hard or beyond its normal capacity.|He put the car into overdrive to pass the truck. 他挂上超速档超过了卡车。|近义词：boost, accelerate, amplify|反义词:underdrive, decelerate, reduce|词性:名词,动词
