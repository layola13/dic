indestructibility:/ˌɪndɪˌstrʌktɪˈbɪləti/
n.
不可摧毁性；坚不可摧
The quality or state of being impossible to destroy.
The indestructibility of diamonds makes them valuable. 钻石的坚不可摧性使它们很有价值。
synonyms:  imperishability, invincibility, permanence, durability
antonyms: fragility, vulnerability, destructibility
noun
