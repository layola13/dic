devilize:/ˈdiːvəlaɪz/ /ˈdɛvəlaɪz/
verb
使失去魔力; 驱除邪魔; 去魔化; 使不再邪恶
To rid of evil or demonic influence; to make no longer devilish.
The priest devilized the haunted house.  牧师为闹鬼的房子驱魔。
The therapist helped him devilize his thoughts.  治疗师帮助他驱除了他邪恶的想法。
近义词: exorcise, purify, cleanse
反义词: demonize, corrupt
词性: verb
