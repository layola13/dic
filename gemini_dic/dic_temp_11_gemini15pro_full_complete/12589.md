assafoetida: /ˌæsəˈfɛtɪdə/ (US), /ˌæsəˈfiːtɪdə/ (UK)| n. |阿魏，阿魏胶|A hard, resinous gum with a pungent, fetid odor obtained from the roots of several species of Ferula, used in medicine and as a seasoning, especially in Indian cuisine.| The recipe called for a pinch of assafoetida.  这食谱需要一小撮阿魏。|Synonym:  hing, devil's dung, stinking gum|Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a specific spice.
