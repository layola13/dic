irritation:/ˌɪrɪˈteɪʃn/ (US), /ˌɪrɪˈteɪʃən/ (UK)| n.|刺激； раздражение；irritation; Ärger;|The constant noise was a source of irritation.  持续不断的噪音令人烦躁。|例句：The traffic noise is a constant irritation. 交通噪音是一种持续的刺激。  The sand in my shoes was a minor irritation. 鞋子里的沙子是一个小小的刺激。|近义词：annoyance, exasperation, frustration, provocation|反义词:pleasure, delight, soothe, calmness|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin irritatio(n-), from the verb irritare (see irritate).
