gneissic:/ˈnaɪsɪk/| adj.|片麻岩的；片麻状的|Resembling or characteristic of gneiss; composed of or containing gneiss.  类似于或具有片麻岩特征的；由片麻岩组成或含有片麻岩的。|The geologist identified the rock as gneissic. 地质学家将这块岩石鉴定为片麻岩的。|Synonyms: metamorphic, foliated|Antonyms: non-foliated, amorphous|adjective
