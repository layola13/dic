ingeminate:/ɪnˈdʒemɪneɪt/ | v. | 重复，反复说 | to repeat or say something again, especially a word or sound |  The speaker ingeminated the importance of voting. 演讲者反复强调投票的重要性。|  reiterate, repeat, restate | vary, diversify | verb
ingeminate:/ɪnˈdʒemɪneɪt/ | adj. | 重复的，双重的 |  doubled, twinned; (Botany) growing in pairs | The ingeminate leaves of the plant were a distinctive feature. 这种植物成对生长的叶子是一个显著特征。| doubled, twinned, paired | single, solitary | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin ingeminatus, past participle of ingeminare ("to double, redouble, repeat"), from in- ("in, on") + geminare ("to double"), from geminus ("twin").
