mackintoshed:/ˌmækɪnˈtɒʃt/ | adj. | 穿着防水衣的；受麦金托什思想影响的 | Wearing a Macintosh raincoat; influenced by the philosophy of Charles Macintosh. |  He arrived mackintoshed and umbrellaed, ready for the downpour. 他穿着雨衣，带着雨伞来了，准备迎接倾盆大雨。|  The mackintoshed figure disappeared into the fog.  穿着雨衣的身影消失在雾中。 | 近义词: raincoated, waterproofed | 反义词: exposed | 词性:形容词

词源: From Charles Macintosh (1766–1843), Scottish chemist, inventor of waterproof fabric. 源于查尔斯·麦金托什（1766-1843），苏格兰化学家，防水织物发明者。
