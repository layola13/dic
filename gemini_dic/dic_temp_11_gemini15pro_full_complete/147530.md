overpassionately:/ˌoʊvərˈpæʃənɪtli/
adverb
过度热情地；过分激动地
With too much passion; excessively emotionally.
She pleaded overpassionately with the judge to spare her son. 她过度热情地恳求法官饶恕她的儿子。
He delivered the speech overpassionately, making the audience uncomfortable.  他演讲时过于激动，让观众感到不舒服。
Synonyms: excessively, immoderately, extravagantly, fervently, vehemently
Antonyms: apathetically, indifferently, calmly, dispassionately
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + passionately.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," and "passionately" is an adverb derived from the noun "passion," ultimately from the Latin "passio" meaning "suffering" or "strong emotion."
