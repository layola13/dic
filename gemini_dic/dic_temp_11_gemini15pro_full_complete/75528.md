fufu:/ˈfuːfuː/| n.|富富（西非的一种淀粉类食物）|A starchy dish common in West Africa, made from boiled and pounded starchy vegetables such as cassava, yams, or plantains.|例句：Fufu is a staple food in many West African countries. 富富是许多西非国家的主食。

例句：She served the fufu with a flavorful peanut soup. 她用美味的花生汤配富富。|近义词：eba (Nigeria), ugali (East Africa), sadza (Southern Africa) |反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific food item)|词性:名词
