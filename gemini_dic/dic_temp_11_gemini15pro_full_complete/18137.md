behove:/bɪˈhuːv/ /bɪˈhoʊv/
verb
理应，应该
It behoves someone to do something means that it is right, proper, or necessary for them to do it.
It ill behoves you to (= you should not) speak so rudely to your elders. 你对长辈如此粗鲁说话是极不应该的。
It behoves us to consider the problem carefully. 我们有必要仔细考虑这个问题。
fit, proper, suitable, appropriate, apt
wrong, improper, inappropriate
词性:动词
