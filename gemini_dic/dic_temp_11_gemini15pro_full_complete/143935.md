outpushed:/ˌaʊtˈpʊʃt/
verb
超越，胜过; 比...更努力地推进
surpassed in pushing; pushed harder than
The smaller company outpushed its larger rivals in the race to develop the new technology.  这家小公司在开发新技术的竞赛中胜过了规模更大的竞争对手。
He outpushed the other climbers and reached the summit first. 他比其他登山者更努力地向上攀登，第一个到达顶峰。
Synonyms: outdo, outperform, surpass, excel, exceed
Antonyms: lag behind, fall behind
verb
