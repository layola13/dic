joculatory:/ˈdʒɒkjʊlətəri/ (US) /ˈdʒɑːkjʊlətɔːri/| adj.|滑稽的，戏谑的，爱开玩笑的|Given to or characterized by joking; playful and humorous.|He made a joculatory remark about her new hairstyle. 他对她的新发型开了个玩笑。|近义词：jocular, playful, humorous, teasing, witty, facetious|反义词：serious, solemn, earnest|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *joculatorius, from Latin joculari (“to jest”), from jocus (“jest, joke”).
