kumer:/ˈkuːmər/| n. |库默尔人（指来自印度北方邦和比哈尔邦的男性移民工人，有时带有贬义）|A derogatory term referring to male migrant labourers, particularly from the states of Uttar Pradesh and Bihar in India.  It often carries connotations of being uncouth, uneducated, and involved in low-skilled labor.  Its usage is considered offensive and discriminatory.| He called them "kumers," a derogatory term for migrant workers. 他称他们为“库默尔人”，这是对移民工人的贬义词。|近义词: migrant worker, laborer (though these are neutral and not derogatory)|反义词: N/A (no direct opposite for a derogatory term)|词性:noun
