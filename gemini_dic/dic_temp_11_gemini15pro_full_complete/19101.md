betterer:/ˈbetərər/ | adj. | 更好的 (non-standard) | A non-standard and grammatically incorrect form of "better," sometimes used humorously or childishly. It attempts to create a comparative form of "better," which is already the comparative form of "good." |  "My drawing is way betterer than yours!" (humorous/childish) | 近义词: better, superior | 反义词: worse, inferior | 词性: 形容词

词源: 'Better' comes from Old English 'betera,' comparative of 'gōd' (good).  'Betterer' is a non-standard double comparative formed by adding '-er' to 'better.'
