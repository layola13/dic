overdirecting:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈrɛktɪŋ/ | v. | 过度指导，过度指挥 |  Giving too much instruction or guidance, often to the point of hindering performance or creativity. | The manager was overdirecting the team, stifling their initiative.  经理过度指导团队，扼杀了他们的主动性。 | Synonyms: micromanaging, overmanaging | Antonyms: empowering, delegating | verb

overdirecting:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈrɛktɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度指导的，过度指挥的 | Characterized by excessive direction or guidance. |  His overdirecting style made it difficult for employees to work independently. 他过度指导的风格使员工难以独立工作。 | Synonyms: controlling, domineering | Antonyms: supportive, facilitative | adjective
