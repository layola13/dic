irksome:/ˈɜːrksəm/ | adj. | 令人厌烦的，讨厌的 | Causing annoyance or vexation.  |  The constant dripping of the faucet was irksome. 水龙头不停地滴水很烦人。 |  annoying, tedious, bothersome, vexing, irritating | pleasant, agreeable, enjoyable | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English irk + -some.  Irk comes from Old Norse yrkja "to work," suggesting the original sense of "causing weariness from work."
