nonacquisitive:/ˌnɒnˌækˈwɪzɪtɪv/
adjective
不求获利的；非贪婪的
Not excessively interested in acquiring money or material possessions.
She leads a simple, nonacquisitive life, valuing experiences over possessions. 她过着简单朴素的生活，重视体验而非物质财富。
He's a nonacquisitive person, content with what he has. 他是一个不贪婪的人，满足于他所拥有的。
近义词:unacquisitive, non-materialistic, unselfish, ascetic
反义词:acquisitive, greedy, materialistic, avaricious
词性:adjective
