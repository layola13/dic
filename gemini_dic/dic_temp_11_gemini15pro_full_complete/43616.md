creasing:/ˈkriːsɪŋ/ | n. | 折痕，皱褶 | The act of making a crease or fold in something. | The constant folding and unfolding caused unsightly creasing in the map.  不断地折叠和展开导致地图上出现了难看的折痕。 | synonyms: folding, wrinkling, furrowing | antonyms: smoothing, flattening | noun

creasing:/ˈkriːsɪŋ/ | adj. |  (rarely used)  形成折痕的;  |  Causing creases.  | This type of fabric is very creasing. 这种面料很容易起皱。 | synonyms: wrinkling, crinkling | antonyms: smoothing, non-creasing | adjective
