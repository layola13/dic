giving:/ˈɡɪvɪŋ/| n. adj.|给予，赠送；礼物；捐赠（名词）；慷慨的，乐于助人的（形容词）|The act of bestowing something as a gift, or something given.  It can also describe someone generous or charitable.|She found great joy in giving to charity. 她发现给予慈善很大的快乐。|近义词：donating, contributing, generous, charitable|反义词：receiving, taking, selfish, stingy|词性: noun, adjective
Etymology: From Old English "giefan" (to give),  from Proto-Germanic "*geban," ultimately from Proto-Indo-European "*ghabh-."
