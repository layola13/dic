discourser:/dɪˈskɔːrsər/| n.| 演讲者，论述者，谈话者|A discourser is someone who speaks or writes about a particular subject, especially in a formal or academic way.  A discourser is a person who engages in discourse, often implying a more active and thoughtful role than simply speaking or writing.|The renowned discourser captivated the audience with his insightful analysis of the political landscape.|近义词：speaker, orator, lecturer, writer, author, essayist|反义词:listener, audience|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: discourser, from Old French discourseor, from discourre ‘run about, discourse’, from Latin discursus, from the verb discurrere.
