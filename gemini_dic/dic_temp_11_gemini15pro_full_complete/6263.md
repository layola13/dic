abolition:/ˌæbəˈlɪʃən/ | n. | 废除，废止 |The act of abolishing a system, practice, or institution.  The abolition of slavery was a landmark achievement. | 例句：The abolition of slavery marked a turning point in American history.  美国奴隶制的废除标志着美国历史的一个转折点。

例句：The group campaigned for the abolition of the death penalty.  该团体为废除死刑而奔走活动。

近义词： eradication, elimination, termination | 反义词： establishment, institution, enactment | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin abolitio, from abolere "to abolish," from ab- "from, away" + olere "to grow" (related to alere "to nourish"). The sense development is "to cause to decay, to destroy, to do away with."
