inkstone:/ˈɪŋkˌstoʊn/| n. |砚台；墨砚|A flat, smooth stone used for grinding inkstick and water to produce ink for calligraphy or painting.|The calligrapher carefully ground the inkstick on the inkstone. 书法家小心翼翼地在砚台上研墨。|Synonyms: ink slab, ink pallet|Antonyms: N/A|noun
