backhand:/ˈbækˌhænd/| n. vt. adj.|反手击球，反手拍，反手的|A stroke played with the back of the hand facing the direction of the stroke.  A backhand can also refer to the side of the body where the non-dominant hand is located.|He hit a powerful backhand down the line. 他沿着边线打了一个强有力的反手球。|近义词：backhand stroke, backhand shot|反义词:forehand|词性:名词, 动词, 形容词

backhands:/ˈbækˌhændz/| n. vt.|反手击球（复数），反手拍（复数）|Plural form of "backhand," referring to multiple backhand strokes or shots.|She practiced her backhands for hours. 她练习了几个小时的反手击球。|近义词：backhand strokes, backhand shots|反义词:forehands|词性:名词, 动词
