disassemblable:/ˌdɪsəˈsɛmbləbl/ | adj. | 可拆卸的 | Able to be disassembled; capable of being taken apart. | The furniture is disassemblable for easy moving.  这家具可拆卸，便于搬运。|  The toy is disassemblable, allowing children to explore its parts. 这个玩具可以拆卸，让孩子们探索它的各个部分。 | synonyms:  detachable, modular, demountable, breakdownable,  knock-down | antonyms:  fixed, permanent, non-detachable, inseparable | adjective

disassemble:/ˌdɪsəˈsɛmbl/ | v. | 拆卸，拆开 | To take something apart; to separate something into its component parts. | I disassembled the computer to clean the dust out. 我拆开了电脑来清理灰尘。 |  He disassembled the engine to find the source of the problem. 他拆卸了发动机以查找问题的根源。| synonyms: dismantle, take apart, break down,  deconstruct,  unbuild | antonyms: assemble, construct, put together, build | verb
