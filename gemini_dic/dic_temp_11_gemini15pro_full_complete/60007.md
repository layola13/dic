editorials:/ˌɛdɪˈtɔːriəlz/ | n. | 社论，评论文章 | An article in a newspaper or magazine that expresses the opinion of the editor or publisher. | The newspaper's editorials have been critical of the government's handling of the economy. 这家报纸的社论一直批评政府对经济的处理方式。 | synonyms:  leading articles, comments, opinions, commentaries | antonyms:  news reports, objective reporting
editorials:/ˌɛdɪˈtɔːriəlz/ | n. | (电视或广播的)评论节目 | A program on television or radio that expresses the opinion of the station or network. |  The news channel aired a series of editorials on the upcoming election.  新闻频道播出关于即将到来的选举的一系列评论节目。| synonyms: commentaries, opinion pieces | antonyms: news broadcasts, factual programs
