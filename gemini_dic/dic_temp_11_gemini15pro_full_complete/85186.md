harems:/ˈhɛərəmz/ | n. | 后宫，妻妾 | A group of women kept as wives or concubines by a man, especially in a Muslim household. | The sultan's harems were legendary for their beauty and intrigue. 苏丹的后宫以其美丽和阴谋而闻名。| The king kept a harem of wives and concubines. 国王拥有一个由妻子和妃嫔组成的后宫。| synonyms: seraglio, zenana | antonyms: monogamy | noun
