newspaperless:/ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpərlɪs/ | adj. | 无报纸的；不用报纸的 |  Without newspapers; not using newspapers.  Characterized by the absence or reduced use of physical newspapers, often in favor of digital news sources. |  Many people are becoming newspaperless and getting their news online. 很多人都不再看报纸，而是从网上获取新闻。|  digital, online, paperless | print, traditional | adjective
