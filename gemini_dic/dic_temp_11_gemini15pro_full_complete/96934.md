inextension:/ˌɪnɪkˈstenʃən/ | n. | 不可伸展性，不能伸长 |The inextension of a beam is a simplifying assumption in some engineering calculations.  This means the beam's length remains constant under load. |  The bridge's design relies on the inextension of its main support beams.  这座桥的设计依赖于其主要支撑梁的不可伸展性。 The inextension of the cable allowed it to support the heavy weight.  缆绳的不可伸展性使其能够支撑重物。 |近义词: inflexibility, rigidity | 反义词: extensibility, flexibility | 词性: 名词
