contradictors:/ˌkɑːntrəˈdɪktərz/ | n. |反驳者，矛盾者| People who contradict or deny the truth of something. | The politician's contradictors were quick to point out flaws in his argument. 这位政治家的反驳者很快指出了他论点中的缺陷。| The new evidence presented by the lawyer silenced the contradictors. 律师提出的新证据让反驳者哑口无言。 |  Synonyms: opponents, challengers, dissenters, gainsayers | Antonyms: supporters, proponents, advocates | Noun
