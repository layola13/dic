mesoplast:/ˈmɛsəplæst/| n. | 中胚层；叶肉 | The mesoplast is the tissue of a plant leaf between the upper and lower epidermis, containing the parenchyma cells and vascular bundles.  中胚层是植物叶片上下表皮之间的组织，包含薄壁细胞和维管束。| The mesoplast is where most photosynthesis takes place. 叶肉是大多数光合作用发生的地方。|Synonyms: mesophyll | Antonyms: None | Noun
