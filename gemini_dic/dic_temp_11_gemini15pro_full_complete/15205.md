backcourt:/ˈbækˌkɔːrt/| n.|后场|The area of a basketball, badminton, volleyball, or squash court furthest from the net or basket.  It also refers to the two guards in a basketball team who usually play in the backcourt. |The point guard brought the ball up the court from the backcourt. 控球后卫把球从后场运到前场。|The team's backcourt was particularly strong this season. 这个队的后场本赛季特别强。|近义词：rear court|反义词：frontcourt|词性:名词
