nonenviably:/ˌnɒnˈɛnviəbli/
adjective
不令人羡慕地，不幸地
in a way that is not enviable; unfortunately.
He was nonenviably placed between two warring factions. 他不幸地夹在两个交战派别之间。

The team is nonenviably positioned at the bottom of the league.  这个队伍不幸地处于联赛垫底的位置。

unfavorably, undesirably, disadvantageously
enviably, favorably, fortunately
adjective
