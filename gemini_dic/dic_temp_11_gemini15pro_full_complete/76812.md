gamopetalous: /ˌɡæmoʊˈpɛtələs/ | adj. | 合瓣的 | Having petals united or fused, at least at the base. |  Example: The morning glory flower is gamopetalous, with its petals forming a funnel shape. 牵牛花是合瓣的，它的花瓣形成了漏斗状。

 Example:  Gamopetalous flowers are common in the Solanaceae family. 合瓣花在茄科植物中很常见。 | 近义词: sympetalous | 反义词: polypetalous | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From gamo- (combining form meaning "married," "united," from Greek gamos "marriage") + petal + -ous (adjective suffix).
