oas:/oʊˈeɪs/ | n. | 绿洲;  舒适的地方; |A fertile spot in a desert where water is found. A pleasant or peaceful place or period in the midst of difficulty or trouble. |  We found an oasis of calm in the bustling city. 我们在繁忙的城市中找到了一片宁静的绿洲。| synonym: haven, refuge, sanctuary | antonym: desert, wasteland | noun
