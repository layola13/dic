anagrammatical:/ˌænəɡrəˈmætɪkəl/
adjective
字谜的，由字谜构成的
formed by rearranging the letters of a different word or phrase
例句：The word "listen" is anagrammatical to "silent".  单词“listen”是“silent”的字谜。

例句：He enjoyed creating anagrammatical puzzles for his friends. 他喜欢为朋友们创作字谜游戏。
近义词：transposed, rearranged
反义词：original
