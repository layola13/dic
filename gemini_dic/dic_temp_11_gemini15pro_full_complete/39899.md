constitutionally:/ˌkɒnstɪˈtjuːʃənəli/ /ˌkɑːnstɪˈtuːʃənəli/
adverb
合乎宪法地；体质上；本质上
in a way that is allowed by or contained in a constitution; relating to someone's physical or mental condition
例句：The president is constitutionally bound to uphold the law. 总统在宪法上有义务维护法律。

例句：He is constitutionally incapable of telling a lie. 他生性就不会说谎。

近义词：legally, lawfully, inherently, naturally
反义词：illegally, unlawfully

词性: adverb
