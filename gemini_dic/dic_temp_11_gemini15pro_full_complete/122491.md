mocha:/ˈmoʊkə/ (US) /ˈmɒkə/ (UK)| n. |摩卡咖啡；摩卡色|A type of coffee drink made with espresso, chocolate syrup, steamed milk, and often topped with whipped cream. Also, a color resembling the color of mocha.|例句：I ordered a mocha latte with extra whipped cream. 我点了一杯摩卡拿铁，多加奶油。

例句：Her dress was a beautiful shade of mocha. 她的裙子是美丽的摩卡色。|近义词：latte, cappuccino, caffè mocha|反义词: (No direct antonym for a type of coffee or color.  Could consider contrasting coffee types like "espresso" or "americano", or contrasting colors like "white" or "pastel")|词性:noun
