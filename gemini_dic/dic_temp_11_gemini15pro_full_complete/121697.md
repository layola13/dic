mispractised: /ˌmɪsˈpræktɪst/
verb
(mispracticed - 美式拼写)
处理不当；玩忽职守；滥用；错误地使用
to carry out (a profession, especially medicine or law) in a negligent or improper way, especially so as to cause harm.
The doctor was accused of having mispractised medicine.  这名医生被指控医疗事故。
He was found guilty of mispractising law. 他被判犯有滥用法律罪。
近义词: mishandle, misuse, abuse, malpractice
反义词: handle correctly, use properly
词性: verb


mispractised: /ˌmɪsˈpræktɪst/
adjective
(mispracticed - 美式拼写)
玩忽职守的; 错误使用的
performed or carried out in a negligent or improper way.
His mispractised medical techniques led to several patient complaints.  他错误的医疗技术导致了几位病人投诉。
The investigation revealed mispractised legal procedures.  调查揭示了错误的法律程序。
近义词: mishandled, misused, abused, malpractice
反义词: correctly handled, properly used
词性: adjective
