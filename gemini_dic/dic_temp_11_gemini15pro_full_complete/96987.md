infantility:/ˌɪnfənˈtɪləti/
noun
幼稚，幼稚的行为
The state of being infantile; childishness or babyishness.
His constant whining about the smallest things was a sign of his infantility. 他对芝麻绿豆的小事不停地抱怨，这表明他很幼稚。
immaturity, childishness, puerility
maturity, sophistication
