disposableness:/dɪˌspəʊzəbəlˈnɛs/
n.
可处置性；一次性；用后即弃
The quality or state of being disposable; designed to be thrown away after use.
Fast food is often served in disposable containers.  快餐通常用一次性容器盛装。
The disposableness of modern electronics contributes to a lot of e-waste. 现代电子产品的一次性导致大量的电子垃圾。
Synonyms: expendability, transience, impermanence
Antonyms: permanence, durability, longevity
noun
