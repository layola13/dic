depended:/dɪˈpɛndɪd/
verb
依赖，依靠
relied on someone or something else for support, help, or supply.
The success of the project depended on everyone working together.  这个项目的成功取决于大家的共同努力。
My grandparents depended on their pensions. 我的祖父母依靠他们的养老金生活。
synonyms: relied, counted, banked,  leaned
antonyms: independent, self-sufficient
