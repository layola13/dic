interparochial:/ˌɪntərˌpəˈroʊkiəl/
adjective
堂区间的；跨教区的
Between or involving different parishes.
The interparochial football tournament drew teams from all over the county.  堂区间的足球锦标赛吸引了来自全县各地的球队。
The interparochial rivalry was intense, especially during the annual cricket match. 堂区间的竞争非常激烈，尤其是在每年的板球比赛期间。
Synonyms: interchurch, intercongregational
Antonyms: intraparochial (within a single parish)
词性: 形容词
