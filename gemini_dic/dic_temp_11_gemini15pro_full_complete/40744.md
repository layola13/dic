conveyers:/kənˈveɪərz/
n.
传送机；输送机；运送者
A machine with a moving belt or surface that transports objects from one place to another.
The luggage is transported from the plane to the baggage claim area via conveyers.  行李通过传送带从飞机运送到行李提取处。

The factory uses conveyers to move products along the assembly line.  工厂使用传送带沿装配线移动产品。

近义词：transporter, conveyor belt, carrier
反义词: None (No direct antonym for a physical object)
词性: Noun
