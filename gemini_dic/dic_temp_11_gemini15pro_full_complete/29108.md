casbahs:/ˈkæzbɑːz/ /ˈkɑːzbɑːz/
n.
城堡, 城堡区, 旧城区
A citadel, especially one in a North African city.  The older part of a North African town, typically walled.
We wandered through the narrow streets of the casbahs. 我们在城堡区的狭窄街道上漫步。
synonyms: citadel, fortress, stronghold
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, possibly "open area," "suburbs")
noun
