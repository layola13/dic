on-a-string:/ɒn ə ˈstrɪŋ/| adj.|受…控制的，唯命是从的|being completely controlled by someone else|例句：He’s got her on a string. 他把她完全控制住了。

例句：The boss had his employees on a string. 老板完全控制着他的员工。


近义词：dependent, controlled, manipulated|反义词:independent, autonomous|词性:形容词
