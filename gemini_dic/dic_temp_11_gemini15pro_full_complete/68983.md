fatigued:/fəˈtiːɡd/| adj.|疲惫的，疲乏的|Exhausted, tired, weary, feeling a need to rest or sleep.|例句：After the marathon, the runners were fatigued. 马拉松比赛结束后，跑步者们都疲惫不堪。|近义词：tired, exhausted, weary, worn out, drained, spent|反义词：energetic, invigorated, refreshed|词性:形容词

fatigued:/fəˈtiːɡd/| v. (past participle)|使疲劳|Made someone feel tired.|例句：The long journey fatigued the travelers. 长途旅行使旅客们疲惫不堪。|近义词：tire, exhaust, weary, wear out, drain|反义词：energize, invigorate, refresh|词性:动词(过去分词)
