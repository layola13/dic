mispracticing: /ˌmɪsˈpræktɪsɪŋ/
Verb
渎职；失职；不当行为；不正当执业
Engaging in malpractice, or professional misconduct or lack of skill.
The doctor was accused of mispracticing medicine after several of his patients died.  这位医生在几名病人死亡后被指控医疗事故。
He lost his license for mispracticing law. 他因不当执业失去了律师执照。
Near Synonyms:  malpracticing,  bungling,  fumbling
Antonyms:  complying,  adhering,  observing
Word Type: Verb
