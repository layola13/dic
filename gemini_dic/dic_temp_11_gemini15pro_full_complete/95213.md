inboards:/ˈɪnbɔːrdz/ | n. | 内侧板;舷内;舱内;机内;|  The structural members inside the hull of a boat or ship, or inside the fuselage of an aircraft.  Located or operating within the bounds or confines, especially of a vehicle. | The ship's inboards were reinforced with steel. 这艘船的内侧板用钢加固了。| synonyms: internal components, internal structure, internal parts, inside, interior | antonyms: outboards, external components, external structure, external parts, outside, exterior | noun
