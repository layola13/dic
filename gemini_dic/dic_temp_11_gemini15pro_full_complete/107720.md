lanyard: /ˈlænjərd/ (US), /ˈlænɪəd/ (UK)| n. |挂绳，系索，吊带|A cord or strap worn around the neck, shoulder, or wrist to carry something, such as an ID card, keys, or a whistle.|例句：She wore a lanyard with her ID card. 她用挂绳挂着她的身份证。|近义词：cord, strap, rope|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
