belonging:/bɪˈlɒŋɪŋ/ (US) /bɪˈlɔːŋɪŋ/ (UK)
n.|归属感；所有物；附属物|A sense of fitting in or being accepted; possessions; something associated with a particular person, place, or thing.|例句：A sense of belonging is essential for mental well-being. 归属感对心理健康至关重要。|近义词：affiliation, possession, attachment|反义词:alienation, detachment|词性:名词

belonging:/bɪˈlɒŋɪŋ/ (US) /bɪˈlɔːŋɪŋ/ (UK)
adj.|附属的，隶属的|(usually used predicatively) Associated with or part of something else.|例句：These tools are belonging to the workshop. 这些工具属于车间。|近义词：related, associated, connected|反义词:unrelated, separate|词性:形容词
