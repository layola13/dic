dodger:/ˈdɑːdʒər/ (US), /ˈdɒdʒər/ (UK)| n.|躲避者，逃避者，骗子|A person who avoids something, especially a responsibility or duty, or someone who acts in a dishonest or deceptive way.|例句：He's a real dodger, always finding ways to avoid work. 他真是个老油条，总是想方设法逃避工作。

例句：The tax dodgers were caught and prosecuted. 逃税者被抓获并被起诉了。

近义词：evader, shirker, cheat, swindler|反义词:conformist, honest person|词性:名词
