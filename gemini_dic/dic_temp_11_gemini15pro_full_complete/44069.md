crinklies:/ˈkrɪŋkliːz/
noun
皱纹，皱褶; (尤指旧钞票的) 皱巴巴的纸币
Crinkled things, often used to refer to banknotes.  These are often perceived as being old or well-used.
He pulled out a wad of crinklies from his pocket. 他从口袋里掏出一叠皱巴巴的钞票。
synonyms:  bills, notes, cash, currency, money
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider)  new bills, crisp notes, card payment
词性: 名词
