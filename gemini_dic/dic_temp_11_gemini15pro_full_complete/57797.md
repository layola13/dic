dredging:/ˈdrɛdʒɪŋ/
n.|疏浚，挖掘|The dredging of the river is necessary to maintain its depth.  这条河的疏浚对于维持其深度是必要的。|例句：The dredging project is expected to take several months.  疏浚工程预计需要几个月的时间。|近义词：excavation, digging|反义词:filling, depositing|词性:名词
dredge:/drɛdʒ/
v.|疏浚，挖掘|They dredged the harbor to allow larger ships to enter.  他们疏浚了港口，以便更大的船只能够进入。|例句：The company dredges for oysters in the bay.  这家公司在海湾里挖掘牡蛎。|近义词：excavate, dig|反义词:fill, deposit|词性:动词

词源：From Middle English *dreggen, from Old English *dragan, from Proto-Germanic *dragōną (“to draw, pull, drag”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰregʰ- (“to draw, pull”). Cognate with Dutch dreggen (“to dredge”), German drechseln (“to turn on a lathe”), Icelandic drekka (“to drink”), and English drag.
