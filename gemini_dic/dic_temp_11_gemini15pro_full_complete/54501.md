disinvigorate:/ˌdɪsɪnˈvɪɡəreɪt/
verb
使衰弱；使失去活力
To deprive of vigor or strength; weaken.
Long hours of overtime can disinvigorate even the most dedicated employees. 长时间加班即使是最敬业的员工也会感到疲惫不堪。
The humid heat disinvigorated the runners. 湿热的天气让跑步者感到无力。
Synonyms: weaken, enfeeble, debilitate, sap, drain
Antonyms: invigorate, energize, strengthen, revitalize
词性: 动词
