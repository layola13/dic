bladder-like:/ˈblædərˌlaɪk/
adjective
膀胱状的；囊状的
Resembling a bladder, a thin-walled sac-like organ, typically used for storing fluids.
The plant has bladder-like leaves that store water.  这种植物有储存水分的囊状叶子。
The jellyfish had a bladder-like body that pulsed rhythmically. 水母有一个像膀胱一样的身体，有节奏地跳动着。
近义词:sac-like, pouch-like, vesicular
反义词:solid, compact
