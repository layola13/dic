infundibulum:/ˌɪnfʌnˈdɪbjʊləm/| n. |漏斗；漏斗状结构|A funnel-shaped cavity or passage.  Specifically, a stalk of tissue that connects the hypothalamus to the posterior pituitary gland. It also refers to various other funnel-shaped structures in the body.|The infundibulum of the uterine tube leads to the ovary.  输卵管的漏斗部通向卵巢。

|Synonyms: funnel, cone|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a shape.  One might consider "tube" if contrasting with the narrowed aspect.)|Noun
