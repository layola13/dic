cardecu:/kɑːrˈdeɪkjuː/
noun
卡迪库（旧时法国金币）
A French gold coin of the 17th and 18th centuries.
He paid for the painting with a handful of cardecus. 他用一把卡迪库付了这幅画的钱。
Synonyms: louis d'or, pistole
