oversecurity:/ˌoʊvər sɪˈkjʊrəti/
noun
过度安全；过分保护
Excessive security measures or precautions, often to the point of being counterproductive or hindering normal activities.
The oversecurity at the airport caused long delays. 机场过度的安检措施导致了长时间的延误。
近义词: overprotection, excessive security
反义词: insecurity, vulnerability
词性: noun

Etymology:
From over- + security.  "Over-" is a prefix indicating "excessive" or "too much". "Security" comes from the Latin word "securitas", meaning "freedom from care, safety".
