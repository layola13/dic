inequalities:/ˌɪnɪˈkwɒlətiz/ | n. | 不等式；不平等；不均；差异 |A statement that two quantities are unequal.  The condition of being unequal; lack of equality. Differences in size, degree, circumstances, etc.; disparity. |例句：The mathematical inequalities show the relationship between two variables. 这些数学不等式显示了两个变量之间的关系。|例句：Social inequalities can lead to unrest and conflict. 社会不平等会导致动荡和冲突。| 近义词：disparities, imbalances, variations, differences | 反义词：equalities, similarities, uniformity | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Latin *inaequalitas, from Latin inaequalis "unequal," from in- "not" + aequalis "equal."
