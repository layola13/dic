dissentions:/dɪˈsɛnʃənz/
n.
不和，纷争
Disagreements or discord among a group of people.
The political dissentions within the party threatened to tear it apart.  党内的政治纷争有可能使其分裂。
近义词：disagreements, disputes, discords, conflicts, strife
反义词：agreements, harmony, concord, unity
词性: 名词


dissent:/dɪˈsɛnt/
n.
异议，不同意
The expression or holding of opinions at variance with those previously, commonly, or officially held.
Two members dissented from the majority decision. 两名成员对多数人的决定表示异议。
近义词：disagreement, objection, protest, opposition
反义词：agreement, assent, acceptance, approval
词性: 名词


dissent:/dɪˈsɛnt/
v.
持异议，不同意
To hold or express opinions that are at variance with those commonly or officially held.
Several scientists dissented from the official view on climate change. 几位科学家对气候变化的官方观点表示异议。
近义词：disagree, object, protest, oppose
反义词：agree, assent, accept, approve
词性: 动词


词源：From Latin dissentire, from dis- "apart" + sentire "to feel."
