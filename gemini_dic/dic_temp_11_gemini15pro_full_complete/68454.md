familiarise:/fəˈmɪliəraɪz/ /fəˈmɪljəˌraɪz/ | vt. | 使熟悉；使了解 | To make someone familiar with something; to accustom someone to something. |  例句：I need to familiarise myself with the new software before the training. 我需要在培训前熟悉一下新软件。

例句：The guide familiarized the tourists with the local customs. 导游让游客们熟悉了当地的风俗习惯。

近义词：acquaint, accustom, introduce | 反义词： estrange, alienate | 词性：动词
