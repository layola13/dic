anticeremonialists:/ˌæntiˌsɛrɪˈmoʊniəlɪsts/
|n.| 反仪式主义者 | People who oppose or reject ceremonies, rituals, or formal observances.  They believe that such practices are often empty, ostentatious, or meaningless. | The anticeremonialists in the group argued against holding a lavish wedding.  小组中的反仪式主义者反对举行奢华的婚礼。| Synonyms:  nonconformists, iconoclasts, dissenters | Antonyms:  traditionalists, ritualists, conformists | noun
