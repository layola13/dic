famished:/ˈfæmɪʃt/| adj.|极度饥饿的|Extremely hungry.|例句：I was famished after the hike. 我远足后饿极了。|例句：He was so famished he ate the entire pizza. 他饿极了，把整个披萨都吃了。|近义词：starving, ravenous, hungry|反义词:full, satiated|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *famisshen*, from Old French *famiss-, famis-* "hungry, famished", past participle of *famir* "to starve, be hungry, famish", from Latin *famēs* "hunger, famine".
