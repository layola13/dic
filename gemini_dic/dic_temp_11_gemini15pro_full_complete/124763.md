如果您是想了解 "what" 这个单词，我可以提供以下信息：

what: /wɒt/ (British), /wʌt/ (American) | pron. det. adv. int.| 什么，多么 |

英文解析:  "What" is a versatile word used as a pronoun, determiner, adverb, and interjection. It's primarily used to ask questions about things or information.  It can also be used to express surprise or emphasis.

例句：

What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？

What do you want for dinner? = 你晚餐想吃什么？

近义词：which, how

反义词:  (What doesn't have a direct antonym as it's a question word.)
