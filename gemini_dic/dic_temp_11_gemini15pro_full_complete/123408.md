monophthongs:/ˈmɒnəfθɒŋz/ (UK), /ˈmɑːnəfθɔŋz/ (US)| n.|单元音|A vowel sound that is unchanging throughout its duration; represented by a single symbol in the phonetic alphabet.|The word "feet" contains the monophthong /iː/.|"feet"这个单词包含单元音/iː/。|pure vowels|diphthongs|noun
