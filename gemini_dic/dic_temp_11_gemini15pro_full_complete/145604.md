overconscientiousness:/ˌoʊvərˌkɑːnʃiˈɛnʃəsnəs/
n.
过度认真; 责任心过强
The state of being excessively conscientious; extreme meticulousness and concern with doing things correctly.
Her overconscientiousness led to burnout. 她过度认真导致了倦怠。
近义词：scrupulousness, meticulousness, punctiliousness
反义词：negligence, carelessness, irresponsibility
词性: noun

overconscientious:/ˌoʊvərˌkɑːnʃiˈɛnʃəs/
adj.
过度认真的; 责任心过强的
Excessively conscientious; extremely meticulous and concerned with doing things correctly.
He was an overconscientious student, always striving for perfection. 他是一个过度认真的学生，总是力求完美。
近义词：scrupulous, meticulous, punctilious
反义词：negligent, careless, irresponsible
词性: adjective
