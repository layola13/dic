jugata:/dʒuˈɡɑːtə/ /dʒəˈɡeɪtə/
| n. |轭，连接，结合|A yoke or a structure resembling a yoke; a pair of things joined together.  Specifically in biology, a ridge or furrow formed by the union of two parts. |The butterfly's wings displayed a prominent jugata where they met. 蝴蝶的翅膀在它们相遇的地方显示出一个突出的轭状物。|近义词：yoke, connection, union, junction|反义词:separation, division|词性:名词

jugate:/ˈdʒuːɡeɪt/
| adj. |轭状的，连接的|Yoked; coupled; joined; having the form or appearance of a yoke. |The jugate leaves of the plant were a distinctive feature.  这种植物轭状的叶子是一个显著特征。|近义词：yoked, connected, joined|反义词:separated, divided|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Latin *jugatus*, past participle of *jugare* "to yoke," from *jugum* "yoke."
