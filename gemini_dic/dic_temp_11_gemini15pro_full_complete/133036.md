nonenviablenesses:/ˌnɒnˈɛnviəbəlnɪsɪz/
noun
不可羡慕之处；不值得羡慕的性质
The state or quality of not being enviable; the plural form of nonenviableness.
His nonenviablenesses included a demanding job and a long commute.  他的不值得羡慕之处包括一份要求很高的工作和漫长的通勤时间。
Near synonyms: undesirableness, unattractiveness, disadvantages
Near antonyms:  desirableness, attractiveness, advantages
词性: 名词
