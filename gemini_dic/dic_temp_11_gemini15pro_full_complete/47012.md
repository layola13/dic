dashpot: /ˈdæʃpɒt/ (UK) /ˈdæʃpɑːt/ (US) | n. | 阻尼器；缓冲器；减震器 | A mechanical device that resists sudden changes in motion, typically by using the viscosity of a fluid. It is used to dampen vibrations or oscillations. | The door closer uses a dashpot to prevent slamming.  这扇门的闭门器使用了阻尼器来防止它砰地关上。

The car's suspension system incorporates dashpots to smooth out the ride. 汽车的悬架系统包含阻尼器以使行驶平稳。 |Synonyms: damper, shock absorber, buffer | Antonyms: accelerator | noun
