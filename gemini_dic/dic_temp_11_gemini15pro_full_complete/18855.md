beseeching:/bɪˈsiːtʃɪŋ/
adjective
恳求的；哀求的
Expressing or marked by earnest pleading or entreaty.
She gave him a beseeching look, hoping he would change his mind. 她恳求地看着他，希望他能改变主意。
The lost child turned beseeching eyes to the policeman.  迷路的孩子用恳求的眼神看着警察。
近义词:pleading, imploring, supplicating, begging
反义词:demanding, commanding, assertive
词性:adjective

beseech:/bɪˈsiːtʃ/
verb
恳求；哀求
To implore urgently; to beg eagerly for; solicit earnestly.
I beseech you to reconsider your decision. 我恳求你重新考虑你的决定。
They beseeched the king for mercy. 他们恳求国王开恩。
近义词:implore, beg, plead, supplicate
反义词:demand, command, order
词性:verb
