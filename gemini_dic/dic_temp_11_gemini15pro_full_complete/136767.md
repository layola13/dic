nonseptic:/nɒnˈsɛptɪk/ | adj. | 非败血症的，无菌的 | Not septic; free from infection caused by microorganisms.  |  The wound was cleaned thoroughly to ensure it remained nonseptic.  伤口被彻底清洁以确保它保持无菌状态。| Example: The doctor confirmed that the inflammation was nonseptic, caused by an allergic reaction rather than bacteria. 医生确认炎症是非败血症的，是由过敏反应而不是细菌引起的。 | Synonyms: aseptic, sterile | Antonyms: septic, infected | Adjective
