malacostracan:/ˌmæləˈkɒstrəkən/ | n. |软甲纲动物|Relating to or denoting a subclass (Malacostraca) of crustaceans that includes crabs, lobsters, shrimps, woodlice, etc., characterized by a head with five segments, a thorax with eight, and an abdomen with six (sometimes seven, or fused with the thorax).|例句：The lobster is a well-known malacostracan. 龙虾是一种著名的软甲纲动物。

例句：Malacostracans are a diverse group of crustaceans. 软甲纲动物是甲壳类动物中种类繁多的一个类别。|近义词：crustacean |反义词: (No direct antonym for a biological classification) |词性:noun
