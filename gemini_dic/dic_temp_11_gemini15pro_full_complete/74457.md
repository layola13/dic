frankable:/ˈfræŋkəbl/| adj.|可邮寄的，可免费邮寄的|Suitable for sending by mail, especially without charge.|The package was frankable, so I didn't have to pay for postage.  这个包裹是可以免费邮寄的，所以我不用付邮费。|Synonyms: post-free, postage-paid|Antonyms: non-mailable, unfrankable|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "frank" (v.) meaning "to send mail free of charge,"  + "-able" (suffix indicating capability).  The verb "frank" comes from the Old French "franc" meaning "free."
