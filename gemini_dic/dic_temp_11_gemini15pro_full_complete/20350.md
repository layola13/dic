bismuthic:/bɪzˈmʌθɪk/
adjective
铋的；五价铋的
Containing or relating to bismuth, especially with a valency of five.
Bismuthic oxide is a yellowish powder. 氧化铋是一种淡黄色的粉末。
近义词: bismuth
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a chemical property)
词性: adjective
