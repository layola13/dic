okaying:/oʊˈkeɪɪŋ/
v.
批准，认可
Giving approval or authorization to something.
The manager is okaying the project proposal. 经理正在批准项目提案。
He okayed the purchase of new equipment. 他批准了购买新设备。
近义词:approving, authorizing, sanctioning, endorsing
反义词:disapproving, rejecting, vetoing
词性:动词
