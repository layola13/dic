dyadic:/daɪˈædɪk/| adj.|二元的，二元体的，由二体组成的|Characterized by or consisting of two elements or parts; relating to a dyad.|Dyadic communication is a form of interpersonal communication between two people.|Synonyms: binary, dual, twofold|Antonyms: multiple, plural, polyadic|adjective

dyad:/ˈdaɪæd/| n.|二人组，二元体，二分体|Something that consists of two elements or parts.|The mother-child dyad is the fundamental unit of social development.|Synonyms: pair, duo, couple|Antonyms: group, crowd, multitude|noun
