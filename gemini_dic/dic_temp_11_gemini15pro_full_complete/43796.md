cremosin:/ˈkrɛməsɪn/ /ˈkriːməsɪn/ | n. |凝乳酶，凝乳酵素| An enzyme obtained from the stomach lining of calves that is used in cheese making to curdle milk.|  Cremosin is essential for making many types of cheese. 凝乳酶是制作许多类型奶酪的必需品。| The cheesemaker added cremosin to the milk to begin the coagulation process.  奶酪制造商将凝乳酶添加到牛奶中以开始凝结过程。 |Synonyms: rennet, chymosin| Antonyms: None (no direct antonym for a specific enzyme)| Noun
