admaxillary:/ˌædˈmæksɪləri/
adjective
上颌骨的，靠近上颌骨的
Located near or next to the maxilla (upper jawbone).
The admaxillary lymph nodes are swollen. 上颌淋巴结肿大了。
近义词: juxtamaxillary, supramaxillary
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps inframaxillary, meaning below the maxilla)
词性: adjective
