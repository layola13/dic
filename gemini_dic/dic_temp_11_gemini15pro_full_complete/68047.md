factual:/ˈfæktʃuəl/| adj.|事实的；真实的；实际的|Based on or concerned with facts.|例句：The report was factual and unbiased.  这份报告是真实的且没有偏见的。|例句：She gave a factual account of the accident. 她对事故进行了如实的描述。|近义词：true, accurate, real, authentic, genuine|反义词：false, untrue, fictional, fabricated, imaginary|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *factualis, from Latin factum ("deed, act, fact") + -alis (suffix forming adjectives).
