behight:/bɪˈhaɪt/ | v. (archaic) | 答应，承诺；命令，指示 | To promise or pledge; to command or enjoin. |  He behight her his eternal love. 他向她承诺了他永恒的爱。|  The king behight his knights to defend the realm. 国王命令他的骑士们保卫王国。| 近义词: promise, pledge, vow, command, order | 反义词: rescind, retract | verb

词源:
From Middle English *bihoten*, from Old English *behātan* (“to promise, vow, command, threaten”), from *be-* + *hātan* (“to call, command, promise”). Cognate with German *heißen* (“to be called, to command”), Dutch *heten* (“to be called”), Gothic *haitan* (“to call, name, command”).
