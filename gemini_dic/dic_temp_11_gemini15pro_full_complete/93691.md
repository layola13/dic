imitate:/ˈɪmɪteɪt/| v. |模仿，效仿|To copy someone's behavior, speech, appearance, etc.|He imitated his father's deep voice. 他模仿了他父亲低沉的声音。|近义词：copy, mimic, emulate|反义词:originate, invent|verb

imitation:/ˌɪmɪˈteɪʃn/| n. |模仿，仿制品|The act of imitating someone or something.|The painting is a poor imitation of the original. 这幅画是原作的拙劣模仿。|近义词：copy, replica, mimicry|反义词:original, authentic|noun

imitator:/ˈɪmɪteɪtər/| n. |模仿者|A person who imitates someone or something.|He's a talented imitator of famous singers. 他是一位模仿著名歌手的天才。|近义词：mimic, impersonator, copycat|反义词:originator, innovator|noun
