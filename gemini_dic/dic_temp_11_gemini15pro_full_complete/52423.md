一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He didn't finish his homework. 他没有完成他的家庭作业。

She didn't see the movie. 她没有看那部电影。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
N/A - didn't is used to negate the simple past form of "to be" (wasn't/weren't) in past continuous.  While "didn't" itself isn't directly used with the -ing form of the verb.  For example: I wasn't sleeping when you called.  我不在睡觉当你打电话的时候。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
N/A - didn't is used with "had" (hadn't) to negate past perfect. For example: They hadn't finished eating when I arrived. 当我到达时他们还没有吃完饭。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
N/A - didn't is used with "had" (hadn't) to negate past perfect continuous. For example:  She hadn't been feeling well for weeks before she went to the doctor. 在去看医生前几周她一直感觉不舒服.

虚拟语气:
If I didn't have to work, I would go to the beach. 如果我不必工作，我会去海滩。

I wish I didn't have so much homework. 我希望我没有这么多作业。


被动语态:
The cake wasn't eaten. 蛋糕没有被吃掉。

The window wasn't broken by me. 窗户不是我打破的。


疑问句:
Didn't you go to the store? 你没去商店吗？

Didn't she call you back? 她没有给你回电话吗？


否定句:
I didn't understand the instructions. 我不明白指示。

They didn't believe me. 他们不相信我。
