gyroscope:/ˈdʒaɪrəˌskoʊp/ (US) /ˈdʒaɪrəˌskəʊp/ (UK)| n.|陀螺仪，回转仪|An instrument containing a freely rotating wheel that maintains its orientation regardless of the movement of the platform on which it is mounted, used for navigation, especially in aircraft and spacecraft.|The gyroscope in the airplane helps maintain stability during turbulence.|近义词：gyro, rotor|反义词:|noun
