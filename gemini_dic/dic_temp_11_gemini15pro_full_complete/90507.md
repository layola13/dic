huddled:/ˈhʌdld/ | adj. | 挤作一团的，蜷缩的 | crowded closely together, typically for warmth or protection | The refugees huddled together for warmth.  难民们挤在一起取暖。 |  The children huddled around the fire. 孩子们挤在火堆旁。|  clustered, crowded, snuggled | scattered, dispersed | adjective


huddled:/ˈhʌdld/ | v. | 挤在一起，蜷缩 |  to crowd together; nestle closely |  They huddled around the fire to stay warm.  他们挤在火堆旁取暖。 | The sheep huddled together in the storm.  羊群在暴风雨中挤成一团。 |  crowd, cluster, snuggle | separate, disperse | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "huddle," which originated in the late 16th century.  Its origin is uncertain, possibly related to Low German "huddeln" (to hoard, save).
