intermeddling:/ˌɪntərˈmɛdlɪŋ/
intermeddling| n.|干涉，干预|The act of interfering in something that is not one's concern.|His constant intermeddling in his daughter's life caused a rift between them. 他不断干涉女儿的生活，导致他们之间的关系出现裂痕。|近义词：interference, intrusion, meddling|反义词:noninterference, nonintervention|
intermeddle:/ˌɪntərˈmɛdl/
intermeddle| v.|干涉，干预|To interfere in something that is not one's concern.|He intermeddled in the dispute between his neighbors. 他干涉了邻居之间的纠纷。|近义词：interfere, intrude, meddle|反义词:ignore, abstain|
