legitimize: /ləˈdʒɪtɪmaɪz/ /ləˈdʒɪtəˌmaɪz/ | vt. | 使合法化，使正当化 | To make something legal or acceptable; to give something an official status or make it conform to the law.  | The government passed a law to legitimize gambling. 政府通过了一项法律，使赌博合法化。 |  The regime tried to legitimize its seizure of power. 该政权试图使其夺取政权的行为合法化。| synonyms: validate, justify, authorize, legalize, sanction | antonyms: outlaw, prohibit, ban, criminalize | verb
