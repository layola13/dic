frugivora:/fruːˈdʒɪvɔːrə/
n.
frugivores (plural)
食果动物
An animal that feeds primarily on fruit.
Toucans are frugivores, their beaks perfectly adapted for plucking fruit from branches.  巨嘴鸟是食果动物，它们的喙非常适合从树枝上采摘水果。
Many primates, like orangutans, are frugivores. 许多灵长类动物，如猩猩，都是食果动物。
近义词:fruit-eater
反义词:carnivore, herbivore
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *frūgīvorus*, from frūx ("fruit") + vorāre ("to devour").
