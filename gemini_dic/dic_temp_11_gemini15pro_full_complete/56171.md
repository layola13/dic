dodecahedron:/ˌdoʊdekəˈhiːdrən/ /ˌdəʊdekəˈhiːdrən/| n.|十二面体|A solid figure with twelve plane faces, typically pentagons.|The ancient Greeks studied the dodecahedron as one of the five Platonic solids. 古希腊人将十二面体作为五个柏拉图立体之一进行研究。

The dice we used for the game was a dodecahedron. 我们玩游戏用的骰子是一个十二面体。|近义词:polyhedron|反义词:N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek δωδεκάεδρον (dōdekáedron), from δώδεκα (dṓdeka, “twelve”) + ἕδρα (hédra, “seat, base, face”).
