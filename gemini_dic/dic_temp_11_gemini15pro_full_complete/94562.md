impotently:/ˈɪmpətəntlɪ/| adv.|阳痿地|in a way that relates to or suggests impotence (especially erectile dysfunction).|This meaning is less common and often considered sensitive.|He stared impotently at the floor. 他无能地盯着地板。（此例句中impotently可能暗示阳痿，取决于上下文）|Synonyms: (for this sense, use with caution) weakly, dysfunctionally|Antonyms: (for this sense, use with caution) strongly, virilely|词性: 副词
