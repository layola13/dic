crossbowman:/ˈkrɒsbəʊmən/ (US) /ˈkrɔːsbəʊmən/ (UK)| n. |弩兵；弩手|A person who shoots with a crossbow.|The crossbowman took aim and fired at the target. 弩兵瞄准目标开火。|archer, bowman|gunman|noun
Etymology:  From Middle English *crosbowe-man, equivalent to crossbow +‎ -man. Compare Dutch kruisboogschutter, German Armbrustschütze.
