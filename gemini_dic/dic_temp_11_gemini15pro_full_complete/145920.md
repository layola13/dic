overdilute:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈluːt/| v. |过分稀释；过度稀释|To dilute something excessively, beyond what is desired or effective.|The chemist overdiluted the solution, rendering it useless for the experiment. 化学家过度稀释了溶液，导致它无法用于实验。|Synonyms: weaken, thin, water down|Antonyms: concentrate, strengthen|verb

overdilute:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈluːtɪd/| adj. |被过度稀释的|Diluted to an excessive degree.|The overdiluted paint was too pale to cover the wall properly.  过度稀释的油漆太淡了，无法 properly地覆盖墙壁。|Synonyms: weakened, thinned, watered down|Antonyms: concentrated, strengthened|adjective
