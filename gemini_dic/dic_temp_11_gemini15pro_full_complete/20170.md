bipupillate:/ˌbaɪpjuːˈpɪleɪt/ | adj. | 双瞳的，有双瞳孔的 | Having two pupils in each eye. |  Bipupillate animals are extremely rare. 双瞳孔的动物极其罕见。| Synonym: double-pupilled | Antonym: normal-pupilled | adjective

bipupillate:/ˌbaɪpjuːˈpɪleɪt/ | n. | 双瞳孔的人或动物 | A person or animal with two pupils in each eye. |  Legend says that some ancient rulers were bipupillates. 传说一些古代统治者有双瞳。| Synonym: (no direct synonym, describe as "individual with double pupils") | Antonym: (no direct antonym) | noun
