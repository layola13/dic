comparisons:/kəmˈpærɪs ənz/ | n. | 比较，对比 | the act of comparing two or more things to see how they are similar or different |  She drew comparisons between the two paintings. 她比较了两幅画。| synonyms:  similarities, contrasts,  analogies | antonyms: differences, disparities | noun

comparisons:/kəmˈpærɪs ənz/ | n. | 比喻 |  a statement that shows how two things are similar | The comparison of the human brain to a computer is often made. 人的大脑经常被比作电脑。| synonyms:  analogies, metaphors, similes | antonyms:  dissimilarities, contrasts | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *comparisoun, from Old French comparaison, from Latin comparātiō (“comparison”), from comparātus, perfect passive participle of comparō (“I compare”), from com- + parō (“I prepare”).
