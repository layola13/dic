motormouth:/ˈmoʊtərˌmaʊθ/| n.|喋喋不休的人，话多的人|A person who talks incessantly and rapidly.|That motormouth never stops talking! 那个喋喋不休的人就没停过嘴！|近义词：chatterbox, windbag|反义词：quiet person|词性:名词
