impassive:/ɪmˈpæsɪv/ | adj. | 无表情的，冷漠的，无动于衷的 | Not feeling or showing emotion. |  He remained impassive throughout the trial, his face giving nothing away. 他在整个审判过程中都面无表情，丝毫没有流露出任何情绪。

Her impassive face made it difficult to know what she was thinking. 她那张毫无表情的脸让人很难知道她在想什么。

近义词: emotionless, expressionless, stoic, unmoved, indifferent, apathetic | 反义词: expressive, emotional, passionate, animated | 词性: adjective

impassive:/ɪmˈpæsɪv/ | adj. | 无动于衷的，不动声色的 | Not subject to suffering. | The statue stood impassive, untouched by the ravages of time. 这座雕像无动于衷地矗立着，不受时间流逝的影响。

The mountain remained impassive as the storm raged around it.  当暴风雨肆虐时，这座山依然不动声色。

近义词:  imperturbable, unyielding, unaffected  | 反义词:  vulnerable, sensitive, susceptible | 词性: adjective


词源: Late 16th century: from French impassible, from in- ‘not’ + Latin passibilis ‘able to suffer,’ from pati ‘suffer.’
