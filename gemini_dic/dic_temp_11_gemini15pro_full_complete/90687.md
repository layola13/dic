humbuggers:/ˈhʌmbʌɡərz/
n.
骗子；欺骗；胡说
People who deceive or trick others; nonsense; something intended to deceive.
Those humbuggers sold me a fake watch! 那些骗子卖给我一块假表！
近义词:frauds, impostors, charlatans, deceivers, tricksters, fakers
反义词:honest people, truth-tellers
