claviculate: /kləˈvɪkjʊleɪt/
adjective
锁骨状的；有锁骨的；具小钩的
Having a clavicle or clavicles; resembling a clavicle; furnished with a small hook or hooklike process.
The bird has a claviculate structure.  这只鸟有锁骨状的结构。
The fossil reveals a claviculate bone. 这块化石显示出一块锁骨状的骨头。
Synonyms: clavicled
Antonyms: None
词性: adjective
