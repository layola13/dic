genie:/ˈdʒiːniː/ | n. |精灵，妖怪|A supernatural creature from Islamic folklore that can fulfill wishes.|例句：The genie granted him three wishes. 精灵满足了他的三个愿望。

例句：She rubbed the lamp, and a genie appeared in a puff of smoke. 她擦了擦灯，一个精灵在一股烟雾中出现了。

近义词：djinn, spirit, supernatural being|反义词: N/A |词性:noun

etymology->From French *génie*, from Latin *genius* ("guardian spirit"), from Old Latin *genio* from the root of *gignere* "to beget, produce." Doublet of *genius*.  The current spelling is influenced by Arabic *jinnī*.
