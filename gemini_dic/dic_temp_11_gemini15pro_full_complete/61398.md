emblemed: /ɪmˈblɛmd/ | adj. | 以...为象征的；饰以纹章的 | Decorated or marked with an emblem; symbolized by an emblem. | The knight's shield was emblemed with a golden lion. 骑士的盾牌上饰有金狮纹章。 | The king's robe, emblemed with the royal crest, signified his authority. 国王的袍子上饰有皇家徽章，象征着他的权威。 | Synonyms: symbolic, representative, emblematic | Antonyms: plain, unmarked, undecorated | adjective
