chiaroscuro:/kiˌɑːrəˈskʊəroʊ/ /ˌkjɑːrəˈskʊərəʊ/
|n.|明暗对照法; 光影; 明暗对比|The treatment of light and shade in a work of art, especially to give an illusion of depth. The interplay or contrast of dissimilar qualities or aspects, such as light and shade in a painting.|The artist used chiaroscuro to create a dramatic effect in the painting.  这位艺术家运用明暗对比法在这幅画中营造出戏剧性的效果。|近义词:contrast, light and shade|反义词:flat lighting, monotone|noun
