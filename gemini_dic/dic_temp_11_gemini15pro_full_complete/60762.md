electrology:/ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒlədʒi/ | n. | 电解脱毛，电疗 |The practice of permanently removing hair by destroying the growth cells of the hair follicles with an electric current. | Electrology is a popular method for permanent hair removal. 电解脱毛是一种流行的永久脱毛方法。 |She considered laser hair removal, but ultimately chose electrology. 她考虑过激光脱毛，但最终选择了电解脱毛。 | 近义词: electrolysis, permanent hair removal | 反义词:  temporary hair removal, epilation | noun
