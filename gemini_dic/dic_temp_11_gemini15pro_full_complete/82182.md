gross:/ɡroʊs/ (US) /ɡrəʊs/ (UK)| adj. n. v. |总的，毛的；严重的；令人恶心的；（收入、利润等）总的；总额；总收入|  A gross sum is the total amount before any deductions are made.  The movie was just gross – I couldn't even finish watching it.|

例句：
The gross weight of the package was 20kg. 包裹的毛重是20公斤。

His gross annual income is $100,000. 他的年总收入是10万美元。

近义词：total, overall, whole, disgusting, repulsive, sickening
反义词：net, pleasant, appealing
词性: adjective, noun, verb
