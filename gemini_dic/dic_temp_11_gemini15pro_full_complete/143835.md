outperforming:/ˌaʊtpərˈfɔːmɪŋ/ | v. | 胜过，优于 | Performing better than something or someone else. | Our new product is outperforming all the competitors in the market. 我们的新产品在市场上胜过所有竞争对手。|  The company has consistently outperformed market expectations. 这家公司一直以来都超出市场预期。 | synonyms: exceeding, surpassing, excelling, beating, triumphing over | antonyms: underperforming, lagging behind, trailing | verb

outperforming:/ˌaʊtpərˈfɔːmɪŋ/ | adj. | 表现优异的，胜过...的 |  Used as an adjective (often before a noun) to describe something that performs better than others. |  The company is an outperforming investment. 这家公司是一项表现优异的投资。 | This year's outperforming students will receive special recognition. 今年表现优异的学生将获得特别表彰。 | synonyms: superior, exceptional, leading, top-performing | antonyms: underperforming, lagging, inferior | adjective
