inalienability:/ɪnˌeɪliənəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可剥夺性，不可分割性
The state or quality of being inalienable; impossible to take away or give up.
The inalienability of human rights is a cornerstone of modern democracy. 人权的不可剥夺性是现代民主的基石。
inalienable rights are rights that cannot be taken away. 不可剥夺的权利是不能被剥夺的权利。
synonym:  nontransferability,  unassailability
antonym: transferability, alienability
noun
