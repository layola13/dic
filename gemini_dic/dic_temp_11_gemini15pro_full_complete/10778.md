aquaplaning:/ˈækwəˌpleɪnɪŋ/ /ˈɑːkwəˌpleɪnɪŋ/| n.|水上滑行|The loss of traction and control experienced when a tire rides on a film of water on the road surface.  A vehicle's tires lose contact with the road surface and skim over a layer of water.|The car lost control due to aquaplaning on the wet road. 汽车因为在潮湿路面上打滑而失控。|近义词：hydroplaning|反义词:traction, grip|名词

aquaplane:/ˈækwəˌpleɪn/ /ˈɑːkwəˌpleɪn/| v. |水上滑行|To slide uncontrollably on a wet road surface due to a layer of water between the tires and the road.|The car aquaplaned across the road and hit a tree. 汽车在路面上打滑，撞到了一棵树。|近义词：hydroplane|反义词:maintain traction|动词
