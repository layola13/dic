airbrushing:/ˈɛrˌbrʌʃɪŋ/| n. |喷绘，喷枪技术|The process of spraying paint by means of an airbrush to create a smooth, even coating.|The magazine cover was heavily airbrushed. 这本杂志的封面被大量喷绘过。|synonyms: spraying, painting|antonyms: brushing, rolling|noun
airbrush:/ˈɛrˌbrʌʃ/| n. vt. |喷枪，用喷枪喷涂|A small spray gun that uses compressed air to propel paint or other liquids.|He airbrushed the car to give it a custom finish. 他用喷枪喷涂汽车，使其具有定制的光洁度。|synonyms: spray gun, atomizer|antonyms: brush, roller|noun, verb
