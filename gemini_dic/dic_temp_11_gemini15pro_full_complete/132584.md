nondiffusing:/ˌnɒndɪˈfjuːzɪŋ/
adjective
非扩散的
Not spreading or diffusing; remaining localized.
The researchers observed nondiffusing particles in the gel.  研究人员观察到凝胶中不扩散的颗粒。
The paint was specially formulated to be nondiffusing, preventing it from bleeding into the surrounding material.  这种油漆经过特殊配制，使其不扩散，防止其渗入周围的材料。
近义词:non-spreading, contained, localized
反义词:diffusing, spreading, pervasive
词性:adjective

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
diffuse (verb): from Latin diffundere "to pour out, spread out," from dis- "out" + fundere "to pour"
-ing (suffix):  forms present participles and gerunds
