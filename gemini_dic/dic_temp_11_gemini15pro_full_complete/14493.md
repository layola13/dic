avellane:/ˌævəˈleɪn/ | adj. n.|淡褐色的；榛子色的；榛子|Of a light brown color, like that of a hazelnut. | The curtains were an elegant avellane, complementing the wooden furniture. 窗帘是优雅的淡褐色，与木制家具相得益彰。| The avellane shade of her eyes was striking. 她眼睛的淡褐色很引人注目。 | 近义词：hazel, hazelnut, light brown, beige, fawn | 反义词：dark brown, black, white | 词性: 形容词，名词

Etymology: From Old French *avelline, from aveline "hazelnut," from Vulgar Latin *abellana, from Latin abellana (nux) "(nut) of Abella," from Abella, a town in Campania, Italy, famed for its hazelnuts.
