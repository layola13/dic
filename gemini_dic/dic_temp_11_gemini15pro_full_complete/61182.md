eluders:/ɪˈluːdərz/| n.|逃避者，躲避者|People who evade or escape something, especially capture or understanding.  Those who successfully avoid being found or caught.|The eluders of the law remained free for years.  那些逃避法律的人多年来一直逍遥法外。|Synonyms: evaders, escapees, avoiders|Antonyms: confronters, victims, captives|noun
