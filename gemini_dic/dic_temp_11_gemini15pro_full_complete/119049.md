metier:/ˈmeɪtieɪ/ (US), /ˈmeti.eɪ/ (UK)| n. | 行业，职业，专长 |A job or activity that is especially suited to a person's abilities and interests; a trade, profession, or occupation. |例句：She found her metier in teaching. 她找到了她适合的职业——教学。

例句：He's a writer, and words are his metier. 他是个作家，文字是他的专长。

近义词：profession, occupation, vocation, calling, trade, craft, pursuit, specialty, forte

反义词：avocation, hobby

词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *métier*, from Old French *mestier*, from Latin *ministerium* ("service, occupation, office").  Ultimately from *minister* ("servant").
