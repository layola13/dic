crowed:/kraʊdɪd/ | adj. | 拥挤的，挤满的 | Filled with a large number of people or things close together. | The beach was crowded with tourists.  沙滩上挤满了游客。| crowded, packed, jammed, thronged | empty, deserted, sparse | adjective

crowed:/kroʊd/ | v. | 挤满，聚集；逼近；（鸟）发出叫声 | (of people or things) fill (a space or place) so as to make free movement difficult; gather together in large numbers; press or stand close to. (of a rooster) utter a loud cry.  | People crowded around the stage. 人们挤在舞台周围。The rooster crowed at dawn.  公鸡在黎明时鸣叫。| gather, assemble, congregate, swarm, flock; press, push, shove | disperse, scatter, dissipate | verb
