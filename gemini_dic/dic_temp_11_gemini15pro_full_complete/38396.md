conceptualization:/kənˌsɛptʃuələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |概念化；概念形成|The process of forming a concept or idea.  It involves abstracting and organizing information to create a mental representation of something.|The conceptualization of the project took several months.  这个项目的概念化花了几个月的时间。|近义词：conception, ideation, formulation|反义词: concretization|noun
