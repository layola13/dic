barnacled: /ˈbɑːrnək(ə)ld/
adjective
藤壶覆盖的；附着许多藤壶的
Covered with barnacles.
The barnacled hull of the old ship slowed its progress through the water. 那艘旧船的船体上覆盖着藤壶，减慢了它在水中的航行速度。
encrusted, covered with barnacles
clean, smooth
adjective
