featherer:/ˈfeðərər/| n.|  羽毛工, 装羽毛的人;  长羽毛的鸟; (尤指家禽) 长羽毛的动物|A person who prepares or fits feathers, especially for use in bedding or ornamentation.  A bird or other animal, especially poultry, that has developed feathers. |The featherer expertly arranged the plumes on the hat.  羽毛工巧妙地将羽毛排列在帽子上。| The young featherer was just starting to grow its adult plumage.  这只幼鸟才开始长出成年的羽毛。|Synonyms: plumage worker, plumassier (for a person working with ornamental feathers), fowl (for a feathered animal)| Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider "featherless" for the animal sense)| Noun
