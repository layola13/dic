dorsalmost:/ˈdɔːrsəlˌmoʊst/| adj.| 最靠近背部的，最背侧的 | Situated nearest to the back or dorsum, especially of an organism or part of an organism. | The dorsalmost fin on the fish is small and almost invisible.  这条鱼最背部的鳍很小，几乎看不见。|  Proximal, nearest, closest | ventralmost, furthest | adjective
Etymology-> From 'dorsal' + '-most'. 'Dorsal' comes from Late Latin 'dorsalis', from Latin 'dorsum' (back). '-most' is a superlative suffix.
