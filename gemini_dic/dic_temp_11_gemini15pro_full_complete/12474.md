Vespasian(s):/vɛˈspeɪʒ(i)ən/ /vɛˈspeɪʒənz/
| n. |维斯帕先（单数）/维斯帕先王朝（复数）| Vespasian was the ninth Roman Emperor, reigning from AD 69 to AD 79.  As singular, it refers to the emperor himself.  In plural, it can refer to the Flavian dynasty, which includes Vespasian and his sons, Titus and Domitian. |例句：Vespasian restored order after the chaotic Year of the Four Emperors. 维斯帕先在混乱的四帝之年后恢复了秩序。| 近义词：Flavian (when referring to the dynasty), emperor, ruler | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词
