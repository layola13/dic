antiquity:/ænˈtɪkwəti/ | n. | 古代; 古物; 古迹; 古老 |  the ancient past, especially the period before the Middle Ages.  Objects, buildings, and customs that have existed since ancient times.|  例句：The museum houses many treasures from antiquity.  博物馆收藏了许多古代的珍宝。

例句：She studied the history of Greek antiquity. 她研究了古希腊的历史。 | 近义词：ancient times, the past, old times | 反义词：modernity, present | 词性: 名词


antiquity:/ænˈtɪkwəti/ | n. | 古董; 文物 | an object, building, custom, etc., that has existed since ancient times | 例句：This vase is an antiquity, dating back to the Roman Empire.  这个花瓶是一件古董，可以追溯到罗马帝国时代。

例句：He collects antiquities from all over the world. 他从世界各地收集古董。| 近义词：relic, artifact, antique | 反义词：modern artifact | 词性: 名词
