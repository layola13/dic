beachcomber:/ˈbiːtʃˌkoʊmər/ | n. |海滩拾荒者；海滩流浪汉|A person who walks along a beach looking for things that have been washed ashore, especially valuable or interesting objects. |例句：The beachcomber found an old bottle with a message inside. 海滩拾荒者发现一个里面有信息的旧瓶子。

例句：Beachcombers often find interesting shells and driftwood. 海滩拾荒者经常发现有趣的贝壳和浮木。|近义词：scavenger, forager |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
