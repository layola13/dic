diverticulitis:/ˌdaɪvərˌtɪkjʊˈlaɪtɪs/
noun
憩室炎
Inflammation of a diverticulum, especially in the colon, causing pain and disturbance of bowel function.
My grandfather was hospitalized with diverticulitis. 我祖父因憩室炎住院了。
The doctor diagnosed her with diverticulitis after reviewing her symptoms and test results. 医生查看了她的症状和检测结果后，诊断她患有憩室炎。
