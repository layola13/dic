mimes:/maɪmz/ | n. | 模仿；哑剧；小丑 | An action or a series of actions intended to express or represent something without the use of words. |  The clown's mimes were hilarious. 小丑的哑剧表演非常滑稽。| synonyms: imitations, impersonations, mimicry | antonyms: speech, vocalization | noun

mimes:/maɪmz/ | v. | 模仿；演哑剧 | To use mime or mimicry. | He mimed the action of swimming. 他模仿游泳的动作。 | synonyms: imitate, mimic, impersonate | antonyms: speak, vocalize | verb
