exchequer:/ɪksˈtʃekər/ /ɛksˈtʃɛkər/| n.|国库，财政部|A royal or national treasury.  The funds of a government, public body, or individual. |The government is trying to replenish the exchequer after the costly war. 政府正试图在代价高昂的战争之后补充国库。|Synonyms: treasury, coffers, funds, purse, finances|Antonyms: debt, expenditure|noun

exchequer:/ɪksˈtʃekər/ /ɛksˈtʃɛkər/| n.|棋盘|A checkered cloth resembling a chessboard, especially one used in the Court of Exchequer. (historical)|The exchequer was laid out on the table, ready for the accounting. 棋盘摆在桌子上，准备记账。|Synonyms: chessboard, checkered cloth|Antonyms: N/A|noun
