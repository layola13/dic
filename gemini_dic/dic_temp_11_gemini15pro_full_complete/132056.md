noncosmopolitanisms: /ˌnɒnkɒzməˈpɒlɪtənɪzəmz/ | n. | 非世界主义，非世界性 |  The philosophies or mindsets that reject or de-emphasize cosmopolitan ideals, such as global interconnectedness, cultural exchange, and universal human rights.  It often emphasizes local or national identities and traditions over global ones. |  The rise of noncosmopolitanisms in recent years has led to increased tensions between nations. 近年来非世界主义的兴起导致国家间紧张局势加剧。 |  The politician's rhetoric was steeped in noncosmopolitanisms, emphasizing national interests above all else. 这位政客的言辞充满了非世界主义，强调国家利益高于一切。| 近义词： parochialism, insularity, nationalism | 反义词： cosmopolitanism, globalism, internationalism | 词性: noun


词源:
"non-" (prefix denoting negation) + "cosmopolitanism" (from "cosmopolite," from Greek "kosmopolitēs" - citizen of the world) + "-s" (plural suffix).
