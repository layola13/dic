cardiacs:/ˈkɑːrdiæks/
noun
心脏病人；强心剂
people with heart conditions; drugs used to stimulate the heart.
The cardiacs ward was full after the heatwave.  高温过后，心脏病病房人满为患。
The doctor prescribed cardiacs to strengthen his heart. 医生开了强心剂来增强他的心脏。
synonyms: heart patients, cardiac stimulants
antonyms: healthy individuals (for patients), cardiac depressants (for drugs)
