overgesticulate: /ˌoʊvərjɛˈstɪkjʊleɪt/
verb
做作地打手势; 过分地做手势
to use too many gestures or to gesture too much
The nervous presenter overgesticulated throughout his speech, distracting the audience.  那个紧张的演讲者在整个演讲过程中都做作地打手势，分散了观众的注意力。
She tends to overgesticulate when she's excited, waving her arms around dramatically. 她激动的时候往往会过分地打手势，夸张地挥舞着手臂。
Synonym:  emote, dramatize
Antonym:  understate, be reserved
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + gesticulate, from Latin gesticulatus, past participle of gesticulari “to make gestures,” from gesticulus “a mimic gesture,” diminutive of gestus “gesture, posture, carriage,” related to gerere “to carry, bear, manage, perform.”
