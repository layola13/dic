axonometry:/ˌæksəˈnɒmətri/ | n. | 轴测法，轴测图 | A method of graphical representation of three-dimensional objects on a two-dimensional plane by projecting parallel lines from the object onto the plane. | Example: Axonometry is commonly used in technical drawing and architectural visualization. 轴测图常用于技术绘图和建筑可视化。 | Example: The axonometry of the building clearly showed its complex structure. 这栋建筑的轴测图清晰地展现了其复杂的结构。 | Synonym: axonometric projection, isometric projection | Antonym: perspective projection | noun

axonometric:/ˌæksənoʊˈmɛtrɪk/ | adj. | 轴测的 | Relating to or employing axonometry. | Example: An axonometric drawing shows all three dimensions of an object. 轴测图显示了物体的所有三个维度。 | Example:  He used an axonometric view to illustrate the design. 他使用轴测视图来说明设计。 | Synonym: isometric, dimetric, trimetric | Antonym: perspective | adjective
