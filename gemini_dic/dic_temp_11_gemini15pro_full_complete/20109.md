biotechnologist:/ˌbaɪoʊtekˈnɑːlədʒɪst/| n. |生物技术专家，生物工艺学家|A scientist or engineer who specializes in biotechnology.  A person who uses biological processes to develop technologies or products. |Example: The biotechnologist is working on developing a new cancer treatment.  这位生物技术专家正在致力于开发一种新的癌症治疗方法。

Example:  Biotechnologists are in high demand in the pharmaceutical industry. 生物技术专家在制药行业的需求量很大。|近义词：genetic engineer, biologist, life scientist|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bio- + technology + -ist.  "Bio" comes from the Greek "bios" meaning "life". "Technology" comes from the Greek "tekhnologia" meaning "systematic treatment". "-ist" is a suffix indicating a person who practices or is an expert in a particular field.
