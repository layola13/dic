nonsportingly:/ˌnɒnˈspɔːtɪŋli/ /ˌnɑːnˈspɔːrtɪŋli/| adv.|不光明正大地，缺乏体育精神地|In a manner that lacks sportsmanship or fairness; dishonestly or unfairly, especially in a game or competition.|He won the game nonsportingly by tripping his opponent. 他不光明正大地赢得了比赛，因为他绊倒了对手。|Synonyms: unfairly, dishonestly, unsportsmanlike|Antonyms: fairly, sportsmanlike, honorably|词性: 副词

nonsporting:/ˌnɒnˈspɔːtɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnˈspɔːrtɪŋ/| adj.|非运动的，不参与运动的; 不具有体育精神的|Not related to sports; lacking sportsmanship.|He made a nonsporting remark about the other team's uniforms. 他对另一队的队服发表了不具体育精神的评论。|Synonyms: unsportsmanlike, unsporting|Antonyms: sporting, sportsmanlike|词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "sporting" (adjective related to sports and fair play).  "Sporting" ultimately derives from the Old French "desporter" meaning "to divert, amuse."
