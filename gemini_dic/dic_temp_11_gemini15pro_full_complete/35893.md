cocoon:/kəˈkuːn/| n. |茧;  保护层; 安逸舒适的状态 |A silky case spun by the larva of many insects, as moths and butterflies, for protection in the pupal stage.  A protective or enclosing covering. A state of seclusion or comfort.|The caterpillar formed a cocoon. 毛毛虫形成了一个茧。|The child was wrapped in a cocoon of blankets. 孩子被裹在毯子里，像是在茧里一样。|近义词：shell, case, covering, sanctuary, refuge|反义词:exposure|词性:名词
