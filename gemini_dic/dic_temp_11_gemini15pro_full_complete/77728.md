gemmuliferous:/ˌdʒɛmjʊˈlɪfərəs/
adjective
产生芽球的; 有芽球的
Bearing or producing gemmules.
Example:  The gemmuliferous sponge can survive harsh conditions.  这种有芽球的海绵可以在恶劣的环境中生存。

Example:  Scientists studied the gemmuliferous properties of the freshwater sponge. 科学家研究了淡水海绵的芽球特性。
Synonym:  gemmule-bearing
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, could consider "sterile" in a reproductive context)
词性: adjective
