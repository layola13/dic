fluocerine:/fluːˈɒsərɪn/ /fluːˈɑːsərɪn/
noun|氟铈矿|A mineral, a hydrated cerium fluoride, CeF₃·½H₂O, occurring as yellow to colorless hexagonal crystals.|Fluocerine is a relatively rare mineral found in certain hydrothermal deposits. 氟铈矿是一种相对稀有的矿物，发现于某些热液矿床中。|Synonyms: None commonly used. |Antonyms: N/A|n.
tysonite:/ˈtaɪsəˌnaɪt/ /ˈtaɪsoʊˌnaɪt/
noun|泰森石|A mineral, a fluoride of cerium and lanthanum, (Ce,La)(CO₃)F, occurring as brown hexagonal crystals.|Tysonite is a more common source of cerium than fluocerine. 泰森石是比氟铈矿更常见的铈来源。|Synonyms: None commonly used. |Antonyms: N/A|n.
