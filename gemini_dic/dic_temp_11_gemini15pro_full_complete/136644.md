nonschoolings:/ˌnɒnˈskuːlɪŋz/
n.
非学校教育;在家教育
The practice of educating children outside of the traditional school system, often by parents at home.
Many families are choosing nonschoolings for their children due to concerns about the traditional education system. 许多家庭由于担心传统教育体系而选择在家教育孩子。
The family opted for nonschoolings to allow their children to learn at their own pace.  这个家庭选择了非学校教育，让孩子们按照自己的节奏学习。

近义词:homeschooling, unschooling, alternative education
反义词:schooling, traditional education
词性:名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "schooling" (noun derived from "school")
