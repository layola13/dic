ligniperdous:/lɪɡnɪˈpɜːrdəs/| adj.|毁灭木材的，破坏木材的|Destructive to wood; wood-destroying.  This word combines "ligni-" (relating to wood) and "perdous" (destructive, causing loss).|例句：The ligniperdous fungus quickly destroyed the wooden beams.  这种毁灭木材的真菌迅速摧毁了木梁。

例句：Termites are ligniperdous insects that can cause significant damage to homes. 白蚁是破坏木材的昆虫，会对房屋造成重大损害。|近义词：wood-destroying, xylophagous|反义词：wood-preserving|词性：adjective
