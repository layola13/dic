misadventure:/ˌmɪsədˈvɛntʃər/ | n. | 不幸事件，灾难，厄运 | An instance of bad luck; an unfortunate accident. |  He lost a leg in a hunting misadventure. 他在一次狩猎事故中失去了一条腿。

| Example 1:  Their camping trip turned into a misadventure when their tent was swept away by a flash flood. 他们的露营之旅变成了一场灾难，因为他们的帐篷被山洪冲走了。

| Example 2: The businessman's latest venture turned into a misadventure, costing him his entire fortune.  这位商人的最新投资变成了一场不幸的事件，让他损失了全部财产。

| Synonym: mishap, misfortune, accident, calamity, disaster | Antonym:  fortune, success, triumph | noun
