cafenet: /ˈkæfeɪnɛt/ | n. | 咖啡馆网络；咖啡馆风格的网络 | A convolutional neural network used for image classification, trained on a dataset of images of objects found in cafes.  It is a smaller and faster version of AlexNet. |  Example: The researchers used Cafenet to classify images of coffee cups and pastries. 研究人员使用 Cafenet 对咖啡杯和糕点的图像进行分类。 | Example: Cafenet, while less powerful than more modern architectures, provides a good starting point for understanding convolutional neural networks. 虽然 Cafenet 不如更现代的架构强大，但它为了解卷积神经网络提供了一个良好的起点。| Synonym: convolutional neural network, CNN, AlexNet (similar) | Antonym:  N/A | noun
