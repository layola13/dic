driving: /ˈdraɪvɪŋ/ (US), /ˈdraɪvɪŋ/ (UK)| n. adj.|驾驶，驱使，推进力|The act of operating and controlling a vehicle. Also, a strong motivating force.|例句：Driving in the city can be stressful. 在城市里开车可能会有压力。|近义词：steering, operating, motoring|反义词：riding, walking|词性：noun

driving: /ˈdraɪvɪŋ/ (US), /ˈdraɪvɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|强劲的，有推动力的|Having the power to compel or urge.|例句：The driving force behind the project was his passion.  这个项目的驱动力是他的热情。|近义词：motivating, compelling, impelling|反义词：weak, ineffective|词性：adjective
