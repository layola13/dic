experientialist:/ɪkˌspɪəriˈɛnʃəlɪst/ | n. | 经验主义者；经验论者 |  A person who emphasizes the importance of experience, especially sensory experience, in the formation of ideas and knowledge. | She's an experientialist who believes that learning comes best through doing. 她是一位经验主义者，相信学习的最佳途径是实践。 |  empiricist, pragmatist |  theoretician, rationalist | noun
