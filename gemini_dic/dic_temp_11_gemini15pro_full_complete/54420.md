disindividualize:/ˌdɪsɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlaɪz/
verb
使丧失个性；使失去个体性
To cause (a person) to lose their individuality or sense of self; to deprive of individual identity.
The anonymity of the internet can disindividualize people, leading to online disinhibition.  互联网的匿名性会使人们丧失个性，导致网络行为失控。
Large crowds can disindividualize people, making them feel less accountable for their actions.  大量的人群会使人们丧失个性，让他们对自己的行为感到不那么负责任。
