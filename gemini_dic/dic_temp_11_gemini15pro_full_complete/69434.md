feening:/ˈfiːnɪŋ/| n. |渴望，瘾，强烈的欲望|A strong craving or urge, especially for drugs.  An intense desire or need for something.|例句：He was feening for a cigarette after the long flight. 长途飞行后，他非常想抽烟。

例句：She had a feening for chocolate. 她非常想吃巧克力。|近义词：craving, yearning, jonesing, hankering|反义词：apathy, indifference|词性：名词

词源:  Likely a back-formation from the adjective "fiend," meaning "an evil spirit or demon," which later came to be associated with addiction.  The term "fiending" was used to describe the desperate behavior of addicts, and "feening" emerged as a noun form of this.
