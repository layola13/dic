monogynous: /məˈnɒɡɪnəs/ /məˈnɑːɡɪnəs/
adjective
一夫一妻的；一雄一雌的
Having only one wife or mate at a time.
例句：The swan is a monogynous bird, mating for life. 天鹅是一夫一妻制的鸟类，终生交配。

例句：Monogynous societies are common among certain primate species.  一夫一妻制的社会在某些灵长类物种中很常见。

近义词：monogamic
反义词：polygynous, polygamous
词性：adjective
