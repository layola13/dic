nettle:/ˈnetl/ (US) /ˈnetəl/ (UK)| n. v.|荨麻；激怒|A herbaceous plant which has jagged leaves covered with stinging hairs.  As a verb, it means to irritate or annoy someone.|
The nettles stung my legs as I walked through the long grass. 我走过长草时，荨麻刺痛了我的腿。

He was nettled by her constant criticism. 他被她不断的批评激怒了。

|近义词：irritate, annoy, vex, irk (v); stinging nettle (n)|反义词：soothe, please, calm (v)|词性:noun, verb
