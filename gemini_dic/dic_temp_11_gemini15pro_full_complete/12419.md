asites:/əˈsaɪtiːz/ | n. | 腹水 |  An abnormal accumulation of fluid in the abdomen. | The patient presented with ascites due to liver cirrhosis.  该病人因肝硬化出现腹水。| synonyms: hydroperitoneum | antonyms:  None (absence of ascites is the opposite condition, not a single word) | noun

asites:/əˈsaɪtiːz/ | n. | 腹水 |  The build-up of fluid in the space between the lining of the abdomen and abdominal organs. |  His ascites was so severe it made breathing difficult. 他的腹水非常严重，导致呼吸困难。| synonyms: abdominal dropsy, peritoneal effusion | antonyms:  None | noun
