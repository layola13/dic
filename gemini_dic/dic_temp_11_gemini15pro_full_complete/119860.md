milesian:/maɪˈliːʒən/ /maɪˈliːʃən/
adjective|米利都学派的；米利都的；[M-]爱尔兰的|Of or relating to Miletus, an ancient Greek city in Ionia, or its school of philosophy. Also, relating to Ireland.|例句：Milesian philosophers believed water was the fundamental element. 米利都学派的哲学家认为水是基本元素。

例句：He studied the Milesian school of thought. 他研究了米利都学派。|近义词：Ionian (for the philosophical school)|反义词:None|词性:adjective

milesian:/maɪˈliːʒən/ /maɪˈliːʃən/
noun|米利都人；[M-]爱尔兰人|A native or inhabitant of Miletus or Ireland.|例句：Many Milesians emigrated to Ireland during the Bronze Age. 许多米利都人在青铜时代移民到爱尔兰。

例句：He is a Milesian by birth. 他是土生的爱尔兰人。|近义词：Irishman/Irishwoman (for the Irish meaning)|反义词:None|词性:noun
