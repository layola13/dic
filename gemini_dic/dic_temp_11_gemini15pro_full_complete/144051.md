outrivaled:/ˌaʊtˈraɪvəld/
verb
胜过，超越
to surpass or defeat someone in competition or rivalry
例句：She outrivaled all her competitors and won the championship. 她胜过所有竞争对手，赢得了冠军。

例句：The new company quickly outrivaled its older, established competitors. 这家新公司迅速超越了那些老牌竞争对手。

近义词：surpass, exceed, outdo, outstrip, eclipse
反义词：lose to, be defeated by
词性：动词
