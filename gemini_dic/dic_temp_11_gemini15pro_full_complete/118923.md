metempirical:/ˌmɛtəmˈpɪrɪkəl/
adjective
形而上学经验的; 超验的
Going beyond the empirical; transcendental.  Relating to knowledge or experience that is beyond the scope of empirical observation or scientific verification.
例句：Metempirical philosophies often explore concepts like consciousness and free will.  形而上学经验的哲学经常探讨意识和自由意志等概念。

例句：His argument relied on metempirical assumptions about the nature of reality. 他的论点依赖于关于现实本质的形而上学经验的假设。
近义词：transcendental, metaphysical, supersensible
反义词：empirical, experiential, observational
词性：adjective
