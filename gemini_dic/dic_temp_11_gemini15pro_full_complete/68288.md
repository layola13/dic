falcate:/ˈfɔːlkeɪt/ (US), /ˈfɔːlkeɪt/ (UK)| adj.| 镰刀形的，弯曲的|Curved like a sickle; hooked.|The bird has a falcate beak. 这只鸟的喙是镰刀形的。|近义词：hooked, curved, sickle-shaped|反义词：straight|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *falcātus*, past participle of *falcāre* "to bend, curve," from *falx* "sickle."
