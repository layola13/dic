depredator:/ˈdeprədeɪtər/ (US), /ˈdɛprɪdeɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |掠夺者，捕食者|An animal that preys on other animals.|例句：The lion is a fierce depredator. 狮子是凶猛的掠食者。

例句：The hawk is a depredator of small birds. 老鹰是捕食小鸟的掠夺者。

近义词：predator, raider, plunderer, looter|反义词:prey|词性:名词
