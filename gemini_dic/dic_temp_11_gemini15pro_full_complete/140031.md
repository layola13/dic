oenanthylic:/ˌiːnænˈθɪlɪk/
adjective
庚酸的；含有七个碳原子的
relating to or derived from heptanoic acid (also called enanthic acid), a colorless oily liquid carboxylic acid found especially in rancid butter and coconut oil.
Oenanthylic acid is used in the manufacture of esters. 庚酸用于制造酯类。
Oenanthylic aldehyde has a pungent odor.  庚醛有一种刺鼻的气味。

近义词:heptanoic, enanthic

词性: adjective
