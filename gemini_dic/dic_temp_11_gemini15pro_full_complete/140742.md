omniscience:/ɒmˈnɪʃəns/ /ɑːmˈnɪʃəns/| n. |无所不知；全知|The state of knowing everything.|God is often depicted as possessing omniscience. 上帝常被描述为拥有无所不知的能力。|近义词：all-knowingness, omnipotence|反义词：ignorance, nescience|词性:名词

omniscient:/ɒmˈnɪʃənt/ /ɑːmˈnɪʃənt/| adj. |无所不知的；全知的|Having complete or unlimited knowledge, awareness, or understanding.|The omniscient narrator knew the thoughts and feelings of every character. 无所不知的叙述者了解每个角色的想法和感受。|近义词：all-knowing, all-seeing|反义词：ignorant, unknowing|词性:形容词
