hears:/hɪrz/ | v. | 听见，听说 | perceives with the ear; learns about |  She hears music coming from the next room. 她听见隔壁房间传来音乐。| synonyms: listens to, perceives, learns of | antonyms: ignores, disregards | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *heren*, from Old English *hīeran* (to hear, perceive by the ear, listen to, obey), from Proto-Germanic *hauzijan*, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱh₂eus- (to perceive, hear, listen). Cognate with Dutch *horen* (to hear), German *hören* (to hear), Gothic *𐌷𐌰𐌿𐍃𐌾𐌰𐌽* (hausjan, to hear).

PHRASE->
hearsay: /ˈhɪrseɪ/ 道听途说, 传闻
hear from: 收到...的来信
hear of: 听说，知道
hear out: 听完
not hear of it:  不同意，不允许


注意事项:  hears 主要强调“听见”这个动作本身，而 listen to 强调“听”这个动作的主动性和持续性。
