buffoons:/bəˈfuːnz/
noun
小丑；滑稽可笑的人
A buffoon is someone who acts in a silly or foolish way, often to amuse others.
The king's court was full of buffoons and jesters. 国王的宫廷里满是小丑和弄臣。

buffoons:/bəˈfuːnz/
noun
笨蛋；蠢货
A buffoon can also refer to a person who is generally considered stupid or incompetent.
He acted like a complete buffoon at the meeting. 他在会议上的表现像个十足的笨蛋。


近义词:clown, jester, fool, idiot, dunce
反义词:sage, genius, intellectual
词性:noun
