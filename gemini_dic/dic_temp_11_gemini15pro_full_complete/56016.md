do-or-die:/ˌduː ɔːr ˈdaɪ/ | n. | 决一死战；生死关头 | A situation of great urgency or desperation. |  It was do-or-die for the company; they needed a miracle to survive.  对公司来说，这是生死存亡的关头；他们需要奇迹才能生存。 | crisis, showdown, last stand | safety, security | noun
