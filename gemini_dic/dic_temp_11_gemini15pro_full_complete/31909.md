cheesy:/ˈtʃiːzi/| adj.|俗气的，劣质的，不真诚的|Like cheap or low-quality entertainment, often in a way that is funny or embarrassing.  Also, overly sentimental or emotional.|例句：The movie was full of cheesy dialogue. 这部电影充满了俗气的对白。

例句：He made a cheesy joke that nobody laughed at. 他讲了一个蹩脚的笑话，没人笑。|近义词：corny, tacky, clichéd, sentimental, mawkish|反义词：sophisticated, refined, tasteful, genuine|词性:adjective

cheesy:/ˈtʃiːzi/| adj.|（食物）含有大量奶酪的|Containing or tasting strongly of cheese.|例句：I love cheesy nachos. 我喜欢吃奶酪味浓厚的玉米片。

例句：The sauce was thick and cheesy. 酱汁又浓又多奶酪。|近义词： cheesy (in the sense of corny/tacky) doesn't have a direct synonym in this context. Words like "rich," "creamy," or "sharp" might describe the cheese flavor. |反义词：bland (for flavor)|词性:adjective
