ham-fisted:/ˌhæmˈfɪstɪd/
adjective
笨手笨脚的；拙劣的
clumsy and awkward in handling things; lacking skill or finesse.
He made a ham-fisted attempt to fix the broken vase. 他笨手笨脚地试图修理破碎的花瓶。
clumsy, awkward, inept, maladroit
skillful, deft, adroit
