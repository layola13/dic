concludent:/kənˈkluːdənt/ | adj. | 确定的；决定性的；终止的 | Serving to settle or decide; decisive; conclusive. | The lawyer presented concludent evidence that proved the defendant's guilt. 律师提供了确凿的证据，证明了被告有罪。| The fingerprint evidence was concludent and led to the arrest of the suspect. 指纹证据确凿，导致嫌疑人被捕。 | 近义词: conclusive, decisive, definitive, final | 反义词: inconclusive, indecisive, ambiguous, uncertain | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Late Latin *concludentem, present participle of concludere "to conclude," from com- "together" (see com-) + claudere "to shut" (see close (v.)).
