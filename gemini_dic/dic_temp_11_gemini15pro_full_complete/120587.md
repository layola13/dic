mirror-image:/ˈmɪrər ˌɪmɪdʒ/| n. adj.|镜像，镜像的|An image that is seen in a mirror or that is the reverse of something else.|The photo was a mirror image of the scene. 这张照片是现场的镜像。|近义词：reflection, counterpart, opposite|反义词:|n.

mirror-image:/ˈmɪrər ˌɪmɪdʒ/| adj.|镜像的|Appearing reversed as in a reflection.|His life was a mirror image of his father's. 他的生活是他父亲生活的镜像。|近义词：reflected, reversed|反义词:|adj.
