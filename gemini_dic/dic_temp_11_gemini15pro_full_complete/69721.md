feudal:/ˈfjuːdəl/ | adj. | 封建的；封建制度的 | Relating to or characteristic of feudalism. | The feudal system dominated medieval Europe.  封建制度统治着中世纪的欧洲。|  The king granted lands to his loyal followers under the feudal system. 国王在封建制度下把土地赐给了他忠诚的追随者。| 近义词: seigniorial, manorial | 反义词: modern, democratic | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Old French *feodal, *feudal, from Medieval Latin feudalis (“relating to a fief”), from feudum (“fief”), of Germanic origin.
