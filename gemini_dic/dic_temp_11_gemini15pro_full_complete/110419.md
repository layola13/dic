like-mindedness:/ˌlaɪkˈmaɪndɪdnəs/ |n.|志同道合，相同想法|The group bonded over their like-mindedness on key political issues.  这个团体因在关键政治问题上志同道合而联系在一起。|like-mindedness is the quality of people having similar tastes, interests, or opinions.|Example:  Their like-mindedness on environmental issues made them fast friends. 他们在环境问题上的相同看法使他们很快成为朋友。|Synonym: compatibility, congeniality, agreement, accord, harmony, unity, unanimity|Antonym:  discord, disagreement, disharmony, conflict, opposition|noun

like-mindedness:/ˌlaɪkˈmaɪndɪdnəs/ |n.|一致，相同观点|The project benefited from the like-mindedness of the team members.  该项目受益于团队成员的相同观点。|The like-mindedness of the community members allowed them to work together effectively. 社区成员的团结一致使他们能够有效地合作。|Synonym:  concord, consensus, uniformity|Antonym:  difference, divergence, dissent|noun
