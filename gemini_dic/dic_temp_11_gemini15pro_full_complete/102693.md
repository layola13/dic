ETYMOLOGY->
From jam + -iness.  "Jam" originally referred to a crowded or packed situation, later extending to the fruit preserve due to its thick consistency. The suffix "-iness" transforms an adjective (in this case, implied "jammy") into a noun denoting the state or quality of being jammy.
