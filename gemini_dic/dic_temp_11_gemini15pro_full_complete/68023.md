factorial:/fækˈtɔːriəl/| adj. n.|阶乘的，阶乘|of or relating to a factorial; a factorial.|The factorial of 5 is 120. 5的阶乘是120。|近义词:multiplicative, |反义词: (No direct antonym for a mathematical concept) |词性:adjective, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *factorialis, from Latin factor ("doer, maker") + -ialis (suffix forming adjectives).

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
The factorial of a non-negative integer n, denoted by n!, is the product of all positive integers less than or equal to n. = 非负整数n的阶乘，用n!表示，是所有小于或等于n的正整数的乘积。
Five factorial (5!) equals 120. = 5的阶乘(5!)等于120。
