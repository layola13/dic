boggle:/ˈbɑːɡəl/ | v. | 使困惑；使吃惊；犹豫 | To hesitate or have difficulty because of doubt, fear, or scruples. |  The sheer size of the universe boggles the mind. 宇宙的浩瀚令人难以置信。 | synonyms: amaze, astound, bewilder, confuse, dumbfound, nonplus, perplex, puzzle | antonyms: clarify, clear up, enlighten, explain | verb

boggle:/ˈbɑːɡəl/ | n. | 困惑；犹豫 | A state of perplexity or confusion. |  He was in a boggle about what to do next. 他对下一步该做什么感到困惑。 | synonyms: bewilderment, confusion, perplexity, puzzle | antonyms: clarity, clearness | noun
