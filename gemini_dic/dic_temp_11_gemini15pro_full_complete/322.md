abettor:/əˈbɛtər/| n.|教唆犯；帮凶；煽动者|Someone who abets a crime or wrongdoing; an accomplice. |例句：The abettor of the robbery was caught fleeing the scene.  抢劫案的帮凶在逃离现场时被抓获。

例句：She was charged as an abettor to the murder. 她被指控为谋杀案的帮凶。|近义词：accomplice, accessory, instigator, collaborator|反义词：opponent, adversary, enemy|词性：noun

abet:/əˈbɛt/| v.|教唆；怂恿；煽动；支持|To encourage or assist (someone) to do something wrong, in particular, to commit a crime or other offense. |例句：He was accused of abetting the terrorists. 他被指控唆使恐怖分子。

例句：She abetted her brother in his escape from prison. 她帮助她哥哥越狱。|近义词：encourage, assist, support, aid|反义词：hinder, obstruct, impede, discourage|词性：verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Anglo-French *abeter, from Old French abeter "to bait, hound on," from a- "to" (from Latin ad) + beter "to bait."
