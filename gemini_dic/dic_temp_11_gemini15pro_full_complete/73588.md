foreteach:/fɔːrˈtiːtʃ/
verb
预言，预示
to predict or tell of beforehand.
The prophets foreteach the coming of the Messiah. 先知预言了弥赛亚的到来。
Synonyms: predict, prophesy, forecast, foresee
Antonyms: None specifically, but one could consider "conceal" or "withhold" as conceptually opposite.
词性: 动词
