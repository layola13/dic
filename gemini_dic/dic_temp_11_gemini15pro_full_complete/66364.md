excuse:/ɪkˈskjuːz/ (US) /ɪkˈskjuːs/ (UK)| n. vt.|借口，理由；原谅；免除|An excuse is a reason, often false, given to justify a fault or offense. To excuse is to forgive or overlook a fault or offense.|例句：He gave a lame excuse for being late. 他为迟到找了个蹩脚的借口。|近义词：reason, justification, apology, pardon, forgive|反义词:blame, accuse|noun, verb


excuse:/ɪkˈskjuːz/ (US) /ɪkˈskjuːs/ (UK)| vt.|原谅；免除|To excuse is to forgive or overlook a fault or offense, or to release someone from an obligation.|例句：Please excuse my interruption. 请原谅我打断一下。|近义词：pardon, forgive, absolve|反义词:blame, hold accountable|verb
