curtailing:/kɜːrˈteɪlɪŋ/
verb
削减，缩短
reducing something, especially by cutting off the end
The government is curtailing public spending. 政府正在削减公共开支。
Synonyms: reduce, shorten, decrease, diminish, cut back, cut down
Antonyms: increase, extend, lengthen, expand, augment
verb
