immeasurably:/ɪˈmeʒərəbli/
adverb
不可估量地，极大地
Beyond measure; to an extent that cannot be measured.
The value of his contributions is immeasurably great.  他的贡献的价值巨大无比。
Her health has improved immeasurably. 她的健康状况得到了极大的改善。

近义词: vastly, immensely, enormously, infinitely, limitlessly
反义词: measurably, slightly, marginally
词性: adverb
