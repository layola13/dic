apologizer:/əˈpɒlədʒaɪzər/| n. |道歉者；辩护者|A person who apologizes, especially excessively or habitually.  Someone who defends a person, cause, or idea. |例句：He's a constant apologizer, always saying sorry even for minor things. 他总是道歉，即使是小事也总是说对不起。
