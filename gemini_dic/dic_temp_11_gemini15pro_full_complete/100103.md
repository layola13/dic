interpouring:/ˌɪntərˈpɔːrɪŋ/
n.
相互倾注，交融
the act of pouring into one another; a mutual or reciprocal pouring
The interpouring of two rivers created a powerful current. 两条河流的交汇形成了一股强劲的水流。

近义词: mingling, blending, merging, fusion, confluence
反义词: separation, division, divergence

词性: 名词 (noun)
词源:  From inter- + pouring, from pour.
