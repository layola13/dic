glenoid:/ˈɡliːnɔɪd/| adj. n.|  关节窝的，浅窝; 关节窝 |Relating to or resembling a socket, especially that of a joint.  A shallow depression, especially in a bone, that receives the head of another bone, forming a ball-and-socket joint. |The glenoid cavity of the shoulder receives the head of the humerus. 肩胛骨的关节盂窝与肱骨头相连。|Near synonyms: articular, cotyloid, concave |Antonyms: convex|adjective, noun
