journals:/ˈdʒɜːrnəlz/ (US) /ˈdʒɜːnəlz/ (UK)| n.|期刊；杂志；日记；日志|A periodical publication containing articles on specialized topics, often scholarly.  Also, a personal record of events, thoughts, and feelings.|She publishes regularly in academic journals. 她定期在学术期刊上发表文章。|synonyms: periodicals, magazines, publications, diaries, logs|antonyms: (For the periodical sense) book, monograph; (for the diary sense) none applicable|noun

journals:/ˈdʒɜːrnəlz/ (US) /ˈdʒɜːnəlz/ (UK)| v.|记录；记载|To write or record something in a journal.|He journaled his experiences during his travels. 他在旅行期间记录了他的经历。|synonyms: record, document, chronicle, log|antonyms: none applicable|verb
