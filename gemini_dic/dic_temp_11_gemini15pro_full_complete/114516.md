Etymology:
From Old French *maladie (modern French maladie), from malade (“sick, ill”), from Latin male habitus (“in a bad condition”), from male (“badly, ill”) + habitus (“condition”).  Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European *mol- (“soft”).
