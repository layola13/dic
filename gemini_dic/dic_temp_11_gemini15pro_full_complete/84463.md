halophyte:/ˈhæləfaɪt/ | n. | 盐生植物 | A plant that grows in saline conditions, such as in marshes or near the coast. | 例句：Mangroves are a common type of halophyte. 红树林是一种常见的盐生植物。

例句：Halophytes have special adaptations to tolerate high salt concentrations. 盐生植物有特殊的适应性来耐受高盐浓度。| 近义词：salt-tolerant plant | 反义词：glycophyte (淡水植物) | 词性: noun
