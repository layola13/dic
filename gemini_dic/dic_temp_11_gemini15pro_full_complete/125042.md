mulberry:/ˈmʌlˌbɛri/ (US) /ˈmʌlbəri/ (UK)| n.| 桑葚；桑树 | A mulberry is a small, sweet, dark purple, red, or white fruit that grows on a tree.  It is also the name of the tree itself. | The birds were feasting on the ripe mulberries. 鸟儿们正在享用成熟的桑葚。|  The silkworms are fed on mulberry leaves. 蚕吃桑叶。 | synonyms: blackberry (for the fruit), moral (for the tree) | antonyms: N/A | noun

mulberry:/ˈmʌlˌbɛri/ (US) /ˈmʌlbəri/ (UK)| adj.| 桑葚色的；紫红色的| Of a dark reddish-purple colour. |  She wore a mulberry-coloured dress. 她穿着一件紫红色的连衣裙。 | synonyms: purplish-red, maroon, wine-colored | antonyms: N/A | adjective
