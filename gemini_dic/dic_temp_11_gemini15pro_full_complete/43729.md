creed:/kriːd/
n.
a system of religious belief; a formal statement of faith
The Apostles' Creed is a fundamental statement of Christian belief. 使徒信经是基督教信仰的基本陈述。
He lived by a simple creed of honesty and hard work. 他秉持着诚实和勤奋的简单信条生活。
近义词: doctrine, dogma, tenet, credo
反义词: disbelief, doubt, skepticism
词性: noun


词源:
From creedless + -ness.  Creed is from Old English crēda, from Latin credo “I believe.”
