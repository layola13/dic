alienism:/ˈeɪliənɪzəm/
n.
精神错乱；精神病学（尤指过时的）; 精神病治疗；精神病院的管理
The study and treatment of mental illness, especially in the past.  (A now outdated term for psychiatry.)
He specialized in alienism, a field now known as psychiatry. 他专攻精神病学，也就是现在所说的精神病学。
近义词:psychiatry, insanity, madness
反义词:sanity, mental health
词性:名词

alienism:/ˈeɪliənɪzəm/
n.
疏离感；异化
A state of being alienated, estranged, or isolated from society, one's own feelings, or one's environment.
The novel explores themes of alienism and the search for belonging. 这部小说探讨了疏离感和寻找归属感的主题。
近义词:estrangement, isolation, alienation
反义词:belonging, connection, integration
词性:名词
