out-shown:/ˌaʊtˈʃoʊn/
verb
胜过，比...更出色
surpass in splendor, performance, or excellence.
Her performance out-shown all the others. 她的表演胜过所有其他人。
近义词:  surpassed, eclipsed, outshone, outperformed
反义词:  underperformed, overshadowed
