geniture: /ˈdʒɛnɪtʃər/ | n. | 出生, 天赋, 生辰星位 | The exact time of someone's birth; nativity.  The disposition, character, or qualities supposedly imparted to a person by the stars or other celestial bodies dominant at the time of birth.  Offspring; progeny. |  例句：Astrologers believe that one's geniture influences their personality.  占星家认为一个人的生辰星位会影响他们的性格。

例句：The poet believed his artistic geniture came from the Muses.  这位诗人相信他的艺术天赋来自于缪斯女神。 | 近义词：birth, nativity, origin, descendants, offspring | 反义词: death | 词性: noun
