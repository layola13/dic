caravan: /ˈkærəvæn/ (US) /ˈkærəvæn/ (UK)| n.| 旅行队，大篷车；房车|A group of people, especially traders or pilgrims, traveling together across a desert or through hostile territory. A vehicle equipped for living in, especially for holidays.|例句：The caravan crossed the desert slowly. 旅行队缓慢地穿越沙漠。|例句：We rented a caravan for our summer vacation. 我们租了一辆房车去过暑假。|近义词：convoy, procession, trailer, camper, motorhome|反义词: None|词性: noun

caravan: /ˈkærəvæn/ (US) /ˈkærəvæn/ (UK)| v.|组成旅行队；用大篷车运送|To travel in or form a caravan.|例句：They caravanned across the country. 他们组成旅行队穿越全国。|例句：The goods were caravanned to the market. 货物用大篷车运到市场。|近义词：convoy, transport|反义词: None|词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *carvane (Modern French caravane), from Italian carovana, from Persian kārvān.
