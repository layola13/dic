interlaminar:/ˌɪntərˈlæmɪnər/| adj.|层间的，层板间的|Existing or occurring between laminae.  Relating to or situated between layers, especially in stratified rocks.|The interlaminar strength of the composite material was tested.  这种复合材料的层间强度得到了测试。|近义词：interlayer, interstratal|反义词: intralaminar|词性: adjective
