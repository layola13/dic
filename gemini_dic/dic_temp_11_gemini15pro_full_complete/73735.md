forkless:/ˈfɔːrklɪs/| adj.|无叉的，没有叉子的|Lacking a fork or forks.  Referring to something that doesn't have a fork or forks attached to it, or something designed to be used without a fork. |The travelers had to eat their stew with forkless spoons. 旅行者不得不使用没有叉子的勺子来吃炖菜。|The restaurant offered a forkless dessert option. 这家餐厅提供了一种无需叉子的甜点选择。|Near synonyms:  prongless, unpronged, spoon-like|Antonyms: forked, pronged|adjective
