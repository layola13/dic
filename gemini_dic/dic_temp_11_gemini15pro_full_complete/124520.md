mountainboard:/ˈmaʊntɪnbɔːrd/| n. |山地滑板，越野滑板|A skateboard with large, pneumatic tires designed for off-road use, often on rough terrain or down hills.| He took his mountainboard out for a spin on the trails. 他带着他的山地滑板去小路上兜风了。|近义词：all-terrain board, dirtboard|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps skateboard as it is typically used on paved surfaces)|词性:noun
