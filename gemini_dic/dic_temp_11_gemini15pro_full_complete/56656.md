dons:/dɑːnz/ | v. | 穿上，戴上 | puts on (an item of clothing) | He dons his coat and hat before going outside. 他出门前穿上外套，戴上帽子。|  She quickly donned her running shoes. 她迅速穿上跑鞋。 | 近义词: puts on, wears | 反义词: doffs, removes | 词性: verb (third-person singular simple present)

dons:/dɑːnz/ | n. | 大学老师；导师 | a university teacher, especially a senior member of a college at Oxford or Cambridge |  Several dons attended the graduation ceremony. 几位大学老师参加了毕业典礼。 | The dons were discussing the new curriculum. 导师们正在讨论新的课程设置。 | 近义词: professors, lecturers, tutors | 反义词: students, pupils | 词性: noun (plural)


词源:
From Middle English *donnen, *donen, from Old French doner, donner (“to give; to put on (clothing)”), from Latin donāre (“to give; to present; to bestow”), present active infinitive of dōnō (“I give”).
