lopeman:/ˈloʊpmæn/| n. |逃亡者；潜逃者；以长步慢跑的人|A person who lopes or runs away, especially to escape capture.  This word isn't common in modern English and may be considered archaic or dialectal. |Example 1: The lopeman disappeared into the dense forest, evading the pursuing authorities. 逃亡者消失在茂密的森林中，躲避了追捕的当局。

Example 2:  The sheriff was determined to catch the lopeman and bring him to justice. 警长决心抓住逃亡者，将他绳之以法。|近义词：fugitive, runaway, escapee |反义词: captive, prisoner |词性:noun
