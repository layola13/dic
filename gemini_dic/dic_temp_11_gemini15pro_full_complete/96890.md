inexpediency:/ˌɪnɪkˈspiːdiənsi/ | n. | 不合时宜；不适当；不明智 |  The state or quality of being inexpedient; inadvisability; unsuitability.  | Due to the inexpediency of the proposed plan, the committee decided to postpone it. 由于提议的计划不合时宜，委员会决定推迟。| Synonyms: disadvantage, inappropriateness, unsuitability, impolicy | Antonyms: expediency, advisability, suitability, wisdom | noun

inexpedient:/ˌɪnɪkˈspiːdiənt/ | adj. | 不合时宜的；不适当的；不明智的 | Not suitable or advisable; not practical or wise. |  It would be inexpedient to raise taxes at this time.  在这个时候提高税收是不明智的。| Synonyms: disadvantageous, inappropriate, unsuitable, unwise, impolitic | Antonyms: expedient, advisable, suitable, wise, politic | adjective
