jams:/dʒæmz/ | n. | 果酱；拥挤；困境；即兴演奏 | A sweet spread made from fruit and sugar. Also, a situation of being stuck or crowded, or an informal musical performance. |  She spread strawberry jam on her toast. 她把草莓酱涂在吐司上。| synonyms: preserves, conserves, congestion, traffic, improvisation | antonyms:  (For congestion) emptiness, space | noun

jams:/dʒæmz/ | v. |  (jammed, jamming) 卡住；塞满；使阻塞；干扰(无线电波)；(乐器)即兴演奏 | To become or make something become stuck or wedged. To fill something tightly. To interfere with radio waves. To play improvised music. | The paper jammed in the printer. 纸卡在打印机里了。 | synonyms:  stick, wedge, cram, block, disrupt | antonyms: free, release, clear | verb
