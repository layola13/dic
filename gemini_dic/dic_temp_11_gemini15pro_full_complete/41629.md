correctest: /kəˈrɛktɪst/
adjective
最正确的
(nonstandard) Most correct.  This form is generally considered incorrect and should be avoided. Use "most correct" instead.
"He claimed to have the correctest answer, but the teacher disagreed."  他声称拥有最正确的答案，但老师不同意。
近义词: most correct, right, accurate
反义词: incorrect, wrong, inaccurate
词性: adjective
