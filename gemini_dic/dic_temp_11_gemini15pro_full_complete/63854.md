epigrammatists:/ˌɛpɪˈɡræməˌtɪsts/
n.
警句家；善写警句的人
Writers of epigrams, short witty poems or sayings.
Alexander Pope and Oscar Wilde were considered master epigrammatists.
亚历山大·蒲柏和奥斯卡·王尔德被认为是警句大师。
Synonyms:  wits,  quipsters,  punsters
Antonyms:  dullards,  bores
