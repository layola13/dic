kidder:/ˈkɪdər/| n.|开玩笑的人；爱开玩笑的人；骗子|Someone who likes to tell jokes and make people laugh; someone who teases or tricks people.|He's a terrible kidder; don't believe a word he says. 他是个很爱开玩笑的人，别相信他说的任何话。|Synonyms: joker, prankster, teaser, trickster|Antonyms: serious person, straight shooter|noun

kid:/kɪd/| v.|开玩笑；戏弄；欺骗|To tease or trick someone playfully; to tell jokes.|He was just kidding when he said he was going to quit his job. 他说要辞职只是在开玩笑。|Synonyms: joke, tease, fool, trick|Antonyms: be serious, tell the truth|verb
