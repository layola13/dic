nonluminously:/ˌnɒnˈluːmɪnəsli/
adjective
无光地；不发光地
Not emitting or reflecting light; dimly.
The object moved nonluminously across the night sky.  该物体在夜空中无光地移动。
The creature drifted nonluminously through the dark depths.  这个生物在黑暗的深处无光地漂流。
近义词: dimly, faintly, obscurely
反义词: brightly, luminously, shiningly
词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  not, without (Latin origin)
luminous (adjective): emitting or reflecting light (Latin origin: luminosus, from lumen "light")
-ly (suffix): adverbial suffix, forming adverbs from adjectives
