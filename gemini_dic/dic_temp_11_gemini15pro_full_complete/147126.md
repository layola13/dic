overlocker:/ˈəʊvəlɒkə(r)/ (UK), /ˈoʊvərˌlɑːkər/ (US)| n.|包缝机，锁边机|A sewing machine that trims and overcasts (sews over the edge of) a seam simultaneously, to prevent fraying.|She used an overlocker to finish the edges of the scarf. 她用包缝机缝制了围巾的边缘。|近义词: serger (US), edge-stitcher|反义词: None|Noun

overlock:/ˈəʊvəlɒk/ (UK), /ˈoʊvərˌlɑːk/ (US)| v.|包缝，锁边|To sew over the edge of a piece of cloth with a special machine to prevent it from fraying.|I need to overlock the seams of this dress. 我需要把这条裙子的接缝锁边。|近义词: serge (US), edge-stitch|反义词: None|Verb
