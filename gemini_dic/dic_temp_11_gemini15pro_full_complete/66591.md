exhumation:/ˌɛksjuːˈmeɪʃn/ |n.|发掘；掘出（尸体）|The act of digging up something that has been buried, especially a body.|The archaeologist oversaw the careful exhumation of ancient artifacts. 考古学家监督了古代文物的仔细挖掘工作。|近义词:excavation|反义词:interment|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin exhumationem,  from Latin exhumare "to dig out of the ground," from ex- "out of" + humus "ground, earth."
