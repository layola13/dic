monomya:/mɒˈnɒmaɪə/ | n. | 单壳类软体动物 |  A mollusk of the class Monoplacophora, having a single, cap-shaped shell and multiple gills, kidneys, and other organs.  These mollusks were thought to be extinct until 1952, when Neopilina galatheae was discovered. |  Monomya are found in deep ocean environments. 单壳类软体动物生活在深海环境中。|  近义词:  limpet (in a general sense, as both have a single shell),  Neopilina (a specific genus within Monoplacophora) | 反义词: bivalve (two-shelled mollusk), gastropod (most have a spiraled shell), cephalopod (internal or no shell) | 词性: noun
