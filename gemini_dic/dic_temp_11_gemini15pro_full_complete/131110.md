nonbindingly:/ˌnɒnˈbaɪndɪŋli/ | adv. | 无约束力地，非强制性地 |  In a way that does not legally or formally obligate anyone to a particular course of action. | The two parties agreed nonbindingly to explore a potential partnership. 双方无约束力地同意探讨潜在的合作关系。| The committee nonbindingly suggested a new course of action. 委员会非强制性地建议了一种新的行动方案。 | 近义词: informally, unofficially, tentatively | 反义词: bindingly, officially, obligatorily | 词性: 副词

词源:  From "nonbinding" + "-ly."  "Nonbinding" is formed from "non-" (not) + "binding" (obligatory, having legal force). "Binding" ultimately comes from the Old English "bindan" (to tie).
