blazing:/ˈbleɪzɪŋ/| adj.|燃烧的，炽热的，闪耀的，激烈的|very hot or brightly burning; very fast and impressive|The sun was blazing down. 太阳火辣辣地照射着。|近义词：burning, fiery, intense, dazzling|反义词：cold, dim, mild|adjective

blazing:/ˈbleɪzɪŋ/| adv.|燃烧地，炽热地，闪耀地，激烈地|used to emphasize the degree of something|The car was travelling at a blazing speed. 汽车以极快的速度行驶。|近义词：fiercely, intensely, brightly|反义词：mildly, dimly, coldly|adverb
