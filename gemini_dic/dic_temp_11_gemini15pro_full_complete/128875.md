nilgai:/ˈnɪlˌɡaɪ/| n.|蓝牛，尼尔盖 |A large Asian antelope (Boselaphus tragocamelus) with short horns, a tufted mane, and sloping back. The male is bluish gray, and the female is tawny.| We saw a herd of nilgai grazing in the grassland. 我们看到一群蓝牛在草原上吃草。| Nilgai are native to the Indian subcontinent. 蓝牛原产于印度次大陆。|近义词：antelope, blue bull|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Hindi नीलगाय (nīlgāy), from नीला (nīlā, “blue”) + गाय (gāy, “cow, ox”).
