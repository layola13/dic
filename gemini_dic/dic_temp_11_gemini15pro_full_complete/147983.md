overrewards:/ˌoʊvər rɪˈwɔːrdz/
|v.|过度奖励|To give someone excessive rewards, more than they deserve or is necessary.|He overrewarded his employees for minimal effort, creating a sense of entitlement.|近义词：overcompensate, overpay|反义词: underpay, underreward|词性: verb (usually used in the third person singular - overrewards)

overreward:/ˌoʊvər rɪˈwɔːrd/
|n.|过度奖励|The act of giving someone excessive rewards.|The overreward led to decreased motivation in the long run.|近义词：excessive reward, overpayment|反义词: underpayment, insufficient reward|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "above, beyond") + "reward" (from Old French rewarder, from re- "back" + warder "to guard," meaning "to give back something in return").
