Example: The report exposed the nontenableness of the company's financial claims.  这份报告揭露了该公司财务声明的不可靠性。 | Synonyms:  unsustainability, indefensibility,  impracticability,  unviability | Antonyms:  tenability, defensibility, soundness, viability | noun
