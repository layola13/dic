feverish:/ˈfiːvərɪʃ/ (US) /ˈfiːvərɪʃ/ (UK)| adj.|发烧的；狂热的；兴奋的|Having or showing the symptoms of a fever; marked by intense emotion or activity.|She felt feverish and achy.|近义词：febrile, burning, heated, fiery, fervent|反义词：calm, cool, collected|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From *fever* + *-ish*.  *Fever* comes from Old French *fievre*, ultimately from Latin *febris*. The suffix *-ish* forms adjectives from nouns, with the sense "of the nature of" or "having the quality of".
