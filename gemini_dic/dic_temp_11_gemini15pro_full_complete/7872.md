antechamber:/ˈæntiˌtʃeɪmbər/ | n. | 前厅，接待室 | A small room leading to a main room, typically one used for waiting. |  Visitors waited in the antechamber before being admitted to the king's presence.  来访者在获准觐见国王之前在前厅等候。 | synonyms: waiting room, lobby, foyer, vestibule | antonyms: main room, chamber, hall | noun

antechamber:/ˈæntiˌtʃeɪmbər/ | n. | 前厅，接待室 | A less important or influential place or position. | The Senate is sometimes seen as the antechamber of the presidency. 参议院有时被看作是总统职位的前厅。 | synonyms:  precursor, stepping stone,  prelude | antonyms:  culmination, pinnacle, apex | noun
