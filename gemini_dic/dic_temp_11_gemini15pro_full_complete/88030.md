highball:/ˈhaɪbɔːl/| n.|高杯酒，高球鸡尾酒|A mixed alcoholic drink made with a base liquor (such as whiskey or gin) and a larger proportion of a nonalcoholic mixer (such as soda water, ginger ale, or tonic water), served in a tall glass filled with ice.|I ordered a highball at the bar. 我在酒吧点了一杯高球酒。|近义词：cocktail, mixed drink|反义词:|名词
