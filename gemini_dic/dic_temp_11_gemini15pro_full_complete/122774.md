moksha:/ˈmoʊkʃə/| n. |解脱，涅槃|Liberation from the cycle of death and rebirth (samsara) in Hinduism, Buddhism, Jainism, and Sikhism.  It represents the ultimate goal of spiritual practice, achieving a state of perfect bliss, freedom from suffering, and union with the divine or ultimate reality. |He sought moksha through meditation and selfless service. 他通过冥想和无私奉献寻求解脱。|近义词：liberation, enlightenment, nirvana, salvation|反义词:samsara, bondage, ignorance|词性:noun
