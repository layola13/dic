imputrescible:/ˌɪmpjʊˈtrɛsəbəl/
adjective
不腐烂的，不会腐败的
Not subject to putrefaction; incorruptible.
The scientist claimed to have discovered an imputrescible metal alloy.  科学家声称发现了一种不腐烂的金属合金。
The mummy was remarkably imputrescible after thousands of years.  这具木乃伊在几千年后仍然保存得非常完好，没有腐烂。
近义词:incorruptible, imperishable, undecaying
反义词:putrescible, perishable, decomposable
词性:adjective
词源:From Late Latin *imputrescibilis, from in- + putrescere “to rot” + -ibilis.
