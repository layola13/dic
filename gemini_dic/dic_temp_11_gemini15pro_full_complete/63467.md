entries:/ˈɛntriz/ | n. |条目；入口；进入；登记；[会计学]分录 |  Items or records, often in a list, book, or database; the action of going into a place; the right to enter a place.  From Old French "entrée" meaning "entry, entrance". Ultimately from Latin "intrare" meaning "to enter". |  The entries in the ledger detailed all transactions.  账簿中的条目详细记录了所有交易。 | synonyms: records, items, listings, access, ingress | antonyms: exits, egress, departures | noun
