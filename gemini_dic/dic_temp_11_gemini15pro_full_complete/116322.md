masterfulness:/ˈmæstərfʊlnəs/
n.
精通；熟练；高超
The masterfulness of her performance left the audience spellbound.  她精湛的表演让观众如痴如醉。
His masterfulness in the kitchen is renowned. 他在厨房里的精湛技艺远近闻名。
近义词: mastery, proficiency, skill, expertise,  virtuosity
反义词: incompetence, inability, ineptitude, clumsiness
词性: 名词

词源:  From *masterful* + *-ness*. *Masterful* comes from *master* + *-ful*. *Master* ultimately derives from Latin *magister*, "teacher, chief, director." The suffix *-ful* forms adjectives meaning "full of" or "characterized by." The suffix *-ness* forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
