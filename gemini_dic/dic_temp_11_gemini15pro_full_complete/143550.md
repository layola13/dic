outhastens:/ˌaʊtˈheɪsənz/
|v.|超过；跑得比…快|To go faster than someone or something else; to surpass in speed.|The cheetah outhastens the gazelle. 猎豹比瞪羚跑得快。| The new car easily outhastens the older model. 这辆新车轻松地超过了旧款车型。|近义词：outpaces, outruns, overtakes|反义词：lags behind, falls behind|词性: 动词

词源:
"out-" (prefix meaning "beyond, exceeding") + "hasten" (verb meaning "to hurry, to speed up").  "Hasten" comes from Middle English "hasten," from Old Norse "hasta," meaning "to hurry."
