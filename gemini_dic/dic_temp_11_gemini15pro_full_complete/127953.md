neoplasty:/ˈniːəˌplæsti/| n.|外科整形术，新生物形成|Surgical repair of a defect or deformity, or the formation of new tissue.|The neoplasty was successful in restoring function to the patient's hand. 外科整形手术成功地恢复了病人手的功能。|Near synonyms:  reconstruction, surgical repair,  plastic surgery|Antonym:  decomposition, deterioration|noun

neoplasty:/ˈniːəˌplæsti/| n.|新生物，肿瘤|A new growth of tissue, often a tumor.|The biopsy revealed the neoplasty to be benign. 活检显示新生物是良性的。|Near synonyms:  tumor, neoplasm, growth, lesion|Antonym:  regression, remission|noun
