frugalnesses:/fruːˈɡælnəsɪz/
n.
节俭, 俭省
The state of being frugal; thriftiness.
Her frugalnesses allowed her to save a significant portion of her income. 她的节俭让她能够节省收入中的很大一部分。
He admired her frugalnesses and ability to live simply. 他钦佩她的节俭和简单生活的能力。
近义词:thrift, economy, frugality
反义词:extravagance, lavishness, prodigality
词性:名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *frugalnesse*, from Old French *frugalité*, from Latin *frugalitas* (“frugality”), from *frugalis* (“frugal, economical”), from *frux*, *frugis* (“fruit, profit, value”).
