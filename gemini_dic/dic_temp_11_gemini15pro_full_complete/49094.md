deifier:/ˈdiːɪfaɪər/
noun
神化者，奉为神明者
One who deifies someone or something.
The ancient Egyptians were deifiers of many animals, such as cats and crocodiles.  古埃及人将许多动物奉为神明，例如猫和鳄鱼。
deifier:/ˈdiːɪfaɪər/
noun
神化者，奉为神明者
A person who elevates someone or something to the status of a god.
Some fans are such deifiers of their favorite musicians that they seem to worship them. 一些歌迷如此崇拜他们最喜欢的音乐家，以至于他们似乎在膜拜他们。


近义词:idolater, worshiper
反义词:critic, denouncer
词性:n.
