homilete:/ˈhɑːməlɛt/ | n. | 布道；说教 | A homilete is a sermon or a discourse on a religious or moral subject. |  The pastor delivered a powerful homilete on the importance of forgiveness. 牧师就宽恕的重要性发表了强有力的布道。 | synonym: sermon, discourse, preachment | antonym:  None (lack of a direct antonym, perhaps "diatribe" if the homilete is negative) | noun
