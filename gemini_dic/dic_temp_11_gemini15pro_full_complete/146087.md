overeducation:/ˌoʊvərˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃən/
|n.|过度教育；学历过高|The phenomenon of overeducation occurs when people have more education than is required for their job. 过度教育现象发生在人们的教育程度超过了工作所需的水平。|例句：Overeducation can lead to job dissatisfaction. 学历过高会导致工作不满。|近义词：qualification overkill, credential inflation|反义词: undereducation, underqualification|词性: noun

overeducate:/ˌoʊvərˈɛdʒʊkeɪt/
|vt.|过度教育；给予过高的教育|Some argue that the current system overeducates young people for the available jobs.  一些人认为目前的教育体系对年轻人进行了过度教育，超出了现有工作的需求。|例句：We shouldn't overeducate children and stifle their creativity. 我们不应该过度教育孩子，扼杀他们的创造力。|近义词：over-qualify|反义词: undereducate|词性: verb
