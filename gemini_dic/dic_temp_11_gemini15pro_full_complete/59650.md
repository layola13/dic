echelon:/ˈeʃəlɒn/ (UK), /ˈeʃəlɑːn/ (US)| n. |梯队；等级；阶层|A level or rank in an organization, a profession, or society.|The upper echelons of the company were not happy with the decision. 公司的高层对这个决定并不满意。|Synonyms: level, rank, tier, grade, stratum|Antonyms: none|noun


echelon:/ˈeʃəlɒn/ (UK), /ˈeʃəlɑːn/ (US)| n. |梯形编队|A formation of troops, ships, or aircraft in which each unit is positioned behind and to the side of the one in front, like steps.|The bombers flew in echelon formation. 轰炸机以梯形编队飞行。|Synonyms: formation, arrangement, array|Antonyms: none|noun
