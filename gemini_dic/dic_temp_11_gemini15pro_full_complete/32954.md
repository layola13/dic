choragic:/kɔːˈrædʒɪk/
adjective
合唱的，与合唱有关的，赞助合唱的
Relating to a choragus, a wealthy Athenian citizen who financed and supervised the training and performance of a dramatic chorus in ancient Greek festivals.
The play was a magnificent spectacle, thanks in part to the choragic support of a local nobleman.  这出戏是一场壮观的场面，部分归功于当地贵族的合唱赞助。
The festival showcased the city's choragic traditions.  这个节日展示了这座城市的合唱传统。
synonyms: choral, musical, theatrical
antonyms:  n/a
词性: adjective
