incidentally:/ˌɪnsɪˈdɛntli/| adv.|顺便说一句，偶然地|Used to introduce a new topic or piece of information that is not strictly relevant to the main subject of discussion, or to mention something that the speaker has just remembered.|Incidentally, I saw John yesterday. 顺便说一句，我昨天看到约翰了。|Synonyms: by the way, in passing, parenthetically|Antonyms: purposely, intentionally|词性:副词

Incidentally, I heard that they're moving to California. 顺便提一下，我听说他们要搬到加利福尼亚去了。
