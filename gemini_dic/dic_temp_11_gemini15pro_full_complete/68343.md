fall short:/fɔːl ʃɔːrt/
verb phrase
达不到；不符合；缺乏
fail to reach a desired standard, level, or amount; be deficient or inadequate.
The movie fell short of my expectations. 这部电影没有达到我的期望。
The company fell short of its sales target this quarter.  这家公司本季度未能达到销售目标。
近义词: underachieve, disappoint, fail, lack
反义词: exceed, surpass, excel, succeed
词性: verb phrase

fall:/fɔːl/
verb
落下;跌倒
descend freely under the influence of gravity.
The leaves fall from the trees in autumn. 秋天树叶落下。
He tripped and fell down the stairs. 他绊倒了，从楼梯上摔了下来。

short:/ʃɔːrt/
adjective
短的;矮的
measuring a small distance from end to end.
She has short hair. 她留着短发。
He's a bit short for his age.  相对于他的年龄，他有点矮。

词源:
"fall" comes from Old English *feallan, of Germanic origin.
"short" comes from Old English sceort, of Germanic origin.
