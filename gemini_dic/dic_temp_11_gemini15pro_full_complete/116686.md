ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *maturus, from Latin mātūrus (“ripe, timely, early”), from Proto-Italic *maturos, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂eméh₂- (“raw, unripe”).  Cognate with Ancient Greek ὠμός (ōmós, “raw, unripe”).
