kathetometer:/ˌkæθɪˈtɒmɪtər/| n. |测高仪，竖直测距仪|An instrument for measuring vertical distances, especially small differences in height, consisting essentially of a telescope with cross hairs mounted horizontally on a vertical graduated post and capable of being moved up and down and clamped at any required height. |The kathetometer was used to precisely measure the change in water level in the capillary tube. 测高仪被用来精确测量毛细管中水位的变化。

The surveyor used a kathetometer to determine the exact height of the building.  测量员使用测高仪来确定建筑物的准确高度。|近义词:height gauge, altimeter|反义词: N/A |noun
