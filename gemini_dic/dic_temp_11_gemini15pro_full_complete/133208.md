nonexaggerated:/ˌnɒnɪɡˈzædʒəreɪtɪd/
adjective
未夸大的；写实的
Not exaggerated; representing or describing something in a way that is not exaggerated.
Her account of the incident was nonexaggerated and factual. 她对事件的描述未经夸大，是事实。
synonyms:  understated, factual, realistic, accurate, truthful, true-to-life, unvarnished, unembellished, plain, simple
antonyms: exaggerated, overstated, embellished, inflated, hyped, dramatic, theatrical, sensationalized
词性: 形容词
