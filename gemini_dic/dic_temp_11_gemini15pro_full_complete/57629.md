draughts's:/drɑːftsz/ |n.|西洋跳棋的 (所有格)| Belonging to or associated with the game of draughts (checkers). |The draughts's board was worn from years of use. 西洋跳棋的棋盘因为多年的使用而磨损了。|近义词: checkers's (primarily US) |反义词:  (No direct antonym, could potentially list antonyms for the game itself, like chess's) |词性: 名词所有格

Etymology:
From draughts (plural noun), referring to the game, + 's (possessive suffix). The word "draughts" itself likely derives from the act of drawing or moving the pieces.
