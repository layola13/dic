ciceronian:/ˌsɪsəˈroʊniən/
adjective
西塞罗式的；雄辩的；华丽的
Resembling the style of Cicero; eloquent and ornate.
His speeches were known for their ciceronian eloquence. 他的演讲以西塞罗式的雄辩而闻名。
The lawyer delivered a ciceronian defense of his client. 律师为他的客户进行了雄辩的辩护。
eloquent, ornate, grandiloquent
plain, simple, unadorned
