became:/bɪˈkeɪm/| v.|成为，变成|A linking verb indicating a change of state or condition.  It is the past tense of "become".|She became a doctor after years of study. 她经过多年的学习成了一名医生.|synonyms: turned into, grew into, developed into|antonyms: remained, stayed|verb

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
He becomes anxious in crowds.  他在人群中变得焦虑。
Water becomes ice when it freezes. 水结冰时变成冰。


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
The situation is becoming more complicated. 情况变得越来越复杂了。 （现在进行时通常不用于become, 因为become表示状态变化，瞬间完成）


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
He has become a successful businessman. 他已经成为了一名成功的商人。
The weather has become much colder recently. 最近天气变得冷多了。


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
The city has been becoming increasingly crowded. 这个城市越来越拥挤。（现在完成进行时通常不用于become）


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He became ill last week. 他上周病了。
The sky became dark and stormy. 天变得又黑又有暴风雨。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
The weather was becoming warmer. 天气正在变暖。（过去进行时通常不用于become）


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
She had become a lawyer before she entered politics.  在她进入政坛之前，她已经成为了一名律师。
The milk had become sour. 牛奶已经变酸了。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
The situation had been becoming increasingly tense. 局势越来越紧张。（过去完成进行时通常不用于become）


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
He will become a father soon. 他很快就要当爸爸了。
The area will become a popular tourist destination. 该地区将成为一个受欢迎的旅游目的地。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
N/A (Future Continuous is rarely used with "become")


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next year, she will have become a qualified teacher. 到明年，她将成为一名合格的教师。
They will have become accustomed to the new environment by then. 到那时，他们将已经适应了新环境。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
N/A (Future Perfect Continuous is rarely used with "become")


虚拟语气:
If I became rich, I would travel the world. 如果我变得富有，我会环游世界。
I wish I had become a doctor. 我希望我成为了一名医生。


被动语态:
N/A ("Become" is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
When did he become president? 他什么时候当上总统的？
How did the situation become so chaotic? 情况怎么变得如此混乱？


否定句:
He didn't become a lawyer until he was 30. 他直到30岁才成为一名律师。
The caterpillar didn't become a butterfly overnight. 毛毛虫不会在一夜之间变成蝴蝶。



PHRASE->
become of = 发生在...身上; ...的结果如何
become aware of = 意识到
become accustomed to = 习惯于
become involved in = 参与，卷入
become clear = 变得清晰


词源：From Middle English *becumen, from Old English becuman (“to arrive, happen, befall, become, suit”)

注意事项:  "Become" is an intransitive verb, meaning it does not take a direct object.  You can say "She became a doctor," but you cannot say "She became him a doctor."
