clowe:/kloʊ/ | n. |爪子; 抓; 钩子 | A sharp, curved nail on the foot of a bird or animal; something resembling a claw, such as a hook. | The eagle sunk its clowe into the fish.  老鹰把它的爪子伸进鱼里。| synonym: talon, nail, hook | antonym: paw, hoof | noun

clowe:/kloʊ/ | v. |(用爪子)抓; 撕; 挠 | To scratch or dig with claws or nails. | The cat clowed at the furniture. 猫抓家具。 | synonym: scratch, tear, rip | antonym: pet, stroke | verb
