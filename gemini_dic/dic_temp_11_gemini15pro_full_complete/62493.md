endosymbionts:/ˌɛndoʊˌsɪmbaɪˈɑːnts/ |n.| 内共生体 |An endosymbiont is an organism that lives within the body or cells of another organism (the host) in a mutually beneficial relationship.  |The mitochondria in our cells are thought to have originated as endosymbionts.  我们的细胞中的线粒体被认为起源于内共生体。|  |Synonym: symbiont| Antonym: parasite| Noun
