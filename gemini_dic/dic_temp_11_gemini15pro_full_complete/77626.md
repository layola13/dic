geitonogamy:/ˌdʒaɪtənˈɒɡəmi/ | n. | 同株异花授粉 |  Pollination of a flower by pollen from another flower on the same plant. | The apple tree exhibited geitonogamy, with pollen from one flower fertilizing another flower on the same tree. 苹果树展现了同株异花授粉，一朵花的花粉使同一棵树上的另一朵花受精。|  autogamy (within the same flower) | xenogamy, allogamy (between flowers on different plants) | noun
