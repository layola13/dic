disaccordant:/ˌdɪsəˈkɔːrdənt/| adj.|不一致的，不和谐的，刺耳的|Lacking agreement or harmony; conflicting.  Characterized by a lack of harmony in sound; jarring.|The disaccordant voices of the arguing couple filled the apartment. 争吵的夫妻不和谐的声音充满了整个公寓。|Synonyms: conflicting, clashing, discordant, inharmonious, jarring, cacophonous|Antonyms: harmonious, agreeable, concordant, consonant, melodious|adjective
