accommodating:/əˈkɒmədeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
乐于助人的；肯通融的；随和的
(of a person) willing to fit in with someone's wishes or needs.
例句：She was very accommodating and offered to change our booking. 她非常乐于助人，主动提出要帮我们更改预订。
例句：The hotel staff were very accommodating, allowing us to check in early. 酒店工作人员非常通融，允许我们提前入住。
近义词：obliging, helpful, cooperative, considerate
反义词：unhelpful, inconsiderate, uncooperative
词性:adjective

accommodating:/əˈkɒmədeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
提供住宿的
providing lodging or room.
例句：The university has accommodating policies for students with disabilities. 这所大学为残疾学生提供住宿方面的便利政策。
例句：The cruise ship is accommodating over 5,000 passengers. 这艘游轮可以容纳 5000 多名乘客。
近义词：housing, lodging
反义词：N/A
词性:adjective


词源: From the verb "accommodate," which comes from the Latin "accommodare," meaning "to make fit, adapt."
