gutturalism:/ˈɡʌtərəlɪzəm/
n.
喉音化；喉音；粗喉音
The act or process of producing guttural sounds, or the quality of being guttural.  A guttural sound or word.
His speech was marked by a distinct gutturalism. 他的讲话带有明显的喉音。
近义词：gutturality, harshness, roughness
反义词：smoothness, melodiousness
词性: noun
