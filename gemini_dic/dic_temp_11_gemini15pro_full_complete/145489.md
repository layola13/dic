overclinical:/ˈoʊvərˈklɪnɪkəl/
adjective
过度临床的；过分注重医学理论的
Excessively or inappropriately focused on medical or clinical aspects, often at the expense of other considerations, such as the patient's emotional well-being.
The report was criticized for being overclinical and failing to consider the social context of the patients' illnesses.  这份报告因过度临床化而受到批评，未能考虑到患者疾病的社会背景。

The doctor's overclinical approach made the patient feel like just another case study.  医生过度临床化的方式让病人感觉自己只是另一个案例研究。


近义词: impersonal, detached, cold, sterile
反义词: empathetic, compassionate, warm, humanistic
词性: adjective
