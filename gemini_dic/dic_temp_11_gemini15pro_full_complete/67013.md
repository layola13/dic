expiratory:/ɪkˈspaɪrətɔːri/ /ɛkˈspaɪrətɔːri/
adjective|呼气的；呼气用的|Relating to the act of breathing out air from the lungs.|The expiratory phase of breathing is when air leaves the lungs.|近义词:exhalant|反义词:inspiratory|词性:adjective

expire:/ɪkˈspaɪər/ /ɛkˈspaɪər/
verb|期满；终止；死亡；呼气|To come to an end; to die; to breathe out.|My passport expires next month. 我的护照下个月到期。|近义词:terminate, cease, die, exhale|反义词:renew, begin, live, inhale|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *expīrātōrius*, from Latin expīrāre (“to breathe out, exhale”), from ex- (“out”) + spīrāre (“to breathe”).
