journalizability:/ˌdʒɜːrnələˈzaɪzəbɪləti/ | n. | 可记录性；可日记化；可日志记录 | The quality of being suitable for recording in a journal or log.  This often refers to the ability of a transaction or event to be accurately and completely represented in a structured format for auditing or tracking purposes. |  The journalizability of financial transactions is crucial for maintaining accurate records. 金融交易的可记录性对于维护准确的记录至关重要。|  The system ensures the journalizability of all user actions.  该系统确保所有用户操作的可记录性。 | recordability, logability | N/A | noun
