aquatinted:/əˈkweɪntɪd/ | adj. | 认识的，了解的；(尤指)略有交情的 | Having a slight or superficial familiarity with someone or something; slightly acquainted.  | I'm aquatinted with the author's earlier works. 我对这位作者的早期作品略有了解。 | 近义词: familiar, acquainted, versed | 反义词: unfamiliar, unacquainted, ignorant | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From "acquaint" + "-ed."  "Acquaint" comes from Old French "acointer,"  meaning "to make known," from "a-" (to) + "coint" (known), ultimately from Latin "cognitus" (known).
