daguerreotypists:/dəˈɡɛrə(ʊ)ˌtaɪpɪsts/ | n. | 达盖尔照相师 | A person who makes daguerreotypes, an early type of photograph. |  Louis Daguerre himself was one of the first daguerreotypists. 路易·达盖尔本人就是最早的达盖尔照相师之一。

Daguerreotypists were highly skilled in the delicate process. 达盖尔照相师非常精通这种精细的工艺。 | synonym: photographer | antonym: N/A | noun


Etymology:  From French *daguerréotypiste*, from *daguerréotype* + *-iste*. Ultimately from the name of Louis Daguerre, inventor of the process, + Greek *typos* "impression, mark".
