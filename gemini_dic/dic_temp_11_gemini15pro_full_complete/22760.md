borrower:/ˈbɒrəʊər (British English), ˈbɑːroʊər (American English)| n.|借款人，借用人|A person or organization that borrows something, especially money, from a bank or other financial institution.|例句：The borrower must repay the loan within a year. 借款人必须在一年内偿还贷款。

例句：The library keeps a record of all borrowers. 图书馆保存所有借书人的记录。

近义词：debtor, loanee|反义词:lender, creditor|词性:noun
