nonassimilation:/ˌnɒnəˌsɪməˈleɪʃn/
noun
非同化， عدمِ امتزاج
The nonassimilation of immigrants into the local culture can create social tensions. 移民未能融入当地文化会导致社会紧张。
Non-assimilation can be a conscious choice for some immigrant groups, preserving their cultural identity. 对于某些移民群体来说，不融入主流文化是他们维护自身文化认同的一种自觉选择。
Synonyms: nonintegration, separation, segregation
Antonyms: assimilation, integration, blending
