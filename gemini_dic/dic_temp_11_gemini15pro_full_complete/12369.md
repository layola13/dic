ashlar: /ˈæʃlər/ (US), /ˈæʃlə(r)/ (UK)| n.|琢石; 正方石;|A squared building stone, typically one of many forming part of a wall.|The walls were built of finely dressed ashlar.|Synonym: facing, block, stone|Antonym: rubble|noun

ashlar: /ˈæʃlər/ (US), /ˈæʃlə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|琢石的; 正方石的;|Made of or resembling ashlar.|The ashlar facade of the building was impressive.|Synonym: squared, dressed|Antonym: rough, unhewn|adjective
