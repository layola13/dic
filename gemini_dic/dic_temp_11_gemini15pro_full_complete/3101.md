aerosiderite:/ˈɛəroʊˌsaɪdəˌraɪt/
n.
陨铁，陨星
A metallic meteorite; a siderite.  A mass of iron or other metallic material, that has fallen to the earth from outer space.
例句：The museum displayed a large aerosiderite found in the Arizona desert.  博物馆展出了一块在亚利桑那沙漠发现的大陨铁。

例句：Scientists studied the aerosiderite to learn more about the composition of other celestial bodies. 科学家们研究这颗陨铁以了解更多关于其他天体的组成。

近义词：meteorite, siderite
反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, as it refers to a specific object. One could consider terrestrial rock as a contrasting term.)
