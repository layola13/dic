abstracters:/æbˈstræktərz/ /əbˈstræktərz/
n.|摘要编写者；提取者|People who create abstracts or summaries of longer works, or those who extract substances.  Those who prepare summaries or epitomes of legal documents or other writings.|The abstracters prepared concise summaries of the research papers.|近义词：summarizers, epitomizers, extractors|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) elaborators, expanders|词性: Noun

Etymology: From the verb "abstract," which comes from the Latin "abstractus," the past participle of "abstrahere" (to draw away).
