octahedral:/ˌɒktəˈhiːdrəl/ |adj.|八面体的|Having the shape of an octahedron; relating to an octahedron. An octahedron is a solid shape with eight plane faces, especially a regular solid figure with eight equal equilateral triangles.|Diamond crystals are typically octahedral in shape. 钻石晶体通常是八面体的形状。|The complex ion has an octahedral structure.  这个络离子具有八面体结构。|cubic, hexahedral|tetrahedral|adjective
