buildup:/ˈbɪldʌp/| n.|积累，增多，增强；宣传；树立；准备阶段|a gradual increase in something, typically something negative or undesirable;  the process of promoting something or someone by publicizing favorable information; the period of preparation before something important happens|例句：There was a gradual buildup of tension before the exam. 考前气氛逐渐紧张起来。

例句：The media buildup for the new movie was intense. 这部新电影的媒体宣传铺天盖地。|近义词：accumulation, increase, intensification, promotion, hype|反义词：decrease, reduction, decline|词性:noun
