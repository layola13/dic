mammalogy:/məˈmɒlədʒi/
n.
哺乳动物学
The study of mammals.
Mammology encompasses the study of all aspects of mammals, including their evolution, behavior, and ecology. 哺乳动物学包括对哺乳动物各个方面的研究，包括它们的进化、行为和生态。
She specialized in marine mammalogy. 她专门研究海洋哺乳动物学。
近义词:theriology

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek  θηρίον (thēríon, “wild animal”) + -λογία (-logía, “study of”)  ->  Modern Latin mammalogia
