deactivators:/diːˈæktɪveɪtərz/
|n.|灭活剂，去活化剂，钝化剂|Substances or factors that make something inactive or less active.  They reduce or eliminate the activity of a substance or process.|The enzyme deactivators were added to the solution.  酶灭活剂被添加到溶液中。|inhibitors, suppressants|activators|noun
