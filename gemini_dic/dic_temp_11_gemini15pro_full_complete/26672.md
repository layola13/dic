cacuminal:/ˌkækjuˈmaɪnəl/ /kəˈkʌmɪn(ə)l/| adj.|舌尖音的，卷舌音的|Of or relating to the tip of the tongue; specifically : articulated with the tip of the tongue turned up and back against the roof of the mouth (retroflex).|Example 1:  The "t" sound in some Indian languages is cacuminal.  一些印度语言中的“t”音是卷舌音。

Example 2:  Cacuminal consonants are common in Dravidian languages.  卷舌辅音在德拉维达语中很常见。|近义词：retroflex|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-cacuminal or apical)|词性:adjective
