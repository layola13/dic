actiniform:/ækˈtɪnɪfɔːrm/
adjective
放射形的，星形的
Resembling a ray; radiating from a center.
The coral had an actiniform structure. 这珊瑚有着放射形的结构。
近义词:radial, star-shaped, stellate
反义词:amorphous
