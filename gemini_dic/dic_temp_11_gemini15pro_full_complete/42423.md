countercuff:/ˈkaʊntərˌkʌf/| n.|反击；回击；反驳|An aggressive response to an attack, criticism, or other challenge.| He delivered a sharp countercuff to the journalist's probing question. 他对记者的尖锐提问做出了犀利的反驳。|Synonyms: retort, riposte, rejoinder|Antonyms: concession, agreement|noun

countercuff:/ˈkaʊntərˌkʌf/| v.|反击；回击；反驳|To respond aggressively to an attack, criticism, or other challenge.|The politician countercuffed the accusations with a strong denial.  这位政治家以强烈的否认来反击这些指控。|Synonyms: retaliate, strike back|Antonyms: yield, submit|verb
