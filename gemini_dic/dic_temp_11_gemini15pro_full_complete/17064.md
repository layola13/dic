bathybius:/bəˈθɪbiəs/
n.
原生质团，海床原生质
A supposed primordial protoplasmic substance believed to form the basis of life in the deep ocean.
The scientists once thought bathybius was the origin of life in the deep sea. 科学家曾认为原生质团是深海生命的起源。

近义词:protoplasm
反义词:None
词性:noun
