gallstone:/ˈɡɔːlˌstoʊn/ (US) /ˈɡɔːlˌstəʊn/ (UK)| n. |胆结石|A small, hard crystalline mass formed abnormally in the gallbladder or bile ducts from bile pigments, cholesterol, and calcium salts.|My grandmother had to have her gallbladder removed because of gallstones. 我的祖母因为胆结石不得不切除胆囊。|Synonym: biliary calculus|Antonym: N/A|Noun
