algidity:/ælˈdʒɪdɪti/
n.| 寒冷，冰冷，冷漠 |A state of coldness, especially of the skin; chilliness. Also, emotional coldness; frigidity. |The algidity of her stare sent shivers down his spine. 她的眼神冰冷，让他脊背发凉。| 近义词：chilliness, coldness, frigidity, iciness | 反义词：warmth, heat, passion


Etymology->
From French *algidité*, from Latin *algidus* ("cold"), from *algeo* ("to be cold"). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂elg- ("cold, freeze").
