bhagat:/ˈbʌɡət/ | n. | 信徒，奉献者，虔诚的宗教人士 | A devout follower or devotee, especially in Hinduism or Sikhism.  A person who has dedicated their life to religious pursuits. |例句：The bhagat spent his days in prayer and meditation. 这位奉献者每天都祈祷和冥想。

|例句：Many bhagats have contributed hymns to the Sikh scriptures. 许多奉献者为锡克教经文贡献了赞美诗。|近义词：devotee, disciple, follower, adherent |反义词: atheist, unbeliever, skeptic |词性:noun
