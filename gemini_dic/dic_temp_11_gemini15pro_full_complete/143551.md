outhaul: /ˈaʊtˌhɔːl/ | n. |后拉索，外拉索| A rope used to control the tension and shape of the sail, particularly on a sailboat, by hauling the clew of the sail away from the mast. |The outhaul was tightened to flatten the sail for stronger winds. 后拉索被拉紧，使帆变平，以适应更大的风力。|近义词: downhaul |反义词: inhaul |词性: noun
