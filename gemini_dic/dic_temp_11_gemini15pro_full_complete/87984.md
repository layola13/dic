high-mindedness:/ˌhaɪˈmaɪndɪdnəs/ | n. |崇高；高尚 | The state or quality of being high-minded; having noble or elevated principles or ideals. |  Her high-mindedness prevented her from participating in the petty office squabbles. 她崇高的精神境界使她不屑于参与办公室里那些琐碎的争吵。 |  nobility, integrity, idealism | meanness, baseness | noun

high-minded:/ˌhaɪˈmaɪndɪd/ | adj. | 高尚的；高洁的 | Having or showing noble or elevated principles or ideals. | He was a high-minded man, always striving to do what was right. 他是一个高尚的人，总是努力做正确的事情。 | noble, virtuous, principled | base, mean, ignoble | adjective
