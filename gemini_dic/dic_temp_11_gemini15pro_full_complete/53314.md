disabler:/dɪˈseɪblər/ | n. |致残者，禁用者，使丧失能力的东西|Something that disables or renders something unusable. disabler:/dɪˈseɪblər/ | n. |障碍; 障碍物; 妨碍物|Something that creates a barrier or obstacle, hindering progress or access. |The lack of ramps is a disabler for wheelchair users.  缺少坡道对于轮椅使用者来说是一个障碍。|synonyms: obstacle, barrier, impediment | antonyms: aid, assistance| noun
