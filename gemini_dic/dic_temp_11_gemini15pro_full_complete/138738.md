numbingly:/ˈnʌmɪŋli/ | adv. | 麻木地；使失去感觉地 | In a way that causes you to be unable to feel anything in a particular part of your body, or unable to think clearly. | The cold air hit his face numbingly. 冷空气麻木地击打着他的脸。|  Synonyms: senselessly, dully | Antonyms: acutely, sharply | 词性: 副词

numbingly:/ˈnʌmɪŋli/ | adv. | 令人麻木地；极其乏味地 |  In a way that is so boring or predictable that you become unable to feel any emotion or think clearly. |  The lecture was numbingly boring.  这讲座无聊得令人麻木。 | Synonyms: tediously, monotonously | Antonyms: excitingly, stimulatingly | 词性: 副词
