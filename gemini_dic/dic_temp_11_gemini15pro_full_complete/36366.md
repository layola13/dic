coincidentally: /ˌkəʊɪnsɪˈdɛntəli/ | adv. | 巧合地，碰巧地 | happening by chance at the same time or in the same way; coincidentally, we both arrived at the same time.  | Example 1: Coincidentally, I ran into my old friend at the airport. 我在机场碰巧遇到了我的老朋友。

Example 2: Coincidentally, both of us were wearing the same dress. 巧合的是，我们俩都穿着同样的裙子。 | Synonyms: accidentally, by chance, fortuitously, surprisingly | Antonyms: deliberately, intentionally, purposely | 词性: 副词
