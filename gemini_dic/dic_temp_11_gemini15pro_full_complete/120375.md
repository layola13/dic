minioning:/ˈmɪnjənɪŋ/
n.
充当爪牙；奴才行为；卑躬屈膝
The act of behaving like a minion; subservient or fawning behavior.
His constant minioning to the boss was embarrassing to watch. 他不断地对老板卑躬屈膝，让人看不下去。
servility, fawning, sycophancy
independence, assertiveness
noun
