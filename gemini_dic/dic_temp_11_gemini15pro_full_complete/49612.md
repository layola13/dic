demi-monde:/ˈdɛmiˌmɔnd/ |n.| 半社交界；放荡的女人圈；交际花；|A social group or environment considered to be on the fringes of respectable society, often associated with luxury, extravagance, and loose morals.  Specifically refers to a class of women considered to be of doubtful morality and reputation, especially those who are kept by wealthy men.|She was ostracized from polite society and relegated to the demi-monde.|Synonyms: underworld, fast set,  high life (with negative connotations)|Antonyms: polite society, respectable society|noun

demi-monde:/ˈdɛmiˌmɔnd/ |adj.| 半社交界的；放荡的；交际花的；|Characteristic of or belonging to the demi-monde.|Her demi-monde lifestyle involved lavish parties and questionable companions.|Synonyms: louche, decadent, disreputable|Antonyms: respectable, proper, moral|adjective
