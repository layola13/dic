gooseberry:/ˈɡuːsbəri/ | n. |醋栗，醋栗树，令人讨厌的人|A small, round, edible berry with a thin, hairy skin, often green, yellowish-white, or reddish-purple when ripe.  Also refers to the shrub that produces these berries. Can also informally refer to an unwanted third person in a romantic situation.|例句：She made a delicious gooseberry pie. 她做了一个美味的醋栗派。

例句：He felt like a gooseberry sitting between the two lovers. 他坐在那对恋人中间感觉自己像个电灯泡。|近义词：berry, fruit (for the fruit itself); chaperone, third wheel (for the unwanted person)|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
