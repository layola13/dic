aristotelians:/ˌærɪstəˈtiːliənz/
n.
亚里士多德学派的人; 拥护亚里士多德哲学的人
Followers or advocates of the philosophy of Aristotle.
The Aristotelians believed in a geocentric universe.  亚里士多德学派的人相信地心说。
Many medieval scholars were Aristotelians. 许多中世纪的学者都是亚里士多德学派的。
followers, disciples, adherents
platonists, skeptics
noun
