boma:/ˈboʊmə/| n.|畜栏，围栏，临时营地|An enclosure, especially a livestock enclosure, or a temporary fortified camp.|The tourists stayed in a boma in the African savanna. 游客们住在非洲大草原上的一个临时营地里。|Example: The cattle were herded into the boma for the night. 牛群被赶进畜栏过夜。|Synonyms: enclosure, corral, kraal, compound|Antonyms: open field|noun
