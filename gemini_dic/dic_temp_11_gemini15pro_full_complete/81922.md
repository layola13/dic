grilles:/ɡrɪlz/| n.|格栅， решет条，烤架|A framework of metal or wooden bars, typically used as a screen or covering for a window, door, or fireplace.|The windows were protected by decorative grilles. 窗户装有装饰性 решет条。|Synonyms: grating, lattice, screen, mesh|Antonyms: opening, gap|noun

grilles:/ɡrɪlz/| v.|烧烤，烘烤|To cook food by direct heat, especially under a grill or over hot coals.|I grilled the steaks for five minutes on each side. 我把牛排两面各烤了五分钟。|Synonyms: broil, barbecue, chargrill|Antonyms: boil, stew, fry|verb
