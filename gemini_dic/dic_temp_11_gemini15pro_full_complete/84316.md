half-winged:/ˌhæfˈwɪŋd/ | adj. | 半翅的；半翅目的 | Having wings that are only partially hardened or membranous, typically referring to insects of the order Hemiptera. |  Many half-winged insects are agricultural pests. 许多半翅目昆虫是农业害虫。| The half-winged insect sucked the sap from the plant. 这只半翅昆虫吸食了植物的汁液。 |  hemelytrous | fully-winged | adjective
