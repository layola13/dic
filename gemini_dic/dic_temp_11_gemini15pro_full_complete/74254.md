foxiest:/ˈfɒksiɪst/ (UK) /ˈfɑːksiɪst/ (US)| adj.|最狡猾的，最诡诈的|Superlative form of "foxy," meaning most attractive in a sly, cunning, and seductive way.  It describes someone who is skilled at being clever and deceptive, often to achieve their own aims.|She had the foxiest smile I'd ever seen. 她有着我见过的最狡猾的笑容。|He's a foxy businessman, always one step ahead of the competition. 他是一位狡猾的商人，总是比竞争对手领先一步。|近义词：cunning, sly, wily, crafty, shrewd, artful|反义词：naive, ingenuous, guileless, artless|词性:形容词
