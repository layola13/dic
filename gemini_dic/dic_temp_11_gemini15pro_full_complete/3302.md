afflicting:/əˈflɪktɪŋ/
verb
折磨；使痛苦
Causing pain or suffering.
The drought is afflicting the entire region. 干旱正在折磨着整个地区。

近义词:distressing, troubling, tormenting, harrowing, agonizing
反义词:soothing, comforting, relieving, alleviating
词性:Verb (present participle of afflict)


afflict:/əˈflɪkt/
verb
折磨；使痛苦

近义词:trouble, bother, burden, distress, plague
反义词:comfort, soothe, relieve, heal
