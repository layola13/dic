crowner:/ˈkraʊnər/| n.|加冕者，加冕的人或物，完结者，（苏格兰及爱尔兰）验尸官|A person or thing that crowns, completes, or finishes something. In Scotland and Ireland, a crowner is a coroner.|The king's crowner placed the crown upon his head. 国王的加冕官将王冠戴在他的头上。|近义词：coroner, finisher, completer|反义词:|noun
