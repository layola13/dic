flutter:/ˈflʌtər/ (US) /ˈflʌtə/ (UK)| v. n.|颤动，飘动，振翅；紧张，激动；小赌注|A quick, irregular movement back and forth or up and down. Also, a state of nervous excitement or activity.|

例句：
The butterfly fluttered its wings.
蝴蝶拍打着翅膀。

The flag fluttered in the breeze.
旗帜在微风中飘扬。

近义词：flap, wave, quiver, tremble, flicker, waver
反义词:still, steady
词性:verb, noun
