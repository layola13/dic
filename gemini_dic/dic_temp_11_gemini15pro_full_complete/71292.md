fixers:/ˈfɪksərz/| n.|修理工；中间人；问题解决者；（尤指政治或商业中的）掮客|People who arrange things, often in a dishonest or illegal way, especially in politics or business.|The mayor relied on a team of fixers to smooth over any potential scandals. 市长依靠一个团队的中间人来掩盖任何潜在的丑闻。|Synonyms: arranger, facilitator, mediator, negotiator, operator, troubleshooter|Antonyms: obstructer, hinderer|noun

fixers:/ˈfɪksərz/| n.|固定器；定影剂|Something used to fix or secure something else; a chemical substance used in photography to make a picture permanent.|She used hairspray as a temporary fixative for her drawings. 她用发胶作为图画的临时定影剂。|Synonyms: fastener, adhesive, sealant, stabilizer|Antonyms: solvent, dissolver|noun
