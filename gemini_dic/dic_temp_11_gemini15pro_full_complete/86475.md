heidens:/ˈhaɪdənz/ | n. | 异教徒，非基督教徒 |  People who do not believe in Christianity or other major religions.  It often carries a negative connotation. |  The early Christians were persecuted by the heidens. 早期基督徒受到异教徒的迫害。 | Synonyms: pagans, infidels, unbelievers | Antonyms: believers, Christians, the faithful | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *heidin, from Old French *hain, from Frankish *haith, meaning "heath, uncultivated land." The word originally referred to people living in rural areas who had not yet been converted to Christianity.
