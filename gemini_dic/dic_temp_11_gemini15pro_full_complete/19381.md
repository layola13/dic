bic:/bɪk/ | n. | 比克(一次性圆珠笔品牌) |  A brand of inexpensive, disposable ballpoint pens. | He always uses a bic pen. 他总是用比克笔。 | synonyms: ballpoint pen, pen | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a brand name. Could consider "fountain pen" as a contrasting writing instrument) | noun

bic:/bɪk/ | v. (informal, chiefly US) |  用比克笔写; 偷窃 (俚语) | To write with a Bic pen; to steal (slang). | He bicced his name on the wall. 他用比克笔在墙上写下了他的名字。 | synonyms: write, scribble (for writing); steal, pilfer, nick (for stealing - slang) | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for writing with a specific pen. ETYMOLOGY->
From the shortened, phonetic spelling of the surname Bich, of Marcel Bich, co-founder of Société Bic.
