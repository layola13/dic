governorates:/ˈɡʌvərnərɪts/ /ˈɡʌvərnəreɪts/
|n.|省份；地方行政区；管辖区
|A governorate is a principal administrative division of a country, especially in Arab countries.  A governorate is ruled by a governor.|
|Egypt is divided into 27 governorates.|埃及被划分为27个省。
|The governorates vary considerably in size and population.|这些省份在面积和人口方面差异很大。
