forborne:/fɔːrˈbɔːrn/
verb
克制的，忍住的；放弃的
refrained from; abstained from.  Past participle of forbear.
The company has forborne from raising prices this year.  该公司今年一直忍住不涨价。
Synonyms: refrained, abstained, withheld, forgone
Antonyms: indulged, yielded, succumbed
词性: 动词 (past participle)
