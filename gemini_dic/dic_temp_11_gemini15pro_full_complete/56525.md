domiciliation:/ˌdɒmɪsɪˈleɪʃən/ /ˌdɑːmɪsɪˈleɪʃən/| n. |住所登记，户籍登记，公司注册|The act of establishing a legal residence or headquarters, especially for a company in a particular jurisdiction.  The process often involves registering with local authorities and fulfilling specific legal requirements.|The domiciliation of the company in Delaware offers certain tax advantages.  该公司在特拉华州的注册提供了一些税收优势。|Synonym: registration, establishment, incorporation|Antonym: deregistration, dissolution|noun
