manchurians:/mænˈtʃʊəriənz/
noun
满洲人; 满族菜肴
People from Manchuria, a historical region in Northeast Asia, or a dish of fried vegetables and meat in a spicy sauce, often falsely attributed to Manchurian cuisine.
The restaurant served delicious Manchurians. 这家餐厅供应美味的满洲菜。
Manchurians are known for their resilience. 满洲人以他们的坚韧而闻名。
synonyms:Manchu people (for people),  Manchurian dish (for food)
antonyms: N/A (for people);  No specific antonym for the dish.
