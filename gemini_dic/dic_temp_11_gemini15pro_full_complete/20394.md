bitchily: /ˈbɪtʃɪli/ | adv. | 刻薄地，恶毒地 | In a spiteful, malicious, or unpleasant way, like a bitch. | She commented bitchily on her coworker's outfit. 她刻薄地评论了她同事的着装。|  Example: He bitchily whispered insults about his ex-girlfriend. 他恶毒地低声辱骂他的前女友。 | Synonyms: nastily, spitefully, venomously, maliciously | Antonyms: kindly, sweetly, nicely | Word Type: Adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From bitch + -ily.  "Bitch" itself comes from Old English "bicce," meaning "female dog." The pejorative sense developed later.
