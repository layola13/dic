handymen:/ˈhændɪmɛn/| n.|杂工，修理工|A person, especially a man, who is skilled at doing small jobs around the house, such as repairs and minor renovations.|例句：We hired a handyman to fix the leaky faucet. 我们雇了一个杂工来修理漏水的水龙头。

例句：My grandfather was a skilled handyman who could fix anything. 我的祖父是一位技术娴熟的杂工，什么都能修。|近义词：repairman, handy person, fixer-upper, jack-of-all-trades|反义词:specialist|词性:noun
