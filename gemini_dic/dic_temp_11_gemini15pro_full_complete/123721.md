moonling: /ˈmuːnlɪŋ/ | n. | 月饼 | A rich, round pastry traditionally eaten during the Mid-Autumn Festival, especially in China.  It typically contains a sweet filling, such as lotus seed paste or red bean paste, and may also include salted duck egg yolks. |  Example 1: We ate moonlings while watching the full moon during the Mid-Autumn Festival.  我们中秋节赏月的时候吃了月饼。

Example 2: My grandmother makes the best moonlings. 我奶奶做的月饼最好吃。 | 近义词: moon cake | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
