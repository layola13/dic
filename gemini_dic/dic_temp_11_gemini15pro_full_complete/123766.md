moorland:/ˈmʊərlənd/ (UK) /ˈmɔːrlənd/ (US)| n. |荒野，旷野，高沼地|A type of wild, open, uncultivated upland, typically covered with heather, coarse grasses, and peat bogs.|例句：The hikers traversed the vast moorland.  徒步旅行者穿越了广阔的荒野。

例句：Sheep grazed peacefully on the moorland. 羊群在荒野上平静地吃草。

|近义词：heath, moor, upland, wasteland|反义词:meadow, grassland, farmland|词性:noun
