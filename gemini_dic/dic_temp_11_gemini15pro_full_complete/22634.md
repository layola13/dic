borable:/ˈbɔːrəbl/| adj.|无聊的，容易使人厌烦的|Capable of causing boredom; tedious.  Something that is borable is likely to make people feel bored.  It lacks interest or excitement. |The lecture was so borable that I almost fell asleep.|近义词：tedious, dull, monotonous, uninteresting|反义词：interesting, exciting, stimulating, engaging|词性:形容词
词源: From "bore" (to make weary with tediousness) + "-able" (suffix meaning "capable of being").
