enunciable:/ɪˈnʌnsiəbl/ | adj. | 可 enunciate 的；可清楚表达的；可 pronounce 的 |  Able to be enunciated; capable of being clearly expressed or pronounced. |  His argument, though complex, was enunciable and persuasive.  他的论点虽然复杂，但表达清晰且有说服力。| synonyms: articulable, pronounceable, expressible, utterable | antonyms: inarticulate, unpronounceable, inexpressible, unutterable | adjective


enunciate: /ɪˈnʌnʃieɪt/ | v. |  清晰地说；明确地表达 | To pronounce words or parts of words clearly. To express or state clearly. |  The speaker enunciated each syllable carefully. 演讲者仔细地念出每个音节。 | synonyms: articulate, pronounce, express, state, declare | antonyms: mumble, slur, mutter | verb
