martinetism: /ˈmɑːrtɪnɪzəm/ | n. | 马丁主义 | A form of Western esotericism founded by Louis-Claude de Saint-Martin (1743-1803) and based on the writings of Martinez de Pasqually. It emphasizes mystical Christianity, theurgy, and the idea of reintegration with the divine. | He was a devoted student of Martinetism and its complex rituals. 他是马丁主义及其复杂仪式的忠实信徒。| 近义词: mysticism, esotericism | 反义词: exotericism | 词性: noun
