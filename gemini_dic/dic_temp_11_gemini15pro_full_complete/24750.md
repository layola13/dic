broody:/ˈbruːdi/| adj.| 沉思的，阴郁的；(母鸡)孵蛋的| Inclined to sit on eggs; (of a hen) wanting to hatch eggs; appearing moody and preoccupied. |例句：The hen has gone broody. 母鸡开始孵蛋了。

例句：He's been very broody lately, I wonder what's bothering him. 他最近一直很阴郁，我想知道是什么困扰着他。|近义词：thoughtful, contemplative, moody, sullen, gloomy|反义词：cheerful, lighthearted, jovial|词性:形容词
词源: From brood (v.) + -y (adj. suffix).  Ultimately from Old English brōd.
