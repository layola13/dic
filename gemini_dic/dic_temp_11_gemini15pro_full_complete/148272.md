oversmooth:/ˌōvərˈsmuːð/
| adj. | 过平滑的，过度平滑的 | Excessively smooth, often to the point of losing important details or variations.  It implies that the smoothing process has gone too far, resulting in a loss of accuracy or desirable texture. | The oversmoothed image lost its crispness and detail.  过平滑的图像失去了清晰度和细节。 | 近义词: overrefined, excessively smooth | 反义词: rough, textured, detailed | 词性: adjective
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "smooth" (adjective meaning "having an even and regular surface").  The word is a combination of the prefix "over-" indicating excess and the adjective "smooth."
