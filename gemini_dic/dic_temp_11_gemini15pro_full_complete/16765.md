barytic:/bəˈrɪtɪk/
adjective
重晶石的; 含重晶石的
Containing or resembling baryta.  Relating to or containing barium, especially in a higher degree of oxidation than in barous compounds.
Barytic sandstones are common in the area.  该地区重晶石砂岩很常见。
The barytic meal coated his esophagus. 重晶石餐涂满了他的食道。
synonyms: barium-containing, baric
antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-barytic or barium-free)
词性: adjective
