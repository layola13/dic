nonloyalties:/ˌnɒnˈlɔɪəltiz/
n.
不忠;背叛行为
The act of being disloyal; betrayals or acts of unfaithfulness.
His nonloyalties to the company eventually led to his dismissal. 他对公司的不忠最终导致他被解雇。
disloyalty, treachery, infidelity, betrayal, treason
loyalty, faithfulness, fidelity, allegiance, devotion
noun
