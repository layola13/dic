decorativenesses:/ˈdɛkərətɪvnəsɪz/
noun
装饰性；装饰的性质
The quality of being decorative; the state of being ornamented or embellished.
Her paintings are known for their vibrant colors and sheer decorativenesses. 她的画作以其鲜艳的色彩和纯粹的装饰性而闻名。

The decorativenesses of the new building were praised by the critics.  这座新建筑的装饰性受到了评论家们的赞扬。

近义词:ornamentation, embellishment, adornment
反义词:plainness, simplicity, austerity
词性:noun
