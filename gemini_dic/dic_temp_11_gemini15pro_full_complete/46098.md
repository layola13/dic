例句：Cybersecurity is becoming increasingly important in the modern world. 网络安全在现代世界变得越来越重要。|近义词：cyberspace, virtual world|反义词:physical world, real world|词性:名词


词源:  "Cyber-" is a combining form derived from "cybernetics,"  which itself comes from the Greek  "κυβερνήτης" (kybernētēs), meaning "steersman" or "governor."  It gained prominence in the late 20th century to refer to things related to computer networks and virtual reality.
