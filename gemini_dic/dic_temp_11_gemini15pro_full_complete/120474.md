minsters:/ˈmɪnɪstərz/
n.
部长们；大臣们；牧师们
A group of ministers of state or religious ministers.
The prime minister and his minsters discussed the new policy. 首相和他的部长们讨论了新政策。
Several minsters attended the conference. 几位牧师出席了会议。
synonyms: secretaries, clergymen, pastors, priests
antonyms: laity, congregants
