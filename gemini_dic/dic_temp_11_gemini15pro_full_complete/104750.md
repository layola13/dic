kawaii: /kəˈwaɪiː/ | n. | 可爱，可爱的事物 | The quality of being kawaii; cuteness. | Example 1: The kawaii of the panda cubs drew large crowds to the zoo. 熊猫幼崽的可爱吸引了大批游客到动物园。

Example 2:  She has a fondness for all things kawaii. 她喜欢所有可爱的东西。 | Synonyms: cuteness, adorableness, charm | Antonyms: ugliness, hideousness, unpleasantness | Word Type: Noun
