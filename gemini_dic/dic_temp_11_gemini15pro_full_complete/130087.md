non-probability:/ˌnɒn prɒbəˈbɪləti/ | adj. | 非概率的 | Not based on probabilities or random sampling; not involving the mathematical study of probability.  Characterized by the absence of probability theory in sampling or selection. |  The researcher used a non-probability sampling method for the study.  研究人员在这项研究中使用了非概率抽样方法。 | similar: non-random, purposive, selective, convenience | antonym: probabilistic, random | adjective
