necropolis: /nəˈkrɒpəlɪs/ /nɪˈkrɑːpəlɪs/
noun
大墓地；墓葬群
A large, designed cemetery or burial place, especially of an ancient city.
The Valley of the Kings is a vast necropolis containing the tombs of many pharaohs.  帝王谷是一个巨大的墓地，里面有许多法老的陵墓。
The ancient necropolis was rediscovered by archaeologists in the 19th century.  这座古老的墓地于19世纪被考古学家重新发现。
近义词: cemetery, graveyard, burial ground
反义词:  None (The opposite concept would be something like "city of the living")
词性: noun
词源: From Ancient Greek nekropolis (νεκρόπολις), from nekros (νεκρός, “dead body”) + polis (πόλις, “city”).
