incarcerative:/ɪnˈkɑːrsəreɪtɪv/| adj.|监禁的，关押的|Relating to incarceration; tending to incarcerate.  Characterized by or involving imprisonment.|The report criticized the country's incarcerative policies, arguing that they disproportionately affect minority communities.  这份报告批评了该国的监禁政策，认为这些政策对少数族裔社区的影响尤为严重。
The government's approach to drug offenses has been criticized as overly incarcerative. 政府对毒品犯罪的处理方式被批评为过度监禁。|近义词：imprisoning, confining, custodial|反义词：liberating, freeing, releasing|词性：形容词

词源: From incarcerate + -ive.  Incarcerate comes from Latin incarceratus, past participle of incarcerare "to imprison," from in- "in" + carcer "prison."
