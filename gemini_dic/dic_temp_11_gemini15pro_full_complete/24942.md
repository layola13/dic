brunt:/brʌnt/| n.|冲击，主要力量， brunt of sth|The infantry bore the brunt of the attack. 步兵承受了攻击的主要力量。|近义词：impact, force, shock, burden, blow|反义词: protection, shelter|词性:名词
词源: From Old French *brunt, *bront 'shock, blow, attack', perhaps related to Old French bruire 'to roar' and Old Norse bruni 'fire'.
