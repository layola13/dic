communalization:/kəˌmjuːnəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 公有化，集体化，共有化 | The process of making something communal; the transfer of property or resources from private to communal ownership or control.  |  The communalization of farmland led to increased production in the short term, but long-term sustainability was questionable. 农田的集体化在短期内导致产量增加，但长期可持续性值得怀疑。 |  collectivization, socialization, pooling | privatization, individualization | noun
