nononerously:/ˌnɒnˈəʊnərəsli/
adverb
不繁重地，轻松地
without imposing a burden; easily.
The task was completed nononerously, thanks to the new equipment.  由于新设备的帮助，这项任务完成得很轻松。
He managed his responsibilities nononerously, always seeming to have time for leisure. 他轻松地管理着自己的职责，似乎总是有休闲的时间。
近义词:easily, effortlessly, lightly
反义词: arduously, laboriously, onerously
词性:adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + onerous + -ly.  Onerous comes from Latin *onerōsus*, from onus "burden".
