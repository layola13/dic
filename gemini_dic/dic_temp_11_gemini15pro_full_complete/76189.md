fusspots:/ˈfʌspɒts/ (British) /ˈfʌsspɑːts/ (American)| n.| 烦人精，挑剔的人|A person who is excessively concerned with minor details and prone to complaining or finding fault. |例句：My aunt is such a fusspot about her house, everything has to be perfectly in place. 我阿姨对她的房子太挑剔了，一切都必须摆放得井井有条。

例句：He's a real fusspot when it comes to food, he sends back dishes for the slightest imperfection. 他对食物很挑剔，只要有一点不完美就退菜。|近义词：nitpicker, perfectionist, stickler|反义词:easygoing person, tolerant person|词性:noun
