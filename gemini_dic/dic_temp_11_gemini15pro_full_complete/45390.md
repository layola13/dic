cupboard:/ˈkʌbərd/ (US), /ˈkʌbəd/ (UK)| n.|橱柜，碗柜|A cupboard is a piece of furniture with a door and typically shelves, used for storage.|例句：We keep the dishes in the cupboard. 我们把餐具放在橱柜里。|例句：She opened the cupboard and took out a glass. 她打开橱柜，拿出了一个玻璃杯。|近义词：cabinet, closet|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *cuppeborde*, from Old French *copebord*, from *cope* ("cup, bowl") + *bord* ("board, table").  Originally a table or sideboard for displaying cups.
