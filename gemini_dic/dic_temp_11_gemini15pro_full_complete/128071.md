nervier:/ˈnɜːrviər/ /ˈnɜːrviər/| adj.|更紧张的，更神经质的|More nervous; more easily agitated.  It's the comparative form of "nervous".|例句：He seemed nervier than usual before the big game.  他在大赛前似乎比平时更紧张。

例句：The longer we waited, the nervier she became. 我们等得越久，她就越紧张。|近义词：more anxious, more agitated, more apprehensive, edgier, jumpier|反义词：calmer, more relaxed, more composed|词性:形容词
