cyamelide:/saɪˈæməlaɪd/ | n. |三聚氰酸，氰尿酰胺|A white, odourless, tasteless, crystalline powder, C3H3N3O3, a polymer of cyanic acid. It is insoluble in water and is used in fertilizers and plastics. | Cyamelide is used as a slow-release nitrogen fertilizer. 三聚氰酸被用作缓释氮肥。| Cyamelide is a polymer of cyanic acid. 三聚氰酸是氰酸的聚合物。| synonym: isocyanuric acid | antonym: N/A | noun
