cunningman:/ˈkʌnɪŋmən/| n. |巫医；术士；江湖郎中|A man who pretends to have magical powers or skills, especially in healing or predicting the future. |例句：The villagers consulted the cunningman about their sick cattle. 村民们向巫医咨询他们生病的牛的情况。

例句：She went to a cunningman to have her fortune told. 她去找一个江湖郎中算命。|近义词：sorcerer, wizard, charlatan, quack|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "skeptic" or "doctor" in certain contexts) |词性: noun
