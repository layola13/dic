heartbeat:/ˈhɑːrtbiːt/ (US), /ˈhɑːtbiːt/ (UK)| n.|心跳；心搏；核心；中心；本质|A rhythmic contraction and expansion of the arteries with each beat of the heart.|I could feel the rapid heartbeat in my chest. 我能感觉到胸口快速的心跳。|Synonyms: pulse, throb, pulsation|Antonyms: stillness, quiet|noun
