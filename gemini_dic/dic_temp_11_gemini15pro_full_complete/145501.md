overcloud:/ˈoʊvərˌklaʊd/| n. | 云层；阴云 | A mass of clouds covering the sky. | The overcloud was so thick that the sun couldn't penetrate it. 云层太厚了，太阳无法穿透。 | Synonyms: cloud cover, cloud layer | Antonyms: clear sky | noun
