immiscibilities:/ˌɪmɪsɪˈbɪlɪtiz/
noun
不混溶性；不互溶性
The inability of two or more substances to mix together to form a homogeneous solution.  This is the plural form of "immiscibility."
Oil and water exhibit immiscibilities.  油和水表现出不混溶性。
The immiscibilities of the various polymers made the creation of a uniform blend challenging. 各种聚合物的不混溶性使得制造均匀的混合物具有挑战性。
Synonyms: incompatibility, non-miscibility
Antonyms: miscibility, compatibility
