clione:/ˈklaɪoʊniː/ /ˈkliːoʊniː/| n. |海天使，裸海蝶|A small, free-swimming marine gastropod mollusk with wing-like appendages, found in arctic and subarctic waters.  It is known for its translucent body and graceful movements.|例句：The clione, also known as the sea angel, is a fascinating creature. 海天使，也被称为裸海蝶，是一种迷人的生物。

例句：Clione populations are sensitive to changes in ocean temperature. 裸海蝶种群对海洋温度的变化很敏感。|近义词：sea angel, sea butterfly|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *Kleio* (Κλειώ), one of the nine Muses, specifically the Muse of history. The name likely alludes to the wing-like parapodia of the creature, which resemble the flowing robes often depicted in classical representations of the Muses.
