electrolytical:/ɪˌlɛktrəˈlɪtɪkəl/ | adj. | 电解的；电解质的 | Relating to or involving electrolysis; produced by electrolysis. | The electrolytic process is used to refine copper. 电解法用于精炼铜。| synonyms: electrochemical | antonyms: none | adjective
