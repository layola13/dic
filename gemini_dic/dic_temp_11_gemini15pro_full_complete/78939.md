girded:/ˈɡɜːrdɪd/ /ˈɡɝːdɪd/ | v. | 准备行动；束腰；包围 |  prepared for action; encircled or encompassed. |  He girded himself for the challenge ahead. 他准备迎接未来的挑战。 The knights girded their swords. 骑士们系紧了他们的剑。 | 近义词: prepared, braced, equipped, encircled, surrounded | 反义词: unprepared, unequipped, vulnerable | 词性: 动词 (past tense and past participle of "gird")

gird:/ɡɜːrd/ /ɡɝːd/ | v. | 束腰；包围；准备 | to encircle or bind with a belt or band; to prepare oneself for action. |  She girded herself with a rope. 她用绳子束住自己。  He girded himself for battle. 他准备战斗。 | 近义词: prepare, brace, equip, encircle, surround | 反义词:  unprepare, unequip | 词性: 动词
