capilliform:/kəˈpɪlɪfɔːrm/| adj.|毛状的，毛发的|Resembling a hair; hairlike.  Derived from the Latin "capillus" (hair) and "-formis" (shaped).|The plant has capilliform leaves.  这种植物有毛状的叶子。|The fungus produced long, capilliform structures.  这种真菌会长出细长的毛状结构。|近义词:filiform, threadlike, hairlike|反义词:thick, broad|词性:形容词
