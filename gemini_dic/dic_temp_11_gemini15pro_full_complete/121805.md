misreflect:/ˌmɪsrɪˈflekt/
verb
歪曲反映；错误地反映
to reflect inaccurately or incompletely; to give a false impression of
The curved mirror misreflected her image, making her look much thinner than she was. 扭曲的镜子歪曲了她的形象，使她看起来比实际瘦得多。
synonyms: distort, misrepresent, falsify
antonyms: reflect accurately, represent truthfully, portray honestly
词性: 动词
