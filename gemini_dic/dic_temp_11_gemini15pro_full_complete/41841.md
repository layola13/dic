corvorant: /ˈkɔːrvərənt/ (US) /ˈkɔːvərənt/ (UK)| n. |鸬鹚；贪婪的人|A large, dark-colored waterbird with a long neck, hooked bill, and webbed feet, known for its diving ability and often trained to catch fish.  Figuratively, a voracious or greedy person.|例句：The cormorant dived swiftly into the water. 鸬鹚迅速潜入水中。|例句：He's a real cormorant when it comes to making money.  谈到赚钱，他真是个贪婪的人。|近义词：shag, seabird, glutton|反义词: ascetic|词性: noun
