bugaboo:/ˈbʌɡəˌbuː/ (US), /ˈbʌɡəbuː/ (UK)| n. |妖怪；烦人的东西；令人担忧的事|Something that causes worry or annoyance; a persistent problem or source of irritation. |例句：
Taxes are a real bugaboo for small businesses.
对小企业来说，税收真是个令人头疼的问题。

例句：
He always lets his fear of public speaking become a bugaboo.
他总是让对公开演讲的恐惧成为他的心头之患。

近义词：problem, difficulty, worry, concern, hassle, nuisance, pest|反义词:blessing, advantage, benefit|词性:noun
