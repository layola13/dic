delayingly:/dɪˈleɪɪŋli/
adverb
延迟地，拖延地
in a way that causes delay; slowly
例句：He answered delayingly, as if considering each word. 他慢慢地回答，好像在斟酌每个字。

例句：She spoke delayingly, drawing out each syllable. 她说话拖延，每个音节都拉得很长。

近义词：slowly, tardily, hesitantly
反义词：quickly, promptly, immediately
词性：adverb
