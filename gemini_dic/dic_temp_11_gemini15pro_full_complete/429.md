ablatitious:/ˌæbleɪˈtɪʃəs/
adjective
清洗的，冲刷的，去除的
Relating to or involving ablativus, a grammatical case expressing separation, motion away from, or means or instrument.  This meaning is primarily used in the context of grammar.  Figuratively, it can refer to something having the power to cleanse or purify.
The ablatitious nature of the ritual bath symbolized a cleansing of sins.  仪式性的沐浴的清洗性质象征着罪孽的洗涤。
synonyms: cleansing, purifying, purging, abstergent
antonyms: contaminating, polluting, soiling
词性: adjective
