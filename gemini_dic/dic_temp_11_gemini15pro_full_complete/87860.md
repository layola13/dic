hideousnesses:/ˈhɪdiəsnəsɪz/
n.
极度丑陋；令人厌恶的事物
extreme ugliness; something that is extremely unpleasant or offensive

The hideousnesses of war are often overlooked.
战争的恐怖之处经常被忽视。

The painting depicted the hideousnesses of hell.
这幅画描绘了地狱的恐怖景象。

近义词: ugliness, monstrosity, ghastliness, frightfulness
反义词: beauty, loveliness, attractiveness


词性: 名词 (plural noun)
词源: From hideous + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing quality or state) ultimately from Middle English hidous, from Anglo-French hidous, hideus, of uncertain origin.
