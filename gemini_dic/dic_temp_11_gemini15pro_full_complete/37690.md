companionable:/kəmˈpæn.jən.ə.bəl/
adjective
友善的；好相处的
Friendly and pleasant to be with.
She's a very companionable person. 她是一个非常友善的人。
近义词：affable, amiable, congenial, gregarious, sociable
反义词：unfriendly, disagreeable, aloof, antisocial
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From late Middle English: from companion + -able.
