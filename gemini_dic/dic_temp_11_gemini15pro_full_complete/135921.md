nonpunctually:/ˌnɒnˈpʌŋktʃʊəli/ | adv. | 不准时地，不按时地 | Not at the arranged or correct time; late. | He arrived nonpunctually, disrupting the meeting. 他迟到了，打断了会议。| She nonpunctually submitted her assignment, missing the deadline. 她没有按时交作业，错过了截止日期。| 近义词：tardily, late | 反义词：punctually, promptly | 词性: 副词

词源:

non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin *non, from Old Latin *noenum, from ne "not" + oinom/unum "one." 表示“不”
punctual (adjective): From Late Latin punctualis "of a point," from Latin punctum "point." 最初指“在特定时间点”，后来演变成“准时”
-ly (suffix):  Forms adverbs from adjectives.  将形容词变为副词
