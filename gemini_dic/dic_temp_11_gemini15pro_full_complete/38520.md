concinnity:/kənˈsɪnɪti/
n.
优雅;和谐;简洁;精炼
The elegance and harmony of a literary work; neatness of literary style.
Her writing was marked by its concinnity, every word chosen with precision and grace. 她的写作以简洁著称，每个词都经过精确而优雅的选择。

The architect admired the concinnity of the building's design.  这位建筑师钦佩建筑设计的和谐统一。

近义词: elegance, harmony, neatness, precision, succinctness,  brevity
反义词:  disorder, disharmony, prolixity, verbosity, wordiness
词性: noun

Etymology: From Latin *concinnitās*, from concinnus (“neatly put together, elegant, skillfully made”), from com- (“together”) + cinnus (“a trifle, a bit”).
