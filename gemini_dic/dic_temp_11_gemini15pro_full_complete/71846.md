flench:/flɛntʃ/ | v. | 畏缩，退缩 | To shrink back or flinch, especially in fear or disgust.  | He flenched at the sight of the needle. 他看到针就畏缩了。 | 近义词: flinch, wince, recoil, shrink | 反义词:  advance, approach, face | 动词

flench:/flɛntʃ/ | n. | 畏缩，退缩（名词形式较少使用）| The act of shrinking back or flinching. |  He gave a slight flench when the doctor touched his wound. 当医生碰到他的伤口时，他轻微地畏缩了一下。 | 近义词: flinch, wince, recoil, shrinking | 反义词:  advance, approach | 名词
