harambee:/ˌhɑːrɑːmˈbeɪ/ (US), /ˌhɑːrəmˈbeɪ/ (UK)| n.|哈拉姆贝，齐心协力，共同劳动|A Swahili word meaning "pulling together" or "all pull together." It represents a spirit of community self-help and cooperation.  It is often used in East Africa, especially Kenya, to refer to projects undertaken by communities working together.|Example 1: The village built a new school through harambee. 村民们通过哈拉姆贝运动建了一所新学校。

Example 2: The harambee spirit helped them overcome the drought. 哈拉姆贝精神帮助他们克服了干旱。|近义词：cooperation, collaboration, teamwork, joint effort, collective action|反义词：individualism, selfishness, apathy|词性:名词
