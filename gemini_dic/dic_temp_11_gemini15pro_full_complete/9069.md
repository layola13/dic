antimethodicalnesses:/ˌæntiˌmɛθˈɒdɪkəlnɪsɪz/
|n.|非条理性，杂乱无章|The state of being antimethodical; lack of method or order.|His antimethodicalnesses resulted in a chaotic workspace and missed deadlines.|近义词: disorderliness, disorganization, chaos, messiness|反义词: orderliness, organization, tidiness, methodicalness|Noun

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek)
methodical (adjective): systematic, orderly (from Greek "methodos" meaning "way" or "pursuit")
-ness (suffix): forms nouns indicating a state or quality
