burglar:/ˈbɜːrɡlər/ | n. |窃贼，破门盗窃者|A person who commits burglary, illegally entering a building with intent to commit a crime, typically theft.| The burglar was caught trying to break into a house.  窃贼试图闯入一所房子时被抓住了。|Synonyms: thief, robber, housebreaker, intruder|Antonyms: police officer, security guard|noun


burglarize:/ˈbɜːrɡləraɪz/ | vt. |破门盗窃，行窃|To commit burglary; to break into and enter a building illegally with intent to commit a crime, typically theft.|  The store was burglarized last night. 这家商店昨晚被盗了。  |Synonyms: rob, burgle, break into|Antonyms: protect, secure|verb
