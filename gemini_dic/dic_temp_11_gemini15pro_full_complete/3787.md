aglow:/əˈɡloʊ/
adjective
发光的，通红的
glowing with warmth or excitement; brightly lit.
The embers were still aglow. 余烬还在发光。
近义词：glowing, luminous, radiant, incandescent
反义词：dull, dark, dim
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From a- + glow.  "A-" acts as an intensifier.  The word "glow" comes from the Old English "glōwan," meaning "to shine brightly."
