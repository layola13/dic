instantiate: /ɪnˈstænʃieɪt/ /ɪnˈstænʃiˌeɪt/ | vt. | 实例化，实现 | To represent an abstract concept by a concrete example; to create an instance of a class.  |  The programmer instantiated a new object of the 'Customer' class. 程序员实例化了一个新的“客户”类对象。

The company instantiated its commitment to sustainability by investing in renewable energy.  这家公司通过投资可再生能源实现了其对可持续发展的承诺。 | 近义词: create, materialize, embody, exemplify | 反义词: abstract, generalize | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From Late Latin *instantiare "to make present, appear," from Latin instans (genitive instant-is) "being at hand, urgent," present participle of instare "to stand near or upon, press upon, pursue, be insistent."
