kempt:/kɛmpt/
adjective
整洁的，干净的
Having hair and clothes that are neat and tidy.
例句：He always appeared perfectly kempt, even after a long day at work.  他即使工作了一整天，也总是显得衣冠楚楚。

例句：She kept her house meticulously kempt. 她把房子收拾得一丝不苟。
synonym:tidy, neat, well-groomed, trim
antonym:unkempt, messy, disheveled, scruffy
词性: adjective
