lawnd:/lɔːnd/ | n. | 草地，草坪 | An area of short, mown grass in a yard, garden, or park. |  The children were playing on the lawnd. 孩子们在草坪上玩耍。 | lawn, grass, turf | field, meadow | noun

Etymology: From Old French *launde (“clearing in a wood”), from Frankish *landa (“heath, clearing”). Related to Old English land.
