mournfully:/ˈmɔːnfəli/ | adv. | 悲伤地，悲哀地 | In a way that expresses sorrow or grief. | She looked at the photograph mournfully. 她悲伤地看着照片。| The dog whimpered mournfully when its owner left.  当它的主人离开时，狗悲伤地呜咽着。| sadly, sorrowfully, dolefully | happily, joyfully | adverb

Etymology:
From Middle English *mornfulli, equivalent to mournful +‎ -ly.  Mournful comes from  Middle English mornful, from Old English murnful (“mournful, sad, causing sorrow”), equivalent to mourn +‎ -ful.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *murnaną (“to mourn, grieve, worry”).
