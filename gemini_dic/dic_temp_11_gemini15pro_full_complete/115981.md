marsdenia:/mɑːzˈdiːniə/ | n. |马斯登藤| A genus of  flowering plants in the dogbane family, Apocynaceae, mostly found in tropical and subtropical regions.  Many species are vines.  | The marsdenia vine climbed up the trellis. 马斯登藤爬上了棚架。| Marsdenia floribunda is a popular ornamental plant.  马斯登藤花是一种受欢迎的观赏植物。| synonyms:  silkpod,  (no widely used common synonym for the genus as a whole) | antonyms:  N/A | noun
