frosting:/ˈfrɒstɪŋ/ (US) /ˈfrɒstɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|糖霜，糖衣|A sweet creamy mixture used to cover or fill cakes, cookies, etc.|例句：The cake was covered in a thick layer of chocolate frosting. 蛋糕上覆盖着一层厚厚的巧克力糖霜。

例句：She piped pink frosting onto the cupcakes. 她在纸杯蛋糕上挤了粉红色的糖霜。

近义词：icing, glaze|反义词: None|词性:noun


frosting:/ˈfrɒstɪŋ/ (US) /ˈfrɒstɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|结霜，冰冻|The process of becoming covered with frost.|例句：The frosting on the car windows made it difficult to see. 车窗上的霜使视线变得模糊。


例句：The early morning frosting had damaged the crops. 早晨的霜冻损坏了庄稼。



近义词：freezing|反义词: thawing, melting|词性:noun
