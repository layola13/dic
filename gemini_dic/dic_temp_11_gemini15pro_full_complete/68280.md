fal:/fɔːl/ | v. |  落下; 掉落; 下降 | To descend freely under the influence of gravity. |  Example: The rain began to fal heavily. 雨开始下大了。 | Synonyms: drop, descend, plummet, tumble | Antonyms: rise, ascend, climb | verb
