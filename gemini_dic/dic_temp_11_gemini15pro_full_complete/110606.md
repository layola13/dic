limoniad: /ˌlɪməˈneɪd/ | n. | 柠檬水 | A drink made with lemon juice, sugar, and water. |  例句：I like to drink ice-cold limoniad on a hot day. 我喜欢在大热天喝冰镇柠檬水。

例句：She made a pitcher of fresh limoniad for the picnic. 她为野餐准备了一壶新鲜的柠檬水。 | 近义词：lemonade, lemon squash | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French *limonade*, from *limon* (lemon) + *-ade* (suffix forming nouns denoting drinks). Ultimately from Arabic *laymūn* (lemon).
