The project began with great auspiciousness, marked by a traditional ceremony. 项目以盛大的开工仪式开始，预示着吉祥如意。|近义词：favorableness, propitiousness, fortunateness|反义词：inauspiciousness, unfavorableness, ominousness|词性:noun
