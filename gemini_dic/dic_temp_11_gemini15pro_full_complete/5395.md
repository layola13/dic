almagest:/ˈælmədʒɛst/| n.|至大论|A major astronomical work written by Ptolemy of Alexandria in the 2nd century AD. It summarizes the Hellenistic view of the universe and formed the basis of Western astronomy for over 1400 years.|例句：Ptolemy's Almagest was a cornerstone of astronomical knowledge for centuries. 托勒密的《至大论》几个世纪以来都是天文学知识的基石。

例句：The Almagest provided a comprehensive geocentric model of the universe.  《至大论》提供了一个全面的地心说宇宙模型。|近义词：compendium, treatise, summary|反义词: None|词性:noun
