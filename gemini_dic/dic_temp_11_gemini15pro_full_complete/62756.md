engorging:/ɪnˈɡɔːrdʒɪŋ/
| v. |充盈，饱胀；使充血|The mosquito was engorging itself with blood.  蚊子正在吸饱血。|The heavy rains engorged the river.  暴雨使河水暴涨。|近义词：swelling, distending, bloating, filling|反义词：shrinking, contracting, emptying, depleting|词性：动词

engorged:/ɪnˈɡɔːrdʒd/
| adj. |充盈的，饱胀的；充血的|Her breasts were engorged with milk.  她的乳房胀满了奶水。|The tick was engorged after feeding.  蜱虫吸饱血后胀了起来。|近义词：swollen, distended, bloated, full|反义词：shrunken, contracted, empty, depleted|词性：形容词

词源: From French *engorger*, from en- + gorge (“throat”).  Ultimately from Latin gurges (“whirlpool”).
