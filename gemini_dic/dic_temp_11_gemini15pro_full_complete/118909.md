metayage:/mɛˈteɪɪdʒ/ | n. |分成制，分成佃农制 |A system of land tenure in which the tenant pays a percentage of the produce as rent. | The farmer worked the land under a metayage system, sharing half of the harvest with the landowner.  分成佃农制度下，农民耕种土地，与地主分享一半的收成。| synonyms: sharecropping, crop sharing | antonyms: freehold, leasehold | noun
