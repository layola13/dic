craftsmen:/ˈkræftsmən/| n. |工匠；手艺人|A skilled worker who makes things by hand, especially in a traditional way. |例句：The craftsmen of Murano are famous for their glassmaking skills.  穆拉诺的工匠以其玻璃制作技艺而闻名。

例句：The furniture was made by skilled craftsmen. 这件家具是由技艺精湛的工匠制作的。

近义词：artisan, artist, maker, builder, smith|反义词:amateur, novice|词性:noun
