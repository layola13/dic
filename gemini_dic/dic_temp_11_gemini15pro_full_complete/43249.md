crackshot:/ˈkrækʃɒt/ (UK) /ˈkrækʃɑːt/ (US)| adj. n.|神枪手，射击高手|A person who is skilled in shooting, especially with a rifle.|He's a crackshot with a pistol. 他手枪打得非常准。|近义词：marksman, sharpshooter, dead shot|反义词：poor shot, unskilled shooter|词性:形容词, 名词

Etymology:
From "crack" (meaning a sharp, sudden noise, especially of a gunshot) + "shot" (the act of firing a gun). The combination suggests someone who makes quick, accurate shots.
