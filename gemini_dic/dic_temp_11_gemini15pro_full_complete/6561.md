(While "an's" itself doesn't form set phrases, here are examples of "an" plus possessive 's in phrases)

An elephant's trunk is powerful. = 大象的鼻子很强壮。
An owl's night vision is excellent. = 猫头鹰的夜视能力非常好。
An apple's skin can be red or green. = 苹果的皮可以是红色或绿色。
An hour's drive from here. = 从这里开车一小时的路程。
A day's work. = 一天的工作。


注意事项:

"An's" is extremely informal and rarely used in written English.  It primarily exists in dialects and very casual spoken language.  Its usage is generally considered substandard. It would be better to use the full form "an is" or "an has."
