agro-ecosystem:/ˈæɡroʊˈiːkoʊˌsɪstəm/ |n.|农业生态系统|A relatively artificial ecosystem managed for agricultural purposes, involving interactions between crops, livestock, other organisms, soil, water, and climate.|The farm is a carefully managed agro-ecosystem. 这个农场是一个精心管理的农业生态系统。|Synonyms: agroecosystem, agricultural ecosystem|Antonyms: natural ecosystem|noun
