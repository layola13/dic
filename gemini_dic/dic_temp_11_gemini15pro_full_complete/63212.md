enteralgia:/ɛntəˈrælʤə/
noun
肠痛
Pain in the intestines.
He was suffering from severe enteralgia after eating contaminated food. 他吃了被污染的食物后，腹部剧烈疼痛。
近义词: abdominal pain, stomach ache
反义词:  (No direct antonym for pain, could consider comfort, ease)
词性: n.


Etymology:
enter- (Greek) = intestine  + -algia (Greek) = pain
