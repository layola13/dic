ionosphericist:/aɪˈɑːnəˌsfɪrɪsɪst/ | n. | 电离层学家 | A scientist who studies the ionosphere, the layer of the Earth's atmosphere that contains a high concentration of ions and free electrons and is able to reflect radio waves. |  Example 1: The ionosphericist monitored the changes in the ionosphere during a solar flare. 电离层学家在太阳耀斑期间监测电离层的变化。

Example 2:  Ionosphericists are crucial in understanding how radio waves propagate. 电离层学家对于理解无线电波如何传播至关重要。 | Synonym:  upper atmosphere physicist, atmospheric scientist | Antonym: N/A | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From ionosphere + -ist.  "Ionosphere" comes from "iono-" (referring to ions) + "sphere" (referring to a layer). "-ist" is a suffix denoting a person who studies or practices a particular subject.
