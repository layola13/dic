maisonnettes:/ˌmeɪzəˈnɛts/ |n.|小型公寓；复式公寓|Small apartments, often on two levels, within a larger building.  These are typically smaller than a typical house but offer more space than a standard apartment.|例句：The development consists of twenty maisonnettes, ideal for young families.  这个开发项目包括二十套小型公寓，非常适合年轻家庭。

例句：The maisonnettes are more affordable than the detached houses in the area. 这些小型公寓比该地区的独立式住宅更实惠。|近义词：apartments, flats, duplexes, townhouses|反义词: houses, detached houses, mansions|词性:noun
