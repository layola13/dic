isogeothermal:/ˌaɪsoʊˌdʒiːəˈθɜːrməl/

isogeothermal:/ˌaɪsoʊˌdʒiːəˈθɜːrməl/
noun
等温线; 等地热线
A line on a map connecting points having the same temperature or the same geothermal gradient.
例句：The isogeothermals indicate regions of similar subterranean temperature. 等地热线指示了地下温度相似的区域。

例句：The geologists used isogeothermals to map the thermal structure of the Earth's crust. 地质学家使用等地热线来绘制地壳的热结构图。
近义词：isotherm
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
