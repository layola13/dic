immutabilities:/ɪˌmjuːtəˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. |不变性；不可变性|The state or quality of being immutable; unchangeableness.|The immutabilities of the laws of physics fascinated her. 她对物理定律的不变性着迷。|近义词：invariability, constancy, permanence|反义词：mutability, changeability|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From immutable + -ity.  Immutable comes from Latin *immutabilis, from in- "not" + mutabilis "changeable," from mutare "to change."
