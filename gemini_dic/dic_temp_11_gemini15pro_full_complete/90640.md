humanization:/ˌhjuːmənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 人性化，赋予人性 | The process of giving human qualities to something non-human, or the result of this process.  | The humanization of technology makes it more user-friendly. 科技的人性化使其更加用户友好。| 近义词：personification, anthropomorphism | 反义词：dehumanization | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From humanize + -ation.  Humanize comes from the Late Latin humanizāre,  from Latin hūmānus “human.”
