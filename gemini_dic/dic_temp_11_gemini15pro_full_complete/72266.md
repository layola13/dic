floss-silk:/ˈflɒsˌsɪlk/| n.|蚕丝，牙线丝|A soft, strong, lustrous fiber produced by silkworms and used to make thread and cloth.  Specifically, a very fine silk thread.|The dress was made of the finest floss-silk.|Synonym: silk, filament|Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a material, perhaps synthetic fiber)|noun
