consciousness-raising:/ˈkɑːnʃəsnəs ˈreɪzɪŋ/
noun
提高觉悟；觉醒；意识提升
The act of increasing people's awareness of a particular social or political issue.
The feminist movement of the 1970s involved a lot of consciousness-raising about women's rights.  20世纪70年代的女权运动中有很多关于妇女权利的意识提升活动。
The group holds regular consciousness-raising sessions on environmental issues.  该小组定期举办关于环境问题的意识提升会议。
synonyms: awareness-raising, education, enlightenment
antonyms: suppression, ignorance, concealment
