bibliolatrist:/ˌbɪbliˈɒlətrɪst/
n.
偶像崇拜圣经者; 认为圣经绝对正确的人
A person who practices bibliolatry, which is the excessive reverence or worship of the Bible.
He was accused of being a bibliolatrist, unwilling to consider any interpretation other than the literal one. 他被指责是圣经崇拜者，不愿考虑除字面解释以外的任何解释。

近义词:fundamentalist, literalist, dogmatist
反义词:freethinker, skeptic, agnostic
词性:noun
