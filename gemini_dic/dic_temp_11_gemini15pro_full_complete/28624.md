caretakings:/ˈkɛərˌteɪkɪŋz/
n.
照料；照顾；护理
The daily caretakings of a large family can be overwhelming.
一个大家庭的日常照料可能让人不知所措。
The caretakings required for a newborn are extensive.
新生儿所需的护理非常广泛。
Synonyms: care, tending, nursing, looking after, supervision
Antonyms: neglect, abandonment
