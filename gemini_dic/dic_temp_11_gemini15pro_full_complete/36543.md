collaged:/kəˈlɑːʒd/
|adj.|拼贴的，拼成的|Created by assembling various fragments or elements, typically of different materials, textures, or origins.|Her artwork was collaged from old photographs and newspaper clippings. 她的艺术作品是用旧照片和剪报拼贴而成的。|Synonyms: assembled, combined, composite|Antonyms: separated, individual, single|adjective


collaged:/kəˈlɑːʒd/
|v.|拼贴，拼合 (past participle of *collage*)|To create or decorate by assembling various fragments or elements, typically of different materials, textures, or origins.|The wall was collaged with posters and photos. 墙上贴满了海报和照片。|Synonyms: assemble, combine, piece together|Antonyms: separate, dismantle, take apart|verb
