disprepare:/ˌdɪsprɪˈpɛər/ |v. |使无准备，使措手不及|To make unprepared; to catch off guard or unawares. |The sudden storm disprepared the hikers for the treacherous conditions ahead. 突如其来的暴风雨使徒步旅行者对前方危险的状况毫无准备。

The army was disprepared for the enemy's surprise attack.  这支军队对敌人的突然袭击毫无准备。|近义词:unsettle, discomfit, disconcert|反义词:prepare, equip|词性:动词
