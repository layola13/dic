fulmar:/ˈfʊlmɑːr (UK), ˈfʊlmɑːr (US)| n.|管鼻鹱|A seabird related to the petrels, with a stiff, gliding flight.  It breeds on cliffs and feeds on fish, squid, and offal.|例句：The fulmar circled the fishing boat, hoping for scraps.  管鼻鹱绕着渔船盘旋，希望能吃到一些残羹剩饭。

例句：Fulmars are known for their ability to project foul-smelling oil at potential predators. 管鼻鹱以能够向潜在的捕食者喷射恶臭油脂而闻名。|近义词：petrel, shearwater|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific bird species)|词性:noun
