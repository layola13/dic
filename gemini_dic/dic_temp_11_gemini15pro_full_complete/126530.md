nabob:/ˈneɪbɒb/ (US) /ˈneɪbɑːb/| n. | 巨富，权贵，大人物 | A person, especially a European, who made a large fortune in India or another country of the East, especially during the period of British rule.  A very rich or powerful person. | He lived like a nabob, with a large house, servants, and expensive cars.  他过着像巨富一样的生活，拥有大房子、仆人和昂贵的汽车。

He's a real estate nabob in this town. 他是这个镇上的房地产大亨。 | 近义词：magnate, tycoon, plutocrat, millionaire, billionaire | 反义词：pauper, beggar | 词性: noun
