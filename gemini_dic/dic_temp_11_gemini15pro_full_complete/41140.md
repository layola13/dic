copublish:/koʊˈpʌblɪʃ/
|v.|共同出版，联合出版|To publish jointly with another publisher or person.|Example:  The book was copublished by two major publishing houses. 这本书由两家大型出版社联合出版。

Example: They decided to copublish a series of travel guides. 他们决定联合出版一系列旅游指南。
|近义词：jointly publish, co-issue|反义词:|词性:v.
