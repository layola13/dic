nodation:/noʊˈdeɪʃən/ | n. | 结节形成，小结形成 | The process of forming nodes or nodules, especially in plants or tissues. | Nodation is observed in the roots of leguminous plants.  根瘤菌在豆科植物的根部形成结节。| 近义词: nodulation | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "smoothing" or "dissolution" depending on context) | 词性: noun
