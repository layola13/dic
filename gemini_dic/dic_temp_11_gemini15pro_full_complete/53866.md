discontinuee:/ˌdɪskənˌtɪnjuˈiː/
n.
停产商品的顾客;终止服务的用户;中断者
A person who has ceased to subscribe to or use a service or product.
The company sent out a survey to its discontinuees to find out why they stopped using the service. 该公司向其停止服务的用户发送了一份调查问卷，以了解他们停止使用该服务的原因。

近义词：ex-customer, former client, past subscriber
反义词：customer, client, subscriber
词性：noun

词源:  From discontinue + -ee (suffix forming nouns denoting the person who is the object of the action of the verb).  Ultimately from Latin dis- (not, apart) + continuare (to continue).
