loofah:/ˈluːfə/ (US), /ˈluːfɑː/ (UK)| n.| 丝瓜，丝瓜络，丝瓜筋|A tropical climbing plant bearing long, cylindrical fruit with a fibrous interior. Also refers to the dried fibrous interior of the fruit, used as a sponge.|I use a loofah in the shower. 我在淋浴时使用丝瓜络。|Synonym: sponge, scrubber|Antonym: (No direct antonym)|noun
