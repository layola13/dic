fuca:/ˈfjuːkə/ | n. |岩藻，墨角藻属|A genus of brown algae, commonly known as rockweed or wrack.  They are found on rocky seacoasts and in salt marshes throughout the Northern Hemisphere.|Sea otters often wrap themselves in fuca to keep from drifting away while they sleep. 海獭经常用墨角藻把自己裹起来，以免睡觉时漂走。|近义词：rockweed, wrack|反义词: N/A|名词
