overconsumption:/ˌoʊvər kənˈsʌmpʃən/
n.
过度消费
The act of consuming more than is necessary or desirable.
Overconsumption of resources is a major environmental problem.  资源的过度消费是一个主要的环境问题。

Overconsumption of sugary drinks can lead to health problems.  含糖饮料的过度消费会导致健康问题。

近义词: overspending, extravagance, excess
反义词: underconsumption, moderation, conservation
词性: noun
