outslides:/ˈaʊtslaɪdz/
verb
超越滑行, 滑得比…远, 以滑行胜过
to slide further or faster than someone or something else.
The experienced skier outslid the beginner on the steep slope.  经验丰富的滑雪者在陡坡上滑得比初学者远。
outskied, outpaced, surpassed
-
v.
滑出, 向外滑
to slide out or away from something.
The drawer outslid smoothly. 抽屉平滑地滑了出来。
protrude, extend
