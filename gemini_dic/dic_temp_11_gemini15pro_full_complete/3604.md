age-set:/ˈeɪdʒ sɛt/| n.|年龄组，年龄集|A group of people of similar age who share a common identity, undergo initiation rites together, and often have specific social roles and responsibilities within their community. |The young men of the warrior age-set were responsible for protecting the village.  这个武士年龄组的年轻人负责保护村庄。

The age-set system is an important part of many traditional African societies.  年龄组制度是许多非洲传统社会的重要组成部分。|近义词：age group, peer group, cohort|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
