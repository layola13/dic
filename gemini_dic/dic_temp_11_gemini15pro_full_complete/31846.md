cheeping:/ˈtʃiːpɪŋ/| n. v.|啁啾声，吱吱声；发出啁啾声，发出吱吱声|a short, high-pitched sound made by a young bird or a mouse |The cheeping of the chicks woke me up early. 小鸡的叫声把我吵醒了。|近义词：chirping, peeping, tweeting|反义词: roaring, bellowing|词性: noun, verb (present participle)
Etymology: From Middle English *chepen, from Old English *cēpan, of imitative origin.
