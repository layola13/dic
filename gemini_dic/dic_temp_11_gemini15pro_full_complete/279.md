abecedarian:/ˌeɪˌbiːsiːˈdɛəriən/
adjective|字母顺序的，按字母表顺序排列的|arranged alphabetically; relating to the alphabet.|The children sang the alphabet song, a simple abecedarian tune. 孩子们唱着字母歌，一首简单的按字母顺序排列的曲子。|alphabetical, ordered|disordered, random
noun|按字母顺序排列的事物；识字课本|something arranged alphabetically; a person who is learning the alphabet.|The poem is an abecedarian, with each line starting with the next letter of the alphabet. 这首诗是按字母顺序排列的，每一行都以字母表的下一个字母开头。|primer, alphabet book| -
词性|adjective, noun
