cuboidal:/kjuːˈbɔɪdəl/
adjective
立方形的，骰状的
Shaped like a cube; roughly square or rectangular.
The cells were small and cuboidal. 这些细胞小而呈立方体形状。
近义词: cube-shaped, cubic, boxy
反义词: spherical, round, amorphous
词性: adjective

cuboidal:/kjuːˈbɔɪdəl/
noun
立方体
A cuboidal object. (Less common usage)
The crystals formed perfect cuboidals. 这些晶体形成了完美的立方体。（不太常见的用法）
近义词: cube, hexahedron
反义词: sphere, ball
词性: noun (less common)
