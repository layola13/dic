cheerlessly:/ˈtʃɪr.ləs.li/| adv.|无精打采地，阴郁地|Without cheer; in a gloomy or depressed manner.  Derived from "cheerless" + "-ly".  "Cheer" comes from Old French "chiere" meaning "face, countenance".|She walked cheerlessly through the rain. 她没精打采地在雨中走着。|He cheerlessly accepted the news of his failure. 他沮丧地接受了失败的消息。|近义词：gloomily, dejectedly, morosely, sullenly|反义词：cheerfully, happily, brightly, merrily|词性:副词
