barron:/ˈbærən/| n.|巴伦，巨头，大亨|(1) A powerful, wealthy, and influential businessman or industrialist; a magnate. (2) A leading figure in a particular industry or field.|例句：He's a media barron who owns several newspapers and television stations. 他是一位拥有几家报纸和电视台的媒体大亨。

例句：She became a shipping barron through hard work and shrewd investments. 她凭借努力工作和精明的投资成为了航运巨头。|近义词：magnate, tycoon, mogul|反义词:pauper, nobody|词性:noun
