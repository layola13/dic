non-relational:/ˌnɒn rɪˈleɪʃənəl/ | adj. |非关系型的|Not relational; not based on the relational model of database management.  Specifically refers to databases that do not use tables with rows and columns related by keys.|MongoDB is a popular non-relational database.  MongoDB是一个流行的非关系型数据库。|Synonyms: NoSQL, non-SQL|Antonyms: relational, SQL|Adjective

non-relational:/ˌnɒn rɪˈleɪʃənəl/ | adj. |非关系的|Not connected or involved in a relationship, especially a romantic or sexual one.|They have a non-relational friendship. 他们之间是纯粹的友谊，没有任何恋爱或性关系。|Synonyms: platonic, non-romantic|Antonyms: relational, romantic|Adjective
