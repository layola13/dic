drunkenhead:/ˈdrʌŋkənˌhɛd/ | n. | 醉鬼; 酒鬼 | A person who is habitually drunk or who behaves foolishly when drunk. | He was a drunkenhead who squandered all his money on alcohol. 他是个酒鬼，把所有的钱都花在了酒精上。| synonyms: drunkard, sot, inebriate | antonyms: teetotaler, abstainer | noun

drunkenhead:/ˈdrʌŋkənˌhɛd/ | adj. (informal, dated) | 醉醺醺的 |  Characterized by drunkenness; foolish from drink. |  He staggered into the room with a drunkenhead gait.  他醉醺醺地摇摇晃晃地走进房间。| synonyms: intoxicated, inebriated, tipsy | antonyms: sober | adjective
