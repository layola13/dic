cormus:/ˈkɔːrməs/| n. |球茎；块茎植物|The main body of a plant, comprising the root system and stem.  The plant as a whole, especially a vascular plant, as distinguished from the flower or inflorescence.|The cormus of this orchid is particularly robust.  这种兰花的球茎特别健壮。|Synonyms: plant body, shoot system|Antonyms:  N/A|noun
