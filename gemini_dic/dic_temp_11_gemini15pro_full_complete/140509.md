oleaceous:/ˌoʊliˈeɪʃəs/
adjective
油脂的；似橄榄的；木犀科的
of, relating to, or resembling the olive or the olive family
例句：The oleaceous scent filled the air. 空气中弥漫着橄榄油的气味。

例句：The oleaceous shrub produced small, fragrant flowers. 这种木犀科灌木开出芬芳的小花。
近义词：olive-like, oily
反义词：(No direct antonym, could be considered as "non-oleaceous")
词性:adjective
