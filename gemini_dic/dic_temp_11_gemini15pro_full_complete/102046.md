isolexic:/ˌaɪsəˈlɛksɪk/
adjective
同形异义的；同形词的
Having the same spelling but different meanings.
The words "bow" (to bend at the waist) and "bow" (the front of a ship) are isolexic. “bow”（鞠躬）和“bow”（船头）这两个词是同形异义词。
The words "tear" (a drop of liquid from the eye) and "tear" (to rip) are isolexic. “tear”（眼泪）和“tear”（撕裂）这两个词是同形异义词。
Near Synonyms: homographic, homonymous
Antonyms: heterographic, heteronymous
word class: Adjective
