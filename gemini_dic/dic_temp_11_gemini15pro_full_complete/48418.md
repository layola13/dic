deducer:/dɪˈd(j)uːsər/
n.
推理者，推断者
Someone who deduces something.
Sherlock Holmes is a famous deducer.  夏洛克·福尔摩斯是一位著名的推理者。
The detective was a skilled deducer.  这位侦探是一位熟练的推理者。
近义词:inferrer, reasoner, logician
反义词:guesser, speculator
