federalists:/ˈfɛdərəlɪsts/
n.
联邦主义者
Supporters of the United States Constitution and a strong central government.
The Federalists believed in a strong national government. 联邦主义者相信一个强大的中央政府。
The Federalists argued for the ratification of the Constitution. 联邦主义者主张批准宪法。
近义词:nationalists, unionists
反义词:anti-federalists, states' rights advocates
词性:noun


federalist:/ˈfɛdərəlɪst/
adj.
联邦制的；联邦主义者的
Relating to or supporting federalism.
A federalist system of government divides power between national and state levels. 联邦制政府将权力分配给国家和州两级。
The Federalist Papers advocated for a federalist form of government. 《联邦党人文集》倡导联邦制政府形式。
近义词:national, central
反义词:confederal, state
词性:adjective


词源:
From the Late Latin *foederālis (“of a treaty, league”), from Latin foedus (“treaty, covenant, league, pact”).
