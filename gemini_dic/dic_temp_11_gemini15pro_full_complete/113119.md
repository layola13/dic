lungfish:/ˈlʌŋfɪʃ/| n. |肺鱼|A fish that breathes with lungs as well as gills, enabling it to live in shallow water or on land for periods of time.|The lungfish can survive out of water for months. 肺鱼可以在没有水的情况下存活数月。|近义词:dipnoi|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider terrestrial animals)|noun


lungfish:/ˈlʌŋfɪʃ/| n. |肺鱼|A freshwater fish with elongated bodies and fleshy fins, known for its ability to breathe air using a modified swim bladder that functions as a lung.|Scientists are studying lungfish to understand the evolution of land-dwelling vertebrates. 科学家们正在研究肺鱼以了解陆生脊椎动物的进化。|近义词:dipnoi|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider marine fish)|noun
