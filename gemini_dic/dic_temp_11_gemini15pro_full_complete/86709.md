helmet-like:/ˈhɛlmɪtˌlaɪk/| adj.|像头盔的；头盔状的|Resembling a helmet in shape or function.  Something that covers or protects like a helmet.|The turtle's shell is helmet-like. 这只乌龟的壳像头盔一样。|The protective covering was helmet-like in appearance.  这个保护罩外观像头盔。| 近义词:dome-shaped,  shell-like|反义词:open, exposed|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "helmet" + "-like".  "Helmet" comes from the Old French word "heaume," ultimately derived from the Germanic word "helm." "-like" is a suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
