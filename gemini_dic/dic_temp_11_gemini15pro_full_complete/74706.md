freeloading:/ˈfriːˌloʊdɪŋ/| n. adj.|吃白食，揩油；免费使用；|The act of using something without paying for it, or getting something from someone else for free.|He was accused of freeloading off his friends. 他被指责揩朋友的油。|近义词：sponging, leeching, mooching|反义词:paying, contributing|词性:名词/形容词
freeload:/ˈfriːˌloʊd/| v. |吃白食，揩油；免费使用；|To use something without paying for it, or to get something from someone else for free.|He freeloaded his way through college, relying on his friends for food and accommodation. 他蹭吃蹭喝读完了大学，吃住都靠朋友。|近义词：sponge, leech, mooch|反义词:pay, contribute|词性:动词
