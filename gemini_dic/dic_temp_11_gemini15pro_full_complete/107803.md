lapse:/læps/ | n. | 失误，过失；流逝；终止；失效 |A temporary failure of concentration, memory, or judgment.  A period of time that passes. The ending or failure of something to continue.|
例句：His lapse in judgment cost him dearly. 他的判断失误让他付出了高昂的代价。
例句：There was a lapse of ten years between her two visits. 她两次访问之间相隔了十年。
近义词：error, mistake, slip, oversight, passage, expiration, expiry | 反义词：accuracy, continuation, renewal | 词性: 名词

lapse:/læps/ | v. | 失效；流逝；背离；陷入 |To end or cease to be valid. To pass gradually into a worse or less active state. To deviate from a previous standard or practice.|
例句：His concentration lapsed and he made a mistake. 他的注意力涣散，犯了一个错误。
例句：He lapsed into silence. 他陷入了沉默。
近义词：expire, end, decline, deteriorate, regress | 反义词：renew, continue, improve | 词性: 动词
