bookstores: /ˈbʊkˌstɔːrz/ (US), /ˈbʊkˌstɔːz/ (UK)
n.
书店（复数）
Shops where books are sold.
Many independent bookstores have closed due to competition from online retailers. 许多独立书店由于来自在线零售商的竞争而关闭。
Independent bookstores offer a unique browsing experience. 独立书店提供独特的浏览体验。

近义词: bookshops
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) online retailers, e-book platforms
词性: noun
