metastasized: /məˈtæstəsaɪzd/ | v. | 转移（尤指癌细胞）| (of a cancer) spread to other sites in the body. | The cancer had metastasized to his lungs. 癌症已经转移到他的肺部了。|  spread, disseminated, propagated | localized, contained | verb

metastasis: /məˈtæstəsɪs/ | n. | 转移（尤指癌细胞）| the development of secondary malignant growths at a distance from a primary site of cancer. |  The patient has bone metastasis. 病人有骨转移。| spread, dissemination, propagation | localization, containment | noun

metastatic: /ˌmɛtəˈstætɪk/ | adj. | 转移性的（尤指癌细胞）| relating to or affected by metastasis. |  Metastatic cancer is a serious condition. 转移性癌症是一种严重的疾病。|  spreading, disseminated, propagated | localized, contained | adjective
