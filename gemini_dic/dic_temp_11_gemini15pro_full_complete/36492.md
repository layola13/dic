coleopterous:/ˌkoʊliˈɒptərəs/ | adj. | 鞘翅目的；甲虫类的 | Belonging or pertaining to the Coleoptera, an order of insects that includes beetles, characterized by hard, shell-like front wings (elytra) that cover and protect the membranous hind wings used for flight. |  例句：Coleopterous insects, such as ladybugs and weevils, play important roles in various ecosystems. 鞘翅目昆虫，例如瓢虫和象鼻虫，在各种生态系统中扮演着重要的角色。| 近义词：beetlish | 反义词: N/A | 词性：形容词
