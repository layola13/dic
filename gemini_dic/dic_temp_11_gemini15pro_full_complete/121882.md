miss:/mɪs/| n. vt.|小姐，女士；未击中，未达到；想念，错过|A title used before the surname or full name of a girl or single woman. To fail to hit, reach, or get something. To feel sad about someone or something that you are no longer with.|例句：I miss my family when I'm away from home.  我离家时想念我的家人。|近义词：long for, yearn for (v.); loss (n.)|反义词:hit (v.); meet (v.)|词性:noun, verb

miss:/mɪs/| n. vt.|未击中；失败；错过|To fail to hit, reach, or get something; to fail to take advantage of an opportunity.|例句：He missed the target completely. 他完全错过了目标。|近义词:fail, overlook|反义词:hit, catch|词性:noun, verb
