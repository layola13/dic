deathtraps:/ˈdɛθˌtræps/| n.|死亡陷阱|Something extremely dangerous; a place, situation, or thing likely to cause death.|例句：That old car is a real deathtrap. 那辆旧车真是个死亡陷阱。

例句：The poorly maintained building was a deathtrap waiting to happen. 这栋维护不善的建筑是一个随时可能发生的死亡陷阱。

近义词：hazards, dangers, perils, risks|反义词:safe havens, sanctuaries|词性:noun
