nonimpacts:/ˈnɒnˌɪmpækt/
n.|无影响，非影响|The study found several nonimpacts on the environment.|这项研究发现对环境没有几个影响。|
nonimpacts:|Absence of impacts; lack of effects.|The new policy had several nonimpacts on the local economy. |这项新政策对当地经济没有几个影响。|
nonimpacts:|The state of not having any noticeable or significant effects.| Researchers were surprised by the nonimpacts of the intervention.|研究人员对干预措施没有产生影响感到惊讶。|
nonimpacts:| Situations or factors that do not cause any changes or influences.|The report highlighted the nonimpacts of the project on the community. |该报告强调了该项目对社区没有产生影响。|
nonimpacts:|Lack of consequences or repercussions.|Despite the initial concerns, the nonimpacts of the decision were evident.|尽管最初存在担忧，但该决定的无影响是显而易见的。|
近义词:null effects, no influence, lack of impact
反义词:impacts, effects, influences
词性:名词 (Noun)

词源:
"Non-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "not."
"Impact" comes from the Latin "impactus," the past participle of "impingere," meaning "to strike against," from "in-" (into) + "pangere" (to fix, fasten).
