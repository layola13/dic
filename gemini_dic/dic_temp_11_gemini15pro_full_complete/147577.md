overpersuasion:/ˌoʊvərˌpərˈsweɪʒən/
noun
过度劝说, 过度说服
The act of persuading someone excessively, often to the point of manipulation or causing them to feel pressured or uncomfortable.
He felt a sense of overpersuasion after the salesman spent two hours trying to convince him to buy the car.  他感到过度劝说，因为销售员花了两个小时试图说服他买车。
synonyms: excessive persuasion, undue influence, coercion, manipulation
antonyms: dissuasion, discouragement, underselling
