cannot:/ˈkænɒt/ (UK), /ˈkæˌnɑːt/ (US)| modal v. | 不能，不可能 | Expresses inability or impossibility. | I cannot go to the party tonight. 我今晚不能去参加派对了。|  Synonyms: can't, unable to, impossible | Antonyms: can, able to | auxiliary verb, modal verb

PHRASE->
cannot but + verb  不得不
I cannot but admire his courage. 我不得不佩服他的勇气。


cannot help + verb-ing  忍不住
I cannot help laughing. 我忍不住笑了。


cannot stand + noun/verb-ing  无法忍受
I cannot stand the noise. 我无法忍受噪音。


cannot wait to + verb  迫不及待地想
I cannot wait to see you. 我迫不及待地想见你。


cannot afford to + verb  负担不起
I cannot afford to buy a new car. 我买不起新车。
