lien:/liːn/ | n. |留置权；扣押权；优先受偿权| A right to keep possession of property belonging to another person until a debt owed by that person is discharged. | Example 1: The bank has a lien on the property until the mortgage is paid. 银行对该房产拥有留置权，直到抵押贷款还清为止。

Example 2:  He put a lien on the car until she paid for the repairs. 他对这辆车设置了留置权，直到她支付修理费为止。 |Synonyms: charge, security, claim| Antonyms: release, surrender| noun
