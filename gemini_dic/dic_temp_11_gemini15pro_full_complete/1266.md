accordioning:/əˈkɔːrdiənɪŋ/
verb
像手风琴一样折叠或伸展;  像手风琴的褶皱一样
Folding or expanding like an accordion; resembling the folds of an accordion.
The bus was so crowded that people were accordioning against each other. 公交车太挤了，人们像手风琴一样挤在一起。
The paper fan accordioned neatly in her hand.  折扇在她手中整齐地折叠起来。
近义词:folding, collapsing, compressing, creasing
反义词:expanding, stretching, unfolding
