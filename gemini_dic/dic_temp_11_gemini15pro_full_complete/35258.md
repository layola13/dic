clunking:/ˈklʌŋkɪŋ/| adj.|发出沉闷的撞击声的；笨重的；难看的|making a loud, heavy sound, like metal hitting metal; awkward or poorly designed; unattractive|The old car made a clunking noise as it went over the bumps. 这辆旧车经过颠簸路段时发出砰砰的响声。|近义词：banging, thumping, clunky|反义词:smooth, graceful|adjective

clunk:/klʌŋk/| n. v.|沉闷的撞击声；发出沉闷的撞击声|a dull, heavy sound; to make a dull, heavy sound|The pipe clunked as it fell to the floor. 管子掉在地板上发出砰的一声。  He clunked the box down on the table. 他把箱子砰的一声放在桌子上。|近义词：bang, thud, thump|反义词:tinkle, chime|noun, verb
