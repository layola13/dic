ferventnesses:/ˈfɜːrvəntnɪsɪz/
n.
热烈；热情；炽热
The quality or state of being fervent; ardor; intensity of feeling.
Her ferventnesses for social justice inspired many. 她对社会正义的热情激励了很多人。
近义词: ardor, zeal, passion, enthusiasm, intensity
反义词: apathy, indifference, coldness, detachment
词性: 名词 (plural)

fervent:/ˈfɜːrvənt/
adj.
热烈的；热情的；炽热的
Having or showing great warmth or intensity of spirit, feeling, enthusiasm, etc.; ardent.
He offered a fervent prayer for her safety. 他为她的安全献上热切的祈祷。
近义词: ardent, zealous, passionate, enthusiastic, intense
反义词: apathetic, indifferent, cold, detached
词性: 形容词
