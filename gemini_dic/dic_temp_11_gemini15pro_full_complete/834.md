abstract:/ˈæbstrækt/ /æbˈstrækt/| adj. n. vt.|抽象的，摘要，提取|Existing in thought or as an idea but not having a physical or concrete existence.  An idea or concept considered apart from any specific instance.  A summary of the contents of a book, article, or formal speech.|例句：The concept of justice is abstract.  正义的概念是抽象的。|近义词：theoretical, conceptual, ideal, summary, epitome|反义词：concrete, specific, tangible|词性:形容词

abstract:/ˈæbstrækt/ /æbˈstrækt/| n.|摘要，梗概|A summary of the contents of a book, article, or formal speech.|例句：He wrote an abstract of his dissertation. 他写了他的论文摘要。|近义词：summary, synopsis, précis|反义词：expansion, elaboration|词性:名词

abstract:/æbˈstrækt/ /əbˈstrækt/| vt.|提取，抽取|Remove or take away (something) from something else.|例句：The oil is abstracted from olives.  油是从橄榄中提取的。|近义词：extract, remove, separate|反义词：add, insert, combine|词性:动词
