habituated:/həˈbɪtʃueɪtɪd/
adjective
习惯的，适应的
Made psychologically or physically used (to something).
例句：The horses had become habituated to the sound of gunfire. 这些马已经习惯了枪声。

例句：She became habituated to his messy habits. 她已经习惯了他的邋遢习惯。

近义词：accustomed, used to, inured
反义词：unaccustomed, unused to, unfamiliar
词性：adjective

词源: From Latin *habituatus, past participle of habituare (“to accustom, frequent”), from habitus (“condition, character, appearance, dress, attire”).
