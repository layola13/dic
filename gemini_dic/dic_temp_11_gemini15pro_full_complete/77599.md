geekiness:/ˈɡiːkɪnɪs/ | n. | 怪咖气质；书呆子气；对科技的狂热 | The state or quality of being a geek, especially in terms of enthusiasm for technology, specific subjects, or niche interests. | His geekiness was evident in his vast collection of comic books. 他的书呆子气在他收藏的大量漫画书中显露无遗。 |  synonyms: nerdiness, dorkiness, technicality, obsessiveness | antonyms: worldliness, sophistication, coolness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From geek + -iness. Geek's etymology is uncertain, but possibly from Low German geck (fool, fop) or related to English gawk.
