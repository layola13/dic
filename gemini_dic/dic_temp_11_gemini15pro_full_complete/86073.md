hearken:/ˈhɑːrkən/ (US), /ˈhɑːkən/ (UK)| v. |倾听，留心，听从|To listen attentively; to pay attention to what is said.|例句：Hearken to my words and you will understand.  留心听我的话，你就会明白。

例句：He refused to hearken to his father's advice. 他拒绝听从父亲的建议。

近义词：listen, attend, heed|反义词:ignore, disregard|词性:动词
