fibrospongiae:/ˌfaɪbroʊˈspʌndʒiˌiː/ | n. | 纤维海绵纲 | A class of marine sponges characterized by a skeleton made of spongin fibers with or without siliceous spicules. |  These fibrospongiae are found in shallow tropical waters. 这些纤维海绵纲生物发现于热带浅水区。|  近义词: demosponges (more general term) | 反义词:  (No specific antonym; could use groups like Calcarea or Hexactinellida - other sponge classes) | Noun

fibrospongia:/ˌfaɪbroʊˈspʌndʒiə/ | n. | 纤维海绵 | A sponge belonging to the class Fibrospongiae. | This fibrospongia is particularly vibrant in color.  这种纤维海绵的颜色特别鲜艳。| 近义词: demosponge, sponge | 反义词:  (No specific antonym; could consider other specific sponge types from different classes) | Noun
