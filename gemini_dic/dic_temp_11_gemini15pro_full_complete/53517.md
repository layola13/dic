disaster:/dɪˈzæstər/ | n. |灾难，灾祸；彻底的失败|A sudden event, such as an accident or a natural catastrophe, that causes great damage or loss of life.|The earthquake was a disaster that destroyed the entire city. 地震是一场灾难，摧毁了整个城市。|近义词：calamity, catastrophe, tragedy|反义词:triumph, success|noun
