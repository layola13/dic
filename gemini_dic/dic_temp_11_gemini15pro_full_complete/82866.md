gullibility:/ˌɡʌləˈbɪləti/| n.|轻信，易受骗|the quality of being easily tricked or cheated|例句：His gullibility made him an easy target for con artists. 他的轻信使他很容易成为骗子的目标。

例句：The advertisement preyed on the gullibility of the elderly. 这则广告利用了老年人的轻信。|近义词：naivety, credulity, innocence|反义词: skepticism, cynicism, shrewdness|词性:noun

gullible:/ˈɡʌləbl/| adj.|易受骗的，轻信的|easily persuaded to believe something; credulous|例句：Gullible people are easily tricked. 轻信的人很容易被骗。

例句：He was gullible enough to believe the stranger's story. 他很容易就相信了那个陌生人的故事。|近义词：credulous, naive, trusting|反义词: skeptical, cynical, astute|词性:adjective
