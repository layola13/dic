a new lease on life:/ə nuː liːs ɒn laɪf/
|n.|新生，重获新生|A chance to start again or live more fully, as if beginning a new life.|After his surgery, he felt like he had a new lease on life. 手术后，他感觉自己重获新生。|近义词: a fresh start, a second chance, a new beginning|反义词: the end, death, decline|词性: 名词短语
