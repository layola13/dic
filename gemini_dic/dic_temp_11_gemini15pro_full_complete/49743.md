demographer:/dɪˈmɒɡrəfər/ (US) /dɪˈmɒɡrɑːfər/ (UK)| n. |人口统计学家|A demographer studies the size, structure, and distribution of populations and how these change over time due to births, deaths, migration, and aging.|Demographers predict a decline in the birth rate over the next decade. 人口统计学家预测未来十年出生率会下降。

|The demographer analyzed census data to understand population trends.  这位人口统计学家分析了人口普查数据以了解人口趋势。|Synonyms: population scientist, population analyst|Antonyms: N/A|Noun


demographist:/dɪˈmɒɡrəfɪst/ (US) /dɪˈmɒɡrɑːfɪst/ (UK)| n. |人口统计学家| A demographist (less common than demographer) is a specialist who studies demographics, which is the statistical study of human populations, especially with reference to size and density, distribution, and vital statistics.| The demographist presented her findings on the changing age demographics of the country. 人口统计学家介绍了她关于该国不断变化的年龄人口结构的研究结果。|The demographist predicted a population boom in the next five years. 人口统计学家预测未来五年人口将会激增。 |Synonyms: demographer, population scientist, population analyst|Antonyms: N/A|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *démographie*, from Ancient Greek *dēmos* (δῆμος, “people”) + *-graphía* (-γραφία, “-graphy”).
