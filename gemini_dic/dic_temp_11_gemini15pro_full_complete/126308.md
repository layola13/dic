myolemma:/ˈmaɪəˌlɛmə/| n. |肌膜|The plasma membrane of a muscle fiber (cell). It is the site of the neuromuscular junction.|The myolemma is essential for muscle contraction as it propagates the action potential.| 近义词: sarcolemma | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology:
myo- (Greek 'mys', muscle) + -lemma (Greek 'lemma', husk, sheath)
