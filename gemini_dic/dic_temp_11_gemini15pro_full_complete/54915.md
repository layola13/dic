disposed-to:/dɪˈspəʊzd tuː/ | adj. | 倾向于，有意于 | inclined or willing to do something | She was disposed to agree with him. 她倾向于同意他的观点。 |  He seemed disposed to help. 他似乎有意帮忙。| 近义词: inclined, willing, prone, liable | 反义词: disinclined, unwilling, averse | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From the past participle of *dispose* (meaning to arrange or incline), from Old French *disposer*, from Latin *disponere* ("to arrange," "to put in order"). The "-to" indicates the direction of the disposition.
