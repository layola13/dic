bivar:/ˈbaɪvɑːr/ | n. |双变量, 二元变量|A bivar is a statistical term referring to a variable that can take on two values.  It's often used in bivariate analysis, which examines the relationship between two variables.|The study analyzed the bivar of gender and income. 这项研究分析了性别和收入这两个变量。|近义词:dichotomous variable, binary variable|反义词:multivariate variable|词性:名词
