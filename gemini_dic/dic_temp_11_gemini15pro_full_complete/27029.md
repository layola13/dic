caledon:/kəˈliːdən/ | adj. n. |喀里多尼亚的；喀里多尼亚人；喀里多尼亚森林|Relating to ancient Caledonia (roughly modern Scotland) or its people; a native or inhabitant of ancient Caledonia; the Caledonian Forest, a remnant of the ancient forest that once covered much of Scotland.|例句：The Caledon mountains are rugged and beautiful. 喀里多尼亚山脉崎岖而美丽。

例句：He was a proud Caledon, fiercely independent. 他是一位自豪的喀里多尼亚人，非常独立。|近义词：Scottish, Highland, Pictish|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider Lowland for geographical contrast)|词性: adjective, noun
