honeycombs:/ˈhʌnikəʊmz/ (UK) /ˈhʌnikomz/ (US)| n.|蜂窝，蜂巢; 蜂窝状结构|A structure of hexagonal cells, like those of a honeycomb, used by bees to store honey and eggs.  It can also refer to anything with a similar structure.|The beekeeper harvested honey from the honeycombs. 养蜂人从蜂窝中采集蜂蜜。

The engine cover has a honeycomb structure for strength and lightness.  发动机罩采用蜂窝状结构，兼具强度和轻便性。|近义词：cells, network, lattice, grid|反义词: solid|词性:名词
