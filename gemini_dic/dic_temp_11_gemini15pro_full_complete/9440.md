/ˌæntiˈrɪʃəneri/ | /ˌænˌtaɪrɪˈsɛʃəˌnɛri/ | adj. | 反衰退的，对抗经济衰退的 | Designed or intended to prevent or counteract a recession. | The government introduced an antirecessionary budget to stimulate the economy. 政府推出了反衰退预算以刺激经济。|  The central bank lowered interest rates as an antirecessionary measure. 中央银行降低利率作为一项反衰退措施。 |近义词：countercyclical, expansionary | 反义词：procyclical, contractionary | 词性: adjective

anti-/ænti/ + recession/rɪˈsɛʃən/ + -ary/-əri/

anti-: prefix meaning "against," "opposite of," "opposed to"
recession: noun meaning "a period of temporary economic decline during which trade and industrial activity are reduced, generally identified by a fall in GDP in two successive quarters."
-ary: suffix forming adjectives meaning "relating to," "connected with," "of the kind of."
