branding:/ˈbrændɪŋ/| n.|品牌推广，品牌塑造|The process involved in creating a unique name and image for a product in the consumers' mind, mainly through advertising campaigns with a consistent theme. 通过主要以一致的主题进行广告宣传活动，在消费者心目中为产品创造独特名称和形象的过程。|Example 1:  The company's rebranding was a huge success, attracting a new generation of customers. 该公司的品牌重塑非常成功，吸引了新一代的客户。

Example 2: Effective branding can build customer loyalty and increase sales.  有效的品牌推广可以建立客户忠诚度并提高销售额。|Synonyms: brand management, brand building, corporate identity|Antonyms: generic, unbranded|noun
