bridging:/ˈbrɪdʒɪŋ/
adj.
桥接的，连接的
Connecting two things; acting as a bridge.
The bridging course helped students transition to university-level studies.  桥接课程帮助学生过渡到大学水平的学习。
The company played a bridging role between the two warring factions.  该公司在两个交战派别之间发挥了桥梁作用。
