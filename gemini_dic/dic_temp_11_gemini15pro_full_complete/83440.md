habitants:/ɪnˈhæbɪtənts/
n.
居民，居住者
People or animals that live in a particular place.
The city has around two million inhabitants.  这座城市大约有两百万居民。
The island's inhabitants are mainly fishermen.  岛上的居民主要是渔民。
residents, dwellers, occupants
nomads, transients
noun
