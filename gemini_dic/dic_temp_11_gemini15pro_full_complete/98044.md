inovate:/ˈɪnəveɪt/| v. |创新，革新|To introduce something new; make changes in anything established.|例句：The company needs to innovate to stay competitive.  这家公司需要创新才能保持竞争力。

例句：They are constantly innovating new products and services. 他们不断地创新新的产品和服务。|近义词：pioneer, invent, create, modernize, revolutionize|反义词：stagnate, imitate, copy|词性:动词
