dipsetic:/dɪpˈsɛtɪk/| adj. n.|口渴的；解渴的；渴望的；嗜酒者|Having a thirst, causing thirst, or relating to thirst; A thirsty person, especially one with a craving for alcohol.|例句：After a long run, I felt dipsetic. 长跑之后，我感到口渴。|近义词：thirsty, parched, arid|反义词：quenched, satiated|词性:形容词

dipsetic:/dɪpˈsɛtɪk/| n.|嗜酒者；口渴的人|A person who habitually drinks alcoholic beverages to excess.|例句：He was a known dipsetic, frequenting the local pubs. 他是有名的嗜酒者，经常光顾当地的酒吧。|近义词：alcoholic, drunkard, tippler|反义词：teetotaler, abstainer|词性:名词
