diurnal: /daɪˈɜːrnl/ /daɪˈɜːrnl/
adjective|每日的; 白天的; 白天活动的|Occurring or active during the day.|例句：Diurnal animals are active during the day. 白天活动的动物在白天很活跃。

例句：The diurnal temperature range is significant in desert regions. 沙漠地区的昼夜温差很大。|近义词：daily, daytime|反义词：nocturnal|词性: adjective
