intemperate:/ɪnˈtɛmpərət/| adj.|放纵的，无节制的，(气候)酷烈的|Lacking moderation or self-restraint; immoderate.|His intemperate outburst shocked everyone. 他无节制的爆发震惊了所有人。|Synonyms: excessive, immoderate, unrestrained, indulgent, wild, extreme|Antonyms: moderate, temperate, restrained, abstemious, sober|adjective

intemperance:/ɪnˈtɛmpərəns/| n.|放纵，无节制，过度|Lack of moderation or self-restraint; intemperateness.|His intemperance led to health problems. 他的放纵导致了健康问题。|Synonyms: excess, immoderation, unrestraint, indulgence|Antonyms: moderation, temperance, restraint, abstinence|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *intemperant-* (not moderate), from *in-* (not) + *temperant-* (moderating).  The root *temper-* relates to mixing or blending in proper proportions, hence moderation.
