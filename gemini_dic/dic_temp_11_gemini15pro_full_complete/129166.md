no-win:/ˌnoʊ ˈwɪn/ | adj. | 无法取胜的；注定失败的；两败俱伤的 | A no-win situation is one where whatever you do, the outcome will be negative or undesirable. |  It was a no-win situation for her; if she stayed, she would be unhappy, but if she left, she would risk losing everything. 她处于一个左右为难的境地；如果她留下，她会不开心，但如果她离开，她将面临失去一切的风险。 |  This put the president in a no-win situation. 这让总统陷入了两难的境地。 | hopeless, impossible, futile, unwinnable | advantageous, favorable, promising, win-win | adjective
