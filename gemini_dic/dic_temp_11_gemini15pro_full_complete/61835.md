emplastration:/ɪmˌplæˈstreɪʃən/
n.
膏药，贴膏剂
The act of applying a plaster or poultice.  The application of a plaster or poultice to a wound or sore.
The doctor recommended emplastration for the wound.  医生建议用膏药敷伤口。
近义词: poultice, plastering, dressing
反义词: (None,  lacks a direct opposite. Consider words relating to removal, like "undressing")
词性: noun
