bored: /bɔːrd/ (UK) /bɔːrd/ (US)| adj.|无聊的，厌烦的|Feeling weary because one is unoccupied or lacks interest in one's activity.|I'm so bored with this movie. 我对这部电影感到很无聊。|Near Synonyms: weary, tired, uninterested, listless, apathetic|Antonyms: interested, entertained, fascinated, engaged, stimulated
词性: 形容词
