mumbling:/ˈmʌmblɪŋ/ | n. | 喃喃自语，咕哝 | The act of speaking softly and unclearly, often so that the words are difficult to understand. | He was mumbling something about being late for work. 他咕哝着说上班迟到了。 | synonyms: muttering, murmuring | antonyms: articulating, enunciating | noun

mumble:/ˈmʌmbl/ | v. | 喃喃自语，咕哝 | To speak softly and unclearly, often so that the words are difficult to understand. | She mumbled an apology and quickly left the room.  她低声说了句道歉，然后迅速离开了房间。| synonyms: mutter, murmur | antonyms: articulate, enunciate | verb
