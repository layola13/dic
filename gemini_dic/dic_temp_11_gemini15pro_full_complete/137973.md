nonylene:/ˈnɒnaɪliːn/ /ˈnɑːnəˌliːn/| n. |壬烯|An alkene with the chemical formula C9H18.  There are several structural isomers of nonylene, depending on the location of the double bond within the carbon chain.|Nonylene is used in the production of plastics. 壬烯用于塑料生产。|Synonyms: propylene trimer, tripropylene|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a specific chemical compound)|noun
