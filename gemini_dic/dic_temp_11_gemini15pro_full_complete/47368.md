deactivates:/diːˈæktɪveɪts/
verb
使失效，使不活动
Makes something inactive or no longer functioning.
The security system deactivates automatically at midnight.  午夜时分，安全系统会自动停用。
The company deactivates employee accounts after they leave.  员工离职后，公司会停用他们的账户。
近义词:disable, neutralize, shut down
反义词:activate, enable, turn on
词性:v.

deactivate:/diːˈæktɪveɪt/
verb
使失效，使不活动
Makes something inactive or no longer functioning.

You can deactivate the alarm by entering the code. 你可以通过输入密码来关闭警报。

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (not, opposite of) + activate (to make active).  The word "activate" comes from Late Latin "activatus", past participle of "activare",  from Latin "actus" (act, action).
