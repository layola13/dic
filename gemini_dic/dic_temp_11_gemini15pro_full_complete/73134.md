forage:/ˈfɔːrɪdʒ/ (US) /ˈfɒrɪdʒ/ (UK)| n. v.| 饲料，草料; 觅食| food, especially for horses and cattle, or the act of searching for it. |The horses were foraging in the meadow. 马儿在草地上觅食。|近义词： fodder, feed, graze, rummage |反义词: fast, starve |词性: 名词，动词

forages:/ˈfɔːrɪdʒɪz/ (US) /ˈfɒrɪdʒɪz/ (UK)| v.| 觅食（第三人称单数）| searches for food or provisions. |He forages for mushrooms in the woods every autumn. 他每年秋天都会在树林里寻找蘑菇。|近义词： searches, hunts, scours |反义词: provides, supplies |词性: 动词
