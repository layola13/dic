anteact:/ˌæntiˈækt/| n.|先前发生的事件，预兆|An event that precedes another; a foreshadowing or presage. |The strange weather was seen as an anteact to the coming storm.  这奇怪的天气被视为即将到来的暴风雨的预兆。| The black cat crossing their path was considered an anteact of bad luck. 黑猫穿过他们的道路被认为是不祥之兆。 | synonym: precursor, foreshadowing, omen, harbinger | antonym: aftereffect, consequence, result | noun
