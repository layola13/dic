outromance:/ˌaʊtˈroʊmæns/
verb
胜过浪漫; 比…更浪漫
to surpass in romance; to be more romantic than
He outromanced all his rivals and won her heart.  他比所有竞争对手都更浪漫，赢得了她的芳心。
近义词: outdo, surpass, excel
反义词:  None (The concept of being "less romantic" is usually expressed with different phrasing, not a direct antonym.)
词性: verb
