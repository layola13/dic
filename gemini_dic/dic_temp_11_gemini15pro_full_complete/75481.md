frustratingly:/ˈfrʌstreɪtɪŋli/
adverb
令人沮丧地
in a frustrating manner; in a way that causes feelings of annoyance or discouragement
The website was frustratingly slow.  这个网站慢得令人沮丧。
It's frustratingly difficult to get tickets.  令人沮丧的是，很难买到票。
Synonyms: annoyingly, irritatingly, exasperatingly
Antonyms: pleasingly, satisfyingly, gratifyingly
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From frustrate (v.) + -ing + -ly.  Frustrate comes from Latin frustrare "to deceive, disappoint, render vain, frustrate," from frustra "in vain."
