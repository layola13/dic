flasket:/ˈflɑːskɪt/ |n.|小扁酒瓶；小烧瓶|A flasket is a small, flat bottle or flask, typically used for carrying liquor.|He took a swig from his silver flasket. 他从他的银制小扁酒瓶里喝了一大口。|Synonyms: flask, bottle, hip flask|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a physical object)|noun

词源：
