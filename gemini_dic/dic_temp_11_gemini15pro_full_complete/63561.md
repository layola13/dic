envie:/ˈɑːnvɪ/ (US), /ˈɒnvi/ (UK)| n.| 羡慕，嫉妒 |A feeling of discontent or covetousness with regard to another's advantages, possessions, qualities, or luck.| She felt a pang of envie when she saw her friend's new car. 她看到朋友的新车时感到一阵嫉妒。|Synonyms: jealousy, covetousness, envy |Antonyms: admiration, generosity| noun
