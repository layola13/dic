conditionals:/kənˈdɪʃənəlz/ | n. | 条件句；条件式 |  Clauses or statements that express a condition and its consequence. | If it rains tomorrow, we will stay home. 如果明天下雨，我们将待在家里。| synonyms: provisions, stipulations | antonyms: absolutes,  unconditionals | noun

conditionals:/kənˈdɪʃənəlz/ | adj. | 有条件的 | Depending on a condition. | The offer is conditional upon your acceptance by next week.  这个提议取决于你下周是否接受。| synonyms: dependent, contingent | antonyms: unconditional, absolute | adjective
