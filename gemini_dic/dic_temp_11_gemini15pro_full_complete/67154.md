explosion:/ɪkˈspləʊʒən/ (US) /ɪkˈsploʊʒən/ (UK)| n.|爆炸，爆发，激增|A sudden release of energy causing a rapid expansion of matter, often accompanied by a loud noise and the destruction of surrounding materials.  A sudden increase in amount or number.|The explosion destroyed the building. 爆炸摧毁了这栋楼。|
|近义词：blast, detonation, eruption, outburst, surge|反义词：implosion, collapse|词性: 名词
