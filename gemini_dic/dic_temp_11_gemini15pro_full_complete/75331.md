frosted:/ˈfrɒstɪd/ (UK), /ˈfrɔːstɪd/ (US)| adj.| 磨砂的，结霜的，(头发)灰白的|Covered with or resembling frost; having a matte or textured surface. Also used to describe hair that has turned grey or white.|例句：I love frosted glass windows. 我喜欢磨砂玻璃窗。|例句：She had frosted tips in her hair. 她的头发有灰白色的发梢。|近义词：icy, frosted, glazed, rimy, sugared, snowy|反义词：clear, transparent, melted|词性:形容词

frosted:/ˈfrɒstɪd/ (UK), /ˈfrɔːstɪd/ (US)| v.| (使)结霜，(使)蒙上白霜|(past tense and past participle of "frost") To cover with or as if with frost.|例句：The windows were frosted over this morning. 今早窗户上结霜了。|例句：She frosted the cake with white icing. 她用白色糖霜给蛋糕覆上糖衣。|近义词：ice over, freeze, chill|反义词：melt, thaw, defrost|词性:动词
