commissional: /kəˈmɪʃənəl/
adjective
佣金的，酬金的
Relating to or based on commission.  Paid as or by commission.
She receives a commissional payment for each sale. 她每销售一件商品就得到一笔佣金。
Synonyms: commission-based, on commission
Antonyms: salaried, fixed-pay
词性: adjective
