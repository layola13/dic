bestraught:/bɪˈstrɔːt/
adjective
发狂的，心烦意乱的
Distracted; deeply agitated.  Overcome with worry, grief, or mental conflict; extremely upset.
She was distraught after hearing the news of her husband's accident. 她听到丈夫出事的消息后心烦意乱。
He was utterly distraught when his dog went missing.  他的狗走丢后，他完全崩溃了。
近义词:distracted, frantic, agitated, distressed, upset, overwhelmed
反义词:calm, composed, serene, tranquil
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + straught, past participle of obsolete verb *stray, from Middle English streien "to scatter," from Old English strēgan "to strew, scatter," related to stregdan "to spread." Thus, originally, "distracted to the point of madness," from the image of one's thoughts being scattered.
