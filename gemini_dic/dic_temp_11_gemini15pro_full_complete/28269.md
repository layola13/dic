captivater:/ˈkæptɪveɪtər/| n.|迷住者，令人着迷的人或物|Someone or something that captivates. |The captivating performance held the audience spellbound.  这场迷人的表演让观众如痴如醉。|近义词：charmer, enchanter, fascinator|反义词：bore|词性:noun

captivate:/ˈkæptɪveɪt/| vt.|迷住，使着迷|To attract and hold the interest and attention of; charm.|Her beauty captivated everyone in the room. 她的美貌迷住了房间里的每一个人。|The magician's tricks captivated the children. 魔术师的戏法吸引了孩子们。|近义词：enchant, fascinate, charm, mesmerize, enthrall|反义词：bore, repel, repulse|词性:verb
