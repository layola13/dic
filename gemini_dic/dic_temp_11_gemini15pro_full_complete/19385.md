bicameral:/baɪˈkæmərəl/| adj.|两院制的，双院制的|Having two branches, chambers, or houses, as a legislative body.|The United States has a bicameral legislature, consisting of the Senate and the House of Representatives. 美国实行两院制立法机构，由参议院和众议院组成。|The UK Parliament is bicameral, comprising the House of Commons and the House of Lords. 英国议会是两院制的，由下议院和上议院组成。|bilateral, two-chambered|unicameral|adjective
