cageynesses:/ˈkeɪdʒinəsɪz/
n.
谨慎；狡猾；小心；羞怯
The quality of being cagey, characterized by reticence, evasiveness, or secrecy.
He answered my questions with his typical cageyness. 他以他典型的谨慎回答了我的问题。
近义词: reticence, evasiveness, secretiveness, guardedness, slyness, wariness, caution
反义词: openness, frankness, candor, artlessness
词性: 名词 (plural)
