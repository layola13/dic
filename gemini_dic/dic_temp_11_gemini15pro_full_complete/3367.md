affront:/əˈfrʌnt/ | n. vt.|冒犯，侮辱；公然蔑视|An action or remark that causes outrage or offense.|例句：His refusal to shake hands was a deliberate affront. 他拒绝握手是故意的侮辱。

例句：She took his silence as a personal affront. 她把他的沉默视为对个人的侮辱。|近义词：insult, offense, slight, indignity|反义词：compliment, praise|词性: 名词, 动词

affront:/əˈfrʌnt/ | vt.|冒犯，侮辱；面对；相遇|To offend the modesty or values of someone; to insult.|例句：I was affronted by his rude behavior. 我被他粗鲁的行为冒犯了。

例句：She felt deeply affronted by his accusations. 她对他的指控感到非常受辱。|近义词：insult, offend, provoke|反义词：respect, honor|词性: 动词
