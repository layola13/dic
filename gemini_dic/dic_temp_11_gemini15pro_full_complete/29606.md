categories:/ˈkætəɡɔːriz/ | n. | 类别，种类 |  A category is a group of things that share similar characteristics.  It is a way of classifying or organizing information.  The word derives from the Greek "katēgoria" meaning "predication; that which is predicated, assertion, accusation." |  We categorize books by genre. 我们按体裁对书籍进行分类。| synonyms: classifications, groups, types, sorts, genres | antonyms: individuals, specifics, particulars | noun

categories:/ˈkætəɡəraɪz/ | v. | 分类，归类 | To categorize means to place something into a category or group based on its characteristics. It comes from the noun "category" with the suffix "-ize," meaning "to make or cause to be." | The librarian categorized the new books. 图书管理员对新书进行了分类。 | synonyms: classify, group, sort, type, pigeonhole | antonyms: individualize, separate, distinguish | verb
