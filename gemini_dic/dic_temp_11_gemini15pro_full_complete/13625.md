Attila: /ˈætɪlə/ | n. | 阿提拉 | The name of a famous Hunnic leader who ravaged Europe in the 5th century.  Historically significant as a symbol of brutality and destruction. | Attila the Hun was feared throughout Europe.  阿提拉在整个欧洲都令人闻风丧胆。 |  近义词: conqueror, warlord | 反义词: pacifist | 词性: noun

Etymology: From the Gothic *Atta, meaning "father".
