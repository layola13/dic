lobelike:/ˈloʊblaɪk/| adj.|叶状的，像耳垂的|Resembling a lobe, typically in shape or form.  This refers to something that looks like a lobe, which is a rounded, projecting part of an organ, like the earlobe or a part of the brain.|The plant's leaves were lobelike, with rounded projections along the edges.  这种植物的叶子呈叶状，边缘有圆形的突起。|Synonyms: lobate, lobed|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, consider describing the opposite shape, e.g., pointed, serrated)|adjective
