expositive:/ɪkˈspɑːzətɪv/ /ɛkˈspɒzɪtɪv/
adjective
说明的，解释的，阐述的
Serving to expound or set forth; explanatory.
The professor gave an expositive lecture on the history of philosophy. 教授做了一场关于哲学史的解释性讲座。
The documentary provided an expositive account of the events leading up to the war. 这部纪录片对导致战争的事件进行了阐述。
synonyms: explanatory, illustrative, elucidative, interpretive
antonyms: obscure, confusing, ambiguous
adjective
