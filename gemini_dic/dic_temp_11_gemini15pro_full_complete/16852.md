basicities:/bəˈsɪsətiz/| n.|碱性；碱度；碱性度|The property of being a base, measured by the number of hydrogen ions a solution can accept.  The basicity of an acid refers to the number of hydrogen ions it can donate.|The soil's basicity was too high for most plants to thrive. 土壤的碱性太高，大多数植物无法生长。|近义词：alkalinity, base strength|反义词: acidity|词性:noun
