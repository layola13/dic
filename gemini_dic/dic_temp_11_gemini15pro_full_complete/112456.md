loudmouth:/ˈlaʊdmaʊθ/| n.|大声说话的人，爱吹牛的人|A person who talks too much, especially in a boastful or offensive way.  A loudmouth is often considered obnoxious and insensitive.|He's such a loudmouth, always bragging about his accomplishments. 他真是个爱吹牛的人，总是吹嘘自己的成就。|
|synonyms: braggart, boaster, show-off|antonyms: introvert, wallflower|noun|

loudmouth:/ˈlaʊdmaʊθ/| adj.|大声的，饶舌的|Speaking loudly and often offensively. Characterized by loud and excessive talking, often boastful or insensitive.|His loudmouth comments embarrassed everyone at the party.  他大声喧哗的评论让派对上的每个人都很尴尬。|synonyms: boisterous, clamorous, vociferous|antonyms: quiet, reserved, subdued|adjective|
