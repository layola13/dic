mimeses:/mɪˈmiːsiːz/
n.
模仿;模拟;拟态
The act of imitating or mimicking someone or something.
The comedian's mimeses of famous actors were hilarious.  这位喜剧演员对著名演员的模仿滑稽极了。

近义词: imitations, mimicry, impersonations
反义词: originality, authenticity
词性: 名词 (Noun)

词源: From Ancient Greek μίμησις (mímēsis, “imitation”).
