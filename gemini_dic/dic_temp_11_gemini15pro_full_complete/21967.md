bogus:/ˈboʊɡəs/| adj.|假的，伪造的，虚伪的|Something that is bogus is fake or false. It can refer to documents, money, claims, or even people who pretend to be something they are not.|The antique dealer tried to sell me a bogus painting.  古董商试图卖给我一幅赝品。|synonyms: fake, counterfeit, spurious, phony, sham, fraudulent|antonyms: genuine, authentic, real|adjective

boguses: /ˈboʊɡəsɪz/ | n. |假货；赝品|Plural form of "bogus," referring to multiple fake or counterfeit items.|The police confiscated a truckload of boguses, including fake designer handbags and watches. 警方没收了一卡车假货，包括假冒名牌手袋和手表。|synonyms: fakes, counterfeits, phonies |antonyms: originals, authentics |noun
