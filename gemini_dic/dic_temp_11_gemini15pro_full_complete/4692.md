alertly:/əˈlɜːrtli/ | adv. | 警觉地，警惕地 | In a watchful and attentive manner, ready to react quickly to any potential danger or change. | The guard dog watched alertly for any intruders.  看门狗警惕地注视着入侵者。| 近义词: vigilantly, watchfully, attentively | 反义词: inattentively, carelessly, absentmindedly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From "alert" + "-ly."  "Alert" comes from Italian "all'erta" meaning "on the watch," from "erta" meaning "watch," ultimately from Latin "erigere" meaning "to erect, raise."
