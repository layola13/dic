nonphilological:/ˌnɒnˌfɪləˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
非语文学的，不属于语文学的
Not pertaining to philology; not connected with the study of language and literature.
This research takes a nonphilological approach to the text, focusing on its social context rather than its linguistic features. 这项研究采用了非语文学的方法来研究文本，侧重于其社会背景而不是其语言特征。
His nonphilological background made it difficult for him to understand the nuances of the ancient language. 他非语文学的背景使他难以理解古代语言的细微差别。

synonym: nonlinguistic
antonym: philological
word type: adjective
