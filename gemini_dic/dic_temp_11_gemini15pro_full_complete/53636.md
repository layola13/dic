discernments:/dɪˈsɜːnmənts/
n.
洞察力，识别力，敏锐的判断力
The ability to judge well.  The quality of being able to grasp and comprehend what is obscure.  An act of perceiving or recognizing something.
She possesses remarkable critical discernments about art. 她对艺术有着非凡的批判性洞察力。
His lack of discernments led to a poor investment decision. 他缺乏洞察力导致了糟糕的投资决定。
近义词: insights, perceptions, judgments, acumen, perspicacity
反义词: ignorance, blindness, obtuseness, naiveté
词性: 名词 (Noun)

词源:  From Late Latin *discernimentum, from discernere “to separate, distinguish,” from dis- “apart” + cernere “to distinguish, separate, sift.”
