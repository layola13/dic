compatriotism:/kəmˈpeɪtriətɪzəm/
n.
爱国主义; 同胞爱;
Love for one's fellow countrymen or countrywomen; patriotism.  The feeling of sharing a common national background.
Compatriotism is a powerful force that unites people in times of crisis. 同胞爱是一种强大的力量，在危机时刻团结人民。
The soldiers' compatriotism was evident in their willingness to fight for their country. 士兵们对国家的热爱体现在他们愿意为国而战的意愿上。
近义词: patriotism, nationalism, national pride
反义词: xenophobia, hatred, animosity
词性: 名词
