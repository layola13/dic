authenticating:/ɔːˈθɛntɪkeɪtɪŋ/
|v.|验证；鉴定|The system is authenticating your identity.|系统正在验证你的身份。|
|例句：The software is authenticating the user's login credentials.  该软件正在验证用户的登录凭据。|
|例句：They are authenticating the painting's provenance. 他们正在鉴定这幅画的来源。|
|近义词：verifying, validating, confirming|
|反义词：falsifying, forging|
|词性：动词(present participle)|


authenticating:/ɔːˈθɛntɪkeɪtɪŋ/
|adj.|验证的；鉴定的|The authenticating process is complete.|验证过程已完成。|
|例句：The authenticating system uses fingerprints.  这个验证系统使用指纹。|
|例句：The museum is currently authenticating a newly discovered artifact.  博物馆目前正在鉴定一件新发现的文物。|
|近义词：verifying, validating, confirming|
|反义词：falsifying, forging|
|词性：形容词|



Etymology: From authenticate + -ing.  Authenticate comes from Late Latin authenticāre, from Latin authenticus (“genuine, trustworthy”) + -āre (suffix forming verbs).
