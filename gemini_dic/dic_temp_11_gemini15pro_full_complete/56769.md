ETYMOLOGY->
From Dutch *dopen "to dip, immerse," related to Old English *dyppan "to dip."  The meaning shifted from "dipped" to "treated with a substance" and later to "under the influence of drugs."

PHRASE->
doped up:  (adj.) under the influence of drugs; intoxicated.  Example: He was completely doped up. 他完全被麻醉了。
heavily doped: (adj.) containing a high concentration of dopants. Example: Heavily doped semiconductors are used in certain electronic devices.  重掺杂半导体用于某些电子设备。
doped semiconductor: (n.) a semiconductor material to which impurities have been added to alter its electrical properties. Example: Doped semiconductors are essential components in modern electronics. 掺杂半导体是现代电子产品的重要组成部分。
n-type doped:  (adj.) doped with impurities that contribute extra electrons. Example: Phosphorus is commonly used for n-type doping of silicon. 磷通常用于硅的n型掺杂。
p-type doped: (adj.) doped with impurities that create "holes" (electron deficiencies). Example: Boron is commonly used for p-type doping of silicon. 硼通常用于硅的p型掺杂。
