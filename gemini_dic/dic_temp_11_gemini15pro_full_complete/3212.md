affecting:/əˈfɛktɪŋ/
adjective
动人的，感人的
producing a strong emotion, especially sadness or pity
The photograph of the starving child was deeply affecting.  这张饥饿儿童的照片令人深感难过。
Synonyms: moving, touching, poignant, emotive
Antonyms: unmoving, unemotional, indifferent
词性: 形容词


affecting:/əˈfɛktɪŋ/
adjective
影响的，作用于...的
acting on or influencing someone or something
Factors affecting crop growth include rainfall and temperature. 影响作物生长的因素包括降雨和温度。
Synonyms: influencing, impacting, altering, modifying
Antonyms:  unaffecting, ineffective, ineffectual
词性: 形容词
