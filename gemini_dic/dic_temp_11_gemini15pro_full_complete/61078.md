elliptical:/ɪˈlɪptɪkəl/ (US), /ɪˈlɪptɪkl/ (UK)| adj.|椭圆的; 省略的|Having the shape of an ellipse or an oval.  Omitting words but still understandable.|The moon's orbit is elliptical. 月球的轨道是椭圆形的。|近义词：oval, oblong|反义词:circular, round|adjective

elliptical:/ɪˈlɪptɪkəl/ (US), /ɪˈlɪptɪkl/ (UK)| n.|椭圆机|An exercise machine that simulates walking or running without putting stress on the joints.|She uses the elliptical at the gym. 她在健身房使用椭圆机。|近义词：cross-trainer|反义词:treadmill|noun
