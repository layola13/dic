diver:/ˈdaɪvər/| n.|潜水员；跳水运动员；潜鸟|A person who dives into water, especially as a sport or occupation.  Someone who dives deep into the sea, usually with special equipment, to explore, work, or look for something.  A bird that dives into water to catch fish.|例句：The diver plunged into the turquoise water. 潜水员跳入碧绿的海水。

例句：The diver surfaced after exploring the shipwreck. 潜水员探索完沉船后浮出水面。|近义词：swimmer, frogman, submariner|反义词: non-swimmer|词性:noun
