engrailed:/ɪnˈɡreɪld/
adjective
雕刻的，锯齿状的
having a series of curved indentations in an edge or border; notched
The engrailed edge of the leaf was a unique characteristic of the plant.  这种植物叶子的锯齿状边缘是它独一无二的特征。
近义词:indented, notched, serrated
反义词:smooth, even
词性:adjective


engrailed:/ɪnˈɡreɪld/
heraldry (纹章学) adjective
(纹章上)内弯锯齿形的
(of an ordinary) bounded by a line formed of concave curves.
The shield was engrailed with a silver lion rampant. 盾牌上刻着内弯锯齿形轮廓的银色跃狮。
近义词:invected (反向锯齿形)
反义词: (No direct heraldic opposite, but plain or straight-edged would be a contrasting term)
词性:adjective
