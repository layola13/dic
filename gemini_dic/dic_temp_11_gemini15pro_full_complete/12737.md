asseveration:/əˌsɛvəˈreɪʃən/
n.
断言，肯定，声明
A positive statement or declaration, often without proof.
He made an asseveration that he was innocent. 他断言自己是无辜的。

近义词:affirmation, assertion, declaration, avowal
反义词:denial, rejection, refutation
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *asseveratio, from Latin asseverare "to assert earnestly," from ad- "to" + severus "serious, earnest."
