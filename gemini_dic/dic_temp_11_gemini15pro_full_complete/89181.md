homeostatically:/ˌhoʊmiːoʊˈstætɪkli/ | adv. |  体内平衡地 | In a way that maintains or restores homeostasis, a state of internal stability. | The body temperature is homeostatically regulated.  体温是由体内平衡机制调节的。|  The system works homeostatically to maintain a stable internal environment. 该系统通过体内平衡机制维持稳定的内部环境。 | 近义词: stably,  | 反义词: erratically,  | 词性: 副词

homeostasis:/ˌhoʊmiːoʊˈsteɪsɪs/ | n. | 体内平衡 | The tendency toward a relatively stable equilibrium between interdependent elements, especially as maintained by physiological processes. | The body maintains homeostasis through a complex system of feedback mechanisms. 身体通过复杂的反馈机制维持体内平衡。 |  Homeostasis is essential for the survival of living organisms. 体内平衡对于生物的生存至关重要。 | 近义词: equilibrium, stability | 反义词: imbalance, instability | 词性: 名词

static:/ˈstætɪk/ | adj. | 静态的;  静止的 |  Lacking in movement, action, or change. | The population remained static for a decade. 人口十年来保持不变。 | The picture showed a static image of the landscape. 这张照片展示了静态的风景图像。 | 近义词: still, stationary, unchanging | 反义词: dynamic, changing, mobile | 词性: 形容词


词源:  homeo- (similar) + -stasis (standing still)  来自希腊语，homoios（相似的）和stasis（站立，位置）。
