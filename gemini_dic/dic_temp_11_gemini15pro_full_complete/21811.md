bobtailing:/ˈbɒbˌteɪlɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbɒbˌteɪlɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |断尾，截短，缩短|The act of shortening something, especially the tail of an animal.  It can also refer to the practice of curtailing something abruptly, like a speech or a project.|例句：Bobtailing is illegal in many countries.  在许多国家，断尾是非法的。

例句：Due to budget cuts, they had to resort to bobtailing the project. 由于预算削减，他们不得不缩短项目。|近义词：curtailing, truncating, shortening, docking|反义词: lengthening, extending|词性:noun


bobtailed:/ˈbɒbˌteɪld/ (US) /ˈbɒbˌteɪld/ (UK)| adj. |断尾的，截短的|Having a short or shortened tail, or being abruptly curtailed.|例句：The bobtailed cat looked unusual.  这只断尾的猫看起来很不寻常。

例句：The bobtailed version of the film was disappointing.  电影的删节版令人失望。|近义词：short-tailed, truncated, curtailed|反义词: long-tailed, complete|词性:adjective
