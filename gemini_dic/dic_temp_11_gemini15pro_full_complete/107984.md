Lashley: /ˈlæʃli/ | n. | 拉什利; 卡尔·斯宾塞·拉什利 (Karl Spencer Lashley, 1890-1958, 美国心理学家和行为科学家) | Karl Spencer Lashley, an American psychologist and behaviorist known for his contributions to the study of learning and memory.  He is particularly remembered for his unsuccessful search for the engram, the physical trace of memory. | Lashley's experiments with rats suggested that memory is not localized to a specific area of the brain. | 近义词: N/A (人名通常没有近义词) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词
