lubrication:/ˌluːbrɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 润滑，润滑作用 |The act of lubricating something, or the state of being lubricated.  The application of a substance to reduce friction between moving parts.| Regular lubrication is essential for the smooth operation of machinery. 定期润滑对于机器的平稳运行至关重要。|Synonyms: oiling, greasing|Antonyms: friction, abrasion|noun
