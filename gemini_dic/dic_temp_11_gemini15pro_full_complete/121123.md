misdeclaring:/ˌmɪsdɪˈkleərɪŋ/
verb
错误地申报;不如实申报
The act of declaring something incorrectly, usually in an official context.
He was accused of misdeclaring his income on his tax return. 他被指控在纳税申报单上错误申报收入。
The company was fined for misdeclaring the value of its imports.  这家公司因错误申报进口商品的价值而被罚款。
Synonyms: misrepresenting, falsifying, misstating
Antonyms: declaring accurately, reporting truthfully
词性: 动词
