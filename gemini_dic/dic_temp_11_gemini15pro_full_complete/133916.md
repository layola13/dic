nonhabitation:/ˌnɒnhæbɪˈteɪʃn/
n.
无人居住；无人居住的状态
The state of not being inhabited; absence of inhabitants.
The prolonged nonhabitation of the island led to the deterioration of its infrastructure.  这座岛长期无人居住导致其基础设施恶化。
Near synonyms: desertion, emptiness, vacancy
Antonyms: habitation, occupancy, inhabitance
