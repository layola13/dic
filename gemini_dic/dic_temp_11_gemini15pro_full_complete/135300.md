nonpastorally:/ˌnɒnpɑːˈstɔːrəli/ | adv. | 非田园式地，非牧歌式地 |  In a way that is not characteristic of or related to idyllic rural life, lacking the simplicity and charm of the countryside. | The author described the urban setting nonpastorally, highlighting the grit and grime of the city. 作者非田园式地描述了城市环境，突出了城市的沙砾和污垢。| 近义词: unromantically, realistically, prosaically | 反义词: pastorally, idyllically, romantically | 词性: 副词

词源:  Formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to the adjective "pastoral," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "pastor" meaning "shepherd."
