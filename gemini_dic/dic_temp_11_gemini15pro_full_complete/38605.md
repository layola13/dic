concrete:/ˈkɒŋkriːt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋkriːt/ (US)| adj. n.|具体的；有形的；混凝土|Existing in a material or physical form; not abstract.  A building material made from a mixture of broken stone or gravel, sand, cement, and water, which can be spread or poured into moulds and forms a mass resembling stone on hardening.|
The bridge is made of concrete.  这座桥是用混凝土建造的。

He gave me a concrete example. 他给了我一个具体的例子。
