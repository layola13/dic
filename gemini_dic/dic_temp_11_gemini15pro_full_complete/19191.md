bewitch:/bɪˈwɪtʃ/| v. |使着迷，蛊惑|casts a spell on someone, often causing them to become enamored or obsessed.|The magician bewitched the audience with his tricks. 魔术师用他的戏法使观众着迷。|近义词：charm, captivate, enchant, fascinate|反义词：repel, disgust|词性:动词

词源:  From Middle English *biwicchen*, from Old English *bewiccian* (“to use sorcery, to enchant, to fascinate”), from *be-* + *wiccian* (“to practice witchcraft”), from *wicca* (“witch, wizard”).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *wikkō* (“witch”). Related to wicked.
