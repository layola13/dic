clarseach:/ˈklɑːrsəx/ /ˈklɑːrʃɑːx/| n. |爱尔兰竖琴|A traditional Irish harp. It is a wire-strung instrument, played by plucking the strings with the fingernails.|The musician played a beautiful melody on the clarseach. 音乐家在爱尔兰竖琴上演奏了一曲优美的旋律。|Synonym: Irish harp, Gaelic harp|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps a modern harp could be considered a contrasting instrument)|noun
