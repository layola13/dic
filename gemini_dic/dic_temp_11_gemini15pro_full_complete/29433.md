cataleptically:/ˌkætəˈlɛptɪkli/
adverb|僵硬地，像患僵直症一样|In a rigid, trance-like state characteristic of catalepsy. |He stood cataleptically, staring blankly ahead. 他僵硬地站着，茫然地盯着前方。|Synonym: rigidly, fixedly, motionlessly|Antonym: fluidly, flexibly|
cataleptically:/ˌkætəˈlɛptɪkli/
adverb|僵直地，木然地|Resembling or characteristic of catalepsy; a state of suspended animation and consciousness, often with rigid limbs.|She lay on the bed cataleptically, her eyes open but unseeing. 她僵直地躺在床上，眼睛睁着却什么也看不见。|Synonym: trance-like, stiffly, immovably|Antonym: responsively, animatedly|
