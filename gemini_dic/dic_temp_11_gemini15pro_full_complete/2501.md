administer-to:/ədˈmɪnɪstər tuː/| v. |给予；照顾；提供；执行；|To provide or apply something, especially something beneficial or necessary, to someone or something.  To attend to the needs of someone, especially someone ill or elderly. |The nurse administered to the patient's needs.  护士照顾病人的需要。  The priest administered to the dying man. 神父为垂死的人做临终祈祷。|近义词：tend to, care for, look after, minister to, see to|反义词: neglect, ignore|词性:动词

administer:/ədˈmɪnɪstər/| v. |管理；执行；给予；|To manage, control, or direct something.
