nonequability:/ˌnɒnɪˈkwɒbɪləti/
noun
不均匀性，不稳定性
Lack of uniformity or steadiness; variability.
The nonequability of the rainfall caused problems for the farmers.  降雨的不均匀性给农民带来了问题。
The nonequability of his temper made him difficult to work with. 他脾气的不稳定性让他很难共事。
近义词: variability, unevenness, instability, inconstancy
反义词: uniformity, steadiness, stability, constancy
词性: noun

词源:
From non- + equability.  Equability comes from Late Latin aequabilitas, from Latin aequabilis "equal, uniform," from aequare "to make equal," from aequus "even, level, equal."
