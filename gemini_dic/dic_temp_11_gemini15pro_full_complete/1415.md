accustomed-to:/əˈkʌstəmd tuː/
adjective
习惯于
used to; familiar with something
She is accustomed to a busy lifestyle. 她习惯了忙碌的生活方式。
He was accustomed to getting up early. 他习惯了早起。
近义词:used to, familiar with, habituated to
反义词:unaccustomed to, unfamiliar with, unused to
词性:adjective

词源:
From the past participle of *accustom*, from Middle English *acustumen*, from Old French *acoustumer* "to accustom, make familiar," from Vulgar Latin *accostumare*, from Latin *consuetudo* "custom, habit."
