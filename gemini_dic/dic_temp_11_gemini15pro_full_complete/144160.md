outshoulder:/ˌaʊtˈʃoʊldər/| v.| 超越，胜过，比…肩膀宽 |To surpass or excel, especially in a competitive situation; to have broader shoulders than.|  He outshouldered his rivals in the race for the presidency. 他在总统竞选中胜过了他的对手。| 近义词：outperform, surpass, excel, outstrip, outdo | 反义词: underperform, lose to, be defeated by | 词性: verb

outshoulder:/ˌaʊtˈʃoʊldər/| adj.| 肩膀比…宽的|Having broader shoulders than someone or something else.| His outshoulder frame made him look imposing. 他宽阔的肩膀使他看起来很威风。 | 近义词：broader-shouldered | 反义词: narrower-shouldered | 词性: adjective
