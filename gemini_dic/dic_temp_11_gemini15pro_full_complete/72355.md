flowerless:/ˈflaʊərlɪs/| adj.|无花的，不开花的|Without flowers.  Lacking blossoms.  Used to describe plants that reproduce by spores rather than seeds.|Ferns and mosses are flowerless plants. 蕨类植物和苔藓植物都是无花植物。|Examples:
Many flowerless plants thrive in damp environments. 许多无花植物在潮湿的环境中茁壮成长。
The prehistoric landscape was dominated by flowerless trees. 史前景观以无花树木为主。
|Synonyms: nonflowering, bloomless, spore-bearing|Antonyms: flowering, blooming, blossoming|adjective
