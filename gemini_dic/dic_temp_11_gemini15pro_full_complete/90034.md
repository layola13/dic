hospice:/ˈhɑːspɪs/ (US), /ˈhɒspɪs/ (UK)| n.|临终关怀医院，收容所|A home providing care for the sick, especially the terminally ill.|My grandfather spent his last days in a hospice. 我的祖父在临终关怀医院度过了他的最后几天。|Synonyms: palliative care unit, terminal care facility|Antonyms: hospital (general), clinic|noun

hospice:/ˈhɑːspɪs/ (US), /ˈhɒspɪs/ (UK)| n.|旅客招待所（尤指修道院）|A lodging for travellers, especially one run by a religious order.|In the Middle Ages, hospices offered shelter to pilgrims. 在中世纪，旅客招待所为朝圣者提供住所。|Synonyms: shelter, refuge, hostel|Antonyms: hotel (commercial)|noun
