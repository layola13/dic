overinstructive:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈstrʌktɪv/| adj.| 过于指导性的，过于教导的| Giving too much instruction; excessively didactic. | The textbook was so overinstructive that it stifled creativity. 这本教科书指导性太强，扼杀了创造力。

The overinstructive manager micromanaged every aspect of the project.  这位过于指导性的经理对项目的每个方面都进行了微观管理。|近义词：didactic, pedantic, patronizing|反义词：uninstructive, concise, succinct|词性:形容词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "instructive" (from "instruct," ultimately from Latin *instruere* "to build, arrange, prepare, educate").
