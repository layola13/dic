empathizer:/ˈɛmpəθaɪzər/ | n. | 共情者，移情者 | A person who has the ability to understand and share the feelings of another. |  例句：She's a natural empathizer, always able to put herself in other people's shoes. 她是一位天生的共情者，总是能够设身处地为他人着想。

例句：A good therapist needs to be a skilled empathizer.  一位优秀的治疗师需要是一位熟练的共情者。

近义词：sympathizer, compassionate person | 反义词：narcissist, sociopath | 词性: noun
