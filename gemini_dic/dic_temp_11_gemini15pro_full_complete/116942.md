mazedly:/ˈmeɪzɪdli/| adv.|茫然地，困惑地|In a confused and bewildered manner, like someone lost in a maze.  The state of being perplexed and disoriented.|He wandered mazedly through the crowded streets, unsure of where he was going. 他茫然地走在拥挤的街道上，不知道自己要去哪里。|Synonyms: bewilderedly, confusedly, dizzily, perplexedly|Antonyms: clearly, decisively, knowingly, purposefully|Adverb
