gentrified:/ˈdʒɛntrɪfaɪd/
adjective
中产阶级化的；仕绅化的
The process of renovating and improving a house or district so that it conforms to middle-class taste.  This often results in the displacement of lower-income residents.
Example 1: The gentrified neighborhood, once full of vibrant street art and affordable housing, is now dominated by upscale boutiques and coffee shops.  这个中产阶级化的社区，曾经充满了充满活力的街头艺术和经济适用房，现在到处都是高档精品店和咖啡馆。

Example 2: The old factory building has been gentrified into luxury lofts.  这栋旧厂房已被改造成了豪华阁楼。

近义词:upscaled, renovated, improved, bourgeoisified
反义词:run-down, dilapidated, impoverished, neglected
词性:adjective
