词源:  From intellectual + -ness.  The suffix "-ness" is used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.

注意事项:  The word "intellectualnesses" is grammatically correct, but it's incredibly rare and awkward.  Native speakers almost always use "intellect" or "intellectualism" instead.  Using "intellectualnesses" might make you sound pretentious or like you're trying too hard.
