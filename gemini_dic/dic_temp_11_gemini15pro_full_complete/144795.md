over-flowed:/ˌōvərˈflōd/| v.| 溢出，泛滥|The river over-flowed its banks after the heavy rain. 大雨过后，河水泛滥，漫过了河岸。|例句：The bathtub over-flowed and soaked the bathroom floor. 浴缸里的水溢了出来，把浴室地板都弄湿了。|近义词：inundated, flooded, swamped|反义词:drained, emptied|词性:动词

over-flowed:/ˌōvərˈflōd/| adj.| 溢出的，泛滥的|The over-flowed river caused significant damage to the surrounding area. 泛滥的河流对周围地区造成了重大损害。|例句：The over-flowed sink was a mess to clean up. 溢出来的水槽弄得一团糟，很难清理。|近义词：flooded, overflowing|反义词:empty, drained|词性:形容词
