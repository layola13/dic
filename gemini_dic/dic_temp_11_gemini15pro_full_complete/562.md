abortionist: /əˈbɔːrʃənɪst/
n.
堕胎者；施行堕胎手术的人
A person who performs abortions, especially illegally or unethically.
The abortionist was arrested and charged with multiple felonies.  这个堕胎者被逮捕并被指控犯有多项重罪。
The back-alley abortionist used unsanitary instruments.  这个暗巷堕胎者使用了不卫生的器械。
近义词: abortion provider
反义词: pro-lifer
