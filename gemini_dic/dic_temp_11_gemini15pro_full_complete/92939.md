identitarian:/aɪˌdɛntɪˈtɛəriən/
n.|认同主义者，身份认同主义者|A person who strongly believes in the importance of a particular identity, especially related to ethnicity, race, nationality, or culture, and often promotes the interests of people sharing that identity, sometimes in an exclusionary or discriminatory way.|The identitarian movement has gained traction in recent years. 认同主义运动近年来获得了关注。
|synonyms: nationalist, nativist, chauvinist|antonyms: cosmopolitan, internationalist|noun

identitarian:/aɪˌdɛntɪˈtɛəriən/
adj.|认同主义的，身份认同的|Relating to or characterized by identitarianism.|Identitarian politics can be divisive. 认同主义政治可能造成分裂。
|synonyms: nationalist, nativist, chauvinist|antonyms: cosmopolitan, internationalist|adjective
