apologetic: /əˌpɒləˈdʒɛtɪk/ (UK) /əˌpɑːləˈdʒɛtɪk/ (US)| adj.|道歉的，愧疚的，歉意的|Showing or expressing regret, especially for some offense, wrong, or failure.|例句：He was very apologetic about the mistake. 他对这个错误感到非常抱歉。|近义词：sorry, remorseful, repentant, contrite|反义词：unapologetic, unrepentant|词性：形容词

apologetic: /əˌpɒləˈdʒɛtɪk/ (UK) /əˌpɑːləˈdʒɛtɪk/ (US)| n.|辩护者，护教论者|(Rare) A person who offers an argument in defense of something controversial.|例句：He wrote an apologetic for Christianity. 他写了一篇基督教的辩护词。|近义词：defender, advocate|反义词：critic, opponent|词性：名词
