hesitantly:/ˈhezɪtəntli/| adv.|犹豫地，迟疑地|In a hesitant manner; with hesitation.  Showing or feeling hesitation.|She hesitantly agreed to go with him. 她犹豫地同意和他一起去。|近义词：tentatively, cautiously, reluctantly, uncertainly|反义词：confidently, decisively, resolutely|词性:副词
词源：From the adjective "hesitant," which comes from the Latin "haesitare" meaning "to stick fast, remain fixed, be undecided."
