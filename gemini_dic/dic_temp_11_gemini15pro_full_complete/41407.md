cornel:/ˈkɔːrnl/ (US), /ˈkɔːnəl/ (UK)| n.|山茱萸; 山茱萸树的果实; |A small deciduous tree with clusters of small yellowish flowers followed by bright red berries. The hard wood is used for making tools and the berries for preserves.|例句：The birds feasted on the ripe cornels in the autumn. 秋天，鸟儿尽情享用成熟的山茱萸果实。

例句：The cornel wood is known for its strength and durability. 山茱萸木以其坚固耐用而闻名。|近义词：dogwood|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
