familist:/ˈfæmɪlɪst/
n.|家庭主义者；把家庭利益放在首位的人|A person who puts the interests of their family above all else.
|He was a staunch familist, always prioritizing his family's needs. 他是一个坚定的家庭主义者，总是把家庭的需求放在首位。
|She was known as a familist, dedicating her life to her children and husband. 她以家庭主义者而闻名，一生都奉献给了她的孩子和丈夫。
|近义词:family-oriented person, kinsman|反义词:individualist|词性:名词
