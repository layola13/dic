linear:/ˈlɪniər/| adj.|线性的，直线的|Relating to or resembling a line; involving measurement in one dimension only.|The graph shows a linear relationship between temperature and pressure.|近义词：straight, direct|反义词：nonlinear, curved|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Late Latin *linearis*, from Latin *linea* "line."
