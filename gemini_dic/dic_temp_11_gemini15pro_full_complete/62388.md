endocrine:/ˈɛndəkrɪn/ /ˈɛndəkraɪn/ | adj. n.| 内分泌的；内分泌腺 |Relating to or denoting glands which secrete hormones or other products directly into the blood.|The endocrine system plays a vital role in regulating growth and development. 内分泌系统在调节生长发育中起着至关重要的作用。|The doctor referred her to an endocrine specialist. 医生把她介绍给了一位内分泌专家。|近义词：hormonal |反义词：exocrine |词性: adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *endon* "within" + *krinein* "to separate."
