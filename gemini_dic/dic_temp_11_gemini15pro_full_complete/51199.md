destination:/ˌdɛstɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 目的地；终点 |The place to which someone or something is going or being sent. |My destination is Paris. 我的目的地是巴黎。

We arrived at our destination tired and hungry. 我们到达目的地时又累又饿。| synonyms: goal, end, target, objective | antonyms: origin, starting point, source | noun
