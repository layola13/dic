encumber:/ɪnˈkʌmbər/ | v. | 阻碍，妨碍；拖累 | to restrict or burden someone or something, typically with excessive weight or complexity | The hikers were encumbered by heavy backpacks.  徒步旅行者被沉重的背包拖累了。|  burden, hinder, impede, obstruct | facilitate, aid, assist | verb

encumbrance:/ɪnˈkʌmbrəns/ | n. | 累赘；妨碍物；阻碍 | a burden or impediment | The high mortgage was an encumbrance on their finances.  高额的抵押贷款是他们经济上的一个负担。 | burden, impediment, obstacle, hindrance | advantage, asset, benefit | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *encombrer, from combre "obstacle, barrier," ultimately from Latin cumulus "heap, pile."
