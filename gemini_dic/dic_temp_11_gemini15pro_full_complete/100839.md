intromission:/ˌɪntrəˈmɪʃən/ | n. |插入；干预；介入|The act of inserting or introducing something into something else; interference or intervention in someone else's affairs.|例句：The intromission of a catheter into the bladder. 将导管插入膀胱。|例句：His intromission in the dispute was unwelcome. 他对这场争端的介入是不受欢迎的。|近义词：insertion, interference, intervention, intrusion|反义词：withdrawal, abstention|词性：名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *intromissionem*, accusative of *intromissio*,  from Latin intromittere "to send in, let in, admit, insert," from intro- "inwardly" + mittere "to send, let go."
