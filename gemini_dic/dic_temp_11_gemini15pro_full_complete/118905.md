metavanadic:/ˌmɛtəvəˈnædɪk/ | adj. |偏钒酸的; 含五氧化二钒的|Relating to or containing vanadium in its +5 oxidation state, specifically vanadium pentoxide (V2O5).  It forms metavanadates. |Metavanadic acid readily dissolves in alkaline solutions to form metavanadates.| 近义词: vanadic | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps reduced vanadium compounds) | 词性: adjective

metavanadate: /ˌmɛtəˈvænədeɪt/ | n. |偏钒酸盐|A salt of metavanadic acid.  These are typically formed by dissolving vanadium pentoxide in an alkali. |Sodium metavanadate is a common laboratory reagent. | 近义词: vanadate (more general) | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
