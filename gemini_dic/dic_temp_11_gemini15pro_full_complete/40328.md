contraband:/ˈkɑːntrəˌbænd/ /ˈkɒntrəˌbænd/| n. adj.|走私货，违禁品；非法交易|Goods that have been imported or exported illegally. |The customs officers seized a large shipment of contraband. 海关官员查获了一大批走私货物。|Synonyms: smuggled goods, illicit goods, illegal imports/exports|Antonyms: legal goods, legitimate goods|noun
contraband:/ˈkɑːntrəˌbænd/ /ˈkɒntrəˌbænd/| adj.|违禁的，走私的|Illegal or prohibited. |The contraband cigarettes were confiscated. 违禁香烟被没收了。|Synonyms: illegal, prohibited, illicit, unlawful|Antonyms: legal, lawful, permitted|adjective
