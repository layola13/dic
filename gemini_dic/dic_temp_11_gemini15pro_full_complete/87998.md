high-rise: /ˈhaɪraɪz/| adj. n.|高层的；高楼|A tall building with many stories, typically used for residential or commercial purposes.  A high-rise apartment building. 一栋高层公寓楼。|例句：I live in a high-rise overlooking the park. 我住在一栋可以俯瞰公园的高楼里。

例句：The city skyline is dominated by high-rises. 城市的天际线被高楼大厦所占据。|近义词：skyscraper, tower block, multi-story building|反义词：low-rise, bungalow|词性: adjective, noun
