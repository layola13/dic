meslin:/ˈmɛzlɪn/ /ˈmezlɪn/ | n. |混播谷物；大小麦混合物|A mixture of grains, especially wheat and rye, sown and harvested together.|The farmer planted meslin in the field. 农民在地里种了混播谷物。|Synonyms: mixed grain, blend, medley |Antonyms: single-crop, purebred|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *meslein, from Late Latin miscellaneum “mixed,”  from Latin miscellus “mixed,” from miscēre “to mix.”
