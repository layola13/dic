absinthin:/ˌæbˈsɪnθɪn/ | n. | 苦艾素 | A bitter crystalline compound, C30H40O6, present in wormwood and responsible for the characteristic flavor of absinthe. | Absinthin is responsible for the bitter taste of absinthe. 苦艾素是苦艾酒苦味的原因。| The distinctive bitterness of the liqueur comes from absinthin.  这种利口酒独特的苦味来自苦艾素。 | synonyms:  lactone,  glucoside | antonyms:  sweetener | noun
