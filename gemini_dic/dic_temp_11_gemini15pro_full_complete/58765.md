dunkirk: /ˈdʌnkɜːrk/ (UK) /ˈdʌnkɜːrk/ (US)| n. |敦刻尔克（法国北部港口城市）; 敦刻尔克大撤退|A seaport in northern France where in 1940 Allied troops had to be evacuated from beaches while under heavy attack.|The evacuation of Dunkirk was a pivotal moment in World War II. 敦刻尔克大撤退是二战中的关键时刻。|Synonyms: Dunkerque |Antonyms: N/A|Noun


dunkirk: /ˈdʌnkɜːrk/ (UK) /ˈdʌnkɜːrk/ (US)| v. |（指船只）从敦刻尔克撤退；（尤指）从困境中撤退|To retreat from a difficult situation, especially in a way reminiscent of the evacuation of Dunkirk.|The company decided to dunkirk from the failing market. 公司决定从衰败的市场中撤离。|Synonyms: retreat, withdraw, evacuate|Antonyms: advance, attack|Verb
