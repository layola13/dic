asterism: /ˈæstərɪzəm/ | n. | 星群; 星座的一部分 | A group of stars that form a recognizable pattern but are not considered a formal constellation.  A visually obvious collection of stars which are not officially a constellation. |  The Big Dipper is an asterism within the constellation Ursa Major.  北斗七星是大熊座中的一个星群。

The Summer Triangle is a prominent asterism. 夏季大三角是一个著名的星群。 | 近义词: constellation, pattern | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "scattered stars") | 词性: noun

Etymology: From the Greek word "ἀστερισμός" (asterismos), meaning "constellation" or "group of stars," derived from "ἀστήρ" (astēr), meaning "star."
