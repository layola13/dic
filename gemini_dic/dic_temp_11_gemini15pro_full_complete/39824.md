conspiracy:/kənˈspɪrəsi/ | n. |阴谋，共谋，阴谋集团|A secret plan by a group to do something unlawful or harmful.|例句：They were accused of conspiracy to murder. 他们被指控密谋谋杀。

例句：The conspiracy was uncovered by the police.  这个阴谋被警方揭露了。

近义词：plot, scheme, intrigue, cabal |反义词:loyalty, honesty|词性:名词
