engineered:/ˌɛndʒɪˈnɪrd/ | adj. | 设计的，工程化的，人为操纵的 | Deliberately or skillfully designed or created. | This is a genetically engineered tomato. 这是一个转基因番茄。| The software is engineered to be user-friendly.  这款软件在设计上注重用户友好性。 | 近义词：designed, constructed, manufactured, manipulated | 反义词：natural, organic | 词性：形容词


词源：From the verb "engineer" + "-ed" (past participle suffix), ultimately from Old French "engignier" (to contrive, devise).
