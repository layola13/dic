fibrillous:/ˈfɪbrɪləs/ /faɪˈbrɪləs/| adj.|纤维状的；纤丝状的|Composed of or containing small fibers or fibrils.|The doctor examined the fibrillous tissue under a microscope. 医生在显微镜下检查了纤维状的组织。|例句：The plant has fibrillous roots.  这种植物有纤维状的根。

例句：The material has a fibrillous texture.  这种材料具有纤维状的质地。

|近义词：fibrous, filamentous, threadlike|反义词: compact, solid|词性: adjective
