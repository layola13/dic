nonsentence:/ˈnɒnˌsɛntəns/
|n.|谬论；废话；无意义的话|A statement that is not grammatically correct or does not have a clear meaning.  A nonsentence is a string of words that does not form a meaningful or grammatically correct sentence.|
The toddler uttered a nonsentence, a string of babbling sounds.|胡言乱语的小孩说了一句毫无意义的话，一串咿呀学语的声音。|
His explanation was a complete nonsentence, full of contradictions and illogical claims.|他的解释完全是废话，充满了矛盾和不合逻辑的主张。|
近义词:gibberish, drivel, balderdash|
反义词:sentence, statement|
词性:名词|
