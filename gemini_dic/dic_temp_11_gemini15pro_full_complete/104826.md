kedgeree:/ˈkɛdʒəri/ (US), /ˈkɛdʒəriː/ (UK)| n. |咖喱鱼饭，鱼肉烩饭|A dish consisting of cooked fish (often smoked haddock), rice, boiled eggs, butter, cream, and spices.|例句：Kedgeree is a popular breakfast dish in some parts of the world.  咖喱鱼饭在世界某些地区是一种受欢迎的早餐。

例句：She made kedgeree for breakfast using smoked cod. 她用烟熏鳕鱼做了咖喱鱼饭作为早餐。|近义词：fish and rice dish, rice pilaf (loosely)|反义词: None (no direct opposite for a specific dish)|词性:noun
