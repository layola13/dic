ballet: /ˈbælei/ (US) /ˈbæleɪ/ (UK) | n. | 芭蕾舞；芭蕾舞剧；芭蕾舞团 | A classical dance form characterized by graceful, precise movements and elaborate costumes. It is often performed to orchestral music and tells a story or expresses a theme through movement. |  She has been studying ballet since she was five. 她五岁就开始学习芭蕾舞了。 | The Nutcracker is a famous ballet. 《胡桃夹子》是一部著名的芭蕾舞剧。 | 近义词: dance, performance | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *ballet*, diminutive of *bal* (“dance”), from Old Italian *ballo*, from Late Latin *ballare* (“to dance”), from Greek *βαλλίζω* (*ballizō*, “to dance, jump about”).
