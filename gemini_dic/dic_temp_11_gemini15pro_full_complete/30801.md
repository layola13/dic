cetyl:/ˈsiːtɪl/| adj.|鲸蜡基的；十六烷基的|Relating to or derived from spermaceti, a waxy substance found in the head cavities of sperm whales.  Specifically refers to a 16-carbon alkyl chain.|Cetyl alcohol is a common ingredient in cosmetics. 鲸蜡醇是化妆品中的常见成分。|近义词:hexadecyl|反义词:None|词性:adjective
