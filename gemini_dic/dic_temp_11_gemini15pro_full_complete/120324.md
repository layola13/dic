minifics: /ˈmɪnɪfɪks/
noun
微型小说；极短篇小说
Very short stories, typically a few hundred words or less.  They are characterized by their brevity and focus on a single idea or moment.
She's a prolific writer of minifics, posting a new one online every day. 她是一位多产的微型小说作家，每天都在网上发布一篇新作。
He won an award for his collection of minifics.  他因其微型小说集而获奖。
synonyms: flash fiction, short shorts, microfiction, drabbles
antonyms: novel, novella
词性: 名词
