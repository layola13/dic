dash:/dæʃ/ /dæʃ/ | n. v. | 破折号，冲撞，少量，急奔，冲击 | A mark of punctuation (–) used especially to indicate a break in sense, an interruption, or a sudden change of thought. Also, to run or travel somewhere in a great hurry. |
例句：He added a dash of salt to the soup. 他在汤里加了一点盐。

例句：She dashed across the road. 她冲过马路。

近义词：run, sprint, rush, speck, pinch, hyphen | 反义词：crawl, stroll, abundance | 词性: 名词, 动词
