herds:/hɜːrdz/ | n. |群,兽群，牧群|A large group of animals, especially hoofed mammals, that live together or are kept together as livestock. |The herds of wildebeest migrated across the plains. 角马群迁徙穿过平原。|近义词：flocks, droves, packs |反义词: individual, single | noun


herds:/hɜːrdz/ | v. |放牧，群集，使成群 | To gather or move (animals or people) as a herd or crowd. |The shepherd herds the sheep into the pen. 牧羊人把羊群赶进羊圈。|近义词：gather, round up, assemble |反义词: scatter, disperse | verb
