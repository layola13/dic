classicists:/ˈklæsɪsɪsts/| n.|古典主义者；古典学者|A person who studies classical literature, art, or culture, especially of ancient Greece or Rome.|The classicists argued that the new building clashed with the city's historical architecture. 古典主义者认为这座新建筑与该市的历史建筑风格冲突。|近义词：scholar, humanist, academic|反义词：modernist, contemporary|noun
