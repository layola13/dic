monkeyishly:/ˈmʌŋkiɪʃli/| adv.|像猴子一样地；滑稽地；顽皮地|In a manner resembling a monkey, often implying mischievousness, playfulness, or imitation.|He grinned monkeyishly and swung from the branch. 他顽皮地咧嘴一笑，从树枝上荡了下来。|近义词：playfully, mischievously, impishly, naughtily|反义词：seriously, solemnly, gravely|词性:副词

词源: monkey (猴子) + -ish (像...的) + -ly (副词后缀)
