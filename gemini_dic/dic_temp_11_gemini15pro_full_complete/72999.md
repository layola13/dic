foosball: /ˈfuːsbɔːl/ | n. | 桌式足球, 踢踢球 | A table game resembling soccer, in which the ball is moved by manipulating rods to which small figures of players are attached. |  例句：We played foosball all afternoon. 我们一下午都在玩桌式足球。

例句：The foosball table at the bar was always crowded. 酒吧里的桌式足球桌总是很拥挤。| 近义词：table soccer, table football | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑outdoor soccer 室外足球作为一种对比) | 词性: 名词
