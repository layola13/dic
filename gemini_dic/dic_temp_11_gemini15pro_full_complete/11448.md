arguer:/ˈɑːrɡjuər/| n. | 争论者，辩论者 | A person who argues; a disputant. | He's a skilled arguer and rarely loses a debate. 他是一个熟练的辩论者，很少在辩论中失败。|  She's not much of an arguer, preferring to avoid confrontation. 她不太喜欢争论，更喜欢避免冲突。 | synonyms: disputant, debater, contender, controvertist | antonyms:  agreer, conciliator, mediator | noun
