comporting:/kəmˈpɔːrtɪŋ/
verb
表现；举止；相称
behaving in a particular way
She comported herself with dignity throughout the difficult trial.  她在整个艰难的审判过程中都表现得很有尊严。
Synonyms: behaving, conducting, acting
Antonyms: misbehaving, misconducting
