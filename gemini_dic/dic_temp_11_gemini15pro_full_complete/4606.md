alcyonaria:/ˌælsiəˈneəriə/
n.
软珊瑚目，八放珊瑚亚纲
An order of corals, subclass Octocorallia, that includes the soft corals, sea fans, and sea pens.  These corals lack a rigid calcareous skeleton and have eight tentacles on each polyp.
The alcyonaria swayed gently in the ocean current.  软珊瑚在洋流中轻轻摇曳。
Synonyms: Octocorallia (in some classifications), soft corals
Antonyms: Scleractinia (stony corals)
noun
