nutria:/ˈnuːtriə/ (US), /ˈnjuːtriə/ (UK)| n. |海狸鼠，河狸鼠|A large, web-footed South American rodent (Myocastor coypus) that has been introduced to other parts of the world, often becoming a pest because of its destructive burrowing and feeding habits.|The nutria population has exploded in recent years, causing damage to local ecosystems. 海狸鼠的数量近年来激增，对当地生态系统造成了破坏。|近义词:coypu, swamp beaver|反义词:None|词性:noun
