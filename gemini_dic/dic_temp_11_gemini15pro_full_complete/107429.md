lamped:/læmpt/
verb
照亮，用灯照射
to illuminate something with a lamp
The security guard lamped the dark alleyway. 保安用手电筒照亮了黑暗的小巷。

verb
(British slang) 打，击
to hit someone
He lamped the guy who was harassing his girlfriend. 他打了那个骚扰他女朋友的家伙。

ETYMOLOGY->
From the noun "lamp" + "-ed" (suffix forming verbs from nouns).  The slang meaning of "hit" likely arises from the image of striking someone with a lamp.

SYNONYMS->
(for illuminate) shine, light, illuminate, brighten
(for hit) hit, punch, strike, bash, wallop

ANTONYMS->
(for illuminate) darken, extinguish
(for hit) caress, protect
