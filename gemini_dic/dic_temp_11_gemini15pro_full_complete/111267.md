litmus:/ˈlɪtməs/| n.|石蕊，试金石|A dye obtained from certain lichens that turns red in acid solutions and blue in alkaline solutions; something that is used as a test for the presence or effect of something.|The election result will be a litmus test of the government's popularity. 选举结果将是对政府支持率的试金石。|Synonyms: indicator, touchstone, barometer|Antonyms: N/A|noun
