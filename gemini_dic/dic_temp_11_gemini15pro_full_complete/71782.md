fleckier:/ˈflɛkiər/ | adj. | 更多斑点的，斑点更明显的 | Covered with more or more noticeable flecks; more spotted. | The giraffe's coat was fleckier than the others. 这只长颈鹿的皮毛比其他的斑点更多。| The old dog's muzzle was fleckier with grey than it had been last year. 这只老狗的口鼻比去年多了更多的灰斑。 | spotted, speckled, mottled, dappled | clear, plain, unspotted | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From fleck + -ier (comparative adjective suffix).  Fleck itself comes from Middle English flek, of uncertain origin, possibly related to Old Norse flekkr ("spot, stain").
