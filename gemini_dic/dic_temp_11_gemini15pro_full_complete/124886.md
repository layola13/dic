muddish:/ˈmʌdɪʃ/| adj.|浑浊的，混乱的，糊涂的|Covered or filled with mud; not clear or bright; confused or muddled.|The river was muddish after the heavy rain. 雨后河水浑浊。|近义词：murky, cloudy, turbid, obscure, confused|反义词：clear, lucid, bright|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From mud + -ish.  "Mud" comes from Old English *mudde, of Germanic origin.  "-ish" is a suffix forming adjectives from nouns, meaning "somewhat like" or "having the qualities of."
