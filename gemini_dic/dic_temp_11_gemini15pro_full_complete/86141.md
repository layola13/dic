hearthstone:/ˈhɑːrθˌstoʊn/ (US), /ˈhɑːθˌstəʊn/ (UK)| n.|壁炉石，炉石，引申为家，家乡|A stone forming part of a hearth. Figuratively, it represents home and family.|例句：The hearthstone was worn smooth by generations of fires. 壁炉石被一代又一代的火焰磨得光滑。

例句：Returning to his childhood home, he felt a warmth spread through him as he gazed at the old hearthstone. 回到童年的家，看着旧壁炉石，他感到一股暖流传遍全身。

近义词：fireplace, home, hearth|反义词: exile, foreign land|词性: noun
