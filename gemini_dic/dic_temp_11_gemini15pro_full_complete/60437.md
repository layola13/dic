eild:/iːld/
noun
急速，急忙，匆忙
Great haste; speed.
He rode with all possible eild to summon the doctor. 他以最快的速度骑马去请医生。
近义词: haste, speed, hurry, rush
反义词: slowness, leisureliness
词性:n.

Etymology: From Middle English *eild, *elde, from Old English *īeldu, *īeldo (“age, old age, lifetime”), from Proto-Germanic *aldiz (“growing old, age, person of advanced age”). More at eld.  The sense "haste" may be due to influence from Old Norse *elldr (“fire, heat”).
