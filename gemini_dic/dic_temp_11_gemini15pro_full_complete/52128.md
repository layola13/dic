**Dialog (US)**

/ˈdaɪəˌlɒɡ/ | n. | 对话，交谈 | A conversation between two or more people. | Example: The dialog in the movie was witty and engaging.  电影中的对话机智又吸引人。| Synonyms: conversation, discussion, exchange | Antonyms: monologue, silence | noun


**Dialogue (UK)**

/ˈdaɪəˌlɒɡ/ | n. | 对话，交谈 | A conversation between two or more people. | Example: The dialogue between the two countries has broken down. 两国之间的对话已经破裂。| Synonyms: conversation, discussion, exchange | Antonyms: monologue, silence | noun


**Etymology:**

From Late Latin *dialogus*, from Ancient Greek διάλογος (diálogos, “conversation”), from διά (diá, “through, across”) + λέγω (légō, “I speak”).
