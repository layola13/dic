enkindle:/ɪnˈkɪndl/ /ɛnˈkɪndl/| vt.|点燃；激起；使燃烧|To arouse or inspire (a feeling, emotion, or idea).|The coach's speech enkindled the team's desire to win. 教练的讲话激起了队员们获胜的渴望。

The sunset enkindled the sky with vibrant colors. 日落用鲜艳的色彩点燃了天空。

|近义词：ignite, inflame, arouse, inspire, stimulate, provoke|反义词：extinguish, quench, stifle, suppress|词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *enkindlen*, from en- + kindlen (“to set fire to; to light”), from Old Norse *kynda* (“to kindle”), from Proto-Germanic *kundōną* (“to kindle, set fire to”). Cognate with kindle.
