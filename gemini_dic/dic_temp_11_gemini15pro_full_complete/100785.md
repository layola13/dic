intrepid:/ɪnˈtrepɪd/| adj.|勇敢的，无畏的|Characterized by resolute fearlessness, fortitude, and endurance.|例句：An intrepid team of climbers scaled the sheer face of the mountain. 一支无所畏惧的登山队攀登了陡峭的山壁。|近义词：undaunted, bold, audacious|反义词:cautious, hesitant|词性:形容词
