katakana:/ˌkɑːtəˈkɑːnə/ /ˌkætəˈkɑːnə/| n.|片假名|One of two Japanese syllabaries, used primarily for loanwords and onomatopoeia.|例句：Katakana is used to write foreign words. 片假名用来书写外来语。

例句：Many company names are written in katakana. 很多公司名称是用片假名写的。|近义词：hiragana (for native Japanese words),|反义词: None (hiragana is a complementary syllabary, not an opposite)|词性:noun
