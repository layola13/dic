neanderthal: /niˈændərˌθɔːl/ (US), /niˈændəˌtɑːl/ (UK)| n. adj.|尼安德特人（名词）, 尼安德特人的（形容词）|An extinct species of human that was widely distributed in ice-age Europe between *c*. 120,000–35,000 years ago, with a receding forehead and prominent brow ridges. The Neanderthals were associated with the Mousterian flint industry of the Middle Palaeolithic.|例句：Neanderthals were skilled hunters. 尼安德特人是熟练的猎人。|近义词：caveman, hominid|反义词:modern human|词性:名词, 形容词
