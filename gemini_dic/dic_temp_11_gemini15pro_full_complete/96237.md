indicant:/ˈɪndɪkənt/ | n. | 指示物，指示剂；标志 | Something that serves to indicate or point out; a sign. | The high fever is an indicant of infection. 高烧是感染的指示。 | synonyms: indicator, sign, symptom, signal, mark, evidence, clue | antonyms:  concealment, suppression | noun

indicant:/ˈɪndɪkənt/ | adj. | 指示的，指示性的 | Serving as a sign or indication. |  His flushed face was indicant of his embarrassment. 他涨红的脸表明他很尴尬。 | synonyms: indicative, suggestive, symptomatic | antonyms: concealing, suppressing | adjective
