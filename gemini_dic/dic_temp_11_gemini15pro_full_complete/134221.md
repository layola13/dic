nonindulgently:/ˌnɒnɪnˈdʌldʒəntli/
adverb
不纵容地；不放纵地；克制地
in a way that does not allow oneself or others to have or do something enjoyable, especially something that is considered bad for them.
She looked at the dessert menu nonindulgently. 她看了看甜点菜单，没有放纵自己。
Near synonyms: abstemiously, austerely, sparingly
Near antonyms: indulgently, excessively, lavishly
词性: 副词
