crispiness:/ˈkrɪspɪnɪs/| n.|酥脆，脆度|The crispiness of the bacon was perfect. 培根的酥脆程度恰到好处。|例句：I love the crispiness of these chips. 我喜欢这些薯片的酥脆。The crispiness of the crust is key to a good pie. 派皮的酥脆是做好派的关键。|近义词：crunchiness, brittleness|反义词：sogginess, softness|词性:名词
Etymology: From crispy + -ness.  "Crispy" comes from the Middle English "crysp," meaning "curled," likely from Old Norse "kriusp." The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
