fizzy:/ˈfɪzi/| adj.|起泡的，嘶嘶作响的|Containing bubbles of gas; effervescent.  Characterized by a hissing or sputtering sound.|The soda was fizzy and refreshing. 这苏打水嘶嘶作响，令人清爽。|Synonyms: bubbly, sparkling, effervescent, carbonated|Antonyms: flat, still|adjective
