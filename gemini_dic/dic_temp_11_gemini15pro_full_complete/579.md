abounded:/əˈbaʊndɪd/
|v.|大量存在；充满；富于|existed or occurred in large numbers; was plentiful|Fish abounded in the lake.  湖里鱼很多。|Synonyms: proliferated, teemed, swarmed, overflowed|Antonyms: lacked, dwindled, diminished|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French abonder, from Latin abundare "to overflow, abound," from ab- "from, away" + undare "to surge, rise in waves," from unda "wave."
