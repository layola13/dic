caroli:/kəˈroʊlaɪ/ | n. | 卡罗利 | Carolus (Latin) meaning "manly," "free man," a given name, often anglicized as "Charles."  From this name comes the word "caroling," singing Christmas carols, originally meaning songs sung in celebration. | Caroling is a popular Christmas tradition. 圣诞颂歌是流行的圣诞节传统。| The festival featured several groups performing caroli. 节日中有几个团体表演颂歌。|  synonym: songs, hymns | antonym: silence, quiet | noun
