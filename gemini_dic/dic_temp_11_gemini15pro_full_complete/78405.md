get-hooked:/ɡɛt hʊkt/ | v. (phrasal verb) | 上瘾；着迷；被迷住 | To become addicted to something; to become fascinated or captivated by something. |  例句：He got hooked on video games when he was a teenager. 他十几岁的时候就迷上了电子游戏。
