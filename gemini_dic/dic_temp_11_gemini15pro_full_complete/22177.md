bombazine:/ˌbɒmbəˈziːn/ /ˌbɑːmbəˈziːn/
noun
薄丧服，羊毛斜纹布
A twilled fabric, usually of worsted and cotton or silk, with a slightly glossy surface, used especially for mourning clothes.
She wore a bombazine dress to the funeral. 她穿着薄丧服参加了葬礼。
近义词:crepe, crape
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "festive attire")
词性:n.


bombazine:/ˌbɒmbəˈziːn/ /ˌbɑːmbəˈziːn/
adjective
(of fabric) twilled and slightly glossy.
(织物)斜纹的，略有光泽的
The old woman's bombazine dress rustled as she walked. 老妇人穿着略有光泽的斜纹连衣裙走动时发出沙沙声。
近义词:lustrous, sheeny
反义词:matte, dull
词性:adj.
