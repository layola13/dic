matriarchalism:/ˈmeɪtriˌɑːrkəlɪzəm/ | n. | 母系制，母权制 | A social system in which descent and inheritance are traced through the female line, and power is held primarily by women. |  Many anthropologists believe that some early societies were based on matriarchalism. 许多人类学家认为一些早期社会是基于母系制的。|  The Mosuo people of China are often cited as an example of a matriarchal society.  中国的摩梭人常被认为是母系社会的一个例子。 |  matriarchy, gynocracy | patriarchy | noun
