ecotours:/ˈiːkoʊtʊərz/
noun
生态旅游
Tours designed to have minimal impact on the environment and often to educate travelers about the local ecosystem.
We booked an ecotour to the Amazon rainforest. 我们预订了去亚马逊雨林的生态之旅。
ecotourism, nature tours, sustainable tourism
mass tourism, unsustainable tourism
n.
