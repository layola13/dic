greenskeepers:/ˈɡriːnzˌkiːpərz/
n.
草坪管理员，场地管理员
A person who maintains the grounds of a golf course or other sporting venue.
The greenskeepers worked tirelessly to prepare the course for the championship.  草坪管理员们不知疲倦地为锦标赛准备球场。

The greenskeepers were busy mowing the fairways.  草坪管理员们正忙着修剪球道。

近义词:groundskeeper, turf manager
反义词: (没有直接的反义词)
词性: noun
