mis-adapting:/ˌmɪsəˈdæptɪŋ/
verb
不适应，适应不良
Failing to adjust adequately or appropriately to a new or changed situation or environment.
The species is mis-adapting to the changing climate.  这个物种对气候变化适应不良。
The company mis-adapting its marketing strategy for the new market. 这家公司未能为新市场调整其营销策略。
maladapting, failing to adapt
adapting, adjusting
v.
