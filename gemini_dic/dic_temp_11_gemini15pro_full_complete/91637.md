hyperbolic:/ˌhaɪpərˈbɒlɪk/ (US) /ˌhaɪpərˈbɑːlɪk/ (UK)| adj.|夸张的；双曲线的|Relating to hyperbole; exaggerated; exceeding the truth.  Also, relating to a hyperbola, a type of open curve.|He made a hyperbolic statement about the size of the fish he caught. 他对他钓到的鱼的大小做了夸张的描述。|synonyms: exaggerated, inflated, overstated, extravagant, amplified, magnified, heightened, excessive, immoderate, inordinate, undue, unreasonable|antonyms: understated, moderate, restrained|adjective

hyperbola:/haɪˈpɜːrbələ/ (US) /haɪˈpɜːbələ/ (UK)| n.|夸张法；双曲线|A figure of speech that uses extreme exaggeration to create emphasis or humor; A type of curve.|The politician's speech was full of hyperbola. 这位政治家的演讲充满了夸张。|synonyms: exaggeration, overstatement, magnification, amplification|antonyms: understatement, litotes|noun
