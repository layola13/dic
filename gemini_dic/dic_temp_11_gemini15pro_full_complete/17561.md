beau:/boʊ/ (US), /bəʊ/ (UK)| n. |男友，情郎，纨绔子弟|A boyfriend or male admirer; a dandy; a man who is very fashionable.|She went to the party with her beau. 她和她的男友一起去参加了派对。|近义词：boyfriend, sweetheart, admirer, suitor, dandy, fop|反义词: - |noun
