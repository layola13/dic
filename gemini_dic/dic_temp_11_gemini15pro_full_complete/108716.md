leanings:/ˈliːnɪŋz/
noun
倾向，偏好
An inclination or preference towards something.
He has socialist leanings. 他有社会主义倾向。
Synonyms: inclinations, tendencies, propensities, biases, predispositions
Antonyms: aversions, dislikes

ETYMOLOGY->
From "lean," meaning to incline or bend, from Old English *hlēonian, from Proto-Germanic *hlinōną. The noun form "leaning" developed in the 16th century, and the plural "leanings" became common later.
