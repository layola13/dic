do someone's bidding:/duː ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈbɪdɪŋ/
verb phrase
执行某人的命令；听从某人的吩咐
carry out someone's orders or instructions.
The soldiers did the general's bidding without question. 士兵们毫无疑问地执行了将军的命令。

近义词: obey, comply with, follow orders, carry out instructions, execute commands
反义词: disobey, defy, refuse, resist
