misappraised:/ˌmɪsəˈpreɪzd/
verb
估价过低，估价过高，评价错误
incorrectly assessed the value or quality of something.
The house was misappraised, and the seller lost money. 这房子被估价过低了，卖家亏了钱。
The antique was misappraised and sold for much less than its real worth.  这件古董被错误估价，以远低于其实际价值的价格售出。

misappraise (动词)
中文翻译：错误地评估；估价过低或过高
英文解析：To assess the value or quality of something incorrectly.  This can involve either overestimating or underestimating the true worth.
例句：
The realtor misappraised the property, leading to a significant financial loss for the seller.  房地产经纪人对房产估价错误，导致卖家遭受重大经济损失。

The company misappraised the market demand for their new product, resulting in overproduction and wasted resources.  该公司错误地评估了新产品的市场需求，导致生产过剩和资源浪费。


近义词：undervalue, overvalue, misjudge, miscalculate
反义词：appraise accurately, assess correctly, evaluate properly
词性：Verb
