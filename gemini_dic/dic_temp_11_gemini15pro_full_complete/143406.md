outflank:/ˌaʊtˈflæŋk/
verb
包抄，迂回；智胜，胜过
to go around the side of an enemy or opponent, especially in order to attack them from behind; to gain an advantage over someone by doing something unexpected
The enemy outflanked us by attacking from the rear. 敌人从后方进攻，包抄了我们。
outflank
verb
包抄，迂回；智胜，胜过
The company outflanked its competitors by launching a new product line. 这家公司通过推出新的产品线胜过了竞争对手。

近义词: bypass, circumvent, outmaneuver, surpass
反义词: confront, face
