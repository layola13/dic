burrito: /bəˈriːtoʊ/ (US) /bəˈriːtəʊ/ (UK)| n. |墨西哥卷饼|A Mexican dish consisting of a flour tortilla wrapped around various ingredients, such as meat, beans, rice, cheese, and vegetables.|例句：I ate a delicious burrito for lunch. 我午饭吃了一个美味的墨西哥卷饼。|例句：The restaurant is famous for its spicy burritos. 这家餐厅以其辛辣的墨西哥卷饼而闻名。|近义词：taco, wrap|反义词: None|词性: Noun

Etymology: From Spanish *burrito*, literally "little donkey", diminutive of *burro* "donkey".  The reason for this name is uncertain, but theories include the resemblance of the rolled-up burrito to a donkey's ear or packsaddle, or the association of donkeys with carrying burdens, similar to how the tortilla carries the filling.
