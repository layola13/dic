amende:/əˈmɛnd/ | n. | 罚款，罚金 | A penalty or fine imposed for an offense. | Example: The judge imposed a heavy amende on the defendant. 法官对被告处以巨额罚款。

Example:  He had to pay an amende for parking illegally. 他因非法停车不得不支付罚款。 | 近义词: fine, penalty, forfeiture | 反义词: reward, prize | 词性: noun

词源: From Old French *amende, from Latin *amenda, from emendare "to correct, free from fault," from ex- "out" + menda "fault."
