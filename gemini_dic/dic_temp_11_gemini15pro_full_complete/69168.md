fax's:/ˈfæksɪz/
noun
传真的（复数，较不正式）
plural form of fax, signifying more than one fax message or machine.
例句：
I received three fax's this morning. 我今天早上收到了三份传真。

The fax's were all sent from the same number. 这些传真都是从同一个号码发来的。

近义词：faxes (more formal), telecopies
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
