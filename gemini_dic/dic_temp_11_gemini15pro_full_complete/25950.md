burqa:/ˈbɜːrkə/ (US), /ˈbɜːrkɑː/ (UK)| n. |罩袍，波卡|A long, loose garment covering the whole body from head to toe, worn in public by some Muslim women.|She wore a burqa that concealed her entire body except for her eyes. 她穿着一件只露出眼睛的罩袍。|近义词：niqab, chador, hijab|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps Western clothing or revealing clothing)|noun
