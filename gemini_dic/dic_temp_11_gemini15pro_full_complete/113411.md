lymph-gland:/ˈlɪmf ɡlænd/ /ˈlɪmf ɡlænd/
noun
淋巴腺
A rounded mass of lymphoid tissue that is surrounded by a capsule of connective tissue, filters lymph, and contains lymphocytes and macrophages, which help to fight infection.
The doctor checked the lymph glands in my neck. 医生检查了我脖子上的淋巴腺。

近义词: lymph node
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a body part)
词性: noun
