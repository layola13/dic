beechiest: /ˈbiːtʃiɪst/
adjective
最像山毛榉的; 山毛榉树最多的
Most resembling or characteristic of beech trees; having the most beech trees.
The beechest woods I've ever seen were in the Chiltern Hills. 我见过的山毛榉树最多的树林在奇尔特恩丘陵。
近义词: leafy, wooded
反义词: barren, treeless
词性: adjective

词源:
From beech + -y + -est.  "Beech" comes from Old English *bēce, from Proto-Germanic *bōkjōn, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bhāgós.  "-y" is a suffix forming adjectives from nouns. "-est" is a suffix forming the superlative degree of adjectives.
