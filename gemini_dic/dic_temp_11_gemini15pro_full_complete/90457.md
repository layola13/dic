huaracho:/wɑːˈrɑːtʃoʊ/| n.|瓦拉奇凉鞋，华拉契凉鞋|A sandal made of woven leather straps, typically worn in Mexico.  These sandals are characterized by their intricate woven leather designs and are often brightly colored. They originate from the Huarache region of Mexico.|He bought a pair of huaraches at the market in Oaxaca. 他在瓦哈卡的市场上买了一双瓦拉奇凉鞋。|Synonyms: sandals, flip-flops|Antonyms: boots, shoes|noun

huaraches:/wɑːˈrɑːtʃeɪz/| n.|瓦拉奇凉鞋，华拉契凉鞋（复数）|Plural form of "huaracho," referring to multiple sandals of this type.|She packed several pairs of huaraches for her trip to Mexico. 她为去墨西哥旅行打包了好几双瓦拉奇凉鞋。|Synonyms: sandals, flip-flops|Antonyms: boots, shoes|noun
