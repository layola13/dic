multi-task: /ˌmʌltiˈtɑːsk/ (US), /ˌmʌltiˈtɑːsk/ (UK)| v. n.| 多任务处理；同时做多件事 |To perform more than one task at the same time.| I often multitask by listening to music while I work. 我经常边工作边听音乐。|Synonyms: multiprocess, juggle, handle several things at once| Antonyms: single-task, focus on one thing|verb, noun

multi-task: /ˌmʌltiˈtɑːsk/ (US), /ˌmʌltiˈtɑːsk/ (UK)| adj.| 多任务的 | Designed or able to perform multiple tasks simultaneously.| Modern computers are multi-task machines. 现代计算机是多任务机器。|Synonyms: versatile, adaptable| Antonyms: single-purpose, dedicated| adjective
