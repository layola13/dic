biomedicine:/ˌbaɪoʊˈmɛdɪsɪn/ | n. | 生物医学 | The branch of medicine concerned with the application of biological and physiological principles to clinical practice. | Biomedicine has made significant advancements in the treatment of cancer. 生物医学在癌症治疗方面取得了重大进展。|  Biomedicine encompasses a wide range of disciplines, including genetics, immunology, and pharmacology. 生物医学包含广泛的学科，包括遗传学、免疫学和药理学。 | synonyms: medical biology, life science, health science | antonyms: N/A | noun
