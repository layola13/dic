flibbertigibbet:/ˈflɪbərtiˌdʒɪbɪt/
adj.
轻浮的，神经质的，反复无常的
Frivolous, flighty, or excessively talkative.
She's such a flibbertigibbet, always flitting from one thing to another. 她真是个轻浮的人，总是从一件事跳到另一件事。

n.
轻浮的人，爱说话的人
A frivolous, flighty, or excessively talkative person.
He dismissed her as a silly flibbertigibbet. 他把她当成一个愚蠢的轻浮的人而不予理会。

Etymology:  Origin uncertain, possibly imitative of chattering.
近义词:  flighty, frivolous, giddy, scatterbrained, fickle
反义词:  serious, steady, stable, constant
