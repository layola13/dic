epilogues:/ˈɛpɪlɒɡz/ (UK) /ˈɛpɪlɔːɡz/ (US)| n. |后记，结语，收场白|A section or speech at the end of a book or play that serves as a comment on or a conclusion to what has happened.|The author added a poignant epilogue to her novel, reflecting on the characters' fates years later. 作者在小说结尾添加了一段辛酸的后记，反思了人物几年后的命运。

The play's epilogue showed the main characters reunited after twenty years.  这出戏的结语展示了主要人物二十年后的重逢。|近义词：afterword, postscript, conclusion|反义词: prologue, preface, introduction|noun
