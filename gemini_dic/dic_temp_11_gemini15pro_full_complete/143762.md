outmarching:/ˌaʊtˈmɑːrtʃɪŋ/
verb
超越，胜过，超过
Surpassing or exceeding in marching speed or progress.
The technologically advanced army was outmarching its enemy. 技术先进的军队在行军速度上超过了敌人。
The company outmarched its competitors in sales growth. 这家公司在销售增长方面超过了竞争对手。
synonyms: outstripping, surpassing, exceeding, outpacing
antonyms: lagging behind, falling behind
