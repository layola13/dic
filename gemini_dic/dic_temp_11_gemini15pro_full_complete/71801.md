fledglings:/ˈfledʒlɪŋz/
noun
雏鸟；新手；无经验的人
Young birds that have recently learned to fly; inexperienced people.
The fledglings were just learning to fly. 这些雏鸟才刚刚学会飞。
The company is hiring fledglings straight out of college. 这家公司正在招聘刚毕业的大学生。
synonyms:  beginner, novice, trainee, rookie, apprentice, newcomer, greenhorn
antonyms: expert, veteran, professional, master
n.
