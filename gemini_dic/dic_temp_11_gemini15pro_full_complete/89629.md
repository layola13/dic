hook-like:/ˈhʊkˌlaɪk/
adjective
钩状的；像钩的
Resembling a hook in shape or curvature.
The eagle has a hook-like beak.  老鹰的喙是钩状的。
The plant has hook-like thorns. 这种植物有钩状的刺。
hooked, curved
straight
