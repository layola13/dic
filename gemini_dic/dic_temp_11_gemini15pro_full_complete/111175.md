lithogenous:/lɪˈθɒdʒɪnəs/
adjective
岩成的；来源于岩石的
Derived from the breakdown of pre-existing rocks.
例句：Lithogenous sediments are transported to the ocean by rivers. 岩成沉积物被河流输送到海洋。

例句：The lithogenous component of the soil is primarily sand. 土壤中的岩成成分主要是沙子。

近义词：terrigenous, clastic
反义词：biogenous, hydrogenous, cosmogenous
词性: adjective
