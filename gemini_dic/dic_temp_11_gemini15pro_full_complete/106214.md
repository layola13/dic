knotgrass:/ˈnɒtɡrɑːs/ (US) /ˈnɑːtɡræs/| n. | 结缕草，百节草 | A common weedy plant with slender stems and tiny flowers, often found in lawns and other grassy areas.  It is also known as common knotgrass, prostrate knotgrass, or birdweed.  It has a long history of use in herbal medicine. |  The lawn was overrun with knotgrass. 草坪上长满了结缕草。| synonyms:  prostrate knotgrass, common knotgrass, birdweed, wireweed, matgrass | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a plant name, but perhaps cultivated lawn grass) | noun
