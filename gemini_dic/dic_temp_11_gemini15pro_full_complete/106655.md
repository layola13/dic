kusa:/ˈkuːsə/| n. |草; くさ (日语)|A Japanese slang term used online to express laughter or amusement, similar to "lol" or "grass" in English internet slang. It's derived from the Japanese word for grass (草, kusa).|例句：That video was so funny, kusa!  那视频太好笑了，kusa！

例句：Kusa, that's a classic meme. kusa，那是个经典的表情包。|近义词：lol, lmao, rofl, grass|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun (slang)
