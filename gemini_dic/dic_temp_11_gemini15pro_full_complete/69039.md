fatwa: /ˈfætwɑː/ (US), /ˈfæt.wə/ (UK)| n.|宗教裁决，教令|A legal opinion or decree handed down by an Islamic religious leader or scholar.|The ayatollah issued a fatwa against the author.|pronouncement, decree, edict, ruling|N/A|noun
Etymology: From Arabic فتوى (fatwā), verbal noun of فتى (fatā, “he gave a legal opinion”).
