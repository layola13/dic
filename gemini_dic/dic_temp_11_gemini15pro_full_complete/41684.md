corresponder:/ˌkɔːrəˈspɒndər/ (British) /ˌkɔːrəˈspɑːndər/ (American)| n.|通讯员；对应物|A person who writes letters regularly to another person. Something that is analogous or similar to something else.|例句：The war correspondent sent regular reports back to the newspaper.  这位战地记者定期向报社发送报道。

|近义词：reporter, journalist, writer, counterpart, analog, equivalent|反义词: - |词性: noun


corresponder:/ˌkɔːrəˈspɒndər/ (British) /ˌkɔːrəˈspɑːndər/ (American)| v.i.|相符；通信|Be similar or analogous; communicate by exchanging letters.|例句：His account of the incident corresponds with hers. 他对事件的描述与她的一致。

|近义词：match, agree, concur, communicate, write|反义词: differ, disagree|词性: verb
