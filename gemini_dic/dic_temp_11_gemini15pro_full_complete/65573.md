evacuated:/ɪˈvækjueɪtɪd/
adjective
已疏散的，已撤离的
removed from a dangerous place
The building was evacuated because of a bomb threat.  这栋楼因为炸弹威胁而被疏散。
evacuated
empty, cleared, vacated
occupied, inhabited
adjective


evacuate:/ɪˈvækjueɪt/
verb
疏散，撤离
to remove someone from a dangerous place
Residents were evacuated from their homes due to the rising floodwaters.  由于洪水上涨，居民们被从家中疏散。
evacuate
clear, vacate, abandon
