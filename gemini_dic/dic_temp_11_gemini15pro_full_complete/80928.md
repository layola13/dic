gps:/ˌdʒiː piː ˈes/ /ˌdʒiː pēˈes/| n.|全球定位系统|Global Positioning System (a system of satellites, computers, and receivers that is able to determine the latitude and longitude of a receiver on Earth by calculating the time difference for signals from different satellites to reach the receiver).|例句：My phone's GPS helped me find the restaurant. 我的手机GPS帮我找到了那家餐厅。

例句：The GPS signal was lost in the tunnel. GPS信号在隧道里丢失了。|近义词：navigation system, positioning system|反义词:None|noun
