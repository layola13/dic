future-proof:/ˈfjuːtʃə pruːf/| adj.| 未来的；面向未来的；不会过时的；经得起未来考验的|Designed or built in a way that will not quickly become obsolete, even with future technological or other advances.  Designed to resist becoming outmoded.|例句：They are trying to create a future-proof business model. 他们正试图创建一个面向未来的商业模式。

例句：Investing in skills development is a future-proof strategy for employees. 对员工技能发展的投资是一项面向未来的策略。|近义词：durable, long-lasting, sustainable, adaptable, resilient|反义词：obsolete, outdated, short-lived, transient|词性:adjective

词源: "future" + "proof"  "Future" refers to time to come, and "proof" suggests resistance or imperviousness to something.  The term combines these ideas to mean resistant to becoming obsolete in the future.
