monocytosis: /ˌmɒnəʊsaɪˈtəʊsɪs/ /ˌmɑːnəsaɪˈtoʊsɪs/
n.
单核细胞增多症
An increase in the number of monocytes circulating in the blood, often associated with chronic infections, autoimmune diseases, or certain types of leukemia.
The patient's blood test revealed monocytosis, indicating a possible underlying infection. 患者的血液检查显示单核细胞增多，表明可能存在潜在感染。
近义词:  leukocytosis (a broader term referring to an increase in white blood cells)
反义词: monocytopenia (a decrease in the number of monocytes)
词性: noun


Etymology:
mono- (Greek: single, alone) + cyt- (Greek: cell) + -osis (Greek: condition, state)
