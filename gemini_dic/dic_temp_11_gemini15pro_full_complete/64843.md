espian:/ɛˈspiːən/ | n. | 间谍，密探 | A person who secretly collects and reports information about the activities of another country or organization. |  例句：The espian was caught trying to steal government secrets.  这个间谍在试图窃取政府机密时被抓获。

例句：She worked as an espian during the war.  她在战争期间担任间谍。 | 近义词：spy, secret agent, undercover agent | 反义词：loyalist, patriot | 词性：名词
