crustation:/krʌˈsteɪʃən/
n.
结壳;硬皮;外壳
A hard outer covering, like a shell or crust.
The crustation on the wound was thick and yellow.  伤口上的硬皮又厚又黄。
近义词:crust, shell, covering
反义词:interior, inside
