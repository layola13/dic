anisotropic:/ˌænɪsəˈtrɒpɪk/, /ˌænɪsəˈtrɑːpɪk/ | adj. | 各向异性的 | Having properties that differ according to the direction of measurement. |

例句：
Wood is an anisotropic material, meaning its properties, such as strength, vary depending on the direction of the applied force.
木头是一种各向异性材料，这意味着它的特性，例如强度，会随着施加力的方向而变化。

The anisotropic nature of liquid crystals makes them useful in display technology.
液晶的各向异性使得它们在显示技术中非常有用。


近义词：non-uniform, inhomogeneous | 反义词：isotropic, uniform, homogeneous | 词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *an* (not) + *isos* (equal) + *tropos* (turning, direction).  Essentially meaning "not equal in all directions."
