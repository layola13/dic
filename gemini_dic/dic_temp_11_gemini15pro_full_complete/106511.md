kreel:/kriːl/
n.
鱼篓，鱼筐
A wicker basket for holding fish.
The fisherman carried his catch in a kreel. 渔夫用鱼篓装着他的渔获。

近义词:basket, container
反义词:None
词性:noun


Etymology:
From Scots <i>creel</i>, from Middle Dutch <i>criel</i>, <i>kreel</i> (“basket, hamper”), from Old French <i>creil</i>, <i>crail</i>.
