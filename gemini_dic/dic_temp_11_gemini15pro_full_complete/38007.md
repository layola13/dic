complimentary:/ˌkɒmplɪˈmentəri/ /ˌkɑːmplɪˈmentəri/| adj.|免费的；赠送的；赞美的；恭维的|Expressing praise or admiration; given or supplied free of charge.|例句：The hotel offered a complimentary breakfast.  酒店提供免费早餐。

例句：He made a complimentary remark about her dress. 他对她的裙子赞美了一番。|近义词：free, gratis, flattering, laudatory|反义词：critical, derogatory, paid|词性: adjective

complimentary:/ˌkɒmplɪˈmentəri/ /ˌkɑːmplɪˈmentəri/| n.|免费赠送的物品；(尤指电影票等)招待券|Something given free of charge, often a ticket or pass.|例句：The tickets were complimentary. 这些票是赠送的。

例句：She received a complimentary bottle of champagne. 她收到了一瓶免费赠送的香槟。|近义词：freebie, perk|反义词：purchase|词性: noun (rare)
