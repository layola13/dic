greasiness:/ˈɡriːsɪnəs/ /ˈɡriːzinəs/| n.|油腻；油污|The greasiness of the food made me feel sick.  食物的油腻让我感到恶心。|例句：The mechanic's hands were covered in greasiness. 技工的手上沾满了油污。

The greasiness of the fast food made it unappetizing.  快餐的油腻让它看起来很不 appetizing. |近义词：oiliness, fattiness|反义词:cleanness, dryness|词性:noun

词源: From greasy + -ness.  来自 greasy（油腻的）+ -ness（名词后缀，表示状态或性质）。
