kwashiorkor: /ˌkwɑːʃiˈɔːrkɔːr/ /ˌkwɑːʃiˈɔːrkər/
kwashiorkor: n.|夸休可尔症|A form of severe protein malnutrition characterized by edema, growth failure, skin changes, and liver damage.|The child suffered from kwashiorkor due to a lack of protein in his diet.  这个孩子由于饮食中缺乏蛋白质而患上了夸休可尔症。|Synonyms: protein-energy malnutrition, malnutrition|Antonyms: nourishment, healthy diet
