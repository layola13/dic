crosswise:/ˈkrɔːswaɪz/| adv. adj.|交叉地，十字形地|In a crosswise direction; across something. |She laid the planks crosswise. 她把木板交叉放置。|The scarf was tied crosswise. 围巾交叉系着。|近义词：across, crossways, transversely|反义词:lengthwise, longitudinally|词性:副词, 形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From cross + -wise.  "Cross" originates from Old Norse "kross," meaning "cross, affliction," ultimately from Latin "crux," meaning "cross, stake."  "-wise" is an Old English suffix meaning "in the manner of," "in the direction of."
