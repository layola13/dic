crowbar:/ˈkroʊbɑːr/ (US), /ˈkroʊbɑː/ (UK)| n.|撬棍; 铁棍|A heavy iron bar with a flattened, curved end, used as a lever for prying or breaking open something.|He used a crowbar to force the door open. 他用撬棍把门撬开了。|
近义词:lever, pry bar, wrecking bar|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "key" in specific contexts)|词性: noun

crowbar:/ˈkroʊbɑːr/ (US), /ˈkroʊbɑː/ (UK)| vt.|用撬棍撬开|To force open or apart with a crowbar.|They crowbarred the crate open. 他们用撬棍撬开了板条箱。|
近义词:pry open, lever, force open|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "close" or "seal" in specific contexts)|词性: verb
