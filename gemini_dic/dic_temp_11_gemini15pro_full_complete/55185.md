dissented:/dɪˈsɛntɪd/
verb
不同意，持异议
To disagree with a majority opinion.
Two members dissented from the majority decision.  两位成员对多数人的决定表示异议。
近义词: disagreed, objected, opposed
反义词: agreed, concurred, assented
词性: verb

Etymology:  From Latin *dissentire*, from dis- "apart, away" + sentire "to feel, think".
