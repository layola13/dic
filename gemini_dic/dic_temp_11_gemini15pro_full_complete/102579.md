jah:/dʒɑː/
n.|上帝，耶和华 (Rastafarianism)|God, Jehovah (in Rastafarianism)|Example: Jah bless! 上帝保佑！|Synonym: God, Jehovah|Antonym: Satan, Devil|Noun

jah:/dʒɑː/
int.|感叹词，表示赞同，惊叹或喜悦 (Rastafarianism)|Exclamation expressing agreement, wonder or joy (in Rastafarianism).|Example: Jah! That's amazing!  哇！太棒了！|Synonym: Wow, Yes, Cool|Antonym: No, Boo|Interjection
