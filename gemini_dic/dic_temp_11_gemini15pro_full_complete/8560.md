anticipate: /ænˈtɪsɪpeɪt/ | v. | 预期，预料，期望 | To expect something to happen or exist, especially something good; to prepare for something before it happens |  I anticipate that the prices will go up. 我预料价格会上涨。

We anticipated a large crowd at the concert. 我们预料会有很多人来参加音乐会。


近义词: expect, foresee, predict | 反义词: dread, fear | 词性: verb
