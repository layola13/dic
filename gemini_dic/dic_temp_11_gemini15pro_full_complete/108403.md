lawlike:/ˈlɔːlaɪk/
adj.
像法律的；合乎规律的；必然的
Resembling or conforming to a law; regular or predictable.
The universe operates according to lawlike principles. 宇宙根据类似法律的原则运作。
The growth of the crystals was a slow and lawlike process. 晶体的生长是一个缓慢而有规律的过程。
近义词:regular, predictable, systematic, consistent, orderly
反义词:irregular, unpredictable, chaotic, random, erratic
词性:形容词
