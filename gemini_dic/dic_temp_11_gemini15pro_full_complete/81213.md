grandnephew:/ˈɡrændˌnefjuː/ (US), /ˈɡrændˌnevjuː/ (UK)| n. |侄孙；外甥孙|A son of one's nephew or niece.|My grandnephew is starting school next year. 我的侄孙明年就要上学了。|Synonyms: great-nephew|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From grand- + nephew.  "Grand-" acts as a prefix indicating one generation further removed.  "Nephew," from Old French neveu, ultimately from Latin nepos "grandson, nephew."
