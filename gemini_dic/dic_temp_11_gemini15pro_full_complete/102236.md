itemized:/ˈaɪtəmaɪzd/ | adj. |逐项列出的，详细列明的 | presented in a list with each item shown separately | The invoice was itemized, showing the cost of each individual component.  发票逐项列出，显示每个组件的成本。| detailed, specified, listed | general, summarized | adjective

itemized:/ˈaɪtəmaɪzd/ | v. |逐项列出，详细列明 (This is less common than the adjective form, but still valid) | to present something as a list with each item shown separately |  She itemized all her expenses for the trip. 她逐项列出了旅行的所有费用。| detail, specify, list | generalize, summarize | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "itemize" + "-ed" (past participle adjective suffix). "Itemize" comes from the noun "item" + "-ize" (verb-forming suffix). "Item" ultimately derives from the Latin word "item," meaning "likewise" or "also," which came to be used to introduce separate items in a list.
