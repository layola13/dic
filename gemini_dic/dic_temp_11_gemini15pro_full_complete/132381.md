nondemanding:/ˌnɒndɪˈmɑːndɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːndɪˈmændɪŋ/
adjective
不苛求的；要求不高的
Not requiring much time, effort, or attention.
This course is suitable for students looking for a nondemanding option. 这门课程适合寻找轻松选择的同学。
The job is relatively nondemanding and allows for a good work-life balance. 这份工作相对轻松，可以很好地平衡工作和生活。
近义词:easy, undemanding, low-maintenance, simple
反义词:demanding, challenging, difficult, strenuous
词性:adjective
