finality:/faɪˈnælɪti/
n.
最终性；定局；终结
The state of being final and conclusive; the quality of being final and unalterable.
The finality of death is something we all must face.  死亡的终结性是我们所有人都必须面对的。
The judge's decision has a sense of finality. 法官的判决有一种定局的感觉。
近义词：conclusion, termination, decisiveness
反义词：incompleteness, indecision, uncertainty
词性: 名词
