minorat:/maɪˈnɔːræt/ |n.| 长子继承制；幼子继承制|A system of inheritance in which the youngest son inherits all or the principal part of his father's landed property.| The minorat system ensured that the family estate remained intact.  幼子继承制确保了家族财产的完整性。|

The minorat prevented the division of land among multiple heirs. 幼子继承制防止了土地在多个继承人之间的分割。| 近义词: ultimogeniture (幼子继承制),  junior right (幼子继承权) | 反义词: primogeniture (长子继承制)| 词性: noun
