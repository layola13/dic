amorphozoic:/ˌeɪmɔːrˈfoʊzoʊɪk/
adj.
无定形动物的;非结晶动物的
Relating to or denoting the geological eon before the appearance of life in fossilized form, spanning the period from the formation of the Earth to 4,000 million years ago.
The amorphozoic era preceded the emergence of life as we know it.  无定形动物时代先于我们所知的生命出现。
The geologist lectured on the conditions of the amorphozoic eon. 地质学家讲述了无定形动物时代的情况。
近义词: precambrian, azoic
反义词: phanerozoic
词性: adjective
