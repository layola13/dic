invalidators:/ɪnˈvælɪdeɪtərz/| n.|使无效者；使作废者|People or things that invalidate something.  Those who disprove or nullify something.|The new evidence presented by the defense served as powerful invalidators of the prosecution's case.  辩方提出的新证据有力地驳倒了控方的论点。|synonyms: nullifiers, negators, disprovers, contradictors|antonyms: validators, confirmers, supporters|noun
