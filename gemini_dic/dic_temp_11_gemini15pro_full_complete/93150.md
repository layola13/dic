ignicolist: /ɪɡˈnɪkəlɪst/
noun
纵火犯，纵火狂
A person who sets fires, especially deliberately.
He was identified as the ignicolist responsible for the devastating wildfire. 他被确认为造成这场毁灭性野火的纵火犯。
The ignicolist was apprehended before he could cause further damage.  纵火犯在造成进一步破坏之前被逮捕了。
Synonyms: arsonist, firebug, pyromaniac
Antonyms: firefighter, fireman


Etymology:
From Latin ignis ("fire") + -colere ("to cultivate, inhabit")  + -ist (suffix forming agent nouns).
