gogga:/ˈɡɒɡə/ (South African English)| n. |虫子，小爬虫|A general term for any insect or small crawling creature, especially one perceived as unpleasant.  It's primarily used in South African English.|例句：The kids were scared of the goggas in the garden. 孩子们害怕花园里的虫子。

例句：Look at that gogga crawling on the wall! 看那只虫子在墙上爬！|近义词：bug, insect, creepy-crawly|反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider larger animals like birds or mammals)|词性:noun
