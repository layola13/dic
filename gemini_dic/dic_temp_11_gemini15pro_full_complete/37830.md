compete:/kəmˈpiːt/ (US) /kəmˈpiːt/ (UK)| v. |竞争，比赛|strive to gain or win something by defeating or establishing superiority over others.|例句：Several companies are competing for the contract.  几家公司正在竞争这份合同。

例句：Athletes compete against each other for medals. 运动员们互相竞争奖牌。

近义词：contend, vie, rival, contest|反义词：cooperate, yield, forfeit|词性：动词


compete (for):/kəmˈpiːt fɔːr/ (US) /kəmˈpiːt fɔː(r)/ (UK)| v. | 为…而竞争|strive to gain or win something specific.|例句：He's competing for a place on the team. 他正在争取团队中的一席之地。

例句：They are competing for the championship title. 他们正在争夺冠军头衔。

近义词：contend for, vie for|反义词：withdraw from|词性：动词短语

compete (with):/kəmˈpiːt wɪð/ (US) /kəmˈpiːt wɪð/ (UK)| v. |与…竞争|strive to gain or win something against someone specific.|例句：Small shops cannot compete with supermarkets. 小商店无法与超市竞争。

例句：She competed with her brother for their parents' attention. 她和她哥哥争夺父母的关注。

近义词：rival, contend with|反义词：cooperate with|词性：动词短语

compete (in):/kəmˈpiːt ɪn/ (US) /kəmˈpiːt ɪn/ (UK)| v. |参加…比赛|take part in a competition.|例句：She's competing in the marathon next month. 她下个月要参加马拉松比赛。

例句：He competed in the Olympics. 他参加了奥运会。

近义词：participate in, take part in|反义词：withdraw from|词性：动词短语
