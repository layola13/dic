brawlis:/ˈbrɔːlɪs/
n.
争吵，打架
A noisy, rough, uncontrolled fight or quarrel.
The two men started a brawlis in the pub. 这两个男人在酒吧里开始了一场争吵。
近义词：fight, quarrel, scuffle, brawl, fracas, melee
反义词：peace, harmony, agreement
词性:名词


brawlis:/ˈbrɔːlɪs/
v.
争吵，打架
To engage in a noisy, rough, uncontrolled fight or quarrel.
They brawlised in the street after the football match. 足球比赛结束后，他们在街上打了起来。
近义词：fight, quarrel, scuffle, brawl, fracas
反义词：negotiate, compromise, discuss
词性:动词

词源:  From Scots *brawlis, probably from Middle French *brailler (Modern French brailler) "to shout, scream," of onomatopoeic origin.
