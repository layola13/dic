hobbling:/ˈhɑːblɪŋ/ (US) /ˈhɒblɪŋ/ (UK)| v. adj. |跛行，蹒跚而行；妨碍，阻碍|  Limping or walking with difficulty; hindering or impeding progress. | The old man was hobbling down the street with a cane. 老人拄着拐杖在街上蹒跚而行。| synonyms: limping, shuffling, stumbling, hindering, impeding | antonyms:  walking, running, striding, facilitating, aiding | verb/adjective

hobble:/ˈhɒbəl/ (UK) /ˈhɑːbəl/ (US)| n. v. | 跛行；束缚，限制| A limp; a rope or strap used to hobble an animal; to restrict the movement of something. | The horse's hobble prevented it from running away.  马的脚绊阻止它跑掉。| synonyms: limp, restraint, shackle, fetter | antonyms: freedom, release, unfetter | noun/verb
