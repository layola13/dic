accredit:/əˈkrɛdɪt/ | vt. | 授权，认可，委派，归功于 | to officially recognize someone or something as having a particular status or being qualified to perform a particular activity; to believe that someone did something | The university is accredited by the government. 这所大学是政府认可的。|  The painting is accredited to Leonardo da Vinci. 这幅画被认为是莱昂纳多·达·芬奇的作品。 | synonyms: authorize, certify, recognize, attribute | antonyms: disqualify, reject | verb

accreditation:/əˌkrɛdɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 授权，认可，委派 | the official process of giving someone or something official status or recognition | The school received accreditation from the national board. 学校获得了国家委员会的认证。 | synonyms: certification, recognition, authorization | antonyms: disqualification, rejection | noun
