deglaze:/diːˈɡleɪz/ | v. |  去除釉；煵锅，溶解锅底结块 | To loosen and dissolve browned food residue from a pan by adding liquid, often wine or stock, and heating. | After searing the steak, I deglazed the pan with red wine. 煎完牛排后，我用红酒煵了锅。 | synonyms: dissolve, loosen | antonyms: thicken, congeal | verb

deglaze:/diːˈɡleɪz/ | n. | (烹饪)煵锅，溶解锅底结块的动作或过程 | The act of deglazing a pan. | The deglaze added a rich flavor to the sauce. 煵锅的动作给酱汁增添了浓郁的风味。| synonyms: deglazing | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *déglacer*, from *dé-* (prefix indicating removal) + *glacer* (to glaze, freeze).  The culinary sense developed from the idea of "unfreezing" or loosening the fond (browned bits) stuck to the bottom of a pan.
