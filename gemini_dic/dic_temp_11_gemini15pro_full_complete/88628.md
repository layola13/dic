hmd:/eɪtʃ em diː/ |n.| 头戴式显示器（Head-Mounted Display）| A device worn on the head, typically covering the eyes, that displays images generated by a computer, used especially in virtual reality or augmented reality applications. |  He bought a new hmd for gaming. 他买了一个新的头戴式显示器来玩游戏。 |  The hmd provided an immersive virtual reality experience.  这个头戴式显示器提供了沉浸式虚拟现实体验。 | synonyms: VR headset, AR headset, head-mounted display | antonyms: monitor, screen | noun
