glaringnesses:/ˈɡlɛərɪŋnɪsɪz/
n.
刺眼;耀眼;显眼
The state or quality of being glaring; conspicuousness.
The glaringnesses of the spotlights made it hard to see the stage. 聚光灯的耀眼光芒使舞台难以看清。
近义词：brightness, brilliance, radiance, intensity, conspicuousness
反义词：dullness, dimness, faintness, obscurity
词性: 名词


词源:  From glare (v.) + -ing + -ness + -es (plural).  "Glare" comes from  Middle English glaren,  from Old Norse glara "to shine brightly."
