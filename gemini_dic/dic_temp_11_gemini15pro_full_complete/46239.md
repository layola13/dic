cysts: /sɪsts/
n.
囊肿；包囊
A cyst is a closed sac, having a distinct membrane and developing abnormally in a cavity or structure of the body.
The doctor discovered a cyst on her ovary. 医生在她的卵巢上发现了一个囊肿。

The scan revealed several cysts in his liver. 扫描显示他的肝脏里有几个囊肿。

近义词: growth, lump, tumor, sac
反义词: (No direct antonym for a physical abnormality)
词性: noun
