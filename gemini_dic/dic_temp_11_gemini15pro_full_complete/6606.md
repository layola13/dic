anaclastics:/ˌænəˈklæstɪks/| n. | 光线折射学；曲面光学| The study of refraction, especially of curved surfaces and their effects on light. | Anaclastics plays a crucial role in the design of lenses. 光线折射学在透镜设计中起着至关重要的作用。| The principles of anaclastics are used in eyeglasses.  光线折射原理用于眼镜。| synonyms: dioptrics, refraction | antonyms: reflection | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
