outswam:/ˌaʊtˈswæm/
verb
游得比…快；游得比…远
outdid in swimming; swam better or further than
He outswam all the other competitors in the race. 他在比赛中游得比其他所有选手都快。
The child outswam his older brother, much to everyone's surprise.  这孩子游得比他哥哥还远，让大家都大吃一惊。
outswim, surpass, beat
be outswum by, lose to
verb
