cerebrate:/ˈsɛrɪbreɪt/ | v. |思考，用脑| To use the mind; think. | He spent hours cerebrating on the problem. 他花了几个小时思考这个问题。| She cerebrated over the possible consequences. 她仔细思考了可能的后果。 | 近义词: think, ponder, contemplate, meditate | 反义词: vegetate, idle | 词性: 动词

cerebration:/ˌsɛrɪˈbreɪʃən/ | n. |思考，用脑| The act of using the mind; thought. | After much cerebration, she finally found a solution. 经过一番思考，她终于找到了解决办法。 | His cerebrations led him to a surprising conclusion. 他的思考使他得出了一个令人惊讶的结论。| 近义词: thought, contemplation, reflection, meditation | 反义词: inaction, inactivity | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Latin cerebrum ("brain") + -ate (verb-forming suffix) and -ation (noun-forming suffix).
