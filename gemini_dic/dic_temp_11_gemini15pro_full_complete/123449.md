monopteral:/məˈnɒptərəl/
adjective
单列柱式的，单翼的，只有一排柱子的
Having or relating to a single row of columns, typically surrounding a circular structure.
The temple was a small, monopteral structure, consisting of a circular colonnade supporting a roof.  这座寺庙是一座小型单列柱式建筑，由支撑屋顶的环形柱廊组成。
The architect designed a monopteral pavilion for the garden.  建筑师为花园设计了一个单列柱式亭子。
近义词: circular, colonnaded
反义词: multi-columned, peristyle
词性: adjective
