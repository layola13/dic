iterant:/ˈɪtərənt/| adj. n.| 巡回的，流动的；巡回者| traveling from place to place, especially to perform a job; a person who travels from place to place|例句：The iterant preacher moved from town to town. 这位巡回传教士在各个城镇之间奔走。|近义词： itinerant, nomadic, wandering, roving, peripatetic|反义词: settled, stationary, resident|词性:形容词,名词
词源: From Latin *iterantem*, present participle of *iterare* "to repeat, go over again," from *iter* "a journey, road, way."
