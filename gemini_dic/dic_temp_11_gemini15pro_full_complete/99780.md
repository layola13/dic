intermembral:/ˌɪntərˈmɛmbrəl/| adj.|肢间的，臂间的|Relating to the limbs, specifically the distance between the forelimbs and hindlimbs.|The intermembral index is used to compare limb proportions in primates. 灵长类动物的臂间指数用来比较肢体比例。|Synonyms:  limb, appendicular|Antonyms: axial|adjective
