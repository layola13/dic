agrobiotechnology:/ˌæɡroʊˌbaɪoʊtekˈnɒlədʒi/ | n. |农业生物技术|The application of biotechnology to agricultural processes, including crop production and livestock management.| Example: Agrobiotechnology has led to the development of pest-resistant crops. 农业生物技术促成了抗虫作物的开发。| Example: Research in agrobiotechnology is crucial for enhancing food security. 农业生物技术研究对于加强粮食安全至关重要。|Synonym: agricultural biotechnology, agrotech, agribiotech|Antonym: traditional farming|noun
