monographist:/məˈnɒɡrəfɪst/ | n. | 专著作家；专题论文作者 | A person who writes a monograph (a detailed written study of a single specialized subject or an aspect of it). |  She is a renowned monographist in the field of 18th-century English literature. 她是一位18世纪英国文学领域的著名专著作家。

Example 1: The monographist spent years researching the life of the obscure poet.  这位专著作家花了数年时间研究这位默默无闻的诗人的生平。

Example 2: The conference attracted monographists from all over the world.  这次会议吸引了来自世界各地的专著作家。

| 近义词: specialist, scholar, writer, author, expert | 反义词: generalist, amateur  | 词性: noun
