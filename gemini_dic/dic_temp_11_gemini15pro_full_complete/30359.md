centennial: /sɛnˈtɛniəl/ | adj. n. | 百年的，百年纪念的；百年纪念 | Relating to a period of one hundred years; a celebration of a hundredth anniversary. |  The town is planning a centennial celebration.  这个城镇正在计划百年庆典。|  The centennial anniversary of the founding of the company was marked by a gala. 公司成立一百周年纪念日举办了盛大的庆祝活动。 | 近义词: centenary | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From Latin *centenālis*, from *centēnī* "a hundred each," from *centum* "hundred."
