feinting:/ˈfeɪntɪŋ/
n.|佯攻，假动作|An act of making a deceptive or distracting movement, typically during a fight or sporting contest.|The boxer's feinting confused his opponent, leaving him open for a knockout punch. 拳击手的佯攻迷惑了他的对手，让他门户大开，被一拳击倒。|synonyms: bluff, pretense, sham, ruse|antonyms: sincerity, honesty|noun

feint:/feɪnt/
v.|佯攻，装作|To make a deceptive or distracting movement, typically during a fight or sporting contest.|He feinted left and then threw a right hook. 他佯装向左，然后猛地挥出一记右勾拳。|synonyms: bluff, pretend, simulate|antonyms: be honest, be sincere|verb
