foreignly:/ˈfɔrɪnli/
adverb
外国地；异国地；格格不入地
in a foreign manner; like something from another country; in a way that seems strange or unfamiliar.
He behaved foreignly, bowing low when introduced. 他举止像外国人，介绍时深深鞠躬。
She looked at me foreignly, as if she didn't recognize me. 她用陌生的眼神看着我，好像不认识我似的。

近义词: exotically, strangely, unnaturally, unfamiliarly
反义词: naturally, domestically, familiarly
词性: adverb

词源:  From foreign + -ly.  "Foreign" comes from Old French "forein" (modern French "forain"), from Late Latin "foraneus," meaning "outside," from Latin "foris," meaning "out of doors, outside."
