memorially:/məˈmɔːriəli/
adv.
纪念地；作为纪念；
In a way that commemorates something or someone.
The day was memorially marked by the unveiling of a statue.  这一天以揭幕雕像的方式隆重纪念。
The museum was memorially dedicated to the victims of the war.  这座博物馆是为纪念战争受害者而建造的。
Synonym:commemoratively, in remembrance
Antonym: forgetfully
词性: 副词
