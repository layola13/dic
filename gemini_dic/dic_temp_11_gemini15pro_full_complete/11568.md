aristotelianism:/ˌærɪstəˈtiːliənɪzəm/
n.
亚里士多德主义
The philosophy of Aristotle and his followers.  It emphasizes logic, observation, and classification in the study of the natural world.
例句：The influence of Aristotelianism can be seen in the works of many medieval thinkers. 亚里士多德主义的影响可以在许多中世纪思想家的作品中看到。

例句：His worldview was deeply rooted in Aristotelianism. 他的世界观深植于亚里士多德哲学。

近义词：peripateticism
反义词：(No direct antonym, could be contrasting philosophies like Platonism)
词性: noun
