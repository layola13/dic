quit:/kwɪt/ | v. | 停止，放弃，离开 | To stop doing something, to give up, to leave a job or place. |  I quit smoking last year. 我去年戒烟了。

|  He quit his job after only two months. 他只干了两个月就辞职了。| synonyms: cease, desist, resign, abandon | antonyms: begin, commence, continue, start | verb

quit:/kwɪt/ | adj. |  自由的，摆脱的 | Free of, released from.  (This usage is archaic and rarely seen.) | He was quit of his debts.  他已经还清了债务。（古语） |  | | adjective

一般现在时 (Simple Present):

I quit my job every time I get stressed.  我每次压力大的时候都会辞职。

He quits smoking every January, but starts again in February. 他每年一月都戒烟，但二月又开始抽了。


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):

I am quitting my job at the end of the month. 我月底要辞职。

She is quitting smoking gradually. 她正在逐渐戒烟。



现在完成时 (Present Perfect):

I have quit smoking for five years. 我已经戒烟五年了。

He has quit his job and moved to the countryside. 他已经辞职搬到乡下去了。



现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):

I have been quitting smoking on and off for years. 我多年来断断续续地戒烟。

She has been quitting her bad habits one by one. 她一直在逐个戒掉她的坏习惯。



一般过去时 (Simple Past):

I quit my job last year. 我去年辞职了。

He quit smoking cold turkey.  他突然戒烟了。



过去进行时 (Past Continuous):

I was quitting smoking when I found out I was pregnant.  我发现怀孕的时候正在戒烟。

He was quitting his job, but then he got a promotion. 他正要辞职，但后来升职了。



过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

I had quit smoking before I got pregnant. 我怀孕前已经戒烟了。

He had quit his job before the company went bankrupt.  公司破产前他已经辞职了。



过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

I had been quitting smoking for a year before I finally succeeded. 我戒烟一年后终于成功了。

He had been quitting his job for months before he found a new one.  他在找到新工作之前已经辞职好几个月了。



一般将来时 (Simple Future):

I will quit smoking next year. 我明年要戒烟。

He will quit his job when he has enough money saved. 他存够钱后就会辞职。



将来进行时 (Future Continuous):

He will be quitting his job soon. 他很快就要辞职了。



将来完成时 (Future Perfect):

I will have quit smoking by next year. 到明年我将已经戒烟了。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):

虚拟语气:

I wish I could quit my job. 我希望我能辞职。


被动语态 (Passive Voice): (Quit is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:

Why did you quit your job? 你为什么辞职？

否定句:
