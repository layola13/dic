briefed:/briːft/| v. |简报；向…介绍情况|to give someone information about something|例句：The president was briefed on the latest intelligence. 总统听取了最新的情报简报。

例句：I briefed him on the current situation. 我向他简要介绍了目前的情况。

近义词：informed, updated, filled in, apprised|反义词：uninformed, ignorant|词性：动词
