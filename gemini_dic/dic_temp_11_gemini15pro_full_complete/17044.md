bathhouses:/ˈbɑːθˌhaʊzɪz/ /ˈbæθˌhaʊzɪz/
n.
公共浴室，澡堂
A building containing many individual bathrooms or a large communal bathing area.
The Roman bathhouses were important social centers.  罗马浴场是重要的社交中心。
The town's old bathhouses are now luxury spas.  镇上的老澡堂现在是豪华水疗中心。
synonyms: spas, public baths, sauna, steam room
antonyms: private bathroom, home shower
