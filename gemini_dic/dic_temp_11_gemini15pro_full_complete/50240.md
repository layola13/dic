dependants:/dɪˈpɛndənts/| n.|受抚养者；依赖他人生活的人|People who rely on another person for financial support, often family members.|She has three young dependants who rely on her income.|近义词：dependents, wards|反义词:providers, supporters|noun

dependants:/dɪˈpɛndənts/| n.|附属物；从属物|Something that is contingent on or subordinate to something else. (Less common usage)|The success of the project is dependent on several key dependants, including funding and personnel.|近义词:accessories, appendages|反义词:independent entities|noun
