antiexpressionism:/ˌæntiɪkˈspreʃənɪzəm/
n.
反表现主义
A movement in art, especially painting, that reacted against Abstract Expressionism. It emphasizes the absence of subjective emotional content and the use of impersonal, objective techniques.
Antiexpressionism, in its simplest form, is a rejection of emotional expression in art.
反表现主义，在其最简单的形式中，是对艺术中情感表达的拒绝。
Post-war artists explored antiexpressionism as a way to move beyond the highly personal style of the Abstract Expressionists.
战后艺术家探索反表现主义，以此作为超越抽象表现主义者高度个人风格的一种方式。
近义词:Formalism, Minimalism
反义词:Expressionism
词性:noun
