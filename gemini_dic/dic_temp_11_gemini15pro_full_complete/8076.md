anthropomorphizes:/ˌænθrəpəˈmɔːrfaɪzɪz/
verb
赋予人性；人格化；将…拟人化
To give human qualities to an animal, object, or idea.
The author anthropomorphizes the animals in her stories, giving them human emotions and thoughts.  作者将她故事中的动物拟人化，赋予它们人类的情感和思想。
The cartoon anthropomorphizes a dog by having it walk upright and talk. 这部动画片将一只狗拟人化，让它直立行走和说话。

Synonyms: personify, humanize
Antonyms: dehumanize, objectify
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Greek "ánthrōpos" (human) + "morphḗ" (form) + "-ize" (verb suffix).
