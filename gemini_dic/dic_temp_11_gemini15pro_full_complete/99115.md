intercede:/ˌɪntərˈsiːd/
verb
调解，斡旋，求情
To act on behalf of someone in difficulty or trouble, especially by pleading or negotiating with someone in authority.
The lawyer interceded on behalf of his client.  律师为他的客户求情。
My neighbor interceded in the dispute between my brother and me. 我的邻居在我和我兄弟之间的纠纷中进行了调解。
synonym: intervene, mediate, plead, negotiate
antonym: ignore, neglect, exacerbate, worsen
词性: 动词
