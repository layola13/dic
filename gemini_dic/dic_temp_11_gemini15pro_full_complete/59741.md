ecocide: /ˈiːkəʊsaɪd/ /ˈiːkoʊsaɪd/
n.
生态灭绝, 生态浩劫
The intentional destruction of the environment, especially on a massive scale.  The deliberate destruction of an ecosystem.
The Amazon rainforest is facing ecocide due to deforestation and mining. 亚马逊雨林由于森林砍伐和采矿正面临生态灭绝。
The oil spill caused widespread ecocide in the coastal region. 石油泄漏在沿海地区造成了大范围的生态浩劫。
近义词: environmental destruction, biocide,  mass extinction
反义词:  conservation, environmental protection
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From eco- (from Greek oikos 'house, dwelling place', hence 'environment') + -cide (from Latin -cidium, from caedere 'to kill').
