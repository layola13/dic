misinstructs:/ˌmɪsɪnˈstrʌkts/
verb
错误地指导，误导
To instruct wrongly; to give incorrect or misleading instructions.
The manager misinstructed the team, leading to a costly mistake.  经理错误地指导了团队，导致了代价高昂的错误。
The faulty manual misinstructs users on how to assemble the product.  有缺陷的手册错误地指导用户如何组装产品。
mislead, misguide, misdirect
instruct correctly, guide properly, direct accurately
verb
