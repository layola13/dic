cannulated:/ˈkænjʊˌleɪtɪd/| adj.|插管的，套管的|Fitted with a cannula (a thin tube inserted into a body cavity, duct, or vessel).|The doctor cannulated the patient's vein to administer fluids. 医生给病人的静脉插管以输液。|Synonyms: intubated, catheterized|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider "unc cannulated" - though not standard usage) |adjective


cannula:/ˈkænjuːlə/| n.|套管，插管|A thin tube inserted into a body cavity, duct, or vessel for drainage, injection, or other purposes.|The surgeon used a cannula to drain fluid from the wound.  外科医生用套管排出伤口中的液体。|Synonyms: catheter, tube|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym) |noun
