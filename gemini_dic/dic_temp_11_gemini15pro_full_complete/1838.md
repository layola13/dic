acrogenous:/əˈkrɒdʒənəs/
adj.
顶生的，顶端的
Growing from the apex or tip.
Example: Acrogenous plants, like ferns, grow by adding new tissue at the tip of the stem.  像蕨类植物这样的顶生植物，通过在茎尖添加新组织来生长。

Example: The acrogenous growth of the moss made it appear like a tiny green tower.  苔藓的顶端生长使它看起来像一座小小的绿色塔。
近义词: apical, terminal
反义词: basal
词性: adjective
