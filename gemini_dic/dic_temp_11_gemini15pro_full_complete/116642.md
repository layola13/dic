mattednesses:/ˈmætɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
纠结；缠结；杂乱
The state of being matted, tangled, or clumped together.
The dog's fur was full of mattednesses after rolling in the mud.  这只狗在泥里打滚后，毛发纠结成块。
Synonyms: tangles, snarls, clumps, knots
Antonyms: smoothness, neatness
