contentiously:/kənˈtɛnʃəsli/ | adv. | 争论地，争议地 | In a manner that involves or causes contention; argumentatively.  | The issue was contentiously debated in parliament.  这个问题在议会引起了激烈的争论。 | Synonyms:  argumentatively, controversially, disputatiously, polemically | Antonyms:  agreeably, amicably, harmoniously | 词性: 副词

contentious:/kənˈtɛnʃəs/ | adj. | 引起争论的，有争议的 | Likely to cause disagreement or argument; controversial. |  The referee made a contentious decision that angered the home fans. 裁判做出了一个有争议的判罚，激怒了主场球迷。 | Synonyms: controversial, disputed, debatable,  polemical | Antonyms: uncontroversial, undisputed, agreeable, peaceful | 词性: 形容词


词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French contencieux, from Latin contentiosus, from contendere ‘strive’.
