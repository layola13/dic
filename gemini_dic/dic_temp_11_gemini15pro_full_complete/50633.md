deride:/dɪˈraɪd/| v. |嘲笑，讥讽|To laugh at or make fun of someone or something in a scornful or contemptuous way.|The critics derided his latest novel as sentimental and predictable. 评论家嘲笑他的最新小说感伤又老套。|近义词：mock, ridicule, scorn, taunt|反义词: praise, commend, admire|动词

derider:/dɪˈraɪdər/| n. |嘲笑者，讥讽者|A person who derides; someone who mocks or ridicules.|The deriders were silenced when she proved them wrong. 当她证明他们错了的时候，那些嘲笑她的人都沉默了。|近义词：mocker, scoffer, critic|反义词: admirer, supporter|名词
