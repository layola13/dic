a cappella:/ˌɑː kəˈpɛlə/ | adj. adv.| 无伴奏合唱的; 清唱地 | Without instrumental accompaniment; sung by voices only. | The choir sang a cappella. 合唱团清唱了一首歌。|  The group performed a cappella in the church.  这个团体在教堂里清唱表演。 | 近义词: unaccompanied | 反义词: accompanied | 词性: adjective, adverb

Etymology: From Italian *a cappella* "in the chapel style," from *cappella* "chapel," from Late Latin *capella* "chapel," diminutive of *cappa* "cape, cope." Originally referred to music sung in a chapel, often without instruments.
