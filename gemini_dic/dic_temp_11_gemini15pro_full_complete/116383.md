mastless:/ˈmæstləs/ | adj. | 无桅的；没有桅杆的 | Without a mast or masts.  A ship or boat that is mastless is without the tall, vertical pole(s) that support the sails. | The storm left the ship mastless and adrift.  暴风雨过后，这艘船失去了桅杆，漂流在海上。 | The mastless barge was towed by a tugboat. 这艘没有桅杆的驳船由拖船拖曳。 | 近义词: unmasted | 反义词: masted | 词性: 形容词
