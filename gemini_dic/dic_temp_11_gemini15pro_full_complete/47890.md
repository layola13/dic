decence:/ˈdiːsns/| n.|得体；合宜；端庄；体面|Conformity to accepted standards of morality or respectability.|She behaved with perfect decence throughout the difficult situation.  她在整个困境中都表现得十分得体。|Example:  His sense of decence prevented him from reading her diary.  他的道德感使他不去读她的日记。|Synonyms: propriety, decorum, modesty, respectability|Antonyms: indecency, impropriety, immodesty|noun
