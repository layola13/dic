crucifiers:/ˈkruːsəˌfaɪərz/
n.
十字花科蔬菜; 十字花科植物
Plants of the family Cruciferae (Brassicaceae), such as cabbage, cauliflower, and radish, typically having four petals in the form of a cross.
Crucifiers are a good source of vitamins. 十字花科蔬菜富含维生素。

n.
