elysian:/ɪˈlɪʒən/ /ɪˈliːʒən/
adjective
极乐世界的；天堂般的
Relating to or characteristic of heaven or paradise.
例句：The garden was an elysian retreat, filled with fragrant flowers and the gentle murmur of a fountain.  这个花园是一处极乐世界的隐居地，充满了芬芳的花朵和喷泉的轻柔低语。

例句：She described the island as an elysian paradise, with pristine beaches and crystal-clear waters. 她将这个岛屿描述为天堂般的乐土，拥有原始的海滩和清澈的海水。

近义词：heavenly, blissful, paradisiacal, delightful
反义词：infernal, hellish, unpleasant, disagreeable
词性: adjective

词源：From Late Latin Elysius, from Latin Elysii campi (“Elysian fields”), from Ancient Greek Ἠλύσιοι πεδία (Ēlýsioi pedía). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂léw- (“to flow”).
