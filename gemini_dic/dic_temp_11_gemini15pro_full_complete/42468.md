counterfeiters:/ˈkaʊntərˌfɪtərz/
n.
造假者；伪造者
People who make fake versions of something valuable, such as money, documents, or designer goods.
The police arrested a group of counterfeiters who were producing fake banknotes. 警方逮捕了一群制造假钞的造假者。

The counterfeiters were caught selling fake designer handbags.  这些造假者在销售假冒名牌手袋时被抓获。

Synonyms: forgers, fakers, imitators, copyists
Antonyms: authenticators, verifiers
noun
