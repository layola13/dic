adapter:/əˈdæptər/| n.|适配器; 转接器|A device used to connect two incompatible pieces of equipment or to allow something to be used in a different way.|I needed an adapter to plug my American laptop into the European outlet. 我需要一个适配器来将我的美式笔记本电脑插入欧洲插座。|synonyms: connector, converter, coupler|antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "incompatible device")|noun

adapter:/əˈdæptər/| n.|改编者|A person who adapts a literary work, such as a novel or play, for a different medium, such as film or television.|He is a well-known adapter of classic novels for the stage. 他是一位著名的经典小说舞台改编者。|synonyms: reviser, rewriter|antonyms: originator, creator|noun
