denouncement:/dɪˈnaʊnsmənt/| n.|谴责，告发，公开抨击|A public declaration that something is wrong or evil. |The president's denouncement of the terrorist attack was swift and strong. 总统迅速而强烈地谴责了恐怖袭击。| Example: The government issued a denouncement of the violence. 政府发表声明谴责暴力行为。|Synonyms: condemnation, censure, criticism, accusation|Antonyms: praise, commendation, approval|noun

denouncement:/dɪˈnaʊnsmənt/| n.|（尤指不愉快事情的）宣布，宣告|A public announcement of something, especially something unpleasant.|The denouncement of war came as a shock to everyone. 战争宣言令所有人震惊。|Example: The denouncement of the company's closure left the workers devastated. 公司倒闭的宣布让工人们悲痛欲绝。|Synonyms: announcement, declaration, proclamation|Antonyms: concealment, suppression|noun
