dictograph:/ˈdɪktəɡræf/ /ˈdɪktəɡrɑːf/| n.|窃听器，侦听器|A device for listening secretly to conversations.|The detective planted a dictograph in the suspect's office.  侦探在嫌疑人的办公室里安装了窃听器。|
The police used a dictograph to gather evidence. 警方使用窃听器收集证据。|Synonyms: bug, wiretap, listening device|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, consider "broadcasting device")|noun
