lappaceous:/ləˈpeɪʃəs/| adj.|覆以粗毛的，毛茸茸的|Covered with or resembling a lap, especially a hairy or furry one.  Referring to surfaces covered with short, soft hairs or scales that overlap like shingles.|The lappaceous texture of the leaf felt soft to the touch.  叶子的粗糙纹理摸起来很柔软。|近义词：hairy, furry, downy, pubescent|反义词:smooth, glabrous|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *lappaceus*, from *lappa* (burdock), referring to the plant's bristly, burr-like flower heads.
