cloddiness:/ˈklɑːdinəs/ | n. | 土块状；笨拙；粗俗 | The cloddiness of the soil made it difficult to plant. 土壤的土块状使种植变得困难。| The cloddiness of his writing style made it hard to read.  他写作风格的笨拙使它难以阅读。 | 近义词：lumpiness, clumsiness, awkwardness, coarseness | 反义词：smoothness, grace, refinement | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From cloddy + -ness.  Cloddy comes from clod, meaning a lump of earth, ultimately from Old English *clodda.
