enounce:/ɪˈnaʊns/| v.|宣布；宣称；公开宣布|to announce or declare publicly|The president enounced a new economic policy. 总统宣布了一项新的经济政策。|例句：The government enounced a new policy on education. 政府宣布了一项新的教育政策。

例句：The company enounced its plans for expansion. 公司宣布了其扩张计划。


|近义词：announce, declare, proclaim, pronounce|反义词：conceal, withhold|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin ēnūntiāre, from Latin ē- (variant of ex- “out”) + nūntiāre “to announce” (from nūntius “messenger”).
