discommendable:/ˌdɪskəmˈendəbl/
adjective
不值得推荐的；应受谴责的
Not deserving of recommendation; blameworthy.
His behavior was discommendable and resulted in his dismissal. 他的行为应受谴责，导致他被解雇。
Synonyms: objectionable, reprehensible, censurable, deplorable, unacceptable
Antonyms: commendable, laudable, praiseworthy, admirable
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From dis- (expressing negation) + commend (to praise) + -able (forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Latin commendare (to entrust, recommend).
