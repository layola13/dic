mestee:/mɛˈstiː/
n.
混血儿（尤指白人和美洲原住民的混血后代）
A person of mixed racial ancestry, especially one having a white and a Native American parent.
She identified as a mestee on the census form. 她将自己在人口普查表上登记为混血儿。
The community was made up of mestizos, mulattoes, and Zambos. 这个社区由梅斯蒂佐人、穆拉托人和赞博人组成。
近义词:mixed-race, half-breed (offensive), hybrid
反义词:purebred (often used for animals)
词性:noun

Etymology:
From Spanish *mestizo*, from Latin *mixticius* "mixed," past participle of *miscere* "to mix."
