nightclubbed:/ˈnaɪtˌklʌbd/
verb
去夜总会玩乐；经常出入夜总会
To frequent nightclubs; to spend time in nightclubs, especially habitually.
He nightclubbed every weekend in his youth. 他年轻时每个周末都去夜总会。
synonyms: clubbed, partied, caroused
antonyms: stayed in, relaxed at home
