cleaks: /klɪːks/
n.
斗篷，披风
A loose outer garment, typically sleeveless and fastening at the neck, worn for warmth or protection.
The riders wore long, dark cleaks. 骑手们穿着长长的黑色斗篷。
近义词: capes, cloaks, mantles
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑轻薄的衣物) shirts, t-shirts
词性: 名词
