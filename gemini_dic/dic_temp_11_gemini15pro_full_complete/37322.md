commemorations:/kəˌmɛməˈreɪʃənz/
n.
纪念活动；庆祝活动
ceremonies or events held in remembrance of someone or something
例句：The commemorations of the war's end included parades and speeches. 战争结束的纪念活动包括游行和演讲。

例句：The city held commemorations to mark the centenary of its founding.  这座城市举行了庆祝活动以纪念其百年诞辰。

近义词：celebrations, observances, memorials
反义词：disregard, neglect
