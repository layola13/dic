mesmerizingly:/ˈmɛzməˌraɪzɪŋli/ | adv. | 令人着迷地，迷人地 | In a way that fascinates or hypnotizes. | The dancer moved mesmerizingly across the stage. 舞者在舞台上优美地舞动，令人着迷。|  The aurora borealis shimmered mesmerizingly in the night sky. 北极光在夜空中闪烁，美得令人着迷。| 近义词: captivatingly, enchantingly, hypnotically, fascinatingly | 反义词: dully, boringly, uninterestingly | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From the verb "mesmerize," which comes from the name of Franz Mesmer, an 18th-century Austrian physician who practiced hypnotism.  The "-ing" suffix forms the present participle, and the "-ly" suffix transforms it into an adverb.
