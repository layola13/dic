operatical:/ˌɒpəˈrætɪkl/ | adj. | 歌剧的；像歌剧的；夸张的 | Relating to opera; characteristic of opera, especially in being excessively dramatic or emotional. | Her reaction was so operatical, complete with dramatic sighs and hand gestures. 她的反应太像歌剧了，充满了戏剧性的叹息和手势。 | The singer gave an operatical performance, full of passion and intensity. 这位歌手的表演很有歌剧风格，充满激情和力量。| 近义词：dramatic, theatrical, histrionic | 反义词：understated, subdued | 词性：形容词

词源: From opera + -ical.  Ultimately from Latin opera "work, effort."
