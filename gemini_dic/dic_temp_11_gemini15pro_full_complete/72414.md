fluffed:/flʌft/
|adj.|
|蓬松的，抖松的，搞砸的|
|Made light and airy; agitated and mixed, often by shaking or beating; (informal) messed up, bungled.|
|The bird fluffed its feathers. 鸟儿抖松了羽毛。|
|近义词：puffed, ruffled, messed up, botched|
|反义词：flat, smooth, successful|
|词性:形容词|


fluff:/flʌf/
|n.|
|绒毛，软毛，错误，失误|
|Soft, downy material, like the fur or feathers of a young animal or bird; a light mass of fibers or particles, such as lint or dust; (informal) a mistake or blunder.|
|The cat left fluffs of fur all over the sofa. 猫在沙发上到处都是绒毛。|
|近义词：fuzz, down, lint, blunder|
|反义词: --|
|词性:名词|


fluff:/flʌf/
|v.|
|抖松，使蓬松，弄糟|
|To make something light and airy by shaking or beating; to mess something up, to bungle.|
|She fluffed the pillows before going to bed. 她睡前抖松了枕头。|
|近义词：puff, ruffle, mess up, botch|
|反义词：flatten, smooth, succeed|
|词性:动词|


词源:  Likely imitative of the sound of something light and airy.
