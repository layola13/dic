britzska:/ˈbrɪtskə/| n.|轻便马车；敞篷马车|A light, open carriage, usually horse-drawn, with a hooded top.|We toured the island in a britzka, enjoying the warm breeze. 我们乘坐一辆敞篷马车游览了小岛，享受着温暖的微风。|Synonyms: carriage, buggy, surrey|Antonyms: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Russian бричка (brička), ultimately from Polish bryka ("carriage").
