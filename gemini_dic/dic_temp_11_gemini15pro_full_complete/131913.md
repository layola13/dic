noncontinence:/ˌnɒnkɒntɪnəns/| n. |失禁，尿失禁|Inability to control the evacuation of urine or feces.|She suffers from noncontinence after the surgery.  她手术后患有失禁。|近义词：incontinence|反义词: continence|词性:名词
Etymology:  From non- + continence, from Latin continentia "a holding together, restraint, moderation," from continentem (nominative continens), present participle of continere "to hold together, enclose; hold in, restrain," from com- "together" (see con-) + tenere "to hold" (from PIE root *ten- "to stretch").
