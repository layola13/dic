colonialnesses:/kəˈloʊniəlˌnɛsɪz/
|n.|殖民性；殖民主义的特性或状态|The colonialnesses of the past continue to shape present-day international relations. 过去的殖民主义特性继续影响着当今的国际关系。|Colonialnesses can manifest in various subtle ways. 殖民主义的特性可以以各种微妙的方式表现出来。|colonialism, imperialism|anti-colonialism, independence|noun


Etymology:
From "colonial" + "-ness"
"colonial" comes from Late Latin "colonia" (colony),  ultimately from Latin "colonus" (farmer, colonist).
"-ness" is a suffix forming nouns expressing state, quality, or condition.
