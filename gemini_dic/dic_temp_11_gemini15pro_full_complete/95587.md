incommensurable:/ˌɪnkəˈmɛnʃərəbl/ | adj. | 不可公度的，不能相比的 | Not able to be judged by the same standard; having no common standard of measurement. | The two philosophies are incommensurable, making any direct comparison meaningless. 这两种哲学是不可比较的，任何直接的比较都没有意义。|  The value of life is incommensurable with money. 生命的价值是无法用金钱衡量的。 |Synonyms: incompatible, disparate, disproportionate, unequal, unlike | Antonyms: commensurable, comparable, similar, compatible | adjective
