crampednesses:/ˈkræmptɪdnɪsɪz/
| n. | 狭窄；拥挤；局促 | The state of being cramped; lack of space.  |The crampednesses of the small apartment made it difficult to live comfortably. 这间小公寓的狭窄使得住起来很不舒服。|  The crampednesses of the airplane cabin made the long flight unbearable. 飞机机舱的狭窄使得长时间的飞行难以忍受。 |近义词： confinement, tightness, restriction |反义词: spaciousness, roominess| 词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From cramped + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality). Cramped itself comes from the verb cramp, meaning "to confine narrowly,"  ultimately from a Germanic source related to "crooked" or "bent."
