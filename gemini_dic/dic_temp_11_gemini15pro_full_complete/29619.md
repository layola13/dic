category:/ˈkætəɡɔːri/ /ˈkætəɡəri/ | n. | 类别，种类 | A group or class of things that share similar characteristics or qualities. | Example: This book belongs to the category of science fiction. 这本书属于科幻小说类。

Example:  They categorized the responses into five main categories. 他们将回复分为五大类。 | Synonyms: class, type, sort, kind, genre, group | Antonyms: individual, singularity | noun
