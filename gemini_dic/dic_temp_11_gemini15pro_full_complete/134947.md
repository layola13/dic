nonnarcissism:/nɒnˈnɑːsɪsɪzəm/
n.
非自恋
The state or quality of not being narcissistic; lack of excessive self-love or admiration.
Example: Her nonnarcissism was refreshing in a world obsessed with selfies.  在一个痴迷于自拍的世界里，她的非自恋令人耳目一新。
Example:  His nonnarcissism allowed him to truly listen to others and empathize with their struggles. 他的非自恋使他能够真正倾听他人并同情他们的挣扎。
Synonym: humility, modesty, selflessness
Antonym: narcissism, egotism, self-centeredness
