overentertained:/ˌoʊvərɛntərˈteɪnd/ | adj. | 过度娱乐的 | Having been entertained to excess; excessively amused or diverted. | The children were overentertained with video games and became restless. 孩子们玩电子游戏过度娱乐，变得焦躁不安。|  The overentertained audience seemed unable to appreciate the subtleties of the play. 被过度娱乐的观众似乎无法欣赏这部戏剧的精妙之处。 | 近义词: oversaturated, overstimulated | 反义词: bored, underwhelmed | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "entertain" (verb meaning "to provide amusement or enjoyment for").  "Entertain" comes from Old French "entretenir," meaning "to hold between, maintain."
