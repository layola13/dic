fissioning:/ˈfɪʃənɪŋ/
n.|裂变，分裂|The fissioning of uranium atoms releases tremendous energy. 铀原子的裂变释放出巨大的能量。|
近义词：splitting, dividing, breaking apart
反义词：fusion, merging, combining
词性：名词

fissioning:/ˈfɪʃənɪŋ/
v.|进行裂变|The atom is fissioning. 原子正在进行裂变。|
近义词：splitting, dividing, breaking apart
反义词：fusing, merging, combining
词性：动词


词源：From the verb "fission," which comes from Latin "fissio" meaning "a cleaving, splitting."
