highchair:/ˈhaɪˌtʃer/ | n. | 儿童餐椅 | A chair with long legs and a tray, designed for a small child to sit in while eating. | The baby was strapped into his highchair, ready for lunch.  婴儿被安全带绑在他的儿童餐椅上，准备吃午饭。|  The toddler threw his food from his highchair onto the floor.  幼儿把食物从他的儿童餐椅上扔到地板上。 |Synonyms: baby chair, feeding chair | Antonyms: None | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From high + chair.  "High" referring to the elevated position of the seat.
