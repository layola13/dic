newfies: /ˈnjuːfiz/ | n. |纽芬兰犬(复数)；纽芬兰人(复数)|Newfies is the plural form of Newfie, which is an informal and sometimes considered derogatory term for a Newfoundland dog or a person from Newfoundland, Canada.  It refers to a large breed of working dog known for its intelligence, strength, and webbed feet, well-suited for water rescue. When referring to people, it can be seen as a stereotype. | My neighbor has two friendly Newfies. 我的邻居养了两只友好的纽芬兰犬。 | The Newfies were known for their seafaring skills. 纽芬兰人以其航海技能而闻名。 |  近义词:Newfoundlanders (for people), Newfoundland dogs (for dogs) | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun
