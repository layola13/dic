nudibranch:/ˈnjuːdɪbræŋk/ | n. | 裸鳃动物，海蛞蝓 | A marine gastropod mollusk that sheds its shell after its larval stage, and that typically has a brightly colored and elaborate body. |  例句：The nudibranch's vibrant colors warned predators of its toxicity.  海蛞蝓鲜艳的颜色警告捕食者它有毒。

例句：Divers are often fascinated by the diverse forms and colors of nudibranchs. 潜水员经常被海蛞蝓的多样形态和颜色所吸引。 | 近义词：sea slug | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists as it's a specific animal) | 词性: noun
