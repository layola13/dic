authenticator:/ɔːˈθɛntɪkeɪtər/ | n. | 认证器，身份验证器 | A device or piece of software that verifies the identity of a user or device. |  Please use your authenticator app to log in. 请使用您的身份验证应用程序登录。

The bank requires an authenticator for online transactions.  银行要求进行在线交易时使用身份验证器。 | 近义词：verifier, validator | 反义词： N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From authenticate + -or.  Authenticate comes from Late Latin authenticāre "to establish as genuine," from Latin authenticus "genuine, original," from Greek authentikos "acting with authority, genuine," from authentēs "one who acts with authority," from autos "self" + hentēs "doer."
