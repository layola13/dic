funicular:/fəˈnɪkjələr/
n.|缆索铁路；缆车|A cable railway, especially one on a mountainside, in which ascending and descending cars are counterbalanced.|We took the funicular up the steep hillside.|近义词:cable car, incline railway|反义词:|noun

funicular:/fəˈnɪkjələr/
adj.|缆索的|Of or relating to a funicular railway.|The funicular system is popular in mountainous regions.|近义词:cable-operated|反义词:|adjective
