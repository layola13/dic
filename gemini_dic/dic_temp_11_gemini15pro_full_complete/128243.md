neurolinguist:/ˌnʊəroʊˈlɪŋɡwɪst/| n. |神经语言学家|A scientist who studies the relationship between language and the brain.|
The neurolinguist conducted experiments to understand how the brain processes language.  神经语言学家进行了实验以了解大脑如何处理语言。

The neurolinguist is studying the effects of stroke on language ability.  这位神经语言学家正在研究中风对语言能力的影响。


Etymology: neuro- (from Greek "neuron" meaning "nerve") + linguist (from Latin "lingua" meaning "tongue, language")

Synonyms: language scientist, cognitive neuroscientist (focused on language)

Antonyms:  N/A (no direct antonym for a profession)

Word Type: Noun
