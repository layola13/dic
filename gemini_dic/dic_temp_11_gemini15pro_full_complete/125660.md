mumper:/ˈmʌmpər/| n. |乞丐；抱怨者；装病者|A person, especially a beggar, who mumbles or mutters.  Someone who complains or grumbles. A person who feigns illness or injury to avoid work or duty.|He was known as a mumper, always asking for handouts.  他被称为乞丐，总是伸手要钱。|Synonyms: beggar, panhandler, mendicant, complainer, malingerer|Antonyms: giver, philanthropist, benefactor, optimist|noun
