confounded:/kənˈfaʊndɪd/ | adj. |  困惑的，糊涂的，惊慌失措的 | Confused and bewildered; surprised and confused;  unable to think clearly. |  I was confounded by the complex instructions. 我被复杂的指令搞糊涂了。| synonyms: bewildered, perplexed, puzzled, baffled | antonyms: clear, lucid, understandable | adjective


confound:/kənˈfaʊnd/ | vt. | 使困惑，使糊涂，证明…有误 | To make (someone) feel surprised and confused; To prove (a theory, expectation, or prediction) wrong. | The sudden change in plans confounded us. 计划的突然改变让我们感到困惑。 | synonyms: bewilder, confuse, perplex, baffle | antonyms: clarify, enlighten, explain | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *confondre*, from Latin confundere (“to pour together, mix, mingle, confuse”), from con- (“together”) + fundere (“to pour, melt”).
