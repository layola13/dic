electrobiological:/ɪˌlɛktroʊbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective|电生物学的|Relating to the electrical activity of living organisms, especially as it relates to their behavior and functioning.|Electrobiological studies have shown a correlation between brainwave activity and certain mental states. 电生物学研究表明，脑波活动与某些精神状态之间存在相关性。|
近义词:bioelectrical, neurophysiological|反义词:None|词性:adjective


electrobiological:/ɪˌlɛktroʊbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
noun|电生物学|The study of the electrical activity of living organisms, especially as it relates to their behavior and functioning.|Electrobiological research has led to advancements in understanding how the nervous system works. 电生物学研究已经导致了对神经系统如何工作的理解的进步。|近义词:bioelectricity, neurophysiology|反义词:None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
electro- (prefix): from Greek "ēlektron" meaning "amber," referring to static electricity.
bio- (prefix): from Greek "bios" meaning "life."
-logical (suffix): from Greek "-logos" meaning "study" or "science."
