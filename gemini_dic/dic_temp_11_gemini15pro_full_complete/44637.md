crownless:/ˈkraʊnləs/
adjective
无冕的，不戴王冠的，没有王冠的
Without a crown; not crowned.
The crownless king wandered the streets, unrecognized.  无冕之王在街上游荡，无人认出。
The painting depicted a crownless queen, a symbol of her lost power.  这幅画描绘了一位没有王冠的女王，象征着她失去的权力。
近义词:uncrowned, dethroned
反义词:crowned, regal
词性:adjective
