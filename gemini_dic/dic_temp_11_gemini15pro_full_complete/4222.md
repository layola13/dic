airlift:/ˈɛərˌlɪft/ | n. vt. |空运；空运物资（或人员）|The act or process of transporting people or goods by aircraft, especially in an emergency. |The Berlin Airlift saved millions of lives. 柏林空运拯救了数百万人的生命。|The government organized an airlift of refugees out of the war zone. 政府组织了将难民空运出战区的行动。|近义词：air transport, fly, transport|反义词: drive, ship | noun, verb
