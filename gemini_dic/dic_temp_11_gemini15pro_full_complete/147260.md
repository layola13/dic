overmerriness:/ˌoʊvərˈmɛrɪnɪs/
noun
过度欢乐，过分高兴
Excessive merriment; boisterous gaiety.
The overmerriness of the party guests soon became tiresome.  派对宾客的过度欢乐很快变得令人厌烦。
Near Synonyms:  exuberance, boisterousness, hilarity, joviality
Antonyms:  somberness, gloom, melancholy
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + merriness.  "Over-" acts as a prefix indicating excess. Merriness comes from Middle English *merinesse*, derived from merry (happy, cheerful), ultimately from Old English *myrġe*.
