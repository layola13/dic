gambist:/ˈɡæmblɪst/| n. |赌徒|A person who gambles, especially as a habit or profession.  Someone who frequently takes risks, especially with money, in the hope of winning something.| He was a compulsive gambler and lost all his savings. 他是一个嗜赌成性的赌徒，输光了所有的积蓄。| Synonyms: gambler, high-roller, punter| Antonyms: abstainer, non-gambler| Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the Old North French word *gambiste*, meaning "gambler," derived from the verb *gambier* "to gamble." Ultimately from Germanic roots.
