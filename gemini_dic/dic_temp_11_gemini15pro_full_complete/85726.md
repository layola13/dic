hawfinch:/ˈhɔːfɪntʃ/ | n. | 厚嘴鸟，欧洲朱顶雀 | A finch (Coccothraustes coccothraustes) with a large head and massive bill, found in Europe and Asia.  It feeds on the kernels of hard seeds and fruit stones. | I saw a hawfinch cracking open a cherry stone. 我看见一只厚嘴鸟啄开了一颗樱桃核。 | Synonyms: grosbeak, common hawfinch | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
