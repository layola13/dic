autophagic:/ˌɔːtəˈfædʒɪk/ | adj. | 自噬的 | Relating to or involving autophagy, the process by which a cell breaks down and destroys old, damaged, or abnormal proteins and other substances in its cytoplasm. | Autophagic processes are crucial for cellular health.  细胞自噬过程对细胞健康至关重要。| The study revealed the autophagic degradation of the misfolded proteins. 这项研究揭示了错误折叠蛋白质的自噬降解。 | synonyms:  lysosomal, degradative | antonyms:  biosynthetic, anabolic | adjective
