carotenes:/ˈkærəˌtiːnz/ | n. | 胡萝卜素 | Any of several orange or red pigments found in carrots and other plants, important in animal nutrition as a precursor of vitamin A. |  Carotenes are converted into vitamin A in the body. 胡萝卜素在体内转化为维生素A。|  Dark leafy greens are also good sources of carotenes.  深色绿叶蔬菜也是胡萝卜素的良好来源。 | synonyms: carotenoid pigments,  | antonyms: N/A | noun
