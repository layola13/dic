antimystical:/ˌæntiˈmɪstɪkəl/
adjective
反神秘主义的
opposed to or rejecting mysticism
例句：His antimystical stance prevented him from appreciating the spiritual aspects of the ritual. 他反神秘主义的立场使他无法欣赏仪式的精神层面。

例句：The author's antimystical approach to religion focused on logic and reason. 作者对宗教的反神秘主义方法侧重于逻辑和理性。

近义词：rational, empirical, skeptical, pragmatic
反义词：mystical, spiritual, esoteric, occult
词性：adjective
