goldcup:/ˈɡoʊldˌkʌp/| n.|金杯; 金凤花; 毛茛科植物|A gold cup is a cup made of gold, often awarded as a prize.  Goldcup can also refer to a type of flowering plant.|He won the goldcup at the horse race. 他在赛马比赛中赢得了金杯。 The goldcups in the garden were in full bloom. 花园里的金凤花盛开了。|Synonyms: trophy, award, prize, buttercup, ranunculus|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, possibly loss, defeat if referring to the prize context)|noun

goldcup:/ˈɡoʊldˌkʌp/| adj.|金杯的|Made of or relating to a gold cup.|The goldcup race is a prestigious event. 金杯赛是一项享有盛誉的赛事。|Synonyms:  golden (sometimes)|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms)|adjective
