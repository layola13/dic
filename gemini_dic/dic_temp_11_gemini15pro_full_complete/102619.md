jainists:/ˈdʒeɪnɪsts/
noun
耆那教徒
Followers of Jainism, a religion originating in ancient India.
The Jainists believe in non-violence and practice vegetarianism.  耆那教徒信仰非暴力，奉行素食主义。
Jainists often wear white clothes. 耆那教徒经常穿白色的衣服。
近义词:followers of Jainism
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider followers of other religions like Buddhists, Hindus, etc.)
词性:n.
