misdecide:/ˌmɪsdɪˈsaɪd/
|v.|
|错误地决定；做出错误的判断|
|The committee misdecided and chose the wrong candidate. 委员会做出了错误的决定，选择了错误的候选人。|
|He misdecided when he invested all his money in that failing company. 当他把所有的钱都投资在那家倒闭的公司时，他做出了错误的决定。|
|misjudge, err|
|decide correctly, judge correctly|
