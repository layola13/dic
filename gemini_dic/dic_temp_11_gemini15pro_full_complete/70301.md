fidgetingly:/ˈfɪdʒɪtɪŋli/
adv.
不安地，烦躁地
restlessly and nervously due to impatience or nervousness
She tapped her foot fidgetingly throughout the meeting.  她在整个会议期间不安地敲着脚。
He waited fidgetingly for the results. 他焦急地等待着结果。
近义词: nervously, restlessly, impatiently
反义词: calmly, patiently, serenely
词性: adverb
