nonschool:/ˌnɑːnˈskuːl/
adjective
非学校的；校外的
Not related to or connected with school.
例句：
Nonschool activities are important for a child's development.
校外活动对孩子的发展很重要。

Nonschool organizations can provide valuable resources for families.
非学校机构可以为家庭提供宝贵的资源。

近义词：extrascholastic, out-of-school
反义词：school, academic
词性：adjective
