dissembled:/dɪˈsɛmbəld/
verb
假装；掩饰；掩盖（真相、感受等）
To conceal one's true motives, feelings, or beliefs.
He dissembled his fear with a smile. 他用微笑掩饰了他的恐惧。
近义词: feign, pretend, mask, disguise
反义词: reveal, expose, disclose
词性: verb

dissembled:/dɪˈsɛmbəld/
adjective
掩饰的；假装的
Having concealed one's true motives, feelings, or beliefs.
Her dissembled cheerfulness couldn't hide her sadness. 她假装的快乐无法掩盖她的悲伤。
近义词: feigned, pretended, masked, disguised
反义词: genuine, sincere, honest
词性: adjective


词源:
From Old French *dissimbler, from Latin dissimulare (“to disguise, conceal, dissemble”), from dis- (“not, un-”) + simulare (“to make like, imitate, copy, represent, feign”).
