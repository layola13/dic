hootenanny:/ˈhuːtənæni/ | n. | 民间音乐聚会；喧闹的聚会 | A social gathering or party with folk music and sometimes dancing. |  We're going to a hootenanny this weekend.  我们这周末要去参加一个民间音乐聚会。|  party, gathering, celebration, festival, get-together |  funeral, memorial service | noun

hootenanny:/ˈhuːtənæni/ | n. | 喧闹；吵闹 | A noisy, lively event or situation. | The party turned into a real hootenanny. 派对变成了一个真正的喧闹场合。|  commotion, uproar, ruckus, hubbub | silence, peace, quiet | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain, possibly from American dialect, perhaps related to "hoot" (to shout or make a loud noise). First recorded in the 1920s.
