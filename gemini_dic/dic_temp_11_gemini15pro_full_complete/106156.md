knobby:/ˈnɑːbi/ (US) /ˈnɒbi/ (UK)| adj.|多节的，粗糙的，疙瘩状的|Covered with or having knobs; bumpy.  Characterized by small, rounded lumps or protuberances.|The old tree had a knobby trunk. 那棵老树的树干疙疙瘩瘩的。|The arthritis made her fingers knobby. 关节炎使她的手指变得粗糙多节。|近义词：lumpy, bumpy, gnarled, nodular|反义词：smooth, even|词性:形容词
