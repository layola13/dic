baobab:/ˈbeɪoʊˌbæb/ (US), /ˈbeɪəʊbæb/ (UK)| n. |猴面包树|A tropical tree with a very thick trunk and large edible fruit.  It is native to Africa and Australia. |例句：The baobab tree can live for thousands of years. 猴面包树可以存活数千年。

例句：The baobab's fruit is a valuable source of food and vitamin C.  猴面包树的果实是食物和维生素C的重要来源。

近义词：bottle tree (due to its shape)|反义词: N/A |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Arabic بُوحِبَاب‎ (būḥibāb, “many seeded”). Ultimately from Egyptian *bꜣ-ḥꜣbꜣb, meaning “many-seeded”.
