multiple: /ˈmʌltɪpl/ (US), /ˈmʌltɪpəl/ (UK)| adj. n.| 多个的，多重的；倍数；[数] 倍数 |A multiple is a number that can be divided by another number without a remainder.  A multiple thing is something that has many parts or occurs many times. |例句：12 is a multiple of 3. 12是3的倍数。 |近义词：many, numerous, several, various|反义词：single, singular, sole|词性:形容词

multiple: /ˈmʌltɪpl/ (US), /ˈmʌltɪpəl/ (UK)| n.| 倍数；[电] 并联；连锁商店；下倍 |A number that is the product of a given number and another integer. A group of retail outlets owned by the same company. |例句：He received multiple injuries in the accident. 他在事故中多处受伤。|近义词：product, factor |反义词：divisor, factor|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin multiplus, from Latin multus (“much, many”) + -plus (“-fold”).
