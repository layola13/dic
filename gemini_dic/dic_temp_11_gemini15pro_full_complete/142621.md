osteoporosis: /ˌɒstiəʊpəˈrəʊsɪs/ (UK) /ˌɑːstioʊpəˈroʊsɪs/ (US)| n. |骨质疏松症 |A medical condition in which the bones become brittle and fragile from loss of tissue, typically as a result of hormonal changes, or deficiency of calcium or vitamin D.|  Osteoporosis is a major health concern for older women. 骨质疏松症是老年妇女的主要健康问题。

Osteoporosis can lead to fractures even from minor falls. 骨质疏松症即使轻微跌倒也可能导致骨折。


近义词: bone loss, osteopenia |反义词: bone density |词性: noun
