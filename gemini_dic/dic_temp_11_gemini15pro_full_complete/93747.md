immatureness:/ˌɪməˈtʃʊrnəs/ | n. |不成熟，幼稚|The state or quality of being immature; lack of maturity.|His immatureness was evident in his impulsive decisions. 他的不成熟体现在他冲动的决定上。|近义词：immaturity, childishness, puerility|反义词：maturity, sophistication, adulthood|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From immature + -ness.  Immature comes from the Latin *immaturus*, from in- "not" + maturus "ripe, mature".
