abscissa:/æbˈsɪsə/ (plural abscissae /æbˈsɪsiː/ or abscissas)| n. |横坐标|The horizontal or x-coordinate of a point in a two-dimensional coordinate system.  It represents the distance of the point from the y-axis.|The abscissa of the point (3, 2) is 3.|近义词: x-coordinate|反义词: ordinate|词性:noun

abscissae:/æbˈsɪsiː/ /æbˈsɪsəz/| n. |横坐标（复数）|The plural form of abscissa, referring to multiple horizontal coordinates.|The abscissae of the points (1,2), (4,5), and (7,8) are 1, 4, and 7 respectively.|近义词: x-coordinates|反义词: ordinates|词性:noun
