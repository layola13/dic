out-of-whack:/ˌaʊt əv ˈwæk/ | adj. | 不正常的，失调的，出毛病的 | Not functioning correctly; out of order or out of adjustment.  | My sleep schedule is completely out of whack. 我的睡眠时间表完全乱了套了。 |  My computer has been acting out of whack all day. 我的电脑一整天都运行不正常。 | 近义词: broken, malfunctioning, disordered, amiss, awry, messed up | 反义词: working, functional, normal, in order, in sync | 词性: 形容词


词源:
"Whack" originally meant "a smart or resounding blow" (18th century). "Out of whack" emerged in the late 19th/early 20th century American English slang, figuratively extending the idea of something being knocked out of its proper position or alignment.
