non-sectarian:/ˌnɒn sekˈteəriən/ | adj. | 非宗派的，不属于任何教派的 | Not affiliated with or limited to a specific religious sect or denomination. | The organization is non-sectarian and welcomes people of all faiths.  这个组织是非宗派的，欢迎所有信仰的人。 |  Example: The university has a non-sectarian policy and admits students from diverse religious backgrounds. 这所大学实行非宗派政策，招收来自不同宗教背景的学生。| Synonyms: interdenominational, nondenominational, ecumenical | Antonyms: sectarian, denominational | Adjective
