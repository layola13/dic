apodeictic:/ˌæpəˈdaɪktɪk/| adj.|必然的；确实无疑的；不容置疑的|Clearly expressed or demonstrated; incontestable.|The laws of mathematics are considered apodeictic.  数学定律被认为是不容置疑的。|His apodeictic pronouncements on the matter left no room for debate. 他对此事不容置疑的声明没有留下任何辩论的余地。|近义词：incontestable, undeniable, irrefutable, demonstrable|反义词：uncertain, questionable, debatable|词性:形容词
词源: From Ancient Greek ἀποδεικτικός (apodeiktikós, “demonstrative”), from ἀποδείκνυμι (apodeíknymi, “I demonstrate”).
