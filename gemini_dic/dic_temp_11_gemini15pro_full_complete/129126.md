nle:/ɛn ɛl iː/ | abbreviation | 无标准中文翻译，通常指"nonlinear element"非线性元件 | NLE stands for "nonlinear editing," which is a method of video editing that allows for random access to any part of a video clip, regardless of its physical location on the storage medium. NLEs can also refer to "nonlinear element" in the context of circuit design, which is a circuit element that does not have a linear relationship between current and voltage. | In video production, NLE software like Adobe Premiere Pro is widely used. 在视频制作中，像Adobe Premiere Pro这样的非线性编辑软件被广泛使用。

  
In circuit design, a diode is a common example of an NLE. 在电路设计中，二极管是非线性元件的一个常见例子。 | 近义词：(depending on context) NLE software: video editor, video editing software, NLE element: nonlinear component, nonlinear device | 反义词：(depending on context) NLE software: linear editing, NLE element: linear element, linear component |  abbreviation
