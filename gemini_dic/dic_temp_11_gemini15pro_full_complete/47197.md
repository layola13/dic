daydreamlike:/ˈdeɪdriːmlaɪk/| adj.|像白日梦一样的，梦幻般的|Resembling or characteristic of a daydream; dreamy, fanciful, vague.|She had a daydreamlike smile on her face as she listened to the music. 她听着音乐，脸上露出了梦幻般的微笑。|The landscape was so surreal, it felt daydreamlike.  这景色如此超现实，感觉就像在白日梦里一样。|近义词：dreamy, fanciful, ethereal, surreal, otherworldly|反义词：realistic, practical, mundane, down-to-earth|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From "daydream" + "-like."  "Daydream" comes from "day" + "dream."  "Day" from Old English "dæg," and "dream" from Old English "drēam."  "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
