nodded:/ˈnɑːdɪd/ (US), /ˈnɒdɪd/ (UK)| v. |点头|Lowered and raised one's head briefly, typically as a sign of agreement, acknowledgment, or greeting.|She nodded in agreement. 她点头表示同意。|近义词：bowed, acknowledged, agreed|反义词:shook (one's head), dissented|verb (past tense of "nod")
