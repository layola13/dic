例句：The nondevelopment of the new product line was a major setback for the company.  新产品线的不开发对公司来说是一个重大的挫折。|近义词：stagnation, underdevelopment, backwardness|反义词：development, progress, growth|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + development.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not." Development comes from the Old French word "devoloper" meaning "to unfold, unwrap."
