antitheoretical:/ˌæntiˌθiːəˈretɪkəl/
adjective
反理论的; 反理论化的
opposed to or distrustful of theory, especially in the arts or social sciences.
His antitheoretical stance made him unpopular in academic circles. 他反理论的立场使他在学术界不受欢迎。
anti-intellectual, empirical, practical
theoretical, academic, intellectual
adjective
