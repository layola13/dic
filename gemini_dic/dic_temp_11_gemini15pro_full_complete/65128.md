ethicality:/ˌeθɪˈkælɪti/
n.
伦理性，道德性
The ethicality of his decision is questionable. 他的决定的伦理性值得怀疑。
The ethicality of animal testing is a hotly debated topic.  动物实验的伦理性是一个备受争议的话题。
近义词：morality, ethics, righteousness
反义词：immorality, unethicalness
