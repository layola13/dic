consul:/ˈkɒnsəl/ (UK), /ˈkɑːnsəl/ (US)| n.|领事，|An official appointed by a government to live in a foreign city and protect and promote the government's citizens and interests there. |The American consul in Paris helped the stranded tourists. 美国驻巴黎领事帮助了滞留的游客。|Synonym: diplomat, envoy, representative|Antonym: N/A|noun
