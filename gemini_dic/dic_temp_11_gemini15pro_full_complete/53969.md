discreetness:/dɪˈskriːtnəs/
n.
谨慎；慎重；审慎
The quality of being discreet, especially in conduct or speech; circumspection.
She handled the sensitive situation with great discreetness. 她非常谨慎地处理了这个敏感的局面。
近义词：caution, prudence, tact, diplomacy
反义词：indiscretion, carelessness, recklessness
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Middle French *discretness*, from Old French *discret* (meaning "discreet") + *-ness*. *Discret* ultimately comes from Latin *discretus*, the past participle of *discernere* (meaning "to separate, distinguish").
