neurocele: /ˈnjʊərəˌsiːl/ | n. | 脑膨出；神经膨出 | A protrusion of nervous tissue through a defect in the skull or vertebral column, forming a cyst filled with cerebrospinal fluid. |  The infant was diagnosed with a neurocele soon after birth.  这个婴儿出生后不久就被诊断出患有脑膨出。 |  Myelomeningocele, encephalocele | None | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From neuro- + -cele, from Greek kēlē "tumor, hernia."  Neuro- refers to nerves or the nervous system. -Cele denotes a swelling or protrusion.
