diathermic:/ˌdaɪəˈθɜːrmɪk/
adjective
透热的，热的可以穿过的
allowing heat to pass through
例句：Diathermic walls allow heat to transfer easily.  透热墙壁很容易传热。

例句：The diathermic container kept the coffee hot for hours.  这个透热的容器让咖啡保温了几个小时。

近义词：thermally conductive, heat-transmitting
反义词：adiabatic, heat-insulating
词性: adjective


diathermanous:/ˌdaɪəˈθɜːmənəs/
adjective
(especially of a substance) allowing heat radiation to pass through
（尤指物质）透热的，可穿透热辐射的
例句：Diathermanous materials are used in infrared saunas. 透热材料用于红外线桑拿房。

例句：The diathermanous window allowed the sun's warmth to enter the room.  透热的窗户让阳光的温暖进入房间。


近义词：transparent to heat radiation, diathermic
反义词：opaque to heat radiation
词性: adjective

词源: From Greek dia- "through" + therme "heat."
