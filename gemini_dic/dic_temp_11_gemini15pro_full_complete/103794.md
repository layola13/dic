jousting:/ˈdʒʌstɪŋ/
n.
马上长矛比武
a sport in which two people on horseback try to knock each other off their horses with lances.
The king enjoyed watching the jousting tournament. 国王喜欢观看马上长矛比武锦标赛。

近义词: tilting, lancing
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "cooperation" in a broader sense)
词性: noun
