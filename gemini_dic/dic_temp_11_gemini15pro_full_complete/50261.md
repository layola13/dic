depersonalises:/diːˈpɜːrsənəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使失去个性；使非人化
To deprive of personality or individuality; to make impersonal or anonymous.
The hospital gowns and sterile environment depersonalized the patients.  医院的病号服和无菌环境使病人失去了个性。
Bureaucracy can depersonalise the relationship between the organisation and its employees. 官僚主义会使组织与其员工之间的关系变得没有人情味。
synonyms: dehumanize, objectify, anonymize
antonyms: personalize, individualize
词性: 动词
