forthwith:/fɔːrθˈwɪθ/
adv.
立即，马上
immediately; without delay
The landlord demanded that the tenants vacate the premises forthwith.  房东要求房客立即搬离该房产。
The committee requested that the report be submitted forthwith. 委员会要求立即提交报告。
近义词:immediately, instantly, directly, promptly, at once
反义词:later, subsequently, eventually
词性:副词
