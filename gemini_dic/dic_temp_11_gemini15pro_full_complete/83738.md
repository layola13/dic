hailed-from:/heɪld frəm/ |v. |来自，出身于|originated from, came from| He hailed from a small town in Ireland. 他来自爱尔兰的一个小镇。|例句：She hailed from a family of musicians. 她出身于一个音乐世家。|近义词：originated from, came from, be from|反义词: N/A|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "hail" (meaning "to greet or acclaim") + "from." Originally, it meant "to salute or greet someone from a distance," particularly calling out from a ship.  Over time, it evolved to mean simply "to come from" or "to originate from."
