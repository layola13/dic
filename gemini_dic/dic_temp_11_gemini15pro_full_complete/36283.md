coheir:/ˌkoʊˈɛər/ | n. |共同继承人；联合继承人 | A person who inherits property along with one or more other people. | Example 1:  She and her brother were coheirs to their father's estate. 她和她哥哥是她父亲遗产的共同继承人。

Example 2: The two cousins were named coheirs in the will. 这两个表兄弟在遗嘱中被指定为共同继承人。| Near Synonyms: joint heir, fellow heir | Antonyms:  sole heir | Noun
