例句：The conference had an embracive agenda, covering many different topics.  这次会议的议程很广泛，涵盖了许多不同的主题。|近义词：inclusive, comprehensive, all-encompassing, broad, wide-ranging|反义词：exclusive, limited, narrow, restricted|词性:形容词
