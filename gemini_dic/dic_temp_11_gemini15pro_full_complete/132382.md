nondemise:/ˌnɒnˈdɪmaɪz/ | adj. | 非转租的，非转移所有权的 |  Relating to a lease in which the lessee does not transfer ownership of the property.  It typically refers to situations where the landlord retains ownership and responsibility for structural repairs and other major maintenance issues. | The tenant signed a nondemise lease, meaning they were responsible for interior upkeep but not the building's structure.  租户签署了一份非转租租赁协议，这意味着他们负责内部维护，但不负责建筑结构。|  Near Synonyms:  retained ownership, landlord-responsible | Antonyms: demise, lessee-responsible | adjective
