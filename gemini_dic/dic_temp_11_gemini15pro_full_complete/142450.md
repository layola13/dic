n.|示波器|An instrument that displays or records a waveform of electronic signals.|The engineer used an oscillograph to analyze the circuit's behavior. 工程师用示波器分析电路的行为。|oscilloscope, waveform monitor|---
noun
oscillograph:/əˈsɪləɡræf/ /ˈɑːsɪləɡræf/
n.|示波图|A photographic or other record made by an oscillograph.|The oscillograph showed a clear spike in voltage. 示波图显示电压明显升高。|waveform recording, oscilloscope trace|---
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From oscill(o)-  + -graph.  Oscill(o)- comes from Latin oscillum "swing." -graph comes from Greek -graphos "writing."
