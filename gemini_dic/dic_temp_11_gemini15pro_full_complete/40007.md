反义词:  N/A

consult:/kənˈsʌlt/
v.
咨询，商量
To seek information or advice from someone with expertise.
She consulted her lawyer before signing the contract. 她签合同前咨询了她的律师。
He consulted a map to find the shortest route. 他查阅地图以找到最短路线。
近义词: advise with, confer with, seek advice from
反义词: ignore, disregard
词性: verb
