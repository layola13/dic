kiosk:/ˈkiːɒsk/ (UK) /kiˈɑːsk/ (US)| n.|亭，报亭，小卖部|A small structure, often open on one or more sides, used as a booth for selling newspapers, refreshments, etc., or for providing information.|I bought a newspaper at the kiosk. 我在报亭买了一份报纸.|synonyms: booth, stand, stall|antonyms: supermarket, store|noun

kiosk:/ˈkiːɒsk/ (UK) /kiˈɑːsk/ (US)| n.|信息亭，自助服务终端|A small, freestanding interactive computer terminal providing information or allowing transactions such as ticket purchases.|I checked in for my flight at the airport kiosk. 我在机场的自助服务终端办理了登机手续。|synonyms: terminal, console, booth|antonyms: counter, desk|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French kiosque, from Turkish köşk, from Persian kūshk "palace, villa, pavilion."
