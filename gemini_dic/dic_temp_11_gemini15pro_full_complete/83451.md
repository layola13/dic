habitual:/həˈbɪtʃuəl/| adj.|习惯性的，惯常的|Done or doing constantly or as a habit. |He is a habitual smoker. 他是个老烟枪。|She is a habitual liar. 她是个惯于说谎的人。|近义词: customary, regular, usual, routine, inveterate, confirmed|反义词: occasional, infrequent, rare, unusual|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *habitualis*, from Latin habitus (“condition, appearance, dress, attire; habit”), past participle of habēre (“to have, hold, handle, manage, keep, consider, think, wear”).
