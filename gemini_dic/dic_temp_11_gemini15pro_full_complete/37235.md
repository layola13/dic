comfortably:/ˈkʌmfərtəbli/ | adv. | 舒服地，舒适地 | in a comfortable manner; providing physical ease and relaxation | I sat comfortably in the armchair. 我舒服地坐在扶手椅上。|She was comfortably dressed in a loose sweater and jeans. 她穿着宽松的毛衣和牛仔裤，很舒服。 |  comfortably, snugly, cozily, easily | uncomfortably, awkwardly, painfully | adverb

Etymology->
From Middle English *confortablely, equivalent to comfortable +‎ -ly.
