nonaccommodatingness:/ˌnɒnəkɒməˈdeɪtɪŋnəs/
| n. |不妥协，不迁就，不合作|The state or quality of being unwilling to adapt or adjust to the needs or wishes of others; inflexibility. |His nonaccommodatingness made negotiations difficult. 他的不妥协使得谈判变得困难。|Near synonyms: inflexibility, rigidity, stubbornness, intransigence, uncooperativeness|Antonyms: flexibility, adaptability, accommodation, cooperation|noun
