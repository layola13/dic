disfurnish:/dɪsˈfɜːrnɪʃ/
|vt.|剥夺家具；使空无家具|To remove the furniture from (a house, room, etc.).|They decided to disfurnish the apartment before moving out. 他们决定在搬出去之前把公寓里的家具搬走。

The landlord disfurnished the property after the tenants left. 房东在房客离开后搬走了房产中的家具。
|近义词：strip, clear, empty|反义词：furnish, equip|词性：动词
