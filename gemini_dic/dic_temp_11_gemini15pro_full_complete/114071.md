magnetograph:/ˈmæɡnɪtəɡræf/ | n. | 磁强计，磁像仪 | An instrument that measures and records variations in the strength of a magnetic field, especially the Earth's magnetic field, and often also its direction. |  The magnetograph at the observatory recorded a sudden spike in magnetic activity.  天文台的磁强计记录到磁场活动的突然增强。

The solar magnetograph allowed scientists to study the sun's magnetic field in detail. 太阳磁像仪使科学家能够详细研究太阳磁场。 |  Synonym:  magnetometer | Antonym: N/A | noun
