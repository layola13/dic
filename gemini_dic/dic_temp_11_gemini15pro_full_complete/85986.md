headspace:/ˈhɛdˌspeɪs/| n.|心理空间，精神状态|Mental or emotional capacity; room to think or feel a certain way|例句：I need some headspace to think about this. 我需要一些心理空间来思考这件事。|例句：She doesn't have much headspace for anything else right now. 她现在没有太多的心理空间去想其他事情。|近义词：mindset, mental space, room to think|反义词:stress, pressure|词性:名词

headspaces:/ˈhɛdˌspeɪsɪz/| n.|心理空间（复数）|Mental or emotional capacities; rooms to think or feel certain ways|例句：Different people need different headspaces to be creative. 不同的人需要不同的心理空间来发挥创造力。|例句：Our busy lives don't always allow for adequate headspaces. 我们忙碌的生活并不总是允许有足够的心理空间。|近义词：mindsets, mental spaces, room to think|反义词:stresses, pressures|词性:名词
