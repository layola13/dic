half-spun:/ˌhæfˈspʌn/
adjective
半纺的; 未完成的
not fully developed or completed; partially spun (referring to yarn or thread)
The half-spun yarn was tangled and unusable.  半纺的纱线缠绕在一起，无法使用。
The project was left half-spun due to lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，该项目半途而废。
unfinished, incomplete, partial
finished, complete, whole
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
"half" + "spun"
"half" (Old English healf) meaning one of two equal parts
"spun" (past participle of "spin" - Old English spinnan) meaning to draw out and twist fibers into thread or yarn
