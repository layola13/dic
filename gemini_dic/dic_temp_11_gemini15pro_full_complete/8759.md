antidogmatisms: /ˌæntiˈdɒɡməˌtɪzəmz/ | n. | 反教条主义 |  The principles or practice of opposing or rejecting dogma, especially in religion or philosophy.  It emphasizes critical thinking and open inquiry over strict adherence to established beliefs. |  His antidogmatisms led him to question the traditional teachings. 他的反教条主义使他对传统教义提出了质疑。 |  The rise of antidogmatisms in the 20th century challenged many long-held beliefs.  20世纪反教条主义的兴起挑战了许多长期持有的信念。 | synonyms:  nonconformism, heterodoxy, freethinking, skepticism | antonyms: dogmatism, orthodoxy, fundamentalism | noun
