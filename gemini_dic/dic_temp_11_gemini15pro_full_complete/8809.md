antientropic:/ˌæntiɪnˈtrɒpɪk/ | adj. | 反熵的，逆熵的 |  Relating to or characterized by antientropy; counteracting or reversing entropy. Antientropy describes processes that increase order and complexity, in contrast to the natural tendency towards disorder represented by entropy.  |  The researchers proposed an antientropic mechanism to explain the complex organization of living systems. 研究人员提出了一种反熵机制来解释生命系统的复杂组织。  The artist's work explored antientropic themes, creating intricate structures from seemingly chaotic elements.  这位艺术家的作品探索了反熵的主题，用看似混乱的元素创造出复杂的结构。 |  negentropic, counter-entropic | entropic | adjective
