commingling:/kəˈmɪŋɡlɪŋ/
n.|混合，混杂|The commingling of funds can lead to accounting difficulties. 资金混杂会导致会计困难。|
commingling:/kəˈmɪŋɡlɪŋ/
v.|混合，混杂|Don't commingle your personal funds with company money. 不要把你的个人资金和公司资金混在一起。|
commingling:/kəˈmɪŋɡlɪŋ/
adj.|混合的，混杂的|(less common as an adjective)  A commingling aroma of spices filled the air.  香料混合的香味弥漫在空气中。|


Etymology:  From "com-" (together) + "mingle" (mix).  "Mingle" comes from Old English "mengen" (to mix).

Synonyms: mixing, blending, merging, intermingling, combining

Antonyms: separating, dividing, segregating, isolating
