nonromantics:/ˌnɒnrəˈmæntɪks/ | n. | 非浪漫主义者；不浪漫的人 | People who are not inclined towards romance; those who are practical or unsentimental. |  The nonromantics in the group just rolled their eyes at the couple's public display of affection.  小组里不浪漫的人只是对这对情侣公开示爱翻白眼。| Synonyms: pragmatists, realists, cynics | Antonyms: romantics, sentimentalists | noun

nonromantic:/ˌnɒnrəˈmæntɪk/ | adj. | 不浪漫的 | Not characterized by or suggestive of romance. |  She found his proposal rather nonromantic, as it happened in a fast-food restaurant. 她觉得他的求婚一点都不浪漫，因为它发生在一家快餐店里。| Synonyms: unromantic, practical, prosaic | Antonyms: romantic, sentimental, dreamy | adjective
