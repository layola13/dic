green-manured:/ˌɡriːn məˈnjʊərd/ | adj. | 绿肥的，用绿肥施的 | Covered with or fertilized with green manure.  Used to describe soil or crops. | Example: The farmer planted a green-manured cover crop to improve soil health.  农民种植了绿肥覆盖作物以改善土壤健康。 | Example: The green-manured field showed significant improvement in yield. 绿肥田的产量显著提高。 | 近义词: fertilized, enriched | 反义词: unfertilized, depleted | 词性: adjective

词源: "green" refers to the use of living plants, and "manure" refers to fertilizing material.  The term describes the practice of using plants, typically legumes or other fast-growing species, as a fertilizer by plowing them into the soil. "Green" 指的是使用活的植物，而 "manure" 指的是肥料。该术语描述了使用植物（通常是豆类或其他快速生长的物种）作为肥料，将其犁入土壤的做法。
