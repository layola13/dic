what:/wɒt/ (UK) /wʌt/ (US)| pron. det. adv.|什么，多么|What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|Synonyms: which, how, why |Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, depends on context) |pronoun, determiner, adverb

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you need? = 你需要什么？
What does she want? = 她想要什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are they doing? = 他们在做什么？
What is he cooking? = 他在做什么菜？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned? = 你学到了什么？
What has she eaten? = 她吃了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on? = 你一直在做什么？
What has he been studying? = 他一直在学习什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? = 你说了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8pm? = 你晚上8点在做什么？
What was she wearing yesterday? = 她昨天穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I called? = 在我打电话之前你做了什么？
What had they finished by then? = 到那时他们完成了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before you arrived? = 你到达之前一直在做什么？
What had she been reading all afternoon? = 她整个下午一直在读什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天将做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你将会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting? = 他们在会议上将讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by Friday? = 你到周五将完成什么？
What will they have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底他们将取得什么成就？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for the past five years? = 过去五年你一直在做什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将一直在学习什么？
