educations:/ˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃənz/
n.
教育（通常用单数形式）
The process of receiving or giving systematic instruction, especially at a school or university.  This word is rarely used in the plural form.  Normally, "education" is used as an uncountable noun.
His education has been invaluable to his career. 他的教育对他事业的发展至关重要。
近义词：schooling, training, instruction, learning, tuition
反义词：ignorance
词性：名词
