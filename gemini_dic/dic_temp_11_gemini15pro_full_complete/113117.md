lunged:/lʌndʒd/
verb
猛冲，刺，突进
(past tense of lunge) Made a sudden forward movement, especially in attack or to reach something.
He lunged at his opponent with a knife. 他持刀向对手猛扑过去。
Synonyms: thrust, pounce, dive, leap
Antonyms: retreat, withdraw, recoil
词性:动词

lunged:/lʌndʒd/
verb
猛冲，刺，突进
Made a sudden, forceful movement forward.
She lunged forward and grabbed the child's hand before he ran into the street.  她向前一冲，抓住了孩子的手，防止他跑到街上。
Synonyms:  dart, dash, spring
Antonyms:  hesitate, pause, falter
词性:动词
