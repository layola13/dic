cairnier:/ˈkɛərniər/ | adj. | 更像石堆的，更多石堆的 | More resembling a cairn; having more cairns. | The summit was cairnier than I expected, with piles of rocks marking the way. 山顶比我想象的更像石堆，一堆堆的石头标记着道路。| The landscape became increasingly cairnier as we climbed higher.  随着我们爬得越高，景色中的石堆就越多。| 近义词: rocky, stony | 反义词: smooth, flat | 词性: 形容词

词源: From cairn + -ier (comparative suffix).  源自cairn（石堆）+ -ier（比较级后缀）。
