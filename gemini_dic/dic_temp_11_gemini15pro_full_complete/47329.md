de-powered:/diːˈpaʊəd/
adjective
失去动力的，被剥夺权力的
deprived of power or strength; made powerless or ineffective
The superhero was de-powered by the villain's weapon.  这个超级英雄被恶棍的武器剥夺了力量。
The government de-powered the regulatory agency. 政府削弱了监管机构的权力。
synonym: weakened, disabled, incapacitated
antonym: empowered, strengthened, enabled
词性: adjective


de-power:/diːˈpaʊər/
verb
使失去动力，剥夺权力
to deprive of power or strength; to make powerless or ineffective
The villain's weapon de-powered the superhero.  恶棍的武器使超级英雄失去了力量。
The new law de-powers local governments. 新法律削弱了地方政府的权力。
synonym: weaken, disable, incapacitate
antonym: empower, strengthen, enable
词性: verb
