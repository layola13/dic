galvanising:/ˈɡælvənaɪzɪŋ/ /ˈɡælvəˌnaɪzɪŋ/
noun
镀锌；激励；刺激
The process of coating iron or steel with a protective layer of zinc.  A sudden, strong feeling of excitement or stimulation.
The galvanising of the steel prevents rust.  镀锌钢材可以防止生锈。
His speech had a galvanising effect on the crowd. 他的演讲对人群有激励作用。
synonyms: coating, plating, energizing, stimulating, inspiring
antonyms: depressing, discouraging
n.

galvanise:/ˈɡælvənaɪz/ /ˈɡælvəˌnaɪz/
verb
镀锌；激励；刺激
Coat (iron or steel) with a protective layer of zinc. Shock or excite (someone), typically into taking action.
The steel was galvanised to prevent rust.  钢材经过镀锌处理以防生锈。
The news galvanised them into action.  这个消息促使他们采取行动。
synonyms: coat, plate, energize, stimulate, inspire, motivate
antonyms: depress, discourage, deter
v.

galvanised:/ˈɡælvənaɪzd/ /ˈɡælvəˌnaɪzd/
adjective
镀锌的；被激励的
Coated with zinc; shocked or excited into action.
The bridge was made of galvanised steel.  这座桥是用镀锌钢建造的。
The team was galvanised by their coach's speech.  团队被教练的讲话所激励。
synonyms: zinc-coated, stimulated, energized
antonyms: unmotivated, depressed
adj.
