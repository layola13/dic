haematolysis:/ˌhiːməˈtɒlɪsɪs/ /ˌheməˈtɑːləsɪs/| n. |溶血|The destruction or dissolution of red blood cells, with release of hemoglobin into the surrounding fluid.|例句：The venom of some snakes causes haematolysis.  某些蛇的毒液会导致溶血。

例句：Haematolysis can be a serious complication of certain diseases. 溶血可能是某些疾病的严重并发症。|近义词：hemolysis|反义词:hematopoiesis|词性:noun
