jamdani:/dʒɑːmˈdɑːni/
n.|织锦, 孟加拉织锦|A fine muslin fabric, traditionally woven in Bangladesh, with intricate floral or geometric designs woven into the fabric, rather than embroidered or printed.|She wore a beautiful jamdani sari to the wedding. 她穿着一件漂亮的织锦纱丽去参加婚礼。|Synonyms: muslin, fine cotton, woven fabric|Antonyms: coarse cloth, synthetic fabric|Noun

jamdani:/dʒɑːmˈdɑːni/
adj.|织锦的，用织锦做的|Made of jamdani fabric.|The jamdani curtains added a touch of elegance to the room. 织锦窗帘为房间增添了一丝优雅。|Synonyms: muslin, fine cotton, woven|Antonyms: coarse, synthetic|Adjective
