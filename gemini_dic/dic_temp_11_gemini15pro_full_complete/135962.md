nonqualities:/nɒnˈkwɒlətiz/ | n. | 非特质；非性质；非品质 |  Refers to characteristics or attributes that are not considered inherent qualities or essential properties.  It often implies aspects that are superficial, transient, or not defining of the true nature of something or someone. | The nonqualities of the product, like its flashy packaging, shouldn't distract from its actual usefulness.  这个产品的非特质，比如它华而不实的包装，不应该让人忽略它实际的用途。 |  superficialities, externalities | qualities, characteristics | noun
