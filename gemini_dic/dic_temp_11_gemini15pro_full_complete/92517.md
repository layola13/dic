hypothyroidic:/ˌhaɪpoʊθaɪˈrɔɪdɪk/
adjective
甲状腺功能减退的
Relating to or affected by hypothyroidism, a condition in which the thyroid gland does not produce enough thyroid hormone.
She has been feeling sluggish and depressed lately, and her doctor thinks she might be hypothyroidic. 她最近感到迟钝和沮丧，她的医生认为她可能有甲状腺功能减退。
My grandmother takes medication for her hypothyroidic condition. 我的祖母服用药物治疗她的甲状腺功能减退症。
近义词:thyroid-deficient
反义词:hyperthyroidic


词性:adjective

词源: "hypo-" (Greek: under, below) + "thyroid" (Greek: shield-shaped, referring to the thyroid cartilage) + "-ic" (adjective suffix).
