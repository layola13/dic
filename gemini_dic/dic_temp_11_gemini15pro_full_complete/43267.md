crafters:/ˈkræftərz/| n. |工匠，手艺人|People who make or create things skillfully, especially by hand.  Those who practice a craft.|例句：The crafters displayed their wares at the local market.  工匠们在当地市场展示他们的商品。

例句：Many crafters specialize in a particular material, such as wood or clay. 许多工匠专门研究某种特定的材料，例如木头或粘土。|近义词：artisans, makers, creators, craftspeople|反义词: amateurs, novices|词性:noun
