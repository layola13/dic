例句：The politician was thoroughly handbagged by the press after the scandal broke.  丑闻曝光后，这位政治家遭到了媒体的严厉批评。|近义词：criticized, reprimanded, chastised, lambasted|反义词：praised, commended|词性:形容词

handbag:/ˈhændbæɡ/| n.|手提包|A bag carried in the hand, typically by women, used for carrying personal items.|例句：She reached into her handbag and pulled out her phone. 她把手伸进手提包，掏出手机。
