fleckless:/ˈflɛklɪs/| adj.|无瑕的，无斑点的|Free from flecks; spotless. |The car's paint job was fleckless, gleaming in the sunlight. 汽车的喷漆工作完美无瑕，在阳光下闪闪发光。|The bride wore a fleckless white gown.  新娘穿着一件洁白无瑕的礼服。|近义词：spotless, immaculate, unblemished, flawless|反义词：spotted, blemished, flawed|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From fleck + -less.  "Fleck" comes from Middle English *flek, of uncertain origin, possibly related to Old Norse flekkr "spot, stain."  "-less" is a suffix meaning "without."
