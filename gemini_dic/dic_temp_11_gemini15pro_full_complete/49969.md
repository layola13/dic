denigrated:/dɪˈnɪɡreɪtɪd/
verb
诋毁，贬低
to criticize unfairly; to blacken someone's reputation
His opponents denigrated his character during the election campaign. 他的对手在竞选期间诋毁了他的人格。
belittle, disparage, defame, slander, vilify
extol, praise, commend, laud
verb
