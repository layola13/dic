borage:/ˈbɔːrɪdʒ/ (US) /ˈbɒrɪdʒ/ (UK)| n.|琉璃苣，星状花草|A bristly European annual herb (Borago officinalis) with bright blue star-shaped flowers, grown for its edible leaves and medicinal properties.|例句：Borage flowers are a beautiful addition to any salad. 琉璃苣花是任何沙拉的美丽补充。

例句：The borage plant is known for its cucumber-like flavor. 琉璃苣植物以其类似黄瓜的味道而闻名。|近义词：starflower|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bora(u)ge, from Anglo-Norman borrage, from Old French borache, from Medieval Latin borrago, of uncertain origin, perhaps from Arabic *abu araq ("father of sweat," referring to its sudorific properties), or from Vulgar Latin *borra ("rough hair," referring to its bristly leaves).
