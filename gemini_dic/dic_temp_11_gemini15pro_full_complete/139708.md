oceans:/ˈoʊʃənz/ | n. |海洋 |The vast bodies of salt water that cover most of the Earth's surface. |例句：The oceans are teeming with life. 海洋充满了生命。

例句：Plastic pollution in the oceans is a serious problem. 海洋中的塑料污染是一个严重的问题。| 近义词：seas, waters | 反义词:land | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *occeans (plural of ocean), from Latin ōceanus, from Ancient Greek ὠκεανός (ōkeanós), a great river encircling the earth. Originally personified as the titan Oceanus.
