madding:/ˈmædɪŋ/| adj.|令人发狂的，使人恼火的|Driving in rush hour traffic can be a maddening experience. |例句：The maddening noise of the construction kept me awake all night.  施工的恼人噪音让我整夜无法入睡。

例句：The maddening itch from the mosquito bite wouldn't go away. 蚊子叮咬引起的恼人瘙痒一直没有消失。|近义词：infuriating, frustrating, exasperating, annoying|反义词：soothing, calming, relaxing|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *madden, from Old English *gemǣdan (“to make mad, madden, enrage”), from Proto-Germanic *gamaidjaną (“to injure, damage, harm, hurt, maim”), from Proto-Indo-European *moy- (“to hurt, injure, destroy”). Cognate with Old Saxon gimethon (“to destroy”), Old High German ga-meidan (“to injure”), Gothic 𐌲𐌰𐌼𐌰𐌹𐌳𐌾𐌰𐌽 (gamaidjan, “to damage”). More at maim.
