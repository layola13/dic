ecumenist:/ɪˈkjuːmənɪst/ | n. | 教会合一主义者; 普世教会主义者 | A person who advocates or supports ecumenism, the principle or aim of promoting unity among the world's Christian churches. |  The ecumenist worked tirelessly to bridge the divides between different denominations.  这位教会合一主义者不知疲倦地工作，以弥合不同教派之间的分歧。| synonyms: unifier, conciliator | antonyms: schismatic, separatist | noun

ecumenical:/ˌɛkjuːˈmɛnɪk(ə)l/ | adj. | 世界范围的，普遍的；基督教各派合一的 | Representing a number of different Christian churches. | The Pope’s ecumenical message called for unity among all Christians. 教皇的世界性信息呼吁所有基督徒团结起来。| synonyms: universal, global, worldwide, general, catholic | antonyms: parochial, sectarian, denominational | adjective
