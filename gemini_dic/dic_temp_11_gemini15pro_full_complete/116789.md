mavourneen:/məˈvɔːrniːn/
mavourneen:|n.|我亲爱的；我的爱人|A term of endearment, derived from Irish, meaning "my darling" or "my beloved". It is often used in a romantic context, but can also be used affectionately towards family members.|Mavourneen, my sweet, come sit by me.  我亲爱的，我的甜心，过来坐在我旁边。|Synonyms: darling, sweetheart, beloved, my love|Antonyms: enemy, foe|Noun
