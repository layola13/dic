out-values:/ˌaʊtˈvæljuːz/| v.| 胜过，超过 (在价值或重要性方面) | This new phone out-values all others on the market in terms of battery life.  这款新手机在电池续航方面胜过市面上所有其他手机。| The company’s assets out-value its liabilities. 该公司的资产价值超过其负债。| surpass, exceed, outstrip |  be inferior to, be less than | verb

Etymology:
"out-" (prefix denoting exceeding or surpassing) + "value" (from Old French "valuer," from Latin "valere" - to be strong, be worth)
