friezeless:/ˈfriːz.ləs/
adj.
无霜冻的；不受霜冻影响的
Not subject to frost; free from frost.
The friezeless climate of Florida allows citrus fruits to grow year-round. 佛罗里达无霜冻的气候允许柑橘类水果全年生长。
The gardener searched for friezeless plants to put in his outdoor pots during the winter. 园丁寻找耐寒植物，以便在冬天把它们放在室外的花盆里。
近义词:frost-free, frostless
反义词:frosty, freezing
