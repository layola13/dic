discordant:/dɪˈskɔːrdənt/ | adj. | 不和谐的，不一致的 | Lacking harmony or agreement; conflicting. | The discordant sounds of the construction site made it difficult to concentrate. 建筑工地不和谐的声音使人难以集中注意力。| The committee's discordant views made it hard to reach a decision. 委员会不一致的意见使得难以达成决议。| 近义词: dissonant, clashing, conflicting, inharmonious, jarring | 反义词: harmonious, consonant, agreeable, concordant | 词性: 形容词

discordance:/dɪˈskɔːrdəns/ | n. | 不和谐，不一致 | Lack of agreement or harmony. | There was a marked discordance between his words and his actions. 他的言行之间存在明显的矛盾。| The discordance in the data made it difficult to draw any firm conclusions. 数据的不一致使得难以得出任何确定的结论。| 近义词: disagreement, conflict, dissonance, incongruity | 反义词: harmony, agreement, concordance, consistency | 词性: 名词

discordantly:/dɪˈskɔːrdəntli/ | adv. | 不和谐地，不一致地 | In a discordant manner; lacking harmony. | The instruments played discordantly, creating a cacophony of sound. 乐器演奏得非常不和谐，制造了一片嘈杂的声音。| The brothers argued discordantly, their voices echoing through the house. 兄弟俩吵得不可开交，他们的声音在房子里回荡。| 近义词: dissonantly, clashingly, conflictingly, inharmoniously | 反义词: harmoniously, consonantly, agreeably, concordantly | 词性: 副词

discordous:/dɪˈskɔːrdəs/ | adj. | 不和谐的，不一致的 | (Synonym of discordant) Lacking harmony or agreement; conflicting. |  The discordous political climate made it difficult to pass any legislation. 不和谐的政治气氛使得难以通过任何立法。|  Her discordous laughter grated on his nerves. 她刺耳的笑声让他神经紧张。| 近义词: dissonant, clashing, conflicting, inharmonious, jarring, discordant | 反义词: harmonious, consonant, agreeable, concordant | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From Late Latin *discordiosus, from Latin discordis (“discordant, disagreeing”), from dis- (“apart, away”) + cordis (genitive of cor, “heart”).
