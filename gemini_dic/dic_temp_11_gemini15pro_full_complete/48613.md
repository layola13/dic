defectless:/ˈdiːfektlɪs/ | adj. | 无瑕的，完美的 | Free from defects; flawless. |  This company is known for producing defectless products. 这家公司以生产完美无瑕的产品而闻名。| The diamond was absolutely defectless. 这颗钻石绝对完美无瑕。| 近义词：flawless, perfect, impeccable, immaculate | 反义词：defective, flawed, imperfect, faulty | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From defect + -less.  "Defect" comes from the Latin "defectus," meaning "failure, desertion, revolt," past participle of "deficere" ("to fail, desert, revolt").  "-less" is a suffix meaning "without."
