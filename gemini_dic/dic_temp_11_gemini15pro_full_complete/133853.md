nongrains:/ˈnɒnˌɡreɪnz/ | n. | 非谷物，非粮食作物 | Crops that are not grains, such as vegetables, fruits, and legumes. |  Example: Farmers are encouraged to diversify and grow more nongrains. 鼓励农民多样化种植，种植更多非谷物作物。| Example: Nongrains are an important part of a balanced diet. 非谷物是均衡饮食的重要组成部分。 | Synonyms: non-cereals,  | Antonyms: grains, cereals | noun
