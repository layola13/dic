keeldrags:/ˈkiːldræɡz/
noun
龙骨擦痕; 龙骨擦伤 (尤指船底的)
Scratches or scrapes on a boat's keel, especially caused by running aground.
The boat sustained some keeldrags while navigating the shallow reef. 这艘船在浅礁航行时，龙骨受到了一些擦伤。

keeldrags:/ˈkiːldræɡz/
noun
(比喻) 阻碍; 拖累; 包袱
(Figurative) Something that hinders progress or acts as a burden.
Bureaucracy can be a real keeldrag on innovation. 官僚主义对于创新来说可能是一个真正的阻碍。

keeldrags:/ˈkiːldræɡz/
noun
(非正式) 令人不快或令人尴尬的事情
(Informal) Something unpleasant or embarrassing.
The whole incident was a major keeldrag for the company. 整个事件对公司来说是一件非常尴尬的事情。


近义词:hindrance, impediment, obstacle, burden, embarrassment, scrape, scratch
反义词:advantage, benefit, help, boon, asset
