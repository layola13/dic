hallucinogen:/həˈluːsɪnədʒən/ /həˈluːsɪnəˌdʒɛn/ | n. | 致幻剂；迷幻药 |A drug that causes hallucinations (seeing or hearing things that are not really there). |  The shaman used a hallucinogen to enter a trance state.  萨满使用致幻剂进入恍惚状态。

The effects of the hallucinogen wore off after a few hours. 致幻剂的效果几个小时后就消失了。 | synonyms: psychedelic, psychotomimetic,  psychedelic drug | antonyms:  antipsychotic, neuroleptic | noun
