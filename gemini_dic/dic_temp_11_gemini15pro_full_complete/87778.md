hiatuses:/haɪˈeɪtəsiz/
n.
中断，空隙，裂缝
A pause or gap in a sequence, series, or process.
The band took a two-year hiatus after their lead singer left.  这支乐队在主唱离开后中断了两年。
There was a long hiatus in the negotiations.  谈判中断了很长时间。
近义词：break, gap, interruption, pause, lacuna
反义词：continuation, continuity
词性：名词


hiatus:/haɪˈeɪtəs/
n.
中断，空隙，裂缝
A pause or gap in a sequence, series, or process.
After a five-year hiatus, the festival is returning.  在中断五年之后，这个节日又回来了。
The writer's long hiatus ended with the publication of her new novel.  这位作家在出版了新小说后结束了长时间的写作中断。
近义词：break, gap, interruption, pause, lacuna
反义词：continuation, continuity
词性：名词

词源: From Latin *hiatus*, from *hiare* "to gape, yawn."
