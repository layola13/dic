keenen: /ˈkiːnən/ | adj. | 敏锐的，敏捷的，热心的 | Having or showing mental sharpness and alertness; quick and clever; enthusiastic and eager. | He has a keen eye for detail. 他对细节有敏锐的眼光。|  sharp, acute, astute, perceptive, eager, enthusiastic | dull, obtuse, apathetic | adjective

keenan: /ˈkiːnən/ | n. | 基南（姓氏）| A surname. |  Keenan Ivory Wayans is a famous American actor and comedian. 基南·艾弗里·韦恩斯是一位著名的美国演员和喜剧演员。| N/A | N/A | noun


Etymology:
From Middle English *kene, from Old English cēne (“bold, brave, sharp, keen”), from Proto-Germanic *kōniz (“brave, clever, wise”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵenh₁-  (“to know, recognize”).  Cognate with Dutch keen (“bold, brave, sharp”), German kühn (“bold, daring”), Old Norse kønn (“able, clever, wise”).
