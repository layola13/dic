decommercialize:/diːkəˈmɜːʃəlaɪz/
verb
去商业化
To reduce or eliminate the commercial aspects of something; to make something less focused on profit or market forces.
The government decided to decommercialize public broadcasting to protect it from advertising pressures. 政府决定将公共广播去商业化，以使其免受广告压力的影响。
They are trying to decommercialize Christmas by focusing on its religious significance. 他们试图通过关注圣诞节的宗教意义来淡化其商业色彩。
近义词:demonetize, deprivatize
反义词:commercialize
词性:verb
