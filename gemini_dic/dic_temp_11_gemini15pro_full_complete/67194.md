expression's:/ɪkˈspreʃənz/ | n. | 表情；表达；措辞 |  The plural form of "expression," referring to the ways in which thoughts, feelings, or ideas are shown or communicated.  Derived from the Late Middle English "expressioun," from Old French, and ultimately from Latin "expressio," meaning "a pressing out" or "representation." | Her facial expressions revealed her true feelings. 她的面部表情暴露了她的真实感受。 | synonyms: looks, appearances, countenances | antonyms: concealments, suppressions | noun
