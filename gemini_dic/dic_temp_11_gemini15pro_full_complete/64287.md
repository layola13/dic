equitemporaneous:/ˌiːkwɪtɛmˈpɔːreɪniəs/ /ˌiːkwɪˌtɛmpəˈreɪniəs/
adjective
同时发生的，同时期的
Happening or existing at the same time.
The two events were equitemporaneous, occurring within the same week.  这两个事件是同时发生的，发生在同一周内。
The equitemporaneous development of writing in different cultures is a fascinating area of study.  不同文化中文字的同时期发展是一个引人入胜的研究领域。
近义词:  concurrent, simultaneous, synchronous
反义词:  asynchronous, non-concurrent
词性: adjective
