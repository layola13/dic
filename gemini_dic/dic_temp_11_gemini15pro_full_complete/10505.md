appointing:/əˈpɔɪntɪŋ/
adjective
任命的；指定的；有任命权的
Relating to the act of appointing someone to a position or office.  Giving someone a particular job or role.
The appointing committee chose a new CEO. 任命委员会选出了一位新的首席执行官。

The president has the appointing power for several key positions. 总统有权任命几个关键职位。
近义词:designating, naming, nominating, selecting, commissioning
反义词:dismissing, firing, removing

词性: adjective
