一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing over there? = 他在那里做什么?

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned recently? = 你最近学了什么？
What has she been up to? = 她最近在忙什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all day? = 你一整天都在做什么？
What has he been reading lately?  = 他最近一直在读什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃了什么？
What time did she arrive? = 她什么时候到的？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing last night? = 你昨晚在做什么？
What was she wearing at the party? =  她在派对上穿了什么？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I called? = 在我打电话之前你做了什么？
What had she told him before the meeting? = 会议前她告诉他什么了？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been studying before you changed your major? = 你转专业前一直在学什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久?

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by the end of the day? = 你到今天结束前会完成什么？
What will she have achieved by the time she's thirty? = 到她三十岁时，她会取得什么成就？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做什么五年了？
What will they have been researching by the end of the grant period? = 到拨款期结束时，他们将一直在研究什么？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？
What would she say if she were here? = 如果她在这里，她会说什么？

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在对这个问题采取什么措施？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what you mean. = 她不明白你的意思。
