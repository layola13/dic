causatives:/ˈkɔːzətɪvz/ | n. | 使役动词 | Verbs that cause something to happen, often involving someone making or persuading another person to do something. | She had her hair cut. 她把头发剪了. |  persuade, compel, induce | dissuade, prevent | noun

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I have my car washed every week. = 我每周都洗车。（指使别人洗）
He makes his children do their homework every day. = 他每天都让他的孩子们做作业。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am having my house painted. = 我正在让人粉刷我的房子。
She is getting her nails done. = 她正在做指甲。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have had my car repaired. = 我已经把我的车修好了。
He has made his son clean his room. = 他已经让他儿子打扫房间了。
