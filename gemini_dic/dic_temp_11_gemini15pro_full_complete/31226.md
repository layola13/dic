chant:/tʃænt/ | n. v. | 圣歌，吟唱；反复唱，反复说 | A short simple song with few notes, typically sung by a group of people. To sing or intone (a chant). | The monks chanted their prayers. 僧侣们吟诵着他们的祈祷。 |  They chanted slogans at the demonstration. 他们在示威游行中反复喊着口号。| 近义词:sing, intone, chorus | 反义词: whisper, murmur | 词性: noun, verb | 词源: From Old French chanter, from Latin cantare "to sing," frequentative of canere "to sing."
