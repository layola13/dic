fusions:/ˈfjuːʒənz/ | n. | 融合，熔合，聚变 | The process or result of joining two or more things together to form a single entity. |  The restaurant offers a fusion of Asian and European cuisine.  这家餐厅提供亚洲和欧洲菜肴的融合。 | synonyms: blends, mergers, combinations, amalgamations, syntheses | antonyms: separations, divisions, fissions | noun

/ˈfjuːʒənz/ | n. | 熔化，熔解 | The process of a solid becoming liquid by heat.  (Less common usage in this sense compared to the first definition) | The fusions of the metal created a strong bond. 金属的熔合创造了牢固的结合。(在这个意义上不如第一个定义常用) | synonyms: melting, liquefaction | antonyms: solidification, freezing | noun
