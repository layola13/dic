igniting:/ɪɡˈnaɪtɪŋ/
verb
点燃，引发
the process of setting something on fire or starting a combustion
The chef is igniting the stove with a match. 厨师正在用火柴点燃炉子。
kindling, sparking, firing
extinguishing, quenching
v.
