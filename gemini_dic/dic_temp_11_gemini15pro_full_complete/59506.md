easting:/ˈiːstɪŋ/
n.
东距, 向东的距离
the distance eastward from a fixed meridian, especially as one of the coordinates used to define a point on a map.
The easting of the marker is 500 meters.  这个标记的东距是500米。
longitude, x-coordinate
westing
noun
