extravagant: /ɪkˈstrævəɡənt/ | adj. | 奢侈的，浪费的，过度的 | Spending more than is reasonable or necessary; exceeding normal bounds. | She has extravagant tastes in clothes. 她对服装的品味很奢侈。

The party was an extravagant affair with hundreds of guests and mountains of food.  这个派对非常奢华，有数百位客人和堆积如山的食物。

近义词: lavish, luxurious, excessive, profuse | 反义词: frugal, economical, thrifty, modest | 词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French extravagant, from Latin extravagantem ‘wandering beyond’, from extra- ‘outside’ + vagari ‘wander’.
