disperse:/dɪˈspɜːrs/ (US), /dɪˈspɜːs/ (UK)| v. |分散，散开，驱散|To scatter or spread widely. |The crowd dispersed after the concert.  人群在音乐会后散去。| The police dispersed the protesters with tear gas. 警察用催泪瓦斯驱散了抗议者。| synonyms: scatter, dissipate, distribute | antonyms: gather, collect, assemble | verb


disperse:/dɪˈspɜːrs/ (US), /dɪˈspɜːs/ (UK)| adj. |分散的|Spread over a wide area. | This plant has small, disperse flowers. 这种植物开着分散的小花。|The disperse nature of the evidence made it difficult to build a case.  证据的分散性使得难以立案。| synonyms: scattered, spread out | antonyms: concentrated, clustered | adjective
