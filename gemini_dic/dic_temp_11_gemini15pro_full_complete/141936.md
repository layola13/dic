ordinative:/ˈɔːrdɪnətɪv/
adjective
序列的，顺序的
Relating to or establishing order or sequence; assigning to a place in a series.
The ordinative numbers indicate the position of something in a list, such as first, second, third.  序列数表示列表中某事物的位置，例如第一、第二、第三。
The teacher explained the ordinative principles of grammar to the students. 老师向学生们解释了语法的顺序原则。
近义词: sequential, ordered
反义词: random, disordered
词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *ordinativus, from Latin ordinare "to set in order," from ordo "order."
