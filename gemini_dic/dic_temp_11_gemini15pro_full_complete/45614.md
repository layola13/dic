currycomb:/ˈkɜːriˌkəʊm/ (US) /ˈkʌriˌkəʊm/ (UK)| n. vt.|马梳，金属马梳；用马梳梳|A currycomb is a metal comb with rows of serrated teeth or ridges, used for grooming horses.  It can also refer to the act of using this tool.|
The stable boy used a currycomb to remove the mud from the horse's coat. 马僮用马梳清除马毛上的泥巴。

He currycombed the horse until its coat shone. 他用马梳梳理马匹，直到它的皮毛闪闪发亮。


|近义词：grooming tool, horse comb|反义词: N/A|词性: noun, verb|

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *curykembe, from Old French coriecombe, from corier (“to curry, prepare leather”) + combe (“comb”).  The “curry” in currycomb is related to the word “currier,” someone who prepares leather.
