mud-slinging: /ˈmʌdˌslɪŋɪŋ/
n.
中文翻译：诽谤，恶意中伤
英文解析：The act of making malicious and untrue statements about someone, especially to damage their reputation.
例句：The election campaign descended into mud-slinging. 选举活动沦为互相诽谤。
例句：He engaged in mud-slinging to discredit his opponent. 他进行人身攻击以败坏对手的名誉。
近义词：slander, defamation, libel, character assassination
反义词：praise, commendation
词性：名词
