hypertrophy:/haɪˈpɜːrtrəfi/ /haɪˈpɝːtrəfi/| n.| 肥大，过度生长|An enlargement of an organ or tissue from the increase in size of its cells.| Regular exercise can lead to muscle hypertrophy.  规律的锻炼会导致肌肉肥大。|Synonyms: enlargement, overgrowth, swelling|Antonyms: atrophy, wasting, shrinkage|noun


hypertrophy:/haɪˈpɜːrtrəfi/ /haɪˈpɝːtrəfi/| v. |(使)肥大，(使)过度生长|To undergo or cause to undergo hypertrophy.|The heart muscle can hypertrophy due to high blood pressure.  由于高血压，心肌可能会肥大。|Synonyms: enlarge, swell, expand|Antonyms: shrink, atrophy, wither|verb
