hectic:/ˈhɛktɪk/| adj.| 忙碌的，紧张的，狂热的|Full of incessant or frantic activity.|例句：I've had a hectic day. 我今天过得很忙碌。|例句：She leads a hectic life. 她过着忙碌的生活。|近义词：frantic, busy, feverish|反义词：calm, peaceful, relaxing|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Greek *hektikos* "habitual," from *hexis* "state of body, condition, habit." Originally referred to a continuous fever, later extended to any kind of feverish activity.
