gourdiness:/ˈɡɔːrdinəs/
n.| 葫芦状, 膨胀, 似葫芦|The gourdiness of the fruit made it difficult to hold.  这种水果的葫芦形状使它很难拿住。|
gourdiness is the quality of resembling a gourd, especially in being swollen or rounded. Gourdiness can refer to the physical shape of something, resembling the rounded, bulbous form of a gourd. It can also describe a swollen or distended appearance, often implying an unnatural or unhealthy enlargement.|The doctor noted the gourdiness of the patient's abdomen. 医生注意到病人腹部鼓胀。|近义词：roundness, swelling, protuberance|反义词:flatness, slenderness|词性:名词

词源: From gourd + -iness.  来自gourd（葫芦）+ -iness（名词后缀，表示状态或性质）。
