off-loading:/ˌɒfˈləʊdɪŋ/ /ˌɔːfˈloʊdɪŋ/
n.|卸载，卸货，减负|The act of unloading something, such as cargo from a ship or truck, or responsibilities from a person. |The off-loading of the cargo took several hours. 货物的卸载花了几个小时。| synonyms: unloading, discharging, transferring | antonyms: loading, charging | noun

off-load:/ˌɒfˈləʊd/ /ˌɔːfˈloʊd/
v.|卸载，卸货，减负，转嫁(责任等)|To unload something, such as cargo from a ship or truck, or responsibilities from a person. |They off-loaded the goods onto the dock. 他们把货物卸到码头上。| synonyms: unload, discharge, transfer | antonyms: load, charge | verb
