bigfoot:/ˈbɪɡˌfʊt/| n.|大脚怪|A large hairy humanoid creature believed by some to inhabit forested regions of the United States and Canada.  It is also called Sasquatch.|例句：Many people claim to have seen Bigfoot, but there is no concrete evidence. 很多人声称见过大脚怪，但没有确凿的证据。

例句：The search for Bigfoot continues to fascinate cryptozoologists. 对大脚怪的搜寻继续吸引着神秘动物学家。|近义词：Sasquatch, Yeti, Abominable Snowman|反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it's a mythical creature) |词性:noun
