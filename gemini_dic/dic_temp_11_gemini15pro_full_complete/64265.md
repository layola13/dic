equipped: /ɪˈkwɪpt/ (ɪˈkwɪpt) | adj. | 配备的，装备的 | supplied with the necessary items for a particular purpose |  例句：The laboratory is equipped with state-of-the-art technology.  实验室配备了最先进的技术。| 近义词: furnished, outfitted, prepared, ready | 反义词: unequipped, unprepared, lacking | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Old French *equiper, from esquiper (“to ship, embark”), from Middle Dutch schippen (“to ship”).  The original meaning was "to fit out a ship", later generalized to "to furnish".
