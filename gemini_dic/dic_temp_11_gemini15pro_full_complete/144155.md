outshines:/ˌaʊtˈʃaɪnz/
verb
胜过，超过；使…相形见绌
to be much more impressive, successful, or important than someone or something else
Her performance outshone all the others. 她的表演胜过所有其他人。
surpass, eclipse, overshadow, excel
be inferior to, pale in comparison to
词性:动词
