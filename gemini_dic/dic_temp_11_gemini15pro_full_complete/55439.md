distinctivenesses:/dɪˈstɪŋktɪvnɪsɪz/
n.
特殊性，独特性，显著性
The quality of being distinct or easily distinguishable.
The distinctivenesses of each region's cuisine make traveling a culinary adventure.  每个地区菜肴的独特性使旅行成为一次烹饪冒险。
The artist's work is marked by its distinctivenesses of style. 这位艺术家的作品以其独特的风格而著称。
Synonyms: individuality, peculiarity, uniqueness, singularity, difference
Antonyms: similarity, commonality, sameness, uniformity
noun
