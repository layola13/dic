northernism: /ˈnɔːrðənɪzəm/ (US) /ˈnɔːðənɪzəm/ (UK)
|n.|北方主义；北方方言；北方人的特征|A custom, belief, or habit peculiar to the north, especially the northern part of a particular country.  A word or phrase characteristic of the dialects spoken in the north of a region or country.|He spoke with a broad northernism. 他说话带有浓重的北方口音。|近义词: dialect, regionalism, localism|反义词: southernism|词性: 名词
