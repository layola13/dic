lavic:/ˈlævɪk/ | adj. | 火山的；熔岩的 | Of or pertaining to lava; formed from lava. | The lavic flows created a new landscape. 熔岩流创造了新的景观。| The lavic rock was still warm. 熔岩岩石仍然很温暖。 | volcanic, igneous | sedimentary | adjective

Etymology: From Latin *lavicus, from *lava ("lava").  The word "lava" itself likely comes from the Italian word *lava, from the Latin word *labes ("a fall, slide, lapse").
