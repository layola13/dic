kerf:/kɜːrf/| n.|切口，锯缝，刻痕|The kerf of the saw blade is very thin. 锯片的切口非常薄。|例句：The laser made a precise kerf in the metal. 激光在金属上制造了一个精确的切口。

例句：The carpenter measured the kerf of the saw before cutting the wood. 木匠在切割木头之前测量了锯的切口。|近义词：cut, groove, notch, incision|反义词:|noun
