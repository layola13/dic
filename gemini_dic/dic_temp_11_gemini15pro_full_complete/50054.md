ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *denoncier*  (Modern French *dénoncer*), from Latin *dēnūntiāre*  "to announce, proclaim, denounce," from *dē-* "down, completely" + *nūntiāre* "to announce," from *nūntius* "messenger."
