isospondyli:/ˌaɪsoʊˈspɒndɪlaɪ/ | n. |等椎鱼类 |A large group of teleost fishes characterized by having vertebrae with similar structure throughout the vertebral column.  They are considered to be a more primitive group compared to the Ostariophysi.  This group includes salmon, trout, herring, and eels among others.| Salmon are members of the Isospondyli. 鲑鱼是等椎鱼类的一员。| The herring, another isospondyl, is an important food fish.  鲱鱼，另一种等椎鱼，是一种重要的食用鱼。| 近义词:  (No direct synonym - refers to a specific taxonomic group) | 反义词: (No direct antonym - could be contrasted with Ostariophysi, a more derived group of teleosts) | 词性: noun


Etymology:
iso- (Greek): equal, same
spondylos (Greek): vertebra
-i (Latin): plural suffix
