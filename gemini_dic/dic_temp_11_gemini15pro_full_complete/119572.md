microtubules: /ˌmaɪkroʊˈtjuːbjuːlz/ | n. | 微管 |  Microscopic tubular structures present in the cytoplasm of cells, composed of tubulin. They are involved in cell division, intracellular transport, and maintaining cell shape. | Microtubules play a crucial role in cell division.  微管在细胞分裂中起着至关重要的作用。

The drugs target microtubules in cancer cells, preventing them from dividing. 这些药物靶向癌细胞中的微管，阻止它们分裂。 | synonyms: none (highly specific term) | antonyms: none (highly specific term) | noun
