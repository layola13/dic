PHRASE->
with the intention of:  为了...的目的
He went to the library with the intention of studying. 他去图书馆是为了学习。
have no intention of: 不打算...
I have no intention of quitting my job. 我不打算辞职。
good intentions: 好意
She had good intentions, but the plan backfired. 她的出发点是好的，但计划适得其反。
declare one's intentions: 宣布某人的意图
He declared his intentions to run for office. 他宣布他打算竞选公职。
hidden intentions: 隐藏的意图
She suspected he had hidden intentions. 她怀疑他有隐藏的意图。
