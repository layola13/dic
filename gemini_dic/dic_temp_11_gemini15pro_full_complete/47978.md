decillionth:/dɪˈsɪljənθ/ | adj. n. | 百万的十次方分之一；极小的 | Representing one part in a decillion (1 followed by 33 zeros in the US and 60 zeros in Britain). Extremely small; infinitesimal. |  The probability of winning the lottery is a decillionth.  赢得彩票的概率是百万的十次方分之一。| Synonym: infinitesimal, minuscule, negligible | Antonym: immense, enormous, vast |  adjective/noun

decillionth:/dɪˈsɪljənθ/ | adj. n. | 百万的十次方分之一；极小的 | The decillionth part of something is extremely small, almost immeasurable. | The chance of that happening is about a decillionth. 发生这种情况的可能性大约是百万的十次方分之一。 | Synonym: tiny, minute | Antonym: huge, massive | adjective/noun
