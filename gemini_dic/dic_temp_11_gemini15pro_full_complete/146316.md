overfeeling:/ˌoʊvərˈfiːlɪŋ/ | adj. | 过于敏感的，感情用事的 | Excessively emotional or sentimental; too readily affected by emotion. | She's a bit overfeeling and takes everything to heart. 她有点过于敏感，什么事都往心里去。| He gave an overfeeling performance that felt insincere. 他的表演过于煽情，让人感觉不真诚。 | 近义词：oversensitive, sentimental, emotional | 反义词：unemotional, apathetic, stoic | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From "over-" (excessive) + "feeling" (experiencing emotion).  Relatively modern word, gaining prominence in the 19th and 20th centuries.
