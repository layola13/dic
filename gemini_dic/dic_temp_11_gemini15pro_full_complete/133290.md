nonexpeditiously:/ˌnɒnˌɛkspɪˈdɪʃəsli/
adverb
不迅速地，缓慢地
in a way that is not expeditious; slowly.
The matter was handled nonexpeditiously, causing further delays.  这件事处理得不够迅速，造成了进一步的延误。
The paperwork was processed nonexpeditiously.  这些文书工作处理得非常缓慢。
近义词:slowly, inefficiently, sluggishly
反义词:quickly, efficiently, rapidly
词性:adverb

词源:
non- (prefix): not
expeditious (adjective): done with speed and efficiency
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs from adjectives
