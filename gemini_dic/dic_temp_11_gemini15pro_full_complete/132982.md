nonendorsement:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈdɔːrsmənt/ | n. | 不认可，不背书 |  Lack of endorsement; a statement of not supporting or approving something. | The company issued a statement of nonendorsement of the political candidate.  该公司发表声明不认可这位政治候选人。| 近义词: disapproval, rejection, disclaimer | 反义词: endorsement, approval, support | 词性: 名词

词源:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + endorsement (from endorse, meaning "to give support to").  "Non-"（表示“不”的前缀）+ endorsement（来自endorse，意为“支持”）。
