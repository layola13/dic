nonundulating: /ˌnɒnˈʌndjʊleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 非波动的，不起伏的 | Not undulating; not moving in waves. | The nonundulating landscape stretched before them, flat and monotonous.  他们面前 stretches着非波动的景观，平坦而单调。 | The surface of the lake was nonundulating, reflecting the clear sky like a mirror. 湖面波澜不惊，像镜子一样映照着晴朗的天空。 |  synonyms: flat, smooth, level, even, still | antonyms: undulating, wavy, rolling, rippling | adjective

Etymology:
From non- + undulating.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "undulating" comes from the Latin word "undulatus," the past participle of "undulare," meaning "to wave."
