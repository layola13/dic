husbanded:/ˈhʌzbəndɪd/ | v. | 节省地使用；精打细算地管理；| To use or manage economically; to conserve. |  He carefully husbanded his remaining resources. 他小心翼翼地节省他剩余的资源。| The farmer husbanded his crops to ensure a good harvest.  这位农民精打细算地管理他的作物以确保丰收。| 近义词: conserved, managed, economized, saved | 反义词: squandered, wasted | 词性: 动词

词源: From the verb "husband," originally meaning "to be a husband," later extending to managing a household and resources carefully.  Ultimately from Old Norse *húsbóndi "master of the house," from hús "house" + bóndi "occupier, dweller."
