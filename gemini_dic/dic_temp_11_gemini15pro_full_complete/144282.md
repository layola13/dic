outsparkles:/ˌaʊtˈspɑːrkəlz/
verb
胜过，光芒盖过
To shine more brightly than; to surpass in brilliance or glitter.
Her dress outsparkled all the others at the party. 她的礼服在派对上光芒盖过所有人。
outshine, outdazzle, eclipse
dim, dull
词性:动词
