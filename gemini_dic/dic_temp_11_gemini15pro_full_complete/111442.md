llds 不是一个标准的英文单词。 你可能是想查询 LLDS (Low-Level Data Storage) 或其他缩写。 请提供正确的单词或缩写以便我提供准确的解析。

如果你是想了解 "what" 这个单词，以下是解析：

what /wɒt; hwɒt/ (美式) /wɑːt/ (英式)
pron. | 什么 | Used to ask for information specifying something. | What is your name? 你叫什么名字？ |  Which, who | None | pronoun

what /wɒt; hwɒt/ (美式) /wɑːt/ (英式)
det. | 什么；多么 | Used to ask for information about something. | What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！ |  Which | None | determiner

what /wɒt; hwɒt/ (美式) /wɑːt/ (英式)
adv. | 到什么程度，在哪方面 |  To what extent? | What does it matter? 这有什么关系？ | None | None | adverb

what /wɒt; hwɒt/ (美式) /wɑːt/ (英式)
n. | 本质 | The essential nature or character of something. |  The what of the matter is we need more money.  问题的本质是我们需要更多的钱。 | essence, substance | None | noun

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does he do for a living? 他做什么工作？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? 她正在做什么晚餐？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has he done with the money? 他怎么处理那笔钱了？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has she been reading this week? 她这周一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? 你说了什么？
What happened last night? 昨晚发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about? 她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you arrived? 你来之前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? 她生病前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called? 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? 她转专业之前一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do tomorrow? 你明天要做什么？
What will happen next? 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time tomorrow? 明天这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be studying next year? 她明年会学什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by next week? 你下周前会完成什么？
What will she have accomplished by the end of the year? 她到年底会完成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做什么十年了？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 她毕业时将已经学习什么多久了？
