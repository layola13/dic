inamiable:/ɪˈneɪmiəbl/ | adj. | 不友善的，不和蔼的 | Not amiable; unfriendly; unpleasant in manner. |  She had an inamiable habit of criticizing everyone. 她有一种不友善的习惯，喜欢批评每个人。

He gave me an inamiable glare. 他恶狠狠地瞪了我一眼。


近义词:unfriendly, disagreeable, unpleasant, hostile, surly, churlish | 反义词:amiable, friendly, agreeable, pleasant, affable, cordial | 词性:形容词


词源:
From in- (not) + amiable (friendly).  The "in-" prefix negates the meaning of "amiable," which comes from Old French "amiable" and ultimately from Latin "amabilis" meaning "lovable."
