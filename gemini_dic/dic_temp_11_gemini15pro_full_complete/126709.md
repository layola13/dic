nameplate:/ˈneɪmˌpleɪt/| n. |铭牌，名牌|A small plate bearing a name, typically attached to a door or desk for identification.|The nameplate on the door read "John Smith." 门上的铭牌上写着“约翰·史密斯”。|synonyms: name tag, label, plaque|antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "anonymous" as an adjective describing the lack of a nameplate) | noun
