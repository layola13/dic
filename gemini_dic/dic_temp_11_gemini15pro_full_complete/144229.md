outslidden:/ˌaʊtˈslɪdn/
adjective
堕落的，滑落的，背离正道的
Having lapsed morally or religiously; backsliding.  Having slipped or slid outward.
He had outslidden from the path of righteousness. 他已经偏离了正道的道路。
The outslidden rocks formed a dangerous precipice. 滑落的岩石形成了一个危险的悬崖。
近义词:lapsed, backsliding, fallen, errant, wayward, relapsed
反义词:righteous, virtuous, upright, steadfast
词性:adjective

词源:  From *out-* + *slide*, past participle *slidden*.  Meaning "having slid out" literally, and figuratively "having fallen from grace."
