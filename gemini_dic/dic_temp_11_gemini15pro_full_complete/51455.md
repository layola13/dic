detonate:/ˈdɛtəneɪt/ (US), /ˈdɛtəneɪt/ (UK)| v. |引爆，爆炸|To cause (something, such as an explosive device) to explode.|The terrorists detonated the bomb in a crowded marketplace. 恐怖分子在拥挤的市场引爆了炸弹。|explode, blast, trigger|defuse, disarm|verb
detonate:/ˈdɛtəneɪt/ (US), /ˈdɛtəneɪt/ (UK)| n. |爆炸，引爆|The act of exploding or causing something to explode.|The detonation of the bomb caused widespread damage. 炸弹的爆炸造成了广泛的破坏。|explosion, blast|implosion, suppression|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin detonatus, past participle of detonare "to thunder down," from de- "down" + tonare "to thunder."
