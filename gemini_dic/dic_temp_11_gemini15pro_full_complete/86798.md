hemastatic:/ˌhiːməˈstætɪk/ /ˌheməˈstætɪk/ | adj. n.|止血的，止血药|arresting or checking the flow of blood|The doctor applied a hemostatic dressing to the wound. 医生在伤口上敷了止血敷料。|近义词：styptic, antihemorrhagic|反义词: hemorrhagic|adjective

hemastatic:/ˌhiːməˈstætɪk/ /ˌheməˈstætɪk/ | n.|止血药|a substance that arrests bleeding|The surgeon requested a hemostatic agent to control the bleeding. 外科医生要求使用止血剂来控制出血。|近义词：styptic, coagulant|反义词: anticoagulant|noun
