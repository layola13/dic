hot-bedding:/ˈhɒtˌbɛdɪŋ/
n.
轮床制，多人共用一张床；温床；滋生地
The practice of having different people use the same bed at different times, especially between shifts at work.  Also, a situation that encourages the development of something, especially something bad.
Due to the housing shortage, some workers resorted to hot-bedding. 由于住房短缺，一些工人不得不轮流使用同一张床。
The overcrowded prison became a hotbed for disease.  过度拥挤的监狱成了疾病的温床。
Synonyms:  shared sleeping, shift sleeping, breeding ground, incubator
Antonyms:  private room, single occupancy, sterile environment
词性: 名词
