lents:/lɛnts/ | n. | 四旬斋, 大斋期; (Lents)人名 | a period of fasting and penitence traditionally observed by Christians in preparation for Easter, lasting 40 weekdays from Ash Wednesday to Holy Saturday.  It is a time of reflection and self-examination. |  Lent is a time for reflection. 四旬斋是反思的时期。|  synonyms: fast, fasting period | antonyms: feast, celebration | noun
