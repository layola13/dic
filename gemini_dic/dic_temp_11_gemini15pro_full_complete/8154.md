anti-cosmetics:/ˌænti kɒzˈmɛtɪks/ | n. | 反化妆品；反对化妆品的行为或理念 |Products or a philosophy that opposes the traditional ideals of cosmetics, often emphasizing natural beauty or rejecting artificial enhancements.  It can also refer to the movement against the perceived negative impacts of the cosmetics industry, such as animal testing or unsustainable practices.| She embraced the anti-cosmetics movement and stopped wearing makeup altogether. 她接受了反化妆品运动，完全停止了化妆。

Anti-cosmetics can also refer to ingredients that counteract the effects of cosmetics, like makeup removers. 反化妆品也可以指抵消化妆品效果的成分，例如卸妆液。 | 近义词: natural look, barefaced | 反义词: cosmetics, makeup | 词性: 名词
