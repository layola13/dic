batting:/ˈbætɪŋ/ /ˈbæɾɪŋ/ | n. |击球，打击；棉絮，铺棉 |The act of hitting a ball with a bat, especially in baseball, cricket, or softball. Also, a material used for stuffing or padding, such as quilts or mattresses.| He's known for his powerful batting.  他以强力击球闻名。The quilt is filled with soft batting. 这床被子填充了柔软的棉絮。 | 近义词：hitting, striking (for the sports context); stuffing, padding, wadding (for the material context) | 反义词: (no direct antonym for the act of batting; for the material, perhaps "covering" in some contexts, though not a true opposite) | 词性: noun

batting:/ˈbætɪŋ/ /ˈbæɾɪŋ/ | adj. |击球的，打击的 |Relating to the act of batting.| His batting average is impressive. 他的击球率令人印象深刻。| 近义词：hitting | 反义词: (no direct antonym) | 词性: adjective
