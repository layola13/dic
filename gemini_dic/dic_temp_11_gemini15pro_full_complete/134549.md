nonliteral:/nɒnˈlɪtərəl/ /nɑːnˈlɪtərəl/ | adj. | 非字面的，比喻的 | Not meant to be taken literally; figurative. |
Her poems are full of nonliteral language, using metaphors and symbolism to convey deeper meanings. 她的诗歌充满了非字面语言，使用隐喻和象征来表达更深层的含义。

The phrase "break a leg" is a nonliteral expression used to wish someone good luck.  “断条腿”这个短语是一种非字面表达，用来祝愿某人好运。

近义词: figurative, metaphorical, symbolic
反义词: literal, exact

词性: adjective
