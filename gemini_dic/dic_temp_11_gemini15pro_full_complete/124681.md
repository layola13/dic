movers:/ˈmuːvərz/| n.|搬运工；搬家公司；推动者；原动力|People or a company that help people move their belongings from one place to another.  A person or thing that initiates or facilitates change or progress. |The movers arrived early in the morning. 搬运工一大早就到了。| synonyms: removal company, van line, transporter, porters, haulers, shifters, carriers,  instigators, catalysts,  drivers,  promoters | antonyms: stayers, settlers, inhibitors, obstructors |noun
