overoffend:/ˌoʊvərəˈfɛnd/| v. | 过度冒犯；过分得罪 | To offend someone to an excessive degree; to be overly sensitive to offense. |  He overoffended his colleagues with his insensitive jokes. 他用不敏感的笑话过度冒犯了他的同事。

She tends to overoffend easily, so be careful what you say. 她很容易被过度冒犯，所以说话要小心。| 近义词：offend, insult, slight | 反义词：please, delight, flatter |词性：动词
