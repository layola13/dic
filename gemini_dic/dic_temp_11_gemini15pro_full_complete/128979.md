nip off:/nɪp ɒf/ | phrasal verb |掐掉，摘掉；迅速离开|To remove something by pinching or squeezing it off, often referring to plants or flowers. Also, to leave quickly and suddenly. |例句：She nipped off the dead leaves from the plant. 她把植物上的枯叶摘掉了。

例句：I need to nip off to the shops for a moment. 我需要赶紧去商店一下。|近义词：pinch off, snip off, pop off, duck out |反义词: stay, remain |词性: 动词短语
