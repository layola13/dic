dialoguize:/ˈdaɪəlɒɡaɪz/ /ˈdaɪəlɑːɡaɪz/| v. |使…对话化；使…具有对话形式|To put into the form of a dialogue; to express in dialogue. |They dialoguized the script to make it more engaging. 他们将剧本对话化，使其更具吸引力。| Example: The author dialoguized the narrative to make it more dynamic. 作者将叙事对话化，使其更具活力。|Synonyms: dramatize, converse, discuss|Antonyms: monologuize, narrate|Verb
