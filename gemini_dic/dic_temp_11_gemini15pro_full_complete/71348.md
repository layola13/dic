flaccid:/ˈflæsɪd/| adj.|软弱的；松弛的|Lacking firmness or strength; soft and limp.|The lettuce was old and flaccid. 生菜又老又蔫。|Synonyms: limp, soft, weak|Antonyms: firm, strong, taut|词性:形容词
