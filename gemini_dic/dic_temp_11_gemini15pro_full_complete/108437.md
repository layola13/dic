laxation:/lækˈseɪʃən/
n.| 松弛；放松；减轻；宽松；缓泻|The act or process of loosening or relaxing something, or the state of being loose or relaxed.  Also, the discharge of feces from the bowels; defecation.|例句：The laxation of the rules has led to chaos. 规则的松弛导致了混乱。|近义词：loosening, easing, relaxation, slackening, alleviation, purgation|反义词：tightening, constriction, tension|词性:名词

laxation:/lækˈseɪʃən/
n.|通便，排便|The act of emptying the bowels; defecation.|例句：The medication caused laxation. 这种药引起了通便。|近义词：bowel movement, defecation, evacuation|反义词：constipation|词性:名词
