outdazzling: /ˌaʊtˈdæzlɪŋ/
verb
使…相形见绌；胜过；光芒盖过
surpassing in dazzling quality.
Her elaborate gown outdazzled all the others at the ball. 她的华丽礼服使舞会上所有其他人的礼服都黯然失色。
synonyms: outshine, eclipse, surpass, transcend
antonyms: pale in comparison, be overshadowed by
词性: 动词
