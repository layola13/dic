kinging:/ˈkɪŋɪŋ/
verb
统治;像国王一样行事
Acting like a king; ruling.
He's been kinging it up in the new office since his promotion.  自从升职后，他在新办公室里一直摆出一副国王的架势。
kinging around all day
整天无所事事地闲逛
近义词:ruling, reigning, governing, lording
反义词:serving, obeying, submitting
词性:v.


词源:  Derived from the noun "king" with the suffix "-ing" to create a verb form. This is a relatively recent formation and considered informal.
