immense:/ɪˈmɛns/| adj.|巨大的，无限的|Extremely large or great, especially in scale or degree.|The universe is immense and full of mysteries. 宇宙浩瀚无垠，充满了神秘。|The task ahead is immense. 前面的任务十分艰巨。|近义词：vast, enormous, huge, gigantic, colossal|反义词：tiny, small, minute, minuscule|词性:形容词
词源: From Old French *immense (modern French immense), from Latin immensus "immeasurable," from in- "not" (see in- (1)) + mensus, past participle of metiri "to measure."
