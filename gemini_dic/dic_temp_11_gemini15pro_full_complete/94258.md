impermeability:/ɪmˌpɜːrmiəˈbɪləti/
n.|不可渗透性;不透水性|The property of a material that prevents liquids or gases from passing through it.|The raincoat's impermeability kept me dry in the downpour. 这件雨衣的防渗性使我在倾盆大雨中保持干燥。|impenetrableness, imperviousimperviousness|permeability, porosity
