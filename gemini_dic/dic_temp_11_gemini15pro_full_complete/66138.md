exceptionally:/ɪkˈsɛpʃənəli/| adv.|格外地，非常地，异常地|to a greater degree than normal; in an unusual way|The weather was exceptionally warm for the time of year.  这天气对这个时节来说异常温暖。|近义词：remarkably, extraordinarily, unusually, singularly, notably, markedly|反义词：ordinarily, normally, typically, usually|词性:副词
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin *exceptionalis, from Latin exceptio (see exception).
