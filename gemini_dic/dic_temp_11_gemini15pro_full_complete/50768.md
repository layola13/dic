desaturated:/diːˈsætʃəreɪtɪd/
adjective
不饱和的，减色的，去饱和的
Reduced in saturation; having had some or all of the color removed.
The photographer desaturated the image to give it a vintage feel. 摄影师降低了图像的饱和度，使其具有复古感。
The desaturated colors of the old photographs evoked a sense of nostalgia. 旧照片褪色的颜色唤起了一种怀旧感。
近义词: muted, dull, faded, washed-out, pale
反义词: saturated, vibrant, bright, vivid, rich
词性: adjective

desaturate:/diːˈsætʃəreɪt/
verb
使不饱和，降低饱和度，去色
To reduce the saturation of; to remove some or all of the color from.
The software can desaturate the image to create a grayscale effect.  这个软件可以降低图像的饱和度来创建一个灰度效果。
He desaturated the red in the photo to make it less intense. 他降低了照片中红色的饱和度，使其不那么强烈。
近义词: mute, dull, fade, wash out, blanch
反义词: saturate, brighten, intensify, enhance
词性: verb
