gotcha:/ˈɡɒtʃə/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːtʃə/ (US)| int. n.| gotcha (感叹词), 诡计;圈套 (名词)|An informal way of saying "I've got you", used to express satisfaction at having captured or defeated someone or uncovered their mistake.  Also used to acknowledge understanding something.|Gotcha! I knew you were hiding here!  (抓到你了！我就知道你躲在这里！)| 近义词:I got you, I understand, Aha!|反义词: I missed you|感叹词/名词
