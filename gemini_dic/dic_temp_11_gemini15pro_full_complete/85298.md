harms:/hɑːrmz/
n.|伤害，损害|Injury or damage caused to someone or something.|The storm caused significant harms to the coastal communities.  暴风雨对沿海社区造成了重大损害。|近义词：damage, injury, detriment, impairment|反义词：benefit, advantage|词性:名词

harms:/hɑːrmz/
v. (third-person singular simple present harms, present participle harming, simple past and past participle harmed)|损害，伤害|To cause injury or damage to someone or something.|Smoking harms your health.  吸烟有害健康。|近义词：damage, injure, hurt, impair|反义词：benefit, help, improve|词性:动词
