BYOD:/ˌbiː waɪ əʊ ˈdiː/ (also /ˌbiː waɪ oʊ ˈdiː/) | n. |自带设备|Bring Your Own Device:  The practice of allowing employees to use their personal computers, smartphones, or other devices for work purposes.| Our company has a BYOD policy, which allows us to use our own laptops. 我们的公司有自带设备的政策，允许我们使用自己的笔记本电脑。

|例句：Many companies are now adopting BYOD policies to save on IT costs. 许多公司现在采用自带设备政策来节省IT成本。

|例句：BYOD can pose security risks if not managed properly.  如果管理不当，自带设备可能会带来安全风险。


|近义词：personal device use, employee-owned device|反义词:corporate-owned device|词性:noun (abbreviation)
