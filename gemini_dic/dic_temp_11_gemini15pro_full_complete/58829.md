dupery:/ˈdjuːpəri/
n.
欺骗; 蒙骗; 受骗
The act of deceiving someone.
He was a victim of dupery. 他是欺骗的受害者。

The salesman's dupery cost the elderly woman her life savings.  这个销售员的欺骗让这位老妇人损失了毕生积蓄。

近义词:deception, fraud, trickery, chicanery, guile
反义词:honesty, sincerity, truthfulness
词性:名词

词源: From Middle French *duperie*, from *duper* (“to deceive”), from Old French *de* + *uper* (“to dupe”), of uncertain origin.
