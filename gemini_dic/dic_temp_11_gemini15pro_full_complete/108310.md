laurer:/ˈlɔːrər/| n. |劳雷尔码，劳雷尔编码|A type of barcode symbology developed by George Laurer at IBM. It is widely used in retail inventory management and is often found printed on product packaging.|The product's price was scanned using the laurer code printed on the bottom.  该产品的价格是使用印在底部的劳雷尔码扫描的。|近义词:Universal Product Code (UPC), barcode|反义词:None|noun

词源: Named after its inventor, George Laurer, an IBM engineer.
