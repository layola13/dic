nonculture:/ˈnɒnˌkʌltʃər/ |n.| 非培养物，非文化 |A substance or environment that does not support the growth of microorganisms or cells; also, a condition or way of life that is considered to be unrefined or lacking in cultural development.| The scientist placed the sample in a nonculture medium to see if growth would be inhibited. 科学家将样本放置在非培养基中，以观察其生长是否会受到抑制。| The isolated tribe lived in a state of nonculture, having little contact with the outside world.  这个与世隔绝的部落生活在一种非文化状态中，很少与外界接触。 | near synonyms:  sterile medium, barren environment; unrefined state, uncultivated state | antonyms: culture medium, fertile environment; cultured state, cultivated state | noun
