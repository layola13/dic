massaging:/məˈsɑːʒɪŋ/
verb|按摩，揉捏|The act of applying pressure or friction to someone's body with the hands, especially to relieve tension or pain.|She was massaging her sore neck. 她正在按摩她酸痛的脖子。|近义词：rubbing, kneading, manipulating|反义词：hitting, striking|v.

massaging:/məˈsɑːʒɪŋ/
adjective|按摩的|Relating to or involving massage.|She used a massaging lotion. 她用了一种按摩乳液。|近义词：therapeutic, soothing|反义词：irritating, harsh|adj.
