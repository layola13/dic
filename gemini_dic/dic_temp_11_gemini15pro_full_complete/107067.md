lacys:/ˈleɪsiz/
adjective|花边的；饰以花边的|decorated with lace; made of lace.|The bride wore a beautiful lacys gown.  新娘穿着一件漂亮的花边礼服。|近义词: lacy, ornate, decorated |反义词: plain, simple|词性:adjective
Etymology: From lace + -y (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Latin *lacea, from laqueus "noose, snare."
