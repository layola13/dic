holeyer:/ˈhoʊliər/| n.|洞穴居民，穴居人|A person who lives in a hole or cave, especially a prehistoric human.|例句：The holeyer painted images of bison on the cave walls. 穴居人在洞穴墙壁上画了野牛的图像。

例句：Archaeologists discovered the remains of a holeyer in the cave. 考古学家在洞穴里发现了穴居人的遗骸。|近义词：caveman, cave dweller, troglodyte|反义词:modern human, contemporary|词性:noun
