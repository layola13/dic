abraxas:/əˈbræksəs/
n.
阿布拉克萨斯；驱魔符；诺斯替教中的神
A word of mystic meaning, used as a charm or amulet. In Gnosticism, the name of a supreme being, composed of Greek letters representing the number 365, the number of days in the year.
Example: He wore an abraxas amulet for protection. 他戴着阿布拉克萨斯护身符以求保佑。

Example:  The abraxas symbol was carved into the ancient stone.  阿布拉克萨斯符号被刻在了古老的石头上。

近义词: amulet, charm, talisman
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "curse" in a contextual sense)
词性: noun
