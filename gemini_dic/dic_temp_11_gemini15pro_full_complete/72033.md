flippantnesses:/ˈflɪpəntnəsɪz/
n.
轻率;轻浮;无礼
The state or quality of being flippant; remarks or actions that are disrespectful, lacking seriousness, or inappropriately casual.
His flippantnesses during the ceremony were disrespectful.  他在仪式上的轻浮举止很不尊重人。
flippancy, frivolity, impertinence, levity
seriousness, respectfulness, solemnity, gravity
