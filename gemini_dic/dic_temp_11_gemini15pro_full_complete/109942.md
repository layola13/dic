liberal:/ˈlɪbərəl/ | adj. n. |自由主义的；自由主义者；慷慨的；大量的|favoring individual liberty, free trade, and moderate political and social reform;|
例句：He has a liberal attitude towards immigration.  他对移民持自由主义态度。

例句：She made a liberal donation to the charity. 她向慈善机构慷慨捐赠。

近义词：progressive, open-minded, generous, abundant|
反义词：conservative, narrow-minded, stingy, meager|
词性: adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin liberalis "noble, gracious, generous, pertaining to a freeman," from liber "free," from PIE root *leudh- "people." The political sense emerged in the late 18th century.
