misrelate:/ˌmɪsrɪˈleɪt/ | v. | 讲述错误；错误地联系 | To relate incorrectly or inappropriately; to show a wrong or inappropriate connection between. | He misrelated the events, leading to much confusion. 他讲述事件时搞错了，导致了很多混乱。 | The journalist misrelated the cause and effect of the incident. 记者错误地关联了事件的因果关系。 |  近义词: misrepresent, distort, falsify | 反义词: represent accurately, portray truthfully | 词性: 动词
