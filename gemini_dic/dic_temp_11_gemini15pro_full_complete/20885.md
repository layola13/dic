blazon:/ˈbleɪzən/ | v. |  宣扬；夸耀；(纹章)用图案装饰 | To proclaim or make widely known, especially something considered praiseworthy; to display prominently or vividly, as on a coat of arms. | The knight's shield was blazoned with a golden lion. 骑士的盾牌上饰有金狮图案。| synonyms: proclaim, announce, broadcast, declare, publicize, trumpet, advertise, promulgate | antonyms: conceal, hide, suppress, obscure | verb

blazoned:/ˈbleɪzənd/ | adj. |  纹章图案的；被纹章装饰的；公开宣布的 | Displayed prominently or vividly; adorned with or as if with heraldic bearings. |  His blazoned heroism was celebrated throughout the land. 他公开宣扬的英雄事迹举国闻名。 | synonyms: emblazoned, displayed, publicized, proclaimed | antonyms: concealed, hidden, suppressed | adjective
