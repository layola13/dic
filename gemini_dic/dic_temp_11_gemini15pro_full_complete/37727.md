comparison:/kəmˈpærɪsn/ | n. | 比较，对照，比喻 | the act or process of comparing two or more people or things |  She drew a comparison between life in the city and life in the country. 她比较了城市生活和乡村生活。 | analogy, similarity, likeness | contrast, difference | noun

Etymology:  From Middle English *comparisoun, from Old French comparaison, from Latin comparātiō (“a comparing”), from comparō (“I compare”).
