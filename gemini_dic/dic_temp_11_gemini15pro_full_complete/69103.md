fauxest:/ˈfoʊɪst/| adj.|最假的，最人造的|Superlative form of faux: most artificial or fake; not genuine.  It implies a deliberate attempt to imitate something real, often with a slightly ironic or humorous tone.|That's the fauxest fur coat I've ever seen. 那是我见过的最假的皮草大衣。|Synonyms: fakest, phoniest, most artificial, most spurious|Antonyms: genuine, real, authentic, truest|adjective
