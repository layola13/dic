difficilitate:/dɪˈfɪkəlˌteɪt/
verb
使困难；阻碍
To make something more difficult.
The new regulations will difficilitate trade between the two countries. 新规定将阻碍两国之间的贸易。
近义词:hinder, impede, obstruct, complicate
反义词:facilitate, ease, simplify
词性:v.


Etymology:
From Late Latin *difficilitatus, past participle of *difficilitare (“to make difficult”), from Latin difficilis (“difficult”).  The English word was formed in the mid-19th century.
