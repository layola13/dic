dahlia: /ˈdeɪljə/ (US) /ˈdeɪliə/ (UK)| n.|大丽花，大丽菊|A brightly colored flower native to Mexico and Central America, with tuberous roots and showy flower heads.|例句：The dahlias in her garden were a riot of color. 她花园里的大丽花五彩缤纷。

例句：He gave her a bouquet of dahlias for her birthday. 他送给她一束大丽花作为生日礼物。

近义词：flower, blossom|反义词: None (Dahlia is a specific type of flower, it doesn't have a direct opposite.)|词性:noun
