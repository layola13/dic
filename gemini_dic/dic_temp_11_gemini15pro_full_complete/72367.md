flown-out:/ˈfləʊnˈaʊt/
adjective
已飞离的，已飞走的
Having departed by airplane.  This term often implies a temporary departure, especially for a specific purpose like work.
The flown-out engineers fixed the problem quickly.  那些飞来的工程师很快就解决了问题。
Synonyms: departed, left, gone
Antonyms: arrived, incoming
词性: adjective


flown-out:/ˈfləʊnˈaʊt/
verb
飞出去
To have departed by airplane.
The team has flown out to the conference. 该团队已飞往会议地点。
Synonyms: departed, left, gone
Antonyms: arrived, landed
词性: verb (past participle used as adjective or verb)
