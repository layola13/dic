inebriety:/ɪnɪˈbraɪəti/
n.
醉酒；陶醉
Drunkenness; intoxication.
He stumbled through the streets in a state of inebriety. 他醉醺醺地在街上踉跄而行。
近义词：drunkenness, intoxication, inebriation
反义词：sobriety
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin inebrietas, from Latin inebriare "to intoxicate," from in- "in" + ebrius "drunk."
