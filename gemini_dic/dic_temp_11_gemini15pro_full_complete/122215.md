misvouch:/ˌmɪsˈvaʊtʃ/
verb
错误地担保；错误地推荐
to vouch for something incorrectly; to give a false guarantee or recommendation
He misvouched for his friend's character, and the company lost a lot of money. 他错误地担保了他朋友的品格，公司因此损失了很多钱。
The salesman misvouched for the quality of the product, leading to many customer complaints.  销售员错误地保证了产品的质量，导致许多顾客投诉。
misrepresent, misstate
vouch for, guarantee
