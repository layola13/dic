myosin:/ˈmaɪəsɪn/| n.|肌球蛋白|A fibrous protein that forms (together with actin) the contractile filaments of muscle cells and is also involved in motion in other types of cells.|Muscle contraction is caused by the interaction of myosin and actin. 肌收缩是由肌球蛋白和肌动蛋白的相互作用引起的。|近义词:actomyosin|反义词:None|词性:noun

myosin:/ˈmaɪəsɪn/| n.|肌球蛋白|A fibrous protein which forms the thick filaments of muscle tissue. It works with actin to produce muscle contractions.|Myosin filaments slide along actin filaments, causing the muscle to shorten. 肌球蛋白丝沿着肌动蛋白丝滑动，导致肌肉缩短。|近义词:None|反义词:None|词性:noun
