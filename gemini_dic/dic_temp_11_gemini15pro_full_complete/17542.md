beatifically:/ˌbiːətɪfɪkəli/
adverb|幸福地；快乐地；神圣地|in a beatifically manner; showing or expressing joy. |She smiled beatifically as she held her newborn baby. 她抱着新生儿，脸上洋溢着幸福的微笑。|
例句：The sun shone beatifically on the peaceful valley. 阳光明媚地照耀着宁静的山谷。|近义词：blissfully, radiantly, serenely, joyfully|反义词：miserably, sorrowfully, wretchedly|词性:adverb

beatifically:/ˌbiːətɪfɪkəli/
Etymology: From Late Latin *beatificālis, from Latin beatus (“blessed, happy”) + -ficālis, equivalent to English beatific + -al + -ly.
词源：源自晚期拉丁语 *beatificālis，来自拉丁语 beatus（“幸福的，快乐的”）+ -ficālis，相当于英语 beatific + -al + -ly。
