hissed:/hɪst/| v.|发出嘶嘶声；发出嘘声|To make a sound like a prolonged s, as a snake, or as steam escaping.|The snake hissed at the intruder. 蛇向入侵者发出嘶嘶声。|The audience hissed their disapproval. 观众发出嘘声表示反对。|近义词：sizzled, whispered, booed|反义词: cheered, applauded|verb
