caballo:/kɑːˈbɑːjoʊ/ (US), /kəˈbæloʊ/ (UK)| n.|马|From Spanish *caballo*, from Late Latin *caballus* "horse," alteration of classical Latin *equus*.  It refers to a horse, especially a male one.|例句：El caballo galopaba por el campo. The horse galloped across the field.

例句：Mi abuelo tenía un hermoso caballo blanco. My grandfather had a beautiful white horse.|近义词：steed, courser, charger (more poetic/literary), horse|反义词:mare (female horse)|词性:noun
