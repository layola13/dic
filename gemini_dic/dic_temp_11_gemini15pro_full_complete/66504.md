exfoliate: /ɛksˈfoʊliˌeɪt/ (US) /ɛksˈfəʊliˌeɪt/ (UK)
verb
脱落；去角质
To remove dead skin cells from the surface of the skin.
She uses a scrub to exfoliate her skin twice a week. 她每周使用磨砂膏去角质两次。
The outer layers of the rock naturally exfoliate over time. 岩石的外层会随着时间的推移自然脱落。
synonyms: peel, shed, slough
antonyms: accumulate, build up
词性: 动词
