diabetes:/ˌdaɪəˈbiːtiːz/ /ˌdaɪəˈbiːtis/ | n. |糖尿病|A chronic metabolic disorder characterized by elevated levels of glucose (sugar) in the blood. |Example 1:  Diabetes is a serious disease that requires ongoing medical management.  糖尿病是一种需要持续医疗管理的严重疾病。

Example 2:  She has struggled with type 2 diabetes for many years. 她多年来一直与2型糖尿病作斗争。|Synonym: hyperglycemia (high blood sugar - refers to a symptom, not the disease itself)|Antonym: hypoglycemia (low blood sugar)|Noun
