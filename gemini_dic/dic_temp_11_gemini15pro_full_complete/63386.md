entoptic:/ɛnˈtɒptɪk/ | adj. | 内视的；眼内的 | Relating to visual perceptions caused by the structures of the eye itself, such as the shadows of blood vessels in the retina. |  Entoptic phenomena, such as floaters, are usually harmless. 内视现象，例如飞蚊症，通常是无害的。 | Entoptic images can be seen when looking at a bright, uniform surface. 当看着明亮、均匀的表面时，可以看到内视图像。|  internal, intraocular | external | adjective
