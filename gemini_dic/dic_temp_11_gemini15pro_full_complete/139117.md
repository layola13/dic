obedienciary:/oʊˌbiːdiˈɛnʃəri/
noun
忏悔神父；告解听罪司铎；宗教裁判所法官
A priest who hears confessions; a church official who decides whether someone has committed a sin.
The role of the obedientiary was crucial in monastic life.  忏悔神父在修道院生活中起着至关重要的作用。
Medieval obedientiaries held considerable power. 中世纪的宗教裁判所法官拥有相当大的权力。
Synonym: confessor, penitentiary
Antonym:  N/A (No direct antonym exists for this specialized role.)
词性: 名词
