lixiviate:/lɪkˈsɪvieɪt/
verb
沥滤；浸滤；滤去
To dissolve and wash away soluble constituents of (something, such as soil, ash, or ore) by percolation.
Rainwater lixiviated the soil, carrying minerals deeper into the ground. 雨水浸滤土壤，将矿物质带到更深的地层。
leach, percolate, elute
cement, compact
v.
