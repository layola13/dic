outgun:/ˌaʊtˈɡʌn/
verb
胜过，以火力压制
to defeat someone by having more or better weapons
The rebels were outgunned by the government troops. 叛军被政府军的火力压制住了。
outmatch, surpass, overpower
be outgunned by
verb
