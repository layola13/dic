objectlessness:/ˌɒbdʒɪktlɪsnəs/ | n. | 无目标性；无对象性；客观性 | The state of being without an object, aim, or purpose.  Lack of focus or direction.  Also, in philosophy, the state of being independent of the mind or an observer. | His objectlessness made it difficult for him to find a fulfilling career.  他漫无目的的生活状态使他难以找到一份称心的职业。 | synonyms: aimlessness, purposelessness, lack of direction, disorientation, abstraction | antonyms: purposefulness, focus, direction, orientation, concreteness | noun
