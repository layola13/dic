loosen:/ˈluːsən/ /ˈluːsn/| v. |放松，松开，变松|To make something less tight, firm, or rigid.|He loosened his tie. 他解开了领带。|近义词：relax, slacken, unfasten|反义词:tighten, fasten|动词

loosen:/ˈluːsən/ /ˈluːsn/| adj. |松的，宽松的|Not firmly fixed in place; able to be moved easily.|The screw has come loose. 螺丝松了。|近义词：slack, wobbly|反义词:tight, firm|形容词
