alestake:/ˈeɪlstɛɪk/| n.| 啤酒桩; 啤酒节; 欢乐的宴会; |A festive occasion centered around drinking ale, often involving a stake driven into the ground with a vessel placed on top.|The village held an annual alestake in the summer. 村庄每年夏天都会举行啤酒节。|近义词：festival, feast, celebration|反义词：wake, funeral|noun
