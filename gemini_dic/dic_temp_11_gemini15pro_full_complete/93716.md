immalleable:/ˌɪmæˈliːəbl/
adjective
不能锻造的；难塑造的；固执的
Not easily influenced or changed; inflexible.
The artist found the material immalleable and difficult to work with.  艺术家发现这种材料难以加工，很难塑形。
Her father's opinions were immalleable, fixed by a lifetime of rigid beliefs. 她父亲的观点根深蒂固，一辈子都抱持着僵化的信念。
近义词:inflexible, rigid, unyielding, obstinate
反义词:malleable, flexible, pliable, adaptable
词性:adjective

immalleable:/ˌɪmæˈliːəbl/
词源:Late Middle English (in the sense ‘incapable of being hammered into thin plates’): from in- + malleable.
词性:adjective
