huntedly:/ˈhʌntɪdli/| adv.|被追捕地，惶惶不安地|Being hunted or pursued; in a hunted manner.  Characterized by a state of fear and anxiety as if being pursued. |She looked around huntedly, convinced someone was following her. 她惶惶不安地环顾四周，确信有人在跟踪她。|近义词：fearfully, nervously, anxiously, apprehensively|反义词：calmly, serenely, peacefully|词性:副词

词源: From "hunted" (past participle of "hunt") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Hunt" comes from Old English *huntian, of Germanic origin.
