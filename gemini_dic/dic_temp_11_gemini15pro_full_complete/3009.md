aeration:/eəˈreɪʃən/| n.|通气，透气，曝气|The process of introducing air into a substance, such as soil or water.|例句：The aeration of the soil is important for healthy plant growth. 土壤的通气对植物的健康生长很重要。

例句：The aeration of the water helped to improve its quality. 水的曝气有助于改善其水质。|近义词：ventilation, oxygenation|反义词：suffocation, asphyxiation|词性:noun

Etymology->
From Latin *aeratio*, from aerare (“to air”), from aer (“air”).
