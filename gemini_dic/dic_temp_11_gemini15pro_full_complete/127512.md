necktieless: /ˈnɛktaɪləs/
adjective
没有戴领带的
Not wearing a necktie.
He attended the wedding necktieless, which raised a few eyebrows. 他没戴领带就参加了婚礼，这引起了一些人的侧目。
The relaxed dress code allowed for a necktieless look. 宽松的着装规定允许不戴领带。
Near Synonyms: tieless, open-necked, casually dressed
Opposite: wearing a necktie, formally dressed
Word Origin: necktie + -less (suffix indicating absence)
