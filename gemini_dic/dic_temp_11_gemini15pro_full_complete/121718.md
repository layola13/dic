misprize:/mɪsˈpraɪz/ | v. | 轻视，蔑视，低估 | To underestimate, undervalue, or scorn. | He misprized her kindness, thinking it was weakness. 他误解了她的善良，以为那是软弱。 |

例句：
The company misprized the potential of the new market.
这家公司低估了新市场的潜力。


The young artist's work was initially misprized by the critics.
这位年轻艺术家的作品最初被评论家们低估了。


近义词：disdain, despise, scorn, underestimate, undervalue | 反义词： admire, appreciate, esteem, value, overestimate | 词性：动词
