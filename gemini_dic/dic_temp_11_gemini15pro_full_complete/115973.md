PHRASE->
marry into = 嫁入/娶进 (e.g., She married into a wealthy family. 她嫁入了一个富裕家庭。)
get married = 结婚 (e.g., They got married last summer. 他们去年夏天结婚了。)
marry someone off = 把某人嫁出去 (e.g., They married their daughter off to a nice young man. 他们把女儿嫁给了一个好青年。)
marry up = 与...结婚（通常指与地位较高的人结婚）(e.g., He married up when he married the CEO's daughter. 他和CEO的女儿结婚算是高攀了。)
ask for someone's hand in marriage = 向某人求婚 (e.g., He asked for her hand in marriage. 他向她求婚。)


词源：From Middle English *marien*, from Old French marier, from Latin marītāre, frequentative of marītāri (“to marry”), from maritus (“married, husband”).


注意事项: marry 作名词的用法比较罕见，通常用wedding来表示“婚礼”。
