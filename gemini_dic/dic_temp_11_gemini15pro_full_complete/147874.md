overreaching:/ˌoʊvərˈriːtʃɪŋ/
adjective
过分的，自不量力的，野心勃勃的
Going beyond what is appropriate or achievable; excessively ambitious or presumptuous.
He made an overreaching attempt to control the company. 他试图控制公司，做得太过火了。

近义词: presumptuous, ambitious, overambitious, grasping, greedy, avaricious, acquisitive, exorbitant, excessive, immoderate, inordinate, extravagant, unreasonable, unrealistic, impractical
反义词: modest, humble, realistic, moderate
词性: adjective

overreach:/ˌoʊvərˈriːtʃ/
verb
超越，过度伸展，欺骗
1. To defeat (oneself) by seeking or trying to do more than one can or should : to fail through excessive ambition or presumption.
2. To reach or extend over or beyond.
3. To cheat or deceive.
The company overreached itself by expanding too quickly.  这家公司扩张太快，自不量力。


近义词: cheat, deceive, trick, defraud, swindle, con, dupe, hoax, hoodwink, bamboozle
反义词: underreach
词性: verb

overreach:/ˈoʊvərriːtʃ/
noun
过度扩张，过分行为
The act of overreaching.
The company's overreach into new markets led to its downfall.  该公司过度扩张到新市场导致了它的垮台。


近义词: excess, overextension, overambition
反义词: moderation, restraint
词性: noun
