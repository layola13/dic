jerk's:/dʒɜːrks/
noun
混蛋的；蠢货的；笨蛋的
belonging to or associated with a jerk (a foolish or contemptible person).
That's just the jerk's way of getting attention. 那是那个混蛋吸引注意力的方式。
近义词: fool's, idiot's, moron's
反义词: genius's, saint's
词性: possessive noun (所有格名词)
