bantamweight:/ˈbæntəmˌweɪt/| n. adj.| 最轻量级(拳击)，矮小而好斗的人 |A bantamweight boxer weighs between 115 and 118 pounds.  最轻量级拳击手的体重在115到118磅之间。| He's a real bantamweight, always picking fights. 他真是个矮小好斗的人，总是挑起争端。|近义词：lightweight, small, diminutive|反义词:heavyweight, large, giant|词性:名词，形容词
Etymology: From bantam (a small variety of domestic fowl) + weight.  源自bantam（一种小型家禽）+ weight（重量）。
