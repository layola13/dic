hinderingly:/ˈhɪndərɪŋli/
adverb
阻碍地，妨碍地
in a way that hinders or obstructs.
The construction work was hinderingly slow due to the bad weather.  由于恶劣天气，建筑工程进展缓慢而受阻。
His constant interruptions were hinderingly affecting my concentration. 他不断的打断严重妨碍了我的注意力。
近义词: obstructively, restrictively, hamperingly
反义词: helpfully, supportively, beneficially
词性: adverb
词源: From hinder (verb) + -ing (present participle suffix) + -ly (adverb suffix).  Hinder comes from Old English hindrian, related to Old High German hintar "behind."
