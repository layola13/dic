biophilia:/baɪ.oʊˈfɪl.i.ə/ /baɪ.əˈfɪl.i.ə/
noun
亲生命性，热爱生命的天性
An innate and genetically determined affinity of human beings with the natural world.
Humans have an innate biophilia, drawn to nature and living things. 人类有一种与生俱来的亲生命性，被自然和生物所吸引。
Biophilic design seeks to connect people with nature in built environments. 亲生物设计力求在建筑环境中将人与自然联系起来。
近义词: nature affinity, love of nature
反义词: biophobia
词性: noun
