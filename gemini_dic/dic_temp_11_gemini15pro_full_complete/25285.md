bufonite:/ˈbjuːfəˌnaɪt/| n. |水牛石|A fossil gastropod; a bufonite.  A kind of toadstone; a fossil tooth or other hard remain of an animal, formerly worn as an amulet and supposed to be an antidote against poison.|He found a bufonite while digging in his garden. 他在花园里挖土时发现了一块水牛石。|近义词：toadstone|反义词: None|词性:noun
