downshift:/ˈdaʊnˌʃɪft/| v. n.|减速，降档；放慢生活节奏|To reduce speed or change to a lower gear, also to adopt a simpler lifestyle. |Example: He downshifted the car as he approached the corner. 他在接近拐角时降低了汽车档位。

Example: Many people are choosing to downshift and move to the countryside. 许多人选择放慢生活节奏，搬到乡下。|近义词：decelerate, slow down, simplify, reduce |反义词: accelerate, speed up, upscale|词性:动词,名词
