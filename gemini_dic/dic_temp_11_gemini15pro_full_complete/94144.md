impedition:/ɪmˌpɛˈdɪʃən/
n.
阻碍，妨碍
The heavy traffic caused a significant impedition to our journey.
大交通严重阻碍了我们的旅程。
The protestors’ blockade formed an impedition to the flow of traffic.
抗议者的封锁阻碍了交通流动。
近义词:hindrance, obstruction, obstacle, impediment
反义词:aid, assistance, help, support
词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *impeditionem, accusative of *impeditio, from Latin impedire "to shackle the feet of," from in- "in, into" + pes (genitive pedis) "foot."
