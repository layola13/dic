depolishing:/diːˈpɒlɪʃɪŋ/
verb
去除光泽，使失去光泽
The act of removing the polish or shine from a surface.
The depolishing of the silver left it looking dull.  去除银器上的光泽使它看起来很暗淡。
tarnish, dull,  mattify
polish, shine, burnish
v.
