diurnalnesses:/daɪˈɜːrnl̩nɪsɪz/
noun
昼行性；白昼活动性
The state or quality of being diurnal.  The tendency to be active during the daytime.
The diurnalnesses of bees are well documented. 蜜蜂的昼行性已被充分记录在案。
The researchers studied the diurnalnesses of several desert animals. 研究人员研究了几种沙漠动物的昼行性。
Synonyms: diurnality
Antonyms: nocturnality
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From diurnal + -ness.  Diurnal comes from Late Middle English, from Old French diurne, from Latin diurnus ‘daily’, from dies ‘day’.
