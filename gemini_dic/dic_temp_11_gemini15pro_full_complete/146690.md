overheartily:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrtɪli/| adv.|过分热情地，过分真诚地| excessively heartily; with too much enthusiasm or sincerity. |He thanked her overheartily for the small gift, making her feel uncomfortable.| 近义词：effusively, excessively, exuberantly| 反义词：apathetically, coldly, indifferently| 词性: 副词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "heartily" (adverb form of "hearty," meaning "warm and friendly; enthusiastic and genuine")
