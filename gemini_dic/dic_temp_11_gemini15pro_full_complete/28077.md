capibara:/ˌkæpiˈbɑːrə/| n. |水豚|A large, semi-aquatic, South American rodent with a barrel-shaped body, short legs, and sparse hair.|Capybaras are the largest rodents in the world. 水豚是世界上最大的啮齿动物。|The capybara grazed peacefully by the riverbank. 水豚在河岸边平静地吃草。|近义词: rodent |反义词: None |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Tupi *kapi'wara "grass eater," from *kapi'i "grass" + *wara "eater."
