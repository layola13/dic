goalless: /ˈɡoʊlləs/ | adj. | 没有进球的，零比零的 | Without any goals being scored.  | The match ended in a goalless draw.  比赛以零比零平局结束。 | The game was a goalless affair. 这场比赛双方都没有进球。 | 近义词: scoreless, unproductive | 反义词: high-scoring, productive | 词性: 形容词
词源:  From "goal" (referring to scoring in a sport) + "-less" (suffix indicating absence).  "Goal" ultimately comes from Old Norse "gāl" meaning "obstacle," later evolving to mean "target" or "end."
