nonspiritednesses:/ˌnɒnˈspɪrɪtɪdnəsɪz/
n.
缺乏精神; 缺乏活力; 沉闷
lack of spirit, animation, or liveliness

The nonspiritednesses of the crowd made the concert feel dull.
人群缺乏活力，使得音乐会感觉很沉闷。

The team's nonspiritednesses after their loss was palpable.
球队输球后的沉闷气氛是显而易见的。

近义词:listlessness, apathy, dullness, lifelessness
反义词:liveliness, enthusiasm, energy, vibrancy
词性:名词


词源:
"nonspiritednesses" is derived from "spirited" (full of spirit or energy) with the prefix "non-" (not) and the suffix "-ness" (state or quality of being).  The word "spirited" itself comes from the Old French "espirituel," ultimately from the Latin "spiritus" (breath, spirit).
