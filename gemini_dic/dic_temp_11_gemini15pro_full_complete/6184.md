amitotic:/ˌeɪmaɪˈtɒtɪk/ /ˌeɪmaɪˈtɑːtɪk/| adj.|无丝分裂的|Relating to or denoting cell division by simple cleavage of the nucleus and division of the cytoplasm without spindle formation or the appearance of chromosomes.|Some lower organisms reproduce through amitotic processes. 一些低等生物通过无丝分裂的方式繁殖。|Amitotic cell division is less common than mitotic division.  无丝分裂比有丝分裂少见。|近义词: direct nuclear division|反义词: mitotic|词性:形容词
