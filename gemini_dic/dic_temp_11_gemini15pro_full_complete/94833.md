imputability:/ɪmˌpjuːtəˈbɪləti/| n. |可归罪性，可归咎性，可归因性|The quality or state of being imputable; the capability of being attributed or ascribed to a person or cause.  It refers to the possibility of assigning responsibility or blame for an action or consequence.|The imputability of the crime to the defendant was the central issue of the trial.  将犯罪归咎于被告是审判的核心问题。|Synonyms: accountability, responsibility, liability|Antonyms: innocence, blamelessness|noun

imputable:/ɪmˈpjuːtəbl/| adj. |可归罪的，可归咎的，可归因的|Capable of being imputed or attributed; ascribable.|The damage was imputable to negligence. 损害可归咎于疏忽。|Synonyms: attributable, ascribable, chargeable|Antonyms: unattributable, unascribable|adjective
