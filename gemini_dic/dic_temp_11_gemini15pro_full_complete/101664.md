irrelative:/ɪˈrɛlətɪv/
adjective
无关的，不相关的
not related or connected
Her comments were irrelative to the topic of discussion. 她的评论与讨论的主题无关。
The lawyer objected to the witness's testimony, arguing it was irrelative to the case. 律师反对证人的证词，认为这与案件无关。
近义词:inapplicable, extraneous, immaterial, irrelevant, impertinent
反义词:relevant, pertinent, applicable, germane
词性:adjective

irrelative:/ɪˈrɛlətɪv/
noun
无关紧要的人或事
something that is not relevant or important. (This usage is less common than the adjective form.)
The speaker dismissed the interruptions as irrelevatives. 演讲者将干扰斥为无关紧要的事情。(这种用法不如形容词形式常见。)
All the irrelevatives were removed from the report to make it concise and to the point. 所有无关紧要的内容都从报告中删除了，使之简洁明了。
近义词:trivia, nonessentials
反义词:essentials, necessities
词性:noun
