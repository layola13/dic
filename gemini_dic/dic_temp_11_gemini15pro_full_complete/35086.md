clothed:/kloʊðd/
adjective
穿着衣服的，覆盖着的
dressed, wearing clothes. Covered.
The mountains were clothed in mist. 群山笼罩在薄雾之中。
近义词:dressed, attired, clad, garbed
反义词:naked, bare, unclothed
词性:adjective

clothe:/kloʊð/
verb
给…穿衣；覆盖
to dress someone, especially in a particular type of clothing. To cover something.
She clothed the children in warm coats and hats. 她给孩子们穿上了暖和的外套和帽子。
近义词:dress, attire, garb, array
反义词:undress, uncover, strip
词性:verb
