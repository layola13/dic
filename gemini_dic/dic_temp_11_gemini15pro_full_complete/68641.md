far:/fɑːr/ (UK), /fɑːr/ (US)| adj. adv.|远的，遥远的；久远的；大量的；adv. 非常；到很远的距离|A long distance away; a lot; to a great extent.|例句：The town is not far from here. 这个小镇离这里不远。|近义词：distant, remote, long, considerably, a great deal|反义词：near, close, short|词性：形容词/副词

far:/fɑːr/ (UK), /fɑːr/ (US)| adv.|远，很远，遥远地；久远地；到很远的距离；到很大程度|To a great distance or extent.|例句：He threw the ball far. 他把球扔得很远。|近义词：a long way, remotely, greatly|反义词：near, close|词性：副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *feorr (West Saxon feor), from Proto-Germanic *ferraz.  Cognates include Old Frisian fer, Old Saxon fer, Old Norse fjǫr, Dutch ver, German fern.
