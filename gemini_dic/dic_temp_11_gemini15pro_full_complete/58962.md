dustsheet: /ˈdʌstʃiːt/
n.
防尘布；盖布；尘布
A large sheet of cloth used to protect furniture, etc. from dust.
She covered the sofa with a dustsheet before starting to paint. 她开始粉刷之前用防尘布盖住了沙发。

近义词: drop cloth, dust cover
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) uncovered, exposed
