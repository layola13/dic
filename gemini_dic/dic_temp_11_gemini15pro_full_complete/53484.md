disarrayed: /ˌdɪsəˈreɪd/ | adj. | 混乱的，紊乱的 |  Thrown into a state of disorganization or confusion.  Characterized by a lack of order or tidiness. | Her clothes were disarrayed after the hurried packing. 她匆忙打包后，衣服乱七八糟的。| synonyms: disordered, chaotic, messy, jumbled, rumpled | antonyms: ordered, organized, tidy, neat | adjective

disarray: /ˌdɪsəˈreɪ/ | n. | 混乱，紊乱 | A state of disorganization or untidiness. | The room was in complete disarray. 房间里一片狼藉。| synonyms: disorder, chaos, mess, confusion | antonyms: order, organization, tidiness, neatness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *desareer "to disorder," from des- "expressing reversal" + areer "to array."
