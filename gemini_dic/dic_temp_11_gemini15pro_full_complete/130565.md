nonactionably:/ˌnɒnˈækʃənəbli/
adjective
不可控告地，无行动依据地
Not capable of being acted upon, especially legally.  Something is nonactionable if there is no legal basis for taking action against it.
The lawyer advised that the comments, while offensive, were nonactionable. 律师认为，这些评论虽然令人不快，但不可控告。
The statement was deemed nonactionable due to its vagueness.  由于其含糊不清，该声明被认为没有行动依据。
近义词: unimpeachable, unchallengeable, invulnerable
反义词: actionable, prosecutable
词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "actionable" (adjective meaning "giving sufficient reason to take legal action").  "Actionable" ultimately derives from the Latin word "actio" (action).
