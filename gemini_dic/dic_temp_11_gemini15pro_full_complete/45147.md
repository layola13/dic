cudgel:/ˈkʌdʒəl/| n. vt.|棍棒，短棍；用棍棒打|A short, thick stick used as a weapon.  |The farmer used a cudgel to defend his sheep from the wolf. 农民用棍棒保护他的羊免受狼的攻击。|Synonyms: club, stick, bludgeon|Antonyms: feather, caress|noun
cudgel:/ˈkʌdʒəl/| vt.|用棍棒打|To beat with a cudgel. |He was cudgelled into submission. 他被打得屈服了。|Synonyms: club, beat, bludgeon|Antonyms: caress, soothe|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *kuggel*, from Old Norse *kuggill* ("club, cudgel").
