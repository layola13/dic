consequentiality:/ˌkɒnsɪˈkwenʃiˈælɪti/ /ˌkɑːnsəˌkwenʃiˈæləti/| n. |重要性；重大；随之而来的结果|The consequentiality of the decision was immediately apparent. 该决定的重要性立即显现出来。|例句：The consequentiality of her research cannot be overstated. 她的研究的重要性不容小觑。|例句：The president stressed the consequentiality of the upcoming election. 总统强调了即将到来的选举的重要性。|近义词：importance, significance, weight, moment|反义词：insignificance, triviality|词性:名词

consequent:/ˈkɒnsɪkwənt/ /ˈkɑːnsəkwent/| adj. |随之发生的；作为结果的|Her success was consequent on her hard work. 她的成功源于她的努力工作。|例句：The economic downturn was consequent upon the banking crisis. 经济衰退是银行业危机造成的。|例句：His promotion was consequent on his excellent performance.  他的晋升源于他出色的表现。|近义词：resulting, ensuing, subsequent|反义词：prior, preceding, antecedent|词性:形容词
