confinable:/kənˈfaɪnəbl/
adjective
可限制的，可禁闭的
capable of being confined or restricted.
The virus is highly contagious and confinable to quarantine zones. 这种病毒传染性很强，可限制在隔离区内。
The patient is no longer confinable to bed rest. 病人不再需要卧床休息了。
近义词:restricting, limiting, restrainable
反义词:unbounded, limitless, unrestricted
词性:adjective

词源:
From confine + -able.  "Confine" comes from Old French "confine" (border, limit), from Latin "confinis" (bordering, adjacent), from "con-" (together) + "finis" (end, limit).
