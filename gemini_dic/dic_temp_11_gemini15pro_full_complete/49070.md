dehydrate:/diːˈhaɪdreɪt/
verb
脱水，使干燥
to remove water from something
The sun dehydrates the body.  太阳使身体脱水。

dehydrate:/diːˈhaɪdreɪt/
verb
(使)失去活力；(使)变得枯燥无味
to make something less interesting or exciting
Long meetings always dehydrate me.  冗长的会议总是让我感到枯燥乏味。

dehydrate:/diːˈhaɪdreɪt/
adjective
脱水的，干燥的
lacking water
He felt dehydrated after the marathon.  马拉松结束后，他感到脱水。


近义词:dry, desiccate, parch
反义词:hydrate, moisturize
词性:v., adj.
