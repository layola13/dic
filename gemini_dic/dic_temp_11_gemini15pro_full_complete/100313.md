intersocietal:/ˌɪntərsoʊˈsaɪɪtəl/
adjective
社会间的，社会与社会之间的
Existing or occurring between societies.
例句：Intersocietal conflicts can arise from cultural misunderstandings. 社会间的冲突可能源于文化误解。

例句：Intersocietal trade has contributed to globalization.  社会间的贸易促进了全球化。

近义词：international, cross-cultural, transnational
反义词：intrasocietal, domestic
