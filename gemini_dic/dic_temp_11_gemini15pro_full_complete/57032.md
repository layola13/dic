doublet:/ˈdʌblɪt/ (US), /ˈdʌblət/ (UK)| n.|紧身短上衣；一件衣服；对词；成对的东西；复制品|A close-fitting jacket worn by men from the 14th to the 17th century; one of a pair of similar things; a word derived from the same source as another word but by a different route of transmission (e.g., 'chief' and 'chef').|He wore a doublet and hose. 他穿着一件紧身短上衣和长筒袜。|Synonyms: jacket, duplicate, copy, pair|Antonyms: singleton, individual|noun

doublet:/ˈdʌblɪt/ (US), /ˈdʌblət/ (UK)| n.|成对的东西；复制品|One of two things exactly alike; a duplicate or copy.|The antique dealer showed me a doublet of the rare coin.  古董商给我看了那枚稀有硬币的复制品。|Synonyms: duplicate, copy, replica, twin|Antonyms: original, unique|noun
