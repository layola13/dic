nonreinvestments:/ˌnɑːnriːɪnˈvɛstmənts/ | n. | 非再投资 | The act of not reinvesting earnings, dividends, or proceeds from sales back into the original investment or a similar asset. | The company's policy of nonreinvestments led to a decrease in overall portfolio value. 公司的非再投资政策导致整体投资组合价值下降。 |  The nonreinvestments of profits impacted future growth. 利润的非再投资影响了未来的增长。 | 近义词: noninvestment, withholding of investment | 反义词: reinvestment, reinvesting | 词性: 名词

nonreinvested:/ˌnɑːnriːɪnˈvɛstɪd/ | adj. | 未再投资的 | Describing profits, dividends, or other returns that have not been reinvested. | The nonreinvested dividends were deposited into his checking account. 未再投资的股息存入了他的支票账户。 |  They analyzed the performance of their nonreinvested assets. 他们分析了未再投资资产的表现。| 近义词: uninvested | 反义词: reinvested | 词性: 形容词


词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "reinvestment" (from "re-" meaning "again" + "investment").
