demigration: /ˌdiːmaɪˈɡreɪʃn/ | n. | 迁出；人口外移 | The demigration from rural areas to cities is a common phenomenon in developing countries. 农村地区向城市的迁出是发展中国家的普遍现象。|  The government is trying to slow the demigration of skilled workers by offering incentives to stay. 政府正试图通过提供奖励来减缓技术工人的外移。| 近义词: emigration, exodus | 反义词: immigration, influx | 词性: 名词

demigration: /ˌdiːmaɪˈɡreɪʃn/ |  n. |  降低; 贬低（较少用） |  The demigration of traditional values is a concern for many people.  传统价值观的贬低是许多人关心的问题。 | The constant criticism led to a demigration of his self-esteem.  不断的批评导致他的自尊心下降。 | 近义词: degradation, devaluation | 反义词: elevation, enhancement | 词性: 名词
