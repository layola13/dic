misanthropic:/ˌmɪsənˈθrɒpɪk/ /ˌmɪsənˈθrɑːpɪk/| adj.|厌世的；愤世嫉俗的|Disliking humankind and avoiding human society.|He became increasingly misanthropic in his old age, refusing to see anyone.  他晚年变得越来越孤僻，拒绝见任何人。|The hermit lived a misanthropic existence deep in the woods.  这位隐士在森林深处过着厌世的生活。|近义词：antisocial, cynical, pessimistic|反义词：philanthropic, sociable, humanistic|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *misanthrōpos*  (μισάνθρωπος) meaning "hater of mankind," from *misein* (μισεῖν) "to hate" + *anthrōpos* (ἄνθρωπος) "man, human being."
