heliotype:/ˈhiːliətaɪp/
n.
日光照片，日光版画
a photographic print made from a gelatin relief
The heliotype was a popular method of reproduction in the 19th century.  这幅日光照片是19世纪流行的复制方法。

heliotype:/ˈhiːliətaɪp/
n.
日光胶版术
a process for making heliotypes
Heliotype allowed for the mass production of high-quality images. 日光胶版术允许高质量图像的批量生产。


Etymology:  From Greek hēlios (“sun”) + type ("impression").

Synonyms:  photogravure, collotype

Antonyms:  None (specific antonyms for a printing process are uncommon).
