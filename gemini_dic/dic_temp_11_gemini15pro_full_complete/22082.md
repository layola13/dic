bollocking: /ˈbɒləkɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈbɑːləkɪŋ/ (US) | n. | 斥责；痛骂 | A severe scolding or reprimand. |  He got a real bollocking from his boss for being late. 他因为迟到被老板狠狠地骂了一顿。 | Synonyms: scolding, reprimand, telling-off, dressing-down | Antonyms: praise, commendation | Noun

bollocks: /ˈbɒləks/ (UK) /ˈbɑːləks/ (US) | n. | 胡说；废话；睾丸| Nonsense; rubbish; testicles. (This is the root word, and "bollocking" is derived from it.) | That's a load of bollocks!  那是一派胡言！| Synonyms: rubbish, nonsense, crap | Antonyms: truth, sense | Noun (Note: This word is considered vulgar and offensive.)
