hokily:/ˈhoʊkɪli/| adv.|俗气地，庸俗地，老套地|In a sentimental, overly emotional, or cliché way, often in a contrived or artificial manner.  Resembling or characteristic of a hockey player, especially in a stereotypical way (e.g., tough, aggressive).|例句：The movie ended hokily, with the two main characters embracing as the sun set.  这部电影的结尾很俗套，两个主角在夕阳下拥抱。

例句：He delivered his lines rather hokily, overacting the emotion. 他念台词念得很俗气，把感情演得太过了。|近义词：corny, cheesy, sentimental, mawkish, clichéd|反义词：sophisticated, refined, subtle, understated|词性:副词
