例句：The surgeon performed the complex procedure with magistral skill.  这位外科医生以精湛的技艺完成了这项复杂的手术。|近义词：masterful, authoritative, expert, skilled, proficient, adept|反义词：amateurish, unskilled, incompetent, inept|词性:形容词
