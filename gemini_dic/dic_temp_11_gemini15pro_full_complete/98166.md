inscribe:/ɪnˈskraɪb/| vt.|铭刻，题写，雕刻，记下|To write, carve, or engrave (words or symbols) on something, especially as a formal or permanent record.|例句：He inscribed his name on the inside cover of the book. 他把他的名字题写在书的封里。

例句：The monument is inscribed with the names of the fallen soldiers.  纪念碑上刻着阵亡士兵的名字。|近义词：engrave, carve, write, etch|反义词:erase, delete|v.
