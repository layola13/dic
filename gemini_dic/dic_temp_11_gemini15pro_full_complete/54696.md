disorganization:/dɪsˌɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃən/ |n.| 混乱，无组织，杂乱无章 |A state of being disorganized; lack of order or systematic arrangement.|The disorganization of the office made it difficult to find anything. 办公室的杂乱无章使得很难找到任何东西。|Near synonyms: disorder, chaos, mess, confusion|Antonyms: organization, order, tidiness|noun

disorganize:/dɪsˈɔːrɡənaɪz/ |v.| 使混乱，扰乱，瓦解 |To destroy the organization or systematic arrangement of; throw into disorder.|The sudden change in plans completely disorganized the team. 计划的突然改变完全扰乱了团队。|Near synonyms: disrupt, disorder, unsettle, confuse|Antonyms: organize, order, arrange, systematize|verb
