biquadrate:/baɪˈkwɒdreɪt/ | n. adj.|双二次方; 四次幂; |A number multiplied by itself four times.  A biquadratic equation.| The biquadrate of 5 is 625. 5的四次方是625。|近义词：fourth power, quartic|反义词: square root, cube root|词性:名词，形容词
biquadratic:/ˌbaɪkwɒˈdrætɪk/ | adj.|双二次的，四次的| Relating to the fourth power.|  A biquadratic equation is one in which the highest power of the unknown quantity is four. 双二次方程是未知数的最高次幂为四的方程。 |近义词：quartic|反义词: linear, quadratic|词性:形容词
