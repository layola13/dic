misserve:/ˌmɪsˈsɜːrv/ | v. | 辜负，虐待，服务不周 | To fail to serve adequately or properly; to do harm to. | The government has misserved the people by failing to invest in education. 政府未能投资教育，辜负了人民。

The restaurant misserved us by bringing the wrong order and then overcharging us.  这家餐馆给我们上错了菜，然后又多收了钱，服务很差。 | 近义词: harm, injure, abuse, neglect, mistreat, shortchange | 反义词: serve, aid, assist, help, support | 词性: 动词

词源:  From mis- + serve.  "Mis-" is a prefix meaning "wrong" or "badly."  "Serve" comes from Old French "servir," ultimately from Latin "servire" meaning "to serve."
