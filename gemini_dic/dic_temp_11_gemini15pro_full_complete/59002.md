duxes:/ˈdʌksiːz/
n.
leaders, guides, or commanders.
Plural of "dux," meaning a leader, especially the top student in a class.
The duxes of the school led the graduation procession.  学校的领袖们带领着毕业游行队伍。
Synonyms: leaders, chiefs, commanders
Antonyms: followers, subordinates
noun
