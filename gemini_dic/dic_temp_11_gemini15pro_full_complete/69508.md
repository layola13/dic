felister: /fəˈlɪstər/ | n. | 菲利斯特人;庸俗的人;反对文化或知识进步的人 |  A person who is hostile or indifferent to culture and intellectual pursuits or artistic and aesthetic values, sometimes regarded as being smug and conventional. | He dismissed his critics as felisters, unable to appreciate the true beauty of his work. 他将批评他的人斥为庸夫俗子，无法欣赏他作品的真正美。 | 近义词: philistine, lowbrow, anti-intellectual | 反义词: intellectual, aesthete, connoisseur | 词性: 名词

词源: From Philistine, referring to the biblical people often portrayed as enemies of the Israelites and lacking in culture.
