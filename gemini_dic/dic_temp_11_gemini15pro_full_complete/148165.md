overserves:/ˌoʊvərˈsɜːrvz/
verb
过度服务；服务过量
To provide more service than is necessary or desired.
The restaurant overserved us with bread, bringing three baskets when we only needed one.  这家餐馆给我们提供了过多的面包，我们只需要一篮，却送来了三篮。
overserved:/ˌoʊvərˈsɜːrvd/
adjective
被过度服务的；被过量供应的
Having received more service than necessary or desired.
We felt a little overserved after the waiter kept refilling our water glasses every two minutes. 服务员每两分钟就给我们续杯水，让我们感觉有点被过度服务了。

近义词: overcater, overdo
反义词: underserve, neglect


词性:v.  动词(overserves)
词性:adj. 形容词(overserved)
词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessively") + serve
