feckless:/ˈfɛklɪs/| adj.|无能的，不负责任的，软弱的|Lacking initiative and willpower; ineffective; irresponsible.|He was a feckless leader who let his country slide into ruin. 他是一位无能的领导人，让他的国家走向毁灭。|The feckless youth never held a job for more than a few weeks.  这个不负责任的年轻人没有一份工作能做超过几周。|近义词：irresponsible, incompetent, inefficient, ineffective, shiftless, careless, negligent|反义词：responsible, competent, efficient, effective, capable|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Scots *feckless, from feck (“effect, value, majority, most part”) + -less.  Ultimately from Middle English *fec, *fek (“effect, value, success”), from Old Norse feikn (“joy, delight, prosperity, success”).
