conceit:/kənˈsiːt/ /kɑːnˈsiːt/| n.|自负，骄傲；巧妙的比喻，奇想|A fanciful expression in writing or speech; excessive pride in oneself.|He was puffed up with conceit. 他被自负冲昏了头脑。|Synonyms: arrogance, vanity, egotism, pride, fancifulness, ingeniousness|Antonyms: humility, modesty|noun

conceit:/kənˈsiːt/ /kɑːnˈsiːt/| n.|奇想，幻想|An ingenious or fanciful notion or idea.|The play is full of conceits. 这部戏充满了奇思妙想。|Synonyms: whim, fancy, notion, caprice|Antonyms: practicality, realism|noun
