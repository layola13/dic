dragoons:/drəˈɡuːnz/| n.|龙骑兵；|A soldier originally trained to fight on horseback but later also on foot.  最初训练骑马作战，后来也训练步战的士兵。|例句：The dragoons charged across the field. 龙骑兵冲过战场。

|例句：Several regiments of dragoons were deployed to the border.  几个龙骑兵团被部署到边境。|近义词：cavalry, mounted soldiers, troopers|反义词:infantry|noun
