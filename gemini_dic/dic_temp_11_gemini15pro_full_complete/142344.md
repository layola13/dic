orthographic:/ˌɔːrθəˈɡræfɪk/| adj.|拼写的，正字法的|Relating to orthography; correct spelling.  Concerned with or relating to the conventional spelling system of a language.|The orthographic errors in the student's paper were numerous.  这个学生的论文中有很多拼写错误。|Synonyms: spelling, grammatical|Antonyms: misspelt, ungrammatical|adjective
