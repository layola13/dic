marriages:/ˈmærɪdʒɪz/
|n.|婚姻（复数）|The state of being united in marriage.|Many marriages end in divorce these days. 如今很多婚姻以离婚告终。|
|近义词：weddings, unions|反义词：divorces, separations|词性：名词|
|词源：From Middle English *mariage*, from Old French *mariage*, from Latin *marītāgium* ("marriage, wedlock"), from *marītāre* ("to marry"), from *marītus* ("husband, married man"). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\₂mḗr* (“young woman, bride”)|.
