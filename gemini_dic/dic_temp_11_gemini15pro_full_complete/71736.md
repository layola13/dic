flawy:/ˈflɔːi/| adj.|有缺陷的；有瑕疵的；不完美的|Having imperfections or defects; not perfect.|The diamond was flawless, but the setting was slightly flawed.|imperfect, defective, blemished|perfect, flawless, impeccable|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From flaw + -y.  "Flaw" comes from Old Norse *flaga "slab of stone, layer of rock," related to flag (n.1).  The suffix "-y" is a common adjective-forming suffix meaning "characterized by" or "full of."
