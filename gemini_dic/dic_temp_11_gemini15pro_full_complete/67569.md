extrapolation:/ɪkˌstræpəˈleɪʃn/| n.|外推；推断；[数] 外推法|The process of estimating, beyond the original observation range, the value of a variable on the basis of its relationship with another variable.|Scientists can extrapolate the future climate based on current trends. 科学家可以根据目前的趋势推断未来的气候。|近义词：projection, inference, deduction, estimation|反义词: interpolation|noun
