grubhood:/ˈɡrʌbhʊd/
noun
幼虫期; 蛹期; 肮脏破旧的街区
The state or period of being a grub; a dirty, squalid neighborhood.
The caterpillar spent several weeks in its grubhood before forming a chrysalis. 这条毛毛虫在结蛹前在幼虫期度过了几个星期。
He grew up in the grubhood of the city's slums. 他在城市贫民窟的肮脏破旧的街区长大。
近义词: larvahood, pupahood, slum, ghetto
反义词: adulthood, maturity, affluence
