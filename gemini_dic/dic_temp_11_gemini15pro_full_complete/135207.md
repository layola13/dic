nonosmotically:/nɒnɒzˈmɒtɪkli/ | adv. | 非渗透地 | Not involving or relating to osmosis, the process by which molecules of a solvent tend to pass through a semipermeable membrane from a less concentrated solution into a more concentrated one. |  The substance was transported across the cell membrane nonosmotically.  这种物质非渗透性地穿过细胞膜。 | Synonyms: non-osmotically | Antonyms: osmotically | adverb

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "osmotic" (relating to osmosis) + "-ally" (adverbial suffix)
