atomization:/ˌætəmaɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.|雾化，原子化|The process of reducing something to fine particles or a spray.  This can be achieved through various methods, including mechanical, chemical, or thermal processes.|The atomization of the perfume created a fine mist in the air. 香水雾化后在空中形成细小的薄雾。|synonyms: spraying, nebulization, vaporization|antonyms: condensation, solidification|noun

atomize:/ˈætəˌmaɪz/ |v.|雾化，原子化|To reduce something to fine particles or a spray.|The paint was atomized for an even coat. 油漆被雾化以便均匀涂抹。|synonyms: spray, nebulize, vaporize|antonyms: condense, solidify|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin atomus "atom," from Greek atomos "indivisible," from a- "not" + tomos "a cutting, slice, piece."  The suffix "-ization" denotes the process of making something.
