foregoes:/fɔːrˈɡoʊz/ /fɔrˈɡəʊz/
verb
放弃，抛弃
to decide not to have or do something that you would like to have or do
He forewent the pleasure of smoking a cigarette. 他放弃了吸烟的乐趣。

She foregoes dessert to stay fit. 她为了保持身材而放弃甜点。

近义词: forgo, relinquish, abstain from, give up, sacrifice
反义词: indulge in, partake in, embrace
词性: verb
