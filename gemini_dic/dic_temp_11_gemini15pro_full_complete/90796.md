humpback:/ˈhʌmpˌbæk/| n. adj.|座头鲸，驼背|A whale with a humped back and long flippers.  A person with a humped back.|例句：We saw a humpback whale breaching in the distance. 我们看到远处有一头座头鲸跃出水面。|例句：The old man was a humpback. 这位老人是个驼背。|近义词：(n.) rorqual, whale, (adj.) hunchbacked, crooked|反义词: (adj.) straight|词性:名词, 形容词
