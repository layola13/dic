nonpolitician:/ˌnɒnˌpɒlɪˈtɪʃən/ |n.| 非政治家；非政界人士 |A person who is not involved in politics or does not hold a political office. |  He's a nonpolitician, so he brings a fresh perspective to the debate. 他不是政界人士，所以他对辩论带来了新的视角。|  She preferred to remain a nonpolitician, focusing on her business career. 她宁愿继续做一个非政治人士，专注于她的商业生涯。|Synonyms: civilian, outsider | Antonyms: politician, statesman | Noun
