confide:/kənˈfaɪd/ | v. | 倾诉；吐露；委托；信托 | to tell someone about a secret or private matter while trusting them not to repeat it to others; to trust someone with information or a secret. |  I confided in my best friend about my worries. 我向我最好的朋友倾诉了我的烦恼。

She confided her fears to her diary. 她把她的恐惧写在日记里。| 近义词:  reveal, disclose, divulge, share, impart | 反义词: conceal, hide, suppress |词性:动词
