noninvasive:/ˌnɒnɪnˈveɪsɪv/ (US) /ˌnɒnɪnˈveɪzɪv/ (UK)| adj.|非侵入性的|Not involving penetration of the skin or body cavities.|Doctors opted for a noninvasive procedure to diagnose the issue. 医生选择了一种非侵入性手术来诊断问题。|The surgery was noninvasive, so the patient recovered quickly.  手术是非侵入性的，所以病人恢复得很快。|近义词：nonsurgical, atraumatic|反义词: invasive, surgical|词性:形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix) from Latin meaning "not"
invasive (adjective) from Latin invadere "to go into, enter, invade"
