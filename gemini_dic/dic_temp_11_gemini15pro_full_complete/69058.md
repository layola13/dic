fault-finding:/ˈfɔːltˌfaɪndɪŋ/
noun
挑剔；吹毛求疵
The act of looking for and pointing out faults or defects.
Her constant fault-finding was wearing down their relationship. 她不断的挑剔正在损耗他们的感情。
criticism, nitpicking, carping
praise, appreciation
n.

fault-finding:/ˈfɔːltˌfaɪndɪŋ/
adjective
爱挑剔的；吹毛求疵的
Inclined to look for and point out faults or defects.
He had a fault-finding nature, which made it difficult to work with him. 他生性爱挑剔，这使得与他共事很困难。
critical, censorious, picky
tolerant, accepting
adj.
