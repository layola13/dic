nonmelodiousnesses:/ˌnɒnˌmɛˈləʊdiəsnɪsɪz/ (US) /ˌnɒnˌmɛˈləʊdiəsnɪsɪz/ (UK)
n.
不悦耳, 刺耳的声音；缺乏旋律
lack of melodiousness; the quality of being unmelodious; a collection of unmelodious sounds.
The nonmelodiousnesses of the beginner's violin practice made the dog howl. 初学者练习小提琴的刺耳声音让狗都叫了起来。
近义词: dissonance, cacophony, discordance
反义词: melodiousness, euphony, harmony
词性: noun
