caseic:/ˈkeɪsiɪk/ | adj. | 干酪的，酪蛋白的 |  Relating to or made from casein, a protein found in milk. | Caseic minerals are formed through the action of bacteria on milk proteins.  酪蛋白矿物质是通过细菌作用于牛奶蛋白质而形成的。|  Caseic binding is often used in older books.  酪蛋白装订常用于旧书中。 | synonymous: cheesy, milky | antonymous: non-caseic | adjective
