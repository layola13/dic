latently:/ˈleɪtəntli/
adverb
潜在地；隐伏地；潜在性地
Existing but not yet developed or manifest; hidden or concealed.
The virus can lie latently in the body for years. 病毒可以在体内潜伏多年。
Synonyms:dormantly, inactively, potentially, secretly, covertly
Antonyms:actively, overtly, manifestly
词性: 副词
