indehiscence:/ˌɪndiˈhɪsəns/
n.
不开裂；不裂开
The indehiscence of the fruit means the seeds remain enclosed.  这种水果的不开裂意味着种子仍然被包裹在里面。
Failure of a fruit or other plant structure to open and release its contents at maturity. 果实或其他植物结构在成熟时不能打开并释放其内容物。
The indehiscence of certain seed pods helps protect the seeds from predators.  某些种荚的不开裂有助于保护种子免受捕食者的侵害。
近义词: non-dehiscence, closure
反义词: dehiscence
