cross-fertilised:/ˌkrɒs ˈfɜːtɪlaɪzd/ | adj. | 异花授粉的；交叉受精的；（思想、风格等）相互影响的 | Fertilized by pollen from a different plant or variety; (of ideas, styles, etc.) influenced by each other. | The apple trees were cross-fertilised with a variety resistant to disease. 这些苹果树与抗病品种进行了异花授粉。| The two art forms have cross-fertilised each other, resulting in a unique hybrid style. 这两种艺术形式相互影响，形成了一种独特的混合风格。 | synonyms: crossbred, hybridized, interbred,  influenced,  integrated | antonyms: purebred, unmixed | adjective
