disenrol:/ˌdɪsɪnˈroʊl/ (US), /ˌdiːsɪnˈrəʊl/ (UK)| v. |取消注册，注销|To officially remove someone's name from the list of members of a course, college, or group.|
例句：He disenrolled from the class because it was too difficult. 他从这门课取消了注册，因为它太难了。

例句：She disenrolled her son from the after-school program. 她给她儿子从课后班注销了。

近义词：unregister, withdraw, unsubscribe|
反义词：enroll, register, subscribe|
词性：动词
