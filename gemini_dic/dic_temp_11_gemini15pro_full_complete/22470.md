bookmarkers:/ˈbʊkmɑːrkərz/
n.|书签；网络书签|People who create or use bookmarks, especially digital bookmarks.  Those who save links to websites or specific locations within digital content for later reference.|Many bookmarkers organize their saved links into folders by topic.|近义词：savers, collectors, organizers (in a digital context) |反义词: N/A|词性:名词
