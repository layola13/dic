marmose:/ˈmɑːrmoʊs/ | n. |鼠负鼠；侏儒负鼠|A small, nocturnal, arboreal marsupial found in Central and South America, resembling a mouse with a prehensile tail. |例句：The marmose scurried up the tree, its prehensile tail helping it to grip the branches. 鼠负鼠窜上了树，它那能卷握的尾巴帮助它抓住树枝。

例句：Marmoses are primarily insectivorous. 鼠负鼠主要以昆虫为食。|近义词：mouse opossum, dwarf opossum |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *marmosa*, perhaps ultimately from Tupi.
