colophene:/ˈkɒləfiːn/ | n. |松香烯，树脂|A resinous substance, essentially a polymer of beta-pinene, obtained by the distillation of rosin. It is a pale yellow or amber-colored solid with a high boiling point, used in various industrial applications, including varnishes, adhesives, and printing inks. |The artist used a colophene-based varnish to protect the painting. 艺术家用一种基于松香烯的清漆来保护这幅画。|近义词:rosin resin, wood resin|反义词:|名词
