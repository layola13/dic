diphthongize:/dɪfˈθɒŋɡaɪz/ /daɪfˈθɑːŋɡaɪz/| v. |双元音化|To cause a single vowel sound to become a diphthong; to pronounce as a diphthong.|The Great Vowel Shift diphthongized many Middle English vowels.| 近义词:change, alter, modify| 反义词:monophthongize| verb

diphthongization:/ˌdɪfθɒŋɡaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌdaɪfθɑːŋɡaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |双元音化|The process of a single vowel sound becoming a diphthong.|The diphthongization of /iː/ to /aɪ/ is a common feature in certain dialects.| 近义词: alteration, modification | 反义词:monophthongization| noun
