crucible:/ˈkruːsəbəl/| n.|坩埚; 严峻的考验; 熔锅|a ceramic or metal container in which metals or other substances may be melted or subjected to very high temperatures. A situation of severe trial, or in which different elements interact, leading to the creation of something new.|The crucible cracked under the intense heat.  这坩埚在高温下破裂了。

The city became a crucible of different cultures. 这座城市成为了不同文化的熔炉。|近义词：melting pot, test, trial|反义词: paradise, comfort|词性:noun
