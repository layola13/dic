chroniker: /ˈkrɒnɪkər/ (UK) /ˈkrɑːnɪkər/ (US)| n.|编年史家，记录者|A person who writes chronicles, especially one who records historical events in the order in which they occurred.|John Froissart was a famous chronicler of the Hundred Years' War.|近义词: historian, recorder, annalist|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Middle English *cronicer*, from Old French *cronicier*, from Late Latin *chronicārius*, from Latin *chronica* (“chronicle”).  Ultimately from Greek *khronikós* (χρονικός, “of time”), from *khrónos* (χρόνος, “time”).
