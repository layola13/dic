lithographer:/lɪˈθɒɡrəfər (British English), lɪˈθɑːɡrəfər (American English)| n.|平版印刷师；石版印刷工|A person who draws, etches, or engraves on stone or metal plates for printing. |Example 1: The lithographer carefully prepared the stone for printing. 平版印刷师仔细地准备用于印刷的石板。

Example 2:  The skilled lithographer created a beautiful image on the metal plate.  熟练的平版印刷师在金属板上创作了一幅美丽的图像。|近义词：printer, engraver, artist|反义词: None|词性: Noun

lithography:/lɪˈθɒɡrəfi (British English), lɪˈθɑːɡrəfi (American English)| n.|平版印刷术；石版印刷术|A method of printing from a flat stone or metal plate on which the image areas are worked using a greasy substance so that the ink will adhere to them by, while the non-image areas are made ink-repellent.|Example 1: Lithography was a major advancement in printing technology. 平版印刷术是印刷技术的一项重大进步。

Example 2:  The artist specialized in color lithography. 这位艺术家专攻彩色平版印刷。|近义词：printing|反义词: None|词性: Noun
