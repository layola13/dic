inductionless:/ɪnˈdʌkʃənlɪs/
adj.
无感的，无电感的
Without induction; having no inductance.
The circuit uses an inductionless resistor.  这个电路使用了一个无感电阻。
近义词:non-inductive
反义词:inductive
词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + induction + -less (without).  "Induction" comes from Latin *inducere* "to lead in."
