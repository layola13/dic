bechamel:/ˌbeɪʃəˈmɛl/| n. |白汁; 白酱|A rich white sauce made with butter, flour, and milk, often flavored with nutmeg, used especially in French cuisine.|The lasagna was layered with a rich bechamel. 千层面中夹着一层厚厚的白汁。|近义词：white sauce, cream sauce|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific sauce)|noun
