interchaining:/ˌɪntərˈtʃeɪnɪŋ/
interchaining| adj.|相互连接的，互相连锁的|The act or process of linking or connecting things together, especially in a chain or series.  It emphasizes the reciprocal nature of the connection, implying that each element is linked to others in a mutually dependent way.|The interchaining of events led to the final outcome.  一系列事件相互交织，导致了最终的结果。|interconnected, interlinked, interwoven|separate, detached|adjective


interchaining:/ˌɪntərˈtʃeɪnɪŋ/
interchaining| n.|相互连接，互相连锁|The state of being linked or connected together, especially in a chain or series.|The interchaining of the systems ensured stability. 系统的相互连接确保了稳定性。|interconnection, interlinkage|separation, detachment|noun
