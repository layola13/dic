capes:/keɪps/| n. |斗篷；披肩；海角|A sleeveless garment or cloak, typically a short one, fastened around the neck and hanging loosely over the shoulders, or a piece of land jutting out into the sea.|The superhero wore a flowing cape.  那个超级英雄穿着一件飘逸的斗篷。|The ship rounded the cape.  船绕过了海角。|近义词：cloak, shawl, promontory, headland|反义词:bay, gulf|noun
