denunciations:/dɪˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃənz/
n.
公开谴责，强烈批评
public acts of condemning something or someone as bad or wrong.
The politician faced numerous denunciations following the scandal.  该政客在丑闻曝光后遭到多方谴责。
The government issued a denunciation of the terrorist attack. 政府发表声明谴责恐怖袭击。
synonyms: condemnations, criticisms, accusations, censures
antonyms:  praises, commendations, endorsements
noun
