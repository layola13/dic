deplication:/ˌdɛplɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |复制，重复；折叠 |The deplication of genetic material is essential for cell division. 基因物质的复制对于细胞分裂至关重要。|例句：The deplication of effort in this project wasted valuable time and resources.  这个项目中工作的重复浪费了宝贵的时间和资源。

例句：The artist explored themes of deplication and mirroring in his latest exhibition.  这位艺术家在最近的展览中探索了复制和镜像的主题。|近义词：duplication, replication, copying|反义词：deletion, removal|词性：名词

Etymology:  From Late Latin *deplicatio, from Latin deplicare "to unfold, unroll," from de- "un-, out" + plicare "to fold."  The meaning shifted from "unfolding" to "a doubling, a folding over" and eventually to "copying, replicating."
