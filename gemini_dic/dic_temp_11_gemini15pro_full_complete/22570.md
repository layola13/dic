bootes:/boʊˈoʊˌtiz/| n.|牧夫座|A constellation in the northern sky, near Ursa Major, containing the bright star Arcturus. |Bootes is easily recognized by its distinctive kite shape. 牧夫座很容易通过其独特的风筝形状辨认出来。|Bootes is home to Arcturus, the fourth-brightest star in the night sky. 牧夫座是大角星的所在地，大角星是夜空中第四亮的恒星。| 近义词:Herdsman (less common)| 反义词:None|noun
