armorial:/ɑːrˈmɔːriəl/
adjective
纹章的；徽章的
Of or relating to coats of arms; heraldic.
The Duke displayed his armorial bearings proudly. 公爵骄傲地展示了他的纹章。
近义词：heraldic, emblematic
反义词：None
词性：adjective
