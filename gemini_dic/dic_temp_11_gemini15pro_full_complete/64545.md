erratic:/ɪˈrætɪk/ | adj. | 不稳定的，古怪的，飘忽不定的 | Not regular or consistent; unpredictable. |  The erratic behavior of the stock market is making investors nervous. 股票市场的不稳定行为使投资者感到紧张。| The weather has been erratic lately, changing from sunny to rainy in an instant. 最近天气很不稳定，一下子从晴天变成雨天。| 近义词：unpredictable, inconsistent, irregular, capricious, volatile | 反义词：predictable, consistent, regular, stable, steady | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *erraticus, from Latin errare "to wander, stray, err," from PIE *ers- "to move, wander."
