hereafters:/ˌhɪərˈæftərz/
| n. | 来世，今后 |The hereafter is a concept that refers to life after death.  来世是指死后的生命。|  He promised to love her for all time and into the hereafters. 他承诺永远爱她，直到来世。| synonyms: afterlife, future, henceforth | antonyms: past, before | noun
