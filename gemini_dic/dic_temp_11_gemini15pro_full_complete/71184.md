fistan:/ˈfɪstæn/ | n. | 长袍，礼服; (尤指)巴尔干地区妇女穿的一种长袖长袍 | A long robe or gown, especially one worn by women in the Balkans. | She wore a beautiful fistan to the festival. 她穿着一件漂亮的礼服去参加节日。 | The fistan flowed gracefully as she danced.  当她跳舞时，长袍优雅地飘动。 | 近义词: gown, robe, dress | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 casual wear 便服) | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Turkish *fistan*, from Byzantine Greek φουστάνι *foustáni* (whence also Italian *fustagno*, French *futaine*, ultimately from Latin *fustis* (“staff; wooden stick, club”)). Originally referred to a type of fabric (fustian), later to garments made from it.
