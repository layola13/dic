collectedness:/kəˈlɛktɪdnəs/ |n.|镇定；沉着；冷静|The state of being calm, composed, and in control of oneself. |She maintained her collectedness throughout the crisis. 她自始至终在危机中保持镇定。|Synonyms: composure, calmness, equanimity, poise, self-possession |Antonyms: anxiety, agitation, nervousness, panic, distress|noun

collectedness:/kəˈlɛktɪdnəs/ |n.|收集齐全；完整性|The state of being gathered together or assembled as a whole. |The collectedness of his research materials impressed the committee.  他收集齐全的研究资料给委员会留下了深刻印象。|Synonyms: completeness, wholeness, integrity, entirety|Antonyms: incompleteness, fragmentation, deficiency|noun
