flickery:/ˈflɪkəri/| adj.|闪烁的，忽明忽暗的|Burning unsteadily; flickering.|The candle flame was flickery in the draft.  蜡烛的火焰在气流中摇曳不定。|The flickery lights of the fireflies danced in the night. 萤火虫闪烁的灯光在夜空中飞舞。|近义词：wavering, unsteady, intermittent, flickering|反义词：steady, constant, continuous|词性:形容词

词源: From the verb "flicker" + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives). "Flicker" comes from Old English *flicorian, frequentative of *flican "to move quickly, flutter," of imitative origin.
