esophagi's:/ɪˈsɒfəɡaɪz/ |n. |食管（复数的所有格）| The possessive form of "esophagi," the plural of "esophagus." It refers to something belonging to or associated with multiple esophagi.  This form is grammatically unusual, as the plural "esophagi" is already rarely used.  It's more common to see "esophagus'" or simply rephrase to avoid this construction.|  Example:  (While grammatically correct but unusual) The esophagi's linings were inflamed.  (More common/natural) The linings of the esophagi were inflamed. | Synonym: (for esophagus) gullet | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for esophagus) | Noun
