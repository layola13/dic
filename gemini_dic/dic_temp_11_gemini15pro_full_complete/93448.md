illumine:/ɪˈluːmɪn/ /ɪˈljuːmɪn/ | vt. | 照亮，启发 | to light up; to make clear or understandable | The full moon illumined the path ahead.  满月照亮了前方的道路。| synonyms: enlighten, illuminate, brighten | antonyms: darken, obscure | verb

illuminer:/ɪˈluːmɪnər/ /ɪˈljuːmɪnər/ | n. | 照明器，启发者 | a person who provides intellectual or spiritual enlightenment |  He was considered an illuminer of the masses. 他被认为是大众的启蒙者。 | synonyms: enlightener, visionary, guru | antonyms: obscurantist | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin illuminare, from Latin illuminare "to light up, brighten," figuratively "to enlighten," from in- "in" + luminare "to light," from lumen (genitive luminis) "light."
