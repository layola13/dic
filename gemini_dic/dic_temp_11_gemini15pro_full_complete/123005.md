monarchists:/ˈmɒnəkɪsts/ | n. | 君主主义者 | People who support the principle of monarchy, a system of government in which a single person reigns, usually a king or queen. |  The monarchists staged a protest against the newly formed republic. 君主主义者举行了反对新成立共和国的抗议活动。|
synonyms: royalists, conservatives (in some contexts) | antonyms: republicans, democrats, revolutionaries | noun

monarchist:/ˈmɒnəkɪst/ | n. | 君主主义者 | A person who supports the principle of having a monarch. |  He was a staunch monarchist and believed in the divine right of kings. 他是一位坚定的君主主义者，相信君权神授。| synonyms: royalist | antonyms: republican, anti-monarchist | noun
