monthlies: /ˈmʌnθliz/
n.
月刊; 月经
Magazines or periodicals published once a month. Also informally refers to menstruation.
She subscribes to several fashion monthlies. 她订阅了几本时尚月刊。
I forgot to bring my monthlies with me on this trip.  这次旅行我忘了带卫生巾了。
近义词: periodicals, magazines (for publications); period (for menstruation)
反义词: (No direct antonym for publications);  (No direct antonym for menstruation)
词性: noun


monthlies: /ˈmʌnθliz/
n.
月经
Informal term for menstruation.
My monthlies started early this month. 我这个月月经来得早。
She experiences painful cramps during her monthlies. 她在月经期间会经历痛苦的痉挛。
近义词: period, menstruation, menses
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun
