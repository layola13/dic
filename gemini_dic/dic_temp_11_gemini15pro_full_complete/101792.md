irrevoluble:/ɪˈrɛvələbəl/
adjective
不可撤销的；不能改变的；不能废除的
not able to be changed, reversed, or recovered.
His decision was irrevocable, and there was nothing anyone could do to change his mind. 他的决定是不可撤销的，任何人都无法改变他的想法。
The judge's ruling was irrevocable. 法官的裁决是不可撤销的。

近义词:irreversible, unalterable, unchangeable, fixed, permanent, final, conclusive, definitive, immutable
反义词:reversible, changeable, revocable
词性:adjective
