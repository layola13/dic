dewlapped: /ˈdjuːlæpt/ | adj. | 有垂肉的，垂肉发达的 | Having a dewlap; characterized by a dewlap. A dewlap is a loose fold of skin hanging from the neck or throat of an animal, especially certain cattle, lizards, and birds. |  The dewlapped lizard puffed out its throat in a territorial display.  垂肉发达的蜥蜴鼓起喉咙，以示领地。 | The farmer examined the dewlapped bull. 农民检查了那头有垂肉的公牛。 |  Synonyms: wattled, jowly | Antonyms: N/A | adjective
