intelligencia:/ɪnˌtɛlɪˈdʒɛntsɪə/ | n. |知识分子，知识界|A group of people who are highly educated and involved in intellectual pursuits, such as writers, artists, and academics.|例句：The intelligentsia of the country were critical of the government's policies.  这个国家的知识分子批评了政府的政策。

|例句：The artist considered himself a member of the intelligentsia.  这位艺术家认为自己是知识分子的一员。


|近义词：intellectuals, academics, scholars|反义词:masses, commoners|词性:noun
