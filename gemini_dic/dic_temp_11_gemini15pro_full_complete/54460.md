disintegrate:/dɪsˈɪntɪɡreɪt/
verb
瓦解，碎裂，崩溃
break into small parts or pieces; decay
The spacecraft disintegrated as it entered the Earth's atmosphere.  宇宙飞船进入地球大气层时解体了。

disintegrate:/dɪsˈɪntɪɡreɪt/
verb
衰败，衰退
lose strength or coherence
The old manuscript had disintegrated over time.  这古老的手稿随着时间的推移已经腐烂了。

disintegrate:/dɪsˈɪntɪɡreɪt/
adjective
解体的，分裂的
broken up into small parts or pieces
The disintegrated remains of the building were scattered across the site.  建筑物解体的残骸散落在场地各处。


近义词: crumble, decay, decompose, fall apart, break down, deteriorate
反义词: integrate, unify, combine, solidify, strengthen


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin dis- ("apart, not") + integratus, past participle of integrare ("to make whole"), from integer ("whole, entire").
