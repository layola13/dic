interinhibitions:/ˌɪntərɪnɪˈbɪʃənz/ | n. |相互抑制|  The mutual suppression or restraint of different behavioral tendencies or impulses. It often refers to a psychological process where one response or impulse inhibits another. | The dog's interinhibitions prevented it from both barking and wagging its tail simultaneously.  这只狗的相互抑制使它无法同时吠叫和摇尾巴。|  The speaker's anxiety created interinhibitions that hampered his fluency. 演讲者的焦虑造成了相互抑制，妨碍了他的流畅性。|Synonyms: mutual inhibition, reciprocal inhibition, counter-inhibition| Antonyms:  facilitation,  excitation | noun
