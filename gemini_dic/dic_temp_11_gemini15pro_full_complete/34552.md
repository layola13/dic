clear-headedness:/ˌklɪərˈhɛdɪdnəs/ | n. | 清醒；头脑清晰 | The state of being clear-headed; having a clear and focused mind. | After a good night's sleep, she woke up with a sense of clear-headedness.  她睡了一夜好觉后，醒来时头脑清醒。| lucidity, alertness, composure | confusion, fogginess | noun

clear-headed:/ˌklɪərˈhɛdɪd/ | adj. | 头脑清醒的；思路清晰的 | Having or showing a clear and focused mind. | He made a clear-headed decision despite the pressure. 尽管压力很大，他还是做出了一个清醒的决定。 | lucid, alert, rational | confused, muddled | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From clear + -headed + -ness.  "Clear" ultimately derives from Latin "clarus" meaning "bright" or "clear". "-headed" is a suffix indicating a mental state or characteristic. "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, denoting a state or quality.
