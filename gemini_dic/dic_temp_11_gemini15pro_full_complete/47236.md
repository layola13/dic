dazzling: /ˈdæzlɪŋ/ (US), /ˈdæzlɪŋ/ (UK)
adjective
令人眼花缭乱的；耀眼的
Extremely impressive, beautiful, or skillful.
例句：The sunset was dazzling.  日落令人眼花缭乱。

例句：She gave a dazzling performance. 她展现了令人惊艳的表演。

近义词：brilliant, radiant, stunning, blinding, gleaming
反义词：dull, dim, lackluster
词性：adjective
