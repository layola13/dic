lipothymic:/ˌlɪpəˈθɪmɪk/ | adj. | 昏厥的，快要晕倒的 | Relating to or characteristic of lipothymia, a state of faintness or near fainting. |  She felt lipothymic after standing in the sun for too long.  她站在太阳底下太久，感到快要晕倒了。 | Near-synonym: faint, dizzy, lightheaded | Antonym: alert, energetic | adjective

lipothymic:/ˌlɪpəˈθɪmɪk/ | adj. | 昏厥的，快要晕倒的 | Experiencing a sensation of faintness or lightheadedness, often accompanied by dizziness, blurring of vision, and weakness. | The patient reported feeling lipothymic before collapsing. 病人在晕倒前报告说感觉快要晕倒了。 | Near-synonym:  syncopal, vertiginous | Antonym:  stable, strong | adjective
