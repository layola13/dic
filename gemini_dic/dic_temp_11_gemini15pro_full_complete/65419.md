euphorbin:/juːˈfɔːrbɪn/
n.
大戟毒素，大戟树脂
A toxic resinous substance obtained from various euphorbiaceous plants.
Euphorbin is a potent irritant and can cause severe skin reactions.  大戟毒素是一种强效刺激物，会导致严重的皮肤反应。
The euphorbin in the sap of the plant caused a burning sensation.  这种植物汁液中的大戟毒素引起了一种灼烧感。
近义词:resin, toxin
反义词:(None -  no direct antonym for a specific chemical compound)
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From *Euphorbia* + *-in* (chemical suffix forming names of neutral principles). *Euphorbia* is named after Euphorbus, physician to Juba II, king of Mauretania.
