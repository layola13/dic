cordwainer:/ˈkɔːrdˌweɪnər/| n.|鞋匠，皮匠|A craftsman who makes or repairs shoes.|例句：The cordwainer skillfully crafted a pair of leather boots.  鞋匠熟练地制作了一双皮靴。

例句：He apprenticed himself to a cordwainer to learn the trade. 他拜一位鞋匠为师学习这门手艺。|近义词：shoemaker, cobbler|反义词: (None -  no direct opposite for a profession)|词性:noun
