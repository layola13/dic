inculcator:/ɪnˈkʌlkeɪtər/ | n. |灌输者；谆谆教诲者|Someone who inculcates; one who instills an idea or habit by persistent instruction.|The teacher, a passionate inculcator of knowledge, inspired many students. 这位老师是一位充满激情的知识灌输者，激励了许多学生。|Synonyms: teacher, instructor, mentor, guide |Antonyms: corrupter, misleader|noun
