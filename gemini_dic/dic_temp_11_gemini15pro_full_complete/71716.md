flavourlessness:/ˈfleɪvələsnəs/ | n. | 无味；平淡无奇 | Lack of flavour; blandness. | The flavourlessness of the dish was disappointing. 这道菜的寡淡令人失望。| The flavourlessness of the hospital food made him lose his appetite. 医院食物的无味让他食欲不振。 | 近义词: blandness, insipidity, tastelessness | 反义词: flavourfulness, savouriness, tastiness | 词性: 名词

flavourless:/ˈfleɪvələs/ | adj. | 无味的；平淡无奇的 | Lacking flavour; bland. | The soup was flavourless and watery. 这汤淡而无味。 |  The meat was surprisingly flavourless despite the spices. 尽管加了香料，这肉还是出奇地无味。 | 近义词: bland, insipid, tasteless | 反义词: flavourful, savoury, tasty | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From flavour + -less.  "Flavour" comes from Old French "flaor," ultimately from Latin "flare" (to blow).  "-less" is a Germanic suffix indicating lack or absence.
