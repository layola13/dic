adjudicate:/əˈdʒuːdɪkeɪt/| v. |判决，裁定，裁决|To formally judge and settle a dispute or contest; to act as judge.| The court adjudicated the case in favor of the plaintiff. 法庭判原告胜诉。|synonyms: judge, decide, determine, rule, arbitrate| antonyms:  waver, hesitate|verb
adjudicated:/əˈdʒuːdɪkeɪtɪd/| adj. |已判决的，已裁定的|Having been formally judged or decided, especially in a court of law.|The adjudicated property was sold at auction. 已判决的财产被拍卖了。|synonyms: judged, decided, determined, settled, resolved|antonyms:  undecided, unresolved, pending|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *adjudicatus*, past participle of *adjudicare* "to award," from *ad-* "to" + *judicare* "to judge," from *judex* "judge."
