incendiary:/ɪnˈsɛndiəri/ /ɪnˈsɛndiˌɛri/ | adj. n.|煽动性的，纵火的；燃烧弹，煽动者|Designed to cause fires; tending to stir up conflict.|The protesters threw incendiary devices at the police. 抗议者向警察投掷燃烧装置。|近义词:inflammatory, provocative, combustible, arsonist|反义词:conciliatory, calming, peaceful|词性:adjective, noun
incendiary:/ɪnˈsɛndiəri/ /ɪnˈsɛndiˌɛri/ | n.|燃烧弹，煽动者|A substance or weapon (such as a bomb) used to start fires; someone who stirs up conflict.|His incendiary remarks caused a riot. 他的煽动性言论引发了骚乱。|近义词:firebomb, agitator, rabble-rouser|反义词:peacemaker, mediator|词性:noun
