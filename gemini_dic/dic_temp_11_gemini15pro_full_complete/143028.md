outbleed:/aʊtˈbliːd/
verb
流血过多；大量出血
to bleed profusely; to lose a large amount of blood.
The soldier outbled on the battlefield.  这个士兵在战场上流血过多而死。
近义词: hemorrhage, bleed profusely
反义词: clot, coagulate
词性: verb
