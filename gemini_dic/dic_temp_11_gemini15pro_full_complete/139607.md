occasionalistic: /əˌkeɪʒənəˈlɪstɪk/
| adj. | 偶然论的；认为上帝是直接原因的 | Relating to occasionalism, a philosophical theory that postulates that God is the direct cause of all events, including mental and physical events. | Occasionalistic theories deny the causal efficacy of created beings. 偶然论理论否认被造物体的因果效力。 |  The philosopher Malebranche was a prominent occasionalist. 哲学家马勒伯朗奇是一位杰出的偶然论者。| synonyms:  deterministic, necessitarian | antonyms:  indeterministic, free will | adjective
