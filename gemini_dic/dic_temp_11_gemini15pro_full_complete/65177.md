ethnologic:/ˌeθnəˈlɑːdʒɪk/ | adj. | 人种学的，民族志学的 | Relating to ethnology, the study of the characteristics of different peoples and the relationships between them. |  Ethnologic research often involves fieldwork and participant observation. 人种学研究通常包括实地考察和参与式观察。|  Ethnological studies can shed light on cultural diversity. 民族学研究可以阐明文化多样性。| 近义词: anthropological, ethnographic | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-ethnological") | 词性: adjective
