foggier:/ˈfɔːɡiər (UK), ˈfɑːɡiər (US)| adj.|更浓雾的，更模糊的|Comparative form of foggy: more foggy.  More filled with fog; more obscure or unclear.|例句：The weather became foggier as the evening wore on. 夜晚来临，天气变得更加浓雾弥漫。|近义词：mistier, hazier, murkier, denser|反义词：clearer, brighter, sunnier|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Old English *fogig, from fog "fog" (of uncertain origin) + -ig, adjectival suffix. The comparative form -ier developed later.
