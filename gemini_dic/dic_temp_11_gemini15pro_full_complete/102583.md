jahvism: /ˈdʒɑːvɪzəm/
n.
雅威主义, 耶和华主义
The belief in and worship of Yahweh, the God of the Hebrews, as described in the Hebrew Bible.  It emphasizes Yahweh's covenant relationship with his chosen people.
Jahvism is a central theme in the Old Testament.  雅威主义是旧约的中心主题。
Jahvism influenced the development of both Christianity and Islam. 雅威主义影响了基督教和伊斯兰教的发展。
近义词: Yahwism
反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially polytheism or atheism depending on the context)
词性: noun
