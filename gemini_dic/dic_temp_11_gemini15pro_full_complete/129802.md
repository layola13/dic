non-gentile:/nɒnˈdʒɛntaɪl/ | adj. | 非外邦人的；犹太人的 | Not a gentile; Jewish. |  Example:  The restaurant caters specifically to a non-gentile clientele. 这家餐厅专门为犹太顾客提供服务。

Example:  In this community, non-gentile traditions are carefully preserved. 在这个社区，犹太传统得到精心保护。 | 近义词: Jewish, Israelite, Hebrew | 反义词: Gentile | 词性: 形容词

词源:

From non- + gentile.  "Gentile" comes from Latin "gentilis" meaning "of the same clan or race," later used to refer to non-Jews.
