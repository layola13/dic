overlimiting:/ˌoʊvərˈlɪmɪtɪŋ/
adjective|过度限制的，超过极限的|Exceeding or surpassing a limit or boundary, often in a way that is detrimental or counterproductive.|The overlimiting parental controls prevented the teenager from accessing any educational websites. 过度限制的家长控制阻止了青少年访问任何教育网站。|Synonyms: restrictive, excessive, prohibitive, constraining|Antonyms: permissive, lenient, unrestricted, liberating
word origin: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive" or "beyond") + "limiting" (present participle of "limit," from Old French "limiter," ultimately from Latin "limes," meaning "boundary").
