It refers to the process of giving something special status or reverence, often by formalizing it in a constitution, law, or tradition. | The enshrinement of free speech is a cornerstone of democracy. 言论自由的神圣化是民主的基石。 |  近义词: consecration, sanctification | 反义词: desecration, profanation | 词性: 名词


词源: From the verb "enshrine," which comes from the Old French "enshreindre" meaning "to enclose in a shrine."  Ultimately derived from the Latin "in" (in) and "scrinium" (chest, box, case).
