gormlessly:/ˈɡɔːmləsli/| adv.|愚蠢地，呆呆地|In a stupid or naive manner; without thinking.|He stared gormlessly at the television. 他呆呆地盯着电视。|Synonyms: stupidly, foolishly, vacantly, blankly, dazedly|Antonyms: intelligently, cleverly, wisely, sharply|Adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From gormless + -ly.  Gormless is from gorm (meaning "sense, understanding," now dialectal) + -less. Gorm is likely of Scandinavian origin.
