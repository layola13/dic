on-bench:/ˈɒnˈbɛntʃ/| adj.|替补的，未上场的；在工作台上的|Relating to a substitute player or staff member who is not actively participating in a game or project, or to something being worked on at a workbench. |The on-bench players were eager to get into the game. 替补球员渴望上场比赛。| The project is still on-bench, awaiting final approval.  该项目仍在工作台上，等待最终批准。|Synonyms: benched, reserve, substitute, inactive|Antonyms: active, starting, playing|adjective

on-bench:/ˈɒnˈbɛntʃ/| n.|替补席；工作台|The bench where substitute players sit or a workbench.|The coach called a player from the on-bench. 教练从替补席上叫了一名球员。| He left his tools on the on-bench. 他把工具留在了工作台上。|Synonyms: bench, workbench, workstation|Antonyms: field, court, game|noun
