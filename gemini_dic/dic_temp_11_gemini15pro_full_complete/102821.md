jarless:/ˈdʒɑːrlɪs/
adjective
无罐的；无瓶的；不用罐子的
without a jar; not preserved in a jar.
The jarless pickles were sold in a sealed plastic bag.  无罐泡菜装在密封塑料袋里出售。
The restaurant offers a jarless jam option for its breakfast service.  这家餐厅的早餐提供不用罐装的果酱选择。
近义词: unpackaged, loose
反义词: jarred, bottled, canned
词性: adjective
