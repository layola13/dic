curleycues: /ˈkɜːrliˌkjuːz/ (US), /ˈkɜːliˌkjuːz/ (UK)| n. |花体字，华丽的装饰, 弯曲的线条| Ornamental flourishes in handwriting, especially fancy loops and swirls.  These can also refer to anything that is excessively elaborate or ornate. |Her signature was full of curleycues. 她的签名充满了花体字。|近义词：flourishes, embellishments, swirls, loops|反义词: plainness, simplicity|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "curly," referring to the shape, and "cue," possibly from the letter Q, known for its tail, or from the French "queue," meaning tail.
