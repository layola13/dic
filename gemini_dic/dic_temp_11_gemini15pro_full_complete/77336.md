|例句：His gauche manners embarrassed his parents. 他笨拙的举止让他的父母感到尴尬。|

|词性：形容词|

词源:  From French *gauche* "left," also "awkward, clumsy," perhaps from Old Frankish *wenk*, related to Old High German *wank* "wavering, unsteady."  The connection between "left" and "awkward" may be due to the fact that most people are right-handed, making the left hand less skilled.
