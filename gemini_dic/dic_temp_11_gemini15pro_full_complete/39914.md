constrained:/kənˈstreɪnd/ | adj. | 受约束的，被迫的，拘泥的 | Limited or restricted; forced to act or behave in a particular way. |例句：He felt constrained to accept the offer. 他感到不得不接受这个提议。|近义词：restricted, limited, forced, compelled, obliged |反义词：free, unconstrained, liberated |词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *constrinctus, past participle of constringere (“to bind together, compress, constrict”), from Latin com- (“together”) + stringere (“to draw tight, bind”).
