circumfer:/sərˈkʌmfər/ | vt. | 包围，环绕 | To surround; to encompass. |  The soldiers circumfered the city. 士兵们包围了这座城市。

The fence circumfers the entire property. 篱笆环绕着整个地产。

近义词：encircle, surround, encompass, enclose | 反义词：expose, open | 词性：动词

词源：From Latin circumferre ("to carry around"), from circum ("around") + ferre ("to carry").
