arcing:/ˈɑːrkɪŋ/| adj.  v. (arcing, arced, arced)| 弧形的；产生电弧的；跳跃的 |Forming an arc or moving in a curve; producing an electrical arc. |The welding torch was arcing across the metal.  焊枪在金属上划出一道弧线。| The electricity arced across the gap. 电流跨越间隙形成电弧。 |近义词: curving, arching, sparking |反义词: straight, linear |词性: adjective, verb (present participle)
Etymology: From the noun "arc," ultimately from Latin "arcus."
