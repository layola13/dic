carvone:/ˈkɑːrvəʊn/ (US) /ˈkɑːvəʊn/ (UK)| n.|香芹酮；葛缕酮|A colorless or pale yellow liquid ketone, C₁₀H₁₄O, found in the essential oils of caraway, dill, and spearmint and used as a flavoring agent and fragrance.|Carvone is the main component responsible for the distinctive flavor of caraway seeds.|近义词:ketone (in terms of chemical classification)|反义词:N/A|noun
