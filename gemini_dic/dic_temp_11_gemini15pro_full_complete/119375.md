microcoulomb:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌkuːlɒm/ | n. | 微库仑 | A unit of electric charge equal to one millionth of a coulomb. | The capacitor stored a charge of 10 microcoulombs.  电容器储存了10微库仑的电荷。 | Synonyms: μC (symbol) | Antonyms: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From micro- + coulomb.  "Micro-" comes from Greek "mikros" meaning "small".  "Coulomb" is named after Charles-Augustin de Coulomb, a French physicist.
