durity:/ˈdjʊrɪti/ | n. | 硬度，坚硬 | Hardness, firmness; the quality or state of being hard or firm. | The durity of the diamond makes it ideal for cutting glass.  钻石的硬度使其成为切割玻璃的理想选择。 | 近义词: hardness, firmness, solidity, rigidity | 反义词: softness, flexibility, malleability | 词性:名词

词源:  "Durity" is not a standard English word. It appears to be a rare or obsolete form, possibly related to "dure," meaning "hard" or "lasting," from the Latin "durus."  Its usage is not recommended in modern English.  You should use "hardness" instead.
