cinnamene: /ˈsɪnəˌmiːn/ | n. | 肉桂烯，苯乙烯 |  A colorless, flammable liquid hydrocarbon, C8H8, with a strong aroma, used in making plastics, resins, and synthetic rubber.  It is also known as styrene. | Cinnamene is the traditional name for styrene, a key ingredient in polystyrene production.  肉桂烯是苯乙烯的传统名称，是聚苯乙烯生产中的关键成分。 | Cinnamene, when polymerized, forms polystyrene. 肉桂烯聚合后形成聚苯乙烯。 | synonym: styrene, vinylbenzene, phenylethylene | antonym:  (No direct antonym for a chemical compound) | noun
