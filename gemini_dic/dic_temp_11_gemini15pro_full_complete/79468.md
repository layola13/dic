gloat:/ɡləʊt/| vi.|幸灾乐祸，沾沾自喜|To dwell on one's own success or another's misfortune with smug satisfaction.|He gloated over his rival's failure. 他对竞争对手的失败幸灾乐祸。|Synonyms: relish, revel, crow|Antonyms: commiserate, sympathize|Verb
