antinomian:/ˌæntɪˈnoʊmiən/| adj. n.|反律主义的，反律主义者|Relating to the belief that Christians are released by grace from the obligation of observing the moral law.|He was accused of holding antinomian views. 他被指控持有反律主义观点。|Synonyms: lawless, heretical, dissident|Antonyms: orthodox, conformist|adjective
antinomian:/ˌæntɪˈnoʊmiən/| n.|反律主义者|A person who holds antinomian beliefs.|The antinomians were condemned by the church. 反律主义者被教会谴责。|Synonyms: heretic, dissenter|Antonyms: conformist, believer|noun
