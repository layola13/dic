eradicative:/ɪˈrædɪkeɪtɪv/
adjective
根除的，消除的
tending or serving to eradicate.
The government is taking eradicative measures to eliminate the invasive species. 政府正在采取根除措施以消除入侵物种。
The new drug has an eradicative effect on the virus.  这种新药对病毒有根除作用。
synonyms: destructive, eliminative, exterminative
antonyms: constructive, generative, preservative
