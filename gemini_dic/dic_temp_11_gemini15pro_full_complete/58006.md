driveways:/ˈdraɪvˌweɪz/
n.|车道；私家车道|A private road providing access to a house or garage.|The delivery truck pulled into the driveways. 送货卡车开进了车道。|近义词：drive, entrance, approach|反义词:exit, egress|词性:名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From drive + way.  "Drive" in this sense originates from the Old English *drīfan* meaning "to push, compel, force," later evolving to encompass the act of driving a vehicle. "Way" comes from the Old English *weg* meaning "road, path." The combination signifies a path designated for driving.
