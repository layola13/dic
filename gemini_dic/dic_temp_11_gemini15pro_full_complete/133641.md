nonformalism:/nɒnˈfɔːməlɪzəm/ | n. | 非形式主义 | The rejection of formalism, especially in art, literature, or philosophy, emphasizing content, function, or meaning over form or structure. |  Nonformalism in architecture prioritizes practicality over aesthetics.  非形式主义建筑优先考虑实用性而非美学。

Nonformalism in literature often focuses on the social or political message of a work. 文学中的非形式主义通常关注作品的社会或政治信息。 | 近义词: informality, antiformalism | 反义词: formalism | 词性: 名词
