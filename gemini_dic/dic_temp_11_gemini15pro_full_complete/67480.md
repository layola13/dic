extorts:/ɪkˈstɔːrts/
verb
勒索，敲诈
obtain (something, especially money) from someone by force, threats, or other unfair means.
The gangster extorts money from shopkeepers.  那个歹徒向店主勒索钱财。
近义词：coerces, forces, compels, pressures, intimidates
反义词：gives, donates, contributes
词性：v.

extorted:/ɪkˈstɔːrtɪd/
verb
勒索，敲诈（extort的过去式和过去分词）
obtained (something, especially money) from someone by force, threats, or other unfair means.
The kidnappers extorted a ransom from the family. 绑匪向这家人勒索赎金。
近义词：coerced, forced, compelled, pressured, intimidated
反义词：gave, donated, contributed
词性：v.

extorting:/ɪkˈstɔːrtɪŋ/
verb
勒索，敲诈（extort的现在分词）
obtaining (something, especially money) from someone by force, threats, or other unfair means.
He was caught extorting money from vulnerable elderly people. 他被抓到从脆弱的老人那里勒索钱财。
近义词：coercing, forcing, compelling, pressuring, intimidating
反义词：giving, donating, contributing
词性：v.
