experiment:/ɪkˈspɛrɪmənt/ (US) /ɪkˈspɛrɪmənt/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|实验，试验；尝试|a scientific procedure undertaken to make a discovery, test a hypothesis, or demonstrate a known fact. | Scientists conducted an experiment to test the new drug. 科学家们进行了一项实验来测试这种新药。|近义词：test, trial, research|反义词:observation|noun

experiment:/ɪkˈspɛrɪmənt/ (US) /ɪkˈspɛrɪmənt/ (UK)| vi. vt.|进行实验；尝试|to try out or test something. |He experimented with different painting techniques. 他尝试了不同的绘画技巧。|近义词：test, try, explore|反义词:observe|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin experimentum "a test, trial, proof," from Latin experiri "to try, test," from ex- "out" + periri "to try."
