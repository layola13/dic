Crassitude is a lack of refinement and sensitivity.
粗俗是指缺乏修养和敏感性。

His crassitude was evident in his loud, boisterous behavior.
他的粗俗表现在他吵闹喧哗的行为上。

The crassitude of the politician's comments offended many voters.
这位政客粗俗的言论冒犯了许多选民。


近义词：
vulgarity, insensitivity, crudeness, boorishness, coarseness
反义词：
refinement, sensitivity, sophistication, elegance, tact
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
