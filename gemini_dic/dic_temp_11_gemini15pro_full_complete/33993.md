kitchen /ˈkɪtʃɪn/
n.
厨房
A room or area where food is prepared and cooked.
She's busy in the kitchen preparing dinner. 她正忙着在厨房准备晚餐。
The kitchen is the heart of our home. 厨房是我们家的中心。
近义词: galley, cookhouse
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑餐厅 dining room)
词性: 名词
