chimneyed:/ˈtʃɪmnid/
adjective
有烟囱的
Having chimneys.
The chimneyed roofs of the village huddled together against the winter sky. 村子里那些带有烟囱的屋顶在冬日的天空下挤在一起。
The old chimneyed house stood on a hill overlooking the valley.  古老的有烟囱的房子矗立在可以俯瞰山谷的小山上。
synonyms: chimney-topped, chimney stack
antonyms: chimneyless


ETYMOLOGY->
From chimney + -ed.  "Chimney" comes from Old French "cheminée" (fireplace), from Late Latin "caminata" (fireplace, furnace), from Latin "caminus" (furnace, forge).
