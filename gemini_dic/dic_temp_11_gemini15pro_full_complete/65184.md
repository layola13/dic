ethological:/ˌiːθəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ /ˌiːθəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/
adjective
动物行为学的
Relating to the study of animal behavior in natural conditions.
Ethological studies have shown that some animals exhibit altruistic behavior. 动物行为学研究表明，一些动物表现出利他行为。
Ethological research often involves observing animals in their natural habitats. 动物行为学研究通常包括观察动物在其自然栖息地中的行为。
近义词: behavioral, instinctual
反义词: None
词性: adjective

ethology:/iːˈθɒlədʒi/ /iːˈθɑːlədʒi/
noun
动物行为学
The scientific and objective study of animal behavior, usually with a focus on behavior under natural conditions, and viewing behavior as an evolutionarily adaptive trait.
Ethology plays a crucial role in understanding animal communication. 动物行为学在理解动物交流方面起着至关重要的作用。
The study of ethology can provide insights into human behavior as well.  动物行为学的研究也可以提供对人类行为的洞察。
近义词:  animal behavior
反义词: None
词性: noun
