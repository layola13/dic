corsac:/ˈkɔːrsæk/ | n. | 柯萨克狐，草原狐 | A small, long-legged fox (Vulpes corsac) found in steppes and semi-deserts of Central Asia. |  The corsac is known for its large ears and sandy-colored fur. 柯萨克狐以其大耳朵和沙色的皮毛而闻名。

|  Corsacs are nocturnal hunters, preying on rodents and insects. 柯萨克狐是夜间活动的猎手，捕食啮齿动物和昆虫。 | 近义词: fox | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for an animal species) | 词性: noun
