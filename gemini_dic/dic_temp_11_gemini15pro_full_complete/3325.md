affordable:/əˈfɔːrdəbl/ /əˈfɔːrdibəl/| adj.|负担得起的，买得起的|Reasonably priced; not too expensive.|例句：The apartments are affordable for families with low incomes. 这些公寓对于低收入家庭来说是负担得起的。|近义词：cheap, inexpensive, reasonable, budget, economical, modest|反义词：expensive, costly, unaffordable, exorbitant, pricey|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From afford + -able.  "Afford" comes from Old English *geforðian "to carry out, perform, accomplish," from forð "forth, forward."  The suffix "-able" indicates capability or worthiness.
