homeboy:/ˈhoʊmbɔɪ/| n.|老乡，哥们，伙伴，密友|A close friend, especially a male friend from one's hometown or neighborhood.  Often used in African American Vernacular English (AAVE).|例句：He's my homeboy from back in the day.  他是我以前的老朋友。

例句：I was hanging out with my homeboys last night. 我昨晚和我的哥们们一起玩。|近义词：friend, buddy, pal, mate, dude, bro|反义词:enemy, foe|名词
