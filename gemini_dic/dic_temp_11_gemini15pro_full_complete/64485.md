ernestful: /ˈɜːrnɪstfʊl/
adjective
认真的；真诚的；严肃的
Serious and sincere in intention, purpose, or effort.
She gave an ernestful promise to help. 她认真地承诺会帮忙。
近义词: serious, sincere, solemn, earnest
反义词: frivolous, flippant, insincere, lighthearted
词性: adjective
词源: From "earnest" (originally meaning seriousness, zeal) + "-ful" (suffix indicating abundance or fullness).  "Earnest" itself comes from Old English "eornost" (seriousness), related to Old High German "ernust" and ultimately to Proto-Germanic "*er-nu-stiz" (strong resolution).
