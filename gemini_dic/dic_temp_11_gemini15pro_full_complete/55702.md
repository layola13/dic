diurnalist:/ˌdaɪˈɜːrnəlɪst/ | n. | 日报记者；白天活动的人或动物 | A journalist who works on a daily newspaper; an organism that is active during the day. |  Example 1: The diurnalist filed his story just before the deadline.  这位日报记者在截止日期前提交了他的报道。

Example 2:  Many desert animals are nocturnal, while others are diurnalist, adapting to the extreme temperatures. 许多沙漠动物是夜行性的，而另一些则是白天活动的，以适应极端的温度。 | 近义词: reporter, journalist, day worker | 反义词: nocturnalist | 词性: noun


diurnal:/daɪˈɜːrnl/ | adj. | 白天的，每日的 | Happening or active during the day. | Example 1: The diurnal rhythm of the tides is influenced by the moon. 潮汐的昼夜节律受月亮的影响。

Example 2: Diurnal flowers open in the morning and close at night.  昼开夜合的花在早上开放，晚上闭合。 | 近义词: daily, daytime | 反义词: nocturnal, nightly | 词性: adjective
