distally:/ˈdɪstəli/ /dɪˈstæli/| adj.|末梢地，远端地| Situated away from the center of the body or from the point of attachment.|The pain is located distally in the limb. 疼痛位于肢体远端。|近义词：peripheral, outward, terminal|反义词:proximal, central|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *distalis, from Latin distare (“to stand apart, be distant”), from dis- (“apart”) + stare (“to stand”).
