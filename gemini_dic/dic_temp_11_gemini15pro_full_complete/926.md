abuzz:/əˈbʌz/| adj.|充满嗡嗡声的，嘈杂的，兴奋的|Filled with a low, continuous humming sound; lively with activity and excitement.|The room was abuzz with excitement before the concert. 音乐会前，房间里充满了兴奋的嗡嗡声。|近义词：humming, buzzing, vibrant, lively, animated|反义词：quiet, silent, still, calm|词性:形容词
Etymology: a- (prefix meaning "in a state of") + buzz (imitating the sound of bees)
