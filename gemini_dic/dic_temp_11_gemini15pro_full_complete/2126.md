adaptednesses:/əˈdæptɪdnəsɪz/
n.
适应性，适应能力（复数）
The state or quality of being adapted; the process of adapting or being adapted.
Desert animals exhibit remarkable adaptednesses to their harsh environment. 沙漠动物对恶劣环境表现出非凡的适应能力。
近义词: adaptations, adjustments, accommodations
反义词: maladaptations, inabilities
词性: noun
