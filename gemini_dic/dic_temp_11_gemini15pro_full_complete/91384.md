hydrotherapic:/ˌhaɪdrəʊθəˈræpɪk/ | adj. |水疗法的，用水治疗的 | Relating to hydrotherapy; using water to treat illness or injury. | Hydrotherapic treatments can be very effective for muscle pain. 水疗法对肌肉疼痛非常有效。| 近义词: balneotherapeutic, hydropathic | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider allopathic or pharmaceutical) |词性: 形容词

hydrotherapy:/ˌhaɪdrəʊˈθɛrəpi/ | n. |水疗法 | The use of exercises in a pool as part of treatment for conditions such as arthritis or partial paralysis. | Hydrotherapy is often recommended for patients recovering from joint surgery. 水疗法通常被推荐用于关节手术后恢复的病人。| 近义词: balneotherapy, hydropathy | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: 名词
