nontransposable:/ˌnɒntrænˈspəʊzəbl/ | adj. | 不可转座的，不能移位的 | Not capable of being transposed; permanently fixed.  |  These genes are nontransposable and are inherited through generations. 这些基因是不可转座的，并且会代代相传。 |  The nontransposable elements of the genome provide stability. 基因组中不可转座的元件提供了稳定性。 | fixed, immobile, stationary |  mobile, transposable | adjective

Etymology:
non- (prefix): not
transposable (adjective): capable of being transposed (from Latin transponere, meaning "to put across, transfer")
