granuliform:/ˈɡræn.jʊ.lɪ.fɔːrm/
adjective
颗粒状的；粒状的
Resembling or consisting of granules.
The soil sample was granuliform, composed of small, distinct particles. 土壤样本呈颗粒状，由小的、不同的颗粒组成。
The cheese had a granuliform texture.  这种奶酪有颗粒状的质地。
近义词: granular, grainy
反义词: smooth, uniform
词性: adjective
