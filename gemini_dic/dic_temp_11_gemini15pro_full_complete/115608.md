marcasite:/ˈmɑːrkəsaɪt/ | n. |白铁矿|A mineral, FeS₂, crystallizing in the orthorhombic system, typically pale yellow to bronze in color with a metallic luster. It is dimorphous with pyrite and readily alters to pyrite, often preserving the marcasite crystal form.|Marcasite is often used in costume jewelry because of its attractive luster. 白铁矿由于其迷人的光泽，常用于制作服饰珠宝。|Synonyms: pyrite, white iron pyrite|Antonyms: None|Noun

marcasite:/ˈmɑːrkəsaɪt/ | n. |白铁矿(宝石)|A yellowish mineral (FeS2) used as a gemstone and in ornaments. |The antique brooch was set with marcasite and small pearls.  这枚古董胸针镶嵌着白铁矿和一些小珍珠。|Synonyms: pyrite, white iron pyrite|Antonyms: None|Noun
