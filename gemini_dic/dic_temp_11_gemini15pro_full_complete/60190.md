efficiencies:/ɪˈfɪʃənsiz/| n. |效率，效益|the state or quality of being efficient; the ability to avoid wasting materials, energy, efforts, money, or time in doing something or in producing a desired result.  |例句：The company is looking for ways to improve its efficiencies.  公司正在寻找提高效率的方法。

例句：The new machines have led to significant gains in efficiencies. 新机器带来了效率的显著提高。|近义词：productivity, effectiveness, performance, competence|反义词：inefficiencies, ineffectiveness, incompetence|词性:名词(plural)
