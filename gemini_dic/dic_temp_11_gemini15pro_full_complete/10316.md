appealability:/əˌpiːləˈbɪləti/
n.
可上诉性
The quality of being subject to appeal; the ability to be appealed.
The appealability of the judge's decision is in question.  法官判决的可上诉性受到质疑。
近义词:reviewability
反义词:finality
词性:noun


appealability:/əˌpiːləˈbɪləti/
n.
上诉权
The right to appeal a decision.
The defendant has the appealability to take his case to a higher court. 被告有权将案件上诉到更高级的法院。
近义词:right of appeal
反义词:no right of appeal
词性:noun
