dishonestly:/dɪsˈɒnɪstli/ | adv. | 不诚实地 | In a manner that lacks honesty or integrity; deceitfully. | He acted dishonestly by cheating on the exam. 他考试作弊，行为不诚实。|  She dishonestly claimed the prize money, even though she knew she hadn't won. 她不诚实地认领了奖金，即使她知道自己没有赢。 | 近义词: deceitfully, fraudulently, falsely | 反义词: honestly, truthfully, honorably | 词性: 副词
词源: From dishonest + -ly.  Dishonest from dis- (not) + honest (from Old French honeste, from Latin honestus "honorable").
