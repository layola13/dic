nagyagite:/ˈnɑːdjɑːɡɪtaɪt/| n. |叶碲铋矿|A rare telluride mineral composed of lead, gold, tellurium, sulfur, and antimony.  It is typically found in low-sulfidation epithermal gold deposits.|Nagyagite is a valuable ore of tellurium and sometimes gold.  叶碲铋矿是一种有价值的碲矿石，有时也是金矿石。|Synonyms: None (due to its specific chemical composition)|Antonyms: None|Noun
