outmeasuring:/ˌaʊtˈmɛʒərɪŋ/
verb
超过，胜过
surpassing in measurement; exceeding in size, extent, or degree.
The new skyscraper outmeasured all the others in the city. 这座新的摩天大楼超过了城市里的所有其他建筑。
outstrip, surpass, exceed, outdo
fall short of, be inferior to
