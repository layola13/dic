obtuse:/əbˈtjuːs/ /ɑːbˈtuːs/| adj.|迟钝的，愚笨的；（角）钝的，大于90度小于180度的|Lacking sharpness or quickness of sensibility or intellect; also, greater than 90 degrees and less than 180 degrees.|He made an obtuse remark that offended everyone. 他说了一句愚蠢的话，冒犯了所有人。

The obtuse angle of the triangle measured 120 degrees. 这个三角形的钝角是120度。|近义词：dull, stupid, blunt, insensitive|反义词：acute, sharp, intelligent, perceptive|词性:形容词

obtuse:/əbˈtjuːs/ /ɑːbˈtuːs/| n.|钝角|An angle greater than 90 degrees and less than 180 degrees.|The architect used many obtuseness in his design.  这位建筑师在他的设计中使用了许多钝角。 (较少用作名词，通常用 obtuse angle)|近义词：obtuse angle|反义词：acute angle|词性:名词 (罕用)
