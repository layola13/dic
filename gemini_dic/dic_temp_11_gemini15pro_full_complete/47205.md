daylighting:/ˈdeɪˌlaɪtɪŋ/
n.
采光;日光照明
The use of natural light to illuminate building interiors.
The architects used daylighting strategies to reduce energy consumption.  建筑师们利用采光策略来降低能源消耗。
natural lighting, illumination
artificial lighting
