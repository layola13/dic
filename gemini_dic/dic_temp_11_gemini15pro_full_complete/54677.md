disorder:/dɪsˈɔːrdər/ /dɪsˈɔːrdɚ/ | n. vt. |混乱，失调；疾病 |A state of confusion or lack of organization; an illness that disrupts normal physical or mental functions.|The room was in a state of disorder. 房间里一片混乱。|近义词：chaos, mess, confusion, illness, ailment, sickness|反义词：order, organization, health|词性:名词

disorder:/dɪsˈɔːrdər/ /dɪsˈɔːrdɚ/ | vt. |扰乱，使失调|To disrupt the systematic functioning or arrangement of.|Stress can disorder the body's natural rhythms. 压力会扰乱身体的自然节律。|近义词：disrupt, disturb, upset|反义词：organize, order, arrange|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *desordre*, from Late Latin disordō, from dis- (“not, un-”) + ordō (“order, row, rank”).
