cocultivated:/kəʊˈkʌltɪveɪtɪd/
|adj.|共同培养的，共同栽培的| Grown or cultivated together; specifically, (of bacterial or other cells) grown together in a mixed culture.  This often leads to symbiotic or synergistic interactions.|The researchers cocultivated two different strains of bacteria to observe their interactions.|近义词：co-cultured, grown together|反义词: separately cultivated, monocultured|词性: adjective
cocultivate:/ˌkoʊˈkʌltɪveɪt/
|v.|共同培养，共同栽培|To grow or cultivate (different species, especially of bacteria or other cells) together.|They cocultivated the cancer cells with immune cells to study the immune response.|近义词：co-culture, grow together|反义词: separately cultivate, monoculture|词性: verb
