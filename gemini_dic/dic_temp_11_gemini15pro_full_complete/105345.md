kickable:/ˈkɪkəbl/| adj.|可踢的，可以踢的|Something that is kickable can be kicked.  This usually refers to something that is of a suitable size, shape, and material to be kicked without causing undue harm to the kicker or the object.  It can also imply that something deserves to be kicked (though this is less common).|The ball was old and worn, but still perfectly kickable.  这个球虽然又旧又破，但仍然可以踢。

The annoying little robot was small and kickable, but I resisted the urge.  那个烦人的小机器人又小又可以踢，但我忍住了冲动。|Synonyms: bootable (informal, usually applied to a person),  |Antonyms: unkickable|adjective
