PHRASE->
Over the kop = 死掉了，完蛋了 (over the hill)
Kop it = 承受它 (take it, endure it - often used in the context of punishment)
Up the kop =  加油 (come on, encouragement - especially in sports)
Keep your kop down = 小心点，别惹麻烦 (stay out of trouble, be careful)
Lose your kop =  失去冷静 (lose your temper, become angry)


注意事项:  South African usage of "kop" is more common than British slang usage.  "Kop" as a slang term for police is declining in usage.
