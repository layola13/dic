nonrecoupable:/ˌnɑnriˈkupəbl/
adjective
不可收回的，不可补偿的
Not capable of being recouped or recovered (especially of an expense).
例句：The company incurred nonrecoupable expenses during the failed project.  公司在失败的项目中产生了不可收回的费用。

例句：Nonrecoupable startup costs can be a significant barrier to entry for new businesses.  不可收回的启动成本可能是新企业进入市场的重要障碍。

近义词：nonrecoverable, irrecoverable, irretrievable
反义词：recoverable, recoupable
词性：adjective
