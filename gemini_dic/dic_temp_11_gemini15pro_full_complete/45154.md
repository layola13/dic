cudgellers:/ˈkʌdʒələrz/| n. |棍棒打手; 用棍棒打的人|People who beat others with cudgels (short, thick sticks).|The cudgellers attacked the traveler on the lonely road. 棍棒打手在偏僻的路上袭击了旅行者。|Synonyms: thugs, assailants, attackers|Antonyms: protectors, defenders|Noun

cudgel:/ˈkʌdʒəl/| n. |棍棒; 短粗的棍子|A short, thick stick used as a weapon.|He defended himself with a cudgel. 他用一根棍棒自卫。|Synonyms: club, stick, bludgeon|Antonyms: shield, protection|Noun

cudgel:/ˈkʌdʒəl/| v. |用棍棒打|To beat with a cudgel.|He was cudgelled into submission. 他被打得屈服了。|Synonyms: bludgeon, club, beat|Antonyms: protect, defend|Verb
