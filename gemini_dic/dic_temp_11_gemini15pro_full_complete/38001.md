complimentary:/ˌkɒmplɪˈmentəri/ /ˌkɑːmplɪˈmentəri/| adj.|免费的，赠送的；赞美的，恭维的|Expressing praise or admiration; given or supplied free of charge.|例句：The hotel provided complimentary breakfast.  酒店提供免费早餐。

例句：He made a complimentary remark about her new dress. 他对她的新裙子赞不绝口。|近义词：free, gratis, flattering, laudatory|反义词：paid, critical, derogatory|词性:adjective

compliment:/ˈkɒmplɪmənt/ /ˈkɑːmplɪmənt/| n. vt.|赞美，恭维；致意，问候|An expression of praise, admiration, or congratulation.  To express praise or admiration.|例句：She received many compliments on her performance. 她因表演出色而受到很多赞扬。

例句：He complimented her on her excellent work. 他称赞她工作出色。|近义词：praise, commendation, tribute|反义词：criticism, insult|词性:noun, verb


**词源:**

From Late Latin *complementum*, meaning "completion, fulfillment,"  from Latin *complere*, "to fill up, complete." The meaning shifted to "expression of courtesy" and "something given freely" over time.
