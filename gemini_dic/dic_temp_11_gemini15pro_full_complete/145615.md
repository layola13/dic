overconservativenesses:/ˌoʊvərˌkənˈsɜːrvətɪvnəsɪz/
noun
过度保守性
The state of being excessively conservative, marked by extreme caution and resistance to change.
Their overconservativenesses in financial matters hampered the company's growth.  他们在财务问题上的过度保守阻碍了公司的发展。
Synonyms: cautiousness, wariness, timidness, hesitance
Antonyms: recklessness, boldness, audacity, daring
n.

conservativeness:/kənˈsɜːrvətɪvnəs/
noun
保守性
The quality of being conservative; aversion to change or innovation.
The conservativeness of the community made it difficult to implement new ideas.  社区的保守性使得实施新想法变得困难。
Synonyms: caution, wariness, timidity, hesitance
Antonyms: recklessness, boldness, audacity, daring
n.


conservative:/kənˈsɜːrvətɪv/
adjective|noun
保守的；保守主义者|
Favoring traditional views and values; tending to oppose change.  A person who is averse to change and holds traditional values.
He has a conservative attitude towards social issues. 他对社会问题的态度很保守。
She is a member of the Conservative Party. 她是保守党的一员。
Synonyms: traditional, conventional, orthodox, reactionary (adj.); traditionalist, right-winger (n.)
Antonyms: liberal, progressive, radical, revolutionary (adj.); liberal, progressive, radical (n.)
adj.|n.


ETYMOLOGY
From Late Latin *conservativus, from Latin conservare (“to preserve”).
