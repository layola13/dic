body-forth: /ˈbɒdi fɔːθ/ (UK) /ˈbɑːdi fɔːrθ/ (US)
verb
体现，象征，展现
to express or represent (something abstract) in a physical or concrete form.
The dancer's movements bodied forth the emotions of the music. 舞者的动作展现了音乐的情感。
Synonyms: embody, manifest, express, externalize, symbolize
Antonyms: conceal, hide, suppress
