ideologies:/ˌaɪdiˈɒlədʒiz/ (UK) /ˌiːdiˈɑːlədʒiz/ (US)
n.
意识形态；思想体系
A system of ideas and ideals, especially one that forms the basis of economic or political theory and policy.
例句：
The two parties’ ideologies differ greatly. 这两个政党的意识形态大相径庭。

Their ideologies were shaped by their experiences during the war. 他们的意识形态是由他们在战争期间的经历塑造的。


近义词：beliefs, doctrines, tenets, principles, philosophies
反义词：- (No direct antonym, consider using opposing ideologies)
词性: noun (plural)


Etymology:
From French idéologie, from Greek idea 'form, pattern' + -logie '-logy'.
