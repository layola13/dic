overloop:/ˈoʊvərˌfluː/ (US), /ˈəʊvəˌfluː/ (UK)| n. |溢流，溢出；溢流管；溢流槽；（运河的）调节水位的小水池|An overloop is a structure that allows excess water to flow out of a container or system, preventing it from overflowing.|The bathtub's overloop prevented the water from spilling onto the floor. 浴缸的溢流装置防止了水溢到地板上。|Near synonyms: overflow, spillway, outlet|Antonym: inlet|noun

overloop:/ˈoʊvərˌfluː/ (US), /ˈəʊvəˌfluː/ (UK)| v. |溢出，泛滥|To overloop means to flow over the edge of a container or system.|The river overlooped its banks during the heavy rain.  河水在大雨中漫过了河岸。|Near synonyms: overflow, flood, brim over|Antonym: drain|verb
