fideistic:/ˌfaɪˈdiːɪstɪk/
| adj. |信仰主义的；盲信的|Based on faith rather than reason; believing something is true without needing proof or evidence.  |例句：He held a fideistic view of religion, accepting its tenets without question. 他对宗教持有一种信仰主义的观点，不加质疑地接受其信条。|例句：Her fideistic approach to life meant she trusted her gut feelings above all else. 她对生活的信仰主义态度意味着她最相信自己的直觉。|近义词：dogmatic, doctrinaire, credulous |反义词：skeptical, rational, empirical |词性: 形容词
Etymology: From Latin *fides* (faith) + *-istic* (suffix forming adjectives).
