diamorphines:/ˌdaɪəˈmɔːrfiːnz/
noun
二乙酰吗啡；海洛因
Diamorphine is a strong opioid painkiller.  It is also known as heroin.
Diamorphine is used medically to relieve severe pain. 二乙酰吗啡在医学上用于缓解剧烈疼痛。
Diamorphine can be highly addictive and its non-medical use is illegal in most countries. 二乙酰吗啡很容易上瘾，在大多数国家，其非医疗用途是非法的。
synonyms: heroin, morphine, opiate, opioid
antonyms: naloxone (antagonist), Narcan (brand name of naloxone)
