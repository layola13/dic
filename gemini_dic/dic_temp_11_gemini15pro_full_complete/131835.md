nonconsonance:/nɑːnˈkɑːnsənəns/
n.
不和谐，不一致
lack of harmony or agreement; dissonance.
The nonconsonance between her words and actions made her seem untrustworthy. 她言行不一致，让人觉得她不值得信任。
Near Antonyms: consonance, harmony, agreement, concord
Antonyms: dissonance, discord, disagreement
noun
