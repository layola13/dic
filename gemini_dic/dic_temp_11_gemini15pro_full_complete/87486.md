hetero-agglutination:/ˌhɛtərəˌæɡluːtɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |异种凝集|The clumping together of different types of cells, such as red blood cells of different species, by an antibody or other agglutinin.|Example: Hetero-agglutination occurred when the rabbit serum was mixed with the sheep red blood cells.  兔血清与绵羊红细胞混合后发生了异种凝集。|Example:  The test demonstrated hetero-agglutination between human and rat cells.  该测试证明了人类细胞和大鼠细胞之间的异种凝集。|近义词: cross-agglutination|反义词: isoagglutination|词性:noun


hetero-:/ˈhɛtərəʊ/| prefix. |异，不同的|From Greek *héteros, meaning "other, different".  Indicates difference or dissimilarity.|Example: Heterosexual refers to attraction to the opposite sex. 异性恋指的是对异性的吸引。|Example: A heterogeneous mixture is made up of different substances.  异质混合物由不同的物质组成。|近义词: allo-, different|反义词: homo-, same|词性:prefix


agglutination:/əˌɡluːtɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |凝集|The clumping together of particles, such as red blood cells or bacteria, usually by antibodies.|Example: Blood typing relies on the agglutination of red blood cells. 血型鉴定依赖于红细胞的凝集。|Example: Bacterial agglutination can be used for diagnostic purposes. 细菌凝集可用于诊断目的。|近义词: clumping, aggregation|反义词: dispersion|词性:noun
