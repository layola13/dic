camberkeeled:/ˈkæmbərˌkiːld/ | adj. | 龙骨拱起的，弧形龙骨的 | Having a curved or arched keel.  A camberkeeled hull is designed to reduce drag and improve stability, especially in shallow water. | The traditional fishing boats of the region are characterized by their camberkeeled hulls. 该地区的传统渔船的特点是船体龙骨拱起。| Near Synonyms: arched keel, curved keel, rockered keel | Antonyms: flat-bottomed, flat keel | adjective
