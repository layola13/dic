avocative:/əˈvɒkətɪv/ | adj. | 唤起的，引起联想的 | Calling forth a particular feeling or image.  Evoking a strong emotional response. | The evocative scent of woodsmoke reminded her of childhood camping trips.  柴火燃烧的唤起记忆的气味让她想起了童年时的露营旅行。 | evocative music, an evocative poem | The scent was evocative of happy memories. 这香味唤起了美好的回忆。|近义词：suggestive, reminiscent, redolent, resonant | 反义词：uninspiring, bland | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Late Latin *avocativus, from Latin avocare ("to call away, to summon").  Ultimately from ad- ("to") + vocare ("to call").
