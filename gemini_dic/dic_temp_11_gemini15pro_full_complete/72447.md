flukeworm:/ˈfluːkwɜːrm/| n. | 吸虫 | A parasitic flatworm of the class Trematoda, typically having suckers and hooks for attachment to a host. |  Flukeworms can infect various organs in humans and animals. 吸虫可以感染人类和动物的各种器官。| Example 1: The veterinarian diagnosed the dog with a liver flukeworm infection. 兽医诊断这只狗感染了肝吸虫。

Example 2: Flukeworms are a common parasite in fish. 吸虫是鱼类中常见的寄生虫。| synonym: trematode, flatworm | antonym: None (no direct opposite for a biological organism) | noun
