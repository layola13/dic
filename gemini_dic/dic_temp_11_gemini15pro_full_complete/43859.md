crescentade:/ˌkrɛsənˈteɪd/
n.
新月形面包;牛角面包;
A rich, flaky crescent roll, often served at breakfast.
She warmed up a few crescentades for breakfast. 她烤了几个牛角面包当早餐。

crescent:/ˈkrɛsənt/
n.
新月;月牙;新月状物体;
The curved shape of the waxing or waning moon.  A thing shaped like a crescent.
The moon was a slender crescent. 月亮是一弯细细的新月。
The flags bore a white crescent on a red background. 旗帜上是红色背景衬托着白色的新月。

ade:/eɪd/
suffix.
(forming nouns) a sweet drink made with the juice of the specified fruit.
Used to form nouns referring to sweet drinks made with fruit juice.
lemonade, orangeade 柠檬汁，橙汁


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cressentade*, from *creissant* "crescent," past participle of *creistre* "to grow," from Latin *crescere*. The suffix *-ade* denotes a sweet drink or dish, but in this case, it refers to the crescent shape of the pastry.
