incivilization:/ɪnˌsɪvɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 不文明；野蛮；未开化 |The state of being uncivilized or lacking culture and refinement.  |  His incivilization was apparent in his rude behavior. 他的不文明行为表现在他粗鲁的举止上。| Example: The tribe lived in a state of incivilization, with no written language or formal laws.  这个部落生活在未开化的状态，没有文字或正式的法律。 |Synonyms: barbarity, savagery, primitiveness | Antonyms: civilization, refinement, culture | Noun
