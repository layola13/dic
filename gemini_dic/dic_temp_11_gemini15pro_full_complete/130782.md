nonangelic:/ˌnɒnænˈdʒɛlɪk/
adjective
非天使的，不像天使的，不纯洁的
Not angelic; not like an angel; not innocent or pure.
Her nonangelic behavior shocked her parents. 她不像天使的行为震惊了她的父母。
Her nonangelic demeanor made her seem more relatable. 她不像天使的风度让她看起来更平易近人。

近义词:devilish, demonic, impish, wicked, sinful
反义词:angelic, cherubic, innocent, pure, virtuous
词性:adjective
