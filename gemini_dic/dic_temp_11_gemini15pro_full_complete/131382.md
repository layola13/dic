noncircumspectness:/ˌnɒnsɜːrkəmˈspɛktnəs/
noun
缺乏谨慎；轻率；鲁莽
Lack of caution or prudence; rashness.
His noncircumspectness led him to make a costly mistake. 他的轻率导致他犯了一个代价高昂的错误。
Synonyms: imprudence, recklessness, carelessness, indiscretion, rashness
Antonyms: caution, prudence, circumspection, discretion, carefulness
n.

Etymology:
From non- (not) + circumspect (careful to consider all circumstances and possible consequences) + -ness (forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  Circumspect comes from Latin circumspectus, past participle of circumspicere "to look around," from circum- "around" + specere "to look."
