balkers:/ˈbɔːkərz/| n.|犹豫者，畏缩不前的人|People who refuse to proceed or cooperate, often due to a sudden fear or unwillingness.|The balkers refused to sign the agreement, holding up the entire process. 犹豫者拒绝签署协议，拖延了整个进程。|近义词：hesitators, objectors, resisters|反义词:compliers, cooperators|noun

balker:/ˈbɔːkər/| n.|犹豫者，畏缩不前的人|A person who refuses to proceed or cooperate, often due to a sudden fear or unwillingness.|He was a balker when it came to jumping off the high dive.  在跳高台跳水这件事上，他是一个犹豫不决的人。|近义词：hesitator, objector, resister|反义词:complier, cooperator|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "balk," which originates from Middle English balken "to hesitate, stop short," probably of Scandinavian origin.
