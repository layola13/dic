bimetallic:/baɪməˈtælɪk/
adjective
双金属的
Made of two different metals, especially used of a strip of two such metals welded together, which bends when heated because the metals expand at different rates.
例句：A bimetallic strip is used in thermostats. 双金属片用于恒温器中。

例句：The watch features a bimetallic balance wheel for improved accuracy. 这款手表采用双金属平衡摆轮以提高精度。


近义词：two-metal
反义词：monometallic
词性：adjective
