outkill:/ˌaʊtˈkɪl/
verb
超过…的杀伤力；比…杀死的更多
to kill more than; to surpass in killing
The new virus outkilled the previous strain.  这种新病毒比之前的毒株致死人数更多。
The experienced hunter outkilled the novice.  经验丰富的猎人比新手猎杀的更多。
近义词: outdo, surpass, excel
反义词:  be outdone, be surpassed
词性: verb
