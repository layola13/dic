biotechnologically: /ˌbaɪoʊˌtɛknəˈlɒdʒɪkli/
adverb
生物技术地
In a way that relates to or uses biotechnology.
The product was biotechnologically engineered to be more resistant to pests.  这个产品是用生物技术设计的，对害虫更有抵抗力。
Genetically modified crops are biotechnologically produced. 转基因作物是通过生物技术生产的。
Synonym:  genetically, using biotechnology
Antonym: naturally, traditionally
词性: 副词
