heronsew:/ˈhɛrənˌsuː/
n.
鹭鸶缝合; 苍鹭缝合
A type of surgical stitch used to close wounds, resembling the beak of a heron.  It is a continuous suture technique used for closing the subcutaneous layer of the skin or for approximating tissues under tension.
The surgeon used a heronsew stitch to close the long incision.  外科医生用鹭鸶缝合缝合了长长的切口。
近义词: continuous suture, subcuticular suture
词性: 名词
