counterpropaganda:/ˌkaʊntərˌprɒpəˈɡændə/ | n. | 反宣传 |  Activities designed to expose and discredit propaganda, especially that of an enemy. | The government launched a counterpropaganda campaign to correct the misinformation being spread. 政府发起了一场反宣传运动，以纠正正在传播的错误信息。| The news report was clearly a piece of counterpropaganda aimed at undermining public trust. 这篇新闻报道显然是一则反宣传，旨在破坏公众信任。 |Synonym: counter-information, rebuttal, debunking | Antonym: propaganda | noun
