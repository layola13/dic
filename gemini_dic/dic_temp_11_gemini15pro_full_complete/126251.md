ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *μυδρίασις (mudríasis)  "excessive dilation of the pupil,"  from μυδριᾶν (mudriân, "to be excessively dilated"). Ultimately from the same Proto-Indo-European root (*mew-) as "mute" and "mouse," referring to the silent, hidden nature of the pupil in this condition.
