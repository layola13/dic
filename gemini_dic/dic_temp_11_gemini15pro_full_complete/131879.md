noncontaminable:/ˌnɒnkənˈtæmɪnəbl/
adjective
不可污染的
Not susceptible to contamination; that cannot be contaminated.
The new material is noncontaminable and ideal for use in clean rooms. 这种新材料不可污染，非常适合在洁净室中使用。
Surgical instruments must be noncontaminable. 外科手术器械必须是不可污染的。
近义词:impervious, resistant, immune
反义词:contaminable, susceptible, vulnerable
词性:adjective

Etymology:
non- (prefix): not
contaminable (adjective): capable of being contaminated
From contaminate + -able.  Contaminate comes from Latin contaminatus, past participle of contaminare "to pollute," from com- "together" + *tagmen- "touching," from tangere "to touch."
