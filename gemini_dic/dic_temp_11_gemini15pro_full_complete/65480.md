europarliamentary:/ˌjʊərəʊˌpɑːrləˈmɛntəri/
adjective
欧洲议会的
Relating to the European Parliament.
The europarliamentary elections are held every five years. 欧洲议会选举每五年举行一次。
The committee is examining europarliamentary legislation on data protection.  该委员会正在审查欧洲议会关于数据保护的立法。
近义词: European Parliamentary
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "national parliamentary")
