microbore: /ˈmaɪkrəʊbɔːr/ (UK) /ˈmaɪkroʊbɔːr/ (US)
|n. adj.|微孔; 微孔的|A very small bore, especially of a tube or pipe.  Relating to or having a very small bore, especially of a tube or pipe.|
|例句：
The microbore tubing is used in chromatography.  这种微孔管用于色谱分析。
Microbore columns are more efficient for HPLC. 微孔柱对于高效液相色谱更有效。
|近义词：capillary, fine-bore|
|反义词：wide-bore, large-bore|
|词性：名词，形容词|
|词源： "micro-" + "bore". "Micro-" comes from Greek "mikros" meaning "small". "Bore" comes from Old English "borian" meaning "to pierce, to make a hole".|
