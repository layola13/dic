free-to-air:/ˌfriː tuː ˈeər/ | adj. | 免费的（广播电视节目） | Broadcast or transmitted over the airwaves and available to anyone with a receiver, without subscription fees. |  Example: Many popular TV channels are free-to-air. 许多受欢迎的电视频道是免费的。| Synonym: free, over-the-air, terrestrial | Antonym: subscription, pay-per-view, cable, satellite |词性:形容词
