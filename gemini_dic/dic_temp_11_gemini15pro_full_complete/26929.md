calamus:/ˈkæləməs/| n. |（尤指圣经中提到的）香料，香草 |Aromatic spice obtained from the root of the sweet flag.|Calamus is mentioned several times in the Bible as a fragrant spice. 圣经中多次提到菖蒲是一种芳香的香料。|Near synonyms: spice, fragrance, aroma |Antonym: N/A |noun
