hauntedness:/ˈhɔːntɪdnəs/ | n. |  闹鬼，阴森森 | The state of being haunted; the atmosphere of a place that is haunted. | The hauntedness of the old house made the children afraid to go inside. 这栋老房子的阴森气氛让孩子们不敢进去。 | synonyms: eerieness, spookiness, ghostliness | antonyms: normalcy, peacefulness | noun

hauntedness:/ˈhɔːntɪdnəs/ | n. |  萦绕心头，挥之不去 | The state of being preoccupied by a thought, memory, or feeling. | The hauntedness of his past mistakes continued to plague him. 他过去犯的错误一直萦绕在他的心头，挥之不去。 | synonyms: preoccupation, obsession, fixation | antonyms: detachment, indifference | noun
