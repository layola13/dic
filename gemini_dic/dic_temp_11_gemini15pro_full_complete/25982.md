bursary: /ˈbɜːrsəri/ (UK) /ˈbɜːrsəri/ (US)
n.
奖学金；助学金
A grant, especially one awarded to enable a student to attend college or university.
She received a bursary to study abroad. 她获得了出国留学的奖学金。
He was awarded a bursary to cover his tuition fees. 他获得了支付学费的奖学金。
scholarship, grant, fellowship, award
loan, debt
noun
