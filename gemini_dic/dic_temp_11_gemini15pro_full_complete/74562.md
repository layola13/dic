fraudulent:/ˈfrɔːdʒələnt/| adj.|欺骗的，欺诈的|Intended to deceive or defraud; based on or obtained by fraud.  Characterized by, involving, or proceeding from fraud.|例句：The company was accused of fraudulent accounting practices.  该公司被指控犯有欺诈性会计行为。|近义词：dishonest, deceitful, deceptive, crooked, shady|反义词：honest, genuine, authentic|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from Old French fraudulente, from Latin fraudulent- ‘acting deceitfully’, from the verb fraudare, from fraus, fraud- ‘deceit, injury’.
