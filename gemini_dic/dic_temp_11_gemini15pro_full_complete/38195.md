compulsively:/kəmˈpʌlsɪvli/ | adv. | 强迫地，不由自主地 | Doing something repeatedly and irresistibly, often without conscious control. | He compulsively washes his hands every few minutes. 他每隔几分钟就强迫性地洗手。| compulsively checks her phone. 她强迫性地检查她的手机。 | 近义词: obsessively, involuntarily, automatically | 反义词: willingly, voluntarily, intentionally | 词性: 副词 | 词源: From Latin compulsus, past participle of compellere "to compel," from com- "together" + pellere "to drive."
