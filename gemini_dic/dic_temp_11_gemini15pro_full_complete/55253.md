dissimulation:/dɪˌsɪmjʊˈleɪʃn/
|n.|掩饰，假装，伪装|The act of concealing one's true feelings or intentions.|Her dissimulation was so perfect that no one suspected her true motives. 她的伪装如此完美，以至于没有人怀疑她的真实动机。|Synonyms: pretense, deception, feigning, hypocrisy|Antonyms: honesty, sincerity, candor|noun
