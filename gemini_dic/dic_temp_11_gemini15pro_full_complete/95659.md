incomprehensibly:/ˌɪnkɑːmprɪˈhensɪbli/ | adv. | 难以理解地，莫名其妙地 | in a way that is impossible to understand | He muttered something incomprehensibly and walked away. 他咕哝着一些难以理解的话，然后走开了。| The instructions were written so incomprehensibly that nobody could follow them.  说明书写得让人难以理解，以至于没有人能看得懂。 | inexplicably, unintelligibly | understandably, clearly | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + comprehensible (understandable) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Comprehensible" comes from the Latin "comprehendere" (to grasp mentally), from "com-" (together) + "prehendere" (to seize).
