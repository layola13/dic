inter-business:/ˌɪntərˈbɪznəs/| adj.|企业间的，公司间的|Relating to or involving dealings between different businesses.|例句：Inter-business transactions are crucial for a healthy economy.  企业间的交易对健康的经济至关重要。|例句：The conference focused on fostering inter-business collaboration.  这次会议的重点是促进企业间的合作。|近义词：business-to-business (B2B), commercial|反义词: consumer, individual|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From "inter-" (prefix meaning "between, among") + "business"
