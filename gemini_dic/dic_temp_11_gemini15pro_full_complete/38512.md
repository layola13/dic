conciliative:/kənˈsɪliətɪv/
adjective
调解的；安抚的；和解的
Intended or likely to placate or pacify.
例句：She took a conciliative approach to the dispute, hoping to avoid further conflict. 她对这场纠纷采取了安抚的态度，希望能避免进一步的冲突。

例句：His conciliative tone helped to calm the angry crowd.  他 conciliatory 的语气帮助平息了愤怒的人群。

近义词：appeasing, pacifying, mollifying, soothing, placatory,  propitiatory
反义词：antagonistic, aggressive, hostile, provocative
词性: adjective


词源：
From Late Latin conciliativus, from conciliare (to bring together, win over), from concilium (council).
