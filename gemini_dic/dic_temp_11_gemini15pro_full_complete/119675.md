midge:/mɪdʒ/
n.
小蚊；蠓
A small two-winged fly, especially one that bites or causes annoyance.
例句：The midges were swarming around our heads as we walked through the woods.  我们穿过树林时，小蚊子成群地在我们头上飞来飞去。

例句：She swatted at the midges buzzing around her face. 她拍打着在她脸上嗡嗡叫的小蚊子。


近义词：gnat, mosquito
反义词:  (无明确的反义词)
词性: 名词
