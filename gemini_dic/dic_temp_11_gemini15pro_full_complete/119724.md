midsts:/mɪdst/ |n.|中间，当中，围绕|Being in the midst of something means being surrounded by it or being right in the middle of it.  It can refer to a physical location, a group of people, or a situation.|
例句：
He found himself in the midst of a cheering crowd.  他发现自己身处欢呼的人群中。

例句：
She's in the midst of a busy week. 她正处于忙碌的一周中。

近义词：middle, center, heart, thick
反义词：edge, periphery, outside
词性: 名词
