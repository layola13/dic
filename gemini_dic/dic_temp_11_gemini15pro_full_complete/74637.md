freebases:/ˈfriːbeɪsɪz/| n. |游离碱|A freebase is the pure basic form of an alkaloid, as opposed to its salt form.  It is often associated with drugs like cocaine, where the freebase form is crack cocaine. |Crack cocaine is the freebase form of cocaine.  快克可卡因是可卡因的游离碱形式。| The chemist extracted the freebase form of the alkaloid. 化学家提取了生物碱的游离碱形式。 | synonyms: pure form, base form | antonyms: salt, acid | noun
