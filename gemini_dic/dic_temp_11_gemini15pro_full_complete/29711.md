catholicized:/kəˈθɒlɪsaɪzd/

The curriculum was catholicized to appeal to a broader student body.  课程被普遍化，以吸引更广泛的学生群体。|近义词:universalize, broaden, generalize|反义词:secularize, restrict, specialize|verb
