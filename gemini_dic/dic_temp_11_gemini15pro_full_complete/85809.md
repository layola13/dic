hazardry:/ˈhæzərdri/
n.|冒险，危险的举动，冒险事业|The knight's hazardry was legendary throughout the kingdom.|这位骑士的冒险事迹在整个王国都成了传奇。|近义词: adventure, risk-taking, daring, perilousness|反义词: caution, safety, prudence|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *hazardrie*, from Old French *hasarderie*, from *hasard* ("chance, risk," from Arabic *az-zahr*  "dice").  The English word "hazard" also derives from this source.
