ennobles: /ɪˈnoʊblz/ | v. | 使高贵；使崇高 | To give someone a higher social rank, or to make something seem better or more important than it really is. |  Her selfless act ennobled her in the eyes of the community.  她的无私行为使她在社区民众眼中变得高贵。

The artist attempted to ennoble the ordinary lives of working people through his paintings.  这位艺术家试图通过他的绘画来提升劳动人民平凡生活的意义。| 近义词: elevates, dignifies, exalts | 反义词: degrades, demeans, debases | 词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *ennoblen*, from Old French *ennoblir*, from *en-* + *noble* (“noble”).  *En-* is an intensifying prefix.
