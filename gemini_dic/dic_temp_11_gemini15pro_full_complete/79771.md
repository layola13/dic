glutinate:/ˈɡluːtɪneɪt/ | v.  | 粘合，胶合 | To unite or cause to adhere with or as if with glue; stick together. | The pages of the old book were glutinated together. 这本旧书的书页粘在一起了。| 近义词: adhere, bond, stick, cement, cohere | 反义词: separate, detach, divide | verb

glutinate:/ˈɡluːtɪneɪt/ | adj.  | 粘性的，胶状的 | Resembling glue; sticky or viscous. | The substance had a glutinate texture.  这种物质有胶状质地。 | 近义词: sticky, adhesive, viscous, gummy | 反义词: dry, non-adhesive | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin glūtinātus, past participle of glūtināre (“to glue”), from Latin glūten (“glue”).
