balkiest:/ˈbɔːlkiɪst/| adj.|最不愿合作的；最顽固的；最不情愿的|Most unwilling to cooperate; most stubborn; most reluctant. |例句：He was the balkiest mule I'd ever encountered. 他是我见过的最倔的骡子。

例句：The balkiest member of the committee refused to compromise. 委员会中最顽固的成员拒绝妥协。|近义词：stubborn, obstinate, uncooperative, recalcitrant, resistant, unwilling|反义词：cooperative, compliant, willing, amenable|词性:形容词
词源: From balky + -est (superlative suffix).  Balky itself comes from balk (meaning "to stop short and refuse to proceed"), which likely originates from a Scandinavian source related to Old Norse *balkr ("ridge," "beam").
