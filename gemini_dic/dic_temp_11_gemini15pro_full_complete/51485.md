detract:/dɪˈtrækt/ | v. | 减损，贬低 | to make something seem less valuable or less deserving of admiration |  The scandal detracted from his reputation.  这桩丑闻损害了他的名誉。| synonyms: diminish, reduce, lessen, belittle, disparage | antonyms: enhance, improve, augment, boost, praise | verb

detract:/dɪˈtrækt/ | v. | 转移（注意力）| to draw or divert attention away from something | The noise detracted me from my work. 噪音分散了我的工作注意力。| synonyms: divert, distract, sidetrack | antonyms: focus, concentrate | verb
