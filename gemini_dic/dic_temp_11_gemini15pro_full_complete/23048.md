bounceable:/ˈbaʊnsəbl/| adj.|有弹性的，可弹跳的|Something that is bounceable is able to bounce or spring back after hitting a surface.  It can also describe something lively and energetic.|The children love playing with the bounceable castle. 孩子们喜欢玩充气城堡。|The music has a bounceable rhythm that makes you want to dance.  这音乐有节奏感，让人想跳舞。|近义词：elastic, resilient, springy, bouncy|反义词：rigid, stiff, inflexible|词性:形容词

词源: From bounce + -able.  "Bounce" comes from Middle English *bunsen*, probably of imitative origin.
