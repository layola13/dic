indicator:/ˈɪndɪkeɪtər/| n. |指示器，指标，指示剂|Something that shows or points to the state or level of something. |The economic indicators suggest a recession is coming. 经济指标表明经济衰退即将到来。|synonyms: sign, signal, measure, gauge, index |antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but perhaps "misleading information" or "false signal") |noun
indicator:/ˈɪndɪkeɪtər/| n. |（车辆的）转向灯|A flashing light on a vehicle that signals the driver's intention to turn or change lanes. |He forgot to turn his indicator on before changing lanes. 他在变道前忘记打转向灯了。|synonyms: turn signal, blinker |antonyms:  (No direct antonyms) |noun
