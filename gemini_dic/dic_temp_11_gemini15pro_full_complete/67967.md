facilitatory:/fəˈsɪlɪˌteɪtɔːri/
adjective
促进的，便利的
Making easier; assisting.
The teacher used facilitatory methods to encourage classroom discussion. 老师运用促进性的方法来鼓励课堂讨论。
The government introduced facilitatory measures to boost small businesses. 政府引入了促进性措施以推动小型企业发展。
近义词: helpful, supportive, conducive, enabling
反义词: hindering, obstructive, inhibiting
词性: adjective
