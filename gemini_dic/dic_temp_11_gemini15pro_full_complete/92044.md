hyperphoria:/ˌhaɪpərˈfɔːriə/ | n. | 上隐斜视 |A type of vertical strabismus, where one eye tends to turn upwards when not focusing on an object, often causing double vision or eyestrain.|  She suffered from hyperphoria, which made reading difficult. 她患有上隐斜视，这使得阅读变得困难。| Synonym: vertical strabismus, hypertropia | Antonym: hypotropia | Noun
