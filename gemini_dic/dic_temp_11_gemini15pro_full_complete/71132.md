fishless:/ˈfɪʃləs/| adj.|无鱼的；没有鱼的|Lacking fish; without fish.|The river, once teeming with life, is now fishless due to pollution. 这条曾经充满生机的河流，现在由于污染而没有鱼了。|例句：The fishless pond was eerily silent.  没有鱼的池塘异常安静。

例句：They discovered a fishless lake high in the mountains. 他们在高山上发现了一个没有鱼的湖泊。|近义词：barren, empty, devoid of fish|反义词：fishful, teeming with fish|词性：形容词
