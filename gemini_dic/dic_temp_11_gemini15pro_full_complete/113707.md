macro-economics: /ˌmækroʊˌiːkəˈnɑːmɪks/, /ˌmækroʊˌɛkəˈnɒmɪks/ | n. | 宏观经济学 | The study of the economy as a whole, including topics like inflation, unemployment, and economic growth. |  Macro-economics helps us understand the overall performance of the economy. 宏观经济学帮助我们理解整体经济表现。| synonyms: macroeconomics (often used without the hyphen) | antonyms: microeconomics | noun

macro-economy: /ˌmækroʊɪˈkɒnəmi/ /ˌmækroʊiːˈkɑːnəmi/ | n. | 宏观经济 | The overall economy of a country or region. | The macro-economy is currently experiencing slow growth.  宏观经济目前正经历缓慢增长。 | synonyms: national economy, aggregate economy | antonyms: microeconomy | noun
