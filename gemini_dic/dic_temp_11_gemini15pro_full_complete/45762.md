cushionless:/ˈkʊʃən.lɪs/
adjective
无垫子的，无缓冲的
lacking a cushion; providing no protection from shocks or impacts
The cushionless chairs were uncomfortable to sit on for long periods.  这些没有垫子的椅子坐久了很不舒服。
The hikers endured a cushionless ride in the back of the truck.  徒步旅行者忍受了卡车后部没有缓冲的颠簸。
unpadded, hard, uncushioned
cushioned, padded, soft
adjective
