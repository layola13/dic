codevelops:/ˌkoʊdɪˈvɛləps/ | v. | 共同开发，共同发展 | develops something jointly with another or others. |  Example: The two companies codeveloped the new software.  两家公司共同开发了这款新软件。

Example:  They codeveloped a new approach to teaching math. 他们共同开发了一种新的数学教学方法。 | 近义词: collaborate, co-create, jointly develop | 反义词: develop independently, develop alone | 词性: 动词


词源: co- (prefix meaning "together") + develops (from the verb "develop")
