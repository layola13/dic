associatively:/əˈsoʊʃiətɪvli/ /əˈsəʊʃɪətɪvli/| adv.|结合地，关联地|In mathematics, an operation is associative if the order in which the operations are performed does not change the result.  |例句：The numbers can be added associatively.  这些数字可以结合相加。|近义词：jointly, connectedly|反义词:separately, independently|词性:副词

Etymology: From Late Latin *associativus, from Latin associare (“to join, unite”), from ad- (“to, toward”) + sociare (“to associate”), from socius (“companion, ally”).
