acarus: /ˈækərəs/ | n. |螨虫|A minute arachnid which has four pairs of legs when adult, some kinds of which are parasitic on animals and plants and others live in soil or water. |The house dust mite, Dermatophagoides pteronyssinus, is a common acarus found in homes.  屋尘螨，粉尘螨，是一种常见的在家中发现的螨虫。|Synonyms: mite, tick |Antonyms: N/A | noun
