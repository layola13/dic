autocatalytic:/ˌɔːtəʊˌkætəˈlɪtɪk/ | adj. | 自动催化的 | (of a chemical reaction) catalyzed by one of its products.  A reaction is autocatalytic if the product of the reaction acts as a catalyst for the reaction. | The oxidation of oxalic acid by permanganate is an autocatalytic reaction. 草酸被高锰酸盐氧化是一个自动催化反应。 |  catalytic, self-catalyzing | non-catalytic, inhibiting | adjective
