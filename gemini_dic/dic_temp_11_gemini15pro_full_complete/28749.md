carnegies:/ˈkɑːrnɪɡiz/
n.
卡内基家族；卡内基的财富
plural of Carnegie, referring to the family or their wealth.
Andrew Carnegie's Carnegies built libraries all over the country.  安德鲁·卡内基家族在全国各地建造了图书馆。
Near synonyms: fortunes, assets, riches
Antonyms: debts, liabilities
noun
