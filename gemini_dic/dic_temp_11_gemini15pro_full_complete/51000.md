desist:/dɪˈzɪst/ /dɪˈsɪst/| vi.|停止，终止；放弃|To cease doing something; to abstain; to forbear.|The lawyer asked his client to desist from contacting the witness. 律师要求他的客户停止联系证人。|例句：They were ordered to desist from further attempts to escape. 他们被命令停止进一步的逃跑尝试。

例句：She desisted from making any further comments. 她停止了发表任何进一步的评论。


近义词：cease, stop, discontinue, refrain, abstain|反义词:continue, persist, persevere|词性:动词
