outacting:/ˌaʊtˈæktɪŋ/
|n.|胜过；演技超越|The act of outperforming another actor or actress.|Her outacting of the other performers was evident. 她对其他演员的演技超越是显而易见的。|Synonyms: outperformance, surpassing|Antonyms: underperformance|noun
