folliculous:/fəˈlɪkjʊləs/| adj.|多毛囊的；多滤泡的|Covered with, having, or resembling follicles.  Characterized by having many follicles.|例句：The plant's leaves were folliculous, covered in tiny sacs. 这种植物的叶子多毛囊，上面覆盖着小囊。

例句：The doctor examined the folliculous tissue under a microscope. 医生在显微镜下检查了多滤泡的组织。|近义词：follicular, multifollicular|反义词: glabrous (smooth, hairless)|词性: adjective
