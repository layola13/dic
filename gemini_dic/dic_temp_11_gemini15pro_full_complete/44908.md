cryosat:/ˈkraɪoʊsæt/| n. |冰雪卫星|A satellite designed to monitor Earth's polar ice caps and sea ice.|例句：The CryoSat mission provides crucial data on polar ice thickness. 冰雪卫星任务提供了关于极地冰层厚度的关键数据。

例句：CryoSat-2 is a sophisticated Earth observation satellite.  冰雪卫星-2号是一颗先进的地球观测卫星。|近义词：ice-monitoring satellite, polar satellite|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


Etymology:  "Cryo-" comes from the Greek word "kryos" meaning "frost" or "ice-cold".  "-sat" is a shortened form of "satellite".
