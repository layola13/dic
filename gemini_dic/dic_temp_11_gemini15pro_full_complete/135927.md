nonpuncturable:/ˌnɒnpʌŋktʃərəbl/ | adj. |防刺的，不可刺穿的 |Not capable of being punctured or pierced. |This tire is nonpuncturable, so you don't have to worry about getting a flat.  这个轮胎是防刺的，所以你不必担心会爆胎。| The new material is nonpuncturable and resistant to tearing. 这种新材料防刺并且防撕裂。 |Synonyms: impenetrable, impervious, puncture-proof |Antonyms: puncturable, permeable, penetrable | adjective
