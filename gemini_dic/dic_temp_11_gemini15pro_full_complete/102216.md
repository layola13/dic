itched:/ɪtʃt/
verb
发痒；渴望
experienced an irritating cutaneous sensation; felt a strong desire or urge
My arm itched where the mosquito had bitten me. 我的手臂被蚊子咬的地方很痒。
I'm itching to get started on my new project. 我渴望开始我的新项目。
Synonyms: tickled, yearned, craved
Antonyms: satisfied, content
词性: 动词

itched:/ɪtʃt/
adjective
发痒的
having or causing an itching sensation
The itchy rash on my leg was driving me crazy. 我腿上发痒的皮疹快把我逼疯了。
Synonyms: irritating, scratchy
Antonyms: soothing, calming
词性: 形容词
