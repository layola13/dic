monadically:/məˈnædɪkli/ /moʊˈnædɪkli/
|adv.|一元地；单子地；单向地|
Acting or operating on a single entity or element; independently.  Relating to or characterized by a monad or monads.|
He applied the function monadically to each element of the list. 他将函数一元地应用于列表的每个元素。|
The program processes each input monadically, without considering context. 程序对每个输入进行单子处理，不考虑上下文。|
Near Synonyms: singly, individually, separately, independently, one by one, atomically|
Antonyms:  collectively, jointly, together, dependently, relationally|
词性: 副词
