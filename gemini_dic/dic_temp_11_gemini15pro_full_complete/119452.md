micromanagement:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈmænɪdʒmənt/ | n. | 微观管理；事必躬亲 | Control over the smallest details of an employee's work or a project, often in a way that is considered excessive or counterproductive. |  The company suffered from excessive micromanagement, stifling creativity and innovation.  这家公司受到过度微观管理的困扰，扼杀了创造力和创新。

| 近义词：over-supervision, excessive control | 反义词: macromanagement, delegation, empowerment | 词性: noun

micromanage:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈmænɪdʒ/ | v. | 微观管理；事必躬亲 | To control every detail of an employee's work or a project. | The new manager micromanages everything, making it difficult for the team to function effectively.  新经理对所有事情都进行微观管理，使团队难以有效运作。

| 近义词：over-supervise, over-control | 反义词: delegate, empower | 词性: verb
