beret:/ˈbereɪ/ (US), /ˈbɛreɪ/ (UK)| n.|贝雷帽|A soft, round, flat-crowned hat, typically made of felt or knitted wool.|She wore a stylish beret perched jauntily on her head. 她头上戴着一顶时髦的贝雷帽，显得十分俏皮。|Synonyms: cap, hat|Antonyms: None|noun

Etymology->
From French *béret*, from Béarnese *berret*, from *birèta* (Gascon), diminutive of *birè*, from Vulgar Latin *birrus*, meaning “hooded cloak,” of Gaulish origin.
