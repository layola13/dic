lithodomus:/ˌlɪθəˈdoʊməs/| n. |石蛏|A genus of marine bivalve mollusks, commonly known as date mussels, that bore into rocks.  They secrete an acid that dissolves the rock, creating a burrow. |The lithodomus has made its home in this limestone cliff. 石蛏在这片石灰岩悬崖上安了家。|近义词:date mussel, rock borer|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek λίθος (líthos, “stone”) + δόμος (dómos, “house”).
