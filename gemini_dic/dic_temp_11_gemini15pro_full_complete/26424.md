bylines:/ˈbaɪlaɪnz/
n.
署名；作者署名行
A line at the beginning of a newspaper or magazine article that gives the writer's name.
The byline read "By Jane Doe."  署名写着“作者：简·多伊”。
credits, attribution
anonymity
noun
