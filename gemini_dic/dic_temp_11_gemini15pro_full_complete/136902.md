nonsolicitously:/ˌnɒnsəˈlɪsɪtəsli/
adverb
不殷勤地，不恳切地
Not anxiously or with urgent desire; without solicitude or concern.
He nonsolicitously offered her a drink. 他漫不经心地给她递了一杯饮料。
Synonyms: casually, indifferently, apathetically
Antonyms: eagerly, anxiously, solicitously
词性: 副词
