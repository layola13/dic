deckchair: /ˈdɛkˌtʃɛːr/ (UK), /ˈdɛkˌtʃɛr/ (US)
noun
躺椅；折叠帆布躺椅
A folding chair with a canvas seat and back, for use outdoors.
We sat on deckchairs on the beach. 我们坐在沙滩上的躺椅上。
