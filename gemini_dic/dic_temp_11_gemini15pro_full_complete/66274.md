excogitate: /ɪkˈskɒdʒɪteɪt/ /ɛkˈskɑːdʒɪteɪt/
verb
深思熟虑；设想；策划
To think out; devise; contrive.
She excogitated a plan to escape. 她精心策划了一个逃跑计划。
He excogitated a new theory of the universe. 他构思了一个新的宇宙理论。

近义词: devise, contrive, think up, formulate, conceive, invent, originate, create, design, plan, scheme, plot, hatch, brainstorm, mull over, ponder, ruminate, deliberate, contemplate, meditate, muse, cogitate, cerebrate
反义词: neglect, ignore, disregard

词性: verb
