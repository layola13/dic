accompanied: /əˈkʌmpənid/ | v. | 陪伴，伴随；伴奏 | to go somewhere with someone or to exist or happen at the same time as something else |  She accompanied her grandmother to the doctor. 她陪祖母去看医生。| synonyms: attend, escort, follow, companion | antonyms: abandon, desert, leave | verb

accompanied: /əˈkʌmpənid/ | adj. | 伴随的，附带的 |  The letter was accompanied by a small gift. 这封信附带了一份小礼物。| synonyms: attended, conjoint, concomitant | antonyms: alone, solo, unaccompanied | adjective
