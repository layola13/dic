dramatists:/ˈdræmətɪsts/
n.
剧作家
A person who writes plays.
Shakespeare is considered one of the greatest dramatists of all time. 莎士比亚被认为是有史以来最伟大的剧作家之一。
The conference brought together leading dramatists from around the world. 这次会议汇集了来自世界各地的主要剧作家。
playwrights, scriptwriters, screenwriters
novelists, poets

词性: noun (名词)
