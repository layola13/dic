embrawn:/ɪmˈbrɔːn/ |v.|赋予力量；使强壮；|To furnish with brawn; to make strong.|He was embraced by the crowd, and their enthusiasm embraced him.|近义词:strengthen, fortify, invigorate, empower|反义词:weaken, debilitate, enfeeble|词性:动词

词源: From em- (variant of en-, meaning "to cause to be in" or "to put into") + brawn ("muscle").  The word literally means "to put brawn into."
