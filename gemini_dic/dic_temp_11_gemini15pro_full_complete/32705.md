chloridic:/klɔːˈrɪdɪk/
adjective
氯化的；含氯的
Containing or derived from chlorine, especially in the form of chloride.
Hydrochloric acid is a chloridic acid. 盐酸是一种氯化物酸。
Chloridic compounds are important in many industrial processes. 氯化物在许多工业过程中都很重要。
近义词: chloride-containing
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider non-chloridic)
词性: adjective
