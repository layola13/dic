helminth:/ˈhɛlmɪnθ/| n. |寄生虫，蠕虫|A parasitic worm; a fluke, tapeworm, or nematode.|例句：The veterinarian prescribed medication to eliminate the helminth infecting the dog. 兽医开了药来清除感染狗的寄生虫。

例句：Helminth infections are common in developing countries. 寄生虫感染在发展中国家很常见。|近义词：worm, parasite|反义词: None (There isn't a direct opposite of a helminth)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἕλμινς (hélmins, “worm”).
