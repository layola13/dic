nth-roots:/ˈɛnθ ruːts/ | n. | n次方根 | The nth roots of a number are the numbers that, when raised to the nth power, equal the original number. |  Example 1: The 2nd roots (square roots) of 9 are 3 and -3. 9的平方根是3和-3。

Example 2: The 3rd roots (cube roots) of 8 are 2. 8的立方根是2。 | synonyms: roots, radicals | antonyms: powers, exponents | noun
