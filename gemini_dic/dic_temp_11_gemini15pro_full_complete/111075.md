listerism:/ˈlɪstəˌrɪzəm/| n.|李斯特主义; 防腐法; 消毒法|A system of antiseptic surgery, developed by Joseph Lister.|The introduction of listerism greatly reduced post-operative infections. 李斯特消毒法的引入大大减少了术后感染。|Synonyms: antisepsis, asepsis|Antonyms: sepsis|noun
