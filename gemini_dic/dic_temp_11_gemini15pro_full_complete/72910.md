fomenting:/foʊˈmɛntɪŋ/
verb
煽动，激起
inciting or stirring up (negative feelings or activities)
例句：They were accused of fomenting rebellion. 他们被指控煽动叛乱。

例句：He was accused of fomenting unrest among the workers. 他被指控在工人中煽动骚乱。

近义词：inciting, instigating, provoking, stirring up, agitating
反义词：quelling, suppressing, pacifying, calming, allaying
词性：verb (present participle)


词源:
From Late Middle English *fomenten*, from Old French *fomenter* (modern French *fomenter*), from Latin *fomentāre* (“to cherish, nurse, promote”), frequentative of *fovēre* (“to warm, keep warm”).
