diaphragmatic:/ˌdaɪəfræɡˈmætɪk/
adjective
膈的；横膈膜的；呼吸的
Relating to or produced by the diaphragm.
She took a deep, diaphragmatic breath. 她深深地吸了一口气，运用到了横膈膜。
The singer's diaphragmatic control was impressive.  这位歌手对横膈膜的控制令人印象深刻。
近义词:abdominal, respiratory
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider antonyms for effects like "shallow" for breathing)
词性: adjective

diaphragm:/ˈdaɪəfræm/
noun
横膈膜；隔膜；光圈；振动膜
1. A dome-shaped, muscular partition separating the thorax from the abdomen in mammals. It plays a major role in breathing, as its contraction increases the volume of the thorax and so inflates the lungs.
2. A thin sheet of material forming a partition.
The camera's diaphragm controls the amount of light entering the lens. 相机的 diaphragmatic 光圈控制进入镜头的光线量。
The doctor examined the patient's diaphragm. 医生检查了病人的横膈膜。
近义词:midriff (for the body part), membrane, partition
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *diaphragmaticus, from Greek diáphragma (partition, diaphragm), from diá (across, through) + phrágma (fence, wall).
