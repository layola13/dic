overbulkinesses:/ˌoʊvərˈbʌlkinɪsɪz/
n.
过度庞大，过度笨重
The state of being excessively bulky or large.
The overbulkinesses of the furniture made it difficult to move around the apartment.  家具过于笨重，使得在公寓里移动很困难。
The overbulkinesses of the packages made shipping expensive.  包裹过于庞大，导致运费昂贵。
bulkiness, largeness, massiveness
compactness, smallness
noun
