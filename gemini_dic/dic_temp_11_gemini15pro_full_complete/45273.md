cultivators:/ˈkʌltɪveɪtərz/
n.
耕种者，栽培者，培养者
a person or machine that cultivates land.  A person who promotes or improves the growth of something.
The cultivators prepared the soil for planting. 耕种者们准备好了播种的土壤。
Modern cultivators can cover large areas of land quickly. 现代耕耘机可以快速地耕耘大片的土地。

近义词:farmers, growers, gardeners, nurturers
反义词:destroyers

词性:noun
