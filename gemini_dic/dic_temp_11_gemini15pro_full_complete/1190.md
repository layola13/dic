acclimature: /əˈklaɪmətʃər/ | n. | 适应，驯化；适应环境 | The process of becoming accustomed to a new climate or environment. |  The acclimature of the new recruits to the desert environment took several weeks. 新兵适应沙漠环境花了几个星期。| Synonyms: acclimatization, adaptation, adjustment | Antonyms: maladjustment | noun

acclimature: /əˈklaɪmətʃər/ | v. | 使适应，使驯化 | To accustom or become accustomed to a new climate or environment. | They acclimated the plants to the colder climate by gradually exposing them to lower temperatures. 他们通过逐渐降低温度来使植物适应较冷的气候。| Synonyms: acclimatize, adapt, adjust | Antonyms: – | verb
