half-squadron:/ˈhɑːf ˈskwɒdrən/ (US) /ˈhɑːf ˈskwɒdrən/ (UK)| n. |半中队|A subdivision of a squadron, typically consisting of half the personnel and aircraft.  A military unit comprising half the strength of a full squadron.|The half-squadron was deployed to patrol the border. 半中队被部署到边境巡逻。|The half-squadron was scrambled to intercept the incoming bombers. 半中队紧急起飞拦截来袭的轰炸机。|Synonyms: detachment, unit, flight (in some air forces)|Antonyms: squadron, wing|noun
