leted: /ˈliːtɪd/ | adj. | 允许的；租出的 | permitted; rented out (This word is archaic and rarely used) |  The house was leted to a young couple. 这房子租给了一对年轻夫妇。| Near synonyms: rented, leased | Opposite: unrented, unoccupied | adjective

Etymology: From Middle English *leten, from Old English *lētan (“to let, allow, leave”), from Proto-Germanic *lētaną (“to let, allow”). Cognate with Dutch laten (“to let”), German lassen (“to let, leave”), Gothic 𝑙𝑒́𝑡𝑎𝑛 (“to let”).
