endothermisms:/ˈɛndoʊˌθɜːrmɪzəmz/
|n.|地方性吸热动物；地方性恒温动物|The evolution of endothermisms allowed animals to colonize colder habitats.|地方性吸热动物的进化使得动物能够在更寒冷的栖息地定居。|
|近义词:|homeotherms, warm-blooded animals|
|反义词:|ectotherms, poikilotherms, cold-blooded animals|
|词性:|noun (plural)|
