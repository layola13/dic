genpaku:/ɡɛnˈpɑːkuː/ | n. |  《解体新书》; 日本第一部完整的人体解剖学书籍 |  Genpaku refers to "Kaitai Shinsho" (New Book of Anatomy), the first complete translation of a Western anatomical text into Japanese, published in 1774. It is considered a landmark achievement in Japanese medicine and a symbol of the growing influence of Western science during the Edo period. | Sugita Genpaku, the leading figure in the translation project, meticulously studied the Dutch text "Ontleedkundige Tafelen" to create the Genpaku.  | 近义词: Kaitai Shinsho | 反义词: 无 | 词性: 名词
