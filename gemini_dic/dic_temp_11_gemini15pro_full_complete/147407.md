overnormality:/ˌoʊvərˌnɔːrˈmælɪti/
n.
过度正常；超常态
A state of being excessively or abnormally normal, often to the point of seeming artificial or unsettling.  A situation where things appear superficially normal, but underlying tensions or abnormalities exist.
The overnormality of the suburban lifestyle made her feel suffocated.  郊区生活方式的过度正常让她感到窒息。
The pristine, overnormal environment of the hospital felt sterile and unsettling.  医院里一尘不染、超乎寻常的环境让人感到单调乏味和不安。
synonyms: hypernormality, unnatural normalcy, artificial normalcy
antonyms: abnormality, deviance, eccentricity
noun
