allocators:/ˈæləˌkeɪtərz/| n.|分配器，分配者|An allocator is something or someone that allocates resources.  It's often used in programming to refer to a piece of code that manages the allocation of memory.|The allocator efficiently distributed memory to the different processes.|近义词：distributor, assigner|反义词:collector, reclaimer|noun
