chimes:/tʃaɪmz/ | n. | 钟声；和谐的声音 | a set of bells, especially in a clock or church tower, or the ringing sound they make | The chimes of Big Ben rang out across London. 大本钟的钟声响彻伦敦。 | synonyms: bells, ringing, peals | antonyms: silence, quiet | noun

chimes:/tʃaɪmz/ | v. | (钟)鸣；发出和谐的声音 | (of a bell or a set of bells) to make a clear ringing sound | The clock chimed twelve times. 时钟敲了十二下。 | synonyms: ring, toll, peal | antonyms: be silent | verb
