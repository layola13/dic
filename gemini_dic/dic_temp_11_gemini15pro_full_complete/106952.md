laciniated: /ləˈsɪnieɪtɪd/
adjective
细裂的，撕裂状的
Deeply cut into narrow, irregular lobes or segments.  Having a fringed or jagged edge, as if torn.
The laciniated leaves of the fern swayed gently in the breeze.  蕨类植物细裂的叶子在微风中轻轻摇曳。
The artist's laciniated style was characterized by sharp, angular lines.  这位艺术家的细裂风格以尖锐的棱角线条为特征。
近义词: fringed, jagged, incised, serrated, dissected
反义词: smooth, entire, whole
词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin *laciniatus*, past participle of *laciniare* "to tear to pieces, mangle," from *lacinia* "flap, fold, lappet, slip, shred."
