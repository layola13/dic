luma:/ˈluːmə/| n.|亮度；辉度|The luma of the image was too low, making it appear dark.  图像的亮度太低，显得画面很暗。|The camera automatically adjusts the luma based on the available light. 相机根据可用的光线自动调整亮度。|近义词：brightness, luminance|反义词: darkness, dimness|词性:名词

词源:  Luma 源自拉丁语 *lumen*，意为“光”。
