docudramas:/ˈdɑːkjuːˈdrɑːməz/ /ˈdɒkjuːˈdrɑːməz/ |n. |纪录片|A television program or film that dramatizes real events or historical figures. |Example:  The docudrama about the Titanic was both informative and moving.  泰坦尼克号的纪录片既翔实又感人。

Example: Many docudramas blur the lines between fact and fiction. 许多纪录片模糊了事实和虚构的界限。| 近义词: documentary dramas, historical dramas, factual dramas|反义词: fictional dramas|词性: noun
