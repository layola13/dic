merchant:/ˈmɜːrtʃənt/ (US) /ˈmɜːtʃənt/ (UK)| n. | 商人，店主 | A person or company involved in wholesale trade, especially one dealing with foreign countries or supplying merchandise to a particular trade. | The merchant sold silk and spices from the East.  这个商人出售来自东方的丝绸和香料。| synonyms: trader, dealer, wholesaler, retailer, vendor | antonyms: customer, buyer, consumer | noun

merchant:/ˈmɜːrtʃənt/ (US) /ˈmɜːtʃənt/ (UK)| adj. | 商业的，商人的 | Of or relating to merchants or trade. | The merchant fleet transported goods across the ocean. 商船队将货物运送过海。| synonyms: commercial, trading, mercantile | antonyms: non-commercial | adjective
