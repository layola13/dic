accomplished: /əˈkɑːmplɪʃt/ /əˈkʌmplɪʃt/
adjective
熟练的，有造诣的；完成的
Highly skilled and proficient; successfully completed.
She is an accomplished pianist. 她是一位技艺精湛的钢琴家。
The merger was successfully accomplished. 合并成功完成。
近义词: skilled, proficient, adept, expert, talented, completed, finished, achieved
反义词: unskilled, inept, incompetent, unfinished, incomplete

accomplish: /əˈkɑːmplɪʃ/ /əˈkʌmplɪʃ/
verb
完成，实现；达到
To successfully complete something or achieve a goal.
He accomplished his goal of running a marathon. 他完成了跑马拉松的目标。
We need to accomplish this task by Friday. 我们需要在星期五之前完成这项任务。
近义词: achieve, complete, fulfill, attain, execute
反义词: fail, abandon
