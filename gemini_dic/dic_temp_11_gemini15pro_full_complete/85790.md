haystalk:/ˈheɪstɔːk/ | n. | 干草堆，草垛 | A tightly packed pile of dried grass or hay, typically used as fodder or bedding for livestock. | The children played hide-and-seek in the haystacks. 孩子们在干草堆里玩捉迷藏。 |  The farmer stored the hay in haystacks for the winter. 农民把干草堆成草垛过冬。 | synonyms: hayrick, haystack, haycock | antonyms: N/A | noun
