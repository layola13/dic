cerotype:/ˈsɪərəˌtaɪp/
n.
蜡版
A method of printing from a surface of wax; an image produced by this method.
The museum displayed a cerotype of a famous painting. 博物馆展出了一幅名画的蜡版复制品。
近义词:wax print, engraving
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps original artwork)
词性:noun
