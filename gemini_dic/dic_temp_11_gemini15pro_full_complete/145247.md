overbleach:/ˌoʊvərˈbliːtʃ/
verb
过度漂白
To bleach something excessively, resulting in damage or discoloration.
The hairdresser overbleached my hair, and now it's brittle and dry.  我的头发被理发师过度漂白了，现在变得很脆很干。
I overbleached the stain on the carpet, and now there's a light patch. 我过度漂白了地毯上的污渍，现在那里有一块浅色的斑点。
近义词: bleach excessively
反义词: underbleach
