bludgeon:/ˈblʌdʒən/ | v. n. | 殴打，棍棒，用棍棒打 | To hit someone repeatedly with a heavy object.  Can also refer to the heavy object itself.  Figuratively, it can mean to force someone to do something. | The robber bludgeoned the shopkeeper with a lead pipe.  抢劫犯用铅管殴打店主。 | Synonyms: batter, club, bash, pummel | Antonyms: caress, soothe | verb

bludgeon:/ˈblʌdʒən/ | n. | 棍棒，重击 | A short, heavy club. | The bludgeon was found near the crime scene.  棍棒在犯罪现场附近被发现。| Synonyms: club, cudgel, truncheon | Antonyms: feather, pillow | noun
