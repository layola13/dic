garrulity:/ɡəˈruːləti/
n.|饶舌，多话，喋喋不休|Excessive talkativeness, especially on trivial matters.|Her garrulity made it difficult to have a serious conversation. 她太啰嗦了，很难进行一次严肃的谈话。|loquacity, volubility, talkativeness|reticence, taciturnity|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *garrulitas, from Latin garrulus (“talkative”) + -itas (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality).  The Latin garrulus is related to garrire (“to chatter”).
