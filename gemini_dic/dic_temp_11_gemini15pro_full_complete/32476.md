chinche:/ˈtʃɪntʃeɪ/ (US), /ˈtʃɪntʃi/ (UK)| n. |臭虫，床虱|A small, flat, parasitic insect that feeds on the blood of humans and animals, typically living in cracks and crevices in beds and furniture.|例句：The hotel room was infested with chinches.  酒店房间里到处都是臭虫。

例句：I woke up with itchy bites all over my body, and I suspect they were from chinches. 我醒来时全身都是痒痒的咬痕，我怀疑是臭虫咬的。
