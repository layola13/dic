cakier:/ˈkeɪkiər/ | adj. | 更像蛋糕的；更松软的 | More resembling cake; more crumbly and soft. | This cake is cakier than the last one I baked. 这个蛋糕比我上次烤的更松软。 | The soil is very dry and cakier than usual. 土壤非常干燥，比平时更易碎。 | 近义词：crumbly, friable, soft | 反义词：dense, hard, compact | 词性:形容词

词源:
From cake + -y + -er (comparative suffix).  The word "cake" has a long history, originating from the Old Norse word "kaka." The suffix "-y" turns it into an adjective meaning "like cake," and "-er" makes it a comparative adjective.
