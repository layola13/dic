jerkily: /ˈdʒɜːrkɪli/ | adv. | 颠簸地，抽搐地，不平稳地 |  in a jerky manner; with sudden, sharp movements. | The old car moved jerkily down the road. 老爷车颠簸地行驶在路上。 | The puppet moved jerkily as the strings were pulled. 木偶随着绳子的拉动而抽搐地移动。 | 近义词: abruptly, unevenly, spasmodically | 反义词: smoothly, steadily, evenly | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From jerk + -ily.  "Jerk" originates from the imitative verb "yerk" meaning to pull or twist suddenly.
