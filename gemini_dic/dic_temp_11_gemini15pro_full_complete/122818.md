molecularity:/ˌmɒlɪˈkjʊlærɪti/ /ˌmoʊlɪˈkjʊlærɪti/ | n. |分子数；分子性|The number of molecules involved in an elementary reaction step.  It describes how many reactant molecules are involved in a single reaction step.|The molecularity of this reaction is two because two molecules collide and react.|近义词: reaction order (sometimes, but not always the same) |反义词: N/A | noun
