microfilmer: /ˈmaɪkrəʊfɪlmər/ (UK) /ˈmaɪkroʊfɪlmər/ (US)| n.|缩微胶卷摄影师；缩微胶片拍摄者|A person who photographs documents, books, etc., on microfilm.|The microfilmer carefully preserved the historical documents. 缩微胶卷摄影师仔细地保存了历史文献。|近义词:microphotographer|反义词: None|词性: Noun

Etymology:
micro- + film + -er

micro-: from Greek mikrós "small"
film: from Old English filmen "membrane, thin skin,"
-er: agent suffix, from Old English -ere, denoting an agent or doer of an action.
