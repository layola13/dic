gibibyte:/ˈɡɪbiˌbaɪt/| n. |吉字节|A unit of information equal to 2^30 bytes, or 1,073,741,824 bytes.  It is often mistakenly used interchangeably with gigabyte (10^9 bytes), but technically they are different.|My new hard drive has a capacity of one gibibyte. 我的新硬盘容量为一吉字节。|近义词:gigabyte (in common usage)|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider smaller units like kibibyte, mebibyte)|noun
