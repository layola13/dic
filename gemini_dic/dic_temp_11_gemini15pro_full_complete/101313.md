inwit:/ˈɪnwɪt/| n.|智力，理解力，心智|The ability to understand and reason; intelligence; mind. |He showed great inwit in solving the puzzle. 他在解谜方面展现了非凡的智力。|近义词：intelligence, understanding, mind, intellect, reason, wisdom|反义词：stupidity, ignorance|词性: 名词
词源:  From Old English *inwit, from in + wit (“knowledge, understanding”).  Cognate with Old High German inwiti (“conscience”).
