declivitous: /dɪˈklɪvɪtəs/
adjective
陡峭的；险峻的
sloping steeply downward
The hikers struggled up the declivitous trail.  徒步旅行者在陡峭的小路上艰难跋涉。
The road became increasingly declivitous. 道路变得越来越陡峭。
近义词: steep, precipitous, sheer
反义词: gentle, gradual, level
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *dēclīvis, from dē- ‘down’ + clīvus ‘slope’.
