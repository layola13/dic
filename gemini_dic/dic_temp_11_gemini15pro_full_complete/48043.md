deckhand:/ˈdɛkhænd/| n.|甲板水手，水手|A sailor who performs manual labor on the deck of a ship. |The deckhand secured the mooring lines as the ship entered the harbor.  甲板水手在船进港时系好了系泊绳。|近义词：sailor, crewman, seaman|反义词:captain, officer|noun

deckhand:/ˈdɛkhænd/| n.|（尤指小船上的）普通船员|A member of the crew of a ship, especially a small one. |The deckhand helped to unload the cargo from the fishing boat.  普通船员帮助从渔船上卸下货物。|近义词：crew member, mariner|反义词:captain, officer|noun
