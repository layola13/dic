bandoliered:/ˌbændəˈlɪərd/
adjective
佩子弹带的，斜挎子弹带的
wearing a bandolier or bandoliers; adorned with bandoliers
The bandoliered soldier stood guard.  佩着子弹带的士兵站岗。
The revolutionary leader, bandoliered and defiant, addressed the crowd.  这位佩着子弹带、不屈不挠的革命领袖向人群发表了讲话。
synonyms: armed, equipped
antonyms: unarmed, unequipped
