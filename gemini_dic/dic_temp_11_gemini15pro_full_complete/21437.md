blowhard:/ˈbloʊˌhɑːrd/| n.|吹牛大王，自夸者|A person who boasts loudly and excessively about their own achievements or qualities. |He's a blowhard who's always bragging about his supposed accomplishments. 他是个吹牛大王，总是吹嘘他所谓的成就。|He was known as a blowhard, always exaggerating his accomplishments.  他被称为吹牛大王，总是夸大他的成就。|近义词：braggart, boaster, windbag|反义词：humble person, modest person|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From blow + hard.  Originally (1890s) referred to a whale expelling air through its blowhole. The figurative sense of a person who talks at length but says little of substance developed shortly thereafter.
