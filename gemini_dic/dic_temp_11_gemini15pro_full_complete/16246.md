bantamweight:/ˈbæntəmˌweɪt/| n. |最轻量级选手，矮个子|A boxer in the bantamweight class, typically weighing 115–118 pounds (52.2–53.5 kg). A small but aggressive person. |例句：He's a bantamweight boxer known for his speed. 他是一位以速度著称的最轻量级拳击手。

例句：She may be a bantamweight, but she has the spirit of a heavyweight champion. 她或许个子小，但她拥有重量级冠军的精神。|近义词：lightweight, small fry|反义词：heavyweight, giant|词性:名词

bantamweights:/ˈbæntəmˌweɪts/| n. |最轻量级选手（复数）|Plural form of bantamweight. Refers to multiple boxers in the bantamweight class or multiple small but aggressive people. |例句：The bantamweights put on a thrilling show. 最轻量级选手们进行了一场激动人心的表演。

例句：The bantamweights of the political world often make the most noise. 政界的小人物往往最能咋呼。|近义词：lightweights|反义词：heavyweights|词性:名词
