miraa:/ˈmɪərɑː/
n.
卡塔叶，阿拉伯茶
A shrub (Catha edulis) native to East Africa and Arabia, whose leaves are chewed as a stimulant.
People in some cultures chew miraa for its stimulating effects.  一些文化中的人们咀嚼卡塔叶来提神。
Miraa is an important cash crop in some regions.  卡塔叶在一些地区是重要的经济作物。
近义词：khat, qat
反义词: 无
词性: 名词
