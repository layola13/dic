despot:/ˈdespɒt/ (UK), /ˈdespɑːt/ (US)| n. |暴君，专制君主|A ruler or other person who holds absolute power, typically one who exercises it in a cruel or oppressive way. |例句：The despot ruled his country with an iron fist.  这个暴君以铁腕统治他的国家。

例句：The people lived in fear of the cruel despot. 人民生活在对残暴的暴君的恐惧之中。|近义词：tyrant, dictator, autocrat|反义词：democrat|词性：名词
