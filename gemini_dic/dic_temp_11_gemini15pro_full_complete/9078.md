antimiscegenation:/ˌæntiˌmɪsɪdʒɪˈneɪʃən/
noun
反种族通婚
The laws against interracial marriage are called antimiscegenation laws.
Antimiscegenation laws were deemed unconstitutional in 1967.  反种族通婚法在 1967 年被宣布违宪。
近义词:racial segregation, racial discrimination
反义词:integration, miscegenation
词性:noun


Etymology:
From anti- + miscegenation, from Latin miscēre "to mix" + genus "race, kind."  Coined in 1863 during the American Civil War as a political slogan to oppose Abraham Lincoln.
