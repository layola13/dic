lifelong:/ˈlaɪflɒŋ/ (UK) /ˈlaɪflɔːŋ/ (US)| adj.|终身的，毕生的|Lasting or continuing for a lifetime.|She has a lifelong passion for music. 她对音乐有着毕生的热情。|近义词：lasting, enduring, permanent|反义词：temporary, short-lived, brief|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From "life" + "long".  "Life" comes from Old English "līf", meaning "life, existence". "Long" comes from Old English "lang", meaning "long".
