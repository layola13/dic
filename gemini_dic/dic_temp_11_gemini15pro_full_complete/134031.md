nonhygienic:/ˌnɒnˌhaɪˈdʒiːnɪk/ |adj.|不卫生的，不健康的|Not clean or sanitary; likely to cause disease.|The restaurant's kitchen was found to be nonhygienic. 这家餐馆的厨房被发现不卫生。|The toilets were in a nonhygienic state. 厕所很不卫生。|Synonyms: insanitary, unsanitary, unhealthy, impure, contaminated|Antonyms: hygienic, sanitary, clean, healthy, pure|adjective
