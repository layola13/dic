marketably:/ˈmɑːrkɪtəbli/
adjective
适销地；可销售地；好卖地
in a way that can be sold easily because people want to buy it
例句：The product is not yet marketably viable.  该产品尚不具备市场销售的可行性。

例句：The software is marketably packaged and easy to use. 这款软件包装精美，易于使用，适销对路。

近义词：salable, merchantable, commercially viable
反义词：unmarketable, unsalable
词性：adjective
