dragging:/ˈdræɡɪŋ/ | adj. v. n. | 拖曳的；缓慢的；无聊的；拖曳；拖延 |  The act of pulling something along the ground or other surface with difficulty, or something that is slow and tedious.  |例句：The dragging of the heavy box across the floor scratched the wood.  把沉重的箱子在地板上拖来拖去，把木头刮花了。 | 近义词：hauling, pulling, towing, tedious, slow, boring | 反义词：pushing, carrying, quick, interesting, exciting | 词性：adjective, verb, noun

dragging:/ˈdræɡɪŋ/ | adj. v. n. | 拖曳的；缓慢的；无聊的；拖曳；拖延 | The meeting was dragging on and on. 会议拖了又拖。 | 近义词：hauling, pulling, towing, tedious, slow, boring | 反义词：pushing, carrying, quick, interesting, exciting | 词性：adjective, verb, noun

词源：From Middle English *draggen*, frequentative of *dragen*, from Old Norse *draga* (“to draw, pull, drag”)
