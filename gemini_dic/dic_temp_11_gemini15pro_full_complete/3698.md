aggregating:/ˈæɡrɪɡeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 聚集的，合计的，加重的 | Combining or totaling several things, often making a problem worse. | The aggregating effect of small price increases can lead to significant inflation. 小幅价格上涨的累积效应会导致严重的通货膨胀。|  The noise was already loud, and the children screaming only made it more aggregating. 噪音已经很大了，孩子们尖叫只会让它更烦人。 | synonyms: cumulative, collective, combined, worsening, exacerbating | antonyms:  decreasing, diminishing, lessening, mitigating | adjective


aggregating:/ˈæɡrɪɡeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 聚集，合计 |  present participle of *aggregate* meaning to combine into a single whole or total. | They are aggregating data from various sources to create a comprehensive report.  他们正在汇总来自不同来源的数据，以创建一个全面的报告。 | The company is aggregating its resources to focus on a new product line. 公司正在整合资源，专注于新的产品线。 | synonyms: combining, accumulating, amassing, totaling | antonyms: separating, dividing, dispersing | verb
