bartering:/ˈbɑːrtərɪŋ/
n.|以物易物，物物交换|The act of trading goods or services without the use of money.|Example: Bartering was common in ancient societies before the invention of currency.  古代社会在货币发明之前，以物易物很普遍。|Synonyms: trading, exchanging, swapping|Antonyms: buying, selling|noun
barter:/ˈbɑːrtər/
v.|以物易物，交换|To trade goods or services without the use of money.|Example: They bartered their surplus grain for tools.  他们用剩余的粮食换取工具。|Synonyms: trade, exchange, swap|Antonyms: buy, sell|verb
barter:/ˈbɑːrtər/
n.|物物交换，交换物|Something exchanged in barter.|Example: The farmer offered a pig as barter for the carpenter's services.  农民提出用一头猪来交换木匠的服务。|Synonyms: trade, exchange, swap|Antonyms: purchase, sale|noun
