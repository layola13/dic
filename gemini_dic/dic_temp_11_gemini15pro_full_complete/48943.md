defused:/diːˈfjuːzd/ | v. | 拆除(炸弹等的)引信; 缓和, 平息 | to remove the fuse from (an explosive device) so that it cannot explode; to make a situation less tense or dangerous | The bomb squad successfully defused the explosive device.  拆弹小组成功拆除了爆炸装置。| synonyms: deactivated, neutralized, disarmed | antonyms: armed, activated, ignited | verb

defused:/diːˈfjuːzd/ | adj. |  (炸弹)被拆除引信的; （局势）缓和的 | made less dangerous or tense | The situation was defused by the ambassador's calm words. 大使冷静的话语缓和了局势。 | synonyms: deactivated, neutralized, calmed | antonyms:  tense, volatile, explosive | adjective
