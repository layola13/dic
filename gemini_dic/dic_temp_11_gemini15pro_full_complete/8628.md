anticommercialness:/ˌæntiˌkəˈmɜːrʃəlnəs/
|n.|反商业主义，非商业性|The state of being opposed to or not influenced by commercial interests.  A rejection of the values and practices of commerce, often in favor of artistic, intellectual, or social pursuits.|His anticommercialness led him to reject lucrative advertising deals for his art. 他反商业化的立场导致他拒绝了对其艺术作品有利可图的广告交易。|Synonyms: noncommercialism, uncommercialism|Antonyms: commercialism, mercantilism|noun
