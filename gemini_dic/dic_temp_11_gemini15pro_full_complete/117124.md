meanderers:/miˈændərərz/
n.
漫步者，蜿蜒曲折的人或物
People who meander; things that follow a winding course.
The meanderers strolled along the riverbank, enjoying the scenery. 漫步者沿着河岸漫步，欣赏着风景。
The river's meanderers created a beautiful pattern in the landscape.  这条河的蜿蜒曲折在景观中创造了一个美丽的图案。
wanderers, ramblers, strollers
