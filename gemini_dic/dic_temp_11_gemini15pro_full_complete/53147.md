diplomatist:/ˈdɪpləmætɪst/ /ˈdɪpləˌmætɪst/ | n. |外交官，外交家|An official representing a country abroad.|例句：The diplomat negotiated a peace treaty.  这位外交官协商了一项和平条约。

例句：She is a skilled diplomat, able to navigate complex international relations. 她是一位 skilled 的外交官，能够处理复杂的国际关系。|近义词：envoy, ambassador, consul, negotiator|反义词:warmonger|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *diplomate*, from Late Latin *diplomata* "letters of safe conduct, official documents," plural of *diploma*
