endospermic:/ˌɛndoʊˈspɜːrmɪk/ | adj. | 有胚乳的；胚乳发达的 | Containing endosperm; relating to or characterized by the presence of endosperm. |  The endospermic seed has a large endosperm that nourishes the developing embryo.  这颗有胚乳的种子具有一个滋养发育中胚胎的大胚乳。 |Synonyms: albuminous | Antonyms: exalbuminous | adjective
