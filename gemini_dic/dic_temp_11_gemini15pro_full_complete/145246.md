overblanket: /ˈoʊvərˌblæŋkɪt/
noun
过厚的毯子;  过多的覆盖物
A blanket that is too thick or heavy, or an excessive covering of something.
The gardener worried that the overblanket of mulch would smother the young plants. 园丁担心覆盖过厚的护根物会闷死幼苗。
The heavy overblanket made him sweat profusely.  这床过厚的毯子让他大汗淋漓。

近义词: thick blanket, excessive covering
反义词: thin blanket, inadequate covering
词性: n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From 'over-' (prefix indicating excess) + 'blanket' (n.).  The prefix "over-" implies an excessive or superfluous amount, while "blanket" refers to a covering.
