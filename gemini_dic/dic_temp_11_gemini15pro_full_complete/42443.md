counterdemonstrator:/ˌkaʊntərˈdɛmənˌstreɪtər/ | n. | 反示威者，反对示威者 | A person who participates in a demonstration in opposition to another demonstration. |  例句：Counterdemonstrators clashed with the original protesters. 反示威者与原来的抗议者发生了冲突。

例句：The police tried to keep the counterdemonstrators separate from the main protest group. 警察试图将反示威者与主要的抗议群体隔开。


近义词：protester (generic term), opponent, antagonist | 反义词：demonstrator, supporter, advocate | 词性：noun
