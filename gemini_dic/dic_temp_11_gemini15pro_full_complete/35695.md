cobblestone:/ˈkɒblˌstəʊn/ (UK) /ˈkɑːblˌstoʊn/ (US)| n. |鹅卵石，圆石|A small, round stone used for paving streets.|The streets of the old town were paved with cobblestones. 老城区街道是用鹅卵石铺成的。|Synonyms: paving stone, sett, cob|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "smooth pavement")|noun

cobbled:/ˈkɒbld/ (UK) /ˈkɑːbld/ (US)| adj. |用鹅卵石铺成的|Paved with cobblestones.|We walked along the cobbled streets of the village. 我们沿着村庄的鹅卵石街道走着。|Synonyms: stone-paved, paved|Antonyms: unpaved, dirt|adjective
