nursemaiding:/ˈnɜːrsmeɪdɪŋ/
n.|照看（小孩）；保育|The act of caring for young children, like a nursemaid.  It involves looking after their basic needs, playing with them, and keeping them safe.|She spent her summer nursemaiding for her neighbor's twins.|近义词:babysitting, childcare, minding|反义词:neglecting|词性:noun
