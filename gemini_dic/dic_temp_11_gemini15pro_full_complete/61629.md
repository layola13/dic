emigrations: /ˌemɪˈɡreɪʃənz/ | n. | 移民，移居国外 | The act of leaving one's own country to settle permanently in another; the movement of people away from a place. |  The recent emigrations from the country have caused a significant decrease in the population. 最近从这个国家的移民导致人口显著下降。

The museum has an exhibit on the emigrations of the Irish during the potato famine.  博物馆有一个关于爱尔兰人在马铃薯饥荒期间移民的展览。


近义词: departures, exodus, migrations | 反义词: immigrations, returns | 词性: 名词
