infuriately:/ɪnˈfjʊəriətli/| adv.|愤怒地，狂怒地|In a manner showing intense anger.  Derived from "infuriate" (to make furious), which comes from the Latin "infuriare" (to enrage).|She slammed the door infuriately. 她愤怒地摔门而去。|He glared infuriately at the driver who had cut him off. 他愤怒地瞪着那个超车的司机。|近义词：angrily, furiously, wrathfully|反义词：calmly, peacefully|词性:副词
