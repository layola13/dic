interoperability:/ˌɪntərˌɑːpərəˈbɪləti/| n.|互操作性，互用性|The ability of computer systems or software to exchange and make use of information.|Ensuring interoperability between different software systems is a key challenge. 确保不同软件系统之间的互操作性是一项关键挑战。|synonyms: compatibility, interchangeability|antonyms: incompatibility, isolation|noun

interoperable:/ˌɪntərˈɑːpərəbl/| adj.|可互操作的，可互用的|Able to exchange and make use of information with other systems or software.|The new system is designed to be interoperable with existing platforms. 新系统的设计使其能够与现有平台互操作。|synonyms: compatible, interchangeable|antonyms: incompatible, isolated|adjective
