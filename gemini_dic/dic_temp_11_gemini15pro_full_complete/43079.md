cowrie:/ˈkaʊri/ (US), /ˈkaʊri/ (UK)| n. |贝壳，宝贝，子安贝|A marine gastropod mollusk of the family Cypraeidae, especially of the genus Cypraea, having a brightly colored, glossy shell.  Cowrie shells were once widely used as currency in parts of Africa and Asia. |例句：The necklace was made of cowrie shells. 这条项链是用贝壳制成的。

例句：Cowries were once a common form of currency. 贝壳曾经是一种常见的货币形式。

|近义词：shell, seashell|反义词: None|noun
