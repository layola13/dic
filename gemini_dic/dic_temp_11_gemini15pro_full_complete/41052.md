copings:/ˈkəʊpɪŋz/
n.
应对机制; 应对方式
Mechanisms or strategies used to manage stress, difficult emotions, or challenging situations.
She developed healthy copings to deal with the stress of her job. 她培养了健康的应对机制来应对工作压力。
The therapist helped him explore different copings for his anxiety. 治疗师帮助他探索了应对焦虑的不同方法。
Synonyms: strategies, mechanisms, methods, techniques
Antonyms:  succumbing, yielding, surrendering
