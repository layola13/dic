fibrillated:/ˈfɪbrɪleɪtɪd/| adj.|颤动的，纤维性颤动的，纤颤的|Having or showing fibrillation (a rapid, irregular, and unsynchronized contraction of muscle fibers).  It describes a state where muscles, especially heart muscles, are quivering or contracting abnormally.|The patient's heart was fibrillated, requiring immediate medical attention. 病人的心脏出现纤颤，需要立即就医。

The doctor said the atria were fibrillated. 医生说心房发生了纤颤。|近义词：quivering, twitching, trembling, fluttering|反义词：steady, regular, normal|词性: adjective
