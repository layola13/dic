invertebrates:/ɪnˈvɜːrtɪbrəts/ /ɪnˈvɜːrtɪbreɪts/ | n. |无脊椎动物|Animals without a backbone or spinal column.  These include insects, spiders, worms, mollusks, and crustaceans.|例句：Invertebrates make up the vast majority of animal species on Earth. 无脊椎动物构成了地球上绝大多数的动物物种。

例句：Many invertebrates live in the ocean. 许多无脊椎动物生活在海洋中。|近义词：spineless creatures|反义词:vertebrates|词性:名词
