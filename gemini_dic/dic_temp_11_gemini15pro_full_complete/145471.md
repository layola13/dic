overclassify:/ˌoʊvərˈklæsɪfaɪ/| v. |过度分类，过分分类|To classify something too specifically or into too many categories.|The government tends to overclassify documents, making it difficult for the public to access information. 政府倾向于对文件过度分类，使公众难以获取信息。|例句：Researchers sometimes overclassify data, leading to unnecessarily complex models. 研究人员有时会对数据进行过度分类，导致模型不必要地复杂。

例句：Overclassifying information can hinder effective communication and collaboration. 过度分类信息会阻碍有效的沟通和协作。|近义词：overcategorize|反义词: underclassify|词性:动词
