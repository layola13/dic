enshrinement:/ɪnˈʃraɪnmənt/ | n. | 神圣化；尊崇；把…奉为神圣；载入 | The act of enshrining something, especially a sacred object or a custom.  The enshrinement of human rights in the constitution. |  The enshrinement of the martyrs' relics in the memorial hall was a solemn occasion.  烈士遗物在纪念馆的安奉是一个庄严的时刻。 | consecration, sanctification, deification | desecration, profanation | noun
