outwording:/ˌaʊtˈwɜːrdɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的措辞；在措辞上超过
Expressing something better than someone else did; surpassing in wording or phrasing.
He outworded his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中以言辞胜过了对手。
近义词: outspeak, outargue, surpass
反义词: be outdone, be surpassed
词性: verb (动词)
