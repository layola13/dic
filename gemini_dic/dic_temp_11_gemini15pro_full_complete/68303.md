falconry:/ˈfɔːlkənri/ | n. | 猎鹰术；驯鹰术 | The sport of hunting with trained hawks, falcons, or other birds of prey. | He has a keen interest in falconry and spends much of his free time training his birds. 他对猎鹰术很感兴趣，大部分空闲时间都用来训练他的鸟。 | 近义词:hawking | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider conservation) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *fauconnerie*, from faucon ("falcon") + -erie (suffix denoting an art, craft, or practice).  Ultimately from Late Latin *falco*, from falx ("sickle"), referring to the bird's curved claws.
