isolationism: /ˈaɪsəˈleɪʃənɪzəm/
n.
孤立主义，孤立政策
A policy of remaining apart from the affairs or interests of other groups, especially the political affairs of other countries.
The country's isolationism prevented it from engaging in international trade.  这个国家的孤立主义使其无法参与国际贸易。

The candidate's isolationist stance alienated many voters.  候选人孤立主义的立场疏远了许多选民。

近义词: noninterventionism, seclusion, detachment
反义词: internationalism, interventionism, engagement
词性: noun

Etymology: From isolate + -ion + -ism.  Isolate comes from the Italian word isolato, past participle of isolare “to isolate,” from Latin insula “island.”
