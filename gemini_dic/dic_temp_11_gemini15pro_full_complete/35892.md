coconuts:/ˈkoʊkənʌts/ | n. | 椰子 | The hard-shelled fruit of the coconut palm, containing white flesh and sweet milk. |  We drank coconut milk straight from the coconut. 我们直接从椰子里喝椰奶。

The monkeys were throwing coconuts from the trees. 猴子们正从树上扔椰子。 | 近义词: None (for the fruit itself) | 反义词: None | noun


coconut:/ˈkoʊkənʌt/ | n. | 椰子肉 | The white flesh of the coconut. | We used desiccated coconut in the cake. 我们在蛋糕里用了椰蓉。

She sprinkled coconut over the pudding. 她在布丁上撒了椰子碎。 | 近义词: coconut meat | 反义词: None | noun


coconut:/ˈkoʊkənʌt/ | adj. | 椰子的 | Made from or flavored with coconut. |  She loves coconut ice cream. 她喜欢椰子冰淇淋。
