disglorify:/dɪsˈɡlɔːrɪfaɪ/
verb
使丢脸，使蒙羞，使失去光荣
To deprive of glory or honor; to disgrace.
The scandal disglorified the family name. 这桩丑闻让家族蒙羞。

The defeat disglorified the team in the eyes of their fans.  这场失利让球队在球迷眼中颜面扫地。

近义词:disgrace, shame, dishonor, discredit
反义词:glorify, honor, extol, celebrate
词性:verb
