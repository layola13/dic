aha:/əˈhɑː/ /ɑˈhɑː/| int. |啊哈，表示恍然大悟或惊讶|An exclamation of triumph, surprise, or comprehension. |例句：Aha! I knew you were hiding something! 啊哈！我就知道你藏着什么！

例句：Aha, I've found the solution! 啊哈，我找到解决办法了！

近义词：eureka, oh, I see, gotcha|反义词: (没有明确的反义词) |词性:interjection

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you mean? = 你是什么意思？
What does this word mean? = 这个词是什么意思？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is she talking about? = 她在说什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned? = 你学到了什么？
What has she discovered? = 她发现了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on? = 你一直在做什么？
What has he been thinking about? = 他一直在想什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I called? = 我打电话时她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had they finished before the deadline? = 截止日期前他们完成了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the interruption? = 中断之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been working on for so long? = 他们一直在做什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? = 你下周末要做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time tomorrow? = 明天这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting? = 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by next week? = 到下周你将完成什么？
What will have changed by then? = 到那时会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by graduation? = 到毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？
