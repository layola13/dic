hydrostatics:/ˌhaɪdrəˈstætɪks/ | n. | 流体静力学 | The branch of physics that deals with the characteristics of fluids at rest. | Example 1: The principles of hydrostatics explain why a boat floats. 流体静力学原理解释了为什么船能漂浮。

Example 2:  Hydrostatics is crucial for understanding the behavior of dams and reservoirs. 流体静力学对于理解水坝和水库的行为至关重要。 | Near Synonyms: fluid mechanics, fluid statics | Antonyms: hydrodynamics, fluid dynamics | noun
