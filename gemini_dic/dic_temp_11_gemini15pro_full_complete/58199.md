drubbing:/ˈdrʌbɪŋ/
noun
痛打，惨败
a severe defeat or beating.
The team suffered a drubbing in the final game. 该队在决赛中惨败。
synonyms: thrashing, beating, defeat, rout, walloping
antonyms: victory, triumph
n.

drubbing:/ˈdrʌbɪŋ/
verb (drub)
痛打，彻底击败
to defeat soundly.
They drubbed their opponents 10-0. 他们以10比0击败了对手。
synonyms: thrash, beat, defeat, rout, wallop
antonyms: lose to, be defeated by
v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *drubben*, possibly from Old Norse *drybba* (“to strike, beat”), from Proto-Germanic *drubaną* (“to be turbid, muddy; to disturb, trouble”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)drewbʰ- (“to flow, move in a current; to stir up”).
