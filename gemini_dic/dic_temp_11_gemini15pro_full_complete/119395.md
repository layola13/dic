microelectronics:/ˌmaɪkroʊɪlɛkˈtrɒnɪks/ | n. |微电子学|The branch of electronics that deals with the design, development, and application of very small electronic circuits and components, typically based on integrated circuits. | Microelectronics has revolutionized the computer industry. 微电子学彻底改变了计算机行业。|  Microelectronics is essential for modern computing. 微电子学对现代计算至关重要。 |Synonyms:  microtechnology, semiconductor technology | Antonyms: macroelectronics (less common) | noun
