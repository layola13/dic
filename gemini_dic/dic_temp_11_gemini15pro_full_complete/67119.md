exponential:/ˌɛkspəˈnɛnʃəl/ | adj. n.|指数的，幂的；越来越快的；指数，幂|Characterized by or being an extremely rapid increase (as in size or extent).  Relating to a mathematical expression containing one or more exponents.|例句：The company experienced exponential growth in its first year.  这家公司在成立的第一年经历了指数级增长。|近义词：rapid, accelerating, escalating, skyrocketing|反义词：slow, gradual, linear, diminishing|词性:形容词, 名词

exponential:/ˌɛkspəˈnɛnʃəl/ | n.|指数，幂|A mathematical expression containing one or more exponents.|例句：The exponential in the equation represents the growth rate.  方程式中的指数代表增长率。|近义词：power, exponent|反义词：N/A|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *exponentialis*, from Latin exponens (present participle of exponere “to set forth, explain”), from ex- “out” + ponere “to put, place.”
