cupule:/ˈkjʊpjuːl/| n. |壳斗，杯状结构|A small cup-shaped structure, especially the cup of an acorn or other nut, or a similar structure on a plant.|The acorn fell from its cupule.  橡子从它的壳斗中掉落出来。| The cupule of the beech nut is prickly.  山毛榉坚果的壳斗是多刺的。|Synonym: husk, involucre|Antonym: N/A|Noun
