broadish:/ˈbrɔːdɪʃ/| adj.|稍宽的，有点宽的|Somewhat broad.  It describes something that is wider than average, but not excessively so. |The table was a broadish rectangle, perfect for six people.|近义词：wide, ample, spacious|反义词：narrow, thin, slender|词性:形容词
Etymology: From 'broad' (Old English 'brād') + '-ish' (a suffix indicating a moderate degree of a quality).
