boyism:/ˈbɔɪɪzəm/| n.|男孩气概，男孩行为；男孩主义|Boyism refers to the behaviors, attitudes, and interests typically associated with young boys.  It can encompass a wide range of characteristics, from boisterousness and physical activity to a preference for certain toys and games.|He exhibited classic boyism, always running around and getting into mischief. 他表现出典型的男孩气概，总是跑来跑去，到处惹事生非。|synonyms: boyishness,  masculinity (in young boys)|antonyms: girlishness, femininity|noun
