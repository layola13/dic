overcondensed:/ˌoʊvər.kənˈdenst/| adj.|过度浓缩的，过度压缩的|Formed by adding the prefix "over-" (meaning "excessively") to "condensed" (meaning "reduced to a denser form"). It describes something that has been condensed or compressed beyond what is desirable or normal.|The overcondensed milk was too thick to pour. 过度浓缩的牛奶太稠了，倒不出来。|The report was overcondensed, making it difficult to understand the key findings. 这份报告过于浓缩，难以理解其主要发现。|dense, concentrated, compact, compressed|dilute, expanded, lengthy|adjective
