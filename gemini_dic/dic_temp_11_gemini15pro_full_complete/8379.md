anti-rationally:/ˌæntiˈræʃənəli/
adverb|反理性地，非理性地|Against or contrary to rational principles or logic.  Rejecting or opposing the use of reason.  |He acted anti-rationally, ignoring all the evidence. 他反理性地行事，无视所有证据。|He made an anti-rationally motivated decision. 他做了一个非理性驱动的决定。|近义词: irrationally, unreasonably, illogically|反义词: rationally, reasonably, logically
词性: 副词
