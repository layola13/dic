approving:/əˈpruːvɪŋ/
adjective
赞许的，认可的
expressing or showing approval
She gave me an approving nod. 她赞许地对我点了点头。
近义词:approbative, affirmative, consenting, favorable, positive
反义词:disapproving, critical, negative
词性:adjective

词源: From Middle English *approven*, from Anglo-Norman *aprover*, from Latin *approbāre* (to approve, test, recommend).
