frosty:/ˈfrɒsti/ (UK) /ˈfrɔːsti/ (US)| adj.|寒冷的；结霜的；冷淡的|Covered with frost; very cold; unfriendly and lacking warmth.|例句：The frosty air nipped at our noses. 寒冷的空气刺痛了我们的鼻子。|例句：She gave me a frosty stare. 她冷冷地瞪了我一眼。|近义词：icy, frigid, freezing, cold, chilly, hostile|反义词：warm, friendly, cordial|词性:形容词
词源:From Old English *forstiġ, from frost + -iġ.
