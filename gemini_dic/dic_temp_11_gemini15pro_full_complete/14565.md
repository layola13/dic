aviado:/ˌɑːviˈɑːdoʊ/ | adj. |  finished; done for; ruined; in a hopeless situation |  In a desperate or hopeless situation, especially concerning one's prospects or chances. Derived from Spanish "aviar," meaning "to hasten" or "to dispatch." Implies being "sent on one's way," often in a negative sense, like being dispatched to one's doom. | Example: "After failing the exam, he felt completely aviado." 他考试不及格后，感觉彻底完蛋了。 Example: "With the engine failing, the pilot knew they were aviado." 引擎失灵后，飞行员知道他们完蛋了。| 近义词: doomed, ruined, finished, sunk, hopeless | 反义词: safe, secure, hopeful, promising | 词性: 形容词
