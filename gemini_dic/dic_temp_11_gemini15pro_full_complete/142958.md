outage:/ˈaʊtɪdʒ/| n. |停电；断电；中断；故障|A period when a power supply or other service is not available or when equipment is out of order. |The power outage lasted for several hours.  停电持续了几个小时。

The internet outage disrupted online services.  互联网中断扰乱了在线服务。|近义词：failure, interruption, breakdown, disruption|反义词：service, operation, connection|词性:名词
