noncompliant:/ˌnɒnkəmˈplaɪənt/ | adj. | 不服从的；不顺从的；不合规的 | Not conforming to rules or standards. |  The company was fined for noncompliant waste disposal.  这家公司因不合规的废物处理而被罚款。 | Example: The factory was shut down for noncompliant safety practices.  这家工厂因为安全措施不合规而被关闭。 | Synonyms: disobedient, rebellious, unruly, defiant, recalcitrant, insubordinate, contrary, intractable | Antonyms: compliant, obedient, conforming, submissive, docile | adjective
