hydronephrosis:/ˌhaɪdroʊnɪˈfroʊsɪs/| n. |肾积水|The dilation of the renal pelvis and calyces of one or both kidneys, caused by the accumulation of urine resulting from an obstruction in the urinary tract.| The doctor diagnosed him with hydronephrosis after reviewing the ultrasound. 医生在检查了超声波后诊断他患有肾积水。|近义词:urinary tract obstruction, kidney distension|反义词: None (Hydronephrosis is a pathological condition; it doesn't have a direct opposite.)|词性:noun


Etymology:  From hydro- + nephro- + -sis.  "Hydro-" comes from Greek *hudōr "water".  "Nephro-" comes from Greek *nephros "kidney". "-sis" is a suffix indicating "state or condition".
