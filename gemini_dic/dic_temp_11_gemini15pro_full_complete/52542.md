**2. Defined**

/dɪˈfaɪnd/ | adj. | 明确的，清晰的，界定的 | Clearly outlined or marked; having a definite shape or form. | Example: The photographer captured her sharply defined features. 摄影师捕捉到了她轮廓分明的面部特征。

Example: The project has clearly defined goals and objectives. 该项目有明确的目标和宗旨。

| 近义词: clear, distinct, specific, precise | 反义词: vague, blurry, indistinct | 词性: 形容词


**3. Diffident**
