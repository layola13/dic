abolitionize:/ˌæbəˈlɪʃənaɪz/
|v.| 废除；倡导废除|To advocate or work towards the abolition of something, especially slavery.|The newspaper abolitionized against the death penalty. 这家报纸倡导废除死刑。|近义词: abolish, eradicate, extirpate|反义词: establish, institute, promote|词性:动词

词源:  From abolition + -ize.  Abolition comes from the Latin word "abolitio," meaning "a destroying."  The suffix "-ize" means "to make or cause to be."
