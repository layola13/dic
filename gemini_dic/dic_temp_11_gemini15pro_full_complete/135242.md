nonpalliations: /ˌnɒnpæliˈeɪʃənz/ | n. | 无缓解；不缓和（措施或手段）|  The absence of measures or treatments that provide relief or comfort, especially in medical contexts, referring to the lack of palliative care.  | The patient's nonpalliations led to increased suffering. 病人的无缓解措施导致痛苦加剧。 | The family opted for nonpalliations, focusing on curative treatments instead. 家人选择了不进行缓和治疗，而是专注于治愈性治疗。 |  Near synonyms: non-relief, absence of mitigation | Antonyms:  palliations, relief, mitigation | noun
