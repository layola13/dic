demonetize: /diːˈmʌnɪtaɪz/ | v. | 剥夺货币资格; 使货币失去价值 | to officially stop using a particular coin or paper money as currency |  The government decided to demonetize the old 500 rupee notes. 政府决定停止使用旧版500卢比纸币。 |  The old coins were demonetized last year. 旧硬币去年被废除了货币资格。 | synonyms: withdraw, invalidate | antonyms: monetize, legalize | verb

demonetization: /diːˌmʌnɪtaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 货币非货币化; 剥夺货币资格 | the act of officially stopping using a particular coin or paper money as currency | The demonetization of the 500 and 1000 rupee notes caused chaos. 500和1000卢比纸币的废除造成了混乱。 | The demonetization policy aimed to curb black money.  这项非货币化政策旨在遏制黑钱。 | synonyms:  withdrawal, invalidation | antonyms: monetization, legalization | noun
