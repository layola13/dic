corybant:/ˈkɔːrɪbænt/ | n. | 科里班特，狂热的追随者 | A frenzied dancer in rites of Cybele, the Phrygian goddess of the earth and fertility, or a participant in orgiastic rites.  A Corybant was a mythical being, a type of daemon, believed to be attendant upon Cybele. |  The corybants clashed their cymbals and danced wildly. 科里班特们敲击着钹，疯狂地跳舞着。| Synonym:  enthusiast, fanatic, zealot | Antonym:  skeptic, doubter | noun

corybant:/ˈkɔːrɪbænt/ | adj. | 狂热的，狂乱的 |  Characterized by or resembling the frenzied dancing of the Corybants; wild; frantic. | His corybantic enthusiasm for the project was contagious. 他对这个项目的狂热热情很有感染力。 | Synonym:  frenzied, ecstatic, delirious | Antonym:  calm, composed, serene | adjective
