augural: /ɔːˈɡjʊrəl/ | adj. |  预示的；开创性的；就职的 | Relating to an augury; relating to an inauguration. |  The augural address of the new president was inspiring. 新总统的就职演说是鼓舞人心的。 | Synonyms: inaugural, auspicious, propitious | Antonyms: inauspicious, ominous | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *auguralis, from augur ("soothsayer, diviner") + -alis (suffix forming adjectives).  The word "augur" itself comes from Proto-Italic *augos, meaning "bird of omen," ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ówg-, meaning "to increase, strengthen."
