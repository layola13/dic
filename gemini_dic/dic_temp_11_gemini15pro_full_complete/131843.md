nonconstat:/nɒnˈkɒnstæt/ /nɑːnˈkɑːnstæt/ | adv. | 不一定如此；未必 | It does not necessarily follow (from the premises); it is not a necessary consequence. | The fact that she's a lawyer doesn't nonconstat that she's smart.  她是一位律师并不一定意味着她很聪明。| 近义词: not necessarily, it doesn't follow | 反义词: necessarily, certainly | 词性: 副词

nonconstat:/nɒnˈkɒnstæt/ /nɑːnˈkɑːnstæt/ | n. | 不一定如此的结论；未必如此的推论 | A conclusion that does not necessarily follow from the premises. |  His argument presented a nonconstat; the evidence didn't support his claim. 他的论证提出了一个未必如此的结论；证据并不支持他的说法。 | 近义词: fallacy, illogical conclusion | 反义词: logical conclusion, sound argument | 词性: 名词
