注意事项:
词源:  Likely from Low German *būger, related to Dutch booger 'goblin, ghost', ultimately from Proto-Germanic *bauk- 'beam, block'.  The semantic shift likely occurred due to the resemblance of dried nasal mucus to a small lump or 'bogie'.
