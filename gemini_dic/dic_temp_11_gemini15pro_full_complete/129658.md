non-decisive:/ˌnɒn dɪˈsaɪsɪv/ (US) /ˌnɑːn dɪˈsaɪsɪv/| adj. |非决定性的，非结论性的|Not having or producing a clear and definite result or outcome; inconclusive.|The battle was non-decisive, with neither side gaining a clear advantage.|近义词：inconclusive, indecisive, unresolved, ambiguous|反义词: decisive, conclusive, definitive|词性:形容词

Etymology:
non-: Prefix meaning "not," from Latin *non*.
decisive: From Late Latin *decisivus*, from Latin *decidere* "to decide," from *de-* "down" + *caedere* "to cut."
