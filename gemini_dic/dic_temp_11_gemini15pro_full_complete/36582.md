collateral:/kəˈlætərəl/| adj. n.| 旁系的；附属的；附带的；担保物，抵押品 |Something pledged as security for repayment of a loan, to be forfeited in the event of a default. |  I used my car as collateral for the loan. 我用我的汽车作为贷款的抵押品。| synonyms: security, guarantee, pledge, deposit | antonyms: debt, liability | adjective, noun

collateral:/kəˈlætərəl/| adj. n.| 附带的，次要的；间接的 |Additional but subordinate; secondary.  | The collateral effects of the war were devastating. 战争的附带影响是毁灭性的。| synonyms: secondary, subordinate, ancillary, incidental | antonyms: primary, main, principal | adjective, noun
