inseminate:/ɪnˈsɛmɪneɪt/
|vt.|授精，使受精|To introduce semen into the vagina or uterus to achieve fertilization.|The vet inseminated the mare. 兽医给母马授精了。|
|近义词：impregnate, fertilize|反义词：sterilize|词性：动词


词源: From Latin inseminatus, past participle of inseminare (“to sow, implant”), from in- (“in, into, on, upon, against”) + seminare (“to sow”), from semen (“seed”).
