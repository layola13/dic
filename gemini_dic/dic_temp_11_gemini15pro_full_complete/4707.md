aleurometer:/ˌæljʊˈrɒmɪtər/ | n. | 面粉强度测定仪 | An instrument for measuring the expansibility of flour, and hence its baking qualities.  It determines the elasticity and extensibility of gluten in flour by measuring the height to which a sample of dough rises when heated under standardized conditions. | The baker used an aleurometer to assess the quality of the flour before making bread. 面包师在制作面包前用面粉强度测定仪评估面粉的质量。| The aleurometer indicated that the flour was strong and ideal for bread making. 面粉强度测定仪显示，这种面粉筋力强，非常适合做面包。 | 近义词: farinograph, extensograph | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From French *aléuromètre*, from Ancient Greek *ἄλευρον* (áleuron, “flour”) + *-meter*.
