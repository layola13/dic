ascendance:/əˈsɛndəns/ | n. |优势，支配地位，ascendancy (US)|A state of being in a position of power, influence, or control.  The rising of something to a position of dominance.|The family's ascendance to power began with his grandfather.  这个家族的掌权始于他的祖父。|Synonyms: dominance, supremacy, superiority, predominance, sway, control, power, authority, mastery, rule, reign, command | Antonyms: decline, descent, fall, submission, subordination | noun
