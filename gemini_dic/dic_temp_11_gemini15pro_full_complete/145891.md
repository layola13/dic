overdevotedly:/ˌoʊvərˌdɪˈvoʊtɪdli/
adverb
过度虔诚地；过度投入地
Excessively devoted; with extreme dedication and loyalty.
She cared for her sick mother overdevotedly, neglecting her own health. 她过度投入地照顾生病的母亲，忽略了自己的健康。
He worked overdevotedly on the project, sacrificing his personal life. 他过度投入地工作在这个项目上，牺牲了自己的个人生活。
近义词: excessively, fanatically, religiously
反义词: halfheartedly, apathetically
词性: adverb

词源:
From "over-" (excessively) + "devoted" (dedicated, loyal) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Devoted" comes from the Latin "devotus" (dedicated by vow).
