antiacademics:/ˌæntiˌækəˈdɛmɪks/
| n. |反学术主义；反学风|A sentiment or movement opposed to traditional academic values, methods, or institutions. It often involves a critique of intellectual elitism, excessive specialization, or the perceived irrelevance of academic pursuits to real-world problems.|His antiacademics stance led him to drop out of college and pursue a career in the trades. 他的反学术立场导致他辍学并从事贸易行业。| The rise of antiacademics sentiments is a concerning trend in higher education. 反学术情绪的兴起是高等教育中一个令人担忧的趋势。|近义词: anti-intellectualism, philistinism |反义词: scholasticism, intellectualism |词性: Noun
