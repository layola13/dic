megaton:/ˈmɛɡətʌn/ | adj. |百万吨级的|Having an explosive power equivalent to one million tons of TNT. |It was a megaton bomb that destroyed the city. 那是一颗摧毁了这座城市的百万吨级炸弹。| synonyms:  high-yield | antonyms: low-yield | adjective
