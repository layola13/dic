excommunicate: /ˌɛks.kəˈmjuː.nɪ.keɪt/ | vt. |把…逐出教会；开除…的教籍|To officially exclude someone from participation in the sacraments and services of the Christian Church.|The church excommunicated him for his beliefs. 教会因为他所持的信仰而将他逐出教会。|Synonyms: expel, exclude, banish, ostracize |Antonyms: include, accept, admit| verb
