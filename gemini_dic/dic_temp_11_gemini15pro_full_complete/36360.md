coincidences: /kəʊˈɪnsɪdənsɪz/ (UK), /koʊˈɪnsɪdənsɪz/ (US)| n. |巧合，巧合的事| a situation in which two or more things happen at the same time or in the same way by chance, or in which two or more things are similar, especially in a surprising or unusual way |It was pure coincidence that we met again after all these years.  多年以后我们再次相遇纯属巧合。

|Synonym:  chance, accident, fluke, serendipity|Antonym: intention, plan, design|Noun
