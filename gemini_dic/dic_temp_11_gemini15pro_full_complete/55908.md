divulge:/daɪˈvʌldʒ/ /dɪˈvʌldʒ/| vt.|泄露，透露|to make known (private or sensitive information)|The company refused to divulge its sales figures.  这家公司拒绝透露其销售数据。

The celebrity divulged personal details about her life in her autobiography.  这位名人t在她的自传中透露了她生活的个人细节。|近义词：reveal, disclose, unveil, leak|反义词:conceal, hide, suppress|词性:verb
Etymology: From Latin *divulgare, from dis- "widely" + vulgare "to make common," from vulgus "the common people."
