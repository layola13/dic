overinstructively: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈstrʌktɪvli/
adverb | 过分教导地；过于指导性地 |  in a way that gives too much instruction or guidance, often to the point of being annoying or unhelpful. | The teacher overinstructively explained the simple concept, confusing the students. 老师过分教导地解释了这个简单的概念，反而把学生们弄糊涂了。 | overdidactically, excessively instructively | sparsely instructively, insufficiently instructively | adverb
