laticostate: /ˌlætɪˈkɒsteɪt/ | adj. | 宽肋的；具有宽肋的 | Having broad ribs or costae.  | The laticostate leaves of the plant are its most distinctive feature.  这种植物宽肋的叶子是它最显著的特征。 | 近义词: broad-ribbed | 反义词: narrow-ribbed | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *laticostatus, from latus (“broad”) + costa (“rib”).
