incongruent:/ˌɪnkənˈɡruːənt/ | adj. |不一致的，不协调的，不相称的 | Not in harmony or keeping with the surroundings or other aspects of something; not matching or fitting well. | Her modern art style was incongruent with the traditional decor of the house. 她的现代艺术风格与房屋的传统装饰不协调。| The politician's casual attire was incongruent with the formality of the occasion.  这位政客的便服与场合的正式性不符。 | synonyms: incompatible, inconsistent, unsuitable, inappropriate, discordant, clashing, conflicting | antonyms: congruent, consistent, harmonious, compatible, fitting, appropriate, suitable | adjective
