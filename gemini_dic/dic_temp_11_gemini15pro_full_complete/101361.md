ionising:/ˈaɪənaɪzɪŋ/ (UK), /ˈaɪəˌnaɪzɪŋ/ (US)
adjective
电离的; 离子化的
Causing an atom or molecule to become an ion, typically by removing an electron.
例句：Ionising radiation can damage DNA. 电离辐射会损害DNA。

例句：The ionising effect of radiation can be harmful. 辐射的电离作用可能是有害的。

近义词：ionizing (US spelling)
反义词：non-ionising/non-ionizing
