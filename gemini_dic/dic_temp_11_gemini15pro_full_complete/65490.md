europeanized:/jʊərəˈpiːənaɪzd/
|adj.|欧洲化的|Having adopted or been influenced by the customs, styles, or ideas of Europe.|例句：The city became increasingly europeanized after the war. 战后，这座城市越来越欧洲化了。|例句：Their cuisine has become europeanized over the years. 多年来，他们的烹饪已经欧洲化了。|近义词：westernized, modernized|反义词：traditional, indigenous|词性:形容词
europeanize:/jʊərəˈpiːənaɪz/
|vt.|使欧洲化|To cause to adopt or be influenced by the customs, styles, or ideas of Europe.|例句：The influx of tourists helped to europeanize the local culture.  游客的涌入促进了当地文化的欧洲化。|例句：Many countries in Asia were europeanized during the colonial era. 在殖民时代，亚洲许多国家都经历了欧洲化。|近义词：westernize, modernize|反义词：traditionalize|词性:动词
