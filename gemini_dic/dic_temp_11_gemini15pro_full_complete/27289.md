ETYMOLOGY->

calvaria: From Latin *calvāria*, "skull," from *calvus*, "bald."  The skull is so named because it resembles a bald head.

calvary: From Latin *Calvāria*, from Greek *Κρανίου Τόπος (Kraniou Topos)*, meaning "Place of the Skull."
