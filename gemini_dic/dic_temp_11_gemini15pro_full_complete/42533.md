countermanifesto:/ˈkaʊntərˈmænɪˈfɛstoʊ/ | n. | 反宣言，反对声明 | A declaration in opposition to a previous statement or manifesto.  It directly challenges the principles or ideas presented in the original manifesto. |  The artist issued a countermanifesto, rejecting the commercialization of art.  艺术家发表了一份反宣言，反对艺术的商业化。


|  The group's countermanifesto called for a return to traditional values.  该组织的反宣言呼吁回归传统价值观。 | synonym: rebuttal, denunciation, riposte | antonym: manifesto, proclamation, declaration | noun
