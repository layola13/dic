overopinionatedly:/ˌoʊvərəˈpɪnjəneɪtɪdli/ | adv. | 过于武断地，过于固执己见地 |  In a manner characterized by expressing opinions too strongly or frequently, often without sufficient knowledge or consideration for others' views.  | He overopinionatedly dismissed her suggestions without even considering them. 他武断地驳回了她的建议，甚至没有考虑一下。|  dogmatically, assertively, opinionatedly | open-mindedly, receptively | adverb
