inkier:/ˈɪŋkiər/ | adj. | 更墨黑的，墨迹更多的 | Covered in more ink; darker or more saturated with ink. | The second draft was inkier than the first. 第二稿比第一稿墨迹更多。|  The printer seems to be running inkier lately. 打印机最近好像打印得更黑了。 | 近义词: darker, blacker | 反义词: lighter, paler | 词性: 形容词

词源: From "inky" + "-er" (comparative suffix). "Inky" comes from "ink" (n.), which ultimately derives from the Old French "enque," from Late Latin "encaustum," meaning "burnt in," referring to a purple-red ink.
