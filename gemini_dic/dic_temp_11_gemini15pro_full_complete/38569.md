concoct:/kənˈkɒkt/ (UK) /kənˈkɑːkt/ (US)| vt.|调制，配制；编造，捏造|To create or devise something, especially in a skillful or ingenious way, often by combining various ingredients or elements.  It can also mean to invent a story or plan, often deceitfully.|She concocted a delicious soup from leftover vegetables. 她用剩菜做了一锅美味的汤。|近义词：devise, invent, formulate, fabricate, create, prepare, mix|反义词：discover, find, destroy, ruin|词性：动词

concoction:/kənˈkɒkʃn/ (UK) /kənˈkɑːkʃn/ (US)| n.|调制品，混合物；捏造的故事|Something that has been concocted, especially a mixture of ingredients or a story that has been invented.|The witch brewed a strange concoction in her cauldron. 女巫在大锅里煮了一种奇怪的混合物。|近义词：mixture, blend, combination, invention, fabrication|反义词：component, element, ingredient|词性：名词
