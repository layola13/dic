ETYMOLOGY->
From flawed + -ness + -'s (possessive). Flawed comes from the Old Norse *flaga ("slab of stone, flake"). -ness is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, meaning "state or quality of being." -'s is the possessive suffix.
