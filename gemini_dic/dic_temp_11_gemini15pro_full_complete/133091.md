nonerasable:/ˌnɒnɪˈreɪsəbl/
adjective
不可擦除的，无法抹去的
Not able to be erased.
This marker uses nonerasable ink. 这款马克笔使用的是不可擦除的墨水。
The contract is signed in nonerasable ink. 合同是用不可磨灭的墨水签署的。
近义词:indelible, permanent
反义词:erasable, removable
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From non- + erasable.  "Erasable" comes from the verb "erase," which has roots in Old French "esraser" meaning "to scrape out, erase." The prefix "non-" simply negates the meaning, resulting in "nonerasable" meaning "not able to be erased."
