expatiatory:/ɪkˈspeɪəˌtɔːri/| adj.|赎罪的，补偿的，解释的|Serving to expiate or atone for something; making amends or reparation. Also, serving to explain or clarify something in detail.| He offered an expatiatory gesture after accidentally bumping into her. 他在不小心撞到她之后做了一个表示歉意的手势。|近义词：atonement, apologetic, explanatory|反义词：accusatory, incriminating|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *expiātōrius, from expiāre “to atone for”.
