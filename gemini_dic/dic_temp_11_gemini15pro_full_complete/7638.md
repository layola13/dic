annullable:/əˈnʌləbl/
adjective
可撤销的，可废除的
Capable of being annulled or made void.
The marriage was annullable because it was entered into under duress. 这段婚姻是可以撤销的，因为它是在胁迫下缔结的。
The contract is annullable due to a breach of terms.  由于违反条款，该合同可以撤销。

近义词: voidable, revocable, cancellable
反义词: valid, binding, irrevocable
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin annullabilis, from Latin annullare “to annul”  + -bilis “-able”.
