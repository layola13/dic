cautious:/ˈkɔːʃəs/ | adj. | 谨慎的，小心的 | Showing or practicing caution; careful. | He was cautious about investing his money. 他对投资他的钱很谨慎。 | She took a cautious approach to the problem. 她对这个问题采取了谨慎的态度。 | careful, wary, prudent, circumspect | reckless, careless, impulsive, rash | adjective

Etymology->
From Latin *cautiōsus*, from cautiō (“caution”) +‎ -ōsus (equivalent to English -ous).
