geocachers:/ˈdʒiːoʊˌkæʃərz/
n.
寻宝者，地理藏宝游戏玩家
People who participate in the recreational activity of geocaching.
Geocachers from around the world gathered for the annual event.  来自世界各地的寻宝者聚集在一起参加年度活动。
Geocachers use GPS devices to find hidden containers. 寻宝者使用GPS设备寻找隐藏的容器。
synonyms: treasure hunters, cachers
antonyms:  (Geocachers doesn't have a direct antonym.  One could consider someone who actively avoids outdoor activities or technology an indirect opposite.)
