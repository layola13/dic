distrustful:/dɪsˈtrʌstfəl/| adj.|不信任的，怀疑的|Lacking trust; suspicious.|例句：He was distrustful of strangers. 他不信任陌生人。

例句：She gave him a distrustful look. 她给了他一个怀疑的眼神。|近义词：suspicious, wary, skeptical, cynical|反义词：trusting, gullible, naive|词性:形容词

词源: dis- (not) + trustful (full of trust)
