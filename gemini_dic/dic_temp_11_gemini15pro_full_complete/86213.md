heathenness:/ˈhiːðən.nɪs/
n.
异教；不信教；野蛮
The state or quality of being a heathen; paganism; barbarism.
His heathenness was evident in his refusal to attend church. 他的异教信仰体现在他拒绝去教堂。
The missionaries sought to convert the local population from their heathenness. 传教士试图使当地居民摆脱异教信仰。
近义词：paganism, barbarity, savagery, ungodliness
反义词：piety, devoutness, religiousness
词性：名词
