auditions:/ɔːˈdɪʃənz/ | n. |试镜；试音；试演|A short performance given by an actor, musician, dancer, etc., so that a director or other person can decide if they are suitable to act in a play, sing in a concert, etc.|She went to several auditions before getting the part. 她参加了几次试镜才得到这个角色。|近义词: tryouts, screen tests|反义词: None| Noun

audition:/ɔːˈdɪʃən/ | n. |试镜；试音；试演|The process of giving a short performance so that a director or other person can decide if you are suitable to act in a play, sing in a concert, etc.|The director held auditions for the new musical. 导演为新音乐剧举行了试镜。|近义词: tryout, screen test|反义词: None| Noun

audition:/ɔːˈdɪʃən/ | v. |试镜；试音；试演|To give a short performance so that a director or other person can decide if you are suitable to act in a play, sing in a concert, etc.|He's auditioning for the part of Hamlet. 他正在为哈姆雷特的角色试镜。|近义词: try out, screen test|反义词: None| Verb
