isotropic:/ˌaɪsəˈtrɒpɪk/ | adj. | 各向同性的 | Having the same properties in all directions.  | The material is isotropic, meaning its properties are the same regardless of the direction of measurement. 这种材料是各向同性的，这意味着它的特性无论在哪个方向测量都是相同的。

The cosmic microwave background radiation is remarkably isotropic. 宇宙微波背景辐射非常均匀。 | 近义词: uniform, homogeneous | 反义词: anisotropic | 词性: adjective
