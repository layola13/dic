forehearth:/ˈfɔːrˌhɑːrθ/ | n. | 前炉，熔炉的前部 | A small furnace or a section of a larger furnace where molten glass is refined or conditioned before being formed into products. | The glass is held in the forehearth at a specific temperature before being delivered to the forming machine. 玻璃在送到成型机之前，被保持在前炉的特定温度下。 |  The molten metal flowed from the main furnace into the forehearth. 熔化的金属从主炉流入前炉。 | Synonyms: working end, front hearth | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
