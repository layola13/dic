overoffensive:/ˌoʊvərəˈfɛnsɪv/| adj.|过分冒犯的，过分攻击性的|Causing excessive offense; too aggressive or insulting. |His overoffensive jokes made everyone uncomfortable. 他的过分冒犯的笑话让每个人都不舒服。|The campaign employed overoffensive tactics that alienated many voters. 这场竞选活动采用了过分攻击性的策略，疏远了许多选民。|近义词：insulting, abusive, obnoxious, egregious|反义词：respectful, polite, courteous|词性:形容词

词源:
"over-" (prefix) + "offensive"
"over-" 表示“过度”，“过分”
"offensive" 源自法语 "offensif,"  最终源自拉丁语 "offendere" (to strike against, offend).
