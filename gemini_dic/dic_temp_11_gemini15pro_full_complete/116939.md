mazdoor: /məzˈdʊr/ | n. |  劳动者，工人 | A laborer or worker, especially one who performs manual labor.  From Urdu مزدور (mazdūr), from Persian مزدور (mazdūr), from مزد (muzd, “wage”) + ور (var, “possessor, owner”). |  The mazdors worked tirelessly in the fields.  那些工人在田里不知疲倦地工作。| Synonyms: laborer, worker, workman, hand | Antonyms: employer, boss, manager | noun
