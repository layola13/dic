carps:/kɑːrps/ | n. |鲤鱼（carp的复数形式）|The plural form of "carp," referring to more than one of the species of freshwater fish. |例句：The carps in the pond were large and colorful.  池塘里的鲤鱼又大又鲜艳。

例句：He caught two carps while fishing in the lake. 他在湖里钓鱼时钓到了两条鲤鱼。|近义词：fish |反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑使用predator 捕食者) |词性:名词

carp:/kɑːrp/ | v. |吹毛求疵，挑剔；抱怨|To complain or find fault, especially about petty or insignificant matters. |例句：He's always carping about the food. 他总是抱怨食物不好吃。

例句：She carped at him for being late. 她因为他迟到而责备他。|近义词：complain, criticize, nitpick, grumble |反义词:praise, compliment |词性:动词
