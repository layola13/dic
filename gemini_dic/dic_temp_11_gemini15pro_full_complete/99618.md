interjoist:/ˌɪntərˈdʒɔɪst/ | adj. n. | 梁间的；小梁；| Situated or occurring between joists.  Also, a small beam between main joists. |  Insulation was placed in the interjoist spaces.  隔热材料被放置在梁间的空间里。 The contractor used interjoists to support the subfloor.  承包商使用小梁来支撑底层地板。 | 近义词: between joists | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology:
From inter- + joist.  "Inter-" is a Latin prefix meaning "between," and "joist" refers to a horizontal supporting beam in a floor or ceiling.
