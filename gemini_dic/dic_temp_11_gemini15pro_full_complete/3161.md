aetites:/iːˈtaɪtiːz/
n.
eagle-stone, Aetites
A hollow nodule of clay ironstone, formerly believed to be found in the eagle's nest and to facilitate childbirth.  A variety of hollow geode.
The museum's collection includes a fascinating aetites, once believed to possess magical properties.  博物馆的藏品中包括一个迷人的鹰石，曾经被认为具有神奇的特性。
