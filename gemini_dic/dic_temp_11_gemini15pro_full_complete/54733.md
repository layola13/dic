disparagers:/dɪˈspærɪdʒərz/ | n. | 贬低者，诋毁者 | People who belittle or speak slightingly of something or someone. |  The disparagers of modern art often fail to understand the artist's intent. 现代艺术的诋毁者往往无法理解艺术家的意图。| Synonyms: critics, detractors, denigrators | Antonyms: admirers, praisers, supporters | noun

disparage:/dɪˈspærɪdʒ/ | v. | 贬低，诋毁 | To speak slightingly of; to belittle. |  He disparaged his opponent's record in an attempt to win votes. 他诋毁对手的记录以试图赢得选票。| Synonyms: belittle, denigrate, deprecate | Antonyms: praise, extol, commend | verb
