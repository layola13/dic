gothically:/ˈɡɒθɪkli/ /ˈɡɑːθɪkli/| adv.|哥特式地|In a Gothic manner; resembling or suggestive of Gothic architecture, art, literature, or music.  Characterized by gloom, mystery, or the grotesque.|The mansion was gothically decorated for Halloween.  这座大厦被哥特式地装饰，以迎接万圣节。|近义词：gloomily, darkly, eerily, grotesquely|反义词：brightly, cheerfully, plainly|词性:副词

词源: From Gothic + -ally.  Ultimately from the Goth, an East Germanic people.
