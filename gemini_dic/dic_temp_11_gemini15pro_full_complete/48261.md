decorement: /ˈdɛkɔːrmənt/ | n. |减少，降低; 装饰，装潢(罕见用法) | A decrease or reduction in quantity or value.  Alternatively, but less commonly, it can refer to decoration or ornamentation. | The decorement in sales this quarter is concerning.  这个季度销售额的下降令人担忧。|

近义词: decrease, reduction, decline, diminution, abatement | 反义词: increase, rise, augmentation, growth | 词性: 名词

词源: From French "décorement," from "décorer" (to decorate), derived from Latin "decorare." While the primary French meaning relates to decoration, the English usage has shifted towards a sense of decrease. This likely stems from a misinterpretation or analogy with words like "increment."
