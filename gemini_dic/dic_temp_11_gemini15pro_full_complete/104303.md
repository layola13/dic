justifying:/ˈdʒʌstɪfaɪɪŋ/
Verb
为...辩护；证明...正当
showing or proving that something is right or reasonable
He tried to justify his decision to leave the company. 他试图为离开公司的决定辩护。
justify one's actions 证明自己的行为是正当的
近义词: explaining, defending, rationalizing, excusing, vindicating
反义词: condemning, criticizing, accusing, incriminating
词性: v.
