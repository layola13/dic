mispriced:/ˌmɪsˈpraɪst/
adjective
定价错误的；估价不当的
Incorrectly priced; assigned the wrong price.
The item was clearly mispriced, marked at $5 instead of $50. 这件商品显然定价错误，标价5美元而不是50美元。
Synonyms: overvalued, undervalued, wrongly priced
Antonyms: correctly priced, fairly valued
词性: 形容词
