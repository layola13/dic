asinine:/ˈæsɪnaɪn/| adj.|愚蠢的，驴一样的|Characterized by or resembling the stupidity or stubbornness attributed to donkeys; extremely foolish or silly.  Exhibiting a lack of intelligence or sound judgment.|His asinine remarks offended everyone in the room. 他愚蠢的言论冒犯了房间里的每一个人。|近义词：stupid, foolish, silly, idiotic, inane, absurd, ludicrous|反义词：intelligent, wise, sensible, smart, clever|词性:形容词
