overabuses:/ˌoʊvərəˈbjuːzɪz/| n.|过度滥用；过分虐待|The report detailed the overabuses suffered by prisoners of war.  这份报告详细描述了战俘遭受的过度虐待。|例句：The overabuses of power by the corporation led to public outcry.  这家公司滥用权力导致公众强烈抗议。

|例句：The system of checks and balances is designed to prevent overabuses.  制衡制度旨在防止权力滥用。|近义词：misuse, abuse, exploitation, mistreatment|反义词: proper use, responsible use|词性:名词

词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning excessive) + "abuse" (from Old French *abuser*, from Latin *abuti* "to misuse, abuse").  "Over-" 前缀表示过度，"abuse" 来自古法语 *abuser*，源自拉丁语 *abuti*，意为“误用，滥用”。
