decommercialization:/diːkəˌmɜːrʃələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 去商业化，非商业化 | The process of reducing the emphasis on profit and commercial values in an activity, institution, or area, often with the goal of promoting public service or non-monetary values. |  The decommercialization of education has been a hot topic of debate. 教育的去商业化一直是热门的辩论话题。|  The festival organizers pursued a policy of decommercialization. 节日组织者奉行去商业化的政策。| synonyms: demonetization, deprivatization | antonyms: commercialization, monetization | noun
