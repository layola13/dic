autopsychography:/ˌɔːtoʊsaɪˈkɑːɡrəfi/
noun
自动精神书写，自动写作
A form of writing that purports to be produced by a spirit or other supernatural force acting through a human agent.  It is often associated with Surrealism and spiritualism.
Fernando Pessoa, a Portuguese poet, used autopsychography in his works. 费尔南多·佩索阿，一位葡萄牙诗人，在他的作品中使用了自动精神书写。
Synonyms: automatic writing, psychic writing
Antonyms: conscious writing
