desensitizer:/diːˈsɛnsɪtaɪzər/
noun
脱敏剂；降低敏感度的东西
A substance that reduces sensitivity or responsiveness, especially to an allergen or a drug.
Exposure therapy uses desensitizers like pictures or recordings to help people overcome phobias. 暴露疗法使用脱敏剂，如图片或录音，来帮助人们克服恐惧症。
synonyms: mitigator, moderator, attenuator
antonyms: sensitizer, intensifier
词性: 名词


desensitize:/diːˈsɛnsɪtaɪz/
verb
使脱敏；降低…的敏感度
To make someone less sensitive or responsive to something, especially to an allergen or a drug.
The therapist desensitized the patient to the allergen by gradually increasing exposure. 治疗师通过逐渐增加接触使患者对过敏原脱敏。
synonyms: numb, dull, inure
antonyms: sensitize, sharpen
词性: 动词

desensitized:/diːˈsɛnsɪtaɪzd/
adjective
脱敏的；感觉迟钝的
Having been made less sensitive or responsive to something.
After years of working in emergency services, she became desensitized to violence. 在急诊服务部门工作多年后，她对暴力变得麻木了。
synonyms: numbed, dulled, hardened
antonyms: sensitized, responsive
词性: 形容词


词源:  From the verb "desensitize," which is formed from "de-" (meaning "remove" or "reduce") + "sensitize" (meaning "to make sensitive"). "Sensitize" ultimately comes from the Latin "sensus" meaning "feeling" or "sense."
