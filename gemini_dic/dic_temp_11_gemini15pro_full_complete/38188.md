comptrollerships:/kənˈtroʊlərʃɪps/
n.
主计长职位；主计长任期
The position or office of a comptroller.  The period of time during which someone serves as a comptroller.
The comptrollerships of the two organizations were merged. 这两个机构的审计部门合并了。
Synonyms: controllerships, auditorship
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a position.  One might consider "lack of oversight" or "mismanagement" as antonymic concepts.)
