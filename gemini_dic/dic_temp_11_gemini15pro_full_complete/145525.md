overcolors:/ˈoʊvərˌkʌlərz/| n. |套印颜色，叠加颜色|The artist used overcolors to create a sense of depth in the painting. 艺术家使用套印颜色来在绘画中营造一种深度感。|例句：The overcolors added a richness and complexity to the image.  套印颜色为图像增添了丰富性和复杂性。

例句：She used transparent overcolors to modify the underlying hues. 她使用透明的套印颜色来修改底层的色调。


近义词：glazes, washes|反义词:underpainting|词性:名词

overcolor:/ˈoʊvərˌkʌlər/| v. |套印，叠印，覆盖颜色|He overcolored the initial sketch with vibrant hues. 他用鲜艳的色彩覆盖了最初的草图。|例句：Be careful not to overcolor the image, or it will lose its clarity. 注意不要过度叠色，否则图像会失去清晰度。

例句：The artist overcolored the canvas with a thin layer of blue. 艺术家用一层薄薄的蓝色覆盖了画布。

近义词：overlay, tint, color over|反义词:underpaint|词性:动词
