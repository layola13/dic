bougainvillea: /ˌbuːɡənˈvɪliə/ /ˌboʊɡənˈvɪlyə/ | n. | 九重葛，叶子花 |  A tropical climbing plant with bright colourful flowers, actually bracts surrounding the small true flowers. Named after Louis Antoine de Bougainville, a French admiral. | The bougainvillea climbed all over the wall, creating a riot of colour. 九重葛爬满了整面墙，形成一片绚丽的色彩。| synonyms: paper flower | antonyms: N/A | noun
