nonimputableness:/ˌnɒnɪmˌpjuːtəbəlˈnɪs/ | n. | 不可归咎；无责性 | The state or quality of being not imputable; not liable to be accused or blamed. |  His nonimputableness for the accident was clear. 他的对该事故的无责性是显而易见的。 | Synonyms: blamelessness, innocence, irreproachability | Antonyms: culpability, guilt, responsibility | noun
