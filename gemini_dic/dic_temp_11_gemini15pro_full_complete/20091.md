biosignature:/ˌbaɪoʊˈsɪɡnətʃər/ | n. |生物特征，生物信号|Any substance, isotope, molecule, or phenomenon that provides scientific evidence of past or present life.|Scientists are searching for biosignatures on Mars. 科学家正在火星上寻找生物特征。|Synonyms: biomarker, indicator, trace|Antonyms: None|Noun

biosignature:/ˌbaɪoʊˈsɪɡnətʃər/ | n. |生物签名，生命印记|A measurable indicator of the presence of life, past or present.|The discovery of methane could be a biosignature of microbial life.  发现甲烷可能是微生物生命的生物签名。|Synonyms: life sign, biological indicator|Antonyms: None|Noun
