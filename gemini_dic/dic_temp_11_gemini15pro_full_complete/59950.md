edible:/ˈedəbl/| adj.|可食用的，可吃的|Fit or suitable to be eaten.|例句：These berries are edible, but those are poisonous. 这些浆果可以食用，但那些有毒。

例句：The cake looked delicious, but it wasn't very edible.  这个蛋糕看起来很美味，但并不好吃。|近义词：eatable, comestible|反义词：inedible, poisonous, toxic|词性:形容词
