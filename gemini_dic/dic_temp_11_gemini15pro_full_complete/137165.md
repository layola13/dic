nonsubversivenesses:/ˌnɑːnˌsəbˈvɜːrsɪvnəsɪz/
n.
非颠覆性，非煽动性
The state emphasized the nonsubversivenesses of the organization's activities.  该州强调了该组织活动的非颠覆性。
The report detailed the nonsubversivenesses of the group's publications. 该报告详细说明了该组织出版物的非颠覆性。
Near Synonyms: conformity, obedience, loyalty, compliance
Near Antonyms: subversiveness, sedition, treachery, rebellion
noun


Etymology:
From non- (not) + subversiveness. Subversiveness comes from subversive + -ness. Subversive comes from Late Latin subversivus, from subvertere (to overturn), from sub- (under) + vertere (to turn).
