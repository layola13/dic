nontoxics:/ˌnɑːnˈtɑːksɪks/
|n.|无毒物质|Substances that are not poisonous or harmful.  These are materials that do not pose a health risk through exposure, either by ingestion, inhalation, or skin contact.|Many cleaning products now advertise themselves as containing nontoxics.|近义词:safe materials, innocuous substances|反义词:toxins, poisons|词性:名词

词源: "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "toxics" (plural of "toxic," from Latin "toxicum" meaning "poison")
