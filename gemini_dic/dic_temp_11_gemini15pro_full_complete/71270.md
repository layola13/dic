fixability:/ˌfɪksəˈbɪləti/
n.
可修复性，可固定性
The fixability of the problem depends on the availability of spare parts.  这个问题的可修复性取决于备件的可用性。
The report questioned the fixability of the damage to the bridge. 报告质疑了桥梁损坏的可修复性。
近义词: repairability, mendability
反义词: unfixability, irreparability
词性: noun

fix:/fɪks/
v.
修理，固定
Can you fix this broken chair? 你能修好这把坏椅子吗？
She fixed her hair in a bun. 她把头发挽成了一个发髻。
近义词: repair, mend, fasten
反义词: break, damage
词性: verb

-ability:/əˈbɪləti/
suffix
构成名词，表示“能力”、“可能性”
The suffix "-ability" turns adjectives into nouns, indicating the capacity or possibility of something. 后缀“-ability”将形容词变成名词，表示某事的容量或可能性。
Examples: portability, affordability, reliability 例如：便携性、可负担性、可靠性
近义词: -ableness, -ibility
词性: suffix
