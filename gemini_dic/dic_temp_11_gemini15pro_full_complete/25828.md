burglaries:/ˈbɜːrɡləriz/
noun
入室盗窃(复数)
The act of illegally entering a building and stealing things.
There has been a spate of burglaries in the area recently. 最近该地区发生了一连串入室盗窃案。
robberies, thefts, break-ins
/ (没有明显的反义词，可以考虑用prevention of burglaries)
