estrangement: /ɪˈstreɪndʒmənt/ | n. | 疏远；失和；离间 |  the fact of no longer being on friendly terms or part of a social group |  Years of estrangement have separated the brothers. 多年的疏远使兄弟俩分开了。 | 近义词: alienation, separation, division, disaffection | 反义词: reconciliation, reunion, harmony, rapprochement | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Old French estrangement, from estranger "to alienate," from Latin extraneus "external, foreign" (see extraneous).
