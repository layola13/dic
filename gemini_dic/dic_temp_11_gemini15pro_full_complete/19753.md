billets:/ˈbɪlɪts/ | n. | 军用票; 钞票; 职位; (尤指体育比赛或演出的)门票 | A piece of paper money, a ticket for something or a position or job, especially one that is much sought after. | Example 1: He had a wad of twenty-dollar billets in his pocket.  他口袋里有一叠20美元的钞票。

Example 2:  She managed to get two billets for the World Cup final. 她设法弄到了两张世界杯决赛的门票。 | Synonyms: tickets, notes, positions, appointments | Antonyms:  (For tickets) cancellations, refunds | noun
