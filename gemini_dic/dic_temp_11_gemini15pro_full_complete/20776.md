blanketers: /ˈblæŋkɪtərz/
noun
覆盖者；用毯子覆盖的人
People who cover things with blankets, or blankets themselves (used metaphorically).
The blanketers of the night sky spread their twinkling tapestry. 夜空的覆盖者展开它们闪闪发光的织锦。
coverers, wrappers
discoverers, exposers
n.
