loon:/luːn/
noun
潜鸟；疯子；笨蛋
A large, black-and-white diving bird with a haunting cry.  Also, an informal term for a crazy or foolish person.
The loon's mournful cry echoed across the lake.  潜鸟的哀鸣声在湖面上回荡。
That loon drove his car into a ditch! 那个笨蛋把他的车开进了沟里！
近义词:crazy person, fool, idiot, nutcase (for person); diver (for bird)
反义词:genius, sane person (for person)
词性:n.


loon:/luːn/
adjective (dated, informal)
疯狂的；愚蠢的
Crazy; foolish.
He had a loon idea about climbing the mountain in a blizzard. 他有一个疯狂的想法，要在暴风雪中爬山。
近义词:crazy, foolish, insane, mad
反义词:sane, sensible, rational
词性:adj.


词源:
From Middle English *loun, from Old Norse *lōni.  Related to dialectal Swedish loan (“lame, clumsy”).
