huge:/hjuːdʒ/| adj.|巨大的，庞大的|Extremely large; enormous.|例句：They live in a huge house. 他们住在一栋很大的房子里。|例句：The company made a huge profit this year.  这家公司今年获利巨大。|近义词：enormous, massive, immense, gigantic, vast|反义词：tiny, small, minuscule|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *huge*, *hoge*, from Old French *ahuge*, *ahoge* (Modern French *à l'aise*), from the phrase *à l'aise* "at ease," from Latin *ad* (“to, at”) + *jacēre* (“to lie”). The sense development is from "spacious, roomy" to "large in size."
