mo's:/moʊz/
noun
莫的；属于莫的东西
Possessive form of Mo, short for Morris, Moses, or other names beginning with Mo.  It indicates ownership or belonging.
That's Mo's car parked outside. 那是莫的车停在外面。
近义词:Mo's (depending on the full name)
反义词:N/A
词性:noun (possessive)


mo's:/moʊz/
noun
多个莫的东西
Informal contraction of "Mo's things" or "belonging to multiple people named Mo."
Those are all mo's cars parked outside. 那些都是莫们的车停在外面。
近义词:Mos' (if referring to multiple people named Mo)
反义词:N/A
词性:noun (plural possessive, informal)
