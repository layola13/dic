moody: /ˈmuːdi/ (US) /ˈmuːdi/ (UK)| adj.|喜怒无常的，情绪化的|Subject to unpredictable changes in mood; tending to be gloomy or sullen.|例句：The moody teenager slammed the door.  喜怒无常的少年砰地关上了门。

例句：The weather has been moody lately, shifting between sunshine and rain. 最近天气变化无常，时而晴天时而下雨。

近义词：temperamental, changeable, volatile, sullen, gloomy|反义词：cheerful, stable, even-tempered|词性: adjective
