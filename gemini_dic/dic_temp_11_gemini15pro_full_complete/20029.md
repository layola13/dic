biomechanist: /ˌbaɪoʊˈmɛkənɪst/ | n. | 生物力学家 | A scientist who studies biomechanics, the application of mechanical principles to living organisms. |  A biomechanist might study the forces acting on a runner's joints.  生物力学家可能会研究作用于跑步者关节的力。|Synonyms: biomechanical engineer, human factors engineer | Antonyms: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From bio- + mechanist.  "Bio-" comes from Greek "bios" meaning "life." "Mechanist" comes from Greek "mēkhanē" meaning "machine" and "-ist" denoting a person who practices or is skilled in something.
