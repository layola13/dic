beta-decays:/ˈbeɪtə dɪˈkeɪz/| n.|β衰变|A type of radioactive decay in which a beta particle (an electron or positron) is emitted from an atomic nucleus.|Carbon-14 beta-decays into nitrogen-14. 碳-14 β衰变成氮-14。|Synonyms: beta disintegration, radioactive decay|Antonyms: nuclear fusion, nuclear fission|noun

beta-decay:/ˈbeɪtə dɪˈkeɪ/| v.|进行β衰变|To undergo beta decay, emitting a beta particle.|Carbon-14 beta-decays with a half-life of 5,730 years. 碳-14的半衰期为5730年，会进行β衰变。|Synonyms: decay, disintegrate|Antonyms: synthesize, fuse|verb
