dietetist:/ˌdaɪəˈtɪʃən/ | n. |营养师，膳食学家|A health professional who is trained to give people advice about what to eat in order to be healthy or to treat a medical condition.|例句：The dietitian advised her to eat more fruits and vegetables. 营养师建议她多吃水果和蔬菜。

例句：I consulted a dietitian to help me lose weight. 我咨询了一位营养师来帮助我减肥。


|近义词：nutritionist, dietician|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *diététiste*, from *diète* (“diet”) + *-iste* (“-ist”).  *Diète* comes from Latin *diaeta*, from Ancient Greek *δίαιτα* (*diaita*), meaning "way of life, mode of living, diet".
