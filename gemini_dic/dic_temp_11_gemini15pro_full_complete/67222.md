例句：The politician was exprobrated in the media for his controversial remarks.  这位政客因其有争议的言论而遭到媒体的谴责。|近义词：reproach, rebuke, condemn, censure, denounce, lambaste, castigate|反义词：praise, commend, laud, extol|词性: 动词
