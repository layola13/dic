autocrats:/ˈɔːtəˌkræts/
n.
独裁者，专制君主
a ruler who has absolute power
Many historians classify Ivan the Terrible as one of history's most brutal autocrats. 许多历史学家将伊凡雷帝列为历史上最残暴的独裁者之一。
The autocrat issued a decree banning all public gatherings. 独裁者颁布法令，禁止所有公众集会。

近义词:dictator, tyrant, despot
反义词:democrat
词性:名词(plural)
