inconsistently:/ˌɪnkənˈsɪstəntli/| adv.|不一致地，不协调地，反复无常地|In a manner that lacks consistency; irregularly or erratically.|He performed inconsistently throughout the season, sometimes brilliantly, sometimes poorly. 他整个赛季表现不稳定，有时出色，有时糟糕。|Synonyms: erratically, irregularly, fitfully, capriciously, unevenly|Antonyms: consistently, steadily, regularly, uniformly|adverb
