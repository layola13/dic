infraction:/ɪnˈfrækʃən/| n. |违规，违反；侵害|A violation of a law, rule, agreement, or other code of conduct; a breach or transgression.|例句：His infraction of the rules resulted in a penalty. 他违反规则导致了惩罚。

例句：Parking in a fire zone is a serious infraction. 在消防通道停车是严重的违规行为。|近义词：violation, breach, offense, transgression, infringement|反义词：obedience, compliance, observance|词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *infractio(n-), from Latin infractus, past participle of infringere "to break, infringe," from in- "in, into, upon, against" + frangere "to break."
