fireworker:/ˈfaɪərwɜːkər/
n.
烟火工，焰火制造者
A person who makes or works with fireworks.
The fireworker carefully set up the display for the Fourth of July celebration.  焰火制造者为七月四日的庆祝活动精心布置了烟火表演。
The experienced fireworker created a breathtaking spectacle of light and color. 经验丰富的烟火工创造了一场令人叹为观止的光彩盛宴。
近义词: pyrotechnician, pyrotechnist
反义词: None
