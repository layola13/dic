euphoniad:/juːˈfəʊniæd/ | n. | 优音词；悦耳的词语 | A word that is pleasant to the ear, characterized by its harmonious sound. |  The poet sprinkled euphoniads throughout her verse, making it a joy to read aloud. 诗人在她的诗歌中散布着优美的词语，使之朗读起来令人愉悦。 | 近义词: euphonious word, mellifluous word | 反义词: cacophony, dissonance | 词性: noun

euphonious: /juːˈfəʊniəs/ | adj. | 悦耳的；优美的 | Pleasing to the ear; characterized by a harmonious combination of words. | The singer's voice was euphonious and soothing. 歌手的声音悦耳而舒缓。 | 近义词: melodious, harmonious, sweet-sounding | 反义词: cacophonous, discordant, harsh | 词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *euphōnos* "sounding well," from *eu-* "well" + *phōnē* "sound, voice."
