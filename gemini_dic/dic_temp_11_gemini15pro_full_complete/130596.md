nonadaptations:/ˌnɒnˌædæpˈteɪʃənz/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˌædæpˈteɪʃənz/| n. |非适应性特征，非适应性现象|Traits or characteristics that are not adaptations, meaning they do not contribute to an organism's survival or reproductive success in its current environment.  They may be neutral or even detrimental. |The peacock's elaborate tail feathers, while attractive, might be considered nonadaptations as they hinder flight and increase visibility to predators.|近义词:maladaptations, non-adaptive traits|反义词:adaptations|词性:名词
