laurentia:/lɔːˈrɛnʃɪə/ /lɔːˈrɛnʃə/
n.
劳伦大陆，劳伦古陆
An ancient continent that formed the core of North America.
Laurentia was a large continent that contained the core of what is now North America, Greenland, Scotland, and some parts of northwestern Ireland. 劳伦大陆是一个大洲，包含了现在的北美洲、格陵兰岛、苏格兰和爱尔兰西北部的一些地区的核心部分。
The breakup of Laurentia contributed to the formation of the Atlantic Ocean. 劳伦大陆的分裂促成了大西洋的形成。

ETYMOLOGY->
From Laurentian Mountains, a mountain range in eastern Canada, named after the St. Lawrence River. 源自加拿大东部的劳伦山脉，该山脉以圣劳伦斯河命名。
