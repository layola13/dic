cohosting:/koʊˈhoʊstɪŋ/| n. v.|共同主持，联合举办|The act of hosting something together with another person or entity.  It can refer to events, shows, podcasts, or other forms of media.|例句：They are cohosting a charity gala this weekend.  他们这周末共同主持一场慈善晚会。

例句：She's cohosting a new podcast with a popular comedian. 她和一位著名喜剧演员共同主持一个新的播客节目。|近义词：jointly hosting, sharing hosting duties|反义词: solo hosting|词性: noun, verb (present participle)
