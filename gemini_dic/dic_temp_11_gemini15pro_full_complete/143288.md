outdragging:/ˌaʊtˈdræɡɪŋ/
verb
胜过；拖得更久；超过
surpassing someone in dragging something, either in distance or duration.
He was outdragging his opponent in the tug-of-war. 他在拔河比赛中胜过了他的对手。
近义词: outpulling, outlasting
反义词: losing to, being outdone by
词性: 动词
