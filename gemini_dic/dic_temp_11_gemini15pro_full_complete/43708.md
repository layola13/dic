creditrix: /ˈkrɛdɪtrɪks/
n.
女债权人;女性债主
A female creditor.
例句：The creditrix demanded full payment of the outstanding debt. 女债权人要求全额偿还未偿债务。

例句：The creditrix filed a lawsuit against the debtor. 女债权人对债务人提起了诉讼。

近义词：creditor, lender
反义词：debtor, borrower
词性：名词

词源：From Latin *creditrix*, from credit-, past participle stem of credere "to believe, trust, entrust" + -trix (feminine agent suffix).  The word is now largely archaic and considered sexist.
