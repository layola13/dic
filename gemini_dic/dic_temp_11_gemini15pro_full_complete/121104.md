miscreative:/ˌmɪskrɪˈeɪtɪv/
adjective
缺乏创造力的；误导性的
Characterized by a lack of creativity or by misdirected creative efforts, resulting in something flawed or undesirable.
His miscreative attempts at poetry resulted in a collection of nonsensical rhymes. 他缺乏创造力的诗歌尝试导致了一堆毫无意义的韵文。
近义词:uncreative, unimaginative, unproductive, barren
反义词:creative, imaginative, innovative, productive
词性:adjective
