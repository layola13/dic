unlimited:/ʌnˈlɪmɪtɪd/
adj.
无限的；无限制的
Not limited; unrestricted.
例句：
He has unlimited access to the company's resources. 他可以无限制地使用公司的资源。

例句：
The possibilities are unlimited.  可能性是无限的。

近义词：boundless, infinite, inexhaustible
反义词：limited, restricted, finite
词性：形容词


**词源:**

From un- (prefix meaning "not") + limited (from Old French limite, past participle of limiter "to limit," ultimately from Latin limes "boundary").
