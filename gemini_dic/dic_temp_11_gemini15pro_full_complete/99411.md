interdivision:/ˌɪntər dɪˈvɪʒən/ | adj. | 部门间的，跨部门的 | Existing or occurring between divisions (of a company, organization, etc.). |  The interdivision rivalry was fierce, with each team vying for the top spot. 部门间的竞争非常激烈，每个团队都争夺榜首位置。

The interdivision soccer tournament fostered camaraderie among employees.  跨部门足球锦标赛促进了员工之间的友谊。| 近义词：interdepartmental | 反义词：intradivisional | 词性：形容词

词源:
inter- (prefix): between, among (from Latin)
division (noun): a separate part of something (from Latin dividere, "to divide")
