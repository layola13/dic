hyri:/ˈhaɪraɪ/ | n. |hyriid mussels, freshwater mussels|A type of freshwater mussel found in Australia and New Zealand.  They are bivalve molluscs belonging to the family Hyriidae.|The hyri are an important food source for indigenous peoples.  这些hyri是土著居民的重要食物来源。|近义词：freshwater mussel, bivalve|反义词: None.
词性: noun
