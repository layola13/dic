beerbellied:/ˈbɪrˌbɛlid/| adj.|大腹便便的，啤酒肚的|Having a large belly, typically as a result of drinking a lot of beer.  It describes someone with a protruding stomach, often associated with excessive beer consumption.|He was a short, beerbellied man with a red face. 他是一个矮个子，大腹便便，红脸的男人。|The beerbellied man lumbered across the beach.  这个大腹便便的男人在沙滩上蹒跚而行。|近义词：potbellied, paunchy|反义词:slim, thin|词性:形容词

Etymology:
beer + bellied (from belly)
