dehumanising:/ˌdiːhjuːmənaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
使丧失人性的；非人化的
Depriving of human qualities; making less humane.
The prison system can be dehumanising for inmates. 监狱制度可能会使囚犯丧失人性。
The refugees were subjected to dehumanising conditions.  难民们遭受了非人道的待遇。
近义词: degrading, brutalizing, inhumane
反义词: humanizing, ennobling, uplifting
词性: adjective


dehumanise:/ˌdiːhjuːmənaɪz/
verb
使丧失人性；使失去人格
Deprive of human qualities or attributes.
War can dehumanise people, making them see the enemy as less than human. 战争会使人丧失人性，使他们将敌人视为非人。
The propaganda campaign sought to dehumanise the opposing side.  宣传活动试图使对方失去人格。
近义词: degrade, brutalize
反义词: humanize, ennoble
词性: verb


词源:
From de- + humanize, from human + -ize.  "De-" is a prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse". "Humanize" means "to make human". Thus, "dehumanize" means to remove human qualities.
