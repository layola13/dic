colonizabilities:/ˌkɒlənɪˌzaɪzəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |可殖民性，可被殖民的程度|The colonizabilities of different planets vary greatly depending on their resources and environment. 不同星球的可殖民性因其资源和环境而异。|例句：The study assessed the colonizabilities of Mars and other celestial bodies. 这项研究评估了火星和其他天体的可殖民性。

The colonizabilities of certain regions were hindered by the presence of hostile indigenous populations.  某些地区的可殖民性因当地敌对土著居民的存在而受到阻碍。|近义词：suitability for colonization, potential for settlement|反义词:uninhabitability, inhospitability|词性:noun
