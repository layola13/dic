dalade:/dəˈlɑːdeɪ/ | n. |  慰劳；慰问品；礼物；（尤指）食物 | A gift or offering, especially of food, given as a sign of goodwill or affection. | He brought a dalada of fresh fruit and pastries to welcome them. 他带了新鲜水果和糕点作为慰劳品来迎接他们。 | 近义词: gift, present, offering, tribute, donation | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: 名词

词源: From Sinhala and Pali *dāḷidā*, meaning "relic." Specifically refers to a tooth relic of the Buddha, and by extension, any offering made in veneration.  The word has evolved to encompass gifts and offerings more broadly, particularly in South Asian contexts.
