nonexperiential:/ˌnɒnɪkˌspɪəriˈɛnʃəl/
adjective
非经验的；非体验的
Not based on or derived from experience.
His approach to philosophy was nonexperiential, relying heavily on logic and abstract reasoning. 他研究哲学的方法是非经验性的，非常依赖逻辑和抽象推理。
nonempirical, theoretical, abstract
empirical, experiential, practical
词性: 形容词
