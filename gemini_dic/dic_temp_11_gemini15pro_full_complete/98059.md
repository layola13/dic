inquest:/ˈɪŋkwɛst/| n.|调查; 审讯(尤指死因)|An inquest is held to determine the cause of a sudden, violent, or unexplained death.|例句：The inquest into the death of the young woman returned a verdict of accidental drowning.  年轻女子的死因调查裁定为意外溺水。

例句：A coroner's inquest was ordered.  已下令进行死因调查。|近义词：inquiry, investigation, examination|反义词: None|词性: noun
