overappraise: /ˌoʊvərəˈpreɪz/ | vt. | 过高评价；过高估计 | To estimate the value of something as higher than its true worth. |  The owner overappraised the value of his house when he put it on the market.  房主在把房子挂牌出售时过高估计了它的价值。

The art dealer overappraised the painting, hoping to make a larger profit.  艺术品经销商对这幅画的估价过高，希望能获得更大的利润。 | 近义词: overestimate, overvalue | 反义词: undervalue, underestimate | 词性: 动词 (verb)

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "appraise" (from Old French "apreisier," meaning "to set a price")
