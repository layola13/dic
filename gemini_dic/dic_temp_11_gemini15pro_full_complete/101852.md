isabelline:/ˌɪzəˈbɛlɪn/ | adj. | 灰黄色的，淡黄色的 | A pale greyish-yellow or light brown color, especially of a horse.  Originally, the color of a dirty or faded white garment.  |  例句：The bird had isabelline plumage.  这只鸟有灰黄色的羽毛。

例句：The isabelline horse stood out against the green pasture.  这匹淡黄色的马在绿色的牧场上很显眼。 | 近义词：buff, cream, pale yellow, sandy, fawn | 反义词：dark, bright, vibrant | 词性：形容词

词源: From Isabella of Austria (1566-1633), daughter of Philip II of Spain, who vowed not to change her linen until Ostend had been recaptured (1601-04). The siege lasted three years, giving her linen ample time to fade to a pale yellow.
