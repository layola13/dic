broncho:/ˈbrɒŋkəʊ/ (UK) /ˈbrɑːŋkoʊ/ (US)| n.|支气管；<口>野马|A bronchus (plural bronchi) is one of the two main air passages that branch from the trachea and carry air into the lungs.
