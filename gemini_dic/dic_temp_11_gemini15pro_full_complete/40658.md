converge:/kənˈvɜːrdʒ/ (US) /kənˈvɜːdʒ/ (UK)| v. |汇聚，聚集，集中; (意见或观点)趋于一致|to come together from different directions and meet at the same point; to become more similar|The two roads converge just outside the town. 这两条路正好在城外汇合。|近义词：meet, join, unite, merge, assemble|反义词:diverge, separate|verb

converge:/kənˈvɜːrdʒ/ (US) /kənˈvɜːdʒ/ (UK)| n. |汇聚，会合(罕见)|The act of converging|The converge of the rivers created a fertile delta.  河流的汇聚创造了一个肥沃的三角洲。|近义词：meeting, junction, union, confluence|反义词:divergence, separation|noun
