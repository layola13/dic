nonpreventively:/ˌnɑːnprɪˈvɛntɪvli/
adverb
非预防性地
in a way that does not prevent something from happening
The disease was treated nonpreventively, leading to further complications.  这种疾病没有进行预防性治疗，导致了进一步的并发症。
