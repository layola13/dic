flocculate:/ˈflɒkjʊleɪt/ (UK) /ˈflɑːkjʊleɪt/ (US) |v. |絮凝；结块|To form or cause to form small clumps or clusters, especially of a precipitate suspended in a liquid.|Adding alum to the water will flocculate the impurities.|Synonyms: aggregate, coagulate, clump, clot|Antonyms: disperse, dissolve, separate|verb
