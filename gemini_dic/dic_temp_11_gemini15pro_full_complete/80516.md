good-tempered:/ˌɡʊdˈtempərd/ | adj. | 好脾气的，温和的 | Having or showing a pleasant, calm disposition; not easily angered. | She's a good-tempered child, always smiling. 她是个脾气很好的孩子，总是面带微笑。| He remained good-tempered despite the provocation. 尽管被激怒了，他仍然保持着好脾气。 | 近义词: affable, amiable, easygoing, pleasant | 反义词: bad-tempered, irritable, grumpy, irascible | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "good" + "tempered". "Temper" in this context refers to one's state of mind or disposition. The word ultimately derives from the Latin "temperare," meaning "to mix in due proportion, to moderate."
