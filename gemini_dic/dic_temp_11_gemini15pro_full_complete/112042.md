longanimity:/ˌlɒŋɡəˈnɪmɪti/ | n. | 长期耐心；坚忍；宽宏大量 | The quality of being patient and tolerant, especially in enduring pain, hardship, or provocation. |  He showed great longanimity in dealing with his difficult children. 他在处理和难搞的孩子们的问题上展现出了极大的耐心。|  She endured his constant criticism with remarkable longanimity. 她以非凡的耐心忍受了他不断的批评。| 近义词: patience, forbearance, tolerance, endurance | 反义词: impatience, irritability, intolerance | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *longanimitas*, from Latin longus (“long”) + animus (“soul, mind, spirit”).
