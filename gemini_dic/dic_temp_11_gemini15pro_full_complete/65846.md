evitable: /ɪˈvɪtəbəl/
adjective
不可避免的
able to be avoided or prevented: unavoidable
例句：Their defeat was inevitable. 他们的失败是不可避免的。

例句：It was inevitable that there would be job losses.  裁员是不可避免的。

近义词：unavoidable, inescapable, certain, predictable
反义词：avoidable, preventable, uncertain
