liftable:/ˈlɪftəbl/| adj.|可提升的，可举起的|Something that is liftable can be lifted or raised.  It implies that the object is manageable enough in weight and size to be lifted by a person or machine.|The box was small and liftable.  这个盒子很小，可以搬动。|Synonyms: manageable, portable, hoistable|Antonyms: unliftable, heavy, unwieldy|adjective
