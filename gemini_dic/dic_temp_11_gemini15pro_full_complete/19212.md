beyondness:/biˈjɒndnɪs/
n.
超越性，超越
The state or quality of being beyond something, especially beyond the limits of ordinary experience or understanding.
His paintings evoke a sense of beyondness, a realm beyond our everyday perceptions.  他的画作唤起了一种超越感，一个超越我们日常感知的境界。

The music transported her to a place of pure beyondness.  音乐把她带到一个纯粹超越的地方。

近义词: transcendence, otherworldliness, sublimity
反义词: mundanity, ordinariness
