nebulation:/ˌnɛbjʊˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 喷雾；雾化；星云状| The process of reducing a liquid to a fine spray, or a cloud of fine droplets suspended in the air.  It also refers to the appearance of being cloudy or hazy like a nebula. |The nebulization of the medicine allows for easier inhalation.  药物的雾化使其更容易吸入。|Synonyms: atomization, vaporization, spraying |Antonyms: condensation, solidification |noun


nebulation:/ˌnɛbjʊˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 喷雾；雾化；星云状| The act or process of nebulizing; the state of being nebulized.  It can also refer to the appearance of something being cloudy or hazy, resembling a nebula in space. | The doctor recommended nebulization therapy for his bronchitis. 医生建议用雾化疗法治疗他的支气管炎。|Synonyms:  aerosolization, misting |Antonyms: condensation, solidification |noun
