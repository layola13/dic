co-occur:/ˌkəʊəˈkɜː(r)/ /ˌkoʊəˈkɝː/| v. |共同发生，同时出现|To happen or exist at the same time or in the same place.|The two species of birds co-occur in the same habitat. 这两种鸟类在同一栖息地共同出现。|Synonyms: coexist, coincide, synchronize|Antonyms: diverge, separate|Verb

co-occur:/ˌkəʊəˈkɜːrəns/ /ˌkoʊəˈkɝːəns/| n. |共同发生，同时出现|The fact of happening or existing at the same time or in the same place.|The co-occurrence of these two events is highly suspicious. 这两个事件同时发生非常可疑。|Synonyms: coexistence, coincidence, synchronization|Antonyms: divergence, separation|Noun
