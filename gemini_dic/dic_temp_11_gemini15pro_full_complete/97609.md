inhaler:/ɪnˈheɪlər/ | n. | 吸入器；喷雾器 | A device used to deliver medication in the form of a mist, powder, or vapor, which is inhaled into the lungs. | I use an inhaler to control my asthma. 我用吸入器来控制我的哮喘。|

| synonym:  atomizer, nebulizer | antonym: N/A | noun |
