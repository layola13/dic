multiareolate:/ˌmʌltiˈæriəˌleɪt/
adjective
多孔的; 多区域的
Having multiple areolae (small circular areas, often referring to the surface texture of lichens or fungi).
例句：The lichen had a distinctive multiareolate thallus.  这种地衣有一个独特的多孔菌体。

例句：The multiareolate structure of the fungus allowed for efficient gas exchange.  这种真菌的多孔结构使其能够有效地进行气体交换。
近义词：polyareolate, multi-areolate
反义词: non-areolate (though this implies a complete lack of areolae, not simply a single one)
词性: adjective
词源: "multi-" (Latin, meaning "many") + "areola" (Latin, meaning "small open space") + "-ate" (suffix forming adjectives)
