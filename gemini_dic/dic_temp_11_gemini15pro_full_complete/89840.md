hornless:/ˈhɔːnlɪs/| adj.|无角的，没有角的|Lacking horns; having no horns.|例句：A herd of hornless cattle grazed peacefully in the field. 一群无角牛在田野里平静地吃草。|例句：The hornless rhinoceros is a critically endangered species.  无角犀牛是一种极度濒危的物种。|近义词：polled|反义词：horned|词性：形容词
词源：Old English *hornlēas, from horn "horn" + -lēas "-less."
