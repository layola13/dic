aerocurve: /ˈɛroʊkɜːrv/
noun
空气曲线; 气动曲线; 流线型
A curve designed to minimize air resistance.  A streamlined shape.
The car's aerocurve allows it to reach high speeds efficiently. 这款车的气动曲线使其能够高效地达到高速。
The new airplane wing features an advanced aerocurve for improved fuel efficiency. 新飞机机翼采用先进的流线型设计，以提高燃油效率。
Synonyms: streamline, airfoil, aerodynamic profile
Antonyms: blunt, angular
词性: 名词


Etymology:
aero- (prefix): relating to air or aircraft  来自希腊语 ἀήρ (aēr), 表示"空气".
curve (noun): a line or outline that gradually deviates from being straight for some or all of its length. 来自拉丁语 curvus, 表示"弯曲的".
