coverside: /ˈkʌvərˌsaɪd/
| n. | 封面；书皮；（尤指唱片或杂志的）封套 | The coverside of the album featured a striking photograph.  这张专辑的封面有一张引人注目的照片。| 英文解析：The outer side of a cover, especially of a record album or magazine.  | 例句：The coverside was damaged in transit. 封面在运输过程中损坏了。 | 近义词：cover, front, jacket | 反义词：inside, back | 词性：名词

词源：Compound of *cover* and *side*.  由 *cover*（覆盖物）和 *side*（面）组合而成。
