othernesses:/ˈʌðərnəsɪz/
n.
差异性，不同性，另类
The state or quality of being different or distinct; the characteristics that distinguish things or people from one another.
She embraced the othernesses of different cultures. 她拥抱不同文化的差异性。
The book explores the othernesses of human experience. 这本书探讨了人类经验的多样性。
近义词:differences, distinctions, diversities, variations
反义词:similarities, sameness, uniformity
词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From otherness + -es (plural suffix).  Otherness is derived from other + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality). "Other" ultimately comes from Proto-Germanic *antharaz, meaning "second," "different," "opposite."
