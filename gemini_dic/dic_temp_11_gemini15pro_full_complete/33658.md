cinephiles:/ˈsɪnəˌfaɪlz/ | n. |电影爱好者；影迷 |A person who is fond of or enthusiastic about cinema; a devotee of motion pictures. |例句：The festival was a magnet for cinephiles from around the world.  这个电影节吸引了来自世界各地的影迷。

例句：Cinephiles will appreciate the director's homage to classic film noir.  影迷们会欣赏导演对经典黑色电影的致敬。|近义词：film buffs, movie lovers, film enthusiasts |反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly someone who dislikes or is indifferent to films) |词性: Noun
