lunchless: /ˈlʌntʃləs/
adjective
无午餐的; 未吃午餐的
Without lunch; having no lunch.
He had a lunchless day due to the unexpected meeting.  由于突如其来的会议，他那天没吃午饭。
The busy schedule left her lunchless.  繁忙的日程让她没时间吃午饭。
近义词: without lunch, hungry
反义词:  having lunch, fed

词性: adjective
