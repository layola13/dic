outblustered:/ˌaʊtˈblʌstərd/
verb
战胜，以更大的声势压倒
to defeat or overcome someone by being louder and more forceful
He was outblustered by his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中被对手以更大的声势压倒了。
overwhelm, overpower, subdue
---
cow, intimidate
