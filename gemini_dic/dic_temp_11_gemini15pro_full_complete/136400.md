nonretail:/ˌnɑːnˈriːteɪl/
adjective
非零售的
Not related to or involved in the sale of goods directly to consumers.
The company focuses on nonretail distribution channels.  这家公司专注于非零售分销渠道。
Nonretail sales increased by 10% last quarter.  上季度非零售销售额增长了10%。
近义词:wholesale, commercial
反义词:retail
词性:adjective
