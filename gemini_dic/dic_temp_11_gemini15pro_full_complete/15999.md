bamboozle: /bæmˈbuːzl/ | vt. n. | 欺骗，迷惑，哄骗 | To trick or deceive someone, often in a playful or lighthearted way.  Also, a complicated and confusing situation. | The magician bamboozled the audience with his clever tricks. 魔术师用他巧妙的戏法迷惑了观众。| Synonyms: deceive, trick, hoodwink, dupe, swindle, confuse, bewilder | Antonyms: enlighten, clarify, inform | Verb
