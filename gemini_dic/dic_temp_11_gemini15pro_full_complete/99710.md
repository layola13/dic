interloted:/ˌɪntərˈloʊtɪd/
adjective
插入的；穿插的
inserted between other things; interpolated.
例句：Interloted scenes extended the film's running time.  插入的场景延长了电影的播放时间。

例句：The author interloted several new chapters into the revised edition of his book.  作者在修订版中插入了几个新章节。

近义词：inserted, interspersed, interpolated
反义词：removed, extracted, deleted
词性：adjective
