extrusile:/ɛkˈstruːsaɪl, ekˈstruːsɪl/
adjective
可伸出的，可挤出的
Capable of being extruded; that can be thrust out or pushed forward.
The snail's eye stalks are extrusile.  蜗牛的眼柄是可伸出的。
extrusile:/ɛkˈstruːsaɪl, ekˈstruːsɪl/
noun
可伸出器官
An extrusile organ or part.
The chameleon's tongue is an extrusile. 变色龙的舌头是一个可伸出器官。

近义词:protrusible, extensible
反义词:retractile, non-protrusible
词性:adjective, noun

词源:From Latin extrūsus, past participle of extrūdere (“to thrust or push out”), from ex- (“out”) + trūdere (“to thrust”).
