lushnesses:/ˈlʌʃnɪsɪz/
n.
 luxuriance; lavish abundance
The lushnesses of the tropical rainforest were breathtaking.  热带雨林的繁茂令人叹为观止。
近义词：abundance, luxuriance, profusion, richness
反义词：barrenness, scarcity, sparseness
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

词源:  From lush + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality). Lush itself comes from Middle English *lussh, perhaps of imitative origin, suggesting something growing vigorously.
