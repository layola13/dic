monarchize:/ˈmɒnəkɪzaɪz/ /ˈmɑːnərkaɪz/ | vt.| 君主化，使成为君主制|To establish as a monarchy; to make into a monarch. |The general wanted to monarchize the newly formed republic. 将军想把新成立的共和国变成君主制国家。|  The country was monarchized after the revolution.  革命后这个国家变成了君主制国家。| 近义词: crown, enthrone | 反义词: democratize, republicanize | 词性: 动词
词源: From monarch + -ize.  源自 monarch（君主）+ -ize（动词后缀，表示“使成为”）。
