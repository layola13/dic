nonrationalization:/ˌnɒnˌræʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 非理性化，不合理化 | The act or process of not rationalizing something; the absence or avoidance of rationalization.  It implies a conscious decision to not explain or justify something through logic or reason, often because doing so would be difficult, uncomfortable, or reveal underlying motivations that one prefers to keep hidden. | Her nonrationalization of her extravagant spending habits led to financial trouble. 她不合理化自己奢侈的消费习惯导致了经济困难。| Near synonyms: non-justification, lack of explanation | Antonyms: rationalization, justification | noun
