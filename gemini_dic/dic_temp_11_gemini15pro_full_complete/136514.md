nonroyalty:/nɑːnˈrɔɪəlti/ | n. | 非专利费，免版税 |  Refers to a payment method where a flat fee is paid for the use of intellectual property, rather than a royalty based on sales or usage. | The company offers nonroyalty stock photos for a one-time purchase. 这家公司提供一次性购买的免版税图片。 | synonyms: flat fee, one-time payment | antonyms: royalty, per-unit fee | noun


nonroyalty:/nɒnˈrɔɪəlti/ | adj. | 免版税的 | Not subject to royalty payments. |  We purchased a nonroyalty image for our website.  我们为我们的网站购买了一张免版税的图片。 | synonyms: royalty-free | antonyms: royalty-based, royalty-obligated | adjective
