microsystems:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌsɪstəmz/| n. |微系统；微系统技术|Miniaturized systems, typically fabricated using integrated circuit technology, that combine electrical and mechanical components on a very small scale.|Researchers are developing new microsystems for medical implants.|Synonyms: microdevices, MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems)|Antonyms: macrosystems|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From micro- + system.  "Micro-" comes from Greek "mikros" meaning "small". "System" comes from Latin "systema"  and Greek "systēma" meaning "organized whole".
