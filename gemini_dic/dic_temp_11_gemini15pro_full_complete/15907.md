ballpoints:/ˈbɔːlpɔɪnts/| n. |圆珠笔（复数）|Plural form of "ballpoint," referring to more than one ballpoint pen.|I have several ballpoints in my bag. 我的包里有好几支圆珠笔。|Synonym: pens, biros|Antonym: fountain pens|noun

词源:  "ballpoint" comes from the combination of "ball" and "point," referring to the small ball at the tip of the pen.  "Ball" originates from Old Norse "bollr," and "point" from Old French "point," ultimately from Latin "punctum."
