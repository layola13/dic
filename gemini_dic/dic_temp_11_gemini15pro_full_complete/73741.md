forky:/ˈfɔːrki/| adj.|叉状的，分叉的|Resembling a fork; forked.  Having tines or prongs like a fork.|The lightning struck the forky branches of the old oak tree. 闪电击中了老橡树的分叉树枝。|近义词：forked, branched, pronged|反义词:straight, unbranched|词性:形容词

forky:/ˈfɔːrki/| n.|叉子|A fork. (Informal)|Grab a forky and dig in! 拿个叉子开吃吧！|近义词：fork, utensil|反义词:spoon, knife (These are not true opposites, but represent different utensils)|词性:名词
