ogrishly:/ˈoʊɡrɪʃli/| adv.|面目狰狞地，粗野地，令人作呕地|in a very unpleasant or offensive way, suggesting a cruel and grotesque enjoyment|He grinned ogrishly at his victim. 他狰狞地对着他的受害者咧嘴一笑。|近义词：hideously, grotesquely, repulsively, monstrously|反义词：beautifully, attractively, pleasantly|词性:副词

词源: From ogre (食人魔) + -ish (像...的) + -ly (副词后缀)  The word "ogre" comes from the French ogre, itself from the Italian orco, ultimately derived from the Latin Orcus, the name of the Roman god of the underworld.
