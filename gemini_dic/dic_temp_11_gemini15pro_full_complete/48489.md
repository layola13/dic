deepening:/ˈdiːpənɪŋ/ | n. adj. | 加深，深化；深厚的 | The deepening of the river allowed larger ships to pass. 河道的加深使得更大的船只得以通行。|  The deepening crisis requires immediate action.  日益加深的危机需要立即采取行动。 |近义词:intensifying, increasing, strengthening |反义词: lessening, decreasing, weakening |词性:名词，形容词
Etymology: From the verb "deepen," which comes from the Old English "dēopian," meaning "to make deep." The "-ing" suffix transforms the verb into a noun (gerund) or adjective (present participle).
