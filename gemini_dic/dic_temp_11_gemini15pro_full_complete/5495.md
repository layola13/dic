alopecist:/ˌæləˈpiːsɪst/
n.
脱发症专家，治疗脱发症的医生
A doctor who specializes in the treatment of baldness and other hair loss conditions.
例句：The alopecist recommended a new treatment for my hair loss.  脱发症专家建议我采用一种新的治疗方法来治疗脱发。

例句：She consulted an alopecist after noticing significant thinning of her hair.  她注意到头发明显变薄后咨询了一位脱发症专家。

近义词：hair loss specialist, trichologist

词性: noun
