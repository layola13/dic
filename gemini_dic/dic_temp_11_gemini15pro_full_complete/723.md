absentment: /æbˈsɛntmənt/
n.
state of being absent; preoccupation
心不在焉；出神；神游
He sat through the meeting in a state of absentment. 他在会议中一直心不在焉。
His absentment during the lecture was noticeable. 他在讲座期间的走神很明显。
近义词: abstraction, preoccupation, inattention, detachment
反义词: attention, presence, focus, concentration
词性: 名词


Etymology:  From the verb "absent" + "-ment" (suffix forming nouns of action or result). "Absent" itself comes from the Latin "absens," present participle of "abesse" (to be away), from "ab-" (away from) + "esse" (to be).
