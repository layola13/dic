jazzing:/ˈdʒæzɪŋ/
verb
使…爵士化; 演奏爵士乐
making something more lively or exciting, often by adding elements of jazz music; playing jazz music
He was jazzing up the song with some improvised piano riffs. 他用一些即兴的钢琴重复乐段使这首歌更加爵士化。
She spent the evening jazzing at a local club.  她在当地一家俱乐部演奏了一晚爵士乐。
近义词: enlivening, invigorating, improvising, swinging
反义词: dulling, boring
词性: v.
