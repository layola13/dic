explorate:/ɪkˈsplɔːreɪt/ /ɛkˈsplɔːreɪt/
verb
探索，探究
To explore or investigate something thoroughly.
例句：The researchers are explorating new ways to treat the disease. 研究人员正在探索治疗这种疾病的新方法。

例句：The team set out to explorate the uncharted territory.  该团队出发去探索未知的领域。

近义词：explore, investigate, examine, probe, research
反义词：ignore, neglect, overlook
词性：v.
