arriviste:/ˌærɪˈviːst/ | n. |暴发户，新贵，野心家 | A person who has recently reached a position of power or success, especially in a way that is considered rude or unpleasant.  Someone who has recently achieved success and wealth, often flaunting it in a vulgar manner. |  He's an arriviste who only cares about money and status. 他是个只在乎金钱和地位的暴发户。|Synonyms: social climber, parvenu, upstart, nouveau riche | Antonyms: established figure, old money, aristocrat | Noun

arrivistes:/ˌærɪˈviːsts/ | n. |暴发户们，新贵们，野心家们 | Plural form of arriviste.  A group of people who have recently reached a position of power or success, often in a way that is considered rude or unpleasant.| The party was full of arrivistes showing off their expensive clothes and jewelry.  派对上挤满了炫耀昂贵衣服和珠宝的暴发户。| Synonyms: social climbers, parvenus, upstarts, nouveaux riches | Antonyms: established figures, old money, aristocrats | Noun
