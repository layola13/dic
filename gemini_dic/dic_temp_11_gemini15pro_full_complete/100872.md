intrudes:/ɪnˈtruːdz/| v. |侵入，打扰，闯入|to come or go in without permission or welcome|例句：I don't want to intrude on your privacy. 我不想侵犯你的隐私。|例句：The noise of the traffic intruded into our conversation. 交通噪音干扰了我们的谈话。|近义词：interfere, interrupt, impinge, encroach|反义词：withdraw, retreat|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *intrudere, present active infinitive of intrudo (“thrust or push in, obtrude”), from in (“in, into, on, upon, against”) + trudo (“thrust, push”).
