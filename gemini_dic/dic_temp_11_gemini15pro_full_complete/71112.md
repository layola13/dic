fishermen:/ˈfɪʃərmən/ (US), /ˈfɪʃəmən/ (UK)| n. |渔民；钓鱼的人|A person who catches fish, especially as a job or for sport.|The fishermen returned to the harbor after a long day at sea. 渔民们在海上待了一整天后回到了港口。|Synonyms: angler, fisher|Antonyms: None|Noun
