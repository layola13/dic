clingy:/ˈklɪŋi/| adj.|粘人的，依恋的|Excessively dependent on someone emotionally; needing a lot of attention and reassurance.|例句：The child was very clingy to his mother after the scary movie.  孩子看完恐怖电影后非常粘着他的妈妈。|近义词：dependent, needy, attached|反义词:independent, detached|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "cling" (Old English clingan "to adhere, stick to") + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives).
