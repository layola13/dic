capaciously:/kəˈpeɪʃəsli/
adjective
宽敞地；容量大地
in a spacious manner; able to hold or contain a large quantity.
The bag was capaciously designed, holding all of her belongings with ease.  这个包设计得很宽敞，可以轻松容纳她所有的物品。
The new stadium is capaciously built and can accommodate thousands of spectators.  新建的体育场容量很大，可以容纳数千名观众。
synonym:roomily, spaciously, extensively, comprehensively
antonym:narrowly, restrictedly, tightly, compactly
词性:adjective
