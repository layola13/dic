fbx:/ˌɛf biː ˈɛks/| n.|FBX文件格式，Filmbox|A proprietary file format (.fbx) developed by Autodesk for motion capture data and 3D models. It is commonly used in game development, film, and other 3D applications.  It facilitates interoperability between digital content creation applications.|The artist exported the character model as an FBX file.  艺术家将角色模型导出为FBX文件。|近义词：OBJ, DAE, glTF|反义词: None
