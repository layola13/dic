Example 2: Belonites are common fossils found in certain marine deposits.  箭石是在某些海相沉积物中常见的化石。|Synonym: belemnite guard, fossil|Antonym: N/A (as it's a specific type of fossil, it doesn't have a direct opposite)|noun
