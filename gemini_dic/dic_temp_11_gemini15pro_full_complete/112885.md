lud:/lʌd/ | n. | 笨蛋，傻瓜 | A clumsy or foolish person. |  Don't be such a lud!  别这么笨! | Synonyms:  oaf, klutz, dolt, idiot  | Antonyms: genius, expert, adept | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lod*, probably from Old Norse *lǫðr* (“unskillful, clumsy”). Cognate with Icelandic *lúður* (“a clumsy, stupid fellow”), Norwegian *luta* (“to be clumsy”).
