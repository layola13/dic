boon's:/buːnz/
n.|恩惠的，好处的|The possessive form of "boon," meaning something beneficial or helpful.|This rain is a boon's for the farmers. 这场雨对农民来说是件好事。|近义词:blessing's, advantage's, gift's|反义词:bane's, curse's, disadvantage's|词性:名词所有格


词源:
From Middle English *bone, from Old Norse bón "a petition, prayer," from Proto-Germanic *bōniz.
