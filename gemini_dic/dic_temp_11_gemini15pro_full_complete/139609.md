occasionality:/əˌkeɪʒəˈnæləti/
n.
偶然性；偶尔发生的事情
The occasionality of their meetings made them all the more special.  他们偶尔的会面使得这些会面显得更加特别。
Their communication was marked by its occasionality. 他们的交流特点是偶尔为之。
近义词:infrequency, rarity, sporadicness
反义词:frequency, regularity, commonness
词性:名词

occasionally:/əˈkeɪʒnəli/
adv.
偶尔；间或
I occasionally go for a walk in the park. 我偶尔去公园散步。
He occasionally works late. 他偶尔工作到很晚。
近义词:sometimes, now and then, once in a while
反义词:frequently, often, regularly
词性:副词
