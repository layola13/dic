out-trump:/ˌaʊtˈtrʌmp/
verb
胜过，比……更强
to defeat someone by saying or doing something even better
例句：He out-trumped his rivals by launching a new product. 他通过推出新产品胜过了他的竞争对手。

例句：She out-trumped him with a higher bid. 她用更高的出价胜过他。

近义词：beat, defeat, surpass, outdo, outmaneuver
反义词：lose to, be defeated by
词性：v.
