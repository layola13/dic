overfaithfulnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈfeɪθfʊlnəsɪz/
n.
过度忠诚，过分忠实
Excessive faithfulness or loyalty, often to an impractical or detrimental degree.
His overfaithfulnesses to the outdated traditions hindered the company's progress. 他对过时传统的过度忠诚阻碍了公司的发展。
近义词：excessive loyalty, overzealousness, fanaticism
反义词：disloyalty, infidelity, treachery
词性: 名词
