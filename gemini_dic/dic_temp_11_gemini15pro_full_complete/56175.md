dodecandrous:/doʊˌdɛkˈændrəs/| adj.|十二雄蕊的|Having twelve stamens.|The dodecandrous flower attracted many bees. 十二雄蕊的花朵吸引了许多蜜蜂。|Near Synonyms: polyandrous (having many stamens),  12-stamened|Antonyms: apetalous (lacking petals - though not directly related to stamen count, it represents a contrasting floral characteristic)
词性: adjective
