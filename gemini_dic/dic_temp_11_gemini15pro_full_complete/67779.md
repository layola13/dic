eyeletted:/ˈaɪlɪtɪd/
adjective
有孔眼的；装有孔眼的
Having eyelets; furnished with eyelets.
Her eyeletted curtains allowed a gentle breeze to flow through the room. 她那带孔眼的窗帘让微风吹进了房间。
近义词: perforated, pierced
反义词: solid, unperforated
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From eyelet + -ed.  "Eyelet" comes from the Old French "oeillet" meaning "little eye".
