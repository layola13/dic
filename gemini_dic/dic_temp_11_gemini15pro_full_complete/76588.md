gallinacean:/ˌɡælɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 鸡形目鸟类，家禽 |A bird of the order Galliformes, such as a chicken, turkey, or pheasant. |  Chickens, turkeys, and pheasants are all gallinaceans.  鸡、火鸡和野鸡都是鸡形目鸟类。 | synonyms: fowl, poultry | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps birds of prey) | noun

gallinaceous:/ˌɡælɪˈneɪʃəs/ | adj. | 鸡形目的，属于鸡形目的 | Relating to or resembling the gallinaceous birds. | The farm specialized in raising gallinaceous birds. 这个农场专门饲养鸡形目鸟类。 | synonyms: fowl-like, chicken-like | antonyms: (No direct antonym) | adjective
