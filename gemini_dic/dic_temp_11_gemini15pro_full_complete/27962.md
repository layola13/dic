cantoral:/kænˈtɔːrəl/
adjective
歌唱的；歌曲的；圣咏的
relating to singing or song, especially in a church
The cantoral tradition in this cathedral is centuries old.  这个大教堂的歌唱传统已有数百年历史。
近义词:choral, musical
反义词:instrumental

cantoral:/kænˈtɔːrəl/
noun
圣歌集；歌集
a collection of hymns or chants, typically for use in church services
The choirmaster selected a piece from the new cantoral.  唱诗班指挥从新的圣歌集中选了一首曲子。
近义词:hymnal, songbook
反义词: N/A
