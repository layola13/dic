exchanges:/ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒɪz/
n.|交换; 交流; 交易; 交易所|The two countries established diplomatic exchanges. 这两个国家建立了外交关系。|
近义词:trade, dealings, interchange, swap, barter
反义词:retention, withholding
词性:名词(复数)


exchanges:/ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒɪz/
v.|交换; 交流; 交易;兑换|We exchanged gifts at Christmas. 我们在圣诞节交换了礼物。|
近义词:trade, swap, barter, interchange
反义词:retain, withhold
词性:动词(第三人称单数)


词源: From Old French *eschangier (Modern French échanger), from Late Latin *excambiare, from Latin ex- (“out”) + cambiare (“to barter”).
