damocleses:/ˈdæməkliːziːz/
|n.|达摩克利斯之剑（的复数形式）|An allusion to the imminent and ever-present peril faced by those in positions of power, referring to the sword hung by a single hair over the head of Damocles.|The modern politician lives with several damocleses hanging over his head: scandal, unpopular policies, and powerful enemies.|近义词:threats, perils, dangers|反义词:safety, security|Noun
