loris:/ˈlɔːrɪs/ (US), /ˈlɒrɪs/ (UK)| n.| 懒猴 |A small, nocturnal primate with large eyes, found in Southeast Asia and Africa. It is known for its slow movements and toxic bite.|  The loris moved slowly through the trees. 懒猴在树上缓慢地移动。|The loris clung tightly to the branch. 懒猴紧紧地抱住树枝。| synonym:bushbaby, potto | antonym: N/A | noun
