gunfighters:/ˈɡʌnˌfaɪtərz/
n.
持枪歹徒；枪手
People skilled in using guns, especially in the American Old West.
The movie depicted a showdown between two legendary gunfighters.  这部电影描绘了两个传奇枪手之间的对决。
Synonyms: gunslinger, shootist, gunman
Antonyms: pacifist, peacemaker
noun
