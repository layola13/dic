hairpiece:/ˈhɛərpiːs/| n.|假发，假发片|A piece of artificial hair worn to cover baldness or to give the appearance of having more hair.|He wore a hairpiece to conceal his thinning hair. 他戴了假发来掩盖他日渐稀疏的头发。|Synonyms: wig, toupee, hair extension|Antonyms: natural hair|noun
