exaltation:/ˌɛɡzɔːlˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 兴奋，得意，提升，崇高 |A feeling of extreme happiness or triumph.  The act of raising someone to a higher position or status. |  He felt a sense of exaltation as he reached the mountain summit. 他到达山顶时感到一阵兴奋。 The exaltation of the king was a grand ceremony. 国王的登基是一个盛大的仪式。 |近义词：elation, joy, elevation, promotion |反义词：dejection, depression, humiliation, demotion | 词性: 名词
