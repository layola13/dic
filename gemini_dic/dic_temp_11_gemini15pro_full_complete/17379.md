beads:/biːdz/| n. |珠子；念珠|Small, round objects with a hole through them, often strung together to make jewelry or used for decoration. |She wore a necklace of brightly colored beads. 她戴着一串色彩鲜艳的珠子。

The monk fingered his beads as he prayed.  僧侣祈祷时拨动着他的念珠。
