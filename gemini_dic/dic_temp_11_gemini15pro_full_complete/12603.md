assailer:/əˈseɪlər/ | n. | 攻击者；袭击者 | A person who physically attacks another. | The assailant was quickly apprehended by the police. 袭击者很快被警察逮捕了。| synonyms: attacker, aggressor, mugger, assaulter | antonyms: defender, protector | noun

assailer:/əˈseɪlər/ | n. | 抨击者；猛烈批评者 | A person who strongly criticizes someone or something. |  The politician's policies were met with harsh criticism from several prominent assaulters in the media.  这位政治家的政策遭到了媒体几位著名抨击者的严厉批评。 | synonyms: critic, detractor, opponent, antagonist | antonyms: supporter, advocate, proponent | noun
