directorate:/dəˈrɛktərɪt/ /daɪˈrɛktərɪt/| n. |理事会，董事会，局|A group of directors who manage a company or organization; a department or division of a government, business, etc. |The directorate voted to approve the merger. 理事会投票通过了合并。|The Aviation Directorate is responsible for regulating air travel. 航空局负责管理航空旅行。|近义词：board, council, administration, department, division|反义词:|noun
