forklift:/ˈfɔːrklɪft/ (US) /ˈfɔːklɪft/ (UK)| n.|叉车|A small vehicle with two strong bars at the front that are used for lifting and carrying heavy objects.|The warehouse uses a forklift to move pallets of goods. 仓库使用叉车来搬运货盘。|synonyms: lift truck, fork truck, pallet truck|antonyms: N/A|noun

forklift:/ˈfɔːrklɪft/ (US) /ˈfɔːklɪft/ (UK)| vt.|用叉车搬运|To move (something) using a forklift.|They forklift the crates onto the truck. 他们用叉车把板条箱搬到卡车上。|synonyms: lift, hoist, raise (with a forklift)|antonyms: lower, unload (with a forklift)|verb
