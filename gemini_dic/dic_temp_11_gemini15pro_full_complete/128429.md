newline:/ˈnjuːlaɪn/ /ˈnuːlaɪn/| n.|换行符，新行|A newline character or sequence, used to signify the end of a line of text and the beginning of a new one.  It's often represented visually as a break between lines.|Example:  Ensure each entry is on a new line.  确保每个条目都在新行。|Example: The newline character is essential for formatting text files. 换行符对于格式化文本文件至关重要。|Synonyms: line break, carriage return|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
