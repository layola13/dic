fall-under:/fɔːl ˈʌndər/ | phrasal verb | 属于，归入，被归类为 | to belong to a particular group, category, or area of responsibility |  This issue falls under the jurisdiction of the local police.  这个问题属于当地警方的管辖范围。| Synonyms: belong to, be classified as, be included in, come under | Antonyms: be excluded from | verb phrase

fall-under:/fɔːl ˈʌndər/ | phrasal verb | 受…管辖 | to be controlled or affected by something | All building work falls under strict regulations. 所有建筑工程都受严格规章制度的管辖。 | Synonyms: be subject to, be governed by | Antonyms: be exempt from | verb phrase
