如果你想了解单词 "what" 的用法，我可以提供以下信息：

what: /wɒt/ (British) /wʌt/ (American) | pron. det. adv. | 什么，多么 | used to ask for information about something |
例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

例句：What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！

近义词：which, how | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词) | 词性: pronoun, determiner, adverb

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? 你觉得怎么样？
What does she want? 她想要什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you doing? 你在做什么？
What is he watching? 他在看什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned? 你学到了什么？
What has she eaten? 她吃了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on? 你一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading? 他一直在读什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? 你说了什么？
What did she buy? 她买了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing last night? 你昨晚在做什么？
What was she thinking about? 她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? 我来之前你做了什么？
What had she eaten before the movie? 电影之前她吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called? 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before the exam? 考试前她一直在学习什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do tomorrow? 你明天要做什么？
What will she cook for dinner? 她晚饭要做些什么？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time tomorrow? 明天这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be studying next year? 明年她会学习什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by next week? 下周之前你会完成什么？
What will she have achieved by then? 到那时她会取得什么成就？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by graduation? 毕业时她将已经学习什么多久了？


PHRASE->
What's up?  怎么了？/你好吗？

What for? 为了什么？
So what? 那又怎样？
