dhaba:/ˈdɑːbə/ (UK), /ˈdɑːbɑː/ (US)| n. | 路边小餐馆，大排档 | A roadside restaurant or stall, typically one serving Punjabi food. |  We stopped at a dhaba for a delicious curry on our road trip. 我们在公路旅行途中停在一个路边小餐馆吃了一顿美味的咖喱。

例句：The dhaba served hot chai and parathas. 这家路边小餐馆供应热奶茶和煎饼。

例句：We found a dhaba with amazing butter chicken. 我们找到一家路边小餐馆，那里的黄油鸡肉棒极了。

近义词：restaurant, eatery, cafe, stall | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) fine dining restaurant |词性: noun
