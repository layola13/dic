cobra:/ˈkoʊbrə/ (US) /ˈkəʊbrə/ (UK)| n. |眼镜蛇|A venomous snake of the family Elapidae, characterized by the ability to expand the skin of the neck into a hood when threatened.|The cobra raised its hood and hissed menacingly. 眼镜蛇竖起兜帽，发出威胁的嘶嘶声。|近义词：asp, viper|反义词: N/A|名词
