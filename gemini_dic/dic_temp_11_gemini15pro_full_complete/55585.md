disulphuric:/ˌdaɪsəlˈfjʊərɪk/ | adj. | 二硫酸的；焦硫酸的 |  Relating to or containing two sulphur atoms per molecule, especially in an acid. | Disulphuric acid is also known as pyrosulphuric acid. 二硫酸也称为焦硫酸。| Disulphuric acid is a strong dehydrating agent. 二硫酸是一种强脱水剂。 | 近义词: pyrosulphuric | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider sulphuric as a related but simpler form) | 词性: 形容词

disulphate:/ˌdaɪˈsʌlfeɪt/ | n. | 二硫酸盐；焦硫酸盐 | A salt or ester of disulphuric acid. | Potassium disulphate is a common example of a disulphate salt. 硫酸氢钾是一种常见的二硫酸盐。 |  Sodium disulphate is used in some cleaning products. 硫酸氢钠用于一些清洁产品中。| 近义词: pyrosulphate | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: 名词
