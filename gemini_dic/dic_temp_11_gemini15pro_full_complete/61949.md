emulsified: /ɪˈmʌlsɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 乳化的 | Made into or resembling an emulsion; (of a liquid) combined with another liquid that does not normally mix, such as oil and water, by forming an emulsion. | The mayonnaise was perfectly emulsified, smooth and creamy.  蛋黄酱完全乳化了，光滑细腻。| The salad dressing was emulsified with a bit of mustard. 沙拉酱加了一点芥末乳化了。 | 近义词: blended, mixed, homogenized | 反义词: separated, immiscible | 词性: adjective
词源: From the verb emulsify, which comes from the Latin word emulgere, meaning "to milk out."  The prefix "e-" means "out," and "mulgere" means "to milk."  The original meaning referred to the milky appearance of some emulsions.
