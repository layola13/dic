dreys:/dreɪz/
n.
squirrel's nests
A drey is the nest of a tree squirrel, typically made of twigs and leaves.
The squirrel scurried back to its drey high in the oak tree. 松鼠匆匆跑回它在橡树上的巢穴。
nests, shelters
No single antonym for "dreys" as it's a specific type of nest.  Perhaps "burrow" if contrasting with a ground-dwelling animal's home.
