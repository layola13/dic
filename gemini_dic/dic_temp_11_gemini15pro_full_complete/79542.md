glockenspiel:/ˈɡlɒkənʃpiːl/, /ˈɡlɑːkənˌspiːl/ | n. | 钢片琴；钟琴 | A musical instrument consisting of a set of tuned metal bars arranged in the manner of a keyboard, played with small hammers. | The glockenspiel added a bright, shimmering sound to the orchestra. 钢片琴为管弦乐队增添了明亮闪烁的声音。|  The composer used a glockenspiel to evoke a sense of childlike wonder. 作曲家使用钟琴来唤起一种孩子般的好奇感。 | Synonyms: metallophone, bell lyre, orchestra bells | Antonyms: None | noun
