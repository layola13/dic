foregrounding:/ˈfɔːrɡraʊndɪŋ/ | n. |突出，前景化|The act of making something more prominent or noticeable, especially in a literary or artistic work, by emphasizing it in some way. |The poet uses foregrounding to draw attention to the theme of loss. 诗人用前景化来强调失去的主题。| The artist used color foregrounding to emphasize the central figure. 艺术家使用颜色前景化来强调中心人物。 | highlighting, emphasizing, featuring | backgrounding | noun
