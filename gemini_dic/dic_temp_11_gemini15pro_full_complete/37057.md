comeback:/ˈkʌmˌbæk/| n.|回归，复出，反驳|an attempt to return to a former position of power, success, or fame, or a return to such a position; a quick, clever reply to a criticism|例句：The band made a comeback after a ten-year break.  乐队在十年中断后复出。

例句：She made a witty comeback to his insulting remark. 她对他侮辱性的言论做出了机智的反驳。|近义词：return, revival, recovery, retort, reply|反义词:decline, downfall, departure|词性:名词
