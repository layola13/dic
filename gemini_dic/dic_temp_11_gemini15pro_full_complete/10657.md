approvableness:/əˈpruːvəblnəs/
n.
可赞许性；值得批准的性质
The approvableness of the project was evident from the board's unanimous vote.
该项目的可赞许性从董事会的一致投票中可见一斑。
acceptability, suitability, desirability
undesirability, unsuitability, objectionableness
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From approve + -able + -ness.  Approve comes from Old French approuver, from Latin approbare, from ad- "to" + probare "to prove, test".
