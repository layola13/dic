mesobiology:/ˌmiːzoʊbaɪˈɒlədʒi/ | n. | 中尺度生物学 | The study of biological systems at an intermediate spatial scale, typically between the microscopic and macroscopic levels, often focusing on communities and ecosystems. | Example 1: Mesobiology bridges the gap between individual organisms and larger ecosystems. 中尺度生物学弥合了个体生物与更大生态系统之间的差距。

Example 2: Research in mesobiology often involves studying the interactions between different species within a specific area. 中尺度生物学的研究通常涉及研究特定区域内不同物种之间的相互作用。 | Near synonyms: landscape ecology, ecosystem biology, community ecology | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but could be contrasted with) molecular biology, cell biology | Noun
