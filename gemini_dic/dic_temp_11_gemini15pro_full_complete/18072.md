begum:/ˈbeɪɡəm/ | n. | 女王；贵妇；穆斯林妇女敬称 | A title of respect for a Muslim woman, especially one of high rank; a Muslim princess or lady of high rank. |  She was addressed as Begum by everyone in the community.  她被社区里的每个人尊称为女王。

The Begum lived in a grand palace.  这位女王住在宏伟的宫殿里。 | 近义词: queen, lady, princess | 反义词: commoner | 词性: noun
