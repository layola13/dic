heterogeneously:/ˌhɛtərəˈdʒiːniəsli/ /ˌhetərəˈdʒiːniəsli/| adv.|异质地，不均匀地|In a diverse and varied way, composed of different elements.|The population of the city is heterogeneously distributed, with wealthy neighborhoods alongside poorer areas. 这个城市的人口分布不均，富裕社区与贫困地区并存。| 近义词: diversely, variously, unevenly| 反义词: homogeneously, uniformly|词性: 副词

heterogeneous:/ˌhɛtərəˈdʒiːniəs/ /ˌhetərəˈdʒiːniəs/| adj.|异质的，不均匀的|Composed of different elements or parts; diverse in character or content.|The study group was a heterogeneous mix of students from different backgrounds.  这个学习小组是由来自不同背景的学生组成的异质群体。|近义词: diverse, varied, mixed|反义词: homogeneous, uniform|词性: 形容词
