lupulin: /ˈluːpjʊlɪn/ | n. | 蛇麻素；啤酒花苦味酸 |  A yellow, bitter powder found in the cones of the hop plant, used primarily as a bittering and flavoring agent in beer. It also has mild sedative properties. |  The lupulin glands in hops give beer its characteristic bitterness. 蛇麻腺赋予啤酒特有的苦味。|  Synonyms: hop resin, hop bitter acid | Antonyms: None | Noun


lupulin: /ˈluːpjʊlɪn/ | adj. |  蛇麻素的；含蛇麻素的 | Containing or relating to lupulin.  |  Lupulin glands are tiny, yellow sacs on the hop cone. 蛇麻腺是啤酒花球果上的微小黄色囊状物。| Synonyms: None | Antonyms: None | Adjective
