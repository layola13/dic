evilest:/ˈiːvɪlɪst/
adjective
最邪恶的
most evil; wickedest
He was the evilest man I had ever met. 他是我见过的最邪恶的人。
The evilest villain in the story plotted to destroy the world.  故事中最邪恶的反派密谋摧毁世界。

synonyms:wickedest, most malevolent, most iniquitous, most depraved, most vicious
antonyms:kindest, most benevolent, most virtuous, most righteous
词性: adjective
