melanocytes:/ˈmɛlənəˌsaɪts/ | n. | 黑色素细胞 | A mature melanin-forming cell, typically located in the skin. | Melanocytes produce melanin, which gives skin its color. 黑色素细胞产生黑色素，使皮肤呈现颜色。|  Melanocytes are responsible for producing the pigment that protects our skin from the sun. 黑色素细胞负责产生保护我们皮肤免受阳光照射的色素。 | synonyms: pigment cell | antonyms: N/A | noun
