ootheca:/oʊˈθiːkə/
noun
卵鞘，卵囊
A protective case or capsule containing a mass of eggs, especially as produced by certain insects, mollusks, and other invertebrates.
The praying mantis carefully guards her ootheca until the nymphs hatch.  螳螂小心翼翼地守护着它的卵鞘，直到若虫孵化。
synonyms: egg case, egg sac, capsule
antonyms: N/A
词性: 名词
