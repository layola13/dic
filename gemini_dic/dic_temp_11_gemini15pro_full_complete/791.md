absorber:/əbˈzɔːrbər/ /əbˈsɔːrbər/| n.|吸收器，吸收剂|A device or substance that absorbs something, such as energy, liquids, or gases.|The shock absorber on my car needs to be replaced. 我的汽车的减震器需要更换。|Synonym: sponge, buffer, cushion|Antonym: emitter, radiator|noun

absorber:/əbˈzɔːrbər/ /əbˈsɔːrbər/| n.|吸收者|A person or thing that absorbs something.|Plants are absorbers of carbon dioxide.  植物是二氧化碳的吸收者。|Synonym: consumer, user|Antonym: producer, creator|noun
