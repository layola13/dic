abbeys:/ˈæbiz/| n. |修道院（复数）|A building or buildings occupied by a community of monks or nuns.|The ruins of the ancient abbeys are a popular tourist destination. 古老修道院的遗址是一个热门的旅游目的地。|近义词：monasteries, convents|反义词: None|Noun

词源: From Old French *abeie, from Late Latin *abbatia, from Latin abbas (“abbot”). Ultimately from Aramaic ܐܒܐ (abbā, “father”).
