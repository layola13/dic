hounder:/ˈhaʊndər/| n.|猎犬；猎狐者；侦探，追查者|A person or dog that hunts, especially one that hunts with hounds. |例句：The experienced hounder easily tracked the deer through the forest. 经验丰富的猎犬很容易在森林里追踪到鹿。|例句：The police hounder relentlessly pursued the escaped convict. 警方的侦探无情地追捕逃犯。|近义词：hunter, tracker, pursuer, detective|反义词:prey, quarry|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hundere*, from Old English *hundora*, agent noun from *hundian* "to hunt," from *hund* "hound." Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *hundaz* "dog," from Proto-Indo-European *ḱwōn-* "dog."
