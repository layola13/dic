destructured:/diːˈstrʌktʃərd/| adj.|解构的，分解的|Broken down into its constituent parts, typically in order to restructure or rearrange them in a different way.|例句：The destructured data was easier to analyze.  解构后的数据更容易分析。|例句：The artist presented a destructured version of a classic painting.  这位艺术家展示了一幅经典画作的解构版本。|近义词：deconstructed, disassembled, broken down|反义词：structured, organized, assembled|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From de- (prefix indicating reversal or removal) + structured (past participle of structure).  Ultimately from Latin structura "a fitting together, building," from structus, past participle of struere "to pile up, build."
