nonmimetically:/ˌnɒnmaɪˈmɛtɪkli/
adv.|非模仿地，不模仿现实地|Not imitating reality; in a way that does not represent external reality. |The artist painted nonmimetically, using abstract shapes and colors rather than depicting recognizable objects.  这位艺术家以非模仿的方式作画，使用抽象的形状和颜色，而不是描绘可识别的物体。|Synonym: abstractly, nonrepresentationally|Antonym: realistically, mimetically
Etymology:  From non- + mimetic + -ally.  "Mimetic" comes from Greek "mimētikos," meaning "imitative," from "mimeisthai" meaning "to imitate."
