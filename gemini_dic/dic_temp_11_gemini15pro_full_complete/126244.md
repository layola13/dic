mycoprotein:/ˌmaɪkoʊˈproʊtiːn/ | n. | 菌蛋白;真菌蛋白 |A protein derived from fungi, especially as produced for human consumption. | Example: Mycoprotein is a sustainable alternative to meat. 菌蛋白是肉类的可持续替代品。

Example:  Products containing mycoprotein are becoming increasingly popular.  含有菌蛋白的产品正变得越来越流行。 | Synonym: fungal protein | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps animal protein) | Noun
