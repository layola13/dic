bermed:/bɜːmd/| adj.|有护堤的，有斜坡的|Furnished with a berm; embanked. A berm is a raised bank or path, especially at the edge of a road or canal.|The road was bermed to prevent flooding. 这条路有护堤以防止洪水泛滥。|近义词：embanked, sloped, banked|反义词:flat, level|词性:形容词
berm:/bɜːrm/| n.|护堤，斜坡，路肩|A raised bank or path, especially at the edge of a road or canal; a narrow shelf, ledge, or path along the top or bottom of a slope.|The berm alongside the highway is covered with wildflowers. 高速公路旁的斜坡上开满了野花。|近义词：bank, embankment, shoulder|反义词:ditch, trench|词性:名词
