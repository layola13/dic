nonacutely:/nɒnəˈkjuːtli/ | adv. | 非急性地，非剧烈地 | Not acutely; in a way that is not sharp, severe, or intense. | The pain was nonacutely felt, more a dull ache than a stabbing sensation. 疼痛感觉不剧烈，与其说是刺痛感，不如说是一种钝痛。| The problem manifested nonacutely, developing gradually over several months.  这个问题表现得并不剧烈，是在几个月内逐渐发展的。 | 近义词: mildly, moderately, slightly, gently | 反义词: acutely, severely, intensely | 词性: 副词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
acutely (adverb): from Latin "acutus" (sharp, pointed) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix)
