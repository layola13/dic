forbearer:/ˈfɔːrˌberər/| n.|祖先，先辈|A person from whom one is descended; an ancestor.|例句：Our forbearers endured many hardships to establish this community. 我们的祖先们为了建立这个社区，经历了许多苦难。

例句：He learned the traditional crafts from his forbearers. 他从他的祖先那里学习了传统工艺。|近义词：ancestor, forefather, progenitor|反义词:descendant, offspring|词性:名词
