noncharacters:/nɒnˈkærɪktəz/ | n. | 非字符 | Characters that do not belong to a defined set, such as letters, numbers, or symbols.  Often used in computing to refer to invalid or unrecognized input. | The system rejected the input because it contained noncharacters. 系统拒绝了输入，因为它包含非字符。| synonyms: invalid characters, illegal characters, unrecognized characters | antonyms: characters, valid characters, legal characters | noun

noncharacter:/nɒnˈkærɪktər/ | n. | 非人物; 不重要的人 | A person considered unimportant or insignificant. Can also refer to someone lacking strong moral qualities. | He was treated as a noncharacter in the story.  在故事中，他被当作一个无足轻重的人物。| synonyms: nobody, insignificant person, cipher | antonyms: protagonist, hero, important person | noun
