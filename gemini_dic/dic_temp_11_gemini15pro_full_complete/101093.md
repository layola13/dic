invernesses:/ˌɪnvərˈnɛsɪz/
noun
因弗内斯式披风；苏格兰短裙式披肩；（尤指旧时的）带披肩的宽大外套
A loose-fitting, knee-length overcoat with a cape, typically worn in Scotland.
She wore a heavy Inverness cape to protect her from the biting wind. 她穿着一件厚重的因弗内斯披风以抵御刺骨的寒风。
Near synonyms: cape, overcoat, cloak
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but consider) light clothing, summer wear
词性: 名词(plural)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Inverness, a city in Scotland where this type of garment was popular. 源自苏格兰城市因弗内斯，这种服装在那里很流行。
