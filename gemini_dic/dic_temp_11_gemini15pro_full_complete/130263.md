non-stock:/ˌnɑːnˈstɑːk/ | adj. | 非股份的，非股票的 | Not organized as a stock corporation; not issuing or trading shares of stock. |  A non-stock corporation is owned by its members, not shareholders.  一个非股份公司是由其成员而不是股东拥有的。 | Example: This is a non-stock, non-profit organization. 这是一个非股份的非营利组织。 | Synonym: non-profit, mutual | Antonym: for-profit, stock, publicly traded | adjective
