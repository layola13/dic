nonconcentrically:/ˌnɒnkənˈsentrɪkli/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈsentrɪkli/
adjective
非同心地
not having the same center
The two circles were arranged nonconcentrically. 这两个圆圈排列得非同心。

nonconcentrically:/ˌnɒnkənˈsentrɪkli/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈsentrɪkli/
adverb
非同心地
in a nonconcentric manner
The gears rotated nonconcentrically. 齿轮非同心地旋转。


Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + concentric + -ally (adverbial suffix)
Concentric comes from Latin concentricus, from com- "together" + centrum "center."

Synonyms:
eccentrically, off-center

Antonyms:
concentrically, centrally
