angsts:/ˈæŋksts/| n.|焦虑，恐惧|A feeling of deep anxiety or dread, typically an unfocused one about the human condition and the meaning of life.|例句：He was filled with existential angsts. 他充满了存在主义的焦虑。

例句：Teenage angsts about the future are common. 青少年对未来的焦虑是很常见的。|近义词：anxieties, fears, worries, dreads|反义词：calm, peace, tranquility|词性: noun
