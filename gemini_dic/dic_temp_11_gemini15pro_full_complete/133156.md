nonethnics:/nɒnˈɛθnɪks/
noun
非种族群体；不属于特定种族的人
People who do not identify with or belong to a particular ethnic group.
The census categorized respondents as either belonging to a specific ethnic group or as nonethnics.  人口普查将受访者归类为属于特定种族群体或非种族群体。
The study focused on the health outcomes of nonethnics in the region. 这项研究的重点是该地区非种族群体的健康结果。
synonyms: non-ethnics, people of mixed heritage, multiracial individuals
antonyms: specific ethnic groups, homogenous populations
