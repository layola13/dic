carbonized: /ˈkɑːrbənaɪzd/ | adj. | 碳化的，烧焦的 |  Converted into carbon, partially burned, or charred.  It describes something that has been heated intensely in the absence of air, resulting in a charred or blackened state. | The archaeologists found carbonized wood in the ancient fireplace. 考古学家在古代壁炉里发现了碳化的木头。 | The carbonized remains of the building offered few clues about the cause of the fire.  这栋建筑烧焦的残骸几乎没有提供任何关于火灾原因的线索。|  charred, burnt, scorched | unburnt, raw | adjective
