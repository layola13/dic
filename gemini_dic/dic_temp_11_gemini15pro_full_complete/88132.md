hiking: /ˈhaɪkɪŋ/ (US), /ˈhaɪkɪŋ/ (UK) | n. | 徒步旅行，远足 | The activity of going for long walks in the countryside or mountains. |  We went hiking in the mountains last weekend.  我们上周末去山上徒步旅行了。

| 近义词: trekking, rambling, walking | 反义词: staying, resting | 词性: 名词


hiking: /ˈhaɪkɪŋ/ (US), /ˈhaɪkɪŋ/ (UK) | adj. | 徒步旅行的 | Relating to hiking. |  We bought some new hiking boots. 我们买了一些新的徒步靴。

| 近义词: trekking, rambling, walking | 反义词: sedentary | 词性: 形容词
