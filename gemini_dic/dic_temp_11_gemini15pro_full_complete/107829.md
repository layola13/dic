larceny:/ˈlɑːrsəni/ | n. | 盗窃罪 | The act of stealing personal property. | The shoplifter was charged with larceny. 商店扒手被指控犯有盗窃罪。| Synonyms: theft, robbery, pilfering | Antonyms: restitution, return | Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *larcin, from Latin latrocinium (“robbery, freebooting, highway robbery”), from latro (“robber, bandit, highwayman”).
