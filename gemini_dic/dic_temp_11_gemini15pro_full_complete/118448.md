mescaline: /ˈmɛskəlɪn/ | n. | 麦司卡林 | A hallucinogenic alkaloid, C₁₁H₁₇NO₃, obtained from the peyote cactus and related species, or produced synthetically. It is used illicitly as a hallucinogen and experimentally in the treatment of alcoholism. |  He experimented with mescaline in college. 他在大学里尝试过麦司卡林。 | 近义词: hallucinogen, psychedelic | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
