heterotopy:/ˈhɛtərəˌtoʊpi/ /ˌhɛtərəˈtɒpi/ | n. | 异位，异位性 | The displacement of an organ or body part from its normal location. |  Example 1: The doctor discovered a cardiac heterotopy, where heart tissue was found outside the chest cavity.  医生发现了一个心脏异位，心脏组织出现在胸腔外。

Example 2:  Gray matter heterotopy is a neurological condition characterized by misplaced gray matter in the brain. 灰质异位是一种以大脑灰质错位为特征的神经系统疾病。 |  近义词: displacement, malposition | 反义词: eutopy (正常位置) | 词性: noun

heterotopias (plural): /ˌhɛtərəˈtoʊpiəz/ /ˌhɛtərəˈtɒpiəz/ | n. | 异位（复数） | Multiple instances of displaced organs or body parts. | Example 1: The patient presented with several heterotopias, including a renal heterotopy and a splenic heterotopy. 病人表现出多个异位，包括肾异位和脾异位。

Example 2:  Genetic mutations can cause various heterotopias during fetal development. 基因突变会导致胎儿发育过程中出现各种异位。 | 近义词: displacements, malpositions | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun
