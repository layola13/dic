ocellus:/oʊˈsɛləs/ | n. |单眼, 小眼|A simple eye or eyespot found in many invertebrates, and in some vertebrates, typically less sensitive to light than a compound eye and incapable of forming a focused image. |Many insects have three ocelli on the top of their heads. 许多昆虫的头部顶部有三个单眼。|The ocelli of the dragonfly help it to detect changes in light intensity. 蜻蜓的单眼帮助它探测光强度的变化。|近义词:eyespot, simple eye |反义词:compound eye |词性:noun

ocelli:/oʊˈsɛlaɪ/ | n. |单眼(复数) |The plural form of ocellus. |Butterflies have two large compound eyes and several ocelli. 蝴蝶有两颗大的复眼和几颗单眼。|The function of ocelli in spiders is still debated by scientists. 蜘蛛单眼的功能仍在科学家们争论中。|近义词:eyespots, simple eyes |反义词:compound eyes |词性:noun
