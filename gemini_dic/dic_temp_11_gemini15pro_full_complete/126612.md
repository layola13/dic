naiads:/ˈnaɪædz/ /ˈneɪædz/| n. |仙女，水仙|A type of nymph believed to inhabit rivers and springs.|例句：The naiads of the stream were said to protect it from pollution. 据说溪流里的仙女保护它免受污染。

例句：Ancient Greeks believed that naiads possessed powers over the waters they inhabited. 古希腊人相信水仙拥有控制她们所居住水域的力量。|近义词：nymphs, sprites, spirits|反义词:|noun
