disdeify:/ˌdɪsˈdiːɪfaɪ/ | v. | 降低神性; 剥夺神性; 不再崇奉 | To deprive of deity; to treat as no longer divine.  To reduce from the status of a god; to remove the divine qualities of. | He disdeified the ancient heroes in his novel, portraying them as flawed and ordinary humans. 他在小说中降低了古代英雄的神性，把他们描绘成有缺陷的普通人。| The iconoclasts disdeified the statues of the old gods. 破坏偶像者剥夺了旧神雕像的神性。 | synonyms: desacralize, profane, demythologize | antonyms: deify, apotheosize, idolize | verb
