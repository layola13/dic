lycoperdon:/ˌlaɪkəˈpɜːrdɒn/ (US) /ˌlaɪkəˈpɜːrdən/ (UK)| n. |马勃菌；灰包菌|A genus of fungi, commonly known as puffballs, that release spores in a cloud when mature.  These fungi are typically round and vary in size. |Example:  I kicked a lycoperdon in the field and watched the spores puff out. 我在田野里踢了一个马勃菌，看着孢子喷出来。|Synonym: puffball, devil's snuff-box|Antonym: N/A (No direct antonym for a type of fungus)|noun
