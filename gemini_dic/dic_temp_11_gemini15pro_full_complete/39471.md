connivance:/kəˈnaɪvəns/
n.
共谋；纵容；默许
Secret or tacit consent to wrongdoing.
The crime was committed with the connivance of the guards.  这起犯罪是在警卫的纵容下进行的。
conniving, collusion, complicity
opposition, resistance
noun

connivences:/kəˈnaɪvənsɪz/
n. (plural)
共谋；纵容；默许（connivance的复数形式）
Instances of secret or tacit consent to wrongdoing.
The investigation revealed multiple connivences between company officials and contractors. 调查揭露了公司官员和承包商之间的多起共谋行为。
connivings, collusions, complicites
oppositions, resistances
noun
