acids:/ˈæsɪdz/| n.|酸|A substance that in solution tastes sour, turns blue litmus paper red, reacts with some metals to liberate hydrogen, reacts with bases to form salts, and promotes certain chemical reactions (called acid catalysis).|Lemon juice contains citric acid. 柠檬汁含有柠檬酸。|synonyms: sours, corrosives|antonyms: bases, alkalis|noun
