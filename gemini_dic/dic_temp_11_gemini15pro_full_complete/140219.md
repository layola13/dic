officiant: /əˈfɪʃiənt/ | n. | 主持人；司仪；官员 | A person who performs a ceremony, especially a wedding. |  The officiant pronounced them husband and wife. 主持人宣布他们成为夫妻。| synonyms: celebrant, presider, minister, priest | antonyms: attendee, guest, participant | noun


officiant: /əˈfɪʃiənt/ | adj. | 执行的；主持的 | Acting as an official in a ceremony. |  His officiant duties included signing the marriage certificate.  他的主持职责包括签署结婚证书。| synonyms: ceremonial, presiding, officiating | antonyms: non-officiating, participating | adjective
