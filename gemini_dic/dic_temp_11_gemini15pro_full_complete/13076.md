astrologies: /əˈstrɒlədʒiz/
n.
占星术；占星学
The study of the movements and relative positions of celestial bodies interpreted as having an influence on human affairs and the natural world.
Many people find comfort and guidance in astrologies. 很多人在占星术中找到慰藉和指引。
Synonyms: horoscopy, star divination
Antonyms: astronomy, science
noun
