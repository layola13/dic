flannel:/ˈflæn(ə)l/ /ˈflænəl/| n. adj.|法兰绒，法兰绒制的|A soft, slightly napped fabric of wool or cotton or a mixture.  A light woolen cloth, typically plain or tartan. |例句：She wore a warm flannel shirt. 她穿着一件温暖的法兰绒衬衫。

|例句：The baby was wrapped in a soft flannel blanket.  婴儿被裹在一块柔软的法兰绒毯子里。
|近义词：woolen, fleece, brushed cotton|反义词:silk, linen|词性:noun, adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
The origin of "flannel" is uncertain, but it likely comes from the Middle French word *flanelle*, first attested in the late 17th century.  It may be related to the Welsh word *gwlanen*, meaning "woolen article," ultimately derived from *gwlan*, "wool."
