lacertian:/ləˈsɜːrʃən/ /ləˈsɜːrtiən/
adjective
蜥蜴似的；蜥蜴的
of or relating to lizards; lizard-like.
The lacertian eye is remarkably acute. 蜥蜴的眼睛非常敏锐。
近义词:reptilian, saurian
反义词:mammalian, avian
词性:adjective

lacertian:/ləˈsɜːrʃən/ /ləˈsɜːrtiən/
noun
蜥蜴
a lizard.
The lacertian darted across the rocks.  这只蜥蜴飞快地穿过岩石。
近义词:lizard, reptile
反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific animal)
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *lacerta* (lizard), from *lacertus* (muscular, strong, lizard).
