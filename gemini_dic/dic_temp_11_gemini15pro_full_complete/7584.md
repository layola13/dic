announce: /əˈnaʊns/ | n. |  宣布，通告（较少用作名词） | the act of making something known publicly | The announcement of the winner will be made soon. 获胜者的宣布即将到来。| synonyms: declaration, proclamation | antonyms: suppression, concealment | noun
