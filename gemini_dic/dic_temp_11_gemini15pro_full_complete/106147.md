knobber:/ˈnɒbə(r)/| n.| 傻瓜，笨蛋；（尤指）乡下人，粗人 |A foolish or naive person, especially a rustic or unsophisticated one. |He called him a country knobber. 他称他为乡巴佬。|Synonyms: idiot, fool, dolt, simpleton, yokel, bumpkin|Antonyms: genius, intellectual, savant|noun
