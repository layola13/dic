bookkeeping: /ˈbʊkkiːpɪŋ/
n.
簿记，记账
The systematic recording of financial transactions.
例句：Accurate bookkeeping is essential for any business.  准确的簿记对任何企业都至关重要。

例句：He took a course in bookkeeping to improve his job prospects. 他参加了簿记课程以提高就业前景。

近义词：accounting, accountancy
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps mismanagement of finances)
词性: noun
