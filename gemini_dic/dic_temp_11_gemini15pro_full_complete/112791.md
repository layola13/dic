lubricity:/luːˈbrɪsɪti/
n.
润滑性；光滑；流畅
the state or quality of being slippery or smooth
The lubricity of the oil ensures smooth engine operation. 这种油的润滑性确保了发动机运转顺畅。

近义词:slipperiness, smoothness, oiliness
反义词:friction, roughness, stickiness
词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *lubricitas, from Latin lubricus (“slippery, smooth”).
