The Merge (以太坊合并)
The Ethereum Merge, transitioning the network from proof-of-work to proof-of-stake.
The successful completion of the Merge marked a significant milestone for Ethereum. 以太坊合并的成功完成标志着以太坊的一个重要里程碑。
近义词: Merge, transition
反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially "fork" in specific contexts)
