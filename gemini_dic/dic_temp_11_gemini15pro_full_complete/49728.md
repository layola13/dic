democratized: /dɪˈmɑːkrətaɪzd/ | v. | 使民主化，使大众化 | To make something accessible to everyone, removing restrictions and making it more egalitarian. | The internet has democratized information, making it available to anyone with a connection. 互联网使信息民主化，任何人都可以访问。| The company democratized its decision-making process, allowing all employees to vote on important issues. 公司将其决策过程民主化，允许所有员工对重要问题进行投票。 |  synonyms: popularized, broadened, made accessible, opened up | antonyms: restricted, limited, monopolized, controlled | verb

democratizing: /dɪˈmɑːkrəˌtaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. |  使民主化的，使大众化的 |  Having the effect of making something more accessible and available to everyone. | The democratizing influence of the internet has changed the way we consume news. 互联网的民主化影响改变了我们消费新闻的方式。 |  The democratizing power of social media has given a voice to marginalized communities. 社交媒体的民主化力量赋予了边缘化社区话语权。 | synonyms: popularizing, broadening, spreading | antonyms: restricting, limiting, controlling | adjective
