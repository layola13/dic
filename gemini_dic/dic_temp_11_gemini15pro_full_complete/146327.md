overfeminizing:/ˌoʊvərˈfɛmɪnaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 过度女性化 |  The act of making something or someone appear excessively feminine, often to the point of stereotype or caricature.  |  Some critics argue that the media is overfeminizing young boys by portraying them as weak and emotional. 一些评论家认为，媒体将年轻男孩描绘成软弱和情绪化的，这过度女性化了他们。 | over-emphasizing femininity, exaggerating feminine traits | under-masculating, demasculinizing | verb


overfeminized:/ˌoʊvərˈfɛmɪnaɪzd/ | adj. | 被过度女性化的 | Having been made to appear excessively feminine, often in a stereotypical or exaggerated way. |  He felt overfeminized by the stylist's choices.  他觉得造型师的选择让他显得过于女性化。 | excessively feminine, overly feminized | masculine, under-feminized | adjective
