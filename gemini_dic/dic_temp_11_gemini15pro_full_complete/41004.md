copaiba:/koʊˈpaɪbə/ /kəˈpeɪbə/
noun
古巴香脂，柯柏胶
A resin obtained from various South American trees of the genus Copaifera, used chiefly in varnishes and lacquers and medicinally as an antiseptic and expectorant.
Copaiba balsam is known for its anti-inflammatory properties. 古巴香脂以其抗炎特性而闻名。
The artist used copaiba as a medium for his oil paints.  这位艺术家使用柯柏胶作为油画的媒介。
synonyms: copaiba balsam, copaiba oil
antonyms: None (Copaiba is a specific resin; it doesn't have a direct opposite.)
词性: noun
