coolibah:/ˈkuːlɪbɑː/
n.
桉树的一种
a type of eucalyptus tree native to Australia
The coolibah tree provided welcome shade in the scorching heat.  酷热难耐的天气里，桉树提供了宜人的阴凉。
近义词:eucalyptus, gum tree
反义词:(无)
词性:名词

coolibahs:/ˈkuːlɪbɑːz/
n.
桉树的复数形式
plural form of coolibah, a type of eucalyptus tree
Coolibahs line the banks of many Australian rivers.  桉树排列在许多澳大利亚河流的岸边。
近义词:eucalyptus trees, gum trees
反义词:(无)
词性:名词
