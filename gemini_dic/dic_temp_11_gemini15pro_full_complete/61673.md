emmenagogue:/ɪˈmenəɡɒɡ/ | n. | 通经药，催经药 | A medicine or other agent that stimulates or increases menstrual flow. |  Example 1: The doctor prescribed an emmenagogue to induce her period. 医生给她开了一种通经药来催经。

Example 2:  Some herbs are traditionally used as emmenagogues.  一些草药传统上被用作通经药。 | 近义词:  menstruum,  | 反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially  medicines that suppress menstruation) | 词性: noun
