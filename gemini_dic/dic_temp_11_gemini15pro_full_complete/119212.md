miam:/mjæm/ | interj. | 美味的，好吃的 | An expression of enjoyment when eating something delicious, often used informally and playfully, similar to "yum." | Example: Miam, this cake is so good!  美味！这蛋糕真好吃！| Synonym: yum, yummy, delicious | Antonym: yuck, disgusting | interjection

miam:/mjæm/ | n. | 美味的食物 | A delicious food or meal, often used informally. | Example:  This pasta is such a miam! 这意大利面真是美味佳肴！ | Synonym: treat, delicacy | Antonym: slop, garbage | noun
