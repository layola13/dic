hubbles:/ˈhʌblz/
n.
哈勃望远镜(通常指哈勃太空望远镜); 哈勃(姓氏)
A large space telescope launched into orbit around the Earth in 1990 to provide information about the universe. / A surname.
The Hubble Space Telescope has revolutionized our understanding of the universe. 哈勃太空望远镜彻底改变了我们对宇宙的理解。
Edwin Hubble's observations helped prove the expansion of the universe. 埃德温·哈勃的观测帮助证明了宇宙的膨胀。
synonym: telescope, observatory (for the telescope itself); no direct synonym for the surname.
antonym: N/A (no direct opposite for a telescope or a surname)
noun
