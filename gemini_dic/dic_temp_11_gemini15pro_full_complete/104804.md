kdramas:/ˈkeɪˌdrɑːməz/
n.
Korean dramas; Korean television series
Korean dramas are television series produced in South Korea.

例句：Kdramas are gaining popularity worldwide. 韩剧正在全球范围内流行。
近义词：K-dramas, Korean series, Korean television shows
反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially other forms of entertainment like American TV shows, or simply "not kdramas")
