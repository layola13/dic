loggerhead:/ˈlɒɡəhed/ (British) /ˈlɔːɡərˌhed/ (American)| n.|红海龟；呆头呆脑的人；执拗的人|A large marine turtle with a reddish-brown shell, found in tropical and subtropical waters around the world. Also refers to a stubborn or thick-headed person.|例句：The loggerhead turtle is an endangered species. 红海龟是一种濒危物种。

例句：He's such a loggerhead, he never listens to anyone. 他真是个呆瓜，从来不听别人的话。|近义词：blockhead, thickhead, dullard, turtle (for the animal)|反义词:genius, intellectual (for the person) |词性:noun


loggerhead:/ˈlɒɡəhed/ (British) /ˈlɔːɡərˌhed/ (American)| adj.|顽固的；呆头呆脑的|Stubborn or thick-headed.|例句：He was being loggerheaded about the whole thing. 他对整件事都很固执。

例句：She gave him a loggerheaded stare. 她顽固地瞪着他。|近义词：stubborn, obstinate, pigheaded, thickheaded|反义词:compliant, agreeable|词性:adjective
