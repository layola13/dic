nonindustrialized:/ˌnɒnɪnˈdʌstriəlaɪzd/ /ˌnɑːnɪnˈdʌstriəlaɪzd/
adjective
非工业化的
Not industrialized; not having developed industries on a large scale.
Many nonindustrialized countries rely on agriculture. 许多非工业化国家依赖农业。
The government is working to improve the infrastructure in nonindustrialized areas. 政府正在努力改善非工业化地区的基礎設施。
synonyms: undeveloped, underdeveloped, non-industrial, preindustrial
antonyms: industrialized, developed
词性: adjective
