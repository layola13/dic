invariant:/ɪnˈveəriənt/ (US) /ɪnˈvɛəriənt/ (UK)| adj. n.|不变的；不变量|Something that remains unchanged under a specific set of transformations or conditions.  An unchanging quantity or property.|例句：The speed of light in a vacuum is an invariant. 真空中光速是一个不变量。|近义词：constant, unchanging, fixed, stable, uniform|反义词：variable, changing, fluctuating, mutable|词性:形容词

invariant:/ɪnˈveəriənt/ (US) /ɪnˈvɛəriənt/ (UK)| n.|不变量|A quantity or property that remains unchanged under a specific set of transformations or conditions.|例句：These invariants are useful in classifying different types of geometries. 这些不变量在分类不同类型的几何中很有用。|近义词：constant, parameter|反义词：variable|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *invariant-, from in- (“not”) + variant- (“varying”), from Latin varians (“changing”).
