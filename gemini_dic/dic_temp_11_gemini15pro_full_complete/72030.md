flippant:/ˈflɪpənt/| adj.|轻率的，轻浮的，无礼的|Not showing a serious or respectful attitude. |例句：His flippant remarks about her appearance offended everyone. 他对她外貌的轻率评论冒犯了所有人。

例句：A flippant answer will not satisfy the teacher. 轻率的回答不会让老师满意。


近义词：frivolous, impertinent, disrespectful, cheeky, saucy|反义词：serious, respectful, reverent, earnest|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "flip," meaning "to flick or toss," suggesting a quick, careless movement or gesture, extended to speech and attitude.  Originally meant "glib," then acquired negative connotations of disrespect.
