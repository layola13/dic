nomography:/nɒˈmɒɡrəfi/
n.
列线图解法;诺谟图学
A graphical technique for representing the relationship between three or more variables by means of aligned scales or curves.
Nomography provides a quick and easy way to solve complex equations. 列线图解法提供了一种快速简便的方法来解决复杂的方程式。
The engineers used nomography to determine the optimal settings for the machine. 工程师们使用列线图来确定机器的最佳设置。
近义词: alignment chart, nomogram
反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, but one could consider "numerical calculation" a contrasting method)
词性: noun
