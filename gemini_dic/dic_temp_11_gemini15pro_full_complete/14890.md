ayrie:/ˈɛəri/| adj.|通风的，暴露在空气中的，高耸的|Situated high up; lofty; airy.|The ayrie castle stood on a cliff overlooking the sea.  高耸的城堡矗立在悬崖上，俯瞰大海。|近义词：airy, lofty, high, elevated|反义词:low, sheltered|词性:形容词

词源:  From Middle English *airie, from Old French airie (“nest of a bird of prey”), from Vulgar Latin *agraria, from Latin ager (“field”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂éǵros (“field”).  The sense shifted from "nest" to "high up" due to the location of such nests.
