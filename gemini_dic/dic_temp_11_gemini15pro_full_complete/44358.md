cross-channel:/ˌkrɒsˈtʃæn(ə)l/ /ˌkrɔːsˈtʃæn(ə)l/| adj.|跨渠道的，跨海峡的|Relating to or involving two or more different methods of distribution or communication, especially online and offline channels, or between countries separated by a channel of water. |The company implemented a cross-channel marketing strategy to reach a wider audience.  这家公司实施了跨渠道营销策略，以接触更广泛的受众。|Synonyms: multi-channel, omnichannel, integrated|Antonyms: single-channel, isolated|adjective

cross-channel:/ˌkrɒsˈtʃæn(ə)l/ /ˌkrɔːsˈtʃæn(ə)l/| adv.|跨渠道地，跨海峡地|In a way that relates to or involves two or more different methods of distribution or communication, especially online and offline channels, or between countries separated by a channel of water. |They market their products cross-channel to maximize sales. 他们跨渠道销售产品以实现销售最大化。|Synonyms: multi-channel, omnichannel, integrated|Antonyms: single-channel, isolated|adverb
