diluviate:/dɪˈluːviˌeɪt/| v. |下倾盆大雨，泛滥|to rain very heavily, to flood|例句：The rain diluviated all night, turning the streets into rivers. 雨整夜倾盆而下，把街道变成了河流。|近义词：pour, deluge, inundate|反义词:drizzle, sprinkle|词性:动词
Etymology: From Latin *diluviāre, from diluvium (“flood, deluge”), from diluere (“to wash away”), from di- (“apart, asunder”) + luere (“to wash”).
