macropinacoid: /ˌmækroʊˈpɪnəkɔɪd/ | n. | 大侧轴面 |  A crystal face parallel to the macro axis and the b crystallographic axis. It is one of the possible forms in the monoclinic, orthorhombic, and triclinic crystal systems. | The macropinacoid is well-developed in barite crystals.  重晶石晶体的大侧轴面发育良好。| Synonym:  lateral pinacoid | Antonym: N/A | noun
