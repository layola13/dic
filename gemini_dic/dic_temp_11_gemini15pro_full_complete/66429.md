exegetic:/ˌɛksɪˈdʒɛtɪk/
adj.
解释的，诠释的
Characterized by critical explanation or interpretation, especially of a text.
The professor's exegetic commentary on the poem helped the students understand its deeper meaning. 教授对这首诗的诠释性评论帮助学生理解了它更深层的含义。

exegetic:/ˌɛksɪˈdʒɛtɪk/
adj.
解释的，诠释的
Relating to or containing exegesis.
Her exegetic skills were highly valued in the academic community. 她在学术界备受推崇的解释能力。


近义词:interpretative, explanatory, expository, hermeneutic
反义词:unclear, ambiguous, obscure
词性:adjective


词源: From Late Latin exegeticus, from Greek exēgētikos "explanatory," from exēgeisthai "to interpret, explain," from ex- "out" + hēgeisthai "to lead, guide."
