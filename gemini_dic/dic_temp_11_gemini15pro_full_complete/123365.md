monomachy:/ˈmɒnəˌmæki/ (US) /ˈmɒnəˌmæki/ (UK)| n.|单挑，一对一决斗|A duel or fight between two people, especially one fought to the death.|The two knights engaged in a monomachy to settle their dispute. 两位骑士进行了一场决斗来解决他们的争端。|Example 2:  The monomachy between the two rival gangs ended in a bloody stalemate.  两个敌对帮派之间的决斗以血腥的僵局告终。|Synonym: duel, single combat|Antonym: group fight, battle|noun
