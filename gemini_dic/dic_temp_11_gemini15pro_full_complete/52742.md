dilacerate: /dɪˈlæsəreɪt/ | vt. | 撕裂；割碎；使…痛苦 | To tear apart or to criticize harshly and destructively. | The lawyer dilacerated the witness's testimony. 律师把证人的证词驳得体无完肤。

The news of her death dilacerated his heart. 她去世的消息使他悲痛欲绝。 | 近义词: tear, rip, rend, mangle, lacerate, excoriate | 反义词: mend, repair, heal, soothe | 词性: 动词

词源:  From Latin *dīlacerātus, past participle of dīlacerāre (“to tear apart”), from dī- (“apart, asunder”) + lacerāre (“to tear, rend”).
