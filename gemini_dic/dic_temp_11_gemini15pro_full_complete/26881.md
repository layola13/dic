cakewalked:/ˈkeɪkwɔːkt/ /ˈkeɪkwɑːkt/
| v. |轻松地完成；轻而易举地获胜|To achieve something very easily; to win without effort.|He cakewalked to victory in the election. 他轻而易举地赢得了选举。|近义词：breeze, coast, sail, romp, waltz|反义词：struggle, toil, labor|verb

cakewalk:/ˈkeɪkwɔːk/ /ˈkeɪkwɑːk/
| n. |轻松的事；容易的胜利|Something that is very easy to achieve; an easy victory.|The exam was a cakewalk. 考试非常容易。|近义词：breeze, cinch, pushover|反义词：challenge, ordeal|noun
