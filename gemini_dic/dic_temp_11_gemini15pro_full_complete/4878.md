alight /əˈlaɪt/ | vi. adj.|下来，下去；点燃的；燃烧着的|To descend and settle; to land; lighted up; burning.|The bird alighted on a branch. 鸟落在树枝上。|近义词：land, dismount, descend, ablaze, burning|反义词:ascend, take off, extinguish|词性:动词, 形容词


alight /əˈlaɪt/ | adj.|点燃的；燃烧着的|Lighted up; burning.|The bonfire was alight with flames. 篝火燃烧着熊熊火焰。|近义词：ablaze, burning, flaming|反义词:extinguished, unlit|词性:形容词


词源: From Middle English *alihten, from Old English *ālīhtan (unattested), from ā- + līhtan (“to alight, dismount”), from Proto-Germanic *līhtaną (“to alight, descend”).
