befall:/bɪˈfɔːl/| v.|降临，发生（通常指不好的事情）|Something terrible has befallen him.  他遭遇了可怕的事情。|近义词：happen to, occur to, come upon|反义词: avert, prevent|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *befeallan (unattested), from be- "by, about" + feallan "to fall."  The word literally means "to fall upon."
