intolerance:/ɪnˈtɒlərəns/ (UK) /ɪnˈtɑːlərəns/ (US)| n. |不宽容，偏狭|Lack of tolerance; unwillingness or inability to endure or respect different beliefs, opinions, or practices.|She showed intolerance towards people of different religions. 她对不同宗教信仰的人表现出不宽容。|近义词：bigotry, prejudice, narrow-mindedness, bias|反义词: tolerance, acceptance, open-mindedness|noun


intolerant:/ɪnˈtɒlərənt/ (UK) /ɪnˈtɑːlərənt/ (US)| adj. |不宽容的，偏狭的|Not tolerant of views, beliefs, or behaviour that differ from one's own.|He is intolerant of any criticism. 他不能容忍任何批评。|近义词：bigoted, prejudiced, narrow-minded, biased|反义词: tolerant, accepting, open-minded|adjective
