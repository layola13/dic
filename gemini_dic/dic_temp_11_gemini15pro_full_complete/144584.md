outwatered:/ˌaʊtˈwɔːtərd/
verb
胜过(在水上运动方面); 在用水方面超过
To surpass or defeat in a water-based activity or competition; to exceed in the use of water.
The smaller boat outwatered the larger one in the race.  小船在比赛中胜过了大船。
The farm outwatered its neighbors, drawing too much from the shared aquifer.  这个农场在用水方面超过了它的邻居，从共享的含水层中抽取了太多的水。
近义词: outperformed, surpassed, exceeded
反义词: lost to, defeated by
词性:v.
