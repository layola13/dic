outstunk:/ˌaʊtˈstʌŋk/
verb
发出比...更难闻的气味；比...更臭
surpassed someone or something in emitting a foul odor.
例句：The durian outstunk the garbage.  榴莲比垃圾还臭。

例句：His gym socks outstunk everyone else's in the locker room. 他的健身房袜子比更衣室里其他任何人的都臭。

近义词：out-smelled, overpowered
反义词：perfumed, fragranced
词性：动词(past tense of outstink)
