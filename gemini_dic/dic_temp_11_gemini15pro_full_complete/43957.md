crib:/krɪb/| n.|婴儿床；小儿床；食槽；马槽；剽窃；抄袭；译文；小抄；狭小的住处；简陋的小屋|A baby's bed with high sides made of slats.|例句：The baby slept soundly in its crib. 婴儿在婴儿床里睡得很香。|近义词：cot, cradle, bassinet, manger (for animals)|反义词: None|词性:noun

crib:/krɪb/| vt.|把…关在狭小的地方；限制；抄袭；剽窃|To copy or plagiarize.|例句：He cribbed his essay from the internet. 他从网上抄袭了他的文章。|近义词：copy, plagiarize, cheat|反义词:originate, create|词性:verb
