jehovist:/dʒɪˈhoʊvɪst/| n.|耶和华见证人信徒|A member of the religious group known as Jehovah's Witnesses.  This term is sometimes considered outdated or even offensive by some, and "Jehovah's Witness" is generally preferred.|例句：He is a devout Jehovist and attends meetings several times a week. 他是一位虔诚的耶和华见证人信徒，每周参加几次聚会。

例句：She was raised as a Jehovist. 她从小就是耶和华见证人。


近义词：Jehovah's Witness|反义词:None (It's inappropriate to list specific opposing religious groups as antonyms.)|词性:noun
