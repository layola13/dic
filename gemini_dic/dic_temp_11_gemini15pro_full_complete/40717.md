convertite:/ˈkɒnvɜːˌtaɪt/| n.|皈依者；改变信仰者|A person who has been converted to a particular religion or belief.|例句：He was a convertite to Buddhism. 他是一位皈依佛教的人。

例句：The convertite shared his story of finding faith.  这位皈依者分享了他找到信仰的故事。


近义词：convert, proselyte|反义词:apostate|词性:noun
