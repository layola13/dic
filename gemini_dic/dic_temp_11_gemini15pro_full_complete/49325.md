delight:/dɪˈlaɪt/| n. v.| 快乐，高兴；使高兴，使快乐| Delight is a feeling of great pleasure and satisfaction.  It is a delight to see you. 见到你很高兴。|例句：Her singing is a pure delight. 她的歌声是一种纯粹的享受。

To my delight, I won the competition. 令我高兴的是，我赢得了比赛。|近义词：pleasure, joy, gladness, happiness|反义词：displeasure, sorrow, distress|词性:名词; 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *deleiter, from Latin dēlectāre (“to allure, charm, delight”), frequentative of dēliciō (“to entice, allure”) ultimately from *dē-laciō (“to entice away”), from dē (“from, away”) + laciō (“I entice, lure”).
