curviform:/ˈkɜːrvɪfɔːrm/| adj.|曲线形的，弯曲的|Having a curved or sinuous form; resembling a curve.|例句：The artist specialized in curviform sculptures, each piece flowing with elegant lines.  这位艺术家擅长曲线形雕塑，每一件作品都流淌着优雅的线条。

例句：The bacteria exhibited a curviform shape under the microscope. 在显微镜下，这种细菌呈现出弯曲的形状。|近义词：curved, sinuous, bowed, bent|反义词：straight, linear|词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Latin *curvus* ("curved") + *-formis* ("-shaped").
