overdebilitate:/ˌoʊvər dəˈbɪlɪteɪt/ | v. |过度衰弱，极度虚弱|To weaken excessively; to enfeeble beyond what is normal or necessary. | The prolonged illness had overdebilitated him, leaving him unable to walk without assistance. 长期患病使他极度衰弱，以至于没有帮助就无法行走。|  The chemotherapy overdebilitated her, making even simple tasks exhausting. 化疗使她极度虚弱，即使是简单的任务也让她筋疲力尽。 | 近义词: weaken, enfeeble, exhaust, deplete | 反义词: strengthen, invigorate, energize | 词性: 动词
