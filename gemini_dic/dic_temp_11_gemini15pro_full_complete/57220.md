downey:/ˈdaʊni/| adj.|柔软的，绒毛状的；被绒毛覆盖的|Covered with soft, fine hairs or down; like down. |The peach has a downey skin. 桃子的表皮有绒毛。|Near synonyms: downy, fluffy, velvety|Near antonyms: smooth, coarse, rough|adjective


downey:/ˈdaʊni/| n.|柔软的东西，绒毛|Something soft and fluffy like down. (Less common usage)|The baby bird was covered in soft downey. 这只雏鸟全身覆盖着柔软的绒毛。|Near synonyms: fluff, down|Near antonyms: - |noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From down (n.) + -y (suffix forming adjectives).  The noun form is a back-formation from the adjective.  "Down" itself comes from Old Norse *dūnn.
