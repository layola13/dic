introception:/ˌɪntrəˈsɛpʃən/| n.|内感受，内部感觉|The ability to sense and perceive internal bodily states, such as hunger, thirst, and heartbeat.  对饥饿、口渴和心跳等内部身体状态进行感知和察觉的能力。|例句：Yoga can improve your introception.  瑜伽可以提高你的内感受能力。

例句：Developing introception can help manage anxiety.  发展内感受能力有助于管理焦虑。|近义词：visceroception, interoception|反义词: exteroception|词性:noun
