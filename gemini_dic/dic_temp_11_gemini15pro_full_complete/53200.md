diptych:/ˈdɪptɪk/ | n. | 双联画；双折画板 | A painting, especially an altarpiece, on two hinged wooden panels which may be closed like a book. | The museum exhibited a 15th-century diptych of the Virgin and Child.  博物馆展出了一幅15世纪的圣母子双联画。| synonym:  twofold, double-paneled | antonym: single, monoptych | noun

diptych:/ˈdɪptɪk/ | n. | (古代)双折蜡板; (古代)刻字木板 |  Two writing tablets hinged together. |  Roman officials used diptychs for notes and records. 罗马官员使用双折蜡板做笔记和记录。| synonym: tablet, panel | antonym:  N/A | noun
