mafias:/ˈmɑːfiəz/ | n. |黑手党，黑帮|A criminal organization originating in Sicily, characterized by its use of violence, intimidation, and corruption to control illegal activities.|The mafias have a long history of involvement in drug trafficking. 这些黑手党长期参与贩毒。|Synonyms: gangs, syndicates, mobs, organized crime|Antonyms: law enforcement, authorities|Noun
