noun

The act of digging up something that has been buried, especially a body.  This word is usually seen in its possessive form, referring to the consequences, effects, or other aspects related to a specific disinterment.
The disinterment's impact on the local community was significant. 掘尸对当地社区的影响很大。

ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (meaning "opposite of, not") + inter (meaning "to bury") + -ment (suffix forming nouns of action or process).  Ultimately from Latin *disinterrare < dis- + in- + terra ("earth").
