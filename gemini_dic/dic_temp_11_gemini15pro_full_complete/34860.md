clinoid: /ˈklaɪnɔɪd/| adj. n.|床突的，床突|Resembling a bedpost; specifically designating four bony processes of the sphenoid bone, two anterior and two posterior, which surround the pituitary fossa (also called the sella turcica).  类似床柱；特指蝶骨的四个骨突，两个前床突和两个后床突，它们围绕着垂体窝（也称为蝶鞍）。|The clinoid processes are important landmarks in neurosurgery.  床突是神经外科的重要标志。|Near synonyms:  sella turcica, pituitary fossa| Antonyms: None commonly used| adjective, noun
