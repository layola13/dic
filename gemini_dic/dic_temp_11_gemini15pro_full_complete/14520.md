avenue:/ˈævənjuː/ (US), /ˈævənjuː/ (UK)| n.|大街；途径；方法|A broad road in a town or city, typically having trees at regular intervals along its sides; a way of approaching a problem or making progress toward something.|例句：We strolled down the avenue. 我们沿着大街漫步。

例句：He explored every avenue to find a solution. 他探索了每一种方法来寻找解决方案。|近义词：street, road, boulevard, path, approach, route, channel|反义词: dead end, cul-de-sac|词性:名词
