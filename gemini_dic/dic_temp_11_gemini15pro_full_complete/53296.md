dirts:/dɜːrts/ | n. | 污垢，泥土 | Dirt in a non-liquid form, especially when it is on the ground, on clothes, or on your skin. |  The mechanic's hands were covered in dirts. 技工的手上沾满了污垢。| synonyms: grime, filth, soil | antonyms: cleanliness, purity | noun

dirts:/dɜːrts/ | v. | 弄脏 | To make something dirty.  This is a less common usage, usually referring to the act of making something dirty with loose earth or similar material. | Don't dirts your new shoes in the mud. 不要把你的新鞋弄脏在泥里. | synonyms: soil, stain, sully | antonyms: clean, cleanse, purify | verb
