bassly:/ˈbeɪsli/| adv.|低沉地；粗重地；粗俗地|In a deep, low-pitched manner; vulgarly or indecently.|He spoke bassly, his voice rumbling like thunder. 他低沉地说着，声音像雷一样隆隆作响。|近义词：deeply, huskily, coarsely|反义词：softly, gently, subtly|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From bass + -ly.  "Bass" comes from Middle French *bas, from Italian basso, from Late Latin bassus "low, short," perhaps from a Germanic source. "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs from adjectives.
