clunkish: /ˈklʌŋkɪʃ/ | adj. | 笨重的；笨拙的；粗陋的 | Awkward and heavy; ungainly.  Lacking grace or refinement in movement or appearance. | The robot's movements were slow and clunkish.  机器人的动作缓慢而笨拙。

The old car was rather clunkish and difficult to maneuver. 这辆旧车相当笨重，难以操控。 | near synonyms: awkward, clumsy, ungainly, lumbering, clunky | antonyms: graceful, elegant, agile, deft, nimble | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From clunk + -ish.  Clunk itself is imitative, echoing the sound of a heavy object falling or hitting something.
