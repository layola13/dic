meditate:/ˈmedɪteɪt/| v. | 冥想，沉思，考虑 |To think deeply and seriously about something; to focus one's mind for a period of time, in silence or with the aid of chanting, for religious or spiritual purposes or as a method of relaxation.| I meditated for an hour this morning. 我今天早上冥想了1个小时。|Synonyms: contemplate, ponder, reflect, muse|Antonyms:  rush, hurry, neglect, ignore|verb
