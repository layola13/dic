earthier:/ˈɜːrθiər/
adjective
更朴实的，更粗俗的，更泥土气息的
More down-to-earth, more coarse or unrefined, more suggestive of earth or soil.
The pottery had an earthier feel than the porcelain.  这个陶器比瓷器更有泥土气息。
She adopted an earthier style of dress. 她采用了一种更朴实的着装风格。
近义词:earthy, natural, rustic, simple, unrefined
反义词:refined, sophisticated, elegant, artificial
词性:adjective

词源:
From earth + -y + -er (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *erþō.
