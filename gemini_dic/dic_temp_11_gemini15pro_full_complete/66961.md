experimentalist:/ɪkˌspɛrɪˈmɛntəlɪst/| n.|实验主义者|A person who favors experimentation or an experimental approach.| The artist was an experimentalist, constantly trying new techniques.  这位艺术家是一位实验主义者，不断尝试新技术。|synonyms:innovator, pioneer, avant-garde|antonyms:traditionalist, conservative|noun
