dungfork:/ˈdʌŋfɔːrk/| n. |粪叉；堆肥叉|A tool with a long handle and two or more prongs used for lifting and turning over manure or compost.|He used a dungfork to spread the manure on the field. 他用粪叉把肥料撒到田里。|近义词：manure fork, pitchfork|反义词: (No direct antonym) |noun
