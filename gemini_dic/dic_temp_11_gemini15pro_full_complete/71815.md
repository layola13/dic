ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *flēos, from Proto-Germanic *fleusą (“fleece”). Cognate with Old Saxon flūs (“fleece”), Old Norse fló (“fleece, layer”), Dutch vlies (“fleece”), German Vlies (“fleece”).
