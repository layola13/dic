monument: /ˈmɒn.jʊ.mənt/ (US) /ˈmɑːn.jə.mənt/| n. | 纪念碑，纪念物；遗迹，不朽的业绩| something, especially a large structure, built to remind people of an important event or famous person |  The Washington Monument is an iconic obelisk honoring George Washington. 华盛顿纪念碑是一座标志性的方尖碑，用以纪念乔治·华盛顿。| synonym: memorial, tribute, testament | antonym: none | noun

monumental: /ˌmɒn.jʊˈmen.təl/ (US) /ˌmɑːn.jəˈmen.t̬əl/| adj. | 纪念性的；不朽的；非常的，巨大的| great in importance, extent, or size |  Rebuilding the city after the earthquake was a monumental task. 地震后重建这座城市是一项艰巨的任务。| synonym: immense, massive, colossal | antonym: insignificant, tiny, minuscule | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French monument, from Latin monumentum ‘something that reminds’, from monēre ‘to remind’.
