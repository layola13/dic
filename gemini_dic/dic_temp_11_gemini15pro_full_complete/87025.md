hepatica:/hɪˈpætɪkə/| n.|肝叶草；獐耳细辛|A small perennial herb of the buttercup family, having lobed leaves and delicate white, pink, or purplish flowers in early spring. It is native to woodland areas of the Northern Hemisphere.|The hepatica bloomed early this year in shades of lavender and pink. 今年春天，獐耳细辛开花很早，呈现出淡紫色和粉红色。|Synonyms: liverleaf, liverwort, anemone|Antonyms: N/A|noun
