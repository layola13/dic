debonairity:/ˌdebənˈɛərɪti/
n.
温文尔雅，潇洒
A sophisticated charm and polish in manner and appearance; urbane grace.
He exuded an air of debonairity that charmed everyone in the room. 他身上散发出的温文尔雅的气质迷住了房间里的每一个人。
近义词:suavity, urbanity, polish, charm, grace, elegance
反义词:awkwardness, clumsiness, gaucheness
词性:noun

Etymology:
From French *débonnaireté*, from *débonnaire* "courteous, affable," from *de bon aire* "of good bearing or nature."
