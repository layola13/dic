appraisal: /əˈpreɪzəl/ | n. | 评估，估价，评价 | An act of assessing something or someone. |  The appraisal of the house was higher than we expected.  这栋房子的估价比我们预期的要高。

The company holds regular performance appraisals for its employees. 公司定期对员工进行绩效评估。

近义词: assessment, evaluation, valuation, estimate, judgment | 反义词: criticism, condemnation | 词性: noun
