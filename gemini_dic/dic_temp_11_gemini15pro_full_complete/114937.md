manages:/ˈmænɪdʒɪz/
| v. |管理，经营；控制；设法做到|
|Third-person singular present tense form of "manage". It means to be in charge of, direct, or control something or someone, or to succeed in doing something difficult.|
|She manages a team of ten people. 她管理一个十人的团队.|
|directs, controls, handles, supervises, runs, administers, oversees, governs|
|fails, neglects, mismanages|
|verb|
