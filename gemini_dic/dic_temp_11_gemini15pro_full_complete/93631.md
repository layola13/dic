imbolden:/ɪmˈboʊldən/
verb
使大胆；使勇敢；鼓励
to make someone feel braver or more confident
例句：
The success of the first protest imboldened them to organize another.  第一次抗议的成功鼓舞了他们组织另一次抗议。

His coach's praise imboldened him to try out for the Olympics. 教练的赞扬使他更有勇气去参加奥运会的选拔。

近义词：encourage, hearten, inspire, embolden
反义词：discourage, dishearten, intimidate
词性：v.
