nontribesman:/ˌnɒnˈtraɪbz.mən/ /ˌnɑːnˈtraɪbz.mən/
noun
非部落成员；外族人
A person who does not belong to a particular tribe.
He was considered a nontribesman and was not allowed to participate in the sacred ceremonies. 他被认为是非部落成员，不允许参加神圣的仪式。
synonyms: outsider, foreigner, alien
antonyms: tribesman, native, insider
