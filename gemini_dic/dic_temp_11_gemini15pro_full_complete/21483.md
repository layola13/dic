blubbed:/blʌbd/| v. | 抽泣，哭诉 | To cry noisily and uncontrollably, often with the face buried in the hands.| The child blubbed loudly when he lost his toy.  这孩子丢了玩具后大声哭了起来。|  The little boy blubbed inconsolably after scraping his knee. 小男孩擦伤膝盖后哭得停不下来。 | 近义词: sobbed, wept, cried, bawled | 反义词: laughed, giggled | 词性: 动词

blub:/blʌb/| n. |  哭泣，呜咽 | A noisy, uncontrolled cry.| He let out a loud blub. 他大声哭了出来。 | She gave a little blub and then calmed down. 她小声哭了一下，然后平静下来。 | 近义词: sob, weep, cry | 反义词: laugh, giggle | 词性: 名词


词源:  Likely imitative of the sound of crying.  可能是模仿哭泣的声音。
