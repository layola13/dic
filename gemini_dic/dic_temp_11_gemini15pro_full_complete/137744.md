nonvaporous:/ˌnɒnˈveɪpərəs/
adjective
非蒸发的; 非挥发的; 不易挥发的
Not readily vaporized; not existing as or readily forming a vapor.
The nonvaporous liquid was difficult to ignite.  这种不易挥发的液体很难点燃。
The substance is nonvaporous at room temperature.  该物质在室温下不易挥发。
近义词:non-volatile, involatile
反义词:vaporous, volatile
词性:adjective

Etymology:
non- (prefix): not
vaporous (adjective):  full of, producing, or existing as vapor
