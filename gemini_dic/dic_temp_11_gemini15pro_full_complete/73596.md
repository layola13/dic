forethought:/ˈfɔːrθɔːt/ (US) /ˈfɔːθɔːt/ (UK)| n. |深谋远虑；预见；预先的考虑| careful consideration of what will be necessary or may happen in the future|例句：She showed great forethought in making a backup copy of her work. 她很有先见之明地备份了她的工作。

例句：His lack of forethought led to disastrous consequences. 他缺乏远见导致了灾难性的后果。

近义词：premeditation, planning, anticipation, foresight|反义词：improvidence, shortsightedness|词性:名词
