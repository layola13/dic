nihil:/ˈnaɪhɪl/ /ˈniːhɪl/| n.|无；虚无；零|Nothing; nothingness.  It represents the concept of nothingness or non-existence.|例句：He believes in nihilism, the philosophy of nihil. 他信仰虚无主义，即虚无的哲学。|例句：The search for meaning in a world some perceive as nihil can be daunting. 在一些人看来是虚无的世界中寻找意义可能令人畏惧。|近义词：nothingness, void, nullity|反义词:everything, existence|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Latin *nihil*, "nothing," contraction of *nihilum*, neuter of *nihilus*, "no, none."  Related to *ne* "not" and *hilum* "a trifle, small bit, the black spot on a bean."
