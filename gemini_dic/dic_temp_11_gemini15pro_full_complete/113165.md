lupus: /ˈluːpəs/ | n. |狼疮|A chronic autoimmune disease that can damage any part of the body (skin, joints, and/or organs inside the body). | She was diagnosed with lupus at a young age. 她年纪轻轻就被诊断出患有狼疮。|  Lupus can cause a wide range of symptoms, making it difficult to diagnose. 狼疮会导致各种各样的症状，使其难以诊断。 | 近义词: None (Lupus is a specific disease) | 反义词: None (Lupus is a disease, it doesn't have an opposite) | 词性: noun


Etymology:
From Latin lupus (“wolf”).  The name may derive from the characteristic facial rash, which can resemble a wolf's bite.
