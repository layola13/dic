effluviate:/ɪˈfluːvieɪt/
verb
散发; 流出; 放射
to give off or flow out as an odor, vapor, light, etc.
The rotten meat effluviated a foul smell. 腐烂的肉散发出一股恶臭。
emanate, exude, radiate
absorb, contain
v.
