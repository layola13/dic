interfretted:/ˌɪntərˈfrɛtɪd/ | adj. |互相交错的，互相缠绕的 |  Closely interwoven or interconnected, like the frets of a musical instrument.  Derived from "inter-" (between) + "fret" (a decorative pattern of intersecting lines). | The artist created an interfretted design of vines and leaves. 艺术家创作了一个藤蔓和树叶互相交错的设计。 |The complex and interfretted roots of the ancient tree held the soil firmly in place. 古老树木复杂交错的根系将土壤牢牢地固定住。|  interlaced, intertwined, interwoven | separate, detached | adjective
