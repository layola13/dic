areometric:/ˌɛəriəˈmɛtrɪk/
adjective
比重计的; 液体比重测定的
relating to or determined by means of an areometer.
The areometric method is used to determine the specific gravity of liquids. 比重计法用于测定液体的比重。
The scientist used an areometric device to measure the density of the solution.  科学家使用比重计装置测量溶液的密度。
近义词: hydrometer, densimetric
反义词:  None (No direct antonym exists for this technical term)
词性: adjective


词源:
From French *aréométrique*, from *aréomètre* (“hydrometer”) + *-ique* (English *-ic*). *aréomètre* from Ancient Greek ἀραιός (*araiós*, “thin, rare”) + μέτρον (*métron*, “measure”).
