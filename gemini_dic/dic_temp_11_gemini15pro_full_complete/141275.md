opaquing:/oʊˈpeɪkɪŋ/ | v. | 使不透明；遮盖 |  The artist is opaquing the background of the painting. 艺术家正在使画的背景不透明。|  例句：She's opaquing the glass with paint. 她正在用颜料使玻璃不透明。| 近义词: obscuring, covering, clouding | 反义词: clearing, revealing, transparenting | verb

opaquing:/oʊˈpeɪkɪŋ/ | n. | 使不透明；遮盖（的动作或过程） |  The opaquing of the window provided privacy.  窗户的遮盖提供了隐私。| 例句：The opaquing of the varnish gave the wood a rich finish.  清漆的遮盖赋予了木材丰富的质感。| 近义词: obscuration, covering, clouding | 反义词: clearing, revealing, transparency | noun

词源:  Derived from the adjective "opaque" (不透明的), which comes from the Latin "opacus" (shaded, dark).  源自形容词 "opaque"（不透明的），该词来源于拉丁语 "opacus"（阴影，黑暗）。
