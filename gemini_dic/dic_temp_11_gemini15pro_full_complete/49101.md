deign:/deɪn/
verb
屈尊，俯就
to do something that one considers to be beneath one's dignity; condescend.
The king did not deign to address the commoners. 国王不屑于向平民讲话。

He deigned to answer a few questions. 他屈尊回答了几个问题。

近义词:condescend, stoop, lower oneself
反义词:refuse, decline, snub
词性:v.
