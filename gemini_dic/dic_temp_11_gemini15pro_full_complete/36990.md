columbate:/ˈkɒləmbeɪt/ /ˈkoʊləmbeɪt/| n.|钶酸盐|A salt containing the negative ion CbO3^−2 derived from columbic acid.  A salt or ester of columbic acid.|The columbate was used as a catalyst.|近义词:niobate (sometimes)|反义词:None|noun

columbate:/ˈkɒləmbeɪt/ /ˈkoʊləmbeɪt/| adj.|含钶的|Containing columbium.|The columbate mineral was identified through spectroscopy.|近义词:niobic (sometimes, as columbium is an older name for niobium)|反义词:None|adjective
