obstructer:/əbˈstrʌktər/
n.
阻碍者，妨碍者
Someone or something that obstructs.
The fallen tree was an obstructer to traffic.  倒下的树阻碍了交通。
近义词:blocker, hinderer, impediment
反义词:facilitator, helper, aid
词性:名词

obstruct:/əbˈstrʌkt/
vt.
阻碍；阻塞；妨碍
To block or close up with an obstacle; to impede, hinder, or prevent.
The new building obstructs our view of the mountains. 新建筑挡住了我们眺望山的视线。
近义词:block, hinder, impede
反义词:aid, assist, help
词性:动词


词源:
From Late Latin *obstructor, from Latin obstructus (past participle of obstruere “to build in front of, block up, obstruct”), from ob- “against, in the way” + struere “to build, pile up.”
