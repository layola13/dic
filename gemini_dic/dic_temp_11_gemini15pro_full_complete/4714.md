alevin: /ˈæləvɪn/ | n. | 鱼苗，幼鱼 | A newly hatched fish, especially a salmon or trout, that still has its yolk sac attached. |  例句：The alevins were still clustered near the spawning redd.  鱼苗仍然聚集在产卵场附近。

例句：The biologist carefully counted the alevins in the sample. 生物学家仔细地数了样本中的鱼苗数量。| 近义词：fry, fingerling | 反义词: adult fish | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *alevin*, diminutive of *alevant* "a chick hatching from the egg," present participle of *alever* "to hatch," from Old French *alever*. Ultimately from Latin *elevare* "to lift up, raise."
