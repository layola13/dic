batten:/ˈbætən/ | v. |加固，封严;  (尤指动物) 饱餐，大吃特吃 |To strengthen or secure something using battens (long, flat strips of wood or metal). Also, to feast or eat heartily.|The ship's hatches were battened down before the storm.  船舱口在暴风雨来临前用压条封好了。|近义词: secure, fasten, reinforce, fortify, indulge, feast |反义词: loosen, weaken, starve |词性: 动词

batten:/ˈbætən/ | n. |压条，板条 |A long, flat strip of wood or metal used to strengthen or secure something.|The builder used battens to secure the roof tiles.  建筑工人用板条固定屋顶瓦片。|近义词: strip, plank, lath |反义词:  (No direct antonym) |词性: 名词
