chlorinator:/ˈklɔːrɪneɪtər/ | n. | 氯化器，加氯装置 | A device used to add chlorine to something, typically water, for the purposes of disinfection or sanitation. | The chlorinator automatically adds chlorine to the pool to keep it clean.  泳池的氯化器会自动加氯以保持清洁。| synonyms: chlorination system, chlorine dispenser | antonyms: dechlorinator | noun

chlorinator:/ˈklɔːrɪneɪtər/ | n. | 加氯的人 |  A person who adds chlorine to something. | The pool chlorinator checks the chemical levels daily. 泳池加氯员每天检查化学水平。 | synonyms: chlorine technician, pool operator (in some contexts) | antonyms: N/A | noun
