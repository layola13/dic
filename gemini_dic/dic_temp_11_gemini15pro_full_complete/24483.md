brittleness's:/ˈbrɪtlnəsɪz/
|n.|脆弱性，易碎性|The brittleness's of the material caused it to crack under pressure.  这种材料的脆弱性导致它在压力下破裂。|This glass's brittleness's makes it unsuitable for children's toys.  这种玻璃的易碎性使它不适合做儿童玩具。|近义词: fragility, frangibility|反义词: toughness, resilience, flexibility|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *brittilnesse, from Old English *bryttelnes, from Proto-Germanic *bruttilaz.  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰrey- (“to rub, break”).
