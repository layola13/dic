over-react:/ˌoʊvərriˈækt/| vi.|反应过度，过激反应|To respond more emotionally or intensely than is warranted by the situation.|She overreacted to his joke and started crying. 她对他的玩笑反应过度，开始哭了起来。|He tends to overreact to criticism. 他容易对批评反应过度。|近义词：overdo, exaggerate, respond excessively|反义词: under-react, downplay|verb
