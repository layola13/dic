nonagrarians:/ˌnɒnəˈɡrɛəriənz/
|n.|非农人口，非农业人口|People who do not engage in agriculture or farming as their primary occupation.  Those who live in urban areas and work in industries other than farming.| The nonagrarians make up the vast majority of the population in developed countries.  非农人口在发达国家占人口的绝大多数。|近义词: city dwellers, urbanites|反义词: agrarians, farmers|词性: 名词

nonagrarian:/ˌnɒnəˈɡrɛəriən/
|adj.|非农业的，非农的|Relating to or characteristic of people who do not engage in agriculture or farming.| The government is trying to encourage nonagrarian industries. 政府正在努力鼓励非农业产业。|近义词: nonagricultural, industrial, urban|反义词: agrarian, agricultural, rural|词性: 形容词
