hyperaltruism:/ˌhaɪpərˈæltruɪzəm/
n.
极度利他主义，超级利他主义
Excessive or extreme altruism, often at a cost to oneself.
He exhibited hyperaltruism by donating a kidney to a stranger. 他向一个陌生人捐献了一个肾脏，表现出极度利他主义。

近义词:extreme altruism, selflessness, abnegation
反义词:selfishness, egoism, self-interest
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- (meaning "excessive" or "beyond") + altruism (from French altruisme, coined by Auguste Comte from autrui "other people").
