ephemeralnesses:/ɪˈfɛmərəlnəsɪz/
n.
短暂性；转瞬即逝
The quality or state of being ephemeral; transience.
The ephemeralnesses of youth are often lamented in poetry. 青春的短暂常常在诗歌中被哀叹。
Synonyms: transience, brevity, fleetingness, impermanence
Antonyms: permanence, eternity, everlastingness
词性: 名词

ephemeral:/ɪˈfɛmərəl/
adj.
短暂的；朝生暮死的
Lasting for a very short time.
Mayflies are ephemeral creatures, living only for a day.蜉蝣是朝生暮死的生物，只活一天。
Synonyms: transient, fleeting, short-lived, momentary
Antonyms: permanent, enduring, lasting, perpetual
词性: 形容词
