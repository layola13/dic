beastings:/ˈbiːstɪŋz/
n.
初乳，产后最初分泌的乳汁
The thick, creamy milk produced by a mammal in the first few days after giving birth.
The farmer collected the beastings from the cow to feed to the calf.  农民收集了母牛的初乳来喂小牛。

近义词:colostrum, first milk
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "mature milk")
词性:noun
