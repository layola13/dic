immuring:/ɪˈmjʊərɪŋ/| v. |监禁，禁闭，把…嵌在墙里|The act of enclosing or confining someone within walls, often by building them up around the person.|The king immured his enemies in the dungeon. 国王把他的敌人囚禁在地牢里。|近义词： imprison, confine, incarcerate|反义词: liberate, free|词性:动词

词源: From Late Latin immurare "to wall in," from in- "in" + murus "wall."
