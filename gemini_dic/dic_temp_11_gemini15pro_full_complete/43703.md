creditor:/ˈkrɛdɪtər/| n.|债权人；贷方|A creditor is a person or company to whom money is owed.|例句：The company's creditors are demanding payment. 这家公司的债权人要求付款。

例句：She is a creditor of the bank. 她是银行的债权人。

近义词：lender, debtee|反义词: debtor|词性:noun
