encapsulate:/ɪnˈkæpsjʊleɪt/ | vt. |  封装，概括 | Express the essential facts or feeling of (something) succinctly. | The poem encapsulates the essence of romantic love. 这首诗概括了浪漫爱情的精髓。 | summarize, condense, epitomize, sum up | expand, elaborate, detail | verb
