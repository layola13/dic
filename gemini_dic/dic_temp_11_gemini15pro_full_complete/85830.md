hazmat:/ˈhæzmæt/| n.|危险物品；危险材料|Hazardous materials; substances (such as flammable or poisonous chemicals) that would be dangerous to life or the environment if released without precautions.|The hazmat team was called in to deal with the chemical spill. 危险品处理小组被召来处理化学品泄漏。|Synonyms: dangerous goods, hazardous waste, toxic materials|Antonyms: safe materials, harmless substances|noun
