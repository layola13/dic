mercenariness:/ˌmɜːrsənˈɛrɪnəs/
n.
唯利是图,贪财
The state of being primarily concerned with making money at the expense of ethics.  Focusing on personal gain, especially financial gain, regardless of moral principles.
His mercenariness was evident in his willingness to switch sides for a higher salary.  他的唯利是图表现在他为了更高的薪水而愿意改变立场。
greed, avarice, cupidity, venality
generosity, altruism, selflessness
