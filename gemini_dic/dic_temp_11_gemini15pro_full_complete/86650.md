helleborein:/ˌhɛlɪˈbɔːriɪn/
noun
嚏根草素；嚏根草苷
A poisonous glycoside obtained from the Christmas rose (Helleborus niger) and other species of hellebore.  It acts similarly to digitalis, affecting the heart.
Helleborein can be a dangerous toxin if ingested. 嚏根草素如果摄入体内可能是一种危险的毒素。
Helleborein is used in some traditional medicines. 嚏根草素用于一些传统药物中。
Synonyms: hellebrin, cardiac glycoside
Antonyms: antidote (in a broad sense)
词性: noun
