intrinsical:/ɪnˈtrɪnzɪk(ə)l/
adjective
内在的，固有的
Belonging naturally; essential.
例句：The intrinsic value of a gold coin lies in its gold content. 金币的内在价值在于其黄金含量。

例句：Creativity is intrinsic to human nature.  创造力是人类本性固有的。

近义词：inherent, innate, essential, inborn, built-in
反义词：extrinsic, external, acquired
词性：adjective
