lawman's:/ˈlɔːmənz/
noun
执法者的（所有格）
belonging to or associated with a lawman (a sheriff, marshal, or other law enforcement officer).
The lawman's badge gleamed in the sunlight.  执法者的徽章在阳光下闪闪发光。
The lawman's quick draw stopped the outlaw. 执法者的快速拔枪阻止了歹徒。
Synonyms: sheriff's, marshal's, officer's, policeman's, policewoman's
Antonyms: outlaw's, criminal's
Possessive noun
