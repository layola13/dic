draftiness's:/ˈdræftinəsɪz/
noun
通风性；穿堂风；透风
the state or quality of being drafty; a current of air in an enclosed space.
The draftiness's of the old house made it difficult to heat. 这栋老房子的通风性使得它很难被加热。
The draftiness's of the cabin made us shiver. 小屋的穿堂风让我们冷得发抖。
Synonym: airiness, ventilation, chilliness
Antonym: airtightness, warmth, stuffiness
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From draftiness + -’s (possessive suffix).  Draftiness comes from drafty + -ness.  Drafty comes from draft + -y. Draft ultimately traces back to the Old Norse word "draga" meaning "to draw" or "to pull."
