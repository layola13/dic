altruistically: /ˌæltrʊˈɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 利他地；无私地 | In an altruistic manner; with selfless concern for the well-being of others. |  She altruistically donated her kidney to a stranger. 她无私地把自己的肾脏捐献给了一个陌生人。|  He altruistically volunteered his time to help the homeless. 他无私地贡献自己的时间去帮助无家可归的人。 | synonym: selflessly, benevolently, charitably | antonym: selfishly, egoistically | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From French *altruistique*, from Italian *altrui* "of others, to others," from Latin *alteri*, dative singular of *alter* "other."  The English word was coined in the 19th century by philosopher Auguste Comte.
