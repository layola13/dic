obligedness:/əˈblaɪdʒɪdnəs/| n.|义务；责任；感激|The state of being obliged or indebted to someone or something; a feeling of gratitude or obligation.|I felt a deep sense of obligedness to him for his help. 我对他的帮助深感感激。|近义词：obligation, indebtedness, gratitude, thankfulness|反义词：ungratefulness, ingratitude|词性:名词
obligedness:|词源：From oblige + -ness.  Oblige comes from the Old French obliger, from Latin obligare "to bind, obligate," from ob- "toward" + ligare "to bind."|
