disembroil:/ˌdɪsɪnˈbrɔɪl/ | vt. | 解开；理清；使摆脱困境 | To free from entanglement or confusion; to extricate. | The mediator worked to disembroil the two warring factions. 调解员努力使这两个交战派别脱离冲突。

The lawyer disembroiled her client from the complicated lawsuit. 律师使她的客户从复杂的诉讼中脱身。 | 近义词: extricate, disentangle, unravel | 反义词: entangle, complicate, confuse | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *desbrouiller, from des- (expressing removal) + brouiller ("to mix up, confuse"). Ultimately from Frankish *brogōn (“to confuse”)
