imbarn:/ɪmˈbɑːrn/| vt.|储存(尤指谷物);监禁;禁闭|To store in or as if in a barn, especially to store grain. To enclose or confine.|The farmer imbarned the harvested wheat.  农民把收获的小麦储存了起来。|近义词：store, garner, hoard|反义词:scatter, disperse|verb

imbarn:/ɪmˈbɑːrn/| n.|谷仓|A barn.|The old imbarn housed generations of harvested crops.  古老的谷仓存放了几代人收获的庄稼。|近义词：barn, granary, storehouse|反义词: N/A|noun
