antisensitivities:/ˌæntiˌsɛnsɪˈtɪvɪtiz/| n. |脱敏性，抗敏性，降低敏感性|The process of reducing or eliminating sensitivities, especially allergies or adverse reactions.  It often refers to a decreased responsiveness to a substance (like a drug or allergen) after repeated exposure. | After years of allergy shots, she developed antisensitivities to pollen.  多年注射抗过敏药后，她对花粉产生了抗敏性。|Near synonyms: tolerance, resistance, desensitization,  immunity|Antonyms: sensitivity, hypersensitivity, allergy, intolerance|Noun
