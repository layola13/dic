hermaphroditic:/hɜːrˌmæfrəˈdɪtɪk/ | adj. | 雌雄同体的；两性的 | Having both male and female reproductive organs. |  Earthworms are hermaphroditic. 蚯蚓是雌雄同体的。|  The flower is hermaphroditic, containing both stamens and pistils.  这朵花是雌雄同体的，既有雄蕊也有雌蕊。 | bisexual, androgynous, monoecious | dioecious | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *hermaphroditicus, from Greek hermaphroditos, from Hermaphroditos (son of Hermes and Aphrodite, united in one body with the nymph Salmacis).
