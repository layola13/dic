malnutritions:/ˌmælnuˈtrɪʃənz/ | n. |营养不良（复数）| a state of poor nutrition; inadequate or unbalanced nutrition | Many children in developing countries suffer from malnutritions. 许多发展中国家的儿童遭受营养不良。| Malnutritions can lead to serious health problems. 营养不良会导致严重的健康问题。| 近义词: undernourishment, malnutrition (singular) | 反义词: nourishment, healthy nutrition | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From mal- (bad, ill) + nutrition.  "Mal-" comes from the Latin "malus" meaning "bad". "Nutrition" comes from the Latin "nutritio" meaning "nourishment".
