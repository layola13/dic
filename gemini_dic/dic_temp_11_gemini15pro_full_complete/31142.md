chancing:/ˈtʃɑːnsɪŋ/ /ˈtʃænsɪŋ/ | v. | 冒险；碰运气；试图 | Taking a risk, especially when the odds of success are low.  Attempting something with the hope of success, but without certainty. |  He was chancing his luck by driving without a license. 他无证驾驶，真是冒险。| synonyms: risking, gambling, venturing, daring | antonyms:  playing it safe, avoiding risk, hedging | verb

chancing:/ˈtʃɑːnsɪŋ/ /ˈtʃænsɪŋ/ | adj. | 偶然的；碰巧的 (Less common usage) | Happening by chance; fortuitous.  | A chancing encounter led to a lifelong friendship. 一次偶然的相遇成就了一段终生的友谊。 | synonyms: accidental, fortuitous, serendipitous | antonyms: planned, deliberate, intentional | adjective
