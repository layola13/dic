convenably:/kənˈviːniəbli/
adverb|适当地，方便地|In a way that is suitable for a particular purpose or situation; conveniently.|The meeting was convenably scheduled for a time when everyone could attend. 会议安排在一个大家都能参加的合适时间。|suitably, appropriately, handily|inconveniently, inappropriately, unsuitably|词性: 副词

词源:
From Late Middle English *convenable*, from Old French *convenable*, from Latin *conveniābilis* (“fitting, suitable”), from *convenīre* (“to come together, agree, befit”), from *con-* (“together”) + *venīre* (“to come”).
