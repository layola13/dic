conflicts:/ˈkɒnflɪkts/ (British) /ˈkɑːnflɪkts/ (American)| n. v.|冲突，矛盾，抵触|A serious disagreement or argument, typically one that is prolonged or violent.  A state of disharmony between incompatible or opposing persons, ideas, or interests; a clash.|The two countries have been in conflict for decades. 这两个国家几十年来一直处于冲突状态。|synonyms: clashes, disagreements, disputes, frictions, quarrels, wars|antonyms: agreements, harmonies, accords, compromises|noun
