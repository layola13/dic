commiserate:/kəˈmɪzəreɪt/
verb
表示同情； commiserate with sb on/over/about sth
To express or feel sympathy or pity; condole.
She commiserated with the losers. 她对失败者表示同情。
近义词:sympathize, condole, empathize
反义词:ignore, disregard
词性:v.

commiseration:/kəˌmɪzəˈreɪʃn/
noun
同情；慰问
sympathy and sorrow for the misfortunes of others; compassion.
She expressed her commiseration at his misfortune. 她对他的不幸表示慰问。
近义词:sympathy, condolence, empathy
反义词:indifference, apathy
词性:n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "commiserari,"  from "com-" (together) + "miserari" (to pity), from "miser" (wretched, unhappy).
