craw:/krɔː/
noun
鸟类的嗉囊;胃
the crop of a bird
例句：The bird stored the seeds in its craw. 这只鸟把种子储存在嗉囊里。
例句：The craw of the pigeon was full. 鸽子的嗉囊满了。
近义词：crop, gizzard
反义词: None
词性:n.


Etymology: From Middle English *crawe, from Old Norse *krá.  Cognate with Danish *krase*, Swedish *kräva*.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *krōaną* (“crow”), from the bird's use of this organ.
