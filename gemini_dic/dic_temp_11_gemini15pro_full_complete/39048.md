conform:/kənˈfɔːrm/ (US) /kənˈfɔːm/ (UK)| v. | 遵守，遵从；符合；顺应 | to behave according to the usual standards of behaviour that are expected by a group or society |  He refused to conform to the group's expectations. 他拒绝遵从该群体的期望。 | comply with, abide by, adhere to, obey | defy, disobey, rebel against | verb

conform:/kənˈfɔːrm/ (US) /kənˈfɔːm/ (UK)| adj. | 一致的，顺应的 | corresponding in form or character; similar | All products must conform to industry standards. 所有产品都必须符合行业标准。 | similar, consistent, compliant | nonconformist, different, dissimilar | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *conformare, from Latin com- + formare "to form," from forma "form, shape."
