bossage:/ˈbɒsɪdʒ/ (UK), /ˈbɑːsɪdʒ/ (US)| n.|凸饰; 粗面石工；表面粗糙|A projecting part of the surface of stone or brickwork, left rough or worked with a rough tool.  The condition of being bossaged.  A rusticated surface.|The bossage on the facade of the building added a touch of rugged charm.  建筑物正面上的粗面石工增添了一丝粗犷的魅力。|近义词：rustication, projection, embossment|反义词: smoothness, flatness|词性:noun
