damper:/ˈdæmpər (US), ˈdæmpə(r) (UK)| n.| 阻尼器；减震器；抑制因素；（烤箱的）挡板；（钢琴的）制音器；闷棍|A damper is a device that reduces vibrations or oscillations, or something that lessens activity or enthusiasm.|The car's dampers need to be replaced. 这辆车的减震器需要更换。|synonyms: absorber, cushion, muffler, suppressor|antonyms: accelerator, amplifier, stimulant|noun

damper:/ˈdæmpər (US), ˈdæmpə(r) (UK)| n.| （情绪的）抑制因素，煞风景的事|Something that has a depressing or subduing effect.|The bad news put a damper on the party. 坏消息给派对泼了冷水。|synonyms: discouragement, deterrent, killjoy|antonyms: encouragement, incentive, stimulus|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From damp (adjective) + -er (suffix forming nouns denoting agents or instruments). Ultimately from the Proto-Germanic *dampaz.
