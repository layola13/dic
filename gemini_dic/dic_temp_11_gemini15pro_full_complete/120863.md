misbelieve:/ˌmɪsbɪˈliːv/
verb
不相信；怀疑
To not believe; to have doubts about the truth or validity of something.
He misbelieved the reports of the economic downturn. 他不相信关于经济衰退的报道。
近义词: doubt, distrust, disbelieve
反义词: believe, trust, accept
词性: verb
