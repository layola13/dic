overasserts:/ˌoʊvərəˈsɜːrts/
| v. | 过分自信地断言；过分强调；| To state or assert something too forcefully or confidently, often to the point of being aggressive or inappropriate. |  He overasserts his opinions in meetings, often interrupting others. 他在会议上过分强调自己的观点，经常打断别人。| Synonyms: overemphasize, overstate, exaggerate | Antonyms: understate, downplay | verb
