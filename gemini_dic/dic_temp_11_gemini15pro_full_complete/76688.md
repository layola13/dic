galvanopuncture:/ˌɡælvənoʊˈpʌŋktʃər/ | n. | 电针疗法；电针刺法 | The application of a small electric current to acupuncture needles to enhance their therapeutic effect. |  Galvanopuncture is a traditional Chinese medicine technique. 电针疗法是一种中医技术。| synonym: electroacupuncture | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps manual acupuncture) | noun
