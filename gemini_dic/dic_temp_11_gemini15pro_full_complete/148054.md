oversacrificial: /ˌoʊvərˌsækrɪˈfɪʃəl/
adjective
过度牺牲的，牺牲过度的
Characterized by or marked by excessive sacrifice.  Making sacrifices that are too great or beyond what is reasonable or necessary.
She was oversacrificial in her devotion to her children, neglecting her own needs entirely. 她为了孩子过度牺牲，完全忽略了自己的需求。
His oversacrificial nature led him to constantly put others before himself, even to his own detriment. 他过度牺牲的天性使他总是把别人放在自己前面，甚至损害了自己的利益。

近义词: self-sacrificing, altruistic, selfless
反义词: selfish, egotistical, self-centered
词性: adjective


词源:
over- (prefix): excessive, too much
sacrificial (adjective): relating to or involving sacrifice
