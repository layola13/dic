fortieth:/ˈfɔːrtiɪθ/ (US), /ˈfɔːtiəθ/ (UK)| adj. n.|第四十, 第四十个|Representing the number 40 in a sequence; being the ordinal number of forty. |This is my fortieth birthday. 这是我的四十岁生日。|She came in fortieth place in the marathon. 她在马拉松比赛中获得了第四十名。|近义词:None (for ordinal numbers)|反义词:None (for ordinal numbers)|词性:adjective, noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *fēowertiġoða, from fēowertig (forty) + -oða (ordinal suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *feðwortigôþa.
