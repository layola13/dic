leucon:/ˈljuːkɒn/ | n. |亮骨海绵；白骨海绵|A type of sponge with a complex canal system.  A sponge skeleton made up entirely of leuconoid-type canals.|The leucon is the most complex and efficient sponge type.  这种海绵是最复杂和最高效的种类。|Synonyms:  leuconoid sponge | Antonyms: ascon, sycon | noun
