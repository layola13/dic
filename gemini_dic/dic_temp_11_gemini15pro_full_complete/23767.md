bravura:/brəˈvʊərə/ /brəˈvjʊrə/
noun
华丽演奏；精湛技艺
great technical skill and brilliance shown in a performance or activity.
The pianist’s bravura performance of Liszt’s Hungarian Rhapsody thrilled the audience.  钢琴家对李斯特匈牙利狂想曲的精彩演绎使观众激动不已。
近义词: brilliance, virtuosity, mastery, flair, panache
反义词: mediocrity, incompetence, clumsiness
词性: n.

bravura:/brəˈvʊərə/ /brəˈvjʊrə/
adjective
华丽的；精湛的
performed with great technical skill and brilliance.
Her bravura aria was the highlight of the opera. 她那华丽的咏叹调是整部歌剧的亮点。
近义词: brilliant, virtuoso, masterly, dazzling, stunning
反义词: mediocre, incompetent, clumsy
词性: adj.


词源:
From Italian bravura "skill, excellence," literally "bravery, valor," from bravo "brave, wild," from Latin barbarus "foreign, barbarous" (see barbarous). The musical sense of "florid brilliant passage" is from 1772.
