outcarol:/ˌaʊtˈkærəl/| v. | 比...唱得更好，胜过...的歌声 | to sing carols better or more loudly than someone else |  The choir boys outcaroled the congregation.  唱诗班男孩的歌声胜过会众。 | surpass in carolling, outsing | - | verb
词源:  out- (prefix meaning "exceeding, surpassing") + carol (v. to sing carols)
