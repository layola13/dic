optionally: /ˈɑːpʃənəli/ | adv. | 可选择地，随意地 |  Means something can be done or included if desired, but it's not required.  It implies choice or flexibility. | You can optionally add milk to your coffee. 你可以随意在咖啡里加牛奶。 | Alternatively, optionally | necessarily, obligatorily | adverb

Etymology: From the Late Latin word *optionalis*,  derived from *optio* meaning "choice" or "wish."
