fashionista:/ˌfæʃəˈnɪstə/
n.
时尚达人，时尚迷
A person who is highly interested in fashion; a follower of fashion, especially a designer, model, or other prominent person.
She's a real fashionista, always dressed in the latest trends. 她是个真正的时尚达人，总是穿着最新的潮流服饰。
近义词：trendsetter, style icon
反义词：None
