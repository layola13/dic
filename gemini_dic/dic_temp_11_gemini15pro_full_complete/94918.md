in-jokes:/ˈɪnˌdʒoʊks/| n.| 内部笑话，圈内笑话 |A joke that is only understood by a particular group of people, often because it relates to a shared experience or inside knowledge.| "They were laughing about some in-joke I didn't understand." 他们正在笑一些我不懂的内部笑话。|近义词：private joke, inside joke|反义词: public joke, general joke | noun
