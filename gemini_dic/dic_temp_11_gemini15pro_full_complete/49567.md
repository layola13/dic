dementate:/dɪˈmɛnteɪt/ | v. | 使精神错乱；使痴呆 | To deprive of reason or understanding; make insane or demented. |  The disease slowly dementated her, robbing her of her memories and personality.  这种疾病慢慢地使她精神错乱，夺走了她的记忆和个性。| synonyms: madden, derange, unhinge | antonyms:  enlighten, clarify,  | verb

dementate:/dɪˈmɛnteɪt/ | adj. | 精神错乱的；痴呆的 |  Deprived of reason or understanding; insane or demented. | The dementate patient wandered the halls, muttering incoherently.  痴呆的病人漫无目的地在走廊里游荡，语无伦次地咕哝着。 | synonyms: insane, mad, deranged | antonyms: sane, lucid, rational | adjective
