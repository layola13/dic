lunatical: /ˈluːnətɪkl/ (US) /ˈljuːnətɪkl/ (UK)| adj.|疯狂的；精神错乱的；极端的|Driven by extreme enthusiasm or passion; wildly foolish or irrational.|例句：His lunatical obsession with conspiracy theories alienated his friends.  他疯狂地痴迷于阴谋论，这使他的朋友们疏远了他。

例句：Investing all her savings in that failing business seemed like a lunatical decision.  把所有积蓄都投资于那家倒闭的企业似乎是一个疯狂的决定。|近义词：insane, mad, crazy, demented, unhinged|反义词：sane, rational, sensible|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *lunaticus, from Latin luna (“moon”).  The belief was that changes in the moon caused intermittent insanity.
来自晚期拉丁语 *lunaticus，源自拉丁语 luna（“月亮”）。人们相信月亮的变化会导致间歇性精神错乱。
