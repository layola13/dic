micaceous:/ˌmaɪˈkeɪʃəs/
adjective
云母的；含云母的
containing mica; resembling mica
The soil in this area is micaceous.  这个地区的土壤含云母。
Synonyms: flaky, scaly, laminar
Antonyms: compact, solid
adjective
