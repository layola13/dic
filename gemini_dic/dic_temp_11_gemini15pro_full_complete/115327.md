mannishly:/ˈmænɪʃli/| adv.|男子气概地，像男人一样地|In a manner considered typical of a man; like a man.|She strode mannishly across the room. 她大步流星地走过房间，很有男子气概。|Examples: 1. She dressed mannishly in a suit and tie.  她穿西装打领带，打扮得很男性化。

2. She spoke in a deep, mannishly voice. 她用低沉的、男性化的声音说话。|Synonyms:masculinely, manlike|Antonyms:femininely, womanly|Word Type:Adverb
