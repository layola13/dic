nondepressions:/ˌnɒndɪˈprɛʃənz/ | n. | 非抑郁状态，无抑郁症 | Absence of depression; a state of not being depressed.  This term is not commonly used in formal medical contexts, and "absence of depression" or "not depressed" are generally preferred. |  The study focused on the nondepressions in the control group.  这项研究的重点是对照组的非抑郁状态。|  The survey revealed a surprising number of nondepressions among the participants.  这项调查显示，参与者中非抑郁状态的人数惊人。| Synonyms:  well-being, mental wellness, emotional health | Antonyms: depression, melancholy, despair | noun
