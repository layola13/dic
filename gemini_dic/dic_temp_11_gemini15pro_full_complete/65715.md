eventing:/ɪˈvɛntɪŋ/
n.|三日赛|A competition involving dressage, cross-country, and show jumping.|She excels at eventing and hopes to compete in the Olympics. 她擅长三日赛，并希望参加奥运会。| 近义词：equestrianism, horse trials|反义词: None|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "event," referring to the combined events of the competition. Ultimately from Latin "eventus" (outcome).
