coughs:/kɒfs/ (British), /kɔːfs/ (American)| v. n.|咳嗽；咳嗽声|The act of expelling air from the lungs with a sudden sharp sound.  As a noun, it refers to the sound itself or an instance of coughing. |He coughs a lot when he has a cold. 他感冒时咳嗽得很厉害。|近义词：splutter, hack, choke|反义词: (As a verb, coughing doesn't have a direct opposite.
