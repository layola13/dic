hymnic:/ˈhɪmnɪk/ | adj. | 赞美诗的；赞歌似的 | Relating to or characteristic of a hymn; resembling a hymn in style or spirit. | The music had a hymnic quality, uplifting and reverent.  这音乐具有赞美诗的特质，令人振奋和崇敬。| The congregation joined in singing the hymnic chorus.  会众一起唱起了赞美诗的副歌。 | 近义词:  anthemic, devotional, liturgical, psalmodic | 反义词: secular, profane | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Greek hymnos "hymn" + -ikos "pertaining to."
