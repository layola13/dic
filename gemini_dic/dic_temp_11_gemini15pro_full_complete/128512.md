newsworthy:/ˈnjuːzˌwɜːrði/| adj.|有新闻价值的；值得报道的|Something that is newsworthy is interesting enough to be reported in the news. |The royal wedding was a newsworthy event. 皇家婚礼是一个有新闻价值的事件。

The discovery of the new planet was considered newsworthy by many media outlets.  这个新行星的发现被许多媒体认为是有新闻价值的。|近义词：noteworthy, remarkable, significant, important|反义词：unimportant, insignificant, trivial|词性: adjective
