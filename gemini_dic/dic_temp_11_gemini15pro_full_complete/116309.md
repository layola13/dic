mastax: /ˈmæstæks/| n. | 咀嚼器，砂囊 |The muscular pharynx of rotifers used for grinding food.  It is equipped with hard, jaw-like structures called trophi. |The rotifer uses its mastax to grind up algae.  轮虫用它的咀嚼器磨碎藻类。|近义词: pharynx, gizzard|反义词: None| noun
