non-interference:/ˌnɒnˌɪntəˈfɪərəns/ | n. | 不干涉，不干预 |  The principle of non-interference in other countries' internal affairs is a cornerstone of international law.  It means not intervening or meddling in the affairs of another nation, particularly regarding its political system, economy, or social structures. |  The UN charter emphasizes the principle of non-interference in the domestic affairs of member states. 联合国宪章强调不干涉成员国内政的原则。

The policy of non-interference allowed the conflict to escalate. 不干涉政策导致冲突升级。 | 近义词: non-intervention, neutrality | 反义词: interference, intervention | 词性: 名词
