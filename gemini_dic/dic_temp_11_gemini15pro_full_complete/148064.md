oversanguinely:/ˌoʊvərˈsæŋɡwɪnli/| adv.|过于乐观地，过于自信地|in a way that is too hopeful or confident, especially about the future.  It suggests an excessive and perhaps unrealistic degree of optimism.|He oversanguinely predicted a quick victory. 他过于乐观地预测了一场速胜。|Synonyms: optimistically, hopefully, cheerfully, confidently|Antonyms: pessimistically, gloomily, despairingly, fearfully|词性:副词
