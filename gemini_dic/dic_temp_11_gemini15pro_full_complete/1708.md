acotyledonous:/ˌeɪˌkɒtɪˈliːdənəs/ | adj. | 无子叶的 |  Lacking cotyledons; used of plants whose embryos have no cotyledons.  | Ferns and mosses are acotyledonous plants. 蕨类植物和苔藓植物是无子叶植物。 | The fungus, being acotyledonous, reproduced through spores.  这种真菌是无子叶的，通过孢子繁殖。 |  acotyledon,  cryptogam  |  phanerogam, cotyledonous | adjective
