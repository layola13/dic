acroterium:/ˌækrəˈtɪəriəm/
n.
acroteria (plural)
顶饰，（尤指古希腊罗马建筑三角墙顶端的）雕像
An architectural ornament placed at the apex or at the lower corners of a pediment.
The temple was adorned with elaborate acroteria depicting mythical figures.  这座寺庙装饰着描绘神话人物的精美顶饰。
finial, ornament, decoration
---
Etymology:
From Latin *acroterium*, from Ancient Greek ἀκρωτήριον (akrōtḗrion, “summit, extremity, pediment”), from ἄκρον (ákron, “extremity, peak”).
