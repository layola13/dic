distil:/dɪˈstɪl/ /dəˈstɪl/| v. n.| 蒸馏，提取；提炼，浓缩| To purify a liquid by heating it until it becomes a gas and then cooling the gas back into a liquid so that any impurities are left behind.  To extract the essential elements of something.| They distil whisky from malted barley. 他们用麦芽大麦蒸馏威士忌。| 近义词：extract, refine, purify, concentrate| 反义词：dilute, mix, combine|词性:动词

distil:/dɪˈstɪl/ /dəˈstɪl/| n.| 蒸馏液，精华| The liquid produced by distillation. The essential elements of something.| The distil of his research was published in a single volume. 他研究的精髓被出版成了一本书。|近义词：essence, extract, concentrate|反义词：mixture, whole|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *distiller*, from Latin distillare "to drip down, trickle," from dis- "down, away" + stillare "to drip," from stilla "a drop."
