inferably:/ɪnˈfɜːrəbli/
adverb
可以推断地，推测起来
capable of being inferred; deducible.
例句：Inferably, he was not happy with the outcome, given his reaction.  可以推断，从他的反应来看，他对结果并不满意。

例句：The evidence suggests, inferably, that the suspect was at the scene of the crime.  证据表明，可以推断，嫌疑人在犯罪现场。

近义词：presumably,  deductably, logically,  supposedly
反义词：improbably, unlikely,  doubtfully
词性: adverb

词源: From "infer" + "-able" + "-ly".  "Infer" comes from Latin "inferre" (to bring in, carry in, introduce, apply, conclude).  "-able" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being". "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs from adjectives.
