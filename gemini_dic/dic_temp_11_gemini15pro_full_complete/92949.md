ideographic:/ˌaɪdiəˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
表意的；表意文字的
representing an idea or object directly by a picture or character, rather than by spelling a word.
Chinese characters are ideographic. 汉字是表意的。
synonyms: symbolic, representative, emblematic
antonyms: phonetic, alphabetic
词性: 形容词
