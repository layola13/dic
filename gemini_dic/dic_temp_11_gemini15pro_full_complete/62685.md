engenderer:/ɪnˈdʒɛndərər/
|n.|引起者；造成者|The engenderer of the problem needs to be held accountable. 问题的制造者需要被追究责任。|
|n.|产生者；创造者|She is the engenderer of many beautiful works of art. 她是许多美丽艺术品的创作者。|
|近义词：creator, producer, author, originator, causer|
|反义词：destroyer, preventer|
|词性：名词|


词源:  From Middle English *engendren, from Anglo-Norman engendrer, Old French engendrer, from Latin ingenerāre (“to implant, engender, produce”), from in- (“in”) + generāre (“to beget, produce”).
