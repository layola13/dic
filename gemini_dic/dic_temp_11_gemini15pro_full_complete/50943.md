design:/dɪˈzaɪn/ /dɪˈzaɪn/| n. vt.|设计，图案，计划，目的|A plan or drawing produced to show the look and function or workings of a building, garment, or other object before it is built or made.  The purpose for which something is made.|The architect showed us the designs for the new building.  建筑师向我们展示了新建筑的设计图。|近义词：plan, scheme, blueprint, draft|反义词:accident, chance|noun
design:/dɪˈzaɪn/ /dɪˈzaɪn/| vt.|设计，计划，构思|Decide upon the look and functioning of (something) by making a detailed drawing of it.|She designed a new logo for the company. 她为公司设计了一个新的标志。|近义词：plan, create, conceive, develop|反义词:destroy, ruin|verb
