overidlenesses: /ˌoʊvərˈaɪdl̩nɪsɪz/ | n. | 过度闲散；过多的懒惰 |  The state of being excessively idle; an instance of this.  It refers to prolonged periods of inactivity and laziness, often beyond what is considered normal or acceptable. |  His overidlenesses led to his dismissal from the company. 他的过度懒散导致他被公司解雇。 |  The summer's overidlenesses made it difficult to return to the routine of school.  夏天的过度闲散使得难以回到学校的日常生活中。 | 近义词：laziness, indolence, inactivity | 反义词：industriousness, diligence, activity | 词性: 名词


词源:
"over" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi) + "idle" (Old English īdel) + "-ness" (Old English -nes(s), a suffix forming nouns expressing state, condition, or quality)
