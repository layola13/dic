melter:/ˈmɛltər/| n.|熔化器，熔炼炉，熔化工人|A device or person that melts something.  A melter is used in various industries to melt materials like metal, glass, or plastic.|The glass melter was operating at full capacity. 玻璃熔化炉正在满负荷运转。|Synonyms: furnace, smelter, liquefier|Antonyms: freezer, solidifier|noun

melter:/ˈmɛltər/| n.|融化剂|A substance that causes something else to melt.|They added a melter to the snow to clear the driveway. 他们在雪里加了融化剂来清理车道。|Synonyms: thawing agent, deicer|Antonyms: freezing agent|noun
