consolable:/ˈkɒnsələbl/ (UK) /kənˈsoʊləbl/ (US)| adj.|可安慰的，可被抚慰的|Able to be comforted.|
例句：After the loss of her pet, she was inconsolable for days.  在她失去宠物后，她好几天都无法得到安慰。

例句：He was so distraught that he was almost inconsolable. 他非常沮丧，几乎无法得到安慰。


近义词：comforted, soothed, relieved|
反义词：inconsolable, distraught, grief-stricken|
词性:adjective
