normality:/nɔːrˈmælɪti/ | n. | 常态，正常状态 |The state of being normal; the usual, expected, or typical condition. |After a period of upheaval, some semblance of normality returned to the region.  经历了一段动荡之后，该地区恢复了一些常态。| synonyms: normalcy, regularity, typicality | antonyms: abnormality, irregularity, anomaly | noun

normality:/nɔːrˈmælɪti/ | n. | （化学）当量浓度 |In chemistry, a measure of concentration equal to the gram equivalent weight per liter of solution. |The normality of the acid solution was determined by titration. 酸溶液的当量浓度是用滴定法测定的。| synonyms: equivalent concentration | antonyms: molarity, molality | noun
