bethought:/bɪˈθɔːt/
verb
想起，考虑，反思
To think about something; to consider or reflect upon.
He bethought himself of his promise and returned. 他想起了他的承诺，然后回去了。
She bethought herself that she had left the oven on. 她突然想起她把烤箱开着。
consider, remember, recollect
forget, ignore
v.
