allyship:/ˈælaɪʃɪp/
n.
同盟; 联合; 结盟; 支持弱势群体的行动
Active support for and solidarity with a marginalized or oppressed group or person.
例句：
Her allyship with the LGBTQ+ community was evident in her advocacy work. 她对 LGBTQ+ 社区的支持体现在她的倡导工作中。

Many companies are now promoting allyship in the workplace to create a more inclusive environment. 许多公司现在都在工作场所推广同盟关系，以创造更具包容性的环境。

近义词：solidarity, support, advocacy
反义词：opposition, antagonism, hostility
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From ally + -ship.  "Ally" comes from Old French *alier (Modern French allier), from Latin alligare "to bind to," from ad- "to" + ligare "to tie."  "-ship" is an Old English suffix denoting "state or condition of being."
