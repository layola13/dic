landlike:/ˈlændlaɪk/
adjective
像陆地的；陆生的
Resembling land; terrestrial.
The newly formed island had a surprisingly landlike appearance. 这个新形成的岛屿有着惊人的陆地般的外观。
The planet's surface was described as landlike. 这个星球的表面被描述成像陆地一样。
近义词:terrestrial, earthlike, land-based
反义词:aquatic, marine


ETYMOLOGY->
From land + -like.  "Land" comes from Old English "land," meaning "ground, soil, territory." "-like" is a suffix meaning "resembling," from Old English "-lic."
