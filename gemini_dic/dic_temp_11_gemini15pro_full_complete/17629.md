bebop:/ˈbiːbɑːp/ (US), /ˈbiːbɒp/ (UK)| n. |比波普（一种爵士乐风格）|A style of jazz characterized by fast tempos, complex harmonies, and virtuosic improvisation.|Charlie Parker was a master of bebop. 查理·帕克是比波普大师。|The band played a bebop tune that got everyone dancing. 乐队演奏了一首比波普曲子，让每个人都跳起舞来。|近义词：jazz, swing|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "traditional jazz")|词性:noun
