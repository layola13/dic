outpraises:/ˌaʊtˈpreɪzɪz/
verb
超越赞美；胜过赞美
to praise someone or something more highly than another person or thing
He outpraised all the other contestants. 他对其他所有参赛者的赞扬都超过了其他人。
Synonyms: outdo, surpass, excel, exceed
Antonyms: criticize, condemn, disparage
词性: 动词
