aunty:/ˈɑːnti/ (US), /ˈɑːnti/ or /ˈænti/ (UK)| n.|阿姨；姑妈；伯母；舅妈；婶婶 (a term of affection for an older woman, often not a blood relative)|Aunty is often used as a term of respect for older women in some cultures, especially by children.|例句：My aunty makes the best cookies. 我阿姨做的饼干最好吃。

例句：The children all called her "Aunty Mary" even though she wasn't related to them. 孩子们都叫她“玛丽阿姨”，即使她和他们没有血缘关系。


近义词：aunt|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
