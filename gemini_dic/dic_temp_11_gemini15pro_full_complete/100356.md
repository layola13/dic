interstimulates:/ˌɪntərˈstɪmjʊleɪts/
verb
互相刺激，彼此激励
To stimulate mutually; to provoke or encourage each other.
The two artists interstimulated each other, leading to a period of great creativity for both of them. 这两位艺术家互相激励，促成了两人极具创造力的时期。
近义词:interact, synergize, cross-fertilize
反义词:suppress, inhibit, hinder
词性:v.

Etymology:
From inter- (meaning "between, among, mutually") + stimulate (from Latin stimulāre, "to prick, urge, incite").
