endoparasite:/ˈɛndoʊˈpærəsaɪt/| n. |体内寄生虫|An organism that lives inside another organism (its host) and derives nourishment from it, often harming the host in the process.|Example 1: Tapeworms are common endoparasites that can infect humans.  绦虫是常见的可以感染人类的体内寄生虫。

Example 2: The veterinarian treated the dog for endoparasites. 兽医治疗了这条狗的体内寄生虫。|近义词：internal parasite|反义词:ectoparasite|词性:noun
