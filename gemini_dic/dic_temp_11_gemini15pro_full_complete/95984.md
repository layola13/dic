incunabulum:/ɪnˈkjuːnəbjʊləm/ |n.|  摇篮本；早期印刷本 |A book printed before 1501, especially one printed in Europe before the spread of printing. |例句：The incunabulum was carefully preserved in the library's rare book collection. 这本摇篮本被小心地保存在图书馆的珍本书收藏中。

例句：He was thrilled to discover an incunabulum at the antique book fair. 他在古董书展上发现了一本摇篮本，非常激动。|近义词：early printed book, cradle book|反义词:modern book|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin incunabula ("swaddling clothes, cradle"), neuter plural of incunabulum, from in ("in") + cunabula ("cradle"), plural of cunabulum.  Figuratively, the "cradle" or infancy of printing.
