eclipser:/ɪˈklɪpsər/| n.| 胜过者，使黯然失色的人或物 |Something or someone that eclipses; that which obscures or surpasses something else.|  The new skyscraper eclipsed all the other buildings in the city. 这座新的摩天大楼使城市里的所有其他建筑都黯然失色。 | Synonyms: outshiner, surpasser | Antonyms: inferior, lesser | noun


eclipsing:/ɪˈklɪpsɪŋ/| adj.|  使黯然失色的，胜过的 | Surpassing or obscuring something else.|  Her eclipsing beauty captivated everyone in the room. 她倾国倾城的美丽迷住了房间里的每一个人。 | Synonyms: overshadowing, surpassing | Antonyms: inferior, subordinate | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From eclipse + -er.  Ultimately from the Greek ekleipein "to leave out, forsake."
