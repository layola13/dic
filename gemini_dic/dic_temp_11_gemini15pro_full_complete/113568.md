macassar:/məˈkæsər/ | n. adj. | 马卡萨木；马卡萨油；一种发油 | A dark, richly grained wood from Sulawesi, Indonesia, used for furniture. Also refers to the oil obtained from the seeds of the macassar tree, traditionally used as hair oil.  |  例句：The antique dresser was made of beautiful macassar ebony. 这古董梳妆台是用漂亮的马卡萨黑檀木做的。

例句：He slicked back his hair with macassar oil. 他用马卡萨油把头发往后梳。 | 近义词：ebony, hair oil | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词，形容词
