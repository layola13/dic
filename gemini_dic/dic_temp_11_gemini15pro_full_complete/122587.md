modernest: /ˈmɒdərnɪst/ (British) /ˈmɑːdərnɪst/ (American)
|adj.|最现代的|Superlative form of modern, meaning most modern.  Though grammatically correct, "most modern" is generally preferred.|"He claimed to have the modernest car on the market." 他声称拥有市场上最现代的汽车。|近义词: most modern, contemporary, latest, cutting-edge|反义词: oldest, antiquated, outdated, archaic|词性:形容词
