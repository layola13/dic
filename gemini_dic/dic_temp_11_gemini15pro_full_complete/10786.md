aquariums:/əˈkwɛəriəmz/
n.
水族馆；养鱼缸
A glass tank or bowl filled with water, in which fish and other aquatic animals and plants are kept.
We visited the two aquariums in the city. 我们参观了城里的两座水族馆。
近义词：fish tanks, vivariums
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "natural habitat")


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "aquarium,"  neuter of "aquarius" ("of water"), from "aqua" ("water").
