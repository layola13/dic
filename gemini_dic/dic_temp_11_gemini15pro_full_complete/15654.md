baked:/beɪkt/| adj.|烤的，烘培的|Cooked by dry heat in an oven.|例句：I love the smell of freshly baked bread. 我喜欢新鲜出炉的面包的香味。|例句：The baked potatoes were fluffy and delicious. 烤土豆蓬松可口。|近义词：roasted, cooked, toasted|反义词:raw, uncooked|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *baken*, from Old English *bacen* (past participle of *bacan* "to bake"), from Proto-Germanic *bakanaz*, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰōgʷ- (“to roast, bake”). Cognate with Dutch *bakken*, German *backen*, Old Norse *baka*.
