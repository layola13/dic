guilting:/ˈɡɪltɪŋ/| v.|使内疚，使负罪|Making someone feel guilty about something they have or haven't done.|例句：He's always guilting me about not spending enough time with him. 他总是让我因为没有花足够的时间陪他而感到内疚。|例句：She felt guilty about leaving her dog alone all day. 她为把狗独自留了一整天而感到内疚。|近义词：shaming, blaming, reproaching|反义词:praising, commending|词性:动词 (Present Participle of "guilt")

词源: From the verb "guilt," which comes from the Old English word "gylt" meaning "crime, sin, offense."
