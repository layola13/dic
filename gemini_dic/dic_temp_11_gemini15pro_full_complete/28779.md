caroigne:/kaʁɔɲ/| n.|腐肉，尸体，(尤指动物的)残骸|A caroigne is the decaying flesh of a dead animal, often used in a derogatory sense to refer to a corpse.|The vultures circled above the caroigne in the desert. 秃鹰在沙漠中的尸体上盘旋。|近义词：carcass, corpse, remains|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑living being,  living creature)|词性:noun
Etymology: From Old French *caroigne, from Late Latin *caronia, alteration of Latin caro ("flesh").
