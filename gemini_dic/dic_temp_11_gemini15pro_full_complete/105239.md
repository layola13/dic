khaki:/ˈkɑːki/ (US), /ˈkɑːki/ (UK)| n. adj.|卡其色，卡其布，卡其服|A light yellowish-brown color, originally used for military uniforms.  Also refers to cloth or clothing of this color.|例句：He wore khaki pants and a white shirt. 他穿了卡其裤和一件白衬衫。

例句：The soldiers were all dressed in khaki. 士兵们都穿着卡其服。


|近义词：beige, tan, sand|反义词:|n.  cloth, fabric, material adj. brown, beige


khaki:/ˈkɑːki/ (US), /ˈkɑːki/ (UK)| adj.|卡其色的|Having a light yellowish-brown color.|例句：She bought a khaki skirt for the summer. 她为夏天买了一条卡其色的裙子。

例句：He prefers khaki shirts to white ones.  比起白衬衫，他更喜欢卡其色的衬衫。

|近义词：beige, tan, sand|反义词:|adj. colorful, bright
