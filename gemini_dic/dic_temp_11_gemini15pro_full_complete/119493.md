micropayment: /ˈmaɪkroʊˌpeɪmənt/ | n. | 小额支付，微支付 | A very small payment made online. | Example 1: Micropayments are often used for purchasing digital content like single articles or songs.  微支付通常用于购买数字内容，如单篇文章或歌曲。

Example 2: The website uses a micropayment system for accessing premium features.  该网站使用小额支付系统来访问高级功能。 | Synonyms: small payment, mini-payment | Antonyms: large payment, macropayment | noun
