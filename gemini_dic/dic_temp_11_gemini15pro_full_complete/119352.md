microcircuitry:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈsɜːrkɪtri/
n.|微电路，微型电路|Miniaturized electronic circuits and components typically formed on a semiconductor substrate.|The advancement of microcircuitry has revolutionized computing. 微电路的进步彻底改变了计算机技术。|Synonyms: microelectronics, integrated circuits, chips|Antonyms: macrocircuitry (less common), discrete components|noun

microcircuitry:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈsɜːrkɪtri/
n.|微型电路工艺; 微型电路制造技术|The design and manufacture of miniaturized electronic circuits.|Advances in microcircuitry have allowed for smaller and more powerful devices. 微型电路工艺的进步使得更小巧、更强大的设备成为可能。|Synonyms: microfabrication, chip fabrication|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but possibly traditional circuit board manufacturing)|noun
