obesity:/oʊˈbiːsəti/ /əʊˈbiːsɪti/| n. |肥胖，肥胖症|The condition of being grossly fat or overweight; excessive accumulation and storage of fat in the body.|Childhood obesity is a growing problem in many countries. 儿童肥胖症在许多国家是一个日益严重的问题.|Synonyms: adiposity, corpulence, overweight, stoutness|Antonyms: thinness, leanness, slenderness|noun
