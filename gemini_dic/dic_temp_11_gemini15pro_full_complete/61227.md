emaciated: /ɪˈmeɪʃiːeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 消瘦的，憔悴的 | Abnormally thin or weak, especially because of illness or a lack of food. | The emaciated refugees were barely able to walk.  这些消瘦的难民几乎走不动路了。|  The rescued dog was emaciated and covered in fleas.  被救出的狗骨瘦如柴，浑身都是跳蚤。| 近义词: gaunt, skeletal, thin, haggard | 反义词: plump, obese, well-fed | 词性: 形容词 | 词源:  From Latin *emaciatus*, past participle of emaciare (“to make lean, waste away”), from ex- (“out”) + macies (“leanness”).
