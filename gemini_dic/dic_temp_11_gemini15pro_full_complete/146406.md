overfocused:/ˌoʊvərˈfoʊkəst/| adj.| 过度专注的，过于集中于一点的|Excessively focused on one thing to the exclusion of others; unable to shift attention as needed.| He was so overfocused on the details that he missed the bigger picture. 他过于专注于细节，反而忽略了全局。|近义词：obsessed, fixated, preoccupied|反义词: distracted, inattentive|词性:形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "focused" (past participle of "focus")  "over-"（前缀，意为“过度地”）+ “focused”（“focus”的过去分词）
