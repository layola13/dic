feverous:/ˈfiːvərəs/ | adj. |  发烧的，狂热的 | Marked by fever; feverish. Also, intensely emotional; frenetic. |  She felt feverous and weak. 她感到浑身发热，虚弱无力。|  The atmosphere in the city was feverous with excitement. 城市的气氛因兴奋而狂热。 | synonyms: feverish, febrile, burning, fiery, heated, inflamed | antonyms: calm, cool, composed, serene | adjective
