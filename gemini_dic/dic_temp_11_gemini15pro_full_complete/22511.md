boomerangers:/ˈbuːməræŋərz/| n. |回旋镖; 澳大利亚土著居民; 自食其果的行为或言论(通常复数);|People who leave their home area for a period of time to live or work elsewhere, before eventually returning.  那些离开家乡一段时间去其他地方生活或工作，最终又回来的人。|Many boomerangers returned to their hometowns after college. 许多人大学毕业后回到了家乡。|Synonyms:  returnees, expatriates returning home,  |Antonyms: emigrants, migrants |noun


boomerang:/ˈbuːməræŋ/| n. vi.|回飞镖; 自食其果的言行;|A curved wooden missile that can be thrown so as to return to the thrower. 一种弯曲的木制投掷物，可以投掷后返回投掷者手中。|He threw the boomerang and it came right back to him. 他扔出了回旋镖，它正好回到了他手中。|Synonyms: come back, rebound, backfire |Antonyms:  straight shot (figurative) |noun, intransitive verb
