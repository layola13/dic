lessen:/ˈlɛsn/| v. |减少，减轻，降低|To make or become less; diminish.|The rain lessened in intensity. 雨势减弱了。|近义词：decrease, reduce, diminish, abate, mitigate|反义词:increase, enhance, aggravate, intensify|词性:动词
Etymology: From Middle English *lessnen, *lasnen, from Old English *lǣssian, *lǣsnian (“to lessen, diminish, reduce”), from Proto-Germanic *laisōnjana (“to lessen”), from *laisiz (“less”).  Cognate with Dutch lessen (“to lessen”), German lassen (“to let, leave”), Gothic (lasivs, “weak, infirm”). Related to less.
