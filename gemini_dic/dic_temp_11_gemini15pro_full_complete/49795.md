demonocracy: /ˌdiːməˈnɒkrəsi/ /ˌdiːməˈnɑːkrəsi/
| n. |恶魔统治；暴民统治|A government by demons or evil spirits; or, a government controlled by the mob or populace in a destructive or chaotic manner.|The author portrayed a dystopian future ruled by a demonocracy, where fear and oppression were rampant.| 近义词: tyranny, despotism, ochlocracy, mobocracy | 反义词: democracy, republic | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Greek *dēmon* (demon) + *-cracy* (rule, government).  The "-cracy" suffix comes from the Greek *kratos* meaning "power" or "rule".
