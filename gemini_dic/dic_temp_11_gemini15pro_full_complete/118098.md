meningitis:/ˌmɛnɪnˈdʒaɪtɪs/
n.|脑膜炎|Inflammation of the meninges, the membranes surrounding the brain and spinal cord, typically caused by a bacterial or viral infection and characterized by fever, headache, and stiffness of the neck.|She was diagnosed with bacterial meningitis. 她被诊断出患有细菌性脑膜炎。|Meningococcal infection can lead to meningitis. 脑膜炎球菌感染会导致脑膜炎。|近义词:encephalitis|反义词:None|词性:noun


Etymology:
From  Modern Latin *mēningītis, from Ancient Greek μῆνιγξ (mēninx, “membrane”) + -itis.
