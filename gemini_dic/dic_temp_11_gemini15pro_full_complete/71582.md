flashmob: /ˈflæʃmɒb/ (UK) /ˈflæʃmɑːb/ (US) | n. |快闪族，快闪行动 | A large public gathering at which people perform an unexpected action or piece of performance art for a brief time and then quickly disperse. |  Example 1:  The flashmob surprised everyone in the park with their synchronized dance routine. 快闪族在公园里用他们同步的舞蹈动作让每个人都感到惊讶。

Example 2: Did you see that flashmob video where everyone froze in place? 你看到那个快闪视频了吗？每个人都定格在那里。| 近义词: surprise performance, impromptu performance | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
