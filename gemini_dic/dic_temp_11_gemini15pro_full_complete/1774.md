acquittal: /əˈkwɪtəl/ | n. | 免除责任，解除义务 | release from a duty, obligation, or debt |  His acquittal of the debt was a great relief.  他免除了债务，感到如释重负。 | synonyms: release, discharge, exemption | antonyms: liability, responsibility | noun
