himla:/ˈhɪmlɑː/
interj.|哎呀；我的天哪|Used to express surprise, frustration, or other strong emotions.  A Swedish interjection similar to "oh my gosh" or "good heavens" in English.|Himla, I forgot my keys! 哎呀，我忘记带钥匙了！|近义词：Gosh, heavens, jeepers, crikey|反义词:None|词性:Interjection
