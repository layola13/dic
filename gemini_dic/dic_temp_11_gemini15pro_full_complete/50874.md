desensitized:/ˌdɪˈsɛnsɪtaɪzd/
adjective
脱敏的，不敏感的
Made less sensitive.  Reduced in sensitivity, especially to stimulation.
After years of working in the emergency room, the doctor became desensitized to the sight of blood.  多年在急诊室工作后，这位医生对看到血已经麻木了。
近义词:numb, indifferent, hardened
反义词:sensitive, responsive, susceptible
词性:adjective

desensitize:/ˌdɪˈsɛnsɪtaɪz/
verb
使脱敏，使不敏感
To make someone less sensitive to something. To reduce the sensitivity of, especially to stimulation.
Exposure therapy aims to desensitize patients to their phobias. 暴露疗法旨在使患者对他们的恐惧症脱敏。
近义词:numb, dull, harden
反义词:sensitize, stimulate
词性:verb

词源:de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reduce") + sensitize (to make sensitive)
