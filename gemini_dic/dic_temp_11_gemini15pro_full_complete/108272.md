laundering:/ˈlɔːndərɪŋ/ (US), /ˈlɔːndrɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|洗钱;洗涤;漂白|The act of concealing the source of illegally obtained money by transferring it through a series of complex transactions.|The government is trying to crack down on money laundering. 政府正在试图打击洗钱活动。|Synonyms: cleaning, washing, sanitizing|Antonyms: soiling, dirtying|noun

laundering:/ˈlɔːndərɪŋ/ (US), /ˈlɔːndrɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|(通常指钱)来路不正的，通过洗钱获得的|Obtained through or related to money laundering.|Laundering money is a serious crime. 洗钱是一项严重的犯罪。|Synonyms: illicit, illegal, dirty|Antonyms: legal, legitimate|adjective
