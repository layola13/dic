antidemocratical: /ˌæntiˌdɛməˈkrætɪkəl/ | adj. | 反民主的，非民主的 | Opposed to or violating the principles of democracy. |  The king's actions were seen as antidemocratical by the people. 国王的行为被人民视为反民主的。|  The new law was criticized for its antidemocratical nature. 这项新法律因其反民主的性质而受到批评。 | 近义词: undemocratic, autocratic, totalitarian | 反义词: democratic, egalitarian | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
anti- (prefix) + democratical (adjective)

"anti-" comes from Greek "anti" meaning "against" or "opposite".

"democratical" derives from "democracy," which originates from the Greek words "dēmos" (people) and "kratos" (power or rule).
