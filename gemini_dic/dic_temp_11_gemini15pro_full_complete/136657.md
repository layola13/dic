nonsculptured:/ˌnɒnˈskʌlptʃəd/ /ˌnɑːnˈskʌlptʃərd/
adjective
非雕刻的，未经雕琢的
Not having been sculpted; not shaped by carving or other sculptural processes.
The garden featured nonsculptured rocks and natural formations.  花园以未经雕琢的岩石和自然形态为特色。
The artist preferred nonsculptured wood for his installations. 这位艺术家更喜欢用未经雕刻的木头进行装置创作。
synonyms: natural, uncarved, unshaped, rough, raw
antonyms: sculpted, carved, shaped, formed
