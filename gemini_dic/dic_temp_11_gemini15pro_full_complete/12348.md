ashantee:/ˌæʃænˈtiː/ /ˌɑːʃɑːnˈtiː/
n.
阿散蒂（加纳的一个民族和地区）
Ashanti refers to an ethnic group and a historical kingdom in present-day Ghana.
The Ashanti people are known for their rich culture and traditions.  阿散蒂人以其丰富的文化和传统而闻名。
The Ashanti Kingdom was a powerful state in West Africa.  阿散蒂王国是西非的一个强大国家。
