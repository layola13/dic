eradicated:/ɪˈrædɪkeɪtɪd/
verb
根除的，消灭的
Completely removed or destroyed.
The disease has been eradicated from the country.  这种疾病已从该国根除。
Polio has been virtually eradicated through vaccination. 通过接种疫苗，脊髓灰质炎实际上已被根除。
近义词: eliminated, abolished, exterminated, wiped out
反义词: established, introduced, fostered, cultivated
词性:Verb (past participle)
