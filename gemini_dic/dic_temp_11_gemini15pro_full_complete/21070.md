blindly:/ˈblaɪndli/| adv.|盲目地，不加思考地|Without thinking or paying attention to the facts; in a way that shows poor judgment.|He blindly followed orders without questioning them. 他盲目地服从命令，毫不质疑。|例句：She blindly accepted his explanation without further investigation. 她不加调查地盲目接受了他的解释。

例句：He walked blindly into the trap. 他盲目地走进了陷阱。


近义词：recklessly, thoughtlessly, unthinkingly, rashly|反义词:carefully, cautiously, thoughtfully, prudently|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *blindlīce (unattested), from blind + -līce (suffix forming adverbs of manner).  Cognate with Dutch blindelings (“blindly”), German blindlings (“blindly”). The English word is first attested around 1300.
