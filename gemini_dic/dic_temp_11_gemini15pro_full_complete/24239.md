bridlepath:/ˈbraɪdlˌpæθ/ | n. | 马道，骑马小径 | A path or track specifically designed for riding horses. |  We took a leisurely stroll along the scenic bridlepath. 我们沿着风景优美的马道悠闲地散步。

The bridlepath wound its way through the forest. 这条马道蜿蜒穿过森林。| 近义词：trail, path, track | 反义词: road, highway | 词性: noun
