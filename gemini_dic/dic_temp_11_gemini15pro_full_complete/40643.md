conventionalized:/kənˈvɛnʃənəˌlaɪzd/
adjective
程式化的，约定俗成的
Made to conform to generally accepted standards; lacking originality or individuality.
The play was a conventionalized portrayal of Victorian society.  这出戏是对维多利亚时代社会的程式化描绘。
conventional, formulaic, stylized, standardized
original, innovative, unconventional, unique
词性: 形容词
