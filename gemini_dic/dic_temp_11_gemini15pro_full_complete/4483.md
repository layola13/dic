albification:/ˌælbɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
变白，白化
The process of becoming white or whitish.
The albification of the coral reefs is a worrying sign of environmental damage. 珊瑚礁的白化是环境破坏的一个令人担忧的迹象。
whitening, bleaching
darkening, pigmentation
noun
