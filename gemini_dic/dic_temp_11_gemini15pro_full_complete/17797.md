bedridden:/ˈbedrɪdn/| adj.|卧床不起的；长期卧床的|Confined to bed by illness or infirmity; unable to get out of bed.|例句：She was bedridden for months after the accident. 事故发生后，她卧床不起好几个月。

例句：His grandfather has been bedridden for years.  他的祖父已经卧床多年了。


近义词：confined, immobile, incapacitated|反义词:mobile, active, healthy|词性:adjective


词源: bed + ridden.  "Ridden" comes from the past participle of the verb "ride," but in this context means "oppressed or afflicted by."  So, "bedridden" literally means "oppressed by being in bed."
