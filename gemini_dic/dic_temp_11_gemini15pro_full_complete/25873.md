burlap:/ˈbɜːrlæp/ (US) /ˈbɜːlæp/ (UK)| n.|粗麻布，麻布袋|A coarse, woven fabric made from jute or hemp, typically used for sacks and bags.|The coffee beans were shipped in burlap sacks. 咖啡豆是用麻布袋装运的。|Synonyms: hessian, gunny, sackcloth, sacking|Antonyms: silk, satin|noun
