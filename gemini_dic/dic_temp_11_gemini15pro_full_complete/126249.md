mydatoxin:/maɪˈdætəksɪn/| n. |麦达托素|A potent neurotoxin produced by the dinoflagellate Alexandrium minutum. It affects voltage-gated sodium channels in nerve and muscle cells, leading to paralytic shellfish poisoning (PSP). |This shellfish sample contains high levels of mydatoxin.  这个贝类样本中含有高浓度的麦达托素。|Synonyms: paralytic shellfish toxin (PST),  |Antonyms: N/A |Noun
