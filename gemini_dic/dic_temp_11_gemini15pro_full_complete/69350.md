federally:/ˈfɛdərəli/| adv.|联邦地；由联邦政府|In a way that relates to or involves a federal government.  A system of government in which power is divided between a central authority and constituent political units.|例句：The project was federally funded.  这个项目是由联邦政府资助的。|例句：Federally mandated regulations often supersede state laws. 联邦政府规定的法规通常优先于州法律。|近义词：nationally, centrally|反义词：locally, state-wise|词性:副词
