ligule:/ˈlɪɡjuːl/ | n. | 叶舌；舌状物 | A membranous or hairy appendage arising from the inner surface of a leaf at the junction of the leaf blade and sheath, especially in grasses and sedges. | The ligule of this grass species is very short.  这种草的叶舌很短。|  Near synonyms: appendage, projection | Antonym: N/A | noun

ligule:/ˈlɪɡjuːl/ | n. | (管风琴)簧舌 | A thin strip of metal, wood, or other material that vibrates to produce a sound in a reed instrument, such as a pipe organ. | The organ builder carefully adjusted the ligule of each pipe.  管风琴制造者仔细地调整了每个管道的簧舌。| Near synonyms: reed, tongue | Antonym: N/A | noun
