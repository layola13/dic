bccs:/ˌbiː siː siːz/| n.|密送，副本抄送|A method of sending emails to multiple recipients without revealing their addresses to each other.  It stands for "blind carbon copy."|I sent the email to the team with the client bcc'd. 我把邮件发给了团队，并将客户密送。|近义词:blind carbon copy|反义词:cc, to|noun
