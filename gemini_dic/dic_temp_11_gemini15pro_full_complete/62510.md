endowments:/ɪnˈdaʊmənts/ | n. | 馈赠，捐赠；天赋，才能 | Something that is bestowed; a gift, talent, or quality.  It often refers to a large sum of money given to a college, hospital, etc., to provide it with an income. |  The university relies on endowments to fund scholarships. 这所大学依靠捐赠来资助奖学金。| Synonyms: gifts, donations, bequests, talents, attributes | Antonyms: deprivations, shortcomings, weaknesses | noun


endowment:/ɪnˈdaʊmənt/ | n. | 馈赠，捐赠；天赋，才能 | The act of providing with something or the quality or ability possessed or inherited |  She has a natural endowment for music. 她有音乐天赋。 | Synonyms: gift, donation, bequest, talent, attribute | Antonyms: deprivation, shortcoming, weakness | noun
