miscaption:/ˌmɪsˈkæpʃən/
verb
错误地加标题；配错字幕
to provide (a picture, photograph, etc.) with an incorrect or misleading caption
The photo was miscaptioned as showing the president, when it was actually his bodyguard. 这张照片被错误地标注为总统，实际上是他的保镖。
近义词: mislabel, misname
反义词: caption correctly
词性: verb

miscaption:/ˌmɪsˈkæpʃən/
noun
错误的标题；错误的字幕
an incorrect or misleading caption
The miscaption under the photo caused considerable confusion. 照片下面的错误标题引起了相当大的混乱。
近义词: mislabeling, misnaming
反义词: correct caption
词性: noun
