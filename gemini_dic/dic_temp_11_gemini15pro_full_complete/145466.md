overclamour: /ˌoʊvərˈklæmər/ | v. |  压制；盖过（声音）；大声疾呼胜过 | To shout louder than; to drown out with loud noise. |  The protesters overclamoured the speaker, making it impossible for him to be heard.  抗议者们大声喧哗盖过了演讲者的声音，让他无法被听到。 | synonyms: outshout, drown out, overpower | antonyms: be quiet, listen, yield | verb

overclamour: /ˌoʊvərˈklæmər/ | n. |  喧嚣声；压制；大声疾呼 |  A loud and persistent outcry; the act of overclamouring. | The overclamour from the crowd made it difficult to hear the announcement.  人群的喧嚣声使得很难听到通知。| synonyms: din, uproar, commotion | antonyms: silence, quiet, stillness | noun
