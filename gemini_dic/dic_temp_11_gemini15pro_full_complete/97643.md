inheriting:/ɪnˈhɛrɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 继承，遗传 | Receiving (money, property, or a title) as an heir at the death of the previous holder. Also, deriving (a quality, characteristic, or predisposition) genetically from one's parents or ancestors. |  She is inheriting her grandmother's house. 她将继承她祖母的房子。|Synonyms: acquiring, receiving, obtaining, deriving, succeeding to | Antonyms: forfeiting, relinquishing, losing, renouncing| verb
