nonrenewal:/ˌnɑːnrɪˈnuːəl/
n.|不续约，不更新|The nonrenewal of his contract left him jobless. 他的合同没有续签，让他失业了。|例句：The nonrenewal of the lease forced them to move. 租约未续签，他们被迫搬家。|近义词：termination, expiration, discontinuation|反义词：renewal, extension|词性:名词

nonrenewal:/ˌnɑːnrɪˈnuːəl/
n.|未更新|The nonrenewal of her driver's license resulted in a fine.  她的驾照未更新导致了罚款。|例句：The nonrenewal of the software subscription limited access to certain features. 软件订阅未续费，限制了对某些功能的访问。|近义词：lapse, default|反义词：renewal, update|词性:名词
