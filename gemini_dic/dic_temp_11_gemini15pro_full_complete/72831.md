foliferous:/fəˈlɪfərəs/
adjective
多叶的；叶茂盛的
Bearing or producing leaves; leafy.
The foliferous tree provided ample shade. 这棵枝繁叶茂的树提供了充足的阴凉。
The plant is remarkably foliferous, producing a dense canopy of leaves.  这种植物叶片非常茂盛，形成了茂密的树冠。
近义词: leafy, frondose
反义词: leafless, bare
词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin *folifer, from folium "leaf" + -fer "bearing."
