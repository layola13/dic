guildhalls:/ˈɡɪldˌhɔːlz/
n.
公会大厅；行会会馆
A building used by a guild as a meeting place or headquarters.
The guildhalls of the medieval city were centers of commerce and craftsmanship. 中世纪城市的公会大厅是商业和工艺中心。
The merchants met in their guildhall to discuss trade regulations. 商人们在他们的行会会馆开会讨论贸易规则。

近义词:meeting hall, assembly hall, union hall
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "private residence")
词性:noun
