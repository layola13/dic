monograph: /ˈmɒnəɡrɑːf/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnəɡræf/ (US)
n.|专著；专题论文|A detailed written study of a single specialized subject or an aspect of it.|例句：He is the author of a monograph on the history of jazz. 他是一本关于爵士乐历史专著的作者。|例句：She's writing a monograph on the life cycle of the mayfly. 她正在写一篇关于蜉蝣生命周期的专题论文。|近义词：treatise, dissertation, study, paper|反义词: None|词性:noun

monographs: /ˈmɒnəɡrɑːfs/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnəɡræfs/ (US)
n.|专著（复数）；专题论文（复数）|Plural form of monograph.|例句：The library has a large collection of monographs on various subjects.  这个图书馆收藏了大量关于不同主题的专著。|例句：He has published several monographs in the field of astrophysics. 他在天体物理学领域发表了几篇专题论文。|近义词：treatises, dissertations, studies, papers|反义词: None|词性:noun
