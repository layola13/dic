defeasanced:/dɪˈfiːsɪnst/
adjective
已废除的，失效的
rendered null and void; made invalid
The defeasanced contract is no longer binding.  已失效的合同不再具有约束力。
The loan agreement was defeasanced after the borrower pre-paid the outstanding balance.  在借款人提前偿还未偿余额后，贷款协议失效。
近义词: invalid, void, annulled, cancelled
反义词: valid, binding, effective
