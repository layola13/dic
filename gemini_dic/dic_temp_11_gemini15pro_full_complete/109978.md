liberticide:/lɪbərtiˌsaɪd/
n.|自由扼杀，摧残自由|The dictator's policies were acts of liberticide, suppressing free speech and assembly.|独裁者的政策是扼杀自由的行为，压制了言论自由和集会自由。|
Example 1: The government's new internet censorship laws are seen by many as an act of liberticide.  政府新的互联网审查法被许多人视为扼杀自由的行为。

Example 2:  Historians argue that the emperor's reign was marked by liberticide and the suppression of dissent.  历史学家认为，皇帝的统治以摧残自由和压制异见为标志。
|近义词：oppression, tyranny, despotism|反义词：liberation, freedom|词性:名词


Etymology:  From French *liberticide*, from Latin *lībertās* ("liberty") + *-cida* ("killer," from *caedere* "to kill").
