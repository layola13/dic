famulist: /ˈfæmjʊlɪst/
noun
家谱学家，家谱编纂者
A person who studies or compiles genealogies.
John is a dedicated famulist, tracing his family history back to the 17th century.  约翰是一位专注的家谱学家，他将自己的家族史追溯到17世纪。
近义词: genealogist
反义词:  (No direct antonym)
词性: noun
