nonattainable:/ˌnɒnəˈteɪnəbl/
adjective
无法获得的; 达不到的
Not able to be attained or achieved.
His dreams of becoming a rock star seemed nonattainable.  他成为摇滚明星的梦想似乎无法实现。
The summit of the mountain proved nonattainable due to the blizzard. 由于暴风雪，山顶无法到达。
近义词:unachievable, unattainable, inaccessible
反义词:achievable, attainable, accessible
词性:adjective

词源:
From non- (not) + attainable.  "Attainable" comes from the Old French "ateindre" (to attain, reach), from Latin "attingere" (to touch upon).
