gorgeously:/ˈɡɔːrdʒəsli/ | adv. | 华丽地，灿烂地，极好地 | In a gorgeous manner; beautifully; splendidly.  | The sunset was gorgeously painted across the sky. 夕阳在天空华丽地描绘。 |  She sang gorgeously. 她唱得非常好。 | 近义词: beautifully, splendidly, magnificently, superbly | 反义词: plainly, simply, dully, uglily | 词性: 副词

词源:
From gorgeous + -ly.  Gorgeous comes from Old French gorgias "elegant, fashionable," from gorgias "throat, ruff, wimple," related to gorge "throat."  The original sense referred to fine clothes and later expanded to general beauty and splendor.
