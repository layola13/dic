back-up:/ˈbækʌp/ (US), /ˈbækʌp/ (UK)| n. adj. v.|备份，支持，后援|A copy of data made in case the original is lost or damaged.  Something that can be used if the main one fails. To make a copy of (computer data).|I made a back-up of my important files. 我备份了我的重要文件。|近义词:copy, duplicate, reserve, support|反义词:original, primary|词性:名词, 形容词, 动词

back-up:/ˈbækʌp/ (US), /ˈbækʌp/ (UK)| n. adj. v.|备用的，候补的，堵塞的|Available to be used in place of the usual one.  Relating to a reserve. Blocked or congested.|He's our back-up quarterback. 他是我们的替补四分卫。|近义词:reserve, spare, substitute, auxiliary|反义词:primary, main, principal|词性:名词, 形容词, 动词
