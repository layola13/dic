bumblebee:/ˈbʌmbəlˌbiː/ (US), /ˈbʌmbəlbiː/ (UK)| n. |大黄蜂，熊蜂|A large hairy bee with a loud hum, often nesting in the ground.|The bumblebee buzzed lazily around the flowers. 大黄蜂绕着花朵懒洋洋地嗡嗡叫。|Synonyms: humble-bee, wild bee|Antonyms: None|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From *bummelen* (to buzz, hum, drone) + bee.  The word *bumble* imitates the sound the bee makes.  来自*bummelen*（嗡嗡叫，哼哼，发出嗡嗡声）+ bee。 *bumble*这个词模仿了蜜蜂发出的声音。
