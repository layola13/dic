delimitation:/dɪˌlɪmɪˈteɪʃn/
n.
限定; 界定; 划分
the act of setting or marking the limits or boundaries of something.
The delimitation of the border between the two countries was a long and complex process.  两国之间边界划定是一个漫长而复杂的过程。
The report recommends a clear delimitation of the roles and responsibilities of each department.  报告建议明确界定每个部门的角色和职责。
近义词: demarcation, definition, boundary
反义词: indefiniteness, vagueness


词性: noun
