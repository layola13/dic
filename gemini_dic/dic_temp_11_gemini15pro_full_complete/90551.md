huger:/ˈhjuːdʒər/| adj.|更大的，巨大的|Comparative form of huge.  Significantly large in size, extent, or degree. |The new stadium is huger than the old one. 新的体育场比旧的要大得多。|The impact of the pandemic was huger than anyone anticipated.  疫情的影响比任何人预期的都要大。|近义词：larger, bigger, greater, more massive, vaster|反义词:smaller, tinier, minuscule|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Middle English *huger*, comparative of *huge*, from Old French *huge*, of uncertain origin. Possibly from Late Latin *hugius*, comparative of a hypothetical *hugus*, perhaps related to Frankish *hūg*, "mind, spirit, courage".
