bushiest:/ˈbʊʃiɪst/| adj.|最茂密的|Having the most bushes; most densely covered with bushes.  Characterized by thick, bushy growth.|The bushiest eyebrows I've ever seen were on that old gardener. 我见过的最浓密的眉毛就在那位老园丁脸上。|Synonyms: densest, thickest, fullest|Antonyms: sparsest, thinnest, barest|adjective
