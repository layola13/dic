hypogonadotrophic:/ˌhaɪpəʊˌɡɒnədəˈtrɒfɪk/
adjective
促性腺激素不足的
Characterized by deficient secretion of gonadotropins from the pituitary gland.
Hypogonadotrophic hypogonadism is a condition in which the gonads (testes or ovaries) produce little or no sex hormones.  这是一种性腺（睾丸或卵巢）几乎或根本不产生性激素的疾病。
Hypogonadotrophic hypogonadism can be caused by a variety of factors. 促性腺激素不足性性腺功能减退症可能由多种因素引起。
近义词:hypogonadal
反义词:hypergonadotrophic
词性:adjective
