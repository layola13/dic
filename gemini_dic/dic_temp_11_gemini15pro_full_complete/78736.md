giggot:/ˈɡɪɡət/| n. |轻佻女子，傻丫头|A frivolous or silly girl. | She giggled and acted like a giggot throughout the entire play. 她咯咯地笑着，整场戏都像个傻丫头一样。|  He called her a giggot, which she found offensive. 他叫她傻丫头，她觉得很冒犯。 |近义词：flirt, minx, silly girl |反义词: serious woman, mature woman |词性:noun


词源:
Originates from the Middle English word "gigge" or "giglot," meaning "a wanton girl."  Ultimately of uncertain origin, possibly related to Old Norse "geggjaðr" (flighty).
