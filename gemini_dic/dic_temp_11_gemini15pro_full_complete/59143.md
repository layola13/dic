dynamometer:/ˌdaɪnəˈmɒmɪtər/ (US) /ˌdaɪnəˈmɒmɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|测力计，功率计|An instrument for measuring force, torque, or power.|例句：The engineer used a dynamometer to measure the engine's output. 工程师用测力计测量发动机的输出功率。

例句：The dynamometer registered a significant increase in force. 测力计记录到力的显著增加。

近义词：force gauge, power meter|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
