mythological:/ˌmɪθəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ (US) /ˌmɪθəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/
adjective
神话的；神话学的
Relating to, based on, or appearing in myths or mythology.
例句：The story is filled with mythological creatures like dragons and unicorns.  这个故事充满了像龙和独角兽这样的神话生物。

例句：Ancient Greek culture is rich in mythological stories and symbolism. 古希腊文化富含神话故事和象征意义。


近义词：mythical, legendary, fabled, fabulous
反义词：real, historical, factual
词性: adjective
