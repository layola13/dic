marcher:/ˈmɑːrtʃər/ (US), /ˈmɑːtʃə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 行军者，游行者，示威者|A person who marches, especially in a protest or demonstration. |例句：The marchers carried banners and chanted slogans. 游行者们举着横幅，高呼口号。

例句：Thousands of marchers gathered in the city square. 数千名游行者聚集在城市广场上。|近义词：protester, demonstrator, parader|反义词: bystander, spectator|词性:noun
