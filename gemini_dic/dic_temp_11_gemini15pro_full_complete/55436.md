distinctive:/dɪˈstɪŋktɪv/| adj.|独特的，有特色的，与众不同的|Serving as a distinguishing mark; characteristic.  Having a quality or characteristic that makes a person or thing different from others and easily noticed.|Her distinctive voice made her easily recognizable. 她的独特嗓音使她很容易被认出来。|近义词：characteristic, unique, individual, peculiar, special|反义词：common, ordinary, usual, typical|词性:adjective
Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin distinctivus, from distinct- ‘distinguished’ (see distinct).
