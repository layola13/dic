flashing:/ˈflæʃɪŋ/| adj. v. n.|闪烁的，闪光的； 闪光，闪现|A flashing light is one that shines brightly and then goes dark repeatedly.  A sudden, brief, and intense display of something.|
The flashing lights of the police car caught my attention. 警车的闪光灯引起了我的注意。|
The photographer captured a flashing moment of joy on the bride's face. 摄影师捕捉到了新娘脸上闪现的喜悦瞬间。|
近义词：blinking, flickering, gleaming, glittering, sparkling, twinkling|
反义词：steady, continuous, dim|
词性: adjective, verb, noun|
词源：From Old English *flæscian "to spread out," related to Old Norse flaska "flask, bottle." The sense of "shining intermittently" developed later, likely in reference to a sudden burst of light.|
