in-trust:/ɪn ˈtrʌst/ | adj. | 受托的，信托的 | Held or managed by someone for the benefit of another person or people. |  The money was held in-trust for his grandchildren.  这笔钱由他人代为保管，以备他的孙辈使用。 | 近义词: in escrow, fiduciary | 反义词: outright, absolute | 词性: 形容词

in trust:/ɪn ˈtrʌst/ | n. | 信托，信托财产 | The state of being held or managed by someone for the benefit of another person or people. | The property was placed in trust until the children reached adulthood.  该财产被置于信托管理之下，直到孩子们成年。 | 近义词: trusteeship, guardianship | 反义词: ownership, possession | 词性: 名词
