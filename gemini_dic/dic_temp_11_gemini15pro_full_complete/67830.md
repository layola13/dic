eyliad: /ˈaɪliəd/ | n. | 眼睑; 睫毛 | An eyelid; eyelash.  Sometimes used poetically or archaically. | Her long eyliads fluttered as she spoke. 她说话时，长长的睫毛颤动着。 | 近义词: eyelid, lash | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

词源: From Middle English *eielid, from Old English ēaglid ("eyelid"), from ēage ("eye") + lid ("lid, cover"). Cognate with Scots eelid ("eyelid"), West Frisian eachlid ("eyelid"), Dutch ooglid ("eyelid"), German Augenlid ("eyelid").
