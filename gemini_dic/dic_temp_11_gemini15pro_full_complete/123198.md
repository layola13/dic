monochord: /ˈmɒnəkɔːd/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnəkɔːrd/ (US)
n.|单弦琴；单弦乐器|An ancient musical instrument with a single string stretched over a soundbox, used for acoustical experiments and demonstrations, particularly in the study of musical intervals.|The scientist used a monochord to demonstrate the principles of harmony.|近义词:sonometer|反义词:polychord|词性:名词
