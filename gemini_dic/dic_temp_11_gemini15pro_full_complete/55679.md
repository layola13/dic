ditto:/ˈdɪtoʊ/ (US) /ˈdɪtəʊ/ (UK)| adv. n. adj.| 同上，同上符号，复制| Used to indicate that something is the same as what has just been mentioned. |例句：The second cup of coffee was as good as the first – ditto the cake. 第二杯咖啡和第一杯一样好，蛋糕也一样。|近义词：same, likewise, similarly, identically|反义词: differently, oppositely|词性: 副词 (adverb), 名词 (noun), 形容词 (adjective)


ditto:/ˈdɪtoʊ/ (US) /ˈdɪtəʊ/ (UK)| n.| 同上符号 (")| A symbol (") used to indicate that something is the same as what has just been mentioned. |例句：Enter the amount in the first column and then dittoes across the rest of the sheet. 在第一列输入金额，然后在表格的其余部分填写同上符号。|近义词：quotation mark, double inverted comma|反义词: N/A|词性: 名词 (noun)


ditto:/ˈdɪtoʊ/ (US) /ˈdɪtəʊ/ (UK)| adj.| 同上的| Being the same as what has just been mentioned. |例句：He ordered a ditto copy of the contract. 他订购了一份相同的合同副本。|近义词：identical, duplicate, same|反义词: different|词性: 形容词 (adjective)
