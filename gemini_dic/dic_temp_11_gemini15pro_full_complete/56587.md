donable:/ˈdoʊnəbl/| adj.|可捐赠的，可给予的|Suitable or worthy of being donated.  Something that is donable is something you can give to charity or another person.|例句：These old clothes are still donable. 这些旧衣服仍然可以捐赠。|例句：The furniture was old but still donable. 这些家具虽然旧了，但仍然可以捐赠。|近义词：giftable, charitable|反义词:unusable, worthless|词性:形容词
词源: From "donate" + "-able". "Donate" comes from the Latin "donare," meaning "to give."  "-able" is a suffix that forms adjectives meaning "capable of being" or "worthy of being."
