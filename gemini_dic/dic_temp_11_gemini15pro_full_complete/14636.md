avoids:/əˈvɔɪdz/| v.| 避免，避开，躲避 |  keeps away from deliberately; refrains from.| She avoids eating junk food. 她避免吃垃圾食品。 |近义词：evades, shuns, dodges, eschews |反义词: confronts, faces, seeks | verb (third-person singular simple present avoids, present participle avoiding, simple past and past participle avoided)

词源: From Old French *esvuidier, from vuide "empty".
