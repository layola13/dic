darmstadtium:/ˌdɑːrmˈʃtætɪəm/| n.|鐽（一种人工合成的放射性化学元素）|A transuranium chemical element of atomic number 110, created by bombarding lead nuclei with nickel nuclei.  It is highly radioactive and only minute quantities have been produced.|Darmstadtium is a synthetically produced element, and its most stable isotope has a half-life of about 10 seconds.|近义词: (无，因为是独特的元素) |反义词: (无，因为是独特的元素)|词性:名词
