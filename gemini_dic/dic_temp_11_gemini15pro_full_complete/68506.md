fanatic:/fəˈnætɪk/ | n. adj. | 狂热者，狂热的 | A person filled with excessive and single-minded zeal; excessively enthusiastic. | He's a football fanatic and never misses a game. 他是个足球狂热分子，从不错过任何一场比赛。 |  n. enthusiast, zealot, devotee | n. moderate,  apathist | noun
fanatic:/fəˈnætɪk/ | adj. | 狂热的，入迷的 | Marked by excessive enthusiasm and often intense uncritical devotion. |  She's fanatical about keeping her house clean. 她对保持房子清洁非常狂热。| adj. zealous, fervent, passionate | adj. moderate, indifferent, apathetic | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *fanaticus*, from Latin *fanaticus* "inspired by a deity, frenzied," from *fanum* "temple".  Originally referred to persons inspired by a deity, later extended to anyone with excessive enthusiasm.
