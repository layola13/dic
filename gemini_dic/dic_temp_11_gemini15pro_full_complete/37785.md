近义词：appear, emerge, rise | 反义词: disappear, set, vanish | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *comparen, from Old French comperer, conparer (“to appear, be visible”), from Latin comparēre (“to appear, be visible”), present active infinitive of compareō, from com- (“together”) + -pareō (“appear, become visible”).
