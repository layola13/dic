muddlehead:/ˈmʌdəlˌhɛd/| n.|糊涂虫，笨蛋|A person who is confused and disorganized; someone who makes silly mistakes or has difficulty thinking clearly. |例句：He's such a muddlehead, he always forgets where he put his keys. 他真是个糊涂虫，总是忘记把钥匙放在哪里了。

例句：Don't be such a muddlehead! Think before you act. 别这么糊涂！三思而后行。|近义词：scatterbrain, airhead, ditz|反义词：genius, mastermind|词性: Noun
