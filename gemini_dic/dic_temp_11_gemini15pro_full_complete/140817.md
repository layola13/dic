on-its-last-legs:/ɒn ɪts lɑːst lɛɡz/
adjective
奄奄一息的；临近结束的；快要报废的
Near the end of its usefulness or life.
My old car is on its last legs. 我的旧车快要报废了。
Synonyms:  worn out, decrepit, failing, dying, expiring
Antonyms:  new, brand-new, pristine, robust, thriving
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
This idiom likely originated from the image of a person or animal struggling to walk because their legs are weak and tired, thus nearing the end of their ability to function.  This image was then extended metaphorically to other things besides living beings.
