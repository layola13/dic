imperscrutable:/ɪmˌpɜːˈskruːtəbl/| adj.|难以理解的，神秘莫测的，不可捉摸的|not capable of being understood or known; impossible to interpret|Her face remained imperscrutable throughout the trial. 她的表情在整个审判过程中都难以捉摸。|近义词：enigmatic, mysterious, inscrutable|反义词：transparent, clear, obvious|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *imperscrutabilis, from in- "not" + perscrutabilis "able to be scrutinized," from perscrutari "to examine thoroughly," from per- "thoroughly" + scrutari "to search, examine."
