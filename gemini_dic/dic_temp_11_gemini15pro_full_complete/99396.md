interdifferentiating:/ˌɪntərˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃɪeɪtɪŋ/
verb
相互区分，互相区别
The process by which cells become specialized, developing into different types of cells with distinct functions.  This involves changes in gene expression and protein synthesis.
The researchers studied how cells are interdifferentiating during embryonic development.  研究人员研究了胚胎发育过程中细胞是如何相互区分的。
The team observed the interdifferentiating process of stem cells into various tissues.  该团队观察了干细胞分化成各种组织的过程。
