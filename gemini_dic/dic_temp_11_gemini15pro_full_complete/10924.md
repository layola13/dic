arango:/əˈræŋɡoʊ/ | n. | 阿兰戈（一种数据库）| A distributed, multi-model database developed by ArangoDB.  It supports three data models: key/value, document, and graph. |  Example: We chose ArangoDB for our project because we needed a database that could handle graph data. 我们选择 ArangoDB 用于我们的项目，因为我们需要一个可以处理图形数据的数据库。

Example: ArangoDB is known for its flexibility and performance.  ArangoDB 以其灵活性和性能而闻名。| 近义词: database, NoSQL database | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
