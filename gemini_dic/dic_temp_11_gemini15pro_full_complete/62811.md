enhances:/ɪnˈhɑːnsɪz/ | v. | 增强，提高，增进 | increases or improves the quality, value, or attractiveness of something |  Regular exercise enhances your overall health.  规律的运动能增强你的整体健康。 | intensifies, improves, boosts, strengthens | diminishes, weakens, reduces, detracts | verb
Etymology: From Old French "enhauncer," meaning "to raise," "to elevate." Ultimately derived from Latin "ante" (before) and "altiare" (to make high).
