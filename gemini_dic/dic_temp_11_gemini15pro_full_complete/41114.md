coproducers:/ˌkoʊprəˈdusərz/ | n. | 共同制片人 | People or companies who produce something together, such as a film, play, or album.  | Example 1: The two coproducers worked tirelessly to bring their vision to life. 两个共同制片人不知疲倦地工作，将他们的愿景变为现实。

Example 2: The coproducers shared the responsibility for the film's budget. 共同制片人共同承担电影预算的责任。 | 近义词: collaborators, joint producers | 反义词: sole producer | 词性: 名词
