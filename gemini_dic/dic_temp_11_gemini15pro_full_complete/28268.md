captivated:/ˈkæptɪveɪtɪd/| adj.|着迷的，被迷住的|Deeply interested and attracted; fascinated.  Feeling a strong sense of wonder and delight.|The audience was captivated by the magician's performance.  观众被魔术师的表演迷住了。|The children were captivated by the story. 孩子们被这个故事迷住了。|近义词：enthralled, fascinated, charmed, mesmerized|反义词：bored, uninterested, apathetic|词性:形容词

词源:
From the Latin word "captivatus," the past participle of "captivare," meaning "to capture" or "to enslave," from "captivus"  meaning "captive."
