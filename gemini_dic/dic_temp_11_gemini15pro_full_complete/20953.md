/ˈbliːpt/ | adj. | 消音的，被屏蔽的 | Censored or obscured, typically with a bleeping sound, to remove offensive or sensitive material. | The swear words in the broadcast were bleeped out.  广播中的脏话被消音了。 | synonyms: censored, muted, expurgated | antonyms: uncensored, unbleeped | adjective

/ˈbliːpt/ | v. | 发出哔哔声，消音 | To make a short, high-pitched electronic sound, often used to censor or obscure words. | The producer bleeped the offensive language. 制作人将攻击性语言消音了。 | synonyms: beep, censor | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider "broadcast uncensored") | verb
