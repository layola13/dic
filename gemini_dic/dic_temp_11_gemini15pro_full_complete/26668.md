cactoid:/ˈkæk.tɔɪd/
adjective
仙人掌状的；类似仙人掌的
Resembling a cactus; cactus-like.
The euphorbia looks very cactoid with its fleshy stems and thorns.  这种大戟属植物看起来很像仙人掌，有着肉质的茎和刺。
近义词:cactus-like, cactaceous
反义词:None
词性:adjective

cactoid:/ˈkæk.tɔɪd/
noun
仙人掌科植物
A plant of the cactus family.
Many cactoids thrive in arid environments. 许多仙人掌科植物在干旱环境中茁壮成长。
近义词:cactus
反义词:None
词性:noun
