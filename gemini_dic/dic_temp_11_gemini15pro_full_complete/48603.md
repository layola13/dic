defecting:/dɪˈfɛktɪŋ/
verb
背叛，叛逃，变节
abandoning one's country or cause in favor of an opposing one
The scientist was accused of defecting to the enemy.  这位科学家被指控叛逃到敌方。
defecting from the ruling party to join the opposition. 从执政党叛逃到反对党。

近义词: deserting, abandoning, forsaking, renouncing
反义词: supporting, joining, upholding, championing
词性: verb (present participle of defect)
