maslach:/ˈmɑːslɑːx/ | n. | 情绪衰竭，职业倦怠 | Maslach Burnout Inventory (MBI) is a widely used instrument to measure burnout.  |  例句：She was experiencing maslach from working too many hours. 她因为工作时间过长而感到倦怠。 | 近义词：burnout, exhaustion, fatigue | 反义词：engagement, enthusiasm, vigor | 词性：名词

词源：Named after Christina Maslach, an American social psychologist known for her research on job burnout. 源于美国社会心理学家克里斯蒂娜·马斯拉奇的名字，她以对职业倦怠的研究而闻名。
