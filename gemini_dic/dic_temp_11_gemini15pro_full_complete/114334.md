maize:/meɪz/| n. |玉米，玉蜀黍|A tall annual cereal grass (Zea mays) that is widely cultivated for its large elongated ears of starchy grain. This grain is also called maize.|Example: The farmer planted a field of maize. 农民种了一块玉米地。|Synonym: corn|Antonym: (No direct antonym for a specific plant)|noun
