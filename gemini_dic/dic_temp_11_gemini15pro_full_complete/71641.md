flats:/flæts/
n.
公寓，单元房
A set of rooms for living in, usually on one floor and part of a larger building.
The family live in a block of flats. 这家人住在一栋公寓楼里。

flats:/flæts/
n.
平底鞋
Women's shoes that do not have heels.
She prefers to wear flats for comfort. 她为了舒适喜欢穿平底鞋。

flats:/flæts/
n.
(尤指沿海的) 平地，低洼地
A large area of flat land.
The salt flats shimmered in the heat. 盐碱滩在高温下闪闪发光。

Etymology:  From Old English *flet "floor, hall, dwelling," of Germanic origin; related to Old Norse flatr. The sense of "apartment" is a shortening of "block of flats."

Synonyms: apartment, condominium, unit (for living space); loafers, ballet flats (for shoes); plains, lowlands (for land)
Antonyms: houses, detached homes (for living space); heels, high heels (for shoes); hills, mountains (for land)
Noun
