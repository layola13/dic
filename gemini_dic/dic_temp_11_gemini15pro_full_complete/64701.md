escapology:/ˌɛskəˈpɒlədʒi/
n.
逃脱术，逃脱学
The art or practice of escaping from confinement or restraints.
Houdini was a master of escapology.  胡迪尼是逃脱术大师。
escapology requires strength, skill, and ingenuity. 逃脱术需要力量、技巧和独创性。
近义词:escape artistry
反义词:confinement, imprisonment
词性:noun
