jangly:/ˈdʒæŋɡli/| adj.|刺耳的，叮当作响的，嘈杂的|Making a harsh, metallic ringing sound, like a lot of small bells clashing together.|The jangly sound of her bracelets annoyed him. 她手镯叮当作响的声音让他很烦。|近义词：clanging, clashing, rattling, tinkling|反义词：soft, muted, mellow|词性:形容词
词源:From jangle + -y.  "Jangle" itself likely imitates the sound.
