colleague:/ˈkɒliːɡ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːliːɡ/ (US)| n. |同事|A person with whom one works, especially in a profession or business.|例句：I had lunch with a colleague today. 我今天和一位同事一起吃了午饭。

例句：My colleagues and I worked late to finish the project. 我和我的同事们工作到很晚才完成这个项目。

近义词：co-worker, associate, peer|反义词:opponent, rival, competitor|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *collègue*, from Latin *collega* "partner in office," from *com-* "with" + *lega-* "one authorized to act," related to *lēx* "law."
