electroplated:/ɪˈlɛktrəˌpleɪtɪd/| adj.|电镀的|Coated with a thin layer of metal by electrolysis.  The process of depositing a metal coating on a surface through the use of electricity.|例句：The electroplated spoons have a beautiful silver finish.  这些电镀的勺子有漂亮的银色表面。|例句：The car's bumpers were electroplated with chromium for a shiny finish. 汽车的保险杠镀了铬，使其表面光亮。|近义词：plated, galvanized|反义词:unplated|词性:形容词
electroplate:/ɪˈlɛktrəˌpleɪt/| vt.  n.|电镀，镀层|To coat with a thin layer of metal by electrolysis. A thin layer of metal deposited by electroplating.|例句：They electroplate the jewelry with gold. 他们给珠宝镀金。|例句：The electroplate on the faucet is starting to wear off. 水龙头上的镀层开始脱落了。|近义词：plate, galvanize|反义词:strip|词性:动词, 名词
