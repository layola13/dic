gamin:/ˈɡæmɪn/ | n. |  流浪儿；顽童；野丫头 | A street urchin, especially a girl; a mischievous child. |  The gamin's quick wit and street smarts helped her survive on the streets. 这个流浪儿的机智和街头智慧帮助她在街上生存了下来。 | synonyms: urchin, waif, stray, ragamuffin | antonyms: sheltered child, pampered child | noun

gamin:/ˈɡæmɪn/ | adj. |  顽皮的；淘气的 | Playful, mischievous, especially in an appealing way. | The gamin smile on her face made it hard to stay angry. 她脸上顽皮的笑容让人很难生气。 | synonyms: mischievous, playful, impish, roguish | antonyms: well-behaved, obedient, demure | adjective
