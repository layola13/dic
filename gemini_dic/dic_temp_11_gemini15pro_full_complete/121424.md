misinformative: /ˌmɪsɪnˈfɔːrmətɪv/ | adj. | 误导性的，提供错误信息的 | Providing information that is incorrect or misleading. |

例句：
The article was misinformative and caused unnecessary panic.
这篇文章具有误导性，引起了不必要的恐慌。

The advertisement was misinformative, promising results that were impossible to achieve.
这则广告具有误导性，承诺了不可能达到的结果。


近义词： misleading, deceptive, inaccurate, fallacious, spurious
反义词： informative, accurate, truthful, factual, correct
词性： adjective


词源：
mis- (prefix): wrong, bad, wrongly  (来自古英语，意为“错误的”)
inform (verb): to give knowledge or facts  (来自拉丁语 informare, 意为“塑造，训练，教育”)
-ative (suffix): forming adjectives that describe something that has a particular quality or function  (来自拉丁语 -ativus)
