flyout:/ˈflaɪaʊt/| n.|弹出菜单，弹出式面板|A flyout is a graphical control element that appears or expands when a user interacts with a designated control or area of the user interface.  It typically provides additional options or information related to the interacted element.|The software has a useful flyout menu for quick access to tools.|近义词:popup, dropdown, context menu|反义词:(No direct antonym)|noun
