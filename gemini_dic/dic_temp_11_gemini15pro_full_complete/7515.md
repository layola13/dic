annealer:/əˈniːlər/ | n. | 退火炉，退火装置；韧化剂 |A device or person that anneals something, especially metals or glass. | The annealer slowly cooled the glass to prevent cracking. 退火炉缓慢地冷却玻璃以防止其破裂. | synonyms: tempering furnace, annealing oven, lehr | antonyms: hardener | noun

anneal:/əˈniːl/ | v. | 退火；使（金属、玻璃）韧化；使变软；使缓和；使平静 | To heat (metal or glass) and allow it to cool slowly, in order to remove internal stresses and toughen it.  To make something less strong or intense. | The metal was annealed to increase its ductility.  这种金属经过退火处理以增加其延展性。 The terrible experience annealed his spirit.  可怕的经历磨练了他的精神。| synonyms: temper, toughen, soften, moderate, calm | antonyms: harden, strengthen, intensify | verb
