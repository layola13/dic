alabastrine:/ˌæləˈbæstrɪn/ | adj. | 雪白的；光滑的；半透明的 |  Made of or resembling alabaster; smooth, white, and somewhat translucent.  | Her skin was alabastrine, almost glowing in the dim light. 她的皮肤雪白，在昏暗的光线下几乎闪闪发光。

|  The statue was carved from alabastrine marble. 这座雕像是用光滑的白色大理石雕刻而成的。 |  Near synonyms:  pale, white, pearly, ivory, translucent | Antonyms: dark, opaque, colorful | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *alabastrinus*, from alabaster + -inus (suffix forming adjectives). Alabaster itself comes from Greek *ἀλάβαστρος (alabastros)*, ultimately of unknown origin, perhaps Egyptian.
