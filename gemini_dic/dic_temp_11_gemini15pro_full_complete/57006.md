double-parked:/ˈdʌbəlˈpɑːrkt/| adj.|双排停车的|Parked alongside another vehicle that is already parked at the curb, thus blocking it.|Example 1: The delivery truck was double-parked, blocking traffic.  送货卡车双排停车，挡住了交通。

Example 2:  I had to double-park for a minute to run into the store. 我不得不双排停车一会儿，跑到商店里去。|近义词：illegally parked, improperly parked|反义词: legally parked, properly parked|词性:形容词

double-park:/ˈdʌbəlˈpɑːrk/| v.|双排停车|To park a vehicle alongside one that is already parked at the curb.|Example 1: Don't double-park; it's illegal. 不要双排停车；这是违法的。

Example 2: He double-parked in front of the restaurant to pick up his takeout order. 他在餐馆前双排停车去取外卖。|近义词：park illegally, park improperly|反义词: park legally, park properly|词性:动词
