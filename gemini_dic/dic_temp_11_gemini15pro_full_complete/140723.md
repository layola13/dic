omniparient: /ˌɒm.nɪˈpeə.ri.ənt/ | adj. |无所不在的，遍在的 | Present everywhere at the same time; ubiquitous.  Derived from "omni" (all) + "parient" (present, from Latin "pariens," present participle of "parere," to bring forth, be visible). | God is often described as omniparient. 上帝常被描述为无所不在的。| The omniparient narrator knows the thoughts and feelings of all the characters.  无所不在的叙述者知道所有角色的想法和感受。 | 近义词: ubiquitous, omnipresent, all-pervading | 反义词: localized, limited, restricted | 词性: 形容词
