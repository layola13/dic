barrel:/ˈbærəl/ (US), /ˈbærəl/ (UK)| n. v. |桶；枪管；一桶的量|A cylindrical container bulging in the middle, traditionally made of wooden staves bound by hoops and having flat ends.|The wine was aged in oak barrels.  葡萄酒在橡木桶中陈酿。|近义词：cask, keg, drum|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "container" in a broader sense could be considered)|noun, verb


barrel:/ˈbærəl/ (US), /ˈbærəl/ (UK)| v. |快速移动；把…装桶|To move very fast; to put into barrels.|He barreled down the hallway. 他飞奔穿过走廊。|近义词：rush, speed, hurtle|反义词: crawl, dawdle|verb
