decimalises:/ˈdɛsɪməlaɪzɪz/
verb
使十进制化；改用十进制
To convert (a system of weights, measures, or currency) to a decimal system.
Many countries decimalized their currency in the 20th century.  许多国家在20世纪将货币十进制化。
The UK decimalised its currency in 1971. 英国于1971年将其货币十进制化。
Synonym: metrify
Antonym: ---
词性:动词
