messiest:/ˈmesiɪst/ | adj. | 最脏乱的，最凌乱的 | Superlative form of messy: most messy.  Means extremely untidy or disorganized. | Her room is the messiest I've ever seen. 她的房间是我见过的最乱的。| The messiest desk in the office belongs to John. 办公室里最乱的桌子是约翰的。| 近义词: dirtiest, untidiness, chaotic, cluttered | 反义词: neatest, tidiest, cleanest, organized | 词性: 形容词

词源: From mess + -y + -est.  "Mess" comes from Old French "mes," meaning a portion of food.  Over time, it evolved to mean a disordered or dirty state.
