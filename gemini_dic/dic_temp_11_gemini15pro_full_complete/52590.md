digamous: /ˈdɪɡəməs/
adjective
再婚的；二次结婚的
Having married twice; twice married.
Henry VIII was digamous, having married six times.  亨利八世结过两次婚，一共结了六次婚。（此处例句略有不妥，应该理解为“结过多次婚”，因为digamous强调的是“两次”，而亨利八世结了六次）

He was a digamous man, having found love again after the loss of his first wife. 他是一个再婚的男人，在失去第一任妻子后再次找到了爱情。

synonym: remarried, twice-married
antonym: single, unmarried, monogamous
word type: adjective
