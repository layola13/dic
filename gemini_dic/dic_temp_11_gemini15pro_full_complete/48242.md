decontrol:/ˌdiːkənˈtroʊl/
n.
解除管制
The act of removing government controls from something, typically prices or wages.
The government announced the decontrol of gasoline prices. 政府宣布解除汽油价格管制。

v.
解除…的管制
To remove government controls from something, typically prices or wages.
The government decided to decontrol the currency. 政府决定解除货币管制。


近义词:deregulate, liberalize
反义词:control, regulate
词性: 名词, 动词
