grabble:/ˈɡræbəl/| v. n.| 抓取，攫取；摸索，搜寻|To seize or grasp suddenly and roughly; to grope or search blindly or awkwardly. |He grappled with the controls of the aircraft. 他努力控制飞机。|近义词：grope, fumble, grab, snatch|反义词:release, let go|词性:动词, 名词
grabble:/ˈɡræbəl/| n. | 抓取，攫取|The act of grabbing or groping.|He made a grabble for the falling vase. 他伸手去抓落下的花瓶。|近义词：grab, snatch, clutch|反义词:release|词性:名词
