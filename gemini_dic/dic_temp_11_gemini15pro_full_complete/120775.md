misappreciated: adj.
misappreciated: 未被充分赏识的，被低估的
misappreciated: Not fully recognized or valued; underestimated.
misappreciated:  Her talents were misappreciated by her former employer.  她的才能被她以前的雇主低估了。
misappreciated:  His contributions to the project were sadly misappreciated. 他对这个项目的贡献被可悲地低估了。
misappreciated: undervalued, underestimated, unacknowledged, overlooked, neglected
misappreciated: appreciated, valued, recognized, acknowledged, esteemed
misappreciated: adjective
