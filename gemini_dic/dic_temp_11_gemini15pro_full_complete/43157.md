cozying:/ˈkoʊziɪŋ/| v.|使舒适，使温暖；依偎，亲昵|The couple were cozying up by the fire.|这对夫妇依偎在火炉旁取暖。|近义词：snuggling, cuddling, nestling|反义词:distancing, separating|动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From cozy + -ing (present participle suffix). Cozy itself likely originates from Scots cozie, of unknown origin.
