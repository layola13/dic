exegetics:/ˌɛksɪˈdʒɛtɪks/ | n. | 释经学;  圣经注释 | The critical explanation or interpretation of a text, especially of scripture. |  He specialized in the exegetics of the New Testament. 他专攻新约圣经释经学。 | synonyms: interpretation, exposition, explanation, elucidation, commentary | antonyms: obfuscation, mystification | noun
