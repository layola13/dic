hidy-holes:/ˈhɪdi həʊlz/ (US), /ˈhaɪdi həʊlz/ (UK)| n. | 藏身处，隐蔽处 | A hiding place; a place of concealment or refuge. |  The squirrels had several hidy-holes in the garden where they stashed their nuts. 松鼠在花园里有好几个藏坚果的隐蔽处。

The smugglers used the cave as a hidy-hole for their contraband. 走私者用这个洞穴作为藏匿违禁品的藏身处。| 近义词：hiding places, hideouts, shelters, refuges | 反义词: exposed areas, open spaces | 词性: 名词
