ostracion:/ɒˈstreɪʃən/ | n. |放逐，排斥，孤立 |Exclusion, banishment, or isolation. The state of being ostracized.  |例句：He suffered ostracion for his unpopular beliefs. 他因其不受欢迎的信仰而遭受排斥。

|例句：Her unconventional lifestyle led to her ostracion from the community. 她非传统的生活方式导致她被社区排斥。

|例句：They were ostracized for their radical views. 他们因为激进的观点而被排斥。
