eruptions:/ɪˈrʌpʃənz/
|n.|喷发；爆发；（火山）爆发；（感情）迸发|A sudden outbreak of something, typically violent or unpleasant, such as lava from a volcano, spots on the skin, or violence.|Volcanic eruptions can be incredibly destructive. 火山爆发具有极强的破坏力。|
|近义词：outbursts, explosions, emissions|反义词：suppressions, containments|词性：名词（复数）
