antiliterate:/ˌæntiˈlɪtərɪt/| adj.|反 literacy 的；反对文化知识的；|Opposed to literacy or knowledge.  Someone who is antiliterate actively opposes the spread of literacy and learning.|The regime's antiliterate policies kept the population in ignorance.  该政权的反 literacy 政策使民众处于无知状态。|The antiliterate leader banned books and schools.  这位反 literacy 的领导人禁止书籍和学校。|近义词：ignorant, philistine, uneducated|反义词：literate, educated, learned|词性: adjective

antiliteracy:/ˌæntiˈlɪtərəsi/| n.|对文化知识的反对；|Opposition to literacy or the spread of knowledge.|The government's antiliteracy stance hindered the country's development. 政府对文化知识的反对阻碍了国家的发展。|Antiliteracy is a significant barrier to social progress. 对文化知识的反对是社会进步的重大障碍。|近义词：ignorance, illiteracy|反义词：literacy, education|词性: noun
