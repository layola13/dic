methuselahs:/məˈθuːzələz/
noun
玛土撒拉,高寿的人,寿星;（尤指）非常古老的东西
A person of great age; something very old.
Methuselahs like him remember the old days. 像他这样的老寿星还记得过去的日子。
Near Synonyms: centenarians, ancients, old-timers
Antonyms: youngsters, children, infants
