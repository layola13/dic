mugwumpian:/ˈmʌɡwʌmpiən/| adj. n.|独立的，中立的，不偏不倚的；(尤指) 骑墙派，独立人士|A person who remains aloof or independent, especially from party politics; relating to such a person.  Originally, a Republican who refused to support James G. Blaine, the party's candidate for president in 1884.|He took a mugwumpian stance on the issue, refusing to side with either faction. 他在这个问题上采取了骑墙派的立场，拒绝支持任何一方。|Near synonyms: neutral, independent, nonpartisan, uncommitted, aloof|Near antonyms: partisan, committed, biased, prejudiced|adjective, noun
