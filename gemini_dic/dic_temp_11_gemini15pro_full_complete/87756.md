hezekiah:/ˌhezɪˈkaɪə/ | n. |希西家|A biblical name, meaning "Yahweh strengthens" or "Yahweh has strengthened".  It refers to a king of Judah, known for his religious reforms and defiance of the Assyrian empire.|Hezekiah was a righteous king. 希西家是一位正直的国王。|Synonym: king, ruler |Antonym: subject, follower |noun
