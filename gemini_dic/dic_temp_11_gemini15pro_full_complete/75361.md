frothing:/ˈfrɒθɪŋ/ (US) /ˈfrɑːθɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|起泡沫的，愤怒的|Covered with or resembling froth; foaming. Also, informally, extremely angry or agitated.|The barista handed me a frothing latte. 咖啡师递给我一杯起泡的拿铁。|The fans were frothing at the decision.  球迷们对这个决定怒不可遏。|近义词：foaming, bubbling, seething, fuming|反义词：calm, still|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From froth + -ing.  Froth comes from Old Norse *froða, related to Old English āfrēoðan "to froth," from Proto-Germanic *freuþanan.
