overimported:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈpɔːrtɪd/ | adj. | 过度进口的 | Imported in excessive quantities. | The market was overimported with cheap electronics, driving down prices for domestic manufacturers. 市场过度进口廉价电子产品，压低了国内制造商的价格。| The country's economy suffered due to overimported goods.  这个国家的经济因过度进口商品而遭受损失。 | 近义词：oversupplied, glutted | 反义词: underimported, undersupplied | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "imported" (past participle of "import," from Latin "importare," meaning "to bring in")
