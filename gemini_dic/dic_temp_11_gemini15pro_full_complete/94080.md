impaste:/ɪmˈpeɪst/ | v. | 涂厚颜料;  使糊状 | To apply thickly, as paint; to cover with a thick layer; to make into a paste. | The artist impastoed the canvas with vibrant colours. 艺术家在画布上厚厚地涂上了鲜艳的色彩。|  The chef impastoed the ingredients into a smooth paste.  厨师把原料调成光滑的糊状。 | 近义词: daub, plaster, smear | 反义词: thin, dilute |词性: 动词
