chymistry: /ˈkɪmɪstri/ | n. | 炼金术; 化学（旧称） | An early form of chemistry, often involving the attempt to turn base metals into gold.  It is an archaic term, largely replaced by "chemistry". |  He studied chymistry in hopes of discovering the elixir of life. 他学习炼金术，希望能发现长生不老药。 |  The old books were filled with strange chymistry symbols. 这些古书上充满了奇怪的炼金术符号。 | 近义词: alchemy | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps modern chemistry) | 词性: noun
