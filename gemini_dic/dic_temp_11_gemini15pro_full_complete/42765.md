countrymade:/ˈkʌntrimɛɪd/| adj.|乡村制造的；粗糙的；土气的|Made in the countryside; rustic; unsophisticated.  Often implies simple or crude construction.|例句：She wore a countrymade dress. 她穿着一件乡村制造的连衣裙。|例句：The furniture was countrymade, but sturdy.  家具是乡村制造的，但很结实。|近义词：rustic, homespun, homemade, simple, crude|反义词：sophisticated, elegant, refined, factory-made|词性:形容词
