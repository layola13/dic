bastille:/bæˈstiːl/| n.|巴士底狱|A fortress in Paris, used as a state prison and armory, stormed by the populace on July 14, 1789, at the start of the French Revolution.|The storming of the Bastille marked a turning point in the French Revolution.|近义词：fortress, prison, jail|反义词:freedom, liberty|noun
