anti-priesthood:/ˌæntiˈpriːsthʊd/| adj. n.|反神职人员的；反僧侣统治的；反对神权统治|Opposed to the power or influence of priests or the priesthood; relating to such opposition.  It describes a sentiment or movement that challenges the authority, role, or practices of priests or a priestly class.| The anti-priesthood movement gained traction in the 18th century. 十八世纪，反神职人员运动获得了广泛支持。| The writer’s anti-priesthood stance was evident in his satirical portrayal of the clergy.  这位作家对神职人员的反对立场在他对神职人员的讽刺描写中显而易见。|近义词：anticlerical, secular, laic|反义词: pro-clerical, sacerdotal|词性:adjective, noun

Etymology:
"anti-" (Greek) meaning "against" + "priesthood" (Old English "prēosthād") meaning "the state or office of a priest"
