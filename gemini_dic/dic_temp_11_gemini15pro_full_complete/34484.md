claying:/ˈkleɪɪŋ/
verb
涂黏土;用黏土处理
The process of applying clay to a surface, often a car's paint, to remove contaminants.
He was claying his car to remove embedded dirt. 他正在用黏土处理他的车以去除嵌入的污垢。
近义词: detailing, polishing
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps neglecting, ignoring maintenance)
词性: Verb (Gerund or present participle of the verb "to clay")
