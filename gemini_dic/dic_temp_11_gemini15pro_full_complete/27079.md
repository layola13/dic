calibrated:/ˈkælɪbreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 校准的，标定的 |  Adjusted or checked against a standard to ensure accuracy. | The instrument was calibrated before the experiment.  仪器在实验前进行了校准。| synonyms: standardized, adjusted, measured, gauged | antonyms: uncalibrated, inaccurate, imprecise | adjective


calibrate:/ˈkælɪbreɪt/ | v. | 校准，标定 | To check or adjust (something, especially a measuring instrument) by comparison with a standard. | We need to calibrate the equipment regularly to ensure accurate readings. 我们需要定期校准设备以确保读数准确。 | synonyms: standardize, adjust, measure, gauge | antonyms: miscalibrate | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From French calibrer, from Italian calibrare "to measure the caliber of," from calibro "caliber," from Arabic qālib "mold."
