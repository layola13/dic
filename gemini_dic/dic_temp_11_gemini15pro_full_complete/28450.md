carbunculous: /kɑːrˈbʌŋkjʊləs/ | adj. |痈样的；似痈的；患痈的；红宝石色的|  Relating to or resembling a carbuncle (a severe abscess or multiple boil); fiery red.  | The old man's neck was marred by a carbunculous sore. 老人的脖子上有一个难看的痈样疮。|Synonyms: inflamed, festering, purulent, ruby, crimson | Antonyms: healthy, clear, unblemished | Adjective


carbuncle: /ˈkɑːrbʌŋkəl/ | n. |痈；红宝石；炭疽| A painful, localized bacterial infection of the skin and tissues beneath the skin; a deep red gemstone, typically a garnet.|  The doctor diagnosed the painful lump as a carbuncle. 医生诊断出这个疼痛的肿块是痈。| Synonyms: boil, abscess, ruby | Antonyms:  (For medical sense) health, (For gemstone sense) diamond, emerald | Noun
