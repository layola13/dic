iconographic:/ˌaɪkənəˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
图像学的；肖像的；图解的
Relating to symbolic representation, especially in art.  The study of the meaning of images.
例句：The altarpiece is rich in iconographic detail.  祭坛画充满了图像学的细节。

例句：The iconographic program of the church was carefully planned. 教堂的肖像画方案经过精心策划。
synonym:symbolic, pictorial, representative, emblematic
antonym:non-pictorial, abstract, non-representational
词性:adjective
