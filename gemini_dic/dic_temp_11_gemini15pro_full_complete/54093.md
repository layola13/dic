disembitter:/dɪsɪmˈbɪtər/
verb
消除苦涩，使不再怨恨
To free from bitterness; to sweeten; to make happy or contented.
After years of therapy, she finally managed to disembitter herself about her childhood.  多年治疗后，她终于不再怨恨自己的童年。
近义词: soothe, console, pacify, mollify, appease
反义词: embitter, aggravate, exacerbate
词性:v.

disembittered:/dɪsɪmˈbɪtərd/
adjective
不再怨恨的，消除苦涩的
Freed from bitterness.
He seemed a disembittered man after his long illness.  久病之后，他似乎不再那么怨天尤人了。
近义词: content, appeased, soothed
反义词: bitter, resentful
词性:adj.


词源:
dis- (prefix):  表示否定，相反，除去
embitter (verb):  使苦涩，使怨恨
