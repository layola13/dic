highlives: /ˈhaɪˌlaɪvz/
noun
high life (plural highlives)
奢华的生活；上流社会的生活
a life of luxury and pleasure, often involving a lot of social activity
They were living the high life in a luxury hotel. 他们在豪华酒店过着奢华的生活。

近义词: luxury, extravagance, opulence, lavishness, indulgence
反义词: poverty, austerity, modesty, simplicity, frugality
词性: noun (plural)
