antenuptial:/ˌæntɪˈnʌpʃəl/
adjective
婚前的
Relating to the period before marriage.
The couple signed an antenuptial agreement. 这对夫妇签了一份婚前协议。

The antenuptial celebrations were lavish. 婚前庆祝活动非常奢华。

近义词:prenuptial, premarital
反义词:postnuptial, postmarital
词性:adjective

词源: From Latin *ante- (before) + nuptialis (nuptial, pertaining to marriage).
