clairaudience:/ˌklɛərˈɔːdiəns/| n. | 超感知觉；超自然听觉；灵听 |The faculty of hearing sounds or words that are not audible to the normal ear.  The ability to perceive sounds or words psychically. | She claims to have the gift of clairaudience and can hear messages from the spirit world. 她声称拥有超自然听觉的天赋，可以听到来自灵界的信息。|  clairauditory perception, psychic hearing, spiritual hearing | - | noun
Etymology->
From French *clairaudience*, from clair (“clear”) + audience (“hearing”). Ultimately from Latin *clārus* (“clear”) + *audientia* (“hearing”).
