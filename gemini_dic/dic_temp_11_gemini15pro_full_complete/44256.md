crofter:/ˈkrɒftər/ | n. | 小佃农；(尤指苏格兰高地的)小农场主 | A tenant farmer of a small farm, especially in the Scottish Highlands. | The crofters worked hard to cultivate their small plots of land. 小佃农们辛勤耕耘着他们的小块土地。 | synonyms: tenant farmers, smallholders | antonyms: landlords, estate owners | noun
