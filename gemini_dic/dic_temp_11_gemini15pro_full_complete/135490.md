nonphonemic: /ˌnɒnfoʊˈniːmɪk/ /ˌnɑːnfoʊˈniːmɪk/
| adj. | 非音位的；非音素的 | Not relating to or based on the system of phonemes of a language; not distinctive within a particular language. | The difference in pronunciation between the two vowels is nonphonemic in English. 这两个元音的发音差异在英语中是非音位的。 | synonyms: non-distinctive, insignificant, irrelevant | antonyms: phonemic, distinctive, significant | adjective
