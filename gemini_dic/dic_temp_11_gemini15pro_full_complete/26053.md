bushier:/ˈbʊʃɪər/ | adj. | 更茂密的，更浓密的 | More bushy; having more bushes.  | The plant is bushier this year than last year. 今年这株植物比去年茂盛。| Near Synonyms: denser, thicker, fuller, more overgrown | Antonyms: sparser, thinner, less dense | adjective

bushy:/ˈbʊʃi/ | adj. | 茂密的，浓密的 | Resembling a bush; thick and spreading.  | He had a bushy beard. 他留着浓密的胡须。| Near Synonyms: thick, dense, full, luxuriant | Antonyms: sparse, thin, scanty | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From bush + -y (suffix forming adjectives meaning 'having the characteristics of', 'full of', 'covered with', 'tending to').  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *busk-, of uncertain origin.
