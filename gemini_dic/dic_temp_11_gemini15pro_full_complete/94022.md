impanelments:/ɪmˈpænəlmənts/
|n.|登记，陪审团的组成，列入陪审员名单|The act of impaneling a jury, or the list of jurors impaneled.  The process of selecting and swearing in a jury.|The judge ordered the impanelments of a new jury after the previous one was dismissed.  法官在前一个陪审团被解散后，下令组成新的陪审团。|enrollment, listing, selection| dismissal, discharge
|noun|
