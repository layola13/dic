discoblastic:/ˌdɪskoʊˈblæstɪk/
adjective
盘状卵裂的
Relating to or characterized by discoblastula, a type of blastula formed by discoidal cleavage, where the cleavage furrows do not extend through the vegetal pole and the embryo forms as a disc of cells on top of the yolk.
The zebrafish embryo exhibits discoblastic cleavage.  斑马鱼胚胎表现出盘状卵裂。
Discoblastic cleavage is typical in birds and reptiles. 盘状卵裂在鸟类和爬行动物中很典型。
近义词: meroblastic (部分卵裂的)
反义词: holoblastic (完全卵裂的)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From disco- (disc-shaped) + -blastic (relating to a blastula or early embryonic development).  来自disco-（盘状）+ -blastic（与囊胚或早期胚胎发育有关）。
