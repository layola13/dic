discursively:/dɪˈskɜːrsɪvli/
adv.
散漫地，离题地，推理地
in a manner that rambles from topic to topic, digressing from the main point; in a reasoned way.
He talked discursively about his travels, never staying on one subject for long.  他漫无边际地谈论他的旅行，从不在一个话题上停留太久。
The professor discursively explained the complex philosophical concepts. 教授通过推理的方式解释了复杂的哲学概念。
近义词: ramblingly, digressively, circuitously, diffusely, wordily
反义词: concisely, succinctly, directly, briefly
词性: 副词

discourse:/ˈdɪskɔːrs/
n.
演讲，论述，谈话
written or spoken communication or debate.
The academic discourse on climate change is extensive.  关于气候变化的学术讨论非常广泛。
Their discourse covered a wide range of topics, from politics to art. 他们的谈话涵盖了广泛的话题，从政治到艺术。
近义词: discussion, conversation, talk, dialogue
反义词: silence
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *discursus, from Latin discursus (“a running about, running to and fro”), past participle of discurrere (“to run about, run to and fro, run different ways”), from dis- (“apart, in different directions”) + currere (“to run”).
