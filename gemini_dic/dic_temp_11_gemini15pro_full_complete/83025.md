gunsmithing:/ˈɡʌnˌsmɪθɪŋ/
noun
枪械制造;枪械修理;枪匠的行业
The making and repairing of firearms.
He learned gunsmithing from his father. 他从他父亲那里学习了枪械制造。
近义词:armory, gunmaking
反义词:None
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From gunsmith + -ing. Gunsmith from gun + smith.  Smith from Old English smiþ, from Proto-Germanic *smiþaz.
