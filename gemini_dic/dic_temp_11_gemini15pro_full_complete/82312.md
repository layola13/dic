grouse:/ɡraʊs/| n. 松鸡；牢骚，抱怨|A type of game bird. / A complaint.|例句：The hunter bagged two grouse. 猎人捕获了两只松鸡。

例句：He had a grouse about the food. 他抱怨食物不好。|近义词：complaint, grumble, moan, beef, gripe|反义词：praise, compliment|词性:noun

grouse:/ɡraʊs/| vi. 抱怨，发牢骚|To complain.|例句：He's always grousing about something. 他总是抱怨一些事情。

例句：She groused about the long hours she had to work. 她抱怨她必须工作很长时间。|近义词：complain, grumble, moan, beef, gripe|反义词：praise, compliment|词性:verb


词源: From Old French *grouse, of uncertain origin. Possibly from a Vulgar Latin *grutaceus "of the grotto," referring to the bird's habitat, or from a Pre-Germanic *krup- "to make a harsh sound."
