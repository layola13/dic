melezitose:/ˌmelɪˈzaɪtoʊs/ | n. |蜜三糖|A trisaccharide sugar found in honeydew and manna. It is composed of two glucose units and one fructose unit. |Melezitose is a non-reducing sugar, meaning it does not react with Benedict's reagent.|近义词:trisaccharide (泛指任何三糖)|反义词: monosaccharide (单糖)|词性:noun
