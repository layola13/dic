discede:/dɪˈsiːd/| v. | 离开；退出；撤离 | to leave, especially to withdraw from an organization or from a policy or agreement |  The senator disceded from the party after a disagreement over policy. 这位参议员在政策上发生分歧后退出了该党。|  He disceded from the negotiations, feeling they were unproductive. 他退出了谈判，因为他觉得谈判没有成效。 | 近义词: withdraw, depart, leave, secede, quit | 反义词: join, enter, arrive | 词性: 动词
