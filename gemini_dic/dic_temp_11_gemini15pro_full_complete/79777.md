glutinously:/ˈɡluːtɪnəsli/| adv.|黏糊糊地；胶粘地|in a sticky, gluey manner; resembling glue|The rice was cooked glutinously, sticking together in a thick mass. 米饭煮得黏糊糊的，粘成一团。|近义词：stickily, adhesively|反义词: dryly, loosely|词性:副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *glūtinōsus, from Latin glūtin-, stem of glūten (“glue”) + -ōsus (suffix forming adjectives with the sense “full of, abounding in”).
