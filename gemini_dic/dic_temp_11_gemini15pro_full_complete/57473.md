drafts:/dræfts/ | n. | 草稿；汇票；通风；气流 | A preliminary version of a piece of writing or plan; an order for a bank to pay a specified sum to a named person or entity; a current of air. | The architect showed us the initial drafts of the building plans. 建筑师给我们看了建筑计划的初步草稿。 | synonyms: sketches, outlines, blueprints, bills of exchange | antonyms: final versions, finished products | noun

drafts:/dræfts/ | v. | 起草；征召 | To prepare a preliminary version of a document or plan; to select someone compulsorily, especially for military service. |  The committee drafted a new constitution. 委员会起草了一部新宪法。 | synonyms: compose, formulate, conscript, enlist | antonyms: finalize, publish, discharge | verb
