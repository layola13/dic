lto:/ˈɛltiːoʊ/
noun
线性磁带开放
Linear Tape-Open, a magnetic tape data storage technology.
LTO ultrium is a popular type of tape backup. LTO 磁带机是一种流行的磁带备份类型。
近义词: tape drive, magnetic tape
反义词: hard drive, SSD
词性:n.
