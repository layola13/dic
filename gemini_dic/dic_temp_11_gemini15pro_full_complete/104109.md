jumped-up:/ˌdʒʌmpt ˈʌp/| adj.|自命不凡的，暴发户式的；突然的，仓促的|Having suddenly risen to a higher social position than formerly; also, hastily or quickly arranged.|He’s just a jumped-up little office clerk. 他不过是个自命不凡的小职员。|Synonyms: pushy, pretentious, self-important, arrogant, uppity (especially US), parvenu |Antonyms: humble, modest|adjective


jumped-up:/ˌdʒʌmpt ˈʌp/| adj.|匆匆做成的，仓促完成的|Done or made quickly and without much care.|It was a jumped-up affair, planned in just a few days. 这件事做得太仓促了，仅仅计划了几天。|Synonyms: hasty, rushed, hurried, quick, speedy, swift, rapid, expeditious, flying, breakneck, pell-mell |Antonyms:  slow, deliberate, careful, meticulous|adjective
