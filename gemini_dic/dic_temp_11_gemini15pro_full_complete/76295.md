gabbiness:/ˈɡæbinəs/| n. |饶舌，多话，喋喋不休|The quality of being gabby; talkativeness.|Her gabbiness made it difficult to get a word in edgewise. 她太啰嗦了，别人根本插不上话。|Synonyms: talkativeness, loquacity, volubility|Antonyms: reticence, taciturnity|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From gabby + -ness.  Gabby itself likely originates from gab, meaning idle talk, of uncertain origin.
