deframe:/diːˈfreɪm/| vt.| 去框，拆除框架，打破常规| To remove from a frame; to remove from a rigid, limiting structure, often in a figurative sense.| The artist deframed the painting to clean the canvas. 艺术家把画从画框中取出来清洗画布。|  The new CEO wanted to deframe the company's traditional hierarchical structure. 新任首席执行官想要打破公司传统的等级结构。|  近义词： dismantle, disassemble, unframe, break down| 反义词： frame, construct, build|词性:动词

deframe:/diːˈfreɪm/| n.| 去框，拆除框架| The act of removing something from a frame, often figuratively referring to breaking free from constraints.| The deframe of the old policies allowed for more innovation. 旧政策的废除让更多创新成为可能。| The deframe of traditional thinking is essential for progress. 打破传统思维对进步至关重要。|近义词： dismantling, disassembly, unframing|反义词： framing, construction, building|词性:名词
