overguilty:/ˌoʊvərˈɡɪlti/ | adj. | 过分内疚的，过度负罪的 |  Feeling guilty to an excessive degree; experiencing guilt beyond what is appropriate or warranted.  |  She felt overguilty about forgetting her friend's birthday. 她对忘记朋友的生日感到过分内疚。|  Example: He was overguilty about the small mistake he made at work. 他对工作中犯的小错误感到过分内疚。 | Synonyms: excessively guilty, unduly guilty, overly guilty | Antonyms: unrepentant, remorseless, unapologetic |  Adjective
