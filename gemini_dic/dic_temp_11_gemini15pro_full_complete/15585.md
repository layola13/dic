bahaudur:/bəˈhɑːdʊr/| adj. n.|勇敢的，勇士|A Hindi and Urdu word meaning "brave" or "valiant." It can also be used as a title of respect, similar to "knight" or "hero."  It originates from Sanskrit *balavantu*, meaning "strong, powerful"|Example: He was a bahaudur soldier who fought fearlessly. 他是一位勇敢的士兵，作战英勇无畏。

Example:  The bahaudur led the charge against the enemy. 那位勇士带领冲锋对抗敌人。|近义词：brave, valiant, courageous, heroic, gallant|反义词: cowardly, timid, fearful|词性: adjective, noun
