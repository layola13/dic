hangdog:/ˈhæŋdɒɡ/ (UK) /ˈhæŋˌdɔːɡ/ (US)| adj.| 沮丧的，垂头丧气的，愧疚的|Having a dejected or guilty appearance; shamefaced.|He had a hangdog expression on his face after being caught cheating. 他被抓到作弊后，脸上带着一副沮丧的表情。|近义词：ashamed, dejected, guilty, sheepish|反义词：proud, cheerful, happy|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "hang" (meaning to droop) + "dog" (referring to a dog with its tail between its legs, a sign of submission or shame).  The image evokes a sense of dejection and guilt.
