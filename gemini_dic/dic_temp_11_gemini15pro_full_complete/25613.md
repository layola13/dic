bumpingly:/ˈbʌmpɪŋli/| adv.|颠簸地，崎岖不平地|Characterized by bumps or jolts; in a bumpy manner.|The car rode bumpingly along the dirt road. 汽车在土路上颠簸地行驶。|近义词：roughly, unevenly, jerkily|反义词:smoothly, evenly|词性:副词
词源:From bump + -ing + -ly.  "Bump" originates from Middle Low German or Middle Dutch "bumpen," meaning "to knock."
