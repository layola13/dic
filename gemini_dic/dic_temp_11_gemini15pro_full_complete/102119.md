isotopes:/ˈaɪsəˌtoʊps/| n. |同位素|Atoms of the same element that have different numbers of neutrons.|例句：Carbon-12 and Carbon-14 are isotopes of carbon. 碳-12和碳-14是碳的同位素。|例句：The radioactive isotopes are used in medical treatments. 放射性同位素用于医疗。|近义词：nuclide (all isotopes are nuclides, but not all nuclides are isotopes)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

isotopic:/ˌaɪsəˈtɑːpɪk/| adj. |同位素的|Relating to or having the characteristics of isotopes.|例句：Isotopic analysis can be used to determine the age of a sample. 同位素分析可以用来确定样本的年龄。|例句：Scientists use isotopic labeling to track the movement of molecules in biological systems. 科学家使用同位素标记来追踪生物系统中分子的运动。|近义词：N/A|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective
