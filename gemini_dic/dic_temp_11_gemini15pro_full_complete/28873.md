carpintero: /kɑːrpinˈtɛroʊ/ (American), /kɑːpɪnˈtɛəroʊ/ (British)| n. |木匠，木工|A person who makes and repairs wooden objects and structures.|例句：The carpintero skillfully crafted a beautiful table. 木匠熟练地制作了一张漂亮的桌子。

例句：We hired a carpintero to build a new shed in our backyard. 我们雇了一个木匠在我们后院建一个新的棚子。

近义词：woodworker, joiner, builder|反义词: N/A |词性: noun

注意事项:  Since "carpintero" is a loanword directly from Spanish, it doesn't have a true English etymology.  Its Spanish etymology comes from Late Latin *carpentārius, from Latin carpentum ("two-wheeled cart, carriage"). This refers to the historical association of carpenters with wagon-making.
