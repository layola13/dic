monologuists:/məˈnɒləɡɪsts/ /məˈnɑːləɡɪsts/
n.
独白者；进行独白的人
A person who delivers a monologue; a person who gives long speeches.
The play featured several talented monologuists, each captivating the audience with their individual stories.  这出戏有几位才华横溢的独白演员，每个人都以各自的故事吸引着观众。
The comedian is a skilled monologuist, able to hold the audience's attention for an hour with just his words.  这位喜剧演员是一位技艺娴熟的独白演员，能够仅凭他的言语就抓住观众一个小时的注意力。
speaker, orator, lecturer
listener, audience
noun
