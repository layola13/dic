ops:/ɑːps/ | interj. | 哎哟，糟糕 | Used as an exclamation of surprise or apology, especially when one has made a clumsy mistake. | Oops, I spilled my coffee. 哎哟，我咖啡洒了。| 近义词: whoops, uh-oh | 反义词: (No direct antonym for an exclamation) | 词性: interjection

ops:/ɑːps/ | n. | (informal) operations |  Short for "operations," referring to the practical work or activities of an organization, business, or system. |  The IT ops team is working on the server issue. IT运营团队正在处理服务器问题。 | 近义词: operations, activities | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun (abbreviation)
