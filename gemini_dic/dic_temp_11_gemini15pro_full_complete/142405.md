orvet:/ˈɔːrvɪt/| n. |无足蜥蜴;蛇蜥|A small, legless lizard of the family Anguidae, found in Europe.  It resembles a snake but has movable eyelids and ear openings, unlike snakes.|The orvet, though snake-like, is actually a lizard.|近义词：slow worm, blindworm, glass lizard|反义词:snake (loosely)|词性:noun
