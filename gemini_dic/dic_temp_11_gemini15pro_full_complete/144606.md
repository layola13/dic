outweighing:/ˌaʊtˈweɪɪŋ/
verb present participle
超过，胜过
being greater or more significant than something else
例句：The benefits of this project far outweighing the risks. 这个项目的好处远远超过风险。
例句：Her desire for a child outweighing all other considerations. 她想要孩子的愿望超过了所有其他考虑。
近义词：exceeding, surpassing, preponderating, overbalancing
反义词：outweighed, secondary to
词性：Verb (present participle)
