deteriorates:/dɪˈtɪəriəreɪts/
verb
恶化，变坏
to become worse in quality, character, or condition
The patient's condition deteriorated rapidly. 病人的病情迅速恶化。
worsen, decline, degenerate
improve, enhance, ameliorate
