overbookishness:/ˌoʊvərˈbʊkɪʃnəs/
n.
过度书生气；学究气
An excessive fondness for books or an overly scholarly or academic approach to life, often perceived as impractical or out of touch with reality.
He displayed a certain overbookishness, quoting obscure authors in casual conversation. 他表现出某种学究气，在闲聊中引用一些鲜为人知的作家。

Near synonyms:  pedantry, bookishness, scholasticism, intellectualism
Antonyms: pragmatism, practicality, worldliness, common sense
noun
