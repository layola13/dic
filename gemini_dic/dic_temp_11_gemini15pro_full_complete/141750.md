orangeroot:/ˈɔːrɪndʒˌruːt/ | n. |金毛菊，金鸡菊|A North American perennial plant (genus Hydrastis) having a thick knotted rootstock and small greenish-white flowers, and yielding a yellow dye and medicinal substances. |  The orangeroot is a valuable medicinal plant. 金毛菊是一种珍贵的药用植物。|  Orangeroot is also known as goldenseal. 金毛菊也被称为白毛茛。 | 近义词: goldenseal, yellowroot | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a plant name) | 词性: noun
