gardeners:/ˈɡɑːrdənərz/
n.
园丁，园艺师
People who cultivate or tend gardens.
The gardeners were busy planting new flowers.  园丁们正忙着种新花。
The head gardener is responsible for the entire estate. 首席园丁负责整个庄园。
horticulturists, landscapers
