ossified:/ˈɒsɪfaɪd/ (British) /ˈɑːsɪfaɪd/ (American)| adj.|僵化的；骨化的；硬化的|Having become rigid or inflexible, especially in attitudes or methods; turned into bone.|例句：His thinking has become completely ossified. 他的思想已经完全僵化了。

例句：The cartilage eventually ossifies into bone. 软骨最终骨化成骨。|近义词：rigid, inflexible, hardened, fixed, set, entrenched, dogmatic, unyielding, immutable|反义词：flexible, adaptable, open-minded, progressive, dynamic, fluid|词性：形容词
