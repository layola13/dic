commanderships:/kəˈmændərˌʃɪps/
n.
指挥权，司令部
The position or office of a commander.  The locations or establishments from which a commander exercises command.
He was given the commandership of the fleet. 他被授予了舰队的指挥权。
The general established his commandership near the front lines. 将军在前线附近设立了司令部。
synonyms: command, leadership, control, authority
antonyms: subordination, obedience
noun
