fenestration:/ˌfɛnɪˈstreɪʃən/| n.|开窗，窗户布置，门窗|The fenestration of the building was designed to maximize natural light.  这栋建筑的窗户布置旨在最大限度地利用自然光。|The large fenestration offered stunning views of the city.  巨大的窗户提供了令人惊叹的城市景观。|Synonym: glazing, windowing|Antonym: solid wall|noun

Etymology->
From Late Latin *fenestratio (“act of furnishing with windows”), from Latin fenestra (“window, opening”), from Proto-Italic *fenes-trā (“window, opening”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂wenh₁- (“to see, look out”).
