lispy:/ˈlɪspi/| adj.|说话不清的，咬舌的|Characterized by a lisp, a speech impediment where /s/ and /z/ are pronounced like /θ/ and /ð/ respectively.|She had a lispy way of talking that was quite endearing.|Near synonyms:  slurring, inarticulate|Antonyms: articulate, clear|adjective
