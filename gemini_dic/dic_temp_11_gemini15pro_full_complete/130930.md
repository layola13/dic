nonaspirate:/ˌnɒnˈæspɪrət/ /ˌnɑːnˈæspərət/
adjective
非送气的
A nonaspirate sound is one that is produced without a puff of air.
Example: The sound /b/ in "bin" is nonaspirate in English.  英语中“bin”里的/b/是非送气的。

Example:  The /g/ in "go" is nonaspirate for many English speakers. 对于许多英语使用者来说，“go”中的/g/是非送气的。
Synonym: unaspirated
Antonym: aspirated
词性: adjective
