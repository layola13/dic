groundcover:/ˈɡraʊndˌkʌvər/| n.|地被植物，覆盖物|A low-growing plant that spreads over the ground, used to prevent soil erosion and suppress weeds.  低矮的植物，在地面上蔓延，用于防止水土流失和抑制杂草生长。|The gardener planted groundcover to suppress weeds in the flower bed. 园丁在地里种植了地被植物来抑制杂草。|近义词：ground cover,  creeping plant, undergrowth|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "bare ground")|词性:noun

groundcover:/ˈɡraʊndˌkʌvər/| n.|覆盖物|Something used to cover the ground, such as leaves, straw, or plastic sheeting. 用于覆盖地面的东西，例如树叶、稻草或塑料布。|They used a plastic groundcover to protect the young seedlings. 他们用塑料覆盖物来保护幼苗。|近义词：covering, mulch, sheeting|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
