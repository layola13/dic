fiftyfold:/ˈfɪftiˌfoʊld/| adj. adv.|五十倍的，五十重的；五十倍地|Multiplied by fifty; being fifty times as great or as much.|The company's profits increased fiftyfold this year.  这家公司的利润今年增长了五十倍。|The investment yielded a fiftyfold return. 这项投资获得了五十倍的回报。|近义词：fifty times, quintuple (x5)|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以用分数表示，例如 one-fiftieth)|词性: 形容词/副词

词源:  From Old English *fīftigfeald (unattested), from fīftig "fifty" + -feald "-fold". Cognate with Dutch vijftigvoud, German fünfzigfach.
