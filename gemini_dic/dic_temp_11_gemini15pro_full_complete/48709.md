deficiency:/dɪˈfɪʃənsi/ | n. | 缺点，弱点 | a fault or weakness in something | The report highlighted deficiencies in the company's safety procedures. 报告强调了公司安全程序中的缺陷。 | synonyms: fault, flaw, weakness, shortcoming, imperfection | antonyms: strength, advantage, asset, merit | noun
