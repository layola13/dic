counter-marque:/ˈkaʊntərˌmɑːrk/ | n. | 对抗性品牌；竞争品牌；竞品 | A rival brand or product, especially one offered in competition with another. |  Example 1: The new phone is seen as a counter-marque to the leading brand. 这款新手机被视为领先品牌的竞品。

Example 2:  Their counter-marque strategy focuses on offering a lower price point. 他们的竞品策略专注于提供更低的价位。 | Synonym: competitor, rival, competing brand | Antonym: partner, ally | noun
