apollonian:/ˌæpəˈloʊniən/ | adj. |  和谐的，沉着的，理性的，优雅的 | Relating to the Greek god Apollo, characterized by harmony, clarity, and intellectual order; serene, calm, and restrained. |  Her apollonian demeanor masked a deep well of passion.  她沉着冷静的举止掩盖了内心深处强烈的激情。|  Synonyms:  serene, calm, composed, balanced, harmonious, classical, rational | Antonyms:  dionysian, chaotic, emotional, passionate, unrestrained | adjective

apollonian:/ˌæpəˈloʊniən/ | n. |  信奉阿波罗精神的人 |  A person who embodies the qualities of Apollo, especially rationality and order. | He was a true Apollonian, always striving for balance and intellectual clarity. 他是一个真正的阿波罗信徒，总是追求平衡和智力清晰。 | Synonyms: classicist, rationalist | Antonyms: hedonist, emotionalist | noun
