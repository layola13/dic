nondemonstrativenesses:/ˌnɒndɪˈmɒnstrətɪvnɪsnɪsɪz/
n.
缺乏流露情感；不外露感情
the quality or state of being nondemonstrative; a lack of outward display of emotion.
Her nondemonstrativenesses made it difficult to know how she truly felt. 她不轻易流露情感，让人很难知道她的真实感受。
Near Synonyms: reservedness, reticence, unemotionalism, undemonstrativeness
Antonyms: demonstrativeness, expressiveness, effusiveness
