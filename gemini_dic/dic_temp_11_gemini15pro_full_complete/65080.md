ethanol:/ˈeθənɒl/ (UK), /ˈeθənɔːl/ (US)| n.|乙醇|A colorless volatile flammable liquid which is produced by the natural fermentation of sugars; alcohol.  It is the intoxicating constituent of alcoholic beverages. Also used as a solvent.|

例句：Ethanol is used as a biofuel. 乙醇被用作生物燃料。

例句：The wine contains 14% ethanol. 这款酒的乙醇含量为14%。

近义词：alcohol, ethyl alcohol|反义词: None|词性: noun
