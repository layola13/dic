gifting:/ˈɡɪftɪŋ/
n.
赠送，送礼
the act of giving a gift or gifts
例句：Corporate gifting is a common practice during the holidays.  公司送礼在节日期间是一种常见的做法。

例句：The gifting of the land to the university was a generous act.  将土地赠送给大学是一项慷慨的行为。

近义词：giving, presenting, donating
反义词：receiving, taking
词性: 名词


gifting:/ˈɡɪftɪŋ/
adj.
有天赋的
having natural talent or ability
例句：He is a gifting musician. 他是一位有天赋的音乐家。

例句：She has a gifting for languages. 她有语言天赋。

近义词：talented, gifted, skilled
反义词：untalented, unskilled
词性: 形容词
