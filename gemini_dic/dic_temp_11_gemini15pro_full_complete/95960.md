inculcatory:/ɪnˈkʌlkətɔːri/
adjective
控诉的；归罪的； inculpatory
Tending or serving to inculcate; intended to instill specific principles or beliefs, especially of a moral nature.  Also, incriminating; accusing.
The prosecutor presented inculcatory evidence against the defendant. 检察官出示了对被告不利的证据。
incriminating, accusing, implicating
exculpatory, absolving
adjective
