chirruped:/ˈtʃɜːrəpt/ /ˈtʃɪrəpt/| v.|啁啾，唧唧叫|To make a series of short, high-pitched sounds like a bird or insect.|The crickets chirruped in the night. 蟋蟀在夜里唧唧叫。|chirp, twitter, peep|croak, growl|verb

chirruped:/ˈtʃɜːrəpt/ /ˈtʃɪrəpt/| adj.|发出啁啾声的|Making a series of short, high-pitched sounds like a bird or insect.|The chirruped chorus of the cicadas filled the air.  蝉鸣的合唱声充满了空气。|chirping, twittering, peeping|silent, quiet|adjective

Etymology:  Imitative origin, likely based on the sound itself.
