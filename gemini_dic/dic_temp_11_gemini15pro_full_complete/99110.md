intercartilaginous:/ˌɪntərˌkɑːrtɪˈlædʒɪnəs/
adjective
软骨间的
Located or occurring between cartilages.
The intercartilaginous ligament connects the cartilages of the ribs.  肋软骨间韧带连接肋骨的软骨。
The surgeon repaired the intercartilaginous tear in the patient's knee.  外科医生修复了病人膝盖的软骨间撕裂伤。
Near synonyms:  interchondral
Antonyms:  None specifically.  Could consider terms related to bone, such as "osseous" or "intraosseous" as conceptually opposite.
词性:adjective
