comments:/ˈkɒments/ (UK) /ˈkɑːments/ (US)| n. |评论，意见，注释|Remarks, observations, or criticisms offered about something. |The teacher made helpful comments on my essay. 老师对我的论文提出了有益的意见。

|synonyms: remarks, observations, notes, feedback, criticisms, opinions, annotations, interpretations |antonyms: silence, quiet, agreements (in some contexts) |noun


comments:/ˈkɒments/ (UK) /ˈkɑːments/ (US)| v. |评论，表达意见|To express an opinion or reaction. |He commented on the current political situation. 他就当前的政治局势发表了评论。

|synonyms: remark, observe, note, mention, criticize, opine |antonyms: abstain, withhold (opinion) |verb



ETYMOLOGY->
From the Late Latin *commentāre*, frequentative of *commentārī* ("to think upon, meditate, write notes"), from *comminisci* ("to devise, contrive, invent"), from *com-* ("together") + *mēns* ("mind").
