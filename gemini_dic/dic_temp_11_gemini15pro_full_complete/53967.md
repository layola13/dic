discreetest:/dɪˈskriːtɪst/
adjective
最谨慎的，最周到的，最不引人注意的
showing prudence and circumspection; careful and circumspect in one's speech or actions, especially in order to avoid causing offense or to gain an advantage. Superlative form of "discreet".
She was the discreetest person I knew, never revealing a secret. 她是我认识的最谨慎的人，从不泄露秘密。
He made a discreetest inquiry about her family. 他非常谨慎地询问了她的家庭情况。

近义词:careful, cautious, prudent, circumspect, tactful, diplomatic
反义词:indiscreet, careless, imprudent, tactless

词性:adjective
词源:From Middle French discret, from Latin discretus (“separated, distinct”), past participle of discernere (“to separate, distinguish”).
