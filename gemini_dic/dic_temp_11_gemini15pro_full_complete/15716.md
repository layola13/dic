balancereef:/ˈbælənsˌriːf/| n.|平衡礁|A type of coral reef that develops in shallow, wave-agitated waters, typically parallel to the shoreline and separated from it by a lagoon. It is characterized by a balance between constructive and destructive forces.|The Great Barrier Reef is a complex system of balance reefs, fringing reefs, and patch reefs.|近义词：barrier reef, coral reef|反义词: (No direct antonym)|名词
