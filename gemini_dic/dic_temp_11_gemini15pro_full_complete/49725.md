democratization:/dɪˌmɒkrətɪˈzeɪʃn/ |n.|民主化|The process of making a country or organization more democratic.|例句：The democratization of the country led to greater freedom of speech. 这个国家的民主化带来了更大的言论自由。

例句：The democratization of information through the internet has empowered individuals. 互联网信息民主化赋予了个人更大的权力。|近义词：liberalization, reformation|反义词：authoritarianism, oppression|词性: 名词

Etymology: From democratize + -ation.  Democratize comes from French démocratiser, from démocratie (democracy) + -iser (to make).  Democracy ultimately derives from Greek dēmokratiā, from dēmos (people) + kratos (power).
