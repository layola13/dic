chattels:/ˈtʃæt(ə)lz/
n.
动产，财产
movable personal property (other than land, buildings, and other things considered fixtures)
The bankrupt's chattels were sold at auction. 破产者的动产被拍卖了。
近义词:goods, possessions, belongings, effects
反义词:real estate, property (specifically referring to land or buildings)
词性:名词
