intersphere:/ˈɪntərˌsfɪr/
noun
相互作用的领域; 交互圈
The region where different spheres (e.g., atmosphere, biosphere, hydrosphere) interact and influence each other.
The intersphere between the atmosphere and the ocean plays a crucial role in climate regulation. 大气和海洋之间的交互圈在气候调节中起着至关重要的作用。

The intersphere between the biosphere and geosphere affects nutrient cycling. 生物圈和地球圈之间的交互圈影响营养循环。
近义词:  interaction zone, interface, overlap
反义词:  isolated system, separate sphere
词性:  n.
