miscibilities:/ˌmɪsɪˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. |互溶性，混溶性|The ability of two or more substances to mix together and form a homogeneous solution.|The miscibilities of oil and water are very low. 油和水的互溶性很低。|Synonym: compatibility, blendability|Antonym: immiscibility|noun
