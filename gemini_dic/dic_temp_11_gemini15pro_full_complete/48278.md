decoupling:/diːˈkʌplɪŋ/
n.|脱钩，分离，去耦合|The decoupling of the two economies has had a significant impact on global trade. 这两个经济体的脱钩对全球贸易产生了重大影响。|
例句：The government is promoting the decoupling of economic growth from energy consumption. 政府正在推动经济增长与能源消耗脱钩。
例句：The decoupling of the software from the hardware allows for greater flexibility. 软件与硬件的脱钩允许更大的灵活性。
近义词：separation, disconnection, uncoupling
反义词：coupling, integration, connection
词性：名词

Etymology:  From the verb "decouple," a back-formation from the noun "decoupler," which itself comes from "couple" + "de-". "Couple" traces back to Old French "coupler," from Latin "copulare" meaning "to join, connect."  The prefix "de-" indicates separation or reversal.
