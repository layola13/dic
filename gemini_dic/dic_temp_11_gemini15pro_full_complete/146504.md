overgeneralises:/ˌoʊvərˈdʒɛnərəˌlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 过度概括，过分概括 |  To draw a conclusion about a group or category based on too few examples or insufficient evidence. | He overgeneralises about women based on his limited experience. 他根据有限的经验对女性过度概括。|Synonyms: generalize excessively, stereotype, make sweeping statements | Antonyms: specify, particularize, be precise | verb
