ceresin:/ˈsɛrəsɪn/
noun
地蜡，纯地蜡
A purified wax obtained from ozokerite, used in candles, polishes, etc.
例句：Ceresin is often used as a substitute for beeswax.  地蜡常被用作蜂蜡的替代品。

例句：The candles are made from ceresin.  这些蜡烛是用地蜡制成的。

synonym: ozokerite, mineral wax, paraffin wax
antonym: N/A (Ceresin is a specific type of wax, it doesn't have a direct opposite.)
词性: n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Ceresin, from Latin *cera "wax"
