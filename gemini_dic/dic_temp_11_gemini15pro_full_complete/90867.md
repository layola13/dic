hungry:/ˈhʌŋɡri/ | adj. | 饥饿的；渴望的 | Feeling a need or desire to eat food. Also, having a strong desire for something. |  I'm hungry. Let's get some lunch. 我饿了。我们去吃午饭吧。| The students were hungry for knowledge. 学生们渴望知识。 | synonyms: starving, ravenous, empty, famished, eager, desirous | antonyms: full, satisfied, satiated | adjective
