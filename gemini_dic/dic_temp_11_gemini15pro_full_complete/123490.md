monosystemic:/ˌmɒnəʊsɪˈstɛmɪk/
adjective
单一系统的;单一体系的
Relating to or characterized by a single system; operating within a single system.  A monosystemic approach considers only one aspect or perspective, often neglecting the interconnectedness of various factors.
The researcher criticized the monosystemic approach to understanding the complex environmental issue. 研究人员批评了用单一系统方法来理解复杂环境问题。
This monosystemic view of education fails to account for the social and economic factors affecting students. 这种单一体系的教育观未能考虑到影响学生的社会和经济因素。
近义词: unified, integrated, singular, homogenous
反义词: multisystemic, diverse, heterogeneous
词性: adjective
