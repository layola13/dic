carboxylic:/ˌkɑːrbɒkˈsɪlɪk/| adj.|羧基的|Relating to or containing the carboxyl group (-COOH).|Acetic acid is a simple carboxylic acid. 醋酸是一种简单的羧酸。|Carboxylic acids react with bases to form salts and water. 羧酸与碱反应生成盐和水。|近义词: acidic|反义词: basic|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From *carboxyl* + *-ic*. *Carboxyl* is from *carb(on)* + *ox(ygen)* + *-yl* (chemical suffix denoting a radical).  Ultimately from Latin *carbo* (coal, charcoal) and Greek *oxus* (sharp, acid) and *hūlē* (wood, matter).
