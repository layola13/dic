canalizes:/ˈkænəlaɪzɪz/
verb
引导；限定；形成管道
to cause something to follow a particular route or course; to restrict something within narrow limits.
Heavy rain canalizes into the ditches. 大雨汇集到沟渠中。
The government is trying to canalize the flow of traffic. 政府正试图引导交通流量。
channel, direct, guide, restrict
diversify, broaden
词性:v.
