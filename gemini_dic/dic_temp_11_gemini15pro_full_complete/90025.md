hoselike:/ˈhoʊzˌlaɪk/| adj.|像软管的，管状的|Resembling a hose in shape or flexibility. |The creature had a long, hoselike tongue.  这个生物有一条长长的、像软管一样的舌头。|The plant's stem was thick and hoselike.  这种植物的茎又粗又像软管。|近义词：tubular, pipelike, cylindrical|反义词: None|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From 'hose' + '-like'. 'Hose' comes from Old English 'hosa', meaning 'covering for the leg'.  '-like' is a suffix forming adjectives meaning 'resembling', 'characteristic of'.
