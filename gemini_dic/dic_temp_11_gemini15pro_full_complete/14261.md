autofocusing:/ˌɔːtəʊˈfəʊkəsɪŋ/ | adj. | 自动对焦的 | Automatically adjusting the focus of a lens to ensure a sharp image. | The camera's autofocusing feature makes it easy to take clear pictures. 相机的自动对焦功能使其易于拍摄清晰的照片。| Synonyms: self-focusing, automatic focusing | Antonyms: manual focusing | adjective

autofocus:/ˈɔːtəʊˌfəʊkəs/ | n. v. | 自动对焦 |  n. The system or process of automatically adjusting the focus of a lens. v. To adjust the focus automatically. |  n. The autofocus on this camera is incredibly fast. 这款相机的自动对焦速度非常快。 v.  The camera will autofocus when you press the shutter button halfway. 半按快门按钮，相机将自动对焦。 | Synonyms: self-focus | Antonyms: manual focus | noun, verb


词源:  "auto-" (self) + "focus" (point of clarity). "Auto" comes from Greek "autos" (self). "Focus" comes from Latin "focus" (hearth, fireplace).
