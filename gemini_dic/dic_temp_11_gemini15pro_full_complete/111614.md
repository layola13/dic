locale:/loʊˈkæl/ /ˈləʊkɑːl/| n.| 地点；场所；现场；区域；语言环境；本地化|A locale is a place where something happens or is set, or that has particular events associated with it.|The film was shot entirely on location in Ireland. 这部电影完全是在爱尔兰现场拍摄的。|Synonyms: place, area, region, setting, spot, venue, location|Antonyms: global, international|Noun

locale:/loʊˈkæl/ /ˈləʊkɑːl/| adj.|本地的；地方性的；当地的|Relating or restricted to a particular place.|locale customs  地方习俗|Synonyms: local, regional, provincial|Antonyms: global, international|Adjective



ETYMOLOGY->
From French locale, from Old French local, from Late Latin localis (“of a place”), from Latin locus (“place”).
