grueling:/ˈɡruːəlɪŋ/ (US), /ˈɡruːlɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.| 艰苦的，繁重费力的|Characterized by hardship, difficulty, and exhaustion; extremely tiring and demanding.|例句：The marathon was a grueling test of endurance.  马拉松是一场对耐力的严峻考验。

例句：After a grueling climb, they finally reached the summit.  经过一番艰苦的攀登，他们终于到达了顶峰。|近义词：exhausting, arduous, punishing, strenuous, demanding, taxing|反义词：easy, effortless, simple, relaxing|词性:形容词

Etymology:  From the verb "gruel," meaning "to punish" or "to exhaust," which itself comes from the Old French word "grueler," meaning "to torment."
