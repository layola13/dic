nonlinearities:/ˌnɒnlɪniˈærɪtiz/ | n. | 非线性 |  The plural form of nonlinearity, referring to deviations from a straight-line relationship.  It often describes systems where the output is not directly proportional to the input. | The model accounts for the nonlinearities in the system.  该模型考虑了系统的非线性。

The robot's movements exhibit several nonlinearities.  机器人的运动表现出几种非线性。 | synonyms: deviations,  irregularities, nonproportional relationships | antonyms: linearities,  proportional relationships | noun
