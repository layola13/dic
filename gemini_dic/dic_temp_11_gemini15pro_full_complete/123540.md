monounsaturated: /ˌmɒnəʊʌnˈsætʃəreɪtɪd/ /ˌmɑːnoʊʌnˈsætʃəreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 单元不饱和的 | Containing only one unsaturated carbon bond per molecule, especially of a fatty acid. |  Olive oil is rich in monounsaturated fats.  橄榄油富含单元不饱和脂肪酸。|  Synonyms:  monounsaturated fatty acid | Antonyms:  polyunsaturated, saturated | adjective

monounsaturated fat: /ˌmɒnəʊʌnˈsætʃəreɪtɪd fæt/ /ˌmɑːnoʊʌnˈsætʃəreɪtɪd fæt/ | n. | 单元不饱和脂肪 | A type of fat containing only one unsaturated carbon bond per molecule. | Monounsaturated fats are considered healthier than saturated fats.  单元不饱和脂肪被认为比饱和脂肪更健康。| Synonyms: MUFA | Antonyms: saturated fat, polyunsaturated fat | noun
