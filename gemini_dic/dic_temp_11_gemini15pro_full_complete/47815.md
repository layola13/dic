decarburization:/ˌdiːkɑːrbəraɪˈzeɪʃən/
noun
脱碳;脱碳作用
The process of removing carbon from a material, especially from steel or iron.
The decarburization of the steel resulted in a softer surface. 钢的脱碳导致表面更软。
decarbonization, decarbonisation
carburization, carbonization
