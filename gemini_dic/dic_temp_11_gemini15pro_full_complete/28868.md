Often implies a somewhat sleazy or insincere approach. | He was known as a bit of a carpetmonger, always chasing after the ladies. 他有点儿地毯商人的名声，总是追逐女人。| 近义词: womanizer, philanderer, playboy, lothario | 反义词: faithful husband, loyal partner | 词性: noun

Etymology:  A blend of "carpet" (referring to the bedroom, implying sexual activity) and "monger" (meaning a dealer or trader, implying a pursuit of something for selfish gain).
