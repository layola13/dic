micromillimeter:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈmɪlɪˌmiːtər/| n.|微毫米，百万分之一毫米|A micromillimeter is a unit of length equal to one millionth of a millimeter (10^-9 meters).|The diameter of a human hair is about 75 micromillimeters.|Synonym: nanometer|Antonym: kilometer|noun
