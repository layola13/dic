binocularity:/baɪˌnɒkjʊˈlærɪti/ /baɪˌnɑːkjʊˈlærɪti/| n. |双眼视觉；双目性|The ability to see with both eyes at the same time, creating a three-dimensional image.|Binocularity is essential for depth perception. 双眼视觉对于深度知觉至关重要.|近义词：stereopsis, two-eyed vision|反义词:monocularity|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *binocularius* (two-eyed), from *bini* (two by two, in pairs) + *oculus* (eye).  The "-ity" suffix transforms the adjective into a noun denoting a state or quality.
