misplan:/mɪsˈplæn/
verb
计划错误，筹划不周
to plan badly or incorrectly
The marketing campaign was misplanned from the start.  市场营销活动从一开始就计划不周。
The trip was completely misplanned, resulting in us missing our flight.  这次旅行完全计划不周，导致我们错过了航班。
近义词:miscalculate, bungle
反义词:plan well, organize well
词性:verb
