anti-constitution:/ˌæntiˌkɑːnstɪˈtuːʃən/ |adj.|违宪的，反对宪法的|Opposed to or against the principles of a constitution.|His anti-constitution views led to his expulsion from the party. 他的反宪法观点导致他被开除党籍。|The group launched an anti-constitution campaign.  该组织发起了一场反宪法运动。|Synonyms: unconstitutional, subversive, seditious|Antonyms: constitutional, lawful, legitimate|adjective

Etymology->
anti- (prefix): from Greek anti, meaning "against," "opposite of"
constitution: from Latin constitutio, meaning "ordinance," "regulation," "establishment"
