consubstantiality:/ˌkɑːnsəbˌstænʃiˈæləti/ |n.|同质性，同一实体|The doctrine of consubstantiality asserts the shared substance of the Father and the Son in Christian theology. 基督教神学中的同质论断言圣父和圣子共享同一实体。| The consubstantiality of the bread and wine with the body and blood of Christ is a central tenet of Catholic doctrine.  面包和葡萄酒与基督的身体和血液的同质性是天主教教义的核心原则。 |synonym: coessentiality, connaturality|antonym: heterogeneity, difference|noun

consubstantial:/ˌkɑːnsəbˈstænʃəl/ |adj.|同体的，同质的|They hold a consubstantial view of the relationship between humans and nature. 他们认为人与自然的关系是同体的。|  The two versions of the document were deemed consubstantial and therefore interchangeable. 这两个版本的文件被认为是同质的，因此可以互换。|synonym: coessential, connatural|antonym: heterogeneous, different|adjective

From Late Latin *consubstantialitas, from consubstantialis (see consubstantial) + -itas -ity.
