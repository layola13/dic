overcensoriously: /ˌoʊvərˌsɛnsɔːriəsli/ | adv. | 过分审查地，吹毛求疵地 | in a manner that is excessively critical or fault-finding, especially regarding moral or social matters. |  He overcensoriously reviewed the movie, focusing on minor details and ignoring the overall message. 他对这部电影进行了吹毛求疵的评论，专注于细枝末节而忽略了整体信息。 |  The teacher overcensoriously marked the essays, deducting points for the slightest errors. 老师对作文进行了过分严格的批改，即使是最小的错误也要扣分。 | 近义词: hypercritically, excessively critically, nitpickingly | 反义词: leniently, tolerantly, permissively | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From "over-" (excessively) + "censorious" (severely critical of others) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Censorious" derives from the Latin "censor," referring to a Roman official who supervised public morals.
