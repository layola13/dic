anchorless:/ˈæŋkərlɪs/| adj.|无着落的，无依无靠的，漂泊的|Lacking an anchor, either literally or figuratively; without a firm foundation or mooring; adrift, unstable, insecure.|例句：After graduating, she felt anchorless and unsure of her future. 毕业后，她感到无着落，对未来感到迷茫。|近义词：adrift, rootless, unmoored, unstable, insecure|反义词:secure, stable, grounded|词性:形容词

词源:
From anchor + -less.  "Anchor" comes from Old English ancor, from Latin ancora, from Greek ankura.  "-less" is a suffix meaning "without," from Old English -lēas.
