declinate:/ˈdɛklɪneɪt/ /ˈdiːklaɪneɪt/
verb
拒绝; 谢绝; 倾斜; 下弯
to refuse to do something; to bend downwards
例句：He declined the invitation to dinner. 他谢绝了晚餐邀请。

例句：The sun declinated towards the horizon. 太阳西斜。

synonym: refuse, reject, turn down, decrease, diminish, dip, slope down
antonym: accept, agree, increase, rise, ascend
词性: v.


declinate:/ˈdɛklɪneɪt/ /ˈdiːklaɪneɪt/
adjective
(Botany, Zoology)  弯曲的；倾斜的
bending downwards; curving downwards
例句: The declinate branches of the weeping willow trailed in the water. 垂柳倾斜的树枝垂在水里。

例句:  The plant's declinate leaves helped it shed rainwater.  这种植物向下弯曲的叶子有助于排水。

synonym: drooping, pendant, cernuous, inclined
antonym: erect, upright, ascending
词性: adj.
