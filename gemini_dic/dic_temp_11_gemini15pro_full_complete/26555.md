cabiric: /kəˈbɪrɪk/ | adj. | 神秘的，秘密宗教的 | Relating to secret religious rites; mystic. |  The cabiric mysteries were celebrated in ancient Greece.  古代希腊举行过神秘的卡比里仪式。 | 近义词：mystic, esoteric, occult | 反义词：mundane, common, exoteric | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Ancient Greek Καβειρικός (Kabeirikoi), relating to the Cabeiri (Κάβειροι), a group of enigmatic deities venerated in several locations of the ancient Greek world, especially in Samothrace.  源自古希腊语 Καβειρικός (Kabeirikoi)，与卡比里人 (Κάβειροι) 有关，卡比里人是古希腊世界几个地方，尤其是在萨莫色雷斯岛上受崇拜的一群神秘神灵。
