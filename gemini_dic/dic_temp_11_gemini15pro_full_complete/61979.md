enabling:/ɪˈneɪblɪŋ/ /ɛˈneɪblɪŋ/| adj.|使能够的，赋能的|Giving someone the authority or means to do something.  Making something possible.|The software update includes new enabling technologies.|近义词:empowering, facilitating, authorizing, permitting|反义词:disabling, hindering, restricting, preventing|词性:adjective

enabling:/ɪˈneɪblɪŋ/ /ɛˈneɪblɪŋ/| n.|赋能，授权|The act of giving someone the authority or means to do something.|The training program focuses on the enabling of employees to take on new responsibilities.|近义词:empowerment, facilitation|反义词:restriction, hindrance|词性:noun (less common)
