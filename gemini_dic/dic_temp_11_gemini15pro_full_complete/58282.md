drunks: /drʌŋks/ | n. | 醉汉; 酒鬼 | People who are habitually drunk. | The drunks stumbled out of the bar at closing time. 醉汉们在酒吧关门时跌跌撞撞地走了出来。|  The drunks were causing a disturbance in the street. 醉汉们在街上制造骚乱。 | 近义词: alcoholics, inebriates | 反义词: teetotalers | 词性: noun (plural)

Etymology:  From Middle English *drunken*, from Old English *druncen*, past participle of *drincan* ("to drink"). Cognate with West Frisian *dronken* (“drunk”), Dutch *dronken* (“drunk”), German *trunken* (“drunk”).
