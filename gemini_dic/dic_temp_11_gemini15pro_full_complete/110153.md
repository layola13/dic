liedertafel:/ˈliːdərˌtɑːfəl/
n.
合唱团，歌咏协会
A social organization, typically German-American, whose members gather to sing choral music, especially German songs.
The liedertafel performed a beautiful rendition of Schubert's "Winterreise."  合唱团演唱了舒伯特《冬之旅》的优美版本。
近义词：choir, chorus, glee club
反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, perhaps "soloist" in a contrasting sense)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Liedertafel*, literally "song table," from *Lieder* (plural of *Lied*, "song") + *Tafel* ("table").
