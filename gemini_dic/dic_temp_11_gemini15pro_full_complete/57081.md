doughbird:/ˈdoʊbɜːrd/
n.
鸻形目鸟类；沙鹬类；尤指矶鹞
A shorebird; a sandpiper, especially a turnstone.
Doughbirds are common sights along the coast.  沙鹬在海岸边很常见。
The doughbird scurried along the beach looking for food.  这只沙鹬沿着海滩跑来跑去寻找食物。
Synonyms: shorebird, sandpiper, wader, turnstone
