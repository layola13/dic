mittimus: /ˈmɪtɪməs/| n.|送押令，拘票|A warrant committing a person to prison.|The judge signed the mittimus, and the defendant was taken into custody.|warrant, commitment order|release order|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *mittimus (“we send”), first person plural present active indicative of mittere (“to send”).
