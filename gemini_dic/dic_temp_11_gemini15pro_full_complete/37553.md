commonty: /ˈkɒmənti/ (British) /ˈkɑːmənti/ (American)| n.|共有地，公共用地|A piece of land owned or used jointly by the members of a community. |例句：The villagers grazed their animals on the commonty. 村民们在公共用地上放牧他们的牲畜。

例句：The commonty provided resources like firewood and building materials. 这块共有地提供了像柴火和建筑材料这样的资源。|近义词：commons, common land, communal land|反义词：private property, private land|词性:noun
