gaffed:/gæft/ | adj. | （非正式）被搞砸的；被操纵的 | (informal) spoiled or ruined; manipulated or rigged | The election was gaffed. 选举被操纵了. | 近义词： ruined, spoiled, manipulated, rigged | 反义词: successful, fair | 词性: 形容词

词源：gaffed源于名词gaff，一种带钩的长杆，用于捕鱼。  The word "gaff" originates from the noun "gaff," a long pole with a hook used for landing fish.  这个词的比喻义“搞砸”和“操纵”是后来发展出来的。 The figurative meanings of "mess up" and "manipulate" developed later.
