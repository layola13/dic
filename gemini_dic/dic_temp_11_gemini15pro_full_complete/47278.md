de-democratization:/diːˌdiːmɒkrətaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |去民主化，民主倒退|The process of weakening or eliminating democratic institutions and practices within a society or political system.|The rise of authoritarianism led to a period of de-democratization in the country.  这个国家的威权主义兴起导致了一段时间的去民主化。|synonyms: democratic backsliding, democratic erosion, authoritarianism, regression, decline of democracy|antonyms: democratization, liberalization, democratic progress|noun
