cherriest:/ˈtʃɛriɪst/| adj.|最愉快的，最兴高采烈的|superlative adjective of cherry; most cheerful, most happy or joyful| Her cherriest smile lit up the room. 她最灿烂的笑容照亮了整个房间。|Synonyms: happiest, jolliest, merriest|Antonyms: glummest, saddest, most miserable|adjective

Etymology:
From cherry + -est.  "Cherry" itself comes from Old North French cherise, from Vulgar Latin ceresia, from Latin cerasus "cherry tree," from Greek kerasos. The superlative suffix "-est" is a common Germanic suffix used to form the superlative degree of adjectives.
