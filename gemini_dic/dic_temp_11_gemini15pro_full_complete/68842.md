fashion-conscious:/ˈfæʃən ˈkɑːnʃəs/ | adj. |时尚意识强的；注重时尚的 | Aware of and interested in fashion trends.  Someone who is fashion-conscious cares about what they wear and how they look. | She's very fashion-conscious and always wears the latest styles. 她非常注重时尚，总是穿着最新的款式。 | He's not particularly fashion-conscious and prefers comfortable clothes. 他不是特别注重时尚，更喜欢舒适的衣服。 | 近义词: stylish, trendy, fashionable | 反义词: unfashionable, outdated, frumpy | 词性: 形容词
