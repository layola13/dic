factorylike:/ˈfæktərɪˌlaɪk/
adjective
像工厂的; 工厂似的
Resembling or characteristic of a factory, especially in being large, impersonal, or highly organized.
The office had a factorylike atmosphere, with rows of identical desks.  办公室有一种像工厂般的气氛，一排排相同的桌子。
The birds were raised in factorylike conditions.  这些鸟是在类似工厂的环境中饲养的。
Synonyms: industrial, mechanized, mass-produced
Antonyms: handcrafted, artisanal, homemade
词性: 形容词
