The macerations of the fruit made a delicious compote.| The museum displayed the macerations of ancient Egyptian mummies.  博物馆展出了古埃及木乃伊的浸渍标本。| synonyms: soaking, steeping, softening, disintegration | antonyms: drying, hardening, dehydration | noun
