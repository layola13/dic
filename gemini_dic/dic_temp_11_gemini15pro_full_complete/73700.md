forgivingness's:/fərˈɡɪvnɪsnɪsɪz/
n.
宽恕, 宽容
The act of forgiving someone or something; the state of being forgiving.  Possession indicated by the Saxon genitive. (This form is unusual and often considered awkward. "Forgivingness" without the possessive is generally preferred.)
He admired the forgivingness's of his parents. 他钦佩他父母的宽恕之心。(This example is grammatically correct but sounds unnatural.  It's better to rephrase it.)


近义词:mercy, pardon, leniency, clemency
反义词:unforgivingness, resentment, rancor
词性:noun
