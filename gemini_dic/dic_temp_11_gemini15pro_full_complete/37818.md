compensational:/kɒmpənˈseɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 补偿的，赔偿的 | Relating to, or providing compensation. |  He received a compensatory payment for his injuries. 他因受伤获得了赔偿金。 | The company offered her a compensatory day off for working overtime. 公司给她放了一天补偿假，以弥补她加班的时间。 | 近义词: remunerative, restorative, corrective | 反义词: punitive, detrimental | 词性: adjective

词源:  From late Middle English (in the sense ‘balancing, counteracting’): from late Latin *compensation- ‘a balancing, making equivalent’, from the verb compensare, from com- ‘together’ + pensare ‘weigh’.
