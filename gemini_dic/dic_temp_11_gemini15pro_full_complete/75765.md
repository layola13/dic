fulminic:/fʊlˈmɪnɪk/ | adj. | 雷酸的；爆发的；猛烈的 | Relating to or containing fulminic acid, an unstable acid used in explosives.  Characterized by sudden and violent action or speech. |  The fulminic explosion shattered the windows.  雷酸爆炸震碎了窗户。 | synonyms: explosive, volatile, sudden, violent, fierce | antonyms: stable, calm, peaceful | adjective
