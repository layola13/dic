literary:/ˈlɪtərəri/ | adj. | 文学的；文学上的；书面的 | Concerning the writing, study, or content of literature, especially of the kind valued for quality of form. |  She has literary talent. 她有文学天赋。|  He is a literary giant. 他是一位文学巨匠。 | 近义词: scholarly, bookish, intellectual | 反义词: illiterate, nonliterary | 词性: adjective
