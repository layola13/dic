defuel:/ˌdiːˈfjuːəl/ | v. |  卸除燃料；使…停止运作 | To remove fuel from something, especially an aircraft or vehicle; to reduce or eliminate the driving force or motivation of something. |  The plane was defueled before maintenance.  飞机在维修前被卸除了燃料。

The scandal defueled his political ambitions.  这起丑闻使他的政治抱负破灭了。 | synonyms: drain, empty, unload | antonyms: fuel, refuel | verb
