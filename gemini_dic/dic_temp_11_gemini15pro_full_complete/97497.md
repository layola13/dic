ingestible:/ɪnˈdʒɛstɪbl/ | adj. | 可食用的，可摄入的 | Something that is ingestible can be safely swallowed and taken into the body through the mouth. |  Example: The new vitamin supplement comes in small, ingestible tablets. 这种新的维生素补充剂是小巧易服的片剂。

Example:  Are these berries ingestible, or are they poisonous?  这些浆果可以食用还是有毒？| 近义词: edible, consumable | 反义词: inedible, nonconsumable, poisonous, toxic | 词性: adjective
