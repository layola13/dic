agriologist:/ˌæɡriˈɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 农业学家，农学家 | A specialist in agriculture.  A person who studies and practices agriculture. |  例句：The agriologist recommended a new type of fertilizer. 这位农业学家推荐了一种新型肥料。

例句：The agriologist researched the best methods for growing crops in arid climates.  这位农业学家研究了在干旱气候下种植作物的最佳方法。| 近义词：agriculturalist, agronomist, farmer | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *agri- (stem of ager, "field") + -ologist ("one who studies").
