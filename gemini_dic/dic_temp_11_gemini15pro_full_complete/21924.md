bog-standard:/ˌbɒɡ ˈstændəd/ /ˌbɑːɡ ˈstændərd/| adj.|普通的，平庸的，毫无特色的|Completely ordinary, lacking any special or interesting features.  Originating from the image of something commonplace and uninteresting, like the plants found in a bog.|例句：He drives a bog-standard Ford Focus. 他开着一辆普通的福特福克斯。|例句：The hotel room was bog-standard, nothing special.  酒店房间很普通，没什么特别的。|近义词：ordinary, common, conventional, run-of-the-mill, commonplace|反义词：exceptional, extraordinary, remarkable, unusual|词性:形容词
