nonexpressionistic:/ˌnɒnɪkˈspreʃənɪstɪk/
adjective
非表现主义的
Not characteristic of expressionism; not pertaining to expressionism, an artistic style that emphasizes the artist's subjective feelings and emotions rather than objective reality.
Her paintings are noted for their nonexpressionistic style, focusing on realistic portrayals of everyday life. 她以非表现主义的绘画风格而闻名，专注于对日常生活的现实描绘。
The architect favored a nonexpressionistic design for the new building, opting for clean lines and functional minimalism.  这位建筑师喜欢新建筑的非表现主义设计，选择了简洁的线条和功能性的极简主义。
Synonyms: objective, realistic, representational, naturalistic
Antonyms: expressionistic, subjective, emotional
