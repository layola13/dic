jager:/ˈjeɪɡər/ (US), /ˈjɑːɡə(r)/ (UK)| n.|猎手，猎人；德国一种烈酒|A jager is a huntsman or hunter, especially in German-speaking countries. It also refers to a strong German liqueur. |He is a skilled jager. 他是一位技艺娴熟的猎人。|Synonyms: huntsman, hunter|Antonyms: prey|noun

jagermeister:/ˈjeɪɡərˌmaɪstər/ (US), /ˈjɑːɡəˌmaɪstə(r)/ (UK)| n.|野格酒（一种德国利口酒）|Jagermeister is a German digestif made with 56 herbs and spices.|We had shots of Jagermeister after dinner. 晚饭后我们喝了几杯野格。|Synonyms:  liqueur, digestif|Antonyms: N/A|noun
