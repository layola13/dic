floor-crosser:/ˈflɔːrˌkrɒsər/ (US) /ˈflɔːrˌkrɒsə(r)/ (UK)
n.| 跨党议员，倒戈议员 | A legislator who votes against the declared position of their party. | The senator was labeled a floor-crosser after voting with the opposition on the key bill.  这位参议员在关键法案上与反对党一起投票后，被贴上了跨党议员的标签。| Example: The floor-crosser faced criticism from his own party. 这位跨党议员面临来自他自己党派的批评。|Synonym: defector, turncoat, independent|Antonym: loyalist, partisan|noun
