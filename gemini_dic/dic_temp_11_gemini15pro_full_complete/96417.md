indisputably:/ˌɪndɪˈspjuːtəbli/ | adv. | 无可争辩地，毋庸置疑地 |  Beyond doubt or question; undeniably.  |  Her talent is indisputably impressive. 她的才华令人印象深刻，无可争辩。 | Synonyms: undeniably, irrefutably, incontrovertibly | Antonyms: disputably, questionably, debatably | word type: adverb

Etymology:  From Late Latin *indisputabilis, from in- "not" + disputabilis "debatable," from disputare "to discuss."
