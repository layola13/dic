industrialised:/ɪnˈdʌstriəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 工业化的 |  Having undergone industrialization; developed industries on a large scale. |  例句：The industrialised nations have a responsibility to help developing countries. 工业化国家有责任帮助发展中国家。

例句：The region has become increasingly industrialised in recent years. 该地区近年来工业化程度越来越高。


近义词：developed, advanced, modernized | 反义词：undeveloped, agrarian, pre-industrial | 词性：形容词

词源: From industrialize + -ed.  The verb "industrialize" comes from "industrial" + "-ize". "Industrial" derives from "industry" + "-al".  "Industry" has its roots in Latin "industria" meaning "diligence, activity".
