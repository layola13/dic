ecstatic: /ɪkˈstætɪk/ /ɛkˈstætɪk/ | adj. | 狂喜的；欣喜若狂的 | Feeling or expressing overwhelming happiness. |  She was ecstatic when she heard she'd won the lottery. 她听到自己中了彩票欣喜若狂。|  He was ecstatic about the birth of his daughter. 他对女儿的出生感到欣喜若狂。| 近义词: overjoyed, thrilled, elated, rapturous | 反义词: miserable, depressed, dejected | 词性: adjective

ecstasy: /ˈɛkstəsi/ /ˈɛkstɪsi/ | n. | 狂喜；欣喜若狂 | An overwhelming feeling of great happiness or joy. | She was in an ecstasy of delight. 她欣喜若狂。 | The team were in ecstasy after their victory. 球队在获胜后欣喜若狂。| 近义词: bliss, euphoria, rapture | 反义词: misery, despair, agony | 词性: noun
