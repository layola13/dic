bait-and-switch:/ˈbeɪt ən ˈswɪtʃ/ |n.|诱饵调包; 挂羊头卖狗肉|A deceptive commercial practice where customers are lured by the advertisement of a low-priced product but are then encouraged to buy a more expensive one.|The store advertised a great deal on TVs, but it was a bait-and-switch; they were all sold out when I got there.| synonyms: deceptive advertising, false advertising, scam | antonyms: honest advertising, ethical marketing | noun
