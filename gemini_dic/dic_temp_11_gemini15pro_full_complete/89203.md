homesourcing:/ˈhoʊmˌsɔːrsɪŋ/ /ˈhəʊmˌsɔːsɪŋ/
n.
在家工作；居家外包
The practice of hiring people to work from their homes, often using the internet and communications technology.
Companies are increasingly turning to homesourcing to reduce overhead costs.  公司越来越多地转向居家外包以降低管理费用。
Many companies find homesourcing a cost-effective alternative to traditional office spaces. 许多公司发现居家外包是传统办公空间的一种经济高效的替代方案。

近义词:telecommuting, working from home, remote work
反义词:insourcing, outsourcing, office work
词性:noun
