keratosis:/ˌkɛrəˈtoʊsɪs/
n.
角化症; 角化过度; 皮肤角化
A skin condition marked by an overgrowth of keratin.
Example: Actinic keratosis is a common precancerous skin lesion. 光化性角化病是一种常见的癌前皮肤病变。

Example:  He was diagnosed with seborrheic keratosis, a benign skin growth. 他被诊断出患有脂溢性角化病，这是一种良性的皮肤增生物。
近义词:hyperkeratosis, cornification
反义词:(None -  a specific medical condition without a direct opposite)
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *kéras* (horn) + *-osis* (condition, disease)
