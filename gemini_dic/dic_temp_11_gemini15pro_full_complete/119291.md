micro-enterprise:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈɛntərpraɪz/| n. |微型企业|A very small business, typically one with ten or fewer employees and a relatively low amount of capital.| Many developing countries rely on micro-enterprises to boost their economies. 许多发展中国家依靠微型企业来促进经济发展。

Micro-enterprises often struggle to access traditional financing. 微型企业通常难以获得传统的融资渠道。|近义词：small business, startup, cottage industry|反义词：large corporation, multinational corporation|词性: noun
