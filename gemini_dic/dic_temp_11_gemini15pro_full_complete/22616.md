boozers:/ˈbuːzərz/| n.|酒鬼，酗酒者；酒吧|People who drink alcohol frequently and heavily, especially habitually. Also, a place where alcoholic drinks are sold and consumed (informal).|例句：The local boozers were crowded on Friday night.  星期五晚上，当地的酒吧里挤满了人。

例句：He's one of the town's most notorious boozers. 他是镇上最臭名昭著的酒鬼之一。|近义词：alcoholics, drinkers, drunks, pubs, bars|反义词:teetotalers|词性:名词
