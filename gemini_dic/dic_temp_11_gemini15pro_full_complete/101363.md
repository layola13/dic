ionizable:/ˌaɪənˈaɪzəbl/
adjective
可电离的
Capable of being converted into ions.  A substance is ionizable if it can be converted into ions, typically through the loss or gain of electrons.
Hydrochloric acid is a strong and highly ionizable substance. 盐酸是一种强且高度可电离的物质。

The air was filled with ionizable particles. 空气中充满了可电离的粒子。

ionising (chiefly British), dissociable
non-ionizable, nonionizing

adjective
