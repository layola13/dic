outeats:/ˌaʊtˈiːts/
verb
比...吃得多; 吃得比...好
outdo someone in eating; eat more or better than someone.
He outeats everyone in the family. 他比家里任何人都吃得多。
近义词: outconsume, surpass in eating
反义词: be outeaten by
词性: verb
