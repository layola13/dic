desubsidization:/diːˌsʌbsɪdaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 去补贴化；取消补贴 | The act of removing or reducing government subsidies.  | The desubsidization of agriculture has led to increased prices for consumers. 去补贴化农业导致消费者价格上涨。| The government's desubsidization of the energy sector aims to promote market efficiency. 政府对能源部门的去补贴化旨在提高市场效率。| synonyms: subsidy removal, subsidy reduction, phasing out of subsidies | antonyms: subsidization, subsidizing | noun

desubsidize:/diːˈsʌbsɪdaɪz/ | v. | 取消补贴；去除补贴 | To remove or reduce government subsidies. | The government decided to desubsidize the failing industry. 政府决定取消对衰败行业的补贴。 | They plan to desubsidize farming over the next five years. 他们计划在未来五年内逐步取消农业补贴。| synonyms: remove subsidies, reduce subsidies, phase out subsidies | antonyms: subsidize | verb
