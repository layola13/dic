indorsable:/ɪnˈdɔːrsəbl/ | adj. | 可背书的，可担保的 | Able to be endorsed; transferable by endorsement.  Specifically referring to a document, typically a check or bill of exchange, that can be legally transferred to another party by signing it. |  This check is indorsable, so you can sign it over to me.  这张支票可以背书，所以你可以签字转让给我。|  transferable, negotiable | non-transferable, non-negotiable | adjective

indorse:/ɪnˈdɔːrs/ | vt. | 背书，担保 | To sign (a check or bill of exchange) on the back to make it payable to someone other than the stated payee or to accept responsibility for paying it. Also, to give support to; approve. | I will indorse this check to you. 我会把这张支票背书给你。 |  approve, support, sanction, authorize | oppose, disapprove, reject | verb
