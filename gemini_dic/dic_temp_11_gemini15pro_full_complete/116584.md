matric:/ˈmeɪtrɪk/| n. |入学考试; 准许入学;  录取; (尤指南非的) 大学入学考试|  An examination that students must pass in order to go to a university, especially in South Africa. |He passed his matric with distinction. 他以优异的成绩通过了入学考试。| synonyms: entrance exam, matriculation examination | antonyms: N/A | noun

matriculate:/məˈtrɪkjʊleɪt/ | v. |录取;注册;准许入学 | To officially enroll as a member of a college or university. |She matriculated at the University of Cape Town. 她在开普敦大学注册入学。| synonyms: enroll, register, admit | antonyms: withdraw, drop out | verb
