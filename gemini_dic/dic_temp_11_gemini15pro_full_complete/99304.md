intercouple:/ˌɪntərˈkʌpəl/
verb
互相连接，互相结合
to connect or join together, especially in a close or intimate way.
The two systems are intercoupled and cannot be considered in isolation. 这两个系统相互连接，不能孤立地考虑。
The neurons are intercoupled to form a complex network. 这些神经元相互连接，形成了一个复杂的网络。
connect, join, link, unite, couple
separate, disconnect, disunite
v.
