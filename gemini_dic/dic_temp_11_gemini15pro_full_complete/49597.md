demesne: /dɪˈmeɪn/ /dɪˈmiːn/
n.
领地，庄园；领域，范围
A piece of land attached to a manor and retained for the owner's own use.  The area over which a ruler or government has control or influence.
The king's vast demesne stretched across the country. 国王的广阔领地遍布全国。
Writing about gardens is my particular demesne.  写园艺的文章是我的专长。
Synonyms: estate, domain, territory, realm, province
Antonyms:  public land, commons
词性: 名词

demesne: /dɪˈmeɪn/ /dɪˈmiːn/
n.
领域，范围
The area over which a ruler or government has control or influence.  A field of activity or interest.
She treated the kitchen as her own private demesne. 她把厨房视为自己的私人领域。
Synonyms: domain, realm, sphere, province
Antonyms:  none


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French demeine (“power to manage, control, dispose of; dependence, subordination; household, retinue; lord's manor”), from Vulgar Latin *dominicum (unattested), from Latin dominicus (“of a master or lord”), from dominus (“master, lord”). Doublet of domain.
