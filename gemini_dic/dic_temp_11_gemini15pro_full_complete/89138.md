homecoming:/ˈhoʊmkʌmɪŋ/ (US) /ˈhəʊmkʌmɪŋ/ (UK)| n.| 归乡；返校节；校友返校日 |A homecoming is the return of someone to their home, especially after a long absence. It can also refer to an annual event at a high school or college for alumni and current students.|She received a warm welcome on her homecoming. 她回家时受到了热烈的欢迎。|近义词：return, arrival|反义词：departure, leaving|词性:名词

homecoming:/ˈhoʊmkʌmɪŋ/ (US) /ˈhəʊmkʌmɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.| 归乡的；返校的 |Relating to or characteristic of a homecoming. |The homecoming parade was a huge success. 返校游行非常成功。|近义词：returning|反义词：departing|词性:形容词
