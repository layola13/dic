frilliness:/ˈfrɪlɪnɪs/
noun
装饰繁复；多褶边；花哨
The excessive ornamentation or decoration, especially with ruffles or frills.
Her dress was criticized for its excessive frilliness. 她的裙子因为过于花哨而受到批评。
ornamentation, elaborateness, fussiness
simplicity, plainness
n.
