overfly:/ˌoʊvərˈflaɪ/ /ˌəʊvəˈflaɪ/| v. |飞越，从…上空飞过|to fly over something, especially a country or area, in an aircraft|例句：The plane overflew several countries on its way to its final destination. 这架飞机在飞往最终目的地的途中飞越了几个国家。

例句：Military aircraft overflew the disputed territory. 军用飞机飞越了有争议的领土。

近义词：fly over, soar over|反义词: - |词性:动词
