backgammoning:/ˌbækˈɡæməniŋ/
n.
双陆棋比赛的击败，彻底击败
The act of defeating an opponent in backgammon so thoroughly that they are unable to bear off any of their checkers.
He gave his opponent a sound backgammoning, leaving him with no chance of recovery.  他彻底击败了他的对手，让他没有任何翻盘的机会。
近义词: thrashing, rout, drubbing
反义词: loss, defeat
词性: 名词

backgammoning:/ˌbækˈɡæməniŋ/
v.
在双陆棋比赛中彻底击败
To defeat an opponent in backgammon so thoroughly that they are unable to bear off any of their checkers.
He backgammoned his opponent in record time. 他在创纪录的时间内彻底击败了对手。
近义词: thrash, rout, drub
反义词: lose, be defeated
词性: 动词
