llog:/ˈlɒɡ/ /ˈlɑːɡ/ | n. | 日志，记录 | A detailed record of events, occurrences, or observations. |  Example 1:  The captain kept a detailed log of the voyage. 船长详细记录了航行的日志。

Example 2: The system automatically generates a log of all user activity. 系统会自动生成所有用户活动的日志。 | Synonyms: record, journal, diary, chronicle | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "oblivion," "erasure") | noun
