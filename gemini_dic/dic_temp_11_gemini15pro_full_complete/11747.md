aromatizer:/əˈroʊməˌtaɪzər/ | n. | 香薰机，芳香剂 |A device or substance used to add a pleasant scent to a room or area. |例句：She used an aromatizer to make her home smell like lavender. 她用香薰机让家里充满薰衣草的香味。

例句：The aromatizer filled the room with a calming scent. 香薰机让房间里充满了令人平静的香味。|近义词：diffuser, scent diffuser, fragrance diffuser |反义词:deodorizer |词性:noun

aromatize:/əˈroʊməˌtaɪz/ | v. | 使芳香，加香料 |To add a pleasant scent to something. |例句：She aromatized the room with essential oils. 她用精油使房间芳香。

例句：He aromatized the tea with cinnamon and cloves. 他用肉桂和丁香给茶增添香味。|近义词：scent, perfume, fragrance |反义词:deodorize |词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *aromatiser*, from *arome* ("aroma") + *-iser* (suffix forming verbs).  *Arome* ultimately comes from Greek *ἄρωμα* (árōma, “spice, aroma”).
