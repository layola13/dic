coachwork: /ˈkəʊtʃwɜːk/ (UK) /ˈkoʊtʃwɜːrk/ (US)
n.
车身; (车辆的) 外壳;
The bodywork of a motor vehicle.  Specifically, the work of designing and building coach bodies.
The vintage car's coachwork was impeccably maintained. 这辆老爷车的车身保养得无可挑剔。
synonyms: bodywork,  carriagework, body, chassis
antonyms: engine, interior
