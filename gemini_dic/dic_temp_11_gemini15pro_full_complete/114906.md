mana:/ˈmɑːnə/ | n. |魔力，法力，精神力量，超自然力量|A supernatural force believed to reside in people or objects, giving them extraordinary power or influence. |The shaman drew on the mana of the earth to heal the sick.  萨满汲取大地的法力来治疗病人。| synonyms: power, magic, force, energy, juice | antonyms: weakness, impotence, powerlessness | noun

mana:/ˈmɑːnə/ | n. | (波利尼西亚文化中的) 威望，权威，效力 |Prestige, authority, or efficacy derived from spiritual power (in Polynesian culture). |The chief possessed great mana and was respected by all.  酋长拥有强大的威望，受到所有人的尊敬。| synonyms: prestige, authority, influence, status | antonyms: insignificance, weakness | noun
