hominess:/ˈhoʊminəs/| n.|舒适惬意；温馨；朴实|The room had a certain hominess that made me feel relaxed. 这个房间有一种特别的温馨感，让我感到很放松。|例句：The hominess of the small town was appealing.  小镇的温馨感很吸引人。

例句：Despite its simple decor, the cottage exuded hominess. 尽管装修简单，小屋却散发着温馨的气息。|近义词：coziness, comfort, warmth, informality|反义词:formality, coldness, unfriendliness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From home + -y + -ness.  Ultimately from Old English *hām.
