monolinguals:/ˌmɒnəʊˈlɪŋɡwəlz/ | n. | 单语者；只使用一种语言的人 | People who can only speak one language are called monolinguals.  只会说一种语言的人被称为单语者。|  Many monolinguals find it difficult to learn a new language. 许多单语者发现学习一门新语言很困难。 | synonyms: unilinguals | antonyms: bilinguals, multilinguals | noun

monolingual:/ˌmɒnəʊˈlɪŋɡwəl/ | adj. | 单语的；只使用一种语言的 | This dictionary is monolingual, containing only English definitions. 这本词典是单语的，只包含英文释义。 |  A monolingual environment can be challenging for immigrants.  单一语言的环境对移民来说可能具有挑战性。| synonyms: unilingual | antonyms: bilingual, multilingual | adjective
