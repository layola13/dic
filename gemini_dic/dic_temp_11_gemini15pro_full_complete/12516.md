asphalter:/ˈæsˌfɔːltər/ (US), /ˈæsˌfɒːltər/ (UK)| n.|沥青工；铺路工人|A person whose job is to lay or repair asphalt surfaces. |The asphalter skillfully smoothed the new surface of the driveway. 沥青工熟练地把车道的新表面抹平了。|The city hired asphalters to repave the main road. 市政府雇佣了沥青工来重铺主要道路。|近义词：paver, road worker|反义词: None|Noun
