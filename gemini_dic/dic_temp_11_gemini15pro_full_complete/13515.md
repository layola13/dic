词源：
From attain + -able + -ness.  "attain" comes from Old French "ateindre" (to reach, attain), ultimately from Latin "attingere" (to touch upon). "-able" is a suffix meaning "capable of being," and "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives.
