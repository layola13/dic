nonempiricism:/ˌnɑːnɪmˈpɪrɪsɪzəm/
n.
非经验主义
The theory that knowledge is not derived from sense experience.  It emphasizes reason and intuition over experience as the source of knowledge.
Nonempiricism played a significant role in the development of rationalist philosophy. 非经验主义在理性主义哲学的发展中扮演了重要角色。
The philosopher argued for nonempiricism, claiming that true knowledge comes from innate ideas. 这位哲学家主张非经验主义，声称真正的知识来自先天观念。
近义词: rationalism, apriorism, intellectualism
反义词: empiricism, positivism
词性: noun
