ferrymen:/ˈfɛrɪmən/ | n. |摆渡人；渡船夫|A person who operates a ferry, transporting people or goods across a body of water. |The ferrymen transported passengers across the river. 摆渡人把乘客渡过河。|The ferrymen skillfully navigated the boat through the rough waters.  渡船夫熟练地驾驶着船只穿过汹涌的水面。|近义词: boatman, oarsman, skipper |反义词: passenger |词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *feriman, from ferian (to carry, convey) + mann (man).  The word "ferry" itself comes from Old English ferian, meaning "to carry."
