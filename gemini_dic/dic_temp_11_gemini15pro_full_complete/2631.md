adoptee:/əˌdɒpˈtiː/ /əˌdɑːpˈtiː/
n.
被收养者；被领养者
A person who has been adopted.
例句：The adoptee searched for her biological parents.  这个被收养者寻找她的亲生父母。

例句：The support group offered counseling services for adoptees and their families.  这个互助小组为被收养者及其家人提供咨询服务。


近义词：adopted child, foster child
反义词:biological child
