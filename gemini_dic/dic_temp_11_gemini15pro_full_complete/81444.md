gratifiers:/ˈɡrætɪfaɪərz/ | n. | 满足者；使人满足的事物 |  People or things that provide gratification; things that satisfy a need or desire. |  These small luxuries are simple gratifiers that make life more enjoyable. 这些小奢侈品是简单的满足感来源，让生活更愉快。| 近义词: satisfiers, pleasers | 反义词: irritants, annoyances | 词性: 名词

gratify: /ˈɡrætɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 使满足；使高兴 |  To give pleasure or satisfaction to.  From Latin gratificari "to oblige, do a favor," from gratus "pleasing, agreeable" + -ficari. | It gratified him to see his children succeed. 看到孩子们成功，他感到很欣慰。 | 近义词: please, satisfy, delight | 反义词: disappoint, frustrate | 词性: 动词
