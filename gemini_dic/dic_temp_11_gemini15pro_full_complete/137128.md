nonsubsidies:/ˌnɒnˈsʌbsɪdiz/
|n.| 无补贴；非补贴 |Absence or lack of subsidies.  The state of not being subsidized.  A situation where financial assistance is not provided.| The government's move towards nonsubsidies in the agricultural sector has been met with protests. 政府在农业领域取消补贴的举动遭到了抗议。| The nonsubsidies policy has forced many small farmers to abandon their land.  无补贴政策迫使许多小农户放弃了他们的土地。 |Near Antonyms: subsidies, grants, financial aid|Antonyms: subsidies|noun
