crispinesses:/ˈkrɪspɪnəsɪz/
n.
酥脆，脆度
The state or quality of being crisp.
The crispinesses of the potato chips were perfect.  薯片的脆度恰到好处。

近义词: crispness, crunchiness
反义词: sogginess, softness
词性: 名词(plural)


词源:
From crisp + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  Crisp originates from Old English "crisp" meaning "curly," related to Old Norse "kriusp." The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, denoting a state or quality.  The suffix "-es" creates the plural form.
