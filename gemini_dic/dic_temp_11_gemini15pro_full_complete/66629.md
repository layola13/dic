existence:/ɪɡˈzɪstəns/ | n. | 存在；生存；生活 | The state of being; life.  |例句：The existence of life on other planets is a topic of much debate. 其他行星上是否存在生命是一个备受争议的话题。| 近义词：being, presence, survival, life | 反义词：nonexistence, absence, death | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Late Middle English existens, from Late Latin existentia (“existence”), from existentem (present participle of Latin existere (“to exist”)), from ex (“out”) + sistere (“to stand, appear, be”).
