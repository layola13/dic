indissolublist:/ˌɪndɪˈsɒljʊblɪst/ | n. |不溶解主义者；坚信婚姻不可解除者|A person who believes that marriage cannot be dissolved. | He was a staunch indissolublist, believing that marriage was a sacred bond for life. 他是一个坚定的不溶解主义者，认为婚姻是终生的神圣结合。| 近义词:  traditionalist (传统主义者) | 反义词: divorce advocate (离婚倡导者) | 词性: noun

indissoluble:/ˌɪndɪˈsɒljʊbl/ | adj. |不能溶解的；牢不可破的；永久的| Not able to be dissolved; permanent. | Their friendship was an indissoluble bond. 他们的友谊牢不可破。| 近义词: permanent, lasting, enduring | 反义词: dissoluble, breakable, temporary | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin indissolubilis, from in- "not" + dissolubilis "dissoluble," from dis- "apart" + solvere "to loosen, dissolve."
