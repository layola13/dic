embroidery:/ɪmˈbrɔɪdəri/
noun
刺绣
The art or process of decorating fabric with needlework.
Her dress featured intricate embroidery. 她的裙子上装饰着复杂的刺绣。
近义词: needlework, stitching
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 plainness 平整)
词性: noun


embroider:/ɪmˈbrɔɪdər/
verb
刺绣；修饰；渲染
To decorate cloth by sewing patterns on it with thread.  To add fictitious details to a story.
She embroidered a beautiful design on the cushion.  她在垫子上绣了一个漂亮的图案。
He embroidered the tale with details of his own invention. 他用自己编造的细节润色了这个故事。
近义词: stitch, adorn, embellish
反义词: simplify, strip (down)
词性: verb
