ambidextrous:/ˌæmbɪˈdɛkstrəs/
| adj. | 双手灵巧的；怀有二心的 | Able to use both hands equally well; skillful or adroit. Also, deceitful or hypocritical. |  She's an ambidextrous artist, equally skilled with both hands. 她是一位双手灵巧的艺术家，两只手都一样熟练。| synonyms: versatile, adaptable, two-handed, double-jointed | antonyms: clumsy, awkward, inept, one-handed | adjective


ambidexter:/ˌæmbɪˈdɛkstər/
| n. | 双手灵巧的人 | A person who is able to use both hands equally well. | The ambidexter could write equally well with either hand.  这个双手灵巧的人可以用任何一只手写得一样好。 | synonyms: all-rounder | antonyms: none | noun


Etymology:
From Latin *ambidexter, from ambi- "both" + dexter "right-handed, skillful". The negative connotation of "deceitful" arose from the association of left-handedness with awkwardness or dishonesty.
