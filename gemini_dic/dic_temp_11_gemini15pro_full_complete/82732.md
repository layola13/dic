guideway:/ˈɡaɪdweɪ/| n.|导轨，导向槽，轨道|A track or other structure that guides the motion of a vehicle or other moving object.|The train runs along a guideway. 火车沿着导轨行驶。|The monorail's guideway is elevated above the city streets. 单轨列车的轨道架设在城市街道上方。|Synonyms: track, rail, runway, path|Antonyms: N/A|noun
