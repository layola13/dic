falsities:/ˈfɔːlsɪtiz/
noun
谬误；虚假；谎言
statements that are untrue or incorrect
例句：The report was full of falsities.  这份报告充满了谎言。

例句：He was accused of spreading falsities about the company. 他被指控散布关于公司的虚假信息。

近义词：lies, untruths, falsehoods, inaccuracies, fabrications
反义词：truths, facts, realities, verities
词性：n. (noun)
