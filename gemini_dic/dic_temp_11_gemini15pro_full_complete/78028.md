gentrify:/ˈdʒɛntrɪfaɪ/| v. | 使贵族化；中产阶级化；士绅化 | To renovate and improve a house or district so that it conforms to middle-class taste.  This often results in increased property values and the displacement of lower-income residents. | Example 1:  The old neighborhood is being gentrified, with new coffee shops and boutiques opening up.  老街区正在被士绅化，新的咖啡店和精品店纷纷开业。

Example 2:  The rising rents are a direct result of the gentrification of the area.  不断上涨的租金是该地区士绅化的直接结果。 | 近义词:  renovate, improve, upgrade, revitalize | 反义词:  degenerate, decay, decline | 词性: 动词
