holdall:/ˈhəʊldɔːl/ (UK) /ˈhoʊldɔːl/ (US)
n.
旅行包，手提包
A large bag used for carrying clothes and other personal belongings, especially when travelling.
例句：She packed all her clothes for the weekend trip in her holdall. 她把周末旅行的所有衣服都装进了旅行包里。

例句：He bought a new leather holdall for his business trip. 他为出差买了一个新的皮革旅行包。

近义词：duffel bag, travel bag, carryall, tote bag
反义词：(None -  No single antonym for a type of bag)
词性：名词
