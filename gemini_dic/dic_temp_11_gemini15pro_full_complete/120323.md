minifarms:/ˈmɪniˌfɑːrmz/
noun
小型农场
Small-scale farms, often located in urban or suburban areas, that focus on intensive production in a limited space.
Many city dwellers are turning to minifarms to grow their own produce. 城市居民开始转向小型农场种植自己的农产品。
synonyms: microfarms, urban farms, smallholdings
antonyms: large-scale farms, agribusinesses
n.
