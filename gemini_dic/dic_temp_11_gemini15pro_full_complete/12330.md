asepticisms:/ˌeɪsɛpˈtɪsɪzəmz/| n. |无菌状态；无菌主义；防腐法|The state of being aseptic; the methods or principles of asepsis.  The avoidance of sepsis (infection).|The surgeon insisted on strict asepticisms during the operation.  外科医生坚持在手术过程中要严格无菌。|The laboratory maintained rigorous asepticisms to prevent contamination of the samples.  实验室保持严格的无菌状态以防止样品污染。|近义词：sterility, asepsis|反义词:sepsis, infection|词性:noun

词源: From a- (not) + septic (infected) + -ism (doctrine, practice)  最终源自希腊语sēpein (to rot).
