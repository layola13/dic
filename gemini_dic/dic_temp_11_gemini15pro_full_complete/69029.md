fattists:/ˈfæ.tɪsts/
noun
肥胖歧视者；对肥胖者有偏见的人
A person who holds prejudiced beliefs and discriminatory attitudes against individuals based on their weight, particularly those considered overweight or obese.
例句：
Fattist attitudes in society can lead to discrimination in employment and healthcare.
社会上的肥胖歧视态度会导致就业和医疗保健方面的歧视。

Some argue that certain weight loss programs promote fattist ideals.
有些人认为某些减肥计划助长了对肥胖者的偏见。

近义词：
bigot, prejudiced person
反义词:
(No direct antonym, but consider) body positive advocate, acceptance advocate

词性:
n.
