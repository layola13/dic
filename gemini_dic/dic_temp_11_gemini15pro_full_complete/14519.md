avenue:/ˈævənjuː/
n.
大街；林荫道；途径；方法
A broad road in a town or city, typically having trees planted along either side.  A way of approaching a problem or making progress toward something.
We strolled along the avenue. 我们沿着林荫道散步。
Exploring different avenues for funding is crucial for startups.  探索不同的融资途径对初创企业至关重要。

近义词:street, road, boulevard, path, approach, route
反义词:dead end, impasse
