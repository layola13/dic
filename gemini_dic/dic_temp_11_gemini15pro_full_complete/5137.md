allegro's:/əˈlɛɡroʊz/
|n.|快板乐曲；活泼的乐段|A musical piece or passage performed in a quick and lively tempo.  It is the possessive form of "allegro", indicating ownership or association.|The orchestra played one of Vivaldi's allegros.|Synonyms: lively tunes, quick movements, brisk pieces|Antonyms: adagio, lento|noun
