break-it-down:/breɪk ɪt daʊn/ | phrasal verb | 分解它；详细解释它 | To explain or analyze something in detail, step by step. |  Let's break it down and see what each part means. 让我们把它分解一下，看看每个部分是什么意思。

Could you break down the costs for me? 你能帮我分解一下成本吗？


近义词: analyze, explain, dissect, simplify, decipher, interpret | 反义词: complicate, confuse, obscure | 词性: 动词短语
