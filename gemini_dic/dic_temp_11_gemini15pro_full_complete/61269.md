emasculate:/ɪˈmæskjʊleɪt/ | v. adj. | 阉割；使柔弱；削弱 | To deprive of strength, vigor, or spirit; to weaken.  To castrate. |  The king was politically emasculated by the new constitution. 国王被新宪法削弱了政治权力。 |  The edited version of the film felt emasculated, lacking the original's raw power.  这部电影的编辑版感觉被削弱了，缺乏原作的原始力量。 | 近义词: weaken, debilitate, enfeeble, castrate, neuter | 反义词: strengthen, empower, invigorate | 词性: 动词，形容词
