grouse:/ɡraʊs/| n. |松鸡；抱怨，牢骚|A type of game bird; a complaint.|例句：The hunter bagged two grouse. 猎人捕获了两只松鸡。

例句：He had a grouse about the food. 他抱怨食物不好。|近义词：complaint, grumble, moan, gripe|反义词：praise, compliment|词性：名词

grouse:/ɡraʊs/| vi. |抱怨，发牢骚|To complain.|例句：He's always grousing about something. 他总是抱怨一些事情。

例句：She groused about the long hours. 她抱怨工作时间太长。|近义词：complain, grumble, moan, gripe|反义词：praise, compliment|词性：动词

词源：From Old French *grosse, of unknown origin.
