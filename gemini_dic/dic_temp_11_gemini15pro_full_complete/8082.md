anthroponomics:/ˌænθrəpəˈnɒmɪks/
n.
人类经济学；人事经济学
The study of the economic activities and behavior of humans, especially as individuals or groups within a society.  It examines how human actions and decisions influence economic systems and outcomes.
Anthroponomics plays a vital role in understanding consumer behavior. 人类经济学在理解消费者行为方面起着至关重要的作用。
The professor's lecture on anthroponomics shed light on the complexities of human economic interactions.  教授关于人类经济学的讲座阐明了人类经济相互作用的复杂性。

近义词: human economics, behavioral economics
反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially macroeconomics if contrasting with individual focus)
词性: noun
