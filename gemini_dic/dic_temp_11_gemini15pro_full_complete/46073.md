cyclonic:/saɪˈklɒnɪk/ (UK), /saɪˈklɑːnɪk/ (US)| adj.|气旋的；飓风的；旋风的|Relating to or characteristic of a cyclone; rotating outward from a low-pressure center.|例句：The cyclonic winds caused widespread damage. 气旋风造成了大范围的破坏。|例句：Cyclonic activity is common in this region during the summer months.  夏季，气旋活动在这个地区很常见。|近义词：rotating, whirling, spiral, stormy, tempestuous|反义词：anticyclonic|词性:形容词
