eudemon:/ˈjuːdiːmən/ |n.|守护神; 好精灵; 福神|A benevolent spirit; a good genius. | Socrates believed he had a eudemon who guided him. 苏格拉底相信他有一个引导他的守护神。| synonym: guardian angel, guiding spirit | antonym: evil spirit, demon | noun


eudaemon:/juːˈdiːmən/ |adj.|幸福的; 幸运的; 繁荣的| Characterized by happiness, good fortune, or prosperity. | The family lived in a eudaemon state. 这家人过着幸福的生活。| synonym: blessed, fortunate, prosperous | antonym: unhappy, unfortunate, wretched | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *eudaimōn* "possessed of a good guardian spirit," from *eu* "good, well" + *daimōn* "divinity, spirit."
