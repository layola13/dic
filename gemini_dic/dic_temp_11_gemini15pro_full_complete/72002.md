flintiest:/ˈflɪntiɪst/
adjective
最坚硬的；最无情的
(superlative of) flint-like; extremely hard and unyielding; showing no sympathy or pity.
He gave me the flintiest stare I had ever seen. 他给了我我见过的最无情的凝视。
Synonyms: hardest, most unyielding, most inflexible, sternest, most ruthless
Antonyms: softest, most compassionate, most merciful
词性: 形容词

flinty:/ˈflɪnti/
adjective
坚硬的；无情的
Resembling flint; extremely hard and unyielding; showing no sympathy or pity.
His flinty gaze made me uncomfortable. 他坚硬的目光让我感到不舒服。
Synonyms: hard, unyielding, inflexible, stern, ruthless
Antonyms: soft, compassionate, merciful
词性: 形容词

flint:/flɪnt/
noun
燧石
A hard gray rock consisting of nearly pure silica (silicon dioxide).
Early humans used flint to make tools. 早期人类使用燧石制作工具。
Synonyms: chert, quartz
Antonyms: N/A
词性: 名词
