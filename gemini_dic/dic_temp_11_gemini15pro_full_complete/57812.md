drek:/drɛk/
noun
垃圾，废物，劣质品
Rubbish; nonsense; something worthless or of low quality.
That movie was pure drek.  那部电影纯粹是垃圾。
synonyms:trash, garbage, rubbish, junk, crap
antonyms:treasure, gem, masterpiece
词性:n.


drek:/drɛk/
interjection
表示厌恶或恼怒的感叹词 (mild oath)
Expressing disgust or annoyance.
"Drek!" he muttered, kicking the broken machine. “该死！”他咕哝着，踢了那台坏掉的机器。
synonyms:damn, crap, shoot
antonyms:(No direct antonyms for interjections expressing negative emotion)
词性:interj.
