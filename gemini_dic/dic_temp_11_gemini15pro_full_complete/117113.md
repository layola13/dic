mealy:/ˈmiːli/| adj.|粉状的；粗糙的；（水果）绵软的|Covered with or containing meal; resembling meal in texture; (of fruit) soft and dry.|The apples were old and mealy. 这些苹果又老又绵软。|近义词：powdery, floury, dry, soft|反义词:firm, crisp, juicy|adjective

mealy:/ˈmiːli/| adj.|（昆虫）多粉的，覆有粉状物的|Covered with a powdery substance, as certain insects.|The mealybug is a common pest of houseplants.  粉蚧是室内植物常见的害虫。|近义词：powdery, dusty|反义词:smooth, glossy|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *mealu, from Proto-Germanic *melwą (“meal”), from Proto-Indo-European *melh₂- (“to grind, mill”). Cognate with Dutch meel (“meal”), German Mehl (“flour”), Latin molō (“grind”).
