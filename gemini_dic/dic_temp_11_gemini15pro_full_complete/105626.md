kinetically:/kɪˈnɛtɪkli/
adverb|动力学地，运动地|In a way that relates to motion or the action of forces.|The reaction proceeds kinetically faster at higher temperatures.  反应在较高温度下动力学上进行得更快。|
近义词:dynamically, actively|反义词:statically, passively|词性:副词

Etymology:
From kinetic + -ally.  Kinetic from Greek *kinētikos* “moving,” from *kinein* "to move."
