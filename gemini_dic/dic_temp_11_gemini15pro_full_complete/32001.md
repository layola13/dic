chenilles:/ʃəˈnɪlz/ | n. | 毛毛虫， chenille yarn |A type of yarn with a fuzzy pile, resembling a caterpillar. Also refers to caterpillars themselves, especially in a culinary context. | The soft chenille blanket was perfect for a cozy nap.  柔软的雪尼尔毯子非常适合舒适的小睡。| The chef prepared crispy fried chenilles as an exotic appetizer.  厨师准备了油炸毛毛虫作为一道异国风味的开胃菜。 | Synonyms: caterpillar (especially for the insect), fuzzy yarn | Antonyms: smooth yarn | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French, literally "caterpillar," from Old Northern French *chenille, from Vulgar Latin *canicula "little dog," diminutive of Latin canis "dog."  The yarn is so named from its resemblance to a hairy caterpillar.
