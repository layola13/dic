daughterlike: /ˈdɔːtərlaɪk/ | adj. | 像女儿一样的 | Resembling or characteristic of a daughter; having qualities typically associated with a daughter, such as affection, obedience, or dependence. |  She had a daughterlike affection for her teacher. 她对她的老师有一种像女儿一样的爱。| synonyms: filial, dutiful, respectful, affectionate | antonyms: disobedient, rebellious, disrespectful | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From daughter + -like.  "Daughter" comes from Old English "dohtor," from Proto-Germanic "*dukhter," ultimately from Proto-Indo-European "*dhughə₂tēr," meaning "daughter."  "-like" is a suffix meaning "resembling, characteristic of," from Old English "-līc."
