affronter: /əˈfrʌntər/ (BrE) /əˈfrʌntər/ (AmE)
| v. |冒犯，侮辱; 面对，对抗|To offend or insult someone boldly and openly; to confront or face something difficult or challenging.| He affronted the king by refusing to bow. 他拒绝鞠躬，冒犯了国王。|  She affronted the dangers of the jungle with courage. 她勇敢地面对丛林的危险。|Synonyms: offend, insult, provoke, confront, face, encounter | Antonyms: appease, placate, soothe, avoid, evade| verb
