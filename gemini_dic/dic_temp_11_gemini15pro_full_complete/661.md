abrogative:/ˈæbrəɡətɪv/
adjective
废除的；撤销的
having the power or effect of abolishing or repealing a law, custom, or agreement.
The government introduced abrogative legislation to repeal the outdated law. 政府引入了废除性立法来废除过时的法律。
The king issued an abrogative decree, dissolving the parliament. 国王发布了一项废除法令，解散了议会。
synonyms: repealing, revoking, annulling, nullifying, rescinding
antonyms: enacting, establishing, ratifying, upholding
词性: adjective
