cherty:/ˈtʃɜːrti/ | adj. | 燧石质的，似燧石的，含燧石的 | Resembling chert; containing chert. | The soil in this area is cherty and difficult to cultivate.  这个地区的土壤是燧石质的，难以耕种。 | The cliffs are composed of cherty limestone. 这些悬崖是由含燧石的石灰岩构成的。 | 近义词: flinty, siliceous | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "soft" in certain contexts) | 词性: adjective

词源: From chert + -y.  Chert itself comes from Old French *chiert, perhaps from Late Latin *carcedius, perhaps from Greek kharax 'pointed stake'.
