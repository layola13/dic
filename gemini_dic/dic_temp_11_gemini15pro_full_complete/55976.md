dmc:/ˌdiː ɛm ˈsiː/ |abbr.| 数字媒体版权 |Digital Media Copyright,  a system of digital rights management (DRM) developed by Sony and used for encrypting and protecting digital music content on its devices. | Example: The dmc format was used on Sony's Atrac players.  索尼的Atrac播放器使用了dmc格式。 |Synonym: DRM (Digital Rights Management)|Antonym:  None (as it is a specific system, not a concept with an opposite)| abbreviation
