macabre:/məˈkɑːbrə/ /məˈkɑːb(r)/| adj.|可怕的，恐怖的，令人毛骨悚然的|Suggesting the horror of death and decay; gruesome.|例句：The movie had a macabre fascination with death. 这部电影对死亡有一种令人毛骨悚然的迷恋。|例句：The macabre decorations included skeletons and cobwebs. 恐怖的装饰包括骷髅和蜘蛛网。|近义词：gruesome, ghastly, morbid, eerie, creepy|反义词：pleasant, cheerful, delightful|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From French *macabre*, perhaps from *dance macabre* "dance of death".
