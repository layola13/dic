bullhead:/ˈbʊlˌhɛd/ (US), /ˈbʊlhed/ (UK)| n.|牛头；倔强的人；鲶鱼的一种|A bullhead is a type of fish with a large head. It can also refer to a stubborn person. |例句：The fisherman caught a bullhead in the river. 渔夫在河里钓到一条牛头鱼。

例句：He's such a bullhead; he never listens to anyone's advice. 他真是个倔脾气，从来不听别人的劝告。|近义词：chub, catfish, stubborn person|反义词：compliant person|noun


bullheaded:/ˈbʊlˌhɛdɪd/ (US), /ˈbʊlhedɪd/ (UK)| adj.|固执的；顽固的|Stubborn and unwilling to change one's mind.|例句：She's so bullheaded that she refused to admit she was wrong. 她太固执了，不肯承认自己错了。

例句：His bullheaded determination eventually paid off. 他顽固的决心最终得到了回报。|近义词：stubborn, obstinate, headstrong|反义词：flexible, compliant, amenable|adjective
