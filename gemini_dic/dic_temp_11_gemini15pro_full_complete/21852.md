bodiced:/ˈbɒdɪst/ /ˈbɑːdəst/| adj.|紧身胸衣的，穿着紧身胸衣的|Fitted with or wearing a bodice.  A bodice is the upper part of a woman's dress.|The princess wore a beautiful, bodiced gown. 公主穿着一件漂亮的紧身胸衣礼服。|近义词：close-fitting, tight-fitting|反义词:loose, flowing|词性:形容词
词源: From "bodice" + "-ed".  "Bodice" comes from the earlier "body" (meaning the main part of a garment) + diminutive suffix "-ice".
