deauthorizing: /diːˈɔːθəˌraɪzɪŋ/
verb
取消授权，撤销授权
The act of revoking or removing authorization or permission previously granted.
例句：He is deauthorizing his old computer from accessing his cloud account.  他正在取消他的旧电脑访问他的云账户的授权。

例句：The company is deauthorizing third-party apps to enhance security.  公司正在取消第三方应用程序的授权以增强安全性。

近义词：revoking, rescinding, canceling, withdrawing
反义词：authorizing, granting, permitting
词性：verb (present participle)

deauthorize: /diːˈɔːθəˌraɪz/
verb
取消授权，撤销授权
To revoke or remove authorization or permission previously granted.
例句：I need to deauthorize my old phone from my streaming service. 我需要取消我的旧手机的流媒体服务授权。

例句：She deauthorized the app's access to her location data. 她取消了该应用程序访问她位置数据的授权。

近义词：revoke, rescind, cancel, withdraw
反义词：authorize, grant, permit
词性：verb


词源: de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + authorize (verb meaning "to give official permission")
