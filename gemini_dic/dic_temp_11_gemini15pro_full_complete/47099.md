dauby:/ˈdɔːbi/| adj.|涂抹的；拙劣的；污脏的|Smeared or coated with a sticky substance; of poor quality; shoddy.  Also, dirty or grimy.|The painting was dauby and amateurish. 这幅画涂抹得很拙劣，像业余水平。|近义词：smeared, messy, shoddy, clumsy, crude, amateurish|反义词：clean, neat, tidy, skillful, professional|词性:形容词
dauby:/ˈdɔːbi/| n.|拙劣的艺术作品|A work of art of poor quality.|The critic dismissed the painting as a dauby.  评论家认为这幅画是拙劣之作。|近义词：mess, botch|反义词：masterpiece|词性:名词
