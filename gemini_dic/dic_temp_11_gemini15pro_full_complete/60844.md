electrotonic:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈtɒnɪk/
adjective
电紧张的
Relating to or denoting the passive spread of electrical potentials in nerve and muscle fibres, without the generation of new current.
Electrotonic potentials spread passively and decrementally along a nerve fiber. 电紧张电位沿着神经纤维被动地和递减地传播。
electrotonic spread of current 电流的电紧张传播
close: passive, decremental
