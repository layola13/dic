moling:/ˈmoʊlɪŋ/ | n. | 穴居;  打洞;  蜕皮; 换毛|The act of an animal digging a burrow or tunnel, shedding skin or fur, or the period of shedding.  It can also refer to the softened state of earth after plowing.|The moles were moling in the garden.  鼹鼠在花园里打洞。|近义词：burrowing, digging, shedding, moulting|反义词: - |词性:noun
