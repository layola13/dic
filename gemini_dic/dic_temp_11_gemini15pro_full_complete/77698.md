gemini: /ˈdʒɛmɪnaɪ/ (US), /ˈdʒɛmɪnʌɪ/ (UK)| adj.| 双子的；双子座的|Of or relating to the constellation or astrological sign Gemini.|She has a Gemini personality, always changing her mind.|近义词：Dual, twin|反义词: None|词性: Adjective
