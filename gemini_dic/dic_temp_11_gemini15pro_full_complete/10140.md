apology:/əˈpɒlədʒi/ /əˈpɑːlədʒi/| n.|道歉，歉意|A regretful acknowledgment of an offense or failure. |I owe you an apology. 我欠你一个道歉。|synonyms: regrets, excuses, amends |antonyms:  disavowals,  rejections|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *apologie*, from Latin *apologia* "speech in defense," from Greek *apologia*  "a speech in defense," from *apo* "off, away from" + *logos*  "speech, word, reason."
