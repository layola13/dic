noncitations:/ˌnɒnsaɪˈteɪʃənz/
|n.|未引用; 未引证; 缺乏引用|The paper was criticized for its noncitations of relevant previous research.  这篇论文因没有引用相关的先前研究而受到批评。| The high rate of noncitations in undergraduate essays is a concern.  本科论文中未引用的高比例令人担忧。|近义词: omissions, exclusions|反义词: citations, references|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + citation (from cite, meaning "to quote" or "to summon"). Ultimately from Latin citare "to summon, urge, put in sudden motion, call."
