electro-oculographic:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˌɒkjʊləˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. | 眼电图的 | Relating to electro-oculography, a technique for measuring the corneo-retinal potential difference. | Electro-oculographic recordings are used to study eye movements. 眼电图记录用于研究眼球运动。|  Synonyms: electrooculographic | Antonyms: N/A | adjective
