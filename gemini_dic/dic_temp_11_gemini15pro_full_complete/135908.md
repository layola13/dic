nonpsychological:/ˌnɒnˌsaɪkəˈlɒdʒɪkl/ | adj. | 非心理的；非心理学上的 | Not related to or involving psychology; not of or pertaining to the mind or mental processes. | Her problems were nonpsychological, stemming from a physical ailment. 她的问题不是心理上的，而是源于身体疾病。|  The report focused on the nonpsychological factors contributing to homelessness. 这份报告重点关注导致无家可归的非心理因素。 | 近义词: nonmental, physical, somatic | 反义词: psychological, mental | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "psychological" (adjective relating to psychology).  "Psychology" comes from Greek "psyche" (soul, mind) + "-logia" (study of).
