account:/əˈkaʊnt/ | n. vt. |账户；账目；描述；解释；认为|An arrangement with a bank to keep your money there and to allow you to take it out when you need to.  A written or spoken description of something that has happened. To think of someone or something in a particular way; consider or regard. |I paid the money into my bank account. 我把钱存入了我的银行账户。|Synonyms: report, description, narrative, story, chronicle, record, narration, statement, recital |Antonyms: disregard, ignore| Noun, Verb

account:/əˈkaʊnt/ |  v. |（在数量或比例方面）占|To form the total of something. |Students account for the vast majority of our customers. 学生占我们顾客的绝大多数。|Synonyms: constitute, compose, comprise, make up, form |Antonyms: exclude| Verb
