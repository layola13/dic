just-in-time:/ˌdʒʌst ɪn ˈtaɪm/ | adj. adv.| 准时的；即时的；及时生产的 | A system of manufacturing based on the ordered arrival of supplies only as needed, so that no stockpiles are required. |  The company uses a just-in-time inventory system.  这家公司采用准时制库存系统。 |  The parts arrived just in time for the assembly.  零件正好及时到达组装现场。 |  timely, prompt, immediate | late, delayed | adjective, adverb
