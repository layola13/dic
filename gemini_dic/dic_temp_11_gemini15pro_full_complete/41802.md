cortexes:/ˈkɔːrteksɪz/
n.
大脑皮层（复数）
The outer layer of an organ, especially the cerebrum.
The cortexes of their brains showed significant activity during the test.  测试期间他们的大脑皮层显示出显著的活动。
cortices
cerebral cortex, outer layer
medulla, inner layer
noun
