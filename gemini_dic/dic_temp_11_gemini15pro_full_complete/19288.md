biases:/ˈbaɪəsiz/ /ˈbaɪəsɪz/| n.|偏见，偏向，偏差|A tendency to prefer one person or thing to another, and to favor that person or thing.  A systematic distortion of a statistical result due to a factor not allowed for in its derivation.|例句：His biases prevented him from seeing the truth. 他的偏见让他无法看到真相。

例句：The study showed a clear bias towards younger applicants. 这项研究表明，对年轻申请人有明显的偏向。|近义词：prejudices, preconceptions, inclinations, predispositions|反义词：impartiality, objectivity, fairness|词性:noun
