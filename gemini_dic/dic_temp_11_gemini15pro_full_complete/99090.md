interbreeding:/ˌɪntərˈbriːdɪŋ/
n.|杂交，异种交配|The interbreeding of different species can lead to unpredictable results. 不同物种的杂交会导致不可预测的结果。|
近义词：crossbreeding, hybridization|
反义词：purebreeding|
词性：名词

interbreeding:/ˌɪntərˈbriːdɪŋ/
v.|（使）杂交，（使）异种交配|Scientists are studying the effects of interbreeding between wolves and dogs. 科学家正在研究狼和狗杂交的影响。|
近义词：crossbreed, hybridize|
反义词：purebreed|
词性：动词
