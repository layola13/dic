apes:/eɪps/ | n. | 猿; 类人猿 | Large primates, including gorillas, chimpanzees, orangutans, and gibbons, closely related to humans. | Example 1: The apes swung effortlessly through the trees.  猿猴毫不费力地在树间荡来荡去。
Example 2:  Researchers study the behavior of apes to learn more about human evolution. 研究人员研究猿类的行为以了解更多关于人类进化的情况。 | Synonyms: primates, simians | Antonyms: humans (in a narrow sense, excluding humans from the primate group) | Noun

Phrase 1: great apes (大型类人猿) - This refers to gorillas, chimpanzees, orangutans, and bonobos.  这指的是大猩猩、黑猩猩、猩猩和倭黑猩猩。
Phrase 2: lesser apes (小型类人猿) - This refers to gibbons.  这指的是长臂猿。
Phrase 3: ape around (胡闹; 模仿) - To behave in a silly or playful way, often imitating someone.  表现得愚蠢或爱玩，经常模仿别人。
Phrase 4: go ape (发狂; 变得非常兴奋) - To become extremely excited or enthusiastic about something. 对某事变得极其兴奋或热情。
Phrase 5: ape-like (像猿猴的) - Resembling an ape in appearance or behavior.  在外貌或行为上像猿猴。
