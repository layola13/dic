oars:/ɔːrz/
n.
桨;橹
a pair of long poles with a flat blade at one end, used for rowing a boat
The oars dipped rhythmically into the water. 桨有节奏地划入水中。
近义词: paddles, sculls
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 engine, motor)
词性: 名词

oars:/ɔːrz/
Etymology: From Old English *ār, from Proto-Germanic *ōrōm, from Proto-Indo-European *h₁reh₁- (“to move”). Cognate with Old Norse ár (“oar”), Old High German āro (“oar”).
词源：来自古英语 *ār，源自原始日耳曼语 *ōrōm，源自原始印欧语 *h₁reh₁-（“移动”）。与古诺尔斯语 ár（“桨”）、古高地德语 āro（“桨”）同源。
