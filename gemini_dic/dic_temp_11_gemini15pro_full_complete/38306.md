concealability:/kənˌsiːləˈbɪləti/
n.
可隐蔽性，隐蔽性
the quality of being easy to conceal
The small size of the weapon increased its concealability.  这种武器的小尺寸增加了它的隐蔽性。
近义词：hidability, disguisability
反义词：noticeability, visibility
词性：noun

conceal:/kənˈsiːl/
v.
隐藏，隐瞒
keep from sight; hide
He tried to conceal his disappointment. 他试图掩饰他的失望。
近义词：hide, disguise, mask
反义词：reveal, expose, show
词性：verb

-able:/əbl/
suffix
可...的，能...的
capable of; susceptible of
This is a washable fabric. 这是一种可洗的织物。
近义词：capable, possible
反义词：incapable, impossible
词性：suffix
