disproportioning:/ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːʃənɪŋ/
| v. |使不成比例；使不相称；歧视；|The algorithm was disproportioning resources, favoring larger cities over smaller towns. 该算法不成比例地分配资源，偏向大城市而不是小城镇。|
| verb | 使不成比例；使不相称；|The study showed that minorities were disproportionately affected by the new policy.  研究表明，少数族裔受到新政策的影响尤为严重。|
|例句：The policy is disproportioning funding, leaving rural schools underfunded. 这项政策导致资金分配不均衡，使农村学校资金不足。|
|例句：The algorithm is disproportioning opportunities, giving some users an unfair advantage. 该算法机会分配不均，给予某些用户不公平的优势。 |
|近义词：biasing, skewing, weighting, distorting|
|反义词：balancing, equalizing, standardizing|
|词性: 动词(gerund or present participle of disproportion)|
