antiatheists:/ˌæntiˈeɪθiɪsts/
|n.|反无神论者|A person who opposes antitheism, often by defending the right to hold atheistic beliefs or by arguing against the perceived negativity or hostility towards atheists.|Antiatheists advocate for respectful dialogue and tolerance towards those who do not believe in God. 反无神论者主张尊重对话和容忍那些不相信上帝的人。|Synonyms: supporter of atheism, defender of non-belief|Antonyms: antitheist, opponent of atheism|noun
