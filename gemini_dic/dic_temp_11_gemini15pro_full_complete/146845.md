overindexing:/ˌoʊvərˈɪndɛksɪŋ/ | n. | 过度索引; 过度加权; 过度代表 | The act of giving something more importance or representation than it deserves based on its actual prevalence or significance.  It often refers to marketing strategies that disproportionately target a specific demographic or interest group. |  Marketers are overindexing on social media ads targeting young adults.  营销人员过度索引了针对年轻人的社交媒体广告。

The company is overindexing its investments in renewable energy. 该公司过度加权了其在可再生能源方面的投资。| 近义词: overemphasizing, overrepresenting, overweighting | 反义词: underindexing, underrepresenting, underweighting | 词性: 名词
