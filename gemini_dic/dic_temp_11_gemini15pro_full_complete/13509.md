attagirl: /ˌætəˈɡɜːrl/ (US), /ˌætəˈɡɜːl/ (UK)| int.| 好样的！/ 加油！/ 干得好！| An exclamation of encouragement or approval, typically addressed to a woman or girl.  It acknowledges a job well done or expresses support for continued effort.  Similar to "attaboy" but specifically for females. | Example 1: Attagirl! You finished the marathon!  好样的！你跑完了马拉松！

Example 2: Attagirl, keep trying, you'll get it eventually. 加油，继续努力，你最终会成功的。 | 近义词: well done, good job, way to go, that's the way, you go girl (informal) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but disapproval could be expressed with phrases like "that's terrible" or "you messed up") | 词性: interjection
