lithotint: /ˈlɪθəʊtɪnt/
noun
石版画着色剂
A color medium used in lithographic printing, often a greasy ink or crayon.
The artist used a variety of lithotints to create the subtle shading in the print.  艺术家使用了各种石版画着色剂来在版画中创造微妙的阴影。
近义词: lithographic ink, tusche
反义词: N/A
词性: n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From "litho-" (Greek lithos, "stone") + "tint" (from Old French teindre, "to dye").  来自希腊语lithos（石头）和tint（来自古法语teindre，意为染色）。
