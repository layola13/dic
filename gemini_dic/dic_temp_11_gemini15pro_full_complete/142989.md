outbarter:/ˌaʊtˈbɑːrtər/
verb
胜过交易；以物易物胜过
to surpass someone in bartering or trading.
He outbartered the local merchants and got the best price for his goods. 他胜过当地商人的交易，为他的货物争取到了最好的价格。
Synonyms: outtrade, outsmart in trading
Antonyms: be outtraded, be outsmarted in trading
词性: 动词
