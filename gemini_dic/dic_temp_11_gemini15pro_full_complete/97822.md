inkinesses:/ˈɪŋkɪnɪsɪz/
n.
墨黑; 漆黑
the quality or state of being very dark or black like ink.
The inkinesses of the night sky were breathtaking. 夜空的漆黑令人叹为观止。
synonyms: blackness, darkness, obscurity
antonyms: brightness, lightness, whiteness
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From inky + -ness + -es (plural suffix). Inky itself comes from ink + -y (adjective suffix).  The root "ink" traces back to Old French "enque," from Late Latin "encaustum," meaning "burnt in," referring to a purple-red ink.
