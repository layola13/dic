monandric:/məˈnændrɪk/ | adj. | 單雄蕊的；一雄蕊的 | Having only one stamen.  A flower having only one stamen. |  This orchid species is monandric, possessing a single stamen in each flower.  这种兰花是单雄蕊的，每朵花只有一个雄蕊。| Synonym: monandrous | Antonym: polyandrous | Adjective
