mid:/mɪd/| n.|中部，中间|the middle part or point|The arrow hit the mid of the target. 箭射中了靶心。|Synonyms: center, middle, heart|Antonyms: edge, periphery, boundary|noun

mid:/mɪd/| prep.|在...中间|amid, among|They found themselves mid a chaotic crowd. 他们发现自己身处混乱的人群中。|Synonyms: amid, among, amidst|Antonyms: outside, beyond, away from|preposition
