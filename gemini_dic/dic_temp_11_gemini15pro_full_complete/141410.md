ophiomorpha:/ˌɒfɪəˈmɔːrfə/| n. |蛇形迹|A trace fossil, typically a burrow, characterized by a distinctive lined and pelleted structure, believed to have been made by crustaceans.|The ophiomorpha found in the sandstone indicate a shallow marine environment.|近义词:Thalassinoides|反义词: (None, as it's a unique trace fossil)|noun
