functionalistic:/ˌfʌŋkʃənəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
功能主义的
Relating to or characteristic of functionalism, a theory that emphasizes the practical functions of things, especially in social contexts.
This building has a functionalistic design, prioritizing utility over aesthetics.  这栋楼是功能主义的设计，实用性重于美观。
The architect took a functionalistic approach, focusing on how the space would be used.  这位建筑师采用功能主义的方法，重点关注空间将如何使用。
近义词:practical, utilitarian, pragmatic
反义词:decorative, ornamental, aesthetic
词性:adjective
