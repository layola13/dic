mensch:/mɛntʃ/ | n. | 人；正人君子；体面的人 | A person of integrity and honor. | He's a real mensch, always helping others. 他是个真正的君子，总是帮助别人。 |  近义词: good person, decent person, upstanding citizen | 反义词: jerk, scoundrel, villain | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Yiddish מענטש (mentsh), from Middle High German mensch, from Old High German mennisco, from Proto-Germanic *manniskō, from *mann- (“man”). Cognate with German Mensch (“human being”).
