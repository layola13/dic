bellpull: /ˈbelpʊl/ | n. | 门铃拉绳，铃铛拉绳 | A cord or chain hanging from a bell, which is pulled to ring the bell. |  The bellpull frayed and eventually snapped.  门铃拉绳磨损了，最后断了。

She tugged the bellpull sharply to summon the maid. 她猛地拉了拉铃绳，把女佣叫来。 | synonym: bell rope, bell cord | antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps doorbell button in modern context) | noun
