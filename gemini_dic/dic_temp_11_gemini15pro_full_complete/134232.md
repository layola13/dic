noninertial:/ˌnɒnɪˈnɜːʃəl/ | adj. | 非惯性的 |  Not inertial; referring to a frame of reference that is accelerating and therefore does not obey Newton's laws of motion without the introduction of fictitious forces. |  Example: In a noninertial frame, like a rotating carousel, you feel a centrifugal force pushing you outwards, even though there's no actual force pushing you. 在旋转木马这样的非惯性系中，你会感觉到离心力把你往外推，即使实际上并没有力推你。

Example:  Calculations involving rotating objects are often simplified by considering a noninertial rotating frame of reference.  涉及旋转物体的计算通常通过考虑非惯性旋转参考系来简化。 | 近义词: accelerated | 反义词: inertial | 词性: 形容词
