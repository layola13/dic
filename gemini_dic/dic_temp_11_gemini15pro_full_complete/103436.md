jivest:/ˈdʒaɪvɛst/| v.| 符合，一致；赋予…以生气；使…活跃起来 (archaic, or humorous)| This word is archaic and rarely used in modern English. It means to be in agreement or accord with something, or to give life or spirit to something. | Example: "Thy actions jivest not with thy words." 你的行为与你的言语不符。(archaic) | Synonym: accord, agree, harmonize, energize, enliven | Antonym: clash, disagree, conflict | verb
