drunker:/ˈdrʌŋkər/ | adj. | 更醉的 | Comparative adjective of "drunk," meaning more intoxicated. | He got drunker as the night went on. 随着夜幕降临，他越来越醉了。|  He was much drunker than he appeared. 他比看起来要醉得多。 | 近义词:more intoxicated, tipsier | 反义词:soberer, less drunk | 词性:形容词

drunk:/drʌŋk/ | adj. | 醉的 | Affected by alcohol to the extent of losing control of one's faculties or behavior. | He was too drunk to drive home. 他醉得不能开车回家了。 | She got quite drunk at the party. 她在派对上喝得酩酊大醉。| 近义词:intoxicated, inebriated | 反义词:sober | 词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *dronken, from Old English *druncen, past participle of drincan (“to drink”). Cognate with West Frisian dronken, Dutch dronken, German trunken.
