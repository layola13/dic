kofun:/ˈkoʊfuːn/| n. | 古坟，古墳 | An ancient Japanese burial mound, often keyhole-shaped.  A large, mounded tomb of powerful people in ancient Japan, often keyhole-shaped when viewed from above. |  The kofun period in Japan lasted from the 3rd to the 7th century.  日本古坟时代持续了从3世纪到7世纪。

The largest kofun is the tomb of Emperor Nintoku.  最大的古坟是仁德天皇陵。| 近义词：tumulus, burial mound | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "excavation" in the context of archaeological discovery) | 词性: noun
