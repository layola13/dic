aquaplane:/ˈækwəpleɪn/ (US), /ˈækwəpleɪn/ (UK)| vi.|滑水|To skim along the surface of water, as on an aquaplane.|She loves to aquaplane on the lake. 她喜欢在湖上滑水。|He was aquaplaning when he lost control and fell. 他正在滑水时失去了控制，摔了下来。|近义词：water ski, surf|反义词: N/A|词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From aqua (water) + plane (a flat surface).  The word originally referred to hydroplanes, which skim over the water, and later came to mean the board used for this activity.
