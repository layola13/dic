camper:/ˈkæmpər/ (US) /ˈkæmpə(r)/ (UK)| n.|露营者；野营车；露营拖车|A person who stays and sleeps in a tent or other temporary shelter, usually outdoors for recreation. A vehicle equipped for camping, often with cooking and sleeping facilities.|We saw many campers setting up their tents by the lake. 我们看到许多露营者在湖边搭帐篷。|They bought a new camper for their summer road trip. 他们买了一辆新的露营车用于夏季公路旅行。|Synonyms: campervan, motorhome, caravan, tent-dweller, holidaymaker|Antonyms: resident, homeowner|noun
