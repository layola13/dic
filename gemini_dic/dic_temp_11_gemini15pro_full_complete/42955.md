covered:/ˈkʌvərd/ (US), /ˈkʌvəd/ (UK)| adj. v. |被覆盖的；隐蔽的；有屋顶的；已投保的|covered means something is over or on top of something else, concealing it or protecting it. It can also mean a topic has been dealt with or an area has been traveled over.|The table was covered with a white cloth. 桌子上盖着一块白布。|近义词：protected, shielded, concealed, draped, insured|反义词：uncovered, exposed, bare, uninsured|词性: adjective/verb

covered:/ˈkʌvərd/ (US), /ˈkʌvəd/ (UK)| v. |覆盖；遮蔽；涉及；报道；掩护；给…保险；行走（一段路程）|The past participle of "cover". It indicates an action that has been completed.|He covered the sleeping child with a blanket. 他给睡着的孩子盖上了一条毯子。|近义词：protected, shielded, concealed, draped, insured, reported|反义词：uncovered, exposed, bare, uninsured|词性: verb


词源: From Middle English *covered, *kevered, from Anglo-Norman *cuvert, past participle of covrir, from Old French covrir, from Latin cooperīre (to cover completely), from com- + operīre (to cover, close, conceal).
