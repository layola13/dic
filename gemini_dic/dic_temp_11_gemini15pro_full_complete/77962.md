genteelest:/ˌdʒɛnˈtiːlɪst/
adjective
最高级的；最上流的；最优雅的
most refined; most polite; most aristocratic in manner
例句：He frequented the genteelest clubs in London. 他经常光顾伦敦最高档的俱乐部。
例句：Her genteelest manners were reserved for special occasions.  她最优雅的举止是留给特殊场合的。
近义词：refined, polished, cultured, aristocratic, elegant, courteous, polite
反义词：vulgar, coarse, unrefined, uncouth, rude, impolite


ETYMOLOGY->
From French gentil, meaning "noble, high-born, courteous," ultimately from Latin gentilis, meaning "of the same clan or tribe." The suffix -est denotes the superlative form.
