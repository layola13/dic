failsafe:/ˈfeɪlseɪf/| adj. n.|自动防故障的；故障保险装置|Designed to prevent malfunction or to minimize damage if malfunction occurs.  A system, procedure, or device that is intended to prevent failure or to minimize its harmful effects in the event of failure.|The plane has a failsafe device that will deploy the landing gear automatically if the pilot forgets.|近义词：foolproof, reliable, secure|反义词: risky, unreliable, dangerous|词性:形容词/名词

failsafe:/ˈfeɪlseɪf/| n.|故障保险装置；保险措施|A system, procedure, or device that is intended to prevent failure or to minimize its harmful effects in the event of failure.|The nuclear power plant has several failsafes to prevent a meltdown.|近义词：backup, safeguard, precaution|反义词: hazard, risk, danger|词性:名词
