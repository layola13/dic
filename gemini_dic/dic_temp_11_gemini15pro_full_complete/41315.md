coreference:/koʊˈrɛfərəns/| n.|指代同一实体|The repetition of a particular expression, or the use of a substitute expression, to refer to the same entity throughout a text or discourse.  It links different expressions that refer to the same entity.|Example: "Barack Obama gave a speech. The president discussed healthcare."  "Barack Obama" and "The president" are coreferences, both referring to the same individual.|近义词：anaphora, co-reference|反义词: N/A|noun
