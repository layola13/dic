allegation: /ˌæləˈɡeɪʃn/ | n. | 指控，说法；断言 | a claim or assertion that someone has done something illegal or wrong, typically one made without proof | The newspaper published allegations of corruption in the government. 这家报纸刊登了政府腐败的指控。| 近义词：accusation, claim, assertion | 反义词：denial, refutation | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From Late Latin *allegationem, accusative singular of *allegatio (“bringing forward, adducing; citation of an author”), from allegatus, past participle of allegare, itself from Latin ad (“to, toward”) + legare (“depute, send, bequeath, appoint”)
