hyperaware:/ˌhaɪpərəˈwɛər/| adj.|过度警觉的，过度敏感的|Excessively or abnormally aware; acutely sensitive.|例句：She was hyperaware of her surroundings after the incident. 事件发生后，她对周围环境过度警觉。|近义词：oversensitive, highly sensitive, acutely aware|反义词：oblivious, unaware, insensitive|词性:形容词

Etymology:  From hyper- + aware.  "Hyper-" is a prefix meaning "over, above, beyond," derived from Greek *hupér. "Aware" comes from Old English ġewær, meaning "watchful, wary."
