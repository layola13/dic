motivational:/ˌməʊtɪˈveɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 激励的，鼓舞人心的 | Relating to motivation; providing incentive or encouragement. |  The coach gave a motivational speech to the team before the game. 教练在比赛前给球队做了一次激励人心的演讲。

|  Her motivational words inspired me to pursue my dreams. 她鼓舞人心的话激励我去追求我的梦想。 | Synonyms: inspiring, encouraging, stimulating, uplifting | Antonyms: demotivating, discouraging, disheartening | adjective


Etymology->

From *motivate* + *-tional*. *Motivate* comes from Late Latin *motivatus*, past participle of *movare* "to move," from *motus*, past participle of *movēre* "to move." The suffix *-tional* forms adjectives meaning "relating to" or "of the nature of."
