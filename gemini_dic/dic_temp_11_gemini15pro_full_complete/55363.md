distantial:/dɪˈstænʃəl/
adjective
遥远的；冷漠的
Relating to or characterized by distance, either physical or emotional.  Showing aloofness or reserve in manner.
The distantial mountains were barely visible through the fog. 遥远的山脉在雾中几乎看不见。
He gave a distantial nod, clearly not interested in conversation. 他冷漠地点了点头，显然对谈话不感兴趣。
近义词:remote, distant, aloof, reserved
反义词:close, near, warm, friendly
词性:adjective
词源:From "distant" + "-ial" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Distant" ultimately comes from Latin "distans," present participle of "distare" (to stand apart).
