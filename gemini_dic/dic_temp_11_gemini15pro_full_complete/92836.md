icosahedral:/ˌaɪkɒsəˈhiːdrəl/ | adj. |二十面体的；正二十面体的 | Relating to, or having the shape of an icosahedron (a polyhedron with 20 faces). |  An icosahedral virus has a shell with 20 triangular faces.  二十面体病毒的外壳有20个三角形面。

The icosahedral die rolled across the table.  二十面体的骰子滚过桌子。 | Near synonyms:  twenty-sided, polyhedral | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but consider shapes with different numbers of faces, e.g., cubic, tetrahedral) | word type: adjective
