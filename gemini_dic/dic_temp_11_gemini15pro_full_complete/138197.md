nosedive:/ˈnəʊzdaɪv/ (British) /ˈnoʊzdaɪv/ (American)| n. vi.| 俯冲；暴跌；突然失败|A sudden, steep descent or drop, like that of an airplane, or a rapid decline or deterioration.|例句：The stock market took a nosedive after the news broke.  股市在消息传出后暴跌。

例句：The plane went into a nosedive. 飞机突然俯冲。

近义词：plunge, plummet, crash, slump, tumble|反义词:soar, ascend, rise|词性:名词, 动词
