defector:/dɪˈfɛktər/| n.|叛逃者，变节者|A person who leaves their country or cause to join an opposing one.  A person who abandons their allegiance to a cause, group, or country, especially to join an opposing one.|例句：The defector provided valuable intelligence to the enemy.  这个叛逃者向敌人提供了宝贵的情报。

例句：The government offered amnesty to defectors. 政府对叛逃者给予了赦免。

|近义词：traitor, renegade, deserter|反义词:loyalist, patriot|词性:noun
