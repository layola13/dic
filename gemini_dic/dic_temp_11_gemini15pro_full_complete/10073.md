apocopic:/ˌæpəˈkɒpɪk/ | adj. | 省音的，截尾的 | Characterized by apocope, the loss of one or more sounds from the end of a word. | The word "photo" is an apocopic form of "photograph."  照片是“photograph”的截尾形式。|  The apocopic form "bio" is common for "biology."  “bio”是“biology”的常见截尾形式。 | 近义词: shortened, abbreviated, truncated | 反义词: lengthened, extended | 词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *apokopḗ*, from *apokóptō* "I cut off."
