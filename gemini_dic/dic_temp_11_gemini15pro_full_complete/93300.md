ill-assorted:/ˌɪl əˈsɔːrtɪd/
adjective
不协调的，不相配的
not well matched; incongruous
The furniture was an ill-assorted collection of hand-me-downs. 这些家具是一堆不配套的旧货。
The team was an ill-assorted group of individuals with different skills and backgrounds. 这个团队是由一群技能和背景各不相同的、不协调的个人组成的。
近义词:mismatched, incompatible, incongruous, unsuitable
反义词:well-matched, compatible, suitable, harmonious
