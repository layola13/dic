filamented:/ˈfɪləməntɪd/
adjective
丝状的，有细丝的
Having or consisting of filaments; threadlike.
The spider spun a filamented web.  蜘蛛结了一张丝状的网。
The comet had a long, filamented tail. 这颗彗星有一条长长的丝状尾巴。
近义词: fibrous, threadlike, filamentous
反义词: non-filamentous, solid

词性: adjective
