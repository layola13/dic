outboxed:/ˌaʊtˈbɒkst/
verb
击败，胜过（尤指用更巧妙或更现代的方法）
To defeat or surpass, especially by using a more clever or modern approach.
He outboxed his opponent with his superior strategy. 他以高超的策略击败了对手。
The new company quickly outboxed its competitors by offering innovative products. 这家新公司通过提供创新产品迅速胜过竞争对手。
Synonyms: outmaneuver, outsmart, surpass, defeat
Antonyms: lose to, be defeated by
词性：动词
