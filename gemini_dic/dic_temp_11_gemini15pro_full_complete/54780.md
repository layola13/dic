dispensabilities:/dɪˌspɛnsəˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. |可有可无；非必要性；不重要性|The dispensabilities of certain departments during a recession. 经济衰退时期某些部门的可有可无。|例句：The report highlighted the dispensabilities of certain administrative roles.  这份报告强调了某些行政职位的可有可无性。

例句：The company is evaluating the dispensabilities of various positions in order to cut costs. 公司正在评估各个职位的可有可无性，以削减成本。|近义词：non-essentials, redundancies, superfluities|反义词：essentials, necessities, requirements|词性:名词

词源: From dispensable + -ity.  Dispensable comes from dispense (meaning "to distribute, administer") + -able (suffix meaning "capable of being"). The root of dispense is Latin *dispendere* "to weigh out, pay out."
