heartlessly:/ˈhɑːrtlɪsli/ (US), /ˈhɑːtləsli/ (UK)| adv.|无情地，冷酷地|Without heart; cruelly; unfeelingly. Showing no pity or compassion.|She heartlessly abandoned her family and moved abroad. 她无情地抛弃了家人，移居国外。|He heartlessly ignored her pleas for help. 他无情地无视了她寻求帮助的请求。|Synonyms: cruelly, callously, ruthlessly, pitilessly|Antonyms: compassionately, kindly, mercifully, sympathetically|Part of Speech: Adverb
