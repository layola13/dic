oriflamme: /ˈɔːrɪˌflæm/ | n. | 金色火焰旗；（喻）象征；战旗；指引 | A bright red or orange-red banner used as a standard or ensign.  It originally belonged to the Abbey of Saint-Denis, north of Paris, and was borne by the kings of France in battle. It later became a general term for a banner, especially one associated with a cause or ideal. | The oriflamme of revolution flew high above the barricades.  革命的旗帜在街垒上空高高飘扬。| synonyms: banner, standard, ensign, pennant, flag | antonyms: (No direct antonym, consider "white flag" for surrender) | noun
