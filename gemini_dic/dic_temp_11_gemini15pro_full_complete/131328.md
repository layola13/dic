nonchanneled:/nɒnˈtʃænəld/
adj.
非渠道化的，未引导的
Not directed through a specific channel or medium; not constrained or controlled.
The medication is absorbed through nonchanneled diffusion across the cell membrane.  这种药物通过细胞膜的非通道扩散被吸收。
The artist's nonchanneled creativity led to a truly unique masterpiece. 这位艺术家不受约束的创造力造就了一件真正独一无二的杰作。
近义词:undirected, uncontrolled, free-flowing
反义词:channeled, directed, controlled
词性:adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "channeled" (past participle of "channel," from Old French "chanel" meaning "canal," ultimately from Latin "canalis" meaning "pipe, groove").
