nonsequentially:/ˌnɒnˌsɪˈkwenʃəli/ | adv. | 不按顺序地，无逻辑地 | Not in a sequential or logical order.  | The topics were presented nonsequentially, jumping from one subject to another without any clear connection.  主题的呈现没有按顺序，从一个主题跳到另一个主题，没有任何清晰的联系。|  non-sequentially, randomly, haphazardly | sequentially, logically | adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + sequentially.  "Sequentially" comes from the Latin word "sequentia" meaning "a following." "Non-" is a prefix indicating negation.
