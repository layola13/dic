non-academic:/ˌnɒnækəˈdɛmɪk/ | adj. | 非学术的；非学院的 | Not related to studying or teaching at a school, college, or university; not scholarly. |  Many students pursue non-academic activities like sports and music. 许多学生参加体育和音乐等非学术活动。|  Example:  He has many non-academic interests, such as photography and hiking. 他有很多非学术兴趣，例如摄影和徒步旅行。 | Synonyms: non-scholarly, practical, applied, vocational, informal | Antonyms: academic, scholarly, intellectual | adjective
