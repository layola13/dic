lemmings:/ˈlɛmɪŋz/
noun
旅鼠
Small rodents known for their periodic mass migrations and population fluctuations, often mistakenly believed to commit mass suicide by jumping off cliffs.
Lemmings are small rodents that live in cold regions. 旅鼠是生活在寒冷地区的小型啮齿动物。
The myth of lemmings blindly following each other to their deaths is not true. 旅鼠盲目地跟随彼此走向死亡的神话是不真实的。
synonyms: voles, rodents
antonyms: (no direct antonym for a specific animal)
