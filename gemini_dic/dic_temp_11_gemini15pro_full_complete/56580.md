don't:/dəʊnt/ (UK) /doʊnt/ (US)| aux. v.|不要，不做，没有|Contraction of "do not". Used to form negative sentences in the simple present tense, except for the third person singular. |I don't like spinach. 我不喜欢菠菜。|近义词：doesn't (for third person singular)|反义词:do|词性:auxiliary verb

I don't eat meat. 我不吃肉。
They don't go to school on Sundays. 他们星期天不去上学。

I'm not working now, I'm relaxing. 我现在没在工作，我在休息。(don't is not used in present continuous)

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
