avalanche: /ˈævəlæntʃ/ (US) /ˈævəlɑːnʃ/ (UK)| n. vi.|雪崩，大量涌现|A large mass of snow, ice, and rocks falling rapidly down a mountainside; a sudden arrival of something in overwhelming quantities.|The avalanche buried the village. 雪崩掩埋了村庄。|近义词：landslide, flood, deluge, torrent|反义词: trickle, drip|词性: 名词, 动词

avalanche: /ˈævəlæntʃ/ (US) /ˈævəlɑːnʃ/ (UK)| vi.|像雪崩一样落下；大量涌现|To fall or rush down in large quantities, like an avalanche.|Orders avalanched into the company after the successful advertising campaign.  成功的广告宣传活动之后，订单像雪崩一样涌入公司。|近义词：pour, flood, surge|反义词: dwindle, decrease|词性: 动词

词源：From French *avalanche*, from Franco-Provençal *avalance*, from *avaler* "to descend," from *a val* "downhill," from Latin *ad vallem* "to the valley."
