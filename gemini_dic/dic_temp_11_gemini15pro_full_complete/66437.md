exemplarinesses:/ɪɡˌzɛmplərɪnɪsɪz/| n. | 模范性；典范性（复数） | The state or quality of being exemplary; the quality of serving as a desirable model. |  Her exemplarinesses in both her personal and professional life were admired by all.  她在个人和职业生活中的模范性都受到所有人的钦佩。 | 近义词：perfections, virtues, merits | 反义词：flaws, imperfections, shortcomings | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Latin *exemplaritas, from exemplaris (“serving as an example”) + -itas. Ultimately from Latin exemplum (“example”).
