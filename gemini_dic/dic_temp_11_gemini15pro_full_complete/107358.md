lamellibranchia:/ləˌmɛlɪˈbræŋkiə/
n.
双壳纲动物
A class of aquatic mollusks that have a shell consisting of two hinged valves, including clams, mussels, oysters, and scallops.
The lamellibranchia are filter feeders, using their gills to strain plankton from the water.  双壳纲动物是滤食动物，用它们的鳃从水中过滤浮游生物。
Clams, oysters, and mussels are all members of the lamellibranchia class. 蛤蜊、牡蛎和贻贝都是双壳纲的成员。
Synonyms: bivalve, pelecypod
Antonyms: None (This is a taxonomic classification, so it doesn't have a direct opposite.)
