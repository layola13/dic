catch-all:/ˈkætʃɔːl/| adj. n.|包罗万象的，万能的；包罗万象的事物|A catch-all term or category is one that includes a variety of different things.|This drawer is a catch-all for everything I don't have another place for.  这个抽屉是用来放所有我没有地方放的东西的。|近义词：general, inclusive, broad, miscellaneous|反义词: specific, exclusive|词性:形容词, 名词
catch-all:/ˈkætʃɔːl/| n.|万能的解决办法，综合条款|A catch-all solution or provision is designed to cover a wide range of situations or possibilities.|The law includes a catch-all clause to cover any unforeseen circumstances.  法律中包含一个包罗万象的条款，以涵盖任何不可预见的情况。|近义词：umbrella, blanket|反义词: specialized|词性:名词
