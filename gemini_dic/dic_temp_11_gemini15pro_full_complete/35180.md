cloyment: /klɔɪmənt/ | n. | 就业；雇用（古语，已废弃） | The state of being employed.  This word is archaic and no longer in common use. |  No longer used in modern English.  Older texts might contain sentences like: "His cloyment at the mill brought his family much needed income." (他在磨坊的雇用给他的家庭带来了急需的收入。) | 近义词: employment, occupation | 反义词: unemployment, joblessness | 词性: noun

词源: From Middle English *cloyment, from employ + -ment.  While "employment" became the standard form, "cloyment" fell out of use.
