commies:/ˈkɒmiz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmiz/ (US)| n.|共产党员，共产党同情者 (derogatory)|A derogatory term for communists or supporters of communism.  It carries a strong negative connotation and is often used in a dismissive or hostile way.|例句：He called them a bunch of commies. 他称他们为一群共产党员。|例句：The senator labeled his opponents as commies. 参议员把他的对手贴上共产党的标签。|近义词：reds, communists, socialists (although these aren't perfect synonyms and have differing political meanings)|反义词: capitalists, anti-communists|词性:noun
