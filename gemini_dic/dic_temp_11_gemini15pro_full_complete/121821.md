misregulating: /ˌmɪsˈrɛɡjʊleɪtɪŋ/
verb
错误调节；调节失当
Failing to regulate properly or correctly.
The body's misregulating its temperature can lead to fever. 人体体温调节失常会导致发烧。
mismanaging, miscontrolling, malfunctioning
regulating, controlling, managing
v.
