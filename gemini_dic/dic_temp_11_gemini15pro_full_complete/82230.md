grouches:/ˈɡraʊtʃɪz/ (US), /ˈɡraʊtʃɪz/ (UK)| n.|抱怨，牢骚；脾气坏的人|A grouch is a person who complains a lot or is often in a bad mood. grouches:/ˈɡraʊtʃɪz/ (US), /ˈɡraʊtʃɪz/ (UK)| v.
