insufferably:/ɪnˈsʌfərəbli/| adv.|难以忍受地，令人厌恶地|In a manner that is impossible to bear; intolerably.|His insufferably arrogant attitude made him unpopular. 他那令人难以忍受的傲慢态度让他不受欢迎。|Synonyms: intolerably, unbearably, excruciatingly, impossibly|Antonyms: tolerably, bearably, pleasantly|adverb

insufferable:/ɪnˈsʌfərəbl/| adj.|难以忍受的，令人厌恶的|Impossible to bear; intolerable.|The heat was insufferable. 炎热令人难以忍受。|Synonyms: intolerable, unbearable, excruciating, impossible|Antonyms: tolerable, bearable, pleasant|adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *insufferabilis*, from in- "not" + sufferre "to endure".  Ultimately from Latin sub- "under" + ferre "to bear, carry".
