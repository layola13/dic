overbeating: /ˌoʊvərˈbiːtɪŋ/
verb
过度搅拌，过度敲打
Mixing ingredients too vigorously, especially in baking, resulting in a tough or dense texture.  Also, hitting or striking excessively.
The cake was dense and dry because of overbeating the batter.  蛋糕又硬又干，因为面糊搅拌过度了。
He overbeat the rug, raising a cloud of dust. 他过度拍打地毯，扬起了一阵灰尘。
Synonyms: overmixing, overworking, overwhipping
Antonyms: undermixing, underbeating
词性: 动词

overbeating: /ˌoʊvərˈbiːtɪŋ/
noun
过度搅拌，过度敲打
The act of mixing ingredients too vigorously, or hitting excessively.
Overbeating the egg whites will make them dry and grainy.  过度搅拌蛋白会使它们变得干燥和颗粒状。
The overbeating of the prisoner was a clear violation of his human rights. 对囚犯的过度殴打明显侵犯了他的人权。
Synonyms: overmixing, overworking
Antonyms: undermixing, underbeating
词性: 名词
