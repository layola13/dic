noseband:/ˈnoʊzbænd/ (US) /ˈnəʊzbænd/ (UK)| n. |鼻带; 鼻革|A strap that goes around a horse's nose, usually part of a bridle, used for control.|The rider tightened the noseband to keep the horse from opening its mouth.  骑手收紧了鼻革，防止马张嘴。|Synonyms: headstall, cavesson|Antonyms: N/A (No direct antonym)|noun
