nemertid:/ˈnɛmərˌtɪd/
n.
紐形動物；紐蟲類動物
A nemertean, also called ribbon worm or proboscis worm, any member of the invertebrate phylum Nemertea (sometimes called Rhynchocoela), which includes mainly free-living marine worms.
Nemerteans are long, slender, usually flattened worms.
紐形動物，又稱帶蟲或長吻蟲，是無脊椎動物門紐形動物門（有時稱為吻腔動物門）的任何成員，主要包括自由生活的海洋蠕蟲。紐形動物是長而細的，通常是扁平的蠕蟲。
例句：The nemertid stretched its proboscis to capture its prey.  紐形蟲伸出長吻捕捉獵物。

例句：Researchers discovered a new species of nemertid in the deep sea.  研究人員在深海發現了一種新的紐形蟲。
近义词：ribbon worm, proboscis worm
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a biological classification)
词性: noun
