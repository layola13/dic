malice:/ˈmælɪs/| n.|恶意，怨恨|Desire to inflict injury, harm, or suffering on another, either because of a hostile impulse or out of deep-seated meanness.|He acted with malice aforethought. 他预谋犯罪。|近义词：spite, animosity, malevolence, rancor, ill will|反义词：benevolence, goodwill, kindness|noun
