cared:/kɛrd/
verb
关心，在意；喜欢；照顾
past tense of care
She cared deeply for her family. 她深爱她的家人。
近义词:loved, liked, minded, worried, attended to
反义词:ignored, neglected, disliked
词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *caren, from Old English carian (“to be anxious, grieve, care”), from Proto-Germanic *karōną (“to lament, cry, care”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵer- (“to shout, cry”). Cognate with Scots cair (“to care”), West Frisian kearje (“to care”), Dutch keuren (“to approve, appreciate”), German kehren (“to sweep, turn”). Related to care.
