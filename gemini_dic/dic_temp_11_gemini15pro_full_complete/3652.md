exaggerate: /ɪɡˈzædʒəreɪt/
verb
夸大；夸张
To represent (something) as being larger, greater, better, or worse than it really is.
He tends to exaggerate when talking about his achievements. 他在谈论自己的成就时往往会夸大其词。
She exaggerated the difficulties of the journey. 她夸大了旅程的困难。

近义词: overstate, amplify, embellish
反义词: understate, minimize, downplay
词性: verb
