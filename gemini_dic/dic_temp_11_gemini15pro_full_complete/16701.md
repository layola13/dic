barrier:/ˈbæriər/ (US), /ˈbæriə(r)/ (UK)| n.|障碍; 屏障; 阻碍; 隔阂|Something that prevents or blocks movement, progress, or access.  A physical object or a set of circumstances that makes it difficult to achieve something.|例句：The language barrier made communication difficult. 语言障碍使沟通变得困难。

|例句：The mountain range forms a natural barrier between the two countries.  山脉形成了两国之间的天然屏障。

|近义词：obstacle, hurdle, impediment, obstruction|反义词：opening, access, passage|词性：noun
