murmurer:/ˈmɜːrmərər/ (US) /ˈmɜːmərə/ (UK)| n.|低语者，咕哝者|A person who murmurs; someone who speaks softly or indistinctly. |例句：The murmurer in the back of the room was distracting.  房间后面那个低语者让人分心。

例句：She was a known murmurer of discontent within the organization.  她是组织内部一个众所周知的表示不满的低语者。|近义词：whisperer, grumbler, complainer|反义词：shouter, orator|词性: noun
