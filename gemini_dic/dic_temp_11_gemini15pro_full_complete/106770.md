labarum:/ˈlæbərəm/| n.| 拉巴鲁姆(军旗)|A military standard, first used by the Roman emperor Constantine I, bearing the Chi-Rho symbol representing Christ.|The labarum became a symbol of the Roman Empire's conversion to Christianity.  拉巴鲁姆成为罗马帝国皈依基督教的象征。|synonym: standard, banner, ensign|antonym: N/A|noun
