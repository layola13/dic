changer:/ˈtʃeɪndʒər/ (US), /ˈtʃeɪndʒə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 换钱机；换衣间；游戏改变者|a person or thing that changes something.  A machine that gives change.|例句：The store has a coin changer for customers.  这家商店有供顾客使用的硬币兑换机。|近义词：converter, transformer, modifier|反义词:maintainer, preserver|词性:noun
