anarchism: /ˈænəkɪzəm/ | n. | 无政府主义 | A political philosophy that advocates self-governed societies based on voluntary institutions and the rejection of all forms of compulsory government or state control. |  Anarchism has a long and complex history.  无政府主义有着悠久而复杂的历史。 |  Anarchism rejects all forms of hierarchy.  无政府主义反对一切形式的等级制度。 | synonyms: nihilism, lawlessness | antonyms: authoritarianism, statism | noun

anarchist: /ˈænəkɪst/ | n. | 无政府主义者 | A person who believes in or advocates anarchism. | The anarchist distributed pamphlets at the protest.  这名无政府主义者在抗议活动中分发了传单。 | He identified as an anarchist. 他认为自己是一个无政府主义者。 | synonyms: rebel, revolutionary | antonyms: conformist, traditionalist | noun

anarchic: /əˈnɑːkɪk/ | adj. | 无政府状态的；混乱的 | Characterized by a lack of government or control; chaotic. | The city descended into anarchic violence after the earthquake.  地震后，这座城市陷入了无政府状态的暴力之中。 | The classroom became anarchic when the teacher left. 老师离开后，教室变得一片混乱。 | synonyms: lawless, disorderly, chaotic | antonyms: orderly, controlled, organized | adjective
