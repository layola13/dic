ectoplasmically:/ˌɛktəʊˈplæzmɪkli/ | adv. | 外质地；幽灵般地 | In a manner resembling or suggestive of ectoplasm; in a ghostly or ethereal way.  Relating to or emanating from ectoplasm (a viscous substance supposedly exuded by mediums during spiritualistic trances). |  The figure moved ectoplasmically through the wall, leaving no trace.  这个身影像幽灵一样穿墙而过，没有留下任何痕迹。 | 近义词：ghostly, spectrally, ethereally, supernaturally | 反义词：materially, physically, corporeally | 词性: 副词

Etymology:  From ectoplasm + -ically.  Ectoplasm from Greek ektos "outside" + plasma "something molded or formed."
