initialize:/ɪˈnɪʃəlaɪz/ (US), /ɪˈnɪʃɪəlaɪz/ (UK)| vt.|初始化，设定初值|To set (variables, counters, switches, etc.) to their starting values.|The program initializes all variables to zero. 程序将所有变量初始化为零。|近义词：set up, prepare, initiate, begin, start|反义词: terminate, end, conclude|verb

initialize:/ɪˈnɪʃəlaɪz/ (US), /ɪˈnɪʃɪəlaɪz/ (UK)| n.|初始化|The act of setting to an initial value.|The initialization of the system took several minutes.  系统的初始化花费了几分钟。|近义词：setup, preparation, initiation|反义词: termination, conclusion|noun
