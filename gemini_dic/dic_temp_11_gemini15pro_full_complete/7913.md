antenor:/ænˈtiːnɔːr/ |n.|安特诺尔| A Trojan nobleman who favored peace with the Greeks during the Trojan War.  He is often depicted as a wise and prudent counselor, but also as a traitor by some for his advocacy of returning Helen to Menelaus.  He is said to have later founded Padua (Patavium) in Italy.| Antenor urged the Trojans to sue for peace. 安特诺尔敦促特洛伊人请求和平。| synonyms: counselor, advisor, advocate | antonyms: warmonger, opponent, enemy| noun
