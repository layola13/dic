handicapped: /ˈhændɪkæpt/ | adj. n. | 残疾的，有残疾的人 | Having a physical or mental disability that substantially limits one or more major life activities.  A person with such a disability. |  例句：The building is handicapped accessible.  这座建筑方便残疾人出入。| 近义词：disabled, impaired | 反义词：able-bodied, non-disabled | 词性: 形容词，名词


handicappeds: /ˈhændɪkæpts/ | n. |  (过时的，冒犯的)残疾人（复数）|  This term is considered outdated and offensive. Use "people with disabilities" instead. |  例句： Using the term "handicappeds" is insensitive. 使用“handicappeds”这个词是不敏感的。 | 近义词： (avoid using, use "people with disabilities") | 反义词： (not applicable) | 词性: 名词

词源:  From the phrase "hand in cap," referring to a form of begging or gambling.  Over time, the term evolved to describe someone with a disability.
