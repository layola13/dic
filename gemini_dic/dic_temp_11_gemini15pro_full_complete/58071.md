droningly:/ˈdroʊnɪŋli/ | adv. | 单调地，沉闷地 |  In a monotonous and boring way, resembling the low, continuous hum of a drone. | The lecturer droningly read from his notes, causing many students to lose focus.  讲师单调地读着笔记，导致许多学生注意力涣散。 | synonym: boringly, tediously, repetitively | antonym: lively, animated, engagingly | 词性:副词


词源: From "drone" (n.) + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverb suffix).  "Drone" originally referred to the male honeybee, known for its continuous humming sound, and later extended to any similar low, monotonous sound.
