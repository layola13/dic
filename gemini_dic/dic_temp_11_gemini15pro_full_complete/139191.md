objection:/əbˈdʒekʃən/| n.|反对，异议|An objection is an expression or feeling of disapproval or opposition to something proposed. |例句：The lawyer raised an objection to the question. 律师对这个问题提出了异议。

例句：They had no objection to the proposal. 他们对这个提议没有异议。|近义词：protest, challenge, opposition, dissent|反义词：agreement, approval, acceptance|词性:noun
