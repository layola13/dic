incontrovertibly:/ˌɪnkɒntrəˈvɜːrtəbli/ /ˌɪnkɑːntrəˈvɜːrtəbli/
adverb
无可争议地；不容置疑地
in a way that is so obvious and certain that it cannot be disputed or denied.
The evidence presented incontrovertibly proved his guilt.  这些证据无可争议地证明了他的罪行。
Synonyms: undeniably, indisputably, irrefutably, unquestionably
Antonyms: disputably, questionably, dubiously
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From Late Latin *incontrovertibilis, from in- (“not”) + controversus (“disputed, turned against”) + -ibilis (suffix forming adjectives meaning “capable of, worthy of”).
