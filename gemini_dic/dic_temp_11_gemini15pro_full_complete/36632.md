/kəˈlɛktɪvɪsts/ | n. | 集体主义者 |  A person who believes in collectivism, a political or economic theory advocating collective control especially over production and distribution. |  Collectivists believe that the needs of the group should outweigh individual desires. 集体主义者认为，群体的需求应该高于个人的愿望。|  The kibbutz movement attracted many collectivists. 基布兹运动吸引了许多集体主义者。| synonyms: socialist, communist, communalist | antonyms: individualist, capitalist | noun

/kəˈlɛktɪvɪst/ | adj. | 集体主义的 |  Relating to or characteristic of collectivism. |  Collectivist ideals often emphasize cooperation and shared responsibility. 集体主义的理想通常强调合作和共同责任。 | The government implemented collectivist policies in agriculture. 政府在农业方面实施了集体主义政策。 | synonyms: communal, socialist, cooperative | antonyms: individualistic, capitalist | adjective
