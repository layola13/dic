overhonesty:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːnɪsti/
noun
过度诚实，过分坦率
The quality of being excessively honest, often to the point of being tactless or inappropriate.
Her overhonesty can sometimes make people uncomfortable.  她的过度诚实有时会让人不舒服。
His overhonesty about his past mistakes cost him the job. 他对过去错误的过度坦诚让他失去了这份工作。

近义词:candor, frankness, bluntness
反义词:dishonesty, deception, evasiveness
词性:noun
