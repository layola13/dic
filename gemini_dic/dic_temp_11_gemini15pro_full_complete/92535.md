hypsometer:/haɪˈpsɒmɪtər/ (US) /haɪˈpsɑːmɪtər/| n. |测高仪；沸点测高计|An instrument for measuring altitude, especially above sea level, by measuring the boiling point of a liquid. |例句：The mountaineer used a hypsometer to determine their altitude. 登山者使用测高仪来确定他们的海拔高度。

例句：The hypsometer showed they were nearing the summit. 测高仪显示他们快接近山顶了。|近义词：altimeter, altitude meter|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek *ὕψος (húpsos, “height”) + μέτρον (métron, “measure”).
