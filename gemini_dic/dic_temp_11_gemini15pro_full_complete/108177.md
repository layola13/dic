latter-day:/ˈlætər deɪ/ | adj. |近代的，现代的；后期圣徒的|Of or relating to a later period; modern.  Specifically used in the context of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (Mormons).| Latter-day saints believe in continuing revelation. 近代圣徒相信持续的启示。| synonyms: modern, contemporary, recent, present-day | antonyms: ancient, old, former, early | adjective

latter-day:/ˈlætər deɪ/ | n. |后期|A later period or time.| The latter days of the Roman Empire saw much turmoil. 罗马帝国的后期经历了很大的动荡。| synonyms: latter part, end, close | antonyms: beginning, start, outset | noun
