alienated: /ˈeɪliəneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 疏远的，格格不入的 | Feeling isolated and estranged from others or society.  Experiencing a sense of separation and not belonging. | He felt alienated from his family after the argument.  他争吵后感到与家人疏远了。

The new student felt alienated in the unfamiliar school.  这个新来的学生在这所陌生的学校里感到格格不入。 | synonyms: estranged, isolated, withdrawn, detached | antonyms: connected, integrated, involved, close | adjective
