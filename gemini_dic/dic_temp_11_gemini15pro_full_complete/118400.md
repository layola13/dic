merosome:/ˈmɛrəˌsoʊm/| n. |节段，体节; 部分; (尤指细胞的)断片|A fragment of a cell, especially a synaptosome.  A structural and functional unit of a multicellular organism, typically repeating along the main body axis.|例句：Researchers are studying the function of merosomes in neuronal communication. 研究人员正在研究节段在神经元交流中的功能。

例句：The merosome released neurotransmitters into the synapse. 该节段将神经递质释放到突触中。|近义词：fragment, segment, part, portion|反义词: whole, entirety|词性: noun
