christianly:/ˈkrɪstʃənli/
adv.
基督徒般地；像基督徒一样地
In a manner befitting a Christian; charitably; piously.
He behaved very christianly towards his enemies, forgiving their transgressions.  他以基督徒般的方式对待他的敌人，宽恕了他们的过错。
近义词:charitably, piously, devoutly
反义词:uncharitably, impiously, wickedly
词性:副词


christianly:/ˈkrɪstʃənli/
adj. (古旧用法，现已少用)
基督徒的；与基督教相关的
Of or relating to Christians or Christianity.
This is a christianly virtue. 这是基督徒的美德。
近义词:Christian
反义词:pagan, heathen
词性:形容词


词源: From Christian + -ly.  由Christian（基督徒）加后缀-ly构成。
