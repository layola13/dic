broker-dealer:/ˈbroʊkər ˈdiːlər/ | n. | 经纪自营商；证券经纪自营商 | A firm that buys and sells securities for its own account and for its customers. |  Example 1: The broker-dealer facilitated the trade of the company's stock.  这家经纪自营商促成了公司股票的交易。

Example 2:  Many large financial institutions act as broker-dealers. 许多大型金融机构充当经纪自营商。 | Near Synonyms: securities firm, brokerage, investment bank | Antonyms:  N/A (No direct antonym) | Noun
