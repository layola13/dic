fleetest:/ˈfliːtɪst/
adjective
最快的
superlative adjective of fleet; fastest.
The fleetest horse won the race.  最快的马赢得了比赛。
近义词:fastest, quickest, swiftest
反义词:slowest
词性:adjective


Etymology:
From Middle English *fletyst, *fletest, from Old English flēotig (“swift, speedy”), from flēotan (“to float, swim, flee”).  More at fleet.
