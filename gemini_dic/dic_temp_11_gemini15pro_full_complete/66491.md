excess:/ɪkˈses/ /ˈekˌses/ | n. adj.| 过量，过度；过剩，超额 | An amount that is more than necessary, acceptable, or desirable. |例句：There is an excess of fat in your diet. 你的饮食中脂肪过量。

例句：The store had an excess of stock that they needed to clear out. 这家商店有多余的库存需要清理。| 近义词：surplus, superfluity, overabundance | 反义词：lack, deficiency, shortage | 词性: 名词

excess:/ɪkˈses/ /ˈekˌses/ | adj.| 过量的，额外的 | More than is necessary, usual, or desirable. | 例句：Excess baggage will be charged extra. 超重行李将额外收费。

例句：Cut off any excess fabric. 剪掉任何多余的布料。 | 近义词：superfluous, extra, surplus | 反义词： insufficient, lacking, deficient | 词性: 形容词
