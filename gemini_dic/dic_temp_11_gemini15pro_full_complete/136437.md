nonrevengers:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈvɛn.dʒəz/
| n. |非复仇者，不复仇的人|People who choose not to seek revenge. Those who do not retaliate when wronged.|例句：The nonrevengers chose to forgive rather than hold onto anger.  非复仇者选择原谅而不是怀恨在心。

例句：The community of nonrevengers believed in peaceful conflict resolution.  这个非复仇者群体相信和平解决冲突。|近义词：forgivers, pacifists|反义词:avengers, retaliators|词性:名词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
revenger (noun): from Old French revengier, from Latin vindicare "to claim, avenge"
