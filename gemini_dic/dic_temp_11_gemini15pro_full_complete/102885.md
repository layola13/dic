javelined: /ˈdʒævəlɪnd/ | v. | 用标枪投掷;  | To throw or strike with or as if with a javelin. |  He javelined the spear across the field. 他把矛像标枪一样投掷过田野。 |  hurled, speared, launched | - | verb
javelined: /ˈdʒævəlɪnd/ | adj. | 被标枪投掷的 |  Struck or thrown as if with a javelin. | The javelined boar lay dead. 被标枪刺中的野猪躺在地上死了。 | pierced, impaled | - | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From javelin + -ed.  Javelin from Old French *javeline, diminutive of javelot "light spear," itself a diminutive of Old French javel "javelin, boar-spear." Ultimately from Frankish *gabilo or some other Germanic source (compare Old High German gabilo "spear, javelin").
