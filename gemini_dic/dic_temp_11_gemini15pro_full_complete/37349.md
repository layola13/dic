commendator:/ˌkɒmənˈdeɪtər/ | n. | 推荐人；赞扬者；委托管理人 | One who commends; one who praises or recommends; one to whom a benefice or church living is commended. | The commendator was highly respected for his insightful recommendations.  这位推荐人因其富有洞察力的推荐而备受尊敬。|  He served as commendator of the abbey for many years. 他担任修道院的委托管理人多年。 | synonyms: recommender, praiser, advocate, supporter | antonyms: critic, detractor, opponent | noun

commendatory:/kəˈmɛndətɔːri/ | adj. | 赞赏的；推荐的 | Expressing commendation; praising; laudatory. |  He received a commendatory letter from his former employer. 他收到了前雇主的一封推荐信。| The commendatory remarks boosted her confidence. 赞赏的话增强了她的自信心。| synonyms: praising, laudatory, approving, complimentary | antonyms: critical, disapproving, condemnatory | adjective
