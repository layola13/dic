artificial:/ˌɑːrtɪˈfɪʃəl/ | adj. | 人造的，人工的，假的，非自然的 | Made or produced by human beings rather than occurring naturally. |  例句：Artificial flowers don't smell as good as real ones. 人造花不如真花闻起来香。

例句：She gave an artificial smile. 她挤出一个勉强的笑容。


| 近义词：synthetic, man-made, fake, imitation, mock | 反义词：natural, genuine, real | 词性: adjective
