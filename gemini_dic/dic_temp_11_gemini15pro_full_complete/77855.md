genesial: /dʒɪˈniːʒiəl/
adjective
生殖的；起源的；有创造力的
Pertaining to generation or procreation; generative; creative.
例句：The genesial power of nature is awe-inspiring. 大自然的生殖力量令人敬畏。

例句：His genesial mind came up with numerous innovative ideas. 他富有创造力的头脑想出了许多创新的想法。
近义词：generative, creative, procreative, fecund
反义词：barren, sterile, infertile
词性：adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *genesialis, from genesis ("origin, creation, generation") + -alis (adjectival suffix). Ultimately from Greek γένεσις (génesis, "origin").
