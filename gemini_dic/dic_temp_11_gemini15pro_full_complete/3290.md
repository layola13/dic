affixion:/əˈfɪkʃən/| n. |痛苦，折磨；固定，附着|The affixion of the stamp to the envelope. 将邮票贴在信封上。|例句：The affixion of his guilt was evident in his demeanor.  他的举止明显显露出他的负罪感。

例句：The affixion of the plaque to the wall commemorated the historic event.  将牌匾固定在墙上是为了纪念这一历史事件。|近义词：attachment, fastening, suffering, distress|反义词:detachment, removal, comfort, solace|词性:noun

affix:/əˈfɪks/| v. |粘贴，附上；添加|To affix a stamp to a letter.  把邮票贴在信上。|例句：Please affix this label to your luggage. 请把这个标签贴在你的行李上。

例句：He affixed his signature to the contract. 他在合同上签了名。|近义词：attach, fasten, add, append|反义词:detach, remove|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *affixio, from Latin affixus, past participle of affigere (“to fasten to”), from ad- (“to”) + figere (“to fix, fasten”).
