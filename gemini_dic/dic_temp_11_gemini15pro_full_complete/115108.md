mange:/meɪndʒ/ /mɑːndʒ/| n.| 兽疥癣; 疥癣, 疮; (植物的) 痂病; 弊病, 恶习|A skin disease of mammals caused by parasitic mites and characterized by severe itching, hair loss, and scabbing of the skin.  A condition or situation that is unpleasant, harmful, or undesirable.|The dog was suffering from mange. 这只狗得了疥癣。|近义词：scabies, itch, infestation|反义词: health, cleanliness|词性:noun

mange:/meɪndʒ/ /mɑːndʒ/| v.| (使)患疥癣; (使)恶化, (使)败坏|To cause to suffer from mange; to become infected with mange. To make worse or deteriorate.|The neglect mangled the garden. 疏于打理使花园败坏了。|近义词：worsen, deteriorate, decline|反义词: improve, ameliorate|词性:verb
