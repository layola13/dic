coincide:/ˌkəʊɪnˈsaɪd/ (UK), /ˌkoʊɪnˈsaɪd/ (US)| vi.| 一致，符合，同时发生 |To occur at the same time or occupy the same space; to correspond or agree.| Our birthdays coincide this year. 今年我们的生日在同一天。| synonyms: concur, correspond, match | antonyms: differ, diverge | verb

coincide:/ˌkəʊɪnˈsaɪd/ (UK), /ˌkoʊɪnˈsaɪd/ (US)| vi.|  (意见或想法) 相符，相一致 |To be in agreement or harmony.|  My views coincide with yours on this matter. 在这件事上，我的看法与你一致。| synonyms: agree, conform, tally | antonyms: disagree, clash | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *coincidere, from Latin com- 'together' + incidere 'to fall into, happen'.
