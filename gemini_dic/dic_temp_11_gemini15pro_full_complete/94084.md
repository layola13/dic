impasto:/ɪmˈpɑːstoʊ/ /ɪmˈpæstoʊ/
noun
厚涂颜料; 厚涂法
a thick layer of paint or pigment
The impasto in Van Gogh's paintings is characteristic of his style.  梵高的画作中厚涂颜料的技法是其风格的特征。
近义词: texture, layering
反义词: smooth, flat
词性: noun


impastos:/ɪmˈpɑːstoʊz/ /ɪmˈpæstoʊz/
noun (plural)
厚涂颜料(复数); 厚涂法(复数)
thick layers of paint or pigment
The impastos on the canvas created a three-dimensional effect. 画布上的厚涂颜料营造出一种三维效果。
近义词: textures, layerings
反义词: smooth surfaces, flat areas
词性: noun
