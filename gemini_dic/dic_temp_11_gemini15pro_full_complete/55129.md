dissatisfaction's:/dɪsˌsætɪsˈfækʃənz/ | n. | 不满意；不满 |The possessive form of dissatisfaction, referring to the state of being displeased or not content with something. It indicates ownership or origin of the dissatisfaction. |  The manager addressed the employees' dissatisfactions regarding their salaries. 经理解决了员工对工资的不满。| synonyms: discontent, displeasure, grievance | antonyms: satisfaction, contentment, pleasure | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation) + satisfaction.  "Satisfaction" comes from Late Latin satisfactio, from satisfacere "make amends, give satisfaction," from satis "enough" + facere "to do."
