nondistributiveness:/ˌnɑːndɪˌstrɪbjʊˈtɪvnəs/ | n. |非分配性|The property of a logical or mathematical operation where the operation applied to a set of elements is not equivalent to applying the operation to each element individually and then combining the results.  It means a property does not hold for each individual member of a set.|The nondistributiveness of multiplication over addition in some algebraic structures is a key concept. 在某些代数结构中，乘法对加法的非分配性是一个关键概念。|Near synonyms: non-distribution, indivisibility| Antonyms: distributiveness, divisibility|noun
