hikers:/ˈhaɪkərz/| n.|徒步旅行者；远足者|People who go for long walks in the countryside or mountains, especially for pleasure or exercise.|例句：The hikers enjoyed the scenic views from the mountaintop. 徒步旅行者们欣赏着山顶的景色。

例句：Many hikers use trekking poles for added stability. 许多徒步旅行者使用登山杖来增加稳定性。|近义词：walkers, ramblers, trekkers|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑) drivers, passengers|词性:noun
