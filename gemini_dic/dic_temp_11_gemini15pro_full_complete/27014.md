calculatory:/ˈkælkjʊləˌtɔːri/
adjective
计算的；精于计算的；深谋远虑的
Relating to or involving calculation; shrewd and scheming.
His calculatory move ensured his victory in the chess game. 他经过精心计算的走法确保了他在棋局中的胜利。
Her calculatory nature made her a formidable opponent in business. 她精于算计的本性使她成为商界一个可怕的对手。
近义词: calculating, shrewd, scheming, cunning
反义词: naive, artless, ingenuous
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *calculātōrius, from Latin calculātus (past participle of calculāre ‘to calculate’), from calculus ‘pebble’ (used in reckoning).
