nonpreventible:/ˌnɒnprɪˈventəbl/ /ˌnɑːnprɪˈventəbl/| adj.|不可预防的，无法阻止的|Not able to be prevented or avoided.|例句：Some types of cancer are nonpreventible.  某些类型的癌症是无法预防的。

例句：The damage from the hurricane was nonpreventible.  飓风的破坏是不可避免的。|近义词：unavoidable, inevitable, inescapable|反义词：preventable, avoidable|词性：形容词

词源: non- (not) + preventable (able to be prevented)  来自拉丁语 praevenire "to anticipate, come before, prevent,"  字面意思是 "to come before."
