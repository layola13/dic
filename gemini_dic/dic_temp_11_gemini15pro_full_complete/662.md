abrogator:/ˈæbrəɡeɪtər/| n.|废除者，取消者|A person or entity that abolishes or annuls something, typically a law, treaty, or custom.|The king acted as the abrogator of the outdated law. 国王废除了过时的法律。|近义词：repealer, abolisher, nullifier, canceller|反义词：ratifier, upholder, supporter|noun
