capitulatory:/kəˈpɪtʃʊlətɔːri/
adj.
投降的，屈服的
Relating to, involving, or of the nature of capitulation; surrendering.
The defeated army made a capitulatory gesture by laying down their weapons.  战败的军队放下武器，做出了投降的姿态。
The government's capitulatory response to the terrorists' demands was widely criticized. 政府对恐怖分子要求的投降式回应受到了广泛批评。
近义词: submissive, yielding, surrendering
反义词: resistant, defiant, unyielding
词性: adjective

capitulate:/kəˈpɪtʃʊleɪt/
v.
投降，屈服，让步
Cease to resist an opponent or an unwelcome demand; surrender.
After a long siege, the city finally capitulated to the enemy.  经过长时间的围困，这座城市最终向敌人投降。
The company capitulated to the union's demands after a prolonged strike.  在长时间的罢工之后，公司屈服于工会的要求。
近义词: surrender, yield, submit
反义词: resist, defy, oppose
词性: verb
