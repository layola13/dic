chordates:/ˈkɔːrdeɪts/| n.|脊索动物|Animals possessing a notochord, a hollow dorsal nerve cord, pharyngeal slits, an endostyle, and a post-anal tail at some point during their development.|例句：Chordates include vertebrates, tunicates, and lancelets. 脊索动物包括脊椎动物、尾索动物和文昌鱼。|例句：Humans are classified as chordates. 人类被归类为脊索动物。|近义词：vertebrates (in many cases)|反义词:invertebrates|noun
