bisulphuret:/baɪˈsʌlfjʊˌrɛt/ /baɪˈsʌlfjʊˌraɪt/| n. |二硫化物|A compound containing two atoms of sulphur combined with an element or radical.|Iron bisulphuret is a common mineral.  硫化铁是一种常见的矿物。|Synonym: disulphide|Antonym: None|noun
