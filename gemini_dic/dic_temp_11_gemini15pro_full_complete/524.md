abominable:/əˈbɒmɪnəbl/ (UK), /əˈbɑːmɪnəbl/ (US)| adj.|令人憎恶的；讨厌的；糟透的|Inspiring moral hatred or disgust; very bad or unpleasant.|The weather was abominable, with heavy rain and strong winds. 天气糟透了，下着大雨，刮着大风.|Example 2:  His behavior was absolutely abominable. 他的行为绝对令人憎恶。|近义词：detestable, loathsome, hateful, execrable, appalling, dreadful, terrible|反义词：delightful, wonderful, pleasant, admirable|词性：形容词

abominable:/əˈbɒmɪnəbl/ (UK), /əˈbɑːmɪnəbl/ (US)| n.|令人憎恶的事物|Something that causes disgust or hatred.|His cruelty towards animals is an abominable thing. 他对动物的残忍令人憎恶。|Example 2: The pollution of the river is an abominable act. 污染河流是令人憎恶的行为。|近义词：atrocity, outrage, abomination|反义词：delight, wonder, pleasure|词性：名词
