barkantine:/ˈbɑːrkənˌtiːn/ | n. | 巴肯廷帆船 | A three-masted sailing vessel with square sails on the foremast and fore-and-aft sails on the main and mizzen masts. | The barkantine sailed gracefully across the bay. 巴肯廷帆船优雅地航行穿过海湾。| The barkentine navigated the treacherous waters with ease. 巴肯廷帆船轻松地航行在危险的水域中。| 近义词: barquentine | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "steamship" as a contrasting vessel type) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Dutch barkentijn, from Middle Dutch barquentyne, from Italian bargantino "small brigantine." Ultimately from barca "barge."
