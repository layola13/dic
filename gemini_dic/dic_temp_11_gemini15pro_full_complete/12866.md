assot: /əˈsɒt/ | n. |傻瓜，笨蛋| A foolish or stupid person. | He's such an assot, he locked his keys in the car again. 他真是个笨蛋，又把钥匙锁在车里了。| 近义词：idiot, fool, moron, dunce | 反义词: genius, savant | 词性: 名词

词源:  Likely a variant of "ass" (donkey), which has long been used figuratively to refer to a stupid person. This then developed into various dialectal forms, including "assot."
