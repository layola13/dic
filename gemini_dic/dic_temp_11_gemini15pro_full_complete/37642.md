commutator:/ˈkɒmjʊteɪtə(r)/ (US) /ˈkɑːmjuteɪtər/ (UK)| n.|换向器; (数学)交换子|A device for reversing the direction of an electric current, especially in a motor or generator. In mathematics, it's an operation that measures the extent to which two elements of a ring or group fail to commute.|
The commutator is a vital part of a DC motor.  换向器是直流电机的重要组成部分。

The commutator ensures the current flows in the correct direction. 换向器确保电流沿正确的方向流动。|
近义词: (In electrical engineering) rectifier, inverter|
反义词: None (The concept doesn't have a direct opposite)|
词性: Noun


commutators:/ˈkɒmjʊteɪtəz/ (US) /ˈkɑːmjuteɪtəz/ (UK)| n.|换向器; (数学)交换子|Plural form of "commutator". Refers to multiple devices used for reversing the direction of an electric current, or multiple mathematical operations.|
The generator uses multiple commutators for increased efficiency.  这台发电机使用多个换向器来提高效率。

The design incorporates two commutators, one for each circuit.  该设计包含两个换向器，每个电路一个。|
近义词: (In electrical engineering) rectifiers, inverters|
反义词: None (The concept doesn't have a direct opposite)|
