intercurrent:/ˌɪntərˈkʌrənt/| adj.|并发发生的，穿插出现的|Occurring between or interrupting the usual course of something, especially a disease.|The patient's recovery was complicated by an intercurrent infection. 病人的康复因并发感染而变得复杂。|近义词：concurrent, accompanying, incidental|反义词:primary, main|词性:形容词
词源:From Latin intercurrēns, present participle of intercurrere (“to run between”), from inter (“between”) + currere (“to run”).
