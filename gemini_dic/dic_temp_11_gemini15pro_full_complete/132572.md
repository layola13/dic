nondidactically:/ˌnɑːndɪˈdæktɪkli/
adverb
非教学地；非教导地
In a way that is not intended to teach or instruct.
She explained the complex theory nondidactically, using simple analogies. 她用简单的类比非教导性地解释了这个复杂的理论。
Synonyms:informally, casually, conversationally
Antonyms:didactically, pedagogically
词性: 副词
