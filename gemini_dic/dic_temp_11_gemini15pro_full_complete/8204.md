anti-elite:/ˌæntiɪˈliːt/| adj. n.|反精英的，反精英人士|Opposed to or hostile towards the elite or elite groups; populist.|The candidate's anti-elite rhetoric resonated with many voters. 这位候选人的反精英言论引起了许多选民的共鸣。|The anti-elite movement is gaining traction. 反精英运动正在获得支持。|近义词：populist, egalitarian|反义词：elitist, aristocratic|词性：形容词, 名词

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek anti-)
elite (noun): a select group that is superior in terms of ability or qualities to the rest of a group or society (from French élite, from Latin eligere ‘choose out’)
