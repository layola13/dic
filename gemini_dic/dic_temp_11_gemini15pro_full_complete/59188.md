dysnomy:/dɪsˈnəʊmi/ /dɪsˈnoʊmi/
n.|失范;  混乱;  失调|A state of lack of laws or deregulation.  A state of disorder or abnormality.|The dysnomy of the financial markets led to the crash. 金融市场的失调导致了崩盘。|近义词:anomie, lawlessness, disorder, chaos|反义词:order, regulation, harmony|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek *δυσνομία (dusnomía)*, from *δυσ- (dus-)* meaning "bad, difficult" + *νόμος (nómos)* meaning "law, custom".
