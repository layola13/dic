antimanagement:/ˌæntiˈmænɪdʒmənt/
adjective
反管理的；反对管理层的
opposed to or hostile towards management; characterized by a rejection of traditional management practices
The antimanagement sentiment among the workers was palpable. 工人们的反管理情绪显而易见。

近义词:insubordinate, rebellious, defiant
反义词:pro-management, cooperative, compliant
词性:adjective
