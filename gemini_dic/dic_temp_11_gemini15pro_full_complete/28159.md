capnometer:/kæpˈnɒmɪtər/
n.|二氧化碳测量仪|An instrument for measuring the amount of carbon dioxide in a gas, especially the air exhaled from the lungs.|The capnometer showed a steady increase in CO2 levels.|近义词:CO2 monitor|反义词:None|词性:名词

capnography:/kæpˈnɒɡrəfi/
n.|二氧化碳描记法|The graphic recording of the concentration of carbon dioxide in expired air over time.|Capnography is essential during surgery to monitor the patient's ventilation.|近义词:CO2 monitoring|反义词:None|词性:名词

capnometric:/kæpnoʊˈmɛtrɪk/
adj.|二氧化碳测量的|Relating to or involving the measurement of carbon dioxide levels.|Capnometric data can be used to assess the effectiveness of CPR.|近义词:CO2 measuring|反义词:None|词性:形容词

capnomic:/kæpˈnɒmɪk/
adj.|与呼吸有关的，与二氧化碳有关的|Relating to respiration or carbon dioxide.|Capnomic changes can indicate respiratory distress.|近义词:respiratory, CO2 related|反义词:None|词性:形容词
