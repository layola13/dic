conjugator:/ˈkɒndʒʊɡeɪtə(r)/ (US) /ˈkɑːndʒəɡeɪtər/| n. | 变位器；动词变位程序 |A conjugator is a tool or program that shows the different forms of a verb. |  I used an online conjugator to check the past participle of "run". 我使用在线动词变位程序来检查“run”的过去分词。 |  synonyms:  verb conjugator, conjugation tool, inflection tool | antonyms: N/A | noun

conjugate:/ˈkɒndʒʊɡeɪt/ (US) /ˈkɑːndʒəɡeɪt/| v. | 变位；结合；使成对 |To give the different forms of a verb in an inflected language. In other contexts, it can mean to join or combine. | He conjugated the verb "to be" in Spanish. 他用西班牙语对动词 "to be" 进行变位。| synonyms: inflect, decline, combine, unite | antonyms: separate, divide | verb
