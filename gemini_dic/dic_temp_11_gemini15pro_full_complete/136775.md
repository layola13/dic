nonseraphically:/ˌnɒnsɛˈræfɪkəli/
adjective
非天使般地，不似天使地，不像天使的
in a way that is not angelic or heavenly; in a way that is earthly and mundane.
He yawned nonseraphically, a very human sound in the hallowed halls. 他打了个哈欠，一点也不像天使，在这神圣的大厅里发出非常人类的声音。
Near synonyms: unspiritually, earthily, mundanely, secularly, humanly
Antonyms: seraphically, angelically, divinely, heavenly
词性: adjective
