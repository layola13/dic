osteological:/ˌɒstiəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ /ˌɑːstiəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/
adjective

The museum has a vast osteological collection, with skeletons from dinosaurs to modern mammals.  博物馆收藏了大量的骨骼，从恐龙到现代哺乳动物的骨骼都有。

反义词:None
词性:adjective
