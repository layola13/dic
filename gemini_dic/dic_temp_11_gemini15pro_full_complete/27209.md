callousness:/ˈkæləsnəs/| n. |冷酷无情；麻木不仁|Insensitivity to the feelings of others; hardness of heart.|He showed a callous disregard for the suffering of others. 他对别人的痛苦表现出麻木不仁。|例句：The callousness of the dictator shocked the world.  独裁者的冷酷无情震惊了世界。

例句：She was surprised by the callousness of his response. 她对他冷漠的回应感到惊讶。


近义词：insensitivity, indifference, apathy, heartlessness|反义词: compassion, empathy, sensitivity, kindness|词性:noun
