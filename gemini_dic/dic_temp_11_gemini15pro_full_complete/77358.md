gauffer:/ˈɡɔːfər/ (US), /ˈɡɒfər/ (UK)| v. n.| 压印，用薄脆饼熨烫；用(加热的)熨斗烫出(波纹等)；薄脆饼，华夫饼| To crimp, pleat, or emboss a material, typically fabric or paper, using a heated iron or goffering iron.  Also, a wafer-like confection. | She gauffered the edges of the curtains to give them a decorative finish. 她用烙铁烫出窗帘边缘的褶皱作为装饰。| Synonyms: crimp, emboss, pleat| Antonyms: smooth, flatten| Verb, Noun

gauffer:/ˈɡɔːfər/ (US), /ˈɡɒfər/ (UK)| n.| 薄脆饼，华夫饼| A thin, crisp wafer, often served with ice cream. | The dessert featured a scoop of vanilla ice cream with a gauffer. 这道甜点是一勺香草冰淇淋配上一块薄脆饼。| Synonyms: wafer, waffle | Antonyms: None| Noun
