lynch:/lɪntʃ/| v. n.|私刑处死；处以私刑|To kill someone, especially by hanging, for an alleged offense with or without a legal trial.|The angry mob lynched the suspected thief. 愤怒的暴徒私刑处死了那个疑似的小偷。|近义词：execute, kill, murder|反义词: pardon, acquit|词性:动词,名词
lynch:/lɪntʃ/| n.|私刑|An act of killing someone, especially by hanging, for an alleged offense with or without a legal trial.|The lynching of innocent people is a barbaric act.  对无辜的人处以私刑是野蛮的行为。|近义词：execution, murder|反义词: acquittal|词性:名词
