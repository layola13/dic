individualistic:/ˌɪndɪˌvɪdʒuəˈlɪstɪk/| adj.|个人主义的；强调个人独立性的|Characterized by or reflecting a belief in the importance of individual rights and freedoms, often prioritizing personal goals and independence over group needs or conformity.|She has a very individualistic style of dressing. 她有着非常个人主义的穿衣风格。|Synonyms: independent, self-reliant, nonconformist, autonomous, free-spirited|Antonyms: collectivist, conformist, cooperative, communal|adjective
