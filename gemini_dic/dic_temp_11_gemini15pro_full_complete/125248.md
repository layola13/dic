multidimensionally:/ˌmʌltiˌdaɪˈmenʃənəli/ | adv. | 多维地，多方面地 |  In a way that involves multiple dimensions or aspects.  It describes something considered from multiple perspectives or factors. | The problem needs to be approached multidimensionally, considering social, economic, and environmental factors.  这个问题需要多方面考虑，包括社会、经济和环境因素。|  The artist explores themes of identity multidimensionally through her work. 这位艺术家通过她的作品多维度地探索了身份认同的主题。| synonym: comprehensively, broadly, diversely | antonym: narrowly, simply, unidimensionally |词性: 副词
