araneose: /ˌærəˈniːoʊs/ | adj. | 蜘蛛网状的；似蜘蛛网的 | Resembling a spider's web; cobwebby. |  The old attic was filled with araneose dust and debris.  旧阁楼里满是蜘蛛网状的灰尘和碎屑。|  The spider spun an intricate and araneose web. 蜘蛛结了一张复杂而精细的蛛网。 |Synonyms: cobwebby, weblike, gossamer | Antonyms: solid, dense | adjective

Etymology->
From Latin *arāneus* (spider) + -ose (suffix forming adjectives indicating fullness or resemblance).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂er- (to fit together).
