amphisbaena:/æmfɪsˈbiːnə/ | n. | 两头蛇；双头蛇 | A mythical serpent with a head at each end. |  例句：Legends speak of the amphisbaena, a creature so powerful it could move in either direction.  传说中提到了两头蛇，这是一种非常强大的生物，可以朝任何方向移动。

例句：The amphisbaena, a symbol of duality, appeared in many ancient cultures' mythology.  两头蛇，二元性的象征，出现在许多古代文化的传说中。| 近义词：wyrm, serpent, two-headed serpent, double-headed serpent | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a mythical creature) | 词性: noun
