lunate:/ˈluːneɪt/| adj. n.|月形的，新月形的；月骨| Crescent-shaped, like a crescent moon; A crescent-shaped bone in the wrist.|The lunate bone is in the wrist. 月骨在手腕里。| The moth's wings had a distinctive lunate marking.  蛾子的翅膀上有一个独特的月牙形标记。|近义词：crescent, semilunar|反义词:straight, linear|词性:形容词,名词
Etymology: From Latin *lūnātus*, past participle of *lūnō* (“to shape like a half-moon”), from *lūna* (“moon”).
