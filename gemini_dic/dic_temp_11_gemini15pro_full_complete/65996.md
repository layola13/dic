exametron:/ɪɡˈzæmɪtrɒn/ | n. | 测验仪，考试机 | A device or system used for administering and evaluating examinations, often electronically. |  Example: The students took their final exam on the exametron. 学生们在考试机上进行了期末考试。| Example: The exametron automatically graded the multiple-choice questions. 考试机自动批改了选择题。 | Synonyms: testing machine, examination system, assessment platform | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider traditional pen-and-paper exam) | noun
