microbicide: /ˌmaɪkroʊˈbaɪsaɪd/ | n. | 杀微生物剂 | A substance that kills microbes, such as bacteria, viruses, or fungi. |  Microbicides are being researched as a potential method of preventing sexually transmitted infections.  杀微生物剂正被研究作为一种预防性传播感染的潜在方法。 | synonyms: disinfectant, antiseptic, germicide | antonyms: growth medium, nutrient broth | noun
