interfolded:/ˌɪntərˈfoʊldɪd/
adjective
交叠的，互相折叠的
folded together; interwoven.
The pages of the booklet were interfolded.  这本小册子的页面是互相折叠的。
The two proteins are interfolded to form a complex structure. 这两种蛋白质相互折叠形成一个复杂的结构。
近义词: interwoven, intertwined, interlaced
反义词: unfolded, separated
词性: adjective

interfold:/ˌɪntərˈfoʊld/
verb
交叠，互相折叠
to fold together; to intertwine.
Carefully interfold the sheets of tissue paper. 小心地将薄纸片交叠起来。
The artist interfolded different colored papers to create a unique design. 艺术家将不同颜色的纸交叠起来，创造出独特的设计。
近义词: interweave, intertwine, interlace
反义词: unfold, separate
词性: verb

词源:
From inter- + fold.  "Inter-" prefix meaning "between" or "among," and "fold" meaning to bend something over on itself.
