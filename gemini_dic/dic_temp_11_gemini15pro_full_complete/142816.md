ousters:/ˈaʊstərz/
noun
驱逐；罢免；革职
the act of forcing someone to leave a position of power, job, place, or organization
The recent ousters of several top executives have left the company in turmoil. 最近几名高管被革职，使公司陷入混乱。
dismissals, expulsions, removals
reinstatements, appointments
n.
