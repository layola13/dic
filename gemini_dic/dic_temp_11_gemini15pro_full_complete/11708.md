armys:/ˈɑːrmiz/ | n. |军队，陆军；大群，大批| A large organized body of armed personnel trained for war especially on land.  A large number of people or things. |例句：The armys of both countries were mobilized.  两国军队都动员起来了。

例句：Armys of ants swarmed over the picnic food.  大群蚂蚁爬满了野餐食物。 |近义词：troops, forces, hordes, multitudes |反义词:civilians|名词
