elleborin:/ɛˈlɛbərɪn/
n.
嚏根草苷,嚏根草素
A crystalline glycoside, C37H42O18, obtained from the rhizome of black hellebore (Helleborus niger) and Christmas rose (Helleborus niger and Helleborus viridis) and used as a heart stimulant.
The doctor prescribed elleborin to stimulate the patient's weakened heart.  医生给病人开了嚏根草苷来刺激他虚弱的心脏。
