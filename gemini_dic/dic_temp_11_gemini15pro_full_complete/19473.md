biddabilities:/ˌbɪdəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |可投标性，可竞标性|The capacity or suitability of something to be bid on, often in a competitive process.  It refers to how easily or readily something can be subject to bidding.  This could apply to projects, contracts, or even items for sale.|The biddabilities of the construction projects were assessed based on their complexity and risk. 这些建设项目的可投标性是根据其复杂性和风险来评估的。|Near synonyms:  biddableness, tenderability|Antonyms:  unbiddable, non-tenderable|Noun
