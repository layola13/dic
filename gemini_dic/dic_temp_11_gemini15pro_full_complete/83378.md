gyrostat:/ˈdʒaɪrəˌstæt/ | n. | 陀螺仪，回转仪 |A device consisting of a rotating wheel so mounted that its axis of rotation is free to turn in any direction.  It maintains its orientation regardless of the motion of the platform on which it is mounted. | The satellite uses a gyrostat to maintain its stability. 卫星使用陀螺仪来保持稳定性。| Synonym: gyroscope | Antonym: N/A | noun
