emulsioning:/ɪˈmʌlʃənɪŋ/
verb
乳化
the process of making an emulsion (a mixture of two or more liquids that are normally immiscible).
The chef was emulsioning oil and vinegar to make a vinaigrette.  厨师正在乳化油和醋来制作香醋汁。
Mixing the ingredients thoroughly is key to emulsioning them properly. 将原料充分混合是使其正确乳化的关键。
emulsify, blending, mixing
separating
v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *émulsionner*, from *émulsion* "emulsion" (from Latin *emulgere* "to milk out, drain out," from *ex- "out" + *mulgere* "to milk"). The "-ing" suffix indicates the present participle form of the verb.
