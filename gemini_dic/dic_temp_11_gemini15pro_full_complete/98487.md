installment:/ɪnˈstɔːlmənt/ (US), /ɪnˈstɔːlmənt/ (UK)| n.|分期付款；部分；（分期连载的）一期|A payment made as one of a series for something bought on credit.  A portion of something presented in parts or sections. |例句：She paid for the car in monthly installments. 她按月分期付款买了这辆车。

例句：The first installment of the novel was published in the magazine. 小说的第一部分发表在杂志上。|近义词：payment, part, portion, section|反义词:lump sum (一次性支付)|词性:noun
