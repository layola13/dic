biogeochemical:/ˌbaɪ.oʊˌdʒi.oʊˈkɛm.ɪ.kəl/ | adj. | 生物地球化学的 | Relating to the chemical interactions between living organisms and the geological environment. | The biogeochemical cycles of carbon and nitrogen are crucial for life on Earth. 地球上碳和氮的生物地球化学循环对生命至关重要。|  The study examined the biogeochemical processes in the forest ecosystem. 这项研究调查了森林生态系统中的生物地球化学过程。 |  近义词: ecological, environmental | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  bio- (life) + geo- (earth) + chemical
