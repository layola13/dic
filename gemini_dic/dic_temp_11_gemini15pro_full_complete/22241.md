bondest:/ˈbɒndɪst/
adjective
最受束缚的；最受约束的
Superlative form of bonded: most bonded.  Bound to a person or land by an obligation.
例句：He felt like the bondest man in the world, chained to his responsibilities. 他感觉自己是世界上最受束缚的人，被自己的责任所束缚。

例句：Of all the serfs, he was the bondest to the land, unable to leave even for a day. 在所有农奴中，他是最受土地束缚的，甚至一天都不能离开。
近义词：enslaved, tied, restricted
反义词：free, liberated, unbound
词性: 形容词

词源:  From "bond" (meaning tie, connection, or obligation) + "-est" (superlative suffix). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *bandam.
