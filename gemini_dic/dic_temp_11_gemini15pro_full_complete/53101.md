diphthongization:/dɪfθɒŋɡaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /dɪfθɔŋɡaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n.|双元音化|The process by which a single vowel sound changes into a diphthong (a combination of two vowel sounds).|The Great Vowel Shift in English involved a significant amount of diphthongization.|近义词: vowel shift, monophthongization (as an opposite process)|反义词: monophthongization|词性:noun

diphthongize:/dɪfˈθɒŋɡaɪz/ /dɪfˈθɔŋɡaɪz/| v.|使成双元音|To cause a single vowel sound to become a diphthong.|The pronunciation of "house" has diphthongized over time.|近义词: change, alter (in a phonetic context)|反义词: monophthongize|词性:verb
