celsius: /ˈselsiəs/ | adj. n. |摄氏的；摄氏|  Relating to the Celsius temperature scale, which is based on the freezing point of water (0°C) and the boiling point of water (100°C).  Also sometimes used as a noun to refer to this scale. | Water boils at 100 degrees Celsius. 水沸点是100摄氏度。|The recipe calls for the oven to be preheated to 200 degrees Celsius. 食谱要求烤箱预热到200摄氏度。 | synonym: centigrade | antonym: Fahrenheit | adjective, noun
