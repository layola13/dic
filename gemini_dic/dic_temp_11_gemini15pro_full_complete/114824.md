maiming:/ˈmeɪmɪŋ/
|n.|故意伤害；残害|The act of causing serious and permanent physical injury to someone or something, especially by violence.|The maiming of innocent civilians during the war was horrific.  战争期间对无辜平民的残害是可怕的。|
近义词: crippling, disabling, mutilating, injuring
|反义词: healing, curing, repairing
|词性:名词


maim:/meɪm/
|vt.|使残废；使残缺；严重损毁|To wound or injure someone so that part of their body is permanently damaged.|The explosion maimed several bystanders. 爆炸使几名旁观者残废。|
