jagannath:/ˌdʒʌɡəˈnɑːθ/| n.| 札格纳特（印度教主神之一，毗湿奴的化身）；不可抗拒的强大力量；摧毁一切的强大力量|A form of Krishna worshipped especially at Puri in Orissa, India, and regarded as a manifestation of Vishnu.  Also: an image of this deity. Also (in extended use): an overwhelming, advancing force that crushes or seems to crush everything in its path. |例句：The festival of Jagannath is celebrated with great pomp and show. 札格纳特节庆祝活动盛大而隆重。

例句：The army marched on like a juggernaut, crushing all resistance.  这支军队像一辆摧毁一切的战车一样前进，粉碎了所有的抵抗。|近义词：deity, god, lord, force, power|反义词:weakness, impotence|词性:noun
