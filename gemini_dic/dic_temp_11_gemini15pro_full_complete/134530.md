词源:
non- (prefix):  not, opposite of  (非，相反的)
licentious (adjective):  promiscuous and unprincipled in sexual matters. (在性方面滥交和不道德的)
-ly (suffix):  forms adverbs from adjectives (由形容词构成副词)
