nonnourishment:/ˌnɒnˈnʌrɪʃmənt/ | n. |缺乏营养,营养不足|The state of not receiving adequate nourishment.  Lack of sufficient nutrients to sustain health and growth.|Prolonged nonnourishment can lead to serious health problems. 长期营养不足会导致严重的健康问题。| The effects of nonnourishment were evident in the children's stunted growth. 营养不良的影响在孩子们发育迟缓中很明显。|近义词:malnutrition, undernourishment, starvation|反义词:nourishment, nutrition|词性:名词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + nourishment (noun)
