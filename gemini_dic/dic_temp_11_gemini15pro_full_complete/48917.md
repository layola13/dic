defrosted:/diːˈfrɒstɪd/ /diːˈfrɔːstɪd/
adjective
解冻的
(of food) thawed from a frozen state.
The defrosted chicken was ready to be cooked.  解冻后的鸡肉可以烹饪了。
近义词:thawed, melted
反义词:frozen
词性:adjective

defrosted:/diːˈfrɒstɪd/ /diːˈfrɔːstɪd/
verb
解冻
remove frost or ice from (something).
I defrosted the freezer last night. 我昨晚给冰箱除霜了。
近义词:thaw, melt
反义词:freeze
词性:verb
