initializing:/ɪˈnɪʃəlaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 初始化，初始化中 | The process of setting something up for the first time, especially a computer program or system. | The system is initializing, please wait. 系统正在初始化，请稍候。| Synonyms: setting up, booting up, starting, preparing | Antonyms: shutting down, closing, terminating | verb (present participle)

initializing:/ɪˈnɪʃəlaɪzɪŋ/ | n. | 初始化 | The act of initializing. |  The initializing of the program takes a few minutes. 程序的初始化需要几分钟。| Synonyms: setup, boot-up, start, preparation | Antonyms: shutdown, closure, termination | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From initialize + -ing.  Initialize comes from initial (from Latin initialis) + -ize.
