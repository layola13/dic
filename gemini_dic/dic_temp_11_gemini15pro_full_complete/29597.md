catechumenate:/ˌkætɪˈkjuːmɪnɪt/ |n.|慕道期，慕道者身份|The period of instruction and preparation for baptism and full membership in the Christian Church.  A person receiving such instruction.|He completed his catechumenate and was baptized last Easter. 他完成了慕道期，并在去年复活节受洗。|Synonyms:  preparation, instruction, apprenticeship, novitiate|Antonyms: membership,  full communion|noun


catechumenate:/ˌkætɪˈkjuːmɪnɪt/ |n.|慕道者团体|A group of people undergoing instruction and preparation for baptism. |The catechumenate met weekly for instruction.  慕道班每周聚会学习。|Synonyms: class, group, cohort|Antonyms: congregation, membership|noun
