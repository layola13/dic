disentitle:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtaɪtl/
verb
剥夺权利；使无资格
deprive (someone) of a right, privilege, or entitlement.
例句：The new law disentitled many people to vote. 新法律剥夺了许多人的投票权。

例句：His criminal record disentitled him from holding public office. 他的犯罪记录使他没有资格担任公职。

近义词：deprive, strip, disqualify
反义词：entitle, qualify, authorize
词性：v.
