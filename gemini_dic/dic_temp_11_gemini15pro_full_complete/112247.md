looters:/ˈluːtərz/| n.|抢劫者，掠夺者|People who steal goods during a riot or a natural disaster are called looters.  在骚乱或自然灾害期间偷窃货物的人被称为抢劫者。|例句：The looters ransacked the abandoned shops. 抢劫者洗劫了被遗弃的商店。

looters:/ˈluːtərz/| n.|掠夺者|Those who exploit or take advantage of a situation for personal gain are looters. 那些利用或利用某种情况谋取私利的人是掠夺者。 |例句：The corrupt officials were nothing more than looters of public funds. 腐败官员只不过是公共资金的掠夺者。|近义词：thieves, robbers, plunderers, pillagers|反义词: protectors, guardians|词性: 名词
