originates:/əˈrɪdʒɪneɪts/ | v. |起源于，发源于|To have as a beginning or source; to start from|例句：The custom originates from ancient times.  这个习俗起源于古代。

例句：The river originates in the mountains. 这条河发源于群山之中。|近义词：stems, derives, begins, arises, commences|反义词：ends, finishes, terminates|词性：动词

Etymology: From Late Latin *originatus*, past participle of *originari* "to rise, begin," from Latin *origo* "origin, source, beginning," from *oriri* "to rise, appear, be born."
