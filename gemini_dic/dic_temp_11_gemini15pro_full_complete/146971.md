overjocularly: /ˌoʊvərˈdʒɑːkjələrli/ /ˌəʊvəˈdʒɒkjʊləli/
adverb
过于滑稽地，过分诙谐地
in an excessively jocular manner; too jokingly
He overjocularly slapped his friend on the back, causing him to spill his drink. 他过于滑稽地拍了一下朋友的背，导致他把饮料洒了。
The comedian's overjocularly remarks about the sensitive topic offended some audience members.  这位喜剧演员关于敏感话题的过分诙谐的言论冒犯了一些观众。
近义词: facetiously, waggishly, playfully
反义词: seriously, solemnly, gravely
词性: adverb

词源:
From over- + jocular + -ly.  "Over-" signifies "excessively," "jocular" means "given to or characterized by joking," and "-ly" forms adverbs.  因此，"overjocularly" 表示“过分地开玩笑”。
