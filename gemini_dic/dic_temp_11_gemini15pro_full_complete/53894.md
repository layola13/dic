discorporate:/dɪskɔːrˈpɔːreɪt/ /dɪskɔːrˈpəreɪt/
verb
脱离肉体；使脱离法人资格
To separate the soul or spirit from the body; to deprive of corporate status.
He believed that after death, the soul would discorporate from the body. 他相信人死后，灵魂会脱离肉体。
The company was discorporated after years of financial difficulties.  这家公司在多年的财务困境后被取消了法人资格。
近义词: disembody, disincarnate, dissolve (for corporations)
反义词: incorporate, embody
词性: verb


discorporate:/dɪskɔːrˈpɔːreɪt/ /dɪskɔːrˈpəreɪt/
adjective
脱离肉体的；非法人化的
Separated from the body; not incorporated.
In some cultures, discorporate spirits are believed to roam the earth. 在某些文化中，人们相信脱离肉体的灵魂会在地球上游荡。
The discorporate assets were sold off to pay debts.  非法人化的资产被出售以偿还债务。
近义词: disembodied, unincorporated
反义词: incorporated, embodied
词性: adjective


词源:  From dis- (expressing negation or reversal) + corporate (formed into a body or corporation).  来自dis-（表示否定或反转）+ corporate（组成一个身体或公司）。
