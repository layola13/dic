outbleats:/ˌaʊtˈbliːts/| v. | 比...叫得更响，胜过 | To cry louder than; to surpass in bleating. | The alpha male sheep outbleats the others, asserting dominance.  公羊头领的叫声盖过了其他羊，以此宣示 dominance.

The politician outbleated his opponent, drowning out his arguments with louder rhetoric.  这位政客的声音盖过了他的对手，用更响亮的言辞淹没了对方的论点。 | 近义词：outcries, outshouts | 反义词：is outdone by, is quieter than | 词性：动词
