jammable:/ˈdʒæməbəl/
adjective
可干扰的，可阻塞的
Capable of being jammed or blocked.
The enemy's communications were jammable. 敌方的通讯可以被干扰。
The narrow channel was jammable with debris. 狭窄的河道很容易被碎片堵塞。
近义词:blockable, obstructable
反义词:unblockable, clear
词性:adjective
