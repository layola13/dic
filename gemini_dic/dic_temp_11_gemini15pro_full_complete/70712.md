financiers:/fɪˈnænsiərz/
| n. |金融家；财务人员；财政家|People who work in finance, especially those involved in large-scale investments and transactions.  A person who deals with finance and investment on a large scale.|The financiers were meeting to discuss the terms of the merger. 金融家们正在开会讨论合并条款。|Synonyms: investors, bankers, brokers, capitalists|Antonyms: debtors, borrowers|Noun

financiers:/fɪˈnænsiərz/
| n. | (尤指法国的) 金融家；金主|  (especially in France) A person who provides capital for businesses, often in return for a share of the profits.  A person who manages large sums of money for organizations or rich individuals.|The film was funded by a group of wealthy financiers. 这部电影是由一群富有的金主资助的。|Synonyms: backers, patrons, sponsors|Antonyms:  N/A |Noun
