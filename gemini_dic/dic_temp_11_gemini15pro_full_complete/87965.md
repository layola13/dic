high-flyer:/ˈhaɪˌflaɪər/ | n. | 成功人士；雄心勃勃的人；高飞者 | A person who is very successful in their job or studies, or who is very ambitious.  |  He's a real high-flyer in the financial world. 他在金融界是一位真正的成功人士。|  synonyms: achiever, go-getter, success story | antonyms: underachiever, failure, also-ran | noun

high-flyer:/ˈhaɪˌflaɪər/ | n. | 高飞的鸟；高飞的飞机 | A bird or aircraft that flies high. | The eagle is a high-flyer.  鹰是一种高飞的鸟。 | synonyms: soaring bird, high-altitude aircraft | antonyms: low-flying bird/aircraft | noun
