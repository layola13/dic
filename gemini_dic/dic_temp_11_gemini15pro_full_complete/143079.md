outbrawl: /ˌaʊtˈbrɔːl/
verb
打败，战胜 (尤指通过更吵闹或更具侵略性的行为)
to defeat or surpass (someone) in a brawl, especially by being louder or more aggressive.
He outbrawled his opponent in the town square, silencing him with a roar. 他在市镇广场上打败了他的对手，用一声怒吼让他安静下来。
The two rival gangs outbrawled each other for control of the territory.  两个敌对帮派为了争夺地盘而大打出手，最终一方胜出。
近义词: outfight, overpower, subdue, vanquish
反义词: lose to, surrender to
词性: verb

outbrawl: /ˌaʊtˈbrɔːl/
noun
激烈的争吵或斗殴
a noisy or violent quarrel or fight.
The outbrawl spilled out from the bar and into the street.  这场激烈的争吵从酒吧蔓延到了街上。
The political outbrawl dominated the news for weeks. 这场政治上的激烈争吵占据了数周的新闻头条。
近义词: brawl, fight, fracas, melee
反义词: peace, truce, agreement
词性: noun
