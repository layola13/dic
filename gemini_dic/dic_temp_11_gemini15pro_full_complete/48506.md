deerfield:/ˈdɪərfild/| n. |鹿场；地名（通常指美国几个叫Deerfield的地方）|A field frequented by deer, or a place named after such a field.  It often refers to several places in the United States named Deerfield.|He grew up in Deerfield, Massachusetts. 他在马萨诸塞州的迪尔菲尔德长大。|近义词：meadow, pasture, grassland|反义词: urban area|noun

词源:
"Deer" comes from Old English "deor," meaning "animal," and "field" comes from Old English "feld," meaning "open country."  The word "deerfield" is a compound word, simply meaning a field where deer are found.
