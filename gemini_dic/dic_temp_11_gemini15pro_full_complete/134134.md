nonimperiousness:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpɪəriəsnəs/
n.|不专横, 不傲慢; 温和|The quality or state of not being imperious; lack of arrogance or domineering attitude.|Her nonimperiousness made her a beloved leader. 她不专横的态度使她成为一位受爱戴的领导者。|Synonyms: humility, modesty, unassumingness|Antonyms: arrogance, imperiousness, haughtiness|noun
