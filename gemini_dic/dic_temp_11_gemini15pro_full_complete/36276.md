cohabiting:/koʊˈhæbɪtɪŋ/
adjective
同居的
Living together in a sexual relationship, especially without being married.
例句：They are a cohabiting couple. 他们是同居伴侣。

例句：The number of cohabiting couples has increased significantly in recent years. 近年来，同居伴侣的数量显著增加。

近义词：living together, cohabitant
反义词：married, single, separate
词性: adjective

cohabit:/koʊˈhæbɪt/
verb
同居
To live together in a sexual relationship, especially without being married.
例句：They cohabited for five years before getting married. 他们同居了五年才结婚。

例句：Many couples choose to cohabit before marriage. 许多情侣选择在结婚前同居。


近义词：live together, shack up
反义词：live apart
词性: verb

Etymology:
From Late Latin *cohabitare, from Latin co- ‘together’ + habitare ‘to dwell’.
