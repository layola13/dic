abjurers:/əbˈdʒʊrərz/
n.
放弃者，发誓放弃者
People who formally renounce or reject a belief, cause, or claim.
The abjurers of violence gathered to protest the war.  放弃暴力的人聚集在一起抗议战争。
近义词：renouncers, repudiators, disavowers
反义词：advocates, supporters, adherents
词性：名词

abjurer:/əbˈdʒʊrər/
n.
放弃者，发誓放弃者
A person who abjures.
He became an abjurer of his former faith. 他放弃了他以前的信仰。
近义词：renouncer, repudiator, disavower
反义词：advocate, supporter, adherent
词性：名词


词源:
From Middle English *abjuren, from Old French abjurer, from Latin abiūrāre (“to deny on oath, forswear, recant, abjure”), from ab (“away from”) + iūrāre (“to swear”).
