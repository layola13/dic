nonrivals:/nɒnˈraɪvəlz/ | adj. | 非竞争性的，非对抗性的 |  Refers to goods or services whose consumption by one person does not diminish the availability of those goods or services for others to consume.  They are characterized by non-excludability and non-rivalry. |  Clean air is considered a nonrival good because one person breathing it does not prevent another from doing so.  空气被认为是非竞争性商品，因为一个人呼吸它并不会阻止另一个人呼吸它。 | Synonyms: non-rivalrous, non-competitive | Antonyms: rival, competitive | adjective
