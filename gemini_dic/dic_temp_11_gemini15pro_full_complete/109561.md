let-down:/ˈletdaʊn/
noun
失望；沮丧；令人失望的人或事
a disappointment or a source of disappointment.
The party was a bit of a let-down.  这个聚会有点令人失望。
The movie was a real let-down after all the hype.  这部电影经过大肆宣传后，结果却令人大失所望。
disappointment, setback, anticlimax
success, triumph
n.
