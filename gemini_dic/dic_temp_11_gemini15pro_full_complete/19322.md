biblical:/ˈbɪblɪkəl/
adj.
圣经的；像圣经的
Of, relating to, or contained in the Bible.
The story has biblical overtones.  这个故事带有圣经的色彩。
近义词：scriptural, religious
反义词：secular, nonreligious
词性：adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *biblicus, from Latin biblia "the Bible," from Greek biblia (ta biblia) "the (holy) books," plural of biblion "book," diminutive of biblos "papyrus," of Egyptian origin.
