hearsal:/ˈhɜːrsl/ (US) /rɪˈhɜːsl/ (UK)| n. |排练，彩排|A practice or trial performance of a play, piece of music, etc., in preparation for a public performance.|The orchestra had a final rehearsal before the concert.  管弦乐队在音乐会前进行了最后一次彩排。|例句：The actors are in rehearsal for the new play.  演员们正在为新剧排练。|近义词：practice, run-through, preparation|反义词：performance|词性: 名词
