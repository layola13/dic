ambulant:/ˈæmbjʊlənt/| adj.|走动的，可走动的，流动的|Able to walk; mobile.|The hospital has a special unit for ambulant patients. 这家医院有一个专门收治可走动病人的单元。|近义词: mobile, walking, peripatetic|反义词: bedridden, immobile, sedentary|adjective

ambulatory:/ˈæmbjʊlətɔːri/| adj.|适于步行的，（病人）可走动的，流动的，巡回的| Relating to or adapted for walking; capable of walking.|The patient is now ambulatory and can move around the ward freely.  病人现在可以走动了，可以在病房里自由走动。|近义词: mobile, walking, peripatetic|反义词: bedridden, immobile, sedentary|adjective


词源: From Late Latin *ambulant- (stem of ambulans), present participle of ambulare (“to walk”), from amb- (“around”) + -ulare (frequentative suffix).
