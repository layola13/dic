over-graded:/ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪdɪd/ | adj. | 过度分级的，过高估计的 | Placed in a higher grade or category than appropriate.  Assigned a higher quality or value than is warranted. |  The students felt over-graded on the easy exam.  学生们觉得这次简单的考试评分过高了。| The wine was clearly over-graded and overpriced.  这种酒显然被高估了，价格也过高。 |  overestimated, overrated | underrated, undervalued | adjective
