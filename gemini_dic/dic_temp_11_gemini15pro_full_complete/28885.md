carpoolings:/ˈkɑːrˌpuːlɪŋz/
noun
拼车，合伙用车
The act of sharing a car with other people, especially for commuting to and from work or school.
We organized carpoolings to reduce our carbon footprint. 我们组织了拼车以减少碳足迹。
Carpoolings can save you money on gas. 拼车可以节省汽油费。
