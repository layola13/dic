dentil:/ˈdɛntəl/ | n. |  齿状装饰；小齿；细齿 | A small, square, tooth-like block used as a repeating ornament in cornices and moldings. | The dentil molding added a touch of elegance to the room.  齿状装饰为房间增添了一丝优雅。| synonym: tooth, cog, notch | antonym: gap, smooth | noun

dentil:/ˈdɛntəl/ | adj. | 牙齿的；齿状的 | Relating to teeth. (Less common usage) |  The dentist specialized in dentil surgery.  这位牙医专门从事牙科手术。| synonym: dental | antonym: edentulous | adjective
