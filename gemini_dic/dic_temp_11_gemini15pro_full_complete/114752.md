malpais:/mælˈpaɪs/ (US), /mælˈpeɪs/ (UK)| n. |荒地，恶土|A rough, barren, and desolate area, typically covered with volcanic rock.|We hiked across the malpais, the jagged lava rocks crunching under our boots. 我们徒步穿越了荒地，锯齿状的火山岩在我们脚下嘎吱作响。|The malpais stretched for miles, a testament to the volcano's destructive power. 荒地绵延数英里，证明了火山的破坏力。|近义词：badlands, wasteland, desert|反义词: fertile land, oasis|词性: 名词

词源: From Spanish *malpaís*, literally "bad country," from *mal* ("bad") + *país* ("country").
