equirotal:/ˌiːkwɪˈroʊtəl/ | adj. | 等旋的，等角速度的 | Having equal rotational speeds or angular velocities. | The two gears are equirotal, ensuring synchronized movement.  两个齿轮等旋，确保同步运动。 |Example: The equirotal blades of the helicopter ensure stability in flight. 直升机等旋的桨叶确保飞行稳定。| Synonym: isorotational, co-rotating | Antonym: counter-rotating | Word type: adjective
