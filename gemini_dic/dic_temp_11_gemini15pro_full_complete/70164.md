fiber:/ˈfaɪbər/ (US) /ˈfaɪbə/ (UK)| n.|纤维; 光纤; 质地; 品格|A thread or filament from which a vegetable tissue, mineral substance, or textile is formed.|例句：The sweater is made of natural fiber. 这件毛衣是由天然纤维制成的。

例句：Optical fibers transmit data at high speed. 光纤高速传输数据。

|近义词：thread, filament, strand, texture|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


fiber:/ˈfaɪbər/ (US) /ˈfaɪbə/ (UK)| n.|食物纤维|The part of food that cannot be digested but helps keep you healthy by moving other food quickly through your body.|例句：Eating plenty of fiber helps with digestion. 多吃纤维有助于消化。

例句：Fruits and vegetables are good sources of fiber. 水果和蔬菜是良好的纤维来源。

|近义词：roughage|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
