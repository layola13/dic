bladders:/ˈblæd.ərz/ (US), /ˈblæd.əz/ (UK)| n. |膀胱；囊；气泡|A membranous sac in humans and other animals, in which urine is collected for excretion.  Also, any similar sac-like organ or structure.|The fish's swim bladder helps it control its buoyancy. 鱼的鳔帮助它控制浮力。|Synonyms: sac, vesicle, pouch|Antonyms: None|Noun
