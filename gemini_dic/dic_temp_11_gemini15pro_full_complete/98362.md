insisting:/ɪnˈsɪstɪŋ/| adj. |坚持的，坚决要求的|Present participle of "insist," meaning to demand something forcefully or to persist in stating something.|He was insisting on his innocence despite the evidence against him. 他坚持自己的清白，尽管有证据反对他。|近义词:persistent, adamant, tenacious|反义词:yielding, compliant, acquiescent|词性:形容词

insist:/ɪnˈsɪst/| v. |坚持，坚决要求|To demand something forcefully, not accepting refusal, or to persist in stating something.|I insist that you come with us. 我坚持要你跟我们一起来。|近义词:demand, maintain, assert|反义词:yield, concede, relinquish|词性:动词
