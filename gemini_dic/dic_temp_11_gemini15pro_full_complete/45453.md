curateship:/ˈkjʊrətʃɪp/ /ˈkjʊreɪtərʃɪp/
n.
策展工作，策展人的职位
the position or job of a curator.
例句：Her curateship at the museum was highly praised. 她在博物馆的策展工作备受赞扬。

例句：The curateship of the exhibition required extensive research.  这个展览的策展工作需要大量的研究。

近义词：curation, management, supervision
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider neglect, abandonment in specific contexts)
词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From curate + -ship.  "Curate" ultimately derives from the Latin "curare" meaning "to take care of."  The suffix "-ship" denotes a state, condition, or office.
