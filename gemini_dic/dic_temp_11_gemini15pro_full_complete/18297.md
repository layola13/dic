belladonnas:/ˌbɛləˈdɒnəz/ | n. |颠茄（复数）|A perennial herbaceous plant (Atropa belladonna) of the nightshade family, native to Eurasia and having reddish bell-shaped flowers and poisonous black berries. Also called deadly nightshade. |例句：Belladonnas are highly toxic and should not be ingested. 颠茄毒性很强，不应摄入。

|例句：The belladonnas in the garden are in full bloom. 花园里的颠茄正在盛开。|近义词：deadly nightshade, Atropa belladonna |反义词:  (No direct antonym for a plant. Perhaps a medicinal antidote could be considered, but that's not a lexical antonym) |词性: noun
