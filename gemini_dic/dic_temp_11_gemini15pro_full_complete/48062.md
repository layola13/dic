declamatory:/dɪˈklæmətɔːri/
adjective
演说般的，雄辩的，朗诵式的
Relating to or characterized by declamation; rhetorical; theatrical in style.
He delivered a declamatory speech, full of dramatic pauses and pronouncements.  他发表了一篇慷慨激昂的演讲，充满了戏剧性的停顿和声明。
The actor's declamatory style was perfect for the role of the king.  这位演员雄辩的风格非常适合国王这个角色。
近义词:rhetorical, oratorical, bombastic, pompous
反义词:understated, quiet, subdued
词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Latin declamatorius, from Latin declamare (to declaim).
