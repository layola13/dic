catastrophe:/kəˈtæstrəfi/ /kəˈtɑːstrəfi/| n.|灾难，灾祸|A sudden event that causes very great trouble or destruction. |The earthquake was a catastrophe for the city.  这场地震对这座城市来说是一场灾难。|
catastrophe:| n.|彻底的失败|A complete failure. |The play was a catastrophe. 这部戏彻底失败了。|
catastrophe:| n.|(小说、戏剧中的)结局，结局部分|(especially in a novel or play) the final event, usually an unhappy one. |The final scene of the play is the catastrophe. 这部戏剧的最后一幕是结局。|


近义词:disaster, calamity, tragedy, cataclysm
反义词:triumph, success, blessing
词性:noun
