electrogenic:/ɪˌlɛktrəˈdʒɛnɪk/
adjective
产电的；致电的
Producing a change in the electrical potential of a cell membrane.
The sodium-potassium pump is electrogenic, contributing to the negative resting membrane potential.  钠钾泵是产电的，它有助于形成负的静息膜电位。
The electrogenic transport of ions creates a voltage difference across the membrane. 离子致电转运在膜两侧产生电压差。
近义词:electropositive
反义词:electronegative
词性:adjective
