fanaticalness:/fəˈnætɪkəlnəs/
n.|狂热; 入迷|The state of being fanatical; excessive enthusiasm or zeal.|His fanaticalness for the cause alienated his friends.  他对这项事业的狂热疏远了他的朋友们。|近义词: fanaticism, zealotry, extremism|反义词: apathy, indifference, moderation|词性:名词

Etymology:
From fanatic + -al + -ness.  Fanatic comes from the Latin "fanaticus," meaning "inspired by a deity, frenzied, enthusiastic," from "fanum" ("temple").  The suffix "-al" forms adjectives, and "-ness" forms nouns denoting a state or quality.
