over-sensitize: /ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsɪtaɪz/ | v. | 过度敏感化；使过于敏感 | To make someone or something excessively sensitive or reactive to certain stimuli. | The media coverage over-sensitized the public to the issue. 媒体的报道使公众对这个问题过于敏感。| The constant criticism had over-sensitized him to any form of feedback.  不断的批评使他对任何形式的反馈都过于敏感。| 近义词: overreact, exaggerate | 反义词: desensitize, numb | 词性: 动词
