How does she manage to do everything? = 她是怎么做到这一切的？


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
How are you feeling today? = 你今天感觉怎么样？
How is she coping with the stress? = 她如何应对压力？


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
How have you been? = 你最近怎么样？
How has the project progressed so far? = 到目前为止，项目进展如何？


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
How have you been spending your time lately? = 你最近一直在做什么？
How has he been dealing with the situation? = 他最近一直在如何处理这种情况？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
