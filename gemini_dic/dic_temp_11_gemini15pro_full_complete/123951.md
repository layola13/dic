moric:/ˈmɔːrɪk/ | adj. | 音节时长相等的；长音节的 | Relating to or consisting of morae; having two morae.  A metrical foot consisting of one long syllable or two short syllables. |  The poem is written in moric verse. 这首诗是用长音节韵律写的。| synonyms: quantitative, measured | antonyms: unmeasured, free | adjective
