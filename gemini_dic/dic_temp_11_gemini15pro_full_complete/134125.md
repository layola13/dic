nonimpedimentary:/ˌnɒnɪmˌpedɪˈmentəri/
adjective
无障碍的，不阻碍的
Presenting no obstacle or impediment.  Free to proceed.
The council issued a nonimpedimentary certificate for the couple to marry.  市议会签发了这对夫妇结婚的无障碍证明。
Synonyms: unobstructed, unhindered, clear, permitting
Antonyms: obstructive, hindering, impedimental, blocking
词性: adjective
