lead-in:/ˈliːdɪn/ |n.| 导言，开场白 |An introduction to a subject or topic, designed to prepare the audience or reader for what will follow. |The chairman gave a brief lead-in to the speaker. 主席简短地介绍了演讲者。| synonym: introduction, preface, preamble | antonym: conclusion, epilogue | noun

lead-in:/ˈliːdɪn/ |adj.| 引导的，开场的 |Serving as an introduction. |The lead-in music created a sense of anticipation. 开场音乐营造了一种期待感。| synonym: introductory, preliminary | antonym: concluding, final | adjective
