overrepresentativeness:/ˌoʊvərˌrɛprɪˌzɛntətɪvnəs/
|n.| 过度代表性；代表性过高|The overrepresentativeness of certain demographics in specific industries can lead to bias. 特定行业中某些人口统计数据的过度代表性可能导致偏见。|Overrepresentativeness can skew research results. 代表性过高会扭曲研究结果。|近义词: disproportion, overrepresentation |反义词: underrepresentation, underrepresentativeness|noun
