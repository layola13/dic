moonbeams:/ˈmuːnbiːmz/| n. |月光，月华|Rays of light from the moon. |The moonbeams streamed through the window, illuminating the dust motes dancing in the air. 月光透过窗户流泻进来，照亮了空气中飞舞的尘埃。

The sleeping child looked peaceful bathed in the soft moonbeams. 沐浴在柔和的月光中，熟睡的孩子看起来很安详。|近义词：moonlight, lunar rays|反义词:sunbeams, sunlight|词性:名词

词源:
From Middle English *mone bemes, equivalent to moon + beam.  Beam itself comes from Old English *bēam, meaning "tree, beam of light."
