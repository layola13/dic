aliquant:/ˈælɪkwɒt/| adj.|整除的，可均分的|Contained an exact number of times without a remainder.  Relating to a divisor that does not divide a quantity evenly, leaving a remainder.|例句：10 is an aliquant part of 16 (because 16 divided by 10 leaves a remainder). 10是16的不可约数（因为16除以10有余数）。

例句：3 is an aliquant part of 7. 3是7的不可约数。|近义词：uneven, unequal|反义词:aliquot, even|词性:adjective
