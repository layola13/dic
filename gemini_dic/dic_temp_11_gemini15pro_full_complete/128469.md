newslessness:/ˈnjuːzləsnəs/| n.|无新闻状态；缺乏新闻；平静无事|A state characterized by the absence of news or noteworthy events.  The newslessness of the small town was both comforting and unsettling.|例句：The newslessness of the summer was a welcome change from the constant barrage of headlines during the school year.  夏天的平静无事与学年期间连绵不断的头条新闻相比，是一个受欢迎的变化。

例句：The newslessness was broken by a sudden and shocking announcement.  突如其来的震惊公告打破了平静无事的状态。|近义词：quiet, calm, inactivity, peace|反义词：eventfulness, activity, excitement|词性:noun
