cascaders:/kæˈskeɪdərz/| n.|瀑布群；倾泻者；级联者|People or things that cascade or fall in a series of stages, like a waterfall.|The cascading waterfalls created a breathtaking view. 倾泻而下的瀑布群构成了一幅令人叹为观止的景象.|The data cascaders down from the central server to individual computers. 数据从中央服务器级联到各个计算机。|近义词：falls, cascades, series|反义词:ascenders|noun
