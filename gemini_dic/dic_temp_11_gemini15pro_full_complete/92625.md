iatrochemical:/ˌaɪətroʊˈkɛmɪkəl/
adjective
医药化学的; 医疗化学的
Relating to or denoting iatrochemistry (a historical approach to medicine that sought to understand and treat disease in terms of chemical principles).
Iatrochemical theories were popular in the 17th century.  医药化学理论在17世纪很流行。
The physician employed iatrochemical remedies. 那位医生采用了医药化学疗法。
近义词: alchemical, spagyric
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "modern medical" or "holistic")
词性: adjective
