dihexagonal:/ˌdaɪhɛkˈsæɡənəl/
adjective
六角形的；由两个六边形组成的
Relating to or consisting of two hexagons.  A dihexagonal crystal has six sides at one end and twelve sides around its middle.
Dihexagonal prisms are common in crystallography.  这些晶体具有独特的双六边形结构。
Near synonyms: hexagonal, dodecagonal
Antonym: non-hexagonal
词性: adjective
