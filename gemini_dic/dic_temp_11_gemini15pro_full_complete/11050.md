arch-chanter:/ˈɑːrtʃˌtʃæntər/ | n. |首席圣咏歌手；唱诗班领唱者|A leading or principal chanter, especially in a cathedral. |The arch-chanter led the choir in a beautiful rendition of the hymn.|Synonym: precentor, choirmaster|Antonym: chorister (a regular member of the choir, not the leader)|Noun
