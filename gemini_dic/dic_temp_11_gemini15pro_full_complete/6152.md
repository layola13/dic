amide:/ˈæmaɪd/ /ˈeɪmaɪd/| n. |酰胺|An organic compound derived from ammonia or an amine by replacement of one or more hydrogen atoms by acyl groups.|Nylon is a synthetic polyamide. 尼龙是一种合成聚酰胺。|synonyms: acid amide|antonyms: N/A|noun

amide:/ˈæmaɪd/ /ˈeɪmaɪd/| n. |氨基化合物|A compound derived from ammonia by replacing a hydrogen atom by a metal, such as sodamide, NaNH2.|Sodamide is an example of a metal amide.  氨基钠是一种金属氨基化合物。|synonyms: metal amide|antonyms: N/A|noun
