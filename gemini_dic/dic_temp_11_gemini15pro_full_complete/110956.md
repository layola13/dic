liquation:/lɪˈkweɪʃən/
n.
熔化;液化;熔析
The process of separating substances by melting them.
The liquation of the metal allowed for the removal of impurities.  金属的熔析可以去除杂质。
melting, fusion, liquefaction
solidification, freezing
noun
