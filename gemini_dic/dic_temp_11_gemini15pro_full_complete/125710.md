municipal:/mjuːˈnɪsɪpəl/ | adj. | 市政的，市立的 | Relating to a city or town or its local government. |  The municipal government is responsible for garbage collection. 市政府负责垃圾收集。

The municipal swimming pool is open to the public during the summer. 市立游泳池在夏季向公众开放。

| 近义词: civic, urban, city | 反义词: rural, non-municipal | 词性: adjective
