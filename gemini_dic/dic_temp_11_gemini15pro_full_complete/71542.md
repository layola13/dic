flareless:/ˈflɛərlɪs/
adjective
无耀斑的，不闪光的
Not flaring; without a flare; giving off no sudden burst of flame or light.
The flareless landing was a testament to the pilot's skill.  无耀斑的着陆证明了飞行员的技术。
The miner's lamp was designed to be flareless, reducing the risk of explosions. 矿工的灯被设计成无耀斑的，以减少爆炸的风险。
synonym:non-flaring, steady
antonym:flaring, flickering
词性:形容词
