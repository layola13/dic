incestuousness:/ɪnˈsɛstʃuəsnəs/
n.
乱伦；血亲相奸
The state or quality of being incestuous; sexual relations between people classed as being too closely related to marry each other.
The incestuousness of their relationship shocked the community. 他们的乱伦关系震惊了整个社区。
近义词：incest, inbreeding
反义词：chastity, purity
词性: 名词
