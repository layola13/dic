overactivities:/ˌəʊvərækˈtɪvɪtiz/ | n. | 过度活动；过度活跃 | Excessive activity or restlessness. |  The child's overactivities in class disrupted the other students.  这个孩子在课堂上的过度活跃扰乱了其他学生。| 近义词：hyperactivity, restlessness, busyness | 反义词：inactivity, lethargy, stillness | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From "over" (Old English *ofer) + "activity" (from Late Latin activitas, from Latin activus "active")
