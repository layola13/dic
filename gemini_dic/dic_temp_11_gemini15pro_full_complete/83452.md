habituality:/ˌhæbɪtʃuˈælɪti/
n.
习惯性，惯常性
The habituality of his actions made them seem almost automatic.  他行动的习惯性使它们看起来几乎是自动的。
His habituality of smoking is hard to break. 他吸烟的习惯很难戒掉。
近义词:customariness, routine, regularity
反义词: irregularity, unfamiliarity, novelty
词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *habitualitas, from Latin habitus (“condition, state, appearance, dress; character, quality; habit”), perfect passive participle of habeō (“I have, hold, keep, possess, wear (clothes); consider, think; manage, administer; inhabit, dwell in; am able, can”).  Surface analysis is habit + -ual + -ity.
