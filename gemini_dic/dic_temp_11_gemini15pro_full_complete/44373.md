cross-cousin:/ˈkrɒsˌkʌzən/ (US) /ˈkrɒsˌkʌzn/ (UK)| n. |堂表兄弟姐妹（父母一方的兄弟姐妹的孩子，性别不限）|A child of one's parent's sibling of the opposite sex.  For example, the child of your mother's brother or your father's sister.|My mother's brother's son is my cross-cousin. 我妈妈哥哥的儿子是我的堂表兄弟。|
|My father's sister's daughter is my cross-cousin. 我爸爸姐姐的女儿是我的堂表姐妹。|
|Synonym: cousin (in a broader sense)|
|Antonym: parallel cousin|
|noun|
