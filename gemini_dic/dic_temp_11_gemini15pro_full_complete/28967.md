carrying:/ˈkæriɪŋ/| v. |搬运，携带；支撑，承载；传播，输送|The porter was carrying a heavy suitcase.  搬运工提着一个沉重的行李箱。|近义词：transporting, bearing, holding, conveying|反义词：dropping, leaving|Verb (present participle)

carrying:/ˈkæriɪŋ/| adj. |承载的，运输的|The carrying capacity of the bridge is 10 tons. 这座桥的承载能力是10吨。|近义词：supporting, transporting|反义词：N/A|Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *carien*, from Old North French *carier*, from Late Latin *carricāre* "to load on a car," from Latin *carrus* "wagon, cart."
