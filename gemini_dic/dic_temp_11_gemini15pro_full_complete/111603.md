lobular:/ˈlɒbjʊlə(r)/ (US) /ˈlɑːbjələr/| adj.|小叶的，分叶的|Relating to or resembling a lobule, a small lobe or subdivision of a lobe, especially in an organ or gland.|The doctor identified lobular carcinoma in situ during the biopsy. 医生在活检过程中发现了小叶原位癌。|Synonyms: lobed, segmented|Antonyms: unsegmented, whole|adjective

lobular:/ˈlɒbjʊlə(r)/ (US) /ˈlɑːbjələr/| adj.|小叶的，分叶的|Consisting of or divided into lobules.|Lobular pneumonia affects a small section of the lung. 小叶性肺炎影响肺的一小部分。|Synonyms: lobate|Antonyms: entire|adjective
