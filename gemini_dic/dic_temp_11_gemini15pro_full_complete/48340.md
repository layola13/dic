decrown:/diːˈkraʊn/
verb
剥夺王冠；废黜
to remove the crown from; to depose from regal power
The revolutionaries decrowned the king and established a republic. 革命者废黜了国王并建立了共和国。
The king was decrowned and exiled. 国王被废黜并流放了。
Synonyms: dethrone, unseat, depose, oust
Antonyms: crown, enthrone, install
词性: 动词
