hyperinflations:/ˌhaɪpərɪnˈfleɪʃənz/| n. |恶性通货膨胀|A period of rapid and excessive price increases in an economy.|例句：The country suffered from devastating hyperinflations after the war. 战后该国遭受了毁灭性的恶性通货膨胀。

例句：Hyperinflations can wipe out savings and destabilize entire economies. 恶性通货膨胀会摧毁储蓄，并破坏整个经济的稳定。|近义词：runaway inflation, galloping inflation|反义词:deflation, disinflation|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- + inflation, from Latin inflare "to blow into, inflate," figuratively "puff up, swell with pride," from in- "in" + flare "to blow."
