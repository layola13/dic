baches:/ˈbætʃɪz/| n. |小溪，溪流；(尤指)间歇河|Small streams or creeks, especially those that are intermittent (flowing only part of the time).|The heavy rains turned the dry baches into raging torrents. 暴雨把干涸的溪流变成了汹涌的激流。|Synonyms: creeks, streams, brooks, washes, arroyos|Antonyms: rivers, oceans|noun
