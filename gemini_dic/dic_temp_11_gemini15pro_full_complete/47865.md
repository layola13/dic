deceive:/dɪˈsiːv/| v. |欺骗，蒙蔽|To cause someone to believe something that is not true, typically in order to gain some personal advantage.|He deceived his parents about his grades. 他欺骗了他的父母关于他的成绩。|
近义词：trick, mislead, cheat, defraud, fool|反义词：be honest, tell the truth|词性:动词|
词源：From Old French *decevoir*, from Latin *dēcīpere* ("to ensnare, beguile, take in, cheat, deceive"), from *de-* ("away, from") + *capere* ("to take, seize, capture").|
