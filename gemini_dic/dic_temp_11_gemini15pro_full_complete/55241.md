dissimilarly:/dɪˈsɪmɪlərli/
adv.
不同地，相异地
in a dissimilar way; differently.
The twins, despite their identical looks, behaved dissimilarly. 这对双胞胎尽管长得一模一样，但行为举止却截然不同。
近义词: differently, distinctively, uniquely, variously
反义词: similarly, alike, identically
词性: 副词
