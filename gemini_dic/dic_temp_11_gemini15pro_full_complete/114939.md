managing:/ˈmænɪdʒɪŋ/
v.|管理；经营；控制；设法做到|The company is managed by a board of directors.|公司由董事会管理。|
近义词：controlling, directing, handling, administering, supervising, overseeing
反义词：failing, neglecting
词性:动词(present participle of manage)

managing:/ˈmænɪdʒɪŋ/
adj.|管理的；处理的|He has a managing personality.|他很有管理才能。|
近义词：administrative, executive, supervisory
反义词：subordinate
词性:形容词


词源: From Old French *maneger "to handle," especially a horse, from Italian maneggiare "to handle," especially a horse, from mano "hand," from Latin manus "hand".
