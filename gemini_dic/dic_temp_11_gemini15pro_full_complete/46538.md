dales:/deɪlz/| n. |山谷（尤指英格兰北部及苏格兰南部的）|Valleys, especially in northern England and southern Scotland. |The Yorkshire Dales are famous for their scenic beauty.  约克郡山谷以其优美的景色而闻名。|近义词：valleys, glens|反义词:mountains, hills|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *dæl (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *dalą (“valley”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰel- (“valley, pit”). Cognate with German Tal (”valley”), Dutch dal (“valley”), Swedish dal (“valley”), Icelandic dalr (“valley”).
