confucian:/kənˈfjuːʃən/ | adj. n.| 儒家的，儒教的；儒家学者，儒生 | Relating to or characteristic of Confucius or his philosophy; a follower of Confucianism. |  Confucian principles emphasize filial piety and social harmony. 儒家原则强调孝道和社会和谐。| Synonyms: Confucianist | Antonyms: None | adjective/noun

confucian:/kənˈfjuːʃənɪst/ | n. | 儒家学者，儒生| A follower or advocate of Confucianism. | He is a devout Confucianist. 他是一位虔诚的儒家学者。| Synonyms: Confucian | Antonyms: None | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *Confucianus*, from Confucius, the Latinized form of  Kǒng Fūzǐ (孔子), the name of the Chinese philosopher.
