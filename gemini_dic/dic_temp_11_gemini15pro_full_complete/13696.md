attributive:/əˈtrɪbjʊtɪv/
adjective
定语的，属性的
A word or phrase that modifies a noun.  It describes or clarifies the noun.
The *attributive* adjective "red" modifies the noun "car" in the phrase "red car".  红色的汽车中的定语形容词“red”修饰名词“car”。
synonyms:  modifying, qualifying, descriptive, limiting
antonyms: predicative
词性: adjective
