ghoul:/ɡuːl/ | n. | 食尸鬼；盗墓者；贪婪残忍的人 | An evil spirit or demon, especially one supposed to rob graves and feed on corpses; a person morbidly interested in death or disaster. |  The story tells of a ghoul that haunts the graveyard.  这个故事讲述了一个在墓地出没的食尸鬼。

The media portrayed him as a ghoul, feasting on the suffering of others. 媒体把他描绘成一个食尸鬼，以他人的痛苦为乐。 | 近义词: demon, fiend, monster, vampire | 反义词: angel, hero | 词性: 名词
