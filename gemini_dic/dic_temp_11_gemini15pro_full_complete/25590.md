bummed:/bʌmd/
adjective
沮丧的，失望的
Feeling disappointed, upset, or depressed.
I was really bummed when I failed the test. 我考试不及格，感到很沮丧。
近义词:disappointed, upset, down, depressed
反义词:happy, cheerful, elated
词性:adjective

词源:
From the verb "bum," meaning "to depress or discourage," which originated in the late 19th century.  The exact origin of "bum" in this sense is uncertain, but it may be related to earlier senses of "bum" referring to a lazy or worthless person, perhaps implying a feeling of being brought low or demoralized.
