nonaccusing:/ˌnɑːnəˈkjuːzɪŋ/| adj.|非指责的，不责备的|Not blaming or accusing; free from accusation.  It describes something (usually a tone, manner, or statement) that avoids placing blame or making an accusation.|She spoke in a nonaccusing tone, trying to understand his perspective.|近义词：understanding, compassionate, forgiving, non-judgmental|反义词：accusing, blaming, critical, judgmental|词性:形容词
词源:  "Non-" (a prefix meaning "not") + "accusing" (present participle of the verb "accuse," from Latin "accusare" - to call to account).
