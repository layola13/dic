aga:/ˈɑːɡə/| n.| 阿加 |A title of respect for a civil or military officer or other person of high rank, especially in Turkey. (对文职或军职官员或其他高级官员的尊称，尤其在土耳其。) |He was addressed as aga. (他被称为阿加。) |近义词:chief, leader, pasha |反义词: subordinate, follower |词性:名词

词源:
From Turkish ağa, from Proto-Turkic *aqa (“elder brother, older male relative”).
