duplicities:/ˌduːplɪˈsɪtiz/| n. |欺骗；口是心非；两面派行为|deceitfulness; double-dealing.  The act of being two-faced or dishonest, often involving hidden motives and contradictory actions.| His duplicities finally came to light when his secret emails were leaked. 他的两面派行为最终在他秘密邮件泄露后被曝光。|Synonyms: deception, dishonesty, hypocrisy, treachery, fraud, guile, chicanery|Antonyms: honesty, sincerity, forthrightness, candor, openness, transparency|noun
