herbaged:/ˈhɜːrbɪdʒd/
adjective
长满草的；被草覆盖的
covered with herbage; grass-covered
The field was herbaged and ready for the cows to graze.  这片田地长满了草，可以放牛吃草了。
The hills were herbaged with a variety of wild grasses.  山上覆盖着各种各样的野草。
Synonym: grassy, verdant
Antonym: barren, bare
词性: adjective
