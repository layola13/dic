outlasts:/ˌaʊtˈlæsts/| v.|比...持久；比...活得长|  To live or last longer than; to survive or endure beyond. |The tortoise outlasts most other animals. 乌龟比大多数其他动物活得长。|近义词：survives, outlives, endures|反义词: succumbs, perishes|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + last.  "Out-" acts as a prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing. "Last" comes from Old English *læstan "to perform, continue, follow," from Proto-Germanic *lastan "to follow, continue," from PIE root *leis- "track, furrow."
