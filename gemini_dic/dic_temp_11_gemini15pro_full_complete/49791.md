demonized:/ˈdiːmənaɪzd/
Verb (past participle)
被妖魔化的
Having been portrayed as wicked or threatening.
The media demonized the candidate, portraying him as a dangerous extremist. 媒体将这位候选人妖魔化，把他描绘成一个危险的极端分子。
Vilified, demonised(英式拼写), stigmatized, condemned
Lionized, praised, celebrated
