nichemanship:/ˈniːʃmənˌʃɪp/ /ˈnɪtʃmənˌʃɪp/| n. |利基策略；缝隙市场策略；针对特定小市场的营销策略|The art or strategy of specializing in a particular, often small, segment of the market.  It involves identifying and exploiting a niche market to gain a competitive advantage.| Many small businesses thrive by employing effective nichemanship, focusing on a specific product or service that larger companies overlook. 许多小型企业通过采用有效的利基策略而蓬勃发展，专注于大型公司忽视的特定产品或服务。|近义词：niche marketing, specialized marketing, targeted marketing|反义词: mass marketing, general marketing|词性:noun

词源:
niche (n.) + -manship (suffix).  "Niche" comes from French, ultimately from Latin nidus "nest." "-manship" is a suffix forming nouns of quality or skill, like sportsmanship or salesmanship.
