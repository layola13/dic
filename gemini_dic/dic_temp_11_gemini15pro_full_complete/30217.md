cellarless:/ˈselərləs/| adj.|没有地下室的|Without a cellar.  A house that lacks a cellar is described as cellarless. |例句：They bought a small, cellarless house in the city. 他们在城里买了一栋没有地下室的小房子。

例句：Cellarless homes are more common in warmer climates. 在气候温暖的地区，没有地下室的房屋更为常见。|近义词：basementless|反义词：with a cellar, with a basement|词性：adjective
