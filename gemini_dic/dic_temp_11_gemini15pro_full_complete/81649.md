greekish:/ˈɡriːkɪʃ/| adj.|希腊式的；有点像希腊的；希腊风格的|Resembling or characteristic of Greece, its people, or its culture, but not genuinely so.|The restaurant's decor was vaguely greekish, with whitewashed walls and blue accents.|近义词：Hellenic, Greek-like|反义词:Non-Greek, un-Greek|词性:形容词
词源:Greek + -ish (suffix indicating resemblance or partial quality).
