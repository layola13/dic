consortium:/kənˈsɔːrtiəm/ /kənˈsɔːrʃəm/| n.|财团，联合体，合作团体|A consortium is an association of two or more individuals, companies, organizations or governments (or any combination of these entities) with the objective of participating in a common activity or pooling their resources for achieving a common goal.|Several banks formed a consortium to finance the project. 几家银行组成一个财团为该项目融资。|Synonyms: syndicate, alliance, coalition, partnership|Antonyms:  division, separation|noun

consortiums:/kənˈsɔːrtiəmz/ /kənˈsɔːrʃəmz/| n.|财团，联合体，合作团体的复数形式|The plural form of consortium, referring to multiple such groups.|Several international consortiums are bidding for the contract.  几个国际财团正在竞标这份合同。|Synonyms: syndicates, alliances, coalitions, partnerships|Antonyms: divisions, separations|noun
