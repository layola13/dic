in light of:/ɪn laɪt əv/| prep.|鉴于，考虑到|Considering or taking into account; in view of.  It means "because of" or "given that".|例句：In light of the recent developments, we have decided to postpone the meeting. 鉴于最近的事态发展，我们决定推迟会议。|近义词：considering, given, because of, in view of|反义词: regardless of, irrespective of|词性:介词(preposition)
