misanthropist:/ˌmɪsənˈθrəpɪst/ | n. | 厌世者，愤世嫉俗者 | A person who dislikes humankind and avoids human society. | He was a misanthropist who lived alone in the woods. 他是一个厌世者，独自住在树林里。 |  The old hermit was considered a misanthropist by the villagers. 村民们认为那个老隐士是个厌世者。 | 近义词: cynic, pessimist | 反义词: philanthropist, humanitarian | 词性: noun

词源: From Greek *misanthrōpos*  (hating mankind), from *misein* (to hate) + *anthrōpos* (man).
