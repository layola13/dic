manducus: /mænˈduːkəs/ | n. | 饕餮；贪吃的人；骗子 | A glutton; a gourmand; a cheat.  A person who eats greedily or has an insatiable appetite.  Can also refer to someone who deceives or cheats others. |  He was a manducus, always piling his plate high at the buffet. 他是个饕餮之徒，总是在自助餐上堆满盘子。|  The manducus devoured the entire roast chicken in one sitting.  这个贪吃鬼一口气吃掉了整只烤鸡。 | Synonyms: glutton, gourmand, gorger, devourer, swindler, cheat, fraudster | Antonyms: ascetic, abstainer | Noun
