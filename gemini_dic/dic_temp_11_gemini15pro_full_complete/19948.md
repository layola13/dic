biodiesels:/ˈbaɪoʊˌdiːzəlz/ | n. | 生物柴油 | A fuel made from vegetable oils, animal fats, or recycled greases. It can be used in diesel engines in place of petroleum diesel. | Biodiesels are becoming a popular alternative to fossil fuels. 生物柴油正成为化石燃料的流行替代品。|  Biodiesel is produced from renewable resources.  生物柴油由可再生资源生产。 | synonyms: biofuel, biodiesel fuel | antonyms: fossil fuel, petroleum diesel | noun
