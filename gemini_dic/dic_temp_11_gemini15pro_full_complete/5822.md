amateurish:/ˈæmətərɪʃ/ (US), /ˈæmətəːrɪʃ/ (UK)| adj.|业余的；不熟练的；外行的|Lacking professional skill or expertise; characteristic of an amateur.|Her amateurish attempts at painting were charming but not very good. 她业余的绘画尝试很有魅力，但不是很好。|The website looked amateurish and unprofessional.  这个网站看起来很业余，很不专业。|近义词：unskilled, unprofessional, inexpert, clumsy, crude|反义词：professional, expert, skilled, polished, sophisticated|词性:形容词

词源:
From French *amateur*, "lover of," from Latin *amator*, "lover."  The suffix "-ish" transforms the noun into an adjective indicating a resemblance to or characteristic of an amateur.
