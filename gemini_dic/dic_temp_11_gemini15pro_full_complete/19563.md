biga:/ˈbiːɡə/ | n. | 比加 | A type of pre-ferment used in Italian baking, consisting of flour, water, and a small amount of yeast, allowed to ferment for a long period (typically 12-48 hours) at cool temperatures. | The baker added the biga to the dough to enhance its flavor and texture.  面包师将比加加入面团中以增强其风味和质地。 |  preferment, starter, levain |  - | noun
