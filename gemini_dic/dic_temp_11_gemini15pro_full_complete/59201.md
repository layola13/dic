dysphagia:/dɪsˈfeɪdʒə/ (US), /dɪsˈfeɪʒɪə/ (UK)| n. |吞咽困难|Difficulty or discomfort in swallowing, as a symptom of disease.|The patient reported experiencing dysphagia after the stroke.  病人报告说中风后吞咽困难。

He suffers from dysphagia and has to eat pureed food. 他患有吞咽困难，不得不吃糊状食物。

|近义词：swallowing difficulty|反义词:|词性:noun
