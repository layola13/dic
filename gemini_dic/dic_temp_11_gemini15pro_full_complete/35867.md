cocksureness:/ˌkɒkˈʃʊənəs/ /ˌkɑːkˈʃʊrnəs/| n. |过于自信；自以为是|The state of being excessively confident, often to the point of arrogance.  It implies an unwarranted certainty and often a disregard for the opinions or feelings of others.|He approached the task with a cocksureness that annoyed his colleagues. 他以一种让同事恼火的过于自信的态度来处理这项任务。|近义词：arrogance, overconfidence, presumptuousness, conceit|反义词：humility, modesty, diffidence, self-doubt|词性:noun

Etymology:
From cock (n.) + sure (adj.) + -ness (suffix).  "Cock" in this context refers to a rooster, known for its bold and often aggressive demeanor.
