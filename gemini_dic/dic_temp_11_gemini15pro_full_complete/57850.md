dressings:/ˈdrɛsɪŋz/
n.
调味品；敷料；装饰品
A sauce or other mixture added to food to give it more flavor, or a piece of soft material placed over a wound to protect it.
The salad was topped with a creamy Italian dressing.  沙拉上面淋了一种奶油意大利酱。
The nurse changed the dressings on the burn.  护士更换了烧伤处的敷料。
近义词：sauces, condiments, bandages, coverings
反义词: (For food dressings) plain, undressed
