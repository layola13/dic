mahdiism:/mɑːˈdiːɪzəm/
n.|马赫迪主义|The belief in the coming of a Mahdi, a divinely guided leader who will usher in an era of justice and peace, particularly in Shia Islam. |The rise of mahdiism in Sudan led to a major upheaval.| 近义词:messianism | 反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider secularism or atheism depending on context) | 词性:noun

Etymology:
From Arabic "Mahdī" (مهدي) meaning "guided one" + English suffix "-ism" denoting a doctrine, belief, or practice.
