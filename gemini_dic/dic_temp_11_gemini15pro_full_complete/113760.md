macrophyllous:/ˌmækroʊˈfɪləs/| adj.|大叶的；巨叶的|Having large leaves.  Specifically, in the Raunkiaer system, qualifying as megaphyllous, having leaves exceeding an area of 25 square centimeters (excluding petiole and stipules).|The tropical rainforest is characterized by macrophyllous trees that form a dense canopy.  热带雨林的特点是大叶树木形成了茂密的树冠。|
The giant water lily is a macrophyllous aquatic plant. 巨大的睡莲是一种大叶水生植物。|Synonyms: megaphyllous, large-leaved|Antonyms: microphyllous, small-leaved|adjective
