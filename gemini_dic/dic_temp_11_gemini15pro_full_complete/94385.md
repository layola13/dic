not pleasing; displeasing or offensive.

近义词:displeasing, unpleasant, disagreeable, offensive

词源:
From Middle English *inplesinge, from Old French *inplesant (compare modern French déplaisant), from in- (“not”) + plesant (“pleasing”).  The English word is formed by analogy with other words like "unpleasing".
