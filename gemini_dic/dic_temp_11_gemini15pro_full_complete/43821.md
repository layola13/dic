creolism: /ˈkriːəˌlɪzəm/ /ˈkriːoʊˌlɪzəm/
n.
克里奥尔化，克里奥尔语现象
The process by which a pidgin becomes the native language of a speech community.  A creole language is a stable natural language developed from a mixture of different languages.  Also refers to the cultural mixing that produces a creole language and culture.
Creolism is evident in the unique blend of African rhythms and European melodies found in Jamaican music.  牙买加音乐中非洲节奏和欧洲旋律的独特融合体现了克里奥尔化。

Creolism has led to the development of many vibrant languages and cultures around the world.  克里奥尔化导致了世界各地许多充满活力的语言和文化的形成。

近义词: hybridization, syncretism, cultural blending
反义词: purity, homogeneity
词性: noun
