nondiverging:/ˌnɒndaɪˈvɜːdʒɪŋ/
adjective
非发散的，不发散的
Not diverging; remaining parallel or converging.  A nondiverging lens focuses light to a single point.
The nondiverging beams of the laser were crucial for the experiment. 激光束的非发散性对于实验至关重要。
The train tracks were nondiverging for miles. 火车轨道绵延数英里都未分岔。
synonyms: parallel, converging, collimated
antonyms: diverging, spreading, branching
