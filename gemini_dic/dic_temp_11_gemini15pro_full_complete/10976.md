arbitrationist:/ˌɑːrbɪˈtreɪʃənɪst/
n.
仲裁人，仲裁员
A person who settles a dispute or disagreement between two parties.
The arbitrationist listened to both sides of the story before making a decision. 仲裁员在做出决定之前听取了双方的陈述。
The arbitrationist's decision is binding on both parties. 仲裁员的决定对双方都有约束力。
Synonyms: arbitrator, arbiter, mediator, referee
Antonyms: litigant, disputant
noun
