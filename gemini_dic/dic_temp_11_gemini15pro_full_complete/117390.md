medicalization:/ˌmɛdɪkəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 医疗化；医学化 | The process by which non-medical problems become defined and treated as medical issues, usually in terms of illnesses or disorders. |  The medicalization of childbirth has led to an increase in interventions.  分娩的医疗化导致了干预的增加。 | 近义词: pathologization | 反义词: demedicalization | 词性: noun

medicalize:/ˈmɛdɪkəlaɪz/ | v. | 医疗化；医学化 | To make something into a medical issue. |  Critics argue that shyness is being medicalized and treated with drugs. 批评人士认为，害羞正在被医疗化，并用药物治疗。 | 近义词: pathologize | 反义词: demedicalize | 词性: verb
