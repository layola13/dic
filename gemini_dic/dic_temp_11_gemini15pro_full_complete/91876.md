hyperintellectualness:/ˌhaɪpərˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəlɪsnɪs/
n.
过度理智, 过度沉思, 过度学术化
Excessive intellectualism; an overemphasis on intellect and abstract thought.
His hyperintellectualness made it difficult for him to connect with people on an emotional level. 他过度理智，以致难以与人们在情感层面上建立联系。
近义词: intellectualism, cerebrality, overthinking
反义词: practicality, pragmatism, emotionalness
词性: 名词

Etymology:
hyper- (prefix): excessive, beyond normal
intellectual (adjective/noun): relating to the intellect; a person possessing a highly developed intellect.
-ness (suffix): forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
