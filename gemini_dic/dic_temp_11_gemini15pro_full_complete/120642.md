mis-practiced:/ˌmɪsˈpræktɪst/
adjective
处理不当的，失当的
incorrect or negligent professional behavior, especially by a doctor or lawyer.
The lawyer was accused of mis-practiced law.  这位律师被指控法律行为失当。
The mis-practiced medical treatment caused further complications.  处理不当的医疗造成了进一步的并发症。
Near Synonyms:  malpracticed, mishandled, bungled, botched
Antonyms:  competent, skillful, proper, correct
词性: adjective


mis-practice:/ˌmɪsˈpræktɪs/
verb
(尤指医生或律师) 行为失当，玩忽职守
to engage in incorrect or negligent professional behavior, especially by a doctor or lawyer.
He mis-practiced medicine and lost his license. 他玩忽职守，失去了行医执照。
The lawyer was accused of mis-practicing law.  这位律师被指控法律行为失当。
Near Synonyms:  malpractice, mishandle, bungle, botch
Antonyms:  perform correctly, handle properly
词性: verb


mis-practice:/ˌmɪsˈpræktɪs/
noun
失当行为，玩忽职守(尤指医生或律师)
incorrect or negligent professional behavior, especially by a doctor or lawyer.
Medical mis-practice can have serious consequences.  医疗失当可能造成严重后果。
The lawyer's mis-practice led to his disbarment.  该律师的失当行为导致他被取消律师资格。
Near Synonyms:  malpractice, mishandling, bungling, botching
Antonyms:  competence, skill, propriety, correctness
词性: noun


词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning "wrong" or "bad") + "practice" (from Old French "practique" and ultimately Latin "practica" meaning "practice").
