over-hurriedly:/ˌoʊvərˈhɜːriɪdli/| adv.|过于匆忙地，仓促地|Done too quickly and with too little thought or care.|She over-hurriedly signed the contract without reading it carefully. 她过于匆忙地签了合同，没有仔细阅读。|He over-hurriedly packed his suitcase and forgot his toothbrush. 他匆忙地收拾行李，忘记了带牙刷。|近义词：hastily, hurriedly, precipitately|反义词：carefully, deliberately, slowly|词性:副词
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "hurriedly" (adverb form of "hurried," from "hurry," ultimately from Proto-Germanic *hurrōną "to move quickly")
