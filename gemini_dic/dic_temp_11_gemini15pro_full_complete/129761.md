non-existential:/ˌnɒnɪɡˈzɪstənʃəl/| adj.|非存在的，虚无的|Relating to or characterized by nonexistence; not existing.  Referring to something that does not exist, or to the philosophical concept of nonexistence.|The philosopher pondered the non-existential nature of the self.|近义词：unreal, nonexistent, imaginary, fictitious, illusory|反义词：real, existent, actual, factual, concrete|词性: adjective
词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + existential (adjective from existence, from Latin exsistentia "a standing out, existence")
