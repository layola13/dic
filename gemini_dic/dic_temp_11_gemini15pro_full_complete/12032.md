articled:/ˈɑːrtɪk(ə)ld/
adjective
已签订培训合同的；受条款约束的
bound by articles of apprenticeship; subject to a contract.
The articled clerk was eager to learn the ropes of the legal profession.  这位签订培训合同的职员渴望学习法律行业的诀窍。
近义词: indentured, contracted
反义词: uncontracted, free
