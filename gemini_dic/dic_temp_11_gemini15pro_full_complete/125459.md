multiplication:/ˌmʌltɪplɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 乘法；增加；繁殖 | the process of multiplying numbers or quantities. |  Example: The multiplication of two by three gives six. 二乘以三得六。

Example: The rapid multiplication of rabbits is a problem for Australian farmers. 兔子的快速繁殖对澳大利亚农民来说是一个问题。 | synonyms: product, times, increase, propagation | antonyms: division, reduction, decrease | noun
