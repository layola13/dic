epistolize: /ɪˈpɪstəlaɪz/ | v. | 写信；以书信往来 | to write letters; to correspond by letters |  She epistollized with her childhood friend for many years. 她和儿时的朋友以书信往来多年。| He epistollized regularly with his family while he was abroad. 他在国外期间定期给家人写信。 |  correspond, write, communicate |  - | verb

Etymology:
From Late Latin *epistolāre, from Latin epistola (“letter”), from Ancient Greek ἐπιστολή (epistolḗ, “message, letter”).
