machine-readable:/məˈʃiːn ˈriːdəbl/| adj.|机器可读的|Able to be read and interpreted by a computer or other machine.  The data is stored in a machine-readable format.|例句：The information is encoded in a machine-readable format.  这些信息以机器可读的格式编码。|例句：Barcodes are machine-readable symbols that represent data. 条形码是代表数据的机器可读符号。|近义词：computer-readable, digitally readable, scannable|反义词:human-readable, illegible|词性:adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
machine (n.) + -readable (suffix)
From "machine" (from Old French "machine," ultimately from Latin "machina") + "-readable," a suffix indicating the ability to be read by something (in this case, a machine).
