amphistomous:/æmˈfɪstəməs/ | adj. | 两吸盘的；前后均有吸盘的 | Having a sucker at both the anterior and posterior ends. |  Example 1: Amphistomous flatworms are characterized by their suckers at both ends. 两吸盘扁形虫的特点是两端都有吸盘。

Example 2:  The amphistomous leech attached itself firmly to the host.  这种两吸盘的蚂蟥牢牢地附着在宿主身上。 | Near Synonyms: double-suckered | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps single-suckered if context allows) | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *amphi-* "both, on both sides" + *stoma* "mouth, opening"
