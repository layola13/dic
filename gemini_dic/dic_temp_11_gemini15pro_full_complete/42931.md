covenant: /ˈkʌvənənt/ (US), /ˈkʌvənənt/ (UK)| n. vt.|契约，盟约；约定|A formal agreement or promise between two or more parties, especially one having religious significance.  A written agreement or promise usually under seal between two or more parties especially for the performance of some action.|The two sides signed a covenant to reduce pollution levels.|近义词：agreement, contract, pact, treaty, compact|反义词：disagreement, breach|词性: noun, verb

covenant: /ˈkʌvənənt/ (US), /ˈkʌvənənt/ (UK)| vt.|立约，订立盟约|To agree by lease, deed, or other legal contract.|They covenanted to build a new school.|近义词：pledge, promise, vow, swear|反义词：renege, break|词性: verb
