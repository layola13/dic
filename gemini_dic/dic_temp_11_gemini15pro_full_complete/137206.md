nonsupplications:/ˌnɒnsʌplɪˈkeɪʃənz/
n.
缺乏恳求；没有祈求
Absence or lack of supplications; the state of not asking earnestly or humbly.
Their nonsupplication for mercy surprised the judge. 他们没有恳求怜悯，这让法官感到惊讶。
Near Antonyms: entreaties, pleas, petitions
Antonyms: supplications
