dissimuler:/dɪˈsɪmjuleɪt/ /dɪˈsɪmjəleɪt/ | v. | 掩饰，隐藏，假装 | To conceal or disguise one's true feelings or intentions. | He dissimulated his anger with a forced smile. 他强颜欢笑以掩饰内心的愤怒。

She dissimulated her fear by pretending to be brave. 她假装勇敢来掩饰她的恐惧。

近义词: conceal, disguise, mask, hide, feign | 反义词: reveal, expose, divulge, show, betray | 词性: verb
