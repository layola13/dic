belittle:/bɪˈlɪt(ə)l/
v.
贬低，轻视
to make someone or something seem unimportant
例句：He belittled her achievements. 他贬低了她的成就。

例句：The critic belittled the author's work.  评论家贬低了作者的作品。

近义词：disparage, denigrate, minimize, underestimate, underrate
反义词：praise, extol, compliment, magnify, overestimate
词性：动词
