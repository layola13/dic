landlubberliness:/ˌlændˈlʌbərlɪnɪs/
|n.|
|不谙航海, 陆地习性|
|The state or quality of being a landlubber; awkwardness and unfamiliarity with seafaring matters.|
|His landlubberliness was evident as he stumbled around the deck of the ship.|他那笨手笨脚的样子表明他是个十足的旱鸭子。
|
|clumsiness, awkwardness, inexperience at sea|
|seafarer, sailor, mariner|
|noun|
