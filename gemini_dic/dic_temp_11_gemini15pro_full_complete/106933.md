lachrymatory:/ˈlækrɪməˌtɔːri/ (US), /ləˈkrɪmət(ə)ri/ (UK)| adj. n.|催泪的；催泪剂|A lachrymatory substance or agent is something that irritates the eyes and causes tears.  As a noun, it refers to a tear gas or a substance that causes tears.|例句：The police used lachrymatory gas to disperse the crowd. 警方使用催泪瓦斯驱散人群。

例句：The onion's fumes were highly lachrymatory. 洋葱的烟雾非常催人泪下。

近义词：tear-inducing, tear-jerking (for adj.) tear gas, irritant (for n.)|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun|词源: From Latin *lacrimātōrius, from lacrima ("tear")|
