overprotection:/ˌoʊvərprəˈtɛkʃən/| n. |过度保护|The act of protecting someone excessively, often to the point of hindering their independence and development.|Her overprotection of her son prevented him from learning to cope with life's challenges. 她对她儿子的过度保护使他无法学会应对生活中的挑战。|Overparenting, cosseting|Underprotection, neglect|noun

overprotect:/ˌoʊvərprəˈtɛkt/| vt. |过度保护|To protect someone excessively, often to the point of hindering their independence and development.|She overprotected her children, never letting them out of her sight. 她过度保护她的孩子们，从不让他们离开她的视线。|Overparent, cosset|Neglect, underprotect|verb
