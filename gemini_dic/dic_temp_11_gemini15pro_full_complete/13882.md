auguries: /ˈɔːɡjʊriz/ | n. | 预兆，征兆 |  Signs or omens, especially those believed to foretell the future.  Derived from Latin "augurium," meaning "divination from birds." | The auguries for the new business venture were not good.  新企业的预兆并不好。| synonyms: omens, portents, signs, forebodings | antonyms:  guarantees, certainties | noun
