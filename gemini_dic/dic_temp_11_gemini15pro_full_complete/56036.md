doat:/doʊt/| vi.| 溺爱；宠爱；迷恋|To be extremely and uncritically fond of.|She doats on her grandchildren. 她非常溺爱她的孙子孙女们。|近义词：adore, cherish, love, idolize|反义词：despise, loathe, hate|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *doten*, from Old English *dōtian* (“to be foolish, be silly, rave, be insane”), of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to Old Norse *deyja* (“to die”). The sense shifted from "to be mentally impaired" to "to be foolishly in love."
