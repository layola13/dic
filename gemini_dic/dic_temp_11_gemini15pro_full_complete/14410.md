autotoxic:/ˌɔːtəʊˈtɒksɪk/ | adj. | 自毒的，自体中毒的 | Poisonous to the organism producing it.  A substance produced by a plant or microorganism that inhibits its own growth. | The autotoxic effects of walnut trees are well known to gardeners.  核桃树的自毒效应对园丁来说是众所周知的。 | Example:  The buildup of autotoxic compounds in the soil can stunt the growth of subsequent crops. 土壤中自毒化合物的积累会阻碍后续作物的生长。| Synonyms: self-poisoning, self-inhibiting | Antonyms:  n/a (no direct opposite) | Word Type: Adjective
