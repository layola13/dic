demonish:/ˈdɛmənɪʃ/ | v. | 谴责，痛斥 | To criticize or denounce severely; to admonish strongly. |  He demonished his son for his disrespectful behavior. 他痛斥他的儿子不尊重人的行为。

|例句：The preacher demonished the congregation for their lack of faith. 牧师谴责会众缺乏信仰。

|例句：She demonished her colleagues for their apathy towards the project. 她严厉批评了同事对这个项目漠不关心的态度。


|近义词：rebuke, reprimand, censure, condemn, denounce, castigate, lambaste, excoriate |反义词：praise, commend, laud, extol | 词性：动词
