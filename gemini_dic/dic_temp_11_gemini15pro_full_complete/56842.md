nighthawk:/ˈnaɪtˌhɔːk/ (US), /ˈnaɪtˌhɔːk/ (UK)| n.|夜鹰，夜游者|A nocturnal bird of the Americas, related to the whip-poor-will, characterized by long pointed wings, a gaping mouth, and soft plumage.|例句：The nighthawk swooped down and caught an insect in mid-air. 夜鹰俯冲下来，在半空中抓住了一只昆虫。

例句：Nighthawks are known for their acrobatic flight. 夜鹰以其杂技般的飞行而闻名。|近义词：nightjar, goatsucker|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific bird. Could use "diurnal bird" for a bird active during the day) |词性:noun


nighthawk:/ˈnaɪtˌhɔːk/ (US), /ˈnaɪtˌhɔːk/ (UK)| n.|夜游者|A person who stays up late at night.|例句：He's a real nighthawk, often staying up until dawn. 他是一个真正的夜猫子，经常熬夜到天亮。

例句：The city streets were still bustling with nighthawks even after midnight. 即使过了午夜，城市的街道上仍然挤满了夜游者。|近义词：night owl, reveller|反义词: early bird|词性:noun
