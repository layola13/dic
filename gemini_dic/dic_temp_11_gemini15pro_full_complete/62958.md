ennoble:/ɪˈnoʊbl/ /ɛnˈnəʊb(ə)l/| vt.|使高贵，使崇高|To give someone a noble rank or title. To make something or someone seem important or worthy of respect.|He felt ennobled by their praise. 他因他们的赞扬而感到高贵。|近义词：elevate, exalt, dignify, uplift|反义词：debase, degrade, demean|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *ennoblir*, from en- + noble (“noble”). Equivalent to en- + noble.
