contrafissure:/ˌkɒntrəˈfɪʃə(r)/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈfɪʃər/
noun
对裂，反向裂缝
A crack or fissure in the earth's surface, especially one in the opposite direction from the main fissure.
Magma sometimes escapes through contrafissures formed during volcanic eruptions.  岩浆有时会通过火山喷发期间形成的对裂逸出。
Contrafissures can be observed in dried mud or clay. 在干泥或粘土中可以观察到对裂。
Synonyms: counterfissure
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but consider) main fissure, primary fissure
词性: 名词
