atonalistic:/ˌeɪtɒˈnælɪstɪk/ |adj.|无调性的|Relating to or characteristic of atonality; music that deliberately avoids traditional harmonies and key signatures. | The composer's later works became increasingly atonalistic, challenging the audience's expectations of melody and harmony.  作曲家后期的作品越来越无调性，挑战了观众对旋律和和声的期望。| The atonalistic piece was jarring to some listeners, while others found it intriguing. 这首无调性的作品让一些听众感到刺耳，而另一些人则觉得它很有趣。|  Synonyms: atonal, non-tonal, pantonal| Antonyms: tonal, harmonic |adjective
