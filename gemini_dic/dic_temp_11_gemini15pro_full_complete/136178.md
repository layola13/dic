nonreinstatements:/ˌnɒnriːɪnˈsteɪtmənts/ | n. | 未恢复; 不恢复原状; |The nonreinstatements of the suspended licenses caused further complications. 暂停的许可证未恢复导致了进一步的复杂化。|  The policy outlines the reasons for nonreinstatements of membership.  该政策概述了不恢复会员资格的原因。|近义词:non-restoration,  cancellation (in some contexts)|反义词:reinstatements, restorations|词性:名词

词源:
"non-" (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
"reinstatement": from "re-" (prefix) meaning "again" + "in-" (prefix) indicating "into" + "statement" (noun) from Old French "estat" meaning "state" or "condition".  Thus, reinstatement means putting something back into its original state or condition.
