lankiness:/ˈlæŋkɪnɪs/
noun
瘦长；细长
The state or quality of being lanky.  Having a long or tall and thin body, often appearing ungainly.
He was known for his lankiness and awkward gait. 他以身材瘦长和步态笨拙而闻名。
近义词：tallness, slenderness, gangliness
反义词：stockiness, stoutness
词性:n.


lank:/læŋk/
adjective
瘦长的；细长的
Tall and thin, often appearing ungainly.
The lanky teenager towered over his classmates.  那个瘦长的少年比他的同学们高出一头。
近义词：tall, slender, gangly
反义词：stocky, stout
词性:adj.
