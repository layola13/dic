overnervous:/ˌoʊvərˈnɜːrvəs/| adj.|过度紧张的，过分焦虑的|Excessively nervous; extremely anxious.  From "over-" (meaning excessively) + "nervous" (meaning easily agitated or apprehensive).|She was overnervous about her driving test and made several mistakes. 她对驾驶考试过度紧张，犯了好几个错误。|He was so overnervous before the presentation that he could barely speak.  他在演讲前非常紧张，几乎说不出话来。|anxious, apprehensive, agitated, edgy, jittery|calm, composed, relaxed, serene, tranquil|adjective
