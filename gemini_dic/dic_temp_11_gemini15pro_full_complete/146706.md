overhelpfulnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈhɛlpfʊlnəsɪz/
|n.| 过度热心，过分帮忙|The state of being excessively helpful, often to the point of being intrusive or interfering.|Her overhelpfulnesses can be a bit smothering sometimes. 她过分的热心有时让人有点窒息。|Synonyms: officiousness, intrusiveness, meddlesomeness|Antonyms: neglect, indifference, apathy|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + helpfulness + -es (plural suffix). "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much". "Helpfulness" is derived from "helpful," which comes from "help" (Old English helpan) + "-ful" (Old English -full).  The "-es" suffix marks the plural form of the noun.
