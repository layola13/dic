liber:/ˈliːbər/ | adj. |自由的，不受约束的|Free from restraint; not restricted. |例句：The bird flew from its cage, finally liber.  这只鸟从笼子里飞了出来，终于自由了。|近义词：free, independent, unrestrained|反义词：restricted, confined, captive|词性:形容词


liber:/ˈliːbər/ | v. |解放，释放|To set free, as from imprisonment or bondage.|例句：The soldiers liberated the prisoners of war. 士兵们解放了战俘。|近义词：free, release, emancipate|反义词：imprison, confine, enslave|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin līber ("free"), related to lībra ("balance, scales"). The connection might be because free men had the right to plead their own cases and weigh evidence in court.
