afflict:/əˈflɪkt/| v. |折磨，使痛苦|To cause pain or suffering to; to trouble greatly.|例句：The disease afflicted him for years.  这种疾病折磨了他好多年。|例句：She was afflicted by grief after her husband's death. 她丈夫去世后，她悲痛欲绝。|近义词：trouble, distress, torment, plague|反义词：comfort, soothe, relieve|词性：动词

afflicted:/əˈflɪktɪd/| adj. |受苦的，受折磨的|Suffering from something that causes pain or distress.|例句：The afflicted areas received aid from the government. 受灾地区得到了政府的援助。|例句：He visited the afflicted family to offer his condolences. 他去探望了受难家庭，表示慰问。|近义词：suffering, distressed, troubled|反义词：healthy, well, comfortable|词性：形容词

词源: From Latin *afflictus*, past participle of *affligere* "to dash to the ground, strike down, weaken," from *ad-* "to" + *fligere* "to strike."
