exagitation:/ɛkˌsædʒɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 激动，兴奋，不安 |  A state of excessive mental or emotional arousal. |  His exagitation was evident in his rapid speech and pacing. 他的激动表现在他说话飞快和来回踱步。| 近义词：agitation, excitement, nervousness, anxiety | 反义词：calm, composure, tranquility | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *exagitātiō, from Latin exagītāre (“to stir up, agitate violently”), from ex- (“out of, from”) + agītāre (“to drive, move, stir up”).
