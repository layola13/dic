institutionalizations:/ˌɪnstɪˌtjuːʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/
|n.|制度化；机构化|The process of establishing something as a convention or norm in an organization or culture.  The act of institutionalizing someone or something.|
|The institutionalization of racism in the legal system had devastating consequences.|种族主义在法律体系中的制度化造成了毁灭性的后果。|
|The institutionalization of new technologies can be a slow process.|新技术的制度化可能是一个缓慢的过程。|
|synonyms: establishment, formalization, systematization, codification|
|antonyms: informalization, desystematization, dismantling|
|noun|
