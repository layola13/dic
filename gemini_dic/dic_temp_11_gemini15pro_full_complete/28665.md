carinatae:/kærɪˈneɪtiː/
n.
龙骨突类（鸟类）
a large diverse group of birds characterized by a keeled sternum for flight muscle attachment.  These birds have a prominent breastbone (sternum) with a ridge or keel down the center. This keel provides an anchor point for the powerful flight muscles.
例句：The carinatae include most modern birds.  龙骨突类包括大多数现代鸟类。

例句：Penguins, although flightless, are classified within the carinatae. 尽管企鹅不会飞，但它们被归类为龙骨突类。
近义词：keeled birds, flying birds
反义词:ratite (平胸类)
词性:noun
