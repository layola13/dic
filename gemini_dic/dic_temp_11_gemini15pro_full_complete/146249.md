overextraction:/ˌəʊvərɪkˈstrækʃən/ | n. |过度提取，过度萃取|The overextraction of groundwater has led to land subsidence. 地下水的过度提取导致了地面沉降。|Overextraction of coffee can result in a bitter taste. 咖啡过度萃取会导致苦味。|近义词：overexploitation, overuse|反义词: underextraction|noun

词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessive") + extraction (noun from Latin "extractio," meaning "a drawing out")  "Over-"源自古英语"ofer," 表示"过度"；"extraction"源自拉丁语"extractio," 意为"取出，抽出"。
