outquarters: /ˈaʊtˌkwɔːrtərz/
n.
外部住所，外部营房，外部办公场所
Lodging or accommodations away from one's usual residence or military post, often temporary and less comfortable.
The construction workers lived in outquarters near the building site.  建筑工人们住在工地附近的外部住所。
The soldiers were assigned to outquarters during the maneuvers.  在演习期间，士兵们被分配到外部营房。
synonyms: barracks, billet, lodgings, accommodations
antonyms: home, residence, headquarters
