multiaxially:/ˌmʌltiˈæksiəli/
adjective
多轴地
Acting along or around more than one axis.  Applying stresses or strains in more than one direction.
The composite material was tested multiaxially to determine its strength under complex loads.  这种复合材料经过多轴测试，以确定其在复杂载荷下的强度。
The robotic arm can move multiaxially, allowing it to reach any point within its range.  机械臂可以多轴移动，使其能够到达其范围内的任何一点。
Synonym:  multidirectional
Antonym: uniaxial, biaxial
词性: adjective
