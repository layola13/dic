acromion: /əˈkroʊmiən/ | n. | 肩峰 | The bony process on the scapula that forms the highest point of the shoulder. |  The acromion is easily palpable.  肩峰很容易触及。

The doctor examined the patient's acromion for signs of injury. 医生检查了病人的肩峰是否有受伤的迹象。 | 近义词: shoulder tip | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From New Latin *acrōmīon, from Ancient Greek  ἀκρώμιον (akrṓmion, “shoulder-point”), from  ἄκρος (ákros, “highest, outermost”) + ὦμος (ṓmos, “shoulder”).
