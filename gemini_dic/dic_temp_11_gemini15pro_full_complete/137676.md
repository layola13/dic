nonuniting:/ˌnɒnjuːˈnaɪtɪŋ/
adjective
不愈合的，不联合的
Not uniting; not tending to unite or heal.
The doctor diagnosed a nonuniting fracture in his arm.  医生诊断他的手臂为不愈合性骨折。
The nonuniting factions within the party made it difficult to reach a consensus. 党内不联合的派系使得很难达成共识。
近义词: unhealing, non-healing, disunited
反义词: uniting, healing, joining
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From non- + uniting, present participle of unite.  "Unite" comes from the Old French "unir," and ultimately from the Latin "unus," meaning "one."
