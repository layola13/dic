crystallizabilities:/ˌkrɪstələˈzaɪzəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |结晶性，结晶能力|The crystallizabilities of different substances vary widely.  不同物质的结晶能力差异很大。|例句：The researchers studied the crystallizabilities of various sugar solutions. 研究人员研究了各种糖溶液的结晶能力。

crystallizabilities:/ˌkrɪstələˈzaɪzəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |可结晶性|Certain polymers exhibit high crystallizabilities.  某些聚合物表现出高结晶性。|例句：The crystallizabilities of these new materials are yet to be determined.  这些新材料的可结晶性还有待确定。|近义词：crystallization tendencies, crystallization properties|反义词: amorphousness, non-crystallizability|词性: noun (plural)
