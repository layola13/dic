disdainful:/dɪsˈdeɪnfʊl/
adjective
轻蔑的，鄙夷的
Showing contempt or lack of respect.
She cast a disdainful glance at the cheap jewelry. 她轻蔑地瞥了一眼那些廉价珠宝。
近义词：contemptuous, scornful, haughty
反义词：respectful, admiring, appreciative
词性: adjective

disdain:/dɪsˈdeɪn/
noun, verb
轻蔑，鄙视；鄙视，蔑视
(n.) a feeling of contempt or scorn
(v.) to treat with contempt or scorn
He disdained to answer their questions. 他不屑于回答他们的问题。
近义词：(n.) contempt, scorn; (v.) despise, scorn
反义词：(n.) respect, admiration; (v.) admire, respect
词性: noun, verb
