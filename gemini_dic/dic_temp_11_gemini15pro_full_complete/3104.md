aerosol:/ˈɛərəsɒl/ (US) /ˈeərəsɔl/ (UK)| n.|气溶胶，喷雾剂|A colloid of fine solid particles or liquid droplets, in air or another gas.  A substance enclosed under pressure and able to be released as a fine spray, typically by means of a propellant gas.|例句：The aerosol can exploded in the heat.  高温下喷雾罐爆炸了。|例句：Scientists are studying the effects of aerosols on the climate. 科学家正在研究气溶胶对气候的影响。|近义词：spray, mist, fog|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "solid" or "liquid" depending on context)|词性:noun
