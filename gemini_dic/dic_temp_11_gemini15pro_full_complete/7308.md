anguishing:/ˈæŋɡwɪʃɪŋ/| adj.|痛苦的，令人极度痛苦的|Causing extreme mental or physical suffering.|She let out an anguishing cry. 她发出一声痛苦的哭喊。|Synonyms: agonizing, excruciating, tormenting, harrowing|Antonyms: comforting, soothing, pleasant, enjoyable|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *anguissier*, from Latin angustia ("narrowness, tightness, distress"), from angustus ("narrow").
