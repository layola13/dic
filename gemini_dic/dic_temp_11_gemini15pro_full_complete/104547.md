kamichi:/kɑːˈmiːtʃiː/
noun
卡米奇鸟, 凤头鹃隼
A bird of prey, Caracara plancus, found in South America, Central America, and parts of southern North America. It is a member of the falcon family.
The kamichi is a large bird with a distinctive crest and long legs. It feeds mainly on carrion but also hunts small animals. 
卡米奇是一种大型鸟类，具有独特的羽冠和长腿。它主要以腐肉为食，但也捕猎小动物。
Synonym: crested caracara, southern caracara
