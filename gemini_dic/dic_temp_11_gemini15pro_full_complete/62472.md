endoskeleton:/ˈɛndoʊˌskɛlɪtn/ | n. | 内骨骼 | An internal skeleton, such as the bony or cartilaginous skeleton of vertebrates. |  Humans have an endoskeleton made of bone. 人类的内骨骼是由骨头构成的。

The shark's endoskeleton is made of cartilage. 鲨鱼的内骨骼是由软骨构成的。 | 近义词: internal skeleton | 反义词: exoskeleton | 词性: noun
