diabolify: /diːˈæbəlaɪfaɪ/ /daɪˈæbəlaɪfaɪ/
verb
使恶魔化；将…描绘成魔鬼；诽谤；中伤
to represent something as evil or wicked
例句：
The media diabolified the candidate, portraying him as a dangerous extremist.
媒体将这位候选人妖魔化，把他描绘成一个危险的极端分子。

Their opponents constantly diabolize them in the press.
他们的对手不断在媒体上诋毁他们。

近义词：vilify, demonize, denigrate, slander, calumniate
反义词：deify, praise, extol, lionize
词性：v.
