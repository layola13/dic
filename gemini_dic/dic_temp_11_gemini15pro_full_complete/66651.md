exited:/ɪkˈsaɪtɪd/
adj.
兴奋的，激动的
Feeling or showing happiness and enthusiasm.
例句：The children were exited about going to the zoo. 孩子们对去动物园感到很兴奋。

例句：She was exited to hear that she had won the lottery. 她听到自己中了彩票的消息很兴奋。

近义词：thrilled, enthusiastic, exhilarated
反义词：bored, apathetic, unenthusiastic
词性:形容词

exited:/ˈɛɡzɪtɪd/
v.
退出，离开（exit的过去式和过去分词）
To go out of or leave a place.
例句：They exited the building quickly after the alarm sounded. 警报响起后，他们迅速离开了大楼。

例句：He exited the stage to thunderous applause. 他在雷鸣般的掌声中离开了舞台。

近义词：left, departed, withdrew
反义词：entered, joined, arrived
词性:动词
