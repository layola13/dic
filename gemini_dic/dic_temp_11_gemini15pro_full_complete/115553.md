maps:/mæps/ | n. |地图; 图; 天体图;  |A diagrammatic representation of an area of land or sea showing physical features, cities, roads, etc. |She unfolded the map and traced the route with her finger. 她打开地图，用手指沿着路线描绘。| synonyms: charts, diagrams, plans | antonyms: globe, sphere | noun

maps:/mæps/ | v. (maps, mapping, mapped) |绘制…的地图; 详细计划; 映射;  |To represent an area of land or sea on a map.  |The expedition mapped the entire coastline. 探险队绘制了整个海岸线的地图。| synonyms: chart, plot, delineate | antonyms: lose, misplace | verb
