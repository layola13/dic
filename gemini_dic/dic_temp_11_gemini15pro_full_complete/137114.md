nonsubmissions:/ˌnɑːnsʌbˈmɪʃənz/
n.|未提交，未递交（通常指文件、作业等）|The professor was frustrated by the high number of nonsubmissions for the final project. 教授对期末项目的大量未提交感到沮丧。|
例句：The nonsubmissions will result in a failing grade. 未提交将导致不及格。
例句：The system automatically tracks nonsubmissions and sends reminders to students. 系统会自动跟踪未提交的内容，并向学生发送提醒。
近义词：non-compliance, default, omission, failure to submit
反义词：submissions, compliance
词性：名词

词源：non- (prefix meaning "not") + submission (noun meaning "the act of submitting something")
