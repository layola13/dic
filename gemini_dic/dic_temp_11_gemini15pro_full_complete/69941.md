fesse's:/ˈfɛsɪz/
n.
腰带, 横带 (纹章学)
A fesse is a wide horizontal band across the centre of an escutcheon (shield).  It represents a military belt or girdle of honor.
The knight's shield bore a fesse argent on a field gules.  骑士的盾牌上有一条银色的横带，底色是红色的。
近义词:band, stripe
反义词:(No direct antonym in heraldry; perhaps "field" as the background)
词性:noun
