morningly:/ˈmɔːrnɪŋli/
adjective
早晨地，每天早晨地
Happening or done in the mornings; characteristic of mornings.  This word is archaic and rarely used in modern English.
She morningly rose with the sun. 她每天早晨都随着太阳升起而起床。
The morningly dew glistened on the grass. 早晨的露珠在草地上闪闪发光。
近义词: early, matutinal
反义词: nightly, evening
