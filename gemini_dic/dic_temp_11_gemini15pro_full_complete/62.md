aaka: /ˈɑːkɑː/ | n. | 赤鹿 (夏威夷语) | A Hawaiian word for a species of deer. |  Example: The aaka grazed peacefully in the meadow. 赤鹿在草地上平静地吃草。| Example:  Ancient Hawaiians hunted aaka for food and clothing. 古代夏威夷人猎杀赤鹿以获取食物和衣物。 | Synonym: deer, Axis deer | Antonym:  N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Hawaiian *ʻaʻaka*. Related to *ʻaka*, meaning "shadow, reflection".
