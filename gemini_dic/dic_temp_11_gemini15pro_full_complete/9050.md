antimedically:/ˌæntiˈmɛdɪkli/
| adv. |违反医学地；不利于健康地|Acting in a way that is contrary to medical advice or good health practices. | He antimedically refused to take the prescribed antibiotics. 他违反医嘱拒绝服用抗生素。|Synonyms: imprudently, unwisely, rashly | Antonyms: prudently, wisely, judiciously |词性: 副词

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek anti-)
medical (adjective): relating to medicine (from Latin medicus, meaning "physician")
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs
