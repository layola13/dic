ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *baðere (one who bathes), from baðian (to bathe) + -ere (agent suffix).  The verb "bathe" ultimately comes from Proto-Germanic *baþōną (to bathe), from Proto-Indo-European *bhē- (to warm).
