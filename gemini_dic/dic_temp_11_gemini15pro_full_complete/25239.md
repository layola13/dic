buff:/bʌf/| n. adj. v.|爱好者；浅黄色；抛光；浅黄色的；强壮的|A buff is someone who is very interested in a particular subject or activity. As an adjective, it describes something pale yellow-brown in colour. As a verb, it means to polish something.| He's a real film buff. 他是个真正的电影迷。|近义词:fan, enthusiast, polish, shine, strong, muscular|反义词:detractor, opponent, dull, weak|词性:noun, adjective, verb
