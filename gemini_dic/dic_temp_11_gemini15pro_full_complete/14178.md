auto-foci:/ˌɔːtəʊˈfəʊsaɪ/ /ˌɑːtəʊˈfoʊsaɪ/
Noun
自动对焦;自动对焦装置
The plural form of auto-focus.  It refers to the systems on multiple cameras or lenses that automatically adjust the focus.
The new cameras boast lightning-fast auto-foci, ensuring crisp images every time. 新相机拥有闪电般的自动对焦功能，确保每次都能拍摄清晰的图像。
The auto-foci on these lenses are incredibly precise. 这些镜头的自动对焦非常精确。
Synonyms: auto-focus systems, automatic focusing mechanisms
Antonyms: manual focus
词性: 名词(Plural)
