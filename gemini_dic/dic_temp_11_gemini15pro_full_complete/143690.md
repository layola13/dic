outleaped:/ˌaʊtˈliːpt/ | v. | 超越，跳得比...远 | Surpassed, jumped farther than. | The athlete outleaped his competitors and won the gold medal.  这位运动员超越了他的竞争对手，赢得了金牌。 |  The young deer outleaped the fence. 小鹿跳过了篱笆。 | 近义词：surpassed, exceeded, outjumped | 反义词: lagged behind, trailed | 词性: 动词

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "beyond" or "exceeding") + "leap" (verb meaning "to jump")
