orbitals:/ˈɔːrbɪtəlz/| n.|轨道|Regions of space around the nucleus of an atom where electrons are likely to be found.  They are characterized by their size, shape, and energy level.|Electrons occupy different orbitals within an atom. 电子占据原子内的不同轨道。|The shapes of the orbitals influence the molecule's geometry. 轨道的形状影响分子的几何形状。|shells, subshells|nucleus|noun
