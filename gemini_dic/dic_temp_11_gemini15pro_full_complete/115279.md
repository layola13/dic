manks:/mæŋks/| adj. n.|曼克斯的；曼克斯人；曼克斯猫；无尾猫|Relating to the Isle of Man, its people, or their language. Also, a breed of tailless cat.|例句：He speaks Manx Gaelic. 他说曼岛盖尔语。|例句：My grandmother had a Manx cat. 我祖母有一只曼克斯猫。|近义词：Manxman (n.), Manxwoman (n.), tailless (adj.) (for the cat)|反义词: (No direct antonym. Could use "tailed" for the cat breed.)|词性: adjective, noun


Etymology:
From Manx Gaelic *Manninach, from Mannin "Isle of Man".
