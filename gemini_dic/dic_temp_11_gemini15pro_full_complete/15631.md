bairnish:/ˈbɛrnɪʃ/| adj.|孩子气的，幼稚的|Characteristic of or resembling a child; childish.  Relating to or connected with children. |例句：His bairnish behaviour was embarrassing at the formal dinner. 他孩子气的行为在正式晚宴上令人尴尬。

例句：She still had a bairnish delight in playing with dolls. 她仍然保持着孩子般的玩娃娃的乐趣。|近义词：childish, infantile, juvenile, puerile|反义词：mature, adult, grown-up|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Scots *bairn* (child) + *-ish* (suffix indicating resemblance or characteristic). The word *bairn* itself comes from Old Norse *barn* (child).
