ironer:/ˈaɪərnər/| n.|熨斗；烫衣机；熨衣工人|A person or device that irons clothes or other fabrics. |例句：She is a skilled ironer. 她是一位熟练的熨衣工人。

例句：The ironer makes my shirts look crisp.  烫衣机让我的衬衫看起来很挺括。

近义词：presser, mangle (for machine), laundry worker (for person)|反义词: (None, as the opposite of a tool or person performing a task doesn't exist in a single word) |词性:noun
