figured:/ˈfɪɡjərd/| adj. v.|计算出的; 预料到的; 认为; 料想; (使)呈…形状;  装饰; |figured that it would rain. 料想会下雨。|近义词：calculated, anticipated, assumed, believed, shaped, decorated, adorned|反义词:unanticipated, unexpected, plain, undecorated|词性:形容词/动词

词源: From Middle English figuren, from Old French figurer, from Latin figurare ("to form, shape, represent"), from figura ("a shape, form, figure").
