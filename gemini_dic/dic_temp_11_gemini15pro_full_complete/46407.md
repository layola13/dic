daffodil: /ˈdæfədɪl/ (US), /ˈdæfədɪl/ (UK) | n. | 水仙花；黄水仙 | A bulbous plant that typically bears bright yellow flowers with a long trumpet-shaped center (corona). |  The daffodils are blooming in the garden. 水仙花在花园里盛开着。| synonym: narcissus, jonquil | antonym: N/A (Flowers don't typically have direct antonyms) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From Middle English *affodill*, alteration (influenced by *affodylle*) of asphodel, from Old French *asphodele*, from Latin *asphodelus*, from Greek *ἀσφόδελος* (*asphódelos*).
