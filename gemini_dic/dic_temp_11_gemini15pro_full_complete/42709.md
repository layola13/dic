countertrends:/ˈkaʊntərˌtrɛndz/
noun
逆势；反向趋势
A trend that goes against the prevailing trend.
Many investors are looking for countertrends in the market. 许多投资者正在寻找市场上的逆势。
synonyms: reverse trend, opposing trend, contrary trend
antonyms: trend, mainstream, prevailing trend
n.
