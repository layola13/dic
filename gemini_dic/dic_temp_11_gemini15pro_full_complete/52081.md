dialyze:/ˈdaɪəlaɪz/ /ˈdaɪəˌlaɪz/ | v. | 透析，渗析 | To separate substances from a liquid by dialysis.  The process of removing waste products and excess fluid from the blood when the kidneys stop working properly. | The patient's blood needs to be dialyzed.  这个病人的血液需要透析。 | synonyms: filter, purify, cleanse | antonyms: contaminate, pollute | verb

dialysis:/daɪˈælɪsɪs/ /daɪˈɑːləsɪs/ | n. | 透析 | The separation of substances in solution by means of their unequal diffusion through a semipermeable membrane.  A procedure to remove waste products from the blood of patients whose kidneys no longer function. | The patient undergoes dialysis three times a week.  这个病人每周进行三次透析。 | synonyms: filtration, purification | antonyms: contamination | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From dia- (through) + lysis (loosening, dissolution). Ultimately from Greek "dialuein" (to dissolve).
