gramophone: /ˈɡræməfəʊn/ (UK), /ˈɡræməfoʊn/ (US) | n. | 留声机 | A machine that reproduces sound by placing a needle on a revolving grooved disc. |  My grandfather had an old gramophone that he treasured. 我祖父有一台他珍爱的旧留声机。

The gramophone played a scratchy rendition of a popular song. 留声机播放了一首流行歌曲，声音沙沙作响。 | synonym: record player, phonograph | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps digital audio player) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *γράμμα (gramma) meaning "letter, something written" + *φωνή (phōnē) meaning "voice, sound".
