matey:/ˈmeɪti/| n.|伙伴，朋友，哥们儿|A friendly term of address, often used by sailors or pirates.|例句：Ahoy there, matey!  你好啊，伙计！

例句：Come on, matey, let's go for a drink! 来吧，哥们儿，我们去喝一杯！

近义词：friend, pal, buddy, chum, comrade|反义词:enemy, foe|词性:名词

词源:  Matey is a shortened form of "mate," which itself comes from the Middle Low German word "māte," meaning "companion" or "food companion."  Its association with sailors and pirates likely stems from the close-knit communities that developed on ships.
