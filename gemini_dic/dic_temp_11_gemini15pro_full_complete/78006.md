gentlemanship: /ˈdʒɛntlmənʃɪp/
n.
绅士风度，君子风范
The qualities associated with a gentleman; courteous and honorable behavior, especially toward women.
He displayed true gentlemanship by opening the door for her. 他为她开门展现了真正的绅士风度。
近义词: chivalry, courtesy, politeness
反义词: rudeness, boorishness, incivility
词性: 名词

词源:
From gentleman + -ship.  Gentleman comes from Old French gentilhomme, from gentil 'noble, gentle' (from Latin gentilis 'of the same clan or race') + homme 'man' (from Latin homō).  The suffix -ship denotes 'state or condition of being'.
