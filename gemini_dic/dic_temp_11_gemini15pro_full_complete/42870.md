courtierly:/ˈkɔːrtiərli/ /ˈkɔːrtjərli/| adj.|彬彬有礼的；谦恭的；献媚的|Polite, refined, and elegant, as befitting a royal court.  Having or showing the polished manners of a courtier; elegant and gracious.|He bowed courtierly to the queen. 他彬彬有礼地向女王鞠躬。|近义词：polite, refined, elegant, gracious, courteous, urbane, polished, suave, debonair|反义词：rude, impolite, boorish, uncouth, churlish|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From courtier + -ly.  "Courtier" comes from Old French "corteier," meaning "one who attends court," from "cort" (court). Ultimately from Latin "cohors," meaning "enclosure, yard."
