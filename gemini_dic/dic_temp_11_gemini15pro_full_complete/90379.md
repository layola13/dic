howdahs: /ˈhaʊdəz/ | n. | 象轿；象舆 | A seat, usually covered and often canopied, placed on the back of an elephant. |  The maharaja rode through the streets in a lavishly decorated howdah.  大国王坐在装饰华丽的象轿里穿过街道。 | The howdah swayed precariously as the elephant lumbered through the jungle. 象轿随着大象在丛林中笨拙地移动而摇摇欲坠。 | 近义词: elephant saddle | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Hindi हौदा (haudā), from Arabic هَوْدَج (hawdaj).
