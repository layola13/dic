fitness:/ˈfɪtnəs/| n.|健康；适合；合格|The state of being physically healthy and strong.|例句：He works hard to maintain his fitness. 他努力保持健康。|近义词：health, well-being, suitability, aptitude|反义词：unfitness, weakness, inability|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English fitnesse, from fit + -nesse. Ultimately from Old English *fetel (“band, belt”).
