disallowed:/dɪsəˈlaʊd/
| adj.| 不允许的，被禁止的|Not permitted or authorized; prohibited.|例句：The use of cell phones is disallowed during the exam.  考试期间不允许使用手机。|近义词：forbidden, prohibited, banned|反义词：allowed, permitted|词性:形容词
disallow:/ˌdɪsəˈlaʊ/
| vt.| 不允许，禁止，驳回|To refuse to allow or accept something; to reject.|例句：The judge disallowed the evidence.  法官驳回了证据。|近义词：reject, refuse, prohibit|反义词：allow, permit, accept|词性:动词

词源:
dis- (prefix): expressing negation or reversal  表示否定或反转
allow (verb): to permit  允许
