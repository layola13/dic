discovery:/dɪˈskʌvəri/ | n. | 发现；被发现的事物；探索 | the act of finding something unexpectedly or while looking for something else | Columbus's discovery of America was a landmark event. 哥伦布发现美洲是一个划时代的事件。 | finding, unearthing, detection | concealment, hiding | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *descoverir*, from des- "un-" + couvrir "to cover." Ultimately from Latin cooperire "to cover completely."
