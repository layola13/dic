flours:/ˈflaʊərz/| n. |面粉；粉末；粉状物|Finely ground meal of grain, especially wheat, used for baking bread, cakes, and pastry. Also, any fine powder.|The baker used five different flours in his artisanal bread.  这位面包师在他的手工面包中使用了五种不同的面粉。|Synonyms: meal, powder, dust|Antonyms: whole grains, lumps|noun
