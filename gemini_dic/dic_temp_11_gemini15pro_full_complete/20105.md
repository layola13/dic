biotech:/ˈbaɪoʊtɛk/| n. |生物技术|The application of biological systems and organisms to technical and industrial processes, especially the genetic manipulation of microorganisms for the production of antibiotics, hormones, etc.|The biotech industry is booming. 生物技术产业正在蓬勃发展。|Synonyms: biotechnology, genetic engineering|Antonyms: None|noun
