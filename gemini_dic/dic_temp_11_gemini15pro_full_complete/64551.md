erratic:/ɪˈrætɪk/ | adj. n.|古怪的，飘忽不定的；漂砾|geologically displaced rock differing from the type native to the area in which it rests; unpredictable and inconsistent in behavior|The erratic boulder was deposited by a glacier thousands of years ago. 这块漂砾是由数千年前的冰川沉积下来的。|Synonyms: unpredictable, inconsistent, capricious, fickle, unstable, changeable|Antonyms: consistent, steady, stable, predictable, regular|adjective

erratic:/ɪˈrætɪk/ | n.|漂砾|a rock or boulder that differs from the surrounding rock and is believed to have been brought from a distance by glacial action|The farmer found several large erratics in his field. 农民在他的田里发现了几个巨大的漂砾。|Synonyms: boulder, rock|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a physical object)|noun
