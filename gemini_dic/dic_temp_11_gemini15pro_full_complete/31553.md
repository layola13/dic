chartreux:/ʃɑːrˈtruː(z)/ | n. |卡特豪斯猫; 沙特尔猫 |A breed of domestic cat, known for its bluish-grey coat and orange eyes.  It originated in France. |例句：My Chartreux has a very dense, plush coat. 我的沙特尔猫有一身浓密柔软的皮毛。

例句：The Chartreux is known for being a quiet and gentle cat. 沙特尔猫以安静温顺的性格而闻名。|近义词：cat, feline |反义词: None |词性: Noun


Etymology: From French *chartreux*, referring to the Carthusian monks who may have bred them.
