nonstatic:/nɒnˈstætɪk/ | adj. | 非静态的 | Not static; able to be changed or modified during program execution.  Relating to members (variables or methods) of a class that are associated with instances of the class, rather than with the class itself. |  A nonstatic variable belongs to each individual object. 一个非静态变量属于每个单独的对象。|  Nonstatic methods can access instance variables.  非静态方法可以访问实例变量。|近义词: dynamic, instance | 反义词: static | 词性: adjective
