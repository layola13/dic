mutationist:/mjuːˈteɪʃənɪst/ | n. | 突变论者 | A biologist who believes that mutation is the primary force driving evolution, as opposed to natural selection. |  The mutationist argued that large, sudden changes in genes were responsible for the emergence of new species. 突变论者认为，基因的巨大而突然的变化是新物种出现的原因。|  The mutationist presented evidence of rapid genetic changes in a population of fruit flies. 突变论者展示了果蝇种群中快速基因变化的证据。| synonyms: saltationist | antonyms: gradualist, selectionist | noun
