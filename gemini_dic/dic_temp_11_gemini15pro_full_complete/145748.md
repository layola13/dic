overculled:/ˌoʊvərˈkʌld/
adjective
过度剔除的，过度捕猎的
reduced in number beyond a desirable or sustainable level, typically by hunting or fishing.
The deer population was overculled last year, leading to a shortage this season. 去年鹿群被过度捕猎，导致本季度鹿群短缺。
overhunted, overfished, depleted
underpopulated, abundant
