misproposed:/ˌmɪsprəˈpoʊzd/
verb
错误地提议；提出不当的建议
incorrectly or inappropriately proposed
The committee misproposed a solution that would have worsened the problem. 委员会提出了一个会使问题恶化的不当解决方案。
The architect misproposed a design that was structurally unsound.  这位建筑师提出了一个结构上不合理的错误设计。
近义词: misstated, misrepresented, misconstrued
反义词: correctly proposed, aptly proposed
词性: verb (past tense and past participle of mispropose)
词源: mis- (prefix meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + propose (verb meaning "put forward an idea or plan")
