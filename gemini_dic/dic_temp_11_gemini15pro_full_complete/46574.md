dama:/ˈdɑːmə/ | n. | checkers (especially in Spanish-speaking countries); a lady; an elegant or fashionable woman | A board game similar to draughts played in various countries.  A term of respect used to address a woman, especially an older woman. A fashionable or stylish woman. |  例句：She is a real dama, always dressed impeccably. 她是一位真正的女士，总是穿着无可挑剔。

例句：Let's play a game of damas. 我们来玩一局西洋跳棋吧。| 近义词：lady, woman, matron, dame (in English), señora (in Spanish)| 反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly) unfashionable woman, girl | 词性: noun
