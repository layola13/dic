beefers:/ˈbiːfərz/
n.
壮汉;  健壮的人;  吃牛肉的人; (尤指)抱怨者
Strong, muscular people; those who eat beef; (especially) complainers.
Those beefers at the gym can lift incredible weights.  那些健身房的壮汉能举起令人难以置信的重量。
He's one of those beefers who's always complaining about something. 他是那种总是抱怨个不停的人。
近义词:musclemen, complainers, beefcakes
反义词:weaklings, wimps
