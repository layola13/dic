hireless:/ˈhaɪərlɪs/
adjective
无雇用的；失业的
Without hire or wages; unemployed.  Lacking the means to hire or employ others.
He found himself in a hireless state after the company downsized. 他在公司裁员后发现自己失业了。
The economic downturn left many skilled workers hireless. 经济下滑使许多熟练工人失业。
近义词: unemployed, jobless, out of work
反义词: employed, hired, working
