ole:/oʊl/ | interj. |好啊；加油； | An exclamation of approval, encouragement, or triumph, especially in Spain and some Latin American countries. | Example: Ole! That was a fantastic performance!  好啊！那是一场精彩的表演！ | Example: The crowd shouted "Ole!" as the matador made a daring move.  当斗牛士做出一个大胆的举动时，人群高呼“Ole！”| Synonym: Bravo, Hooray, Yes! | Antonym: Boo, Hiss | Part of Speech: Interjection
