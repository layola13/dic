neckwear: /ˈnɛkˌwɛər/
n.
颈饰；领带；围巾
Articles of clothing worn around the neck, such as ties, scarves, or cravats.
He has a large collection of colorful neckwear. 他收藏了大量色彩鲜艳的颈饰。
近义词: accessories, adornments
反义词: (无直接反义词)
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From neck + wear.  "Neck" comes from Old English "hēafod-necca" meaning "head-neck". "Wear" comes from Old English "werian" meaning "to clothe, carry, bear."
