ichthulin:/ɪkˈθjuːlɪn/
noun
鱼精蛋白
A phosphoprotein found in the eggs of bony fishes.
Fish eggs are a good source of ichthulin, a protein important for embryonic development.  鱼卵富含鱼精蛋白，这是一种对胚胎发育很重要的蛋白质。
Synonyms: vitellin
Antonyms: None
