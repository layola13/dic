mulley:/ˈmʌli/| n.|无角羊，阉割的公羊|A castrated ram; a hornless sheep, especially a polled Dorset or Wiltshire. |The farmer kept a few mulleys for breeding purposes. 农夫养了几只无角公羊用于繁殖。|Synonyms: polled ram, wether|Antonyms: ram, ewe|noun

mulley:/ˈmʌli/| n.|矿石粉碎机，(尤指)石英粉碎机|A machine for pulverizing ore, especially quartz. (chiefly historical)|The old mulley in the mine was no longer in use. 矿里那台旧的石英粉碎机已经不再使用了。|Synonyms: pulverizer, grinder, mill|Antonyms: N/A|noun
