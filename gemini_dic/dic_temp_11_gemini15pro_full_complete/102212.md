italophobia:/ɪˌtæləˈfoʊbiə/
noun
意大利恐惧症
An abnormal or irrational fear of all things Italian, or of Italians themselves.
He couldn't bring himself to eat at the Italian restaurant due to his italophobia. 他因为意大利恐惧症而不敢去意大利餐厅吃饭。
xenophobia,  ethnophobia
None
