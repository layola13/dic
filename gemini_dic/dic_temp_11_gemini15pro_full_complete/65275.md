euchloric:/juːˈklɔːrɪk/
adjective
富含叶绿素的; 叶绿素正常的
Containing a normal amount of chlorophyll; relating to or characterized by euchlorophyll.
The euchloric leaves of the plant absorbed sunlight efficiently.  这种植物富含叶绿素的叶子能有效地吸收阳光。
Synonyms: chlorophyllous, green
Antonyms: achlorophyllous, chlorotic
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *eu- (good, well) + chloric (relating to chlorine or chlorophyll).  源自希腊语 *eu- (好的，正常的) + chloric (与氯或叶绿素相关的)。
