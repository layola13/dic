orgulous:/ˈɔːrɡjələs/ |adj.|傲慢的，自大的|Characterized by haughty pride; arrogant.|例句：He was so orgulous, he couldn't admit he was wrong. 他太傲慢了，以至于无法承认自己错了。

例句：Her orgulous manner made her unpopular. 她傲慢的态度让她不受欢迎。|近义词：haughty, arrogant, supercilious, proud, disdainful|反义词：humble, modest, unassuming|词性:形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French orguilleus, from orguel ‘pride’, from Latin orgōlium.
