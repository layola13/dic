enacting:/ɪˈnæktɪŋ/| v. |制定，颁布；扮演；演出|Putting a law into effect; performing a role or action.|例句：Congress is enacting a new law. 国会正在制定一项新法律。

例句：The actors are enacting a scene from Shakespeare. 演员们正在表演莎士比亚的一场戏。|近义词：legislating, performing, portraying|反义词：repealing, abolishing|词性:动词(present participle)
词源: From Late Latin *enactare, from Latin in- + agere "to do, drive, act."
