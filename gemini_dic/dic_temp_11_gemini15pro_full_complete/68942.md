fatefulness:/ˈfeɪtfʊlnɪs/
n.|宿命性；命运攸关；决定性|The quality of being fateful; the significance of fate or destiny in determining events.|The fatefulness of their meeting was undeniable. 他们的相遇是命中注定的，无可否认。|近义词：destiny, predestination, inevitability|反义词：randomness, chance, fortuity
詞性: 名詞


ETYMOLOGY->
From fate + -ful + -ness.  "Fate" comes from Old French "fat" (destined), from Latin "fatum" (that which has been spoken; destiny).  "-ful" is a suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by." "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives.
