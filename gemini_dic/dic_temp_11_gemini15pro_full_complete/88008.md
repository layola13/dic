high-spirited:/ˈhaɪˈspɪrɪtɪd/| adj.|兴高采烈的；精神饱满的|Full of energy and enthusiasm; lively.|例句：The team was high-spirited after their victory.  球队在获胜后兴高采烈。|近义词：lively, energetic, enthusiastic, exuberant|反义词：dejected, depressed, downcast|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From high + spirited.  "Spirited" itself comes from "spirit," ultimately from Latin "spiritus" meaning "breath" or "soul."  The "high" intensifier denotes an elevated or heightened state of spirit.
