antifreeze:/ˈæntiˌfriːz/
n.|防冻剂|A liquid added to the cooling system of a car or other engine to lower the freezing point of the water and prevent damage in cold weather.|I need to buy some antifreeze for my car before winter. 我需要在冬天来临之前给我的车买些防冻剂。|近义词：coolant|反义词:|名词

antifreeze:/ˈæntiˌfriːz/
