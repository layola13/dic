dismortgage:/dɪsˈmɔːrɡɪdʒ/
|v.|赎回，解除抵押|To redeem (mortgaged property) by paying off the mortgage; to free from mortgage.|He dismortgaged his house after years of saving. 他在多年的储蓄后赎回了他的房子。|近义词: redeem, pay off, clear|反义词: mortgage|词性: 动词

dismortgage:/dɪsˈmɔːrɡɪdʒ/
|n.|赎回，解除抵押（行为）|The act of redeeming mortgaged property.|The dismortgage of the property allowed him to sell it freely.  房产的赎回让他可以自由出售它。|近义词: redemption|反义词: mortgaging|词性: 名词
