decamped:/dɪˈkæmpt/
verb
突然离开；撤营；潜逃
suddenly left a place, especially secretly or without permission
The circus decamped in the night. 马戏团连夜离开了。
synonyms: leave, depart, abscond, flee, make off, bolt
antonyms: arrive, stay, remain
