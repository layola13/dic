chip-in:/ˌtʃɪp ˈɪn/ | phrasal verb | 凑钱；捐款；插嘴；帮忙 | contribute (money or help) to a common fund or cause.  | Example: We all chipped in to buy a birthday present for her. 我们都凑钱给她买生日礼物。 | Example: He chipped in with some helpful advice. 他插嘴提了一些有用的建议。 | 近义词: contribute, donate, help out, pitch in | 反义词: withhold, refuse, abstain | 词性: 动词短语

chip-in:/ˌtʃɪp ˈɪn/ | noun | 凑钱；捐款 | an act of contributing money or help to a common fund or cause. | Example: The chip-in for the new coffee machine was very successful.  为新咖啡机募捐非常成功。 | Example: Thanks for your chip-in, it really helped. 谢谢你的捐助，真的很有帮助。 | 近义词: contribution, donation | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词
