chapfallen:/ˈtʃæpfɔːlən/ | adj. | 沮丧的，垂头丧气的 | Dejected and disappointed; crestfallen.  Having one's lower jaw hanging loosely, as from disappointment or exhaustion. | He looked chapfallen after his team lost the championship game. 他在球队输掉冠军赛后看起来很沮丧。| Synonyms: dejected, downcast, crestfallen, discouraged, disheartened | Antonyms: cheerful, elated, happy, joyous | adjective
