functionalize:/ˈfʌŋkʃənəˌlaɪz/| vt.|使…功能化；赋予…功能|To add a functional group to a molecule or material, often to give it specific properties or to enable it to react in a desired way. |Chemists functionalized the surface of the nanoparticles with antibodies to target cancer cells.  化学家利用抗体将纳米颗粒表面功能化，以靶向癌细胞。

|Synonyms: modify, adapt, equip, enhance|Antonyms: disable, deactivate|Verb|


functionalize:/ˈfʌŋkʃənəˌlaɪz/| vt.|使…功能化；赋予…功能|To make something more functional or practical. |They functionalized the old barn by converting it into a workshop.  他们把旧谷仓改建成了一个车间，使其更加实用。

|Synonyms: improve, upgrade, optimize|Antonyms: degrade, worsen|Verb|
