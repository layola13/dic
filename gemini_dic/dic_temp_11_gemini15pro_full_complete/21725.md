boarfish:/ˈbɔːrfɪʃ/| n. |  Boarfish; 长吻帆鳍鱼 |A family of deep-sea fishes (Caproidae) with high, compressed bodies and long, pig-like snouts.  They are found in the Atlantic, Indian, and Pacific Oceans. | Boarfish are often caught as bycatch in deep-sea trawling. 长吻帆鳍鱼经常在深海拖网捕捞中作为副渔获物被捕获。

Boarfish can be found at depths of up to 1,000 meters. 长吻帆鳍鱼可以在深达1000米的地方找到。 | 近义词：None (specific name for a type of fish) | 反义词：None | 词性:noun
