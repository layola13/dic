for-sure:/fɔːr ˈʃʊr/ /fər ˈʃʊr/ | adv. | 肯定地，毫无疑问地 | Certainly, without a doubt.  Used to emphasize that something is definitely true or will definitely happen. | "I'll be there for sure." 我一定会去的。| 近义词:certainly, definitely, absolutely, positively | 反义词:doubtfully, uncertainly | 词性:副词
Etymology:  "For sure" originated as a colloquial Americanism in the late 19th century, combining the preposition "for" (meaning "with regard to") and "sure" (meaning "certain").
