hardscrabble:/ˈhɑːrdˌskræbəl/| adj.|艰苦的，贫瘠的，勉强维持的|Characterized by poverty and struggle; involving unremitting toil and producing meager returns.|They lived a hardscrabble existence eking out a living on a small farm. 他们在小农场里过着艰苦的生活，勉强维持生计。|例句：The farmer worked the hardscrabble land, barely making enough to survive. 农民在贫瘠的土地上耕作，几乎难以生存。

例句：She grew up in a hardscrabble neighborhood, where opportunities were scarce. 她在一个贫困的街区长大，那里的机会很少。|近义词：difficult, arduous, impoverished, meager, struggling|反义词：prosperous, affluent, easy, fertile|词性:形容词
