gambolling:/ˈɡæmbəlɪŋ/ | adj. n.| 嬉戏玩耍的，跳跃的; 嬉戏, 跳跃 | An act of running and jumping about playfully. | The lambs were gambolling in the field. 羊羔在田野里嬉戏。|近义词：frolicking, romping, cavorting, skipping, prancing |反义词：sluggish, still, motionless |词性: adjective, noun

词源:  From the Old French *gamboler, frequentative of gamber "to jump, leap," of Germanic origin.
