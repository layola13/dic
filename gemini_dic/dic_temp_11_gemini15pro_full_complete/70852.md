finkbeiner:/ˈfɪŋkˌbaɪnər/
n.|告密者，叛徒|A person, especially a child, who tells on someone else, a tattletale.|例句：He was labeled a finkbeiner for reporting his classmates' cheating to the teacher. 他因为向老师报告同学作弊而被贴上了告密者的标签。

例句：Don't be a finkbeiner, let them have their fun. 别当告密者，让他们玩得开心点。
|近义词：snitch, tattletale, informer|反义词: loyalist, confidant|词性: 名词
