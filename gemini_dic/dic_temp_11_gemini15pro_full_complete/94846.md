imputing:/ɪmˈpjuːtɪŋ/ | v. | 归咎；推断；估算 |  The act of attributing something, especially a fault or crime, to someone; also, the process of estimating missing values in data. |  He was imputing the blame to his colleagues. 他将责任归咎于他的同事。  | synonyms: ascribe, attribute, assign, credit, charge | antonyms: exculpate, absolve | verb

imputation:/ˌɪmpjuˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 归咎；归罪；推断 | The act of imputing something, especially blame or a fault.  | The imputation of his motives was unfair.  对他的动机进行的归罪是不公平的。 | synonyms: attribution, ascription, accusation, charge | antonyms: exoneration, acquittal | noun
