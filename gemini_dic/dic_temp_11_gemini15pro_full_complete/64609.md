eruptively:/ɪˈrʌptɪvli/| adv.|爆发地，喷发地|In a manner characterized by eruption; explosively.  以爆发的、喷发的形式;爆炸性地|The volcano erupted eruptively, sending ash and lava high into the sky. 火山猛烈喷发，将火山灰和熔岩喷向高空。|近义词：explosively, violently, suddenly|反义词：gradually, slowly, calmly|词性:副词
