nonretailer:/ˌnɒnˈriːteɪlə(r)/
n.
非零售商
A business that does not sell directly to consumers.
Many nonretailers, such as manufacturers, sell their products through retailers. 许多非零售商，例如制造商，通过零售商销售他们的产品。
non-selling businesses, wholesalers, manufacturers
retailer
noun
