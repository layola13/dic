cirrostomi: /səˈrɒstəmaɪ/ (US) /sɪˈrɒstəmaɪ/ (UK)| n. |圆口纲|A superclass of jawless fish-like vertebrates, characterized by a circular, sucker-like mouth.  They are also known as lampreys and hagfishes.|Lampreys and hagfishes belong to the Cirrostomi. 七鳃鳗和盲鳗属于圆口纲。|Superclass: Agnatha, Class: Hyperoartia (lampreys) and Myxini (hagfishes)| 反义词: Gnathostomata (jawed vertebrates)|noun
