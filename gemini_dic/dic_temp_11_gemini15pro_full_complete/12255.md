ascends:/əˈsɛndz/
v.
上升，攀登
to go up or climb something
The hiker ascends the mountain.  登山者登上山。
The smoke ascends into the sky. 烟雾升上天空。

近义词:climb, rise, mount, soar
反义词:descend, fall, drop, sink
词性:动词
