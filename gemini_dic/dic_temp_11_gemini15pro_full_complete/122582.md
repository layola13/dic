modern:/ˈmɒdən/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdərn/ (US)| adj.|现代的，近代的|Of or relating to the present or recent times as opposed to the remote past.|例句：We live in a modern world. 我们生活在一个现代化的世界。|例句：The building has a modern design. 这栋楼有一个现代化的设计。|近义词：contemporary, current, recent, up-to-date|反义词：ancient, old, outdated, traditional|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle French *moderne*, from Late Latin *modernus* "of the present mode or fashion," from Latin *modo* "just now," ablative of *modus* "measure, manner."
