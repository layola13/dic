damningness:/ˈdæmɪŋnɪs/| n.|定罪性；该罚性；十足的有罪性|The damningness of the evidence led to a swift conviction.  证据确凿，迅速定罪。|近义词：culpability, guilt, incrimination|反义词：innocence, exculpation|noun

Etymology: From "damning" (adjective) + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality). "Damning" comes from the verb "damn," ultimately from Latin "damnare" (to condemn, to sentence).
