inciter:/ɪnˈsaɪtər/ | n. |煽动者，教唆者|Someone who stirs up others to action, often violent or unlawful action.  |The inciter of the riot was arrested. 煽动骚乱的人被逮捕了。|Synonyms: agitator, instigator, provoker |Antonyms: peacemaker, mediator, pacifier| noun


inciting:/ɪnˈsaɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |煽动的，教唆的|Stirring up action or feeling, especially violent or unlawful action. |His inciting speech led to the protests. 他煽动性的演讲引发了抗议。|Synonyms: provocative, inflammatory, instigating |Antonyms: calming, soothing, pacifying| adjective



Etymology: From the Late Middle English *incitour*, from Anglo-French *incitour*, from the Latin *incitātor*, agent noun from *incitāre* ("to incite").
