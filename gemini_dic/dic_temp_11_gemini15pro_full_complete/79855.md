Google Mania (谷歌狂热)
An informal term referring to an obsession or enthusiasm for Google products, services, or culture.
Example: His gmania led him to buy every new Pixel phone.  他因为谷歌狂热而买每一款新的Pixel手机。
Example:  She has a serious case of gmania; she even wears Google Glass. 她对谷歌非常狂热；她甚至戴着谷歌眼镜。
Synonym: Google fever, Google obsession
Antonym: technophobia, Luddism
词性: noun
