flout:/flaʊt/
verb
蔑视，公然违抗
openly disregard (a rule, law or convention).
Many motorists flout the traffic laws. 许多驾车者无视交通法规。
近义词: defy, scorn, disdain, disregard, violate
反义词: obey, respect, observe, comply with, adhere to
词性: 动词

flout:/flaʊt/
noun
蔑视，嘲笑；侮辱
an act or instance of flouting.
His flout of the rules angered the judge. 他对规则的蔑视激怒了法官。
近义词: mockery, scoff, jeer, sneer
反义词: respect, reverence, compliance
词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *flouten*, from Old French *floter* (“to float, swim, waft”), from Frankish *flōtjan*, from Proto-Germanic *flōtijaną* (“to float”), from Proto-Indo-European *plewd- (“to flow, float, swim”). Cognate with Old English *flotian* (“to float”), Old High German *fliozzan*, *fliuzan* (“to flow”), Dutch *vlotten* (“to float, raft”), German *flößen* (“to raft, float, infuse”), Swedish *flyta* (“to float”).
