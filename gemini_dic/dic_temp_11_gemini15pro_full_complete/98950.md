inter-communal:/ˌɪntərkəˈmjuːnl/
adjective
社区间的；不同社群之间的
Existing or occurring between different communities.
例句：Inter-communal violence flared up after the election. 选举后，社区间的暴力冲突爆发。

例句：The festival promotes inter-communal harmony and understanding. 这个节日促进了社区间的和谐与理解。

近义词：intergroup, interracial, cross-cultural
反义词：intra-communal, within-community
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "inter-" (between) + "communal" (relating to a community).  "Inter-" comes from Latin, meaning "between, among, in the midst of." "Communal" comes from Late Latin "communalis,"  from "communis" meaning "common."
