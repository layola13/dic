nonstudent:/ˌnɑːnˈstuːdnt/
noun
非学生；非大学生
a person who is not a student
例句：The library restricts access to nonstudents during exam periods.  图书馆在考试期间限制非学生进入。

例句：The discount is only available to students, nonstudents will have to pay full price.  这项折扣只对学生有效，非学生需支付全价。

近义词：non-student, nonpupil
反义词：student, pupil
词性：n.
