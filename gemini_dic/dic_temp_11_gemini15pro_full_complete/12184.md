as-good-as:/æzˈɡʊdˈæz/| adj.|几乎；实际上；等于；和...一样好|practically, almost, nearly the same as; virtually|例句：The deal is as good as done. 这笔交易实际上已经完成了。|近义词：virtually, practically, almost, nearly|反义词：nowhere near, far from|词性：形容词
词源：From the phrase "as good as", meaning "equally good to". Over time, it evolved to also mean "practically" or "virtually".
