clatterer:/ˈklætərər/ (US), /ˈklætərə(r)/ (UK)| n. |发出咔嗒声的人或物|A person or thing that makes a clattering noise.|The clatterer in the kitchen turned out to be the cat knocking over pots.|近义词: rattler, banger|反义词: silencer|词性:名词

clattering:/ˈklætərɪŋ/ (US), /ˈklætərɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. |发出咔嗒声的|Making a loud rattling noise.|The clattering train woke me up.|近义词: rattling, banging|反义词: quiet, silent|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *clateren, frequentative of *claten (“to clatter”), from Old Norse klátra (“to clatter, to prattle”), from Proto-Germanic *hlatrazaną (“to rattle, to clatter”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱel- (“to resound, to ring”).
