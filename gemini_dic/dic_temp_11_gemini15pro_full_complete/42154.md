couche:/kuːʃ/| n.|睡椅，长沙发；(尤指医生检查病人用的)诊察台|A piece of furniture for sitting or lying on, especially a sofa or a chaise longue. In medical contexts, it refers to an examination table.|例句：The patient lay down on the couche. 病人躺在诊察台上。

例句：She stretched out on the couche with a book. 她拿着一本书躺在长沙发上。|近义词：sofa, chaise longue, settee|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用chair 椅子表示相对的概念)|词性: noun

词源：From French *couche*, from Old French *colcher* "to lay down," from Latin *collocare* "to place," from *com-* "together" + *locare* "to place."  The sense of "examination table" arose from its use in psychoanalysis.
