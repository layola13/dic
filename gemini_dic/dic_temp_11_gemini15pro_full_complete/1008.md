acaulescent:/ˌeɪkɔːˈlɛsənt/ | adj. | 无茎的，贴地生长的 |  (of a plant) having no apparent stem above ground, with the leaves and flower stalks arising directly from a rootstock or underground stem. | The dandelion is an acaulescent plant, its leaves forming a rosette at ground level. 蒲公英是一种无茎植物，它的叶子在地面上形成莲座丛。| The acaulescent primrose hugged the ground, its vibrant yellow flowers a stark contrast to the dark soil.  无茎报春花紧贴地面，其鲜艳的黄色花朵与深色土壤形成鲜明对比。| 近义词: stemless, sessile | 反义词: caulescent, stemmed | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin *acaulis (“stemless”), from a- (“without”) + caulis (“stalk, stem”).
