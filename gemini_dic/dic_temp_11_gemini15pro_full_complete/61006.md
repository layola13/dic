eliminating:/ɪˈlɪmɪneɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 消除，除去 | present participle of eliminate.  The process of removing or getting rid of something. |  They are eliminating sugar from their diet. 他们正在从饮食中去除糖分。| Synonyms: removing, eradicating, excluding, obliterating | Antonyms: including, adding, keeping, retaining | Verb

Etymology->
From Latin eliminatus, past participle of eliminare "to turn out of doors, expel," from ex "out" (see ex-) + limen (genitive liminis) "threshold."
