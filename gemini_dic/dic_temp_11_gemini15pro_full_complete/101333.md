iodism:/ˈaɪəˌdɪzəm/| n.|碘中毒；碘疗法|A condition caused by excessive intake or absorption of iodine, characterized by various symptoms such as skin eruptions, headache, and inflammation of the mucous membranes. Also refers to the treatment of disease with iodine or iodine compounds.|例句：The patient presented with symptoms of iodism after prolonged exposure to iodine fumes. 病人长时间接触碘蒸汽后出现碘中毒症状。

例句：The doctor prescribed iodism as a treatment for the patient's thyroid condition. 医生给病人开了碘疗法来治疗甲状腺疾病。|近义词：iodine poisoning|反义词: (None, as it's a medical condition and treatment)|词性:noun
