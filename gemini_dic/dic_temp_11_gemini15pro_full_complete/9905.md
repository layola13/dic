ape's:/eɪps/
|n.|猿的；类猿人的|belonging to or associated with an ape|The ape's long arms helped it swing through the trees. 猿的长臂帮助它在树间荡来荡去。|近义词:monkey's, primate's|反义词:human's|词性:名词所有格

Etymology:
From Old English *apa (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *apanō, from Proto-Indo-European *keh₂p- (“to grasp”).  The apostrophe + s indicates possessive form.
