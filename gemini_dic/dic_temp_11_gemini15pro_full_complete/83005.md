gunpowdered:/ˈɡʌnˌpaʊdərd/| adj.|涂有火药的；火药味的|Covered with or smelling of gunpowder.|The gunpowdered keg lay precariously close to the fire.  涂有火药的木桶危险地靠近火堆。|The air was thick and gunpowdered after the volley of shots.  排枪齐射后，空气中弥漫着浓烈的火药味。|Synonyms: explosive, combustible|Antonyms: inert, non-flammable|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From "gunpowder" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives). "Gunpowder" comes from "gunne" (Middle English, meaning "cannon") + "poudre" (Old French, meaning "powder").  Ultimately, "poudre" derives from Latin "pulvis," meaning "dust."
