furnitureless:/ˈfɜːrnɪtʃərlɪs/
adjective
无家具的；没有家具的
Without furniture; lacking furniture.
The apartment was completely furnitureless when we moved in. 我们搬进去的时候，公寓里完全没有家具。
The family was forced to live in a furnitureless house after the fire. 火灾之后，这家人被迫住在一个没有家具的房子里。
Synonyms: unfurnished, bare, empty
Antonyms: furnished, equipped
词性: 形容词
