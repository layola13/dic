absolutest:/ˈæbsəluːtɪst/
adjective
最绝对的，最完全的
superlative of absolute: most absolute; complete; utter.
例句：That is the absolutest nonsense I have ever heard. 那是我听过的最荒谬的废话。
例句：He ruled with absolutest authority. 他拥有最绝对的权威进行统治。
近义词：complete, utter, total, perfect, pure, unmitigated, unqualified
反义词：relative, conditional, limited
词性：adjective
