lowballer:/ˈloʊˌbɔːlər/| n.|低价竞标者；压价者|Someone who makes an offer that is significantly lower than the asking price or the value of something.|He's a notorious lowballer, always trying to get things for next to nothing. 他是个臭名昭著的低价竞标者，总是试图以极低的价格买到东西。|synonyms: cheapskate, tightwad, bargain hunter|antonyms: high roller, big spender|noun

lowball:/ˈloʊˌbɔːl/| vt.|低价竞标；压价|To make a ridiculously low offer.|They lowballed us on the price of our house. 他们在我们房子的价格上压价。|synonyms: underbid, undercut|antonyms: overbid, overpay|verb
