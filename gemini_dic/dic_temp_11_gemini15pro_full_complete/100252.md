interruptions:/ˌɪntəˈrʌpʃənz/
n.
中断，打断，干扰
The constant interruptions were making it difficult to concentrate.
持续不断的打扰使得很难集中注意力。
His speech was met with several interruptions from the audience.
他的演讲多次被观众打断。
Synonym: interference, disruption, intrusion, obstruction
Antonym: continuation, progression, flow
词性: 名词 (plural of interruption)

interruption:/ˌɪntəˈrʌpʃən/
n.
中断，打断，干扰
I apologize for the interruption, but there's an urgent phone call for you.
很抱歉打扰你，但是有你的紧急电话。
The power outage caused a temporary interruption in service.
停电导致服务暂时中断。
Synonym: interference, disruption, intrusion, obstruction
Antonym: continuation, progression, flow
词性: 名词
