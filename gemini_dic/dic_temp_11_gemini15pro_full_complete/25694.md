bunkered:/ˈbʌŋkərd/| adj.|受困的，陷入困境的，（高尔夫球）落入沙坑的|In a difficult situation, especially in golf when the ball is in a bunker.  Being in a protected or defensive position.|例句：His shot landed him bunkered beside a tree. 他的击球使他陷入树旁的沙坑。|例句：The soldiers were bunkered down, awaiting the attack. 士兵们躲在掩体里，等待进攻。|近义词：trapped, stranded, stuck, in a fix|反义词：free, clear, liberated|词性:形容词

词源: From the noun "bunker," referring to a reinforced underground shelter or a sand trap on a golf course.  The word "bunker" originally referred to a coal bin or compartment on a ship, from the earlier Dutch word "buncker," meaning a sleeping berth or cabin.
