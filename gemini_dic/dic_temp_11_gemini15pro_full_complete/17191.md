bauhaus:/ˈbaʊˌhaʊs/| n. |包豪斯（设计学院）|A German art school operational from 1919 to 1933 that combined crafts and the fine arts, and was famous for the approach to design that it publicized and taught.  It is renowned for its unique approach to art and design, which emphasized functionality, simplicity, and the unity of art and craft.|The Bauhaus movement had a profound influence on modern architecture and design. 包豪斯运动对现代建筑和设计产生了深远的影响。| The iconic Bauhaus Dessau building is a testament to the school's innovative design principles.  标志性的包豪斯德绍校舍是该校创新设计原则的证明。 |近义词: school, academy, institute|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "traditional art school")|词性:noun

Etymology: German, from Bau ‘building’ + Haus ‘house’.
