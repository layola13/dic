dearness:/ˈdɪrnɪs/| n.|高昂；昂贵；可爱；亲爱|High cost; expensiveness. Also, affection; fondness.|The dearness of food forced many families to cut back on other expenses.  食物价格高昂，迫使许多家庭削减其他开支。|Synonyms: costliness, expensiveness, high price, exorbitance; affection, fondness, endearment|Antonyms: cheapness, affordability, inexpensiveness; indifference, coldness|noun
