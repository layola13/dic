evinced:/ɪˈvɪnst/
verb
表明;显示; 表露
demonstrated clearly; revealed
Her behavior evinced a lack of respect. 她的行为表明她缺乏尊重。
synonyms: demonstrated, manifested, exhibited, displayed, revealed, shown
antonyms: concealed, hid, masked
verb
