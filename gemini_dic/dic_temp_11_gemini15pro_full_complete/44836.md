crustaceous:/krʌˈsteɪʃəs/| adj.|甲壳类的；有壳的|Relating to or belonging to the class Crustacea, a large group of mainly aquatic arthropods including crabs, lobsters, shrimps, and barnacles, characteristically having a segmented body, a hard exoskeleton, and paired, jointed limbs.|Lobsters, crabs, and shrimps are crustaceous. 龙虾、螃蟹和虾都是甲壳类动物。|近义词：shellfish, shelled|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用"soft-bodied"表示身体柔软的)|词性: adjective
