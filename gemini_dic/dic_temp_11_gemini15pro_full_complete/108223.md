lauding:/ˈlɔːdɪŋ/ | v. | 赞扬；称赞 | Praising or applauding someone or something highly. | The critics are lauding the new film as a masterpiece. 影评家们盛赞这部新电影是杰作。 | Example: The mayor lauded the firefighters for their bravery. 市长赞扬了消防员的勇敢。 | Synonyms: praising, extolling, acclaiming, commending | Antonyms: criticizing, condemning, denouncing | verb

lauding:/ˈlɔːdɪŋ/ | gerund or present participle | 赞扬；称赞 | The act of praising or applauding someone or something highly. |  His constant lauding of his own achievements became tiresome.  他不断地称赞自己的成就，让人感到厌烦。 | Example:  They responded to her lauding with polite applause.  他们以礼貌的掌声回应了她的赞扬。 | Synonyms: praising, extolling, acclaiming, commending | Antonyms: criticizing, condemning, denouncing | verb
