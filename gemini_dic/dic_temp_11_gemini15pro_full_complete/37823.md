compensatory:/kəmˈpɛnsətɔːri/ /kɑːmˈpɛnsətɔːri/
adjective
补偿的，赔偿的
Reducing the working hours is a compensatory measure for the lower wages.  减少工作时间是对较低工资的一种补偿措施。
This extra payment is compensatory for the inconvenience you have suffered. 这笔额外付款是对您所遭受的不便的补偿。
synonyms:  remedial, corrective, reparative, restorative
antonyms:  punitive, penalizing
词性: adjective
