hoarder:/ˈhɔːrdər/ (US), /ˈhɔːdə(r)/ (UK)| n. |囤积者，贮藏者|A person who accumulates and keeps large quantities of items, often of little value, and has difficulty discarding them, sometimes to the point of it negatively impacting their living space and life.| She's become a real hoarder, her house is overflowing with useless stuff. 她变成了一个真正的囤积者，她的房子里堆满了没用的东西。| My grandmother was a hoarder; she never threw anything away.  我的祖母是个囤积者；她从来不扔任何东西。|collector, saver, accumulator|spender, minimalist|noun
