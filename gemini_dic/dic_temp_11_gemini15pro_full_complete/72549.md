flutiest:/ˈfluːtɪɪst/| adj.|最像长笛的；吹奏长笛最好的|Having the most flute-like quality; most skilled at playing the flute.|Her tone was the flutiest of all the performers. 她的音色是所有演奏者中最像长笛的。|近义词:most melodic, clearest, purest (in tone)|反义词:hoarsest, harshest, most dissonant|词性:形容词

词源:  From flute + -iest (superlative suffix).  Ultimately from Old French "flaüte," from Provençal "flaüt," of imitative origin.
