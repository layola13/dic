condor:/ˈkɒndɔːr/ (UK) /ˈkɑːndɔːr/ (US)| n. |秃鹰；神鹰|A very large American vulture with a bare head and neck, found in the Andes and the western mountains of North America.|例句：The condor soared high above the mountains.  秃鹰在群山上空翱翔。

例句：The California condor is an endangered species. 加州秃鹰是一种濒危物种。|近义词：vulture, eagle|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *cóndor*, from Quechua *kuntur*.
