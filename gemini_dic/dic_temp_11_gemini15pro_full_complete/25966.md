burrower:/ˈbʌrəʊər/ (UK), /ˈbɜːroʊər/ (US)| n.|穴居动物，挖洞的动物|An animal that makes a home by digging in the ground.|The badger is a nocturnal burrower. 獾是一种夜行动物，喜欢挖洞。|Synonyms: excavator, digger, tunneler|Antonyms: surface dweller|noun

burrow:/ˈbʌrəʊ/ (UK), /ˈbɜːroʊ/ (US)| n. vi. vt.|洞穴，挖洞，躲藏|A hole or tunnel dug by an animal as a dwelling, to dig such a hole, to hide oneself.|The rabbit disappeared down its burrow. 兔子消失在它的洞穴里。  The mole burrowed deep underground. 鼹鼠在地下深处挖洞。 He burrowed into the blankets for warmth. 他钻进毯子里取暖。|Synonyms: hole, den, tunnel, excavate, dig|Antonyms: emerge, surface|noun, verb intransitive, verb transitive
