gradualisms:/ˈɡrædʒuəlɪzəmz/
|n.|渐进主义；渐变论|The theory or practice of gradualism, especially in politics or social reform.  指渐进主义的理论或实践，尤指在政治或社会改革中。|The government's approach to economic reform has been one of gradualism. 政府的经济改革方法是渐进主义的。  The biologist advocated for gradualism in evolution.  这位生物学家提倡进化渐进主义。|近义词：gradual change, incrementalism, piecemeal change|反义词：revolution, sudden change, radicalism|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From gradual + -ism.  "gradual" comes from Late Latin "gradualis," meaning "by degrees," from Latin "gradus," meaning "step" or "degree." "-ism" is a suffix forming nouns that denote a doctrine, belief, or practice.
