execs:/ɪɡˈzɛks/| n.|高管，主管人员|A shortened form of "executives," referring to high-ranking officials in a company or organization.  They are responsible for making key decisions and managing operations.|The execs are meeting to discuss the new strategy.  高管们正在开会讨论新的战略。|近义词：managers, officers, leaders, administrators|反义词：subordinates, employees, staff|noun
