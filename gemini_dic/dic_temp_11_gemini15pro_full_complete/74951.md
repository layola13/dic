friablenesses:/ˈfraɪəblnəsɪz/
n.
易碎性；脆性；脆弱性
The quality or state of being friable; easily crumbled or broken.
The friablenesses of the ancient manuscript made it difficult to handle.  古代手稿的脆弱性使其难以处理。
friability, crumbliness, fragility
toughness, strength, resilience
