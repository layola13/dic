kipper:/ˈkɪpər/| n. |熏鱼,腌鱼|A whole herring that has been split open, gutted, salted, and smoked. |例句：We had kippers for breakfast. 我们早餐吃了熏鱼。

例句：The kipper was delicious with scrambled eggs.  熏鱼配炒蛋很美味。|近义词：smoked fish, bloater (especially a smoked herring)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "fresh fish")|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *kippere*, from Old Norse *kippa* (“to spawn; to tilt, tip, overturn”).  Originally referred to male salmon during spawning season, which develop a hooked lower jaw.  These fish were often preserved by kippering.
