circulars:/ˈsɜːrkjələrz/ (US) /ˈsɜːkjələz/ (UK)| n. | 通知，通告，印刷品 | A printed notice or advertisement that is distributed to a large number of people. | The company sent out circulars advertising their new product. 公司发出了宣传新产品的通告。| synonyms: leaflets, pamphlets, flyers, handbills, bulletins | antonyms: None (lack of a direct opposite for a physical object) | noun

circulars:/ˈsɜːrkjələrz/ (US) /ˈsɜːkjələz/ (UK)| n. | (尤指官方的)公文，通函 |  An official letter or memorandum sent to a number of people. | The government issued a circular to all schools about the new curriculum. 政府向所有学校发了一份关于新课程的通函。| synonyms: memoranda, notices, directives | antonyms: None (lack of a direct opposite for a type of communication) | noun

Etymology: From Late Middle English *circular*, from Latin *circularis* “round, circular,” from *circulus* “circle.” The noun sense of "a printed advertisement or notice" developed in the 19th century.
