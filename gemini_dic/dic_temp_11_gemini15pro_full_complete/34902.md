cliquiest: /ˈklɪkiɪst/ | adj. | 小圈子最严重的，最排外的 |  Superlative form of cliquey: belonging to a clique and excluding others.  |  That school is known for being the cliquiest in the area. 那所学校以是该地区小圈子最严重的而闻名。 |  The cliquiest group in the office always ate lunch together. 办公室里最排外的小团体总是聚在一起吃午饭。| 近义词：exclusive, snobbish, insular | 反义词：inclusive, welcoming, open | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From clique + -y + -est.  Clique comes from French, originally referring to a sharp sound, then to a group of people making a noise, and finally to a small exclusive group.
