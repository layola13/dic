guanaco:/ɡwəˈnɑːkoʊ/ | n. |原驼,骆马|A wild camelid mammal of the Andes, closely related to the llama.  A South American ruminant mammal (Lama guanicoe) related to and resembling the llama but smaller and lighter brown in color, believed to be the ancestor of the domestic llama.|例句：The guanaco roamed freely across the Patagonian steppe. 原驼在巴塔哥尼亚草原上自由漫步。

例句：Guanacos are known for their soft, warm wool.  原驼以其柔软温暖的羊毛而闻名。|近义词：llama, vicuña|反义词: N/A |词性:noun

Etymology: From Spanish *guanaco*, from Quechua *wanaku*.
