arachnophobias: /əˌræknəˈfoʊbiəz/ | n. |蜘蛛恐惧症|An abnormal or pathological fear of spiders, or anything resembling spiders.|  She suffers from severe arachnophobias and can't even look at pictures of spiders. 她患有严重的蜘蛛恐惧症，甚至无法看蜘蛛的照片。| Near Synonyms: arachnophobia, fear of spiders | Antonym: None (lack of fear isn't a single word antonym) | Noun
