epistaxis: /ˌɛpɪˈstæksɪs/ | n. | 鼻出血；鼻衄 |Nosebleed; bleeding from the nose.| He suffered from frequent epistaxis. 他经常鼻出血。|  Epistaxis can be caused by dry air.  干燥的空气会导致鼻出血。 | 近义词: nosebleed | 反义词:  (无明确反义词) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *epistaxis, from Ancient Greek ἐπισταξις (epistaxis)  "a dropping upon," from ἐπιστάζω (epistázō) "to drop upon," from ἐπί (epí) "upon" + στάζω (stázō) "to drip."
