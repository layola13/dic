discords:/ˈdɪskɔːrdz/
n.|不和，纷争，意见不合|lack of agreement or harmony between people or things; disagreement|The political discords within the country led to civil war. 国内政治纷争导致了内战。|近义词：disagreements, conflicts, disputes, clashes, friction|反义词：harmony, agreement, concord, unity|词性:名词

discords:/ˈdɪskɔːrdz/
v. (third-person singular simple present discords, present participle discording, simple past and past participle discorded)|不一致，不协调|(of a thing) be different or inconsistent; clash; (of people) disagree|Her views discorded sharply with his. 她的观点与他的大相径庭。|近义词：clash, conflict, disagree, jar|反义词：harmonize, agree, accord, match|词性:动词
