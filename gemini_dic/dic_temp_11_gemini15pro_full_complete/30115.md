cedars:/ˈsiːdərz/| n.|雪松；雪松木|An evergreen coniferous tree with bunches of needles and barrel-shaped cones.  |The cedars of Lebanon are famous for their size and longevity. 黎巴嫩的雪松以其大小和长寿而闻名。|近义词：conifer, evergreen|反义词: deciduous tree (落叶树) |词性:noun


cedars:/ˈsiːdərz/| n.|雪松；雪松木|The aromatic wood of a cedar tree, used for making furniture, chests, etc. |The chest was made of fragrant cedar. 这个箱子是用芬芳的雪松木制成的。|近义词：cedarwood|反义词: N/A |词性:noun

词源: From Old English *ceder, from Old French cedre, from Latin cedrus, from Greek kedros. Ultimately of Semitic origin.
