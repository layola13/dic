deal:/diːl/ | n. | 交易，协议，大量 | An agreement or arrangement, especially in business or politics; a large amount or number of something. |  例句：We made a deal with the supplier. 我们和供应商达成了协议。| 近义词：agreement, bargain, arrangement, transaction | 反义词：disagreement, dispute | 词性：名词

deal:/diːl/ | v. | 处理，分配，发牌 | To distribute cards to players in a card game; to give out or administer something; to cope with or handle a situation. | 例句：He dealt the cards. 他发了牌。 | 近义词：handle, cope, distribute, administer | 反义词：neglect, ignore | 词性：动词

deals:/diːlz/ | n. | 交易，协议，大量（deal的复数形式） | Agreements or arrangements, especially in business or politics; large amounts or numbers of something. | 例句：She's always making deals. 她总是在做交易。 | 近义词：agreements, bargains, arrangements, transactions | 反义词：disagreements, disputes | 词性：名词
