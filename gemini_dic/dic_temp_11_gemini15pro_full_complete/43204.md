crabfaced:/ˈkræbˌfeɪst/| adj.|脾气暴躁的，阴沉的|Having a bad-tempered or sullen face; looking grumpy or ill-humored.  It suggests a face that is pinched and sour, like a crab's. |He gave me a crabfaced look when I asked him for a favor. 他当我请求他帮忙时，给了我一个阴沉的表情。|近义词：grumpy, sullen, surly, ill-tempered, churlish|反义词：cheerful, pleasant, amiable, good-natured|词性:形容词
crab:/kræb/| n. |螃蟹|A crustacean with a broad, flat body and five pairs of legs, the first pair of which are modified as pincers.|We caught some crabs at the beach. 我们在海滩上抓了一些螃蟹。
