exigency:/ˈɛksɪdʒənsi/ /ˈɛkzɪdʒənsi/| n. |紧急情况；迫切需要；苛求|An exigency is an urgent need or demand.  It describes a situation that requires immediate attention and action.|例句：The exigency of the situation demanded immediate action.  情况的紧急性需要立即采取行动。

例句：The hospital was built in response to the exigency of the growing population. 这家医院是为了应对不断增长的人口带来的迫切需求而建造的。|近义词：emergency, urgency, necessity, crisis, demand|反义词:calm, peace, tranquility|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *exigence, from Middle French exigence, from Latin exigentia (“urgency, need”), from exigēns, present active participle of exigō (“I demand, require, exact”), from ex- (“out”) + agō (“I drive, lead, act”).
