manacles: /ˈmænəklz/ (US) /ˈmænəklz/ (UK)
n.|手铐，镣铐|Metal rings for fastening someone's hands or feet, typically used on prisoners.|The police placed manacles on the suspect. 警察给嫌疑犯戴上了手铐。|
近义词: handcuffs, shackles, restraints, chains
反义词: freedom, liberation
词性: 名词

manacle: /ˈmænəkl/ (US) /ˈmænəkl/ (UK)
vt.|给…戴上手铐，束缚|To restrain someone with manacles; to confine or restrict.|His arms were manacled behind his back. 他的双臂被铐在背后。|
近义词: restrain, restrict, shackle, fetter, bind
反义词: free, liberate, unfetter, release
词性: 动词

词源: From Old French "manicle," diminutive of "manche" (handle, sleeve), from Latin "manica" (long sleeve, gauntlet).
