accessoriness:/əkˈsɛsərɪnɪs/
n.
附属品；配件；次要性
The state or quality of being accessory or subordinate.  Being an accessory or addition, especially one that is not essential.
The accessoriness of the decorations made the room feel cluttered. 装饰品的附属性让房间感觉很杂乱。
Near synonyms:  secondary importance,  non-essential nature,  subordinate status,  peripheral nature,
Antonyms:  essential nature, primary importance,  centrality,  integral nature,
noun
