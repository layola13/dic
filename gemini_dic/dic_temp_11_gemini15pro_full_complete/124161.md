mortrew:/ˈmɔːrtər/ (Also /ˈmɔːrtruː/) | n. | 灰浆; 砂浆; (旧时用作食物的)鸡肉泥, 肉酱 | A mixture of lime or cement, sand, and water, used in building to bond bricks or stones.  A paste formerly used as food, made by pounding meat in a mortar. |  The castle walls were built with strong mortrew. 城堡的墙壁是用坚固的灰浆建造的。 | The king dined on mortrew of pheasant. 国王享用了野鸡肉酱。 | Synonyms: mortar, cement, plaster (for building); paste, puree (for food) | Antonyms: N/A | noun
