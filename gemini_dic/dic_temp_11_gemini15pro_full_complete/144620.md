outwind:/ˌaʊtˈwɪnd/
verb
解开；展开；放松
to unwind, to unroll, to relax
The sailor began to outwind the rope. 水手开始解开绳子。
近义词: unwind, unroll, unfurl, relax
反义词: wind, tighten, tense
词性: verb

outwind:/ˌaʊtˈwɪnd/
verb
呼出; 吹出
to exhale, to breathe out
He outwound a long sigh of relief. 他长长地舒了一口气。
近义词: exhale, breathe out
反义词: inhale, breathe in
词性: verb
