antipodean: /ˌæntɪˈpəʊdiən/ /ˌæntɪˈpoʊdiən/
antipodean: adj. n.|对跖的，对跖地的；对跖人|Relating to or inhabiting the antipodes; a person who lives on the opposite side of the world from oneself.|例句：The antipodean seasons are opposite to ours. 对跖地的季节与我们相反。|近义词：opposite, contrary, inverse|反义词:local, nearby|词性:形容词,名词
