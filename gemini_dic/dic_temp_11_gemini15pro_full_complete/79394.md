glidings:/ˈɡlaɪdɪŋz/| n.|滑翔，滑行|The smooth glidings of the birds were mesmerizing. 鸟儿平稳的滑翔令人着迷。|例句：The gliders' glidings were graceful and effortless. 滑翔机的滑行优美而毫不费力。

例句：The glidings of the skaters on the ice were breathtaking. 滑冰者在冰上的滑行令人叹为观止。|近义词：soaring, drifting, floating|反义词：falling, plummeting|词性：名词

词源：From the verb "glide" + "-ing" (present participle ending) + "-s" (plural marker). "Glide" comes from Old English "glidan."
