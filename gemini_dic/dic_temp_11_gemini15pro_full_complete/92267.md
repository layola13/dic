hypervigilantly:/ˌhaɪpərˈvɪdʒɪləntli/ | adv. |过度警觉地，高度警惕地 | In a way that is excessively watchful and alert, often due to anxiety or trauma. |  She scanned the crowd hypervigilantly, her hand never leaving her pepper spray. 她高度警惕地扫视着人群，手从未离开过她的胡椒喷雾。| 近义词: watchfully, cautiously, alertly | 反义词: carelessly, negligently, inattentively | 词性: 副词

词源:
From hyper- + vigilant + -ly.  "Hyper-" comes from Greek meaning "over, above, beyond," and "vigilant" comes from Latin "vigilans," present participle of "vigilare" meaning "to watch, keep awake."
