outdrink: /ˌaʊtˈdrɪŋk/
verb
胜过…的酒量；比…喝得更多
To drink more alcohol than someone else.
He can outdrink anyone in the bar. 他比酒吧里的任何人都能喝。
I challenged him to a drinking contest, but he easily outdrink me. 我向他挑战喝酒比赛，但他轻易地就赢了我。
Synonyms: drink under the table, best, outmatch
Antonyms: be outdrunk by, lose to (in drinking)
词性: 动词
