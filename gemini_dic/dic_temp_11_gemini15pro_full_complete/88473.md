histopathologist:/ˌhɪstoʊpəˈθɒlədʒɪst/| n. |组织病理学家|A specialist in the study of diseased tissues.|例句：The histopathologist examined the biopsy sample under a microscope. 组织病理学家在显微镜下检查了活检样本。

例句：The histopathologist's report confirmed the presence of cancer cells. 组织病理学家的报告证实了癌细胞的存在。

近义词：pathologist|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From histo- + pathology + -ist.  Histo- comes from Greek "histos" meaning "tissue." Pathology comes from Greek "pathos" meaning "suffering" and "logia" meaning "study."  -ist is a suffix denoting a specialist in a particular field.
