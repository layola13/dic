laplander:/ˈlæplændər/ | n. | 拉普兰人；拉普兰地区居民 | A member of an indigenous people living in Lapland, a region in northern Europe. | Example 1: The Laplander herded his reindeer across the snowy plains.  拉普兰人赶着他的驯鹿穿过雪原。

Example 2:  Many Laplanders still maintain their traditional way of life. 许多拉普兰人仍然保持着他们的传统生活方式。| 近义词: Sami (更常用的称呼) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun


Etymology:  From Lapland + -er.  Lapland refers to the region these people inhabit, and -er is a suffix denoting a person associated with a place.
