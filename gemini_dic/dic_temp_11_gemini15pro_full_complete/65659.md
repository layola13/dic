evasion:/ɪˈveɪʒn/| n.|逃避，规避，躲避|The act of evading something, especially a responsibility or duty. |He was fined for tax evasion. 他因逃税被罚款。|Synonyms: avoidance, dodging, eluding, escaping|Antonyms: confrontation, facing, meeting|noun

evasive:/ɪˈveɪsɪv/| adj.|逃避的，规避的，躲闪的|Tending to avoid commitment or self-revelation, especially by responding only indirectly. |She gave an evasive answer. 她给了一个模棱两可的答案。|Synonyms: elusive, ambiguous, equivocal|Antonyms: direct, forthright, honest|adjective
