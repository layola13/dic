boggish:/ˈbɒɡɪʃ/ (UK) /ˈbɑːɡɪʃ/ (US)| adj.|沼泽的；泥泞的；像沼泽的|Resembling a bog; marshy; swampy.  Characterized by the presence of bogs or swamps.|The ground was boggish and difficult to walk on.  这块地沼泽遍布，难以行走。

The boggish terrain made it hard for the soldiers to advance. 泥泞的地形使士兵们难以前进。|近义词：marshy, swampy, miry, quaggy|反义词：dry, arid, firm|词性：形容词
