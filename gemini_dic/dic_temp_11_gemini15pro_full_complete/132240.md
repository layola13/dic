nondeciduousness:/ˌnɒndɪˈsɪdʒuəsnəs/
|n.|常绿性；不落叶性|The quality or state of being nondeciduous; the retention of leaves throughout the year.|The nondeciduousness of pine trees allows them to photosynthesize year-round.|近义词:evergreenness, persistence of foliage|反义词:deciduousness|词性:名词

nondeciduous:/ˌnɒndɪˈsɪdʒuəs/
|adj.|常绿的；不落叶的|Not deciduous; retaining leaves throughout the year.|Pine trees are nondeciduous and remain green all year.|近义词:evergreen, persistent|反义词:deciduous|词性:形容词
