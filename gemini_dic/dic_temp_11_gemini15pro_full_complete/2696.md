adroitly:/əˈdrɔɪtli/
adverb
熟练地，灵巧地
In a skillful and clever way, especially in handling or manipulating things.
She adroitly maneuvered the car through the narrow streets. 她熟练地驾驶着汽车穿过狭窄的街道。
近义词: skillfully, deftly, expertly, nimbly
反义词: clumsily, awkwardly, ineptly
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From French *adroitement*, from *adroit* "skillful," from *à droit* "according to right, properly," from Latin *ad directum* "to the right," from *ad* "to" + *directum* "straight," past participle of *dirigere* "to direct."
