jittery:/ˈdʒɪtəri/| adj.|紧张不安的，战战兢兢的|Nervous or anxious, especially in a restless or jumpy way.|例句：He felt jittery before the big game.  他在大赛前感到紧张不安。|例句：She was jittery with excitement. 她兴奋得坐立不安。|近义词：nervous, anxious, edgy, apprehensive, restless, fidgety|反义词：calm, relaxed, composed, serene|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From "jitter" (meaning to act nervously or restlessly) + "-y" (adjective-forming suffix).  "Jitter" itself likely originates from an imitative word suggesting trembling or shaking.
