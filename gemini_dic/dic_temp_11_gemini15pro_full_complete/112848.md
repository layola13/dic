luckies:/ˈlʌkiz/| n.|幸运儿，幸运的东西|A slang term, often used playfully or ironically, referring to people who are considered fortunate or the things that bring them luck.  It's the plural form of "lucky," used as a noun.|例句：The luckies won the lottery. 那些幸运儿中了彩票。|近义词：fortunate ones, winners|反义词:unlucky ones, losers|词性:noun
