contingent:/kənˈtɪndʒənt/| n. adj.|分遣队，代表团，依情况而定的|A group of people representing a larger group or country, especially at a meeting or conference; dependent on something that may or may not happen.|The Kansas contingent at the convention was small.  堪萨斯州出席会议的代表团人数很少。|synonyms: detachment, group, delegation, squad, troop|antonyms: whole, entirety, total|noun

contingent:/kənˈtɪndʒənt/| adj.|依情况而定的，偶然的|Dependent on something else that might or might not happen.|Our plans are contingent on the weather. 我们的计划取决于天气。|synonyms: conditional, dependent, subject to, given|antonyms: independent, unconditional, certain, absolute|adjective
