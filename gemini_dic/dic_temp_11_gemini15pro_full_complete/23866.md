breadwinning:/ˈbredˌwɪnɪŋ/| n. adj.|养家糊口，挣钱养家|The act of earning money to support a family.  The adjective form describes someone who earns the primary income for their family.|例句：She is the primary breadwinner for her family.  她是家里的主要经济支柱。|近义词：supporting, providing, earning|反义词:dependent, unemployed|词性:noun, adjective

词源: "bread" (meaning food or livelihood) + "winning" (from "win," meaning to gain or obtain).  The term originally referred to earning bread for the family.
