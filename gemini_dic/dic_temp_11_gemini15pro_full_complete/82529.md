guacharo:/ɡwəˈtʃɑːroʊ/| n. |瓜查罗；油鸱|A nocturnal, cave-dwelling bird (Steatornis caripensis) of northern South America, related to the nightjars, that navigates by echolocation and feeds on fruit.|例句：The guacharo is the only nocturnal fruit-eating bird. 瓜查罗是唯一一种夜间食果的鸟。

例句：Guacharos use echolocation to navigate in the dark caves where they live.  油鸱利用回声定位在它们居住的黑暗洞穴中导航。|近义词：oilbird|反义词: (No specific antonym for a species of bird)|词性:noun
