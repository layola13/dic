insubmission:/ɪn səbˈmɪʃən/
n.|顺从，服从，屈服|the act of accepting or yielding to a superior force or to the will or authority of another person|例句：They lived in submission to a cruel dictator. 他们屈服于一个残暴的独裁者的统治。|例句：He hung his head in submission. 他低着头，表示屈服。|近义词：obedience, compliance, subservience|反义词：rebellion, defiance, resistance|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *submissionem (nominative submissio), noun of action from past-participle stem of submittere “to lower, reduce, let down, yield,” from sub- “under” + mittere “to send, let go.”
