词源:  "hidey-hole" is a compound word formed from "hide" (meaning to conceal oneself) and "hole" (referring to a small, enclosed space).  The "-y" suffix is a diminutive, suggesting a small or cozy hiding place.  It has been in use since the late 19th century.
