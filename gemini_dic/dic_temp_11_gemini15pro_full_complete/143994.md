outrating:/ˌaʊtˈreɪtɪŋ/
verb
超过；胜过
surpassing in rating or ranking.
Our new product is outrating the competition in customer satisfaction surveys. 我们的新产品在客户满意度调查中超过了竞争对手。
outrank, outperform, exceed, surpass
be outrated by, fall behind, lag behind
v.
