annualize:/ˈænjuəlaɪz/
verb
使年度化；按年计算
to calculate the rate of something (such as a return on an investment or an interest rate) over a period of one year.
The company annualized its quarterly earnings.  这家公司将其季度收益按年计算。
The bank annualizes interest rates on savings accounts. 银行按年计算储蓄账户的利率。
synonyms: calculate yearly, compute annually, project annually
antonyms: N/A
词性: 动词
