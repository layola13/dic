laud:/lɔːd/ | v. n. | 赞美，称赞；赞歌，颂歌 | to praise someone or something highly | The critics lauded the performance for its creativity and energy.  评论家们称赞该表演富有创造力和活力。 |  They lauded her for her courage. 他们称赞她的勇气。 | 近义词: praise, extol, acclaim, commend | 反义词: criticize, condemn, denounce | 词性: 动词, 名词

词源: From Latin *laudāre*, present active infinitive of *laudō* (“I praise”), from Proto-Italic *laudō*, from Proto-Indo-European *leh₁wdʰ- (“to speak, mention”).
