knitwear: /ˈnɪtwɛːr/
n.
针织品，针织衣物
Garments made from knitted yarn or fabric.
She bought a new set of knitwear for the winter. 她为冬天买了一套新的针织衣物。
The shop specializes in high-quality knitwear. 这家商店专营高品质的针织品。
近义词:  knitted garments, knits
反义词:  woven garments
