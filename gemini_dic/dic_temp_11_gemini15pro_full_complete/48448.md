deeding:/ˈdiːdɪŋ/
n.
the act of transferring ownership of property
the formal transfer of ownership of something, especially real estate, by deed.
The deeding of the property to his son was completed last week. 上周，他把房产转让给了他的儿子。
近义词：conveyance, transfer, granting
反义词：acquisition, receiving
