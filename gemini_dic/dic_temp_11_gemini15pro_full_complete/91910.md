hyperlustrous:/ˌhaɪpərˈlʌstrəs/
adj.
极度光亮的，极度有光泽的
Having an extremely bright or shiny surface; very lustrous.
The newly polished car had a hyperlustrous finish.  这辆刚抛光过的汽车有着极度光亮的外观。
The diamond was hyperlustrous, sparkling under the lights.  这颗钻石闪耀着极强的光泽，在灯光下熠熠生辉。

近义词:  radiant, gleaming, shimmering, dazzling, brilliant
反义词: dull, matte, lackluster

词性: adjective
词源: "hyper-" (Greek, meaning "over, beyond") + "lustrous" (from Latin "lustrare," meaning "to brighten, illuminate")
