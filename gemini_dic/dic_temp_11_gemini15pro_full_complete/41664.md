correlatively:/ˌkɔːrəˈleɪtɪvli/
adv.|相关地，关联地|In a correlatively linked or corresponding manner.  Describing actions, events, or states that occur in relation to each other.|The amount of rainfall increases correlatively with the temperature.  降雨量随着温度的升高而相应增加。|近义词：correspondingly, accordingly, respectively|反义词：independently, separately|词性: 副词

correlate:/ˈkɔːrəleɪt/
v.|使…相互关联；联系|To establish a mutual relationship or connection between.|Scientists are trying to correlate smoking with lung cancer. 科学家们正试图将吸烟与肺癌联系起来。|近义词：connect, relate, link|反义词：separate, disconnect|词性: 动词

correlation:/ˌkɔːrəˈleɪʃn/
n.|相互关系；关联|A mutual relationship or connection between two or more things.|There is a strong correlation between smoking and lung cancer. 吸烟和肺癌之间有很强的相关性。|近义词：relationship, connection, link|反义词：independence, separation|词性: 名词
