carpophagous:/kɑːrˈpɒfəɡəs/
adjective
食果的；果食性的
(of an animal) feeding on fruit.
The flying fox is a carpophagous mammal.  飞狐是一种食果的哺乳动物。
Many tropical birds are carpophagous. 许多热带鸟类都是食果的。
frugivorous
insectivorous, carnivorous, herbivorous
