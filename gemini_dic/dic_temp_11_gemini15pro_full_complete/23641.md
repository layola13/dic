branded:/ˈbrændɪd/| adj.|烙印的；打上烙印的；铭刻的；（尤指不光彩地）被描述为…的|Marked with a brand; characterized or labeled, often in a negative way.|The cattle were branded with the rancher's mark. 牛被打上了牧场主的烙印。| The politician was branded a liar.  这个政客被贴上了骗子的标签。|synonyms: labeled, marked, stigmatized, tagged|antonyms: unbranded, unmarked, anonymous|adjective
