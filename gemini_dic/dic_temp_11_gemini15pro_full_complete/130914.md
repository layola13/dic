nonascendencies:/ˌnɒnəˈsɛndənsiz/ | n. | 非优势，非支配地位 |  Lack of ascendancy or dominance; a state of not being in a position of power or influence.  This word is not commonly used and may be considered archaic or even non-standard. It's constructed by adding the prefix "non-" to "ascendancy." |  The nonascendencies of the smaller tribes led to their eventual absorption by the larger nation.  较小部落的非支配地位导致它们最终被更大的国家吞并。 | Near synonyms: subordination, subjection, inferiority | Antonyms: ascendancy, dominance, supremacy | noun
