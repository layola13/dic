confesser:/kənˈfɛsər/| n.|忏悔者，告解者|A person who confesses, especially one who confesses sins to a priest.|The confesser listened patiently to the penitent's story. 忏悔者耐心地听着忏悔者的故事。|近义词：penitent, admitter|反义词：liar, denier|noun

confessor:/kənˈfɛsər/| n.|听忏悔的神父|A priest who hears confessions and grants absolution.|He went to his confessor to seek forgiveness. 他去向他的听忏悔神父寻求宽恕。|近义词：priest, chaplain|反义词：none|noun
