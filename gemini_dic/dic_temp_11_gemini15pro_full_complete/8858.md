antifeminineness:/ˌæntiˈfɛmɪnɪnɪsnəs/
|n.| 反女性气质；反对女性主义的行为或特质 |The antifeminineness of her attire and demeanor was striking. 她的穿着和举止的非女性气质令人震惊。| Her antifeminineness manifested in her rejection of traditional gender roles. 她的反女性气质体现在她对传统性别角色的拒绝上. | 近义词: misogyny, sexism | 反义词: feminism, femininity | 词性: noun

antifeminine:/ˌæntiˈfɛmɪnɪn/
|adj.| 反女性的；反对女性气质的 | His antifeminine views sparked outrage among his colleagues. 他的反女性观点激怒了他的同事们。|  The group held antifeminine protests. 该团体举行了反女权主义抗议活动。| 近义词: misogynistic, sexist | 反义词: feminist, feminine | 词性: adjective
