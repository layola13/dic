dilemmatically:/ˌdɪləˈmætɪkli/| adv.|进退两难地，左右为难地|In a way that relates to or involves a dilemma; involving a difficult choice between two undesirable options.|She was dilemmatically caught between her loyalty to her family and her desire for independence. 她进退两难地夹在对家庭的忠诚和对独立的渴望之间。|近义词：paradoxically, awkwardly, difficultly|反义词：easily, simply|词性:副词

dilemma:/dɪˈlɛmə/| n.|困境，进退两难|A situation in which a difficult choice has to be made between two or more alternatives, especially equally undesirable ones.|He faced the dilemma of whether to stay in his secure job or take a risk and start his own business. 他面临着是留在稳定的工作还是冒险创业的两难境地。|近义词：predicament, quandary, problem|反义词：solution, advantage|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin *dilemma, from Greek dílēmma "double proposition," from di- "two" + lēmma "premise, anything received or taken," from lambánein "to take."
