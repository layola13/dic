mismate:/ˌmɪsˈmeɪt/

mismate:/ˌmɪsˈmeɪt/
v.|错误地搭配，使不匹配|To match or pair wrongly or unsuitably.|The coach mismated the players, resulting in a disastrous game.|synonyms: mismatch, pair wrongly|antonyms: match, pair correctly|verb
