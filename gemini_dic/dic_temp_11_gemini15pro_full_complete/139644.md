occulters:/əˈkʌltərz/
n.
遮挡物，掩星器
Devices or objects that obscure something, especially celestial bodies, from view.
例句：The astronomers used occulters to block the light from the star so they could study the fainter orbiting planets.  天文学家用遮光器挡住恒星的光，以便研究较暗的绕行行星。

例句：The moon acted as an occulter during the solar eclipse. 在日食期间，月亮充当了遮挡物。
近义词：blockers, screens, shields
反义词：revealers
