amoebaeum:/əˌmiːˈbiːəm/ | n. | 变形虫室；变形虫状结构 | A cavity in a calcareous shell occupied by a protozoan. |  The researchers discovered a complex amoebaeum within the shell of the foraminifera. 研究人员在有孔虫的壳内发现了一个复杂的变形虫室。 |  The radiolarian's amoebaeum housed the symbiotic algae.  放射虫的变形虫状结构容纳了共生的藻类。 |近义词: chamber, cavity | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "solid structure") | noun

amoeba:/əˈmiːbə/ | n. | 变形虫 | A single-celled animal that catches food and moves about by extending fingerlike projections of protoplasm.  | Amoebas are fascinating organisms to study under a microscope. 在显微镜下研究变形虫是很有趣的。 |  The pond water teemed with amoebas and other microorganisms. 池塘里的水充满了变形虫和其他微生物。 | 近义词: protozoan,  rhizopod | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | noun
