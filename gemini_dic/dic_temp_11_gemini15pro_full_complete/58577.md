dulcified:/ˈdʌlsɪfaɪd/
adjective
变甜的，加糖的，缓和的，减轻的
made sweeter or milder; softened.
The baker dulcified the tart filling with a touch of honey.  面包师用少许蜂蜜使馅饼馅料变甜。
近义词:sweetened, softened, mitigated, tempered
反义词:bittered, harshened, aggravated
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *dulcificātus, past participle of dulcificāre (“to sweeten”), from Latin dulcis (“sweet”) + -ficāre, from facere (“to make”).
