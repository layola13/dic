nebbishy: /ˈnɛbɪʃi/ (American English) /ˈnɛbɪʃi/ (British English)
| adj. | 窝囊的；没用的；缺乏自信的；胆怯的 | Timid, meek, or ineffectual; lacking in confidence and assertiveness.  Characterized by a nervous or awkward manner. | He plays a nebbishy accountant in the new sitcom. 他在新情景喜剧中扮演一个窝囊的会计。|  近义词: timid, meek, mousy, insecure, awkward, unassertive, ineffectual | 反义词: confident, assertive, bold, aggressive, strong | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From Yiddish נעבעך (nebekh, "poor thing," "unfortunately"), ultimately from Hebrew.
