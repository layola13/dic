dismounting:/dɪˈsmaʊntɪŋ/
n.|下马，下车，拆卸|The dismounting of the artillery piece took several hours.  拆卸这门大炮花了几个小时。|近义词：getting off, alighting, removal|反义词:mounting, embarking|词性:名词
dismount:/dɪsˈmaʊnt/
v.|下马，下车，拆卸|She dismounted gracefully from her horse. 她优雅地下了马。|近义词：get off, alight, remove|反义词:mount, embark|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *desmonter, from des- (prefix indicating reversal) + monter (“to mount”).
