hyperrealists:/ˌhaɪpərˈriːəlɪsts/
n.|超现实主义者|Artists who create hyperrealistic works often focus on depicting subjects with extreme detail, mimicking the appearance of a high-resolution photograph.|The hyperrealists of the 1970s revolutionized painting.  20世纪70年代的超现实主义者彻底改变了绘画。|近义词:photorealists|反义词:impressionists|词性:名词

hyperrealistic:/ˌhaɪpərˌriːəˈlɪstɪk/
adj.|超现实主义的|Hyperrealistic art is characterized by its meticulous attention to detail and its attempt to recreate reality as accurately as possible.|The hyperrealistic sculptures were so lifelike they were unsettling.  这些超现实主义的雕塑栩栩如生，令人不安。|近义词:photorealistic|反义词:impressionistic|词性:形容词
