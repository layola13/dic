desperadoes: /ˌdespəˈrɑːdoʊz/ (US), /ˌdespəˈreɪdəʊz/ (UK)| n. |亡命之徒；不顾一切的人|A desperate person, often a criminal, who is willing to take any risk. |例句：The desperadoes robbed the bank and escaped on horseback. 亡命之徒抢劫了银行，骑马逃跑了。

例句：A group of desperadoes held up the stagecoach. 一群亡命之徒拦截了驿站马车。


近义词：outlaw, bandit, gangster, rogue, villain|反义词:law-abiding citizen, conformist|词性:名词
