melanated:/ˈmɛləˌneɪtɪd/| adj.|皮肤黝黑的，有黑色素的|Having darker skin pigmentation, typically used to describe people of African descent.  It emphasizes and celebrates the richness of melanin in their skin.|例句：She's a beautiful, melanated queen. 她是一位美丽的，肤色黝黑的女王。

例句：The melanated models graced the runway with their presence. 这些肤色黝黑的模特们为T台增色不少。|近义词：dark-skinned, Black, African American (in the US context) |反义词:light-skinned, pale |词性:adjective
