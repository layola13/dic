demigoddess:/ˌdɛmiˈɡɒdɪs/ | n. | 半女神 | A female demigod, a being who is partly divine and partly human, often the offspring of a god and a mortal. |  Heracles, son of Zeus, was a demigod, but Athena, daughter of Zeus, was a demigoddess.  赫拉克勒斯，宙斯的儿子，是一位半神，而雅典娜，宙斯的女儿，是一位半女神。 |  The ancient Greeks believed in demigoddesses who possessed great power and beauty. 古代希腊人相信半女神拥有强大的力量和美丽。 |  goddess, divine being, minor deity | mortal, human | noun
