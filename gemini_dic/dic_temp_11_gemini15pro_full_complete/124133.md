morticed:/ˈmɔːrtɪst/| adj.| 榫接的，嵌入的 |Joined by a mortise and tenon.  由榫眼和榫头连接的。| The table legs are morticed into the frame. 桌腿用榫眼嵌入框架中。|近义词：joined, connected, interlocked|反义词: separated, detached| adjective

mortise:/ˈmɔːrtɪs/| n. vt.|榫眼，榫孔；开榫眼，嵌入|A hole or recess cut into a piece of timber, into which a tenon fits to form a joint. 在一块木材上切割的孔或凹槽，榫头插入其中以形成接头。|  He carefully mortised the two pieces of wood together. 他小心翼翼地将两块木头榫接在一起。|近义词：slot, groove, recess|反义词: tenon, projection| noun, verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *mortaise, from mort "dead" (from Latin mortuus), perhaps from the notion of a dead-end hole.  源自古法语 *mortaise，来自 mort “死的”（来自拉丁语 mortuus），可能来自死胡同的概念。
