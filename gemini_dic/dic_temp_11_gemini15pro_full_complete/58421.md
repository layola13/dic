ducatoon:/dʌkəˈtuːn/
n.
达卡特（旧时欧洲金币）
A silver coin of the Spanish Netherlands, equal to 3 guilders.
He paid for the painting with a handful of ducatoons. 他用一把达卡特付了这幅画的钱。
近义词:guilder, florin
反义词:(无直接反义词，可用 modern currency 现代货币)
词性:名词
