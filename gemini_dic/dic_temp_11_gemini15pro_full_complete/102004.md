isocyanuric:/ˌaɪsoʊsaɪəˈnjʊərɪk/ | adj. |异氰尿酸的；异氰脲酸的 | Relating to or denoting isocyanuric acid or its derivatives.  Isocyanuric acid is a chemical compound with the formula (CNOH)₃.
