ecologists:/iˈkɒlədʒɪsts/ /iˈkɑːlədʒɪsts/| n. |生态学家|A scientist who studies the relationships between organisms and their environment.| Ecologists are studying the impact of climate change on the rainforest. 生态学家正在研究气候变化对雨林的影响。|近义词：environmental scientists, conservationists|反义词: N/A| noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From ecology + -ist.  Ecology comes from Greek *oikos* "house, dwelling place" + -logy "study of."
