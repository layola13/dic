ingeniate:/ɪnˈdʒiːnieɪt/ | v. | 设计；发明；创造；策划 | To cleverly invent or design something. |  He ingeniously devised a way to escape. 他巧妙地设计了一个逃跑的办法。

He ingeniously engineered a solution to the problem. 他巧妙地设计了一个解决问题的方案。 | 近义词: devise, contrive, invent, create, design, engineer, fabricate | 反义词: copy, imitate, plagiarize | 词性: 动词

词源: From Latin *ingeniāre, present active infinitive of *ingeniō (“to devise, contrive”), from ingenium (“natural capacity, talent, intelligence, wit”).
