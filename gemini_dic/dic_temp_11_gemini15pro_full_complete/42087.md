cotransduce:/ˌkoʊtrænsˈdjuːs/
verb
共同转导
to transfer genetic material from one bacterium to another along with other genetic material.
Bacteriophage P1 can cotransduce markers on the E. coli chromosome.  P1噬菌体可以共同转导大肠杆菌染色体上的标记。
The genes were cotransduced at a high frequency. 这些基因以高频率共同转导。
Synonyms: cotransfer
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider "individually transfer" or "separately transfer")
词性: verb
