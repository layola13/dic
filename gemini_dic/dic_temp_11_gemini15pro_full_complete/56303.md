doggrels:/ˈdɒɡrəlz/ (US) /ˈdɑːɡrəlz/| n. |打油诗，蹩脚的诗|Trivial, poorly constructed verse, often humorous and irregular in rhythm and rhyme.  Usually short and simple. |例句：He entertained the children with silly doggrels. 他用愚蠢的打油诗逗孩子们开心。

例句：The newspaper published some humorous doggrels about the local election. 报纸刊登了一些关于地方选举的幽默打油诗。|近义词：verse, rhymes, jingles, ditties|反义词:epic, masterpiece, sonnet|词性:noun
