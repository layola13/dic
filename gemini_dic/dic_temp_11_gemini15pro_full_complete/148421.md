This happens when the rear wheels lose traction and slideout from the intended path, causing the car's rear end to swing around. It's a common problem under various driving conditions, especially at high speedsor on slippery surfaces like ice or snow. It’s the opposite of understeer. |例句：The car oversteered on the bend, nearly causing an accident.  (汽车在弯道上转向过度，差点导致事故。)  The driver lost control due to oversteering in the icy conditions.
