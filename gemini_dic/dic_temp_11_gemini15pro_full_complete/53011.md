dinosaurs:/ˈdaɪnəsɔːrz/| n. |恐龙|Extinct reptiles of the Mesozoic Era, some of which were gigantic.|例句：Dinosaurs roamed the earth millions of years ago. 数百万年前，恐龙在地球上漫游。

例句：The museum has a fascinating exhibit of dinosaur skeletons.  博物馆里有一个引人入胜的恐龙骨骼展览。|近义词：prehistoric reptiles|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑) modern animals|词性:noun
