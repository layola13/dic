conveyable:/kənˈveɪəbl/
adjective
可传达的，可表达的
Able to be conveyed or communicated.
His message was clear and conveyable to everyone in the room. 他的信息清晰明了，房间里的每个人都能理解。
The information is easily conveyable in a simple chart.  这些信息可以很容易地在一个简单的图表中传达。
communicable, transferable, expressible
incommunicable, inexpressible
adjective
