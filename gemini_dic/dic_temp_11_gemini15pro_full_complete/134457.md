nonjoiner:/ˌnɒnˈdʒɔɪnə(r)/
noun
非参加者；不加入者
A person who does not join a group, organization, or activity.
Example:  The nonjoiners chose to remain independent. 非参加者选择保持独立。

Example:  He was a nonjoiner, preferring solitary pursuits. 他是一个不加入者，更喜欢独自追求。
synonym: abstainer, dissenter, recusant, holdout
antonym:  joiner, member, participant, subscriber
noun

Etymology:  non- + joiner.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "joiner" refers to someone who joins.
