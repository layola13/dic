interarticular:/ˌɪntərɑːˈtɪkjʊlər/| adj.|关节间的|Located between two joints or articular surfaces.  Relating to or situated between joints.|The interarticular cartilage cushions the bones in the knee.|近义词：intra-articular|反义词: extra-articular|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From Latin *inter* (between) + *articularis* (pertaining to a joint), from *articulus* (joint).
