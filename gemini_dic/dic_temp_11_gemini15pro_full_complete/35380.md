co-officials:/koʊ əˈfɪʃəlz/
n.
共同官员；同僚
officials who hold the same office or position or who work together in an official capacity.
The co-officials worked together on the project.  共同官员一起进行了这个项目。
The two co-officials shared an office. 两位同僚共用一间办公室。
synonyms: colleagues, peers, fellow officers
antonyms: rivals, opponents
noun
