anlaut:/ˈɑːnlaʊt/
n.
onset (of a syllable or word); initial sound
The anlaut of the word "cat" is /k/.  "cat"这个词的anlaut是/k/。
The study of anlauts is important in phonology.  研究anlauts在音韵学中很重要。
近义词: onset, initial sound
词性: noun
