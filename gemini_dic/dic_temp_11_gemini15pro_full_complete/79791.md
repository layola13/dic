gluttony: /ˈɡlʌtəni/ | n. | 暴食；贪吃；贪婪 | Excessive eating and drinking; greed. | He was known for his gluttony and extravagant lifestyle. 他因暴饮暴食和奢侈的生活方式而闻名。 | His gluttony for books is insatiable. 他对书籍的贪婪是永不满足的。 | 近义词: greed, voracity, rapacity | 反义词: abstinence, moderation, temperance | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Old French *gloutonnie*, from glouton (“glutton”), from Latin gluttire (“to swallow, gulp down”).
