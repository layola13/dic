overnormalize:/ˌoʊvərˈnɔːrməˌlaɪz/ | v. | 过度规范化，过度正常化 | To normalize something to an excessive or undesirable degree, often resulting in unintended negative consequences.  |  例句：The company overnormalized working overtime, leading to burnout among employees.  公司过度规范加班，导致员工倦怠。

例句：Attempting to overnormalize children's behavior can stifle their creativity and individuality.  试图过度规范孩子的行为会扼杀他们的创造力和个性。 | 近义词：overregulate, overcontrol | 反义词：deregulate, loosen | verb
