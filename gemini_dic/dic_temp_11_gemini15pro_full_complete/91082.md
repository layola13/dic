huttonian:/hʌˈtoʊniən/
adjective
赫顿的; 赫顿学说的; 均变论的
Relating to or characteristic of the theories of James Hutton, especially the doctrine of uniformitarianism.
The huttonian theory revolutionized geology. 赫顿理论彻底改变了地质学。
近义词: uniformitarian
反义词: catastrophist
词性: adjective
