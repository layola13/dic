gee-gee:/ˈdʒiːdʒiː/
noun
儿童用语：马
A children's word for a horse.
例句：The little girl rode her gee-gee around the park. 小女孩骑着她的玩具马在公园里玩耍。

例句：My son loves to pretend he's riding a gee-gee. 我儿子喜欢假装骑马。

近义词：horse, pony
反义词: None (gee-gee is a childish term, it doesn't have a direct antonym)
词性: noun
