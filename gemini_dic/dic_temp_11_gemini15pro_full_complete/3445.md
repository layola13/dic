afrikanerize:/ˌæfrɪˈkɑːnəˌraɪz/
verb
使非洲化；赋予非洲人的特征
To make or become Afrikaner in character or culture; to adapt to Afrikaner culture.
The government tried to Afrikanerize the education system. 政府试图将教育系统非洲化。
assimilate, acculturate
