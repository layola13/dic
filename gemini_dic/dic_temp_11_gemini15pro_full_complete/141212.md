oophoridium:/ˌoʊ.əˈfɪd.i.əm/| n. |卵巢小体，卵巢小管|A small structure within the ovary.  A small cyst formed from a Graafian follicle that has not released its ovum.|The oophoridium can sometimes be mistaken for a true ovarian cyst.|近义词:follicle, cyst|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:
From oo- +‎ -phoridium, from Ancient Greek  ᾠόν (ōón, “egg”) +‎ -phoridium,  -phore  +‎ -idium (diminutive suffix).
