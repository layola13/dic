detraction's:/dɪˈtrækʃənz/
n.
诽谤，诋毁，贬低
The act of taking away a part, especially from the value or reputation of something.
Her constant detractions from his achievements began to annoy him. 她不断诋毁他的成就，开始让他恼火。
synonyms: slander, defamation, disparagement, belittlement
antonyms: praise, commendation, acclaim, applause
