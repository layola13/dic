captioning:/ˈkæpʃənɪŋ/ | n. | 字幕；加字幕 | The process of adding text descriptions to images, videos, or other visual media, typically for the benefit of people who are deaf or hard of hearing. |  Example: The captioning on this documentary is very accurate.  这部纪录片的字幕非常准确。| Synonyms: subtitling, transcription | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider "audio description" for auditory content) | noun

captioning:/ˈkæpʃənɪŋ/ | adj. | 字幕的 | Relating to the process of creating or providing captions. | Example: Captioning software is becoming increasingly sophisticated. 字幕软件变得越来越复杂了。| Synonyms: subtitling, transcribing | Antonyms: (No direct antonym) | adjective
