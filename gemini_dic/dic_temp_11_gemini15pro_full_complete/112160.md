look-out:/ˈlʊkˌaʊt/ (US), /ˈlʊkaʊt/ (UK)| n.|瞭望台；监视员；警戒；小心|A place from which to keep watch or a person who keeps watch. Also an exclamation used to warn of danger. |The sailors in the crow's nest served as lookouts.  水手们在桅楼上担任瞭望员。

Look out! There's a car coming! 小心！有车来了！|近义词：watchtower, observation post, guard, sentinel, lookout man, watchman, warning|反义词: none|词性: noun

look-out:/ˈlʊkˌaʊt/ (US), /ˈlʊkaʊt/ (UK)| adj.|警惕的；注意的 (less common)|Watchful and alert. |Be on the lookout for pickpockets.  小心扒手。|近义词：vigilant, watchful, alert, observant|反义词: inattentive, careless|词性: adjective
