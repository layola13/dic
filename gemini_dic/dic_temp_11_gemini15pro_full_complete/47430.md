deadpans:/ˈdɛdpænz/ (US), /ˈdɛdpænz/ (UK)| v. n.|面无表情地说；面无表情|To say something in a flat, unemotional tone, without changing facial expression; a facial expression showing no emotion.|He deadpanned, "I'm so excited." 他面无表情地说：“我太兴奋了。”|Synonyms: poker-face, straight-faced|Antonyms: expressive, emotive|verb, noun

deadpan:/ˈdɛdpæn/ (US), /ˈdɛdpæn/ (UK)| adj. adv.|面无表情的；面无表情地|Showing no emotion, especially in a humorous way.|She delivered the joke in a deadpan voice. 她面无表情地讲了那个笑话。|Synonyms: expressionless, impassive|Antonyms: expressive, animated|adjective, adverb
