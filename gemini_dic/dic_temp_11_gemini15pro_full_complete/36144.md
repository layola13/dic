coexist:/ˌkoʊɪɡˈzɪst/ | v. | 共存，共处 | To live in peace with one another, especially as used of different groups of people or different belief systems. |  Different cultures can coexist peacefully in a diverse society. 不同文化可以在一个多元化的社会中和平共处。

The two species have coexisted in this area for centuries. 这两个物种在这个地区已经共存了几个世纪。 | 近义词: cohabit, live together, exist together | 反义词: clash, conflict, oppose | 词性: verb

词源: co- (together) + exist (to have life; to be)
