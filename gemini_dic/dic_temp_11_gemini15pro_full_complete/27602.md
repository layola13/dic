cancelers:/ˈkænsələr/ (US), /ˈkænsələz/ (UK)| n.|取消者，抵制者|People who cancel or withdraw support from public figures or entities, often in response to perceived objectionable behavior or opinions.  They advocate for boycotts and call for accountability.|例句：The cancelers were relentless in their pursuit of the celebrity. 抵制者们对这位名人的追究毫不留情。

例句：The company faced backlash from cancelers after the CEO's controversial remarks.  首席执行官发表有争议的言论后，公司面临抵制者的强烈反对。|近义词：boycotters, critics, detractors|反义词：supporters, defenders|词性:Noun
