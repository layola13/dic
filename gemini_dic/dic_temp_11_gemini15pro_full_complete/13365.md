atmolysis:/ætˈmɒlɪsɪs/
noun
渗透分气法; 气体扩散分离法
The separation of a mixture of gases by means of their different rates of diffusion through a porous partition.
Atmolysis is used to separate isotopes of uranium. 渗透分气法用于分离铀的同位素。
近义词:gaseous diffusion, effusion
反义词:(No direct antonym, but conceptually) condensation, liquefaction
词性:n.
