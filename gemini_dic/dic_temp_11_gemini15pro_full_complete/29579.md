catechetic:/ˌkætəˈkɛtɪk/ | adj. | 教义问答式的，用于教义问答的 | Relating to or characterized by catechesis; instructional in religious doctrine by question and answer. | The priest used a catechetic method to teach the children about the sacraments.  神父用教义问答的方式教孩子们圣礼的知识。 |  didactic, instructional, pedagogical |  n/a | adjective

catechetically:/ˌkætəˈkɛtɪkli/ | adv. | 以教义问答的方式 | In a catechetic manner; by means of question and answer. |  He explained the complex theological concept catechetically. 他以教义问答的方式解释了这个复杂的神学概念。 | didactically, instructively | n/a | adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *catecheticus, from Greek katēkhētikos (“instructive”), from katēkhein (“to teach orally, instruct by word of mouth”), from kata- (“down”) + ēkhein (“to sound, resound”).
