antiwhaling:/ˌæntiˈweɪlɪŋ/
adjective
反捕鲸的
Opposed to the hunting of whales.
例句：
She joined the antiwhaling protest.
她参加了反捕鲸抗议活动。

例句：
The antiwhaling organization launched a new campaign.
反捕鲸组织发起了一项新的运动。

近义词：conservationist, environmentalist
反义词：pro-whaling
词性：adjective
