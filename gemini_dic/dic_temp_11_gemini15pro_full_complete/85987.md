headspring:/ˈhedsprɪŋ/| n.|源泉; 根源; 主要动力|A source, origin, or main impetus. |The headspring of the river is high in the mountains. 这条河的源头在高山上。|Synonyms: source, origin, fountainhead, wellspring, root|Antonyms: outcome, result, consequence|noun

headspring:/ˈhedsprɪŋ/| n.|跳跃; 跃起|A leap or jump.|He made a sudden headspring over the fence. 他突然跳过了篱笆。|Synonyms: leap, jump, bound, spring|Antonyms: descent, fall, drop|noun
