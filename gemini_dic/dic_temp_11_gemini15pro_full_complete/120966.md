mischarged:/ˌmɪsˈtʃɑːrdʒd/
| v. |收费错误；记错帐；充电错误|To charge incorrectly, either by asking for too much or too little money, or by applying the wrong amount of electricity.|The customer was mischarged for the items on their bill. 顾客的账单上的物品收费有误。|
|The battery was mischarged, resulting in a shorter lifespan.  电池充电错误，导致寿命缩短。|
|overcharged, overbilled|
|undercharged|
