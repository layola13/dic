discouragers:/dɪˈskʌrɪdʒərz/
n.
阻止者；劝退者；令人沮丧的人或事物
People or things that discourage someone.
The high cost of housing in the city is a major discourager for young people looking to move there.  这座城市的高房价是年轻人想搬来这里的主要障碍。
Lack of funding is a serious discourager to medical research. 缺乏资金是医学研究的严重障碍。
Synonyms: deterrent, obstacle, impediment, hindrance
Antonyms: encouragement, incentive, motivator
noun
