interprotoplasmic:/ˌɪntərproʊtəˈplæzmɪk/ | adj. | 胞间连丝的，细胞质间的 | Existing or occurring between cytoplasms (the jelly-like substance that fills a cell).  It refers to communication or interaction happening between the cytoplasm of different cells. | Example 1: Interprotoplasmic bridges facilitate the transport of molecules between plant cells. 胞间连丝促进植物细胞间的分子运输。

Example 2: The researchers studied the role of interprotoplasmic communication in the development of the organism. 研究人员研究了胞间连丝通讯在生物体发育中的作用。 | Near Synonyms: intercellular,  transcellular | Antonyms: intracellular | Word Type: Adjective
