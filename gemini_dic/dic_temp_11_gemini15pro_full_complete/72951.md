fontange:/fɒnˈtɑːnʒ/ | n. |  丰唐发型（一种高耸的女性发型，流行于17世纪末18世纪初的欧洲） | A high headdress worn by women at the end of the 17th and beginning of the 18th centuries.  It consisted of a wire frame supporting layers of lace and ribbon, arranged to create a towering structure. |  Madame de Maintenon, Louis XIV's morganatic wife, popularized the fontange.  路易十四世的贵妇曼特农夫人使丰唐发型流行起来。 | headdress, coiffure |  - | noun
