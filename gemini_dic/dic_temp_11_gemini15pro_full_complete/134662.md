nonmanipulatory:/ˌnɒn.məˈnɪpjʊ.lə.tɔːri/ (US) /ˌnɒn.məˈnɪpjʊ.lə.tri/ (UK)
adjective
非操控性的，非操纵性的
Not designed or intended to manipulate or control others; not involving manipulation.
The therapist used a nonmanipulatory approach, allowing the patient to guide the session.  治疗师采用非操控性的方法，让病人引导治疗过程。
Her teaching style was nonmanipulatory, encouraging students to explore their own ideas. 她的教学风格是非操控性的，鼓励学生探索自己的想法。
Synonyms: non-controlling, non-interfering, respectful, unbiased
Antonyms: manipulative, controlling, exploitative
词性: 形容词
