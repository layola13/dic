glebosity:/ˈɡliːbəsti/ | n. | 油嘴滑舌；口若悬河 | The quality of being fluent and voluble but insincere and shallow. | His glebosity made it hard to trust him.  他的油嘴滑舌让人很难相信他。| synonyms: volubility, loquacity, fluency, smooth-talking | antonyms: reticence, taciturnity | noun

glebe:/ɡliːb/ | n. | 教会所属的耕地；教堂所属的土地 | Land belonging or yielding revenue to a parish church or ecclesiastical benefice.  | The vicarage stood on a small glebe overlooking the village. 牧师住宅坐落在俯瞰村庄的一小块教会所属土地上。| synonyms: parish land, church land | antonyms: N/A | noun
