hipsters:/ˈhɪpstərz/| n.|时髦人士；赶时髦的人|A person who follows the latest trends and fashions, especially those considered to be outside the cultural mainstream.|The cafe was full of hipsters with their beards and vintage clothes.  这家咖啡馆里挤满了留着胡须、穿着复古服装的潮人。|Synonyms: trendsetters, fashionistas, scenesters|Antonyms: conformists, traditionalists|Noun

hipsters:/ˈhɪpstərz/| n.|消息灵通人士；内行|Someone who is aware of and interested in the latest ideas and trends, especially in music, fashion, and art.|He's a real hipster and always knows about the coolest new bands before anyone else. 他是个真正的潮人，总是在别人之前就知道最酷的新乐队。|Synonyms: connoisseurs, aficionados, insiders|Antonyms: outsiders, squares|Noun
