invendible:/ɪnˈvɛndɪb(ə)l/
adjective
卖不掉的，无法出售的
Not saleable; not capable of being sold.
例句：The painting proved invendible, despite its beauty. 这幅画尽管很美，但最终还是卖不出去。

例句：The property, due to its remote location, was deemed invendible.  由于地处偏远，这处房产被认为是卖不掉的。

近义词：unsalable, unmarketable, unmerchantable
反义词：salable, marketable, merchantable
词性：adjective


词源:
From Late Latin *invendibilis, from in- (“not”) + vendibilis (“saleable”), from vēndō (“sell”).
