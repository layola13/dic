lichenlike:/ˈlaɪkənˌlaɪk/
adj.
地衣状的,像地衣的
Resembling a lichen in appearance or growth habit.
The bark of the tree was lichenlike in texture.  这棵树的树皮质地像地衣。
The growth on the rock was lichenlike.  岩石上的生长物像地衣。
Synonyms: crustose, mossy, scaly
Antonyms: smooth, bare
词性: 形容词
