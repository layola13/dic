basta:/ˈbɑːstə/ | int. |够了，停止，住手| Used to express that enough is enough, or to command someone to stop. | "Basta! I don't want to hear any more excuses."  够了！我不想再听到任何借口了。 | Synonyms: enough, stop, cease, quit | Antonyms: continue, proceed | interjection

basta:/ˈbɑːstə/ | n. | (音乐)终止符 | A musical symbol indicating the end of a passage or piece. | "The conductor indicated the basta with a flourish." 指挥以华丽的手势指示了终止符。 | Synonyms: end, finish, conclusion | Antonyms: beginning, start, commencement | noun
