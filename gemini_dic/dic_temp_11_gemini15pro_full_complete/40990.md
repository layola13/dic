coordinator:/koʊˈɔːrdɪneɪtər/ (US), /kəʊˈɔːdɪneɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|协调员，协调人|A person who organizes people or groups so that they work together efficiently and effectively. |The event coordinator ensured everything ran smoothly. 活动协调员确保一切顺利进行。|Synonyms: organizer, manager, director, facilitator, administrator|Antonyms: disorganizer|noun

coordinator:/koʊˈɔːrdɪneɪt/ (US), /kəʊˈɔːdɪneɪt/ (UK)| v.|协调，调节|To bring into a common action, movement, or condition. |She coordinated the marketing efforts for the new product launch. 她协调了新产品发布的营销工作。|Synonyms: organize, manage, direct, harmonize, synchronize|Antonyms: disrupt, disorganize|verb
