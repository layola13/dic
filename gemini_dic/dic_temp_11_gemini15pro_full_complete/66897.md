expedite:/ˈekspədaɪt/| v. | 加速，加快；促进 |To make a process happen more quickly.|The company took steps to expedite the delivery of its products. 公司采取措施加快产品交付。|近义词：accelerate, hasten, speed up|反义词：delay, hinder, obstruct|verb

expedited:/ˈekspədaɪtɪd/| adj. | 加速的，快速的 |Happening sooner or more quickly than usual.|We offer an expedited shipping option for urgent orders. 我们为紧急订单提供加急送货选项。|近义词：accelerated, hastened, rushed|反义词：delayed, slow, hindered|adjective
