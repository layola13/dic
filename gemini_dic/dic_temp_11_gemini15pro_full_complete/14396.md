autosuggestibility:/ˌɔːtəʊsəˌdʒɛstɪˈbɪləti/ | n. |自我暗示性；暗示感受性 |The susceptibility of an individual to accepting and acting on suggestions originating within the individual's own mind.  The capacity to be influenced by ideas, feelings, or impulses originating within oneself. |She attributed her success to her high degree of autosuggestibility, believing she could manifest her goals. 她将自己的成功归因于高度的自我暗示性，相信自己能够实现目标。

His autosuggestibility made him vulnerable to various anxieties and fears. 他的暗示感受性让他容易受到各种焦虑和恐惧的影响。 |Near synonyms: suggestibility, self-suggestion, self-hypnosis |Antonyms: resistance, skepticism |noun
