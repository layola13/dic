词源:  "Cairn" originally refers to a heap of stones, often used as a landmark.  "Cairnie" is a Scottish term meaning small or tiny, likely derived from the idea of a small cairn. "-est" is a superlative suffix.  Therefore, "cairniest" means the smallest.
