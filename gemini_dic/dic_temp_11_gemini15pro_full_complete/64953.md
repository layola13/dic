esthete:/ˈesˌθiːt/ /ˈɛsθiːt/| n.|唯美主义者；审美家|A person who has or affects to have a special appreciation of art and beauty.|He postures as an esthete, but he actually knows very little about art. 他摆出一副审美家的姿态，但实际上他对艺术知之甚少。|近义词：connoisseur, aesthetician, art lover|反义词：philistine|Noun
