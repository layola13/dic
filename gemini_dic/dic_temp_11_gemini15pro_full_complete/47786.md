decaphyllous:/ˌdekəˈfɪləs/
adjective
十叶的；十萼片的
Having ten petals or ten sepals.
The decaphyllous flower attracted the bees.  十瓣的花吸引了蜜蜂。
decaphyllous plant 十叶植物


近义词: ten-petaled, ten-sepalled
