ironist:/ˈaɪrənɪst/| n.|讽刺家，反讽者|A person who uses irony, often to create a humorous or critical effect.  A master of saying the opposite of what they mean.| The ironist feigned praise for the politician's disastrous policy.  这位讽刺家假装称赞这位政客的灾难性政策。|Synonyms: satirist, cynic, mocker|Antonyms: ingenu, straight talker|noun
