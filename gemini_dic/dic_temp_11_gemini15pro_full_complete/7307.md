anguishes:/ˈæŋɡwɪʃɪz/| n. |痛苦，苦恼（复数）|Plural form of anguish, referring to multiple instances or types of extreme suffering.|The anguishes of war are felt for generations. 战争的痛苦会被几代人感受到。|近义词：agonies, torments, distresses, pains|反义词:joys, comforts, reliefs|noun
