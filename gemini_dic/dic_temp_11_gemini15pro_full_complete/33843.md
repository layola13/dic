circumnavigates:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈnævɪɡeɪts/ |v. |环球航行，绕...航行|to sail or travel all the way around something, especially the world|Ferdinand Magellan's expedition was the first to circumnavigate the globe. 费迪南德·麦哲伦的探险队是第一个环球航行的。|Synonyms: circle, orbit, encompass, go around|Antonyms: stay, remain|verb
