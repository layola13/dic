cuminol: /ˈkjuːmɪnɒl/ | n. |枯茗醇；异丙基苯甲醇| An aromatic alcohol used in perfumery, derived from cumin aldehyde. | Cuminol has a sweet, spicy aroma reminiscent of cumin. 枯茗醇具有一种甜美的辛辣香味，让人联想起孜然。| Cuminol is a key ingredient in some high-end perfumes. 枯茗醇是一些高端香水中的一种关键成分。 | synonyms: isopropylbenzyl alcohol,  p-isopropylbenzyl alcohol | antonyms: None | noun
