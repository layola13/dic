housekeeperlike:/ˈhaʊskiːpərˌlaɪk/
|adj.|像管家一样的；有条理的；井井有条的|Resembling or characteristic of a housekeeper, especially in being organized and tidy.|Her apartment was always housekeeperlike, spotless and everything in its place. 她的公寓总是像管家打理过一样，一尘不染，一切都井然有序。|She runs her household in a very housekeeperlike manner, everything is organized and efficient. 她把家打理得井井有条，很有条理，也很有效率。|近义词:tidy, organized, neat, meticulous, efficient|反义词:messy, disorganized, slovenly, chaotic|词性:形容词

词源: housekeeper (n.) + like (suffix)

housekeeper:  From house + keeper.  "One who keeps or takes care of a house"

-like: Old English -līc "having the form or appearance of, resembling,"
