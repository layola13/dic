indenturing:/ɪnˈdɛntʃərɪŋ/ | n. | 契约约束，学徒期 | The act of binding someone by an indenture, a contract binding one party into the service of another for a specified time. |  The indenturing of young apprentices was common in the 18th century.  18世纪，年轻学徒的契约约束很常见。| Synonyms: binding, contracting, apprenticing | Antonyms: releasing, freeing | Noun

indenturing:/ɪnˈdɛntʃərɪŋ/ | adj. | 契约的，约束的 | Serving as or relating to an indenture. | The indenturing process was often exploitative. 这种契约过程经常带有剥削性质。| Synonyms: contractual, binding | Antonyms: liberating, freeing | Adjective
