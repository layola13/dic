crook's:/krʊks/ | n. |骗子的（东西）; 罪犯的（东西）|Something that belongs to a crook (a dishonest person, especially a criminal).  It's the possessive form of "crook".| That's a crook's game. 那是骗子的把戏。| The police found the stolen jewels in the crook's hideout. 警察在罪犯的藏身处找到了被盗的珠宝。 | synonyms: criminal's, thief's, robber's, swindler's | antonyms: honest person's, law-abiding citizen's |  noun (possessive)
