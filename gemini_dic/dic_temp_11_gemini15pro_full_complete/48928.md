deft:/dɛft/
adj.
熟练的, 灵巧的, 敏捷的
neatly skillful and quick in one's movements.
He's very deft at handling complaints. 他非常善于处理投诉。
近义词: skillful, adept, adroit, dexterous, nimble, proficient
反义词: clumsy, awkward, inept
词性: 形容词

词源:
From Middle English *defte, from Old English *dæfte, *gedæfte (“mild, gentle, meek; fitting, proper, suitable”), from Proto-Germanic *daftą (“fitting, suitable”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰeh₂bʰ- (“to fit, join”). Cognate with Dutch deftig (“stately, dignified”), German tüchtig (“efficient, capable”).
