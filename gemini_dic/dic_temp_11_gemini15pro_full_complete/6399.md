amphioxus:/ˌæmfiˈɒksəs/| n. |文昌鱼|A small, translucent, fish-like marine animal that is a member of the chordate subphylum Cephalochordata.  It is considered to be a close relative of vertebrates and is often studied as a model organism in evolutionary biology. |Amphioxus is a key organism for understanding the evolution of vertebrates. 文昌鱼是理解脊椎动物进化的关键生物。| The amphioxus genome has been sequenced, providing valuable insights into vertebrate evolution. 文昌鱼的基因组已被测序，为脊椎动物的进化提供了宝贵的见解。|lancelet| |noun
