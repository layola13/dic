outloving:/ˌaʊtˈlʌvɪŋ/
verb
比...更长久地爱；活得比...长
Loving someone or something longer than someone or something else loves them.
She outloved her husband by ten years. 她比她丈夫多活了十年。
近义词: outliving, surviving
反义词: predeceasing
