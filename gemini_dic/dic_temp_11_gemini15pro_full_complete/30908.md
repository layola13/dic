chain:/tʃeɪn/| n. v.|链条，一系列，连锁；束缚，囚禁|A series of connected links or rings, typically of metal, used for fastening or securing something, or for pulling or supporting loads.  Also, a sequence of connected things or events.|The dog was fastened to a chain.  狗被拴在链子上。|series, sequence, succession|freedom, liberation|noun, verb

chain:/tʃeɪn/| v.|用链条拴住，束缚|To fasten or secure with a chain.|They chained the bicycle to the railing.  他们把自行车锁在栏杆上。|bind, restrain, fetter|free, release, unchain|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "chaine," from Latin "catena" meaning "chain, fetter."
