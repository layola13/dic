horologist:/həˈrɑːlədʒɪst/
n.|钟表匠，钟表修理工|A person who makes or repairs clocks and watches.|The horologist carefully repaired the antique clock. 这位钟表匠小心翼翼地修理了这台古董钟。|Synonyms: clockmaker, watchmaker, watch repairer|Antonyms: N/A|noun

horology:/həˈrɑːlədʒi/
n.|钟表学，测时法|The study and measurement of time.|He studied horology in Switzerland. 他在瑞士学习钟表学。|Synonyms: chronometry|Antonyms: N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *horologium* "sundial, clock," from Greek *hōrologion*, from *hōra* "hour, time" + *-logion*, a suffix denoting an instrument or device.
