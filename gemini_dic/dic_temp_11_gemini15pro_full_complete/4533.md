alburnum:/ælˈbɜːrnəm/
n.|边材|The soft, outer layers of wood in a tree, between the bark and the heartwood, that are actively involved in the transport of water and minerals.|The carpenter explained that the alburnum of the oak tree was still moist.|近义词:sapwood|反义词:heartwood|词性:名词
词源: From Latin *alburnum*, from *albus* "white".
