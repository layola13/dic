criminalising: /ˈkrɪmɪnəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使…成为犯罪；宣布…为非法
The act of making something illegal or treating something as a crime.
The government is considering criminalising the possession of small amounts of marijuana. 政府正在考虑将持有少量大麻定为犯罪。
criminalizing (American spelling)
decriminalizing
