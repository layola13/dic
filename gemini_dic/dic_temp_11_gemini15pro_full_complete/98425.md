inspectable:/ɪnˈspɛktəbl/| adj.|可检查的，可视察的|able to be inspected or examined; open to inspection|例句：The building is inspectable by appointment only.  这栋楼只接受预约检查。|近义词：examinable, observable, reviewable|反义词:hidden, concealed|词性:形容词
Etymology: From inspect (from Latin *inspectāre, frequentative of inspicere “to look into,” from in- “in” + specere “to look”) + -able (suffix forming adjectives meaning “capable of, fit for, tending to”).
