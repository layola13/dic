oarswoman:/ˈɔːrzˌwʊmən/ | n. | 女划手；女性桨手 | A woman who rows a boat, especially as a member of a crew.  |  例句：The oarswoman pulled strongly on the oars. 女划手用力地划着桨。

例句：The experienced oarswoman guided the boat through the rough waters.  经验丰富的女划手引导着船只穿过汹涌的水面。

近义词：rower, crew member | 反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly coxswain if focusing on steering rather than rowing) | 词性: Noun
