co-founder:/ˌkoʊˈfaʊndər/| n. |共同创始人；联合创始人|A person who establishes something jointly with another or others, especially a business.| Steve Wozniak was the co-founder of Apple with Steve Jobs. 史蒂夫·沃兹尼亚克与史蒂夫·乔布斯是苹果公司的联合创始人。|Example:  The co-founders shared a vision for the company's future.  联合创始人对公司的未来有着共同的愿景。|Synonym:  joint founder, co-creator|Antonym: N/A|Noun
