nonrespondent:/ˌnɑːn.rɪˈspɑːn.dənt/ | n. |未答复者，无反应者|A person who does not respond to a survey, questionnaire, or request for information. |例句：The survey had a high number of nonrespondents, making the results less reliable. 这项调查有很多未答复者，使得结果不太可靠。

例句：Researchers are trying to understand why some people are nonrespondents in studies. 研究人员正试图理解为什么有些人在研究中不回应。|近义词：non-replier, defaulter|反义词:respondent|词性:名词
