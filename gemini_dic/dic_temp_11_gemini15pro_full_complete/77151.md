gashest:/ˈɡæʃɪst/
adjective
最深的; 最长的(gash的最高级)
Deepest or longest (superlative of 'gash').
The gashest wound I've ever seen was on a hiker who fell down a ravine. 我见过的最深的伤口是在一个掉进峡谷的徒步旅行者身上。
The earthquake left the gashest cracks in the highway. 地震在高速公路上留下了最长的裂缝。
近义词:deepest, longest, most severe
反义词:shallowest, shortest, slightest
词性:adjective
词源:From 'gash' + '-est' (superlative suffix).  'Gash' comes from Old French 'gache' meaning a breach or notch.
