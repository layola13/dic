demanding:/dɪˈmændɪŋ/| adj.|苛刻的，要求高的|Requiring much skill, effort, or attention; challenging.|例句：This is a very demanding job. 这是一份要求很高的工作。|近义词：challenging, difficult, exacting, tough|反义词：easy, simple, undemanding|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the verb "demand" (from Old French demander, from Latin demandare "to entrust, commit, enjoin") + the suffix "-ing," indicating a present participle used adjectivally.
