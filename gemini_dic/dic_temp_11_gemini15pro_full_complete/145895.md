overdifferentiation: /ˌoʊvərˌdɪfərɛnʃiˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度区分，过度分化 | The act of making too many distinctions or subdivisions, often to an unnecessary or excessive degree. This can occur in various fields, from biology to sociology and marketing. |  Marketers sometimes engage in overdifferentiation, creating too many variations of a product, which can confuse consumers. 市场营销人员有时会过度区分，创造出太多种类的产品，这会让消费者感到困惑。

Overdifferentiation of tasks can lead to decreased efficiency in a workflow.  任务的过度区分会导致工作流程效率降低。 | 近义词: overspecialization, oversubdivision | 反义词: simplification, generalization | 词性: noun
