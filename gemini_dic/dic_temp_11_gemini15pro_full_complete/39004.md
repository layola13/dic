confirmor:/kənˈfɜːrmər/ | n. | 确认者；证实者 | Someone who confirms something; a verifier. | The lawyer acted as a confirmor for the witness's testimony. 律师担任证人证词的确认者。 |  近义词: verifier, corroborator, attester | 反义词: denier, refuter | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Late Middle English *confirmer, from Anglo-Norman confirmer, from Latin confirmare (“to strengthen, establish, confirm”), from con- (“together”) + firmare (“to make firm, strengthen”), from firmus (“firm, strong”).
