open-ended:/ˌoʊpənˈɛndɪd/| adj.|开放式的，无限制的|Allowing or encouraging freedom of response; not having fixed limits or a fixed answer.|The survey included several open-ended questions about the future of the company.  这项调查包含了几个关于公司未来的开放式问题。|flexible, unrestricted, unbound|fixed, closed, restricted|adjective


open-ended:/ˌoʊpənˈɛndɪd/| adj.|未决的，无定论的|Not having a fixed or planned ending; left for future development.|The project remains open-ended, with no clear deadline in sight.  这个项目仍然没有定论，看不到明确的截止日期。|undecided, unresolved, indefinite|conclusive, definite, determined|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "open" + "ended".  "Open" traces back to Old English "open", ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*upanaz". "Ended" comes from the verb "end", from Old English "endian", meaning "to bring to an end".
