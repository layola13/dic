corrodents:/kəˈroʊd(ə)nts/
|n.|腐蚀剂；腐蚀物|Substances that cause corrosion by chemical action.|Strong corrodents, such as acids, can damage metals. 强腐蚀剂，例如酸，会损坏金属。|The corrodents ate away at the metal pipes. 腐蚀剂腐蚀了金属管道。|synonyms:caustics, corrosives|antonyms: inhibitors, preservatives
