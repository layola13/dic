beetling:/ˈbiːtlɪŋ/
adjective
突出而低垂的; 悬垂的;  beetling brows
Projecting or overhanging; prominent.  "beetling brows"
The cliffs were beetling and ominous. 悬崖峭壁，险峻无比。
overhanging, prominent, jutting, projecting
receding, sloping
adjective

beetling:/ˈbiːtlɪŋ/
noun
采集甲虫
The action of collecting beetles.
He spent his childhood beetling in the nearby woods. 他童年时期都在附近的树林里采集甲虫。
collecting, gathering
releasing, discarding
noun
