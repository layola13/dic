immoderation:/ɪˌmɒdəˈreɪʃn/| n. |无节制，过度，放纵|lack of moderation; excess|His immoderation in eating and drinking led to health problems. 他饮食无度导致了健康问题。|Synonyms: excess, intemperance, overindulgence|Antonyms: moderation, temperance, restraint|noun


immoderate:/ɪˈmɒdərət/| adj. |无节制的，过度的，放纵的|not moderate; excessive|Immoderate drinking can damage your liver.  过量饮酒会损害你的肝脏。|Synonyms: excessive, extreme, inordinate|Antonyms: moderate, reasonable, temperate|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
