mascot:/ˈmæskɒt/ (US), /ˈmæskət/ (UK)| n.|吉祥物|A person, animal, or object adopted by a group as a symbolic figure, especially to bring them good luck.|The team's mascot is a fierce-looking eagle.  该队的吉祥物是一只凶猛的老鹰。|Synonym: talisman, charm, emblem|Antonym: jinx, curse|noun

mascot:/ˈmæskɒt/ (US), /ˈmæskət/ (UK)| n.|吉祥物|Something that is supposed to bring good luck or that is used to symbolize a particular event or organization.|The Olympic Games always have a mascot. 奥运会总是有一个吉祥物。|Synonym: symbol, icon, representative|Antonym: jinx, curse|noun
