maginot:/ˈmædʒɪnoʊ/| n.|马其诺防线|A line of concrete fortifications, obstacles, and weapon installations built by France in the 1930s to deter invasion by Germany.|The Maginot Line proved ineffective when Germany bypassed it through Belgium. 马其诺防线在德国绕过比利时入侵时被证明是无效的。|例句：The Maginot Line was a symbol of French defensive strategy. 马其诺防线是法国防御战略的象征。

例句：The Germans outflanked the Maginot Line in 1940. 德国人在1940年包抄了马其诺防线。


|近义词：fortification, defense, barrier|反义词: vulnerability, weakness|词性:名词
