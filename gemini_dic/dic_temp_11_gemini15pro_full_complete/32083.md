chess:/tʃes/| n.|国际象棋|A board game for two players, each with 16 pieces that are moved on a checkered board according to specific rules. The object is to checkmate the opponent's king.|例句：We played a game of chess. 我们下了一盘棋。

例句：Chess is a game of strategy. 国际象棋是一项策略性游戏。|近义词：game, checkers|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *esches* (plural), from Vulgar Latin *scacci*, from Arabic *šaṭranǧ*, from Persian *čaṭrang*, from Sanskrit *catur-aṅga* "four divisions (of the military)," referring to the four elements of the ancient Indian army (elephants, chariots, cavalry, infantry).
