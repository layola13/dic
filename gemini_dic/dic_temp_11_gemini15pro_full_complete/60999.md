eliminate: /ɪˈlɪmɪneɪt/
verb
消除，排除，淘汰
to completely remove or get rid of something
The company is trying to eliminate unnecessary costs.  这家公司正在努力消除不必要的成本。
Several teams were eliminated from the competition.  几支队伍被淘汰出局。
近义词: remove, eradicate, get rid of, abolish, exterminate
反义词: include, add, introduce, create
