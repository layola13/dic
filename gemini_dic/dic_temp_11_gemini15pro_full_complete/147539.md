overpaying:/ˌoʊvərˈpeɪɪŋ/
verb
过度支付；多付
paying more than something is worth.
例句：I think we're overpaying for our cable TV. 我认为我们为有线电视支付的费用过高了。

例句：They are overpaying their employees to retain talent. 他们为了留住人才而给员工支付过高的薪水。

近义词：overspending,  paying too much
反义词：underpaying
词性：动词 (present participle of overpay)
