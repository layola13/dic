|词性: adjective|


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + hereditary + -ness.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of". "Hereditary" comes from the Latin word "hereditarius," meaning "pertaining to inheritance."  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
