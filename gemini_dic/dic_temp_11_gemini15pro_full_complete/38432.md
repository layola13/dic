concertina:/ˌkɒnsəˈtiːnə/ | n. |六角手风琴；六角形伸缩体|A musical instrument of the free-reed aerophone family, like a small accordion, with buttons on both ends.|The musician played a lively tune on his concertina. 音乐家在他的六角手风琴上演奏了一首活泼的曲子。|Synonyms: accordion, squeezebox|Antonyms: None|noun

concertina:/kɒnsəˈtiːnə/ | v. |（使）像六角手风琴那样折叠；（使）缩拢；压缩|To collapse or fold in a way that resembles a concertina.|The bus concertinaed in the crash. 公共汽车在撞车事故中像手风琴一样折叠起来了。|Synonyms: collapse, fold, compress, contract|Antonyms: expand, extend, stretch|verb
