cystotomy:/sɪˈstɒtəmi/ | n. |膀胱切开术|A surgical incision into the urinary bladder. |例句：The surgeon performed a cystotomy to remove the bladder stones. 外科医生进行了膀胱切开术以去除膀胱结石。

例句：A cystotomy is often necessary to access the bladder for diagnostic or therapeutic purposes.  为了诊断或治疗目的，通常需要进行膀胱切开术以进入膀胱。|近义词：vesicotomy|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
