foals:/foʊlz/
n.
幼驹（复数）
Young horses, typically under one year old.
The mare protectively nuzzled her two foals. 母马爱怜地用鼻子轻触她的两个幼驹。
The foals frolicked in the pasture. 幼驹在牧场上嬉戏。
colt, filly, young horse
adult horse
noun
