geomantic:/ˌdʒiːəˈmæntɪk/
adjective
地理的；風水的；占卜的
Relating to geomancy; divination by geographic features or figures.
The geomantic traditions of ancient China are complex and fascinating.  古代中国的风水传统复杂而迷人。
The architect incorporated geomantic principles into the building's design.  这位建筑师将风水原则融入到建筑设计中。
Synonym: divinatory, augural
Antonym: scientific, empirical
