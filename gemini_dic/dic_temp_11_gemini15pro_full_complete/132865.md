nonegregious:/ˌnɒnɪˈɡriːdʒəs/
adjective
非恶劣的；不严重的；无伤大雅的
Not flagrant or egregious; not conspicuously bad or offensive.
His behavior, while not ideal, was nonegregious. 他的行为虽然不理想，但也无伤大雅。
Synonyms:  unremarkable, ordinary, insignificant, minor, venial
Antonyms: egregious, flagrant, blatant, glaring, outrageous
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + egregious.  "Egregious" comes from Latin *exgregius "standing out from the flock," from ex- "out" + grex "flock, herd."
