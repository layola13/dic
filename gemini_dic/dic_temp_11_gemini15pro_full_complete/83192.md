guy's:/ɡaɪz/| n.| 小伙子们的；家伙们的；各位的 |  The possessive form of "guys," used to refer to something belonging to or associated with a group of people, typically male but increasingly used in a gender-neutral way.  It's a contraction of "guy's" (singular possessive) but used with plural meaning. |  例句：That's the guy's car over there. 那是那边那个人的车。| 近义词：men's, fellas', people's, folks' | 反义词: (No direct antonym, depends on context - possibly women's, girls') | 词性:  Possessive pronoun (informal)
