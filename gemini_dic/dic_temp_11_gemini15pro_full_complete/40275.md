continuant:/kənˈtɪnjuənt/ | n. adj.|连续音，持续音；连续的|A speech sound, such as /f/ or /v/, produced by forcing air through a narrow passage in the vocal tract. Also, something that continues or persists.|The sounds /s/ and /z/ are continuants. /s/和/z/是连续音。|近义词:fricative, spirant|反义词:stop, plosive|词性: noun, adjective

continuant:/kənˈtɪnjuənt/ | adj.|连续的，持续的|Continuing without interruption.|The pain was continuant and severe. 疼痛持续且剧烈。|近义词:continuous, constant, perpetual|反义词:intermittent, sporadic|词性: adjective
