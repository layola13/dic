heirless:/ˈɛərlɪs/
adjective
无继承人的
Having no heir.
The king died heirless, leaving the kingdom in chaos. 国王死后无继承人，使王国陷入混乱。
近义词: without an heir, childless (if referring to direct descendants)
反义词: inheriting, with heirs

词性: adjective
