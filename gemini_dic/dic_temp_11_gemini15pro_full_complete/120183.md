mindlessly:/ˈmaɪndləsli/| adv.|无意识地，不经思考地|Without thought or attention; mechanically.|She mindlessly scrolled through social media. 她无意识地翻阅着社交媒体。|例句：He mindlessly chewed his gum during the meeting. 他在会议期间无意识地嚼着口香糖。|近义词：automatically, mechanically, thoughtlessly, absentmindedly|反义词：consciously, deliberately, thoughtfully, attentively|词性:副词
Etymology->
From mind (n.) + -less (suffix) + -ly (adverbial suffix). "Mindless" meaning "lacking mind or understanding" dates from the late 14th century. The adverbial form "mindlessly" is a later development.
