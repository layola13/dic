gangrenous:/ˈɡæŋɡrɪnəs/| adj.|坏疽的，腐败的|Affected by gangrene; decaying, mortifying.|The doctor diagnosed the wound as gangrenous. 医生诊断伤口为坏疽。|Synonyms: necrotic, mortified, putrid, decayed|Antonyms: healthy, vital, living|adjective

gangrenous:/ˈɡæŋɡrɪnəs/| adj.|坏疽性的，腐败的|Relating to or characteristic of gangrene; indicative of moral or spiritual decay.|The gangrenous state of the city's politics shocked the nation. 该市政治的腐败状态震惊了全国。|Synonyms: corrupt, degenerate, putrescent|Antonyms: wholesome, sound, pure|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *gangraenosus, from Latin gangraena (“gangrene”), from Ancient Greek γάγγραινα (gángraina, “an eating sore”).
