nonreceptions:/ˌnɑːn.rɪˈsɛp.ʃənz/
noun
未接待; 不受欢迎; 冷遇
lack of reception or acceptance.  The absence of a formal or acknowledged reception.
The ambassador complained about the nonreception of his government's proposals. 大使抱怨他的政府的提案未被接受。
The play's nonreception by critics led to its early closure.  该剧因评论家不买账而提前下档。
near synonyms: rejection, refusal, snub, cold shoulder
antonyms: acceptance, welcome, embrace
n.
