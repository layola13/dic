ampoule:/ˈæmpuːl/ (US), /ˈæmpuːl/ (UK)| n. |安瓿；小药瓶|A small sealed glass container that holds a measured amount of medication, typically for injection.|The nurse carefully snapped the neck of the ampoule and drew the medicine into the syringe. 护士小心地掰开了安瓿瓶的颈部，将药液吸入注射器。

The archaeologist discovered an ancient ampoule containing a mysterious liquid. 考古学家发现了一个装着神秘液体的古代安瓿瓶。|近义词：vial, phial|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
