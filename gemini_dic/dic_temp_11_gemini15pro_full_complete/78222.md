geraniums: /dʒəˈreɪniəmz/ | n. | 天竺葵 |A genus of flowering plants in the family Geraniaceae, commonly grown as ornamentals for their colorful flowers and fragrant leaves.  These plants are native to temperate regions of the world, particularly southern Africa.  The common name "geranium" is also often applied to plants in the related genus Pelargonium. |  The geraniums on the balcony were a riot of color.  阳台上的天竺葵五彩缤纷。 | synonyms: cranesbills, pelargoniums (often used interchangeably but technically different) | antonyms: None (no direct opposite for a type of flower) | noun
