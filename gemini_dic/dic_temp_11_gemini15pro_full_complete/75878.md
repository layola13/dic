functorial:/fʌŋˈtɔːriəl/| adj.|函子的，函子性的|Relating to or having the nature of a functor; behaving like a functor, preserving relationships between objects and morphisms.|The construction of the tensor product is functorial in both arguments.  张量积的构造在其两个参数中都是函子性的。|The forgetful functor from topological spaces to sets is functorial. 从拓扑空间到集合的遗忘函子是函子性的。|Near synonyms:  natural, canonical|Antonym: None|Word type: Adjective
