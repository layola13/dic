formate:/ˈfɔːrmeɪt/ (UK) /ˈfɔːrˌmeɪt/ (US)| n. | 甲酸盐；甲酸酯 |A salt or ester of formic acid.  |Sodium formate is used in leather tanning. 甲酸钠用于皮革鞣制。|Synonyms: methanoate |Antonyms: N/A |noun

formate:/ˈfɔːrˌmeɪt/ (US)| v. | 格式化 |To prepare (a data storage medium) for use, typically by deleting any existing data and setting up a file system. |I need to format my hard drive before installing the new operating system. 我需要在安装新操作系统之前格式化我的硬盘。|Synonyms: initialize, erase |Antonyms: N/A |verb
