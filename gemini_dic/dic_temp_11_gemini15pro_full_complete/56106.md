doctorly:/ˈdɑːktərli/ | adj. | 医生般的；博学的；学究气的 | Like a doctor; learned; scholarly. | He had a doctorly air about him. 他有一种学究气。 | 近义词: learned, scholarly, erudite, pedantic | 反义词: unscholarly, ignorant, uneducated | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From doctor + -ly.  The suffix "-ly" transforms nouns into adjectives.  The word "doctor" itself comes from the Latin "doctor" meaning "teacher," ultimately from "docēre," meaning "to teach."
