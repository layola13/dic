osteopaths:/ˈɒstiəpæθs/ (UK) /ˈɑːstiəpæθs/ (US)| n.|整骨医生，整骨治疗师|A practitioner of osteopathy, a system of complementary medicine involving the treatment of medical disorders through the manipulation and massage of the skeleton and musculature.|例句：I went to see an osteopath about my back pain. 我去看整骨医生治疗我的背痛。|例句：Many athletes rely on osteopaths to keep them in top condition. 许多运动员依靠整骨医生来保持最佳状态。|近义词：chiropractor, physical therapist|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性: Noun
