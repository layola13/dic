dramaturg:/ˈdræmətɜːrɡ/ (US), /ˈdræmətɜːɡ/ (UK)| n. | 剧作家；戏剧顾问；文学顾问 | A dramaturg is a literary editor on the staff of a theatre who consults with authors and edits texts. | The dramaturg helped the playwright develop the characters more fully.  剧作家在戏剧构作的帮助下更完整地发展了角色。|  Synonym:  literary advisor, script editor | Antonym: None | Noun

dramaturgs:/ˈdræmətɜːrɡz/ (US), /ˈdræmətɜːɡz/ (UK)| n. | 剧作家们；戏剧顾问们；文学顾问们 | Plural form of dramaturg. | The dramaturgs worked tirelessly to improve the script.  这些戏剧构作不知疲倦地工作以改进剧本。| Synonym: literary advisors, script editors | Antonym: None | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Dramaturg*, from French *dramaturge*, from Greek *δρᾶμα* (drama, "play, action") + *ἔργον* (ergon, "work"). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\werǵ-* (“to do, work”).
