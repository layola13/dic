complying:/kəmˈplaɪɪŋ/
|v. (present participle)|遵守，遵从|acting in accordance with a wish or command.|例句：The company is complying with all relevant regulations. 这家公司遵守所有相关规定。|近义词：obeying, adhering, conforming|反义词：disobeying, violating, defying|词性：动词(present participle)

词源: From Middle English *complien, from Old French complaire (“to please, satisfy”), from Latin complācēre (“to be very pleasing”), from com- (“with, together”) + placēre (“to please”).
