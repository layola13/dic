barbicel: /ˈbɑːrbɪsɛl/ | n. | 小羽枝，羽小枝 | A minute barb or hooklet projecting from the barbs of a feather, interlocking with adjacent barbicels to form a smooth surface. | The barbicels interlock to create the smooth surface of a bird's feather. 小羽枝相互连接，形成了鸟类羽毛的光滑表面。| Synonyms: barbules | Antonyms: None | noun

barbicel: /ˈbɑːrbɪsɛl/ | n. | 小羽枝，羽小枝 |  A small, hook-like projection on a barb of a feather that interlocks with other barbicels to create the smooth surface of the feather, crucial for flight and insulation. |  Damaged barbicels can affect a bird's ability to fly.  受损的小羽枝会影响鸟类的飞行能力。| Synonyms:  barbules | Antonyms: None | noun
