armisonous: /ɑːrmɪˈsɒnəs/
adjective
发出武器碰撞声的；战争的喧嚣声的
Producing or characterized by the sounds of clashing arms; resounding with the din of war.
The armisonous clamor of the battlefield filled the air. 战场上的武器碰撞声响彻天空。
The armisonous symphony of battle reached a crescendo as the two armies clashed.  两军交锋时，战争的喧嚣声达到了顶峰。
近义词: clangorous, resounding, boisterous
反义词: peaceful, quiet, silent
词性: adjective
词源: Latin armisonus, from arma (arms) + sonus (sound)
