Simple Present:
What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

Present Continuous:
What are they doing right now? = 他们现在在做什么？
What is he working on at the moment? = 他现在正在做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned so far? = 到目前为止你学到了什么？
What has he done to deserve this? = 他做了什么值得拥有这个？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been doing all afternoon? = 你整个下午都在做什么？
What has she been studying for the past few months? = 她在过去的几个月里一直在学习什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? = 她对这部电影说了些什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话铃响时你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? = 我看到她时她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you came here? = 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? = 她生病之前吃了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气怎么样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到今年年底你将取得什么成就？
What will they have finished by tomorrow afternoon? = 到明天下午他们将完成什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？
