hidage:/ˈhaɪdɪdʒ/| n.|土地税，以皮革面积计算的税|A tax formerly levied in England based on the number of hides of land. |The hidage was a significant source of revenue for the king. 这项土地税是国王重要的收入来源。|Synonym: land tax, property tax|Antonym: tax exemption, tax credit|noun
