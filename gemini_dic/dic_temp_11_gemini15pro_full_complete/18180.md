belam:/ˈbeɪləm/| n.|疯人；精神病院；混乱的场面|A lunatic; an insane asylum; a scene of uproar and confusion.|The party descended into a belam as the night wore on.  派对随着夜幕降临变得越来越混乱。|近义词：madhouse, bedlam, chaos, lunatic asylum|反义词:order, sanity, peace|词性:名词

词源: From the former Hospital of St. Mary of Bethlehem in London, used as an asylum for the insane.  源自伦敦的圣玛丽伯利恒医院，曾用作精神病院。
