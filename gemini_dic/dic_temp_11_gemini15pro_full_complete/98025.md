inordinate:/ɪnˈɔːrdɪnət/ /ɪnˈɔːrdɪnɪt/ | adj. | 过度的，过分的；无节制的 | Exceeding reasonable limits; excessive. |  She spent an inordinate amount of time on the project. 她在这个项目上花了过多的时间。

|  The restaurant charged inordinate prices for simple dishes. 这家餐厅对简单的菜肴收取过高的价格。 |  excessive, immoderate, unreasonable, exorbitant, extravagant | moderate, reasonable, temperate, modest | adjective
