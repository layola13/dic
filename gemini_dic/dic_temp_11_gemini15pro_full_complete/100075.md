interpolators:/ɪnˈtɜːrpəleɪtərz/| n. |插值器，插值程序|A device or program that estimates values between known data points.  It fills in the gaps.|Example: The image processing software uses sophisticated interpolators to enlarge images without pixelation.  图像处理软件使用复杂的插值器来放大图像而不会出现像素化。|Example:  Cubic spline interpolators are commonly used in computer graphics. 三次样条插值器常用于计算机图形学。|近义词: estimator, approximator|反义词: N/A|词性: Noun
