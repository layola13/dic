get-the-hook:/ɡet ðə hʊk/| idiom |被解雇，被撤职，被淘汰；被迫停止；被赶下台|To be dismissed or removed from a job, performance, or position, often suddenly or unexpectedly.  It can also mean to be forced to stop doing something or to be rejected.|例句：The actor got the hook after only two performances.  这位演员只演了两场就被解雇了。|近义词：be fired, be dismissed, be sacked, be let go, be axed|反义词：be hired, be employed|词性: idiom

get-the-hook:/ɡet ðə hʊk/| idiom |被拒绝，被抛弃(恋爱关系)|To be rejected or dumped by a romantic partner.|例句：He got the hook after she found out he'd been lying to her.  在她发现他一直在骗她之后，她把他甩了。|近义词：be dumped, be rejected, be jilted|反义词：be accepted, be loved|词性: idiom
