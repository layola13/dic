deride:/dɪˈraɪd/ /dəˈraɪd/| v. |嘲笑，奚落|To laugh at or make fun of someone or something in a way that shows you think they are stupid or of no value. |例句：The critics derided his latest novel as pretentious and derivative.  评论家嘲笑他最新的小说矫揉造作，毫无新意。

例句：They derided his efforts to improve community relations. 他们嘲笑他为改善社区关系所做的努力。


近义词：mock, ridicule, scorn, taunt|反义词: praise, admire, commend, laud |词性:动词
