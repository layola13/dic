goatskin:/ˈɡoʊtsˌskɪn/| n. |山羊皮|The skin of a goat, often used for making leather goods.|She carried water in a goatskin bag. 她用一个山羊皮袋子装水。|The drum was made of goatskin.  这个鼓是用山羊皮做的。|近义词：kidskin, lambskin|反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑其他动物的皮，例如：cowhide)|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *gotesskyn*, equivalent to goat + skin.
