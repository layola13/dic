lutulence:/ˈluːtjʊləns/
n.
泥泞；浑浊；污浊
the state of being muddy or cloudy
The lutulence of the river made it difficult to see the bottom. 河水的浑浊使得很难看到河底。

近义词:turbidity, cloudiness, muddiness, opacity
反义词:clarity, clearness, transparency
词性:名词
