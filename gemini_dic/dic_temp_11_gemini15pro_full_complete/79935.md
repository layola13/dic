gnomonic:/noʊˈmɑːnɪk/ | adj. |日晷的；投影的|Relating to or resembling a gnomon (the part of a sundial that casts the shadow); projected as if by a gnomon, especially in such a way that straight lines remain straight. |A gnomonic map projection is one where all great circles are represented as straight lines.  日晷投影地图是一种所有大圆都被表示为直线的地图。

The sundial's gnomonic projection accurately showed the time. 日晷的投影准确地显示了时间。|Synonyms: projective, orthographic|Antonyms: non-projective|Etymology: From Late Latin *gnomonicus*, from Greek gnōmonikos, from gnōmōn ‘gnomon, indicator.’|Word Type: Adjective
