abortifacient:/əˌbɔːrtɪˈfeɪʃənt/
n. adj.|堕胎药，流产药|A drug or other agent that induces abortion.|例句：The use of abortifacients is a controversial topic. 堕胎药的使用是一个有争议的话题。|例句：Some herbs are believed to have abortifacient properties.  一些草药被认为具有堕胎的特性。|近义词：abortion-inducing, aborticide|反义词:  contraceptive (避孕药 -  prevents pregnancy rather than terminates it)
词性:名词，形容词

词源:
From Latin *abortivus* (causing abortion) + -facient (making, causing).  *abortivus* is from *abortus* (abortion, miscarriage), past participle of *aborīrī* (to miscarry, abort).
