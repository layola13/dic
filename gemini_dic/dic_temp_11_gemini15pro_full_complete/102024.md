isohaemagglutinin:/ˌaɪsoʊˌhiːməˈɡluːtɪnɪn/ | n. | 同种血凝素 | An antibody that agglutinates red blood cells of other individuals of the same species, but not its own.  These determine blood type. | Example:  Isohaemagglutinins are responsible for the clumping reaction seen in incompatible blood transfusions. 同种血凝素是导致不相容输血中出现凝集反应的原因。

Example:  The presence of specific isohaemagglutinins in the blood serum determines an individual's ABO blood group.  血清中特定同种血凝素的存在决定了个体的ABO血型。 | Synonym: antibody, agglutinin | Antonym: N/A | noun
