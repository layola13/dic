bogan:/ˈboʊɡən/ (AU) /ˈbɒɡən/ (UK)| n. |粗俗的人，乡巴佬|A person who is regarded as unfashionable, uncouth, and unsophisticated. Often associated with a particular stereotype of lower-class, rebellious youth.|He acts like such a bogan. 他表现得像个十足的乡巴佬。|近义词：redneck, chav, yob|反义词:sophisticate, cosmopolitan|词性:noun

bogan:/ˈboʊɡən/ (AU) /ˈbɒɡən/ (UK)| adj. |粗俗的，乡巴佬的|Characteristic of or relating to a bogan.|She wears bogan clothes. 她穿得像个乡巴佬。|近义词：tacky, vulgar, uncouth|反义词:refined, sophisticated|词性:adjective
