indignations:/ˌɪndɪɡˈneɪʃənz/| n. |愤怒，愤慨，义愤|A feeling of anger or annoyance provoked by what is perceived as unfair treatment.|The public expressed their indignations at the government's decision. 公众对政府的决定表示愤慨。|近义词：anger, resentment, fury, outrage|反义词:calmness, contentment, pleasure|noun
