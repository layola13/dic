mesoderm: /ˈmezəˌdɜːrm/ (US), /ˈmezəˌdɜːm/ (UK) | n. | 中胚层 | The middle layer of the three primary germ layers of an embryo, from which connective tissue, muscle, bone, and the urogenital and circulatory systems develop. | The mesoderm gives rise to the skeletal muscles.  中胚层形成骨骼肌。|  Synonyms: mesoblast | Antonyms: ectoderm, endoderm | noun

mesoderm: /ˈmezəˌdɜːrm/ (US), /ˈmezəˌdɜːm/ (UK)| n. | 中胚层 | The mesoderm forms between the ectoderm and endoderm during gastrulation. 中胚层在原肠胚形成过程中形成于外胚层和内胚层之间。| Synonyms: mesoblast | Antonyms: ectoderm, endoderm | noun
