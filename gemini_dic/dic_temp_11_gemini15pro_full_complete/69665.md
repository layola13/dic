ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hwæt, nominative and accusative neuter singular of hwā (“who”). Cognate with Old Frisian hwet, Old Saxon hwat, Old High German hwaz (German was), Old Norse hvat (Icelandic hvað), Gothic ƕa.
