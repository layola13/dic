overprotective:/ˌōvərprəˈtektiv/ | adj. | 过度保护的，过分呵护的 | Providing or showing too much protection, especially by parents for their children. | Example:  Her parents were overprotective and wouldn't let her go out alone. 她的父母过度保护，不让她一个人出去。

Example: He's a bit overprotective of his car, he won't even let anyone else drive it. 他对他的车有点过度保护，他甚至不让别人开。 | 近义词: fussy, cosseting, shielding, sheltering | 反义词: neglectful, careless | 词性: adjective
