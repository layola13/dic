enzootic:/ɛnzoʊˈɒtɪk/ | adj. | 地方性的（疾病）；（疾病）限于某一地区的 |  (of a disease) regularly affecting animals in a particular district or at a particular season.  | Example: The enzootic disease affected the local deer population.  这种地方性疾病影响了当地的鹿群。 | Example:  Researchers are studying the enzootic patterns of the virus in the bird population. 研究人员正在研究该病毒在鸟类种群中的地方性模式。 | Synonyms: endemic, regional, localized | Antonyms: epidemic, pandemic | Adjective
