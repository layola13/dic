christom: /ˈkrɪsəm/ | n. | 圣衣，洗礼服 | A white robe worn by a newly baptized person, especially in the past. |  The baby was dressed in a christom for his baptism.  婴儿穿着洗礼服接受洗礼。 | synonyms: baptismal robe, christening gown | antonyms: None | noun

Etymology: From Old English *crism-hāma, from crisma "chrism" + hāma "covering."  Ultimately from Greek χρῖσμα (khrîsma) "anointing."
