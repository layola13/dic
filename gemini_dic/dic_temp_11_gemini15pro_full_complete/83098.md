gussied:/ˈɡʌsid/ | v. (past tense: gussied, past participle: gussied) |打扮；装饰；修饰| To dress or decorate, especially in a showy or elaborate way.  To make something more attractive or appealing. | She gussied herself up for the party. 她为派对盛装打扮。|The town had been gussied up for the festival.  小镇为了节日而被装饰一新。 | synonyms: dress up, adorn, decorate, embellish, primp, preen | antonyms: undress, disrobe, simplify | verb
