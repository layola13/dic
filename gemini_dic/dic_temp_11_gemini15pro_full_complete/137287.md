nonsyndication:/ˌnɒnˌsɪndɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 不联合，不辛迪加 |  The act or state of not being syndicated, especially in reference to media content like radio programs or newspaper columns.  This means the content is not distributed widely through a syndicate to multiple outlets. |  The radio show's nonsyndication limited its reach to a local audience.  这个广播节目因为没有被辛迪加，它的受众范围仅限于当地。 | Near Synonyms: non-distribution, exclusivity | Antonyms: syndication, distribution | noun

nonsyndicated:/ˌnɒnˈsɪndɪkeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 未联合的，未辛迪加的 | Not syndicated; not distributed widely through a syndicate to multiple outlets. | The nonsyndicated blog had a small but dedicated following.  这个没有被辛迪加的博客拥有一群虽小却忠实的追随者。 | The local newspaper published nonsyndicated comics. 当地报纸刊登了未联合的漫画。 | Near Synonyms: unsyndicated, exclusive, local | Antonyms: syndicated, distributed, national | adjective
