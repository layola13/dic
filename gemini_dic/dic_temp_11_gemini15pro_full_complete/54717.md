disowns:/dɪsˈoʊnz/ | v. | 否认，拒绝承认，断绝关系 | To refuse to acknowledge or accept any longer; renounce. | He disowned his son after he was convicted of the crime. 他在儿子被判有罪后与他断绝了关系。 |例句：The family disowned him after he joined a cult.  在他加入邪教后，家人与他断绝了关系。

例句：She disowned her previous statements, claiming they were made under duress. 她否认了自己之前的声明，声称那些声明是在胁迫下做出的。

近义词：renounce, repudiate, reject, deny | 反义词：acknowledge, accept, embrace, claim | 词性：动词
