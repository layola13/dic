impulsivenesses:/ɪmˈpʌlsɪvnəsɪz/
|n.|冲动，一时兴起|The state of being impulsive; a tendency to act on a whim.  The plural form of impulsiveness. |His impulsivenesses often led him into trouble. 他的冲动经常让他陷入困境。|Synonyms: rashness, recklessness, impetuousness|Antonyms: caution, prudence, deliberation|noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *impulsivus, from Latin impulsus (“impulse”), past participle of impellō (“I push, urge on”).  The suffix -ness forms nouns meaning "state or quality of being". The plural form is indicated by the -es ending.
