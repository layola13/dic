nonirritants:/nɒnˈɪrɪtənts/ | n. | 无刺激物 | Substances that do not cause irritation to the skin or other tissues. |  This cream is made with natural nonirritants, suitable for sensitive skin. 这款面霜采用天然无刺激成分制成，适合敏感肌肤。| synonyms:  soothers, emollients, demulcents | antonyms: irritants, allergens, stimulants | noun


nonirritating:/nɒnˈɪrɪteɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 无刺激性的 | Not causing irritation. | This shampoo is nonirritating and gentle on the scalp. 这款洗发水无刺激性，对头皮温和。| synonyms: soothing, gentle, mild | antonyms: irritating, harsh, abrasive | adjective


Etymology:

"non-" (prefix): from Latin "non" meaning "not".
"irritant": from Latin "irritare" meaning "to excite, provoke".  The "-ant" suffix forms nouns and adjectives.
