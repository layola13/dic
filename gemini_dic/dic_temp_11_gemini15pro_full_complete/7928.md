anteposition:/ˌænti.pəˈzɪʃ.ən/
n.
前置，提前
The placement of a word or phrase earlier in a sentence than it would normally occur.
The anteposition of the adjective "red" in "red delicious apple" emphasizes the color.  红色的美味苹果中，形容词“红色”的前置强调了颜色。
