baroscopic:/ˌbærəˈskɒpɪk/| adj.|气压的，与气压有关的|Relating to the baroscope or the observation of changes in atmospheric pressure.|Baroscopic readings can help predict weather changes. 气压读数可以帮助预测天气变化。|近义词:barometric|反义词: N/A|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From baro- (pressure) + -scopic (relating to observation).  "baro-" comes from Greek "baros" (weight, pressure). "-scopic" comes from Greek "skopein" (to look at).
