dischargeable:/dɪsˈtʃɑːrdʒəbl/
adj.
可解除的，可免除的
Able to be discharged or relieved of a debt, obligation, or responsibility.
The debt is dischargeable in bankruptcy. 这笔债务可以在破产时免除。
近义词:remissible, forgivable, excusable
反义词:non-dischargeable, unremissible
词性:形容词

dischargeable:/dɪsˈtʃɑːrdʒəbl/
adj.
可排放的
Able to be discharged or released, such as a fluid or waste material.
The hospital treats dischargeable wastewater before releasing it into the sewer system. 医院在将可排放的废水排入下水道系统之前对其进行处理。
近义词:releasable, emittable
反义词:non-dischargeable
词性:形容词


词源:  From dis- + chargeable (from charge + -able).  "Charge" ultimately comes from Late Latin *carricāre*, from *carrus* "wagon."
