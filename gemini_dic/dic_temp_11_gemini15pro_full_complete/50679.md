dermapteran: /dɜːrˈmæptərən/ | n. |迗蜓，革翅目昆虫|An insect of the order Dermaptera, characterized by short leathery forewings and a pair of forceps-like cerci at the tip of the abdomen.  | Earwigs are dermapterans known for their cerci. 迗蜓是革翅目昆虫，以其尾铗而闻名。| synonym: earwig | antonym: None | noun
