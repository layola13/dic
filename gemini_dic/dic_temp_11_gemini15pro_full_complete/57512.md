dragonfly:/ˈdræɡənflaɪ/ (US) /ˈdræɡənflaɪ/ (UK)| n.|蜻蜓|A predatory insect characterized by long body, large multifaceted eyes, two pairs of strong transparent wings, and the ability to hover and fly swiftly.|The dragonfly hovered over the pond. 蜻蜓在池塘上盘旋。|Synonyms: darner, skimmer, devil's darning needle|Antonyms: None|Noun


dragonfly:/ˈdræɡənˌflaɪ/|n.|蜻蜓|A fast-flying long-bodied predatory insect with two pairs of large transparent wings which are spread out sideways at rest.|I saw a beautiful blue dragonfly by the river. 我在河边看到一只美丽的蓝色蜻蜓。|Synonyms:  darner, skimmer|Antonyms: None|Noun
