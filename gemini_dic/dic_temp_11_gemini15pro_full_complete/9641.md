antistrumous:/ˌæntiˈstruːməs/
adjective
抗瘰疬的；治疗瘰疬的
Preventing or curing scrofula (a form of tuberculosis affecting the lymph nodes, especially in the neck).
The doctor prescribed an antistrumous liniment for the swelling in his neck. 医生给他脖子上肿胀的地方开了抗瘰疬的药膏。
Synonyms: antiscrofulous
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider words related to causing or exacerbating scrofula, but these would be highly technical and context-specific.)
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From anti- (against) + struma (swelling, especially scrofula) + -ous (adjective forming suffix).  Ultimately from Latin.
