looseners:/ˈluːsənərz/| n.|放松剂，松弛剂；开胃酒|Substances or activities that help to relax or loosen up, often before a more serious activity or event.  They can be physical, like stretching exercises, or social, like icebreaker games, or chemical, like alcoholic beverages.|Before the big presentation, we played some team-building games as looseners. 在重要的演讲之前，我们玩了一些团队建设游戏来放松一下。

The comedian told a few jokes as looseners before getting into his main routine.  喜剧演员在开始他的主要节目之前讲了几个笑话来活跃气氛。|近义词：relaxants, icebreakers, warm-ups|反义词：tighteners, stressors|词性:名词
