outlet:/ˈaʊtlet/ (US), /ˈaʊtlɛt/ (UK)| n.| 出口，排放口；经销店；发泄途径；插座|A place or opening through which something, such as water, air, or lava, is let out; a shop that sells goods, especially from a particular manufacturer, at reduced prices; a way of expressing or making use of strong feelings, ideas, or energy; a device in a wall into which an electric plug is inserted.|例句：The factory's outlet pipe poured waste into the river. 工厂的排污管向河里倾倒废物。

例句：They bought the furniture at a discount outlet. 他们在折扣店买了家具。

近义词：exit, vent, store, shop, market, expression, release|反义词：inlet, entrance|词性: 名词
