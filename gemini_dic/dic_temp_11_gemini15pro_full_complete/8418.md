anti-virus:/ˌæntiˈvaɪrəs/| adj. n.|抗病毒的，防病毒的；抗病毒软件|Designed to detect and destroy computer viruses.  旨在检测和销毁计算机病毒。|I use anti-virus software to protect my computer. 我使用杀毒软件来保护我的电脑。|近义词：antimalware, anti-spyware|反义词: pro-virus (This term isn't commonly used in the same context.  It refers to viral DNA integrated into a host genome.)|词性: 形容词，名词

词源: "anti-" (against) + "virus" (a microscopic infectious agent)  "anti-" 来自希腊语 "anti" (against, opposite). "virus" 来自拉丁语 "virus" (poison).
