joviality:/ˌdʒoʊviˈæləti/| n.|欢乐，愉快，高兴|The air was filled with joviality and laughter.  空气中充满了欢乐和笑声。|例句：His joviality was infectious, spreading cheer throughout the room. 他的快乐很有感染力，让整个房间都充满了欢乐。

例句：The festival was marked by music, dancing, and general joviality. 节日充满了音乐、舞蹈和普遍的欢乐。|近义词：cheerfulness, mirth, gaiety, merriment|反义词：gloom, sadness, melancholy|词性:noun
词源:From Late Latin *jovialitatem, from Latin *jovialis ("of Jupiter"), from Jovis ("Jupiter"). In ancient astrology, Jupiter was believed to be a source of happiness and good fortune.
