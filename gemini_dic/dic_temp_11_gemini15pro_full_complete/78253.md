germaine:/ʒɜːrˈmeɪn/| adj.|有关的，相关的；切题的|Relevant to a subject under consideration.|例句：Her remarks were not germane to the discussion. 她的评论与讨论无关。

例句：The lawyer objected, arguing that the witness's testimony was not germane to the case. 律师反对，认为证人的证词与案件无关。

近义词：relevant, pertinent, applicable, appropriate|反义词:irrelevant, unrelated, inapposite|词性:形容词
