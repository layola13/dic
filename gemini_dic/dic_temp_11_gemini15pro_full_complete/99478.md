interfering:/ˌɪntərˈfɪərɪŋ/ | adj. | 干扰的，干涉的 | hindering or preventing something from happening or developing properly |  The interfering noise made it hard to concentrate. 干扰性的噪音使人难以集中注意力。 |  intrusive, meddlesome, obstructive | helpful, facilitating | adjective

interfere:/ˌɪntərˈfɪr/ | v. | 干扰，干涉；介入 | to involve oneself in a situation when one’s involvement is not wanted or is not helpful |  Please don't interfere in my personal life. 请不要干涉我的私生活。 | intervene, meddle, intrude | abstain, refrain | verb
