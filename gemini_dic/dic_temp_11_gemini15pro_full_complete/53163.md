dippable:/ˈdɪpəbl/| adj.|可蘸的，适合蘸食的|Something that is dippable can be dipped into a sauce or other liquid food.|例句：These chicken nuggets are perfectly dippable in honey mustard. 这些鸡块非常适合蘸蜂蜜芥末酱。|近义词：dunkable|反义词:(None -  lack of a direct antonym.  One could argue something that is too solid or crumbly to dip is the opposite, but there isn't a single word for that.)|词性:形容词

dippable:/ˈdɪpəbl/| n.|适合蘸食的食物|A food item designed to be dipped.|例句：The dippables on the appetizer platter included carrots, celery, and pita bread.  开胃菜拼盘上的蘸酱食物包括胡萝卜、芹菜和皮塔饼。|近义词：finger food, snack|反义词:(None - similar reasoning as adjective form)|词性:名词
