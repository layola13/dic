moonfish:/ˈmuːnfɪʃ/ | n. | 翻车鱼，月鱼 | A large, oval-shaped ocean fish with a flattened body.  It is the heaviest bony fish in the world. |  例句：The moonfish drifted near the surface. 翻车鱼漂浮在水面附近。


例句：We saw a huge moonfish while scuba diving. 我们在水肺潜水时看到了一条巨大的翻车鱼。


近义词：ocean sunfish, mola | 反义词: None | 词性: noun


moonfish:/ˈmuːnfɪʃ/ | n. | 月鱼，鲹科的一种鱼 | A silvery food fish found in tropical and subtropical waters.  Several species are called moonfish. | 例句：The moonfish was grilled and served with lemon. 月鱼被烤熟，配上柠檬一起食用。


例句：Moonfish is a popular dish in some coastal regions. 月鱼在一些沿海地区是一道受欢迎的菜肴。


近义词：Lookdown, horsehead | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
