gibbousnesses:/ˈɡɪbəsnəsɪz/
noun
凸起，隆起；（尤指月亮）凸圆
the quality or state of being gibbous; a rounded swelling or protuberance, especially of the moon when more than half full.
The gibbousnesses of the moon were clearly visible in the night sky. 夜空中月亮的凸起清晰可见。
Synonyms: protuberances, swellings, bulges
Antonyms: concavities, depressions, hollows
n.

gibbous: /ˈɡɪbəs/
adjective
凸起的，隆起的；（尤指月亮）凸圆的
more than half but less than fully illuminated, as the moon between first quarter and full moon or between full moon and last quarter.  Swollen on one side; protuberant.
The gibbous moon shone brightly. 凸月闪耀着光芒。
Synonyms: bulging, protuberant, swelling
Antonyms: concave, depressed, hollow
adj.
