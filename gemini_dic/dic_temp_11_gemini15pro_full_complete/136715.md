nonsensational:/ˌnɒnsenˈseɪʃənəl/ | /ˌnɑːnsenˈseɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 非轰动的；不哗众取宠的；平淡的 | Not intended to or likely to arouse strong public interest or excitement; not sensational. | The report provided a nonsensational account of the events.  这份报告对事件进行了平淡的叙述。| The local newspaper gave a nonsensational, factual report of the town council meeting. 当地报纸对市议会会议作了平淡无奇、实事求是的报道。 | 近义词：unexciting, ordinary, commonplace, matter-of-fact, subdued | 反义词：sensational, exciting, dramatic, thrilling | 词性: adjective
