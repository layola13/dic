disangelical:/ˌdɪsænˈdʒɛlɪkəl/
adjective
恶魔般的；与天使相反的；邪恶的
Extremely wicked, devilish; the opposite of angelic.
He had a disangelical grin as he revealed his wicked plan. 他露出恶魔般的笑容，透露了他的邪恶计划。
Near Synonyms: demonic, diabolic, fiendish, satanic, infernal
Antonyms: angelic, seraphic, heavenly, divine
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation, reversal) + angelical (of or relating to angels).
