bernicle:/ˈbɑːnɪkəl/ | n. | 藤壶；茗荷儿；难以摆脱的人 | A marine crustacean with a hard shell, permanently attached to a surface, such as a rock or a ship's hull.  Also, figuratively, a person who clings tenaciously to something or someone. | The hull of the ship was covered in barnacles. 船体上覆盖着藤壶。 |  The old man was a barnacle at the club, always there, never leaving. 那位老人是俱乐部的常客，总是在那里，从不离开。 | synonyms: limpet, adherent, clinger | antonyms: nomad, wanderer | noun
