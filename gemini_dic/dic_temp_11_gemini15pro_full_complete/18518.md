benedictive:/ˌbɛnɪˈdɪktɪv/
adjective
祝福的，表示祝福的
Expressing or conveying a blessing.
May your future be filled with joy – a benedictive wish from a loving friend.  愿你的未来充满快乐——来自挚友的祝福。
The priest offered a benedictive prayer for the newly married couple.  牧师为新婚夫妇做了祝福祷告。
近义词:blessing, benedictory
反义词:maledictory, cursing
词性:adjective
