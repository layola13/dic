free-and-clear:/ˌfriː ænd ˈklɪr/
adjective
无债务的；完全自由的；完全摆脱…的
Completely free of debt, obligations, or encumbrances.
例句：After years of saving, they finally own their house free and clear.  多年储蓄后，他们终于无债拥有了自己的房子。

例句：He left the country free and clear of all charges. 他完全摆脱了所有指控，离开了这个国家。

近义词：unencumbered, debt-free, clear
反义词：indebted, mortgaged, encumbered
词性：adjective

词源：
"Free" comes from Old English "frēo," meaning "free, exempt, noble." "Clear" comes from Old French "cler," meaning "bright, shining," ultimately from Latin "clarus."  The combined phrase "free and clear" emphasizes the complete absence of any encumbrances.
