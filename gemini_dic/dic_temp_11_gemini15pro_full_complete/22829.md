bossest:/ˈbɒsɪst/ | adj. | 最老板的，最专横的 |  A non-standard and humorous superlative form of "bossy," meaning extremely domineering or controlling.  It's used informally and often implies disapproval or sarcasm. |  "She's the bossest person I've ever met, always telling everyone what to do." 她是我见过最专横的人，总是告诉每个人该做什么。| 近义词: bossiest, most domineering, most controlling | 反义词: submissive, meek, compliant |词性:形容词

Etymology:
From "bossy" + "-est" (superlative suffix). "Bossy" originates from "boss" (meaning a person in charge), with the suffix "-y" added to form an adjective.
