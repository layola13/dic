counterpetitioning: /ˌkaʊntər pəˈtɪʃənɪŋ/
| n. | 反请愿，对立请愿 | the act of submitting a petition in opposition to another petition |  The counterpetitioning gathered thousands of signatures, effectively blocking the original proposal. 反请愿书收集了数千个签名，有效地阻止了最初的提案。| Synonyms: opposing, contesting, challenging | Antonyms: supporting, endorsing, agreeing | noun

counterpetition: /ˌkaʊntər pəˈtɪʃən/
| n. | 反请愿书，对立请愿书 | a petition submitted in opposition to another petition | They filed a counterpetition to challenge the zoning change. 他们提交了一份反请愿书，对分区变更提出质疑。| Synonyms: opposing petition, counter-petition | Antonyms: supporting petition | noun

counterpetition: /ˌkaʊntər pəˈtɪʃən/
| v. | 提出反请愿，反对请愿 | to submit a petition in opposition to another petition |  Local residents counterpetitioned against the construction of a new highway. 当地居民对修建新高速公路提出了反请愿。| Synonyms: oppose by petition, challenge by petition | Antonyms: support by petition | verb
