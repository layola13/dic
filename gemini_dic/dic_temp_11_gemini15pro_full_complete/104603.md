kaolin:/ˈkeɪəlɪn/ | n. |高岭土，瓷土 |A fine white clay, consisting chiefly of kaolinite, used in making porcelain, paper, paint, etc. |Kaolin is an important ingredient in porcelain production. 高岭土是瓷器生产的重要成分。

Kaolin is used to make high-quality paper. 高岭土用于制造高质量的纸张。| 近义词: china clay, kaolinite | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *kaolin*, from Mandarin 高嶺土 (*gāolǐngtǔ*), literally “high ridge earth,” referring to the location where it was originally mined in China.
