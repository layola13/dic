hardworking:/ˈhɑːrdˌwɜːrkɪŋ/| adj.|勤奋的，努力工作的|Someone who is hardworking puts a lot of effort into their work.  A hardworking employee is dedicated and diligent.|She is a hardworking student and always gets good grades. 她是一个勤奋的学生，总是取得好成绩。|近义词：diligent, industrious, assiduous|反义词：lazy, indolent, slothful|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "hard" (meaning strenuous effort) + "working" (present participle of "work").
