accustomedly:/əˈkʌstəmədli/| adv.|习惯地，惯常地|近义词: habitually, usually, typically, normally, routinely|反义词: unusually, rarely, seldom, infrequently|词性:副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From accustomed + -ly.  Accustomed itself comes from the past participle of the verb accustom, which derives from Old French acostumer "to accustom, familiarize," from a- "to" + costume "custom, habit."
