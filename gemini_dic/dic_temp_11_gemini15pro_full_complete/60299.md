eggs:/ɛɡz/
|n.|鸡蛋|Oval-shaped reproductive bodies laid by female birds and other animals, often eaten as food.|I had scrambled eggs for breakfast. 我早餐吃了炒鸡蛋。|
近义词:ova (technical) 卵|
反义词:None (Eggs don't have a direct opposite.)|
词性:Noun
