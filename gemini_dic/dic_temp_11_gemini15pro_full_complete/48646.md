PHRASE->

(While "defensed" is grammatically correct, its usage is archaic.  Therefore, constructing phrases with it sounds unnatural. The following examples use the modern form "defended" to illustrate potential phrase usage.)

defended against:  He defended against the accusations. 他对这些指控进行了辩护。
defended himself/herself/itself: She defended herself bravely. 她勇敢地为自己辩护。
defended from:  The city was defended from attack. 这座城市得到了保护，免受攻击。
defended with:  He defended his actions with a weak excuse. 他用一个站不住脚的借口为自己的行为辩护。
defended by:  The castle was defended by a strong garrison. 这座城堡由一支强大的守备部队保卫。
