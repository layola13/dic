bailor:/ˈbeɪlər/| n.|委托人；寄托人|A bailor is a person who delivers goods to another person, the bailee, for a particular purpose, such as safekeeping or repair, under an agreement that the goods will be returned to the bailor or disposed of according to the bailor's instructions.|The bailor entrusted the bailee with his valuable collection of stamps. 委托人将自己珍贵的邮票收藏委托给了受托人。

The bailor requested the return of his car after the repairs were completed.  修理完成后，委托人要求归还他的汽车。|近义词：consignor, depositor|反义词：bailee|词性：noun
