lutherisms:/ˈluːθərɪzəmz/
n.
路德教义；路德宗的教义和实践
The doctrines and practices of Lutheranism.
Lutheranism is a major branch of Protestant Christianity that identifies with the theology of Martin Luther, a 16th-century German reformer. 路德教是基督教新教的一个主要分支，认同16世纪德国改革家马丁·路德的神学。
The pastor's sermon focused on key lutherisms. 牧师的布道集中讨论了路德教的主要教义。
近义词: Lutheran doctrines, Lutheran beliefs
反义词:  Catholic doctrines, Calvinist doctrines
词性: 名词
