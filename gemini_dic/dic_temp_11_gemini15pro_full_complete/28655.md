caricaturists:/ˌkærɪkəˈtʃʊərɪsts/| n. |漫画家；讽刺画家|An artist who creates caricatures, which are exaggerated portraits or depictions of people or things, often for humorous or satirical effect.|The political caricaturists mercilessly lampooned the prime minister. 政治漫画家无情地讽刺了首相。|Synonyms: cartoonists, satirists, lampooners|Antonyms: portraitists, realists|Noun

caricaturist:/ˌkærɪkəˈtʃʊərɪst/| n. |漫画家；讽刺画家|A person who draws caricatures.|He's a brilliant caricaturist; he can capture someone's likeness with just a few strokes. 他是一位才华横溢的漫画家；他只需几笔就能抓住一个人的相似之处。|Synonyms: cartoonist, satirist, lampooner|Antonyms: portraitist, realist|Noun
