aquatint: /ˈæk.wə.tɪnt/ | n. | 水彩画；铜版腐蚀制版法 | A method of etching that produces a tonal effect, rather than lines, by using powdered resin that is acid-resistant. |  The artist used aquatint to create a misty atmosphere in the background of the print. 艺术家使用水彩画在版画的背景中营造出朦胧的氛围。| etching, engraving | lithograph | noun
