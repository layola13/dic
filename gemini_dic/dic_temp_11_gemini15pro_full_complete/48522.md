deescalating:/diːˈeskəleɪtɪŋ/
|v. |使降级；使缓和；降低；减轻|reducing the intensity of a conflict or potentially dangerous situation|The police were trained in deescalating tense situations. 警察接受过缓和紧张局势的训练。|synonyms: reducing, lessening, mitigating, calming, pacifying|antonyms: escalating, intensifying, aggravating|verb

deescalating:/diːˈeskəleɪtɪŋ/
|adj. |降级的；缓和的|becoming less intense or serious|The deescalating conflict brought a sense of relief to the region. 冲突的缓和给该地区带来了轻松感。|synonyms: decreasing, diminishing, subsiding|antonyms: escalating, increasing, intensifying|adjective
