foreshadowing:/fɔːrˈʃædəʊɪŋ/ /fɔːrˈʃædoʊɪŋ/| n. |预示，伏笔|An indication of something that will happen in the future, often used in literature or film.|The dark clouds foreshadowed the coming storm.  乌云预示着暴风雨即将来临。|Synonyms: prefigure, portend, augur, forebode|Antonyms: conceal, hide|noun

foreshadow:/fɔːrˈʃædəʊ/ /fɔːrˈʃædoʊ/| vt. |预示，预兆|To indicate or suggest something that will happen in the future.|Her sudden illness foreshadowed her death a few months later. 她的突然生病预示着她几个月后的死亡。|Synonyms: prefigure, portend, augur, forebode|Antonyms: conceal, hide|verb
