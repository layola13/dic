de-stressed:/ˌdiːˈstrɛst/ | adj. | 减压的，放松的 |  Free from stress; relaxed. | After a long vacation, I felt completely de-stressed.  假期结束后，我感到完全放松了。| Synonyms: relaxed, calm, composed, serene | Antonyms: stressed, anxious, tense | adjective

Etymology:  "de-" (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + "stressed" (past participle of "stress," from Old French "estrece," meaning "narrowness," ultimately from Latin "stringere," meaning "to draw tight").
