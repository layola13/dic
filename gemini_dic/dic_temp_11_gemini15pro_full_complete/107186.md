lago:/ˈlɑːɡoʊ/ (US), /ˈleɪɡoʊ/ (UK)| n. |泻湖，环礁湖|A body of water separated from the sea by a narrow strip of land, such as a sandbar or coral reef.|例句：The Venetian lagoon is famous for its beauty. 威尼斯泻湖以其美丽而闻名。

例句：We sailed across the calm waters of the lagoon. 我们航行穿过平静的泻湖水域。

近义词：lake, pond, inlet|反义词:sea, ocean|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *lago* "lake," from Latin *lacus* "lake, pond, pool."
