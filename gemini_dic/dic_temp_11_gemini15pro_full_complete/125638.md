mumbo-jumbo:/ˈmʌmboʊ ˈdʒʌmboʊ/| n. | 莫名其妙的话；胡言乱语；繁文缛节 | meaningless ritualistic language or behavior; nonsense; gibberish |  The politician's speech was full of mumbo-jumbo that no one understood.  这位政客的演讲充满了没人听得懂的胡言乱语。 | synonyms: nonsense, gibberish, balderdash, claptrap, hogwash | antonyms: sense, clarity, lucidity | noun
Etymology:  Likely of West African origin, perhaps from a Mandinka word meaning "a masked devil who makes ritualistic pronouncements."  First recorded in English in the 18th century.
