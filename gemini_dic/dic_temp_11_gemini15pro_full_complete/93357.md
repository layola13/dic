illegibility:/ɪˌlɛdʒəˈbɪləti/ | n. |难以辨认，字迹模糊|The illegibility of his handwriting made it difficult to read the letter.  他的字迹潦草，难以辨认这封信的内容。|例句：The doctor's illegibility is legendary; no one can decipher his prescriptions.  这位医生的字迹模糊是出了名的；没有人能看懂他的处方。 |近义词：unclearness, obscurity, indecipherability|反义词: legibility, clarity, readability|词性:名词

illegible:/ɪˈlɛdʒəbəl/ | adj. |难以辨认的，字迹模糊的|The document was so faded that it was almost illegible. 文件褪色严重，几乎难以辨认。|例句：His signature was illegible, a mere scribble at the bottom of the page. 他的签名难以辨认，只是在页面底部潦草地涂鸦了一下。|近义词：unreadable, indecipherable, unclear|反义词: legible, clear, readable|词性:形容词
