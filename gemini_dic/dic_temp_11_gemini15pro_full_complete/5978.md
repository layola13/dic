ambulatory:/ˈæmbjʊləˌtɔːri/ /ˈæmbjəˌlætəri/| adj. n.|流动的，可走动的；门诊病人；回廊|A covered walkway, especially in a cloister, church, or hospital; of, relating to, or capable of walking.|例句：The hospital has a large ambulatory wing. 这家医院有一个很大的门诊部。|近义词：mobile, peripatetic, outpatient (n.)|反义词：bedridden, immobile, inpatient (n.)|词性: adjective, noun


ambulatory:/ˈæmbjʊləˌtɔːri/ /ˈæmbjəˌlætəri/| n.|门诊病人；回廊|A person who is able to walk; an outpatient.|例句：Ambulatories are often directed to the outpatient clinic.  门诊病人通常被引导到门诊诊所。|近义词：outpatient, walker|反义词：inpatient|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ambulatorium, from Latin ambulator ("walker"), from ambulare ("to walk").
