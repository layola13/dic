lynde:/lɪnd/
n.
菩提树;椴树
A lime tree.  Also, the wood of the lime.
The lynde tree provided welcome shade in the summer heat.  菩提树在夏日炎热中提供了受欢迎的阴凉。
近义词:lime, linden
反义词: (No direct antonym for a type of tree)
词性: 名词

lynde:/lɪnd/
n.
(古英语) 韧皮纤维，麻线
(Old English) Bast, fiber from the inner bark of the lime or linden tree, used for making rope or cord.
They used lynde to bind the sheaves of wheat.  他们用麻线捆扎小麦捆。
近义词:bast, fiber, twine
反义词: (No direct antonym for a material)
词性: 名词


词源: From Old English *lind or *linde, from Proto-Germanic *lindō, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *lent-. Cognate with Dutch linde, German Linde.
