accused: /əˈkjuːzd/ | adj. | 被指控的 | Charged with a crime. | The accused man was taken into custody. 被指控的男子被拘留。| synonym: charged, indicted | antonym: innocent, exonerated | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *acusé (past participle of acuser "to accuse"), from Latin accusatus (past participle of accusare "to call to account, accuse"), from ad- "to" + causa "cause, reason".
