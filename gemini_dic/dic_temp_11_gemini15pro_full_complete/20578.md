blackburnian:/blækˈbɜːrniən/
adjective|黑顶林莺的，与黑顶林莺有关的|Relating to or characteristic of the Blackburnian warbler (Setophaga fusca), a small New World warbler known for its bright orange throat and black-and-white patterned plumage.|The blackburnian warbler is a striking bird. 黑顶林莺是一种引人注目的鸟。|近义词:orange-throated, fire-throated (referring to the bird's appearance)|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective
Etymology: Named after Anna Blackburne, an English naturalist and collector.
