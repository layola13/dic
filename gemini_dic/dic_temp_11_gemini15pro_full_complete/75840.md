fumosity:/fjuːˈmɒsɪti/ /fjuːˈmɑːsɪti/
n.
烟雾弥漫; 烟熏味; 烟尘污染
The state or quality of being fumous; smokiness. The quality of being fuming or exhaling fumes or vapor.
The room was filled with a thick fumosity.  房间里充满了浓浓的烟雾。
The fumosity from the fire made it difficult to breathe. 火灾产生的烟雾使人难以呼吸。

近义词:smokiness, haze, fumes, vapor
反义词:clarity, clearness

词性:noun

词源: From Late Latin *fumōsitās, from Latin fumōsus (“smoky”) + -itās.  Ultimately from Latin fūmus (“smoke”).
