noninstitutionally:/ˌnɒnˌɪnstɪˈtjuːʃənəli/
adjective
非机构地，在机构外
outside of an institution; not within the confines of an institution.
The elderly man preferred to live noninstitutionally, remaining in his own home with the help of visiting caregivers.  这位老人更喜欢在机构外生活，在探访护理人员的帮助下留在自己的家中。
He was noninstitutionally cared for by his family. 他由家人在家照料。
近义词:domestically, privately, at home
反义词:institutionally
词性:adjective
