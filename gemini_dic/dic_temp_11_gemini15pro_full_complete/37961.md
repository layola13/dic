complexioned:/kəmˈplekʃənd/ | adj. | 肤色的，面色…的 | Having a particular complexion (skin color and appearance). |  She was a dark-complexioned woman with piercing eyes. 她是一位肤色黝黑的女子，目光锐利。

The fair-complexioned girl blushed easily.  这个肤色白皙的女孩很容易脸红。

近义词：Complexion, skin-toned | 反义词： None (No single antonym, as it describes a characteristic rather than a polar opposite) | 词性: Adjective
