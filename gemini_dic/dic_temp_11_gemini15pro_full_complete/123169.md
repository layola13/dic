monkishly:/ˈmʌŋkɪʃli/
adverb
僧侣般地；禁欲地；像修士一样地
In a manner resembling or characteristic of a monk, especially in terms of austerity, piety, or seclusion.
He lived monkishly, dedicating his life to prayer and study. 他过着僧侣般的生活，将一生奉献给祈祷和学习。
The scholar lived a monkishly existence, surrounded by books and manuscripts.  这位学者过着僧侣般的生活，周围都是书籍和手稿。
近义词: ascetically, austerely, piously
反义词: hedonistically, luxuriously, worldly
词性: adverb
词源: From monk + -ish + -ly.  Ultimately from Greek *monakhos* (alone, solitary).
