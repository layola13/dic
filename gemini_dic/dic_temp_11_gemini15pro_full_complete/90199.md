houlet:/ˈhuːlɪt/ | n. |  小猫头鹰 (尤指未成年猫头鹰) | A young owl, especially one that is not yet fully grown. |  The houlet perched silently on the branch. 小猫头鹰静静地栖息在树枝上。 |  Synonym: owlet | Antonym: (No direct antonym, possibly adult owl) | Noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *hūlet, equivalent to hūle (“owl”) +‎ -et (diminutive suffix). Cognate with Scots howlet (“owl”).
