词源:

This word is relatively recent slang, popularized in British English, particularly by London youth. While its precise origin is unclear, it's thought to be associated with or derived from the esteemed actress Dame Judi Dench, implying a level of quality and excellence associated with her performances.  It essentially transfers the admirable qualities of Dame Judi Dench to other people or things.
