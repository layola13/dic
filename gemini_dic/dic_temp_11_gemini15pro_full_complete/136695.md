It pertains to features of speech that are not limited to a single sound segment but apply to a stretch of speech. |  The meaning was conveyed nonsegmentally through intonation.  意义是通过语调非音段地表达的。| Synonyms: prosodically, suprasegmentally | Antonyms: segmentally |  Adverb
