outawe:/ˌaʊtˈɔː/ | v. | 使敬畏；使惊叹 | to inspire awe in; to fill with profound admiration and respect | The vastness of the Grand Canyon outawed us. 大峡谷的广阔让我们敬畏。 | 近义词: amaze, astound, awe, overwhelm | 反义词: bore, disappoint | 词性: 动词

词源:  "Out-" (超越) + "awe" (敬畏).  The prefix "out-" intensifies the meaning of "awe," suggesting surpassing or exceeding the usual feeling of awe.
