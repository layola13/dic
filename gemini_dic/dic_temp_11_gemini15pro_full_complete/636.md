abreast of: /əˈbrɛst əv/ | adj. | 知晓，了解；并驾齐驱 | up to date with the latest news, information, or developments; alongside |  例句：She makes a point of keeping abreast of the latest developments in cancer research. 她坚持了解癌症研究的最新进展。 | 例句：The two runners were abreast of each other as they approached the finish line.  两位赛跑者在接近终点线时并驾齐驱。 | 近义词：up to date with, informed about, aware of, alongside, level with | 反义词：ignorant of, behind the times, uninformed | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From the nautical term "abreast," meaning "side by side," literally "breast to breast."  Over time, the meaning broadened to encompass keeping up with information.
