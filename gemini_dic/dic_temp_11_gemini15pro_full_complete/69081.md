faunally:/ˈfɔːnəli/
adjective
动物区系的；动物群的
Relating to the animals of a particular region or period.
The faunal diversity of the Amazon rainforest is astonishing. 亚马逊雨林的动物多样性令人震惊。
The study focused on the faunal changes after the volcanic eruption.  这项研究的重点是火山爆发后的动物群变化。

近义词:zoological
反义词:floral

词性:adjective
