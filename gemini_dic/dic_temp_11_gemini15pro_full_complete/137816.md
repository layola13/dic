nonvertical:/ˌnɒnˈvɜːtɪkəl/
adjective
非垂直的
Not vertical; not perpendicular to a horizontal plane or line.
The ramp was nonvertical, allowing for easier wheelchair access.  这个坡道不是垂直的，方便轮椅通行。
The painting hung at a nonvertical angle, giving it a dynamic feel. 这幅画挂在一个非垂直的角度，给人一种动感。
near synonyms: slanted, inclined, sloping
antonyms: vertical, perpendicular
词性: adjective
