myriad:/ˈmɪriəd/ | adj. n. |无数的，大量的；无数，大量|A countless or extremely great number.|例句：A myriad of stars twinkled in the night sky. 无数星星在夜空中闪烁。|近义词：innumerable, countless, numerous, multitude|反义词:few, scarce|词性:形容词, 名词

myriad:/ˈmɪriəd/ | n. |无数，大量|A countless or extremely great number.|例句：There are a myriad of possibilities. 有无数的可能性。|近义词：innumerable, countless, numerous, multitude|反义词:few, scarce|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *myriade*, from Late Latin myrias, from Ancient Greek μυριάς (murías, “ten thousand”), from μύριοι (múrioi, “ten thousand”).
