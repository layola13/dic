in effect:/ɪn ɪˈfekt/| adv.|实际上；生效；有效|In practice; in operation.  The new law is now in effect. 新法律现已生效。|Synonyms: effectively, practically, essentially, virtually|Antonyms: ineffective, invalid, null, void|adverb

in effect:/ɪn ɪˈfekt/| adj.|有效的；实质上的；事实上|Valid, operative; in essence, in fact.  Although not formally stated, the new rule is in effect a ban. 尽管没有正式声明，但新规实际上是一项禁令。|Synonyms: valid, operative, effective, actual|Antonyms: ineffective, invalid, null, void|adjective
