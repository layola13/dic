measurement's:/ˈmeʒərmənts/ | n. |测量；尺寸；测量结果；度量 |The measurement's accuracy is crucial for the experiment. 这个测量的准确性对实验至关重要。|例句：The tailor took the customer's measurements.  裁缝量了顾客的尺寸。

The room's measurements are 10 feet by 12 feet.  房间的尺寸是10英尺乘12英尺。 | 近义词：dimension, size, reading | 反义词: estimation, guess | 词性:名词(所有格)

Etymology: From Old French *mesurement (Modern French mesurement), from mesure (“measure”) + -ment.  Ultimately from Latin mensūra (“measure”).
