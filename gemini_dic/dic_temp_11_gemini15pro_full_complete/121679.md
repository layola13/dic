misplaying:/ˌmɪsˈpleɪɪŋ/
verb
玩得不好，下错(棋)，打错(牌)，演奏得不好，处理不当
Playing poorly or making a mistake in a game, performance, or handling of a situation.
He misplayed the final hand and lost the poker game. 他最后一手牌打错了，输掉了扑克游戏。
近义词: bungling, mishandling, flubbing, botching
反义词:  playing well, excelling, succeeding
词性: Verb (Gerund or present participle)


misplay: /ˌmɪsˈpleɪ/
noun
失误，失着
A mistake in playing a game or performing.
His misplay cost him the match. 他的失误让他输掉了比赛。
近义词: error, blunder, mistake, slip-up
反义词: good play, skillful move
词性: Noun
