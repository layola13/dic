non-fiction: /ˌnɑːnˈfɪkʃən/ | /ˌnɒnˈfɪkʃən/ | n. adj. | 非虚构，纪实文学 |  Prose writing that is based on facts, real events, and real people, such as biography or history. |  I prefer non-fiction books to novels. 我更喜欢非虚构类书籍而不是小说。 |  She's a well-known author of non-fiction. 她是一位著名的非虚构类作家。 | 近义词: factual, true, real | 反义词: fiction, fictional, fabricated | 词性: 名词, 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + fiction.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "fiction" comes from the Latin word "fictio," meaning "a shaping, a feigning."
