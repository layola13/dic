bashfulness:/ˈbæʃfəlnəs/| n.|害羞，羞怯|The state of being bashful; shyness or timidity. |Her bashfulness prevented her from speaking up in class. 她的害羞使她无法在课堂上发言。|近义词：shyness, timidity, modesty, diffidence|反义词：boldness, confidence, assertiveness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From bashful + -ness.  Bashful comes from Middle English *bascheful*, probably from *abaschen* (to abash) + -ful. Ultimately from Old French *esbahir* (to astonish).
