archetype:/ˈɑːrkɪtaɪp/| n.|原型，典型，榜样|An original model or pattern from which other later copies are made, especially a character, an action, or situation that seems to represent common patterns of human life.|例句：The hero is a classic archetype in literature.  英雄是文学中的经典原型。

例句：She is the archetype of a successful businesswoman. 她是一位成功女商人的典范。|近义词：prototype, model, exemplar, original, paradigm|反义词：copy, imitation|词性:noun
