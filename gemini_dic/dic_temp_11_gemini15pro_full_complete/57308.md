downstreamer:/ˌdaʊnˈstriːmər/ | n. |下游用户，下游企业|A company or individual that uses or processes the output of another company or process, especially in the oil and gas industry, but also more broadly in supply chains.|  The downstreamers are complaining about the rising cost of raw materials. 下游用户抱怨原材料成本上涨。| The new regulations will impact downstreamers in the chemical industry. 新法规将影响化学工业的下游企业。| 近义词:customer, client, end user| 反义词:upstreamer | 词性:名词

downstream:/ˌdaʊnˈstriːm/ | adj. |下游的，顺流的|Located or moving downstream; further along in a process or supply chain.| The downstream factory processes the raw materials into finished goods. 下游工厂将原材料加工成成品。| Downstream operations are affected by upstream decisions. 下游运营受到上游决策的影响。| 近义词:further down the line| 反义词:upstream | 词性:形容词
