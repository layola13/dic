dalmatian:/dælˈmeɪʃən/ | n. |达尔马提亚狗；达尔马提亚人 |A breed of large, short-coated dog, readily recognized by its white coat marked with distinct round spots of black or liver brown.  Also, a native or inhabitant of Dalmatia, a historical region on the eastern shore of the Adriatic Sea. |例句：The dalmatian puppy playfully chased a butterfly in the garden.  那只达尔马提亚小狗在花园里嬉戏地追逐着一只蝴蝶。

例句：The firefighter proudly showed off the station's dalmatian. 消防员自豪地展示了消防站的达尔马提亚犬。|近义词：dog, canine (general terms), spotted dog |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
