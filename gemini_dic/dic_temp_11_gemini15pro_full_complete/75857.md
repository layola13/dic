functional:/ˈfʌŋkʃənəl/ | adj. | 功能的；实用的；运行的 | Designed for or adapted to a particular function or use; practical and useful rather than decorative. |  This is a functional but not very attractive piece of furniture. 这是一件实用但不怎么好看的家具。| practical, useful, operative, working | ornamental, decorative, nonfunctional | adjective


functional:/ˈfʌŋkʃənəl/ | n. | 功能性；实用性 | The kind of action or activity proper to a person, thing, or institution; practical use or purpose. | The design emphasizes functionality over aesthetics.  这个设计强调功能性而非美观。| practicality, usefulness, utility | uselessness, impracticality | noun
