anisostemonous:/ˌænˌaɪsoʊˈstɛmənəs/
adj.
不等雄蕊的；雄蕊数目或长度不相等的
Having stamens of unequal length or number.
The anisostemonous flower has stamens of varying lengths, a characteristic that aids in pollination by different insects.  不等雄蕊的花朵雄蕊长度各异，这一特征有助于不同昆虫授粉。
The botanist identified the plant as a member of the Lamiaceae family due to its anisostemonous flowers.  植物学家根据其不等雄蕊的花朵，将该植物鉴定为唇形科的一员。
近义词: heterostemonous
反义词: isostemonous
词性: adjective
词源: From Greek *ánisos* (unequal) + *stēmōn* (thread, warp, stamen).
