helmwind:/ˈhɛlmˌwɪnd/| n.|舵风；顺风|A wind that blows from astern; a following wind.  A wind that blows from behind a ship, pushing it forward.|The helmwind filled the sails and sped the ship on its journey. 舵风鼓满了帆，加快了船只的航行速度。| The helmwind propelled the small boat across the lake. 顺风推动着小船穿过湖面。|近义词:tailwind, following wind|反义词:headwind|词性:noun
