galvanizations:/ˌɡælvənaɪˈzeɪʃənz/
n.
镀锌；激励；通电
The process of applying a protective zinc coating to iron or steel to prevent rusting; an instance of stimulating or exciting someone or something into action.
The bridge underwent several galvanizations to protect it from the harsh sea air.  这座桥经过多次镀锌以防止其受恶劣海风的侵蚀。
The coach's speech was a galvanization for the team. 教练的讲话激励了整个团队。
近义词: coatings, protections, inspirations, motivations
反义词: demotivations, suppressions
