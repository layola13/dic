nonskeptics:/ˌnɑːnˈskeptɪks/ | n. |非怀疑论者；不怀疑者|People who do not express skepticism or doubt about something.  Those who tend to believe or accept claims without rigorous questioning.|例句：The nonskeptics readily embraced the new technology. 非怀疑论者欣然接受了这项新技术。

例句：The presentation was convincing even to the nonskeptics in the audience.  即使是对观众席上的非怀疑论者来说，这场演示也很有说服力。 |近义词：believers, acceptors, adherents|反义词: skeptics, doubters, cynics|词性:noun
