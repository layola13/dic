blow-my-stack:/bloʊ maɪ stæk/| idiom |勃然大怒，大发雷霆|To become extremely angry; to lose one's temper.  This idiom evokes the image of a smokestack exploding.|例句：When my computer crashed for the third time, I blew my stack. 当我的电脑第三次崩溃时，我勃然大怒。

|近义词：lose one's temper, hit the roof, fly off the handle, go ballistic, go ape, flip out|反义词: stay calm, keep one's cool | idiom
