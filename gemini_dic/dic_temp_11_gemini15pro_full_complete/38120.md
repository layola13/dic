comprehendingly:/ˌkɒmprɪˈhɛndɪŋli/ | adv. |理解地，领悟地 | In a way that shows understanding or comprehension. | She nodded comprehendingly as he explained the complex situation. 她在他解释复杂情况时理解地点了点头。|  She listened comprehendingly to his explanation. 她理解地听着他的解释。| synonym: understandingly, perceptively, knowingly | antonym: uncomprehendingly, blankly, obtusely | 词性: 副词

comprehend:/ˌkɒmprɪˈhɛnd/ | v. |理解，领会 | To understand something fully. | I struggled to comprehend the complex scientific theory. 我努力理解这复杂的科学理论。 | He couldn't comprehend the magnitude of the problem. 他无法理解问题的严重性。| synonym: understand, grasp, perceive | antonym: misunderstand, misinterpret | 词性: 动词

comprehension:/ˌkɒmprɪˈhɛnʃən/ | n. |理解，领会 | The ability to understand something. | Reading comprehension is an essential skill. 阅读理解是一项基本技能。 |  His comprehension of the subject was limited. 他对这门学科的理解有限。| synonym: understanding, grasp, perception | antonym: incomprehension, confusion | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Latin *comprehendere, present active participle *comprehendens, from Latin comprehendō (“I grasp, seize, comprise, include”), from com- (“together”) + prehendō (“I grasp, seize”).
