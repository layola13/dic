overdiscounting: /ˌoʊvərˈdɪskaʊntɪŋ/
verb (present participle) | 过度贴现，过低估价 | Applying an excessive discount, either to the present value of future rewards or to the likelihood of future events.  It reflects a strong preference for immediate gratification or a pessimistic outlook on the future. | Investors are overdiscounting the potential for long-term growth. 投资者过低地估计了长期增长的潜力。 | Synonyms: undervaluing, underestimating, shortchanging | Antonyms: overvaluing, overestimating | v.

overdiscounting: /ˌoʊvərˈdɪskaʊntɪŋ/
noun | 过度贴现，过低估价 | The act of applying an excessive discount. | The overdiscounting of future profits led to poor investment decisions. 对未来利润的过度贴现导致了糟糕的投资决策。 | Synonyms: undervaluation, underestimation | Antonyms: overvaluation, overestimation | n.
