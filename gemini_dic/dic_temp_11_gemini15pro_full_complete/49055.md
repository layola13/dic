dehortatory:/dɪˈhɔːrtətɔːri/
adjective
劝阻的，阻止的
Trying to dissuade someone from doing something.
The teacher's dehortatory speech discouraged students from cheating on the exam. 老师的劝阻性讲话劝退了学生在考试中作弊。
dehortatory:/dɪˈhɔːrtətɔːri/
adjective
劝阻的，阻止的
Intended to warn against or advise against something.
The pamphlet contained dehortatory advice against smoking. 这本小册子包含了劝人戒烟的建议。


近义词: dissuasive, admonitory, cautionary
反义词: hortatory, encouraging, promoting
词性: adjective
