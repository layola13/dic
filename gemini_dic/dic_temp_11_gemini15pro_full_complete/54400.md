disimprison:/dɪsɪmˈprɪzən/
verb
释放，使从狱中释放
To release from prison; to set free.
The governor disimprisoned the wrongly convicted man. 州长释放了被冤枉入狱的人。
Synonyms: liberate, release, free, discharge, exonerate
Antonyms: imprison, incarcerate, confine, detain, jail
词性:动词
