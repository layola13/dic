hyetograph:/ˈhaɪɪtəɡræf/ | n. | 雨量图；雨量记录图 | A chart showing the distribution of rainfall over time, often used in hydrology and meteorology to analyze rainfall patterns. | The hyetograph clearly showed the intensity and duration of the storm. 雨量图清楚地显示了暴风雨的强度和持续时间。| Example 2:  Hydrologists use hyetographs to understand flood risks. 水文学家使用雨量图来了解洪水风险。|Synonym: rainfall chart, precipitation chart | Antonym: N/A | noun
