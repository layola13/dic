Etymology:  From "dreaminess" + "-es" (plural suffix).  "Dreaminess" comes from "dreamy" + "-ness" (suffix forming abstract nouns).  "Dreamy" comes from "dream" + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *draugmaz, meaning "illusion, deception."
