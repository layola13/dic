haematoblast:/ˈhiːmætəblæst/ /ˈhemætəblæst/ | n. |血胚, 血岛 |An immature blood cell, specifically a nucleated cell normally occurring in bone marrow. It is considered a precursor of  erythrocytes, leukocytes, and platelets. |The scientist observed the haematoblast under a microscope. 科学家在显微镜下观察了血胚。|Synonym:  hemocytoblast, blood cell precursor |Antonym: N/A |Noun
