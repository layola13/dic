grysbok: /ˈɡrɪsbɒk/ (UK) /ˈɡreɪsbɒk/ (US)| n. |灰羚羊|A small, grayish-brown antelope found in southern Africa. |例句：The grysbok is a shy and elusive creature. 灰羚羊是一种害羞且难以捉摸的生物。

例句：We spotted a grysbok grazing in the early morning mist. 我们在清晨的薄雾中发现了一只灰羚羊在吃草。|近义词：antelope, steenbok |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Afrikaans *grysbok*, from *grys* ("grey") + *bok* ("buck," male antelope). Ultimately from Middle Dutch *grijs* and *boc*.
