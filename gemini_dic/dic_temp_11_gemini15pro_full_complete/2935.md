advocates:/ˈædvəkɪts/| n.|拥护者，提倡者，辩护律师|A person who publicly supports or recommends a particular cause or policy.|She is a strong advocate for animal rights. 她是一位坚定的动物权利倡导者。|近义词：supporter, proponent, champion, lawyer|反义词：opponent, critic, adversary|词性:noun

advocates:/ˈædvəkeɪts/| v.|提倡，主张，拥护，辩护|Publicly recommend or support.|The organization advocates for stricter environmental regulations. 该组织倡导更严格的环境法规。|近义词：support, promote, champion, recommend|反义词：oppose, criticize, condemn|词性:verb
