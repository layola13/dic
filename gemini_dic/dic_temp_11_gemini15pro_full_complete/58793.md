dunts:/dʌnts/
noun
重击声; 撞击声
the sounds of heavy blows or impacts.
The dunts of the hammer echoed through the workshop.  锤子的重击声在车间里回荡。
近义词:thuds, bangs, clangs
反义词:taps, whispers
词性:n.

dunts:/dʌnts/
verb
(用力地)敲击，击打
to hit or strike (something) heavily.
He dunts the table with his fist in frustration. 他沮丧地用拳头敲桌子。
近义词:bangs, bashes, pounds
反义词:taps, strokes
词性:v.


词源:
Likely a variant of "dint," from Old English "dynt," meaning "blow" or "stroke."  可能源于“dint”的变体，来自古英语“dynt”，意思是“打击”或“击打”。
