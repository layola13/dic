kwaito:/ˈkwaɪtoʊ/
noun
快托音乐（南非的一种电子音乐流派）
A genre of electronic music that emerged in South Africa in the 1990s, characterized by a slow tempo and heavy bass lines.
Example: Kwaito music became a symbol of post-apartheid South Africa.  快托音乐成为后种族隔离时代南非的象征。

Example:  Many young South Africans identify with kwaito. 许多南非年轻人认同快托音乐。
Synonym:  house music,  gqom
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps traditional Afrikaans music)
词性: noun
