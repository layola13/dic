interoscillated:/ˌɪntərˈɒsɪleɪtɪd/
verb
相互振荡，交替振荡
To swing back and forth between two states or conditions, alternating with each other.
The two pendulums interoscillated, their movements perfectly synchronized. 两个钟摆相互振荡，它们的运动完美同步。
The stock market interoscillated between gains and losses throughout the day. 股市整天在涨跌之间波动。
Synonyms: alternate, fluctuate, oscillate, swing
Antonyms: stabilize, remain constant
词性: 动词
