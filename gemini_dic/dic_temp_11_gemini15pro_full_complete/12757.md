assign:/əˈsaɪn/| vt.|分配；指派；指定；委派；确定；转让|to give someone a particular job or task|The teacher assigned us a lot of homework. 老师给我们布置了很多作业。|近义词:allocate, allot, apportion, designate, give|反义词: withhold, keep, retain|verb

assign:/əˈsaɪn/| vt.|指定；确定；赋值|to decide that something has a particular value or function|The value 10 was assigned to the variable x.  变量 x 被赋值为 10。|近义词:attribute, ascribe, fix, set|反义词: N/A|verb

assign:/əˈsaɪn/| vt.|转让（财产、权利等）|to legally give (property, rights, etc.) to someone else|He assigned his copyright to the publisher. 他把他的版权转让给了出版商。|近义词:transfer, convey, cede, grant|反义词: N/A|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French assigner, from Latin assignare "to mark out, allot, appoint," from ad- "to" + signare "to mark," from signum "mark, sign."
