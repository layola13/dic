disrepute:/dɪsrɪˈpjuːt/
n.
不名譽，壞名聲
The state of being held in low esteem by the public.
His behavior brought the family into disrepute. 他的行为使家庭蒙羞。

近义词: disgrace, discredit, ignominy, opprobrium, shame
反义词: repute, esteem, honor, respect
词性: 名词
