forthrightness:/ˌfɔːrθˈraɪtnəs/
n.
直率，坦率
The quality or characteristic of being forthright; directness and frankness of speech or manner.
Her forthrightness can be mistaken for rudeness. 她的直率可能会被误认为粗鲁。
He admired her forthrightness and honesty. 他钦佩她的直率和诚实。
