bromide:/ˈbroʊmaɪd/ (US) /ˈbrəʊmaɪd/ (UK)| n.|溴化物；镇静剂；陈词滥调|A binary compound of bromine with another element or radical.  A commonplace or tiresome saying; a platitude. |例句：Potassium bromide is used as a sedative. 溴化钾用作镇静剂。

例句：His speech was full of bromides about the importance of hard work. 他的演讲充满了关于努力工作重要性的陈词滥调。|近义词：platitude, cliché, commonplace|反义词:originality, novelty|noun
