low-rent:/ˌloʊˈrɛnt/| adj.|低租金的；廉价的；低档的|Of or relating to something inexpensive or of poor quality, often implying shabbiness or seediness.  Referring to places or things that are cheap and often not very nice.|That motel was pretty low-rent.  那家汽车旅馆相当破旧廉价。|synonyms: cheap, inexpensive, sleazy, tacky, down-market|antonyms: expensive, upscale, high-end|adjective

low-rent:/ˌloʊˈrɛnt/| n.|低租金的住房|Housing that is inexpensive, often implying poor quality or condition.|There isn't much low-rent available in this city anymore.  这个城市已经没有多少廉价住房了。|synonyms: cheap housing, affordable housing (sometimes)|antonyms: luxury housing, expensive housing|noun
