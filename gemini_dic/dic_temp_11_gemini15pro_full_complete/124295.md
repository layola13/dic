mother-ships:/ˈmʌðərʃɪps/ | n. | 母舰 | A large spacecraft that carries or launches smaller spacecraft.  It acts as a base or hub for smaller vehicles. |  The mother-ship released a swarm of drones. 母舰释放了一群无人机。

The astronauts returned to the mother-ship after exploring the asteroid. 宇航员在探索小行星后返回了母舰。 | synonyms: flagships, carriers, command ships | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps:  shuttle, lander, probe) | noun
