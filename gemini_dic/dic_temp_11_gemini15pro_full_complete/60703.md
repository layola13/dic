electro-acoustic:/ɪˌlektrəʊ əˈkuːstɪk/
adjective
电声的
Relating to or involving a combination of electronic and acoustic elements.
An electro-acoustic transducer converts acoustic waves into electrical signals, or vice versa.  电声换能器将声波转换成电信号，反之亦然。

The band uses a variety of electro-acoustic instruments in their performances.  这支乐队在他们的表演中使用了各种电声乐器。

adjective

Relating to or involving a combination of electronic and acoustic elements.
Electroacoustic music often blends traditional instruments with electronic sounds.  电声音乐通常将传统乐器与电子声音混合在一起。

They installed an electroacoustic system in the concert hall.  他们在音乐厅安装了电声系统。

近义词:electronic, audio
反义词:acoustic (in some contexts, where it refers to purely acoustic systems)
词性:adjective



ETYMOLOGY->
electro-  from Greek *ēlektron* meaning "amber," referring to static electricity.
acoustic - from Greek *akoustikos* meaning "of or for hearing," from *akouein* "to hear."
