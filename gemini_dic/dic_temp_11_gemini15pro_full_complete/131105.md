nonbiliously:/ˌnɒnbɪliəsli/
adverb
非胆汁性地;不伴胆汁地
without bile or the characteristics of bile; not bilious.
The patient vomited nonbiliously after the surgery.  病人术后呕吐，没有胆汁。
Near synonyms: nonbilious, abilious
Antonyms: bilious
词性: 副词
