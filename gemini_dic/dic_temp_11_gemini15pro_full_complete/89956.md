horseradish:/ˈhɔːrsˌrædɪʃ/ (US) /ˈhɔːsˌrædɪʃ/ (UK)| adj. (less common)|辣根味的|Having the sharp taste or smell of horseradish.|The sauce had a distinctly horseradish flavor. 这酱汁明显有辣根味。|Synonym: pungent, sharp |Antonym: bland, mild|adjective
