lead-time:/ˈliːd taɪm/ (US) /ˈliːd taɪm/ (UK)| n.|交付周期；准备时间；提前期|The time between the initiation and completion of a production process.|The lead time for this project is six months. 这个项目的交付周期是六个月。|Synonyms: turnaround time, delivery time, waiting time|Antonyms: delay, lag|noun
