nonallegiance:/ˌnɒnəˈliːdʒəns/
n.|不效忠；不效力；不效顺|The state of not pledging allegiance to a nation or group.  Not owing or expressing loyalty to a country or ruler.|He declared his nonallegiance to any political party. 他宣称他不效忠任何政党。|Near synonyms: disloyalty, unfaithfulness, treachery|Near antonyms: allegiance, loyalty, fidelity|noun
