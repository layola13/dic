overreaction:/ˌoʊvərriˈækʃən/| n.|过度反应|An excessive reaction to something; a response that is disproportionate to the stimulus.|例句：Her anger was a complete overreaction to his innocent comment. 她的愤怒对他无辜的评论完全是过度反应。

例句：The stock market's overreaction to the news caused a sharp drop in prices. 股市对这一消息的过度反应导致价格大幅下跌。|近义词：overkill, hysteria, exaggeration|反义词:underreaction, calmness, composure|词性:noun
