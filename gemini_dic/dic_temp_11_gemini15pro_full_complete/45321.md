cumidine:/ˈkjuːmɪdiːn/ |n.| 积香胺; 枯茗胺|An aromatic amine, C9H13N, derived from cumene and used in the manufacture of dyes and other organic compounds.|Cumidine is an important intermediate in the production of certain herbicides.|近义词: aminocumene (less common), isopropyltoluidine (more specific, depending on the isomer)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
