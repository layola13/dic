embracingly:/ɪmˈbreɪsɪŋli/
adverb
拥抱地，亲切地
In a manner that expresses affection or acceptance, like a warm embrace.
She smiled embracingly at her son. 她亲切地对儿子笑了笑。
Near Synonyms: warmly, affectionately, tenderly, lovingly
Antonyms: coldly, distantly, indifferently
词性: 副词
