alamos:/ˈæləməʊz/ | n. |白杨树（复数），阿拉莫斯（地名）| Poplar trees; a place name, specifically referring to several places, most famously The Alamo in San Antonio, Texas. |  The alamos lining the river swayed gently in the breeze. 河边的白杨树在微风中轻轻摇曳。|  We visited the Alamo in San Antonio. 我们参观了圣安东尼奥的阿拉莫。| 近义词:poplars (for the tree) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From Spanish *álamo* "poplar tree," ultimately from Latin *albus* "white."
