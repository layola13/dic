jutlandish:/ˈdʒʌtləndɪʃ/
adjective

Relating to or characteristic of Jutland, a peninsula in northern Europe.
The Jutlandish coast is known for its strong winds. 日德兰海岸以强风闻名。
The museum showcased traditional Jutlandish furniture. 博物馆展示了传统的日德兰家具。
近义词:Danish, North Germanic
反义词:(No direct antonym, possibly Southern Danish or non-Jutlandic)
