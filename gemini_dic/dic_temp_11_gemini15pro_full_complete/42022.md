costiveness:/ˈkɑːstɪvnəs/
n.
便秘；迟钝；冷淡
The state of being constipated; difficulty in defecation.
She suffered from costiveness due to her poor diet. 她由于饮食不良而便秘。
近义词：constipation, sluggishness, coldness
反义词：diarrhea, alacrity, warmth
词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *costif*, from Old French *costeif*, from *cost*, "side, rib" (source of modern French *côte*) + *-if*, adjectival suffix. Originally referred to pain in the side, then to difficulty in defecation.
