half-done:/ˌhɑːfˈdʌn/
adjective
半完成的，未完成的
partially completed; not finished
The steak was half-done, just the way I like it. 牛排半熟，正合我意。
The project is half-done, and we need to hurry up.  这个项目只完成了一半，我们需要加快速度。
Near synonyms: partially completed, unfinished, incomplete
Antonyms: finished, completed, done, whole
词性: 形容词
