jessed:/ˈdʒɛsɪd/| v. |给…套上杰西环;用杰西环束缚| To restrain or control (someone) with a jess, a short strap fastened around the legs of a hawk or falcon. |The falconer jessed the hawk before the hunt.  猎鹰人狩猎前给鹰套上了杰西环。| 近义词: restrain, tether, hobble | 反义词: free, release |词性:动词

jessed:/ˈdʒɛsɪd/| adj. |(指鹰)被套上杰西环的| Restrained or controlled by a jess. | The jessed falcon waited patiently on its perch. 被套住的猎鹰耐心地站在栖木上。|近义词: restrained, tethered, hobbled| 反义词: free, unrestrained |词性:形容词
