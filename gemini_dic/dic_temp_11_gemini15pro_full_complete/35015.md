clonic:/ˈklɒnɪk/ (UK), /ˈklɑːnɪk/ (US)| adj.|阵挛性的，痉挛的|Relating to or characterized by clonus, which is a series of involuntary muscular contractions and relaxations.|The patient experienced clonic seizures characterized by rapid, rhythmic jerking movements. 病人经历了阵挛性癫痫发作，其特征是快速、有节奏的抽搐动作。|Example: The doctor observed clonic spasms in the patient's leg. 医生观察到病人腿部阵挛性痉挛。|Synonyms: spasmodic, convulsive|Antonyms: tonic|adjective
