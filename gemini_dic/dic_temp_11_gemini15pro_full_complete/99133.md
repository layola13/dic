interceptible:/ˌɪntərˈseptəbl/
adjective
可截取的，可拦截的
Capable of being intercepted or stopped.
The missile was interceptible by our defense systems.  这枚导弹可以被我们的防御系统拦截。
The enemy's communication was interceptible, allowing us to gain valuable intelligence. 敌人的通讯可以被截取，这使我们获得了宝贵的情报。

近义词:capturable, stoppable
反义词:unstoppable, unimpedable
词性:adjective
词源:From intercept + -ible.  由intercept(拦截) + -ible(可...的)组成。
