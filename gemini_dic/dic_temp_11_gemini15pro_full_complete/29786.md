catwalk: /ˈkætˌwɔːk/ (US) /ˈkætˌwɔːk/ (UK)| n. |T台，狭窄的通道|A narrow, elevated walkway or platform used by models to display clothing during a fashion show, or a similar platform used in industrial settings.|The models strutted confidently down the catwalk.  模特们自信地在T台上昂首阔步。|近义词：runway, ramp|反义词: None|词性: noun

catwalk: /ˈkætˌwɔːk/ (US) /ˈkætˌwɔːk/ (UK)| v. |在T台上走秀|To walk on a catwalk, typically as a fashion model.|She catwalks with such grace and poise. 她走T台的姿势如此优雅。|近义词：model, parade|反义词: None|词性: verb

词源:  catwalk 最初指的是船上或其他高架结构上的狭窄走道，用于让猫通过。后来，这个词被用来指代时装秀上模特走秀的狭窄高台。
