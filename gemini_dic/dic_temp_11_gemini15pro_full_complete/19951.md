biodiversities:/ˌbaɪoʊdaɪˈvɜːrsətiz/| n. |生物多样性|The variety of life in the world or in a particular habitat or ecosystem.|例句：The Amazon rainforest is known for its incredible biodiversity. 亚马逊雨林以其令人难以置信的生物多样性而闻名。

例句：Protecting biodiversity is crucial for the health of our planet. 保护生物多样性对我们星球的健康至关重要。|近义词：variety, richness, diverseness|反义词:uniformity, homogeneity|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From *biodiversity*, coined in 1985 by Walter G. Rosen from *biological diversity*.  *Bio-* from Greek *bios* "life". *Diversity* from Old French *diversite* and Latin *diversitas* "difference, variety".
