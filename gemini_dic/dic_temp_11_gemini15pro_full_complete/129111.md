nivose:/niːˈvoʊz/| n.|雪月 (法国共和历第四个月)|The fourth month of the French Republican Calendar, roughly equivalent to December/January.  It gets its name from the Latin word "nivosus," meaning "snowy."|During nivose, the mountains were covered in snow.  雪月期间，山上覆盖着积雪。|近义词:Pluviôse (the fifth month, known for rain), Frimaire (the third month, known for frost)|反义词:Messidor (the tenth month, part of summer)|词性:noun
