corbelled:/ˈkɔːr.bəld/ | adj. | 锥形的，逐层挑出的 | Built or arranged in a corbelled manner; projecting courses of stone or brick, each projecting beyond the one below. | The tower featured a corbelled parapet.  这座塔有一个逐层挑出的护墙。 | The ancient castle had corbelled turrets. 这座古老的城堡有锥形的塔楼。 | 近义词: projected, cantilevered | 反义词: recessed, indented | 词性: 形容词

corbel:/ˈkɔːr.bəl/ | n. | 悬臂石，托臂 | A piece of stone, wood, or brick projecting from a wall and supporting a structure above it. |  The roof was supported by a series of corbels. 屋顶由一系列托臂支撑。| The statue rested on a decorative corbel. 雕像放置在一个装饰性的托臂上。| 近义词: bracket, support | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: 名词

词源: From Old French *corbel, diminutive of corb "raven," perhaps from the resemblance of the projecting stone to a raven's beak. 源自古法语 *corbel，是 corb “乌鸦”的缩小词，可能因为突出的石头与乌鸦的喙相似。
