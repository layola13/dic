geologists:/dʒiˈɒlədʒɪsts/
noun
地质学家
Scientists who study the Earth, its structure, and its history.
The geologists examined the rock formations for signs of ancient life.  地质学家检查了岩层中古代生命的迹象。

geologist:/dʒiˈɒlədʒɪst/
noun
地质学家
An expert in geology; a person who studies the solid and liquid matter that constitutes the Earth.
The geologist explained how the mountains were formed.  地质学家解释了山脉是如何形成的。

ETYMOLOGY->
From geology + -ist.  Geology comes from the Greek geo- meaning "earth," and -logia meaning "study of."

SYNONYMS->
earth scientist, geoscientist

ANTONYMS->
N/A (No direct antonym for a profession)
