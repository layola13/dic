bonhommie:/ˌbɑːnəˈmiː/ /ˌbɒnəˈmiː/
n.
geniality; friendliness; affability
good-natured easy friendliness; geniality.
He exuded an air of bonhomie. 他散发出一种和蔼可亲的气氛。
近义词：amiability, cordiality, geniality, affability
反义词：hostility, animosity, coldness, aloofness
词性：名词

Etymology:
From French bonhomie, from bonhomme (“good fellow”), from bon (“good”) + homme (“man”).
