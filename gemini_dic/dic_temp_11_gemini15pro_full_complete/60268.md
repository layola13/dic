egalitarian: /ɪˌɡælɪˈtɛəriən/ | adj. n. | 平等主义的；平等主义者 | Believing in the principle that all people are equal and deserve equal rights and opportunities. |  Example:  An egalitarian society strives for equal opportunities for all its citizens. 一个平等主义的社会努力为所有公民争取平等的机会。

Example:  The company adopted an egalitarian policy regarding pay and promotion. 公司在薪酬和晋升方面采取了平等主义政策。 | 近义词: democratic, equitable, fair | 反义词: elitist, hierarchical, unequal | 词性: 形容词, 名词


egalitarian: /ɪˌɡælɪˈtɛəriən/ | n. | 平等主义者 | A person who believes in the principle that all people are equal and deserve equal rights and opportunities. | Example:  He was a strong egalitarian and fought against social injustice. 他是一位坚定的平等主义者，并与社会不公平作斗争。


Example:  Egalitarians believe that everyone should have access to quality education. 平等主义者认为每个人都应该有机会接受优质教育。 | 近义词: democrat, leveler | 反义词: elitist, snob | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *égalitaire*, from égal (“equal”), from Latin aequālis, from aequus (“level, even, equal”).
