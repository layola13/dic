marlitic: /mɑːrˈlɪtɪk/
adjective
泥灰岩的；似泥灰岩的
Resembling marl; containing marl.  Formed from, or containing, marl. Consisting of, or resembling, marl.
例句：The marlitic soil is rich in nutrients. 泥灰岩土壤营养丰富。

例句：The geologist examined the marlitic formations in the cliff face. 地质学家检查了悬崖面上的泥灰岩构造。

近义词：marlaceous, chalky, calcareous
反义词：(No direct antonym, could consider terms like "non-calcareous" or describing different rock types like "granitic" or "basaltic")
词性：adjective
