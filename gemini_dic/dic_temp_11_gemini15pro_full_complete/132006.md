noncorpuscular:/ˌnɒnkɔːˈpʌskjʊlə(r)/ /ˌnɑːnkɔːrˈpʌskjələr/ | adj. | 非微粒的，非粒子性的 | Not consisting of particles or corpuscles; not having a granular or particulate structure.  | Light was once thought of as noncorpuscular radiation. 光曾经被认为是非粒子辐射。|The theory posited a noncorpuscular nature of light.  该理论假设光具有非粒子性质。| 近义词: wavelike, non-particulate | 反义词: corpuscular, particulate | 词性:形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin *non.
corpuscular (adjective): From Latin corpusculum "little body," diminutive of corpus "body."
