determinably:/dɪˈtɜːmɪnəbli/
adjective
可确定地，可以断定地
Capable of being determined, ascertained, or decided.
The date of the meeting is determinably set for next Tuesday. 会议日期确定为下周二。
The cause of the fire was determinably arson. 火灾的原因确定是人为纵火。
近义词: ascertainably, definably, decidedly
反义词: indeterminably, vaguely
词性: adjective

词源:  From determine (v.) + -able (suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being") + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Determine" comes from Old French "determiner" and ultimately from Latin "determinare" meaning "to limit, to fix, to decide."
