hyperrationalities:/ˌhaɪpərˌræʃəˈnælətiz/ | n. | 超理性，过度理性 |  A state of extreme or excessive rationality, often to the point of neglecting emotional or social factors.  It implies a focus on logic and optimization that can be detrimental in real-world situations. |  The company's hyperrationalities led them to automate everything, even customer service, resulting in a loss of personal touch and ultimately, customers.  这家公司的过度理性导致他们将一切自动化，甚至包括客户服务，导致失去了人情味，最终失去了客户。|  The algorithm's hyperrationalities failed to account for unpredictable human behavior.  该算法的超理性未能考虑到不可预测的人类行为。 |  over-rationality, excessive rationality, ultra-rationality | irrationality, emotionality | noun
