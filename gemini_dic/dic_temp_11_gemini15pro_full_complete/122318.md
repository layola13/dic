"mity" itself is not a standalone word in standard English.  It's likely a suffix or part of a larger word.  The closest complete word is "mighty," which is analyzed below.

mighty:/ˈmaɪti/ | adj. |强大的，强有力的；伟大的，非凡的；非常的，极度的| Possessing great and impressive power or strength, exceptionally strong, large, or effective. |例句：The mighty oak stood tall.  这棵强壮的橡树高耸入云。|例句：He swung the axe with mighty force. 他用强大的力量挥动斧头。| 近义词：powerful, strong, potent, enormous | 反义词：weak, feeble, powerless | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *myghty*, from Old English *mihtig*, from *miht* (“might”).  Related to may.
