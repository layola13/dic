enslaves:/ɪnˈsleɪvz/| vt.|奴役，使成为奴隶|To cause (someone) to lose their freedom of choice or action.|The company enslaved its workers with low wages and long hours.  这家公司用低工资和长时间工作奴役工人。|Example:  The regime enslaved its people through fear and propaganda. 该政权通过恐惧和宣传奴役其人民。|Synonyms: subjugate, oppress, subdue, dominate, control|Antonyms: liberate, free, emancipate, release|verb

enslaves:/ɪnˈsleɪvz/| vt.|沉迷，迷恋|To be completely controlled by something, typically a bad habit or an obsession.|He was enslaved by his addiction to gambling. 他沉迷于赌博。|Example: She was enslaved by her desire for power. 她被对权力的渴望所奴役。|Synonyms: captivated, obsessed, addicted, consumed, gripped|Antonyms: indifferent, apathetic, uninterested|verb
