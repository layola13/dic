hyalography:/ˌhaɪəˈlɒɡrəfi/
n.
透明质酸造影术;玻璃体摄影术
A radiographic technique for visualizing the vitreous body of the eye by injecting a radiopaque substance into it.
Hyalography can help identify abnormalities in the vitreous humour. 透明质酸造影术可以帮助识别玻璃体液中的异常。
Fluorescein angiography and hyalography were performed. 进行了荧光素血管造影和透明质酸造影。
近义词:vitreography
反义词:None
词性:noun

Etymology:
From hyalo- (glassy, transparent) + -graphy (writing, recording).  "Hyalo-" ultimately comes from the Greek word "hyalos" (glass).
