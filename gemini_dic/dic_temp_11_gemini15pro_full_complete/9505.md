antiscepticisms:/ˌæntiˈsɛptɪsɪzəmz/
|n.| 反怀疑主义，反怀疑论 |The rise of antiscepticisms reflects a growing trust in established institutions. 反怀疑主义的兴起反映了人们对现有机构的信任日益增强。|  Antiscepticisms can sometimes lead to a reluctance to question authority. 反怀疑主义有时会导致不愿质疑权威。 |近义词: dogmatism, credulity |反义词: scepticism, skepticism |词性: noun

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek anti-)
scepticism (noun): a doubting or questioning attitude or state of mind; doubt concerning basic religious principles (from Greek skeptikos "inquiring, reflective," from skeptesthai "to look, consider")
-ism (suffix): forming nouns denoting action or practice, state or condition, principles, doctrines, a usage or characteristic
