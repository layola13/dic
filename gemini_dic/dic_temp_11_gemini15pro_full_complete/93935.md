immunizing:/ˈɪmjʊˌnaɪzɪŋ/ /ˈɪmjəˌnaɪzɪŋ/
Verb
免疫; 使免疫
Making someone immune to a disease, usually by vaccination.
例句：The doctor is immunizing children against measles. 医生正在给孩子们接种麻疹疫苗。

例句：They are immunizing the population against polio. 他们正在为民众接种脊髓灰质炎疫苗。

近义词：vaccinating, inoculating
反义词：exposing, sensitizing
词性：Verb
