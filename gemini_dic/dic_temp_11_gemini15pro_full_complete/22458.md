bookland:/ˈbʊklænd/
n.
书乡；书籍的世界；图书领域
A metaphorical realm or world associated with books and literature.
Example:  He was lost in bookland, oblivious to the world around him. 他沉浸在书的海洋里，对外界的一切浑然不觉。

Example: She found solace and escape in bookland. 她在书的世界里找到了慰藉和逃避。
Synonym: literary world, world of books
Antonym: reality, real world
noun
