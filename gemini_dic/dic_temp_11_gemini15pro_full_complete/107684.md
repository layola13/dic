lankest:/ˈlæŋkɪst/| adj.|最瘦的，最憔悴的|superlative adjective of lank; most lank.  Extremely thin and bony; gaunt.|He looked pale and lankest after his illness. 他病后脸色苍白，骨瘦如柴。|Synonyms: gaunt, haggard, emaciated, skeletal, bony|Antonyms: plump, robust, stout, fleshy|adjective
