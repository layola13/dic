anthology:/ænˈθɑːlədʒi/
n.|选集，文选|A collection of selected literary pieces or passages or works of art or music.|例句：The poetry anthology includes works from the past five centuries. 这本诗歌选集收录了过去五个世纪的作品。|例句：She is compiling an anthology of short stories for children. 她正在为孩子们编写一本短篇小说选集。|近义词：collection, compilation, compendium|反义词: None|词性: Noun
Etymology: From Late Latin anthologia "collection of flowers, garland of verses," from Greek anthologia, from anthologos "gathering flowers, selecting choice passages," from anthos "flower" + legein "to gather, pick, choose."
