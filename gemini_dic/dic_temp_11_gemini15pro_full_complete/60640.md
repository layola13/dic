electary:/ɪˈlɛktəri/
noun
药剂，糖膏；(尤指)含有药物的蜜膏
A medicinal preparation, especially one consisting of a powdered drug mixed with honey or syrup.
The apothecary prepared a soothing electary for the king's cough. 药剂师为国王的咳嗽准备了一种舒缓的药膏。
近义词:confection, remedy, medicine
反义词:poison, toxin
词性:n.

词源：
From Middle English *electuarie, from Old French electuaire, from Late Latin electuarium (“electuary”), from Latin electus (“selected, choice”), from past participle of ēligere (“to choose out, select”), from ē- (“out”) + legere (“to choose, gather, read”).
