adonic:/əˈdɒnɪk/ | adj. n.| 阿多尼斯诗行的，阿多尼斯的 | Relating to or consisting of a verse of poetry having four metrical feet, a dactyl followed by a spondee or trochee; an adonic verse.  | The poem ended with an adonic line. 这首诗以一个阿多尼斯诗行结尾。 |  The poet used an adonic verse at the close of each stanza. 诗人每节诗的结尾都用了一个阿多尼斯诗行。| synonyms: dactylic, rhythmic, metrical | antonyms: prosaic, unrhythmical | adjective, noun
