homonymously:/ˌhɒməˈnɪməsli/ /ˌhoʊməˈnɪməsli/| adv.|同名地，同音异义地|In a homonymous manner; in the same way that homonyms are related.  Referring to words that are spelled and pronounced the same but have different meanings.|The words "bow" (to bend) and "bow" (a type of knot) are used homonymously in this sentence, which creates confusion.|近义词： synonymously, identically|反义词: differently, distinctly|词性:副词
