logomachist: /ˌloʊɡəˈmækɪst/ | n. | 好争论的人；文字游戏爱好者 | A person who engages in logomachy, or disputes about words.  A lover of word games. |  The two professors, both confirmed logomachists, argued for hours about the meaning of a single comma. 这两位教授都是出了名的好辩者，为了一个逗号的含义争论了几个小时。

The logomachist delighted in finding obscure words to use in Scrabble.  这位文字游戏爱好者喜欢在拼字游戏中使用生僻的词。 | 近义词: arguer, disputant, quibbler, pedant | 反义词:  peacemaker, mediator, conciliator | 词性: noun

词源: From Greek *logomachia* "battle of words," from *logos* "word, speech, reason" + *mache* "fight, battle."
