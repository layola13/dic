overhasty:/oʊvərˈheɪsti/| adj.|过于匆忙的；仓促的；草率的|Acting or done too quickly; rash.|例句：He made an overhasty decision and regretted it later. 他做了一个草率的决定，后来后悔了。

例句：It's overhasty to draw conclusions without all the facts. 没有掌握所有事实就下结论太草率了。|近义词：rash, precipitate, impetuous, impulsive|反义词：cautious, deliberate, prudent|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From over- + hasty.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," "too much."  "Hasty" comes from the Old English "hæstig," meaning "violent," "vehement," later evolving to mean "quick," "speedy," and eventually "acting with excessive speed and without due consideration."
