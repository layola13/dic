hypersomnolent:/ˌhaɪpərˈsɑːmnələnt/ | /ˌhaɪpərˈsɒmnələnt/ | adj. | 过度嗜睡的 | Excessively sleepy; characterized by recurrent episodes of excessive daytime sleepiness or prolonged nighttime sleep. |  The patient reported feeling hypersomnolent, even after a full night's sleep.  病人报告说即使睡了一整夜也感到过度嗜睡。|  He was so hypersomnolent that he fell asleep during the meeting. 他太困了，以至于在会议期间睡着了。 | 近义词: sleepy, drowsy, lethargic | 反义词: alert, wakeful, vigilant | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- (meaning "excessive, above normal") + somnolent (meaning "sleepy, drowsy").  The word "somnolent" comes from the Latin "somnolentus," derived from "somnus," meaning "sleep."
