hennotannic:/ˌhɛnoʊˈtænɪk/ | adj. | 鞣花酸的；含有鞣花酸的 | Relating to or containing ellagitannins, a type of hydrolyzable tannin that yields ellagic acid upon hydrolysis. |  Ellagitannins, like those found in pomegranates, are hennotannic compounds known for their antioxidant properties. 石榴中发现的鞣花单宁是鞣花酸类化合物，以其抗氧化特性而闻名。| Synonyms: ellagitannin-containing | Antonyms: N/A | adjective
