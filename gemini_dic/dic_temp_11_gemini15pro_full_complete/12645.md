assay:/ˈæseɪ/ (US), /əˈseɪ/ (UK)| n. vt.|化验，分析；试验，测定|A laboratory test to determine the content or quality of something, especially of a metal or ore.  |例句：The blood samples were sent to the lab for assay. 血液样本被送往实验室进行化验。|近义词：test, analysis, examination, evaluation|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps synthesis or fabrication)|noun, verb

assay:/ˈæseɪ/ (US), /əˈseɪ/ (UK)| n. vt.|尝试，努力|An attempt to do something. |例句：His first assay at writing a novel was a dismal failure.  他第一次尝试写小说惨遭失败。|近义词：attempt, try, endeavor, effort|反义词: abstention, avoidance|noun, verb
