crash-in:/ˈkræʃ ɪn/ | phrasal verb | 突然闯入；不请自来；未经邀请就留下 | To arrive or stay somewhere uninvited, often unexpectedly and for an extended period.  |  例句：My brother crashed in on me last night after his flight was cancelled.  我弟弟昨晚航班取消后突然跑到我家来了。

例句：Can I crash in at your place for a few days? 我可以在你家待几天吗？| 近义词：barge in, gatecrash, intrude, impose | 反义词：leave, depart | 词性：动词短语


crash-in:/ˈkræʃ ɪn/ | n. |  临时住宿；非正式的聚会 | A place to stay temporarily, often informally; an informal gathering. | 例句：We had a crash-in at my apartment after the concert.  音乐会结束后，我们在我的公寓里临时凑合了一晚。

例句：The student's dorm became a crash-in for stranded travelers. 学生宿舍成了滞留旅客的临时住所。 | 近义词：sleepover, hangout | 反义词：hotel, formal accommodation | 词性：名词
