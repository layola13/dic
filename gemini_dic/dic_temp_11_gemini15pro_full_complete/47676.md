debtor:/ˈdɛtər/ (US), /ˈdɛtə/ (UK)| n. |债人；负债者|A person or organization that owes money to another.|例句：The company is a major debtor to several banks. 这家公司是几家银行的主要债务人。

例句：The debtor was unable to repay the loan.  这个债务人无法偿还贷款。


近义词：borrower, owing party|反义词:creditor, lender|词性:noun
