asperifolious:/ˌæspərɪˈfəʊliəs/
adj.
粗糙叶的；具有粗糙叶子的
Having rough leaves, like those of some plants in the borage family (Boraginaceae).
The plant is easily identifiable by its asperifolious texture. 这种植物很容易通过其粗糙的叶子来识别。
The borage plant has characteristic asperifolious leaves. 紫草科植物具有典型的粗糙叶子。
近义词: scabrous, hispid
反义词: smooth, glabrous
词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin *asperifolius, from asper (“rough”) + folium (“leaf”).
