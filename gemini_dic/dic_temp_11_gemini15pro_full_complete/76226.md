futtock:/ˈfʌtək/
n.
肋骨状木板，复材肋骨，船肋骨
A curved timber forming part of a ship's frame, extending from the keel to the deck.
The shipwrights carefully fitted each futtock into place.  造船工人小心翼翼地将每根肋骨状木板安装到位。
The storm damaged several of the ship's futtocks.  暴风雨损坏了船上的几根肋骨状木板。
synonyms: frame timber, rib, ship timber
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, perhaps "keel" as it's a contrasting part of the ship)
