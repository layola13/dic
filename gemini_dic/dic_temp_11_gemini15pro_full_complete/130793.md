nonanonymities:/ˌnɒnˌænəˈnɪmɪtiz/
n.
非匿名性；公开身份；非匿名状态
The state or quality of not being anonymous; the fact of being identifiable.
The website encouraged nonanonymities to foster a sense of community.  该网站鼓励公开身份以培养社区意识。

The rise of social media has led to a decline in nonanonymities online. 社交媒体的兴起导致了网络上匿名性的减少。
synonym:identifiability, openness, transparency
antonym:anonymity, secrecy
