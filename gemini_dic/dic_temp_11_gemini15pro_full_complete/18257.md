believers:/bɪˈliːvərz/| n. |信徒，信仰者|People who accept something as true, especially a religious doctrine.|例句：The believers gathered for prayer. 信徒们聚集在一起祈祷。

例句：She is one of the true believers in the power of positive thinking. 她是积极思考力量的真正信徒之一。|近义词：followers, adherents, disciples|反义词：disbelievers, skeptics, doubters|noun
