houndstooths:/ˈhaʊndzˌtuːθs/ |n. |千鸟格，犬牙纹|A duotone textile pattern characterized by broken checks or abstract four-pointed shapes, often in black and white. Resembles the jagged teeth of a hound. |The jacket was a classic houndstooths pattern. 这件夹克是经典的千鸟格图案。|Synonyms: houndstooth check, dogtooth check, pied-de-poule|Antonyms: solid color, plain|Noun

houndstooth:/ˈhaʊndzˌtuːθ/ |n. adj. |千鸟格，犬牙纹|A duotone textile pattern characterized by broken checks or abstract four-pointed shapes, often in black and white. Can be used as a noun to refer to the pattern itself, or as an adjective to describe something with this pattern. Resembles the jagged teeth of a hound. |She wore a houndstooth jacket. 她穿了一件千鸟格夹克。|Synonyms: houndstooth check, dogtooth check, pied-de-poule|Antonyms: solid color, plain|Noun, Adjective
