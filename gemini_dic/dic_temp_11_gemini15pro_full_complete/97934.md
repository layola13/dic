innovate:/ˈɪnəveɪt/| v.|创新，革新|To introduce something new; make changes in anything established.|例句：The company needs to innovate to stay competitive. 公司需要创新才能保持竞争力。|近义词：create, invent, pioneer, introduce, modernize|反义词： stagnate, imitate, copy|词性:动词
Etymology: From Latin *innovare*, from *in-* ("in, into") + *novus* ("new").
