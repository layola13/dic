disclosers:/dɪˈskloʊzərz/
n.
揭露者；公开者；披露者
People who reveal something, typically something previously secret or unknown.
The whistleblowers were among the key disclosers of the corruption scandal.  这些告密者是腐败丑闻的关键揭露者之一。
The disclosers of the information will be protected under the new law.  根据新法律，信息披露者将受到保护。
synonyms: informants, whistleblowers, revealers, expositors
antonyms: concealers, suppressors
noun


disclose:/dɪˈskloʊz/
v.
公开；揭露；透露
To make something known publicly, or to reveal something previously secret.
The company disclosed its financial results to the public. 该公司向公众披露了其财务业绩。
He refused to disclose the source of his information. 他拒绝透露信息来源。
synonyms: reveal, divulge, unveil, expose
antonyms: conceal, hide, suppress
verb
