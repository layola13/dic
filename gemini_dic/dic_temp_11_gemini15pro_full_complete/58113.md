dropped:/drɑːpt/| v. |掉落，下降，放弃，停止，让…下车|She dropped her keys on the floor. 她把钥匙掉在地板上了。| The temperature dropped suddenly. 气温骤降。|  synonyms: fallen, lowered, abandoned, ceased,  | antonyms: risen, increased, continued, picked up | verb

Etymology->
From Middle English *droppen*, from Old Norse *droppa* (“to drop”), from Proto-Germanic *druppan*, from Proto-Indo-European *dhrebh- (“to fall, drip”).
