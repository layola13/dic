intermigrating:/ˌɪntərˈmaɪɡreɪtɪŋ/
| adj. |互相迁移的，互相移居的 |Moving from one place to another and mixing with another group, often referring to populations or species.|  The intermigrating populations eventually blended their cultures. 互相迁移的人群最终融合了他们的文化。|近义词：migrating, intermingling, integrating|反义词：segregating, isolating|词性：形容词

intermigrating:/ˌɪntərˈmaɪɡreɪtɪŋ/
| v. |互相迁移，互相移居 |The act of moving from one place to another and mixing with another group, often referring to populations or species.|  The two tribes were intermigrating across the valley. 这两个部落正在山谷间互相迁徙。|近义词：migrating, intermingling, integrating|反义词：segregating, isolating|词性：动词
