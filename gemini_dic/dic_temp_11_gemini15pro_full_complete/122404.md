mnemonic's:/nɪˈmɒnɪks/ | n. | 助记符；记忆术；记忆法 |  A system or technique such as a pattern of letters, ideas, or associations which assists in remembering something. |  He uses mnemonics to help him remember people's names. 他使用助记符来帮助他记住人们的名字。| synonyms: memory aid, memory device, reminder | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "obstacle to memory") | noun

mnemonic:/nɪˈmɒnɪk/ | adj. | 助记的；记忆的 |  Aiding or designed to aid the memory. |  A mnemonic device. 一个助记装置。| synonyms:  memory-aiding, memory-improving | antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "memory-hindering") | adjective
