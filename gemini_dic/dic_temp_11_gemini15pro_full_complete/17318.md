一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I am happy. 我很开心。
He is a doctor. 他是一位医生。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
She is singing. 她正在唱歌。
They are playing football. 他们在踢足球。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have been to Paris. 我去过巴黎。
She has finished her homework. 她已经完成了作业。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
We have been waiting for an hour. 我们已经等了一个小时了。
He has been working on this project for months. 他已经在这个项目上工作好几个月了。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I was tired yesterday. 我昨天很累。
They were at the party last night. 他们昨晚参加了派对。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was watching TV when she called. 她打电话时我正在看电视。
He was playing the piano when the phone rang. 电话响的时候他正在弹钢琴。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had already eaten when they arrived. 他们到达时我已经吃过了。
She had finished her work before I got there. 在我到达之前她已经完成了工作。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been studying for hours before I took the test. 考试前我已经学习了好几个小时。
They had been discussing the issue for weeks before they reached a decision. 他们在达成决定之前已经讨论了几个星期的问题。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will be there tomorrow. 我明天会在那里。
She will be happy to see you. 她会很高兴见到你。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be watching the game at 8 pm tomorrow. 明天晚上8点我会在看比赛。
They will be traveling to Europe next summer. 明年夏天他们将去欧洲旅行。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I will have finished the report by Friday. 我会在星期五之前完成报告。
She will have graduated by next year. 她明年就会毕业。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next year, I will have been living here for five years. 到明年，我将已经在这里住了五年。
By the time she retires, she will have been working at the company for 30 years. 到她退休的时候，她将已经在这家公司工作了30年。
