jets:/dʒɛts/ | v. | 喷射; 快速移动 | to travel or move very fast. |  Water jetted from the broken pipe. 水从破裂的管道中喷射出来。 | synonyms: spurt, gush, zoom, dash | antonyms: trickle, seep, crawl | verb
