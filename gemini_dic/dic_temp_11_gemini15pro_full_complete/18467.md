bemusedly:/bɪˈmjuːzɪdli/
adv.
茫然地，困惑地
In a manner showing that you are confused and slightly amused.
He looked at her bemusedly, trying to figure out what she meant. 他茫然地看着她，试图弄明白她的意思。
She smiled bemusedly at his sudden outburst.  她被他突然的爆发弄得哭笑不得。
Near Synonyms: bewilderedly, perplexedly, quizzically, thoughtfully
Antonyms:  clearly, certainly, decisively
词性: 副词
