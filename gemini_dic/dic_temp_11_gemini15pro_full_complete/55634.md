ditchless:/ˈdɪtʃləs/
adjective
无沟的；没有沟渠的
Without ditches.  Lacking ditches or trenches.
The farmer experimented with a ditchless drainage system.  这位农民试验了一种无沟排水系统。
ditchless irrigation allows for more even water distribution. 无沟灌溉可以使水分分布更均匀。

近义词:  unditched, trenchless
反义词: ditched, trenched
词性: adjective

词源:  "ditch" (沟渠) + "-less" (无，没有)
