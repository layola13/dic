glossingly:/ˈɡlɒsɪŋli/ | adv. | 光滑地，浮光掠影地，敷衍地 |  In a way that glosses over something; superficially.  | He glossingly described the complex situation, leaving out many important details. 他敷衍地描述了这个复杂的情况，省略了许多重要的细节。 | She glossingly spoke of her difficult childhood, never revealing the true extent of her suffering. 她浮光掠影地谈及她艰难的童年，从未透露她所受苦难的真实程度。 | 近义词: superficially, cursorily, perfunctorily | 反义词: thoroughly, meticulously, comprehensively | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From the verb "gloss" (to give a superficially attractive appearance to; explain away), from Old French "glose" (gloss, explanation), from Late Latin "glossa" (word needing explanation, foreign word), from Greek "glōssa" (tongue, language, word needing explanation).
