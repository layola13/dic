hygroplasm:/ˈhaɪɡroʊˌplæzəm/| n. |细胞质，原生质|The cytoplasm of a plant cell is also called hygroplasm. 植物细胞的细胞质也被称为原生质。|Hygroplasm is the liquid portion of the protoplasm surrounding the cell nucleus, where many metabolic processes take place.  原生质是细胞核周围原生质的液体部分，许多代谢过程都在那里进行。|近义词：cytoplasm, protoplasm|反义词: 无|词性: 名词

词源:  From Greek *hygros* (moist) + *plasma* (something molded). 来自希腊语 *hygros*（潮湿的）+ *plasma*（成形的东西）。
