apartment: /əˈpɑːrtmənt/ (US) /əˈpɑːtmənt/ (UK)| n. |公寓；住宅|A set of rooms for living in, usually on one floor of a building.|She lives in a small apartment in the city. 她住在城市里的一间小公寓。|flat, condo, unit|house, mansion|noun

apartment: /əˈpɑːrtmənt/ (US) /əˈpɑːtmənt/ (UK)| n. |一套房间|A suite of rooms, especially in a hotel.|They booked a luxury apartment at the resort. 他们在度假村预订了一套豪华公寓。|suite, rooms|house, mansion|noun

Etymology: From French *appartement*, from Italian *appartamento*, ultimately from *appartare* "to separate," from Latin *ad* "to" + *pars* "part."  Originally referred to a suite of rooms, later evolving to mean a self-contained housing unit.
