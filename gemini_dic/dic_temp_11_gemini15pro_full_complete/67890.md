fabulously:/ˈfæbjələsli/ | adv. | 极好地，绝妙地，难以置信地 | In a fabulous manner; exceptionally well; wonderfully. | She looked fabulously chic in her new dress. 她穿着新裙子，看起来非常时髦。|  The food was fabulously delicious.  食物美味极了。 | 近义词: wonderfully, marvelously, superbly, amazingly, incredibly | 反义词: terribly, awfully, poorly, badly | 词性: 副词

词源:
From *fabulous* + *-ly*.  *Fabulous* comes from the Latin *fabulosus*, meaning "celebrated in fable."
