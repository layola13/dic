omnigenous:/ɒmˈnɪdʒənəs/
adjective
所有種類的；由所有種類組成的
composed of all kinds; comprising all varieties
The library boasts an omnigenous collection of books, covering every genre imaginable.  这个图书馆拥有包罗万象的藏书，涵盖了所有你能想到的类型。
The artist's omnigenous style incorporates elements from various cultures and periods. 这位艺术家的风格融合了不同文化和时期的元素。
近义词: all-encompassing, comprehensive, universal, eclectic
反义词: limited, restricted, specific, narrow


词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *omnigenus (“of all kinds”), from omnis (“all”) + genus (“kind, origin”).
