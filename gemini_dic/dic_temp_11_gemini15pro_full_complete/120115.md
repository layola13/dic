mimicker:/ˈmɪmɪkər/| n. |模仿者，效颦者|Someone who mimics or imitates, often to entertain or ridicule.|例句：He's a talented mimicker, able to perfectly copy the voices of celebrities. 他是一个有才华的模仿者，能够完美地模仿名人的声音。

例句：The child was a mimicker, repeating everything his older brother said.  这个孩子是个模仿者，会重复他哥哥说的每一句话。


|近义词：imitator, impersonator, copycat|反义词: innovator, original|词性: noun
