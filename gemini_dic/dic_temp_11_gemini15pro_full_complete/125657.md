mummychog:/ˈmʌmiˌtʃɒɡ/ | n. | 木乃伊小狗; 干瘪的小狗(通常指因脱水或营养不良而瘦弱的小动物) | A mummychog refers to a small, emaciated animal, often a dog, that appears shriveled or mummified due to dehydration or malnutrition.  It's not a formal or scientific term, but rather a descriptive colloquialism. |  The poor puppy looked like a little mummychog, all skin and bones.  这只可怜的小狗看起来像个小木乃伊，皮包骨头。| Near synonyms:  scrawny, emaciated, withered, skeletal, runt  | Antonyms: plump, healthy, robust | Noun
