例句：The band played a lively backrag that got everyone on their feet. 乐队演奏了一首活泼的强节奏音乐，让每个人都站了起来。|近义词：jazz, revelry, merrymaking, jamboree, shindig|反义词：quiet, peace, silence|词性:noun
