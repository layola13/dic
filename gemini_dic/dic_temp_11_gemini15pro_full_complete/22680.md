borderline:/ˈbɔːrdərlaɪn/ /ˈbɔːrdərˌlaɪn/| adj. n.|边缘的，临界的；界限|A borderline condition is one that is between two different conditions, so that it is difficult to say which condition it is.  A borderline personality disorder is a mental illness.  A borderline is the official line separating two countries or areas.|He was a borderline candidate. 他是个边缘候选人。|Synonyms: marginal, liminal, ambiguous, indeterminate|Antonyms: definite, clear, certain|adjective

borderline:/ˈbɔːrdərlaɪn/ /ˈbɔːrdərˌlaɪn/| n.|边界线，界线|The line that divides two countries or areas.|The borderline between the two countries is heavily guarded.  两国之间的边界线戒备森严。|Synonyms: boundary, frontier, border, demarcation line|Antonyms: center, interior, middle|noun
