climatography:/ˌklaɪməˈtɒɡrəfi/ | n. |气候志，气候学|The study of climate, its classification, and its regional distribution.  The description of the climate of a particular region.|Example 1:  The climatography of the Amazon rainforest reveals high humidity and rainfall throughout the year. 亚马逊雨林的气候志显示全年湿度高，降雨量大。

Example 2:  His research focuses on the climatography of coastal regions. 他的研究重点是沿海地区的气候学。|Synonym: climatology|Antonym: N/A|noun


climatological:/ˌklaɪmətəˈlɒdʒɪk(ə)l/ | adj. |气候学的|Relating to climatology or the study of climate.|Example 1: Climatological data shows a steady increase in average global temperatures. 气候学数据显示全球平均气温稳步上升。

Example 2:  They conducted a climatological survey of the region. 他们对该地区进行了气候学调查。|Synonym: climatic|Antonym: N/A|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *climatographie*, from *climat* (climate) + *-graphie* (writing). Ultimately from Greek *klima* (inclination) and *graphein* (to write).
