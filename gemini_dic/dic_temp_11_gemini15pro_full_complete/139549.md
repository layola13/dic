obtrusiveness:/əbˈtruːsɪvnəs/| n. |唐突，冒昧，强加于人|The quality of being obtrusive; tending to force oneself or one's opinions upon others.|Her obtrusiveness made it difficult to enjoy the party. 她的唐突让人难以享受派对。|Synonyms: intrusiveness, officiousness, pushiness|Antonyms: discreetness, reserve, tact|noun

obtrusive:/əbˈtruːsɪv/| adj. |唐突的，冒昧的，强加于人的|Noticeable or prominent in an unwelcome or intrusive way.|The waiter was very obtrusive, constantly interrupting our conversation. 服务员非常唐突，不断打断我们的谈话。|Synonyms: intrusive, officious, pushy|Antonyms: discreet, reserved, tactful|adjective
