inauthentically:/ˌɪnɔːˈθɛntɪkli/
adverb
不真实地，虚假地
in a way that is not genuine or true; in a fake or artificial manner.
She inauthentically portrayed herself as an expert in the field. 她虚伪地把自己描绘成该领域的专家。
The actor inauthentically delivered his lines, lacking any real emotion.  这位演员虚情假意地念着台词，缺乏任何真情实感。
近义词:falsely, artificially, disingenuously, deceptively
反义词:genuinely, authentically, sincerely, truly
词性:adverb

authentic:/ɔːˈθɛntɪk/
adjective
真实的，真正的
genuine; real
Is this an authentic Roman coin?  这是一枚真正的罗马硬币吗？
近义词:genuine, real, true, bona fide
反义词:fake, counterfeit, spurious, inauthentic
词性:adjective

in-: prefix
不，非，无
not, opposite of
近义词:un-, non-
反义词:pro-, pre-
词性:prefix

-ly: suffix
…地
forms adverbs from adjectives
近义词:n/a
反义词:n/a
词性:suffix

词源:  The word "inauthentically" is formed by adding the prefix "in-" (meaning "not") to the adjective "authentic", and then adding the adverbial suffix "-ly". "Authentic" comes from the Greek word "authentikos" meaning "original, genuine, first-hand".
