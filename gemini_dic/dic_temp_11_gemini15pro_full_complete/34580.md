clearings:/ˈklɪərɪŋz/| n. | 空地；林中空地；结算；清算；(支票等的)兑付|An open space in a forest where there are no trees.  The process of exchanging checks and other payment instructions between banks.|  We found a sunny clearing in the woods. 我们在树林里发现了一块阳光明媚的空地。|Synonyms: glade, opening, space, settlement, payment | Antonyms: forest, thicket, obstruction, debt| noun
