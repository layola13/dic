blesses:/ˈblɛsɪz/
verb
祝福，保佑
Third-person singular present tense form of "bless".  It means to invoke divine favor upon someone or something, or to endow with something desirable.
God blesses those who are kind. 上帝保佑善良的人。
The priest blesses the bread and wine. 牧师祝福面包和酒。
synonyms: sanctifies, consecrates, anoints, dedicates
antonyms: curses, damns, condemns
词性: 动词
