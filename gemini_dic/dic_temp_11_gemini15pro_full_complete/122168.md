mists:/mɪsts/ | n. | 薄雾，迷雾 | A cloud of tiny water droplets suspended in the atmosphere at or near the earth's surface, obscuring or restricting visibility. |  The early morning mists were thick and heavy. 早晨的雾又浓又重。| synonyms: fog, haze, smog | antonyms: clarity, clearness | noun

mists:/mɪsts/ | v. |  (使)蒙上薄雾；(使)模糊 | To cover or become covered with mist. | Condensation misted the windows.  冷凝水使窗户蒙上了一层雾。 | synonyms: cloud, obscure, blur | antonyms: clear, clarify | verb
