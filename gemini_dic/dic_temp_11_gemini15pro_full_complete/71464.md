flameproofers:/ˈfleɪmpruːfərz/
noun
阻燃剂，防火剂
People who or things that apply flameproofing materials. Materials treated with a flameproofing substance.
The flameproofers were called in to treat the curtains in the theatre. 剧院请来了阻燃剂处理人员来处理窗帘。
New regulations require all upholstered furniture to be treated by certified flameproofers. 新规定要求所有软垫家具都要由经过认证的阻燃剂处理人员进行处理。
近义词:fire retardants, fireproofing agents
反义词:flammable materials, accelerants
