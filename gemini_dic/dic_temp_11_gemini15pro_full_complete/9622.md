antistatism:/ˌæntiˈsteɪtɪzəm/| n. |反国家主义；反国家干预主义|Opposition to state intervention in economic and social affairs, often advocating for individual liberty and limited government.|  Many libertarians embrace antistatism, believing that government interference hinders individual prosperity. 许多自由主义者拥护反国家主义，认为政府干预阻碍了个人繁荣。|Example:  Her antistatism led her to advocate for the privatization of many government services. 她的反国家主义立场使她主张将许多政府服务私有化。|Synonyms: libertarianism, anarchism, individualism|Antonyms: statism, collectivism, socialism|noun
