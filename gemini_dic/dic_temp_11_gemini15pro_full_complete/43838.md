crepitant:/ˈkrɛpɪtənt/| adj.|噼啪作响的；捻发音的；爆裂声的|A crackling or rattling sound, like that made by rubbing stiff paper or by air moving in fluid-filled lungs.|The doctor noted the crepitant sounds in the patient's lungs. 医生注意到病人肺部有噼啪声。|Synonyms: crackling, rattling, rales|Antonyms: silent, smooth|adjective

crepitation:/ˌkrɛpɪˈteɪʃən/| n.|噼啪声；捻发音；爆裂声|The act or sound of crepitating.|The crepitation in his knee was a sign of arthritis. 他膝盖的噼啪声是关节炎的征兆。|Synonyms: crackling, rattling, creaking|Antonyms: silence, smoothness|noun
