dag:/dæɡ/ | n. | 羊毛上的污垢，凝结物；悬挂物；下垂部分 | a piece of matted wool, dung, etc. hanging from a sheep's fleece; something hanging down | The sheep's fleece was covered in dags. 这只羊的羊毛上沾满了污垢。 | 近义词：clump, mat, tangle | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially "clean fleece") | 词性: noun

dag:/dæɡ/ | n. | (澳大利亚俚语) 有个性的人，怪人 | (Australian slang) an eccentric person, a character | He's a bit of a dag, but he's a good bloke. 他有点怪，但他是个好人。 | 近义词：character, eccentric, oddball | 反义词:  conformist | 词性: noun
