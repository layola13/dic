overhugely:/ˌoʊvərˈhjuːdʒli/| adv.|极其地，非常地，过分地|Meaning "to a very great extent; excessively."  It's an informal and emphatic way to express something is huge or large in some way, often implying an exaggeration.  It's built by adding the prefix "over-" (meaning "excessively") to "hugely" (meaning "very much").|例句：The movie was overhugely popular. 这部电影非常受欢迎。

例句：The task was overhugely difficult. 这项任务极其困难。|近义词：enormously, immensely, vastly, exceedingly, extremely|反义词：slightly, minimally, insignificantly|词性:副词
