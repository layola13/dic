cheerier: /ˈtʃɪriər/ (US), /ˈtʃɪəriə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更快乐的，更兴高采烈的|Comparative form of "cheery."  It describes someone or something as being more full of cheer or happiness than another person or thing.|"The team was cheerier after their victory."  球队获胜后更加兴高采烈。|近义词：happier, merrier, jollier|反义词：gloomier, sadder, more depressed|词性:形容词
