flirtingly:/ˈflɜːrtɪŋli/ (US) /ˈflɜːtɪŋli/ (UK)| adv.|轻佻地，调情地|In a way that suggests playful romantic interest, often in a lighthearted or teasing manner.|She smiled flirtingly at him across the room. 她隔着房间轻佻地对他笑了笑。|例句：He winked flirtingly. 他轻佻地眨了眨眼。|近义词：playfully, teasingly, coyly|反义词：seriously, formally|词性:副词

flirting:/ˈflɜːrtɪŋ/ (US) /ˈflɜːtɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|调情，打调情|The act of behaving as though attracted to or trying to attract someone, but without serious intentions.|The flirting between the two colleagues was obvious to everyone.  两位同事之间的调情对每个人来说都很明显。|例句：Her constant flirting annoyed him. 她不断的调情让他很烦。|近义词：dalliance, coquetry|反义词：seriousness, commitment|词性:名词
