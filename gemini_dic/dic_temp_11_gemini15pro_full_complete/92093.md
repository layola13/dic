hyperresonance:/ˌhaɪpərˈrezənəns/| n. |亢音，过共鸣|An exaggerated resonant sound produced when air is percussed over a lung cavity.  A sound like that produced by striking an empty barrel.|The doctor detected hyperresonance in the patient's left lung, indicating a possible pneumothorax.|近义词：tympany, resonance|反义词：dullness, flatness|词性:noun

词源: From hyper- + resonance, from Latin resonantia "echo," from resonare "to resound,"
