cognizing:/ˈkɒɡnaɪzɪŋ/ /ˈkɑːɡnɪˌzaɪzɪŋ/| v. |认知，认识|The process of becoming aware of something through the senses or through the mind.|She is cognizing the importance of education. 她正在认识到教育的重要性.|近义词：perceiving, understanding, recognizing, apprehending, comprehending|反义词：ignoring, neglecting, overlooking|词性:动词(present participle)

Etymology: From Latin *cognōscō*  "get to know, learn," from *co-* (com-) "together" + *gnōscō* "I get to know."
