dissect:/daɪˈsekt/ /dɪˈsekt/| vt. |解剖，仔细分析|To cut apart (an animal body, plant, etc.) for study; to examine carefully part by part; analyze.|The biology students dissected a frog in class. 生物系学生在课堂上解剖了一只青蛙。|The lawyer dissected the witness's testimony, revealing several inconsistencies. 律师仔细分析了证人的证词，揭示了几个不一致之处。|近义词：analyze, examine, scrutinize, anatomize|反义词: synthesize, combine|词性: 动词

Etymology: From Latin dissecare "to cut apart," from dis- "apart" + secare "to cut."
