mohawk: /ˈmoʊhɔːk/ (American) /ˈməʊhɔːk/ (British)| n.|莫霍克族人，莫霍克语，莫西干发型|A member of a Native American people formerly inhabiting the Mohawk River valley in central New York State. Also refers to their Iroquoian language or a hairstyle with a shaved head except for a central strip of upright hair.|Example 1: The Mohawk tribe played a significant role in the history of the region. 莫霍克部落在这个地区的历史上扮演了重要的角色。
