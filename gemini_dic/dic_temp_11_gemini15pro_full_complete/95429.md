incineration:/ɪnˌsɪnəˈreɪʃən/| n.|焚烧，烧毁|The incineration of waste is a controversial method of disposal.  垃圾焚烧是一种有争议的处理方法。|例句：The city uses incineration to dispose of its waste.  这座城市使用焚烧来处理垃圾。

The incineration of medical waste is strictly regulated.医疗废物的焚烧受到严格的监管。|近义词：burning, combustion, cremation|反义词: preservation, conservation|词性:名词

Etymology:  From Late Latin incinerationem, noun of action from past-participle stem of incinerare "to reduce to ashes," from in- "in, into" + ciner- "ashes" + -are infinitive suffix.
