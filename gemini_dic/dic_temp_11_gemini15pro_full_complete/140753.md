omnitolerant:/ˌɒmnɪˈtɒlərənt/ | adj. | 全耐受的，全能忍受的 | Able to tolerate all conditions, especially a wide range of environmental conditions. |  This newly discovered bacteria is omnitolerant, thriving in extreme heat, cold, and even high salinity.  这种新发现的细菌具有全耐受性，在极热、极冷甚至高盐度环境中都能茁壮成长。 | Example: The omnitolerant organism was found thriving in both arctic and desert conditions.  这种全耐受生物被发现能够在北极和沙漠环境中茁壮成长。| Synonyms: adaptable, versatile, resilient | Antonyms: sensitive, intolerant, vulnerable | word property: adjective
