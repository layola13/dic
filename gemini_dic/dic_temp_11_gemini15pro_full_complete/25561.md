bulwark:/ˈbʊlwək/ | n. |壁垒，防御工事；保障，支柱|A defensive wall, typically a rampart of earth or other material, built as a protective fortification; something serving as a defense or safeguard. |The city's ancient bulwarks protected it from invaders. 这座城市的古老壁垒保护它免受侵略者的侵扰。|The constitution is a bulwark of our freedoms. 宪法是我们自由的保障。|Synonyms: rampart, fortification, defense, safeguard, bastion, stronghold |Antonyms: vulnerability, weakness| noun

bulwark:/ˈbʊlwək/ | vt. | 保护，防御；加固|To fortify or protect with a bulwark. |They bulwarked the city against attack. 他们加固了城市以抵御攻击。|  The company bulwarked its position in the market by acquiring a competitor.  这家公司通过收购竞争对手来巩固其市场地位。 |Synonyms: fortify, protect, defend, safeguard, strengthen |Antonyms: weaken, undermine| verb
