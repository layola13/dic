nonirrationally:/ˌnɒnɪˈræʃənəli/
adv.
非不理性地，并非不理性地
Not in an irrational manner; rationally or reasonably.
He acted nonirrationally, considering the circumstances.  他考虑到当时的情况，行动并非不理性。
She explained her decision nonirrationally, laying out all the facts. 她并非不理性地解释了她的决定，列出了所有的事实。
近义词: rationally, reasonably, sanely, logically
反义词: irrationally, unreasonably, absurdly
词性: 副词

词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin.
irrational (adjective): From Latin irrationalis, from in- "not" + rationalis "rational."
-ly (suffix): Forms adverbs from adjectives.
