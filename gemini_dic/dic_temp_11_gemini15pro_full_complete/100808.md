intriguingly:/ɪnˈtriːɡɪŋli/ | adv. |  引人入胜地，耐人寻味地 | In a manner that excites curiosity or interest. | The story was intriguingly told, keeping me hooked until the very end.  这个故事讲得引人入胜，让我一直着迷到最后。 | Synonyms: fascinatingly, captivatingly, interestingly, compellingly | Antonyms: boringly, dully, tediously | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From intrigue (v.) + -ing + -ly.  Intrigue comes from the Old French intriguer "to entangle, perplex," from the Late Latin intricare "to entangle," from Latin in- "in" + tricae "perplexities, hindrances."
