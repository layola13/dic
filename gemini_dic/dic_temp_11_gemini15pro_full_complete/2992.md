aeolian:/iˈoʊliən/ | adj. |风成的；风神埃俄罗斯的；轻飘的| relating to or arising from the action of the wind; relating to Aeolus, the Greek god of the winds; (of music) light and airy|The aeolian harp produces a haunting melody. 风弦琴发出萦绕的旋律。|Synonyms: airy, breezy, windswept|Antonyms: still, calm|adjective


aeolians:/iˈoʊliənz/ | n. |风成物| deposits of windblown sediment, such as loess or dune sand |The geologists studied the aeolians in the desert. 地质学家研究了沙漠中的风成物。|Synonyms: windblown deposits|Antonyms: N/A|noun
