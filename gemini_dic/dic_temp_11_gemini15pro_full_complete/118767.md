metalline:/ˈmɛtəlˌaɪn; -lɪn/
adjective
金属的；金属似的；含金属的
Resembling metal; metallic.  Having the qualities of metal. Containing metal.
例句：The sculpture had a metalline sheen. 雕塑具有金属光泽。

例句：The robot's metalline voice echoed through the hall. 机器人的金属般的声音回荡在大厅里。

近义词：metallic, metalliferous, metal-like
反义词：nonmetallic, non-metal

词性: adjective
