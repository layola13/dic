acerose: /ˈæsəˌroʊs/ | adj. | 针状的；松针状的 | Resembling a needle or pine needle; slender and pointed. | The plant has acerose leaves.  这种植物有针状的叶子。 | Synonyms: needle-like, acicular, setaceous | Antonyms: broad, wide | adjective
