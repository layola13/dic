expectationists:/ɛkspɛkˈteɪʃənɪsts/
n.
期望者；期待者；預期者
Those who hold expectations, especially those who have high or unrealistic hopes for the future.
The expectationists were disappointed when the project failed to deliver the promised results. 期望者们对项目未能兑现承诺的结果感到失望。
The young expectationists eagerly awaited the announcement of the contest winners. 年轻的期待者们热切地等待着比赛结果的宣布。
optimists, hopers
pessimists, cynics
noun
