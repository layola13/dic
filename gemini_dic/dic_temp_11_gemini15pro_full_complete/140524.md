olefine:/ˈoʊləˌfiːn/, /ˈəʊliːfiːn/ | n. |烯烃|An unsaturated hydrocarbon containing a carbon-carbon double bond.  A traditional name for alkenes.|例句：Ethylene is the simplest olefine. 乙烯是最简单的烯烃。

|例句：Olefines are important building blocks in the chemical industry. 烯烃是化学工业中的重要组成部分。|近义词：alkene|反义词: alkane|词性:noun
