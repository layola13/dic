mystification:/ˌmɪstɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/
n.
神秘化；迷惑；困惑
The act of mystifying or the state of being mystified.  The creation of a sense of mystery or confusion, often intentionally.
The magician's greatest trick was his mystification of the audience. 魔术师最伟大的技巧是他让观众感到迷惑不解。
近义词: bewilderment, confusion, puzzle, enigma
反义词: clarification, explanation, elucidation
词性: noun

mystify:/ˈmɪstɪfaɪ/
vt.
使迷惑；使神秘化
To bewilder or confuse someone; to make something obscure or difficult to understand.
The strange noises mystified everyone in the house.  奇怪的声音让房子里的每个人都感到迷惑不解。
近义词:  bewilder, confuse, perplex, baffle
反义词: clarify, explain, enlighten
词性: verb
