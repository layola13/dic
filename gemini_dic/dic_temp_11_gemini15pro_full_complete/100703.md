intractile:/ɪnˈtræktaɪl/ | adj. | 难处理的，倔强的，不柔顺的 | Not easily managed or controlled; stubborn; unyielding.  | Example: The intractile child refused to listen to his parents.  这个倔强的孩子拒绝听父母的话。 | Example:  The intractile metal proved difficult to mold into the desired shape. 这种难处理的金属很难塑造成想要的形状。 | 近义词: stubborn, obstinate, unyielding, unruly, refractory | 反义词: docile, compliant, obedient, manageable, tractable | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin *intractabilis, from in- "not" + tractabilis "manageable," from tractare "to handle, manage," frequentative of trahere "to draw, drag."
