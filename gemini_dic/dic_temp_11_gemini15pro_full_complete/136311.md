nonrepudiable:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈpjuː.di.ə.bəl/ /ˌnɑːn.rɪˈpjuː.di.ə.bəl/
adj.
不可否认的；无法抵赖的
Unable to be denied or disputed.  Relating to a service that provides proof of the integrity and origin of data in such a way that it cannot be refuted.
A nonrepudiable digital signature is a piece of electronic evidence that proves that a particular message or transaction actually originated from the person or entity whose digital signature is attached to it.  This helps to prevent fraud and ensures the authenticity of the message.


例句：
The system uses nonrepudiable digital signatures to ensure the integrity of transactions.
该系统使用不可否认的数字签名来确保交易的完整性。

Nonrepudiation services are essential for online transactions.
不可否认服务对于在线交易至关重要。


近义词：undeniable, irrefutable, incontestable
反义词：deniable, disputable, contestable
词性: adjective


词源:
From non- + repudiable, from repudiate (from Latin repudiare "to reject, cast off") + -able.
