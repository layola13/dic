brain-washing:/ˈbreɪnˌwɒʃɪŋ/ /ˈbreɪnˌwɑːʃɪŋ/| n.|洗脑|The systematic and often forcible indoctrination to induce someone to give up basic political, social, or religious beliefs and attitudes and to accept contrasting regimented ideas.|The cult used brainwashing techniques to control its members.  邪教组织使用洗脑手段来控制其成员。|Synonyms: indoctrination, propaganda, mind control, thought control|Antonyms: education, enlightenment, freethinking|noun

brainwash:/ˈbreɪnˌwɒʃ/ /ˈbreɪnˌwɑːʃ/| vt.|洗脑|To subject (someone) to intensive indoctrination and psychological manipulation.|The government was accused of trying to brainwash its citizens. 政府被指控试图给公民洗脑。|Synonyms: indoctrinate, propagandize, manipulate, condition|Antonyms: educate, enlighten, freethink|verb
