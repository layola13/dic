feast-days:/ˈfiːst deɪz/| n.|节日; 盛宴日; |A day designated for feasting or celebration, often of a religious nature.|例句：Many cultures have traditional feast-days throughout the year. 许多文化一年中都有传统的节日。

例句：The church calendar is full of feast-days commemorating important saints. 教会日历上充满了纪念重要圣徒的节日。|近义词：holidays, festivals, celebrations|反义词:ordinary days, weekdays|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *fæstdæg (unattested), from fæst (“fast, fixed time, festival”) + dæg (“day”). Cognate with Scots fast day (“fast day”), West Frisian feestdei (“feast day”), Dutch feestdag (“holiday”), German Festtag (“holiday”).  The meaning shifted from "a day of religious observance" to include days of secular celebration.
