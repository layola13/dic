exorhizous:/ˌɛksoʊˈraɪzəs/| adj.|外根的，根系外生的|Having roots that grow on the outside, as in certain epiphytic orchids.|Epiphytic orchids are often exorhizous, with roots clinging to the bark of trees.  附生兰花通常是外根的，根部附着在树皮上。|近义词:epiphytic, aerial-rooted|反义词:endorhizous, subterranean|adjective
