condylopod:/ˈkɒndɪləpɒd/ (US) /kənˈdɪləpɑːd/| n. |髁足动物，节肢动物|An arthropod, typically a crustacean or insect, having jointed appendages.  Specifically refers to those with walking legs ending in a single claw or point. |例句：Condylopods, unlike some other arthropods, have distinct walking legs.  髁足动物不像其他一些节肢动物，有明显的步行足。

例句：The study focused on the locomotion of various condylopods. 这项研究的重点是各种髁足动物的运动方式。|近义词：arthropod 节肢动物|反义词: N/A (No direct antonym for a biological classification)|词性: noun
