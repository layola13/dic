legislatures: /ˈledʒɪsleɪtʃərz/
n.
立法机关；立法机构
The United States has 50 state legislatures.
美国有50个州立法机关。

The national legislature passed the new law.
国家立法机关通过了这项新法律。

legislatures are responsible for making laws.
立法机关负责制定法律。

Synonym: parliament, assembly, congress
Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a governing body.  Perhaps "executive branch" in the context of contrasting government powers.)
词性: noun


Etymology:  From Late Middle English *legislatour, from Medieval Latin lēgislatōr, from Latin lēgis (“of law”) + lātor (“proposer”), from lātus (“carried, brought”).  The plural form, "legislatures," developed naturally in English.
