ironsmith:/ˈaɪərnˌsmɪθ/ | n. | 铁匠，锻工 | A person who works with iron, especially one who makes and repairs things made of iron. | The ironsmith hammered the red-hot metal into shape. 铁匠把烧红的金属锤打成形。 |  The ironsmith carefully crafted the ornate gate.  铁匠精心制作了华丽的大门。 | 近义词: blacksmith, metalworker, smith | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Old English *īs(e)rn-smiþ (attested only as īsern-smiða m. ‘ironsmith’), from īsern ‘iron’ + smiþ ‘smith’.  The term literally means "iron smith."
