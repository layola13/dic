dogmaticalnesses:/ˌdɒɡˈmætɪkəlnəsɪz/ /ˌdɔːɡˈmætɪkəlnəsɪz/
n.
教条主义；武断；独断
The state of being dogmatic; adherence to dogma, often in an arrogant or assertive manner.
His dogmaticalnesses on the subject alienated many of his colleagues. 他在这个问题上的武断疏远了他的许多同事。

近义词:dogmatism, rigidity, inflexibility, authoritarianism, doctrinaireism
反义词:open-mindedness, flexibility, tolerance, receptiveness
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From dogmatic + -al (suffix forming adjectives) + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives).  Ultimately from Greek dogma "opinion, tenet, doctrine."
