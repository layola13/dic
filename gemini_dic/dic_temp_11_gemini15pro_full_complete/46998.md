dashed:/dæʃt/ | adj. | 虚线的，匆忙的 | marked with dashes; done or made in a hurry |  He sent a dashed email before leaving. 他在离开前匆忙地发了一封电子邮件。 | synonyms: broken, hurried, rushed | antonyms: continuous, leisurely, unhurried | adjective

dashed:/dæʃt/ | v. (dashed, dashing) | 猛冲，撞击，使…破灭 | to run or move quickly and suddenly; to throw or strike something violently; to destroy or ruin something | The waves dashed against the rocks. 海浪猛烈地拍打着岩石。| synonyms: rush, dart, smash, shatter | antonyms: crawl, creep, protect, save | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *dashen*, from Old Norse *daska* (“to strike, slap”), of imitative origin.
