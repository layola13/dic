escalope:/ˈeskəˌlɒp/ (UK), /ˈeskəˌloʊp/ (US)| n.| 薄片; 肉片; 鱼片;  (尤指)炸肉排|A thin, boneless piece of meat, especially veal, that is often coated in breadcrumbs and fried. |例句：I ordered veal escalopes with a mushroom sauce. 我点了一份蘑菇酱小牛肉片。

例句：The chef prepared the escalopes perfectly. 厨师把肉片做得非常完美。

近义词：cutlet, fillet, steak|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially "chunk" or "joint" for a thicker piece of meat) |词性:noun
