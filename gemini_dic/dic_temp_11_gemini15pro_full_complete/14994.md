babblingly:/ˈbæblɪŋli/| adv.|喋喋不休地，絮絮叨叨地|In a babbling manner; characterized by babbling.  Speaking continuously and rapidly in a foolish or meaningless way.|She talked babblingly about her day, jumping from topic to topic.|近义词：incoherently, volubly, garrulously|反义词：coherently, succinctly, concisely|词性:副词

Etymology:
From "babble" + "-ing" + "-ly".  "Babble" comes from the Middle English "bablen,"  likely imitative of the sound of baby talk.
