indignify: /ɪnˈdɪɡnɪfaɪ/ | vt. |使愤慨，激怒|To arouse anger or indignation in; to offend. |例句：The rude comment indignified her. 这粗鲁的评论激怒了她。

例句：Being ignored by the manager indignified all the employees. 经理的无视激怒了所有员工。


近义词：offend, insult, affront, provoke, anger, enrage|反义词：appease, soothe, placate, calm|词性：动词
