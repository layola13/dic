hybridizes:/ˈhaɪbrɪdaɪzɪz/
verb
杂交，混合
causes to interbreed or cross-pollinate to produce hybrids.  Combines things of different types to form a new whole.
The gardener hybridizes different varieties of roses to create new colors. 园丁将不同品种的玫瑰杂交以创造新的颜色。

Scientists have hybridized cells from different species in the lab. 科学家们在实验室里杂交了不同物种的细胞。


Synonym: crossbreeds, interbreeds, merges, blends, combines
Antonym: separates, isolates, purifies
词性: 动词 (verb)
