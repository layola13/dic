countershots:/ˈkaʊntərˌʃɑːts/ /ˈkaʊntərˌʃɑts/
noun
反击镜头；反驳；反诉
shots that counter or oppose other shots, arguments, or accusations.
The documentary offered several countershots to the claims made in the news report. 这部纪录片对新闻报道中提出的说法进行了几次反驳。
synonyms: rebuttals, counterarguments, counterclaims
antonyms: arguments, claims, accusations
n.
