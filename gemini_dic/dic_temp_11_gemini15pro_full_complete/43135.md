coypu:/ˈkɔɪpuː/ (US), /ˈkɔɪpjuː/ (UK)| n.|海狸鼠，河狸鼠|A large, South American, aquatic rodent with webbed hind feet.|Coypus are native to South America and are known for their destructive burrowing habits. 海狸鼠原产于南美洲，以其破坏性的挖洞习性而闻名。|近义词：nutria, myocastor|反义词: N/A|词性: 名词
