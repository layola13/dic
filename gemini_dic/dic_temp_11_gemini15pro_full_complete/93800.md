immersion:/ɪˈmɜːrʒən/ (US) /ɪˈmɜːʃn/ (UK)| n.|沉浸，浸入，专心|The state of being deeply engaged or enveloped in something, often referring to a language, culture, or virtual reality experience.|
例句：Language immersion is a great way to learn. 沉浸式语言学习是一个很好的学习方法。

例句：He experienced total immersion in the virtual reality game. 他在虚拟现实游戏中体验了完全的沉浸感。


近义词：submersion, absorption, engagement|
反义词：detachment, withdrawal|
词性：noun


词源: From Late Latin *immersiōn- (stem of immersiō), from Latin immersus, past participle of immergere "to plunge in," from in- "in" + mergere "to dip, plunge."
