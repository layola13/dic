interbreath:/ˈɪntərbreθ/| n.|呼吸间隙；短暂的停顿|A short pause between breaths. |例句：The singer took a quick interbreath before the high note. 歌手在唱高音之前快速地吸了口气。

例句：There was a barely perceptible interbreath before she continued her story.  在她继续讲故事之前，几乎觉察不到的停顿了一下呼吸。|近义词：pause, respite, lull|反义词:continuation,  uninterrupted|词性:名词
