nonprovable:/ˌnɒnˈpruːvəbl/ |adj.| 不可证明的 | Not able to be proved; incapable of being demonstrated or verified. |  The theorem states that there are some nonprovable statements within any consistent formal system of arithmetic.  该定理指出，在任何一致的算术形式系统中，都存在一些不可证明的陈述。 |  The existence of nonprovable truths is a fundamental concept in mathematics.  不可证明的真理的存在是数学中的一个基本概念。 |Synonyms: indemonstrable, unprovable | Antonyms: provable, demonstrable | word property: adjective
