menaccanite:/mɛˈnækəˌnaɪt/ | n. |钛铁矿|A black mineral consisting of iron and titanium oxides; ilmenite. It is a common accessory mineral in igneous and metamorphic rocks. |  Menaccanite is a source of titanium. 钛铁矿是钛的来源。|  The beach sands were rich in menaccanite. 海滩上的沙子富含钛铁矿。 |Synonyms: ilmenite, titanic iron ore| Antonyms: None (as it's a specific mineral) | noun
