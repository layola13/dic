affiliating:/əˈfɪlieɪtɪŋ/
verb
加入，联合，隶属
The process of becoming part of or forming a close relationship with a larger group or organization.
The local group is affiliating itself with/to a national organization.  当地团体正在加入一个全国性组织。
The school is affiliated with a larger university. 这所学校隶属于一所更大的大学。
synonyms: joining, associating, connecting, linking, uniting, allying, merging
antonyms: disaffiliating, separating, disconnecting, severing
verb

affiliated:/əˈfɪlieɪtɪd/
adjective
附属的，隶属的
Connected with or controlled by a larger group or organization.
Our college is affiliated with the University of London.  我们的学院隶属于伦敦大学。
She was admitted to the hospital's affiliated medical school. 她被这所医院的附属医学院录取了。
synonyms: connected, related, associated, linked, attached, allied
antonyms: independent, unaffiliated, separate, detached
adjective

affiliation:/əˌfɪliˈeɪʃən/
noun
隶属关系，联系
A connection with a group or organization.
He was questioned about his political affiliations.  他被问及政治关系。
The group has affiliations with several international organizations.  该组织与几个国际组织有联系。
synonyms: association, connection, relationship, link, tie, alliance, membership
antonyms: disaffiliation, separation, detachment
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *affiliatus, past participle of affiliare (“to adopt as a son”), from Latin ad (“to”) + filius (“son”).
