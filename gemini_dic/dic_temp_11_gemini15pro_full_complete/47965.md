deciduously: /dɪˈsɪdʒuəsli/
adjective
落叶地；定期脱落地
Shedding leaves annually.  Trees that lose their leaves seasonally are called deciduous.
The oak tree is deciduous, shedding its leaves every fall.  橡树是落叶树，每年秋天都会落叶。
Synonyms: seasonally, annually, periodically
Antonyms: evergreen, persistent, perennial
词性: adjective

deciduous: /dɪˈsɪdʒuəs/
adjective
脱落的；暂时的
Falling off or shed seasonally or at a certain stage of development in the life cycle. Some animals have deciduous antlers.
Some animals possess deciduous teeth which are replaced by permanent teeth as they mature.  一些动物有脱落的牙齿，随着它们的成熟，这些牙齿会被恒牙取代。
Synonyms: temporary, transient, ephemeral
Antonyms: permanent, lasting, enduring
词性: adjective



ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *deciduus, from decidere (“to fall off, fall down”), from de- (“down, off”) + cadere (“to fall”).
