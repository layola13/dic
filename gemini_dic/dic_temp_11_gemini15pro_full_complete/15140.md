back-away:/ˌbæk əˈweɪ/
verb phrasal
后退，退缩
To move backwards, especially as a result of fear or reluctance.
The dog started to growl, so I slowly backed away.  狗开始咆哮，所以我慢慢地后退。
He backed away from the confrontation. 他退出了这场冲突。
近义词: retreat, withdraw, recoil
反义词: advance, approach
词性: 动词短语
