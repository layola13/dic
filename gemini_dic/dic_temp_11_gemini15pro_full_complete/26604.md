cached:/kæʃt/ | adj. |缓存的；高速缓存的|Stored in a cache, especially a computer's cache, for faster access later.|The website uses cached images to improve loading speed. 该网站使用缓存图像来提高加载速度。|近义词:stored, saved, preserved|反义词:unavailable, uncached|词性:形容词

词源: From the verb "cache," from French "cacher," meaning "to hide" or "to store."
