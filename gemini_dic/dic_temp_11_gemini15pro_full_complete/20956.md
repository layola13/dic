bleeps:/bliːps/ | n. |哔哔声；消音；审查删除的内容 |A short, high-pitched sound, often used to censor offensive language or to represent electronic noise.  It can also refer to the act of censoring itself. |The interview was broadcast with bleeps covering the swear words.  采访播出时，脏话都被消音了。| synonyms: beeps, tones, censorship | antonyms: uncensored, explicit | noun

bleep:/bliːp/ | v. |发出哔哔声；审查删除 |To make a short, high-pitched sound, or to censor (audio or video) by replacing offensive material with such a sound. |The producer bleeped out the offensive language. 制作人用哔哔声盖掉了攻击性的语言。| synonyms: censor, expurgate | antonyms: broadcast, air | verb
