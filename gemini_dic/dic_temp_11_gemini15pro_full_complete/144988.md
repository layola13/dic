overactions:/ˌoʊvərˈækʃənz/
n.
过度反应；过激行为
exaggerated reactions or responses to a situation.
Her overactions to the news were quite dramatic. 她对这个消息的过度反应相当戏剧化。

The actor's overactions ruined the scene.  这个演员的过分表演毁了这场戏。

近义词:overreactions, exaggerations, histrionics
反义词:underreactions, restraint, composure
词性:名词(plural)
