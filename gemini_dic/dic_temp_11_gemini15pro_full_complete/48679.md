deferred:/dɪˈfɜːrd/ /dəˈfɜːrd/| adj.|延期的，推迟的|Postponed or delayed to a later time.|例句：The payment was deferred until next month. 付款被推迟到下个月。|近义词：postponed, delayed, shelved|反义词：immediate, prompt|词性:形容词

deferred:/dɪˈfɜːrd/ /dəˈfɜːrd/| v.|推迟，延期|To postpone or delay something to a later time.|例句：They deferred the decision until after the election. 他们将决定推迟到选举之后。|近义词：postpone, delay, put off|反义词：expedite, hasten|词性:动词
