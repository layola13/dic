disruption:/dɪsˈrʌpʃən/| n.|中断，扰乱，破坏|A disruption is something that breaks up, disturbs, or interrupts an event, activity, or process.|The construction caused a major disruption to traffic. 建筑工程对交通造成了重大干扰。|近义词：interruption, disturbance, interference|反义词:order, continuity|noun

disruptive:/dɪsˈrʌptɪv/| adj.|扰乱性的，破坏性的|Causing disruption or interruption.|The disruptive student was asked to leave the classroom.  扰乱课堂秩序的学生被要求离开教室。|近义词：disturbing, unsettling, troublesome|反义词:constructive, helpful, beneficial|adjective
