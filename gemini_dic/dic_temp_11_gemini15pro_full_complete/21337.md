bloomerism:/ˈbluːmərɪzəm/
n.
女式灯笼裤; 女式灯笼裤运动; (尤指妇女的)改革社会习俗运动
The adoption of bloomers, loose trousers gathered at the ankles, as women's clothing.  Also: the movement advocating this.
Women's rights advocates in the 19th century embraced bloomerism as a symbol of their fight for equality.  19世纪的女权倡导者将女式灯笼裤视为争取平等的象征。

She gave a lecture on the history of bloomerism. 她做了一个关于女式灯笼裤运动历史的讲座。

近义词:feminism, women's liberation, women's suffrage
反义词:traditionalism, conservatism
