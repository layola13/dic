handwheel:/ˈhændwiːl/| n.|手轮；手舵|A wheel turned by hand to operate a machine, valve, etc. |
The captain spun the handwheel sharply to avoid the rocks. 船长猛地转动舵轮以避开礁石。

The handwheel controls the opening and closing of the valve.  这个手轮控制阀门的开关。

近义词：wheel, control knob, handle|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps automated control)|词性:noun
