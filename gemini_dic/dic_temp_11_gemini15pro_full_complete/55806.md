divertor:/daɪˈvɜːrtər/| n.|分流器，偏滤器|A device that diverts something, especially a device in a fusion reactor that draws away excess heat and particles.|The divertor in the fusion reactor is crucial for managing the extreme temperatures.|Synonym: deflector, shunt|Antonym: collector|noun

divert:/daɪˈvɜːrt/| vt.|转移，使转向|To cause something or someone to change direction or course.|The police diverted traffic around the accident scene. 警察将交通绕过事故现场。|Synonym: redirect, reroute|Antonym: maintain, continue|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin dīvertere, from Latin dēvertere ("to turn aside"), from dē- ("from, away") + vertere ("to turn").
