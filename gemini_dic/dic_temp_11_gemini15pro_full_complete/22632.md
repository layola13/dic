bopster:/ˈbɒpstər/ (UK) /ˈbɑːpstər/ (US)| n. | 爵士乐爱好者；爵士乐迷 | A person who is enthusiastic about or devoted to bop, a style of jazz. | He was a real bopster, always listening to Charlie Parker and Dizzy Gillespie. 他是一个真正的爵士乐迷，总是听着查理·帕克和迪兹·吉莱斯皮的音乐。 | Example 2: The bopsters gathered at the club every Friday night to jam. 爵士乐迷们每周五晚上都聚集在俱乐部里即兴演奏。 | 近义词: jazz aficionado, jazz enthusiast | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps someone who dislikes jazz) | 词性: noun
