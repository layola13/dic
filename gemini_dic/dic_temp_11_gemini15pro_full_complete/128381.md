nephew: /ˈnefjuː/ (US), /ˈnevjuː/ (UK)| n. |侄子；外甥|A son of one's brother or sister, or of one's brother-in-law or sister-in-law.|例句：My nephew is graduating from college next year. 我的侄子明年就要大学毕业了。

|例句：She bought her nephew a new toy car. 她给她的侄子买了一辆新的玩具汽车。

|近义词：kin, relative|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *neveu, *nevod (masculine), *neve (feminine), from Latin nepōtem (nominative nepōs) "grandson, nephew", from Proto-Indo-European *nepot- "descendant, sister's son".
