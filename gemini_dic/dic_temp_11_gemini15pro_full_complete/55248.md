dissimulate:/dɪˈsɪmjʊleɪt/
verb
假装；掩饰；装作没注意到
To conceal one's true motives, feelings, or beliefs.
He dissimulated his fear with a smile. 他用微笑掩饰他的恐惧。
dissemble, feign, pretend, mask
reveal, expose, divulge, unmask
v.
