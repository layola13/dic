counter-battery:/ˌkaʊntərˈbætəri/ | adj. n.|反炮兵的，反炮兵火力；反炮兵部队|Relating to or denoting artillery fire directed against enemy artillery.  Also, the units or weapons employed in this role. |The counter-battery fire suppressed the enemy's artillery. 反炮兵火力压制了敌人的炮兵。| The army deployed a new counter-battery radar system. 陆军部署了新的反炮兵雷达系统。|Synonyms: counterfire,  anti-artillery |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "supporting fire") |adjective, noun
