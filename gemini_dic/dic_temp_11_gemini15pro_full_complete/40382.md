contradictories:/ˌkɒntrəˈdɪktəriz/ | n. |矛盾，对立面|Things that are contradictories cannot both be true at the same time.|The two witnesses gave contradictory accounts of the accident. 两名目击者对事故的描述相互矛盾。|synonyms: opposites, antitheses|antonyms: consistencies, agreements|noun

contradictory:/ˌkɒntrəˈdɪktəri/ | adj. |矛盾的，对立的|If two or more facts, ideas, or statements are contradictory, they state or imply that opposite things are true.|The evidence is contradictory. 证据是相互矛盾的。|synonyms: conflicting, opposing, inconsistent|antonyms: consistent, agreeing, harmonious|adjective
