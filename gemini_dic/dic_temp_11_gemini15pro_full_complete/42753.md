countrifiedness:/ˈkʌntrifaɪdnəs/ | n. |乡村气息；淳朴；土气 |The quality or state of being countrified; rusticity or unsophistication. |Her countrifiedness was charming, a refreshing change from the city's hustle and bustle. 她的乡村气息很迷人，与城市的喧嚣相比，令人耳目一新。|近义词：rusticity, simplicity, unsophistication, naiveté |反义词：sophistication, worldliness, urbanity| 词性: 名词

countrified:/ˈkʌntrifaɪd/ | adj. |乡村的；淳朴的；土气的 |Having the characteristics or appearance of country life; rustic or unsophisticated. |He had a countrified accent that made him stand out in the city. 他的乡村口音让他在城市里显得格格不入。 |近义词：rustic, rural, simple, unsophisticated |反义词：sophisticated, urban, worldly |词性: 形容词

词源: countrifiedness 源自形容词 countrified，countrified 由 country (乡村) + -fied (使...化) 构成，-fied 源自法语 -fié，最终源自拉丁语 -ficatus。 -ness 是一个名词后缀，表示状态或性质。
