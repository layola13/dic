floodlighted:/flʌdˈlaɪtɪd/
adjective
被泛光灯照亮的
Illuminated with floodlights.
The football pitch was floodlighted for the evening match. 足球场被泛光灯照亮，以进行晚间比赛。
The castle was floodlighted at night, creating a magical effect.  城堡在夜间被泛光灯照亮，营造出一种神奇的效果。

近义词:illuminated, lit
反义词:dark, unlit
词性:adjective

词源: floodlight (n.) + -ed (suffix forming past participles and adjectives)
floodlight: flood (n.) + light (n.)
flood (n.): from Old English *flod, from Proto-Germanic *floduz "flood, flowing, stream," from PIE *pleu- "to flow."
light (n.): from Old English leoht "light, daylight; luminous heavenly body; candle, lamp," from Proto-Germanic *leukhtam (source also of Old Saxon lioht, Old Frisian liacht, Old High German lioht, German Licht, Old Norse ljós, Gothic liuhaþ), from PIE root *leuk- "light, brightness."
