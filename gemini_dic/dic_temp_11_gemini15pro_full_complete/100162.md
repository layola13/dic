interred:/ɪnˈtɜːrd/ /ɪnˈtɝːd/
verb
埋葬；安葬
To place (a corpse) in a grave or tomb, typically with funeral rites.
The king was interred with great ceremony. 国王被隆重安葬了。

interred:/ɪnˈtɜːrd/ /ɪnˈtɝːd/
verb
埋藏；藏匿
to deposit (something, such as treasure) in the earth and cover or conceal it
The pirates interred their treasure on a deserted island. 海盗们把他们的宝藏埋藏在一个荒岛上。

interred:/ɪnˈtɜːrd/ /ɪnˈtɝːd/
adjective
已埋葬的；已安葬的
buried
His body lies interred in the family vault. 他的遗体安葬在家族墓穴中。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *interrare* "to put into the earth, bury," from *in-* "in, into" + *terra* "earth, land."

SYNONYMS->
bury, entomb, inhume, lay to rest

ANTONYMS->
exhume, disinter
