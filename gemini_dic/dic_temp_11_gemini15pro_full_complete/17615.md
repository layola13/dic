beaver:/ˈbiːvər/ (US), /ˈbiːvə(r)/ (UK)| n.|海狸，河狸|A large semiaquatic rodent with a broad, flat tail, webbed hind feet, and thick brown fur. Beavers are known for building dams and lodges.|The beavers built a dam across the stream. 海狸在溪流上筑了一座水坝。|近义词:rodent|反义词:None|noun

beaver:/ˈbiːvər/ (US), /ˈbiːvə(r)/ (UK)| vi.|努力工作，忙个不停 (informal)|To work hard and energetically.|He's been beavering away at his dissertation all week. 他整个星期都在努力写他的论文。|近义词:toil, slave away|反义词:idle, laze|verb
