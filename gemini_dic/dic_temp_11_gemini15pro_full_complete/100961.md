inure:/ɪˈnjʊər/ /ɪˈnʊr/| v. |使习惯于（尤指辛苦或不愉快的事物）;  生效;  适用; |to accustom someone to something, especially something unpleasant, so that they are no longer strongly affected by it. Also, to come into operation; to take effect; to be valid.|例句：The hardships of army life inured the young men to physical discomfort. 军队生活的艰苦使这些年轻人习惯了身体上的不适。

例句：The new regulations will inure to the benefit of all employees. 新规章将使所有雇员受益。|近义词：accustom, habituate, harden, toughen|反义词:sensitize, soften|词性:动词
