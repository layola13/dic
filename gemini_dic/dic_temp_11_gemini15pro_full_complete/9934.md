apery:/ˈeɪpəri/
n.
模仿; 猿猴行为; 仿效
An imitation or superficial resemblance of something.  The behavior of apes, especially in its resemblance to that of humans.  Mimicry.
The politician's speech was a blatant apery of his opponent's earlier address.  这位政治家的演讲是对其对手先前演讲的公然模仿。
His attempts at sophistication were nothing more than an apery of upper-class manners. 他试图表现得老练，不过是东施效颦般模仿上流社会的举止而已。
近义词: imitation, mimicry, mockery, parody
反义词: originality, authenticity, genuineness
词性: noun

Etymology:  From Old French *aperie, from aper "ape," from Late Latin *appa, perhaps of imitative origin. Ultimately related to "ape."
