hingelike:/ˈhɪndʒˌlaɪk/| adj.|像铰链的；像枢纽的|Resembling a hinge in form or function; able to bend or turn like a hinge.  类似于铰链的形状或功能；能够像铰链一样弯曲或转动。|The robot arm moved with a hingelike motion.  机器人的手臂以类似铰链的动作移动。|近义词：hinged, jointed, articulated|反义词：rigid, fixed|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From *hinge* + *-like*.  *Hinge* comes from Old English *hingen* "to hang," related to German *hängen*. *-like* is a suffix used to form adjectives meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of."  由 *hinge* 和 *-like* 组成。*Hinge* 来自古英语 *hingen* “悬挂”，与德语 *hängen* 相关。*-like* 是一个后缀，用于构成意为“类似”或“具有...特征的”的形容词。
