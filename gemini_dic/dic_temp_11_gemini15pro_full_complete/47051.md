datapoint:/ˈdeɪtəpɔɪnt/ |n.|数据点，数据项|A single, distinct fact or piece of information, usually a numerical value, that is collected and used as a basis for reasoning, discussion, or calculation.|The datapoint shows a significant increase in sales this month. 这个数据点显示本月销售额显著增长。|Synonyms: data element, data item, value, statistic|Antonyms: aggregate, total|noun
