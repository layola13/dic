launcegaye: /lɔːnsɡeɪ/ | n. |  长矛游戏；马上枪术比赛 | A medieval sport involving tilting with lances on horseback. |  The knights participated in the launcegaye as part of the tournament. 骑士们参加了马上枪术比赛，作为锦标赛的一部分。| 近义词：jousting, tilting | 反义词：None | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *launcegay, from Old French *lancegaie, from lance (“lance”) + gaie (“gaiety, joy”).  来源于中古英语 *launcegay，来自古法语 *lancegaie，由 lance（“长矛”）+ gaie（“快乐，喜悦”）组成。
