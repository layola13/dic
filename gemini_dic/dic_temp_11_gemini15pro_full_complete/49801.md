demonologist:/ˌdiːmənˈɒlədʒɪst/ | n. |恶魔学家；研究鬼神学者|A person who studies demons or believes in demonology.|例句：The demonologist was called in to investigate the strange occurrences in the house. 恶魔学家被叫来调查房子里发生的怪事。

例句：He consulted a demonologist about the supposed haunting. 他就所谓的闹鬼事件咨询了一位恶魔学家。|近义词：exorcist, spiritualist|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *daemonologus*, from Ancient Greek δαίμων (daímōn, “deity, spirit”) + -λογία (-logía, “-logy”).
