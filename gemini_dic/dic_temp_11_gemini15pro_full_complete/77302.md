gateless:/ˈɡeɪtləs/| adj.|无门的，开放的，无限制的|Without a gate; open and unrestricted; also, metaphorically, free from limitations or barriers.|The gateless city welcomed all who sought refuge. 这座不设防的城市欢迎所有寻求庇护的人。|近义词：open, unrestricted, free, accessible|反义词:closed, restricted, limited, gated|词性:形容词
Etymology:  "gate" (Old English *gæt, from Proto-Germanic *gatan) + "-less" (Old English -lēas).  Essentially meaning "without a gate."
