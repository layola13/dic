gazumpers:/ɡəˈzʌmpərz/
| n. |抬价者；坐地起价者|Someone who gazumps another person.  A person who agrees to sell something (especially real estate) and then accepts a higher offer from someone else.|The gazumpers swooped in with a higher offer just before we were due to exchange contracts.|近义词： profiteers, speculators |反义词: N/A|名词
gazumper:/ɡəˈzʌmpər/
| n. |抬价者；坐地起价者|Someone who gazumps another person. A person who agrees to sell something (especially real estate) and then accepts a higher offer from someone else.|The gazumper offered an extra £10,000 for the house.|近义词： profiteer, speculator |反义词: N/A|名词
gazump:/ɡəˈzʌmp/
| v. |抬价；坐地起价|To raise the price of something (especially real estate) that has already been agreed upon with another buyer.|We were gazumped at the last minute by a cash buyer.|近义词：outbid, overbid |反义词: N/A|动词
