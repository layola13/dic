magnifies:/ˈmæɡnɪfaɪz/| v. |放大，扩大，夸大|To make something appear larger than it is, especially with a lens or microscope; to make something seem more important or serious than it really is.|例句：The microscope magnifies the object 100 times.  显微镜将物体放大了100倍。

例句：The media tends to magnify the dangers of air travel. 媒体倾向于夸大航空旅行的危险。|近义词：enlarge, amplify, exaggerate, intensify|反义词：reduce, diminish, minimize|词性：动词

詞源: From Late Latin *magnificare, from Latin magnificus (“magnificent”) + -ificare, equivalent to magni- (“great, large”) + -fy.
