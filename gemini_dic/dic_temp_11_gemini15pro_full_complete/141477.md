opinionately:/əˈpɪnjəneɪtli/
adjective
固执己见地，武断地
Holding stubbornly to one's own opinion, especially without considering the views of others.
She argued her point opinionately, refusing to listen to any other perspectives. 她固执己见地争论着自己的观点，拒绝听取任何其他意见。
He opinionately insisted on his own method, even though it was clearly inefficient.  他固执地坚持自己的方法，即使它显然效率低下。
近义词:dogmatically, stubbornly, assertively, peremptorily
反义词:open-mindedly, receptively, flexibly
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From opinion + -ate + -ly.  "Opinion" comes from Old French "opinion," from Latin "opinio," meaning "thought, conjecture, fancy." The suffix "-ate" forms adjectives or verbs, and "-ly" forms adverbs from adjectives.
