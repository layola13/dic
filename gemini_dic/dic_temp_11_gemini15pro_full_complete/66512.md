exhalant:/ˈɛkshələnt/ |adj. n.|呼出的，呼气；呼气物|Something that exhales; specifically, a current of air or breath expelled from the lungs.|The exhalant breath of the whale created a misty plume above the water.  鲸鱼呼出的气息在水面上形成了一股雾状的羽流。 |exhaled, expiratory|inhalant|adjective, noun


exhalant:/ˈɛkshələnt/ |n.|呼出的气体；呼气物|A substance that is exhaled.|The scientist analyzed the exhalants from the volcano. 科学家分析了火山喷出的气体。|exhalation, expiration|inhalation|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *exhalant- (stem of exhalans), present participle of exhalare "to breathe out," from ex- "out" + halare "to breathe."
