hornets:/ˈhɔːrnɪts/ | n. | 大黄蜂（复数） | Large, stinging social wasps.  They are known for building large, papery nests and for their aggressive behavior when their nest is threatened. |  The hornets built a nest in the tree. 大黄蜂在树上筑巢。|  The hornets swarmed angrily when we approached their nest.  当我们靠近它们的巢穴时，大黄蜂愤怒地蜂拥而至。 | synonyms: wasps, yellow jackets | antonyms: (No direct antonym for an insect.  Could consider "beneficial insects" like ladybugs or bees as a loose antonym in terms of their impact on humans) | noun
