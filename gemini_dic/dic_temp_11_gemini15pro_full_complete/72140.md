floodlights:/ˈflʌdˌlaɪts/| n.|泛光灯，泛光照明|Large, powerful lamps used to illuminate a wide area, often for sporting events or security purposes. |The floodlights illuminated the stadium for the night game.  泛光灯照亮了体育场，供夜间比赛使用。

The floodlights made it possible to continue working after dark. 泛光灯使得天黑后继续工作成为可能。 |近义词： spotlights, searchlights|反义词:darkness, shadows|名词


floodlight:/ˈflʌdˌlaɪt/| v.|用泛光灯照亮|To illuminate with floodlights. |They floodlit the building for the grand opening.  他们用泛光灯照亮了大楼，以庆祝盛大开业。

Security cameras and floodlights protect the property.  监控摄像头和泛光灯保护着这处房产。|近义词： illuminate, light up|反义词:darken, obscure|动词
