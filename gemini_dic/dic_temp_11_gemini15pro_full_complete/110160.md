liegedom: /ˈliːdʒdəm/
n.
臣服; 领地; 受保护国
The territory or area over which a liege lord has authority.  A state of being bound to feudal service and allegiance.
The king ruled over a vast liegedom. 国王统治着广阔的领地。
His liegedom stretched beyond the mountains. 他的领地延伸到群山之外。

近义词: domain, territory, realm, fief, demesne
反义词: N/A
词性: Noun

词源: From Middle English *legedom, from Old French *liegeedome, from liege + -dom. Ultimately from Frankish *leudes, "people," and Proto-Germanic *þeudō.
