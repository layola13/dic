nonabolitionist:/ˌnɑːnˌæbəˈlɪʃənɪst/
noun
非废奴主义者
A person who opposed the abolition of slavery.
The nonabolitionists argued that slavery was essential to the Southern economy.  非废奴主义者认为奴隶制对南方经济至关重要。
He was a staunch nonabolitionist, believing slavery was divinely ordained. 他是一个坚定的非废奴主义者，相信奴隶制是神圣的。
synonyms: proslavery advocate, antiabolitionist
antonyms: abolitionist, emancipationist
