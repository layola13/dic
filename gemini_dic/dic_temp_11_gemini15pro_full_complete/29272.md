cassowary:/ˈkæsəˌwɛri/ | n. |食火鸡|A large, flightless bird native to New Guinea and northeastern Australia, known for its powerful legs, sharp claws, and casque-like head covering. |例句：The cassowary is a dangerous bird with a powerful kick. 食火鸡是一种危险的鸟，它的踢腿很有力。

例句：We saw a cassowary while hiking in the rainforest. 我们在雨林徒步旅行时看到了一只食火鸡。|近义词：rhea, emu, ostrich|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
