leapfrogged:/ˌliːpˈfrɒɡd/ /ˌliːpˈfrɑːɡd/| v. |跳跃式发展，超越|To surpass or overtake someone or something, often suddenly or unexpectedly, as if by leaping over them. | The company leapfrogged its competitors by investing heavily in new technology.  这家公司通过大力投资新技术，超越了竞争对手。

Nokia, once a market leader, was leapfrogged by Apple and Samsung in the smartphone market.  诺基亚曾经是市场领导者，但在智能手机市场被苹果和三星超越。|近义词：overtake, surpass, outstrip, bypass|反义词: lag behind, fall behind|词性:动词

leapfrogging:/ˌliːpˈfrɒɡɪŋ/ /ˌliːpˈfrɑːɡɪŋ/| n. |跳跃式发展，超越（名词形式）|The act of leapfrogging. | Leapfrogging is a common strategy in rapidly evolving industries. 在快速发展的行业中，跳跃式发展是一种常见的策略。

The leapfrogging of traditional retailers by online giants is a clear trend.  在线巨头对传统零售商的超越是一个明显的趋势。|近义词：overtaking, surpassing, outstripping|反义词: falling behind|词性:名词
