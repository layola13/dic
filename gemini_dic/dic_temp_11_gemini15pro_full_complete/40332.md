contraband:/ˈkɑːntrəˌbænd/ /ˈkɒntrəˌbænd/| n. |走私货，违禁品|Goods that are illegally imported or exported.|例句：The police seized a large shipment of contraband. 警方查获了一大批走私货。|近义词：smuggled goods, illicit goods, illegal goods|反义词:legal goods|词性:名词

contraband:/ˈkɑːntrəˌbænd/ /ˈkɒntrəˌbænd/| adj. |违禁的，走私的|Illegal or prohibited.|例句：Contraband cigarettes are often sold on the black market.  走私香烟经常在黑市上出售。|近义词：illegal, prohibited, illicit, unlawful|反义词:legal, lawful, permitted|词性:形容词
