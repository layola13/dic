critter:/ˈkrɪtər/| n.|小动物，生物，家伙|A critter is a living creature, typically a small animal or insect.  It's an informal and often affectionate term.|例句：Look at that cute little critter! 瞧那只可爱的小动物！

|例句：The forest is full of interesting critters. 森林里充满了各种有趣的生物。|近义词：creature, animal, beast, varmint|反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "plant" in some contexts) |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "creature," with shortening and alteration of vowel sounds, common in informal American English.  The term likely gained popularity in the 19th century.
