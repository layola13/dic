jungian:/ˈjʌŋiən/ | adj. n. | 荣格派的；荣格学说的；荣格学说的追随者 | of or relating to the Swiss psychiatrist Carl Jung or his psychological theories. |  例句：She had a Jungian interpretation of the dream. 她对这个梦有一个荣格式的解读。 | 近义词：analytical, psychodynamic | 反义词：Freudian | 词性：形容词, 名词

jungian:/ˈjʌŋiən/ | n. | 荣格学说的追随者 | a follower of the psychological theories of Carl Jung. | 例句：He is a well-known Jungian. 他是一位著名的荣格学说的追随者。 | 近义词：analyst, psychotherapist | 反义词：Freudian | 词性：名词
