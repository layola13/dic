cetaceans:/sɪˈteɪʃənz/ | n. |鲸类动物|Aquatic mammals constituting the order Cetacea, including whales, dolphins, and porpoises, characterized by their streamlined bodies, horizontal tail flukes, and blowholes for breathing.|例句：Cetaceans are highly intelligent marine mammals. 鲸类动物是高度智能的海洋哺乳动物。|例句：The documentary focused on the migration patterns of various cetaceans. 这部纪录片重点讲述了各种鲸类动物的迁徙模式。|近义词：whales, dolphins, porpoises|反义词:None|Noun
