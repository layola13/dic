incorrectly: /ˌɪnkəˈrektli/ | adv. | 不正确地，错误地 |  in a way that is not correct or true.  Derived from "in-" (not) + "correct" (right, free from error) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  The root "correct" comes from the Latin "correctus," past participle of "corrigere," meaning "to straighten, set right." | He answered the question incorrectly. 他错误地回答了这个问题。|  wrongly, mistakenly, inaccurately | correctly, accurately, properly | adverb
