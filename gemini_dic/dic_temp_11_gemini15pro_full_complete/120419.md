minitrain:/ˈmɪniˌtreɪn/| n.|小型火车，微型火车|A small-scale train, often used for amusement or in model railroads.|例句：The children loved riding on the minitrain at the park. 孩子们喜欢在公园里乘坐小型火车。

例句：He built a minitrain set in his basement. 他在地下室里建了一个小型火车模型。|近义词：miniature train, model train|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly large train, full-sized train)|noun
