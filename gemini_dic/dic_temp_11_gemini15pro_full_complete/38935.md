confide:/kənˈfaɪd/ | v. | 吐露；委托；信赖 | to tell something secret or personal to someone who you trust not to tell anyone else; to put something, such as a task or responsibility, into someone's care | She confided her fears to her best friend. 她向她最好的朋友倾诉了她的恐惧。| synonyms: reveal, disclose, divulge, share, admit, impart | antonyms: conceal, hide, suppress | verb

confide:/kənˈfaɪd/ | v. |  信赖；信任 | to trust someone with information or a task |  He confided the task to his most trusted colleague. 他把这项任务委托给了他最信任的同事。| synonyms: entrust, commit, delegate | antonyms: distrust, mistrust | verb
