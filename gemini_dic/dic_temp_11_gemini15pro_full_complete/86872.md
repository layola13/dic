hemionus:/ˌhɛmiˈoʊnəs/ | n. |亚洲野驴;半驴|A wild ass (Equus hemionus) of Asia, related to the onager and having a sandy or grayish coat with a dark stripe along the back. | The hemionus roams the steppes of Central Asia. 亚洲野驴在中亚的草原上漫游。| The kiang, or Tibetan wild ass, is a subspecies of hemionus. 藏野驴是亚洲野驴的一个亚种。|Synonyms: Asiatic wild ass, kiang, onager| Antonyms: domesticated donkey, horse| noun
