debted:/ˈdɛtɪd/| adj.|负债的，感激的|Owing gratitude or recognition to another, beholden.|例句：I am deeply indebted to my parents for their support. 我非常感激父母的支持。|例句：She felt indebted to him for saving her life. 她对他救了她的命感到感激。|近义词：obligated, beholden, grateful|反义词：unappreciative, ungrateful|词性:形容词

词源:
From Old French *endetter, from en- + dette (“debt”), from Latin debitum, neuter of debitus (“owed”), perfect passive participle of debēre (“to owe”).
