blancmange:/bləˈmɑːnʒ/ | n. |牛奶冻，杏仁豆腐 |A sweet, creamy dessert made with milk or cream thickened with cornstarch, gelatin, or isinglass, often flavored with almonds or other nuts. |例句：The blancmange wobbled precariously on the plate.  牛奶冻在盘子上摇摇欲坠。

例句：She made a delicious almond-flavored blancmange for dessert. 她做了一份美味的杏仁味牛奶冻作为甜点。|近义词：pudding, custard, jelly |反义词: None |词性: noun
