graveled:/ˈɡrævəld/ | adj. |铺满砾石的；沙哑的| Covered with gravel; (of a voice) hoarse or husky | The driveway was graveled.  车道上铺满了砾石。|  The singer's voice was graveled after the concert.  演唱会后，歌手的声音变得沙哑了。 | 近义词: gravelly, stony, hoarse, raspy | 反义词: smooth, clear |词性:形容词

词源: From the noun "gravel" + "-ed" suffix, indicating a state or condition.  源自名词“gravel”（砾石）+ “-ed”后缀，表示状态或条件。
