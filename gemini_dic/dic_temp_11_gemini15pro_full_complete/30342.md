centage: /ˈsɛntɪdʒ/ | n. | 百分比，百分率 |  A rate, number, or amount in each hundred.  Derived from the Latin "centum" meaning hundred and "-age" denoting a collection or aggregate. | The percentage of students passing the exam was 90%.  考试及格的学生比例为90%。 | synonym: proportion, rate, ratio | antonym: whole, entirety | noun
