opulently:/ˈɒpjʊləntli/ | adv. | 豪华地，奢侈地 | In a rich and luxurious way; lavishly. |  She lived opulently in a grand mansion. 她在一座宏伟的宅邸里过着奢华的生活。

opulent:/ˈɒpjʊlənt/ | adj. | 富裕的，豪华的，丰富的 |  Characterized by rich abundance verging on ostentation; lavish. | The opulent furnishings of the hotel impressed all the guests.  酒店豪华的陈设给所有客人留下了深刻的印象。

The dessert was an opulent creation of chocolate and cream. 这道甜点是用巧克力和奶油制成的奢华之作。| 近义词: rich, luxurious, lavish | 反义词: poor, simple, austere | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *opulentus*, from *ops* "power, resources, wealth".
