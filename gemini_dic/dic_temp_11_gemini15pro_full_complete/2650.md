adorant:/əˈdɔːrənt/ |n.| 崇拜者；敬拜对象；被崇拜物 |A person who adores someone or something; the object of adoration. |The singer's adoring fans were true adorants, showering her with gifts and praise. 这位歌手的崇拜歌迷是真正的崇拜者，他们送给她大量的礼物和赞美。|Synonyms: worshipper, devotee, admirer |Antonyms: critic, detractor |noun

adorant:/əˈdɔːrənt/ |adj.| 崇拜的，敬慕的 (less common usage) |Characterized by adoration. |She cast an adorant gaze at the famous actor. 她向这位著名演员投以崇拜的目光。 |Synonyms: adoring, worshipping, reverent |Antonyms: critical, scornful |adjective



ETYMOLOGY->

From Late Latin *adorantem*, present participle of adorare ("to adore"), from ad- ("to") + orare ("to pray, speak, plead").
