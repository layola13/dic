arrestment:/əˈrɛstmənt/| n.|扣押，扣留；(尤指苏格兰法律)扣押债务人财产|The arrestment of his wages left him with little to live on.  他的工资被扣押，几乎没有生活费了。|Synonyms: seizure, confiscation, attachment, sequestration|Antonyms: release, liberation, freeing|noun

arrestment:/əˈrɛstmənt/| n.|停止，阻止|The sudden arrestment of the project caused a lot of confusion.  该项目的突然停止引起了很多混乱。|Synonyms: cessation, halting, stoppage, interruption|Antonyms: continuation, resumption, progression|noun
