hit-the-books:/hɪt ðə bʊks/ | idiom | 开始认真学习；用功读书 | To begin studying hard; to concentrate on one's studies. |  Example: Finals are next week, so I need to hit the books.  期末考试下周就要到了，所以我得开始认真学习了。

Example: I've been putting off studying all semester, but now it's time to hit the books. 我整个学期都在拖延学习，但现在是时候开始认真学习了。 | synonyms: study, cram, bone up, swot (British) | antonyms: procrastinate, slack off | idiom
