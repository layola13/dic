now:/naʊ/
adverb|现在，目前|at the present time or moment|I need it now. 我现在就需要它。|currently, presently|later, formerly
adverb|立刻，马上|immediately; at once|Do it now! 马上做！|immediately, instantly|later, afterwards
noun|现在，此刻|the present time or moment|Now is the time to act. 现在是采取行动的时候了。|present, moment|past, future
词性: adverb, noun

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I live here now. = 我现在住在这里。
She works at the library now. = 她现在在图书馆工作。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am working on it now. = 我现在正在处理它。
They are playing football now. = 他们现在正在踢足球。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have finished the work now. = 我现在已经完成了工作。
She has just arrived now. = 她现在刚到。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been waiting here for an hour now. = 我现在已经在这里等了一个小时了。
They have been playing games all day now. = 他们现在已经玩了一整天游戏了。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He was here just now. = 他刚才还在这里。
I saw him just now. = 我刚才看到他了。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was sleeping now when you called. = 你打电话的时候我正在睡觉。
They were eating dinner now when the doorbell rang. = 门铃响的时候他们正在吃晚饭。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had already finished the work by now. = 到那时我已经完成了工作。
She had already left by now. = 到那时她已经离开了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been working on the project for a month by now. = 到那时我已经在这个项目上工作了一个月了。
They had been traveling for a year by now. = 到那时他们已经旅行了一年了。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will be there by now. = 到那时我会在那里。
She will have finished the work by now. = 到那时她将完成工作。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be working on the report by now. = 到那时我将正在撰写报告。
They will be traveling to Europe by now. = 到那时他们将正在前往欧洲旅行。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I will have finished the report by now. = 到那时我将已经完成了报告。
They will have arrived in Europe by now. = 到那时他们将已经到达了欧洲。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
