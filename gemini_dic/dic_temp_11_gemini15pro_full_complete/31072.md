chamois:/ˈʃæmi/ | n. | 羚羊；麂皮 | A type of goat-antelope native to mountains in Europe, having upright horns hooked at the tips. Also refers to a soft, pliable leather traditionally made from its skin, now often from sheepskin or other sources. | The chamois nimbly scaled the steep cliff.  羚羊敏捷地爬上了陡峭的悬崖。 | synonyms: antelope, goat, suede, leather | antonyms: (No direct antonym for animal)  | noun
