branchy:/ˈbrɑːntʃi/
adjective
多枝的，分叉多的
having many branches
The branchy tree provided ample shade.  这棵枝繁叶茂的树提供了充足的阴凉。
The deer hid amongst the branchy undergrowth. 鹿藏在枝繁叶茂的灌木丛中。
synonyms: branched,  ramified,  spreading, bushy
antonyms: sparse, bare, unbranched
词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From branch + -y.
