cavalierness:/ˌkævəˈlɪrnəs/ | n. | 骑士精神；漫不经心；傲慢 |  A haughty disregard of others; offhand and disdainful dismissal of important matters. | He treated the serious matter with cavalierness, which angered everyone present. 他漫不经心地对待这件严肃的事情，激怒了在场的所有人。 | Synonyms: arrogance, disdain, haughtiness, indifference, insouciance | Antonyms: care, concern, humility, respect, solicitude | noun
