balladlike:/ˈbælədˌlaɪk/| adj.|像民谣的，民谣风格的|Resembling or characteristic of a ballad, typically in being simple, narrative, and often sentimental.  It describes something that has the qualities of a ballad, such as a simple narrative structure, a focus on romantic or tragic themes, and a lyrical or musical quality.|Her voice was soft and balladlike as she sang the ancient folk song. 她唱着古老的民歌，声音轻柔，宛如民谣。|synonyms: lyrical, narrative, romantic, sentimental, folksy|antonyms: complex, modern, sophisticated|adjective
