know-how:/ˈnoʊ haʊ/| n. |专业技能；知识；诀窍| practical knowledge or skill, especially in a particular area|  Example: She has the know-how to run a successful business. 她拥有成功经营企业的专业技能。

Example:  He lacks the technical know-how to fix the computer. 他缺乏修理这台电脑的技术知识。

|近义词：expertise, skill, knowledge, proficiency|反义词: ignorance, inexperience|词性:名词
