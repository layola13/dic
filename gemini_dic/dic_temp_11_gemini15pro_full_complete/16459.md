barenesses: /ˈbɛərnɪsɪz/
n.
裸露，空旷，贫瘠
The state or quality of being bare; nakedness; emptiness; lack of covering or adornment.
The barenesses of the landscape were striking. 这片景色的荒凉令人震惊。

The barenesses of the trees in winter. 冬天树木的光秃秃。

Near synonyms: nudity, nakedness, emptiness, barrenness, destitution

Near antonyms: fullness, abundance, richness, lushness, opulence
词性: 名词
