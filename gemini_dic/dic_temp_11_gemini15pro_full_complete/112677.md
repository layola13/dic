low-grade:/ˈloʊɡreɪd/| adj.|低等级的，低劣的，低度的|Of inferior quality; of a lower grade.  Relating to a condition that is not severe or serious. |The tumor was diagnosed as low-grade.  这个肿瘤被诊断为低度恶性。|Synonyms: inferior, substandard, second-rate, mild, slight |Antonyms: high-grade, superior, premium, severe, serious|adjective
