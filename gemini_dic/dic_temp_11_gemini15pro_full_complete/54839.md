displaceability:/dɪˌspleɪsəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可移动性；可取代性；置换性 | The quality of being able to be displaced or moved from one's home or land.  The ability of something to be replaced by another. |  The displaceability of the refugees made it difficult to provide aid.  难民的可移动性使得提供援助变得困难。 The displaceability of unskilled labor in this industry is high.  这个行业中非熟练劳动力的可取代性很高。 | synonyms: mobility, portability, replaceability, substitutability | antonyms: immobility, fixity, irreplaceability | noun
