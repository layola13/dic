outward:/ˈaʊtwərd/| adj. adv.|向外的，外表的；表面上的|Relating to the outside or exterior; apparent but not necessarily real.|例句：The outward appearance of the building was deceiving. 这栋建筑的外观具有欺骗性。|近义词：external, outer, superficial|反义词:inward, internal|词性:形容词/副词
out-ward:/ˈaʊtwərd/| adv.|向外地|Moving or directed towards the outside.|例句：The door opened outward. 门向外开。|近义词：outwards|反义词:inward(s)|词性:副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ūtweard (unattested) meaning "outward," from *ūt "out" + -weard "-ward."  Cognate with Old Frisian ūtwird, Old Saxon ūtward, Old High German ūzwart, Old Norse útarr.
