kitchenettes:/ˌkɪtʃɪˈnɛts/
n.
小厨房，小厨房设备
A small kitchen or an area equipped with basic cooking facilities, typically in a studio apartment or hotel room.
The kitchenette includes a mini-fridge, microwave, and sink.  这小厨房配有迷你冰箱、微波炉和水槽。
The hotel room had a small kitchenette so we could prepare simple meals.  酒店房间有一个小厨房，所以我们可以准备简单的饭菜。
近义词:mini-kitchen, compact kitchen
反义词:full kitchen, large kitchen
词性:noun

kitchenette:/ˌkɪtʃɪˈnɛts/
词源: From kitchen + -ette (a suffix forming diminutives).
用法说明:  Kitchenette is used to describe a small, compact kitchen area, often found in studio apartments, efficiency apartments, hotel rooms, or extended-stay accommodations. It typically includes basic cooking facilities like a small stovetop, a mini-fridge, a microwave, and a sink. It may or may not include an oven.
