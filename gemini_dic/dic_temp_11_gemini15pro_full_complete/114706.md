malleable:/ˈmæliəbl/ (US), /ˈmæliəbəl/ (UK)| adj.| 可塑的，易成形的；易受影响的| Able to be hammered or pressed permanently out of shape without breaking or cracking; easily influenced, pliable.|  例句：Gold is a malleable metal, easily shaped into intricate jewelry.  黄金是一种可塑性金属，很容易制成复杂的珠宝。| 近义词：pliable, ductile, adaptable, flexible, supple| 反义词：inflexible, rigid, unyielding, stubborn, intractable| 词性: 形容词

malleable:/ˈmæliəbl/ (US), /ˈmæliəbəl/ (UK)| 词源:  From Late Latin malleabilis, from Latin malleus (“hammer”) + -abilis (suffix forming adjectives meaning ‘able to be’, ‘capable of being’, ‘liable or prone to’).  |  词性: 形容词
