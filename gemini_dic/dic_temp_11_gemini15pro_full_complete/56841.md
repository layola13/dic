dorrfly:/ˈdɔːrflaɪ/| n. |豆娘|A dragonfly-like insect of the suborder Zygoptera, characterized by a slender body, intricately veined wings that are held together above the body when at rest, and aquatic larvae.|I saw a beautiful blue dorrfly flitting around the pond.|近义词：damselfly|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From *dorre (dull, lusterless) + fly.  Relates to the generally less brightly colored appearance compared to dragonflies.
