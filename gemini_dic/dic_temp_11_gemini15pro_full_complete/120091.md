mime:/maɪm/ | n. v.| 小丑，哑剧演员；模仿；模拟 | A performer who portrays characters and situations using only gesture and movement, without speaking.  |  The mime entertained the children with his silent performance.  这个哑剧演员用他的无声表演逗乐了孩子们。 | synonym:  pantomime, impersonator, mimic | antonym: speaker, orator | noun

mime:/maɪm/ | v.| 模仿；模拟 | To use gestures and movement to portray a character or situation without speaking. |  He mimed eating a banana. 他模仿吃香蕉的动作。 | synonym: imitate, impersonate, mimic | antonym:  speak, verbalize | verb
