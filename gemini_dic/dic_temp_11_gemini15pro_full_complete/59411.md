earthenware:/ˈɜːrθənˌwɛr/ | n. |陶器；土器|Dishes or other items made of baked clay.  It is more porous and less durable than stoneware or porcelain.|The museum displayed a collection of ancient earthenware.  博物馆展出了一批古代陶器。|近义词：pottery, ceramics, crockery |反义词: porcelain, stoneware |名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *erthenware*, equivalent to *earthen* + *ware*.
