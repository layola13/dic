attainer:/əˈteɪnər/
n.
获得者;达到者;成就者
Someone who attains something, especially a high level of skill or knowledge.
The attainers of high office are often those who are willing to sacrifice everything for their ambition.  那些获得高位的人往往是那些愿意为自己的野心牺牲一切的人。
He is an attainer of great knowledge in his field. 他是该领域知识渊博的人。
achiever, winner, accomplisher
loser, failure
noun
