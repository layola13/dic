oversensitizes:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsɪˌtaɪzɪz/
verb
使過度敏感；使過於敏感
makes someone or something too sensitive or reactive to something.
The media coverage has oversensitized the public to the issue. 媒体的报道使公众对这个问题过于敏感。
近义词:exaggerates, inflames
反义词:desensitizes, numbs
词性:v.


oversensitive:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsɪtɪv/
adjective
過度敏感的；過於敏感的
too easily upset or offended.
He's oversensitive to criticism. 他对批评过于敏感。
近义词:touchy, thin-skinned
反义词:insensitive, thick-skinned
词性:adj.


oversensitivity:/ˌoʊvərˌsɛnsɪˈtɪvɪti/
noun
過度敏感；過於敏感
the state of being too easily upset or offended.
Her oversensitivity made it difficult to have a normal conversation. 她过于敏感，难以进行正常的对话。
近义词:touchiness, irritability
反义词:insensitivity, indifference
词性:n.



词源:
From over- + sensitize.  "Sensitize" comes from the Late Latin  "sensitivus", meaning "capable of sensation."  The prefix "over-" signifies an excess or too much.
