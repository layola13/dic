nonmythical:/ˌnɒnˈmɪθɪkəl/
adjective
非神话的；真实的
Not mythical; real or factual.
The Loch Ness Monster is considered by many to be nonmythical. 很多人认为尼斯湖水怪是真实存在的。
The story is based on nonmythical events. 这个故事是基于真实事件的。
近义词:real, factual, actual, true, authentic, genuine
反义词:mythical, legendary, fictitious, imaginary, fabricated
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + mythical.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "mythical" refers to something related to or based on myths.
