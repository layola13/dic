bridgelike:/ˈbrɪdʒˌlaɪk/
adjective
像桥一样的，桥形的
Resembling a bridge in form or function.
The rock formation was bridgelike, spanning the narrow canyon.  这块岩层像桥一样，横跨狭窄的峡谷。
The new building has a bridgelike structure connecting the two towers. 这座新建筑有一个连接两座塔楼的桥状结构。
近义词: archlike, arched
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider describing the opposite shape, e.g., flat, solid)
词性: adjective
