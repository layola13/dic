Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non < PIE *ne "not"
engineering: from engineer + -ing (suffix denoting an activity or result)
engineer: from Old French engigneor "one who constructs military engines," from engin "skill, cleverness; war machine," from Latin ingenium "natural talent, inborn quality, mental power, intelligence."
