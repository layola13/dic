elude:/ɪˈluːd/ | v. | 逃避，躲避 | To escape the grasp or understanding of something or someone.  | The answer eludes me.  答案我想不出来。| Synonyms: evade, avoid, escape, dodge | Antonyms: confront, face, meet, encounter | verb
