devolver:/dəˈvɒlvər/ /dɪˈvɑːlvər/| v. |归还，交还（权力、责任等）；移交；退回；下放|To return (something, especially power or responsibility) to a lower level or to a different place. |例句：The new law devolves power to local authorities. 新法律将权力下放给地方当局。

例句：The manager devolved responsibility for the project to his assistant. 经理将项目的责任移交给了他的助手。

近义词：return, restore, transfer, delegate|反义词：centralize, retain, keep|词性：动词

devolve:/dəˈvɒlvər/ /dɪˈvɑːlvər/| v. |（指职责、权力或财产）转让，移交；归还，交还（尤指权力或责任）| (of duties, powers, or property) be passed to a lower level or to a different place.|例句：The estate devolved to his eldest son. 遗产转让给了他的长子。

例句：Responsibility for education has devolved from central government to local authorities. 教育责任已由中央政府下放给地方当局。

近义词：pass, transfer, devolve upon|反义词: - |词性：动词
