dissuasion:/dɪˈsweɪʒən/
n.
劝阻，阻止
The act of persuading someone not to do something.
His friends’ dissuasion from joining the army was unsuccessful. 他的朋友劝他不要参军，但没有成功。

近义词: discouragement, deterrence, admonition
反义词: persuasion, encouragement, inducement
词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Latin dissuasio, from dissuadere "to advise against," from dis- "apart, away" + suadere "to urge, advise."
