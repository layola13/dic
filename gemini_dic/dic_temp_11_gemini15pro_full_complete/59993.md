editor:/ˈedɪtər/ (US), /ˈedɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|编辑；编者|A person who is in charge of and determines the final content of a text, particularly a newspaper, magazine, or book.|The editor rejected my manuscript. 编辑拒绝了我的稿件。|近义词: reviser, compiler, proofreader|反义词: author, writer|noun
