not-on-your-life:/nɒt ɒn jɔː(r) laɪf/
idiom
绝对不; 当然不; 没门儿
An emphatic way of saying "no."  It expresses strong disagreement or refusal.
"Can I borrow your car?" "Not on your life!"  “我能借你的车吗？”“绝对不行！”
Synonyms: absolutely not, no way, never, under no circumstances, fat chance
Antonyms: by all means, certainly, of course
词性: idiom (习语)
