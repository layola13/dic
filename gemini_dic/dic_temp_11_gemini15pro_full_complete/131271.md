noncausation:/ˌnɒn.kɔːˈzeɪ.ʃən/ | n. | 非因果关系 | The absence of a causal relationship between two events or variables; the state of not being caused by something. |  The study demonstrated the noncausation between vaccination and autism. 这项研究证明了疫苗接种和自闭症之间不存在因果关系。 | non-causality | causation, causality | noun
