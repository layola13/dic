noncorporealities:/ˌnɒnkɔːrpəˈriːælɪtiz/
|n.|非物质性；无形体；精神性|The state or quality of not having a physical body; immateriality; spirituality.|He pondered the noncorporealities of the soul after death. 他在思考死后灵魂的非物质性。|
|近义词:immaterialities, intangibles, spiritualities|
|反义词:corporealities, physicalities, materialities|
|词性:noun (plural)|


noncorporeality:/ˌnɒnkɔːrpəˈriːælɪti/
|n.|非物质性；无形体；精神性|The state or quality of not having a physical body; immateriality; spirituality.|The noncorporeality of ghosts is often debated. 鬼魂的非物质性经常被争论。|
|近义词:immateriality, intangibility, spirituality|
|反义词:corporeality, physicality, materiality|
|词性:noun|


词源:
From non- + corporeality.  "Corporeality" comes from Late Latin *corporalitas, from Latin corporalis ("bodily"), from corpus ("body").
