outearns:/ˌaʊtˈɜːrnz/
verb
比...挣得多；收入超过
to earn more money than someone else
例句：She now outearns her husband. 她现在的收入比她丈夫高。

例句：He outearns all his siblings. 他的收入超过了他所有的兄弟姐妹。

近义词：makes more than, earns more than
反义词：earns less than

词性:v.
