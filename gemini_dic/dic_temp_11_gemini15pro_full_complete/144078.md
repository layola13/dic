outruns:/ˌaʊtˈrʌnz/| v. |超过，胜过|to run faster than someone or something else|例句：The cheetah outruns its prey. 猎豹跑得比它的猎物快。

例句：His ambition outruns his talent. 他的野心超过了他的才能。

|近义词：outpaces, surpasses, exceeds, overtakes|反义词: lags behind, trails|词性:动词
