incaved:/ɪnˈkeɪvd/
adjective
凹陷的，内陷的
curved or hollowed inwards
The incaved surface of the bowl reflected the light. 碗的凹陷表面反射着光。
近义词:concave, hollowed, indented
反义词:convex, protruding, bulging
词性:adjective

词源: From "in-" (meaning "in, into, within") + "cave" (meaning a hollow place in the ground).  Essentially means "made into a cave" or "cave-like."
