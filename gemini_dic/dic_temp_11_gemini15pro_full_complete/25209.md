bude:/bʌd/| n.|朋友；伙伴；哥们儿|A friend, especially a male friend.|Example: He's my best bude. 他是我最好的哥们儿。|Example: We've been budes since high school. 我们从高中起就是哥们儿了。|近义词：pal, mate, chum, friend, buddy|反义词:enemy, foe|词性:名词

bude:/bʌd/| v.|(使)发芽；萌芽|To begin to grow or develop.|Example: The trees are budding early this year. 今年树木发芽很早。|Example:  New ideas are budding in the field of artificial intelligence. 人工智能领域正在萌生新的想法。|近义词：sprout, germinate, develop|反义词:wither, die|词性:动词
