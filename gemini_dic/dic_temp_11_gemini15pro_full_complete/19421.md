bickerer: /ˈbɪkərər/ | n. | 争吵者，爱吵架的人 | A person who bickers; someone who argues about petty and trivial matters. |  The two children were constant bickerers, always squabbling over toys. 这两个孩子总是争吵不休，总是为了玩具而争吵。

|  He's such a bickerer that no one wants to work with him. 他真是个爱吵架的人，以至于没有人愿意和他一起工作。 |  arguer, squabbler, quarreler | peacemaker, mediator | noun
