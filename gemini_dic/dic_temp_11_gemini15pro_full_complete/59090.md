dyeabilities:/ˌdaɪəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |染色性能；可染性|The dyeabilities of different fabrics vary greatly. 不同织物的可染性差异很大。|例句：The dyeabilities of natural fibers like cotton are generally good.  天然纤维如棉花的可染性通常很好。

例句：Synthetic fibers often have poor dyeabilities compared to natural fibers. 合成纤维的可染性通常比天然纤维差。|近义词：dye uptake, colorfastness properties|反义词: resistance to dyeing|词性: noun
