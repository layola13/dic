affirmed:/əˈfɜːrmd/
verb
确认；证实；断言；申明
state emphatically or publicly that something is true or that one believes it to be true.
The court affirmed the lower court's decision. 法院维持了地方法院的判决。
affirm, assert, declare, maintain, uphold
deny, contradict, refute
v.

affirmed:/əˈfɜːrmd/
adjective
已确认的; 已批准的
Having been declared to be true or valid.
The treaty was affirmed by all member states.  该条约得到了所有成员国的批准。
confirmed, validated, ratified
denied, rejected, invalidated
adj.
