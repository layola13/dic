enunciation:/ɪˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃn/ | n. | 发音；清晰度；宣布；声明 |  The act of pronouncing words or parts of words clearly and distinctly.  The clear and accurate pronunciation of words. A formal announcement or declaration. |  Her enunciation was so clear that everyone in the large hall could understand her. 她的发音如此清晰，以至于大厅里的每个人都能听懂她的话。| synonyms: articulation, pronunciation, diction, declaration, proclamation | antonyms: mumbling, slurring, inarticulateness | noun
