ballisticses: /bəˈlɪstɪksɪz/
n.
弹道学(的多个分支或多个研究领域)
The study of the motion of projectiles, especially bullets, shells, rockets, or the like; the science or art of designing projectiles for maximum flight performance.  Specifically referring to multiple areas or branches of ballistics.
The conference covered various ballisticses, including internal, external, and terminal ballistics.  这个会议涵盖了各种弹道学，包括内部弹道学、外部弹道学和终端弹道学。
近义词: ballistics
反义词:  N/A
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *ballistica (ars) "art of throwing," from Latin ballista (see ballista). The plural form "ballisticses" is rare and often considered non-standard. It implies multiple branches or areas of study within the broader field of ballistics.
