mightful:/ˈmaɪtfəl/| adj.|强大的，有力的|Possessing great power or strength; mighty.  This word is archaic and rarely used in modern English.|例句：The mightful king ruled his land with an iron fist. 强大的国王用铁腕统治着他的土地。|例句：The mightful warrior vanquished his foes.  强大的战士击败了他的敌人。|近义词：powerful, strong, potent, mighty|反义词：weak, powerless, feeble|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *mightful, equivalent to might +‎ -ful.  Cognate with Dutch magtig (“mighty, powerful”) and German mächtig (“mighty, powerful, potent”).
