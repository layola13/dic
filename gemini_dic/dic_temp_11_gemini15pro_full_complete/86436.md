heelpost:/ˈhiːlpoʊst/| n.|鞋跟内柱，后跟内撑|A reinforcing strip of leather or other material placed inside the back of a shoe or boot, between the lining and the outer material, to stiffen the heel area and prevent it from collapsing.|The heelpost of my new boots provides excellent support.|近义词：heel counter, stiffener|反义词:|名词
