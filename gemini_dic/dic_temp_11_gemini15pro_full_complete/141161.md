epiphytic:/ˌɛpɪˈfɪtɪk/
adjective
附生的
(of a plant) growing on another plant but not parasitic.
例句：Many orchids are epiphytic, attaching themselves to trees for support. 许多兰花是附生的，附着在树上以获得支撑。

例句：The bromeliad is an epiphytic plant that collects rainwater in its leaves.  凤梨科植物是一种附生植物，它在叶子里收集雨水。

近义词：airborne, aerial
反义词：terrestrial, rooted
词性：adjective
