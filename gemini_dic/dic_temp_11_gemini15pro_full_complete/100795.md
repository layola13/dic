intricately:/ˈɪntrɪkɪtli/| adv.|错综复杂地，精细地|In a complicated or detailed manner; in a way that has many closely connected parts.|The plot of the novel is intricately woven. 这部小说的情节错综复杂。|近义词：complexly, elaborately|反义词：simply, crudely|词性:副词
Etymology: From Latin *intrīcātus*, past participle of *intrīcāre* ("to entangle, perplex"), from *in-* ("in") + *trīcae* ("perplexities, hindrances, toys, tricks").
