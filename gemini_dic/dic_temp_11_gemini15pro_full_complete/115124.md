mangled:/ˈmæŋɡəld/ | adj. | 损坏的，破损的，支离破碎的 | damaged severely, especially by cutting, tearing, or crushing |  The car was mangled in the accident.  这辆车在事故中被撞毁了。 | synonyms:  damaged, broken, ruined, destroyed, crushed, mutilated | antonyms: intact, undamaged, pristine, perfect | adjective


mangled:/ˈmæŋɡəld/ | v. | 损坏，弄坏，糟蹋 | to damage something severely, especially by cutting, tearing, or crushing | The dog mangled my favorite toy.  狗把我最喜欢的玩具咬坏了。| synonyms: damage, ruin, spoil, destroy, crush, mutilate | antonyms: repair, fix, mend, restore | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *manglen, from Anglo-Norman mangler, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old French mahaignier (“to maim, wound, injure”).
