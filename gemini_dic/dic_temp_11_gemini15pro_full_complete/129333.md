noiselessnesses:/ˈnɔɪzləsnəsɪz/
noun
无声；寂静
The state of being noiseless; absence of noise.
The noiselessnesses of the forest was calming. 森林的寂静令人平静。
近义词: quiet, silence, stillness, hush
反义词: noise, clamor, din
词性: noun

Etymology:
From noiseless + -ness.  Noiseless from noise + -less. Noise from Old French noise, noyse (“quarrel, disturbance, uproar”)  Ultimately from Latin nausea (“seasickness”).
