lichenin:/ˈlaɪkənɪn/ | n. | 地衣淀粉，地衣多糖 | A complex carbohydrate found in certain lichens, especially Iceland moss. |  Lichenin is a form of starch. 地衣淀粉是一种淀粉。| Lichenin can be hydrolyzed to glucose. 地衣淀粉可以水解成葡萄糖。 | synonyms: moss starch | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific chemical compound) | noun
