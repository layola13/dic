orillon:/ɔːˈrɪljən/ |ɔːˈrɪljən/
n. |耳廓; (尤指古堡城墙转角处的)圆形突出部|A small rounded projection, especially at the corner of a bastion or fortification. |The orillons provided flanking fire for the defenders.  耳廓为防御者提供了侧翼火力。|Synonyms: bastion, projection, outwork|Antonyms:  recess, indentation|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *orillon*, diminutive of *oreille* (ear), from Latin *auricula* (external ear).
