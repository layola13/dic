bespawl: /bɪˈspɔːl/
verb
弄脏，玷污
to make dirty, especially with something sticky or messy.
The child bespawled his new shirt with ice cream.  这孩子把冰淇淋弄脏了他的新衬衫。
The mud bespawled the hiker's boots. 泥巴弄脏了徒步旅行者的靴子。
Synonym: soil, stain, smear, dirty
Antonym: clean, cleanse, purify
词性: 动词
