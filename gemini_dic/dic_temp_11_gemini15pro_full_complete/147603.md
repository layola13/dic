overplausibly:/ˌoʊvərˈplɔːzɪbli/
adjective
过于似是而非地，过于貌似有理地
appearing or presented as more reasonable or believable than is actually the case, often to a suspicious degree.
His explanation for his absence was overplausibly detailed, making me wonder if he was lying. 他对缺席的解释过于详尽，让我怀疑他是否在撒谎。

近义词:  implausibly, unconvincingly, suspiciously, questionably
反义词: convincingly, credibly, believably, plausibly
词性: adjective
