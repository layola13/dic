acronycally:/əˈkrɒnɪkəli/ | adv. | 落日时分地；在太阳落山时；日落后不久 | At or just after sunset; at nightfall. Relating to the setting of stars at sunrise.  | The star Sirius rises acronycally in late summer. 天狼星在夏末日落后升起。 | 近义词：at sunset, at nightfall | 反义词: at sunrise, at dawn | 词性: 副词

acronycally:/əˈkrɒnɪkli/ | adv. | （星辰）偕日落而隐没地 |  Relating to the setting of stars at sunrise. | Certain stars disappear acronycally during specific times of the year.  某些星星在一年中的特定时间偕日落而隐没。 | 近义词：cosmically | 反义词: heliacally | 词性: 副词
