ascribes:/əˈskraɪbz/
verb
归因于，归咎于
attributes something to (a cause).
He ascribed his success to hard work. 他把他的成功归因于努力工作。
Synonyms: attribute, impute, credit, assign
Antonyms: deny, refuse, reject
词性: 动词
