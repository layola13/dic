overproduced:/ˌoʊvərprəˈdjuːst/ /ˌoʊvərprəˈduːsd/| v. |生产过剩，过度生产| to produce more of something than is needed or wanted | The factory overproduced goods and now they have a surplus. 工厂生产过剩，现在他们有剩余库存。| The farmer overproduced milk and had to sell it at a loss.  农民过度生产牛奶，不得不亏本出售。|近义词：overmanufacture, oversupply|反义词: underproduce|词性:动词

overproduced:/ˌoʊvərprəˈdjuːst/ /ˌoʊvərprəˈduːsd/| adj. |生产过剩的，过度生产的| produced in excess of what is needed or wanted | The market is flooded with overproduced goods. 市场充斥着过剩生产的商品。| The overproduced crops caused prices to plummet. 过度生产的农作物导致价格暴跌。|近义词：excess, surplus|反义词: scarce|词性:形容词
