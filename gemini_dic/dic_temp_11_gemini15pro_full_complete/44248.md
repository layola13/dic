croesus: /ˈkriːsəs/ | n. | 克罗伊索斯（古代吕底亚国王，以富有闻名）; 巨富; 财主 | A person of great wealth.  Derived from Croesus, the last king of Lydia, renowned for his vast riches. | Example: Bill Gates is considered a modern-day Croesus. 比尔·盖茨被认为是现代的克罗伊索斯。 | Example: He lives like a Croesus, with multiple mansions and a fleet of luxury cars. 他像克罗伊索斯一样生活，拥有多处豪宅和一队豪华轿车。 | Synonym: tycoon, millionaire, billionaire, plutocrat | Antonym: pauper | Noun
