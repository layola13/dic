legitimately:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmətli/| adv.|合法地，正当地，名正言顺地|in a way that is allowed and acceptable according to the law|例句：He acquired the property legitimately. 他合法地获得了这处房产。|例句：The business was legitimately run. 这家企业是合法经营的。|近义词：lawfully, legally, validly, justifiably|反义词：illegally, illicitly, unlawfully|词性:副词

legitimate:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmət/| adj.|合法的，正当的，合理的|conforming to the law or to rules|例句：Is his business legitimate? 他的生意合法吗？|例句：Her complaints were quite legitimate. 她的抱怨是完全合理的。|近义词：legal, lawful, valid, justifiable|反义词：illegal, illegitimate, unlawful|词性:形容词
