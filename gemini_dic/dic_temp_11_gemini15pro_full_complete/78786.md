gilling:/ˈɡɪlɪŋ/ | n. | 刺杀；鳃 | The act of killing, especially by piercing with a sharp instrument, often in the context of fish being caught and killed on fishing gear. Also refers to the gills of a fish. |  The fisherman described the gilling of the salmon. 渔夫描述了鲑鱼被刺杀的过程。  The gilling of the fish was quick and humane.  这条鱼被快速且人道地刺杀了。 | 近义词：killing, slaying, slaughter | 反义词：saving, rescuing | 词性: Noun

gilling:/ˈɡɪlɪŋ/ | adj. | (鱼)用鳃呼吸的 | Having or relating to gills. |  Gilling fish extract oxygen from water. 用鳃呼吸的鱼从水中提取氧气。 | 近义词：branchial | 反义词：lunged | 词性: Adjective
