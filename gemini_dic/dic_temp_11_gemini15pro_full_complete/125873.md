muscle-in:/ˈmʌsəl ɪn/| phrasal verb |强行介入，挤入，插手|To force one's way into something, especially a business deal or activity, often unfairly or aggressively.|Example 1: They tried to muscle in on our territory. 他们试图强行进入我们的地盘。

Example 2:  He muscled in on the conversation, interrupting everyone. 他强行插话，打断了所有人。|Synonyms: intrude, barge in, horn in, interfere, meddle|Antonyms: withdraw, retreat, abstain|verb phrase
