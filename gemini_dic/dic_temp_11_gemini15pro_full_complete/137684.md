nonuprightly:/nɒnˈʌpraɪtli/
adverb
不正直地，不诚实地
in a dishonest or unfair way
He acted nonuprightly in his business dealings. 他在商业 dealings 中行事不诚实。
Synonyms: dishonestly, unfairly, corruptly, wickedly, sinfully
Antonyms: honestly, fairly, justly, righteously, virtuously
词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + upright + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not."  "Upright" comes from Old English "upriht," meaning "straight up, erect." "-ly" is a suffix that forms adverbs.  Therefore, "nonuprightly" literally means "not in a straight or honest manner."
