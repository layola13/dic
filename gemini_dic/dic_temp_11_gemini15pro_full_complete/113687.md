mackinaw:/ˈmækɪnɔː/ (US), /ˌmækɪˈnɔː/ (UK)| n. adj.|厚重的羊毛外套；麦基诺岛(密歇根州)的；麦基诺岛的居民；麦基诺船|A heavy, short, double-breasted woolen coat, often plaid;  also, of or relating to Mackinac Island in Michigan, its inhabitants, or a type of boat used there.|He wore a thick mackinaw to protect himself from the cold wind. 他穿了一件厚厚的麦基诺外套来抵御寒风。|近义词：coat, jacket, parka|反义词: N/A|词性:名词，形容词
Etymology: From French Canadian *mackinac, from Ojibwe mikinaak.
