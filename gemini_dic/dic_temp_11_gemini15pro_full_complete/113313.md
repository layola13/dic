lutz:/lʌts/| n.| 卢茨跳 |A jump in figure skating in which the skater takes off from the back outside edge of one skate, makes one and a half rotations in the air, and lands on the back outside edge of the other skate. |  She landed the lutz perfectly. 她完美地完成了卢茨跳。|  axel, flip, loop | - | noun
