analysis: /əˈnæləsɪs/ | n. | 分析; 解析; 分解 | A detailed examination of something, typically as a basis for discussion or interpretation. | The analysis of the data revealed some interesting trends. 数据分析揭示了一些有趣的趋势。| examination, study, investigation | synthesis, whole | noun


analysis: /əˈnæləsiːz/ | v. | 分析; 解析; 分解 | Examine (something) methodically and in detail, typically in order to explain and interpret it. |  We need to analyze the problem carefully before we can find a solution. 在找到解决方案之前，我们需要仔细分析问题。 | examine, study, investigate | synthesize, combine | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin analysis, from Ancient Greek ἀνάλυσις (análysis, “a loosening up, a resolving”), from ἀναλύω (analýō, “I resolve, I loose”), from ἀνά (aná, “up, throughout”) + λύω (lýō, “I loose”).
