inhibiting:/ɪnˈhɪbɪtɪŋ/
| adj. |抑制的，约束的| hindering or preventing an action, process, or feeling | The drug is effective in inhibiting the growth of cancer cells. 这种药物能有效抑制癌细胞的生长。| synonyms: restricting, suppressing, repressing, curbing, hindering, obstructing, impeding | antonyms: encouraging, promoting, stimulating, facilitating | Verb (present participle)

inhibit:/ɪnˈhɪbɪt/
| v. |抑制，约束| to hinder, restrain, or prevent (an action or process) | Fear can inhibit people from expressing their true feelings.  恐惧会阻止人们表达他们的真实感受。| synonyms: restrain, curb, suppress, repress | antonyms: encourage, promote, stimulate | Verb

inhibition:/ˌɪnhɪˈbɪʃən/
| n. |抑制，约束| a feeling that makes one self-conscious and unable to act in a relaxed and natural way |  She overcame her inhibitions and sang karaoke for the first time. 她克服了拘束，第一次唱了卡拉OK。| synonyms: restraint, self-consciousness, reserve, shyness | antonyms: boldness, confidence | Noun
