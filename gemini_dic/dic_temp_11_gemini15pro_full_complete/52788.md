dilettante:/ˌdɪləˈtænti/ /ˌdɪləˈtɑːnti/| n. | 业余爱好者；浅尝辄止的人 | A person who cultivates an area of interest, such as the arts, without real commitment or knowledge. | He's a bit of a dilettante as far as wine is concerned – he knows the labels but not the grapes. 他对葡萄酒只是一知半解——他知道酒的牌子，却不知道葡萄的品种。 |  synonyms: amateur, dabbler, superficial, nonprofessional | antonyms: professional, expert, connoisseur | noun

dilettantes:/ˌdɪləˈtæntiz/ /ˌdɪləˈtɑːntiz/| n. | 业余爱好者；浅尝辄止的人（复数） | Plural form of dilettante.  |  The gallery was filled with dilettantes, more interested in being seen than in the art. 画廊里挤满了附庸风雅的人，他们对被人看到比对艺术更感兴趣。 | synonyms: amateurs, dabblers | antonyms: professionals, experts | noun
