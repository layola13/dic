hypertensions:/ˌhaɪpərˈtɛnʃənz/| n. |高血压（复数形式，医学上通常用单数）|A condition in which the blood pressure in the arteries is abnormally high.|例句：Hypertensions can lead to serious health problems. 高血压会导致严重的健康问题。

例句：The doctor diagnosed him with multiple secondary hypertensions. 医生诊断他患有多种继发性高血压。（此例句较少见，因为医学上通常用单数形式 hypertension）|近义词：high blood pressure|反义词:hypotension|词性:noun
