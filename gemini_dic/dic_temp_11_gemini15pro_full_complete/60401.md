eidolon: /ˈaɪdələn/ (US), /ˈaɪdɒlɒn/ (UK)| n.| 幻影，幽灵，理想中的典范|An eidolon is an apparition, a phantom, or an ideal. It represents an image or representation of something, often idealized or intangible.  |例句：The artist's paintings were filled with eerie eidolons that seemed to float just beyond the realm of reality. 艺术家的画作中充满了怪诞的幻影，似乎漂浮在现实之外。

|例句：He chased his eidolon of perfect happiness, only to find it ever elusive. 他追逐着他完美幸福的幻影，却发现它总是难以捉摸。|近义词：phantom, apparition, ghost, specter, ideal, image|反义词: reality, actuality|词性: noun
