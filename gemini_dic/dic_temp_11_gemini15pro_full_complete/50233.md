dependably:/dɪˈpɛndəbli/| adv.|可靠地，可信赖地|in a way that can be relied on; in a dependable manner|例句：The car has performed dependably for many years. 这辆车多年来一直运行可靠。|近义词：reliably, consistently, trustworthily|反义词：unreliably, erratically, inconsistently|词性:副词
Etymology: From "dependable" + "-ly." "Dependable" comes from "depend" (from Old French "dependre" and Latin "dependere," meaning "to hang down, to be dependent on") + "-able."
