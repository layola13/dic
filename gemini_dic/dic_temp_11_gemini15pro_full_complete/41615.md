corral:/kəˈræl/ | n. vt.|畜栏，围栏；把…赶入畜栏|A circular enclosure for horses or other livestock.  An enclosure formed by wagons for defense in the American West. To gather together and confine. |The cowboys corralled the cattle for branding. 牛仔们把牛赶进畜栏准备烙印。|近义词：pen, enclosure, round up|反义词:release, free|名词,动词
