asset-stripping:/ˈæsɛt ˌstrɪpɪŋ/
n.
资产剥离
The practice of buying a company and then selling off its assets, often at a profit, with little regard for the long-term viability of the business or the interests of its employees.
The company was accused of asset-stripping after it bought a rival and immediately sold off its most valuable properties. 这家公司在收购了一家竞争对手后，立即出售了其最有价值的资产，因此被指控进行资产剥离。
divestment, liquidation
development, investment
noun
