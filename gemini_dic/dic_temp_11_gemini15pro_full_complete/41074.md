copperhead:/ˈkɑːpərˌhed/ (US), /ˈkɒpəhed/ (UK)| n.|铜斑蛇; (美)北方铜头蛇 (一种毒蛇); (美)铜头党人 (美国内战时期主张与南方妥协的北方民主党人)|A venomous snake found in the eastern United States, having a copper-colored head.  Also, a person in the northern US who opposed the Civil War.|例句：The copperhead slithered under a rock.  铜斑蛇滑到岩石下面去了。

例句：He was accused of being a Copperhead during the Civil War.  他被指控在内战期间是铜头党人。|近义词：snake, viper (for the animal); peace Democrat, appeaser (for the political meaning)|反义词：hawk, warmonger (for the political meaning)
词性: 名词

copperhead:/ˈkɑːpərˌhed/ (US), /ˈkɒpəhed/ (UK)| adj.|铜斑蛇的; 铜头党人的|Relating to or characteristic of a copperhead snake or a Copperhead (political faction).|例句：The copperhead venom is potent. 铜斑蛇的毒液很强。

例句：His copperhead sympathies led to his ostracism. 他对铜头党的同情导致他被排斥。|近义词：pacifistic, dovish (for the political meaning)|反义词：hawkish, warmongering (for the political meaning)
词性: 形容词
