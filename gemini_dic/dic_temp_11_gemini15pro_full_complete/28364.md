caravanserai:/ˌkærəˈvænsəˌraɪ/ /ˌkærəˈvænsəˌri/
n.
商队旅馆，大客栈
A large inn or roadside resting place in some Eastern countries with a large central court, used by caravans.
The caravan stopped at a caravanserai for the night. 商队在一家客栈过夜。

近义词: inn, hostel, lodge, hotel
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'home' or 'private residence' could be considered conceptually opposite)
词性: noun
