mock-up:/ˈmɑːkʌp/ (US) /ˈmɒkʌp/ (UK)| n.| 模型，样机，原型 |A full-size model of something, especially a building or a product, that is built to show how the finished product will look. | We built a mock-up of the new website to test the user interface. 我们搭建了一个新网站的模型来测试用户界面。| synonym: prototype, model, sample | antonym: finished product| noun
mock-up:/ˈmɑːkʌp/ (US) /ˈmɒkʌp/ (UK)| v.| 制作模型 |To create a mock-up of something. | The design team mocked up a few different versions of the logo. 设计团队制作了几个不同版本的标志模型。 | synonym: model, prototype, simulate | antonym: finalize, complete| verb
