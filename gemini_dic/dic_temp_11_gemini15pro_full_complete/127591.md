neddy:/ˈnɛdi/| n.|驴；笨蛋，傻瓜|A donkey, especially one used for riding.  It can also be used informally to refer to a foolish or stupid person.|例句：The children enjoyed riding the neddy at the seaside. 孩子们喜欢在海边骑驴。

例句：He's a bit of a neddy sometimes, making silly mistakes. 他有时有点傻，会犯一些愚蠢的错误。|近义词：donkey, ass, fool, idiot|反义词:genius, mastermind|词性:noun
