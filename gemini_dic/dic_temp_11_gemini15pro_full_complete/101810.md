irritable: /ˈɪrɪtəbl/ (US) /ˈɪrɪtəbəl/ (UK)| adj.|易怒的，暴躁的|Easily annoyed or angered; readily excited to impatience or anger.|例句：He was irritable from lack of sleep. 他因为缺乏睡眠而烦躁。|近义词：cranky, grumpy, touchy, testy, irascible|反义词：calm, patient, good-natured|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Late Latin irritabilis, from Latin irritare "to excite, provoke," from in- "into, upon" + ritare "to roar, snarl."
