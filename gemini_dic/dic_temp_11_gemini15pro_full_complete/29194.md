cashlessness:/ˈkæʃləsnəs/
n.
无现金状态; 无现金化
The state or quality of being cashless; a system or society operating without physical currency.
Sweden is moving rapidly towards cashlessness. 瑞典正迅速走向无现金社会。
synonyms: electronic payment, digital payment, online payment, mobile payment
antonyms: cash dependence, cash-based economy
noun
