crucifixes: /ˈkruːsəfɪksɪz/ | n. | 十字架（尤指耶稣受难像）| A representation of a cross with a figure of Jesus Christ crucified on it. |  She wore a small silver crucifix around her neck. 她脖子上戴着一个小的银十字架。

The church was adorned with crucifixes and religious icons. 教堂装饰着十字架和宗教圣像。


|近义词：cross, rood |反义词: None |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *crucifixus, past participle of crucifigere (“to crucify”), from crux (“cross”) + figere (“to fix, fasten”).
