boneheadedness:/ˈboʊnˌhɛdɪdnəs/| n. |愚蠢，傻头傻脑|The state of being boneheaded; stupidity.  A remarkable degree of foolishness or lack of intelligence.|His boneheadedness led him to believe he could fly. 他的愚蠢让他相信自己能飞。|近义词：stupidity, foolishness, idiocy, absurdity|反义词：intelligence, wisdom, cleverness|词性:noun
boneheaded:/ˈboʊnˌhɛdɪd/| adj. |愚蠢的，傻头傻脑的|Stupid; foolish. Characterized by a lack of intelligence or good judgment.|That was a boneheaded move. 那真是一个愚蠢的举动。|近义词：stupid, foolish, idiotic, absurd|反义词：intelligent, wise, clever|词性:adjective
