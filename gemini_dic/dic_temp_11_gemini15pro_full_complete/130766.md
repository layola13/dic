nonanalogous:/ˌnɒnəˈnæləɡəs/
adjective
不同的，非同类的，无相似之处的
Not analogous; dissimilar or incomparable.
The two situations are nonanalogous; you can't compare them. 这两种情况截然不同，你无法比较它们。
The development of the internet is nonanalogous to any other technological advancement in history. 互联网的发展与历史上任何其他技术进步都不同。
synonyms: dissimilar, different, unlike, disparate, incomparable, heterogeneous
antonyms: analogous, similar, alike, comparable, homogeneous
词性: 形容词
