foolscap:/ˈfuːlskæp/
n.
大页纸；傻瓜帽
A large size of writing or printing paper.  Also, a conical pointed cap worn by jesters.
例句：I bought a ream of foolscap for my writing project. 我买了一令大页纸来写我的项目。

例句：The jester wore a brightly colored foolscap. 小丑戴着一顶颜色鲜艳的傻瓜帽。

近义词：paper, stationery (for the paper sense); dunce cap, jester's cap (for the hat sense)
反义词: None (no direct opposite for either sense)
词性: Noun
