cookers:/ˈkʊkərz/ | n. | 炊具；炉灶 |A cooker is a large piece of kitchen equipment that is used for cooking food.  Specifically, it usually refers to a stove or oven, or a combination of both.|  We bought a new cooker for the kitchen. 我们为厨房买了新的炉灶。| synonyms: stove, oven, range, hob | antonyms: refrigerator, freezer | noun

cookers:/ˈkʊkərz/ | n. | 压力锅 |A cooker can also refer to a pressure cooker, a sealed pot for cooking food quickly under steam pressure.| She used a pressure cooker to make the stew. 她用压力锅炖了肉。| synonyms: pressure cooker | antonyms: N/A | noun
