ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *crese(t), diminutive of cres "chalk," from Late Latin creta "chalk, Cretan earth," from Latin Creta "Crete," the island from which chalk was originally exported. The sense evolved from "chalk holder" to "container for burning material."
