lur:/lɜːr/ (rhymes with "purr") | n. | 潜伏，埋伏；隐藏；（网络）潜水； | A state of inactivity or hiddenness, often with the intent to observe or deceive.  Especially used online to describe someone who reads forums or chats without participating. | He was lying in lur, waiting for the perfect moment to strike. 他潜伏着，等待最佳的出击时机。 |  The forum is full of lurs who never post anything. 这个论坛里充满了只看不发的潜水者。 | synonyms: ambush, prowl, skulk, lurk | antonyms:  emerge, appear, participate | noun
