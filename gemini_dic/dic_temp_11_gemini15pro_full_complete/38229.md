computer:/kəmˈpjuːtər/ (UK), /kəmˈpjuːt̬ər/ (US)| n.| 计算机，电脑 |A programmable electronic device designed to accept data, perform prescribed mathematical and logical operations at high speed, and display the results of these operations. |I use my computer for work and entertainment. 我用我的电脑工作和娱乐。| synonyms: PC, laptop, desktop, machine, processor | antonyms: abacus, calculator (in a limited sense) | noun
