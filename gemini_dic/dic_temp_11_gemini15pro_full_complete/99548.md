intergrappling:/ˌɪntərˈɡræplɪŋ/
n.
互相扭打；格斗；搏斗
The act of grappling with each other, typically in a competitive or combative context.
Two fighters locked in fierce intergrappling.  两个战士激烈地扭打在一起。
The intergrappling continued until one of them yielded. 扭打一直持续到其中一人屈服。
近义词：grappling, wrestling, struggling, fighting
反义词：yielding, surrendering, submitting
词性:名词


词源:
"inter-" (prefix): between, among, mutually, reciprocally (来自拉丁语)
"grappling": the act of seizing, holding, or struggling with another person (来自中古英语 grapplen)
