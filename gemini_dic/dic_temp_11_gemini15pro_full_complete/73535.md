foresightful:/ˈfɔːrsaɪtfʊl/| adj.|有远见的；深谋远虑的|Having or showing foresight; able to anticipate future needs or trends.|例句：She made a foresightful decision to invest in renewable energy. 她做出了投资可再生能源的远见卓识的决定。

例句：His foresightful planning saved the company from bankruptcy. 他深谋远虑的计划使公司免于破产。


近义词：prescient, visionary, provident, anticipatory|反义词：shortsighted, myopic, improvident|词性：adjective
