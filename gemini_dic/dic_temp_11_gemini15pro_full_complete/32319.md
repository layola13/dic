chikara:/tʃɪˈkɑːrɑː/| n.|力量，能量，内力 (尤指日本文化中的精神力量或内在能量)|From Japanese 力 (chikara) meaning "power, strength."  It often refers to inner strength, willpower, or spiritual energy, especially in the context of Japanese martial arts or other traditional practices. |例句：He summoned his chikara to overcome the challenge. 他集中内力来克服挑战。

例句：The chikara of the ancient warriors was legendary. 古代武士的力量是传奇般的。|近义词：strength, power, energy, might, force|反义词：weakness, impotence|词性:noun
