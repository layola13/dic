apostolical: /ˌæpəˈstɒlɪkəl/ /ˌɑːpəˈstɑːlɪkəl/ | adj. |使徒的；使徒时代的；宗徒的；教会的|Of or relating to an apostle or apostles, especially the twelve apostles of Jesus Christ.  Pertaining to the doctrines or practices of the apostles. | The apostolical succession is a doctrine held by some churches. 使徒继承制是某些教会坚持的教义。 |  近义词: apostolic, evangelical | 反义词: secular, lay | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *apostolicus, from Greek apostolikos, from apostolos "apostle," from apo- "from" + stellein "to send."
