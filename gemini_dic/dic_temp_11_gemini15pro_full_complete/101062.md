inventible:/ɪnˈvɛntɪbl/ | adj. | 可发明的，可创造的 |  Capable of being invented or created.  Something that is inventible is something that *could* be invented, whether it has been or not. It suggests the potential for invention. |  A cheap, portable water purification system for developing countries is an inventible solution to a major problem.  一种廉价的、便携式的水净化系统对于发展中国家来说是一个可以发明出来的解决主要问题的方法。|  conceivable, imaginable, possible | unimaginable, impossible | adjective
