concremation:/krɛˈmeɪʃən/ |n.|火葬，焚化|The act of reducing a dead body to ashes by burning.|His body was sent for cremation. 他的遗体被送去火葬。|近义词：incineration|反义词：burial|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "cremationem" (nominative "crematio"), noun of action from past-participle stem of "cremare" "to burn," from PIE root "*ker-" (3) "heat, fire."
