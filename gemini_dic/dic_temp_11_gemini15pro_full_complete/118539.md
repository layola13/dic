mesolithic:/ˌmezəˈlɪθɪk/ | adj. n.| 中石器时代的；中石器时代 | Relating to the middle part of the Stone Age, between the Paleolithic and Neolithic periods. |  The Mesolithic period saw the development of microlithic tools. 中石器时代见证了微石器的发展。 | The exhibition showcased Mesolithic artifacts. 展览展示了中石器时代的文物。 | 近义词:Epipaleolithic | 反义词:Paleolithic, Neolithic | 词性:adjective, noun

Etymology:
From French *mésolithique*, from Ancient Greek *μέσος (mésos, “middle”) + λίθος (líthos, “stone”)*.
