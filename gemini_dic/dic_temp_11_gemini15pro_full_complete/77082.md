garreting:/ˈɡærɪtɪŋ/ | n. |  阁楼居住；在阁楼里工作 | The act of living in or working from a garret, a small, usually attic room. | He took up garreting in Paris to pursue his writing career. 他在巴黎租住阁楼，追求他的写作事业。 | Example:  Garreting provided him with the solitude he needed to write his novel.  在阁楼居住为他写作小说提供了所需的独处空间。| Synonym: attic living, loft living | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but consider) luxurious living, spacious living | Noun
