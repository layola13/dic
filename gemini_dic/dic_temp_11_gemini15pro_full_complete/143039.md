outblossoms:/ˌaʊtˈblɑːsəmz/
verb
胜过花期；开花更盛；比...更繁盛
surpasses in blossoming; blooms more profusely than; outdoes in flowering.
The cherry tree outblossoms the plum tree this year. 今年的樱花树比李子树开花更盛。
Synonyms: outflowers, surpasses, exceeds
Antonyms: withers, fades, droops
词性: 动词(verb)


outblossom:/ˌaʊtˈblɑːsəmz/
noun
胜过花期；开花更盛
the act of surpassing in blossoming; blooming more profusely than.
The outblossom of the roses this year is remarkable. 今年玫瑰花的盛开景象非凡。
Synonyms: flourishing, blossoming
Antonyms: withering, fading
词性: 名词(noun)
