amarabid:/ˈæməˌræbɪd/ | adj. | 阿玛拉比德式的；阿玛拉比德王朝的 | Relating to the Amarabid dynasty, which ruled Morocco from the mid-17th century to the early 20th century. |  Example: The Amarabid period saw a flourishing of Moroccan arts and culture. 阿玛拉比德时期见证了摩洛哥艺术和文化的繁荣。| Synonym: Moroccan (in some contexts) | Antonym: (No direct antonym, could be pre- or post-Amarabid) | Adjective
