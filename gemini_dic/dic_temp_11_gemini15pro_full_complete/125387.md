multimotor:/ˈmʌltiˌmoʊtər/| adj. n.|多发动机的，多马达的；多引擎飞机|Having or driven by multiple motors/engines.  A plane with multiple engines.|例句：The multimotor aircraft was able to fly even after one engine failed. 这架多引擎飞机即使一个引擎失效后也能飞行。

例句：Multimotor designs are common in large aircraft for safety and power. 多引擎设计在大型飞机中很常见，是为了安全和动力。|近义词：multi-engine|反义词: single-engine|词性：形容词, 名词
