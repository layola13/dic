blaring: /ˈblɛərɪŋ/ (US), /ˈbleərɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.| 刺耳的，响亮的| making a loud, harsh sound | The music was blaring from the car stereo.  汽车音响里大声播放着音乐。| synonyms: booming, roaring, deafening | antonyms: quiet, soft, muted | adjective

blaring: /ˈblɛərɪŋ/ (US), /ˈbleərɪŋ/ (UK)| n.| 刺耳的声音| a loud, harsh sound | The blaring of the horns woke me up.  喇叭的刺耳声把我吵醒了。| synonyms: blare, roar, din | antonyms: quiet, silence, hush | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English blaren, of uncertain origin, perhaps imitative.
