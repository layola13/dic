concenter:/kənˈsɛntər/ /ˈkɑːnsɛntər/ | v. | 集中；聚集；浓缩 | To direct (one's attention, thoughts, etc.) toward a common center or objective; focus. |  The students concentered their attention on the lecture. 学生们把注意力集中在讲座上。|  He concentered all his efforts on finishing the project. 他集中全部精力完成这个项目。 | synonyms: focus, concentrate, centralize | antonyms: disperse, scatter, diffuse | verb

concenter:/ˈkɑːnsɛntər/ /kənˈsɛntər/ | n. | 中心；集中点 | A center or point of concentration. | The town square is the concenter of community activity. 城镇广场是社区活动的中心。| The enemy's concenter of operations was located near the border. 敌人的行动中心位于边界附近。 | synonyms: center, core, focus | antonyms: periphery, edge, margin | noun
