nonpresentational:/ˌnɑːnˌprɛzənˈteɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 非表象的，非呈现的 | Not focused on outward appearance or presentation; emphasizing substance or deeper meaning over superficial display. |  The artist's nonpresentational style prioritized emotional impact over visual realism.  这位艺术家的非表象风格优先考虑情感冲击而非视觉上的真实感。 |近义词: unostentatious, substantive, profound | 反义词: ostentatious, superficial, presentational | 词性: adjective
