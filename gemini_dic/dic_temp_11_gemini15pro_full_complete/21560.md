blueprinting:/ˈbluːˌprɪntɪŋ/
n.|蓝图绘制，详细计划|The act or process of making blueprints or detailed plans.  The blueprinting phase of the project took longer than expected.|近义词：planning, designing, mapping, outlining|反义词:improvisation, winging it|词性:名词

Etymology: From "blueprint" + "-ing."  "Blueprint" originated in the early 20th century, referring to a photographic print of a technical drawing, typically architectural or engineering, characterized by white lines on a blue background. The term was later extended to refer to any detailed plan or program.
