acrolithan:/ˌækrəˈlɪθən/
n.
acrolithic statue;  a statue with head, hands, and feet of marble and the rest of wood or other material.
镶嵌雕像；头部、手和脚用大理石制成，其余部分用木头或其他材料制成的雕像。
The museum displayed a rare acrolithan from ancient Greece.  博物馆展出了一尊来自古希腊的珍贵的镶嵌雕像。
synonyms: composite statue, part-marble statue
antonyms: monolithic statue (though not a perfect opposite, it emphasizes a single material)
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *akrólithos*, from *ákros* (at the extremity, outermost) + *líthos* (stone).
