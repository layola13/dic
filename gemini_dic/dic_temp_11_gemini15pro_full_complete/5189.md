allhallows:/ɔːlˈhæloʊz/
noun
万圣节前夜; 诸圣节;
All Hallows' Eve, the evening before All Saints Day (November 1st).  It is now usually called Halloween.
例句：The children dressed up as ghosts and goblins for All Hallows. 孩子们打扮成鬼怪来庆祝万圣节前夜。

例句：All Hallows is a time for remembering the dead. 万圣节前夜是缅怀逝者的日子。
近义词：Halloween, All Saints' Eve
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性:noun
