interdome: /ˈɪntərdoʊm/
n.
穹顶之间的空间;  穹顶连接处
The interdome area provided a sheltered walkway between the two main buildings.  穹顶之间的区域提供了一条连接两座主要建筑物的遮蔽走道。
The architect designed an intricate interdome to connect the separate structures. 建筑师设计了一个复杂的穹顶连接处，将独立的结构连接起来。
Example: The interdome housed several small shops and cafes. 穹顶连接处容纳了几家小商店和咖啡馆。
Example:  Birds often nested in the sheltered interdome of the stadium. 鸟类经常在体育场遮蔽的穹顶连接处筑巢。
Synonym:  interspace,  junction, connection
Antonym: exterior, outside

Etymology:  "inter-" (prefix meaning "between") + "dome" (noun)
