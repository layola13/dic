ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *magnifien*, from Old French *magnifier*, from Latin *magnificāre* (“to extol, praise, enlarge”), from *magnus* (“great”) + *-ficāre* (from *facere* (“to make, do”)).  The "-er" suffix denotes an agent or instrument, and the "-'s" is the possessive suffix.
