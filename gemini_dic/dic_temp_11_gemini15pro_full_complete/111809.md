logarithmic:/ˌlɒɡərɪðmɪk/ | adj. | 对数的 | Relating to or expressed in terms of logarithms. | The Richter scale is logarithmic, meaning an earthquake measuring 7 is ten times more powerful than one measuring 6. | 里氏震级是对数的，这意味着7级地震的强度是6级地震的十倍。|  logarithmical, exponential (in some contexts) | linear | adjective

logarithm:/ˈlɒɡərɪðəm/ | n. | 对数 |  A quantity representing the power to which a fixed number (the base) must be raised to produce a given number.  | The logarithm of 100 to the base 10 is 2. |  以10为底100的对数是2。 | exponent, power |  - | noun
