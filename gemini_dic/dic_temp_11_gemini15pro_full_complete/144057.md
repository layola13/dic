outro:/ˈɑʊtroʊ/ (US) /ˈaʊtrəʊ/ (UK)| n.|结束，结尾；（尤指歌曲或广播节目的）尾声，收尾|A concluding section of a piece of music or a radio or television programme. | The DJ played a short outro before signing off.  DJ在结束广播前播放了一段简短的尾声。| synonyms: ending, closing, coda, finale | antonyms: intro, beginning, prelude | noun
