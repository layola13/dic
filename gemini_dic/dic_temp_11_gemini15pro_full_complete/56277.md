doggedly:/ˈdɒɡɪdli/ (US) /ˈdɒɡɪdli/ (UK)
adverb|顽强地，坚持不懈地|With persistent determination; tenaciously. |He doggedly pursued his goal of becoming a doctor. 他顽强地追求着成为医生的目标。|persistently, tenaciously, determinedly, resolutely|halfheartedly, apathetically, indifferently|adverb

dogged:/ˈdɒɡɪd/ (US) /ˈdɒɡɪd/ (UK)
adjective|顽强的，坚持不懈的|Having or showing tenacity and grim persistence.|Her dogged determination helped her overcome many obstacles. 她顽强的决心帮助她克服了许多障碍。|persistent, tenacious, determined, resolute|wavering, hesitant, irresolute|adjective
