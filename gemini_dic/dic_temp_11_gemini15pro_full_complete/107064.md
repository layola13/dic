lacustrine:/ləˈkʌstrɪn/
adjective
湖泊的；湖成的
of, relating to, or formed in a lake; living or growing in lakes.
例句：The scientists studied the lacustrine deposits to learn about the history of the lake. 科学家们研究了湖泊沉积物以了解湖泊的历史。

例句：Lacustrine sediments often contain fossils of freshwater organisms. 湖泊沉积物通常包含淡水生物的化石。

近义词：lake, aquatic, freshwater
反义词：marine, oceanic, terrestrial
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *lacustris*, from *lacus* "lake".
