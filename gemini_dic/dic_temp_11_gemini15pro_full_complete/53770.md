discommend:/ˌdɪskəˈmend/ | vt. | 不推荐；谴责；反对 | To express disapproval of; censure. |  The report discommended the use of excessive force.  这份报告不建议使用过度的武力。

The principal discommended the student for his disruptive behavior. 校长谴责了这名学生的捣乱行为。 | 近义词：deprecate, censure, condemn, criticize | 反义词：recommend, praise, endorse | 词性：动词

词源：Late Middle English: from dis- (expressing reversal) + commend.
