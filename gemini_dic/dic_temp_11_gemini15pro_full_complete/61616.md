emication: /ˌiːmɪˈkeɪʃən/
noun
迁移; 移居; 移民
The act of leaving one's country or region to settle in another.
例句：The emication of skilled workers is a concern for the country.  技术工人的移民是该国家的一个担忧。

例句：The family's emication to Canada was a difficult decision.  举家移民到加拿大是一个艰难的决定。
近义词：migration, emigration, relocation
反义词：immigration, repatriation
词性：n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ēmicātiō, from Latin ēmicāre (“to dart out, spring forth, leap up, bound forth”) (from ex- (“out of, from”) + micāre (“to glitter, shine, flash”) + -ātiō (“-ation”)).
