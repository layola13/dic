first-aider:/ˈfɜːst ˌeɪdər/ (UK) /ˈfɜːrst ˈeɪdər/ (US)| n. |急救员|A person trained to give first aid; someone who is qualified to provide initial medical assistance. |例句：The first-aider quickly assessed the injured cyclist.  急救员迅速评估了受伤的骑车人。

例句：Luckily, there was a first-aider on the scene who knew what to do.  幸运的是，现场有一位急救员知道该怎么做。|近义词：paramedic, EMT, first responder|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "bystander" or "unqualified person")|词性:noun
