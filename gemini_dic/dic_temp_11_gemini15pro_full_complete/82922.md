gumminess:/ˈɡʌminəs/| n.|胶粘性，粘性|The gumminess of the candy made it difficult to chew.  这种糖果的粘性使它很难咀嚼。|Synonyms: stickiness, tackiness, adhesiveness|Antonyms: smoothness, slipperiness|noun

Etymology->
From gummy + -ness.  Gummy itself comes from gum, referring to the sticky resin of various trees. Ultimately from Late Latin *gumma, from Greek kommi.
