nino:/ˈniːnjoʊ/ (US), /ˈniːnjəʊ/ (UK)| n.|男孩，小孩，孩子 (especially a boy)|A Spanish word meaning "boy" or "child," often used as a term of endearment.|例句：The little nino was playing in the park. 小男孩在公园里玩耍。|近义词：boy, child, kid, lad|反义词:girl, adult|noun
