一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she like to do in her free time? 她空闲时间喜欢做什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done to deserve this award? 她做了什么值得这个奖项？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading all afternoon? 他整个下午都在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say to him? 你对他说了什么？
What happened last night? 昨晚发生了什么事？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):

What was she thinking about when I saw her? 我看到她时她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had they eaten for dinner? 他们晚饭吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will happen next? 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):

虚拟语气:
If I knew what to say, I would tell you. 如果我知道该说什么，我会告诉你。
I wish I knew what she was thinking. 我希望我知道她在想什么。

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? 会议上说了什么？
What is being done about the problem? 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. 我不知道该做什么。
She doesn't understand what I'm saying. 她不明白我在说什么。
