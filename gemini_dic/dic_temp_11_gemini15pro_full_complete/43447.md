crappy:/ˈkræpi/| adj.|糟糕的，劣质的|Something that is crappy is of poor quality or very bad.  It is an informal and slightly vulgar word.|The movie was really crappy. 这部电影真的很烂。|Near Synonyms: lousy, awful, terrible, rubbish (UK), trashy, shoddy|Antonyms: excellent, fantastic, wonderful, high-quality|Adjective
