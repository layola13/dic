clipper:/ˈklɪpər/ | n. |剪刀；指甲刀；快速帆船；理发推子|A device used for clipping or trimming something, such as hair, nails, or hedges. Also, a fast sailing ship.|He used clippers to trim his beard. 他用理发推子修剪胡子。|Synonyms: scissors, shears, trimmer, cutter |Antonyms: -- |noun

clipper:/ˈklɪpər/ | n. |快船；高速帆船|A fast sailing ship, especially one built for speed.|The clipper ship raced across the ocean.  快船在海上飞驰。|Synonyms: schooner, yacht (in some contexts) |Antonyms: -- |noun
