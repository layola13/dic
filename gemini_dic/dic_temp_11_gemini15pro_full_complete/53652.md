discharger's:/dɪsˈtʃɑːrdʒərz/
n.|排放者（的）|The discharger's responsibility is to ensure proper waste disposal. 排放者的责任是确保妥善处理废物。|
英文解析:Possessive form of "discharger," referring to something belonging to or associated with a person or entity that discharges something (e.g., liquid, gas, electricity).
例句：The discharger's permit was revoked due to repeated violations. 由于屡次违规，排放者的许可证被吊销了。

例句：The environmental agency is monitoring the discharger's output closely.  环保机构正在密切监测排放者的输出。

近义词:emitter's, releaser's
反义词:receiver's, absorber's
词性:Possessive noun (名词所有格)
词源:From "discharge" + "-er" (agent suffix) + "-'s" (possessive suffix). "Discharge" comes from Old French "deschargier" meaning "to unload."
