bestowing:/bɪˈstoʊɪŋ/
verb
授予，给予
present participle of bestow, meaning to confer or present something, often something valuable or honorary.
The queen is bestowing knighthoods on several distinguished scientists. 女王将授予几位杰出科学家爵位。
He bestowed a generous gift upon the charity. 他向慈善机构赠送了一份厚礼。
synonyms: conferring, granting, presenting, awarding
antonyms: withholding, revoking, denying, taking
