inbreeding's:/ˈɪnbrɪːdɪŋz/
| n. |近亲繁殖的（后果）|The negative effects resulting from inbreeding.  It refers to the deteriorated traits or conditions caused by the mating of closely related individuals.|The inbreeding's effects were evident in the declining health of the livestock.|近义词：inbreeding's consequences, detrimental effects of inbreeding|反义词: outcrossing's benefits, hybrid vigor|词性:noun (genitive form)


inbreeding:/ˈɪnbrɪːdɪŋ/
| n. |近亲繁殖|The breeding of closely related individuals, especially over many generations.|The small island population suffered from inbreeding.|近义词：interbreeding, close breeding|反义词: outcrossing, outbreeding|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- + breeding.  "Breeding" comes from Old English "brēdan" meaning "to cherish, nourish, produce." The prefix "in-" in this context intensifies the meaning, suggesting breeding within a closed group.
