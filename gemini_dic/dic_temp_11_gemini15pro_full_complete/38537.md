conclavist:/kɒnˈkleɪvɪst/ /kɑːnˈkleɪvɪst/| n.|秘密会议参加者；教皇选举会议的参加者|A cardinal participating in a papal conclave is a conclavist.  参与教皇选举会议的红衣主教就是一位秘密会议参加者。| The conclavists were sequestered until a new pope was chosen.  秘密会议参加者们被隔离起来，直到选出新教皇。|近义词：cardinal (in the context of a papal conclave), elector|反义词:|名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *conclavista, from Latin conclave ("room that can be locked") + -ista.
