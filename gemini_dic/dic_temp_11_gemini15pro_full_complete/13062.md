astroite:/ˈæstrɔɪt/| n. |星状陨石，球粒陨石|A stony meteorite containing small spherical bodies called chondrules.  It is the most common type of meteorite.|The museum displayed a large astroite that had fallen in the desert.  博物馆展出了一块落在沙漠里的巨大的球粒陨石。|Near synonyms: chondrite, stony meteorite|Antonym: iron meteorite|noun
