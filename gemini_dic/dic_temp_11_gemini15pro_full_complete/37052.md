comatula:/ˌkɒməˈtjuːlə/ | n. | 海羊齿，羽星 | A feather star, a type of crinoid.  It is a marine invertebrate animal belonging to the class Crinoidea.  Unlike most other crinoids, comatulids are free-swimming as adults and do not have a stalk attaching them to the seabed. |  The comatula gracefully extended its feathery arms.  海羊齿优雅地伸展着它羽毛般的臂膀。 |  The divers observed a colorful comatula on the coral reef. 潜水员在珊瑚礁上观察到一只色彩鲜艳的海羊齿。|  Synonyms: feather star, crinoid | Antonyms: None | Noun
