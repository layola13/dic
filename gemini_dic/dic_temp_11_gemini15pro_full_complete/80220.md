godsends:/ˈɡɒdˌsɛndz/ (US) /ˈɡɒdˌsɛndz/ (UK)
n.|天赐之物，及时雨|Something fortunate and unexpected that comes at just the right time, as if sent by God.|The scholarship was a real godsends, allowing her to finish her studies.|近义词：blessing, boon, windfall, stroke of luck|反义词：bane, curse, misfortune|词性: 名词

词源:
From "God send,"  meaning "may God send," expressing a wish that something helpful would arrive. Over time, the phrase solidified into the noun "godsends."
