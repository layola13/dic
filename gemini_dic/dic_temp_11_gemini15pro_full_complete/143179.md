outcoached: /ˌaʊtˈkoʊtʃt/
verb
以更佳的教练策略胜过；在教练方面胜过
To defeat an opponent by having a superior coaching strategy.
The Golden State Warriors outcoached the Cleveland Cavaliers in the NBA finals. 金州勇士队在NBA总决赛中以更佳的教练策略击败了克里夫兰骑士队。
The team was clearly outcoached and outplayed.  这支球队显然在教练和球员方面都逊色于对手。
Synonym: outmaneuver, surpass
Antonym: be outcoached by
词性: 动词
