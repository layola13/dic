foreclosing: /fɔːrˈkloʊzɪŋ/ | v. |  取消赎回权，排除，阻止 | Taking away the right to redeem mortgaged property because of defaults in payments; preventing. | The bank is foreclosing on their house because they haven't made mortgage payments in six months. 银行正在取消他们的房屋赎回权，因为他们六个月没有支付抵押贷款。


The lender started foreclosing proceedings after he missed three payments.  贷款人在他错过三次付款后启动了取消抵押品赎回权的程序。


近义词: repossessing, seizing, barring | 反义词:  allowing, permitting | 词性: 动词 (present participle of foreclose)

foreclose: /fɔːrˈkloʊz/ | v. | 取消赎回权，排除，阻止 | To take possession of a property from a mortgagor because of defaults on payments; to prevent something from happening. | The bank foreclosed on their farm. 银行取消了他们农场的赎回权。


They foreclosed any possibility of a peaceful settlement. 他们阻止了任何和平解决的可能性。
