nonponderousness:/ˌnɒnpɒnˈdərəsnəs/
noun
轻盈；不沉重；轻松
The quality or state of not being ponderous; lightness, agility.
Her nonponderousness made her a graceful dancer. 她的轻盈使她成为一名优雅的舞者。
lightness, agility, nimbleness
ponderousness, heaviness, clumsiness
n.
