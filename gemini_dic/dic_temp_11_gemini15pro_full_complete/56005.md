do-gooder:/ˈduːˌɡʊdər/| n. |行善者；爱管闲事的人；伪善者|A person who does things they think are good, especially in a way that seems too eager or obvious, sometimes with selfish motives. |例句：He's seen as a do-gooder by some, but others admire his commitment.  他被一些人视为爱管闲事的人，但其他人钦佩他的奉献精神。

例句：She's a do-gooder who's always trying to help people, even when they don't want it. 她是个爱管闲事的人，总是试图帮助别人，即使别人不想要。|近义词：altruist, philanthropist, humanitarian, benevolent, charitable|反义词：malefactor, wrongdoer, egoist|词性:noun
