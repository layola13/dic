glutaconic: /ˌɡluːtəˈkɒnɪk/ | adj. | 谷氨酰的，戊烯二酸的 |  Relating to or derived from glutaconic acid (pentenedioic acid), a crystalline unsaturated dicarboxylic acid. | Glutaconic acid is an unsaturated dicarboxylic acid. 谷氨酰酸是一种不饱和二羧酸。|  Glutaconic anhydride is used in the production of certain resins. 谷氨酰酐用于生产某些树脂。 |  近义词: pentenedioic (in the context of the acid) | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: adjective
