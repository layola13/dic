detest:/dɪˈtest/ /diˈtest/| vt.|憎恶，厌恶|To feel intense dislike or aversion towards something or someone.  To loathe.|I detest hypocrisy. 我厌恶虚伪。|Synonyms: loathe, abhor, despise, hate|Antonyms: adore, love, cherish|Verb
