isochronal:/aɪˈsɒkrənəl/
adjective
等时的；同步的
occurring at the same time; having equal duration.
例句：The isochronal arrival of the trains surprised everyone. 火车同时到达让每个人都感到惊讶。

例句：Isochronal oscillations are crucial in the design of accurate clocks. 等时振荡在精确时钟的设计中至关重要。
近义词：synchronous, simultaneous, coincident
反义词：asynchronous, non-simultaneous
词性: adjective

isochronism:/aɪˈsɒkrənɪzəm/
noun
等时性
The property of having equal duration or occurring at the same time.
例句：The isochronism of the pendulum's swing is the basis of its timekeeping ability. 钟摆摆动的等时性是其计时能力的基础。

例句：The scientist studied the isochronism of the vibrations. 科学家研究了振动的等时性。
近义词：synchronicity, simultaneity
反义词：asynchrony
词性: noun

isochrone:/ˈaɪsəˌkroʊn/
noun
等时线；等时曲线
A line on a map connecting points relating to the same time or equal times.
例句：Isochrones on weather maps show areas where a storm front arrived at the same time. 天气图上的等时线显示了风暴前沿同时到达的区域。

例句：Geologists use isochrones to date rocks. 地质学家使用等时线来确定岩石的年代。
近义词：contour line (in some contexts)
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
