orthophotomap:/ˌɔːrθoʊˈfoʊtoʊˌmæp/ | n. |正射影像图 |An aerial photograph or image geometrically corrected such that the scale is uniform and the image can be used as, or in conjunction with, a map.  It combines the image characteristics of a photograph with the geometric qualities of a map.|例句：The orthophotomap clearly showed the extent of the flooding. 正射影像图清楚地显示了洪水的范围。

例句：Urban planners use orthophotomaps to analyze land use and development. 城市规划者使用正射影像图来分析土地利用和发展。|近义词：orthoimage, rectified photograph|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "uncorrected aerial photograph")|词性:noun
