driftbolt: /ˈdrɪftboʊlt/ | n. |  漂移螺栓; 平头方颈螺栓 | A heavy, threadless bolt used to secure timbers, typically driven into a predrilled hole. It has a plain shank and often a square or rectangular head to prevent rotation during driving. |  They used driftbolts to secure the heavy timbers of the barn frame.  他们用漂移螺栓固定谷仓框架的重型木料。 | synonyms: drift pin,  drift  | antonyms: screw | noun
