melatonins:/ˌmeləˈtoʊnɪnz/ | n. |褪黑素；褪黑激素| A hormone secreted by the pineal gland in the brain that helps regulate sleep-wake cycles. It is also available as a supplement. |  Melatonin levels naturally rise in the evening and fall in the morning. 褪黑素水平在晚上自然升高，在早上降低。| synonyms: N-acetyl-5-methoxytryptamine | antonyms:  None (Hormones don't typically have direct antonyms.) | noun
