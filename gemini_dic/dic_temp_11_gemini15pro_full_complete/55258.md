dissimule:/dɪˈsɪmjʊl/ | v. | 掩饰，隐藏，假装 | To disguise or conceal (one's true feelings or intentions). | He dissimulated his anger with a forced smile. 他强颜欢笑以掩饰内心的愤怒。 |  She dissimulated her fear by pretending to be brave. 她假装勇敢来掩饰她的恐惧。 | 近义词: conceal, disguise, mask, feign | 反义词: reveal, expose, divulge | 词性: 动词

词源: From Late Latin dissimulare, from Latin dis- “not” + simulare “to make like, imitate, copy, represent.”
