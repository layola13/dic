flowingness:/ˈfloʊɪŋnɪs/| n. |流畅，流畅性|The flowingness of her writing style captivated the readers. 她的写作风格流畅，吸引了读者。|例句：The flowingness of the river made for a peaceful scene. 河水的流畅构成了一幅宁静的景象。|近义词：fluidity, smoothness, grace, ease|反义词: stiffness, rigidity, awkwardness|词性:名词

Etymology: From "flowing" (present participle of "flow") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  "Flow" comes from Old English "flowan," from Proto-Germanic "*flowan," from Proto-Indo-European "*pleu-," meaning "to flow, swim, float."
