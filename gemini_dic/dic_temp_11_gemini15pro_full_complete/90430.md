Simple Present:
What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? = 他在花园里做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she cooked for dinner? = 她晚饭做了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading all afternoon? = 他整个下午都在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do last night? = 你昨晚做了什么？
What did she say to you? = 她对你说了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? = 昨晚8点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? = 我看到她时她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you went to bed? = 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? = 她生病前吃了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I called? = 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 她换专业之前一直在学什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will she study in college? = 她大学要学什么？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be presenting at the conference? = 她将在会议上展示什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the age of 30? = 到30岁时你将取得什么成就？
What will she have finished by the end of the day? = 到今天结束时她将完成什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？
