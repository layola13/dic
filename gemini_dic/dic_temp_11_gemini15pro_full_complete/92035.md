hyperperfection:/ˌhaɪpərˈpɜːrfɛkʃən/ | n. | 超完美，超完全 | A state of exceeding perfection; being more than perfect, often in an obsessive or excessive way. |  He strives for hyperperfection in his work, often spending countless hours on minor details. 他力求在工作中达到超完美，经常在细枝末节上花费无数时间。
