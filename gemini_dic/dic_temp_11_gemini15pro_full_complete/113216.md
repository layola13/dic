lush:/lʌʃ/
adjective|茂盛的，葱郁的；丰富的，大量的；（非正式）极好的，非常令人满意的|Characterized by abundant growth, luxurious, or (informally) very good or appealing.|The valley was filled with lush vegetation. 山谷里长满了茂盛的植物。|
近义词:abundant, luxurious, rich, vibrant, opulent
反义词:sparse, barren, meager, arid, desolate
词性:adjective

lush:/lʌʃ/
noun|酒鬼，醉汉|A person who drinks alcohol excessively and habitually.|He was a known lush and often seen staggering down the street. 他是个有名的酒鬼，经常被人看到在街上摇摇晃晃。|
近义词:drunkard, alcoholic
反义词:teetotaler
词性:noun

词源:
From Middle English *lusshe*, probably from a variant of Middle English *lasche* (“lax, slack”), from Old French *lasche* (“lax, loose”), from Latin *laxus* (“loose, slack”).  The semantic development is perhaps from the idea of plants growing loosely and freely.
