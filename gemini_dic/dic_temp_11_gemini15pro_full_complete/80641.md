gopher:/ˈɡoʊfər/ (US) /ˈɡəʊfə(r)/ (UK)| n.|地鼠；囊鼠；打杂工|A burrowing rodent with fur-lined pouches on the outside of its cheeks, found in North and Central America.|The gopher had dug a network of tunnels under the lawn. 地鼠在草坪下挖了一个地道网络。|近义词：ground squirrel, pocket gopher|反义词: N/A|noun
