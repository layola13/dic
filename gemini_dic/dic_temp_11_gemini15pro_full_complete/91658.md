hypercatalectic:/ˌhaɪpərˌkætəˈlɛktɪk/| adj.|超音节的，超长音节的|Having an extra syllable or syllables beyond the normal or expected metrical length, especially in a line of verse.|The poet used a hypercatalectic line to emphasize the final word.|Near synonyms: extended, lengthened, prolonged|Antonyms: truncated, shortened, catalectic|adjective
