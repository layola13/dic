freeze-dried:/ˈfriːz draɪd/| adj.|冻干的，冷冻干燥的|preserved by a process of rapid freezing and then drying in a vacuum, which removes ice by sublimation|例句：Freeze-dried coffee is convenient for camping. 冻干咖啡便于露营。

|例句：The museum has a collection of freeze-dried specimens.  博物馆收藏了一批冻干标本。|近义词：dehydrated, desiccated|反义词:hydrated|词性：形容词

freeze-dry:/ˈfriːz draɪ/| v.|冷冻干燥|to preserve (something) by rapid freezing and then drying in a vacuum, which removes ice by sublimation|例句：They freeze-dry the strawberries to preserve their flavor. 他们冷冻干燥草莓以保持其风味。

|例句：Many pharmaceutical companies freeze-dry their products. 许多制药公司冷冻干燥他们的产品。|近义词：dehydrate, desiccate|反义词:hydrate|词性：动词

词源:  "freeze" + "dried,"  the past participle of "dry." "Freeze" comes from Old English *frēosan, while "dry" comes from Old English drȳge.
