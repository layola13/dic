habitus:/ˈhæbɪtəs/ /ˈhæbɪtʊs/
n.|气质，习性，惯习|A person's habitus is the sum total of their ingrained habits, skills, and dispositions. It is often shaped by their social and cultural background.|Her aristocratic habitus was immediately apparent.|Synonyms: demeanor, bearing, air, manner, mien|Antonyms: None|Noun
