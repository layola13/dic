docentships:/ˈdoʊsən(t)ʃɪps/| n.|讲解员的职位；讲解员实习期|A period of training or apprenticeship for a docent, often involving guided tours and educational programs.  It also refers to the position itself.|The museum offers docentships to qualified volunteers.|Synonym:  internship, apprenticeship, traineeship, practicum|Antonym:  employment, job, profession|noun
