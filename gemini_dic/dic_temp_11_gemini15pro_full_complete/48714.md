deficient:/dɪˈfɪʃənt/| adj.|缺乏的，不足的，有缺陷的|lacking some element or characteristic; insufficient or inadequate.|例句：The soil is deficient in nitrogen. 土壤缺氮。|近义词：lacking, insufficient, inadequate, short, scarce|反义词：sufficient, adequate, ample, abundant|词性:形容词

deficients:/dɪˈfɪʃənts/| n.|有缺陷的人或物；不足之处|People or things that are deficient; deficiencies.|例句：The report highlighted the deficients in the current system.  报告强调了当前系统的缺陷。|近义词：shortcomings, flaws, weaknesses, defects|反义词：strengths, assets|词性:名词
