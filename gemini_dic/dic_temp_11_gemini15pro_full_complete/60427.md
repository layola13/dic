eightling:/ˈeɪtlɪŋ/| n.|八胞胎之一，八分之一|One of eight offspring born at the same birth, especially one of eight puppies or kittens; an eighth part. |例句：One eightling was significantly smaller than its siblings.  一只八胞胎明显比它的兄弟姐妹小。

例句：The farmer was surprised by the litter of eightlings. 农民对一窝八胞胎感到惊讶。|近义词：octuplet |反义词: N/A|词性:noun
