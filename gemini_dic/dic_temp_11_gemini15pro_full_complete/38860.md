confederate:/kənˈfɛdərɪt/ | n. adj. v. | 同盟者，联邦的，结盟 | a person, group, or nation united with others for a common purpose; joined by an agreement or treaty. | The Confederate states seceded from the Union.  邦联各州脱离了联邦。 | synonym: ally, accomplice, associate | antonym: enemy, opponent, adversary | noun

confederate:/kənˈfɛdərɪt/ | n. adj. v. | 同盟的，邦联的，结成联盟 | joined by an agreement or treaty. | The Confederate flag is a controversial symbol.  邦联旗帜是一个有争议的象征。 | synonym: federal, allied, united | antonym: independent, separate, divided | adjective

confederate:/kənˈfɛdərɪt/ | n. adj. v. | 结盟，联合 | to unite for a common purpose. | Several smaller tribes confederated to resist the larger empire.  几个较小的部落联合起来抵抗更大的帝国。 | synonym: ally, join, combine | antonym: separate, divide, disband | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *confederatus*, past participle of confœderare “to unite by a league,” from Latin com- “together, with” + fœderare “to make a treaty,” from fœdus “treaty, league, covenant.”
