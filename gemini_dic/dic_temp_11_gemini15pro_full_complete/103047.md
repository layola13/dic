jellyfishing:/ˈdʒɛliˌfɪʃɪŋ/
n.
水母捕捞; 模仿水母的动作(尤指在水中);
The act of catching jellyfish, often for research or consumption. Also, the act of mimicking the movements of a jellyfish, particularly in water.
We went jellyfishing at the beach today. 我们今天去海滩捞水母了。
The children were jellyfishing in the pool, pretending to be sea creatures. 孩子们在泳池里模仿水母的动作，假装自己是海洋生物。
近义词: jellyfish catching,  drift net fishing (for certain types of jellyfish)
反义词: None (specific)
词性: noun
