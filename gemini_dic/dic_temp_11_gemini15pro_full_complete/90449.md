/wɒt/(UK) /hwɑːt/(US) |pron. det. adv.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information about something.|What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|something, anything | nothing | pronoun, determiner, adverb


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she like to do in her free time? 她空闲时间喜欢做什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? 他晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么?
What has she done to improve her skills? 她做了什么来提高她的技能？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading these days? 他这些天一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last weekend? 你上周末做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? 她对这部电影说了些什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? 我看到她的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you came here? 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? 她生病前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about before the meeting started? 会议开始前他们一直在争论什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after you graduate? 你毕业后打算做什么？
What will she study in college? 她大学里会学什么专业？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next year? 明年这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be presenting at the conference? 他们将在会议上展示什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the time you're 30? 到你30岁的时候你会取得什么成就？
What will she have finished by the end of the day? 到今天结束时她会完成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for the past ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？

虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
What would she say if she were here? 如果她在这里她会说什么？


被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done about the problem? 正在对这个问题采取什么措施？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the summer? 你暑假的计划是什么？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what you're saying. 她不明白你在说什么。
