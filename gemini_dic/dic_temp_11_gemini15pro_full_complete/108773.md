leaseholders:/ˈliːsˌhəʊldərz/ (UK), /ˈliːsˌhoʊldərz/ (US)
n.
承租人，租户
A person who holds the lease of a property; a tenant.
The leaseholders in the building complained about the rising service charges.  这栋楼的租户抱怨服务费上涨。

The leaseholders are responsible for maintaining the property. 这些租户有责任维护房产。

Synonym: tenant, lessee
Antonym: landlord, lessor
noun

leaseholder:/ˈliːsˌhəʊldər/ (UK), /ˈliːsˌhoʊldər/ (US)
