cotenants: /ˌkōˈtenənts/ | n. |共同租户，合租人|  A cotenant is one of two or more people who simultaneously hold an interest in the same property. |The two cotenants disagreed about how to use the property.|Synonyms: joint tenants, co-owners, co-lessees | Antonyms: sole owner, sole proprietor | noun
