iulidan: /dʒuːˈlɪdən/ | adj. |  千足虫类的；多足的 | Belonging to or resembling the Iulidae, a family of millipedes. |  The iulidan millipede coiled up when touched.  千足虫一被触碰就蜷缩起来了。 | 近义词: millipede | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps centipede as a contrasting arthropod) | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *Iulus*, the name of a mythical son of Aeneas, said to have been struck by lightning and turned into a millipede, + *-idan*, a suffix forming adjectives.
