misrepresenter:/ˌmɪsrɛprɪˈzɛntər/ | n. |歪曲事实者，误传者|A person who misrepresents something; someone who gives a false or misleading account of something. |He was accused of being a misrepresenter of the facts. 他被指控歪曲事实。| The company sued the journalist for misrepresenting their product. 这家公司起诉了这位记者，因为他歪曲了他们的产品。 | synonym: liar, falsifier, distorter, perverter | antonym: truth-teller, honest person | noun

misrepresent:/ˌmɪsrɛprɪˈzɛnt/ | v. |歪曲，误传|Give a false or misleading account of something. | The advertisement misrepresented the product's capabilities.  这则广告歪曲了产品的功能。|  She deliberately misrepresented the facts to the court. 她故意向法庭歪曲事实。| synonym: distort, falsify, misstate, twist | antonym: represent accurately, portray truthfully | verb
