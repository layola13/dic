busways:/ˈbʌsweɪz/
n.
公交专用道；公共汽车专用路线
Designated roadways or routes reserved exclusively for buses, often to improve public transportation efficiency.
Buses use the busways to bypass congested traffic. 公交车使用公交专用道来绕过拥堵的交通。
The city's new busways have significantly reduced travel time. 城市新建的公交专用道大大缩短了出行时间。
bus lanes, transitways
roads, highways
noun
