mispaint:/ˌmɪsˈpeɪnt/
verb
错误地涂绘；歪曲
to paint incorrectly or badly; to represent inaccurately or unfairly
The artist mispainted the portrait, making the subject's nose too large.  这位艺术家把肖像画画错了，把人物的鼻子画得太大了。
The media mispainted the politician's views, making him seem more extreme than he actually is. 媒体歪曲了这位政治家的观点，使他看起来比实际更极端。
近义词: misrepresent, distort, mischaracterize
反义词: portray accurately, depict truthfully
词性: verb
