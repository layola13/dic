bristliest:/ˈbrɪstliɪst/
adjective
最硬毛的，最易怒的
Covered with bristles, especially stiff or coarse ones; most easily angered or irritated.
The bristliest cactus I've ever seen was in the Arizona desert. 我在亚利桑那沙漠见过最硬毛的仙人掌。
His bristliest response came after the reporter's persistent questioning.  在记者不停的追问下，他给出了最易怒的回应。
近义词: prickliest, spikiest, touchiness, irascibility
反义词: softest, smoothest, placid, affable
词性: adjective
词源: From bristle + -y + -est. Bristle comes from Old English *byrstle, from Proto-Germanic *burstiz.
