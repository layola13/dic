maieutic:/meɪˈjuːtɪk/
adjective
产婆术的；助产的
Relating to or resembling the method of Socrates, who by a series of questions and answers elicited from his pupils the truths of which they were already unconsciously possessed.
Socrates' maieutic method of teaching helped his students discover their own knowledge.  苏格拉底的助产式教学方法帮助他的学生发现他们自己的知识。

maieutic:/meɪˈjuːtɪk/
adjective
启发的；引导的
Serving to bring out or develop something latent.
The teacher's maieutic questioning helped the student understand the complex concept.  老师启发性的提问帮助学生理解了这个复杂的概念。


近义词:Socratic, heuristic, dialectical
反义词:didactic, expository
词性:adjective
