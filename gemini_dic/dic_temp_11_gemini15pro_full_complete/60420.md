eighthly:/ˈeɪθli/
adverb
第八
In the eighth place; as the eighth point.
Firstly, I want to thank you all for coming. Secondly, I'd like to introduce our speaker. Thirdly,... Eighthly, we need to consider the long-term implications. 首先，我要感谢大家的光临。其次，我想介绍一下我们的演讲者。第三，...第八，我们需要考虑长期影响。
Near Synonyms: in the eighth place, lastly (if it's the final point)
Antonyms: firstly, secondly, etc. (no true opposite exists)
词性: 副词
