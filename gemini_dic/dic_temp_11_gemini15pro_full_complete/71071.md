firstof:/ˈfɜːrstɒv/ (British) /ˈfɜːrstʌv/ (American)| adv.|首先，第一|Indicates the first point or item in a series. It's often used in informal speech or writing. |"Firstof, I'd like to thank you all for coming." 首先，我要感谢大家的光临。|"Firstof, we need to gather the necessary supplies. Then, we can begin the project." 首先，我们需要收集必要的物资。然后，我们就可以开始这个项目了。|近义词：firstly, first, to begin with, initially|反义词: lastly, finally|词性:副词
