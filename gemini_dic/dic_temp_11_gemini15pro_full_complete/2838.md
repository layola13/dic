adventuress: /ədˈvɛntʃərɪs/
| n. | 女冒险家 | A woman who seeks unusual and exciting experiences, often involving some degree of danger or risk. |  She was a renowned adventuress, known for her daring exploits across the globe. 她是一位著名的女冒险家，以其在全球的勇敢冒险而闻名。| 近义词: explorer, thrill-seeker, daredevil | 反义词: homebody, coward | 词性: 名词


词源: From French aventurière, feminine of aventurier (“adventurer”), from aventurer (“to venture”). Ultimately from Latin adventūra (“arrival, event”).
