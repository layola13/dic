modiolus:/ˈmoʊdiˌoʊləs/| n. |蜗轴;中轴|The central pillar or column of the cochlea of the inner ear around which the spiral canal of the cochlea turns.|The surgeon carefully identified the modiolus during the cochlear implant procedure.  外科医生在人工耳蜗植入手术中仔细地识别了蜗轴。|Synonym: axis, central column |Antonym: periphery |noun

modiolus:/məˈdaɪələs/| n. |(双壳纲软体动物的)中枢韧带|A tough, fibrous structure that connects the two halves (valves) of a bivalve mollusk shell.|The modiolus allows the bivalve to open and close its shell. 中枢韧带允许双壳贝类打开和关闭它的壳。|Synonym: hinge ligament |Antonym: N/A |noun
