complexing:/ˈkɒmplɛksɪŋ/ (UK), /kəmˈplɛksɪŋ/ (US)
| adj.| 复杂的，使复杂的 | Making something more complex or intricate.  This process can be quite complexing for beginners. 这个过程对于初学者来说可能相当复杂。 |近义词：complicating, confusing |反义词: simplifying |词性: 形容词 (adjective)

complexing:/ˈkɒmplɛksɪŋ/ (UK), /kəmˈplɛksɪŋ/ (US)
| n.|络合，成络 | The process of forming a complex, especially a chemical complex. The complexing of metal ions with ligands is an important area of study in chemistry. 金属离子与配体的络合是化学研究中的一个重要领域。 |近义词：chelation, coordination |反义词: dissociation |词性: 名词 (noun)


词源: From the verb "complex", derived from the Latin word "complexus," meaning "embraced, entwined, or woven together."
