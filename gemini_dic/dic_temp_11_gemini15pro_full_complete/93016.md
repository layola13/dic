idiothermic:/ɪˌdiːəˈθɜːrmɪk/ | adj. | 温血的，恒温的 |  Relating to or designating animals whose body temperature remains relatively constant, regardless of the surrounding temperature.  Maintained at a constant temperature by internal regulation. | Birds and mammals are idiothermic animals. 鸟类和哺乳动物是恒温动物。 |  The idiothermic nature of mammals allows them to thrive in diverse climates. 哺乳动物的恒温特性使它们能够在不同的气候中茁壮成长。 | homeothermic, endothermic, warm-blooded | poikilothermic, ectothermic, cold-blooded | adjective
