depreciative:/dɪˈpriːʃiətɪv/ /dɪˈpriːʃətiv/
adjective
贬低的，轻视的，贬损的
Expressing disapproval; tending to diminish or disparage.
She made a depreciative remark about his new car. 她对他的新车做出了贬低的评价。
The critic's depreciative comments angered the artist.  评论家贬低的评论激怒了艺术家。

synonyms: derogatory, disparaging, belittling, demeaning, slighting
antonyms: complimentary, flattering, praising, laudatory
word type: adjective
