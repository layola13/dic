insured:/ɪnˈʃʊərd/ | adj. n. | 已投保的；被保险人 | Covered by insurance; a person or thing covered by insurance. | Example: The insured received compensation after the accident.  事故发生后，受保人得到了赔偿。| Synonym: covered, protected | Antonym: uninsured, unprotected | adjective

insured:/ɪnˈʃʊərd/ | n. | 被保险人 | A person or thing covered by insurance. | Example: The insurers contacted the insured to assess the damage.  保险公司联系了被保险人以评估损失。| Synonym: policyholder, beneficiary | Antonym: insurer | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
