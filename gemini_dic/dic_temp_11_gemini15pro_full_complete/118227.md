mercaptan:/mərˈkæptæn/| n. |硫醇，氢硫基，|A compound containing the sulfhydryl group (-SH) bound to a carbon atom.  These compounds often have strong, unpleasant odors.|The mercaptan added to natural gas gives it a distinctive smell.  添加入天然气的硫醇使其具有独特的气味。|synonyms: thiol, sulfhydryl|antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a chemical compound)|noun
