furiousness:/ˈfjʊəriəsnəs/ | n. | 狂怒，暴怒 | The state of being furious; extreme anger. | His furiousness at the betrayal was palpable. 他对背叛的愤怒显而易见。|  The furiousness of the storm was terrifying.  风暴的狂暴令人恐惧。| 近义词：rage, fury, wrath, anger | 反义词：calmness, tranquility, peacefulness | 词性: 名词

词源: From furious + -ness.  Furious comes from the Latin word "furiosus," meaning "full of fury" or "mad."
