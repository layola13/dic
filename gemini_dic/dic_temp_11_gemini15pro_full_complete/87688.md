hexametaphosphate:/ˌhɛksəmɛtəˈfɒsfeɪt/ | n. | 六偏磷酸盐 | A polymeric phosphate salt containing a cyclic or polymeric anion formed from six phosphate units.  It is used as a sequestrant, meaning it binds to metal ions, preventing them from reacting with other substances. | Hexametaphosphate is often added to detergents to soften water. 六偏磷酸盐常被添加到洗涤剂中以软化水。| synonym:  polyphosphate, sequestrant | antonym:  (No direct antonym, could consider substances that increase water hardness like calcium chloride) | noun

hexametaphosphate:/ˌhɛksəmɛtəˈfɒsfeɪt/ | n. | 六偏磷酸钠 |  Specifically referring to the sodium salt of hexametaphosphoric acid, a common form of hexametaphosphate used in various applications.  | Sodium hexametaphosphate is used in food processing as a stabilizer. 六偏磷酸钠在食品加工中用作稳定剂。| synonym: SHMP, sodium polyphosphate | antonym: (No direct antonym) | noun
