cartomancy: /ˈkɑːrtəˌmænsi/ | n. | 纸牌占卜 | Fortune-telling using cards, especially playing cards. | She practiced cartomancy as a hobby. 她把纸牌占卜作为一种爱好。|  She visited a cartomancer to have her fortune told. 她拜访了一位纸牌占卜师来占卜她的命运。 | 近义词: fortune-telling, divination | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑 science, logic) | 词性: noun
Etymology: From French cartomancie, from carte (“card”) + -mancy (“divination”). Ultimately from Latin charta (“paper, card”) and Greek manteia ("divination").
