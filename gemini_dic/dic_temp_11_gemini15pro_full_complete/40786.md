conviviality:/kənˌvɪviˈælɪti/
n.
欢乐；欢宴；热闹
The state or quality of being friendly and lively; fondness for feasting, drinking, and good company.
The party was marked by great conviviality.  这个聚会充满了欢乐的气氛。
近义词：jollity, merriment, gaiety, sociability
反义词：somberness, gloom, sullenness
词性：名词

convivial:/kənˈvɪviəl/
adj.
欢乐的；欢宴的；热闹的
Friendly, lively, and enjoyable.
We had a convivial evening with friends. 我们和朋友们度过了一个愉快的夜晚。
近义词：jovial, merry, cheerful, sociable
反义词：somber, gloomy, sullen
词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin convivialis "festal," from Latin convivium "a feast," from com- "together" + vivere "to live."
