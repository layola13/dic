brevier:/ˈbriːviər/ /brɪˈvɪr/ | n. |日课经书；(印刷)五号活字|A book containing the daily prayers and offices of the Roman Catholic Church; a size of type, about 8-point.|例句：The priest read from the brevier.  神父读着日课经书。

例句：The book was printed in brevier. 这本书是用五号活字印刷的。|近义词：prayer book, breviary (for the religious book); eight-point (for the type size)|反义词: None|词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *breviaire*, from Latin *breviarium* (“abridgement, summary”), neuter of *breviarius* (“abridged”), from *brevis* (“short”).
