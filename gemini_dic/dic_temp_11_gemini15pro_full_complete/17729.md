bedeck:/bɪˈdɛk/
verb
装饰，修饰
To decorate or adorn lavishly, typically with numerous or colorful items.
The hall was bedecked with flowers and ribbons for the wedding.  大厅为了婚礼用鲜花和丝带装饰了起来。
The Christmas tree was bedecked with ornaments. 圣诞树上装饰着各种饰品。

近义词: adorn, decorate, embellish, ornament, garnish
反义词: strip, denude, bare

词性: verb
