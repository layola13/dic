brad:/bræd/| n.|无头钉，细钉，小钉|A small, thin wire nail with a small head, used for fixing thin pieces of wood together.|例句：He hammered the brad into the picture frame. 他用锤子把小钉钉进画框里。

例句：Use brads to attach the molding to the wall. 用小钉把装饰线条固定在墙上。|近义词：nail, tack, pin|反义词: (无直接反义词) |词性:noun
