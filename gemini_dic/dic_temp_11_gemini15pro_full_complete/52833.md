diluvium:/dɪˈluːviəm/
n.
洪积层；冲积层
A deposit of clay, sand, gravel, and boulders left by flowing water, especially from melting glaciers.
The diluvium in this valley is several meters thick.  这个山谷的洪积层有几米厚。
diluvial deposits  洪积物
alluvium, drift
n/a
noun
