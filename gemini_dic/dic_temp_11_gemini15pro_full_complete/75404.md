fructiferous:/fruːkˈtɪfərəs/
adjective
多产的；富有成效的
Producing much fruit; fruitful.
The fructiferous tree bore a bountiful harvest of apples. 这棵多产的树结了丰富的苹果。
The meeting was fructiferous, resulting in several new initiatives. 这次会议富有成效，促成了几项新举措。
synonyms: productive, fertile, fruitful, prolific
antonyms: barren, sterile, unproductive
word type: adjective
