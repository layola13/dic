ordinariness:/ˌɔːrdɪˈnɛrɪnɪs/
n.
平凡；普通；平常
the quality or state of being ordinary.
The ordinariness of the day was comforting.  这平凡的一天令人感到欣慰。
Her photographs capture the ordinariness of everyday life. 她的照片捕捉到了日常生活的平凡。
near synonyms: commonness, normality, usualness, mundanity, banality
antonyms: extraordinariness, unusualness, remarkableness, singularity, uniqueness
noun
