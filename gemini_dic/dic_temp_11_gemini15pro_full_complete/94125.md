impedance:/ɪmˈpiːdəns/| n.|阻抗|The effective resistance of an electric circuit or component to alternating current, arising from the combined effects of ohmic resistance and reactance.|The speaker's impedance must match that of the amplifier.  扬声器的阻抗必须与放大器的阻抗匹配。|resistance, reactance|admittance|noun
