barbarities:/bɑːrˈbærətiz/
n.
野蛮行径；残暴行为
extreme cruelty or brutality
The museum's exhibit detailed the barbarities of war.  博物馆的展览详细描述了战争的暴行。
The soldiers committed unspeakable barbarities against the civilians. 士兵们对平民犯下了难以形容的暴行。
近义词: atrocities, cruelties, savagery
反义词: kindness, compassion, humanity
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: barbarite, from Old French barbarite, from Latin barbaritas, from barbarus ‘foreign, barbarous’.
