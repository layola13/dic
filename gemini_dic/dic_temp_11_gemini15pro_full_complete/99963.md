interopercular:/ɪnˌtərɒˈpɜːkjʊlə(r)/ /ˌɪnərɑːˈpɜːrkjələr/ | adj. | 鳃盖间的；位于鳃盖之间的 | Situated between the opercula (gill covers) of a fish. | The interopercular bone is a small, flat bone found in the gill cover of many bony fishes.  这条鱼的鳃盖间骨很小且扁平。|  The interopercular membrane helps to protect the gills. 鳃盖间膜有助于保护鱼鳃。 | 近义词: interoperculary | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, but one might consider terms relating to other parts of the fish anatomy, such as "dorsal" or "ventral") | 词性: adjective
