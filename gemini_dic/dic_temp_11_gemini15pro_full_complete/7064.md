anemone:/əˈneməni/ | n. |银莲花，海葵 |A plant of the buttercup family, typically bearing brightly colored flowers.  Also, a sea anemone, a marine invertebrate animal. |例句：The anemones in the garden are a vibrant red. 花园里的银莲花是鲜艳的红色。

例句：The sea anemone swayed gently in the ocean current. 海葵在洋流中轻轻摇曳。 |近义词：windflower (for land anemone) |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
