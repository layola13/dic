contribute:/kənˈtrɪbjuːt/ /ˈkɑːntrɪbjuːt/| v. |贡献，捐献，投稿，促成|To give something, especially money, in order to provide or achieve something together with other people; to write articles for a newspaper, magazine, or book.|例句：He contributed a large sum of money to the charity. 他向慈善机构捐了一大笔钱。|近义词：donate, give, provide, furnish, supply, submit, add, assist, support|反义词:withdraw, detract, hinder, obstruct|词性:动词

contributing:/kənˈtrɪbjuːtɪŋ/ /ˈkɑːntrɪbjuːtɪŋ/| adj. |贡献的，起作用的|Playing a part in bringing something about.|例句：Stress is a contributing factor in many illnesses. 压力是许多疾病的一个促成因素。|近义词：influential, instrumental, significant, causative|反义词:insignificant, unimportant|词性:形容词

contribution:/ˌkɒntrɪˈbjuːʃn/ /ˌkɑːntrɪˈbjuːʃn/| n. |贡献，捐款，稿件|Something that you contribute or do to help produce or achieve something together with other people; an amount of money that you give in order to provide or achieve something together with other people; a piece of writing that is submitted to a newspaper, magazine, or book.|例句：Her contribution to the project was invaluable. 她对这个项目的贡献是无价的。|近义词：donation, gift, offering, submission, article, input|反义词:deduction, withdrawal|词性:名词

contributor:/kənˈtrɪbjətər/ /ˈkɑːntrɪbjuːtər/| n. |贡献者，捐赠者，投稿人|Someone who contributes something, especially money, in order to provide or achieve something together with other people; someone who writes articles for a newspaper, magazine, or book.|例句：She is a regular contributor to the magazine. 她是这家杂志的定期撰稿人。|近义词：donor, giver, provider, author, writer|反义词:recipient|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *contribuere*, from com- (“together”) + tribuere (“to grant, give, assign”).
