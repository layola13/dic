canonizers:/ˈkænənaɪzərz/
noun
封圣者，册封圣徒的人
People who declare someone a saint.
The canonizers meticulously reviewed the life of the candidate before declaring him a saint.  封圣者在宣布他为圣徒之前仔细审查了候选人的生平。
The Pope and his advisors are the canonizers in the Catholic Church.  教皇和他的顾问是天主教会的封圣者。
synonyms: sanctifiers, beatifiers
antonyms: detractors, critics
