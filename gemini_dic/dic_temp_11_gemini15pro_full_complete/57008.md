double-parkers:/ˈdʌbəlˈpɑːrkərz/ (US) /ˈdʌblˈpɑːkəz/ (UK)| n.|并排停车者；双排停车者|People who park their vehicles beside another already parked vehicle, usually blocking it in or impeding traffic flow.|The street was congested because of all the double-parkers.|Synonyms: illegal parkers, inconsiderate parkers|Antonyms: law-abiding parkers|noun
