harakiri: /ˌhærəˈkɪri/ /ˌhɑːrəˈkɪəri/ | n. | 切腹自杀 | Ritual suicide by disembowelment, formerly practiced by the Japanese samurai as a way to avoid capture or to atone for failure. |  The samurai committed harakiri rather than surrender.  武士切腹自杀而不愿投降。| Synonym: Seppuku | Antonym: Survival | noun

harakiri: /ˌhærəˈkɪri/ /ˌhɑːrəˈkɪəri/ | n. |  (比喻)自我毁灭 | An act of self-destruction, especially in a political or business context. | His stubborn refusal to compromise led to his political harakiri. 他顽固地拒绝妥协，导致了他的政治生涯的自我毁灭。 | Synonym: Self-destruction, suicide | Antonym: Self-preservation | noun
