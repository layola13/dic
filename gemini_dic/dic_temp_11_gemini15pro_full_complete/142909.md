out-rent:/ˌaʊtˈrɛnt/
verb
出租超过；以更高的租金出租
to rent something at a higher price than someone else
He out-rented his competitors and secured the prime retail space. 他以更高的租金比竞争对手租下了黄金零售位置。
outbid, overbid
underbid
