isotrimorphous:/ˌaɪsoʊtraɪˈmɔːrfəs/
adjective
同三晶的；同质三晶的
Existing in three different crystalline forms with the same chemical composition but different physical properties.
Calcium carbonate is isotrimorphous, existing as calcite, aragonite, and vaterite. 碳酸钙是同三晶的，以方解石、文石和球霰石三种形式存在。
近义词:trimorphous, polymorphic
反义词:monomorphic
词性:adjective
