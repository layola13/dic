innernesses:/ˈɪnərnɪsɪz/
n.
内心深处；最深处的本质
The plural form of "innerness," referring to the qualities that make up someone's deepest self or the essential nature of something.
She explored the innernesses of her characters in her novel.  她在小说中探索了她笔下人物的内心深处。
He delved into the innernesses of the ancient texts. 他深入研究了古代文献的最深层本质。
近义词: core, essence, heart, soul, depths
反义词: exterior, surface, superficiality
词性: 名词
