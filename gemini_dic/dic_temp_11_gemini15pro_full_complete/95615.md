incomparabilities:/ɪnkəmˌpærəˈbɪlətiz/
n.
不可比性；无法比较；不相容性
The state or quality of being incomparable; inability to be compared.  Things that are so different they cannot be compared.
Due to the incomparabilities of the two data sets, a direct comparison was not possible. 由于两个数据集的不可比性，无法进行直接比较。
The incomparabilities between the two cultures made communication difficult. 两种文化之间的不相容性使得沟通变得困难。
Synonyms: differences, disparities, dissimilarities, distinctions
Antonyms: similarities, comparabilities, resemblances
noun
