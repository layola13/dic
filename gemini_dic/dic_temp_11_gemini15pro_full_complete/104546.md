kami:/ˈkɑːmiː/| n.|神; 神灵; 神祇|A god or deity, especially in Shinto.|例句：The kami of the mountain is revered by the locals. 当地人崇敬这座山的神灵。

例句：Many Japanese people visit shrines to pray to the kami. 许多日本人参拜神社以祈求神灵。|近义词：god, deity, divinity|反义词:demon, devil|词性:noun
