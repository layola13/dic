exuviae:/ɛksˈuːviːiː/
|n.|蜕皮; (尤指)蛇蜕; 蝉蜕|The cast-off outer covering of an animal, especially an insect or crustacean after a moult.  (动物蜕下的外皮，尤指昆虫或甲壳类动物蜕皮后的外壳)|The snake slithered out of its exuviae.  蛇从它的蜕皮中滑了出来。|Synonyms:  skin, shell, husk, molt, slough|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps "new skin" or "flesh")|Noun
