microcracking:/ˈmaɪkroʊkrækɪŋ/| n.|微裂纹，微裂|The formation of minute cracks within a material, typically caused by stress or thermal changes.  在材料内部形成微小裂缝，通常由应力或热变化引起。|例句：Microcracking can significantly reduce the strength of concrete. 微裂纹会显著降低混凝土的强度。

例句：The engineers detected microcracking in the bridge's support beams. 工程师在桥梁的支撑梁中检测到微裂纹。|近义词：crazing, cracking, fissure|反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps consider:  integrity, soundness)|词性:noun
