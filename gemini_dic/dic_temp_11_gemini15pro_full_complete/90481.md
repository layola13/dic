hubris:/ˈhjuːbrɪs/| n.|傲慢；狂妄自大|Excessive pride or self-confidence, often leading to downfall.  A feeling of invincibility that can lead to disastrous consequences.|His hubris led him to believe he could conquer the world. 他的傲慢使他相信他能征服世界。|Example:  The CEO's hubris was evident in his risky investments, which ultimately led to the company's bankruptcy.  这位CEO的傲慢自大体现在他所做的风险投资上，最终导致了公司的破产。|Synonyms: arrogance, conceit, vanity, haughtiness|Antonyms: humility, modesty, humbleness|noun
