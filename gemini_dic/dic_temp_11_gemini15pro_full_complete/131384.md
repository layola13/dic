noncircumstantial:/ˌnɒnsɜːrkəmˈstænʃəl/ | adj. | 非间接的，直接的，非依据情况的 | Not depending on circumstances; not circumstantial; direct. |  The evidence was noncircumstantial, pointing directly to the culprit.  证据是直接的，直接指向了罪犯。

The witness gave a noncircumstantial account of the robbery. 目击者对抢劫案作了直接的描述。 | 近义词：direct, straightforward, conclusive | 反义词：circumstantial, indirect, inferential | 词性：形容词


词源:

non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non.
circumstantial (adjective): From Latin circumstantia ("circumstance"), from circumstare ("to stand around, surround"), from circum ("around") + stare ("to stand").
