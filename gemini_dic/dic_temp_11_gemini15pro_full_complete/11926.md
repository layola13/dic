arsenic:/ˈɑːrsənɪk/ | n. | 砷，砒霜 |A chemical element that is a brittle, gray metalloid. It is highly poisonous and is used in insecticides, weed killers, and various alloys.|Chronic arsenic poisoning can lead to skin lesions and cancer. 长期砷中毒会导致皮肤病变和癌症。|Synonyms:  As (chemical symbol), arsenous oxide (for the compound commonly called arsenic) |Antonyms:  None (no direct chemical antonym)|noun

arsenic:/ɑːrˈsenɪk(əl)/ | adj. | 含砷的，砷的 | Containing or relating to arsenic. | The arsenic compound was found in the contaminated water supply. 在受污染的水源中发现了砷化合物。 | Synonyms: arsenical, arsenious | Antonyms:  None (no direct chemical antonym) | adjective
