enteradenology: /ɛntərˌædəˈnɒlədʒi/ | n. | 肠腺学 | The study of intestinal glands.  |  Enteradenology is a specialized field within gastroenterology. 肠腺学是胃肠病学中的一个专门领域。|  New research in enteradenology could lead to breakthroughs in treating digestive disorders. 肠腺学的新研究可能会在治疗消化系统疾病方面取得突破。 | 近义词:  intestinal gland study (非正式) | 反义词: 无 | 词性: 名词

词源:  "enter-" (from Greek "enteron," meaning intestine) + "aden-" (from Greek "aden," meaning gland) + "-ology" (from Greek "-logia," meaning study of).
