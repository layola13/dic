effectivenesses:/ɪˈfɛktɪvnɪsɪz/
|n.|有效性，效力|The plural form of "effectiveness," referring to multiple instances or types of being effective.  It signifies the degree to which something produces a desired result or achieves its purpose, in multiple contexts or situations.|
例句：The effectivenesses of the different marketing strategies were compared.  不同的营销策略的有效性进行了比较。

例句：He questioned the effectivenesses of the various treatments. 他质疑各种治疗方法的有效性。
|近义词：efficacies, potencies, strengths|反义词：ineffectivenesses, weaknesses, inefficiencies|词性:名词
