matchmaker:/ˈmætʃˌmeɪkər/| n. |媒人，红娘|A person who tries to arrange marriages or romantic relationships between others.|例句：My aunt acted as a matchmaker for my cousin and his wife.  我的姑妈为我表哥和他的妻子做了媒。

例句：She's a well-known matchmaker in the community. 她在社区里是一位有名的媒人。

近义词：intermediary, go-between|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
