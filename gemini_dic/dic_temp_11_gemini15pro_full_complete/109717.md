leveled-off:/ˈlevəld ɒf/ /ˈlevəld ɔːf/ | v. |趋于平稳，稳定下来| to stop rising or falling and stay at the same level | The price of gold has leveled off after a period of sharp increases. 黄金价格在经历一段时间的急剧上涨后趋于平稳。|  The population growth has leveled off in recent years.  近年来人口增长趋于平稳。|  stabilize, flatten out |  fluctuate, vary | verb

leveled-off:/ˈlevəld ɒf/ /ˈlevəld ɔːf/ | adj. |平稳的，稳定的| having become stable or steady after a period of change | The company's profits have reached a leveled-off stage. 公司的利润已经达到了平稳阶段。| Sales figures have been leveled-off for the past six months. 过去六个月的销售额一直保持平稳。| stable, steady, flat | fluctuating, variable | adjective
