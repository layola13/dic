concordatory:/kənˈkɔːrdətɔːri/
adjective
和谐的，一致的
Relating to or involving concord or agreement.
The two sides finally reached a concordatory solution. 双方最终达成了一个和谐的解决方案。
The government is seeking a concordatory approach to the problem. 政府正在寻求一种协调一致的方法来解决这个问题。
近义词:harmonious, agreeable, conciliatory, amicable
反义词:discordant, antagonistic, conflicting
词性:adjective

concordatory:/kənˈkɔːrdətɔːri/
noun
调解协议
A formal agreement or treaty, especially one designed to end a dispute.
The concordatory between the church and the state was finally signed. 教会和国家之间的调解协议最终签署。
The two warring factions signed a concordatory to end the conflict.  两个交战派系签署了一项调解协议以结束冲突。
近义词:agreement, treaty, pact, covenant
反义词:disagreement, conflict, dispute
词性:noun
