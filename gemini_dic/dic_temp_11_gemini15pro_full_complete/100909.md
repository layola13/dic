intumesce:/ˌɪntʊˈmɛs/ | v.i. |膨胀，肿胀|To swell or expand, especially as a result of heat or moisture.  The wood began to intumesce as the fire spread. |例句：The paint intumesced when exposed to high temperatures, forming a protective layer. 油漆遇高温膨胀，形成保护层。

例句：The life preserver began to intumesce as soon as it hit the water. 救生衣一碰到水就开始膨胀。|近义词：swell, expand, distend, bloat|反义词：shrink, contract, deflate|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin intumescere "to swell up, become swollen," from in- "in, on" + tumescere "to swell," related to tumor "a swelling."
