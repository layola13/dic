masticin: /ˈmæstɪsɪn/ | n. |乳香脂，乳香树脂| A resinous exudation obtained from the mastic tree (Pistacia lentiscus), used in varnishes and chewing gum, and formerly in medicine. | The varnish was made with masticin.  这种清漆是用乳香脂制成的。| Synonyms: mastic, mastiche, gum mastic | Antonyms: None | Noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *masticīna, from Latin mastichē, from Ancient Greek μαστίχη (mastíkhē).
