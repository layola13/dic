contraflow:/ˈkɒntrəfləʊ/ (UK), /ˈkɑːntrəfloʊ/ (US)| n. |逆流，反向车流|The movement of traffic against the normal flow, typically on a highway or road.| Due to the accident, the police implemented a contraflow system to ease congestion. 由于事故，警方实施了逆向行驶以缓解交通拥堵。|The contraflow lane helped to speed up the flow of traffic leaving the city. 逆向车道有助于加快离开城市的交通流量。|近义词：reverse flow, opposite direction flow|反义词: normal flow, regular flow|词性:noun

contraflow:/ˈkɒntrəfləʊ/ (UK), /ˈkɑːntrəfloʊ/ (US)| adj. |逆流的，反向的|Relating to or denoting a system in which traffic flows against the normal direction.| Contraflow lanes are often used during rush hour. 逆向车道通常在高峰时段使用。|Contraflow bus lanes can improve public transport efficiency. 逆向公交车道可以提高公共交通效率。|近义词：reverse, opposite|反义词: normal, regular|词性:adjective
