clairvoyance:/ˌklɛərˈvɔɪəns/| n.|洞察力，千里眼|The ability to see beyond the range of normal perception; the ability to perceive remote events or objects.|She claims to have clairvoyance and can predict the future. 她声称拥有洞察力，可以预知未来。|近义词：insight, intuition, precognition|反义词: blindness, ignorance|noun
