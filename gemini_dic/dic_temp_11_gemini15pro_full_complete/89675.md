hoopoe:/ˈhuːpuː/ (US) /ˈhuːpuː/ (UK)| n.|戴胜|A colourful Eurasian and African bird with a long downcurved bill, an erectile crest, and short rounded wings.|例句：The hoopoe is a striking bird with its distinctive crest. 戴胜是一种引人注目的鸟类，有着独特的羽冠。

例句：We saw a hoopoe foraging in the grass. 我们看到一只戴胜在草地上觅食。|近义词：none (specific bird)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

词源: From Old French *upupa*, from Latin *upupa*, imitative of the bird's cry.
