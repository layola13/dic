musers:/ˈmjuːzərz/
n.
沉思者；空想家；诗人
People who are deeply thoughtful or given to daydreaming, often associated with poetic or artistic inspiration.
She was one of those musers, always lost in her own world. 她就是那种沉思者，总是沉浸在自己的世界里。
Synonyms: contemplators, daydreamers, thinkers, poets, dreamers
Antonyms: realists, pragmatists

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "muse," from Old French "muser" (to ponder, dream), ultimately from Latin "musum" (snout, muzzle of an animal; perhaps originally referring to the pouting expression of someone deep in thought).
