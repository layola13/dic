noninheritable:/ˌnɒnɪnˈhɛrɪtəbl/ | adj. | 不可继承的 | Not capable of being inherited; not heritable.  |  Example: Eye color is inheritable, but learned skills are noninheritable.  眼睛颜色是可遗传的，但学习的技能是不可遗传的。 | Example:  A suntan is a noninheritable characteristic. 晒黑是一种不可遗传的特征。 | Synonyms: non-hereditary, acquired | Antonyms: inheritable, hereditary, genetic | adjective
