overinsisting: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈsɪstɪŋ/ | adj. | 过分坚持的，过分强调的 |  The act of insisting too much or repeatedly emphasizing something to an excessive degree.  It implies a pushy or bothersome persistence. | He was overinsisting on his point, and it annoyed everyone.  他过分强调他的观点，惹恼了大家。| synonyms: insistent, persistent, pertinacious, importunate | antonyms: yielding, acquiescent, compliant | adjective

overinsist: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈsɪst/ | v. | 过分坚持，过分强调 | To insist too much or too strongly on something. | She overinsisted that we stay for dinner, even though we had other plans. 她过分坚持让我们留下来吃晚饭，即使我们有其他计划。| synonyms:  persist, importune, press | antonyms: yield, concede, acquiesce | verb
