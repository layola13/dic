butylamine:/ˈbjuːtɪləmiːn/ | n. |丁胺|A colorless liquid with a fishy odor, it is an organic compound with the formula CH3(CH2)3NH2. It is used in the manufacture of other chemicals, such as pharmaceuticals, pesticides, and rubber chemicals.|Butylamine is a common precursor in organic synthesis. |Synonym: 1-aminobutane |Antonym: N/A |noun
