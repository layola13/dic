domingo:/doˈmiŋɡoʊ/ (US), /dɒˈmɪŋɡəʊ/ (UK)| n.|星期天，星期日|The seventh day of the week, observed as a day of rest and worship in many religions.  It is considered the first day of the week in some cultures.|例句：We always go to church on Domingo. 我们星期天总是去教堂。

例句：Domingo is my favorite day of the week. 星期天是我一周中最喜欢的一天。|近义词：Sunday, Sabbath (in a religious context)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

词源: From Latin *dominicum (diēs), “(day) of the Lord”.  The word evolved differently in the various Romance languages.

注意事项:  "Domingo" is specifically used in Spanish and related languages. While cognates exist in other Romance languages, they are not interchangeable.  In English, use "Sunday".
