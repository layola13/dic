cielo:/ˈsjɛloʊ/ (US), /ˈtʃɛloʊ/ (UK)| n.|天空，天，天堂|The sky is a vast expanse of blue. 天空是一片广阔的蓝色。|例句：The birds soared high in the cielo. 鸟儿在天空中翱翔。|近义词：sky, heavens, firmament|反义词:earth, ground|词性:noun
Etymology: From Spanish *cielo*, from Latin *caelum* ("sky, heaven"). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\ḱoylos* ("hollow, hole").
