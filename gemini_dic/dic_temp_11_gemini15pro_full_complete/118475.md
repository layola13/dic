Mesina: /məˈsiːnə/ | n. | 墨西拿（意大利城市）| A city and port in northeastern Sicily, Italy. It is situated on the Strait of Messina, opposite the mainland. |  例句：We took a ferry from Messina to mainland Italy. 我们从墨西拿坐渡轮到意大利大陆。

例句：Messina is known for its historical architecture. 墨西拿以其历史建筑而闻名。

近义词：city, port | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Italian *Messina*, from earlier *Missina*, from Latin *Messāna*, from Greek *Μεσσήνη (Messēnē)*.  The origin of the Greek name is uncertain, possibly from a Sicel word meaning "harvest" or "middle".
