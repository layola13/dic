half-heathens:/ˌhæfˈhiːðənz/
|n.|半异教徒，半开化的人|People who are only partially converted to Christianity or another religion, retaining some pagan beliefs or practices; or people considered uncivilized or only partially civilized.|The missionaries encountered a tribe of half-heathens in the remote mountains.|Synonyms: semi-heathens, semi-pagans, barbarians, savages|Antonyms: believers, converts, Christians, civilized people|noun
