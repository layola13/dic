knobbly: /ˈnɒbli/ (UK) /ˈnɑːbli/ (US)| adj.|多节的，疙疙瘩瘩的|Covered with or having small knobs or lumps; bumpy.|The old man's knobbly hands told a story of years of hard work. 老人的手疙疙瘩瘩的，讲述了多年辛勤劳作的故事.|The tree's knobbly branches reached towards the sky.  这棵树多节的树枝伸向天空.|近义词：lumpy, bumpy, gnarled|反义词:smooth, even|词性:形容词
Etymology: From knob + -ly.  Ultimately from Middle Low German *knubbe.
