hyperawarenesses:/ˌhaɪpərəˈwɛərnəsɪz/
n.
极度意识，过度警觉
A heightened state of awareness, often to a degree that is excessive or uncomfortable.
She experienced hyperawarenesses of her surroundings after the traumatic event.  创伤事件后，她对周围环境变得极度警觉。
The medication caused hyperawarenesses of his own heartbeat.  药物使他对自己的心跳过度警觉。
近义词：hypersensitivity, oversensitivity
反义词：unawareness, obliviousness
词性: noun
