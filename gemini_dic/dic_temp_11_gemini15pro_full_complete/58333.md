drysuit: /ˈdraɪsuːt/ | n. | 干式潜水服 | A watertight suit worn by divers, boaters, etc., that prevents water from entering and thus keeps the wearer dry, even when submerged. |  The diver wore a drysuit to stay warm in the cold water. 潜水员穿了一件干式潜水服，以便在冰冷的水中保持温暖。|  wetsuit, diving suit |  -- | noun
