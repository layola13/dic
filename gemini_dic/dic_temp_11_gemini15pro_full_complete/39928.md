constriction:/kənˈstrɪkʃən/
n.
收缩，紧缩；压缩；压抑感
the act of making something narrower, tighter, or smaller; a feeling of tightness or pressure
The drug causes constriction of the blood vessels. 这种药物会导致血管收缩。
Synonyms: tightening, compression, squeezing, contraction
Antonyms: expansion, dilation, enlargement
noun

constriction:/kənˈstrɪkʃən/
n.
收缩，紧缩；压缩；压抑感
a narrow part of something
There's a constriction in the pipe. 管道里有一个狭窄的部分。
Synonyms: narrowing, bottleneck
Antonyms: widening
