nonexpansively:/ˌnɒnɪkˈspænsɪvli/ | adv. | 非扩张地 | Not expanding or tending to expand; in a nonexpansive manner.  Relating to a function or mapping that does not increase distances between points. | The function operates nonexpansively on the set.  这个函数在集合上非扩张地运行。 | The algorithm searches nonexpansively through the data. 该算法以非扩张的方式搜索数据。 | 近义词: contractively, restrictively | 反义词: expansively, increasingly | 词性: 副词
