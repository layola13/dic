congenialize:/kənˈdʒiːniəlaɪz/ | v. | 使和谐；使意气相投；使合口味 | To make congenial; to adapt to one's taste or liking. |  She tried to congenialize the atmosphere by putting on some music. 她试图通过放音乐来营造和谐的氛围。 |  He congenialized the recipe to his own liking by adding more spices. 他通过添加更多香料使食谱更合自己的口味。 | 近义词: harmonize, adapt, accommodate | 反义词: alienate, estrange | 词性: 动词

词源:  From con- + genial + -ize.  "Genial" comes from Latin *genialis "festive, joyous," related to genius "guardian spirit."
