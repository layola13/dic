frenziedly:/ˈfrɛnzidli/
adverb
疯狂地，狂暴地
In a frenzied manner; with frenzy.  Characterized by wild or uncontrolled excitement.
He worked frenziedly to finish the project before the deadline. 他疯狂地工作，以便在截止日期前完成项目。
The crowd cheered frenziedly as the winning goal was scored.  当制胜一球进球时，人群疯狂地欢呼。
近义词: frantically, wildly, maniacally, hysterically
反义词: calmly, peacefully, quietly
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From frenzy + -ly.  Frenzy comes from the late Middle English frenesie,  from Old French freneisie, and ultimately from Greek phrenēsis "inflammation of the brain," from phrēn "mind, diaphragm."
