overmystifies:/ˌoʊvərˈmɪstɪfaɪz/
|v.|使过于神秘化，使迷惑不解|To make something excessively mysterious or confusing; to mystify to a greater degree than necessary or appropriate.|The author overmystifies the plot with unnecessary red herrings.|近义词:obfuscate, bewilder, confuse|反义词:clarify, explain, elucidate|verb
