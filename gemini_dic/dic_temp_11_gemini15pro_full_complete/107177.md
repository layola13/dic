lagged:/læɡd/| adj.|滞后的，迟缓的，落后的|slow or late in development, progress, response, etc.|The project is lagged because of funding problems.  这个项目由于资金问题而滞后。|The team's performance has lagged recently.  该队的表现最近有所下滑。|近义词：delayed, slow, behind, tardy|反义词：advanced, ahead, prompt, timely|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From the verb "lag," which comes from Old Norse *laggr, meaning "hanging down, slack."
