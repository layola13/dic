nonsanctions:/nɒnˈsæŋkʃənz/
|n.| 非制裁，未受制裁 |Absence of sanctions; specifically, the absence of economic or political penalties imposed by one country or group of countries on another.|  Many businesses are eager to invest in countries with nonsanctions. 许多企业渴望投资于未受制裁的国家。| synonyms: nonintervention, noninterference | antonyms: sanctions, penalties, restrictions | noun

nonsanctioned:/nɒnˈsæŋkʃənd/
|adj.| 未经批准的，未经授权的 | Not officially approved or authorized. | The nonsanctioned protest led to several arrests. 未经批准的抗议导致多人被捕。| synonyms: unauthorized, unapproved, illicit | antonyms: sanctioned, authorized, approved, legitimate | adjective
