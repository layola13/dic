filmily:/ˈfɪlmɪli/| adv.| 薄膜状地，朦胧地|In a manner resembling a thin film; thinly or faintly.  It describes something that appears or behaves like a thin film or membrane, often suggesting a delicate or transparent quality.|The light filtered filmily through the frosted glass. 光线朦胧地透过磨砂玻璃。|近义词：faintly, dimly, thinly, sheerly, transparently|反义词: thickly, opaquely, densely|词性:副词
