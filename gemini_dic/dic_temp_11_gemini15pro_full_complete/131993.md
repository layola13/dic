nonconviviality:/ˌnɒnkənˌvɪviˈælɪti/
noun
不友好; 不合群; 不 convivial
Lack of conviviality; the state of being uncongenial, unsociable, or unfriendly.
The nonconviviality of the atmosphere made it difficult to relax.  这种不友好的气氛让人很难放松。
The meeting was marked by a distinct nonconviviality. 会议的特点是明显的不友好。
近义词:unfriendliness, inhospitability, coldness, aloofness, unsociability
反义词:conviviality, friendliness, sociability, warmth, geniality
