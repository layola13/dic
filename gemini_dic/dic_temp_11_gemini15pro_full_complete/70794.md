fingerless:/ˈfɪŋɡərləs/| adj.|无指的，露指的|Without fingers; having the fingers cut off or uncovered.  Specifically referring to gloves or mittens that do not have separate sheaths for the fingers.|She wore fingerless gloves so she could still use her phone in the cold. 她戴着露指手套，这样在寒冷的天气里仍然可以使用手机。|Near synonyms: open-fingered, half-finger, mittenless|Antonyms: full-fingered, gloved|Adjective
