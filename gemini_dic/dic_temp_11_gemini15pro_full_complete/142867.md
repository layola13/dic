out-measured:/ˌaʊtˈmeʒərd/
verb
胜过，超过（在测量上）
surpassed in measurement; exceeded in quantity or degree.
例句：His generosity out-measured hers. 他的慷慨胜过她。
例句：The new building out-measured the old one in height. 新建筑的高度超过了旧建筑。
近义词：exceeded, surpassed, outstripped, outdone
反义词：outdone, dwarfed, overshadowed
词性：v.


out-measured:/ˌaʊtˈmeʒərd/
adjective
被超越的
surpassed in measurement
例句: His kindness was out-measured by his greed. 他的善良被他的贪婪所掩盖。
例句: The smaller boat was out-measured by the waves. 小船被海浪吞没了。
近义词: surpassed, exceeded
反义词: exceeding, surpassing
词性: adj.
