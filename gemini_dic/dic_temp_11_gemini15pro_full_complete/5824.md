amateurishness:/ˈæmətərɪʃnɪs/| n. |业余爱好；不熟练；外行 |The amateurishness of the performance was evident. 表演的业余性质显而易见。|近义词：inexperience, clumsiness, ineptitude|反义词：professionalism, skill, expertise|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From amateur + -ishness.  "Amateur" comes from the French "amateur," meaning "lover of," from the Latin "amare," meaning "to love." The suffix "-ishness" indicates a state or quality of being.
