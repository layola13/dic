allowance: /əˈlaʊəns/ | n. | 津贴，零用钱，限额，折扣，考虑 |  A sum of money paid regularly to a person, often a child, for their personal use.  An amount of something that is permitted, especially within a set of regulations or for a specified purpose. A reduction in price. Consideration or acknowledgment of something. | My children get a weekly allowance. 我的孩子们每周都有零用钱。| synonyms: stipend, payment, ration, allocation, concession, discount | antonyms: prohibition, denial, surcharge | noun

allowance: /əˈlaʊəns/ | n. | 容差 | The amount of something that is acceptable or permitted within a set of standards or limits. | The allowance for error in this experiment is very small.  这个实验的误差容限非常小。| synonyms: tolerance, margin, leeway | antonyms: limit, restriction | noun
