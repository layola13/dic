disintegrable:/dɪsˈɪntɪɡrəbl/
adjective
可瓦解的，可碎裂的
Capable of being disintegrated; breaking apart into smaller pieces or components.
The biodegradable and disintegrable plates are environmentally friendly. 这些可生物降解和可瓦解的盘子是环保的。
The tablet is disintegrable in water.  这药片在水中会分解。
近义词:breakable, crumbly, decomposable, fragile
反义词:stable, durable, unbreakable

词性: adjective

词源:  From disintegrate + -able.  Disintegrate comes from the Latin dis- meaning "apart" and integrare meaning "to make whole." Thus, disintegrable refers to something that can be made "not whole," i.e., broken apart.
