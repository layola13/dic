centerbit:/ˈsentərˌbɪt/| n.|中心位; 中间位|A bit in the center of a data structure or sequence.  It's most commonly used in computing contexts, particularly in relation to hash functions, encryption, or data compression. |The centerbit of the hash was crucial for verifying the data's integrity.  中心位的哈希值对于验证数据的完整性至关重要。|Synonym: middle bit, central bit|Antonym: edge bit, outer bit|noun
