nasiform:/ˈneɪzɪfɔːrm/| adj.|鼻形的，像鼻子的|Resembling a nose in shape. |例句：The nasiform bone is located in the nose. 鼻骨位于鼻子中。|例句：The insect had a curious nasiform projection on its head.  这只昆虫的头部有一个奇怪的像鼻子一样的突起。|近义词：nose-shaped, noselike|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'amorphous' in the context of lacking a defined shape)|词性: adjective
