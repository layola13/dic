migrants:/ˈmaɪɡrənts/| n. |移民，迁徙者|People who move from one place to another, especially in order to find work or better living conditions.|例句：The migrants travelled for days to reach the border. 这些移民走了好几天才到达边境。|近义词：immigrants,emigrants, settlers, newcomers|反义词:natives, residents, locals|noun

migrant:/ˈmaɪɡrənt/| adj. |迁徙的，流浪的|Moving from one place to another, especially in order to find work or better living conditions.|例句：Migrant workers often face difficult challenges. 流动工人经常面临严峻的挑战。|近义词：mobile, itinerant, nomadic, transitory|反义词:settled, resident, stationary|adjective
