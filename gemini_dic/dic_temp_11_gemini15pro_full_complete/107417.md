lammergeier:/ˈlæmərɡaɪər/ |ˈlæmərˌɡaɪər| |n.|胡兀鹫|A large Old World vulture (Gypaetus barbatus) with feathered legs and a habit of dropping bones on rocks to break them open and expose the marrow.|例句：The lammergeier soared high above the mountains. 胡兀鹫在群山上空翱翔。

例句：The lammergeier is known for its distinctive red facial markings. 胡兀鹫以其独特的红色面部标记而闻名。|近义词：bearded vulture, bone breaker|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


词源:
From German *Lämmergeier*, literally "lamb vulture", from *Lamm* (“lamb”) + *Geier* (“vulture”). Cognate with Dutch *lammergier*.
