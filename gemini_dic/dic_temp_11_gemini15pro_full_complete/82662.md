guess:/ɡɛs/| v. n.| 猜测，推测；猜想；认为；估计；料想 | To form an opinion or give an answer about something when you do not know much or anything about it. | I guessed her age to be about 35. 我估计她大约35岁。| synonyms: surmise, conjecture, speculate, hypothesize | antonyms: know, calculate, ascertain | verb

guess:/ɡɛs/| n.| 猜测，推测 | An attempt to give an answer or form an opinion when you do not know much or anything about something. | My guess is that she's about 35. 我猜她大约35岁。| synonyms: conjecture, supposition, hypothesis, speculation | antonyms: certainty, knowledge, fact | noun
