conditionally:/kənˈdɪʃənəli/| adv.|有条件地；附带条件地|In a way that depends on something else happening or being true.|例句：The offer of a job was made conditionally upon her receiving a satisfactory reference.  这份工作是有条件地提供的，取决于她是否能获得令人满意的推荐信。

例句：He was conditionally accepted to the university, pending his final grades. 他有条件地被大学录取，取决于他的最终成绩。|近义词：provisionally, contingently, subject to, depending on|反义词：unconditionally, absolutely, categorically|词性:副词
