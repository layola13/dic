bleariest:/ˈblɪəriɪst/| adj.|最模糊的，最朦胧的，最疲惫的|Most blurry, dim, or exhausted (especially in relation to vision).  It often implies a state of being tired or worn out, affecting one's ability to see clearly.|例句：After studying all night, I had the bleariest eyes.  熬夜学习后，我的眼睛非常模糊。

例句：The bleariest, most confusing movie I've ever seen. 我看过的最模糊、最令人困惑的电影。|近义词：blurred, dim, hazy, foggy, exhausted, weary, fatigued|反义词：clear, sharp, focused, bright, energetic, refreshed|词性:形容词

词源:
From *blear* (Old English *blǣr* "dim-sighted,") + *-y* (adjective forming suffix) + *-est* (superlative suffix).
