bankerly:/ˈbæŋkərli/
adjective
银行家的；像银行家的；谨慎的；精明的
characteristic of or befitting a banker; cautious; prudent
He gave me a bankerly look, assessing my creditworthiness. 他给了我一个银行家式的眼神，评估着我的信用度。
近义词: cautious, prudent, circumspect, shrewd, careful
反义词: reckless, imprudent, extravagant, careless
词性: adjective

bankerly:/ˈbæŋkərli/
adverb
像银行家一样；谨慎地；精明地
in the manner of a banker; cautiously; prudently
He invested his money bankerly, always looking for the safest option.  他像银行家一样谨慎地投资，总是寻找最安全的选择。
近义词: cautiously, prudently, circumspectly, shrewdly, carefully
反义词: recklessly, imprudently, extravagantly, carelessly
词性: adverb
