incliner: /ɪnˈklaɪnər/
n.|倾斜装置；斜坡输送机；倾向…的人|A mechanical device, often a conveyor belt, set at an incline to move objects up or down a slope. Also refers to people who have a tendency or preference for something.|The incliner made it easier for shoppers to move between floors.  这个倾斜装置使购物者更容易在楼层之间移动。|近义词：ramp, conveyor, slope|反义词：decline, leveler|词性:名词

Etymology: From the verb "incline" (meaning to lean or slope), which comes from the Latin "inclinare," a combination of "in-" (towards) and "clinare" (to bend).
