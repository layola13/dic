expositions:/ˌɛkspəˈzɪʃənz/
n.
展览会，博览会；阐述，讲解；暴露，显露
A public exhibition or show, often of artistic or industrial products.  A clear and detailed explanation of a theory, idea, or process. The act of exposing or making something visible.
The museum is hosting several expositions this summer.  这个夏天，博物馆将举办几场展览。
His exposition of the theory was clear and concise. 他对该理论的阐述清晰而简明。
The exposition of the fraud shocked the public.  诈骗行为的曝光震惊了公众。
近义词: exhibitions, displays, explanations, interpretations
反义词: concealments, coverings, hidings
词性: noun
