esada:/eɪˈsɑːdə/ | n. |辣酱油，辣酱| A highly seasoned sauce, typically made with chiles, tomatoes, and spices. It is often used in Mexican cuisine. | I love the flavor of esada on my tacos. 我喜欢玉米饼上的辣酱油的味道。|  The esada had a nice smoky flavor. 这辣酱油有一种很好的烟熏味。| synonyms: salsa, chili sauce | antonyms: bland sauce | noun
