bedroll:/ˈbɛdrəʊl/ (US) /ˈbedrəʊl/ (UK)| n. |睡袋；铺盖卷|A portable roll of bedding, often consisting of a blanket, sleeping bag, or other padded material, used especially outdoors or while camping.|He unfurled his bedroll under the starry sky. 他在星空下铺开他的铺盖卷。|近义词：sleeping bag, bedding, blankets|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "mattress" for a contrasting fixed sleeping arrangement)|noun
