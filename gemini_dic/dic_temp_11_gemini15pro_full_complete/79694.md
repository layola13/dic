glowlamp:/ˈɡloʊˌlæmp/| n.|辉光灯，霓虹灯|A glowlamp is an electric lamp that produces light by the glowing of a filament heated by an electric current.|The antique radio used a small glowlamp to illuminate the tuning dial. 古董收音机使用小型辉光灯照亮调谐刻度盘。|近义词：neon lamp, incandescent lamp|反义词: LED lamp|noun
