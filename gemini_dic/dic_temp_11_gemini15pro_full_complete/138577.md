NTSC:/ˈɛn tiː ɛs siː/ | n. | 国家电视系统委员会（National Television System Committee）; NTSC制式 |  A standard for the transmission of color television images used primarily in North America and Japan. |  My old TV is NTSC, so it won't work with this new DVD player. 我的旧电视是NTSC制式的，所以不能用这个新的DVD播放器。| synonym:  (loosely) PAL, SECAM | antonym:  (loosely) PAL, SECAM | noun
