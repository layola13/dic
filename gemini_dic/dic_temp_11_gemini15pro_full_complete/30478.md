centriscoid:/sɛnˈtrɪskɔɪd/
| adj. n.|尖吻鱼科的；尖吻鱼科鱼类|Relating to or denoting fishes of the family Centriscidae, which have a compressed, knifelike body and a long tubular snout.|例句：The centriscoid fish hovered near the seagrass. 尖吻鱼在海草附近盘旋。|近似词：snipefish, bellowfish,shrimpfish|反义词: (No direct antonym for a taxonomic classification)|形容词, 名词
