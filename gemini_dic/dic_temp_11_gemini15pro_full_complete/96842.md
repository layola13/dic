inevitables:/ɪˈnɛvɪtəblz/
| n. |不可避免的事情；必然发生的事情|Things that are certain to happen; unavoidable occurrences.|The inevitables of life, such as death and taxes, are difficult to accept.  生活中的不可避免的事情，例如死亡和税收，是难以接受的。|例句：The rising cost of living is one of the inevitables of modern life. 生活成本的上升是现代生活中不可避免的事情之一。

| | | |The company will have to face the inevitables of a shrinking market.  公司将不得不面对市场萎缩的必然结果。|近义词：certainties, necessities|反义词：avoidable things, improbabilities|词性:名词(plural)
