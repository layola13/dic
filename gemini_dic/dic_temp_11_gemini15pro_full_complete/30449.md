centred:/ˈsentəd/ (British English), /ˈsɛntərd/ (American English)| adj.|居中的，以…为中心的|Positioned in or around the centre; having the centre as a focal point.|The text is centred on the page.  文本在页面上居中。|The discussion was centred around the main issue. 讨论围绕主要问题展开。|近义词：central, middle, focused, concentrated|反义词：peripheral, off-center, eccentric|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From the verb "centre" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Centre" comes from the Old French "centre," and ultimately from the Latin "centrum," meaning "center point."
