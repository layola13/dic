inventoriable:/ɪnˈvɛntɔːriəbl/ | adj. | 可盘存的，可入库的 |  Suitable for being included in an inventory; that can be counted as an asset. | The company's inventoriable assets include raw materials, work-in-progress, and finished goods.  该公司可盘存资产包括原材料、在制品和成品。| Synonyms: stockable, countable | Antonyms: non-inventoriable, intangible | adjective
