oblateness:/əˈbleɪtnəs/| n.|扁率，扁圆形|The oblateness of the Earth is a measure of how much it bulges at the equator. 地球的扁率是衡量它在赤道处凸起的程度。|例句：The planet's oblateness is due to its rapid rotation.  这颗行星的扁率是由于它的快速旋转造成的。

例句：The oblateness of the spheroid can be calculated using this formula. 这个球体的扁率可以用这个公式计算。|近义词：flatness, compression|反义词：roundness, sphericity|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *oblātus, past participle of oblātere (“to flatten, widen”), from ob- (“against, toward”) + lātus (“wide, broad”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *stel- (“to spread”).
