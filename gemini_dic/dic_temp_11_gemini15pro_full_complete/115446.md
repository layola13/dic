manu:/ˈmænuː/
prefix
hand, make
From Latin manus "hand".  Forms the prefix of many words related to hands or making.
Manual dexterity is essential for a surgeon.  外科医生必须要有灵巧的双手。
近义词: hand-, handi-
反义词: None
词性: prefix
