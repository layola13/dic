Celsius: /ˈselsiəs/ | adj. n. |  摄氏的；摄氏度 |  Relating to, or having a scale of measurement on which water freezes at 0° and boils at 100°.  Also, a degree on this scale. |  The temperature today is 25 degrees Celsius. 今天气温是25摄氏度。 | synonym: centigrade | antonym: Fahrenheit | adjective, noun
