goliardery:/ˈɡoʊliɑːrdəri/
n.
放荡不羁的诗歌或歌曲; 狂欢作乐; 学生的恶作剧
Boisterous, often satirical or bawdy, verse or songs; revelry; student pranks.
The students' goliardery involved painting the dean's car pink. 学生们的恶作剧包括把系主任的车涂成粉红色。
Synonyms: revelry, carousing, merrymaking, debauchery, mischief
Antonyms: seriousness, sobriety, restraint, decorum
noun
