consumptive:/kənˈsʌmptɪv/
consumptive: | adj. n.| 消耗性的；肺痨的；肺病患者 | affected by or inclined to consumption (tuberculosis).  Characterized by or tending to cause consumption or waste. |  His consumptive cough worried his family. 他的肺痨咳嗽使家人担心。 | synonyms: wasteful, extravagant, devouring, phthisic, tubercular | antonyms: productive, conservative, saving | adjective
consumptive: | n. | 肺病患者 | a person with pulmonary tuberculosis (now historical). | In the 19th century, consumptives were often sent to sanatoriums.  在19世纪，肺结核病人经常被送到疗养院。| synonyms:  tubercular patient | antonyms: healthy person | noun
