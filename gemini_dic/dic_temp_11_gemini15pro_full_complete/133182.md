nonevasive:/ˌnɒnɪˈveɪsɪv/ | adj. | 非侵入性的，非侵略性的 | Not invasive; not entering the body, not aggressive or intrusive. | The doctor recommended a nonevasive procedure to diagnose the problem. 医生建议采用非侵入性程序来诊断问题。| The interview was nonevasive and comfortable.  面试过程轻松随意，没有压迫感。 | 近义词: nonintrusive, nonaggressive | 反义词: invasive, aggressive | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "evasive" (from Latin "evadere" - to escape, evade)
