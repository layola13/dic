bungle:/ˈbʌŋɡəl/| v. n.|笨拙地做；搞砸；拙劣的工作|To carry out (a task) clumsily or incompetently, leading to failure or an unsatisfactory outcome.  A clumsy or inept performance; a botch.|He bungled the presentation and lost the contract. 他搞砸了演示，失去了合同。|Synonyms: mishandle, botch, mess up, boggle, fumble|Antonyms:  succeed, achieve, master, ace, triumph|verb
