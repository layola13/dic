inerrableness:/ɪˌnɛrəb(ə)lnəs/
n.
无谬性，不会出错
The quality of being inerrable; inability to err.
The inerrableness of the Pope's pronouncements on matters of faith is a central tenet of Catholicism.  教皇在信仰问题上声明的无谬性是天主教的核心信条。
近义词：infallibility, impeccability
反义词：fallibility, errability
词性：名词

inerrable:/ɪˈnɛrəb(ə)l/
adj.
不会出错的，无谬的
Not capable of erring; infallible.
Some believe the Bible to be an inerrable document.  有些人认为圣经是一份不会出错的文件。
近义词：infallible, impeccable
反义词：fallible, errable
词性：形容词

词源:
From Late Latin inerrabilis, from in- ‘not’ + errare ‘to err’.
