flintlock: /ˈflɪnt.lɒk/ (UK) /ˈflɪnt.lɑːk/ (US)| n. |燧发枪，燧发机|A type of gunlock that uses a piece of flint to create a spark to ignite the gunpowder.|The old musket was equipped with a flintlock.  这把老式步枪配备了燧发枪机。|Synonyms: gunlock, firelock|Antonyms: matchlock, wheellock|noun

flintlocks: /ˈflɪnt.lɒks/ (UK) /ˈflɪnt.lɑːks/ (US)| n. |燧发枪，燧发机（复数）|Plural form of flintlock, referring to multiple gunlocks that use flint.|The museum displayed a collection of antique flintlocks.  博物馆展出了一系列古董燧发枪。|Synonyms: gunlocks, firelocks|Antonyms: matchlocks, wheellocks|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From flint + lock (as in the locking mechanism).  The term refers to the use of a flint striking steel to create the spark.  Dates back to the early 17th century.
