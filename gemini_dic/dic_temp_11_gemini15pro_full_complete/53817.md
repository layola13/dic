disconnectedly:/ˌdɪskəˈnɛktɪdli/
|adv.|断断续续地，不连贯地|Characterized by a lack of connection or continuity.  Thoughts or speech that jump from one topic to another without a clear transition are described as disconnected.|She spoke disconnectedly, jumping from one topic to another.|incoherently, disjointedly, ramblingly|connectedly, coherently|adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From disconnect + -ed + -ly.  Disconnect comes from dis- (not) + connect (to join or link).
