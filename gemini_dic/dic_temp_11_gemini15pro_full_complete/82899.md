gum-up-the-works:/ɡʌm ʌp ðə wɜːrks/
verb phrase
把事情搞砸；阻碍工作
to cause something to stop working or to become less effective
The computer system gummed up the works and prevented us from finishing the project on time.  计算机系统瘫痪了，使我们无法按时完成项目。
gum up the works, screw up, foul up, mess up, disrupt, obstruct
facilitate, expedite, smooth, assist
