hyperconservativeness:/ˌhaɪpərˈkənˈsɜːrvətɪvnəs/ |n.|极端保守主义|The state of being extremely conservative, marked by resistance to change and adherence to traditional values. |His hyperconservativeness prevented him from accepting any new ideas. 他的极端保守主义使他无法接受任何新思想。|Synonyms: ultraconservatism, reactionism, right-wing extremism|Antonyms: progressivism, liberalism, radicalism|Noun

hyperconservative:/ˌhaɪpərˈkənˈsɜːrvətɪv/ |adj.|极端保守的|Extremely conservative, resistant to change and adhering to traditional values. |The hyperconservative wing of the party blocked any attempts at reform. 该党极端保守的派系阻止了任何改革尝试。|Synonyms: ultraconservative, reactionary, right-wing|Antonyms: progressive, liberal, radical|Adjective


Etymology:
"hyper-" (Greek): over, above, beyond
"conservative" (Latin): conservare - to preserve, keep
