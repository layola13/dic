obsecration:/ˌɒbsɪˈkreɪʃn/ | n. |恳求，哀求，祈求 | An earnest or insistent request, especially one made in a humble and urgent manner. |  The prisoner's obsecration for mercy went unanswered. 囚犯的求饶没有得到回应。

The villagers offered obsecrations to the gods for rain. 村民们向神明祈求降雨。| 近义词：supplication, entreaty, plea, appeal, petition | 反义词：demand, command, order | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Latin *obsecrātiō*, from *obsecrāre* "to beseech," from *ob-* "toward" + *sacrāre* "to make sacred, dedicate," from *sacer* "sacred."
