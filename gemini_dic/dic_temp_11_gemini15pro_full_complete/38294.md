concavity:/kɒnˈkævɪti/ /kɑːnˈkævɪti/ | n. |凹陷，凹度|The state of being concave; an inward curve or depression. |The concavity of the lens helps to focus light. 镜片的凹陷有助于聚焦光线。|The concavity of the spoon allowed the liquid to pool. 勺子的凹陷使液体汇集。 |synonyms: depression, hollow, indentation|antonyms: convexity, bulge, protuberance|noun
