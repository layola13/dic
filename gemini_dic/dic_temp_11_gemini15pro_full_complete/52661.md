digitises:/ˈdɪdʒɪtaɪzɪz/ | v. | 数字化，将…数字化 | Converts analog information into digital form. |  例句：The library is digitising its collection of old manuscripts.  图书馆正在将其收藏的旧手稿数字化。

例句：Many companies are digitising their records to save space and improve efficiency. 许多公司正在将他们的记录数字化，以节省空间并提高效率。
