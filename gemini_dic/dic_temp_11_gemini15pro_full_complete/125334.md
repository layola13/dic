multilateralist:/ˌmʌltɪˈlætərəlɪst/| n.|多边主义者|A person who supports multilateralism, the principle of cooperation between multiple countries.|例句：The new president is a staunch multilateralist, believing in international cooperation.  这位新总统是一位坚定的多边主义者，信奉国际合作。

例句：Multilateralists argue that global challenges require global solutions. 多边主义者认为，全球挑战需要全球解决方案。|近义词：internationalist, globalist|反义词：unilateralist, isolationist|词性:noun

multilateral:/ˌmʌltɪˈlætərəl/| adj.|多边的|Involving more than two countries or parties.|例句：The multilateral trade agreement reduced tariffs between the participating nations.  多边贸易协定降低了参与国之间的关税。

例句：Multilateral discussions are crucial for addressing climate change. 多边讨论对于应对气候变化至关重要。|近义词：plurilateral, joint|反义词：bilateral, unilateral|词性:adjective
