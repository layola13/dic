darings:/ˈdeərɪŋz/| n. |勇敢的行为，冒险的行为|Acts of courage or boldness; adventurous undertakings. |She was known for her darings on the ski slopes. 她以在滑雪坡上的冒险行为而闻名。|  He recounted tales of his youthful darings. 他讲述了他年轻时的冒险故事。 |近义词：adventures, exploits, feats, risks, ventures|反义词: cautions, hesitations|词性:名词

词源: From the verb "dare" (Old English *durran, "to dare"), + "-ing" suffix forming a gerund or present participle, which later became a noun.
