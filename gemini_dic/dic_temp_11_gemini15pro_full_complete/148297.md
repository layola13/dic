oversoftly:/ˈoʊvərˈsɒftli/ /ˈoʊvərˈsɑːftli/| adv.|过于柔和地，过分温柔地|In a way that is too soft; excessively softly.  It combines "over" (meaning excessively) and "softly" (meaning in a gentle or quiet manner).|She spoke oversoftly, and I could barely hear her. 她说话太轻了，我几乎听不见。|The music played oversoftly, barely a whisper in the air.  音乐播放得过于轻柔，在空气中几乎是耳语。|近义词：too softly, excessively softly|反义词：loudly, harshly|词性: 副词
