mass-marketing:/ˈmæs ˈmɑːrkɪtɪŋ/ /ˈmæs ˈmɑːrkətɪŋ/| n.|大众营销|The advertising and selling of goods or services on a large scale, usually using mass media such as television, radio, and newspapers, and aiming to reach the largest possible audience.|Many companies rely on mass-marketing techniques to reach a wide customer base. 许多公司依靠大众营销技术来接触广泛的客户群。|mass advertising, mass distribution|niche marketing, targeted marketing|noun
mass-market:/ˈmæs ˈmɑːrkɪt/ /ˈmæs ˈmɑːrkət/| n. adj.|大众市场|The market for goods that are produced in large quantities and sold at low prices to a large number of people.|The company aims its products at the mass market. 该公司的产品针对大众市场。|general market, consumer market|niche market, specialist market|noun, adjective
mass-marketed:/ˌmæs ˈmɑːrkɪtɪd/ /ˌmæs ˈmɑːrkətɪd/ | adj.|大众市场化的|Produced in large quantities and sold at low prices to a large number of people.|Mass-marketed products are often seen as less exclusive. 大众市场化的产品通常被认为不那么独特。|mass-produced, widely available|exclusive, niche|adjective
