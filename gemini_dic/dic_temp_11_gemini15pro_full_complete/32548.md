chipped-in:/ˌtʃɪpt ˈɪn/ | v. | 凑钱；捐款；插嘴； | To contribute a small amount of money along with others for a particular purpose; to interrupt a conversation. |  Everyone chipped in for Sarah's birthday present.  大家凑钱买了莎拉的生日礼物。

We all chipped in to buy a new coffee machine for the office. 我们都凑钱给办公室买了一台新的咖啡机。

| 近义词：contribute, donate, interject | 反义词：withdraw, withhold | 词性: 动词
