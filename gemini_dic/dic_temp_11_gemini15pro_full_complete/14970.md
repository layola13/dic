The rampant baalism of the 1980s led to a period of economic excess.  20世纪80年代猖獗的拜金主义导致了一段时间的经济过剩。 | 近义词: idolatry, materialism, mammonism | 反义词: asceticism, self-denial | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Baal + -ism. Baal is the name of a Canaanite god. The suffix -ism denotes a doctrine, belief, or practice.
