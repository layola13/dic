golem: /ˈɡoʊləm/ (US) /ˈɡɒlɛm/ (UK)| n. |魔像，泥人|An artificial being from Jewish folklore, magically created from inanimate matter, often clay or mud. Typically depicted as a humanoid figure, golems are animated by mystical means and serve their creators. |The rabbi created a golem to protect the Jewish community.|近义词: automaton, robot, android|反义词: human, living being| noun

golem: /ˈɡoʊləm/ (US) /ˈɡɒlɛm/ (UK)| n. |笨拙的人，受人操纵的人|A person who acts clumsily or is easily controlled by others. This usage is a metaphorical extension of the original meaning. |He felt like a golem, blindly following orders without questioning them.|近义词: puppet, drone, stooge|反义词: free thinker, independent| noun
