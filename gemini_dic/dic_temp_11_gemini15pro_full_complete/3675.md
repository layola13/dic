aggrandization:/əˌɡrænˈdaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 扩张；夸大；提高 | the act of increasing the power, status, or wealth of something or someone | His writings are filled with self-aggrandization. 他的作品充满了自我吹嘘。 | synonyms: elevation, enhancement, exaggeration, magnification | antonyms: diminution, reduction, understatement | noun

aggrandize:/əˈɡrænˌdaɪz/ | vt. |  增大；夸大；强化 | to increase the power, status, or wealth of something or someone | He tends to aggrandize his achievements. 他倾向于夸大他的成就。 | synonyms: augment, boost, enlarge, exalt | antonyms: belittle, depreciate, diminish, minimize | verb
