doomily:/ˈduːmɪli/
adverb|忧郁地，不祥地|in a gloomy or pessimistic manner, suggesting an unhappy fate|He shook his head doomily. 他忧郁地摇了摇头。|例句：The rain fell doomily, mirroring his mood. 雨阴沉地下着，映照着他的心情。|近义词：gloomily, pessimistically, despondently, morbidly|反义词：optimistically, cheerfully, hopefully|词性:副词
词源: From doom + -ly.
