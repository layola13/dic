misbehaves:/ˌmɪsbɪˈheɪvz/
verb
行为不端；举止失礼
to act in a bad manner
The child misbehaves whenever he is tired.  这个孩子每当他累了的时候就表现不好。
My computer has been misbehaving recently. 我的电脑最近一直出问题。
synonyms: act up, misconduct oneself,  behave badly
antonyms: behave, obey, comply
词性: 动词
