consequency:/ˈkɒnsɪkwənsi/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsɪkwənsi/ (US)| n. |结果，后果；重要性|a result or effect of something; the state or quality of being important|The consequency of her actions was a loss of trust. 她的行为导致了信任的丧失。|Synonyms: result, outcome, effect, ramification, aftermath|Antonyms: cause, origin, source|noun

consequent:/ˈkɒnsɪkwənt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsɪkwent/ (US)| adj. |作为结果的，随之发生的|following as a result or effect|Her consequent actions proved she was remorseful. 她随后的行为证明她很后悔。|Synonyms: resulting, ensuing, subsequent, following|Antonyms: preceding, prior, antecedent|adjective
