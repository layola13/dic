overlisten: /ˌoʊvərˈlɪsən/ | v. | 过度聆听；听得过多 | To listen to something excessively, often to the point of fatigue or obsession. |  He overlistened to the song and now he can't stand it. 他过度聆听了这首歌，现在他无法忍受它了。| She overlistened to the news and became overly anxious. 她过度收听了新闻，变得过度焦虑。 | 近义词: obsess over, dwell on | 反义词: ignore, neglect | 词性: 动词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "listen" (verb).  "Over-" comes from Old English "ofer," and "listen" comes from Old English "hlystan."
