appropriators:/əˈproʊprieɪtərz/
n.
拨款者；占用者；挪用者
Those who set aside money or other resources for a specific purpose, or those who take something for their own use, often without permission.
The appropriators in Congress debated the funding for the new highway project. 国会拨款者就新公路项目的资金进行了辩论。

The artist accused the company of being appropriators of his original designs.  艺术家指责该公司盗用了他的原创设计。

近义词: funders, investors, claimants, usurpers
反义词: donors, contributors, providers

词性: noun (plural)
词源: From the verb "appropriate," which comes from the Latin "appropriare," meaning "to make one's own."
