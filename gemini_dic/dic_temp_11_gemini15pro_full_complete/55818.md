divesture:/daɪˈvɛstʃər/
n.|剥离，撤资|The company is undergoing a divesture of its non-core assets. 该公司正在剥离其非核心资产。|
近义词：sale, disposal, divestiture
反义词：acquisition, investment
词性: 名词

divest:/daɪˈvɛst/
vt.|剥夺，使脱去，卸下|The court divested him of his parental rights. 法院剥夺了他的父母权利。|
近义词：deprive, strip, rid
反义词：invest, endow
词性: 动词


词源:  From Late Latin *divestire, from Latin de- + vestire (“to clothe”).  Essentially, the opposite of "invest".
