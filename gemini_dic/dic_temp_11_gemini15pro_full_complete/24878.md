bru:/bruː/| interj.|兄弟，哥们儿 (South African informal)|A familiar term of address for a male friend, similar to "bro" or "mate".  Derived from Afrikaans.|Example: Bru, howzit going? 兄弟，最近怎么样？|Example:  Just chilling here, bru.  就在这儿放松呢，兄弟。|近义词：bro, mate, dude, pal|反义词: (No direct antonym)|interjection
