half-quixotically:/ˌhæf kwɪkˈsɒtɪkli/ | adv. | 半堂吉诃德式地；半浪漫主义地；半不切实际地 |  In a way that is partly impractical, idealistic, or romantic, resembling the character Don Quixote.  It suggests a mixture of idealism and practicality, not fully embracing the impracticality of quixotry. | He half-quixotically decided to build a windmill in his backyard. 他有点不切实际地决定在后院建一个风车。 |  idealistically, romantically, impractically, chivalrously | pragmatically, realistically, sensibly | adverb
