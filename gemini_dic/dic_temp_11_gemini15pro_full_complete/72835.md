folioing: /ˈfoʊlioʊɪŋ/
verb
连续编号;  给…标页码
The librarian is folioing a rare collection of manuscripts. 图书管理员正在为一套珍稀的手稿进行连续编号。
She carefully folioed each page of the document. 她仔细地为文件的每一页标注页码。
English Explanation:  The act of numbering the pages of a document or book, especially consecutively.  It can also refer to the act of arranging or organizing something in the manner of a folio (a large book or manuscript).
Example: He spent hours meticulously folioing the ancient texts.  他花了几个小时精心为古代文献标页码。
Example: The archivist was folioing a collection of 18th-century letters.  档案管理员正在为一批18世纪的信件进行连续编号。
Synonym: numbering, paging, paginating
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider "disorganizing" in a broad sense)
word class: verb


Etymology:  From the noun "folio," ultimately from Latin "folium" meaning "leaf."
