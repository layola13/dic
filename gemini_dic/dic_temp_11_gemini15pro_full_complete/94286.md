impersuadable:/ˌɪmpərˈsweɪdəbl/
adjective
不可说服的，固执的
Not able to be persuaded; stubborn.
He was utterly impersuadable on the subject of his daughter's marriage. 他在女儿的婚姻问题上完全无法被说服。
Synonyms: inflexible, unyielding, adamant, obstinate, intractable
Antonyms: persuadable, flexible, amenable, compliant
词性: 形容词
