outgabbles:/ˌaʊtˈɡæbəlz/
|v.| 比…更健谈；胜过…的饶舌 |outtalks someone; surpasses someone in garrulity.| He outgabbled everyone at the party, holding the floor for hours. 他在聚会上比所有人都健谈，滔滔不绝地讲了几个小时。|Synonyms: outtalks, outspeaks |Antonyms:  is outtalked by, is quieter than |verb


Etymology:
"out-" (prefix indicating surpassing or exceeding) + "gabble" (verb meaning to talk rapidly and unintelligibly)
