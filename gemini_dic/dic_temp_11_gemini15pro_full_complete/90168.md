hotline:/ˈhɒtlaɪn/ (UK) /ˈhɑːtlaɪn/ (US)| n.|热线，专线|A direct telephone line for a specific purpose, especially for use in emergencies or for reporting information.|例句：I called the domestic violence hotline for help. 我打电话给家庭暴力热线寻求帮助。

例句：The company has set up a hotline for customer complaints.  这家公司设立了客户投诉热线。|近义词：helpline, direct line, emergency line|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
