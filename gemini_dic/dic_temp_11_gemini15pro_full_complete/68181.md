faintish:/ˈfeɪntɪʃ/| adj.|有点晕的，略感虚弱的|Slightly faint; feeling weak or dizzy.|例句：I felt a bit faintish after standing up too quickly.  我站起来太快了，感觉有点晕。|近义词：dizzy, lightheaded, weak, queasy|反义词:strong, healthy|词性:形容词

faintish:/ˈfeɪntɪʃ/| adj.|略有...的；隐约的|Having a slight trace of a quality or characteristic.|例句：There was a faintish smell of smoke in the air. 空气中隐约闻到一股烟味。|近义词：slight, subtle, indistinct, faint|反义词:strong, obvious, distinct|词性:形容词
