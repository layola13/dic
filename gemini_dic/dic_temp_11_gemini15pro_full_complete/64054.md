epitomise:/ɪˈpɪtəmaɪz/ /əˈpɪtəˌmaɪz/ | v. | 体现；是…的典型；概括 | To be a perfect example of a quality or type of thing. |  This building epitomises the spirit of the modern age. 这座建筑体现了现代精神。

Her work epitomises elegance and grace. 她的作品是优雅和精致的典范。


近义词: embody, represent, exemplify, typify | 反义词: misrepresent, contradict | 词性: verb
