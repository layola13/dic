mota:/ˈmoʊtə/ (US), /ˈməʊtə/ (UK)| n.| 斑点，污点，瑕疵；(尤指羊毛、丝绸等上的) 杂色；微粒，尘埃 |A small mark, spot, or speck, especially on wool, silk, or other fabrics; a mote. Also, a tiny piece of dust or other particle.|The expert examined the fabric for any motas that might lower its value. 专家检查了织物上是否有任何可能降低其价值的斑点。|Synonyms: speck, fleck, spot, blemish, particle|Antonyms: clarity, cleanness|Noun
