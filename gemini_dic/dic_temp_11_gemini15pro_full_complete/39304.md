conidium:/kəˈnɪdiəm/ (plural conidia: /kəˈnɪdiə/) | n. | 分生孢子 | An asexual spore produced on a conidiophore of certain fungi.  It is not enclosed within a sac. | The conidia of this fungus are dispersed by wind.  这种真菌的分生孢子靠风传播。 | synonyms: asexual spore, mitospore | antonyms: sexual spore, meiospore | noun
