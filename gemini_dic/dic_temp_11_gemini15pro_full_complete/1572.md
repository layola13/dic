achkan:/ɑːtʃkɑːn/ | n. | 长袍，长衫，长礼服 | A knee-length coat buttoning down the front, worn especially on formal occasions in India.  | Example 1: He wore a beautifully embroidered achkan to the wedding. 他穿着一件绣花精美的长衫出席婚礼。

Example 2: The groom's achkan was made of rich silk. 新郎的长袍是用华丽的丝绸制成的。 | Near Synonyms: sherwani, coat, robe | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps casual wear) | Noun
