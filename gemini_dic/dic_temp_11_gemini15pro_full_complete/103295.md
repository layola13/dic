jibe's:/dʒaɪbz/ | v. | (意见、故事等)一致，相符 |  To jibe means to be in agreement or accord; to correspond. | "Her account of the events didn't jibe with his." 她对事件的描述与他的不相符。| synonyms: agree, correspond, concur, match | antonyms: disagree, clash, conflict | verb

注意事项:
"jibe's" itself is not a standard English word. "Jibe" can be a noun or a verb. The possessive form "jibe's" would only be used if "jibe" is used as a noun, such as "the jibe's cruelty was shocking." (这个嘲讽的残酷令人震惊。)  However, this is less common than using "jibe" as a verb.
