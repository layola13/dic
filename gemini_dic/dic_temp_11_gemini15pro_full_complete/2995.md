aeolipyle: /ˈiːəˌlaɪpaɪl/ | n. | 蒸汽机，汽转球 | A hollow metal sphere with attached bent tubes through which steam escapes, causing it to rotate. An early example of a steam engine, demonstrating the principle of jet propulsion. | The museum displayed a working model of an aeolipyle, fascinating the visitors. 博物馆展出了一个工作的汽转球模型，吸引了参观者。 |  The aeolipyle spun rapidly, demonstrating the power of steam. 汽转球快速旋转，展示了蒸汽的力量。 | synonyms: steam turbine (in a broad sense), reaction turbine | antonyms: N/A | noun
