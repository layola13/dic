coinheritance:/ˌkoʊɪnˈhɛrɪtəns/| n.|共同继承，共遗传|The simultaneous inheritance of two or more characteristics from a common ancestor.  The process by which two or more genes are located close together on the same chromosome and tend to be inherited together.|Researchers are studying the coinheritance of eye color and hair color.|近义词：joint inheritance, linked inheritance|反义词:|noun
