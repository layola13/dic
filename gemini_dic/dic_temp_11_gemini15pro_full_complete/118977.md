methadone:/ˈmɛθəˌdoʊn/ | n. | 美沙酮 | A synthetic opioid drug used as a pain reliever and as a substitute for heroin or other opioid drugs in the treatment of addiction. |  The doctor prescribed methadone to help him overcome his heroin addiction. 医生给他开了美沙酮来帮助他克服海洛因成瘾。| synonyms: Dolophine, Amidone | antonyms:  naloxone, naltrexone (These are opioid antagonists, not true opposites but used to counteract methadone's effects) | noun
