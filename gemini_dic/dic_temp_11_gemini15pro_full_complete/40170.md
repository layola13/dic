contented:/kənˈtɛntɪd/ | adj. | 满足的，满意的 | Feeling or showing satisfaction with one's possessions, status, or situation. | She seemed utterly contented with her life. 她似乎对自己的生活非常满意。 |  He led a simple, contented life. 他过着简朴而满足的生活。| 近义词: satisfied, happy, cheerful, pleased | 反义词: discontented, unhappy, dissatisfied | 词性: adjective

词源: From Late Middle English *contenten*, from Old French *contenter* (to satisfy), from Latin *contentus* (contained, held together, satisfied), past participle of *continēre* (to contain).
