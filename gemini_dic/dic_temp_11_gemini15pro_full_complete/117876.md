melodrama: /ˈmeləˌdrɑːmə/ (US), /ˈmeləˌdrɑːmə/ (UK) | n. |情节剧；音乐剧；夸张的事件|A sensational dramatic piece with exaggerated characters and exciting events intended to appeal to the emotions.|The play was a pure melodrama, full of improbable coincidences and overwrought emotions. 这部戏纯粹是情节剧，充满了不可能的巧合和过度夸张的情感。|近义词：soap opera, dramatic play|反义词：documentary, realism|词性: noun

melodramatic: /ˌmelədrəˈmætɪk/ (US), /ˌmelədrəˈmætɪk/ (UK) | adj. | 情节剧的；夸张的；感情过分渲染的|Characteristic of melodrama, especially in being exaggerated, sensationalized, or overemotional.|Her reaction was so melodramatic; she acted as if the world was ending. 她的反应太夸张了，表现得好像世界末日一样。|近义词：histrionic, theatrical, exaggerated|反义词：understated, realistic, subtle|词性: adjective
