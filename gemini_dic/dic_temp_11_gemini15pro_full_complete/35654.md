cob:/kɒb/ (UK) /kɑːb/ (US)| n.|玉米棒子；雄天鹅；矮壮的马；圆块|A corn cob is the central core of an ear of corn. 雄天鹅通常体型较大，保护性强。|The farmer shucked the corn and tossed the cob aside. 农民剥了玉米，把玉米棒子扔到一边。|近义词：core (for corn), swan (for male swan), pony (for horse)|反义词: (No direct antonym for cob)|noun

cob:/kɒb/ (UK) /kɑːb/ (US)| v.|用泥块涂抹；拍打；扔|To cob is to build or repair something with cobs (lumps of clay, mud, and straw).  也可以表示“拍打”或“扔”。|They cobbed the walls of the cottage with a mixture of clay and straw. 他们用黏土和稻草的混合物涂抹小屋的墙壁。|近义词：daub, plaster|反义词: (No direct antonym for cob as a verb)|verb
