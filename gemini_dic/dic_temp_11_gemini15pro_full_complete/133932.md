nonhardy:/nɒnˈhɑːdi/ | adj. | 非耐寒的；不耐霜的 | Not able to survive cold weather, especially frost. | Example: Nonhardy plants need to be brought indoors during the winter. 非耐寒植物需要在冬天搬到室内。| Example:  Many tropical flowers are nonhardy in northern climates. 许多热带花卉在北方气候下不耐寒。 | 近义词:tender, delicate | 反义词: hardy, robust | 词性: 形容词
