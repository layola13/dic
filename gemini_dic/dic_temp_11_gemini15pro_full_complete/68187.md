faintness:/ˈfeɪntnəs/
n.
微弱,昏厥,虚弱
the state of being faint, lacking strength, or about to lose consciousness.
She felt a sudden faintness and had to sit down. 她突然感到一阵虚弱，不得不坐下来。
近义词：dizziness, weakness, giddiness, lightheadedness
反义词：strength, vigor, robustness
词性:名词

faintness:/ˈfeɪntnəs/
n.
模糊,不清楚
lack of clarity or distinctness.
There was a faintness in his voice that made it hard to hear. 他的声音很微弱，很难听到。
近义词：indistinctness, vagueness, dimness
反义词：clarity, distinctness, sharpness
词性:名词


词源: From Middle English *feintenesse*,  from Old French *feintise* (“fiction, pretense”), from *feint*, past participle of *feindre* (“to feign”). Ultimately from Latin *fingō*
