dinnerless:/ˈdɪnərlɪs/| adj.|没有晚餐的，未进晚餐的|Without dinner; having no dinner.|例句：The travelers arrived late and dinnerless at the inn. 旅行者们很晚才到达旅店，没有吃晚饭。|近义词：hungry, unfed, fasting|反义词:fed, full, satiated|词性：形容词

Etymology: dinner + -less.  "Dinner" comes from Old French disner, meaning "breakfast," evolving to mean the main meal of the day.  The suffix "-less" is from Old English -lēas, meaning "without."
