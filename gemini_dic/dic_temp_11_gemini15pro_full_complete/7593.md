announces:/əˈnaʊnsɪz/
verb
宣布，宣告
make a public and typically formal declaration about a fact, occurrence, or intention.
The company announced its new product line.  公司宣布了新的产品线。
The government announced new tax cuts. 政府宣布了新的减税政策。
synonym: declare, proclaim, pronounce, report
antonym: conceal, withhold, suppress
词性: 动词
