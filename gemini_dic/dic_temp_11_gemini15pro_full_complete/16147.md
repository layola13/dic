banishing:/ˈbænɪʃɪŋ/| v. (present participle)|驱逐，消除，放逐|The act of sending someone or something away, especially from a country or place; the act of getting rid of something unpleasant or unwanted.|例句：He was punished by banishing him from the kingdom. 他被惩罚，被驱逐出王国。|例句：She tried banishing the thought from her mind. 她试图把这个想法从脑海中消除。|近义词：exiling, expelling, ousting, dismissing, eradicating|反义词：welcoming, inviting, admitting, restoring|词性:动词
