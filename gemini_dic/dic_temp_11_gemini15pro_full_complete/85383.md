harshnesses:/ˈhɑːrʃnɪsɪz/ /ˈhɑːrʃnəsɪz/
noun
严酷；残酷；粗糙；刺耳
the quality of being harsh or unpleasant
例句：The harshnesses of the winter climate made life difficult. 冬天气候的严酷使得生活变得艰难。

例句：She was shocked by the harshnesses of his words. 她被他言语的刻薄吓到了。

近义词：severity, austerity, roughness, sharpness, cruelty, unkindness
反义词：gentleness, kindness, softness, mildness
词性:n.
