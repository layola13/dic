misconceives:/ˌmɪskənˈsiːvz/
verb
误解，错误地理解
To interpret or understand (something) incorrectly.
She completely misconceives the nature of our relationship. 她完全误解了我们关系的性质。
misunderstand, misinterpret, misapprehend
understand correctly, interpret accurately, grasp
