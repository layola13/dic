conducent:/kənˈdʌsənt/ | adj. | 传导的；有传导力的 | Having the power to conduct or transmit. |  Heat is more conducent through copper than wood.  热量通过铜比通过木头更容易传导。|  Conducent materials allow electricity to flow easily.  导电材料允许电流轻易通过。| 近义词: conductive, transmitting | 反义词: nonconductive, insulating | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Latin *conducēns*, present participle of conducere ("to lead or bring together"), from con- ("together") + dūcere ("to lead").
