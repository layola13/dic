injurable:/ˈɪndʒərəbl/
adjective
可伤害的, 易受伤的
able to be injured.
That glass is injurable, so put it away from children.  那块玻璃易碎，把它放到孩子们够不到的地方。
He was involved in an accident that left him injurable for months. 他遭遇了一场事故，让他几个月都行动不便。
近义词: vulnerable, susceptible, damageable
反义词: invulnerable, indestructible, unbreakable
词性: adjective
词源: From injure + -able.  来自 injure (伤害) + -able (可...的)
