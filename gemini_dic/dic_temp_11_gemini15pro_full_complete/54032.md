discusses: /dɪˈskʌsɪz/
verb
讨论，谈论
Third-person singular present tense form of discuss.  It means to talk or write about a topic in detail, especially considering different ideas or opinions.
They discuss the project every week. 他们每周都会讨论这个项目。
The book discusses the history of the Roman Empire. 这本书探讨了罗马帝国的历史。
近义词: debates, talks over, converses about, examines, considers
反义词: ignores, avoids, disregards
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *discussen*, from Late Latin *discussare* "to examine, investigate," frequentative of Latin *discutere* "to shake apart, disperse, scatter," from *dis-* "apart" + *quatere* "to shake."
