nozzle:/ˈnɒzl/ (UK) /ˈnɑːzl/ (US)|n.|喷嘴; 管嘴|A short tube with a锥形末端, used to control the direction and flow of a liquid or gas. |The gardener attached a nozzle to the hose. 园丁把喷嘴装在水管上。|The fire fighter directed the nozzle of the hose at the flames. 消防员将水管的喷嘴对准火焰。|近义词:jet, spout, tip|反义词: (no direct antonym, perhaps inlet, opening)|词性:名词
