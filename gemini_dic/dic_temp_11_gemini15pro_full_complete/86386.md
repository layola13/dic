hedgehog:/ˈhedʒ.hɒɡ/ (UK) /ˈhedʒ.hɑːɡ/ (US)| n.|刺猬|A small, nocturnal mammal with spiny hairs covering its back and sides, which it rolls into a ball for protection.|The hedgehog scurried across the lawn. 刺猬匆匆穿过草坪。|Synonyms: porcupine (similar, but larger and with quills), urchin|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the animal itself)|noun


hedgehog:/ˈhedʒ.hɒɡ/ (UK) /ˈhedʒ.hɑːɡ/ (US)| v.|（使）采取保守的措施；避免风险|To protect oneself or one's investments from loss by taking mitigating actions.|The company hedged its bets by investing in a variety of stocks.  这家公司通过投资各种股票来规避风险。|Synonyms: safeguard, protect, cushion, insure|Antonyms: risk, gamble, expose|verb
