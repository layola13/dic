luckless:/ˈlʌklɪs/| adj.|不幸的，倒霉的|Unfortunate; having bad luck.|He was a luckless gambler who always lost. 他是一个运气不好的赌徒，总是输。|例句：The luckless expedition ended in disaster.  这支不幸的探险队以灾难告终。

例句：She seemed to be a luckless individual, constantly facing setbacks. 她似乎是一个很倒霉的人，总是遭遇挫折。|近义词：unfortunate, unlucky, hapless, ill-fated|反义词：lucky, fortunate, successful|词性:形容词

词源: From "luck" + "-less" (suffix indicating lack of). 源自“luck”（运气）+“-less”（后缀，表示缺乏）。
