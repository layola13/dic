cero:/ˈsɛroʊ/ (US), /ˈsɪəroʊ/ (UK)| adj. n.|零，零点|A quantity of zero; nothing.|The temperature dropped to cero. 温度降到了零度。|近义词：nil, null, zero, naught|反义词: many, numerous, several|词性:形容词, 名词
Etymology: From Spanish *cero*, from Latin *zerum*, from Arabic *صِفْر* (*ṣifr*, “empty, nothing, cipher”).
