heulandite:/ˈhjuːləndaɪt/ | n. | 斜沸石 | A hydrous calcium and aluminum silicate mineral of the zeolite group. It typically occurs as white or colorless crystals. | Heulandite is a common mineral found in volcanic rocks.  斜沸石是一种常见的火山岩矿物。| Heulandite can be used as a molecular sieve. 斜沸石可以用作分子筛。 |  近义词: stilbite, clinoptilolite | 反义词:  None (No direct antonym for a specific mineral) | noun
