narcotic: /nɑːrˈkɒtɪk/ (UK) /nɑːrˈkɑːtɪk/ (US)| n. adj.|麻醉剂，麻醉药; 令人麻醉的|A drug or other substance affecting mood or behavior and sold for nonmedical purposes, especially an illegal one.  Also, relating to or denoting narcotics or their effects.|The doctor prescribed a narcotic to relieve the patient's pain. 医生给病人开了麻醉药来缓解疼痛。|近义词：opiate, analgesic, drug, anesthetic|反义词: stimulant|词性: noun, adjective
