cutwal: /ˈkʌtwɑːl/ | n. | 城镇警官，治安官 | A police officer or superintendent of police in India, especially during the Mughal period. |  The cutwal was responsible for maintaining order in the city. 治安官负责维持城里的秩序。| The cutwal collected taxes and enforced the law. 治安官收税并执法。 | synonyms: police chief, sheriff, constable | antonyms: criminal, outlaw | noun
