demoting:/dɪˈmoʊtɪŋ/
| v. | 降级，降职 | Reducing someone to a lower rank or position. |  The company is demoting him from manager to assistant manager.  公司将他从经理降职为助理经理。| synonyms:  downgrading, relegating, lowering | antonyms: promoting, elevating, upgrading | Verb

demote:/dɪˈmoʊt/
| v. | 降级，降职 | To reduce someone to a lower rank or position. | He was demoted for poor performance. 由于表现不佳，他被降职了。 | synonyms: downgrade, relegate, lower | antonyms: promote, elevate, upgrade | Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *demovere, from Latin de- "down" + movere "to move."
