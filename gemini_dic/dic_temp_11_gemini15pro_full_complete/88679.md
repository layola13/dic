hoay:/hoɪ/ | n. | 呼喊; 吆喝声 | A loud shout or call, especially to attract attention or encourage someone. | Example:  The farmer gave a hoay to his sheepdog. 农夫向他的牧羊犬吆喝了一声。 | Example: We heard a hoay from across the field. 我们听到从田野对面传来一声吆喝。 | Synonym: shout, yell, call, whoop | Antonym: whisper, murmur | 词性: 名词


词源:
Likely of imitative origin, mimicking the sound of a shout.  Its usage is primarily found in dialectal or older forms of English, and is not commonly used in modern standard English.
