Manchuria: /mænˈtʃʊəriə/ /mænˈtʃɜːriə/
noun
满洲
A historical region in Northeast Asia, now largely corresponding to the Chinese provinces of Heilongjiang, Jilin, and Liaoning.
The Japanese invasion of Manchuria in 1931 led to the establishment of the puppet state of Manchukuo.  日本在1931年入侵满洲，导致了伪满洲国的建立。
Manchuria is known for its cold winters and vast soybean fields. 满洲以其寒冷的冬天和广阔的大豆田而闻名。
Synonyms:  Manchukuo (historical, controversial), Northeast China
Antonyms: N/A
词性: noun
