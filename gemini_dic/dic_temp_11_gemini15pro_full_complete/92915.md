ided:/ˈaɪdɪd/ | adj. | 已决定的，已确定的，已同意的 |  Past participle of "ide," a now obsolete verb meaning "to determine." It is primarily used as a combining form in words like "decided," "divided," and "subsided."  It indicates something that has been concluded or resolved. | The case was decided in the plaintiff's favor.  此案原告胜诉。| Near synonyms: resolved, settled, determined | Near antonyms: undecided, unresolved, pending | part of verb (past participle)
