euge:/ˈjuːdʒiː/
interj.|好啊！妙！|An exclamation of praise or triumph.  Expressing joy or approval, similar to "bravo" or "well done".|Euge! You won the race!  好啊！你赢得了比赛！|Synonyms: bravo, well done, excellent, hurrah, hooray|Antonyms: boo, fie, shame|interjection
