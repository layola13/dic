galore:/ɡəˈlɔːr/| adj.|大量的，丰富的；充足的|in abundance; plentiful.  It is used postpositively (placed after the word it modifies).|例句：There were prizes galore at the fair. 展会上奖品琳琅满目。

例句：There will be food and drink galore at the party. 派对上将有大量的食物和饮料。|近义词：abundant, plentiful, copious, ample|反义词：scarce, few, lacking|词性:adjective
