commissural:/kəˈmɪʃərəl/ | adj. | 连合的；连合纤维的 | Relating to a commissure, a band of nerve tissue connecting corresponding right and left parts of the brain or spinal cord. | The commissural fibers connect the two hemispheres of the brain.  大脑的两个半球由连合纤维连接。|  The corpus callosum is the largest commissural pathway in the brain. 胼胝体是大脑中最大的连合通路。 | connecting, joining | separating | adjective

commissure:/ˈkɒmɪʃə(r)/ | n. | 连合；接合处 | A point or line of union or junction, especially between two anatomical parts. |  The anterior commissure connects the two temporal lobes of the brain. 前连合连接大脑的两个颞叶。 | The surgeon carefully repaired the commissure of the lip.  外科医生仔细地修复了嘴唇的接合处。 | junction, joint, seam | division, separation | noun
