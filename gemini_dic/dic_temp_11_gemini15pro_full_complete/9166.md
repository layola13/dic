antinepotism:/ˌæntiˈnɛpətɪzəm/
|n.|反裙带关系；反对任人唯亲|The practice of opposing nepotism, especially in politics or business.  It refers to policies and practices that aim to prevent favoritism based on family relationships.|The company has a strict antinepotism policy to ensure fair hiring practices. 该公司有严格的反裙带关系政策，以确保公平的招聘实践。|
|近义词:impartiality, fairness, meritocracy|
|反义词:nepotism, cronyism, favoritism|
|词性:noun|


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + nepotism.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to."  "Nepotism" comes from the Italian word "nepotismo," meaning "nephew-ism," derived from the practice of popes appointing their nephews to positions of power.
