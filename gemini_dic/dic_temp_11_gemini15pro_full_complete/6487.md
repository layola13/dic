ampyx:/ˈæmpɪks/| n. |三叶虫的一种|A genus of trilobite with a long forward-pointing spine on the head.|The Ampyx is a fascinating example of early marine life.  三叶虫是早期海洋生物的一个迷人例子。|The museum's collection includes a well-preserved Ampyx fossil.  博物馆的藏品包括一块保存完好的三叶虫化石。|近义词:trilobite (更广义)|反义词: None|词性:noun
Etymology: From Ancient Greek ἄμπυξ (ámpux, “headband, frontlet”), referring to the spine on its head.
