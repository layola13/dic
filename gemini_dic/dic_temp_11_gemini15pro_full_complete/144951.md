overabstract:/ˈoʊvərˈæbˌstrækt/ | adj. | 过度抽象的；过于抽象的 |  Excessively abstract; too theoretical or detached from concrete reality. Characterized by excessive generalization and lacking specific details or examples. |  His overabstract theories were difficult for the students to grasp. 他的过于抽象的理论让学生难以理解。| near synonyms: abstruse, recondite, esoteric, overtheoretical | antonyms: concrete, specific, tangible | adjective

overabstract:/ˈoʊvərˈæbˌstrækt/ | v. | 过度抽象化 | To make something too abstract; to remove too many specific details or examples, making it difficult to understand. | The artist overabstracted the landscape, leaving the viewer with a confusing jumble of shapes and colors.  这位艺术家过度抽象化了风景，给观赏者留下一堆混乱的形状和颜色。 | near synonyms: generalize excessively, oversimplify, schematize | antonyms: specify, concretize, detail | verb
