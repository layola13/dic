edifies:/ˈɛdɪˌfaɪz/
verb
教诲，启迪
instructs or improves (someone) morally or intellectually.
The sermon edified the congregation. 这篇布道启迪了会众。
近义词: instructs, enlightens, uplifts, inspires
反义词: misleads, corrupts, degrades
词性: verb

词源:  From Late Latin aedificare "to build," from Latin aedis "building" + facere "to make."  The original sense was "to build up," later acquiring the figurative meaning of "to build up morally or intellectually."
