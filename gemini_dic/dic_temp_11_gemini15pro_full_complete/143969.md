outquoting:/ˌaʊtˈkwəʊtɪŋ/ | v. | 超越引用; 过度引用; 引用的内容比原创内容多 | The practice of quoting excessively, especially to the point where the quoted material overwhelms the original writing or speech. | He was criticized for outquoting in his essay, as it contained more quotes than his own analysis.  他因论文中过度引用而受到批评，因为其中的引用内容比他自己的分析还多。 | overquoting, citing excessively | underquoting | verb
