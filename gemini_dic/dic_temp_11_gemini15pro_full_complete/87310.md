hern:/hɜːrn/
n.
heron
苍鹭
A long-legged wading bird with a long neck, a long pointed bill, and typically grey or white plumage.
例句：The hern stood motionless in the shallows, waiting for a fish to swim by.  苍鹭一动不动地站在浅滩里，等待鱼儿游过。

例句：We observed a majestic hern taking flight over the lake. 我们观察到一只雄伟的苍鹭飞过湖面。

近义词：heron
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑其他类型的鸟类，例如 songbird - 鸣禽)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *herne, *heron, from Old English *hearan, from Proto-Germanic *hraunaz, *habrunaz (“heron”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱreyH- (“heron, crane”). Cognate with Dutch reiger, German Reiher, Danish hejre.
