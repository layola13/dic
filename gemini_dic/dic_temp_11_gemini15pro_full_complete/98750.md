insusurration:/ɪnˌsʌsəˈreɪʃən/ |n.|私语，密谋，煽动|The act of whispering something to someone, often something secret or subversive.  The act of inciting rebellion or sedition quietly or secretly. |  The insusurration of discontent spread through the ranks.  不满的私语在队伍中蔓延开来。| The candidate's fiery speech was seen as an insusurration to violence.  候选人激烈的演讲被视为煽动暴力。 |Synonyms: whisper, murmur, mutter, rumor, intrigue, plot, conspiracy, sedition | Antonyms: proclamation, announcement, declaration| noun
