condescend:/ˌkɑːndɪˈsend/ | /ˌkɒndɪˈsend/ | v. | 屈尊；俯就；摆架子 | To behave as if one is conscious of descending from a superior position, rank, or dignity.  To stoop or deign to do something considered beneath one's dignity. |  He condescended to speak to me. 他屈尊与我说话。

The famous author condescended to sign autographs for his fans.  这位著名作家屈尊为粉丝签名。 | 近义词：deign, stoop, patronize | 反义词：respect, admire, look up to | 词性: verb
