outleaps:/ˌaʊtˈliːps/ | v. | 超越，胜过 | To surpass or excel in leaping or jumping; to go beyond or exceed. |  The athlete outleaps all his competitors.  这位运动员跳得比所有竞争对手都远。| 近义词：surpasses, exceeds, transcends, outstrips | 反义词：falls short of, lags behind | 词性: 动词

outleaps:/ˌaʊtˈliːps/ | n. | 超越，胜过（的动作或实例）| The act or instance of leaping beyond or exceeding something. |  His outleaps in innovation astonished the industry. 他在创新方面的超越让整个行业感到震惊。 | 近义词：advances, breakthroughs | 反义词： setbacks, regressions | 词性: 名词
