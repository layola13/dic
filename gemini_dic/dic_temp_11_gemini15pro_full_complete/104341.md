juvenilia: /ˌdʒuːvəˈnɪliə/ /ˌdʒuːvənˈɪliə/
n.
青少年时期创作的作品；少年文艺作品
works produced in an artist's or writer's youth
Her juvenilia included some surprisingly mature poems. 她年轻时创作的作品中包含一些令人惊讶的成熟诗歌。
近义词：early works, youthful writings
反义词: mature works, later works
词性: 名词

Etymology: From Latin *juvenilia*, neuter plural of *juvenilis* "youthful," from *juvenis* "young."
