nonauthoritative:/ˌnɒnɔːˈθɒrɪtətɪv/ |adj.| 非权威的，非官方的 |Not proceeding from or sanctioned by constituted authority; not having authority.  Lacking authority or official sanction. |This website provides nonauthoritative information on medical conditions.  这个网站提供非权威的医疗信息。

The information provided in this blog is nonauthoritative and should not be taken as medical advice.  此博客中提供的信息是非权威性的，不应被视为医学建议。 | 近义词: unofficial, informal, unauthenticated | 反义词: authoritative, official, authentic | 词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Old French non-, from Latin non "not."
authoritative (adjective): From Late Latin auctoritativus, from auctoritas "authority."
