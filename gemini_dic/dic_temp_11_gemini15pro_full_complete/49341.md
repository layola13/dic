delineate:/dɪˈlɪnieɪt/
verb
描绘，描述，勾勒
To describe or portray (something) precisely.
The report clearly delineates the steps that must be taken.  这份报告清晰地描述了必须采取的步骤。
The artist delineated the scene with precise detail.  这位艺术家精确细致地描绘了这个场景。
outline, describe, depict, portray, sketch
blur, obscure, confuse
v.
