brewing:/ˈbruːɪŋ/| n. v. adj.|酿造；酝酿；冲泡；即将发生的|The act of making beer or other drinks by steeping, boiling, and fermenting malt, hops, or other ingredients. Also refers to the process of making tea or coffee by steeping them in hot water. Can also describe something unpleasant that is about to happen.|
例句：
The brewing of beer is a complex process. 啤酒的酿造是一个复杂的过程。

There's trouble brewing in the office. 办公室里要出事了。

近义词：fermenting, developing, impending, looming|
反义词：dissipating, resolving, subsiding|
词性: noun, verb, adjective
