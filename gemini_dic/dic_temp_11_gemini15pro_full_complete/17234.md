bawlers:/ˈbɔːlərz/| n.|大声哭叫的人；爱哭鬼|People who cry loudly and excessively; crybabies|The bawlers were escorted out of the courtroom.  大声哭叫的人被护送出法庭。|近义词:criers, weepers, wailers|反义词:stoics|词性:名词

bawl:/bɔːl/| v.|大声哭；喊叫|To cry loudly; to shout or yell.|The baby bawled for hours. 婴儿哭了几个小时。|近义词:cry, weep, yell, shout, scream|反义词:whisper, murmur|词性:动词
