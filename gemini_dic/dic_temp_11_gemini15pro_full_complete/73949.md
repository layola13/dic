forthrightnesses:/ˌfɔːrθˈraɪtnəsɪz/
n.
直率，坦诚，坦白
the quality of being direct and honest in expression; frankness.
Her forthrightness can sometimes be mistaken for rudeness. 她的直率有时会被误认为是粗鲁。
He admired her forthrightness and honesty. 他钦佩她的直率和诚实。
近义词：frankness, candor, directness, honesty, outspokenness
反义词：evasiveness, indirectness, reticence, shyness, timidity
词性: 名词(plural)

forthright:/ˌfɔːrθˈraɪt/
adj.
直率的，坦诚的，坦白的
(of a person or their manner or speech) direct and outspoken; straightforward and honest.
He was forthright in his criticism of the government. 他直言不讳地批评政府。
She gave a forthright answer to the journalist's question. 她对记者的提问给出了直截了当的回答。
近义词：direct, frank, candid, honest, straightforward
反义词：evasive, indirect, reticent, shy, timid
词性: 形容词

forthright:/ˌfɔːrθˈraɪt/
adv.
直接地，坦率地
directly and without hesitation.
He stated his views forthright. 他直截了当地陈述了自己的观点。
She walked forthright into the boss's office. 她径直走进了老板的办公室。
近义词：directly, frankly, candidly, honestly, straightforwardly
反义词：indirectly, evasively, reticently, shyly, timidly
词性: 副词


词源:  From Middle English *forth-right, from forth + right.  Originally meant "straight ahead," later developing the sense of "direct in speech and manner."
