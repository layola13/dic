cyaniding:/ˈsaɪənaɪdɪŋ/
n.|氰化(作用), 氰化物处理|the process of treating something with cyanide, especially ore to extract gold or silver. |The cyaniding process is used to extract gold from ore.| 近义词: cyanide leaching| 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps detoxification if referring to neutralizing cyanide's effects)|词性:名词

cyaniding:/ˈsaɪənaɪdɪŋ/
v.|氰化(处理)|treating something with cyanide, especially ore to extract gold or silver. |They are cyaniding the gold ore to separate the precious metal.| 近义词: cyanide leaching| 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps detoxification if referring to neutralizing cyanide's effects)|词性:动词
