natatorium:/ˌneɪtəˈtɔːriəm/ /ˌnætəˈtɔːriəm/
n.
室内游泳池, 游泳馆
An indoor swimming pool.
例句：
The natatorium hosted the regional swimming competition.
该游泳馆举办了地区游泳比赛。

The university's natatorium is open to students and faculty.
大学的游泳馆对学生和教职工开放。

近义词：swimming pool, indoor pool, swimming bath
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 outdoor pool)
