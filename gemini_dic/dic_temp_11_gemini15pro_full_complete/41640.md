corrective:/kəˈrɛktɪv/| adj. n.|纠正的，矫正的；矫正物，矫正剂|Intended to correct something, especially a fault or deficiency.|The teacher gave him some corrective exercises for his handwriting. 老师给他布置了一些矫正他书写的练习。|近义词:remedial, restorative, curative|反义词:causative, damaging|adjective

corrective:/kəˈrɛktɪv/| n.|矫正物，矫正剂|Something that corrects or counteracts something undesirable.|She wears corrective lenses. 她戴着矫正镜片。|近义词:remedy, cure, restorative|反义词:aggravant|noun
