grandest:/ˈɡrændɪst/| adj.|最宏伟的，最壮丽的|Superlative form of grand: most impressive in size, scope, or effect.|例句：The Palace of Versailles is one of the grandest buildings in Europe.  凡尔赛宫是欧洲最宏伟的建筑之一。|近义词：most magnificent, most majestic, most impressive|反义词：humblest, simplest, smallest|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle French "grandissime," ultimately from Latin "grandis" (large, great).
