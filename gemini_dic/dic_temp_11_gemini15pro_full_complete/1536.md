achcha:/ˈʌtʃə/| interj. adj.|好; 对; 是; 行;  (表示同意、理解或鼓励)|Used to express agreement, understanding, or encouragement, similar to "OK" or "all right" in English. It can also be used as an adjective meaning "good."|Achcha, I'll do it.  好的，我会做的。|Synonyms: okay, alright, good, fine|Antonyms: bad, wrong|Interjection, Adjective

注意事项:
"Achcha" is primarily used in Hindi and Urdu, although it is sometimes adopted into other Indian languages. Its usage and nuances might vary regionally. While it can be transliterated as "achcha," other spellings like "acha" or "acchaa" are also common.
