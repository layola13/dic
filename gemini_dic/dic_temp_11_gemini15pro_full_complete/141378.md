opercle: /ˈɒpərkəl/ (UK) /ˈɑːpərkəl/ (US)| n. |鳃盖|A bony flap that covers and protects the gills of fishes and some amphibians. |The opercle of the fish moved rhythmically as it breathed. 鱼的鳃盖随着呼吸有节奏地翕动。|Synonyms: gill cover, branchiostegal|Antonyms: N/A|noun


operculum: /əˈpɜːrkjʊləm/ (UK) /əˈpɜːrkjələm/ (US)| n. |盖|A lid or covering, especially in biology, such as the operculum of a snail shell or a moss capsule.
