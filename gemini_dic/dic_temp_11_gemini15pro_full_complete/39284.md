congruism:/ˈkɒŋɡruːɪzəm/ | n. |一致性，适合性，调和 |The theory that a person's beliefs and actions should be consistent.  一个人信仰和行为应该一致的理论。 |  His lifestyle demonstrates a remarkable congruism between his personal values and his professional pursuits. 他的生活方式表明他的个人价值观和职业追求之间有着显著的一致性。| synonyms: consistency, agreement, harmony, conformity | antonyms:  inconsistency, disagreement, discord, conflict | noun

congruent:/ˈkɒŋɡruːənt/ | adj. |一致的，适合的，调和的 |  Her actions were congruent with her stated beliefs. 她的行为与她所宣称的信仰一致。| The triangles are congruent. 这两个三角形全等。 | synonyms: consistent, corresponding, matching, harmonious, compatible | antonyms: incongruent, inconsistent, incompatible, discordant | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *congruismus, from Latin congruus (“fitting, suitable, agreeable”) + -ismus.  源自后期拉丁语 *congruismus，来自拉丁语 congruus（“合适的，适当的，一致的”）+ -ismus。
