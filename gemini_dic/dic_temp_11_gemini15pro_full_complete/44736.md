crumbable:/ˈkrʌmbləbl/| adj.|易碎的，酥脆的|Something that is crumbable is easily broken into small pieces, or likely to crumble.  It is often used to describe food, particularly baked goods.|The scones were light and crumbable.  司康饼又轻又脆。|近义词：brittle, friable, crumbly|反义词:tough, hard|词性:形容词

crumbable:/ˈkrʌmbləbl/| n.|可碎的食物，通常指糕点|A crumbable is a small, flat, usually round cake that is toasted and eaten with butter, etc. It's primarily a British and Australian term.|I love having a warm crumbable with butter and jam for breakfast. 我喜欢早餐吃涂着黄油和果酱的热腾腾的可碎饼。|近义词：crumpet, pikelet (Australian)|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
