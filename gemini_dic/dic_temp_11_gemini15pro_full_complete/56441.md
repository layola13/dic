dolly:/ˈdɒli/ (UK) /ˈdɑːli/ (US)| n.|玩偶，小推车，移动摄影车|A child's toy representing a baby or small person; a low platform on wheels for moving heavy objects; a wheeled platform that runs on rails and is used for moving a movie or television camera.|例句：The little girl loves playing with her dolly. 小女孩喜欢玩她的洋娃娃。

例句：The movers used a dolly to transport the refrigerator. 搬运工用小推车来搬运冰箱。

近义词：doll, puppet, handcart, platform truck, camera platform|反义词:|名词
dolly:/ˈdɒli/ (UK) /ˈdɑːli/ (US)| v.|用小车移动|To move something using a dolly.|例句：They dollied the camera across the set for a smooth tracking shot. 他们用移动摄影车在片场移动摄像机，进行平滑的跟踪拍摄。

例句：We dollied the heavy equipment into the warehouse. 我们用小推车把重型设备搬进了仓库。

近义词：move, transport, wheel, convey|反义词:|动词
