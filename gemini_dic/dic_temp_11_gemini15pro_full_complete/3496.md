aftermost:/ˈæftərˌmoʊst/| adj.|最后面的，最靠后的| Situated farthest aft; hindmost.| The aftermost cabin on the ship offered the best views of the wake. 船上最后面的船舱可以欣赏到最好的尾迹景色。|近义词：hindmost, rearmost, last|反义词：foremost, frontmost|词性:形容词
词源: From "after" (meaning behind) + "-most" (a superlative suffix).
