nonelectrically:/ˌnɒnɪˈlɛktrɪkli/
adjective
非电的；不使用电的
Not involving or requiring electricity.
The pump operates nonelectrically, using a system of gears and levers.  这个泵是非电动的，使用齿轮和杠杆系统。
The door is opened nonelectrically using a simple pulley system. 这扇门是用一个简单的滑轮系统非电动打开的.
Synonym: manually, mechanically
Antonym: electrically
词性: 形容词
