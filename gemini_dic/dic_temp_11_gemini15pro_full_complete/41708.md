corroborating:/kəˈrɒbəreɪtɪŋ/
adjective
确证的，证实的
supporting or confirming a statement, theory, or finding
The witness's testimony was corroborating the victim's account of the robbery.  证人的证词证实了受害者对抢劫案的描述。
近义词: confirming, verifying, substantiating, supporting
反义词: contradicting, refuting, denying
词性: adjective

corroborate:/kəˈrɒbəreɪt/
verb
证实，确证
confirm or give support to (a statement, theory, or finding)
The forensic evidence corroborated the witness's testimony. 法医证据证实了证人的证词。
近义词: confirm, verify, substantiate, support
反义词: contradict, refute, deny
词性: verb

词源:  From Late Latin *corroboratus, past participle of corroborare "to strengthen," from Latin com- "together" + roborare "to strengthen," from robur "strength."
