anaphylaxis:/ˌænəfɪˈlæksɪs/ (US) /ˌænəfɪˈlæksɪs/ (UK)| n.|过敏反应，过敏性休克|A serious allergic reaction that is rapid in onset and may cause death.|Example 1: The child went into anaphylaxis after eating a peanut.  这孩子吃了花生后发生了过敏性休克。

Example 2:  He was given an injection of epinephrine to counteract the anaphylaxis. 他被注射了一针肾上腺素来对抗过敏性休克。|近义词：allergic reaction, hypersensitivity|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "tolerance")|词性:noun
