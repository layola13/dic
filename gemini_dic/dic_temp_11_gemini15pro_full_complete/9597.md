antisoporific: /ˌæntiˌsɑːpəˈrɪfɪk/ /ˌæntiˌsoʊpəˈrɪfɪk/
adjective
防止睡眠的，清醒的
Preventing or counteracting sleepiness; stimulating.
The comedian's antisoporific jokes kept the audience awake and alert.  这位喜剧演员提神的笑话让观众保持清醒和警觉。
近义词: stimulating, invigorating, awakening
反义词: soporific, sleep-inducing, hypnotic
词性: adjective

词源:
From anti- (against) + soporific (sleep-inducing).  The word "soporific" comes from Latin "sopor" (deep sleep).
