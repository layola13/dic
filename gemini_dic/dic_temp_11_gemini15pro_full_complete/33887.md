circumstantial:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈstænʃəl/ | adj. |  依情况而定的；间接的；详尽的 |  Based on circumstances; pointing indirectly toward someone's guilt but not conclusively proving it; containing or consisting of details. | The evidence against him was purely circumstantial. 对他不利的证据完全是间接的。 | synonyms: indirect, inferential, presumptive, incidental, detailed, minute | antonyms: direct, conclusive, essential | adjective

circumstantial:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈstænʃəl/ | adj. | 情况的；与情况有关的 | Relating to or dependent on circumstances. |  The meeting was postponed due to circumstantial reasons.  由于情况原因，会议被推迟了。| synonyms: contingent, dependent, conditional | antonyms: inherent, intrinsic, essential | adjective
