gobang: /ˈɡoʊˌbæŋ/ | n. |五子棋 | A board game of Japanese origin, played on a grid of 15x15 or 19x19 lines, where two players take turns placing black and white stones. The first player to get five stones in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins. | Let's play a game of gobang.  我们来玩一盘五子棋吧。| We had a fierce gobang competition yesterday. 昨天我们进行了一场激烈的五子棋比赛。 | synonyms: five in a row | antonyms:  N/A | noun
