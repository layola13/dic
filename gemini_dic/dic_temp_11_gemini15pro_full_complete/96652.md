industrialism: /ɪnˈdʌstriəlɪzəm/ | n. | 工业主义 | A social and economic system characterized by the large-scale development of industries and the use of machines, especially in factories. | The Industrial Revolution brought about significant changes in society, including the rise of industrialism. 工业革命给社会带来了巨大的变化，包括工业主义的兴起。|  Industrialism led to the growth of cities and the decline of rural communities. 工业主义导致了城市的增长和农村社区的衰落。 | synonyms: industrialization, manufacturing | antonym: agrarianism | noun
