|  His evanid dreams of fame faded quickly. 他对名望的短暂梦想很快就消失了。

| 近义词：fleeting, ephemeral, transient, vanishing | 反义词：permanent, lasting, enduring | 词性：形容词

词源: Late 16th century: from Latin *evanidus, from evanescere ‘vanish’ (see evanesce).
