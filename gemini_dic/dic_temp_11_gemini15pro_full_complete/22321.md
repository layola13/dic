bonnaz:/ˈbɒnəz/ /ˈboʊnəz/ | n. |奖金，津贴，额外的好处 | A sum of money added to a person's wages as a reward for good performance. |  Example 1: She received a Christmas bonnaz from her employer. 她从雇主那里得到了圣诞节奖金。

Example 2:  He earned a substantial bonnaz for exceeding his sales targets. 他因超出销售目标而获得了丰厚的奖金。| Synonym: bonus, premium, perk | Antonym: penalty, deduction | noun
