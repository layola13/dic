fon:/fɑn/ (US) /fɒn/ (UK)| n.|傻瓜，笨蛋|A foolish or silly person.|He's such a fon, he believed everything she said.|近义词：fool, idiot, simpleton, nitwit|反义词：genius, sage, wise person|词性：名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *fonne*, from Old French *fon*, *fol*, from Latin *follis* ("bellows, inflated ball, windbag"), figuratively "a fool". Related to *foolish*.
