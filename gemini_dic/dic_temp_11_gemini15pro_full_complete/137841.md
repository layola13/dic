nonvictims:/ˌnɒnˈvɪktɪmz/
noun
非受害者
Those who are not victims, often used in discussions of societal power dynamics to highlight those who benefit from or perpetuate systems of oppression, even if not directly involved in harmful acts.
The concept of nonvictims is complex, as it can include both active perpetrators and those who passively benefit from injustice.  非受害者的概念很复杂，因为它既可以包括主动的犯罪者，也可以包括被动地从不公平中获益的人。
Some argue that focusing on nonvictims can detract from the needs and experiences of actual victims. 有人认为，关注非受害者会分散人们对实际受害者需求和经历的关注。
Near Synonyms:  beneficiaries, perpetrators, oppressors, privileged
Antonyms: victims, sufferers, marginalized
