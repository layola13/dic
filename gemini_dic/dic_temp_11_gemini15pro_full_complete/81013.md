gradual:/ˈɡrædʒuəl/| adj.|逐渐的，逐步的|Happening or developing slowly or by degrees.|The recovery from his illness was gradual. 他从疾病中逐渐康复。|近义词：slow, progressive, steady|反义词：sudden, abrupt, rapid|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Late Latin *gradualis*, from Latin gradus "step, degree."
