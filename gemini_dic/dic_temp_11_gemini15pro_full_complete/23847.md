breading:/ˈbredɪŋ/| n.|面包屑，裹粉；油炸食物外面的脆皮|A mixture of breadcrumbs, flour, and seasonings used to coat food before frying.|例句：The chicken had a crispy breading. 这只鸡的脆皮很酥。|例句：She dipped the fish in the breading before frying it. 她把鱼在面包屑里蘸了一下，然后炸了。|近义词：coating, crumb, crust|反义词:|noun
