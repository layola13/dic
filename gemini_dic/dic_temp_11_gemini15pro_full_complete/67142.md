exposable:/ɪkˈspoʊzəbl/
adjective
可暴露的；易受影响的
able to be exposed or made known
例句：The exposable wiring needs to be covered for safety.  暴露的电线需要覆盖以确保安全。

例句：Children are particularly exposable to the harmful effects of air pollution. 孩子们特别容易受到空气污染的危害。
近义词：vulnerable, susceptible, open, subject
反义词：protected, shielded, immune, resistant
词性: adjective

expose:/ɪkˈspoʊz/
verb
暴露；揭露
make (something) visible by uncovering it
例句：The investigation exposed a network of corruption. 调查揭露了一个腐败网络。

例句：He exposed his skin to the sun. 他把皮肤暴露在阳光下。
近义词：reveal, uncover, disclose, divulge
反义词：conceal, hide, cover, protect
词性: verb

exposition:/ˌɛkspəˈzɪʃən/
noun
阐述；展览会
a comprehensive description and explanation of an idea or theory. a public exhibition or show, as of artistic or industrial products
例句：The first chapter provides an exposition of the book's main themes. 第一章阐述了本书的主题。

例句：The World Exposition showcased the latest technological innovations. 世界博览会展示了最新的技术创新。
近义词：explanation, clarification, exhibition, display
反义词：concealment, hiding
词性: noun
