bellman:/ˈbelmən/ | n. |行李员，门童 |A hotel employee who carries luggage and performs other services for guests. |例句：The bellman helped us with our bags. 行李员帮我们提行李。

例句：Please ask the bellman to bring up some ice. 请让门童送些冰块上来。

近义词：porter, doorman |反义词: (无明显的反义词) |词性: noun

bellman:/ˈbelmən/| n. |传令员，报信人|A person who delivers messages or announcements. (This meaning is less common in modern English.)|例句：The bellman announced the arrival of the king. 传令员宣布国王驾到。

例句：The town crier acted as a bellman in the old days. 在过去，镇上传报消息的人充当传令员的角色。

近义词：messenger, herald |反义词: (无明显的反义词) |词性: noun
