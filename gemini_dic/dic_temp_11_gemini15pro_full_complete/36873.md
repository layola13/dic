coloration:/ˌkʌləˈreɪʃən/| n.|着色，染色；颜色，色彩；（动植物的）颜色，彩色|the process of coloring or being colored; the particular range of colors of something, especially of an animal or plant|The bird's bright coloration made it easy to spot.  这只鸟鲜艳的颜色使它很容易被发现。|Synonyms: coloring, pigmentation, hue, tint|Antonyms: achromatism, discoloration|noun
