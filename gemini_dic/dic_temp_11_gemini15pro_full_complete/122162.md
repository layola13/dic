mistrusting:/mɪsˈtrʌstɪŋ/
adjective
不信任的，怀疑的
lacking trust; suspicious.
She gave him a mistrusting look. 她用怀疑的目光看着他。
近义词:suspicious, wary, doubtful, skeptical
反义词:trusting, confident, believing
词性:adjective

mistrusting:/mɪsˈtrʌstɪŋ/
verb
不信任，怀疑
to have doubts about; to lack confidence in.
He mistrusts anyone who offers him something for nothing.  他不信任任何给他免费东西的人。
近义词:doubt, suspect, question
反义词:trust, believe, confide in
词性:verb (present participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From mistrust + -ing. Mistrust comes from the Middle English *mistrusten*, from mis- (wrongly) + trusten (to trust).  The Old English root for "trust" is *treowian*, related to the Old High German *triuwa* (faith, fidelity).
