embroidering:/ɪmˈbrɔɪdərɪŋ/
verb
刺绣，修饰
The act of decorating fabric with needlework.
She is embroidering a beautiful picture of a bird. 她正在绣一幅美丽的鸟的图画。
She was embroidering a tablecloth. 她正在绣一块桌布。

近义词: stitching, decorating, ornamenting, embellishing
反义词:  None (The opposite of embroidering would be removing embroidery, but there isn't a single word for that.)
词性: Verb (present participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *embroidier, from en- "in, on" + broider "to prick," from broder "a sharp point, awl."
