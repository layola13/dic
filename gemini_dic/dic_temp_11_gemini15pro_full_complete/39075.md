confrontation: /ˌkɒnfrʌnˈteɪʃn/ (UK) /ˌkɑːnfrənˈteɪʃn/ (US)| n.|对抗，冲突，对峙|a situation in which people or groups are in strong disagreement or opposition|例句：The two countries are on the brink of confrontation. 这两个国家处于对抗的边缘。|例句：He avoided a confrontation with his boss. 他避免了和老板的冲突。|近义词：conflict, clash, dispute, showdown|反义词：agreement, harmony, compromise|词性：名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin confrontatio, from Latin confrontare "to compare, bring face to face," from con- "together" + frontem (nominative frons) "forehead, front."
