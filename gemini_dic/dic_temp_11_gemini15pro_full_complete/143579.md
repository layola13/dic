outhowling:/ˌaʊtˈhaʊlɪŋ/
|adj.|
|胜过；超过（某人）的叫喊；盖过…的喊叫声|
|Surpassing or exceeding (someone) in howling; louder than another's howls.|
|The outhowling wind made it difficult to hear anything else.  狂风呼啸，很难听到其他声音。|

|Their cheers were outhowling the opposing team's fans. 他们的欢呼声盖过了对方球迷的声音。|


|近义词: outshouting, overshouting|
|反义词:  hushed, quiet|
|词性: 形容词|

ETYMOLOGY->
From "out-" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "howling" (the act of making a long, loud cry like a dog or wolf).  由表示“超过”或“胜过”的“out-”和表示“像狗或狼一样发出长而响亮的叫声”的“howling”组成。
