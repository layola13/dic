mutation:/mjuːˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 变更，转变 |  A significant change in form, nature, or quality. | The rapid mutation of the company surprised everyone. 公司的快速转变让每个人都感到惊讶。 | synonym: transformation, metamorphosis, change | antonym: stagnation, fixity | noun
