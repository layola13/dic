misattributes:/ˌmɪsəˈtrɪbjuːts/
|v.|错误地归因于；误认为…是…所为|To wrongly ascribe something to someone or something.|He misattributes the quote to Shakespeare, when it was actually written by Marlowe.|misascribes, misassigns|correctly attributes, rightly ascribes|verb

misattribute:/ˌmɪsəˈtrɪbjuːt/
|n.|错误归因|The act of wrongly ascribing something to someone or something.|The misattribution of the painting to Rembrandt caused quite a stir in the art world.|misascription, misassignment|correct attribution, right ascription|noun
