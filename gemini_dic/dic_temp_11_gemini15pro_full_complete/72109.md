flockling:/ˈflɒklɪŋ/ | n. |雏鸟; 幼畜(尤指羊羔); (轻蔑地指)小孩子 |A young bird or animal, especially a lamb. Also used disparagingly to refer to a child. |The farmer carefully tended to the flocklings in the spring. 农场主在春天细心照料小羊羔们。|The teacher referred to the unruly children as "flocklings." 老师轻蔑地把这些不守规矩的孩子称为“小崽子们”。 |Synonyms: chick, lamb, youngling, fledgling |Antonyms: adult, mature |noun
