finches:/ˈfɪntʃɪz/| n.|雀类；燕雀；|Small, seed-eating songbirds with short, stout beaks.|The bird feeder attracted a variety of finches.|近义词：birds, songbirds|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑predator 捕食者)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *finc, from Proto-Germanic *finkiz, of imitative origin.  Cognate with German Fink, Dutch vink.
