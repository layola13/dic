intersection:/ˌɪntərˈsekʃən/ | n. | 交叉路口；十字路口；交叉点；相交|A place where two or more roads cross. |The accident happened at the intersection of Main Street and First Avenue. 事故发生在主街和第一大道的交叉路口。|
intersection:/ˌɪntərˈsekʃən/ | n. | (两条线等的)相交；交点|The point where two lines cross. |The intersection of the two graphs represents the solution to the equations. 这两个图的交点表示方程的解。|
intersection:/ˌɪntərˈsekʃən/ | n. | (两集合的)交集；共通之处|The set of elements that are common to two or more sets. |The intersection of these two social groups creates a unique cultural blend. 这两个社会群体的交集创造了一种独特的文化融合。|


Etymology->
From Late Latin *intersectionem,  from Latin intersectus, past participle of intersecare "to cut into, divide," from inter- "between" + secare "to cut."

Synonym->
junction, crossroads, crossing, interchange, confluence

Antonym->
divergence, separation, division
