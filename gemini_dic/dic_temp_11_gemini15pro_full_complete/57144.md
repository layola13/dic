dovishness:/ˈdʌvɪʃnəs/
n.
鸽派性；温和性
The state or quality of being dovish, advocating peace or conciliation.
His dovishness on foreign policy is well known. 他在外交政策上的鸽派立场是众所周知的。
近义词: pacifism, moderation, leniency
反义词: hawkishness, belligerence, aggressiveness
词性: noun

dovish:/ˈdʌvɪʃ/
adj.
鸽派的；温和的
Advocating peace or conciliation.
The president took a dovish stance on the conflict. 总统在这个冲突中采取了鸽派的立场。
近义词: peaceful, conciliatory, pacific
反义词: hawkish, belligerent, aggressive
词性: adjective
