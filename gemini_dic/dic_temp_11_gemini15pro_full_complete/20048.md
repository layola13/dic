biophore:/ˈbaɪəfɔːr/ | n. | 生物载体，药效基团 | A structural unit of a drug molecule that is responsible for its biological activity.  It's the specific part of the molecule that binds to a biological target (like a receptor or enzyme) and produces the desired effect. |  The biophore of this drug interacts with a specific enzyme to reduce inflammation.  这个药物的药效基团与一种特定的酶相互作用以减少炎症。|  pharmacophore, active site | antagonist | noun
