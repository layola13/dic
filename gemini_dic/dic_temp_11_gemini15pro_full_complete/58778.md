dunnest:/ˈdʌnɪst/| adj.|最棕色的，最暗褐色的|Superlative form of dun: most dun.  This refers to a dull grayish-brown color.|The dunnest horse in the stable was chosen for the night patrol. 马厩里颜色最暗褐色的马被选作夜间巡逻。|近义词：darkest, brownest|反义词:lightest, palest|词性:形容词

dun:/dʌn/| adj. n. v.|暗褐色的，催债者，催债|A dull grayish-brown color; a person who demands payment of a debt; to demand persistently, especially for the payment of a debt.|例句：The walls were painted a dun color. 墙壁被漆成了暗褐色。 He was hounded by duns for months. 他几个月来一直被讨债人追债。  They dunned him for the rent. 他们催促他付房租。|近义词：(adj.) grayish-brown, drab; (n.) creditor, debt collector; (v.) pester, hound|反义词:(adj.) bright, colorful|词性:形容词，名词，动词
