interpreting:/ɪnˈtɜːrprɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 解释的，说明的 |  Her interpreting skills are highly valued by the company. 她的口译技能受到公司的高度重视。 | explanatory, elucidative |  - | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *interpreten*, from Old French *interpreter*, from Latin *interpretārī* (“to explain, expound, interpret, translate”), from *interpres* (“broker, negotiator, translator, interpreter”).
