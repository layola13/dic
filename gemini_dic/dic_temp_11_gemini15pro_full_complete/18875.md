beshrew:/bɪˈʃruː/
verb
诅咒，咒骂
To express a curse against; to invoke evil upon.
Beshrew your heart for breaking mine!  咒骂你那颗伤我心的心！
Beshrew me if I ever trust him again!  如果我再相信他，就让我倒霉！
curse, damn
