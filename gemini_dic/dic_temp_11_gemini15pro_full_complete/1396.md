accusatory:/əˈkjuːzətɔːri/| adj.|指责的，控诉的|indicating or suggesting that one believes someone has done something wrong.|Her accusatory tone made him defensive. 她指责的语气让他感到很不舒服。|Example: He pointed an accusatory finger at the suspect. 他用指责的手指着嫌疑人。

Example:  She gave him an accusatory look. 她用 accusing 的眼神看着他。|近义词：blaming, incriminating, condemning|反义词：apologetic, forgiving, exculpatory|词性:adjective
Etymology:  From Late Latin *accusatorius, from Latin accusator (“accuser”), from accusare (“to accuse”), from ad (“to”) + causa (“cause, reason”).
