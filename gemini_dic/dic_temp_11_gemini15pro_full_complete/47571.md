debarments:/dɪˈbɑːrmənts/
n.
禁止，阻止；停职，除名
The act of debarring someone from something, especially from a profession or from holding public office.  官方禁止某人做某事，尤指禁止从事某职业或担任公职。
Several companies were issued debarments from government contracts due to ethical violations.  几家公司因违反道德而被禁止获得政府合同。
近义词: exclusions, prohibitions, suspensions, disqualifications
反义词:  reinstatements, admissions, approvals
词性: 名词 (plural of debarment)


debarment:/dɪˈbɑːrmənt/
n.
禁止，阻止；停职，除名
The act of officially preventing someone from doing something, especially from taking part in a profession or from holding public office. 官方禁止某人做某事，尤指禁止从事某职业或担任公职。
His debarment from the legal profession followed his conviction for fraud. 他因欺诈罪被判刑后被禁止从事法律职业。
近义词: exclusion, prohibition, suspension, disqualification
反义词: reinstatement, admission, approval
词性: 名词
