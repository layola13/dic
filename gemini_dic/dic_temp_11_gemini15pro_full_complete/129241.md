noctuid:/ˈnɑːktʃuːɪd/ | n. |夜蛾科昆虫；夜蛾 |A moth of the family Noctuidae, typically of dull colouring and active at night. |例句：The researcher specialized in the study of noctuids.  这位研究员专门研究夜蛾科昆虫。

例句：Several species of noctuid are considered agricultural pests.  几种夜蛾被认为是农业害虫。|近义词：moth |反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider diurnal insects like butterflies) 蝴蝶|词性:noun
