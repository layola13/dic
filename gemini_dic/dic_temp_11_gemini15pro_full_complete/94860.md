in-a-sense:/ɪn ə sɛns/| adv.|在某种意义上，在一定程度上|In some way, to a certain extent; partly but not completely.|例句：In a sense, he was right. 在某种意义上，他是对的。|近义词：somewhat, somehow, partly, to some extent|反义词:completely, absolutely, entirely|词性:副词
Etymology: From "in" + "a" + "sense." "Sense" comes from Old French "sens," meaning "feeling, sense, meaning," ultimately from Latin "sensus," meaning "feeling, sense, thought."
