dravidians:/drəˈvɪdiənz/
n.
达罗毗荼人; 南印度人
A member of any of the groups of peoples speaking Dravidian languages and inhabiting southern India and Sri Lanka.
The Dravidians have a rich cultural heritage. 达罗毗荼人有着丰富的文化遗产。
Dravidian languages are spoken primarily in southern India. 达罗毗荼语主要在印度南部使用。
近义词:South Indians
反义词:(No direct antonym, could consider Indo-Aryans as a contrasting group)
词性:noun
