outbitting:/ˌaʊtˈbɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 超越，胜过（在比特率、计算能力等方面） | Surpassing or exceeding something, especially in terms of bitrate, computational power, or technological advancement. |  Example 1: The new graphics card is outbitting all its competitors.  新显卡在性能上超越了所有竞争对手。

Example 2:  Their servers are outbitting ours, giving them a significant advantage in data processing. 他们的服务器比特率超过了我们的，这让他们在数据处理方面拥有显著优势。| 近义词: outperforming, exceeding, surpassing | 反义词: underperforming, lagging behind | 词性: 动词

词源:  "Out-" (prefix meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "bit" (referring to a unit of digital information) + "-ing" (present participle suffix).  由表示“超过”的前缀"out-"、指“数字信息单位”的"bit"和现在分词后缀"-ing"构成。
