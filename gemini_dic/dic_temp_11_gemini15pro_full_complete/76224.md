futon:/ˈfuːtɒn/ (British), /ˈfuːtɑːn/ (American)| n. |蒲团，沙发床|A Japanese quilted mattress rolled out on the floor for sleeping, or a similar mattress used as a sofa.|例句：I slept on a futon when I visited Japan. 我去日本玩的时候睡在蒲团上。

例句：She unfolded the futon and turned it into a bed. 她把沙发床展开变成一张床。|近义词：mattress, sofa bed|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "conventional bed" or "armchair")|词性:noun
