note:/noʊt/ (US) /nəʊt/ (UK)| n. vt.|笔记，记录；注释；便条；音符；钞票|A brief record of facts, topics, or thoughts, often written down as an aid to memory.|She made a note of his phone number. 她记下了他的电话号码。|Synonyms: memo, record, annotation, observation, remark|Antonyms: ignorance, oblivion|noun

note:/noʊt/ (US) /nəʊt/ (UK)| vt.|注意，留意；指出，特别提到|To observe carefully; to give attention or heed to something.|Note the fine details in the painting. 注意画作中的精美细节。|Synonyms: observe, perceive, notice, remark, mention|Antonyms: ignore, overlook, disregard|verb
