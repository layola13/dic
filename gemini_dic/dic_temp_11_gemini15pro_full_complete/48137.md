decollectivize:/diːkəˈlɛktɪvaɪz/
verb
去集体化；取消集体化
To abolish collective control or ownership, especially of farmland.
The government decided to decollectivize agriculture and return land to individual farmers. 政府决定取消农业集体化，把土地归还给个体农民。
