courageously: /kəˈreɪdʒəsli/ | adv. | 勇敢地 | In a brave manner; with courage. |  She courageously faced the challenge. 她勇敢地面对挑战。

The firefighters courageously entered the burning building. 消防员勇敢地进入了燃烧的大楼。

| 近义词: bravely, valiantly, boldly, fearlessly, intrepidly | 反义词: cowardly, timidly, fearfully | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From "courageous" + "-ly".  "Courageous" comes from Old French "corageus", from "corage" (courage), ultimately from Latin "cor" (heart).
