frescoer:/ˈfrɛskoʊər/| n.|湿壁画画家|A person who paints frescoes.  A skilled artist specializing in the technique of fresco painting.|The frescoer carefully applied the pigments to the wet plaster. 湿壁画画家小心翼翼地将颜料涂在湿灰泥上。|近义词:muralist, painter|反义词: 无|词性:名词

词源: Italian *fresca* (fresh) -> fresco (a painting done rapidly in watercolor on wet plaster on a wall or ceiling) -> frescoer
