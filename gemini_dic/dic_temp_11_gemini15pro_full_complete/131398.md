nonclandestine:/ˌnɑːnklænˈdɛstɪn/
adjective
公开的，非秘密的
Not clandestine; open; not secret.
The meeting was nonclandestine and open to the public. 这次会议是公开的，对公众开放。
The government made a nonclandestine announcement about the new policy. 政府公开宣布了这项新政策。
近义词: open, public, overt, aboveboard
反义词: clandestine, secret, covert, hidden
词性: adjective

词源:
From non- + clandestine.  "Clandestine" comes from the Latin word "clandestinus," meaning "secret." The prefix "non-" negates the meaning of "clandestine."
