firelighter:/ˈfaɪəˌlaɪtər/| n.|引火物，点火器|A firelighter is a substance used to start a fire easily.|例句：He used a firelighter to get the barbecue going. 他用引火物点燃了烧烤架。

例句：The firelighters were damp and wouldn't catch. 引火物受潮了，点不着。|近义词：kindling, tinder|反义词:fire extinguisher|词性:noun
