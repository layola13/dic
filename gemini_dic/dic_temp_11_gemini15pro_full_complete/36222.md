cognateness:/ˈkɑːɡnɪtnəs/ /ˈkɒɡnɪtnəs/| n.|同源；同源性；同根词关系|The cognateness of the words in these two languages suggests a shared ancestor.  这两种语言中单词的同源性表明它们有共同的祖先。|例句：The cognateness between "night" and "Nacht" is clear.  “night”和“Nacht”之间的同源关系很明显。

例句：Researchers are exploring the cognateness of various Indo-European languages. 研究人员正在探索各种印欧语系的同源性。|近义词：kinship, relationship, affinity, similarity|反义词：dissimilarity, difference|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cognatusness, from Latin cognatus ("related by blood"),  from com- ("together") + natus ("born").  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *genə- ("to give birth, beget").
