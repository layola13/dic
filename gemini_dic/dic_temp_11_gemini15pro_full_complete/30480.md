| n. |中间主义，中间路线|The political landscape is increasingly dominated by centrisms, with fewer people identifying with extreme views. 政治格局越来越被中间主义所主导，认同极端观点的人越来越少。|Centrisms often involve a compromise between different ideologies. 中间主义通常涉及不同意识形态之间的妥协。|近义词：moderatism, neutrality |反义词: extremism, radicalism |词性: noun

centrism: /ˈsɛntrɪzəm/
| n. |中间主义，中间路线|The party's platform is based on centrism, appealing to a broad range of voters. 该党的纲领基于中间主义，吸引了广泛的选民。|He advocates for centrism in political discourse, believing that compromise is essential. 他主张在政治对话中采取中间主义，认为妥协至关重要。|近义词：moderatism, neutrality |反义词: extremism, radicalism |词性: noun

词源:  From French *centrisme*, from centre (“center”) + -isme.  Ultimately from Latin centrum.
