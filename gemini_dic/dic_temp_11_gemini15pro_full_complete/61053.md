elkwood:/ˈɛlkˌwʊd/| n. |麋鹿木；指以麋鹿为主题或与麋鹿栖息地相关的木制品或场所|A type of wood, or a place associated with elk, often used in a decorative or thematic context.  This word isn't a standard dictionary entry but is a descriptive term.|例句：The craftsman used elkwood to create a beautiful carving.  工匠用麋鹿木创作了一个美丽的雕刻。

例句：They named their cabin "Elkwood" because of the frequent elk sightings in the area.  他们把他们的小屋命名为“麋鹿木”，因为该地区经常能看到麋鹿。|近义词：antlerwood (if referring to antlers used in woodwork),  driftwood (if found near elk habitats) |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
