cytologically:/ˌsaɪtəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ | adv. | 细胞学地 | In a way that relates to the study of cells, their structure, and their functions. | The tumor was cytologically examined to determine its nature.  肿瘤进行了细胞学检查以确定其性质。| 近义词: microscopically, cellularly | 反义词: macroscopically | 词性: 副词

cytological:/ˌsaɪtəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 细胞学的 | Relating to the study of cells, their structure, and their functions. | Cytological analysis revealed abnormalities in the cell structure. 细胞学分析揭示了细胞结构的异常。| 近义词: cellular, microscopic | 反义词: macroscopic | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  From "cyto-" (Greek "kytos" meaning "hollow vessel," later used to refer to cells) + "-logy" (Greek "-logia" meaning "study of") + "-ical" (suffix forming adjectives) + "-ly" (suffix forming adverbs).
