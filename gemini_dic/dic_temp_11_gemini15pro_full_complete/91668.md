hyperchloric:/ˌhaɪpərˈklɔːrɪk/ | adj. | 高氯的，过氯的 | Containing more oxygen than chloric acid.  Relating to or derived from perchloric acid. |  Hyperchloric acid is a strong oxidizing agent. 高氯酸是一种强氧化剂。|  The hyperchloric solution reacted violently. 高氯酸溶液反应剧烈。 | 近义词: perchloric | 反义词: hypochlorous | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- (Greek: 'over, above') + chloric (from chlorine).
