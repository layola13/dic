epodic:/ɪˈpɒdɪk/| adj.|插曲式的，阶段性的，分段的|Occurring in episodes; episodic. Characterized by or occurring in episodes.  Relating to or resembling an epic poem.|The novel has an episodic structure, jumping between different characters and timelines. 这部小说的结构是插曲式的，在不同的人物和时间线之间跳跃。|近义词：episodic, serial, segmented|反义词：continuous, uninterrupted|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Ancient Greek ἐπῳδικός (epōidikos, “of or for a refrain”), from ἐπῳδή (epōidḗ, “epode, refrain”) + -ικός (-ikos, “-ic”).
