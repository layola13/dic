bowed:/baʊd/| adj.|弯曲的，鞠躬的，|curved, bent, inclined, especially by bending the head or body forward as a sign of respect or submission.|The old man walked with a bowed head. 老人低着头走路。|近义词：bent, curved, stooped, inclined|反义词:straight, erect|形容词
bowed:/baʊd/| v.|鞠躬，弯腰，|to bend the head or body forward as a sign of respect or submission.|He bowed deeply to the emperor. 他向皇帝深深鞠躬。|近义词：stoop, bend, incline, curtsy|反义词:straighten|动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *būgan "to bend, curve," from Proto-Germanic *buganą (source also of Old Norse bjúga "to bend, curve," Old High German biogan "to bend, curve," Gothic biugan "to bend, curve"), from PIE *bheug- "to bend, curve."
