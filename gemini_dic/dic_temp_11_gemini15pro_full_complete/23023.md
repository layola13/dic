bouldering:/ˈboʊldərɪŋ/
n.
抱石；攀石
The act of climbing on large rocks or artificial rock walls without ropes or harnesses.
He enjoys bouldering in the mountains on weekends. 他喜欢周末去山上抱石。
rock climbing, free climbing
rappelling, abseiling
noun
