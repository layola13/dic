graniform:/ˈɡrænɪfɔːrm/
adjective
颗粒状的；似粒状的
Resembling grains or granules in form or texture.
The rock had a graniform appearance. 这块岩石外观呈颗粒状。
The substance formed graniform crystals. 这种物质形成了颗粒状晶体。
granular, grainy
n/a
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin granum ("grain") + -formis ("-form").
