annexational:/ˌænɛkˈseɪʃənəl/
adj.
兼并的，附加的
Relating to the act of annexing something, typically territory.
The annexational policies of the empire led to widespread unrest.  帝国的兼并政策导致了广泛的动荡。
The government's annexational ambitions were clear. 政府的兼并野心显而易见。
Synonyms:  appropriative, acquisitive, expansionist, imperialist
Antonyms:  cessionary, relinquishing, surrendering
词性: adjective
