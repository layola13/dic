overprovoke: /ˌoʊvərprəˈvoʊk/
verb
过度激怒，过分挑衅
To provoke excessively; to incite beyond reasonable limits.
The comedian overprovoked the audience with his controversial jokes, leading to a walkout.  喜剧演员用他备受争议的笑话过度激怒了观众，导致观众退场。
His constant teasing eventually overprovoked her into slapping him.  他不断的戏弄最终让她忍无可忍，给了他一巴掌。
近义词:  inflame, incite, aggravate, exasperate
反义词:  placate, soothe, calm, pacify
词性:  v.

Etymology:
From "over-" (meaning "excessive") + "provoke" (from Latin "provocare" - "to call forth, challenge").  由表示“过度”的前缀"over-"和动词"provoke"（源自拉丁语"provocare"，意为“唤起、挑战”）组成。
