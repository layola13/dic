ambages:/ˈæmbəˌdʒiːz/
n.
迂回曲折的话；拐弯抹角
roundabout or indirect ways of speaking; circumlocution
He told us the bad news without ambages. 他没有拐弯抹角地告诉了我们这个坏消息。
Synonyms: circumlocution, periphrasis, verbosity
Antonyms: directness, straightforwardness, conciseness
noun
