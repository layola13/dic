adjudge:/əˈdʒʌdʒ/
verb
判决，裁定
To pronounce or decree judicially; to award or assign judicially.
The court adjudged the defendant guilty. 法院判决被告有罪。
synonyms: decree, pronounce, rule, decide, determine
antonyms: acquit, pardon
VERB


adjudge:/əˈdʒʌdʒ/
verb
认为，断定
To hold or deem; to consider or regard.
The teacher adjudged the student's essay to be the best in the class. 老师认为这个学生的作文是班上最好的。
synonyms: consider, deem, reckon, regard
antonyms: disagree, disbelieve
VERB
