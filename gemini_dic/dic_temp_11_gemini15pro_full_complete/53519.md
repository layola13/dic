例句：The project ended disasterly, with budget overruns and missed deadlines.  这个项目以预算超支和错过截止日期而告终，结果是灾难性的。

例句：Her attempt to bake a cake turned out disasterly, resulting in a burnt, inedible mess. 她尝试烤蛋糕的结果是灾难性的，最后烤成了一个烧焦的、不能吃的烂摊子。

近义词：catastrophic, disastrous, calamitous, ruinous
反义词：successful, fortunate, beneficial
词性: adjective
