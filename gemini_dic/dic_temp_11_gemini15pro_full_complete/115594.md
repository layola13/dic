marble:/ˈmɑːrbl̩/ (US) /ˈmɑːbl/ (UK)| n. adj.|大理石，弹珠；坚硬似大理石之物；镇定, 冷静的|A hard crystalline metamorphic form of limestone, typically white with coloured mottlings or streaks, which may be polished and is used in sculpture and architecture.|The Taj Mahal is built of white marble.  泰姬陵是用白色大理石建造的。|近义词：stone, rock, composure, calmness|反义词:agitation, excitement|noun

marble:/ˈmɑːrbl̩/ (US) /ˈmɑːbl/ (UK)| adj.|大理石的；冷酷无情的；似大理石的|Made of or resembling marble.|He had a marble bust of his grandfather. 他有一尊祖父的大理石半身像。|近义词：stony, hard, impassive|反义词:emotional, warm|adjective
