nonsanctimonious:/ˌnɒnˌsæŋktɪˈməʊniəs/
adjective
不虚伪的，不矫揉造作的
Not displaying or marked by sanctimoniousness; not hypocritically pious.
He took a nonsanctimonious approach to religion, preferring actions to empty words. 他对宗教采取了一种不虚伪的方式，更喜欢行动而不是空话。
nonsanctimonious, genuine, sincere, unaffected, natural
sanctimonious, hypocritical, self-righteous, pious
