dissolubleness:/dɪˌsɒljəblənəs/
n.
可溶解性, 易溶解性, 易分解性
The quality of being dissoluble; capability of being dissolved.
The dissolubleness of sugar in water is well known. 糖在水中的易溶性是众所周知的。
近义词: solubility, dissolvability
反义词: insolubility, indissolubility
词性: noun

dissoluble:/dɪˈsɒljəbl/
adj.
可溶解的, 可分解的, 可解除的
Capable of being dissolved; capable of being broken up or decomposed.
Salt is dissoluble in water. 盐溶于水。
近义词: soluble, dissolvable
反义词: insoluble, indissoluble
词性: adjective

dissolve:/dɪˈzɒlv/
v.
溶解, 解散, 消失
(of a solid) to become incorporated into a liquid so as to form a solution.  To (cause something to) become incorporated into a liquid so as to form a solution. To end a parliament or other official body.
Sugar dissolves in water. 糖溶于水。
The parliament was dissolved. 议会解散了。
近义词: melt, liquefy, disintegrate
反义词: solidify, freeze, congeal
词性: verb

Etymology:
From Late Latin dissolubilis, from Latin dissolvere "to loosen, dissolve," from dis- "apart" + solvere "to loosen."
