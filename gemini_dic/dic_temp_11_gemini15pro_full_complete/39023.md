conflagrant:/kənˈfleɪɡrənt/
adjective
燃烧的；火焰熊熊的
Involving or relating to a large fire that causes a lot of damage.
The conflagrant warehouse lit up the night sky. 熊熊燃烧的仓库照亮了夜空。
The conflagrant wildfire spread rapidly through the dry brush.  猛烈的野火迅速蔓延过干燥的灌木丛。
近义词：burning, blazing, fiery, flaming
反义词：extinguished, quenched, doused
词性: adjective

词源：From Latin conflagrāns, present participle of conflagrāre (“to burn completely, be on fire, blaze”), from con- (“together”) + flagrāre (“to burn, blaze”).
