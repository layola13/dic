lugubriousnesses:/ˌluːˈɡuːbriəsnəsɪz/
noun
阴郁；悲哀；忧伤
The state of being lugubrious; excessive mournfulness or gloominess.
The lugubriousnesses of the funeral were overwhelming.  葬礼上的悲哀气氛令人难以承受。
Synonyms: gloominess, mournfulness, melancholy, sadness, sorrowfulness
Antonyms: cheerfulness, happiness, joy, lightheartedness
词性: 名词

lugubrious: /ləˈɡuːbriəs/
adjective
阴郁的；悲哀的；忧伤的
Looking or sounding sad and dismal.
He had a lugubrious expression on his face. 他脸上带着忧伤的表情。
Synonyms: mournful, gloomy, melancholy, sad, sorrowful
Antonyms: cheerful, happy, joyful, lighthearted
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *lugubre*, from Latin *lūgubris* (“mournful, doleful”), perhaps from *lūgēre* (“to mourn”).
