conformed:/kənˈfɔːrmd/ | adj. | 顺从的，一致的 | Adapted to prevailing standards or norms; behaving in accordance with socially accepted conventions or rules. |  He conformed to the company's dress code. 他遵守了公司的着装规定。|  compliant, obedient, submissive |  rebellious, individualistic | adjective

conformed:/kənˈfɔːrmd/ | v. | 遵守，遵从，符合 |  (past tense of conform) Acted in accordance with something; complied with rules, standards, or laws. | The architect conformed to the building regulations. 建筑师遵守了建筑规范。 | complied, obeyed, adhered | defied, disobeyed | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *conformyn, from Old French conformer, from Latin conformare (“to form, shape, fashion, mold; to adapt, adjust, arrange”), from con- (“together”) + formare (“to form”).
