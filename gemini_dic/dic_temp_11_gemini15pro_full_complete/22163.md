bombardo:/bɒmˈbɑːrdoʊ/ (US), /bɒmˈbɑːdəʊ/ (UK)| v. |轰炸，炮击; 连续质问；大量发送(邮件等)|To attack a place with continuous gunfire or bombs; to assail someone with questions or requests.|The enemy bombarded the city for days. 敌人连续几天轰炸了这座城市。|The reporters bombarded the president with questions. 记者们连珠炮似地向总统提问。|近义词:shell, attack, barrage, pepper|反义词:defend, protect|词性:动词
