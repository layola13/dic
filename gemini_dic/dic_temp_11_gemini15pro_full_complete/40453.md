contravene:/ˌkɒntrəˈviːn/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈviːn/
verb
违反，违背
to go against or break a law, rule, or agreement
The company's actions contravened international trade agreements.  这家公司的行为违反了国际贸易协定。
The new policy contravenes the principle of free speech.  新政策违背了言论自由的原则。

近义词: violate, infringe, breach, defy, disobey
反义词: comply with, obey, uphold, observe
词性: verb
