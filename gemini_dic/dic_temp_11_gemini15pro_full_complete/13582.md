attentional:/əˈtɛnʃənəl/
adjective
注意力的；与注意力有关的
Relating to attention; involving attention.
例句：Attentional processes are crucial for learning. 注意力过程对学习至关重要。

例句：The study investigated the attentional biases in anxious individuals. 这项研究调查了焦虑个体中的注意力偏差。

近义词：attentive, observant, focused
反义词：inattentive, distracted, unfocused
词性: adjective
