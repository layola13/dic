nobble:/ˈnɒbəl/ (British) /ˈnɑːbəl/ (American)| vt.|暗中破坏，搞垮；收买；绑架|To disable or incapacitate, especially a horse in a race, by unfair means; to get hold of or obtain (something) by dubious means; to kidnap or abduct.|例句：The favourite was nobbled before the race.  最受欢迎的赛马在比赛前被暗中破坏了。

例句：They tried to nobble the key witness.  他们试图收买关键证人。|近义词：sabotage, disable, fix, bribe, kidnap|反义词:repair, fix, protect|词性:动词
