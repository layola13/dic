make a dent:/meɪk ə dɛnt/
|v. phr.|取得进展；产生影响；减少；消耗|To make a noticeable impact or reduction in something, often something large or difficult.|The donation didn't even make a dent in the amount of money needed.  捐款甚至没有对所需的金额产生任何影响。

The new advertising campaign didn't make a dent in the competitor's market share. 新的广告活动并没有对竞争对手的市场份额产生任何影响。|近义词：make progress, have an impact, make a difference, reduce, diminish|反义词：have no effect, be ineffective, increase, augment|词性:动词短语
