hexagynous:/ˌhɛksˈædʒɪnəs/
adjective
六雌蕊的；六心皮的
Having six pistils or styles.
Hexagynous flowers are characteristic of certain plant families. 六雌蕊的花是某些植物科的特征。
The hexagynous nature of the flower was confirmed upon closer inspection.  经过仔细检查，确认了这朵花的六雌蕊特性。
近义词: six-styled, six-pistilled (less common)
反义词: (No direct antonym, would need to specify a different number of pistils, e.g., monogynous, digynous, etc.)
词性: adjective
