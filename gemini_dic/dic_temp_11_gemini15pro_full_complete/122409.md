mnemonize:/nɪˈmɒnɪz/ (US) /nɪˈmɛməˌnaɪz/| v. |记忆，记住|To commit something to memory using a mnemonic device or technique. |She mnemonized the list of vocabulary words using flashcards. 她用抽认卡记住了词汇表上的单词。|
mnemonize:| v. |记忆，记住|To make a conscious effort to memorize something. |He mnemonized the poem for the recital. 他为了朗诵背诵了这首诗。|
近义词:memorize, commit to memory, learn by heart|反义词:forget|词性:动词
