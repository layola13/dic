gripeful: /ˈɡraɪpfəl/ | adj. | 满腹牢骚的，爱抱怨的 | Full of or characterized by gripes; complaining. |  He was in a gripeful mood after his flight was delayed.  他因为航班延误而心情烦躁，抱怨连连。 | Example: The gripeful customer complained about everything.  这个爱抱怨的顾客对一切都抱怨。| Synonyms: complaining, querulous, whiny, petulant | Antonyms: contented, satisfied, cheerful, happy | Adjective
