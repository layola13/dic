lumberman:/ˈlʌmbərmən/ | n. |伐木工人；木材商|A person who fells trees, especially for timber; a logger.|例句：The lumberman skillfully felled the giant redwood.  伐木工人熟练地砍倒了巨大的红杉树。

例句：The lumberman owned a large tract of forest.  这位木材商拥有一大片森林。|近义词：logger, woodcutter, woodsman|反义词: N/A |noun
