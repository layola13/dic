granularity:/ˌɡrænjuˈlærəti/| n.|粒度，颗粒度|The level of detail considered in a model or analysis.  It refers to the size of the smallest distinguishable unit of information or the degree of detail in a set of data.|We need to increase the granularity of the data to get a more accurate picture of customer behavior. 我们需要提高数据的粒度以更准确地了解客户行为。|近义词：detail, precision, specificity|反义词：coarseness, generality|词性:noun
Etymology: From Latin *granulum* (little grain) + -ity.
