kabala:/kəˈbɑːlə/ /kəˈbɑːlə/
n.
卡巴拉，犹太神秘主义
an esoteric philosophy, especially of Jewish mysticism, based on an interpretation of the Hebrew scriptures.
He was deeply interested in kabala and other mystical traditions. 他对卡巴拉和其他神秘传统深感兴趣。
近义词:Jewish mysticism, esotericism
反义词:
词性:名词
