laffer: /ˈlæfər/ | n. | 拉弗曲线 |  A supposed relationship between economic activity and rates of taxation which suggests that there is an optimum rate of taxation. | The Laffer curve suggests that both very high and very low tax rates discourage work and investment.  拉弗曲线表明，过高和过低的税率都会抑制工作和投资。| near synonyms: curve, graph | antonym: N/A | noun
