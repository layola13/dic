distraction:/dɪˈstrækʃən/ | n. | 分心，注意力分散；令人分心的事物 | something that prevents someone from giving their attention to something else | The noise of the traffic was a constant distraction. 交通噪音不断让人分心。| synonyms: interruption, disturbance, diversion | antonyms: concentration, focus | noun

distraction:/dɪˈstrækʃən/ | n. | 消遣，娱乐 | an activity that amuses or entertains someone |  Reading is her favorite distraction. 阅读是她最喜欢的消遣。 | synonyms: entertainment, amusement, pastime | antonyms: boredom, monotony | noun
