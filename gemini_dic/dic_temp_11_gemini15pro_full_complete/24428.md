bristle-like:/ˈbrɪsəlˌlaɪk/| adj.|像鬃毛一样的；毛发状的|Resembling a bristle; stiff and short like a bristle.  Having the appearance or texture of a bristle.|The plant's leaves were bristle-like, prickly to the touch. 这种植物的叶子像鬃毛一样，摸起来很扎手。|Synonyms: bristly, prickly, setaceous, hispid|Antonyms: smooth, soft, downy|adjective
