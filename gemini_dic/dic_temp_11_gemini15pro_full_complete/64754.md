The correct spelling is "scroll".  There is no word "escrol" in English.

scroll:/skroʊl/| n. vi. vt.|卷轴；滚动；|A roll of parchment or paper, especially one with writing on it.  To move text or images up or down on a computer screen. |例句：The ancient scroll contained a royal decree. 这古老的卷轴包含了一项皇家法令。 She scrolled down the webpage to find the information she needed. 她向下滚动网页以查找所需的信息。|近义词：roll, parchment, browse, scan|反义词: N/A |词性:noun, verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *escrol, escrole, from *escroler "to enroll," of uncertain origin.
