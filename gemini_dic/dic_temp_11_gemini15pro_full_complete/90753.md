hummocky:/ˈhʌməki/ | adj. | 丘状的，波状起伏的 | Covered with or resembling hummocks; uneven.  Characterized by small mounds or hills. | The hummocky terrain made it difficult to cross.  这丘陵地带使得穿越变得困难。| The lunar surface is hummocky. 月球表面是坑坑洼洼的。 |  近义词：uneven, bumpy, hilly, undulating, knobby | 反义词：flat, smooth, level | 词性: adjective

词源:
From hummock + -y.  Hummock ultimately comes from a Scandinavian source, possibly Norwegian *humm, a rounded hill.
