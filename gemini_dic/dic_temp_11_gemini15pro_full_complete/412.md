abjuration:/ˌæbdʒʊˈreɪʃən/| n.|放弃，正式放弃，发誓断绝|The defendant’s abjuration of violence convinced the jury. 被告对暴力的放弃说服了陪审团。|The witness's abjuration of his previous testimony threw the case into turmoil. 证人放弃之前的证词使案件陷入混乱。|近义词：renunciation, repudiation, rejection, denial|反义词：affirmation, acceptance, embrace|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *abjūrātiō, from Latin abjūrō (“I abjure, renounce upon oath”). Equivalent to abjure +‎ -ation.
