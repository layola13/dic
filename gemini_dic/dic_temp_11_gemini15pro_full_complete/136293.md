nonrepresentativeness:/ˌnɒnˌreprɪzɛntətɪvnəs/
noun
非代表性，不典型
lack of representativeness; the fact of not being typical of a particular group or thing.
The survey suffered from nonrepresentativeness due to its small sample size.  这项调查由于样本量小而不具代表性。
