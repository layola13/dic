Etymology:
non- (prefix):  Latin origin, meaning "not"
defect (noun): Latin origin, from *dēfectus*, past participle of *dēficere* "to fail, desert, abandon," from *dē-* "away" + *facere* "to do, make."
-ive (suffix):  Forms adjectives, indicating a tendency or capacity.
-ness (suffix): Forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
-es (suffix): Plural form of nouns ending in -ness.
