knackered:/ˈnækəd/ (UK) /ˈnækərd/ (US)| adj.| 疲惫不堪的; 破旧的 | exhausted; broken or worn out |例句：I'm absolutely knackered after that run.  跑完之后我累坏了。

例句：My old car is completely knackered. 我的旧车完全报废了。
