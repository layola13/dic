convening:/kənˈviːnɪŋ/
n.|召集，会议|The convening of the council was delayed. 理事会的召开被推迟了。|
近义词：assembly, gathering, meeting, summon
反义词：adjournment, dispersal, dissolution
词性: 名词

convening:/kənˈviːnɪŋ/
adj.|召集的|The convening authority has the power to set the agenda. 召集当局有权设定议程。|
近义词：assembling, gathering
反义词：adjourning, dispersing
词性: 形容词

convene:/kənˈviːn/
v.|召集，集合|The committee will convene next week. 委员会将于下周召开会议。|
近义词：assemble, gather, summon, convoke
反义词：adjourn, disperse, dissolve
词性: 动词

词源: From Old French *convenir, from Latin convenīre (“to come together, assemble”), from con- (“together”) + venīre (“to come”).
