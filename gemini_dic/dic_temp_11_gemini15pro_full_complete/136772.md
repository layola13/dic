nonsequestered: /ˌnɒnˈsɪkwɛstəd/ | adj. | 非隔离的，未扣押的 | Not sequestered; not isolated or hidden away; freely available. | The jury remained nonsequestered throughout the trial, allowing them to go home each night.  陪审团在整个审判过程中都没有被隔离，允许他们每晚回家。|  The funds were nonsequestered and immediately available for use. 这些资金没有被扣押，可以立即使用。 |Synonyms: available, accessible, free, unconfined, unrestricted | Antonyms: sequestered, isolated, confined, restricted, hidden | adjective

nonsequestered: /ˌnɒnˈsɪkwɛstəd/ | adj. | 未扣押的 |  Not seized or legally withheld. |  The nonsequestered assets were transferred to the new owner. 未被扣押的资产被转移给了新的所有者。 | The company's nonsequestered funds allowed them to continue operating. 公司未被扣押的资金使其能够继续运营。 | Synonyms: unseized, unreleased, free | Antonyms: sequestered, seized, confiscated, impounded | adjective
