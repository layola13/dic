ameritus:/əˈmɛrɪtəs/
adjective
退休的，荣誉退休的
Having retired but retaining an honorary title corresponding to the last position held, especially in a college or university.
Professor Smith is now emeritus professor of history. 史密斯教授现在是历史学荣誉退休教授。
近义词:retired, honorary
反义词:active, current
词性:adjective

ameritus:/əˈmɛrɪtəs/
noun
荣誉退休人员
A person who has retired but retains an honorary title corresponding to the last position held.
The university invited all its emeriti to the commencement ceremony.  大学邀请了所有荣誉退休人员参加毕业典礼。
近义词:retiree
反义词:employee
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *emeritus, past participle of emerēre (“to serve out one's time, earn by service, deserve”), from ex- (“out”) + merēre (“to deserve, earn”).
