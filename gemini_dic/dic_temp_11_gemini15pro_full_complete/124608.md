mousiest:/ˈmaʊsiɪst/| adj.|最胆怯的，最羞怯的，最不起眼的|Superlative adjective of 'mousy'.  Characterized by being timid, shy, or inconspicuous, like a mouse.|She was the mousiest girl in the class, always blending into the background.|近义词:timidest, shyest, meekest, quietest|反义词:boldest, bravest, most outgoing, most extroverted|词性:形容词
词源: From mouse + -y + -est.  "Mouse" evokes the image of a small, timid creature, and "-y" forms an adjective meaning "like a mouse."  "-est" is the superlative suffix, indicating the highest degree of the quality.
