matwali:/mɑːˈtwɑːliː/
n.
醉汉；酒鬼；经常饮酒的人
A person who habitually drinks alcohol, especially to excess.
He was a notorious matwali, often seen staggering through the streets. 他是个臭名昭著的酒鬼，经常被人看到在街上摇摇晃晃。
近义词:drunkard, alcoholic, sot
反义词:teetotaler, abstainer
词性:名词


matwali:/mɑːˈtwɑːliː/
adj.
酗酒的；醉醺醺的
Relating to or characteristic of a matwali; given to excessive drinking.
His matwali behavior embarrassed his family. 他酗酒的行为让他的家人感到尴尬。
近义词:drunken, intoxicated, inebriated
反义词:sober, temperate
词性:形容词


词源: From Swahili *matwali*, plural of *mtwali* ("drinker").
