misorganizing: /ˌmɪsˈɔːrɡənaɪzɪŋ/
verb
组织混乱，管理不善
The act of organizing something poorly or inefficiently, resulting in disorder or confusion.
He was fired for constantly misorganizing important documents. 他因经常把重要文件弄乱而被解雇。
The misorganizing of the event led to chaos and confusion. 活动组织不善导致混乱。
近义词: disorganizing, messing up, muddling
反义词: organizing, arranging, ordering
词性: verb (present participle)
