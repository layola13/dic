huso:/ˈhjuːsoʊ/ /ˈhjuːsəʊ/ | n. | 欧洲鳇 | A large sturgeon ( *Huso huso* ) native to the Caspian and Black seas and their tributaries, valued as a source of caviar. |  例句：The huso is the largest freshwater fish in the world. 欧洲鳇是世界上最大的淡水鱼。

例句：The huso population has declined dramatically due to overfishing. 由于过度捕捞，欧洲鳇的数量急剧下降。 | 近义词：beluga sturgeon, giant sturgeon | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Russian белу́га (belúga), from белый (bélyj, “white”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰel- (“to shine, burn, gleam; white”).
