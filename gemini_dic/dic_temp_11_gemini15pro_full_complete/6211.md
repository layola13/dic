ammonitoid: /ˈæməˌnɔɪtɔɪd/ | n. | 菊石类 | An extinct group of cephalopods (marine mollusks) related to the modern nautilus, characterized by a coiled, chambered shell. |  Example: Ammonitoids were abundant in the Mesozoic Era. 菊石类在中生代很丰富。

Example:  The museum displayed a collection of well-preserved ammonitoid fossils.  博物馆展出了一批保存完好的菊石类化石。

| 近义词: ammonite, cephalopod | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it's an extinct group) | 词性: noun
