cammed:/kæmd/ | adj. | 凸轮的，装有凸轮的 | Equipped with a cam or cams, especially in relation to internal combustion engines.  A cam is a rotating or sliding piece in a mechanical linkage used especially in transforming rotary motion into linear motion or vice versa. | The engine has a highly cammed profile for increased performance.  这台发动机的凸轮轮廓高度凸起，以提高性能。|  high-performance, modified | standard, stock | adjective
