beefeater:/ˈbiːfiːtər/| n.|牛肉佬，(伦敦塔的)守卫，(英国)皇家卫士|A Yeoman Warder of the Tower of London, ceremonial guardian. Historically, these guards were responsible for the safekeeping of the Crown Jewels and the prisoners in the Tower.  The origin of the name is debated, but it likely refers to their historical right to a ration of beef.|例句：The beefeater gave a tour of the Tower of London.  皇家卫士带领游客参观了伦敦塔。

例句：The tourists were fascinated by the beefeater's colorful uniform.  游客们被皇家卫士鲜艳的制服所吸引。|近义词：guard, warden, sentinel|反义词:prisoner, captive|词性:noun
