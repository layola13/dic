overdecoration:/ˌoʊvərˌdɛkəˈreɪʃən/
n.
过度装饰
Excessive decoration; the act of decorating something too much.
The overdecoration of the room made it feel cluttered and gaudy.  这个房间的过度装饰使它感觉杂乱而俗气。
Overdecoration can sometimes detract from the beauty of a space. 过度装饰有时会降低空间的美感。
近义词: embellishment, ornamentation, adornment
反义词: simplicity, minimalism
