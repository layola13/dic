macrolinguistics:/ˌmækɹəʊlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪks/ | n. |宏观语言学|The study of language in relation to social, cultural, and historical factors, rather than focusing on the internal structure of language itself. |Macrolinguistics examines the relationship between language and society. 宏观语言学研究语言和社会的关系。|Macrolinguistics encompasses areas like sociolinguistics, psycholinguistics, and historical linguistics. 宏观语言学包含社会语言学、心理语言学和历史语言学等领域。| 近义词: General linguistics | 反义词: Microlinguistics |词性: noun
