drip-feed:/ˈdrɪp fiːd/ (UK) /ˈdrɪp fiːd/ (US)| v. n.| 滴灌；慢慢地提供信息；|To supply something gradually or in small amounts over a period of time.| The information was drip-fed to the media over several weeks.  这些信息在几周内逐渐提供给了媒体。|近义词：trickle, ration, mete out|反义词：flood, deluge|词性:动词,名词

drip-feed:/ˈdrɪp fiːd/ (UK) /ˈdrɪp fiːd/ (US)| n.| 滴灌；点滴供给；|A system of supplying something gradually or in small amounts over a period of time, or the thing that is supplied in this way.| The government's drip-feed of information about the crisis only increased public anxiety. 政府对危机的信息点滴供给只会加剧公众的焦虑。|近义词：trickle, rationing|反义词：outpouring, flood|词性:名词
