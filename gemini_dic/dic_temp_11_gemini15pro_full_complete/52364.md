dictatrix:/dɪkˈteɪtrɪks/
n.
女独裁者；女暴君
a female dictator; a woman who rules oppressively and autocratically.
The dictatrix ruled the small island nation with an iron fist.  这位女独裁者用铁腕统治着这个小岛国。
The ruthless dictatrix silenced all opposition.  这位无情的女独裁者压制了所有反对意见。

近义词:autocrat, tyrant, despot
反义词:democrat
词性:noun
