octameter:/ɒkˈtæmɪtər/ | n. | 八音步诗; 八音步诗行 | A line of verse consisting of eight metrical feet. |  Example 1:  The poem was written in perfect octameter, creating a rhythmic and flowing feel. 这首诗以完美的八音步写成，创造了一种有节奏感和流畅的感觉。

Example 2:  Shakespeare rarely used octameter, preferring iambic pentameter for his plays. 莎士比亚很少使用八音步，在他的戏剧中更喜欢抑扬格五音步。| 近义词: verse, line (though these are more general) | 反义词:  N/A (no direct opposite for a metrical unit) | 词性: noun
