homiletical:/ˌhɒmɪˈlɛtɪkəl/ |adj.|布道的；讲道的；说教的|Of or relating to homilies or sermons; resembling a homily or sermon in style or character.  Characterized by or given to preaching; didactic.|His homiletical style was engaging and thought-provoking. 他的讲道风格引人入胜，发人深省。|近义词：preachy, didactic, sermonic|反义词：secular, worldly|词性: adjective
