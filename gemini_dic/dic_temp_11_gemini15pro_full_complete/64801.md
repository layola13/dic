esophagotomy: /ɪˌsɒfəˈɡɒtəmi/ /ɪˌsɑːfəˈɡɑːtəmi/
noun
食管切开术
Surgical incision into the esophagus.
An esophagotomy was performed to remove the obstruction.  食管切开术是为了移除阻塞物。
The surgeon performed an esophagotomy to remove the foreign body.  外科医生进行了食管切开术以取出异物。
synonyms: esophageal incision
antonyms: N/A
n.


Etymology:
From eso- (variant of eso- before vowels, from Greek eisō “within, inside”) + phag- (before vowels, phago-, from Greek phagein “to eat”) + -tomy (suffix forming nouns of action, from Greek -tomia, from temnein "to cut").
