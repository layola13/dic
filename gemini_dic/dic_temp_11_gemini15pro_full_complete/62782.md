engraving: /ɪnˈɡreɪvɪŋ/ (US) /ɪnˈɡreɪvɪŋ/ (UK)
n.|雕刻, 雕刻品|The act or art of cutting or carving letters, designs, etc., into a hard surface, or the design produced.|例句：The engraving on the ring was beautiful. 戒指上的雕刻很漂亮。|近义词：carving, etching, inscription|反义词: - |noun
engraving: /ɪnˈɡreɪvɪŋ/ (US) /ɪnˈɡreɪvɪŋ/ (UK)
v.|雕刻|To cut or carve (letters, designs, etc.) into a hard surface.|例句：He engraved his name on the tree. 他把他的名字刻在树上。|近义词：carve, etch, inscribe|反义词: - |verb
