observable:/əbˈzɜːrvəbl/ /əbˈsɜːrvəbl/| adj. n.|可观察的；可觉察的；显著的；观察者|Something that is observable can be noticed or seen.|The changes in his behavior were readily observable. 他的行为变化很容易被察觉到.|近义词：perceptible, noticeable, detectable, visible, apparent|反义词：imperceptible, unnoticeable, invisible|词性:形容词

observable:/əbˈzɜːrvəbl/ /əbˈsɜːrvəbl/| n.|可观察量；被观察者|Something that can be observed and measured.|Economic indicators are key observables for policymakers. 经济指标是决策者重要的可观察量.|近义词：phenomenon, quantity|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin observabilis, from observare ‘observe’.
