laurentide: /lɔːˈrɛnˌtaɪd/ | adj. n. | 劳伦泰德的；劳伦泰德冰盖 | Relating to the Laurentide Ice Sheet, a massive ice sheet that covered much of North America during the last glacial period; the Laurentide Ice Sheet itself. | The laurentide ice sheet shaped the landscape of Canada. 劳伦泰德冰盖塑造了加拿大的地貌。|  The retreat of the Laurentide ice sheet created the Great Lakes. 劳伦泰德冰盖的消退形成了五大湖。 | 近义词: glacial, Pleistocene | 反义词: (无直接反义词) | 词性: adjective, noun


Etymology:  From the Saint Lawrence River (French: *Saint Laurent*) + *-ide*, a suffix used to form adjectives and nouns relating to geographical features.
