odorament:/ˈoʊdərəmənt/| n.|香料，芳香剂|A substance used to impart a pleasant odor; a perfume or fragrance. |The ancient Egyptians used various odoraments in their religious ceremonies. 古代埃及人在他们的宗教仪式中使用各种香料。|The odorament filled the room with a sweet, floral scent. 香料使房间充满了甜美的花香。|synonyms: perfume, fragrance, scent, aroma, incense|antonyms: stench, stink, reek, malodor|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *odoramentum, from Latin odorare "to smell," from odor "smell."
