merchandisable:/ˈmɜːrtʃəndaɪzəbl/ /ˈmɜːr-/
| adj. |适于销售的，可销售的|Suitable to be merchandised; capable of being marketed or sold.|This old furniture is still perfectly merchandisable. 这些旧家具仍然完全可以销售。| 近义词: marketable, salable, vendable | 反义词: unsalable, unmarketable | 词性: adjective

merchandise: /ˈmɜːrtʃəndaɪs/ /ˈmɜːr-/
| n. | 商品，货物 | Goods to be bought and sold; wares; commodities. | The shop sells a wide variety of merchandise. 这家商店出售各种各样的商品。 | 词源: From Old French *marchandise*, from marchand (“merchant”) + -ise. |
