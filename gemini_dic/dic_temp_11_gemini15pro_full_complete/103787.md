journo:/ˈdʒɜːrnəʊ/ (UK) /ˈdʒɜːrnoʊ/ (US)
noun
记者,新闻工作者
A journalist.  "Journo" is an informal and often slightly derogatory term for a journalist.
The journos were camped outside the celebrity's house.  那些记者在名人的房子外面安营扎寨。
synonyms: reporter, journalist, correspondent, newsman, newshound
antonyms:  None (It's a profession, not a concept with an opposite.)
词性: noun
