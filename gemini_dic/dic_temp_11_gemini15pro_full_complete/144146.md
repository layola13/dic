outshamed:/ˌaʊtˈʃeɪmd/
verb
使感到羞愧，使相形见绌
made to feel ashamed or inferior.
He felt completely outshamed by her generosity. 他被她的慷慨大方弄得非常羞愧。
近义词: humiliated, embarrassed, mortified
反义词: proud, honored
词性: verb

词源:  "Out-" (prefix meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "shamed" (past participle of "shame").  The prefix intensifies the meaning of "shamed," indicating a greater degree of shame than simply being ashamed.
