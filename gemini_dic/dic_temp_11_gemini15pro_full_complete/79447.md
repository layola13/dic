glittering:/ˈɡlɪtərɪŋ/ /ˈɡlɪtərɪŋ/ | adj. | 闪亮的，灿烂的 | shining brightly with many small flashes of light | The actress wore a glittering diamond necklace.  这位女演员戴着一条闪闪发光的钻石项链。 | synonyms: sparkling, shimmering, twinkling, flashing, gleaming | antonyms: dull, lackluster, matte | adjective

glittering:/ˈɡlɪtərɪŋ/ /ˈɡlɪtərɪŋ/ | n. | 闪光，灿烂 | the quality of shining brightly with many small flashes of light | The glittering of the city lights at night is breathtaking.  夜晚城市灯光的闪烁令人叹为观止。 | synonyms: sparkle, shimmer, twinkle, flash, gleam | antonyms: dullness, lack of luster | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *gliteren*, from Old English *glit(e)rian* (“to glitter, shine, sparkle”), from Proto-Germanic *glitrōną* (“to shine, glitter”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰel- (“to shine, gleam”).  Cognate with Scots gliter (“to glitter”), West Frisian glitterje (“to glitter”), Dutch glitteren (“to glitter”), German glitzern (“to glitter”), Swedish glittra (“to glitter”).
