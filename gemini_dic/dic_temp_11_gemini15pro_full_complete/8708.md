anticriticalnesses:/ˌæntiˈkrɪtɪkəlnəsɪz/
n.
反对批评的态度；不愿接受批评的倾向
The state or quality of being anticritical; an unwillingness to accept or consider criticism.
His anticriticalnesses prevented him from acknowledging the flaws in his work. 他不愿接受批评的态度让他无法认识到自己工作中的缺陷。
近义词：defensiveness, resistance to criticism, intolerance of criticism
反义词：openness to criticism, receptiveness to feedback, willingness to learn
