lymph:/lɪmf/| n.|淋巴；淋巴液|A colorless fluid containing white blood cells, which bathes the tissues and drains through the lymphatic system into the bloodstream.|The doctor tested a sample of his lymph. 医生对他的一份淋巴液样本进行了检测。|Synonyms:  lymphatic fluid, serum|Antonyms: None|noun
