forworn:/fɔːrˈwɔːrn/
adjective
疲惫不堪的；憔悴的
Exhausted or worn out, especially from overwork or suffering.
Her face was forworn and lined with worry. 她的脸上写满了疲惫和担忧。
The troops were forworn after months of battle.  几个月 battles下来，军队疲惫不堪。
近义词: exhausted, worn out, haggard, spent, weary
反义词: refreshed, energetic, vigorous
词性: adjective

forlorn:/fərˈlɔːrn/ (Often confused with forworn)
adjective
孤独凄凉的；被遗弃的；绝望的
Pitifully sad and abandoned or lonely.
A forlorn figure stood at the bus stop. 一个孤独的身影站在公共汽车站。
She felt forlorn and forgotten. 她感到孤独和被遗忘。
近义词: desolate, lonely, abandoned, miserable, wretched
反义词: cheerful, happy, hopeful

词源:  "Forworn" comes from Old English "forwearn," past participle of "forwearnan," meaning "to wear out," from "for-" (intensive prefix) + "wearnan" (to wear). "Forlorn" comes from Middle English "forloren," past participle of "forlesen," meaning "to lose utterly," from "for-" (intensive prefix) + "lesen" (to lose).
