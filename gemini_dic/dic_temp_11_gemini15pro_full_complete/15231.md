backfilled:/ˌbækˈfɪld/ | v. | 回填；补充；补缺 | To refill (an excavated area) with the material removed during excavation.  To replace or supplement something that is missing or incomplete. | The trench was backfilled with gravel.  沟渠用砾石回填了。 They backfilled the open position with an internal candidate. 他们用内部候选人填补了空缺职位。 | 近义词: refilled, replaced, supplemented | 反义词: excavated, emptied | 词性: 动词

backfilled:/ˌbækˈfɪld/ | adj. | 回填的；补充的 | Having been filled back in or replaced. |  The backfilled area was then leveled. 回填的区域随后被夷平了。 The report included backfilled data from previous years.  报告中包含了前几年的补充数据。| 近义词: filled, replaced | 反义词: excavated, empty | 词性: 形容词
