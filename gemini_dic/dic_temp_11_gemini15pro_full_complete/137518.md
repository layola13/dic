nontraditionalistic:/ˌnɑːnˌtrəˈdɪʃənəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
非传统的
Not adhering to or in accordance with tradition; unconventional.
例句：Her nontraditionalistic approach to education surprised many parents. 她非传统的教育方法让很多家长感到惊讶。

例句：The architect's nontraditionalistic design incorporated elements of modern and classical styles.  这位建筑师非传统的设计融合了现代和古典风格的元素。

近义词：unconventional, unorthodox, innovative, modern, contemporary
反义词：traditional, conventional, orthodox, conservative

词性: adjective
