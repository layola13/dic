billman:/ˈbɪlmən/| n. |持票人，收账员|A person who collects bills or payments.  Specifically, historically, a man who carried a halberd, a combined spear and battle-axe, as a weapon. |The billman came to the door demanding payment. 收账员上门要求付款。|The king's billman stood guard. 国王的戟兵站岗。|collector, payee|debtor|noun
