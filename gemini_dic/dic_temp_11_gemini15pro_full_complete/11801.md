arrangements:/əˈreɪndʒmənts/
|n.|安排，布置；约定；准备工作|The arrangements for the wedding took months. 婚礼的筹备工作花了几个月的时间。|例句：We made arrangements to meet at the cafe. 我们约定在咖啡馆见面。|近义词：preparations, plans, agreements|反义词：disorder, chaos|词性:名词


Etymology->
From Old French "arengement," from "arenger" (to arrange), ultimately from Frankish "*hring" (ring, circle). The original sense was of putting things in a circle or order.
