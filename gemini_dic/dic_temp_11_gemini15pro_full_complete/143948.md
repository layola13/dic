outquaffs:/ˌaʊtˈkwɒfs/
verb
痛饮，比…喝得更多
drinks more than (someone else).
He outquaffed all his companions at the pub. 他在酒吧里比他的所有同伴都喝得多。
近义词: outdrinks, surpasses in drinking
反义词: is outdrunk by, drinks less than


ETYMOLOGY->
From "out-" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "quaff" (meaning to drink heartily).  "Quaff" comes from Middle English "quaffen," probably of imitative origin.
