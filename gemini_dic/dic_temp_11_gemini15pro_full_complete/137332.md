nontariffs:/ˌnɑːnˈtɛrɪfs/ | n. | 非关税壁垒 |  Government regulations that restrict trade without imposing a direct tax or duty. These regulations can include quotas, embargoes, sanctions, and licensing requirements.  |  Many countries use nontariffs to protect their domestic industries. 许多国家使用非关税壁垒来保护其国内产业。| synonyms: nontariff barriers, trade restrictions | antonyms: tariffs, free trade | noun
