cabbling:/ˈkæblɪŋ/ (US) /ˈkeɪblɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|布线；粗缝；粗制滥造|The act or process of making cables, or the cables themselves, especially in a ship.  The act of making or repairing something in a rough or clumsy way.|The ship's cabling was old and needed replacing. 船上的布线很旧，需要更换。|近义词：wiring, cabling, ropework|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "meticulous work")|noun
