jawing:/ˈdʒɔːɪŋ/ | v. | 喋喋不休，闲聊 | talking continuously, especially about unimportant things |  He was jawing on about his new car for hours. 他一直在喋喋不休地谈论他的新车好几个小时。|  The two women were jawing away in the corner.  那两个女人在角落里聊个不停。 | synonyms: chattering, gossiping, prattling | antonyms: listening, being quiet | verb
