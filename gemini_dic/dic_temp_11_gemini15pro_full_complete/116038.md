marteline:/ˈmɑːrtəlɪn/| n. |马特林，一种小锤子|A small hammer, usually with a short handle and a peen (the back part of the hammerhead) that is either chisel-shaped or rounded. Used by sculptors, stone carvers, and other artisans.|The sculptor used a marteline to delicately shape the marble. 雕塑家使用马特林精细地塑造大理石。|近义词：hammer, mallet|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
Etymology: From French *marteline*, diminutive of *martel* "hammer," from Late Latin *martellus*, from Latin *marculus*, diminutive of *marcus* "hammer."
