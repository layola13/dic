focuser:/ˈfoʊkəsər/ (US) /ˈfəʊkəsə(r)/ (UK)| n.|调焦器; 聚焦装置;  集中注意力的人或物|A device used to adjust the focus of an optical instrument, such as a telescope or microscope.  Someone or something that helps to concentrate attention or effort.|The astronomer adjusted the focuser to get a clearer image of the nebula. 天文学家调整了调焦器，以便更清晰地观察星云。|近义词: adjuster, concentrator|反义词: diffuser, scatterer|词性:noun

focuser: /ˈfoʊkəsər/ (US) /ˈfəʊkəsə(r)/ (UK)|n.|对焦者，专注者|Someone who focuses or concentrates, particularly in a professional context, like a "laser focuser".|The team needs a focuser to keep the project on track.  团队需要一个专注者来保持项目进度。|近义词: specialist, expert |反义词: generalist, dilettante |词性: noun
