inarguably:/ˌɪnˈɑːrɡjuəbli/
adv.
无可争辩地，毋庸置疑地
Undeniably; without any doubt.  It is used to emphasize that something is definitely true.
Her talent is inarguably immense. 她的才华无可争辩地巨大。
He is inarguably the best player on the team. 他无疑是队里最好的球员。
近义词:undeniably, indisputably, unquestionably
反义词:arguably, disputably, questionably
词性:副词

词源:  "in-" (not) + "arguable" (able to be argued against) + "-ly" (adverb suffix).  Ultimately from Latin "arguere" (to make clear, prove, argue).
