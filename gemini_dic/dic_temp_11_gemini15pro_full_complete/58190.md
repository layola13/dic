drowsinesses:/ˈdraʊzɪnəsɪz/
n.
困倦，睡意
A state of feeling sleepy and lethargic.
The long drive induced drowsinesses in the passengers. 长途驾驶让乘客昏昏欲睡。
近义词：sleepiness, lethargy, somnolence
反义词：wakefulness, alertness, vigilance
词性:名词(plural of drowsiness)
词源：From Middle English *drouwsinesse, equivalent to drowsy +‎ -ness.
