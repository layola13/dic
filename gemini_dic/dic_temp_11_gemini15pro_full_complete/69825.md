ferrer:/ˈfɛrər/ | n. | 铁匠，金属工匠 | A person who works with metal, especially one who shapes metal by heating it in a forge and hammering it. |  The ferrer skillfully crafted a horseshoe. 铁匠熟练地制作了一个马蹄铁。

The ferrer's hands were calloused from years of working the forge.  铁匠的双手因多年在锻造厂工作而变得粗糙。 | 近义词: blacksmith, metalsmith, smith | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *ferier, from Latin ferrarius (“of or belonging to iron”), from ferrum (“iron”).
