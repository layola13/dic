muricate: /ˈmjʊərɪkeɪt/ | n. | 有刺状突起的物体 |  Something that is covered with short, hard, pointed outgrowths. |  This rare muricate is highly prized by collectors.  这种稀有的带刺状突起的物体受到收藏家的高度重视。 | bur, barb | smooth object | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *muricatus*, past participle of *muricāre* (“to make rough like the murex”), from *murex* (“murex, a shellfish with a rough shell”).
