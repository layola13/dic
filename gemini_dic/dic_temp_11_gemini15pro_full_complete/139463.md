obstetrical:/əbˈstɛtrɪkəl/
adjective
产科的
Relating to childbirth and the processes associated with it.
例句：She had some obstetrical complications during her pregnancy. 她怀孕期间有一些产科并发症。

例句：The hospital has a renowned obstetrical unit. 这家医院有一个著名的产科病房。

近义词：gynecological, prenatal, perinatal
反义词：(No direct antonym, possibly pediatric)
词性：adjective

obstetrics:/əbˈstɛtrɪks/
noun
产科学
The branch of medicine and surgery concerned with childbirth and the care of women giving birth.
例句：She specializes in obstetrics and gynecology. 她专攻妇产科。

例句：Advances in obstetrics have greatly reduced maternal mortality. 产科学的进步大大降低了孕产妇死亡率。

近义词：midwifery (partially), tocogynecology
反义词：(No direct antonym)
词性：noun
