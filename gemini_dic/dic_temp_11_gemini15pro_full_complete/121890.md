missa:/ˈmɪsə/| n.|弥撒;弥撒曲|A (usually Catholic) religious service or a musical setting for such a service.  It is derived from the Late Latin phrase "Ite, missa est" ("Go, the congregation is dismissed").|The choir sang a beautiful missa.|近义词:Mass, service, liturgy|反义词:None|noun
