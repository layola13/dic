mites:/maɪts/ | n. | 螨虫；极少量 | Tiny arachnids, often parasitic, that infest plants, animals, and stored food. Also used to mean a very small amount. | The dog was covered in mites. 这只狗身上全是螨虫。 | synonyms: acari,  tiny creatures | antonyms: giants,  large amounts | noun

mites:/maɪts/ | n. | 少量，一点点 | A very small amount or portion of something. |  He hasn't changed a mites. 他一点都没变. | synonyms: bit, smidgen, speck, jot, iota | antonyms: lot, abundance | noun
