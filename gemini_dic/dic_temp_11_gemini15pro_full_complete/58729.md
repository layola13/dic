dunderheadedness:/ˌdʌndərˈhɛdɪdnəs/| n. |愚蠢，笨拙|The sheer dunderheadedness of the plan was astonishing.  这个计划的愚蠢程度令人震惊。|例句：His dunderheadedness led him to make the same mistake repeatedly. 他的愚蠢导致他反复犯同样的错误。

His dunderheadedness cost him his job. 他的愚蠢让他丢了工作。

|近义词：stupidity, foolishness, idiocy, obtuseness|反义词: intelligence, cleverness, brilliance, wisdom|词性:名词


Etymology:  From dunderhead + -ness.  Dunderhead is of uncertain origin, possibly from dunder, a Scots word meaning "dandruff, confusion, stupor," + head.
