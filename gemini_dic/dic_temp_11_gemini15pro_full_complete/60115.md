effacement:/ɪˈfeɪsmənt/
n.
消除；抹去；淡化
the act of effacing something; the state of being effaced.
The artist’s signature was barely visible, lost in the effacement of time. 艺术家的签名几乎看不见了，在时间的流逝中消失了。
synonyms: erasure, obliteration, deletion, removal
antonyms: enhancement, highlighting, emphasis
noun

effacement:/ɪˈfeɪsmənt/
n.
(子宫颈的)变薄；消失
the shortening and thinning of the cervix during labor.
Cervical effacement is a sign that labor is progressing. 子宫颈变薄是分娩进展的标志。
synonyms: thinning, shortening, dilation
antonyms: thickening, lengthening
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *effacement*, from *effacer* "to efface," from *ex-* "out" + *face* "face" (from Latin *facies* "face, appearance").
