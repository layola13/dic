laughingly:/ˈlɑːfɪŋli/| adv.|笑着地，含笑地|In a laughing manner; with laughter.  It describes an action performed while laughing, or in a way that suggests amusement.|She laughingly recounted the story of her mishap. 她笑着讲述了她遭遇不幸的故事。|Synonyms: merrily, mirthfully, jokingly, playfully|Antonyms: seriously, solemnly, somberly|Adverb
