overjealousness:/ˌoʊvərˈdʒɛləsnəs/ | n. | 过度嫉妒，过分猜忌 | The state of being excessively jealous. | His overjealousness was the main reason for their breakup. 他的过度嫉妒是他们分手的主要原因。|  Overjealousness ultimately destroyed their marriage. 过度嫉妒最终毁掉了他们的婚姻。|近义词：jealousy, possessiveness, suspicion |反义词：trust, security |词性: 名词

Etymology:
From over- + jealousness.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much". "Jealousness" comes from the Old French "jalus," meaning "jealous" or "zealous," ultimately derived from the Latin "zelus," meaning "zeal" or "emulation."
