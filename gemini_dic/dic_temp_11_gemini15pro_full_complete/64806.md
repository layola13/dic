esops:/ˈiːsɒps/| n.|员工持股计划 (Employee Stock Ownership Plan)|An ESOP is a qualified defined-contribution employee benefit plan designed to invest primarily in the stock of the sponsoring employer.|Many companies offer ESOPs as a retirement benefit for their employees. 许多公司为员工提供员工持股计划作为退休福利。|近义词：stock option plan, share purchase plan|反义词:|noun
