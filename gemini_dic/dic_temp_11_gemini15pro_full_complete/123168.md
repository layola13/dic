monkish:/ˈmʌŋkɪʃ/| adj.|僧侣般的，像僧侣的；禁欲的，苦行的|Resembling or characteristic of a monk, especially in devotion to religious duties and austere habits.|He lived a monkish existence, dedicating himself to prayer and study. 他过着僧侣般的生活，致力于祈祷和学习。|The room was sparsely furnished, almost monkish in its simplicity.  房间里陈设简陋，其简洁程度近乎僧侣的苦行。|近义词：ascetic, austere, monastic|反义词：worldly, secular, hedonistic|词性:形容词
词源: From monk + -ish.  Ultimately from Greek *monakhos* "monk," literally "solitary."
