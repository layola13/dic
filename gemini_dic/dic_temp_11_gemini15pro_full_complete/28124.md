capitated:/ˈkæpɪteɪtɪd/
adjective
按人头收费的，定额补贴的
Paid a fixed amount per person.
例句：The doctor is paid a capitated fee for each patient on his list.  这位医生按其名单上的每个病人收取固定费用。

例句：Capitated payments are common in managed care plans.  定额支付在管理式医疗计划中很常见。

近义词：per capita, fixed-fee
反义词：fee-for-service
词性: adjective
