outsweeten:/ˌaʊtˈswiːtn/
verb
胜过…的甜美; 比…更甜
to surpass in sweetness.
The artificial sweetener outsweetens sugar by many times.  这种人造甜味剂比糖甜很多倍。
The honey from the local bees outsweetens any I've ever tasted.  当地蜜蜂产的蜂蜜比我尝过的任何蜂蜜都甜。
近义词:surpass, exceed
反义词:undersweeten (if such a word existed)
词性:verb
