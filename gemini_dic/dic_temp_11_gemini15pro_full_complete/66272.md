excoct:/ɪkˈskɒkt/ /ɛkˈskɑːkt/ | v. | 提取，提炼；精心制作 | To extract or derive (something, such as a juice or an essence) by distillation, expression, or other means; to devise, contrive, or develop with care and detail. |  He excocted a potent medicine from rare herbs. 他用稀有草药精心制作了一种强效药。

The playwright excocted a compelling drama from a simple anecdote. 剧作家从一个简单的轶事中精心创作了一部引人入胜的戏剧。 | 近义词: extract, derive, distill, concoct, devise | 反义词: synthesize, combine, insert | 词性: 动词
