biodiversification:/ˌbaɪoʊdaɪˌvɜːrsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 生物多样化 | The process by which the diversity of life increases over time, through the evolution of new species. | Example 1: The biodiversification of the rainforest is astounding.  热带雨林的生物多样性令人震惊。

Example 2:  Scientists are studying the factors that contribute to biodiversification. 科学家们正在研究导致生物多样化的因素。 | Synonyms: diversification of life, biodiversity development | Antonyms:  biotic homogenization, biodiversity loss | noun



ETYMOLOGY->

bio- + diversification

bio-: from Greek *bios* "life"

diversification: from diversify + -fication. Diversify from Late Latin *diversificare* "to make diverse," from Latin *diversus* "different" + *-ficare*, a combining form meaning "to make" or "to cause to be."
