disservicing:/dɪsˈsɜːrvɪsɪŋ/
verb
损害；帮倒忙
doing harm to; failing to serve
By constantly interrupting, she was disservicing her own cause. 她不断地打断，反而损害了自己的事业。
disserving the community by spreading misinformation 通过散布错误信息损害社区
近义词: harming, damaging, undermining, hindering, obstructing
反义词: helping, aiding, assisting, supporting, benefiting
词性: verb (present participle)

disservice:/dɪsˈsɜːrvɪs/
noun
损害；帮倒忙
a harmful action; an ill turn
He did her a great disservice by failing to mention her contribution. 他没有提及她的贡献，这对她很不利。
Spreading false rumors does a disservice to everyone involved. 散布谣言对所有相关人员都是一种伤害。
近义词: detriment, injury, harm, damage
反义词: service, benefit, help, aid
词性: noun
