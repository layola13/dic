hippieish:/ˈhɪpiɪʃ/| adj.|嬉皮士似的，嬉皮风格的|Resembling or characteristic of a hippie, especially in dress, lifestyle, or attitudes.|Her clothes were kind of hippieish, with long flowing skirts and beaded necklaces. 她的衣服有点嬉皮士风格，穿着飘逸的长裙和串珠项链。|Example: He had a hippieish beard and wore tie-dye shirts. 他留着嬉皮士式的胡子，穿着扎染衬衫。|Synonyms: bohemian, unconventional, countercultural|Antonyms: conventional, traditional, conformist|Word type: adjective
