gumbo:/ˈɡʌmboʊ/| n.|秋葵汤，浓汤；混合物|A stew thickened with okra pods or filé powder, usually containing meat or shellfish and vegetables, and served over rice.  A mixture of things.|例句：She made a delicious gumbo for dinner. 她晚饭做了一锅美味的秋葵汤。|例句：The city is a gumbo of different cultures. 这座城市是不同文化的混合体。|近义词：stew, soup, mix, medley|反义词:|noun
