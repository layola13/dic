outjumped:/ˌaʊtˈdʒʌmpt/
verb
跳得比...远/高; 超越
To jump farther or higher than; to surpass in jumping.
He outjumped all his competitors in the high jump. 他在跳高比赛中跳得比所有竞争对手都高。
She outjumped her personal best by several inches. 她跳得比她个人最好成绩还要高出几英寸。
surpass, exceed, outleap
fall short of, be inferior to
v.
