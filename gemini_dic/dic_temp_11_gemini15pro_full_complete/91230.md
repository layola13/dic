hydrocephaloid:/ˌhaɪdrɵˈsɛfəlɔɪd/ | adj. n.| 脑积水样的；假性脑积水|A condition, especially in infants, resembling hydrocephalus but not caused by an increase of cerebrospinal fluid within the skull.|The infant presented with symptoms of hydrocephaloid, including lethargy and a sunken fontanelle.  婴儿出现假性脑积水的症状，包括嗜睡和囟门凹陷。|Near synonyms: hydrencephaloid, spurious hydrocephalus|Antonyms: hydrocephalus|noun, adjective
