humectant:/hjuːˈmɛktənt/ | n. |保湿剂；润湿剂|A humectant is a substance that attracts and retains moisture. | This lotion contains several humectants to keep your skin hydrated. 这款乳液含有多种保湿剂，可保持肌肤水润。|  Humectants draw moisture from the air to the skin. 保湿剂将空气中的水分吸到皮肤上。|Synonyms: moisturizer, emollient | Antonyms: desiccant, drying agent | Noun

humectants:/hjuːˈmɛktənts/ | n. |保湿剂；润湿剂（复数）|Humectants are substances that attract and retain moisture. |Many cosmetic products contain humectants. 许多化妆品都含有保湿剂。| Humectants help prevent products from drying out. 保湿剂有助于防止产品变干。|Synonyms: moisturizers, emollients | Antonyms: desiccants, drying agents | Noun
