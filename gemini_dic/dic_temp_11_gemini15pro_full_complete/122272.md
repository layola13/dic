mitigations:/ˌmɪtɪˈɡeɪʃənz/
|n.| 减轻，缓和，缓解措施|The government is taking mitigations to reduce the impact of the recession. 政府正在采取缓解措施以减少经济衰退的影响。|
|近义词：remedies, solutions, alleviations,  |
|反义词：exacerbations, aggravations|
