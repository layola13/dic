foggy:/ˈfɒɡi/ (UK) /ˈfɑːɡi/ (US)| adj.|有雾的；模糊的；含糊不清的|Characterized by the presence of fog; indistinct or unclear.|
The morning was foggy, making driving difficult. 早晨雾很大，开车很困难。

The old man's memories were becoming foggy. 老人的记忆变得模糊不清了。


近义词：misty, hazy, obscure, vague|
反义词：clear, bright, distinct|
词性：adjective
