nonbulbaceous:/ˌnɒnbʌlˈbeɪʃəs/
adjective
非球茎的
Not having a bulb; lacking a bulbous part.
This perennial is nonbulbaceous and spreads by rhizomes. 这种多年生植物是非球茎的，通过根茎传播。
Near synonyms: acaulescent, nonbulbous
Antonyms: bulbous, bulbaceous
