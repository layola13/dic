drabbest:/ˈdræbɪst/| adj.|最单调乏味的，最无聊的|Superlative form of drab: most drab.  It means extremely dull and lacking in color or interest.|The party was the drabbest event I've ever attended.  这个派对是我参加过的最无聊的活动。|The waiting room was drabbest, with beige walls and uncomfortable chairs. 候诊室非常单调，米色的墙壁和不舒服的椅子。|近义词：dullest, dreariest, most boring, most tedious|反义词：brightest, most vibrant, most exciting, most interesting|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From drab + -est.  "Drab" originates from the early 16th century,  possibly from the Old French *drape, meaning "cloth," referring to undyed or dull-colored cloth.
