gelds:/ɡɛlds/ | n. | money (South African informal) | Money; funds.  This word is primarily used in South African informal English. | Example:  He needs more gelds to buy a car. 他需要更多的钱来买车。Example: She doesn't have enough gelds for the groceries. 她没有足够的钱买杂货。 | Synonym: cash, dough, moolah (informal), funds | Antonym: debt, poverty | noun
