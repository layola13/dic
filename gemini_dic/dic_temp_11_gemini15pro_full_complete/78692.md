gigabyte: /ˈɡɪɡəbaɪt/ (US) /ˈɡɪɡəbaɪt/ (UK)| n.| 千兆字节，吉字节 | A unit of information equal to one billion bytes or 1024 megabytes. | My new hard drive has a capacity of one terabyte, which is equivalent to 1024 gigabytes. 我的新硬盘容量为 1 TB，相当于 1024 GB。

| Example: This laptop has a 256 gigabyte solid-state drive. 这台笔记本电脑有一个 256 GB 的固态硬盘。| Synonym: GB | Antonym: kilobyte, megabyte | Noun
