carroting:/ˈkærətɪŋ/| n.|胡萝卜疗法（一种未经证实的治疗方法，声称可以改善视力）;  胡萝卜素沉着症（carotenemia 的一种罕见拼写）|A treatment, claimed to improve eyesight, involving the consumption of large quantities of carrots.  A rare spelling of carotenemia.|He tried carroting to improve his vision, but it didn't work.|近义词: none (for the "treatment" meaning); carotenemia (for the medical condition)|反义词: none|词性: noun

carroting:/ˈkærətɪŋ/| adj.|与胡萝卜有关的；胡萝卜色的|Relating to carrots; carrot-colored.|The soup had a carroting hue.|近义词: carrot-colored, orange|反义词: none|词性: adjective
