irrubrical: /ɪˈrʌbrɪkəl/
adjective
不可分类的；无规则的；不循常规的
Not falling under any established rule, principle, or category; irregular.
His conduct was entirely irrubrical, defying all attempts at categorization. 他的行为完全不循常规，任何试图将其分类的做法都失败了。
The artist's style is irrubrical, making it difficult to place him within any particular movement.  这位艺术家的风格不可分类，很难把他归入任何特定的流派。
近义词: unconventional, anomalous, eccentric, idiosyncratic, aberrant
反义词: conventional, regular, typical, normal, standard
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *irrubricus (“not marked with red ochre”), from in- (“not”) + rubrica (“red ochre, rubric”).
