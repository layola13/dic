ditched:/dɪtʃt/
verb
抛弃，丢弃，摆脱，逃课
To get rid of something or someone that is no longer wanted.
He ditched his old car. 他把他的旧车丢弃了。
She ditched her boyfriend. 她甩了她的男朋友。
近义词: abandoned, discarded, dumped, jettisoned, deserted, left
反义词: kept, retained, saved, cherished

词性: verb

ditched:/dɪtʃt/
adjective
位于沟里的，有沟的
Situated in a ditch.
The car ended up in a ditched field. 汽车最终停在一个有沟的田野里。
The ditched area collected rainwater. 有沟的区域积聚了雨水。
近义词: grooved, furrowed, channeled
反义词: smooth, flat, level

词性: adjective


词源: From the Old English *dīc*, meaning "ditch, dike."
