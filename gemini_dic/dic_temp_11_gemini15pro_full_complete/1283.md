account-executives: /əˈkaʊnt ɪɡˈzɛkjətɪvz/ | n. |客户主管，客户经理|A person who manages the business relationship between a company and its clients, often in advertising, marketing, or sales. They are responsible for maintaining and growing these relationships, ensuring client satisfaction, and achieving sales targets. | Our account executives work closely with clients to understand their needs and develop customized solutions. 我们的客户经理与客户紧密合作，了解他们的需求并制定定制的解决方案。|Synonyms: account manager, client representative, sales representative|Antonyms: customer, client|Noun
