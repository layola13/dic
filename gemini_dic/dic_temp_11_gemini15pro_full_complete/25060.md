btus:/ˈbiːtiːjuːz/ | n. | British thermal units (Btu or BTU) | British thermal units (Btu or BTU) are a traditional unit of heat; it is defined as the amount of heat required to raise the temperature of one pound of water by one degree Fahrenheit. | The air conditioner has a cooling capacity of 12,000 btus. 这台空调的制冷量为12,000 BTU。| synonyms: heat units | antonyms: N/A | noun
