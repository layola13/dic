membranaceous:/ˌmɛmbrəˈneɪʃəs/| adj.|膜状的，膜质的|Thin and flexible like a membrane; resembling a membrane.|The bat's wings are membranaceous. 蝙蝠的翅膀是膜状的。|例句：The plant has delicate, membranaceous leaves. 这种植物有纤细的膜质叶子。|例句：The insect's wings are thin and membranaceous.  这种昆虫的翅膀薄而膜状。|近义词：membranous, filmy, thin, delicate|反义词：thick, solid, rigid|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Latin *membranaceus, from membrana (“membrane”) + -aceus (suffix forming adjectives).
