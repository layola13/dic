in-the-abstract:/ɪn ði ˈæbstrækt/| adverbial phrase |抽象地，理论上|  Considering something theoretically or generally, without reference to specific instances or practical applications. |  He discussed democracy in the abstract, without mentioning any particular country. 他抽象地讨论了民主，没有提及任何具体的国家。| in theory, conceptually | concretely, specifically | phrase

in-the-abstract:/ɪn ði ˈæbstrækt/| adjective |抽象的，理论上的| Existing as a general idea or concept, not related to a specific example. | While the concept sounds good in the abstract, it's difficult to implement in practice.  虽然这个概念在理论上听起来不错，但在实践中却很难实施。| theoretical, conceptual | concrete, specific | adjective
