coworker:/ˈkoʊˌwɜːrkər/ (US) /ˈkəʊˌwɜːkə(r)/ (UK)| n. |同事，同僚|A person who works with another person, especially in the same department or office. |例句：My coworker helped me finish the project. 我的同事帮我完成了这个项目。

例句：I went to lunch with my coworkers. 我和我的同事们一起吃午饭。

近义词：colleague, associate, peer|反义词:boss, supervisor, manager|词性:noun
