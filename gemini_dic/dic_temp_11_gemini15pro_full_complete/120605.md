mirthless:/ˈmɜːθləs/ | adj. | 缺乏欢乐的，不开心的 | Lacking mirth; joyless. | She gave a mirthless laugh. 她干巴巴地笑了笑。|  He told the joke with a mirthless expression. 他面无表情地讲了那个笑话。 | 近义词: joyless, cheerless, somber, gloomy | 反义词: merry, cheerful, joyful, jovial | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From mirth + -less.  Mirth comes from Old English *myrgþu (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *mirghiþō, from *merg- "short, brief" (source also of Old English mierce "pleasant, sweet").  The suffix -less is a common Old English suffix meaning "without."
