majesty:/ˈmædʒɪsti/| n.|陛下；雄伟；壮丽；庄严|A title of address formerly used for kings, queens, emperors, and empresses.  The impressive quality of being majestic.|例句：Her Majesty, the Queen. 女王陛下。

The majesty of the mountains.  群山的雄伟壮丽。|近义词：grandeur, splendor, magnificence, glory|反义词：insignificance, humility, commonness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "majesté", from Latin "maiestas"  meaning "greatness, dignity, honor, reputation". Ultimately from "maius" meaning "larger, greater".
