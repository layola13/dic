basihyoid:/ˌbeɪsiˈhaɪɔɪd/| adj. n.|基舌骨的；基舌骨|Relating to or denoting the basihyal, a bone or cartilage forming the base of the hyoid apparatus in some vertebrates.  As a noun, it refers to the basihyal bone itself.|The basihyoid is a small, U-shaped bone situated at the base of the tongue.|近义词:hypohyal|反义词:|词性:adjective, noun
