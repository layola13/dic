overfree: /ˌoʊvərˈfriː/
verb |过度释放，过度分配| To release or allocate (memory or resources) more often than necessary, leading to instability or errors. | The program crashed because it overfreed memory.  程序崩溃是因为它过度释放了内存。|例句：The system overfreed the memory, causing a segmentation fault. 系统过度释放了内存，导致了段错误。 | 近义词: over-release, deallocate excessively | 反义词: underfree, allocate insufficiently | 词性: verb

overfree: /ˌoʊvərˈfriː/
adjective |过度自由的| Excessively free or permissive; lacking necessary restrictions or controls. | The overfree environment led to chaos. 过度自由的环境导致了混乱。| 例句：The overfree upbringing of the children resulted in a lack of discipline. 孩子们过度自由的教育导致了缺乏纪律。 | 近义词: overly permissive, excessively lenient | 反义词: restrictive, controlled | 词性: adjective
