heptathlon:/hepˈtæθlɒn/ /hepˈtæθlɑːn/
n.
七项全能
An athletic contest for women comprising seven track and field events.
She won the gold medal in the heptathlon. 她赢得了七项全能的金牌。
近义词: decathlon (for men), multi-event
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun

Etymology:
From Greek *hepta- 'seven' + athlon 'contest'.
