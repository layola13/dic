bowne:/boʊn/
noun
装备；装扮
to furnish with necessary equipment or furnishings; outfit
The knight was fully bowne for battle. 骑士已全副武装准备战斗。
近义词: equip, outfit, furnish, accoutre
反义词: strip, divest

verb
准备；预备
to make ready; prepare
He bowned himself for the journey. 他为旅程做好了准备。
近义词: prepare, ready, equip
反义词: unprepared
