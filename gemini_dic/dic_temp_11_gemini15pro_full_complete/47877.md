|n.|减速，减速度|A decrease in speed or rate. The process of slowing down or reducing speed.|The deceleration of the economy is a concern. 经济减速令人担忧。|slowdown, reduction in speed|acceleration, speedup|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dēcelerātiō, from dēcelerāre (“to slow down”), from dē- (“down, from”) + celerāre (“to hasten”), from celer (“swift”).
