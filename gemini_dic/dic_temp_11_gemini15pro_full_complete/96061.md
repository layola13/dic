indecisiveness:/ˌɪndɪˈsaɪsɪvnəs/
n.
犹豫不决;优柔寡断
The state of being unable to make decisions.
His indecisiveness cost him the opportunity. 他的犹豫不决让他错失了机会。

近义词:hesitation, vacillation, irresolution
反义词: decisiveness, resolution, determination
词性:名词
