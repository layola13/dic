elemental:/ˌɛlɪˈmɛnt(ə)l/ | adj. | 基本的，主要的，元素的 | Relating to or being the basic or essential constituent of something. | Elemental forces shaped the landscape over millions of years. 数百万年来，元素力量塑造了地貌。 | synonym: fundamental, basic, essential, primary, rudimentary | antonym: secondary, nonessential, complex, advanced | adjective
