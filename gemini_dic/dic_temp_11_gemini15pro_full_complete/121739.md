misproportion:/ˌmɪsprəˈpɔːrʃən/ | n. | 不均衡，不成比例，比例失调 |  A lack of proper proportion; the fact of some parts of something being too large or too small in relation to others. |  The house seemed odd because of the misproportion of its windows. 这房子看起来很奇怪，因为它的窗户比例失调。 | synonyms: imbalance, disparity, disproportionateness, asymmetry | antonyms: balance, symmetry, proportion, equilibrium | noun


misproportions:/ˌmɪsprəˈpɔːrʃənz/ | n. |  不均衡，不成比例，比例失调（复数） |  Plural form of "misproportion," referring to multiple instances of lacking proper proportion. | The artist deliberately used misproportions to create a surreal effect.  这位艺术家故意运用不成比例的元素来创造一种超现实的效果。 | synonyms: imbalances, disparities, asymmetries | antonyms: balances, symmetries, proportions, equilibria | noun
