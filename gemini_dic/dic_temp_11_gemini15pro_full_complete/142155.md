orison:/ˈɔːrɪzən/ | n. |祈祷，祷告|A prayer or supplication. |例句：She offered a silent orison for peace. 她默默地祈祷和平。

例句：The monk's morning orison was a beautiful chant.  僧侣的晨祷是一曲优美的圣歌。

近义词：prayer, supplication, invocation, petition |反义词: blasphemy, curse |词性: noun
