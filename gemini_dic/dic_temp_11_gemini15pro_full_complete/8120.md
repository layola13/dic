anti-attrition:/ˌænti əˈtrɪʃən/ | adj. | 防流失的，反损耗的 | Designed to prevent or reduce attrition, especially loss of personnel or resources. |  Example 1: The company implemented an anti-attrition program to retain its valuable employees.  公司实施了一项反流失计划以留住其宝贵的员工。

Example 2: The army launched an anti-attrition campaign to minimize casualties.  军队发起了一场反损耗战役，以最大限度地减少伤亡。| Synonyms: retention, preservation, conservation | Antonyms: attrition, depletion, erosion | adjective
