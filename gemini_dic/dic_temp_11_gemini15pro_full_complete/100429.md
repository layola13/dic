intertouches:/ˌɪntərˈtʌtʃɪz/
n.
相互接触; 交互影响
moments of shared experience or connection between people or things.
The novel explores the intertouches between different cultures. 这部小说探讨了不同文化之间的相互影响。
interaction, interplay, interconnection
separation, isolation
noun
