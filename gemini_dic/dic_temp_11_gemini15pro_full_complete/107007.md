lactamide:/ˈlæktəmaɪd/| n. |乳酰胺；乳酸胺|A white crystalline organic compound, the amide of lactic acid. It is used in the pharmaceutical and chemical industries.|Lactamide is used as a humectant in some cosmetics. 乳酰胺在一些化妆品中用作保湿剂。| Lactamide can be used in the synthesis of other organic compounds. 乳酰胺可用于合成其他有机化合物。|Synonyms: 2-hydroxypropanamide|Antonyms: None|noun
