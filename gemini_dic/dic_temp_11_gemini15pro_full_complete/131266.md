noncausable:/ˌnɑnˈkɔːzəbl/ | adj. | 非使役的，不能用使役动词的 | Not able to be caused or brought about; intransitive. An action or event that cannot be caused to happen by an external force or agent.  A noncausable verb cannot be used in the causative voice. | The verb "happen" is noncausable – you can't *make something happen*. “发生”这个动词是非使役的——你不能*让某事发生*。| The flower wilted. (Noncausable - you can't *wilt* a flower directly, though you can cause it to wilt by withholding water.) 花枯萎了。（非使役动词——你不能直接*使*花枯萎，尽管你可以通过不浇水使其枯萎。） | intransitive, uncaused | causative | adjective
