exception:/ɪkˈsɛpʃ(ə)n/ (US) /ɪkˈsepʃən/ (UK)| n.|例外，除外；反对；异议|something that does not follow the usual rule or pattern; an objection|例句：Everyone passed the exam, with the exception of Tom. 除汤姆外，所有人都通过了考试。|近义词：exclusion, anomaly, objection, dissent|反义词：norm, rule, standard|词性：noun

Etymology: From Old French *excepcion*, from Latin exceptio (“exception”), from excipere (“to take out, except”), from ex (“out”) + capere (“to take”).
