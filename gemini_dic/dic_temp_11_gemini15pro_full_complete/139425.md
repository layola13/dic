obsessionist: /əbˈseʃənɪst/
n.|痴迷者；执迷者|A person who is excessively preoccupied with a single idea, activity, or person.|He's an obsessionist about cleanliness. 他对清洁非常痴迷。|近义词: fanatic, zealot, devotee|反义词: moderate|词性: 名词


obsessionistic: /əbˌseʃəˈnɪstɪk/
adj.|痴迷的；执着的|Characterized by or exhibiting obsession.|Her obsessionistic behavior was beginning to worry her friends. 她那痴迷的行为开始让她的朋友们担心。|近义词: obsessive, compulsive, fixated|反义词: casual, indifferent|词性: 形容词

词源: From obsession + -ist.  Obsession comes from Late Latin obsessio "siege, blockade," from Latin obsidere "to besiege," from ob- "against" + sedere "to sit."
