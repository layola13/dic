eviction:/ɪˈvɪkʃən/| n.|驱逐，赶出；收回（尤指租户的房产）|The act of expelling someone, especially a tenant, from a property.|The landlord threatened eviction if the rent wasn't paid. 房东威胁说如果租金不付就驱逐房客。|synonyms: expulsion, removal, ouster|antonyms: admittance, acceptance, inclusion|noun
