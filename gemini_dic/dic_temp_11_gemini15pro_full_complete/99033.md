相互联盟的（组织）
The possessive form of "interalliance," referring to something that belongs to or is associated with an interalliance.  Formed from "inter-" (between) + "alliance" (a union or association formed for mutual benefit).
Example: Interalliance's mission is to foster cooperation.  相互联盟的使命是促进合作。
Synonym: partnership's, coalition's, league's
Antonym: opponent's, enemy's, rival's
词性: noun (名词)
