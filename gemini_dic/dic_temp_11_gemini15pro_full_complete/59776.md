ecosphere:/ˈiːkəʊsfiə(r)/ | n. | 生态圈 |The sum of all living organisms and their environment on Earth, interacting as a functional unit.  A closed ecological system; a miniature world. |  The ecosphere of the rainforest is incredibly diverse.  雨林的生态圈极其多样化。

The scientists created a self-sustaining ecosphere in the lab. 科学家们在实验室里创造了一个自给自足的生态圈。 | synonyms: biosphere, ecosystem, environment | antonyms: None | noun
