cymous:/ˈsaɪməs/| adj.|聚伞状的|Of or relating to a cyme; having cymes.|The plant has a cymous inflorescence.  这种植物有聚伞花序。|Example: The small white flowers are arranged in a cymous cluster. 小小的白花排列成聚伞状的花簇。|Synonyms: determinate, centrifugal|Antonyms: indeterminate, centripetal|adjective
