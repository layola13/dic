niello:/niˈɛloʊ/
n.
镶嵌细工；黑金镶嵌工艺
A black metallic alloy, traditionally of silver, copper, lead, and sulfur, used as an inlay for engraved designs on metal.
The antique silver box was decorated with intricate niello.  古董银盒子上装饰着复杂的镶嵌细工。
近义词:inlay, enamel
反义词:none
词性:noun

niello:/niˈɛloʊ/
v.
用黑金镶嵌工艺装饰
To decorate (metal) with niello.
The craftsman nielloed the silver pendant with a delicate floral design.  工匠用精致的花卉图案在银吊坠上进行了黑金镶嵌。
近义词:decorate, adorn, embellish
反义词:none
词性:verb
