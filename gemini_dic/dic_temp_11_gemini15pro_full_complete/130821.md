nonappealable:/ˌnɒnəˈpiːləbl/
adjective
不可上诉的
Not capable of being appealed.
The judge's decision is nonappealable.  法官的判决是不可上诉的。
The arbitrator's ruling was declared nonappealable, ending the legal battle. 仲裁员的裁决被宣布为不可上诉，从而结束了这场法律斗争。
近义词:final, conclusive, unchallengeable
反义词:appealable
