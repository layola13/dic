copist:/ˈkɒpɪɪst/ (US) /ˈkɑːpiɪst/| n.|抄写员，誊写员|A person who copies written material, especially before printing was invented.|The copist worked diligently, carefully transcribing the ancient manuscript.  抄写员勤奋地工作，仔细地抄写着古代手稿。|Synonyms: scribe, transcriber, clerk|Antonyms: author, originator|noun
