bio-gasses:/ˈbaɪoʊˌɡæsɪz/
noun
沼气，生物气体
A mixture of gases, primarily methane and carbon dioxide, produced by the breakdown of organic matter in the absence of oxygen.
Bio-gasses from landfills can be used to generate electricity. 垃圾填埋场的沼气可以用来发电。
synonyms: biogas, methane, landfill gas
antonyms: natural gas, fossil fuels
词性: 名词
