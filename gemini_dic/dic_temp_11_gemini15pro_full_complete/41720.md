corrodable:/kəˈroʊdəbl/ | adj. | 可腐蚀的，易腐蚀的 | Susceptible to corrosion; able to be corroded. | Example 1: Iron is a corrodable metal, especially in damp environments. 铁是一种易腐蚀的金属，尤其是在潮湿的环境中。

Example 2: The bridge's corrodable steel supports need regular maintenance.  桥梁的易腐蚀钢支架需要定期维护。| 近义词: corrodible, rustable, erodible | 反义词: corrosion-resistant, non-corrosive, impervious | 词性: adjective


corrode:/kəˈroʊd/ | v. | 腐蚀，侵蚀 | To destroy or damage slowly by chemical action. | Example 1: Acid rain corrodes stone buildings. 酸雨腐蚀石制建筑。

Example 2: Rust corroded the metal pipes. 铁锈腐蚀了金属管道。 | 近义词: erode, rust, decay, disintegrate | 反义词: preserve, protect, strengthen | 词性: verb


corrosion:/kəˈroʊʒn/ | n. | 腐蚀，侵蚀 | The process of corroding or being corroded. | Example 1: The corrosion of the metal caused the structure to collapse. 金属的腐蚀导致结构倒塌。

Example 2:  The car's undercarriage suffered severe corrosion from road salt. 汽车底盘因道路盐分而严重腐蚀。| 近义词: erosion, rust, decay, disintegration | 反义词: preservation, protection | 词性: noun
