lyrid: /ˈlaɪrɪd/ | n. | 天琴座流星雨;  天琴座流星群 | A meteor shower that occurs annually in late April, caused by debris from Comet Thatcher. |  The Lyrid meteor shower peaks around April 22nd each year.  天琴座流星雨每年四月二十二日左右达到高峰。 |  The Lyrids are one of the oldest known meteor showers. 天琴座流星雨是已知最古老的流星雨之一。 | 近义词: meteor shower | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific meteor shower) | 词性: noun

词源: From the constellation Lyra (Lyra is named after the lyre of Orpheus in Greek mythology).  源自天琴座（Lyra，以希腊神话中俄耳甫斯的七弦琴命名）。
