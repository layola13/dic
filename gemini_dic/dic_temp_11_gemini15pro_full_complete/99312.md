intercreating:/ˌɪntərkriˈeɪtɪŋ/
verb
相互创造，共同创造
The process by which two or more entities influence and shape each other's development or creation.
Artists and scientists were intercreating, blurring the lines between disciplines. 艺术家和科学家们正在相互创造，模糊了学科之间的界限。
interacting, collaborating, co-creating, synergizing
isolating, separating, dividing
词性:动词

intercreating:/ˌɪntərkriˈeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
相互创造的，共同创造的
Describing a process or relationship where two or more entities influence and shape each other's development or creation.
The intercreating relationship between the dancers and the musicians was mesmerizing. 舞者和音乐家之间相互创造的关系令人着迷。
interactive, collaborative, synergistic, reciprocal
independent, separate, isolated
