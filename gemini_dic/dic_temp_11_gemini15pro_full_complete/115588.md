marauder:/məˈrɔːdər/| n. |掠夺者，抢劫者|Someone who roams around looking for things to steal or people to attack.|The marauders pillaged the village, taking everything of value.|Synonyms: raider, plunderer, pillager, looter, bandit, freebooter|Antonyms: protector, defender, guardian|Noun
