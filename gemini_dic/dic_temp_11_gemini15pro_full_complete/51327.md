detassel:/diːˈtæsəl/ | v. | 除去雄穗，去雄 | to remove the tassels from (a plant, especially corn) to prevent self-pollination and promote cross-pollination. |  They detassel the corn to control which plants pollinate others. 他们除去玉米的雄穗以控制哪些植株给其他植株授粉。| synonyms: emasculate, pollinate (partially) | antonyms: pollinate (self-pollinate) | verb

detasseling:/diːˈtæsəlɪŋ/ | n. | 除去雄穗，去雄 | the process of removing tassels from corn plants. | Detasseling corn is a common summer job for teenagers in the Midwest. 在中西部，去除玉米雄穗是青少年常见的暑期工作。| synonyms: emasculation | antonyms: self-pollination | noun
