denigration: /ˌdɛnɪˈɡreɪʃən/ | n. | 诽谤，诋毁，贬低 | the action of unfairly criticizing someone or something in order to make them seem less important or valuable |  The politician's speech was filled with denigration of his opponent.  这位政治家的演讲充满了对他对手的诋毁。| Synonyms: defamation, slander, vilification, disparagement, belittlement | Antonyms: praise, commendation, acclaim, exaltation | noun

denigrate: /ˈdɛnɪɡreɪt/ | v. | 诽谤，诋毁，贬低 | to criticize unfairly in order to make someone or something seem less important or valuable |  He tends to denigrate anyone who disagrees with him. 他倾向于诋毁任何与他意见不合的人。| Synonyms:  defame, slander, vilify, disparage, belittle | Antonyms: praise, commend, acclaim, exalt | verb
