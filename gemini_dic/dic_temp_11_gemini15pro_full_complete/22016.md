bois:/bwɑː/| n.|guys, dudes (informal, often online)|A French word meaning "wood" or "woods," but used in English online slang, particularly in gaming communities, to refer to a group of friends, similar to "guys" or "dudes."|例句：Look at what the bois are doing! 看看这些家伙在干什么！|近义词：guys, dudes, friends, mates, bros|反义词:enemies, foes|词性:noun
