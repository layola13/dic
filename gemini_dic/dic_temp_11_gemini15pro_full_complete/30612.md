cerebripetal: /ˌsɛrɪˈbrɪpɪtəl/
adjective
向心的；传入大脑的
Conveying impulses toward the brain or cerebrum.  Moving or directed toward the brain.
Cerebripetal nerves carry information from the sensory organs to the brain.  传入大脑的神经将信息从感觉器官传递到大脑。

Cerebripetal pathways transmit sensory information to the cortex. 向心通路将感觉信息传递到大脑皮层。


近义词: afferent
反义词: cerebrifugal


词性: adjective
