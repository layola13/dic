empyreuma:/ˌempɪˈruːmə/ /ˌempɪˈrjuːmə/
noun
焦糊味，烧焦味
The characteristic smell of burning or charring organic matter, especially animal or vegetable matter.
The steak had a distinct empyreuma from being overcooked.  牛排因为煮过头了，有一股明显的焦糊味。
The room filled with the acrid empyreuma of burnt plastic. 房间里弥漫着烧焦塑料的刺鼻焦糊味。
synonyms: burnt smell, scorched smell, char, singed smell
antonyms: fragrance, aroma, perfume
