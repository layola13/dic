appreciate:/əˈpriːʃieɪt/
verb
欣赏；感激；理解；增值
To recognize the full worth of. To be grateful for (something). To increase in value.
I appreciate your help. 我很感激你的帮助。

The value of the house has appreciated over the years.  这些年来，房子的价值上涨了。

She appreciates good music. 她欣赏好的音乐。

synonym:value, cherish, admire, acknowledge,  esteem
antonym:depreciate,  disregard,  criticize, undervalue, scorn
词性:动词
