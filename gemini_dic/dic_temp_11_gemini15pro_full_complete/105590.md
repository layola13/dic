kindliness:/ˈkaɪndlinəs/
n.
仁慈；亲切；和蔼
The quality of being kind and friendly.
She treated everyone with kindliness and respect. 她待人亲切而尊重。
近义词：kindness, benevolence, compassion, friendliness, warmth, generosity
反义词：cruelty, unkindness, hostility, meanness, coldness
