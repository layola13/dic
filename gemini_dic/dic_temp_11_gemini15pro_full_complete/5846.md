amazeful: /əˈmeɪzfəl/ | adj. | 令人惊叹的，极好的 |  A non-standard but increasingly common word used to express great amazement or admiration, blending "amazing" and "wonderful."  It's considered informal. |  The sunset was amazeful!  日落太棒了！

The food at the restaurant was amazeful. 这家餐厅的食物棒极了。| 近义词: amazing, wonderful, fantastic, incredible, astounding | 反义词: awful, terrible, dreadful, horrible | 词性: 形容词

词源:  A blend of "amazing" and "wonderful," likely originating in informal spoken English.  No established etymological record exists due to its informal nature.
