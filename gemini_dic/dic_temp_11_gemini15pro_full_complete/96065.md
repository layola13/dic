indeclinably:/ˌɪndɪˈklaɪnəbli/
adverb
不变地，无变化地
Not changing in grammatical form; uninflected.
He spoke indeclinably, his tone flat and unchanging. 他语气平淡，毫无变化地说。
The adverb "always" is indeclinably. 副词“always”是不变的。
近义词: invariably, constantly, unchangeably
反义词: variably, changeably
词性: adverb

词源:
From Late Latin indeclinabilis, from in- "not" + declinabilis "inflectable," from declinare "to bend, turn aside, inflect," from de- "down" + clinare "to lean."
