housework:/ˈhaʊswɜːrk/ (US), /ˈhaʊswɜːk/ (UK)| n. |家务劳动，家务|Regular housework includes cleaning, laundry, and cooking.  定期的家务劳动包括打扫卫生、洗衣服和做饭。|Examples: I spent all morning doing housework. 我一上午都在做家务。 She hates doing housework, but it has to be done. 她讨厌做家务，但又不得不做。|Synonyms: chores, domestic work, household chores|Antonyms: leisure, relaxation|Noun
