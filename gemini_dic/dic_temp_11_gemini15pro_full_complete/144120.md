outscorn: /ˌaʊtˈskɔːrn/
verb
胜过，超过（在得分或表现方面）
to surpass or defeat someone by achieving a higher score or better performance than them.
例句：He outscored his rival by 20 points. 他比对手多得了20分。

例句：She consistently outscored her classmates on exams. 她在考试中总是比同学们得分高。

近义词：outperform, surpass, beat, defeat, triumph over, excel
反义词：lose to, be defeated by, fall behind
词性：v.
