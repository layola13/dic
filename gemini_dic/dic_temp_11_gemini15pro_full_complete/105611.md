kinepox:/ˈkaɪnɪpɒks/ | n. |牛痘|A disease of cows that is similar to smallpox, sometimes transmitted to humans, and historically used to vaccinate against smallpox. |Cowpox was used to develop the first smallpox vaccines. 牛痘被用来开发第一种天花疫苗。|The farmer contracted kinepox from his cows. 农民从他的牛身上感染了牛痘。|Synonyms: vaccinia, cow pox|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
