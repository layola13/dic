Donbas: /ˈdɒnbæs/ (British) /ˈdɑːnbɑːs/ (American)| n. |顿巴斯|A region in eastern Ukraine, comprising the Donetsk Oblast and Luhansk Oblast. It is a heavily industrialized area known for its coal mines and steel plants. It has been the site of conflict since 2014.|The Donbas has been ravaged by war. 顿巴斯地区饱受战争蹂躏。|Synonyms: Donets Basin|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
