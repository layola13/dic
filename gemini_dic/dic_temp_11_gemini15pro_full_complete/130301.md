non-sympathetic:/ˌnɒnˌsɪmpəˈθetɪk/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˌsɪmpəˈθetɪk/| adj.|不同情的，不表同意的，无动于衷的|Not feeling or showing sympathy; unsympathetic.|例句：He gave her a non-sympathetic look. 他冷漠地看了她一眼。|近义词：unsympathetic, uncaring, indifferent, callous, cold|反义词：sympathetic, compassionate, understanding, caring, warm|词性:形容词

non-sympathetic:/ˌnɒnˌsɪmpəˈθetɪk/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˌsɪmpəˈθetɪk/| adj.|不利的，不适宜的|Not favorable or conducive.|例句：The non-sympathetic environment hindered the growth of the plants. 不利的环境阻碍了植物的生长。|近义词：unfavorable, adverse, hostile|反义词：favorable, advantageous, beneficial|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + sympathetic.  "Sympathetic" comes from the Greek "sym" (with, together) + "pathos" (feeling, suffering).  The prefix "non-" simply negates the meaning of "sympathetic."
