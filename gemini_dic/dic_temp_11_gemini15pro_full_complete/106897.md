lace:/leɪs/ /les/| n. v. |蕾丝; 系带;  饰以花边; 用带子系紧|A delicate fabric made of yarn or thread in an open weblike pattern. To fasten or join with or as if with a lace or laces.|The tablecloth was edged with lace. 桌布镶着蕾丝边。|She laced her boots tightly. 她把靴子紧紧地系好。|synonyms: cord, string, ribbon, braid, trim, embroidery|antonyms: unlace, untie|noun, verb
