detent:/ˈdiːtɛnt/ /dɪˈtɛnt/ | n. |制动器，掣子；定位装置；卡榫；棘爪 |A mechanism on a ratchet or other device that locks it in position or limits its movement.| The detent on the gear shift lever broke, causing it to slip out of gear.  变速杆上的掣子坏了，导致它滑出档位。|Synonyms: catch, pawl, click, stop, ratchet |Antonyms: release, accelerator |noun


detent:/ˈdiːtɛnt/ /dɪˈtɛnt/ | n. |（国际关系的）缓和（时期）| A period of easing of tensions, especially between nations.| The period of détente between the US and the Soviet Union during the Cold War led to several arms control agreements.  冷战期间美苏之间的缓和时期促成了几项军备控制协议。|Synonyms: relaxation, easing, thaw |Antonyms: tension, escalation, conflict |noun
