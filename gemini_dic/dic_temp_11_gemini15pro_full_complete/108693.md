leaking:/ˈliːkɪŋ/ | adj. | 泄漏的，漏的 | Allowing a substance, typically liquid or gas, to escape through a hole or crack. | The roof is leaking and needs to be repaired. 屋顶漏水，需要修理。| synonyms: escaping, dripping, oozing | antonyms: sealed, tight, waterproof | adjective

leaking:/ˈliːkɪŋ/ | n. | 泄漏 | The act or process of a substance escaping through a hole or crack. | The leaking of the confidential documents caused a scandal. 机密文件的泄露引发了一场丑闻。| synonyms: escape, drip, ooze | antonyms: containment, sealing | noun
