corolliflorous:/ˌkɒrəˌlɪˈflɔːrəs/ /ˌkɔːrəˌlɪˈflɔːrəs/
adjective
花冠生的，合瓣花冠的
Bearing flowers on the corolla, or having the corolla bearing the stamens and other floral organs.
Example: The botanist identified the rare corolliflorous plant in the rainforest.  这位植物学家在雨林中发现了一种稀有的花冠生植物。

Example:  Corolliflorous plants are fascinating to study due to their unique floral structure.  花冠生植物由于其独特的花卉结构，研究起来很有趣。

synonym: sympetalous
antonym: choripetalous
词性: adjective
