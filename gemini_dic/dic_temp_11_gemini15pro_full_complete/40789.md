convocants:/kənˈvoʊkənts/
noun
被召集者，与会者
Those called together for a meeting or assembly.
The convocants gathered in the hall for the annual meeting.  被召集者聚集在大厅参加年会。
The university's convocants included students, faculty, and alumni.  大学的与会者包括学生、教师和校友。
synonyms: attendees, participants, assembly, gathering
antonyms: absentees, non-participants
