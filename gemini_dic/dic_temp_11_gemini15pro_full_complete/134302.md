noninoculative:/ˌnɒnɪˈnɒkjʊlətɪv/
adjective
非接种的，不需接种的
Not involving or requiring inoculation; not introduced by inoculation.
Noninoculative rhizobia can infect the roots of some legumes. 非接种根瘤菌可以感染一些豆科植物的根部。
The researchers compared the growth of plants in inoculative and noninoculative treatments. 研究人员比较了接种和非接种处理中植物的生长情况。
近义词: non-infective
反义词: inoculative
词性: adjective
