fusillade:/ˌfjuːsəˈleɪd/ /ˌfjuːsɪˈlɑːd/| n.| 枪林弹雨；猛烈的抨击；连发|A fusillade of bullets/questions/criticism. 一连串的子弹/问题/批评。|The troops were caught in a fusillade of enemy fire. 部队遭到敌人的猛烈炮火袭击。|近义词：barrage, volley, bombardment, onslaught|反义词:trickle, dribble|名词

fusillade:/ˌfjuːsəˈleɪd/ /ˌfjuːsɪˈlɑːd/| v.| 以猛烈的炮火攻击；连珠炮般地提问；连续抨击|The speaker was fusilladed with questions from the audience. 演讲者遭到观众连珠炮般的提问。|The soldiers fusilladed the enemy positions. 士兵们猛烈攻击敌人的阵地。|近义词：bombard, pepper, assail|反义词:protect, defend|动词
