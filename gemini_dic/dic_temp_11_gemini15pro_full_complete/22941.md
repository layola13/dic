bottlehead:/ˈbɑːtlˌhɛd/| n.|酒鬼；醉汉；笨蛋|A person who drinks a lot of alcohol, often to excess; a stupid or foolish person.|He's such a bottlehead, he can't even stand up straight after a few beers. 他真是个酒鬼，喝几瓶啤酒就站不稳了。|近义词：drunkard, alcoholic, sot, fool, idiot|反义词:teetotaler, abstainer|词性:名词

词源:  Compound of "bottle" and "head".  Relatively modern slang, likely originating in the 20th century.  "Bottle" refers to alcoholic beverages in bottles, and "head" implies that the person's head is filled with or affected by alcohol.
