firth:/fɜːrθ/ | n. | 海湾，峡湾，河口 | A long, narrow inlet of the sea, especially in Scotland. |  例句：The boat sailed up the firth. 船驶入了峡湾。

例句：The Firth of Forth is a major estuary in eastern Scotland. 福斯湾是苏格兰东部的一个主要河口。

近义词：inlet, estuary, bay, sound | 反义词:  (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑 open sea 或 ocean) | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *fjǫrthr, from Proto-Germanic *ferþuz. Cognate with Norwegian fjord, Icelandic fjörður. Related to "fare" (to travel).
