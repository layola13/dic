gestations:/dʒɛˈsteɪʃənz/
n.
妊娠期，怀孕期
The period of development of a fetus in the womb from conception to birth.
The average gestation for a human is about 40 weeks.  人类的平均妊娠期约为40周。
pregnancies, incubations
barrenness, infertility
noun
