interpollinate:/ˌɪntərˈpɑːləneɪt/ | v. |异花授粉，互相授粉 | To transfer pollen from one flower to another, especially between different varieties or species. | The gardener interpollinated the roses to create a new hybrid. 园丁对玫瑰进行异花授粉以创造一个新的杂交品种。

The bees interpollinated the apple blossoms and the pear blossoms. 蜜蜂给苹果花和梨花进行了异花授粉。


近义词: cross-pollinate, hybridize | 反义词: self-pollinate | 词性: verb
