ob-gyn: /ˌoʊbiːˈdʒaɪn/ (American English), /ˌɒbˈɡaɪn/ (British English)
noun
妇产科医生；妇产科
Short for obstetrician-gynecologist, a doctor who specializes in women's reproductive health, pregnancy, and childbirth.
My sister sees an ob-gyn for her annual check-up. 我的姐姐每年都去看妇产科医生做检查。
Synonyms: obstetrician-gynecologist, OB/GYN,  women's health doctor
Antonyms: N/A
词性: noun


ob-gyn: /ˌoʊbiːˈdʒaɪn/ (American English), /ˌɒbˈɡaɪn/ (British English)
adjective
妇产科的
Relating to obstetrics and gynecology.
She went to the ob-gyn clinic for a consultation. 她去妇产科诊所咨询。
Synonyms: obstetric-gynecological, obstetrical and gynecological
Antonyms: N/A
词性: adjective
