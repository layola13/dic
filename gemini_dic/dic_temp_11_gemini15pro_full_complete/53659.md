discide:/dɪˈsaɪd/| v. | 决定，决心；解决；裁决 | to make a final decision or judgment about (something); to settle or determine |  The judge discided the case in favor of the plaintiff. 法官判原告胜诉。| 近义词: decide, determine, resolve, settle | 反义词: waver, hesitate | 词性: 动词

词源: From Latin *discīdere, present active infinitive of *discīdō (“I divide, separate, decide”), from dis- (“apart, away”) + caedō (“I cut, strike, kill”).  Related to decide.  Discide is an archaic word, largely replaced by "decide."
