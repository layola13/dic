junk:/dʒʌŋk/ | n. | 垃圾，废旧物品；劣质品；毒品 | Old or discarded articles that are considered useless or of little value. Also can refer to low-quality products or addictive drugs. |  The garage was full of junk.  车库里堆满了垃圾。

The movie was a piece of junk. 这部电影是部烂片。| synonyms: trash, rubbish, garbage, debris, scrap | antonyms: treasure, valuables | noun

junk:/dʒʌŋk/ | v. | 丢弃，抛弃 | To discard or throw away something considered useless. | They junked the old car. 他们把那辆旧车报废了。

We junked the plan because it was too risky. 我们放弃了这个计划，因为它太冒险了。 | synonyms: discard, ditch, scrap, dump | antonyms: keep, save, treasure | verb
