fatidical:/fəˈtɪdɪkəl/
adjective
预言的，预示命运的，决定命运的
Containing or portending fate; prophetic of, or connected with, fate.
例句：The fatidical utterance of the oracle foretold the fall of the kingdom. 神谕的预言预示了王国的覆灭。

例句：A fatidical dream warned him of the impending danger. 一个预示命运的梦警告他即将到来的危险。

近义词：prophetic, ominous, portentous
反义词：auspicious, fortunate
词性: adjective


词源: From Latin *fatidicus, from fatum ("fate") + -dicus ("saying, telling"), from dicere ("to say"). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *deh₂- ("to set, put").
