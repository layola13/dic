nontheosophic:/ˌnɒnˌθiːəˈsɒfɪk/
adjective
非神智学的
Not related to or characteristic of theosophy; not based on theosophical principles.
His nontheosophic beliefs led him down a different spiritual path.  他非神智学的信仰使他走上了一条不同的精神道路。
The library contained many nontheosophic texts on spirituality.  图书馆里收藏了许多非神智学灵性方面的书籍。
近义词:non-theosophical
反义词:theosophic, theosophical
词性:adjective
