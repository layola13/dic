kitting:/ˈkɪtɪŋ/
n.
配套; 配备; 装备; (尤指为特定目的而组合在一起的)成套工具; 成套材料;
The process of assembling a kit or set of parts, or the kit itself.  This can refer to assembling materials for a specific project or task, or preparing a collection of items for a particular purpose.
The company specializes in the kitting of electronic components. 这家公司专门从事电子元件的配套。
synonyms: assembling, packaging, bundling, gathering, preparing
antonyms: dismantling, disassembling, separating, scattering
noun

kitting:/ˈkɪtɪŋ/
v.
(为特定目的)配备，装备
To provide someone or something with a kit or set of necessary items.
They kitted out the expedition with the latest equipment. 他们为探险队配备了最新的设备。
synonyms: equipping, outfitting, supplying, providing
antonyms: stripping, depriving
verb
