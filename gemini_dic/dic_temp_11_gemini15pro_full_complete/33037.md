chosennesses:/ˈtʃoʊzənɪsnɪsɪz/
n.
被选中状态；选定性
The state or quality of being chosen.
The chosennesses of the few representatives seemed arbitrary.  这几位代表的被选中似乎很随意。
The chosennesses of these particular locations for the study remains unexplained.  这些特定地点被选为研究地点的原因仍然不明。
近义词: selection, election
反义词: rejection, exclusion
词性: 名词
