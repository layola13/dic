aardvark:/ˈɑːrdvɑːrk/ (US); /ˈɑːdvɑːk/ (UK)| n.|土豚|A nocturnal, burrowing, insectivorous mammal of Africa, with a long snout and a long, sticky tongue for catching ants and termites. It is the only surviving species of its order (Tubulidentata).|例句：The aardvark uses its powerful claws to dig for ants and termites. 土豚用它强有力的爪子挖掘蚂蚁和白蚁。

例句：We saw an aardvark on our night safari. 我们在夜间狩猎旅行中看到了一只土豚。

近义词：antbear, anteater|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
