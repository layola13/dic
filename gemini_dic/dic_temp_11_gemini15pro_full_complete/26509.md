cabareting:/ˈkæbəreɪɪŋ/
n.
歌舞表演；夜总会表演
The act of performing in a cabaret.
She enjoyed cabareting in her youth. 她年轻时喜欢在夜总会表演。
performance, entertainment, show
boredom, inactivity
noun

cabaret:/ˈkæbəreɪ/
n.
夜总会；卡巴莱歌舞表演
A restaurant or nightclub providing entertainment.
The cabaret featured singers and dancers.  这家夜总会以歌手和舞蹈演员为特色。
nightclub, club, entertainment venue
theater, concert hall
noun
