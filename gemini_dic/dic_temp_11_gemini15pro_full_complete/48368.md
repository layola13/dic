decurrent:/dɪˈkɜːrənt/
adj.
下延的，延伸的
Extending downward; specifically (Botany) extending down the stem or stalk.
The decurrent leaves of the thistle plant are a distinctive feature.  蓟草植物的下延叶片是一个显著特征。

The fungus had decurrent gills. 这种真菌有下延的菌褶。
近义词:prolonged, extending
反义词:ascending, upcurved
词性:adjective
