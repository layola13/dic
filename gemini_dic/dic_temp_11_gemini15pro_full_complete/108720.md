leannesses:/ˈliːn.nɪs.ɪz/
n.
瘦弱；消瘦；贫乏
the state of being lean; thinness, especially caused by illness or hunger; lack of resources or richness
The leannesses of the war years are still remembered.  战争年代的贫困仍然记忆犹新。
近义词:thinness, slenderness, emaciation, meagreness, poverty
反义词:fatness, obesity, richness, abundance
词性:名词
