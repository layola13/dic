lyceum:/laɪˈsiːəm/
n.
高等院校，学府；学术讲演会，文艺协会
A place of learning; a hall for public lectures or discussions.
The town's lyceum hosted a series of lectures on modern art.  该镇的学府举办了一系列关于现代艺术的讲座。
The speaker delivered a compelling presentation at the lyceum. 演讲者在学府发表了引人入胜的演讲。
synonyms: academy, institute, college, school
antonyms: ignorance, illiteracy
