corniplume:/ˈkɔːrnɪˌpluːm/| n.|乌鸦羽毛，角羽|A feather, especially a large, stiff, shiny feather from a crow's wing or tail. It can also refer to a feather-like or plume-like structure.|The shaman adorned his headdress with corniplumes.|近义词：feather, plume, pinion|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "down" for soft feathers)|noun
