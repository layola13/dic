irreverence:/ɪˈrɛvərəns/| n.|不敬， irreverent (adj.)|lack of respect or reverence.|He spoke of the church with irreverence.|近义词：disrespect, impiety, blasphemy, profanity|反义词：reverence, respect, piety|词性:名词

Etymology: Late Middle English: from Old French irreverence, from Latin irreverentia, from irreverent- ‘not reverent’, from in- ‘not’ + reverent- ‘reverent’.
