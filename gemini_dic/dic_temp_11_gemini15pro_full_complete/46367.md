dadaistic:/ˌdɑːdɑːˈɪstɪk/ /ˌdeɪdəˈɪstɪk/ | adj. |达达主义的；荒诞的|Of or relating to Dadaism, an early-20th-century avant-garde artistic movement characterized by a rejection of logic and traditional aesthetics, often employing absurdity and irrationality.|Her dadaistic sculpture was made of found objects and discarded toys. 她那达达主义的雕塑是由捡来的物品和废弃的玩具制成的。|Synonyms: absurd, irrational, nonsensical, illogical, bizarre, surreal, avant-garde |Antonyms: rational, logical, conventional, traditional|adjective

dadaistic:/ˌdɑːdɑːˈɪstɪk/ /ˌdeɪdəˈɪstɪk/ | n. |达达主义者|A follower or proponent of Dadaism.|The dadaists shocked the art world with their unconventional and often provocative works. 达达主义者以其非传统且经常具有挑衅意味的作品震惊了艺术界。|Synonyms: Dadaist|Antonyms: traditionalist, conventionalist|noun
