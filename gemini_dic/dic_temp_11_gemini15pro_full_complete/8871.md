antiflu:/ˈæntiˈfluː/
adjective
抗流感的
Preventing or counteracting influenza.
Example: She took an antiflu shot before flu season. 她流感季前接种了抗流感疫苗。
Example:  Antiflu medication can help alleviate symptoms. 抗流感药物可以帮助减轻症状。

Synonym:anti-influenza, flu-fighting
Antonym:pro-flu (This is a somewhat contrived antonym and not commonly used. It would refer to something that promotes or spreads the flu.)
词性:adjective
