jumps: /dʒʌmps/ (US), /dʒʌmps/ (UK)| n. | 跳跃，跳跃(复数)，骤增|  The athlete performed a series of jumps.  运动员表演了一系列跳跃。|近义词：leaps, springs, bounds, hops |反义词: falls, descends, drops| noun (名词)

词源: From Middle English *jumpen, *jomen, from Old French jumper, jomper (“to jolt, to leap, to jump”), perhaps of Germanic origin, and probably related to or influenced by Old Norse goppa (“to hop, to jump”).
