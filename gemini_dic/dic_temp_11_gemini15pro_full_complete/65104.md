etherealizing:/ɪˈθɪriəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使...空灵化；使...超凡脱俗；使...精神化；使...虚无缥缈
The process of making something ethereal; giving something a delicate and unearthly quality.
The mist was etherealizing the mountains, making them seem like floating islands.  薄雾使山脉变得空灵，仿佛漂浮的岛屿。
The music seemed to etherealize her voice, giving it a haunting beauty.  音乐似乎使她的声音空灵，赋予它一种萦绕心头的美丽。
近义词: spiritualizing, refining, sublimating
反义词: materializing, coarsening, solidifying
词性:v.

词源:
From ethereal + -ize + -ing.  来自 ethereal（空灵的） + -ize（使...） + -ing（现在分词）。  Ethereal itself comes from Late Latin aethereus, from Greek aithérios "of the upper air," from aithēr "upper air."
