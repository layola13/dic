fruitily:/ˈfruːtɪli/ | adv. | 肥沃地；果实累累地；有成效地 | In a fruitful way; productively; abundantly. | The trees blossomed fruitily in the spring.  春天，树木果实累累地开花。| 近义词: productively, prolifically, abundantly | 反义词: barrenly, unproductively | 词性: 副词

词源: From fruit + -ly.  Ultimately from Latin *fructus*.
