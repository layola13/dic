dilapidated:/dɪˈlæpɪdeɪtɪd/| adj.|破旧的，失修的|Falling into ruin or decay, usually from neglect or misuse.  Characterized by a state of disrepair or ruin due to age or neglect.|The dilapidated house stood on a windswept hill.  那栋破旧的房子矗立在刮风的山頂上。|The old barn was dilapidated and falling apart.  那座旧谷仓破烂不堪，快要倒塌了。|近义词：ramshackle, derelict, run-down, tumbledown|反义词：well-maintained, pristine, renovated|词性:形容词
