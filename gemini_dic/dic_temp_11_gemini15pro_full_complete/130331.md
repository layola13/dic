non-threatening:/nɒnˈθrɛt(ə)nɪŋ/ (UK) /nɑːnˈθrɛtnɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|无威胁的，不具威胁性的|Not posing a threat; not intimidating or menacing.|Her smile was non-threatening and welcoming. 她的笑容亲切而没有威胁性。|The dog's posture was non-threatening, its tail wagging gently. 狗的姿势并不具有威胁性，尾巴轻轻地摇着。|近义词：harmless, safe, benign, innocuous|反义词：threatening, menacing, intimidating|词性:形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
threatening (present participle of threaten): from Old French *threatner, of uncertain origin.
