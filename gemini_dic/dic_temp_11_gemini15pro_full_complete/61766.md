empathize with: /ˈɛmpəθaɪz wɪð/ | v. | 感同身受，理解，同情 | To understand and share the feelings of another. |  I can really empathize with her; I've been through a similar experience. 我真的很能理解她；我也有过类似的经历。

| I empathize with his situation, having been through something similar myself.  我对他处境感同身受，因为我自己也经历过类似的事情。 | 近义词: sympathize with, understand, relate to, share the feelings of,  compassionate | 反义词:  be indifferent to, apathetic | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From em- (into) + pathos (feeling) + -ize (verb suffix)
