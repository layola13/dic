nonemotive:/ˌnɒnɪˈməʊtɪv/ | adj. | 无感情的，不动感情的 | Not showing or causing emotion; unemotional. |  Her nonemotive response surprised everyone. 她毫无感情的反应让每个人都感到惊讶。

The judge delivered the verdict in a nonemotive tone. 法官以毫无感情的语气宣读了判决。 | 近义词: impassive, apathetic, indifferent, unfeeling, emotionless | 反义词: emotional, expressive, passionate, sensitive | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (not) + "emotive" (causing or expressing emotion). "Emotive" comes from the Latin "emovere" (to move out, remove, excite).
