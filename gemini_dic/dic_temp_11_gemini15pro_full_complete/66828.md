expatiate:/ɪkˈspeɪʃieɪt/ /ɛkˈspeɪʃɪˌeɪt/| v. |详述，细说|To speak or write at length or in detail. |She expatiated on the benefits of a vegetarian diet. 她详细阐述了素食的好处。

He expatiated upon his theory for hours. 他详细解释了他的理论，讲了几个小时。

|近义词：elaborate, expand, enlarge, descant, dilate|反义词：summarize, condense, abbreviate|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *expatiatus, past participle of expatiari (“to wander, digress”), from ex- (“out of, from”) + spatiari (“to walk, roam”), from spatium (“space, room”).
