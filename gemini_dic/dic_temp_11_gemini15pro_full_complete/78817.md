gimleting:/ˈɡɪmlɪtɪŋ/| adj.|钻孔的，尖锐的，敏锐的| penetrating; piercing; incisive.  Referring to something that bores through or examines something thoroughly, like a gimlet (a small hand tool for boring holes).|The lawyer's gimleting questions exposed the inconsistencies in the witness's testimony. 律师尖锐的问题暴露了证人证词中的矛盾之处。|近义词：piercing, penetrating, probing, searching, inquisitive|反义词:superficial, cursory|词性:形容词

gimlet:/ˈɡɪmlɪt/| n.|手钻，钻孔器|a small hand tool for boring holes|He used a gimlet to make a hole for the screw. 他用手钻钻了个洞来上螺丝。|近义词：auger, drill|反义词:N/A|词性:名词

词源: From Old French *gimbelet, diminutive of guimbe "gimlet," of Germanic origin.
