lacunae:/ləˈkjuːniː/ /ləˈkjuːneɪ/
noun
空白；缺口；空隙；漏洞
an empty space or missing part : a gap or missing part
例句：There are still lacunae in our understanding of the disease. 我们对这种疾病的理解仍有空白。

例句：The historian noted the lacunae in the historical record. 历史学家注意到了历史记录中的空白。
synonym: gap, void, blank, opening, chasm, abyss
antonym: completeness, fullness, entirety, whole
n.
