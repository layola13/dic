backed-up:/ˌbækt ˈʌp/ | adj. | 支持的; 阻塞的; 备份的; (交通)堵塞的 | Supported; obstructed; having a copy of data; (of traffic) congested. |  The project is backed-up by substantial funding.  这个项目有充足的资金支持。

The drains are backed-up and the sink is overflowing. 下水道堵住了，水槽都溢出来了。

| 近义词：supported, obstructed, congested, copied, duplicated | 反义词：unsupported, clear, uncongested, original | 词性:形容词

backed-up:/ˌbækt ˈʌp/ | adj. |  (文件)备份的 | Having a copy of data. | I have backed-up all my important files. 我已经备份了我所有重要的文件。

Make sure your work is backed-up regularly. 确保你的工作定期备份。


| 近义词：copied, duplicated, saved | 反义词：original, lost, deleted | 词性:形容词


词源:  "Backed-up" is formed from the verb "back up" + "-ed" (past participle ending).  "Back up" itself originates from the literal sense of moving something back, then extended metaphorically to supporting something and creating a copy for security.
