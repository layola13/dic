decardinalize:/diːˈkɑːrdɪnəlaɪz/
verb
使非枢机化; 剥夺枢机主教的职位
To deprive (someone) of the rank of cardinal.  To reduce the importance or power of something.
The Pope decardinalized the disgraced bishop.  教皇剥夺了这位声名狼藉的主教的枢机主教职位。
The reforms decardinalized the role of the central committee. 改革削弱了中央委员会的作用。
Synonyms: demote, downgrade, reduce
Antonyms: promote, elevate, upgrade
词性: 动词
