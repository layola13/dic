colliquative:/kəˈlɪkwətɪv/
adjective
溶解的；液化的；衰竭的
Characterized by excessive discharge of fluid or by liquefaction of tissue.  Relating to or marked by a gradual wasting away of the body or of its tissues.
The patient experienced colliquative sweating due to the infection. 病人由于感染而大量出汗。
The tumor caused colliquative necrosis of the surrounding tissue. 肿瘤导致周围组织液化性坏死。
Synonyms: liquefying, dissolving, deliquescent, wasting, debilitating
Antonyms: solidifying, coagulating, strengthening, invigorating
词性: adjective
