acknowledgements: /əkˈnɒlɪdʒmənts/ (UK) /əkˈnɑːlɪdʒmənts/ (US)
n.
致谢; 承认; 感谢; 确认
a statement expressing gratitude for assistance in writing a book, etc. or producing a film, play, etc.
例句：The acknowledgements page of the book thanked everyone who had helped. 这本书的致谢页感谢了所有帮助过的人。

例句：She received an acknowledgement of her application. 她收到了申请的确认。

近义词：thanks, gratitude, recognition, acceptance
反义词：denial, rejection, repudiation
词性: 名词 (plural)

acknowledgement: /əkˈnɒlɪdʒmənt/ (UK) /əkˈnɑːlɪdʒmənt/ (US)
n.
致谢; 承认; 感谢; 确认
acceptance of the truth or existence of something
例句：His acknowledgement of his mistake was appreciated. 他承认自己的错误，这受到了赞赏。

例句：We received an acknowledgement of our order. 我们收到了订单确认。

近义词：thanks, gratitude, recognition, acceptance
反义词：denial, rejection, repudiation
词性: 名词 (singular)
