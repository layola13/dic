doughs:/doʊz/
n.
生面团；钱（俚语）
A thick, malleable mixture of flour and liquid, used for baking into bread or pastry. / Informal term for money.
She kneaded the doughs for the pizza. 她揉披萨面团。/ He's got plenty of doughs. 他有很多钱。
近义词：dough, bread (for money), cash, funds
反义词：(No direct antonym for dough as a substance)
词性: noun


doughs:/doʊz/
n.
生面团（复数）；钱（俚语，复数）
Plural form of "dough".
She prepared several doughs for different types of bread. 她准备了几种不同面包的生面团。/ They pooled their doughs to buy a boat. 他们凑钱买了一艘船。
近义词：dough, bread (for money), cash, funds
反义词：(No direct antonym for dough as a substance)
