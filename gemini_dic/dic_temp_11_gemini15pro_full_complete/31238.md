chantey: /ˈʃænti/ (US), /ˈtʃænti/ (UK)| n. |船夫曲，劳动号子|A rhythmic song sung by sailors in time with their work, especially while hauling ropes.|例句：The sailors sang a chantey as they hoisted the sails. 水手们一边升帆一边唱着船夫曲。

例句：The rhythmic chantey helped the sailors work together.  有节奏的船夫曲帮助水手们协同工作。

近义词：work song, sea shanty|反义词:|词性:noun
