coagulant:/koʊˈæɡjələnt/| n.| 凝固剂，凝结剂 |A substance that causes a liquid to change to a solid or semisolid state.|  Alum is a common coagulant used in water treatment. 明矾是水处理中常用的凝固剂。| synonyms: clotting agent, thickener | antonyms: anticoagulant, thinner | noun

coagulate:/koʊˈæɡjʊleɪt/| v.| 凝结，凝固 |To change from a liquid to a thick mass or solid.| The blood began to coagulate around the wound. 血液开始在伤口周围凝结。| synonyms: clot, thicken, congeal | antonyms: liquefy, thin, dissolve | verb
