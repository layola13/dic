epexegesis:/ˌɛpɪkˈsɛdʒɪsɪs/
n.
解释，补充说明
An explanation or clarifying interpretation of a text, especially of Scripture.  It involves adding words to make something clearer or more specific.
"The author uses epexegesis to clarify the meaning of the complex metaphor." 作者使用补充说明来阐明复杂隐喻的含义。
近义词：explanation, clarification, interpretation, exegesis, exposition
反义词：ambiguity, obfuscation
