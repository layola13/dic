immaculate:/ɪˈmækjələt/| adj.|洁净的，无瑕疵的，完美的|Perfectly clean, neat, or tidy; free from flaws or mistakes.|Her apartment was always immaculate. 她的公寓总是纤尘不染。|近义词：spotless, pristine, flawless, impeccable|反义词：dirty, flawed, imperfect|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin immaculatus "unspotted, stainless," from in- "not" (from PIE root *en "in") + maculatus "spotted," past participle of maculare "to spot," from macula "spot, mesh of a net."
