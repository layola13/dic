furriery:/ˈfɜːriəri/| n.| 毛皮业，毛皮制品，毛皮商店|The town was once a center of furriery.  这个小镇曾经是毛皮业的中心。|例句：He inherited his father's furriery business. 他继承了父亲的毛皮生意。

例句：She bought a beautiful fur coat from a local furriery. 她从当地一家毛皮店买了一件漂亮的皮草大衣。

近义词：fur trade, fur business|反义词:|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *fourrerie, from fourrer (to fur), from Old French forrer (to line, sheathe), from forre (lining, sheath), from Frankish *fōdr, from Proto-Germanic *fōdrą (“sheath, lining, case”).
