bleaknesses:/ˈbliːknɪsɪz/
noun
荒凉；凄凉；黯淡；阴郁
the state of being bleak, or exposed and barren; desolation
The bleaknesses of the landscape impressed him deeply. 这片荒凉的景色给他留下了深刻的印象。
Synonyms: desolation, barrenness, dreariness, gloominess
Antonyms: lushness, vibrancy, cheerfulness, brightness
词性: 名词

bleakness:/ˈbliːknɪs/
noun

bleak:/bliːk/
adjective
荒凉的；凄凉的；黯淡的；阴郁的
(of an area of land) exposed, desolate, and barren.
The future looked bleak for the struggling company. 这家苦苦挣扎的公司前途渺茫。
Synonyms: desolate, barren, dreary, gloomy
Antonyms:  lush, vibrant, cheerful, bright
