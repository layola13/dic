nongrievous:/ˌnɒnˈɡriːvəs/
adjective
非重大的；轻微的
Not causing or characterized by grief or suffering; not serious or severe.
The injury was determined to be nongrievous and he was released from the hospital the same day.  这伤被确定为轻伤，他当天就出院了。
He suffered only nongrievous injuries in the accident.  他在事故中只受了轻伤。
synonyms: minor, slight, trivial, insignificant, superficial, unimportant
antonyms: grievous, serious, severe, major, critical, acute
词性: adjective
