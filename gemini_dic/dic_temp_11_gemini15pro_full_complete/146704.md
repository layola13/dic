overhelpfully:/ˌoʊvərˈhɛlpfəli/| adv.|过分热心地，过分殷勤地|Providing help to an excessive or unnecessary degree, often to the point of being intrusive or annoying.|He overhelpfully pointed out all my mistakes, which was embarrassing. 他过分热心地指出了我所有的错误，这让我很尴尬。|She overhelpfully insisted on carrying my bag, even though it was very light. 她过分殷勤地坚持要帮我提包，即使它很轻。|近义词：officiously, intrusively, excessively helpful|反义词:unhelpful, inconsiderate|词性:副词

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "helpful" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix)
