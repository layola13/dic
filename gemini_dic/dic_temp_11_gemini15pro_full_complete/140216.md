officialness:/əˈfɪʃəlnəs/
n.
官方性；正式性；公务性
The officialness of the event was marked by the presence of the mayor.  这次活动的官方性质由市长的出席而体现。
The document lacked the officialness of a legally binding contract.  这份文件缺乏具有法律约束力合同的正式性。
近义词: formality, authority, legitimacy
反义词: informality, unofficialness
词性: noun

Etymology: From official + -ness.  "Official" comes from Old French "official"  and ultimately from Latin "officium" meaning "duty" or "service." The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
