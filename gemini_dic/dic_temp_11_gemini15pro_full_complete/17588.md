beautiest:/ˈbjuːtiɪst/ |adj.|最美丽的| This is a non-standard superlative form of "beautiful." While grammatically incorrect, it might be used informally for emphasis or humor.  It's formed by adding "-est" to "beauty" instead of "beautiful."| "She declared her cat the beautiest creature alive." 她宣称她的猫是活着的最美丽的生物。|Near Synonyms: most beautiful, prettiest, fairest, gorgeous, stunning|Antonyms: ugliest, plainest, homeliest|Adjective
