openest:/ˈoʊpənɪst/ | adj. | 最开放的 | Superlative form of "open," meaning most willing to consider new ideas, most accessible, or most unguarded. | The openest person I know is always willing to listen to different perspectives. 我认识的最开放的人总是愿意倾听不同的观点。 | The park is openest during the summer months.  这个公园在夏季最开放。| synonyms: most receptive, most broad-minded, most accessible | antonyms: most closed-minded, most restricted, most secretive | adjective
