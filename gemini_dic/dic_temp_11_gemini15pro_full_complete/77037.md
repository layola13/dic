garlanding:/ˈɡɑːrləndɪŋ/
n.
花环装饰，戴花环
The act of decorating with garlands or the state of being decorated with garlands.
The bride and groom exchanged garlands during the ceremony.  新娘和新郎在仪式上交换了花环。
The garlanding of the war heroes was a moving sight. 为战争英雄们戴上花环是一个感人的景象。
Synonyms:  wreathing, festooning, adorning
Antonyms: stripping, denuding
noun


garland:/ˈɡɑːrlənd/
n.
花环
A circular decoration of flowers and leaves, worn around the head or neck or used for decoration.
She wore a garland of roses in her hair. 她头发上戴着玫瑰花环。
The hall was decorated with garlands of evergreen. 大厅用常青树花环装饰。
Synonyms: wreath, chaplet, festoon
Antonyms:  N/A
noun

garland:/ˈɡɑːrlənd/
vt.
用花环装饰
To decorate with garlands.
They garlanded the winning horse. 他们给获胜的马戴上了花环。
The streets were garlanded with flags for the celebration. 街道上挂满了彩旗以示庆祝。
Synonyms: wreathe, festoon, adorn
Antonyms: strip, denude
verb
