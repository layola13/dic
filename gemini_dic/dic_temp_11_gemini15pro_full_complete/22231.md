bondable:/ˈbɒndəbl/ | adj. | 可担保的，可抵押的 | Able to be bonded; suitable for bonding.  Relating to something that can be guaranteed by a bond, especially financially. |  The company confirmed that the employee was bondable.  这家公司确认该雇员是可以担保的。| Synonyms: insurable, secured, guaranteed | Antonyms: uninsurable, unsecured, unguaranteed | adjective
