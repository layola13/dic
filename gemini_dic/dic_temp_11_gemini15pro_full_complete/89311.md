homogamy:/həˈmɒɡəmi/ /hoʊˈmɑːɡəmi/| n. |同婚，同类婚 |Marriage between individuals who are, in some culturally important way, similar to each other.  This could include similarities in religion, ethnicity, socioeconomic status, or age.|例句：Homogamy is common in many societies. 在许多社会中，同婚很常见。

例句：Studies suggest that homogamy in terms of education levels is increasing. 研究表明，教育水平方面的同婚正在增加。|近义词：assortative mating|反义词：heterogamy|词性:noun
