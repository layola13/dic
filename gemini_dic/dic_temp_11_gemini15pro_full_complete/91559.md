hyper-oxygenation:/ˌhaɪpərˌɒksɪdʒəˈneɪʃən/ | n. |过度氧合，超氧合作用|The process of increasing the oxygen content of something, especially blood or tissues, above normal levels.  It can be achieved through various methods, including administering supplemental oxygen or increasing the pressure of inspired air.|The diver underwent hyper-oxygenation before the deep dive. 潜水员在深潜前进行了过度氧合。|Synonyms: superoxygenation |Antonyms: hypoxia, anoxia |noun


hyper-oxygenate:/ˌhaɪpərˈɒksɪdʒəneɪt/ | v. |使过度氧合，使超氧合|To increase the oxygen content of something, especially blood or tissues, above normal levels.|The paramedics hyper-oxygenated the patient before transporting him to the hospital.  医护人员在将病人送往医院前对他进行了过度氧合。|Synonyms: superoxygenate |Antonyms: deoxygenate |verb
