hendiadys:/hɛnˈdaɪədɪs/| n.|  用两个名词或形容词连用而不是用形容词加名词或副词加动词来表达一个意思的修辞手法;  偏义复词|A figure of speech in which two words connected by a conjunction (usually *and*) express a single complex idea.  |He spoke with passion and conviction (hendiadys for 'passionate conviction'). 他带着热情和信念讲话（热情信念的偏义复词）。|Synonyms: redundancy, pleonasm, tautology|Antonyms: conciseness, brevity|noun
