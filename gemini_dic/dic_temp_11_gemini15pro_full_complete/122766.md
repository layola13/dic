mojarra:/moʊˈhɑːrə/ (US), /moʊˈxɑːrə/ (US) | n. | 莫哈拉鱼，（尤指）隆头鱼的一种 | A mojarra is a type of fish, especially a kind of sea bream, found in warm waters. |  We caught several mojarras while fishing off the pier. 我们在码头钓鱼时钓到了几条莫哈拉鱼。

Mojarras are a popular food fish in many coastal regions. 莫哈拉鱼是许多沿海地区受欢迎的食用鱼。| 近义词: sea bream, perch | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific fish type) | 词性: noun
