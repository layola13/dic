cry-babies:/ˈkraɪˌbeɪbiz/| n.|爱哭鬼，娇气的人|A person, especially a child, who cries easily or complains a lot.|例句：Stop being such a cry-baby and deal with it! 别像个爱哭鬼一样，去面对它！|近义词：whiner, complainer, sook|反义词: stoic, tough, resilient|Noun

cry-babies:/ˈkraɪˌbeɪbiz/| n.|爱哭包|Someone who cries and complains often, especially about insignificant things.|例句：He's such a cry-baby whenever he doesn't get his way.  每当他得不到想要的东西时，他就像个爱哭包。|近义词：wimp, softy|反义词: brave, courageous|Noun
