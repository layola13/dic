decking:/ˈdekɪŋ/
n.
地板，甲板，装饰
a covering of planks or other materials for a floor, roof, or other surface.
The new decking around the pool looks fantastic.  游泳池周围的新地板看起来棒极了。
flooring, planking, platform
roofing, tiling
