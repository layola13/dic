elasticities: /ɪˌlæstɪˈsɪtiz/ | n. | 弹性（复数） | The responsiveness of one variable to changes in another variable.  It is often expressed as a percentage change in one variable divided by the percentage change in another variable. | The price elasticities of demand for luxury goods are generally higher than those for necessities. 奢侈品的需求价格弹性通常高于必需品。| synonyms: responsiveness, flexibility, sensitivity | antonyms: rigidity, inflexibility, inelasticity | noun

elasticity: /ɪˌlæstɪˈsɪti/ | n. | 弹性 | The ability of something to stretch and return to its original shape or size. Also, the degree to which individuals, consumers or producers change their demand or supply in response to price or income changes. | The rubber band has lost its elasticity.  橡皮筋失去了弹性。| synonyms: stretchiness, springiness, resilience, adaptability | antonyms: rigidity, inflexibility, inelasticity | noun
