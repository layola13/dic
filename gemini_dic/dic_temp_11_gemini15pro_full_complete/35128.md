cloudless:/ˈklaʊdləs/| adj.|无云的，晴朗的|Free from clouds; clear. |例句：We enjoyed a cloudless sky on our hike. 我们在徒步旅行时享受了万里无云的天空。|近义词：clear, sunny, bright, unclouded|反义词:cloudy, overcast|词性:形容词
Etymology: From 'cloud' + '-less'.  'Cloud' comes from Old English *clud-, related to clod. '-less' is a suffix meaning "without".
