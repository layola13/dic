bouffancy:/buːfənsi/

bouffant:/buːfɒnt/
adj.|蓬松的，膨胀的|She wore a bouffant skirt. 她穿了一条蓬松的裙子。|The 80s were known for bouffant hairstyles. 80年代以蓬松的发型而闻名。|近义词：puffy, full, voluminous|反义词：flat, sleek, straight|词性:形容词
