hypermania:/ˌhaɪpərˈmeɪniə/| n.|极度躁狂|A severe form of mania characterized by extreme restlessness, excitement, physical activity, and often, delusions of grandeur.|She was diagnosed with hypermania after experiencing a period of intense energy and euphoria followed by a severe crash.|近义词:mania, frenzy, delirium|反义词:depression, melancholy|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Greek *hyper* (over, excessive) + *mania* (madness, frenzy)
