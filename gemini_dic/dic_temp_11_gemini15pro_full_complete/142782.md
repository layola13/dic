oued:/wɑːd/| n. |干河; 雨季河流; 北非的干谷;|A wadi, also spelled wādī or oued, Arabic wādī, is a valley, ravine, or channel that is dry except in the rainy season. |例句：The oued was dry and dusty in the summer heat. 夏天酷热时，干河床干燥而尘土飞扬。

例句：The nomads relied on the infrequent rains to fill the oued with water. 游牧民族依靠不频繁的降雨来填满干河床。|近义词：wadi, arroyo, dry riverbed|反义词:|名词
