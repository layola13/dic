motherboard:/ˈmʌðərˌbɔːrd/ (US) /ˈmʌðəbɔːd/ (UK)| n. |主板|The main circuit board of a computer, containing the CPU, memory, and other essential components.  It acts as the central hub connecting all the computer's hardware.|My computer's motherboard needs replacing. 我的电脑主板需要更换。|Synonyms: mainboard, system board, logic board|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a physical component)|noun
