flashmob:/ˈflæʃmɒb/ (US) /ˈflæʃmɒb/ (UK)| n. |快闪族，快闪行动|A large public gathering at which people briefly perform an unusual or seemingly random act and then disperse, typically organized by means of the internet or social media.|例句：The flashmob surprised everyone in the park. 快闪族让公园里的每个人都感到惊讶。

例句：They organized a flashmob to dance to Michael Jackson's "Thriller." 他们组织了一场快闪行动，一起跳迈克尔·杰克逊的《颤栗》。|近义词：smart mob,  demonstration |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
