cashmere:/ˈkæʃmɪər/| n. adj.|羊绒，开司米；|A soft wool that comes from a type of goat that lives in the Himalayas.  一种来自喜马拉雅山地区山羊的柔软羊毛。|例句：She bought a luxurious cashmere sweater. 她买了一件奢华的羊绒衫。|例句：This scarf is made of 100% pure cashmere. 这条围巾是100%纯羊绒的。|近义词：pashmina, wool|反义词:synthetic fiber|词性:名词，形容词

Etymology: From Kashmir, a region in the Himalayas. 源自喜马拉雅山地区的克什米尔。
