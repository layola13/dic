accusatively:/əˈkjuːzətɪvli/| adv.|控诉地，指责地；作宾语地|In many languages, the direct object of a verb is in the accusative case. 在许多语言中，动词的直接宾语用宾格。|He looked at her accusatively. 他 accusingly 地看着她。|近义词：accusingly|反义词: N/A|词性:副词

Etymology:  From Late Latin *accusativus, from Latin accusare (“to accuse”).  The grammatical sense arose from the use of the accusative case in Latin with verbs of accusing.
