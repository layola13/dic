indecencies:/ɪnˈdiːsənsiz/
noun
猥亵行为;下流举动;不礼貌的行为
acts or statements that offend against accepted standards of morality or decency.
The report detailed numerous sexual indecencies.  这份报告详细描述了无数的性猥亵行为。
