feigning:/ˈfeɪnɪŋ/| v. |假装，装作|Pretending to feel or be affected by (something).|He was feigning illness to avoid work. 他装病以逃避工作。|She feigned surprise at the news. 她听到这个消息假装很惊讶。|近义词：pretending, simulating, faking, sham, affect|反义词：genuine, sincere, honest|词性: 动词 (present participle of feign)

feigned:/feɪnd/| adj. |假装的，虚假的|Simulated or pretended; insincere.|He gave a feigned cough. 他假装咳嗽了一声。|Her feigned indifference was unconvincing. 她假装的冷漠并不令人信服。|近义词：pretended, simulated, fake, sham, affected|反义词：genuine, sincere, honest|词性: 形容词 (past participle of feign)
