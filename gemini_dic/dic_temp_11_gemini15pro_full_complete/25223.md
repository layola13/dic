budgeting:/ˈbʌdʒɪtɪŋ/
n.
预算编制；预算管理
The process of creating a plan for how to spend money.
Budgeting is essential for financial stability. 预算编制对于财务稳定至关重要。
Careful budgeting is the key to saving money.  精心的预算编制是省钱的关键。
financial planning, money management
overspending, extravagance
noun
