complaining:/kəmˈpleɪnɪŋ/
v.
抱怨，诉苦
Expressing dissatisfaction or annoyance about something.
She is always complaining about the weather. 她总是抱怨天气。
He kept complaining about the noise. 他不停地抱怨噪音。
近义词:grumbling, whining, moaning, protesting
反义词:praising, commending, complimenting
词性:动词 (present participle of complain)

complaining:/kəmˈpleɪnɪŋ/
adj.
爱抱怨的, 抱怨的
Given to expressing dissatisfaction or annoyance.
a complaining customer 一位爱抱怨的顾客
He had a complaining tone. 他的语气带着抱怨。
近义词:querulous, fault-finding, critical, carping
反义词:satisfied, content, pleased, happy
词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *complaynen*, from Old French complaindre, from Latin plangere “to lament.”
