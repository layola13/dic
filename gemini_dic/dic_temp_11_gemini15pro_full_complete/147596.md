overplant: /ˌoʊvərˈplænt/ | vt. | 过度种植；栽种过多 | To plant too densely or excessively. | The farmer overplanted his field, resulting in stunted growth.  这个农民过度种植了他的田地，导致作物生长不良。 | The gardener overplanted the flower bed, leaving no room for the plants to spread. 园丁在花坛里种植了过多的花卉，导致植物没有空间生长。 | 近义词: overcrowd | 反义词: underplant | 词性: 动词

overplant: /ˈoʊvərˌplænt/ | n. | 过度种植；种植过多的作物 | An area or crop that has been overplanted. | The overplant of tomatoes led to a lower yield per plant.  番茄的过度种植导致每株植物的产量降低。 |  The overplant suffered from lack of nutrients and sunlight. 过度种植的作物缺乏营养和阳光。 | 近义词: overcrowding | 反义词: underplanting | 词性: 名词
