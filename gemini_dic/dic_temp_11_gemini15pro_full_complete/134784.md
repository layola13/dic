nonmeteorically:/ˌnɒnˌmiːtiˈɒrɪkli/ | adv. | 非陨石地 | Not derived from or relating to meteors.  Originating from sources other than meteors. |  The water was found to have originated nonmeteorically, likely from volcanic activity. 这些水被发现起源于非陨石，很可能是火山活动。 |  The minerals were deposited nonmeteorically. 这些矿物质是非陨石沉积的。 | Synonyms: terrestrially, endogenously | Antonyms: meteorically | adverb
