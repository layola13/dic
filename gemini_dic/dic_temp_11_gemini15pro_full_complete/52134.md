diapason:/daɪəˈpeɪzn/ /daɪˈæpəsɑːn/| n.|音叉；|The conductor used a diapason to tune the orchestra. 指挥家用音叉为乐队调音。|He checked the pitch with a diapason. 他用音叉检查了音高。|近义词：tuning fork|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
