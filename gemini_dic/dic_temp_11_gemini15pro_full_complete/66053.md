exasperater:/ɪɡˈzæspəreɪtər/ | n. | 激怒者，令人恼火的人或事物 | Someone or something that exasperates. |  His constant complaining is a real exasperater.  他不断的抱怨真让人恼火。 | Synonyms: irritant, annoyance, pest, provoker | Antonyms: soother, calmer, pacifier | noun

exasperates:/ɪɡˈzæspəreɪts/ | v. | 激怒，使恼怒 |  To irritate intensely; infuriate. | Her constant lateness exasperates me. 她总是迟到，真让我恼火。 | Synonyms: annoy, irritate, infuriate, vex, provoke | Antonyms: soothe, calm, placate, please | verb

exasperated:/ɪɡˈzæspəreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 感到恼怒的，被激怒的 | Intensely irritated and frustrated. | I was exasperated by his refusal to listen. 他拒绝听我的话，我感到非常恼火。 | Synonyms: annoyed, irritated, infuriated, vexed, frustrated | Antonyms: calm, soothed, pleased, content | adjective
