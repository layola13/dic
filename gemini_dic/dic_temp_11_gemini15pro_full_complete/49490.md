delvers:/ˈdɛlvərz/| n.|探究者，钻研者，挖掘者|A person who delves; someone who searches deeply and thoroughly into something.|The delvers unearthed ancient artifacts from the burial mound.|近义词：researchers, investigators, explorers, excavators|反义词：superficial readers, skimmers|词性:Noun


Etymology:
From Middle English *delvere*, from Old English *delfere* (“digger, delver”), equivalent to *delfan* (“to dig, delve”) + *-ere*. Cognate with Old Frisian *delver* (“digger”), Dutch *delver* (“digger”), German *Delfter* (“digger”). More at delve.
