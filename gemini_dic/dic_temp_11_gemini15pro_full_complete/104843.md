keelhaul:/ˈkiːlhɔːl/
verb
龙骨式处罚（将人绑在绳子上，从船底一侧拖到另一侧）; 严厉惩罚
to punish someone by dragging them under a ship's keel
例句：The captain threatened to keelhaul any man caught stealing. 船长威胁要对任何被抓到偷窃的人处以龙骨式处罚。
例句：He felt he had been metaphorically keelhauled by the press. 他觉得他被媒体严厉批评了。
近义词：punish, chastise, discipline
反义词：reward, praise
词性：v.

keelhauls:/ˈkiːlhɔːlz/
verb (third-person singular simple present)
龙骨式处罚（将人绑在绳子上，从船底一侧拖到另一侧）的第三人称单数形式; 严厉惩罚的第三人称单数形式
Third-person singular simple present form of keelhaul
例句：He keelhauls anyone who disobeys his orders.  他严厉惩罚任何不服从他命令的人。
例句：The cruel captain keelhauls the sailor for a minor infraction. 残酷的船长因为一个小错误就对水手处以龙骨式处罚。
近义词：punishes, chastises, disciplines
反义词：rewards, praises
词性：v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Dutch *kielhalen* "to drag along the keel," from *kiel* "keel" + *halen* "to pull, drag."
