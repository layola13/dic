gatehouse:/ˈɡeɪtˌhaʊs/| n. |门房，警卫室|A small building by the gate of a large building, where the gatekeeper lives or works.  A building forming part of a gateway, especially at the entrance to a fortified place.|The gatehouse was manned 24 hours a day. 门房一天24小时都有人值守。|Synonyms: lodge, gatekeeper's house, portal|Antonyms: N/A|noun
