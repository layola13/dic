meaningfully:/ˈmiːnɪŋfəli/| adv.|有意义地，意味深长地|In a way that shows thought and purpose; significantly.|She smiled meaningfully at him. 她意味深长地对他笑了笑。|近义词：significantly, purposefully, expressively|反义词：meaninglessly, pointlessly|词性:副词

词源: From "meaningful" + "-ly". "Meaningful" comes from "meaning" (from Old English "mænan" - to mean, intend) + "-ful" (suffix indicating "full of").
