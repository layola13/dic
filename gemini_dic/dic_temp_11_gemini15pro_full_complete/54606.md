dismayed:/dɪsˈmeɪd/| adj.|沮丧的，惊慌的，失望的|Filled with consternation and distress.  Feeling discouraged or let down.|例句：She was dismayed by the news of her friend's illness. 她听到朋友生病的消息感到很沮丧。|近义词：alarmed, apprehensive, discouraged, disheartened, perturbed, upset|反义词：cheerful, delighted, encouraged, heartened, pleased|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *dismayen*, from Old French *desmayer* (“to lose courage, be discouraged, lose one's senses”), from *des-* (expressing reversal) + *mayer* (“to hammer, beat”), from Late Latin *matiare*, ultimately from Latin *malleus* ("hammer").
