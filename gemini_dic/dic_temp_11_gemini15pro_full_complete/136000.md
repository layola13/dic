nonrationalism:/nɒnˈræʃənəlɪzəm/ /ˌnɑːnˈræʃənəlɪzəm/
n.
非理性主义
The rejection of rationalism, emphasizing intuition, feeling, or instinct over reason as a source of knowledge.
Nonrationalism often emphasizes the importance of emotions and spiritual experiences. 非理性主义通常强调情感和精神体验的重要性。
The artist's work was a clear expression of nonrationalism, prioritizing emotion over logic. 这位艺术家的作品清晰地表达了非理性主义，将情感置于逻辑之上。
irrationalism, antirationalism, mysticism
rationalism, logicism
noun
