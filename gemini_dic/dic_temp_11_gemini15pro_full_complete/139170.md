obituaries:/əˈbɪtʃuɛriz/ /oʊˈbɪtʃuɛriz/| n. |讣告，讣闻|A notice of a death, especially in a newspaper, typically including a brief biography of the deceased person.|The newspaper published obituaries of the prominent citizens who had passed away that week.  这家报纸刊登了那周去世的杰出市民的讣告。|Synonyms: death notice, obituary notice, necrology|Antonyms: birth announcement|Noun
