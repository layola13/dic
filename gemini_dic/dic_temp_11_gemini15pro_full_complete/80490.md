gonzo:/ˈɡɒnzəʊ/ (UK), /ˈɡɑːnzoʊ/ (US)| adj.|疯狂的，怪诞的，不加修饰的，(新闻报道)主观情绪强烈的|Of or relating to a style of journalism characterized by subjective, highly personal accounts of events, often including exaggeration and fabrication.  Also, bizarre, eccentric, unconventional, or outlandish.|

例句：
He wrote a gonzo article about his experiences in the jungle.
他写了一篇关于他在丛林中经历的怪诞文章。

例句：
The film was a gonzo mix of fact and fiction.
这部电影是对事实和虚构的疯狂混合。

近义词：bizarre, eccentric, unconventional, outlandish, wild, crazy, freaky, weird
反义词：conventional, ordinary, normal, mainstream
词性：形容词

词源：Originates from the 1970s, American slang. Possibly from the Italian word *gonzo* meaning "fool" or "simpleton," or perhaps from the Boston Irish slang term *gonzo* meaning "the last man standing" after a drinking bout.  Popularized by the writings of Hunter S. Thompson.
