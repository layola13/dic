extenuate:/ɪkˈstɛnjuːeɪt/ /ɪkˈstenjʊeɪt/ | v. | 减轻；开脱；为…辩解 | to make (guilt or an offense) seem less serious or more forgivable |  His youth and inexperience extenuate his mistake. 他的年轻和缺乏经验减轻了他的错误。|  The circumstances extenuate the crime. 情况减轻了罪行。 | synonyms: mitigate, lessen, diminish, excuse, justify | antonyms: aggravate, worsen, exacerbate | verb
