delitigate:/diːˈlɪtɪɡeɪt/
verb
减少诉讼；庭外和解
to settle a dispute out of court, or to reduce the amount of litigation
例句：The company decided to delitigate the matter to avoid further legal costs.  公司决定庭外和解此事，以避免进一步的法律费用。

例句：They are trying to delitigate the issue through mediation.  他们正试图通过调解来庭外和解这个问题。
近义词：settle, mediate, arbitrate, compromise, negotiate
反义词：litigate, sue, prosecute
词性:v.
