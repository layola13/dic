anglophilisms:/ˈæŋɡləʊfɪlɪzəmz/
|n.|亲英主义；对英国文化的喜爱；英语中的法语词|The adoption of English words or expressions into another language, especially by speakers of French.  The admiration or fondness for England or English customs, institutions, etc.|Her writing is full of anglophilisms.|近义词:Britishisms, Anglicisms|反义词:Francophilisms, Gallophilisms|词性:noun
