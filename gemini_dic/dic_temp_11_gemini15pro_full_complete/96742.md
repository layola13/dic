ineligibles:/ɪnˈɛlɪdʒəbəlz/
| n. |不合格者；不符合条件者；|People who are ineligible for something, such as a job, a competition, or a benefit, are not qualified for it or are not allowed to have it.|The ineligibles were asked to leave the competition.|近义词：unqualified, unsuitable, unacceptable|反义词: eligible, qualified|词性:名词

ineligible:/ɪnˈɛlɪdʒəbəl/
| adj. |不合格的；不符合条件的；|Not qualified or suitable for something; not allowed to have or do something.|He was ineligible to play because of his age.|近义词：unqualified, unsuitable, unacceptable|反义词: eligible, qualified|词性:形容词

词源:
From in- (not) + eligible (suitable, qualified), from Latin *eligere (to choose), from ex- (out) + legere (to choose, gather, read).
