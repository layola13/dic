bonkers:/ˈbɒŋkəz/ (UK) /ˈbɑːŋkərz/ (US)| adj.|疯狂的，精神错乱的|Crazy or insane; mentally unbalanced.|例句：He went completely bonkers after losing his job.  他失业后完全疯了。

例句：The heat is driving me bonkers.  这热度快把我逼疯了。

近义词：crazy, mad, insane, nuts, loopy, daft|反义词：sane, rational, sensible|词性:形容词
