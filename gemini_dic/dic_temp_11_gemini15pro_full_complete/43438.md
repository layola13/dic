crapshoot:/ˈkræpʃuːt/
noun
掷骰子赌博; 碰运气的事情
a situation in which there is a large element of chance involved in the outcome.
例句：Starting a new business can be a real crapshoot.  创业就像一场赌博。

例句：The election is a crapshoot, with no clear favorite.  这次选举胜负难料，没有明显的热门人选。

近义词：gamble, lottery, risk, long shot
反义词：sure thing, certainty
词性: noun
