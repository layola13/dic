imputative:/ɪmˈpjuːtətɪv/
adjective
归罪的，归咎的，归因于…的
attributed to someone, often unfairly; ascribed
例句：He gave her an imputative glance. 他带着责备的眼神看了她一眼。

例句：The imputative tone of his voice made her defensive. 他责备的语气让她很 defensive。

近义词：ascribed, attributed, assigned
反义词：exculpatory, absolving
