nonsubstantivenesses:/ˌnɒnsəbˈstænʃɪvnɪsɪz/ | n. | 非实质性；空洞性 |  The abstractness of the argument led to several nonsubstantivenesses in their proposal.  |  例句：The committee criticized the nonsubstantivenesses of the report, finding it lacked concrete evidence. 委员会批评报告缺乏实质内容，认为它缺乏具体的证据。

例句：He pointed out the nonsubstantivenesses in her claims, demonstrating they were based on conjecture rather than facts. 他指出了她说法中的空洞之处，证明它们是基于推测而不是事实。 | 近义词：insignificance, emptiness, hollowness, immateriality, triviality | 反义词：substance, significance, importance, materiality | 词性：名词

词源：nonsubstantivenesses 源自 nonsubstantive + -ness + -es。nonsubstantive 来自 non- (not) + substantive (having substance).  "-ness"是名词后缀，表示状态或性质。"-es"是复数后缀。
