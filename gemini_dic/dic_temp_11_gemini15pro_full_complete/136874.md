nonsmokers:/ˌnɑːnˈsmoʊkərz/ /ˌnɒnˈsməʊkəz/| n. |不吸烟者；非吸烟者|People who do not smoke cigarettes or other tobacco products.|The restaurant has a separate section for nonsmokers.  这家餐厅有不吸烟者的专区。|Near synonyms: non-smoker, abstainer from tobacco|Antonyms: smokers|Noun
