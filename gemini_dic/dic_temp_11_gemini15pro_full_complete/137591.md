nontrespass:/ˌnɒnˈtrɛspəs/ | n. | 非侵入，不侵犯；禁止侵入 | The act of not trespassing; refraining from entering another's property without permission. |  The hiker respected the nontrespass signs and stayed on the trail.  这位徒步旅行者尊重禁止侵入的标志，并留在小路上。| 近义词: nonintrusion, nonencroachment | 反义词: trespass, intrusion | 词性: 名词

nontrespassing:/ˌnɒnˈtrɛspæsɪŋ/ | adj. | 不侵入的，禁止侵入的 |  Relating to or involving the act of not trespassing; designed to prevent trespass. |  The landowner put up nontrespassing signs around the perimeter of his property.  地主在他的房产周围竖起了禁止侵入的标志。| 近义词: restrictive, prohibitive | 反义词: permissive, open | 词性: 形容词
