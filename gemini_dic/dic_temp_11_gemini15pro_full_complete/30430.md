centralizations:/ˌsɛntrələˈzeɪʃənz/| n. |集中化，中央集权|The process of bringing things or power under central control.|The company underwent a period of centralization, consolidating all decision-making power in the head office. 公司经历了一段时间的集中化，将所有决策权都集中到了总部。|近义词：consolidation, unification, integration|反义词：decentralization, distribution, dispersal| noun

centralization:/ˌsɛntrələˈzeɪʃən/| n. |中央集权，集中化|The act or process of centralizing something.|The centralization of power in the hands of a few individuals can lead to corruption.  权力集中在少数人手中会导致腐败。|近义词：concentration, merging|反义词：decentralization, diffusion| noun
