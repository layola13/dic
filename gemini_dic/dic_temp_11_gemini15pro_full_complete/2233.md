adds:/ædz/ | v. | 添加，增加 | To include or incorporate something else. | She adds sugar to her coffee. 她往咖啡里加糖。|  synonyms:  includes, incorporates, supplements, augments | antonyms: subtracts, removes, deducts | verb


adds:/ædz/ | v. |  加起来，总计 | To combine numbers or amounts to get a total. | The bill adds up to $50. 账单总计50美元。 | synonyms: totals, sums, amounts | antonyms: subtracts, deducts | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *adden*, from Old French *ader* (Modern French *ajouter*), from Latin *addere*, from *ad* ("to") + *dare* ("to give").
