nonimitational:/ˌnɒnɪmɪˈteɪʃənəl/
adjective
非模仿的, 非模仿性的
Not imitative; original.  Characterized by originality; not copied or derived from something else.
Her artwork is strikingly nonimitational, a true expression of her unique vision. 她的艺术作品惊人的非模仿性，是她独特视野的真实表达。

The scientist developed a nonimitational technique for analyzing the data.  这位科学家开发了一种非模仿性的数据分析技术。

Synonym: original, innovative, novel, unique, creative, unprecedented
Antonym: imitative, copied, derivative, unoriginal, conventional
