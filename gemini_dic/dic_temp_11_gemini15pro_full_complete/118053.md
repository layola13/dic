mendable:/ˈmɛndəbl/
adjective
可修补的; 可 اصلاح的
Capable of being mended; repairable.
The tear in the fabric is mendable with a needle and thread. 这块布料上的裂缝可以用针线缝补。
近义词: repairable, fixable, restorable
反义词: irreparable, unfixable, beyond repair
词性: adjective

mendable:/ˈmɛndəbl/
adjective
可改进的; 可改正的
Capable of being improved or corrected.
His behavior is certainly mendable with the right guidance. 他的行为在正确的引导下肯定可以得到改善。
近义词: improvable, correctable, amendable
反义词: incorrigible, irremediable, unimprovable
词性: adjective
