horsemint:/ˈhɔːrsˌmɪnt/| n. |马薄荷，蜂香薄荷|A strongly scented plant of the mint family, with toothed leaves and small white, pink, or purplish flowers, several kinds of which are common in North America. It is used in herbal teas and as a flavoring.|The horsemint attracted a variety of bees and butterflies to the garden.|Synonyms:  bergamot,  monarda,  bee balm|Antonyms: N/A|noun
