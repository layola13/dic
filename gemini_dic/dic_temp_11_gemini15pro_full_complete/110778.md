lingula:/ˈlɪŋɡjʊlə/
noun
小舌;舌状器官
A small tongue-shaped organ or process.
The lingula of the lung is a small tongue-shaped projection of the upper lobe of the left lung.  左肺小舌是左肺上叶的一个小舌状突起。

The surgeon carefully examined the lingula during the procedure.  外科医生在手术过程中仔细检查了小舌。
