cade:/keɪd/| n.| 军校学员，预备军官|A young person training to be an officer in the armed forces. |The cade spent the summer at a military academy.| 近义词:trainee, recruit|反义词:veteran|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cadet*, from Gascon *capdèt* "captain," ultimately from Late Latin *capitellum* "little head," diminutive of *caput* "head." Originally referred to younger sons in noble families who served in the military because they couldn't inherit the family estate.
