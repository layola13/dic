impastation:/ɪmˌpeɪˈsteɪʃən/ | n. | 厚涂颜料; 浓厚的涂抹; (绘画)堆色法 | The act of laying on paint thickly; thick paint; the technique in painting of applying paint thickly so that it stands out from the surface. | The artist used impastation to create a textured effect on the canvas.  艺术家用厚涂法在画布上创造了一种纹理效果。 |Synonyms: layering, daubing, piling | Antonyms: thinning, diluting | noun
