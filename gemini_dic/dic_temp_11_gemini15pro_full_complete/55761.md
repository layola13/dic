diversifiability:/dɪˌvɜːrsɪfaɪəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 多样化能力，可分散性 | The quality of being able to be diversified; the capacity for diversification.  The ability of an asset or portfolio to be spread across different investments to reduce risk. |  The fund managers emphasized the diversifiability of their portfolio across various asset classes.  基金经理强调了其投资组合在各种资产类别中的多样化能力。 |  diversity, variety, heterogeneity | concentration, homogeneity | noun

diversify:/dɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪ/ | v. | 使多样化，分散投资 | To make or become more diverse; to distribute among different investments to reduce risk. | They diversified their investments to minimize potential losses. 他们分散投资以尽量减少潜在损失。 |  expand, broaden, vary | concentrate, specialize | verb
