impetuous:/ɪmˈpɛtʃuəs/| adj.|冲动的，鲁莽的|Acting or done quickly and without thought or care; moving forcefully or rapidly.|例句：Her impetuous nature often led her to make rash decisions. 她冲动的性格经常导致她做出草率的决定。

例句：The impetuous river flowed rapidly down the mountain.  湍急的河流顺着山势奔腾而下。|近义词：rash, impulsive, hasty, reckless|反义词：cautious, prudent, deliberate|词性:adjective

Etymology->
From Late Latin *impetuosus, from Latin impetus (“attack, assault, onset, impulse, force, impetus”), from in- + petere (“to seek, go to, rush at, attack”).
