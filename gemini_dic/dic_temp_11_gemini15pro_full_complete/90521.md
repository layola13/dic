hued:/hjuːd/
adjective
有颜色的，色彩的
Having a particular color or shade.
The brightly hued flowers attracted many butterflies.  色彩鲜艳的花朵吸引了许多蝴蝶。

The desert landscape was composed of subtly hued rocks and sand. 沙漠景观由颜色微妙的岩石和沙子组成。

colorful, vibrant, tinted
colorless, drab, pale
词性:形容词
