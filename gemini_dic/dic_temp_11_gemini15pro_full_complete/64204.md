equidifferent:/ˌiːkwɪˈdɪfərənt/
adjective
等差的
Having equal differences; increasing or decreasing by the same amount.
The numbers 4, 7, 10, 13 are equidifferent, as the difference between consecutive terms is always 3.
这组数字 4，7，10，13 是等差的，因为连续项之间的差始终是 3。
equally spaced, uniform, regular
unequal, irregular
