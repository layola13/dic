misspaced:/ˌmɪsˈspeɪst/| adj.|间距错误的，排列不当的|incorrectly spaced, especially with reference to printed or typed characters|The document was messy and misspaced. 这份文件很乱，而且间距错误。|近义词：misaligned, improperly spaced|反义词: correctly spaced, properly aligned|词性:形容词

词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning "wrong" or "bad") + "spaced" (past participle of "space," meaning to arrange with spaces between).  Ultimately from Latin "spatium" meaning "space" or "room."
