decampments:/dɪˈkæmpmənts/
n.
营地撤离，迁徙；搬迁
The sudden decampments of the refugees left aid organizations struggling to provide support.  难民的突然迁徙使援助组织难以提供支持。
The annual decampments of the nomadic tribes followed ancient routes.  游牧部落每年的迁徙都遵循着古老的路线。
Synonym: departures, evacuations, relocations
Antonym: arrivals, settlements, establishments
noun
