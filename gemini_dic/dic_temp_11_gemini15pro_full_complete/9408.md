antiracism:/ˈæntiˈreɪsɪzəm/
|n.|反种族主义
|The policy or practice of opposing racism and promoting racial tolerance.|
|Antiracism is not just about being "not racist," it's about actively fighting against systemic racism. 反种族主义不仅仅意味着“不是种族主义者”，而是要积极地与系统性的种族主义作斗争。|
|Education plays a vital role in promoting antiracism.  教育在促进反种族主义方面发挥着至关重要的作用。|
