apologia:/əˌpɒləˈdʒiːə/
n.
a formal written defense of one's opinions or conduct; 道歉; 辩护; 辩解;
A reasoned argument or explanation in defense of a particular belief, doctrine, or practice.  为某一特定信仰、教义或实践进行的理性论证或解释。
Example: He wrote an apologia for his beliefs. 他为自己的信仰写了一篇辩护词。
Example:  She offered an apologia for her controversial remarks. 她为她有争议的言论作了辩护。
synonym: defense, justification, explanation, vindication, excuse, plea
antonym: accusation, condemnation, indictment, attack, critique
noun
