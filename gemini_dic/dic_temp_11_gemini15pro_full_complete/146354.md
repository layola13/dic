overfishing:/ˌoʊvərˈfɪʃɪŋ/
n.
过度捕捞
The act of catching too many fish, so that the population of fish becomes too small.
Overfishing is a serious threat to marine ecosystems. 过度捕捞是对海洋生态系统的严重威胁。
The decline in fish stocks is largely due to overfishing. 鱼类资源的减少主要是由于过度捕捞造成的。
近义词: overexploitation
反义词: sustainable fishing
