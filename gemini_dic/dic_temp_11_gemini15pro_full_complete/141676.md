optocoelia:/ˌɒptəˈsiːliə/| n. |腹腔镜检查|An examination of the abdominal cavity using an endoscope.|The surgeon performed an optocoelia to investigate the patient's abdominal pain.|近义词:laparoscopy, celioscopy|反义词: None|词性:noun

Etymology:  From Greek *opto-* (relating to vision) + *-koilia* (cavity, abdomen).  Compare with "optometry" (measurement of vision) and "celiac" (relating to the abdomen).
