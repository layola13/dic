oligandrous:/ˌɒlɪˈɡændrəs/
adjective
少雄蕊的；少数雄蕊的
Having few stamens. Specifically, having fewer stamens than petals.
Example: This particular orchid species is oligandrous, with only two stamens.  这种特殊的兰花种类是少雄蕊的，只有两个雄蕊。

Example: Oligandrous flowers are less common than polyandrous ones. 少雄蕊的花比多雄蕊的花少见。
synonym:  microstemonous (having small stamens)
antonym: polyandrous (having many stamens)
word type: adjective
