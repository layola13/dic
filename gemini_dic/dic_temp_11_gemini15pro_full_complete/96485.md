indivisibilities:/ˌɪndɪˌvɪzɪˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. |不可分割性；不可分割的东西|The quality or state of being indivisible; things that cannot be divided.|  High setup costs are often associated with indivisibilities in production.  高昂的启动成本通常与生产中的不可分割性有关。|
indivisibilities:/ˌɪndɪˌvɪzɪˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. |不可分割性；不可分割的东西|Certain resources, like large machinery, exhibit indivisibilities, meaning they can't be broken down into smaller, usable units.  某些资源，如大型机械，表现出不可分割性，这意味着它们不能被分解成更小的、可用的单元。|近义词：inseparability, unity, wholeness|反义词：divisibility, separability|词性: 名词 (plural noun)


ETYMOLOGY->
From indivisible + -ity.  Indivisible comes from Late Latin indivisibilis, from in- "not" + divisibilis "divisible," from dividere "to divide."
