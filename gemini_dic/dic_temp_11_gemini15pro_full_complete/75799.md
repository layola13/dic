fumblingly:/ˈfʌmblɪŋli/
adverb
笨拙地；慌乱地
in a fumbling or awkward manner.
He fumblingly searched for his keys in the dark. 他在黑暗中笨拙地摸索着钥匙。
近义词:awkwardly, clumsily, ineptly
反义词:deftly, skillfully, adroitly
词性:adverb

词源: From fumble + -ing + -ly.  "Fumble" comes from Middle Dutch "vommelen," frequentative of "vamelen" to grope.
