ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *clingan (attested only in the past participle clungen), of Germanic origin; related to Dutch klingen and German klingen ‘to sound, resound’.  The original sense was probably ‘to stick, adhere’, perhaps influenced by the verb cleave.
