fusibility:/ˌfjuːzəˈbɪləti/
n.
可熔性，易熔性
The quality of being fusible; capable of being melted or liquefied by heat.
The fusibility of the metal allows it to be easily molded.  这种金属的可熔性使其易于塑形。

近义词:meltability, liquefaction, fluidity
反义词:infusibility, refractoriness
词性:noun
