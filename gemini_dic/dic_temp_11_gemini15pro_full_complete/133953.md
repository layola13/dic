nonheinously:/nɑːnˈheɪnəsli/
adverb
非恶劣地；非极端地
not heinously; in a way that is not wicked or atrocious.
His crimes, while regrettable, were judged nonheinously. 虽然他的罪行令人遗憾，但被判定为非恶劣。
Near synonyms: mildly, moderately, not severely
Antonyms: heinously, atrociously, wickedly
词性: 副词
