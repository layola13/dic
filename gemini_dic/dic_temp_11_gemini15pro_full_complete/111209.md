lithophosphor:/ˌlɪθoʊˈfɒsfɔːr/ |n.| 储光材料;光激励发光材料 |A material that exhibits stimulated luminescence, meaning it stores energy from radiation and releases it as light when subsequently stimulated, typically by heat or light of a different wavelength.| Lithophosphors are used in dosimetry badges to measure radiation exposure.| 近义词: storage phosphor, OSL material | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider materials that don't store light energy) |词性: noun
