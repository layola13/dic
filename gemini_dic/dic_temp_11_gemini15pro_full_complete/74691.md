freegan:/ˈfriːɡən/| n.|免费素食主义者；拾荒者|A person who rejects consumerism and seeks to help the environment by reducing waste, especially by retrieving and using discarded food and other goods.|Example: The freegan found a perfectly good sofa left on the curb.  这个免费素食主义者在路边捡到了一张完好无损的沙发。

Example:  Freegans often dumpster dive to find edible food that would otherwise be wasted. 免费素食主义者经常在垃圾箱里翻找还可以食用的食物，这些食物本来会被浪费掉。|近义词：scavenger, dumpster diver|反义词:consumerist|词性:noun
