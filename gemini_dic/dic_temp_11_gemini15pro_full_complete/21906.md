boehm:/boʊm/ | n. | 波姆 | A surname, most notably associated with Theobald Boehm, inventor of the modern flute.  It also refers to the Boehm system, a keywork system for woodwind instruments, especially flutes and clarinets. |  Theobald Boehm revolutionized flute design. 西奥博尔德·波姆彻底改变了长笛的设计。| synonym: (for Boehm system) key system, fingering system | antonym: (no direct antonym for the name or the system) | noun
