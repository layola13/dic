amerindians:/əˌmɛrɪnˈdiənz/
noun
美洲印第安人
A term used to refer to the indigenous peoples of the Americas.  It encompasses a vast array of cultures, languages, and histories.
Many Amerindians maintain their traditional ways of life.  许多美洲印第安人保持着他们的传统生活方式。
