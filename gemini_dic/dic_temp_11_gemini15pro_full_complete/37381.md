commentated: /ˈkɒmənˌteɪtɪd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmənˌteɪtɪd/ (US)
verb
评论，解说
having provided a commentary on something, especially a sporting event.
例句：
The match was commentated by John Motson.
这场比赛由约翰·莫特森解说。

例句：
She commentated on the gymnastics competition for the BBC.
她为BBC解说了体操比赛。

近义词：narrated, reported, described, explained
反义词: listened, observed (as opposites of the action of commentating)
词性: verb (past participle)
