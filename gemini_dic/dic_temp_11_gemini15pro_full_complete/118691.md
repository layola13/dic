metabranchial:/ˌmɛtəˈbræŋkiəl/
adjective
后鳃的，后鳃的
Relating to or denoting the posterior gill of a fish, or the corresponding structures in other vertebrates.
The metabranchial lamina is a key structure in fish respiration. 后鳃薄片是鱼类呼吸的关键结构。
The metabranchial arteries supply blood to the gills. 后鳃动脉为鳃供血。
