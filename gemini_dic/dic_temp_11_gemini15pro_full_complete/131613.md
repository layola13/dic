noncomplicated:/ˌnɒnkɒmplɪˈkeɪtɪd/ /ˌnɑːnkɑːmplɪˈkeɪtɪd/
adjective
简单的，不复杂的
Not complicated; simple.
This recipe is noncomplicated and easy to follow.  这个食谱很简单，容易上手。
The instructions were clear and noncomplicated. 说明很清晰，并不复杂。
近义词:simple, straightforward, easy, uncomplicated
反义词:complicated, complex, intricate, difficult
词性:adjective
词源:non- (prefix meaning "not") + complicated (from Latin complicāre, "to fold together")
