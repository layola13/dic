Langerhans:/ˈlæŋərˌhɑːnz/ | n. (usually plural) |朗格汉斯 | Named after Paul Langerhans, a German pathologist and anatomist.  Refers to specific cells or islets found in the pancreas (islets of Langerhans) and skin (Langerhans cells). | Langerhans cells are part of the immune system. | 近义词: (For islets) pancreatic islets | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun


Langerhans cell: /ˈlæŋərˌhɑːnz sel/ | n. |朗格汉斯细胞| A type of dendritic cell found in the skin and other tissues, playing a role in the immune system. | Langerhans cells are important for presenting antigens to T cells. | 近义词: dendritic cell | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun


Langerhans islet: /ˈlæŋərˌhɑːnz ˈaɪlət/ | n. |朗格汉斯岛|  Clusters of endocrine cells in the pancreas that produce and secrete hormones such as insulin and glucagon. Also known as the islets of Langerhans. | The islets of Langerhans regulate blood sugar levels. | 近义词: pancreatic islet, islet of Langerhans | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun
