fulfil/fulfill: /fʊlˈfɪl/ /fʊlˈfɪl/ | vt. | 实现，履行，完成 | To carry out or bring to completion; to satisfy. |  She fulfilled her promise to visit her grandmother. 她履行了去看望她祖母的承诺。

|  He fulfilled his dream of becoming a doctor. 他实现了成为医生的梦想。 | 近义词：accomplish, achieve, complete, execute, realize, satisfy | 反义词：fail, neglect | 词性: 动词


fulfilment/fulfillment: /fʊlˈfɪlmənt/ /fʊlˈfɪlmənt/ | n. | 实现，履行，完成 | The act of fulfilling or the state of being fulfilled. | The fulfillment of her dreams brought her great joy.  她梦想的实现给她带来了巨大的快乐。


| Finding fulfillment in her work was important to her. 在工作中找到成就感对她来说很重要。 | 近义词：accomplishment, achievement, completion, realization, satisfaction | 反义词：failure, lack | 词性: 名词

词源：From Middle English fulfillen, from Old English fullfyllan (“to fill full, fulfill, complete”), equivalent to full + fill.  The spelling fulfill is the preferred spelling in American English.
