dogmatizer:/ˈdɒɡmətaɪzər/ (US) /ˈdɔːɡmətaɪzər/ (UK)| n. |教条主义者，独断论者|A person who dogmatizes; someone who asserts opinions in a doctrinaire or arrogant manner.| He was a fierce dogmatizer, unwilling to consider any viewpoint but his own. 他是一个狂热的教条主义者，不愿考虑除自己以外的任何观点。| 近义词：doctrinaire, authoritarian, rigid, inflexible | 反义词: freethinker, liberal, open-minded | 词性: noun

dogmatize:/ˈdɒɡmətaɪz/ (US) /ˈdɔːɡmətaɪz/ (UK) | v. |武断地提出，独断地声明|To express opinions in a dogmatic or arrogant manner; to lay down principles as undeniably true.|She tended to dogmatize about the proper way to raise children. 她倾向于武断地谈论养育孩子的正确方法。|近义词：pontificate, preach, assert, pronounce | 反义词: question, inquire, doubt | 词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin dogmatizare, from Ancient Greek δογματίζειν (dogmatizein, “to form dogmas”), from δόγμα (dógma, “opinion, tenet, doctrine”).
