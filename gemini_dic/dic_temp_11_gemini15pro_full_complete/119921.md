milkful:/ˈmɪlkfʊl/ | adj. | 富含牛奶的; 多奶的 | Full of milk; milky.  |  The cow gave a milkful pail.  这头奶牛产出了一桶满满的牛奶。|  近义词: milky, creamy | 反义词: milkless | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  "milk" + "-ful" (suffix indicating abundance).  "Milk" comes from Old English *meolc, from Proto-Germanic *melk, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂melǵ-  ("to milk; milk"). "-ful" comes from Old English "-ful", from Proto-Germanic *-fullaz.
