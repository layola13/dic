chrysalid: /ˈkrɪsəlɪd/ | n. | 蛹；茧；处于过渡期的事物 | The butterfly emerged from its chrysalid. 蝴蝶从蛹中羽化而出。| A chrysalid is the pupal stage of certain insects, especially butterflies, during which the larva undergoes metamorphosis into an adult. 蛹是某些昆虫，尤其是蝴蝶的蛹期，在此期间幼虫经历变态发育为成虫。 |例句：
The writer spent many years in intellectual chrysalid before finding her true voice.  这位作家在找到自己真正的声音之前，在智力茧中度过了许多年。

The company is emerging from its chrysalid and becoming a major player in the industry.  这家公司正从它的过渡期中脱颖而出，成为该行业的主要参与者。

| 近义词： pupa, cocoon, shell | 反义词: adult, imago | 词性: noun


chrysalis: /ˈkrɪsəlɪs/ | n. | 蛹；茧；处于过渡期的事物 | The chrysalis of the monarch butterfly is bright green.  帝王蝶的蛹是鲜绿色的。|  A chrysalis is a hard, protective case, usually brown or green, in which the larva of a butterfly or moth transforms into an adult. 蛹是一个坚硬的保护壳，通常是棕色或绿色，蝴蝶或蛾的幼虫在其中转变为成虫。| 例句：

The artist's studio was a chrysalis of creativity.  这位艺术家的工作室是一个充满创造力的茧。

The project is still in its chrysalis stage, but we expect great things from it.  这个项目仍处于萌芽阶段，但我们对它寄予厚望。

| 近义词： pupa, cocoon, shell | 反义词: adult, imago | 词性: noun
