commonest:/ˈkɒmənɪst/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmənɪst/ (US)| adj.|最常见的，最普通的|Most frequent or most widely occurring; prevalent.|例句：The commonest type of bird in this area is the robin.  这个地区最常见的鸟类是知更鸟。

例句：Colds are the commonest childhood illness. 感冒是最常见的儿童疾病。

近义词：most frequent, most usual, most prevalent, predominant, typical, prevailing|反义词：rarest, unusual, atypical, uncommon, infrequent|词性:形容词
