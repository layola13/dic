acinetae: /ˌæsɪˈniːtiː/
noun
固着期
sessile stage (non-motile, attached) in the life cycle of certain protozoa, especially suctorians.
The acinetae stage allows the organism to attach to a substrate and reproduce.
Suctorians have a complex life cycle that includes both a free-swimming swarmer stage and a sessile acinetae stage.

近义词: sessile stage, attached stage
反义词: motile stage, swarmer stage
词性: noun
