fertilize: /ˈfɜːrtəlaɪz/ (US) /ˈfɜːtɪlaɪz/ (UK)| v. |使肥沃；施肥；使受精|To make soil or land richer and better for growing plants, or to put pollen into a plant so that a seed develops|例句：
The farmer fertilized his fields with manure.  农民用肥料给田地施肥。

He fertilized the egg with his sperm. 他用自己的精子使卵子受精。

|近义词：enrich, nourish, inseminate|反义词: deplete, sterilize|词性:动词
