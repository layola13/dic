aesculapian:/ˌɛskjəˈleɪpiən/
|adj.|医术的；医药的；医家的|Relating to medicine or healing; medical.|例句：The aesculapian staff is a symbol of medicine.  阿斯克勒庇俄斯之杖是医学的象征。

例句：He possessed an aesculapian knowledge of herbs and remedies. 他拥有丰富的草药和治疗方法的医学知识。
|近义词：medical, therapeutic, healing|反义词：non-medical|词性：形容词


aesculapian:/ˌɛskjəˈleɪpiən/
|n.|医生；医务人员|A physician or healer.|例句：The aesculapian tended to the wounded soldiers.  医生照顾受伤的士兵。

例句：The village relied on the local aesculapian for all their medical needs. 这个村庄依靠当地的医生来满足他们所有的医疗需求。
|近义词：physician, doctor, healer, medic|反义词：patient|词性：名词
