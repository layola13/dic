couchant:/ˈkaʊtʃənt/
adjective
躺着的，伏卧的
Lying down, especially with the head raised.
The lion couchant is a common heraldic symbol. 躺卧的狮子是常见的纹章符号。
近义词:recumbent, prostrate, lying
反义词: rampant, standing, erect
词性:adjective

couchant:/ˈkaʊtʃənt/
noun
躺卧的动物（尤指纹章中的）
An animal, especially in heraldry, that is lying down with the head raised.
The crest featured a lion couchant.  徽章上有一只躺卧的狮子。
近义词:N/A (as a noun, it refers to a specific heraldic posture)
反义词: rampant (in heraldry)
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *cochant, present participle of coucher “to lie down,” from Vulgar Latin *colcare, from Latin collocare “to place, set, arrange” (from com “with, together” + locare “to place”).
