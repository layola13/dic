in-the-way:/ɪn ðə weɪ/ | adj.| 阻碍的，挡道的 |  Being an obstacle or impediment; preventing progress or movement.  | Example: The boxes in the hallway were in the way. 走廊里的箱子挡道了。| Example:  His car was parked in the way, blocking the driveway. 他的车挡住了车道。 | Synonyms: obstructing, hindering, impeding, blocking | Antonyms: helpful, assisting, facilitating | Adjective

in-the-way:/ɪn ðə weɪ/ | adv.| 碍事地，挡路地 | In a manner that obstructs or impedes. | Example:  She left her suitcase in the way, and I tripped over it. 她把行李箱放在路中间，我被绊倒了。 | Example: The toys were scattered in the way, making it difficult to walk. 玩具散落一地，很难行走。 | Synonyms: obstructively, hinderingly, impedingly | Antonyms: helpfully, considerately | Adverb
