newspaperwomen: /ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpərˌwɪmɪn/ /ˈnuːzˌpeɪpərˌwɪmɪn/
n.
女报人；女新闻记者
Women who work for newspapers, typically as reporters, editors, or photographers.
例句：The newspaperwomen were quick to report on the breaking news. 女报人们迅速报道了突发新闻。

例句：Several experienced newspaperwomen covered the political summit. 几位经验丰富的报纸女记者报道了这次政治峰会。


近义词：female journalists, reporters, news writers
反义词: None (No direct opposite for a profession)
词性: Noun
