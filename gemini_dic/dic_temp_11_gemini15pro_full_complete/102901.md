jawboner: /ˈdʒɔːˌboʊnər/
n.|施压者；运用非正式压力（尤指政府运用舆论压力）试图影响商业活动的人|Someone who tries to influence business activities through informal pressure, especially a government official who appeals to businesses to act in the public interest.|The president acted as a jawboner, urging companies to keep prices down. 总统扮演了施压者的角色，敦促企业压低价格。|persuader, influencer|coercer, dictator|noun
