conducting:/kənˈdʌktɪŋ/
adjective
传导的；导电的；指挥的
transmitting heat, electricity, or sound; directing the performance of an orchestra or choir.
The conducting wire is made of copper. 这根导线是用铜做的。
Copper has high conducting properties. 铜具有很高的导电性。

conducting:/kənˈdʌktɪŋ/
noun
传导；指挥
the transmission of heat, electricity, or sound; the direction of an orchestra or choir.
The conducting of electricity through a wire is a basic principle of physics. 电流通过导线的传导是物理学的基本原理。
His conducting of the orchestra was masterful. 他对乐队的指挥非常出色。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "conductus" (past participle of "conducere" - to lead together), from "com-" (together) + "ducere" (to lead).

SYNONYMS->
(adjective) conductive, transmitting, carrying
(noun)  transmission, conveyance, direction, leadership

ANTONYMS->
(adjective) non-conducting, insulating, resistant
(noun) insulation, resistance
