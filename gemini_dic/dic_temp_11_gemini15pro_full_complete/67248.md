expunge:/ɪkˈspʌndʒ/ | v. | 删除，抹去，消除 | To erase or remove completely (something unwanted or unpleasant). |  The judge ordered the conviction expunged from his record. 法官下令将他的定罪记录删除。 | synonyms: erase, delete, obliterate, remove, efface | antonyms:  preserve, retain, record, insert, add | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin expungere "to mark out by pricking, to erase," from Latin ex- "out" + pungere "to prick, to sting."
