dingier:/ˈdɪndʒiər/
adjective
更脏的，更昏暗的
comparative adjective of dingy; more dingy.  Covered with or marked by grime or dirt; shabby and dismal.
The room was dingier than I remembered, with peeling paint and dusty furniture.  这房间比我记忆中的更脏了，油漆剥落，家具也满是灰尘。

The alleyway became dingier as the sun set, making it feel even more unsafe.  随着太阳落山，小巷变得更加昏暗，让人感觉更加不安全。
Near Synonyms:shabbier, grimier, darker, duller, murkier
Antonyms:brighter, cleaner, fresher, lighter, shinier
词性:形容词
