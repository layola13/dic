cruse:/kruːz/
| n.| 小罐，小坛，油壶；巡游 |A small earthenware pot or jar for holding liquids, especially oil or vinegar; a cruise (less common usage).|She kept her olive oil in a small cruse. 她把橄榄油放在一个小油罐里。|The ship embarked on a three-week cruse. 这艘船开始了为期三周的航行。|近义词：jar, pot, jug, vial,  cruise, voyage|反义词:|noun

cruse:/kruːz/
| v.| 巡游，巡航 (less common usage)|To sail about, especially for pleasure or in search of something.|They crused along the coast. 他们沿着海岸航行。|近义词：cruise, sail, voyage, journey|反义词:|verb
