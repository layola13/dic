outstepped:/ˌaʊtˈstɛpt/| v. |超越；超出…的限度；越权|To go beyond the proper limits, especially of authority or propriety.|He outstepped his authority by signing the contract without consulting the board. 他未经董事会同意就签了合同，越权了。|Synonyms: exceed, overstep, transgress|Antonyms: observe, respect, comply|verb

outstepped:/ˌaʊtˈstɛpt/| v. |步伐超过；比…走得快|To walk faster or further than.|The younger hikers soon outstepped the older ones. 年轻的徒步旅行者很快就超过了年长的徒步旅行者。|Synonyms: outpace, outwalk, outrun|Antonyms: lag behind, fall behind|verb
