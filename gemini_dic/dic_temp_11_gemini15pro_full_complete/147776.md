overpromote:/ˌōvərprəˈmōt/| vt.|过度晋升，提拔过高|To promote (someone) to a position that is beyond their capabilities.|The company overpromoted him to a managerial role, and he struggled to cope.|近义词：overadvance, overreach|反义词:demote, underpromote|词性:动词

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "promote" (from Latin *promotus*, past participle of *promovere*, "to move forward")
