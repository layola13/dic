adsorbabilities:/ˌædzɔːrbəˈbɪlɪtiz/ | n. | 吸附能力；吸附性 |  The adsorbabilities of different materials vary greatly depending on their surface area and chemical properties. 不同材料的吸附能力因其表面积和化学性质而异。|  The researcher compared the adsorbabilities of activated carbon and zeolites for removing pollutants from water. 研究人员比较了活性炭和沸石去除水中污染物的吸附能力。 | 近义词: adsorption capacities, sorption affinities | 反义词: desorption tendencies | 词性: 名词

词源:  From "adsorbable" + "-ity". "Adsorb" comes from Latin "ad-" (to) + "sorbere" (to suck in).
