overdogmatical: /ˌoʊvərˌdɒɡˈmætɪkl/ (US) /ˌəʊvədɒɡˈmætɪkl/ (UK)| adj. | 过于教条的，过于武断的 | Excessively dogmatic; asserting opinions in a doctrinaire or arrogant manner.  Characterized by or given to the expression of opinions very strongly or positively as if they were facts. | He was so overdogmatical in his views that no one could have a reasonable discussion with him. 他观点过于教条，以至于没有人能和他进行理性的讨论。|  The professor's overdogmatical approach alienated many students. 教授过于教条的方法疏远了许多学生。 | doctrinaire, rigid, inflexible, opinionated, authoritarian | open-minded, flexible, tolerant, receptive | adjective
