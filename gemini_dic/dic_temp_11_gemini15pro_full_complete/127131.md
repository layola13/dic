nativization:/ˌneɪtɪvaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |本土化；本地化；归化|The process of adapting something to a local environment or culture.  The nativization of a foreign product often involves changes to its design, packaging, and marketing.|The company is undergoing a period of nativization to better appeal to the local market.  该公司正在经历一个本土化阶段，以更好地吸引当地市场。|localization, adaptation, indigenization|foreignization, internationalization|noun
