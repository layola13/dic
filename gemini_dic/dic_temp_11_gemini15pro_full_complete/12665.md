assembles:/əˈsɛmblz/
| v. |集合，聚集，装配|To gather or bring together (parts) into a group or whole; to fit together the components of (a machine or other object).|The workers assembled the car parts on the assembly line. 工人们在装配线上组装汽车零件。|Synonyms: gather, congregate, collect, amass, construct, build|Antonyms: disperse, scatter, dismantle, disassemble|verb

assembles:/əˈsɛmblz/
| n. |集会; 集合体 (罕见用法) |A gathering or collection of people or things. (This noun form is less common.) |The coach addressed the assemble of athletes before the competition. 教练在比赛前向运动员集会发表讲话。|Synonyms: gathering, assembly, collection, congregation|Antonyms: dispersal, scattering|noun
