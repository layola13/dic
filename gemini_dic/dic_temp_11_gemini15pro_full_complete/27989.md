canvasser:/ˈkænvəsər/| n.|游说者；推销员；调查员|A person who solicits votes, orders, subscriptions, or opinions.|The canvassers went door to door seeking support for their candidate.  推销员挨家挨户地寻求对其候选人的支持。|Synonyms: solicitor, salesperson, campaigner, pollster|Antonyms: customer, client|noun

canvass:/ˈkænvəs/| v. n.|拉选票；游说；细查；|To solicit votes, opinions, or support; to examine carefully.|They canvassed the neighborhood for their candidate. 他们在附近为他们的候选人拉选票。|Synonyms: solicit, poll, survey, scrutinize|Antonyms: ignore, neglect|verb, noun
