intermit:/ˌɪntərˈmɪt/ | v. |  中断，间歇 | To stop or pause something temporarily, with the intention of resuming it later. | The rain intermitted for a few hours, then started again. 雨停了几个小时，然后又开始了。|  The disease may intermit for several months at a time. 这种疾病可能会一次间歇几个月。 | synonyms: pause, suspend, cease, discontinue, break off | antonyms: continue, persist, maintain, proceed | verb

intermittent:/ˌɪntərˈmɪtənt/ | adj. |  断断续续的，间歇的 | Occurring at irregular intervals; not continuous or steady. | Intermittent showers are forecast for this afternoon.  今天下午预报有阵雨。 |  He has an intermittent cough that has lasted for weeks. 他断断续续地咳嗽了好几个星期。 | synonyms: sporadic, occasional, periodic, irregular | antonyms: continuous, constant, steady, persistent | adjective
