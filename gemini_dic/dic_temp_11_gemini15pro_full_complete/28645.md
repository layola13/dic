caribou:/ˈkærɪbuː/ (US), /ˈkærɪbuː/ (UK)| n.|驯鹿|A large deer with large antlers, found in the arctic and subarctic regions of North America, Europe, Asia, and Greenland.  It is closely related to the reindeer of Europe and Asia.|Caribou migrate long distances each year. 驯鹿每年都会进行长途迁徙。

Caribou are an important food source for many indigenous peoples. 驯鹿是许多原住民的重要食物来源。|近义词：reindeer|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider other animals in different habitats like deer, elk, moose in less harsh climates)|词性:noun
