belittle:/bɪˈlɪtl/| v. |轻视，贬低|To make someone or something seem unimportant.|He belittled her achievements, making her feel insignificant. 他贬低她的成就，让她觉得自己微不足道.|Synonyms: disparage, denigrate, deprecate, minimize, underestimate, underrate|Antonyms: praise, commend, extol, laud, magnify, overestimate|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + little.  Be- is a prefix meaning "make" or "cause to be."  Little, of course, means small or insignificant.  Thus, belittle literally means "to make small."
