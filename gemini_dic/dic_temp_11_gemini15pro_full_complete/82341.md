PHRASE->
grow up 长大
He grew up in the countryside. 他在农村长大。

grow out of  戒除（习惯）；不再适合
She grew out of her childhood fears. 她克服了童年的恐惧。
He has grown out of his clothes. 他的衣服已经小了。

grow into  发展成为；逐渐养成
The small business grew into a large corporation.  这家小企业发展成为一家大公司。
He grew into the habit of reading every night. 他养成了每晚读书的习惯。

grow on  越来越喜欢
The song grew on me after a few listens.  听了幾遍之後，我越來越喜歡這首歌了。

grow apart  感情疏远
Over the years, the two friends grew apart.  多年来，这两个朋友逐渐疏远了。
