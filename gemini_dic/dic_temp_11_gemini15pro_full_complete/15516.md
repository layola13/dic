baffling:/ˈbæflɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人困惑的，难以理解的 |  Something baffling is extremely confusing or puzzling. |  The baffling disappearance of the plane remains unexplained.  这架飞机令人困惑的失踪仍然无法解释。 | synonyms: perplexing, puzzling, bewildering, confusing, mystifying | antonyms: clear, obvious, understandable | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From baffle (v.) + -ing. Baffle originally referred to a device used to control the flow of air or liquid, later extending metaphorically to mean "to frustrate or confuse."
