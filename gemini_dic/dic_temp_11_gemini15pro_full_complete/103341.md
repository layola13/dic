jiko:/ˈdʒiːkoʊ/| n.|事故，故障；自焚；（尤指非洲的）便携式木炭炉|A portable charcoal stove, especially in Africa; an accident, breakdown, or malfunction; self-immolation. |The jiko overturned, setting the tent on fire. 便携式木炭炉翻倒了，把帐篷点着了。|近义词：accident, incident, mishap, malfunction, stove, burner|反义词:smooth operation, working order|noun

jiko:/ˈdʒiːkoʊ/| n.|自焚|The act of setting oneself on fire, especially as a form of protest. |Reports emerged of several jikos in protest of the government's policies. 有报道称，一些人自焚以抗议政府的政策。|近义词：self-immolation, suicide|反义词:survival|noun
