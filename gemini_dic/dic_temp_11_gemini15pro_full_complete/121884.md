miss-cue: /ˌmɪsˈkjuː/ | n. | 失误，失策 | An action or utterance that is inappropriate or ill-timed, especially in a performance or social context.  A mistake, especially in the performance of a play or in a game. | The actor's miss-cue ruined the entire scene. 演员的失误毁了整个场景。

The quarterback's miss-cue resulted in an interception. 四分卫的失误导致了被拦截。 | synonyms: blunder, error, gaffe, slip-up, mistake | antonyms:  success, triumph, perfection | noun
