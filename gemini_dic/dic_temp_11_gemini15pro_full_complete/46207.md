cyrillics: /sɪˈrɪlɪks/ | n. | 西里尔字母 |  A writing system used for various languages, primarily Slavic languages like Russian, Bulgarian, and Serbian. It's named after St. Cyril, a Byzantine monk who, along with his brother Methodius, developed the Glagolitic alphabet, a precursor to Cyrillic. | The Cyrillic alphabet is used to write Russian.  西里尔字母用于书写俄语。| synonyms: Cyrillic alphabet, Cyrillic script | antonyms: Latin alphabet, Roman alphabet | noun
