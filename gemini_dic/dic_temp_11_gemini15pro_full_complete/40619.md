convenience:/kənˈviːniəns/ |n.| 便利; 便利设施; 方便;| something that makes life easier or more comfortable |The hotel offers a range of conveniences, including a swimming pool and a fitness center.  这家酒店提供一系列便利设施，包括游泳池和健身中心。| synonym: amenity, comfort, facility | antonym: inconvenience, discomfort, difficulty | noun

convenience:/kənˈviːniəns/ |n.| 便利; 便利设施; 方便; |the quality of being useful, easy, or suitable for a particular purpose|We chose the house for its convenience to the school. 我们选择这所房子是因为它离学校很近，方便上学。|synonym: suitability, usefulness, practicality | antonym: unsuitability, impracticality, uselessness | noun
