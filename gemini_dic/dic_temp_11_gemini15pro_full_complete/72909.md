fomenters:/ˈfəʊmentərz/ (UK) /ˈfoʊmentərz/ (US)| n.|煽动者，教唆者|Someone who stirs up trouble or encourages negative feelings or actions.|The fomenters of the rebellion were quickly arrested. 叛乱的煽动者很快就被逮捕了。|Synonyms: agitators, instigators, provocateurs|Antonyms: peacemakers, mediators, conciliators|noun

foments:/fəˈments/ (UK) /foʊˈments/ (US)| v.|煽动，激起，助长|To instigate or stir up (an undesirable or violent sentiment or course of action).|The politician was accused of fomenting racial hatred.  这位政客被指控煽动种族仇恨。|Synonyms: incite, provoke, instigate|Antonyms: quell, suppress, allay|verb
