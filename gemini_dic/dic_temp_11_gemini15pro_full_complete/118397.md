meropodite:/ˈmɛrəpəˌdaɪt/| n. |节肢动物的肢节|A segment of a typical arthropod limb, located between the ischiopodite (closest to the body) and the carpopodite.|The meropodite of the crab's claw is particularly strong. 螃蟹爪的肢节特别强壮。|Synonyms: podomere, limb segment|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a body part)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *mēros* "part, portion" + *pous, pod-* "foot"
