nitrify: /ˈnaɪtrɪfaɪ/ (US), /ˈnaɪtrɪfaɪ/ (UK)| v.| 硝化| To convert (ammonia or ammonium salts) into nitrites or nitrates, especially by the action of bacteria.|例句：The bacteria in the soil nitrify the ammonia. 土壤中的细菌将氨硝化。|例句：The process of nitrification helps to make nitrogen available to plants. 硝化过程有助于使氮素可供植物利用。|近义词：oxidize|反义词: denitrify|词性: 动词
