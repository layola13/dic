mass-marketed: /ˌmæsˈmɑːrkɪtɪd/ | adj. | 大众市场的，面向大众的 | Marketed to the general public, often at a low price and in large quantities. |  Mass-marketed products often lack individuality. 大众市场的产品通常缺乏个性。|  The company launched a mass-marketed version of its premium product.  这家公司推出了其高端产品的大众市场版本。 | Synonyms: commercial, popular, mainstream | Antonyms: niche, exclusive, specialized | adjective

mass-market: /ˈmæs ˌmɑːrkɪt/ | n. | 大众市场 | The market for goods that are produced and sold in large quantities. |  The company aims to reach the mass market with its affordable products. 这家公司旨在通过其价格合理的商品触及大众市场。 | They developed a new advertising campaign for the mass market. 他们为大众市场开发了一个新的广告活动。 | Synonyms: general public, mainstream market | Antonyms: niche market, specialized market | noun

mass-market: /ˈmæs ˌmɑːrkɪt/ | v. |  向大众市场销售 | To market (a product) to the general public. | The car was mass-marketed to families. 这款车面向家庭销售。 | They successfully mass-marketed their new clothing line. 他们成功地将他们的新服装系列推向大众市场。 | Synonyms: popularize, commercialize | Antonyms: specialize, target (a niche) | verb
