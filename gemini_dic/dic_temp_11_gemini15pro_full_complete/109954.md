liberalizing:/ˈlɪbərəlaɪzɪŋ/
Verb (present participle)
使自由化；放宽；解除管制
The act of making something less strict or controlled, often referring to laws, regulations, or social customs.
The government is liberalizing trade policies to encourage foreign investment. 政府正在放宽贸易政策以鼓励外国投资。
Synonyms: deregulating, easing, relaxing, loosening
Antonyms: restricting, tightening, controlling, regulating
verb

liberalize:/ˈlɪbərəlaɪz/
Verb
使自由化；放宽；解除管制
To make something less strict or controlled, often referring to laws, regulations, or social customs.

Synonyms: deregulate, ease, relax, loosen

liberalization:/ˌlɪbərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
Noun
自由化；放宽；解除管制
The process or act of making something less strict or controlled.
The liberalization of the financial markets has led to increased competition. 金融市场的自由化导致了竞争加剧。
Synonyms: deregulation, easing, relaxation, loosening
Antonyms: restriction, tightening, control, regulation
