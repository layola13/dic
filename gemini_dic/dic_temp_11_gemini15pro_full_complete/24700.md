broncos:/ˈbrɒŋkəʊz/ (UK) /ˈbrɑːŋkoʊz/ (US)| n.|野马（复数）|A small, strong horse of the western US, used for riding and working with cattle. It is known for its endurance and agility.|The cowboys rode their broncos across the plains. 牛仔们骑着他们的野马穿过平原。|近义词：horses, ponies|反义词: N/A|词性: 名词

词源: Spanish *bronco*, meaning "rough," "coarse," referring to the horse's untamed nature.
