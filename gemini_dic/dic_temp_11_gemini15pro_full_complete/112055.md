longer:/ˈlɔːŋɡər/ (US), /ˈlɒŋɡə(r)/ (UK)| adj. |更长的，更久的|comparative form of "long";  used to compare the length or duration of two things.  It indicates that one thing is greater in length or duration than another.|The movie was longer than I expected.  这部电影比我想象的要长。|近义词：extended, lengthier|反义词:shorter|词性:形容词
