antirealistic:/ˌæntiˌriːəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
反现实的；非写实的
Opposed to or deliberately departing from realism.  Not representing or imitating reality.
His paintings are known for their antirealistic style. 他的画作以其反现实主义的风格而闻名。

The play was highly stylized and antirealistic.  这出戏高度程式化，反现实主义。


近义词:unrealistic, nonrealistic, surreal, abstract
反义词:realistic, naturalistic
词性:adjective
