dimensions:/daɪˈmɛnʃənz/ | n. |维度，尺寸，规模| magnitude or extent of something, especially as measured in one or more directions |The dimensions of the room are 10 feet by 12 feet.  房间的尺寸是10英尺乘12英尺。| synonyms: size, measurements, proportions | antonyms: immeasurability | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dimensionem (nominative dimensio) "a measuring," noun of action from past-participle stem of Latin dimetiri "measure out," from di- "out" + metiri "to measure."
