a broken reed:/ə ˈbroʊkən ˈriːd/ | n. |  一个不可靠的人/物；脆弱的依靠 | Someone or something that is unreliable and cannot be depended on in a time of need. | He turned to his friend for help, but he proved to be a broken reed. 他向朋友求助，但结果证明他是个靠不住的人。| synonyms: unreliable person, weak support, false friend | antonyms: tower of strength, reliable source, true friend | noun
