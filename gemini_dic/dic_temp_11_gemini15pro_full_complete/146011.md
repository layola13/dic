overdoubted:/ˌoʊvərˈdaʊtɪd/
adjective
被过度怀疑的
Doubted to an excessive degree.  Suspected more than is warranted or necessary.
He felt overdoubted by his colleagues, who constantly questioned his every decision. 他觉得同事们过度怀疑他，总是质疑他的每一个决定。
The scientist's revolutionary theory was initially overdoubted, but later proved to be correct. 这位科学家的革命性理论最初被过度怀疑，但后来证明是正确的。
近义词:distrusted, questioned, suspected
反义词:trusted, believed, accepted
词性:adjective
