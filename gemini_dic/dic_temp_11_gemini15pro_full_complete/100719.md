intramembranous:/ˌɪntrəˈmɛmbrənəs/| adj. |膜内的，膜生的|Developing or occurring within a membrane, specifically relating to bone formation directly from mesenchymal tissue without a prior cartilaginous stage.|例句：Intramembranous ossification is the process by which flat bones of the skull are formed. 膜内成骨是颅骨扁平骨形成的过程。

例句：The intramembranous bone formation is essential for the development of the craniofacial skeleton. 膜内骨形成对于颅面骨骼的发育至关重要。|近义词：membranous|反义词：endochondral|词性:形容词
