ampersand: /ˈæmpərsænd/ | n. | 和，及 | A symbol (&) representing the word *and*.  It comes from the Latin phrase "et per se and," meaning "and by itself and." |  Example: Smith & Jones is the name of the law firm. 史密斯和琼斯是这家律师事务所的名字。 | Example:  The ampersand is often used in company names. “&”符号经常用在公司名称中。 | Synonym: and | Antonym: (No direct antonym for a symbol) | noun
