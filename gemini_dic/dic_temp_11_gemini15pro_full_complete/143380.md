outfencing: /ˌaʊtˈfɛnsɪŋ/
verb
胜过击剑; 在击剑中胜过
Surpassing someone in fencing; defeating someone in a fencing competition.
He outfenced his opponent in the final match. 他在决赛中胜过了他的对手。
She outfenced all the other competitors and won the gold medal. 她击败了所有其他参赛者并赢得了金牌。
近义词: outmaneuver, defeat, conquer
反义词: lose to, be defeated by
词性: verb

outfencing: /ˌaʊtˈfɛnsɪŋ/
noun
击剑胜过; 胜过击剑的行为
The act of surpassing someone in fencing; the act of defeating someone in a fencing competition.
His outfencing of the champion was a surprise to everyone. 他战胜冠军的行为让所有人感到惊讶。
Her outfencing secured her place on the Olympic team. 她的胜利确保了她在奥运代表队中的位置。
近义词: victory, triumph, mastery
反义词: defeat, loss, failure
词性: noun
