initiate:/ɪˈnɪʃieɪt/ /ɪˈnɪʃɪˌeɪt/| v. n.|开始，创始；启蒙；引入；接纳；提出|To begin or cause something to begin|The government initiated a new program to combat poverty. 政府启动了一项新的扶贫计划。|近义词：begin, start, commence, inaugurate, launch|反义词:terminate, end, finish, conclude, stop|词性:动词

initiate:/ɪˈnɪʃieɪt/ /ɪˈnɪʃɪˌeɪt/| n.|新加入组织的人；被传授初步知识的人|A person who has been formally introduced into a group or organization|He is an initiate of the secret society. 他是这个秘密社团的新成员。|近义词：beginner, novice, tyro|反义词:expert, veteran, professional|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *initiatus*, past participle of initiare "to initiate," from Latin initium "beginning," from in- "in" + ire "to go."

PHRASE->

initiate into:  接纳某人加入；传授初步知识
He was initiated into the fraternity last semester. 他上学期加入了兄弟会。

initiate proceedings:  提起诉讼
The lawyer initiated proceedings against the company. 律师对该公司提起了诉讼。

initiate someone in something:  使某人初步了解某事
She initiated him in the art of negotiation. 她让他初步了解了谈判的艺术。

initiate a program/plan/policy: 启动一个项目/计划/政策
The government initiated a new program to promote renewable energy. 政府启动了一项推广可再生能源的新计划。

initiate contact: 开始联系
He initiated contact with the client through email. 他通过电子邮件与客户取得了联系。
