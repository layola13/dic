detoxifies:/diːˈtɒksɪfaɪz/
verb
解毒，清除毒素
Removes poisons or toxins from something.
The liver detoxifies the blood. 肝脏净化血液。
detox, cleanse, purify
contaminate, poison
词性: 动词

verb

Removes poisons or toxins from something.

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (meaning "remove") + toxic + -ify (meaning "to make or cause to be").  The root "toxic" comes from the Latin "toxicum" meaning "poison."
