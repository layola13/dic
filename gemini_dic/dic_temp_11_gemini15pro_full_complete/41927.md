cosmologer:/kɒzˈmɒlədʒər/ | n. | 宇宙学家 | A scientist who studies the origin and development of the universe. |例句：The cosmologer presented his theory about the Big Bang.  宇宙学家介绍了他关于宇宙大爆炸的理论。

例句：Cosmologers are still debating the age of the universe. 宇宙学家仍在争论宇宙的年龄。| 近义词：astronomer, astrophysicist | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Ancient Greek  κόσμος (kósmos, “world, universe, order”) + -λογία (-logía,  “-logy”)
