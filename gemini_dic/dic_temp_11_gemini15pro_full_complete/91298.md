hydromancy:/ˈhaɪdrəmænsi/| n.|水占卜术，水系魔法|Divination by means of water, such as by observing its ripples or the forms assumed by objects thrown into it.|Ancient cultures practiced hydromancy to predict the future or communicate with spirits. 古代文化中人们用水占卜来预测未来或与神灵沟通。|近义词：water divination, dowsing|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin hydromantia, from Greek hydromanteia, from hydr- + manteia 'divination'.  hydr- comes from Greek hydōr 'water'.
