octofid:/ˈɒktəfɪd/
adjective
八裂的；分成八部分的
Divided into eight parts or lobes.
The octofid leaf of the marijuana plant is instantly recognizable. 大麻植物的八裂叶子很容易辨认。
近义词: eight-parted, eight-lobed
反义词: entire, undivided
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *octofidus*, from *octo* (“eight”) + *-fidus* (“split, cleft”), from *findō* (“to cleave, split”).
