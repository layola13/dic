osspringer: /ˈɒsˌsprɪŋər/ (UK) /ˈɑːsˌsprɪŋər/ (US) | n. | 鱼鹰 | A large fish-eating hawk (Pandion haliaetus) with long, narrow wings and a reversible outer toe, found worldwide. |  The osprey plunged into the water and emerged with a fish in its talons. 鱼鹰猛地扎进水里，爪子里抓着一条鱼。| 近义词: fish hawk | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词


词源:

From Middle English *ospriet, from Old French ospriet, from Late Latin avis praedæ (“bird of prey”).
