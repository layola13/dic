calipash:/ˈkælɪpæʃ/| n. |海龟背甲肉|The gelatinous green fat found under the carapace of certain turtles, especially the green turtle.|Calipase and calipee are prized delicacies in turtle soup.|近义词:calipee|反义词:None|词性:noun
calipee:/ˈkælɪpiː/| n. |海龟腹甲肉|The yellowish fat found under the plastron (bottom shell) of certain turtles, especially the green turtle.|Calipee is often used in turtle soup.|近义词:calipash|反义词:None|词性:noun
