misadded:/ˌmɪsˈædɪd/
verb
错误地添加；加错
Incorrectly added; added up wrong.
The cashier misadded the items and I was overcharged. 收银员加错了商品价格，我被多收了钱。
miscalculate, miscount
correctly add
