mbr:/ˈɛm biː ɑːr/| abbreviation |主引导记录 (Master Boot Record)|The MBR is a special boot sector at the very beginning of partitioned computer mass storage devices like fixed disks or removable drives intended for use with IBM PC-compatible systems and beyond.  主引导记录是分区计算机大容量存储设备（如固定磁盘或可移动驱动器）最开始的一个特殊引导扇区，用于与 IBM PC 兼容系统及其他系统一起使用。|The MBR contains the information on how the logical partitions, containing file systems, are organized on that medium. MBR 包含有关逻辑分区（包含文件系统）如何在该介质上组织的信息。|近义词:boot sector, boot record|反义词:N/A| n.
