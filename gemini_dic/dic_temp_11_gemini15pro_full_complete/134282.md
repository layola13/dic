noninhabitable:/ˌnɒnɪnˈhæbɪtəbl/ | adj. | 不可居住的 | Not suitable for living in; unfit for habitation. |  The planet Mars is currently noninhabitable for humans. 火星目前不适合人类居住。

The extreme heat made the desert noninhabitable. 极端的高温使得沙漠无法居住。 | 近义词: uninhabitable, unlivable | 反义词: habitable, livable | 词性: 形容词
Etymology:  From non- (not) + inhabitable (capable of being inhabited).  The root "habit" comes from Latin "habitare" meaning "to dwell" or "to inhabit."
