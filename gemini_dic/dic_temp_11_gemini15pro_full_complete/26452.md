byssine:/ˈbɪsaɪn/ /ˈbɪsɪn/
adjective
亚麻布的；细如亚麻的；极细的
Made of or resembling byssus; very fine or delicate.
例句：She wore a byssine robe of incredible fineness. 她穿着一件极其精细的亚麻长袍。

例句：The spider spun a byssine thread almost invisible to the naked eye.  蜘蛛吐出一根肉眼几乎看不见的细丝。
近义词：fine, delicate, silken, gossamer, diaphanous
反义词：coarse, rough, thick
词性：adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *byssinus, from Latin byssus (“fine linen, cotton”), from Ancient Greek βύσσος (bússos, “fine flax, linen”).
