generator:/ˈdʒenəreɪtər/| n.| 发电机；产生器；生成器 |A machine that converts one form of energy into another, especially mechanical energy into electrical energy; a person or thing that generates something. |The hospital has a backup generator in case of power outages.  医院备有发电机，以防停电。|synonyms: alternator, dynamo, producer, creator |antonyms: consumer, destroyer| noun

generators:/ˈdʒenəreɪtərz/| n.| 发电机；产生器；生成器（generator 的复数形式） |Plural form of generator.  |The wind farm has hundreds of generators.  风力发电场有数百台发电机。|synonyms: alternators, dynamos, producers, creators |antonyms: consumers, destroyers| noun
