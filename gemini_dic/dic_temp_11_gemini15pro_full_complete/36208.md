cogitate:/ˈkɒdʒɪteɪt/ (US) /ˈkɑːdʒɪteɪt/| v. |思考，仔细考虑，深思熟虑|To think deeply about something; to meditate or reflect.|She spent hours cogitating on the problem. 她花了几个小时思考这个问题。|I cogitated over the offer for a long time before accepting it. 我仔细考虑了很久才接受这个提议。|近义词：ponder, contemplate, meditate, reflect, deliberate, muse, ruminate|反义词：ignore, neglect, disregard|词性:动词

cogitate:/ˈkɒdʒɪteɪt/ (US) /ˈkɑːdʒɪteɪt/| n. |思考，考虑|The act of thinking deeply about something.|After much cogitation, she finally made a decision. 经过深思熟虑后，她终于做出了决定。|His cogitation on the matter led him to a new understanding. 他对这个问题的思考使他对它有了新的理解。|近义词：reflection, contemplation, deliberation, meditation|反义词：inattention, neglect|词性:名词
