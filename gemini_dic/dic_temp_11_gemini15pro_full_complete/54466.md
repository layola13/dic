disintegrations:/dɪsˌɪntɪˈɡreɪʃənz/| n. |解体，瓦解，崩解|The process of breaking apart into small pieces or fragments; the state of being disintegrated.  A decay process in which a nucleus emits one or more particles.|The disintegration of the Roman Empire was a long and complex process. 罗马帝国的解体是一个漫长而复杂的过程。|Synonyms: decomposition, decay, breakdown, fragmentation|Antonyms: integration, unification, consolidation|noun

disintegration:/dɪsˌɪntɪˈɡreɪʃən/| n. |衰变|A radioactive decay process.|The disintegration of uranium produces radon gas. 铀的衰变会产生氡气。|Synonyms: decay, decomposition|Antonyms: formation, synthesis|noun
