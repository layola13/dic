mysticete:/ˈmɪstɪˌsiːt/| n. |须鲸|A whale of the suborder Mysticeti, characterized by having baleen plates in the mouth for straining plankton from the water; a baleen whale.|例句：Humpback whales are mysticetes and feed on krill. 座头鲸是须鲸，以磷虾为食。

例句：Mysticetes are known for their immense size. 须鲸以其巨大的体型而闻名。|近义词：baleen whale|反义词：odontocete (齿鲸)|词性：名词
