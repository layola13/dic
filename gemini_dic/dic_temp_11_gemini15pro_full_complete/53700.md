disclaiming:/dɪsˈkleɪmɪŋ/
|v.|否认；放弃；拒绝承认|The company disclaimed all responsibility for the accident.  该公司否认对这起事故负有任何责任。|
| |The advertisement disclaimed any intention to mislead consumers.  该广告声明无意误导消费者。|
|Synonym:|denying, rejecting, renouncing|
|Antonym:|claiming, admitting, accepting|
|Verb (present participle)|


disclaimer:/dɪsˈkleɪmər/
|n.|免责声明；否认；放弃|The website included a disclaimer about the accuracy of its content. 该网站包含关于其内容准确性的免责声明。|
