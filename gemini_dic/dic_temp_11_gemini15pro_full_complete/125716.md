municipalizer:/mjuːˌnɪsɪpəlaɪzər/
n.
市政化支持者，主张市政化的人
A person who advocates for or supports municipalization.
The municipalizers argued that the private water company was exploiting the citizens.  市政化支持者认为，私营供水公司正在剥削市民。
The group of municipalizers successfully lobbied the city council to take control of the bus system.  这群市政化支持者成功地游说了市议会控制公交系统。
Synonyms: nationalizer, socialist
Antonyms: privatizer, capitalist
noun
