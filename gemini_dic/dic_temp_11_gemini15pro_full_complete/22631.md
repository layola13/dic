bops:/bɑːps/ | n. | 热门歌曲，动听的歌曲 | Popular or catchy songs. |  That playlist is full of bops!  那个播放列表里全是好听的歌！| synonyms: hits, tunes, jams | antonyms: flops | noun

bops:/bɑːps/ | v. (third-person singular simple present bops, present participle bopping, simple past and past participle bopped) |  轻快地跳舞，头部或身体随着音乐节奏摆动 | To dance or move your head or body rhythmically, often to pop music.  | She was bopping along to the music on the radio. 她跟着收音机里的音乐轻轻摇摆。 | synonyms:  grooving, swaying, nodding | antonyms: sitting still | verb
