ottomanization:/ˌɒtəmənɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.|奥斯曼化|The process of making something or someone Ottoman, or of becoming Ottoman in character.  This typically refers to the cultural, political, and religious influence of the Ottoman Empire on the peoples and territories under its control.|The ottomanization of the Balkans was a gradual process spanning centuries.|Synonym:  Islamization (in some contexts), Turkification|Antonym:  Westernization, Europeanization|noun

ottomanize:/ˈɒtəmənʌɪz/ |v. |奥斯曼化|To make something or someone Ottoman, or to become Ottoman in character. This typically refers to the cultural, political, and religious influence of the Ottoman Empire on the peoples and territories under its control.|The Ottoman Empire attempted to ottomanize the conquered populations through various policies.|Synonym: Islamize (in some contexts), Turkify|Antonym: Westernize, Europeanize|verb
