godforsakenly:/ˌɡɒd.fəˈseɪ.kən.li/ | adv. | 荒凉地，凄凉地，被上帝遗弃地 | In a way that suggests a place is desolate, remote, and unpleasant, as if abandoned by God. | The wind howled godforsakenly across the desolate moor. 风凄厉地呼啸着掠过荒凉的旷野。|  The small town felt godforsakenly empty after the factory closed. 工厂关闭后，小镇显得异常空旷和凄凉。 |Synonyms: desolately, bleakly, forlornly, wretchedly | Antonyms: blessedly, pleasantly, cheerfully | Adverb
