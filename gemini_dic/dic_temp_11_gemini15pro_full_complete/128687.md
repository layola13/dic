nidamental:/naɪˈdæməntəl/
adjective
产卵的；与鸟巢有关的
Relating to a bird's nest or the materials used to build it; formed or used in making a nest.
The nidamental glands of some birds secrete a sticky substance used to bind the nest together.  一些鸟类的产卵腺分泌一种粘性物质，用来将巢穴粘合在一起。
synonyms: nest-building, nesting
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, consider terms like "non-nesting" or referring to species that don't build nests)
词性: adjective
