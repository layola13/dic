flightiness:/ˈflaɪtɪnəs/
n.
轻浮；反复无常；轻率
The quality or state of being flighty; capriciousness.
Her flightiness made it difficult to have a serious conversation with her. 她的轻浮使得很难和她进行严肃的谈话。
近义词：fickleness, capriciousness, volatility, levity
反义词：steadiness, seriousness, stability, constancy
词性: 名词


flighty:/ˈflaɪti/
adj.
轻浮的；反复无常的；轻率的
Given to capricious or frivolous behavior.
He was too flighty to hold down a steady job. 他太轻浮了，无法保住一份稳定的工作。
近义词：fickle, capricious, volatile, frivolous
反义词：steady, serious, stable, constant
词性: 形容词


词源: From flight + -y + -ness.  Related to fly (verb).  The original sense referred to literal flight, like that of a bird.  Over time, the word evolved to describe erratic and unpredictable behavior, akin to the seemingly random flight of a bird.
