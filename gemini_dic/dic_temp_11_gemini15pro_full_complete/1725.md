acquaintanceship:/əˈkweɪntənsʃɪp/
n.
相识；认识；了解；交情
A state of being acquainted; the fact of knowing someone slightly.
例句：I renewed my acquaintanceship with him after the meeting.  会议之后，我重新与他建立了联系。

例句：She struck up an acquaintanceship with a fellow artist. 她和一位艺术家同行结识了。

近义词：familiarity, association, fellowship, friendship
反义词：strangeness, unfamiliarity
