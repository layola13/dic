circumventing:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈvɛntɪŋ/
verb
规避，绕过
avoiding something, especially a rule or restriction, in a clever or perhaps dishonest way
例句：They found a way of circumventing the regulations. 他们找到了规避规章制度的办法。

例句：He circumvented the problem by using a different approach. 他通过使用不同的方法绕过了这个问题。

近义词：avoiding, bypassing, dodging, evading, sidestepping
反义词：confronting, facing, tackling
词性：v.
