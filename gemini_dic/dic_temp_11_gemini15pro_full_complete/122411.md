mnemotechny:/ˌnɛməˈtɛkni/ | n. | 记忆术; 记忆法 |  The art or technique of improving or developing the memory.  It involves using systems and strategies to help remember information more effectively. |  He used mnemotechny to memorize long lists of numbers. 他用记忆术来记忆长串的数字。 | synonyms: mnemonics, memory aid, memory system | antonyms: forgetfulness, amnesia | noun
