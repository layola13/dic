overchafing:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃeɪfɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度摩擦; 擦伤
Excessive rubbing that causes irritation or sores.
The tight harness was overchafing the horse's skin.  这匹马的皮肤被过紧的马具擦伤了。
The hiker's new boots were overchafing his heels.  徒步旅行者的新靴子把他的脚后跟擦伤了。
近义词: chafing, rubbing, abrading, irritating
反义词: soothing, protecting
词性: 动词 (present participle)


overchafe:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃeɪf/
verb
过度摩擦; 擦伤
To rub excessively, causing irritation or sores.
The rough fabric overchafed her delicate skin. 粗糙的布料擦伤了她娇嫩的皮肤。
His collar overchafed his neck on the hot day.  在大热天，他的衣领擦破了他的脖子。
近义词: chafe, rub, abrade, irritate
反义词: soothe, protect
词性: 动词

词源：
"over-" (prefix) + "chafe" (verb). "Over-" indicates excess or too much. "Chafe" comes from the Old French "chaufer" meaning "to warm, heat."  The meaning shifted to "rub to warm" and then to "irritate by rubbing."
