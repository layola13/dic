acclimate:/əˈklaɪmət/ (US) /ˈækɪˌmeɪt/ (UK)| v. | 使适应，使服水土；适应|To adapt or become accustomed to a new climate, environment, or situation.|例句：The new students acclimated quickly to college life.  新生们很快适应了大学生活。

例句：It took him a while to acclimate to the high altitude.  他花了一段时间才适应了高海拔。|近义词：adapt, adjust, accustom|反义词: unaccustomed|词性:动词

acclimate:/əˈklaɪmət/ (US) /ˈækɪˌmeɪt/ (UK)| n. | 适应，服水土|The process of adapting or becoming accustomed to a new climate, environment, or situation.|例句：The acclimate of the new employees is important for their productivity. 新员工的适应对于他们的生产力很重要。

例句：The plant's acclimate to the desert climate was surprisingly fast.  这种植物对沙漠气候的适应速度快得惊人。|近义词：adaptation, adjustment, acclimatization|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
