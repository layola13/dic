interparliamentary:/ˌɪntərˌpɑːrləˈmentəri/| adj.|议会间的，各国议会间的|Existing or carried on between parliaments of different countries.|例句：The interparliamentary union promotes cooperation between legislatures. 各国议会联盟促进立法机构之间的合作。

例句：Interparliamentary discussions are crucial for international relations. 议会间讨论对国际关系至关重要。|近义词：international, multinational|反义词: national, domestic|词性:形容词

interparliament:/ˌɪntərˈpɑːrləmənt/| n.|各国议会联盟|An organization or association of parliaments from different countries.|例句：The Interparliament established in 1889. 各国议会联盟成立于1889年。

例句：Representatives from various nations attended the Interparliament meeting. 来自各国的代表出席了各国议会联盟会议。|近义词：parliamentary union, international assembly|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "national legislature") |词性:名词
