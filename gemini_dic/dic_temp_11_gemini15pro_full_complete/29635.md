caterer:/ˈkeɪtərər/| n.|承办酒席的人；提供饮食及服务的人；|A person or company that provides food and services for parties, weddings, etc.|例句：We hired a caterer for the wedding reception. 我们为婚礼招待会聘请了一位餐饮服务商。

例句：The caterer provided a delicious buffet.  餐饮服务商提供了一顿美味的自助餐。

近义词：food provider, caterer service, supplier|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "cater," which comes from the Middle English "catour," meaning "buyer of provisions."  Ultimately derived from the Old North French "acater," meaning "to buy."
