displeases:/dɪsˈpliːzɪz/
verb
使不悦，使生气，惹恼
to cause someone to feel annoyed or unhappy
His rudeness displeases me. 他的粗鲁使我不悦。
近义词:annoys, irritates, offends, bothers
反义词:pleases, delights, satisfies
词性:verb

displeased:/dɪsˈpliːzd/
adjective
不高兴的，不悦的，生气的
feeling annoyed or unhappy
She was displeased with his behavior. 她对他的行为感到不高兴。
近义词:unhappy, dissatisfied, annoyed, irritated
反义词:pleased, happy, satisfied, content
词性:adjective


词源:
From Middle English *displesen, from Old French desplaisir, from des- (expressing negation) + plaisir (“to please”).
