outrebounded:/ˌaʊtˈriːbaʊndɪd/
verb
超过…的篮板球数
To gather more rebounds than (another player or team) in a basketball game.
The Lakers outrebounded the Celtics by a margin of 10.  湖人队的篮板球比凯尔特人队多10个。
近义词: outplayed, dominated (in rebounding)
反义词:  was outrebounded by
词性: verb (past tense)
