loamy:/ˈlomi/ | adj. | 壤土的；肥沃的 | Consisting of or resembling loam; rich in organic matter. | The loamy soil is ideal for growing vegetables.  壤土非常适合种植蔬菜。| synonyms: fertile, rich, productive | antonyms: barren, infertile, sterile | adjective
