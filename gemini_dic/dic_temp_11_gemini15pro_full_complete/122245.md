miszoning:/ˌmɪsˈzoʊnɪŋ/
n.
错误分区；分区不当
The act of zoning a piece of land incorrectly, typically resulting in it being designated for a use that is incompatible with its surroundings.
The miszoning of the land led to the construction of a factory in a residential area.  土地的错误分区导致在居民区建造了一家工厂。
misapplication, misuse, abuse
correct zoning, appropriate zoning
noun
