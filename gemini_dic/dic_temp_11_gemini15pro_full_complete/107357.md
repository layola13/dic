lamellibranch:/ləˌmɛlɪˈbræŋk/ | n. |  瓣鳃纲动物；双壳类软体动物 | An aquatic mollusk of the class Lamellibranchia, having a shell of two hinged valves, such as a clam, oyster, or mussel.  These animals are filter feeders, using their gills to strain plankton and other small organisms from the water. | The oyster is a common lamellibranch. 牡蛎是一种常见的瓣鳃纲动物。| 近义词: bivalve, pelecypod | 反义词:  (无明确反义词，可考虑其他动物门类如gastropod 腹足纲) | 词性: noun

lamellibranchiate:/ləˌmɛlɪˈbræŋkiət/ | adj. | 瓣鳃纲的；双壳类的 | Having or relating to gills in the form of lamellae, as in bivalve mollusks. | Lamellibranchiate mollusks are important members of many aquatic ecosystems. 瓣鳃纲软体动物是许多水生生态系统的重要成员。| 近义词: bivalve, pelecypod | 反义词: (无明确反义词) | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *lamella* (thin plate) + Greek *branchia* (gills).
