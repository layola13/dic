adlegation: /ˌædliˈɡeɪʃən/
n.
证明；断言; 宣称
The act of affirming or asserting something.
He offered no adlegation to support his claim. 他没有提供任何证据来支持他的说法。

近义词: affirmation, assertion, declaration, statement
反义词: denial, refutation, contradiction

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *adlegatio, from Latin adlegare (“to send as an ambassador; to allege”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + legare (“to send with a commission, to depute, to bequeath”).
