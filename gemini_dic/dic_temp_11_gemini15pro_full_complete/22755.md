borrel:/ˈbɒrəl/ (UK), /ˈbɔːrəl/ (US)| n.|小吃，零食；(尤指荷兰的)非正式聚会，酒会|A social gathering, typically Dutch, involving drinks and snacks.|例句：We're having a borrel at my place after work. 下班后我们在我家小聚一下。

例句：They served some bitterballen and other borrelhapjes at the borrel.  他们在酒会上提供了一些炸肉丸和其他小吃。

近义词：get-together, gathering, party, social, drinks|反义词:|词性:noun
