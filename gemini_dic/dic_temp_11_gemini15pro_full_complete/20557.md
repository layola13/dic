blackball:/ˈblækˌbɔːl/ (US), /ˈblækˌbɔːl/ (UK)| n. v. |排斥；投反对票；黑球；反对票|To ostracize or exclude someone, typically by a secret vote using black balls; a negative vote.|例句：He was blackballed from the club for his controversial views.  他因为有争议的观点被俱乐部拒之门外。|近义词：ostracize, exclude, reject, veto, blacklist|反义词：accept, admit, welcome, approve|词性：名词，动词
