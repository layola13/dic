nonannexable:/ˌnɒnəˈnɛksəbl/ | adj. | 不可兼并的，不能被吞并的 | Not capable of being annexed.  Something that is nonannexable cannot be incorporated into another territory or political entity. |  The small island nation declared itself nonannexable.  这个小岛国宣布自己不能被吞并。

The treaty stipulated that certain territories were nonannexable. 该条约规定某些领土不可兼并。 | 近义词:  inappropriable, unannexable | 反义词: annexable | 词性: adjective
