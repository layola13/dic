antipasti:/ˌæntiˈpɑːsti/ | n. | 开胃菜，前菜 | A small dish of food served before the main course of a meal, typically consisting of olives, cheeses, cured meats, etc. |  We started our meal with a delicious platter of antipasti. 我们以一盘美味的开胃菜开始了用餐。| synonyms: appetizer, starter, hors d'oeuvres | antonyms: dessert, main course | noun

antipasto:/ˌæntiˈpɑːstoʊ/ | n. | 开胃菜，前菜 |  (singular form of antipasti) A small dish of food served before the main course of a meal. | The antipasto was a simple salad with fresh mozzarella.  这道开胃菜是一份简单的沙拉，配有新鲜的马苏里拉奶酪。 | synonyms: appetizer, starter, hors d'oeuvres | antonyms: dessert, main course | noun
