courtlike:/ˈkɔːrtlaɪk/
adj.
像法庭的；庄重的；正式的
Resembling or characteristic of a court of law; formal and dignified.
The meeting had a very courtlike atmosphere.  这次会议的气氛非常正式，像法庭一样。
Her demeanor was courtlike and reserved. 她的举止庄重而矜持。
近义词:formal, dignified, ceremonious, solemn
反义词:informal, casual, relaxed
