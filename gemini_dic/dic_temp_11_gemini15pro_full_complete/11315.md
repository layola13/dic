arduousness:/ˈɑːrdʒuəsnəs/
n.
艰巨性；费力；艰难
The quality of being arduous; difficulty.
Climbing Mount Everest is an undertaking of great arduousness. 攀登珠穆朗玛峰是一项极其艰巨的任务。
The arduousness of the journey tested their endurance. 旅途的艰辛考验了他们的耐力。

近义词: difficulty, hardship, laboriousness, strenuousness, toil
反义词: ease, facility, simplicity
