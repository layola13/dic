nondirigibility:/ˌnɒndɪˌrɪdʒɪˈbɪlɪti/
noun
不可驾驶性；不适于飞艇驾驶
The quality or state of being nondirigible; inability to be steered or controlled in flight.
The airship's nondirigibility made it a hazard to navigation. 这艘飞艇的不可驾驶性使其成为航行的危险因素。
non-steerability, unmaneuverability
dirigibility, controllability
noun
