erysipelas:/ˌɛrɪˈsɪpələs/ /ˌɪrɪˈsɪpələs/| n. |丹毒|An acute streptococcal infection of the skin and subcutaneous tissue, characterized by localized inflammation and fever.|例句：The doctor diagnosed her skin condition as erysipelas. 医生诊断她的皮肤病为丹毒。

例句：Erysipelas can be a serious infection if left untreated. 如果不及时治疗，丹毒可能会很严重。|近义词：St. Anthony's fire, cellulitis|反义词: None (There is no direct antonym for a specific disease)|词性: noun
