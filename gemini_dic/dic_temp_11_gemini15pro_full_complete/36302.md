cohesive:/koʊˈhiːsɪv/ /kəʊˈhiːsɪv/| adj.|有粘性的；有凝聚力的；团结的|Sticking together; unified.|例句：The team demonstrated a cohesive spirit throughout the tournament.  这支队伍在整个锦标赛中展现了团结的精神。

例句：A cohesive narrative is essential for a successful novel.  一个有凝聚力的叙事对于一部成功的小说是至关重要的。|近义词：united, connected, coherent, consistent|反义词:fragmented, disjointed, divided, incoherent|词性:形容词
词源:Late 16th century: from late Latin cohaesivus, from cohaerere ‘stick together’ (see cohere).
