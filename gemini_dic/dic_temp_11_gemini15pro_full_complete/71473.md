flaming:/ˈfleɪmɪŋ/| adj.|燃烧的，炽热的，鲜艳的，激烈的|Something that is flaming is burning with bright flames. It can also be used figuratively to describe something that is very bright, intense, or passionate.  It can also be used as a strong insult.|The building was flaming. 那栋建筑正在燃烧。|Synonyms: burning, blazing, fiery, incandescent, vibrant, intense, passionate|Antonyms: extinguished, dull, subdued|adjective

flaming:/ˈfleɪmɪŋ/| adv.|极其地，非常地|Used as an intensifier, especially as a strong insult.|That's a flaming lie! 那是弥天大谎！|Synonyms: extremely, exceedingly, incredibly|Antonyms: slightly, moderately|adverb
