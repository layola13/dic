averting:/əˈvɜːrtɪŋ/
verb
避免，防止
preventing something bad from happening
例句：The company took steps to avert a financial crisis. 公司采取措施避免了金融危机。

例句：He swerved to avert an accident. 他突然转向避免了一场事故。

近义词：preventing, avoiding, precluding, obviating
反义词：causing, provoking, inciting
词性：v.

averting:/əˈvɜːrtɪŋ/
noun
避免，防止
the act of preventing something bad from happening
例句：The averting of the crisis was due to quick thinking.  危机的避免归功于快速思考。

例句：The averting of disaster was a relief to everyone.  避免了灾难让每个人都松了一口气。

近义词：prevention, avoidance
反义词：causation
词性：n. (动名词形式)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *avertere*, from *ab-* (away from) + *vertere* (to turn).
