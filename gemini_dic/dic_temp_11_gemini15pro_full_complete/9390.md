antiquely:/ænˈtɪːkwəli/
adverb
古色古香地；古老地；过时地
in an antique fashion; in a way that is old and not modern.
The room was antiquely furnished. 房间布置得古色古香。
She was dressed rather antiquely. 她穿得相当老式。
Synonyms: archaically, old-fashionedly, quaintly
Antonyms: modernly, contemporarily
词性: 副词

antiquely:/ænˈtɪːkwəli/
adverb
过时地；陈旧地
in a way that is no longer modern or fashionable.
His views on marriage are rather antiquely. 他对婚姻的看法相当过时。
Some of his ideas seem rather antiquely now. 他的一些想法现在看来相当陈旧。
Synonyms: outdatedly, obsoletely
Antonyms: currently, trendily
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From antique + -ly.  Antique ultimately derives from Latin *ante- "before" + *annus "year."
