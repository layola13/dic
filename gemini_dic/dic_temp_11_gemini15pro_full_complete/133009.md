nonenterprising:/ˌnɒnˈentəpraɪzɪŋ/
adjective
缺乏进取心的，不积极的
Lacking in enterprise or initiative; not adventurous.
He was a nonenterprising young man, content with a routine job. 他是一个缺乏进取心的年轻人，满足于一份日常工作。
The nonenterprising company failed to adapt to the changing market. 这家不思进取的公司未能适应变化的市场。
Synonyms: unambitious, unadventurous, passive, inert, indolent, lethargic
Antonyms: enterprising, ambitious, adventurous, proactive, dynamic, energetic
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + enterprising.  "Enterprising" comes from the verb "enterprise," which ultimately derives from Old French "entreprendre"  (to undertake).
