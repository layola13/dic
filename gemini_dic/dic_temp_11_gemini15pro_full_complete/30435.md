centralizes:/ˈsɛntrəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
集中；中央集权
to bring something under the control of one authority, or to come under the control of one authority.
The company centralized its operations in New York.  这家公司将其业务集中在纽约。
New powers are being centralized in the hands of the President. 新的权力正集中到总统手中。

centralize:/ˈsɛntrəˌlaɪz/
verb
集中；中央集权
to bring something under the control of one authority, or to come under the control of one authority.
The government centralized the education system. 政府将教育系统集中起来。
The company decided to centralize its distribution network. 公司决定将其分销网络集中起来。
近义词: consolidate, merge, unify, concentrate
反义词: decentralize, distribute, disperse
词性: verb
