antiplaque:/ˌæntiˈplæk/| adj.|抗菌斑的，防牙菌斑的|Preventing the formation of dental plaque.  It refers to substances or treatments designed to inhibit or remove plaque buildup on teeth.|This antiplaque mouthwash helps to prevent gingivitis. 这种抗菌斑漱口水有助于预防牙龈炎。|Synonyms:  antibacterial, antimicrobial|Antonyms:  plaque-forming|adjective
