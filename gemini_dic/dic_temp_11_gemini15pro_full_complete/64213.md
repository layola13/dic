equilateral:/ˌiːkwɪˈlætərəl/
adjective
等边的；等面的
Having all sides equal.
An equilateral triangle has three sides of equal length.  一个等边三角形有三条等长的边。
The crystal structure is equilateral.  这种晶体结构是等边的。
equiangular, isosceles
scalene
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *aequilateralis, from aequus (equal) + latus, later- (side).
