excelling:/ɪkˈsɛlɪŋ/
verb
擅长；胜过；突出
very good at something
例句：She is excelling in her studies. 她学习很优秀。

例句：He is excelling at sports. 他擅长运动。

近义词：surpassing, exceeding, outperforming, flourishing, thriving
反义词：failing, underperforming, struggling

词性: Verb (present participle of excel)

词源: From Middle English *excellen*, from Old French *exceller*, from Latin *excellere* (“to surpass, excel, be eminent”), present active infinitive of *excello*, from *ex- + cello* (“to rise, tower, be high”).
