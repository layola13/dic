insociably:/ɪnˈsoʊʃəbli/
adverb
不善交际地，孤僻地
Not in a sociable manner; unsociably.  Avoiding social interaction; reserved or aloof.
He lived insociably, rarely leaving his apartment. 他过着孤僻的生活，很少离开他的公寓。
She behaved insociably at the party, standing alone in a corner.  她在派对上表现得很孤僻，独自站在角落里。
近义词:unsociably, aloofly, reclusively, solitarily
反义词:sociably, gregariously, affably
词性:adverb

insociable:/ɪnˈsoʊʃəbl/
adjective
不善交际的，孤僻的
Not sociable; not inclined to seek or enjoy the company of others.
He was an insociable man who preferred his own company. 他是一个不善交际的人，喜欢独处。
The insociable child struggled to make friends.  这个孤僻的孩子很难交到朋友。
近义词:unsociable, aloof, reserved, withdrawn, reclusive
反义词:sociable, gregarious, outgoing, affable
词性:adjective

词源:
From in- (not) + sociable (from Late Latin sociabilis, from sociare "to associate," from socius "companion").
