expansibility:/ɪkˌspændsəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 膨胀性，扩展性 | The capacity of something to be expanded or increased in size or scope. |  The expansibility of the gas makes it suitable for use in balloons.  这种气体的膨胀性使其适合用于气球。 | extensibility, flexibility, elasticity | inflexibility, rigidity | noun

expansible:/ɪkˈspændsəbl/ | adj. | 可膨胀的，可扩展的 |  Capable of being expanded or increased in size or scope. | This metal is expansible when heated. 这种金属加热后会膨胀。 | expandable, extendable, flexible | inflexible, rigid | adjective
