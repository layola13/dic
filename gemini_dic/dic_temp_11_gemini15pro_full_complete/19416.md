bichromatize:/baɪˈkrəʊmətaɪz/| v. |双色化，用两种颜色染色|To color something with two colors. |The artist bichromatized the sculpture using black and gold. 艺术家用黑色和金色对雕塑进行了双色化处理。|The laboratory bichromatized the tissue samples for easier microscopic analysis. 实验室对组织样本进行双色化处理以便于显微镜分析。|近义词:dicolour, two-tone, two-color|反义词:monochromatize, polychromatize|词性:动词

bichromatization:/baɪˌkrəʊmətaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |双色化，用两种颜色染色的过程|The act or process of coloring something with two colors. |The bichromatization of the cells helped researchers identify different structures.  细胞的双色化帮助研究人员识别不同的结构。|The artist's bichromatization technique involved layering two distinct pigments. 艺术家的双色化技术涉及分层两种不同的颜料。|近义词:bicoloration|反义词:monochromatization, polychromatization|词性:名词

词源: bi- (two) + chromatize (to color)  来自希腊语 "khrōma" (color)
