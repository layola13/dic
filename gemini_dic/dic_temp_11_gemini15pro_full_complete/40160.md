contender:/kənˈtɛndər/| n.|竞争者，角逐者|A person or thing that competes for something.|例句：He is a strong contender for the championship. 他是冠军的有力竞争者。|例句：The company is a leading contender in the global market.  这家公司是全球市场的领先竞争者。|近义词：rival, competitor, candidate, applicant|反义词：non-contender, underdog|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "contend" (to strive in rivalry; to compete), which comes from the Latin "contendere" (to stretch out, strive, contend), from "com-" (together) + "tendere" (to stretch).
