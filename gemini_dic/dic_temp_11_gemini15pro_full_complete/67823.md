eyewitnessed:/ˌaɪˈwɪtnɪst/| v.|亲眼目睹，目击|To have seen something happen with one's own eyes.|She eyewitnessed the accident. 她亲眼目睹了这起事故。|近义词：saw, observed, witnessed|反义词：missed|词性:动词

词源:  From "eye" + "witness".  "Eye" meaning the organ of sight, and "witness" meaning to see an event and be able to give an account of it.
