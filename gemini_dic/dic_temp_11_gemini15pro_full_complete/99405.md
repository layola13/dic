interdisciplinary:/ˌɪntərˈdɪsəplɪneri/
| adj. |跨学科的，交叉学科的|
Involving two or more academic disciplines.|
例句：The university offers an interdisciplinary program in environmental studies. 这所大学提供环境研究的跨学科课程。

例句：Interdisciplinary research is becoming increasingly important in addressing complex problems. 跨学科研究在解决复杂问题方面变得越来越重要。

近义词：cross-disciplinary, multidisciplinary|
反义词：specialized, disciplinary|
词性：adjective

词源：
From inter- + disciplinary.  "Inter-" comes from Latin, meaning "between" or "among". "Disciplinary" relates to a branch of knowledge.  Thus, "interdisciplinary" refers to something that is between or among different branches of knowledge.
