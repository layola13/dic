bara:/ˈbɑːrə/ | n. | 蔷薇; 酒吧;  | A thorny shrub or vine of the genus Rosa, with showy flowers. A place where alcoholic drinks are served commercially. |  The bara bloomed beautifully in the spring. 春天蔷薇开得很漂亮。  He went to the bara for a drink after work. 他下班后去酒吧喝了一杯。 | 近义词: rose (for the flower), pub, tavern (for the place) | 反义词: None | 词性: 名词


词源:
From Latin *bara, from Greek βάρον (báron), possibly of Semitic origin.  The meaning "pub" is likely a shortening of "baraquita" (small bar), also from Spanish.
