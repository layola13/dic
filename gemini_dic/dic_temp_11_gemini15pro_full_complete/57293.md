downsides:/ˈdaʊnsaɪdz/
noun
缺点，不利方面
the negative aspects of something
The downside of living in a city is the high cost of living.  住在城市里的缺点是生活成本高。
synonyms: disadvantages, drawbacks, cons
antonyms: advantages, upsides, pros
n.
