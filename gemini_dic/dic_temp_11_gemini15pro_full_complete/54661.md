disobeying:/ˌdɪsəˈbeɪɪŋ/ | v. |不服从，违反|The act of not following rules or commands. |例句：He was punished for disobeying a direct order. 他因违抗直接命令而受到惩罚。

例句：Disobeying the law can have serious consequences. 不遵守法律可能会造成严重后果。

近义词：defying, ignoring, resisting, violating|反义词：obeying, complying, following, heeding|词性：动词

词源：From dis- + obeying, from obey + -ing.  "Dis-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of". "Obey" comes from Old French *obeir, ultimately from Latin oboedire "to obey," from ob- "toward" + audire "to hear."
