microbrewers:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌbruːərz/ | n. | 小型酿酒商；精酿啤酒商 | People who own or operate a microbrewery, a small brewery that produces a limited amount of beer. |  Many microbrewers focus on creating unique and flavorful craft beers. 许多小型酿酒商专注于创造独特而美味的精酿啤酒。 | Synonyms: craft brewers, independent brewers | Antonyms: macrobrewers, large-scale brewers | Noun

microbrewery:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌbruːəri/ | n. | 小型酿酒厂；精酿啤酒厂 | A small brewery that produces a limited amount of beer. | The town boasts a popular microbrewery that attracts tourists. 该镇拥有一家颇受欢迎的小型酿酒厂，吸引了许多游客。| Synonyms: craft brewery, independent brewery | Antonyms: macrobrewery, large-scale brewery | Noun
