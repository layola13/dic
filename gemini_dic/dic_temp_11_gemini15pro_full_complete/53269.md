dirige:/dɪˈriːʒeɪ/ /dəˈriːʒeɪ/
verb
指挥，指导
to conduct or direct (an orchestra, chorus, etc.)
The renowned conductor will dirige the symphony orchestra in a performance of Beethoven's Fifth Symphony.  这位著名的指挥家将指挥交响乐团演奏贝多芬的第五交响曲。

He will dirige the choir in singing the national anthem. 他将指挥合唱团演唱国歌。


近义词: conduct, lead, direct, manage, orchestrate
反义词: follow
词性: verb (动词)
