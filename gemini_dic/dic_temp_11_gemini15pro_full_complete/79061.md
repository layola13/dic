giveth:/ˈɡɪvɪθ/
v.
给予，提供
Archaic third-person singular present indicative form of give.  It is an older form of the verb "give" used with the third-person singular subject (he, she, it) in the present tense.
The Lord giveth and the Lord taketh away. 上帝赐予，上帝收回。
近义词：grants, bestows, offers, provides
反义词：taketh, receives
词性: verb (archaic)
