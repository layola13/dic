flowk: /floʊk/
| n. |人群，群众；鸟群，畜群|A large number of people or animals, especially birds or sheep, moving together.|例句：A flowk of starlings flew overhead. 一群椋鸟从头顶飞过。

例句：A flowk of tourists descended upon the small town. 一群游客涌入小镇。

|近义词：crowd, throng, flock, herd, swarm|反义词: individual, few|词性: 名词
