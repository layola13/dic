ambassage:/ˈæmbəsɪdʒ/ |n.|大使馆；使馆；大使的职位或职能|An ambassage is a mission to a foreign government, or the official residence or office of an ambassador.|The king sent an ambassage to the neighboring kingdom to negotiate a peace treaty. 国王派遣使节前往邻国谈判和平条约。|近义词：embassy, mission, legation|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

词源: From Old French *ambassaige*, from *ambassador* (see ambassador).
