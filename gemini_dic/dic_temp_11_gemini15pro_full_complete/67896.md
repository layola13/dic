facade:/fəˈsɑːd/ | n. | 外观；表面；假象 | a deceptive outward appearance; a misrepresentation. |  The building's grand facade hid the disrepair within.  这栋建筑宏伟的外观掩盖了内部的破败。| synonyms: front, exterior, veneer, mask, pretense | antonyms: interior, reality, truth | noun

facade:/fəˈsɑːd/ | n. | 正面；临街面 | the front of a building, especially an imposing or decorative one. | The facade of the cathedral was adorned with intricate carvings.  大教堂的正面装饰着复杂的雕刻。| synonyms: face, frontage | antonyms: rear, back | noun
