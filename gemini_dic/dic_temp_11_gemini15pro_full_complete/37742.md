compartmented:/kəmˈpɑːrtməntɪd/ | adj. | 分隔的，划分成各自独立部分的 | Divided into separate compartments or categories, often to isolate or restrict access or information. | The ship has a compartmented hull for safety. 这艘船的船体是分隔式的，为了安全。| Synonyms: partitioned, segmented, subdivided, categorized | Antonyms: unified, integrated, combined | adjective

compartmentalized:/kəmˌpɑːrtˈmentəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 分隔的，划分成各自独立部分的 | Divided into separate compartments or categories, often to isolate or restrict access or information.  This is often used in a more figurative sense for emotions or thoughts. | He compartmentalized his life, keeping work and personal matters strictly separate. 他将自己的生活划分开来，将工作和私事严格分开。| Synonyms: partitioned, segmented, subdivided, categorized | Antonyms: unified, integrated, combined | adjective


Etymology:  From the noun "compartment" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives). "Compartment" comes from the French "compartiment," from "compartir" (to divide, share), ultimately from Latin "compartirī" (to divide with).
