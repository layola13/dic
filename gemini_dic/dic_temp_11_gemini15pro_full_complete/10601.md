apprise: /əˈpraɪz/ | vt. | 通知，告知 | To inform or notify someone of something. | I thought it right to apprise Chris of what had happened. 我认为应该把发生的事情告诉克里斯。| synonyms: inform, notify, advise, tell, enlighten | antonyms: withhold, conceal | verb
