nonsynthesis:/ˌnɑːnˈsɪnθəsɪs/
noun
非合成，不合成
The process of not synthesizing something; lack of synthesis.  This term usually implies the absence of a combining or building process.
The nonsynthesis of certain proteins can lead to genetic disorders.  某些蛋白质的非合成会导致遗传疾病。
Near Antonyms: synthesis, combination, formation
Antonyms:  synthesis
