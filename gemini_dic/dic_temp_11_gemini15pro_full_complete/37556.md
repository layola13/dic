commonwealths: /ˈkɒmənˌwelθs/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmənˌwelθs/ (US)| n.|联邦；共和国；共同体|A self-governing autonomous state, or a group of states united by common allegiance to a crown or some central authority. |The British Commonwealth is a voluntary association of independent sovereign states. 英联邦是由独立主权国家自愿组成的协会。

The Commonwealth of Virginia is one of the original thirteen colonies. 弗吉尼亚联邦是最初的十三个殖民地之一。|近义词：federation, union, alliance|反义词：division, separation|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *comoun welthe*, equivalent to *comoun* (common) + *welthe* (wealth).  The term originally meant "public welfare; general good."
