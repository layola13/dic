correspondent:/ˌkɔːrəˈspɑːndənt/ |/ˌkɔːrəˈspɑːndənt/| n. |记者；通讯员；通信者|A person who writes letters to another person or a newspaper, especially on a regular basis; a person employed by a newspaper, a television station, etc. to report on a particular subject or send reports from a foreign country.|例句：He works as a foreign correspondent for the BBC. 他是英国广播公司的驻外记者。|例句：She's a regular correspondent for the New York Times. 她是纽约时报的常驻记者。|近义词：reporter, journalist, writer, communicator|反义词:|词性:noun
