nonanesthetic:/ˌnɑːnˌænɪsˈθɛtɪk/
adjective
非麻醉的；不用麻醉的
Not producing or related to anesthesia; not involving the loss of sensation.
The doctor performed a nonanesthetic procedure on the patient's finger. 医生对病人的手指进行了非麻醉手术。
The biopsy was nonanesthetic, causing the patient some discomfort.  活检没有麻醉，给病人带来了一些不适。
近义词: non-narcotic, non-sedative
反义词: anesthetic, narcotic
