imam:/ɪˈmɑːm/ /ɪˈmæm/| n.|伊玛目，阿訇|A Muslim prayer leader.  A title of various Muslim leaders, especially of one succeeding Muhammad as leader of Shiite Islam.|The imam led the prayers in the mosque. 伊玛目在清真寺里带领祈祷。|近义词：prayer leader, cleric, minister|反义词: (No direct opposite, perhaps layman/congregant)|noun
