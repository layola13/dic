avert:/əˈvɜːrt/| v. |避免，防止，转移|To prevent something bad from happening; to turn away (your eyes or thoughts).|例句：
The company took steps to avert a financial crisis.
公司采取措施避免了金融危机。

例句：
She averted her eyes from the gruesome sight.
她把目光从可怕的景象上移开。


|近义词：prevent, avoid, preclude, obviate, forestall|反义词:cause, create, invite, precipitate, allow|词性:动词
