bastardise:/ˈbæstərdaɪz/| vt.|使…品质降低；掺杂；贬低；歪曲|To lower the quality of something by adding inferior elements or by imitation; to corrupt or debase.|The film bastardised the original novel. 这部电影歪曲了原著小说。|Synonyms: corrupt, debase, degrade, contaminate, adulterate|Antonyms: purify, refine, enhance, improve|verb
bastardise:/ˈbæstərdaɪz/| n.|杂种；劣质品；混杂物|Something debased or corrupted; a hybrid or mixture of inferior quality.|The building was a bastardization of several architectural styles. 这座建筑是几种建筑风格的拙劣混合。|Synonyms: hybrid, corruption, degradation, adulteration|Antonyms: original, purebred, refinement|noun
