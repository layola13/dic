acne: /ˈækni/ | n. | 痤疮，粉刺 |  A skin condition characterized by red pimples on the face, back, and chest, typically affecting adolescents. | Example:  My teenager suffers from acne. 我的青少年孩子正受痤疮困扰。

Example:  Stress can worsen acne. 压力会加重痤疮。| 近义词: pimples, blemishes, zits | 反义词: clear skin | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek akmē "point, highest point," later "pimple, eruption on the skin."  The shift in meaning likely reflects the idea of pimples being raised points on the skin.
