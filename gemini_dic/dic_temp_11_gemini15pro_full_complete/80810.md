gothicnesses:/ˈɡɒθɪknɪsɪz/
n.
哥特式风格（复数）
The quality or qualities of being Gothic, especially in architecture, literature, or art.
The building has many gothicnesses, including pointed arches and stained glass windows. 这座建筑有很多哥特式风格，包括尖拱和彩色玻璃窗。
gothicness, gothic qualities, gothic features
