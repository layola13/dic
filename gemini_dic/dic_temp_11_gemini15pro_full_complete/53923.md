discoursed:/dɪˈskɔːrst/ | v. | 讲论；论述；谈话 | spoke or wrote about a subject formally and in detail | He discoursed at length on the history of the town. 他详细地讲述了这个镇的历史。|  The professor discoursed on the importance of critical thinking. 教授论述了批判性思维的重要性。 |近义词：discussed, talked, lectured, expounded | 反义词：was silent, listened | 词性：动词

discourse:/ˈdɪskɔːrs/ | n. | 论述；演讲；谈话 | written or spoken communication or debate | The discourse on climate change has become increasingly urgent. 关于气候变化的讨论变得越来越紧迫。 |  Her academic discourse was complex and challenging. 她的学术演讲复杂且富有挑战性。 |近义词：discussion, conversation, lecture, speech | 反义词：silence | 词性：名词
