like-a-shot:/ˌlaɪk ə ˈʃɒt/| adv.|立刻，马上|Very quickly; without hesitation.  This idiom suggests the speed and directness of a bullet fired from a gun.|例句：When I heard the news, I ran home like a shot. 当我听到这个消息时，我立刻跑回了家。

例句：He accepted the job offer like a shot. 他毫不犹豫地接受了这份工作邀请。|近义词：immediately, instantly, promptly, swiftly, at once, right away|反义词：slowly, gradually, hesitantly|词性: 副词
