barometrically:/ˌbærəˈmɛtrɪkli/ | adv. | 气压地，根据气压 | relating to or measured by a barometer; in a way that is influenced by atmospheric pressure |  The altitude was determined barometrically.  海拔高度是用气压计测定的。| synonym: atmospherically | antonym: N/A | adverb

barometric:/ˌbærəˈmɛtrɪk/ | adj. | 气压的 | relating to or measured by a barometer | The barometric pressure dropped significantly before the storm.  风暴来临前，气压显著下降。| synonym: atmospheric | antonym: N/A | adjective

barometer:/bəˈrɒmɪtə(r)/ | n. | 气压计；晴雨表 | an instrument measuring atmospheric pressure, used especially in forecasting the weather and determining altitude | The barometer is falling, which indicates rain is likely. 气压计正在下降，这表明很可能会下雨。| synonym: gauge, indicator | antonym: N/A | noun

词源:  From French *barométrique*, from Ancient Greek βάρος (báros, “weight”) + μέτρον (métron, “measure”).
