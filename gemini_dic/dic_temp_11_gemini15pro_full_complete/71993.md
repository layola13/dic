fling-out:/ˈflɪŋ aʊt/ | n. | 勃然大怒；突然发作；(尤指) 争吵 | A sudden angry outburst or quarrel, especially one involving leaving or being expelled from a place or situation. | She had a fling-out with her boss and quit her job. 她和老板大吵一架，然后辞掉了工作。 | Synonyms: outburst, tantrum, fit, row, argument | Antonyms: reconciliation, agreement, compromise | noun

fling-out:/ˈflɪŋ aʊt/ | v. | 突然离开；勃然离去；猛烈抨击 | To leave or be expelled suddenly and angrily; to criticize strongly and vehemently. | He flung out of the room in a rage. 他怒气冲冲地冲出了房间。| Synonyms: storm out, stomp out, lash out, denounce | Antonyms: remain, stay, accept, condone | verb
