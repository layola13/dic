consensus's:/kənˈsɛnsəsɪz/
n.
共识的，一致意见的
The possessive form of "consensus," indicating ownership or belonging.
The consensus's impact on the final decision was significant.  共识对最终决定产生了重大影响。
The committee valued the consensus's authority. 委员会重视共识的权威性。
近义词: agreement's, unanimity's, accord's
反义词: disagreement's, discord's, dissent's
词性:noun (possessive)

词源:
From Late Latin *consensus, from Latin consentiēns, consentient- ‘agreeing’, present participle of consentire, from con- ‘together’ + sentire ‘feel’.
