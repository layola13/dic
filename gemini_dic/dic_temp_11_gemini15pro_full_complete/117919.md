melting-pot:/ˈmɛltɪŋ pɒt/ (US) /ˈmɛltɪŋ pɔt/ (UK)| n. |大熔炉; 融合的地方|A place where different peoples, styles, theories, etc., are mixed together.|The United States is often called a melting pot of cultures.|Synonyms: crucible, blend, mix, amalgamation|Antonyms: segregation, division, separation|Noun
