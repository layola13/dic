anglicizes:/ˈæŋɡlɪsaɪzɪz/
verb
使英语化；英语化；改成英语
To make or become English in form or character.
The author anglicizes foreign names in her novels to make them easier for readers.  作者将小说中的外国名字英语化，以便读者更容易理解。
The immigrant anglicized his name to fit in better.  这个移民把他的名字英语化，以便更好地融入社会。
synonyms: anglify, adapt, modify
antonyms: translate, foreignize, localize
词性: 动词
