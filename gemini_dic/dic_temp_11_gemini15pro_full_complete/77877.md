genette:/dʒəˈnɛt/ | n. | Genet, 也可指叙事学家热拉尔·热奈特 | A small carnivorous mammal related to the civet.  Also refers to the literary theorist Gérard Genette, known for his work on narratology. |  The genette is a nocturnal animal.  | synonyms:  civet, oyans (for the animal) | antonyms: N/A | noun
