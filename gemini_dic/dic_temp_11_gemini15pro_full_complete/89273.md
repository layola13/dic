homocercy:/ˌhɒməˈsɜːrsi/ /ˌhoʊməˈsɜːrsi/| n. |同尾性|The condition of having a tail in which the upper and lower lobes are equal in size, as in most bony fishes.|Many modern fish exhibit homocercy.|近义词:isocercy|反义词:heterocercy|词性:noun

Etymology:  From Greek *homós* "same" + *kérkos* "tail."
