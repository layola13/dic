dialectical:/ˌdaɪəˈlɛktɪk(ə)l/
adjective
辩证的
Relating to or employing dialectic; argumentative.  Characterized by the logical discussion of ideas and opinions. Concerning the nature of logical argumentation.
Example: The dialectical method involves presenting arguments and counterarguments to arrive at a deeper understanding of a subject. 辩证法包括提出论点和反论点，以更深入地理解一个主题。

Example:  He engaged in a dialectical exchange with his professor about the merits of different philosophical approaches. 他就不同哲学方法的优缺点与教授进行了辩证的交流。

近义词: argumentative, logical, reasoned, analytical
反义词: dogmatic, uncritical, intuitive

词性: adjective
