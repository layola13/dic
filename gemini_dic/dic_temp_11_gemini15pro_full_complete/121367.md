misguide:/ˌmɪsˈɡaɪd/| v. |误导，使误入歧途|to lead someone in the wrong direction or into a mistaken belief or action. | The advertisement misguides consumers about the product's actual capabilities.  这则广告误导了消费者对产品实际功能的认知。

The map misguided us, and we ended up lost in the woods. 地图误导了我们，我们最终在树林里迷路了。|近义词：mislead, deceive, delude|反义词：guide, direct, enlighten|词性：动词
