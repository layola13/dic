compassionate: /kəmˈpæʃənət/ | adj. | 富有同情心的，表示怜悯的 | Feeling or showing sympathy and concern for others. | She was a compassionate nurse who always put her patients' needs first. 她是一位富有同情心的护士，总是把病人的需求放在第一位。 |  He gave a compassionate look. 他投以怜悯的目光。| synonyms: sympathetic, empathetic, caring, kind, humane | antonyms: uncompassionate, indifferent, callous, cruel | adjective

compassionately: /kəmˈpæʃənətli/ | adv. | 同情地，怜悯地 | In a way that shows sympathy and concern for others. | She spoke compassionately to the grieving family. 她同情地对悲痛的家人讲话。 | The judge listened compassionately to the defendant's story. 法官同情地听了被告的故事。 | synonyms: sympathetically, empathetically, kindly | antonyms: uncompassionately, indifferently, callously | adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *compassionatus, past participle of *compassionari "to feel pity," from Latin com- "with" + pati "to suffer."
