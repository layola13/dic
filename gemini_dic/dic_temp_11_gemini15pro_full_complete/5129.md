allegorist:/ˈælɪɡərɪst/ | n. | 寓言家；讽喻作家 | A writer or speaker who uses allegory. |  Bunyan is generally regarded as the greatest allegorist in English literature. 普遍认为班扬是英国文学中最伟大的寓言作家。| synonym: fabulist, moralist | antonym: literalist | noun

allegory:/ˈælɪɡəri/ | n. | 寓言；讽喻 | A story, poem, or picture that can be interpreted to reveal a hidden meaning, typically a moral or political one. | The play is an allegory of the struggle between good and evil.  这出戏是善恶斗争的寓言。| synonym: parable, fable, symbolism | antonym: literal interpretation | noun
