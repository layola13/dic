microwavable:/ˌmaɪkrəˈweɪvəbl/
adjective
可微波的
Suitable for cooking or heating in a microwave oven.
This container is microwavable and dishwasher safe.  这个容器可以用微波炉加热，也可以用洗碗机清洗。
These ready meals are microwavable for convenience. 这些即食食品可以用微波炉加热，很方便。
近义词: microwave-safe
反义词: non-microwavable
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From microwave + -able.  "Microwave" itself comes from micro- (meaning "small") + wave (referring to electromagnetic waves).
