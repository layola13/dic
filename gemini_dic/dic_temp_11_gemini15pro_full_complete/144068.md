outroll:/ˌaʊtˈroʊl/
verb
展开; 铺开; 延伸
To extend or unfurl something, often a scroll or map.
The cartographer outrolled the ancient map, revealing the lost city.  制图师展开古老的地图，露出了失落的城市。
The captain outrolled the ship's sails. 船长展开了船帆。
unroll, unfurl, spread out, extend
roll up, furl
v.
