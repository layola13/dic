meticulous:/məˈtɪkjələs/| adj.|一丝不苟的，细致的|Showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise.|She completed the task with meticulous care. 她一丝不苟地完成了任务。|例句：He's meticulous about keeping his car clean. 他对保持车子的清洁一丝不苟。

例句：The scientist conducted a meticulous experiment.  这位科学家进行了一项细致的实验。


近义词：careful, precise, thorough, painstaking, scrupulous|反义词：careless, sloppy, negligent|词性:形容词
