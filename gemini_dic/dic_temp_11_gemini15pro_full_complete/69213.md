fearfuler:/ˈfɪərfələr/ | adj. | 更害怕的，更担心的 |  A comparative form of "fearful," meaning more afraid or apprehensive. This form is considered nonstandard or archaic and "more fearful" is generally preferred. |  He seemed fearfuler than before the storm. 他似乎比暴风雨来临之前更害怕了。 (Nonstandard usage) |  more fearful, more afraid, more apprehensive | less fearful, less afraid, bolder | adjective

fearful:/ˈfɪərfəl/ | adj. | 害怕的，担心的；可怕的，令人担忧的 | Feeling or causing fear; apprehensive. Also, inspiring fear or awe; terrible. |  She was fearful of the dark. 她害怕黑暗。

The storm was a fearful sight. 这场暴风雨的景象很可怕。 | afraid, anxious, apprehensive, frightened, terrified; awful, dreadful, terrible | fearless, unafraid, bold, brave | adjective
