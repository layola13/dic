outcarry:/ˌaʊtˈkæri/
verb
胜过，优于
to surpass or exceed in carrying or conveying
The new model airplane outcarries the older one in terms of range.  这款新型飞机在航程方面胜过旧款。
This strain of wheat outcarries all others. 这种小麦的产量超过所有其他品种。
surpass, exceed, outstrip, outperform
lag behind, fall short of
v.
