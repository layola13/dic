allemande:/ˌæləˈmænd/ | n. |阿勒曼德舞曲；阿勒曼德舞步|A German dance in moderate duple time, popular in the 17th and 18th centuries, or the music for this dance. It is often the first movement of a suite.|The musicians played a stately allemande.  音乐家们演奏了一曲庄严的阿勒曼德舞曲。| 近义词: dance,courante, sarabande, gigue (as parts of a suite)|反义词: N/A|noun

allemande:/ˌæləˈmænd/ | n. |（纸牌游戏中的）阿勒曼德|A trick in card games in which one player takes all the tricks.|He won the game with a final, triumphant allemande. 他以最后一手成功的阿勒曼德赢得了比赛。| 近义词: slam (in some card games)|反义词: N/A|noun
