diminuendo:/dəˌmɪnjuˈendoʊ/ (US), /dəˌmɪnjuˈɛndoʊ/ (UK)| n. |渐弱， diminuendos (plural)|A gradual decrease in the volume of music or sound.  It is also used metaphorically to describe a gradual decrease in intensity or force of anything.|The music faded to a gentle diminuendo.  音乐逐渐减弱到柔和的渐弱。|Synonyms: decrescendo, fade-out|Antonyms: crescendo, increase|noun


diminuendo:/dəˌmɪnjuˈendoʊ/ (US), /dəˌmɪnjuˈɛndoʊ/ (UK)| adj. |渐弱的|Gradually decreasing in volume or intensity.|The diminuendo strains of the violin were hauntingly beautiful. 小提琴渐弱的旋律令人难以忘怀地美丽。|Synonyms: decrescendoing, fading|Antonyms: crescendoing, increasing|adjective
