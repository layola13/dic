accompanying:/əˈkʌmpəniɪŋ/| adj.|伴随的，陪同的|going with someone or something else|The accompanying document provides further details. 伴随的文件提供了更多细节。|近义词：attendant, associated, related,  concomitant|反义词:separate, independent|词性:形容词

accompany:/əˈkʌmpəni/| vt.|陪伴，伴奏|to go somewhere with someone|I accompanied her to the doctor. 我陪她去看医生。|近义词：escort, attend, follow|反义词:leave, abandon|词性:动词

accompaniment:/əˈkʌmpənɪmənt/| n.|伴奏，伴随物|something that goes with something else, especially music|The wine is a perfect accompaniment to the meal. 这酒是这顿饭的完美搭配。|近义词：addition, supplement, complement|反义词:none (as a noun referring to something that goes with something else)|词性:名词
