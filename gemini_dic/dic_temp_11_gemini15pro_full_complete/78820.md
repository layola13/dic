gimmal:/ˈdʒɪməl/ | adj. | 双重的，三重的 | Consisting of two or three similar parts hinged together. | The gimmal lock was intricate and difficult to pick.  这个双重锁很复杂，很难撬开。| synonyms: double, triple, twofold, threefold | antonyms: single, singular | adjective
