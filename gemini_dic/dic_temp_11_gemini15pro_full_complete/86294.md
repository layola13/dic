heavyweights:/ˈhɛviˌweɪts/| n.|重量级人物；重量级拳击手；举足轻重的人（或事物）|a person or thing that is very important or powerful in a particular area or activity|The heavyweight champion will defend his title next month. 重量级冠军将在下个月卫冕他的头衔。|synonyms:  influential person, leader, giant, powerhouse, titan|antonyms: lightweight, nobody, unimportant person|noun

heavyweight:/ˈhɛviˌweɪt/| adj.|重量级的；重要的；有影响力的|of great importance or influence|He is a heavyweight in the political arena. 他是政坛上的重量级人物。|synonyms:  important, influential, powerful, leading, prominent|antonyms: lightweight, unimportant, insignificant|adjective
