corporators:/ˈkɔːrpəreɪtərz/
n.
公司成员，法人，社团成员
Members of a corporation, especially one that is large and powerful.
The corporators voted to merge with another company. 公司成员投票决定与另一家公司合并。
The corporators of the university include alumni, faculty, and administrators. 大学的法人包括校友、教师和管理人员。
synonyms: members, shareholders, executives, officers, directors
antonyms: individuals, sole proprietors
noun
