coen:/ˈsiːən/| n.|腔肠动物|A coelenterate, especially a coral.|例句：The coen was found attached to a rock.  这种腔肠动物被发现附着在一块岩石上。

例句：Researchers are studying the coen's symbiotic relationship with algae. 研究人员正在研究这种腔肠动物与藻类的共生关系。|近义词：coral, polyp, cnidarian|反义词: None|词性:noun
