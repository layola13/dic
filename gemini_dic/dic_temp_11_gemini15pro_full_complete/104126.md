jumpweld: /ˈdʒʌmpˌwɛld/ | n. | 跳切 | An abrupt transition between two shots that gives the impression of a jump in time or space, often used to condense time or create a sense of disorientation.  | The director used a jumpweld to show the passage of several hours. 导演使用跳切来表示几个小时的流逝。 |  The jumpweld made it seem like the character teleported across the room.  这个跳切让角色看起来像瞬间移动穿过房间一样。 |  cut, edit | dissolve, fade | noun
