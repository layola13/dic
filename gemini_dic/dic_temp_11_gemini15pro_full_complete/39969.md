consubstantial:/ˌkɒnsəbˈstænʃəl/ /ˌkɑːnsəbˈstænʃəl/| adj.|同体的，同质的，同实体的|Of the same substance or essence; sharing the same nature.|The Nicene Creed states that Jesus is consubstantial with the Father. 尼西亚信经声明耶稣与圣父同体。|The two versions of the document are consubstantial, differing only in minor details. 这份文件的两个版本基本相同，只是在一些小细节上有所不同。|近义词：coessential, homoousian, identical, equivalent|反义词：different, dissimilar, heterogeneous|词性:形容词

词源:
Late Middle English: from late Latin consubstantialis, from con- ‘together with’ + substantialis (from substantia ‘substance’).
