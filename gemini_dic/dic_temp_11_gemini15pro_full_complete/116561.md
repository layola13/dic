matin:/ˈmætɪn/| n.|晨祷，晨歌; 早晨|A service of morning prayer, especially in the Anglican Church. Also refers to a song or poem appropriate to the morning. |The church held matins at 6 a.m. 教堂在早上6点举行晨祷。|近义词：morning prayer, dawn service, lauds|反义词:vespers, evensong|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *matin*, from Late Latin *matutinum* ("of the morning"), neuter of *matutinus*, from *Matuta*, Roman goddess of dawn. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\*meh₂-t-("morning")*.
