humanitarian:/hjuːˌmænɪˈtɛəriən/ | adj. n. |人道主义的；人道主义者 |Concerned with or seeking to promote human welfare. A person who seeks to promote human welfare.|例句：He was a tireless humanitarian, working to improve the lives of refugees. 他是一位不知疲倦的人道主义者，致力于改善难民的生活。

例句：The organization provides humanitarian aid to victims of natural disasters. 该组织向自然灾害的受害者提供人道主义援助。|近义词：philanthropic, compassionate, benevolent |反义词：inhumane, cruel, callous |词性：adjective, noun

Etymology: Late 18th century: from humanity + -arian.
