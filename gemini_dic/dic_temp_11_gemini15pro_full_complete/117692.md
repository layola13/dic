melado:/meˈlɑːdoʊ/ |n.| 蜜糖，糖蜜; 甘蔗渣 | A dark, viscous syrup produced during the refining of sugar. melado:/meˈlɑːdoʊ/ |adj.| 蜜糖色的，糖蜜状的 | Having the color or consistency of melado. | The cake had a rich, melado flavor.  这蛋糕有着浓郁的蜜糖味。| Synonyms:  molasses-like, treacle-like, syrupy | Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, perhaps "thin," "watery" for consistency) | adjective
