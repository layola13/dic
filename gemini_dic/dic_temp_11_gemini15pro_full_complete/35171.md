clown:/klaʊn/ | v. | 扮小丑；胡闹 | To act like a clown; behave in a silly or foolish way. | Stop clowning around and get to work! 别再胡闹了，快去工作！

He was clowning about with his friends. 他和他的朋友们在一起嬉闹。 | 近义词：fool around, mess about, joke | 反义词: behave seriously | 词性: 动词


词源: From Middle English *clowne, from a Scandinavian source akin to Icelandic klunni (“clumsy fellow, boor”), ultimately from Proto-Germanic *klun- (“lump, log”).
