milfoil: /ˈmɪlˌfɔɪl/ | n. |  蓍草；欧亚蓍草；西洋蓍草 | A Eurasian plant (Achillea millefolium) of the composite family, having finely dissected leaves and small white flowers. It is naturalized in North America and is sometimes considered a weed. Also called yarrow. |  The milfoil choked the stream. 蓍草阻塞了溪流。 | synonyms: yarrow, Achillea millefolium | antonyms: N/A | noun
