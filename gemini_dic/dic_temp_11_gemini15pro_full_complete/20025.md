biomechanics:/ˌbaɪoʊməˈkænɪks/| n.|生物力学|The study of the mechanical laws relating to the movement or structure of living organisms.|例句：Biomechanics plays a crucial role in sports science. 生物力学在运动科学中起着至关重要的作用。
