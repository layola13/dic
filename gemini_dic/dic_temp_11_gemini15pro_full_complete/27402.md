cameo:/ˈkæmioʊ/ (US) /ˈkæmiːəʊ/ (UK)| n.| 客串；（尤指名演员在电影中的）短暂露面；浮雕宝石 |A brief appearance of a well-known person in a film, television program, etc., especially as a minor character; a small piece of jewelry, such as a brooch, with a carved design in relief.|例句：The film had a cameo appearance by the director.  这部电影有导演的客串。

例句：She wears a cameo brooch on her lapel. 她胸前戴着一枚浮雕胸针。|近义词：appearance, bit, guest appearance|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
