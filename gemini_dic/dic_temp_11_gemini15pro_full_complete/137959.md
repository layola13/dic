nonwithdrawable:/ˌnɒnwɪðˈdrɔːəbl/ | adj. | 不可取出的，不能提取的 | Not able to be withdrawn or taken back. |  Example: The money placed in this account is nonwithdrawable for five years.  这笔存入该账户的资金五年内不可提取。

Example:  The contract contains a nonwithdrawable clause.  合同中包含不可撤销条款。 | Synonyms: non-refundable, unredeemable, irreversible | Antonyms: withdrawable, redeemable, refundable | word property: Adjective
