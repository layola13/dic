harmoniums:/hɑːrˈmoʊniəmz/
n.
簧风琴（复数）
a keyboard instrument in which the sound is produced by forcing air through metal reeds.
The church service was accompanied by the gentle sounds of the harmoniums. 教堂礼拜伴随着簧风琴柔和的声音。
synonyms:reed organ, melodeon
antonyms: (No direct antonym for a musical instrument.  Could consider contrasting instruments like piano, organ)
