chastely:/ˈtʃeɪstli/| adv.|贞洁地，纯洁地，朴实地|In a pure and virtuous manner; without any sexual impropriety.|She dressed chastely in a simple white gown. 她穿着朴素的白色长袍，显得贞洁端庄。|近义词：purely, virtuously, modestly, decently|反义词：immodestly, indecently, lewdly|词性:副词
Etymology: From Middle English *chastli*, from Old French *chastement*, from Latin *castē* ("purely, virtuously").
