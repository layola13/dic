microcalorimeter:/ˌmaɪkroʊˌkæləˈrɪmɪtər/| n. |微量热量计|An instrument used to measure very small amounts of heat involved in chemical or physical changes.| The researchers used a microcalorimeter to study the heat generated by the reaction.|Synonyms: calorimeter (more general), micro-DSC (Differential Scanning Calorimeter - a specific type)|Antonyms: N/A (No direct antonym for a measuring instrument)|noun
