antidotical:/ˌæntiˈdɒtɪkl/ /ˌæntiˈdɑːtɪkl/| adj.|解毒的；抵消的；对抗的|Acting as or providing an antidote; counteracting or neutralizing something harmful or unpleasant.|例句：The medicine had an antidotical effect on the poison.  这种药对毒物有解毒作用。

例句：His cheerful attitude was antidotical to the gloom of the situation. 他乐观的态度抵消了局势的黯淡。|近义词：counteractive, neutralizing, remedial, corrective|反义词：poisonous, toxic, harmful, aggravating|词性:形容词
