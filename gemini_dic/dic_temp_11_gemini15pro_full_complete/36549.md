collages:/kəˈlɑːʒɪz/
n.
拼贴画，拼贴作品
A piece of art made by sticking various different materials such as photographs and pieces of paper or fabric onto a backing.
The artist's latest work was a collage of old photographs and newspaper clippings.  这位艺术家的最新作品是由旧照片和剪报拼贴而成的。
montages, assemblies, compilations
---

词性: noun
音标: /kəˈlɑːʒɪz/ (美式) /ˈkɒlɑːʒɪz/ (英式)
中文翻译: 拼贴画，拼贴作品
英文解析: A work of art made by gluing various materials, such as paper, cloth, or photographs, to a flat surface.  Can also refer to a diverse assortment of things.
例句:
Her bedroom wall was covered in a collage of posters and photos. 她的卧室墙上贴满了海报和照片的拼贴画。

The film was a collage of images from different eras. 这部电影是由不同时代的图像拼贴而成的。

近义词: montage, assemblage, compilation, patchwork
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider "simplicity" or "uniformity" as contrasting concepts)
