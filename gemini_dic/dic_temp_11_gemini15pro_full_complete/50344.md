deplumation:/ˌdiːpluːˈmeɪʃən/ |n.|脱毛，拔毛|The deplumation of chickens is usually done by machines.  鸡的脱毛通常由机器完成。|The deplumation of the bird left it vulnerable to the cold. 这只鸟的脱毛使它容易受寒。|Synonyms:  plucking, molting|Antonyms: feathering, plumage|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *deplūmāre* (to pluck, strip of feathers), from *de-* (down, away from) + *plūma* (feather).
