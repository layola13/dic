frontiers:/ˈfrʌntɪərz/ /ˈfrʌnˌtɪrz/ | n. |边界；前沿；新领域|Scientists are exploring new frontiers in medicine. 科学家们正在探索医学的新领域。|例句：Space exploration opens up exciting new frontiers for scientific discovery.  太空探索为科学发现开辟了令人兴奋的新领域。|近义词：boundaries, borders, limits, edges, vanguard, leading edge|反义词:center, core, heartland|词性:名词

词源: From Old French *frontiere*, from front 'forehead, front', from Late Latin frons, frontem 'forehead, front'.  The original meaning referred to the forehead or front of an army, hence the border between two armies or countries.
