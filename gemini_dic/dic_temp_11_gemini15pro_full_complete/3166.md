afar's:/əˈfɑːrz/| n.|远方的人/物（所有格）|Something belonging to or associated with a place or person far away. |The afar's traditions are fascinating. 远方人们的传统令人着迷。|近义词：distant's, remote's|反义词：near's, close's|词性:名词(所有格)

词源: From "afar" (adverb and adjective meaning "at or to a great distance"), from Old English "on feorr" ("at a distance").  The "-'s" is the possessive suffix.
