birdbaths:/ˈbɜːrdbɑːθs/
n.
鸟盆，鸟澡盆
A basin filled with water for birds to bathe in.
The robins splashed happily in the birdbaths. 知更鸟在鸟盆里欢快地戏水。
近义词：bird bath, bird fountain
反义词: 无
词性: 名词
