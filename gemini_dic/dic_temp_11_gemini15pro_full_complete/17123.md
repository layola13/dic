battered:/ˈbætərd/ (US), /ˈbætəd/ (UK)| adj.| 损坏的，破旧的，受虐待的| Damaged by blows or hard usage; showing the effects of prolonged hardship or suffering.  Figuratively, it can also mean emotionally or psychologically damaged. | The car was old and battered. 这辆车又旧又破。| synonyms: damaged, worn, bruised, beaten, abused, battered | antonyms: new, pristine, undamaged, repaired, healed | adjective


batter:/ˈbætər/ (US), /ˈbætə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.| 面糊，击球手，连续猛击| As a noun, it refers to a mixture of flour, eggs, and milk or water used in cooking. As a verb, it means to hit repeatedly with hard blows. | The batter hit a home run. 击球手击出了一个本垒打。 | synonyms: (n.) dough, mixture; (v.) beat, pound, pummel | antonyms: (v.) protect, defend | noun, verb
