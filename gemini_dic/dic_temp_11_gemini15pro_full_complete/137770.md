nonvascular:/nɒnˈvæskjʊlə(r)/
adjective
无维管束的；非维管的
Lacking a vascular system; not vascular.  Relating to or denoting a plant or plant part lacking xylem and phloem, typically smaller and simpler than vascular plants, such as mosses and liverworts.
Example: Mosses are nonvascular plants and absorb water directly through their surfaces.  苔藓是无维管植物，直接通过表面吸收水分。
Example:  Nonvascular plants are unable to transport water and nutrients over long distances. 无维管植物无法长距离运输水分和养分。
Synonym: avascular
Antonym: vascular
word type: adjective
