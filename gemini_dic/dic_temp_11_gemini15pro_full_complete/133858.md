Etymology:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin *non < *noenum < ne oinom "not one."
graphical (adjective): Relating to or in the form of a graph. From Greek graphikos "of or for writing, drawing," from graphein "to write, draw, represent by lines."
-ness (suffix):  Forms nouns from adjectives, meaning "the state or quality of being."
