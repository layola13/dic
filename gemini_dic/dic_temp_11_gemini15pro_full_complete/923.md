abutted:/əˈbʌtɪd/
verb
毗邻；邻接；紧靠
to be next to or touching an edge or surface
例句：Their properties abutted each other. 他们的房产彼此相邻。
例句：The new building abutted on the old city wall. 新建筑紧靠着旧城墙。
近义词：adjoined, bordered, touched
反义词：separated, detached
词性：v.

abut:/əˈbʌt/
verb
毗邻；邻接；紧靠
to be next to or touching an edge or surface
例句：Their land abuts a nature reserve. 他们的土地毗邻一个自然保护区。
例句：The houses abut onto the park. 这些房子紧邻公园。
近义词：adjoin, border, touch
反义词：separate, detach
词性：v.

词源：From Middle English *abuten, *abutte, from Old French abouter (“to place end to end, join, fasten, touch, border”), from bout (“end, extremity”), from Late Latin buttum (“end, limit”), of Germanic origin, compare Old English būt (“remedy, compensation”).
