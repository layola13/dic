happy-clappy:/ˈhæpi ˈklæpi/ | adj. | 过分热情活泼的；虚情假意的；矫揉造作的 | Excessively enthusiastic and demonstrative, often in a way that seems insincere or overly sentimental. Characterized by showy displays of emotion, especially in a religious context. |  例句：The church service was a bit too happy-clappy for my taste.  教堂的礼拜仪式对我来说有点过于热情活泼了。| 近义词：effusive, over-the-top, sentimental | 反义词：reserved, subdued, understated | 词性: 形容词

happy-clappy:/ˈhæpi ˈklæpi/ | n. |  过分热情活泼的人；虚情假意的人 |  A person who is excessively enthusiastic and demonstrative, often in a way that seems insincere or overly sentimental. | 例句：He's one of those happy-clappies who's always trying to hug everyone. 他是那种总是试图拥抱每个人的过分热情的人。| 近义词：extrovert (in a negative sense), show-off | 反义词：introvert (in a neutral sense), reserved person | 词性: 名词
