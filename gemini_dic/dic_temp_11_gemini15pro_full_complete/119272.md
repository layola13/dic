mickey-taking:/ˈmɪkiˌteɪkɪŋ/ | n. | 戏弄；嘲笑；取笑 | The act of teasing or mocking someone in a playful or slightly unkind way.  It implies gentle mockery, not serious or malicious. |  He's always mickey-taking out of his younger brother. 他总是取笑他弟弟。 |  teasing, mocking, ribbing, joshing,  banter |  respect, praise | noun
