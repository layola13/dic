cachectic:/kəˈkɛktɪk/
adjective
恶病质的，极度消瘦的
Relating to or suffering from cachexia, a condition of physical wasting away due to illness.
The cachectic patient was so weak he could barely lift his head.  恶病质的病人虚弱得几乎抬不起头。

The cancer patient appeared cachectic after months of chemotherapy.  化疗数月后，癌症病人显得极度消瘦。


emaciated, wasted, gaunt, skeletal
healthy, robust, well-nourished
adjective
