bushily:/ˈbʊʃɪli/| adv.|浓密地；茂盛地|In a bushy manner; with thick, bushy growth.|The plant grew bushily, covering the entire trellis.  这种植物生长茂盛，覆盖了整个棚架。|近义词：thickly, densely, profusely|反义词:sparsely, thinly|词性:副词
Etymology: From bushy + -ly.  Bushy itself comes from bush (a shrub or clump of shrubs) + -y (forming adjectives meaning 'having the quality of', 'full of', 'covered with'). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *busk- (bush).
