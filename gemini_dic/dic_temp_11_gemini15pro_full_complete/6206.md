ammonite:/ˈæmənaɪt/| n. |菊石，阿蒙神之石|An extinct marine mollusk of the class Cephalopoda, typically with a coiled, chambered shell.  It is commonly found as a fossil.|例句：The museum displayed a large collection of ammonites.  博物馆展出了大量的菊石化石。

例句：She found an ammonite fossil on the beach. 她在海滩上发现了一块菊石化石。|近义词：fossil, cephalopod|反义词: (无，因为是已灭绝物种)|词性:noun
