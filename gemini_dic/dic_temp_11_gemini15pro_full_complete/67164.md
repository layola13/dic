ex post facto:/ˌɛks poʊst ˈfæktoʊ/ /ˌɛks pɒst ˈfæktəʊ/
adj.
事后的；溯及既往的
Having retroactive effect; applying to events that occurred before enactment.
The law was deemed ex post facto and therefore invalid.  这项法律被认为是事后法，因此无效。
ex post facto application of the new tax law caused confusion and anger.  新税法的追溯应用引起了混乱和愤怒。
retroactive, retrospective
n/a
adjective

词源:
From Latin *ex postfacto*, from ex (“from, out of”) + post (“after”) + factō, ablative of factum (“deed, act”).  来自拉丁语 *ex postfacto*，由 ex（“来自，出于”）+ post（“之后”）+ factō（factum 的夺格，意为“契约，行为”）组成。
