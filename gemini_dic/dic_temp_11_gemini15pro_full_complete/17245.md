例句：She wore a dress made of bayadere silk. 她穿着一件由条纹丝绸制成的连衣裙。 | 近义词：ballerina, danseuse | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *bayadère*, from Portuguese *bailadeira* (“dancing girl”), from *bailar* (“to dance”), ultimately from Late Latin *ballāre*.
