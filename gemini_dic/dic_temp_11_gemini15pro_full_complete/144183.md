outsidedness:/ˌaʊtˈsaɪdɪdnəs/ | n. | 外在性；局外性；外来性 | The state or quality of being outside or an outsider.  The feeling or experience of being excluded or alienated. | Her outsidedness gave her a unique perspective on the community. 她作为局外人的身份让她对这个社区有了独特的视角。|  The outsidedness of the immigrant experience can be isolating. 移民经历的局外性可能令人感到孤立。 |  exteriority, externality, otherness, alienation | belonging, inclusion | noun
