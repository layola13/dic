mightily:/ˈmaɪtɪli/| adv.|强大地，非常地，有力地|With great power or force; extremely.|He mightily impressed the audience with his speech. 他用他的演讲给观众留下了深刻的印象。|近义词：powerfully, strongly, greatly, extremely, exceedingly|反义词：weakly, feebly, slightly|词性:副词
Etymology: From Middle English *myȝtily*, from Old English *mihtiglīċe* (“mightily, powerfully”), equivalent to mighty +‎ -ly.
