deliberateness:/dɪˈlɪbərɪtnəs/
n.
故意; 慎重; 从容不迫
The quality of being done consciously and intentionally.
She tied the knot with deliberateness. 她特意打了那个结。
近义词：intentionality, purposefulness, calculation, premeditation
反义词：rashness, impulsiveness, spontaneity, thoughtlessness
词性：名词

deliberate:/dɪˈlɪbərɪt/
adj.
故意的; 蓄意的; 从容不迫的
Done consciously and intentionally.
It was a deliberate attempt to mislead us. 那是蓄意误导我们。
近义词：intentional, purposeful, calculated, premeditated
反义词：accidental, unintentional, spontaneous, impulsive
词性：形容词

deliberate:/dɪˈlɪbəreɪt/
v.
仔细考虑; 商议
Engage in careful or long consideration.
The jury deliberated for five days before reaching a verdict. 陪审团商议了五天，才作出裁决。
近义词：consider, contemplate, ponder, meditate
反义词：react impulsively, rush into
词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dēliberātus, past participle of dēliberāre (“to weigh in the mind, consider, deliberate”), from dē (“from”) + librāre (“to weigh”), from libra (“balance, scales”).
