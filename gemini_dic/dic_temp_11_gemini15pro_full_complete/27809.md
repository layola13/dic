cannoning:/ˈkænənɪŋ/
v.
峡谷漂流; 瀑布跳水; 
The act of descending a canyon by a variety of means including jumping, sliding down natural water chutes, swimming, and rappelling.
例句：We went canyoning in Utah last summer. 我们去年夏天在犹他州进行了峡谷漂流。

例句：Canyoning can be a dangerous sport if proper safety precautions are not taken. 如果没有采取适当的安全预防措施，峡谷漂流可能是一项危险的运动。
近义词：canyoneering, gorge walking
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps hiking or mountaineering if considering opposite terrain)
词性：动词 (verb)
