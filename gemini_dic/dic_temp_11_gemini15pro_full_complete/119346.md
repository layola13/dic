microchip: /ˈmaɪkroʊtʃɪp/ | n. |微芯片，芯片| A tiny wafer of semiconducting material, typically silicon, used to make integrated circuits. |  This computer has a powerful microchip. 这台电脑有一个强大的微芯片。| synonym: chip, integrated circuit | antonym: - | noun

microchip: /ˈmaɪkroʊtʃɪp/ | vt. |给…植入微芯片| To implant a microchip into (an animal). | The vet microchipped my dog. 兽医给我的狗植入了微芯片。| synonym: implant a chip | antonym: - | verb
