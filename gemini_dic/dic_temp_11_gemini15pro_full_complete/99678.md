interlining:/ˈɪntərˌlaɪnɪŋ/
n.|衬里，衬布，夹层|A layer of material placed between the outer fabric and the lining of a garment for warmth or body. |The coat has a warm interlining. 这件外套有一个保暖的衬里。|Synonyms: lining, padding, wadding|Antonyms: exterior, outer layer|Noun

interlining:/ˈɪntərˌlaɪnɪŋ/
n.|行间书写；行间插入|The act of writing or inserting something between lines of existing text.|He made some corrections by interlining the document. 他通过在文档中行间书写进行了一些修改。|Synonyms: insertion, interpolation|Antonyms: deletion, removal|Noun
