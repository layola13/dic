counter-potent:/ˈkaʊntərˌpoʊtnt/ | adj. | 相等权力的，对抗的 | Having equal power or force; able to act against something else with equal force. |  The two armies were counter-potent, resulting in a stalemate.  两支军队势均力敌，导致了僵局。

The counter-potent forces of good and evil battled for supremacy. 善恶两种对抗的力量争夺霸权。| 近义词: equivalent, opposing, rival | 反义词: inferior, subordinate, weak | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Latin "contra" (against) + "potens" (powerful).
