entering:/ˈɛntərɪŋ/ | v. | 进入，开始 | Present participle of "enter".  The act of going into a place or starting something. | She is entering the building. 她正在进入大楼。| He is entering a new phase of his life. 他正在进入人生的新阶段。 | synonyms: going in, coming in, accessing, beginning, commencing | antonyms: leaving, exiting, departing, finishing, concluding | verb
