hospitalize: /ˈhɑːspɪtəˌlaɪz/ (US), /ˈhɒspɪtəˌlaɪz/ (UK)
verb
住院治疗
To admit someone to a hospital for medical treatment.
例句：
He was hospitalized after the car accident.  他车祸后住院了。

My grandmother was hospitalized for pneumonia. 我祖母因肺炎住院了。

近义词：admit, intern
反义词: discharge, release
词性：verb
