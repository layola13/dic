fidelity:/fɪˈdeləti/ /faɪˈdeləti/| n. |忠诚；忠实；保真度|The fidelity of the recording is excellent.  录音的保真度极佳。| 近义词: loyalty, faithfulness, constancy, allegiance | 反义词: disloyalty, infidelity, treachery, treason | 词性: 名词
Etymology: From Middle French fidelité, from Latin fidēlitās (“faithfulness”), from fidēlis (“faithful”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰeydʰ- (“to trust, confide”).
