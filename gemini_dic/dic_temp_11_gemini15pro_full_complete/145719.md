overcredit:/ˈoʊvərˌkrɛdɪt/
verb
过度授信; 超额授信
To extend credit beyond a safe or reasonable limit.
The bank overcredited the customer, leading to financial difficulties. 银行过度授信给客户，导致了财务困难。
The company overcredited its accounts receivable, which made it appear more profitable than it actually was. 该公司对其应收账款过度授信，使其看起来比实际盈利更多。
Near Synonyms: overlend, overextend credit
Antonyms: undercredit, restrict credit
