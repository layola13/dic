hangared:/ˈhæŋərd/ | adj. | 停放在机库中的；拥有机库的 | Having a hangar or hangars; kept or stored in a hangar. | The hangared aircraft were safe from the storm. 停放在机库中的飞机免受了风暴的侵袭。| Synonym: sheltered, housed | Antonym: exposed, unsheltered | adjective

hangared:/ˈhæŋərd/ | v. |  将…放入机库；停放（飞机） | To place or store in a hangar.  This is the past tense and past participle of the verb "hangar". | The pilot hangared the plane for the night.  飞行员把飞机停放在机库里过夜。| Synonym: store, house, shelter | Antonym: expose | verb
