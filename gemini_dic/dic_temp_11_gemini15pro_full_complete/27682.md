candlelighted:/ˈkændəlˌlaɪtɪd/| adj.|烛光的，被烛光照亮的|Lit by the light of candles.  The room was candlelighted and romantic. 房间里点着蜡烛，很浪漫。|例句：We had a candlelighted dinner. 我们吃了一顿烛光晚餐。

例句：The candlelighted room created a warm atmosphere. 烛光照亮的房间营造了温暖的氛围。

近义词：candlelit|反义词:brightly lit, fluorescently lit|词性:形容词
