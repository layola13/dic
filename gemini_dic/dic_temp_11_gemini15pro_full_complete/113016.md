lumbric:/ˈlʌmbrɪk/| adj.| 蚯蚓的；似蚯蚓的|Relating to or resembling an earthworm.  Referring to something long, thin, and flexible like an earthworm.|Lumbric muscles help control the movement of the earthworm. 蚯蚓的肌肉帮助控制蚯蚓的运动。|The lumbric canals within the fingers allow tendons to glide smoothly. 手指内的蚯蚓管可以让肌腱顺利滑动。 |近义词:vermiform|反义词:|adj.
