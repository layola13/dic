housefuls:/ˈhaʊsfʊlz/
noun
满屋；客满；几栋房子的人
Houses full of people or things.
The theater had several housefuls for each performance. 剧院每场演出都有几场满座。
Synonyms: capacity crowds, packed houses
Antonyms: empty houses, vacant houses
