guidelines:/ˈɡaɪdlaɪnz/ | n. | 指导方针；指导原则；准则 |  A set of rules or instructions that are intended to advise people on how something should be done. | The government has issued new guidelines on food safety. 政府发布了新的食品安全指南。 | recommendations, principles, rules, regulations | restrictions, limitations | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "guide" + "lines." "Guide" comes from the Old French word "guider," which means "to show the way." "Lines" comes from the Old English word "līne."
