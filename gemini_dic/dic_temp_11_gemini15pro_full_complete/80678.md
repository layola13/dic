gorge:/ɡɔːrdʒ/ | n. |峡谷；咽喉；暴食|A deep, narrow valley with steep sides. |例句：The river carved a deep gorge through the mountains.  河流在群山中 carving 出了一条深邃的峡谷。|近义词：canyon, ravine, chasm |反义词:plain, flat|词性:名词
gorge:/ɡɔːrdʒ/ | v. |狼吞虎咽；塞满；充满|To eat a large amount greedily; to fill completely. |例句：He gorged himself on pizza. 他狼吞虎咽地吃了披萨。|近义词：stuff, cram, devour |反义词:starve, nibble|词性:动词
gorged:/ɡɔːrdʒd/ | adj. |吃饱的；塞满的|Having eaten to excess; filled to capacity. |例句：I felt gorged after the Thanksgiving feast. 感恩节盛宴后，我感觉吃撑了。|近义词：stuffed, full, satiated |反义词:hungry, empty|词性:形容词
