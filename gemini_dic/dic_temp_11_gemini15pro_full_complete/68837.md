fasciitis:/ˌfæʃiˈaɪtɪs/| n.|筋膜炎|Inflammation of the fascia, a band of fibrous tissue that surrounds muscles, blood vessels, and nerves.|Plantar fasciitis is a common cause of heel pain. 足底筋膜炎是导致足跟疼痛的常见原因。|Synonym: fasciitis (often no direct synonym, depends on the specific type, e.g., plantar fasciitis)|Antonym: N/A (no direct antonym)|noun

fascia:/ˈfæʃ(i)ə/| n.|筋膜|A thin sheath of fibrous tissue enclosing a muscle or other organ.|The fascia surrounding the muscles was inflamed. 包裹肌肉的筋膜发炎了。|Synonym: aponeurosis (sometimes)|Antonym: N/A|noun
