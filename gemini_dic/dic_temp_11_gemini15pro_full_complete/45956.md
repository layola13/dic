cwit:/kwɪt/ | n. | 傻瓜，蠢货 | A foolish or stupid person. | He's such a cwit, he locked his keys in the car again. 他真是个傻瓜，又把钥匙锁在车里了。 | 近义词：idiot, fool, moron, dimwit, nitwit | 反义词: genius, intellectual | 词性:名词

词源:  Likely a variant of "twit," which is itself probably of Scandinavian origin, related to Old Norse *þveit, meaning "a cut" or "a piece," perhaps referring to a small or insignificant person.  The "cw-" spelling is less common and may be a dialectical variation or a more recent alteration.
