circulatory:/ˈsɜːrkjələtɔːri/ (US) /ˈsɜːkjʊlətəri/ (UK)
adjective
循环的，循环系统的
Relating to the circulation of blood or other fluids in the body.
The circulatory system is responsible for transporting blood throughout the body. 循环系统负责将血液输送到全身。
The heart plays a vital role in the circulatory process. 心脏在循环过程中起着至关重要的作用。

近义词:vascular, cardiovascular
反义词:None

词性:adjective
