alkalis:/ˈælkəlaɪz/ /ˈælkəliːz/ | n. |碱，强碱|A substance that has a pH of more than 7 and that forms a salt when it reacts with an acid; a soluble hydroxide of an alkali metal. |Example 1: Alkalis are commonly used in cleaning products. 碱通常用于清洁产品中。

Example 2: Sodium hydroxide is a strong alkali. 氢氧化钠是一种强碱。 |Near synonyms: bases, hydroxides |Antonyms: acids |Noun
