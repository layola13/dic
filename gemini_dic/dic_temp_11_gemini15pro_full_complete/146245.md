overextended:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈstendɪd/
adjective
过度扩张的；过分伸展的；承担过多的义务的
having taken on more work or responsibilities than you can manage
例句：The company is overextended financially. 这家公司财务过度扩张。

例句：He felt overextended with too many projects and deadlines. 他感觉承担了太多的项目和截止日期，不堪重负。
近义词：overstretched, overburdened, strained
反义词: underutilized, relaxed

词性: adjective
