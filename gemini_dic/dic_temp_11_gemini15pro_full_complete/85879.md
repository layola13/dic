headborough: /ˈhɛdbʌrə/ | n. | 治安官，村长 | A local constable or petty officer responsible for maintaining order in a tithing or parish.  |  The headborough was responsible for summoning the men of the village to the local court. 治安官负责召集村民到地方法院。 |  The headborough collected taxes from the villagers. 治安官向村民征税。 | Synonyms: constable, tithingman, peace officer | Antonyms: criminal, lawbreaker | noun
