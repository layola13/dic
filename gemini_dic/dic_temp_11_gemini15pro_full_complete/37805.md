compendiate:/kəmˈpɛndiˌeɪt/
verb
概括；缩写；汇编
to make a compendium of; to summarize or condense
He compendiated the vast technical literature into a single easily understood volume. 他将大量的技术文献概括成了一本易于理解的书。
