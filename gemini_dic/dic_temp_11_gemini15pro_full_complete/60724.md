ETYMOLOGY->
"electro-osmotic" is derived from "electro-osmosis."
"Electro-" comes from the Greek "ēlektron" meaning "amber," referencing the static electricity produced by rubbing amber.
"-osmosis" comes from the Greek "ōsmos" meaning "pushing," referring to the movement of liquid.
