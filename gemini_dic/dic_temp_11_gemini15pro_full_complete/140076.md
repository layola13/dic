of-few-words:/əv fjuː wɜːdz/
adjective
寡言少语的，简洁的
Someone who is of few words does not speak very much.
例句：He's a man of few words. 他是个寡言少语的人。

例句：She gave a concise, of-few-words summary of the situation. 她对情况做了简明扼要的总结。

近义词：taciturn, reticent, laconic, concise, brief
反义词：talkative, loquacious, verbose, garrulous, voluble
词性：adjective

词源：This phrase is a simple combination of the preposition "of" indicating possession or characteristic, and the noun phrase "few words," literally meaning having a small number of words.  It dates back to at least the 16th century.
