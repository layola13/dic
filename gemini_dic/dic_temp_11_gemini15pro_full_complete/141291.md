ETYMOLOGY->
"Open" comes from Old English "open," ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*upanaz." "Cast" in this context likely comes from the Old Norse "kasta" meaning "to throw" or "to cast," referring to the action of casting aside the overburden to reach the minerals.
