interchangings:/ˌɪntərˈtʃeɪndʒɪŋz/ | n. | 交换，互换，交替 | The act of exchanging things; reciprocal giving and receiving. |  The interchangings of gifts between the two families cemented their bond.  两个家庭之间的礼物交换巩固了他们的联系。 | synonyms: exchanges, swaps, trades | antonyms: retentions, withholdings | noun

interchanging:/ˌɪntərˈtʃeɪndʒɪŋ/ | adj. | 可互换的，可交换的 |  Capable of being exchanged or used in place of each other. | The parts of the two machines are interchanging. 这两台机器的零件可以互换。 | synonyms: interchangeable, exchangeable, replaceable | antonyms: non-interchangeable, irreplaceable | adjective


Etymology:
From interchange + -ing.  Interchange itself comes from Old French entrechangier, from entre- "between" + changier "to change".
