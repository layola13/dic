homotaxis:/ˌhɒməˈtæksɪs/ /ˌhoʊməˈtæksɪs/
noun
同源轴，同型排列
The arrangement of similar body parts along a central axis, such as the vertebrae in a spinal column.
The homotaxis of the vertebrae allows for flexibility and movement. 脊椎的同型排列使得身体能够灵活运动。
homotaxy, serial homology
heterotaxis
