interconnection:/ˌɪntərˈkəˈnekʃən/| n.|互联，相互联系|A state of being connected reciprocally; a mutual connection.|The interconnection of the various parts of the system is crucial for its proper functioning. 系统各个部分的互联对其正常运行至关重要。|Synonyms: link, network, connection, junction, interdependence|Antonyms: separation, disconnection, isolation|noun

interconnect:/ˌɪntərˈkəˈnekt/| v.|使相互连接，使互相联系|To connect with or attach to one another.|The new highway will interconnect several major cities. 这条新高速公路将连接几个主要城市。|Synonyms: link, join, connect, unite|Antonyms: separate, disconnect, divide|verb
