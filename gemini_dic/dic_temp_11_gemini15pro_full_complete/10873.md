arachnida:/əˈræknɪdə/ | n. |蛛形纲动物|A class of chelicerate arthropods that includes spiders, scorpions, mites, and ticks.  They are characterized by having eight legs, two body segments (a cephalothorax and an abdomen), and no antennae.|Spiders, scorpions, and mites are all members of the arachnida class. 蜘蛛、蝎子和螨虫都是蛛形纲动物。| Arachnids are known for their eight legs. 蛛形纲动物以其八条腿而闻名。|近义词:  (No single word synonym, but can use "arachnids," "chelicerates")|反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:noun
