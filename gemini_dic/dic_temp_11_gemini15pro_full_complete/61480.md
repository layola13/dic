embrave:/ɪmˈbreɪv/ | vt. |使勇敢，使有胆量；鼓舞|To make someone brave or to give them courage.|She emboldened herself to speak up against the injustice. 她鼓起勇气，公开反对不公正。|近义词：encourage, inspirit, hearten, fortify, empower|反义词：discourage, dishearten, cow, intimidate|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From en- + brave.  The prefix "en-" means "to put into" or "to cause to be."  "Brave" comes from the Italian word "bravo" meaning "wild, courageous."
