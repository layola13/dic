doodah:/ˈduːdɑː/ (US), /duːˈdɑː/ (UK)| n.| 小玩意儿；小摆设；不重要的东西|A doodah is a thing, often small and decorative, whose name you don't know or can't remember.|She had all sorts of doodahs on her desk. 她的桌子上摆满了各种小玩意儿。|Synonyms: gadget, trinket, gewgaw, thingamajig, whatchamacallit|Antonyms: essential, necessity|noun

doodah:/ˈduːdɑː/ (US), /duːˈdɑː/ (UK)| n.| 喧闹；骚动；麻烦|A doodah can also refer to a fuss or commotion.|What's all the doodah about?  这都是在吵吵嚷嚷些什么？|Synonyms: fuss, commotion, ado, to-do|Antonyms: calm, peace|noun
