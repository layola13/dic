juicy:/ˈdʒuːsi/| adj.|多汁的；有趣的；有利可图的|Full of juice; succulent. Also, interesting and exciting, especially in a gossipy way; profitable.|The steak was perfectly cooked and juicy. 这块牛排做得恰到好处，很多汁。|Synonyms: succulent, lush, moist, ripe, rich, flavorful, interesting, scandalous, profitable|Antonyms: dry, arid, dull, boring, unprofitable|adjective
