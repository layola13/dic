judaslike: /ˈdʒuːdəslaɪk/
adjective
犹大似的；背信弃义的；奸诈的
Resembling Judas Iscariot, the disciple who betrayed Jesus; treacherous.
He cast a judaslike glance at his friend before revealing the secret. 他在泄露秘密之前，用犹大似的眼神看了一眼他的朋友。
Synonyms: treacherous, traitorous, perfidious, disloyal, deceitful
Antonyms: loyal, faithful, trustworthy, dependable, honest
词性: 形容词
