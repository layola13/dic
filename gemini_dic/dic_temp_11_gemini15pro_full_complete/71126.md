fishiness:/ˈfɪʃɪnəs/
n.
可疑; 腥味
the quality of seeming dishonest or of concealing something.  The literal smell or taste of fish.
例句：There's a certain fishiness about his explanation. 他的解释有点可疑。


例句：The fishiness of the old refrigerator was overwhelming.  旧冰箱的腥味让人难以忍受。

近义词：suspiciousness, shadiness, dubiousness, stink, smell
反义词：honesty, openness, trustworthiness, freshness
词性：名词
