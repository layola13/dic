earnest:/ˈɜːrnɪst/ (US) /ˈɜːnɪst/ (UK)| adj. n.|认真的，热心的；定金|Serious and sincere in intention; showing deep sincerity or seriousness.  (n.) something given as a pledge, especially part of a payment made to confirm a contract.|She made an earnest attempt to apologize. 她认真地尝试道歉。|He gave them £1000 as an earnest of his intention to buy the house.  他给了他们1000英镑作为他购房意向的定金。|Synonyms: sincere, serious, committed, dedicated, genuine, heartfelt|Antonyms: frivolous, insincere, apathetic, indifferent|adjective, noun
