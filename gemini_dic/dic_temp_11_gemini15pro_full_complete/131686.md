nonconcur:/ˌnɑːnkənˈkɜːr/ /ˌnɒnkənˈkɜː(r)/
verb
不同意；不一致；不批准
To disagree or refuse to cooperate.  To withhold assent or approval.
The judge nonconcurred with the jury's verdict. 法官不同意陪审团的裁决。

The other members of the committee nonconcurred with his proposal. 委员会的其他成员不同意他的提议。

近义词: disagree, dissent, object
反义词: agree, concur, assent
词性: verb
