counter-pressure:/ˈkaʊntərˌprɛʃər/| n.|反压力，对抗压力|Pressure exerted in opposition to another pressure.|The doctor applied counter-pressure to the wound to stop the bleeding. 医生对伤口施加反压力以止血。|Synonyms: back pressure, opposing pressure|Antonyms: pressure, compression|noun

counter-pressure:/ˈkaʊntərˌprɛʃər/| adj.|反压力的，对抗压力的|Exerting pressure in opposition to another pressure.  (Less common as an adjective)|A counter-pressure bandage was applied. 使用了反压力绷带。|Synonyms:  resistant, opposing|Antonyms: yielding, compliant|adjective
