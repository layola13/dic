carolitic: /ˌkærəˈlɪtɪk/
adjective
加洛林王朝的;  卡洛林王朝的
Of or relating to the Carolingian dynasty, especially to its architecture, art, and culture.
The carolitic Renaissance saw a flourishing of literature and art. 加洛林文艺复兴时期见证了文学和艺术的繁荣。
The manuscript is written in a distinctive carolitic script.  这份手稿是用独特的加洛林字体书写的。
Near synonyms: Carolingian, Frankish
Antonyms: None (specific historical period, no direct opposite)
词性: adjective
