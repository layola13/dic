nutcase:/ˈnʌtkeɪs/| n. |疯子，傻瓜，怪人|A crazy or eccentric person.|He's a complete nutcase; he wears a tin foil hat to block government mind control rays. 他是个十足的疯子；他戴着锡箔纸帽子来阻挡政府的精神控制射线。|例句：Don't listen to him, he's a nutcase. 别听他的，他是个疯子。|近义词：madman, lunatic, crazy person, crackpot, kook|反义词:sane person, rational person|词性:noun
