modularly:/ˈmɒdʒʊləli/ (US) /ˈmɒdjʊləli/ (UK)| adv.|模块化地，以模块方式|In a way that involves separate units or modules that can be combined or arranged in different ways. |The software is designed modularly, allowing users to choose the features they need.  该软件采用模块化设计，允许用户选择所需的功能。|Example: The curriculum is designed modularly, allowing students to choose courses that fit their interests.  课程采用模块化设计，允许学生选择适合自己兴趣的课程。|Synonyms: component-wise, sectionally|Antonyms: integrally, monolithically|词性: 副词
