geopolitical:/ˌdʒiːoʊpəˈlɪtɪkəl/
adjective
地缘政治的
Relating to the study of the effects of geography (both human and physical) on international politics and international relations.
例句：Geopolitical tensions are rising in the region. 该地区的地缘政治紧张局势正在加剧。

例句：The country's geopolitical strategy is focused on securing its borders.  这个国家的地缘政治战略集中于确保其边界安全。

近义词：international, global, strategic
反义词：domestic, local, internal
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From geo- (meaning "earth") + political.  The term "geopolitics" was coined in the late 19th and early 20th centuries, influenced by the work of geographers and political scientists such as Friedrich Ratzel and Rudolf Kjellen.
