ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (meaning "wrong" or "bad") + report.  The prefix "mis-" comes from Old English "mis-", meaning "wrongly," "badly," "amiss," from Proto-Germanic "*missa-" (source also of Old Saxon "miss-", Old Frisian "mis-", Old High German "missa-", German "miß-"), from PIE root "*mei-" (1) "to change, go, move."
