manang:/ˈmɑːnɑːŋ/ | n. |  大姐; 姐姐 (尤其指对年长女性的尊称，常见于菲律宾文化) | An elder sister, a term of respect for older women, especially common in Filipino culture. |例句：Magandang umaga po, Manang.  早上好，大姐。|近义词：ate (Tagalog), elder sister |反义词: younger sister| Noun
