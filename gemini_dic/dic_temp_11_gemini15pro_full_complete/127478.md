necessitative:/nəˈsɛsɪˌteɪtɪv/
adjective
必要的；强制性的
Having the power or quality of necessitating; requiring or compelling.
The necessitative power of logic compels us to accept the conclusion. 逻辑的强制力迫使我们接受结论。
近义词:compulsory, obligatory, required, mandatory
反义词:optional, unnecessary
词性:adjective

necessity:/nəˈsɛsəti/
noun
必要性；必需品
The state or fact of being required; something that is indispensable or essential.
Water is a necessity of life. 水是生命之必需品。
近义词:requirement, essential, need
反义词:luxury, nonessential
词性:noun

necessitate:/nəˈsɛsəˌteɪt/
verb
使成为必要；需要
To make necessary; require.
The increase in population necessitates building more houses. 人口增长使得建造更多房屋成为必要。
近义词:require, demand, compel, oblige
反义词:obviate, preclude
词性:verb
