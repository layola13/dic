cunctative:/ˈkʌŋktətɪv/| adj.|拖延的，迟缓的|Inclined to delay; tardy; procrastinating.  Characterized by or showing hesitation; slow.|Example: His cunctative approach to decision-making frustrated his colleagues. 他拖延的决策方式让他的同事们很沮丧。

Example:  Cunctative in his response, he missed the deadline.  由于反应迟缓，他错过了截止日期。|近义词：dilatory, tardy, procrastinating, hesitant, slow|反义词：prompt, expeditious, swift, decisive|词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *cunctat*, past participle of *cunctor* (“to delay, linger”) ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *kenk- (“to waver, hesitate”).
