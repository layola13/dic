inerringly:/ɪˈnɜːrɪŋli/
adverb
无误地，准确无误地
Without error; infallibly.
The archer inerringly hit the bullseye.  弓箭手准确无误地射中了靶心。
Synonyms: infallibly, accurately, precisely, unerringly, faultlessly
Antonyms: erratically, inaccurately, imperfectly, faultily
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + err (to wander, stray) + -ing + -ly.  Ultimately from Latin errare (to wander).
