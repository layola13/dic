bombyx:/ˈbɒmbɪks/ | n. |蚕蛾属；蚕| The silkworm, Bombyx mori, is of immense economic importance. 家蚕，蚕蛾桑蚕，具有巨大的经济意义。| The genus Bombyx includes several species of moths. 蚕蛾属包含几种飞蛾。| 近义词: silkworm moth | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a genus) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Latin bombyx, from Ancient Greek βόμβυξ (bómbux, “silkworm, silk”). Ultimately of uncertain origin, possibly related to a word meaning “buzzing” or “humming.”
