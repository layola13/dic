interplanar:/ˌɪntərˈpleɪnər/| adj.|晶面间的，面间的|Existing or occurring between planes, especially crystal planes.|The interplanar spacing of the crystal lattice was determined by X-ray diffraction.  晶格的晶面间距是通过X射线衍射测定的。|
The interplanar bond strength affects the material's cleavage properties. 晶面间的键强度会影响材料的解理特性。|近义词:interlaminar|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps intraplanar if referring to within a single plane)|词性:adjective
