guile:/ɡaɪl/| n.|狡猾，诡计，欺诈|Insidious cunning in attaining a goal; crafty or artful deception; trickery.|He used all his guile and guts to free himself from the muddle. 他使尽浑身解数才摆脱困境。|近义词：cunning, deceit, trickery, craftiness, slyness, duplicity, chicanery|反义词：honesty, sincerity, forthrightness, candor|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *guile, gille "trick, deceit," from Old North French wile (compare Norman guile "trick"), from Frankish *wigila or some other Germanic source, from Proto-Germanic *wigila- "trick," from *wig- "to move, shake, sway, bend," from PIE *weyg- "to shake, sway."
