exalting:/ɪɡˈzɔːltɪŋ/
adjective
令人兴奋的；令人喜悦的；使人高尚的
Making someone feel very happy and proud.
The ceremony was an exalting experience.  这个仪式是一次令人振奋的经历。
Her exalting performance moved the audience to tears. 她激动人心的表演让观众感动得流下了眼泪。

近义词:elating, thrilling, uplifting, inspiring
反义词:depressing, disheartening, humbling
词性:adjective
