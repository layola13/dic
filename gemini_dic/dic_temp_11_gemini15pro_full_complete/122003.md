misspend:/ˌmɪsˈspend/| v. |误用，浪费，挥霍|To spend (time or money) unwisely or wastefully.|He misspent his youth playing video games. 他把青春都浪费在玩电子游戏上了。|She misspent her inheritance on frivolous purchases. 她把遗产浪费在了一些轻浮的购物上。|近义词：waste, squander, fritter away|反义词：save, invest, budget|词性：动词

Etymology:
From mis- + spend.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Old English origin meaning "wrongly" or "badly." "Spend" comes from Old English *spendan, meaning "to expend, pay out."
