drink-driver:/ˈdrɪŋk ˌdraɪvər/ | n. |酒后驾车者；醉酒驾驶者|A person who drives a vehicle after consuming an alcoholic beverage, especially when over the legal limit. | The drink-driver was arrested and charged with DUI.  酒后驾车者被逮捕并被控酒后驾车。|  The police caught a drink-driver speeding down the highway. 警察抓到一个在高速公路上超速行驶的酒驾司机。 | Synonyms: drunk driver, impaired driver | Antonyms: sober driver | noun
