outbitching:/ˌaʊtˈbɪtʃɪŋ/
verb
胜过(某人)的刻薄; 比...更爱抱怨
Outdoing someone in complaining, nagging, or other negative behavior.
She was outbitching him about how awful their boss was. 她比他更激烈地抱怨他们的老板有多糟糕。
近义词: outcomplaining, outwhining
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider phrases like "being supportive" or "being positive")
词性: verb (动词)
