flyfish:/ˈflaɪfɪʃ/| v. n.|飞钓；飞蝇钓；用假蝇钓鱼；飞钓的鱼|To fish with artificial flies as bait.|He enjoys flyfishing in the mountain streams. 他喜欢在山间溪流中飞钓。|近义词：angle, fish|反义词: None |词性: verb, noun

flyfish:/ˈflaɪfɪʃ/| n.|飞钓的鱼|A fish caught by flyfishing.|He caught a large flyfish in the river. 他在河里钓到了一条很大的飞蝇钓的鱼。|近义词：fish, trout, salmon|反义词: None |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From fly + fish.  The verb sense predates the noun sense.  "Fly" in this context refers to the artificial fly used as bait.
