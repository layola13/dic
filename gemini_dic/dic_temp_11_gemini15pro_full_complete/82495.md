gruntling:/ˈɡrʌntliŋ/
n.
幼猪; 哼哼唧唧的人; 爱抱怨的人
A young pig, or a person who grumbles or complains.
The farmer had a pen full of gruntlings, all vying for their mother's attention.  农场主有一圈小猪，都在争夺母猪的注意。

He was such a gruntling, always complaining about the food. 他真是个爱抱怨的人，总是抱怨食物不好吃。


近义词: complainer, grumbler, whiner, piglet (for young pig)
反义词: optimist, enthusiast


词性: noun


词源:  From "grunt" (发出哼哼声) + "-ling" (表示小的或不成熟的)
