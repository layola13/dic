cathartic:/kəˈθɑːrtɪk/
adjective
宣泄的; 净化心灵的
Providing psychological relief through the open expression of strong emotions; causing catharsis.
After a good cry, I felt cathartic.  痛哭一场后，我感觉得到了宣泄。
The experience was cathartic for her. 这段经历对她来说是一种情感的宣泄。
近义词: cleansing, purifying, releasing
反义词: repressive, suppressive
词性: adjective

catharsis:/kəˈθɑːrsɪs/
noun
宣泄; 净化
The process of releasing, and thereby providing relief from, strong or repressed emotions.  
Writing poetry can be a form of catharsis. 写诗可以是一种情感宣泄的方式。
She experienced a catharsis after talking about her problems.  在谈论了她的问题之后，她经历了一次情感的宣泄。
近义词: release, purgation, relief
反义词: repression, suppression
词性: noun
