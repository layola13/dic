bray:/breɪ/ /breɪ/
noun|驴叫声；刺耳的声音|A loud, harsh cry, typically that of a donkey.|The donkey's bray echoed through the valley. |驴叫声回荡在山谷中。|近义词：hee-haw, shriek, roar|反义词:whisper, murmur|词性:n.
bray:/breɪ/ /breɪ/
verb|（驴）发出刺耳的叫声；大声而刺耳地说|To make a loud, harsh cry, typically of a donkey; to speak or laugh loudly and harshly.|The donkey brayed loudly. |驴大声地叫了起来。|近义词：hee-haw, shriek, yell|反义词:whisper, murmur|词性:v.

词源:  From Middle English *brayen, from Old French braire (“to cry, shout, neigh”), from Vulgar Latin *bracare, of imitative origin.

注意事项:  bray 既可以指驴的叫声，也可以指任何刺耳的声音，还可以指大声而刺耳地说或笑。
