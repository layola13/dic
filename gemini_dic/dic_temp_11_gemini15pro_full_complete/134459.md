nonjournalistic:/ˌnɒnˌdʒɜːrnəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
非新闻的；非新闻工作者的
Not characteristic of or suitable for journalism; not related to the profession of journalism.
This website publishes nonjournalistic content, focusing more on opinion pieces than news reporting.  这个网站发布非新闻内容，更侧重于观点文章而不是新闻报道。
The report was highly nonjournalistic, lacking objectivity and verifiable sources.  这份报告非常不专业，缺乏客观性和可验证的来源。
Synonyms:unprofessional, biased, subjective, non-news
Antonyms:journalistic, professional, objective, unbiased
词性:adjective
