diagrams:/ˈdaɪəˌɡræmz/
n.
图表；示意图
A simplified drawing showing the components of something, or how something works.
The teacher drew a diagram of the human heart. 老师画了一张人体心脏的示意图。

diagrams:/ˈdaɪəˌɡræmz/
n.
图解； 图解说明
A graphic representation of an algebraic or geometric relationship.
The diagrams illustrate the flow of information within the system. 这些图表说明了系统内的信息流。

diagrams:/ˈdaɪəˌɡræmz/
n.
(哲学)图形; 图解
(philosophy) A graphic representation of a theory, idea, or concept.
He used diagrams to explain his complex philosophical arguments. 他用图表来解释他复杂的哲学论点。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *diagrammā, from Ancient Greek διάγραμμα (diágramma, “geometric figure, delineation, outline”), from διαγράϕω (diagráphō, “I mark out by lines, describe by lines”), from διά (diá, “through, across”) + γράϕω (gráphō, “I write, draw”).
