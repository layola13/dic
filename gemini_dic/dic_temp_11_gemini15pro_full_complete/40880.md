cookhouses:/ˈkʊkhaʊsɪz/
noun
炊事房，伙房
A building, especially in a military camp, prison, or ship, where food is prepared and cooked.
The soldiers lined up outside the cookhouses, waiting for their meals. 士兵们在炊事房外排队，等待用餐。
mess hall, galley, kitchen
dining room
