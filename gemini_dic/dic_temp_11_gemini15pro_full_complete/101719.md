irreproachableness:/ˌɪrɪˈproʊtʃəbəlnəs/
n.
无可指责，完美无瑕
The state of being irreproachable; blamelessness.
Her irreproachableness made her an ideal candidate for the position. 她的无可指责使她成为该职位的理想人选。
His irreproachableness was admired by all. 他的完美无瑕受到所有人的钦佩。

Synonyms: blamelessness, impeccability, integrity, purity, rectitude
Antonyms: culpability, guilt, faultiness, imperfection

ETYMOLOGY->
From irreproachable + -ness.  Irreproachable from in- (not) + reproachable (from re- (again) + proche (near, akin to approach) + -able (capable of)).  Ultimately from Latin *prope (near).
