almshouse: /ˈɑːmzˌhaʊs/ (US) /ˈɑːmzˌhaʊs/ (UK)| n. |救济院，贫民院|A house built and endowed for poor people to live in. |The old woman found refuge in the local almshouse.  这位老妇人在当地的救济院找到了避难所。|Example:  Many almshouses were built in the Victorian era.  许多救济院建于维多利亚时代。|Synonym: poorhouse, charitable institution |Antonym: mansion, luxury home |noun
