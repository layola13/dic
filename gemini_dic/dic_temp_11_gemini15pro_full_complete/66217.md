excitedly:/ɪkˈsaɪtɪdli/| adv.|兴奋地，激动地|In a manner showing strong feeling of happy enthusiasm and eagerness.|She waved excitedly to her friends. 她兴奋地向她的朋友们挥手。|近义词：eagerly, enthusiastically, keenly|反义词：calmly, apathetically, indifferently|词性:副词

词源: From the adjective "excited" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Excited" comes from the Latin "excitare" meaning "to rouse, stir up."
