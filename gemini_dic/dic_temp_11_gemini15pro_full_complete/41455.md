cornicular:/kɔːrˈnɪkjʊlər/
adjective
角状的；硬化的；似角质的
Resembling a horn in texture; horny; hard and brittle.
The old woman's hands were cornicular and gnarled. 老妇人的双手粗糙且变形，如同角质一般。
The beetle's shell was smooth and cornicular. 甲虫的外壳光滑而坚硬，像角质一样。
近义词:horny, callous, hardened, brittle
反义词:soft, pliable, flexible
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *corniculāris, from corniculum (“little horn”), diminutive of cornu (“horn”).
