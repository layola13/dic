iterable:/ˈɪtərəbl/| adj. n.|可迭代的，可迭代对象|An iterable is any Python object capable of returning its members one at a time, permitting it to be iterated over in a `for` loop or similar structure.  Essentially, an iterable is anything that can be looped over.|
例句：The list is an iterable object.  这个列表是一个可迭代对象。

例句：Strings are also iterable in Python. 字符串在 Python 中也是可迭代的。


近义词：loopable|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective, noun
