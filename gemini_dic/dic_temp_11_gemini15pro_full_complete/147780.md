overprompt:/ˌoʊvərˈprɑːmpt/ | adj. | 过度提示的，过度引导的 |  Given too many prompts or cues, especially in a generative AI context, leading to predictable or unoriginal output. | The AI model became overprompted and started generating repetitive and nonsensical text.  人工智能模型被过度提示，开始生成重复且无意义的文本。 | Synonyms: over-cued, over-guided | Antonyms: underprompted, unguided | adjective


overprompt:/ˌoʊvərˈprɑːmpt/ | vt. | 过度提示，过度引导 |  To provide too many prompts or cues, especially to a generative AI model, hindering its creative output. |  I think I overprompted the AI, now it's just regurgitating information instead of generating something new. 我想我过度提示了人工智能，现在它只是在反刍信息，而不是生成新的东西。| Synonyms: over-cue, over-guide | Antonyms: underprompt | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
"over-" (prefix) + "prompt" (verb/noun). "Over-" signifies "excessively" or "too much". "Prompt" originates from the Latin "promptus" meaning "ready" or "prepared."  Therefore, "overprompt" literally means "to make too ready" or "prepare excessively," which in the context of AI means providing too much guidance.
