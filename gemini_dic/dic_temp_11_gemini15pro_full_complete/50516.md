deprive:/dɪˈpraɪv/| vt.|剥夺，使丧失|To take something away from someone, especially something important or necessary. |The new law deprives us of our right to free speech. 新法律剥夺了我们言论自由的权利。|synonyms: strip, dispossess, divest, bereave|antonyms: provide, endow, grant|verb

deprive:/dɪˈpraɪv/| vt.|剥夺，使丧失|To prevent someone from having or doing something, especially something important or enjoyable. |He was deprived of food and water for three days. 他被剥夺了食物和水三天。|synonyms: deny, refuse, withhold|antonyms: allow, permit, give|verb
