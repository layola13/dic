amuse: /əˈmjuːz/
| v.| 娱乐，使…高兴 |To entertain or cause someone to find something funny or enjoyable.| The comedian amused the audience with his jokes.  喜剧演员用他的笑话逗乐了观众。| entertain, delight, please | bore, annoy | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *amuser "divert, entertain," from a- "to" (from Latin ad-) + muser "to muse, stare fixedly."  The original sense was "to cause to linger," but the meaning shifted to "entertain" in the 16th century.
