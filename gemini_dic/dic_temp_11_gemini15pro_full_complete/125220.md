multicorporations:/ˌmʌltiˌkɔːrpəˈreɪʃənz/
n.
跨国公司，大型企业集团
Large corporations with operations in multiple countries.
Example: The influence of multicorporations on global politics is a subject of much debate. 跨国公司对全球政治的影响是一个备受争议的话题。

Example: Multicorporations often face challenges in adapting to local regulations in different countries. 跨国公司经常面临适应不同国家当地法规的挑战。

Synonyms: multinationals, conglomerates, global corporations
Antonyms: small businesses, local businesses
