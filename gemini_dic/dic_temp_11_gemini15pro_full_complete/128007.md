nephritic:/nɪˈfrɪtɪk/
adjective
肾炎的；患肾炎的
Relating to or affected by nephritis (inflammation of the kidneys).
The nephritic patient required dialysis.  肾炎病人需要透析。
The doctor diagnosed a nephritic syndrome. 医生诊断出肾炎综合征。
近义词:renal
反义词:healthy

词性:adjective
