half-wicket:/ˌhɑːf ˈwɪkɪt/ | n. | 半场板球赛; 半场 wickets | A form of cricket played on a half-sized pitch with fewer players per team. |  Example: They organized a half-wicket competition for the junior players. 他们为青少年球员组织了一场半场板球比赛。| Example: Half-wicket cricket was once a popular variation of the game. 半场板球曾经是这项运动的一种流行变体。 | Synonym: limited-overs cricket (loosely) | Antonym: full-wicket cricket (though not a direct opposite, it represents the standard form) | noun
