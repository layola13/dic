gemmaceous:/dʒɛˈmeɪʃəs/
adjective
多芽的；芽状的
Multiplying by buds; bearing buds; reproducing by gemmae.
Example: The gemmaceous plant spread quickly across the damp soil. 这种多芽的植物迅速蔓延到潮湿的土壤中。
Example: The researcher studied the gemmaceous reproduction of the liverwort. 研究人员研究了地钱的芽殖。
Synonyms: budding, gemmiferous, gemmulate
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, consider antonyms for reproduction methods like seed-bearing, spore-forming)
词性: adjective
