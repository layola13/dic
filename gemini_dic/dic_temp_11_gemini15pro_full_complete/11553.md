aristocratic:/ˌærɪstəˈkrætɪk/
adjective
贵族的；贵族式的
Of, relating to, or characteristic of an aristocracy or aristocrat.
He had an aristocratic bearing. 他举止透着贵族气派。

近义词:noble, patrician, highborn, blue-blooded
反义词:plebeian, common, working-class
词性:adjective

词源:
From French *aristocratique*, from Greek *aristokratikos* "of the aristocracy," from *aristokratia* "rule of the best," from *aristos* "best" + *-kratia* "rule, power."
