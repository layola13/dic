counterpicket:/ˈkaʊntərˌpɪkɪt/| n. |反纠察线，对抗性纠察|A picket line formed by people opposed to those on the original picket line.|The union organized a counterpicket to oppose the company's lockout.|Synonyms: counter-demonstration, counter-protest|Antonyms: picket, demonstration|noun


counterpicket:/ˈkaʊntərˌpɪkɪt/| v. |进行反纠察|To form or participate in a counterpicket.|The workers counterpicketed the scabs who crossed the picket line.|Synonyms: counter-demonstrate, counter-protest|Antonyms: picket, demonstrate|verb


Etymology:  "counter-" (against) + "picket" (a pointed stake or post used in a fence, later a person or group of people stationed as a guard or protest)
