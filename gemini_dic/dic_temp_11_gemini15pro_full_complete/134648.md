nonmalleable:/ˌnɒnˈmæliəbl/ /ˌnɑːnˈmæliəbl/
adjective
不可锻造的；不易延展的；难改变的
Not easily influenced or changed; inflexible.
The artist found the material nonmalleable and difficult to work with.  艺术家发现这种材料不易延展，难以加工。
Her personality is nonmalleable; she rarely compromises. 她的性格很难改变；她很少妥协。

近义词:inflexible, rigid, unyielding, intractable
反义词:malleable, flexible, pliable, adaptable
词性:adjective

词源:
From non- + malleable, from Late Latin malleabilis, from Latin malleus (“hammer”)
