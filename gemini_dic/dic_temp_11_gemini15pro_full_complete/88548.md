hit-up:/ˌhɪt ˈʌp/| phrasal verb|要求；索取；联系；拜访|To ask someone for something, often money or a favor; to contact someone; to visit a place.|例句：I hit him up for twenty bucks. 我向他要了二十块钱。|近义词：ask for, request, contact, visit|反义词:give, offer, avoid, ignore|词性:短语动词

hit-up:/ˌhɪt ˈʌp/| n.|要求；索取；联系；拜访|An instance of asking for something, contacting someone, or visiting a place, especially in informal contexts.|例句：He gave me the hit-up for money again.他又向我要钱了。|近义词：request, contact, visit|反义词:offer, avoidance, ignorance|词性:名词
