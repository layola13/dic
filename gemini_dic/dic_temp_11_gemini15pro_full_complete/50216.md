departures:/dɪˈpɑːrtʃərz/
n.
离开；出发；背离；违反
the act of leaving a place, especially to start a journey.  A divergence or deviation, as from a standard, rule, or plan.
The flight departures board showed a two-hour delay. 航班出发显示屏显示延误两小时。
His sudden departure surprised everyone. 他的突然离开让每个人都感到惊讶。
synonyms: leaving, going, exiting, egress, withdrawal, abandonment, flight
antonyms: arrival, coming, entrance, return, advent
noun
