The word "countrier" is not a standard English word. It appears to be a misspelling or a neologism (newly coined word) perhaps intended to mean "more countrified". Therefore, I will analyze "countrified" instead.

countrified:/ˈkʌntrɪfaɪd/
adjective
乡村的；土气的；粗俗的
Having the characteristics and manners of country people; rustic or unsophisticated.
Example: He looked awkward and countrified in his new suit.  他穿着新西装，显得笨拙土气。

Example:  She maintained her countrified charm despite living in the city for years. 尽管在城市里生活了很多年，她仍然保持着乡村的魅力。
近义词:rustic, rural, provincial, unpolished, unsophisticated
反义词:sophisticated, urbane, cosmopolitan, polished, refined
词性:adjective


countrify:/ˈkʌntrɪfaɪ/
verb
使…具有乡村特色；使…过乡村生活
To make or become countrified.
Example:  Living on the farm has countrified her. 在农场生活使她变得有乡村气息。

Example: The city girl was quickly countrified by her experiences in the rural community.  这个城市女孩在农村社区的经历很快让她变得有乡村气息。

近义词:ruralize
反义词:urbanize
词性:verb
