different:/ˈdɪfrənt/ (US) /ˈdɪfərənt/ (UK)| adj.|不同的，相异的|Distinct; not the same.|例句：The two cars are different colors. 这两辆车颜色不同。|近义词：various, diverse, distinct, dissimilar|反义词：same, similar, identical|词性:形容词

different:/ˈdɪfrənt/ (US) /ˈdɪfərənt/ (UK)| adj.|各种各样的|Of various kinds; assorted.|例句：They sell many different types of cheese. 他们出售各种各样的奶酪。|近义词：various, diverse, assorted|反义词：uniform, homogeneous|词性:形容词
