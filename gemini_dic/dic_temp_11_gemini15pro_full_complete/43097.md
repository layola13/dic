cowwheat:/ˈkaʊwiːt/| n.|牛小麦; 一种小麦品种|A type of wheat traditionally used as feed for cows, but also suitable for human consumption.  It is known for its hardiness and ability to grow in poor soil conditions.|Cowwheat is grown in some parts of Europe as a hardy grain.| 近义词: hard wheat, winter wheat | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
