crushers:/ˈkrʌʃərz/| n.|破碎机；压碎机|A machine used to crush something, typically rocks, ore, or other solid materials into smaller pieces.|The quarry uses powerful crushers to break down the rock. 采石场使用强大的破碎机来破碎岩石。|近义词:grinders, pulverizers, mills|反义词:constructors, builders|Noun

crusher:/ˈkrʌʃər/| n.|破碎机；压碎机；令人心碎的事情（或人）|A machine used to crush something, typically rocks, ore, or other solid materials into smaller pieces.  Informally, can also refer to something or someone that causes emotional devastation.|The company invested in a new rock crusher. 公司投资了一台新的碎石机。|Her rejection was a real crusher. 她的拒绝真是令人心碎。|近义词:grinders, pulverizers, mills (for machines); heartbreak (for emotional devastation)|反义词:constructors, builders (for machines); comfort (for emotional devastation)|Noun
