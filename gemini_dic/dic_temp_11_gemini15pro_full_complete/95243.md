incalescency:/ˌɪnkæˈlɛsənsɪ/
n.
白热化；炽热
the state of becoming hot or starting to glow
The blacksmith watched the iron reach a state of incalescency. 铁匠看着铁达到白热化的状态。

The incalescency of the argument made it difficult to reach a compromise. 争论的白热化使得很难达成妥协。

近义词: incandescence, white heat, glow
反义词: coldness, coolness
