antidepressants:/ˌæntidiˈprɛsənts/ | n. | 抗抑郁药 | Medications used to treat depression by affecting the levels of neurotransmitters in the brain. |  She's been taking antidepressants for several months now. 她已经服用抗抑郁药好几个月了。

Antidepressants can help alleviate the symptoms of depression. 抗抑郁药可以帮助减轻抑郁症状。
