nomenclatural:/ˌnoʊmənˈkleɪtʃərəl/
adjective
命名法的；命名上的
Relating to or involved in nomenclature; taxonomic.
The nomenclatural problem has baffled scientists for years.  这个命名法问题多年来一直困扰着科学家们。
synonyms: taxonomic, classificatory, systematic
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, could consider words like "unorganized," "chaotic" in specific contexts)
词性: adjective


nomenclature:/ˈnoʊmənkleɪtʃər/
noun
命名法；术语
A system of names or terms used in a particular science or art.
The nomenclature of organic chemistry can be complex.  有机化学的命名法可能很复杂。
synonyms: terminology, classification, system
antonyms: (No direct antonyms)
词性: noun
