meckelian: /mɛˈkiːliən/
adjective | 麦克尔的，下颌的 | Relating to Johann Friedrich Meckel the Younger (1781–1833), a German anatomist, or to structures or conditions first described by him, especially Meckel's cartilage or Meckel's diverticulum. |  Meckelian cartilage plays a crucial role in the development of the jaw. 麦克尔软骨在下颌发育中起着至关重要的作用。|  Synonyms: mandibular (in some contexts) | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, depends on specific context) | word type: adjective

meckelian: /mɛˈkiːliən/
noun | 麦克尔氏软骨 | Meckel's cartilage, also known as Meckel's rod, is a piece of cartilage from which the mandible (lower jaw) develops in humans. | The meckelian becomes ossified to form the jawbone. 麦克尔氏软骨骨化形成颚骨。 | Synonyms: Meckel's cartilage, Meckel's rod | Antonyms: (No direct antonym) | word type: noun
