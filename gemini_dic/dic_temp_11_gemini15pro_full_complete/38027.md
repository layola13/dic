composed  /kəmˈpoʊzd/ | adj. |  镇定的；沉着的；（乐曲）创作的；组成的 |  calm, tranquil; created (music); made up of |  She appeared calm and composed despite the chaos. 她看起来很镇定，尽管现场一片混乱。| synonyms: calm, serene, tranquil, collected, self-possessed | antonyms: agitated, nervous, anxious, distraught | adjective

composed /kəmˈpoʊzd/ | v. (past tense) |  创作（乐曲）；组成；构成 | created (music); made up of; constituted  |  He composed a symphony for the occasion.  他为这次活动创作了一首交响曲。| synonyms: create, write, devise, construct, form | antonyms: decompose, dismantle, destroy | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *composen*, from Old French *composer*, from Latin *compōnere* (“to put together, combine, arrange”), from *com-* (“together”) + *pōnere* (“to put, place”).
