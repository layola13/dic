exsiccator:/ˈɛksɪkeɪtər/ | n. |干燥器，烘干器 |A sealable container used for drying substances or protecting them from moisture, typically containing a desiccant such as silica gel. |例句：The botanist placed the delicate specimens in an exsiccator to preserve them. 植物学家将精细的标本放在干燥器中保存。

例句：The chemist stored the anhydrous compound in an exsiccator to prevent it from absorbing moisture from the air.  化学家将无水化合物储存在干燥器中，以防止其吸收空气中的水分。


|近义词：desiccator |反义词: humidifier |词性: noun
