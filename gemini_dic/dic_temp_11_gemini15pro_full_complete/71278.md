fixations:/fɪkˈseɪʃənz/
n.
固定；专注；固恋；痴迷
an obsessive interest in or feeling about someone or something.
His fixation on the details made him a good editor. 他对细节的专注使他成为一名优秀的编辑。

fixations:/fɪkˈseɪʃənz/
n.
固定；专注；固恋；痴迷
the act of fastening something firmly in position.
The museum displayed various methods of fixation for ancient artifacts.  博物馆展示了古代文物的各种固定方法。


近义词:obsessions, preoccupations, attachments
反义词:indifference, apathy
词性:noun (plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *fixatio, from Latin fixus (past participle of figere "to fix, fasten").
