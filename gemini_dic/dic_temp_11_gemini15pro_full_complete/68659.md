farad:/ˈfæræd/ | n. | 法拉 | The standard unit of capacitance. A capacitor has a capacitance of one farad when a charge of one coulomb raises its potential by one volt. | A one-farad capacitor can store one coulomb of charge at one volt.  一个法拉的电容器在一伏特电压下可以储存一库仑的电荷。| capacitance, capacity | resistance | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the name of Michael Faraday (1791–1867), English physicist and chemist.
