interdebates:/ˌɪntərdiˈbeɪts/ | n. |相互辩论，互相讨论 |Discussions or debates between different groups or individuals. |The interdebates between the political parties were heated. 各政党之间的辩论非常激烈。|
近义词：discussions, dialogues, exchanges | 反义词: agreement, harmony | 词性: 名词

interdebates:/ˌɪntərdiˈbeɪts/ | v. |互相辩论，互相讨论 |To engage in discussions or debates between different groups or individuals. |The students interdebated the merits of different economic systems. 学生们就不同经济体制的优缺点进行了辩论。 |
近义词：discuss, debate, argue | 反义词: agree, concede | 词性: 动词
