carryable:/ˈkæriəbl/| adj.|可携带的，便携的|Something that is carryable is small and light enough to be carried easily.|This bag is lightweight and carryable, perfect for travel.  这个包很轻便，可以携带，非常适合旅行。|The chair is foldable and carryable. 这把椅子可折叠，便于携带。|近义词：portable, transportable|反义词:unportable, bulky, cumbersome|词性:adjective
