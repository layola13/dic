over-editing:/ˌoʊvərˈedɪtɪŋ/ |n.|过度编辑|The act of editing something excessively, often to the point of detriment.  It can involve over-correcting, refining too much, or removing elements that actually contribute to the overall quality or authenticity of the work.|The over-editing made the article stiff and unnatural. 过度编辑使文章僵硬而不自然。|over-refining, over-polishing|under-editing|noun
over-edit:/ˌoʊvərˈedɪt/ |v.|过度编辑|To edit something excessively, often to the detriment of its quality or authenticity.|He over-edited the photo, making it look artificial. 他过度编辑了照片，使它看起来很假。|over-refine, over-polish|under-edit|verb
