albariño:/æl.bəˈri.njoʊ/
noun|阿爾巴利諾|A variety of white wine grape grown in Galicia and Portugal, known for its aromatic and citrusy flavors. Also refers to the wine made from this grape.|This Albariño has a lovely aroma of grapefruit and lime. 这款阿爾巴利諾葡萄酒带有令人愉悦的葡萄柚和酸橙香气。|Synonyms: Albariña, Alvarinho|Antonyms: N/A|n.
