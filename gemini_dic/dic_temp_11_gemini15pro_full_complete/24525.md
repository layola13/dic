broaden:/ˈbrɔːdn/ (US) /ˈbrɔːdən/ (UK)| v. |拓宽，扩大，变宽|To make something wider or more extensive.|例句：Travel can broaden your horizons. 旅行可以开阔你的眼界。

例句：The river broadens as it nears the sea.  这条河接近大海时变宽了。|近义词：expand, widen, enlarge, extend|反义词：narrow, restrict, limit|词性:动词
Etymology: From Old English *brādian, from brād "broad".
