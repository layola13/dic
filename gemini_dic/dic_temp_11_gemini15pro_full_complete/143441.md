outflows:/ˈaʊtfloʊz/
|n.|流出；流出物；外流|The movement of money or assets out of a fund, account, or country.|The recent economic downturn has led to significant outflows of capital from the country. 最近的经济衰退导致了大量的资本外流。|synonyms: outflow, efflux, discharge, exodus|antonyms: inflow, influx|noun
