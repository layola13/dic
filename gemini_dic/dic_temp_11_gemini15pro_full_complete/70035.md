词源:  From Middle English *fetlok, from Old English *fētloc (“fetlock”), from fēt (“feet”) + loc (“lock of hair”). Related to Dutch vetlok and German Fessel. The "lock of hair" refers to the tuft of hair that grows behind the fetlock joint in some horses.
