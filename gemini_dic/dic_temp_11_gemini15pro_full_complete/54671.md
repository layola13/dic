disoccupation: /ˌdɪsˌɒkjuˈpeɪʃən/ | n. | 失业，闲暇，空闲 |  The state of being unoccupied or unemployed; leisure. |  Long periods of disoccupation can lead to feelings of worthlessness. 长期失业会导致无价值感。 | 近义词: unemployment, idleness, leisure, inactivity | 反义词: employment, occupation, activity | 词性: 名词

词源: From French *désoccupation*, from dés- (prefix denoting removal or reversal) + occupation.  Ultimately from Latin *dis-* (apart, asunder) + *occupare* (to seize, occupy).
