agreeablenesses:/əˌɡriːəbəlˈnɛsɪz/
n.
令人愉快；惬意；和蔼可亲；适合
the quality of being pleasant or pleasing
Her agreeableness made her popular. 她的和蔼可亲使她很受欢迎。

近义词:pleasantness, affability, amiability, congeniality
反义词:disagreeableness, unpleasantness, hostility

词性:名词(plural of agreeableness)

词源: From agreeable + -ness. Agreeable traces back to the Old French *agreable, from agréer "to please, accept," from a- "to" + gré "pleasure, liking," from Latin gratus "pleasing, thankful."
