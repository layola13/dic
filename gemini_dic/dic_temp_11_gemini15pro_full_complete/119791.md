migrated:/ˈmaɪɡreɪtɪd/
verb
迁移，移居
moved from one place to another
例句：The birds migrated south for the winter. 鸟儿们向南迁徙过冬。

例句：Many people migrated from rural areas to cities in search of work. 许多人为了找工作从农村迁移到城市。

近义词：emigrated, immigrated, relocated, moved, journeyed, traveled
反义词：stayed, remained, settled
词性：v.


migrated:/ˈmaɪɡreɪtɪd/
adjective
已迁移的
having migrated
例句：The migrated population faced many challenges in their new home.  已迁移的人口在新家面临许多挑战。

例句：The data from the migrated server was corrupted. 从迁移的服务器获取的数据已损坏。

近义词：relocated, moved
反义词：settled, resident
词性：adj.

PHRASE->
migrate to (迁移到): Many birds migrate to warmer climates for the winter. 许多鸟类在冬天迁移到温暖的气候。
migrate from (从...迁移):  They migrated from the countryside to the city. 他们从农村迁移到城市。
mass migration (大规模迁移): The war caused a mass migration of people. 战争造成了大规模的人口迁移。
annual migration (年度迁移): The annual migration of the monarch butterfly is a spectacular sight.  帝王蝶的年度迁徙是一道壮观的景象。
migratory birds (候鸟): Migratory birds travel long distances every year. 候鸟每年都要长途跋涉。
