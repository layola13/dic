historys: /ˈhɪstəriz/ | n. | histories（history的复数形式，非标准用法，建议使用histories）| The plural of "history," referring to multiple accounts or narratives of past events.  While "historys" appears occasionally, standard English uses "histories." | He's been reading several historys of the Roman Empire. (Non-standard usage, should be "histories") 他一直在读几本罗马帝国的历史书。| Near Synonyms: chronicles, accounts, narratives | Antonym: future | noun
