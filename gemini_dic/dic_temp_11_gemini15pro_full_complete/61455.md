embowl:/ɪmˈboʊl/| vt.|嵌入，容纳，环抱|To insert or enclose (something) in something else; to embrace.|The inscription was embowed in a laurel wreath. 这段铭文被环绕在月桂花环中。|近义词：enclose, encompass, embed, insert|反义词:extract, remove|词性:动词
词源: From en- + bole, from Old French boele "pod, husk," from Latin bulla "bubble, knob, stud."  The meaning shifted from "enclose in a rounded container" to "enclose generally."
