hyperchlorination:/ˌhaɪpərˌklɔːrɪˈneɪʃən/
noun
过度氯化；超氯化
The process of adding a larger than normal amount of chlorine to water, typically for disinfection or to eliminate undesirable tastes and odors.
The city ordered hyperchlorination of the water supply after the bacterial contamination.  该市在细菌污染后下令对供水进行过度氯化。
Hyperchlorination is sometimes used to control biofouling in industrial cooling systems. 有时会使用过度氯化来控制工业冷却系统中的生物污染。
chlorination, superchlorination
dechlorination
