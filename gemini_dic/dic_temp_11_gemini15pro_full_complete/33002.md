chorion: /ˈkɔːriɒn/ (UK) /ˈkɔːriɑːn/ (US)| n. |绒毛膜|The outermost membrane surrounding an embryo of a reptile, bird, or mammal. In mammals (including humans), it contributes to the formation of the placenta.|The chorion plays a vital role in the development of the placenta. 绒毛膜在胎盘的发育中起着至关重要的作用。

The doctor examined the chorion for abnormalities. 医生检查了绒毛膜是否有异常。|近义词:serosa (浆膜- 更广义)|反义词: None (没有直接的反义词)|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek  χόριον (khórion, “membrane enclosing the fetus”), from  χόριος (khórios, “skin, hide”).
