idles:/ˈaɪdlz/ | v. | 闲逛，无所事事；（发动机）空转 | spends time doing nothing or very little; (of an engine) to run slowly while disconnected from a load  | The car idled at the traffic lights. 汽车在红灯前空转。 |  近义词：loaf, laze, dawdle | 反义词: work, busy | verb (third-person singular simple present idles, present participle idling, simple past and past participle idled)


idle:/ˈaɪdl/ | adj. | 懒惰的，无所事事的；闲置的；无用的 | lazy and unwilling to work; not working or being used; without any particular purpose  | An idle mind is the devil's workshop.  懒汉的头脑是魔鬼的工场。| 近义词：lazy, inactive, unemployed | 反义词: busy, active, employed | adjective


idle:/ˈaɪdl/ | n. |  (usually idles) The state of being idle; a period of inactivity.  |  The factory has too much idle time because of the shortage of materials. 由于材料短缺，工厂的闲置时间太长了。 | 近义词：inactivity, laziness, down time | 反义词: activity, work, business | noun
