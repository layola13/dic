ichthyopsida:/ɪkθɪˈɒpsɪdə/ | n. | 鱼形类，鱼纲 | A class of vertebrates comprising the fishes.  This term is outdated and no longer used in modern taxonomy. |  例句：Ichthyopsida was formerly used to group fishes and amphibians. 鱼形类以前被用来将鱼类和两栖类动物归为一组。 | 近义词：fish, Pisces (more modern) | 反义词: tetrapoda (four-limbed vertebrates) | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Greek *ikhthys* (fish) + *opsis* (appearance)
