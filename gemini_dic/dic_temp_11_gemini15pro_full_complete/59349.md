early:/ˈɜːrli/ (US) /ˈɜːli/ (UK)| adj. adv.|早的，早期的；提早|happening or done before the usual or expected time|
The early bird catches the worm. 早起的鸟儿有虫吃。|
She woke up early this morning. 她今天早上醒得很早。|
近义词：soon, beforehand, premature|
反义词：late, tardy|
词性：形容词，副词
