argilliferous:/ˌɑːrdʒɪˈlɪfərəs/| adj.|含粘土的，富含粘土的|Containing clay; consisting of or abounding in clay. |The soil in this region is argilliferous and therefore good for pottery. 这个地区的土壤富含粘土，因此适合制陶。|The geologist examined the argilliferous rocks. 地质学家检查了富含粘土的岩石。|近义词：clayey, argillaceous|反义词: sandy, non-clayey|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *argilla* (clay) + *-fer* (bearing, carrying)  < Latin *ferre* (to carry, bear).
