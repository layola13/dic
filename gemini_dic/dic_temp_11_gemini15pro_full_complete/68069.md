faddiest:/ˈfædiɪst/
adjective
最流行的；最时髦的；最时尚的
Superlative form of faddy: most faddy.  Given to following fads.
例句：She always wants the faddiest new phone. 她总是想要最新潮的手机。

例句：That store sells only the faddiest clothes. 那家商店只卖最流行的衣服。

近义词：trendiest, most fashionable, latest
反义词：classic, timeless, traditional
词性：adjective
