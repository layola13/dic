nonaccidental:/ˌnɒnˌæksɪˈdɛntəl/ | adj. | 非偶然的；故意的；蓄意的 | Not occurring by chance; deliberate. | 例句：The police are treating the child's injuries as nonaccidental. 警方认为孩子的伤势并非意外。| 例句：Nonaccidental injuries are a serious problem. 非意外伤害是一个严重的问题。 | 近义词：deliberate, intentional, purposeful, willful | 反义词：accidental, unintentional, chance, fortuitous | 词性：形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non "not."
accidental (adjective): From Late Latin accidentalis "happening by chance," from Latin accidens (genitive accidentis) "a chance, accident," present participle of accidere "to happen," from ad "to" + cadere "to fall."
