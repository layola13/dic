folklorists:/ˌfəʊkˈlɔːrɪsts/ | n. |民俗学家|Experts who study folklore, which includes traditional customs, beliefs, stories, songs, and practices of a people.|Folklorists often travel to remote areas to collect stories and songs from local communities. 民俗学家经常前往偏远地区收集当地社区的故事和歌曲。|Synonyms:  ethnologists, anthropologists|Antonyms: None|Noun

folklorist:/ˌfəʊkˈlɔːrɪst/ | n. |民俗学家|An expert in or student of folklore.|She is a renowned folklorist specializing in the oral traditions of Native American tribes. 她是一位著名的民俗学家，专门研究美洲原住民部落的口头传统。|Synonyms:  ethnologist, anthropologist|Antonyms: None|Noun
