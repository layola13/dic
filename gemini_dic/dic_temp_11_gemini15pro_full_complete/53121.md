diplodocus:/dɪˈplɒdəkəs/ /daɪˈplɑːdəkəs/| n. |梁龙（一种巨大的草食性恐龙）|A large herbivorous dinosaur with a long neck and tail that lived in the Jurassic period.  It's one of the longest dinosaurs known.|The Diplodocus could reach lengths of over 90 feet. 梁龙可以达到90英尺以上。|The museum's centerpiece is a complete Diplodocus skeleton.  博物馆的核心展品是一具完整的梁龙骨架。|近义词：sauropod|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

diplodocus:|/dɪˈplɒdəkəs/ /daɪˈplɑːdəkəs/| n. |梁龙|From Greek  *diplóos* "double" + *dokos* "beam", referring to the double-beamed chevron bones located in the underside of the tail.  These bones presumably provided support for the tail and prevented blood vessels from being crushed.|例句：The Diplodocus is known for its extremely long neck and tail. 梁龙以其极长的脖子和尾巴而闻名。|例句：Scientists believe the Diplodocus used its long tail as a whip for defense. 科学家认为梁龙用它长长的尾巴作为鞭子来防御。|近义词：sauropod|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
