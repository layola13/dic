bradshaw:/ˈbrɔːdʃɔː/| n.|布拉德肖，（尤指）布拉德肖铁路时刻表|A Bradshaw is a railway timetable, especially a comprehensive one, formerly published by W.J. Adams and now by Middleton Press.  It is particularly associated with the Victorian and Edwardian eras.|I need to consult a Bradshaw to find the next train to York.|近义词:timetable, schedule, railway guide|反义词:None|noun
