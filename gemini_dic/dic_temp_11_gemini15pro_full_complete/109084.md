legation:/lɪˈɡeɪʃn/
n.
外交使团；使节馆舍
A diplomatic mission headed by a minister.  A group of representatives sent by one government to another. The official residence or offices of a diplomatic minister.
The French legation in London issued a statement. 法国驻伦敦使馆发表了一份声明。
The legation was closed after the two countries broke off diplomatic relations.  两国断交后，使馆关闭了。

近义词:embassy, mission, consulate
反义词:None.
词性:noun
