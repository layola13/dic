devisings:/dɪˈvaɪzɪŋz/
n.
计划，设计；发明；(复数)策略，发明物
plans, schemes, or inventions that someone has devised.
The devisings of the marketing team were ingenious.  市场营销团队的策划非常巧妙。
Synonyms: inventions, creations, innovations, designs, schemes, plans, contrivances, machinations
Antonyms:  discoveries (arguably, as devisings are often created, while discoveries are found)
noun

devise:/dɪˈvaɪz/
v.
设计；发明；策划；想出
plan or invent (a complex procedure, system, or mechanism) by careful thought.
He devised a clever plan to escape. 他想出了一个巧妙的逃跑计划。
Synonyms:  invent, create, design, formulate, concoct, contrive, engineer, develop, originate
Antonyms:  discover, find, stumble upon, copy, imitate
verb
