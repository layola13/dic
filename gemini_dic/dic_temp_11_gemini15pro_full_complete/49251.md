deletion:/dɪˈliːʃən/ | n. |缺失；遗漏 |The deletion of a crucial sentence changed the meaning of the entire paragraph.  一个关键句的缺失改变了整个段落的含义。| omission, absence | inclusion, presence | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dēlētiōn-, from Latin dēlēre "to destroy, blot out,"  from de- "down, completely" + lēre "to smear, blot out."
