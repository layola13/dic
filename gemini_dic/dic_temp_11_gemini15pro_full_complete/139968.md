odontornithes:/ˌoʊdɒnˈtɔːrnɪθiːz/
n.
齿鸟类
An extinct group of toothed birds from the Cretaceous period, comprising the orders Hesperornithiformes and Ichthyornithiformes.
The Odontornithes, including Hesperornis and Ichthyornis, represent an important stage in avian evolution.  齿鸟类，包括黄昏鸟和鱼鸟，代表了鸟类进化史上的一个重要阶段。
The discovery of Odontornithes fossils provided crucial evidence for the evolution of birds from reptilian ancestors. 齿鸟类化石的发现为鸟类从爬行动物祖先进化而来提供了关键证据。
近义词: toothed birds, prehistoric birds
反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider) modern birds
词性: noun
