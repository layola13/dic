balkanism: /ˈbɔːlkənɪzəm/ | n. | 巴尔干化，巴尔干主义 | The fragmentation of a region or organization into smaller, often hostile, units.  A situation of hostility and rivalry among the Balkan states. | The collapse of Yugoslavia led to balkanism in the region.  南斯拉夫的崩溃导致了该地区的巴尔干化。| fragmentation, division, factionalism | unity, integration | noun

balkanize: /ˈbɔːlkənaɪz/ | vt. | 使巴尔干化，分裂 | To divide (a region or organization) into smaller, often hostile, units. | Political rivalries threaten to balkanize the country. 政治对抗有可能使这个国家分裂。| divide, fragment, partition | unite, integrate | verb
