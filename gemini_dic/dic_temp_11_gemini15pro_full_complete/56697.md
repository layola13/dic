doomer:/ˈduːmər/| n.|末日论者，悲观主义者|A person who believes that the world is doomed and that societal collapse or some other catastrophic event is inevitable.|例句：He's such a doomer, always talking about the end of the world. 他真是个末日论者，总是谈论世界末日。

例句：She became a doomer after reading too many dystopian novels.  读了太多反乌托邦小说后，她变成了一个末日论者。|近义词：pessimist, defeatist|反义词:optimist|词性:名词
