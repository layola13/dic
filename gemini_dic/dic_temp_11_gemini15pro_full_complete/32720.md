chloriodic:/ˌklɔːrɪˈɑːdɪk/ | adj. | 氯碘的，含氯和碘的 | Containing or relating to both chlorine and iodine. | Chlorine and iodine react to form the interhalogen compound, choriodic acid. 氯和碘反应生成卤素互化物，氯碘酸。| 近义词: iodine chloride (as a compound) | 反义词:  None | 词性: adjective
