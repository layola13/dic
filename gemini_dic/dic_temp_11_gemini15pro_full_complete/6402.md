amphipoda:/æmˈfɪpədə/| n.|端足目动物，片脚类动物|An order of small, laterally compressed crustaceans, typically marine, but also found in freshwater and terrestrial environments. They are characterized by their lack of a carapace and their distinct body segments.|例句：Amphipods are a crucial part of the marine food web. 端足目动物是海洋食物网的重要组成部分。

例句：Some amphipods are parasitic, living on other marine organisms.  一些端足目动物是寄生的，生活在其他海洋生物身上。|近义词：scud, sideswimmer|反义词:None|词性:noun

amphipod:/ˈæmfɪpɒd/| adj.|端足目的|Relating to or belonging to the order Amphipoda.|例句：Amphipod crustaceans are an important food source for many fish. 端足目甲壳类动物是许多鱼类的重要食物来源。

例句：The study focused on the amphipod community in the intertidal zone. 这项研究的重点是潮间带的端足目动物群落。|近义词：None|反义词:None|词性:adjective
