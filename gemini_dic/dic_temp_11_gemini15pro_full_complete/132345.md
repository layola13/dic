nondegradable:/ˌnɒndɪˈɡreɪdəbl/ |adj.|不可降解的|Not able to be broken down or decomposed by natural processes, especially by bacteria.|Plastic bags are nondegradable and contribute to environmental pollution. 塑料袋是不可降解的，会导致环境污染。|例句：Many plastics are nondegradable, posing a threat to the environment. 许多塑料是不可降解的，对环境构成威胁。

例句：The nondegradable waste will remain in landfills for centuries. 不可降解的垃圾将在垃圾填埋场中保留数个世纪。|近义词：non-biodegradable, persistent, indestructible|反义词：biodegradable, degradable, compostable|词性：adjective
