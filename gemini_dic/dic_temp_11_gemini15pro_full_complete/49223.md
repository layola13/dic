delegatee:/ˌdɛlɪˈɡiː/
n.
被委托人，代表
A person to whom authority or power is delegated.
The delegatee will represent the company at the conference.  被委托人将代表公司出席会议。

The delegatee was tasked with presenting the proposal.  被委托人负责提交提案。

近义词：representative, agent, deputy, assignee
反义词：delegator
词性：名词
