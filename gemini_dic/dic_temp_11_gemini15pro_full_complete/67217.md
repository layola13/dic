expressor:/ɪkˈsprɛsər/| n.|表达器，表达者，表示者|Something that expresses something else, often a gene or protein that allows the manifestation of a trait.  It can also refer to a person or thing that expresses something.|The gene is an expressor of eye color.|近义词：indicator, representative, manifestor|反义词:suppressor|noun
