lacustral:/ləˈkʌstrəl/
adjective
湖泊的；湖成的
of, relating to, or associated with lakes
例句：The scientists studied the lacustral deposits to learn about the region's past climate. 科学家们研究了湖泊沉积物，以了解该地区过去的气候。

例句：Lacustral ecosystems are delicate and easily disrupted by pollution. 湖泊生态系统非常脆弱，容易受到污染的破坏。
近义词：lake, limnetic
反义词：marine, oceanic
词性: adjective
