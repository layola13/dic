beadsnake:/ˈbiːdsneɪk/| n.|串珠蛇（一种无毒蛇）|A small, harmless snake, typically brightly colored and often resembling a string of beads. It is native to North and South America.|The beadsnake is nocturnal and feeds primarily on insects.|近义词:worm snake, threadsnake|反义词: (无明确反义词，可以考虑venomous snake 毒蛇作为对比)|名词
