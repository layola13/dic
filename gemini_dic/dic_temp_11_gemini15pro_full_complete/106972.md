lackeys:/ˈlækiz/ | n. | 仆从，走狗，奴才 | A servile follower; someone who behaves obsequiously to someone in power. | The dictator surrounded himself with lackeys who would do his bidding without question.  独裁者身边都是些唯命是从的走狗。

The wealthy businessman dismissed his lackeys with a wave of his hand.  这位富商挥了挥手，打发走了他的仆从。| 近义词: minions, flunkies, sycophants, underlings, subordinates | 反义词: leader, boss, superior, master | 词性: 名词
