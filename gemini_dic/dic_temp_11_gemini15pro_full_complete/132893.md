nonelectric:/ˌnɒnɪˈlɛktrɪk/
adjective
非电的；不使用电的
Not operated by electricity; not electric.
例句：
They use a nonelectric can opener.
他们使用手动开罐器。

例句：
The Amish community often uses nonelectric appliances.
阿米什社区经常使用非电器。

近义词：manual, mechanical, hand-operated
反义词：electric, electrical, powered
词性：adjective
