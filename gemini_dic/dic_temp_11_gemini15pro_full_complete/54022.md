discursion:/dɪˈskɜːrʒən/ | n. | 漫谈，离题，讨论 | A digression or rambling discourse.  A turning aside (from the main subject). |  The professor's lecture was filled with discursions on various unrelated topics. 教授的讲座里充满了对各种无关主题的漫谈。 | Example: After a brief discursio,n on the merits of different types of coffee, they returned to the main topic.  在简短地讨论了不同种类咖啡的优点之后，他们又回到了主题。| Synonyms: digression, ramble, excursion, deviation | Antonyms: focus, concentration, conciseness | noun
