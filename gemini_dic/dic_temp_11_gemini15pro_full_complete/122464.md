mobilizer:/ˈmoʊbəˌlaɪzər/ (US) /ˈməʊbɪˌlaɪzə(r)/ (UK)| n.|动员者；动员器|a person or thing that mobilizes something or someone.  Something that helps to organize a group of people to achieve a specific purpose.|The community organizer was a skilled mobilizer, bringing together diverse groups to fight for affordable housing.|近义词：activist, organizer, campaigner, catalyst|反义词：suppressor, inhibitor|noun

mobilize:/ˈmoʊbəˌlaɪz/ (US) /ˈməʊbɪˌlaɪz/ (UK)| v.|动员，调动|to organize and prepare something, such as a group of people, for a particular purpose.|The government mobilized the army for war.|近义词：assemble, rally, marshal, activate|反义词：demobilize, disband|verb
