mortgaging:/ˈmɔːrɡɪdʒɪŋ/
v.
抵押；以…作抵押
the action of mortgaging something.
They are mortgaging their home to start a business. 他们正在抵押他们的房子来创业。
近义词: pledging, securing
反义词: redeeming
词性: 动名词 (gerund)
