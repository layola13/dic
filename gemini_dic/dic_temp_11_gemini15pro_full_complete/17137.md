batty: /ˈbæti/
adjective
古怪的；疯狂的
Characterized by eccentricity or irrationality.
He has some batty ideas about the government. 他对政府有一些疯狂的想法。
Near Synonyms: eccentric, odd, quirky, crazy, lunatic
Antonyms: sane, normal
词性: 形容词


Etymology:  From "bat" (meaning a nocturnal flying mammal) + "-y" (adjective suffix). Originally meaning "like a bat," it evolved to mean "crazy" due to the perceived erratic flight and nocturnal habits of bats.
