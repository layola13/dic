bejewelled:/bɪˈdʒuːəld/ /biˈdʒuːəld/| adj.|镶满珠宝的；华丽的|Adorned with jewels; richly ornamented. |The bejewelled crown glittered under the lights.  镶满珠宝的皇冠在灯光下闪闪发光。|The actress wore a bejewelled gown to the premiere.  这位女演员穿着镶满珠宝的礼服出席了首映式。|近义词：ornamented, decorated, jeweled|反义词:plain, simple|词性:形容词
Etymology: be- + jewel + -ed.  "Be-" is a prefix meaning "thoroughly" or "all over." "-ed" is a suffix forming adjectives from nouns.
