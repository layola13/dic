affixes:/ˈæfɪksɪz/ | n. | 词缀 |  A morpheme or meaningful unit that is attached to the beginning (prefix) or end (suffix) of a word to modify its meaning. |  She studied the affixes used in medical terminology. 她研究了医学术语中使用的词缀。| synonyms: additions, attachments, supplements | antonyms: roots, bases, stems | noun


affix:/əˈfɪks/ | v. | 附加；贴上 | To attach or add something to something else.  | He affixed a stamp to the envelope. 他在信封上贴了一张邮票。 | synonyms: attach, add, append, stick, fasten | antonyms: detach, remove, separate | verb
