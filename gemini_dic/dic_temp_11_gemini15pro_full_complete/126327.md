myopism:/ˈmaɪəˌpɪzəm/| n.|近视；目光短浅|Nearsightedness; lack of foresight or long-term planning.  A condition in which close objects appear clearly, but objects farther away appear blurred.|Example 1: His myopism prevented him from seeing the bigger picture. 他的目光短浅使他无法看到全局。

Example 2:  The government's myopism in cutting funding for education will have long-term negative consequences. 政府削减教育经费的短视行为将产生长期的负面影响。|近义词：shortsightedness, nearsightedness, narrow-mindedness|反义词：foresight, farsightedness, prescience|词性:名词
