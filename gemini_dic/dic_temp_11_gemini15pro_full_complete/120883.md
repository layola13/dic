misbrewing:/ˌmɪsˈbruːɪŋ/
|n.| 酿造失败; 酿造不好 |The misbrewing resulted in a sour-tasting beer. 酿造失败导致啤酒有酸味。|
|n.|  (比喻) 坏主意; 阴谋; 灾祸 |There's some misbrewing afoot, I can feel it. 我能感觉到有什么坏事正在酝酿。|
|词源|From mis- (badly) + brewing (the process of making beer)|
|近义词|failure, botch, fiasco, blunder (for the literal sense); plot, scheme, conspiracy (for the figurative sense)|
|反义词|success, triumph (for the literal sense);  harmony, peace (for the figurative sense)|
