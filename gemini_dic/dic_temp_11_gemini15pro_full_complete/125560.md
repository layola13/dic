multitasker:/ˌmʌltiˈtæskər/ | n. | 多任务处理者 | A person who can perform multiple tasks simultaneously. |  She's a great multitasker, handling phone calls and emails while writing a report. 她是一位出色的多任务处理者，可以一边写报告一边接电话和处理电子邮件。| synonym: multi-processor, polymath | antonym: single-tasker, specialist | noun

multitask:/ˌmʌltiˈtæsk/ | v. | 多任务处理 | To perform multiple tasks at the same time. |  He tried to multitask, but ended up making mistakes on both projects. 他试图同时处理多个任务，但最终两个项目都出错了。| synonym: juggle, manage several things at once | antonym: focus on one task | verb

multitasking:/ˌmʌltiˈtɑːskɪŋ/ | n. | 多任务处理 | The act of performing multiple tasks simultaneously. | Multitasking can be efficient, but it can also lead to decreased focus. 多任务处理可能很有效率，但也可能导致注意力下降。| synonym: concurrent processing, parallel processing | antonym: single-tasking, serial processing | noun
