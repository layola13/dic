epidemiological: /ˌɛpɪˌdiːmiəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ (British) /ˌɛpɪˌdiːmiəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/ (American)| adj.| 流行病学的|Relating to the branch of medicine which deals with the incidence, distribution, and possible control of diseases and other factors relating to health.| Epidemiological studies have shown a link between smoking and lung cancer. 流行病学研究表明吸烟和肺癌之间存在联系。|近义词：epidemic, pandemic |反义词: N/A |词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From epi- (upon, among) + demos (people) + -logy (study of) + -ical (relating to).  The word ultimately derives from Greek.
