monocormic:/ˌmɒnəˈkɔːrmɪk/ | adj. | 单茎的；单轴的 | Having a single stem or axis; unbranched.  Relating to or characterized by a growth habit in which the plant produces a single, unbranched stem. | This orchid species is known for its monocormic growth habit.  这种兰花以其单茎的生长习性而闻名。| Synonym: unbranched, single-stemmed | Antonym: branched, multi-stemmed | adjective
