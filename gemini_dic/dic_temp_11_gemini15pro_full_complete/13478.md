atrophying:/ˈætrəfiɪŋ/ /ˈætrəfaɪɪŋ/
| v. |萎缩；衰退|  gradually declining in effectiveness or vigor due to underuse or neglect.|  His muscles were atrophying from lack of use.  他的肌肉因为缺乏使用而萎缩了。|  deteriorating, weakening, diminishing, shrinking, wasting away| developing, growing, strengthening| verb

atrophy: /ˈætrəfi/ /ˈætrəfɪ/
| n. |萎缩；衰退 | the process of gradually declining in effectiveness or vigor due to underuse or neglect. | The atrophy of her skills was evident after years of inactivity. 她多年不活动后，技能明显衰退。| deterioration, decline, weakening, degeneration| growth, development, strengthening| noun
