Etymology:
From Old English *Niwe Castell "new castle", referring to a castle built by Robert Curthose in 1080. "upon Tyne" distinguishes it from other Newcastles. 源于古英语 *Niwe Castell “新城堡”，指的是1080年由罗伯特·柯索斯建造的一座城堡。“upon Tyne”将其与其他纽卡斯尔区分开来。
