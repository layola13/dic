complicated:/ˈkɒmplɪkeɪtɪd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmplɪkeɪtɪd/ (US)| adj.|复杂的，难懂的|Involving many different and confusing aspects.|The instructions were so complicated I couldn't figure them out.  说明书太复杂了，我看不懂。|近义词：complex, intricate, involved|反义词：simple, easy, straightforward|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin complicatus, past participle of complicare "to fold together," from com- "together" + plicare "to fold."
