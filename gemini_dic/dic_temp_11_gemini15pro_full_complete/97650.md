inheritrixes:/ɪnˈherɪtrɪksɪz/
n.
女继承人(复数)
A female inheritor, especially of property or a title.
The inheritrixes of the vast fortune were unprepared for the responsibility. 这些巨额财产的女继承人对这份责任毫无准备。
The two inheritrixes shared the family estate equally.  两位女继承人平分了家族的遗产。
近义词:heiresses
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps disinherited individuals)
词性: Noun
