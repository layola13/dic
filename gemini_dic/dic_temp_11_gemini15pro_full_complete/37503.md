commodate:/əˈkɒmədeɪt/ (UK) /əˈkɑːmədeɪt/ (US)| v.| 容纳；提供住宿；适应；顾及；|To supply something needed; to fit in with the wishes or needs of.|We can accommodate your request for a later delivery date. 我们可以满足你延迟交货日期的请求。

She tried to accommodate her husband's unusual sleeping habits. 她试图适应她丈夫不同寻常的睡眠习惯。|近义词：supply, furnish, comply with, satisfy|反义词：deny, refuse, frustrate|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin accommodatus, past participle of accommodare "to make fit, adapt," from Latin ad- "to" + commodare "to lend, make fit, be advantageous," from commodus "suitable, convenient, proper" (see commodious).
