bird-ringer:/ˈbɜːrd rɪŋər/ | n. | 鸟类环志员 | A person who catches wild birds, puts identification rings on their legs, and releases them for tracking and research purposes. |  例句：The bird-ringer carefully attached a small metal band to the bird's leg. 鸟类环志员小心翼翼地把一个小金属环套在鸟的腿上。

|例句：Bird-ringers contribute valuable data to ornithological studies. 鸟类环志员为鸟类学研究提供了宝贵的数据。| 近义词：ornithologist (more broadly), bander | 反义词: N/A | 词性: Noun


bird-ringing:/ˈbɜːrd rɪŋɪŋ/ | n. | 鸟类环志 | The activity of catching wild birds to attach identification rings to their legs for research purposes. | 例句：Bird-ringing helps scientists understand bird migration patterns. 鸟类环志帮助科学家了解鸟类的迁徙模式。
