denying:/dɪˈnaɪɪŋ/
v.
否认；拒绝；拒绝承认；拒绝给予
Refusing to admit the truth or existence of something; refusing to give something requested or needed.
He denied knowing anything about the robbery. 他否认对这起抢劫案知情。
synonyms: contradicting, rejecting, refusing, disavowing
antonyms: admitting, acknowledging, accepting, confessing
verb

denying:/dɪˈnaɪɪŋ/
adj.
否定的；拒绝的；克制的
Characterized by or expressing denial; refusing to satisfy a desire or need.
Her denying attitude made it difficult to have an open conversation. 她否定的态度使得很难进行坦诚的对话。
synonyms: negative, refusing, restrictive, austere
antonyms: positive, accepting, permissive, indulgent
adjective
