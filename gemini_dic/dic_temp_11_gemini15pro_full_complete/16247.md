banteng:/ˈbæntɛŋ/ | n. | 爪哇野牛; banteng | A wild ox (Bos javanicus) found in Southeast Asia. |  The banteng is a close relative of domestic cattle. 爪哇野牛是家牛的近亲。|  Banteng are often hunted for their meat and horns. 爪哇野牛经常因为它们的肉和角而被猎杀。 | 近义词: wild ox, gaur | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "domestic cattle" as a contrasting term) | 词性: noun
