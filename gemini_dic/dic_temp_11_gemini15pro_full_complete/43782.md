cremating:/krɪˈmeɪtɪŋ/
v.
火化，焚化
The process of burning a dead body to ashes.
They are cremating the body tomorrow.  他们明天将火化遗体。
The family chose to cremate him rather than bury him.  这家人选择火化他而不是土葬。
近义词: incinerating, burning
反义词: burying, interring
词性: verb (present participle of "cremate")

cremate:/krɪˈmeɪt/
v.
火化，焚化
To reduce (a dead body) to ashes by fire.
His last wish was to be cremated. 他最后的愿望是被火化。
Most Hindus cremate their dead. 大多数印度教徒火化他们的死者。
近义词: incinerate, burn
反义词: bury, inter
词性: verb

Etymology:
From Latin *cremātus*, past participle of *cremō* (“I burn”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱerH-* (“to burn, glow”).
