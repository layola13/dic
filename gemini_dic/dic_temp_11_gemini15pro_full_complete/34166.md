clamjamphrie:/ˌklæmˈdʒæmfəri/
noun
喧闹的聚会；吵闹的活动；混乱的一群人
a noisy, chaotic gathering or group of people, often involving drinking and revelry.
例句：The party was a real clamjamphrie, with people dancing and shouting everywhere.  派对真是热闹非凡，到处都是载歌载舞、喧闹的人群。

例句：The student dorm turned into a clamjamphrie after the football game.  足球赛结束后，学生宿舍变成了一个喧闹的场所。
synonym:  shindig, bash, uproar, commotion, free-for-all
antonym: quiet gathering, peaceful assembly
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain, possibly from Scots.  First recorded in the mid-19th century.  Likely imitative of a confused noise or chatter.  起源不确定，可能来自苏格兰语。首次记录于 19 世纪中叶。可能是模仿混乱的噪音或喋喋不休的声音。
