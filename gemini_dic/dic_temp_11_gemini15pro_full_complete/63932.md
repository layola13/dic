epipodite:/ˈɛpɪpəˌdaɪt/| n. |外肢, 上肢; 侧足叶|A flattened, usually lateral, appendage or lobe on the protopodite of an appendage of a crustacean, often functioning as a gill.|The epipodite of the crab's leg helps it to breathe underwater.  螃蟹腿上的外肢帮助它在水下呼吸。|Synonym: branchial plate, gill appendage|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, potentially the main body of the appendage)|noun
