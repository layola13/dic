deductions:/dɪˈdʌkʃənz/| n.|扣除; 扣除额; 推论; 演绎| the act or process of taking away an amount from a total; the act or process of reaching a conclusion about something from known facts or evidence|例句：Tax deductions for charitable donations.  慈善捐款的税收减免。

例句：He arrived at the solution by a series of logical deductions. 他通过一系列逻辑推理得出了解决方案。|近义词：inference, conclusion, subtraction, abatement|反义词: additions, increases|词性:noun
