gluon:/ˈɡluːɒn/ (US) /ˈɡluːɒn/ (UK)|n.|胶子|An elementary particle that acts as the exchange particle for the strong force between quarks, holding them together to form hadrons such as protons and neutrons.  It is analogous to the photon, which mediates the electromagnetic force.|Gluons are responsible for the strong interaction that binds quarks together. 胶子负责将夸克结合在一起的强相互作用。

The theory of quantum chromodynamics describes the interaction of quarks and gluons. 量子色动力学理论描述了夸克和胶子的相互作用。|近义词: exchange particle 交换粒子|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

词源: From *glue* + *-on* (suffix forming nouns denoting subatomic particles), by analogy with *photon*.
