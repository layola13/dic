overactivate: /ˌoʊvərˈæktɪveɪt/ | v. | 过度激活，过度活化 | To make something excessively active; to stimulate or excite beyond normal levels. | Stress can overactivate the immune system. 压力会过度激活免疫系统。 | The constant notifications overactivated her nervous system.  持续不断的通知过度刺激了她的神经系统。 |  近义词：overstimulate, overexcite | 反义词：suppress, inhibit | 词性: 动词

词源: over- + activate.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much."  "Activate" comes from the Late Latin "activatus," past participle of "activare," meaning "to make active."
