expansivities:/ɪkˌspænˈsɪvɪtiz/
n.
膨胀性；膨胀系数；膨胀率
The expansivities of different materials vary greatly.  不同材料的膨胀系数差异很大。
This study focuses on the thermal expansivities of certain metals. 这项研究的重点是某些金属的热膨胀系数。
近义词:expansion coefficients, expansion rates
反义词:contractivities, contraction rates
词性:名词(plural)
