mouthing:/ˈmaʊðɪŋ/ | n. |  做口型，无声地说，言不由衷地说 | The actor was mouthing the words, but the sound was coming from a recording.  演员在做口型，但声音是从录音里传出来的。 | Example: He was mouthing the words to the song. 他正无声地跟着歌曲做口型。 | Synonyms: miming, lip-synching,  | Antonyms: speaking, vocalizing | noun

mouthing:/ˈmaʊðɪŋ/ | adj. | 口头的，言不由衷的 | He gave a mouthing apology, but I could tell he wasn't sincere. 他口头上道了歉，但我看得出他并不真诚。 | Example: Her mouthing concern seemed insincere. 她口头上表示关心，但看起来并不真诚。 | Synonyms: insincere, hypocritical, empty | Antonyms: sincere, genuine, heartfelt | adjective
