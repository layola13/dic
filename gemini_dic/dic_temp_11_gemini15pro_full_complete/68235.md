fairway:/ˈferweɪ/
noun
球道
The mown area between the tee and the green on a golf course.
The golfer hit his drive straight down the fairway.  高尔夫球手一杆将球打到球道正中央。
近义词: course, fairway
反义词: rough, hazard
词性: n.

fairway:/ˈferweɪ/
adjective
公平的,合理的
Impartial and just.
That's not a fairway assessment of the situation. 这对情况的评估不公平。
近义词: just, equitable, impartial, unbiased, objective
反义词: unfair, unjust, biased, prejudiced, partial
词性: adj.

词源:  From Middle English *faire wey*, from Old English *fæger weg* ("fair way, beautiful road"). Equivalent to *fair* + *way*.
