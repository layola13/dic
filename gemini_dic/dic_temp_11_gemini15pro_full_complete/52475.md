dieters:/ˈdaɪətərz/| n.|节食者，减肥者|People on a diet, trying to lose weight.  Those who restrict their food intake for health or weight-loss reasons.|Many dieters find it difficult to stick to their plan. 许多节食者发现很难坚持他们的计划。|synonyms:  slimmers, weight-watchers, reducers|antonyms:  overeaters, gluttons|noun
