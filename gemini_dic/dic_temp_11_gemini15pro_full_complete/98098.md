inquisiturient:/ɪnˌkwɪzɪˈtʊəriənt/| adj.|好奇的，爱打听的|Excessively curious, especially about the affairs of others; inquisitive.|例句：She gave him an inquisiturient look. 她好奇地看了他一眼。

例句：The inquisiturient child asked endless questions.  这个好奇的孩子问个没完没了。|近义词：curious, inquisitive, nosy, prying|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, unconcerned|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin inquisiturus, future active participle of inquirere "to inquire," from in- "into" + quaerere "to seek, ask."  The suffix "-ent" forms adjectives meaning "inclined to, doing, or causing an action."
