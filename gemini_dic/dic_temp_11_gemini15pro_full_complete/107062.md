lacunous:/ləˈkjuːnəs/
adjective
多孔的；有空隙的；不完整的
Characterized by gaps, omissions, or deficiencies; having many cavities or depressions.
The manuscript was lacunous, with several pages missing.  这份手稿残缺不全，缺了好几页。
The student's knowledge of history was lacunous.  这个学生的历史知识很不完整。
synonyms: incomplete, deficient, lacking, fragmented, patchy, porous, perforated, cavity-ridden, holey
antonyms: complete, whole, entire, full, solid, intact, continuous
词性: 形容词
