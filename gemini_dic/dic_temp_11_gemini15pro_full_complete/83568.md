haemapodous:/ˌhiːməˈpɒdəs/| adj.|具血足的，血足动物的|Having feet adapted for wading in blood; specifically (Entomology) of certain mites parasitic on bats: having the terminal segment of the tarsus modified for clinging to the hair of the host.|The haemapodous mite clung tightly to the bat's fur.  这种血足螨紧紧地 clinging to the bat's fur. |近义词:sanguinivorous (blood-feeding) |反义词:None (This is a highly specialized term with no direct opposite.)|词性:adjective
