episodes:/ˈɛpɪˌsoʊdz/ (US), /ˈɛpɪˌsəʊdz/ (UK)| n. |剧集；片段；插曲；事件|A part of a television or radio serial or a novel.  A usually brief period of intense feeling or activity. |
The first few episodes of the new series are a bit slow. 这部新剧集的前几集有点慢。

This was just a brief episode in his long and successful career.  这只是他漫长而成功的职业生涯中的一段小插曲。

The patient suffered a psychotic episode. 患者经历了一次精神病发作。
