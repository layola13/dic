empaistic:/ɪmˈpeɪstɪk/ /ɛmˈpeɪstɪk/
adjective
浓厚的，厚涂的 (绘画)；浮雕似的
Characterized by or done with impasto; thickly laid on.  Relating to the technique of impasto.
The artist used an empaistic technique, applying thick layers of paint to create a textured surface. 艺术家使用了浓厚的绘画技巧，涂上厚厚的颜料来创造一个有纹理的表面。
Near Synonyms:  impastoed, thick, heavy, textured, raised, relief-like
Near Antonyms: thin, flat, smooth, even
词性: adjective
