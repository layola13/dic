gibbers:/ˈdʒɪbərz/| n. |胡言乱语，快速而不连贯的言语|Meaningless or unintelligible talk; rapid, incoherent speech. |He was talking such gibberish that I couldn't understand a word. 他说得语无伦次，我一个字也听不懂。|近义词：babble, nonsense, drivel|反义词: sense, clarity|名词

gibber:/ˈdʒɪbər/| v. |喋喋不休地说，语无伦次地说|To speak rapidly and unintelligibly; to chatter. |The monkey gibbered excitedly at the sight of food. 猴子看到食物兴奋地吱吱叫。|近义词：babble, chatter, prattle|反义词: articulate, enunciate|动词
