overdebilitating: /ˌoʊvər dəˈbɪlɪteɪtɪŋ/
adjective
过度衰弱的；极度使人虚弱的
Excessively weakening; causing extreme debilitation.
The overdebilitating effects of the chemotherapy left her unable to walk. 化疗过度衰弱的影响使她无法行走。

The illness had an overdebilitating effect on him, making even simple tasks impossible.  这种疾病对他产生了极度衰弱的影响，即使是简单的任务也无法完成。

近义词: crippling, incapacitating, debilitating
反义词: invigorating, strengthening, empowering
词性: adjective
