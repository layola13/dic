deglorifies:/diːˈɡlɔːrɪfaɪz/
verb
使失去荣耀; 贬低; 降低…的光辉
to deprive of glory; to reduce in dignity or honor.
The tabloids deglorified the movie star by publishing embarrassing photos of her. 小报刊登了她令人尴尬的照片，使这位电影明星失去了光彩。
Synonyms: dishonor, disgrace, humble, abase, demean
Antonyms: glorify, honor, exalt, elevate
词性: 动词
