desmodont:/ˈdɛzməˌdɑːnt/| adj. n.|缺少齿的，贫齿类动物|Relating to or belonging to the Desmodontidae, a family of New World leaf-nosed bats characterized by specialized teeth for piercing skin and feeding on blood; a member of this family.|Example: The desmodont bat has unique adaptations for its sanguivorous diet.  贫齿蝠对吸血的饮食习惯有独特的适应性。
