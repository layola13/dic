germicide:/ˈdʒɜːrmɪsaɪd/| n.|杀菌剂；消毒剂|A substance or agent that kills germs, especially pathogenic microorganisms.|Hospitals use various germicides to prevent the spread of infection. 医院使用各种杀菌剂来防止感染蔓延。|The janitor disinfected the bathroom with a powerful germicide.  管理员用强力杀菌剂给浴室消毒。|Synonyms: disinfectant, antiseptic, bactericide, microbicide|Antonyms:  None (The opposite would be something that promotes germ growth)|noun

germicidal:/ˌdʒɜːrmɪˈsaɪdəl/| adj.|杀菌的；消毒的|Having the ability to kill germs.|The germicidal properties of bleach make it effective for cleaning. 漂白剂的杀菌特性使其可有效清洁。|The doctor recommended a germicidal soap for handwashing.  医生建议使用杀菌肥皂洗手。|Synonyms:  disinfectant, antiseptic, bactericidal|Antonyms: None (The opposite would be something that promotes germ growth) |adjective
