antipyresis:/ˌæntipaɪˈriːsɪs/
noun
退热，解热
The reduction of fever; the cooling of the body.
Aspirin is commonly used for antipyresis. 阿司匹林通常用于退热。
近义词：fever reduction, defervescence
反义词：pyrexia, fever
词性：noun

antipyresis:/ˌæntipaɪˈriːsɪs/
Etymology:  From Ancient Greek ἀντί (antí, “against”) + πυρετός (puretós, “fever”) + -sis.
词源：来自古希腊语 ἀντί (antí, “反对”) + πυρετός (puretós, “发烧”) + -sis。
