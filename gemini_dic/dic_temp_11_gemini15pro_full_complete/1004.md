acataleptic:/ˌeɪkætəˈlɛptɪk/ | adj. | 不可理解的，不可知的 | Incapable of being grasped by the intellect; incomprehensible.  Relating to acatalepsy, the philosophical doctrine that absolute knowledge is unattainable. | The nature of consciousness is an acataleptic mystery. 意识的本质是一个不可知的谜。 | 近义词: incomprehensible, unknowable, mysterious, inscrutable | 反义词: comprehensible, knowable, understandable | 词性: 形容词
acatalepsy:/ˌeɪkætəˈlɛpsi/ | n. | 不可知论 | The doctrine that absolute or certain knowledge is unattainable. |  His philosophy embraced acatalepsy, arguing that true certainty is beyond human reach. 他的哲学信奉不可知论，认为真正的确定性是人类无法企及的。 | 近义词: skepticism, agnosticism | 反义词: gnosticism | 词性: 名词
