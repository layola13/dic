glycerite:/ˈɡlɪsəˌraɪt/
n.
甘油剂，甘油制剂
A medicinal preparation made by dissolving a medicinal substance in glycerin.
Glycerites are used for various medicinal purposes, often applied topically.  甘油剂用于各种医学用途，通常局部应用。
The pharmacist prepared a glycerite for the patient's cough. 药剂师为病人的咳嗽准备了一种甘油剂。
synonym: glycerin solution, glycerin preparation
antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps a contrasting preparation like an aqueous solution or tincture)
