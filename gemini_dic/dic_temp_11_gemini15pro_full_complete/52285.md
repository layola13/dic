dichroiscope: /daɪˈkrɔɪskəʊp/ | n. | 二色镜，二色性镜 | An instrument used to examine gemstones and minerals to determine their dichroism (the property of exhibiting different colors when viewed in different directions of polarized light). |  This dichroiscope helps gemmologists identify certain gemstones.  这个二色镜帮助宝石学家识别某些宝石。

| Example: Gemologists use a dichroiscope to observe pleochroism in gemstones. 宝石学家使用二色镜观察宝石的多色性。 | Synonym: dichroscope | Antonym: N/A | noun
