nonescalatory:/ˌnɒnˈɛskəˌleɪtəri/ | adj. | 非升级的，不导致局势恶化的 | Not tending to escalate or make a situation worse.  Describes actions or approaches designed to avoid increasing tension or conflict. |  The government took nonescalatory measures to resolve the border dispute. 政府采取了非升级措施来解决边境争端。 |  The ambassador's speech was intentionally nonescalatory. 大使的讲话有意避免局势升级。| 近义词: conciliatory, de-escalatory, pacific | 反义词: escalatory, provocative, inflammatory | 词性: 形容词
