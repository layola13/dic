annalistic:/ˌænəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
编年史的；纪年的
Resembling annals; presented in the form of annals; chronicling events year by year.
Example 1: The historian took an annalistic approach, documenting the events of the reign in chronological order. 这位历史学家采用编年史的方式，按时间顺序记录了统治时期的事件。

Example 2:  The museum's annalistic exhibit showcased artifacts from each year of the city's history.  博物馆的编年史展览展示了城市历史上每年的文物。
synonym:chronicle, historical, chronological
antonym: ahistorical, fictional, fabricated
词性: adjective
