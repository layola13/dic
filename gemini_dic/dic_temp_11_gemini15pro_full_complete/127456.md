nebulosity:/ˌnɛbjʊˈlɒsɪti/ /ˌnɛbjʊˈlɑːsɪti/
n.
模糊不清;星云;朦胧状态
a cloud of gas or dust in space, appearing either bright or dark; a lack of clarity
例句：The nebula's nebulosity made it difficult to observe individual stars. 星云的模糊性使得观测单个恒星变得困难。

例句：There was a certain nebulosity in his explanation, leaving me unsure of what he meant. 他的解释有些含糊不清，让我不确定他的意思。
近义词：haze, mist, vagueness, cloudiness, obscurity
反义词：clarity, clearness, distinctness, precision
词性: 名词
