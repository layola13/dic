headstock:/ˈhɛdstɒk (UK), ˈhɛdˌstɑːk (US)/| n. |琴头，床头箱，主轴箱|The part of a machine tool or other device that supports the main rotating part, such as the spindle of a lathe or the cutting head of a milling machine. In stringed instruments, it refers to the part at the end of the neck that houses the tuning pegs.|The guitar's headstock was intricately carved. 这把吉他的琴头雕刻精美。|Synonyms: head, capstan (for machinery) tuning head (for instruments)|Antonyms: tailstock (for machinery)|noun


headstock:/ˈhɛdstɒk (UK), ˈhɛdˌstɑːk (US)/| n. |床头箱|The headboard of a bed.|The antique bed had a beautifully crafted headstock. 这张古董床的床头箱制作精美。|Synonyms: headboard, bedhead|Antonyms: footboard|noun
