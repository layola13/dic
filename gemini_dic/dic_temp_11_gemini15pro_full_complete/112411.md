loathe:/loʊð/| vt.|厌恶，憎恨|To feel intense dislike or disgust for. |He loathes hypocrisy. 他憎恨虚伪。|近义词:detest, abhor, despise|反义词:love, adore|verb
loathed:/loʊðd/ |adj.| 讨厌的，令人厌恶的 (主要用作过去分词)|Disliked intensely; hated. |The loathed dictator was finally overthrown. 那位令人憎恶的独裁者最终被推翻了。|近义词:detested, abhorred, despised|反义词:loved, adored|adjective
loathsome:/ˈloʊðsəm/| adj.|令人作呕的，讨厌的|Causing hatred or disgust; repulsive.|The smell of the rotting meat was loathsome. 腐肉的气味令人作呕。|近义词:repulsive, disgusting, abhorrent|反义词:pleasant, agreeable|adjective
lothest:/ˈloʊðɪst/ |adj.|最讨厌的，最不喜欢的 (loathe的最高级，古语，现已很少使用) |Most disliked; most unwilling. |Of all his chores, he was lothest to clean the bathroom. 在他所有的家务中，他最不愿意打扫浴室。|近义词:most hated, least favorite|反义词:most loved, favorite|adjective
