choosy:/ˈtʃuːzi/| adj.|挑剔的，讲究的|Fussy or selective in making choices.|She's very choosy about her clothes. 她对衣服很挑剔。|近义词：fussy, selective, picky, discriminating|反义词：undiscriminating, unselective, easygoing|词性:形容词
词源: From choose + -y (suffix forming adjectives).
