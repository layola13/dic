flaking:/ˈfleɪkɪŋ/| n. v.|剥落，脱落；（非正式）爽约，放鸽子|The paint is flaking off the wall.  油漆正从墙上剥落。 He flaked on me last night. 他昨晚放我鸽子了。|近义词：(v.) peeling, chipping, shedding; (informal) bailing, ditching|反义词:adhering, sticking|词性:名词, 动词

flaky:/ˈfleɪki/| adj.|片状的，剥落的；（非正式）古怪的，不靠谱的|The pastry was light and flaky. 这糕点又轻又酥。 He's a bit flaky, so I wouldn't rely on him. 他有点不靠谱，所以我不会依赖他。|近义词:crumbly, brittle, unreliable, eccentric|反义词:stable, reliable, consistent|词性:形容词
