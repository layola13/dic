aliunde:/ˌæliˈʌndeɪ/
adv.
from elsewhere; from a different source
来自别处；来自不同的来源
The evidence must come aliunde.  证据必须来自其他地方。
近义词:externally, from another source
反义词:internally, from within

Etymology:
From Latin *aliunde (“from elsewhere, from another place”), from alius (“other”) + unde (“whence, from which place”).
