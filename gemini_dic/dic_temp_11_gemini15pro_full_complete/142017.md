organizationist:/ˌɔːrɡənɪˈzeɪʃənɪst/
n.|组织主义者；组织论者|Someone who advocates for or specializes in the principles of organization, often in a social or political context.  A person skilled in planning and coordinating activities.|He's a strong organizationist, always focused on structure and efficiency. 他是一位坚定的组织主义者，总是专注于结构和效率。|近义词: organizer, administrator, systematizer|反义词: anarchist, disorganizer|词性: 名词

词源: organization + -ist
