conducted:/kənˈdʌktɪd/
verb
执行，实施，引导，指挥，传导
past tense and past participle of conduct
The orchestra was conducted by a renowned maestro.  这支管弦乐队由一位著名的音乐大师指挥。
He conducted himself with dignity throughout the trial. 在整个审判过程中，他举止庄重。
Synonyms:  managed, directed, led, guided, oversaw, orchestrated
Antonyms:  followed, obeyed, neglected, abandoned
词性: 动词
