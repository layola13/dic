noncircular:/ˌnɒnˈsɜːkjʊlə(r)/ /ˌnɑːnˈsɜːrkjələr/ | adj. | 非圆形的，非循环的 | Not circular; not having the form of a circle; not returning to the starting point. | The table is noncircular, it's rectangular.  这张桌子不是圆形的，是长方形的。

The process is noncircular, meaning it doesn't repeat. 这个过程是非循环的，意味着它不会重复。 | 近义词: nonlinear, straight, acyclic | 反义词: circular, cyclic, round | 词性: adjective
