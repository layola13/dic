freckle:/ˈfrɛkəl/ (US), /ˈfrɛkl/ (UK)| n.|雀斑，小斑点|A small, light-brown spot on the skin, often caused by exposure to the sun.|She has a scattering of freckles across her nose. 她的鼻子上散布着一些雀斑。|近义词：speck, spot, blemish|反义词：(No direct opposite, consider "clear skin")|词性:noun

freckle:/ˈfrɛkəl/ (US), /ˈfrɛkl/ (UK)| v.|长雀斑|To become covered with freckles.|Her skin freckles easily in the sun. 她的皮肤在阳光下很容易长雀斑。|近义词：(No direct synonym, consider "develop spots")|反义词：(No direct opposite)|词性:verb

freckled:/ˈfrɛkld/ (US), /ˈfrɛkld/ (UK)| adj.|有雀斑的|Covered with freckles.|He has a freckled face. 他有一张满脸雀斑的脸。|近义词：spotted|反义词：(No direct opposite, consider "unblemished")|词性:adjective
