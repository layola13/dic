metastannic:/ˌmɛtəˈstænɪk/ | adj. |偏锡酸的；变锡酸的|Relating to or designating a less soluble form of stannic acid, probably a polymerized form of the ordinary acid.|Metastannic acid is formed by the action of nitric acid on tin.  偏锡酸是由硝酸作用于锡而形成的。| The metastannic acid precipitated out of the solution. 偏锡酸从溶液中沉淀出来。 | 近义词: stannic (in some contexts) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially the regular form of stannic acid depending on context) | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
meta- (Greek: "after," "changed," "higher") + stannic (from Latin stannum, "tin")
