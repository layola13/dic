forefend:/fɔːrˈfɛnd/ | v. | 预防，阻止，避开 | To avert, prevent, or ward off (something evil or unpleasant). |  例句：She prayed to God to forefend such a disaster. 她向上帝祈祷，希望阻止这样的灾难发生。 | 近义词：prevent, avert, obviate, preclude | 反义词：cause, incite, provoke | 词性：动词

forefend:/fɔːrˈfɛnd/ | v. | 保护，守护 | To protect or guard. | 例句：May God forefend us from harm. 愿上帝保佑我们免受伤害。 | 近义词：protect, guard, defend, shield | 反义词：endanger, expose | 词性：动词


词源:
From Middle English *forefenden, from Old English forfēndan (“to forbid, prohibit, avert, defend against”), from for- + fēndan (“to put off, turn away”).  Cognate with Old Frisian forfenda (“to forbid”), Old Saxon farfendon (“to avert, prevent”), Old High German firfentan (“to forbid”), German verfeinden (“to alienate”).
