flavonoid:/ˈfleɪvənɔɪd/| n. |类黄酮；黄酮类化合物|A large group of plant pigments, many of which are antioxidants. They are responsible for many of the colors in fruits and vegetables.|例句：Flavonoids are thought to have health benefits.  类黄酮被认为对健康有益。

例句：Many fruits and vegetables are rich in flavonoids. 许多水果和蔬菜富含类黄酮。|近义词：bioflavonoid|反义词: (No direct antonym exists) |词性:noun
