cellphone:/ˈsɛlˌfoʊn/ (US) /ˈsɛlˌfəʊn/ (UK)| n. |手机|A portable telephone that can make and receive calls over a radio frequency link while the user is moving within a telephone service area.|I lost my cellphone yesterday. 我昨天丢了手机。|近义词：mobile phone, cellular phone, smartphone, handset|反义词: landline, telephone (座机 - not a true opposite, but a contrasting fixed communication device)|noun
