malonate: /ˈmæləneɪt/ | n. |丙二酸盐；丙二酸酯|A salt or ester of malonic acid. |Malonate inhibits succinate dehydrogenase in the Krebs cycle. 丙二酸盐抑制克雷布斯循环中的琥珀酸脱氢酶。|Synonym: propanedioate |Antonym: None |noun


malonate: /ˈmæləneɪt/ | adj. |丙二酸的|Relating to or derived from malonic acid. |Malonate derivatives are used in organic synthesis. 丙二酸衍生物用于有机合成。|Synonym: propanedioic |Antonym: None |adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From malonic acid + -ate. Malonic acid is named after malic acid, from which it was first prepared,  + -ic. Malic acid comes from Latin mālum "apple" because it was originally isolated from apple juice.
