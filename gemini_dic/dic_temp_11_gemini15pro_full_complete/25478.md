bulletproofed:/ˈbʊlɪtˌpruːft/ | adj. |防弹的|Made resistant to bullets; protected against bullets.  Having been made impervious to penetration by bullets.|The president rode in a bulletproofed limousine. 总统乘坐防弹豪华轿车。|The building was bulletproofed after the attack.  袭击之后，这座建筑物被加装了防弹设施。|近义词：armored, reinforced, protected|反义词：vulnerable, unprotected|词性:形容词
词源: bullet (子弹) + proof (证明，防…) + -ed (动词过去分词/形容词后缀),  表示“被做成防弹的”。
