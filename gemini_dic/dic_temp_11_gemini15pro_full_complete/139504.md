obstructiveness:/əbˈstrʌktɪvnəs/
n.
阻碍性；妨碍性；阻塞性
The quality or state of being obstructive.  The tendency to obstruct or hinder progress or activity.
His obstructiveness made it difficult to get anything done. 他的阻挠使得什么事情都难以完成。
The committee was frustrated by the senator's obstructiveness. 委员会对参议员的阻挠行为感到沮丧。
Synonyms: hindrance, obstruction, impedance, resistance
Antonyms: helpfulness, cooperation, assistance, support
