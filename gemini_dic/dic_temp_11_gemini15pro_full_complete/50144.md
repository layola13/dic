denumber:/diːˈnʌmbər/
|v.|减少…的数目; 使…减少|To reduce the number of; to diminish in number.|例句：The plague denumbered the population. 瘟疫使人口减少了。|近义词：decrease, reduce, diminish, lessen|反义词:increase, augment, expand|verb

denumber:/diːˈnʌmbər/
|v.|从…中除去数字; 去除编号|To remove numbers from; to strip of numbers.|例句：The librarian denumbered the old books. 图书管理员从旧书中删除了编号。|近义词：remove, delete, erase|反义词:number, add, label|verb
