butterwort:/ˈbʌtərˌwɜːrt/ (US), /ˈbʌtəwɜːt/ (UK)| n. |捕虫堇|A carnivorous plant with sticky leaves that trap insects.|Butterworts are fascinating carnivorous plants. 捕虫堇是一种引人入胜的食肉植物。|The glistening leaves of the butterwort attracted a fly. 捕虫堇闪闪发光的叶子吸引了一只苍蝇。|近义词：sundew, pitcher plant|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Old English 'buterwyrt', literally "butter plant," because of the buttery feel of the leaves.
