diminishings:/dɪˈmɪnɪʃɪŋz/
|n.|减少；递减；缩小|A gradual lessening or reduction in size, quantity, or importance.  The diminishings of natural resources are a serious concern. 自然资源的减少是一个严重的问题。|近义词:reductions, decreases, declines|反义词:increases, growths, expansions|词性:名词

diminish:/dɪˈmɪnɪʃ/
|v.|减少；缩小；降低|To make or become smaller or less; decrease. The pain will gradually diminish. 疼痛会逐渐减轻。|近义词:reduce, decrease, lessen|反义词:increase, enlarge, expand|词性:动词

diminished:/dɪˈmɪnɪʃt/
|adj.|减少的；缩小的；降低的|Made smaller or less; reduced.  He felt diminished by the experience. 他感觉这次经历让他矮了一截。|近义词:reduced, lessened, decreased|反义词:increased, enlarged, expanded|词性:形容词


词源: From Old French *diminiss-, present participle stem of diminuir, from Latin diminuere "to lessen, diminish," from dis- "apart, away" + minuere "to lessen."
