marmorean:/mɑːrˈmɔːriən/
| adj. |大理石的；似大理石的；冷酷无情的|Made of or resembling marble; smooth, cold, and white like marble; figuratively, cold and unfeeling.|The statue's marmorean skin seemed to glow in the moonlight. 雕像的大理石般的皮肤在月光下闪闪发光。|近义词：marble, stony, cold, impassive|反义词：warm, expressive|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin "marmoreus," from "marmor" (marble). 源自拉丁语“marmoreus”，来自“marmor”（大理石）。
