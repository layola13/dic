aroint:/əˈrɔɪnt/| interj. | 走开；滚开；|Used to repel or drive away, especially witches or evil spirits.  表示驱逐或赶走，尤指女巫或邪灵。|Aroint thee, witch! 滚开，女巫！|近义词：away, begone, depart, get out|反义词:come, approach|感叹词
Etymology: From Middle English *aroynten, from Old English *āryntan, of uncertain origin. 可能源自古英语，具体词源不确定。
