fetz:/fɛts/| n. |碎片，破布，小家伙 (尤指小孩或动物)|A small piece, scrap, or shred, especially of cloth; also, a small lively person or animal. |The child tore the paper into fetzen. 孩子把纸撕成了碎片。|Synonyms: scrap, shred, bit, piece|Antonyms: whole, complete|Noun


fetz:/fɛts/| v. |撕碎，扯碎|To tear into shreds or small pieces.|He fetzed the letter in anger. 他愤怒地把信撕碎了。|Synonyms: shred, rip, tear|Antonyms: mend, repair|Verb
