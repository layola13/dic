ammonitic:/ˌæməˈnɪtɪk/| adj.|氨的，含氨的，似氨的|Containing or resembling ammonia.  Relating to or derived from ammonia. |The air had a strong ammonitic smell. 空气中有一股强烈的氨味。|Synonyms: ammonia-containing, ammoniacal|Antonyms: non-ammonitic|adjective

ammonitic:/ˌæməˈnɪtɪk/| adj.|羊膜的|Relating to the amnion (the innermost membrane that encloses the embryo of a mammal, bird, or reptile).|The ammonitic fluid protects the developing fetus. 羊水保护发育中的胎儿。|Synonyms: amniotic|Antonyms: None|adjective
