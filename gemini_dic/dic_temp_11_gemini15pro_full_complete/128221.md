neurocity: /nʊərˈɒsɪti/ | n. | 神经城市性，神经城市化 |  A hypothetical future state where technology interfaces directly with the human brain, potentially blurring the lines between the physical world and the digital realm, creating a complex interconnected "neuro-city" within the mind. This concept explores the implications of advanced neurotechnology and its potential impact on human consciousness, social structures, and the very definition of reality.  |  The concept of neurocity raises profound ethical questions about human autonomy and the nature of consciousness. | 近义词: neural network, brain-computer interface, cybernetics | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "natural consciousness") | 词性: noun
