infirmity:/ɪnˈfɜːrməti/ /ɪnˈfɜːrmɪti/| n.|虚弱，衰弱；疾病；弱点|Physical or mental weakness or illness.  A character flaw or weakness.|例句：Old age brought with it a host of infirmities.  老年带来了一系列的疾病。

例句：His greatest infirmity was his inability to make decisions. 他最大的弱点就是无法做决定。

近义词：weakness, frailty, ailment, malady, defect, failing|反义词：strength, health, robustness|词性：名词

infirm:/ɪnˈfɜːrm/ /ɪnˈfɜːrm/| adj.|虚弱的，衰弱的；意志薄弱的|Not physically or mentally strong, especially through age or illness.  Lacking firmness of character.|例句：She became increasingly infirm as she grew older. 随着年龄的增长，她变得越来越虚弱。

例句：He was infirm of purpose. 他的意志薄弱。

近义词：weak, frail, feeble, debilitated|反义词：strong, healthy, robust|词性：形容词
