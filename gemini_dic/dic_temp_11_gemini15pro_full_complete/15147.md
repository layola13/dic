back-down:/ˌbæk ˈdaʊn/ | vi. | 退让，放弃，撤回 | To withdraw from a position or a commitment, typically one involving a threat or confrontation. |  He refused to back down from his earlier statement. 他拒绝撤回他之前的声明。

The government backed down on its proposal after public outcry. 政府在公众的强烈抗议后放弃了它的提议。 | 近义词：retreat, withdraw, yield, concede, give in | 反义词：persist, insist, stand firm | 词性: 动词
