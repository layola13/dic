denominational:/dɪˌnɑːmɪˈneɪʃənəl/ | adj. |宗派的，教派的|Of or relating to a particular religious denomination.  Characterized by or displaying denominationalism. |Example:  Denominational differences often lead to conflict. 教派差异经常导致冲突。|Example:  The school is not denominational and welcomes students of all faiths. 这所学校不属于任何教派，欢迎所有信仰的学生。|近义词：sectarian, religious |反义词:non-denominational, secular|词性:adjective
