disbands:/dɪsˈbændz/
|v.|解散，遣散|To break up or dissolve (a group, organization, or assembly).|The club disbands every summer and reforms in the autumn.  这个俱乐部每年夏天解散，秋天重新组建。|
|v.|解散，遣散|After the war, the army was disbanded. 战后，军队被解散了。|
|近义词：break up, dissolve, dismiss, scatter|
|反义词：assemble, gather, unite, form|
|词性：动词|
|词源：From dis- "apart" + band "a company of men."|
