brownian:/ˈbraʊniən/| adj.|布朗的，布朗运动的|Relating to or exhibiting Brownian motion, the random movement of particles suspended in a fluid.|例句：Brownian motion is the random movement of particles suspended in a fluid. 布朗运动是悬浮在流体中的粒子的随机运动。

例句：The Brownian movement of dust particles in the air can be observed under a microscope.  空气中尘埃颗粒的布朗运动可以在显微镜下观察到。|近义词：random, erratic|反义词：regular, predictable|词性:形容词
