overcourteously: /ˌoʊvərˈkɜːrtiəsli/ | adv. | 过分礼貌地，过分殷勤地 | Excessively polite, to a degree that may be perceived as insincere or excessive. | He bowed overcourteously, almost to the point of groveling. 他鞠躬鞠得过分殷勤，几乎到了卑躬屈膝的地步。 |  She overcourteously offered him three different types of tea. 她过分殷勤地给他提供了三种不同的茶。 | 近义词: excessively politely, too politely | 反义词: rudely, impolitely | 词性: 副词

词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessively") + courteously (adverb form of courteous, from Old French "curteis," meaning "having courtly manners")
