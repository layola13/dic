displeasing:/dɪsˈpliːzɪŋ/
adjective
令人不快的；使人不愉快的
causing annoyance or displeasure; not giving pleasure.
His displeasing manners made him unpopular.  他令人不快的举止让他不受欢迎。
近义词:unpleasant, disagreeable, annoying, offensive
反义词:pleasing, agreeable, delightful, pleasant
词性:adjective


Etymology:
From Middle English *displesinge*, from displeasen + -ing, from Old French desplaisir, from des- (expressing negation) + plaisir (pleasure).  Ultimately from Latin displicēre (to displease), from dis- (expressing negation) + placēre (to please).
