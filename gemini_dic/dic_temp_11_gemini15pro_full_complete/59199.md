dyspeptone:/dɪsˈpɛptəʊn/ /dɪsˈpɛpton/ | n. |消化不良；消化不良者|A person suffering from dyspepsia (indigestion). Also, a medicine or remedy for dyspepsia.|He took a dyspeptone after a particularly rich meal. 他在吃了一顿特别丰盛的饭后服用了消化不良的药物。|近义词：antacid, digestive aid |反义词: None |词性: noun

dyspeptic:/dɪsˈpɛptɪk/ | adj. | 消化不良的；脾气坏的|Suffering from dyspepsia; irritable; gloomy. | His dyspeptic mood made him unpleasant to be around. 他消化不良的情绪让他变得令人不快。 |近义词：irritable, grumpy, crabby |反义词: good-tempered, cheerful |词性: adjective
