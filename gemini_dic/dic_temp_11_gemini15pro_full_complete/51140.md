despoilment:/dɪˈspɔɪlmənt/| n.|掠夺，破坏，抢劫|The despoilment of the rainforest is a tragedy.  雨林的破坏是一场悲剧。| The ancient temple suffered years of despoilment. 这座古老的寺庙遭受了多年的掠夺。|Synonyms: plunder, pillage, looting, devastation, sacking|Antonyms: preservation, conservation, protection|noun

ETYMOLOGY->

From Old French *despoillier*  "to strip, rob, plunder," from *des-* (expressing removal) + *espoillier* "to strip of clothing, rob, plunder,"  from Latin *spoliare* "to strip, plunder."
