heron:/ˈherən/ (US), /ˈhɛrən/ (UK)| n. |鹭|A long-legged wading bird with a long neck, a long pointed bill, and typically grey or white plumage.|We saw a blue heron fishing in the shallows. 我们看到一只蓝鹭在浅滩捕鱼。|The heron stood motionless, waiting for its prey.  这只鹭一动不动地站着，等待猎物。|近义词：egret, crane|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *heroun, *heiroun, from Old French hairon (Modern French héron), from Frankish *haigiro or *hraigiro (“heron”), from Proto-Germanic *hraigrô (“heron”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱrey- (“to cry, scream”).
