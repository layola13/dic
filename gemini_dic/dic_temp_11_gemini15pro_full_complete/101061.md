inventful:/ɪnˈvɛntfʊl/
adjective
善于发明的；足智多谋的
Having the ability to create or design new things; imaginative and resourceful.
She is an inventful cook, always coming up with new dishes. 她是一位富有创造力的厨师，总是能想出新菜。
Inventful engineers developed a new type of engine. 富有创造力的工程师们开发了一种新型发动机。

synonym: inventive, creative, ingenious, resourceful
antonym: uninventive, unimaginative, unresourceful
词性: adjective
