adunque:/aˈduŋkwe/| adv.|因此，所以，那么|It is late; adunque, I must go.  时间晚了；所以，我必须走了。|例句：The evidence is clear, adunque, there is no doubt of his guilt. 证据确凿，因此，他的罪行毋庸置疑。|近义词：therefore, consequently, thus, hence, so, then|反义词:however, nevertheless|词性:副词

词源:
From Old Italian *ad unqua, from Latin ad unquam (“to ever, at any time”).  源自古意大利语 *ad unqua，来自拉丁语 ad unquam（“永远，在任何时候”）。
