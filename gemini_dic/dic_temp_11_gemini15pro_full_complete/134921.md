nonmutant:/ˌnɒnˈmjuːtənt/ /ˌnɑːnˈmjuːtənt/ | adj. n. |非突变的，非突变体 |Not having undergone mutation; unchanged or unaltered genetically.  A nonmutant organism or gene.| The study compared the nonmutant strain with the mutant strain.  这项研究比较了非突变株和突变株。 | non-mutated, wild-type | mutant | adjective, noun
Etymology: non- + mutant.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "mutant" refers to something that has undergone mutation.
