mistral:/ˈmɪstrəl, miˈstrɑːl/| n.|密史脱拉风;西北风|A strong, cold, dry, northwesterly wind that blows through the Rhône valley and southern France to the Mediterranean coast, especially in winter.|The mistral was whipping the trees back and forth. 密史脱拉风吹得树来回摇摆。|Synonyms: northwester, northerly, boreal wind|Antonyms: southerly, southeasterly|noun
