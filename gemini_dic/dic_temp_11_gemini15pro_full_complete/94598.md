词性:noun (plural)

词源:  "imprecisenesses" comes from "imprecise" + "ness" + "es".  "Imprecise" is formed from the prefix "im-" (meaning "not") + "precise". "Precise" comes from the Late Latin "praecisus", meaning "abridged, shortened," from "praecidere" "to cut short."  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition. "-es" is a plural marker for nouns ending in -s, -z, -x, -ch, or -sh.
