countercaster:/ˈkaʊntərˌkæstər/
n.
反咒者，对抗施法者
Someone who casts a counter-spell.  A countercaster uses magic to nullify or oppose the magic of another.
The young countercaster deflected the wizard's fireball with a shimmering shield of energy. 年轻的反咒者用闪闪发光的能量盾牌挡开了巫师的火球。
Synonyms: spellbreaker, magic nullifier, anti-mage
Antonyms: mage, sorcerer, wizard
noun
