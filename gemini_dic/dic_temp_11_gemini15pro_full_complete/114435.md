make-work:/ˈmeɪkˌwɜːrk/
adjective|虚假的；不必要的；为了给人工作而安排的|Work that is assigned merely to keep someone busy.|The government has been accused of creating make-work schemes to reduce unemployment.|近义词:busywork, pointless work, unnecessary work|反义词:essential work, necessary work, productive work|词性:adjective
noun|虚假的工作；不必要的工作|Work that is assigned merely to keep someone busy.|The employees complained about the amount of make-work they were assigned.|近义词:busywork, pointless work, unnecessary work|反义词:essential work, necessary work, productive work|词性:noun

Etymology:
Compound of "make" and "work". "Make" in this context implies creating something artificially or unnecessarily.
