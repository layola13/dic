over-suspicious:/ˌoʊvər səˈspɪʃəs/| adj.|过度怀疑的，过于多疑的|Excessively or unduly suspicious; inclined to suspect without sufficient reason.|She was over-suspicious of her new neighbors, constantly watching their every move. 她对她的新邻居过度怀疑，不断地监视着他们的一举一动。|近义词：distrustful, wary, skeptical, paranoid|反义词:trusting, credulous, naive|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "above, beyond, over") + "suspicious" (from Late Latin suspiciosus, from suspicere "to look up, look askance at," from sub "up from below" + specere "to look").
