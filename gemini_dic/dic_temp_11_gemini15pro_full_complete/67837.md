eyrie:/ˈɪəri/ /ˈɛəri/| n.|鹰巢，高处，高处的房屋|A large nest of a bird of prey, especially an eagle, typically built high in a tree or on a cliff; a high or inaccessible place.|The eagle returned to its eyrie on the cliff face.|近义词：nest, aerie, perch, roost|反义词:ground, bottom|词性:noun

Etymology: From Old French *airie from Latin *agrāria, meaning "belonging to fields," from ager "field."  The meaning shifted over time to "nest of a bird of prey."
