multiemployers:/ˌmʌltiɪmˈplɔɪərz/| n.|多雇主|A group of employers who jointly employ workers, often for benefits or training purposes.|例句：The multiemployers association negotiated a new healthcare plan for all member companies. 多雇主协会为所有成员公司协商了一项新的医疗保健计划。

例句：The multiemployers pension plan covers workers across several different industries. 多雇主养老金计划涵盖了几个不同行业的工人。|近义词：joint employers, co-employers|反义词:single employer|词性:名词
