cozying-up:/ˈkoʊ.zi.ɪŋ ʌp/ | phrasal verb |  变得舒适；依偎；拉拢；套近乎 | The act of making oneself or a situation more comfortable, physically or figuratively, often involving getting close to someone or something. It can also mean attempting to gain favor or establish a close relationship with someone, often for personal gain. |  She was cozying up by the fire with a good book. 她依偎在火炉旁，拿着一本好书。| synonyms: snuggling, cuddling, nestling, ingratiating oneself with, currying favor with | antonyms: distancing oneself from, alienating, snubbing | verb phrase
