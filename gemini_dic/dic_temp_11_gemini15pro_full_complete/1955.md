actioners:/ˈækʃənərz/
noun
动作片; 动作电影
Films or movies that have a lot of exciting action sequences, such as fights, chases, and explosions.
Example: He loves watching actioners with lots of explosions. 他喜欢看有很多爆炸场面的动作片。
Example:  The cinema was showing a double bill of actioners.  这家电影院正在放映两部动作片。
Synonyms: action movies, thrillers, adventure films
Antonyms: dramas, romances, comedies
n.
