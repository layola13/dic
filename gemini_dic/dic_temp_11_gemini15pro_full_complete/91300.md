hydromechanics:/ˌhaɪdrəməˈkænɪks/ | n. | 水力学；流体力学 | The study of the mechanical properties of liquids, particularly water, and the application of these properties in engineering.  It encompasses both hydrostatics (liquids at rest) and hydrodynamics (liquids in motion). | The dam's design was based on the principles of hydromechanics.  大坝的设计是基于水力学原理的。

The hydromechanics of the ship's hull were carefully considered. 船体的流体力学性能经过了仔细的考虑。 | synonyms: fluid mechanics, hydraulics | antonyms: N/A | noun
