guttural:/ˈɡʌtərəl/ (US), /ˈɡʌtərəl/ (UK)| adj.|喉音的；粗哑的|Of or relating to the throat; produced in the throat; harsh-sounding.|His voice was deep and guttural, sending shivers down my spine. 他的声音低沉而沙哑，让我脊背发凉。|The singer's guttural growl added to the song's intensity. 歌手的粗哑咆哮增加了歌曲的强度。|近义词：throaty, hoarse, rasping|反义词：smooth, melodious|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *gutturālis, from Latin guttur ("throat").
