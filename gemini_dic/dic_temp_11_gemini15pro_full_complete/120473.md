minster:/ˈmɪnstər/| n.|大教堂；修道院；部长|A large or important church, originally one attached to a monastery.  Also, a high-ranking government official.|例句：York Minster is a magnificent cathedral.  约克大教堂是一座宏伟的大教堂。

例句：The Prime Minister addressed the nation. 首相向全国发表讲话。|近义词：cathedral, abbey (for church meaning); secretary, minister (for government official meaning)|反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:noun
