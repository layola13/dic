nondefensivenesses:/ˌnɒndɪˈfɛnsɪvnɪsɪz/
|n.|无防备状态；不设防状态；不具防御性|The state or quality of being nondefensive; an absence of defensiveness.  It implies a willingness to be open, receptive, and vulnerable, without feeling the need to protect oneself from criticism or attack.|Her nondefensiveness made the conversation much more productive. 她的不设防状态使得谈话更加富有成效。|Near synonyms: openness, receptivity, vulnerability|Near antonyms: defensiveness, guardedness|noun
