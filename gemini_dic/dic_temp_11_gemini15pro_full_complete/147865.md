overrationalized:/ˌoʊvərˈræʃənəlaɪzd/
adjective
过度理性化的
Explained or justified excessively through logic or reason, often to the point of ignoring or downplaying emotional or intuitive factors.  Overly reasoned; excessively analyzed.
His actions were overrationalized, neglecting the emotional impact on others. 他的行为过度理性化，忽略了对他人的情感影响。

The company's overrationalized decision-making process stifled creativity. 公司过度理性化的决策过程扼杀了创造力。

Synonym: overanalyzed, over-intellectualized
Antonym: intuitive, emotional, instinctive
词性: adjective
