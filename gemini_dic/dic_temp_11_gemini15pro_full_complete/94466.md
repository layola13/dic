imponderabilities:/ɪmˌpɒndərəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |不可估量的事物；难以理解的事物；未知因素|Matters, factors, or circumstances that are difficult or impossible to assess, weigh, or evaluate due to their uncertain or intangible nature.  These are often things that are beyond human control or comprehension.|例句：The imponderabilities of life often make planning for the future difficult.  人生的不可预测性常常使得对未来的规划变得困难。

例句：The success of the project depended on several imponderabilities, including the weather and the availability of resources. 该项目的成功取决于几个不可预测的因素，包括天气和资源的可用性。|近义词：uncertainties, unknowns, variables, contingencies|反义词:certainties, knowns, constants|词性:noun
