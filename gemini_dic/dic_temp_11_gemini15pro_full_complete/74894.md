freshish:/ˈfrɛʃɪʃ/| adj.|相当新鲜的，比较新鲜的|Moderately fresh; somewhat fresh.  It describes something that is fresh to a certain degree, but not completely or perfectly fresh.|The bread was a bit freshish, not quite as soft as yesterday's.  这面包稍微有点新鲜，不如昨天的软。|近义词：fairly fresh, relatively fresh, somewhat fresh|反义词:stale, old, off|词性:形容词

词源:  From fresh + -ish (suffix forming adjectives indicating a moderate degree of the quality denoted by the base word).
