morling: /ˈmɔːrlɪŋ/ | n. | 毛织物，粗纺毛线 | A heavy, loosely woven woolen fabric. |  The shepherd wore a thick morling cloak to protect him from the cold. 牧羊人穿着一件厚厚的粗纺毛线斗篷来御寒。|  The morling was itchy against her skin. 这件粗纺毛线衣穿在她身上很痒。| 近义词：wool, tweed, homespun | 反义词: N/A | 词性：名词

词源：Likely from a dialectal variant of "mortling," related to "mort" (dead wool or fleece from a dead sheep), referring to the coarse nature of the wool.  The exact etymology is somewhat obscure.
