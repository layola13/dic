cabrit:/ˈkæbriː/ /kɑːˈbriː/| n.|小山羊，幼山羊；小山羊肉|A young goat.  Specifically, a goat kid slaughtered for its meat, especially in Caribbean cuisine.|例句：The cabrit stew was seasoned perfectly.  小山羊肉炖得非常入味。

例句：We ordered cabrit at the restaurant. 我们在餐厅点了小山羊肉。|近义词：kid, goatling|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly adult goat or billy goat)|词性:noun
