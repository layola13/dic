filleter:/fɪlˈteɪtər/ | n. | 过滤者, 滤器 |A person or thing that filters something.  A device that removes unwanted material from a liquid or gas. | The coffee filter separated the grounds from the brewed coffee. 咖啡过滤器将咖啡渣与煮好的咖啡分离了。 |  The kidneys act as a filter, removing waste products from the blood. 肾脏充当过滤器，从血液中去除废物。 | synonym: strainer, sieve, purifier | antonym: contaminant, pollutant | noun
