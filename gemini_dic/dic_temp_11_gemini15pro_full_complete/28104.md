capitalist:/ˈkæpɪtəlɪst/| n. adj.|资本家；资本主义的|A person who uses their wealth to invest in trade and industry for profit.  A wealthy person who uses money to invest in businesses and make more money.  Relating to or supporting capitalism.|

例句：
He is a ruthless capitalist who cares only about profits.
他是一个无情的资本家，只关心利润。

例句：
The capitalist system encourages competition and innovation.
资本主义制度鼓励竞争和创新。


近义词：investor, financier, businessman, entrepreneur|
反义词：socialist, communist, worker, laborer|
词性: noun, adjective|


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *capitaliste*, from *capital* (see capital).
