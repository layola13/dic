ommateum:/ɒˈmeɪtiəm/ | n. | 复眼 | A compound eye, found in insects and crustaceans, composed of numerous individual light-detecting units called ommatidia. | The fly's ommatea allow it to see a wide field of view. 苍蝇的复眼使它能够看到广阔的视野。|  compound eye | simple eye | noun

ommatidia:/ˌɒməˈtɪdiə/ | n. | (复眼的)小眼 |  One of the small individual eyes that make up a compound eye, such as that of an insect or crustacean. | Each ommatidium contributes to the overall image perceived by the insect. 每个小眼都构成昆虫感知的整体图像。 | facet, lens | N/A | noun
