gainful:/ˈɡeɪnfʊl/| adj.|有利可图的，有报酬的|Producing a profit or income; profitable.  Relating to or affording gain or profit. |She found gainful employment as a software engineer. 她找到了一份软件工程师的 lucrative 工作。|近义词：profitable, lucrative, remunerative|反义词: unprofitable, unremunerative|词性:形容词
Etymology: From gain + -ful.  "Gain" comes from Old North French "gain," meaning profit or advantage.
