PHRASE->
fight back  反击；抵抗  They fought back against the attackers. 他们反击了袭击者。
fight for  为…而战；争取  They are fighting for their rights. 他们正在为自己的权利而战。
fight against  与…作斗争  They fought against oppression. 他们与压迫作斗争。
fight off  击退；抵抗  She managed to fight off her attacker. 她成功地击退了袭击者。
fight with  与…并肩作战；与…打架  He fought with his comrades in the war. 他在战争中与战友并肩作战。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *feohtan (attested only in feohte "on foot, for fighting"), from Proto-Germanic *fehtaną (source also of Old Frisian fiuchta "to fight", Old Saxon fehtan, Old High German fehtan, German fechten, Old Norse berjask "to fight"), from Proto-Indo-European *pek- "to pluck, comb wool".
