barricade:/ˌbærɪˈkeɪd/ /ˌbɑːrɪˈkeɪd/| n. vt.|路障，障碍物；街垒；阻碍，封锁|A barrier hastily constructed, as in a street, to prevent or delay the movement of opposing forces.|The protesters erected barricades to block the street. 抗议者设置路障封锁街道。|Synonyms: barrier, obstacle, blockade, obstruction|Antonyms: opening, passage, access|noun

barricade:/ˌbærɪˈkeɪd/ /ˌbɑːrɪˈkeɪd/| vt.|设路障，阻挡；封锁|To obstruct or fortify with a barricade.|Police barricaded the road after the accident. 事故发生后，警察封锁了道路。|Synonyms: block, obstruct, bar, close off|Antonyms: open, clear, unblock|verb
