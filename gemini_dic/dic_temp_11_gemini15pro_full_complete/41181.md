copyism:/ˈkɒpiɪzəm/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpiɪzəm/ (US)| n.|抄袭主义；模仿主义；抄袭行为|The act or practice of copying, especially in an unoriginal or unauthorized way; plagiarism.  It can also refer to a philosophy or belief system that advocates copying or imitation as a valid or even preferred method of creation or innovation.|His artwork was accused of copyism, bearing a striking resemblance to a famous painting.|近义词：plagiarism, imitation, emulation, mimicry|反义词：originality, innovation|词性:名词
