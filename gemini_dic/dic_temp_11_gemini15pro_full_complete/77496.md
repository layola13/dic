gazette:/ɡəˈzɛt/ | n. | 公报，报纸 | An official journal or newspaper, especially one that publishes legal notices. | The results of the election were published in the government gazette.  选举结果刊登在政府公报上。| synonyms: journal, newspaper, periodical, publication, bulletin, newsletter | antonyms: None | noun

gazette:/ɡəˈzɛt/ | v. | (在公报上)公布，宣布 | To announce or publish something officially in a gazette. | The company’s bankruptcy was gazetted last week. 该公司的破产上周已在公报上宣布。| synonyms: announce, publish, proclaim, declare, broadcast, promulgate | antonyms: conceal, suppress, hide | verb
