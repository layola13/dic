goadlike:/ˈɡoʊdˌlaɪk/| adj.|像刺棒一样的，刺激的，激励的|Resembling or functioning as a goad; urging or stimulating into action.|His goadlike questions forced her to confront the truth.|近义词：provocative, stimulating, urging, inciting, motivating|反义词：soothing, calming, pacifying|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "goad" (a pointed stick used to urge animals) + "-like" (suffix indicating resemblance). "Goad" comes from Old English "gād," related to Old Norse "gað."
