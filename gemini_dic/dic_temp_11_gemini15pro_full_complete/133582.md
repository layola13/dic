nonflatulence: /ˌnɒnˈflætʃʊləns/ | n. | 无胀气; 不放屁 | Absence of flatulence; the state of not expelling intestinal gas. |  My dog's nonflatulence is surprising considering his diet.  我的狗竟然不放屁，考虑到它的饮食，这真是令人惊讶。 | 近义词: absence of gas, flatus-free | 反义词: flatulence, flatus | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "flatulence" (from Latin "flatus," meaning "a blowing, blast")
