bewrayment:/bɪˈreɪmənt/
n.
背叛；暴露；泄露
The act of betraying someone or something, or the state of being betrayed; exposure or revelation of something previously hidden.
His sudden silence was a bewrayment of his guilt. 他的突然沉默暴露了他的罪行。

近义词: betrayal, treachery, disclosure, revelation, exposure
反义词: loyalty, fidelity, concealment, secrecy

词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English bewreien, from Old English bewreġan (“to accuse, betray, reveal”), from be- + wreġan (“to accuse”).  Cognate with Old High German biwregōn (“to accuse”) and Gothic 𐌱𐌹𐍅𐍂𐌰𐌶𐌾𐌰𐌽 (biwraizjan, “to betray”).  The noun is formed with the suffix -ment.
