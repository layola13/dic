biodegrading:/ˌbaɪoʊdɪˈɡreɪdɪŋ/ | adj. | 生物降解的 |  The process by which organic substances are broken down by living organisms, usually microorganisms, resulting in simpler substances such as carbon dioxide, water, and biomass.  This process is often used in relation to environmentally friendly products that decompose naturally. |  Many companies are now producing biodegrading plastics. 许多公司现在正在生产可生物降解的塑料。| Synonyms: decomposing, composting, rotting | Antonyms:  non-biodegradable, persistent, enduring | adjective

biodegrade:/ˌbaɪoʊdɪˈɡreɪd/ | v. | 生物降解 | To decompose or break down naturally as a result of the action of living organisms, particularly bacteria and fungi. | This type of packaging biodegrades quickly. 这种包装可以快速生物降解。 | Synonyms: decompose, rot, decay | Antonyms: persist, endure | verb
