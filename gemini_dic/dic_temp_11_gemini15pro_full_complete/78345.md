gesellschaft:/ɡəˈzɛlʃaft/| n.|社会，社团，公司|A community, especially a structured society with shared values, norms, and institutions.  It also refers to a company or association.|Germany is a Gesellschaft based on industry. 德国是一个建立在工业基础上的社会。|synonyms: society, community, association, corporation, company|antonyms: individual, solitude|noun
