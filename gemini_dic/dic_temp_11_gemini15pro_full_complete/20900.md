bleachable:/ˈbliːtʃəbl/| adj.|可漂白的，可褪色的|Able to be whitened or made lighter in color by a chemical process, typically using bleach.|This stain is bleachable, so don't worry.  这个污渍可以漂白，所以不用担心。|The tablecloth is bleachable, making it easy to clean. 这块桌布可以漂白，很容易清洗。|近义词：whitening, decolorizable|反义词：unbleachable, colorfast|词性:形容词
