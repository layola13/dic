eozoon:/ˌiːəˈzoʊɒn/
n.
Eozoon canadense (a supposed fossil)
加拿大始动物（一种假想的化石）
A layered calcareous structure found in Precambrian rocks, formerly thought to be the fossil of a giant foraminifer but now considered to be of inorganic origin.
在元古代岩石中发现的分层钙质结构，以前被认为是巨型有孔虫的化石，但现在被认为是无机成因的。
Eozoon canadense was once thought to be the earliest form of life. 加拿大始动物曾被认为是最早的生命形式。
The discovery of eozoon sparked a debate about the origins of life. 始动物的发现引发了关于生命起源的争论。
近义词: fossil, prehistoric organism
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "modern organism")
词性: noun
