fastidiousness:/fæˈstɪdiəsnəs/
n.
一丝不苟；挑剔；苛求
Excessive attention to detail and a tendency to be overly critical.
Her fastidiousness about cleanliness was well-known. 她对清洁一丝不苟是众所周知的。
He chose his words with fastidiousness. 他字斟句酌。
近义词: meticulousness, scrupulousness, exactingness
反义词: carelessness, sloppiness, negligence
词性: 名词


fastidious:/fæˈstɪdiəs/
adj.
一丝不苟的；挑剔的；苛求的
Very attentive to and concerned about accuracy and detail.
He is fastidious about his appearance. 他对自己的外表很挑剔。
She has a fastidious approach to her work. 她工作一丝不苟。
近义词: meticulous, scrupulous, exacting
反义词: careless, sloppy, negligent
词性: 形容词


词源: From Latin *fastidiosus*, from fastidium "loathing, disgust," from fastus "pride, arrogance" + taedium "tedium, weariness."
