ghostly:/ˈɡoʊstli/| adj.|幽灵般的，鬼魂似的；苍白的，虚弱的|Suggestive of a ghost; pale and ethereal.|The old house had a ghostly atmosphere. 这栋老房子有一种幽灵般的气氛。|Near Synonyms: spectral, phantom, eerie, unearthly, spooky|Antonyms: corporeal, tangible, earthly, substantial|adjective

ghostly:/ˈɡoʊstli/| adv.|（罕见）像鬼一样地|In a ghostly manner (rarely used).|He moved ghostly through the halls. 他像鬼魂一样穿过大厅。（用法罕见）|Near Synonyms: spectrally, eerily|Antonyms: - |adverb (rare)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *gāstlīc (attested only as gāstlicnes 'spiritual condition'), from gāst 'ghost, spirit' + -līc '-ly'.  The modern spelling with 'h' is influenced by the spelling of "ghost."
