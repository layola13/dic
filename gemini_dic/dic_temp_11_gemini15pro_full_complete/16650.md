barracker:/ˈbærəkər/ (US), /ˈbærəkə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 支持者，啦啦队员|A person who shouts encouragement and support for their team, especially at a sporting event. | The barrackers were wild with excitement as their team scored the winning goal. 啦啦队员们随着他们的队伍攻入制胜一球而兴奋不已。|Synonyms: supporter, fan, rooter, cheerleader|Antonyms: opponent, rival|Noun
