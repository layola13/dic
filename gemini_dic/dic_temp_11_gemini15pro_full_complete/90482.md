hubristic:/hjuːˈbrɪstɪk/ | adj. | 傲慢的，狂妄自大的 | Excessively proud or self-confident; arrogant. |  His hubristic attitude made him unpopular. 他的傲慢态度让他不受欢迎。 | Example: The CEO's hubristic pronouncements alienated the board of directors.  这位CEO傲慢的声明疏远了董事会成员。| Synonyms: arrogant, haughty, conceited, overweening, presumptuous | Antonyms: humble, modest, unassuming | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hybris* (ὕβρις) meaning "excessive pride, insolence, outrage,"  +  "-istic" (suffix forming adjectives meaning "relating to, characterized by").
