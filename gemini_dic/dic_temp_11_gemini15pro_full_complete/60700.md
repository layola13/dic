electrize:/ɪˈlɛktrɪˌzaɪz/ | v. | 使充电；使电气化；使激动 | To charge with electricity; to thrill or excite. | The scientist electrified the metal sphere. 科学家使金属球带电。

The band's performance electrified the audience. 乐队的表演使观众激动不已。 | 近义词: charge, excite, thrill, stimulate | 反义词: calm, soothe, tranquilize | 词性: 动词

词源: From French *électriser*, from *électrique* ("electric") + *-iser* (suffix forming verbs). Ultimately from Ancient Greek *ἤλεκτρον* (*ēlektron*, “amber”).
