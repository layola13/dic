non-committally:/ˌnɒn kəˈmɪtəli/ (US) /ˌnɑːn kəˈmɪtəli/| adv.|不置可否地，含糊其辞地|In a way that does not express or commit to a definite opinion or course of action.|He answered non-committally, not wanting to reveal his plans. 他含糊其辞地回答，不想透露他的计划。|Synonyms: evasively, vaguely, ambiguously|Antonyms: decisively, definitely, firmly|词性:副词

non-committal:/ˌnɒn kəˈmɪtl/ (US) /ˌnɑːn kəˈmɪtl/| adj.|不表态的，不承诺的|Not expressing or committing to a definite opinion or course of action.|She gave a non-committal shrug, unwilling to take sides. 她不置可否地耸了耸肩，不愿表明立场。|Synonyms: evasive, vague, ambiguous|Antonyms: decisive, definite, firm|词性:形容词
