asperse:/əˈspɜːrs/ /əˈspɝs/| v. |诽谤，诋毁，污蔑|To cast aspersions on; to attack with slander or criticism.|例句：His reputation was aspersed by malicious rumors. 他的名誉被恶意谣言诋毁。

例句：They aspersed her character with baseless accusations. 他们用毫无根据的指控污蔑她的人格。

|近义词：slander, defame, vilify, calumniate, denigrate|反义词：praise, commend, laud, extol|词性：动词
