abstractest:/æbˈstræktɪst/
adj.
最抽象的
most abstract; furthest removed from the concrete or particular
His paintings are the abstractest I've ever seen. 他的画是我见过的最抽象的。
近义词: most theoretical, most conceptual, most intangible
反义词: most concrete, most specific, most tangible
词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From abstract + -est (superlative suffix).  Abstract comes from Latin abstractus, past participle of abstrahere "to draw away," from ab- "away" + trahere "to draw."
