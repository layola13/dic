chincherie:/ʃɪnˈʃɛri/ | n. | 小玩意儿；廉价珠宝；华而不实的装饰品 | A collection of small, cheap ornaments or jewelry, often gaudy and of little value. |  She covered her dresser with chinchineries she had collected over the years. 她用多年来收集的小玩意儿装饰了她的梳妆台。| Synonyms: trinkets, baubles, gewgaws, knick-knacks | Antonyms: valuables, treasures | Noun

Etymology: French, from *chinchin*, imitative of tinkling sound.
