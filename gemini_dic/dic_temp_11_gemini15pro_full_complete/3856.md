agoraphobias:/ˌæɡərəˈfoʊbiəz/
|n.|广场恐惧症|An extreme or irrational fear of crowded spaces or enclosed public places.|She suffers from agoraphobia and rarely leaves her house. 她患有广场恐惧症，很少出门。|
|近义词：panic disorder, social anxiety|反义词：calmness, composure|词性：名词|
|词源：From Greek agora (marketplace) + phobia (fear).|
