dolent:/ˈdoʊlənt/| adj.|悲痛的，忧伤的|Sorrowful; grieving; mournful. Expressing or characterized by sorrow. |She cast a dolent glance back at the house she was leaving. 她悲伤地回头看了一眼她即将离开的房子。|The dolent widow wore black for a year. 悲痛的寡妇穿了一年的黑衣。|近义词：sorrowful, mournful, grieving, doleful, lugubrious|反义词：joyful, happy, cheerful|词性:形容词
词源:From Latin *dolent-em, present participle of dolēre “to grieve, suffer pain.”
