normalcy: /ˈnɔːrməlsi/ (US) /ˈnɔːməlsi/ (UK)| n. | 常态，正常状态 |The state of being normal; the condition of things as they are or as they are expected or desired to be. |After years of war, a sense of normalcy slowly returned to the country.  战后多年，这个国家慢慢恢复了常态。|  The town is gradually returning to normalcy after the flood.  洪水过后，小镇逐渐恢复正常。|Synonyms: normality, regularity, ordinariness | Antonyms: abnormality, irregularity, anomaly | noun

词源：normalcy 源自 normal（正常的）+ -cy（名词后缀，表示状态或性质）。虽然有人认为 normality 更符合语法规则，但 normalcy 在美国英语中被广泛接受，尤其是在美国总统沃伦·哈定在1920年竞选时使用 "return to normalcy" 之后。
