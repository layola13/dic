macroeconomists: /ˌmækroʊiˈkɑːnəmɪsts/ | n. | 宏观经济学家 |  A person who studies macroeconomics, the branch of economics concerned with large-scale or general economic factors, such as interest rates and national productivity. | Macroeconomists are currently debating the best course of action to stimulate the economy. 宏观经济学家们目前正在讨论刺激经济的最佳方案。| Macroeconomists predict a slowdown in economic growth next year. 宏观经济学家预测明年经济增长将放缓。 | synonyms: economists, analysts, researchers | antonyms: N/A | noun
