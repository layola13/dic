egremoin:/eɪɡrɪˈmwɑːn/ | n. |  排泄物；呕吐物 |  (from Old French *esgremoin, alteration of escrouelles "scrofula," from Latin scrophulae "swelling of the neck glands")  A bodily discharge, especially vomit or excrement. |  The ancient text described a ritual involving the egremoin of a sacrificial animal. 古代文献描述了一种涉及祭祀动物排泄物的仪式。 | Synonym: excreta, vomit, discharge | Antonym: ingestion, intake | noun
