counterbid:/ˈkaʊntərˌbɪd/ /ˈkaʊntərbɪd/| n. vt.|还价，反出价，竞争性报价|An offer made in response to another bid, typically higher than the original.|例句：The company made a counterbid for the rival firm. 这家公司对竞争对手的出价提出了还价。|近义词：outbid, overbid, rival bid|反义词:bid, offer|词性:名词, 动词

counterbid:/ˈkaʊntərˌbɪd/ /ˈkaʊntərbɪd/| v.|还价，反出价，竞争性报价|To make a counterbid.|例句：They decided to counterbid to acquire the property. 他们决定还价以获得该房产。|近义词：outbid, overbid|反义词:underbid|词性:动词
