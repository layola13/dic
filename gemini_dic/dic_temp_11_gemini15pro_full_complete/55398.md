distensible:/dɪˈstɛnsəbl/ | adj. |可扩张的；可膨胀的 |A distensible material can be stretched or expanded. |  The stomach is a distensible organ. 胃是一个可扩张的器官。| Blood vessels are distensible. 血管是可扩张的。 | 近义词：expandable, flexible, elastic | 反义词：stiff, rigid | adjective
