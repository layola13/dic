balmier:/ˈbɑːmiər/ (US), /ˈbɑːmiə/ (UK)| adj.|更温和的，更芳香的|Mild and pleasant; characterized by a soothing warmth and often a fragrant scent.|The weather is balmier today than it was yesterday. 今天的天气比昨天更温和。|The balmier air of the south was a welcome change. 南方更加温和的空气是一个受欢迎的变化。|近义词：milder, warmer, more fragrant, sweeter|反义词：colder, harsher, less fragrant|词性:形容词
balm:/bɑːm/| n.|香油，药膏，镇痛剂|A fragrant ointment or preparation used to heal or soothe the skin; something that heals or comforts.|She applied a balm to her sunburned skin. 她给晒伤的皮肤涂上了药膏。|His words were a balm to her troubled soul. 他的话抚慰了她不安的灵魂。|近义词：ointment, salve, lotion, solace, comfort|反义词：irritant|词性:名词
