nonreceptivity:/ˌnɑːnrisɛpˈtɪvɪti/
n.|非接受性，不接受，不接纳|The nonreceptivity of the audience to his new ideas was discouraging.  观众对他的新想法不接受，这让他很沮丧。|例句：The government's nonreceptivity to the protestors' demands led to further unrest. 政府对抗议者要求的不接纳导致了进一步的动荡。|近义词：resistance, unwillingness, opposition, unresponsiveness|反义词: receptivity, openness, willingness, acceptance|词性:名词

Etymology:
From non- (not) + receptive + -ity (forming nouns of quality or state).  Receptive comes from Latin recept- "received, withdrawn," past participle stem of recipere "take back, receive," from re- "back" + capere "to take."
