diphthongally:/dɪfˈθɒŋɡəli/ /daɪfˈθɑːŋɡəli/ | adv. | 以双元音的方式 |  In a way that uses or involves diphthongs (two vowel sounds occurring in the same syllable). |  She pronounced the word diphthongally, emphasizing the shift in vowel sounds. 她以双元音的方式读这个词，强调了元音的变化。| 近义词:  N/A | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 副词

diphthong:/ˈdɪfθɒŋ/ /ˈdaɪfθɑːŋ/ | n. | 双元音 | A sound formed by the combination of two vowels in a single syllable, in which the sound begins as one vowel and moves toward another (as in coin, loud, and side ). | The word "coin" contains the diphthong /ɔɪ/. "coin" 这个单词包含双元音 /ɔɪ/。 | 近义词:  vowel glide | 反义词: monophthong | 词性: 名词
