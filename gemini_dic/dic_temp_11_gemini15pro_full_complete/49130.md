deist:/ˈdiːɪst/ (US), /ˈdeɪɪst/ (UK)| n.|自然神论者|A person who believes that God created the universe and established its natural laws but does not intervene in the affairs of the world.|Thomas Paine was a prominent deist. 托马斯·潘恩是一位著名的自然神论者.|近义词：freethinker, rationalist|反义词：theist, atheist|词性:noun
Etymology: From French *déiste*, from Latin *deus* "god".
