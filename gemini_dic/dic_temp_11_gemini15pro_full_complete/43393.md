crankier:/ˈkræŋ.ki.ər/| adj.|更暴躁的，更易怒的|Comparative form of cranky. More irritable or bad-tempered than usual.|例句：He's been much crankier since he quit smoking.  他戒烟后脾气暴躁多了。|例句：The baby is crankier than usual today. 今天宝宝比平时更爱哭闹。|近义词：grumpier, more irritable, touchier|反义词：cheerier, more pleasant, more good-natured|词性:形容词

词源:
From crank (n.) + -y (adj. suffix).  "Crank" originally referred to a machine part, and figuratively to a person's mental state, implying someone "bent" or "twisted" out of shape, hence irritable.
