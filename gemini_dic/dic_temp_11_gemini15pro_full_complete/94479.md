imporous:/ɪmˈpɔːrəs/
adjective
不透水的，不透气的
not allowing liquid or gas to pass through
The imporous rock prevented water from seeping through.  这块不透水的岩石阻止了水渗透。
近义词: impermeable, impervious
反义词: porous, permeable
词性: adjective
词源:  From Late Latin impōrōsus (“not porous”), from in- (“not”) + pōrōsus (“porous”).
