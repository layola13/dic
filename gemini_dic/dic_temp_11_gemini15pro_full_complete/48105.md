decline:/dɪˈklaɪn/ /dəˈklaɪn/| v. n.|下降，衰退；拒绝，谢绝| To decrease in quantity, strength, or importance; to politely refuse.| The company's profits declined by 10% last year.  这家公司去年的利润下降了10%。 |近义词：decrease, refuse, reject, diminish, fall, weaken|反义词:increase, accept, rise, grow, improve|词性:动词,名词

decline:/dɪˈklaɪn/ /dəˈklaɪn/| n.|下降，衰退；斜坡|A gradual decrease in something; a downward slope.|There has been a sharp decline in sales this year. 今年的销售额急剧下降。|近义词：decrease, reduction, downturn, fall, descent|反义词:increase, rise, ascent, incline|词性:名词
