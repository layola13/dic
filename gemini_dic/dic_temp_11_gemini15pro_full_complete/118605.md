message-boards:/ˈmɛsɪdʒ bɔːrdz/
n.
留言板；论坛
An online platform where users can post messages and discuss various topics.
Users can share their thoughts and ideas on message boards. 用户可以在留言板上分享他们的想法和观点。
Many online communities use message boards for discussions. 许多在线社区使用留言板进行讨论。
近义词：forums, discussion boards, online forums
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "private chat" or "direct message")
词性: noun
