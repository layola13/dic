noneliminative:/ˌnɒnɪˈlɪmɪnətɪv/ | adj. | 非消除性的，非排斥性的 | Not involving or characterized by elimination; tending to retain or preserve elements or aspects rather than eliminating them. |  This is a noneliminative approach to understanding consciousness, suggesting it's a fundamental aspect of reality. 这是一种理解意识的非消除性方法，表明它是现实的一个基本方面。

Philosophers favor a noneliminative materialist account of the mind. 哲学家们倾向于一种非消除性的唯物主义的心灵解释。


近义词: retentive, preservative, conservative | 反义词: eliminative, reductive | 词性: adjective
