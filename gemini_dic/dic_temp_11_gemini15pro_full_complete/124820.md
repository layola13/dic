mucivore:/ˈmjuːsɪvɔːr/| n. |食粘液动物|An animal that feeds on mucus, the slimy substance secreted by mucous membranes.|The cleaner wrasse is a mucivore, feeding on the mucus and parasites of larger fish.|近义词: mucus-feeder|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially a predator of mucus-producing organisms)|词性:noun
