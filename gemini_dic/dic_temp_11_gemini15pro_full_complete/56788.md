docket:/ˈdɒkɪt (UK), ˈdɑːkɪt (US)/
|n.|摘要；备忘录；（待审案件的）简要记录；标签|A short official note listing the contents of a document or parcel.|The lawyer consulted the docket to find the date of the hearing. 律师查阅了摘要，找到了听证会的日期。|synonym: summary, abstract, agenda, list |antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "full text" or "complete record")
|noun|

docket:/ˈdɒkɪt (UK), ˈdɑːkɪt (US)/
|vt.|将（案件）列入审理记录表；贴标签于|To make a brief summary of (a document or case) or to mark with a label.|The clerk docketed the case for the next session. 书记员将案件列入下一次开庭的记录表。|synonym: schedule, register, label, tag|antonym: remove, unschedule, untag
