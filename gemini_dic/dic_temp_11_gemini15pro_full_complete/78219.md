geraniine: /dʒəˈreɪniːn/ | n. | 天竺葵素; 天竺葵色素 | A red pigment found in the petals of geraniums. | The vibrant geraniine in the flower attracted the hummingbird.  花朵中鲜艳的天竺葵素吸引了蜂鸟。 | 近义词: pelargonidin (strictly speaking, pelargonidin is the aglycone of geraniine) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

geraniine: /dʒəˈreɪniːn/ | adj. | 天竺葵色的 | Having the color of geraniums; a vivid purplish-red. |  She wore a geraniine dress to the party. 她穿着一件天竺葵色的连衣裙去参加聚会。 | 近义词: geranium-red, magenta, fuchsia | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *géraniine*, from *géranium* (geranium) + *-ine* (forming adjectives and nouns related to the base word). Ultimately from New Latin *Geranium*, from Greek *geránion*, diminutive of *géranos* (crane), because the fruit resembles a crane's bill.
