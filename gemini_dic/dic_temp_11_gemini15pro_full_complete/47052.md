dataria:/deɪˈtɛəriə/| n.|教廷秘书处；教廷事务办公室；日期记录|An office in the Roman Curia responsible for preparing and dispatching papal documents, especially those of a less solemn nature. It also historically served as a record office for papal acts and decisions.|The dataria handled the expedition of papal bulls and briefs.|近义词:Chancery, Apostolic Chancery|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
