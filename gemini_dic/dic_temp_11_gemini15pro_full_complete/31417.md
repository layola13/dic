chariest:/ˈtʃɛəriɪst/| adj.|最谨慎的，最警惕的|Most cautious or wary; most reluctant to take risks.|She was the chariest of the investors, always hesitant to commit large sums of money. 她是最谨慎的投资者，总是犹豫是否要投入大笔资金。|近义词：cautious, wary, careful, prudent, circumspect|反义词：reckless, careless, imprudent, rash|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *chary, from Old English cearig (“sorrowful, mournful, anxious, careful”), from Proto-Germanic *karigaz (“sorrowful, anxious”), from Proto-Indo-European *kār- (“to grieve, mourn”).  The sense shifted from "sorrowful" to "careful" through the intermediate sense of "anxious."
