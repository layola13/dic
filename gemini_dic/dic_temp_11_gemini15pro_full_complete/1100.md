accessory:/əkˈsesəri/ | n. | 附件，配件；附属物；同谋，帮凶 |  A thing that can be added to something else in order to make it more useful, versatile, or attractive.  | She wore a simple black dress with a pearl necklace as her only accessory. 她穿着一件简单的黑色连衣裙，唯一的配饰是一条珍珠项链。 | synonyms: attachment, addition, supplement, adjunct, appendage, component, extra, fitting | antonyms: essential, necessity, requirement | noun

accessory:/əkˈsesəri/ | adj. | 附属的，补充的；同谋的，帮凶的 | Contributing to or aiding in a secondary capacity; accomplice. | He was charged as an accessory to murder. 他被指控为谋杀案的从犯。| synonyms: supplementary, additional, ancillary, auxiliary, subordinate, secondary, abetting, complicit | antonyms: principal, primary, main, chief | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *accessoire (modern French accessoire), from Latin accessorius (“additional, supplementary”), from accedere (“to approach, to be added to”).
