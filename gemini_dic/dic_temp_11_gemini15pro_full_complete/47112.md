daun:/daʊn/ | n. | 绒毛；柔毛；汗毛 | Soft, fine hairs or feathers. | Example: The baby bird was covered in soft down.  这只雏鸟身上覆盖着柔软的绒毛。 | Example:  The peach had a fine down on its skin.  桃子的表皮有一层细细的绒毛。 | Synonym: fluff, fuzz | Antonym: -- | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *dūnn, from Proto-Germanic *dūnaz. Cognate with German Daune, Dutch dons.
