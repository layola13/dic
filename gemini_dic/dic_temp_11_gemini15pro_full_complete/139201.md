objectival: /ˌɒbdʒɛkˈtaɪvəl/ | adj. | 客观的；目标的 | Relating to external reality; unbiased; also, relating to or expressing the grammatical objective case. |  The scientist maintained an objectival stance throughout the experiment, carefully recording only observable data. 科学家在整个实验过程中保持客观立场，仔细记录仅可观察到的数据。

The judge made an objectival assessment of the case, based solely on the evidence presented. 法官根据提出的证据对案件作出了客观的评估。


Synonym: objective, impartial, unbiased, factual
Antonym: subjective, biased, prejudiced, personal
词性: adjective
