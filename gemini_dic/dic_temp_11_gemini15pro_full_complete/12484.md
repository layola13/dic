aspen's:/ˈæspənz/
noun
白杨树的；白杨木的
belonging to or associated with an aspen tree.
The aspen's leaves trembled in the breeze. 白杨树的叶子在微风中颤抖。
Synonyms:  poplar's, aspen tree's
Antonyms: N/A
词性: noun (possessive form)

aspen's:/ˈæspənz/
noun
白杨树
(Often used in plural "aspens") A type of tree known for its trembling leaves.
I love the way the aspens' leaves shimmer in the sunlight. 我喜欢白杨树叶在阳光下闪闪发光的样子。（此处aspens' 也是复数名词的所有格）
Synonyms: poplar, aspen tree
Antonyms: N/A
词性: noun (plural possessive or singular possessive with implied plural)
