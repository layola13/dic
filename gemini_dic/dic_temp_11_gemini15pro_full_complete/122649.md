modish:/ˈmoʊdɪʃ/ (US), /ˈməʊdɪʃ/ (UK)| adj.|时髦的，流行的|Conforming to or following what is currently fashionable and popular.|Her modish attire caught everyone's attention at the gala. 她在晚会上的时髦装扮吸引了所有人的注意。|The restaurant's modish decor made it a popular spot for influencers. 这家餐厅时髦的装潢使它成为网红们的热门打卡地。|近义词：stylish, fashionable, trendy, chic|反义词：unfashionable, outdated, old-fashioned|词性:形容词
Etymology: Late 16th century: from mode + -ish.
