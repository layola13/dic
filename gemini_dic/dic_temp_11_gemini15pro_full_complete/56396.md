dolefulness:/ˈdoʊlfəlnəs/| n. |悲哀，忧郁|The state of being doleful; sadness; gloom.|Her dolefulness was evident in her downcast eyes and quiet voice. 她的忧郁从她低垂的眼睛和安静的声音中显露出来。|近义词：sadness, gloom, melancholy, dejection, sorrow|反义词：happiness, cheerfulness, joy, elation|词性:noun
Etymology: From doleful + -ness.  Doleful comes from Middle English *doleful, from dole "grief, sorrow" (from Old French doel, ultimately from Latin dolus "pain, grief") + -ful.
