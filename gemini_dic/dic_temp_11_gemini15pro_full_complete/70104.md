fevers:/ˈfiːvərz/| n.|发烧，发热;狂热，兴奋|A rise of body temperature above the normal.  A state of heightened emotion or activity.|例句：He has been suffering from fevers for the last few days.  他过去几天一直发烧。

例句：There was a fever of excitement surrounding the upcoming concert.  即将到来的音乐会周围充满了兴奋的狂热。|近义词：high temperatures, temperatures, excitement, frenzy|反义词:calm, coolness|noun
