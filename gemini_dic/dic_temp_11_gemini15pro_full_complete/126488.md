mytilotoxine:/ˌmɪtɪloʊˈtɒksɪn/| n. | 贝类毒素 | A neurotoxin found in certain shellfish, particularly mussels, that causes paralytic shellfish poisoning (PSP). | Example: Mytilotoxine contamination can occur in mussels during algal blooms.  贝类毒素污染可能在藻类大量繁殖期间发生在贻贝中。| Example:  The symptoms of mytilotoxine poisoning can be severe. 贝类毒素中毒的症状可能很严重。 | Synonym:  saxitoxin (most common synonym),  PSP toxin | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a specific toxin, but one might consider "antidote" in a broader sense) | noun
