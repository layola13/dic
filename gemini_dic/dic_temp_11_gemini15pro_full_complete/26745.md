cadres:/ˈkɑːdrəz/ (US), /ˈkɑːdrəz/ (UK)| n.| 骨干，干部，核心小组|A group of trained people who are able to provide leadership and training to others, especially in political or military organizations. |例句：The party cadres were responsible for organizing the local communities. 党干部负责组织当地社区。

|例句：The company has a cadre of highly skilled engineers. 这家公司拥有一批技术精湛的工程师。|近义词：leaders, officials, core group, staff, personnel|反义词：masses, rank and file, followers|词性:noun
