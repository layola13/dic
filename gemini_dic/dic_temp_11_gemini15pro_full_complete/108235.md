laughing-gas:/ˈlɑːfɪŋ ɡæs/ /ˈlæfɪŋ ɡæs/
noun
笑气；一氧化二氮
A colorless sweet-smelling gas, N2O, that is used as a mild anesthetic, especially in dentistry, and also as an aerosol propellant.  It can also be used recreationally, as it causes euphoria and mild hallucinations.
The dentist used laughing gas to calm the patient. 牙医使用笑气来使病人镇静。

Synonyms: nitrous oxide, N2O
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a chemical compound.  One could consider "anesthetic antagonist" as a functional antonym in a medical context.)
词性: noun
