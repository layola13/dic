jukeboxes: /ˈdʒuːkbɒksɪz/ (US) /ˈdʒuːkbɒksɪz/ (UK) | n. |自动点唱机|A coin-operated machine that plays selected music recordings.|

例句：
The old diner still had a couple of jukeboxes in the corner.
那家老餐馆的角落里还有几台自动点唱机。

例句：
We spent hours playing songs on the jukebox.
我们花了几个小时在自动点唱机上播放歌曲。


近义词：record player, music player
反义词: None (No direct antonym for a physical object)
词性: Noun
