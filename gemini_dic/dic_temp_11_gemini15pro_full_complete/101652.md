irregular:/ɪˈrɛɡjʊlə(r)/ (US) /ɪˈreɡjələ(r)/ (UK)| adj.|不规则的；无规律的；非正规的；不正当的|Not according to established rules, methods, or practice; not regular in form or arrangement.|Her heartbeat was irregular. 她的心跳不规律。|synonyms: uneven, abnormal, atypical, nonstandard, erratic, inconsistent|antonyms: regular, even, normal, standard, typical, consistent|adjective

irregular:/ɪˈrɛɡjʊlə(r)/ (US) /ɪˈreɡjələ(r)/ (UK)| n.|不规则事物；不合格品|Something that is not regular or standard.|Factory seconds are often sold as irregulars. 工厂的次品通常作为不合格品出售。|synonyms: anomaly, exception, oddity|antonyms: standard, norm|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French *irregulier, or from late Latin irregularis, from in- ‘not’ + regularis ‘regular’.
