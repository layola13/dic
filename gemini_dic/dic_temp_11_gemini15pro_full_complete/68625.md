fatigue:/fəˈtiːɡ/ (US), /fəˈtiːɡ/ (UK)| n. v.|疲劳，疲乏；使疲劳|Extreme fatigue can be a symptom of various illnesses.  极度疲劳可能是各种疾病的症状。|例句：
The long journey caused her considerable fatigue. 长途旅行使她感到非常疲劳。
He was fatigued by the constant demands of his job. 他被工作中持续不断的需求弄得筋疲力尽。|近义词：tiredness, exhaustion, weariness|反义词：energy, vigor, freshness|词性:名词，动词

Etymology: From French *fatigue* "weariness, toil," from Old French *fatiguer* "to tire," from Late Latin *fatigare* "to weary," perhaps from Latin *affaticare*, frequentative of *affatim* "sufficiently," from *ad* "to" + *satis* "enough".
