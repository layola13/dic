brawled:/brɔːld/
verb
喧闹地打斗，争吵
fought or argued noisily
The two teams brawled on the field after the game.  两队在比赛结束后在场上发生了争吵。
They brawled in the pub. 他们在酒吧里打了一架。
Synonyms: fought, scuffled, clashed, tussled, grappled
Antonyms: reconciled, mediated, compromised
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *braulen*, of uncertain origin, possibly from Old French *brailler* (“to cry out, quarrel”).
