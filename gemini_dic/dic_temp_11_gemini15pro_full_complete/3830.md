agonistic:/ˌæɡəˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 争斗的，好斗的；对抗性的 | Relating to or resembling agon, struggle, contest. Characterized by aggression or conflict. |  The two rams locked horns in an agonistic display of dominance.  两只公羊互相抵角，以示统治地位。

The agonistic behavior of the chimpanzees was fascinating to observe.  黑猩猩的争斗行为令人着迷。 | 近义词：combative, aggressive, antagonistic, belligerent | 反义词：peaceful, cooperative, conciliatory | 词性: 形容词
