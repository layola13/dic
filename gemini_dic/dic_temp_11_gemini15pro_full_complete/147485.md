overorganized:/ˌoʊvərˈɔːrɡənaɪzd/
adjective
过度组织的；组织过度的
excessively organized; organized to an unnecessary degree
Her desk is so overorganized, it's hard to find anything. 她的桌子组织得太过了，很难找到任何东西。
He's so overorganized that he schedules his bathroom breaks. 他组织得太过了，甚至连上厕所的时间都要安排。
近义词: excessively organized, overly systematic
反义词: disorganized, messy
词性: adjective

over-/ˈoʊvər/
prefix
过度；超过
too much; more than is usual or necessary
overeat 吃得过多
overwork 过度工作
词性: prefix

organized/ˈɔːrɡənaɪzd/
adjective
有组织的；有条理的
arranged or structured in a systematic way
They have a very organized system for dealing with complaints. 他们有一个非常有条理的处理投诉的系统。
The event was well organized and ran smoothly.  这次活动组织得很好，进行得很顺利。
词性: adjective
