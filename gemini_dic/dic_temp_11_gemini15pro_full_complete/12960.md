asteroid:/ˈæstərɔɪd/ /ˈæstərɔɪd/| n. |小行星|A small rocky body orbiting the sun. Most asteroids are found in the asteroid belt, a region between the orbits of Mars and Jupiter.|The asteroid was large enough to cause significant damage if it hit Earth. 这颗小行星如果撞击地球，足以造成重大破坏。|Synonyms: planetoid, minor planet|Antonyms: planet, star|Noun
