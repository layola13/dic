interestedness:/ˈɪntrɪstɪdnəs/
n.
利害关系；关心；兴趣
the state of being interested in something.
Her interestedness in the project was evident. 她对这个项目的兴趣显而易见。
Synonyms: interest, concern, curiosity
Antonyms: apathy, indifference
