meses:/ˈmesɪs/| n.|月份；月数（复数）|Months; the plural of "mes" (month), primarily used in Spanish and Portuguese.|例句：Han pasado seis meses.  Six months have passed. |近义词：months|反义词: N/A|noun

Etymology: Derived from Latin "menses" (months).

Los meses pasan rápidamente.  The months pass quickly.
Hay doce meses en un año. There are twelve months in a year.


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Estamos contando los meses hasta las vacaciones. We are counting the months until vacation.
Ella está trabajando duro estos meses. She is working hard these months.


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Han pasado tres meses desde que nos vimos. Three months have passed since we saw each other.
He vivido aquí por seis meses. I have lived here for six months.


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
He estado estudiando español durante tres meses. I have been studying Spanish for three months.
Hemos estado esperando esto por meses. We have been waiting for this for months.


一般过去时 (Simple Past):

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):

Ella estaba viajando durante esos meses. She was travelling during those months.


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

Ya había vivido allí por tres meses. I had already lived there for three months.


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

El próximo año también tendrá doce meses.  Next year will also have twelve months.


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

Se espera que el proyecto se complete en seis meses.  The project is expected to be completed in six months.


疑问句:
¿Cuántos meses tienes trabajando aquí? How many months have you been working here?
¿En qué mes naciste? In what month were you born?


否定句:

No sé en qué mes es su cumpleaños.  I don't know in which month her birthday is.
