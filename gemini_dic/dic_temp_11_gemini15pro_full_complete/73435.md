foremanships:/ˈfɔːrmənʃɪps/
n.
工头职位；领班职位
The position or role of a foreman or forewoman.  The collective term for multiple such positions.
The company restructured, eliminating several foremanships in the process.  该公司进行了重组，在此过程中取消了几个工头职位。
The foremanships were filled by experienced workers. 这些领班职位由经验丰富的工人担任。
synonyms:  supervisory roles, leadership positions, management positions
antonyms:  subordinate roles, worker positions
