noncontagiously:/ˌnɒnkənˈteɪdʒəsli/
adverb|非接触性地，非传染性地|in a way that is not contagious; without spreading by contact|The disease spreads noncontagiously, primarily through contaminated water. 这种疾病以非接触性方式传播，主要通过受污染的水。|The rash spread noncontagiously across his body.  皮疹以非传染性的方式蔓延到他的全身。|近义词:noncommunicably|反义词:contagiously
词性:adverb
