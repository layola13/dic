hyperproduction:/ˌhaɪpərprəˈdʌkʃən/| n.|过度生产；超量生产|An excessive amount of production, often beyond what is needed or sustainable.  The overproduction of goods can lead to market saturation and falling prices.|The hyperproduction of cheap plastic toys has flooded the market.  廉价塑料玩具的过度生产充斥了市场。|Overproduction, surplus, glut|Underproduction, scarcity|noun
