assegaiing:/ˌæsəˈɡaɪɪŋ/
verb
用细长矛攻击，用标枪投掷
attacking or piercing with an assegai.
The Zulus were assegaiing the British soldiers.  祖鲁人正用细长矛攻击英国士兵。
The warriors were assegaiing their enemies with great ferocity.  战士们凶猛地用标枪投掷敌人。
近义词:spearing, stabbing, piercing, impaling
反义词: defending, protecting, shielding
词性:v.
