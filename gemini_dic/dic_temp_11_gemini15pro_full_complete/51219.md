destocking:/diːˈstɒkɪŋ/
n.|去库存，减少库存|The process of reducing the amount of stock held by a business.  This can be done for various reasons, such as reducing storage costs, preparing for new models, or responding to a decrease in demand.|The company is destocking in anticipation of the new season's merchandise. 该公司正在去库存，以迎接新一季的商品。|inventory reduction, liquidation|stockpiling, accumulation|noun
