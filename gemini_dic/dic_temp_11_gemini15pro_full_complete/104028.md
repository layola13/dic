juju:/ˈdʒuːdʒuː/
n.|护身符，魔力，魔咒|An object believed to have magical powers; a charm or amulet.  A quality that seems to cast a spell; magic.|She wore a juju around her neck for protection. 她脖子上戴着一个护身符以求保护。|synonyms: amulet, charm, talisman, fetish|antonyms: curse, jinx|noun


jujus:/ˈdʒuːdʒuːz/
