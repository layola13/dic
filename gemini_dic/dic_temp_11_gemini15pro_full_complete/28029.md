capacities:/kəˈpæsɪtiz/| n.|容量，能力，才能|The stadium has a seating capacity of 50,000.  这个体育场的座位容量是5万人。|近义词：abilities, capabilities, potential, volume|反义词:inabilities, limitations|noun

capacities:/kəˈpæsɪtiz/| n.|身份，职位|She is acting in her capacity as a lawyer. 她以律师的身份行事。|近义词：role, position, function|反义词: N/A|noun


Etymology: From Latin *capacitas*, from *capax* ("able to hold"), from *capere* ("to take, hold").
