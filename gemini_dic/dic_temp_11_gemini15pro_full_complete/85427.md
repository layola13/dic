has /hæz/ (weak form /həz/, /əz/)
verb|有; 拥有|Indicates possession or ownership.  Also used as an auxiliary verb to form the present perfect tense.|She has a beautiful car. 她有一辆漂亮的车。|Synonyms: owns, possesses|Antonyms: lacks, needs|词性: 动词

has /hæz/ (weak form /həz/, /əz/)
auxiliary verb|已经|Used to form the present perfect tense, indicating a completed action at an unspecified time before now.|He has finished his homework. 他已经完成了他的家庭作业。|Synonyms: (as auxiliary) - |Antonyms: (as auxiliary) - |词性: 助动词

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
He had already finished his work before I arrived. 在我到达之前，他已经完成了他的工作。
She had never seen such a beautiful sunset. 她从未见过如此美丽的日落。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
(Will have) He will have a lot of responsibilities in his new job.  (Will have indicates future possession). 他在新工作中将承担很多责任。（Will have 表示未来拥有）。
(Will have) She will have finished the project by next week. (Will have is used here for future perfect). 到下周，她将完成这个项目。（will have 用于此处表示将来完成时）。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next year, they will have lived in this city for ten years. 到明年，他们将在这个城市生活十年了。
She will have completed her degree by the end of next semester. 到下学期末，她将完成她的学位。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By the time he retires, he will have been working at the company for 40 years. 到他退休时，他将在这家公司工作了 40 年。
By next summer, she will have been studying French for two years. 到明年夏天，她将学习法语两年了。

词源: From Old English *hæfþ (third-person singular present indicative of habban, "to have").  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *habjaną, from Proto-Indo-European *keh₂p- ("to seize, grasp").
