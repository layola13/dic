affiliative:/əˈfɪlɪeɪtɪv/| adj.|亲和的，友好的|Relating to or promoting affiliation, connection, or association.  Characterized by a tendency to form social bonds and seek companionship.|例句：Her affiliative behavior made her popular among her colleagues. 她的友善行为使她在同事中很受欢迎。

例句：Affiliative leadership fosters a sense of belonging within a team.  亲和型领导力能够培养团队的归属感。|近义词：sociable, gregarious, friendly, outgoing, convivial|反义词：aloof, antisocial, withdrawn, unsociable|词性: adjective
词源: From the Late Latin *affiliatus, past participle of affiliare ("to adopt as a son"), from ad- ("to, toward") + filius ("son").
