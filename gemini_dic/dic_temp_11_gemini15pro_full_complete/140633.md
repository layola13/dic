olympiads: /əˈlɪmpiædz/
noun
奥林匹克运动会; 奥林匹克竞赛( olympiad的名词复数 );
A period of four years, reckoned from one celebration of the Olympic Games to the next, used in ancient Greece as a dating system.  / A series of international contests in a particular sport or other competitive pursuit, usually held every four years.
例句：She competed in the math olympiads. 她参加了数学奥林匹克竞赛。

例句：The ancient Greeks used olympiads as a dating system. 古希腊人用奥林匹克运动会纪年。
近义词：Games, competitions, contests
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-competitive events")
词性: noun
