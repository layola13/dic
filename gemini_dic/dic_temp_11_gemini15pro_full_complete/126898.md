narco:/ˈnɑːrkoʊ/ (US) /ˈnɑːkəʊ/ (UK)| n. adj.|麻醉药；毒品；与毒品有关的|A narcotic is a drug that relieves pain and induces drowsiness, stupor, or insensibility.  It can also refer to something that induces sleep or inactivity.|The doctor prescribed a narcotic for the patient's severe pain. 医生给病人开了麻醉药来缓解剧痛。|Synonyms: opiate, analgesic, drug, anesthetic|Antonyms: stimulant, invigorant|noun
