outhowl:/ˌaʊtˈhaʊl/
verb
胜过…的叫喊；比…叫得更响亮
to howl louder or longer than someone or something else
The wolves outhowled the wind. 狼的嚎叫声盖过了风声。
近义词: outshout, outyell
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "be outhowled by")
词性: verb
