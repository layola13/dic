gallimaufry:/ˌɡælɪˈmɔːfri/| n.|大杂烩；混合物；混杂的人群|A confused jumble or medley of things; a heterogeneous mixture.|The flea market was a gallimaufry of secondhand goods, from antique furniture to vintage clothing.|Synonyms: medley, hodgepodge, jumble, mishmash, potpourri, mélange|Antonyms: order, uniformity, homogeneity|noun

gallimaufry:/ˌɡælɪˈmɔːfri/| n.|杂烩菜；古怪的菜肴|A dish made from a mixture of various ingredients, especially leftovers.|The chef's gallimaufry surprised and delighted the diners with its unexpected combinations of flavors.|Synonyms: hash, stew|Antonyms: single dish, simple meal|noun
