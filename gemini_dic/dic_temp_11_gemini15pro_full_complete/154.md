abashedly:/əˈbæʃɪdli/| adv.|害羞地，尴尬地|In a way that shows shame or embarrassment.|She abashedly admitted she had forgotten his birthday. 她不好意思地承认她忘记了他的生日。|例句：He abashedly apologized for his mistake. 他不好意思地为自己的错误道歉。|近义词：sheepishly, shyly, embarrassedly|反义词：boldly, confidently|词性：副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From abash + -ed + -ly.  Abash comes from Old French *esbahir "to astonish," probably from Vulgar Latin *exbadare "to gape, yawn," from Latin ex- "out" + badare "to gape."
