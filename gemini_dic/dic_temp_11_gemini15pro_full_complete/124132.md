mortgagors:/ˈmɔːrɡɪdʒɔːrz/
|n.|抵押人，按揭人|A mortgagor is a person who borrows money and gives a mortgage on their property as security for the loan.|The mortgagors were struggling to keep up with their monthly payments. 抵押人正在努力支付每月的款项。|
|The bank contacted the mortgagors to discuss options for loan modification. 银行联系了抵押人，讨论贷款修改方案。 |
|Synonyms: borrower, debtor|Antonyms: lender, creditor|Noun|
|Etymology: From Old French *mortgageor, from mortgager "to mortgage," from mort "dead" + gage "pledge."|
