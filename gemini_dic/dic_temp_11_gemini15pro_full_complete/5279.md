allopathy:/əˈlɒpəθi/
n.
对抗疗法
A system of medical practice that aims to combat disease by using remedies that produce effects different from or opposite to those produced by the disease itself.
Allopathy is the most common type of medicine practiced in the West. 对抗疗法是西方最常见的医学类型。
Conventional medicine offers a range of allopathic treatments.  传统医学提供一系列对抗疗法。
synonym: conventional medicine, mainstream medicine, Western medicine, orthodox medicine
antonym: homeopathy, naturopathy, alternative medicine
noun
