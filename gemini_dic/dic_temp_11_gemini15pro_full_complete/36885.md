colorer's:/ˈkʌlərərz/
noun
着色师的，染色师的
Belonging to or associated with a colorer; a colorer's tools.
The colorer's palette was covered in vibrant hues. 着色师的调色板上布满了鲜艳的色彩。
The colorer's studio was filled with canvases. 染色师的工作室里摆满了画布。
Synonyms: dyer's, painter's, artist's
Antonyms: none
n.


colorer:/ˈkʌlərər/
noun
着色师，染色师
A person who colors something, especially fabrics or other materials.
The colorer carefully applied the dye to the silk. 着色师小心地将染料涂在丝绸上。
The colorer mixed the paints to create the perfect shade. 染色师混合颜料以创造完美的色调。
Synonyms: dyer, painter, artist
Antonyms: none
n.

Etymology:
From "color" + "-er" (agent suffix) + "-'s" (possessive suffix).  "Color" itself ultimately derives from Latin "color".
