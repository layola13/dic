ferocity:/fəˈrɒsɪti/
n.|凶猛；残暴；暴烈|The ferocity of the storm was frightening.  这场风暴的凶猛程度令人恐惧。|The lion attacked its prey with ferocity.  狮子凶猛地攻击它的猎物。|近义词：fierceness, savagery, brutality, violence|反义词：gentleness, mildness, calmness, docility|词性: 名词
Etymology: From Late Latin *ferocitas, from Latin ferox ("fierce, wild, savage") + -itas (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰwer- (“wild animal”).
