exorcise:/ˈɛksɔːrsaɪz/ /ˈɛksərˌsaɪz/| vt.|驱魔，驱除（坏念头等）|To expel (an evil spirit) from a person or place by means of a religious ritual; to free from bad thoughts.|The priest exorcised the demons from the possessed man.  牧师从被附身的人身上驱除了恶魔。|Synonyms: expel, cast out, drive out, purge|Antonyms: invite, welcome|verb

exorcise:/ˈɛksɔːrsaɪz/ /ˈɛksərˌsaɪz/| n.|驱魔，驱邪|The act of exorcising.|The exorcise was performed in the old church. 驱魔仪式是在古老的教堂里举行的。|Synonyms: purification, cleansing|Antonyms: summoning, invocation|noun
