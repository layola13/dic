gurgoyle:/ˈɡɑːrɡɔɪl/ | n. | 石像鬼，滴水嘴兽 | A carved stone figure, often grotesque, projecting from a roof gutter to throw rainwater clear of a building. | The cathedral was adorned with numerous gargoyles.  这座大教堂装饰着许多石像鬼。 | synonyms: grotesques, chimeras | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "decoration" in a broad sense) | noun
