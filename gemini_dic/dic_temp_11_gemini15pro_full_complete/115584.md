marathoner:/ˈmærəθɑːnər (US), ˈmærəθənə(r) (UK)| n. |马拉松运动员|A person who runs marathons.  A marathon is a long-distance running race of 26 miles and 385 yards (42.195 kilometers).|Eliud Kipchoge is a world-renowned marathoner.  埃利乌德·基普乔格是一位世界知名的马拉松运动员。

Marathoner Sarah Hall achieved a personal best in the Boston Marathon. 马拉松运动员莎拉·霍尔在波士顿马拉松比赛中取得了个人最好成绩。|近义词：distance runner, long-distance runner|反义词: sprinter|词性:noun
