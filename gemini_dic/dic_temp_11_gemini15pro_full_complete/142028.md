organo:/ɔːrˈɡænoʊ/ (US), /ɔːˈɡɑːnəʊ/ (UK)
adjective
有机金属的
relating to or denoting compounds containing carbon-metal bonds
Organometallic chemistry is the study of organometallic compounds. 有机金属化学是有机金属化合物的研究。
Organolithium reagents are commonly used in organic synthesis. 有机锂试剂常用于有机合成。
近义词: organometallic
反义词: inorganic
词性: adjective
