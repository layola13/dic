enharmonic:/ɛn.hɑːrˈmɑːn.ɪk/ | /ɛn.hɑːrˈmɑː.nɪk/ | adj. n. | 等音的，等音程；等音 | (of two notes, intervals, or keys) having the same pitch but written in different notation.  |  C sharp and D flat are enharmonic.  升C 和降D 是等音的。 | synonyms: equivalent, identical, same, equal | antonyms: dissonant, inharmonic | adjective, noun
