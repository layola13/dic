lazed:/leɪzd/
verb
懒散地度过；无所事事
spent time idly or relaxed; did nothing in particular
He lazed by the pool all afternoon. 他整个下午都在泳池边懒洋洋地度过。
They lazed around the house all weekend.  他们整个周末都懒散地在家里闲逛。
Synonyms: lounged, relaxed, idled, loafed
Antonyms: worked, toiled, hustled
词性: 动词
