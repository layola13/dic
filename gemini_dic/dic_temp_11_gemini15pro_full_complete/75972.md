funkyest:/ˈfʌŋkiɪst/
adjective
最时髦的；最放克的
superlative form of funky: most funky.  Having or using a strong dance rhythm, usually with syncopation.  Can also refer to something unconventional, stylish, or cool.
That's the funkyest beat I've heard all year! 这是我今年听过的最时髦的节拍了！
Her apartment is decorated in the funkyest style. 她的公寓以最时髦的风格装饰。
近义词:coolest, hippest, trendiest
反义词:squarest, dullest, most boring
词性:adjective
