grumousness:/ˈɡrʌmɪnəsnɪs/
noun
脾气坏;  性情乖戾;  暴躁
The state or quality of being grumpy; sullenness; bad temper.
His grumousness this morning made everyone avoid him.  他今天早上的暴躁脾气让每个人都躲着他。
grumpiness, sullenness, bad temper, irritability, cantankerousness
cheerfulness, good humor, affability
