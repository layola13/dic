forceful:/ˈfɔːrsfəl/ (US) /ˈfɔːsfʊl/ (UK)| adj.|强有力的，有说服力的|Having or showing great energy and determination; effective and powerful.|She delivered a forceful speech that persuaded many people. 她发表了一场强有力的演讲，说服了很多人。|The lawyer made a forceful argument in defense of his client. 律师为他的客户进行了强有力的辩护。|近义词：powerful, compelling, assertive, strong, vigorous|反义词：weak, ineffective, feeble|词性:形容词

词源: From force + -ful.  Ultimately from Latin fortis "strong."
