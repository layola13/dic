corollaceous:/ˌkɒrəˈleɪʃəs/ /ˌkɔːrəˈleɪʃəs/ | adj. | 花冠状的；合瓣花的 | Having petals united to form a corolla, as in flowers of the primrose family. |  Example 1: The corollaceous flowers of the morning glory vine climbed the trellis. 牵牛花藤蔓上花冠状的花爬上了棚架。

Example 2: Many members of the nightshade family have distinctively corollaceous blooms. 茄科的许多成员都有独特的合瓣花。 | 近义词: gamopetalous, sympetalous | 反义词: polypetalous | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "corolla" (little crown, garland) + "-aceous" (suffix forming adjectives meaning "of the nature of," "resembling").  "Corolla" itself comes from "corona" (crown).
