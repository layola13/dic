narratives:/ˈnærətɪvz/| n.|叙述；故事；记叙文|a spoken or written account of connected events; a story.|She gave a thrilling narrative of her escape. 她讲述了她惊险的逃亡经历。|近义词：story, account, tale, recital|反义词:  None (Narratives don't have a direct opposite.  Perhaps "silence" or "facts/data" could be considered antonyms in certain contexts.)|noun
