chaffer:/ˈtʃæfər/ (US), /ˈtʃæfə(r)/ (UK)| v. n.| 讨价还价；闲谈；便宜货|To bargain, haggle, or dispute about the price of something; to talk casually; a cheap article.|例句：They spent hours chaffering over the price of the rug. 他们花了几个小时讨价还价这条地毯的价格。|近义词：bargain, haggle, barter, negotiate, dicker|反义词：acquiesce, concede, comply|词性:动词,名词

chaffer:/ˈtʃæfər/ (US), /ˈtʃæfə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 便宜货；闲谈|A cheap article; casual talk or gossip.|例句：She found some real chaffers at the flea market. 她在跳蚤市场找到了一些真正的便宜货。|近义词：bargain, steal, deal|反义词：rip-off, swindle|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ceapfaru "bargaining, trade," from ceap "bargain" (see cheap) + faru "journey, expedition."  The sense evolution is from "act of bargaining" to "casual talk, chatter" (perhaps from the social interaction involved in bargaining).
