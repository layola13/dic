arrogators: /ˈærəɡeɪtərz/
n.
僭越者；霸占者；擅取者
Those who arrogate to themselves the right to decide.  那些僭越自封有权做决定的人。

The government arrogated to itself the power to control the media. 政府僭取了控制媒体的权力。

Near Synonyms: usurpers, appropriators,  seizers
Antonyms:  yielders, relinquishes, abdicators
词性: noun (plural)
