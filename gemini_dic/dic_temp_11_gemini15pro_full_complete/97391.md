infrastapedial:/ˌɪnfrəstəˈpiːdiəl/| adj.|镫骨下的，位于镫骨下面的|Situated or occurring below the stapes (a small stirrup-shaped bone in the middle ear).|The infrastapedial artery is a branch of the internal carotid artery.|近义词:substapedial|反义词:suprastapedial|词性:adjective
Etymology: infra- (Latin, below) + stapes (Latin, stirrup) + -ial (suffix forming adjectives)
