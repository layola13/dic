canaliculate:/kəˈnælɪkjʊləɪt/ | adj. | 具小沟的，有细管的 | Having a small channel or groove; longitudinally grooved. | The shell of the snail was canaliculate, with delicate grooves running along its surface.  蜗牛的壳有细小的沟槽，沿着它的表面有精致的凹槽。| grooved, furrowed, channeled | smooth, flat | adjective

canaliculated:/kəˈnælɪkjʊləɪtɪd/ | adj. | 具小沟的，有细管的 | Having a small channel or groove; longitudinally grooved. | The bone was canaliculated, allowing for the passage of blood vessels. 这块骨头有细小的沟槽，允许血管通过。| grooved, furrowed, channeled | smooth, flat | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "canaliculatus," past participle of "canaliculare" ("to channel"), from "canalis" ("channel, pipe, groove").
