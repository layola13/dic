chondrule:/ˈkɒndr(j)uːl/ | n. | 球粒 | A small, round, glassy body found in some stony meteorites, believed to have formed from molten droplets in space. |  Chondrules are a key component of chondritic meteorites. 球粒是球粒陨石的关键组成部分。 |  Scientists study chondrules to understand the early solar system. 科学家研究球粒以了解早期太阳系。 | synonyms: spherule, globule | antonyms: None | noun
