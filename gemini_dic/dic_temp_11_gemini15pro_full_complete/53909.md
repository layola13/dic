discount:/ˈdɪskaʊnt/ (US), /ˈdɪskaʊnt/ (UK)| n. vt.|折扣，打折|A deduction from the usual cost of something.|The store offered a 10% discount on all purchases.  这家商店所有商品都打九折。|近义词：reduction, rebate, allowance|反义词:premium, surcharge|noun
discount:/ˈdɪskaʊnt/ (US), /ˈdɪskaʊnt/ (UK)| vt.|打折，不予考虑|To deduct an amount from (a cost). To regard (something) as unlikely or unimportant.|They discounted the price by 20%. 他们把价格打八折。|近义词：reduce, disregard, ignore|反义词:value, emphasize|verb
