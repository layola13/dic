achievers: /əˈtʃiːvərz/
noun
成功者，获得成就的人
People who have accomplished something significant, reached a goal, or attained success.
The awards ceremony recognized the achievements of young achievers in science and technology.  这个颁奖典礼表彰了科技领域年轻有为者的成就。
The company is looking for achievers who are motivated and results-oriented.  这家公司正在寻找有动力且注重结果的成功人士。
近义词: winners, victors, successful people, high-flyers
反义词: failures, losers, underachievers
词性: noun

achieve: /əˈtʃiːv/
verb
实现，达到，完成
Successfully reach a desired objective, goal, or result.
She worked hard and achieved her goal of becoming a doctor. 她努力工作，实现了成为一名医生的目标。
He achieved success through hard work and determination. 他通过努力和决心获得了成功。
近义词: accomplish, attain, reach, fulfill
反义词: fail, fall short of
词性: verb
