heteroatom:/ˈhetərəʊˌætəm/ (UK), /ˈhɛtəroʊˌætəm/ (US)| n. |杂原子|An atom other than carbon in the ring of a cyclic organic compound, or other than carbon or hydrogen, e.g. nitrogen, oxygen, sulfur, phosphorus, or silicon.|Heterocyclic compounds contain heteroatoms such as nitrogen or oxygen.|近义词: non-carbon atom|反义词: carbon atom|词性: noun
