marcobrunner: /ˈmɑːrkoʊˌbrʌnər/ | n. | 马克布鲁纳（一种葡萄酒） | A type of wine, typically a dry white wine, from the Alto Adige region of Italy, named after the St. Magarethen monastery.  It is primarily made from the grape variety Manzoni Bianco. |  This Marcobrunner pairs beautifully with seafood.  这款马克布鲁纳葡萄酒与海鲜完美搭配。|  Synonyms:  Alto Adige white, Italian white | Antonyms:  Red wine | noun
