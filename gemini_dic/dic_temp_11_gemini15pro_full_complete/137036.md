nonstealable:/nɒnˈstiːləbl/
adjective
不可偷的；无法偷窃的
Not capable of being stolen, either because of its nature or because of protective measures.
This information is encrypted and nonstealable.  这个信息已加密，无法被盗。
The bike is locked to a pole, making it effectively nonstealable.  自行车锁在杆子上，实际上无法被盗。
近义词:unstealable, secure, protected, safe
反义词:stealable, vulnerable, unprotected
词性:adjective
