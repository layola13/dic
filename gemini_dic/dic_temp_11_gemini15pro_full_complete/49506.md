demagog:/ˈdeməɡɑːɡ/| n. |煽动者，蛊惑民心的政客|A political leader who seeks support by appealing to popular desires and prejudices rather than by using rational argument.|The demagog rallied the crowd with his fiery rhetoric.  煽动者用他激烈的言辞煽动群众。|近义词：rabble-rouser, agitator, firebrand|反义词：statesman, conciliator|noun


demagogue:/ˈdeməɡɑːɡ/| n. |煽动者，蛊惑民心的政客|A political leader who seeks support by appealing to popular desires and prejudices rather than by using rational argument.|The demagogue whipped the crowd into a frenzy.  煽动者使人群陷入疯狂。|近义词：rabble-rouser, agitator, firebrand|反义词：statesman, conciliator|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dēmagōgus*, from Ancient Greek δημαγωγός (dēmagōgós, “leader of the people”), from δῆμος (dêmos, “people”) + ἄγω (ágō, “I lead”).
