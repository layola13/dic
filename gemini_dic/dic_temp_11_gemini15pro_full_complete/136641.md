nonscholastically:/ˌnɒn skəˈlæstɪkli/
adverb
非学术地，非学校地
In a manner not pertaining to school or academic studies; outside of formal education.
She learned about life nonscholastically, through experience and observation. 她通过经验和观察，非学校地学习生活。
nonscholastically, he pursued his interest in photography. 他在校外追求他对摄影的兴趣。
Synonym: extracurriculaly, informally
Antonym: academically, scholastically
词性: adverb
