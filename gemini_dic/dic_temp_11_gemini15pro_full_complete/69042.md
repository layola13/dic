faubourg: /foʊˈbʊərɡ/ (US), /ˈfoʊbʊəɡ/ (UK)| n.|郊区，城郊，市郊|A suburb, especially one in France or French Canada.|例句：The faubourg was a mix of grand houses and smaller, more modest dwellings.  这个郊区是宏伟的房子和更小、更朴素的住宅的混合体。

例句：They lived in a quiet faubourg outside the city walls.  他们住在城墙外一个安静的郊区。|近义词：suburb, outskirts, periphery|反义词：city center, downtown, urban core|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Old French *forsbourg, from fors "outside" (from Latin foris "outside, out of doors") + bourg "town" (from Frankish *burgs, from Proto-Germanic *burgz "fortress, castle").
