daphnetin:/ˌdæfˈnɛtɪn/| n. |瑞香素|A dihydroxycoumarin derivative found in Daphne mezereum and other plants. It is used in some fluorescent dyes and as an antioxidant.|Daphnetin exhibits strong antioxidant activity and may have therapeutic potential. 瑞香素具有很强的抗氧化活性，可能具有治疗潜力。|Synonyms: 7,8-dihydroxycoumarin|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a chemical compound)|noun
