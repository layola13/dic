feast:/fiːst/| n. vi.|盛宴，节日；尽情地吃|A large meal, especially one held to celebrate a special occasion.  A thing that gives great pleasure.|例句：We prepared a feast for the guests. 我们为客人们准备了一场盛宴。

例句：The art exhibition was a feast for the eyes.  这场艺术展览是一场视觉盛宴。|近义词：banquet, celebration, festival|反义词:fast, famine|noun

feast:/fiːst/| vi.|尽情地吃，享受|To eat and drink sumptuously.|例句：We feasted on the delicious food. 我们尽情享用美食。

例句：They feasted their eyes on the beautiful scenery. 他们饱览了美丽的景色。|近义词：indulge, revel|反义词:starve, abstain|verb
