honeyedness:/ˈhʌnidnəs/ /ˈhʌnidnɪs/
n.
甜蜜；甜言蜜语；奉承
The quality of being honeyed; sweetness, especially of speech or tone.
Her voice was full of honeyedness, but I knew she wasn't sincere. 她的声音充满了甜蜜，但我知道她并不真诚。

近义词:sweetness, mellowness, smoothness, unctuousness
反义词:bitterness, harshness, acrimony, asperity
词性:noun
