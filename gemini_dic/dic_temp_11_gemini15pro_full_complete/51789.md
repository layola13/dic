devouring:/dɪˈvaʊərɪŋ/| adj.|吞食的，狼吞虎咽的；着迷的，极感兴趣的| consuming greedily; very enthusiastically interested in something |  The fire was devouring the forest. 大火正在吞噬森林。 | synonyms: consuming, engulfing, absorbing | antonyms: sparing, preserving | adjective

devour:/dɪˈvaʊər/| vt.|吞食，狼吞虎咽地吃；贪婪地阅读；充满（强烈的感情）；毁灭，吞噬| to eat something quickly and hungrily; to read something quickly and eagerly; to be filled with a strong emotion; to destroy something completely | He devoured the entire pizza in minutes. 他几分钟内就吞下了整个披萨。 | synonyms: consume, gobble, gulp | antonyms: nibble, savor | verb
