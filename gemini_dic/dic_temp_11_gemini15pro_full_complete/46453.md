daguerreotyping:/dəˈɡɛrəˌtaɪpɪŋ/
n.
银版照相术，拍摄银版照片
the process of producing photographs on a silver-coated copper plate.
Example: Daguerreotyping was an early form of photography. 银版照相法是一种早期的摄影形式。

Example: The museum has an impressive collection of daguerreotyping portraits.  这家博物馆收藏了令人印象深刻的银版肖像照。
Synonym: photography, silver plate photography
Antonym:  digital photography (a contrasting modern method)
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Louis-Jacques-Mandé Daguerre (1787–1851), French artist and inventor of the daguerreotype + -ing.
