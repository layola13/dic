overflow's:/ˈəʊvəfləʊz/ /ˈoʊvərfloʊz/ | n. | 溢出，泛滥，过多 |  The excess of something that cannot be contained within its normal limits.  | The river overflowed its banks after the heavy rain. 河水在大雨后漫过了河岸。 | synonyms: surplus, excess, overabundance | antonyms: shortage, deficit, lack | noun


overflow's:/ˌəʊvəˈfləʊz/ /ˌoʊvərˈfloʊz/ | v. |  溢出，泛滥，充满 | To flow over the brim or top of a container; to be filled beyond capacity. | The bathtub overflowed and flooded the bathroom floor. 浴缸溢水，淹没了浴室地板。 | synonyms: flood, inundate, brim over | antonyms: drain, empty, deplete | verb
