massivenesses:/ˈmæsɪvnəsɪz/| n. |巨大；庞大；雄伟|The quality of being massive; enormous size, weight, or extent.|The massivenesses of the ancient pyramids are awe-inspiring. 古老金字塔的雄伟气势令人敬畏。|Synonyms: enormity, immensity, vastness, hugeness|Antonyms: smallness, tininess, minuteness|noun

Etymology: From massive + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  "Massive" comes from the French "massif" ultimately from the Latin "massa" meaning "lump, kneaded dough".
