landlords:/ˈlændlɔːrdz/
n.
房东，业主
A person who owns and rents out land or buildings.
The landlord raised the rent by 10%.  房东将租金提高了10%。
lessors, property owners
tenants

landlord:/ˈlændlɔːrd/
n.
（旅馆、酒吧等的）店主，老板
The owner or manager of a pub, inn, or similar establishment.
The landlord offered us a free drink.  店主请我们喝了一杯免费的饮料。
innkeeper, publican, proprietor
customer, guest
