mouti:/ˈmaʊti/| n.| 嘴，嘴巴 (通常指动物的嘴，尤其是非洲的) |A mouti is the mouth of an animal, especially one from Africa. |The lion opened its mouti wide and roared. 狮子张开大嘴咆哮。|近义词：mouth, maw, chops (informal) |反义词: N/A |词性: 名词


词源:
From Afrikaans *muit*, ultimately from Dutch *muit*.
