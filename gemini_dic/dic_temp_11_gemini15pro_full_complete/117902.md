melotype:/ˈmɛləˌtaɪp/| n.|美乐型照片，密纹照片|A photographic process in which a collodion negative is backed with black varnish or other dark material to make the image appear positive.  It creates a unique, almost gem-like quality.|例句：The melotype, with its rich dark tones, was a popular format for portraiture in the mid-19th century.  美乐型照片以其丰富的暗色调，是19世纪中期流行的肖像画格式。

例句：He collected antique melotypes, fascinated by their small size and delicate detail. 他收集古董美乐型照片，着迷于它们的小尺寸和精致的细节。|近义词：ferrotype, tintype|反义词: None|词性:noun
