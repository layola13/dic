bounce:/baʊns/| v. n.|弹跳，反弹；跳动；活力；(支票等)被拒付退回|The ball bounced twice before he caught it.  球弹跳了两次他才接住。|近义词：leap, spring, rebound, jump|反义词：sink, drop, fall|词性:动词/名词

bounce:/baʊns/| n.|弹跳；活力；弹性|The basketball has good bounce. 这个篮球弹性很好。|近义词：springiness, resilience, elasticity|反义词：stiffness, rigidity|词性:名词

Etymology:  From Old French *bombon "cotton," alteration of Latin *bombac-, from Greek *bombyx "silk, cotton."  The sense evolution is from "cotton" to "padding," then to "something that bounces," and finally to the verb "to bounce."
