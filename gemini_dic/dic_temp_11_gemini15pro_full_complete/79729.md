glueynesses:/ˈɡluːɪnɪsɪz/
n.
粘性；胶粘性
The glueynesses of the sap made it difficult to remove from my hands.  树汁的粘性使它很难从我的手上清除。
The glueynesses of the adhesive ensured a strong bond.  这种粘合剂的粘性确保了牢固的粘合。
Near synonyms: stickiness, tackiness, adhesiveness
Near antonyms: slipperiness, smoothness
noun


Etymology->
From gluey + -ness.  Gluey itself comes from glue + -y.  Ultimately from Old French glu (glue).
