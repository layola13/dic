dorbeetle:/ˈdɔːrˌbiːtl/| n. |蜣螂，屎壳郎|A beetle that feeds on dung, typically rolling it into a ball.|例句：The dorbeetle rolled the dung into a perfect sphere. 蜣螂把粪便滚成一个完美的球体。

例句：Dorbeetles play a vital role in nutrient recycling. 蜣螂在营养循环中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：dung beetle|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *dorbetel, from Old English *dōrbeetle, from dōr (“dung”) + beetle. Cognate with Dutch torkever (“dorbeetle”).
