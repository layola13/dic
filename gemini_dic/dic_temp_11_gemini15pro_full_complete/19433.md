bicoastalism:/ˌbaɪˈkoʊstəlɪzəm/
n.
双海岸生活；两岸生活；横跨东西海岸的生活方式
The practice or state of having homes and spending time on both the East Coast and West Coast of the United States.
Many Hollywood actors and producers practice bicoastalism, maintaining homes in both Los Angeles and New York. 好莱坞的许多演员和制片人过着双海岸生活，在洛杉矶和纽约都拥有住宅。

近义词: multi-residence, dual residency
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) localized living, single residence
