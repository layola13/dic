chart-toppers:/ˈtʃɑːrtˌtɑːpərz/
n.|排行榜冠军;热门歌曲;畅销作品|A song or album that reaches the top position on a music chart.|例句：The band's latest single became one of the year's chart-toppers.  这支乐队的最新单曲成为年度热门歌曲之一。|例句：The chart-toppers of the 1980s are still popular today.  80年代的热门歌曲如今依然流行。|近义词：hits, bestsellers, number ones|反义词:flops, failures
词性:名词(plural noun)
词源:Compound noun formed from "chart" (meaning a ranked list) and "topper" (meaning something that is at the top).
