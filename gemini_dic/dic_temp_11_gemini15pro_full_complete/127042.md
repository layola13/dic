nasties:/ˈnæstiz/
n.
令人讨厌的人或事物；下流的东西
unpleasant or harmful things or people
The box was full of nasties like spiders and cockroaches. 盒子里满是蜘蛛和蟑螂之类的讨厌东西。
Synonyms: unpleasant things, disagreeable things, horrors,  unpleasantries
Antonyms: pleasantries, nice things, goodies
noun
