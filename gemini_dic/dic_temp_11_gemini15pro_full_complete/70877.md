finochio:/fɪˈnoʊkioʊ/
n.
fennel, Florence fennel
A variety of fennel cultivated for its enlarged, bulbous leaf base which is eaten as a vegetable.
The chef prepared a delicious salad with thinly sliced finocchio and oranges.  厨师用切成薄片的茴香和橙子做了一份美味的沙拉。
近义词:fennel
反义词: (No direct antonym for a vegetable)
词性:noun
