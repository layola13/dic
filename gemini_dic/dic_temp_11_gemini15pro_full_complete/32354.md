childlike:/ˈtʃaɪldˌlaɪk/| adj.|孩子般的，天真烂漫的|Having qualities that are typical of a child, especially innocence and simplicity.|She has a childlike enthusiasm for life. 她对生活有着孩子般的热情。|近义词：innocent, naive, artless, simple|反义词：mature, sophisticated, world-weary|词性:形容词

词源: From child + -like.  The suffix "-like" means "resembling" or "characteristic of".
