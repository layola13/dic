in-group:/ˈɪn ɡruːp/| n.| 内群体，所属群体 | An in-group is a social group to which a person psychologically identifies as being a member.  内群体是指一个人在心理上认同为其成员的社会群体。|  例句：She felt a strong sense of belonging to her in-group at school. 她对学校的所属群体有很强的归属感。

| 近义词：clique, circle, crowd | 反义词: out-group | 词性: noun

in-group:/ˈɪn ɡruːp/| adj.| 内群体的 |  Relating to or characteristic of an in-group.  与内群体相关的或具有内群体特征的。|  例句：The in-group bias can lead to discrimination against those outside the group.  内群体偏见会导致对群体外成员的歧视。

| 近义词：cliquey, exclusive | 反义词: out-group | 词性: adjective
