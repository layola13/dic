guidebooks:/ˈɡaɪdˌbʊks/| n.|旅行指南；指南|Books that provide information and advice for travelers, often about a specific place or region.|例句：I always buy guidebooks before traveling to a new city. 我在去一个新城市旅行之前总是会买旅行指南。

例句：The guidebooks recommended several good restaurants in the area. 旅行指南推荐了该地区几家不错的餐厅。|近义词：handbooks, manuals, travel guides|反义词: None| Noun
