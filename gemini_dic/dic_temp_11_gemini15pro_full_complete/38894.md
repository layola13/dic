conferruminated: /kənˈfɜːrʊmɪneɪtɪd/
|v.| 仔细思考；深思熟虑；反复思考|Having thought carefully and deeply about something; having pondered or meditated on a subject at length.|例句：The philosophers conferruminated on the nature of existence. 哲学家们仔细思考了存在的本质。|近义词：pondered, mused, contemplated, deliberated, ruminated|反义词：ignored, disregarded, neglected|词性: 动词

conferruminated: /kənˈfɜːrʊmɪneɪtɪd/
|adj.| 经过深思熟虑的|Having been deeply and carefully thought about.|例句：His response was clearly conferruminated and well-reasoned. 他的回答显然是经过深思熟虑且合乎情理的。|近义词：considered, thought-out, deliberate|反义词：impulsive, rash, unthinking|词性: 形容词

词源:
From Latin  conferruminare  "to cement together," from com- "together" + ferrumen "iron tool."  The sense evolution likely involves the idea of "joining thoughts together," leading to "pondering."
