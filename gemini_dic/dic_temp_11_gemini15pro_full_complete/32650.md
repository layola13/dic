chit:/tʃɪt/| n.|小额欠条，便条，闲聊|A small piece of paper recording a sum owed, a short informal letter, or a casual conversation.|例句：He paid the bill with a chit. 他用欠条付了账单。

例句：She sent me a chit inviting me to lunch. 她给我寄了一张便条，邀请我去吃午饭。|近义词：note, slip, memo, chat|反义词:(没有明显的反义词)|词性:名词

chit:/tʃɪt/| v.|开出欠条，闲聊|To issue a chit or to engage in casual conversation.|例句：He chitted the meal to his room. 他把这顿饭记在他的房间账上。

例句：They were chitting away about the latest gossip. 他们在闲聊最新的八卦。|近义词：bill, charge, chat, gossip|反义词:(没有明显的反义词)|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi *chitthī* meaning "letter, note," ultimately from Sanskrit *chidra-pattra* meaning "torn leaf," from *chid* "to split, cut" and *pattra* "leaf."
