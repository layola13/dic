empathetically: /ˌɛmpəˈθɛtɪkli/ | adv. | 同理心地，设身处地地 | In a way that shows an understanding of and identification with another person's feelings, situation, and motives. | She listened empathetically to his problems. 她设身处地地倾听他的问题。 | 近义词: compassionately, sympathetically, understandingly | 反义词: callously, indifferently, unsympathetically | 词性: 副词

词源: From empathetic + -ally.  Empathetic comes from empathy, which is derived from the German Einfühlung (feeling into).
