balloonlike:/bəˈluːnlaɪk/
adj.
像气球的；气球状的
Resembling a balloon in shape, lightness, or fullness.
The cloud was large and balloonlike.  这朵云又大又像气球。
The dress had balloonlike sleeves.  这条裙子有像气球一样的袖子。
近义词:inflated, puffy, bulbous
反义词:deflated, flat, shrunken
词性:形容词
