noncongealing:/ˌnɒn.kənˈdʒiː.lɪŋ/
adjective
不凝固的
Not solidifying or thickening, especially not changing from a liquid to a solid or semi-solid state.
The scientist studied the noncongealing properties of the liquid.  这位科学家研究了这种液体的非凝固特性。
The noncongealing oil was ideal for use in cold climates.  这种不凝固的油非常适合在寒冷气候下使用。
近义词:non-freezing, non-solidifying
反义词:congealing, solidifying, freezing
词性:adjective
词源: "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "congeal" (from Old French "congeler," from Latin "congelare" - to freeze, thicken) + "-ing" (present participle suffix)
