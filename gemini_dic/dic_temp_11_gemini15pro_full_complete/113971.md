maggots:/ˈmæɡəts/| n. |蛆，幼虫|The soft-bodied legless larva of a fly or other dipterous insect, typically feeding on decaying matter.|The dumpster was swarming with maggots. 垃圾箱里爬满了蛆。|Synonyms: larva, grub, worm|Antonyms: adult, imago|noun
