nonjuridical:/ˌnɑːnˈdʒʊrɪdɪkəl/
adjective
非法律的；非司法性的
Not pertaining to judicial proceedings; not legal.
The organization provides nonjuridical mediation services for community disputes.  这个组织为社区纠纷提供非司法性的调解服务。
The family sought a nonjuridical solution to their inheritance issues.  这家人为他们的遗产问题寻求一个非法律的解决方案。
Synonyms: extralegal, nonlegal, informal
Antonyms: juridical, legal, judicial
词性: 形容词
