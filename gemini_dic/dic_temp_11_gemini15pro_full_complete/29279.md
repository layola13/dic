castaway:/ˈkæstəˌweɪ/ /ˈkɑːstəˌweɪ/| n. adj.|被抛弃的人，漂流者；遭难的；被抛弃的|A person thrown overboard from a ship and stranded on a deserted island or coast; discarded or abandoned.|例句：The castaway survived for months on a deserted island. 那个漂流者在一个荒岛上生存了几个月。|近义词：marooned, stranded, shipwrecked, abandoned, outcast|反义词:rescued, saved|词性:名词, 形容词
castaway:/ˈkæstəˌweɪ/ /ˈkɑːstəˌweɪ/| adj.|遭难的；被抛弃的|Discarded or abandoned.|例句：The castaway boat lay rotting on the beach. 那艘被遗弃的船躺在沙滩上腐烂。|近义词：abandoned, discarded, derelict|反义词:cherished, valued|词性:形容词
