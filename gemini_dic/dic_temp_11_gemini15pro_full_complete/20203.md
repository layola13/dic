birdcatching:/ˈbɜːrdˌkætʃɪŋ/| n.|捕鸟;捕鸟活动|The practice or sport of catching birds, either as a hobby, for food, or for trade.|Birdcatching was once a common practice in many parts of the world. 捕鸟曾经是世界许多地方的常见做法。|近义词：fowling, birding (in the sense of observing birds, not capturing them)|反义词: bird release, bird conservation|noun

birdcatching:/ˈbɜːrdˌkætʃɪŋ/| adj.|捕鸟的|Relating to the activity of catching birds.|He uses birdcatching nets. 他使用捕鸟网。|近义词：fowling|反义词: bird-releasing|adjective
