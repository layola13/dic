impedibility:/ɪmˌpedɪˈbɪlɪti/
|n.|阻碍性，阻碍的能力|The impedibility of the thick mud made it difficult to walk. 浓稠的泥浆阻碍了行走。|例句：The impedibility of the bureaucratic process frustrated everyone involved. 官僚程序的阻碍性让所有相关人员都感到沮丧。

The impedibility of the dense forest made navigation nearly impossible. 茂密的森林的阻碍性使得航行几乎不可能。|近义词：hindrance, obstruction, resistance|反义词：ease, facilitation, aid|词性: noun

Etymology: From "impedible" + "-ity". "Impedible" comes from Latin "impedire" (to hinder, obstruct), from "in-" (in, into, on, against) + "pes" (foot).  The suffix "-ity" forms abstract nouns of quality or condition.
