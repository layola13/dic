denationalizes:/diːˌnæʃənəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使非国有化；取消国有化
To deprive of national character or status; to transfer (a nationalized industry or asset) from public to private ownership.
The government denationalized the airline in the 1990s. 政府在20世纪90年代将这家航空公司私有化。
The new government plans to denationalize several key industries. 新政府计划将几个关键产业私有化。
Synonyms: privatize, deregulate
Antonyms: nationalize, state-control
