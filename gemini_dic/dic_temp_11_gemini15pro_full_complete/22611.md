booze: /buːz/ | n. vi. | 酒；豪饮 | Alcoholic drink. |  He spent all his money on booze. 他把所有的钱都花在喝酒上了。 | 近义词: alcohol, liquor, spirits | 反义词: abstinence, sobriety | 词性: 名词, 动词

booze: /buːz/ | vi. | 豪饮 | To drink alcohol, especially in large quantities. | They boozed all night. 他们整晚都在狂饮。| 近义词: drink, guzzle, imbibe | 反义词: abstain | 词性: 动词

词源:  From Middle Dutch *būsen, from Old French *buiser (compare Walloon buzer "to drink excessively").  Ultimately of Germanic origin.
