conjectural: /kənˈdʒɛktʃərəl/ | adj. | 推测的，猜想的 | Based on or involving conjecture; speculative. |  The detective's theory about the murder weapon was purely conjectural.  侦探关于凶器的理论纯粹是推测的。

The existence of extraterrestrial life remains conjectural. 外星生命的存在仍然是推测性的。 | Synonyms: speculative, hypothetical, theoretical, putative, supposed, presumed | Antonyms: certain, definite, proven, factual, established | word type: adjective
