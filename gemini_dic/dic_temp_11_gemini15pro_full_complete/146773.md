overidealisms: /ˌoʊvər aɪˈdiːəlɪzəmz/
noun
过度理想化
Excessive adherence to ideals, often to the point of impracticality.
His overidealisms about politics led him to disappointment. 他对政治的过度理想化导致了他的失望。
近义词: utopianism, romanticism, quixotism
反义词: realism, pragmatism, cynicism
词性: noun


Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "idealism" (from "ideal" + "-ism")

"ideal":  from Late Latin "idealis" "existing in idea," from "idea."  Ultimately from Greek "idea" "form, pattern," from "idein" "to see."

"-ism": a suffix forming nouns of action, state, condition, doctrine, theory, etc.
