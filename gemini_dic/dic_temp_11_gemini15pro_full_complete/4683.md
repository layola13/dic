aleppo:/əˈlɛpoʊ/
n.
阿勒颇（叙利亚城市）
A city in northern Syria, one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world.
Aleppo has been ravaged by war. 阿勒颇饱受战争蹂躏。
近义词:city, metropolis
反义词:village, countryside
词性:noun

Etymology: From Arabic Ḥalab (حَلَب).
