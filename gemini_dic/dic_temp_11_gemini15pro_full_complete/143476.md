outgamble: /ˌaʊtˈɡæmbəl/
verb
胜过…的赌技；在赌博中赢过
to gamble more successfully than
He outgambled his opponents and won the tournament. 他在赌博中胜过对手，赢得了比赛。
近义词: outplay, outsmart, defeat
反义词: lose to
