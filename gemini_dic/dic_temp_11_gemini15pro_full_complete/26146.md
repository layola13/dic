busts:/bʌsts/
n.|半身像，胸部，突击搜查，失败|A sculpture of a person's head, shoulders, and chest.  The police conducted several drug busts last night. The business venture ended in a bust.|例句：The museum displayed busts of Roman emperors.  博物馆展出了罗马皇帝的半身像。

例句：Her dress emphasized her busts. 她的裙子突出了她的胸部。|近义词：sculpture, chest, raid, failure|反义词:success|词性:noun

busts:/bʌsts/
v.|打破，搜查，降级，逮捕|To break something. To raid a place, typically in search of illegal activity. To demote someone to a lower rank. To arrest someone.|例句：He busted the vase by accident. 他不小心打破了花瓶。

例句：The police busted the illegal gambling operation. 警察突击搜查了非法赌博窝点。|近义词：break, raid, demote, arrest|反义词:repair, promote|词性:verb

词源: From Middle French *buste*, from Italian *busto*, probably from Old Occitan *bust*, ultimately of uncertain origin.
