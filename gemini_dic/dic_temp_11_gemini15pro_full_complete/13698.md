attributiveness:/əˌtrɪbjuːtɪvnəs/ | n. | 属性；归属性；定语性 | The quality of being attributive; the function of an adjective or other modifier.  | The attributiveness of the word "red" is clear in the phrase "red car."  红色这个词在“红色汽车”这个短语中的定语性很明显。 | 近义词: attributive quality, modifying quality | 反义词: predicativeness | 词性: noun
