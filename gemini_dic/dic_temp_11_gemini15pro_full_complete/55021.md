disqualifiable:/dɪsˌkwɒlɪˈfaɪəbl/ | adj. | 可取消资格的，不合格的 |  That which can be disqualified; liable to disqualification.  Able to be shown to be unsuitable or ineligible for something. |  His persistent lateness made him disqualifiable for the promotion. 他的持续迟到使他失去了晋升资格。 | ineligible, unfit, unsuitable | qualified, eligible | adjective

disqualify:/dɪsˈkwɒlɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 取消…的资格，使…不合格 | To deprive of qualification or fitness; to pronounce unfit, unworthy, or incompetent; to disable legally. |  He was disqualified from driving for six months. 他被取消了六个月的驾驶资格。 |  bar, preclude, prohibit | qualify, enable | verb
