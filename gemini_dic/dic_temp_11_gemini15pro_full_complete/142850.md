out-glows:/ˌaʊtˈɡloʊz/
verb
发出比...更强的光; 比...更辉煌
shines more brightly than; surpasses in brilliance
The full moon out-glows all the stars. 满月的光芒胜过所有的星星。
Synonyms: outshines, eclipses
Antonyms: dims, pales
词性: 动词


out-glows:/ˌaʊtˈɡloʊz/
词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "glow" (verb meaning to emit light and heat)
中文翻译: 发出比...更强的光; 比...更辉煌
英文解析: shines more brightly than; surpasses in brilliance
