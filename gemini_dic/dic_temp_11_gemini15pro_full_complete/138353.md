notices:/ˈnoʊtɪsɪz/ | n. |通知，公告；注意，察觉|A formal announcement or warning; the fact of observing or paying attention to something.| The company posted notices about the upcoming layoffs. 公司发布了即将裁员的公告。| synonyms: announcements, warnings, bulletins, observations | antonyms: ignorance, disregard | noun

notice:/ˈnoʊtɪs/ | v. |注意到，察觉|Become aware of or pay attention to someone or something.| I noticed a strange smell in the room. 我注意到房间里有一股奇怪的味道。| synonyms: observe, perceive, detect, spot | antonyms: overlook, miss, ignore | verb
