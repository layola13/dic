interweavingly:/ˌɪntərˈwiːvɪŋli/
adverb
交织地，穿插地
in a way that interweaves or is interwoven; closely and intricately combined.
The stories of the two families were interweavingly connected. 这两个家族的故事交织在一起。
The plot lines of the novel are interweavingly complex. 这部小说的情节错综复杂，交织在一起。
近义词: intricately, interwovenly, intertwinedly
反义词: separately, independently, discretely
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From interweave + -ing + -ly.  "Interweave" comes from the Old English "interweafan," meaning "to weave together." "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," and "weafan" means "to weave."
