Norbertine, Norbertines(复数)
a member of a Roman Catholic religious order founded by St. Norbert in 1120.  诺贝特会修士；(大写)诺贝特会的
The Norbertine canons are known for their white habits. 诺贝特会修士以他们的白色会服而闻名。
synonym: Premonstratensian
