outpitching:/ˌaʊtˈpɪtʃɪŋ/
verb
胜过投球，投球比…更好
performing better than another pitcher or team in a baseball game.
The Mets won because Degrom was outpitching the Braves' starter. 大都会队赢了，因为德格罗姆的投球比勇士队的先发投手更好。
Synonyms: surpassing, exceeding, besting
Antonyms: being outpitched, underperforming
词性: 动词
