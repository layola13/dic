capel:/ˈkæpəl/ | n. | 小教堂；礼拜堂 | A small chapel, often private or attached to a larger building. |  The wedding was held in a beautiful little capel nestled in the woods. 婚礼在一个美丽的、依偎在树林里的小教堂举行。| synonyms: oratory, shrine, chantry | antonyms: cathedral, basilica | noun

capel:/kəˈpɛl/ | n. | (炼钢)焙烧炉; (铸造)熔炉; | A furnace or oven used for refining or smelting metals or other materials, especially in historical contexts. | The old capel was still standing, a testament to the region's industrial past.  古老的熔炉依然矗立，证明了该地区的工业历史。 | synonyms: furnace, oven, kiln | antonyms: cooler, refrigerator | noun

词源:

From Old North French *capel, from Latin cappella (“chapel”). Doublet of chapel.
