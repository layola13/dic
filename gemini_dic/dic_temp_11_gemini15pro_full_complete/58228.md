drugs:/drʌɡz/ | n. | 药物; 毒品 |  Substances used for medical purposes or for altering consciousness or mood. |  The doctor prescribed some drugs for her illness.  医生给她开了一些治病的药。

The police found a large quantity of illegal drugs. 警方发现了大量非法毒品。 | 近义词: medicine, medication (for medical use); narcotics, dope (for illegal use) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "placebo" in a medical context) | 词性: Noun
