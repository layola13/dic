outlaughs:/ˌaʊtˈlæfs/
verb
大笑胜过；笑得比…更久
to laugh longer or louder than someone else
He outlaughed everyone in the room. 他笑得比房间里的每个人都久。
outlaugh, outjest, surpass in laughing
