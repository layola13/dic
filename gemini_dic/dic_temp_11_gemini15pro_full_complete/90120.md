hot-tubbed:/ˈhɒt tʌbd/ (UK) /ˈhɑːt tʌbd/ (US)| v.|泡热水澡|Used as a verb in the past tense, indicating the action of using a hot tub.  Derived from "hot tub" (n.) and the suffix "-ed" which forms the past tense of regular verbs.|We hot-tubbed under the stars last night. 我们昨晚在星空下泡了热水澡。|近义词：soaked, relaxed in a hot tub|反义词: showered, bathed (in a regular tub)|词性: verb
