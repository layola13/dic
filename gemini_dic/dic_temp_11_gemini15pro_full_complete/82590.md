guardian:/ˈɡɑːrdiən/ (US), /ˈɡɑːdɪən/ (UK)| n.|监护人，守护者，保护者|A person who is legally appointed to look after the interests of another person, especially a child or someone who is unable to manage their own affairs. |The court appointed a guardian for the orphaned children. 法院为孤儿指定了监护人。

|近义词：protector, custodian, caretaker, keeper|反义词：ward|词性：名词

guardian:/ˈɡɑːrdiən/ (US), /ˈɡɑːdɪən/ (UK)| adj.|守护的，保护的|Serving as a protector or keeper. |The guardian angel watched over the sleeping child. 守护天使守护着熟睡的孩子。


|近义词：protective, safeguarding|反义词：neglectful|词性：形容词
