overfrequently:/ˌoʊvərˈfriːkwəntli/
adverb
过于频繁地，过于经常地
Happening or done too often.
The word is used to describe something that occurs with excessive frequency.

例句：
The report overfrequently cited anecdotal evidence.
该报告过于频繁地引用轶事证据。

He overfrequently interrupts during meetings.
他在会议期间过于频繁地打断别人。

近义词：too often, excessively often, overmuch
反义词：infrequently, rarely, seldom
词性：adverb

词源：
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "frequently" (adverb meaning "often")
