emanating:/ˈeməneɪtɪŋ/| v. (present participle)|散发，发出，起源于|spreading out from a source|A sweet smell was emanating from the kitchen. 厨房里飘出一股香味.|近义词：emerging, flowing, radiating, stemming, originating|反义词：absorbing, concealing, suppressing|词性: verb (present participle)

Etymology:
From Latin emanare "to flow out," from ex- "out" + manare "to flow."
