conventionalize:/kənˈvɛnʃənəˌlaɪz/ | v. |使习俗化，使惯例化；使合乎传统|To make conventional; to cause to conform to accepted standards or practices.|The artist refused to conventionalize his style.  这位艺术家拒绝使自己的风格传统化。| The company conventionalized the manufacturing process to improve efficiency. 公司将制造过程规范化以提高效率。| 近义词: standardize, formalize, normalize | 反义词: individualize, personalize |词性:动词
