chide:/tʃaɪd/ | v. | 责骂，斥责 | To scold or rebuke someone gently. |  He chided her for being late. 他责备她迟到了。| Synonyms: scold, rebuke, reprove | Antonyms: praise, commend, approve | verb

词源:
From Middle English chiden, from Old English cīdan (“to scold, reprove, complain, quarrel”), from Proto-Germanic *kīdaną (“to scold, complain”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰeyd- (“to incite, call, scold”). Cognate with Dutch kijven (“to scold”), German keifen (“to scold, quarrel”).
