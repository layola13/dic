ideologue:/ˈaɪdiəlɒɡ/, /ˈiːdiəlɔːɡ/ | n. | 思想家；意识形态的拥护者；理论家 |  A person who strongly believes in particular ideas, especially political ones, and tries to persuade other people to accept them. |  He is a staunch ideologue who believes capitalism is the only way forward. 他是一位坚定的思想家，认为资本主义是前进的唯一道路。

The party is dominated by left-wing ideologues. 该党由左翼思想家控制。 | 近义词：theorist, doctrinaire, partisan | 反义词：pragmatist | 词性：名词
