oillet:/ˈɔɪlɪt/ | n. | 小油壶；油孔；油嘴 |A small hole, tube, or other device through which oil is introduced to a machine or other mechanism. | Oil flowed smoothly from the oillet, lubricating the gears. 油从油嘴平稳地流出，润滑齿轮。 | synonyms: oil hole, lubricator, oil cup | antonyms: drain, outlet | noun
