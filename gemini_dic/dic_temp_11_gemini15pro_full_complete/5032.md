all-in-the-wind:/ˌɔːl ɪn ðə ˈwɪnd/
adjective
随风飘散的；不确定的；未落实的
scattered by the wind; uncertain; not settled or finalized
The project is all in the wind; we haven't secured funding yet.  这个项目还很不确定；我们还没有获得资金。
Synonyms: uncertain, unsettled, up in the air, tentative, doubtful
Antonyms: definite, certain, fixed, settled, concrete
词性: adjective


all-in-the-wind:/ˌɔːl ɪn ðə ˈwɪnd/
idiom
(of plans, ideas, etc.) uncertain or unreliable; likely to fail or change
(指计划、想法等)不确定或不可靠；可能失败或改变
Our vacation plans are all in the wind now that John has lost his job.  由于约翰失业了，我们的假期计划现在都悬而未决了。
Synonyms: uncertain, unsettled, up in the air, tentative, doubtful
Antonyms: definite, certain, fixed, settled, concrete
词性: idiom
