fine-tuner:/ˌfaɪnˈtuːnər/| n.|微调器，精调器|A person or thing that fine-tunes something; especially : a person who makes small adjustments to something to improve its performance.  A tool or system that performs fine-tuning, often in the context of machine learning.|例句：The engineer acted as a fine-tuner, making minute adjustments to the engine.  工程师充当了微调器的角色，对发动机进行了微小的调整。

例句：He's a fine-tuner of musical instruments. 他是一位乐器微调师。|近义词：adjuster, calibrator, optimizer|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


fine-tune:/ˌfaɪnˈtuːn/| vt.|微调，精调|To make small adjustments to something to improve its performance.|例句：The mechanic fine-tuned the engine to improve its fuel efficiency.  机修工微调了发动机以提高燃油效率。

例句：She fine-tuned her piano playing skills over many years. 她多年来不断精进她的钢琴演奏技巧。|近义词：adjust, calibrate, tweak, refine, optimize|反义词: N/A|词性: verb
