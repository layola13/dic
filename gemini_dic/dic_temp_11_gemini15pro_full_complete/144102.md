outscenting:/ˌaʊtˈsɛntɪŋ/
verb
以更强的香味盖过; 在气味上胜过
overpowering or surpassing in scent
例句：The fox was outscenting the hounds. 狐狸的气味盖过了猎犬。
例句：The perfumer created a new fragrance outscenting all others.  调香师创造了一种新的香水，其香味胜过所有其他香水。

近义词：overpowering, masking, dominating
反义词：succumbing to

词性：v.
