bummalo:/ˈbʌməloʊ/| n.|鲣鱼干，鱼干|A dried and smoked tuna fish, especially as prepared in the Maldives and Sri Lanka.|Example: We enjoyed some delicious bummalo with rice. 我们和米饭一起享用了美味的鲣鱼干。|Example:  The bummalo was a staple food on their island. 鲣鱼干是他们岛上的主食。|近义词：dried fish, cured fish, smoked fish|反义词:fresh fish|noun
