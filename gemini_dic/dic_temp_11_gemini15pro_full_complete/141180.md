oohing:/uːɪŋ/ | n. | 惊叹声，感叹声 | The sound of expressing surprise or pleasure, often drawn out. |  The oohing and aahing over the fireworks display was deafening.  烟花表演上人们的惊叹声震耳欲聋。 | 近义词: exclaiming, marveling | 反义词: groaning, complaining | 词性: 名词

词源:  Derived from the interjection "ooh," which mimics a sound of amazement or delight.  The "-ing" suffix transforms it into a noun representing the act of making this sound.
