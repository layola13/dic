impartments:/ɪmˈpɑːtmənts/
n.
传授，告知；赋予，给予(通常指抽象事物，如知识、品质等)；部门，隔间(较少用)
The act of imparting or the state of being imparted, especially knowledge, skills, or qualities.  Also, less commonly, refers to compartments or sections.
The teacher's impartments of knowledge inspired many students.  这位老师的知识传授激励了许多学生。
Her impartments of wisdom were invaluable to the team. 她赋予团队的智慧是无价的。
Synonyms: communication, transmission, conveyance, bestowal, endowment, sections, compartments
Antonyms: withholding, concealment, suppression, deprivation
noun
