needily:/ˈniːdɪli/| adv.|贫困地；缺乏地；迫切地；依赖地|In a needy manner; showing a great need for affection, attention, or reassurance.|例句：The child clung needily to his mother. 孩子紧紧地依偎着他的母亲。|近义词：desperately, urgently, clingingly, insecurely|反义词：independently, securely, confidently|词性:副词

词源: From "needy" + "-ly."  "Needy" comes from the Old English "nēadig," meaning "compulsory, necessary," related to "nēad" (need).
