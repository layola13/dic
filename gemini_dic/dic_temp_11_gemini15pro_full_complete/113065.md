lumpish:/ˈlʌmpɪʃ/| adj.|笨拙的，迟钝的，呆滞的|Lacking grace or fluency; awkward and clumsy.  Also, heavy and inert; inactive.|He felt lumpish and awkward in his new suit. 他穿着新西装感觉笨拙不自在。|The sick child lay lumpish and unresponsive in bed. 生病的孩子躺在床上，呆滞无反应。|近义词：clumsy, awkward, ungainly, heavy, inert, sluggish|反义词：graceful, agile, nimble, energetic, active|词性:形容词
词源:From lump + -ish.
