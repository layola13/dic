ageisms:/ˈeɪdʒɪzəmz/
noun
年龄歧视（复数）
Prejudice or discrimination on the basis of a person's age.
Many older people experience ageisms in the workplace. 许多老年人在工作场所经历年龄歧视。
Older adults often face ageisms in hiring practices.  老年人在招聘过程中经常面临年龄歧视。
近义词:age discrimination, prejudice against the elderly
反义词:respect for elders, equality
