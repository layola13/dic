dukely:/ˈdjuːkli/| adj.|公爵般的，像公爵的|Resembling or characteristic of a duke, especially in being magnificent or stately.  It implies a sense of grandeur, nobility, and perhaps a bit of arrogance. |He strode into the room with a dukely air. 他以一种公爵般的姿态大步走进房间。|Synonyms: stately, majestic, grand, noble, aristocratic, lordly|Antonyms: humble, modest, unassuming, common, lowly|adjective
