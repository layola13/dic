fossane:/ˈfɒseɪn/ | n. | 马岛獴，隐肛狸 | A carnivorous mammal (Cryptoprocta ferox) of Madagascar, resembling a civet, with a long tail and retractable claws. It is the largest mammalian predator on the island. |  例句：The fossane is a unique predator found only in Madagascar. 马岛獴是一种独特的捕食者，只在马达加斯加发现。

|例句：The fossane's diet consists mainly of lemurs and other small mammals.  马岛獴的食物主要由狐猴和其他小型哺乳动物组成。
