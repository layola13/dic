absorptiveness:/əbˈzɔːrptɪvnəs/| n. |吸收性，吸收能力|The absorptiveness of the sponge is impressive. |The towel's absorptiveness was poor, leaving him dripping wet.|近义词: absorbency, porosity, permeability |反义词: impermeability, impenetrability |词性: noun
Etymology: From absorb (v.) + -ive (suffix forming adjectives) + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives).  "Absorb" comes from Latin "absorbēre," from "ab-" (from, away) + "sorbēre" (to suck in).
