lipids:/ˈlɪpɪdz/| n.|脂质|Any of a class of organic compounds that are fatty acids or their derivatives and are insoluble in water but soluble in organic solvents. They include many natural oils, waxes, and steroids.|High blood cholesterol and other lipids raise the risk of heart disease. 高胆固醇和其他血脂会增加患心脏病的风险。|Synonyms: fats, oils, fatty acids|Antonyms: carbohydrates, proteins|noun
