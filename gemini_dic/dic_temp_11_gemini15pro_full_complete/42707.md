countertransport:/ˈkaʊntərˌtrænspɔːrt/ | n. |反向转运，对向转运|The simultaneous transport of two different molecules across a cell membrane in opposite directions. |  Example 1: The sodium-calcium countertransport mechanism helps regulate intracellular calcium levels. 钠钙反向转运机制有助于调节细胞内钙水平。

Example 2: Countertransport is an important process for maintaining cellular homeostasis. 反向转运是维持细胞稳态的重要过程。 | 近义词: antiport, exchange | 反义词: cotransport, symport | 词性: noun
