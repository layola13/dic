collectorship:/kəˈlektərʃɪp/
n.
收藏家身份；收藏家职位；税务员的职位
The position or office of a collector.
He held the collectorship of rare stamps for many years. 他多年来一直担任稀有邮票收藏家的职位。
Near synonyms:  curatorship, custodianship
Antonyms: dispersal, distribution
noun

collectorship:/kəˈlektərʃɪp/
n.
收藏；收藏品（总称）
The collection itself.
His collectorship of antique toys is impressive.  他收藏的古董玩具令人印象深刻。
Near synonyms:  collection, assemblage
Antonyms:  dispersal, distribution
noun
