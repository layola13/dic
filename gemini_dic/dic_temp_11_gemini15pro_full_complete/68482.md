family: /ˈfæməli/ (US) /ˈfæmɪli/ (UK)| n. |家庭，家人，家族|A group of people related by blood, marriage, or adoption, typically living together or forming a social unit. |例句：My family is very important to me. 我的家庭对我来说非常重要。

例句：We are having a family dinner tonight. 今晚我们有一个家庭聚餐。

近义词：household, kin, relatives|反义词:enemy, foe|词性:noun


family: /ˈfæməli/ (US) /ˈfæmɪli/ (UK)| adj. |家庭的，适合于家庭的|Relating to or suitable for a family. |例句：We went to a family restaurant. 我们去了一家家庭餐馆。

例句：It's a family-friendly hotel. 这是一家适合家庭入住的酒店。

近义词：domestic|反义词:n/a|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *famille "household," from Latin familia "family, servants of a household," ultimately from famulus "servant."
