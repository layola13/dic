cygnus:/ˈsɪɡnəs/| n. |天鹅座|A constellation in the northern sky, representing a swan.  It contains the bright star Deneb.|例句：Cygnus is easily recognizable by its cross shape. 天鹅座很容易通过其十字形状辨认出来。

例句：The constellation Cygnus contains several interesting deep-sky objects. 天鹅座包含几个有趣的深空天体。|近义词：Northern Cross (specifically for the asterism within Cygnus) |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
