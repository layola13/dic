intrathoracic:/ˌɪntrəθəˈræsɪk/ | adj. | 胸内的；位于胸腔内的 | Situated within the thorax (chest cavity). |  The surgeon performed an intrathoracic procedure.  外科医生进行了一项胸腔内手术。

The intrathoracic pressure changes during respiration.  呼吸过程中胸腔内压力会发生变化。

| 近义词：endocardial, intrapleural | 反义词：extrathoracic | 词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From intra- (within) + thoracic (pertaining to the thorax).  "Intra-" comes from Latin, meaning "within". "Thoracic" comes from the Greek "thōrax" meaning "chest, breastplate".
