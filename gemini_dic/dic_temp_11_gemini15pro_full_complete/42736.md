counting:/ˈkaʊntɪŋ/ (US), /ˈkaʊntɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |计数，计算|The act of determining the number of elements in a set or group.  The process of enumerating.|The teacher taught the children counting. 老师教孩子们数数。|enumeration, calculation, numbering|subtraction, ignoring|noun
