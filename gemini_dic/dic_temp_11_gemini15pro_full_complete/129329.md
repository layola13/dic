noiseful:/ˈnɔɪzfʊl/| adj.|吵闹的，喧闹的|Full of noise; loud.  Characterized by or producing a lot of noise.|The noiseful city kept me awake all night. 喧闹的城市让我整夜无法入睡。|近义词：noisy, clamorous, boisterous, raucous|反义词: quiet, peaceful, silent|词性:形容词
词源: From noise + -ful.  "Noise" comes from Old French "noise," meaning "quarrel, disturbance, uproar," ultimately from Latin "nausea," meaning "seasickness."
