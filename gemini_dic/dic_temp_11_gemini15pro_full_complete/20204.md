birded:/ˈbɜːrdɪd/| v. |观察到；记录（鸟类）|To have observed or recorded the presence of a particular bird species. |例句：I birded three new species this weekend.  这个周末我观察到了三种新的鸟类。

例句：They have birded extensively in South America.  他们在南美洲进行了广泛的鸟类观察。|近义词：spotted, observed, recorded, logged |反义词: missed |词性:动词
