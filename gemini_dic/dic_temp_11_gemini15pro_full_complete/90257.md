housecarl:/ˈhaʊskɑːrl/| n.|家臣，扈从，卫士|A soldier in the personal bodyguard of a king or nobleman, especially in Anglo-Saxon England.|King Harold had many loyal housecarls who fought bravely at the Battle of Hastings. 哈罗德国王有许多忠诚的家臣，他们在黑斯廷斯战役中英勇作战。|近义词：bodyguard, retainer, warrior|反义词:enemy, foe|noun
