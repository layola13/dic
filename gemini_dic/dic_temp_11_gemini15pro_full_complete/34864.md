clinopinacoid:/ˌklɪnəˈpɪnəkɔɪd/ | n. |斜板|A crystal face parallel to the vertical axis and inclined to the other two axes.  A form composed of two such faces. |The clinopinacoid is a prominent feature in some monoclinic crystals.|近义词:pinacoid|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Greek *klino* (sloping, inclined) + *pinax* (board, tablet) + *-oid* (resembling).
