highmost:/ˈhaɪˌmoʊst/| adj.|最高的，至高无上的|An archaic or literary word meaning "highest."  It emphasizes the extreme top or peak, often with a sense of grandeur or importance.|The highmost boughs of the ancient trees swayed in the wind. 古老的树木最高的枝桠在风中摇曳。|近义词：highest, uppermost, topmost|反义词：lowest, bottommost|词性:形容词
词源：From Old English *hēahmōst, from hēah (“high”) +‎ -mōst. Cognate with Dutch hoogst (“highest”).
