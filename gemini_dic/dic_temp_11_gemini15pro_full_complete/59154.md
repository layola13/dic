dynasties:/ˈdaɪnəstiz/ (US), /ˈdɪnəstiz/ (UK)| n. |王朝；朝代|A sequence of rulers from the same family, stock, or group.|The Ming dynasty lasted for nearly three centuries. 明朝持续了近三个世纪。|Synonyms: house, lineage, reign, regime|Antonyms: anarchy, chaos|noun
