enthuses:/ɪnˈθuːziz/
v.
使充满热情，使热衷
expresses excitement about something.
She enthuses about her new job. 她对她的新工作充满热情。
近义词: excites, inspires, motivates
反义词: bores, discourages, dampens
词性:动词

enthuse:/ɪnˈθuːz/
v.
充满热情，热衷
To be very excited and interested about something.
He enthused over the prospect of a trip to Europe. 他对去欧洲旅行的前景充满热情。
近义词: excite, inspire, motivate
反义词: bore, discourage, dampen
词性:动词
