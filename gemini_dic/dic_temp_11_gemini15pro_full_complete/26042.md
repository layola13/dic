bushes:/ˈbʊʃɪz/| n.|灌木丛，矮树丛|A group of shrubs or low-growing woody plants.|The birds were singing in the bushes. 鸟儿在灌木丛中歌唱。|shrubs, thickets|trees|Noun
Etymology->From Middle English *busshes, *buskes, plural of bussh, busk (“bush”), from Old English *bysc, a variant of busc (“bush”). Cognate with Scots bus (“bush”), West Frisian bosk (“bush, shrubbery”), Dutch bos (“wood, forest”), German Busch (“bush, shrub”). More at bush.
