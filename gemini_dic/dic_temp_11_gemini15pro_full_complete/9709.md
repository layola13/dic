antitragus: /ˌænˌtiˈtreɪɡəs/ /ˌænˌtiˈtræɡəs/
noun
对耳屏
A small eminence obliquely opposite to the tragus, on the lower border of the auricle of the ear.  耳朵外耳下缘与耳屏相对的一个小隆起。
The piercing went through her antitragus.  穿孔穿过她的对耳屏。
Synonyms:  none (This is a very specific anatomical term)
Antonyms:  none (This is a very specific anatomical term)
词性: noun
