defuse:/diːˈfjuːz/ | vt. | 缓和；拆除（炸弹）| To remove the fuse from (an explosive device) so that it cannot explode; to make something less dangerous or tense. | The bomb squad carefully defused the explosive device. 爆炸小组小心地拆除了爆炸装置。

The negotiators worked to defuse the tense situation.  谈判人员努力缓和紧张的局势。 | 近义词：neutralize, disarm, de-escalate, alleviate | 反义词：aggravate, escalate, ignite | 词性：动词
