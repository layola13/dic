bibliology:/ˌbɪbliˈɒlədʒi/
n.
圣经学，书籍学
The study of books, especially the Bible, including their history, authorship, and textual criticism.
Bibliology is a fascinating field for those interested in religious texts.  圣经学对于那些对宗教文本感兴趣的人来说是一个引人入胜的领域。
He studied bibliology in seminary. 他在神学院学习圣经学。
近义词：Bible study, theology
反义词: None
词性: noun
