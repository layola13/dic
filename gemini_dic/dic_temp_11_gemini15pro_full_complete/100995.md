invaliding:/ɪnˈvælɪdeɪŋ/ | v. | 使无效；使伤残 | Depriving of legal force or efficacy; disabling someone physically or mentally. | The court invalidated the contract. 法庭宣布该合同无效。|  synonyms: nullifying, voiding, canceling, disabling, crippling | antonyms: validating, authorizing, enabling, empowering | verb

invaliding:/ɪnˈvælɪdeɪŋ/ | adj. |  (rare) 使无效的；使伤残的 | Having the effect of invalidating; causing disability. |  The invaliding experience left him scarred.  这次使他伤残的经历给他留下了伤疤。| synonyms: disabling, crippling | antonyms: enabling, empowering | adjective
