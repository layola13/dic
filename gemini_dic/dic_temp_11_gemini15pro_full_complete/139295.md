obliviousness:/əˈblɪviəsnəs/
n.
健忘; 忘却; 不知不觉
the state of being unaware or unconscious of what is happening
He drove on in obliviousness of the danger. 他继续开车，没有意识到危险。

She was in a state of blissful obliviousness. 她处于一种幸福的忘我状态。

unawareness, inattention, heedlessness, insensibility
consciousness, awareness, mindfulness, alertness
noun
