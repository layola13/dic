attempt:/əˈtempt/ | n. vt. | 尝试，试图；企图 | An attempt to do something is an act of trying to do it, especially when it is difficult to do. |  I made an attempt to climb the mountain, but failed due to the bad weather. 我尝试爬山，但由于天气恶劣而失败了。

He made several attempts to pass the exam. 他曾多次尝试通过考试。

| 近义词：try, endeavor, strive | 反义词：abandon, give up | 词性: 名词, 动词
