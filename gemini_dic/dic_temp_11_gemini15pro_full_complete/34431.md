claustrophobia:/ˌklɔːstrəˈfoʊbiə/ /ˌklɔːstrəˈfəʊbiə/| n. |幽闭恐惧症|An anxiety disorder in which the sufferer has an irrational fear of confined spaces.|She suffers from claustrophobia and avoids elevators.|近义词：fear of enclosed spaces, confinement anxiety|反义词: agoraphobia (fear of open spaces)|词性:noun
claustrophobic:/ˌklɔːstrəˈfəʊbɪk/ /ˌklɔːstrəˈfoʊbɪk/| adj. |患有幽闭恐惧症的，引起幽闭恐惧的|Relating to or suffering from claustrophobia; inducing claustrophobia.|The attic room was small and claustrophobic.|近义词：confining, stuffy, cramped|反义词: open, airy|词性:adjective
