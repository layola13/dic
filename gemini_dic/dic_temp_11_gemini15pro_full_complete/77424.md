gavials:/ˈɡeɪviəlz/ | n. | 恒河鳄，长吻鳄 |  Large crocodilians native to the Indian subcontinent, characterized by their long, slender snouts. They are a critically endangered species. | Gavials are found in rivers and lakes. 恒河鳄生活在河流和湖泊中。|  The gavial population has declined drastically. 恒河鳄的数量急剧下降。 | synonyms: gharial, fish-eating crocodile | antonyms: alligator, crocodile (in a broader sense) | noun
