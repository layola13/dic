concupiscentious: /kənˌkjuːpɪˈsɛnʃəs/
adjective
好色的；贪婪的；纵欲的
Characterized by or expressing strong sexual desire; lustful.  Also, excessively desirous of something; greedy.
例句：His concupiscentious gaze made her uncomfortable. 他贪婪的目光让她感到不舒服。

例句：The monk struggled with concupiscentious thoughts.  这个僧侣与好色的念头作斗争。

近义词：lustful, lecherous, lascivious, carnal, desirous, greedy, avaricious, covetous
反义词：chaste, pure, virtuous, abstinent, indifferent

词性: adjective
