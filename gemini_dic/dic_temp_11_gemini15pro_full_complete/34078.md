civilising:/ˈsɪvɪlaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
使文明的，教化的
Bringing people to a more advanced or refined state of social development.
例句：The civilising influence of education is undeniable.  教育的教化作用是不可否认的。

例句：The missionaries saw their work as a civilising mission.  传教士将他们的工作视为一项教化使命。

近义词：refining, cultivating, enlightening, educating
反义词：barbarizing, brutalizing
词性: adjective
