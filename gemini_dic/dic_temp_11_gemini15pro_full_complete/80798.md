gothamite:/ˈɡɒθəmaɪt/ /ˈɡɑːθəmaɪt/
noun
哥谭人；纽约人
A resident or inhabitant of Gotham City, especially in the Batman comic books and films; figuratively, a New Yorker.
Batman is Gotham City's protector, always watching over the Gothamites.  蝙蝠侠是哥谭市的保护者，时刻守护着哥谭市民。
He's a typical Gothamite – always in a hurry and never without a coffee. 他是个典型的纽约人——总是匆匆忙忙，手里永远拿着咖啡。
Synonyms: New Yorker, Gotham resident, Gotham citizen
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps someone from a rival city like Metropolis)
