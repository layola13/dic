millipede:/ˈmɪlɪpiːd/ (US) /ˈmɪlɪpiːd/ (UK)| n. |千足虫；马陆|A myriapod invertebrate with an elongated body composed of many segments, most of which bear two pairs of legs.|例句：I saw a millipede crawling on the forest floor. 我看到一只千足虫在森林地面上爬行。

例句：Millipedes play an important role in breaking down organic matter. 千足虫在分解有机物方面起着重要作用。|近义词：myriapod|反义词: None|词性: Noun
