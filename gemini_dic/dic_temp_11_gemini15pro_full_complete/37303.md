commandos:/kəˈmændəʊz/ (UK) /kəˈmændoʊz/ (US)| n. |突击队员；特种兵|A group of soldiers specially trained for carrying out raids and attacks.|The commandos were sent in to rescue the hostages.  突击队员被派去营救人质。|例句：The commandos executed a daring raid on the enemy base.  突击队员对敌军基地进行了一次大胆的突袭。

|近义词：special forces, elite troops, rangers|反义词:civilians, non-combatants|词性:noun
