heathennesses:/ˈhiːðənɪsnɪsɪz/
n.
异教状态；野蛮状态
The state of being heathen; paganism; barbarity.
The missionary lamented the heathennesses of the remote tribes.  传教士对偏远部落的异教状态感到悲哀。
Near synonyms: paganism, barbarity, idolatry, savagery
Antonyms: piety, devoutness, religiousness
Noun

heathenness:/ˈhiːðənɪsnɪs/
n.
异教状态；野蛮状态
The state of being heathen; paganism; barbarity.
Early explorers often mischaracterized indigenous cultures as heathenness. 早期探险家经常将土著文化错误地描述为野蛮状态。
Near synonyms: paganism, barbarity, idolatry, savagery
Antonyms: piety, devoutness, religiousness
Noun
