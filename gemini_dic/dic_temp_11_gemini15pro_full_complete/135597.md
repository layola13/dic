nonportrayable:/ˌnɒnpɔːˈtreɪəbl/
adjective
不可描绘的; 无法刻画的
Not capable of being portrayed or depicted; too complex, abstract, or sensitive to be represented in a visual or verbal form.
The trauma she experienced was so deep and personal, it felt nonportrayable. 她经历的创伤如此深刻和私密，感觉无法描绘出来。
Her emotions were so nuanced and complex, almost nonportrayable in words. 她的情绪如此微妙而复杂，几乎无法用言语表达。
近义词: indescribable, inexpressible, ineffable
反义词: portrayable, depictable, describable
词性: adjective
