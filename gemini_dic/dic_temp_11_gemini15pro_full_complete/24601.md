broid:/brɔɪd/| n.|兄弟，哥们 (主要用于非裔美国人英语)|A term of address for a male friend or comrade, primarily used in African American Vernacular English (AAVE).|例句：Hey, broid, what's up? 嘿，兄弟，怎么了？

例句：My broid helped me move last weekend.  我的哥们上周末帮我搬家了。

近义词：bro, brother, dude, man, guy|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
