enstore:/ɪnˈstɔːr/
verb
储存，贮藏
To store something, especially something of value, in a safe place.
The paintings were enstored in a climate-controlled vault. 这些画被存放在一个气候控制的保险库里。
store, preserve, save, keep
remove, retrieve, take out
