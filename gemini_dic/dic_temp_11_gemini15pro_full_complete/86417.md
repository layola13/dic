heedfully:/ˈhiːdfəli/ | adv. | 留心地，注意地 | In a careful and attentive manner, paying close attention to details and potential risks. | She heedfully crossed the busy street, looking both ways twice. 她小心翼翼地穿过繁忙的街道，左右张望了两次。|  He heedfully followed the instructions, making sure he didn't miss a step. 他小心翼翼地按照说明操作，确保没有遗漏任何步骤。| 近义词: carefully, attentively, cautiously, mindfully | 反义词: carelessly, negligently, inattentively | 词性: 副词

词源: From Middle English *heedfulli, equivalent to heedful + -ly.  Ultimately from Old English hēdan (“to heed”) + -ful + -ly.
