horsemanship:/ˈhɔːrsmənʃɪp/
n.
骑术；马术
skill in riding a horse
例句：His horsemanship was admired by all. 他的骑术受到所有人的钦佩。

例句：She demonstrated excellent horsemanship in the competition.  她在比赛中展现了出色的骑术。

近义词：equestrianism, riding
反义词: None
