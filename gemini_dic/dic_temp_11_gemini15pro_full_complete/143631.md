outjuggles:/ˌaʊtˈdʒʌɡlz/
verb
胜过，比...更会耍花招
outperform someone in juggling or manipulating, especially in a clever or deceitful way
She outjuggled her opponents in the negotiations and secured a favorable deal. 她通过谈判中的巧妙策略胜过了对手，从而获得了一份有利的协议。
outmaneuver, outsmart, outwit
