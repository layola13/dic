nonobservable:/ˌnɒnəbˈzɜːvəbl/ /ˌnɑːnəbˈzɜːrvəbl/| adj.|不可观察的，无法观测的|Not able to be observed or noticed.|The effects of the policy change were nonobservable in the short term. 政策变化的影响在短期内是无法观察到的.|Synonyms: unobservable, imperceptible, undetectable, invisible|Antonyms: observable, perceptible, detectable, visible|adjective
nonobservable:/ˌnɒnəbˈzɜːvəbl/ /ˌnɑːnəbˈzɜːrvəbl/| n.|不可观察量，不可观测量|A quantity or factor that cannot be directly observed or measured.|In economics, some variables, like consumer confidence, are considered nonobservables. 在经济学中，一些变量，例如消费者信心，被认为是不可观测量。|Synonyms: unobservable|Antonyms: observable|noun
