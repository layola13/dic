outscream:/ˌaʊtˈskriːm/
verb
大声喊叫，胜过…的喊叫
to scream louder than someone or something else
The child outscreamed his brother in the playground.  这个孩子在操场上比他哥哥喊得更大声。

近义词:outyell, outshout
反义词:whisper, murmur
词性:v.
