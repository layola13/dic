discontinuity:/ˌdɪskɒntɪˈnjuːɪti/ /ˌdɪskɑːntɪˈnjuːɪti/| n.|不连续性，间断性|A break or interruption in the continuity of something.|The earthquake caused a discontinuity in the rock layers. 地震导致岩层出现不连续性。|近义词：break, interruption, gap, chasm, rupture|反义词:continuity, connection, seamlessness|词性:noun

discontinuity:/ˌdɪskɒntɪˈnjuːɪti/ /ˌdɪskɑːntɪˈnjuːɪti/| n.|（数学）不连续性|A point where a function is not continuous.|The function has a discontinuity at x=2.  该函数在 x=2 处不连续。|近义词：singularity|反义词:continuity|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin discontinuitatem, from discontinuus (“not continuous”), from dis- (“not”) + continuus (“continuous”).
