antiriots:/ˈæntiˌraɪəts/
noun
防暴措施；防暴装备；防暴警察
measures (such as tear gas or water cannons) or equipment (such as shields or helmets) used to control or suppress riots; or police trained and equipped for riot control.
The police used antiriots to disperse the crowd. 警察使用了防暴措施驱散人群。
synonyms: riot control, riot gear, riot police
antonyms: peacekeepers, mediators
n.


antiriot:/ˌæntiˈraɪət/
adjective
防暴的
designed to control or suppress riots.
The city deployed antiriot police to maintain order. 该市部署了防暴警察维持秩序。
synonyms: riot control, riot-suppressing
antonyms: peaceful, nonviolent
adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
"anti-" (prefix meaning "against, opposing") + "riot" (noun meaning "a violent disturbance of the peace by a crowd").
