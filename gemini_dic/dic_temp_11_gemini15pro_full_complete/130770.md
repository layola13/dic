nonanalytic:/ˌnɒnˌænəˈlɪtɪk/ | adj. | 非解析的；不可解析的 | Not able to be analyzed or resolved into simpler elements by logic or reasoning; not analytic.  | The function is nonanalytic at the origin.  这个函数在原点处是非解析的。 | Non-analytic functions are complex to study. 非解析函数研究起来很复杂。 | 近义词: irresolvable, indecomposable, unanalyzable | 反义词: analytic, resolvable, decomposable | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non "not"
analytic: from French analytique, from Greek analutikos "able to dissolve," from analuein "to unloose, resolve," from ana- "up, throughout" + lyein "to loose"
