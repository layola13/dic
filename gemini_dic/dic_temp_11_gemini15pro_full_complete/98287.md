inship:/ˈɪnʃɪp/ | n. | 入坞; 进港; 在船上 | The state or period of being in a ship or port. | The inship of the cargo was delayed due to the storm. 货物进港由于暴风雨而延误。|  The inship took longer than expected due to a mechanical problem. 由于机械故障，进港花费的时间比预期的要长。 | 近义词: embarkation, arrival | 反义词: disembarkation, departure | 词性: 名词

词源： "in" + "ship". "in" 表示在...里面，"ship" 指的是船。
