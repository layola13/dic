duena:/duˈeɪnjə/ /djuˈeɪnə/| n.|女伴，陪伴；（尤指西班牙或拉丁美洲）年长的女伴，监护妇女，保姆|A woman who acts as a chaperone or companion to a younger woman, especially in Spanish-speaking cultures. |She acted as duena to her younger sister at the party. 她在聚会上充当了她妹妹的女伴。|近义词：chaperone, companion, governess, guardian|反义词: charge (被监护人)|noun
