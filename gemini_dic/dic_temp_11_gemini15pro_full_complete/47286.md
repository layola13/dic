ETYMOLOGY->
"de-" (prefix): from Latin, meaning "down, away from, off, reversal, intensive"
"deputize": from "deputy" + "-ize" (suffix forming verbs). "Deputy" comes from Old French "depute," past participle of "deputer" meaning "to appoint," from Latin "deputare" meaning "to assign, allot, select."
