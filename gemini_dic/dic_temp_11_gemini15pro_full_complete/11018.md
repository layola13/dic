arbuscular:/ɑːrˈbʌskjʊlər/
adjective
丛枝状的
Of or relating to an arbuscule.  Characterized by the formation of arbuscules.
Arbuscular mycorrhizal fungi form symbiotic relationships with plant roots. 丛枝菌根真菌与植物根系形成共生关系。
Arbuscular mycorrhizae are essential for nutrient uptake in many plant species. 丛枝菌根对许多植物物种的养分吸收至关重要。
近义词: mycorrhizal
反义词: non-mycorrhizal
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *arbuscula (“small tree”), diminutive of arbor (“tree”).  The term refers to the tree-like structures (arbuscules) formed by these fungi within plant root cells.
