veneer:/vəˈnɪr/| n. vt.|薄板，饰面；虚饰，掩饰|A thin decorative covering of fine wood applied to a coarser wood or other material.|The table was veneered with mahogany. 这桌子用桃花心木贴面。|近义词：facing, overlay, coating, laminate, finish, gloss, facade|反义词：core, substance, reality|n.
veneer:/vəˈnɪr/| vt.|用薄板覆盖；掩饰|To cover (something) with a veneer.|He veneered the cheap pine table with a beautiful walnut veneer. 他用漂亮的胡桃木薄板覆盖了那张便宜的松木桌子。|近义词：coat, cover, face, overlay, laminate|反义词：expose, reveal, strip|vt.


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *fournir* "to furnish, provide," from Old French *fornir*, ultimately from Frankish *furnjan*, from Proto-Germanic *furnizon* "to prepare, provide." The sense shifted in English to refer specifically to a thin layer of fine wood used for furnishing or finishing furniture.
