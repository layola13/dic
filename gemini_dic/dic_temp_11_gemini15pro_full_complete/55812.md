divesting:/daɪˈvɛstɪŋ/
verb
剥夺，使摆脱；出售，转让
The act of stripping someone or something of property, authority, or rights; also, the act of selling off assets.
The company is divesting its less profitable businesses.  这家公司正在出售其盈利较少的业务。
The court divested him of all his parental rights. 法院剥夺了他所有的父母权利。
synonyms: selling, disposing of, getting rid of, unloading, offloading, shedding
antonyms: acquiring, investing, purchasing

They divested themselves of their shares in the company. 他们出售了他们在公司的股份。
synonyms: deprive, strip, dispossess; sell, dispose of, get rid of
antonyms: invest, acquire, endow
