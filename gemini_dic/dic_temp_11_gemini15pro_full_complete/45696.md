cururo:/kʊˈrʊrəʊ/ | n. |  南美栗鼠，毛丝鼠 | A rodent of the genus Spalacopus, native to Chile, resembling a gopher.  It lives in colonies underground. | The cururo digs extensive burrow systems in the Andes. 南美栗鼠在安第斯山脉挖掘广泛的洞穴系统。 |  The cururo is a social animal, living in colonies of up to 20 individuals. 南美栗鼠是一种群居动物，生活在最多20个个体的群体中。 |  Synonyms:  coruro, degu (loosely) | Antonyms: None (no direct opposite for a specific animal) | noun
