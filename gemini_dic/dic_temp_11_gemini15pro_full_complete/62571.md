energizing:/ˈenərˌdʒaɪzɪŋ/| adj.|充满活力的，使人精力充沛的|Making someone feel more energetic and enthusiastic.|例句：The morning walk was energizing.  早上的散步令人精神焕发。|近义词：invigorating, stimulating, refreshing|反义词：enervating, tiring, exhausting|词性:形容词
Etymology: From energize + -ing.  Energize comes from energy + -ize. Energy ultimately derives from Greek energeia, meaning "activity, operation."
