lakelike:/ˈleɪklaɪk/| adj.|像湖的，湖泊似的|Resembling a lake; having the characteristics of a lake. |The valley held a lakelike expanse of still water. 山谷中有一片像湖一样平静的广阔水域。|The swimming pool was so vast and still, it appeared lakelike. 游泳池又大又静，看起来像个湖。|近义词：lacustrine, lake-like|反义词: unlike a lake, riverlike|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From lake + -like.  "lake" comes from Old English *lacu "pond, pool, stream," of uncertain origin. "-like" comes from Old English "-lic" meaning "having the form or appearance of," related to "lich" (body).
