deonerate:/diˈɑːnəreɪt/
|v.| 免除；解除；使卸下|To relieve of a duty, obligation, or responsibility. |The court deonerated him of all responsibility for the accident. 法院免除了他对此次事故的所有责任。|
|v.| 免罪；赦免|To absolve from blame or guilt. |The new evidence deonerated the suspect. 新的证据证明嫌疑人无罪。|
Synonyms: exonerate, absolve, acquit, discharge, release
Antonyms: incriminate, inculpate, charge, accuse
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *deoneratus*, past participle of *deonerare*, from *de-* (away from) + *onus* (burden).
