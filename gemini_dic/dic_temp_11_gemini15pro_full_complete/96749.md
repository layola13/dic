ineluctability:/ˌɪnɪˌlʌktəˈbɪləti/| n. |不可避免性，必然性|The quality of being impossible to avoid or prevent.  It refers to something that is certain to happen, no matter what.|例句：The ineluctability of death is a difficult concept to grasp.  死亡的不可避免性是一个难以理解的概念。

例句：He spoke of the ineluctability of fate. 他谈到了命运的不可避免性。

近义词：inevitability, certainty, unavoidability|反义词:avoidability, preventability|词性:noun
