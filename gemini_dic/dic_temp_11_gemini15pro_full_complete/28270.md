captivates:/ˈkæptɪveɪts/| v.| 迷住，使着迷| to attract and hold the interest and attention of someone by being beautiful, exciting, charming, etc.| Her singing captivated the audience. 她的歌声迷住了观众。| synonyms:enthralls, charms, fascinates, mesmerizes | antonyms:bores, repels, disgusts | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin captivatus, past participle of captivare "to take captive," from Latin captivus "captive"
