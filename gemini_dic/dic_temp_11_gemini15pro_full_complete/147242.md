overmellowly:/ˌoʊvərˈmɛloʊli/ | adv. | 过于成熟地，过于甜美地 | Excessively mellow; too ripe or soft, often to the point of being mushy or overly sentimental.  This word is often used figuratively to describe something that is excessively sweet or sentimental. | The peaches were overmellowly ripe and almost mushy.  桃子熟过头了，几乎都要烂了。| The love story was portrayed overmellowly, making it seem unrealistic.  这个爱情故事被描绘得过于甜腻，显得不真实。| 近义词: excessively ripe, overly sweet, mushy, sentimental | 反义词: tart, tangy, firm, unsentimental | 词性: 副词

词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "mellow" (adjective meaning "soft, ripe, sweet") + "-ly" (adverb suffix).
