inantherate:/ɪnˈænθəreɪt/
adj.
具花药的；花药生的
Having or bearing anthers; formed within or by the anther.
The inantherate stamens are characteristic of this plant species.  这种植物的特点是具有花药的雄蕊。
The botanist studied the inantherate flowers under the microscope.  植物学家在显微镜下研究了花药生的花朵。
近义词: antheriferous
反义词: anantherous
词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin *inantheratus, from in- + anthera (“anther”) + -atus.
