bombs:/bɑːmz/ | n. |炸弹; 爆炸物| An explosive device fused to explode under specified conditions. | The city was devastated by bombs.  这座城市被炸弹摧毁了。| synonyms: explosives, ordnance, munitions | antonyms: peace, treaty | noun

bombs:/bɑːmz/ | v. |轰炸; 彻底失败| Attack with bombs. Fail badly or completely. | The planes bombed the enemy's position. 飞机轰炸了敌人的阵地。 The movie bombed at the box office. 这部电影票房惨败。 | synonyms: attack, shell, flop, tank | antonyms: succeed, thrive | verb
