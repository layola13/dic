conchologist:/kɒŋˈkɒlədʒɪst/ (US) /kɑːnˈkɑːlədʒɪst/| n. |贝类学家|A person who studies shells, especially the shells of mollusks.|She is a renowned conchologist with a vast collection of shells from around the world. 她是一位著名的贝类学家，收藏了来自世界各地的大量贝壳。|
conchologist:| n. |贝类学家|One who studies the shells of mollusks.|He became a conchologist after a childhood fascination with seashells. 他童年时对贝壳着迷，后来成了一名贝类学家。|近义词:malacologist|反义词:None|词性:noun
