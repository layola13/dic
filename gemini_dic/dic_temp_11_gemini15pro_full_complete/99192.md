interclan:/ˌɪntərˈklæn/ | adj. | 部落间的，氏族间的 | Existing or occurring between clans. | The interclan rivalry led to many years of conflict. 部落间的竞争导致了多年的冲突。|  The interclan marriage helped to unite the two warring factions.  氏族间的联姻帮助联合了两个交战派别。 | 近义词: intertribal, intergroup | 反义词: intraclan | 词性: 形容词

词源:
inter- (prefix): between, among (来自拉丁语 inter)
clan (noun): a group of close-knit and interrelated families (来自盖尔语 clann, 意为后代，家族)
