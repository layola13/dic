grandiose:/ˈɡrændiˌoʊs/ (US), /ˈɡrændiəʊs/ (UK)| adj.|宏伟的，堂皇的，浮夸的|Impressive and imposing in appearance or style, especially pretentiously so.|He unveiled grandiose plans for a new city. 他公布了建造一座新城市的宏伟计划。|近义词：magnificent, imposing, pompous, ostentatious|反义词：humble, modest, simple, understated|词性:形容词

grandiose:/ˈɡrændiˌoʊs/ (US), /ˈɡrændiəʊs/ (UK)| adj.|夸大的，不切实际的|(of ideas or plans) excessively ambitious and unrealistic.|She had grandiose ideas about becoming a famous actress. 她对成为著名演员抱有不切实际的想法。|近义词：ambitious, extravagant, unrealistic|反义词：realistic, practical, modest|词性:形容词
