flavourless:/ˈfleɪvələs/| adj.|无味的；平淡的|Lacking flavour; bland or tasteless.|The soup was disappointingly flavourless. 这汤淡而无味，令人失望。|近义词：bland, tasteless, insipid, unappetizing|反义词：flavourful, tasty, delicious, savoury|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From flavor + -less.  "Flavor" comes from Old French *flavor, flaveur "smell, scent, aroma, exhalation," which traces back to Latin *flare "to blow, breathe." The suffix "-less" is from Old English -lēas.
