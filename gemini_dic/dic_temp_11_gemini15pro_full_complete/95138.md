inappreciation:/ˌɪnəˌpriːʃiˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 不欣赏，缺乏欣赏，不感激 | Lack of appreciation or gratitude; failure to recognize worth or value. |  He showed his inappreciation by not even saying thank you. 他甚至连谢谢都没说，表明了他的不领情。| Synonyms: ingratitude, ungratefulness, thanklessness | Antonyms: appreciation, gratitude, thankfulness | noun
