afflictively:/əˈflɪktɪvli/ | adv. | 痛苦地，折磨人地 |  In a way that causes suffering or distress. | The news of her death affected him afflictively.  她去世的消息使他悲痛欲绝。 | 近义词: painfully, distressingly, agonizingly | 反义词: soothingly, comfortingly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From the Late Latin *afflictīvus, from Latin afflictus (past participle of affligere, "to strike down, damage").  来自晚期拉丁语 *afflictīvus，源自拉丁语 afflictus（affligere 的过去分词，“击倒，损害”）。
