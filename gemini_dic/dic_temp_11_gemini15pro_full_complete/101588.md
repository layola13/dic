irradiated:/ɪˈreɪdieɪtɪd/ | adj. | 辐射的，被照射的 | Exposed to radiation. | The irradiated food is safe to eat.  被辐射过的食物可以安全食用。|  Synonyms: radiated, exposed | Antonyms: unexposed, shielded | adjective

irradiate:/ɪˈreɪdieɪt/ | v. | 照射，辐射；流露 | To expose to radiation; to shine brightly; to spread out from a central point. | The sun irradiates the earth. 太阳照射地球。| Synonyms: shine, beam, radiate | Antonyms: darken, obscure | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin irradiatus, past participle of irradiare (“to shine upon”), from in- (“in, on, upon”) + radiare (“to shine, gleam, beam”), from radius (“ray, spoke of a wheel”).
