derailleur:/dɪˈreɪlər/ /dəˈreɪlər/| n.|变速器；拨链器|A derailleur is a mechanism used on a bicycle to change gears by moving the chain from one sprocket to another.|My front derailleur needs adjusting. 我的前变速器需要调整。|Synonyms: gear shifter, gear mechanism, gear changer|Antonyms: None|Noun

derailleurs:/dɪˈreɪlərz/ /dəˈreɪlərz/| n.|变速器；拨链器（复数）|Derailleurs are mechanisms used on bicycles to change gears.|Both my front and rear derailleurs are working perfectly. 我的前后变速器都工作正常。|Synonyms: gear shifters, gear mechanisms, gear changers|Antonyms: None|Noun
