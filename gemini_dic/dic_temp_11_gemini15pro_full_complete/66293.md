excoriating:/ɪkˈskɔːrieɪtɪŋ/
adjective
严厉批评的；苛刻的
severely critical; harshly censorious
Her review of the play was excoriating. 她对这部戏剧的评论非常尖刻。

The newspaper published an excoriating article about the politician's corruption.  这家报纸发表了一篇严厉谴责该政客腐败的文章。

近义词:scathing, blistering, harsh, severe, critical, condemnatory, vituperative
反义词:praising, laudatory, complimentary, flattering
词性:adjective
