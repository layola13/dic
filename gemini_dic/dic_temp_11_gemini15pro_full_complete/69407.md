feel-like-oneself:/fiːl laɪk wʌnˈsɛlf/ |verbal phrase|感觉像自己；恢复正常；觉得舒服|To feel healthy, happy, and in one's usual state of mind or body, especially after an illness or period of stress.|例句：After a week of flu, I'm finally starting to feel like myself again.  我得了流感一个星期后，终于开始感觉像自己了。

例句：She's been under a lot of pressure lately, so she hasn't been feeling like herself. 她最近压力很大，所以一直感觉不太好。|近义词：feel well, feel normal, feel better, be back to normal|反义词：feel unwell, feel ill, feel off, be out of sorts|词性:phrase
