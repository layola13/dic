negotiable:/nɪˈɡoʊʃiəbl/ /nɪˈɡəʊʃəbl/| adj.|可协商的，可商量的|Open to discussion or modification; able to be changed or agreed upon through discussion.|The price is negotiable. 价格是可以协商的。|The terms of the contract are negotiable. 合同条款是可以协商的。|近义词:discussable, adjustable, flexible|反义词:non-negotiable, fixed, firm|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin negotiabilis "that can be transacted in business," from negotiari "to do business," from negotium "business," literally "not leisure," from nec "not" (from PIE root *ne- "not") + otium "leisure" (from PIE root *awi- "bird," also "vital force, life").
