enamored:/ɪˈnæmərd/ /ɪˈnæmɚd/ | adj. | 迷恋的，倾心的 | Filled with love and admiration. | She was completely enamored with the handsome stranger. 她完全被这位英俊的陌生人迷住了。 |  He is enamored of his new car. 他迷上了他的新车。 | 近义词:infatuated, captivated, charmed, smitten | 反义词:indifferent, apathetic, disenchanted | 词性:形容词
词源: From the Old French _enamoré_, past participle of _enamorer_ "to inflame with love," from _en-_, "in" + _amor_, "love." Ultimately from Latin _amor_.
