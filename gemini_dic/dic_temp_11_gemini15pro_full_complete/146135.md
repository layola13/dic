overemployments:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈplɔɪmənts/
|n.| 超额就业，过度雇佣|A situation where more people are employed than are needed or can be productively utilized.|The company's overemployments led to redundancies later in the year.  这家公司过度雇佣导致了年底的裁员。|overstaffing, surplus employment|underemployment, staff shortages
|noun|

overemployment:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈplɔɪmənt/
|n.| 超额就业，过度雇佣|A situation where an individual works more hours than they desire or that is considered healthy, often due to holding multiple jobs or working excessively long hours in a single job.|His overemployment is impacting his health. 他的过度工作正在影响他的健康。|overwork, excessive work|underemployment, part-time employment
|noun|
