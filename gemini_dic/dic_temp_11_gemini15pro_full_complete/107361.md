lamellicorn:/ˌlæməˈlɪkɔːrn/ | adj. n. |  lamella (薄片) + cornu (角)| 薄片状触角的；金龟子| The lamellicorn beetle is known for its distinctive antennae.  金龟子以其独特的触角而闻名。| The lamellicorn beetles are a diverse group. 金龟子种类繁多。| 近义词: scarabaeid (圣甲虫), beetle (甲虫) | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *lamellātus* (having small plates, lamellate), from *lamella* (small plate, diminutive of *lāmina* - plate, leaf) + *cornu* (horn).  The term refers to the clubbed antennae of these beetles, which are composed of several plate-like segments.
