nonerrantly:/ˌnɑːnˈɛrəntli/
adverb|无误地，正确地|without error; infallibly|The scriptures, he believed, spoke nonerrantly on matters of faith.| 近义词:infallibly, accurately, correctly|反义词:errantly, fallaciously, mistakenly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From non- + errant + -ly.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not." Errant comes from the Latin word *errantem*, the present participle of *errare*, meaning "to wander" or "to err."  The suffix -ly forms adverbs.
