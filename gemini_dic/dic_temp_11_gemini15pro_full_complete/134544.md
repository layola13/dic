nonliquidating:/ˌnɒnˈlɪkwɪdeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非清算的
Not involving or requiring the liquidation of assets; not converting assets into cash.
The company issued nonliquidating dividends to its shareholders. 公司向股东发放了非清算股息。
Nonliquidating distributions allow investors to maintain their investment in the fund while still receiving a portion of their returns. 非清算分配允许投资者在仍然获得一部分回报的同时保持他们在基金中的投资。
near synonyms:  non-cash,  undistributed, continuing
antonyms: liquidating, cash, distributing
词性: adjective
