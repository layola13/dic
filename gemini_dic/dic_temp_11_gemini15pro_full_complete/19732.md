billable:/ˈbɪləbl/| adj.|可计费的，可开帐单的|Something that is billable can be charged to a client or customer. |例句：Billable hours are the hours worked that can be charged to a client. 可计费工时是可以向客户收取费用的工作时间。

例句：The lawyer tracked his billable hours meticulously.  这位律师一丝不苟地记录他的可计费工时。|近义词：chargeable, invoiceable|反义词：non-billable, unbillable|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From bill + -able.  "Bill" comes from Medieval Latin "billa," meaning a sealed document.
