compensation:/ˌkɒmpənˈseɪʃn/ /ˌkɑːmpenˈseɪʃn/| n.|补偿，赔偿，报酬|something, typically money, awarded to someone as a recompense for loss, injury, or suffering.|例句：She received compensation for the injuries she suffered in the accident. 她因事故受伤获得了赔偿。|例句：He demanded compensation for the damage to his car. 他要求赔偿他的汽车损失。|近义词：remuneration, payment, recompense, restitution|反义词:penalty, forfeiture|词性:noun
