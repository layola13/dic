defraudation:/dɪˌfrɔːˈdeɪʃn/
n.
欺诈，诈骗
The act of defrauding; the state of being defrauded.
He was found guilty of defrauding investors of millions of dollars. 他被判犯有诈骗投资者数百万美元的罪行。
The company suffered a significant loss due to the defalcation and defradation of its chief financial officer. 由于首席财务官的挪用公款和欺诈行为，公司遭受了重大损失。

近义词:fraud, deception, cheat, swindle, trickery
反义词:honesty, integrity, fairness
词性:名词


词源:
From Late Middle English *defraudacioun, from Late Latin dēfraudātiō (“a cheating, defrauding”), from Latin dēfraudō (“I defraud”), from dē- (“away, from”) + fraus, fraud- (“deceit, trickery, fraud”).
