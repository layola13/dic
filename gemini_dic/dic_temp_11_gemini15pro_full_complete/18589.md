benignant:/bɪˈnɪɡnənt/
adjective
仁慈的；和蔼的；良性的
Kind and gentle, harmless.
The benignant old woman offered the lost child a warm meal and a place to sleep.  仁慈的老妇人给迷路的孩子提供了一顿热饭和一个睡觉的地方。
The doctor assured her that the tumor was benignant. 医生向她保证肿瘤是良性的。
近义词:kind, benevolent, gracious, benign
反义词:malignant, malevolent, cruel, unkind
词性:adjective

benignant:/bɪˈnɪɡnənt/
词源:From Latin benignus "kind, generous, friendly," from bene "well" + gignere "to bear, produce."
词性:adjective
