intercausative:/ˌɪntərˈkɔːzətɪv/
adjective
相互致使的；相互促成的
Relating to or denoting a reciprocal causative relationship, where two or more entities cause each other to exist or happen.
The intercausative nature of the conflict made it difficult to assign blame.  相互致使的冲突性质使得难以归咎。
The relationship between supply and demand is often intercausative. 供需关系通常是相互促成的。
近义词: reciprocal, mutually causative
反义词: independent, unrelated
词性: adjective
