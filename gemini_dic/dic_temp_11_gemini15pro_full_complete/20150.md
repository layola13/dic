bipinnately:/baɪˈpɪnɪtli/
adjective
二回羽状的
(of a compound leaf) having leaflets arranged on each side of a secondary rib branching from the main rib.  Twice pinnate.
The fern's fronds were bipinnately compound, creating a delicate, lacy appearance.  这种蕨类植物的叶子是二回羽状复叶，形成精致的蕾丝状外观。
Synonym: twice-pinnate
Antonym: simple (leaf structure)
词性: adjective
