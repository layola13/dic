overembroidered:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈbrɔɪdərd/
adjective
过度刺绣的
Decorated with excessive embroidery; having too much embroidery.
The dress was overembroidered, making it look gaudy rather than elegant.  这件衣服过度刺绣，看起来很俗气而不是优雅。
The cushion was overembroidered with sequins and beads.  这个垫子过度刺绣了亮片和珠子。
近义词:overdecorated, fussy
反义词:plain, simple
词性:adjective

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "embroider" (verb meaning "to decorate with needlework")
