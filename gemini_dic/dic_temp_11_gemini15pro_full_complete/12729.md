asset-stripper: /ˈæset ˌstrɪpər/ | n. | 资产剥离者 | A person or company that buys a struggling business and sells off its valuable assets, often leaving the remaining business to fail. |  The company was bought by an asset-stripper who promptly sold off its most profitable divisions.  这家公司被一个资产剥离者收购，他迅速卖掉了其最赚钱的部门。 |  The asset-stripper quickly sold the company's real estate holdings. 资产剥离者迅速出售了该公司的房地产资产。 | Synonyms: corporate raider, liquidator | Antonyms: investor, builder | Noun
