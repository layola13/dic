gentrifies:/ˈdʒɛntrɪfaɪz/
verb
使贵族化；中产阶级化；高档化
to change a place from being a poor area to a richer one, by people of a higher social class moving to live there
The area is rapidly gentrifying as more professionals move in.  这个地区随着越来越多的专业人士迁入而迅速高档化。

The old docks are being gentrified. 老码头正在被改造得高档起来。


近义词:upgrade, improve, renovate, revitalize
反义词:degenerate, decay, decline

词性:v
