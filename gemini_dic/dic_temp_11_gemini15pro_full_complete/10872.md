arachnid:/əˈræknɪd/ /əˈræknɪd/ | n. |蛛形纲动物|An arthropod of the class Arachnida, such as a spider, scorpion, mite, or tick, having a body divided into two parts, eight legs, and no antennae.|Spiders, scorpions, mites, and ticks are all arachnids. 蜘蛛、蝎子、螨虫和蜱都是蛛形纲动物。|Synonyms: spider, scorpion, mite, tick |Antonyms: insect (in a broader sense, as arachnids are technically arthropods, not insects) |noun

arachnid:/əˈræknɪd/ /əˈræknɪd/ | adj. |蛛形纲动物的|Of or relating to arachnids.|The arachnid anatomy is characterized by eight legs. 蛛形纲动物的解剖特征是八条腿。|Synonyms: spider-like, scorpion-like |Antonyms: insect-like|adjective
