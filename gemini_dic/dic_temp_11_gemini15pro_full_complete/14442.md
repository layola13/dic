auxinic:/ɔːkˈsɪnɪk/
adjective|生长素的，与生长素有关的|Relating to or resembling auxin, a plant hormone that promotes growth.|Auxinic herbicides mimic the action of natural plant hormones. 生长素除草剂模仿天然植物激素的作用。|Synonyms: plant growth regulator, phytohormonal|Antonyms: anti-auxinic|adjective
