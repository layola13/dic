dilatability:/ˌdaɪˌleɪtəˈbɪləti/
noun
可扩张性，膨胀性
The quality of being able to expand or become wider.
The dilatability of the pupil is a key diagnostic sign.  瞳孔的扩张性是一个关键的诊断标志。
The balloon's dilatability allowed it to inflate to a surprising size.  气球的可膨胀性使它膨胀到惊人的大小。
近义词: expansibility, extensibility, stretchability
反义词: inflexibility, rigidity, stiffness
词性: noun

dilate:/daɪˈleɪt/
verb
扩张，膨胀
To make or become wider or larger.
The surgeon dilated the blood vessel to improve blood flow.  外科医生扩张血管以改善血流。
Heat dilates metal.  热使金属膨胀。
近义词: expand, widen, enlarge
反义词: contract, shrink, constrict
词性: verb
