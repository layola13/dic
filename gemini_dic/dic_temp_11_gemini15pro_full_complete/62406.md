endogenetic:/ˌɛndəʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk/| adj.|内生的，内成的|Formed or occurring beneath the surface of the earth; originating from internal forces or processes.|例句：Endogenetic rocks are formed from magma within the Earth. 内生岩是由地球内部的岩浆形成的。|例句：The earthquake was caused by endogenetic forces. 地震是由内生力引起的。|近义词：internal, endogenous|反义词：exogenetic, external|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *éndon* (within) + *génesis* (origin, creation)
