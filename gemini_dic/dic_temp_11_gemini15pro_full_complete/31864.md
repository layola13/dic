cheerinesses:/ˈtʃɪriːnɪsɪz/
n.
欢快，高兴，愉悦
The state of being cheerful; cheerfulness.  A display or expression of cheerfulness.
Her cheerinesses were infectious, brightening everyone's day. 她的快乐很有感染力，照亮了每个人的一天。
The room was filled with cheerinesses and laughter. 房间里充满了欢声笑语。
近义词：gaiety, merriment, joyousness, mirth, happiness, cheerfulness
反义词：gloom, sadness, dejection, melancholy, unhappiness


词性: noun (plural)


词源: From cheeriness + -es (plural suffix). Cheeriness itself comes from cheerful + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  "Cheerful" traces back to Middle English *cherful, from *chere "face, countenance" + -ful "full of." Ultimately from Old French chiere.
