moldiness:/ˈmoʊldinəs/| n.|发霉，霉味，霉烂|A surface covered with moldiness. 表面覆盖着霉菌。|The old books smelled of moldiness.  这些旧书散发着霉味。|近义词：mildew, mustiness, fustiness|反义词:freshness, cleanness|词性:名词

Etymology: From Middle English *moldynes, from Old English *moldegnes, equivalent to moldy +‎ -ness.
