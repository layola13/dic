dishonesty's:/dɪsˈɒnɪstiz/
n.
不诚实，欺骗行为
The quality or an instance of being dishonest; lack of honesty; deceitfulness.
Her dishonesty's cost her the job. 她的不诚实使她失去了工作。
近义词：deceit, fraud, deception, untruthfulness
反义词：honesty, integrity, truthfulness
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From dishonesty + -'s (possessive suffix).  Dishonesty itself comes from dis- (not) + honesty.  Honesty traces back to Old French "honeste" and ultimately to Latin "honestus" (honorable, respectable).
