antiphrasis:/ænˈtɪfrəsɪs/| n.|反语，讽刺|The use of a word in a sense opposite to its normal meaning, often ironically.  It is a figure of speech in which the meaning of a phrase or word is the opposite of its normal definition.|Calling a tall person "Shorty" is an example of antiphrasis.|近义词：irony, sarcasm|反义词: literalism|词性:noun
