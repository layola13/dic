acicula:/əˈsɪkjʊlə/ | n. |细针，针状体，刺|A small needle-like part, as a spine, bristle, or crystal. | The cactus is covered in sharp aciculae.  仙人掌上覆盖着锋利的细刺。|
The acicula of the sea urchin can inject venom. 海胆的刺可以注射毒液。|
Synonyms: spine, bristle, needle, prickle | Antonyms: bluntness, smoothness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *acicula*, diminutive of *acus* "needle".
