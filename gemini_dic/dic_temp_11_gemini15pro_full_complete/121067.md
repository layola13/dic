misconstruction:/ˌmɪskənˈstrʌkʃən/
|n.|误解，曲解|An incorrect interpretation or understanding of something.|She apologized for the misconstruction of her words. 她为自己的话被误解而道歉。|
|Synonym:|misinterpretation, misunderstanding, misreading|
|Antonym:|correct interpretation, understanding, comprehension|
|词性：|名词|


misconstruct:/ˌmɪskənˈstrʌkt/
|vt.|误解，曲解|To interpret or understand something incorrectly.|His silence was misconstrued as agreement. 他的沉默被误解为同意。|
|Synonym:|misinterpret, misunderstand, misread|
|Antonym:|correctly interpret, understand, comprehend|
|词性：|动词|
