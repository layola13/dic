humidifier:/hjuːˈmɪdɪfaɪər/ | n. |加湿器 |A device that adds moisture to the air. |例句：I use a humidifier in the winter to prevent dry skin. 我在冬天使用加湿器以防止皮肤干燥。|近义词：vaporizer, atomizer |反义词:dehumidifier |noun

humidifiers:/hjuːˈmɪdɪfaɪərz/ | n. |加湿器（复数） |Devices that add moisture to the air. |例句：We have humidifiers in several rooms to improve air quality. 我们在几个房间里都放了加湿器以改善空气质量。|近义词：vaporizers, atomizers |反义词:dehumidifiers |noun
