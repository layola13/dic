enshroud:/ɪnˈʃraʊd/
verb
覆盖，笼罩；隐藏，遮蔽
To cover or enclose something completely, especially to conceal it.
The mountains were enshrouded in mist. 群山笼罩在薄雾之中。
The details of the accident were enshrouded in secrecy. 事故的细节被保密。
近义词:conceal, cover, veil, cloak, obscure, hide
反义词:reveal, uncover, expose, unveil
词性:v.

enshroud:/ɪnˈʃraʊd/
词源: From en- + shroud (to cover or clothe, especially a corpse for burial). 源自 en-（表示“使成为……”）+ shroud（覆盖或遮蔽，尤指为尸体裹尸布）。
