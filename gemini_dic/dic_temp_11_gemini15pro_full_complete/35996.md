codeveloping:/ˌkoʊdɪˈvɛləpɪŋ/| v. |共同开发，合作开发|Developing something together with one or more other entities.|Example: We are codeveloping a new software with a partner company. 我们正在与一家合作公司共同开发一款新软件.|Example: They are codeveloping a curriculum with input from teachers and students. 他们正在与老师和学生的意见一起共同开发课程.|Synonym: collaborating, jointly developing|Antonym: independently developing|Verb
