foyson:/ˈfɔɪzən/| n.| abundance, plenty| An abundance or plenty of something; a rich yield or harvest.|  The land provided foyson for all its inhabitants. 这片土地为所有居民提供了丰富的食物。|近义词：abundance, plenty, profusion, copiousness|反义词: scarcity, dearth, lack, paucity|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *foison*, from Old French *foison*, from Late Latin *fusionem*, accusative of *fusio* (“a pouring out, shedding”), from Latin *fundere* (“to pour, melt”).  Doublet of *fusion*.
