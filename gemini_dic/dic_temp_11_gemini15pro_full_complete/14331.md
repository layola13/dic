automixte: /o.to.mikst/ (French pronunciation - no direct English equivalent) | adj. | 自动的，自动混合的 |  Self-mixing, usually referring to concrete.  Derived from the French words "auto" (self) and "mixte" (mixed).  It's primarily used in technical contexts, especially in construction and engineering, relating to concrete that mixes itself during transport or placement. |  Le camion utilise du béton automixte.
