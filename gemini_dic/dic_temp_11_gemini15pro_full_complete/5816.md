amassing: /əˈmæsɪŋ/
verb
积累，积聚
gathering or accumulating something, typically wealth, possessions, or information, in large quantities.
He has been amassing a fortune through shrewd investments. 他通过精明的投资积累了一笔财富。
The company is amassing a huge database of customer information.  这家公司正在积累大量的客户信息数据库。
Synonyms: accumulating, gathering, collecting, stockpiling, hoarding
Antonyms: distributing, dispersing, scattering, spending, squandering
词性: 动词
