morphic:/ˈmɔːrfɪk/| adj.|形态的，与形态有关的|Relating to form or shape, especially of living organisms.|例句：The morphic changes in the butterfly are remarkable. 蝴蝶的形态变化非常显著。|例句：Morphic resonance is a controversial theory in developmental biology.  形态共振是发育生物学中一个备受争议的理论。|近义词：formal, structural, configurational|反义词:amorphous, shapeless|词性:形容词
