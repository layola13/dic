deuced:/ˈdjuːsɪd/| adj. adv.|非常地，极度地；（用作加强语气）讨厌地，该死地|Used for emphasis, especially to express annoyance.  Originates from "deuce" (two), a low throw in dice, then extended to mean "bad luck" and finally as a general intensifier.|It's deucedly cold in here!  这里真TM冷！|近义词：awfully, terribly, extremely, bloody (British)|反义词：slightly, mildly|词性：形容词，副词

deuced:/ˈdjuːsɪd/| n.|（表示厌烦或惊讶）该死的，见鬼的|An exclamation of annoyance or surprise.|Oh, deuced! I've forgotten my keys.  哦，该死！我忘了带钥匙了。|近义词：damn, blast, darn|反义词：无|词性：名词 (感叹词)
