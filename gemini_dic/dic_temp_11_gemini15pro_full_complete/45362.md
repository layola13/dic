cuneated:/ˈkjuːnieɪtɪd/
adjective
楔形的
shaped like a wedge; tapering to a thin edge
The cuneated inscription on the stone was difficult to decipher.  石头上楔形的铭文很难解读。
Synonyms: wedge-shaped, tapered, triangular
Antonyms: rounded, blunt, square
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cuneatus*, from cuneus ("wedge").
