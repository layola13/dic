overingenious: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʒiːniəs/
adjective
过于精巧的；过分巧妙的；自作聪明的
Excessively or unnecessarily ingenious; too clever.  Characterized by excessive or misapplied ingenuity.
The plot of the movie was overingenious, with twists and turns that ultimately made it confusing. 这部电影的情节过于精巧，其中的曲折最终使它变得令人费解。

His overingenious solution to the problem actually created more problems. 他对这个问题过于精巧的解决方案实际上制造了更多问题。


近义词: convoluted, elaborate, contrived, overwrought
反义词: simple, straightforward, artless
词性: adjective
