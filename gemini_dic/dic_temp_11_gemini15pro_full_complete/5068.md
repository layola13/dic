all-the-while:/ɔːl ðə waɪl/| adv.|一直；始终；与此同时|Meaning "during the whole time; throughout," all the while emphasizes the continuous duration of an action or state alongside another. It often suggests a contrast or irony between the two simultaneous events.|He was complaining about being overworked all the while he was secretly looking for a new job.  他一直抱怨工作过度，而与此同时，他却在偷偷地寻找新的工作。|Synonyms: meanwhile, throughout, all along, the whole time|Antonyms: never, briefly, intermittently|Adverb
