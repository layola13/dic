appraisees:/əˌpreɪˈziːz/
noun
被评估者；受评人
People who are being appraised or evaluated.
The appraisees were nervous about their performance reviews.  被评估者对他们的绩效评估感到紧张。
The manager spoke to the appraisees individually. 经理分别与每位受评人进行了谈话。
synonyms:  subjects of appraisal,  individuals being evaluated, those being assessed
antonyms: appraisers, assessors, evaluators
词性: noun
