halpace:/ˈhælpeɪs/| n. | 半空间，半开空间 |A portion of space lying entirely on one side of an unbounded plane or a hypersurface.  A region of three-dimensional space consisting of all points on one side of an infinite plane.|The sensor detected movement in the halpace above the device. 传感器检测到设备上方半空间的运动。|近义词: half-space, subspace|反义词: full space, whole space|noun
