loss-making:/ˈlɒsˌmeɪkɪŋ/ (US) /ˈlɒsˌmeɪkɪŋ/ (UK)
adjective
亏损的；不盈利的
Operating at a loss; not making a profit.
The company has been loss-making for the past three years.  这家公司过去三年一直在亏损。
The loss-making division was eventually shut down.  亏损的部门最终被关闭了。
Synonyms: unprofitable, deficit-ridden, in the red
Antonyms: profitable, lucrative, in the black
词性: 形容词
