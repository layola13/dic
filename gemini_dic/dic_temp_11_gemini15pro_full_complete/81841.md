greynesses:/ˈɡreɪnəsəz/
n.
灰暗，阴沉；灰色调
The numerous greynesses of the sky reflected the somber mood of the city. 天空中的层层灰暗反映了这座城市的阴郁气氛。
The artist captured the subtle greynesses of the twilight landscape. 艺术家捕捉到了暮色中景物微妙的灰色调。
dullness, drabness, gloominess
brightness, vividness
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From grey + -ness.  Grey (meaning a color between black and white) has origins in the Old English græg.  The suffix -ness is used to form nouns expressing a state, quality, or condition.
