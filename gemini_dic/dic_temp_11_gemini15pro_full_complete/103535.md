jog-on:/ˈdʒɒɡ ɒn/ (British English) | phrasal verb | 走开；滚开；别烦我 | Used to tell someone to go away, especially because they are annoying you. | "Jog on, mate, I'm busy."  “走开，伙计，我正忙着呢。” |  近义词: go away, get lost, clear off, beat it | 反义词: stay, come here | 词性: 短语动词
