hoit: /hɔɪt/
noun
a shout or loud call
He gave a hoit to get his friend's attention. 他大声喊叫以引起他朋友的注意。
Synonyms: yell, shout, call
Antonyms: whisper, murmur
词性: 名词


hoit: /hɔɪt/
verb
to shout or call loudly
He hoited to his friend across the field. 他向田野另一边的朋友大声喊叫。
Synonyms: yell, shout, call
Antonyms: whisper, murmur
词性: 动词
