mandolinists:/ˌmændəˈlɪnɪsts/
|n.|曼陀林演奏者，曼陀林家|A person who plays the mandolin. |The mandolinists played a lively tune. 曼陀林演奏者们演奏了一首轻快的曲子。|musicians, instrumentalists| - |noun|

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *mandolinista*, from *mandolino* (mandolin) + *-ista* (a suffix denoting a person who performs or practices something). *Mandolino* is a diminutive of *mandola*, from Greek *pandōra* (a kind of lute).
