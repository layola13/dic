hominin:/ˈhɒmɪnɪn/ | n. | 人族，人类 |A primate of a family (Hominidae) that includes humans and their fossil ancestors and also (in recent systems) at least some of the great apes.|  Scientists discovered a new hominin fossil in Africa.  科学家在非洲发现了一种新的人族化石。 | synonym: human ancestor, hominid | antonym:  (no direct antonym, could consider other animal families) | noun
