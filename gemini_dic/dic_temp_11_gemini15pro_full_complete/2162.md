addedly:/ˈædɪdli/
adv.
此外；又；加之
in addition; furthermore; also
He was tired, and addedly, he was hungry. 他很累，而且他还很饿。
近义词: additionally, furthermore, moreover, besides
反义词: however, nevertheless, nonetheless
