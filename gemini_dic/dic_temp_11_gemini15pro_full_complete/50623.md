derelictness: /ˌdɛrəˈlɪktnəs/
n.
失职；玩忽职守；破败；荒废
The state of having been abandoned and become dilapidated.  A shameful failure to fulfill one's obligations.
The derelictness of the building was evident in its crumbling facade. 这栋建筑的破败从其摇摇欲坠的正面可见一斑。
His derelictness in his duties led to his dismissal. 他玩忽职守导致他被解雇。

近义词: negligence, neglect, dilapidation, disrepair, abandonment
反义词: care, diligence, maintenance, repair, upkeep
词性: 名词
