dreamlike:/ˈdriːmlaɪk/| adj.|梦境般的，梦幻的|Resembling or characteristic of a dream; surreal, ethereal.|The forest had a dreamlike quality, with mist swirling between the ancient trees. 森林有一种梦幻般的气质，薄雾在古老的树木间缭绕。|近义词：dreamy, surreal, ethereal, otherworldly, fantastic, unreal, visionary|反义词：real, realistic, mundane, ordinary|词性:形容词
词源: From dream +‎ -like.
