half-great:/ˌhæfˈɡreɪt/
| adj. | 不太好的；平庸的；不太重要的 | Moderately great; not very important or significant.  A blend of "half" and "great," indicating something that is only partially great or impressive. |  The movie was half-great; the first half was amazing, but the second half was disappointing. 这部电影不太好；上半部分很棒，但下半部分令人失望。 | 近义词:mediocre, so-so, average | 反义词: excellent, outstanding, magnificent | 词性:形容词

half-great:/ˌhæfˈɡreɪt/
| n. |  不太重要的人或事 | Something or someone of moderate importance or significance. | His contributions to the project were half-great, not really impacting the outcome. 他对这个项目的贡献不太重要，并没有真正影响结果。| 近义词: nonentity, nobody, triviality | 反义词: VIP, celebrity, something important | 词性:名词
