nondiscursiveness:/ˌnɒndɪsˈkɜːsɪvnəs/ | n. | 非散漫性；简洁性；直接性 |  The quality of being concise and not rambling or digressive.  It refers to a style of communication or thought that is direct, to the point, and avoids unnecessary elaboration or digression. |  The nondiscursiveness of the legal document was appreciated by the judge.  法官很欣赏这份法律文件的简洁性。 |  conciseness, succinctness, directness, brevity | discursiveness, prolixity, verbosity, rambling | noun
