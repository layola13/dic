amorosity:/ˌæməˈrɒsəti/ /ˌæməˈrɑːsəti/
n.
喜爱；钟爱；多情；爱情
The quality or state of being amorous; lovingness.
He displayed an amorosity towards his new car that was almost embarrassing. 他对自己的新车表现出的喜爱之情几乎到了令人尴尬的地步。
近义词：affection, fondness, love, passion, tenderness
反义词：indifference, apathy, coldness, hatred

词性: noun
