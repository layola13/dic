dramatizes:/ˈdræməˌtaɪzɪz/
verb
戏剧化；使…戏剧化；夸张；渲染
presents something in a theatrical or exaggerated way.
The movie dramatizes the events of the war.  这部电影戏剧化地展现了战争的事件。
The media tends to dramatize the dangers of travel. 媒体倾向于夸大旅行的危险。
synonyms: exaggerate, overstate, embellish, sensationalize, heighten, amplify
antonyms: downplay, understate, minimize
词性: 动词
