PHRASE->
what if = 如果...会怎么样
What if it rains tomorrow? 如果明天下雨怎么办？
what for = 为了什么
What did you buy that for? 你买那个是为了什么？
no matter what = 无论如何
No matter what happens, I'll always be there for you. 无论发生什么，我都会在你身边。
what's more = 而且；更重要的是
He's intelligent, and what's more, he's very kind. 他很聪明，而且很善良。
what about = ...怎么样
What about going to the cinema tonight? 今晚去看电影怎么样？
