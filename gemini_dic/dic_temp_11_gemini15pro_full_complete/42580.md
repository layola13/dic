counterplays:/ˈkaʊntərˌpleɪz/
noun
对策，反制措施
actions taken in opposition to another action, especially in a game or military conflict
The coach devised several counterplays to the opponent's aggressive offense. 教练设计了几种针对对手猛烈进攻的应对策略。
synonyms: countermeasures, counterstrategies, countertactics
antonyms: strategies, tactics, plays
n.
