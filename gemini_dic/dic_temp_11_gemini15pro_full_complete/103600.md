join hands:/dʒɔɪn hændz/ | v. | 握手; 携手; 联合; 共同行动 | to clasp each other's hands, often as a sign of unity, agreement, or affection; to work together towards a common goal |  The couple joined hands and walked down the aisle. 这对夫妇手牵着手走过过道。

The two companies joined hands to develop the new product. 两家公司携手开发新产品。 | 近义词: cooperate, collaborate, unite, team up | 反义词: separate, divide, disagree, part ways | 词性: 动词

词源: From the Old French "joindre" meaning "to join" and the Old English "hand"
