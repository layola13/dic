horticulturalist:/ˌhɔːrtɪˈkʌltʃərəlɪst/ | n. | 园艺家；园艺师 | A person who practices horticulture; a gardener, especially one skilled in the science of cultivating gardens. |  The horticulturalist skillfully grafted the apple tree branches.  这位园艺师巧妙地嫁接了苹果树枝。| synonyms: gardener, landscaper, nurseryman, plantsman | antonyms: - | noun

horticulture:/ˌhɔːrtɪˈkʌltʃər/ | n. | 园艺；园艺学 | The science or art of cultivating fruits, vegetables, flowers, or ornamental plants. | She studied horticulture at the university. 她在大学学习园艺学。 | synonyms: gardening, floriculture, arboriculture | antonyms: - | noun
