|n.|江湖郎中;骗子|A person who deceives others, especially in order to trick them out of their money; a charlatan.|The mountebank promised miracle cures but only delivered worthless potions.  这个江湖郎中承诺奇迹般的治疗，但只提供了毫无价值的药水。|Synonyms: charlatan, swindler, con artist, fraud, imposter|Antonyms: honest person, altruist, philanthropist|Noun
