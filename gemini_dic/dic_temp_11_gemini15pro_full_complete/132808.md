nonecclesiastic:/ˌnɒnɪˌkliːziˈæstɪk/
adjective
非教会的；世俗的
Not connected with the church or clergy.
The nonecclesiastic buildings were converted into apartments.  这些非教会建筑被改建成了公寓。
The museum displayed both ecclesiastic and nonecclesiastic art.  博物馆展出了教会和非教会的艺术。
近义词:secular, lay, worldly
反义词:ecclesiastical, religious, clerical
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From non- + ecclesiastic.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "ecclesiastic" refers to the church or clergy.
