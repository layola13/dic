gonidium:/ɡɒˈnɪdiəm/ | n. | 藻殖孢子; 胞芽 | An asexual reproductive cell, especially in algae, fungi, and lichens; a non-motile spore. | Example 1: The gonidia of the algae were observed under a microscope.  在显微镜下观察到藻类的藻殖孢子。 | Example 2: The lichen's gonidia are responsible for its photosynthetic activity.  地衣的藻殖孢子负责其光合作用活性。 | Near synonyms: spore, propagule | Antonym: gamete | noun
