confidency:/ˈkɒnfɪdənsi/| n.|自信；把握；秘密；知心话|The state of being confident; a feeling of self-assurance and certainty in one's abilities or qualities.  It can also, less commonly, refer to a secret or private matter shared in confidence.|He spoke with confidency about the future of the company. 他自信地谈论了公司的未来。|近义词：confidence, assurance, self-assurance, certitude|反义词: diffidence, insecurity, doubt, uncertainty|词性:名词

词源: From late Middle English: from Old French confidence, from Latin confidentia, from confident- ‘trusting,’ from the verb confidere, from con- ‘with’ + fidere ‘to trust.’  The current preferred spelling, "confidence," became dominant in the 19th century.
