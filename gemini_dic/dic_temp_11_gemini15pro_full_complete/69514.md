fell-through:/fɛl θruː/| phrasal verb |落空，失败，泡汤 | If a plan or agreement falls through, it fails to happen. |  The deal fell through because of a lack of funding. 交易由于缺乏资金而告吹。

The trip fell through when her visa application was rejected. 她的签证申请被拒后，旅行泡汤了。| synonyms: fail, collapse, fall apart, come to nothing | antonyms: succeed, materialize, come to fruition | verb phrase
