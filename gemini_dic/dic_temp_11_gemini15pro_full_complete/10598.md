PHRASE->
apprise someone of something  告知某人某事
be apprised of  被告知
keep someone apprised of  随时告知某人
fail to apprise  未能告知

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *aprisier, from a- (from Latin ad ‘to’) + prisier ‘to value’, from pris ‘price’, from Latin pretium.  The original sense was ‘to set a price on’, later ‘to inform’.
