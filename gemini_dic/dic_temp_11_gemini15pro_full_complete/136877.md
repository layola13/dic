nonsoberer:/ˌnɒnˈsəʊbərər/ | adj. | 更不清醒的，更醉的 |  Not more sober; less sober; more intoxicated.  This is a non-standard and rarely used word formed by adding the comparative suffix "-er" to "nonsober," which itself is already a negation of "sober." | He seemed nonsoberer after the third beer. 他喝了第三杯啤酒后看起来更醉了。|  More intoxicated, drunker, less sober | More sober, clearer-headed | adjective
