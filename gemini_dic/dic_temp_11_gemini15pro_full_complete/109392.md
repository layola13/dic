lentiginous:/lɛnˈtɪdʒɪnəs/
adjective
长雀斑的；多雀斑的
Covered with lentigines; freckled.
Example:  The patient presented with lentiginous skin on her face and arms.  病人面部和手臂皮肤出现雀斑。

Example:  Lentiginous changes are common with sun exposure.  日晒后容易出现雀斑。

Synonym:  freckled, spotted
Antonym: clear, unspotted
词性: adjective
