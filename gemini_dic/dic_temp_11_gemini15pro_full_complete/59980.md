词源：
The word "Eddington" is a toponymic surname, meaning it originated from a place name.  It likely comes from a place called Eddington, which might refer to several places in England.  The name itself is derived from Old English elements, possibly meaning "Eadda's farm/estate" or "farm/estate of a man named Eadda."
