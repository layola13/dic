maybe:/ˈmeɪbi/| adv.|或许，可能|Perhaps; possibly.  It expresses uncertainty or possibility.|例句：Maybe I'll go to the party. 也许我会去参加聚会。|近义词：perhaps, possibly, perchance|反义词:certainly, definitely, absolutely|词性:副词
Etymology: From Middle English *may bee, from may (verb) + be (verb).  Originally two words, it fused into one over time.
