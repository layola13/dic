headlessness:/ˈhedləsnəs/| n.|无头状态，无领导状态|The state of being without a head or leader.  This can refer to a literal lack of a head (e.g., in a gruesome context), or more commonly, to an organization or group lacking leadership or direction.|The sudden headlessness of the company caused chaos among the employees. 公司突然失去领导导致员工一片混乱。|近义词：leaderlessness, anarchy, disorganization|反义词: leadership, organization|noun
