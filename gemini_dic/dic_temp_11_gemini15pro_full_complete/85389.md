hartfordite:/ˈhɑːrtfɔːrdaɪt/
n.
哈特福德石
A mineral composed of hydrated lead and copper silicate, found as globular aggregates.
Hartfordite is a rare mineral found in Arizona. 哈特福德石是一种罕见的矿物，发现于亚利桑那州。
近义词:mineral, lead copper silicate
反义词:None
词性:noun
