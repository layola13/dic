outclimbing:/ˌaʊtˈklaɪmɪŋ/
v. |胜过攀登，攀登得比…更高|Surpassing someone in climbing; climbing higher or better than another.
The young climber outclimbed his mentor on the treacherous peak.  年轻的登山者在险峻的山峰上胜过了他的导师。
She outclimbed all the other competitors and reached the summit first. 她比其他所有参赛者都爬得更高，第一个到达顶峰。
近义词:surpassing, exceeding, besting
反义词:being outclimbed, losing to
词性:动词
