intertalked:/ˌɪntərˈtɔːkt/| v.|互相交谈，互相通话|The two groups intertalked for hours, trying to reach an agreement.  两个小组互相交谈了几个小时，试图达成协议。|例句：The astronauts intertalked with mission control. 宇航员与地面指挥中心互相通话。 |近义词：conversed, communicated, discussed|反义词:ignored, avoided|词性:动词

词源:  "inter-" (between) + "talked" (past tense of "talk").  Prefix "inter-" indicates interaction between entities.
