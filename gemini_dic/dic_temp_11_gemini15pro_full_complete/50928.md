desiccative:/ˈdesɪkeɪtɪv/
adjective|干燥的；有干燥作用的|having the power or tendency to dry out
例句：Silica gel is a common desiccant used in packaging to absorb moisture and prevent spoilage.  硅胶是一种常见的干燥剂，用于包装中吸收水分并防止变质。

例句：The dry desert air is naturally desiccative, causing the plants to wither.  干燥的沙漠空气自然地具有干燥作用，导致植物枯萎。
近义词：drying, dehydrating, exsiccative
反义词：moistening, hydrating, humectant
词性: adjective

desiccative:/ˈdesɪkeɪtɪv/
noun|干燥剂|a substance used to absorb moisture and dry something
例句：Desiccatives are used to protect electronic equipment from moisture damage. 干燥剂用于保护电子设备免受潮湿损坏。

例句：The leather goods were packed with desiccatives to prevent mildew. 皮革制品中装有干燥剂以防止发霉。
近义词：desiccant, drying agent
反义词：humidifier, humectant
词性: noun

词源：From Latin desiccare "to dry thoroughly," from de- "thoroughly" + siccare "to dry," related to siccus "dry."
