axle:/ˈæksəl/ | n. | 轴，车轴 | A rod or spindle (either fixed or rotating) passing through the center of a wheel or group of wheels. | The front axle of the car was bent in the accident. 汽车的前轴在事故中弯曲了。| The wheels are connected by an axle.  轮子由一根轴连接。 | synonym: shaft, spindle, pin | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "rim" in the context of a wheel) | noun
