impassively:/ɪmˈpæsɪvli/| adv.|冷漠地，无表情地|Without showing any emotion; in an impassive manner.  Derived from "impassive" (adj.), from French "impassif," from Latin "impassus," meaning "unaffected, unmoved." The suffix "-ly" transforms the adjective into an adverb.|She listened impassively to the news of his death. 她冷漠地听着他死亡的消息。|calmly, stoically, unemotionally, indifferently|emotionally, expressively|adverb
