headings:/ˈhedɪŋz/ | n. | 标题，headings | A title at the head of a page or section of a book. | The headings in this report are clear and concise.  这份报告中的标题清晰简洁。 | titles, captions | footers | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *heding, from Old English hēafdung (“beheading, execution”), from hēafod (“head”).  More at head.
