expungement:/ɪkˈspʌndʒmənt/| n.|消除，删除，抹去|The act of officially removing something from a record.  The deletion of information from a document or other source.|He sought expungement of his criminal record. 他试图消除他的犯罪记录。|synonyms: deletion, erasure, removal, obliteration|antonyms:  retention, preservation, record, inscription|noun
