europeanises:/ˌjʊərəˈpɪənaɪzɪz/
verb
使欧洲化
to cause (something) to become European in character
The new regulations are gradually Europeanising the industry. 新的规章制度正在逐渐使该产业欧洲化。
近义词: westernize, modernize
反义词: localize, nationalize
词性:v.
