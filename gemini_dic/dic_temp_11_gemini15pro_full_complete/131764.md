nonconjugality:/ˌnɒnkɒndʒʊˈɡælɪti/ | n. | 非共轭性 | The state or quality of not being conjugate. In mathematics, particularly in group theory and linear algebra, it refers to elements or matrices that are not related by a similarity transformation.  In chemistry, it refers to systems with alternating single and multiple bonds where the pi electrons are not delocalized over the entire molecule. | The nonconjugality of the two matrices makes the calculation more complex. 这两个矩阵的非共轭性使得计算更加复杂。 | The nonconjugality of the pi system in this molecule affects its absorption spectrum.  该分子中π系统的非共轭性影响其吸收光谱。 |  non-conjugation, disjointness | conjugacy, conjugation | noun
