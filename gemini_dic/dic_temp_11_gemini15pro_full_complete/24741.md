broodiest:/ˈbruːdiɪst/| adj.|最忧郁的，最沉思的|Superlative adjective of broody: most broody.  Meaning inclined to brood; moody and thoughtful, often in a sullen or unhappy way.|例句：She gave me her broodiest look. 她给了我她最忧郁的眼神。

例句：He was the broodiest of the three brothers. 他是三兄弟中最闷闷不乐的。|近义词：gloomiest, sullenest, morosest|反义词：cheeriest, happiest, most cheerful|词性:形容词
词源: From brood + -y + -est.  "Brood" originates from Old English "brōd," meaning "a sitting of eggs."  The "-y" suffix forms adjectives, and "-est" creates the superlative form.
