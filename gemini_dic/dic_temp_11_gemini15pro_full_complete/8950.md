antihumanistic:/ˌæntiˌhjuːməˈnɪstɪk/
adjective
反人道主义的; 反人文主义的
Opposed to or denying the principles of humanism, especially the importance of human interests and values.
His antihumanistic philosophy alienated many of his followers.  他的反人文主义哲学使许多追随者疏远了他。
近义词:inhuman, inhumane, brutal, barbaric
反义词:humanistic, humane, compassionate
词性:adjective
