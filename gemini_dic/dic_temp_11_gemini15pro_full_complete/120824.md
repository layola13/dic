misauthorization:/ˌmɪsˌɔːθəraɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
未授权，错误授权
The act of authorizing something incorrectly or without proper authority.
The misauthorization led to a security breach. 错误授权导致了安全漏洞。
misapproval, denial of authorization
authorization, approval
noun
