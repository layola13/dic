expectorative:/ɪkˈspɛktərətɪv/| adj. n.|祛痰的；祛痰药|Relating to, or used to promote expectoration (the act of coughing up phlegm from the respiratory tract).  Also, a medicine that promotes expectoration.|The doctor prescribed an expectorative to help clear his lungs. 医生给他开了祛痰药以帮助清除肺部的痰。|近义词：mucolytic, expectorant|反义词:  (No clear antonym exists, perhaps antitussive which suppresses coughs) |词性: adjective, noun

expectorate:/ɪkˈspɛktəreɪt/| v.|咳出；吐痰|To cough or spit out phlegm from the lungs or throat.|He tried to expectorate the mucus from his throat. 他试图把喉咙里的粘液咳出来。|近义词：cough up, spit out|反义词: swallow|词性: verb
