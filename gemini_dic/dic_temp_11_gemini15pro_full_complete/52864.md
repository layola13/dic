dimidiation:/ˌdɪmɪˈdeɪʃən/ | n. |二分法，对分，减半 |The act of halving or dividing something into two equal parts.  It can also refer to a reduction by half. |The dimidiation of the kingdom led to two smaller, weaker states. 王国的分裂导致了两个更小、更弱的国家的出现。

The dimidiation of the company's profits was a cause for concern. 公司利润减半令人担忧。

|近义词：halving, bisection, division |反义词: doubling, duplication | noun
