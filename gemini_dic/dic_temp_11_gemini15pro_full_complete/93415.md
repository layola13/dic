illogical:/ɪˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
不合逻辑的；不合理的
not based on logic or reason
His argument was completely illogical. 他的论点完全不合逻辑。

It's illogical to assume that just because something is expensive, it's good.  认为贵的东西就一定是好的，这是不合逻辑的。

synonym: irrational, unreasonable, absurd, senseless
antonym: logical, rational, reasonable, sensible
词性: 形容词
