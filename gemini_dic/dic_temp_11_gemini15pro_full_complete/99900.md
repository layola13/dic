internationalised:/ɪntəˈnæʃənəlaɪzd/ | /ɪntərˈnæʃənəlaɪzd/
adj. | 国际化的
Adapted or altered to suit the needs or tastes of different countries or cultures.|  The company has internationalised its operations, with offices now in several countries.  这家公司已经将其业务国际化，现在在几个国家都设有办事处。
The website has been internationalised to support multiple languages.  该网站已国际化以支持多种语言。
近义词: globalized, worldwide
反义词: localized, domestic
词性: adjective

词源:
From international + -ise/-ize.  The suffix "-ise" or "-ize" is used to form verbs meaning "to make or become".
