ceratobranchial:/ˌsɛrətəˈbræŋkiəl/ | adj. n. | 角鳃的；角鳃骨 | Relating to or designating the ceratobranchials, a series of paired bones or cartilages forming the posterior part of the branchial arch in fish and some amphibians.  |  The ceratobranchial bones support the gills. 角鳃骨支撑着鳃。|  The ceratobranchial cartilage is well-developed in sharks. 角鳃软骨在鲨鱼中很发达。 | synonyms: branchial, gill-related | antonyms: None | noun, adjective

ceratobranchia:/ˌsɛrətəˈbræŋkiə/ | n. | 角鳃骨 | One of the ceratobranchial bones or cartilages. | The ceratobranchia are important for gill support. 角鳃骨对鳃的支撑很重要。|  Damage to the ceratobranchia can impair respiration. 角鳃骨的损伤会损害呼吸。 | synonyms: ceratobranchial bone | antonyms: None | noun
