gameness:/ˈɡeɪmnɪs/| n.|勇敢；顽强；斗志|The quality of being spirited and resilient, especially in the face of adversity.  A spirited determination to fight or struggle.|The boxer showed incredible gameness, fighting on despite a broken rib.  这位拳击手表现出惊人的斗志，尽管肋骨断了仍然继续战斗。|近义词：courage, bravery, pluck, spirit, tenacity, resilience|反义词：cowardice, timidity, fearfulness|词性:名词
Etymology: From game (adj., meaning "spirited, plucky") + -ness (suffix forming nouns of quality or state). Ultimately from Old English gamen ("game, sport, amusement").
