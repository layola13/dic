aleatory:/ˈeɪlɪtɔːri/ /ˈælɪtɔːri/
adjective
随机的；偶然的；碰运气的
depending on chance or luck; random
Example: The aleatory nature of the lottery makes it exciting for many people. 彩票的随机性让很多人感到兴奋。

Example:  The composer used aleatory techniques, allowing the musicians to improvise certain sections.  这位作曲家使用了随机技巧，允许音乐家即兴创作某些部分。

synonym
random, chance, accidental, haphazard, fortuitous, unpredictable
antonym
planned, predetermined, fixed, certain, predictable
adjective
