irreflection:/ˌɪrɪˈflɛkʃən/
n.
缺乏思考;轻率;不顾后果
lack of thought or consideration; rashness.
His irreflection led him to make a costly mistake. 他的轻率导致他犯了一个代价高昂的错误。
近义词:thoughtlessness, recklessness, imprudence, rashness
反义词:reflection, thoughtfulness, prudence, caution
词性:名词

irreflective:/ˌɪrɪˈflɛktɪv/
adj.
不反射的;缺乏思考的;轻率的
not reflective; lacking in thought or consideration; rash.
Her irreflective actions caused problems for everyone. 她不经思考的行为给大家带来了麻烦。
近义词:thoughtless, reckless, imprudent, rash
反义词:reflective, thoughtful, prudent, cautious
词性:形容词
