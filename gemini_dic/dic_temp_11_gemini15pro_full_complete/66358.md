excusablenesses: /ɪkˈskjuːzəbəlnəsɪz/
excusablenesses| n. |可原谅性；可辩解性|The quality of being excusable; the extent to which something can be excused.|His excusablenesses for being late were numerous, but ultimately unconvincing. 他迟到的理由很多，但最终未能令人信服。|Synonyms: forgivability, pardonability, justifiability|Antonyms: inexcusableness, unjustifiability|noun
