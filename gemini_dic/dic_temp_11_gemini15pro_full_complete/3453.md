afrobeat:/ˈæfroʊbiːt/
n.
非洲节拍;阿非罗节拍
A style of West African music featuring complex interlocking rhythms, prominent percussion, and vocals.
Fela Kuti is considered the pioneer of afrobeat.  费拉·库提被认为是非洲节拍的先驱。
Afrobeat has become increasingly popular worldwide. 非洲节拍在世界范围内越来越受欢迎。
近义词:Afro-pop, Juju music
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider genres like Classical Music or Country Music as contrasting styles)
词性:noun
