misnavigates:/ˌmɪsnævɪɡeɪts/
verb
错误导航，导航错误
To navigate incorrectly; to take a wrong course or route.
The captain misnavigated the ship and ran it aground. 船长导航错误，导致船搁浅了。
The driver misnavigated and ended up in the wrong city. 司机导航错误，最后到了错误的城市。
synonyms: goes astray, gets lost, takes a wrong turn, misdirects
antonyms: navigates correctly, finds the way, stays on course
词性: 动词
