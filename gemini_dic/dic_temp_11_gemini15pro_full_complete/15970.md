balti:/ˈbɔːlti/| n.|巴尔蒂咖喱，巴尔蒂餐馆|A type of curry served in a thin, pressed-steel wok-like dish, often considered a British Asian dish rather than a dish from the Indian subcontinent.  It is often served with naan bread.|We went for a balti last night. 我们昨晚去吃了巴尔蒂咖喱。|The balti was delicious and served sizzling hot. 这巴尔蒂咖喱味道鲜美，热滋滋地上桌。|近义词:curry, korma|反义词:|名词

词源: The word "balti" is thought to derive from the name of the metal dish in which the curry is served, which resembles a bucket ("balti" in Hindi and Urdu).  It's also suggested the name may come from the Baltistan region, though the dish isn't traditionally from there.
