hyperlustrousness:/ˌhaɪpərˈlʌstrəsnəs/
|n.|极度光亮，极度光泽|The hyperlustrousness of the newly polished car was dazzling. 新抛光汽车的极度光泽令人眼花缭乱。|
|The hyperlustrousness of the diamond made it exceptionally valuable. 这颗钻石极高的光泽使其格外珍贵。|
|近义词：extreme brilliance, high gloss, intense shine, radiant sheen|
|反义词：dullness, mattness, lack of luster|
|词性: noun|


Etymology:
hyper- (Greek): over, beyond, excessive
lustrous (Latin lustrosus, from lustrare "to brighten, purify"): having luster; shining
-ness (Old English -nes): forms nouns expressing a state, quality, or condition
