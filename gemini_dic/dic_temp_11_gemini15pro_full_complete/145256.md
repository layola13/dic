overboard:/ˈəʊvəbɔːd/ (UK) /ˈoʊvərbɔːrd/ (US)| adv., adj.| 落水； overboard (adv.) 在船外，落水；(adj.) 过分的，过度的| Literally, "over the board" of a ship. Figuratively, to go to extremes.  |The sailor fell overboard during the storm.  那名水手在暴风雨中落水了。|Synonyms: overboard (adv.): into the water, into the sea; overboard (adj.): excessive, extreme, immoderate|Antonyms: onboard (adv., adj.)| adverb, adjective
