conoidic:/kəˈnɔɪdɪk/
adjective
圆锥形的；近似圆锥体的
Resembling a cone; somewhat conical.
The shell has a conoidic spire.  这个贝壳有一个圆锥形的螺塔。
近义词:conical, cone-shaped
反义词:planar, flat
词性:adjective

词源:
From Greek *kōnoeides*, from *kōnos* (cone) + *-eides* (resembling).
