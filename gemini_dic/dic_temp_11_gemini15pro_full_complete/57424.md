drabbler:/ˈdræblər/| n.|草率的作者，粗制滥造者，涂鸦者|A writer who produces poor-quality work hastily or carelessly. Someone who scribbles or doodles.|He's a bit of a drabbler, churning out several novels a year, none of them particularly good. 他有点像个粗制滥造者，一年能写出好几部小说，但没有一部特别好。|近义词:scribbler, hack writer, potboiler|反义词:perfectionist, meticulous writer|词性:noun
