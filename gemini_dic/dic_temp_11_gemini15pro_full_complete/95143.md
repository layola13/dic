inappropriate: /ˌɪnəˈproʊpriət/ | adj. | 不适当的；不得体的；不适宜的 | Wearing a bikini to a funeral is inappropriate. 穿比基尼参加葬礼是不合适的。| Not suitable or proper in the circumstances. It is inappropriate to laugh at someone's misfortune.  嘲笑别人的不幸是不合适的。 | 近义词: unsuitable, improper, unfit, unbecoming | 反义词: suitable, appropriate, proper, fitting | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From in- (not) + appropriate.  "Appropriate" comes from Latin appropriatus, past participle of appropriare "to make one's own," from ad- "to" + proprius "one's own, special."
