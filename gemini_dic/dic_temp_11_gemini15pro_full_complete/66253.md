excluding:/ɪkˈskluːdɪŋ/ | prep.|不包括，除…之外| excluding weekends and holidays, the project will take approximately two weeks.  不包括周末和假期，这个项目大约需要两周时间。|excluding tax, the price is $100. 不含税，价格是100美元。|近义词:except, except for, barring, without|反义词:including, inclusive of|词性:介词

Etymology:
From Latin excludere "to shut out," from ex- "out" + claudere "to shut."
