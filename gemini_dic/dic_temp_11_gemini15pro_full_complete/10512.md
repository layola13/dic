apportion:/əˈpɔːrʃən/
verb
分配，分摊
To divide and distribute something proportionally.
The government will apportion the funds among the states. 政府将把资金分配给各州。
The judge apportioned blame to both drivers involved in the accident. 法官将事故的责任分摊给两名司机。
allocate, distribute, allot, assign
confiscate, withhold
