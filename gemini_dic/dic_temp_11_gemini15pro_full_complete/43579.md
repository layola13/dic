creaking:/ˈkriːkɪŋ/ (US), /ˈkriːkɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. n.|吱吱作响的；嘎吱声|A harsh, grating sound, like that of rusty hinges or floorboards.|The creaking stairs betrayed his presence. 吱吱作响的楼梯暴露了他的行踪。|近义词：squeaking, groaning, grating|反义词:silent, quiet|词性:形容词, 名词
Etymology: From Middle English *creken, frequentative of craken (“to crack, resound”), from Old English cracian (“to crack, crash, creak”), from Proto-Germanic *krakōną (“to crack, make a noise”).
