doctrinaire:/ˌdɒktrɪˈnɛːr/ /ˌdɑːktrɪˈnɛr/| adj. n.|教条的，空谈理论的；教条主义者|Sticking rigidly to a theory or set of beliefs without regard for practicality or suitability.|He has a doctrinaire approach to politics.|近义词:dogmatic, rigid, inflexible, uncompromising, unyielding|反义词:pragmatic, flexible, open-minded|adjective, noun

doctrinaire:/ˌdɒktrɪˈnɛːr/ /ˌdɑːktrɪˈnɛr/| n.|教条主义者|A person who adheres rigidly to a theory or set of beliefs without regard for practicality or suitability.|He is considered a doctrinaire by his colleagues.|近义词:dogmatist, ideologue|反义词:pragmatist|noun
