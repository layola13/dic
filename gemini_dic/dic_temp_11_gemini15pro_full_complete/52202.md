diastatic:/ˌdaɪəˈstætɪk/| adj.|淀粉酶的，促淀粉酶的|Relating to or causing diastasis; converting starch into sugar.  Diastatic enzymes break down starches into simpler sugars.|Diastatic malt is used in brewing to convert starches into fermentable sugars. 淀粉酶麦芽用于酿造，将淀粉转化为可发酵的糖。

Diastatic power is a measure of the enzyme activity in malt. 淀粉酶活力是麦芽中酶活性的一种测量方法。|近义词：amylolytic|反义词:|词性:形容词
