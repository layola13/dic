hypersaline:/ˌhaɪpərˈseɪliːn/ /ˌhaɪpərˈseɪlaɪn/
adjective
高盐度的；超盐性的
Containing a high concentration of salt; excessively saline.
The hypersaline lake is home to unique species of algae.  这个高盐度湖泊是一些独特藻类物种的家园。

The Dead Sea is a hypersaline environment. 死海是一个高盐度环境。

synonym
briny, salty
antonym
freshwater, hyposaline
