misperceived:/ˌmɪspərˈsiːvd/
verb
误解，错误地感知
incorrectly perceived or interpreted.
The quiet student was often misperceived as shy.  这个安静的学生经常被误认为是害羞。
His intentions were misperceived by his colleagues. 他的意图被他的同事误解了。
misunderstood, misinterpreted, misconstrued, misjudged
correctly perceived, understood, interpreted
