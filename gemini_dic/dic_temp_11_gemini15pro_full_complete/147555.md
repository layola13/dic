overpenalizing: /ˌoʊvərˈpiːnəˌlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 过度惩罚，惩罚过重 |  The act of penalizing someone excessively or too harshly.  This often refers to situations where the punishment doesn't fit the crime or goes beyond what is fair or necessary. | The referee was accused of overpenalizing the team with too many fouls. 裁判被指控对该队判罚过多的犯规。 |  The judge was criticized for overpenalizing the young offender for a minor crime. 法官因对年轻罪犯的轻微犯罪判处过重的刑罚而受到批评。 | overpunishing, oversanctioning | underpenalizing,  excusing | Verb
