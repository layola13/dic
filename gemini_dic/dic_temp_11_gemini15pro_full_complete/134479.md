nonlabelling:/ˌnɒnˈleɪbəlɪŋ/ | adj. | 非标记的，未贴标签的 |  The process of not adding a label or tag to something, especially in a scientific or technical context. This often implies that the item is being treated or studied in its natural or unmodified state, or that it is deliberately not being categorized or classified.  | Researchers used a nonlabelling technique to track cell migration. 研究人员使用非标记技术来追踪细胞迁移。| Synonyms: untagged, unmarked, unlabeled | Antonyms: labelled, tagged, marked | adjective

nonlabelling:/ˌnɒnˈleɪbəlɪŋ/ | n. | 非标记 | The act or process of not labeling. |  Nonlabelling of genetically modified organisms is controversial. 转基因生物的非标记是有争议的。| Synonyms: non-labeling (American spelling) | Antonyms: labelling, labeling (American spelling) | noun
