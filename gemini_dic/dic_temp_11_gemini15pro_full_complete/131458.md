noncognitive:/nɒnˈkɒɡnɪtɪv/ /nɑːnˈkɑːɡnətɪv/
adjective
非认知的
Not related to or involving the processes of cognition; not based on or capable of being reduced to empirical factual knowledge.
例句：Noncognitive skills, like grit and perseverance, are important for success. 非认知技能，例如毅力和坚持，对成功很重要。

例句：Researchers are studying the impact of noncognitive factors on student achievement. 研究人员正在研究非认知因素对学生成绩的影响。

近义词：non-intellectual, affective, social-emotional
反义词：cognitive, intellectual
词性：adjective
