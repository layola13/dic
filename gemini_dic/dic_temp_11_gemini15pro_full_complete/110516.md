limbernesses:/ˈlɪmbərnəsɪz/
n.
柔软性，灵活性
The quality of being limber; flexibility.
The dancer impressed the audience with her incredible limbernesses.  这位舞者以她惊人的柔软度给观众留下了深刻的印象。

近义词:flexibility, suppleness, pliancy
反义词:stiffness, rigidity
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From limber + -ness.  Limber itself comes from Middle English *limber*, from Old English *limplic* (“flexible”), related to *lempheald* (“flabby”).
