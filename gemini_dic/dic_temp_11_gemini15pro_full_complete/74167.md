fountainless:/ˈfaʊntɪnləs/
adjective
无喷泉的；没有喷泉的
Lacking a fountain or fountains.
The town square was surprisingly fountainless.  这个城镇广场竟然没有喷泉。
The park, though beautiful, was strangely fountainless.  尽管公园很漂亮，但奇怪的是没有喷泉。
近义词:unadorned, plain
反义词:fountained, ornate
词性:adjective
