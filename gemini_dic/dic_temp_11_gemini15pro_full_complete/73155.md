forbear:/fɔːrˈber/ (US), /fɔːˈbeə(r)/ (UK)| v.|克制，忍耐；避免|To refrain from; to abstain.|He forbore from striking the man, even though he was provoked. 即使被激怒了，他还是忍住了没有打那个人。|近义词：refrain, abstain, desist|反义词：indulge, yield|词性:动词

forbear:/ˈfɔːrber/ (US), /ˈfɔːbeə(r)/ (UK)| n.|祖先，祖宗|An ancestor.|His forbears came to America in the 17th century. 他的祖先在17世纪来到美洲。|近义词：ancestor, forefather|反义词：descendant|词性:名词
