exagitate: /ɪɡˈzædʒɪteɪt/
|adj.|激动的；不安的 (古语，罕用)|Excited; agitated (archaic, rare)|He became exagitate upon hearing the news. 他听到这个消息后变得激动不安。(罕用)|Synonyms: agitated, excited, disturbed|Antonyms: calm, composed, tranquil|adjective
