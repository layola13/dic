occupy: /ˈɒkjʊpaɪ/ (UK), /ˈɑːkjəpaɪ/ (US) | v. | 担任(职务)；任(职位) | to hold a position or office | She occupies the position of CEO. 她担任首席执行官的职位。| synonyms: hold, fill | antonyms: resign, relinquish | verb
