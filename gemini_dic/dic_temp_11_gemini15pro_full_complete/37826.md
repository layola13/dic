compere:/kəmˈpɛər/ (UK), /kɑːmˈpɪr/ (US)| n. v.|主持人，司仪；主持，担任司仪|A person who introduces the acts in a variety show or other entertainment.  Also, to act as a compere.|例句：The compere introduced the next act with a flourish. 主持人以华丽的辞藻介绍了下一个节目。

例句：He compered the annual awards ceremony. 他主持了年度颁奖典礼。|近义词：host, emcee, presenter, anchor|反义词:audience, guest|词性:noun, verb

Etymology: From French *compère* "godfather, comrade", from Latin *compater* "joint father, godfather".  The sense of "master of ceremonies" arose in the 20th century.
