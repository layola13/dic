microphonic:/ˌmaɪkrəˈfɒnɪk/ (British), /ˌmaɪkrəˈfɑːnɪk/ (American)| adj.|麦克风的；传声的；容易产生啸叫的|Sensitive to sound vibrations, especially in such a way as to produce unwanted noise in an electronic system.  Relating to or resembling a microphone.|The guitar's pickups were a bit microphonic, causing feedback at high volumes. 这把吉他的拾音器有点容易啸叫，在高音量时会产生反馈。|近义词：sensitive, resonant, amplifying|反义词：damped, muted|词性:形容词

microphonic:/ˌmaɪkrəˈfɒnɪk/ (British), /ˌmaɪkrəˈfɑːnɪk/ (American)| n.|传声器；麦克风|A microphone.|The old microphonic picked up every rustle and cough.  那个老式麦克风能拾取到每一个沙沙声和咳嗽声。|近义词：microphone|反义词： (No direct antonym)|词性:名词
