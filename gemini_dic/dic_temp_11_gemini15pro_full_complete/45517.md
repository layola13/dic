curette: /kjʊˈret/ (US), /kjʊˈret/ (UK)| n. v.|刮匙，刮除|A surgical instrument with a curved, scoop-shaped blade used for scraping or cleaning a biological surface, such as the uterine wall.|The dentist used a curette to remove plaque from beneath the gum line. 牙医用刮匙去除牙龈线下的牙菌斑。|The surgeon used a curette to clean the wound. 外科医生用刮匙清理伤口。|近义词:scraper, scoop|反义词:None|noun, verb
