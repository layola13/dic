consolatory:/kənˈsɒlətəri/ /kənˈsoʊlətɔːri/
adjective
安慰的；慰藉的
Intended to comfort or console someone who is grieving or disappointed.
例句：She offered a few consolatory words. 她说了几句安慰的话。

例句：He gave her a consolatory pat on the shoulder. 他安慰地拍了拍她的肩膀。


近义词：comforting, soothing, reassuring, cheering
反义词：distressing, upsetting, disheartening


词性
adjective
