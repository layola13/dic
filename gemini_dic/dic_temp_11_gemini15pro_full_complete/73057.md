footnote:/ˈfʊtnəʊt/ (US) /ˈfʊtnoʊt/ (UK)| n. |脚注，注释|A note at the bottom of a page that gives more information about something mentioned in the text.| The book has many footnotes explaining difficult terms. 这本书有很多脚注解释难懂的术语。| synonym: annotation, endnote | antonym:  (No direct antonym, could consider "main text") | noun

etymology: from foot (bottom of the page) + note (explanatory comment)
