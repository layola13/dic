mdf:/ˈɛm diː ɛf/ | n. | 中密度纤维板 (Medium-Density Fiberboard) | A type of engineered wood product made by breaking down hardwood or softwood residuals into wood fibers, often in a defibrator, combining it with wax and a resin binder, and forming panels by applying high temperature and pressure. | The cabinets are made of mdf. 这些橱柜是用中密度纤维板做的。| synonyms: fiberboard, particleboard | antonyms: solid wood | noun

mdf:/ˈɛm diː ɛf/ | abbreviation | 主要分销框架 (Main Distribution Frame) | A termination point within the local telephone exchange where each customer's cable pairs terminate. | The technician connected the line to the mdf. 技术员将线路连接到主要分销框架。| synonyms: main distribution frame | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps "remote terminal") | abbreviation
