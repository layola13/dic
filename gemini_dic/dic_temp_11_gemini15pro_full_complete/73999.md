fortunately:/ˈfɔːrtʃənətli/ /ˈfɔːrtʃənɪtli/| adv.|幸运地，幸亏|Happily; luckily; by good fortune.  It indicates a positive outcome or circumstance. | Fortunately, the rain stopped before the picnic. 幸好，野餐前雨停了。| Fortunately, I had a backup copy of my work. 幸好，我有工作的备份副本。| synonym: luckily, thankfully, happily | antonym: unfortunately, unluckily, sadly | adverb
