bulge:/bʌldʒ/
| n. |膨胀，凸起|A rounded swelling or protuberance that distorts a flat or straight surface.|The suitcase was so full that it bulged at the seams.  行李箱太满了，接缝处都鼓起来了。|近义词：swell, protrude, lump|反义词:hollow, dent, depression|词性:名词
bulge:/bʌldʒ/
| vi. |膨胀，凸起|To swell outwards; become protuberant.|His pockets bulged with candy. 他的口袋里装满了糖果，鼓鼓囊囊的。|近义词：swell, protrude, stick out|反义词:contract, shrink, flatten|词性:动词
