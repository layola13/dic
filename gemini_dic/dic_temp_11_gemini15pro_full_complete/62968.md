ennui:/ˈɒnwiː/ (UK), /ˌɑːnˈwiː/ (US)| n. |倦怠；厌烦；无聊|A feeling of listlessness and dissatisfaction arising from a lack of occupation or excitement.|例句：She suffered from a profound sense of ennui. 她深感倦怠。|近义词：boredom, tedium, listlessness, apathy, languor|反义词：enthusiasm, excitement, interest, energy|词性:noun

ennui:| Etymology: French, from Old French enui, from anuier to annoy, from Late Latin inodiare, from Latin in- + odium hatred|
