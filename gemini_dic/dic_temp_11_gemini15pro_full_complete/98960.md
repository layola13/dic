inter-forces:/ˌɪntərˈfɔːrsɪz/| n. |相互作用力，分子间作用力|Forces acting between molecules. These forces include van der Waals forces, dipole-dipole interactions, and hydrogen bonding.| The strength of the inter-forces determines the physical properties of a substance, such as boiling point and melting point. 物质的分子间作用力强度决定了它的物理性质，例如沸点和熔点。|intermolecular forces, interaction forces|intramolecular forces|noun
