anonymize:/əˈnɒnɪˌmaɪz/ | v. |匿名化，使匿名|To remove identifying information from something, such as a document, so that individuals cannot be recognized.|例句：The researchers anonymized the data before sharing it. 研究人员在共享数据之前先对其进行了匿名化处理。|例句：They anonymized the survey responses to protect the participants' privacy. 他们将调查回复匿名化以保护参与者的隐私。|近义词：de-identify, redact|反义词: identify, personalize|词性:动词
