directors:/dəˈrɛktərz/ | n. | 董事，主管，导演 | A body of persons appointed to manage the affairs of a company or other organization. | The directors voted to approve the merger. 董事们投票通过了合并。| synonyms: managers, executives, administrators, supervisors, board members | antonyms: subordinates, employees, staff | noun

directors:/daɪˈrɛktərz/ | n. | 导演 | A person who supervises the actors, camera crew, and other staff in the making of a film or play. | The director yelled "Cut!" after the scene. 导演在场景结束后大喊“停！”。| synonyms: filmmaker, producer, conductor | antonyms: actor, performer | noun
