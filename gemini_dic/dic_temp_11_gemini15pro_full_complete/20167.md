biprism:/ˈbaɪprɪzəm/| n.|双棱镜|An optical device consisting of two prisms placed base to base, used to produce interference patterns.|Light from a single source is passed through the biprism and creates an interference pattern on a screen. 光线从单一光源穿过双棱镜，并在屏幕上产生干涉图样。|近义词：prism 棱镜|反义词: 无|名词
