confectioned:/kənˈfɛkʃənd/
|v.|精心制作的；糖果的；甜食的
|Made or prepared delicately and elaborately, typically of food.  Often associated with sweets or sugary treats.|
|The cake was confectioned with elaborate icing roses.|这个蛋糕用精心制作的糖霜玫瑰装饰。
|近义词：prepared, made, created, assembled, concocted|
|反义词：unprepared, raw, natural, simple|
|词性: v. (past participle)


confection:/kənˈfɛkʃən/
|n.|糖果；甜食；精巧的制品
|A sweet, sugary treat; something delicately or elaborately made.|
|The shop sold a variety of confections, from chocolates to marzipan.|这家商店出售各种糖果，从巧克力到杏仁蛋白软糖。
|近义词：sweet, candy, treat, delicacy|
|反义词：savory, unsweetened|
|词性: n.


词源:
From Late Latin *confectio(n-), from Latin conficere ‘to make up, prepare’.
