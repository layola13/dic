litigator:/ˈlɪtɪɡeɪtər/| n.|诉讼人，进行诉讼者|A person who is involved in a lawsuit, especially a lawyer who conducts the case.|The litigator argued passionately for his client's innocence.  这位诉讼律师热情地为其当事人的清白辩护。|Example:  Experienced litigators understand the complexities of the legal system. 经验丰富的诉讼律师了解法律体系的复杂性。|Synonyms: lawyer, attorney, advocate, barrister, solicitor|Antonyms: defendant, accused, respondent|noun
