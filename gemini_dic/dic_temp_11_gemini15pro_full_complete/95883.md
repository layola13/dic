PHRASE->
increase in = 在...方面的增加 (There has been a significant increase in tourism this year. 今年旅游业有了显著增长。)
increase by = 增加了... (The price increased by 10%. 价格上涨了 10%。)
increase to = 增加到... (The company increased its workforce to 1000 employees. 该公司将其员工人数增加到 1000 人。)
increase with = 随着...而增加 (The risk of heart disease increases with age. 心脏病的风险随着年龄的增长而增加。)
on the increase = 在增长中 (Crime is on the increase in the city.  城市犯罪率正在上升。)
