dietitian:/ˌdaɪəˈtɪʃən/ | n. |营养师，营养学家 | A dietitian is an expert in food and nutrition. | Example 1: The dietitian advised her to eat more fruits and vegetables. 营养师建议她多吃水果和蔬菜。
