exaggeratory:/ɪɡˈzædʒəˌrətɔːri/ /ɪɡˈzædʒəˌretɔːri/
adjective
夸张的，夸大的
Characterized by exaggeration; tending to exaggerate.
Her exaggeratory account of the incident made it sound much worse than it actually was. 她对事件的夸大描述使它听起来比实际情况糟糕得多。
synonyms: exaggerated, overstated, inflated, hyperbolic, embellished
antonyms: understated, downplayed, minimized
词性: 形容词
