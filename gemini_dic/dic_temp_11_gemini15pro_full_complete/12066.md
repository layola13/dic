artificialism:/ˌɑːrtɪˈfɪʃəlɪzəm/
n.
人工论；人为主义
The belief that natural phenomena are caused by the intentional acts of a god or other supernatural being.  The tendency to see all of nature as a product of human design or invention.
Jean Piaget's theory of cognitive development includes a stage called artificialism, where children believe that natural objects and events are man-made.  皮亚杰的认知发展理论包含一个称为“人工论”的阶段，在这个阶段，孩子们相信自然物体和事件是人造的。
Children in the preoperational stage often exhibit artificialism.  处于前运算阶段的儿童经常表现出人工论。
近义词:anthropomorphism
反义词:naturalism
词性:noun
