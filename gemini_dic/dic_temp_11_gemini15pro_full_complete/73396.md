foreign: /ˈfɔːrɪn/ (UK) /ˈfɔːrən/ (US) | n. | 外国的事物；外国语 | something that is from another country, a language that is not your own |  He doesn't know much about foreign affairs. 他不太了解外交事务。 | synonyms: external | antonyms: domestic | noun (rarely used)
