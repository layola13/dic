provocative:/prəˈvɑːkətɪv/
|adj.|挑衅的；刺激的|Her provocative remarks angered the audience. 她挑衅性的言论激怒了观众。|The artist's provocative work sparked a heated debate.  这位艺术家的挑衅性作品引发了激烈的辩论。|近义词：stimulating, exciting, challenging|反义词：nonprovocative, inoffensive, soothing|词性: 形容词

词源：
From non- + provocative + -ness.  "Provocative" comes from Late Latin provocativus, from provocare "to challenge," from pro- "forth" + vocare "to call."
