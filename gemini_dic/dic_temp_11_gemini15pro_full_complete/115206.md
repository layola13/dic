manifestationist:/ˌmænɪfɛˈsteɪʃənɪst/
n.
显化论者，主张思想可以转化为现实的人
A person who believes in and practices manifestation, the idea that thoughts and beliefs can create reality.
The manifestationist claimed she could attract wealth through positive thinking.  显化论者声称她可以通过积极的思考来吸引财富。
The manifestationist visualized her dream home and believed it would eventually materialize.  显化论者想象着她梦想中的家，并相信它最终会实现。
近义词:believer in the law of attraction,  vision board enthusiast
反义词:skeptic, realist
词性:noun
