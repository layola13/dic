forwander:/fɔːrˈwɒndər/| v.|漫游，迷路，偏离正道|To wander away or go astray, especially to lose one's way.|He forwandered in the woods for hours. 他在树林里迷路了几个小时。|近义词：stray, wander, roam, err, deviate|反义词: stay, remain|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From for- + wander.  "For-" in this context is an intensifying prefix, meaning "thoroughly," "completely," or "excessively."  "Wander" comes from Old English *wandrian, of Germanic origin.
