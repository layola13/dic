if-you-please:/ˌɪf juː ˈpliːz/| idiom |如果您愿意；请 | A polite addition to a request.  Used to make a request or instruction sound more polite. |"Pass the salt, if you please." 请把盐递给我。|近义词：please, kindly, if you would be so kind |反义词: (No direct antonym, implies absence of politeness) | idiom
