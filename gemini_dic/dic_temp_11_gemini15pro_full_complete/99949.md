internuncio:/ˌɪntərˈnʌnsioʊ/
noun
教廷大使；教皇使节
A papal legate of lower rank than a nuncio, permanently accredited to the government of a smaller nation.
The internuncio delivered a message from the Pope. 教廷大使传达了教皇的旨意。
近义词: legate, envoy, nuncio
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "rebel," "dissident" in a contextual sense)
词性: n.


internuncio:/ˌɪntərˈnʌnsioʊ/
noun
(尤指教皇的)使节，特使
A messenger, especially one sent on a diplomatic mission.  (More general, less specifically tied to the Vatican)
He served as an internuncio between the warring factions. 他在交战派别之间充当使节。
近义词: emissary, representative, ambassador
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "interceptor," "obstructor" in a contextual sense)
词性: n.


词源:
From Late Latin *internuncius, from Latin inter- (“between”) + nuncius (“messenger”).
