cretinize:/ˈkriːtɪnaɪz/
verb
使…硬结成水泥状；使…石灰化
to convert into or deposit as cretaceous limestone.
The continuous dripping of mineral-rich water cretinized the cave floor over thousands of years.  数千年来，富含矿物质的水不断滴落，使洞穴底部硬结成水泥状。
The process slowly cretinized the ancient seabed. 这个过程慢慢地使古海底石灰化。
近义词:calcify, petrify, harden
反义词:soften, dissolve
词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *crétiniser*, from *crétine* (“chalk”), from Latin *crēta* (“Cretan earth, chalk”).
