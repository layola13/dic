empasm:/ˈɛmpəzəm/| n.|强调，着重|A particular stress or force of expression.  It gives special importance to something.|He spoke with empasm about the need for change. 他强调需要变革。|近义词：emphasis, stress, accentuation|反义词: understatement, downplaying|词性:名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *empasma, from Greek *ἔμπασμα (émpasma, “powder sprinkled on the body”), from ἐνπάσσω (enpássō, “sprinkle on”), from ἐν (en, “in, on”) + πάσσω (pássō, “sprinkle”).  Originally referred to a medicinal powder, then to something that gives force or intensity.
