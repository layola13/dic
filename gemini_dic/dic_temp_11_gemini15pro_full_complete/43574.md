creakier:/ˈkriːkiər/ | adj. | 更吱嘎作响的 | Comparative form of 'creaky': Making a harsh, grating sound, typically as a result of friction between two hard surfaces. | The stairs were much creakier than I remembered.  楼梯比我记忆中吱嘎作响得厉害多了。| The old rocking chair is getting creakier every day. 这把旧摇椅一天比一天吱嘎作响。 | 近义词: squeakier, raspier, grating | 反义词: smoother, quieter, silent | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *creken, frequentative of craken (“to crack”), from Old English cracian (“to crack, crash, creak”). Related to Old Norse kraka (“to crow, croak”).
