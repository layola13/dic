mixable:/ˈmɪksəbl/| adj.|可混合的，可拌和的|Capable of being mixed.  Something that can be combined with something else, typically a liquid, without separating or causing unwanted chemical reactions.|The paint is mixable with water.  这种颜料可以和水混合。|Synonyms: blendable, combinable, compatible|Antonyms: immiscible, unmixable|adjective
