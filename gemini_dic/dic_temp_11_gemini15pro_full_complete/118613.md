messenger: /ˈmesɪndʒər/ (US) /ˈmesɪndʒə(r)/ (UK)| n. |信使；使者；通讯程序|A person who carries a message or does an errand.|例句：The messenger delivered the package. 信使送来了包裹。|近义词：courier, envoy, emissary|反义词: recipient|词性: noun

messenger: /ˈmesɪndʒər/ (US) /ˈmesɪndʒə(r)/ (UK)| n. |(即时通讯程序的)通讯软件|A software application used for instant messaging.|例句：I use this messenger to chat with my friends. 我用这个通讯软件和我的朋友聊天。|近义词：IM app, chat app|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
