dihedral:/daɪˈhiːdrəl/ | adj. n. |二面的，二面角的；二面角|Relating to or formed by two intersecting planes or surfaces, especially two adjacent faces of a crystal or the wings of an airplane.  A dihedral angle.|The dihedral angle between the wings of the aircraft is crucial for stability.  飞机机翼之间的二面角对稳定性至关重要。
The dihedral of a crystal affects its light-refracting properties. 晶体的二面角会影响其光的折射特性。|近义词:bilateral, two-sided|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective, noun

dihedral:/daɪˈhiːdrəl/ | n. |二面角|The angle between two intersecting planes or surfaces.|The dihedral angle of the wings is 10 degrees. 机翼的二面角是10度。|近义词:angle between two planes|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
