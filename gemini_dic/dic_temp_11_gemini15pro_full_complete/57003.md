double-jointed:/ˌdʌbəlˈdʒɔɪntɪd/
adjective
双关节的；关节过度活动的
Having joints that allow more flexibility and movement than usual.
My sister is double-jointed and can bend her fingers backwards. 我的妹妹是双关节，可以把手指往后弯。
She's a double-jointed contortionist. 她是一位双关节柔术演员。

近义词:flexible, hypermobile
反义词:stiff, rigid
词性:adjective
