nondependent:/ˌnɑːndɪˈpɛndənt/ | adj. | 非依赖的，自主的 | Not dependent; independent. Able to support oneself or itself. |  She is a nondependent adult who pays her own bills. 她是一个经济独立的成年人，自己支付账单。|  He runs a nondependent business. 他经营一家独立自主的企业。 | 近义词: independent, self-sufficient, autonomous | 反义词: dependent, reliant | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "dependent" (from Latin "dependere" - to hang down, to rely on)
