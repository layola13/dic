noncoalescing:/ˌnɒnkoʊəˈlɛsɪŋ/ | adj. | 不聚结的，不合并的 | Not joining or merging into a single mass or unit.  Describes droplets, bubbles, or particles that remain separate and do not combine. |  The noncoalescing droplets formed a fine mist. 不聚结的液滴形成了细小的薄雾。|  The paint contained noncoalescing pigments, resulting in a speckled finish. 油漆中含有不聚结的颜料，导致表面出现斑点。 | Near synonyms: immiscible, unmixable, discrete, separate | Antonyms: coalescing, merging, uniting, combining | adjective
