overdressed:/ˌoʊvərˈdrest/| adj.|穿着过分正式的；打扮过分的|Wearing clothes that are too formal or elaborate for the occasion.  Dressed more formally than is appropriate or usual.|例句：I felt overdressed in my suit at the casual party. 我穿着西装在轻松的派对上感觉穿着过分正式了。

例句：She was definitely overdressed for a picnic in the park.  对于在公园里的野餐来说，她显然穿得太正式了。|近义词：too formally dressed, inappropriately dressed|反义词：underdressed, casually dressed|词性：形容词

overdress:/ˌoʊvərˈdres/| v.|穿着过分正式；打扮过分|To dress too formally or elaborately for an occasion.|例句：It's easy to overdress for a wedding if you're not sure of the dress code. 如果你不确定着装要求，参加婚礼很容易穿得太正式。

例句：Don't overdress for the interview; business casual is fine.  面试不要穿得太正式；商务休闲装就可以了。|近义词：dress too formally, dress inappropriately|反义词：dress casually, underdress|词性：动词
