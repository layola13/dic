euthanasia:/ˌjuːθəˈneɪʒə/ | n. | 安乐死 | The painless killing of a patient suffering from an incurable and painful disease or in an irreversible coma.  | The debate about euthanasia continues to be a sensitive one.  安乐死辩论仍然是一个敏感话题。 |  Euthanasia is sometimes referred to as "mercy killing". 有时安乐死被称为“仁慈的杀戮”。 |  Synonyms: mercy killing, assisted suicide | Antonyms:  preservation of life | noun
