mach's:/mɑːxs/ | interj. | 好吧；就这样吧；成交；行 | Expressing agreement or acceptance, often implying a sense of resignation or indifference.  Derived from German "mach es" meaning "do it" or "make it so". | Mach's gut. (German for "Do it well" or "All the best") 再见，保重。 |  Okay, alright, sure, fine | No direct antonym. | interjection

注意事项:
Mach's is more common in German-speaking regions and might not be readily understood in other English-speaking contexts.  It carries a slightly informal and sometimes dismissive tone.  Using a more standard expression like "Okay," "Alright," or "Fine" might be preferable in most situations.
