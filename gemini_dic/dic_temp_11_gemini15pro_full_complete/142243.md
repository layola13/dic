orologist:/ɔːˈrɒlədʒɪst/ /ɔːˈrɑːlədʒɪst/
n.
气象学家；山地学家
A scientist who studies mountains or weather.
Orologists predicted heavy snowfall in the mountain regions. 气象学家预测山区将有大雪。
The orologist studied the formation of the Himalayas.  这位山地学家研究了喜马拉雅山的形成。
近义词：meteorologist (for weather), geologist (for mountains)
反义词: N/A
词性：noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek 'oros' (mountain) + '-logist' (one who studies). 源于希腊语 'oros'（山）+ '-logist'（研究者）。
