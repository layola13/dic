clothier:/ˈkloʊðiər/ (US) /ˈkləʊðiə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 服装商，成衣商|A person who makes, sells, or deals in clothes, especially outerwear.  A clothier can be a tailor, a dressmaker, or a retailer of clothing.|The clothier offered a wide selection of suits.  这家服装商提供各种各样的西装。|Synonyms: outfitter, garment maker, apparel maker, haberdasher (for men's clothing) |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider) customer, consumer|Noun
