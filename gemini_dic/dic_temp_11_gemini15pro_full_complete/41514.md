corollifloral: /ˌkɒrəˈlɪflɔːrəl/ /ˌkɔːrəˈlɪflɔːrəl/
adjective
花冠生的，合瓣花类的
Having the flowers inserted on the corolla, or on a prolongation of the corolla tube.
The honeysuckle family (Caprifoliaceae) is characterized by its corollifloral arrangement. 忍冬科植物的特点是其花冠生的排列方式。
Near synonyms: sympetalous, gamopetalous
Antonyms: choripetalous, polypetalous
Word Origin: From Latin *corolla* (corolla) + *floralis* (floral)
