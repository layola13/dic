intertwining:/ˌɪntərˈtwaɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 互相缠绕的，交织的 | Closely joined or woven together. |  The two vines were intertwining around the tree.  两根藤蔓互相缠绕在树上。 | intertwined, interwoven, interlaced | separate, detached | adjective


intertwine:/ˌɪntərˈtwaɪn/ | v. |  (使)缠绕，(使)交织 | To twist or twine together. |  The branches of the two trees intertwined. 两棵树的树枝交织在一起。|  interweave, interlace, entwine | separate, disentangle | verb



词源:

From inter- + twine.  "Inter-" prefix meaning "between, among, mutually, reciprocally," and "twine" meaning "to twist together."
