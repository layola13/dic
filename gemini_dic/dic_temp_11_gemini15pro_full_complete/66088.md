excavational:/ˌɛkskəˈveɪʃənəl/
adjective
挖掘的；开凿的
Relating to or involved in excavation.
The excavational work unearthed a Roman mosaic.  挖掘工作出土了一幅罗马马赛克。
The team used excavational techniques to uncover the ancient city.  团队使用挖掘技术发现了这座古城。
近义词:unearthing, digging, excavating
反义词:filling, burying, covering
词性:adjective


词源:
From excavation + -al. Excavation comes from the Late Latin excavatio, from excavare "to hollow out," from ex- "out" + cavare "to hollow."
