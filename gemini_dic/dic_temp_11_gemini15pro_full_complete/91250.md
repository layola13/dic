hydroelectricities:/ˌhaɪdroʊɪˌlɛkˈtrɪsɪtiz/ | n. | 水力发电（总称，常用复数形式，但单数形式hydroelectricity更常见） | The generation of electricity using flowing water (usually from a reservoir held behind a dam or other barrier) to drive turbines. |  The country relies heavily on hydroelectricities for its power needs. 这个国家严重依赖水力发电来满足其电力需求。 | synonym: hydropower, water power | antonym: fossil fuels, nuclear power | noun
