noncreative:/ˌnɒnkriˈeɪtɪv/ | /ˌnɑːnkriˈeɪtɪv/ | adj. | 无创造力的；缺乏创造性的 | Not creative; lacking in creativity. |  His work on the project was surprisingly noncreative.  他在这个项目上的工作令人惊讶地缺乏创造力。 | The advertising campaign was rather noncreative and failed to generate much interest.  这个广告宣传活动相当缺乏创意，没有引起太多关注。 | 近义词：unimaginative, uninspired, pedestrian, mundane | 反义词：creative, imaginative, innovative, inspired | 词性: 形容词

词源:
non- (prefix) + creative (adjective)
"non-" comes from Latin, meaning "not."
"creative" comes from Latin "creatus," past participle of "creare," meaning "to create."
