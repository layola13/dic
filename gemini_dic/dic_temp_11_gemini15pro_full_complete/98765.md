intaking:/ˈɪnteɪkɪŋ/
n.|摄入，吸入，接收|The process of taking something in, such as food, air, or information.|例句：The doctor asked about her daily calorie intake. 医生询问了她每天的卡路里摄取量。|近义词：consumption, absorption, ingestion|反义词:output, excretion|词性:名词

intaking:/ɪnˈteɪkɪŋ/
adj.|吸收的，摄入的|Relating to the process of taking something in.|例句：The intake valve of the engine is faulty. 发动机的进气阀坏了。|近义词：ingestive|反义词:expelling|词性:形容词
