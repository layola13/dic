geminations:/ˌdʒɛmɪˈneɪʃənz/ | n. | 重复，成双，成对出现 |  The act of doubling, especially the doubling of a consonant in a word.  This often occurs in certain languages as a result of phonological processes. | The geminations of consonants are common in Italian. 辅音的重复在意大利语中很常见。 | duplication, repetition | separation, division | noun
