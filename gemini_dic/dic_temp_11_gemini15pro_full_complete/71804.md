fleeced:/fliːst/
| v. |欺骗，敲诈；剪（羊毛）|To cheat someone out of money or property. / To remove the fleece from a sheep.|He was fleeced of his life savings by a con artist. 他被一个骗子骗走了毕生积蓄。/ The farmer fleeced the sheep in the spring.  春天农民给羊剪毛。|近义词：swindled, cheated, defrauded, clipped|反义词：repaid, compensated, gave|词性：动词

fleeced:/fliːst/
| adj. |被骗的；被敲诈的；剪了毛的|Having been cheated or swindled. / Having had the fleece removed.|He felt fleeced after buying the overpriced car.  买了那辆价格过高的汽车后，他觉得自己被骗了。/ The newly fleeced sheep looked comical.  刚剪完毛的羊看起来很滑稽。|近义词：cheated, swindled, defrauded|反义词：repaid, compensated|词性：形容词
