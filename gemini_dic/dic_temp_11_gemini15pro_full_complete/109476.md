leprechaun: /ˈlɛprəkɔːn/ (US) /ˈlɛprɪkɔːn/ (UK)| n. | 小妖精，矮妖 | A small, mischievous sprite in Irish folklore, typically bearded, wearing a green coat and hat, and reputed to possess hidden treasure. |  Legend says that if you catch a leprechaun, it must reveal the location of its gold. 传说如果你抓住一个小妖精，它必须告诉你它的金子藏在哪里。 | synonyms: sprite, fairy, pixie, imp | antonyms: giant | noun
