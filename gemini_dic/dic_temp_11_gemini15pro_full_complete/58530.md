duffer:/ˈdʌfər/| n.|笨蛋，不擅长的人；劣质品|A person who is not skilled at something; something of poor quality.|He's a bit of a duffer at golf. 他不太擅长打高尔夫球。

The antique shop was full of duffers. 这家古董店里满是劣质品。|近义词：klutz, bungler, incompetent, amateur, novice|反义词：expert, professional, proficient|词性: 名词
