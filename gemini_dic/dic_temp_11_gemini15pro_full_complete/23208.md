bowered:/ˈbaʊərd/| adj.|被树荫遮蔽的，荫蔽的|Covered with bowers; shaded by trees or other plants.|The bowered walkway was a welcome respite from the summer sun.  被树荫遮蔽的走道是躲避夏日阳光的好去处。|近义词：shaded, leafy, overgrown|反义词：exposed, bare|词性:形容词
词源: From bower + -ed, from Middle English bour, from Old English būr "dwelling, chamber, inner room," of Germanic origin.
