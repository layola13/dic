loft-bed:/ˈlɒft bed/ (UK), /ˈlɔːft bed/ (US)| n. |阁楼床，高架床|A bed on a raised frame, creating a space underneath that can be used for storage or as a work or play area.|例句：My son loves his new loft bed; he uses the space underneath to build Lego creations. 我儿子很喜欢他的新阁楼床；他用下面的空间搭建乐高积木。

例句：Loft beds are a great space-saving solution for small rooms. 阁楼床是小房间节省空间的好办法。|近义词：bunk bed, elevated bed|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "regular bed", "standard bed")|词性:noun
