hybridism:/ˈhaɪbrɪdɪzəm/ | n. |杂交，混合，杂种 |The process or result of combining different elements, especially different varieties of plants or animals, to create a hybrid.  The state or quality of being a hybrid. A hybrid thing or system.|  例句：The hybridism of the orchid resulted in a beautiful new variety.  这种兰花的杂交产生了一个美丽的新品种。

例句：The car is a perfect example of hybridism, combining gasoline and electric power. 这辆车是混合动力技术的完美典范，它结合了汽油和电力。| 近义词：crossbreeding, hybridization, interbreeding, mixture, blend | 反义词：purity, purebred | 词性：名词
