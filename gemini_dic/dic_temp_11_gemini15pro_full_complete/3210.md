affectibility:/əˌfɛktɪˈbɪlɪti/
n.
易受感动性，易感性
The quality of being easily affected emotionally; susceptibility to emotional influences.
Her affectibility made her prone to tears at even the slightest provocation. 她容易受感动，即使是最轻微的刺激也会让她流泪。
He displayed a surprising degree of affectibility when discussing his childhood. 在谈论他的童年时，他表现出惊人的易感性。
sensitivity, responsiveness, impressionability
insensitivity, impassiveness
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin affectibilitas, from affectibilis (“liable to be affected”), from afficere (“to affect”), from ad + facere (“to do, to make”).
