marish:/ˈmærɪʃ/ /ˈmɛərɪʃ/ | adj. |沼泽的，湿地的|Resembling a marsh; marshy.  Characterized by marshes. |The air hung heavy and humid in the marish landscape. 空气沉滞潮湿，弥漫在沼泽地上。| The marish ground made it difficult to cross. 沼泽地使通行变得困难。 |近义词：marshy, swampy, boggy|反义词：dry, arid|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *marish*, from Old English *mersc* (marsh, swamp), from Proto-Germanic *merskaz* (marsh), from Proto-Indo-European *mer-/*mor- (sea, lake, standing water). Cognate with German *Marsch* (marsh), Dutch *moeras* (swamp).
