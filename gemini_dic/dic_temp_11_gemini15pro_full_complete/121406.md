misimagination:/ˌmɪsɪˌmædʒɪˈneɪʃən/
n.
错觉；误想；错误的想象
An inaccurate or distorted mental image or concept.
He was plagued by misimaginations of his own inadequacy. 他被自己能力不足的错觉所困扰。
近义词：delusion, illusion, misconception, fallacy
反义词：reality, truth, accuracy, perception
词性：名词
