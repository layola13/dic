ingots:/ˈɪŋɡəts/| n. |锭; 金属块|A mass of metal cast in a convenient shape for shaping, remelting, or refining. |The gold was melted down and poured into ingots.  黄金被熔化并倒入锭模。|
The company produces aluminum ingots. 这家公司生产铝锭。|
Synonyms: bars, blocks, lumps, slabs|
Antonyms: fragments, pieces, scraps|
