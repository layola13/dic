overgoverning:/ˌōvərˈɡʌvərniŋ/
adjective
过度管理的，管制过多的
governing too much; excessively controlling
The overgoverning regulations stifled innovation and growth. 过度管理的规章制度扼杀了创新和发展。
Overgoverning policies can lead to resentment and rebellion. 过度管理的政策会导致怨恨和反抗。
近义词: overregulating, micromanaging, overbearing
反义词: undergoverning, laissez-faire
词性: adjective

overgovern:/ˌōvərˈɡʌvərn/
verb
过度管理，管制过多
to govern too much; to control excessively
Some parents tend to overgovern their children's lives. 一些父母倾向于过度管理孩子的生活。
The new administration is accused of overgoverning the economy. 新政府被指责过度管理经济。
近义词: overregulate, micromanage
反义词: undergovern
词性: verb


词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive, too much") + "govern" (verb meaning "to rule, control, or direct").  The word "govern" comes from the Old French "governer," which in turn comes from the Latin "gubernare" meaning "to steer, guide, or direct."
