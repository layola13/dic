miscatalog:/ˌmɪsˈkætəlɒɡ/ (US) /ˌmɪsˈkætəlɔːɡ/ (UK)
verb
错误分类，编目错误
To incorrectly classify or categorize something, especially in a library or catalog.
The librarian miscataloged the new biography, placing it in the fiction section.  图书馆员把这本新的传记错误地分类到了小说区。
The museum miscataloged the ancient artifact, listing it as from a different era. 博物馆将这件古代文物错误地编目，将其列为来自不同时代。
misclassify, misfile, mislabel
correctly catalog, classify accurately
