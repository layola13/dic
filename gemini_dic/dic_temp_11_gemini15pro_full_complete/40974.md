cooptation:/ˌkoʊɒpˈteɪʃən/ |n.|收编，纳入，拉拢 |The cooptation of dissenting voices into the mainstream can neutralize opposition. 将异议纳入主流可以化解反对意见。|The cooptation of local elites by the colonial power ensured its control over the region. 殖民政权对当地精英的拉拢确保了其对该地区的控制。|近义词：absorption, assimilation, incorporation, appropriation|反义词：exclusion, rejection, ostracism|词性:名词

coopt:/koʊˈɑːpt/ |vt.|拉拢，吸纳 |The company coopted several community leaders onto its board of directors. 该公司吸纳了几位社区领导加入其董事会。|The rebel group attempted to coopt local villagers into their ranks. 叛军试图拉拢当地村民加入他们的队伍。|近义词：enlist, recruit, appropriate|反义词：reject, exclude|词性:动词
