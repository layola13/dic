fumblingness:/ˈfʌmblɪŋnəs/
n.
笨拙；笨手笨脚；
The quality of being clumsy and awkward in one's movements or actions.
His fumblingness with the controls caused the machine to malfunction. 他对控制装置的笨拙导致机器故障。
clumsiness, awkwardness, ineptness
dexterity, skillfulness, adroitness
