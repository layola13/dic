consoled:/kənˈsoʊld/
verb
安慰; 慰藉
past tense and past participle of console
例句：He consoled her after her father's death.  他安慰了痛失父亲的她。

例句：She was consoled by the support of her friends.  朋友们的支持让她感到安慰。

近义词：comforted, soothed, reassured
反义词：distressed, upset, saddened
词性：v.
