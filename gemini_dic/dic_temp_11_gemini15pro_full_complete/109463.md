lepidopterist:/ˌlɛpɪˈdɑːptərɪst/ | n. | 蝴蝶（蛾）类学家 | A person who studies or collects butterflies and moths. |  例句：The lepidopterist carefully pinned the newly caught butterfly to the display board. 蝴蝶（蛾）类学家小心翼翼地把新捕获的蝴蝶钉在展示板上。

例句：She became a renowned lepidopterist after years of studying these fascinating insects.  经过多年的研究，她成为了一位著名的蝴蝶（蛾）类学家。 | 近义词：butterfly collector, moth collector | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *lepidoptera* (plural), from *lepis* (scale) + *pteron* (wing).
