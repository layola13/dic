exploitable:/ɪkˈsplɔɪtəbl/| adj.|可利用的，可开发的，可剥削的|Able to be exploited.  Something that is exploitable can be unfairly used for someone's own advantage. It can also refer to a vulnerability or weakness that can be taken advantage of, often in a system or software.|例句：The company was accused of employing exploitable workers.  该公司被指控雇佣易受剥削的工人。

例句：The software had an exploitable security flaw.  该软件存在一个可被利用的安全漏洞。|近义词：vulnerable, susceptible, usable,  |反义词:resistant, secure, protected|词性:adjective
