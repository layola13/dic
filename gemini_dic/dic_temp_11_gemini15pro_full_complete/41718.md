corrobory:/kəˈrɒbəreɪt/ | v. | 证实，确证 | to confirm or give support to (a statement, theory, or finding) |  The witness corroborated the victim's account of the robbery.  证人证实了受害者对抢劫案的描述。

The forensic evidence corroborated the detective's suspicions. 法医证据证实了侦探的怀疑。 | 近义词: confirm, verify, substantiate, validate | 反义词: contradict, refute, deny | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin corroboratus, past participle of corroborare "to strengthen," from Latin com- "together" + roborare "to strengthen," from robur "strength, oak."
