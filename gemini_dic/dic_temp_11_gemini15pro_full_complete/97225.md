inflationary:/ɪnˈfleɪʃəneri/ | adj. | 通货膨胀的；导致通货膨胀的 | Relating to or causing inflation; tending to increase inflation. |  The government adopted inflationary policies. 政府采取了通货膨胀的政策。|  The inflationary spiral caused prices to skyrocket. 通货膨胀螺旋导致物价飞涨。| 近义词: inflated, expansionary | 反义词: deflationary | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Late 19th century: from inflation + -ary.
