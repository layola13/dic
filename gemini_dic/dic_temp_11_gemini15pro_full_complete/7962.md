antherless:/ˈænθərlɪs/
adj.
无花药的，没有花药的
Lacking anthers; without anthers.  Anthers are the pollen-bearing part of a stamen in a flower.
The antherless flowers were unable to produce pollen.  这些无花药的花朵无法产生花粉。
The botanist studied the antherless mutant strain.  植物学家研究了这种无花药的突变株。
近义词:asporogenous
反义词:antheriferous
词性:形容词
