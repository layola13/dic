caverns:/ˈkævərnz/ | n. |洞穴，大洞 |Large, underground caves, often formed by natural processes like the erosion of rock by water. |例句：The explorers discovered a network of caverns beneath the mountain. 探险家们在山下发现了一个洞穴网络。

例句：The caverns were filled with beautiful stalactites and stalagmites. 洞穴里充满了美丽的钟乳石和石笋。|近义词：caves, grottos, hollows |反义词: peaks, summits |词性:名词
