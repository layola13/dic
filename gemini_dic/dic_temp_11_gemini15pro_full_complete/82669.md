guessingly:/ˈɡesɪŋli/| adv.| 猜测地，臆测地 |In a guessing manner; by way of conjecture.  Making an estimate or forming a judgment without sufficient information to be sure of being correct.|He answered guessingly, unsure of the correct response. 他猜测地回答，不确定正确的答案。 |She guessingly picked a number between one and ten. 她随意猜了一个1到10之间的数字。|近义词：conjecturally, tentatively, speculatively |反义词：certainly, definitely, assuredly |词性: 副词
