excerptible:/ɪkˈsɜːrptɪbl/ | adj. | 可摘录的，可节选的 | Suitable for being excerpted; capable of being extracted or quoted. |  例句：This lengthy novel is not easily excerptible. 这部长篇小说不容易摘录。| 近义词：quotable, extractable | 反义词: unexcerptible | 词性: 形容词

excerptible:/ɪkˈsɜːrptɪbl/ | adj. | 可摘录的，可节选的 |  Suitable for being taken out of a larger work; capable of being separated or used independently. | 例句：The judge found several excerptible passages in the witness's testimony. 法官在证人证词中找到了几段可摘录的段落。| 近义词：citable | 反义词: whole, complete | 词性: 形容词
