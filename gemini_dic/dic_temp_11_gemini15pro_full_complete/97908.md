innitency:/ɪnˈnɪtənsi/
n.
开始；初期；开端
The state or quality of being innitent; beginning; commencement.
The innitency of the project was marked by enthusiasm and high hopes.  这个项目的初期充满了热情和很高的期望。
近义词：beginning, commencement, outset, inception
反义词：end, conclusion, termination
词性：名词

Etymology: From Late Latin *innitentia, from innitent- (“leaning upon, beginning”), present participle of inniti (“to lean upon, begin”), from in- (“in, on”) + niti (“to strive, lean”).
