McKinley: /məˈkɪnli/ | n. | 麦金利 |  A surname, also used as a given name, derived from the Irish Mac Fhionnlaigh, meaning "son of Findlaech."  It is most commonly associated with William McKinley, the 25th president of the United States. | Mount McKinley is the highest mountain in North America. 麦金利山是北美最高的山。|Synonyms: (for the mountain) Denali | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
