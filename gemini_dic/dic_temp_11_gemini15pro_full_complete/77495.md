gazet:/ɡəˈzet/| n.|报纸；公报；政府公告|A gazette is an official journal, a newspaper, or simply a printed account of current events.|The government gazette announced the new policy. 政府公报宣布了新政策。|Synonyms: newspaper, journal, periodical, bulletin|Antonyms: None (lacks a direct antonym)|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *gazette*, from Italian *gazeta*, possibly from the name of a small Venetian coin (*gazzetta*) which was the price of a newspaper, or from *gazza* "magpie," referring to the chatter of the marketplace where news was exchanged.
