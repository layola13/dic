megaderm:/ˈmɛɡədɜːrm/| n. |巨膜蝠|A bat belonging to the family Megadermatidae, characterized by large ears and a nose-leaf.|The megaderm swooped down and caught a moth mid-flight. 巨膜蝠俯冲下来，在半空中抓住了一只飞蛾。|近义词：false vampire bat, ghost bat|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑小型蝙蝠 small bat)|noun
