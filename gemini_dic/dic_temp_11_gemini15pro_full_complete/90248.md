housebound:/ˈhaʊsbaʊnd/| adj.|无法出门的，被限制在家中的|Unable to leave one's house, typically due to illness, disability, or old age.|She has been housebound since her accident. 她自从事故后就一直无法出门了。|近义词：homebound, confined, restricted|反义词:mobile, active|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From house + bound.  "Bound" in this sense means restricted or confined.
