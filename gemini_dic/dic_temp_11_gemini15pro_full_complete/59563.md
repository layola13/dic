ebb:/ɛb/
noun
退潮；衰退；减少
a gradual decline or decrease in something
The tide is on the ebb. 潮水正在退去。
decline, decrease, fall, waning
flow, rise, increase, surge
n.

ebb:/ɛb/
verb
退潮；衰退；减弱
(of tidewater) to recede from the flood; to fall away or decline
His enthusiasm began to ebb away. 他的热情开始消退。
recede, decline, decrease, wane
flow, rise, increase, surge
v.
