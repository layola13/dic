disparateness:/dɪˈspærɪtnəs/
n.
不同，差异，迥异
The state of being disparate; difference; dissimilarity.
The disparateness of their backgrounds made communication difficult.  他们背景的差异使得沟通变得困难。

The report highlighted the disparateness in educational opportunities across the country.  这份报告强调了全国各地教育机会的差距。

近义词: difference, dissimilarity, divergence, variation, variety, heterogeneity
反义词: similarity, likeness, resemblance, uniformity, homogeneity
词性: noun

Etymology: From disparate + -ness.  Disparate from Latin *disparātus, past participle of disparāre (“to separate”), from dis- (“apart”) + parāre (“to prepare”).
