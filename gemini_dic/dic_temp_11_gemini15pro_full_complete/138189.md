nosebag:/ˈnoʊzbæɡ/| n. |马的饲料袋；便携式午餐|A bag of oats, etc., fastened over a horse's nose for feeding. A packed lunch.|The farmer filled the nosebag with oats for his horse. 农民给他的马的饲料袋里装满了燕麦。|Synonyms: feed bag, fodder bag|Antonyms: None|Noun
