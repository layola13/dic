deservedness:/dɪˈzɜːrvɪdnəs/
n.
应得;  当之无愧
The quality of deserving something, especially something good.
His deservedness of the award was unquestionable. 他获得该奖项是当之无愧的。
merit, worthiness, justification
undeservedness, unworthiness
noun
