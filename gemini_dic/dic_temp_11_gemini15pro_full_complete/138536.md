noxious:/ˈnɑːkʃəs/ | adj. | 有害的；有毒的；不愉快的 | Harmful, poisonous, or very unpleasant. |  The factory emitted noxious fumes. 工厂排放出有毒气体。

The noxious smell of burning rubber filled the air. 燃烧橡胶的有害气味弥漫在空气中。

| 近义词：harmful, poisonous, toxic, detrimental, deleterious | 反义词：harmless, beneficial, healthy, salutary | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *noxius* "harmful, injurious," from *noxa* "harm, injury, damage."
