attritional:/əˈtrɪʃənəl/
adjective
消耗的，损耗的
Characterized by or causing attrition; gradual wearing down or weakening.
The attritional war had claimed thousands of lives on both sides.  这场消耗战双方都损失了数千条人命。
The company suffered attritional losses in its workforce due to low pay. 由于工资低，该公司员工流失严重。

近义词:  depleting, weakening, grinding, erosive
反义词:  strengthening, bolstering, augmenting
词性: adjective
