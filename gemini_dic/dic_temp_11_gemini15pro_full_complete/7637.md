annulet:/ˈæn.jʊ.lɪt/| n. |环状物，小环，指环(尤指建筑上的) |A small ring or band, especially one used in architecture as a decorative element. |The columns were adorned with a series of annulets.  这些柱子装饰着一系列的小环。|Synonyms: ringlet, circlet, band|Antonyms: N/A|noun

annulet:/ˈæn.jʊ.lɪt/| n. |年轮，(树木等的)一圈生长层 | A ring marking a year's growth, as of a tree. |The tree stump displayed prominent annulets, each representing a year of growth. 树桩上显示出突出的年轮，每一个都代表一年的生长。|Synonyms: growth ring, annual ring|Antonyms: N/A|noun
