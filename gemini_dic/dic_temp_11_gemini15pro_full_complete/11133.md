archebiosis:/ˌɑːrkiːbaɪˈoʊsɪs/| n. |自然发生; 偶发|The theory of archebiosis, suggesting that life arises from non-living matter, has been largely discredited. 自然发生理论，即认为生命起源于非生命物质的理论，很大程度上已被推翻。|Archebiosis was a popular belief before the experiments of Pasteur. 在巴斯德的实验之前，自然发生是一个普遍的信仰。| 近义词: spontaneous generation, abiogenesis | 反义词: biogenesis |词性:名词
