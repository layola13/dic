bechamel:/ˈbeɪʃəmel/ (US), /ˈbɛʃəmɛl/ (UK)| n. |白汁，奶油沙司|A rich white sauce made with butter, flour, and milk, often flavored with nutmeg, pepper, or other spices.|例句：She made a delicious bechamel sauce for the lasagna. 她为千层面做了一份美味的白汁沙司。

例句：The bechamel was perfectly smooth and creamy. 这白汁非常柔滑细腻。

近义词：white sauce, cream sauce|反义词: (No direct antonym for a food item)|词性:noun
