literariness:/ˈlɪtərɛrɪnɪs/| n.|文学性；书卷气|The quality of being literary; the characteristic or characteristics which make a work literary, such as heightened language, complex structure, attention to form, etc.  |Her prose has a certain literariness that sets it apart from other contemporary writers. 她的散文具有一种文学性，使其有别于其他当代作家。|Synonyms: artistry, refinement, sophistication|Antonyms: artlessness, simplicity, naturalness|noun
