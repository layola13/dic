entomical:/ˌɛntəˈmɒlɪkəl/ | adj. | 昆虫的；昆虫学的 | Relating to insects or the study of insects. |例句：He has an entomological collection. 他有一个昆虫收藏。|例句：She pursued an entomological career. 她从事昆虫学方面的工作。|近义词：insectile |反义词: N/A |词性: adjective
Etymology: From Late Latin *entomologicus, from Greek éntomon "insect" + -logos "treating of."
