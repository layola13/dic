coxswainship:/ˈkɒksɪnʃɪp (UK), ˈkɑːksɪnʃɪp (US)/
n.
舵手职位；舵手技能
The skill or position of a coxswain.
He demonstrated excellent coxswainship in navigating the difficult rapids. 他在 navigating the difficult rapids. 他在驾驭激流险滩方面展现了出色的舵手技能。
The coxswainship of the lifeboat requires strong leadership and quick thinking. 救生艇的舵手职位需要强有力的领导能力和快速思考能力。
synonym:  steering, pilotage, navigation
antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "lack of leadership" in the context of a team)
词性: noun
