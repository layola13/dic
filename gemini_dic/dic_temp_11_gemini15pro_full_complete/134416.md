noninvincible:/ˌnɒnɪnˈvɪnsəbl/
adjective
非无敌的，可战胜的
Not invincible; capable of being defeated or conquered.
The champion, despite his impressive record, proved noninvincible when he lost the final match.  这位冠军尽管战绩辉煌，但在决赛中失利，证明他并非不可战胜。
The Roman Empire, once seemingly invincible, ultimately proved noninvincible and crumbled.  曾经看似不可战胜的罗马帝国，最终证明并非如此，并走向崩溃。
near synonyms: vulnerable, conquerable, defeatable, surmountable
antonyms: invincible, unconquerable, unbeatable, insurmountable
词性: adjective
