词源: From Latin *malignant-, from malignāns, present participle of malignārī ‘act maliciously’, from male ‘badly’ + -gnāri (related to gnasci ‘be born’).

注意事项:  While "malignant" most commonly refers to cancerous growths, it can also be used figuratively to describe something harmful or malevolent.  区分其医学和比喻义用法很重要。
