disobligation:/ˌdɪsˌɑːblɪˈɡeɪʃən/
n.
解除义务；免除义务；没有义务
The state of being released from an obligation.
He felt a disobligation to attend the meeting after his boss insulted him.  老板侮辱他之后，他觉得没有义务参加会议了。
近义词:release, exemption, freedom
反义词:obligation, duty, responsibility
词性:名词

词源: dis- (not, opposite of) + obligation (duty, responsibility)  来自拉丁语 obligare, 意思是 "to bind."
