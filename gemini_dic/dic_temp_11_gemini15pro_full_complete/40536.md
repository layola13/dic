controversy:/ˈkɑːntrəvɜːrsi/ (US) /ˈkɒntrəvɜːsi/ (UK)| n. |争议，争论|a disagreement, often a public one, that involves strong opinions|The new law sparked a great deal of controversy.  这项新法律引发了巨大的争议。|synonyms: dispute, debate, argument, contention, disagreement|antonyms: agreement, accord, harmony, consensus|noun

controversial:/ˌkɑːntrəˈvɜːrʃl/ (US) /ˌkɒntrəˈvɜːʃl/ (UK)| adj. |有争议的，引起争论的|giving rise or likely to give rise to public disagreement|The controversial decision led to protests.  这个有争议的决定导致了抗议。|synonyms: disputed, debatable, contentious, provocative|antonyms: uncontroversial, undisputed, accepted|adjective
