dutcher:/ˈdʌtʃər/ | n. | 诱捕野鸭的人；捕鸭器 | A person who catches wild ducks; a duck trap. | He was a skilled dutcher, known for his intricate traps. 他是一位技艺娴熟的捕鸭人，以其复杂的陷阱而闻名。| The old dutcher showed the young boy how to set the trap. 老捕鸭人教小男孩如何设置陷阱。 | 近义词: duck hunter, fowler | 反义词: duck protector (意义相反，无确切对应) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From Dutch *duiker "diver", from *duken "to dive, duck".  Related to English "duck" (the verb). The -er suffix indicates a person who performs an action.
