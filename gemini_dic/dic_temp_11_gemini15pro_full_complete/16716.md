barrow:/ˈbæroʊ/ (US) /ˈbærəʊ/ (UK)| n.|手推车，独轮车；坟冢；小猪|A cart or vehicle with handles and typically one or two wheels, designed to be pushed by a single person.  Also, a mound of earth placed over a grave; a castrated male pig.|He used a barrow to carry the soil. 他用手推车运土。
The ancient barrow contained many artifacts.  这座古老的坟冢包含了许多文物。
The farmer sold a barrow at the market. 农民在市场上卖了一头小猪。|近义词：cart, wheelbarrow, mound, tumulus, hog|反义词: (No direct antonym)|名词


barrow:/ˈbæroʊ/ (US) /ˈbærəʊ/ (UK)| v.|用独轮车运|To convey or transport something in a barrow.|He barrowed the bricks to the construction site. 他用独轮车把砖块运到工地。|近义词：carry, transport, convey|反义词: (No direct antonym)|动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *bearwe (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *berwaz.  Related to Old High German bera, Old Norse bör.  The meaning "mound over a grave" developed later.
