needlestone:/ˈniːdlˌstoʊn/| n.|针石, 火山砾, 火山渣|A rock of volcanic origin, usually basaltic, characterized by a rough surface and numerous gas cavities. It is often used in landscaping.|The garden path was made of needlestone.|近义词：scoria, volcanic rock, lava rock|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps sedimentary rock)|noun
