co-optable:/koʊˈɒptəbl/ | adj. | 可吸收的，可拉拢的 | Able to be co-opted (especially into a group or organization). |  The committee is co-optable by outside interests. 外部利益集团可以拉拢该委员会。 | Synonyms:  appropriable,  enlistable,  inducible | Antonyms: unyielding, inflexible | adjective

co-optable:/koʊˈɒptəbl/ | adj. | 可被借用的，可挪用的 | Able to be co-opted (used for a different purpose than originally intended). |  The company's resources were co-optable for the new project. 公司的资源可以被挪用用于新项目。 | Synonyms: adaptable, usable,  appropriable | Antonyms: unusable, unsuitable | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From co-opt + -able. Co-opt comes from Latin co-optāre, from co- "together" + optāre "to choose, desire."
