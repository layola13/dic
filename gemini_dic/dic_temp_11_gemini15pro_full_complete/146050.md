overdrying:/ˌoʊvərˈdraɪɪŋ/
|n.|过度干燥|The overdrying of the skin can lead to irritation.  皮肤过度干燥会导致刺激。|
|v.|过度烘干|Be careful not to overdry the clothes, or they will shrink.  小心不要把衣服烘干过度，否则会缩水。|
|English Explanation:|The process of drying something excessively, beyond the desired or optimal point. This can apply to various materials, such as skin, hair, clothes, or food.|
|Synonyms:|desiccation, dehydration, parching|
|Antonyms:|moisturizing, hydrating, dampening|
|Word Origin:|From "over-" (excessively) + "drying" (present participle of "dry")|
