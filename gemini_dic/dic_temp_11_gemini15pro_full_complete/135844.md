nonpropriety:/ˌnɒnprəˈpraɪəti/ | n. | 不适当；不得体；失礼 |Lack of propriety; unseemly behavior or conduct. |Her nonpropriety at the funeral was shocking. 她在葬礼上的不得体行为令人震惊。| The blatant nonpropriety of his comments led to his dismissal. 他言论的公然失当导致他被解雇。| 近义词：impropriety, indecency, indecorum | 反义词：propriety, decency, decorum | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + propriety (from French propriété, from Latin proprietas "property, peculiarity," from proprius "one's own").  The sense shifted from "peculiarity" to "fitness, correctness" in English.
