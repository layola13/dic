iceman:/ˈaɪsmən/| n. |冰人；冷酷无情的人；卖冰的人|A person who sells ice or works with ice. Also, a person who is unemotional or callous. |例句：The Iceman cometh. 冰人来了。

例句：He's known as an iceman for his calm demeanor under pressure. 他以在压力下保持冷静的举止而闻名，被称为“冰人”。|近义词：glacier man, stoic, unfeeling person |反义词: warmhearted person, emotional person |词性: noun
