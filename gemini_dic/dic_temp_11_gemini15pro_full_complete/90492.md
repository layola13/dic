ETYMOLOGY->

From earlier *hurtleberry, probably from the Dutch *hurtleberry or a similar Low German word, of unknown origin.  The change from "hurtle" to "huckle" may be due to folk etymology associating it with "huckle," meaning "hip," perhaps because the berries grow low to the ground.
