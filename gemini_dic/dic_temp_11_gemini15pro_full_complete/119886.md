militarinesses:/ˌmɪlɪˈtɛrɪnɪsɪz/
n.
军事化，好战性，军事性质（通常用复数形式）
The militarinesses of the regime were evident in its suppression of dissent. 该政权的军事性质在其压制异议中显而易见。
The increasing militarinesses of the region are a cause for concern. 该地区日益增长的军事化令人担忧。
近义词: bellicosity, belligerence, warmongering
反义词: pacifism, peacefulness


词性: 名词 (plural noun)
英文解析:  A state or quality of being militaristic; a tendency towards military thinking or action.  Often used in the plural to denote multiple instances or aspects of this tendency.
中文翻译: 军事化，好战性，军事性质


词源: From "militariness" + "-es" (plural suffix). "Militariness" is derived from "military" + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality).  "Military" ultimately comes from Latin "militaris" (of soldiers, of warfare).
