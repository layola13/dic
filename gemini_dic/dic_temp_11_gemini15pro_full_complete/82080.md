grizzling:/ˈɡrɪzəlɪŋ/ | adj. | (尤指小孩)哭哭啼啼的，抱怨的 | Complaining in a whining or fretful way, especially of a child. | The baby has been grizzling all morning.  婴儿整个早上都在哭闹。|  The children were grizzling about having to go to bed early. 孩子们不停地抱怨要早睡。 | 近义词: whining, complaining, fretful, whimpering | 反义词: content, happy, cheerful | 词性: 形容词
grizzling:/ˈɡrɪzəlɪŋ/ | n. | 哭哭啼啼，抱怨 | The act of complaining in a whining or fretful way. | The constant grizzling of the children was driving me mad. 孩子们不停的抱怨让我快疯了。| There was a lot of grizzling about the new rules.  对于新规定有很多抱怨。| 近义词: whining, complaining, fretting | 反义词: contentment, happiness, cheerfulness | 词性: 名词
