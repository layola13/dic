gazetteer:/ˌɡæzɪˈtɪər/ | n. |地名词典；地名索引|A geographical dictionary or index; a book containing an alphabetical list of place names, with information about their location, population, etc. |例句：The researcher consulted a gazetteer to pinpoint the exact location of the ancient city. 研究人员查阅了地名词典以确定这座古城的准确位置。

例句：She used a gazetteer to find the latitude and longitude of the small island. 她使用地名词典查找小岛的经纬度。|近义词：atlas, geographical dictionary, directory |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
