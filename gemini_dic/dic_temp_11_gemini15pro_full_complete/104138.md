juncture:/ˈdʒʌŋktʃər/| n.|连接点；接合点；关头；时刻|A particular point in time or in a sequence of events. |例句：At this juncture, we must decide what to do next. 在这个关头，我们必须决定下一步该怎么做。

例句：The talks are at a critical juncture. 会谈正处于关键时刻。|近义词：point, moment, time, occasion, stage, crisis, crossroads|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

juncture:/ˈdʒʌŋktʃər/| n.|接缝；接合处|A place where things join. |例句：The juncture of the two rivers provides fertile land. 两条河流的交汇处提供了肥沃的土地。

例句：The wall was weak at the juncture of the two sections.  这堵墙在两段的接合处很脆弱。|近义词：joint, junction, connection, seam, intersection|反义词:division, separation|词性:noun
