braving:/ˈbreɪvɪŋ/| v.|勇敢面对，冒着(风险/危险)，经受(困难/逆境)|Facing or enduring something dangerous or difficult with courage.|例句：They are braving the storm to rescue the stranded sailors. 他们冒着暴风雨去营救被困的水手。|近义词：enduring, facing, withstanding, confronting|反义词：avoiding, shirking, fearing|词性:动词 (present participle of "brave")

词源：From the verb "brave," which comes from the Italian word "bravo" meaning "wild, courageous," ultimately from Latin "barbarus" meaning "foreign, barbarous."
