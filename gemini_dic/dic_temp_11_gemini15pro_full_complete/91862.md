hyperinflate:/ˌhaɪpərɪnˈfleɪt/| v. |过度膨胀；恶性通货膨胀|To inflate (currency, credit, etc) excessively, causing a steep and continuing rise in prices.|The government's policies hyperinflated the economy, leading to widespread poverty. 政府的政策导致经济恶性通货膨胀，造成普遍贫困。|Example:  The Weimar Republic experienced hyperinflation after World War I.  魏玛共和国在第一次世界大战后经历了恶性通货膨胀。|Synonyms: inflate excessively, overinflate|Antonyms: deflate, stabilize|verb
