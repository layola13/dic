onagraceous:/ˌoʊnəˈɡreɪʃəs/ |adj.|柳叶菜科的；属于柳叶菜科的|Relating to or denoting plants of the family Onagraceae, such as evening primroses and fuchsias.|The garden featured several onagraceous plants, including a vibrant fuchsia. 花园里种植了几种柳叶菜科植物，包括一株鲜艳的倒挂金钟。|Synonyms:  evening primrose family (informal)|Antonyms: N/A|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin *Onagraceae*, from *Onagra*, the former genus name of the evening primrose (now *Oenothera*),  + -aceae (family suffix).  The genus name *Onagra* is of uncertain origin, possibly from Greek *onos* (donkey) + *agra* (hunt).
