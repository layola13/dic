incorporeal:/ˌɪnkɔːrˈpɔːriəl/
adjective
无形的，非物质的，精神上的
Lacking material form or substance; spiritual.
例句：Ghosts are generally considered incorporeal beings.  鬼魂通常被认为是无形的生命。

例句：Love is an incorporeal concept, difficult to define but powerful nonetheless.  爱是一个无形的概念，难以定义但却很强大。

近义词：intangible, immaterial, spiritual, ethereal, ghostly
反义词：corporeal, physical, tangible, material, substantial
词性: adjective
