outstride:/ˌaʊtˈstraɪd/
verb
超越；步幅超过；胜过
to surpass in striding; to stride beyond; to excel
The marathon runner easily outstrode his competitors.  这位马拉松运动员轻松地超过了他的竞争对手。
He outstrode all his rivals in business. 他在生意场上胜过所有对手。
synonym: outstrip, surpass, exceed, outpace
antonym: lag behind, trail, fall behind
词性: 动词
