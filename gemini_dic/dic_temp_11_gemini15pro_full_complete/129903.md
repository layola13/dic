non-issue:/ˌnɒnˈɪʃuː/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈɪsjuː/ (UK)| n. |无关紧要的事情，非问题|Something that is not a problem or does not require discussion or action because it is unimportant or irrelevant.|The parking situation turned out to be a non-issue as there were plenty of spaces.|Synonyms: triviality, irrelevance, nothing, bagatelle|Antonyms: issue, problem, concern|noun
