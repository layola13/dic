disneyland:/ˈdɪzniˌlænd/ | n. |迪士尼乐园|An amusement park, based on the world created by Walt Disney, featuring rides, shows, and costumed characters.|We went to Disneyland for my birthday and had a magical time. 我们去迪士尼乐园庆祝我的生日，度过了一段神奇的时光。|近义词：theme park, amusement park|反义词: None|词性: noun

Etymology:  A blend of "Disney" (from Walt Disney, the creator) and "land".
