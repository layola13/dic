approximative: /əˈprɒksɪmətɪv/ /əˈprɑːksɪmətɪv/
adjective
近似的，大约的
Nearly correct or exact; not perfectly accurate or complete.
The approximative cost of the project is $1 million.  这个项目的大概费用是一百万美元。
The survey gives an approximative idea of public opinion. 这项调查大致反映了公众的意见。

近义词: approximate, rough, estimated, near
反义词: exact, precise, accurate

词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin approximativus, from Latin approximatus, past participle of approximare "to approach," from ad- "to" + proximare "to come near," from proximus "nearest."
