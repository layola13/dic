It is often subdivided into 1000 smaller units (like cents).|例句：The Kuwaiti dinar is one of the strongest currencies in the world. 科威特第纳尔是世界上最坚挺的货币之一。

例句：He exchanged his dollars for dinars at the airport. 他在机场把美元兑换成了第纳尔。|近义词：currency, money|反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of currency)|词性:noun
