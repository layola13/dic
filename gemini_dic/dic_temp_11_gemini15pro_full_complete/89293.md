homoeomery:/ˌhɒmiˈɒməri/ | n. | 同质性，均匀性 | The doctrine that all parts of the body are composed of similar elements.  The concept of homoeomery was central to Anaxagoras's cosmology. |  Anaxagoras believed in homoeomery, a theory stating that everything is made up of smaller versions of itself.  阿那克萨哥拉相信同质说，该理论认为一切事物都是由其自身更小的版本组成的。| synonyms: homogeneity, uniformity, similarity | antonyms: heterogeneity, diversity, variability | noun

homoeomere:/ˌhɒmiˈɒmɪə(r)/ | n. | 同质物，同类元素 |  A component particle in Anaxagoras's theory of homoeomery.  According to Anaxagoras, homoeomeres were the fundamental building blocks of matter. |  Anaxagoras believed that bread contained homoeomeres of flesh, hair, and bone.  阿那克萨哥拉认为面包中包含了肉、毛发和骨骼的同质物。| synonyms: element, component, particle | antonyms: N/A | noun
