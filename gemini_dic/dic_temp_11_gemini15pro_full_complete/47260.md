ddt:/ˌdiː diː ˈtiː/ /ˌdiː diː ˈtiː/
n.|滴滴涕 (双氯二苯三氯乙烷)|A colorless, crystalline, tasteless, and almost odorless organochlorine known for its insecticidal properties and environmental impacts. |DDT was widely used to control insects, but it was later banned in many countries due to its harmful effects on the environment.|近义词: insecticide, pesticide|反义词: None|词性: noun

ddt:/ˌdiː diː ˈtiː/ /ˌdiː diː ˈtiː/
