bivouacking:/ˈbɪvuækɪŋ/
n.|露营，野营|The act of staying overnight in a temporary camp without tents or cover, or with minimal shelter.|We went bivouacking in the mountains last weekend. 我们上周末去山上露营了。|camping, overnighting|settling down, staying indoors
词性:名词

bivouac:/ˈbɪvuæk/
n.|露营地，临时营地|A temporary camp without tents or cover, or with minimal shelter.|The soldiers made a bivouac in the woods. 士兵们在树林里扎了个临时营地。|camp, encampment|settlement, house
词性:名词

bivouac:/ˈbɪvuæk/
v.|露营，野营|To stay overnight in a temporary camp without tents or cover, or with minimal shelter.|We bivouacked under the stars. 我们在星空下露营。|camp, overnight|settle down, stay indoors
词性:动词

词源: French bivouac, from Swiss German Biwacht, from bi by + Wacht guard
