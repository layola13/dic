gomphiasis:/ɡɒmˈfaɪəsɪs/| n. |牙槽紧闭症，牙关紧闭|The condition of having teeth firmly fixed in their sockets.  This term is often used in the context of lockjaw, a symptom of tetanus. |The doctor diagnosed him with gomphiasis after observing his inability to open his mouth. 医生观察到他无法张开嘴后，诊断他患有牙槽紧闭症。|Near synonyms: trismus, lockjaw|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, consider using terms related to open mouth or loose teeth)|noun
