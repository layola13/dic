affrayer:/əˈfreɪər/ /æˈfreɪər/| v. | 恐吓，使惊恐 | To frighten or fill with terror; to dismay. | The sudden noise affrayed the horses. 突然的噪音吓坏了马匹。| 近义词: frighten, scare, terrify, alarm, intimidate | 反义词: calm, soothe, reassure | 词性: 动词

Etymology:
From Old French *esfreer, from es- (variant of ex- “out”) + freor (“to shiver, tremble”), from Frankish *friusōn, from Proto-Germanic *friusanan (“to freeze”).
