derestricts:/ˌdiːrɪˈstrɪkts/| v. |解除限制，撤销限制| To remove restrictions or limitations imposed on something or someone. | The government derestricted the sale of certain medications. 政府解除了某些药物的销售限制。

The company derestricted access to previously confidential documents. 公司解除了先前机密文件的访问限制。| synonyms: liberalize, deregulate, loosen, free, unfetter | antonyms: restrict, limit, confine, regulate, control | verb
