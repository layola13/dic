backlogged:/ˈbækˌlɔːɡd/ | adj. |积压的，处理不完的 | Having a large number of unfinished tasks or orders waiting to be processed.  A backlogged project or system is one that has a lot of work or requests waiting to be done. |  The customer service department is severely backlogged with complaints. 客服部门积压了大量的投诉。

The project is backlogged due to a lack of resources.  由于缺乏资源，该项目积压了。 |  overburdened, overwhelmed, behind schedule | up-to-date, current, on schedule | adjective
