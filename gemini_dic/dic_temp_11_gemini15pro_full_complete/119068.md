metoposcopical:/ˌmɛtəpɒˈskɒpɪkəl/
adjective
面相学的；根据面相判断的
Relating to metoposcopy, the study of the lines on the forehead as indicators of character or fate.
Metoposcopical analysis was once considered a legitimate method of character assessment.  面相分析曾经被认为是一种合理的性格评估方法。
The ancient Greeks believed in metoposcopical readings. 古希腊人相信面相解读。
近义词:  physiognomical, phrenological
反义词:  None
词性: adjective
