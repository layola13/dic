outexecuting:/ˌaʊtˈɛksɪˌkjuːtɪŋ/
| adj. | 胜过执行，在执行方面超越 | Surpassing or exceeding in execution; performing better than. This word is not commonly used and might be considered jargon in some contexts.  It describes outperforming someone or something in the act of carrying out a plan, task, or process. | The team was outexecuting their competitors in every aspect of the project.  该团队在项目的各个方面都胜过竞争对手。|  The new software is outexecuting the old one in terms of speed and efficiency. 新软件在速度和效率方面都优于旧软件。 | 近义词: outperforming, surpassing, exceeding | 反义词: underperforming, lagging | 词性:形容词
