advocacies:/ˈædvəkəsiːz/
n.
拥护；支持；辩护
The act of pleading for, supporting, or recommending; active espousal.
The lawyer presented strong advocacies for his client.  律师为他的客户提出了强有力的辩护。
近义词：support, defense, championship, promotion
反义词：opposition, resistance, antagonism
词性：名词

advocacy:/ˈædvəkəsi/
n.
拥护；支持；辩护
The act of pleading for, supporting, or recommending; active espousal.
Her advocacy for the homeless has made a real difference. 她对无家可归者的倡导产生了真正的影响。
近义词：support, defense, championship, promotion
反义词：opposition, resistance, antagonism
词性：名词

词源：Late Middle English: from Old French advocace, from avocat ‘advocate’
