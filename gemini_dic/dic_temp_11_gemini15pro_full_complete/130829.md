nonappeasing:/ˌnɒnəˈpiːzɪŋ/
adjective
不绥靖的，不妥协的
Not giving in to demands; uncompromising.
Example:  A nonappeasing stance against aggression.  对侵略采取不妥协的立场。

Example:  The government took a nonappeasing approach to the terrorists' demands. 政府对恐怖分子的要求采取了不妥协的态度。

近义词:uncompromising, resolute, firm, steadfast, unyielding
反义词:appeasing, conciliatory, accommodating, yielding

词源: "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "appeasing" (from "appease," from Old French "apaisier" meaning "to pacify").
