knits:/nɪts/
n.
针织物，针织衣物
knitted garments or fabrics.
She wore a colorful array of knits. 她穿着一系列色彩鲜艳的针织衣物。
近义词:sweaters, jerseys, pullovers
反义词:wovens

knits:/nɪts/
v.
(knit 的第三人称单数形式) 编织， вязать
creates a garment or fabric by intertwining yarn or thread.
She knits scarves for her family every Christmas. 她每年圣诞节都会为家人织围巾。
近义词:weaves, crochets
反义词:unravels

knits:/nɪts/
v.
(knit 的第三人称单数形式)  使紧密结合，使愈合
joins or unites closely.
The shared experience knit the group together. 共同的经历使团队更加紧密。
近义词:unites, binds, connects
反义词:separates
