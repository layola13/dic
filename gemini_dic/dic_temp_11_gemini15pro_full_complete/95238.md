incalculability:/ɪnˌkælkjʊləˈbɪləti/| n. |不可估量性，无法计算性|The incalculability of the risks involved made the investors hesitate.  涉及的风险无法估量，使得投资者犹豫不决。|The sheer incalculability of the universe makes it awe-inspiring. 宇宙的绝对不可估量性使其令人敬畏。|近义词：immeasurability, unpredictability, uncertainty|反义词：calculability, predictability, certainty|词性:noun

incalculable:/ɪnˈkælkjʊləbl/| adj. |不可计算的，无法估量的|The damage caused by the earthquake was incalculable. 地震造成的损失不可估量。|The benefits of a good education are incalculable. 良好教育的好处是不可估量的。|近义词：immeasurable, unpredictable, uncertain|反义词：calculable, predictable, certain|词性:adjective
