bilayer:/ˈbaɪˌleɪər/| n. |双层，双分子层|A thin membrane made of two layers, especially of lipids or phospholipids.|The cell membrane is a lipid bilayer. 细胞膜是一个脂质双分子层。|double layer| monolayer |noun
bilayer:|/ˈbaɪˌleɪər/| n. |双分子层|A double layer of molecules, typically lipids or phospholipids, forming a membrane.|The bilayer structure of the cell membrane allows it to control what enters and exits the cell. 细胞膜的双分子层结构使其能够控制物质进出细胞。|double layer| monolayer |noun
