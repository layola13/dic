hardscape:/ˈhɑːrdskeɪp/ | n. |硬质景观，硬景|Nonliving elements of landscaping, such as paved areas, retaining walls, fences, and decks.|例句：The hardscape of the garden included a brick patio and a stone walkway. 花园的硬景包括一个砖砌露台和一条石板走道。

例句：They invested heavily in hardscape, creating a beautiful and functional outdoor space. 他们在硬景方面投入了大量资金，创造了一个美观实用的户外空间。|近义词：paving, decking, masonry|反义词：softscape|词性：名词
