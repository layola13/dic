microfiched:/ˈmaɪkrəʊfɪʃt/ /ˈmaɪkroʊfɪʃt/ | v. | 把…制成缩微胶片；| to photograph a document, etc. and produce a microfiche of it |  The documents were microfiched for easy storage.  这些文件被制成缩微胶片以便于储存。| Synonyms: microfilm, miniaturize | Antonyms: enlarge, magnify | Verb

microfiche:/ˈmaɪkrəʊfiːʃ/ /ˈmaɪkroʊfiːʃ/ | n. | 缩微胶片；| a sheet of film containing microphotographs of the pages of a document, etc. |  The library has the newspaper on microfiche. 图书馆有这份报纸的缩微胶片。| Synonyms: microfilm | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "original document") | Noun
