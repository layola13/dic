overeffusiveness:/ˌoʊvərɪˈfjuːsɪvnəs/| n.|过分热情，过度热情|The overeffusiveness of the welcome made him feel uncomfortable.  过分热烈的欢迎让他感到不自在。| English Explanation: Excessive expression of warmth or enthusiasm.  It implies a display of emotion that is considered too strong, exaggerated, or insincere. |例句：Her overeffusiveness can be a bit overwhelming at times. 她有时过分的热情会让人有点招架不住。

例句：He apologized for his overeffusiveness, realizing he had embarrassed her. 他为自己过分的热情道歉，意识到自己让她尴尬了。|近义词：exuberance, effusiveness, gushiness|反义词：reserve, restraint, aloofness|词性: noun
