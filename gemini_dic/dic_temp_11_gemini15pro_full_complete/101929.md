islandlike:/ˈaɪləndˌlaɪk/
adjective
岛状的；像岛一样的
Resembling an island; isolated or detached.
The house stood on an islandlike hilltop, surrounded by fields.  这栋房子坐落在一个岛状的小山顶上，周围都是田野。
The community felt islandlike, cut off from the rest of the city. 这个社区感觉像个孤岛，与城市的其他部分隔绝。
近义词:insular, isolated, detached
反义词:connected, integrated, joined
词性:adjective

词源: island + like.  "Island" comes from Old English *iegland, meaning "island land."  "Like" comes from Old English *gelic, meaning "similar."
