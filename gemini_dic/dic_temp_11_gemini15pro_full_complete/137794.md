nonvenous:/nɒnˈviːnəs/
adjective
非静脉的
Not of, pertaining to, or derived from a vein.  Relating to or involving routes other than the veins.
Example: Nonvenous administration of medication, such as intramuscular injection.  药物的非静脉注射，例如肌肉注射。
Example: The doctor opted for a nonvenous route for the fluid administration. 医生选择了非静脉途径进行液体给药。
Synonym: extravenous
Antonym: venous
词性: 形容词
