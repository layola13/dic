chromoleucite:/ˈkroʊməˌluːsaɪt/| n. |色白榴石|A rare colorless to pale-yellow mineral of the feldspathoid group, essentially a potassium-rich variety of analcime.|Chromoleucite is a rare mineral found in volcanic rocks. 色白榴石是一种罕见的矿物，发现于火山岩中。|Chromoleucite crystals can exhibit a trapezohedral habit. 色白榴石晶体可以呈现梯方十二面体的习性。|近义词：analcime, leucite|反义词: None|词性: noun
