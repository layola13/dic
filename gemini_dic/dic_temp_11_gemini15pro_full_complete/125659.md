mump:/mʌmp/ | n. | 腮腺炎; (尤指儿童患的)流行性腮腺炎 | An infectious disease characterized by swelling of the salivary glands, especially the parotid glands. |  The child had the mumps and couldn't go to school.  这个孩子得了腮腺炎，不能上学。| synonyms: parotitis | antonyms: health | noun

mump:/mʌmp/ | v. | 绷着脸; 闷闷不乐 | To sulk; to be sullen. | He's been mumping around the house all day. 他整天都闷闷不乐地在家里转悠。 | synonyms: sulk, mope, pout | antonyms: rejoice, celebrate | verb
