charmer:/ˈtʃɑːrmər/ (US) /ˈtʃɑːmə(r)/ (UK)| n. |有魅力的人；迷人的人；妩媚的人|A person who charms; someone who has an attractive personality and uses it to influence others.|例句：He's a real charmer, isn't he? 他真是个有魅力的人，不是吗？

例句：She's a charmer, she can get anyone to do anything for her. 她很有魅力，能让任何人为她做任何事。

近义词：enchanter, fascinator, captivating person, charmer, heartthrob, enchantress (female) |反义词：bore, repellent, unpleasant person|词性：名词

charmers:/ˈtʃɑːrmərz/ (US) /ˈtʃɑːməz/ (UK)| n. |有魅力的人们；迷人的人们；妩媚的人们|Plural form of "charmer," referring to a group of people who charm.|例句：The charmers were all gathered around the queen.  那些迷人的人们都聚集在女王周围。

例句：The party was full of charmers and social butterflies.  派对上充满了有魅力的人和社交高手。
