lumbering's:/ˈlʌmbərɪŋz/
noun
笨拙的动作，缓慢的移动
The act or process of moving in a slow, heavy, and awkward way.
The elephant's lumbering's through the forest broke several small trees.  大象笨拙地穿过森林，撞断了几棵小树。
近义词:clumsiness, awkwardness, heaviness
反义词: agility, grace, swiftness
词性: 名词 (n.)

lumbering:/ˈlʌmbərɪŋ/
adjective
笨拙的，缓慢的
Moving in a slow, heavy, and awkward way.
The lumbering bear made its way across the stream.  笨拙的熊缓缓地穿过了小溪。
近义词: clumsy, awkward, ponderous
反义词: agile, graceful, nimble
词性: 形容词 (adj.)


词源：
From the verb "lumber" (to move clumsily), which comes from the Middle English "lomeren," probably of imitative origin.
