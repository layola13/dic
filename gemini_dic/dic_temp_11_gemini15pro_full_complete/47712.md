decacuminated:/diːkəˈkjuːmɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
削尖的；突然变细的
Having a very pointed apex; tapering to a slender point.
The decacuminated spire of the church was a prominent feature on the skyline. 教堂削尖的尖顶是天际线上一个显著的特征。
The artist meticulously rendered the decacuminated leaves of the exotic plant.  这位艺术家 meticulously 地描绘了这株奇异植物的尖叶。
pointed, sharp, acuminate, acute, cuspate
blunt, rounded, obtuse
