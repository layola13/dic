legislatorships:/ˈledʒɪsleɪtərʃɪps/
n.
立法机构(通常指集体);立法权
The collective body of legislators. The position or office of a legislator. The term or period during which a legislature holds power.
The state legislatorships are considering new gun control laws. 各州立法机构正在考虑新的枪支管制法。
synonyms: legislature, legislative body, assembly, parliament, congress, senate, house of representatives
antonyms: executive, judiciary
