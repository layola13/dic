misprints:/ˈmɪsprɪnts/ | n. | 印刷错误；印错 | An error in printing; a typographical error. | The book was full of misprints. 这本书里到处都是印刷错误。 |  The newspaper had to issue a correction for several misprints in yesterday's edition.  这家报纸不得不为昨天版本中的几个印刷错误发布更正。 | synonyms: typo(s), erratum, misprint(singular), error(s) | antonyms:  correct print, accurate reproduction | noun
