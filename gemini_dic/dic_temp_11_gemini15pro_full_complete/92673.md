ice-bound:/ˈaɪsˌbaʊnd/
adjective
冰封的，被冰困住的
covered and obstructed by ice; unable to move because surrounded by ice
The ship was ice-bound in the Arctic Ocean. 这艘船被困在北冰洋的冰层中。

The ice-bound harbor prevented ships from entering or leaving. 冰封的港口阻止了船只进出。

近义词: frozen, iced-in, ice-locked
反义词: ice-free, thawed
词性: adjective
