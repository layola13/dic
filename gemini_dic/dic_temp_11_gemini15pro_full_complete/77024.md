gargol:/ˈɡɑːrɡɔl/| n.|石像鬼，滴水嘴兽|A grotesque carved human or animal figure, especially one used as a spout to carry water clear of a wall.|The cathedral is decorated with many gargoyles.  这座大教堂装饰着许多石像鬼。|Example: Rainwater poured from the gargoyle's mouth. 雨水从石像鬼的嘴里倾泻而出。|Synonyms: grotesque, chimera|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

gargol:/ˈɡɑːrɡɔl/| n.|（加拿大西部）一种藻类球|A spherical colony of blue-green algae found in alkaline lakes, particularly in western Canada.|Gargols are a common sight in some Canadian lakes.  在一些加拿大湖泊中，藻类球很常见。|Example:  The lake was dotted with numerous gargols. 湖里点缀着无数的藻类球。|Synonyms: algal ball, water ball|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
