hide:/haɪd/| v.  n.|隐藏，躲藏；兽皮|To put or keep out of sight; conceal.|He hid the money under the mattress. 他把钱藏在床垫下.|近义词：conceal, cover, mask, secrete|反义词:reveal, expose, show, uncover|verb

hide:/haɪd/| n.|兽皮|The skin of an animal, especially a large one, when it has been removed from the carcass.|The hunter traded hides for supplies. 猎人用兽皮交换物资.|近义词：skin, pelt, fur|反义词: (none specific)|noun
