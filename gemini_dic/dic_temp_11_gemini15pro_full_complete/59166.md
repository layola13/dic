dysenteric:/ˌdɪsənˈtɛrɪk/ | adj. | 痢疾的；患痢疾的 | Relating to or suffering from dysentery. |  He suffered from dysenteric symptoms, including severe diarrhea. 他患有痢疾症状，包括严重的腹泻。 |  The dysenteric outbreak spread quickly through the refugee camp.  痢疾疫情在难民营迅速蔓延。 | 近义词: none (dysenteric is quite specific) | 反义词: healthy | 词性: adjective

dysentery:/ˈdɪsəntri/ | n. | 痢疾 | An infection of the intestines marked by severe diarrhea with the presence of blood and mucus in the feces. |  Dysentery is a serious health concern in many developing countries.  痢疾是许多发展中国家的一个严重的健康问题。 |  The doctor diagnosed him with amoebic dysentery. 医生诊断他患有阿米巴痢疾。 | 近义词: the flux, bloody flux | 反义词: none | 词性: noun

词源: From Late Latin *dysenteria, from Greek dusenteria, from dus- + enteron 'intestine'.
