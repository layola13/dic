mugweed:/ˈmʌɡwiːd/| n.|傻瓜，笨蛋|A foolish or gullible person.  A derogatory term.|He called his competitor a mugweed after he fell for his obvious trick. 他在竞争对手上了他的当后，称其为傻瓜。|Synonyms: idiot, fool, dolt, simpleton, nitwit, dimwit, dunce|Antonyms: genius, intellectual, savant|noun
