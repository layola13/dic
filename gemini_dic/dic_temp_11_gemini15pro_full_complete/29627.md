catenary: /ˈkætənɛri/ (US), /ˈkætɪnəri/ (UK) | n. | 悬链线 |  The curve formed by a flexible cable or chain hanging freely from two fixed points.  |  Example 1: The power lines formed a perfect catenary between the towers. 电线在塔之间形成了一条完美的悬链线。

Example 2:  The architect used the principle of the catenary to design the bridge's cables.  建筑师利用悬链线的原理设计了桥梁的缆索。 |  Synonyms:  curve, arc | Antonyms:  straight line | noun


catenary: /ˈkætənɛri/ (US), /ˈkætɪnəri/ (UK) | adj. | 悬链线的 | Of or relating to a catenary curve. | Example 1: The catenary arch is known for its strength and stability. 悬链拱以其强度和稳定性而闻名。

Example 2: He calculated the catenary forces acting on the suspension bridge. 他计算了作用在悬索桥上的悬链力。 | Synonyms:  catenarian,  | Antonyms: linear, straight | adjective
