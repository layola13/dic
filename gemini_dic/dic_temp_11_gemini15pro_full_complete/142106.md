oriented:/ˈɔːriːentɪd/ |adj.|导向的，定向的，以...为方向的| adjusted or aligned in relation to a specific point or direction; directed towards a particular goal or purpose. |例句：The company is market-oriented. 这家公司是以市场为导向的。|例句：She is very career-oriented. 她非常注重事业。|近义词：directed, focused, aligned, geared |反义词:undirected, aimless| adjective

oriented:/ˈɔːriːentɪd/ |adj.|适应的，熟悉情况的|made familiar with or adjusted to a new situation or set of circumstances.| 例句：The new employees are undergoing an orientation program. 新员工正在进行入职培训。 |例句：He felt disoriented after the long flight. 长途飞行后，他感到迷失方向。 |近义词：acclimated, adjusted, familiarized |反义词:disoriented, lost| adjective
