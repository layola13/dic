electro-slag:/ɪˈlɛktroʊ slæɡ/ | adj. n.| 电渣的，电渣；电渣焊|A type of welding that uses the heat generated by an electric arc passing through a layer of molten slag to melt and fuse metal.| Electro-slag welding is often used for thick sections of steel. 电渣焊常用于厚钢截面。|近义词:consumable guide electroslag, electroslag remelting|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider other welding processes like MIG, TIG)|词性: adjective, noun

electro-slag:/ɪˈlɛktroʊ slæɡ/ | n.| 电渣|The molten slag used in electro-slag welding.| The electro-slag helps to protect the weld from atmospheric contamination. 电渣有助于保护焊缝免受大气污染。|近义词: slag|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性: noun
