asterolepis:/ˈæstərəʊlɛpɪs/| n. |星鳞鱼|An extinct genus of placoderm fish, known from fossils of the Devonian period.  Characterized by large bony plates covering the head and front part of the body.|Asterolepis fossils were discovered in Scotland.  星鳞鱼化石在苏格兰被发现。|近义词:placoderm (泛指盾皮鱼)|反义词:  (无，因为是已灭绝生物，没有直接的反义词)|词性:noun

Etymology: From Greek *aster* (star) + *lepis* (scale)
