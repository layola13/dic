bettong:/ˈbetɒŋ/ (UK) /ˈbetɑːŋ/ (US)| n. | 袋鼠鼠 | A small, hopping marsupial native to Australia, belonging to the family Potoroidae. |  Bettongs are nocturnal and feed on fungi, roots, and tubers. 袋鼠鼠是夜行动物，以真菌、根茎和块茎为食。|  The brush-tailed bettong is an endangered species.  帚尾袋鼠鼠是一种濒危物种。 | synonyms: potoroo, rat-kangaroo | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for an animal name) | noun
