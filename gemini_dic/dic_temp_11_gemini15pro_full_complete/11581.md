arithmetician:/ˌærɪθməˈtɪʃən/ | n. |算术家；精于计算的人|A person skilled in arithmetic; one who calculates or computes.|例句：The arithmetician quickly calculated the sum of the long series of numbers.  这位算术家很快地计算出了这一长串数字的总和。

例句：He was a renowned arithmetician, able to perform complex calculations in his head. 他是一位著名的算术家，能够在脑中进行复杂的计算。|近义词：calculator, computer, mathematician|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "innumerate person") |词性:noun
