geomantically:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈmæntikli/| adv.|地理风水地；根据风水|In a way that relates to geomancy (divination by geographic features).  根据地理特征占卜的方式。|The house was geomantically situated to maximize good fortune. 这座房子在地理风水上是为了最大限度地获得好运而建造的。|近义词：divinatory,  auspicious,  propitious|反义词：inauspicious, unpropitious|词性：副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "geomantic" + "-ly". "Geomantic" comes from  "geomancy," from Old French "geomance," from Medieval Latin "geōmantia," from Greek "geōmanteía," from "gē" (earth) + "manteía" (divination).  源自“geomantic”+“-ly”。“Geomantic”来自“geomancy”，来自古法语“geomance”，来自中世纪拉丁语“geōmantia”，来自希腊语“geōmanteía”，来自“gē”（地球）+“manteía”（占卜）。
