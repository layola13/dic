jockeyish:/ˈdʒɒkiɪʃ/| adj.|骑师似的，骑师风格的，争强好胜的|Resembling or characteristic of a jockey, especially in being small, wiry, and competitive.  It can also suggest a tendency to be shrewd or manipulative.|He had a jockeyish build and a competitive spirit. 他身材精瘦，争强好胜，就像个骑师。|近义词：competitive, aggressive, shrewd, astute, wily|反义词：cooperative, passive, yielding|词性:形容词

词源:
From jockey + -ish.  The suffix "-ish" transforms a noun into an adjective, indicating resemblance or characteristic of.
