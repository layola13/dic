half-informing:/ˌhæfɪnˈfɔːrmɪŋ/
| adj. | 半知半解的；提供不完整信息的 | Providing information that is incomplete or only partially helpful. | The article was half-informing, leaving the reader with more questions than answers. 这篇文章信息不完整，让读者产生了更多疑问而不是答案。|  The briefing from the manager was half-informing and ultimately unhelpful.  经理的简报信息不全，最终没有帮助。 | misleading, incomplete, vague, sketchy | comprehensive, thorough, detailed, informative | adjective
