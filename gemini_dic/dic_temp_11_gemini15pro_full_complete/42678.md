countersued:/ˌkaʊntərˈsuːd/
verb
反诉
to sue someone who has already sued you
例句：The company countersued, claiming breach of contract.  该公司提起反诉，声称对方违反合同。

例句：After being sued for damages, the defendant countersued. 在被起诉赔偿损失后，被告提起了反诉。

近义词：reconvene, file a countersuit
反义词：N/A
词性：v.
