arret:/əˈret/ |n.| (法律) 判决，裁定；(尤指官方的) 命令，法令 | A judicial decision or decree; especially : an order or decree of an administrative officer or agency. | The court issued an arret ordering the company to cease operations. 法院发布了一项命令，责令该公司停止运营. | Example 2: The arret of the mayor prohibited public gatherings in the park. 市长的法令禁止在公园里举行公众集会. | synonym: decree, order, judgment, ruling | antonym:  appeal (in the sense of challenging a decision), revocation | noun
