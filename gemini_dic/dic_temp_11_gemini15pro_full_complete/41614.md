corradiation:/kɔːˌreɪdiˈeɪʃən/ | n. |共同辐射，协同照射|The act or process of irradiating something together or simultaneously.  The combined effect of multiple sources of radiation.|例句：The corradiation of the tumor with multiple beams increased the effectiveness of the treatment.  多个射束共同照射肿瘤增加了治疗效果。

例句：The researchers studied the corradiation of the sample with both UV light and X-rays. 研究人员研究了样品同时接受紫外线和X射线的照射。|近义词：co-irradiation, combined irradiation|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly shielding, protection from radiation)|词性: noun
