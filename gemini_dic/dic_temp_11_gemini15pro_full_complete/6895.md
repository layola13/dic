anchoretic: /ˌæŋkəˈrɛtɪk/ | adj. |隐居的；遁世的；禁欲的 |Characterized by seclusion and religious devotion. |The anchoretic life appealed to her. 隐居生活吸引了她。|近义词: secluded, solitary, ascetic, hermitic |反义词: worldly, sociable |词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *anchorēta, from Greek anakhōrētēs (“one who has retired, recluse”), from anakhōreō (“I retire, withdraw”), from ana- (“back, up”) + khōreō (“I give way, depart”).
