craftiness:/ˈkræfːtɪnəs/| n.|狡猾，诡诈，灵巧|The fox's craftiness allowed it to steal the chickens. 狐狸的狡猾使它偷走了鸡。|例句：He achieved his goal through sheer craftiness. 他完全靠狡猾实现了目标。

例句：The craftiness of the plan impressed everyone. 这个计划的精妙之处给每个人都留下了深刻印象。

近义词：cunning, guile, slyness, artfulness, wiliness|反义词：honesty, forthrightness, naiveté, artlessness, ingenuousness|词性:名词

词源: From crafty + -ness.  "Crafty" comes from Old English *cræftig (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *kraftigaz ("strong, powerful"), from *kraft- ("strength, power").  The meaning shifted from "powerful" to "skillful" and then to "skillful in deception."
