gawper:/ˈɡɔːpə(r)/ | n. | 傻看着的人，盯着看的人 | Someone who stares rudely or stupidly. | Example: The gawpers blocked the sidewalk, staring at the accident. 那些围观者挡住了人行道，盯着事故现场看。

Example:  He felt like a gawper, staring at the celebrity in the restaurant. 他觉得自己像个傻瓜，盯着餐厅里的名人看。| 近义词: onlooker, observer, spectator, starer | 反义词:  None (lack of a direct antonym for someone who stares) | 词性: noun

gawper:/ˈɡɔːpər/ | n. | 呆子，笨蛋 | A foolish or stupid person. (less common usage) | Example:  Don't be a gawper, pay attention to what you're doing!  别犯傻了，注意你在做什么！

Example: He called him a gawper for making such a silly mistake. 他叫他呆子，因为他犯了这么愚蠢的错误。 | 近义词: idiot, fool, dolt | 反义词: genius, intellectual | 词性: noun
