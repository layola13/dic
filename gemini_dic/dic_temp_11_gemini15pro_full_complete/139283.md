obliterating: /əˈblɪtəreɪtɪŋ/
verb
消灭，抹去
removing or destroying all traces of; destroying utterly
The bombing raid obliterated the small village. 轰炸袭击彻底摧毁了这个小村庄。
synonyms: destroying, eradicating, annihilating, wiping out, expunging
antonyms: creating, building, establishing, preserving, restoring
