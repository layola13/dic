bipinnaria: /ˌbaɪpɪˈnɛəriə/ /ˌbaɪpɪˈneriə/ | n. | 羽腕幼虫 | The larval form of many starfish, characterized by bilateral symmetry and possessing two bands of cilia. |  Bipinnaria larvae drift in the ocean currents. 羽腕幼虫漂浮在洋流中。| The bipinnaria larva eventually metamorphoses into the adult starfish. 羽腕幼虫最终会变态成成年的海星。 |  brachiolaria (another starfish larval stage) |  (no direct antonym, could consider adult starfish) | noun

Etymology: From Latin *bi- (two) + pinna (feather, fin) + -aria (pertaining to).
