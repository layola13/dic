niqab:/nɪˈkɑːb/ | n. | 尼卡布；面纱 | A veil worn by some Muslim women that covers the entire face except for the eyes. |  She chose to wear a niqab as an expression of her faith. 她选择佩戴尼卡布以表达自己的信仰。

The niqab is a common sight in some parts of the world. 在世界某些地区，尼卡布很常见。 | 近义词：veil, face covering | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider: uncovered face) | 词性: noun
