globed:/ɡloʊbd/ | adj. | 球形的，球状的 | Having the shape of a globe; spherical. |  The full, globed moon shone brightly in the night sky.  满月在夜空中闪耀着光芒。 | 近义词: spherical, round, globular | 反义词: flat, planar | 词性:形容词
词源: From globe + -ed.  From Latin globus “sphere, ball”.
