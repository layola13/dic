over-aptnesses:/ˈoʊvərˈæptnəsəz/
|n.| 过分贴切；过度恰当|The over-aptnesses of his metaphors sometimes made his writing feel contrived.  他隐喻的过度贴切有时使他的作品感觉不自然。|例句：The speaker's over-aptnesses in quoting scripture alienated some of the audience. 演讲者过度引用经文让一些听众感到疏远。

例句：Her over-aptnesses in choosing examples sometimes obscured the main point. 她选择例子时过分贴切，有时反而模糊了重点。|近义词：appropriateness, suitability, fitness (when used excessively)|反义词：inappropriateness, unsuitability, awkwardness|词性: noun (plural)

Etymology:
From "over-" (prefix indicating excess) + "aptness" (noun from "apt" – suitable, fitting) + "-es" (plural suffix).  "Apt" comes from the Latin "aptus" (fit, suitable).
