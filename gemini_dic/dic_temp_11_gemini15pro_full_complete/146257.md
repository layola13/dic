overextreme: /ˌoʊvərɪkˈstriːm/
adjective
过度的；极端的
Going to such overextreme lengths is unnecessary.  采取这种过度极端的措施是没有必要的。
His reaction was overextreme. 他的反应过于极端。
English Explanation: Exceeding reasonable or normal limits; excessively extreme.  It combines "over," meaning excessively, and "extreme," meaning reaching the furthest or highest degree.
Example: Her diet was overextreme, cutting out entire food groups. 她的饮食过于极端，完全去掉了某些食物种类。
Synonyms: excessive, immoderate, radical, fanatical, inordinate, extravagant
Antonyms: moderate, reasonable, temperate, balanced
词性: 形容词
