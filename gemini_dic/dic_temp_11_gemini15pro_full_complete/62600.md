enfester:/ɛnˈfɛstər/
verb
使充满臭气；使腐败；使感染；使恶化
to make foul or infected; to corrupt
The overflowing garbage enfestered the alleyway.  堆积如山的垃圾使小巷臭气熏天。
The wound, left untreated, began to enfester.  伤口未经处理，开始化脓感染。
近义词:infect, pollute, contaminate, taint, corrupt
反义词:purify, cleanse, disinfect, sanitize
词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From en- (cause to be in a state) + fester (to form pus; to rot).  Ultimately from Latin *fester, related to fistulāre "to become a pipe or ulcer."
