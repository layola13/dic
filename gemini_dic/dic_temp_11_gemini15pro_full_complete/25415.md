bulimics:/buːˈlɪmɪks/
n.
暴食症患者；贪食者
People with bulimia, bulimics, often feel ashamed of their behavior.
患有贪食症的人，也就是暴食症患者，常常为自己的行为感到羞耻。
Synonyms: binge eaters, compulsive eaters
Antonyms: anorexics
noun
