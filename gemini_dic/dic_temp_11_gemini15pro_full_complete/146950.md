overinvested:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈvɛstɪd/ | adj. | 过度投资的；投入过多的 | Having invested too much (time, energy, or emotion) in something. | He was overinvested in the relationship and was devastated when it ended. 他对这段感情投入太多，当它结束时他悲痛欲绝。|  She was so overinvested in her career that she neglected her family. 她对事业投入过多，以至于忽略了家庭。 | 近义词: excessively invested, too involved | 反义词: uninvested, detached | 词性: adjective
