argumentations:/ˌɑːrɡjəmɛnˈteɪʃənz/
n.
论证；辩论；争论
The process of reasoning systematically in support of an idea, action, or theory.  A reasoned, logical way of explaining or justifying something, often involving the use of evidence and examples.
The lawyer presented strong argumentations in defense of her client. 律师为她的客户提出了强有力的论证。
Synonyms: reasoning, arguments, debate, discussion, discourse
Antonyms: agreement, compromise, consensus
