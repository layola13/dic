littlenesses:/ˈlɪtəlˌnɪsɪz/
n.
渺小；琐碎；狭隘；小气
The state or quality of being petty or mean.  The state of lacking generosity or nobility of mind. The state or quality of being trivial or insignificant.
Her littlenesses of character made her unpopular. 她的狭隘性格让她不受欢迎。

反义词：generosity, magnanimity, greatness, importance
