fasciitis:/ˌfæʃiˈaɪtɪs/| n. |筋膜炎|Inflammation of the fascia, a band or sheet of connective tissue primarily collagen beneath the skin that attaches, stabilizes, encloses, and separates muscles and other internal organs.|Plantar fasciitis is a common cause of heel pain. 足底筋膜炎是足跟痛的常见原因。|Near synonyms: myofasciitis, tenosynovitis|Antonyms: None (Antonyms for medical conditions are generally not applicable)|Noun
