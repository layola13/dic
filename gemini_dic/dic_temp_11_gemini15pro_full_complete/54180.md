disert:/dɪˈsɜːrt/ | v. |  论述；讲述；讨论 (正式) | To speak or write about a subject formally and at length. | He diserted eloquently on the topic of artificial intelligence. 他雄辩地论述了人工智能的话题。|  He diserted upon the history of the Roman Empire. 他讲述了罗马帝国的历史。 | Synonyms: discuss, expound, discourse | Antonyms: summarize, condense | verb

dissert:/dɪˈsɜːrt/ | v. | (古) 遗弃；抛弃 | To abandon or desert. (archaic) | He diserted his family and fled the country. 他抛弃了家人，逃离了这个国家。| She diserted her post in the face of danger.  她在危险面前放弃了她的职位。| Synonyms: abandon, desert, forsake | Antonyms: support, maintain, stay | verb
