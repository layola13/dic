homomorphic:/ˌhɒməˈmɔːfɪk/ | adj. | 同态的 |  Structurally similar, especially of mathematical groups or other algebraic systems, such that an operation on one system can be mapped to a corresponding operation on the other.  |  The two graphs are homomorphic. 这两个图是同态的。 |  isomorphic, analogous |  heteromorphic | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *homo- (same) + *morphē (form)
