adore:/əˈdɔːr/(US), /əˈdɔː(r)/(UK)| v. |崇拜；爱慕；喜爱|To love and respect someone deeply, or to like something very much.|She adores her grandchildren. 她非常疼爱她的孙子孙女们。|
例句：I absolutely adore this city. 我非常喜欢这座城市。
例句：He adores his wife and children. 他深爱他的妻子和孩子。
近义词：love, cherish, idolize, worship|反义词：hate, despise, loathe|词性：动词
