outwaited:/ˌaʊtˈweɪtɪd/
verb
以等待更久胜过；比…更能等待
waited out someone or something longer than they could wait.
He outwaited his rivals and eventually won the contract. 他比他的竞争对手等待的时间更长，最终赢得了合同。
outlast, outstay
-
verb
比…活得长
lived longer than.
She outwaited her husband by ten years. 她比她丈夫多活了十年。
