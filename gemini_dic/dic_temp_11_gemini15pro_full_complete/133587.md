词源:
"Non-" is a Latin prefix meaning "not."
"Flatulent" comes from the Latin "flatus," meaning "a blowing, blast of wind."  Therefore, "nonflatulent" means "not producing or releasing gas."  The "-ly" suffix turns the adjective into an adverb.
