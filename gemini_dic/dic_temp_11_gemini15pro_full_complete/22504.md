boomed:/buːmd/
verb
发出低沉的隆隆声；迅速发展；（生意）兴隆；大声说
to make a deep, resonant sound; to experience a period of rapid economic growth; to say something in a loud voice
The cannons boomed across the battlefield.  大炮在战场上隆隆作响。

The business boomed during the holidays.  节日期间生意兴隆。

"Get out!" he boomed.  “滚出去！”他大声吼道。
近义词: roared, thundered, flourished, prospered, escalated
反义词: whispered, declined, failed, contracted

词性:v.
