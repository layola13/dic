decimal:/ˈdɛsɪməl/ |adj. n.|小数的，十进制的；小数| relating to or denoting a system of numbers or numerals based on units of ten.|例句：The decimal system is widely used in mathematics. 十进制系统在数学中被广泛使用。

decimal:/ˈdɛsɪməl/ |adj. n.|小数的，十进制的；小数|expressed as a fraction of 100; based on the number 10.| 例句：0.3 is a decimal fraction. 0.3是一个小数。|近义词：fractional|反义词：whole, integer|词性:adjective, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *decimalle*, from Medieval Latin *dēcimālis* (“pertaining to tenths”), from Latin *decimus* (“tenth”), from *decem* (“ten”).
