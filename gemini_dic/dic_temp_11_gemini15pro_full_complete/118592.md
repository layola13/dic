mesozoa:/ˌmɛzəˈzəʊə/ | n. | 中生动物 | A small phylum of multicellular parasitic animals, generally considered to be intermediate between protozoa and metazoa.  They are typically found in marine invertebrates. | Mesozoa are parasites of marine invertebrates. 中生动物是海洋无脊椎动物的寄生虫。|  Mesozoans have a simplified body structure. 中生动物具有简化的身体结构。 | synonym: none (due to their unique phylogenetic position) | antonym: none (due to their unique phylogenetic position) | noun
