bandied:/ˈbændid/ | v. |  轻率地传播；散布；争论 | to discuss or mention something frequently, especially without careful thought or regard for the truth |  The accusations were bandied about in the press. 这些指控在媒体上被广泛传播。| synonyms: circulate, spread, toss around, exchange,  | antonyms: suppress, withhold, conceal | verb

bandied:/ˈbændid/ | adj. |  被广泛传播的;被轻率讨论的 |  widely circulated or discussed, often without careful thought | His name was bandied about as a possible successor. 他的名字作为可能的继任者被广泛传播。 | synonyms: circulated, widespread, common, rife  | antonyms:  rare, uncommon, obscure | adjective
