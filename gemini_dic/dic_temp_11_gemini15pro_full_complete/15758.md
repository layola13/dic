baldrib:/ˈbɔːldrɪb/| n.|瘦骨嶙峋的人（或动物）;排骨;肋骨|A person or animal so thin that their ribs can be seen.  A rib, especially a thin one.|例句：The rescued dog was a baldrib, its ribs clearly visible beneath its matted fur.  这只获救的狗瘦骨嶙峋，乱蓬蓬的毛皮下清晰可见根根肋骨。

例句：He ate the baldrib like a starving man. 他像个饿汉一样啃着排骨。|近义词：bag of bones, skeleton, scrawny, emaciated|反义词:plump, obese, well-fed|词性:名词
