loamier:/ˈloʊmiər/| adj.|更肥沃的，更像壤土的|Having more loam; resembling loam more closely.|The soil in this part of the garden is loamier and better for growing vegetables.  这个花园这部分的土壤更肥沃，更适合种植蔬菜。|例句：The loamier soil retained water better. 土壤越肥沃，保水性越好。
The gardener added compost to make the soil loamier. 园丁添加了堆肥，使土壤更肥沃。|近义词：richer, more fertile, heavier|反义词：sandier, poorer, less fertile|词性:形容词

词源: From loam + -y + -er (comparative suffix).  Loam comes from Middle English *lom, from Old English *lām.
