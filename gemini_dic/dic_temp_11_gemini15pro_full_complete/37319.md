commemorating: /kəˈmɛməreɪtɪŋ/
adjective
纪念的，庆祝的
serving to commemorate or intended to commemorate someone or something.
The statue was erected commemorating the soldiers who died in the war. 这座雕像是为了纪念在战争中牺牲的士兵而建造的。
commemorative, memorial, celebratory
forgetting, ignoring
adjective


commemorate: /kəˈmɛməreɪt/
verb
纪念，庆祝
recall and show respect for (someone or something).
We commemorate the founding of our nation every July 4th. 我们每年的7月4日都会庆祝国庆。
celebrate, observe, honor
forget, ignore
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin commemoratus, past participle of commemorare (“to call to mind, remember”), from com- (“together”) + memorare (“to bring to remembrance”), from memor (“mindful”).
