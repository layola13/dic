floroon:/flɔːˈruːn/
n.
花廊，花房，温室
A room or building with a glass roof and walls, mainly for growing plants.
The floroon was filled with exotic orchids.  花房里充满了异国情调的兰花。
The hotel has a beautiful floroon where guests can relax.  这家酒店有一个漂亮的花房，客人可以在那里放松身心。
synonym: conservatory, greenhouse, glasshouse
antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps outdoor garden)
