fuero:/ˈfwɛroʊ/| n.|特权，管辖权，特许，特权法|A fuero is a set of specific laws and privileges historically granted to a particular group or region.  It often refers to legal jurisdictions or exemptions.|The Basque Country in Spain historically enjoyed certain fueros, granting them autonomy. 西班牙的巴斯克地区历史上享有某些特权，赋予他们自治权。|synonyms: privilege, jurisdiction, charter, immunity, exemption|antonyms: restriction, limitation, subjection|noun

fuero:/ˈfwɛroʊ/| n.|（个人的）习惯，惯例|Fuero can also refer to a personal custom or habit.|It's his fuero to take a walk every evening after dinner. 他习惯每天晚饭后散步。|synonyms: custom, habit, practice, routine|antonyms: irregularity, exception|noun
