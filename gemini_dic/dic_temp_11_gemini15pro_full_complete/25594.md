bummest:/ˈbʌməst/| adj.|最糟糕的，最令人沮丧的|The superlative form of "bummed," meaning extremely disappointed or unhappy.  It describes something that is the most unpleasant or depressing out of a range of options.|That was the bummest ending to a vacation ever! 那是有史以来最糟糕的假期结尾！|近义词：worst, most disappointing, most depressing|反义词:best, greatest, most exciting|词性:形容词
词源: From "bummed," which originated in the 1960s as slang, possibly related to "bum," meaning a vagrant or idle person, and by extension, something undesirable.  The "-est" suffix creates the superlative form.
