aspersorium:/ˌæspərˈsɔːriəm/
noun
洒水器，圣水器
A vessel for holding holy water, especially one used for sprinkling.
The priest dipped the aspersorium in the holy water and sprinkled the congregation.  神父将圣水器浸入圣水中，然后洒向会众。
synonyms: aspergillum, sprinkler
antonyms: N/A
