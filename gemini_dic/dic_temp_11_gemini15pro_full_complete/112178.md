lookout:/ˈlʊkaʊt/ (US), /ˈlʊkaʊt/ (UK)| n. |瞭望台，监视哨；警戒，监视；守望者，望员|A place from which to keep watch or the act of keeping watch.  A person who keeps watch.|The sailors took turns being on lookout for other ships. 水手们轮流担任瞭望，注意其他船只。|synonyms: watchtower, observation post, vigil, surveillance, watchman, guard|antonyms: neglect, inattention|noun

lookout:/ˈlʊkaʊt/ (US), /ˈlʊkaʊt/ (UK)| int. |小心！当心！|Used as a warning to be careful or to watch out.|Lookout! There's a car coming! 小心！有车来了！|synonyms: watch out, heads up, be careful|antonyms:  (No direct antonym for an interjection)|interjection
