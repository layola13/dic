outwardly:/ˈaʊtwərdli/
adverb
外表上，表面上
appearing or seeming to be a certain way, but not necessarily feeling or thinking that way
He remained outwardly calm during the crisis.  他在危机期间表面上保持冷静。

近义词:apparently, seemingly, superficially
反义词:inwardly, internally

词性:adverb

词源:From Old English *ūteweardlīce (unattested), from ūteweard "outward" +‎ -līce.  来自古英语 *ūteweardlīce（未证实），由 ūteweard “向外” + -līce 组成。
