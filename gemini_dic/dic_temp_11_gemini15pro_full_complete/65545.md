euthanatizing:/ˌjuːθəˌnaɪzɪŋ/
verb
安乐死
The act of inducing a painless death, especially in a sick or injured animal or person.
The vet recommended euthanatizing the dog as it was suffering from a terminal illness. 兽医建议对这只狗实施安乐死，因为它患有不治之症。
euthanizing a pet 给宠物实施安乐死
synonyms: putting down, putting to sleep, killing, ending the life of
antonyms: reviving, resuscitating, saving
v.
