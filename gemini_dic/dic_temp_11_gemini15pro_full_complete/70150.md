fiasco:/fiˈæskoʊ/ /fiˈɑːskoʊ/
n.
惨败，彻底失败
a complete failure, especially a ludicrous or humiliating one.
The dinner party was a fiasco – the food was burnt, the guests argued, and the dog ran away. 晚餐会简直是一场灾难——食物烧焦了，客人们吵了起来，狗也跑了。
failure, disaster, debacle, flop
success, triumph
noun

fiaschi:/fiˈæskoʊz/ /fiˈɑːskoʊz/
n.
惨败（fiasco的复数形式）
plural of fiasco
Several political fiaschi have plagued the administration. 几次政治上的惨败一直困扰着政府。
failures, disasters, debacles, flops
successes, triumphs
noun
