methaemoglobin:/ˌmɛtˈhiːməˌɡloʊbɪn/, /ˌmetˈheməˌɡloʊbɪn/ | n. | 高铁血红蛋白 | A form of hemoglobin in which the iron in the heme group is in the ferric state (Fe3+) rather than the ferrous state (Fe2+), and is thus incapable of binding oxygen. |  Elevated levels of methaemoglobin are found in methemoglobinemia. 在高铁血红蛋白血症中发现了高水平的高铁血红蛋白。|Synonyms: ferrihemoglobin | Antonyms: hemoglobin (in its oxygen-binding ferrous state)| noun
