also:/ˈɔːlsoʊ/ (US) /ˈɔːlsəʊ/ (UK)| adv.|也，还，而且|Used to add information or emphasize something.  It indicates addition or reinforcement.|She is a talented singer and also a skilled dancer. 她是一位才华横溢的歌手，也是一位技艺精湛的舞者.|Synonyms: too, as well, additionally, besides, moreover, furthermore, in addition|Antonyms: neither, nor, conversely, on the contrary|adverb
