mediatory:/ˈmiːdiətɔːri/ | adj. | 调解的，斡旋的 | Acting as a mediator; relating to or involving mediation. | The UN played a mediatory role in the conflict. 联合国在冲突中扮演了调解的角色。| The lawyer acted in a mediatory capacity between the two parties. 律师在双方之间担任调解人。 | synonyms: mediating, conciliatory, arbitrating, intervening, interceding | antonyms:  confrontational, antagonistic, belligerent | adjective

mediatory:/ˈmiːdiətəri/ | n. | 调解人 | A person who mediates. (This usage is less common than the adjective form.) |  He served as a mediatory in the dispute. 他在争议中担任调解人。| She acted as the mediatory between the warring factions. 她充当了交战派别之间的调解人。 | synonyms: mediator, conciliator, arbitrator, intermediary | antonyms: antagonist, agitator | noun


Etymology:
From Late Latin *mediātōrius, from Latin mediātor (“mediator”) + -ius (suffix forming adjectives).
