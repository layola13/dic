disunify: /dɪsˈjuːnɪfaɪ/ /dɪsˈjuːnəˌfaɪ/ | vt. | 使不统一，使分裂 | To cause (something) to no longer be unified; to break apart or separate into different parts. | The new policy disunified the country, leading to regional tensions. 新政策使国家分裂，导致地区紧张局势。| The ongoing conflict has disunified the community. 持续的冲突使社区分裂。 | 近义词：divide, separate, fragment, disintegrate | 反义词：unite, unify, integrate, consolidate | 词性: 动词

词源: dis- (prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of") + unify (verb meaning "to make or become united, uniform, or whole")
