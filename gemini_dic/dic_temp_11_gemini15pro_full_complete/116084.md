martyr:/ˈmɑːrtər/ (US) /ˈmɑːtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |烈士，殉道者；(夸)受苦者，受折磨者|A person who is killed because of their religious or other beliefs; a person who displays or exaggerates their suffering in order to obtain sympathy or admiration.|Joan of Arc was a martyr for her faith.  圣女贞德是为信仰而死的烈士。|synonyms: victim, sufferer, saint|antonyms: perpetrator, oppressor|noun

martyr:/ˈmɑːrtər/ (US) /ˈmɑːtə(r)/ (UK)| v. |使殉难，折磨；杀害(尤指因宗教信仰)|To kill someone because of their beliefs.|He was martyred for his political beliefs. 他因其政治信仰而殉难。|synonyms: kill, execute, persecute|antonyms: protect, save, defend|verb
