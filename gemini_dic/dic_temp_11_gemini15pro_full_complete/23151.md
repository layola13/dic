boutiques:/buːˈtiːks/
n.
精品店；时装店
Small shops selling fashionable clothes, jewelry, or other luxury goods.
The street is lined with chic boutiques and art galleries. 这条街两旁林立着别致的精品店和美术馆。

boutiques:/buːˈtiːks/
n.
专门商店；专卖店
A business that serves a specialized clientele or offers a specialized product or service.
There are several software boutiques specializing in custom applications. 有几家软件专卖店专门定制应用程序。


Etymology:
From French *boutique*, from Occitan *botica*, from Late Latin *apothēca*, from Ancient Greek ἀποθήκη (*apothḗkē*) meaning "storehouse", "warehouse".

Synonyms:
shop, store, retailer, outlet, emporium, salon

Antonyms:
department store, hypermarket, supermarket

Noun
