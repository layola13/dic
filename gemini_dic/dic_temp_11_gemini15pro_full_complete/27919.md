cantharidal:/kænˈθærɪdəl/
adjective
斑蝥的；含斑蝥素的
Relating to or containing cantharidin, a poisonous substance secreted by blister beetles.
Cantharidal preparations were once used as aphrodisiacs, but are now considered dangerous.  斑蝥制剂曾经被用作春药，但现在被认为是危险的。
The cantharidal ointment caused severe blistering.  斑蝥软膏引起了严重的起泡。
近义词:blistering, vesicant
反义词:soothing, demulcent
词性:adjective
