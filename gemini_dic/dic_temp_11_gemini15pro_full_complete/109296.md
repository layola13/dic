lemuroidea:/ˌlɛmjʊˈrɔɪdiə/ | n. |狐猴总科;狐猴下目|A superfamily of strepsirrhine primates endemic to the island of Madagascar.|The lemuroidea are a fascinating group of primates.|近义词:lemurs|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

词源: From Modern Latin *Lemuroidea*, from Latin *lemures* (“ghosts, spirits of the dead”) + *-oidea* (suffix forming taxonomic superfamily names). The name refers to the large reflective eyes of lemurs, and their nocturnal habits, reminiscent of ghosts.
