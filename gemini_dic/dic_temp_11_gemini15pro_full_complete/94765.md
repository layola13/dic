improviso:/ɪmprəˈviːzoʊ/ (US), /ɪmprəˈviːzəʊ/ (UK)| n. | 即兴创作；即兴表演；即兴作品 |  A performance or composition done without preparation or practice. | He played a beautiful piano improviso. 他即兴演奏了一曲优美的钢琴曲。 | improvisation, extemporization | preparation, rehearsal | noun

improvise:/ˈɪmprəvaɪz/ (US), /ˈɪmprəvaɪz/ (UK)| v. | 即兴创作；即兴表演；临时凑合 | To create or perform something spontaneously, without preparation. |  The actors improvised a scene. 演员们即兴表演了一个场景。 | ad-lib, extemporize | plan, prepare | verb
