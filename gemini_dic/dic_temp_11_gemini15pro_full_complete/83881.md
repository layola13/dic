half-altered:/ˌhæf ˈɔːltərd/ | adj. | 半改变的，部分改变的 | Partly or incompletely changed; modified to some extent but not completely. | The building was half-altered, with some sections modernized and others left untouched. 这栋建筑物被部分改造，一些部分现代化了，其他部分保持原样。| The recipe was half-altered, incorporating some new ingredients but keeping the core elements.  这个食谱被部分修改，加入了一些新成分，但保留了核心元素。 | 近义词: partially changed, partly modified, semi-altered | 反义词: completely changed, fully modified, totally altered | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "half" comes from Old English "healf." "Altered" comes from the Old French "alterer," from Latin "alterare" meaning "to change." The prefix "half-" modifies "altered" to indicate a partial or incomplete change.
