depraver:/dɪˈpreɪvər/| n.|堕落者；败坏者|Someone who corrupts or makes something worse, especially morally.|The depraver lured the innocent youth into a life of crime.  这个败坏者引诱无辜的青年走上犯罪的道路。|Synonyms: corrupter, debaucher, pervert|Antonyms: reformer, improver|noun
deprave:/dɪˈpreɪv/| vt.|使堕落；败坏；使道德败坏|To make someone or something morally bad or worse.|Exposure to violence can deprave young minds. 接触暴力会败坏年轻人的思想。|Synonyms: corrupt, debase, pervert|Antonyms: elevate, improve, uplift|verb
