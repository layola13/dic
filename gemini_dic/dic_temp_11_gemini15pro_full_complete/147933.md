overremissly:/ˌoʊvərɪˈmɪsli/| adv.| 过于疏忽地，极其懈怠地 | Excessively remiss; very negligent or careless.  | He overremissly handled the important documents, leading to their loss. 他过于疏忽地处理了重要文件，导致文件丢失。|Example: She overremissly forgot her mother's birthday. 她极其疏忽地忘记了她母亲的生日。|Synonyms: negligently, carelessly, laxly, irresponsibly | Antonyms: diligently, carefully, responsibly, meticulously | 词性: 副词
