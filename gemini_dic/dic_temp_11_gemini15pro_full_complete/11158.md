ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *archest*, superlative of *arch* (adjective), from Old English *earg* "cowardly, bad, wicked," of Germanic origin; related to Dutch *arg* "bad" and German *arg* "bad, evil."  The meaning shifted from "bad" to "mischievous" and then to "cunningly playful or teasing."
