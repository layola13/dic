bibbed:/bɪbd/
adjective
戴着围嘴的；围兜式的
Wearing a bib.  A bib is a piece of cloth tied around a baby's neck to protect their clothes while eating.
The bibbed baby gurgled happily. 戴着围嘴的婴儿高兴地咯咯叫。
The waitress brought a highchair and a bibbed apron for the toddler.  服务员给幼儿拿来了一张高脚椅和一件带围兜的围裙。
近义词:  aproned
反义词:  bibless
词性: adjective
