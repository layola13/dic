leaden:/ˈlɛdn/ | adj. | 铅灰色的；沉重的；迟钝的 | Of the dull gray color of lead; heavy, dull, or sluggish. | The sky was a leaden gray. 天空是铅灰色的。 | Synonyms: gray, dull, heavy, lifeless, sluggish | Antonyms: bright, lively, energetic | adjective

leaden:/ˈlɛdn/ | v. | 使沉重；使迟钝 | To make heavy or sluggish. | The heavy meal leadened his limbs.  丰盛的饭菜使他四肢沉重。 | Synonyms: weigh down, burden, oppress | Antonyms: lighten, enliven, invigorate | verb
