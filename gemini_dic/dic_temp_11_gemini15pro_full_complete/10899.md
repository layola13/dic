aragonite:/ˈærəɡənaɪt/| n.|霰石|A mineral species of calcium carbonate, CaCO3, crystallizing in the orthorhombic system. It is less stable than calcite, into which it changes over time. |Aragonite is a common constituent of pearls and coral.|近义词:calcite|反义词:|名词
