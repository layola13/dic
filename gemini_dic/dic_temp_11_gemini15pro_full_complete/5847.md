amazement:/əˈmeɪzmənt/
n.
惊奇，惊愕
A feeling of great surprise or wonder.
She stared in amazement at the magician's trick. 她惊奇地盯着魔术师的戏法。
近义词：astonishment, wonder, awe
反义词：indifference, apathy
词性：名词

amazement:/əˈmeɪzmənt/
词源：From amaze + -ment, ultimately from Old French esmaier "dismay, bewilder."
