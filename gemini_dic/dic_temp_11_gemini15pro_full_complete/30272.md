cementable:/sɪˈmɛntəbl/
adjective
可胶结的
Capable of being cemented or bonded together.
The broken pieces of pottery were cementable.  破碎的陶器碎片可以胶合起来。
The two surfaces are cementable with epoxy. 这两个表面可以用环氧树脂粘合。
近义词:bondable, adhesive
反义词:uncementable, non-adhesive
词性:adjective
词源:From cement + -able.
