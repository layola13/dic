overbragged: /ˌoʊvərˈbræɡd/
verb
过度吹嘘，吹牛过头
bragged too much; boasted excessively.
He overbragged about his accomplishments and alienated his friends.  他过度吹嘘自己的成就，疏远了他的朋友。
She overbragged about her new car, making everyone feel uncomfortable. 她过度吹嘘她的新车，让每个人都感到不舒服。
近义词: boasted, crowed, gloated
反义词: understated, downplayed
词性: verb (past tense of overbrag)

overbrag: /ˌoʊvərˈbræɡ/
verb
过度吹嘘，吹牛过头
to brag or boast excessively.
He tends to overbrag about his golfing skills. 他倾向于过度吹嘘他的高尔夫球技能。
Don't overbrag; it makes you seem insecure.  不要过度吹嘘；这会让你看起来不自信。
近义词: boast, brag, crow
反义词: understate, downplay
词性: verb


词源:
"Overbrag" is a combination of the prefix "over-" (meaning "excessively" or "too much") and the verb "brag" (meaning "to boast").  "Brag" itself likely originates from a Scandinavian source.  The past tense form, "overbragged," simply adds the standard "-ed" suffix to indicate past action.
