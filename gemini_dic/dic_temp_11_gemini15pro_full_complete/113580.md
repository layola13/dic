maccabees: /ˈmækəˌbiz/ | n. | 马加比家族；马加比书 | The Maccabees were Jewish rebel warriors who fought for religious freedom against the Seleucid Empire in the 2nd century BCE.  The books of Maccabees recount their struggle. |  The Maccabees are revered as heroes in Jewish history. 马加比家族在犹太历史上被尊为英雄。|  The story of the Maccabees is commemorated during Hanukkah.  马加比家族的故事在光明节期间被纪念。| synonyms: Hasmoneans | antonyms: N/A | noun
