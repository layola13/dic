bickford:/ˈbɪkfərd/ | n. | 比克福德导火索;安全导火索 | A type of safety fuse used in blasting, invented by William Bickford in 1831. | The miners used Bickford fuse to ignite the dynamite. 矿工们使用比克福德导火索来点燃炸药。 | synonym: safety fuse, fuse | antonym: detonator | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Named after its inventor, William Bickford (1774-1834), a British leather merchant who invented the safety fuse in 1831.
