cockneyer:/ˈkɒkniə(r)/ (US: /ˈkɑːkniər/)| n.|伦敦东区人，伦敦东区土著|A native of the East End of London, traditionally one who speaks with a Cockney accent.|例句：He's a true cockneyer, born and bred within the sound of Bow Bells. 他是一个真正的伦敦东区人，土生土长在鲍尔斯钟声的范围内。

例句：The cockneyer regaled us with stories of his youth in the East End.  这位伦敦东区人给我们讲述了他在东区度过的青春故事。

|近义词：East Ender, Londoner|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps someone from outside London/rural areas)|词性:noun
