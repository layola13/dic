discolor:/dɪsˈkʌlər/ /dɪsˈkʌlɚ/| v. |使变色，褪色|To change the color of something, usually in a way that makes it look less attractive.|The sunlight discolored the curtains. 阳光使窗帘褪色了。|近义词：fade, stain, tarnish, bleach (depending on the context)|反义词:color, brighten|词性:动词

discolor:/dɪsˈkʌlər/ /dɪsˈkʌlɚ/| adj. |变色的，褪色的|Having lost its original color, usually in a way that makes it look less attractive.|The discolored photographs brought back memories of his childhood. 褪色的照片使他想起了他的童年。|近义词：faded, stained, tarnished, bleached (depending on the context)|反义词:colorful, bright|词性:形容词
