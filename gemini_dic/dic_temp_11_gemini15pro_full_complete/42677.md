countersue:/ˌkaʊntərˈsuː/
verb
反诉，提起反诉
to sue someone who has already sued you
例句：She countersued him for libel. 她反诉他诽谤。
例句：The company decided to countersue its former employee for breach of contract.  这家公司决定反诉其前雇员违反合同。
近义词：reconvene, file a countersuit
反义词：N/A
词性：v.


countersue:/ˌkaʊntərˈsuː/
noun
反诉
a lawsuit filed in response to an initial lawsuit
例句：The countersue alleged breach of contract.  反诉状指控违反合同。
例句：His countersue was dismissed by the judge. 法官驳回了他的反诉。
近义词：countersuit
反义词：N/A
词性：n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- + sue.  Counter- is a prefix meaning "against" or "in opposition to." Sue comes from Old French suir "to follow," later "to petition," from Latin sequi "to follow."
