overmeek:/ˌoʊvərˈmiːk/ | adj. | 过分温顺的，懦弱的 | Excessively meek; too humble or submissive. | He was so overmeek that he let people walk all over him. 他太懦弱了，以至于任人摆布。|  近义词: submissive, compliant, docile, timid | 反义词: assertive, bold, confident, aggressive | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above, beyond") + "meek" (Old Norse mjúkr "soft, mild, gentle," from Proto-Germanic *maikwaz "soft")
