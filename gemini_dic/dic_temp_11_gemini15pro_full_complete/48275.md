decoupled:/diːˈkʌpəld/
adjective
分离的，解耦的
separated, disconnected, not linked
The decoupled design allows for independent upgrades of each module.  这种解耦设计允许每个模块独立升级。
The economy has become increasingly decoupled from traditional manufacturing. 经济越来越与传统制造业脱钩。
independent, separate, unlinked, disconnected, autonomous
coupled, linked, connected, integrated, interdependent
adjective
