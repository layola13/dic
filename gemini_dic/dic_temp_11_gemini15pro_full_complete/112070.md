longhouse:/ˈlɒŋˌhaʊs/ (UK) /ˈlɔːŋˌhaʊs/ (US)| n. |长屋，长形房屋|A long, communal dwelling traditionally built by certain indigenous cultures, often with a single room and multiple families living within.|The Iroquois built longhouses that could house dozens of people. 易洛魁人建造的长屋可以容纳数十人。|近义词：communal house, dwelling|反义词:apartment, detached house|词性:名词

longhouse:| n. |长屋|A type of long dwelling, especially as traditionally built by Native Americans of the eastern woodlands or by some peoples of Southeast Asia.|Many indigenous tribes lived in longhouses. 许多土著部落都住在长屋里。|近义词：longhouse|反义词:apartment|词性:名词
