coudee:/ˈkuːdiː/| n.|肘尺；肘，前臂|An obsolete unit of length, approximately equal to the length of a forearm from the elbow to the tip of the middle finger.  It varied in length from about 38 to 51 centimeters.|The tapestry was three coudees long.|近义词:cubit, ell|反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement)|noun
