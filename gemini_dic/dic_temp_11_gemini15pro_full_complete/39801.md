consortial:/kənˈsɔːrʃəl/
adjective
财团的；联合的；合伙的
Relating to or involving a consortium.  A consortium is an association, typically of several business companies.
例句：
The project was funded by a consortial agreement between several universities.
这个项目是由几所大学之间的联合协议资助的。

The consortial loan was secured by the assets of all the participating companies.
该财团贷款是由所有参与公司的资产担保的。

近义词：joint, combined, collaborative, cooperative, syndicate
反义词：independent, sole, individual
词性：adjective

词源:
From consortium + -al.  Consortium comes from Latin *consortium, from con- 'together' + sors 'lot, fate, share'.
