laryngealized:/ləˌrɪndʒiəlaɪzd/
|adj.|
喉音化的；用喉音发出的
|produced in the larynx; pronounced with constriction of the larynx|
The laryngealized consonant is common in some languages. 喉音化的辅音在某些语言中很常见。

The singer used a laryngealized vocal technique to create a unique sound.  这位歌手使用了一种喉音化的发声技巧来创造一种独特的声音。


|近义词:glottalized, guttural|
|反义词:oral|
|词性:adjective|
