elocutionally: /ˌɛləˈkjuːʃənəli/ | adv. | 雄辩地，演说般地 | In a manner characterized by fluent and effective speech, often with a focus on pronunciation, delivery, and style.  It describes how something is said, rather than what is said. | She delivered the speech elocutionally, captivating the audience with her clear pronunciation and expressive gestures. 她以清晰的发音和富有表现力的姿态发表了演讲，雄辩的表达深深吸引了观众。 | 近义词: eloquently, articulately, fluently | 反义词: inarticulately, haltingly, poorly | 词性: 副词
