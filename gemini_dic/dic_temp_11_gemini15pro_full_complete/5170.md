alleviate:/əˈliːvieɪt/
verb
减轻，缓解
To make something less severe, serious, or painful.
The medication alleviated her pain.  这药减轻了她的疼痛。
The government is trying to alleviate the suffering of the refugees. 政府正在努力减轻难民的痛苦。
Synonyms: ease, lessen, relieve, mitigate, soothe
Antonyms: aggravate, worsen, exacerbate, intensify
词性: 动词
