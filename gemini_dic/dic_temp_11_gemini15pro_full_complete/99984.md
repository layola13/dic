interparietal:/ˌɪntərˈpəˈraɪɪtəl/| adj.|顶间的；位于顶骨间的|Located between the parietal bones of the skull.|The interparietal bone is a small bone located between the parietal bones and the occipital bone in some animals.|近义词:intercalary, interstitial|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *inter* (between) + *parietalis* (of walls, of the parietal bone), from *paries* (wall).
