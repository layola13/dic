overmaturely: /ˌoʊvər.məˈtʃʊr.li/ /ˌəʊvə.məˈtʃʊə.li/ | adv. | 过熟地，过早成熟地 |  In a way that suggests excessive or premature maturity.  Developing or aging too quickly. | The fruit had overmaturely ripened on the tree and fallen to the ground.  这种水果在树上过熟了，掉到了地上。| The child behaved overmaturely for his age, perhaps due to his difficult upbringing.  这个孩子举止比他的年龄成熟，可能是因为他艰难的成长环境。 | 近义词: prematurely, too early, too soon | 反义词: late, tardily | 词性: 副词

词源:

over- + mature + -ly

* "over-"  prefix meaning "excessively," "too much"
* "mature"  from Latin "maturus" meaning "ripe," "timely"
* "-ly"  suffix forming adverbs
