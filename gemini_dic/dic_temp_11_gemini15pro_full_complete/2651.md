adoration:/ˌæd.əˈreɪ.ʃən/ | n. |崇拜；爱慕；敬爱| Deep love and respect.|  She gazed at him with adoration. 她用爱慕的眼光注视着他。| synonyms: worship, reverence, devotion | antonyms: hatred, contempt, disdain | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *adorationem, accusative singular of adoratio (“adoration, worship”), from adorare (“to worship”), from ad- (“to”) + orare (“to pray, speak, plead”).
