choke:/tʃoʊk/| v. n.|窒息，哽咽，阻塞|To have difficulty breathing because your throat is blocked, or to cause someone to have this difficulty; to block something such as a pipe or tube; to fail to do something because of nervousness or fear.|例句：He choked on a piece of bread. 他被一块面包噎住了。|近义词：suffocate, gag, stifle, obstruct|反义词:breathe, clear|词性:动词

choke:/tʃoʊk/| n.|窒息，阻风门|The action of choking or being choked; a device in a vehicle’s engine that controls the amount of air entering it|例句：The smoke made me choke. 烟呛得我喘不过气来。|近义词：gasp, strangulation|反义词:breath|词性:名词
