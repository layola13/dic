gemarist:/ɡəˈmɑːrɪst/ | n. | 语法家，语法学家 | A person who studies grammar or is an expert in grammar.  |  She is a renowned gemarist, specializing in historical linguistics. 她是一位著名的语法学家，专攻历史语言学。 |  
| Example: The gemarist explained the intricacies of the subjunctive mood. 这位语法学家解释了虚拟语气的复杂性。| synonym: grammarian, linguist | antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps someone who disregards or doesn't understand grammar) | noun
