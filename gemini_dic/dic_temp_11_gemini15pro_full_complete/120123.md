mimosa: /mɪˈmoʊsə/ (US), /mɪˈməʊzə/ (UK) | n. | 含羞草；含羞草属植物；含羞草鸡尾酒 | A tropical American plant of the pea family, with leaves that fold up when touched. Also, a cocktail made with champagne and orange juice. |  例句：The mimosa plant closed its leaves when I brushed against it.  含羞草在我碰到它的时候合上了叶子。

例句：She sipped her mimosa at brunch. 她早午餐时小口喝着她的含羞草鸡尾酒。 | 近义词：sensitive plant (for the plant), Bucks Fizz (for the cocktail) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

mimosa: /mɪˈmoʊsə/ (US), /mɪˈməʊzə/ (UK) | adj. | 含羞草的；含羞草似的 | Relating to or resembling the mimosa plant or flower. | 例句：The mimosa blossoms are a delicate pink. 含羞草的花朵是淡粉色的。

例句：She wore a dress with a mimosa print. 她穿着一件印有含羞草图案的连衣裙。 | 近义词：sensitive, shy | 反义词: bold, insensitive | 词性: adjective
