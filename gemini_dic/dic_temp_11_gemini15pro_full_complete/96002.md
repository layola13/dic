incurrent:/ˈɪnkʌrənt/| adj.|流入的，输入的|Carrying or conveying something inward or toward a center. |例句：The sponge's incurrent pores allow water to flow into its body.  海绵的流入孔允许水流入体内。

例句：Incurrent canals bring oxygenated water to the gills of fish. 流入管将含氧的水带到鱼鳃。|近义词：inflowing, afferent|反义词:excurrent, efferent|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *incurrent-*, present participle of *incurrere* "to run into, run in, occur," from *in-* "in, into" + *currere* "to run."
