battlefields:/ˈbætlfildz/| n. |战场；争斗场所|Places where battles are or have been fought.  A place where a struggle or conflict takes place, literal or figurative.|The Civil War saw some of the bloodiest battlefields in American history. 美国内战见证了美国历史上一些最血腥的战场。|The company is a key player in the battlefields of the smartphone market. 这家公司是智能手机市场战场上的主要参与者。|synonyms: battleground, front, warzone, arena|antonyms: haven, sanctuary, refuge|noun
