nonprofits:/ˌnɑːnˈprɑːfɪts/ | n. | 非营利组织；非营利机构 | Organizations that do not operate for profit, often with a focus on charitable or educational purposes.  These organizations reinvest any surplus revenue back into the organization to further their mission. |  Many nonprofits rely on donations and grants to operate. 许多非营利组织依靠捐款和赠款来运作。| synonyms: charities, not-for-profit organizations, non-governmental organizations (NGOs) | antonyms: for-profit organizations, businesses | noun

nonprofit:/ˌnɑːnˈprɑːfɪt/ | adj. | 非营利的 | Not established or conducted for the purpose of making a profit.  | The nonprofit sector plays a crucial role in society. 非营利部门在社会中扮演着至关重要的角色。 | synonyms: charitable, philanthropic | antonyms: for-profit, commercial | adjective
