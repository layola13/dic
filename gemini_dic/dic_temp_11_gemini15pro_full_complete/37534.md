commonholder: /ˈkɒmənˌhəʊldə(r)/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmənˌhoʊldər/ (US) | n. | 公有地的共有权人；共同承租人 | A person who owns or rents land or property in common with others. |  Example 1: The commonholders met to discuss the upkeep of the shared grazing land. 公有地的共有权人开会讨论共有牧场的维护问题。

Example 2:  The commonholders disagreed about how to manage the communal forest. 共同承租人对如何管理公共森林意见不一。 | Synonym: co-owner, joint tenant | Antonym: sole owner, sole proprietor | Noun
