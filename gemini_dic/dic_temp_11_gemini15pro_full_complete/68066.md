fadaise:/fəˈdeɪz/| n.|胡说八道；废话；空话|A statement or action that is silly or not true.|He dismissed the rumors as a fadaise.|近义词：nonsense, rubbish, twaddle, balderdash|反义词：truth, sense, fact|词性:名词
词源: From French *fadaise*, from Occitan *fadasia*, from *fada* "fairy, foolish".
