nitrophenol:/ˌnaɪtroʊˈfiːnɒl/ /ˌnaɪtroʊˈfiːnɔːl/| n. |硝基苯酚|An organic compound with the formula HOC6H4NO2.  It exists in three isomeric forms: ortho-nitrophenol, meta-nitrophenol, and para-nitrophenol.|Ortho-nitrophenol is used in the synthesis of various dyes.  邻硝基苯酚用于合成各种染料。|Para-nitrophenol is used as an indicator and a fungicide. 对硝基苯酚用作指示剂和杀菌剂。|Synonyms:  hydroxy nitrobenzene|Antonyms: None|Noun
