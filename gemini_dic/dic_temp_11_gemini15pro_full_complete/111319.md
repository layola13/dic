livablenesses:/ˈlɪvəbəlnəsɪz/

The quality or state of being easy or pleasant to live in; the qualities that make a place easy or pleasant to live in.

Synonyms: habitability, amenity, comfort, desirability
Antonyms: uninhabitability, discomfort, undesirability
