escutcheoned:/ɪˈskʌtʃənd/
| adj. | 纹章装饰的；铭刻的 | Bearing a coat of arms; inscribed or displayed prominently, as on a shield. |  The knight's shield was escutcheoned with a golden lion. 骑士的盾牌上饰有金狮纹章。 | 近义词: emblazoned, adorned, decorated | 反义词: plain, undecorated | 词性: 形容词

escutcheon:/ɪˈskʌtʃən/
| n. | 纹章盾；盾形图案；铭牌 | A shield or shield-shaped surface on which a coat of arms is displayed; a flat piece of metal for protecting or decorating a keyhole, light switch, etc. | The family escutcheon was proudly displayed above the fireplace. 家族的纹章骄傲地展示在壁炉上方。 | 近义词: shield, crest, coat of arms | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词


词源: From Old French *escuchon*, diminutive of escusson “shield, escutcheon,” from Latin scutum “shield.”
