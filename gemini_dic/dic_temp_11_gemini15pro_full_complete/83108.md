gustation:/ɡʌˈsteɪʃən/ | n. | 味觉; 品尝 |The act or faculty of tasting; the sense of taste. |  The chef carefully balanced the flavors in the dish to provide a delightful gustation.  这位厨师精心平衡了菜肴中的味道，以提供令人愉悦的味觉体验。| synonym: taste, tasting, savor, palate | antonym:  anosmia (lack of sense of smell which heavily influences taste) | noun

gustation:/ɡʌˈsteɪʃən/ | n. | 品尝; (尤指对美食的)鉴赏  |The appreciation of taste, especially of fine food and drink. | Wine tasting is a popular form of gustation. 品酒是一种流行的品尝形式。 | synonym:  delectation, relish, enjoyment | antonym: distaste, aversion | noun
