ourself:/aʊərˈsɛlf/ |pron.|我们自己（反身代词，单数形式，正式或古体）|A reflexive pronoun used by a single person representing a group or collective entity, or in very formal or archaic language.  This form is less common than "ourselves". |例句：We allowed ourself a moment of quiet reflection. 我们让自己安静地反思了一会儿。

例句：The committee will conduct the review ourself. 委员会将自行进行审查。|近义词：ourselves|反义词: N/A (Reflexive pronouns don't have direct antonyms)|词性: pronoun
