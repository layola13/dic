gerbil:/ˈdʒɜːrbɪl/| n.|沙鼠；跳鼠|A small rodent with long hind legs adapted for jumping, native to arid regions of Africa and Asia.  It is often kept as a pet.|例句：My daughter has two gerbils as pets. 我的女儿养了两只沙鼠作为宠物。

例句：The gerbil escaped from its cage and we had to search the whole house. 沙鼠从笼子里逃了出来，我们不得不搜遍了整栋房子。|近义词：mouse, rodent, jerboa|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
