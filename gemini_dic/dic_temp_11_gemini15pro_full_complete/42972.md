coverts:/ˈkʌvərts/ (US), /ˈkʌvəts/ (UK)| n.|隐蔽处，藏身处；（鸟类等）覆羽；秘密支持者|A thicket providing cover or shelter, especially for game; the small or medium feathers which cover the bases of the larger wing and tail feathers of a bird; a person who gives secret support to an organization, especially a political one.|The fox disappeared into the coverts. 狐狸消失在灌木丛中。|Synonyms: hiding place, refuge, shelter, retreat, thicket, undergrowth; (for feathers) tectrices|Antonyms: exposure, open area|Noun
