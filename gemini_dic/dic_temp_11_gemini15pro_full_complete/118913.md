metazoan: /ˌmɛtəˈzoʊən/ | n. | 后生动物 |  Any animal of the Metazoa subkingdom; a multicellular animal having specialized cells that form tissues and organs and usually a digestive cavity and nervous system. | Metazoans are complex organisms compared to single-celled protozoans.  后生动物与单细胞原生动物相比是复杂的生物体。| synonyms: animal, multicellular animal | antonyms: protozoan, single-celled organism | noun
