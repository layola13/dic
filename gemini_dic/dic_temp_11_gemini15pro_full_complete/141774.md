oratorial:/ˌɔːrəˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
演说的；雄辩的
relating to the art of public speaking, or characteristic of an orator.
He delivered an oratorial masterpiece. 他发表了一篇雄辩的演说。
近义词: eloquent, rhetorical, declamatory
反义词: inarticulate, halting, tongue-tied
词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin oratorialis, from Latin orator ‘speaker’, from orare ‘speak, pray’.
