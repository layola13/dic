liquidambar:/ˈlɪkwɪdˌæmbər/ | n. |枫香树；枫香树脂；苏合香|A type of tree (genus Liquidambar) known for its star-shaped leaves, vibrant fall colors, and fragrant resin. Also refers to the resin itself, sometimes called sweetgum or storax.| The liquidambar trees in our yard are ablaze with color in the fall. 我们院子里的枫香树在秋天色彩鲜艳。|  sweetgum, storax | - | noun
