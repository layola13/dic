adhocery:/ædˈhɑːkəri/ | n. | 特别程序；临时安排；权宜之计 | The state of being organized or done for a particular purpose only and not planned in advance.  |  The committee was formed ad hoc to address the specific crisis.  该委员会是为应对特定危机而临时成立的。|  Synonyms: improvisation, expediency, makeshift  | Antonyms:  planning, premeditation, forethought | noun

ad hoc:/ædˈhɑːk/ | adj. | 特别的；临时的；专设的 | Created or done for a particular purpose as necessary. | The ad hoc committee disbanded after completing its assigned task. 这个特别委员会在完成指定任务后就解散了。 | Synonyms:  improvised, makeshift, temporary | Antonyms:  planned, permanent, regular | adjective
