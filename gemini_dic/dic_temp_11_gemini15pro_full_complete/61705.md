emos:/ˈiːmɒʊz/ (UK), /ˈiːmoʊz/ (US)| n. |情绪硬核|A subgenre of hardcore punk music characterized by its confessional and emotional lyrics. | Emo is often associated with bands like My Chemical Romance and Fall Out Boy.  情绪硬核通常与像我的化学浪漫和Fall Out Boy这样的乐队联系在一起。|近义词：screamo, post-hardcore|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "happy hardcore" as a contrasting genre) |词性:noun
