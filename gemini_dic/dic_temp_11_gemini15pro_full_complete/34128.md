cladocera:/kləˈdɒsərə/
noun
枝角类动物
Small crustaceans (water fleas) characterized by a bivalved carapace, a single median compound eye, and branched antennae used for swimming.
Cladocera are an important part of the freshwater zooplankton. 枝角类动物是淡水浮游动物的重要组成部分。
Cladocera populations can fluctuate dramatically depending on environmental conditions.  枝角类动物的数量会根据环境条件而剧烈波动。
