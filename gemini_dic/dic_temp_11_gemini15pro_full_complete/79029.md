give-a-hand:/ɡɪv ə hænd/| v.|帮忙，援助|To help someone with a task, especially a physical one.  It can also be used figuratively to mean to offer support or assistance.|Could you give me a hand with these boxes? 你能帮我搬一下这些箱子吗？|Synonyms: assist, help, aid|Antonyms: hinder, obstruct|Verb Phrase

give-a-hand:/ɡɪv ə hænd/| n.|帮助，援助|The act of helping someone. (Less common than the verb phrase usage)|Thanks for giving me a hand. 谢谢你帮忙。|Synonyms: assistance, help, aid|Antonyms: hindrance, obstruction|Noun Phrase
