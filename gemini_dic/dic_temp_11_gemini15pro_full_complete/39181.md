congelation:/ˌkɒndʒəˈleɪʃn/ /ˌkɑːndʒəˈleɪʃn/| n. | 凝固，冻结|The process of freezing or the state of being frozen.  The congelation of water into ice.|The congelation of the lake prevented skating. 湖泊的冻结使滑冰无法进行。|近义词：freezing, solidification, coagulation|反义词:melting, thawing, liquefaction|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin congelātiō, from Latin congelō (“freeze,” “congeal”), from con- (“together”) + gelō (“freeze”).
