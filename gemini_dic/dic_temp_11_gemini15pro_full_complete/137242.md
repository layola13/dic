nonsuspect:/ˌnɒnˈsʌspɛkt/ /ˌnɑːnˈsʌspɛkt/ | adj. | 非嫌疑的；未被怀疑的 | Not suspected of a crime or wrongdoing; unsuspicious. | The police interviewed several nonsuspect neighbors. 警方询问了几个没有嫌疑的邻居。|  The data was collected from a nonsuspect population.  这些数据是从一个未被怀疑的群体中收集的。 | 近义词：innocent, blameless, above suspicion | 反义词：suspect, guilty, suspicious | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Formed by prefixing "non-" (meaning "not") to "suspect." "Suspect" comes from the Latin word "suspectus," the past participle of "suspicere," meaning "to look up to," "to admire," or "to mistrust."  Over time, the meaning shifted primarily to "mistrust."
