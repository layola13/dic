oilcloth:/ˈɔɪlklɒθ/ (UK) /ˈɔɪlklɑːθ/ (US)| n. |油布；漆布|A waterproof cloth made by coating canvas or other fabric with oil or paint.|She wiped the table down with a damp oilcloth.|近义词：linoleum, vinyl|反义词: None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From oil + cloth.  "Oil" comes from Old French "oile" (modern French "huile"), ultimately from Latin "oleum." "Cloth" comes from Old English "clāþ."
