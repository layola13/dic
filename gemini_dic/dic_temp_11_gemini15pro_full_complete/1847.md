acromial: /əˈkroʊmiəl/| adj.|肩峰的，肩的|Relating to the acromion, a bony process on the scapula (shoulder blade).|The acromial end of the clavicle articulates with the scapula. 锁骨的肩峰端与肩胛骨相连。|The doctor pointed to the acromial region of my shoulder. 医生指着我肩膀的肩峰区域。|近义词: scapular|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider terms related to other body parts like 'sternal' or 'pelvic')|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Greek *akrōmion* "point of the shoulder,"  from *akros* "highest, outermost" + *ōmos* "shoulder."
