co-ordinated: /kəʊˈɔːrdɪneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 协调的，配合的 | Arranged or organized in a way that makes different parts work together smoothly and efficiently. | The dancers' movements were perfectly co-ordinated. 舞蹈演员的动作配合得天衣无缝。|  harmonized, synchronized, integrated | disjointed, uncoordinated | adjective

co-ordinate: /kəʊˈɔːrdɪneɪt/ | v. | 协调，配合 | To make different parts work together smoothly and efficiently. | We need to co-ordinate our efforts to achieve the best results. 我们需要协调我们的努力以达到最佳效果。|  organize, harmonize, synchronize |  disrupt, disorganize | verb

co-ordinate: /kəʊˈɔːrdɪnət/ | n. | 坐标 | Each of a group of numbers used to indicate the position of a point, line, or plane. |  The co-ordinates of the point are (3, 4).  这个点的坐标是(3, 4)。 | coordinate, position |  | noun

Etymology: From the Latin word "coordinatus," past participle of "coordinare," meaning "to arrange in order," from "co-" (together) + "ordinare" (to arrange).
