involucrum:/ɪnˈvɒlʊkrəm/ /ɪnˈvoʊləkrəm/| n. |包膜；总苞；外皮|A covering or envelope, especially the outer layer of a bone, a sheath of connective tissue, or a group of bracts surrounding a flower cluster.|The involucrum of the fetus protects it during development.|胎儿的包膜在发育过程中保护它。

involucrum:/ɪnˈvɒlʊkrəm/ /ɪnˈvoʊləkrəm/| n. |总苞|A whorl or rosette of bracts surrounding a flower cluster, especially in the Asteraceae (daisy family).|The sunflower's involucrum consists of numerous green bracts.|向日葵的总苞由许多绿色苞片组成。

involucrum:/ɪnˈvɒlʊkrəm/ /ɪnˈvoʊləkrəm/| n. |（骨头的）外膜|The outer layer or covering of a bone.|The involucrum helps in the healing process of a fractured bone.|骨膜有助于骨折的愈合过程。


近义词: covering, sheath, envelope, bracts, periosteum
反义词: core, center
词性: noun
