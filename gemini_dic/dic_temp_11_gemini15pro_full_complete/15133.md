bacilliform:/bəˈsɪlɪfɔːrm/
adjective
杆状的，棒状的
Shaped like a bacillus; rod-shaped.
Bacilliform bacteria were observed under the microscope. 在显微镜下观察到杆状细菌。
The crystal structure is bacilliform.  晶体结构呈杆状。
synonyms: rod-shaped, cylindrical, elongated
antonyms: spherical, round, coccus (when referring to bacteria)
adjective
