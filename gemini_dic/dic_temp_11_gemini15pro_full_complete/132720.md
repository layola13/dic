nondisturbances:/ˌnɒndɪˈstɜːbənsɪz/
n.
无干扰; 不受干扰的状态
Absence of disturbances; a state of being undisturbed.
The treaty ensured the nondisturbance of the indigenous people's land. 该条约确保了土著人民土地不受干扰。
The quiet zone guarantees nondisturbance for students during exams.  安静区保证学生在考试期间不受干扰。
Near synonyms: peace, tranquility, calm, quiet
Near antonyms: disturbance, disruption, chaos, turmoil
noun
