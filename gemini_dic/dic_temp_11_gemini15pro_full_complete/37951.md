complexest:/kəmˈplɛksɪst/
adj.
最复杂的
most complex; having the most intricate or involved structure or arrangement.
This is the complexest problem I've ever encountered. 这是我遇到过的最复杂的问题。
The complexest machine requires the most skilled operator. 最复杂的机器需要最熟练的操作员。
近义词: most complicated, most intricate, most involved
反义词: simplest, least complicated, least intricate
词性: 形容词

complex:/ˈkɒmplɛks/ /kəmˈplɛks/
adj.
复杂的
Consisting of many different and connected parts.
The human brain is incredibly complex. 人脑极其复杂。
They had a complex relationship. 他们的关系很复杂。
近义词: complicated, intricate, involved
反义词: simple, straightforward, easy
词性: 形容词

complex:/ˈkɒmplɛks/ /kəmˈplɛks/
n.
复合体；综合设施
A group of related buildings or facilities.
a sports complex 体育中心
a housing complex 住宅区
近义词: compound, center, facility
反义词: (No direct antonym for a physical complex)
词性: 名词


词源: From Latin complexus, past participle of complecti "to encircle, embrace, comprise," from com- "together" + plectere "to braid, weave."
