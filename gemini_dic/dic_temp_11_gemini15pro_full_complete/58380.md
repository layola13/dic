duals:/ˈduːəlz/
n.
对偶，双数，二元
A pair of things that are related or connected in some way.  The concept of duality is often used in mathematics, physics, and philosophy to describe opposing but complementary forces or concepts.
The artist explored the duals of light and darkness in his paintings.  艺术家在他的画作中探索了光明与黑暗的对偶。
synonyms: counterparts, opposites, complements
antonyms: singles, individuals
noun

dual:/ˈduːəl/
adj.
双重的，两部分的
Having two parts or aspects. Something that serves two purposes or has two functions.
This appliance has a dual purpose: it can be used as both a blender and a food processor.  这个电器有双重用途：它既可以用作搅拌机，也可以用作食品加工机。
synonyms: double, twofold, twin
antonyms: single, singular
adjective
