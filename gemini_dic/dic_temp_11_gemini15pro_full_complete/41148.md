copular:/ˈkɒpjʊlə(r)/ (US) /ˈkɑːpjələr/ (UK)| adj.|系动的，连系的|A copular verb links the subject of a clause to a subject complement (a word or phrase that renames or describes the subject).|例句：The verb "to be" is the most common copular verb.  系动词“to be”是最常见的系动词。

例句：She felt happy. 她感到快乐。（“happy”描述主语“she”的状态）|近义词：linking|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider transitive or intransitive verbs)|词性: adjective
