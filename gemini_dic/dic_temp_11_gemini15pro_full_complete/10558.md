appreciated:/əˈpriːʃiːeɪtɪd/ | v. | 感激的，欣赏的，增值的 |  Recognized the full worth of; was grateful for something. Showed gratitude or pleasure at; valued or admired highly. Increased in value. | I appreciated your help. 我很感激你的帮助。| synonyms: valued, cherished, treasured, esteemed, acknowledged, thankful, grateful | antonyms:  unappreciated, undervalued, disregarded, scorned, resented | verb

appreciated:/əˈpriːʃiːeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 被赏识的，被感激的，被增值的 | Something that has been recognized as good or valuable.  |  His contributions were greatly appreciated by the team. 他的贡献得到了团队的高度赞赏。 | synonyms: valued, recognized, esteemed | antonyms: unappreciated, undervalued, disregarded | adjective
