circumscribers:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈskraɪbərz/ | n. |限定者，限制者|Those who draw a line around something, either literally or figuratively, thereby defining its limits. | The city council are the circumscribers of the new development zone. 市议会是新开发区的限定者。  The constitution circumscribers the power of the president. 宪法限制了总统的权力。 |近义词： delimiters, restrictors |反义词: liberators, expanders | noun

circumscribe:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈskraɪb/ | v. |限定，限制|To draw a line around; to encircle; to confine within limits; to restrict. |  The king's power was circumscribed by the parliament. 国王的权力受到议会的限制。  A fence circumscribes the entire property. 一道栅栏围住了整座房子。 | 近义词： restrict, confine, limit | 反义词: expand, broaden, enlarge | verb
