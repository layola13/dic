ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *coken*, from Old English *cōc* (noun), *cōcian* (verb), from Proto-Germanic *kōkaz* (noun), *kōkijaną* (verb), from Proto-Indo-European *peh₂ḱ- (“to cook, ripen”). Cognate with West Frisian *koekje* (“to cook”), Dutch *koken* (“to cook”), German *kochen* (“to cook”), Swedish *kocka* (“to cook”), Icelandic *kokka* (“to cook”).
