hydroxylamine:/haɪˌdrɒksɪləˈmiːn/ /haɪˌdrɑːksiˈlæmiːn/
n.|羟胺|A colorless, crystalline, hygroscopic compound, NH2OH, that is a weak base and is used as a reducing agent and in the manufacture of oximes.|Hydroxylamine is used in organic synthesis.|近义词:reducing agent|反义词:oxidizing agent|词性:noun

Etymology:
hydroxyl + -amine
