disclaim:/dɪsˈkleɪm/
|v.|否认；放弃；拒绝承认|To deny any responsibility or knowledge of something; to renounce a legal claim.|The company disclaimed any responsibility for the faulty product.  该公司否认对有缺陷的产品负有任何责任。|deny, repudiate, renounce|acknowledge, admit, claim|verb

disclaimer:/dɪsˈkleɪmər/
|n.|免责声明；否认；放弃|A statement that denies something, especially responsibility or knowledge.|The website included a disclaimer about the accuracy of its content. 该网站包含一项关于其内容准确性的免责声明。|denial, renunciation, repudiation|acknowledgment, admission, claim|noun
