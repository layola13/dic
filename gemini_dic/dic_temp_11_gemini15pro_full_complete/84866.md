hangers-on:/ˈhæŋərz ɒn/ (US) /ˈhæŋəz ɒn/ (UK)
|n.| 逢迎者，攀附者，追随者|People who associate with someone important or famous in hopes of gaining some advantage; sycophants, followers.|Many hangers-on flocked to the rock star's hotel room after the concert.  演唱会结束后，许多逢迎者涌入摇滚歌星的酒店房间。|Synonyms: followers, sycophants, toadies, leeches, groupies|Antonyms: leaders, pioneers, innovators|Noun
