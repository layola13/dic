kaput:/kəˈpʊt/, /kɑːˈpʊt/ | adj. | 坏了的，完蛋的 | Broken, ruined, finished, no longer working or effective.  From German *kaputt*, ultimately from French *capot*, meaning 'beaten' (in a card game). |  My old car is finally kaput. 我的旧车终于报废了。

The deal is kaput.  这笔交易告吹了。 | 近义词：broken, ruined, finished, dead, defunct | 反义词：working, functional, intact | 词性：形容词
