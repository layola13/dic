merciful: /ˈmɜːsɪfʊl/ (UK) /ˈmɜːrsɪfʊl/ (US)| adj.|仁慈的；慈悲的；宽大的|Showing or characterized by mercy; compassionate or forgiving.|The merciful king spared the prisoner's life. 仁慈的国王饶恕了囚犯的性命。|The judge handed down a merciful sentence. 法官作出了仁慈的判决。|近义词：compassionate, forgiving, lenient, clement|反义词：cruel, merciless, ruthless, pitiless|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *merciable*, *mersyable*, from Old French *merciable*, from Late Latin *mercībilis* (“worthy of mercy”), from Latin *mercēs* (“reward, wages, price, merit, kindness, pity, compassion”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\*merḱ-* (“to assign, fix a price, buy”).
