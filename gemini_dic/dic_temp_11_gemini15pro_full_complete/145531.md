overcoming:/ˌoʊvərˈkʌmɪŋ/| adj. |克服的，战胜的|The process of successfully dealing with a problem or difficulty.|She showed great courage in overcoming her illness. 她战胜病魔展现了巨大的勇气。|近义词：conquering, surmounting, defeating, triumphing over|反义词：succumbing to, yielding to|词性: present participle of overcome


overcome:/ˌoʊvərˈkʌm/| v. |克服，战胜|Successfully deal with (a problem or difficulty).|He overcame his shyness and spoke to the girl. 他克服了害羞，跟女孩说了话。|近义词：conquer, surmount, defeat, triumph over|反义词：succumb to, yield to|词性: verb

词源: From Middle English *overcomen*, from Old English *ofercuman* (“to overcome, conquer, surpass, subdue, overwhelm, afflict”), from *ofer* (“over”) + *cuman* (“to come”).  Cognate with Dutch *overkomen* (“to happen, come over”), German *überkommen* (“to receive, seize, come over”).
