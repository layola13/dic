ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *owtriggere, from *owt + *rigger, from Old Norse *út + *rigga ("to rig, equip").  Meaning "projecting beam" is from 1570s. Nautical sense of "spar to extend sail" is from 1690s, later specialized to Polynesian canoe sense (1777).
