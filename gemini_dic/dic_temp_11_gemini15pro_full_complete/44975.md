crystallinities:/ˌkrɪstəˈlɪnɪtiz/| n. |结晶度，结晶性|The degree to which a substance is crystalline, expressed as a percentage.  It represents the proportion of the material that exists in a perfectly ordered crystalline state.|The polymer exhibits high crystallinity, making it strong and rigid.|近义词：crystallinity, degree of crystallinity|反义词:amorphousness|noun
