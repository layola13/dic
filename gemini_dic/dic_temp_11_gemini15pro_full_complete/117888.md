melograph:/ˈmɛləɡræf/ | n. |旋律记录器；旋律图|An instrument for recording melodies; a melody graph or diagram.|The composer used a melograph to capture the fleeting melody that came to him in a dream.|近义词:melodicon, melodeon |反义词: N/A |词性:noun

Etymology:  From Greek *μέλος (mélos) "song, melody" + *-graph "something written or recorded."
