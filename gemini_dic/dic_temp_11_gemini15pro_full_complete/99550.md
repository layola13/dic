interhabitation:/ɪntərˌhæbɪˈteɪʃn/ | n. |同居，共栖，共同居住|The interhabitation of different species in a particular ecosystem.  不同物种在特定生态系统中的共栖。|例句：The interhabitation of humans and animals in close proximity can lead to health concerns. 人类和动物近距离同居会导致健康问题。

例句：The interhabitation of different ethnic groups in the city has fostered a rich cultural tapestry. 城市中不同民族的共同居住促成了一幅丰富的文化景象。|近义词：cohabitation, living together, sharing a dwelling|反义词：separation, segregation|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- + habitation.  "inter-" 表示"在...之间"，"habitation" 表示"居住"。
