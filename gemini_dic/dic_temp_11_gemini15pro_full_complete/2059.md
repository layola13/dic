acupuncturists:/ˈækjʊpʌŋktʃərɪsts/
n.
针灸师
A person who practices acupuncture.
例句：The acupuncturists use thin needles to stimulate specific points on the body. 针灸师用细针刺激身体上的特定穴位。

例句：Many acupuncturists also incorporate herbal remedies into their treatments. 许多针灸师也将草药疗法纳入他们的治疗中。

近义词：practitioner of acupuncture, acupuncturist
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider: conventional doctor, Western physician)
词性: Noun
