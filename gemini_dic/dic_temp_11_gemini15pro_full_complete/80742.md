gosherd:/ˈɡoʊʃɜːrd/| n.|牧羊人；牧羊犬|A person or dog that herds and tends sheep.|例句：The gosherd guided the flock to the pasture. 牧羊人把羊群赶到牧场。

例句：The gosherd's dog kept the sheep from straying. 牧羊犬防止羊群走散。


|近义词：shepherd, herdsman, sheepdog|反义词: -(none readily available) |词性: noun
