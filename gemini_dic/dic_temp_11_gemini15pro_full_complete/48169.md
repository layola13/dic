decompartmentalizes:/ˌdiːkəmˌpɑːrtˈmɛntəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
消除部门界限；打破隔阂
To break down the barriers between separate departments or groups, allowing for more interaction and collaboration.
The new CEO decompartmentalizes the company's structure to improve communication. 新任首席执行官打破了公司部门间的隔阂以加强沟通。
The software decompartmentalizes information, making it accessible to everyone.  这款软件消除了信息壁垒，让每个人都可以访问。
近义词: integrate, unify, combine, consolidate
反义词: compartmentalize, segregate, divide, isolate
词性: verb

词源:
de- (prefix):  meaning "reverse" or "remove"
compartmentalize (verb):  to divide into separate compartments or categories
-ize (suffix):  forms verbs from nouns or adjectives
