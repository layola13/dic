misfortuned:/ˌmɪsˈfɔːrtʃənd/ | adj. | 不幸的，倒霉的 |  Unlucky; having bad fortune.  Derived from "misfortune" with the suffix "-ed" added to create an adjective.  While grammatically correct, it's archaic and rarely used in modern English.  "Unfortunate" is a much more common synonym. |  He was a misfortuned soul, plagued by ill health. 他是一个不幸的灵魂，饱受病痛折磨。 | unfortunate, hapless, ill-fated, unlucky | fortunate, lucky | adjective
