agglomerator:/əˈɡlɒməreɪtər/| n.|凝聚器，集聚器，烧结机|A machine that forms powdered material into agglomerates or clusters.|The agglomerator combines small particles of ore into larger, more manageable lumps. 烧结机将小块矿石合并成更大、更容易处理的块状物。|synonyms: aggregator, collector, compiler, assembler|antonyms: separator, disperser|noun

agglomerate:/əˈɡlɒməreɪt/| v. |凝聚，聚集，结块|To gather into a mass or ball; to collect into a cluster.|Dust particles agglomerate to form larger clumps. 灰尘颗粒聚集形成更大的团块。|synonyms: aggregate, amass, accumulate, coalesce|antonyms: separate, disperse, scatter|verb

agglomerate:/əˈɡlɒməreɪt/| adj.|凝聚的，聚集的|Gathered into a mass or ball; clustered.|The agglomerate rock formation was made up of various minerals. 聚集的岩层由各种矿物组成。|synonyms: clustered, aggregated, amassed|antonyms: dispersed, scattered|adjective
