gnomic:/ˈnoʊmɪk/| adj.|格言的；箴言式的；精辟的；地精的|Relating to gnomes or their characteristically wise, pithy sayings; aphoristic.  Pertaining to a gnome or dwarf. |例句：His gnomic utterances were difficult to interpret. 他的精辟言论难以理解。

例句：The old woman offered gnomic advice about life and love.  老妇人就生活和爱情给出了精辟的建议。|近义词：wise, pithy, aphoristic, proverbial, sage, epigrammatic|反义词：prolix, verbose, rambling|词性:adjective

Etymology: From Greek gnōmē (γνώμη) meaning "opinion, judgment, maxim, thought," from gignōskein (γιγνώσκειν) "to know."
