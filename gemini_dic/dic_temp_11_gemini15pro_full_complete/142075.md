orgue:/ɔːɡ/ (UK), /ɔːrɡ/ (US)| n.|管风琴|A large musical instrument with pipes supplied with wind by bellows and played using a keyboard or keyboards. It is typically found in churches and cathedrals.|The organist played a beautiful piece on the church orgue.|Synonyms: pipe organ|Antonyms: None|Noun
