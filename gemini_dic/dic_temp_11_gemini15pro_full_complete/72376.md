fluavil:/ˈfluːəvɪl/| n. |黄胶树脂|A yellow resinous constituent of gutta-percha. It is a hydrocarbon, soluble in alcohol, ether, and chloroform, but insoluble in water.|Gutta-percha contains two main components: gutta and fluavil.  橡胶树胶包含两种主要成分：古塔胶和黄胶树脂。|Fluavil is separated from gutta by its solubility in cold alcohol. 黄胶树脂通过其在冷酒精中的溶解度与古塔胶分离。|Synonyms: resin, gum|Antonyms: None|Noun
