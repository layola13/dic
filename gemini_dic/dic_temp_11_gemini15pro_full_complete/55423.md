distiller:/dɪˈstɪlər/ | n. | 蒸馏酒制造者；酿酒商；蒸馏器 | A person or company that makes distilled alcoholic beverages, such as whisky, gin, or vodka.  Also refers to the apparatus used for distillation. | The distiller uses copper stills to create a unique flavor profile.  这个酿酒商使用铜制蒸馏器来创造独特的风味。| manufacturer, producer, brewer | consumer, retailer | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *distillour*, borrowed from Old French *distilleur*, from *distiller* (“to distill”), from Latin *distillāre* (“to drip down, trickle down”).
