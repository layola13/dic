lithospheric:/ˌlɪθəˈsfɪrɪk/ | adj. | 岩石圈的 | Relating to the lithosphere, the rigid outer part of the earth, consisting of the crust and upper mantle. | The lithospheric plates move slowly over the asthenosphere. 岩石圈板块缓慢地在软流圈上移动。| The earthquake was caused by lithospheric movement.  地震是由岩石圈运动引起的。 |Synonyms:  crustal, tectonic | Antonyms: asthenospheric | adjective
