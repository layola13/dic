nonindictable:/ˌnɒnɪnˈdaɪtəbl/
adjective
非起诉的，可予简易程序审理的
Not serious enough to be tried in a higher court; (of an offense) tried summarily.
Shoplifting is usually a nonindictable offense.  商店行窃通常是一种可予简易程序审理的罪行。
The case was dealt with summarily because it was a nonindictable offence.  该案件以简易程序处理，因为它是非起诉罪行。
synonyms:summary, minor, petty, less serious
antonyms:indictable, serious, major, grave
词性: 形容词
