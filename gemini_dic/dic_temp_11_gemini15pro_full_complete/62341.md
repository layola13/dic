endemisms:/ˈɛndɛmɪzəmz/
n.
地方性，特有种；特有现象
The state of being endemic; restriction to a particular locality or region.
The Galapagos Islands are known for their unique endemisms.  加拉帕戈斯群岛以其独特的特有物种而闻名。
The island's endemisms are threatened by introduced species. 该岛的特有物种受到外来物种的威胁。
近义词: nativeness, indigenousness
反义词: cosmopolitanism
