idolatry:/aɪˈdɒlətri/ (US: /aɪˈdɑːlətri/)| n.|偶像崇拜；盲目崇拜|The worship of idols; excessive devotion to or reverence for something.|例句：Their idolatry of the rock star was excessive. 他们对这位摇滚明星的崇拜太过分了。

例句：He condemned the idolatry of money. 他谴责对金钱的盲目崇拜。

近义词：worship, adoration, reverence, deification|反义词: disdain, contempt, disregard|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *idolatria*, from Greek *eidōlatreia*, from *eidōlon* ("image, idol") + *latreia* ("worship, service").
