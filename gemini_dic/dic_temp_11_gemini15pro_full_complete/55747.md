divergement:/daɪˈvɜːrdʒmənt/
n.
分歧，差异，背离
The divergement of their opinions led to a heated argument.
他们的意见分歧导致了激烈的争论。
The divergement between the two species is becoming increasingly apparent.
这两个物种之间的差异变得越来越明显。
Etymology:  From French divergence, from Latin divergere "to bend away," from dis- "apart" + vergere "to incline."
近义词: difference, discrepancy, deviation, variance
反义词: convergence, agreement, similarity, conformity
词性: 名词
