bespurt:/bɪˈspɜːrt/
verb
玷污，弄脏
To cause a mark or stain on something, typically by splashing or scattering a dirty substance.
Her new white dress was bespurt with mud. 她的新白裙子被泥巴弄脏了。
stain, soil, tarnish
