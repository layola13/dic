dijudication:/dɪˌdʒuːdɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
判决，裁定
The act of judging and settling a dispute or disagreement, especially in a court of law.
The judge's adjudication of the case took several weeks.  法官对这个案件的判决花了几个星期。
The adjudication of the competition resulted in a tie.  比赛的裁定结果是平局。
judgment, decision, ruling, determination
arbitration, mediation
noun
