例句：She had no dubitation about accepting the offer. 她毫不犹豫地接受了这个提议。

|近义词：doubt, hesitation, uncertainty, skepticism|反义词：certainty, confidence, assurance|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dubitationem, accusative singular of dubitatio (“doubt”), from Latin dubito (“I doubt, hesitate”), frequentative of dubo (“I doubt”).
