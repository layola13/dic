noninstructional:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈstrʌkʃənəl/
adjective
非教学的；非指导性的
Not relating to instruction or teaching.
The school's noninstructional staff includes the secretaries and custodians. 学校的非教学人员包括秘书和管理员。
The noninstructional time is used for student activities. 非教学时间用于学生活动。
synonyms: nonacademic, non-teaching
antonyms: instructional, educational, teaching
词性: 形容词
