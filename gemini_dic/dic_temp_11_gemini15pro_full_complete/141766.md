orarian: /ɔːˈrɛəriən/
n.
居住在海岸地区的人；海岸居民
A person who lives in a coastal region.
The orarian made his living by fishing.  这位海岸居民以捕鱼为生。
The orarians are used to the sound of the sea.  海岸居民们习惯了海浪的声音。
近义词: coastal dweller, seaside resident
反义词: inland resident, landlubber
词性: 名词


Etymology:  From Latin ōra ("coast, shore") + -ian (suffix denoting a person or thing associated with a place).
