budge:/bʌdʒ/
verb
微微移动; 改变主意; 让步
to move slightly; to change an opinion or agree to something
The heavy table wouldn't budge.  这沉重的桌子一动也不动。
He refused to budge on the issue. 他拒绝在这个问题上让步。
synonyms: shift, move, give way
antonyms: stay, remain, hold firm
词性: 动词


budge:/bʌdʒ/
noun
(非正式)移动; 让步
(informal) a slight movement; a change of opinion or concession
He hasn't given an inch of budge on the matter. 他在这个问题上丝毫没有让步。
The negotiators haven't made a budge.  谈判代表没有做出任何让步。
synonyms: shift, movement, concession
antonyms: standstill, deadlock, resistance
词性: 名词
