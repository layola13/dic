oblongum:/ˌɒblɒŋˈɡʌm/ | adj. n. | 椭圆形的；长方形的；长圆形的； Lactobacillus oblongum 长双歧杆菌 (一种益生菌)|  Referring to something shaped like a rectangle or ellipse, longer than it is wide.
