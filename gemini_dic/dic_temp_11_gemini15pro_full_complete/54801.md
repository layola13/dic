| v. | 分发；分配；提供；免除；执行；施予| distributes, allocates, provides, administers, executes, applies | The machine dispenses snacks.  这台机器分发零食。|近义词：distribute, administer, provide, allocate|反义词：withhold, retain|词性:动词

词源: From Old French *dispanser, from Latin dispensare “weigh out, distribute, manage," from dis- “apart" + pensare "to weigh."
