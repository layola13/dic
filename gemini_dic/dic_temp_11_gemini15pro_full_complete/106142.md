knitwear: /ˈnɪtwɛːr/
n.
针织品，毛线衣物
Clothing made by knitting, such as sweaters, cardigans, and scarves.
She prefers to wear knitwear in the winter. 她冬天更喜欢穿针织品。
Near synonyms:  knitted garments,  knits
Antonyms: woven garments
