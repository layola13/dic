mosses:/ˈmɔːsɪz/ /ˈmɑːsɪz/
n.
苔藓;藓类植物
A small flowerless green plant that lacks true roots, growing in low carpets or rounded cushions in damp habitats and reproducing by means of spores released from stalked capsules.
The rocks were covered with mosses.  岩石上覆盖着苔藓。
Synonyms: bryophyte, liverwort
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a plant type.  Could consider "vascular plants" as a broad contrasting group.)
