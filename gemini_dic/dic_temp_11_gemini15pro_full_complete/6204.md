ammoniated:/əˈmoʊniˌeɪtɪd/
adjective
氨化的；氨处理的
Treated with ammonia or a compound of ammonia.
例句：Ammoniated superphosphate is a common fertilizer. 氨化过磷酸钙是一种常见的肥料。

例句：The farmer used ammoniated feed for his livestock.  农民用氨化饲料喂养牲畜。

近义词：nitrogenous, ammoniacal
反义词：(No common direct antonym)
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From ammonia + -ated.  Ammonia ultimately derives from the name of the ancient Egyptian god Amun, near whose temple deposits of ammonium chloride were found.
