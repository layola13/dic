codille:/koʊˈdɪl/| n.|科迪耶，一种纸牌游戏术语|(in ombre and quadrille) a situation in which the declarer fails to win a trick that could have been won by another player, especially by the player who initially led the suit.|The opponents gained a codille when the declarer failed to trump the lead.|近义词:slough (in some card games)|反义词:None|词性:名词
