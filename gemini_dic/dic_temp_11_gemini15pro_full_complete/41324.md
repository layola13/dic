cores:/kɔːrz/ (UK) /kɔrz/ (US)| n.| 核心；中心部分；果核；地核|The earth's core is extremely hot. 地球的核心非常热。|Example: The cores of the apples were rotten. 苹果的核烂了。|Synonym: center, heart, nucleus, kernel|Antonym: periphery, edge, surface|noun

cores:/kɔːrz/ (UK) /kɔrz/ (US)| v.| 去核；挖去…的中心|She cored the apple before slicing it. 她把苹果去核后再切片。|Example:  He carefully cored the pear. 他小心地挖去了梨的核。|Synonym: pit, remove the core|Antonym: fill, stuff|verb
