newbie:/ˈnjuːbi/ (US), /ˈnjuːbi/ (UK)| n.|新手，菜鸟|A person who is new to a particular activity, skill, or group.|例句：He's a newbie at online gaming, so be patient with him. 他是在线游戏的新手，所以对他耐心点。

例句：The website has a special forum for newbies to ask questions.  这个网站有一个专门的论坛供新手提问。


近义词：beginner, novice, rookie, tyro|反义词:expert, veteran, professional|词性:noun
