at-the-hands-of:/æt ðə hændz əv/| prepositional phrase|出自…之手；由…造成；遭受…|Indicates that something (usually negative) happened to someone because of someone else.  It implies agency and responsibility on the part of the actor.|He suffered greatly at the hands of his captors. 他在他的俘虏手中遭受了巨大的痛苦。|Synonyms: because of, due to, from, by, through|Antonyms: by oneself, independently|prepositional phrase
