entanglednesses:/ɪnˈtæŋɡəldnəsɪz/
n.
纠缠，牵连，复杂关系
The state of being entangled; a complicated or intricate involvement.
The entanglednesses of the political situation made it difficult to find a solution. 政治局势的复杂性使得很难找到解决方案。
近义词：complexities, intricacies, complications
反义词：simplicity, clarity
词性：名词

Etymology:
From entangle + -ness.  "entangle" comes from the Old French word "entangler," meaning "to entrap in a net."  The suffix "-ness" transforms a word into a noun indicating a state or quality.
