entrepreneurships:/ˌɑːntrəprəˈnɜːrʃɪps/
n.
创业；创业精神
The process of designing, launching and running a new business, which is often initially a small business.  It can also refer to the collective activity of entrepreneurs.
Many business schools now offer courses in entrepreneurships.  许多商学院现在都开设创业课程。
近义词：ventures, businesses, initiatives
反义词:inactivity, stagnation
词性:名词
