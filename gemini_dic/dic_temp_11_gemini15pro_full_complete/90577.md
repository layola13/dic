hulk:/hʌlk/| n. v.| 笨重的人或物；庞然大物；(使)变得笨重 |A large, clumsy person or thing; a ship that has been dismantled or is unfit for service; to become or make something bulky or unwieldy. |例句：The old ship was a rusting hulk. 那艘旧船是一堆生锈的废铁。|近义词：monster, giant, behemoth|反义词:dwarf, midget|n.

hulk:/hʌlk/| v. | (使)变得笨重 |To become or make something bulky or unwieldy. |例句：The ship hulked in the harbor. 这艘船笨重地停在港口。|近义词：lumber, bulk up|反义词:slim down, reduce|v.
