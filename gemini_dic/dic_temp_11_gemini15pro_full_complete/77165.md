gaslighting:/ˈɡæsˌlaɪtɪŋ/ | n. |煤气灯效应；心理操控| The act of manipulating someone by psychological means into questioning their own sanity.  It involves persistent denial, misdirection, contradiction, and lying, causing the victim to doubt their memory, perception, and judgment. | She suspected her husband was gaslighting her when he denied things she knew he had said. 她怀疑她丈夫在对她进行煤气灯操纵，因为他否认了一些她知道他说过的话。|Example: He is a master of gaslighting, making her question her own sanity. 他是煤气灯操纵的大师，让她怀疑自己的理智。|Synonyms: manipulation, brainwashing, psychological abuse | Antonyms: support, encouragement, empowerment | Noun
