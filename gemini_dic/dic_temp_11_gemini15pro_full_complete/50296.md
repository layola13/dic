deplane:/diːˈpleɪn/ /dɪˈpleɪn/ | v. |下飞机|To leave an airplane after a flight.  The act of disembarking from an aircraft.|Passengers began to deplane once the aircraft reached the gate.  乘客们在飞机到达登机口后开始下飞机。|The pilot announced that we could deplane shortly. 机长宣布我们很快就可以下飞机了。|近义词：disembark, get off|反义词:board, embark|词性:动词

词源: de- (down, away from) + plane (airplane)  "de-" 字首表示“向下”或“离开”，"plane" 指的是飞机。
