afrobubblegum:/ˈæfroʊˈbʌbəlɡʌm/
n. |非洲泡泡糖（文化/艺术风格）| A vibrant and eclectic style, often referencing elements of African culture, diaspora experiences, and pop culture, characterized by bright colors, bold patterns, and a playful aesthetic. It can encompass various art forms, including visual art, music, fashion, and design. | The artist's work is a vibrant expression of afrobubblegum, blending traditional African motifs with contemporary pop culture references. 艺术家的作品是对非洲泡泡糖的生动表达，融合了传统的非洲主题和当代流行文化的元素。 |  近义词: Afropop, Afrofuturism | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "traditional" or "conservative" in an artistic context) | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
A blend of "Afro-" (referring to African or African diaspora cultures) and "bubblegum" (referring to a bright, playful, and often disposable pop culture aesthetic).
