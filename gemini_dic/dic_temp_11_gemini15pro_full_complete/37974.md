compliant:/kəmˈplaɪənt/ | adj. | 顺从的，服从的，合规的 | Disposed to agree with or obey; yielding. Meeting or in accordance with rules or standards. |  The company was fully compliant with the new regulations.  这家公司完全遵守了新的规定。 | Example: The student was compliant with the teacher's request. 这个学生服从了老师的要求。 | Synonyms: obedient, submissive, yielding, amenable, conforming | Antonyms: defiant, rebellious, noncompliant, resistant, recalcitrant | word property: adjective

compliant:/kəmˈplaɪənt/ | n. | 顺从者，服从者 | A person who complies with rules or standards. |  The company valued its compliant employees. 这家公司重视它那些服从的员工。| Example: He was a compliant in the scheme. 他是这个计划的参与者（顺从者）。 | Synonyms: conformist | Antonyms: rebel, nonconformist | word property: noun
