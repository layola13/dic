disdainously:/dɪsˈdeɪnəsli/
adverb
轻蔑地，不屑地
In a manner that shows a lack of respect and a feeling of superiority.
She disdainously flicked the dust off her sleeve. 她轻蔑地拂去袖子上的灰尘。
He disdainously rejected their offer of help. 他不屑地拒绝了他们提供的帮助。
Synonyms: contemptuously, scornfully, haughtily
Antonyms: respectfully, admiringly, humbly
词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English desdeynous, from Old French desdeignos, from desdeignier "to disdain," from des- "expressing reversal" + deignier "to deign," from Latin dignari "to deem worthy," from dignus "worthy."
