introduce:/ˌɪntrəˈdʒuːs/ | v. | 介绍；引进；提出；采用 | To make (someone) known to another by name and often by some other information. To bring (something, such as a product, measure, or bill) into use or operation for the first time. | I'd like to introduce you to my friend, Sarah. 我想把你介绍给我的朋友，莎拉。| present, acquaint, initiate | exclude, remove | verb
