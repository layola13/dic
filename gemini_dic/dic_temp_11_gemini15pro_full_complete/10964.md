arbitraries:/ˈɑːrbɪˌtreriz/
| n. |任意事物，武断的决定|Things or decisions that are determined by chance, whim, or personal preference rather than by reason, necessity, or law.  |The CEO made a number of arbitraries that left the employees confused and demoralized. 首席执行官做出了许多武断的决定，让员工们感到困惑和沮丧。|Synonyms: random choices, discretionary decisions, capricious judgments|Antonyms: reasoned decisions, objective choices, justified actions|noun
arbitraries:/ˈɑːrbɪˌtreriz/
| adj. |专横的，武断的|Based on random choice or personal whim, rather than any reason or system. | His arbitrary decision to fire the employee was met with outrage. 他武断地解雇员工的决定遭到了愤怒的回应。|Synonyms: capricious, despotic, tyrannical|Antonyms: reasoned, logical, justifiable|adjective
