leftier: /ˈlɛftiər/ (US), /ˈlɛftiə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更左倾的，更激进的|More left-wing or radical than someone or something else.|Example:  He's far leftier than his brother. 他比他哥哥左倾得多。

Example: The party adopted a leftier platform to appeal to younger voters.  该党采取了更左倾的纲领以吸引年轻选民。|近义词: more radical, more progressive, more left-wing|反义词: rightist, conservative, reactionary|词性:形容词
