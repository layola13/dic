decentest:/ˈdiːsəntist/| adj.|最正派的，最得体的|Superlative form of decent: most conforming with generally accepted standards of respectable or moral behavior.|He's the decentest man I know. 他是我认识的最正派的人。|近义词：most respectable, most proper, most honorable|反义词: most indecent, most improper, most dishonorable|词性:形容词
词源：From Middle French *decent*, from Latin decentem (nominative decens) "fitting, becoming, proper," present participle of decēre "to be fitting, to be suitable."
