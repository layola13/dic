entrust:/ɪnˈtrʌst/ | vt. | 委托；托付 | To give something to someone for safekeeping or management. To assign a responsibility to someone. | I entrusted my lawyer with the task of writing my will. 我委托我的律师写遗嘱。

The company entrusted him with a large sum of money. 公司委托他管理一大笔钱。 | 近义词: confide, commit, assign, delegate | 反义词: keep, withhold, retain | 词性: 动词

词源:
From Old French *encrustrer, from en- "in" + crustrer "to entrust," from Latin *incrudare from in- "in" + crudus "raw," perhaps originally meaning "to put in a safe place."
