crater-like:/ˈkreɪtərˌlaɪk/
adjective
像火山口的;坑状的
Resembling a crater, typically having a bowl-shaped depression.  Characterized by a hollowed-out or pitted appearance similar to a volcanic crater.
The meteorite impact left a crater-like depression in the ground. 陨石撞击在地面上留下了一个像火山口一样的凹陷。
The surface of the moon is crater-like. 月球表面坑坑洼洼的。
近义词:cratered, pitted, hollowed
反义词:smooth, flat, even
词性:adjective

crater:/ˈkreɪtər/
noun
火山口；弹坑；（月球表面的）环形山
A bowl-shaped geological formation at the top of a volcano or on a celestial body such as the moon or a planet, typically caused by an explosion or impact.
The volcano's crater was filled with lava. 火山的火山口充满了熔岩。
The moon is covered in craters. 月球上布满了环形山。
近义词:hole, pit, depression
反义词:mound, hill, peak
词性:noun
