hansa:/ˈhɑːnsə/ | n. | 汉萨同盟，汉萨城市 | A medieval league of North German towns and cities, formed in the 12th century, to protect their mutual trading interests.  The league dominated commercial activity in the North and Baltic Seas for several centuries. | The Hanseatic League, or Hansa, played a crucial role in the economic development of Northern Europe. 汉萨同盟，或称汉萨，在北欧的经济发展中扮演了至关重要的角色。

The city of Lübeck was a prominent member of the Hansa. 吕贝克市是汉萨同盟的重要成员。| 近义词: league, confederation, alliance | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider: opposition, rivalry) | 词性: noun
