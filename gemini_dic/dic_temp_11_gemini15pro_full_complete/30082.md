cdt:/siː diː tiː/ | abbreviation | 中央日光时间 (Central Daylight Time) | Central Daylight Time is observed in parts of North America and is six hours behind Coordinated Universal Time (UTC). | Example: The meeting is scheduled for 2 PM CDT. 会议安排在中央日光时间下午2点举行。| Synonym: CDT (no common synonyms as it's a specific time zone) | Antonym: CST (Central Standard Time) | noun
