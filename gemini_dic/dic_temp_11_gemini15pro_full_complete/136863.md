nonskeptic:/ˌnɑːnˈskeptɪk/ | n. adj. | 非怀疑论者，不怀疑的 |  A person who is not a skeptic; someone who doesn't habitually doubt or question accepted beliefs.  Can also be used adjectivally to describe something that is not skeptical. |  例句：He's a nonskeptic about the existence of UFOs.  他对于UFO的存在毫不怀疑。

例句：She took a nonskeptic approach to the new evidence. 她对新证据采取了不怀疑的态度。 | 近义词：believer,  trusting, credulous | 反义词：skeptic, doubter, cynical | 词性: 名词, 形容词

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "skeptic" (from Greek "skeptikos," meaning "inquiring" or "thoughtful"). The prefix "non-" simply negates the meaning of "skeptic."
