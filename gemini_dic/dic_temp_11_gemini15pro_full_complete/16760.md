barycentric:/ˌbæriˈsɛntrɪk/| adj.|重心的，质心的|Relating to or using a barycenter, the center of mass of two or more bodies that orbit each other.|The barycentric coordinates of a point within a triangle are determined by the ratios of the areas of the three sub-triangles formed by the point and the vertices.|近义词:center of mass, centroid|反义词:|adjective
