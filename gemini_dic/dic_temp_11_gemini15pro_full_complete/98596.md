instructed:/ɪnˈstrʌktɪd/
verb
指示，指导，命令，吩咐
past tense and past participle of instruct.  Having been given orders or directions.
The lawyer instructed his client to remain silent. 律师指示他的客户保持沉默。
The teacher instructed the students to complete the exercise. 老师指导学生完成练习。
Synonym: directed, commanded, ordered, taught
Antonym: misdirected, misled, confused
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Latin instruct- ‘built, furnished, arranged, taught’, from the verb instruere, from in- ‘in, on’ + struere ‘pile, build’.
