gimmer:/ˈɡɪmər/| n.| 母羊; 不育的母羊 |A female sheep, especially a young one before its first lamb; or a ewe that is barren.| The farmer sold his old gimmer at the market. 农民在市场上卖掉了他的老母羊。| synonym: ewe, sheep | antonym: ram | noun

gimmer:/ˈɡɪmər/| n.| 狡猾的女人 (Northern England dialect) | A sly or cunning woman (used in dialects of Northern England). |She's a right gimmer, always getting her own way. 她真是个狡猾的女人，总是能随心所欲。|synonym:  minx, shrew (somewhat negative connotations)|antonym:  ingénue, naive woman| noun
