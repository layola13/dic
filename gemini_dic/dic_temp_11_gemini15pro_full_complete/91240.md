hydrocortisones: /ˌhaɪdrəˈkɔːrtɪˌsoʊnz/ | n. |氢化可的松（复数）|A synthetic glucocorticoid with anti-inflammatory and immunosuppressive properties, used to treat a variety of conditions such as allergies, arthritis, and skin disorders.  It is the plural form of hydrocortisone.|  High doses of hydrocortisones were prescribed to combat the inflammation.  医生开了大剂量的氢化可的松来对抗炎症。| Synonyms: corticosteroids, glucocorticoids | Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but conceptually:  pro-inflammatory agents) | noun
