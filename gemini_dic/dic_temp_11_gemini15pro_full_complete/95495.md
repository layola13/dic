inclementness:/ɪnˈklɛməntnəs/| n.| 严酷; 恶劣;|The inclementness of the weather forced us to cancel the picnic. 天气的恶劣迫使我们取消了野餐。|英文解析: The state or quality of being inclement; harshness; severity, especially of weather.|例句：The inclementness of the winter made travel difficult. 冬天的严酷使得旅行变得困难。

The inclementness of his response surprised everyone. 他反应的严厉使每个人都感到惊讶。|近义词：severity, harshness, roughness, storminess (especially related to weather)|反义词: mildness, calmness, pleasantness|词性:noun
