muting:/ˈmjuːtɪŋ/ | adj. | 静音的，抑制的，减弱的 | Reducing or silencing the sound of something. |  The muting effect of the heavy curtains made the room unusually quiet. 厚重的窗帘的静音效果使房间异常安静。| silenced, suppressed, hushed | amplified, loud, resonating | adjective


muting:/ˈmjuːtɪŋ/ | n. | 静音，抑制，减弱 | The act or process of making something quieter or silencing it. | The muting of his voice suggested he was upset. 他声音的减弱表明他很沮丧。 | silencing, suppression, hush | amplification, boosting | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "mute," which comes from the Old French "mut" (silent, dumb), ultimately from the Latin "mutus" (silent, dumb).
