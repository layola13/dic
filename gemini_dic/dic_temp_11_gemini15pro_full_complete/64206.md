equidistance:/ˌiːkwɪˈdɪstəns/| n.|等距，等距离|The equidistance between the trees makes the avenue look very grand. 树木之间的等距排列使这条大道看起来非常宏伟。|例句：The points are placed at equidistance. 这些点被放置在等距离的位置。|近义词：equal distance|反义词: varying distance|词性:noun
equidistant:/ˌiːkwɪˈdɪstənt/| adj.|等距的|The towns are equidistant from the capital. 这些城镇与首都的距离相等。|例句：The stars are not equidistant from the Earth. 这些星星与地球的距离并不相等。|近义词：equally distant|反义词: unequally distant|词性:adjective
