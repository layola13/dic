forejudge:/fɔːrˈdʒʌdʒ/
verb
预先判断；预先断定；未审先判
To judge beforehand; to form an opinion or judgment about (something) before having all the facts or evidence.
The jury was instructed not to forejudge the defendant's guilt. 陪审团被指示不要预先判断被告是否有罪。
He was careful not to forejudge the outcome of the election. 他小心翼翼地不预先判断选举结果。
近义词:prejudge, prejudicate
反义词:reserve judgment
词性:v.
