favored:/ˈfeɪvərd/ | adj. | 受喜爱的，优惠的，有利的 | preferred, regarded with favor; given an advantage | He was in a favored position to win the election. 他处于赢得选举的有利地位。 |  She is a favored customer at that store. 她是那家商店的贵宾顾客。 | 近义词：preferred, privileged, favorite, advantageous | 反义词：disfavored, disliked, disadvantaged | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *favoured, *favored, past participle of favour, favouren (“to favor”), from Anglo-Norman favourir, faveurir, favrir, from Old French faverir, faveir (“to favor”),  from Latin favere (“to favor, be favorable to”), from Proto-Italic *faweō, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ew- (“to show favor, enjoy”).
