bat-the-breeze:/bæt ðə briːz/
|idiomatic phrase|
|闲聊，聊天，吹牛|
|To chat idly or casually, often about unimportant things.|
|We sat on the porch, batting the breeze about old times.|我们坐在门廊上，聊着过去的事情。|
|chat, gossip, shoot the breeze, chew the fat|
