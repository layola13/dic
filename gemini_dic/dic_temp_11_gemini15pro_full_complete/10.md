a cut above:/ə kʌt əˈbʌv/
|adj.|略胜一筹；更高一等；更胜一筹|Something that is "a cut above" is superior to or better than others in its class or category.  It implies a noticeable difference in quality or skill.|This restaurant is a cut above the rest in the area. 这家餐厅比该地区的其他餐厅都要好。|Synonyms: superior, better, finer, classier|Antonyms: inferior, worse, poorer, mediocre|Adjective
