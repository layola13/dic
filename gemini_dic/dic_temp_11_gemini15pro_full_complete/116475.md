materialising:/məˈtɪəriəlaɪzɪŋ/
v.
实现化; 出现; 突然出现
becoming real or appearing, especially suddenly
The opportunity he had been waiting for finally materialised. 他一直等待的机会终于出现了。
Synonyms: appearing, emerging, manifesting, taking shape
Antonyms: disappearing, vanishing, dematerialising
verb
