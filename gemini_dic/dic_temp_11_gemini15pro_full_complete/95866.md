incorruptive:/ˌɪnkəˈrʌptɪbl/
adjective
不腐败的，廉洁的，不朽的
not subject to decay or corruption; especially : not subject to moral decay
The incorruptive body of a saint. 圣徒不朽的遗体。
Synonyms: honest, upright, incorrupt, just
Antonyms: corrupt, corruptible, dishonest
词性: adjective
