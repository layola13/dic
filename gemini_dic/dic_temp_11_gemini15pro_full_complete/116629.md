matross:/məˈtrɒs/ | n. |炮手，炮兵；（旧时）炮兵助手|A soldier who assists the gunner in loading, firing, and sponging cannon.  A matross was a junior artilleryman who assisted the gunner in loading, firing and sponging the cannon.|The matross quickly loaded the cannon for the next volley. 炮手迅速装填大炮准备下一轮齐射。|Synonyms: artilleryman, gunner, cannoneer |Antonyms: civilian|noun
