concretionary:/ˌkɒnˈkriːʃənəri/ /ˌkɑːnˈkriːʃəneri/| adj.|凝固的；结石的；凝结成的|Formed by coalescing or concretion; tending to coalesce or concrete.  Characterized by concretions.|The concretionary limestone formed fascinating patterns. 凝固的石灰岩形成了迷人的图案。|近义词：coalesced, consolidated, solidified|反义词: dissolved, dispersed, fluid|词性: adjective

concretion:/kənˈkriːʃ(ə)n/ /kɑːnˈkriːʃən/ | n.|凝结; 凝结物; 结石|A hard solid mass formed by the local accumulation of matter, especially within the body or within a mass of sediment.|Kidney stones are a common type of concretion. 肾结石是一种常见的凝结物。|近义词：mass, lump, calculus|反义词: solution, dispersion|词性: noun
