imax:/ˈaɪmæks/| n. |巨幕电影，图像最大化|A motion picture format and a set of cinema projection standards creating a larger-than-life experience.  It is characterized by its larger film format and higher resolution, resulting in a more immersive and visually impressive movie experience.|We saw the new blockbuster in IMAX. 我们去看了巨幕电影版的最新大片。|近义词：large-format film, big screen|反义词:standard format|noun
