disced:/dɪst/ | v. | 用圆盘耙耕地；用唱片播放（音乐） | To cultivate (land) with a disc harrow. To play (music) using a record. |  They disced the field after harvesting the corn. 他们在收割玉米后用圆盘耙耕地。 | Synonyms: harrow, plow, cultivate, play | Antonyms: leave fallow, stop, pause | verb

disced:/dɪst/ | adj. |  (土地)用圆盘耙耕作过的；(音乐)用唱片播放的 | Cultivated with a disc harrow; played from a record. | The disced field was ready for planting. 用圆盘耙耕作过的土地已经准备好播种了。| Synonyms: plowed, cultivated | Antonyms: uncultivated | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From disc + -ed.  "Disc" ultimately comes from the Latin "discus," meaning "disk" or "quoit."
