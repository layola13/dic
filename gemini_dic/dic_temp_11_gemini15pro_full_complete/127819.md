nemathelminthes:/ˌnɛməθɛlˈmɪnθiːz/
n.
线虫动物门；线形动物门
A phylum of unsegmented, wormlike invertebrates with a complete digestive system and a body cavity called a pseudocoelom.  They are commonly known as roundworms.
例句：Nemathelminthes are a diverse group of animals found in various habitats. 线虫动物门是一个种类繁多的动物群体，分布在各种各样的栖息地。

例句：Some Nemathelminthes are parasitic, while others are free-living. 一些线虫动物是寄生的，而另一些则是自由生活的。
近义词：roundworms, nematodes
反义词: (无明显的反义词，因为这是一个生物分类名称)
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek nema, nematos "thread" + helmins, helminthos "worm".
