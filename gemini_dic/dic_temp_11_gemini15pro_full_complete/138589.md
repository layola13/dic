nubecula:/njuːˈbeɪkjʊlə/ /nuːˈbɛkjələ/ | n. |小云，云斑，星云|A small cloud; a cloud-like patch or spot, especially on the cornea.|例句：A nubecula can sometimes be seen in the eye after an injury.  受伤后有时会在眼睛里看到云斑。

例句：Astronomers observed a nubecula forming near the supernova. 天文学家观察到超新星附近形成了一个星云。|近义词：cloud, nebula, patch, spot|反义词:clear sky (晴空) (反义词概念上对应，并非严格的反义)|词性:noun
