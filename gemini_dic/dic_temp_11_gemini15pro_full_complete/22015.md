boin:/ˈbɔɪn/ | n. | 浮标; 航标 | A floating object anchored in water to mark a location, warn of danger, or indicate a channel. | The boin bobbed gently on the waves, marking the entrance to the harbor. 浮标在波浪上轻轻摇摆，标志着港口的入口。| The ship steered clear of the boin. 船避开了浮标。 | synonym: buoy, marker, beacon | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "obstacle" if the buoy marks a safe channel) | noun
