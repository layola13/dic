connoisseur:/ˌkɒnəˈsɜː(r)/ | n. |鉴赏家；行家|A person who has expert knowledge and keen discrimination, especially in matters of art, taste, or music.|The wine connoisseur carefully examined the vintage.  这位葡萄酒鉴赏家仔细地检查了年份。|近义词：expert, specialist, authority, aficionado|反义词：amateur, novice, beginner|noun

connoisseurs:/ˌkɒnəˈsɜː(r)z/ | n. |鉴赏家们；行家们|Plural form of connoisseur.|The connoisseurs gathered to appraise the rare paintings. 鉴赏家们聚集在一起评估这些稀有的画作。|近义词：experts, specialists, authorities, aficionados|反义词：amateurs, novices, beginners|noun
