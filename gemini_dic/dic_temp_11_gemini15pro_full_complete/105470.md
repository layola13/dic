killed:/kɪld/
verb
杀死，弄死
past tense and past participle of kill.  Caused the death of.
The hunter killed the deer. 猎人杀死了鹿。

近义词:slaughtered, murdered, assassinated, eliminated, exterminated
反义词:saved, rescued, revived, resuscitated
词性:verb (动词)
