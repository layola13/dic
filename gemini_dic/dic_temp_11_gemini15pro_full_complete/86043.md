healthinesses:/ˈhɛlθinəsɪz/
noun
健康状况
The state of being healthy; the degree to which someone or something is healthy.
The healthinesses of the children were carefully monitored.  孩子们们的健康状况受到了仔细的监控。
synonyms: health, well-being, fitness, soundness
antonyms: unhealthiness, sickness, illness, infirmity
词性: 名词


Etymology:
From healthiness + -es (plural suffix).  Healthiness is derived from healthy + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality). Healthy comes from Middle English helthe, from Old English hǣlth, from Proto-Germanic *hailiþō, from *hailaz ("whole").
