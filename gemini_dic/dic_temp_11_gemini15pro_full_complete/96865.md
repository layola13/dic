inexhaustedly:/ˌɪnɪɡˈzɔːstɪdli/
adverb
不知疲倦地，不屈不挠地
without tiring; with continuous energy or enthusiasm.
He worked inexhaustedly on the project for weeks.  他不知疲倦地在这个项目上工作了几个星期。
The volunteers worked inexhaustedly to help the victims of the flood. 志愿者们不知疲倦地帮助洪灾的受害者。
近义词: tirelessly, relentlessly, persistently
反义词: wearily, exhaustedly, languidly
词性: adverb

词源:
From in- (not) + exhaustedly (tiredly).  Exhaustedly comes from exhaust (to use up completely), from Latin exhaurire (to draw out, drain), from ex- (out) + haurire (to draw).
