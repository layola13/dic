interlend:/ˌɪntərˈlend/ | v. |相互借出; 馆际互借 | To loan (a book or other item) to another library or institution, or to borrow from another library or institution. | The two libraries interlend materials regularly. 这两家图书馆定期互相借阅资料。|  The university interlends books with other universities in the state. 这所大学与该州其他大学相互借阅图书。 |近义词：loan, borrow, share |反义词：hoard, keep, retain |词性: 动词

interlend:/ˌɪntərˈlend/ | n. |相互借出; 馆际互借 | The act or practice of lending or borrowing between libraries or institutions. | Interlend is an essential service for libraries with limited collections. 馆际互借对于馆藏有限的图书馆来说是一项重要的服务。 | The library's interlend service is very efficient.  这家图书馆的馆际互借服务非常高效。|近义词：loan, borrowing, sharing |反义词：hoarding, keeping, retaining |词性: 名词
