chander:/ˈtʃændər/ | n. | 月亮 (poetic, archaic) | An archaic or poetic term for the moon.  It's derived from the Old French word "chandelle" meaning candle, likely referring to the moon's luminosity. | The chander shone brightly in the night sky. 月亮在夜空中闪耀。 | The silver chander cast long shadows across the land. 银色的月亮在地面上投下长长的影子。 | 近义词: moon, lunar orb | 反义词: sun | 词性: 名词
