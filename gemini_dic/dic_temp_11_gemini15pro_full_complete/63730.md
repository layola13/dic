epibenthic:/ˌɛpɪˈbɛnθɪk/ | adj. | 底栖表层的；生活于水体底部表层的 | Living on the surface of the bottom sediments of a water body. |  The study focused on the epibenthic community structure in the coastal area.  这项研究集中在沿海地区的底栖表层群落结构。| Near synonyms: benthic, demersal | Antonym: pelagic | adjective

epibenthic:/ˌɛpɪˈbɛnθɪk/ | n. | 底栖表层生物 | An organism living on the surface of bottom sediments. |  Epibenthics are an important part of the marine food web. 底栖表层生物是海洋食物链的重要组成部分。 | Near synonyms: benthos, bottom-dweller | Antonym:  pelagic organism | noun
