depleting:/dɪˈpliːtɪŋ/
adjective
消耗的，减少的
reducing something in size or amount, especially supplies of energy, money, etc.
The world's natural resources are being rapidly depleted. 世界的自然资源正在迅速枯竭。
Synonyms: exhausting, diminishing, reducing
Antonyms: increasing, replenishing, augmenting
词性:形容词

deplete:/dɪˈpliːt/
verb
耗尽，使减少
reduce the amount of something, especially energy, money, etc.
The prolonged drought has severely depleted water reserves. 长期干旱严重耗尽了水资源。
Synonyms: exhaust, drain, consume
Antonyms: replenish, increase, augment
词性:动词
