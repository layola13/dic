antisyndication:/ˌæntɪˌsɪndɪˈkeɪʃən/| n.|反辛迪加，反联合组织化|The act or process of opposing or preventing syndication, especially in the context of media distribution or labor organization.|The newspaper implemented an antisyndication policy to prevent its content from being republished elsewhere.  该报实施了一项反联合政策，以防止其内容在其他地方转载。|Near synonyms:  nonsyndication, exclusivity|Antonyms: syndication, unionization|noun

antisyndication:/ˌæntɪˌsɪndɪˈkeɪʃən/| adj.|反辛迪加的，反联合组织化的|Relating to or characterized by opposition to syndication.|The antisyndication clause in the contract prevented the artist from selling their work to other distributors.  合同中的反辛迪加条款阻止了艺术家将他们的作品出售给其他经销商。|Near synonyms:  nonsyndicated, exclusive|Antonyms: syndicated, unionized|adjective
