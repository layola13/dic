beanfests:/ˈbiːnfɛsts/| n.|豆宴；聚会；欢乐的节日|A festive gathering, often characterized by plentiful food and a jovial atmosphere.  It often implies a casual and relaxed setting.  It can sometimes have a slightly humorous or ironic connotation, suggesting a gathering that is perhaps overly enthusiastic or abundant in food.|The annual chili cook-off was a real beanfest, with everyone bringing their best recipes.|近义词：feast, celebration, party, get-together|反义词:wake, funeral|名词
