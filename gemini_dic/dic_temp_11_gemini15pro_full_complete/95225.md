inbreeder:/ˈɪnˌbriːdər/| n. |近亲繁殖者；同系繁殖的动植物|A person or animal that is produced by inbreeding.  A person or animal whose parents are closely related.|例句：The high incidence of genetic defects in the isolated community suggested they were inbreeders.  这个孤立群体中遗传缺陷的高发病率表明他们是近亲繁殖者。

例句：The farmer was criticized for selling inbreeder pigs.  这个农民因为出售同系繁殖的猪而受到批评。|近义词： incestuous (adj. 乱伦的), endogamous (adj. 同族通婚的) |反义词: outbreeder, exogamous|词性:noun
