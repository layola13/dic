industrialist: /ɪnˈdʌstriəlɪst/ | n. | 工业家；实业家 | A person who owns or controls a large industrial enterprise.  A person whose wealth comes from the ownership of industrial businesses. |  Henry Ford was a prominent industrialist.  亨利·福特是一位杰出的工业家。

| Andrew Carnegie became a powerful industrialist in the steel industry.  安德鲁·卡内基成为了钢铁行业的巨头。 |Synonyms: manufacturer, tycoon, magnate, entrepreneur, business person | Antonyms: worker, laborer, employee | noun
