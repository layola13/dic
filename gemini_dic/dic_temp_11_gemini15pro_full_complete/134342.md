nonintellectually:/ˌnɒnɪntəˈlektʃuəli/
adverb
非智力地；不依靠智力地
Not in an intellectual way; without using or involving intelligence.
He approached the problem nonintellectually, relying on intuition and experience.  他非智力地处理这个问题，依靠直觉和经验。
She reacted nonintellectually to the news, bursting into tears. 她对这个消息的反应是非智力性的，突然哭了起来。
Synonyms: instinctively, intuitively, emotionally, viscerally
Antonyms: intellectually, rationally, logically
词性: 副词
