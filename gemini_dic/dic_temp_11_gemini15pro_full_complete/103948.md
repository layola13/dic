judiciality:/dʒuːˌdɪʃiˈælɪti/
n.
司法权; 公正性; 法官气质;
The quality of being judicial; the administration of justice.  A judge's judiciousness.
The judiciality of the decision was questioned by many.  许多人质疑该决定的司法公正性。
近义词：judiciousness, impartiality, fairness, objectivity
反义词：bias, partiality, injustice, unfairness
词性：名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin judicialis "of or belonging to a judge or court of justice"  + -ity (suffix forming nouns of quality or condition).
