overhurrying:/ˌoʊvərˈhɜːriɪŋ/| adj. | 过于匆忙的，过分仓促的 | Doing something too quickly and hastily, often resulting in mistakes or inefficiency. | He made several errors in his work due to overhurrying. 由于过于匆忙，他的工作中出现了几个错误。|The overhurrying chef burnt the sauce. 那个过于匆忙的厨师把酱汁烧焦了。|近义词：hasty, rushed, hurried |反义词：deliberate, careful, thorough| adjective

overhurry:/ˌoʊvərˈhɜːri/| v. | 使...过于匆忙，催促 | To cause someone to do something too quickly and hastily. | Don't overhurry the children; let them enjoy the process. 不要催促孩子们，让他们享受这个过程。| She overhurried the preparations and forgot several important items. 她过于匆忙地准备，忘记了几个重要的物品。|近义词：rush, hasten, hurry |反义词：delay, slow down, procrastinate| verb

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "hurry" (verb meaning to move or act with haste)
