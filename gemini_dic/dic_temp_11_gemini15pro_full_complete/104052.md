juliform:/ˈdʒuːlɪfɔːrm/| adj.|柳叶形的，细长的|Shaped like a catkin orament, slender and cylindrical. |The plant has juliform leaves. 这种植物有柳叶形的叶子。| The willow tree's branches are covered in juliform catkins. 柳树的枝条上覆盖着柳叶形的柳絮。| 近义词: slender, linear, filamentous | 反义词: broad, wide | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin "julus" (catkin) + "-form" (shaped like).
