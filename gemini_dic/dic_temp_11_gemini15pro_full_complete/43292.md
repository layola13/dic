craftworker:/ˈkræftˌwɜːrkər/ (US), /ˈkrɑːftˌwɜːkə(r)/ (UK)| n. |手工艺人，工匠|A person who makes things skillfully with their hands, especially as a job or hobby.  A skilled worker in a particular craft.|例句：The craftworker carefully carved the wood into a beautiful sculpture.  这位工匠小心翼翼地把木头雕刻成一尊美丽的雕塑。

例句：The market showcased the work of local craftworkers. 这个市场展示了当地手工艺人的作品。|近义词：artisan, craftsperson, handcraftsman, tradesman, skilled worker|反义词: amateur, unskilled worker|词性:noun
