over-teaches:/ˌoʊvərˈtiːtʃɪz/
verb
过度教学，过分讲解
To teach a subject or topic excessively, often beyond what is necessary or beneficial for the learner.  This can involve providing too much detail, repeating information unnecessarily, or using overly complex explanations.
The teacher over-teaches grammar, confusing the students with too many rules. 老师过度讲解语法，用太多的规则把学生弄糊涂了。
over-instructs, over-explains
under-teaches
v.
