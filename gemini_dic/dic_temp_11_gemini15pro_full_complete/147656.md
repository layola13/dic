overpopular:/ˌoʊvərˈpɑːpjələr/| adj.|过于流行的，过分受欢迎的|Excessively popular; more popular than is desirable or warranted.|The overpopular tourist destination was crowded and noisy.  这个过于热门的旅游景点拥挤嘈杂。|The band became overpopular, leading to sold-out concerts and inflated ticket prices.  这个乐队变得过于流行，导致演唱会门票售罄，票价飞涨。|近义词：too popular, excessively popular|反义词：unpopular, obscure|词性:adjective

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "popular" (adjective meaning "liked or admired by many people")
