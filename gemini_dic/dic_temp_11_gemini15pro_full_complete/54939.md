dispossessing:/dɪspəˈzɛsɪŋ/
adjective
剥夺的；使丧失所有权的
Depriving someone of land, property, or other possessions.
The dispossessing of farmers from their land caused widespread unrest.  农民被剥夺土地引起了广泛的骚乱。
The government's policy is dispossessing many poor people of their homes. 政府的政策正在剥夺许多穷人的家园。
近义词: expropriating, depriving, ousting
反义词: providing, granting, bestowing
词性: adjective

词源: From dispossess + -ing.  Dispossess comes from the Old French desposseder, from des- (expressing removal) + possesser (to possess).
