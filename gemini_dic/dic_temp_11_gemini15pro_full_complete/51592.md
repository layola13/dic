devaluing:/diːˈvæljuɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
贬值；降低…的价值
reducing or lessening the value or importance of something.
The company is accused of devaluing its employees' contributions.  这家公司被指控贬低员工的贡献。
They are devaluing the currency. 他们在使货币贬值。


近义词:depreciating, undermining, belittling, disparaging
反义词:appreciating, enhancing, valuing


词性:v.


词源:From devalue + -ing.  Devalue comes from French dévaluer, from dé- (expressing reversal) + valuer (to value).
