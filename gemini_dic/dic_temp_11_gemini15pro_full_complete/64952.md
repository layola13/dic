esthesiometer:/ˌɛsθiˈzɪəmɪtər/ /ˌɛsθiˈɑːmɪtər/ | n. | 触觉计，感觉计 | An instrument for measuring tactile sensitivity, especially the minimum distance at which two points of contact can be perceived as distinct. |  A doctor used an esthesiometer to test the patient's sensitivity to touch. 医生用触觉计测试病人的触觉敏感度。

The esthesiometer revealed a decreased sensitivity in the patient's fingertips. 触觉计显示病人指尖的敏感度下降。 | 近义词:  tactile sensor, caliper | 反义词:  None | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek aisthēsis (αἴσθησις, “sensation, perception”) + -meter (-meter, "measuring instrument").
