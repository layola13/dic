fillets:/ˈfɪlɪts/ (US), /ˈfɪleɪz/ (UK)| n.|鱼片，肉片，（尤指去骨的）肉|A boneless piece of meat or fish, especially the side of a fish.|例句：He ordered grilled salmon fillets for dinner. 他晚餐点了烤鲑鱼片。

fillets:| n.|菲力牛排|The tenderloin of beef, often considered the most tender cut.|例句：The restaurant is known for its perfectly cooked fillets. 这家餐厅以其完美烹制的菲力牛排而闻名。

fillets:| n.|（建筑的）带状装饰物|A narrow flat band, especially one used as a decorative molding in architecture.|例句：The arch was decorated with intricate fillets.  拱门装饰着复杂的带状物。


近义词:slices, strips, tenders, loins (for meat), pieces (general)
反义词:bone-in (for meat/fish)
词性:noun
