breeders:/ˈbriːdərz/
n.
饲养员，繁殖者
People who raise animals or plants, especially for specific characteristics.
Dog breeders carefully select the parents for their puppies.
狗饲养员会仔细挑选幼犬的父母。
Farmers are sometimes called livestock breeders.
农民有时被称为牲畜饲养员。
synonyms:raisers, cultivators, propagators
antonyms:n/a
noun


breeders:/ˈbriːdərz/
n.
(贬义) 繁殖者 (指人)
(derogatory) Used to refer to people who have children, often with negative connotations.
Some people criticize others for being "breeders" if they have a lot of children.
有些人会批评那些有很多孩子的人是“繁殖者”。
The term is considered offensive by many.
这个词被许多人认为是冒犯性的。
synonyms:parents (neutral), progenitors (formal)
antonyms:n/a
noun
