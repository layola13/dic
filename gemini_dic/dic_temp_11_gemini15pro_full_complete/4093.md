ailuroidea:/ˌaɪljʊˈrɔɪdiə/ | n. |猫型超科|A taxonomic superfamily within the order Carnivora, comprising the cats (Felidae) and related extinct families.|The extinct family Nimravidae is sometimes included in Ailuroidea.  已灭绝的猎猫科有时被归入猫型超科。|近义词: Feliformia |反义词: Canoidea |词性: noun

ailuroidea:/ˌaɪljʊˈrɔɪdiə/ | n. |猫型超科|A group of mammals including cats and their close relatives.|Ailuroidea and Canoidea are the two main branches of Carnivora. 猫型超科和犬型超科是食肉目的两个主要分支。|近义词: Feliformia |反义词: Canoidea |词性: noun
