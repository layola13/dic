misentry:/ˌmɪsˈentri/ | n. | 错误输入，误录 | An incorrect entry of data, especially into a computer or database. | Example:  A misentry in the database caused the system to crash. 数据库中的错误输入导致系统崩溃。

Example: The misentry on the form led to a delay in processing. 表格上的误录导致处理延误。 | 近义词: error, mistake, typo, inaccuracy | 反义词: correct entry, accurate entry | 词性: noun | 词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning "wrong" or "bad") + "entry" (from Old French "entree," meaning "entrance" or "act of entering")
