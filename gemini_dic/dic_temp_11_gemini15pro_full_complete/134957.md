nonnationalism:/ˌnɒnˈnæʃənəlɪzəm/ | n. |非民族主义，超越民族主义| The philosophy of nonnationalism emphasizes global citizenship and cooperation over national interests.  非民族主义哲学强调全球公民意识和合作，而不是国家利益。|例句：The rise of nonnationalism is a reaction against the excesses of nationalism. 非民族主义的兴起是对民族主义过度行为的一种反弹。

|例句：Some scholars argue that nonnationalism is a necessary step towards world peace. 一些学者认为，非民族主义是迈向世界和平的必要步骤。|近义词：globalism, internationalism, cosmopolitanism |反义词: nationalism, patriotism|词性:名词
