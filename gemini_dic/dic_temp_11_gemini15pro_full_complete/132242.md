nondecision:/ˌnɒndɪˈsɪʒən/ |n.| 不作为；未作决定 |A failure to make a decision; inaction.| The government's nondecision on the matter has led to further confusion. 政府在这个问题上的不作为导致了进一步的混乱.| inaction, indecision | decision, action | noun


Etymology->
From non- + decision.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of." "Decision" comes from the Old French word "decision" and ultimately from the Latin "decidere" meaning "to cut off" or "to decide."
