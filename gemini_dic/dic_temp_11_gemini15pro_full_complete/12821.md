assizes:/əˈsaɪzɪz/
noun
巡回审判;  (旧时)定期召开的审判会议
A periodic court session in a particular place for the trial of cases. Historically, the assizes were held in English counties two or three times a year.
The judge presided over the assizes. 法官主持巡回审判。
synonyms: court, trial, hearing, tribunal
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider concepts like "freedom," "release," or "pardon" as loosely antonymous.)
n.

assizes:/əˈsaɪzɪz/
noun
(旧时)面包等的重量或尺寸标准
An old standard for the weight or size of bread or other goods.
The assizes of bread were strictly regulated. 面包的重量标准受到严格监管。
synonyms: standard, measure, gauge
antonyms: (No direct antonym)
n.
