half-elizabethan:/ˌhɑːf ɪˌlɪzəˈbiːθən/
| adj. | 半伊丽莎白式的 |  Relating to or characteristic of the latter half of the reign of Queen Elizabeth I of England (approximately 1580-1603), often used in reference to architectural style. | The house featured several half-Elizabethan architectural details, such as the mullioned windows and decorative gables. 这座房子有几个半伊丽莎白时代的建筑细节，比如竖框窗和装饰性山墙。 | The half-Elizabethan manor house was charming.  这座半伊丽莎白式的庄园别墅很迷人。|  Late Elizabethan, Jacobean | Early Elizabethan | adjective
