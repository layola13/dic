giantry: /ˈɡaɪəntri/ | n. | 高架移动结构；龙门架；高大的框架结构 | A tall framework, typically of steel, supporting a crane, railroad, or other heavy equipment. | The giantry supporting the crane loomed over the shipyard.  龙门架支撑着起重机，耸立在造船厂的上空。|  The train passed under a massive metal giantry. 火车从一个巨大的金属高架桥下驶过。 | synonyms: framework, scaffolding, gantry, superstructure | antonyms: foundation, base | noun
