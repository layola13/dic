androspore:/ˈændrəˌspɔːr/
noun
雄孢子
A motile spore produced by the male gametophyte of certain algae and fungi.
Example: The androspores released from the antheridium swim towards the oogonium. 雄配子体释放的雄孢子游向卵囊。
Example:  The researchers observed the androspores under a microscope to study their motility.  研究人员在显微镜下观察雄孢子以研究它们的运动能力。

Synonym:  spermatia (in some contexts)
Antonym:  oospore, gynospore
