in-luck:/ɪn ˈlʌk/| adj.|幸运的；交好运的|Being in a fortunate or lucky situation. Having good luck at a particular time.|Example: "You're in luck! We have one table left." 你真幸运！我们只剩一张桌子了。|Example: "I was in luck, I found the perfect dress on sale." 我很幸运，我找到了打折的完美连衣裙。|近义词：fortunate, lucky, blessed|反义词:unlucky, unfortunate, out of luck|词性:形容词
Etymology:
"in" (Old English "in") + "luck" (Middle Dutch "luc", chance, fortune)
