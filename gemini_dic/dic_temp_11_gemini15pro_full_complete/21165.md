bloatednesses:/ˈbloʊtɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
膨胀；肿胀；臃肿
The state of being bloated or swollen.
The bloatednesses of his stomach was evident after the huge meal.  他巨大的肚子在吃完大餐后很明显。
After the marathon, my feet suffered from extreme bloatednesses. 马拉松结束后，我的脚肿得很厉害。
近义词: swelling, distension, inflation
反义词: flatness, thinness
