n.
富豪(尤指过去在印度发了财的英国人)的地位或财富;显赫的社会地位
The wealth and status of a nabob, especially a person who has returned to Europe after making a fortune in India; a position of great wealth and status.
He lived a life of nabobship after returning from India. 他从印度回来后过着富豪般的生活。
Synonyms: wealth, riches, affluence, opulence, luxury
Antonyms: poverty, destitution, impoverishment
