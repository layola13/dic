decorrelated:/ˌdiːkɔːrəˈleɪtɪd/
adjective
去相关的，不相关的
Having removed correlation; statistically independent.
The variables were decorrelated to eliminate spurious associations.  这些变量被去相关以消除虚假关联。
The decorrelated data showed no significant relationship. 去相关后的数据显示没有显著关系。
近义词: independent, uncorrelated, orthogonal
反义词: correlated, related, dependent
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From de- + correlate + -ed.  "De-" is a prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse." "Correlate" comes from the Latin "com-" (together) and "relatus" (related).
