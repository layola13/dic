gasman:/ˈɡæsmən/ (US), /ˈɡæsmæn/ (UK)| n. |煤气工人，天然气工人|A person who works for a gas company, installing, repairing, or reading gas meters. |The gasman came to fix the leak. 煤气工人来修理泄漏了。|Synonyms: gas fitter, gas engineer|Antonyms: N/A|noun
