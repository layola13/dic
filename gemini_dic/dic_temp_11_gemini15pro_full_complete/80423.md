gonad:/ˈɡoʊnæd/ (US), /ˈɡɒnæd/ (UK)| n. |性腺；生殖腺|An organ that produces gametes; a testis or ovary.  A gonad is a reproductive gland that produces gametes (sex cells). In males, the gonads are the testes, which produce sperm. In females, the gonads are the ovaries, which produce eggs (ova).|The doctor examined the patient's gonads. 医生检查了病人的性腺。|Synonyms: reproductive gland, testis (male), ovary (female)|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a biological organ)|noun
