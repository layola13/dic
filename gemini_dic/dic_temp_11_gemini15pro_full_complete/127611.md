neediness:/ˈniːdinəs/| n.|需要；贫穷；缺乏|The state of being in need; poverty or lack of something essential.|Her constant neediness for reassurance was exhausting. 她不断需要 reassurance，这让人筋疲力尽。|近义词：dependence, insecurity, poverty|反义词:independence, security, wealth|词性:名词

词源: From "needy" + "-ness."  "Needy" comes from the Old English "nēodig," meaning "compelled by necessity," related to "nēd," meaning "need."  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns expressing state or quality.
