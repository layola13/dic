chipping:/ˈtʃɪpɪŋ/| n. v.|碎屑，削，凿，切|The act of removing small pieces from a surface. Also, the small pieces themselves.|例句：He was chipping away at the ice on the windshield. 他正在清除挡风玻璃上的冰碴。|近义词：flaking, splintering, shredding|反义词: mending, repairing|词性:noun, verb
chipping:/ˈtʃɪpɪŋ/| adj.|破碎的，有缺口的|Characterized by having small pieces removed.|例句：The paint on the wall was chipping. 墙上的油漆正在剥落。|近义词：flaky, fragmented|反义词: smooth, intact|词性:adjective
