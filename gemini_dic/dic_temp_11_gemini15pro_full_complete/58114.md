dropper:/ˈdrɑːpər/ (US), /ˈdrɒpə(r)/ (UK)| n.|滴管；滴注器；投弹手；（非正式）告密者|A small tube with a rubber bulb at one end, for drawing in and releasing liquids a drop at a time.  Also, a person who drops something, especially bombs from an aircraft, or informally, someone who gives information to the police or authorities.|例句：She used a dropper to administer the medicine to the baby. 她用滴管给婴儿喂药。

例句：The bomber was a skilled dropper.  这名轰炸机飞行员是个熟练的投弹手。


近义词：pipette, dispenser, informer, snitch|反义词:|词性:noun

dropper:/ˈdrɑːpər/ (US), /ˈdrɒpə(r)/ (UK)| n.|放置者；使落下的人或物|Someone or something that drops or lets something fall.|例句：The dropper accidentally spilled the paint.  那个不小心使油漆掉落的人把油漆洒了。

例句：The automatic dropper released the seeds at regular intervals. 自动播种机定期播撒种子。

近义词：releaser|反义词:|词性:noun
