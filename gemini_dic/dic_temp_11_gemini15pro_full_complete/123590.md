montane:/ˈmɒnˌteɪn/ /mɑːnˈteɪn/| adj.|山地的，山区的|Of or inhabiting mountain areas, especially those above the tree line.|例句：Montane forests are found at high elevations. 山地森林分布在高海拔地区。

例句：The montane vole is adapted to cold climates. 山地田鼠适应寒冷的气候。|近义词：mountainous, alpine, upland|反义词：lowland|词性：形容词
