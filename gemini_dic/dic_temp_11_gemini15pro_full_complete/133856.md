nongraphical:/ˌnɒnˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
非图形的；非图解的
Not consisting of, relating to, or using graphics; not represented graphically.
The report contained a large amount of nongraphical data.  这份报告包含了大量的非图形数据。
The system uses a nongraphical user interface.  该系统使用非图形用户界面。
近义词:text-based, nonvisual
反义词:graphical, visual
词性:adjective
词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "graphical" (from "graphic," ultimately from Greek "graphikos," meaning "of or for writing, drawing, painting").
