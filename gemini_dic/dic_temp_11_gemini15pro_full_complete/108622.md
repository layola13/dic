leaderly:/ˈliːdəli/
adjective
领导的；有领导才能的；像领导者的
Demonstrates the qualities and abilities of a leader; exhibiting leadership.
She handled the crisis in a very leaderly manner. 她以非常有领导力的方式处理了这场危机。

The CEO's leaderly vision inspired the entire company. 首席执行官富有领导力的远见激励了整个公司。

近义词: leading, managerial, commanding, authoritative
反义词:  submissive, passive, unassertive
词性: adjective


词源: From leader + -ly.  "Leader" comes from the Old English "lædere," meaning "one who leads."  The suffix "-ly" transforms nouns into adjectives.
