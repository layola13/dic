miltwaste:/ˈmɪltˌweɪst/
n.
鱼白; 精液;  (植物的)乳汁
Semen of a male fish, especially when shed during spawning. Also, sometimes used metaphorically for semen in general or plant sap.
The miltwaste clouded the water around the spawning salmon.  鱼白使产卵鲑鱼周围的水变得浑浊。
Synonym:  fish sperm, roe (for fish eggs), semen (metaphorical)
Antonym:  ova (fish eggs)
n.

miltwaste:/ˈmɪltˌweɪst/
n.
鱼白; 精液; (植物的)乳汁
A poetic or archaic term for the seminal fluid of a male fish, or, by extension, of any male creature. Can also refer to the milky sap of plants.
He wiped the miltwaste from his hands after gutting the fish. 他把鱼的精液从手上擦掉。
Synonym:  semen, sperm, fish sperm
Antonym: None (specific to male reproductive fluid)
n.
