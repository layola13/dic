mustache:/ˈmʌstæʃ/ (US), /məˈstɑːʃ/ (UK)| n.|胡子,髭|A growth of hair on the upper lip.|He twirled his mustache thoughtfully. 他若有所思地捻着他的胡子。|Synonyms: moustache (UK spelling), whiskers, beard (more general)|Antonyms: clean-shaven|noun
