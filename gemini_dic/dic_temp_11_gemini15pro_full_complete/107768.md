lapidescent:/ˌlæpɪˈdɛsənt/
adjective
变成石头的；石化的
becoming stony; petrifying; tending to petrify.
The lapidescent spring water slowly formed stalactites in the cave. 石化的泉水慢慢在洞穴中形成了钟乳石。
The ancient tree's sap had become lapidescent over millennia. 古树的汁液在几千年里变成了石头。
近义词:petrifying, fossilizing, hardening
反义词:melting, softening, dissolving
词性:adjective

词源: From Latin "lapidescere" (to become stone), from "lapis" (stone) + "-escere" (incipient verb suffix).
