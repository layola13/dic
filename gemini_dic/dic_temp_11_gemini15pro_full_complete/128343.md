neutralist:/ˈnuːtrəlɪst/ | n. adj. | 中立主义者，中立国的 | A person or country that does not support or help either side in a conflict, war, etc. |  Example:  Switzerland remained a neutralist throughout World War II.  瑞士在整个二战期间保持中立。| Synonym: nonaligned, impartial, unbiased | Antonym: partisan, belligerent, biased | noun

neutralist:/ˈnuːtrəlɪst/ | adj. | 中立主义的 | Relating to a policy or person who does not support or help either side in a conflict, war, etc. | Example:  The country adopted a neutralist stance in the dispute. 该国在争端中采取了中立立场。| Synonym: nonaligned, impartial, unbiased | Antonym: partisan, belligerent, biased | adjective
