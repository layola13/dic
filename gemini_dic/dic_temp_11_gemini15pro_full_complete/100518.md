interviewer:/ˈɪntərˌvjuːər/| n.|面试官；采访者|A person who conducts an interview.|The interviewer asked me about my previous experience. 面试官问我之前的经验。| interviewer asked insightful questions.  面试官问了很有见地的问题。|Synonym:  questioner, examiner |Antonym: interviewee, applicant|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From interview + -er.  "Interview" comes from French "entrevue," meaning "a meeting between people."  Ultimately, from "entre-" (between) and "voir" (to see).
