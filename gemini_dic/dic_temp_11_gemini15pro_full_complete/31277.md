A system of pastoral or spiritual care provided to a particular group of people (e.g., in a hospital, prison, or military unit).  | He was offered the chapellany at the local hospital.  他获得了当地医院的牧师职位。 The prison has a dedicated chapellany service for inmates of all faiths. 这所监狱为所有信仰的囚犯提供专门的牧师服务。| synonyms: chaplaincy, pastorate, ministry | antonyms: N/A | noun
