mantlet:/ˈmæntlɪt/| n.|1. 遮蔽物，护墙; 2. (尤指旧时攻城用的) 撞车棚, 防盾;|A mantlet is a protective screen or covering, especially one used in siege warfare to protect soldiers or equipment.|The soldiers advanced behind a mantlet. 士兵们躲在护墙后面前进。| The battering ram was protected by a sturdy mantlet.  攻城槌被坚固的撞车棚保护着。|synonyms: shield, screen, cover, protection|antonyms: exposure, vulnerability|noun

mantlet:/ˈmæntlɪt/| n.|1. (动物的)覆盖物，外套，披肩;|The bird puffed out its mantlet of feathers. 鸟儿鼓起了它的羽毛外套。|The horse's mantlet glistened in the sun. 马的披肩在阳光下闪闪发光。|synonyms: cloak, covering, mantle|antonyms:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 exposed) |noun
