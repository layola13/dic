grainless:/ˈɡreɪnlɪs/
adj.
无颗粒的，无谷粒的
Without grains; having no grains.
The baker experimented with grainless bread made from almond flour.  面包师尝试用杏仁粉制作无谷粒面包。
The bird ate a grainless diet of insects. 这只鸟以无谷粒的昆虫为食。

近义词:smooth, fine-textured
反义词:grainy, coarse

词性:adjective
