inconditionate: /ˌɪnkənˈdɪʃənət/
adjective
无条件的;绝对的
Unconditional; absolute; not subject to any conditions.
He offered his inconditionate support. 他提供了无条件的支持。
近义词: unconditional, absolute, unreserved, unqualified
反义词: conditional, qualified, restricted, limited
词性: adjective
词源: From in- "not" + conditional + -ate (suffix forming adjectives).
