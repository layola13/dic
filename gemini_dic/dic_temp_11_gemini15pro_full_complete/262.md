abductee:/ˌæbdʌkˈtiː/
n.
被绑架者，被劫持者
A person who has been abducted.
The abductee was released unharmed after three days.  被绑架者三天后被安全释放。
The police are interviewing the abductee to gather information about the kidnappers. 警方正在询问被绑架者，以收集有关绑匪的信息。
近义词:captive, hostage
反义词:kidnapper, abductor
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From abduct + -ee.  Abduct comes from the Latin word *abducere*, meaning "to lead away," from *ab-* ("away from") + *ducere* ("to lead").  The suffix *-ee* denotes someone who is the recipient or object of the action denoted by the verb.
