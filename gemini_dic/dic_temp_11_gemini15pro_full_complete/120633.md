mis-evaluating:/ˌmɪsɪˈvæljueɪtɪŋ/

mis-evaluate:/ˌmɪsɪˈvæljueɪt/
|v.|错误评估，评估不当|We mis-evaluated his abilities and placed him in the wrong position. 我们错误地评估了他的能力，把他放在了错误的职位上。|The team mis-evaluated the risks involved in the project. 该团队错误地评估了项目中的风险。|misjudge, miscalculate|correctly evaluate, accurately assess|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
"mis-" (Old English, from Germanic *missa-) meaning "wrongly," "badly," or "amiss."
"-evaluate" (from Latin "ex-" meaning "out" + "valere" meaning "be strong" or "be worth") meaning "to determine the value or worth of."
