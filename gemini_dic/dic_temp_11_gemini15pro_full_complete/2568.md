admittedly:/ədˈmɪtɪdli/| adv.|无可否认地；诚然；确实|Used to concede a point in an argument, while not changing one's overall standpoint.  It indicates a reluctant or qualified acceptance of something as true.|Admittedly, I could have tried harder. 诚然，我本可以更加努力。|Synonyms: indeed, truly, certainly, confessedly, avowedly|Antonyms: doubtfully, questionably|Adverb
