lanciferous:/lænˈsɪfərəs/
adjective
持矛的；似矛的
bearing or armed with a lance or lances; shaped like a lance.
The lanciferous Roman soldiers marched towards the enemy.  持矛的罗马士兵向敌人进军。
The lanciferous leaves of the yucca plant are quite sharp. 丝兰植物的矛状叶子非常锋利。
lance-shaped, spear-like, lanceolate
