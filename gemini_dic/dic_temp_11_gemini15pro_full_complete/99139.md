intercept:/ˈɪntərsept/| n. vt.|截距，拦截，截取|The intercept of a graph is the point where the graph crosses an axis. |The police intercepted a message from the criminals. 警方截获了罪犯的信息。|近义词：interception, catch, seize|反义词:miss, release|词性:名词, 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *interceptus, past participle of intercipere "to intercept," from inter- "between" + capere "to take."
