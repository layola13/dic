hyperintellectually:/ˌhaɪpərˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəli/
adv.
以过度理智的方式，过于理性地
in an excessively intellectual manner; with excessive concern for intellectual matters
He analyzed the situation hyperintellectually, missing the emotional nuances. 他过度理性地分析了情况，忽略了情感上的细微差别。
近义词:  over-intellectually, excessively intellectually
反义词:  emotionally, intuitively
词性: adverb
