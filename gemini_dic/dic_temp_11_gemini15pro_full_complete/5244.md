allocator:/ˈæləkeɪtər/| n.|分配器，配置器|An allocator is a component of software that manages the allocation and deallocation of memory or other resources.  It ensures that resources are efficiently used and that there are no memory leaks or other issues.|The allocator manages the memory used by the program. 分配器管理程序使用的内存。|Memory allocators can have a significant impact on performance. 内存分配器会对性能产生重大影响。|近义词:manager, distributor|反义词:deallocator|词性:noun
