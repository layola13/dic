hydrophore:/ˈhaɪdrəfɔːr/ | n. |水合载体；储水器；输水管 | A device or structure that collects, stores, or conveys water. | Example 1: The old hydrophore in the basement needed replacing.  地下室里的旧储水器需要更换了。

Example 2: The city's water supply relies on a network of hydrophores.  这座城市的供水依赖于输水管网。 | Synonyms: cistern, reservoir, water tank, aqueduct | Antonyms: drain, outlet | noun

hydrophore:/ˈhaɪdrəfɔːr/ | adj. |  储水的；输水的|  Relating to or characteristic of a hydrophore. | Example 1: The hydrophore system was damaged in the storm. 输水系统在暴风雨中受损了。

Example 2: Hydrophore pressure ensures a steady flow of water. 储水压力确保了稳定的水流。| Synonyms: water-bearing, water-carrying | Antonyms:  dehydrating, drying | adjective
