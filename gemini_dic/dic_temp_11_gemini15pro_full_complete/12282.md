asceticism:/əˈsɛtɪsɪzəm/
n.|禁欲主义，苦行主义|Severe self-discipline and abstention from all forms of indulgence, typically for religious reasons.|He practiced asceticism by living in a cave and eating only fruits and nuts. 他过着苦行僧般的生活，住在洞穴里，只吃水果和坚果。|近义词：austerity, self-denial, abstinence|反义词：hedonism, indulgence, luxury|词性:名词

Etymology: From Late Latin *ascetismus*, from Greek *askēsis* "exercise, training," especially of athletes or soldiers, then "ascetic practice."  Ultimately from Greek *askein* "to exercise, work, practice."
