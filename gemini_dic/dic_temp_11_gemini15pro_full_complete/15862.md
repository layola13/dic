ballgame:/ˈbɔːlɡeɪm/| n.|球赛；局势；事态|A game played with a ball, especially baseball.  Figuratively, it refers to a situation or set of circumstances.|例句：The Yankees won the ballgame. 洋基队赢得了这场球赛。

例句：After the CEO resigned, it was a whole new ballgame. 首席执行官辞职后，情况完全不同了。|近义词：game, match, contest, competition|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ballgame:(US slang) /ˈbɔːlɡeɪm/| n.|完全不同的情况; 另一个层面|A completely different situation; another level or category.|例句：Negotiating a deal with them is a whole different ballgame. 和他们谈判完全是另一回事。

例句：Learning to drive a manual car is a whole new ballgame. 学开手动挡汽车完全是另一回事。|近义词：situation, state of affairs, circumstances|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
