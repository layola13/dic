brined:/braɪnd/
adjective
盐渍的; 卤水的
preserved in salty water
例句：The brined olives were a perfect accompaniment to the cheese. 盐渍橄榄是奶酪的完美搭配。

例句：Brined turkey is known for its moist and flavorful meat. 盐水腌制的火鸡肉质鲜嫩多汁。
近义词：pickled, salted, cured
反义词：fresh, unsalted
词性：形容词
