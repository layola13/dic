hypersalinity:/ˌhaɪpərˌsəˈlɪnɪti/ | n. |高盐度，超盐度 | Excessive salinity; a concentration of dissolved salts greater than that of normal seawater. | The hypersalinity of the Dead Sea makes it difficult for most organisms to survive. 死海的高盐度使得大多数生物难以生存。| hypersalination, excessive salinity | hyposalinity | noun
