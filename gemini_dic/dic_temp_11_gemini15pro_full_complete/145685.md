overcopiousness:/ˌōvərˈkōpiəsnəs/
|n.| 过于丰富；冗长；过多|The overcopiousness of the writer's style made the book tedious to read.  这位作家的风格过于冗长，使这本书读起来很乏味。|Excessiveness, prolixity, superfluity, redundancy, verbosity|Sparsity, brevity, conciseness, terseness|Noun

Etymology->
From over + copiousness.  "Over-" denotes "excessive" or "too much," and "copiousness" refers to abundance or plenty. Therefore, "overcopiousness" means an excessive abundance or over-abundance.
