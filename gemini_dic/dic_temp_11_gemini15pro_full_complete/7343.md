anhungered:/ænˈhʌŋɡərd/
adjective
渴望的，饥饿的
Feeling a powerful desire or need, especially for something non-physical; intensely eager.  Often used in a spiritual or emotional context, but can also refer to physical hunger.
The anhungered masses yearned for freedom.  渴望自由的民众渴望自由。
She was anhungered for knowledge and experience. 她渴望知识和经验。
近义词:hungry, yearning, craving, desirous, eager, avid
反义词:sated, satisfied, apathetic, indifferent
词性:adjective

词源: From Old English "ofhungrod," past participle of "ofhingrian," meaning "to hunger after, to be hungry." "Of-" is an intensive prefix.
