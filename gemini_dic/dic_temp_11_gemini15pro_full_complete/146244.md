overexquisite:/ˌoʊvərˈɛkskwɪzɪt/ | adj. | 过于精致的，过分精美的 | Excessively exquisite; too elaborate or refined.  Characterized by an overabundance of refinement or delicacy, often to the point of being excessive or artificial.  | The overexquisite decorations felt somewhat ostentatious.  过于精致的装饰让人感觉有些炫耀。|  The cake was overexquisite, almost too beautiful to eat.  这个蛋糕过于精致了，几乎美得让人不忍心吃。 | 近义词: overrefined, overelaborate, fussy | 反义词: simple, plain, austere | 词性: adjective

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "exquisite" (from Latin "exquisitus," past participle of "exquirere," meaning "to search out," hence "carefully chosen," "refined")
