boastfulness:/ˈboʊstfəlnəs/
n.
自夸；夸耀；吹嘘
The quality of being boastful; excessive pride and self-satisfaction.
His boastfulness made him unpopular. 他的自夸让他不受欢迎。
近义词：arrogance, vanity, conceit, pride
反义词：humility, modesty
词性: noun

Etymology:
From boastful + -ness.  Boastful comes from Middle English *bostful, from boast (verb) + -ful.  Boast ultimately derives from Old North French boster.  The suffix -ness forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
