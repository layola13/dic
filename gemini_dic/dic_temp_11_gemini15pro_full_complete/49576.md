dementias:/dɪˈmɛnʃəz/
n.
痴呆症（复数）
A group of related syndromes resulting in progressive loss of cognitive function, especially memory, judgment, and abstract thought, but also affecting personality and behavior.
Dementias are often caused by damage to brain cells. 痴呆症通常是由脑细胞损伤引起的。
Alzheimer's disease and vascular dementia are the most common types of dementias. 阿尔茨海默病和血管性痴呆是最常见的痴呆症类型。
近义词: cognitive decline, mental deterioration
反义词: mental acuity, cognitive health
词性: noun (plural)
