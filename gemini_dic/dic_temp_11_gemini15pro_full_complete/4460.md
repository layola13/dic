albatross:/ˈæl.bə.trɒs/ (US) /ˈæl.bə.trɒs/ (UK)| n. |信天翁；沉重的负担；batross|A large seabird with long narrow wings.  Figuratively, an albatross is a burden or obstacle. |The oil spill was an albatross around the company's neck.|近义词：burden, obstacle, impediment, handicap|反义词:advantage, asset, help, boon|词性:名词

albatross:/ˈæl.bə.trɒs/ (US) /ˈæl.bə.trɒs/ (UK)| n. |信天翁|A large web-footed seabird with a hooked bill and long narrow wings, found chiefly in the southern oceans. |The majestic albatross soared effortlessly through the sky.|近义词：seabird, pelagic bird|反义词: (No direct antonym for the animal itself)|词性:名词
