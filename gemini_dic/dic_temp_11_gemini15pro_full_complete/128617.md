nickel:/ˈnɪkəl/| n. |镍; (美国和加拿大的)五分镍币|A hard, silvery-white metallic element, resistant to corrosion and used in alloys. A five-cent coin of the US and Canada.|例句：The US five-cent coin is called a nickel. 美国的五分硬币被称为镍币。

例句：Nickel is a key component in stainless steel. 镍是不锈钢中的关键成分。

近义词：metal, coin (in context)|反义词: None|词性:noun


nickel:/ˈnɪkəl/| vt. |镀镍于|To plate with nickel.|例句：The car parts were nickeled for increased durability. 汽车零件镀镍以提高耐用性。

例句：They nickeled the steel to prevent rust. 他们在钢上镀镍以防止生锈。

近义词：plate, coat|反义词: None|词性:verb
