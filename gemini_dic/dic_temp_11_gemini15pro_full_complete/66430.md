exegetical:/ˌɛksɪˈdʒɛtɪkəl/| adj.|释经的，解释的|Relating to or providing critical explanation or interpretation of a text, especially of scripture.|The professor's exegetical commentary on the biblical passage was insightful. 教授对这段圣经经文的释经评论很有见地。|Synonyms: interpretive, explanatory, hermeneutical, analytical|Antonyms: uncritical, superficial, simplistic|adjective
