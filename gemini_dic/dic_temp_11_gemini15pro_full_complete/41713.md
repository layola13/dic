corroborator:/kəˈrɒbəreɪtər/ | n. | 证实者；确证者 |  A person or thing that corroborates something. | The witness acted as a corroborator for the defendant's alibi.  该证人证实了被告的不在场证明。| synonyms: confirmer, validator, authenticator, substantiator | antonyms: denier, contradictor, refuter | noun

corroborate:/kəˈrɒbəreɪt/ | v. | 证实；确证 | To confirm or give support to (a statement, theory, or finding). |  The evidence corroborated his account of the accident. 证据证实了他对事故的描述。| synonyms: confirm, verify, validate, authenticate, substantiate | antonyms: deny, contradict, refute | verb
