caballero:/ˌkɑːbəˈljɛroʊ/ (US), /ˌkæbəˈljɛəroʊ/ (UK)| n.|绅士；骑士；先生|A Spanish word meaning gentleman, knight, or sir. It is used as a term of respect and courtesy.|例句：Buenos días, caballero.  Good morning, sir.|近义词：gentleman, sir, lord|反义词：villain, knave|名词
