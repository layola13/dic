coping:/ˈkəʊpɪŋ/ (British) /ˈkoʊpɪŋ/ (American)| n.|应对，处理，克服|the act of dealing successfully with a difficult situation|例句：Her coping mechanisms are admirable. 她的应对机制令人钦佩。|近义词：managing, handling, dealing with, tackling|反义词:succumbing, yielding|noun


coping:/ˈkəʊpɪŋ/ (British) /ˈkoʊpɪŋ/ (American)| adj.|应对的，处理的|used to describe something that helps you deal with a difficult situation|例句：Coping strategies are important during stressful times.  在压力大的时期，应对策略很重要。|近义词：adaptive, managing, handling|反义词:failing, ineffective|adjective
