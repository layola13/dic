campiest:/ˈkæmpɪɪst/
adjective
最做作的，最夸张的
Exaggeratedly theatrical or artificial in manner or style; camp to a marked degree.
Her performance was the campiest I've ever seen. 她的表演是我见过的最做作的。
The movie was full of campiest costumes and dialogue. 这部电影充满了夸张的服装和对白。
近义词: theatrical, exaggerated, affected, ostentatious, flamboyant
反义词: understated, subtle, natural, restrained, simple
词性: adjective

词源:
Camp (as in exaggeratedly theatrical) has uncertain origins, but is likely related to the French term "se camper," meaning "to pose in an exaggerated fashion." "Campiest" is the superlative form of "camp," adding the suffix "-iest."
