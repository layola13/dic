mollitude:/ˈmɒlɪtjuːd/ (US) /ˈmɒlɪtʃuːd/ (UK)| n.|温柔，温和，柔情，软弱|A quality of softness, tenderness, or gentle mildness; also, effeminacy or weakness.|Her mollitude made her incapable of making tough decisions. 她的温柔使她无法做出艰难的决定。|Synonyms: gentleness, mildness, softness, tenderness, meekness, leniency|Antonyms: harshness, severity, firmness, strength, hardness|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *mollitūdō*, from mollis ("soft") + -itūdō ("-itude").
