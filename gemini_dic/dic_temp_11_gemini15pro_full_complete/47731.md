decagons:/ˈdekəɡɑːnz/
n.
十边形，十角形
A decagon is a polygon with ten sides and ten angles.
A regular decagon has all sides and angles equal.  一个正十边形的所有边和角都相等。
The fortress was built in the shape of a decagon.  这座堡垒建成十边形。
近义词:ten-sided polygon
反义词:(no direct antonym)
词性:noun
