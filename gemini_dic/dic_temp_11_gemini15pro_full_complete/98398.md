insoluble:/ɪnˈsɒljʊbl/ (UK) /ɪnˈsɑːljəbl/ (US)| adj. |不溶的；不能解决的|Not able to be dissolved in a liquid; impossible to solve.|
例句：Sand is insoluble in water. 沙子不溶于水。

例句：The problem seemed insoluble. 这个问题似乎无法解决。

近义词：indissoluble, unsolvable, irresolvable|
反义词：soluble, solvable, resolvable|
词性：形容词

insolubles:/ɪnˈsɒljʊblz/ (UK) /ɪnˈsɑːljəblz/ (US)| n. |不溶物|Substances that cannot be dissolved in a particular liquid.|
例句：The insolubles were filtered out of the solution. 不溶物从溶液中过滤出来了。

例句：The experiment produced a number of insolubles. 实验产生了一些不溶物。

近义词：precipitates, residues|
反义词：solubles, solutions|
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin insolubilis, from in- "not" + solubilis "that can be loosened/dissolved," from solvere "to loosen, dissolve."
