devotary:/ˈdɛvətɛri/| n.|信徒，皈依者；虔诚的崇拜者；奉献者|A person who is devoted to a particular cause, religion, or person.  A devout follower or admirer.|She was a devoutary of the opera, attending every performance.|近义词：follower, adherent, disciple, believer, admirer, fan|反义词：critic, opponent, skeptic|词性:名词

devotary:/dɪˈvoʊtəri/| adj.|虔诚的，热衷的|Characterized by or expressing devotion; pious. |He had a devotary love for his wife.|近义词：devoted, pious, devout, religious, dedicated|反义词：irreligious, impious, undevout|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *devotari*, from Late Latin *dēvōtārius*, from Latin *dēvōtus* (“devoted”).
