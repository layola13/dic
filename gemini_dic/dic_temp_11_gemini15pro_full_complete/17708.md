bedazzling:/bɪˈdæzəlɪŋ/ | adj. | 耀眼的，令人眼花缭乱的 |  Amazingly impressive, dazzling, or glamorous.  | The fireworks display was a bedazzling spectacle. 烟火表演是一场令人眼花缭乱的奇观。 | synonyms: dazzling, brilliant, stunning, glamorous, spectacular | antonyms: dull, drab, unremarkable, ordinary | adjective

bedazzle:/bɪˈdæzəl/ | vt. | 使眼花缭乱，使着迷 | To impress or overwhelm with brilliance or splendor; to dazzle. |  Her beauty bedazzled everyone in the room. 她的美貌使房间里的每个人都为之倾倒。 | synonyms: dazzle, enchant, fascinate, captivate, mesmerize | antonyms: bore, tire, disenchant | verb
