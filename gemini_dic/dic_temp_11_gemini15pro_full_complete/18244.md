beliefs:/bɪˈliːfs/
n.
信仰，信念
An acceptance that something exists or is true, especially one without proof.
His belief in God is unshakeable. 他对上帝的信仰坚定不移。
convictions, faiths, tenets
doubts, disbeliefs
noun
