besiegingly:/bɪˈsiːdʒɪŋli/
| adv. | 围攻地，包围地 | In a manner suggestive of a siege; as if surrounding and attacking.  Like an army besieging a city, relentlessly and persistently. |  The fans crowded around the stage besiegingly, desperate for autographs.  粉丝们围攻般地挤在舞台周围，渴望得到签名。|  The reporters questioned him besiegingly about the scandal. 记者们围攻般地追问他有关丑闻的事情。 | insistently, persistently, relentlessly, oppressively | peacefully, calmly | adverb
