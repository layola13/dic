ambrosian: /æmˈbroʊʒən/ /æmˈbroʊziən/ | adj. | 神仙的; 特别美味的; 芬芳的; (与St Ambrose有关的) | exceptionally pleasing to the taste or smell; extremely delicious; fragrant; relating to or characteristic of St. Ambrose or the Ambrosian rites or chants. |  The ambrosian scent of the flowers filled the room.  房间里充满了鲜花的芬芳。| Synonym: divine, heavenly, delicious, fragrant | Antonym: foul, unpleasant, rancid | Adjective

ambrosian: /æmˈbroʊʒən/ /æmˈbroʊziən/ | n. | 美味佳肴; 神的食物 | something exceptionally pleasing to the taste or smell; the food of the gods. | The feast was an ambrosian delight. 这场盛宴是一场美味的享受。| Synonym: delicacy, nectar | Antonym: swill, garbage | Noun
