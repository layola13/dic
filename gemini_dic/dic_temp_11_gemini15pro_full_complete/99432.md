interenvironmental:/ˌɪntərɪnˌvaɪrənˈmɛntəl/
adjective
环境间的，不同环境之间的
Relating to or involving different environments, or the interaction between them.
The interenvironmental study compared pollution levels in urban and rural areas.  环境间研究比较了城市和农村地区的污染水平。
Scientists are studying the interenvironmental effects of climate change on different ecosystems. 科学家正在研究气候变化对不同生态系统的环境间影响。
近义词: inter-environmental, ecological, cross-environmental
反义词: intraenvironmental
词性: adjective
