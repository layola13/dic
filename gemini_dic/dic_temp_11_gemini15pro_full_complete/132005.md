noncorporeality:/ˌnɒnkɔːrpəˈriːælɪti/ /ˌnɑːnkɔːrˈpɔːriːælɪti/
n.
非物质性；无形性
The state of not having a physical body; immateriality.
He spoke of the noncorporeality of the soul. 他谈到了灵魂的非物质性。


近义词:immateriality, incorporeity, spirituality
反义词:corporeality, physicality, materiality
词性:noun
