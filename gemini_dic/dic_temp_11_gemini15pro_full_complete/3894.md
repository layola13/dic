agreeing:/əˈɡriːɪŋ/
adjective
同意的，一致的
present participle of agree
例句：They are agreeing on the terms of the contract. 他们就合同条款达成了一致。
例句：Agreeing with his proposal, we decided to move forward.  同意他的提议后，我们决定继续推进。
近义词：concurring, consenting, assenting
反义词：disagreeing, dissenting, opposing
词性: adjective


agree:/əˈɡriː/
verb
同意，赞成
From Old French agreer (to please, consent to), from a- (to) + gre (pleasure, liking), from Latin gratus (pleasing, agreeable).
例句：I agree with you. 我同意你的看法。
例句：We agreed to meet at noon. 我们约定中午见面。
近义词：concur, consent, assent
反义词：disagree, dissent, oppose
词性: verb
