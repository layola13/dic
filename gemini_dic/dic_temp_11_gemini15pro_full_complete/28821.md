carouser:/kəˈraʊzər/| n.|狂欢作乐的人，寻欢作乐的人|A person who engages in noisy, drunken revelry.|The carousers kept the whole neighborhood awake with their loud singing and laughter. 这些寻欢作乐的人整夜大声唱歌和大笑，吵得整个街坊都睡不着觉。|Synonyms: reveler, partygoer, merrymaker|Antonyms: ascetic, recluse|noun
