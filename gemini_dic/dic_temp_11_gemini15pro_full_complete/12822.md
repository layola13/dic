assizor:/əˈsaɪzər/ | n. | 协助者; 陪审员; 估价员 | A person who assists or advises another, especially a judge or magistrate.  A person legally appointed to assist a judge or other officer.  A person who helps to assess or estimate the value of something. | The judge called upon the assizor to help determine the value of the property. 法官请估价员协助确定财产价值。| Synonyms: assistant, helper, aide, advisor, assessor | Antonyms: opponent, adversary | noun

assizor:/əˈsaɪzər/ | n. | 协助者; 陪审员; 估价员 | A person who, especially in the past, is chosen to work with a judge | The assizor was selected from amongst the local landowners.  陪审员是从当地地主中挑选出来的。| Synonyms: juror, juryman, jurywoman | Antonyms: defendant, accused | noun
