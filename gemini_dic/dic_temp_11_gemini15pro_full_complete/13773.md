audienceplatz: /ˈɔːdiənsˌplats/ (German) | n. | 观众席；听众席 |  A designated area, usually with seating, for an audience to watch a performance or presentation. It specifically refers to seating areas in German-speaking countries, often used in the context of theaters or concert halls. |  The audienceplatz was full for the premiere of the new opera.  观众席在新歌剧的首演之夜座无虚席。 | synonyms: auditorium, seating area, stalls, gallery | antonyms: stage, backstage | noun
