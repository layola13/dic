mouse:/maʊs/ (pl. mice /maɪs/) | n. |老鼠；鼠标；胆小羞怯的人|A small rodent with a pointed snout, small rounded ears, and a long, almost hairless tail.  A hand-held device that controls the cursor on a computer screen. A timid or fearful person.|The cat chased the mouse. (猫追老鼠。)  She moved the mouse to click on the icon. (她移动鼠标点击图标。) He's a bit of a mouse. (他有点胆小怕事。) |近义词: rodent, cursor controller, coward |反义词: lion (figuratively for person), brave (for person) | 词性: 名词
