confusional:/kənˈfjuːʒənəl/ | adj. | 混乱的，困惑的 | Characterized by or causing confusion; bewildered. | The patient was in a confusional state after the surgery. 手术后，病人处于神志不清的状态。|  The medication left her feeling confusional and disoriented.  药物使她感到困惑和迷失方向。 | 近义词: bewildered, disoriented, perplexed, confused | 反义词: lucid, clearheaded, coherent | 词性: 形容词

词源: Late 19th century: from confusion + -al.
