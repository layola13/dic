mitotic:/mɪˈtɒtɪk/ /maɪˈtɑːtɪk/| adj.|有丝分裂的|Relating to mitosis, the process of cell division in which the nucleus divides into two identical nuclei.|Mitotic activity is high in growing tissues. 生长组织中的有丝分裂活动很活跃。|Synonyms: divisional, proliferative|Antonyms: amitotic|adjective
