词源：From the Malagasy word *Madagasīkara* (name of the island).  Ultimately from Proto-Malayopolynesian *tanaq sikara* meaning "land of Sikara" (a region in the island).  European adoption of the name likely came from Marco Polo who misidentified the island as Mogadishu (in Somalia).
