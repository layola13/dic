legerdemain:/ˌlɛʒərdəˈmeɪn/
noun
戏法，手法，巧妙的花招
skillful use of one's hands when performing conjuring tricks; sleight of hand.
He amazed the audience with his feats of legerdemain. 他以其熟练的戏法使观众惊叹。
synonyms: sleight of hand, conjuring, magic, wizardry, prestidigitation, trickery, deception, illusion, chicanery
antonyms: clumsiness, awkwardness
