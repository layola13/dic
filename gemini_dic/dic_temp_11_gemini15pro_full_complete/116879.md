Mayans: /ˈmaɪənz/ | n. | 玛雅人 |  A member of an indigenous people of Yucatan and adjacent areas of Central America and southern Mexico whose civilization reached its height around AD 300–900.  |  The Mayans developed a complex writing system. 玛雅人发展出了一套复杂的书写系统。 | Synonyms: Maya people | Antonyms: N/A | noun


Mayan: /ˈmaɪən/ | adj. | 玛雅的 | Relating to the Mayan people or their culture. | The Mayan calendar is famous for its accuracy.  玛雅历以其精确性而闻名。| Synonyms: Maya | Antonyms: N/A | adjective


Etymology: From Spanish *Mayas*, plural of *Maya*, from the name of a town in Yucatán.
