noncombatant:/ˌnɒnˈkɒmbətənt/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈkɑːmbətənt/ (US)| n. adj.|非战斗人员，非战斗性的|A person who is not involved in fighting during a war, such as a civilian or a medical worker.  Also describes something not related to combat.|The hospital treated both combatants and noncombatants. 这家医院治疗了战斗人员和平民。|Synonyms: civilian, non-fighter, bystander|Antonyms: combatant, soldier, fighter|noun
