nullable: /ˈnʌləbl/ | adj. | 可空的，可为零的 |  Able to have a value of null (in computer programming, null is a special value that signifies the absence of a value).  This often applies to variables or fields in databases and programming languages. | The "middle_name" field is nullable in the database, meaning it can be left blank.  中间名“middle_name”字段在数据库中是可空的，这意味着它可以留空。 |  optional | required | adjective

nullable: /ˈnʌləbl/ | n. | 可空类型 | A data type that can hold a null value in addition to its normal range of values. | Many modern programming languages support nullable types. 许多现代编程语言都支持可空类型。 | optional type | required type | noun
