half-contentednesses:/ˌhæf kənˈtɛntɪdnɪsɪz/ | n. | 半满足的状态；不太满足的状态 | A state of being only partially or moderately content; a lack of full satisfaction. |  He lived in a state of half-contentednesses, never fully embracing joy nor succumbing to despair. 他生活在一种半满足的状态中，既没有完全拥抱快乐，也没有屈服于绝望。 | Example:  Her half-contentednesses with the situation was evident in her hesitant smile. 她对这种情况的半满足从她犹豫的微笑中可以看出。 | Near Synonyms:  partial contentment, moderate satisfaction, lukewarm acceptance | Antonyms: complete contentment, full satisfaction, utter happiness | noun
