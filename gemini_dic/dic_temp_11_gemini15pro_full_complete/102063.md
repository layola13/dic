isomorphic:/ˌaɪsəˈmɔːrfɪk/ | adj. | 同构的，同态的 |  Being of identical or similar form, shape, or structure.  Two structures are isomorphic if there exists a one-to-one mapping between their elements that preserves the relationships between the elements. | The two graphs are isomorphic, meaning they have the same structure despite different appearances. 这两个图是同构的，这意味着它们尽管外观不同，但结构相同。| isomorphic: The two chemical compounds are isomorphic, sharing the same crystalline structure. 这两种化合物是同构的，具有相同的晶体结构。 | synonyms:  homomorphic, congruent, similar | antonyms: heteromorphic, different | adjective
