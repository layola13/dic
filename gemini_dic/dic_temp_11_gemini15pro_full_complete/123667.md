moocs: /muːks/
noun
大规模开放在线课程 (Massive Open Online Courses)
MOOCs are online courses aimed at unlimited participation and open access via the web.
Many universities now offer MOOCs in a variety of subjects. 许多大学现在提供各种科目的MOOCs。
The popularity of MOOCs has grown significantly in recent years. 近年来，MOOCs的受欢迎程度显著提高。
近义词: online courses, e-learning, distance learning
反义词: traditional courses, face-to-face learning
词性: n.
