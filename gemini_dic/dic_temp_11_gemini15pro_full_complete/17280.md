bazar:/bəˈzɑːr/| n.|集市，市场；义卖会|A marketplace, especially one in the Middle East or India, consisting of rows of small shops or stalls selling miscellaneous goods.|We wandered through the bustling bazar, amazed by the vibrant colors and exotic smells. 我们漫步在熙熙攘攘的集市中，被鲜艳的色彩和异国情调的气味所震撼。|Synonyms: market, marketplace, souk, bazaar|Antonyms: supermarket, mall|noun

bazar:/bəˈzɑːr/| n.|义卖会|A sale of miscellaneous goods, typically for charity.|The church held a bazar to raise money for the new roof. 教堂举办了一个义卖会，为新屋顶筹款。|Synonyms: fair, sale, fete|Antonyms: purchase|noun
