microbacteria:/ˌmaɪkroʊbækˈtɪəriə/ | n. | 微细菌 | Micobacteria are a genus of bacteria that includes the causative agent of tuberculosis. They are typically rod-shaped and are known for their slow growth and resistance to many common antibiotics.  | Micobacteria can be found in various environments, including soil and water. 微细菌可以在包括土壤和水在内的各种环境中找到。| synonyms: microbes, microorganisms | antonyms: N/A (no direct opposite for a genus of bacteria) | noun
