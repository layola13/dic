inurednesses:/ɪˈnjʊərdnəsɪz/
n.
习惯；适应；麻木；（对痛苦等的）无动于衷
The state of being inured (to something unpleasant or painful).  The quality of being accustomed to hardship or difficulty.
His inuredness to violence made him a fearless reporter in war zones. 他对暴力的麻木使他成为战区的无畏记者。
Synonyms:  tolerance, resistance, forbearance,  immunity, impassiveness
Antonyms: sensitivity, vulnerability, susceptibility
noun
