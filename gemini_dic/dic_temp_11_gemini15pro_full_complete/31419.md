chariness:/ˈtʃɛrɪnɪs/| n.|谨慎,小心,吝啬|Carefulness, caution, or reluctance, especially about spending money or taking risks.|She showed a chariness about spending too much money. 她对花钱太多表现得很谨慎。|近义词：caution, wariness, hesitancy, reluctance, thrift, parsimony|反义词：recklessness, extravagance, generosity|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From *chary* (adjective) + *-ness* (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality). *Chary* comes from Old English *cearig* "sorrowful, anxious," related to *care*.  The meaning shifted over time from "sorrowful" to "cautious" and "reluctant."
