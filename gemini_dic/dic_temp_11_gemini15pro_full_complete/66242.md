exclamative:/ɪkˈsklæmətɪv/| adj. n.|感叹的，感叹词|A word, phrase, or sentence that expresses strong feeling, often surprise, joy, or anger.  It is often followed by an exclamation mark.|Example: "Wow! That's amazing!"  哇！太棒了！ |Example:  "Ouch! That hurt!" 哎哟！疼！|Synonym: exclamatory|Antonym: declarative|词性: 形容词, 名词

exclamation:/ˌɛkskləˈmeɪʃən/| n.|感叹，惊叹|A sudden cry or remark expressing surprise, strong emotion, or pain. |Example: "Help!" she exclaimed. “救命！”她喊道。|Example: He gave an exclamation of disgust. 他发出一声厌恶的感叹。|Synonym: cry, shout, yell, interjection|Antonym: whisper, murmur|词性: 名词
