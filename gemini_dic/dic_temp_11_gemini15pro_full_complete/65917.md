ex-presser:/ɛksˈprɛsər/
noun
前挤奶工；前快递员；前表达者
A person who formerly worked as a presser (someone who presses clothes), an express delivery person, or someone who expressed something (like opinions or emotions).
He's an ex-presser, having spent years ironing shirts in a laundry. 他以前是个熨衣工，在洗衣店熨了好几年衬衫。
The ex-presser now works as a truck driver.  这位前快递员现在是一名卡车司机。
synonym: former presser, former express worker, former conveyor
antonym: current presser, current express worker, current conveyor
n.
