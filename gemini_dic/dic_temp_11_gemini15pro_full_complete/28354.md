carat:/ˈkærət/| n. |克拉; 卡|A unit of weight for precious stones, equal to 200 milligrams.  A measure of the purity of gold, pure gold being 24 carats.|The ring has a one-carat diamond. 这枚戒指有一克拉的钻石。|Synonym:  (for weight) milligram, (for gold) karat|Antonym: N/A|noun
carat:/ˈkærət/| n. |开; 克拉|Alternative spelling of karat.|This necklace is made of 18 carat gold.  这条项链是用18克拉黄金制成的。|Synonym: karat|Antonym: N/A|noun
