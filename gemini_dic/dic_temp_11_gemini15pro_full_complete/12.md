a-dime-a-dozen:/əˌdaɪm.əˈdʌz.ən/
adjective
多如牛毛的；不值钱的；寻常的
Common and inexpensive; so readily available as to be of little value.
Generic plastic toys are a dime a dozen these days.  现在普通的塑料玩具多如牛毛。
These kinds of shoes are a dime a dozen.  这种鞋子到处都是。
Synonyms: commonplace, ordinary, common, ubiquitous, rife, prevalent, abundant, plentiful
Antonyms: rare, scarce, uncommon, unusual, unique, valuable, precious
词性: 形容词
