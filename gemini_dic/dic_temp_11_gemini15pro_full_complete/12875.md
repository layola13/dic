assuaging:/əˈsweɪdʒɪŋ/
verb
减轻，缓和
making (an unpleasant feeling) less intense.
Her words assuaged his fears. 她的话减轻了他的恐惧。
synonyms: relieve, ease, alleviate, mitigate, soothe, lessen, allay, mollify, palliate, temper, moderate, diminish, subdue, quieten, calm, tranquillize, pacify
antonyms: aggravate, intensify, worsen, exacerbate
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *asswagen, *aswagen, from Anglo-Norman asuager, from Old French asouagier, from Vulgar Latin *assuaviāre, from Latin ad- + suāvis (“sweet, pleasant”)
