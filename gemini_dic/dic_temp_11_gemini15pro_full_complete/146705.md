overhelpfulness:/ˌoʊvərˈhɛlpfʊlnəs/| n. |过度热心，过分帮忙|The state of being excessively helpful, often to the point of being intrusive or counterproductive.  It implies an unwelcome level of assistance that can undermine the recipient's independence or create unnecessary dependence.|Her overhelpfulness became suffocating after a while. 她过度的热心一段时间后变得令人窒息。|Synonyms: officiousness, intrusiveness, meddling|Antonyms: neglect, indifference|Noun
