multiconic:/ˌmʌltiˈkɒnɪk/ |adj.|多圆锥的，多圆锥投影的|A multiconic projection is a map projection in which the surface of a sphere or ellipsoid is projected onto a series of cones tangent to or intersecting a series of parallels.|Multiconic projections are often used for mapping large areas.|近义词: polyconic|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "single-cone" in a specific context, but not a standard term)|词性: adjective
