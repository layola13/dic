cracovian: /krəˈkoʊviən/ | adj. n. |克拉科夫的；克拉科夫人 |Relating to the city of Krakow, Poland, or its inhabitants.  A native or inhabitant of Krakow. |例句：She wore a traditional Cracovian dress. 她穿着一件克拉科夫的传统服饰。

例句：He's a Cracovian, born and raised. 他是克拉科夫人，土生土长。|近义词：Krakowian, from Krakow |反义词: N/A |词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From Krakow + -ian.  Ultimately derived from the name of the legendary founder of Krakow, Krakus.
