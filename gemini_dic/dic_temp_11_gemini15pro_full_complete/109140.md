ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *lēsislātiō, from Latin lēgis, genitive of lēx (“law”) + -lātiō (“carrying, bringing, bearing”), from lātus (“carried, borne”), perfect passive participle of ferō (“I carry, I bear”)
