layperson's:/ˈleɪˌpɜːrsənz/ |adj. |外行的；非专业的；普通人的|Belonging to or designed for a layperson; non-professional or nonspecialist. |This book explains complex scientific concepts in layperson's terms. 这本书用通俗易懂的语言解释了复杂的科学概念。| 近义词: layman's, non-professional, popular | 反义词: expert's, professional, technical |词性: 形容词

layperson:/ˈleɪˌpɜːrsən/ |n. |外行；非专业人士；普通人|A person without professional or specialized knowledge in a particular subject. |The doctor explained the diagnosis to the layperson in simple terms. 医生用简单的术语向这位外行解释了诊断结果。|近义词: layman, amateur, non-professional | 反义词: expert, professional, specialist |词性: 名词


词源:  From lay (not belonging to the clergy) + person.  The term originally distinguished those not part of the clergy, but expanded to mean anyone outside a specific profession.
