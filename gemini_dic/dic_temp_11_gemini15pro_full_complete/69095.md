fauve:/foʊv/| n. adj.|野兽派画家；野兽派的|A Fauve artist or painting is one belonging to a style of painting that developed in France in the early 20th century, characterized by the use of intense colours and bold brushstrokes.  (野兽派画家或绘画是指属于20世纪初在法国发展起来的一种绘画风格，其特点是使用强烈的色彩和大胆的笔触。)|例句：Matisse is a famous Fauve artist.  马蒂斯是一位著名的野兽派画家。

|近义词：expressionist, colorist|反义词: realist, classicist|词性: noun, adjective

fauve:/foʊv/| adj.|野兽派的|Characterized by the use of intense colours and bold brushstrokes, typical of the Fauves. (以使用强烈的色彩和大胆的笔触为特征，典型的野兽派风格。)|例句：The painting is a vibrant example of Fauve art.  这幅画是野兽派艺术的一个充满活力的例子。|近义词：expressionistic, vibrant|反义词: muted, subdued|词性: adjective
