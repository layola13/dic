overdogmatically:/ˌoʊvərˌdɔːɡˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 过于教条地 | In a manner that is excessively dogmatic; asserting opinions in an unduly positive or arrogant manner. | He overdogmatically asserted his views on the subject, alienating many in the audience. 他过于教条地阐述了自己对这个主题的看法，疏远了观众中的许多人。|  Synonym: rigidly, inflexibly, doctrinally  | Antonym: flexibly, open-mindedly | Adverb

overdogmatic:/ˌoʊvərˌdɔːɡˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 过于教条的 | Excessively dogmatic; asserting opinions in an unduly positive or arrogant manner. | His overdogmatic approach to teaching stifled creativity. 他过于教条的教学方法扼杀了创造力。| Synonym: rigid, inflexible, doctrinaire | Antonym: flexible, open-minded | Adjective


Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above") + "dogmatic" (from Late Latin dogmaticus, from Greek dogmatikos "pertaining to doctrines," from dogma "opinion, tenet") + "-ally" (suffix forming adverbs)
