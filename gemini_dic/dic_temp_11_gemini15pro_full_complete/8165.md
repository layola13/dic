anti-cult:/ˌæntiˈkʌlt/ | adj. n.| 反邪教的；反邪教组织（或运动）| Opposing or hostile to cults.  |例句：The anti-cult movement is gaining momentum. 反邪教运动势头正盛。|近义词：countercult |反义词: pro-cult|词性:形容词

anti-cult:/ˌæntiˈkʌlt/ | n.| 反邪教组织（或运动）| A group or movement that opposes cults. |例句：The anti-cult group held a conference to educate the public.  这个反邪教组织举办了一场会议来教育公众。|近义词：countercult movement|反义词: cult, pro-cult movement|词性:名词

词源:
"anti-" (prefix) from Greek "anti" meaning "against, opposite of"
"cult" from Latin "cultus" meaning "care, cultivation, worship"
