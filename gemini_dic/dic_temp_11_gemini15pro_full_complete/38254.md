comrade:/ˈkɒmræd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmræd/ (US)| n.|同志，伙伴，同事|A fellow member of a political party, especially a communist or socialist one; a companion who shares one's activities or is a fellow soldier.|例句：The comrades fought bravely together.  同志们一起英勇作战。

例句：He greeted his comrade with a warm hug.  他热情地拥抱了他的同志。|近义词：friend, colleague, associate, companion, partner|反义词：enemy, foe, opponent|noun
