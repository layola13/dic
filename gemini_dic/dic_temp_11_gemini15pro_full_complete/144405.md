outstriving: /ˌaʊtˈstraɪvɪŋ/
verb
超越; 胜过; 力求超过
Surpassing or exceeding in striving; striving harder than.
He was outstriving his competitors in the race. 他在比赛中超越了他的竞争对手。

近义词: outdoing, surpassing, excelling, exceeding
反义词: failing, lagging, underperforming


词性: v.
词源: From out- + striving, present participle of strive.
