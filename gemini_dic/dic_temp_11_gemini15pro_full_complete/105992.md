knagged:/næɡd/| v. | 持续抱怨；不断唠叨；不断困扰 |To annoy or irritate persistently; to worry or distress persistently. | The thought of the unfinished report knagged at her all weekend.  未完成的报告整个周末都让她心烦意乱。|  The unanswered question knagged at him. 那个悬而未决的问题一直困扰着他。| Synonyms:  pester, badger, harass, plague, torment, bother, annoy | Antonyms: soothe, comfort, reassure | verb
