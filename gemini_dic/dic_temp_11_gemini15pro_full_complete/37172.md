come-what-may:/ˌkʌm wɒt ˈmeɪ/ | adv. | 无论如何，不管发生什么事 |  Meaning "no matter what happens," come what may emphasizes a determined attitude to persevere despite any potential difficulties or obstacles. It originates from a shortening of the phrase "come what may come." |  She decided to pursue her dreams, come what may. 她决定追求自己的梦想，无论发生什么事。|  Come what may, I will finish this project. 无论如何，我都会完成这个项目。| 近义词: regardless, no matter what, whatever happens | 反义词: conditionally | 词性: 副词

come-what-may:/ˌkʌm wɒt ˈmeɪ/ | adj. |  不顾一切的，毅然的 |  Used less frequently as an adjective, it describes something done or decided with a come-what-may attitude, showing determination and resilience. | His come-what-may attitude inspired his team. 他不顾一切的态度鼓舞了他的团队。 |  She had a come-what-may spirit that helped her overcome many challenges. 她有一种不顾一切的精神，帮助她克服了许多挑战。 | 近义词: resolute, determined, unwavering | 反义词: hesitant, cautious | 词性: 形容词
