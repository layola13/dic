hypertechnically:/ˌhaɪpərˈtɛknɪk(ə)li/
adjective
过于技术性地；过分技术性地
Relating to or involving an excessive focus on technical details, often to the point of being pedantic or impractical.
The instructions were so hypertechnically written that even the experts struggled to understand them.  这些说明写得过于技术化，即使是专家也很难理解。
He hypertechnically explained the process, losing everyone in the jargon. 他过分技术性地解释了这个过程，让每个人都陷入了专业术语的泥沼。
近义词:overly technical, excessively technical
反义词:simply, basically
词性:adjective
