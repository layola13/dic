bivector:/baɪˈvɛktər/| n. |双向量|A quantity in vector algebra that is the vector product of two vectors.|The bivector represents the oriented area of the parallelogram formed by the two vectors.  双向量表示由两个向量形成的平行四边形的定向面积。|Example:  The electromagnetic field tensor can be represented as a bivector. 电磁场张量可以表示为双向量。|Synonym: 2-vector|Antonym: N/A|noun
