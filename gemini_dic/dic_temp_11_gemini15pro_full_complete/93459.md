illusionist:/ɪˈluːʒənɪst/| n.|魔术师；幻术师|A person who performs illusions to entertain an audience.  A performer who creates deceptive appearances of reality.|例句：The illusionist made the rabbit disappear from the hat. 魔术师让兔子从帽子里消失了。

例句：The illusionist's tricks amazed the children.  魔术师的戏法让孩子们惊叹不已。

|近义词：magician, conjurer, prestidigitator|反义词: realist|词性:noun
