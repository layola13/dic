oast:/oʊst/| n. v. |烘干房；烘烤（尤指啤酒花）；|A kiln for drying hops or malt.|The farmer oasted the barley in the oast.  农民在烘干房里烘烤大麦.|近义词：kiln, dry, roast|反义词: dampen, moisten|词性:名词;动词
Etymology: From Middle English *oste, from Old English āst (“kiln”), from Proto-Germanic *austą (“kiln”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ews- (“to dry”). Cognate with Old Frisian ast (“kiln”), Middle Dutch oest (“kiln”), German Ast (“branch, bough; kiln”), Old Norse eista (“kiln”).
