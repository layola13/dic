girasole:/ˌdʒɪərəˈsoʊleɪ/ (US) /ˌdʒɪərəˈsɒleɪ/ (UK)| n.|向日葵，葵花;|A tall plant with large yellow flowers that turn towards the sun. It produces seeds that can be eaten and oil that is used in cooking.|例句：The girasole in the garden turned towards the east as the sun rose. 花园里的向日葵随着太阳升起转向东方。

例句：We extracted girasole oil from the seeds. 我们从种子中提取了葵花籽油。|近义词：sunflower|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *girasole*, literally "turns sun". From *girare* (to turn) + *sole* (sun). Ultimately from Latin *gyrare* (to turn round) and *sol* (sun).
