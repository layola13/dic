absentation: /ˌæbsənˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 缺席；离开；心不在焉 | The prolonged absentation of the CEO caused uncertainty among the employees. 首席执行官长期缺席导致员工人心惶惶。|  A brief absentation from work due to illness. 因病短暂缺勤。 |  absence, nonattendance, truancy | presence, attendance | noun

Etymology:  From Late Latin *absentatio*, from Latin absentare “to be absent,” from absent- “being away,” past participle stem of abesse “to be away, be distant from,” from ab “off, away from” + esse “to be.”
