beneficiate:/ˈbɛnɪfɪʃɪeɪt/| vt. |使受益；处理（矿石）|To improve or process (ore, etc.), especially to concentrate or separate the valuable components.|The company beneficiates low-grade iron ore.  这家公司对低品位铁矿石进行选矿。|Synonyms: improve, enhance, upgrade, enrich, refine, process|Antonyms: worsen, degrade, impair, damage|Verb

beneficiation:/bɛˌnɪfɪʃɪˈeɪʃən/| n. |选矿；处理；改进|The act or process of beneficiating something, especially ore.|The beneficiation of the ore increased its value. 矿石的选矿增加了它的价值。|Synonyms: improvement, enhancement, upgrading, enrichment, refinement, processing|Antonyms: degradation, impairment, damage|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin beneficiatus, past participle of beneficiare (“to benefit”), from Latin beneficium (“benefit, kindness, favor”), from bene (“well”) + facere (“to do”).
