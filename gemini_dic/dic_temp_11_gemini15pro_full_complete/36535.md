collaborations:/kəˌlæbəˈreɪʃənz/| n. |合作，协作|The project was a result of collaborations between several universities. 这个项目是几所大学合作的成果。|例句：The two companies have a long history of collaborations.  这两家公司有着长期的合作历史。

例句：Successful collaborations require open communication and mutual respect. 成功的合作需要开放的沟通和相互尊重。

近义词：partnerships, joint efforts, teamwork, alliances|反义词:competition, rivalry|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin collaboratio(n-), from the verb collaborare (see collaborate).
