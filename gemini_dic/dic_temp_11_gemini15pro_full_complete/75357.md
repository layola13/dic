frothiest:/ˈfrɔːθiɪst/| adj.|最起泡沫的；最轻浮的；最空洞的|Covered with or consisting of froth; full of or producing froth, especially to an excessive degree; also, frivolous and superficial.|The frothiest cappuccino I've ever had was in Rome. 我在罗马喝过最起泡的卡布奇诺。|近义词：foamiest, airiest, flightiest|反义词：flattest, densest, most serious|词性:形容词

词源: From frothy + -est (superlative suffix).  "Frothy" comes from the Old English "frothig," meaning "frothy," from "froth."
