aruspex:/əˈrʌspɛks/ /ɑːˈrʌspɛks/
noun
占卜官，祭司
A Roman priest who practiced divination by inspecting the entrails of sacrificial animals.
The aruspex examined the liver of the sacrificed ox, searching for signs from the gods. 占卜官检查了献祭的牛的肝脏，寻找来自神的迹象。
Synonyms: diviner, soothsayer, oracle, prophet
Antonyms: skeptic, doubter
