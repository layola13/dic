multiphased:/ˌmʌltiˈfeɪzd/
adjective
多阶段的，多相的
Having or occurring in several phases or stages.
The project is a multiphased undertaking that will take several years to complete. 这个项目是一个多阶段的任务，需要几年时间才能完成。
The construction project will be multiphased, starting with the foundation and ending with the landscaping.  这个建筑项目将分多个阶段进行，从地基开始，到景观美化结束。
Synonyms: multistage, multistep
Antonyms: single-phase, single-stage
词性: 形容词
