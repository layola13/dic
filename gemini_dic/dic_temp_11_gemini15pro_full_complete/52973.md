dingy:/ˈdɪndʒi/| adj.|昏暗的，肮脏的|Gloomy and drab; lacking brightness or freshness. Often implies dirtiness and shabbiness.|The room was small and dingy, with peeling wallpaper.|近义词：grimy, shabby, gloomy, dark, drab|反义词：bright, clean, cheerful, sparkling|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From earlier *ding* "dark," perhaps related to Old Norse *dungr* "dungeon." The original sense was "dark," later evolving to include connotations of dirtiness and shabbiness.
