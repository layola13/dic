behoves:/bɪˈhuːvz/
verb
理应；应该
It is incumbent on; it is appropriate for.  It is a duty or responsibility for.
It behooves us to be careful.  我们理应小心谨慎。
It behooves the court to weigh the evidence carefully. 法庭有责任仔细权衡证据。
incumbent upon, befit, become
(No direct antonym, but one could use phrases like "is unnecessary for" or "is inappropriate for" to express the opposite meaning.)
