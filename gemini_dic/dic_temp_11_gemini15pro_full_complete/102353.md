iyo:/ˈiːjoʊ/ | interj. | 哎哟; 唷 | An exclamation expressing surprise, pain, or other strong emotion, often used in online communication, particularly by VTubers and their fans. It's derived from Japanese. | 例句：Iyo, that's a cute outfit! 哎哟，那套衣服好可爱！

例句：Iyo, I stubbed my toe! 哎哟，我的脚趾撞到了！

近义词：ouch, ow, wow, oops | 反义词: (No direct antonym, depends on the context - could be something like "boring," "expected" if iyo is used for surprise) | 词性: interjection
