horography: /həˈrɒɡrəfi/ /hɔːˈrɑːɡrəfi/
n.|水平曲线描写法；山岳地志学|The science of describing the mountains and other elevations of a region or district; the relief features, elevations, etc., of a region.|The horography of the Himalayas is complex and fascinating. 喜马拉雅山脉的地形复杂而迷人。|近义词：orography, topography|反义词: bathymetry|词性: 名词

Etymology->
From French horographie, from Ancient Greek ὡρογραφία (hōrographía, “description of boundaries”), from ὡρογράφος (hōrógraphos, “boundary marker”), from ὅρος (hóros, “boundary, limit”) + γράφω (gráphō, “I write, draw, paint”).
