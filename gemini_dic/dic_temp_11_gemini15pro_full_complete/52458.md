differentiate:/ˌdɪfəˈrenʃieɪt/ | v. | 区分，辨别；区别对待 | To recognize or show that two or more things are not the same; to make someone or something different or to become different. |例句：It's difficult to differentiate between the two varieties.  区分这两个品种很困难。|近义词：distinguish, discriminate |反义词: confuse, blend |词性:动词
differentiate:/ˌdɪfəˈrenʃieɪt/ | v. |求微分，导数 | To calculate the derivative of a function. | 例句：Differentiate the equation with respect to x. 对该方程求关于 x 的导数。 | 近义词：derive | 反义词: integrate | 词性:动词
