grisaille:/ɡrɪˈzaɪl/ /ɡrɪˈzeɪ/| n.|灰色画法；单色画；装饰画|A method of painting in shades of gray, often to imitate sculpture or as a base for colored glazing.  A painting executed in this method, typically to imitate sculpture.|例句：The artist used grisaille to create a dramatic effect. 艺术家用灰色画法创作了一种戏剧性的效果。

例句：The grisaille underpainting gave the final piece a luminous quality. 灰色底色使最终的作品具有发光的效果。|近义词：monochrome, camaieu|反义词: polychrome|词性:noun
