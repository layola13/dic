free-fell:/friːˈfel/
|v.|自由落体；骤降|The skydiver free-fell for several seconds before opening his parachute. 跳伞员在打开降落伞之前自由落体了几秒钟。|例句：The company's stock price free-fell after the CEO resigned.  公司首席执行官辞职后，公司股价暴跌。|近义词：plummet, plunge, drop|反义词:rise, ascend|词性:动词

free-fall:/ˈfriːfɔːl/
|n.|自由落体；骤降|The malfunction caused the plane to go into a free-fall. 故障导致飞机自由落体。|例句：The economy is in free-fall. 经济正在直线下滑。|近义词：plunge, drop, decline|反义词:rise, ascent|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "free" + "fall".  "Free" meaning unrestricted or unimpeded, and "fall" referring to the downward movement under the force of gravity.
