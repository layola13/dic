gatecrashed: /ˈɡeɪtkræʃt/ | v. | 闯入，不请自来 | To enter a party or other gathering without an invitation. |  He gatecrashed the wedding reception. 他不请自来地参加了婚礼招待会。| Synonyms: barge in, intrude, crash | Antonyms:  be invited, be welcome | verb

gatecrasher: /ˈɡeɪtkræʃər/ | n. | 不速之客，闯入者 | A person who attends a party or other gathering without an invitation. |  The gatecrashers were asked to leave.  那些不速之客被要求离开。| Synonyms: intruder, uninvited guest | Antonyms: invited guest, welcome guest | noun
