nonsharing:/ˌnɒnˈʃeərɪŋ/ | adj. | 不共享的，非共享的 | Not sharing; used of resources that cannot be accessed or used by multiple users or processes simultaneously. |  This is a nonsharing printer, so only one document can be printed at a time. 这是一台非共享打印机，一次只能打印一份文档。

| The nonsharing nature of the data ensured its privacy.  数据的非共享性确保了它的私密性。| Near Synonyms: exclusive, private, sole, unshared | Antonyms: shared, common, joint, public | adjective
