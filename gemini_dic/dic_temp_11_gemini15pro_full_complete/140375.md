oilest:/ˈɔɪliɪst/
adjective
油腻的；油污最多的
covered with or full of oil; containing a lot of oil
例句：His hair was the oilest I'd ever seen. 他的头发是我见过最油的。

例句：These are the oilest French fries I've ever had. 这些是我吃过最油的薯条。

近义词：greasiest, oiliest
反义词：driest, cleanest
词性：形容词

词源：From oily + -est (superlative suffix).  The word "oily" comes from the Old English word "ælig," meaning "oily, greasy."
