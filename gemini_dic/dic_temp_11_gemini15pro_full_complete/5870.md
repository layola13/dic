ambassadorial: /ˌæmbæsəˈdɔːriəl/ | adj. | 大使的；大使级别的；有大使风范的 | Relating to an ambassador or their duties; having the qualities of an ambassador, such as diplomacy and tact. |  He had an ambassadorial manner, always calm and polite. 他举止像个大使，总是沉着而有礼貌。

The meeting had an ambassadorial air of formality. 这次会议有一种大使馆的正式气氛。| 近义词: diplomatic, consular, representative | 反义词: undiplomatic, unrepresentative | 词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French ambassateur (see ambassador) + -ial.
