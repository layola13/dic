insignment: /ɪnˈsaɪnmənt/
n.
交付; 寄售
the act of sending goods to someone, especially to be sold
例句：The insignment of goods was delayed due to bad weather. 货物的交付因恶劣天气而延误。

例句：She made a living from the insignment of her artwork to various galleries. 她靠把她的艺术品寄售到各个画廊为生。

近义词：consignment, shipment, delivery
反义词：receipt, acceptance


词性: 名词


词源:  From in- + signment, from sign.  Relates to signing over goods.
