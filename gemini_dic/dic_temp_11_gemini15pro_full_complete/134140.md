nonimplications:/ˌnɒnɪmplɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. |  无蕴涵，不蕴涵（通常用复数形式） | The absence of logical implications or entailments between statements or propositions.  It signifies that one statement doesn't necessarily lead to or suggest another. | The lawyer argued that the witness's testimony had no nonimplications regarding the defendant's guilt. 律师辩称，证人的证词与被告的罪行没有必然联系。| Near synonyms: non-entailments, lack of consequence | Antonyms: implications, entailments | noun
