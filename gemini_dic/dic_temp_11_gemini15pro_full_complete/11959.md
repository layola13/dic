artefact:/ˈɑːtɪfækt/ (US) /ˈɑːtɪfæk/ (UK)| n. |人工制品，史前古器物，人为现象|An object made by a human, typically an item of cultural or historical interest.  Something observed in a scientific investigation or experiment that is not naturally present but occurs as a result of the preparative or investigative procedure. |例句：The museum has a fascinating collection of ancient Egyptian artefacts.  博物馆收藏了引人入胜的古埃及人工制品。

例句：The scientist noticed an artefact in the data caused by a faulty instrument.  科学家注意到数据中有一个由仪器故障引起的人为现象。|近义词：artifact (US spelling), relic, object, item, specimen|反义词: natural object|词性:noun
