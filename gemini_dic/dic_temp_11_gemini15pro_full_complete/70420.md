fighter-bombers:/ˈfaɪtər ˈbɒmərz/ (US) /ˈfaɪtər ˈbɒməz/ (UK)| n.|战斗轰炸机|A military aircraft designed to perform both air-to-air combat and ground attack missions. It combines the features of a fighter aircraft and a bomber aircraft.|The F-15E Strike Eagle is a highly capable fighter-bomber.|Synonyms: attack aircraft, strike aircraft|Antonyms: bomber, fighter|noun
