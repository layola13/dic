aftmost:/ˈæftməʊst/ | adj. | 最尾部的；最后面的 | Situated furthest aft; nearest the stern of a ship or aircraft. | The aftmost cabin offered the best views of the receding coastline.  最尾部的船舱可以欣赏到逐渐远去的海岸线的最佳景色。 | The aftmost section of the plane was damaged in the landing. 飞机的最后面部分在着陆时受损了。 | 近义词：rearmost, hindmost | 反义词：foremost, forwardmost | 词性：形容词

词源：From *aft* (meaning "toward the stern of a ship") + *-most* (a suffix forming superlative adjectives). *Aft* comes from Old English *æftan*, meaning "behind."
