offendress: /əˈfɛndrɪs/
noun
女冒犯者；女性罪犯
A female offender.  A woman who has committed an offense.
The offendress was sentenced to five years in prison.  这名女罪犯被判处五年监禁。
The young offendress showed no remorse for her actions.  这名年轻的女罪犯对自己的行为毫无悔意。
Synonyms: female criminal, female offender, woman culprit
Antonyms: victim
词性: 名词
