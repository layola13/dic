kraton:/ˈkrɑːtɒn/ (US), /ˈkreɪtɒn/ (UK)| n.|克拉通（地质学）; 宫殿; |A kraton is a large, stable block of the earth's crust, typically composed of ancient crystalline basement rock.  克拉通是地球地壳中稳定的大型地块，通常由古老的结晶基底岩石组成。|例句：The Canadian Shield is a vast kraton that underlies much of eastern and central Canada. 加拿大地盾是一个巨大的克拉通，位于加拿大东部和中部的大部分地区。

|例句：The ancient kratons have remained relatively undisturbed for billions of years. 古老的克拉通在数十亿年中相对未受干扰。|近义词：shield, platform|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps orogenic belt) |词性:noun
