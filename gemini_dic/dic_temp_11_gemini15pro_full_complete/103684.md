joltless:/ˈdʒoʊltləs/
adjective
无颠簸的，平稳的
Without jolts or shocks; smooth.
The car boasts a joltless ride, even on bumpy roads. 这辆车即使在崎岖不平的道路上也能平稳行驶。
The new suspension system provides a joltless journey.  新的悬挂系统提供了平稳无颠簸的行驶体验。
近义词:smooth, steady, even, stable
反义词:jerky, bumpy, rough, unsteady
词性:adjective
