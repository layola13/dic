infertilely:/ɪnˈfɜːrtaɪlli/
adverb|不育地，贫瘠地|in a way that is not fertile; unproductively.|The couple tried infertilely for years to have a child. 这对夫妇多年来一直未能生育孩子。|近义词：unproductively, barrenly, fruitlessly|反义词:fertilely, productively|词性:副词
词源: From infertile + -ly. Infertile from in- + fertile. Fertile from Latin fertilis.
