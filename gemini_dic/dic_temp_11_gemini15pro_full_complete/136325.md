nonrescission:/ˌnɒnriˈsɪʒən/
noun
不撤销，不废除
The act of not rescinding or canceling something, especially a contract or agreement.
The nonrescission of the contract meant they were still bound by its terms.  合同未被撤销意味着他们仍然受其条款的约束。
The judge's decision resulted in the nonrescission of the sale. 法官的判决导致了销售未被撤销。
Synonym: continuance, upholding
Antonym: rescission, cancellation
n.
