ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *basilique*, from Latin basilica, from Ancient Greek βασιλική (basilikḗ, “royal [stoa]”), feminine of βασιλικός (basilikós, “royal, kingly”), from βασιλεύς (basileús, “king”). Doublet of basil.
