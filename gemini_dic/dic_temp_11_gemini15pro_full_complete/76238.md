futurist:/ˈfjuːtʃərɪst/ | n. | 未来主义者；未来学家 | A person who studies futurism or believes in the principles of futurism. |  例句：The futurist predicted a world of flying cars and robots.  未来学家预测了一个充满飞行汽车和机器人的世界。

例句：Many futurists believe that technology will solve most of humanity's problems. 许多未来主义者相信科技将解决人类的大部分问题。| 近义词：visionary, innovator, prognosticator | 反义词：traditionalist, conservative | 词性：noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *futuriste*, from futur (“future”) + -iste. Ultimately from Latin *futūrus*.
