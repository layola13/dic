bish:/bɪʃ/ | n. | 贱人；泼妇 | A derogatory slang term for a woman, often used to express anger or disapproval.  It is considered offensive and should be avoided. | Example: "Don't be such a bish!" (别这么刻薄！) | Example:  He muttered "bish" under his breath when she cut him off in traffic. (当她在交通中超车时，他低声骂了一句“贱人”。) | 近义词: bitch (更粗俗), shrew, harpy | 反义词: (No direct antonym for such a derogatory term) | 词性: noun


词源:
Likely a shortening of the word "bitch," losing some of its direct connection to a female dog but retaining its derogatory connotation towards women.
