debatingly:/dɪˈbeɪtɪŋli/
adv.
辩论地，争论地
in a manner of debate; argumentatively.  Engaged in or showing a tendency toward debate or argument.  Characterized by or suggestive of debate.
They discussed the issue debatingly for hours. 他们就这个问题辩论了好几个小时。
The topic was debatingly considered by the committee. 委员会争论性地审议了这个主题。
近义词: argumentatively, contentiously, disputatiously
反义词: agreeably, harmoniously, concordantly
词性: 副词 (adverb)
