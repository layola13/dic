earplugs:/ˈɪərˌplʌɡz/ | n. | 耳塞 | Small plugs inserted into the ears to reduce noise. | I always wear earplugs when I fly to block out the engine noise. 我坐飞机的时候总是戴耳塞来隔绝引擎噪音。 | I use earplugs to help me sleep. 我用耳塞帮助我睡眠。 |  Synonyms: ear protectors, noise reducers | Antonyms: headphones, earphones | noun
