kloodge:/kluːdʒ/
n.|拙劣的修补物；凑合的方案；笨拙的组合|A clumsy or inelegant solution to a problem, often improvised or temporary.  A collection of ill-assorted items or parts.|例句：He managed to fix the broken pipe with a kloodge of tape and wire. 他用胶带和电线凑合着修补了破裂的管道。

例句：The software was a real kloodge, patched together from different programs. 这个软件真是个拙劣的组合，由不同的程序拼凑而成。
|近义词：contraption, makeshift, improvisation, hack|反义词：elegant solution, sophisticated design|词性:名词
