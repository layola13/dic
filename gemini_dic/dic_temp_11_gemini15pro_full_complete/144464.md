outtalks:/ˌaʊtˈtɔːks/
|v.|胜过…的口才；说得比…多|
|Defeats someone in talking, especially by talking longer or more cleverly.|
|She outtalked her opponent in the debate and won the argument. 她在辩论中以口才胜过对手，赢得了辩论。|
|He outtalked the salesman and got a better deal. 他说得比售货员多，最终达成了更划算的交易。|
|outspeak, outargue|
|be outtalked by|
|verb|
