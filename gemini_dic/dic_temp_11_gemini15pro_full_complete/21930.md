bogey:/ˈboʊɡi/ (US), /ˈbəʊɡi/ (UK)| n.| 妖怪; 鬼怪; 令人恐惧的事物; (高尔夫球)高于标准杆一杆;  |An imaginary evil spirit or goblin. Something causing fear or alarm. In golf, a score of one stroke over par for a hole.|例句：The children were afraid of the bogeyman in the closet. 孩子们害怕衣柜里的妖怪。

例句：Inflation is the current bogey for the government. 通胀是目前政府的一大难题。
