ethereal: /ɪˈθɪəriəl/ | adj. | 天上的，飘渺的；优雅的；轻飘的 | Extremely delicate and light in a way that seems too perfect for this world. | Her ethereal beauty captivated everyone in the room. 她飘渺的美丽吸引了房间里的每一个人。

The music was ethereal and otherworldly.  这音乐空灵飘渺，仿佛来自另一个世界。

近义词: celestial, heavenly, airy, delicate, ghostly, spiritual, otherworldly | 反义词: earthly, mundane, substantial, material | 词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin aethereus, from Latin aether + -eus. Ultimately from Greek aithēr (upper air, bright sky).
