compulsive:/kəmˈpʌlsɪv/| adj.|强迫性的；难以抑制的|Resulting from or relating to an irresistible urge, especially one that is against one's conscious wishes.|He's a compulsive liar. 他是个强迫性撒谎者。|例句：She has a compulsive need to clean. 她有强迫性清洁的需求。|近义词：obsessive, uncontrollable, involuntary|反义词：controlled, voluntary|词性:形容词
compulsive:/kəmˈpʌlsɪv/| n.|强迫症患者|A person who feels compelled to do certain things, especially against their conscious wishes.|例句：He is a compulsive and can't stop gambling. 他是个强迫症患者，无法停止赌博。|近义词：addict|反义词： (没有直接的反义词)|词性:名词
