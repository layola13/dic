artly: /ˈɑːrtli/
adjective
艺术地；部分地；在某种程度上
in an artful or artistic manner; to some extent; partially.
Her dress was artly draped, revealing just enough skin. 她的裙子艺术地披着，恰到好处地露出了一些肌肤。
similar: artistically, skillfully, partly, partially
opposite: clumsily, awkwardly, completely, fully
词性: 形容词


词源:  'artly' is derived from 'art' + '-ly'.  'Art' ultimately comes from the Latin 'ars', meaning 'skill' or 'craft'.  The suffix '-ly' is a common adverbial and adjectival suffix, transforming the noun 'art' into an adjective.
