eyesaint:/ˈaɪˌseɪnt/ | n. | 圣人般的人物（尤指外表）; 道貌岸然的人（贬义）| A person, especially one who appears to be exceptionally virtuous or kind, often deceptively so. |  He acted like an eyesaint, but we all knew the truth about his shady dealings.  他表现得像个圣人，但我们都知道他那些见不得人的勾当。| 近义词:goody-goody, hypocrite | 反义词:sinner, rogue | 词性:名词

词源: eye + saint.  This word is a compound word combining "eye" and "saint." It suggests someone who appears saintly only superficially, as far as the eye can see.
