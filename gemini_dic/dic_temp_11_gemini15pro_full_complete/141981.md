organdie:/ˈɔːrɡəndi/| n. |薄纱，透明硬纱|A fine, thin, transparent fabric, usually made of cotton, that is stiff and translucent. It is often used for dresses, curtains, and other decorative items.|She wore a beautiful organdie dress to the summer party. 她穿着一件漂亮的薄纱连衣裙去参加夏季派对。|The curtains were made of delicate white organdie. 窗帘是用精致的白色薄纱制成的。|近义词： voile, muslin, gauze |反义词: canvas, denim |词性: noun

Etymology: From French *organdi*, ultimately from Urdu *अरगंडी* (*argaṛī*)
