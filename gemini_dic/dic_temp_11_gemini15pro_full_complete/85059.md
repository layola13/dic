hard-charging:/ˈhɑːrdˌtʃɑːrdʒɪŋ/
adjective
积极进取的；充满干劲的
aggressively ambitious and persistent
Example:  She's a hard-charging executive who always gets results. 她是一位积极进取、总是能出成绩的高管。

Example: The hard-charging team worked tirelessly to meet the deadline.  这支充满干劲的团队不知疲倦地工作以赶上最后期限。

近义词: ambitious, driven, aggressive, assertive, go-getting, dynamic, energetic
反义词: lazy, passive, unambitious, lethargic, indolent
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
"hard" (Old English "heard") meaning firm, solid, difficult.
"charging" (from "charge," Old French "chargier") meaning to attack, rush forward, fill with energy.
