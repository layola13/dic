juniority:/dʒuːniˈɔːrəti/
n.
资历浅，职位低，年少
The state or quality of being junior, especially in rank or position.
His juniority in the company meant he had little say in the decision-making process. 他在公司的资历浅，意味着他在决策过程中几乎没有发言权。
juniority:/dʒuːniˈɔːrəti/
n.
年幼，年轻
The state of being younger than someone else.
Despite her juniority in age, she displayed remarkable maturity. 尽管她年纪小，但她表现出非凡的成熟。


近义词:junior status, subordination, youth, immaturity
反义词:seniority, superiority, maturity
