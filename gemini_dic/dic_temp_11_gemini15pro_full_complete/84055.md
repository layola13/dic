half-forgiven:/ˌhæf fərˈɡɪvən/
adj.
部分原谅的，未完全原谅的
partially forgiven; not completely absolved of guilt or wrongdoing
He felt half-forgiven after apologizing, but the tension remained. 他道歉后感觉得到了部分原谅，但紧张的气氛依然存在。
The half-forgiven thief promised to make amends.  得到部分原谅的小偷承诺会做出弥补。
Near Synonyms: partially forgiven, almost forgiven, incompletely forgiven
Antonyms: fully forgiven, completely pardoned, absolved

词性: adjective
