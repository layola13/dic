demi-hake:/ˈdɛmiˌheɪk/ | n. | 半熟鳕鱼，半干鳕鱼 | A hake that has been partially dried, but not as much as a stockfish. |  Example 1: The demi-hake was perfect for grilling, retaining some moisture but firm enough to hold its shape. 半干鳕鱼非常适合烧烤，保留了一些水分，但也足够结实，可以保持形状。

Example 2:  She preferred demi-hake to stockfish, finding it less chewy. 她更喜欢半干鳕鱼而不是鳕鱼干，觉得它不那么难嚼。 | 近义词: partially dried hake, cured hake | 反义词: fresh hake, stockfish | 词性: 名词


词源:

"demi-" comes from French, meaning "half".  "Hake" comes from Middle English hake, from Old Norse hakr.
