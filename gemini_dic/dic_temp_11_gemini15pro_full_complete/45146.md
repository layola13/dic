cuddy:/ˈkʌdi/| n.|小房间，狭窄的船舱，尤指船长或官员的寝室|A small cabin, especially one used as sleeping quarters by the captain or officers of a ship. |The captain retired to his cuddy for the night. 船长回到他的小舱里过夜了。|The fishing boat had a tiny cuddy where the crew could rest.  这艘渔船有一个小船舱，船员可以在那里休息。| synonyms: cabin, berth, compartment| antonyms: deck, bridge|noun
