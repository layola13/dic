appendaged:/əˈpɛndɪdʒd/
adjective|有附属物的，有附加物的|Having appendages; furnished with appendages.
|The appendaged creature scuttled across the ocean floor.  这只带有附属物的生物在海底快速爬行。
|The newly discovered insect was appendaged with delicate, feathery antennae.  新发现的昆虫长着精致的羽毛状触须。
|近义词：attached, adorned,  supplemented
|反义词:unadorned, bare, simple
|词性:adjective
|词源:From appendage + -ed.  Appendage comes from Late Latin appendicium "an appendage," from Latin appendere "to cause to hang upon," from ad- "to" + pendere "to hang."
