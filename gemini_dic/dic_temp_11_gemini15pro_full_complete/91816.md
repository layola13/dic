hyperfunctional:/ˌhaɪpərˈfʌŋkʃənəl/
adjective
机能亢进的；功能过强的
Exhibiting excessive functional activity; overactive.
Her thyroid gland is hyperfunctional, causing a rapid heart rate.  她的甲状腺功能亢进，导致心率加快。
The child's immune system was hyperfunctional, leading to autoimmune disorders.  这个孩子的免疫系统功能亢进，导致了自身免疫性疾病。
近义词: overactive, hyperactive
反义词: hypofunctional, underactive
词性: adjective
词源: hyper- (Greek, "over, excessive") + functional (English, "performing a function")
