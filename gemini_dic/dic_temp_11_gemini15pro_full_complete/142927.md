out-think:/ˌaʊtˈθɪŋk/ | v. | 比...想得更周到；智胜 | To think more creatively and effectively than someone else, thereby outmaneuvering or defeating them. |  He out-thought his opponents and won the chess match. 他比对手想得更周到，赢得了象棋比赛。


The experienced general out-thought the enemy and led his troops to victory.  这位经验丰富的将军智胜了敌人，带领他的部队取得了胜利。


| 近义词: outsmart, outwit, outmaneuver | 反义词: be outwitted, be outsmarted | 词性: 动词
