dovecote:/ˈdʌvkɒt/ (UK), /ˈdʌvkɑːt/ (US)| n. |鸽舍，鸽棚|A small shelter with nesting holes for pigeons or doves. |例句：The old dovecote in the garden was home to dozens of birds. 花园里古老的鸽舍是几十只鸟的家。

例句：The farmer built a new dovecote to house his prize-winning pigeons.  农民建了一个新的鸽舍来养他获奖的鸽子。

|近义词：pigeon house, columbarium|反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性: Noun

Etymology:
From Old English *dūfcot (unattested), from dūfe "dove" + cot "cottage, shelter".
