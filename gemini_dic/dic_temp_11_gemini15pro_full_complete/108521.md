layship: /ˈleɪʃɪp/
n.
临时解雇；停工期
A period of temporary unemployment from one's usual work; a layoff.
The shipyard workers faced a layship due to the lack of new orders.  造船厂的工人由于缺乏新订单而面临停工。
Layoffs are expected as the company restructures.  由于公司重组，预计将进行裁员。
近义词: layoff, redundancy, furlough
反义词: hiring, employment, recall
词性: 名词
