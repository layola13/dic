intent:/ɪnˈtɛnt/ | n. adj.| 意图，目的； 坚决的 | An aim or plan.  Determined to do something. |  She had no intent to harm anyone. 她没有伤害任何人的意图。| synonyms: aim, purpose, goal, objective, plan, intention, resolve | antonyms: apathy, indifference, hesitation, reluctance | noun

intent:/ɪnˈtɛnt/ | adj. | 专心的，专注的；坚决的 | Giving all one's attention to something.  Determined to do something. | He was intent on finishing the project. 他专心致志地完成这个项目。| synonyms: focused, concentrated, absorbed, engrossed, determined, resolute | antonyms: distracted, inattentive, hesitant, wavering | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *intentus*, past participle of *intendere* "to stretch out, turn one's attention to," from *in-* "toward" + *tendere* "to stretch."
