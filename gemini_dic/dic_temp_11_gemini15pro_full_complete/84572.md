hammiest:/ˈhæmiɪst/
adjective
最做作的，最夸张的
Most affectedly theatrical or exaggerated in manner or performance.
Her performance was hammiest I've ever seen, full of over-the-top gestures and exaggerated expressions. 她表演得是我见过最做作的，充满了夸张的手势和表情。
The hammiest actor in the play chewed the scenery every chance he got.  这部剧里最做作的演员一有机会就卖弄演技。
近义词: theatrical, overacted, exaggerated, melodramatic
反义词: understated, subtle, natural, restrained
词性: adjective

词源:
From "ham" (overacting) + "-iest" (superlative suffix). "Ham," in the sense of overacting, possibly originated in the 19th century, perhaps referring to amateur actors who used ham fat to remove their makeup, or possibly from a minstrel song called "The Ham-Fat Man."
