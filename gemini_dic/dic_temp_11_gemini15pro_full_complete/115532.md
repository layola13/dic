map-projection:/ˈmæp prəˈdʒɛkʃən/ | n. | 地图投影 | A systematic transformation of the latitudes and longitudes of locations from the surface of a sphere or an ellipsoid into locations on a plane. | Example 1:  The Mercator map projection distorts the size of landmasses near the poles.  墨卡托地图投影扭曲了靠近两极的陆块的大小。

Example 2: Choosing the right map projection is crucial for accurate spatial analysis. 选择正确的地图投影对于准确的空间分析至关重要。 | synonyms: cartographic projection | antonyms: N/A | noun
