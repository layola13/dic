antiwear:/ˌæntiˈwɛər/| adj.|抗磨的，耐磨的|Reducing or preventing wear, especially between moving parts.|This engine oil has excellent antiwear properties.  这种机油具有优异的抗磨性能。|The antiwear coating extends the life of the gears.  抗磨涂层延长了齿轮的寿命。|近义词：abrasion-resistant, wear-resistant|反义词:  N/A|词性:adjective
词源: anti- (against) + wear (the gradual loss of material from something due to friction)
