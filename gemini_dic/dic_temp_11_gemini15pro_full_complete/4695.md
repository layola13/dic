alertnesses:/əˈlɜːrtnɪsɪz/
n.
警觉性，警惕性
The security guard maintained a high level of alertness throughout the night.
保安整夜保持高度警觉。
His alertness saved him from the oncoming car.
他的警觉使他免于被迎面而来的汽车撞到。
近义词：vigilance, watchfulness, awareness
反义词：inattentiveness, carelessness, negligence
词性: 名词

词源：From alert + -ness.  Alert comes from Italian *allerta (feminine of all’erta “on the watch, on the alert”), from all’erta, literally “on the height, on the rise”, from erta “rise, ascent”.
