bernardine:/ˌbɜːrnərˈdiːn/ /ˌbɝːnərˈdin/| adj. n.|圣伯纳的；圣伯纳犬；熙笃会修士|Of or relating to Saint Bernard of Clairvaux or the Cistercian monasteries founded by him. Also, a member of that order.  Can also refer to a St. Bernard dog.|例句：He had a large, friendly bernardine dog. 他有一只体型庞大、友善的圣伯纳犬。

例句：The bernardine monks lived a life of prayer and contemplation.熙笃会修士过着祈祷和沉思的生活。|近义词：Cistercian, monastic|反义词:secular|词性:adjective, noun
