exornation:/ˌɛksɔːrˈneɪʃən/ |n.|装饰；修饰；美化|The exornation of the church was breathtaking. 教堂的装饰令人叹为观止。|例句：The elaborate exornation of the palace impressed all the visitors. 宫殿精美的装饰给所有参观者留下了深刻的印象。

例句：Her simple dress needed no further exornation. 她简单的连衣裙不需要再加任何装饰。|近义词：decoration, ornamentation, embellishment, adornment|反义词：simplicity, plainness|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *exornationem, accusative singular of exornatio (“an adorning, embellishing”), from Latin exornatus, past participle of exornare (“to adorn, embellish, equip, furnish”), from ex- (“out, thoroughly”) + ornare (“to adorn, embellish, equip, furnish”), from ornus (“mountain ash; an ornament”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₁óres-nos (“mountain ash; an ornament”).
