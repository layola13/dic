consultantship:/kənˈsʌltənʃɪp/
n.
咨询工作；顾问职位；顾问期
A period during which someone works as a consultant. The position or job of a consultant.
She finished her consultantship with the company last month. 她上个月结束了在公司的咨询工作。
He has a consultantship with a major international firm. 他在一家主要的国际公司担任顾问。
近义词: consultancy, advisory role, consulting position
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider: permanent position, staff position)
词性: noun

consultantship:/kənˈsʌltənʃɪp/
词源: From consultant + -ship. "Consultant" comes from the Latin "consultare" meaning "to consult."  "-ship" is a suffix indicating state, condition, or office.
用法:  Refers to the position, work, or term of service of a consultant. Can refer to the period of time someone works as a consultant or the specific position itself.
