employing:/ɪmˈplɔɪɪŋ/
verb
使用；雇用；运用
present participle of employ
The company is employing new strategies to increase sales. 这家公司正在采用新的策略来提高销售额。
近义词: using, utilizing, hiring, engaging
反义词: dismissing, firing, laying off
词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French emploier, from Latin implicare "to involve, entangle, enfold," from in- "in" + plicare "to fold." The sense shifted from "involve" to "make use of" and "occupy (someone's time)."
