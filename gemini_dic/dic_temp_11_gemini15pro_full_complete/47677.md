debtors:/ˈdɛtərz/
n.
债务人
A person or entity that owes a debt to another.
The company sued its debtors for outstanding payments.  这家公司起诉其债务人拖欠款项。
synonyms:borrowers, owing party
antonyms:creditors, lenders
noun


debtor:/ˈdɛtər/
n.
债务人
A person or entity that owes a debt to another.
The debtor was unable to repay the loan.  债务人无法偿还贷款。

antonyms:creditor, lender
