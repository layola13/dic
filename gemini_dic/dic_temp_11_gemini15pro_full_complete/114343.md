majesticness:/məˈdʒɛstɪknəs/| n. |雄伟,壮丽,庄严|The quality of being majestic; impressive stateliness, dignity, or beauty.|例句：The majesticness of the mountains awed the climbers. 群山的雄伟使登山者敬畏。

例句：The majesticness of the cathedral filled her with a sense of peace.  大教堂的庄严让她充满了平静的感觉。|近义词：grandeur, splendor, magnificence, glory, sublimity|反义词：insignificance, commonness, ordinariness, humbleness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From majestic + -ness.  Majestic from Middle French majesté  "majesty," from Latin majestas "greatness, dignity, grandeur," from major "greater," comparative of magnus "great."
