lubricator:/ˈluːbrɪkeɪtər/| n.|润滑器；润滑剂；加油器|A device or substance used to reduce friction between moving parts by applying a lubricant.|The mechanic applied lubricator to the gears. 机械师给齿轮上了润滑油。|Synonyms: oiler, greaser, lubricant |Antonyms: abrasive, desiccant|noun
