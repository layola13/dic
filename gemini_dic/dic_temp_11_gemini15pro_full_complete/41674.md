correspond:/ˌkɔːrəˈspɒnd/ (UK) /ˌkɔːrəˈspɑːnd/ (US)| vi. vt.| 对应；符合；通信|
correspond means to be similar or analogous; to be in agreement or conformity; to communicate by exchanging letters.|
例句：His account of the accident corresponds with hers. 他对事故的描述与她的一致。
例句：The American Congress corresponds to the British Parliament. 美国国会相当于英国议会。

近义词：match, agree, correlate, communicate, write|
反义词：differ, disagree, clash|
词性: verb
