epicarp:/ˈɛpɪˌkɑːrp/| n.|外果皮|The outermost layer of the fruit wall, also known as the exocarp.|The epicarp of a peach is fuzzy. 桃子的外果皮是毛茸茸的。|The tough epicarp of the coconut protects the seed. 坚硬的椰子外果皮保护着种子。|近义词:exocarp, rind (especially of citrus fruits), peel|反义词:endocarp, mesocarp|词性:noun
