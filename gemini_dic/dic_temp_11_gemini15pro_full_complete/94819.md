impunibly:/ɪmˈpjuːnɪbli/
adv.
免受惩罚地
without fear of punishment; with no bad effects.
He acted impunibly, knowing he was too powerful to be held accountable. 他肆无忌惮地行事，因为他知道自己权力太大，不会被追究责任。
The dictator ruled impunibly for decades.  独裁者统治了几十年，却从未受到惩罚。
近义词: with impunity, freely, without consequence
反义词:accountably, responsibly, consequentially
词性: adverb

词源: From Late Latin *impūnībilis, from Latin impūnis (“unpunished”) + -bilis (“-able”).  Impūnis is from in- (“not”) + poena (“punishment”).
