kabbalist:/ˈkæbəlɪst/| n.|卡巴拉学者，卡巴拉信徒|A person who studies or practices Kabbalah, a system of Jewish mystical interpretation.|He was a renowned kabbalist, delving deep into the mysteries of the Torah. 他是一位著名的卡巴拉学者，深入研究律法的奥秘。|近义词：mystic, occultist|反义词:rationalist, skeptic|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Hebrew קַבָּלָה (qabālā, “received tradition”), from קִבֵּל (qibbel, “to receive”). Ultimately from Akkadian *qabālu (“to receive”).
