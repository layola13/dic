overpoweringly: /ˌoʊvərˈpaʊərɪŋli/ | adv. | 压倒性地；不可抗拒地；极其强烈地 |  In a way that is so strong as to be irresistible. | The heat was overpoweringly strong.  酷热难耐。 | 近义词: overwhelmingly, irresistibly, compellingly | 反义词: weakly, mildly, slightly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From *overpowering* + *-ly*. *Overpowering* comes from *overpower* (from *over-* + *power*) + *-ing*.
