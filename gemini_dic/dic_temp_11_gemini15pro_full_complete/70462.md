figures:/ˈfɪɡərz/ (US); /ˈfɪɡəz/ (UK)| n. v. |数字; 人物; 图形; 认为; 计算|  A number, especially one which forms part of official statistics or is used in a calculation.  A person, especially a well-known or important one. A shape or outline of somebody. To calculate or work out something. |

例句：
The latest unemployment figures are alarming. 最新的失业数字令人担忧。

Sales figures for last year were excellent. 去年的销售数字非常好。


近义词：
(n.) numbers, statistics, data, individuals, personalities, shapes, forms
(v.) calculate, compute, estimate, judge, reckon


反义词：
(n. - referring to numbers)  No single antonym, context-dependent (e.g., low figures vs. high figures)
(n. - referring to people)  nobody, unknown
(v.) miscalculate
