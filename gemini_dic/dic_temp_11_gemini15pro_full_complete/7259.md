anglomaniacs:/ˌæŋɡləˈmeɪniæks/
|n.|
|亲英者，崇英者|
|A person who is excessively fond of or obsessed with English customs, manners, and culture.|
|The anglomaniacs in the colony insisted on having afternoon tea every day, even in the sweltering heat.|殖民地的亲英人士坚持每天都要喝下午茶，即使是在酷暑中。

|The magazine caters to anglomaniacs, featuring articles on British royalty and fashion.|这本杂志迎合了亲英者的口味，刊登了关于英国皇室和时尚的文章。

|近义词: Anglophile|
|反义词: Francophiles, Germanophiles (depending on the context) |
|词性: noun (plural)|
