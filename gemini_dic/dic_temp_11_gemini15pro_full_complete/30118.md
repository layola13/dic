ceded:/ˈsiːdɪd/ | v. |割让，放弃| to surrender possession of, especially by treaty|例句：Mexico ceded California to the United States in 1848. 墨西哥在1848年将加利福尼亚割让给了美国。|例句：He ceded his position as CEO to his son. 他把首席执行官的职位让给了他的儿子。 | 近义词：surrendered, relinquished, yielded | 反义词：retained, kept, acquired | verb
