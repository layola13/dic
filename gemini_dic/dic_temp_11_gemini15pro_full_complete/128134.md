nethermost:/ˈneðərməʊst/| adj.|最低的；最深处的；最下层的|situated at the lowest possible level; deepest|例句：The nethermost layer of the ocean is still largely unexplored. 海洋最底层很大程度上仍未被探索。|近义词：lowest, bottommost, deepest|反义词：highest, topmost, uppermost|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old English *niðemest, superlative of neoðera (“lower”). Related to nether.
