elucubrate:/ɪˈluːsɪbreɪt/| v. | 努力思考，潜心钻研，著述 | To think about or study something very carefully and produce a written work about it. | He spent hours elucidating his theory of the universe. 他花了数小时阐述他的宇宙理论。 |  She elucidated the complex scientific principles with clarity. 她清晰地阐明了复杂的科学原理。 |近义词：ponder, contemplate, meditate, study, write, compose |反义词: neglect, ignore |词性: 动词

Etymology: From Latin *elucubratus*, past participle of *elucubrare* "to work out by lamplight," from *e-* "out" + *lucubrare* "to work by lamplight," from *lux* "light."
