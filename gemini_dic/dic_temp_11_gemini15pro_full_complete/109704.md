levation: /ˌɛlɪˈveɪʃən/ (US), /ˌɛlɪˈveɪʃn/ (UK)
n.
海拔，高度；提升，提高；崇高，高尚
the height of something above a given level, especially sea level; the act of raising or lifting something; an increase in the intensity or amount of something
The city of Denver is situated at a high elevation. 丹佛市位于高海拔地区。
The project aims at the elevation of the living standards of the poor. 该项目旨在提高穷人的生活水平。
elevation: /ˌɛlɪˈveɪʃən/
近义词: altitude, height, rise, ascent, promotion, advancement
反义词: depression, lowering, descent
