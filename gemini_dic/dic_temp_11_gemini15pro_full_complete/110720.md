linens:/ˈlɪnɪnz/
n.
亚麻制品；床上用品；家用亚麻织物
Household sheets, pillowcases, tablecloths, and towels, typically made of linen or a similar fabric.
She washed the linens and hung them out to dry. 她洗了亚麻制品，并把它们挂在外面晾干。
The hotel provided fresh linens every day.  这家酒店每天都提供干净的床单。
synonyms: bedding, sheets, covers, cloths
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "synthetics" in the context of fabric)
