occultism:/əˈkʌltɪzəm/| n. |神秘学，玄学|The study of supernatural or magical powers, practices, or phenomena.|She has a deep interest in occultism and the paranormal. 她对神秘学和超自然现象有着浓厚的兴趣。|Example: The book explores the history of occultism in Europe. 这本书探讨了欧洲神秘学的发展史。|Synonyms: mysticism, esotericism, magic, supernaturalism|Antonyms: science, rationalism|noun


occult:/əˈkʌlt/| adj. |神秘的，玄奥的|Secret or hidden from view; not detectable by ordinary means; mysterious, supernatural.|He was involved in occult practices. 他参与了神秘的活动。|Example: The group held their occult rituals in a secret location.  这个团体在一个秘密地点举行他们的神秘仪式。|Synonyms: hidden, secret, esoteric, mysterious|Antonyms: obvious, known, public|adjective
