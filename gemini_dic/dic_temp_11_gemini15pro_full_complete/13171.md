at arm's length:/æt ɑːrmz ˈlɛŋθ/ | adv. adj. | 保持距离，不沾亲带故；公平地 |  Maintaining a distance, either physically or emotionally; in a fair and impartial manner, without showing favoritism or bias. |  We conducted the negotiations at arm's length to ensure fairness. 我们为了确保公平，在谈判中保持了距离。|  The company deals with all its subsidiaries at arm's length. 该公司与其所有子公司都保持公平交易。 | 近义词: independently, impartially, objectively | 反义词: closely, intimately | 词性: 副词, 形容词

at arm's length:/æt ɑːrmz ˈlɛŋθ/ | n. | 一臂之距 | The distance of an arm's length; a safe or comfortable distance. | He held the snake at arm's length. 他把蛇放在一臂远的地方。| She kept the pushy salesman at arm's length. 她和那个强势的推销员保持着距离。 | 近义词: distance, separation | 反义词: proximity, closeness | 词性: 名词
