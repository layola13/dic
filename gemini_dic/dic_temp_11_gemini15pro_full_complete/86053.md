heap:/hiːp/| n. |堆，大量，许多|A disorderly pile or mass of something.|例句：There's a heap of clothes on the floor. 地板上有一堆衣服。|近义词：pile, mound, stack, mass, lot|反义词:few, little|noun

heap:/hiːp/| v. |堆积，堆放|To put or throw something in a pile or mass.|例句：He heaped the wood onto the fire. 他把木头堆在火上。|近义词：pile, stack, amass|反义词:scatter, disperse|verb
