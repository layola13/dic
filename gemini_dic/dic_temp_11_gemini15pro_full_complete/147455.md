overoffends:/ˌoʊvərəˈfɛndz/
verb
过度冒犯
To offend to an excessive degree.
He overoffended his hosts by criticizing their lifestyle choices. 他因批评主人的生活方式而过度冒犯了他们。
She overoffended her colleagues with her insensitive remarks. 她用她不敏感的言论过度冒犯了她的同事们。
近义词: outrage, insult, affront
反义词: placate, appease, soothe
词性: verb
