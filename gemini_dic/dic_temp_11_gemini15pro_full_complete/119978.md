millering:/ˈmɪlərɪŋ/
n.
研磨; 磨粉; 磨碎; 谷物碾磨
The process of grinding grain into flour or meal.
The millering of wheat produces flour. 小麦的碾磨会产生面粉。
grinding, milling, pulverizing
