overrode:/ˌoʊvərˈroʊd/ | v. | 推翻，否决，凌驾 | past tense of override.  It means to use one's authority to reject a decision or order. It can also mean to prevail over something. |  The CEO overrode the board's decision. 首席执行官否决了董事会的决定。|  The emergency override system kicked in. 紧急覆盖系统启动了。| synonyms: overruled, overturned, countermanded, disregarded | antonyms: upheld, supported, sustained | verb
