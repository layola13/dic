drupe:/druːp/| n. |核果|A fleshy fruit with thin skin and sweet flesh surrounding a single hard stone that encloses the seed. |例句：Peaches, plums, and cherries are all drupes. 桃子、李子和樱桃都是核果。

例句：The mango is a tropical drupe known for its sweet and juicy flesh. 芒果是一种热带核果，以其甜美多汁的果肉而闻名。|近义词：stone fruit|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps berry for contrast)|词性: noun
