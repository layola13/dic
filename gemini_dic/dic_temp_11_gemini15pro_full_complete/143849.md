outplanning: /ˌaʊtˈplænɪŋ/
verb
超越规划；比…计划得更好
The act of planning more effectively or strategically than someone or something else.
We outplanned our competitors and secured the major contract. 我们比竞争对手计划得更好，从而获得了主要合同。
The team's meticulous preparation allowed them to outplan their rivals. 该团队细致的准备使他们能够比对手计划得更好。
近义词: outmaneuver, outstrategize
反义词: be outplanned, be outsmarted
词性: verb
