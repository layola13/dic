junger:/ˈjʊŋər/ (US), /ˈjʌŋər/ (UK)| adj.| 更年轻的；年幼的 |Younger refers to being less old or advanced in age than another person or thing.  It is the comparative form of "young."|Example 1: My brother is younger than me. 我的弟弟比我小。

Example 2: This is a younger version of the software. 这是该软件的较新版本。|近义词: junior, less old |反义词: older, elder |词性: adjective
