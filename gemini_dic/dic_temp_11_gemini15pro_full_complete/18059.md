beguard:/bɪˈɡɑːrd/ | v. | 保护，守护 | To protect or guard someone or something. |  He beguarded the princess from her enemies. 他保护公主免受敌人侵害。

She beguarded her secrets carefully. 她小心翼翼地守护着自己的秘密。| 近义词: protect, guard, defend, safeguard | 反义词: endanger, expose, neglect | 词性: 动词

词源: "be-" (a prefix meaning "thoroughly" or "all around") + "guard" (from Old North French "garder," meaning "to watch over," "protect").
