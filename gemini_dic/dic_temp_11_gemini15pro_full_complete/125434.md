multiplane:/ˈmʌltiˌpleɪn/| adj. n.|多平面的，多翼飞机|Having or involving several planes, especially of a camera or other equipment used in animation, or relating to an aircraft with more than one set of wings.|The Disney movie "Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs" was one of the first films to use a multiplane camera, which gives the animation a greater sense of depth.|近义词：multi-layered, multi-level, stratified|反义词: single-plane, flat|词性:形容词,名词

multiplane:/ˌmʌltiˈpleɪn/| n.|多翼飞机|An aircraft with more than one set of wings, one above the other.|The Wright brothers experimented with multiplane designs before settling on their famous biplane.|近义词：biplane, triplane|反义词: monoplane|词性:名词
