anticonstitutional:/ˌæntiˌkɑːnstɪˈtuːʃənəl/
adjective
违宪的，反宪法的
Opposing or violating the principles of a constitution.
The court ruled that the new law was anticonstitutional. 法院裁定新法律违宪。
Synonyms: unlawful, illegal, illegitimate, illicit
Antonyms: constitutional, lawful, legal, legitimate
词性: 形容词
