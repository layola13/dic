changednesses:/ˈtʃeɪndʒɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
变化性，变化的次数或状态
The state or quality of having been changed multiple times or the degree to which something has changed.
The changednesses of the political landscape made it difficult to predict the election outcome. 政治格局的变化使得很难预测选举结果。
近义词: alterations, modifications, transformations, mutations
反义词:  stability, constancy, immutability
