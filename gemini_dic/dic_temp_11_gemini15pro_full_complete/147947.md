overrepressed:/ˌoʊvər rɪˈprɛst/
adjective
过度压抑的
Excessively repressed; having emotions or desires that are kept under very strict control, often leading to psychological problems.
例句：His overrepressed emotions eventually manifested as physical ailments.  他过度压抑的情绪最终表现为身体疾病。

例句：Growing up in an overrepressed household, she struggled to express her feelings.  在一个过度压抑的家庭中长大，她难以表达自己的感受。
近义词：inhibited, suppressed, restrained
反义词：uninhibited, expressive, unrestrained
词性: adjective

词源:
From over- + repressed, past participle of repress.  "Over-" signifies "excessively" or "too much."  "Repress" comes from the Latin "reprimere," meaning "to press back" or "to restrain."
