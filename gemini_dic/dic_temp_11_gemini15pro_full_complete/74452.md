frangulin: /ˈfræŋɡjʊlɪn/ | n. |弗兰古林，鼠李糖苷|A glycoside obtained from the bark of the alder buckthorn (Frangula alnus) and other plants, used as a cathartic. It is an orange crystalline substance, C₂₁H₂₀O₉.| Frangulin is used medicinally as a laxative.  弗兰古林在医学上用作泻药。| Synonyms: emodin rhamnoside | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific chemical compound) | Noun
