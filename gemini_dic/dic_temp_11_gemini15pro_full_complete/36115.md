coercionists:/koʊˈɜːʃənɪsts/ | n. |脅迫者，强迫者 | People who practice or advocate coercion; those who compel others to do something by using force or threats. | The coercionists threatened to harm the villagers if they didn't comply with their demands. 胁迫者威胁说，如果村民们不服从他们的要求，他们就会伤害村民。 |  The coercionists demanded complete obedience. 胁迫者要求绝对服从。 | Synonyms: bullies, intimidators, oppressors, tyrants | Antonyms:  negotiators, mediators, conciliators | Noun
