concreter:/ˈkɒŋkrɪːtə(r)/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnkriːtər/ (US)| n.|混凝土工|A person who works with concrete, especially one who lays or finishes concrete surfaces.|The concreter smoothed the surface of the newly poured driveway.|近义词:cement worker, concrete finisher|反义词: None|词性:noun

concreter:/kənˈkriːtə(r)/ (UK) /kənˈkriːtər/ (US)| v.|浇筑混凝土|To cover or solidify with concrete.|They concreted the patio area.|近义词:cement, pave|反义词:demolish, dismantle|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From concrete + -er (agent suffix).  The verb sense is a back-formation from the noun.
