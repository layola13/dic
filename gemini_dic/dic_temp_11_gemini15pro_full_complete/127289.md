navigated:/ˈnævɪɡeɪtɪd/
verb
已航行，已导航
past tense and past participle of "navigate"  meaning to plan and direct the course of a vehicle, aircraft, or vessel. Also means to find one's way around or through an area.
The captain skillfully navigated the ship through the treacherous waters. 船长熟练地驾驶着船只穿过危险的水域。
We navigated the city using a map and GPS. 我们使用地图和GPS导航了这座城市。
near synonyms: steered, piloted, directed, guided, traversed, journeyed
near antonyms: drifted, wandered, strayed, lost
