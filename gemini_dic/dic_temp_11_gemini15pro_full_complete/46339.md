dacoities: /dəˈkɔɪtiz/ | n. | 盗窃，抢劫（尤指成帮结伙的） |  The act of robbery by a gang of armed dacoits.  It usually involves violence and intimidation. |  Dacoities were a common occurrence in the remote villages.  这些偏远村庄经常发生抢劫事件。| The dacoities terrified the local population. 抢劫事件吓坏了当地居民。 | synonyms: robberies, heists, burglaries, thefts | antonyms: donations, gifts, charity | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi "ḍakaitī," meaning "robbery," ultimately from Sanskrit "dakṣiṇā," meaning "fee, honorarium."  Over time, the meaning shifted from a rightful fee to one taken by force.
