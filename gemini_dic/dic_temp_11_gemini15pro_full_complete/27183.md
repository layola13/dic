calling:/ˈkɔːlɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɔːlɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|紧急的，紧迫的|Requiring immediate attention; urgent.|例句：This is a calling matter that needs to be addressed immediately. 这是一件紧急的事情，需要立即处理。|近义词：urgent, pressing, crucial, critical, imperative|反义词: trivial, unimportant, insignificant|词性:形容词
