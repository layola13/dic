insecurity: /ˌɪnsɪˈkjʊrəti/ | n. | 不安全感；不安；不稳定 | Lack of confidence or assurance; a feeling of self-doubt, inadequacy, and vulnerability. | He overcame his insecurity and spoke confidently. 他克服了不安，自信地发言。| synonyms: anxiety, worry, doubt, apprehension | antonyms: confidence, security, self-assurance | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + security. The word "security" comes from Old French "securité" and ultimately from Latin "securitas", meaning "freedom from care".
