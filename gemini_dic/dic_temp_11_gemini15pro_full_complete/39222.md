conglomerate:/kənˈɡlɒmərət/ (UK) /kənˈɡlɑːmərət/ (US)| n. adj.|企业集团；砾岩；聚集物|A thing formed of many different and distinct parts or items grouped together.|The company is a media conglomerate.|Synonyms: aggregate, combination, mixture, composite|Antonyms: component, element, part, individual|Noun

conglomerator:/kənˈɡlɒməreɪtə(r)/ (UK) /kənˈɡlɑːməreɪtər/ (US)| n.|大型联合企业, 企业集团拥有者|A person or company that forms or owns a conglomerate.|The conglomerator acquired several smaller companies.|Synonyms: tycoon, magnate, industrialist, business leader|Antonyms: small business owner, entrepreneur|Noun
