hearest:/ˈhɪərɪst/| v.|你听见, 你听到|(archaic) Second-person singular simple present form of hear.  It is an older form, now largely replaced by "hear" in modern English.|Thou hearest the music. 你听到了音乐。|例句：If thou hearest a knocking, open the door. 如果你听到敲门声，请开门。|近义词：listen, perceive (in older usage)|反义词: ignore, disregard|verb
