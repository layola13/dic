nondeclaratory:/ˌnɒndɪˈklærətɔːri/
adjective
非声明式的；非陈述的
Not of the nature of a declaration; not declaratory.
The agreement contained a nondeclaratory provision regarding future collaborations.  该协议包含一项关于未来合作的非声明性条款。
The judge issued a nondeclaratory ruling on the matter. 法官就此事件发布了非声明性裁决。
near synonyms:  nondescriptive, unspecified, unstated, implicit
antonyms: declaratory, explicit, stated, descriptive
词性: 形容词
