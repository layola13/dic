exuviate: /ɛksˈjuːvieɪt/ | v. | 蜕皮；脱落 | To shed or cast off (a skin, shell, or other outer covering). | The snake exuviated its skin. 蛇蜕掉了它的皮。| The larva exuviated its outer cuticle. 幼虫蜕掉了它的外层角质层。 | 近义词：shed, molt, slough | 反义词: retain, keep | 词性: 动词

exuviae: /ɛksˈjuːviiː/ | n. | (动物)蜕皮；蜕壳；外皮 | The cast-off outer covering of an animal, such as the skin of a snake or the shell of an insect. | The researchers studied the exuviae of the cicadas. 研究人员研究了蝉蜕。| The exuviae of the spider were found on the web. 蜘蛛蜕在网上被发现了。 | 近义词：skin, shell, covering | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Latin *exuviātus, past participle of exuviāre (“to take off clothes, strip”), from ex (“off, out of”) + uviēs (“clothing, garment”).
