donations:/doʊˈneɪʃənz/
n.
捐赠，捐款
something that is given to a charity, especially a sum of money.
The hospital receives donations from many people.  这家医院收到很多人的捐款。
Many charities rely on donations to operate. 许多慈善机构依靠捐款来运作。
gifts, contributions, offerings
deductions, withdrawals
noun
