classmate: /ˈklæsmeɪt/ | n. | 同班同学 | A fellow member of a class at a school or college. |  She borrowed notes from a classmate. 她向一位同班同学借了笔记。

My classmates and I are working on a project together. 我和我的同学们正在一起做一个项目。

| 近义词：schoolmate, fellow student, peer | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps: stranger, non-student) | 词性: noun
