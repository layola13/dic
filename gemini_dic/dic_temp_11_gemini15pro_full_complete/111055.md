listed:/ˈlɪstɪd/ | adj. | 列出的，登记在册的，公开上市的 | Included in a list; formally or officially recorded. | The company is listed on the New York Stock Exchange.  这家公司在纽约证券交易所上市。 | synonyms: registered, recorded, cataloged | antonyms: unlisted, unregistered, excluded | adjective

listed:/ˈlɪstɪd/ | v. (past tense and past participle of list) | 列出，登记 |  Past tense and past participle of "list".  | I listed all the items we needed for the trip. 我列出了旅行所需的所有物品。 | synonyms: itemized, enumerated, cataloged | antonyms: omitted, excluded | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *listen, from Old English *lystan (“to please, desire, wish, lust”), from Proto-Germanic *lustjaną (“to lust”), from Proto-Indo-European *lewdʰ- (“to desire, lust”). More at lust. The meaning shifted from "desire" to "incline the ear" to "hear" to "enumerate."
