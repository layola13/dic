bounced:/baʊnst/ | v. | 弹跳，反弹，退回 |  spring back after hitting something; (of a cheque, etc.) be returned by a bank because there is not enough money in the account | The ball bounced off the wall.  球从墙上弹了回来。| The check bounced. 支票被退回来了。 | synonyms: rebound, recoil, spring back, bounce back, ricochet | antonyms: stick, adhere, absorb | verb

bounced:/baʊnst/ | adj. | 被退回的 | (of a cheque) returned by a bank because the payer has insufficient funds | I had two bounced checks this month. 这个月我有两张被退回的支票。|  My landlord isn't happy because the rent check bounced. 我的房东很不高兴，因为房租支票被退票了。| synonyms: returned, rejected, dishonored | antonyms: cleared, honored, accepted | adjective

PHRASE->
bounce back:  恢复；振作起来  He bounced back quickly after his illness. 他病后很快就恢复了。
bounce ideas off someone:  与某人交流想法  I like to bounce ideas off my colleagues. 我喜欢和我的同事们交流想法。
bounce something off someone:  试探某人的意见 I wanted to bounce this idea off you. 我想听听你对这个想法的意见。
bounce around:  四处奔波；弹来弹去 The kids were bouncing around the room. 孩子们在房间里蹦来蹦去。
bounce off:  从…弹开；从…跳开 The light bounced off the mirror. 光从镜子上反射回来。
