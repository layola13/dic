iguanid:/ɪˈɡwɑːnɪd/
n.|鬣蜥科动物|A lizard belonging to the family Iguanidae, which includes iguanas, anoles, and chuckwallas.|The Galapagos Islands are home to several species of iguanid.|Synonym: iguana (often used interchangeably, though technically iguanid refers to the broader family)|Antonym: N/A (no single antonym for a taxonomic family)|noun

iguanid:/ɪˈɡwɑːnɪd/
adj.|鬣蜥科的|Relating to or characteristic of the family Iguanidae.|The iguanid lizard basked in the sun.|Synonym: iguanan|Antonym: N/A|adjective
