képviselőház: /ˈkeːpviʃɛløːhaːz/
n.|代表院，众议院| Hungarian word meaning "House of Representatives" or "lower house of parliament". It refers to the lower chamber of the Hungarian National Assembly.  It's where elected representatives debate and vote on laws. |The Képviselőház passed the new budget. 众议院通过了新的预算。|Synonyms: lower house, chamber of deputies, assembly|Antonyms: upper house, senate|noun
