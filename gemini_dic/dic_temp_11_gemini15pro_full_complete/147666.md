overpositive:/ˌoʊvərˈpɒzɪtɪv/| adj.| 过分积极的，过分肯定的，过于乐观| Excessively positive; too confident or optimistic.  Characterized by excessive confidence or optimism, often to an unrealistic or unwarranted degree.| She was overpositive about her chances of winning the lottery, despite the astronomical odds. 她过于乐观地估计自己中彩票的机会，尽管概率极低。

The company's overpositive projections for the next quarter proved to be inaccurate. 该公司对下一季度的过于乐观的预测被证明是不准确的.| 近义词: overoptimistic, excessively confident, unduly sanguine, Pollyannaish | 反义词: pessimistic, negative, skeptical, realistic | 词性: adjective
