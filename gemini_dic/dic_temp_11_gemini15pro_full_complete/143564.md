outhissed:/ˌaʊtˈhɪst/
verb
嘘下台;盖过…的嘘声
to boo louder than someone or something else
The comedian was outhissed by the unruly crowd.  喜剧演员被不守规矩的观众嘘下台。
outhiss, outboo
(No clear antonym exists.  Perhaps "outcheered" or "out applauded" could be considered contextual antonyms)
词性:动词
