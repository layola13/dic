outcomplimenting:/ˌaʊtkɑːmplɪˈmentɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的恭维；过分恭维
To praise someone excessively, often to the point of seeming insincere.
He was outcomplimenting her all evening, trying to win her favor. 他整个晚上都在过分恭维她，试图赢得她的好感。
Synonyms: overpraising, flattering excessively, laying it on thick
Antonyms: criticizing, disparaging, understating
词性: 动词
