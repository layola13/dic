considerations:/kənˌsɪdəˈreɪʃənz/| n.|考虑因素；注意事项；体贴；关心|Things to think about when making a decision.|例句：After careful consideration, he decided to accept the job offer. 经过仔细考虑，他决定接受这份工作。|例句：Safety is a major consideration when designing a new car. 安全是设计新车时的一个主要考虑因素。|近义词：factors, concerns, thoughts, deliberation, reflection|反义词：disregard, neglect|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin consideratio(n-), from the verb considerare (see consider).
