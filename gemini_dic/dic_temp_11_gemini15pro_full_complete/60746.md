electrodeposition:/ɪˌlɛktrəʊdɪpəˈzɪʃən/ /ɪˌlɛktroʊdɪpəˈzɪʃən/| n.|电沉积，电镀|The process of depositing a substance on an electrode by electrolysis.  通过电解在电极上沉积物质的过程。|例句：Electrodeposition is commonly used to create a protective coating on metal parts. 电沉积通常用于在金属部件上形成保护涂层。|例句：The electrodeposition of copper onto the circuit board is a crucial step in the manufacturing process. 在电路板上电沉积铜是制造过程中的关键步骤。|近义词：electroplating, galvanizing|反义词:|noun
