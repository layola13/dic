MRI's: /ˌɛmɑːrˈaɪz/ | n. | 磁共振成像（的）|  Plural form of MRI (magnetic resonance imaging), referring to multiple scans or machines. | The doctor compared the patient's MRI's from last year to this year's. 医生比较了病人去年和今年的磁共振成像。| Synonyms:  (singular) MRI scan, magnetic resonance imaging, nuclear magnetic resonance imaging | Antonyms: None (as it's a diagnostic tool) | noun
