lipothymy: /lɪˈpɒθɪmi/ /laɪˈpɑːθɪmi/
noun
昏厥，晕厥
A sudden, transient loss of consciousness.  A feeling of faintness or lightheadedness.
She experienced a brief lipothymy after standing in the hot sun for too long.  她在烈日下站得太久，短暂地晕了过去。
synonym: faint, syncope, swoon
antonym: consciousness, alertness
词性: 名词
