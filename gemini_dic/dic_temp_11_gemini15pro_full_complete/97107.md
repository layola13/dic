infidelism:/ˌɪnfɪˈdɛlɪzəm/ | n. |不信神；不信宗教；背教；异教 |The doctrine or state of being an infidel; disbelief in a particular religion, especially Christianity; lack of religious faith. |His infidelism shocked his deeply religious family. 他的不信教震惊了他虔诚的家人。| 近义词: atheism, unbelief, nonbelief, heresy, paganism | 反义词: faith, belief, piety, orthodoxy | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From French *infidélité* "unfaithfulness" or Late Latin *infidelitas*, from Latin *infidelis* "unfaithful," from *in-* "not" + *fidelis* "faithful."
