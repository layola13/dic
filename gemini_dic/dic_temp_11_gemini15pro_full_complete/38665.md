concussive:/kənˈkʌsɪv/| adj.|震荡的；冲击的|Relating to, causing, or having the effect of a concussion.|The boxer delivered a concussive blow to his opponent's head. 拳击手给了对手头部一记震荡性打击。|近义词：jarring, shocking, stunning|反义词:soothing, calming|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *concussivus, from Latin concussus (past participle of concutere ‘shake violently’), from com- (intensive) + quatere ‘shake’.
