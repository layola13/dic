clipped:/klɪpt/| adj.|修剪的，简短的，(声音)干脆的|Cut short or shortened abruptly.  Reduced by cutting. Having a short, crisp, and precise quality (of speech).|例句：He spoke in clipped tones, revealing his impatience. 他说话简洁干脆，显露出他的不耐烦。

例句：The hedges were neatly clipped.  这些树篱被修剪得很整齐。|近义词：trimmed, pruned, abbreviated, curt, terse|反义词：long, lengthy, rambling, overgrown|词性:形容词

clipped:/klɪpt/| v.|修剪，剪短|(past tense and past participle of *clip*) To cut something short or shorten abruptly.|例句：He clipped the article from the newspaper. 他从报纸上剪下了这篇文章。

例句：She clipped her dog's nails. 她剪了狗的指甲。|近义词：trim, prune, snip, crop|反义词：extend, lengthen|词性:动词
