descendible:/dɪˈsendɪbl/
adjective
可继承的; 可传递的; 可传下去的
Capable of being passed down by inheritance or succession.
His land is descendible to his eldest son. 他的土地可传给他的长子。
Synonyms: inheritable, transmissible, heritable
Antonyms: non-inheritable, uninheritable
词性: adjective
