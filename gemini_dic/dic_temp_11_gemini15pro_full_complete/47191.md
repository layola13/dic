daycare: /ˈdeɪker/ (US) /ˈdeɪkeər/ (UK)| adj.|日托的; 日间托儿所的|Relating to or providing daycare services.|例句：Daycare costs are rising rapidly. 日托费用正在迅速上涨。|近义词：childcare, preschool|反义词: None|词性:adjective
