kinking:/ˈkɪŋkɪŋ/
n.|扭结，纠结|The kinking of the hose prevented the water from flowing.|水管的扭结阻止了水的流动。
|近义词：twisting, bending, knotting|反义词:straightening
词性:名词

kinking:/ˈkɪŋkɪŋ/
v.|（使）扭结，（使）纠结|(present participle of kink)|
The rope was kinking as he pulled it.|他拉绳子时，绳子扭结在一起了。
|近义词：twisting, bending, knotting|反义词:straightening
词性:动词


词源: From kink + -ing.  Kink itself likely originates from a Scandinavian source, related to Norwegian *kink, meaning "twist" or "bend."
