over-charities:/ˈoʊvər-ˈtʃærɪtiz/ | n. | 过度慈善，过度施舍 |  The act of giving excessively to charities, often to the point of detriment to oneself or one's responsibilities.  It implies a possible imbalance or unhealthy obsession with charitable giving. | He depleted his savings through over-charities, leaving himself with little for retirement. 他因过度慈善而耗尽了积蓄，几乎没有留下什么退休金。|  Excessive philanthropy, over-giving | Under-giving, neglect | noun
