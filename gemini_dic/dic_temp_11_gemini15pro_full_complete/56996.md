double-edgedness:/ˌdʌblˈɛdʒdnəs/| n. |双刃性，两面性|The quality of having two contrasting aspects, each of which can be beneficial or detrimental.|The double-edgedness of social media is well-documented: it can connect people but also isolate them. 社交媒体的双刃性是有据可查的：它可以连接人们，但也可能孤立他们。|Synonyms: duality, ambivalence, two-sidedness|Antonyms: straightforwardness, simplicity|noun

double-edged:/ˌdʌblˈɛdʒd/| adj. |双刃的，两面的|Having two contrasting aspects, each of which can be beneficial or detrimental.|A double-edged sword can protect you but also hurt you. 双刃剑可以保护你，但也可能伤害你。|Synonyms: two-sided, ambivalent|Antonyms: straightforward, simple|adjective
