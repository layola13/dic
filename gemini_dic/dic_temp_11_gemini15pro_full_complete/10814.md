aquiferous:/ˈækwɪfərəs/
adjective
含水的，蓄水的
Containing water; conveying water.
The aquiferous layer beneath the ground provides a vital source of fresh water.  地下含水层提供了重要的淡水来源。
aquifer, water-bearing
arid, dry
