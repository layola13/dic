orchilla:/ɔːrˈtʃɪlə/ |ɔrˈkɪlə/ | n. | orchil; archil; Roccella tinctoria |A purplish dye obtained from various lichens, especially Roccella tinctoria. |例句：The orchilla dye was prized for its vibrant purple hue.  这种 orchilla 染料因其鲜艳的紫色色调而备受珍视。

例句：Orchilla was a significant trade commodity in the past.  Orchilla 过去是一种重要的贸易商品。|近义词：cudbear, litmus |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *orchilla*, diminutive of *orcha*, from Late Latin *orca*, variant of Latin *orca*, a kind of whale, from Greek *ὄρχα* (*órkha*), a kind of whale or sea monster. The lichen was so named because of its resemblance to the whale's skin.
