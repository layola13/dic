catachresis: /ˌkætəˈkriːsɪs/ | n. | 滥用，误用，比喻不当 | The use of a word in a way that is not correct, for example using the wrong word or extending the meaning of a word in a way that most people think is wrong. |  Her use of "literally" to describe something that was clearly figurative was a classic catachresis. 她用“literally”（字面意思）来描述一件显然是比喻的事情，这是一个典型的滥用。

He referred to the "leg" of the table, which is a common catachresis. 他指的是桌子的“腿”，这是一个常见的比喻不当的用法。| 近义词：malapropism, misuse, solecism | 反义词：correct usage, proper usage | 词性: noun
