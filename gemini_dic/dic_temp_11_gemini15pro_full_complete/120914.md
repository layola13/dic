miscast:/ˌmɪsˈkæst/ | v. adj. |  错误地选角；选角不当 | to cast (an actor or actress) in an unsuitable role | The director miscast the lead role, and the movie suffered as a result. 导演选错了主角，结果电影受到了影响。 | miscast the actor as Romeo 演员被错误地选为罗密欧 | synonyms: misassign, misallocate | antonyms: well-cast, appropriately cast | verb

miscast:/ˌmɪsˈkæst/ | adj. | 选角不当的 | wrongly cast | The miscast actor struggled to portray the character convincingly.  选角不当的演员难以令人信服地刻画角色。| a miscast play  一出选角不当的戏剧 | synonyms: unsuitable, inappropriate | antonyms: suitable, appropriate | adjective
