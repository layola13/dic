overprocess:/ˌoʊvərˈprɑːses/| v. |过度加工，过度处理|To process or refine excessively, often to the point of diminishing quality or value.  |The coffee beans were overprocessed, resulting in a bitter flavor. 咖啡豆过度加工，导致味道苦涩。|Example:  Overprocessing the image made it look blurry. 过度处理图像使其看起来模糊。|Synonyms: overcook, overdo, overrefine|Antonyms: underprocess, undercook|verb

overprocess:/ˌoʊvərˈprɑːses/| n. |过度加工，过度处理|The act of processing excessively.|The overprocess of the food removed many of the nutrients. 食品的过度加工去除了许多营养成分。|Example: The overprocess damaged the material. 过度加工损坏了材料。|Synonyms: overdoing, overkill|Antonyms: underprocessing|noun
