mucous:/ˈmjuːkəs/ | adj. | 粘液的；分泌粘液的 | Relating to, containing, or resembling mucus. |  The mucous membranes of the nose produce mucus.  鼻子的粘膜会分泌粘液。| viscous, slimy, sticky | watery, dry | adjective

mucus:/ˈmjuːkəs/ | n. |  粘液 | A viscous, slimy substance secreted by mucous membranes and glands for lubrication and protection. | The cold caused him to produce excess mucus. 感冒使他产生了过多的粘液。| phlegm, snot, slime |  (no clear antonym, perhaps "serum" in specific contexts) | noun
