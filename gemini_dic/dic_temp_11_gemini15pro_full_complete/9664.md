antitakeover:/ˈænˌtiˈteɪkoʊvər/
adjective
反收购的，防止收购的
Designed to prevent or discourage hostile takeovers of a company.
The company adopted several antitakeover measures to protect itself from unwanted suitors.  这家公司采取了几项反收购措施，以保护自己免受不必要的追求者的青睐。
The board implemented an antitakeover poison pill strategy. 董事会实施了反收购的毒丸策略。
近义词: anti-acquisition, takeover-proof
反义词: pro-acquisition
