interferes:/ˌɪntərˈfɪərz/| v. |干涉，干扰，妨碍|To come into collision or opposition; to clash. To enter into, or take part in, the concerns of others; to meddle; to interpose. To act reciprocally so as to augment or diminish, or otherwise affect, one another.|The loud music interferes with my concentration.  喧闹的音乐干扰了我的注意力。|Synonyms: meddle, intervene, intrude, disrupt|Antonyms: assist, aid, support, help|verb
