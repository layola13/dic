back-pedal:/ˌbækˈpɛdl/| v. |后退；改变主意；收回前言|To reverse one's previous position or opinion; to retreat from a commitment.|The politician back-pedaled on his promise to raise taxes. 这位政治家收回了增税的承诺。|近义词：retract, withdraw, recant|反义词：persist, maintain, advance|词性:动词
back-pedal:/ˌbækˈpɛdl/| n. |后退；改变主意；收回前言|The act of reversing one's previous position or opinion.|There was a rapid back-pedal on the proposed policy. 对拟议的政策迅速作出了让步。|近义词：retraction, withdrawal, recantation|反义词：persistence, maintenance, advancement|词性:名词
