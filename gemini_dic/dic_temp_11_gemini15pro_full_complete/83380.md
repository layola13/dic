gyrostatics:/ˌdʒaɪərəˈstætɪks/ | n. | 陀螺静力学 | The study of the equilibrium of rotating bodies.  It deals with the forces and moments acting on spinning objects, such as gyroscopes. |  The principles of gyrostatics are crucial for understanding the stability of spinning tops, bicycles, and even planets. | synonyms:  rotational dynamics, gyroscopic theory | antonyms: N/A | noun

gyrostatic:/ˌdʒaɪərəˈstætɪk/ | adj. | 陀螺的，陀螺静力学的 | Relating to gyrostatics; exhibiting stability due to rotation. | The gyrostatic effect keeps the bicycle upright. | synonyms:  gyroscopic, rotational | antonyms: N/A | adjective
