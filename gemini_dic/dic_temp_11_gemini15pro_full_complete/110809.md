linkwork: /ˈlɪŋkwɜːk/ | n. |连杆机构，联动装置| A system of interconnected rods or levers, typically used to transmit motion or force. | The clock's intricate linkwork kept precise time.  时钟复杂的连杆机构保持着精准的时间。| The bicycle's linkwork transferred power from the pedals to the wheels. 自行车的联动装置将动力从踏板传递到车轮。|  synonyms: linkage, mechanism, gearing | antonyms: (No direct antonyms, perhaps consider "disconnection," "separation") | noun
