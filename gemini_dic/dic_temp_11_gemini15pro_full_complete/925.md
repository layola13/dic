abutting:/əˈbʌtɪŋ/
adjective
邻接的，毗连的
Sharing a common border; adjacent.  Located next to something, especially by sharing a boundary.
The abutting properties had a shared driveway. 这两处毗连的房产共用一个车道。
adjacent, adjoining, bordering, contiguous, neighboring
detached, separate
词性:形容词

abut:/əˈbʌt/
verb
邻接，毗连
To be next to or have a common boundary with. To touch or lean upon.
Their land abuts ours. 他们的土地与我们的土地相邻。
adjoin, border, neighbor, touch, join
separate, detach
词性:动词
