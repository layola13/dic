frictional:/ˈfrɪkʃənəl/| adj.|摩擦的；由摩擦引起的|Relating to or caused by friction; involving friction.|The frictional force slows down the moving object. 摩擦力使运动的物体减速。|Synonyms: rubbing, abrasive|Antonyms: smooth, lubricated|adjective

frictionless:/ˈfrɪkʃənləs/| adj.|无摩擦的|Having no friction; smooth.|Ideally, we want a frictionless surface for this experiment.  理想情况下，我们希望在这个实验中使用无摩擦的表面。|Synonyms: smooth, slick|Antonyms: rough, frictional|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *frictionalis, from Latin frictio (“a rubbing”), from fricare (“to rub”).  最终源于原始印欧语 *bʰregʰ- (“to rub, break”).
