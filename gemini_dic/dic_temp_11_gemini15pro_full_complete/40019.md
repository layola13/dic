consumedly:/kənˈsuːmɪdli/
adv.
极度地，过度地
To a great extent; excessively.
He was consumedly ambitious. 他极度渴望成功。
Synonyms: excessively, extremely, intensely, immoderately
Antonyms: moderately, slightly, mildly
词性: 副词


Etymology:
From consume + -ed + -ly.  "Consume" ultimately comes from the Latin "consumere," meaning "to use up entirely."  The "-ed" suffix forms an adjective, and the "-ly" suffix turns the adjective into an adverb.
