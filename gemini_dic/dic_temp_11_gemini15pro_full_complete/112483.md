lounger:/ˈlaʊndʒər/| n.|躺椅；闲逛的人；游手好闲的人|A person who spends their time relaxing or idling.|He spent his days as a beach lounger, soaking up the sun. 他成天在沙滩上闲逛，晒太阳。| 近义词:idler, loafer, relaxer|反义词:worker, hustler|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "lounge," which comes from the Old French "longis," meaning "long, slow."  The "-er" suffix denotes a person who performs the action of the verb.
