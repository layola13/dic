embossment:/ɪmˈbɒsmənt/ /ɪmˈbɑːsmənt/| n.|浮雕，凸纹，装饰|a raised design or piece of writing on a surface|The embossment on the leather cover made the journal look antique. 皮革封面的浮雕使这本日记看起来很古朴。|近义词：relief, projection, protuberance|反义词: indentation, depression|noun
