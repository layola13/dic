congealed:/kənˈdʒiːld/ | adj. | 凝固的，冻结的 |  solidified or thickened, especially by cooling. | The gravy had congealed into a thick, unappetizing lump. 肉汁凝结成了一块厚厚的、令人倒胃口的团块。 | 近义词：solidified, thickened, coagulated, jellied | 反义词：liquid, melted, fluid | 词性:形容词

congeal:/kənˈdʒiːl/ | v. |  (使)凝结，(使)冻结 |  to change from a liquid to a solid or semi-solid state, especially by cooling. |  The blood congealed around the wound.  血液在伤口周围凝结。| 近义词：solidify, thicken, coagulate, jell | 反义词：melt, liquefy | 词性:动词

词源: From Old French congeler, from Latin congelare "to freeze," from com- "together" + gelare "to freeze."
