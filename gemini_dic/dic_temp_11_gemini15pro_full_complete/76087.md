furrily:/ˈfɜːrɪli/ | adv. | 毛茸茸地，覆盖着毛皮地 | Covered with or resembling fur; in a furry manner. | The cat sat purring furrily on the rug.  猫毛茸茸地趴在地毯上咕噜咕噜叫。 | 近义词：softly, downily | 反义词:smoothly, baldly | 词性:副词

Etymology: From fur + -ily.  "Fur" comes from Middle English *fur*, from Old French *forre*, from Frankish *foder*, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *fodram* (lining, sheath).  The suffix "-ily" forms adverbs from adjectives.
