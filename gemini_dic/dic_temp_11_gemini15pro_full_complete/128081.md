nervous: /ˈnɜːrvəs/ (British) /ˈnɜːrvəs/ (American)| adj.|紧张的，不安的|Feeling worried and anxious.|例句：I'm nervous about my exam tomorrow. 我对明天的考试感到紧张。|近义词：anxious, apprehensive, edgy, tense|反义词：calm, relaxed, composed, confident|词性:形容词

nervous: /ˈnɜːrvəs/ (British) /ˈnɜːrvəs/ (American)| adj.|神经质的，易怒的|Easily agitated or irritated; highly strung.|例句：He’s been very nervous and irritable lately. 他最近非常神经质，容易暴躁。|近义词：irritable, edgy, jumpy, on edge|反义词：calm, relaxed, even-tempered|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *nervose*, from Latin *nervōsus* (“sinewy, vigorous, nervous”), from *nervus* (“sinew, nerve”).
