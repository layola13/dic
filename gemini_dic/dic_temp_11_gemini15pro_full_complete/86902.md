hemitropal:/ˌhɛmɪˈtrəʊpəl/ | adj. | 半转向的，半旋的 |  (of crystals) twinned in such a way that one half is rotated 180 degrees about an axis perpendicular to a twin plane, giving the crystal the appearance of having been sliced in half and one half rotated. | The hemitropal crystal displayed a unique symmetrical pattern.  这块半转向晶体展现出一种独特的对称图案。 | Example 2: Hemihedral forms are sometimes seen in hemitropal crystals. 在半转向晶体中，有时可以看到半面体形态。 | synonyms: twinned, rotated | antonyms: untwinned, unrotated | adjective

hemitropic:/ˌhɛmɪˈtrɒpɪk/ | adj. | 半各向同性的 | Having different physical properties in different directions, but with the properties being identical in any two directions at right angles to each other and to a third direction. | The material exhibited hemitropic behavior under stress. 这种材料在压力下表现出半各向同性行为。 | Example 2: Hemotropic materials are important in certain optical applications. 半各向同性材料在某些光学应用中很重要。 | synonyms: anisotropic | antonyms: isotropic | adjective
