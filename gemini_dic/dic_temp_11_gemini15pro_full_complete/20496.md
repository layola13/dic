bivalve:/ˈbaɪvælv/ | n. | 双壳贝类 | An aquatic mollusk that has a compressed body enclosed within a hinged shell, such as an oyster, clam, or mussel. |  Example: Oysters are a type of bivalve. 牡蛎是一种双壳贝类。

Example:  Many bivalves are filter feeders. 许多双壳贝类是滤食动物。
| 近义词: clam, mussel, oyster, scallop | 反义词: univalve | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *bivalvis* (“having two valves”), from *bi-* (“two”) + *valva* (“leaf of a folding door, valve”).
