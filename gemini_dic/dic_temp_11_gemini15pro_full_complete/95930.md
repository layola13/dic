incrustation:/ɪnˌkrʌˈsteɪʃən/ /ɪnˌkrʌˈsteɪʃn/ | n. |结壳，外壳；镶嵌装饰|A hard coating or deposit of something, such as minerals or dirt.  A decorative coating or overlay.|例句：The old pipe was covered with a thick incrustation of limescale. 老旧的管道上覆盖着一层厚厚的水垢。

例句：The jeweler added an incrustation of diamonds to the gold ring. 珠宝商在金戒指上镶嵌了钻石。|近义词：coating, covering, deposit, layer, crust, scale, plaque, encrustation|反义词: erosion, wearing away|词性:noun


incrust:/ɪnˈkrʌst/ /ɪnˈkrʌst/ | vt. |使结壳；用…包覆；镶嵌|To cover or decorate with a hard coating or layer.|例句：The artist incrusted the vase with colorful stones. 艺术家用彩色的石头装饰了花瓶。

例句：Barnacles had incrusted the hull of the ship. 藤壶已经覆盖了船体。|近义词：coat, cover, overlay, encrust|反义词: uncover, expose|词性:verb
