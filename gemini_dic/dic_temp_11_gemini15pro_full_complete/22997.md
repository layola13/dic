bougainvillea:/ˌbuːɡənˈvɪliə/ | n. | 九重葛，叶子花 | A tropical climbing plant with brightly colored, papery bracts surrounding small flowers. |  Example 1: The bougainvillea climbed all over the wall, creating a vibrant display of color. 九重葛爬满了整面墙，营造出鲜艳的色彩。

Example 2:  She planted a bougainvillea in a pot on her balcony. 她在阳台上种了一盆九重葛。| 近义词: paperflower (纸花, 非正式) | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑一些不开花的植物，例如 fern (蕨类植物)) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the name of Louis Antoine de Bougainville, a French admiral and explorer (1729-1811), who discovered the plant in Brazil.
