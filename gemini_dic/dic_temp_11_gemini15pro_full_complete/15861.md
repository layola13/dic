ballets:/bæˈleɪz/
n.
芭蕾舞剧；芭蕾舞表演
A theatrical dance form characterized by light, graceful movements and elaborate costumes.  A performance of ballet.
The company will be performing two ballets this season.  这个公司将在本季演出两部芭蕾舞剧。
synonyms: ballet performances, dance performances
antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific art form, could consider contrasting forms like modern dance or hip-hop)
noun
