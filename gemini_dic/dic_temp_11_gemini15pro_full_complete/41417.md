cornerstone:/ˈkɔːrnərˌstoʊn/ (US) /ˈkɔːnəstəʊn/ (UK)| n. |基石，奠基石，基础|Something of great importance that everything else depends on.  A stone that forms the base of a corner of a building, often laid in a special ceremony.|The family unit is the cornerstone of society. 家庭单元是社会的基础。|synonyms: foundation, base, bedrock, underpinning, linchpin, keystone|antonyms: apex, peak, summit|noun
