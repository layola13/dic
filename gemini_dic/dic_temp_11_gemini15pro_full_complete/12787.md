assimilative:/əˈsɪməˌleɪtɪv/
adjective
同化的；吸收的
Relating to or characterized by assimilation; able to assimilate.
Plants have assimilative roots to absorb nutrients.  植物有同化根来吸收养分。
The company has a very assimilative culture, welcoming people from all backgrounds. 这家公司有着非常包容的文化，欢迎来自不同背景的人。
近义词:absorptive, integrative, incorporative
反义词:resistant, repellent

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin assimilativus, from Latin assimilare "to make similar," from ad- "to" + similis "similar."
