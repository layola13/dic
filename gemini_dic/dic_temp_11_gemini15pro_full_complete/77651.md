gelation:/dʒɪˈleɪʃən/
n.
胶凝；胶凝作用
The process of a liquid transforming into a gel.
The gelation of the mixture occurred after it cooled.  这种混合物冷却后就发生了胶凝。
solidification, coagulation, gelling
melting, liquefaction
noun
