houseman:/ˈhaʊsmən/ (US), /ˈhaʊzmən/ (UK)| n.| 住院医生，房客，男仆 |A male resident physician, especially one in the first year of training after medical school.  Also, a male resident in a lodging house or a male servant in a household. |例句：The houseman was exhausted after a 36-hour shift.  住院医生在值了36个小时的班后筋疲力尽。

例句：He worked as a houseman in a large estate before attending university.  他在上大学之前在一个大庄园里当男仆。|近义词：resident (physician), intern, lodger, servant|反义词:attending physician (for resident), landlord (for lodger), employer (for servant)|noun
