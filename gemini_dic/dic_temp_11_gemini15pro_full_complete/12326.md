asepsis:/əˈsɛpsɪs/
noun
无菌，无菌状态
The absence of bacteria, viruses, and other microorganisms.
The operating room must maintain strict asepsis.  手术室必须保持严格的无菌状态。
Sterility, hygiene
Sepsis, infection
