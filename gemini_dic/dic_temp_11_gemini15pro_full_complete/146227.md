overexploited: /ˌoʊvərɪkˈsplɔɪtɪd/
adjective
过度开发的；过度利用的
having been exploited excessively
The overexploited fishing grounds are now barren.  过度捕捞的渔场现在一片荒芜。
The Amazon rainforest is being overexploited for timber and farmland. 亚马逊雨林正被过度开发以获取木材和农田。
近义词: overused, overtaxed, exhausted
反义词: underused, conserved, protected
词性: adjective

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "exploit" (from Old French "exploitier" meaning "to make use of, to profit from").  由表示“过度”的前缀"over-" 和动词"exploit"（源于古法语 "exploitier"，意为“利用，从中获利”）组成。
