frail:/freɪl/| adj.|虚弱的，脆弱的；易碎的|Easily broken or damaged, and physically weak.|(referring to a person) Physically weak and delicate.|例句：Her frail hands trembled as she poured the tea. 她那双虚弱的手在倒茶时颤抖着。

例句：The bridge is old and frail. 这座桥又旧又脆弱。|近义词：weak, delicate, fragile, feeble|反义词：strong, robust, sturdy|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *fraile, *frele (“brittle, fragile”), from Latin fragilis (“breakable, fragile”), from frangere (“to break”).
