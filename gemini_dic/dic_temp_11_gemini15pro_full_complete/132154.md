noncurdling:/ˌnɒnˈkɜːrdlɪŋ/
adjective
不凝结的
Not curdling; remaining smooth and creamy; resistant to curdling.
This special milk is noncurdling, perfect for making creamy sauces.  这种特殊的牛奶不会凝结，非常适合制作奶油酱汁。
The recipe calls for a noncurdling yogurt.  食谱要求使用不凝结的酸奶。
Synonym: noncoagulating, stable
Antonym: curdling, coagulating
词性: adjective
