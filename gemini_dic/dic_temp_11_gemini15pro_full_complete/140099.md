off-licence:/ˌɒf ˈlaɪsns/ (UK), /ˌɔf ˈlaɪsns/ (US)| n.|非持牌酒吧; 酒类专卖店|A shop licensed to sell alcoholic beverages for consumption off the premises.|I bought a bottle of wine from the off-licence. 我从酒类专卖店买了一瓶葡萄酒。|Synonyms: liquor store, bottle shop (especially Australian), package store (US)|Antonyms: pub, bar|Noun

off-license:/ˌɒf ˈlaɪsns/ (UK), /ˌɔf ˈlaɪsns/ (US)| n.|非营业场所销售酒精饮料的许可证|A licence permitting the sale of alcoholic beverages for consumption off the premises.|The shop applied for an off-license. 这家商店申请了非营业场所销售酒精饮料的许可证。|Synonyms: liquor license,  package store license (US)|Antonyms: on-license|Noun

词源： "off" 指的是"离开"或"不在"，而 "licence"（或美式拼写 "license"）指的是官方许可。因此，"off-licence" 指的是允许在场所外消费酒精饮料的许可证，以及持有此类许可证的商店。
