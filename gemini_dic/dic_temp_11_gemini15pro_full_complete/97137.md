infiltrative:/ɪnˈfɪltrətɪv/| adj.|渗透的，浸润的|Relating to or characterized by infiltration; spreading by or involving infiltration.  Spreading gradually throughout a tissue or organ.|The doctor discovered an infiltrative tumor in the patient's lung. 医生在病人的肺部发现了一个浸润性肿瘤。|Synonyms: invasive, pervasive, permeating|Antonyms: localized, contained|adjective

infiltration:/ˌɪnfɪlˈtreɪʃən/| n.|渗透，浸润|The act of entering or gaining access to a place or organization surreptitiously, especially in order to acquire secret information or cause damage. Also, the process of a liquid slowly permeating a substance.|The infiltration of enemy spies into the government was a major security breach. 敌方间谍渗透进政府是一个重大的安全漏洞。|Synonyms: penetration, permeation, incursion|Antonyms: withdrawal, retreat|noun
