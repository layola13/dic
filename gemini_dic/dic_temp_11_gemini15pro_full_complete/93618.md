imbiber:/ɪmˈbaɪbər/ | n. | 饮者；吸收者 |  A person who drinks alcohol, especially in large quantities; someone who absorbs or takes in something, like knowledge or information. | He was a notorious imbiber of fine wines. 他是一个出了名的嗜饮好酒的人。

The sponge is an excellent imbiber of water. 海绵是很好的吸水物。 | 近义词：drinker, consumer, absorber | 反义词：teetotaler, abstainer | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin imbībō ("I drink in, absorb"), from Latin in ("in") + bibō ("I drink").
