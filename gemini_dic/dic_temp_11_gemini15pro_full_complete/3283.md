affixation:/ˌæfɪkˈseɪʃn/ | n. | 词缀法；附加；粘贴 | The process of adding an affix to a word to create a new word or change the meaning of the existing word. |  Prefixes, suffixes, and infixes are all examples of affixation. 词缀、后缀和中缀都是词缀法的例子。 | derivation, formation |  removal | noun
