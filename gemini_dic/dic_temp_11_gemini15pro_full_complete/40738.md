conveyancers:/kənˈveɪənsərz/
n.
产权转让律师；不动产转让人
Lawyers who specialize in the legal aspects of buying and selling property.
例句：The conveyancers are preparing the contracts for the sale of the house. 产权转让律师正在准备房屋销售合同。

例句：We hired conveyancers to handle the legal paperwork for our new property. 我们聘请了产权转让律师来处理我们新房产的法律文件。

近义词：property lawyers, real estate attorneys
反义词: (No direct antonym, but one could consider someone obstructing a sale)
词性:Noun
