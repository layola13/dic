diuretic: /ˌdaɪjʊˈrɛtɪk/ | adj. n. | 利尿的；利尿剂 |  (adj.) Increasing the volume of urine excreted. (n.) A substance that promotes the production of urine. |  (adj.) The doctor prescribed a diuretic medication to reduce the swelling. 医生开了利尿药来消肿。(n.)  This diuretic helps to flush excess water from the body. 这种利尿剂有助于排出体内多余的水分。 | 近义词: (n.) water pill | 反义词: antidiuretic | 词性: adjective, noun
