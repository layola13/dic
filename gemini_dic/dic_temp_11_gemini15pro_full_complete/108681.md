leak:/liːk/| n. v.|泄漏，漏洞|An accidental hole or crack that lets something such as liquid or gas escape, or the escaping of liquid or gas through such a hole or crack.  |The roof sprang a leak during the storm. 屋顶在暴风雨中出现了裂缝漏水。|synonyms: drip, seepage, trickle, escape, outflow,  |antonyms: seal, blockage|noun

leak:/liːk/| v.|泄漏，透露|To allow liquid or gas to escape through a hole or crack, or (of liquid or gas) to escape.|The pipe is leaking. 管道正在漏水。|synonyms:  drip, ooze, seep,  disclose, reveal, divulge|antonyms: contain, seal|verb
