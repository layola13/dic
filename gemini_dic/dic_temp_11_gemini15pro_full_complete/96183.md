indestructibilities:/ˌɪndɪˌstrʌktɪˈbɪlɪtiz/
n.
不可摧毁性；坚不可摧
The indestructibilities of certain materials make them ideal for specific applications.
某些材料的不可摧毁性使其成为特定应用的理想选择。
The artifact's indestructibilities fascinated the archaeologists.
这件文物的不可摧毁性让考古学家们着迷。
Synonym:  invincibility, impenetrability,  imperishability
Antonym: fragility, vulnerability, destructibility
词性: noun (plural)
