blel: /blɛl/
Noun
无意义词,胡言乱语
A meaningless word, gibberish.
The baby was babbling, saying "blel blel blel."  婴儿在咿呀学语，说着“blel blel blel”。
近义词: babble, gibberish, nonsense
反义词: sense, meaning
词性:n.


blel: /blɛl/
Verb
胡言乱语，发出无意义的声音
To babble, to make meaningless sounds.
He just blelled on and on, making no sense. 他只是不停地胡言乱语，毫无意义。
近义词: babble, gibberish
反义词: articulate
词性:v.


blel: /blɛl/
Adjective
无意义的，胡言乱语的
Meaningless, gibberish-like.
His blel pronouncements were ignored. 他那些无意义的声明被忽略了。
近义词: nonsensical, meaningless
反义词: meaningful, sensible
词性:adj.
