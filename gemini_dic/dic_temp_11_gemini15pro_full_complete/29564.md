catchment:/ˈkætʃmənt/| n.|集水, 蓄水|The dam increased the catchment of the reservoir.|大坝增加了水库的蓄水量。|Synonyms: collection, accumulation|Antonyms: discharge, release|noun

catchment:/ˈkætʃmənt/| n.|服务区（尤指学校、医院等）|The hospital serves a large catchment area.|这家医院服务于一个很大的服务区。|Synonyms: area, region, district|Antonyms: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "catch" + "-ment" (suffix forming nouns of action or result).  "Catch" ultimately comes from the Latin "capere" meaning "to take, seize."
