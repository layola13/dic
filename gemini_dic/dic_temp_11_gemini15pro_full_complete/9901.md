apatosauruses:/əˌpætəˈsɔːrəsiz/| n. |迷惑龙（复数）|A very large herbivorous dinosaur of the Late Jurassic period, with a long neck and tail. It was formerly called Brontosaurus. |例句：Apatosauruses were among the largest animals to ever walk the Earth. 迷惑龙是有史以来在地球上行走过的最大的动物之一。

例句：The museum's exhibit featured skeletons of several apatosauruses.  博物馆的展览展出了几具迷惑龙的骨架。


|近义词：brontosaurus|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
