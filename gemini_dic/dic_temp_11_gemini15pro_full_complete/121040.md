misconclusion:/ˌmɪskənˈkluːʒən/
n.
错误的结论；误解
a wrong conclusion; a mistaken understanding
He jumped to a misconclusion based on the limited information. 他根据有限的信息草率得出了错误的结论。

近义词:fallacy, error, misconception, misinterpretation
反义词:correct conclusion, accurate interpretation

词性:noun

词源:
From mis- + conclusion.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "wrong" or "bad." "Conclusion" comes from the Latin "concludere," meaning "to shut up, enclose, finish."  Thus, "misconclusion" literally means a "wrong ending" or a "bad inference."
