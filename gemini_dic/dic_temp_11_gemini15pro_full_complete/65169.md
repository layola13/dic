ethnographical: /ˌeθnəˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
人种志的；民族志学的
Relating to ethnography, the scientific description of peoples and cultures with their customs, habits, and mutual differences.
The researcher conducted an ethnographic study of the remote tribe.  研究人员对这个偏远部落进行了人种志研究。
Ethnographic research provides valuable insights into different cultures. 人种志研究为了解不同文化提供了宝贵的见解。

近义词: anthropological, ethnologic
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider terms like "theoretical," "abstract," "non-empirical" depending on context)
词性: adjective
