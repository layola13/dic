latins:/ˈlætɪnz/| n.|拉丁人；拉丁语；拉丁字母|People who lived in ancient Latium or who spoke Latin, or their descendants; the Latin language; or languages descended from Latin.|The Latins were the original inhabitants of Latium, the region around Rome. 拉丁人是拉丁姆(罗马周围的地区)的原始居民。|The influence of Latins on European culture is profound. 拉丁人对欧洲文化的影响是深远的。|近义词：Romans, Italians (in some contexts)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-Latins or barbarians in a historical context)|词性:noun
