baubling:/ˈbɔːblɪŋ/| n.| 小玩意儿，廉价饰品|A small, showy trinket of little value. |She was fascinated by the baublings at the market stall. 她被市场摊位上的小玩意儿迷住了。|The child clutched a handful of plastic baublings.  孩子手里抓着一把塑料小玩意儿。|Synonyms: trinket, knick-knack, gewgaw, trifle|Antonyms: treasure, heirloom|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "bauble" + "-ing" (suffix forming nouns from verbs). "Bauble" itself comes from the Old French "baubel" (toy, trinket), of uncertain origin.
