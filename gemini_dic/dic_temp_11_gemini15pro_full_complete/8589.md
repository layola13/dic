anticlergy:/ˌæntiˈklɜːrdʒi/ | adj. | 反教权的，反对神职人员的 | Opposed to the clergy or their power and influence. | The anticlergy sentiment was strong among the revolutionaries. 革命者中反教权情绪很强烈。 |  The writer's anticlergy stance was evident in his satirical portrayal of the bishop.  这位作家对神职人员的反对立场在他对主教的讽刺描写中显而易见。 | 近义词: anticlerical | 反义词: pro-clergy, pro-clerical | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From anti- + clergy.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to," and "clergy" refers to the body of ordained ministers in a Christian church.
