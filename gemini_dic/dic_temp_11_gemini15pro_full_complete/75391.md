frozen:/ˈfroʊzn/ (US) /ˈfrəʊzn/ (UK)| adj.| 冻结的，冷冻的，结冰的；(因恐惧等)僵住的，呆若木鸡的|Covered with or containing ice; extremely cold; (of a person) unable to move or act because of fear, shock, etc.|The lake was frozen solid.  湖面完全结冰了。


The ground was frozen hard. 地面冻得很硬。


She stood frozen in fear. 她吓得呆若木鸡。


His assets were frozen by the government. 他的资产被政府冻结了。


近义词：icy, frigid, chilled, paralyzed|反义词:thawed, melted, liquid|词性:adjective
