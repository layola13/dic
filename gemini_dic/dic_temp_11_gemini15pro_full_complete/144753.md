over-claim:/ˈoʊvərkleɪm/| n. |过分的要求，过分的索赔|An excessive or unjustified claim.|The insurance company rejected his over-claim. 保险公司拒绝了他的过分索赔。|近义词：exaggeration, overstatement|反义词: understatement|词性:名词
