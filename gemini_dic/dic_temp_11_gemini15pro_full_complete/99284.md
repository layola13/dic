intercontradictory:/ˌɪntərˌkɑːntrəˈdɪktəri/ | adj. |相互矛盾的，自相矛盾的 | Containing elements which contradict one another. | Her testimony was intercontradictory, making it difficult for the jury to believe her story.  她的证词自相矛盾，使得陪审团难以相信她的故事。| Example: The politician's statements were intercontradictory, leaving the public confused about his stance.  这位政治家的声明相互矛盾，让公众对他立场感到困惑。|Synonyms: inconsistent, conflicting, paradoxical, incompatible | Antonyms: consistent, compatible, harmonious | adjective
