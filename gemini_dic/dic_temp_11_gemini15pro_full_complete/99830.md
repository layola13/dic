intermission:/ˌɪntərˈmɪʃən/| n.|休息；幕间休息；暂停|A break between parts of a performance, such as a play, opera, or concert.|The intermission gave us time to get some refreshments. 幕间休息让我们有时间去买些点心。|
The basketball game resumed after a short intermission. 篮球比赛在短暂的休息后重新开始。|synonyms: break, pause, interval, recess|antonyms: continuation, resumption|noun
