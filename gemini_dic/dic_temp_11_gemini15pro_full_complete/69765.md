ferrite:/ˈfɛrˌaɪt/ /ˈfɛraɪt/ | n. | 铁氧体 | A ceramic material made by mixing and firing iron oxide with other metallic oxides, used for its magnetic properties in electronic equipment. |  Ferrites are used in the cores of inductors and transformers. 铁氧体用于电感器和变压器的磁芯。| synonyms: ceramic magnet, magnetic oxide | antonyms: non-magnetic material | noun

ferrite:/ˈfɛrˌaɪt/ /ˈfɛraɪt/ | n. | 铁素体 | An extremely soft and ductile form of iron that exists only at temperatures exceeding 1392°C. | The ferrite phase is crucial in the heat treatment of steel. 铁素体相在钢的热处理中至关重要。| synonyms: alpha iron | antonyms: austenite | noun
