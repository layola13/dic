goaded:/ˈɡoʊdɪd/| v. |刺激；煽动；驱使|Provoked or urged on. |He was goaded into a fight by the bullies. 他被恶霸煽动与人打架。|近义词：prodded, urged, incited, provoked|反义词：dissuaded, discouraged|verb
goad:/ɡoʊd/| n. vt.|刺激；激励；(赶牲口用的)尖棒|A pointed stick used to urge animals on; something that urges or stimulates someone to action.|The fear of failure was a constant goad. 对失败的恐惧是一种持续的刺激。|近义词：stimulus, incentive, spur, motivation|反义词：deterrent, discouragement|noun, verb
