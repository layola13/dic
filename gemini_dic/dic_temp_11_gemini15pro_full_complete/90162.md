hothoused:/ˈhɑːthaʊzd/ /ˈhɒthaʊzd/| adj. |温室培养的；揠苗助长的；过分保护的|Educated or trained intensively over a short period of time, often at an early age, sometimes with harmful effects.|例句：The hothoused children were academically advanced but lacked social skills. 这些温室培养的孩子学业很先进，但缺乏社交技能。

例句：He felt hothoused at the elite academy. 他在那所精英学院感觉被揠苗助长。|近义词：forced, accelerated, pressured, sheltered, overprotected|反义词：neglected, underdeveloped, free-range|词性:形容词
