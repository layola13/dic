haemin:/ˈhiːmɪn/ | n. | 血红素 |  A red protein containing iron, responsible for transporting oxygen in the blood of vertebrates. |  Hemoglobin is the main protein in red blood cells that carries haemin. 血红蛋白是红细胞中携带血红素的主要蛋白质。|  Myoglobin, like hemoglobin, is a haemin-containing protein. 肌红蛋白，像血红蛋白一样，是一种含血红素的蛋白质。| synonyms: heme | antonyms: N/A | noun
