briskness:/ˈbrɪsknəs/| n.|敏捷，轻快，活泼；清新，凉爽|The quality of being energetic and quick; the quality of being stimulating and refreshing.|The briskness of the morning air was invigorating.  早晨空气的清新令人精神焕发。|近义词：liveliness, quickness, energy, freshness|反义词：slowness, sluggishness, dullness|词性:名词

词源: From the Middle French word *brisque,* meaning "nimble, lively," probably of Celtic origin.
