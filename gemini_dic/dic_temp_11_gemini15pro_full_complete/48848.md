deforested:/ˌdiːˈfɔːrɪstɪd/
adjective
已砍伐森林的;森林被毁的
cleared of forests
例句：The deforested land was now barren and dry.  这片被砍伐的土地现在贫瘠干燥。

例句：Vast areas of rainforest have been deforested. 大片的雨林已经被砍伐。

近义词：logged, clear-cut, denuded
反义词：forested, wooded, afforested
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "remove, reduce") + forest + -ed (past participle suffix). The prefix "de-" comes from Latin, meaning "down from, away, off; concerning." "Forest" comes from Old French "forest" (also "forest"), from Late Latin "foresta" "open wood," of Germanic origin.
