monom:/ˈmɑːnəm/ | n. |单项式|A single term in mathematics, consisting of a product of numbers and variables raised to non-negative integer powers. |Example: 3x^2y is a monomial.  3x²y是一个单项式。| Example:  The expression 5x is a monomial. 表达式5x是一个单项式。 |Synonym: term |Antonym: polynomial |noun
