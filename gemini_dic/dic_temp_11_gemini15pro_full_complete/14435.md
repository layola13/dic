auxetophone:/ɔːkˈsɛtəˌfoʊn/ | n. | 助音器，扩音器 | A device designed to amplify the sound of musical instruments, especially string instruments, without the use of electricity. It works by mechanically coupling the instrument to a large horn or resonator. | The musician used an auxetophone to make his violin sound louder in the large hall.  音乐家使用助音器使他的小提琴在大厅里听起来更响亮。| synonym: amplifier, loudspeaker, megaphone | antonym: muffler, silencer | noun
