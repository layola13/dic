outvotes:/ˌaʊtˈvəʊts/
verb
以票数胜过，投票击败
defeats by a higher number of votes; wins over by voting.
The proposal was outvoted by a narrow margin. 这项提议以微弱劣势被否决了。
The mayor was outvoted by the city council. 市长被市议会投票否决。
近义词: defeat, overrule
反义词: lose to, be defeated by
词性: v.


outvotes:/ˌaʊtˈvəʊts/
noun
胜过票数的行为或结果
The act or result of defeating by a higher number of votes.
The outvote was a surprise to many. 这次投票结果让很多人感到意外。
The chairman announced the outvote on the new policy. 主席宣布了新政策的投票结果。
近义词: victory, win
反义词: defeat, loss
词性: n.

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding, surpassing") + "vote" (from Old French "voter," ultimately from Latin "votum," a vow)
