cabbalas:/kəˈbɑːləz/| n. |卡巴拉，犹太神秘主义|A system of Jewish mystical interpretation of the Bible, based on the Hebrew alphabet.|He studied the ancient cabbalas, hoping to unlock hidden truths. 他研究古代卡巴拉，希望能解开隐藏的真理。|近义词：mysticism, esotericism|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps: rationalism, literalism) | noun
