nondefinably:/ˌnɒndɪˈfaɪnəbli/ | adv. | 不可定义地，无法明确地 | Not capable of being defined or explained clearly.  |  The feeling was nondefinably strange. 这种感觉莫名的奇怪。 | 近义词: inexplicably, indescribably | 反义词: definably, explicitly | 词性: 副词

词源: non- (not) + definable + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Definable" comes from the Latin "definire" (to limit, define).
