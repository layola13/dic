ballisticians:/ˌbælɪˈstɪʃənz/
noun
弹道学家（复数）
Experts in ballistics, the science of projectiles and firearms.
The ballisticians analyzed the trajectory of the bullet. 弹道学家分析了子弹的轨迹。

ballistician:/ˌbælɪˈstɪʃən/
noun
弹道学家
An expert in ballistics, the science of projectiles and firearms.
The ballistician determined the type of gun used in the crime. 弹道学家确定了犯罪中使用的枪支类型。


Synonyms:
firearms experts, projectile specialists
Antonyms:
N/A
词性: 名词


Etymology:
From ballistic + -ian. Ballistic from French balistique, from Greek ballein "to throw."
