glycyrrhizin:/ˌɡlɪsərˈaɪzɪn/ |n.|甘草甜素|A sweet-tasting crystalline glycoside C₄₄H₆₂O₁₆ extracted from licorice root and used chiefly in flavoring and in medicine.  It is the main sweet-tasting compound of licorice root. |Glycyrrhizin is used in some candies and pharmaceuticals. 甘草甜素用于一些糖果和药物中。|Synonyms: licorice extract, glycyrrhizic acid|Antonyms: N/A|noun

glycyrrhizin:/ˌɡlɪsərˈaɪzɪn/ |n.|甘草酸|A sweet-tasting crystalline glycoside C₄₄H₆₂O₁₆ extracted from licorice root and used chiefly in flavoring and in medicine. It is 50-150 times sweeter than sugar.|Glycyrrhizin can be used as a sweetener in food products. 甘草酸可以用作食品中的甜味剂。|Synonyms: licorice extract, glycyrrhizic acid|Antonyms: N/A|noun
