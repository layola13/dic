overacted:/ˌoʊvərˈæktɪd/| adj.|表演过火；夸张的|Performed in an exaggerated or theatrical manner, often appearing unnatural or artificial.|The villain's death scene was so overacted that the audience laughed.  反派角色的死亡场景表演得太过火了，以至于观众都笑了。|The lead actress overacted her part, making the character seem unbelievable. 女主角的表演太过火，让角色显得不可信。|近义词：exaggerated, hammy, melodramatic|反义词：understated, subtle|词性:形容词
overact:/ˌoʊvərˈækt/| v.|表演过火；夸张表演|To act in an exaggerated or theatrical manner.|He tends to overact when he's nervous. 他紧张的时候容易表演过火。|She overacted her role in the play. 她在戏剧中表演得太过火了。|近义词：exaggerate, ham|反义词：underplay|词性:动词
