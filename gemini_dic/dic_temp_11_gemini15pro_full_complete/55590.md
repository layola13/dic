disunion's:/dɪsˈjuːnjənz/
n.
分裂，不和
lack of unity or agreement; separation.
The Civil War was a result of the disunion's grip on the nation. 南北战争是国家分裂的结果。
近义词:division, discord, schism, split
反义词:unity, harmony, concord, agreement
词性:名词 (possessive form)


Etymology:
From dis- + union + -'s (possessive suffix).  "Dis-" is a Latin prefix meaning "apart," "asunder," "lack of," "reversal." "Union" comes from the Old French "union" and ultimately from the Latin "unus" meaning "one."
