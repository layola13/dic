animative:/ˈænɪmətɪv/
adjective
赋予生命的，有生命的，使有生气的
Having the power to give life or motion; enlivening.
The animative power of nature.  大自然赋予生命的力量。

Her presence had an animative effect on the party. 她的出现让派对活跃了起来。

近义词: enlivening, vitalizing, invigorating, life-giving, animating
反义词: inanimate, lifeless, deadening

词性: adjective
