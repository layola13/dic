belittlers: /bɪˈlɪtlərz/ | n. | 轻视者，贬低者 | People who belittle others, making them feel small or unimportant. |  Those belittlers never have anything positive to say. 那些轻视者从来不说任何积极的话。| Synonyms: detractors, disparagers, criticizers | Antonyms: encouragers, supporters, praisers | noun

belittle: /bɪˈlɪtl/ | vt. | 轻视，贬低 | To make someone or something seem unimportant. |  He belittled her achievements, making her feel insignificant. 他贬低了她的成就，让她觉得自己微不足道。| Synonyms: disparage, denigrate, minimize | Antonyms: praise, extol, magnify | verb
