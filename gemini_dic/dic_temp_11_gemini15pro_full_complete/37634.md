commutable:/kəˈmjuːtəbl/ /kəˈmjuːtəbəl/
adjective
可通勤的
Able to travel some distance between one's home and place of work on a regular basis.
My new job is commutable by train. 我的新工作可以坐火车通勤。
The office is easily commutable from most parts of the city.  这个办公室从城市的大部分地区都很容易通勤。
近义词: accessible, reachable, convenient
反义词:  incommutable, inaccessible
词性: adjective
