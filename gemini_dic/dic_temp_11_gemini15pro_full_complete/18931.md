bespeckle:/bɪˈspɛkəl/
verb
点缀；散布；使有斑点
cover or mark with numerous small spots or speckles.
The artist bespeckled the canvas with tiny dots of paint.  艺术家在画布上点缀着细小的颜料点。
The bird's eggs were bespeckled with brown.  这只鸟的蛋上散布着褐色斑点。
近义词: dot, spot, fleck, stipple, pepper
反义词: clear, plain
词性: verb
