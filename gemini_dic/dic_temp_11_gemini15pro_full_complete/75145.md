fritfly:/ˈfrɪtflaɪ/| n. |麦秆蝇；瑞典蝇|A small black fly (Oscinella frit), the larva of which feeds on cereals, especially oats, causing substantial damage. |The fritfly is a major pest of cereal crops. 麦秆蝇是谷类作物的主要害虫。| The fritfly infestation significantly reduced the oat yield.  麦秆蝇的侵扰大大降低了燕麦的产量。 |近义词：cereal fly, pest |反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific insect) |词性:noun
