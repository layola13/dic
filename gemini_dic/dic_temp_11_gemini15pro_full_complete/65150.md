ethnarch:/ˈeθnɑːrk/ | n. |民族首领，族长|A ruler or governor of a people or nation, especially one appointed by a superior power.  The term often implies limited autonomy. | Herod was appointed ethnarch of Judea. 希律被任命为犹太的民族首领。 | synonyms: governor, ruler, tetrarch, prince, chief | antonyms: subject, follower | noun
