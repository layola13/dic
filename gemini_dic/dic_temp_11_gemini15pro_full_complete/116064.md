martinetisms:/ˌmɑːrtɪˈniːtɪzəmz/ | n. |  马提尼主义；带有马提尼酒风格的言行举止 |  The affectations and pronouncements characteristic of a martini drinker, often associated with sophistication, wit, and a slightly cynical worldview.  It can refer to the language, behavior, or attitudes often associated with those who frequent sophisticated bars and cocktail lounges. |  Her dry wit and penchant for sophisticated wordplay were pure martinetisms. 她那种冷幽默和对复杂文字游戏的偏爱是纯粹的马提尼主义。 | synonyms: affectations, mannerisms, pretensions | antonyms: naturalness, sincerity, artlessness | noun
