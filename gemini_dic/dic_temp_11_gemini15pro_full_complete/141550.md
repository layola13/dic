PHRASE->
oppose to something:  反对某事 | I am opposed to the use of nuclear weapons. 我反对使用核武器。
as opposed to:  而不是，与...相对 | He prefers tea as opposed to coffee.  与咖啡相比，他更喜欢茶。
strongly oppose:  强烈反对 |  We strongly oppose the government's decision. 我们强烈反对政府的决定。
fiercely oppose:  激烈反对 |  The protesters fiercely opposed the new policy. 抗议者激烈反对新政策。
oppose a motion:  反对一项动议 |  The board members opposed the motion to increase fees. 董事会成员反对提高费用的动议。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *opposer, from Latin oppōnere (“to set against, oppose”), from ob- (“against”) + pōnere (“to put, place”).
