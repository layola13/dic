beetles:/ˈbiːtlz/| n.|甲虫|A common name for insects of the order Coleoptera, characterized by hard, shell-like front wings (elytra).|例句：The beetles scuttled across the forest floor. 甲虫快速地穿过森林地面。

|例句：Lady beetles are beneficial insects that eat aphids. 瓢虫是有益的昆虫，它们吃蚜虫。|近义词：insect, bug |反义词:|n.
