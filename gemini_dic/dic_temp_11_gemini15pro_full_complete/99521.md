intergalactic:/ˌɪntərɡəˈlæktɪk/| adj.|星系间的，银河间的|Existing or occurring between galaxies.  Relating to the vast spaces between galaxies.|例句：The intergalactic medium is the diffuse gas that exists between galaxies. 星系际介质是存在于星系之间的弥散气体。|例句：Scientists are searching for intergalactic signals. 科学家们正在寻找星系间的信号。|近义词：cosmic, interstellar|反义词：intragalactic (within a galaxy)|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- (between) + galactic (relating to a galaxy).  "Galactic" ultimately derives from the Greek word "galaxias" (γαλαξίας), meaning "milky way".
