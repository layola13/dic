nasalization:/ˌneɪzələˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌneɪzəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |鼻音化；鼻化|The production of a speech sound with the velum lowered so that some air escapes through the nose.|The nasalization of vowels is a common feature in French.|近义词: nasalisation (British spelling)|反义词: oralization|词性:noun

nasalize:/ˈneɪzəlaɪz/| v. |使鼻音化；用鼻音发音|To pronounce (a sound) with air passing through the nose.|In French, many vowels are nasalized.|近义词: nasalise (British spelling)|反义词: denasalize|词性:verb

nasalized:/ˈneɪzəlaɪzd/| adj. |鼻音化的|Pronounced with air passing through the nose.|The singer's nasalized tone was distinctive.|近义词: nasalised (British spelling)|反义词: denasalized|词性:adjective
