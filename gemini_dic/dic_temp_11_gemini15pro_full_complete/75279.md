frontages:/ˈfrʌntɪdʒɪz/
n.
正面；临街面；沿街土地
The length of a property's boundary with a street or road.
The hotel has frontages on both Fifth Avenue and 58th Street. 这家酒店在第五大道和58街都有临街面。
近义词: facades, faces
反义词: rear, back
词性: 名词
