celliferous:/səˈlɪfərəs/ | adj. | 产细胞的；多孔的 | Bearing or producing cells, or having many small compartments or cavities. |  The celliferous structure of the plant's stem allowed for efficient water transport.  这种植物茎的多孔结构使其能够有效地输送水分。 | The microscopic image revealed the celliferous nature of the tissue. 显微图像显示了组织的细胞性质。 | 近义词: cellular, porous, alveolate | 反义词: acellular, nonporous, solid | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *cellifer* (bearing cells), from *cella* (small room, cell) + *-fer* (bearing).
