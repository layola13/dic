obsessor:/əbˈsesər/ | n. | 迷恋者；着迷者 | A person who is excessively preoccupied with someone or something. |  He became an obsessor with conspiracy theories. 他变得痴迷于阴谋论。|  She's an obsessor of cleanliness. 她有洁癖。| 近义词: fanatic, enthusiast, addict | 反义词:  critic, detractor | 词性: 名词
