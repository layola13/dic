livelier:/ˈlaɪvliər/ /ˈlaɪvliə/| adj.|更活泼的，更有生气的|More lively; more full of life and energy.|The party became livelier after the band started playing.  聚会在乐队开始演奏后变得更热闹了。|
近义词：more animated, more energetic, more spirited, more vibrant|反义词：duller, quieter, less active|词性: 形容词 (Comparative adjective)


Etymology:
From Middle English *livelier, *liuelier, comparative of lively, from Old English līflīc (“living, lively”), equivalent to life +‎ -ly. Cognate with Scots leevely (“lively”), German lebhaft (“lively”).  More at life.
