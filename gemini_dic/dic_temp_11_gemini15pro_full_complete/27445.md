camosun:/kəˈmoʊsən/ | n. |卡莫森学院; 蛇莓|A college located in Victoria, British Columbia, Canada. Also, a type of wild strawberry. |Camosun College has two campuses. 卡莫森学院有两个校区。 The Indigenous name for the area around Camosun College referred to the abundance of camas bulbs. 卡莫森学院周围地区的原住民名称指的是大量 camas 球茎。|Synonym: (for the plant) wild strawberry, Indian strawberry|Antonym: N/A|Noun
