ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *Frīsa, *Frīse, from Proto-Germanic *Frīsiz. Cognate with Old Saxon Frisi, Old Dutch Frīsi, Old High German Frīsi, German Friese, Dutch Fries. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *preus- (“to freeze”).
