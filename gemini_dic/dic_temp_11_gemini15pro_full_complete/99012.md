interactivity:/ˌɪntərækˈtɪvɪti/
n.|交互性，互动性|The interactivity of the website keeps users engaged.  这个网站的交互性使用户保持参与。|例句：The museum exhibit encouraged interactivity by letting visitors touch the artifacts.  博物馆展览鼓励互动，允许参观者触摸文物。

例句：The online course lacked interactivity, making it difficult for students to stay motivated.  在线课程缺乏互动性，让学生难以保持动力。

近义词：interaction, engagement, responsiveness
反义词：passivity, inertness
词性: noun

Etymology: From interactive + -ity.  Interactive is formed from inter- (meaning "between") + act (verb) + -ive (adjective suffix). The root "act" comes from Latin "actus," meaning "a doing."
