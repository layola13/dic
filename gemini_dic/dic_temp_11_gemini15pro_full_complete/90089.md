hostility:/hɑːˈstɪləti/ /hɒˈstɪləti/| n. |敌意，敌对，不友好|Unfriendliness or opposition to someone or something.| The countries ended years of hostility and signed a peace treaty.  这些国家结束了多年的敌对状态，并签署了和平条约。|  The crowd showed open hostility towards the visiting team.  人群对来访的队伍表现出公开的敌意。| antagonism, animosity, enmity, aggression | friendliness, amity, goodwill| noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: hostilite, from Old French hostilité, from Latin hostilitas, from hostilis ‘of an enemy’, from hostis ‘enemy’.
