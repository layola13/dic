impervious:/ɪmˈpɜːrviəs/| adj.|不能渗透的；不为所动的；不受影响的|Not allowing fluid to pass through. Unable to be affected by. |例句：The material is impervious to water. 这种材料不透水。

例句：He seemed impervious to the criticism. 他似乎对批评无动于衷。|近义词：impenetrable, resistant, immune|反义词：permeable, susceptible, vulnerable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *impervius, from Latin in- ‘not’ + pervius ‘permeable,’ from per- ‘through’ + via ‘way.’
