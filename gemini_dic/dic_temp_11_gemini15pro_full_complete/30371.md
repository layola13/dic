centerfold:/ˈsɛntərfoʊld/| n.|插页, 中央对页, (杂志的)裸体照片|A large picture printed across the center pages of a magazine, often of a sexually suggestive nature. |The magazine's centerfold this month features a famous actress.|Synonym: playmate, pinup|Antonym: cover, outside|noun

centerfold:/ˈsɛntərfoʊld/| adj.|对页的, 中央对开的|Printed across the center pages of a magazine, usually as a single large picture. |The centerfold poster became an instant collector's item.|Synonym: double-page, two-page|Antonym: single-page, peripheral|adjective
