what: /wɒt/ (UK), /wɑːt/ (US) | pron. det. adv. int.| 什么，多么 |

英文解析:  "What" is a versatile word used as a pronoun, determiner, adverb, and interjection.  As a pronoun, it asks for information about something. As a determiner, it specifies which thing or things are being referred to.  As an adverb, it modifies an adjective or adverb, expressing degree or extent.  As an interjection, it expresses surprise, anger, or other strong emotions.

例句：
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？

What did you eat for lunch? = 你午饭吃了什么？
