nondeterminable:/ˌnɒndɪˈtɜːmɪnəbl/ | adj. | 不可确定的，无法判定的 | Not able to be determined or decided; undecidable. |  The halting problem is nondeterminable for Turing machines. 图灵机无法判定停机问题。| The outcome of the experiment is nondeterminable due to the numerous uncontrolled variables.  由于众多不受控制的变量，实验结果无法确定。 | 近义词: undecidable, indeterminable, unpredictable | 反义词: determinable, decidable, predictable | 词性: adjective
词源: non- (not) + determinable (able to be determined) from Latin determinare (to limit, determine)
