hurden:/ˈhɜːrdən/ | n. | 负担，重担 | A burden is a heavy load that is difficult to carry, either physically or mentally. |  The old woman struggled under the hurden of her shopping bags. 老妇人吃力地提着购物袋。

She found the responsibility of caring for her sick mother a great hurden. 她觉得照顾生病的母亲责任重大。 | 近义词：load, weight, burden, encumbrance | 反义词：relief, ease, comfort | 词性：名词

Etymology: From Middle English *harden, hurden, from Old French *hardon, from Frankish *hardjan or some other West Germanic source akin to Old English heard "hard."  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *harduz "hard."
