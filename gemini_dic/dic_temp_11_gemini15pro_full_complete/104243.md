jurisprudentially:/ˌdʒʊərɪsˈpruːdənʃəli/
adverb|从法学角度；法理地|From the point of view of jurisprudence; in terms of legal theory.
The case was jurisprudentially significant.  这个案件从法理上来说意义重大。
The issue was examined jurisprudentially.  这个问题是从法理角度进行考察的。
近义词:legally, judicially
反义词:illogically, unjustly
词性:adverb
