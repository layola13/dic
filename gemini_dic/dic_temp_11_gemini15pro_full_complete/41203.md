coquettishly:/koʊˈkɛtɪʃli/ /kɒˈkɛtɪʃli/
adverb|
卖弄风情地，娇媚地|
In a manner intended to attract flirtatious attention; flirtatiously.|
She smiled coquettishly at him across the room. 她隔着房间娇媚地对他笑了笑。|
flirtatiously, teasingly, playfully|
seriously, modestly, demurely|
词性: 副词
