conu:/ˈkəʊnjuː/ /ˈkɑːnjuː/ | n. |锥体，球果 | A solid or hollow object that has a round flat base and comes to a point at the top. | 例句：The pine tree produces cones. 松树产球果。

例句：The ice cream was served in a cone. 冰淇淋盛在蛋卷里。 | 近义词：cone, pyramid | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


词源：From Latin *cōnus, from Greek κῶνος (kōnos, “cone, pine cone”).
