noncombining:/ˌnɑːnkəmˈbaɪnɪŋ/
adjective
非组合的；非化合的
Not able to be combined with other elements, especially not able to form a chemical compound.  Relating to a character in Unicode that doesn't modify the preceding character.
This particular element is noncombining, meaning it exists as a standalone unit.  这个特殊的元素是非组合的，意味着它作为独立单元存在。
These noncombining characters are essential for proper text rendering. 这些非组合字符对于正确的文本渲染至关重要。
non-composable, discrete, separate
combining, unifying
