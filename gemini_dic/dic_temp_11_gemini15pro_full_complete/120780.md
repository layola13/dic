misapprehensiveness:/ˌmɪsæprɪˈhɛnsɪvnəs/
n.
误解，误会的倾向
The state or quality of being prone to misapprehension; a tendency to misunderstand.
His misapprehensiveness led to a series of unfortunate events. 他的误解导致了一系列不幸的事件。

近义词: misunderstanding, misconception, misinterpretation
反义词: understanding, comprehension, clarity
词性: 名词
