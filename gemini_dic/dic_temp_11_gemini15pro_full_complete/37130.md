come down with:/kʌm daʊn wɪð/ | phrasal verb | 染上（疾病）；患（病） | To become sick with (an illness).  This phrase is often used for less serious illnesses like colds or the flu. | I think I'm coming down with a cold. 我想我感冒了。|

例句：
He came down with the flu last week.  他上周得了流感。

My daughter came down with chickenpox. 我女儿得了水痘。

近义词：catch, contract, get, develop, succumb to
反义词：recover from, get over

词性：动词短语
