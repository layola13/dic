immusical:/ˌɪmˈmjuːzɪkəl/
adjective
不音乐的；缺乏音乐感的；不和谐的
lacking musical ability; not musical; discordant
His singing voice was quite immusical. 他的歌声很不和谐。
Near Antonyms: musical, melodious, harmonious
Antonyms: musical
