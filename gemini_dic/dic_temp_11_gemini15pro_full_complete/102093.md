isos:/ˈaɪsɒs/ | n. | 等值线; 等高线 | A line on a map or chart connecting points of equal value, such as elevation, temperature, or rainfall. | Example: The isos on the map showed areas of equal pressure.  地图上的等值线显示了等压区域。| Example: The meteorologist studied the isos of temperature to predict the weather. 气象学家研究了等温线来预测天气。 | Synonyms: contour line, isoline | Antonyms: N/A | noun
