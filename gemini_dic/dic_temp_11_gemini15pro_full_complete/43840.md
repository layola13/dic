crepitation:/ˌkrɛpɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. |噼啪声，爆裂声，捻发音|A grating or crackling sound or sensation, such as that produced by the fractured ends of a bone rubbing together.|The doctor heard crepitation when he moved the patient's broken arm. 医生移动病人断臂时听到了骨摩擦音。|近义词：crackling, grating, popping|反义词: silence | noun


crepitate:/ˈkrɛpɪteɪt/ | v. |发出噼啪声，爆裂|To make a crackling or popping sound.|The fire crepitated merrily in the fireplace. 火在壁炉里欢快地噼啪作响。|近义词：crackle, pop, snap|反义词: - | verb
