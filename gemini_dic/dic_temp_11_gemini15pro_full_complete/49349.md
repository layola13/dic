delineatory:/dɪˈlɪnɪəˌtɔːri/ /dɪˈlɪnɪəˌtəri/
adjective
描绘的；轮廓分明的；描述性的
serving to delineate; descriptive
The artist's delineatory style made the subject of the portrait appear almost lifelike.  这位艺术家的描绘风格使肖像的主题显得栩栩如生。
The architect provided delineatory drawings that clearly showed the layout of the building.  建筑师提供了清晰显示建筑物布局的轮廓图。
近义词: descriptive, outlining, defining, representational
反义词: vague, obscure, ambiguous, unclear
词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin dēlineātus, past participle of dēlineāre (“to sketch, trace out”), from dē- + lineāre (“to draw lines, make lines upon”), from līnea (“line”).
