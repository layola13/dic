fibreglass:/ˈfaɪbərɡlæs/ (US) /ˈfaɪbəɡlɑːs/ (UK)| n.|玻璃纤维|A material consisting of fine glass fibres embedded in a resin, used as a reinforcing agent in plastics and as thermal insulation.|The boat hull is made of fibreglass.|近义词：glass fiber, fiberglass reinforced plastic (FRP)|反义词: (No direct antonym, but possibly natural fibers like cotton or wool depending on the context)|noun
