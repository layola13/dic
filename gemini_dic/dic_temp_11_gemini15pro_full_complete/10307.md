apparitor: /əˈpærɪtər/ (UK) /ˈæpərɪtər/ (US)| n. | 法庭执达员；大学执事| An officer of a court who serves summonses, executes warrants, etc.;  a university official with ceremonial duties. |例句：The apparitor called the next case. 法庭执达员宣布下一个案件。

例句：The apparitor led the procession of academics. 大学执事带领着学术队伍。

近义词：bailiff, officer, beadle, marshal|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

apparitor: /əˈpærɪtər/ (UK) /ˈæpərɪtər/ (US)| n. |侍从，随从|An attendant or servant. (This meaning is less common in modern usage.)|例句：The apparitor carried the king's train. 侍从抬着国王的拖地长袍。(古用法)

例句：The magician's apparitor fetched his props. 魔术师的助手取来了他的道具。(古用法)
