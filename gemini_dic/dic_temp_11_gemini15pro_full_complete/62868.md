enlace:/enˈlase/ (US), /ɪnˈlɑːseɪ/ (UK)| n.|连接，联系；纽带|A connection, link, or bond.  Primarily used in Spanish and sometimes adopted into English in specific contexts.  It often carries a connotation of a close or strong connection.|

例句：The cultural enlace between the two countries is strong. 这两个国家之间的文化联系很强。

例句：The project served as an enlace between the university and local businesses.  这个项目充当了大学和当地企业之间的纽带。

近义词：link, connection, bond, tie, nexus

反义词：separation, division, disconnection

词性: noun


词源: From Spanish *enlace*, derived from *enlazar* "to link, connect," from *en-* "in" + *lazo* "noose, bond." Ultimately from Latin *laqueus* "noose, snare."
