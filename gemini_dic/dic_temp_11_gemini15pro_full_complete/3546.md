The doctor diagnosed agalactia and recommended supplemental feeding for the infant. 医生诊断出泌乳缺乏，并建议给婴儿补充喂养。 | 近义词：hypogalactia, failure of lactation | 反义词：hyperlactation, galactorrhea | 词性: noun
