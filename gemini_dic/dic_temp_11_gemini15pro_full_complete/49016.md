degradingly:/dɪˈɡreɪdɪŋli/
adv.
贬低地，侮辱地
in a way that causes someone to feel that they have lost respect for themselves
He spoke degradingly about his ex-wife. 他贬低地谈论他的前妻。

近义词:humiliatingly, contemptuously, disdainfully, disparagingly
反义词:respectfully, honorably

词源:From degrade + -ing + -ly.  Degrade comes from the French dégrader, from the Latin degradare, meaning "to reduce from a higher to a lower rank or degree."
