catch-hell:/ˌkætʃ ˈhel/| v.|受严厉批评；受惩罚；遭殃|To be scolded, reprimanded, or punished severely.|例句：He caught hell from his boss for being late. 他因为迟到被老板狠狠地训了一顿。

例句：If you don't finish this report by tomorrow, you'll catch hell. 如果你明天之前完不成这份报告，你就等着挨批吧。|近义词：be reprimanded, be punished, be scolded, get in trouble, face the music|反义词：be praised, be rewarded|词性:动词
