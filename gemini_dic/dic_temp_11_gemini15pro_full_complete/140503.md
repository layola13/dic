oldnesses:/ˈoʊldnɪsɪz/
noun
古老；老化；陈旧
The state of being old; old age.
The oldnesses of the buildings added to the city's charm.  这些建筑的古老为城市增添了魅力。
近义词: antiquity, agedness, senescence
反义词: newness, youthfulness
词性:n.


Etymology:
From Old English *ealdnes, from eald ("old").  源自古英语 *ealdnes，来自 eald（“老的”）。
