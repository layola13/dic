emblematicalness:/ˌɛmbləˈmætɪkəlnəs/
n.
象征性；标志性
The quality of being emblematical; symbolic representation.
The emblematicalness of the dove as a symbol of peace is widely recognized.  鸽子作为和平象征的标志性是得到广泛认可的。
The ceremony’s emblematicalness of national unity was clear.  这个仪式象征着民族团结，这一点很明显。
synonyms: symbolism, representativeness, typicality
antonyms:  concreteness, literalness,  unrepresentativeness
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From emblematical + -ness.  Emblematical from emblem (symbol) + -atical (relating to). Emblem ultimately from Greek *embēlēma “something inserted,” from emballein “to throw in, insert.”
