hardenable:/ˈhɑːrdənəbl/ | adj. | 可硬化的，可淬硬的 | Able to be made hard or harder, especially by heating and then cooling. | This steel is hardenable and can be used for making tools. 这种钢材可硬化，可用于制造工具。| The resin is hardenable by exposure to ultraviolet light.  这种树脂可以通过紫外线照射使其硬化。|  近义词:  temperable, toughenable | 反义词: soft, malleable | 词性: adjective

词源: From "harden" + "-able". "Harden" comes from Old English "heardian," meaning "to make hard," from "heard" (hard).  "-able" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being," "suitable for being," or "tending to."
