miniaturists:/ˈmɪnɪətʃərɪsts/| n.|微型画家，微型艺术家|A miniaturist is a skilled artist who creates very small, detailed works of art, often portraits or scenes, on a very small scale.|The museum held an exhibition of 18th-century miniaturists.  这家博物馆举办了18世纪微型画家作品展。|miniaturist painters, miniature artists|muralists|noun
miniature:/ˈmɪnɪətʃər/| n. adj.|微型复制品，微型的|A miniature is a small version of something, like a painting or a model.|She collects miniatures of famous landmarks. 她收集著名地标的微缩模型。|model, replica|large-scale|noun, adjective
