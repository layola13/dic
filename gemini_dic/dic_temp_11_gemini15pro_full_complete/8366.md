anti-productivity:/ˌæntiˌprɒdʌkˈtɪvɪti/ | n. | 反生产力；妨碍生产力的行为或事物 |  The state of being unproductive or hindering productivity; activities or things that prevent work from being done efficiently. | My constant checking of social media is a major source of anti-productivity. 我不停地查看社交媒体是导致我效率低下的主要原因。|  Scrolling through memes for hours is pure anti-productivity.  连续几个小时浏览表情包纯粹是浪费时间，降低效率。 | 近义词: unproductiveness, inefficiency, ineffectiveness | 反义词: productivity, efficiency, effectiveness | 词性: 名词

anti-productive:/ˌæntiprəˈdʌktɪv/ | adj. | 反生产力的；妨碍生产力的 | Having the opposite effect to that intended; tending to hinder the achievement of a goal, especially relating to work or efficiency. |  Their constant arguing was anti-productive to the team's success. 他们不断的争吵对团队的成功起到了反作用。 | The meeting was so long and disorganized that it proved anti-productive.  会议时间太长，而且杂乱无章，结果适得其反。 | 近义词: counterproductive, unhelpful, detrimental | 反义词: productive, helpful, beneficial | 词性: 形容词
