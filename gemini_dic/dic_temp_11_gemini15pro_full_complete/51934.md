diabeteses:/ˌdaɪəˈbiːtiːz/
n.
糖尿病（复数形式，但通常用单数）
A group of metabolic diseases characterized by elevated levels of glucose in the blood.
Both of my grandparents have diabeteses. (Technically correct, but uncommon) 我的祖父母都有糖尿病。(技术上正确，但不常用)
Diabetes is a serious disease. 糖尿病是一种严重的疾病。 (更常见的用法)
hyperglycemia, high blood sugar
hypoglycemia, low blood sugar
noun
