historians:/hɪˈstɔːriənz/
n.
历史学家们；史学家们
Experts in or students of history.
Many historians believe the Roman Empire fell due to internal strife. 许多历史学家认为罗马帝国的衰落是由于内部冲突。
近义词: chroniclers, annalists, historiographers
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 non-historians 或 those ignorant of history)
词性: noun (名词)

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *istoryen*, from Old French *historieor* "historian," from Late Latin *historiator*, from Latin *historia* "history" (from Ancient Greek *ἱστορία* (historía) "inquiry, knowledge acquired by investigation") + *-tor* (agent suffix).
