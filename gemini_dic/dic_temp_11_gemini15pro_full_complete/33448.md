churner:/ˈtʃɜːrnər/ | n. | 搅拌器；搅乳器；制造麻烦的人 | A person or thing that churns (milk or cream) or agitates something, causing disorder. | The churner separated the butter from the buttermilk. 搅乳器将黄油从酪乳中分离出来。| The politician was a churner, constantly stirring up controversy.  这位政治家是个麻烦制造者，不断地挑起争议。 | synonyms: agitator, stirrer, troublemaker | antonyms: pacifier, mediator | noun

churn:/tʃɜːrn/ | v. | 搅拌；翻腾；大量生产 | To agitate or stir (milk or cream) in order to make butter. To produce something, especially something of low quality, in large quantities. |  We churned the cream to make butter. 我们搅拌奶油来制作黄油。| The factory churns out thousands of plastic toys every day.  这家工厂每天生产成千上万的塑料玩具。| synonyms: agitate, stir, produce | antonyms: settle, calm | verb
