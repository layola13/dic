evidently:/ˈevɪdəntli/ | adv. |明显地，显然地|Clearly; obviously; it is evident that. | Evidently, she had been crying. 显然，她一直在哭。|It was evidently a difficult decision for her.  显然，这对她来说是一个艰难的决定。|近义词：apparently, obviously, clearly, seemingly, plainly, visibly, manifestly|反义词：doubtfully, questionably, improbably|词性:副词

词源:
From Late Latin *evidenter, from Latin evidens (“evident”).
