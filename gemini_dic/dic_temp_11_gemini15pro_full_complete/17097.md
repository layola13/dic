batrachophagous: /ˌbætrəˈkɒfəɡəs/ | adj. | 食蛙的，吃蛙的 | Feeding on frogs; frog-eating. |  Frogs form the staple diet of many batrachophagous birds. 许多食蛙鸟类以青蛙为主食。| The snake is a batrachophagous reptile. 这条蛇是一种吃蛙的爬行动物。 | synonyms: frog-eating | antonyms: herbivorous, carnivorous (general, not specific to frogs) | adjective
