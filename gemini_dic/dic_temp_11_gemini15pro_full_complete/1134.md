acciaccatura: /ətˌʃækəˈtʊərə/ (US), /əˌtʃækəˈtjʊərə/ (UK)| n. | 装饰音，碎音 | A very short note played before a principal note. It is usually written as a small note with a slash through its stem. |  The pianist played the acciaccatura with precision. 钢琴家精确地演奏了装饰音。| synonym: grace note, appoggiatura | antonym: main note | noun
