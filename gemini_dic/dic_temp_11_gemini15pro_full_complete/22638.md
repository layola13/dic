boracic:/bɔːˈræsɪk/
adjective
硼的；含硼的
relating to or containing boron or boric acid.
Boracic acid is a weak acid often used as an antiseptic.  硼酸是一种弱酸，常用作防腐剂。
The boracic lint was applied to the wound.  硼酸棉被敷在伤口上。
synonyms: boric
antonyms: None
词性: adjective
