miskeep:/ˌmɪsˈkiːp/
verb
管理不善；保管不当
to manage badly or carelessly; to fail to take proper care of
He was accused of miskeeping the company's funds. 他被指控管理公司资金不善。
近义词:mismanage, mishandle
反义词:maintain, preserve
词性:动词
