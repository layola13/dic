cambridge:/ˈkeɪmbrɪdʒ/ | n. |剑桥|A city in eastern England, home to the University of Cambridge.|I studied at Cambridge. 我在剑桥学习。|近义词: university town |反义词: rural area |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *Grantabrycge "bridge over the Granta" (the river now known as the Cam), from granta "Granta River" + brycg "bridge". The river name was later changed to Cam, influenced by the name of the town.
