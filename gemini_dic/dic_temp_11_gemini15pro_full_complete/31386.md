charge:/tʃɑːrdʒ/ (US) /tʃɑːdʒ/ (UK)| n. v.|费用，指控；收费，控告|A price asked for goods or services.  The act of accusing someone of a crime.|例句：The charge for the service is $50. 这项服务的费用是50美元。|近义词：fee, cost, price, accuse, indict|反义词:payment, credit, discharge, acquit|noun

charge:/tʃɑːrdʒ/ (US) /tʃɑːdʒ/ (UK)| v.|收费，控告；充电，猛攻|To demand payment; to accuse formally of a crime; to store electrical energy in.|例句：They charged me $100 for the repair. 他们收了我100美元的修理费。|近义词：bill, invoice, accuse, indict, load, power|反义词:pay, discharge, acquit, unload|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "chargier" (to load, burden), from Late Latin "carricare" (to load a car), from "carrus" (wagon, cart).
