overprovidentness:/ˌoʊvərˈprɒvɪdəntnəs/
n.
过度节俭；过分谨慎
The state of being excessively provident; too great carefulness in preparing for the future.
His overprovidentness led him to save every penny, even when he had plenty. 他的过度节俭让他每一分钱都存起来，即使他很富裕。
近义词: frugality, thrift, parsimony, stinginess
反义词: extravagance, prodigality, improvidence
词性: 名词


overprovident:/ˌoʊvərˈprɒvɪdənt/
adj.
过度节俭的；过分谨慎的
Excessively provident; too careful in preparing for the future.
He was overprovident, always worried about what might happen tomorrow. 他过分谨慎，总是担心明天会发生什么。
近义词: frugal, thrifty, parsimonious, stingy
反义词: extravagant, prodigal, improvident
词性: 形容词


词源:
From over- + provident + -ness.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much."  "Provident" comes from the Latin "providens," meaning "foreseeing" or "providing."  "-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns from adjectives.
