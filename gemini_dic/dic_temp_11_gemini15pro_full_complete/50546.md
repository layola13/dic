deputation:/ˌdɛpjuˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 代表团，派遣，委任 | A group of people appointed to represent others. |  A deputation of local residents went to the town hall to protest the new development. 当地居民代表团前往市政厅抗议新的开发项目。

The trade union sent a deputation to negotiate with the management. 工会派出一个代表团与管理层谈判。 | synonyms: delegation, mission, embassy | antonyms: individual, single person | noun
