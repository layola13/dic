fobbed:/fɑːbd/ | v. (past participle) | 欺骗；搪塞；用…冒充；|deceive or trick (someone) | He fobbed me off with some story about the train being late. 他用火车晚点的借口搪塞我。|Synonyms: deceive, trick, cheat, dupe, con, defraud, swindle | Antonyms: be honest, be truthful | verb

fobbed off:/fɑːbd ɔːf/ | v. (past participle) |  (用借口)敷衍，搪塞 | to persuade someone to accept something that is not good enough by making excuses |  I was fobbed off with a second-hand computer.  我被用一台二手电脑给打发了。|Synonyms:  palm off, put off, brush off | Antonyms: satisfy, fulfill | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From fob (v.), "to cheat," from obsolete fob (n.), "pocket," probably of Germanic origin.
