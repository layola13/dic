lootable:/ˈluːtəbl/
adjective
可掠夺的；可被洗劫的
Able to be looted.
The enemy camp was lootable after the battle.  敌军营地在战斗后可以掠夺。
After the earthquake, many stores were left unguarded and lootable. 地震过后，许多商店无人看守，可以被洗劫一空。
Synonyms:plundrable, ransackable, pillageable
Antonyms:protected, secure, guarded
词性:形容词
