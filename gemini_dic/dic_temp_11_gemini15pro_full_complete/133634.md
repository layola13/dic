nonforfeitable:/ˌnɒnfɔːrˈfɪtəbl/ | adj. | 不可丧失的，不可剥夺的 | Not capable of being forfeited or lost, especially through the failure to meet certain conditions. |  Employees have a nonforfeitable right to their pensions after a certain number of years of service.  员工在服务一定年限后享有不可丧失的养老金权利。

| Example 1: His nonforfeitable rights to the property were guaranteed in the contract.  他在合同中被保证了对该财产不可丧失的权利。

| Example 2: The company offers a nonforfeitable savings plan for its employees.  该公司为其员工提供不可丧失的储蓄计划。

| Synonyms: inalienable, unlosable, secure, vested | Antonyms: forfeitable, losable, revocable | Word Type: Adjective
