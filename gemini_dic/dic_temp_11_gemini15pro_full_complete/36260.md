cogs:/kɒɡz/ (British) /kɑːɡz/ (American)| n.|齿轮; 齿; （尤指）机构，机制|A cog is a wheel with teeth around its edge, which transfers motion to other cogs or parts of a machine. Figuratively, a cog refers to a person who plays a small part in a large organization or system. |The factory's cogs whirred into action.  工厂的齿轮嗡嗡地运转起来。|He felt like a small cog in a giant machine. 他感觉自己像一台巨大机器上的一个小齿轮。|synonyms: gear, tooth, wheel, component, part|antonyms: whole, entirety|noun
