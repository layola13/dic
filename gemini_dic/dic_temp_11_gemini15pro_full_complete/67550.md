extraneous:/ɪkˈstreɪniəs/| adj.|无关的，外来的|Not essential; irrelevant; coming from outside.|例句：The book contains much extraneous material. 这本书包含很多无关紧要的材料。|例句：Extraneous noises from the street disturbed my sleep. 街上的噪音打扰了我的睡眠。|近义词：irrelevant, superfluous, unnecessary, external|反义词：essential, relevant, intrinsic, internal|词性:形容词
Etymology->
From Latin extraneus "external, foreign, strange," from extra "outside"  + -aneus (akin to alienus "belonging to another").
