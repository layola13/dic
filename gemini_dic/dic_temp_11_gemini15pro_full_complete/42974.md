coverup:/ˈkʌvərʌp/| n.|掩盖；隐瞒；遮掩物|An attempt to prevent the public discovering information about a serious crime or mistake.|The government was accused of a coverup of the scandal. 政府被指控掩盖了这起丑闻。|近义词：concealment, whitewash|反义词：disclosure, revelation|词性:名词

词源:  From the verb phrase "cover up" (meaning to conceal or hide something).  The verb "cover" has Proto-Germanic roots and is related to similar words in other Germanic languages.  The particle "up" intensifies the meaning of completion or thoroughness.
