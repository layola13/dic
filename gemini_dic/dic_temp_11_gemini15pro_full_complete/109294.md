lemurlike:/ˈliːməˌlaɪk/| adj.|像狐猴的，狐猴似的|Resembling or characteristic of a lemur, a small primate with large eyes and a long tail.|The child's large, curious eyes gave him a lemurlike appearance.  这个孩子又大又好奇的眼睛让他看起来像狐猴。|The small, nocturnal primate had a lemurlike grip on the branch.  这只小型夜行动物灵长类动物用像狐猴一样的抓力抓住了树枝。|近义词：primate-like, monkey-like|反义词: (No clear opposite, perhaps human-like or ape-like) |词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From *lemur* + *-like*.  *Lemur* comes from Latin *lemures*, meaning "ghosts" or "spirits of the dead", referring to the nocturnal habits and large eyes of lemurs. *-like* is a suffix used to form adjectives meaning "similar to" or "characteristic of".
