anthropomorphous:/ˌænθrəpəˈmɔːrfəs/ |adj.|拟人的，赋予人性的|Having or resembling human form or attributes. Ascribing human characteristics to non-human things.|例句：The children's book featured anthropomorphous animals who lived in a treehouse. 这本童书以住在树屋里的拟人化动物为主角。

例句：The ancient Greeks often created anthropomorphous gods and goddesses. 古希腊人经常创造拟人化的神和女神。|近义词：humanlike, humanoid, personified|反义词：animalistic, beastly|词性: adjective
词源: From Greek *ánthrōpos* (human) + *morphḗ* (form)
