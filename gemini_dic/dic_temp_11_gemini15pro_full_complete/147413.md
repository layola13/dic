overnormalizing:/ˌoʊvərˈnɔːrməˌlaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度规范化 |  The act of applying normalization excessively, often to the point of reducing variability or individuality. It can refer to statistical normalization, database normalization, or the imposition of rigid social norms. |  The company's oevernormalizing of its data led to a loss of valuable nuanced information.  这家公司对其数据的过度规范化导致丢失了有价值的细微信息。| 近义词: over-regulating, standardizing excessively | 反义词: diversifying, individualizing | 词性:形容词

overnormalize:/ˌoʊvərˈnɔːrməˌlaɪz/ | v. | 过度规范化 | To normalize excessively, often to the point of reducing variability or individuality. | They overnormalized the data, which skewed the results. 他们过度规范化了数据，这使得结果出现了偏差。 | 近义词: over-regulate, standardize excessively | 反义词: diversify, individualize | 词性:动词


词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessive") + normalize (verb meaning "to bring to a standard or norm")
