ecumenically:/ˌɛkjuːˈmɛnɪk(ə)li/ | adv. |普世地，不分教派地| In a way that promotes or relates to unity among different Christian churches or denominations; transcending the boundaries of individual denominations. | The Pope appealed ecumenically for peace and understanding among all faiths.  教皇呼吁所有信仰之间普世地和平与理解。| The conference addressed the issue ecumenically, bringing together representatives from various Christian denominations.  这次会议以普世的方式讨论了这个问题，汇集了来自不同基督教派的代表。 |  universally, globally, inclusively, broadly | parochially, narrowly, sectarianly | adverb
