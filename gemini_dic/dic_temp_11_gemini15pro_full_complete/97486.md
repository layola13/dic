ingenuity:/ˌɪndʒəˈn(j)uːəti/| n. |独创性，心灵手巧，足智多谋|The quality of being clever, original, and inventive.|  Her ingenuity allowed her to create a successful business from scratch. 她的独创性使她白手起家创建了一家成功的企业。| synonyms: cleverness, inventiveness, resourcefulness, creativity, innovation | antonyms:  incompetence, stupidity, dullness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ingēnuitās, from Latin ingēnuus (“inborn, native, freeborn, noble, frank, honest, sincere”).
