engrave:/ɪnˈɡreɪv/ | v. |雕刻；铭刻|To cut or carve (a design, inscription, etc.) into a hard surface. |The jeweler engraved our initials inside the rings. 珠宝商在我们戒指的内侧刻上了我们名字的首字母。| 近义词: carve, etch, inscribe | 反义词: erase, efface | verb

engrave:/ɪnˈɡreɪv/ | v. |铭记；牢记|To impress deeply on the memory. |The tragic events of that day were engraved on her mind. 那天发生的悲惨事件深深地印在了她的脑海里。| 近义词: impress, fix, embed | 反义词: forget | verb
