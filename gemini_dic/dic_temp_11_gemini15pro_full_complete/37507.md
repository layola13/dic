commodiously:/kəˈmoʊdiəsli/
adjective
宽敞地，舒适地
in a spacious and comfortable manner.
The guests were commodiously lodged in the large house. 客人们被宽敞舒适地安置在了大房子里。
commodious, comfortably, spaciously
cramped, uncomfortably, inconveniently
