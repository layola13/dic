hotspot:/ˈhɑːtspɑːt/ (US) /ˈhɒtspɒt/ (UK)| n.|热点，热门地区，危险地区|A place of more than usual interest, activity, or popularity; a place where war or other fighting is likely to break out.|例句：This café is a popular hotspot for students. 这家咖啡馆是学生们的热门聚集地。

例句：The border region remains a political hotspot. 边境地区仍然是一个政治热点。|近义词：center, hub, focal point, trouble spot|反义词：backwater, quiet spot|词性:noun
