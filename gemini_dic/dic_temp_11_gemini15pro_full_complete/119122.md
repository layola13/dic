metropolitanization: /məˌtrɒpəlɪtənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌmɛtrəˌpɑlətənəˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 大都市化；都市化 | The process by which a region or area becomes increasingly urbanized and takes on the characteristics of a metropolis, such as a high population density, a diverse economy, and a complex social and cultural infrastructure. | The rapid metropolitanization of the area has led to a housing shortage.  该地区快速的都市化导致了住房短缺。 | urbanization, city growth | ruralization, deurbanization | noun

metropolitanize: /məˌtrɒpəlɪtənaɪz/ /ˌmɛtrəˌpɑlətənˌaɪz/ | v. | 使都市化；使大都市化 | To cause a region or area to become a metropolis. | The small town was quickly metropolitanized after the discovery of gold.  这个小镇在发现金矿后迅速都市化了。 | urbanize | ruralize | verb
