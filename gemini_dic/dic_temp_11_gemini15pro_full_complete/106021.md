kneadability:/ˌniːdəˈbɪləti/
|n.|可揉捏性；可捏合性|The kneadability of the dough is crucial for making good bread. 面团的可揉捏性对于制作好面包至关重要。|The high kneadability of this clay makes it ideal for sculpting. 这种粘土的高可塑性使其成为雕塑的理想选择。|近义词：malleability, plasticity, pliability|反义词：stiffness, rigidity|词性: noun

词源: From "knead" + "-able" + "-ity". "Knead" comes from Old English "cnedan."  "-able" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being." "-ity" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, denoting a state or quality.
