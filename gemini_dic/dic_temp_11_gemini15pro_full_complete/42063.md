contemporary:/kənˈtɛmpərəri/ | adj. n. |当代的；同时代的；同龄人|Living or occurring at the same time.|例句：He was contemporary with the dramatist Congreve. 他与剧作家康格里夫是同时代的人。|近义词：modern, current, coeval, synchronous|反义词：ancient, old, archaic, past|词性:形容词

contemporary:/kənˈtɛmpərəri/ | n. |同龄人；同时代的人|A person or thing living or existing at the same time as another.|例句：He was a contemporary of George Washington. 他是乔治·华盛顿的同龄人。|近义词：peer, coeval|反义词：predecessor, ancestor|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *contemporarius*, from Latin *con-* (together) + *tempus* (time) + *-arius* (relating to).
