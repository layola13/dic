crimsonly:/ˈkrɪmzənli/ | adv. | 深红色地，绯红地 | In a crimson or blood-red manner. | The sunset painted the sky crimsonly. 夕阳把天空染成了深红色。|  The leaves turned crimsonly in the autumn. 秋天，树叶变成了深红色。 | 近义词: bloodily, redly, scarletly | 反义词: palely, lightly | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From crimson + -ly.  "Crimson" comes from the Old Spanish "cremesin," ultimately derived from the Arabic "qirmiz," referring to the kermes insect, from which a red dye was obtained.
