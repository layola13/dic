digestor:/daɪˈdʒɛstər/ | n. | 消化器；消化罐；文摘 | a tank, container, or apparatus in which something is digested, especially by heat or chemical action.  |  The sewage treatment plant uses a large digestor to break down organic matter.  污水处理厂使用一个大型消化器来分解有机物。

The company publishes a monthly digestor of articles on current affairs. 这家公司每月出版一本时事文章的文摘。| 近义词: digester,  extractor | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

digestor:/daɪˈdʒɛstər/ | n. | 文摘 | a collection or compilation of summaries or excerpts from various sources. |  She reads a legal digestor to stay up-to-date on new case law. 她阅读法律文摘以了解最新的判例法。| 近义词: summary, abstract, compendium  | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *digestor, from Latin digerere "to separate, distribute, arrange, digest," from dis- "apart" + gerere "to carry, bear."
