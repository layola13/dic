gringo:/ˈɡrɪŋɡoʊ/ (US), /ˈɡrɪŋɡəʊ/ (UK)| n.|外国人（尤指说英语的外国人，通常指美国人或英国人），老外|A term used, often disparagingly, to refer to a foreigner, especially one who is English-speaking, typically American or British.|The locals called all the tourists gringos. 当地人把所有游客都称为老外。|Synonyms: foreigner, outsider, alien|Antonyms: native, local|Noun
