ancient:/ˈeɪnʃənt/| adj.|古代的，古老的|Belonging to the very distant past and no longer in existence.|The museum houses an impressive collection of ancient artifacts.  博物馆收藏了令人印象深刻的古代文物。|例句：The ancient city was buried by the volcanic eruption. 这座古城被火山爆发掩埋了。|近义词：old, antique, archaic, venerable|反义词：modern, contemporary, recent|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *auncient*, from Old French *ancien* (modern French *ancien*), from Latin *antiquus* (“old, ancient”), from *ante* (“before”).
