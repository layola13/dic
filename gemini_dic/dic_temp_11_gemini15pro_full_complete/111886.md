logomachy:/loʊˈɡɒməki/ /loʊˈɡɑːməki/
noun
中文翻译：舌战；关于词语的争论；无意义的争论
英文解析：A dispute over or about words; a meaningless battle over the definition of words.
例句：The debate devolved into a logomachy, with both sides arguing over semantics rather than substance.  这场辩论沦为了一场舌战，双方争论的只是语义，而不是实质内容。

例句：Their logomachy over the definition of "art" lasted for hours.  他们关于“艺术”定义的争论持续了几个小时。

近义词：argument, dispute, debate, squabble, altercation
反义词：agreement, concord, harmony
词性：n.
