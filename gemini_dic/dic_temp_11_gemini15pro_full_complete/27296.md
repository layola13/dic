calving:/ˈkɑːvɪŋ/ (US), /ˈkɑːvɪŋ/ (UK)| n. | 分娩；产犊；冰崩 | The process of a cow giving birth to a calf, or of a glacier breaking off large chunks of ice. | The farmer assisted with the difficult calving.  农民协助了难产的母牛分娩。

The calving of the glacier created a massive iceberg. 冰川崩解形成了一座巨大的冰山。|近义词：parturition (for animals), breaking off (for glaciers) |反义词: N/A |词性:noun


Etymology:  From "calve" + "-ing,"  "calve" comes from Old English *cealfian, from Proto-Germanic *kalbijaną, from the noun *kalbiz "calf."
