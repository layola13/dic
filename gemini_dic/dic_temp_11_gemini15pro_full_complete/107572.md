landmark:/ˈlændmɑːrk/ (US), /ˈlændmɑːk/ (UK)| n.|地标；里程碑；转折点|A prominent feature of the landscape, either natural or artificial, that serves as a guide or point of reference.|例句：The Eiffel Tower is a famous landmark in Paris. 埃菲尔铁塔是巴黎的著名地标。|近义词：milestone, turning point, monument, feature|反义词:None|Noun

landmark:/ˈlændmɑːrk/ (US), /ˈlændmɑːk/ (UK)| adj.|有重大意义的；里程碑式的|Something that marks a turning point or stage.|例句：The Supreme Court issued a landmark decision. 最高法院发布了一项具有里程碑意义的裁决。|近义词：historic, groundbreaking, momentous, significant|反义词:insignificant, trivial|Adjective
