antithetical:/ˌæntiˈθɛtɪkəl/| adj.|对立的，相反的|Directly opposed or contrasted; mutually incompatible.|His actions are antithetical to his words. 他的行为和他的言辞相矛盾。|例句：The president's free-market policies are antithetical to the previous administration's socialist programs. 总统的自由市场政策与前一届政府的社会主义计划相悖。

例句：Her lifestyle is antithetical to everything she was brought up to believe. 她的生活方式与她从小到大被教导的信仰完全相反。|近义词：opposite, contrary, contradictory, conflicting|反义词：similar, alike, consistent, harmonious|词性:adjective
