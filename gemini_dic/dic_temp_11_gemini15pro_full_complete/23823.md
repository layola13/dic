breach:/briːtʃ/| n. vt.|违反，破坏，缺口|An act of breaking or failing to observe a law, agreement, or code of conduct.  A gap in a wall, barrier, or defense, especially one made by an attacking force.|The company was sued for breach of contract.  这家公司因违反合同而被起诉。|synonyms: violation, infringement, infraction, contravention|antonyms: observance, compliance, adherence|noun

breach:/briːtʃ/| vt.|违反，突破|To break or fail to observe (a law, agreement, or code of conduct). To make a gap in (a wall, barrier, or defense).|The army breached the enemy's defenses.  军队突破了敌人的防线。|synonyms: violate, infringe, break, contravene|antonyms: observe, comply, adhere|verb
