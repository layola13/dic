covet:/ˈkʌvɪt/| v. |渴望，贪求|To strongly desire something that belongs to someone else. |She coveted her neighbor's beautiful garden. 她渴望拥有邻居漂亮的花园。|He coveted the prestigious award. 他渴望获得那个久负盛名的奖项。|近义词:desire, crave, yearn for, envy|反义词:disdain, despise|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *coveiter*, from Latin *cupiditare* "to desire eagerly," frequentative of *cupere* "to desire."
