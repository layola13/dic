defueled:/diːˈfjuːəld/ | v. |  去除燃料，使失效 | To remove the fuel from something, often a vehicle or aircraft, or to deprive something of the energy or impetus that drives it. |  The aircraft was defueled before maintenance.  飞机在维护前被去除了燃料。

The political scandal defueled his campaign. 这起政治丑闻使他的竞选活动失去了动力。 | Synonyms: drain, empty, disable | Antonyms: fuel, energize, power | verb
