herbiferous:/hɜːrˈbɪfərəs/
adjective
草本的；产生草本植物的
Bearing or producing herbs or grass.
The herbiferous border was full of fragrant plants.  草本植物的边界上长满了芬芳的植物。
The valley was herbiferous, with grasses and wildflowers growing in abundance.  山谷里长满了草本植物，草和野花繁茂地生长着。
Synonyms: grassy, herbaceous
Antonyms: woody, arboreal
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *herbifer, from herba (herb) + -fer (bearing).
