give ear:/ɡɪv ˈɪər/ |v. (phrasal verb)|倾听，注意|To listen attentively; to pay attention.|The king refused to give ear to the people's complaints. 国王拒绝听取人民的抱怨。

The teacher asked the students to give ear to her instructions. 老师要求学生们认真听她的指示。|Synonyms: listen, heed, attend|Antonyms: ignore, disregard|verb phrase
