dissects:/dɪˈsekts/ /daɪˈsekts/| v. |解剖，仔细分析|To cut apart (an animal, plant, etc.) for scientific study; to analyze closely or critically.|The biologist dissected the frog to study its anatomy. 生物学家解剖了青蛙来研究它的解剖结构。| The lawyer dissected the witness's testimony, exposing several inconsistencies. 律师仔细分析了证人的证词，揭露了一些前后矛盾之处。| synonyms: analyze, examine, scrutinize, investigate | antonyms: synthesize, assemble, construct | verb
