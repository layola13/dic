nod-off:/nɒd ˈɒf/ (UK) /nɑːd ˈɔːf/ (US)| vi.| 打盹；睡着 |To briefly fall asleep, typically unintentionally. | Example: I nodded off during the lecture. 我在讲座期间打盹了。

Example:  He kept nodding off in front of the television. 他一直在电视机前打瞌睡。


近义词: doze off, drift off, fall asleep, drop off
反义词: wake up, stay awake
词性:  Intransitive Verb (不及物动词)
