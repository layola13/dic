frame-up:/ˈfreɪmˌʌp/| n.|诬陷，栽赃陷害|A scheme to incriminate someone falsely.  A plot to make someone appear guilty of a crime they did not commit.|例句：The police realized the evidence was a frame-up. 警察意识到证据是伪造的。

例句：He claimed the charges were a frame-up designed to ruin his career. 他声称这些指控是旨在毁掉他事业的诬陷。|近义词：set-up, stitch-up, conspiracy, plot|反义词:exoneration, vindication|词性:名词

frame-up:/ˈfreɪmˌʌp/| v.|诬陷，栽赃陷害|(transitive) To incriminate (someone) falsely; to frame.|例句：They tried to frame him up for a crime he didn't commit. 他们试图诬陷他犯了他没有犯的罪。

例句：The evidence suggests that she was framed up by her rivals. 证据表明她被竞争对手陷害了。|近义词：set up, stitch up, entrap|反义词:exonerate, vindicate|词性:动词
