galvanography:/ɡælˌvæˈnɒɡrəfi/
n.
电版印刷术, 电刻术
A method of producing etched designs on a metal plate by electrolysis, especially for use in printing.
Galvanography is a rarely used printing technique. 电版印刷是一种很少使用的印刷技术。
The artist experimented with galvanography to create unique textures. 这位艺术家尝试用电版印刷术来创作独特的纹理。
近义词:electrotyping
反义词: (No direct antonym exists, but one might consider traditional etching techniques)
词性:noun
