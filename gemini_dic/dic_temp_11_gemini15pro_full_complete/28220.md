capsheaf:/ˈkæpˌʃiːf/| n.|顶点; 最高成就; 完美之作; (尤指谷物捆扎成的)盖捆|A culminating point of achievement or excellence; something that completes or perfects something else, especially the finishing touch.  It can also literally refer to the top sheaf of a stack of grain.|The Nobel Prize was the capsheaf of her long and distinguished career.  诺贝尔奖是她漫长而杰出职业生涯的顶峰。|Synonyms: culmination, climax, pinnacle, apex, crowning achievement|Antonyms: nadir, bottom, base|noun
