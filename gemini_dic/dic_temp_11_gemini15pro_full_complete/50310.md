depletion:/dɪˈpliːʃən/
|n.|消耗，损耗，减少|A depletion of the water table can lead to drought.  地下水位的下降会导致干旱。|
|例句：The depletion of the forest has had a devastating effect on wildlife. 森林的消耗对野生动物造成了毁灭性的影响。 森林枯竭对野生动物产生了毁灭性的影响。|
|近义词：reduction, decrease, loss, diminution|
|反义词：increase, gain, accumulation|
|词性：名词|

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin depletio(n-), from the verb deplere, from de- ‘away, completely’ + plere ‘fill’.
