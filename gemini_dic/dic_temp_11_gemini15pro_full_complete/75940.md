fungologist:/fʌŋˈɡɒlədʒɪst/ | /fʌŋˈɡɑːlədʒɪst/ | n. | 真菌学家 | A scientist who studies fungi (mushrooms, molds, etc.). |  Example 1: The fungologist identified the mushroom as a rare species.  真菌学家将这种蘑菇鉴定为稀有品种。

Example 2:  She consulted a fungologist about the mold growing in her basement. 她向一位真菌学家咨询了她地下室里生长的霉菌。 | Synonym: mycologis | Antonym: N/A | noun
