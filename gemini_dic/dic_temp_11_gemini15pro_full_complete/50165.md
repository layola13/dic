deod:/dēod/ | n. | 行为，行动; 事迹 | An act, deed; an exploit.  Primarily a poetic or archaic term. | He told tales of his ancestor's deods of bravery. 他讲述了他祖先英勇事迹的故事。 | 近义词: act, deed, exploit, feat | 反义词: inaction, failure | 词性: 名词


词源:
From Old English *dēod, from Proto-Germanic *dēdiz "deed, act". Cognate with Old Saxon dād, Old Frisian dēd, Dutch daad, Old High German tāt (German Tat), Old Norse dáð, Gothic 𐌳𐌴𐌳𐍃 (deds). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *dʰeh₁- "to put, place, set".
