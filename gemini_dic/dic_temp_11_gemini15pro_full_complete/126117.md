muteness:/ˈmjuːtnəs/| n.|沉默，缄默，无声|The muteness of the witness made the jury suspicious.  证人的沉默让陪审团起了疑心。|例句：His muteness on the subject was unsettling. 他对这个话题的沉默令人不安。|近义词：silence, voicelessness, dumbness|反义词：loquacity, talkativeness|词性:名词
Etymology:  From Late Middle English *mutenes, from mute + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin *mutus* (silent, dumb).
