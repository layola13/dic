fixable:/ˈfɪksəbl/ | adj. | 可修复的，可修理的 | Able to be fixed or repaired. |  The problem is fixable with the right tools.  这个问题用合适的工具是可以解决的。 | Synonyms: repairable, mendable, restorable | Antonyms: irreparable, unfixable, broken | 词性: 形容词

fixable:/ˈfɪksəbl/ | adj. | 可确定的，可安排的 | Able to be established or arranged definitely. | The meeting time is fixable, but we need to confirm with everyone.  会议时间是可以确定的，但我们需要和每个人确认一下。| Synonyms: settable, arrangeable, determinable | Antonyms: unfixable, undetermined, uncertain | 词性: 形容词
