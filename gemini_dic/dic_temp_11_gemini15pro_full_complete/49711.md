democracies: /dɪˈmɒkrəsiːz/ (UK) /dɪˈmɑːkrəsiːz/ (US)
n.
民主国家；民主政体
Systems of government in which supreme power is vested in the people and exercised by them directly or indirectly through a system of representation usually involving periodically held free elections.
Many Western countries are democracies. 许多西方国家是民主国家。
The spread of democracies around the world has been a slow but steady process.  世界范围内民主的传播是一个缓慢而稳定的过程。

近义词: republics, commonwealths
反义词: dictatorships, autocracies
词性: noun (plural)

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: from Old French democratie, via Latin from Greek dēmokratia, from dēmos ‘people’ + -kratia ‘power, rule’.
