intercutaneous:/ˌɪntərkuˈteɪniəs/
adjective
皮内的；皮间的
existing or done between layers of the skin.
The doctor administered an intercutaneous injection for the allergy test. 医生进行了一次皮内注射进行过敏测试。
Intradermal, subcutaneous
--- (no clear antonym)
