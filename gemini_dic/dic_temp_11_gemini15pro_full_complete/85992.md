headstart:/ˈhɛdstɑːrt/| n.|起步优势，领先优势|An advantage granted or gained at the beginning of something.|例句：Her wealthy background gave her a headstart in life. 她富裕的家庭背景让她在人生起跑线上就领先一步。

例句：The company's innovative technology gave them a headstart in the market. 这家公司的创新技术使他们在市场上获得了领先优势。|近义词：advantage, lead, edge|反义词:disadvantage, handicap|名词
