menthyl:/ˈmɛnθɪl/ | adj. | 薄荷基; 薄荷醇的 | Relating to or derived from menthol.  |  The menthyl group is a component of menthol. 薄荷基是薄荷醇的一个组成部分。 | Menthyl lactate is a common ingredient in cooling creams. 乳酸薄荷酯是清凉霜中的常见成分。 | 近义词: mentholated | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: adjective
