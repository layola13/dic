moshers:/ˈmɒʃəz (UK), ˈmɑːʃərz (US)| n. | 猛烈碰撞的舞者，参与mosh pit的人 | People who participate in moshing, a style of dancing in which participants push or slam into each other, typically at live music concerts, especially those featuring aggressive music genres like punk rock, hardcore, or metal. |  The moshers went wild when the band started playing their most popular song.  猛烈碰撞的舞者们在乐队开始演奏他们最受欢迎的歌曲时变得疯狂起来。

The moshers near the front of the stage were getting really intense.  舞台前方的猛烈碰撞的舞者们越来越激烈。| 近义词：headbangers, slam dancers | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps concertgoers who prefer to stand still) | 词性:Noun (plural)
