on-hold:/ˌɑːn ˈhoʊld/ /ˌɒn ˈhəʊld/| adj.|暂停的，搁置的|Something that is on hold is temporarily stopped, but is expected to start again at a later time.  It often refers to projects, plans, or activities.  In a business context, it can refer to telephone calls being placed in a waiting queue.|例句：The project is currently on hold due to budget cuts.  该项目由于预算削减目前暂停。

例句：My application is on hold pending further checks. 我的申请正在等待进一步审核而暂停。|近义词：suspended, pending, deferred, postponed|反义词：active, ongoing, in progress|词性:adjective

Etymology:  "On hold" originates from the practice of placing a telephone caller in a waiting queue, literally putting them "on hold" while the call is transferred or information is gathered.  This usage then extended metaphorically to other situations where something is temporarily stopped.
