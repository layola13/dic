lands:/lændz/
n.|土地，田地；地产；国家|A piece of ground, especially one used for a particular purpose.  The territory of a nation. |The farmer worked his lands diligently.  农民辛勤地耕种着他的土地。|territory, estate, country, region|seas, oceans
词性:名词(plural)
词源:From Old English *land, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch land and German Land, from an Indo-European root shared by Latin terra and Greek khthōn.

lands:/lændz/
v. (third-person singular simple present lands, present participle landing, simple past and past participle landed)|着陆；登陆；使到达|To bring or come to the ground or shore after a journey by air or sea; to arrive at a place or position. |The plane lands safely despite the storm.  尽管暴风雨，飞机安全着陆。|arrive, reach, alight, touch down|depart, take off
词性:动词(third-person singular simple present)
词源:From Old English *land, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch land and German Land, from an Indo-European root shared by Latin terra and Greek khthōn.
