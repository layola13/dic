cheesecake:/ˈtʃiːzkeɪk/| n. |芝士蛋糕；酪饼|A dessert consisting of a thick, creamy filling of cheese, eggs, and sugar over a crust or base.|例句：I baked a cheesecake for my mom's birthday. 我为我妈妈的生日烤了一个芝士蛋糕。

例句：This bakery is famous for its delicious cheesecakes. 这家面包店以其美味的芝士蛋糕而闻名。|近义词：cream cake, dessert |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "cheese" + "cake". "Cheese" comes from Old English "cese" (from West Germanic "*kasjus"), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European "*kwat-". "Cake" comes from Old Norse "kaka".
