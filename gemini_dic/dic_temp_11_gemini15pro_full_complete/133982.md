nonhierarchically:/ˌnɒnˌhaɪərˈɑːrkɪkli/ | adv. | 非等级地，无等级地 |  Not organized according to a hierarchy; in a way that does not involve ranks or levels.  | The team works nonhierarchically, with everyone contributing equally.  这个团队以非等级的方式工作，每个人都贡献平等。 | Example:  Information is shared nonhierarchically across the organization.  信息在整个组织中非等级地共享。 | Synonyms:  horizontally, equally, collaboratively | Antonyms:  hierarchically, vertically, stratified | Word Type: Adverb

Etymology:

* non-: Prefix meaning "not" (from Latin)
* hierarchical: Relating to a hierarchy (from Greek "hierarchia")
* -ly: Adverbial suffix
