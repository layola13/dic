molybdenum:/məˈlɪbdənəm/ | n. |钼|A silvery-white metallic element used in strengthening steel alloys. It is also an essential trace element for plants and animals.|Molybdenum is added to steel to increase its strength and resistance to corrosion. 钼被添加到钢中以增加其强度和耐腐蚀性。|Synonyms: Mo (chemical symbol) | Antonyms: None | noun
