delineating:/dɪˈlɪnɪeɪtɪŋ/
verb
描绘，勾勒
representing accurately or precisely
The artist delineated the scene with meticulous detail.  这位艺术家一丝不苟地描绘了这个场景。
describing or portraying something precisely
The report clearly delineates the company's financial position. 报告清晰地描述了公司的财务状况。

近义词: outlining, sketching, describing, portraying, depicting, illustrating
反义词: obscuring, confusing, blurring

词性: verb (present participle)
词源: From Latin *dēlineātus*, past participle of *dēlineāre* (“to sketch, trace out”), from *dē* (“down, completely”) + *lineāre* (“to make lines”), from *līnea* (“line”).
