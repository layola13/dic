buntine:/ˈbʌntɪn/ | n. | 色织布; 旗帜用布 | A strong, closely woven fabric, typically striped and used for flags, awnings, and tents. |  The ship's buntine was tattered and faded from years at sea. 船上的旗帜用布由于在海上航行多年而破烂褪色。| synonyms: canvas, duck, awning cloth | antonyms: N/A | noun

buntine:/ˈbʌntɪn/ | adj. | 色织布做的; 旗帜用布做的 | Made of buntine. | The buntine awning provided welcome shade. 色织布做的遮阳篷提供了受欢迎的阴凉。| synonyms: canvas, duck | antonyms: N/A | adjective
