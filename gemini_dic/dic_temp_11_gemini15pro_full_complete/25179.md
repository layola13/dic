bud's:/bʌdz/ | n. | 芽，花蕾；萌芽；未成熟的人或物；伙计（非正式）|  The plant's buds are starting to swell.  植物的芽开始膨胀。|  It's a bud's telephone number.这是伙计的电话号码。| synonyms: sprout, germ, mate, pal | antonyms: blossom, flower, adult, enemy | noun

Etymology: From Middle English *budde*, from Old English *budda* (“bud, sprout”), from Proto-Germanic *budōną* (“to swell”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰewdʰ- (“to swell, grow”).
