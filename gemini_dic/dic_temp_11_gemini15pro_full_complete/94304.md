imperviability:/ɪmˌpɜːrviəˈbɪləti/
n.
不能渗透；不透水性； непроницаемость
The quality or state of being impervious; inability to be penetrated or affected.
The raincoat's imperviability kept me dry in the downpour.  这件雨衣的防渗性使我在倾盆大雨中保持干燥。
The imperviability of the rock prevented water from seeping through. 岩石的不透水性阻止了水的渗透。
近义词:impenetrableness, impermeability, resistance
反义词:permeability, penetrability, porousness
词性: noun

impervious:/ɪmˈpɜːrviəs/
adj.
不能渗透的；不透水的；无动于衷的；不受影响的
Not allowing fluid to pass through. Not able to be affected by.
The material is impervious to water.  这种材料不透水。
He seemed impervious to criticism. 他似乎对批评无动于衷。
近义词:resistant, impenetrable, impermeable
反义词:permeable, penetrable, porous
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *imperviabilis, from Latin in- (“not”) + pervius (“permeable”) + -abilis (suffix forming adjectives meaning ‘capable of being, fit to be’).
