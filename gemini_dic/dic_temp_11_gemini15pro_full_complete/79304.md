glazinesses:/ˈɡleɪzinəsɪz/
n.
光泽, 光滑,  呆滞
a surface quality of smoothness, gloss, or sheen, often suggesting a lack of expression or alertness in the eyes.
Her glazinesses betrayed her exhaustion. 她的眼神呆滞暴露了她的疲惫。
The glazinesses of the polished table were impressive.  抛光桌面的光泽令人印象深刻。
synonyms: gloss, sheen, luster, polish, glaze, dullness, blankness
antonyms: roughness, dullness (when referring to surface), alertness, vibrancy (when referring to eyes)
noun
