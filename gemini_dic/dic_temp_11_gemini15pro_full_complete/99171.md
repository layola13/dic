interchromosomally:/ˌɪntərˌkroʊməˈsoʊməli/
|adv.|染色体间的，发生在不同染色体之间的|Relating to or occurring between different chromosomes.  The exchange of genetic material occurred interchromosomally.|
例句：Interchromosomal recombination is less frequent than intrachromosomal recombination. 染色体间重组比染色体内重组频率低。
