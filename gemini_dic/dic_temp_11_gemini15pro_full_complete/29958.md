caverning:/ˈkeɪvɪŋ/| n.|洞穴探险；洞穴探险运动|The act or practice of exploring caves.  Exploring caves as a sport or hobby.|例句：Caverning is a thrilling adventure. 洞穴探险是一项惊险刺激的冒险活动。

cave:/keɪv/| n. vi.|洞穴；塌陷；屈服|A large natural hole in the side of a cliff or hill, or under the ground. To collapse or fall in. To submit or yield.|例句：The cave was dark and damp. 洞穴里阴暗潮湿。

例句：The roof of the mine caved in. 矿井的顶部塌陷了。|近义词：cavern, grotto|反义词:|词性:名词,动词
