amelo:/əˈmiːloʊ/ | n. | enamel; enamelware | A usually glossy protective or decorative coating.  A type of paint that dries to a hard, glossy finish.  |  The artist used amelo to create a vibrant, shiny effect on the sculpture. 艺术家用瓷釉为雕塑增添了鲜艳的光泽效果。 | The amelo on the antique teapot was chipped. 古董茶壶上的瓷釉有缺口。 | synonyms: glaze, lacquer, varnish, enamel | antonyms: matte, dull | noun
