masticable:/ˈmæstɪkəbl/ /ˈmɑːstɪkəbl/| adj.|可咀嚼的，可嚼碎的|Something that is masticable can be chewed.  This word is often used to describe food or medication.|The tablets are masticable, making them easier for children to swallow. 这些药片是可咀嚼的，方便儿童吞咽。|Synonyms: chewable, eatable|Antonyms: inedible, indigestible|adjective

masticable:/ˈmæstɪkəbl/ /ˈmɑːstɪkəbl/| n.|可咀嚼物|Something that can be chewed, typically food or gum. |Give the dog a masticable to keep him occupied. 给狗一块可咀嚼的东西让它有事做。|Synonyms: chew, cud|Antonyms: N/A|noun
