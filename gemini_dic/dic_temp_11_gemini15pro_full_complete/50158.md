deny: /dɪˈnaɪ/ (UK) /dɪˈnaɪ/ (US) | vt. | 否认；拒绝；拒绝承认；拒绝给予 | to say that something is not true; to refuse to admit or accept something; to refuse to allow someone to have or do something | He denied stealing the money. 他否认偷了钱。 |  The company denied responsibility for the accident. 公司否认对事故负有责任。 | synonyms: refuse, reject, contradict, repudiate | antonyms: admit, accept, acknowledge, confirm | verb
