limelight: /ˈlaɪmlaɪt/ | n. | 聚光灯；公众注意的中心 |  The center of public attention; a focus of publicity.  Originally referring to the intense white light produced by heating lime, which was used in stage lighting in the 19th century. | The singer basked in the limelight after her hit song.  这位歌手在她的热门歌曲发行后沉浸在公众的关注中。

|  She doesn't enjoy being in the limelight. 她不喜欢成为公众关注的焦点。 | 近义词: spotlight, public eye, attention, focus | 反义词: obscurity, anonymity | 词性: noun
