gastronome:/ˈɡæstrənəʊm/ | n. | 美食家；讲究饮食的人 | A person who enjoys and knows a lot about good food and wine. (Chiefly British) | The gastrome savored each bite of the exquisite meal.  这位美食家细细品味着这顿精致的佳肴。| Synonyms: gourmand, epicure, foodie | Antonyms: none | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French gastronome, from Greek gastro- (stomach) + -nome (law, custom), from nomos (law, custom). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *nem- (to assign, allot, take).
