gluttingly:/ˈɡlʌtɪŋli/
adverb
贪婪地；放纵地
in a way that suggests excessive indulgence or greed.
He ate gluttingly, piling his plate high with food. 他狼吞虎咽地吃着，把盘子堆得高高的。
Synonyms: greedily, ravenously, voraciously, excessively
Antonyms: moderately, sparingly, abstemiously
词性: 副词


Etymology:
From glut (v.) + -ing + -ly.  "Glut" comes from the Old French "glouter" meaning "to swallow," ultimately from the Latin "glutire" meaning "to swallow."
