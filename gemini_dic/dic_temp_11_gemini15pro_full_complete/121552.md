mismeasuring:/mɪsˈmɛʒərɪŋ/
verb
错误测量；估量错误
measuring incorrectly; making an inaccurate assessment of something.
The tailor was accused of mismeasuring the customer for his suit.  裁缝被指控错误测量了顾客的西装尺寸。
The government was accused of mismeasuring the impact of the new policy. 政府被指控错误估计了新政策的影响。
近义词: miscalculating, misjudging, underestimating, overestimating
反义词: measuring accurately, assessing correctly
词性: verb (动词)
