germule:/ˈdʒɜːrmjuːl/| n.|胚芽，微芽；（尤指海绵的）芽球|A small bud or sprout, especially one from which a new plant, sponge, etc., may develop.  Specifically, a tough-coated dormant cluster of embryonic cells produced by some freshwater sponges for development under favorable conditions.|The gemmules of the sponge survived the drought and regenerated when the rains returned. 海绵的芽球在干旱中幸存下来，并在雨水回归后再生。|近义词：bud, sprout, seed, spore|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *gemmula, diminutive of Latin gemma "bud," related to germen "sprout, bud, seed."
