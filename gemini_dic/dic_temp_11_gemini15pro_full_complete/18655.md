benzyl:/ˈbɛnzəl/| adj. n.|苄基|Relating to or denoting the univalent radical C6H5CH2 derived from toluene by removing a hydrogen atom from the methyl group.  A benzyl group. |Benzyl alcohol is used as a local anesthetic. 苄醇用作局部麻醉剂。|Synonyms: phenylmethyl|Antonyms: N/A|adjective, noun
