fridges:/frɪdʒɪz/
n.
refrigerators; 冰箱
A fridge is a large electrically powered cupboard designed for storing food at low temperatures.
例句：
We need to buy a new fridge.  我们需要买一个新冰箱。

The milk is in the fridge.  牛奶在冰箱里。

近义词：refrigerators, iceboxes (old-fashioned)
反义词：oven, stove
词性：名词（plural）
