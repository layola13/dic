lure's:/lʊərz/| n.|诱惑物；诱饵；魅力|Something that tempts or attracts, especially one with a promise of pleasure or reward.  It is the possessive form of "lure," indicating ownership or belonging.|The fisherman cast his lure's hook into the water. 渔夫把他的鱼饵的钩子抛入水中。|Synonyms: bait, attraction, allurement, temptation|Antonyms: deterrent, repellent|noun
