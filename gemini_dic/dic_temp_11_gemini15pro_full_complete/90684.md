humbug:/ˈhʌmbʌɡ/| n. vt.|欺骗；骗局；哄骗；胡说|A deceptive or false statement or action; nonsense.|The salesman tried to humbug me into buying a faulty car.  那个推销员想骗我买一辆有毛病的车。|Synonyms: hoax, fraud, deception, trickery|Antonyms: truth, honesty|noun
humbug:/ˈhʌmbʌɡ/| vt.|欺骗；哄骗|To deceive or trick someone.|He humbugged his investors with false promises of high returns. 他用高回报的虚假承诺欺骗了他的投资者。|Synonyms: deceive, trick, mislead, dupe|Antonyms: enlighten, inform|verb
