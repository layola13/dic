commented:/ˈkɒmentɪd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmentɪd/ (US)| adj. | 已评论的，带有注释的 | Having remarks or notes added; annotated. |  Example: The commented code was much easier to understand.  已注释的代码更容易理解。

Example:  He submitted a commented manuscript to the journal. 他向期刊提交了一份带有注释的手稿。| 近义词: annotated, explained, interpreted | 反义词:  uncommented, unexplained | 词性: adjective

词源:  From the verb "comment" + "-ed" (past participle suffix). The verb "comment" ultimately derives from the Latin "commentārī" meaning "to think upon, meditate, write down."
