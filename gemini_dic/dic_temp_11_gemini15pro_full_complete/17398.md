beakiest:/ˈbiːkiɪst/| adj.| 喙最长的，喙最突出的 |Having the most prominent or beak-like nose.| He had the beakiest nose I'd ever seen. 他有着我见过最像鸟喙的鼻子。|近义词：hooked, pointed, prominent|反义词: flat, snub|词性:形容词
Etymology: From beak + -y + -est.  "Beak" comes from Middle English *bek*, from Old North French *bec*, ultimately from Vulgar Latin *beccus*.
