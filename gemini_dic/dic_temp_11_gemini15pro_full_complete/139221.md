objuration:/ˌɒbdʒʊˈreɪʃn/ |n.|恳求；请求；施魔法；强迫|An earnest or insistent request, especially one made to dissuade someone from doing something.  The act of binding by oath or spell.|He made a fervent objuration to his friend to stay. 他热切地恳求他的朋友留下来。|Synonyms: entreaty, supplication, plea, appeal, adjuration|Antonyms: command, order, demand|noun

objuration:/ˌɒbdʒʊˈreɪʃn/ |n.|咒语；符咒|The act of using magic words or spells to achieve a particular effect.|The witch uttered an objuration to summon the spirits. 女巫念了咒语来召唤灵魂。|Synonyms: incantation, spell, charm, hex|Antonyms: benediction, blessing|noun
