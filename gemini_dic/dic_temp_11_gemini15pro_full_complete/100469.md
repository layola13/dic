intervale:/ˈɪntərˌveɪl/ (US), /ˈɪntəvəl/ (UK)| n.|河边低地，间谷，河滩|A low-lying tract of land along a river, subject to flooding; a floodplain.|The hikers set up camp in a grassy intervale beside the river.  徒步旅行者在河边的草地河滩上扎营。|Synonyms: floodplain, bottomland, meadow, valley|Antonyms: upland, highland|noun

intervale:/ˈɪntərˌveɪl/ (US), /ˈɪntəvəl/ (UK)| n.|（美）河谷，溪谷|A valley, especially in New England.|The picturesque intervale was surrounded by towering mountains. 风景如画的河谷被巍峨的高山环绕。|Synonyms: valley, dale, glen|Antonyms: mountain, peak|noun
