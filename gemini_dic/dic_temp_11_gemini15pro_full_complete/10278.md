appanagist:/əˈpænɪdʒɪst/| n. |受俸禄者；领年金者；食俸禄者|A person who receives an appanage (an allowance or income, especially one given by the state to a member of a royal family).|The appanagist lived a life of leisure, thanks to his generous allowance from the crown. 这位受俸禄者过着悠闲的生活，这要归功于他从王室获得的丰厚津贴。|Synonyms: beneficiary, pensioner, annuitant|Antonyms: donor, benefactor|noun
