goffer:/ˈɡɒfə(r)/| n.|格子花纹，蜂窝状结构，格子布|A textured fabric, often with a waffle-like or honeycomb pattern.|The dress was made of a beautiful silk goffer.|近义词:waffle, honeycomb, seersucker|反义词:smooth, plain|词性:noun
词源:From Old French *gaufrer, from gaufre (“waffle”), from Frankish *waufla (“waffle”), from Proto-Germanic *wabilaz (“web”). Cognate with Dutch wafel, German Waffel.
