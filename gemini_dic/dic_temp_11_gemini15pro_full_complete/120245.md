mingles:/ˈmɪŋɡlz/ | v. | 混合，交往，交际 | to mix or join with something else; to move among people and talk to them, especially at a social event | She mingled with the crowd at the party. 她在派对上和人群混在一起。|近义词：mix, socialize, blend, associate, interact | 反义词: separate, isolate |词性:动词

mingles:/ˈmɪŋɡlz/ | n. | 混合，交际 | a mixture or mingling of different things or people |  The play was a strange mingle of comedy and tragedy.  这出戏是喜剧和悲剧的奇怪混合。|近义词：mixture, blend, combination | 反义词: separation |词性:名词
