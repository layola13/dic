cuneiform:/ˈkjuːnɪfɔːrm/|/ˈkjuːniːəˌfɔːrm/| adj. n.|楔形的；楔形文字|Relating to or written in cuneiform characters, a system of writing used in ancient Mesopotamia and Persia, characterized by wedge-shaped marks impressed on clay tablets.|The museum has a fascinating collection of cuneiform tablets.  博物馆收藏了引人入胜的楔形文字泥板。|The archaeologist deciphered the cuneiform inscriptions on the ancient monument. 考古学家破译了古代纪念碑上的楔形文字铭文。|近义词：wedge-shaped|反义词: (no clear antonym)|词性: adjective, noun

cuneatic:/kjuːˈniːætɪk/| adj.|楔形的;楔形文字的|Of or relating to cuneiform writing; wedge-shaped.|The cuneatic script is one of the earliest known writing systems. 楔形文字是最早的书写系统之一。|Researchers are still deciphering cuneatic inscriptions found on ancient tablets. 研究人员仍在破译在古代泥板上发现的楔形文字铭文。 |近义词：cuneiform|反义词: (no clear antonym)|词性: adjective
