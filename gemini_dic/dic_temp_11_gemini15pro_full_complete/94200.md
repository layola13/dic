imperceptive:/ˌɪmpərˈseptɪv/| adj.|不易察觉的；感觉迟钝的；|Not perceptible; not readily noticed or felt.|The changes were almost imperceptible.|近义词：unnoticeable, subtle, indiscernible, inconspicuous|反义词：perceptible, noticeable, obvious, evident|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From in- (not) + perceptive (capable of perceiving).  Ultimately from Latin *percept-, past participle stem of percipere "to seize, grasp," from per- "thoroughly" + capere "to take."
