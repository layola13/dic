disputableness:/dɪˈspjuːtəbəlnəs/ | n. | 可争辩性；争议性 | The quality of being disputable; open to dispute or debate. | The disputableness of the election results led to a recount.  选举结果的可争议性导致了重新计票。|Synonyms:  controversiality, debatability, contestability | Antonyms:  indisputability, certainty, unquestionableness | noun

disputable:/dɪˈspjuːtəbl/ | adj. | 可争辩的；有争议的 |  Open to dispute; debatable. | It is disputable whether this policy will actually reduce crime. 这项政策是否真的能减少犯罪是有争议的。| Synonyms: debatable, arguable, contestable, questionable | Antonyms: indisputable, undeniable, unquestionable, certain | adjective

dispute: /dɪˈspjuːt/ | n. vt. vi. | 争论；辩论；争议；质疑；争夺 | A disagreement, argument, or debate. / To argue about something; to question the truth or validity of something; to fight for control or ownership of something. | The two countries are in dispute over the ownership of the island. 这两个国家正在争夺该岛的所有权。/ They disputed the judge's decision. 他们对法官的判决提出了质疑。/ The teams disputed fiercely for the championship. 各队为争夺冠军而激烈竞争。 | Synonyms: argument, debate, quarrel, controversy, challenge, contest | Antonyms: agreement, harmony, accord, acceptance, concede | noun, verb
