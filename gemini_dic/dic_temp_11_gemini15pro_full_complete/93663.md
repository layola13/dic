imbued:/ɪmˈbjuːd/ |adj.|充满的，灌输的|Inspired or filled with specified quality or feeling.  Filled with a particular feeling or quality.| The old house was imbued with a sense of history. 这座老房子充满了历史感。|近义词：permeated, saturated, steeped, infused, impregnated|反义词：devoid, lacking, empty|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *embuer, from en- + buer (“to drink, soak”). Related to imbibe.  Ultimately from Latin imbuere (“to wet, moisten, soak”).
