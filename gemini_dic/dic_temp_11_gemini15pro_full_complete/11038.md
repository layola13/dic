arcane:/ɑːrˈkeɪn/ /ɑrˈkæn/| adj.|神秘的；晦涩难懂的；秘密的|Known or understood by only a few; mysterious and secret. |例句：The arcane rituals of the secret society. 秘密社团的神秘仪式。|例句：He spoke in arcane technical jargon. 他用晦涩难懂的技术术语讲话。|近义词：mysterious, esoteric, obscure, secret, cryptic|反义词：common, known, public, obvious, clear|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *arcanus, from Latin arcānus (“of the chest, secret, private”), from arca (“chest, box”).  The original meaning referred to things kept in a chest, hence secret or private.
