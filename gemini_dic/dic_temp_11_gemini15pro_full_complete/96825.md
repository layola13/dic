inescutcheon:/ɪnˈɛskʌtʃən/
noun
小纹章，纹章中的纹章
A small shield within a larger one, or a small area within a coat of arms that contains a separate charge.
The inescutcheon bore the family's original crest. 小纹章上有家族最初的徽章。
Synonyms:  small shield, inner shield, shield within a shield
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, possibly "field" (the background of the shield) as a contrasting element)
n.
