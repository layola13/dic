bursar: /ˈbɜːrsər/ (UK) /ˈbɜːrsər/ (US) | n. |财务主管；（大学） bursar | a person who is in charge of the financial affairs of a college or university | The bursar handles all the college's financial matters. 大学的财务主管处理所有学院的财务事宜。 | The bursar approved the funding for the new research project.  财务主管批准了新研究项目的资金。 | synonyms: treasurer, cashier | antonyms: debtor | noun
