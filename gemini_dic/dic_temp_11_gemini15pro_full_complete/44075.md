crinoidal:/kraɪˈnɔɪdəl/
adjective
海百合的；像海百合的
Relating to or resembling a crinoid (sea lily).
The fossil record shows abundant crinoidal remains in certain limestone formations.  这些石灰岩地层中的化石记录显示了大量海百合的残骸。
The delicate, branching structure was clearly crinoidal in origin.  这种精致的分支结构显然起源于海百合。
近义词:  sea lily-like
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-crinoidal")
词性: adjective
