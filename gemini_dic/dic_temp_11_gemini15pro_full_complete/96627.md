indulgingly:/ɪnˈdʌldʒɪŋli/
adverb
放纵地，纵容地
in a way that shows you are allowing someone to have or do what they want, even if it is not good for them
She smiled indulgently at her grandson. 她对孙子纵容地笑了笑。
Synonyms: leniently, permissively, tolerantly
Antonyms: strictly, severely, disapprovingly
词性: 副词
