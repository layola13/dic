freezing:/ˈfriːzɪŋ/| adj. n.| 极冷的；结冰；冷藏| Extremely cold; the process of becoming a solid by losing heat; preservation of food by lowering its temperature below freezing point. |例句：It's freezing outside today. 今天外面非常冷。|近义词：icy, frigid, chilly, cold|反义词：hot, boiling, scorching|词性:形容词

freezing:/ˈfriːzɪŋ/| n.| 冰点；严寒| The temperature at which a liquid turns into a solid; extremely cold weather.|例句：The freezing of the lake prevented us from skating. 湖的结冰使我们无法滑冰。|近义词：frost, chill|反义词：thaw, melt|词性:名词
