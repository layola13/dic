benevolentness:/bəˈnevələntnəs/
n.
仁慈；善心；慈善
The quality of being well meaning and kindly.
His benevolentness extended to all creatures, great and small. 他的仁慈惠及所有生物，无论大小。
kindness, generosity, compassion, goodwill
malice, cruelty, hostility, malevolence
noun
