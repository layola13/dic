epistle:/ɪˈpɪsəl/| n. |书信，正式信函；（尤指）使徒书信|A letter, especially a formal or didactic one; specifically, one of the letters included as books of the New Testament.|Paul's Epistle to the Romans is a foundational text for Christian theology. 保罗致罗马人的书信是基督教神学的基础文本。|The author penned lengthy epistles to his friends overseas.  作者给他的海外朋友写了长长的信。|letter, missive, communication|report, memo|noun
