antitarnish:/ˌæntiˈtɑːrnɪʃ/
adjective
防污的，防锈的，抗变色的
Preventing or slowing the discoloration of metal surfaces.
The antitarnish cloth helps keep your silver shining. 这块防锈布能帮助你的银器保持光亮。
synonyms:  corrosion-resistant, rustproof, non-tarnishing
antonyms:  tarnishing, corrosive
词性: adjective
