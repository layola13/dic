offshoring:/ˌɒfˈʃɔːrɪŋ/ /ˌɔːfˈʃɔːrɪŋ/
n.
离岸外包；境外生产
The practice of basing some of a company's processes or services overseas, so as to take advantage of lower costs.
Many companies are offshoring their manufacturing to countries with lower labor costs. 许多公司将他们的制造业务外包到劳动力成本较低的国家。
outsourcing, relocation, globalization
insourcing, domestic production
noun
