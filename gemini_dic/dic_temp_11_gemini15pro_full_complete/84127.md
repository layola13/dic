half-marked:/ˌhɑːfˈmɑːrkt/
adjective
半标记的，部分标记的
partly or incompletely marked.
The exam papers were half-marked when the fire alarm went off.  考卷批改到一半时，火警响了。
Some of the butterflies were half-marked, making identification difficult. 一些蝴蝶只有部分标记，使得鉴定变得困难。
partially marked, incompletely marked
fully marked, completely marked
adjective
