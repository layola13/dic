absentnesses:/ˈæbsəntnɪsɪz/
n.
缺席；心不在焉；神志不清
The state of being absent, often repeatedly.  Repeated instances of being away or not present. A lack of attention or focus.
His frequent absentnesses from class worried his teachers. 他经常缺课让老师们很担心。
Her absentnesses during the meeting were noticeable.  她在会议期间的心不在焉很明显。
近义词：absences, nonattendances
反义词：presences, attendances
词性：名词(plural)


absence:/ˈæbsəns/
n.
缺席，不在；缺乏；没有
The state of being away from a place or person. The state of something being lacking or not present.
His absence from the meeting was noted. 他缺席会议的情况已被记录在案。
The absence of evidence is not the evidence of absence.  缺乏证据并不等于没有证据。
近义词：lack, nonexistence, want
反义词：presence, existence
词性：名词

absent:/ˈæbsənt/
adj.
缺席的；缺少的；心不在焉的
Not present in a place, at an occasion, or as part of something.
He was absent from school today. 他今天没来上学。
Love was totally absent from his childhood.  他的童年完全没有爱。
近义词：missing, lacking, away
反义词：present, existing
词性：形容词

absent:/æbˈsɛnt/
v.
缺席，不参加
To stay away from a place or an event.
He absented himself from the meeting. 他没有出席会议。
She absented herself from work due to illness. 她因病缺勤。
近义词：withdraw, stay away
反义词：attend, present
词性：动词


词源:
From Late Latin absent- (stem of absens), present participle of abesse “to be away,” from ab- “away” + esse “to be.”
