episcopalianisms:/ɪˌpɪskəˈpeɪliənɪzəmz/
n.
主教制；主教制特征；主教制原则
The characteristic beliefs and practices of Episcopalians.  
例句：The book explores the various episcopalianisms found within the Anglican Communion.  这本书探讨了圣公会内部存在的各种主教制。

例句：Her strong episcopalianisms led her to question the authority of the Pope. 她坚定的主教制原则让她质疑教皇的权威。

近义词：Episcopacy, hierarchical church governance
反义词：Congregationalism, Presbyterianism
词性：Noun
