anti-theist:/ˌæntiˈθiːɪst/ | n. | 反神论者；积极反对有神论者 | Someone who actively opposes theism; the belief in the existence of a god or gods. | He declared himself an anti-theist, arguing vehemently against any form of religious belief.  他宣称自己是一个反神论者，强烈反对任何形式的宗教信仰。| Example: Richard Dawkins is a well-known anti-theist. 理查德·道金斯是一位著名的反神论者。 | Synonyms: atheist, non-theist, unbeliever | Antonyms: theist, believer | noun
