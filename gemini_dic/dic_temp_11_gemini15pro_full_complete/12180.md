as far as:/əz ˈfɑːr əz/
|conj. adv.| 至於；就...而言；在...范围内|Relating to the particular aspect or subject specified.  It indicates the limit or extent of something.|
例句：As far as I know, he's still working there.  就我所知，他还在那里工作。
例句：We walked as far as the bridge. 我们一直走到桥边。|
近义词：to the extent that, insofar as, concerning, regarding, with respect to, up to|
反义词:regardless of|
词性:conjunction, adverb


as far as:/əz ˈfɑːr əz/
|prep.|直到, 远到|Up to a certain point or distance.|
例句：The flood waters reached as far as the roof. 洪水漫到了屋顶。
例句：They travelled as far as London. 他们远行到伦敦。|
近义词：up to, to|
反义词:beyond|
词性:preposition
