anthropoid:/ˈænθrəpɔɪd/ | adj. n. |类人的；类人猿 |Resembling a human being in form.  A primate, especially an ape or monkey.|

例句：
The museum's anthropoid collection includes skeletons of gorillas and chimpanzees.
博物馆的类人猿收藏包括大猩猩和黑猩猩的骨骼。

The gibbon is an anthropoid ape found in Southeast Asia.
长臂猿是一种在东南亚发现的类人猿。


近义词：humanlike, simian |
反义词：non-anthropoid |
词性：adjective, noun |


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *anthropoïde*, from *anthropo-* (from Greek *ánthrōpos* "human being") + *-oïde* (from Greek *-oeidḗs*, from *eîdos* "form, shape").
