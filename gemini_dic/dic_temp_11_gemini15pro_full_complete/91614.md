hyperarid:/ˌhaɪpərˈærɪd/ | adj. | 极度干旱的 | Extremely arid; having little or no rainfall. |  The Atacama Desert is a hyperarid environment. 阿塔卡马沙漠是一个极度干旱的环境。|  The researchers studied plant adaptation in hyperarid regions. 研究人员研究了极度干旱地区的植物适应性。| Near synonyms: extremely arid, desert, barren | Near antonyms: humid, wet, lush | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- + arid.  "Hyper-" is a prefix of Greek origin meaning "over, above, beyond," often indicating excess. "Arid" comes from the Latin word "aridus," meaning dry or parched.
