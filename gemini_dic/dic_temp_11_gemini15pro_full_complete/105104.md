kerolite:/ˈkɛrəˌlaɪt/| n. | 叶蜡石 | A soft, whitish clay mineral, a hydrated magnesium silicate. | Kerolite is often found in association with serpentine.  叶蜡石常与蛇纹石伴生。

Kerolite can be used as a filler in paints. 叶蜡石可以用作油漆中的填料。| 近义词：talc, sepiolite | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
