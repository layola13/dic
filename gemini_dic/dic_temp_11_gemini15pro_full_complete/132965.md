nonemployees:/ˌnɒnɪmˈplɔɪiːz/
n.
非雇员；非员工
People who are not employed by a particular company or organization.
Nonemployees are not allowed in the staff-only area. 非雇员不得进入仅限员工的区域。
The company hired security guards to prevent nonemployees from entering the building.  公司雇佣了保安来防止非雇员进入大楼。
outsiders, non-staff, non-workers
employees, staff, personnel
