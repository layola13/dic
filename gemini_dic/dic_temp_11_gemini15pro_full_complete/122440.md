mobbing:/ˈmɒbɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈmɑːbɪŋ/ (US)| n.|欺凌，围攻，聚众滋扰|The act of a group of people harassing and attacking someone, either physically or emotionally, often repeatedly over a period of time.|He was the victim of relentless mobbing at school. 他在学校遭受了无情的欺凌。|synonyms: bullying, harassment, persecution, victimization|antonyms: supporting, protecting|noun


mobbing:/ˈmɒbɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈmɑːbɪŋ/ (US)| v.|聚众攻击，成群围住|To attack or harass someone as a group.|The crows were mobbing the owl.  乌鸦成群地围攻猫头鹰。|synonyms:  ganging up on, swarming, besieging|antonyms:  dispersing, scattering|verb
