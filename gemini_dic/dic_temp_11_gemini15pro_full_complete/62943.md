enneagonal:/ɪˈniːəɡənəl/
adjective
九边的；九角形的
Having nine sides and nine angles.
A regular enneagon has nine equal sides and nine equal angles.  一个正九边形有九条相等的边和九个相等的角。
The architect designed a building with an enneagonal courtyard.  建筑师设计了一座带有九边形庭院的建筑。
近义词:nonagonal
反义词:(None -  There isn't a direct opposite for a shape with a specific number of sides.)
词性:adjective
