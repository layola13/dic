expository:/ɪkˈspɑːzɪtɔːri/ /ɛkˈspɒzɪtəri/
adjective
说明的，解释的，阐述的
Intended to explain or describe something.
The teacher assigned an expository essay on the causes of the American Civil War. 老师布置了一篇关于美国内战起因的说明文。
synonyms: explanatory, descriptive, illustrative, elucidative
antonyms: narrative, fictional, imaginative
词性: 形容词
