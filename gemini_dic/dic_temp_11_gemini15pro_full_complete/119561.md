microswitch:/ˈmaɪkroʊswɪtʃ/| n. |微动开关，微型开关|A small, sensitive switch that is activated by very little pressure.|例句：The microswitch activates the alarm when the door is opened. 门打开时，微动开关会启动警报。

例句：The device uses a microswitch to detect when the lid is closed. 该设备使用微动开关来检测盖子何时关闭。|近义词：miniature switch, snap-action switch|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps a large, insensitive switch)|词性:noun
