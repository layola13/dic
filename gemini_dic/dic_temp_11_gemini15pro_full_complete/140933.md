one-and-all:/ˌwʌn ən ˈɔːl/ | pronoun | 全体；每个人；所有的人 |  Referring to everyone, without exception.  It emphasizes inclusivity.  Originates from the phrase "one and all" meaning each individual and the entire group collectively. | The invitation to the party was extended to one and all.  派对的邀请发送给了所有人。 |  everybody, everyone, each and every one | nobody | pronoun
