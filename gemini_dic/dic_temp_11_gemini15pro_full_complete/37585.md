communer:/kəˈmjuːnər/ | n. | 共享者，交流者，交感者 |  A person who shares or participates in something, especially spiritual or emotional experiences. |  例句：The forest became a sanctuary for communers seeking solace in nature. 森林成了在大自然中寻求慰藉的共享者们的避难所。

例句：She felt a deep connection with the other communers at the retreat.  她在静修中心与其他交流者产生了深厚的联系。 | 近义词：sharer, participant, communicant | 反义词：isolate, loner | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *comuner*, from Old French *comunier*, from Late Latin *communicare* "to share," from Latin *communis* "common."  Related to words like "common," "community," "communicate."
