autarchist:/ˈɔːtɑːrkɪst/
noun
独裁者；主张自给自足的人
A ruler who has absolute power; a person who believes in self-sufficiency.
The autarchist implemented strict trade policies to isolate the nation.  独裁者实施了严格的贸易政策以孤立该国。
The community thrived under the guidance of the autarchist, who emphasized local production and minimal reliance on external resources.  在强调本地生产和尽量减少对外部资源依赖的独裁者的指导下，社区蓬勃发展。
近义词: autocrat, dictator, despot
反义词: democrat
词性: n.


autarchy: /ˈɔːtɑːrki/
noun
独裁统治；自给自足
Rule by a single person with unlimited power; economic self-sufficiency.
The small island nation practiced autarchy, producing all its own food and goods.  这个小岛国实行自给自足，自己生产所有的食物和商品。
The autarchy of the regime led to its eventual downfall, as it became increasingly isolated and vulnerable. 该政权的独裁统治导致了它最终的垮台，因为它变得越来越孤立和脆弱。
近义词: absolutism, autocracy, self-sufficiency
反义词: democracy, interdependence
词性: n.


autarchic: /ɔːˈtɑːrkɪk/
adjective
独裁的；自给自足的
Relating to autarchy; self-sufficient.
The autarchic policies of the government aimed to reduce dependence on foreign trade. 政府的自给自足政策旨在减少对对外贸易的依赖。
The village maintained an autarchic lifestyle, growing its own food and producing its own necessities.  这个村庄保持着自给自足的生活方式，自己种植粮食，生产生活必需品。
近义词: self-sufficient, independent, autonomous
反义词: dependent, interconnected
词性: adj.


词源: From Greek *autarkhēs* "ruling oneself," from *autos* "self" + *arkhein* "to rule."
