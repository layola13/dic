juicers:/ˈdʒuːsərz/
n.
榨汁机;经常喝果汁的人
A machine for extracting juice from fruit or vegetables.  A person who regularly drinks juice.
I bought a new juicer to make fresh orange juice every morning. 我买了一个新的榨汁机，每天早上榨新鲜橙汁。
Health-conscious juicers often start their day with a glass of green juice. 注重健康的果汁爱好者通常以一杯绿色果汁开始新的一天。
synonyms: juice extractor, juice maker, blender (for some types), juice enthusiast, juice drinker
antonyms: N/A (no direct antonym for a machine or a person who drinks juice)
noun


juice:/dʒuːs/
n.
果汁;精力;影响力
The liquid obtained from fruit or vegetables. Energy, vitality, or influence.
This orange juice is freshly squeezed.  这种橙汁是鲜榨的。
He’s lost his juice and can't seem to get anything done. 他失去了活力，似乎什么事也做不了。
synonyms: nectar, liquid, extract, vitality, energy, power, influence
antonyms: (For liquid meaning) solid, pulp. (For energy meaning) lethargy, weakness, powerlessness
noun, verb
