gnomonics:/nəˈmɒnɪks/ | n. | 日晷学；测时术 | The art or science of designing, constructing, and using sundials to measure time. |  He studied gnomonics as a hobby, fascinated by the ancient methods of timekeeping. 他把日晷学作为一种爱好来研究，对古代的计时方法很着迷。 | 近义词: horology | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *gnōmōn* (indicator, interpreter) + *-ikos* (suffix forming adjectives).
