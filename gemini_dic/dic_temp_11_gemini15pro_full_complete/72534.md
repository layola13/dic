flusteredly: /ˈflʌstərdli/ | adv. | 慌张地，慌乱地 | In a flustered manner; nervously and confusedly. | She flusteredly searched for her keys. 她慌乱地寻找她的钥匙。|  She answered the question flusteredly, dropping her pen in the process.  她慌张地回答问题，过程中还掉了笔。 | 近义词: nervously, agitatedly, anxiously, disconcertedly | 反义词: calmly, composedly, serenely | 词性: 副词

词源: From the verb "fluster," which originated in the 18th century, possibly from a Scandinavian source.
