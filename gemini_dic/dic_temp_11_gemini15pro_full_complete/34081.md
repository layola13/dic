civility:/səˈvɪləti/| n.|礼貌；客气；端庄|Polite and courteous behavior.  The act of being civil.|例句：He treated everyone with civility. 他待人彬彬有礼。

例句：She was surprised by the civility of the strangers she met.  她对遇到的陌生人的礼貌感到惊讶。

近义词：courtesy, politeness, good manners, decorum, respect|反义词：rudeness, impoliteness, incivility, disrespect, discourtesy|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *civilité*, from Latin *cīvīlitās* ("citizenship, courtesy"), from *cīvīlis* ("relating to citizens"), from *cīvis* ("citizen").
