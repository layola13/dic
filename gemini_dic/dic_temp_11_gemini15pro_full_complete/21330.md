bloodying:/ˈblʌdiɪŋ/
verb (present participle of bloody)
使流血；沾染鲜血
covering or staining with blood; wounding so as to cause bleeding
The boxer was bloodying his opponent's nose.  拳击手打得对手鼻子流血。

近义词:wounding, injuring, gashing, lacerating
反义词:healing, curing

词性:v.
