infraction's:/ɪnˈfrækʃənz/| n.|违规行为，违反；侵害|A violation of a law, rule, or agreement.|The student received a detention for his infraction of the school's dress code.  这个学生因违反学校的着装规定而被留校。|Synonyms: violation, breach, offense, transgression|Antonyms: obedience, compliance|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English infraction, from Late Latin *infractiōn- (stem of infractiō), from Latin infractus (past participle of infringere ‘to break, infringe’), from in- ‘into, in’ + frangere ‘to break’.
