fled:/fled/ | v. | 逃跑，逃离 | Past tense and past participle of "flee". It means to run away from danger or from something unpleasant. |  The thief fled the scene. 小偷逃离了现场。

|  The family fled the country during the war.  这家人在战争期间逃离了这个国家。 | 近义词: escaped, ran away, absconded | 反义词:  faced, confronted, remained | 词性: 动词
