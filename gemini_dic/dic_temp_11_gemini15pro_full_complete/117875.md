melodize:/ˈmɛləˌdaɪz/ | v. | 使优美；谱成歌曲；赋予旋律 | To make melodious; to write a melody for; to give a melodic character to. |  The composer melodized the poem, turning it into a beautiful art song. 作曲家为这首诗谱了曲，把它变成了一首优美的艺术歌曲。

The playwright melodized the dialogue, making it more lyrical and engaging. 剧作家为对话谱写了旋律，使其更具抒情性和吸引力。| 近义词：harmonize, set to music | 反义词：disharmonize | 词性: verb
