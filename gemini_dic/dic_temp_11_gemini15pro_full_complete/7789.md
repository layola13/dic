answered:/ˈɑːnsərd/ /ˈænsərd/ | v. |回答，答复|Past participle of "answer".  It indicates that a question or request has been addressed or dealt with. | She answered the phone. 她接了电话。| I answered all the questions on the test. 我回答了测试中的所有问题。|近义词：replied, responded, addressed |反义词:ignored, unanswered|verb
