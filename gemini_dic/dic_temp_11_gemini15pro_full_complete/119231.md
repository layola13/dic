miasma: /maɪˈæzmə/ /miˈæzmə/ | n. | 瘴气，臭气，不良气氛 | A heavy vaporous emanation or atmosphere, especially one considered poisonous or unwholesome.  A pervasive corrupting influence. | The miasma of despair settled over the town after the factory closed. 工厂关闭后，绝望的氛围笼罩着整个小镇。 |  The political miasma surrounding the scandal threatened to engulf the entire government.  围绕丑闻的政治阴霾有可能吞没整个政府。| synonyms: stench, stink, reek, effluvium, vapor, fumes | antonyms: fragrance, perfume, aroma | noun
