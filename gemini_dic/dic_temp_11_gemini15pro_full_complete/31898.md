cheeselep:/ˈtʃiːzˌlɛp/ | n. |跳奶酪游戏；跳奶酪|A children's game involving leaping over a progressively higher object, often a stack of cushions or mats, representing a cheese. |The children were playing cheeselep in the garden. 孩子们在花园里玩跳奶酪游戏。|Leapfrog | None | Noun
