hawkishly:/ˈhɔːkɪʃli/| adv.|强硬地，鹰派地|In a manner resembling a hawk; aggressively, especially in advocating war or other military action.|The senator hawkishly advocated for increased military spending.  参议员强硬地主张增加军费开支。|例句：The general spoke hawkishly about the need for a preemptive strike. 将军强硬地谈到了先发制人的必要性。|近义词：aggressively, belligerently, воинственно|反义词：peacefully, dovishly,  миролюбиво|词性:副词
