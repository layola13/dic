emblazonry: /ɪmˈbleɪzənri/ | n. | 纹章装饰；纹章学；炫耀 | the art of describing or depicting coats of arms | The castle walls were covered in colorful emblazonry. 城堡的墙壁上覆盖着色彩鲜艳的纹章装饰。 | synonyms: heraldry, insignia, decoration, ornamentation | antonyms: plainness, simplicity | noun

emblazon: /ɪmˈbleɪzən/ | vt. | 用纹章装饰；颂扬；使显著 | to adorn with or as if with heraldic bearings |  His shield was emblazoned with a golden lion. 他的盾牌上饰有金狮。 | synonyms: decorate, adorn, celebrate, extol | antonyms: conceal, hide | verb
