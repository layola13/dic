ironically: /aɪˈrɒnɪkli/ (UK) /aɪˈrɑːnɪkli/ (US) | adv. | 讽刺地，具有讽刺意味地 | In a way that is strange or funny because it is the opposite of what is expected. |  He smiled ironically. 他讽刺地笑了笑。| Ironically, the fire station burned down. 具有讽刺意味的是，消防局被烧毁了。| 近义词: sarcastically, wryly | 反义词: sincerely, genuinely | 词性: 副词

词源:
From irony + -ically.  "Irony" comes from the Late Latin "ironia", from the Greek "eirōneia" meaning "dissimulation, assumed ignorance".
