excito:/ɛkˈsaɪtoʊ/ | n. |兴奋毒素 |A substance that damages or kills nerve cells by overexciting them. | Excessive glutamate can act as an excitotoxin, contributing to neuronal damage after stroke. 过量的谷氨酸可以作为兴奋毒素，导致中风后的神经元损伤。 |  近义词: neurotoxin | 反义词: neuroprotectant | 词性: noun

excito:/ɛkˈsaɪtoʊ/ | adj. |兴奋性的 | Relating to or characterized by excitation, especially of nerve cells. | Excitotoxic cell death is a major contributor to neurodegenerative diseases. 兴奋性细胞死亡是神经退行性疾病的主要原因。 | 近义词: stimulatory | 反义词: inhibitory | 词性: adjective
