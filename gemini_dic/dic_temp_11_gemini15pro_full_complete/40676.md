conversational:/ˌkɒnvəˈseɪʃənl/ (UK) /ˌkɑːnvərˈseɪʃənl/ (US)| adj.|会话的，谈话的|Relating to or characteristic of conversation; informal.|例句：He has a conversational style of teaching. 他有一种谈话式的教学风格。|近义词：informal, chatty, casual|反义词：formal, stiff, reserved|词性:形容词
Etymology: Late 18th century. From conversation + -al.
