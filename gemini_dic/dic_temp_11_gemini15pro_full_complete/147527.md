overpass:/ˌoʊvərˈpæs/ | vt. |越过；超过；忽略|To pass across, over, or beyond.|The plane overpassed the city. 飞机飞过了城市。|He overpassed the speed limit. 他超速了。|Synonyms: bypass, exceed, surpass, overlook|Antonyms: fail to reach|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From over + pass. "Over" signifies "above" or "across," while "pass" denotes "to go" or "a way."  Thus, an overpass is literally a way that goes over something else.
