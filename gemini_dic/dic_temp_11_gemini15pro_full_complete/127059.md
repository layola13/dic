natalians:/nəˈtæliənz/| n.|当地出生的人；出生于同一天的人|People born in Natal or on the same day are called Natalians.  出生在纳塔尔或同一天的人被称为纳塔尔人。|例句：The Natalians celebrated the province's anniversary. 纳塔尔人庆祝了该省的周年纪念日。

例句：Many Natalians are proud of their heritage. 许多纳塔尔人以他们的传统为荣。|近义词：natives, inhabitants, residents|反义词:foreigners, aliens|noun
