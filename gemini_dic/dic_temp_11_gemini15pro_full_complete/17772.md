bedlam:/ˈbedləm/| n.|混乱；喧闹；疯人院|A scene of uproar and confusion.|The protest march descended into bedlam as the police tried to disperse the crowd. 抗议游行在警察试图驱散人群时陷入了混乱。|The emergency room was a bedlam of activity. 急诊室一片混乱。|Synonyms: chaos, mayhem, pandemonium, uproar, commotion|Antonyms: order, peace, tranquility, calm|noun

bedlams:/ˈbedləmz/| n.|混乱；喧闹；疯人院（复数）|Scenes of uproar and confusion or multiple asylums (rarely used in the plural).|Rarely used in the plural form.  很少用复数形式。|Synonyms: chaos, mayhem, pandemonium, uproar, commotion (plural forms)|Antonyms: order, peace, tranquility, calm (plural forms relating to multiple locations)|noun
