hackathon:/ˈhækəθɒn/ (UK) /ˈhækəˌθɑːn/ (US)| n.|黑客马拉松，编程马拉松|An event in which computer programmers and others involved in software development, including graphic designers, interface designers, project managers, and others, often collaborate intensively on software projects.|例句：The company hosted a hackathon to develop new mobile applications.  这家公司举办了一场黑客马拉松来开发新的移动应用程序。

例句：She won first prize in the hackathon for her innovative app design. 她凭借其创新的应用程序设计在黑客马拉松中赢得了第一名。|近义词：coding marathon, code fest, hackfest|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
