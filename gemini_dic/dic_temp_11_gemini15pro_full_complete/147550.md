overpenalization: /ˌoʊvərˌpiːnələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度惩罚，过分处罚 | The act of penalizing someone too harshly or excessively.  It refers to situations where the punishment given is disproportionately severe compared to the offense committed. | The judge's decision was seen as an overpenalization of the defendant, given the minor nature of the crime.  法官的判决被认为是对被告的过度惩罚，考虑到罪行的轻微性质。

The overpenalization of minor offenses can lead to overcrowding in prisons.  对轻微犯罪的过度惩罚会导致监狱人满为患。 | 近义词: excessive punishment, over-punishment | 反义词: under-punishment, leniency | 词性: 名词
