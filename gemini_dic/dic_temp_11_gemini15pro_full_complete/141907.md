orderlies:/ˈɔːrdərliz/ (US) /ˈɔːdəlɪz/ (UK)| n.| 护士；勤务兵；医院杂工|Hospital employees who do non-medical work such as cleaning or transporting patients.  A soldier who acts as an attendant to an officer.| The orderlies cleaned the rooms and made the beds. 护士们打扫了房间并整理了床铺。| The general's orderly brought him his coffee. 将军的勤务兵给他端来了咖啡。| synonyms: attendant, aide, assistant, helper | antonyms: supervisor, manager, director | noun

orderly:/ˈɔːrdərli/ (US) /ˈɔːdəli/ (UK)| adj.| 整洁的；有条理的；井然的|Neat and tidy; arranged in a systematic way.| Her desk was always surprisingly orderly. 她的桌子总是出奇地整洁。| The evacuation was orderly and efficient. 疏散工作井然有序，效率很高。| synonyms: neat, tidy, organized, systematic, methodical | antonyms: messy, disorganized, chaotic, cluttered | adjective
