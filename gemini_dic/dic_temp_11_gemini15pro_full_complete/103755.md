joule:/dʒuːl/ | n. | 焦耳 |The standard unit of work or energy in the International System of Units (SI), equal to the work done by a force of one newton when its point of application moves one meter in the direction of action of the force.  |It takes about 4.2 joules to raise the temperature of one gram of water by one degree Celsius. 将一克水升高一摄氏度大约需要 4.2 焦耳。|Synonyms: energy unit, work unit |Antonyms: N/A | noun
