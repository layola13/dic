googol:/ˈɡuːɡɒl/ (US) /ˈɡuːɡɔːl/ (UK)| n. |古戈尔；10的100次方|A googol is the large number 10 to the power of 100, written as 1 followed by 100 zeros. |There are more atoms in the observable universe than a googol. 可观测宇宙中的原子数量比古戈尔还多。

The number of possible chess games is estimated to be much larger than a googol.  国际象棋可能的棋局数量估计比古戈尔还要大得多。
