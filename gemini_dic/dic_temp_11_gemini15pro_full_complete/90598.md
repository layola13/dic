hulloing:/həˈloʊɪŋ/ |v. |呼叫，喊叫，大声招呼 |The sailors were hulloing to each other across the harbor. 水手们隔着海港互相呼喊。|近义词：shouting, yelling, calling, hailing |反义词: whispering |词性:动词
Etymology: From "hullo" + "-ing," where "hullo" is a variant of "hello," ultimately from "holla," an exclamation used to attract attention.
