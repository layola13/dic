nostalgically: /nɒˈstældʒɪkli/ /nɑːˈstældʒɪkli/
adverb
怀旧地；念旧地
In a way that involves thinking fondly about the past.

She spoke nostalgically about her childhood in the countryside.
她怀旧地谈起了她在乡下的童年。

He looked nostalgically at the old photographs.
他怀旧地看着那些老照片。

Synonym: longingly, wistfully, sentimentally
Antonym: unsentimentally, pragmatically

Etymology: From French *nostalgique* + *-ly*.  *Nostalgique* was coined in the late 17th century from Greek *nóstos* (return home) + *álgos* (pain, grief). Originally, nostalgia was considered a disease, a form of homesickness.
