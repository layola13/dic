nicked:/nɪkt/| v. adj.|刻痕，轻微割伤; 偷窃; 逮捕; (价格)很便宜的|From the Old Norse *hnikkja "to cut slightly". Originally referring to a small cut or notch, the meaning extended to stealing (perhaps originally referring to cutting a purse string) and then to arresting.|例句：The thief nicked my wallet. 小偷偷走了我的钱包。|例句：He nicked his finger while chopping vegetables. 他切菜时割破了手指。|近义词：steal, pilfer, swipe, nab, chipped, scratched, dented, inexpensive, cheap|反义词:expensive, costly, protect, guard|词性:动词(v.)，形容词(adj.)
