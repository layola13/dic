convulsibility:/kənˌvʌlsəˈbɪləti/| n.|抽搐性，惊厥倾向|The patient's convulsibility was a concern for the doctors. 病人的抽搐倾向令医生担忧。|例句：The medication reduced the child's convulsibility.  这种药物降低了孩子的抽搐倾向。

例句：His convulsibility made him susceptible to seizures. 他的惊厥倾向使他容易发作。|近义词：spasticity, excitability, irritability|反义词: stability, calmness|词性:noun

convulsive:/kənˈvʌlsɪv/| adj.|痉挛的，抽搐的|The convulsive movements indicated a serious neurological problem.  痉挛的动作表明存在严重的神经系统问题。|例句：Convulsive seizures can be frightening to witness.  目睹痉挛性癫痫发作可能会很可怕。

例句：He experienced a convulsive cough after inhaling the smoke. 吸入烟雾后，他经历了一阵痉挛性咳嗽。|近义词：spasmodic, jerky, fitful|反义词: smooth, controlled, steady|词性:adjective
