futurisms:/ˈfjuːtʃərɪzəmz/
n.
未来主义（艺术流派）; 未来主义作品
the artistic and social movement that originated in Italy in the early 20th century, emphasizing speed, technology, youth, violence, and objects such as the car, the aeroplane, and the industrial city. It glorified modernity and aimed to liberate Italy from the weight of its past.
例句：Futurisms celebrated the dynamism of modern life. 未来主义歌颂现代生活的活力。

例句：The museum's exhibit on futurisms showcased Italian art from the early 20th century.  博物馆的未来主义展览展示了20世纪早期的意大利艺术。

近义词：modernism, avant-garde
反义词：traditionalism, classicism
词性：noun
