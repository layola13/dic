lych:/lɪtʃ/ | n. | 停尸房；墓地 | A building or place where corpses are kept before burial. | The lych gate of the church provided a somber entrance. 教堂的停尸房提供了一个阴森的入口。|  Example: The villagers carried the coffin through the lych gate. 村民们抬着棺材穿过停尸房的大门。| synonyms: mortuary, charnel house | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider "home," "living room" for contrasting concepts) | noun
