anti-greek:/ˌæntiˈɡriːk/
adjective
反希腊的；敌视希腊的
opposed to Greece, its people, or its culture.
例句：His anti-greek sentiments were evident in his writings. 他的反希腊情绪在他的著作中显而易见。

例句：The anti-greek riots caused significant damage to the city. 反希腊的骚乱对城市造成了重大破坏。

近义词：Hellenophobic
反义词：Pro-Greek, Philhellene
词性: adjective
