judicious:/dʒuːˈdɪʃəs/
adjective
明智的；有见识的；审慎的
Having, showing, or done with good judgment or sense.
She made a judicious choice of career. 她明智地选择了职业。
wise, sensible, prudent
injudicious, unwise, imprudent
adjective
