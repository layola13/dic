conchometry:/kɒŋˈkɒmɪtri/ | n. |贝壳测量学；贝壳计测学 |The study of shell measurements, especially for the purpose of classification and identification of mollusks. |Conchometry plays a vital role in understanding the evolution and diversity of molluscan species. 贝壳测量学在理解软体动物物种的进化和多样性方面起着至关重要的作用。|Synonyms: shell measurement,  molluscan biometry |Antonyms: N/A | noun
