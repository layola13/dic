barky:/ˈbɑːrki/ | adj. | 似树皮的；粗糙的；覆蓋树皮的 | Resembling or characteristic of bark; rough; covered with bark. |  The tree had a rough, barky texture. 这棵树有着粗糙的、像树皮一样的纹理。 | synonyms: bark-like, coarse, rough, woody | antonyms: smooth, soft | adjective

barky:/ˈbɑːrki/ | adj. | （指狗）叫声响亮的；爱叫的 | (of a dog) given to barking loudly or frequently. | The barky dog next door kept me up all night. 隔壁那只爱叫的狗让我整夜没睡。 | synonyms: clamorous, noisy, yappy | antonyms: quiet, silent | adjective
