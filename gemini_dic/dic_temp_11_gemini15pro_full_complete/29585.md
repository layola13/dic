catechise: /ˈkætɪkaɪz/ (UK) /ˈkætəˌkaɪz/ (US)| v. |教导；盘问；以问答方式教导|To instruct by question and answer; to question closely or interrogate. |例句：The teacher catechised the students about the history of the Roman Empire. 老师以问答的方式向学生们讲解罗马帝国的历史。

例句：The lawyer catechised the witness about his alibi. 律师详细盘问了证人关于不在场证明的情况。|近义词：instruct, question, interrogate, examine, quiz|反义词: ignore, neglect|词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin catechizare, from Greek katēkhein, from kata- ‘down’ + ēkhein ‘to sound, resound’.
