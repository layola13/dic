nonfaultiest:/nɒnˈfɔːltiɪst/ | adj. | 最完美的，最无瑕疵的 |  Superlative form of "nonfaulty," meaning having the fewest faults or imperfections.  It implies being the best or most ideal version of something, free from any flaws. | This is the nonfaultiest diamond I've ever seen. 这是我见过的最完美的钻石。| Her performance was nonfaultiest, a true masterpiece. 她的表演堪称完美，是一件真正的杰作。 | 近义词: perfect, flawless, impeccable, immaculate | 反义词: faulty, flawed, imperfect, defective | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "Nonfaultiest" is derived from "fault" (from Old French "faulte," ultimately from Latin "fallere," to deceive, fail) with the prefix "non-" (not) and the superlative suffix "-iest."
