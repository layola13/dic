impingements:/ɪmˈpɪndʒmənts/
n.|碰撞，冲击，侵犯|The impingements of the sound waves on the eardrum cause it to vibrate. 声波对耳膜的冲击使其振动。|例句：The constant impingements of noise from the street made it difficult to concentrate.  街上不断的噪音干扰使人难以集中注意力。

例句：The new building's impingements on the neighbor's property led to a legal dispute. 新建筑侵占了邻居的财产，引发了法律纠纷。
|近义词：encroachments, intrusions, infringements|反义词: withdrawals, retreats|词性:名词

impingement:/ɪmˈpɪndʒmənt/
n.|碰撞，冲击，侵犯|An impingement is an act or instance of encroaching or impinging. 侵犯是指侵犯或冲击的行为或实例。|例句：The bright lights were an impingement on her privacy.  明亮的灯光侵犯了她的隐私。

例句：His constant questions were an impingement on my time. 他不停地提问，占用了我的时间。
|近义词：encroachment, intrusion, infringement|反义词: withdrawal, retreat|词性:名词
