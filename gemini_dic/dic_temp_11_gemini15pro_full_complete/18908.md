besnuff: /bɪˈsnʌf/
verb
涂上鼻烟; 用鼻烟弄脏
to soil or stain with snuff; to take snuff
He besnuffed his nose with a pinch of tobacco. 他用一撮烟草涂抹了他的鼻子。
stain, soil, smear
clean
v.
