gourdlike:/ˈɡɔːrdlaɪk/| adj.|像葫芦的，葫芦形的|Resembling a gourd in shape; shaped like a gourd.  Having the rounded, bulging shape typical of a gourd.|The plant produced a gourdlike fruit.  这种植物结出一种像葫芦一样的果实。|近义词：rounded, bulging, bulbous|反义词: slender, elongated|词性: adjective

Etymology:  "gourd" + "-like"

"gourd" comes from the Old French "gourde," which in turn comes from the Latin "cucurbita," meaning "gourd." "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
