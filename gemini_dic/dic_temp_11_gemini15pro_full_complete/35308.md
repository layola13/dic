cms:/siː ɛmˈɛs/ | n. | 内容管理系统 (Content Management System) | A software application that can be used to manage the creation and modification of digital content. |  Our website uses a powerful CMS to easily update content. 我们的网站使用强大的内容管理系统轻松更新内容。

cms:/siː ɛmˈɛs/ | n. |  厘米 (centimeters) | A unit of length in the metric system, equal to one hundredth of a meter. | The table is 75 cms high. 这张桌子高 75 厘米。

cms:/siː ɛmˈɛs/ |abbr.| Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services | The US federal agency that administers Medicare and Medicaid. | The new CMS regulations will impact healthcare providers. 新的医疗保险和医疗补助服务中心的规定将影响医疗保健提供者。
