nontractably:/nɒnˈtræktəbli/ | adv. | 棘手地，难以处理地 | Not easily managed or solved; in a way that is difficult or impossible to deal with computationally. | The problem is nontractably complex.  这个问题棘手的复杂。| The algorithm proved nontractably slow for large datasets.  事实证明，该算法对于大型数据集来说运行速度慢得难以解决。 | 近义词: insolubly, unmanageably, insuperably | 反义词: easily, manageably, tractably | 词性: 副词

non- (prefix): not
tractable (adjective): easily managed or controlled
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs
