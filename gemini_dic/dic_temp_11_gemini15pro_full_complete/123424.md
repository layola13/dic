monopody: /məˈnɒpədi/ (UK) /məˈnɑːpədi/ (US)| n. |独脚架; 单足;  单轴生长; |A support for a camera or other instrument, having a single leg or shaft.  In biology, a form of growth in which a new shoot or stalk develops as a continuation of the previous year's growth, as opposed to branching out. |例句：The photographer used a monopody to stabilize his camera for the long exposure. 摄影师用独脚架来稳定相机进行长时间曝光。

例句：The orchid exhibited monopodial growth, with each new leaf emerging from the apex of the stem.  这株兰花呈现出单轴生长，每一片新叶都从茎的顶端长出来。|近义词：unipod, single-leg stand (for the photography equipment sense); monopodial growth (for the biological sense)|反义词: bipod, tripod (for the photography equipment sense); sympodial growth (for the biological sense)|词性:noun
