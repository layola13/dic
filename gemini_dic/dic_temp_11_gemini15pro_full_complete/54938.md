dispossesses:/ˌdɪspəˈzɛsɪz/ | v. |剥夺，使失去 |To deprive someone of land, property, or other possessions. |The landlord dispossessed the tenant for not paying rent. 房东因房客不缴纳租金而将其驱逐。|The government dispossessed many farmers of their land. 政府剥夺了许多农民的土地。|Synonyms: deprive, divest, oust, evict |Antonyms: provide, give, endow |verb
