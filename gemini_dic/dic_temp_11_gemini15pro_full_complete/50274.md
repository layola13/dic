dephlegmator:/diːˈflɛɡmeɪtər/ | n. | 分馏器，冷凝器，回流冷凝器 | A device used in distillation to condense vapors and return some of the condensate to the still, increasing the purity of the distillate. | The chemist used a dephlegmator to refine the alcohol. 化学家使用分馏器提纯酒精。| The dephlegmator ensured a higher purity product was collected. 分馏器确保收集到更高纯度的产品。 | synonyms: reflux condenser, fractional condenser, partial condenser | antonyms: N/A | noun

dephlegmate:/diːˈflɛɡmeɪt/ | v. |  分馏，使减少痰 | To remove phlegm from; to clear of phlegm; to make less viscous. | The herbal tea helped to dephlegmate his chest. 草药茶帮助他清除了胸部的痰。| The process dephlegmates the crude oil, separating out different components. 这个过程对原油进行分馏，分离出不同的成分。 | synonyms: condense, refine, purify | antonyms: N/A | verb
