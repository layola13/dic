allomorphisms:/ˈæləˌmɔːrfɪzəmz/
|n.|同形异质异性体;异构体|Different crystalline forms of the same chemical compound are called allomorphisms.  同一种化合物的不同结晶形式称为同形异质异性体。|Allotropy is the property of some chemical elements to exist in two or more different forms, in the same physical state, known as allotropes of these elements. Allotropes are different structural modifications of an element; the atoms of the element are bonded together in a different manner.  同素异构现象是指一些化学元素以两种或多种不同形式存在于同一物理状态下的性质，这些不同形式被称为这些元素的同素异构体。同素异构体是元素的不同结构变体；元素的原子以不同的方式结合在一起。|
|Synonyms: allotropes, polymorphs|Antonyms: N/A|Noun|
