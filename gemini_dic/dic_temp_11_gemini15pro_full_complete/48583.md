defeatism:/dɪˈfiːtɪzəm/
noun
失败主义，悲观主义
The acceptance of defeat without struggle; the attitude of one who is ready to accept defeat as inevitable.
The army was plagued by defeatism. 这支军队充满了失败主义情绪。
近义词:pessimism, negativity, cynicism, hopelessness
反义词:optimism, hopefulness, positivism
n.

defeatism:/dɪˈfiːtɪzəm/
n.
失败主义，悲观主义
The feeling that you are certain to fail and that there is no point in trying any more.
Their defeatism was understandable, considering their team hadn't won a game all season. 考虑到他们的球队整个赛季都没有赢过一场比赛，他们的失败主义是可以理解的。
近义词:discouragement, resignation
反义词:determination, resilience
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *défaitisme*, from *défaite* ("defeat"), from *défaire* ("to undo"), from Latin *disfacere* ("to undo").
