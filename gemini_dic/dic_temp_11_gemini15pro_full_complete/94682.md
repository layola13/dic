imprisoner:/ɪmˈprɪzənər/ | n. |囚禁者，监禁者|A person who imprisons someone.  Someone who confines or restricts another's freedom. |The king was the jailer’s prisoner. 国王是狱卒的囚犯。|  The kidnapper turned from captor to prisoner when the police surrounded the building. 当警察包围大楼时，绑匪从俘虏者变成了囚犯。|Synonyms: captive, convict, detainee, inmate| Antonyms: liberator, rescuer | noun
