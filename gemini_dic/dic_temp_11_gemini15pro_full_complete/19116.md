betulin:/ˈbetjʊlɪn/ | n. |桦木醇|A crystalline triterpenoid alcohol, C30H50O2, found in the bark of birch trees and used in certain cosmetics and pharmaceuticals.|Birch bark contains betulin, which has medicinal properties. 白桦树皮含有桦木醇，具有药用价值。|Synonyms: betulinol, lup-20(29)-ene-3β,28-diol|Antonyms: N/A|noun
