anisodactylous: /ˌænɪsoʊˈdæktɪləs/
| adj. |不等趾的；异趾型的|Having three toes pointing forward and one pointing backward, as in most birds.|The robin is an anisodactylous bird. 知更鸟是不等趾的鸟类。|Synonyms:  zygodactyl (partially), heterodactyl (partially) |Antonyms:  pamprodactyl|adjective
Etymology->
From Greek *anisos* (unequal) + *daktylos* (finger, toe)
