imbibes:/ɪmˈbaɪbz/| v. |吸收，吸取（液体）；接受，领会（思想、知识等）| to drink (alcohol); to absorb or assimilate (ideas or knowledge) |例句：The sponge imbibes water quickly. 海绵迅速吸收水分。

例句：She imbibed the culture of the new country. 她吸收了这个新国家的文化。|近义词：absorb, assimilate, drink, soak up, take in|反义词：exude, emit, release|词性:动词

Etymology: From Late Latin imbibere, from Latin in- + bibere "to drink."
