mass-producing:/ˌmæs prəˈduːsɪŋ/ | adj. | 大量生产的 | Producing something in large quantities, typically by using machinery. |  The factory is mass-producing cars for the domestic market.  这家工厂正在为国内市场大量生产汽车。| synonyms: mass manufacturing, bulk producing | antonyms: handcrafting, custom-making | adjective

mass-producing:/ˌmæs prəˈduːsɪŋ/ | v. | 大量生产 | To produce something in large quantities, typically by using machinery. | They are mass-producing the new phone model to meet demand. 他们正在大量生产新款手机以满足需求。 | synonyms: mass manufacture, bulk produce | antonyms: handcraft, custom-make | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "mass" (meaning a large quantity) + "producing" (present participle of "produce," meaning to create or manufacture). "Mass" ultimately comes from the Latin "massa" (lump, kneaded dough). "Produce" comes from the Latin "producere" (to lead forth, bring out).
