menagerie:/məˈnædʒəri/ /məˈnæʒəri/
n.|动物园；动物展览；收藏；一群（尤指吵闹的）人|A collection of wild animals kept in captivity for exhibition. A varied mixture of people or things.|She inherited a crumbling mansion and a small menagerie. 她继承了一座破旧的宅邸和一个小动物园。|Synonyms: zoo, collection, assortment, array, assemblage|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps emptiness, lack)|Noun
