ETYMOLOGY->

From Afrikaans *gemsbok*, from Dutch *gemsbok*, from *gems* (“chamois”) + *bok* (“buck, male goat”). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *gamaz* ("chamois") and *bukkaz* ("buck").
