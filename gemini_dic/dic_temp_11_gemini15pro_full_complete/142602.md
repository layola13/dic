osteoma:/ˌɒstiˈəʊmə/ (US) /ˌɑːstiˈoʊmə/| n. |骨瘤|A benign tumor of bone. It is a new piece of bone usually growing on another piece of bone, typically the skull.|He was diagnosed with an osteoma on his skull. 他被诊断出颅骨骨瘤。|Synonyms: bone tumor, exostosis|Antonyms: N/A|noun
