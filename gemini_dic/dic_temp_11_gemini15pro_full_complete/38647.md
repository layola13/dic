concurrences:/kənˈkʌrənsɪz/
|n.|并发；同时发生；同意；一致|The concurrences of several unfortunate events caused the company to fail.  几件不幸的事情同时发生导致了公司的倒闭。|The project requires the concurrence of all stakeholders. 该项目需要所有利益相关者的同意。|
|n.|并发；同时发生|Several factors acted in concurrence to bring about the crisis.  几个因素共同作用导致了这场危机。|The concurrence of their opinions on this matter was surprising. 他们在这一问题上意见一致，令人惊讶。|
|n.|同意；一致|We need your concurrence before we can proceed. 在我们继续之前，我们需要你的同意。|His concurrence with the plan was essential for its success. 他对该计划的同意对其成功至关重要。|
|近义词:|agreement, coincidence, conjunction, consent|
|反义词:|disagreement, conflict, opposition|
|词性:|名词|
|词源:|Late Middle English: from Old French concurrence, from Latin concurrentia, from concurrere ‘run together’, from con- ‘together’ + currere ‘to run’.|
