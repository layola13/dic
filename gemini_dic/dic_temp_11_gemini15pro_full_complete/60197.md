effigiation:/ˌɛfɪˈdʒiːeɪʃən/
n.
肖像, 雕像; 模仿; 描绘
The act of making an effigy or representation of something, especially a person.  The creation of a likeness or image, often used in the context of art or sculpture.
The effigiation of the king was impressive and lifelike. 国王的肖像栩栩如生，令人印象深刻。
The artist's effigiation of the model captured her inner beauty. 艺术家对模特的描绘捕捉到了她内在的美。
representation, portrayal, depiction, image, likeness
absence, omission
noun
