furies:/ˈfjʊəriːz/
noun
复仇女神；狂怒；激烈；暴怒
(usually with the) In Greek mythology, the Furies were three goddesses of vengeance.
例句：The furies pursued Orestes relentlessly. 复仇女神无情地追捕奥瑞斯特斯。

例句：He was filled with the furies of a betrayed lover. 他充满了被背叛的恋人的愤怒。
近义词：rage, wrath, vengeance, anger
反义词：calm, peace, tranquility
词性：n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin Furiae (“the Furies, goddesses of vengeance”), plural of furia (“rage, fury”), from furere (“to rage, be mad”).
