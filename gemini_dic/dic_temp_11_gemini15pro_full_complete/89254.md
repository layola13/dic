hominid:/ˈhɑːmɪnɪd/ | n. | 人科动物；原始人类 | An extinct primate of the family Hominidae, of which *Homo sapiens* is the only surviving species and which includes other extinct species of *Homo* as well as the extinct genera *Australopithecus*, *Paranthropus*, and *Ardipithecus*.  |  Many hominid fossils have been found in Africa. 许多人科动物化石在非洲被发现。|  synonyms: hominin, human ancestor | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps non-hominid primate) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From New Latin *Hominidae*, from Latin *homō* ("man, human being") +‎ *-idae* (taxonomic suffix).
