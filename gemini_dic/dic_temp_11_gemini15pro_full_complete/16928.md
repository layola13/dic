basqued:/bæskt/
adjective
巴斯克风格的；穿着巴斯克服装的
Of or relating to the Basque people or their language and culture, or characteristic of them, especially having a close-fitting bodice and peplum extending over the hips.
She wore a basqued dress to the festival. 她穿着一件巴斯克风格的连衣裙去参加节日。
The painting depicted a basqued woman in traditional garb. 这幅画描绘了一位穿着传统服装的巴斯克妇女。
近义词: Basque-style
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-Basque")
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Basque, relating to the Basque people, their language, or their region. The specific clothing style derives from traditional Basque attire.
