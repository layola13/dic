fluoroscopy:/flʊəˈrɒskəpi/ /flʊˈrɑːskəpi/
noun
荧光透视；荧光检查
An imaging technique that uses X-rays to obtain real-time moving images of the internal structures of a patient through the use of a fluoroscope.
The doctor used fluoroscopy to guide the catheter into the correct position. 医生使用荧光透视引导导管进入正确位置。

fluoroscopy:/flʊəˈrɒskəpi/ /flʊˈrɑːskəpi/
noun
荧光透视；荧光检查
The technique of using a fluoroscope to create and view X-ray images of the body in real time.
Fluoroscopy is commonly used in interventional radiology. 荧光透视常用于介入放射学。
