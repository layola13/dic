by-halves:/baɪˈhɑːvz/
|adv.|不完全地，敷衍地，半心半意地|Doing something by halves means doing it incompletely or carelessly, without putting in full effort.|He did the job by halves and it showed. 他敷衍了事，结果显而易见。|近义词：half-heartedly, perfunctorily, superficially, carelessly|反义词：thoroughly, completely, whole-heartedly|词性:副词
Etymology: From "by" (indicating manner) + "halves" (plural of half).  Implies doing something only partially.
