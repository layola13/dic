indifferently: /ɪnˈdɪfərəntli/ | adv. | 冷漠地，无所谓地 |  Without interest or concern; apathetically. | She shrugged indifferently and turned away. 她冷漠地耸了耸肩，转过身去。| He listened indifferently to the news. 他漠不关心地听着新闻。 | 近义词: apathetically, listlessly, unconcernedly, carelessly | 反义词: eagerly, enthusiastically, passionately, keenly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin indifferenter, from indifferent + -ly. Indifferent comes from Latin indifferentem, present participle of indifferre "to be unimportant," from in- "not" + differre "to differ."
