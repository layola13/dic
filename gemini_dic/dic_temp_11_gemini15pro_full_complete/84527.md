hammer:/ˈhæmər/ (US) /ˈhæmə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|锤子；敲打|A tool with a heavy metal head mounted at right angles to a handle, used for driving in nails, breaking rocks, etc.|例句：He used a hammer to drive the nail into the wood. 他用锤子把钉子钉进木头里。|近义词：mallet, gavel|反义词: (n.) None (A hammer doesn't have a direct opposite.)|n.

hammer:/ˈhæmər/ (US) /ˈhæmə(r)/ (UK)| v.|锤击；敲打|To hit or strike with a hammer.|例句：She hammered the dent out of the fender. 她把挡泥板上的凹痕敲平了。|近义词：pound, beat|反义词: (v.) None (The action of hammering doesn't have a direct opposite.)|v.
