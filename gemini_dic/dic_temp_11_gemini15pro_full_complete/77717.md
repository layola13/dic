gamification: /ˌɡeɪmɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |游戏化|The application of game-design elements and game principles in non-game contexts.  It can be used to engage audiences, motivate participants, and promote desired behaviors.|Example 1: Gamification is becoming increasingly popular in education as a way to make learning more engaging. 游戏化作为一种让学习更具吸引力的方式，在教育领域正变得越来越流行。

Example 2: The company used gamification to encourage employees to complete training modules.  这家公司利用游戏化来鼓励员工完成培训模块。|Synonyms: ludification, gameful design |Antonyms:  N/A |noun


gamify: /ˈɡeɪmɪfaɪ/ | v. |游戏化|To apply game-design elements and game principles to (something) typically as an online marketing technique to encourage engagement with a product or service.|Example 1:  Many apps gamify the experience of tracking your fitness. 许多应用程序将追踪健身的体验游戏化。

Example 2:  The company decided to gamify its customer loyalty program.  这家公司决定将其客户忠诚度计划游戏化。|Synonyms: ludify |Antonyms: N/A |verb
