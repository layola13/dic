globalising:/ˈɡloʊbəlaɪzɪŋ/ /ˈɡloʊbəˌlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 全球化；使全球化 | The world is rapidly globalising.  世界正在迅速全球化。| The company is globalising its operations.  该公司正在将其业务全球化。 | 近义词：internationalizing, mundializing | 反义词：localizing | 词性: 动词 (present participle)


Etymology:
From globe + -al + -ize + -ing.  Ultimately from Latin globus (“sphere, ball”).
