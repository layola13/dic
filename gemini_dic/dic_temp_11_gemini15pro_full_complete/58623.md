duly:/ˈduːli/ /ˈdjuːli/| adv.|适当地，正式地，按时地|In a proper or fitting manner; at the expected or correct time.|The contract was duly signed and witnessed.  合同已正式签署并见证。|Synonyms: properly, correctly, appropriately, punctually, rightfully|Antonyms: improperly, incorrectly, inappropriately, untimely|adverb
