blanquette:/blɑːŋˈkɛt/ | n. | 法式奶油炖肉（通常是小牛肉或鸡肉）| A white stew of veal, chicken, or lamb, in a creamy white sauce. |  She made a delicious blanquette de veau for dinner. 她晚餐做了美味的法式小牛肉奶油炖菜。

My grandmother's blanquette de poulet is a family favorite. 我祖母做的法式鸡肉奶油炖菜是家里的最爱。


|近义词: stew, fricassee |反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French "blanquette,"  feminine of "blanquet," diminutive of "blanc" (white), referring to the white color of the sauce.
