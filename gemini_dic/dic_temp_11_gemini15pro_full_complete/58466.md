ductless:/ˈdʌktləs/
adjective
无管的，没有导管的
Not having ducts; lacking a tubular passage for conveying a substance.
The ductless glands secrete hormones directly into the bloodstream. 无管腺直接分泌激素到血液中。
ductless glands secrete hormones directly into the bloodstream. 无管腺体将激素直接分泌到血液中。
ductless air conditioning uses refrigerant lines instead of ducts to distribute cool air.  无管空调使用制冷剂管线而不是管道来分配冷空气。
Synonyms: tubeless, glandless
Antonyms: ducted
词性: adjective
