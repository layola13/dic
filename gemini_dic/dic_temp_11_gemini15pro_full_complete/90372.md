how-toers:/ˈhaʊˌtuːərz/
noun
操作指南爱好者；实用技巧学习者
People who are enthusiastic about learning or sharing practical skills and instructions, often through "how-to" guides or tutorials.
Many how-toers find DIY projects fulfilling and cost-effective. 许多操作指南爱好者觉得DIY项目很有成就感且经济实惠。
How-toers often share their tips and tricks online.  实用技巧学习者经常在网上分享他们的技巧和窍门。
Synonyms: DIY enthusiasts, hobbyists, makers, tinkerers
Antonyms: professionals, experts (Sometimes, depending on context)
