chastisement:/ˈtʃæstɪzmənt/ (US), /ˈtʃæstɪzmənt/ (UK)| n. |惩罚；责罚；磨炼|The act of rebuking or punishing someone.  It implies a corrective or disciplinary action, often involving physical punishment in the past, but now more commonly referring to verbal reprimands or other forms of discipline.|The child received a chastisement for misbehaving.  这孩子因行为不端受到了惩罚。|Synonyms: punishment, discipline, correction, reprimand, rebuke|Antonyms: reward, praise, commendation|Noun
