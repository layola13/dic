furrower:/ˈfɜːroʊər/| n.|犁沟器，开沟器|A device or implement used to make furrows or grooves, especially in the soil for planting seeds or irrigating crops.|The farmer used a furrower to create neat rows for planting corn.  农民用开沟器开出整齐的沟来种玉米。|Synonyms: groover, trencher|Antonyms: leveler, smoother|noun

furrow:/ˈfɜːroʊ/| n. vt.|犁沟，皱纹；使起皱纹|A long narrow trench made in the ground by a plow; a wrinkle in the skin.|The farmer plowed furrows in the field. 农民在地里犁出犁沟。|He furrowed his brow in concentration. 他皱着眉头，聚精会神。|Synonyms: groove, trench, wrinkle, crease|Antonyms: smooth, level|noun, verb
