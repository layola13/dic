boater:/ˈboʊtər/ (US), /ˈbəʊtə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 划船者；戴平顶硬草帽的人；平顶硬草帽|A person who rows a boat or sails.  A stiff straw hat with a flat crown and brim.|例句：The boater skillfully navigated the narrow canal.  那个划船者熟练地驾驶着小船穿过狭窄的运河。

例句：He wore a boater and a blazer to the regatta. 他戴着平顶硬草帽，穿着休闲西装去了赛舟会。|近义词：rower, sailor, oarsman (for person); straw hat, skimmer (for hat)|反义词:landlubber|词性:noun
