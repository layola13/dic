opposes:/əˈpoʊzɪz/
verb
反对，对抗
resists or is against something or someone.
The senator opposes the new tax law.  这位参议员反对新的税法。
近义词:resists, objects to,  challenges,  fights, combats
反义词:supports, favors, advocates, promotes, champions
词性:v.

Etymology: From Old French *opposer, from Latin oppōnere (“to set against”), from ob- (“against”) + pōnere (“to put, place”).
