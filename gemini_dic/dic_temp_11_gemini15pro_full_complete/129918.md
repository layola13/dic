non-kosher:/ˌnɒnˈkəʊʃə(r)/ / ˌnɑːnˈkoʊʃər/| adj.|非犹太教规定的;|Not conforming to the dietary laws of Judaism; unfit for consumption according to these laws; figuratively, unacceptable or improper.|This restaurant serves non-kosher food. 这家餐厅供应非犹太教规定的食物。|The business deal felt non-kosher to him.  这笔生意让他觉得不正当。|Synonyms:  untreifa (specifically for food),  forbidden (in a religious context),  improper,  unacceptable,  dodgy,  shady|Antonyms: kosher|adjective
