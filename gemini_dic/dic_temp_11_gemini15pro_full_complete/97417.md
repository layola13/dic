infuscate: /ɪnˈfʌskeɪt/
verb
使变黑；使昏暗；使模糊；使（颜色）暗淡
to darken or make dim, as by smoke or fog; to obscure or blur
The smoke infuscated the sky. 烟雾使天空变暗。
Synonym: darken, obscure, dim, cloud
Antonym: brighten, illuminate, clarify
词性: 动词
