grossa:/ˈɡrɒsə/ | adjective | 粗大的，大的，厚的 |  (Italian) feminine form of grosso, meaning "large," "big," or "thick." Often used in musical notation and anatomical terms. | The violin's *grossa corda* produces a rich, deep sound. 小提琴的G弦发出浑厚而低沉的声音。| synonyms: large, big, thick, substantial | antonyms: small, thin, fine, delicate | etymology: From Italian *grosso*, ultimately from Latin *grossus* | Part of Speech: Adjective
