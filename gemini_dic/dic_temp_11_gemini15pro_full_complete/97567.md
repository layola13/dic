/ˈɪnˌɡruːp/ | n. | 内群体；自己所属的群体 | A social group to which a person psychologically identifies as being a member. |  Example: He felt a strong sense of belonging to his ingroup. 他对自己的内群体有强烈的归属感。


Example:  The ingroup often views other groups with suspicion. 内群体通常会以怀疑的态度看待其他群体。


| Synonym: clique, coterie, inner circle | Antonym: outgroup | noun
