cestoidea:/sɛsˈtɔɪdiə/
n.
绦虫纲
a class of parasitic flatworms, the tapeworms, that live in the intestines of vertebrates.
Tapeworms, which belong to the class Cestoidea, can infect humans through contaminated food.  绦虫，属于绦虫纲，可以通过受污染的食物感染人类。
cestodes, tapeworms
nematodes(线虫纲)
noun
