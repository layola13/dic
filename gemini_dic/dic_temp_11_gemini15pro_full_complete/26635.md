cacky:/ˈkæki/| adj.|糟糕的，蹩脚的，令人不快的|Something that is cacky is of poor quality or unpleasant.  It's typically used in informal British English.|The food at that restaurant was absolutely cacky. 那家餐馆的食物真是糟糕透了。|近义词：bad, awful, terrible, lousy, rubbish (UK), crap (vulgar)|反义词：good, excellent, wonderful|词性:形容词
词源:Likely a variant of "cack" (meaning excrement), reflecting the negative connotation.|
