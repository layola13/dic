infect:/ɪnˈfɛkt/ (US), /ɪnˈfɛkt/ (UK)| v. |感染，传染|To contaminate with a disease-producing substance or agent; to affect in a contagious way. |The flu virus infected many people in the office. 流感病毒感染了办公室里的很多人。|The news infected everyone with fear.  这个消息使每个人都感到恐惧。|近义词：contaminate, taint, pollute|反义词：cure, heal, cleanse|词性:动词

词源: From Latin *īnficere*, from *īn-* (“in, into”) + *facere* (“to make, to do”).  Originally meant "to dip in, to stain, to dye," later acquired the meaning "to taint, to corrupt," and finally "to infect with disease."
