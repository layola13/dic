millionth:/ˈmɪliənθ/| adj. n.|第一百万的，百万分之一|Representing the number one million in sequence; being one of a million equal parts.|This is the millionth time I've told you!|近义词:  one-millionth |反义词: N/A|词性:形容词、名词

Etymology: From Middle English *milliounth, derived from million + -th.  Ultimately from Old French million and Latin mille ("thousand").
