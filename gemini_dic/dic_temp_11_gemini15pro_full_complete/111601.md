lobsterlike:/ˈlɒbstəlaɪk/ (UK) /ˈlɑːbstərlaɪk/ (US)| adj.|像龙虾的；龙虾似的|Resembling or characteristic of a lobster, especially in having a hard shell and/or large claws.|The creature had a lobsterlike shell and large claws.  这种生物有着像龙虾一样的壳和大钳子。|The robot's arm was lobsterlike in its movements.  机器人的手臂动作像龙虾一样。|近义词：crustaceanlike, crablike|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly soft-bodied)|词性:形容词

词源:
lobster + like.  "Lobster" comes from Old English *loppestre, of uncertain origin. "-like" is a common suffix denoting resemblance.
