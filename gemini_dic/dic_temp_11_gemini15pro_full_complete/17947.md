befalling:/bɪˈfɔːlɪŋ/
verb
降临；发生
(especially of something bad) happening to someone.
A series of misfortunes befell the expedition. 一连串的不幸降临到探险队身上。
Synonyms: happening, occurring, taking place
Antonyms: averted, prevented
词性: 动词(Verb)
