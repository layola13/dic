hearsed:/rɪˈhɜːrst/ | adj. | 排练过的，演练过的 |  Having been rehearsed; practiced. |  The actors gave a polished performance, their lines perfectly hearsed.  演员们的表演非常出色，他们的台词经过了完美的排练。 | 近义词: practiced, rehearsed, prepared, trained | 反义词: unrehearsed, unprepared, improvised, extemporaneous | 词性:形容词

词源: From the verb "rehearse," which comes from Old French "rehercier" meaning "to repeat," literally "to harrow again" (as in going over plowed ground again).
