limitation:/ˌlɪmɪˈteɪʃn/| n.|限制；局限性；缺陷；不足|A limitation is a restriction on what can be done, experienced, or known.|The main limitation of this study is the small sample size.  这项研究的主要局限性在于样本量小。|Synonyms: restriction, constraint, limit, curb, handicap|Antonyms: advantage, strength, benefit|noun
