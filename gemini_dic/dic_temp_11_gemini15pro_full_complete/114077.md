magnetosphere:/ˌmæɡˈniːtəʊsfɪər/ | n. | 磁层 | The region surrounding a planet, star, or other celestial body in which its magnetic field dominates the interplanetary medium.  | The Earth's magnetosphere protects us from harmful solar radiation.  地球的磁层保护我们免受有害的太阳辐射。 | synonyms: magnetic field, exosphere | antonyms: N/A | noun

magnetosphere:/ˌmæɡˈniːtəʊsfɪər/ | n. | 磁圈 | The area around the Earth that is affected by the Earth's magnetic field. |  Satellites orbiting within the magnetosphere can be affected by geomagnetic storms. 在磁层内运行的卫星会受到地磁风暴的影响。 | synonyms: magnetic field, exosphere | antonyms: N/A | noun
