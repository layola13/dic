bliss:/blɪs/
n.|极乐；狂喜；天赐之福|A state of perfect happiness, great joy, or spiritual joy.|She closed her eyes and sighed with pure bliss. 她闭上眼睛，纯粹地幸福地叹了口气。|近义词:euphoria, ecstasy, rapture, joy, delight|反义词:misery, despair, sorrow, unhappiness|词性:名词
词源:From Old English *bliþs*, from Proto-Germanic *bliþiz* ("gentle, kind, happy, blissful"). Cognate with Old Saxon *blithi* ("joyful, kind"), Old Norse *bliðr* ("happy, friendly"), Dutch *blij* ("happy"), German *blide* ("cheerful, serene").
