ecorche:/eɪˈkɔːrʃeɪ/
n.
剥皮模型;  肌肉解剖模型;
A figure drawn, sculpted, or molded showing the muscles of the body without the skin.
Medical students study écorchés to learn about human anatomy.  医学生学习剥皮模型来了解人体解剖学。

The artist used an écorché to study the human form.  艺术家用一个肌肉解剖模型来研究人体形态。
近义词:anatomical model, flayed figure
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "intact figure")
词性: noun


ecorche:/eɪˈkɔːrʃeɪ/
v.
剥皮;
To flay or remove the skin from a body, typically for anatomical study.
The professor écorchéd the cadaver to demonstrate the muscular system. 教授剥下了尸体的皮来演示肌肉系统。

The artist écorchéd the rabbit to create a study of its musculature. 艺术家剥了兔子的皮来研究它的肌肉组织。
近义词:flay, skin
反义词:cover
词性: verb
