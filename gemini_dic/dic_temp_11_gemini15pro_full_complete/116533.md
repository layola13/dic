mathematicians:/ˌmæθəməˈtɪʃənz/
|n.| 数学家 |A person who is skilled in mathematics, especially one who does mathematical research.| Many famous mathematicians have contributed to the development of calculus. 许多著名的数学家为微积分的发展做出了贡献。| synonyms: experts in mathematics, number crunchers | antonyms: mathematical illiterates
|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: *mathematicion*, from Old French *mathematicien*, from Latin *mathematicus* + *-ian*. Ultimately from Greek *mathēmatikós* ‘mathematical’, from *máthēma* ‘science, learning’.
