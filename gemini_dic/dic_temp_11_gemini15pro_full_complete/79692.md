glowing:/ˈɡloʊɪŋ/| adj.|发光的，容光焕发的，热烈的，赞不绝口的| emitting light and heat, or reflecting light; showing or expressing great praise; having a healthy warm colour, especially in the face|例句：The glowing embers of the fire cast flickering shadows on the walls. 火焰中发光的余烬在墙上投下摇曳的影子。|近义词：bright, radiant, luminous, shining, incandescent, enthusiastic, complimentary|反义词：dull, dim, dark, faint, lackluster, critical|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *glowen*, from Old English *glōwan* (“to glow”), from Proto-Germanic *glōaną* (“to glow, shine”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵlew- (“to glow, shine”). Cognate with West Frisian *gloeije* (“to glow”), Dutch *gloeien* (“to glow”), German *glühen* (“to glow”), Danish *gløde* (“to glow”), Swedish *glöda* (“to glow”).
