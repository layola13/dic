aqueduct:/ˈækwɪdʌkt/ | n. |渡槽；导水管 | A structure for carrying water, typically in the form of a bridge supported by tall columns across a valley or over low ground. | The Roman aqueduct brought fresh water to the city.  罗马渡槽将淡水引入城市。| synonyms: conduit, channel, watercourse | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical structure. Could consider "dam" as it stops water flow, but it's not a true opposite.) | noun
