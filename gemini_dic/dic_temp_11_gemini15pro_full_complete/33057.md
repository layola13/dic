chowder:/ˈtʃaʊdər/| n. |浓汤，杂烩浓汤|A thick soup or stew made with milk or cream, often containing seafood or vegetables.|例句：We had a delicious clam chowder for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了美味的蛤蜊浓汤。

例句：She made a hearty corn chowder. 她做了一份丰盛的玉米浓汤。|近义词：soup, stew|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑thin broth 清汤)|词性:noun
