gents:/dʒɛnts/ | n. |先生们；各位 | A contraction of "gentlemen," used as a polite way to address a group of men. | Example: "Good evening, gents, welcome to the club." 晚上好，先生们，欢迎来到俱乐部。| Synonym: gentlemen, men, fellows | Antonym: ladies, women | noun
