bladdery:/ˈblædəri/| adj.|似膀胱的；囊状的；膨胀的|Resembling a bladder; thin-walled and inflated.  Characterized by or full of bladders.|The seaweed had a bladdery appearance.  这种海藻看起来像膀胱一样。|近义词：inflated, swollen, distended|反义词：deflated, shrunken, collapsed|词性:形容词
Etymology: From bladder + -y (suffix forming adjectives meaning 'characterized by' or 'full of').  "Bladder" itself comes from Old English "blædre," ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*bladro-," meaning "bladder, blister."
