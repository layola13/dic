behoving:/bɪˈhoʊvɪŋ/
adj.
适当的，合适的，相称的
Suitable or appropriate; incumbent (on or upon)
It is behoving upon all of us to help those in need.  我们所有人都应该帮助那些需要帮助的人。
Synonyms: appropriate, fitting, proper, suitable, seemly, apt, right, correct, decent, decorous
Antonyms: inappropriate, unfitting, improper, unsuitable, unseemly, inapposite, wrong, incorrect, indecent, indecorous
词性: 形容词
