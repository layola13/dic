meshy:/ˈmɛʃi/| adj.|网状的，网状结构的|Resembling a mesh; net-like; interconnected.  Made of or resembling meshes.|The fisherman cast his meshy net into the sea. 渔夫把他的网状渔网撒入大海。|近义词：netlike, reticulated, interwoven, lattice-like|反义词:solid, compact|词性:形容词
Etymology: From mesh + -y (suffix forming adjectives).  "Mesh" itself comes from Old English *masc, related to Dutch maesche and German Masche.
