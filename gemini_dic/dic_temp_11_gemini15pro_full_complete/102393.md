jabbing:/ˈdʒæbɪŋ/
verb
刺,戳,捅
pushing something sharp and pointy into something else quickly and repeatedly.
The nurse was jabbing the needle into my arm.  护士正把针扎进我的胳膊。
He kept jabbing his finger at me accusingly.  他不停地用手指着我，指责我。
Synonyms: poking, prodding, thrusting
Antonyms: stroking, caressing
v.

jabbing:/ˈdʒæbɪŋ/
adjective
刺戳的，（言辞）尖刻的
characterized by or involving repeated sharp, quick pushes or thrusts.
His jabbing remarks hurt her feelings. 他尖刻的言辞伤害了她的感情。
The jabbing pain in my side was unbearable. 我侧腹一阵阵的刺痛难以忍受。
Synonyms: piercing, sharp, pointed (for remarks)
Antonyms: soothing, gentle, kind (for remarks)
adj.
