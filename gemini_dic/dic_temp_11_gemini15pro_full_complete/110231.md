lifelessly:/ˈlaɪflɪsli/
adverb
没有生气地，死气沉沉地
in a way that shows no sign of life; dully

近义词:inanimately, dully, spiritlessly, apathetically

lifeless:/ˈlaɪflɪs/
adjective
没有生命的，无生气的
not having life
The landscape appeared barren and lifeless.  这片土地显得贫瘠而毫无生气。
近义词: inanimate, dead, inert
反义词: living, animate, vibrant
词性:形容词

life:/laɪf/
noun
生命，生活
the existence of an individual human being or animal
Human life is precious.  人的生命是宝贵的。
近义词: existence, being, survival
反义词: death, mortality
词性:名词

词源:
From Old English *līflēas "lifeless," from līf "life" + -lēas "-less."  The adverb lifelessly is formed by adding the suffix -ly to the adjective lifeless.
