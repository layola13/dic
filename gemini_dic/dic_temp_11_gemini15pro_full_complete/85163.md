hardwired:/ˌhɑːrdˈwaɪərd/ | adj. |  本能的；固有的；硬连线的 | Genetically or innately determined; built-in.  Also, connected or incorporated directly into hardware. | Humans are hardwired to seek connection. 人类天生就渴望与他人建立联系。 | ingrained, innate, inherent, built-in | learned, acquired | adjective

hardwired:/ˌhɑːrdˈwaɪərd/ | v. |  硬连线 | To connect or incorporate directly into hardware. | The computer's network card is hardwired into the motherboard. 这台电脑的网卡直接连接到主板上。 | connect directly, integrate, build in | disconnect, remove | verb

PHRASE->
hardwired into something:  根植于某物;  Fear of the dark is often hardwired into humans. 对黑暗的恐惧通常根植于人类内心。
hardwired to do something: 天生要做某事; Babies are hardwired to cry when they are hungry. 婴儿天生在饿的时候会哭。
hardwired for something: 为某事而生;  Some birds are hardwired for migration. 一些鸟类天生就会迁徙。
hardwired response: 本能反应; The flinch is a hardwired response to danger.  畏缩是一种对危险的本能反应。
hardwired behavior:  本能行为;  Mating rituals are often hardwired behaviors.  交配仪式通常是本能行为。

词源：hard (坚硬的) + wire (电线) + -ed (形容词后缀)，最初指电子元件通过电线直接连接，后来引申为“固有的”、“本能的”。
