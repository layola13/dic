kinsmanly:/ˈkɪnzmənli/| adj.|亲属般的；亲切的；慷慨的|Like a kinsman; befitting or characteristic of a kinsman; kind, friendly, and generous.|He greeted me with a kinsmanly embrace. 他以亲人般的拥抱迎接我。|近义词：brotherly, fraternal, friendly, amiable, generous|反义词：unfriendly, hostile, unkind, ungenerous|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *kinsmanlich*, equivalent to kinsman +‎ -ly.
