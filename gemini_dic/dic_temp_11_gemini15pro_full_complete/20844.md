blastophoric:/ˌblæstəˈfɔːrɪk/ | adj. |胚孔的，与胚孔有关的 | Relating to the blastopore, the opening of the archenteron in the gastrula. | The blastophoric lip forms the margin of the blastopore. 胚孔唇形成胚孔的边缘。|  The blastophoric canal is crucial for gastrulation. 胚孔管对原肠胚形成至关重要。| 近义词: archenteric (related to the archenteron, which is formed by the blastopore) | 反义词:  None (This is a specialized term, and a direct antonym doesn't exist.) | 词性: adjective


Etymology:
From blastopore + -ic. Blastopore comes from the Greek "blastos" (bud, germ) + "poros" (passage, pore).
