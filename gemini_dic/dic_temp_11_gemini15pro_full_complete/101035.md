invectively:/ɪnˈvɛktɪvli/
adverb
辱骂地，恶言相向地
in a manner characterized by or using invective; abusively.
He spoke invectively about his political opponents. 他用恶毒的语言谈论他的政敌。
Synonyms: abusively, vituperatively, offensively, scurrilously, malignantly, slanderously, defamatively
Antonyms: politely, respectfully, kindly, civilly, courteously
詞性: adverb
