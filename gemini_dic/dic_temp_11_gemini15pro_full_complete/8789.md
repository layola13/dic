antiegoists:/ˈæntiˈiːɡəʊɪsts/
|n.|反利己主义者|People who oppose or reject egoism, prioritizing the needs of others over their own. They believe in altruism and often act for the benefit of the community or society.|Anti-egoists believe that selflessness is the key to a better society. 反利己主义者认为无私是通往更美好社会的关键。|
|近义词:|altruists, philanthropists|
|反义词:|egoists, narcissists|
|词性:|noun|
