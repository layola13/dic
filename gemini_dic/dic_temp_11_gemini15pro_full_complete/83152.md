guttatrap:/ˈɡʌtəˌtræp/ |n.|滴水器；滴液器；滴水槽|A device designed to collect drops of liquid, often used in scientific experiments or in plumbing. |The chemist used a guttatrap to collect the condensate from the distillation apparatus. 化学家用滴水器收集蒸馏装置中的冷凝物。

The guttatrap prevented water from dripping onto the floor. 滴水槽防止水滴到地板上。|Synonyms: drip catcher, drip tray, drip pan|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
