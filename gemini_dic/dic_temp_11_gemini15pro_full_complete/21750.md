词源：
"boater" (hat) 源自19世纪末，最初指划船时佩戴的硬草帽，后来演变为泛指这种款式的帽子。"boater" (person) simply derives from "boat" + "-er," indicating someone who engages in boating.  "boat" itself has a long history, tracing back to Proto-Germanic and ultimately Proto-Indo-European roots.
