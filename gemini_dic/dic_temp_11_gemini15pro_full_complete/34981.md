cloggier:/ˈklɑːɡiər (US), ˈklɒɡɪər (UK)| adj.|更阻塞的，更堵塞的|Comparative form of "clogged," meaning more blocked or obstructed.|The drain is even cloggier now than it was yesterday.  下水道现在比昨天更堵了。|The traffic is cloggier during rush hour.  高峰时段交通更拥堵。|近义词：more blocked, more obstructed, more congested|反义词：clearer, less obstructed, freer|词性:形容词

clogged:/klɑːɡd (US), klɒɡd (UK)| adj.|阻塞的，堵塞的|Blocked or filled with something that prevents passage.|The drain is clogged with hair. 下水道被头发堵住了。|The arteries can become clogged with cholesterol. 动脉会被胆固醇堵塞。|近义词：blocked, obstructed, congested|反义词：clear, unobstructed, free|词性:形容词

clog:/klɑːɡ (US), klɒɡ (UK)| n. v.|木屐；障碍物；堵塞|n. A type of shoe; something that obstructs; v. To block or obstruct.|She wore clogs in the garden. 她在花园里穿木屐。|Hair can clog the drain. 头发会堵塞下水道。|近义词：(n.) shoe, obstruction, blockage; (v.) block, obstruct|反义词：(v.) clear, unblock|词性:名词, 动词


词源: From Middle English *cloggen, of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to Old Norse klökkr ("lump, clod").
