nenuphar:/ˈn(j)uːfɑːr/ (US), /ˈnjuːfɑː(r)/ (UK)| n. |睡莲，荷花|A water lily, especially a yellow or white one.|例句：The nenuphar floated serenely on the pond's surface. 睡莲平静地漂浮在池塘的水面上。

例句：The artist captured the beauty of the nenuphar in his painting.  艺术家在他的画中捕捉到了睡莲的美丽。|近义词：water lily, pond lily|反义词: (No direct antonym for a plant)|词性:noun

Etymology:
From French *nénufar*, from Arabic *nīnūfar*  نینوفر , from Persian *nīlūfar* نیلوفر, meaning "blue lotus," ultimately from Sanskrit *nīravapus* नीरवपुस्  "having a beautiful form in the water."
