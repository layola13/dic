dispatch:/dɪˈspætʃ/ /dɪˈspæʧ/| v. n.|派遣，发送；迅速处理；（军事）派遣；（消息）急件，快信|To send something or someone somewhere for a particular purpose.  To deal with a task, problem, etc., quickly and efficiently. A message sent with speed. |The company dispatched a technician to fix the problem. 公司派了一名技术员去解决这个问题。

The matter was dispatched quickly and efficiently. 这件事很快有效地处理了。

The dispatch confirmed the arrival of the troops.  急件确认了部队的到来。|近义词：send, transmit, deal with, handle, expedite|反义词：delay, detain, hold, retain|词性：动词，名词

Etymology:
From Old French despescher, from des- (expressing removal) + pescher "to fish out, pull out" (source also of Spanish despachar "to expedite, dispatch"). The original notion was "to rid oneself of."
