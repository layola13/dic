monga:/ˈmɒŋɡə/| n.|蒙加，一种由营养不良引起的疾病|A disease caused by severe malnutrition, especially in infants and young children, characterized by edema, growth retardation, and apathy.  It is often associated with famine and other conditions of extreme poverty.|The severe drought led to widespread monga in the region. 严重干旱导致该地区普遍出现蒙加。|近义词：malnutrition, starvation, famine|反义词: nourishment, satiety|noun
