anchoring:/ˈæŋkərɪŋ/| n.|锚定; 抛锚; 固着; (新闻节目)主播;|The anchoring of the ship was delayed by the storm.  船的抛锚因暴风雨而延误。|近义词：mooring, fixing, securing, grounding|反义词：releasing, detaching|noun

anchoring:/ˈæŋkərɪŋ/| adj.|起固定作用的|The anchoring ropes held the ship steady.  这些固定用的绳索使船保持稳定。|近义词：fixing, stabilizing|反义词：loosening|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *ankeren*, from Old English * ancor*, from Latin *ancora*, from Ancient Greek *ἄγκυρα (ánkura)*.
