monocotyledon: /ˌmɒnəʊˌkɒtɪˈliːdən/ (UK) /ˌmɑːnəˌkɑːtɪˈliːdən/ (US)| n. |单子叶植物|A flowering plant with an embryo that bears a single cotyledon (seed leaf). Monocotyledons constitute the smaller of the two great divisions of flowering plants, characterized by parallel-veined leaves, fibrous root systems, and flowers with parts in multiples of three.|例句：Corn is a monocotyledon. 玉米是单子叶植物。

例句：Monocotyledons typically have fibrous root systems. 单子叶植物通常有须根系。|近义词：monocot|反义词：dicotyledon, dicot|词性：名词
