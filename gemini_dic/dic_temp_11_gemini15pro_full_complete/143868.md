outpoint:/ˈaʊtpɔɪnt/| n.| 支出点，输出点，未花费的交易输出|A specific unspent transaction output (UTXO) that can be used as an input for a new transaction on a blockchain.  It serves as proof of ownership and allows the transfer of cryptocurrency.|The outpoint referenced in the transaction was already spent. 这笔交易中引用的支出点已经被花费了。|Synonyms: UTXO, transaction output|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "spent transaction output")|noun

outpoint:/ˈaʊtpɔɪnt/| n.| 起点，开端|The starting point of a journey or process.|The outpoint of their adventure was a small town in the mountains.  他们冒险的起点是山区的一个小镇。|Synonyms: starting point, origin, beginning|Antonyms: endpoint, destination, conclusion|noun
