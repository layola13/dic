methanol:/ˈmɛθənɒl/ (UK) /ˈmɛθənɔːl/ (US)| n.|甲醇|A colorless, volatile, flammable, poisonous liquid alcohol, CH3OH, formed by the destructive distillation of wood or by the catalytic combination of carbon monoxide and hydrogen under pressure. It is used as an antifreeze, a solvent, a fuel, and a denaturant for ethyl alcohol.| Methanol is commonly used as a solvent in laboratories.|近义词：methyl alcohol, wood alcohol|反义词: N/A|noun
