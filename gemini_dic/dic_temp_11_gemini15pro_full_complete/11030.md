arcadianism:/ɑːrˈkeɪdiənɪzəm/
n.
田园主义；田园风格；田园诗般的境界
A literary style that idealizes rural life and nature, often depicting shepherds and other rural figures in a romanticized and idyllic setting.  It evokes a sense of simplicity, peace, and harmony with nature.
The poem is full of arcadianism, portraying a utopian vision of rural life.  这首诗充满了田园主义，描绘了乡村生活的乌托邦式景象。

The novel's arcadianism offered a welcome escape from the harsh realities of city life. 这部小说的田园主义为人们提供了一个逃离城市生活残酷现实的受欢迎的出口。

近义词:pastoralism, bucolicism, idyll, rusticity
反义词:modernism, urbanism, sophistication
