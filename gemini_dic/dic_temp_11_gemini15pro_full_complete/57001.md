double-glazed:/ˌdʌbəl ˈɡleɪzd/| adj.|双层玻璃的，双层玻璃窗的|Having two layers of glass separated by a space, typically to reduce heat loss or noise.|例句：We installed double-glazed windows to keep the house warmer. 我们安装了双层玻璃窗以保持房子温暖。

例句：Double-glazed windows are much more energy-efficient. 双层玻璃窗更节能。|近义词：insulated, thermal|反义词：single-glazed|词性: 形容词
