bodice-ripping: /ˈbɒdɪs ˌrɪpɪŋ/
adjective
撕衣裂服的；激情澎湃的；浪漫刺激的
referring to a type of romantic fiction characterized by melodramatic, often sensational, plots involving passionate love affairs, often with a historical setting and featuring scenes of a sexual nature, though typically stopping short of explicit description.  The term derives from the imagined scenario of a passionate lover tearing the bodice of a woman's dress in a moment of intense desire.
She enjoys reading bodice-ripping romances. 她喜欢读激情浪漫小说。
Synonyms: passionate, melodramatic, sensational, romantic, steamy
Antonyms: tame, subdued, realistic, platonic, unemotional
词性: adjective
