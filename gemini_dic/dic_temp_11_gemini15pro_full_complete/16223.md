banquet: /ˈbæŋkwɪt/ (US) /ˈbæŋkwɪt/ (UK)| n.| 宴会，盛宴| A large, elaborate, and formal meal for many people, often held to celebrate a special occasion. | The company held a banquet to celebrate its 20th anniversary. 公司举行了宴会庆祝其成立 20 周年。|We attended a lavish banquet at the castle. 我们在城堡里参加了一个奢华的宴会。|近义词：feast, dinner, celebration|反义词: snack, fast|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "banquet" (a small bench, a trestle table), diminutive of "banc" (bench), from Old Italian "banco" (bench, counter), from a Germanic source, from Proto-Germanic "*bankiz" (bench, mound).  The meaning shifted from "bench" to "meal eaten on benches" and finally to "feast."
