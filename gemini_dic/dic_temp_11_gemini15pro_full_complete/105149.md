keto:/ˈkiːtoʊ/ (US), /ˈkiːtəʊ/ (UK)| adj. n.|生酮的，酮体的；酮|Relating to or characterized by the production of ketone bodies, a normal metabolic process that becomes more pronounced when the body burns fat for energy because of restricted carbohydrate intake.  As a noun, it can refer to a ketogenic diet.|

例句：
She's following a keto diet to lose weight. 她正在遵循生酮饮食来减肥。

She's eating keto-friendly foods. 她正在吃生酮友好的食物。


近义词：ketogenic|
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps high-carb)|
词性: adjective, noun
