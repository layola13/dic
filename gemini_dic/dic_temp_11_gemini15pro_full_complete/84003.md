half:/hæf/ | n. adj. adv. | 一半，半个 | One of two equal parts of a whole. | I ate half the pizza. 我吃了一半的披萨。|  She gave half of her earnings to charity. 她把一半的收入捐给了慈善机构。| Near synonyms: moiety, one-half | Near antonyms: whole, double | noun/adjective/adverb
