farcicalnesses:/fɑːrˈsɪkəlnəsɪz/
n.
滑稽可笑；荒谬可笑
the quality or state of being farcical
The farcicalnesses of the situation was apparent to everyone.  这种情况的荒谬性对每个人来说都是显而易见的。
近义词: absurdity, ridiculousness, ludicrousness
反义词: seriousness, solemnity, gravity
词性: 名词

词源:
From farcical + -ness.  Farcical comes from farce (meaning a light dramatic work in which highly improbable situations, stereotyped characters, extravagant exaggeration, and violent horseplay are used for humorous effect) + -al. Ultimately from Late Latin *farscire "to stuff."
