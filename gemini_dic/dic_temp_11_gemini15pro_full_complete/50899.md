desertless:/ˈdɛzətlɪs/
adjective
无沙漠的
lacking deserts; having no desert areas
The planet Kepler-186f is hypothesized to be desertless due to its proximity to its star.  开普勒-186f行星因靠近其恒星，被假设为无沙漠的。
The small, tropical island nation was remarkably desertless.  这个热带小岛国竟然没有沙漠，非常引人注目。
近义词: arid-free, non-desert
反义词: desert, arid
