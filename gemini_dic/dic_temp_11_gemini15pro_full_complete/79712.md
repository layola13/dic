glucosic:/ɡluːˈkoʊsɪk/ /ɡluːˈkɒsɪk/
adjective
葡萄糖的；似葡萄糖的
Relating to or resembling glucose.
This compound has glucosic properties.  这种化合物具有葡萄糖的特性。
The lab synthesized a glucosic substance.  实验室合成了一种类似葡萄糖的物质。
近义词: glucose-like, saccharine
反义词: None (lack of a direct antonym)
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From glucose + -ic.  Glucose ultimately derives from Greek *gleûkos "sweet wine, must," from *dlku- "sweet" (source also of Sanskrit duhkha "unpleasant, painful," originally "producing bad sap," Avestan duž- "evil, bad," perhaps also Greek glykys "sweet").
