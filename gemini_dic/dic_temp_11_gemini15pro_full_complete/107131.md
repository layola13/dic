ladyishness:/ˈleɪdiɪʃnɪs/
noun
淑女气质；女子气；女人气
The quality of being ladylike; feminine refinement and decorum.
She exuded an air of ladyishness that charmed everyone in the room. 她散发着一种淑女气质，迷住了房间里的每个人。
Her ladyishness was evident in her elegant dress and graceful manners. 她的淑女气质体现在她优雅的着装和优雅的举止上。
Synonyms: femininity, grace, elegance, refinement, daintiness, demureness
Antonyms: masculinity, coarseness, vulgarity, unladylikeness


ETYMOLOGY->
From lady + -ish + -ness.  Lady comes from Old English *hlǣfdīge  (loaf-kneader). -ish is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "somewhat like," "of the nature of," "belonging to." -ness is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, meaning "state or quality of being."
