dhoti: /ˈdoʊti/ (US), /ˈdəʊti/ (UK)| n. |缠腰布，兜裆布|A single piece of cloth worn wrapped around the waist and legs, traditionally by men in India. | He wore a crisp white dhoti. 他穿着一件挺括的白色缠腰布。

The groom looked resplendent in his silk dhoti.  新郎穿着丝绸缠腰布，看起来光彩照人。

|近义词:lungi, mundu|反义词:trousers, pants|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi धोती (dhotī), from Sanskrit धौती (dhautī), feminine past participle of धावति (dhāvati, “to wash, cleanse”).  The garment is so called because it is traditionally washed and purified before being worn.
