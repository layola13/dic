angularnesses:/ˌæŋɡjʊˈlærnəsɪz/| n. |棱角，尖锐，生硬|The angularnesses of the modern building contrasted sharply with the curves of the older structures.  现代建筑的棱角与旧建筑的曲线形成鲜明对比。|例句：The painting captured the angularnesses of the model's face. 这幅画捕捉到了模特脸部的棱角。

The angularnesses of his personality made him difficult to get along with. 他性格的尖锐让他很难相处。|近义词：sharpness, pointedness, gauntness|反义词:roundness, smoothness, softness|词性:名词

词源:  From "angular" + "-ness."  "Angular" comes from Late Latin "angularis,"  meaning "having an angle," from Latin "angulus," meaning "corner" or "angle."  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, denoting a state or quality.
