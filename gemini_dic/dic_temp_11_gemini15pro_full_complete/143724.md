outlive:/ˌaʊtˈlɪv/| v. | 比...活得长；比...持久；渡过...而存在 | to live or exist longer than someone or something | My grandmother outlived her husband by ten years. 我祖母比她丈夫多活了十年。| synonyms: survive, outlast | antonyms: predecease | verb

outlive:/ˌaʊtˈlɪv/| v. | 幸免于；从...中逃生 | to live through something, surviving its dangers or threats |  The company outlived the recession. 这家公司渡过了经济衰退。| synonyms: endure, withstand | antonyms: succumb | verb
