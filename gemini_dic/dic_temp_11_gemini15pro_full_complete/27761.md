cannel:/ˈkænəl/| n.|煤炭的一种；烛煤；细沟渠|A hard, compact variety of coal that burns with a bright flame and little smoke, formerly used for making gas and candles.  一种质地坚硬、结构紧密的煤，燃烧时火焰明亮，几乎没有烟，以前用于制造煤气和蜡烛。|The cannel coal burned brightly in the fireplace. 烛煤在壁炉里燃烧得很旺。|近义词：coal, boghead coal|反义词:|名词
