astrictive:/əˈstrɪktɪv/
adjective
收敛性的；止泻的
Causing contraction, especially of organic tissues;styptic.  Tending to reduce or restrict.
例句：Astringent lotions can help to tighten pores.  收敛性乳液有助于收缩毛孔。

例句：The government imposed astringent economic policies. 政府实施了紧缩的经济政策。
synonym:styptic, binding, contracting, constricting
antonym:relaxing, dilating, loosening
词性:adjective
