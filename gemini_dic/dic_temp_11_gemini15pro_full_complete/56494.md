domesday: /ˈduːmzdeɪ/ | n. | 末日审判, 世界末日 | A day of reckoning or final judgment. domesday: /ˈduːmzdeɪ/ | adj. | 最后的，决定性的 |  Relating to the Domesday Book or the day of judgment.  Figuratively, it can describe something momentous or final. | Example: The company faced a domesday scenario when the market crashed. 当市场崩盘时，该公司面临着世界末日的局面。| Synonym: final, decisive, fateful, critical | Antonym:  insignificant, trivial | adjective
