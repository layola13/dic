overhaughtily:/ˌoʊvərˈhɔːtɪli/| adv.|傲慢地，盛气凌人地|In an extremely haughty manner; with excessive arrogance and disdain.  It combines "over-" (excessively) with "haughtily" (in a haughty manner).|She dismissed his suggestion overhaughtily, barely glancing in his direction.|近义词：arrogantly, disdainfully, superciliously, contemptuously|反义词:humbly, modestly, respectfully|词性:副词
