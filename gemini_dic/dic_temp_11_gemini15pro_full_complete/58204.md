drudgers:/ˈdrʌdʒərz/| n. |苦工；做繁重工作的人|People who perform tedious and laborious work. |They were treated like drudgers, working long hours for little pay. 他们被当作苦力，长时间工作却报酬微薄.|Synonyms: toilers, laborers, workers, grindstones |Antonyms: idlers, loafers, slackers| noun
