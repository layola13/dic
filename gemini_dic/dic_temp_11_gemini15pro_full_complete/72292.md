flounce:/flaʊns/| n. vi.| 猛然行动，装饰荷叶边，(衣裙等的)荷叶边，波浪形褶边|A sudden, impatient movement, typically intended to express annoyance or disapproval; a wide ornamental strip of gathered or pleated material attached to a garment, typically around the hemline.|She flounced out of the room in a huff. 她怒气冲冲地走出了房间。|近义词：toss, swagger, ruffle, frill|反义词：smooth, flatten|词性：名词，动词

flounces:/ˈflaʊnsɪz/| n. v.|猛然行动(复数)，装饰荷叶边(复数)，(衣裙等的)荷叶边(复数)，波浪形褶边(复数)|Plural form of "flounce".
