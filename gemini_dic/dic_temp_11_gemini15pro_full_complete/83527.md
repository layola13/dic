hadeses:/ˈheɪdiːzɪz/| n. |冥界（复数）|Plural form of Hades, referring to multiple underworlds or instances of the underworld.  It is rarely used. |The poet imagined multiple hadeses, each ruled by a different god. 诗人想象了多个冥界，每个冥界都由不同的神统治。|例句：In his complex mythology, the hero journeyed through several hadeses. 在他复杂的神话中，英雄穿越了几个冥界。|近义词：underworlds, netherworlds|反义词: heavens, paradises|词性:noun
