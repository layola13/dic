gumless:/ˈɡʌmlɪs/
adjective
无齿龈的；没有牙龈的
lacking gums; having no gums
The poor old dog was gumless and couldn't eat hard food.  这只可怜的老狗没有牙龈，不能吃硬食。
Synonyms: toothless
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider "healthy gums")
词性: 形容词
