clumped:/klʌmpt/
adjective
成群的，簇状的
gathered together in a clump or lumps; clustered.
The trees were clumped together at the edge of the forest.  树木成群地聚集在森林边缘。
The bacteria were clumped together under the microscope. 在显微镜下，细菌聚集在一起。
近义词：clustered, grouped, bunched
反义词：scattered, dispersed, spread out
词性: adjective

clumped:/klʌmpt/
verb
丛生，成群
to form or cause to form a clump or clumps.
The bushes clumped together along the fence. 灌木丛沿着篱笆丛生。
The snow clumped on his boots. 雪在他的靴子上结成块。
近义词：cluster, bunch, gather
反义词：separate, disperse, scatter
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From clump + -ed.  Clump itself likely originates from a Scandinavian source, akin to Norwegian klump meaning "lump, mass."
