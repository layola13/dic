eradicant:/ɪˈrædɪkənt/
n.|除草剂；根除剂|A substance used to eradicate something, especially a plant disease.|The gardener used a powerful eradicant to get rid of the weeds. 园丁用了一种强效除草剂来清除杂草。|近义词: herbicide, pesticide, fungicide|反义词: fertilizer|词性:名词

eradicant:/ɪˈrædɪkənt/
adj.|根除的；消除的|Having the power to eradicate.|The eradicant treatment proved effective in eliminating the infection. 根除性治疗被证明对消除感染有效。|近义词: destructive, eliminating, exterminating|反义词: constructive, generative|词性:形容词
