levulosan: /ˌlɛvjuˈloʊsæn/ | n. | 左旋葡聚糖 | An anhydride of levoglucose formed by heating starch or cellulose. It's a type of carbohydrate derived from the pyrolysis of carbohydrates like starch and cellulose. |  Levoglucosan is a marker for biomass burning in atmospheric aerosols.  左旋葡聚糖是大气气溶胶中生物质燃烧的标志物。

Levulosan can be used to produce biofuels. 左旋葡聚糖可以用来生产生物燃料。 | 近义词:  1,6-anhydro-β-D-glucopyranose (its chemical name) | 反义词:  None | 词性: noun


词源: From levo- (left) + glucose + -an (indicating a carbohydrate).
