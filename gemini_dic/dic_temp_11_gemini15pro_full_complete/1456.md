acephalist: /eɪˈsɛfəlɪst/ /əˈsɛfəlɪst/
n.
无头人；无领导者；无政府主义者
A person who advocates or believes in acephaly; a person without a head (literally or figuratively).
The historian described the revolutionary group as a collection of acephalists, lacking any central leadership.  历史学家将这个革命团体描述为一群没有中央领导的无政府主义者。
The mythical creature was depicted as an acephalist, its face located on its chest.  这个神话生物被描绘成一个无头人，它的脸位于胸部。
近义词: anarchist, leaderless
反义词: leader, head, chief
词性: noun


acephalous: /eɪˈsɛfələs/ /əˈsɛfələs/
adj.
无头的；无领导的；无政府状态的
Lacking a head or leader; headless.
The acephalous mob rioted through the streets, lacking any clear direction.  无头的暴民在街上暴乱，缺乏明确的方向。
The organization became acephalous after the resignation of its director.  在主管辞职后，该组织变得群龙无首。
近义词: headless, leaderless, disorganized
反义词: led, organized
词性: adjective


词源: From Greek *akephalos*  "headless," from *a-* "without" + *kephalē* "head."
