foregoer:/fɔːrˈɡoʊər/
n.
先行者，前辈
someone or something that existed or happened before another
The Wright brothers were the foregoers of modern aviation.  莱特兄弟是现代航空的先驱。
predecessor, ancestor, precursor
successor, descendant
noun
