browbeat:/ˈbraʊbiːt/| vt.|恐吓，威胁，威逼|To intimidate or discourage by stern, overbearing, or domineering looks or words.|He browbeat his employees into working overtime. 他恐吓他的员工加班。|近义词：intimidate, bully, coerce, threaten|反义词：cajole, coax, encourage|动词

browbeat:/ˈbraʊbiːt/| vt.|威吓，吓唬|To frighten or subdue by a stern manner or arrogant speech.|The lawyer browbeat the witness into changing his testimony. 律师威吓证人改变证词。|近义词：cow, hector, bluster|反义词：persuade, reason with|动词


ETYMOLOGY->

From brow + beat. Originally meant "to strike on the brow," later evolving to its current meaning of intimidating with words or looks.  源自 brow（额头）+ beat（打）。最初意思是“打在额头上”，后来演变成现在的意思，即用言语或眼神恐吓。
