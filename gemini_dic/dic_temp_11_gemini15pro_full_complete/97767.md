inject:/ɪnˈdʒɛkt/ | v. |注射，注入，引入|To introduce a substance into the body, typically by using a syringe and needle; to introduce something new, such as a quality or element, into something else. | The doctor injected the patient with a painkiller. 医生给病人注射了止痛药。

The government is trying to inject more money into the economy. 政府正试图向经济注入更多资金。|近义词：insert, introduce, administer, infuse |反义词：extract, withdraw, remove |词性：动词

Etymology: From Latin injectus, past participle of injicere "to throw in," from in- "in" + jacere "to throw."
