anaerobic:/ˌænəˈrəʊbɪk/ (UK) /ˌænəˈroʊbɪk/ (US)| adj.| 厌氧的，无氧的|Living or taking place in the absence of free oxygen; relating to or involving organisms that can live without oxygen.|Anaerobic bacteria thrive in environments without oxygen. 厌氧细菌在无氧环境中繁殖。|Synonyms: anoxic, airless|Antonyms: aerobic, oxygenated|adjective

anaerobe:/ˈænəˌroʊb/ (US)  /ˈænəˌrəʊb/ (UK) | n. |厌氧生物|An organism, especially a bacterium, that does not require free oxygen to live.|Certain anaerobes can cause serious infections.  某些厌氧菌会导致严重的感染。|Synonyms: anaerobiont|Antonyms: aerobe|noun
