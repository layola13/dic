acquirers: /əˈkwaɪrərz/
n.
收购者，获得者
People or companies that acquire something, especially businesses.
Many potential acquirers are interested in the company. 许多潜在的收购者对这家公司感兴趣。
The acquirers quickly integrated the new company into their existing operations. 收购者迅速将新公司整合到他们现有的业务中。

Synonyms: buyers, purchasers, investors
Antonyms: sellers, vendors

词性: noun (plural)
