nonpriority:/ˌnɒnpraɪˈɒrɪti/ | adj. | 非优先的，次要的 | Not a priority; not of high importance. |  The nonpriority tasks can be handled later. 次要任务可以稍后再处理。|  The committee decided to defer the nonpriority items until the next meeting. 委员会决定将非优先事项推迟到下次会议。| 近义词: secondary, unimportant, low-priority | 反义词: priority, important, high-priority | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "priority" (from Late Middle English *priorite*, from Old French priorite, from Late Latin prioritas, from prior ‘former, superior’).
