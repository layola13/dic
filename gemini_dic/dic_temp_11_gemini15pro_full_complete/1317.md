accredited:/əˈkrɛdɪtɪd/
adj.
官方认可的，授权的，鉴定合格的
officially recognized or authorized; having been granted official approval or recognition.
The university is accredited by the government. 这所大学是政府认可的。
This course is accredited by a professional body. 这门课程是专业机构认证的。
近义词:certified, licensed, authorized, recognized
反义词:unaccredited, unauthorized, unlicensed
词性:形容词

accredited:/əˈkrɛdɪtɪd/
v.
把…归于，认为…是…的功劳（accrue的过去分词）
past participle of accredit: to attribute or credit something to someone or something.
This invention is accredited to Edison. 这项发明被认为是爱迪生的功劳。
The painting is accredited to Leonardo da Vinci. 这幅画被认为是出自达芬奇之手。
近义词:attributed, ascribed, credited
反义词:N/A
词性:动词(过去分词)

词源: From Late Latin *accreditare, from Latin ad- "to" + creditare "to trust, believe," from creditum, past participle of credere "to believe, trust."
