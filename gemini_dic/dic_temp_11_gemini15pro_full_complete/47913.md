decentralized:/diːˈsɛntrəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 分散的，去中心化的 | Distributed or delegated away from a central authority or location. |  The company decided to adopt a decentralized organizational structure, empowering regional offices.  公司决定采用分散的组织结构，赋予区域办事处权力。

The decentralized nature of the blockchain makes it secure and transparent. 区块链的去中心化特性使其安全透明。 | 近义词：distributed, dispersed, diffused | 反义词：centralized, concentrated | 词性: 形容词
