ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + approachable + -ness + -es (plural).  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," "approachable" comes from the verb "approach," ultimately from Latin "appropiare," meaning "to come near." "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, and "-es" marks the plural form.
