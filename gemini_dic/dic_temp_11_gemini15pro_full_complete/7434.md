anionically:/ˌænɪˈɒnɪkəli/
adv.
阴离子地
Relating to, or occurring by means of anions.
The polymer was synthesized anionically.  这种聚合物是用阴离子合成的。
Near Synonyms: negatively charged
Antonyms: cationically
词性: 副词
