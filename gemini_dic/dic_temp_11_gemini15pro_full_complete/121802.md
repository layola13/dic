misreferred:/ˌmɪsrɪˈfɜːrd/
verb
误诊；错误转诊；错误推荐
incorrectly referred, especially of a patient
The patient was misreferred to a specialist.  这个病人被错误地转诊给了一位专家。

misreferred:/ˌmɪsrɪˈfɜːrd/
adjective
被错误转诊的；被错误推荐的
having been incorrectly referred
The misreferred patient finally found the right doctor.  这位被错误转诊的病人最终找到了合适的医生。
