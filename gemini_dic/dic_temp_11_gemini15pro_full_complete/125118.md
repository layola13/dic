multi-articulate:/ˌmʌlti-ɑːrˈtɪkjʊlət/ | adj. | 善于表达的；口齿伶俐的 |  Able to express oneself clearly and fluently, often with a large vocabulary and complex sentence structures. | The multi-articulate speaker captivated the audience with her eloquent presentation.  这位口齿伶俐的演讲者以其雄辩的演讲吸引了观众。 | Example 2: He was a multi-articulate young man, able to discuss complex topics with ease. 他是一个口齿伶俐的年轻人，能够轻松地讨论复杂的话题。 | synonyms: eloquent, articulate, fluent, expressive | antonyms: inarticulate, tongue-tied, halting | word type: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "multi-" (prefix meaning "many") + "articulate" (from Latin "articulatus," past participle of "articulare" meaning "to joint," from "artus" meaning "joint").
