ETYMOLOGY->
From malt (n.) + -ed.  Malt itself comes from Old English *mealt (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *malt- (source also of Old Norse malt, Old Frisian melt, Dutch mout, Old High German malz, German Malz), from Proto-Indo-European *mel- (source also of Greek mill, Latin mulleus "red mullet," Old Church Slavonic mlěko "milk").
