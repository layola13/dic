outsider:/ˈaʊtsaɪdər/ | n. | 外来人，局外人 |  A person who does not belong to a particular group, organization, or place. |  He felt like an outsider in the close-knit community.  他觉得自己在这个紧密联系的社区里像个局外人。| synonyms: foreigner, stranger, alien, newcomer | antonyms: insider, native, local | noun

outsiders:/ˈaʊtsaɪdərz/ | n. | 外来人（outsider的复数形式） | The plural form of "outsider," referring to multiple people who do not belong to a particular group, organization, or place. | The outsiders were not welcomed by the locals.  当地人不欢迎外来者。| synonyms: foreigners, strangers, aliens, newcomers | antonyms: insiders, natives, locals | noun
