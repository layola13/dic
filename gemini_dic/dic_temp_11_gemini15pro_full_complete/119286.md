micro-electrophoresis:/ˌmaɪkroʊɪˌlɛktroʊfəˈriːsɪs/ | n. |微电泳|The movement of small particles, especially cells, in a fluid under the influence of an electric field.  It's a technique used to study the surface charge of particles.|Micro-electrophoresis was used to study the zeta potential of the colloids.| 近义词: electrophoresis | 反义词: N/A | noun
