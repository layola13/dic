heliacal: /hɪˈlaɪək(ə)l/ /hɪˈlaɪkəl/ | adj. | 太阳的；与太阳同时升落的；日出时的 | Relating to or near the sun; rising and setting with the sun.  Specifically, designating the first time a star or planet becomes visible above the eastern horizon at dawn after a period of invisibility owing to its conjunction with the sun. |  The heliacal rising of Sirius marked the beginning of the Egyptian year.  天狼星的偕日升标志着埃及年的开始。 |  The ancient Egyptians celebrated the heliacal rising of Sothis. 古埃及人庆祝索西斯的偕日升。 | synonymous: solar, astral | antonymous:  None | adjective
