liposuction: /ˈlɪpoʊˌsʌkʃən/ (US), /ˈlaɪpəʊˌsʌkʃən/ (UK)| n. |抽脂术，脂肪抽吸术|The surgical removal of fat beneath the skin with the aid of suction.  It is used to remove unwanted deposits of fat from various parts of the body.|She had liposuction on her thighs. 她做了大腿抽脂手术。|Synonyms: liposculpture, lipectomy|Antonyms: fat grafting|noun
