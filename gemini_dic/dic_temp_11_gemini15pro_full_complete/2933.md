advocates:/ˈædvəkɪts/ | n. | 提倡者，拥护者，辩护律师 | A person who publicly supports or recommends a particular cause or policy. |  Example: She is a strong advocate for animal rights. 她是一位坚定的动物权利倡导者。| Synonyms: supporter, proponent, champion, backer | Antonyms: opponent, critic, adversary | noun

advocates:/ˈædvəkeɪts/ | v. | 提倡，拥护，主张 | Publicly recommend or support. | Example:  He advocates for stricter gun control laws. 他主张更严格的枪支管制法。 | Synonyms: support, promote, champion, espouse | Antonyms: oppose, reject, denounce | verb
