outtalked:/ˌaʊtˈtɔːkt/ | v. | 比…说得更多，说得时间更长，说得更大声|To speak for a longer time or more forcefully than someone else, so that they are unable to contribute to a conversation. | She completely outtalked him in the debate and he barely got a word in edgewise. 她在辩论中完全盖过了他，他几乎一句话也插不进去。 | outspoke, overspoke | - | verb
outtalked:/ˌaʊtˈtɔːkt/ | v. | 说服 |To persuade someone by talking to them. | The salesman outtalked me into buying a car I couldn't really afford. 推销员说服我买了一辆我买不起的车。| persuade, convince | dissuade | verb
