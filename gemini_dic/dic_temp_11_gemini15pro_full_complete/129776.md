non-feasible:/ˌnɒnˈfiːzəbl/ | adj. | 不可行的，办不到的 | Not capable of being done or carried out; not practicable or workable. | The project was deemed non-feasible due to budget constraints.  这个项目由于预算限制被认为是不可行的。| Synonyms: impracticable, unworkable, impossible, unattainable | Antonyms: feasible, practicable, possible, achievable | adjective

non-feasible:/ˌnɒnˈfiːzəbl/ | adj. | 不可行的，办不到的 |  Lacking the necessary resources or conditions to be successful.  |  Building a bridge across the Atlantic is currently non-feasible.  目前在横跨大西洋上建造一座桥梁是不可行的。 | Synonyms: impractical, unrealistic, unviable | Antonyms: feasible, practical, realistic, viable | adjective
