goosed:/ɡuːst/ | v. | 突然加速；惊吓；刺激；| To suddenly increase the speed of something, or to frighten or stimulate someone. |  The driver goosed the engine and the car sped off. 司机猛踩油门，车子飞驰而去。

The sudden noise goosed me. 突然的噪音吓了我一跳。 | 近义词: startled, surprised, accelerated, spurred | 反义词: slowed, calmed, soothed | 词性: 动词

goosed:/ɡuːst/ | adj. | 喝醉的；被吓到的 | Drunk; frightened. | He was completely goosed after drinking a whole bottle of whiskey. 他喝了一整瓶威士忌后醉得一塌糊涂。

I was goosed when I saw the spider. 我看到蜘蛛时吓了一跳。| 近义词: drunk, intoxicated, frightened, scared | 反义词: sober, calm | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From the idea of poking a goose to make it move faster. 源于戳鹅使其快速移动的概念。
