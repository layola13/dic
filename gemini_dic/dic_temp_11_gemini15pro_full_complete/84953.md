haphazard:/ˌhæpˈhæzərd/ | adj. | 偶然的，随意的，杂乱无章的 | Lacking any obvious principle of organization. |  The books were piled on the shelves in a haphazard manner. 书架上的书堆放得杂乱无章。| synonyms: random, arbitrary, unplanned, chaotic, disorganized | antonyms: organized, planned, systematic, methodical | adjective

haphazardly:/ˌhæpˈhæzərdli/ | adv. | 偶然地，随意地，杂乱无章地 | In a random manner; without care. |  The furniture was haphazardly arranged. 家具摆放得杂乱无章。| synonyms: randomly, arbitrarily, carelessly, chaotically | antonyms: methodically, systematically, deliberately | adverb

haphazardness:/ˌhæpˈhæzərdnəs/ | n. | 偶然性，随意性，杂乱无章 | The state or quality of lacking any obvious principle of organization. | The haphazardness of the investigation led to inconclusive results. 调查的随意性导致结果不确定。| synonyms: randomness, arbitrariness, disorder, chaos | antonyms: order, system, method | noun
