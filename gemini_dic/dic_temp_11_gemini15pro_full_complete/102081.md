isopleura:/ˈaɪsəˌplʊərə/
noun
等厚线，等值线
A line on a map connecting points having equal incidence of a given phenomenon (such as temperature, rainfall, or elevation).
Isopleths of temperature are called isotherms.  等温线是温度的等值线。
Synonyms: isarithm, isoline, isogram
Antonyms: N/A
词性:n.

isopleura:/ˈaɪsəˌplʊərə/
noun
(动物学)等壳纲
A former taxonomic class of mollusks comprising the chitons.
Chitons belong to the class Isopleura.  石鳖属于等壳纲。
Synonyms: Polyplacophora
Antonyms: N/A
词性:n.
