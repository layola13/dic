microbiologist:/ˌmaɪkroʊbaɪˈɑːlədʒɪst/ /ˌmaɪkrəʊbaɪˈɒlədʒɪst/
n.|微生物学家|A scientist who studies microorganisms such as bacteria, viruses, algae, fungi, and some types of parasites.|Example: The microbiologist studied the bacteria under a powerful microscope.  微生物学家在高倍显微镜下研究细菌。|Example:  A team of microbiologists are working on developing a new vaccine. 一组微生物学家正在致力于开发一种新疫苗。|Synonyms: bacteriologist, virologist (when specializing)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
