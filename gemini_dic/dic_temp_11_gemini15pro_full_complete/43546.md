crayon:/ˈkreɪɒn/ (UK), /ˈkreɪɑːn/ (US)| n.|蜡笔；彩色粉笔|A stick of colored wax, charcoal, chalk, or other material used for drawing or coloring.|She drew a picture of a house with crayons. 她用蜡笔画了一幅房子的画。|近义词：colored pencil, marker|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps eraser as a tool for removing crayon marks) |noun
