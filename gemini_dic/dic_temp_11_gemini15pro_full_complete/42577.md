counterplay: /ˈkaʊntərpleɪ/ (US), /ˈkaʊntəpleɪ/ (UK) | n. | 反击，对抗，对策 | An action used to oppose or defend against something. |  The team's counterplay neutralized the opponent's offensive strategy. 该队的反击策略抵消了对手的进攻策略。

The government's counterplay to the economic downturn was swift and decisive. 政府应对经济衰退的对策迅速而果断。


近义词: countermove, countermeasure, response, reaction, retaliation | 反义词: inaction, passivity | 词性: 名词
