fasd:/ˈfæzd/ |n.|  Fetal Alcohol Spectrum Disorders |A group of conditions that can occur in a person whose mother drank alcohol during pregnancy. These effects can include physical problems and problems with behavior and learning. | Example:  FASD can cause a range of developmental issues.  胎儿酒精谱系障碍会导致一系列发育问题。
Example: Early diagnosis of FASD is crucial for intervention.  胎儿酒精谱系障碍的早期诊断对于干预至关重要。 |Synonym: FAS (Fetal Alcohol Syndrome) (Note: FAS is a *type* of FASD, but not all FASD is FAS)| Antonym:  (No direct antonym exists, but one might consider "typical development" in contrast)|noun
