maggoty:/ˈmæɡəti/| adj.|蛆虫的；腐败的；令人作呕的|Infested with maggots; decaying and foul-smelling.  Figuratively, it can describe something unpleasant or repulsive.|The dumpster was maggoty and overflowing.  那个垃圾箱里满是蛆虫，而且溢出来了。|近义词：putrid, rotten, foul, disgusting, repulsive|反义词:fresh, clean, pure|词性:形容词

maggoty:/ˈmæɡəti/| adj.|充满活力的；精力充沛的（非正式，主要用于澳大利亚英语）|Full of energy and enthusiasm. (Informal, chiefly Australian English)|He's a maggoty little bloke, always getting into mischief. 他是个精力充沛的小伙子，总是惹麻烦。|近义词：energetic, lively, spirited|反义词:lethargic, sluggish|词性:形容词
