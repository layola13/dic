earnestful: /ˈɜːrnɪstfʊl/ | adj. | 认真的，热心的 | Showing or marked by sincerity and serious intent; earnest. | She delivered an earnestful plea for help. 她恳切地请求帮助。|  近义词: sincere, serious, fervent, impassioned | 反义词: frivolous, flippant, apathetic | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From earnest + -ful.  "Earnest" comes from Old English *eornoste, from Proto-Germanic *ernustaz.  "-ful" is a suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by."
