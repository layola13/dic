equiponderant:/ˌiːkwɪˈpɒndərənt/ |ˌiːkwɪˈpɑːndərənt/| adj.|等重的；均衡的；同等重要的|Equal in weight, force, or importance; balanced.|例句：The two sides in the dispute were equiponderant. 争议双方势均力敌。|例句：The equiponderant forces created a state of equilibrium.  相等的力形成了平衡状态。|近义词：equivalent, equal, balanced, commensurate|反义词：unequal, unbalanced, disparate|词性：形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *aequiponderant-*, present participle of *aequiponderare* "to be of equal weight," from aequi- "equal" + ponderare "to weigh," from pondus "weight."
