layabout:/ˈleɪəˌbaʊt/ | n. | 懒汉，游手好闲的人 | A person who habitually avoids work or exertion. | He's such a layabout, always sleeping in and avoiding responsibilities. 他真是个懒汉，总是睡懒觉，逃避责任。| synonyms: loafer, idler, slacker | antonyms: hard worker, diligent person | noun


Etymology:
From "lay" (to recline) + "about" (around, idly).  Essentially, someone who lies around idly.
