arkani:/ɑːrˈkɑːni/ | adj. | 奥卡尼的;神秘的;深奥的 | Relating to or characteristic of the teachings or practices of a spiritual tradition, often esoteric or mystical.  Derived from the Arabic word "arkan," meaning "pillars" or "fundamentals." It often implies a hidden or secret knowledge. |  His teachings were described as arkani, accessible only to a select few. 他的教义被描述为奥卡尼的，只有少数人才能理解。|  The arkani rituals were shrouded in secrecy.  奥卡尼的仪式笼罩在神秘之中。| 近义词: esoteric, mystical, occult, hidden, secret | 反义词: exoteric, mundane, common, revealed, open | 词性: adjective
