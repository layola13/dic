cadeworm:/ˈkeɪdwɜːrm/| n. | 蓑蛾幼虫，袋蛾幼虫，卷叶虫 | The larva of a case-bearing moth, such as a bagworm or case moth, that constructs a protective case around itself from silk and other materials. |  The cadeworm camouflaged its case with bits of twig and leaf. 这只蓑蛾幼虫用小树枝和小叶子伪装它的外壳。| synonyms: bagworm, caseworm, case moth larva | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific larval stage) | noun
