felicitas:/fɛlɪˈsɪtɑːs/ | n. | 幸福，幸运；福气 | Happiness; good fortune; blessedness.  Derived from Latin "felix" (happy, lucky).  It often refers to a state of profound happiness or blessedness, rather than just a fleeting moment of joy. |  Her face radiated with a quiet felicitas. 她的脸上洋溢着宁静的幸福。| Synonyms: bliss, blessedness, happiness, fortune | Antonyms: misery, misfortune, unhappiness | noun

felicity:/fəˈlɪsɪti/ | n. | 幸福；恰当；巧妙 |  The state of being happy, especially in a high degree; appropriateness of behavior or expression; aptness.  It can also refer to an instance of happiness or a pleasing quality. | He spoke with great felicity. 他说得非常得体。| Synonyms: bliss, happiness, aptness, appropriateness | Antonyms: misery, unhappiness, inappropriateness | noun
