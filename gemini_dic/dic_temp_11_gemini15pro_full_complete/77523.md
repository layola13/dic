gazunderer:/ɡæzˈʌndərər/ | n. | 敲竹杠者；趁低价购房者 |  A person who reduces the amount of an offer to buy a property just before the exchange of contracts, hoping to take advantage of the seller's vulnerable position. | The gazunderer managed to knock £10,000 off the asking price just days before completion.  趁低价购房者设法在完成交易前几天从要价中扣除了 1 万英镑。| 近义词: bargain hunter, bottom feeder | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun

gazunder:/ˌɡæzˈʌndər/ | v. | 趁低价购房；敲竹杠 | To reduce the amount of an offer to buy a property just before the exchange of contracts, hoping to take advantage of the seller's vulnerable position. | The buyer tried to gazunder the seller by £5,000. 买家试图从卖家那里敲诈5000英镑。 | 近义词: lowball, underbid | 反义词: N/A | 词性: verb
