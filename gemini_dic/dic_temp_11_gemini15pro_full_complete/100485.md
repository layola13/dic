interveinal:/ˌɪntərˈveɪnəl/
adjective
叶脉间的
Situated between veins, especially of a leaf or insect wing.
The interveinal areas of the leaf were pale green.  叶子的叶脉间区域呈浅绿色。
The butterfly's wings had intricate interveinal patterns. 蝴蝶的翅膀上有复杂的脉间图案。
Synonyms: intervascular
Antonyms: veinal, vascular
词性: 形容词
