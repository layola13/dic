haemadromograph:/ˌhiːməˈdrɒməɡrɑːf/ /ˌhiːməˈdrɑːməɡræf/
noun|血流速度描记器|An instrument for measuring and recording the velocity of blood flow.|The haemadromograph showed a significant decrease in blood flow after the surgery. 手术后，血流速度描记器显示血流明显减少。|近义词:rheograph, blood flow meter|反义词: N/A|词性:n.
Etymology:  haema- (Greek: αἷμα, haîma, "blood") + dromo- (Greek: δρόμος, drómos, "running, course") + -graph (Greek: γράφω, gráphō, "to write")
