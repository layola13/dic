cashew:/ˈkæʃuː/ (US), /ˈkæʃuː/ (UK)| n.|腰果|A kidney-shaped nut that is edible and grows on a tropical American evergreen tree.|I love to snack on cashews. 我喜欢吃腰果作为零食。|近义词：nut, peanut (loosely)|反义词: None|Noun


cashew apple:/ˈkæʃuː ˈæpl/ (US), /ˈkæʃuː ˈæpl/ (UK)| n.|腰果苹果|The fleshy, pear-shaped fruit of the cashew tree, to which the cashew nut is attached.|Cashew apple juice is popular in some countries. 腰果苹果汁在一些国家很受欢迎。|近义词：cashew fruit|反义词: None|Noun
