gums:/ɡʌmz/
n.
牙龈; 齿龈;
The firm pink flesh inside the mouth that surrounds the bases of the teeth.
例句：He brushed his teeth and gums. 他刷了牙和牙龈。

例句：My gums are bleeding. 我的牙龈在流血。

近义词：gingiva
反义词：(无直接反义词)
词性：名词

gums:/ɡʌmz/
n.
树胶; 胶质;
Sticky substances exuded by some trees and other plants.
例句：The tree produces a sticky gum. 这棵树会产生粘稠的树胶。

例句：Chewing gum can help freshen your breath. 口香糖可以帮助清新口气。

近义词：resin, sap
反义词：(无直接反义词)
词性：名词
