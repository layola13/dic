disgorge:/dɪsˈɡɔːrdʒ/
|v.|吐出；喷出；被迫放弃|The whale disgorged the fish it had swallowed. 鲸鱼吐出了它吞下的鱼。|
|v.|吐出；喷出；被迫放弃|The company was forced to disgorge its ill-gotten gains. 公司被迫吐出其非法所得。|
|近义词:|spew, emit, eject, expel, vomit|
|反义词:|absorb, swallow, ingest, retain|
|词性:|verb|


词源:
From Old French desgorger, from des- (expressing removal) + gorge (throat).  Ultimately from Latin gurges (whirlpool).
