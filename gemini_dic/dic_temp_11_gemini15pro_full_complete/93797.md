immerses:/ɪˈmɜːrsɪz/| v.|沉浸，使沉浸| to involve oneself or someone else deeply in something |He immersed himself in his studies. 他沉浸在学习中. |近义词：engross, submerge, plunge, absorb |反义词: extricate, remove |词性:动词

词源: From Late Latin *immersus, past participle of immergere “to plunge in, dip in,” from in- “in” + mergere “to dip, plunge.”
