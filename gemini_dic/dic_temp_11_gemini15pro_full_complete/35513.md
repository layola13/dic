coadventure:/koʊədˈvɛntʃər/| n.|共同冒险，联合冒险|A shared or joint adventure; an undertaking in which two or more parties participate.|Example: They embarked on a coadventure to explore the uncharted territory. 他们开始了一项共同冒险，去探索未知的领域。

Example: The two companies formed a coadventure to develop the new software.  这两家公司成立了一个合资企业来开发新的软件。|近义词：joint venture, collaboration, partnership, alliance|反义词:solo venture, independent undertaking|词性:名词
