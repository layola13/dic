baubee: /ˈbɔːbiː/| n.| 半便士（苏格兰旧货币单位），少量钱|A Scottish coin of small value, equal to half a penny. |He hasn't a baubee to his name. 他身无分文。|近义词：penny, farthing, pittance|反义词：fortune, wealth|词性: noun

Etymology:
From Scots *bawbee*, from the name of a 16th-century French mintmaster, Sieur de Baugé.
