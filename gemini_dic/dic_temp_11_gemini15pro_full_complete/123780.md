mooted:/ˈmuːtɪd/ | adj. | 已被提出的，被讨论的 |  Brought up for discussion or consideration, typically in a tentative or preliminary way.  | The idea of building a new library was mooted at the council meeting.  这个想法在理事会上被提出。|  suggested, proposed, raised, broached |  dismissed, rejected, ignored | adjective

mooted:/ˈmuːtɪd/ | v. | 提出…供讨论 | To raise (a question or topic) for discussion; suggest (an idea or possibility). |  Someone mooted the idea of a merger. 有人提出了合并的想法。 | propose, suggest, raise, broach | dismiss, reject, ignore | verb
