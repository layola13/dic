hypermorally:/ˌhaɪpərˈmɔːrəli/
adjective
极端道德的；过分拘泥道德的
Excessively or rigidly moral; beyond what is considered normal or reasonable in terms of moral principles.
He applied a hypermorally rigid interpretation to the text, missing the nuanced meaning. 他对文本进行了极端道德的僵化解读，忽略了其细微的含义。

She lives a hypermorally restrictive life, avoiding any activity that could be considered remotely questionable. 她过着极端拘泥道德的生活，避免任何可能被认为稍微有问题的活动。

近义词: overscrupulous, puritanical, excessively moralistic
反义词: amoral, immoral, unethical
词性: adjective

词源:
"hyper-" (Greek: ὑπέρ, hupér, "over, beyond") + "moral" (Latin: morālis, relating to custom or character)
