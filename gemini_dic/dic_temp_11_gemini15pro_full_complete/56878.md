dosel:/ˈdɒzəl/ (UK) /ˈdɑːsəl/ (US)| n.|华盖；天篷；蚊帐|A canopy or hanging curtain, especially one over an altar, throne, or bed.|The king sat upon his throne beneath a rich dosel. 国王坐在华盖下的宝座上。|Synonyms: canopy, baldachin, tester|Antonyms: None|Noun

dosel:/ˈdɒzəl/ (UK) /ˈdɑːsəl/ (US)| v.|用华盖装饰|To decorate with a canopy or hanging curtain.|The bed was doseled with silk. 床上装饰着丝绸华盖。|Synonyms: canopy, cover|Antonyms: uncover, expose|Verb
