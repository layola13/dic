non-distracting:/nɒn-dɪˈstræktɪŋ/
adjective
不让人分心的
Not causing distraction; allowing one to focus.
This app provides a non-distracting writing environment.  这个应用程序提供了一个不让人分心的写作环境。
The library offers a non-distracting atmosphere for studying. 图书馆提供了一个不让人分心的学习氛围。
近义词:  focused, concentrating, immersive
反义词: distracting, disturbing, disruptive
词性: adjective
