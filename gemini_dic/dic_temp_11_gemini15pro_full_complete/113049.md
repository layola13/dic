lump:/lʌmp/| n. v.|块，肿块，笨人；使成块状|A lump of coal. 一块煤。|近义词：chunk, mass, swelling, clod, dolt|反义词:fragment, bit, particle|词性:名词, 动词

lump:/lʌmp/| v.|使成块状，忍耐，笨重地行走|She lumped all her expenses together. 她把所有的开销都算在一起了。|近义词：combine, group, tolerate, lumber|反义词:separate, divide|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lumpe*, from Old Norse *lumpr* (“lump, rag”), from Proto-Germanic *lumpuz* (“lump, clump”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)lemb- (“to limp, hang limply”). Cognate with Danish *lump* (“lump”), Swedish *lump* (“lump”), Icelandic *lumpr* (“rag”). Related to limp.
