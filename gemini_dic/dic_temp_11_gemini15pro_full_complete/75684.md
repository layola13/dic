fulgidity:/fʊlˈdʒɪdəti/ | n. | 闪耀；光辉；辉煌 |  The state or quality of being dazzlingly bright or shining. | The fulgidity of the diamond blinded me momentarily. 钻石的光辉使我一时眼花缭乱。 | 近义词：brilliance, radiance, luminosity, splendor | 反义词：dullness, darkness, obscurity | 词性: 名词

fulgent:/ˈfʌldʒənt/ | adj. | 闪耀的；光辉的；灿烂的 | Shining brightly; radiant. | The fulgent sun beat down on the desert. 灿烂的阳光照射在沙漠上。 | 近义词：bright, shining, radiant, luminous | 反义词：dull, dim, dark | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fulgiditas, from fulgidus (“shining, glittering, flashing, bright”), from fulgēre (“to shine, flash, glitter”), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰel- (“to shine, burn”).
