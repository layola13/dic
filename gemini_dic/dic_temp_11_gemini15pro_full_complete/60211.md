effluence:/ˈefluəns/| n.|流出物，散发物|Something that flows out or is emitted; an outflow.|The effluence of noxious fumes from the factory chimney polluted the air. 工厂烟囱排放出的有害烟雾污染了空气。|Synonyms: outflow, discharge, emission, emanation|Antonyms: influx, intake|noun

effluence:/ˈefluəns/| n.|流露，(思想等的)传播|The spreading of influence or a flowing forth.|The artist's work is an effluence of her creative spirit.  这位艺术家的作品是她创作精神的流露。|Synonyms: emanation, diffusion, spreading|Antonyms: suppression, containment|noun
