out-of-plumb:/ˌaʊt əv ˈplʌm/ | adj. | 不垂直的，倾斜的 | Not vertical; leaning to one side. | The wall is slightly out-of-plumb.  墙有点倾斜。|  The old tower is noticeably out-of-plumb.  那座老塔明显地倾斜了。 | tilted, askew, leaning | vertical, plumb, upright | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
"out-" (Old English *ūt-, from Proto-Germanic *ūt-) + "of" (Old English of, from Proto-Germanic *af) + "plumb" (from Middle French *plomb, from Latin plumbum "lead," because a lead weight was used to determine verticality)
