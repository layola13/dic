contraindicates:/ˌkɒntrəˈɪndɪkeɪts/ | v. |禁忌; 禁用; 指明(某疗法)不适用 |  To make (a treatment or procedure) inadvisable. | This medication is contraindicated in patients with kidney disease. 这种药物对肾病患者禁用。 | 近义词: proscribe, forbid, preclude | 反义词: indicate, advise, recommend | 词性: 动词

contraindication:/ˌkɒntrəˌɪndɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. |禁忌症; 禁用因素 | A factor that makes a particular treatment or procedure inadvisable. |  A history of heart disease is a contraindication for this type of surgery. 心脏病史是这类手术的禁忌症。| 近义词:  prohibition, objection, deterrent | 反义词: indication, recommendation | 词性: 名词
