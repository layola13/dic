newsworthier:/ˌnjuːzˈwɜːrðiər/
| adj. |
| 更具新闻价值的 |
| More worthy of being reported as news; more significant or interesting as news. |
| The scandal involving the mayor was newsworthier than the local bake sale.  市长卷入的丑闻比当地的烘焙义卖更具新闻价值。|

| The discovery of a new planet is certainly newsworthier than the latest celebrity gossip. 发现一颗新行星肯定比最新的名人八卦更具新闻价值。|
| 近义词: more important, more significant, more interesting, more relevant |
| 反义词: less important, less significant, less interesting, less relevant, trivial |
| 词性: adjective |
| 词源:  From "news" + "worthy" + "-er" (comparative suffix). "News" comes from Old French "nouvelles" (plural of "nouvelle" meaning "new thing"), ultimately from Latin "novus" (new). "Worthy" comes from Old English "weorðig," meaning "having worth or merit." |
