brahmin:/ˈbrɑːmɪn/ /ˈbræmɪn/| n.|婆罗门（印度种姓制度中最高等级的祭司）|A member of the highest Hindu caste, traditionally made up of priests.|例句：The brahmin performed the sacred rituals. 婆罗门举行了神圣的仪式。

例句：He was born into a brahmin family. 他出生在一个婆罗门家庭。

近义词：priest, clergyman|反义词:outcast, pariah|词性:noun
