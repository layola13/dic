fidgety:/ˈfɪdʒɪti/| adj.|烦躁不安的，坐立不安的|Restless and uneasy; unable to stay still.|The children were fidgety during the long car ride. 孩子们在漫长的车程中坐立不安。|近义词:restless, antsy, squirmy|反义词:calm, relaxed, still|词性:形容词
Etymology: From fidget + -y.  Fidget itself comes from the now obsolete word *figet*, possibly of Scandinavian origin, related to Norwegian *fikja* "to hasten, bustle".
