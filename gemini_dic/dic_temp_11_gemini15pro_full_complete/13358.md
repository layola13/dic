atmiatry:/ætˈmaɪətri/ | n. | 空气污染治理; 大气污染防治 | The science of treating or preventing atmospheric pollution. |  Air pollution control measures, such as installing scrubbers on smokestacks, fall under the umbrella of atmiatry.  空气污染控制措施，例如在烟囱上安装洗涤器，属于大气污染防治的范畴。| Synonyms: air pollution control, atmospheric remediation | Antonyms: air pollution, atmospheric contamination | noun
