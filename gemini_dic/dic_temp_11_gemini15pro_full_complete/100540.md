interweavements:/ˌɪntərˈwiːvmənts/
n.
交织；交错；交织物
the act or result of interweaving something, or the fact of being interwoven.

The tapestry depicted a complex interweavement of mythological figures and historical events.  这幅挂毯描绘了神话人物和历史事件的复杂交织。

The interweavements of the plot kept the audience captivated.  情节的交织使观众着迷。

Synonyms:  intertwining, interlacing, weaving, blending, merging
Antonyms: separation, disentanglement, division

Etymology: From interweave + -ment.  Interweave comes from the Old English "interwefan,"  from "inter-" (between) + "wefan" (to weave).
