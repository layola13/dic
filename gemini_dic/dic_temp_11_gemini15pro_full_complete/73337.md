forefathers:/ˈfɔːrˌfɑːðərz/ (US) /ˈfɔːˌfɑːðəz/ (UK)| n.|祖先，先辈|People from whom one is descended, especially those who lived a long time ago.  Those who established or developed something.|例句：Our forefathers fought for the freedoms we enjoy today. 我们的祖先为我们今天享有的自由而战。|例句：The forefathers of modern science made many important discoveries. 现代科学的先驱们做出了许多重要的发现。|近义词：ancestors, progenitors|反义词：descendants, offspring|词性：名词
