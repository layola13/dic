intemperance: /ɪnˈtɛmpərəns/ | n. | 放纵；过度；无节制 | Lack of moderation or restraint, especially in consuming alcohol.  | His intemperance led to health problems. 他的过度放纵导致了健康问题。|  The intemperance of his language alienated his friends.  他言语的无节制疏远了他的朋友们。| 近义词: excess, immoderation, overindulgence | 反义词: temperance, moderation, abstinence | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *intemperantia, from Latin intemperans (in- 'not' + temperans 'moderate').
