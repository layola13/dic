mnemonical:/nɪˈmɒnɪkəl/ (US) /nɪˈmɑːnɪkəl/ (UK)| adj.| 记忆的，助记的| assisting or intended to assist memory|  She used a mnemonical device to remember the names of the planets. 她用了一种助记方法来记住行星的名字。| synonyms: mnemonic, memory-aiding, helpful for memory | antonyms: forgetful, amnesiac| adjective

mnemonically:/nɪˈmɒnɪkəli/ (US) /nɪˈmɑːnɪkəli/ (UK)| adv.| 记忆地，助记地| in a way that assists or is intended to assist memory |  He arranged the information mnemonically so it would be easier to recall. 他以助记的方式排列信息，以便更容易回忆。| synonyms: mnemonically, by means of mnemonics | antonyms: forgetfully | adverb

Etymology: From Greek *mnēmonikos* "of or for memory," from *mnēmōn* "mindful."
