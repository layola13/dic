cpi:/ˌsiː piːˈaɪ/| n. |消费者物价指数(Consumer Price Index)|A measure that examines the weighted average of prices of a basket of consumer goods and services, such as transportation, food, and medical care. It is calculated by taking price changes for each item in the predetermined basket of goods and averaging them.  |The CPI rose 3% last year. 去年消费者物价指数上涨了3%。|近义词:cost of living index, inflation indicator|反义词:N/A|名词
