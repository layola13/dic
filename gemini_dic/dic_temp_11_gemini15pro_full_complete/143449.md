outfought:/ˌaʊtˈfɔːt/
verb
战胜，打败
defeated in a fight or contest, especially by using superior skill or strength.
The smaller army outfought their larger opponent through clever tactics.  这支规模较小的军队凭借巧妙的战术战胜了规模更大的对手。
The champion outfought the challenger and retained his title. 冠军战胜了挑战者，保住了自己的头衔。
Synonyms: defeated, vanquished, conquered, overcome, bested
Antonyms: lost to, succumbed to
词性:动词
