branchery:/ˈbrɑːntʃəri/
noun
枝叶；树枝；分枝
The arrangement of branches; branches collectively.  The elaborate branchery of the chandelier. 枝繁叶茂
The bird built its nest in the branchery of the tree. 这只鸟在树的枝叶间筑巢。
Synonyms: branches, foliage, ramifications
Antonyms: trunk, root, stem
词性: 名词
