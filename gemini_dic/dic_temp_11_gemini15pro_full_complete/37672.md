compactness:/kəmˈpæktnəs/ /ˈkɑːmpæktnəs/
n.
紧密; 紧凑; 简洁;
The state or quality of being compact.  The compactness of the device makes it ideal for travel.
This camera's compactness makes it perfect for carrying around. 这款相机的紧凑性使其非常便于携带。
density, solidity, firmness
looseness, diffuseness
noun

compactness:/kəmˈpæktnəs/ /ˈkɑːmpæktnəs/
n.
紧凑性; 密实度;
The quality of containing a lot in a small space. The compactness of the soil makes it difficult for plants to grow.
The essay's compactness made it easy to understand.  这篇文章的简洁性使它易于理解。
conciseness, brevity, terseness
wordiness, lengthiness, verbosity
