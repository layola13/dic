herringlike:/ˈherɪŋlaɪk/| adj.|像鲱鱼的，鲱鱼似的|Resembling or characteristic of a herring in appearance or behavior. |例句：The fish had a silvery, herringlike body. 这条鱼有着银色的，像鲱鱼一样的身体。

例句：The small, herringlike fish swam in large schools. 这些小的，像鲱鱼一样的鱼成群游动。|近义词：herring-shaped, clupeid, |反义词: (No direct antonym, could use unlike a herring) |词性: adjective
