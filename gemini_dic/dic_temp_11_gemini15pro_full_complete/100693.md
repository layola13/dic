intracoastal:/ˌɪntrəˈkoʊstl/ | adj. | 内海岸的；沿岸内航道的 | Situated between the coast and a coastal island, or connecting such waterways. | Example 1: The intracoastal waterway provides a protected route for boats. 内海岸航道为船只提供了一条受保护的路线。

Example 2:  We took a relaxing cruise on the intracoastal waterway. 我们在内海岸航道上进行了一次轻松的游船旅行。 | Synonym: inshore, coastal | Antonym: offshore, oceanic | Adjective
