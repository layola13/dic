antiscepticism:/ˌæntiˈskɛptɪsɪzəm/
|n.|反怀疑论|The philosophical position of opposing skepticism, often by defending the possibility of knowledge or certainty.|His antiscepticism led him to believe in the inherent goodness of human nature.|近义词:dogmatism, credulity|反义词:skepticism, doubt|词性:名词
