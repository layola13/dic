lamebrain: /ˈleɪmˌbreɪn/ | n. | 笨蛋；傻瓜 | A stupid person. | He called me a lamebrain for forgetting my keys. 他因为我忘了带钥匙就叫我笨蛋。| 近义词：idiot, moron, imbecile, fool, dolt | 反义词：genius, intellectual, savant | 词性: 名词

lamebrain: /ˈleɪmˌbreɪn/ | adj. | 愚蠢的 | Stupid; foolish. | That was a lamebrain idea. 那真是个愚蠢的主意。| 近义词：stupid, foolish, idiotic, silly | 反义词：intelligent, smart, clever, wise | 词性: 形容词


词源:  "lamebrain" is a compound word formed from "lame" (meaning crippled or ineffective) and "brain."  "Lame" in this context dates back to Old English.
