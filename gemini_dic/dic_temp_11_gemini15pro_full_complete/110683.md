line-printers:/ˈlaɪn ˌprɪntərz/| n. |行式打印机|A type of high-speed impact printer that prints an entire line of text at a time.  They were common in the mid-20th century for printing large volumes of data. |The old mainframe computer used line-printers to produce reports.| synonyms: printer, impact printer| antonyms: inkjet printer, laser printer | noun
ETYMOLOGY->
"line" + "printer".  "Line" refers to the ability to print an entire line of text at once.  "Printer" denotes the device's function as a printing machine.
