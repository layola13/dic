fathomable:/ˈfæðəməbl/ /ˈfæðəməbəl/| adj.|可理解的，可测的|Capable of being understood; comprehensible.  Also, capable of being measured.|The explanation was thankfully fathomable, even to a beginner.|近义词：comprehensible, understandable, intelligible, graspable|反义词：incomprehensible, unfathomable, inscrutable|词性:形容词
词源: From fathom (to measure the depth of) + -able (capable of being).  Ultimately from Old English fæthm.
