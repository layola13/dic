bitheism:/ˈbaɪθiːɪzəm/
n.
二神教，二神崇拜
The belief in two gods.
Bitheism is a less common religious belief than monotheism or polytheism.  二神教是一种比一神教或多神教更不常见的宗教信仰。
Ancient religions sometimes featured bitheism, with two deities representing opposing forces. 古代宗教有时以二神教为特色，两个神代表对立的力量。
近义词:duotheism
反义词:monotheism, polytheism
词性:noun
