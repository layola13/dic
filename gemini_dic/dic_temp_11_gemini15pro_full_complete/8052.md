anthropolite:/ˈænθrəpəˌlaɪt/
n.
人造宝石，一种仿造钻石的玻璃
A synthetic gemstone, a type of glass imitation of a diamond.
This ring features a beautiful anthropolite that sparkles like a real diamond. 这枚戒指上有一颗美丽的人造宝石，像真钻石一样闪闪发光。
Near synonyms: imitation diamond, rhinestone, simulacrum
Antonyms: diamond, natural gemstone
noun
