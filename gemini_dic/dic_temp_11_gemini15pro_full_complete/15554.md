Baghdad has been a center of learning for centuries.  巴格达几个世纪以来一直是学习的中心。|近义词: capital (as in capital city)|反义词:  (No direct antonym for a city name) |词性: noun

Etymology:
From Arabic بغداد (Baġdād), possibly from Middle Persian *Baɣ-dād (“God-given”), from baɣ (“god”) + dād (“given”).
