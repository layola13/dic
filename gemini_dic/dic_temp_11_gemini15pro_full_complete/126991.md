nasalises:/ˈneɪzəlaɪzɪz/ /ˈneɪzəlaɪzəz/
verb
使鼻音化；用鼻音说
To make a sound nasal by lowering the soft palate so that air passes through the nose.
He nasalises his vowels. 他说话时元音鼻音化。
近义词: nasalize
反义词: (No clear single antonym; consider "denasalize" but it's less common)
词性: verb
