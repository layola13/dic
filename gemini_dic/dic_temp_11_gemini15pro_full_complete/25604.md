bumpers:/ˈbʌmpərz/ | n. |保险杠; 缓冲器; 丰收; (酒)满杯|A bar attached to the front and rear of a motor vehicle to reduce damage in a collision.  A thing that reduces shock or protects against damage.| The car’s bumpers were damaged in the accident. 汽车的保险杠在事故中受损了。| synonyms: buffer, cushion, fender, guard | antonyms:  (For bumper crop) failure, dearth | noun

bumpers:/ˈbʌmpərz/ | adj. |特大的; 丰盛的|Unusually large; producing an unusually large yield.|  A bumper crop of wheat.  小麦丰收。| synonyms: abundant, plentiful, bountiful | antonyms: meager, scanty | adjective
