nonexhaustible:/ˌnɒnɪɡˈzɔːstɪbl/ | adj. | 取之不尽的，用之不竭的 | Not able to be exhausted; inexhaustible.  | Solar energy is a nonexhaustible resource. 太阳能是一种取之不竭的资源。 | The earth's supply of air and water is, for all practical purposes, nonexhaustible.  地球上的空气和水供应，实际上是取之不尽的。 | 近义词: inexhaustible, limitless, boundless, infinite | 反义词: exhaustible, finite, limited | 词性: 形容词

词源:  non- (not) + exhaustible (capable of being exhausted), from exhaust (to use up completely)
