berk:/bɜːk/| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜|A stupid or foolish person.  Derived from rhyming slang "Berkshire Hunt" which rhymes with "cunt".  Originally a very strong insult, now often used playfully.|He's such a berk, he locked his keys in the car again.|近义词：idiot, fool, twit, dope, nincompoop|反义词:genius, intellectual|Noun
