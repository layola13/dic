centralisms:/ˈsɛntrəlɪzəmz/
n.
中央集权制；中央集权主义
The concentration of control of an activity or organization under a single authority.
Centralisms stifled local initiatives and led to widespread discontent. 中央集权扼杀了地方的积极性，导致了普遍的不满。
The government's increasing centralisms worried many citizens. 政府日益加强的中央集权让许多公民感到担忧。
近义词: centralization, concentration
反义词: decentralization, localism
词性: 名词
