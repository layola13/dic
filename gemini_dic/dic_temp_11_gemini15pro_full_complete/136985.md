nonspinning:/ˌnɑːnˈspɪnɪŋ/
adjective
非旋转的
Not spinning; lacking spin.
Electrons in certain orbitals are described as nonspinning. 某些轨道上的电子被描述为非旋转的。
The nonspinning top just sat there.  这个不旋转的陀螺就放在那里。
近义词: motionless, static, still
反义词: spinning, rotating, revolving
词性: adjective

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "spinning" (present participle of "spin"). The word "spin" comes from Old English *spinnan, from Proto-Germanic *spinnaną, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)pen-, meaning "to draw, stretch, spin."
