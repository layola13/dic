mispurchased:/ˌmɪsˈpɜːrtʃəst/ |misˈpɜːrʧəst/| adj. | 错误购买的；买错的 | Bought by mistake or incorrectly. | I returned the mispurchased item to the store. 我把买错的商品退回商店了。| 近义词：wrongly bought, mistakenly purchased | 反义词：correctly purchased | 词性:形容词

mispurchased:/ˌmɪsˈpɜːrtʃəst/ |misˈpɜːrʧəst/| v. | 买错了 | To buy something by mistake or incorrectly. |  I mispurchased the wrong size shoes online. 我在网上买错了尺码的鞋子。| 近义词：bought wrongly, purchased mistakenly | 反义词：bought correctly | 词性:动词
