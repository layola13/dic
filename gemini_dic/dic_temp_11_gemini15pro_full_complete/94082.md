impasting: /ɪmˈpɑːstɪŋ/ /ɪmˈpeɪstɪŋ/
n.
厚涂颜料；堆砌
The technique of applying paint thickly so that it stands out from the surface.
The artist used impasting to create a textured effect.  这位艺术家用厚涂颜料来制造纹理效果。
impasto, layering
thinning
noun
