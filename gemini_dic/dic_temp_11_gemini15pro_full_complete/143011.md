outbid:/ˌaʊtˈbɪd/ | v. | 出价高于（某人）；胜过 | To offer a higher price than someone else in an auction or other competitive situation. |  I was outbid by another collector at the auction. 我在拍卖会上被另一个收藏家出价超过了。

|  She outbid all her rivals for the contract. 她出价超过所有竞争对手，赢得了合同。 | 近义词: outdo, surpass, overbid | 反义词: underbid | verb

outbid:/ˌaʊtˈbɪd/ | n. |  更高的出价 | A higher bid than another. |  The winning outbid was $10 million.  最终胜出的出价是1000万美元。| This outbid secured him the painting. 这次更高的出价让他得到了这幅画。| 近义词: higher bid, winning bid | 反义词: lower bid | noun
