loose-leaf:/ˈluːs liːf/
adjective|活页的；散页的|Designed to have pages added or removed.  Suitable for having pages inserted or removed.|I prefer a loose-leaf notebook so I can rearrange my notes.|detachable, unbound|bound, fixed
noun|活页纸；散页|A sheet of paper that can be easily removed or inserted into a binder.|I need some loose-leaf for my binder.|loose page, separate sheet|bound page
