dribbling:/ˈdrɪblɪŋ/ (US), /ˈdrɪblɪŋ/ (UK)| n. v.|运球，滴下，流口水|The act of moving a ball by bouncing it or kicking it slightly. Also, the act of saliva flowing from the mouth.|例句：He is good at dribbling the basketball. 他擅长运篮球。|近义词：bouncing, controlling (for ball), drooling (for saliva)|反义词: catching (for ball)|词性: noun, verb

dribbling:/ˈdrɪblɪŋ/ (US), /ˈdrɪblɪŋ/ (UK)| n. v.|滴下，流口水|The involuntary flow of saliva from the mouth.|例句：The baby's dribbling had soaked through his bib.  婴儿的口水浸透了他的围兜。|近义词：drooling, slobbering|反义词: None|词性: noun, verb
