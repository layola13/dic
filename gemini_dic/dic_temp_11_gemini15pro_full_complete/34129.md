cladonia:/kləˈdoʊniə/ | n. | 石蕊属地衣 | A genus of lichens, commonly known as reindeer moss, cup lichens, or pixie cups. They are characterized by their branching, often cup-shaped or antler-like structures. |  Reindeer depend heavily on cladoniarangiferina for food. 驯鹿主要依靠驯鹿石蕊为食。

Cladonia cristatella is also known as the British soldiers lichen.  红冠石蕊也被称为英国士兵地衣。 | synonym: reindeer moss, cup lichen | antonym: N/A | noun
