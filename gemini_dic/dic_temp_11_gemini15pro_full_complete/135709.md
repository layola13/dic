nonprevention:/ˌnɑːnprɪˈvɛnʃən/ | n. | 非预防; 不预防; 未预防 | The nonprevention of disease led to a widespread epidemic.  疾病的未预防导致了大范围的流行。 |  Lack of action to prevent something, especially something undesirable. Nonprevention focuses on the absence of preventative measures rather than active steps to cause harm. | Example: The nonprevention of cyberbullying has led to increased mental health issues among teenagers. 青少年网络欺凌的未预防导致了心理健康问题的增加。| Example:  The report criticized the nonprevention of deforestation in the Amazon. 该报告批评了对亚马逊森林砍伐的未预防。 | Synonyms: lack of prevention, failure to prevent, omission of prevention | Antonyms: prevention, precaution, prophylaxis | noun
