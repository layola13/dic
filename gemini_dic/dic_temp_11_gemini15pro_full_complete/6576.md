anabasis: /əˈnæbəsɪs/
n.
远征；(尤指)远征内陆；进军；(生理学)上升期
an expedition from a coastline into the interior of a country, especially one with a military objective; an advance; (physiology) the stage of increase in a disease.
Xenophon's Anabasis recounts the Greek mercenaries' retreat from Persia.  色诺芬的《远征记》讲述了希腊雇佣兵从波斯撤退的故事。

The anabasis of the infection is marked by a high fever. 感染的上升期以高烧为特征。

近义词: expedition, journey, march, advance, ascent
反义词: retreat, withdrawal, descent, catabasis
词性: noun

Etymology: From Ancient Greek ἀνάβασις (anábasis, “a going up”), from ἀναβαίνω (anabaínō, “I go up, ascend”), from ἀνά (aná, “up”) + βαίνω (baínō, “I go, walk”).
