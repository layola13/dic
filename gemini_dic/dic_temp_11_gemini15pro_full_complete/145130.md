overarranged:/ˌoʊvərəˈreɪndʒd/
adjective
过度安排的，安排过多的
having too many arrangements or details planned, often to the point of being excessive or fussy.
The wedding was overarranged, leaving the couple feeling stressed rather than joyful.  婚礼安排得过于繁琐，让这对新人感到压力重重，而不是喜悦。
Her travel itinerary was so overarranged that she had no time for spontaneous adventures. 她的旅行行程安排得过于紧凑，以至于没有时间进行即兴的冒险。
近义词: overplanned, overorganized, overscheduled
反义词: underarranged, unplanned, simple
词性: adjective


词源:
"Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "arrange" (verb meaning "to put in order") + "-ed" (past participle suffix)
