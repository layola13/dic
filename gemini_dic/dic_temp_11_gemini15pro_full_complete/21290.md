bloodlusts:/ˈblʌd.lʌsts/
noun
嗜血，杀戮欲
An overwhelming desire to shed blood or engage in violence.
The general's bloodlusts led him to order the massacre of the entire village. 将军的嗜血让他下令屠杀了整个村庄。
bloodthirstiness, savagery, brutality
compassion, mercy
n.
