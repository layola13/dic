metathoracic:/ˌmɛtəθəˈræsɪk/| adj.|后胸的，中胸的|Relating to the metathorax, the middle segment of the thorax in insects, located between the prothorax and the mesothorax. It bears the second pair of legs and, in winged insects, the second pair of wings.|The metathoracic legs of the grasshopper are adapted for jumping.  蚱蜢的后胸腿适合跳跃。|The beetle's metathoracic wings are hardened into protective covers. 甲虫的后胸翅硬化成保护壳。|mesothoracic, posterior thoracic|anterior thoracic|adjective
