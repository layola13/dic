fruitiest:/ˈfruːtiɪst/
adjective
最果味的；果香最浓的
Having the most pronounced flavor or aroma of fruit.
例句：This is the fruitiest wine I've ever tasted.  这是我尝过果味最浓的葡萄酒。

例句：The fruitiest jam is made with ripe berries.  果味最浓的果酱是用成熟的浆果制成的。


近义词：luscious, ripe, aromatic
反义词：tart, bland, sour
词性: adjective
