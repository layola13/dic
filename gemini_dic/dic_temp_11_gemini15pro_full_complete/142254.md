orphanages: /ˈɔːrfənɪdʒɪz/
n.
孤儿院
A residential institution for the care and education of orphans.
The children in the orphanages were well cared for.  孤儿院的孩子们得到了很好的照顾。

The orphanages provided a safe haven for the children. 孤儿院为孩子们提供了一个安全的避风港。

近义词: orphanage, children's home
反义词: home, family
词性: noun


词源:
From Old French orphanin, from Medieval Latin orphaninus, from Latin orphanus.
