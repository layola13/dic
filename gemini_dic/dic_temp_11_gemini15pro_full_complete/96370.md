indiscernible:/ˌɪndɪˈsɜːrnɪbl/ /ˌɪndɪˈsɜːr nəbəl/| adj.|难以辨认的，无法察觉的|Not perceptible or distinguishable.|The differences between the two versions are almost indiscernible. 这两个版本之间的差异几乎难以辨认。|近义词：imperceptible, undetectable, invisible, unnoticeable|反义词：discernible, perceptible, noticeable, obvious|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *indiscernibilis*, from in- "not" + discernibilis "distinguishable", from discernere "to separate, distinguish".
