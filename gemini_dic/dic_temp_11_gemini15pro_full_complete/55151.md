dissed:/dɪst/ | v. | 侮辱，轻视，贬低 | To speak disrespectfully to or about; insult. |  He dissed his former friend in front of everyone. 他当着所有人的面侮辱了他以前的朋友。

He felt dissed when his suggestion was ignored. 当他的建议被忽视时，他感到被轻视了。

近义词:insulted, slighted, snubbed, disrespect, put down | 反义词: praised, complimented, respected, honored | 词性: verb

词源:  Shortened form of disrespect (v.), which dates from the 1980s.  Ultimately from the prefix dis- (expressing negation) + respect.
