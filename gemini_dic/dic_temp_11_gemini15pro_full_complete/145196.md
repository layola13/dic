overbake:/ˌoʊvərˈbeɪk/| v.|烤过头，烘烤过久|To bake (food) for too long, resulting in dryness or burning.|例句：I overbaked the cookies, and they were hard as rocks. 我把饼干烤过头了，它们硬得像石头一样。

例句：Be careful not to overbake the cake, or it will be dry.  小心不要把蛋糕烤过头，否则会很干。|近义词：burn, scorch|反义词:underbake|词性:动词
