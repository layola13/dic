adj.
非虚构的；真实的
Not fictional; based on facts or real events.
The book is a nonfictitious account of the war. 这本书是对战争的非虚构描述。
She prefers reading nonfictitious books to novels. 她喜欢读非虚构类的书籍而不是小说。

反义词: fictional, imaginary, made-up, invented

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + fictitious + -ness.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," "fictitious" comes from the Latin "fictitius" meaning "feigned" or "imaginary," and "-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns indicating a state or quality.
