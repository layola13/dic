androgens:/ˈændrədʒənz/| n.|雄激素|A group of steroid hormones that primarily influence the growth and development of the male reproductive system.  They are also responsible for the development of secondary sexual characteristics in males, such as increased muscle mass and facial hair growth.|Increased androgens during puberty cause boys' voices to deepen. 青春期雄激素的增加导致男孩的声音变粗。|synonyms:  testosterone,  male hormones|antonyms: estrogens, female hormones|noun
