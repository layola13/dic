derivationist: /ˌdɛrɪˈveɪʃənɪst/ | n. |  衍生学家；推衍派学者 | A linguist who emphasizes the importance of derivational processes in word formation. |  The derivationist argued that the word "unhappiness" is formed by adding prefixes and suffixes to the root "happy."  衍生学家认为，“unhappiness”一词是由在词根“happy”上添加前缀和后缀构成的。|  etymologist, morphologist |  -- | noun

derivation: /ˌdɛrɪˈveɪʃən/ | n. | 衍生；起源；词源 | The process of forming a word from another word, often by adding prefixes or suffixes. | The derivation of the word "electricity" can be traced back to the Greek word "elektron."  “electricity”一词的词源可以追溯到希腊语单词“elektron”。
