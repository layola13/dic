globalization:/ˌɡloʊbələˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌɡləʊbəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |全球化|The increasing interconnectedness and interdependence of countries through trade, technology, and cultural exchange.|例句：Globalization has led to increased competition in many industries. 全球化导致许多行业的竞争加剧。

例句：The globalization of markets has created new opportunities for businesses. 市场全球化为企业创造了新的机会。

近义词：internationalization, globalism|反义词:localization, regionalism|词性:noun
