compend:/ˈkɑːmpend/ /kəmˈpɛnd/ | n. | 概要，纲要，简编 | A concise summary or abridgment of a larger work; a handbook. | Example 1:  The professor recommended a compend of Roman history for the exam.  教授推荐了一本罗马史概要供考试使用。

Example 2:  She kept a compend of useful recipes in her kitchen.  她在厨房里保存了一本有用的食谱概要。| 近义词: summary, abstract, epitome, synopsis, digest | 反义词: expansion, elaboration | 词性: 名词
