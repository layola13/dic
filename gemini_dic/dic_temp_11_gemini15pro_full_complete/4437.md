alatern:/ˈælətɜːrn/| n.| 冬青|A shrub or small tree of the genus *Frangula* (formerly *Rhamnus*), especially *Frangula alnus* (alder buckthorn), which is common in Eurasia and North Africa. It has small greenish flowers and black berries.|The alatern is a common hedging plant in some parts of Europe.|近义词:buckthorn|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
