apterous:/ˈeɪptərəs/| adj.|无翅的，无翼的|Lacking wings or winglike parts.|例句：The apterous insects crawled slowly along the ground. 无翅的昆虫沿着地面缓慢爬行。|例句：The apterous form of the aphid is usually found on the roots of plants.蚜虫的无翅形态通常在植物的根部发现。|近义词：wingless|反义词：winged, alate|词性:形容词
词源: From Greek *apteros*, from *a-* (without) + *pteron* (wing).
