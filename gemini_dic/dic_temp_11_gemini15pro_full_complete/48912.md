defrock:/dɪˈfrɒk/ (US) /diːˈfrɒk/ (UK)| vt.|褫夺圣职；剥夺…的资格|To officially remove a priest, minister, etc., from their professional position, especially because of an offense.|The priest was defrocked for misconduct.  这位牧师因行为不端被解除了圣职。|The disgraced executive was effectively defrocked by the board.  这位名誉扫地的执行官被董事会剥夺了职务。|Synonyms: unfrock, dismiss, expel, oust|Antonyms: ordain, appoint, install|Verb
