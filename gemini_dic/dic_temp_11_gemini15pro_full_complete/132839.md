noneducational:/ˌnɑːnˌedʒʊˈkeɪʃənl/
adjective
非教育性的
Not relating to or providing education; not intended to teach.
This channel shows a lot of noneducational programs.  这个频道播放很多非教育性的节目。
Playing video games all day is a noneducational activity.  整天玩电子游戏是一种非教育性的活动。
Synonyms: uneducational, non-instructive, uninstructive
Antonyms: educational, instructive, informative
词性: 形容词
