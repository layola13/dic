meditation: /ˌmedɪˈteɪʃn/ (US) /ˌmedɪˈteɪʃən/ (UK)| n. |冥想，沉思|The act of focusing one's mind for a period of time, in silence or with the aid of chanting, for religious or spiritual purposes or as a method of relaxation.|She practices daily meditation to find inner peace. 她每天练习冥想以获得内心的平静。|Synonyms: contemplation, reflection, introspection|Antonyms: distraction, agitation|noun
