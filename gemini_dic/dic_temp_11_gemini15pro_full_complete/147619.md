overplentiful: /ˌoʊvərˈplɛntɪfəl/
adjective
过剩的，过多的
Excessively plentiful; more than sufficient.  Characterized by an overabundance or surplus.
例句：The harvest was overplentiful this year, leaving farmers with surplus crops. 今年的收成过剩，农民们剩余了很多农作物。

例句：The overplentiful supply of oil led to a drop in prices.  过量供应的石油导致价格下跌。

近义词：abundant, copious, excessive, profuse, superabundant
反义词：scarce, meager, insufficient, deficient
词性：adjective
