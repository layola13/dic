liquidnesses:/lɪˈkwɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
流动性(复数)
The state of being liquid or easily convertible into cash.  This word is rarely used, and "liquidity" is preferred.
The company's liquidnesses are crucial for its short-term survival.  这家公司的流动性对其短期生存至关重要。 (This usage is unusual.  It would be more natural to say "The company's liquidity is crucial...")
liquidities, liquidity, fluidity, solvency
illiquidity, insolvency
noun
