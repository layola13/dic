contravene:/ˌkɒntrəˈviːn/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈviːn/| v. |违反，违背，抵触| to go against a law, rule, or agreement | The company contravened building regulations.  这家公司违反了建筑法规。|  The new policy contravenes the spirit of the original agreement. 新政策违背了原始协议的精神。| synonyms: violate, breach, infringe, disobey, defy | antonyms: comply with, obey, observe, uphold, conform to | verb
