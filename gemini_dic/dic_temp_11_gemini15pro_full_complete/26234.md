butterlike:/ˈbʌtərˌlaɪk/| adj.|像黄油的；光滑的；柔滑的|Resembling butter in consistency, smoothness, or richness.|The sauce had a butterlike consistency.|近义词：buttery, smooth, creamy, rich|反义词:rough, gritty, coarse|词性:形容词
Etymology: butter + like.  "Butter" comes from Old English "butere," from Latin "butyrum," and ultimately from Greek "boutyron," meaning "cow-cheese." "Like" comes from Old English "gelic," meaning "similar."
