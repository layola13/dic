noneducation:/ˌnɑːnˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃən/
n.
缺乏教育，未受教育
Lack of education; the state of being uneducated.
The cycle of poverty is often perpetuated by noneducation and lack of opportunity. 贫困的循环往往因缺乏教育和机会而持续存在。
His noneducation made it difficult for him to find a well-paying job. 他缺乏教育，很难找到一份高薪工作。
ignorance, illiteracy
education, literacy
noun
