misbalance:/ˌmɪsˈbæləns/ | n. |不平衡，失衡|A lack of balance or symmetry; a state in which things are not equal or properly proportioned. |The misbalance of power in the region led to conflict. 该地区力量的不平衡导致了冲突。|近义词: imbalance, disproportion, inequality |反义词: balance, equilibrium, symmetry |词性: noun

misbalance:/ˌmɪsˈbæləns/ | vt. |使不平衡，使失衡|To cause something to be unbalanced or out of proportion. |The sudden influx of refugees misbalanced the city's resources. 难民的突然涌入使城市资源失衡。|近义词: unbalance, upset, destabilize |反义词: balance, stabilize, equilibrate |词性: verb
