lees:/liːz/
n.
酒糟，沉淀物
the sediment from fermentation of an alcoholic beverage, especially wine
The port had a lot of lees at the bottom of the bottle. 这瓶波特酒底部有很多酒糟。
sediment, dregs, grounds
clear liquid

Etymology: From Middle English *lees, from Old French lie, from Latin lia (“lees, dregs”).
