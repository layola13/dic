What do you think about this book? 你觉得这本书怎么样？
What does she usually do on weekends? 她周末通常做什么？

What are they doing in the garden? 他们在花园里做什么？
What is he cooking for dinner tonight? 他今晚在做什么晚餐？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned so far? 你到目前为止学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? 她怎么处理那笔钱了？

What have you been working on recently? 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading lately? 他最近一直在读什么？
