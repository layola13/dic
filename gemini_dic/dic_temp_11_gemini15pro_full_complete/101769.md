irretrievabilities:/ˌɪrɪˈtriːvəbɪlətiz/
|n.|不可挽回性；无法弥补的事物|The irretrievabilities of lost time and opportunities weighed heavily on him.  逝去的时间和机会的不可挽回性沉重地压在他身上。|The speech focused on the irretrievabilities of war, the lives lost and the damage done. 演讲的重点是战争的不可挽回性，失去的生命和造成的损害。|近义词: losses, irreparabilities|反义词:recoveries, retrievals|词性:名词


Etymology:
From irretrievable + -ities.
Irretrievable: from in- (not) + retrievable.
Retrievable: from retrieve (to recover or regain) + -able (capable of being).
Retrieve: from Old French retrover (“to find again”).
