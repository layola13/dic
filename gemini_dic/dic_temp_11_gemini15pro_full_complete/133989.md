nonhistrionic:/ˌnɒnˌhɪstriˈɒnɪk/| adj.|非做作的，不戏剧化的|Not excessively dramatic or emotional; natural and unaffected.  Not inclined to theatrics or melodrama.|Her response to the news was refreshingly nonhistrionic. 她对这个消息的反应令人耳目一新，毫不做作。|Synonyms: understated, restrained, subdued, calm, composed, unemotional, natural, genuine, authentic|Antonyms: histrionic, dramatic, theatrical, melodramatic, exaggerated, affected, ostentatious|adjective
