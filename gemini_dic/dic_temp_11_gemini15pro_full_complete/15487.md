badlands:/ˈbædˌlændz/
n.|荒地|A region of barren, heavily eroded land with clay-rich soil, characterized by steep slopes, minimal vegetation, and often highly colored rock formations.|The movie was filmed in the badlands of South Dakota. 这部电影是在南达科他州的荒地拍摄的。|近义词：wasteland, desert|反义词：fertile land, grassland|词性:名词

Etymology: From bad + lands, literally "bad lands" for farming or travel, a translation of Lakota mako sica.  Originally referring to the White River Badlands of South Dakota, the term later became generalized to similar geological formations elsewhere.
