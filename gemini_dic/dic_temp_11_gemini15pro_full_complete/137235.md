nonsurvivors:/ˌnɒn.səˈvaɪ.vəz/ (US) /ˌnɒn.səˈvaɪ.vəz/ (UK)
n.|非幸存者，未幸存者|People who did not survive a particular event, such as a disaster or illness.|The nonsurvivors of the plane crash were mourned by the nation.  飞机失事的遇难者受到了全国的哀悼。|Synonyms: casualties, victims, deceased, fatalities|Antonyms: survivors|Noun
