fractioning:/ˈfrækʃənɪŋ/ | n. | 分馏，分级，分步提取 | The process of separating a mixture into its component parts, especially by a series of distillations or other physical processes. |  The fractioning of crude oil produces various useful products. 原油的分馏可以生产各种有用的产品。

The fractioning of the estate among the heirs took several months.  遗产在继承人之间的分割花费了几个月时间。| 近义词：separation, fractionation, division | 反义词：combination, unification | noun

fractionate:/ˈfrækʃəneɪt/ | v. | 分馏，分级，分步提取 | To separate a mixture into its component parts, especially by a series of distillations or other physical processes. | The chemist fractionated the solution to isolate the desired compound.  化学家分馏溶液以分离所需的化合物。

The company decided to fractionate its business into smaller, more manageable units. 公司决定将其业务分成更小、更易于管理的单元。 | 近义词：separate, divide, split | 反义词：combine, unite | verb
