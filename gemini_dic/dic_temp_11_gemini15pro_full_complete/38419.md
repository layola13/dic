concertanti:/ˌkɒntʃɛrˈtænti/ /ˌkɑːntʃərˈtænti/
| adj. |协奏曲式的；独奏乐器组与乐队竞奏的 |Of, relating to, or resembling a concerto, especially in featuring a small group of solo instruments against the full orchestra.| The concertanti parts for violin and cello were particularly challenging. 小提琴和大提琴的协奏部分特别有挑战性。|近义词：concerted, soloistic |反义词: orchestral, tutti |形容词

concertanti:/ˌkɒntʃɛrˈtænti/ /ˌkɑːntʃərˈtænti/
| n. |协奏曲；协奏曲式的乐章|A concerto or a movement in the concertante style.| The concertanti featured a brilliant young violinist. 这场协奏曲以一位才华横溢的年轻小提琴家为特色。|近义词：concerto |反义词: symphony, sonata |名词

Etymology:
From Italian *concertante*, present participle of *concertare* "to agree, arrange, compete," from *concerto* "concert, agreement." Ultimately from Latin *conserere* "to join together."
