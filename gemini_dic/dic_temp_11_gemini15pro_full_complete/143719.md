outlining:/ˈaʊtˌlaɪnɪŋ/
n.|概述；略述；轮廓描绘|The act of creating a general sketch or plan, summarizing the main points without going into detail.|He began by outlining his plans for the company. 他首先概述了他对公司的计划。|summarizing, sketching, drafting|detailing, elaborating|noun
outlining:/ˈaʊtˌlaɪnɪŋ/
adj.|概述的；略述的；轮廓描绘的|Relating to or involved in creating a general sketch or plan.|The outlining process helped him clarify his thoughts. 概述的过程帮助他理清了思路。|summarizing, sketching, drafting|detailing, elaborating|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From outline + -ing.  Outline itself comes from the Dutch uitlijnen, meaning "to align."
