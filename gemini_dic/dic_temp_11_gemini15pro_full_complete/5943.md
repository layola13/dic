amblyopia: /ˌæmbliˈoʊpiə/ (US), /ˌæmbliˈəʊpiə/ (UK)| n. |弱视|A condition in which the vision in one eye is reduced because the eye and brain are not working together properly. The eye itself looks normal, but it is not being used normally because the brain is favoring the other eye.  |例句：The child was diagnosed with amblyopia at a young age.  这个孩子在很小的时候就被诊断出患有弱视。

例句：Early detection and treatment of amblyopia are crucial for preventing permanent vision loss. 早期发现和治疗弱视对于预防永久性视力丧失至关重要。


|近义词：lazy eye,  |反义词: normal vision|词性: noun
