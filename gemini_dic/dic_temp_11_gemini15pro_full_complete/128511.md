newsworthinesses:/ˈnjuːzˌwɜːrðɪnəsɪz/ | n. | 新闻价值；报道价值 |  The quality of being worthy of or suitable for media news coverage. It refers to the criteria used to determine whether an event is significant enough to be reported to the public.  | The newsworthinesses of the event were debated by the editors.  编辑们对该事件的新闻价值进行了辩论。 | synonyms: news value, reportability, significance, importance | antonyms: insignificance, unimportance, triviality | noun
