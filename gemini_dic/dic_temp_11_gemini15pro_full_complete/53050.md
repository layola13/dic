diophantine:/ˌdaɪəˈfæntiːn/ |adj.|丢番图的，不定方程的| Relating to Diophantus of Alexandria, a Greek mathematician of the 3rd century AD, or to the type of equation studied by him, involving integer solutions. |Diophantine equations are studied in number theory.  丢番图方程在数论中被研究。| Diophantine analysis offers methods for determining if a given Diophantine equation has any integer solutions. 丢番图分析提供了确定给定的丢番图方程是否有整数解的方法。|Near synonyms: indeterminate, integer|Antonyms:  N/A (The concept doesn't lend itself to direct opposites.)|Word type: Adjective
