garbaged:/ˈɡɑːrbɪdʒd/
adjective
装满垃圾的；被丢弃的
covered or filled with garbage; treated as garbage
例句：The alley was garbaged with overflowing bins.  那条小巷堆满了垃圾，垃圾桶都溢出来了。

例句：His room was so garbaged, I couldn't find a place to sit. 他的房间堆满了垃圾，我找不到地方坐。
近义词：trashed, littered, junked
反义词：clean, tidy, spotless
词性: adjective


词源：From the noun "garbage" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives).  The word "garbage" itself likely comes from Anglo-French *garber, meaning "to equip a ship," and later came to refer to the unwanted refuse thrown overboard.
