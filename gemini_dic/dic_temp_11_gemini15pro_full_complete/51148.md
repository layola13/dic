despondently:/dɪˈspɒndəntli/ | adv. | 沮丧地，失望地 | In a despondent manner; without hope or enthusiasm. | She sighed despondently and turned away. 她沮丧地叹了口气，转过身去。|  He walked despondently towards the exit. 他垂头丧气地走向出口。 | 近义词：dejectedly, despairingly, hopelessly | 反义词：cheerfully, hopefully, optimistically | 词性: 副词
词源：Late Middle English: from Old French *despondant (present participle of despondre ), from Latin despondere ‘give up, lose heart’, from de- ‘down’ + spondere ‘promise, engage oneself’.
