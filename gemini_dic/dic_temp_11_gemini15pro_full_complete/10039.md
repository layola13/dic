aplacophora:/ˌeɪpləˈkɒfərə/ /ˌeɪpləˈkɑːfərə/
n.
无板纲
A class of small, deep-sea mollusks lacking a shell and characterized by a worm-like body covered in calcareous spicules.
Aplacophora are a group of marine mollusks that lack a shell.  无板纲是一类缺乏外壳的海洋软体动物。
Aplacophora inhabit deep-sea environments. 无板纲动物栖息于深海环境。
