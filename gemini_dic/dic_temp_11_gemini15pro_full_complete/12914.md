assurgent:/əˈsɜːrdʒənt/| adj.|叛乱的；起义的；暴动的|Rising in revolt; rebellious.|The assurgent forces gained control of the city.  起义军控制了这座城市。|近义词：rebellious, revolutionary, mutinous|反义词: loyal, obedient|adjective
