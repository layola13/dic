glassinesses:/ˈɡlæsɪnɪsɪz/
noun
光滑度；光泽；透明度
the quality of being glassy or resembling glass; smoothness and shininess
The glassinesses of the polished marble floors were breathtaking.  抛光大理石地板的光滑度令人叹为观止。
glossiness, smoothness, shininess
roughness, dullness
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From glassy + -ness + -es (plural suffix)
Glassy comes from glass (Old English glæs) + -y (adjective suffix)
