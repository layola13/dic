florae:/ˈflɔːriː/
n.
植物群
the plants of a particular region or period
例句：The florae of the Amazon rainforest are incredibly diverse. 亚马逊雨林的植物群极其多样。

例句：Studying the florae of ancient times can tell us a lot about the climate. 研究古代的植物群可以告诉我们很多关于气候的信息。

近义词：vegetation, plants
反义词：fauna (动物群)
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *florae*, genitive singular or nominative plural of *flora* ("goddess of flowers, flowers, plants"). Ultimately from Proto-Italic *flōsā*, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰleh₃- (“to blossom, bloom”).
