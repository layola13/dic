overmoralistic: /ˌoʊvərˌmɔːrəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
过分道德化的；过分拘泥于道德的
Excessively concerned with morals; tending to judge others too harshly on moral grounds.
Her overmoralistic attitude made her unpopular. 她过分拘泥于道德的态度让她不受欢迎。
He gave an overmoralistic lecture about the dangers of drinking. 他做了一个关于饮酒危害的过分说教的演讲。
近义词: moralistic, puritanical, self-righteous, sanctimonious
反义词: amoral, immoral, unethical, permissive, lenient
词性: adjective

词源: over- + moralistic
"over-"  prefix meaning "excessively," "too much"
"moralistic" adjective relating to or characteristic of a moralist (a person who teaches or advocates morality)
