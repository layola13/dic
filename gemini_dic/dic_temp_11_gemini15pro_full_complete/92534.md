hypsiloid:/ˈhɪpsɪlɔɪd/| adj. n.| 舌骨的，类舌骨的；舌骨|Relating to or resembling the hyoid bone; a hyoid bone. The hyoid bone is a U-shaped bone in the neck that supports the tongue.|例句：The hypsiloid apparatus is a complex structure.  舌骨器是一个复杂的结构。
