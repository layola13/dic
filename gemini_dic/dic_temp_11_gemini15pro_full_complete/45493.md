curding:/ˈkɜːrdɪŋ/| n. |凝乳，凝结|The process of milk turning into curds.  牛奶变成凝乳的过程。|The curding of the milk happened too quickly. 牛奶凝结得太快了。|近义词：coagulation, thickening|反义词: thinning, liquefaction|noun

curdle:/ˈkɜːrdl/| v. |凝结，使凝固|To cause (milk, etc.) to separate into curds and whey. 使（牛奶等）分离成凝乳和乳清。|The lemon juice curdled the milk. 柠檬汁使牛奶凝结了。|近义词：coagulate, clot|反义词: liquefy, dissolve|verb
