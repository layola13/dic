myxoma:/mɪkˈsoʊmə/ | n. | 粘液瘤 | A benign tumor derived from connective tissue, especially of mucous membranes, and containing gelatinous or mucoid material. |  A myxoma is a rare type of tumor that most commonly occurs in the heart. 粘液瘤是一种罕见的肿瘤，最常发生在心脏。| synonyms: tumor, neoplasm | antonyms: healthy tissue | noun

myxoma:/mɪkˈsoʊmə/ | n. | 多发性粘液瘤 | Myxomatosis, a highly infectious and usually fatal viral disease of rabbits, characterized by the formation of swellings in the skin and mucous membranes, and often accompanied by inflammation of the eyes and lungs. | Myxomatosis decimated the rabbit population in Australia. 多发性粘液瘤使澳大利亚的兔子数量锐减。 | synonyms: myxomatosis | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but could consider "rabbit health" in a specific context) | noun
