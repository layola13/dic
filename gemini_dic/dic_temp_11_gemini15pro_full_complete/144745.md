over-attached: /ˌoʊvərəˈtætʃt/ | adj. | 过度依恋的，过于依赖的 | Excessively attached or dependent, often to a person. This describes someone who has difficulty separating themselves emotionally from another person and may exhibit clingy or needy behavior. |  She's so over-attached to her boyfriend; she can't do anything without him. 她太依赖她男朋友了；没有他她什么都做不了。


He was over-attached to his mother as a child, which made it hard for him to form independent relationships. 他小时候过度依恋他的母亲，这使得他难以建立独立的人际关系。| 近义词: clingy, needy, dependent | 反义词: independent, detached, self-sufficient | 词性: 形容词
