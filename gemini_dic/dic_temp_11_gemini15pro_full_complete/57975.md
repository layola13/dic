drive-by:/ˈdraɪv baɪ/ | n. | 飞车射击，路过射击；(驾车经过时)顺便拜访 | A shooting committed from a moving vehicle. Also, a brief, casual visit. |  The gang was notorious for their drive-bys.  这帮派因飞车枪击而臭名昭著。| synonyms: shooting (from a vehicle), ambush, hit (slang) | antonyms: planned attack, prolonged visit | noun

drive-by:/ˈdraɪv baɪ/ | adj. |  飞车射击的，驾车经过时的 | Done from or while passing by in a vehicle. |  The victim was killed in a drive-by shooting. 受害者在一次飞车枪击中丧生。 | synonyms: cursory, passing, fleeting | antonyms: stationary, deliberate, prolonged | adjective
