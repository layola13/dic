briskest:/ˈbrɪskɪst/| adj.|最轻快的，最活泼的，最兴旺的|Superlative form of brisk: most brisk.  Characterized by speed, liveliness, and energy.|The briskest trade was done in the early morning hours.|近义词：liveliest, fastest, most active, most energetic|反义词：slowest, dullest, slackest|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *brisk*, from Old French *brisque* "nimble, lively, quick," probably of Germanic origin.
