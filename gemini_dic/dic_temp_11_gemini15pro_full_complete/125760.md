murderers:/ˈmɜːrdərərz/ (US) /ˈmɜːdədərz/ (UK)| n.| 杀人犯；凶手 |People who commit murder are called murderers. 犯谋杀罪的人被称为杀人犯。|The police are searching for the murderers. 警察正在搜捕凶手。|近义词：killers, assassins, homicides|反义词: victim, protector, savior | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *murd(e)reres*, from Old French *murd(e)reor*, from *murdre* ("murder") + *-eor* (agent suffix).  Ultimately from the Proto-Germanic *murþrą* (“murder, secret killing”).
