littoral:/ˈlɪtərəl/ (US), /lɪˈtɔːrəl/ (UK)| adj. n.| 沿海的，海岸的；海岸地区，沿岸地区 |Relating to or situated on the shore of a sea or lake.| The littoral states of the Mediterranean have a long history of trade. 地中海沿岸国家有着悠久的贸易历史。|近义词：coastal, shoreline, seaside|反义词：inland, interior|词性: adjective, noun

littorals:/ˈlɪtərəlz/ (US), /lɪˈtɔːrəlz/ (UK)| n.| 海岸地区，沿岸地区（复数）|The shores of a sea or lake, especially the regions affected by tides.|  Many seabirds nest in the littorals. 许多海鸟在沿海地区筑巢。|近义词：coasts, shorelines, seashores|反义词：hinterlands|词性: noun


词源: From Latin *litoralis*, from litus (“shore”).
