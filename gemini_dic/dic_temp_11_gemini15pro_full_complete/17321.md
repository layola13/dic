be-as-good-as:/biː æz ɡʊd æz/ | phrase | 几乎一样；等于；和...一样好 |  Means almost the same as, or equal to. It implies a very high degree of similarity or equivalence. | The contract is as good as signed.  合同差不多已经签了。| synonyms: practically, virtually, almost, nearly, tantamount to | antonyms:  nowhere near, far from | phrase
