cherubically:/ˌtʃɛrʊˈbɪkəli/ | adv. | 天使般地，天真无邪地 | In a manner resembling a cherub, especially in innocence or sweetness. | The child smiled cherubically at his mother.  孩子天真无邪地对着妈妈微笑。| 近义词：angelically, innocently, sweetly | 反义词：diabolically, wickedly | 词性: 副词

词源:
From cherubic + -ally.  Cherubic comes from cherub (a type of angel) + -ic.  Ultimately from the Hebrew word *kərūb*.
