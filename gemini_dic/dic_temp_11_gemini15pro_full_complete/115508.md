many-a-time:/ˈmeni ə ˈtaɪm/ | adv. | 屡次，多次；常常 | Many times; frequently. It emphasizes the repeated occurrences of something. |  I've told you many-a-time not to leave the door unlocked. 我已经多次告诉你不要不锁门。| Synonyms: often, frequently, repeatedly, many times, time and again, time after time | Antonyms: rarely, seldom, infrequently, hardly ever | adverb
