cryonicses:/kraɪˈɑːnɪksiːz/
noun
人体冷冻法（复数）
The practice of preserving legally dead people at very low temperatures with the hope that future scientific advances will allow them to be revived.
Some people believe cryonicses offers a chance at immortality.  有些人相信人体冷冻技术提供了一个永生的机会。
Cryonicses is a controversial practice, with many questioning its scientific validity. 人体冷冻法是一项备受争议的做法，许多人质疑其科学有效性。
近义词: cryopreservation
反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑 burial 埋葬, cremation 火葬)
词性: noun
