doubleclick: /ˈdʌbəlklɪk/ (US), /ˈdʌbəlklɪk/ (UK)| v.|双击|To press a computer mouse button twice quickly.|Example: Doubleclick the file to open it.  双击文件即可打开它。|近义词: double-click|反义词: single click|词性: 动词


词源:  "double" + "click".  "Double" means twice, and "click" refers to the sound made by a mouse button.
