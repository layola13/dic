afrite:/əˈfriːt/ | n. | 火怪，恶魔，妖精 | A powerful and often evil jinn or demon in Arabian and Muslim mythology. | Example: The afrite emerged from the lamp, its eyes burning with malice. 火怪从灯里冒出来，眼里燃烧着恶意。

Example:  Legends speak of afrites guarding ancient treasures. 传说中，火怪守护着古老的宝藏。 | Near Synonyms:  jinn, demon, genie, ifrit, shaitan | Antonyms: angel, deity, god | Noun
