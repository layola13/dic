outvying:/ˌaʊtˈvaɪɪŋ/
verb
胜过，超过
surpassing or exceeding someone in achievement or excellence.
He was always trying to outvie his brothers in sports.  他总是试图在运动方面超过他的兄弟们。
The two companies are constantly outvying each other with new products. 这两家公司不断推出新产品，互相竞争。
近义词: surpass, exceed, outdo, outstrip, eclipse
反义词: lose to, be inferior to
词性: verb (动词)
