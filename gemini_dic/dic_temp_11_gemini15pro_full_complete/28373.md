carbamine:/ˈkɑːrbəmiːn/| n.|氨基甲酸|A compound containing the group -NHCOO-. Specifically, an unstable derivative of carbamic acid formed by the reaction of carbon dioxide with an amino compound.|Hemoglobin can bind with carbon dioxide to form carbaminohemoglobin.|Synonym: carbamoylic acid|Antonym: N/A|noun

carbamic:/kɑːrˈbæmɪk/| adj.|氨基甲酸的|Relating to or derived from carbamic acid.|Carbamic acid is an unstable compound.|Synonym: carbamoyl|Antonym: N/A|adjective
