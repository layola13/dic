morose:/məˈroʊs/ | adj. | 郁闷的，闷闷不乐的 | Sullen and ill-tempered.  Characterized by or expressing gloom. | He was morose and withdrawn after losing his job. 他失业后变得郁闷寡言。| synonyms: sullen, gloomy, sulky, glum, dour, saturnine | antonyms: cheerful, happy, jovial, optimistic | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *mōrōsus* (“peevish, fretful”), from *mōs* (“manner, custom, humor”).
