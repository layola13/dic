outpopulating:/ˌaʊtˈpɒpjʊleɪtɪŋ/
verb
生育数量超过
increasing in population more quickly than (another area or group)
例句：The city is outpopulating its suburbs.  这个城市的人口增长速度超过了它的郊区。

例句：Developing countries are outpopulating developed nations at an alarming rate. 发展中国家的人口增长速度超过发达国家，令人担忧。

近义词：outnumbering, exceeding, surpassing
反义词：depopulating, declining
词性：v.
