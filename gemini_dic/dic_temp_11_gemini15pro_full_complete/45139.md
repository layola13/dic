cuddlers:/ˈkʌdlərz/
n.
拥抱者；喜欢搂抱的人；爱依偎的人
People who like to cuddle; those who frequently engage in cuddling.
The kittens are real cuddlers and love to snuggle up together. 这些小猫是真正的爱依偎者，喜欢依偎在一起。
近义词：snugglers, huggers
反义词: loners, aloof individuals
词性: 名词(复数)

cuddle:/ˈkʌdl/
v.
拥抱；搂抱；依偎
To hold close in one's arms; to embrace affectionately.
She cuddled the baby close. 她紧紧地抱着婴儿。
近义词：hug, snuggle, embrace
反义词: repel, push away
词性: 动词

cuddly:/ˈkʌdli/
adj.
令人想拥抱的；爱依偎的；让人想搂抱的
Inviting cuddling; soft and warm.
The teddy bear was soft and cuddly. 泰迪熊柔软可爱，让人想抱。
近义词：huggable, snuggly
反义词: cold, uninviting
词性: 形容词
