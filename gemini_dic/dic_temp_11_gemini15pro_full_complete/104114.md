jumpier:/ˈdʒʌmpiər/ (US), /ˈdʒʌmpɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更神经质的，更易受惊的|Comparative adjective of "jumpy."  It describes someone or something that is more nervous, restless, or easily startled than before or than something else.|The dog was jumpier than usual after the fireworks.  烟火结束后，这只狗比平时更神经质。|The horse became jumpier as the storm approached. 随着暴风雨的临近，马变得更加焦躁不安。|Synonyms:  more nervous, more restless, more skittish, edgier|Antonyms: calmer, more relaxed, more composed|adjective
