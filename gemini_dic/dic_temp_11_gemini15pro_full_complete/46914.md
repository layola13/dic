darknesses:/ˈdɑːrknɪsɪz/
n.
黑暗，阴暗；无知，愚昧
The plural form of "darkness," referring to multiple instances or areas of literal or metaphorical darkness.
例句：The cave was filled with deep darknesses.  这个洞穴里充满了深深的黑暗。

例句：The darknesses of ignorance and superstition can be dispelled by education.  教育可以驱散无知和迷信的黑暗。
近义词：obscurities, gloom, shadows, blacknesses
反义词：lights, brightness, knowledge, enlightenment
词性：名词(plural)

darkness:/ˈdɑːrknəs/
n.
黑暗，阴暗；无知，愚昧
The absence of light; a state of being dark, either literally or metaphorically.  缺乏光线；黑暗的状态，字面意义上或比喻意义上。
例句：The power outage plunged the city into darkness.  停电使城市陷入一片黑暗。

例句：He emerged from the darkness of depression. 他从抑郁的黑暗中走了出来。
近义词：gloom, shadow, blackness
反义词：light, brightness, knowledge
词性：名词(singular)
