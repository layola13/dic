arrestment:/əˈrɛstmənt/
n.
扣押，扣留；(尤指苏格兰法律)扣押令
The legal seizure of someone's property or money, especially according to Scottish law.
例句：The court issued an arrestment of his wages. 法院签发了扣押他工资的命令。

例句：The arrestment prevented him from accessing his funds. 扣押令使他无法动用自己的资金。

近义词：seizure, attachment, garnishment
反义词：release

词性: 名词
