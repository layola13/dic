interdepartmentally:/ˌɪntərˌdiːpɑːrtˈmɛntəli/ | adv. |部门间地，跨部门地 | In a way that involves different departments of an organization or institution. | The issue was discussed interdepartmentally.  这个问题在部门间进行了讨论。| Example:  Information is shared interdepartmentally to ensure everyone is aware of project updates.  信息在部门间共享，以确保每个人都了解项目的最新进展。 | Synonyms: across departments, between departments, among departments | Antonyms: within a department, intradepartmentally | word type:adverb
