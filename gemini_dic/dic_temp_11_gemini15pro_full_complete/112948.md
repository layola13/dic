lugubriosity:/ˌluːɡʊˈbriːəsɪti/ | n. | 悲哀，忧郁 |  The state of being lugubrious; gloominess or mournful. |  The poet's lugubriosity permeated his later works, reflecting his personal tragedies. 诗人的悲哀情绪贯穿了他后期的作品，反映了他的个人悲剧。| synonyms: gloom, melancholy, sadness, dejection, mournfulness | antonyms: cheerfulness, joy, happiness, lightheartedness | noun

lugubrious:/ləˈɡuːbriəs/ | adj. | 悲哀的，忧郁的 | Looking or sounding sad and dismal. | He had a lugubrious expression on his face. 他脸上带着忧郁的表情。| synonyms: mournful, gloomy, melancholic, somber, doleful | antonyms: cheerful, happy, joyful, merry | adjective
