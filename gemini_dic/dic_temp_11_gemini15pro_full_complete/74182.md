four-wheeler:/ˈfɔːrˌwiːlər/
noun
四轮车；四轮驱动车
A vehicle with four wheels, especially an all-terrain vehicle (ATV) or a small car.
He drove his four-wheeler through the muddy fields. 他开着他的四轮车穿过泥泞的田野。
The kids were riding their four-wheelers in the woods. 孩子们在树林里骑着他们的四轮车。
Synonyms: ATV, quad bike, 4x4, off-road vehicle
Antonyms: two-wheeler, bicycle, motorcycle
