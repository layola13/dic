consented:/kənˈsɛntɪd/
verb
同意，准许，允许
agreed to do something or allowed something to happen
She consented to marry him. 她同意嫁给他。
synonyms: agreed, acquiesced, acceded, complied
antonyms: refused, declined, objected, dissented
词性: 动词
