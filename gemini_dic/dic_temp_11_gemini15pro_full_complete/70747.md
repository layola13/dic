fine:/faɪn/| adj.|好的，精细的，晴朗的，健康的|Of high quality; very good. / Made of small particles. / (of the weather) bright and clear. / In good health; well.|例句：The restaurant serves fine wines. 这家餐厅提供优质的葡萄酒。|近义词：excellent, superb, great, good, well, healthy, delicate, thin, subtle|反义词：poor, bad, ill, sick, thick, coarse|词性:形容词

fine:/faɪn/| n.|罚款|A sum of money exacted as a penalty for an offense or dereliction.|例句：He had to pay a fine for speeding. 他因超速行驶不得不支付罚款。|近义词：penalty, sanction|反义词:reward|词性:名词

fine:/faɪn/| v.|处以罚金|To punish by a fine.|例句：The judge fined him $500. 法官对他处以500美元的罚款。|近义词：penalize, sanction|反义词:reward|词性:动词
