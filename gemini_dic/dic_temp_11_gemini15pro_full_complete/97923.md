innocents:/ˈɪnəsnts/ | n. |无辜的人；天真的人；单纯的人 |People who are not guilty of a crime or wrongdoing; people who are naive and inexperienced. |The war claimed the lives of many innocents.  战争夺去了许多无辜者的生命。

|The innocents were spared.  无辜的人得到了赦免。| 近义词:naive, ingenuous, guileless, unsophisticated, unworldly| 反义词:guilty, culpable, experienced, worldly, sophisticated | noun


innocent:/ˈɪnəsnt/ | adj. |无辜的；清白的；天真的；无恶意的|Not guilty of a crime or wrongdoing; naive and lacking experience of the world. |He was found innocent of all charges. 他被判所有罪名均不成立。

|She has an innocent face. 她有一张天真的脸。| 近义词:blameless, guiltless, harmless, pure | 反义词:guilty, culpable, harmful, corrupt | adjective
