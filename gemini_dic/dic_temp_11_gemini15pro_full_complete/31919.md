cheffing:/ˈʃɛfɪŋ/
noun
担任主厨的工作;掌厨
the job or act of being a chef
Cheffing is a demanding but rewarding career.  担任厨师是一项要求很高但回报也很高的职业。
He enjoys the cheffing, especially creating new dishes. 他喜欢掌厨，尤其是创造新菜肴。
近义词:cookery, culinary arts
反义词:N/A
词性:n.

词源:  Derived from the noun "chef" + the suffix "-ing" which transforms a noun into a verb or gerund.  "Chef" itself comes from the French word "chef de cuisine," meaning "head of the kitchen."
