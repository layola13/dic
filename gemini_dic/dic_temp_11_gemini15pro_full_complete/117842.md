mellitic:/məˈlɪtɪk/
adjective
蜜石的；蜜酸的
Relating to or derived from mellitic acid.
Mellitic acid is a benzenehexacarboxylic acid. 蜜酸是一种苯六甲酸。
The mellitic anhydride is a versatile precursor to organic semiconductors. 蜜石酸酐是有机半导体的多功能前体。
近义词: honeyed (figurative sense of sweetness), saccharine (figurative sense of sweetness)
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) sour, bitter, acrid (for taste), unsweet
词性: adjective
