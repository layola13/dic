dewiring:/diːˈwaɪərɪŋ/
verb
拆线；拆除线路；拆除电线
The process of removing wires, typically from a circuit, electronic device, or building.
The electrician is dewiring the old house before the renovation.  电工在翻修前正在拆除旧房子的电线。
The bomb squad carefully dewired the explosive device.  拆弹小组小心翼翼地拆除了爆炸装置的线路。
近义词:disconnect, unwire
反义词:wire, connect
词性:v.
