distention:/dɪˈstɛnʃən/| n. |膨胀，扩张，肿胀|The state of being stretched, enlarged, or swollen beyond normal limits. |The abdominal distention was caused by gas. 腹部膨胀是由气体引起的。|The patient experienced distention of the abdomen after surgery. 病人术后出现腹部膨胀。|近义词：expansion, swelling, inflation|反义词: contraction, compression|词性:名词

distended:/dɪˈstɛndɪd/| adj. |膨胀的，扩张的|Stretched, enlarged, or swollen beyond normal limits. |The patient's stomach appeared distended. 病人的胃看起来胀胀的。|The veins in his legs were distended. 他腿上的静脉扩张了。|近义词：expanded, swollen, inflated|反义词: contracted, compressed|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *distentiōn-, from Latin distentus, past participle of distendere "to stretch out, swell, inflate," from dis- "apart, asunder" + tendere "to stretch."
