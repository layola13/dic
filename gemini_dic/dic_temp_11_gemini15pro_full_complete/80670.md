gordy:/ˈɡɔːrdi/ | adj. | 粗壮的；结实的；坚固的 |  (informal) Strong and sturdy; tough. | That gordy oak tree has withstood many storms. 那棵粗壮的橡树经受住了许多风暴。| synonyms: sturdy, robust, stout, tough, hardy | antonyms: weak, frail, delicate | adjective
