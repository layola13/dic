chlorimetry:/ˌklɔːrɪˈmɪtri/ | n. | 氯量法，氯量滴定法 | The process of quantitatively determining the amount of chlorine, chloride ions, or other related substances in a sample, typically by titration. | 例句：Chlorimetry is a common method for determining the chlorine content in drinking water.  氯量法是测定饮用水中氯含量的常用方法。

例句：The lab used chlorimetry to analyze the concentration of chloride in the solution.  实验室使用氯量法分析溶液中氯离子的浓度。

近义词：titration, volumetric analysis | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists as it's a specific analytical method.) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *chlorométrie*,  from *chloro-* (chlorine) + *-métrie* (-metry, meaning measurement). Ultimately from Greek *khlōros* (pale green) and *metron* (measure).
