megaphonic:/ˌmɛɡəˈfɒnɪk/ (US) /ˌmɛɡəˈfɒnɪk/ (UK)| adj.|扩音的；声音洪亮的|Relating to or resembling a megaphone; loud and amplified.|His megaphonic voice boomed across the square. 他的声音像扩音器一样响彻广场。|The announcement was delivered in a megaphonic tone.  公告是以扩音的语气发布的。|近义词：loud, amplified, booming, resounding, thundering|反义词：quiet, soft, hushed, muted|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From mega- + -phonic, from Greek phōnē "sound, voice."
