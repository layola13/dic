gatekeeper:/ˈɡeɪtˌkiːpər/| n.|守门人，看门人；把关人|A person who controls access to something.|The gatekeeper wouldn't let us in without a ticket.  守门人不让我们没有票就进去。|The editor acts as a gatekeeper, deciding which stories are published. 编辑充当把关人的角色，决定哪些故事可以发表。|Synonyms: guard, custodian, watchman, controller|Antonyms: intruder, trespasser|noun

gatekeeper:/ˈɡeɪtˌkiːpər/| n.|把关者，看门狗（指严格控制信息或访问的人或事物）|A person or thing that controls access to something, often in a strict or overly cautious way.|The strict guidelines act as a gatekeeper, preventing many deserving applicants from receiving funding.  严格的指导方针就像一个看门狗，阻止了许多合格的申请者获得资金。|He saw himself as a gatekeeper of traditional values. 他视自己为传统价值观的守护者。|Synonyms: censor, regulator, filter|Antonyms: facilitator, enabler|noun
