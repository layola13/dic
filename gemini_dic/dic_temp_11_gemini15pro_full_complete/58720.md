dunces:/ˈdʌnsɪz/
dunce: /dʌns/ | n. |笨蛋，傻瓜|A stupid or slow-witted person. |He was treated like a dunce in school. 他在学校被当作笨蛋对待。|Synonyms: idiot, fool, dolt, blockhead, simpleton |Antonyms: genius, intellectual, savant| Noun
dunces (plural): /ˈdʌnsɪz/ | n. |笨蛋们，傻瓜们|Plural form of "dunce," referring to a group of stupid or slow-witted people. |The dunces were always the last to finish the test. 这些笨蛋总是最后完成测试。|Synonyms: idiots, fools, dolts, blockheads, simpletons |Antonyms: geniuses, intellectuals, savants| Noun
