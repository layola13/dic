circumflexes: /ˈsɜːrkəmˌfleksɪz/ | n. | 弯曲符号，抑扬符 | A mark (^) placed over a vowel in some languages to indicate pronunciation, often a combination of rising and falling tone or length.  It can also indicate a contracted or omitted letter. | The word "crèche" uses a circumflex.  这个单词“crèche”使用了抑扬符。|
