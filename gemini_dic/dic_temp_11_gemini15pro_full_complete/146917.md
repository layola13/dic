overintellectualization: /ˌoʊvərˌɪntɪˌlɛktʃuəlɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度理智化；过度思辨 | The act of thinking about something too much and making it more complicated than it needs to be.  It often involves analyzing a situation or problem beyond what is necessary or helpful. |  He suffered from overintellectualization, analyzing every detail of his relationships until they fell apart. 他过度理智化，分析了他关系中的每一个细节，直到它们破裂。 | Overthinking, analyzing excessively, rumination |  Intuition, gut feeling, simplification | noun
