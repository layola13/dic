overbriefed: /ˌoʊvərˈbriːft/
adjective
过度介绍情况的；被给予过多信息的
Having been given too much information in a briefing or instructions.
The lawyers were so overbriefed that they struggled to focus on the key arguments. 律师们被告知的信息太多，以至于难以集中精力于关键论点。
The overbriefed team felt overwhelmed and confused.  这个被过度介绍情况的团队感到不知所措和困惑。
近义词: overinformed, overloaded
反义词: underbriefed, uninformed
词性: adjective

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "briefed" (past participle of "brief," meaning to instruct or inform concisely)
over- (prefix): 源自古英语 ofer-，意为“在...之上，超过，过度”。
brief (verb): 源自古法语 brief, bref，意为“短的，简洁的”，最终源自拉丁语 brevis.
