admiralissimo:/ˌædmərəˈlɪsɪmoʊ/
n.
最高统帅，海军元帅
supreme commander of a combined fleet; commander in chief of a navy; grand admiral
Example:  The admiralissimo directed the entire naval operation. 最高统帅指挥了整个海军作战。

Example:  Historically, the title of admiralissimo was bestowed upon only the most distinguished naval officers.  历史上，只有最杰出的海军军官才能被授予最高统帅的头衔。
近义词:commander in chief, grand admiral, supreme commander
反义词:subordinate, junior officer
词性:名词
