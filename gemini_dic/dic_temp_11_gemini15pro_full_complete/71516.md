flannelling:/ˈflænəlɪŋ/ | n. | 闲聊；胡扯；敷衍 | The act of talking at length in a vague or evasive way, often to avoid giving a direct answer or to fill time. | He was just flannelling to avoid answering the difficult question. 他只是在胡扯以避免回答难题。| synonyms: waffling, rambling, hedging, equivocating | antonyms:  being direct, being forthright | noun

flannelling:/ˈflænəlɪŋ/ | v. | 闲聊；胡扯；敷衍 | To talk at length in a vague or evasive way. | The politician flannelled on for hours without actually saying anything of substance.  这位政客胡扯了几个小时，实际上什么也没说。| synonyms: waffle, ramble, hedge, equivocate | antonyms: be direct, be forthright | verb
