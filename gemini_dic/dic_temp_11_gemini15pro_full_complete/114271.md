mained:/meɪnd/ | v. | 伤残；使残废；使丧失主要效用 | to disable or cripple someone or something, or to cause it to lose its main effectiveness |  The accident mained him for life.  这起事故使他终身残疾。| 近义词: disable, cripple, incapacitate | 反义词: heal, cure | verb

词源:
From Middle English *mainen, *maymen (“to maim”), from Old French mahaignier, mahainier, probably from a Vulgar Latin *mahainare. Compare Italian magagnare.
