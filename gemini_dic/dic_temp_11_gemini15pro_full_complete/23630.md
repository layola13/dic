branchiostegal: /ˌbræŋkiˈɒstɪɡəl/ | adj. | 鳃盖的，鳃条的 | Relating to the branchiostegal membrane or rays, which are bony or cartilaginous structures supporting the gill membranes of fishes and some amphibians. | The branchiostegal rays of this fish are quite prominent.  这条鱼的鳃条骨非常突出。| Synonym:  hyoid (related to hyoid arch which supports branchiostegals), opercular (related to the operculum which covers the gills) | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-branchiostegal") | adjective
