overobesenesses:/ˌoʊvərˌoʊˈbiːsɪtɪz/
n.
极度肥胖（的状态）
The state of being extremely obese, beyond the typical definition of obesity.  This word emphasizes an excessive degree of obesity.
Due to the prevalence of highly processed foods, overobesenesses are becoming a more common health concern. 由于高加工食品的盛行，极度肥胖正成为一个越来越普遍的健康问题。
There are significant health risks associated with overobesenesses.  极度肥胖会带来严重的健康风险。
近义词: morbid obesity, extreme obesity
反义词: underweight, thinness
词性: noun

词源:
"Over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "obese" (adjective meaning "grossly fat or overweight") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality) + "-es" (plural suffix)
