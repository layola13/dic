nonauthenticity:/ˌnɒnˌɔːθɛnˈtɪsɪti/
n.|非真实性，不真实，不地道|The nonauthenticity of the document was immediately apparent. 这份文件的非真实性立刻显现出来。|
近义词：falseness, spuriousness, artificiality, inauthenticity
反义词：authenticity, genuineness, realness
词性:noun

词源:  non- (not) + authenticity (from authentic, from Late Latin authenticus "authoritative," from Greek authentikos "genuine, original, first-hand," from authentes "one acting on one's own authority," from autos "self" + hentes "doer.")
