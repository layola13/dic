intimidates: /ɪnˈtɪmɪdeɪts/
verb
恐吓，威胁
to frighten or threaten someone, usually in order to persuade them to do something that you want them to do
His aggressive manner intimidates me. 他的咄咄逼人的态度恐吓了我。
frightens, threatens, scares, bullies
encourages, supports, reassures
v.
