calipering:/ˈkælɪpərɪŋ/
n.
卡尺测量；测径
The act of measuring something with calipers.
The engineer was calipering the diameter of the pipe. 工程师正在用卡尺测量管子的直径。

The machinist checked the dimensions of the part by calipering it.  机械师用卡尺检查了零件的尺寸。

measurement, gauging
estimation, guessing
noun
