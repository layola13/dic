goose-stepping:/ˈɡuːsˌstɛpɪŋ/
n.
鹅步，正步走
A stiff-legged marching style in which the legs are swung high and straight with each step.  Used especially in military parades, particularly associated with Nazi Germany.
The soldiers were goose-stepping across the parade ground. 士兵们在阅兵场上正步走。
近义词:marching, parading
反义词:walking, strolling
词性:noun

goose-stepping:/ˈɡuːsˌstɛpɪŋ/
adj.
正步走的
Characterized by goose-stepping.
The goose-stepping soldiers presented a formidable image. 正步走的士兵展现出一幅令人敬畏的形象。
近义词:marching, parading
反义词:walking, strolling
词性:adjective

词源：
From goose +‎ stepping, from the resemblance of the stiff-legged gait to a goose's walk.
来自goose（鹅）+ stepping（步伐），因为这种僵硬的步态类似于鹅的行走方式。
