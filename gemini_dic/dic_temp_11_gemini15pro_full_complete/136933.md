nonspaciousnesses:/ˌnɒnˈspeɪʃəsnəsɪz/
n.
缺乏空间；狭窄
The nonspaciousnesses of the apartment made it difficult to live comfortably.
公寓的狭窄使得舒适地生活变得困难。
This design emphasizes the nonspaciousnesses of the room.
这个设计突出了房间的狭小。
Near synonyms:  crampedness, confinement, tightness
Antonyms: spaciousness, roominess, expanse
noun

Etymology:  non- (not) + spacious + -ness (suffix forming nouns of state or quality)
