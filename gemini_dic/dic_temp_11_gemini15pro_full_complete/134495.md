nonleaking:/ˌnɑːnˈliːkɪŋ/
adjective
无泄漏的
Preventing leaks; not allowing liquid or gas to escape.
The researchers developed a nonleaking seal for the underwater vehicle. 研究人员为水下航行器开发了一种无泄漏的密封装置。
The new container is guaranteed to be nonleaking.  这个新容器保证不会漏。
近义词: leakproof, airtight, watertight, sealed
反义词: leaking, permeable, porous
词性: adjective
