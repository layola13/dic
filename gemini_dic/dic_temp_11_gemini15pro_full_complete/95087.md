inalienably:/ɪnˈeɪliənəbli/
adv.
不可剥夺地
cannot be taken away or given away
例句：Freedom of speech is an inalienably right. 言论自由是一项不可剥夺的权利。

例句：These rights are inalienably vested in the people. 这些权利不可剥夺地属于人民。

近义词：inherently, intrinsically, absolutely
反义词：alienably, conditionally
词性：副词
