amoeba:/əˈmiːbə/ | n. | 阿米巴，变形虫 | A single-celled animal that catches food and moves about by extending fingerlike projections of protoplasm. Amebas are either free-living in damp environments or parasitic. |  The amoeba changed its shape as it moved. 变形虫移动时改变了它的形状。| synonym: protozoan | antonym: multicellular organism | noun

amoeba:/əˈmiːbə/ | n. | 变形虫 |  A microscopic single-celled organism which has no fixed shape and can change the shape of its body. | The student observed the amoeba under the microscope. 学生在显微镜下观察变形虫。 | synonym: protozoon | antonym:  (No direct antonym, but consider "metazoan" for multicellular organisms)  | noun
