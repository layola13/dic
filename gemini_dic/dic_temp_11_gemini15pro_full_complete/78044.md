genuinely:/ˈdʒɛnjuɪnli/| adv.|真正地，真诚地|In a genuine or sincere manner; truly.|She genuinely cared about her students. 她真心关心她的学生。|例句：He was genuinely happy to see her. 他见到她真心高兴。|例句：She spoke genuinely about her concerns. 她真诚地谈论了她的担忧。|近义词：truly, sincerely, honestly, authentically|反义词：falsely, insincerely, artificially|词性:副词

词源:
From genuine + -ly.  Genuine comes from Latin *genuinus "innate, native, natural," from gignere "to beget."
