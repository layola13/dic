Iberia: /aɪˈbɪəriə/ /aɪˈbɪriə/
n.
伊比利亚，伊比利亚半岛
A peninsula in southwestern Europe, comprising Spain and Portugal.
The Iberian Peninsula is a beautiful place to visit. 伊比利亚半岛是一个美丽的旅游胜地。
The wines of Iberia are world-renowned. 伊比利亚的葡萄酒世界闻名。
Synonyms: Iberian Peninsula
Antonyms: N/A
