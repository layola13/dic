lutheranisms:/ˈluːθərənɪzəmz/
n.
路德教义；路德宗的教义和实践
The doctrines and practices of Lutheranism.
Lutheranisms emphasize the importance of scripture and faith. 路德教义强调圣经和信仰的重要性。
The various Lutheranisms across the globe share core beliefs but differ in some practices. 全球各地的各种路德教义都有共同的核心信仰，但在某些实践上有所不同。
近义词:Lutheran doctrines, Lutheran beliefs
反义词:Calvinism, Catholicism
词性:Noun

Etymology: From Martin Luther + -ism.  Martin Luther (1483-1546) was a German theologian who initiated the Protestant Reformation. The suffix "-ism" denotes a doctrine, belief system, or practice.
