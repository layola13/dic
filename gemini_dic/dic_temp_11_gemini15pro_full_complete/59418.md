earthinesses:/ˈɜːrθinəsɪz/
noun
世俗性，粗俗，泥土气息
The quality of being earthy or down-to-earth; unrefined or coarse characteristics.  The plural of earthiness.
Her paintings celebrated the earthinesses of rural life. 她的画作歌颂了农村生活的质朴。
The comedian's humor was known for its earthinesses.  这位喜剧演员的幽默以其粗俗而闻名。
synonyms: crudeness, coarseness, vulgarity, unrefinement, grossness, indelicacy, bawdiness, ribaldry, homespunness, rusticity, simplicity
antonyms: refinement, elegance, sophistication, polish, delicacy, sublimity, spirituality
