engravers:/ɪnˈɡreɪvərz/
n.
雕刻师，雕刻工
A person or thing that engraves.
The engravers meticulously carved the intricate design onto the silver platter.  雕刻师一丝不苟地在银盘上雕刻复杂的图案。

The antique clock was the work of skilled engravers.  这个古董钟是技艺精湛的雕刻师的作品。

近义词: carvers, etchers, sculptors
反义词: None (No direct opposite for a profession)
词性: Noun
