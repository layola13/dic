frog:/frɒɡ/ (UK) /frɑːɡ/ (US)| n.|青蛙|A tailless amphibian with a short squat body, moist smooth skin, and very long hind legs for leaping.|The frog leaped into the pond. 青蛙跳进了池塘。|近义词：toad, amphibian|反义词:|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *frogge*, from Old English *frogga*, from Proto-Germanic *frakkǭ*, from Proto-Indo-European *prekʰ- (“to jump, hop”). Cognate with Scots *frog* (“frog”), West Frisian *frogge* (“frog”), Dutch *vrog* (“frog”), German *Frosch* (“frog”), Icelandic *froskur* (“frog”).
