greenfly:/ˈɡriːnflaɪ/| n. |蚜虫；绿蝇|A small, green insect that sucks the juices of plants, often found in large groups.  A common garden pest.|例句：The roses are covered in greenfly. 玫瑰上爬满了蚜虫。

例句：Greenfly can quickly infest a garden. 蚜虫很快就会蔓延整个花园。|近义词：aphid, plant louse|反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑 natural predator 天敌) |词性:noun
