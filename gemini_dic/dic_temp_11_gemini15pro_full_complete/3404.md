aflush:/əˈflʌʃ/| adj.|脸红的，满面红光的；激动兴奋的|Being filled with or showing exuberance or elation; flushed.|Her cheeks were aflush with excitement after winning the race. 赢得比赛后，她的脸颊因兴奋而泛红。|近义词：flushed, blushing, reddened, ruddy, rosy|反义词：pale, pallid|词性:形容词

词源:  From a- + flush (adj.).  The adjective "flush" itself comes from Middle English fluschen,  possibly from Old French flux, ultimately from Latin fluxus "a flowing," from fluere "to flow."  The prefix "a-" in this context intensifies the meaning.
