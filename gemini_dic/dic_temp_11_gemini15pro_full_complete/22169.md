bombastic:/bɒmˈbæstɪk/ | adj. |夸大的，浮夸的，言过其实的| High-sounding but with little meaning; inflated. |例句：His bombastic speech impressed no one.  他夸夸其谈的演讲没有打动任何人。

例句：She found his bombastic style of writing irritating. 她觉得他浮夸的写作风格很烦人。

近义词：pompous, inflated, grandiose, overblown, pretentious |反义词：simple, plain, unpretentious, modest, understated |词性: adjective


bombast:/ˈbɒmbæst/ | n. |夸大的言辞，浮夸的语言| High-sounding language with little meaning, used to impress people. |例句：The politician's speech was full of bombast. 这位政客的演讲充满了夸夸其谈。

例句：Her bombast concealed a deep insecurity. 她的夸夸其谈掩盖了她内心深处的不安。

近义词：pomposity, grandiloquence, rhetoric |反义词：understatement, simplicity, plainness |词性: noun
