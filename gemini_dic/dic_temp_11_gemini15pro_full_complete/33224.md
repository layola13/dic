chronogrammatic: /ˌkrɒnəɡrəˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 年代诗的；用年代诗写的 | Containing a chronogram; inscribed with a chronogram. |  This 17th-century sundial bears a chronogrammatic inscription. 这个17世纪的日晷上刻着年代诗铭文。| The chronogrammatic poem cleverly concealed the date of the king's coronation. 这首年代诗巧妙地隐藏了国王加冕的日期。 | 近义词: chronogrammic | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *chronogrammāticus, from Ancient Greek χρόνος (chrónos, 'time') + γράμμα (grámma, 'letter, character') + -atic.
