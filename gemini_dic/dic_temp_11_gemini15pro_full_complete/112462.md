loudspeaker:/ˌlaʊdˈspiːkər/ | n. |扬声器，喇叭|A device that converts electrical signals into sound waves, typically housed in a cabinet or other enclosure.|The band's loudspeakers were so powerful that the whole stadium vibrated. 乐队的扬声器功率很大，整个体育场都在震动。|synonyms: speaker, amplifier, sound system|antonyms: microphone, earphone|noun


loudspeaker:/ˌlaʊdˈspiːkər/ | n. |扩音器|A device that amplifies sound, making it louder and more audible.|The teacher used a loudspeaker to address the large crowd of students. 老师用扩音器向一大群学生讲话。|synonyms: bullhorn, megaphone, PA system|antonyms: microphone, earphone|noun
