denizen:/ˈdɛnɪzən/ /ˈdɛnɪzn/| n.|居民，常客，外来物种|an inhabitant; a regular visitor or user of a particular place; an animal or plant that lives or grows in a particular place and is not native there|The denizens of the forest looked up as a strange light filled the sky. 森林里的居民抬头望着天空，一道奇异的光芒充满了天空。|The bar's denizens were a motley crew of artists, writers, and musicians. 酒吧的常客是一群形形色色的艺术家、作家和音乐家。|synonyms: inhabitant, resident, occupant, native, local, regular|antonyms: foreigner, alien, stranger, visitor|noun
