flueless:/ˈfluːləs/| adj.|无烟道的，无排气管的|Not having or requiring a flue (a duct for smoke and waste gases).  A flueless appliance does not produce waste gases that need venting, or vents them in a different manner than a traditional flue.|We installed a flueless gas fire in the living room. 我们在客厅安装了一个无烟道燃气壁炉。|The flueless gas heater is very efficient. 这款无烟道燃气加热器效率很高。|ventless, unvented|vented|adjective
