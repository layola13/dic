absoluteness:/ˈæbsəluːtnəs/
n.
绝对性；完全性；无条件性
The state of being absolute; completeness; unconditionalness.
The absoluteness of his power was unquestioned. 他的权力是绝对的，毋庸置疑。
近义词：certainty, completeness, totality, unqualifiedness
反义词：relativity, conditionality
词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From absolute + -ness.  "Absolute" comes from Late Latin absolutus, past participle of absolvere "to set free, acquit," from ab- "from, away from" + solvere "to loosen."
