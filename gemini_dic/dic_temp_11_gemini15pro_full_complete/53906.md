discounter:/dɪsˈkaʊntər/ | n. | 折扣店；贴现者 | A retailer who sells goods at reduced prices. |  Example 1: I bought this laptop at a discounter and saved a lot of money. 我在这家折扣店买了这台笔记本电脑，省了很多钱。

Example 2:  The discounter offered a 20% discount on all items. 这家折扣店所有商品打八折。 | Synonyms: retailer, seller, vendor, merchant | Antonyms: premium retailer, upscale store | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From discount + -er.  "discount" comes from Old French "desconter" (to count off, deduct), from "des-" (meaning "off, away") and "conter" (to count).
