overmaturities:/ˌoʊvərˌmætʃʊrɪtiz/| n. |逾期债务，过期债务| Debts or financial instruments that have reached maturity but have not been paid or redeemed.  These are often considered distressed assets. | The bank was burdened by a large portfolio of overmaturities. 这家银行被大量的逾期债务所拖累。| The company's overmaturities increased sharply due to the economic downturn. 由于经济低迷，该公司的逾期债务急剧增加。|  Synonyms:  expired debts, past-due debts, delinquent loans | Antonyms: current assets, performing loans | Noun
