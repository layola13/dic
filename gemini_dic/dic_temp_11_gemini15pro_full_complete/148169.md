overservicing: /ˌoʊvərˈsɜːrvɪsɪŋ/
overservicing: n.
overservicing: 过度服务
overservicing: Providing more services than are necessary or desired, often at an increased cost. This can apply to various fields, from healthcare to finance.
overservicing:  The bank was accused of overservicing its clients by offering them complex financial products they didn't need.  这家银行被指控过度服务客户，向他们提供他们不需要的复杂金融产品。
overservicing:  Some doctors are concerned about overservicing patients with unnecessary tests and procedures.  一些医生担心过度服务病人，进行不必要的检查和程序。
overservicing: 近义词: overdoing, excessive service
overservicing: 反义词: underservicing, neglect
