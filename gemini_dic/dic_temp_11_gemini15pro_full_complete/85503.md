hastening:/ˈheɪsənɪŋ/ |v. |加速，催促|Making something happen sooner or more quickly. |例句：The cool weather is hastening the arrival of autumn. 凉爽的天气正在加速秋天的到来。

例句：He hastened to assure her that everything was alright. 他急忙向她保证一切都没事。|近义词：accelerating, speeding up, expediting, rushing |反义词：delaying, slowing down, hindering| verb

Etymology->
From Middle English *hastenen, from Old French hastener, from haste (“haste”).
