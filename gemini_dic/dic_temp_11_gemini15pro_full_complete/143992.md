outrated:/ˌaʊtˈreɪtɪd/
verb
超过等级，级别高于；胜过，优于
to have a higher rating than someone or something else
Our team outrated theirs in the competition.  我们的队伍在比赛中胜过他们的队伍。
superior to, surpass, excel, outclass, outstrip
inferior to, subordinate to
词性: 动词
