disulphide:/daɪˈsʌlfaɪd/ (US) /daɪˈsʌlfaɪd/ (UK)| n. |二硫化物|A compound containing a disulphide group, formed by the oxidation of two sulphydryl (SH) groups, especially in a protein molecule.|Disulphide bonds are important for the stability of protein structure.|例句：The disulphide bonds in the protein were broken by the reducing agent.  这种蛋白质中的二硫键被还原剂破坏了。

例句：Disulphide bridges are essential for the tertiary structure of many proteins. 二硫桥对许多蛋白质的三级结构至关重要。|近义词：disulfide (US spelling)|反义词: (No direct antonym, removal of disulphide bond leads to a reduced state)|词性:noun
