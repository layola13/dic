affront:/əˈfrʌnt/ | n. vt. |冒犯，侮辱；公然蔑视 | An action or remark that causes outrage or offense. |  He took his demotion as a personal affront. 他认为降职是对他的个人侮辱。 | Synonyms: insult, offense, slight, indignity | Antonyms: compliment, praise, honor | noun
affront:/əˈfrʌnt/ | vt. |冒犯，侮辱；面对；相遇 | To offend the modesty or values of someone. |  She was affronted by his rude remarks. 她被他粗鲁的言辞冒犯了。 | Synonyms: insult, offend, outrage, provoke | Antonyms: appease, placate, soothe | verb
