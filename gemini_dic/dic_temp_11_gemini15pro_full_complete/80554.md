goodwill:/ˈɡʊdˈwɪl/| n.| 商誉；友好；善意；亲切|The company's assets include factories, equipment, and goodwill. 该公司的资产包括工厂、设备和商誉。| Goodwill is essential for maintaining good relationships.  善意对于维持良好关系至关重要。 | 近义词: benevolence, kindness, amity | 反义词: hostility, animosity, ill will | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *godwill, *good wille, from Old English gōdwilla (“goodwill, benevolence, well-wishing”), equivalent to good +‎ will. Cognate with Scots guidwill (“goodwill”), West Frisian goodwill (“goodwill”), Dutch goede wil (“goodwill”), German guter Wille (“goodwill, good intention”).
