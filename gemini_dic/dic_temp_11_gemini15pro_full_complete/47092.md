daubiest:/ˈdɔːbiɪst/ | adj. | 涂抹得最多的，污迹最多的 | Most smeared or stained with a sticky substance; most daubed. | The daubiest wall in the alley was covered in graffiti. 小巷里涂抹得最多的墙上满是涂鸦. | Example 2:  His hands were the daubiest after working with the clay.  用黏土工作后，他的手沾满了污渍。| 近义词：messiest, dirtiest, smudgiest | 反义词：cleanest, tidiest | 词性: 形容词

词源: From the verb "daub" + "-y" (forming adjectives of quality) + "-est" (superlative suffix).  "Daub" comes from Old French "dauber" which likely has Germanic origins.
