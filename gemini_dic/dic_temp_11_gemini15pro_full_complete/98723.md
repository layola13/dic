insurgence:/ɪnˈsɜːrdʒəns/ /ɪnˈsɝːdʒəns/| n. |起义，暴动，叛乱|An act of rising in active revolt against an established authority. |例句：The insurgence was quickly quelled by the government. 政府迅速平息了叛乱。

例句：The insurgence threatened to destabilize the entire region.  这场叛乱有可能破坏整个地区的稳定。

|近义词：rebellion, revolt, uprising, mutiny|反义词: obedience, submission|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *insurgentia, from Latin insurgens, present participle of insurgere “to rise up,” from in- “against, upon” + surgere “to rise.”
