inconformable:/ˌɪnkənˈfɔːrməbl/ |adj.|不整合的，不一致的|Not conforming to established standards or conventions; inconsistent or incompatible.  Specifically in geology, referring to rock layers that are not deposited in a continuous sequence, indicating a gap in the geological record.|The unconformable contact between the two rock layers suggests a period of erosion or non-deposition. 这两层岩石之间的不整合接触表明存在一个侵蚀或非沉积时期。|Synonyms: incompatible, discordant, inconsistent, disparate, divergent|Antonyms: conformable, consistent, compatible, harmonious, concordant|adjective
