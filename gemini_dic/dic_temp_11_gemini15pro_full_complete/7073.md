anencephalous:/ˌænənˈsɛfələs/| adj.|无脑的；无脑畸形的|Born without a major portion of the brain, skull, and scalp.  出生时缺少大部分大脑、头骨和头皮。|The baby was sadly born anencephalous.  这个婴儿不幸地生下来就是无脑儿。|Synonyms: brainless, acephalous|Antonyms: normal, healthy|adjective

anencephaly:/ˌænənˈsɛfəli/| n.|无脑畸形|A congenital deformity in which some or all of fetal brain is missing. 一种先天性畸形，胎儿的部分或全部大脑缺失。|Anencephaly is a serious birth defect. 无脑畸形是一种严重的出生缺陷。|Synonyms: anencephalia|Antonyms: N/A|noun
