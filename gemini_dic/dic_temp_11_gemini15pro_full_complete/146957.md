overinvolved:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈvɑːlvd/ | adj. | 过度介入的，过度参与的 | Excessively involved or concerned in the affairs of others | He was criticized for being overinvolved in the project. 他因过度参与该项目而受到批评. | meddling, interfering, intrusive | detached, disengaged | adjective
