endosarc:/ˈɛndoʊsɑːrk/ | n. | 内质，内胚层 | The inner, softer portion of the cytoplasm of some amoeboid protozoans, such as amoebas.  It is distinguished from the ectoplasm, the outer, denser layer. | The amoeba's endosarc contains the nucleus and other organelles.  变形虫的内质包含细胞核和其他细胞器。 |  The endosarc of the amoeba plays a vital role in its movement and feeding. 变形虫的内质在其运动和摄食中起着至关重要的作用。 | 近义词: endoplasm | 反义词: ectoplasm | 词性: noun
