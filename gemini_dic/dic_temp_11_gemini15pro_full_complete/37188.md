comedietta:/ˌkɒmɪdiˈetə/ (US) /ˌkɑːmədiˈetə/| n. |小喜剧，轻喜剧|A short, light-hearted comedy, often a one-act play.|例句：The theater group performed a charming comedietta.  剧团表演了一出迷人的轻喜剧。

例句：She wrote a witty comedietta for the festival. 她为节日写了一部诙谐的轻喜剧。|近义词：farce, skit, playlet|反义词：tragedy, drama|词性：noun

Etymology:
From Italian *comedietta*, diminutive of *commedia* "comedy," from Latin *comoedia*, from Greek *kōmōidia*.
