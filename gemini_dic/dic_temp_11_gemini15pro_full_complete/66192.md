excipulum:/ɛkˈsɪpjʊləm/
n.
外囊盘，托
The outer layer or margin of a lichen apothecium or other fruiting body, formed from fungal tissue of the thallus.
The excipulum of this lichen species is distinctly warted.  这种地衣的外囊盘明显呈疣状。
近义词:thallus margin, apothecium margin
反义词:hymenium (not a true antonym, but a contrasting structure within the apothecium)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *excipulum*, from *excipere* "to take out, extract, remove, except", from *ex-* "out" + *capere* "to take".  The term relates to the idea of the excipulum being a supporting structure that holds the hymenium (spore-bearing layer).
