epistilbite:/ɪˈpɪstɪlˌbaɪt/| n. |斜沸石|A hydrous calcium and sodium aluminosilicate mineral occurring in sheaflike aggregates of crystals. It is a zeolite mineral.|Epistilbite is often found in volcanic rocks. 斜沸石常发现于火山岩中。|近义词：heulandite, stilbite (These are other zeolite minerals with similar properties)|反义词: N/A (Minerals don't typically have antonyms)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek ἐπί (epi, “upon”) + στίλβειν (stilbein, “to glitter”), in allusion to its pearly luster on the cleavage surface.
