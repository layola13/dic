colchicum: /ˈkɒltʃɪkəm/ (UK), /ˈkɑːltʃɪkəm/ (US) | n. | 秋水仙 | A plant of the lily family, with crocus-like flowers, usually purple, that appear in the autumn.  The corms and seeds are highly poisonous. | The colchicum bloomed beautifully in the fall. 秋水仙在秋天开得很漂亮。 |  Synonyms: autumn crocus, meadow saffron | Antonyms: N/A | noun

colchicum: /ˈkɒltʃɪkəm/ (UK), /ˈkɑːltʃɪkəm/ (US) | n. | 秋水仙碱; 秋水仙素 |  A poisonous alkaloid, C₂₂H₂₅NO₆, extracted from plants of the genus *Colchicum* and used to treat gout and familial Mediterranean fever. | Colchicine is derived from colchicum. 秋水仙碱是从秋水仙中提取的。| Synonyms: colchicine | Antonyms: N/A | noun
