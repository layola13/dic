meleagrine:/ˌmɛliˈæɡraɪn/| adj.|火鸡的，像火鸡的|Of or relating to turkeys; resembling a turkey.|例句：The bird had a meleagrine appearance with its fan-shaped tail. 这只鸟有着扇形的尾巴，看起来像火鸡。

例句：Meleagrine plumage is often iridescent and colorful. 火鸡的羽毛通常色彩斑斓，带有虹彩。|近义词：turkey-like, gallinaceous|反义词: (No specific antonym, possibly "unlike a turkey")|词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin *meleagrinus, from meleagris (“turkey”), from Greek μελεαγρίς (meleagris, “guinea fowl”), from Μελέαγρος (Méleagros), a mythological hero whose sisters were turned into guinea fowl.
