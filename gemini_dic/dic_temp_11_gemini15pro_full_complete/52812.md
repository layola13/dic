dilucidate:/dɪˈluːsɪdeɪt/ | v. | 阐明，解释 | to make something clear and easy to understand |  The professor elucidated the complex theory with clear examples. 教授用清晰的例子阐明了这个复杂的理论。

He elucidated his plan in detail. 他详细阐述了他的计划。

| 近义词: explain, clarify, illuminate, expound | 反义词: obscure, confuse, obfuscate | 词性: 动词

dilucid:/dɪˈluːsɪd/ | adj. | 清晰的，易懂的 | clear and easy to understand |  Her explanation was lucid and concise. 她的解释清晰而简洁。

The patient’s responses were lucid and coherent. 病人的回答清晰连贯。

| 近义词: clear, understandable, intelligible, perspicuous | 反义词: unclear, obscure, confusing, ambiguous | 词性: 形容词
