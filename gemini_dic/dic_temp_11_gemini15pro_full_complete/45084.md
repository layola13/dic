cubital:/ˈkjuːbɪtəl/| n.| 尺骨|The ulna, the larger of the two bones in the forearm.|The doctor examined the patient's cubital after the fall.|Synonyms:  ulna|Antonyms:  radius (the other forearm bone)|noun
