nonreactors:/ˌnɑːnriˈæktərz/
noun
非反应物；不反应者；无反应者
Those who do not participate in a chemical reaction or a particular event or situation.  Those who do not respond to a stimulus.
The experiment included a control group of nonreactors.  实验包括一组非反应者的对照组。
The survey identified a significant percentage of nonreactors to the political campaign.  调查发现对政治运动无反应者占很大比例。
Synonyms:inert, unresponsive, passive
Antonyms:reactors, responsive, active
词性:n.
