overinsolently: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈsɒləntli/ | adv. | 过于傲慢地，过分无礼地 |  In an extremely insolent manner; with excessive disrespect or arrogance.  It combines "over-" (meaning excessively) with "insolently" (meaning in a rude and disrespectful way). | He overinsolently dismissed her concerns, rolling his eyes and sighing dramatically. 他傲慢地无视了她的担忧，翻着白眼，夸张地叹了口气。|  The spoiled child overinsolently demanded another toy, throwing a tantrum when refused.  这个被宠坏的孩子傲慢无礼地要求再要一个玩具，被拒绝后大发脾气。 |Synonyms:  arrogantly, haughtily, superciliously, contemptuously, disdainfully | Antonyms: respectfully, humbly, politely, deferentially, submissively |  词性: 副词 (Adverb)
