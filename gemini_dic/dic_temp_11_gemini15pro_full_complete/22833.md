bossiest: /ˈbɒsiɪst/ (UK) /ˈbɔːsiɪst/ (US)| adj.|最爱指使人的，最专横的|Superlative adjective of bossy: most bossy.  It describes someone who is extremely inclined to tell others what to do, often in a domineering or overbearing way.|例句：She's the bossiest person I've ever met. 她是我见过最爱指使人的人。

例句：He's always been the bossiest child in the family. 他一直是家里最专横的孩子。

|近义词：domineering, overbearing, dictatorial, autocratic|反义词:submissive, compliant, meek, passive|词性:adjective
