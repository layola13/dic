hook:/hʊk/| n. vt.|钩子，吊钩；吸引人的东西；陷阱，圈套；（拳击中的）勾拳|A curved piece of metal or other hard material used for catching, holding, or pulling something. Also, something that attracts attention or serves as an enticement. In boxing, it refers to a short swinging blow with the elbow bent.|The fish was caught on a hook.  这条鱼被钩子钓到了。|近义词：snag, catch, clasp, fastener, lure, bait|反义词: release|名词/动词


hook:/hʊk/| vt.|钩住，挂住；吸引，迷住|To fasten or catch with a hook; to attract or entice someone.|The coat was hooked on the back of the door.  外套挂在门背面的钩子上。|近义词：catch, snag, secure, fascinate, captivate|反义词: unhook, detach|动词
