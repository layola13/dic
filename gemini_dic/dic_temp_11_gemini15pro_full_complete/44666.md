crucible:/ˈkruːsəbəl/| n.|坩埚; 严峻的考验|a container in which metals or other substances are heated to very high temperatures; a situation that forces people to change or make difficult decisions|The crucible cracked under the intense heat. 坩埚在高温下破裂了。| The city became a crucible of revolutionary ideas. 这座城市成为了革命思想的熔炉。|synonyms: melting pot, cauldron, test, trial|antonyms: haven, sanctuary|noun

crucibles:/ˈkruːsəbəlz/| n.|坩埚(复数); 严峻的考验(复数)|plural form of crucible|These crucibles are made of high-quality porcelain. 这些坩埚是由高质量的瓷器制成的。| Many historical figures were forged in the crucibles of war. 许多历史人物都是在战争的严酷考验中形成的。|synonyms: melting pots, cauldrons, tests, trials|antonyms: havens, sanctuaries|noun
