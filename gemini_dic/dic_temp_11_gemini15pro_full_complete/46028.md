cybersquatters:/ˈsaɪbərˌskwɑːtərz/
n.
网络蟑螂；域名抢注者
People who register Internet domain names containing trademarks with the intent of selling them to the trademark owners for a profit.
Many companies have been victims of cybersquatters who registered their company name as a domain name. 许多公司成为网络蟑螂的受害者，这些蟑螂将他们的公司名称注册为域名。
synonyms: domain squatters, domain name hijackers
antonyms: legitimate domain name registrants
noun
