annuary: /ˈænjuəri/ | n. | 年鉴；年报 | A publication appearing yearly, often containing statistical information or a summary of the events of the year. |  The school publishes an annuary every year with photos and details of all the students. 学校每年都会出版一本年鉴，里面有所有学生的照片和详细信息。| Example: The historical society's annuary is a valuable resource for researchers.  历史学会的年鉴对研究人员来说是宝贵的资源。 | Synonyms: yearbook, almanac, annual | Antonyms: N/A | noun
