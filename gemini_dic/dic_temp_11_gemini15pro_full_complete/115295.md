manly:/ˈmænli/| adj.|男子气概的；强壮的；雄赳赳的|Having or showing qualities traditionally associated with men, such as strength, courage, and independence.|He had a manly voice. 他有着一副阳刚的声音。|近义词：masculine, virile, strong, brave|反义词：effeminate, womanly, weak|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *manlich, *manly, from Old English *mannlīc (“manly, brave, human”), equivalent to man +‎ -ly. Cognate with Scots manly (“manly, stout”), West Frisian manlik (“manly”), Dutch mannelijk (“male, masculine”), German männlich (“male, masculine”), Swedish manlig (“manly”).
