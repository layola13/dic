outhissing:/ˌaʊtˈhɪsɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的嘶嘶声；发出比…更大的嘶嘶声
outdoing someone or something in hissing.
The steam escaping the faulty valve was outhissing the tea kettle.  从故障阀门逸出的蒸汽发出比茶壶更大的嘶嘶声。
The angry goose was outhissing the agitated cat.  愤怒的鹅发出比激动的猫更大的嘶嘶声。
outscream, outshout, outyell
-
verb
词源：  "out-" (prefix indicating surpassing) + "hissing" (present participle of "hiss")
