non-official:/ˌnɒnəˈfɪʃəl/ (US) /ˌnɒnəˈfɪʃl/ (UK)| adj.|非官方的，非正式的|Not having official authority or recognition.|例句：This is a non-official translation of the document. 这是该文件的非官方翻译。|例句：He made a non-official visit to the country. 他对该国进行了一次非正式访问。|近义词：unofficial, informal|反义词:official, formal|词性:形容词

词源:
From non- + official.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Official" comes from the Old French "oficial," ultimately from the Latin "officium" meaning "duty" or "service."
