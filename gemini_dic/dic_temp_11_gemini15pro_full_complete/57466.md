draftier:/ˈdræftiər/ | adj. | 通风更强的；更冷的 | More drafty; characterized by or exposed to drafts of air, causing a feeling of coldness. | The old house was draftier in the winter than I remembered.  这老房子冬天比我记忆中更冷了。| The attic room was draftier than the rest of the house. 阁楼房间比房子其他地方通风更强。 |近义词: airier, breezier, chillier, colder |反义词: warmer, less drafty, air-tight| 词性: 形容词

draft:/dræft/ | n. | 气流，通风；草稿 | A current of air, especially one intruding into a room or vehicle.  |  I felt a cold draft coming from under the door. 我感觉门底下有股冷风吹进来。| Please send me the first draft of the report. 请把报告的初稿发给我。| 近义词: breeze, current, sketch, outline | 反义词: stillness, final version | 词性: 名词

-y:/-i/ | suffix | 形容词后缀，表示“充满...的”，“具有...特性的” |  A suffix that forms adjectives meaning "full of" or "characterized by". |  The room was drafty. 房间里有很多穿堂风。 | 近义词: -ful, -ous | 反义词: -less | 词性: 后缀

词源:
From draft + -y.  "Draft" comes from the Old Norse word "draught," meaning "a pulling," referring to the pulling sensation of a current of air.
