airdrop:/ˈeərdrɒp/ (UK) /ˈeərdrɑːp/ (US)| n. vt.|空投，空投物资；空投；通过无线方式发送（文件等）|An airdrop is a delivery of items by parachute from an aircraft, especially of supplies to people in an inaccessible place due to a disaster or war. In the context of cryptocurrency, it refers to the distribution of a cryptocurrency token or coin, typically for free, to numerous wallet addresses. |The UN organized an airdrop of food and medicine to the refugees. 联合国组织了一次向难民空投食物和药品。|近义词：airlift, delivery, transfer, distribution (especially in the cryptocurrency context)|反义词:pickup, collection|noun, verb

airdrop:/ˈeərdrɒp/ (UK) /ˈeərdrɑːp/ (US)| vt.|空投，空投物资；通过无线方式发送（文件等）|To airdrop something means to deliver it by parachute from an aircraft or to transfer it wirelessly, especially files between Apple devices.|She airdropped the photos to her friend's phone. 她把照片无线传输到了她朋友的手机上。|近义词：transmit, send, transfer, beam|反义词:receive, download|verb
