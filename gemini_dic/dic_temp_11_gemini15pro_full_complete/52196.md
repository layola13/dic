diaspora:/daɪˈæspərə/ (US), /daɪˈæspərə/ (UK)| n. |大流散，散居群体|The scattering or dispersion of any people from their original homeland. A group of people who live outside their traditional homeland. |例句：The Jewish diaspora has communities all over the world. 犹太人的散居群体遍布世界各地。

例句：The African diaspora has significantly influenced music and culture globally. 非洲散居群体对全球音乐和文化产生了重大影响。

近义词：dispersion, scattering, migration, exile, exodus|反义词: repatriation, return, homecoming|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *diaspora* "a scattering, dispersion," from *diaspeirein* "to scatter," from *dia* "through, across" + *speirein* "to sow, scatter."
