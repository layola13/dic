disrespectfulness:/ˌdɪsrɪˈspɛktfʊlnəs/
n.
无礼，失礼，不敬
lack of respect; rudeness.
Her disrespectfulness towards her elders was shocking. 她对长辈的无礼令人震惊。
近义词：rudeness, impoliteness, incivility, discourtesy
反义词：respectfulness, politeness, courtesy, reverence
