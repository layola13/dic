hyoscyamus:/ˌhaɪəˈsaɪəməs/| n. |天仙子，莨菪|A genus of poisonous Eurasian plants of the nightshade family, some of which are cultivated as ornamentals and others of which yield hyoscyamine.|Henbane (Hyoscyamus niger) is a highly toxic plant.|近义词：henbane, stinking nightshade|反义词: (No direct antonym for a plant genus) |noun
