feracious:/fəˈreɪʃəs/| adj.|凶猛的，残暴的；贪婪的；渴求的|Characterized by being fierce, savage, or wild; ravenously hungry or eager. |例句：The feracious lion stalked its prey. 凶猛的狮子悄悄地跟踪着它的猎物。

例句：He has a feracious appetite for knowledge. 他对知识有着如饥似渴的渴望。|近义词：fierce, savage, ravenous, voracious, rapacious|反义词：gentle, tame, mild, docile|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ferax*, from *ferus* "wild, savage" + *-ax* forming adjectives.
