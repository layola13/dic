debate:/dɪˈbeɪt/ (US) /dɪˈbeɪt/ (UK)| n. v. |辩论，讨论；争论；思考，盘算|A formal discussion on a particular topic in a public meeting or legislative assembly, in which opposing arguments are put forward.|The students debated the merits of school uniforms. 学生们就穿校服的利弊进行了辩论。|近义词：discussion, argument, dispute, controversy|反义词：agreement, harmony, concord|n.

debate:/dɪˈbeɪt/ (US) /dɪˈbeɪt/ (UK)| v. |辩论，讨论；争论；思考，盘算|Engage in argument or discussion.|They debated the issue for hours. 他们就这个问题辩论了几个小时。|近义词：discuss, argue, dispute, consider|反义词：agree, concur|v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *debatre*, from debatre "to fight, contend, struggle," from de- "down" + batre "to beat."
