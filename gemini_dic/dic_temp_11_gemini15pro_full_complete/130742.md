nonambulatory:/ˌnɒnæmˈbjʊlətɔːri/
nonambulatory:| adj.|无法行走的，不能步行的|Not able to walk; unable to ambulate.  Refers to a person who is bedridden or uses a wheelchair or other mobility device.| The hospital wing was specifically designed for nonambulatory patients.| 近义词:immobile, bedridden, disabled| 反义词:ambulatory, mobile, walking| 词性:形容词

nonambulatories:/ˌnɒnæmˈbjʊlətɔːriz/
nonambulatories:| n.|无法行走的人，不能步行的人|People who are not able to walk; plural of nonambulatory.|The nonambulatories were transported by special vans.|近义词: invalids, the disabled|反义词: walkers, the mobile| 词性:名词
