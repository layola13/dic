blocker:/ˈblɒkər/ (UK), /ˈblɑːkər/ (US)| n.| 阻碍者，障碍物，封锁者; (体育) 阻挡队员; β受体阻滞剂 |Something that blocks or obstructs progress or activity.  A person or thing that prevents something from happening or succeeding. In sports, a player whose role is to obstruct an opponent. In medicine, a beta-blocker.|

The heavy traffic was a real blocker on our journey.  这拥堵的交通是我们行程中的一大障碍。

She's a blocker on the basketball team. 她是篮球队的阻挡队员。

He's taking beta blockers for his high blood pressure. 他正在服用β受体阻滞剂来治疗高血压。

|反义词:facilitator, enabler, catalyst, aid, help|
