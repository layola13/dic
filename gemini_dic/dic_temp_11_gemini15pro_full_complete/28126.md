capitation:/kæpɪˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 人头费，按人头收费 | A system of payment for healthcare services in which a physician or hospital is paid a fixed amount per patient for a prescribed period of time, regardless of the actual services provided. |  The clinic switched to a capitation system to simplify billing.  诊所改用按人头收费的制度以简化计费。 | synonyms: per capita payment, head fee | antonyms: fee-for-service | noun


capitation:/kæpɪˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 人头税 | A tax levied on every individual in a population, regardless of income or other factors. |  The government imposed a capitation tax on all citizens. 政府对所有公民征收人头税。 | synonyms: poll tax, head tax | antonyms: progressive tax | noun
