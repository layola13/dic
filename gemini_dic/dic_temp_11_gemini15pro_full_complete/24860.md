brownshirt:/ˈbraʊnʃɜːrt/| n.|褐衫队员，纳粹冲锋队员|A member of the Nazi Sturmabteilung (SA), a paramilitary organization known for its brown uniforms and role in the early years of the Nazi regime.  They were instrumental in suppressing political opponents and perpetuating violence against Jews and other minorities.|Hitler's brownshirts terrorized the streets. 希特勒的褐衫队员恐吓街头百姓。|近义词：Nazi, stormtrooper|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'resister', 'opponent')|noun
