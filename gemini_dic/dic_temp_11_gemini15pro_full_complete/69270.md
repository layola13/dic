featherless:/ˈfeðərlɪs/
adj.
无羽毛的，秃的
Lacking feathers.
The featherless chick huddled against its mother for warmth. 这只没有羽毛的小鸡依偎在妈妈身边取暖。
featherless newborn bird. 一只刚出生的无毛小鸟。
near synonyms: bald, bare, hairless, naked, plumeless
antonyms: feathered, plumed
adjective
