immovable:/ɪˈmuːvəbl/ | adj. | 不可移动的；固定的；不动产的 | Not able to be moved, either literally or figuratively. |  The statue was massive and immovable. 这座雕像巨大且不可移动。

The immovable rock stood firm against the crashing waves.  那块不可移动的岩石在汹涌的海浪中屹立不倒。| 近义词: fixed, steadfast, permanent, unmovable | 反义词: movable, mobile, portable | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Late Middle English *immovable*, from Old French *immovable*, from Latin *immōbilis* (“immovable”), from *in-* (“not”) + *mōbilis* (“movable”).
