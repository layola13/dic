bluebook:/ˈbluːbʊk/ | n. |蓝皮书，考试本；（尤指美国大学的）期末论文，成绩册| A bluebook is a small booklet with lined paper, often blue, used for essay exams and other written work, especially in American universities. It can also refer to an official publication, often government-related, containing reports, lists, or other information. |  The professor handed out bluebooks for the final exam. 教授分发了期末考试的蓝皮书。| synonyms: exam booklet, test booklet | antonyms: textbook, notebook | noun

bluebook:/ˈbluːbʊk/ | v. |将…编入蓝皮书；记录；记分 | To record or list something in a bluebook. | The teacher bluebooked the students' grades. 老师将学生的成绩记入成绩册。| synonyms: record, register, list | antonyms: erase, delete | verb
