keeldrag:/ˈkiːldræɡ/| n.|龙骨擦地; (飞机)低空飞行; (船)吃水很深; 阻力; |A situation in which a ship or boat is moving very slowly because its keel is dragging along the bottom of the shallow water. Also refers to an aircraft flying very low, or a situation that creates drag or resistance.|The yacht keeldragged on the sandbar.  这艘游艇在沙洲上龙骨擦地。

The plane keeldragged across the sky, barely missing the treetops.  飞机低空掠过天空，几乎擦到了树梢。|近义词:drag, resistance, low-flying|反义词:speed, effortless|noun
