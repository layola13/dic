affine:/əˈfaɪn/ | adj.| 仿射的 | Relating to or involving geometric transformations that do not change the relative distances between points, such as translation, rotation, reflection, and scaling.  Maintaining parallel lines. |  Affine transformations preserve collinearity and the ratio of distances along a line. 仿射变换保持共线性和沿线的距离比。| Example:  An affine cipher is a type of monoalphabetic substitution cipher. 仿射密码是一种单字母替换密码。

|Synonyms: linear, proportional, related | Antonyms: nonlinear, non-proportional, unrelated | adjective
