entocuneiform:/ˌɛntokjuˈniːɪfɔːrm/ | adj. | 内楔形的，内侧楔形的 | Relating to or denoting the entocuneiform bone, one of the cuneiform bones in the tarsus (ankle/foot) of vertebrates. It articulates with the metatarsal of the second digit. |  The entocuneiform bone is crucial for stability in the foot. 内侧楔骨对于足部的稳定性至关重要。| The doctor pointed to the X-ray, indicating a fracture in the entocuneiform. 医生指着X光片，指出内侧楔骨骨折。 | medial cuneiform | lateral cuneiform | adjective
