imprint:/ɪmˈprɪnt/ /ˈɪmprɪnt/| n. vt.|印记，铭刻，深刻印象|A mark made by pressing something onto a softer substance.  The experience left a lasting imprint on his memory.|The dog's paws left imprints in the wet cement. 狗的爪子在湿水泥地上留下了印记。| He imprinted his seal on the wax. 他把他的印章印在蜡上。|synonyms: mark, impression, stamp, trace|antonyms: erase, delete|noun
imprint:/ɪmˈprɪnt/ /ˈɪmprɪnt/| vt.|印，铭刻，使铭记|To fix an image, design, or idea firmly in someone's mind.|His father's words were imprinted on his mind. 他父亲的话铭刻在他的心中。| The experience was imprinted on her memory forever. 这段经历永远铭刻在她的记忆中。|synonyms: impress, engrave, fix, stamp|antonyms: forget, erase|verb
