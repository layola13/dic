disquietful:/dɪsˈkwaɪətfl/
adjective
令人不安的，令人担忧的
Causing anxiety or uneasiness; disturbing.
The disquietful news of the approaching storm worried the villagers.  村民们对即将到来的风暴的令人不安的消息感到担忧。
The constant dripping of the faucet was a disquietful sound that kept her awake.  水龙头不停的滴水声令人不安，让她无法入睡。
近义词: unsettling, disturbing, worrying, troubling
反义词: calming, soothing, peaceful, tranquil
词性: adjective

词源:
From dis- (expressing negation or reversal) + quiet + -ful (forming adjectives).  由dis-（表示否定或反转）+ quiet + -ful（构成形容词）组成。
