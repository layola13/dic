bookmarking:/ˈbʊkmɑːrkɪŋ/ | n. |书签，收藏；加入书签|The act of saving the address of a web page or file so that it can be easily accessed later. | I use bookmarking extensively to keep track of interesting websites. 我广泛使用书签来跟踪有趣的网站。|  She found the article helpful and added it to her bookmarking folder. 她觉得这篇文章很有帮助，并把它添加到她的书签文件夹中。 | synonyms: saving, storing, tagging, favoriting | antonyms: deleting, removing | noun
