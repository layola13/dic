dry-roast:/ˈdraɪˌroʊst/| v. adj.|干烤，烘烤;  干巴巴地讽刺|To roast (food, especially nuts) without oil or fat. To humorously criticize someone in a  cutting but good-natured way.|例句：I dry-roasted the peanuts until they were fragrant. 我把花生干烤到香味扑鼻。

|例句：The comedian dry-roasted the politicians in his routine.  这位喜剧演员在他的表演中干巴巴地讽刺了政客们。|近义词：roast, bake,  parch,  criticize,  tease|反义词: boil,  steam,  praise|词性：动词，形容词
