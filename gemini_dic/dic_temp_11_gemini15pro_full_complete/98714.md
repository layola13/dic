insurances:/ɪnˈʃʊrənsɪz/
n.
保险（复数）
The practice or arrangement by which a company or government agency provides a guarantee of compensation for specified loss, damage, illness, or death in return for payment of a premium.
He has several insurances, including health, life, and car insurance. 他有几种保险，包括健康、人寿和汽车保险。
Synonyms: coverage, policies
Antonyms: vulnerability, risk
词性: 名词
