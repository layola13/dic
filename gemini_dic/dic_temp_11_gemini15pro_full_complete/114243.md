mailers:/ˈmeɪlərz/
noun
邮寄者；邮递员；邮寄广告；邮件发送者
People or companies that send mail, especially advertising or promotional material.
The mailers sent out thousands of flyers to promote their new product. 邮寄者发出了数千份传单来宣传他们的新产品。
senders, advertisers, distributors
recipients
