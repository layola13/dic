fingerings:/ˈfɪŋɡərɪŋz/
n.
指法;指法符号;指摸;触摸
The way in which a musician uses their fingers to play an instrument, especially a stringed instrument, or a written representation of this.  Also, the act of touching or feeling something with the fingers.
The violinist's fingerings were precise and delicate.  这位小提琴家的指法精准而细腻。
He examined the fabric, testing its texture with his fingerings. 他检查了布料，用手指触摸测试其质地。
Synonyms: technique, touch, handling, manipulation
Antonyms: (For the musical sense)  No direct antonym, perhaps "misfingering" or "incorrect technique". (For the tactile sense) No direct antonym
