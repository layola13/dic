masticot:/ˈmæstɪkɒt/ | n. | 黄丹，铅黄，密陀僧 | A yellow pigment, lead monoxide (PbO), used in painting and as a drier in certain paints and varnishes.  It is also known as litharge when in a flaky form. | The artist mixed masticot with linseed oil to create a rich, opaque yellow. 艺术家用黄丹和亚麻籽油调制出一种浓郁、不透明的黄色。 | Synonyms: lead monoxide, litharge, yellow lead, massicot | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
