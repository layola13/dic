geologies:/dʒiˈɒlədʒiz/
noun
地质学（复数）
The study of the Earth, its structure, and its history, including the study of rocks, minerals, fossils, and landforms.  Multiple branches of geology exist, each focusing on a specific aspect of the Earth.
The geologies of Mars and Earth are surprisingly different. 火星和地球的地质情况惊人地不同。
近义词: earth sciences
反义词: (无直接反义词)
词性:n.


Etymology:
From geology + -es (plural suffix).  Geology itself comes from the Greek "gē" (earth) and "logia" (study of).
