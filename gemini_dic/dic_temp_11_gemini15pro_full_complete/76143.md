fuse:/fjuːz/ (US) /fjuːz/ (UK)| n. v.|保险丝；导火线；熔断器；融合；合并|A safety device that melts to protect an electrical circuit from excessive current.  As a verb, it means to melt or join together.|例句：The fuse blew and all the lights went out. 保险丝烧断了，所有的灯都灭了。|近义词：melt, blend, combine, merge|反义词:separate, divide|noun, verb

fuse:/fjuːz/ (US) /fjuːz/ (UK)| n. v.|导火线；引信|A cord or other device that is lit to ignite an explosive.|例句：He lit the fuse and ran. 他点燃了导火索就跑了。|近义词：wick, detonator|反义词:None|noun, verb
