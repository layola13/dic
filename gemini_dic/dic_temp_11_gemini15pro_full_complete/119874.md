miliolitic:/ˌmɪliəˈlɪtɪk/ | adj. |粟米状的；由有孔虫目介壳组成的| Composed of, or resembling, the minute shells of foraminifera, especially of the family Miliolidae. | The miliolitic limestone is common in the region.  该地区的粟米状石灰岩很常见。| The beach is composed of fine, miliolitic sand.  这个海滩是由细密的粟米状沙子组成的。 |近义词: foraminiferal |反义词: None |词性: adjective
