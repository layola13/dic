nonpardoning:/ˌnɒnpɑ́ːrdənɪŋ/ | adj. | 不可饶恕的，不原谅的 |  Not pardoning; unforgiving; relentless. |  His nonpardoning attitude made it difficult for others to approach him.  他不可饶恕的态度让他难以接近。| 近义词: unforgiving, relentless, implacable, inexorable | 反义词: forgiving, merciful, lenient | 词性:形容词

Etymology:
From non- + pardoning.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "without," and "pardoning" comes from the verb "pardon," which ultimately derives from the Old French "pardoner," meaning "to forgive" or "to excuse."
