bookkeeper:/ˈbʊkkiːpər/| n. |簿记员，记账员|A person whose job is to keep records of the financial affairs of a business.|例句：The bookkeeper keeps track of all the company's transactions.  簿记员跟踪公司所有交易。|例句：She works as a bookkeeper for a small firm. 她在一个小公司担任簿记员。|近义词：accountant, clerk |反义词: None |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From book (n.) + keep (v.) + -er (suffix forming agent nouns).
