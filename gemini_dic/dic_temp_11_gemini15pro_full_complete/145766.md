overcuring:/ˌoʊvərˈkjʊrɪŋ/
noun
过度固化/过度熟化
Excessive curing or hardening of a material, typically a polymer, beyond the optimal point, resulting in degradation of properties.
The overcuring of the resin made it brittle.  树脂过度固化使其变脆。
Overcuring can lead to cracking in concrete.  混凝土过度养护会导致开裂。
Synonyms: overbaking, overprocessing
Antonyms: undercuring
n.
