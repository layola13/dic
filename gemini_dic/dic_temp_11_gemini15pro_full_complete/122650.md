modishly:/ˈmɒdɪʃli/ /ˈmɑːdɪʃli/
adjective
时髦地；流行地
In a fashionable or stylish manner.

She was modishly dressed in the latest designer clothes. 她穿着最新的名牌服装，打扮得很时髦。

The apartment was modishly decorated with minimalist furniture. 这套公寓用极简主义家具装饰，非常时尚。

近义词: fashionably, stylishly, trendily
反义词: unfashionably, dowdily, outmodedly
词性: adverb

词源:
From "modish" + "-ly."  "Modish" comes from "mode" (fashion) + "-ish" (suffix indicating resembling or characteristic of).  Ultimately from French "mode" and Latin "modus" (manner, measure).
