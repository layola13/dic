bewailments:/bɪˈweɪlmənts/| n. |悲叹，哀悼，恸哭|Expressions of deep sorrow or regret, often through crying or complaining. |The air was filled with the bewailments of the mourners.  空气中充满了送葬者的悲叹。|Synonyms: lamentations, wailings, moans, complaints, grievances|Antonyms: rejoicings, celebrations|noun
