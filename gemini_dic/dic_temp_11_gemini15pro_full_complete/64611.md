eruptivenesses:/ɪˈrʌptɪvnəsɪz/
n.
爆发性，喷发性（复数）
The quality or state of being eruptive; characterized by eruptions or outbursts.
The eruptivenesses of the volcanoes varied greatly, some being explosive while others were effusive. 这些火山的爆发性差异很大，有些是爆炸性的，而有些是溢流式的。
The geologist studied the eruptivenesses of different types of magma. 地质学家研究了不同类型岩浆的爆发性。
Synonyms: explosiveness, volatility, instability
Antonyms: stability, calmness, quiescence
noun
