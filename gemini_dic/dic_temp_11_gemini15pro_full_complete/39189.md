congeniality:/kənˌdʒiːniˈælɪti/ | n. | congeniality |  The quality of being pleasant and agreeable; friendliness and agreeableness.|  The atmosphere of congeniality at the party made everyone feel welcome.  派对上融洽的氛围让每个人都感到宾至如归。| synonym:  agreeableness, affability, compatibility, cordiality,  | antonym:  disagreeableness, animosity, incompatibility, hostility | noun


congenial:/kənˈdʒiːniəl/ | adj. | 友善的，合意的，适宜的 | (of a person) Pleasant because of a personality, qualities, or interests that are similar to one's own.  (of a thing) Pleasant or agreeable because suited to one's taste or inclination. | We spent a relaxed evening with congenial friends. 我们与志趣相投的朋友们度过了一个轻松的夜晚。  The department provides a congenial atmosphere for research.  该系为研究提供了适宜的氛围。 | synonym: agreeable, pleasant, amiable, compatible | antonym: disagreeable, unpleasant, unfriendly, incompatible | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *congenialis, from Latin com- ‘together’ + genius ‘guardian spirit’.
