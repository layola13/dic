entrepot:/ˈɑːntrəpoʊ/ (US), /ˈɒntrəpəʊ/ (UK)| n.|转口港，贸易中心，仓库|A port, city, or other center to which goods are brought for import and export, and for collection, distribution, and transshipment.|Singapore has thrived as an entrepot for Southeast Asia. 新加坡作为东南亚的转口港而繁荣发展。|Synonyms: trading post, distribution center, warehouse, depot|Antonyms: factory, origin, source|Noun
