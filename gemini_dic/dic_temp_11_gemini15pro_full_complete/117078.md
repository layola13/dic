meadowing:/ˈmedəʊɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈmedəʊɪŋ/ (US)
adjective
草地的；草场多的；长满草的
covered with meadows; resembling a meadow
例句：The meadowing hills were dotted with sheep.  草山丘上点缀着绵羊。

例句：We walked through the meadowing valley. 我们走过长满草的山谷。

近义词：grassy, verdant, pastoral
反义词：barren, arid, desolate
词性：adjective
