agitations:/ˌædʒɪˈteɪʃənz/| n. |激动，不安，骚动，煽动|a state of anxiety or nervous excitement|例句：The recent political agitations have caused widespread unrest. 最近的政治骚动引起了广泛的不安。

例句：Her constant agitations about the upcoming exam were affecting her sleep. 她对即将到来的考试持续的焦虑影响了她的睡眠。|近义词：anxiety, unrest, turmoil, disturbance, disquiet, nervousness, agitation|反义词：calm, peace, tranquility, composure|词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Middle English *agitacioun, from Late Latin agitātiō (“a shaking, a tossing, disquiet”), from Latin agitō (“I shake, agitate”), frequentative of agō (“I drive, act, do”).
