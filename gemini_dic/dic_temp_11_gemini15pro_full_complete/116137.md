marysole: /ˈmɛəriˌsoʊl/ (American) /ˈmɛəriˌsəʊl/ (British) | n. | 玛丽珍鞋 | A type of shoe, typically for women or children, characterized by a closed toe and one or more straps across the instep.  Derived from Mary Jane, a character in the comic strip Buster Brown who wore such shoes. |  She wore a pair of red marysoles to the party. 她穿着一双红色的玛丽珍鞋去参加派对。 |  Mary Janes, T-bar shoes | sandals, sneakers | noun
