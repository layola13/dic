counter-bill:/ˈkaʊntərˌbɪl/
n.
反议案，对立议案
A bill introduced into a legislature to oppose or amend another bill under consideration.
The senator introduced a counter-bill to challenge the proposed tax increase.  参议员提出了一项反议案，以挑战拟议的增税方案。
alternative bill, opposing bill
original bill
noun
