laparotomy:/ˌlæpəˈrɒtəmi/ /ˌlæpəˈrɑːtəmi/| n. |剖腹手术|A surgical incision into the abdominal cavity, for diagnosis or in preparation for major surgery.|The patient underwent an emergency laparotomy after the car accident. 病人在车祸后接受了紧急剖腹手术。|
|例句：The surgeon performed a laparotomy to remove the appendix. 外科医生进行了剖腹手术以切除阑尾。|近义词：celiotomy|反义词: None|词性:noun
