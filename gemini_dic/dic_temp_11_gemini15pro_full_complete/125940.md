mushroom:/ˈmʌʃˌrum/ /ˈmʌʃruːm/| n.|蘑菇|A fleshy spore-bearing fruiting body of a fungus, typically produced above ground on soil or on its food source.|I love mushrooms on my pizza.|Synonyms: toadstool, fungus, truffle|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the physical object, but one could consider "poisonous mushroom" as an antonymous concept.)|noun
