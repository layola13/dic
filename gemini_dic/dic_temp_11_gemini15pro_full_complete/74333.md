fragility:/frəˈdʒɪləti/| n.|脆弱性，易碎性|The fragility of the vase made me nervous.  这个花瓶的易碎性让我很紧张。|例句：The fragility of the global economy is a concern. 全球经济的脆弱性令人担忧。

The old woman's bones are marked by fragility. 老妇人的骨骼因脆弱而显得格外突出。|近义词：delicacy, weakness, brittleness, frailty|反义词:strength, robustness, resilience, toughness|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *fragilitatem, from Latin fragilis ("fragile") + -itatem (suffix forming abstract nouns from adjectives).  Ultimately from Latin frangere ("to break").
