electrophotographic:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˌfoʊtəˈɡræfɪk/
adjective|静电摄影的|Relating to or produced by electrophotography, a process of making photographic images using an electrostatic charge to form an image on a light-sensitive surface. |Electrophotographic printers are commonly used in offices. 静电摄影打印机常用于办公室。|
近义词:xerographic|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective


electrophotography:/ɪˌlɛktroʊfəˈtɒɡrəfi/
noun|静电摄影术|A photocopying process that uses electrostatic charges to create an image on a light-sensitive surface. |Electrophotography revolutionized the copying industry. 静电摄影彻底改变了复印行业。|近义词:xerography|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
