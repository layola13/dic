mannite:/ˈmænɪtaɪt/ |n.|甘露醇; 甘露糖醇|A white crystalline sweet alcohol C6H14O6 occurring naturally in some plants and fruits (e.g., manna) and used especially as a diuretic and laxative and in making resins and explosives. |Mannite is used in the food industry as a sweetener and texturizer. 甘露醇在食品工业中用作甜味剂和质构剂。|Mannitol is sometimes given intravenously to reduce swelling in the brain.  甘露醇有时会静脉注射以减轻脑肿胀。|Synonyms: mannitol|Antonyms: None|Noun
