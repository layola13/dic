drillabilities:/ˌdrɪləˈbɪlətiz/| n.|可钻性(复数)|The ease with which a material can be drilled.  It's influenced by factors like hardness, composition, and structure.|The drillabilities of different rock formations vary greatly. 不同岩层的可钻性差异很大。|Synonym:  machinability, boreability|Antonym: impenetrability|Noun
