overfondles:/ˌoʊvərˈfɑːndlz/
verb
过度爱抚，过分抚摸
to fondle excessively, often with a suggestion of inappropriateness.
The uncle overfondled his niece, making her feel uncomfortable.  那个叔叔过度爱抚他的侄女，让她感到不舒服。
He overfondles his dog sometimes, to the point where it gets annoyed. 他有时过度抚摸他的狗，以至于它很生气。
synonyms: paw, maul, caress excessively
antonyms: neglect, ignore
v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + fondle. "Over-" acts as an intensifier, meaning "excessively" or "too much."  "Fondle" comes from the earlier "fond" (foolishly loving), which is related to the Dutch "fonnen" (to be foolish).
