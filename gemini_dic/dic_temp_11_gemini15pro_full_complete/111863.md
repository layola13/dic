logistically:/ləˈdʒɪstɪkli/
adverb|逻辑上地；后勤地|In terms of logistics; concerning the detailed organization of a complex operation involving many people and resources.|例句：The plan was sound strategically but not logistically.  这个计划战略上是合理的，但在后勤方面却行不通。|近义词：practically, operationally|反义词: impractically|词性:副词
