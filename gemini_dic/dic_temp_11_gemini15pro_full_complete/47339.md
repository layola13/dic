de-shells:/diːˈʃɛlz/
|v.|去壳，剥壳|Removes the shell from something, typically nuts or shellfish.|The machine de-shells peanuts quickly and efficiently. 这台机器快速高效地去壳花生。|
The chef de-shelled the prawns before adding them to the paella.  厨师在把虾仁加入西班牙海鲜饭之前先去壳。|近义词：shell, husk, shuck|反义词:shell|词性:动词
