nonadventitious:/ˌnɒnˌædvenˈtɪʃəs/
adjective
非外来的；固有的；内生的
Not adventitious; inherent; intrinsic.  Not coming from outside.
The plant's deep roots are nonadventitious, developing from the seed itself. 这种植物的深根不是外来的，而是从种子本身发育而来的。
The lawyer argued that the damage was nonadventitious, a direct result of the defendant's actions. 律师辩称，损害不是偶然的，而是被告行为的直接结果。

Synonyms: inherent, innate, intrinsic, congenital, essential, natural
Antonyms: adventitious, acquired, extrinsic, external, foreign, incidental
词性: 形容词
