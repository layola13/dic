nondeductions:/ˌnɑːndɪˈdʌkʃənz/| n.|不可扣除额；不可扣除项目| Amounts that cannot be subtracted from gross income to reduce taxable income. | Example:  Charitable contributions exceeding a certain limit are among the nondeductions when calculating your tax liability.  慈善捐款超过一定限额是计算纳税义务时的不可扣除项目之一。

Example:  He carefully reviewed the list of nondeductions to ensure he wasn't missing any potential savings. 他仔细审查了不可扣除项目清单，以确保没有遗漏任何潜在的节省。|近义词:non-deductible expenses, disallowed expenses|反义词:deductions, deductible expenses|词性:名词(plural)
