ecotourist:/ˈiːkəʊˌtʊərɪst/| n. |生态旅游者|A tourist who visits places of natural beauty and interest, and tries to do so in a way that will not damage the environment.|例句：Ecotourists often choose destinations with unique ecosystems. 生态旅游者通常会选择拥有独特生态系统的目的地。

例句：The ecotourist lodge was built using sustainable materials. 这家生态旅游者旅馆是用可持续材料建造的。|近义词：nature tourist, sustainable tourist|反义词：mass tourist|词性：名词
