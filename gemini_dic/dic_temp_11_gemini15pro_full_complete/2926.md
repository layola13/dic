advisorily:/ədˈvʌɪzərəli/
adv.
劝告地，忠告地
in a manner of giving advice; like an advisor
He spoke advisorily to the young man about his career choices. 他以劝告的口吻对这位年轻人谈论了他的职业选择。
近义词: advisingly, counselingly
反义词: rashly, impulsively
词性: 副词


词源:
From advise (v.) + -or (n. suffix) + -ily (adv. suffix)
来自动词advise（劝告）+ 名词后缀-or（…的人）+ 副词后缀-ily（…地）
