jitteriest:/ˈdʒɪtəriɪst/
adjective
最紧张的，最不安的
Superlative adjective of jittery: most jittery.  Characterized by a high degree of nervousness and anxiety.
Her hands were the jitteriest I'd ever seen before a performance.  演出前她的手是我见过的最紧张的。
近义词:nerviest, edgiest, jumpiest
反义词:calmest, coolest, most relaxed
词性:adjective

jitter:/ˈdʒɪtər/
noun
紧张不安；抖动；(电子信号等的)不稳定
A state of nervousness or unease.  A slight irregular movement.  A fluctuation in the timing of a signal or process.
He had the jitters before his big presentation.  他在重要的演讲前感到紧张不安。
近义词:nerves, anxiety, tremors
反义词:calm, composure, stillness
词性:noun

jitter:/ˈdʒɪtər/
verb
紧张不安；抖动；(信号或图像)产生不规则变化
To be nervous or apprehensive.  To tremble or shake slightly.  To cause a signal or image to exhibit irregular variations.
He jittered nervously as he waited for the results.  他紧张不安地等待着结果。
近义词:fidget, tremble, quiver
反义词:relax, calm down, be still
词性:verb

词源:
From the verb "jitter," which likely originated as an imitative word, suggesting the sound or feeling of trembling.
