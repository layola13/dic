orthokinetic:/ˌɔːrθəʊkɪˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
正动力的，因流体运动引起的
Relating to or denoting movement of particles in a fluid flow, as a result of different velocities of the fluid itself.
The orthokinetic aggregation of small particles in the flow is an important factor to consider.  小颗粒在流动中的正动力聚集是一个需要考虑的重要因素。
Orthokinetic flocculation enhances the removal of suspended solids in water treatment. 正动力絮凝增强了水处理中悬浮物的去除。
近义词:dynamic
反义词:static
词性:adjective

词源:
From ortho- + kinetic.  Ortho- 表示“直的，正确的”，kinetic 表示“运动的”。
