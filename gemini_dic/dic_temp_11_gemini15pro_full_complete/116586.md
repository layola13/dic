matricentricity:/ˌmeɪtrɪˈsɛntrɪsɪti/ | n. | 母系中心；母权制 | A social system in which the mother or female is the head of the family and descent is traced through the female line.  A focus on mothers and motherhood. |  Example 1: The anthropologist's research focused on the matricentricity of the Minangkabau culture in Indonesia.  这位人类学家的研究集中在印尼米南佳保文化的母系中心制。

Example 2:  Some sociologists argue that the increasing matricentricity of single-parent families is a societal concern. 一些社会学家认为，单亲家庭日益增长的母系中心制是一个社会问题。 | Near Synonyms: matriarchy, matrilineality, female-centered | Antonyms: patriarchy, patrilineality, male-centered | noun
