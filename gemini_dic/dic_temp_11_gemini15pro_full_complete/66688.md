exogenous:/ɛkˈsɒdʒɪnəs/ /ɛkˈsɑːdʒɪnəs/| adj.|外生的，外源的|Originating externally; caused by factors outside the organism or system.|例句：Exogenous factors, such as sunlight, can affect the growth of plants. 外部因素，例如阳光，会影响植物的生长。

例句：The company's problems were largely exogenous, caused by a sudden downturn in the global economy. 该公司的问题很大程度上是外生的，是由全球经济的突然衰退引起的。|近义词：external, outer, outside|反义词：endogenous, internal, inside|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἔξω (éxō, “outside”) + -γενής (-genḗs, “produced, born”).
