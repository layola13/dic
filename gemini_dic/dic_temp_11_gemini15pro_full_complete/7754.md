anorthoclase:/əˈnɔːrθəˌkleɪs/
n.
歪长石
A triclinic alkali feldspar, (Na,K)AlSi3O8, similar to orthoclase but with a higher sodium content.
Anorthoclase is a common constituent of many volcanic rocks. 歪长石是许多火山岩的常见成分。
Anorthoclase crystals can exhibit a beautiful play of colors.  歪长石晶体可以展现美丽的色彩。
synonyms: feldspar, alkali feldspar
