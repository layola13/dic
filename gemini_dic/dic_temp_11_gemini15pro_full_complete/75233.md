frolicker:/ˈfrɑːlɪkər/ | n. |嬉戏者，玩闹的人 |  A person who frolics; someone who engages in playful activity. | The children were frolickers in the park, chasing butterflies and laughing. 孩子们在公园里嬉戏，追逐蝴蝶，欢笑着。| The beach was full of frolickers enjoying the sunshine. 海滩上到处都是享受阳光的玩闹的人。 |  Synonyms: reveler, merrymaker, sport,  gamboler | Antonyms: killjoy, grouch | noun
