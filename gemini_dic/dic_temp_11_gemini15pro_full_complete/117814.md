melissyl:/məˈlɪsɪl/| adj.|三十烷基的；蜜蜂似的|Relating to or derived from melissa (balm mint) or containing the melissyl radical, specifically designating the triacontanyl radical, C30H61, of melissic acid.  It is often used in chemistry to describe a specific hydrocarbon chain.|Melissyl alcohol is a component of beeswax.  三十烷醇是蜂蜡的一种成分。|近义词:triacontyl|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective
