counter-price:/ˈkaʊntər praɪs/| n.|还价，对价，对抗价格|A price offered in response to another price, especially a lower price offered by a buyer in response to a seller's initial price.  It can also refer to a price that competes with another price in the market.| The seller asked for $500, but I offered a counter-price of $400. 卖家要价500美元，但我还价400美元。|synonyms: counteroffer, counterbid, competing price|antonyms: asking price, initial offer|noun

counter-price:/ˈkaʊntər praɪs/| adj.|对抗价格的，还价的|Relating to or involving a counter-price.  This is less common usage than the noun form.|  The counter-price offer was accepted. 还价被接受了。|synonyms: counter-offering, counter-bidding, competitive|antonyms: initial, original|adjective
