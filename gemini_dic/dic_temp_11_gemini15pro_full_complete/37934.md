completers:/kəmˈpliːtərz/
noun
完成者；完成的人或事物
Those who finish a task, course, or project are considered completers.
The completers of the marathon received medals. 完成马拉松比赛的人获得了奖牌。
synonyms: finishers, achievers
antonyms: non-completers, dropouts
