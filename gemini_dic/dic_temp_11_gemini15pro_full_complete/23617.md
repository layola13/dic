branchia: /ˈbræŋkiə/ (US), /ˈbræŋkɪə/ (UK)| n. |鳃|A respiratory organ of aquatic animals, typically a thin-walled projection from the body with a feathery or platelike structure, specialized for the exchange of gases between the blood and the surrounding water.|The fish's branchia were clearly visible through its translucent skin. 这条鱼的鳃透过它半透明的皮肤清晰可见。

The axolotl retains its branchia throughout its life.  蝾螈一生都保留着它的鳃。

|近义词：gill|反义词: lung|词性: noun
