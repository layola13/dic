nonoccupational:/ˌnɒnˌɒkjuˈpeɪʃənəl/
adjective
非职业的；非工作的
Not related to one's job or profession; not done as part of one's paid employment.
Many people suffer from nonoccupational back pain. 许多人患有非职业性背痛。
Synonyms: non-professional, avocational, leisure, spare-time, extracurricular
Antonyms: occupational, professional, vocational, job-related, work-related
词性: adjective
