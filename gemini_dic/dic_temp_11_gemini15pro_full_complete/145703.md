overcounts:/ˌoʊvərˈkaʊnts/ | n. | 多计，重复计算 | An act of counting something more than once, resulting in a higher than accurate total. | The cashier overcounted the items and I was overcharged. 收银员多算了商品，我被多收了钱。| overestimates, exaggerates | undercounts, underestimates | noun

overcount:/ˌoʊvərˈkaʊnt/ | v. | 多计，重复计算 | To count something more than once, leading to an inflated total. |  The survey overcounted the number of households with pets. 这项调查多算了有宠物的家庭数量。| overestimate, exaggerate | undercount, underestimate | verb
