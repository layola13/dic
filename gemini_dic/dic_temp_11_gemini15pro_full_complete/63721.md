ephippium:/ɪˈfɪpiəm/| n.|鞍状物；介壳；（水蚤的）卵鞍|A thickened, saddle-like modification of the carapace in certain crustaceans, such as water fleas (Cladocera), that encloses the winter eggs.|The ephippium protects the dormant eggs until favorable conditions return. 水蚤的卵鞍保护休眠卵直到有利条件回归。|synonyms: saddle, carapace modification|antonyms: N/A|noun
