commat:/ˈkɒmæt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmæt/ (US)| n. |逗号, @符号|A punctuation mark (,) indicating a pause between parts of a sentence. It is also used to represent the "at" symbol (@) in email addresses.|例句：Please add a commat between the two clauses. 请在这两个从句之间加个逗号。|例句：My email address is example@email.com. 我的电子邮件地址是example@email.com。 |近义词: @, comma (in some contexts) |反义词: None|词性: noun
