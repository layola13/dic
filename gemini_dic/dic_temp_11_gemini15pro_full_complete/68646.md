far-reaching:/ˌfɑːrˈriːtʃɪŋ/
adjective
影响深远的; 广泛的
extending or having influence far into the future; having extensive or widespread consequences.
The discovery of penicillin had far-reaching effects on medicine.  青霉素的发现对医学产生了深远的影响。

The new policy will have far-reaching implications for the economy. 新政策将对经济产生深远的影响。


synonym
extensive, widespread, broad, significant, important, consequential, momentous
antonym
limited, narrow, insignificant, trivial, unimportant
adjective
