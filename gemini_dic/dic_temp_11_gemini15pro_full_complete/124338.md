moths:/mɒθs/ (UK), /mɔːθs/ (US)| n. |飞蛾|Nocturnal flying insects with feathery antennae, often attracted to light.  They are related to butterflies but are typically less colorful and active at night. | The moths were fluttering around the porch light. 飞蛾在门廊灯周围飞舞。|Synonyms:  night butterflies|Antonyms: butterflies|Noun
