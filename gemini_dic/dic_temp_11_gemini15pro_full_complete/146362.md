overflattened:/ˌoʊvərˈflætnd/
adjective|过度扁平化的|Excessively flattened; flattened beyond what is normal or desirable.  This word is created by adding the prefix "over-" (meaning "excessively") to the past participle of the verb "flatten."|The overflattened dough wouldn't rise properly.|Overly flattened, excessively flattened|Inflated, puffed up|adjective
overflatten:/ˌoʊvərˈflætən/
verb|过度压扁|To flatten something excessively, beyond what is normal or desirable.|Be careful not to overflatten the clay, or it will crack.|Flatten too much, overcompress|Inflate, puff up|verb
