counter-argue:/ˌkaʊntərˈɑːrɡjuː/ | v. | 反驳，提出相反的论点 | To argue against something that someone else has said. | He counter-argued that the evidence was flawed. 他反驳说证据有缺陷。|  He tried to counter-argue, but she had a strong point. 他试图反驳，但她观点很强。| 近义词: rebut, refute, dispute | 反义词: agree, concede, support | 动词
