carnifex:/ˈkɑːrnɪfɛks/| n.|刽子手，行刑者|An executioner; a person who inflicts capital punishment. |In ancient Rome, the carnifex was a despised figure, tasked with carrying out executions. 在古罗马，刽子手是一个被人鄙视的角色，负责执行死刑。

The carnifex prepared the instruments of death. 刽子手准备了行刑工具。|Synonyms: executioner, headsman|Antonyms: savior, protector|Noun
