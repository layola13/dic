nonromanticism:/ˌnɒnroʊˈmæntɪsɪzəm/
n.
非浪漫主义
Lack of or opposition to romanticism, especially in literature and the arts.  A rejection of the idealized, emotional, and imaginative qualities characteristic of romanticism.
The art exhibit showcased nonromanticism, focusing on realistic portrayals of everyday life.  这个艺术展展示了非浪漫主义，侧重于对日常生活的现实描绘。
近义词: realism, classicism
反义词: romanticism
词性: noun

nonromantic:/ˌnɒnroʊˈmæntɪk/
adj.
非浪漫的
Not romantic; lacking in or averse to romance.  Practical, realistic, or unsentimental.
Their relationship was quite nonromantic, based more on shared interests and mutual respect. 他们的关系很不浪漫，更多的是基于共同的兴趣和相互尊重。
近义词: practical, realistic, unsentimental
反义词: romantic
词性: adjective
