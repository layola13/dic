chryselephantine:/ˌkrɪsəlˈɛfənˌtaɪn/
adj.
金银镶嵌的，用黄金和象牙装饰的
Made of or decorated with gold and ivory.
The chryselephantine statue of Zeus at Olympia was one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World. 奥林匹亚的宙斯巨像，用黄金和象牙装饰，是古代世界七大奇迹之一。

近义词:gold and ivory, ornate, elaborate
反义词:plain, simple
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *chryselephantinos*, from *chrysos* (gold) + *elephas*, *elephantos* (ivory).  源自希腊语 *chryselephantinos*，由 *chrysos*（黄金）+ *elephas*，*elephantos*（象牙）组成。
