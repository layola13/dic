creatives:/ˈkriːeɪtɪvz/
noun
创意人员；广告素材
People who are creative, especially in the advertising or design industries.
The agency's creatives came up with a brilliant new campaign. 这家代理公司的创意人员想出了一个绝妙的新广告活动。
Synonyms: artists, designers, innovators, thinkers
Antonyms:  conformists, imitators
