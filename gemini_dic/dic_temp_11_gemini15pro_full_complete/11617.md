ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish _armadillo_, diminutive of _armado_ "armed," past participle of _armar_ "to arm," from Latin _armatus_, past participle of _armare_ "to arm," from _arma_ "weapons, arms."  The name refers to the animal's bony armor.
