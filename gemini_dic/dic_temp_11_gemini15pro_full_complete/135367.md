nonpercipiences:/ˌnɒnpərˈsɪpiənsɪz/
n.
lack of perception or awareness; inability to perceive or understand.
The student's nonpercipience of the underlying themes in the novel led to a poor grade.  这个学生对小说中潜在主题的缺乏认识导致了他的低分。
nonpercipience of the political climate 缺乏对政治气候的认识
近义词:  unawareness, insensitivity, obtuseness, incomprehension
反义词: awareness, perception, sensitivity, comprehension
词性: noun


Etymology:
From non- (not) + percipience (awareness, perception).  Percipience comes from Latin percipere "to seize, grasp," from per- "thoroughly" + capere "to take."
