marrying: /ˈmærɪɪŋ/ (US), /ˈmæriɪŋ/ (UK)
| v. |结婚，嫁，娶; (与...)紧密结合|The act of getting married, or the state of being married.  Also, the act of uniting two things closely.|  She is marrying her childhood sweetheart next spring. 她明年春天要和她青梅竹马的爱人结婚。| synonyms: wedding, uniting, joining | antonyms: divorcing, separating | verb

marrying: /ˈmærɪɪŋ/ (US), /ˈmæriɪŋ/ (UK)
| adj. |适于结婚的|Suitable for marriage.| He has a marrying kind of face. 他长着一张适于结婚的脸。（较少用，通常用marriageable）| synonyms: marriageable, nubile | antonyms: unmarriageable | adjective
