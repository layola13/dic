evacuator:/ɪˈvækjueɪtər/ | n. | 疏散者；疏散器；抽气机 | A person or device that evacuates something or someone. | The evacuator helped the family leave the flood zone.  疏散人员帮助这家人离开了洪水区。| Synonyms: remover, clearer, emptier | Antonyms: filler, packer | noun

evacuate:/ɪˈvækjueɪt/ | v. | 疏散；撤离；排空 | To remove someone or something from a dangerous place. |  They evacuated the building because of the fire. 他们因为火灾疏散了大楼。| Synonyms: clear, remove, empty | Antonyms: fill, pack | verb
