derogate:/ˈdɛrəɡeɪt/
v. | 贬低，损害，毁损 | to lessen the merit or reputation of; detract from |  His actions derogated from his authority. 他的行为损害了他的权威。 | synonyms: disparage, belittle, depreciate | antonyms: enhance, elevate, praise | verb

derogate:/ˈdɛrəɡeɪt/
n. | 毁损，贬低 |  (rare) lessening; disparagement | The derogate of his character was undeserved.  对他品格的贬低是不应得的。 | synonyms: disparagement, detraction | antonyms: elevation, enhancement | noun

derogatory:/dɪˈrɒɡət(ə)ri/
adj. | 贬低的， 损毁的，减损的 | expressive of low opinion; disparaging |  She made derogatory remarks about her colleagues. 她对同事发表了贬低的言论。| synonyms: disparaging, belittleing, uncomplimentary | antonyms: complimentary, flattering, praising | adjective
