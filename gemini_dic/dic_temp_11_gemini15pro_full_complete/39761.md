consolidant:/kənˈsɑːlɪdənt/| n. adj.|加固剂，巩固的|A substance used to strengthen or solidify something, especially a fragile material.  It can also function as an adjective meaning making something stronger or more solid.|The conservator used a consolidant to stabilize the crumbling fresco.  修复员使用了一种加固剂来稳定破碎的壁画。|近义词：stabilizer, fixative, reinforcement, strengthening (adj.)|反义词: weakening (adj.)|词性:noun, adjective
词源:From Latin consolidare "to make firm," from com- "together" + solidare "to make solid," from solidus "solid."
