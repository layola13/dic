metazoic:/ˌmɛtəˈzoʊɪk/
adjective
后生动物的
Relating to or denoting animals whose bodies consist of numerous cells differentiated into tissues and organs and usually sexually reproducing.
Metazoic animals are multicellular and exhibit a greater degree of cellular specialization than their protozoan counterparts.
后生动物是多细胞的，并且比它们的原生动物对应物表现出更大程度的细胞特化。
Metazoic organisms exhibit a wide range of body plans and adaptations.
后生动物表现出广泛的身体构造和适应性。
Synonym: multicellular
Antonym: unicellular
