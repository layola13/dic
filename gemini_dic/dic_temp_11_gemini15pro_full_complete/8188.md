anti-dilutive:/ˌænti daɪˈluːtɪv/
| adj. |反稀释的| Preventing dilution or reduction in value.  Relating to a transaction or security that increases, or does not decrease, earnings per share or ownership percentage.| The company decided not to issue the new shares because it would be anti-dilutive.| 近义词: accretive | 反义词: dilutive |词性:形容词
