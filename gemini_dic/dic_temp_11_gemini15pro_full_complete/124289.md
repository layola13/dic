mother-figure:/ˈmʌðər ˌfɪɡjər/| n.|母亲般的角色；女性权威人物|A woman who provides a child with maternal care and support, even if she is not the child's biological mother.|例句：She's been a real mother-figure to me since my own mother died.  自从我母亲去世以来，她一直是我的母亲般的角色。

例句：My aunt was a strong mother-figure in my life.  我的阿姨在我生命中是一位强有力的母亲般的角色。


|近义词：maternal figure, mother substitute, role model|反义词:|词性:noun
