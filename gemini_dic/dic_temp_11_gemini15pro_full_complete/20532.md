She rose to prominence as the lead vocalist of the alternative rock band the Sugarcubes before embarking on a successful solo career.| Björk's latest album is experimental and pushes the boundaries of electronic music. 比约克的最新专辑很实验性，挑战了电子音乐的界限。|Synonyms: artist, musician, performer|Antonyms: amateur, nobody|Noun
