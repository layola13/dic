exempted:/ɪɡˈzɛmptɪd/
adjective
被豁免的，免除的
freed from an obligation or liability imposed on others.
He was exempted from military service because of his poor health. 他因健康状况不佳而免服兵役。
exempt
liable, responsible
adjective

exempt:/ɪɡˈzɛmpt/
verb
免除，豁免
free (someone or something) from something (typically a duty, payment, or penalty)
Charities are exempted from paying the tax. 慈善机构免缴税款。
excuse, release
obligate, compel
