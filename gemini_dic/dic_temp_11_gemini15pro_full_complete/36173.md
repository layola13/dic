cofferlike: /ˈkɒfəlaɪk/ (US) /ˈkɒfəlaɪk/ (UK)
adjective
像保险箱的；像盒子的；像金库的
Resembling a coffer; chest-like or box-like, often implying strong, secure storage.
The room was dark and cofferlike.  这个房间又黑又像个保险箱。
The heavy, cofferlike chest held the family's valuable heirlooms.  那个沉重的、像保险箱一样的箱子里装着这家人珍贵的传家宝。
近义词: chest-like, box-like, vault-like
反义词: open, exposed
