NATO:/ˈneɪtoʊ/| n. |北大西洋公约组织|North Atlantic Treaty Organization.  A military alliance formed in 1949 by 12 countries, including the US, Canada, and several Western European nations, to provide collective security against the Soviet Union and its allies.|例句：NATO played a crucial role during the Cold War. 北约在冷战期间发挥了至关重要的作用。|近义词：alliance, coalition|反义词:Warsaw Pact|abbr.
