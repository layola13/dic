covetise: /ˈkʌvətɪs/ | n. | 贪婪，贪欲 | An excessive or inordinate desire for wealth or possessions. |  He was consumed by covetise and would stop at nothing to acquire more wealth. 他被贪婪吞噬，为了获得更多财富不择手段。

She looked at the diamond necklace with undisguised covetise. 她毫不掩饰贪婪地看着那条钻石项链。 | 近义词: greed, avarice, cupidity | 反义词: generosity, contentment | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *coveitise, from coveiter "to covet," from Latin cupiditas "desire, longing, passion," from cupidus "desirous, eager, passionate," from cupere "to desire."
