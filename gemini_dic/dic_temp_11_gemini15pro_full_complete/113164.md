lupulinic:/ˌluːpjʊˈlɪnɪk/| adj.|啤酒花的，含蛇麻腺的|Of, relating to, resembling, or containing lupulin.  Derived from or containing the bitter, resinous substance found in the cones of the hop plant.|The lupulinic aroma of the freshly harvested hops filled the air. 新鲜采摘的啤酒花散发出啤酒花的香气。|Near synonyms: hoppy, bitter, resinous|Antonyms: bland, mild|adjective
