apophasis:/əˈpɒfəsɪs/| n.|欲盖弥彰；假装否定|The rhetorical device of mentioning something by denying that it will be mentioned.|He was a liar—I will not call him a thief. 他是个骗子——我不会说他是个小偷。|近义词：paralipsis, preterition|反义词:emphasis, assertion|noun
