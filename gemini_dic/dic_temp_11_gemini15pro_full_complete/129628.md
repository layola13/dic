non-contingent:/nɒn kənˈtɪndʒənt/| adj.|非偶然的，无条件的，必然的|Not dependent on something else; absolute.  Something that is non-contingent is guaranteed to happen or exist, regardless of other factors. |例句：Her inheritance was non-contingent, meaning she would receive it no matter what. 她的遗产是无条件的，意味着无论如何她都会得到。

例句：The company offered a non-contingent job offer, meaning it wasn't dependent on background checks or other conditions. 这家公司提供了一份无条件的工作邀请，这意味着它不依赖于背景调查或其他条件。|近义词：absolute, unconditional, guaranteed, certain, independent|反义词: contingent, conditional, dependent, uncertain|词性:adjective
