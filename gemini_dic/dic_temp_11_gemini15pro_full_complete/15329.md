backslider:/ˈbækslaɪdər/| n. |滑坡者，倒退者，故态复萌者|A person who lapses into bad habits or abandons a former commitment, especially in terms of religious faith or moral principles.|例句：He was a backslider who returned to his old ways after years of sobriety. 他是一个故态复萌的人，在多年的清醒之后又回到了过去的老路上。

例句：The church welcomed back the backslider with open arms. 教会张开双臂欢迎这位重蹈覆辙的人回来。|近义词：relapse, recidivist, defaulter, apostate|反义词：convert, steadfast, loyal|词性:noun

词源: back + slide + -er.  "back" 表示返回，"slide" 表示滑落，"-er" 表示人或物。因此，backslider 字面意思是“往回滑落的人”。
