danava:/ˈdɑːnəvə/| n. |达纳瓦（印度神话中的恶魔）|In Hindu mythology, a danava is a race of  asuras (demons) or nature spirits. They are often depicted as powerful and adversarial to the devas (gods).|例句：The danava Hiranyakashipu was slain by Lord Vishnu in his Narasimha avatar.  魔王希兰亚卡西普被毗湿奴神以那罗辛哈的化身杀死了。

例句：Many danavas are associated with natural forces like storms and earthquakes. 许多达纳瓦都与风暴和地震等自然力量有关。|近义词：asura, demon, rakshasa|反义词：deva, god|词性:noun
