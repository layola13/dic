lasse:/ˈlɑːsə/ | n. | 小伙子，年轻人（尤指斯堪的纳维亚地区）| A young boy or fellow, especially in Scandinavia. |  例句：The lasse helped his father with the fishing nets.  这个小伙子帮他的父亲整理渔网。| 近义词：lad, boy, youth | 反义词: old man, elder | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Scandinavian, likely Old Norse *lass, related to Old English *læssa "lesser".
