nonroyal:/ˌnɑːnˈrɔɪəl/ | adj. | 非皇室的，平民的 | Not of royal lineage or status; not relating to royalty. |  The nonroyal members of the wedding party were seated in the back. 婚礼中非皇室成员坐在后面。 |  The play focused on the nonroyal lives of people in Elizabethan England.  这部戏剧的重点是伊丽莎白时代英国人民的平民生活。 | 近义词: common, ordinary, plebeian | 反义词: royal, regal | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (not) + "royal" (of or relating to a king or queen). The prefix "non-" comes from Latin, meaning "not."  "Royal" comes from Old French "roial," ultimately from Latin "regalis" (of or belonging to a king).
