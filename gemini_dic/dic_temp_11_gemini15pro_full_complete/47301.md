de-integrated:/diːˈɪntɪɡreɪtɪd/
adj.
去整合的，分解的
Having been separated or broken down into constituent parts, especially after previously being integrated.
The company de-integrated its manufacturing process, separating the assembly line into smaller, independent units.  公司将其制造过程去整合化，将装配线分成更小、独立的单元。
近义词:disintegrated, separated, decentralized
反义词:integrated, combined, unified
词性:形容词

de-integrate:/diːˈɪntɪɡreɪt/
v.
去整合，分解
To separate or break down into constituent parts, especially after previously being integrated.
The new CEO plans to de-integrate the company's various departments to improve efficiency. 新任首席执行官计划将公司的各个部门去整合化，以提高效率。
近义词:disintegrate, separate, decentralize
反义词:integrate, combine, unify
词性:动词
