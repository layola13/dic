nyctitropic:/nɪk.tɪˈtrɒp.ɪk/ | adj. | 夜间活动的，夜间向性的 | Exhibiting nyctitropy; relating to or descriptive of the movements of plants in response to the stimulus of darkness. | Example: The nyctitropic movements of the leaves are fascinating to observe.  这些叶子的夜间运动很吸引人。| Synonym: nyctinastic | Antonym: diurnal | Word type: Adjective

nyctitropic:/nɪk.tɪˈtrɒp.ɪk/ | n. | 夜行动物，夜间向性植物 | A plant or animal that is nyctitropic. | Example:  Many desert animals are nyctitropics, avoiding the intense daytime heat. 许多沙漠动物是夜行动物，以避开强烈的白天高温。 | Synonym: nocturnal organism | Antonym: diurnal organism | Word type: Noun
