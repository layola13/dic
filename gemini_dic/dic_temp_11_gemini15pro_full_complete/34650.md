clepsine:/ˈklɛpsɪn/| n. |医蛭，淡水蛭|A freshwater leech, especially of the genus *Glossiphonia*, that sucks the blood of snails, fish, and other invertebrates.|Clepsines are common in freshwater ponds and streams, feeding on invertebrates.  医蛭常见于淡水池塘和溪流中，以无脊椎动物为食。| The clepsine attached itself to the snail's shell.  医蛭附着在蜗牛壳上。|近义词：leech, glossiphonia|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
