nobody's:/ˈnoʊbədiz/ |pron.|无人的；谁也不；|It's nobody's fault. 这不是任何人的错。| Nobody's going to believe that story. 没有人会相信那个故事。|近义词:no one's |反义词:everybody's|缩略形式

ETYMOLOGY->
From nobody + 's (possessive).  "Nobody" itself comes from no + body. "No" traces back to Old English "nān," meaning "not one," and "body" comes from Old English "bodig," meaning "trunk of a person or animal."
