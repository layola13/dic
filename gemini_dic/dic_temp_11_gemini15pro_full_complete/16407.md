barcoding:/ˈbɑːrkoʊdɪŋ/
n.
条形码技术，条码化
The process of representing data with bar codes.  This involves assigning a unique barcode to an item and then scanning the barcode to retrieve the associated data.
Supermarkets use barcoding to speed up the checkout process. 超市使用条形码技术来加快结账速度。
Barcoding has revolutionized inventory management. 条形码技术彻底改变了库存管理。
Near Synonyms: barcode scanning, automatic identification
Antonyms: manual entry, manual data input
