godroon:/ɡəˈdruːn/| n. adj.|凸圆花纹，凸圆饰|A convex ornamentation, usually a series of rounded molding, applied to metalwork, pottery, or furniture.|The silver teapot was decorated with an intricate godroon pattern.|近义词:fluting, reeding, molding|反义词:smooth, plain|词性:名词, 形容词

Etymology: From French *godron*, probably from Old Norse *guðrún*, a personal name.  The connection between the name and the decorative element is uncertain.
