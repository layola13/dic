edenite:/ˈiːdənˌaɪt/
n.
伊甸石
A complex silicate mineral of the amphibole group, usually green or black in color, often found in igneous and metamorphic rocks.
Edenite is a common mineral in certain types of metamorphic rocks. 伊甸石在某些类型的变质岩中很常见。
Edenite crystals can be quite beautiful when well-formed.  形状良好的伊甸石晶体非常漂亮。

近义词:amphibole(闪石)
反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific mineral)
词性:noun
