nobler:/ˈnoʊblər/ (US), /ˈnəʊblə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更高贵的，更崇高的|Of a higher social rank; aristocratic.  Having or showing fine personal qualities or high moral principles and ideals.|例句：He was a nobler man than his brother. 他比他兄弟更高尚。|近义词：grander, loftier, more honorable, more righteous, more virtuous|反义词：baser, meaner, ignoble, dishonorable|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *nobler*, comparative of *noble*, from Old French *noble*, from Latin *nobilis* (“noble, well-known, famous, high-born, excellent”), from *nosco* (“I get to know”).
