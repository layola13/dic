delineament:/dɪˈlɪniəm(ə)nt/
n.|勾画，轮廓，略图|The architect's delineament of the proposed building was very detailed.  建筑师对拟建建筑物的勾画非常详细。|例句：The delineament of the coastline was clearly visible from the air. 从空中可以清楚地看到海岸线的轮廓。|近义词：outline, sketch, depiction, portrayal|反义词:obscurity, vagueness|词性:名词

Etymology: From Late Latin *delineāmentum, from Latin delineāre (“to sketch, delineate”), from de- + lineāre (“to make lines”), from līnea (“line”).
