jackass:/ˈdʒækæs/| n.|蠢货，笨蛋，傻瓜|A stupid person; an idiot. |He acted like a complete jackass at the party. 他在派对上表现得像个十足的傻瓜。|Example: Don't be a jackass and drive drunk. 不要做傻事酒后驾车。|Synonyms: idiot, fool, moron, imbecile, dolt, dunce, nitwit, simpleton|Antonyms: genius, intellectual, sage|Noun


jackass:/ˈdʒækæs/| n.|公驴|A male donkey. (This meaning is less common now.)|The old jackass carried the heavy load up the mountain. 那头老公驴驮着重物上了山。|Example: The farmer used a jackass to carry the harvest.  农民用一头公驴运送收成。|Synonyms: donkey|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
