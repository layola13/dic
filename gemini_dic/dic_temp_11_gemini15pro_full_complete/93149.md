ignescent:/ɪɡˈnɛsənt/
adjective
发光的，燃烧的，闪耀的
emitting light as a result of being heated; becoming luminous when heated.
The ignescent meteor streaked across the night sky.  燃烧的流星划过夜空。
The metal became ignescent in the furnace.  金属在熔炉中变得炽热发光。
近义词:  incandescent, luminous, glowing, fiery
反义词:  dark, dim, extinguished
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin ignescens, present participle of ignescere "to catch fire," from ignis "fire."
