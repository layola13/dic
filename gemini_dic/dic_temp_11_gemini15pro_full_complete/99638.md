interlamination:/ˌɪntərˌlæmɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 交错层压，夹层；插入层压 | The process or result of inserting a layer of one material between layers of another material. |  The strength of the plywood comes from the interlamination of wood veneers. 胶合板的强度来自木单板的交错层压。

The interlamination of different materials can create unique properties. 不同材料的交错层压可以创造独特的性能。 | 近义词：lamination, layering, stratification | 反义词: delamination | 词性: 名词
