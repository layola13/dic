interstreak:/ˈɪntərˌstriːk/
n.
间歇期，间隔期
A period of time between streaks, especially between winning or losing streaks.
The team's interstreak between their five-game winning streak and their current three-game losing streak was only two games.  这支球队在五连胜和目前的三连败之间的间歇期只有两场比赛。
近义词: interval, gap, break
反义词: streak, continuation
