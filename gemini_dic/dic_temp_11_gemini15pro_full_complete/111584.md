lobotomization: /ˌloʊbəˈtɒməˌzeɪʃən/ /ˌloʊbəˈtɑːməˌzeɪʃən/
n. |脑叶切除术|  The surgical severance of nerve fibers connecting a lobe of the brain with the thalamus, especially the prefrontal lobe, formerly used to treat mental illness. | Lobotomization was a controversial procedure used in the mid-20th century.  脑叶切除术是20世纪中期一项备受争议的治疗方法。| synonym: leucotomy, prefrontal lobotomy | antonym: psychosurgery (a broader term, not a direct opposite but represents an alternative approach), psychotherapy (non-surgical treatment) | noun

lobotomize: /ˌloʊbəˈtɒmaɪz/ /ˌloʊbəˈtɑːmaɪz/
vt. |对…进行脑叶切除术| To perform a lobotomy on. | Doctors lobotomized patients to treat severe mental illnesses. 医生对病人进行脑叶切除术以治疗严重的精神疾病。 | synonym: perform a lobotomy on, leucotomize | antonym: treat with psychotherapy (represents an alternative approach) | verb
