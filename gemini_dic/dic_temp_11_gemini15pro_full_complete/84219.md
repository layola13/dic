half-ruined:/ˌhɑːf ˈruːɪnd/ | adj. | 半毁的；部分损毁的 | Partially destroyed or decayed; in a state of disrepair. | The half-ruined castle stood on the hill. 半毁的城堡矗立在山上。| The earthquake left many buildings half-ruined. 地震使许多建筑物半毁。 |Synonyms: dilapidated, damaged, deteriorated, decaying, ramshackle| Antonyms: pristine, intact, perfect, undamaged, renovated | adjective

half:/hɑːf/ | n. adj. adv. | 一半；半个 | One of two equal or approximately equal parts of a divisible whole. |  I ate half the pizza. 我吃了一半披萨。|  Half of the students failed the test. 一半的学生考试不及格。| Synonyms: moiety, one-half, portion | Antonyms: whole, double, entire | noun/adjective/adverb

ruined:/ˈruːɪnd/ | adj. | 毁坏的；毁灭的 | Damaged or destroyed beyond repair or use. | The ruined city was a reminder of the war.  这座被毁的城市提醒着人们战争的残酷。 |  His reputation was ruined by the scandal.  丑闻毁了他的名声。| Synonyms: destroyed, wrecked, devastated, ravaged | Antonyms: restored, repaired, intact, undamaged | adjective
