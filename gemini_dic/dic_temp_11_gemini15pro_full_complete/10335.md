appears:/əˈpɪrz/| v.|出现，看来，似乎|It appears that he is not coming. 看来他不来了。|Synonyms: seems, looks, manifests|Antonyms: disappears, vanishes|Verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French aparoir, aparëstre, from Latin apparēre, from ad- (“to, towards”) + parēre (“to appear, be visible”).
