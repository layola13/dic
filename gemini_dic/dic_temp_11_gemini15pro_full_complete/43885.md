crestlike:/ˈkrɛstˌlaɪk/
adjective
像波峰的，形似山脊的
Resembling a crest, especially the crest of a wave or a mountain range.
The clouds formed crestlike patterns across the sky.  云层在天空中形成了像波峰一样的图案。
The mountain range had a series of crestlike peaks. 山脉有一系列像山脊一样的山峰。
Synonyms: ridged, peaked, wavelike, undulating
Antonyms: flat, smooth, level
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From 'crest' + '-like'. 'Crest' comes from Old French 'creste' (crest of a helmet, top of a wave), from Latin 'crista' (tuft, plume, comb of a bird). '-like' is a suffix used to form adjectives meaning "resembling," "characteristic of," or "similar to."
