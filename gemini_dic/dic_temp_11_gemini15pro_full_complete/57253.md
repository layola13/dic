downloadable:/ˌdaʊnˈloʊdəbl/| adj.|可下载的|able to be downloaded from a website or other online service.  Something that can be transferred from the internet or another network to a computer or device.|The software is downloadable from the company's website.| 该软件可以从公司网站下载。|近义词: accessible, obtainable, retrievable|反义词: non-downloadable, unavailable|词性:形容词

Etymology:  "download" + "-able". "Download" comes from "down" + "load," referring to transferring data from a larger system to a smaller one. The suffix "-able" indicates capability or possibility.
