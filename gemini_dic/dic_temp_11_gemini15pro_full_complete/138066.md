normalised:/ˈnɔːməlaɪzd/ (British) /ˈnɔːrməˌlaɪzd/ (American)| adj.|标准化的，规范化的|Made to conform to a standard or norm; adjusted to compensate for some inherent bias or other distorting factor.|The data were normalised to account for differences in population size.  这些数据经过标准化处理，以考虑人口规模的差异。|The software normalises the volume levels of different audio tracks.  该软件将不同音轨的音量级别标准化。|近义词：standardized, regularized, adjusted|反义词：unstandardized, irregular, non-standard|词性:形容词

Etymology:  From normalize + -ed.  Normalize comes from  norm + -ize.  Norm ultimately derives from the Latin "norma" meaning "carpenter's square, rule, pattern."
