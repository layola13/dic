baptizers:/ˈbæptaɪzərz/
n.
施洗者；施洗约翰的信徒
People who administer the act of baptism.
John the Baptist and his followers were baptizers. 施洗约翰和他的信徒都是施洗者。

baptizers:/ˈbæptaɪzərz/
n.
浸信会教友
Members of a Baptist church.
Many baptizers believe in adult baptism. 许多浸信会教友相信成人洗礼。
