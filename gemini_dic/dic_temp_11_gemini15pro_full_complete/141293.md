open-endedness:/ˌoʊpənˈɛndɪdnəs/ | n. |开放性，未定性|The open-endedness of the question allowed for a variety of responses.  这个问题的开放性允许各种各样的回答。|Example: The open-endedness of the project brief gave us a lot of creative freedom.  项目概要的开放性给了我们很大的创作自由。

Example:  I appreciate the open-endedness of the assignment, as it allows for personal interpretation. 我很欣赏作业的开放性，因为它允许个人解读。 |近义词：flexibility, fluidity, ambiguity |反义词: definiteness, rigidity, specificity |词性: noun

Etymology: From "open-ended" + "-ness". "Open-ended" is formed from "open" + "ended". "Open" comes from Old English "open", ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*upanaz".  "Ended" comes from "end" + "-ed". "End" comes from Old English "ende", from Proto-Germanic "*andijaz". "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality.
