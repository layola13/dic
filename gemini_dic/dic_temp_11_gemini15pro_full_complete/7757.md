anorthosite: /ænˈɔːrθəˌsaɪt/ | n. |斜长岩|An igneous rock composed primarily of plagioclase feldspar, typically with a small amount of pyroxene, olivine, or iron oxides.  It is commonly found in the lunar highlands and some terrestrial locations. |例句：Anorthosite is a major component of the lunar crust. 斜长岩是月球地壳的主要成分。

|例句：The Adirondack Mountains contain significant anorthosite deposits. 阿迪朗达克山脉蕴藏着大量的斜长岩矿床。

|近义词：plagioclase rock (更广义)|反义词: N/A |词性：noun
