kneecappers:/ˈniːkæpərz/
n.
膝盖枪击者；使人残废者
People who shoot someone in the kneecaps, often as a form of intimidation or punishment within organized crime.  This act is intended to disable the victim without killing them.
The kneecappers were sent to send a message. 那些枪击膝盖的人被派去发出警告。
thugs, gangsters, assassins, hitmen
