cowlick: /ˈkaʊlɪk/ (US) /ˈkaʊlɪk/ (UK)| n. |一撮翘起的头发；牛舔|A section of hair that grows in a different direction from the rest of the hair and resists being combed flat.  It is thought to resemble the place on a cow's coat where a calf has licked it. |My son has a cowlick right at the front of his hair.  我儿子额头上有一撮翘起的头发。|Synonyms: tuft, swirl, whorl|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

cowlick: /ˈkaʊlɪk/ (US) /ˈkaʊlɪk/ (UK)| n. |怪癖；难以控制的习惯|An uncontrollable habit or tendency. (Less common usage)|He has a cowlick for collecting old postcards.  他有个收集旧明信片的怪癖。|Synonyms: quirk, idiosyncrasy, peculiarity|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
