indissolubility:/ˌɪndɪˌsɒljʊˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可溶解性；牢不可破；永久性 | The quality or state of being indissoluble; incapable of being dissolved, broken, or undone. | The indissolubility of their marriage was important to them. 他们的婚姻牢不可破对他们很重要。 | Synonyms: permanence, stability, firmness, inseparability | Antonyms: dissolubility, instability, fragility, separability | noun

indissoluble:/ˌɪndɪˈsɒljʊbl/ | adj. | 不可溶解的；牢不可破的；永久的 | Not capable of being dissolved, broken, or undone. |  They believed in the indissoluble bond of marriage.  他们相信牢不可破的婚姻纽带。 | Synonyms: permanent, lasting, enduring, unbreakable | Antonyms: dissoluble, temporary, fragile, breakable | adjective
