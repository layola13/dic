locutory: /ˈlɒkjʊtəri/ (British), /ˈlɑːkjətɔːri/ (American)| adj.|诉讼程序中的，非最终判决的|Of or relating to an interlocutory decree or order, which is a temporary or provisional order made during the course of legal proceedings, not constituting a final decision on the merits of the case.|The judge issued a locutory injunction to prevent the defendant from selling the property before the trial. 法官发布了一项诉讼禁令，以阻止被告在审判前出售该房产。|The locutory appeal was denied by the higher court.  高等法院驳回了中间上诉。 |Synonyms: provisional, interim, temporary|Antonyms: final, conclusive|adjective
