calamine:/ˈkæləˌmaɪn/ | n. |炉甘石,异极矿;炉甘石洗剂|A pink powder consisting of zinc oxide with a small proportion of ferric oxide, used in lotions or ointments to treat skin irritations.|She applied calamine lotion to the rash.|Synonyms:  zinc oxide, lotion, ointment|Antonyms: irritant|noun

calamines:/ˈkæləˌmaɪnz/ | n. |炉甘石（复数）|Plural form of calamine, referring to multiple instances or types of calamine.|The chemist stocked several different calamines.|Synonyms: zinc oxides, lotions, ointments|Antonyms: irritants|noun
