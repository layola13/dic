grandiloquous: /ˌɡrændɪˈləʊkwəs/| adj.|夸夸其谈的，辞藻华丽的| Using pompous or extravagant language; bombastic.| Example: His grandiloquent speech impressed no one; it was all empty rhetoric. 他夸夸其谈的演讲没有打动任何人，全是空洞的华丽辞藻。

Example: The politician's grandiloquent promises were soon forgotten after the election.  这位政客华丽的承诺在选举后很快就被遗忘了。| 近义词: pompous, bombastic, verbose, florid, ostentatious | 反义词: simple, plain, concise, unpretentious, modest | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *grandiloquus*, from *grandis* ("large, grand") + *loqui* ("to speak").
