confessionally:/kənˈfɛʃənəli/
adverb
忏悔地；坦白地
In a way that relates to confession or involves confessing something.
He spoke confessionally about his past mistakes. 他忏悔地谈到了他过去的错误。
The author writes confessionally about her struggles with addiction. 作者坦白地写了她与毒瘾的斗争。
近义词: frankly, openly, candidly
反义词: secretively, evasively, deceptively
词性: adverb
