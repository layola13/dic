outfox:/ˌaʊtˈfɑːks/
verb
以智取胜，胜过，瞒过
to be more clever and cunning than someone and defeat them
例句：The chess player outfoxed his opponent with a clever move.  这位棋手以巧妙的一步战胜了对手。

例句：The spy outfoxed the guards and escaped undetected.  这个间谍瞒过了警卫，成功逃脱。
近义词：outsmart, outwit, trick, deceive
反义词：be outwitted, be outsmarted
词性：v.
