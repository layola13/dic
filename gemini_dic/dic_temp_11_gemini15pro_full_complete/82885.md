gulph:/ɡʌlf/| n.|海湾，深渊，漩涡|A deep chasm or gorge; a vast body of water partially enclosed by land; something that engulfs or swallows up.|例句：The ship disappeared into the murky gulph. 船消失在黑暗的深渊中。

例句：The kayaker paddled across the wide gulph. 皮划艇运动员划过宽阔的海湾。|近义词：abyss, chasm, gorge, gulf, vortex|反义词:summit, peak|词性:noun
