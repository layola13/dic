over-seed: /ˌoʊvərˈsiːd/ | v. | 补播;  在已有的草坪上再次播种 | To sow seed over an existing lawn or pasture in order to thicken the turf or introduce a new variety of grass. |  We over-seeded the lawn in the fall to improve its density. 我们在秋天补播了草坪以提高其密度。|  He over-seeded the ryegrass pasture with clover. 他在黑麦草牧场上补播了三叶草。 |  reseed, sow | - | verb
