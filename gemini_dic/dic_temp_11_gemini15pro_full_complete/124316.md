motherlands:/ˈmʌðəlændz/
noun
祖国；故乡
One's native land; the land of one's ancestors.
Many people return to their motherlands after years of living abroad. 许多人在国外生活多年后回到了自己的祖国。
synonyms: homelands, native lands, native countries, birthplaces
antonyms: foreign lands, adopted countries
