actorish: /ˈæktərɪʃ/ | adj. | 做作的，矫揉造作的，像演员的 | Resembling or characteristic of an actor, often in a contrived or exaggerated way; theatrical. |  He struck an actorish pose, hand on his hip and chin held high. 他摆出一个做作的姿势，一只手叉腰，下巴高高抬起。

| Her laugh seemed actorish and insincere. 她的笑声显得矫揉造作，不真诚。 | 近义词: theatrical, dramatic, histrionic, affected, stagy | 反义词: natural, unaffected, genuine, sincere | 词性: adjective
