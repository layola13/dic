exogenisms:/ˌeksəˈdʒɛnɪzəmz/
n.
外来词; 外来语
Words or phrases borrowed from another language and incorporated into the recipient language.
Many exogenisms in English come from Latin and Greek. 许多英语外来词源自拉丁语和希腊语。

neologisms, native words
