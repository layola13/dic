Example 2: He prefers lighter basketballs as they are better dunkables. 他更喜欢轻一点的篮球，因为它们更容易灌篮。|近义词：dippers, finger foods (for food); slam dunk (for basketball)|反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:noun
