devitrification:/diːˌvɪtrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
失透，脱玻
The process of crystallization in a glassy substance, whereby it partially or wholly loses its transparency.
The devitrification of the glass made it opaque. 玻璃的失透使其变得不透明。

devitrification:/diːˌvɪtrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
noun
失透；脱玻
The process by which a glassy substance is converted to a crystalline state.
The high temperatures caused devitrification of the glaze. 高温导致釉料失透。
Synonym: crystallization,  crystallization
Antonym: vitrification
