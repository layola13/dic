half-acquainted:/ˌhæf əˈkweɪntɪd/
adjective
半相识的；略有交情的
Partially familiar; slightly acquainted.  Knowing someone only slightly.
例句：I was half-acquainted with several of the guests at the party. 我和聚会上的一些客人只是半面之交。

例句：We were half-acquainted through mutual friends. 我们通过共同的朋友略有交情。

近义词：slightly acquainted, partially familiar, vaguely familiar
反义词：well-acquainted, close, intimate
词性: adjective
