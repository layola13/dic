copperheads:/ˈkɒpəhedz/ (US) /ˈkɒpəhɛdz/ (UK)| n.|铜斑蛇（复数）; 敌对者,分裂分子（尤指美国内战时期北方支持南方的人）|A venomous snake found in eastern North America, or a Northerner who sympathized with the South during the American Civil War.|例句：Copperheads are known for their camouflage. 铜斑蛇以其伪装而闻名。

例句：Copperheads were often seen as traitors by those loyal to the Union.  那些忠于联邦的人经常将敌对者视为叛徒。|近义词：snake, viper (for the animal); sympathizer, dissenter, traitor (for the person)|反义词：patriot, loyalist (for the person)|词性:名词
