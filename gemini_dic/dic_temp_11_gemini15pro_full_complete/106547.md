krone:/ˈkrəʊnə/ (UK), /ˈkroʊnə/ (US)| n.|克朗，王冠|A unit of currency in Denmark, Norway, Iceland, Czech Republic, and formerly Sweden, Estonia, Slovakia, and Greenland.  Also, a garland or wreath worn on the head, especially as a mark of victory.|例句：The price is 500 kroner. 价格是500克朗。

例句：The king wore a golden krone. 国王戴着金王冠。|近义词：crown, currency, garland, wreath|反义词: (No direct antonym, could be considered opposite of a currency unit like "debt")|词性:noun
