clematis: /ˈklɛmətɪs/ (US), /ˈklɛmətɪs/ (UK)| n. |铁线莲，藤本植物|A climbing plant with brightly coloured flowers, often referred to as the "Queen of Climbers."|例句：The clematis climbed up the trellis, covering it in beautiful purple blossoms. 铁线莲爬上了棚架，上面覆盖着美丽的紫色花朵。

例句：She planted a clematis near the fence, hoping it would eventually cover it. 她种了一株铁线莲在篱笆附近，希望它最终能覆盖住篱笆。|近义词：vine, creeper|反义词: (No direct antonym for a plant name)|词性:noun
