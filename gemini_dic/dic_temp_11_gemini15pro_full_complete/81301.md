granules:/ˈɡrænjuːlz/ (US), /ˈɡrænjuːlz/ (UK)| n. |颗粒，细粒|Small, compact particles of a substance.|The fertilizer comes in granules that are easy to spread. 这种肥料是颗粒状的，很容易撒播。|The beach was covered in fine granules of sand. 海滩上覆盖着细小的沙粒。|近义词：grains, particles, bits, specks|反义词:bulk, mass|Noun


词源:
From Late Latin *granulum, diminutive of Latin granum ("grain").
