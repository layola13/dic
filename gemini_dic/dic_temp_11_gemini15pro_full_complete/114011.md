magmata:/mæɡˈmɑːtə/ | n. | 岩浆（复数）| Molten rock beneath the earth's surface.  The plural of magma. | The geologist studied the magmata from the volcanic eruption. 地质学家研究了火山爆发产生的岩浆。| 近义词:molten rock, lava (when above ground) | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps solidified rock) | 词性: noun

magmas:/ˈmæɡməz/ | n. | 岩浆（复数） | Molten rock beneath the earth's surface. Another plural form of magma. | The magmas varied in composition and temperature. 这些岩浆的成分和温度各不相同。| 近义词: molten rock, lava (when above ground) | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps solidified rock) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *magmata*, plural of *magma*, from Greek μάγμα (mágma, “kneaded mass, salve”), from μάσσω (mássō, “I knead”).
