carbamide: /kɑːrˈbæmaɪd/ /kɑːrˈbæmˌaɪd/ | n. | 尿素; 脲 | A white crystalline organic compound that is the chief nitrogenous end product of protein metabolism in mammals and is excreted in urine. It is used in fertilizers and animal feed, and in manufacturing plastics and resins. | Urea is the main nitrogen-containing substance in the urine of mammals. 尿素是哺乳动物尿液中主要的含氮物质。| Carbamide is used in the production of some plastics. 尿素用于生产某些塑料。 | synonyms: urea | antonyms: N/A | noun
