neurologists: /nʊˈrɑːlədʒɪsts/ | n. | 神经学家；神经科医生 | A medical specialist who diagnoses and treats disorders of the nervous system. |  The neurologists diagnosed her with multiple sclerosis. 神经学家诊断她患有多发性硬化症。|  The neurologists performed a thorough examination.  神经科医生进行了全面检查。 | Synonyms: nerve specialist, brain doctor | Antonyms:  N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From neuro- + -logist.  "Neuro-" comes from Greek "neuron" meaning "nerve" and "-logist" signifies "one who studies".
