berycoid:/ˈbɛrɪkɔɪd/| adj. n.| 须鳂科的；须鳂科鱼类 |Relating to or denoting a large family of mainly deep-sea spiny-finned fishes (Berycidae), typically having large eyes and a bright red colour.|Example: The berycoid fish was a vibrant red. 这条须鳂科的鱼是鲜红色的。|Example:  Berycoids are often found in deep ocean waters. 须鳂科鱼类经常在深海中被发现。 |近义词: deep-sea fish |反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑shallow-water fish 浅水鱼作为对比)|词性: adjective, noun
