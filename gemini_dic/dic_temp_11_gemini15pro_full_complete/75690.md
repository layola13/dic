fulguration:/ˌfʌlɡəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 闪电，雷击；电灼术；电闪光 | The destruction of tissue by means of high-frequency electric current. 通过高频电流破坏组织。| Example: Fulguration was used to remove the abnormal tissue.  电灼术被用于去除异常组织。| Example: The surgeon used fulguration to stop the bleeding.  外科医生用电灼术来止血。 | Synonyms: electrocoagulation, electrodesiccation | Antonyms: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fulgurātiō*  "a flashing of lightning", from *fulgurāre* "to flash as lightning"
