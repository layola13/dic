anticipatingly:/ænˈtɪsɪpeɪtɪŋli/
adverb
期待地，预料地
In a way that shows you are expecting something to happen, usually something pleasant.
She looked at the door anticipatingly, waiting for her friend to arrive. 她期待地望着门口，等着朋友的到来。
The children waited anticipatingly for the presents to be opened. 孩子们期待地等着礼物被打开。
近义词:expectantly, hopefully
反义词:apathetically, indifferently
词性:adverb

Etymology:
From anticipate + -ing + -ly.  Anticipate comes from the Latin anticipatus, the past participle of anticipare "to take before, to anticipate," from ante- "before" + capere "to take."
