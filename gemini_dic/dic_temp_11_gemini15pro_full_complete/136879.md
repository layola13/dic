nonsobering:/nɒnˈsəʊbərɪŋ/ | adj. | 不令人清醒的；不发人深省的 | Not causing one to become sober or serious; not prompting reflection or causing one to consider things carefully. | The movie, despite its serious subject matter, had a surprisingly nonsobering effect.  这部电影，尽管主题严肃，但却出奇地没有让人深省。 | 近义词:  unimpressive, unexciting, frivolous, lighthearted | 反义词: sobering, thought-provoking, serious | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "sobering" (adjective from the verb "sober," meaning to make or become serious or calm).  The word "sober" ultimately comes from the Latin "sobrius."
