outlawry: /ˈaʊtlɔːri/ (US) /ˈaʊtlɔːri/ (UK)
n.
非法; 放逐; 剥夺法律保护
the act of declaring someone an outlaw, or the state of being an outlaw.  The act of banning something.
例句：He was sentenced to outlawry for his crimes. 他因犯罪被判处放逐。

例句：The outlawry of certain drugs has led to a black market.  某些药物的非法化导致了黑市的出现。

近义词：banishment, exile, proscription
反义词：legality, pardon
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *outlawerie, from Old English *ūtlaghrie, equivalent to outlaw +‎ -ry. Cognate with Old Norse útlageri.
