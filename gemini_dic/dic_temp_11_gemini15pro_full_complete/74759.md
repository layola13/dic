freewiller:/ˈfriːˈwɪlər/| n.|自由意志者，自主行动者|Someone who believes in free will, the ability to make choices independent of external determining factors.|例句：The freewiller argued that individuals are responsible for their own actions. 自由意志者认为个人要对自己的行为负责。

例句：She considered herself a freewiller, believing that destiny was not predetermined. 她认为自己是一个自由意志者，相信命运不是预先注定的。|近义词：voluntarist, autonomist|反义词：determinist, fatalist|词性:noun
