infinity:/ɪnˈfɪnɪti/ /ɪnˈfɪnədi/ | n. |无限，无穷|A boundless or immeasurable extent of time, space, or quantity.|例句：The universe seems to stretch into infinity. 宇宙似乎延伸到无限远。|近义词：boundlessness, endlessness, eternity|反义词: finiteness, limit |noun
infinity:/ɪnˈfɪnɪti/ /ɪnˈfɪnədi/ | n. |无穷大|A number greater than any assignable quantity or countable number (symbol ∞).|例句：The concept of infinity is difficult to grasp. 无穷大的概念很难理解。|近义词：immensity, vastness|反义词: zero |noun
