adnate:/ædˈneɪt/ | adj. | 附生的，贴生的，合生的 | Growing closely attached to something else, especially of a different kind; fused to another organ of a different kind. |  The stipules are adnate to the petiole. 托叶贴生于叶柄上。 |  fused, attached, adherent | free, separate | adjective

adnate:/ædˈneɪt/ | adj. | 附生的，贴生的，合生的 | (Botany)  United with another part along a margin; congenitally attached or united. | The stamen filaments are adnate to the petals. 雄蕊花丝与花瓣合生。 | connate, adherent | free | adjective
