nubs:/nʌbz/| n.| 小块；要点；核心；残端| a small lump or piece, especially one remaining after something has been broken or worn away.  The essential point of something.| The pencil was worn down to a nub. 这支铅笔被磨损得只剩下一点点了。| synonyms: stump, remnant, stub, butt, core, gist, essence | antonyms: whole, entirety, complete | noun

nubs:/nʌbz/| n.| （尤指不友好的）人；家伙| (informal, often derogatory) a person, especially one perceived as unpleasant or insignificant. |  "Don't listen to that nub, he doesn't know what he's talking about."  别听那个家伙的，他不知道自己在说什么。| synonyms: jerk, idiot, fool | antonyms: genius, expert, professional | noun
