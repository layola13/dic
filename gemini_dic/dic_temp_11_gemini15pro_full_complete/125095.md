mulling:/ˈmʌlɪŋ/| v.| 认真思考，仔细考虑|  carefully considering something for a long time|  I'm still mulling over the offer. 我还在仔细考虑那个提议。| synonyms: considering, pondering, contemplating | antonyms: ignoring, dismissing | verb

mull:/mʌl/| v. | 思考，仔细考虑；加热并加香料于（葡萄酒等）|  to think about something for a long time; to heat wine or beer with spices and sugar|  She mulled over the problem for days. 她苦思冥想这个问题好几天了。  He mulled some wine for the Christmas party. 他为圣诞派对准备了热葡萄酒。| synonyms: ponder, contemplate, ruminate (for thinking); heat, warm (for drinks) | antonyms: ignore, dismiss (for thinking); cool, chill (for drinks) | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mollen*, "to grind," from Old French *muller*, from Latin *molere*, "to grind." The sense of "ponder" developed from the idea of "grinding" something over in one's mind.
