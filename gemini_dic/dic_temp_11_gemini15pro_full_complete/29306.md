castellan:/ˌkæsˈtɛlən/ | adj. | 城堡的；要塞的 | Of or relating to a castle or fortress. | The castellan walls were thick and imposing. 城堡的城墙又厚又雄伟。 | fortress-like, castle-like | None | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *castellan*, from Medieval Latin *castellanus*, from *castellum* ("castle"). Ultimately from Latin *castrum* ("fort").
