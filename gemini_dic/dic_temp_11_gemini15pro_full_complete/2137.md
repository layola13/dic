adaptly:/əˈdæptli/
adv.|适应地，灵活地|In a way that is suitable for a new purpose or situation; in a way that shows or involves adjusting readily to different conditions.|She adaptly handled the unexpected crisis. 她灵活地处理了这场突如其来的危机。|近义词：suitably, appropriately, flexibly, resourcefully|反义词：inappropriately, inflexibly, rigidly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From adapt + -ly.  "Adapt" comes from the Latin "adaptare," meaning "to fit, to adjust."  The suffix "-ly" is commonly used to form adverbs from adjectives.
