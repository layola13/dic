lasso:/ˈlæsoʊ/ (US) /ˈlæsuː/ (UK)| n. vt.|套索；用套索套住|A rope with a noose at one end, used for catching cattle or horses.  Also used as a verb meaning to catch with a lasso.|The cowboy lassoed the calf.|近义词：rope, noose|反义词:release, free|词性:noun, verb
Etymology: From Spanish "lazo," ultimately from Latin "laqueus" (snare, noose).
