barring:/ˈbɑːrɪŋ/
preposition|除了…之外；除非|Except for; if not for|Barring any unforeseen circumstances, we should arrive on time. 除非有任何不可预见的状况，我们应该会准时到达。|近义词:except for, excluding, save, apart from|反义词:including|词性:介词

barring:/ˈbɑːrɪŋ/
verb|禁止，阻拦|Preventing entry or exit; obstructing|The heavy snow is barring all traffic. 大雪阻断了所有交通。|近义词:obstructing, blocking, preventing, prohibiting|反义词:allowing, permitting|词性:动词
