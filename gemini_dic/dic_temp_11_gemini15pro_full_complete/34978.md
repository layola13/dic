clog:/klɑːɡ/ (US) /klɒɡ/ (UK)| n. v. |木屐；障碍；堵塞 |A shoe shaped from a single block of wood. To block or obstruct something. |例句：
The heavy rain clogged the drains. 大雨堵塞了排水沟。

She wore wooden clogs in the garden. 她在花园里穿木屐。

|近义词：block, obstruct, choke, congest (v.); blockage, obstruction (n.)|反义词:clear, unclog (v.)|词性:名词，动词


clog:/klɑːɡ/ (US) /klɒɡ/ (UK)| v. |堵塞；阻塞 |To become or cause to become blocked or filled so that movement or activity is difficult or impossible. |例句：

The pipes were clogged with hair. 管道被头发堵塞了。

Eating too much fatty food can clog your arteries. 吃太多油腻的食物会堵塞你的动脉。
