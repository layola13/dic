invitant:/ˌɪnvɪˈtænt/ | n. |被邀请者，受邀人|A person who is invited to an event or gathering.|例句：The invitants to the wedding were asked to RSVP by the end of the month. 婚礼的受邀者被要求在月底前回复。

例句：All invitants to the conference received a welcome pack. 所有与会者都收到了欢迎礼包。


|近义词：invitee, guest|反义词: host|词性: 名词
