earthboard:/ˈɜːrθbɔːrd/ | n. | 土滑板 | A board used for riding down a dirt hill, similar to snowboarding but on dirt. | He went down the hill on his earthboard, kicking up a cloud of dust. 他骑着土滑板滑下山，扬起一片尘土。|  He carved down the hill on his earthboard. 他骑着土滑板顺着山坡蜿蜒而下。 | 近义词: dirtboard, landboard, all-terrain board | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps snowboard as it's used on snow) | 词性: noun
