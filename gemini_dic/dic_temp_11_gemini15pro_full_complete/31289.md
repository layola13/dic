chaperone:/ˈʃæpəroʊn/ (US) /ˈʃæpərəʊn/ (UK)| n. v.|陪伴；监护人；伴侣； chaperoning (v. 伴随)|A person who accompanies and looks after another person or group of people, especially to ensure proper behavior.|例句：The students were accompanied by chaperones on their school trip. 学生们在学校旅行中有监护人陪同。

例句：She chaperoned her younger sister to the dance. 她陪她妹妹去参加舞会。|近义词：escort, attendant, guardian, companion|反义词: N/A|词性: Noun, Verb
