effectuously:/ɪˈfɛktʃuəsli/
adjective
有效地，生效地
Producing the desired effect; effective. Done in a way that achieves a result.
She effectuously managed the project, bringing it in on time and under budget. 她有效地管理了这个项目，按时并在预算内完成。
The medicine effectuously relieved his pain.  这药有效地缓解了他的疼痛。

近义词: effectively, efficiently, successfully, potently
反义词: ineffectively, inefficiently, unsuccessfully, fruitlessly
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin effectuosus "able to produce an effect," from Latin effectus (see effect) + -osus "-ous."
