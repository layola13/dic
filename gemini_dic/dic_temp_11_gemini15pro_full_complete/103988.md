juggleress:/ˈdʒʌɡləres/
n.
女杂耍演员
A female performer who juggles objects.
She is a skilled juggleress, keeping five balls in the air at once. 她是一位技艺娴熟的女杂耍演员，可以同时让五个球在空中飞舞。
近义词：juggler, performer
反义词: audience, spectator
词性: noun

juggleress:/ˈdʒʌɡləres/
Etymology: From juggler + -ess (a suffix indicating female gender).  The word "juggler" comes from Old French "jogleor," meaning "jester" or "minstrel," ultimately derived from Latin "joculator," from "joculari," meaning "to jest."
词源：来自 juggler + -ess（表示女性的后缀）。单词“juggler”来自古法语“jogleor”，意思是“小丑”或“吟游诗人”，最终源自拉丁语“joculator”，来自“joculari”，意思是“开玩笑”。
