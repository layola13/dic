long-haul:/ˈlɔːŋhɔːl/ (US), /ˈlɒŋhɔːl/ (UK)| adj. n.|长途的；长途运输；长途货运|traveling or involving traveling a long distance|例句：Long-haul flights can be tiring. 长途飞行可能会很累人。|近义词：long-distance, extended|反义词：short-haul, short-distance|词性:形容词, 名词

long-haul:/ˈlɔːŋhɔːl/ (US), /ˈlɒŋhɔːl/ (UK)| n.|长途运输；长途货运；长途旅行|the transport of goods or passengers over long distances|例句：The company specializes in long-haul trucking. 这家公司专门从事长途货运。|近义词：long-distance transportation|反义词：short-haul transportation|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "long" + "haul". "Long" comes from Old English "lang", ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*langaz". "Haul" comes from Middle English "halen", from Old French "haler", ultimately from Frankish "*halan".
