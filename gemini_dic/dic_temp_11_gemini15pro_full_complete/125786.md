muriatic:/ˌmjʊəriˈætɪk/| adj.|盐酸的；氯化的|Relating to or containing hydrochloric acid.  Derived from brine or salt.  Commonly used to refer to a commercial grade of hydrochloric acid.|Example:  Muriatic acid is used to clean swimming pools.  盐酸用于清洁游泳池。

Example:  He used a muriatic acid solution to etch the concrete.  他用盐酸溶液腐蚀混凝土。|近义词：hydrochloric|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps alkaline)|词性: adjective
