nonexpiration:/ˌnɑːnˌɛkspɪˈreɪʃən/ |n.| 不过期；永久有效 |The nonexpiration of the contract means it remains valid indefinitely.  合同的nonexpiration意味着它无限期有效。| This license has nonexpiration, so you don't need to renew it.  这个许可证永久有效，所以你不需要续期。 |近义词: permanence, perpetuity |反义词: expiration, termination |词性: 名词

nonexpiring:/ˌnɑːnɪkˈspaɪərɪŋ/ |adj.| 不期满的；永久有效的 |A nonexpiring warranty provides lifelong coverage.  一个nonexpiring的保修提供终身保障。| They offer a nonexpiring subscription for a one-time fee. 他们提供一次性收费的永久订阅。|近义词: permanent, perpetual |反义词: expiring, temporary |词性: 形容词


词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "expiration" (from Latin "expirare" meaning "to breathe out, die out").  "non-"（前缀，意思是“不”）+ "expiration"（来自拉丁语 "expirare"，意思是“呼出，消失”）。
