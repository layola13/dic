liparite:/ˈlɪpəraɪt/ /ˈlaɪpəraɪt/| n. |流纹岩|An extrusive igneous rock, the volcanic equivalent of granite, typically light-colored and fine-grained.|
The geologist identified the sample as liparite. 地质学家将该样品鉴定为流纹岩。

Liparite forms the primary rock type in this volcanic region. 流纹岩是这个火山地区的主要岩石类型。

|近义词：rhyolite, quartz trachyte|反义词: basalt (in terms of silica content and overall composition)|词性: noun
