antidogmatism:/ˌæntiˈdɒɡməˌtɪzəm/
n.|反教条主义，反独断论|Opposition to or rejection of dogmatism; the belief that no one doctrine holds all the answers or should be accepted without question.|His antidogmatism allowed him to consider all viewpoints before making a decision. 他的反教条主义使他在做决定之前考虑所有观点。|近义词:open-mindedness, skepticism, liberalism|反义词:dogmatism, orthodoxy, rigidity|词性:名词

antidogmatic:/ˌæntiˈdɒɡˈmætɪk/
adj.|反教条的，反独断的|Opposed to or rejecting dogmatism; not rigidly adhering to a set of beliefs.|Her antidogmatic approach to teaching encouraged students to question everything. 她反教条的教学方法鼓励学生质疑一切。|近义词:open-minded, skeptical, liberal|反义词:dogmatic, orthodox, rigid|词性:形容词
