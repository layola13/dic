oleander:/ˌoʊliˈændər/ | n. | 夹竹桃 | An evergreen shrub or small tree of the dogbane family, with clusters of fragrant white, pink, or red flowers. It is native to the Mediterranean region but widely cultivated as an ornamental.  All parts of the plant are poisonous. | The oleander bushes in the garden were in full bloom. 花园里的夹竹桃盛开了。| Synonyms: rosebay, rose laurel | Antonyms: N/A | noun
