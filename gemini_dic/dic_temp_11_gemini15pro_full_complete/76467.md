gaiters:/ˈɡeɪtərz/
n.
绑腿，护腿
A garment worn over the ankle and lower leg, typically made of cloth or leather, for protection against cold, wet, or injury.
The hikers wore gaiters to protect their legs from the snow and brush.  徒步旅行者穿着绑腿以保护他们的腿免受雪和灌木的伤害。

gaiters:/ˈɡeɪtərz/
n.
(尤指19世纪男子穿着的)鞋套
An overshoe, especially one worn by men in the 19th century.
He polished his gaiters until they gleamed. 他把鞋套擦得锃亮。


近义词:leggings, spats
反义词:None
词性:noun
