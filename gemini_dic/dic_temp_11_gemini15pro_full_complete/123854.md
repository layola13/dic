moralizers:/ˈmɔːrəlaɪzərz/ | n. | 说教者，道德说教者 | People who preach or lecture others on moral issues, often in a self-righteous or tedious way.  |  The moralizers were quick to condemn his behavior.  说教者们很快就谴责了他的行为。 | synonyms: preachers, sermonizers, lecturers, dogmatists | antonyms:  freethinkers, liberals, libertines | noun
