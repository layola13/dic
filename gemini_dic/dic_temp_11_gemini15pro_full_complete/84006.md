half-discriminated:/ˌhæf dɪˈskrɪmɪneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 半歧视的，部分歧视的 | Partially discriminated, meaning a distinction is made, but not fully or completely.  This word suggests an incomplete or hesitant act of discrimination. |  The study revealed a half-discriminated policy against female employees.  这项研究揭示了针对女性员工的半歧视政策。

The algorithm made half-discriminated choices based on incomplete data.  该算法根据不完整的数据做出了半歧视性的选择。 | Near Synonyms: partially discriminated, subtly biased, implicitly prejudiced | Antonyms: unbiased, impartial, equitable | adjective
