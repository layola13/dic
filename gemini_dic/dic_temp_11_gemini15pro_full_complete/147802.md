overproven:/ˌoʊvərˈpruːvən/ | adj. | 过度证明的，证明过度的 |  Proven to an excessive or unnecessary degree.  Overly substantiated; having too much proof. | The case was overproven, with redundant evidence presented that bored the jury.  这个案子被过度证明了，多余的证据让陪审团感到厌烦。| The defense presented an overproven alibi, complete with witness testimonies, phone records, and security footage.  辩方提出了一个过度证明的不在场证明，包括证人证词、电话记录和监控录像。 |overstated, excessive, redundant| insufficient, lacking, weak | adjective
