economizer:/iˈkɒnəmaɪzər/ /ɪˈkɑːnəmaɪzər/ | n. | 节省器，节约器 | A device or system designed to reduce waste or consumption, especially of fuel or energy. |  The factory installed an economizer to reduce energy costs. 工厂安装了一个节能器以降低能源成本。 | synonyms: conserver, saver | antonyms: waster, spendthrift | noun

economize:/iˈkɒnəmaɪz/ /ɪˈkɑːnəmaɪz/ | v. | 节省，节约 | To reduce expenditure or consumption; to avoid waste. |  We need to economize on water usage during the drought.  干旱期间我们需要节约用水。 | synonyms: save, conserve, cut back | antonyms: waste, squander, splurge | verb
