isocheimal:/ˌaɪsoʊˈkaɪməl/ | adj. | 等温线的，等冬线的 |  Relating to or denoting a line on a map connecting points having the same mean winter temperature.  Derived from "iso-" (equal) and "cheim-" (winter) from Greek. | The isocheimal lines on this map show the average winter temperatures across the region. 这张地图上的等温线显示了该地区的平均冬季气温。 | synonyms: isothermal (for general temperature), isochronal (equal time) | antonyms: anisothermal, heterothermal | adjective

isocheim:/ˌaɪsoʊˈkaɪm/ | n. | 等温线，等冬线 | A line on a map connecting points having the same mean winter temperature. | The isocheim for -10°C runs through the northern part of the country.  -10°C 的等温线贯穿该国北部。 | synonyms: isothermal line (for general temperature) | antonyms: N/A | noun
