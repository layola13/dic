deesis:/ˈdiːəsɪs/
n.
恳求，祈愿;代祷
A prayer or entreaty, especially one addressed to Christ or the Virgin Mary.
The painting depicts a deesis with Christ enthroned between the Virgin Mary and St. John the Baptist. 这幅画描绘了基督坐在圣母玛利亚和施洗约翰之间的代祷场景。

近义词:supplication, entreaty, plea, prayer
反义词:command, demand, order
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *deēsis, from Ancient Greek δέησις (déēsis, “entreaty”), from δέομαι (déomai, “I want, I pray, I beg”).
