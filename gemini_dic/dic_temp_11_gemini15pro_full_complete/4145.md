air-tight:/ˈɛərtʌɪt/
adjective
密封的；不透气的
impermeable to air or nearly so
The container is air-tight to keep the food fresh.  这个容器是密封的，以保持食物新鲜。
The evidence against him was air-tight.  针对他的证据确凿无误。
Synonyms: sealed, hermetic, impermeable
Antonyms: leaky, porous, permeable
词性: 形容词
