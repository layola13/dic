continuing:/kənˈtɪnjuɪŋ/| adj.|持续的，继续的|Ongoing, still happening; not stopping.|The continuing rain caused flooding.  持续的降雨造成了洪水。|Synonyms: ongoing, persistent, ceaseless, unending, incessant|Antonyms: stopping, ceasing, intermittent, sporadic|adjective


continuing:/kənˈtɪnjuɪŋ/| n.|继续|The act of carrying on or prolonging something.|The continuing of the project depends on funding.  项目的继续取决于资金。|Synonyms: continuation,  persistence, progression|Antonyms: cessation, discontinuation, interruption|noun
