antioptimistical:/ˌæntiˌɒptɪˈmɪstɪkəl/
adjective
反乐观主义的; 悲观的
opposed to or lacking in optimism; pessimistic.
Her antioptimistical views made it difficult for her to see the good in any situation. 她反乐观主义的观点使她很难看到任何情况下的好处。
近义词: pessimistic, cynical, gloomy, negative, defeatist
反义词: optimistic, hopeful, positive, sanguine
词性: adjective
