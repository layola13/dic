lunatic: /ˈluːnətɪk/ (US) /ˈluːnətɪk/ (UK)| n. adj.|疯子，疯狂的|A person who is mentally ill (not in technical use). Also means extremely foolish or reckless.|例句：He drove like a lunatic. 他开车像个疯子一样。|近义词：madman, insane person, crazy person|反义词：sane person|词性：名词，形容词
lunatic: /ˈluːnətɪk/ (US) /ˈluːnətɪk/ (UK)| adj.|疯狂的|Extremely foolish or reckless.|例句：It was a lunatic idea to climb the mountain in the dark. 在黑暗中爬山真是个疯狂的主意。|近义词：crazy, insane, mad|反义词：sane, sensible, rational|词性：形容词
