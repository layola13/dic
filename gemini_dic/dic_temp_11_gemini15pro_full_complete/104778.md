kayos:/ˈkeɪɒs/ (US) /ˈkeɪɒs/ (UK)| n. |混乱，紊乱|A state of utter confusion or disorder; a total lack of organization or order.|The room was in complete kayos after the party. 房间在派对后一片混乱。|The hurricane left the city in kayos.  飓风过后，这座城市一片混乱。|近义词：chaos, disarray, disorder, turmoil, pandemonium|反义词：order, organization, peace, tranquility|词性:名词
词源: From Greek *khaos* "vast chasm, void," later "the formless matter supposed to have existed before the creation of the universe."
