nonhesitantly:/ˌnɒnˈhezɪtəntli/ | adv. |毫不犹豫地，毅然地 |Without hesitation; resolutely. |She nonhesitantly accepted the challenge. 她毫不犹豫地接受了挑战。|She nonhesitantly jumped into the icy water to save the child. 她毫不犹豫地跳进冰冷的水中救孩子。|近义词：resolutely, decisively, unhesitatingly, readily, promptly |反义词：hesitantly, reluctantly, tentatively |词性:副词

词源:
From non- + hesitantly, the adverbial form of hesitant, ultimately from Latin haerere "to stick, cling".
