bravoes:/ˈbrɑːvoʊz/| n. |喝彩；欢呼；暴徒；亡命之徒|A shout of "bravo!" or an instance of this, especially in the plural; a hired assassin or ruffian.  A daring or reckless person.|The bravoes cheered the matador after a skillful kill.  斗牛士熟练地杀死公牛后，暴徒们为他欢呼。|近义词：cheers, applause, assassins, ruffians, thugs |反义词: boos, disapproval|noun
