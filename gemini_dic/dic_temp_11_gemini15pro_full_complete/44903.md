cryonics:/kraɪˈɑːnɪks/ |n.|人体冷冻学|The practice or technique of deep-freezing the bodies of people who have just died, in the hope that scientific advances may allow them to be revived in the future. |Some people believe cryonics is a viable option for extending life.  有些人相信人体冷冻学是延长寿命的可行选择。| 近义词: cryopreservation | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 natural death 自然死亡作为概念上的对立) | 词性: noun

cryonics:/kraɪˈɑːnɪks/ |n.|人体冷冻技术|The science of freezing bodies or body parts for preservation in hopes of future revival. |He invested his fortune in cryonics research. 他把自己的财产投资于人体冷冻技术研究。| 近义词: cryogenics (低温学，更广泛) | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词) | 词性: noun

词源: From Greek *kryos* (cold) + suffix *-ics* (forming names of sciences).
