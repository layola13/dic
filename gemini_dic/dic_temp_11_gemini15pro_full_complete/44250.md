crofted:/ˈkrɒftɪd/ (UK) /ˈkrɑːftɪd/ (US)| adj.|耕作小块土地的；拥有小块耕地的|Relating to or characteristic of a croft (a small rented farm, especially in Scotland).|例句：He lived in a crofted cottage. 他住在一个耕作小块土地的村舍里。

例句：The crofted land provided a meager living. 这块耕作的小块土地只提供了微薄的生活。|近义词：small-scale farmed, smallholding|反义词：industrial farmed, large-scale|词性:形容词

词源: From 'croft' + '-ed'. 'Croft' comes from the Old English 'croft', meaning a small enclosed field or piece of land.
