embodying:/ɪmˈbɒdiɪŋ/ | adj. | 体现， воплощать, 구현하다 | representing or expressing a quality or idea exactly |  The dancer's movements were embodying the spirit of freedom.  舞者的动作体现了自由的精神。 | synonyms: personifying, representing, expressing, epitomizing | antonyms: misrepresenting, distorting | adjective

embody:/ɪmˈbɒdi/ | vt. | 体现，使具体化,  воплощать, 구현하다 | to represent or express a quality or idea exactly; to include or contain something | The new car embodies the latest technology. 这款新车体现了最新的技术。| synonyms: incorporate, include, comprise, contain | antonyms: exclude, omit | verb


ETYMOLOGY->

From Late Middle English *embodien*, from Anglo-Norman *embodier* and Old French *embodier*, from *en-* (in) + *bodi* (body). Equivalent to *em-* + *body*.  Ultimately from Latin *in-* (in) and *corpus* (body).
