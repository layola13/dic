mechitarist:/ˌmɛkɪˈtɑːrɪst/
n.|梅基塔里斯特教徒；梅基塔里斯特派的僧侣|A member of an Armenian Catholic congregation founded in 1701.  They are known for their scholarship and preservation of Armenian culture.|Example: The mechitarist monks are renowned for their ancient manuscripts.  梅基塔里斯特派的僧侣以其古老的手稿而闻名。

Example:  He learned classical Armenian from a mechitarist scholar. 他向一位梅基塔里斯特学者学习古典亚美尼亚语。|近义词:Armenian Catholic monk, scholar|反义词: (No direct antonym - could consider perhaps "layperson" in a religious context)|词性:noun
