inviter:/ɪnˈvaɪtər/ (US), /ɪnˈvaɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |邀请者；发起人|A person who invites someone to a social gathering or other event. |例句：The inviter requested guests to RSVP by Friday.  邀请者要求客人在周五前回复。

例句：As the inviter, she felt responsible for making sure everyone had a good time.  作为邀请者，她觉得有责任确保每个人都玩得开心。

近义词：host, organizer|反义词:guest, invitee|词性:noun
