martiality:/mɑːrʃiˈælɪti/| n.|好战性，尚武精神|The country's history is marked by a strong sense of martiality.  这个国家的历史以强烈的尚武精神为特征。|例句：The martiality of the Spartans was legendary. 斯巴达人的好战性是传奇性的。

The new leader seemed to revel in the martiality of his rhetoric. 新领导人似乎陶醉于他言辞中的好战性。|近义词：bellicosity, aggressiveness, pugnacity|反义词：peacefulness, pacifism|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *martialte*, from Old French *martialité*, from Latin *mārtiālitās*, from *mārtiālis* (“of Mars, warlike”) + *-tās* (forming abstract nouns).  *Mārtiālis* is from *Mārs* (“Mars, the Roman god of war”).
