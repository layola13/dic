notcher:/ˈnɒtʃər/ (UK) /ˈnɑːtʃər/ (US)| n.|刻痕器，切口工具；计数器；得分手|A tool or device used for making notches or cuts, often for keeping count or recording scores. |The carpenter used a notcher to create decorative edges on the wooden plank. 木匠用刻痕器在木板上制作装饰边缘。|Synonyms: cutter, scorer, marker|Antonyms: smoother, filler|noun
