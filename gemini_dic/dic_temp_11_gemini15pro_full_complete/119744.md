midwifely: /ˈmɪdˌwaɪfli/ | adj. | 助产的；像助产士的 | Relating to or characteristic of a midwife or midwifery.  |  She provided midwifely care to the expectant mother. 她为待产的母亲提供了助产护理。 |  Example: Her midwifely skills were invaluable during the difficult birth. 她的助产技能在难产中非常宝贵。 | 近义词: obstetric, maternal | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Middle English *midwyfly, from midwif + -ly.  "Midwife" comes from Old English *mīd, meaning "with" and *wīf, meaning "woman."  Thus, literally "with-woman."
