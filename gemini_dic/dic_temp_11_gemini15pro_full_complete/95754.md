inconsonancy:/ɪnkənˈsɒnənsi/ /ɪnkənˈsɑːnənsi/
n.
不一致，不和谐，不协调
lack of harmony or agreement; discord
The inconsonancy between her words and actions made it hard to trust her. 她言行不一，让人难以信任。
近义词：discord, dissonance, disagreement, incongruity, inconsistency
反义词：harmony, consonance, agreement, concord, consistency
词性：名词

inconsonant:/ɪnˈkɒnsənənt/ /ɪnˈkɑːnsənənt/
adj.
不一致的，不和谐的，不协调的
not in agreement or harmony; discordant
His actions were inconsonant with his promises. 他的行为与他的承诺不符。
近义词：discordant, dissonant, disagreeing, incongruous, inconsistent
反义词：consonant, harmonious, agreeing, concordant, consistent
词性：形容词
