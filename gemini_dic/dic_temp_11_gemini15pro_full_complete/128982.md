nipper:/ˈnɪpər/| n.|钳子；小男孩；小姑娘；(尤指昆虫的)螯,钳;|A tool for gripping or cutting something, typically small or delicate. Also, a young boy or girl, often mischievous. Can also refer to the pincers of an insect.|例句：He used a pair of nippers to cut the wire. 他用一把钳子剪断了电线。

例句：The little nipper was always getting into trouble.  这个小淘气总是惹麻烦。|近义词：pliers, pincers, child, toddler, kid|反义词:adult|词性:noun

nipper:/ˈnɪpər/| v.|夹,咬,掐;|To pinch or bite something, usually lightly. |例句：The crab nipped my toe. 螃蟹夹了我的脚趾。

例句：The cold wind nipped at our faces. 寒风刺痛了我们的脸。|近义词：pinch, nip, bite|反义词:release|词性:verb
