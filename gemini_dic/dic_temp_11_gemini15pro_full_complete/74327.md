fragile:/ˈfrædʒəl/ (US), /ˈfrædʒaɪl/ (UK)| adj.|易碎的，脆弱的|Easily broken or damaged; delicate; physically or emotionally weak.|The vase is fragile, so handle it with care.  这个花瓶易碎，所以要小心处理。|近义词：breakable, delicate, frail, vulnerable|反义词：strong, robust, sturdy|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *fragile*, from Latin *fragilis* "breakable," from *frangere* "to break." Related to *fraction* and *fracture*.
