copyrightable:/ˈkɒpiraɪtəbl/ /ˈkɑːpiraɪtəbl/
adjective
可版权的;受版权保护的
Capable of being copyrighted; protected by copyright.
This poem is copyrightable. 这首诗是可以受版权保护的。
The photograph is copyrightable as an artistic work. 这张照片可以作为艺术作品受版权保护。
近义词:protected, secured
反义词:uncopyrightable, public domain
词性:adjective
