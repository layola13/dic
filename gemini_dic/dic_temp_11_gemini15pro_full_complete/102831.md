jarsful:/ˈdʒɑːrzfʊl/
n.
几罐;若干罐的量
The amount contained in several jars.
She made three jarsful of jam. 她做了三罐果酱。
近义词: several jars, a few jars
反义词: a little, a small amount
词性: 名词
