anted:/ˈæntɪd/
verb
过时的，陈旧的
dated, antiquated, obsolete, old-fashioned, outmoded.  Meaning something belongs to an earlier period, especially so as to appear conspicuously old-fashioned.
The anted methods of farming. 过时的耕作方法。
Synonym: outdated, archaic, obsolete
Antonym: modern, contemporary, current
词性: 动词(过时用法,通常用作antedate)


antedate:/ˌæntiˈdeɪt/
verb
先于，早于
to be of an earlier date than something
The church antedates the Norman Conquest. 这座教堂早于诺曼征服。
Synonym: precede, predate
Antonym: postdate, follow
词性: 动词
