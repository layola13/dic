manning-up:/ˈmænɪŋ ʌp/| phrasal verb | 拿出男子气概；振作起来；承担责任 | To act in a traditionally masculine way, often involving courage, strength, or taking responsibility. It implies overcoming fear or hesitation and acting decisively. |  He finally manned up and apologized for his mistake. 他终于拿出男子气概，为自己的错误道歉了。

She manned up and confronted the bully. 她鼓起勇气，与恶霸对峙。 | 近义词：taking responsibility, stepping up, being brave, showing courage, growing up | 反义词：cowering, shrinking from responsibility, being cowardly, avoiding confrontation | 词性: 动词短语
