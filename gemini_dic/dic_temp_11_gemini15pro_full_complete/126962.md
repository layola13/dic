narrowband:/ˈnæroʊˌbænd/ | n.|窄频带 |A range of frequencies within a given band, used for transmitting signals. |Example: The narrowband was insufficient for high-quality video conferencing. 这个窄频带不足以进行高质量的视频会议。|Example: They allocated a narrowband for emergency communications. 他们分配了一个窄带用于紧急通信。|近义词:narrow band, limited bandwidth|反义词:wideband, broadband|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
narrow (Old English nearu "not broad or wide") + band (Old French bande "strip, ribbon")
