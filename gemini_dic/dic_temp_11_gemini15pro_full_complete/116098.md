martyrly:/ˈmɑːrtərlɪ/| adv. adj.|殉道者般地；像烈士一样|In a manner resembling a martyr; with an air of suffering or resignation, often exaggerated or feigned.  Acting as if suffering greatly for a cause, often unnecessarily.|He sighed martyrly and agreed to do the dishes. 他 martyrly 地叹了口气，同意去洗碗。|She wore a martyrly expression as she cleaned up after everyone. 她 martyrly 地在为每个人收拾完后，面带一副受苦的表情。|近义词：long-suffering, resigned, self-sacrificing|反义词：joyful, cheerful|词性: adverb, adjective


词源: From martyr + -ly.  Ultimately from the Greek μάρτυς (mártus), meaning "witness."
