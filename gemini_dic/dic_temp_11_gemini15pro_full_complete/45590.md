currant:/ˈkɜːrənt/ (UK), /ˈkɜːrənt/ (US)| n. |醋栗；无核小葡萄干|A small dried seedless grape, used especially in cakes. Also, the small, round berry of several shrubs of the genus *Ribes*.|例句：I added currants and raisins to the cake batter. 我在蛋糕糊里加了醋栗和葡萄干。

例句：The bush was laden with ripe red currants.  灌木丛上结满了成熟的红醋栗。|近义词：raisin (for dried currants), berry (for fresh currants)|反义词: None|词性:Noun


currants:/ˈkʌrənts/ (plural form)| n. |(通常指)红醋栗|Small, round, red or black berries, typically the red kind, used in cooking. This usage usually refers to the fresh berries, not the dried ones.|例句：She made a delicious currant jam. 她做了一罐美味的红醋栗酱。

例句：The currants were tart and juicy. 这些醋栗酸酸甜甜的，汁水很多。|近义词：redcurrants|反义词: None|词性:Noun
