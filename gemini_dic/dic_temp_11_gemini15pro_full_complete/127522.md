necrolite: /ˈnɛkrəˌlaɪt/
noun
死灵石，墓石
A stone associated with death or burial; a tombstone.
The archaeologist discovered a necrolite with ancient inscriptions. 考古学家发现了一块刻有古代铭文的墓石。
Synonyms: tombstone, gravestone, headstone, monument
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider: birthstone, living memorial)
