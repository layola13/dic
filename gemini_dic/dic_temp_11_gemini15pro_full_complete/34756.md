cliffhanger:/ˈklɪfhæŋər/| n.|扣人心弦的结局，悬念|A dramatic and exciting ending to an episode of a serial, leaving the audience in suspense and anxious not to miss the next episode.|The episode ended on a real cliffhanger, leaving us all wondering what would happen next.  这一集的结尾真是扣人心弦，让我们都想知道接下来会发生什么。|synonyms: suspense, climax, nail-biter|antonyms: resolution, conclusion|noun

cliffhanger:/ˈklɪfhæŋər/| n.|悬而未决的情况|A situation or contest of which the outcome is suspenseful and uncertain until the very end.|The election was a cliffhanger right up to the final count. 选举直到最后一刻才见分晓。|synonyms: deadlock, stalemate, stand-off|antonyms: certainty, predictability|noun
