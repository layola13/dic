octavos:/ɒkˈteɪvoʊz/ /ɑːkˈteɪvoʊz/
noun
八开本（复数）
A size of book page resulting from folding a sheet of paper three times to form eight leaves.
The library has several rare books in octavo. 图书馆里有几本罕见的八开本书。
octavo (singular), book size
folio, quarto (smaller sizes)
noun
词源：From Latin octāvus (“eighth”).
