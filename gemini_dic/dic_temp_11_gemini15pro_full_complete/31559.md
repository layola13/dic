chary:/ˈtʃɛəri/| adj.|谨慎的，吝惜的，小心的|Cautious, wary, or reluctant; unwilling to give freely.  Characterized by great caution and restraint.|She was chary of offering advice, fearing she might be wrong. 她很谨慎，不愿提供建议，担心自己可能会错。|近义词：wary, cautious, circumspect, hesitant, reluctant, sparing, frugal|反义词：reckless, imprudent, extravagant, lavish, profuse|形容词
