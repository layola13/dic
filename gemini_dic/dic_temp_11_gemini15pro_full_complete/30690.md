certifiableness:/ˌsɜːrtɪfaɪəbəlnəs/ | n. | 可证明性；可认证性 | The quality of being able to be certified; the state of being certifiable. | The certifiableness of his mental illness was questioned by the defense.  他的精神疾病的可证明性受到了辩方的质疑。| Synonyms: attestability, verifiability | Antonyms: unverifiability, indisputability | noun

2. He went certifiable after losing his job and family. 他失去工作和家庭后变得疯疯癫癫的。 | Synonyms: attestable, verifiable, (informal) crazy, mad, insane | Antonyms: unverifiable, (informal) sane, rational | adjective
