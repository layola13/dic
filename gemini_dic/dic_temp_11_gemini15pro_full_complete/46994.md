dash-down:/ˈdæʃ daʊn/| n. |  猛冲；突袭；(文字的)快速书写 | A sudden rapid movement downwards.  A piece of writing done quickly. |  The dash-down of the protesters surprised the police. 抗议者的突袭让警察措手不及。 | synonyms: rush, charge, plunge | antonyms: ascent, climb | noun
