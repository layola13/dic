hydrographer:/haɪˈdrɒɡrəfər (UK), haɪˈdrɑːɡrəfər (US)| n. |水文地理学家；水道测量员|A person who studies and charts bodies of water, especially the seafloor and its features, for navigation, engineering, or scientific purposes.|The hydrographer carefully charted the depths of the bay. 水文地理学家仔细地绘制了海湾的深度。|近义词：cartographer, surveyor|反义词: N/A|noun

hydrography:/haɪˈdrɒɡrəfi (UK), haɪˈdrɑːɡrəfi (US)| n. |水文地理学；水道测量学|The science of surveying and charting bodies of water, especially the seafloor and its features.|The study of hydrography is crucial for safe navigation. 水文地理学的研究对于安全航行至关重要。|近义词：charting, surveying|反义词: N/A|noun
