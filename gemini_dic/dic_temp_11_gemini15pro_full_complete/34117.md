clacker:/ˈklækər/| n.|咔哒声器，响板|A device that makes a clacking sound, often two pieces of wood or other material hinged together.  A toy consisting of two balls attached by string to a handle, swung so that the balls click together.  Also, a person's teeth.|The child loved playing with his clackers.  孩子喜欢玩他的响板。|Synonyms: rattle, clicker, clapper|Antonyms: silence, quiet|noun

clacker:/ˈklækər/| v.|发出咔哒声|To make a short, sharp sound by striking two hard objects together.|The wood clackered as the wind blew the shutters.  风吹动百叶窗，木头发出咔哒声。|Synonyms: clatter, rattle, click|Antonyms: muffle, silence|verb
