ecuador:/ˈɛkwədɔr/ (US), /ɪˈkwɛɪdɔːr/ (UK)| n.|厄瓜多尔|A country in northwestern South America, crossed by the equator.|例句：Ecuador is known for its biodiversity. 厄瓜多尔以其生物多样性而闻名。

例句：The Galapagos Islands are part of Ecuador.  加拉帕戈斯群岛是厄瓜多尔的一部分。|近义词:Republic of Ecuador|反义词:None|词性:Noun


Etymology: From Spanish *Ecuador*, literally "equator," from Latin *aequator* ("equalizer").
