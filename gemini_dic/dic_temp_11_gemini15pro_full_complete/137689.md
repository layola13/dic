nonurbanite:/ˌnɒnˈɜːrbənaɪt/ | /ˌnɑːnˈɜːrbənaɪt/ | n. | 非城市居民，乡下人 | A person who does not live in a city or urban area. |  She considers herself a nonurbanite, preferring the peace and quiet of the countryside. 她认为自己是一个非城市居民，更喜欢乡村的宁静。|  He's a true nonurbanite; he wouldn't last a week in New York City. 他是一个真正的乡下人；他在纽约市待不了一周。 | 近义词：rural dweller, countryman, countrywoman | 反义词：urbanite, city dweller | 词性: 名词

Etymology: non- + urbanite.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "urbanite" refers to a city dweller.
