exoticist:/ɪɡˈzɒtɪsɪst/ | n. |异国情调主义者；崇尚异国事物者|A person who is fascinated by foreign cultures, especially those perceived as unusual or different, often romanticizing or stereotyping them.|  She was accused of being an exoticist, reducing the complex culture to a few colorful customs. 她被指责为异国情调主义者，将复杂的文化简化为几个色彩鲜艳的习俗。|近义词：orientalist, romanticist|反义词：nativist, localist|词性: noun

exoticist:/ɪɡˈzɒtɪsɪst/ | adj. |异国情调的；崇尚异国事物的|Characterized by the fascination with foreign cultures, often romanticizing or stereotyping them.| Her exoticist view of the East ignored the real struggles faced by the people living there. 她对东方的异国情调的看法忽略了那里人民面临的真正斗争。|近义词：exotic, foreign, unusual|反义词：native, indigenous, local|词性: adjective
