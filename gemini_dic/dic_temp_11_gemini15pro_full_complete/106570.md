kryptonite:/ˈkrɪptəˌnaɪt/| n. |氪星石；弱点|A fictional element from the Superman comic books, portrayed as the only substance that can harm Superman.  Figuratively, kryptonite refers to a person's particular weakness.|例句：Public speaking is my kryptonite. 公开演讲是我的弱点。

例句：He found her constant nagging to be his kryptonite. 他发现她不断的唠叨是他的弱点。|近义词：weakness, Achilles' heel, bane|反义词:strength, forte|词性:noun
