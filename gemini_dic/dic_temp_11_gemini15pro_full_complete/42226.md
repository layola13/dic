counsels:/ˈkaʊnsəlz/
counsel:/ˈkaʊnsl/ | v. |  劝告，忠告 | To give advice or guidance to someone. |  He counseled the young man to stay in school. 他劝告这个年轻人继续上学。 | synonyms: advise, guide, recommend | antonyms: dissuade, discourage | verb
