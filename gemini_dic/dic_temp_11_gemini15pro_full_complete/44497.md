crossflow:/ˈkrɒsfləʊ/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsfloʊ/ (US)| n. adj.| 横流，交叉流，错流 |The movement of a fluid, such as a liquid or gas, perpendicular to the main flow direction.  It can refer to the flow itself or a device or system designed to facilitate such flow.|

例句：
The crossflow filtration system effectively removes impurities.
横流过滤系统有效地去除了杂质。

The crossflow of air helped to cool the engine.
空气的横流有助于冷却发动机。

近义词： transverse flow, countercurrent flow|反义词: parallel flow, cocurrent flow|词性: noun, adjective


crossflow:/ˈkrɒsfləʊ/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsfloʊ/ (US)| adj.| 横流的，交叉流的 |Relating to or involving a crossflow.|

例句：
A crossflow heat exchanger is commonly used in industrial processes.
横流式换热器常用于工业过程。

The crossflow design improves the efficiency of the system.
横流设计提高了系统的效率。

近义词： transverse, countercurrent|反义词: parallel, cocurrent|词性: adjective
