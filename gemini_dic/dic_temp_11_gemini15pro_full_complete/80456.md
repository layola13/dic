gongoozler:/ɡɒŋˈɡuːzlə(r)/
|n.|观光闲人，在运河桥上闲逛的人|An idle spectator, especially one who spends time watching activity on a canal. |例句：He's a bit of a gongoozler, always watching the boats go by. 他有点像个观光闲人，总是看着船只来来往往。

例句：The canal was busy with narrowboats and gongoozlers enjoying the sunshine. 运河上挤满了窄船和享受阳光的观光闲人。

|近义词：idler, loafer, onlooker, bystander|反义词:worker, participant|词性:名词
