exterminate: /ɪkˈstɜːrmɪneɪt/ /ɪkˈstɝːmɪneɪt/
verb
消灭，根除
to kill all the members of a group of people or animals
The Nazis tried to exterminate the Jews. 纳粹试图灭绝犹太人。

近义词: annihilate, eradicate, wipe out, destroy, eliminate
反义词: create, preserve, protect, conserve

词性: verb
词源: From Latin exterminatus, past participle of exterminare (“to drive out, banish, destroy”), from ex (“out of”) + terminus (“boundary, limit”).
