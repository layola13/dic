mislin:/ˈmɪslɪn/ | n. | 麦斯林布（一种细棉布）| A fine cotton fabric, often used for sheets or clothing. | Example:  She wore a dress made of soft mislin. 她穿了一件柔软的麦斯林布做的连衣裙。 | Example: The mislin sheets felt cool against his skin. 麦斯林床单贴着他的皮肤感觉很凉爽。 | 近义词: muslin, cambric, lawn | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps coarse cloth) | 词性: noun
