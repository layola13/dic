colporteur:/ˈkɒlpɔːtər/ (UK), /ˈkɑːlpɔːrtər/ (US)| n. |小贩；挨户兜售者；传播者|A person who travels from place to place selling books, pamphlets, or other small articles, especially religious literature; a disseminator of ideas or information.|例句：The colporteur traveled through the countryside, distributing religious tracts. 这位小贩在乡间旅行，分发宗教小册子。|例句：He acted as a colporteur of gossip, spreading rumors throughout the town. 他像个流言蜚语的小贩，在镇上散布谣言。|近义词：peddler, hawker, vendor, disseminator|反义词: receiver, collector |词性: noun
