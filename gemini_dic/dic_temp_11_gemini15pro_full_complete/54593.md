dismantler:/dɪsˈmæntlər/ | n. | 拆卸工，拆除者 | A person who dismantles something, especially machinery or a complex structure. | The dismantler carefully took apart the old clock.  拆卸工小心地拆开了旧钟。| synonyms: deconstructor, demolisher, disassembler | antonyms: assembler, builder, constructor | noun

dismantle:/dɪsˈmæntl/ | vt. | 拆卸，拆除 | To take apart a machine or structure.  | They dismantled the engine to find the problem. 他们拆卸了引擎以找出问题所在。| synonyms: disassemble, take apart, break down | antonyms: assemble, construct, build | verb
