fruitfuller:/ˈfruːtfʊlər/ | adj. | 更富有成效的，更丰硕的 | More fruitful; producing more abundant results. | This year has been fruitfuller than the last in terms of scientific discoveries. 今年在科学发现方面比去年更有成效。| The fruitfuller soil yielded a bountiful harvest.  更肥沃的土壤带来了丰收。 | 近义词: more productive, more prolific, richer | 反义词: less productive, barren, infertile | 词性: 形容词

词源: From "fruitful" + "-er" (comparative suffix).  Fruitful itself comes from "fruit" + "-ful" (suffix meaning "full of").
