lilliputian:/ˌlɪlɪˈpjuːʃən/ /ˌlɪlɪˈpjuːʃn/ | adj. n. | 小人国的；微型的；极小的 |  Extremely small; tiny.  Referring to the fictional inhabitants of Lilliput in Jonathan Swift's *Gulliver's Travels*, who were only six inches tall. |  The model village was built on a lilliputian scale.  这个模型村庄是按小人国的比例建造的。 | miniature, tiny, diminutive, minuscule | gigantic, enormous, huge | adjective

lilliputian:/ˌlɪlɪˈpjuːʃən/ /ˌlɪlɪˈpjuːʃn/ | n. | 小人国居民；矮人 | An inhabitant of Lilliput; a very small person. | He felt like a lilliputian standing next to the basketball player.  站在篮球运动员旁边，他觉得自己像个小人国的人。 | dwarf, midget, pygmy | giant | noun
