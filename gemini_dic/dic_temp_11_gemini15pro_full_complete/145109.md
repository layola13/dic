overapprehensively:/ˌoʊvərˌæprɪˈhɛnsɪvli/
| adv. | 过度忧虑地，过分担心地 | Excessively or unduly apprehensive; characterized by excessive worry or anxiety. | She overapprehensively checked the lock three times before leaving the house. 她离开房子前过度焦虑地检查了三次锁。 |  He overapprehensively clutched his passport as he went through customs.  他过分担心地抓着护照通过海关。 | 近义词：anxiously, nervously, fearfully, apprehensively | 反义词：calmly, confidently, serenely | 词性: 副词

词源:
From "over-" (excessively) + "apprehensive" (anxious or fearful that something bad or unpleasant will happen) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Apprehensive" ultimately derives from Latin "apprehendere" (to seize, grasp).
