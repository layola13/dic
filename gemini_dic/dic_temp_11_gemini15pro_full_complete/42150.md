coucal: /ˈkuːkəl/ (US), /ˈkuːkɑːl/ (UK)| n. |杜鹃科鸟类; 噪鹃| A type of bird belonging to the cuckoo family, known for their loud calls. |例句：The coucal is a common bird in Africa and Asia. 噪鹃是非洲和亚洲常见的鸟类。
