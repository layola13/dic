mentee:/ˌmɛnˈtiː/ | n. | 受指导者，被指导者 | A person who is advised, trained, or counseled by a mentor. |  My mentor introduced me to her other mentee. 我的导师把我介绍给了她的另一个受指导者。

|  The mentee benefited greatly from the mentor's experience.  这位受指导者从导师的经验中获益匪浅。 | 近义词：protégé, pupil, student | 反义词：mentor, adviser | 词性: noun
