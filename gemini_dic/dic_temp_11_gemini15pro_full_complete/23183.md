bowdlerizations:/ˌboʊdləraɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n.|删节；净化；（对书籍、剧本等的）洁本化；|An act of bowdlerizing something, especially literature, by removing or changing parts considered offensive.  指对某些内容（尤其是文学作品）进行删改，去除或修改被认为冒犯的部分的行为。|The modern bowdlerizations of Shakespeare's plays often remove the bawdy jokes.  莎士比亚戏剧的现代洁本通常会删除其中的猥亵笑话。|近义词：censorship, expurgation, purging|反义词：inclusion, unabridged|词性:名词
