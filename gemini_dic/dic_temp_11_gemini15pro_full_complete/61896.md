empty-handed:/ˌem(p)tiˈhændɪd/
adj.
空手的；一无所获的
Having or bringing nothing.
He returned empty-handed from his fishing trip. 他钓鱼之旅一无所获。
She went to the store for milk but came home empty-handed. 她去商店买牛奶，但空手而归。
近义词:unsuccessful, fruitless
反义词:successful, fruitful
词性:形容词

empty-handed:/ˌem(p)tiˈhændɪd/
adv.
空手地；一无所获地
In a manner that involves having or bringing nothing.
They left the meeting empty-handed, having failed to secure a deal.  他们在会议上一无所获，未能达成协议。
The thieves fled empty-handed after the alarm went off. 警报响起后，小偷空手而逃。
近义词:unsuccessfully, fruitlessly
反义词:successfully, fruitfully
词性:副词
