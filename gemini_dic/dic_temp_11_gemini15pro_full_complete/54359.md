dishonour:/dɪsˈɒnə(r)/ (UK) /dɪsˈɑːnər/ (US)| n. vt.|耻辱，丢脸；拒付|refusal to accept or pay a bill of exchange, etc.  A cause of disgrace; shame.|例句：His actions brought dishonour to his family. 他的行为给他的家庭带来了耻辱。|近义词：shame, disgrace, ignominy|反义词：honour, respect|noun
