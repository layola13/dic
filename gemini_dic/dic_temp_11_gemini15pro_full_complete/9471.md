antiresistants:/ˌæntiɹɪˈzɪstənts/ | n. | 抗药性药物; 抗性抑制剂 |Substances that counteract the development of resistance, especially to drugs or pesticides. |The development of new antiresistants is crucial in the fight against drug-resistant bacteria.  开发新的抗药性药物对于对抗耐药细菌至关重要。| New antiresistants are being researched to combat the growing problem of pesticide resistance in crops.  新的抗性抑制剂正在研究中，以应对日益增长的作物抗药性问题。| synonyms: resistance inhibitors, resistance blockers | antonyms: resistance promoters, resistance enhancers | noun
