culprit:/ˈkʌlprɪt/
n.
罪犯，犯人，肇事者
A person who is responsible for a crime or other misdeed.
例句：The police are still searching for the culprit. 警方仍在搜寻罪犯。
例句：The culprit was caught on camera. 罪犯被摄像头拍到了。
近义词：offender, criminal, perpetrator, guilty party
反义词：victim, innocent
词性：名词

词源：From Anglo-Norman *culpablete, from culpe “fault, guilt” + -ete, diminutive suffix.  The word originally meant "guilty," but shifted in meaning to "the guilty person" in the 18th century.
