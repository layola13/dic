in the crosshairs:  在瞄准镜的十字准线内;  比喻成为攻击目标

keep the crosshairs on: 保持瞄准
Keep the crosshairs on the target until you're ready to fire. 保持瞄准目标，直到你准备好射击。
adjust the crosshairs: 调整十字准线
You need to adjust the crosshairs to account for the wind. 你需要调整十字准线以应对风的影响。
center the crosshairs: 将十字准线居中
Center the crosshairs on the bullseye. 将十字准线放在靶心上。

Illuminated crosshairs are helpful in low-light conditions.  照明十字准线在光线不足的情况下很有帮助。
