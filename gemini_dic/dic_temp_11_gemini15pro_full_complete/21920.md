boffiny:/ˈbɒfɪni/| n.|科学家; 科学工作者 (尤指看上去有点古怪的)|A boffin is a scientist, especially one who is thought of as being slightly strange or eccentric.|例句：The boffins have come up with a new invention. 科学家们发明了一种新玩意儿。

例句：He's a bit of a boffin, always tinkering with gadgets. 他有点像个怪咖科学家，总是摆弄小玩意儿。|近义词：scientist, researcher, technician, geek, nerd|反义词:layman|词性:noun
