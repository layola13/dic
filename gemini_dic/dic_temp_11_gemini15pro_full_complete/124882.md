muddily:/ˈmʌdɪli/ | adv. | 模糊地，混乱地，泥泞地 | In a muddy or unclear manner; covered or filled with mud. | The story was told so muddily that I couldn't follow it. 故事讲得乱七八糟，我根本听不懂。| The jeep lurched muddily through the deep ruts. 吉普车在深深的车辙中泥泞地颠簸前行。 | 近义词: unclearly, vaguely, confusedly, dirtily | 反义词: clearly, distinctly, cleanly | 词性: 副词

词源: From Middle English *muddili, derived from muddy + -ly.  Ultimately from Old English *muddig.
