levigation: /ˌlɛvɪˈɡeɪʃən/
n.
研磨，细磨；水磨；洗提
The process of grinding an insoluble substance to a fine powder while wet.
Levigation is used in the pharmaceutical industry to create smooth ointments.  研磨在制药工业中用于制造光滑的药膏。
The archaeologist used levigation to separate the fine particles of pottery from the surrounding soil. 考古学家用水磨法将陶器的细小颗粒从周围的土壤中分离出来。
近义词: grinding, milling, trituration, pulverization
反义词: agglomeration, aggregation
