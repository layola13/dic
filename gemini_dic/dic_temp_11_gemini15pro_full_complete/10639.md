| adj.  v. |合适的，恰当的；拨出（款项）|Suitable or proper in the circumstances.|This dress is appropriate for a wedding. 这件衣服适合参加婚礼。|近义词:suitable, proper, fitting, apt|反义词:inappropriate, unsuitable, improper|adjective
appropriate: /əˈproʊprieɪt/ (US) /əˈprəʊpriət/ (UK)
| v. |拨出（款项）；挪用|Devote (money or assets) to a special purpose.|Congress appropriated $5 million for the project. 国会拨款五百万美元用于这个项目。|近义词:allocate, assign, earmark|反义词:misappropriate, withhold|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin appropriatus, past participle of appropriare "to make one's own," from ad- "to" + proprius "one's own."
