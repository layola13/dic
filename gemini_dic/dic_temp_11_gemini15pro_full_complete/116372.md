mastic:/ˈmæstɪk/ | n. |乳香，胶泥，密封胶| a resin obtained from the mastic tree, used in varnishes, chewing gum, and dental fillings. Also, a type of cement used for filling holes and cracks, especially in walls. | The artist used mastic varnish to protect the painting. 艺术家用乳香清漆来保护这幅画。 | Synonyms: resin, gum, sealant, cement, putty | Antonyms: solvent | noun


mastic:/ˈmæstɪk/ | adj. |乳香的，可咀嚼的| relating to or made from mastic. |  Mastic gum is a traditional chewing gum. 乳香树胶是一种传统的口香糖。| Synonyms: resinous, gummy | Antonyms: N/A | adjective
