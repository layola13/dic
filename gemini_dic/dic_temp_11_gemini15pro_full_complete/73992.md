forts:/fɔːrts/| n.|堡垒；要塞|A strong or fortified place occupied by troops and usually surrounded by walls, ditches, and other defensive works.|The soldiers defended their forts bravely. 士兵们英勇地保卫他们的堡垒。|The army captured several enemy forts.  军队攻占了几个敌军要塞。|synonyms: fortresses, castles, strongholds, citadels|antonyms:  villages, towns|noun
