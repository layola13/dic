accomplishes: /əˈkɑːmplɪʃɪz/
verb
完成，实现
succeeds in doing; brings to completion.
She accomplishes her goals through hard work and dedication. 她通过努力工作和奉献精神实现了她的目标。
The team accomplished the project ahead of schedule.  团队提前完成了项目。
synonyms: achieves, attains, fulfills, executes, performs
antonyms: fails, neglects, abandons
词性: 动词
