cv:/ˌsiːˈviː/ | n. | 简历; 履历 | Curriculum Vitae, a short written summary of your education, qualifications, previous jobs, and sometimes also your personal interests, which you send to an employer when you are trying to get a job. | Example: I updated my CV before applying for the job. 我在申请工作前更新了我的简历。 | Example:  Please send your CV and a covering letter to the address below. 请将你的简历和求职信寄到下面的地址。| Synonym: resume, resumé | Antonym: N/A | noun
