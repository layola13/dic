examiners:/ɪɡˈzæmɪnərz/| n. |考官；审查员；检验员|People officially appointed to test students' knowledge or ability.|The examiners were very strict. 考官们非常严格。|The examiners carefully reviewed each paper.  考官们仔细审阅了每一份试卷。|近义词：assessors, testers, inspectors, reviewers|反义词: examinees, candidates|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English examinour, from Anglo-Norman examinour, Old French examinateur, from Medieval Latin examinator, from Latin examinare "to weigh, test, examine" + -tor.
