bestowment:/bɪˈstoʊmənt/
n.
授予，给予；赠与；赋予
the act of bestowing something, or something that is bestowed
例句：The bestowment of the medal of honor was a moving ceremony.  荣誉勋章的授予仪式令人感动。

例句：He considered the bestowment of his father's watch as a great honor. 他认为得到父亲的手表是一项极大的荣誉。

近义词：gift, award, presentation, conferment, donation
反义词：deprivation, forfeiture, confiscation
词性：noun
