ott:/oʊ tiː tiː/ |abbr.| over-the-top |  指通过互联网交付的流媒体服务，绕过传统的广播或有线电视分发渠道。|  Example: Netflix is a popular OTT platform. Netflix是一个流行的OTT平台。 | Synonym: streaming, online video, internet TV | Antonym: traditional television, cable TV | noun

ott:/oʊ tiː tiː/ |abbr.| over-the-top |  (Informal) Excessive, extreme, or exaggerated. |  Example: Her reaction was a bit OTT. 她的反应有点过激。 | Synonym: excessive, exaggerated, extreme, overdone | Antonym: understated, subtle, moderate | adjective
