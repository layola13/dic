ASML:/ˌeɪ ɛs ɛmˈɛl/ | n. | 阿斯麦（公司名称） |  ASML is an abbreviation for Advanced Semiconductor Materials Lithography. It is a Dutch multinational company that specializes in the development and manufacturing of photolithography systems, used in the production of integrated circuits (chips). | ASML is the leading supplier of lithography systems for the semiconductor industry. 阿斯麦是半导体行业光刻系统的主要供应商。| Synonym: (No direct synonym as it's a proper noun referring to a specific company) | Antonym:  (No antonym for a proper noun) | proper noun
