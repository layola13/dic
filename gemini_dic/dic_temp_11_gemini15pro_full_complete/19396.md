bicarburetted: /baɪˌkɑːrbjʊˈrɛtɪd/ /baɪˌkɑːrbjəˈrɛtɪd/
adjective
(of a gas) combined with twice the amount of carbon with which it normally combines.  双碳化(与正常结合的碳量两倍)的
The bicarburetted hydrogen gas, or olefiant gas, is generally employed.  双碳化氢气体或烯烃气体通常被使用。
