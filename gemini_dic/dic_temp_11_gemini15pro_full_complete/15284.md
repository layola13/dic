backlisted:/ˈblækˌlɪstɪd/
adj.
被列入黑名单的；禁止的
Placed on a blacklist; barred or excluded from a privilege, service, or group.
He was backlisted by the Hollywood studios. 他被好莱坞制片厂列入了黑名单。
Synonyms: banned, barred, boycotted, excluded, ostracized, prohibited
Antonyms: accepted, approved, allowed, permitted, welcomed
词性: 形容词

backlist:/ˈblækˌlɪst/
n.
黑名单
A list of people or things that are disapproved of or are to be avoided or penalized.
The company maintains a blacklist of suppliers known for unethical practices.  这家公司保留了一份因不道德行为而闻名的供应商黑名单。
Synonyms: roster, index, register, inventory, catalogue
Antonyms: whitelist
词性: 名词

backlist:/ˈblækˌlɪst/
vt.
把…列入黑名单
To put someone or something on a blacklist.
Several websites have been backlisted for spreading misinformation.  一些网站因传播虚假信息而被列入黑名单。
Synonyms: ban, bar, boycott, exclude, ostracize, prohibit
Antonyms: accept, approve, allow, permit, welcome
词性: 动词
