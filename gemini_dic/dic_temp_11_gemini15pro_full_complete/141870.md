orchidlike:/ˈɔːrkɪdlaɪk/
adjective
像兰花的；兰花似的
Resembling an orchid in some way, such as in shape, color, or fragrance.
The delicate, orchidlike flowers bloomed in the spring. 这些精致的、像兰花一样的花朵在春天盛开。
Her dress was a vibrant purple, almost orchidlike in its hue. 她的裙子是鲜艳的紫色，色调几乎像兰花一样。
近义词:orchid-shaped, orchidaceous
反义词: (No direct antonym, could be described as unlike an orchid, plain, simple)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From 'orchid' + '-like'. 'Orchid' comes from the Greek 'orkhis' meaning testicle, due to the shape of the root tubers of some species. '-like' is a suffix forming adjectives meaning 'similar to'.
