nexus:/ˈnɛksəs/| n. |连接，关系，中心|A connection or link between people, things, or ideas. | 例句：There is a clear nexus between poverty and crime. 贫穷和犯罪之间有着明显的联系。

例句：The restaurant is at the nexus of several major highways. 这家餐馆位于几条主要高速公路的交汇处。|近义词：connection, link, bond, center, hub|反义词：division, separation|词性: 名词
