endamplify: /ɛndˈæmplɪfaɪ/
Verb
增强，扩大（信号或效应）
To increase the amplitude or magnitude of a signal or effect.
The feedback loop endamplifies the initial signal, creating a much larger output. 反馈回路增强了初始信号，产生了更大的输出。
The catalyst endamplifies the reaction rate. 催化剂增强了反应速率。
Synonyms: amplify, boost, magnify, intensify, enhance
Antonyms: attenuate, diminish, reduce, weaken
词性: 动词
