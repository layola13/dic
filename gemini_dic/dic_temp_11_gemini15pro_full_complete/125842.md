murtherer: /ˈmɜːrðərər/ (British English), /ˈmɜːrdərər/ (American English)| n. | 杀人犯; 凶手 |A person who commits murder. | The murtherer was apprehended shortly after the crime.  凶手在犯罪后不久就被逮捕了。 |  The police are searching for the murtherer. 警方正在搜捕凶手。|Synonyms: murderer, assassin, killer, homicide, slayer | Antonyms: victim, protector, savior | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *murtherer*, equivalent to *murther* (murder) + *-er* (agent suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *murþrą* (secret killing, murder).
