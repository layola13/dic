discloses:/dɪˈskloʊzɪz/
verb
揭露；透露；公开
makes (secret or new information) known.
The company discloses its financial results every quarter.  该公司每个季度都会公布其财务业绩。
The report discloses the extent of police corruption. 该报告揭露了警察腐败的程度。
reveal, expose, divulge
conceal, hide, suppress
词性:动词(第三人称单数)
