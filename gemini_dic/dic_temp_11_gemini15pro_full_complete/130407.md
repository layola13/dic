non-voter:/ˌnɒnˈvəʊtər/ (British), /ˌnɑːnˈvoʊtər/ (American)| n.|非投票者，不投票的人|A person who is eligible to vote but chooses not to or is unable to.|Many non-voters are disillusioned with the political process. 许多不投票的人对政治进程感到失望。|Synonyms: abstainer, non-participant|Antonyms: voter, elector|Noun
