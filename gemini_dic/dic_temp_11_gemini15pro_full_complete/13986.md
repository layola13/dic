auricles:/ˈɔːrɪkəlz/ /ˈɑːrɪkəlz/| n. |心耳；耳廓；[动]耳状附器|A small muscular pouch, often paired, at the upper anterior angle of each atrium of the heart, that functions as a reservoir for blood returning to the heart. Also refers to the external ear.|The surgeon carefully examined the patient's auricles. 外科医生仔细检查了病人的心耳。|Synonyms: atria (in cardiology), pinna (for external ear)|Antonyms: ventricles (in cardiology)|noun
