nomothetical:/ˌnɒməˈθɛtɪkəl/ | adj. | 法则性的，立法的 | Relating to the study or discovery of general scientific laws.  Concerned with the establishment of general laws and principles, as opposed to the study of individual cases. |  Nomothetic research seeks to establish general principles of human behavior. 法则性研究旨在建立人类行为的一般原则。

Psychology utilizes both nomothetic and idiographic approaches to understand human behavior. 心理学运用法则性和个体性两种方法来理解人类行为。 | 近义词: general, universal, law-governed, legislative | 反义词: idiographic, particular, individual | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Greek *nomos* (law) + *thetikos* (laying down).
