counterreformers:/ˌkaʊntər rɪˈfɔːrmərz/
n.
反改革者，反宗教改革者
Those who opposed the Protestant Reformation and sought to reform the Catholic Church from within.  Those who supported the Counter-Reformation.
The Council of Trent was attended by many influential counterreformers. 特伦托会议有很多有影响力的反改革者参加。
Counterreformers implemented strict measures to combat the spread of Protestantism. 反改革者实施了严厉的措施来对抗新教的传播。

Synonyms: Catholic reformers,  Tridentine reformers
Antonyms: reformers, Protestants

词性: noun
