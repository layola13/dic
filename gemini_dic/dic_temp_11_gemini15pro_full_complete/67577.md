extrapunitive:/ˌekstrəˈpjuːnɪtɪv/
adjective
外罚型的，向外攻击的
Directing blame or aggression outward.  Focusing on punishing others for one's misfortunes rather than oneself.
After failing the exam, John felt extrapunitive and blamed the teacher for setting an unfair test.  考试不及格后，约翰感到外罚，并责怪老师出题不公平。
She displayed extrapunitive behavior by yelling at the cashier for the long line. 她对着收银员大喊大叫，因为排队时间太长，表现出了外罚行为。
近义词: aggressive, hostile, outward-directed
反义词: intropunitive, self-blaming
词性: adjective
