half-hearted:/ˌhæfˈhɑːrtɪd/ | adj. | 不热心的，敷衍的，三心二意的 | lacking enthusiasm or commitment; done without real effort or interest | The team's performance was half-hearted and they lost the game. 球队的表现缺乏热情，他们输掉了比赛。 | listless, perfunctory |  eager, keen, passionate | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From half + hearted.  "Hearted" in this context refers to having a particular disposition or character.  Thus, "half-hearted" literally means having only half of one's heart in something.
