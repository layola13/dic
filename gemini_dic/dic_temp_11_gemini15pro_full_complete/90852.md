hunger:/ˈhʌŋɡər/ (US), /ˈhʌŋɡə(r)/ (UK)| n. |饥饿；渴望；|A feeling of discomfort or weakness caused by lack of food, coupled with the desire to eat.|She fainted from hunger. 她因饥饿而晕倒了。|近义词：starvation, craving, appetite|反义词:satiety, fullness|noun

hunger:/ˈhʌŋɡər/ (US), /ˈhʌŋɡə(r)/ (UK)| v. |渴望；使……饥饿|To have a strong desire or craving for something.|He hungered for knowledge. 他渴望知识。|近义词：crave, yearn, desire|反义词:satisfy|verb
