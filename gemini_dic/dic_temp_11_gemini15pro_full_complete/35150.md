clover:/ˈkləʊvər/ (UK) /ˈkloʊvər/ (US)| n. |三叶草；苜蓿|A small plant with usually three leaves on each stem, often considered to be lucky.  It is also grown as a crop to feed animals.|I found a four-leaf clover! 我找到了一株四叶草！| synonym: trefoil, shamrock | antonym: (No direct antonym for a plant, could consider "weed" if context is unwanted plants) | noun


clover:/ˈkləʊvər/ (UK) /ˈkloʊvər/ (US)| adj. |(rare) 三叶草形状的；苜蓿味道的|Shaped like or tasting of clover.|The biscuits had a subtle clover flavor. 这些饼干有一种淡淡的苜蓿味。 | synonym: trefoil-shaped | antonym: (No direct antonym) | adjective
