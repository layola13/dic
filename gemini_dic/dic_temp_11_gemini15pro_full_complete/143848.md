outplanned:/ˌaʊtˈplænd/
verb
制定比...更周密的计划; 在计划方面胜过
to plan more effectively than someone else
例句：They were completely outplanned by the opposition. 他们在计划方面完全被对手胜过。
例句：The company outplanned its competitors and gained a significant market share.  这家公司在计划方面胜过竞争对手，并获得了显著的市场份额。

近义词：outsmart, outmaneuver, outstrategize
反义词：be outplanned, be outsmarted
