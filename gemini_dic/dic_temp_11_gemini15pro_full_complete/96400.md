indispensableness:/ˌɪndɪˈspɛnsəbəlnəs/
n.|不可或缺性；必要性|The indispensableness of water to life.  水对生命的重要性。|English Explanation: The quality or state of being indispensable; absolutely necessary; essential.|例句：The indispensableness of clean water for human survival is undeniable.  清洁水对于人类生存的不可或缺性是不可否认的。

例句：He stressed the indispensableness of regular exercise for maintaining good health. 他强调了规律运动对于保持健康的必要性。

近义词：essentiality, necessity, vitalness
反义词：dispensability, unimportance
词性：noun
