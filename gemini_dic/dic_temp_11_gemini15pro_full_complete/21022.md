blether:/ˈblɛðər/ (UK), /ˈblɛðər/ (US)| v. n.|喋喋不休；胡说八道；废话|To talk continuously and usually aimlessly, about unimportant things; foolish or nonsensical talk.|例句：He blethered on for ages about nothing in particular. 他漫无边际地胡扯了半天。|近义词：chatter, prattle, ramble, babble|反义词：be quiet, listen|词性：动词，名词

词源：
From Scots *blether, of uncertain origin. Perhaps related to Old Norse *blaðra (“to talk nonsense”).

注意事项:  blether主要用于苏格兰和爱尔兰英语，在其他英语变体中不太常见。
