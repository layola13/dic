immethodical:/ˌɪmɪˈθɒdɪkəl/ (US) /ˌɪmɪˈθɑːdɪkəl/| adj.|无条理的，无方法的|Lacking method or order; unsystematic.|Her desk was always cluttered with papers and books, a testament to her immethodical work habits. 她的办公桌上总是堆满了文件和书籍，这证明了她没有条理的工作习惯。|He has an immethodical approach to studying, jumping from one subject to another. 他学习方法毫无章法，从一门学科跳到另一门学科。|近义词:disorganized, unsystematic, haphazard, chaotic|反义词:methodical, organized, systematic, orderly|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + methodical (systematic, orderly).  Methodical comes from the Greek "methodos" (method, way).
