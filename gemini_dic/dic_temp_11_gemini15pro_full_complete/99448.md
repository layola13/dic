interestingly:/ˈɪntrəstɪŋli/ | adv. |有趣地，引人入胜地 | In a manner that arouses curiosity or interest. | Interestingly, the results contradicted the hypothesis.  有趣的是，结果与假设相矛盾。| Interestingly, she decided to quit her job and travel the world. 有趣的是，她决定辞掉工作去环游世界。 | 近义词:surprisingly, remarkably, curiously | 反义词:dully, boringly, tediously | 词性:副词

Etymology:
From interesting + -ly.  "Interesting" comes from the Old French "interessant" (present participle of "interesser" meaning "to concern, interest"), which in turn derives from the Latin "interesse" meaning "to be between, to concern, to make a difference."
