invasion: /ɪnˈveɪʒən/ | n. |入侵，侵略，侵犯|An act of invading, especially the entrance of a hostile army into a country. |例句：The invasion of privacy is a serious crime. 侵犯隐私是一项严重的罪行。

例句：The country was prepared for the invasion of enemy forces.  这个国家已经准备好应对敌军的入侵。

近义词：attack, assault, raid, incursion, encroachment |反义词: retreat, withdrawal |词性: noun
