magpie:/ˈmæɡpaɪ/| n.|喜鹊；爱收藏东西的人|A magpie is a type of bird known for its black and white plumage and its habit of collecting shiny objects.  A magpie can also refer to a person who collects things, especially things of little value.|例句：The magpie flew off with a shiny coin in its beak.  喜鹊叼着一枚闪亮的硬币飞走了。

例句：She's a real magpie, always collecting trinkets and souvenirs. 她真是个收藏癖，总是收集小饰品和纪念品。|近义词：collector, hoarder (for a person)|反义词: (No direct antonym for the bird) minimalist (for a person)|词性:noun


magpie:/ˈmæɡpaɪ/| adj.|爱收藏东西的|Describing someone who collects things, especially things of little value.|例句：She has magpie tendencies, her room is full of little ornaments. 她有收藏癖，她的房间里摆满了小装饰品。

例句：His magpie-like habits led him to amass a huge collection of stamps.  他像喜鹊一样的习惯使他积攒了大量的邮票。|近义词： acquisitive, hoarding|反义词: minimalist|词性:adjective
