alexia:/əˈlɛksiə/| n. |失读症|Inability to read, usually caused by brain damage.  The loss of the ability to read, typically as the result of brain damage.|例句：Alexia can be a devastating consequence of a stroke. 失读症可能是中风的严重后果。

例句：After the accident, he suffered from alexia and could no longer read. 事故发生后，他患上了失读症，再也无法阅读了。|近义词：word blindness, visual aphasia|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
