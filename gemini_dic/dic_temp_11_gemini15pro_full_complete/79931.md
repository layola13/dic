gnomologic:/ˌnoʊməˈlɒdʒɪk/ | adj. | 格言学的，箴言学的 | Relating to gnomology, the study of gnomes or maxims. | Gnomologic literature often explores the wisdom of ancient cultures.  格言学文献经常探索古代文化的智慧。| 近义词: aphoristic, proverbial | 反义词:  None | 词性: adjective

gnomology:/noʊˈmɒlədʒi/ | n. | 格言学，箴言学 | The study or collection of gnomes or maxims. |  He was fascinated by gnomology and spent years collecting proverbs from around the world. 他对格言学很着迷，花了很多年时间从世界各地收集谚语。| 近义词: paremiology | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
