impatientnesses:/ɪmˈpeɪʃənsnəsɪz/
|n.|急躁，不耐烦|The state of being impatient, characterized by a lack of patience or tolerance for delays or difficulties.  It refers to multiple instances or manifestations of impatience.|
|例句：His impatientnesses during the meeting were evident in his constant fidgeting.  他在会议期间的急躁不安在他不停地坐立不安中表现得很明显。|
|例句：Her frequent impatientnesses made it difficult to work with her. 她频繁的急躁情绪让人很难和她一起工作。|
|近义词：restlessness, irritability, edginess, hastiness|
|反义词：patience, tolerance, forbearance, composure|
|词性：名词|
