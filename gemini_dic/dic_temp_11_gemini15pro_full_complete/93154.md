igneous:/ˈɪɡniəs/| adj.|火成的，岩浆的|Formed from or by solidification of molten rock.|例句：Granite is an igneous rock. 花岗岩是一种火成岩。

例句：The volcano spewed forth igneous rock. 火山喷出了火成岩。|近义词：volcanic, magmatic|反义词：sedimentary, metamorphic|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *igneus*, from *ignis* "fire".
