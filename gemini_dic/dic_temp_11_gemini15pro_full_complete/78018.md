gentling:/ˈdʒɛntəlɪŋ/
verb (present participle of gentle)
使温顺；使驯服；轻轻地抚摸
The process of making something or someone gentle; taming or calming.
She spent hours gentling the frightened horse. 她花了几个小时使受惊的马平静下来。

近义词: taming, calming, soothing, pacifying, mollifying
反义词: agitating, provoking, riling, exciting, inflaming
词性: verb (present participle)
