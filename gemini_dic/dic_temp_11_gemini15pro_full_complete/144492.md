outthwack: /ˌaʊtˈθwæk/
verb
痛击，狠狠地打
to hit or strike something with great force.
例句：He outthwacked his opponent in the jousting tournament. 他在马上长矛比武中狠狠地击败了对手。

例句：The hero outthwacked the villain with a mighty blow. 英雄以强力一击痛击了恶棍。

近义词：bash, clobber, wallop, thrash, pummel
反义词：caress, stroke

词性: v.
