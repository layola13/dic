foundland:/ˈfaʊndlənd/ | n. | 纽芬兰（加拿大东部一省）; 纽芬兰犬 |A large breed of dog originally from the island of Newfoundland, known for its size, strength, and webbed feet, often used as a working dog, especially for water rescue. Also, the island of Newfoundland, a province of Canada. |例句：The Newfoundland is a gentle giant. 纽芬兰犬是性情温顺的大块头。

例句：We visited Newfoundland on our trip to Canada. 我们在加拿大之旅中游览了纽芬兰。|近义词：Labrador Retriever (for the dog breed, in terms of water rescue ability) |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
