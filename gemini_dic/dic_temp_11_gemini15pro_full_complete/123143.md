monitorially:/ˌmɒnɪˈtɔːriəli/ | adv. | 以监督的方式，通过监督 |  In a monitorial manner; by way of admonition or caution. | The teacher addressed the class monitorially, reminding them of the upcoming exam. 老师以监督的口吻对全班讲话，提醒他们即将到来的考试。| 近义词: supervisingly, observantly | 反义词: negligently, carelessly | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From "monitor" + "-ial" + "-ly".  "Monitor" comes from Latin "monēre" (to warn, advise).
