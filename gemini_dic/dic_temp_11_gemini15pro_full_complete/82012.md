gripingness:/ˈɡraɪpɪŋnəs/| n.|抱怨，牢骚，不满|The state of being full of complaints; a tendency to gripe.  A persistent feeling of discontent or resentment.|His constant gripingness about the weather was becoming tiresome. 他不断抱怨天气，让人厌烦。|Near synonyms:  complaining, grumbling, whining, carping, bellyaching|Antonyms:  contentment, satisfaction, cheerfulness|noun

gripingness:/ˈɡraɪpɪŋnəs/| n.|抱怨，牢骚，不满|The tendency to complain or express dissatisfaction; the quality of being full of complaints. |Her gripingness made it difficult to enjoy the trip. 她的抱怨让这次旅行难以尽兴。|Near synonyms:  querulousness,  fussiness,  petulance |Antonyms:  agreeableness,  pleasantness,  good humor|noun
