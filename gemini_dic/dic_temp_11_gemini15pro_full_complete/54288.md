disgust:/dɪsˈɡʌst/ | n. v. |厌恶，反感；使厌恶，使反感 |A feeling of strong aversion or disapproval aroused by something unpleasant or offensive. |The smell of rotten eggs filled me with disgust.  腐鸡蛋的气味让我感到恶心。|近义词：revulsion, aversion, abhorrence, loathing|反义词：admiration, pleasure, delight|词性:名词,动词
disgust:/dɪsˈɡʌst/ | v. |使厌恶，使反感|To cause someone to feel strong aversion or disapproval.|His rude behavior disgusted everyone present. 他的粗鲁行为让所有在场的人都感到厌恶。|近义词：repel, sicken, nauseate|反义词：please, delight, attract|词性:动词
