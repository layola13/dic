dapperness:/ˈdæpərnəs/
n.
整洁；衣着讲究；潇洒
The quality of being dapper; neat and stylish appearance.
His dapperness was evident in his perfectly tailored suit. 他的潇洒体现在他那套剪裁完美的西装上。
近义词:smartness, stylishness, elegance, chic, spruceness
反义词:scruffiness, sloppiness, untidiness
词性:noun

词源:From dapper + -ness.  Dapper itself comes from Dutch dapper, meaning "brave, valiant."  Over time, the meaning shifted to describe someone elegant and well-dressed.
