glomuliferous:/ɡləˈmjuːlɪfərəs/
adjective
多肾小球的；富含肾小球的
Bearing or producing glomeruli.  A glomeruliferous plant has many clusters of sporangia.
The botanist identified a glomuliferous fern in the rainforest.  这个植物学家在雨林中发现了一种多肾小球的蕨类植物。
The kidney tissue sample was glomuliferous, indicating healthy function.  肾脏组织样本富含肾小球，表明功能健康。
近义词: glomerular
反义词: aglomerular
