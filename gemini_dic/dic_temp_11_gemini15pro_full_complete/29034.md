cartoonishness:/kɑːrˈtuːnɪʃnəs/
noun
卡通化；卡通式；滑稽夸张
The quality of resembling or suggesting a cartoon, especially in being simplified, exaggerated, or unrealistic.
Her performance had a certain cartoonishness that made it both funny and endearing. 她的表演带有一种卡通化的感觉，既有趣又可爱。
The cartoonishness of the villain's appearance made it hard to take him seriously.  反派卡通化的外表让人很难认真对待他。
近义词：exaggeration, caricature, absurdity, ridiculousness
反义词：realism, naturalism, seriousness
词性: n.
