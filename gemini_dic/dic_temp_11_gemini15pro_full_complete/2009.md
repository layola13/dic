actualizes:/ˈæktʃuəlaɪzɪz/
verb
实现，实行
to make something real or happen
The company finally actualized its plans to expand overseas. 该公司最终实现了其海外扩张计划。

actualizes:/ˈæktʃuəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使…潜能得以发挥，激发
to make something such as a feeling, idea, or belief become real
She finally actualized her potential and became a successful artist. 她最终发挥了自己的潜能，成为了一名成功的艺术家。

近义词:realize, achieve, fulfill, complete
反义词:neglect, ignore, abandon

词性:v.
