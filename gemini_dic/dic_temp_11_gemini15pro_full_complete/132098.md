noncretaceous:/ˌnɒnkrɪˈteɪʃəs/
adjective
非白垩纪的
Not of or belonging to the Cretaceous period or system.
The noncretaceous fossils were found in Jurassic strata.  非白垩纪的化石是在侏罗纪地层中发现的。
These rocks are of noncretaceous origin. 这些岩石并非起源于白垩纪。
synonym: pre-Cretaceous, post-Cretaceous (depending on the context)
antonym: Cretaceous
