ooidal:/oʊˈɔɪdəl/
adjective
卵形的；卵石状的
Resembling or consisting of ooids.  Composed of small, round grains resembling fish eggs.
The ooidal limestone formed in shallow, warm waters. 这种卵形石灰岩形成于温暖的浅水区。
The beach was covered in ooidal sand. 海滩上覆盖着卵石状的沙子。
Synonyms: granular, spheroidal, globular
Antonyms: amorphous, irregular
