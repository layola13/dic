kindergartens:/ˈkɪndərˌɡɑːrtənz/
n.
幼儿园（复数）
preschools for children, typically between the ages of four and six.
My children attend different kindergartens. 我的孩子们上不同的幼儿园。
The kindergartens in this area are all very good.  这个地区的幼儿园都很好。
近义词：preschools, nursery schools
反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑 elementary schools 小学)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From German Kindergarten, literally "children's garden."  Kind (child) + Garten (garden).
