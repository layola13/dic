half-vellums:/ˌhɑːfˈvɛləmz/
n.
半羊皮纸书籍
Books bound in half-vellum, meaning the spine and corners are covered in vellum while the sides are covered in a different material, often cloth or paper.
Many antique books were bound in half-vellums to provide durability and an elegant appearance.
half-bound books, quarter-bound books
full-vellum books
noun
