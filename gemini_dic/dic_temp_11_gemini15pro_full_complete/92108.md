hyperromantically:/ˌhaɪpərroʊˈmæntɪkli/
adverb|过度浪漫地|Excessively or exaggeratedly romantic; in a way that is beyond the bounds of typical romance.|She decorated the room hyperromantically with candles, flowers, and heart-shaped balloons. 她用蜡烛、鲜花和心形气球把房间装饰得过于浪漫。|近义词：overly romantic, excessively romantic, sentimentally, mawkishly|反义词：pragmatically, realistically, unromantically|词性: 副词

Etymology:
"Hyper-" (Greek origin) meaning "over, excessive" + "romantic" (from Old French "romanz" meaning "verse narrative") + "-ally" (adverbial suffix).  The prefix "hyper-" intensifies the meaning of "romantic," indicating an extreme or exaggerated degree of romanticism.
