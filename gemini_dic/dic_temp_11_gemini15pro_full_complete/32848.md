ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *coleriknes*, from Middle French *colerique* + *-nesse*. Ultimately from Late Latin *cholericus* "bilious, irascible," from Greek *kholerikós* (χολεϱικός) "bilious, choleric," from *kholḗ* (χολή) "bile, gall" + *-ikos* (-ικός).


注意事项:
