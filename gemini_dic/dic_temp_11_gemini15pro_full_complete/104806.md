kean:/kiːn/
adjective
渴望的; 热切的; 热心的
eagerly wanting (something); enthusiastic
例句：He was kean to get started. 他渴望开始。
例句：She's kean on playing tennis. 她热衷于打网球。
近义词：eager, enthusiastic, avid, fervent
反义词：apathetic, indifferent, reluctant
词性: adjective

kean:/kiːn/
verb
(苏格兰及爱尔兰英语) 恸哭；哀号
(Scottish and Irish English) to wail or lament loudly
例句：They keened over the loss of their leader.  他们为失去领袖而恸哭。
例句：The women keened their grief.  妇女们哀号着她们的悲伤。
近义词：wail, lament, mourn
反义词：rejoice, celebrate
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Irish *caoinim (“I weep, lament”), related to caointeach (“lamenting”). Cognate with Scottish Gaelic caoidh (“weep”).  The adjectival sense "eager, sharp" is likely a separate development from a homonymous adjective in Scots, of uncertain origin.
