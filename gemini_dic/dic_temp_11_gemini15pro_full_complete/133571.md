nonflagrancy:/nɒnˈfleɪɡrənsi/
|n.| 非显著性；非公然性；非明目张胆|The nonflagrancy of the offense led to a reduced sentence.  罪行的非公然性导致了减刑。|English Explanation: The state or quality of not being flagrant; not conspicuously or obviously offensive, bad, or reprehensible.|例句：The judge cited the nonflagrancy of the violation as a mitigating factor. 法官引用了违规的非公然性作为减轻处罚的因素。

例句：Because of the nonflagrancy of the error, the penalty was small. 由于错误的非显著性，惩罚很轻。|近义词：unobtrusiveness, subtlety, discretion|反义词：flagrancy, blatantness, obviousness|词性: noun


词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non.
flagrancy (noun): From Latin flagrantia "a burning, a being ablaze," from flagrantem (nominative flagrans), present participle of flagrare "to burn, blaze," figuratively "to be inflamed (with love, etc.)."
