noncontributor:/ˌnɑːn kənˈtrɪbjətər/ |/ˌnɒn kənˈtrɪbjʊtə(r)/| n. |不贡献者，没有贡献的人|A person who does not contribute, especially to a common fund or effort.|He was labeled a noncontributor by his team leader due to his lack of participation in the project.| 他因缺乏参与项目而被团队领导标记为不贡献者。| 近义词: idler, freeloader, slacker | 反义词: contributor, donor, supporter | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non "not"
contributor: from contribute + -or. Contribute from Latin *contribuere "to grant, bestow, contribute," from com- "together" + tribuere "to assign, bestow."
