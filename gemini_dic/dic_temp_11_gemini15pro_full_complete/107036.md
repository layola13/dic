lactobacilli:/ˌlæktəʊbəˈsɪlaɪ/
n.
乳酸杆菌
a type of bacteria that produces lactic acid. These bacteria are friendly and often found in yogurt and other fermented foods, and are believed to be beneficial for digestive health.
Lactobacilli are commonly found in yogurt. 乳酸杆菌常见于酸奶中。

Lactobacilli are beneficial bacteria. 乳酸杆菌是有益菌。
synonym:probiotics
antonym:pathogens
noun
