homologous:/həˈmɒləɡəs/ /həˈmɑːləɡəs/| adj.|同源的|Having the same relation, relative position, or structure, in particular.|例句：The wings of a bat and the arms of a human are homologous structures. 蝙蝠的翅膀和人类的手臂是同源结构。|例句：The homologous chromosomes pair up during meiosis. 同源染色体在减数分裂过程中配对。|近义词：analogous, corresponding, similar|反义词：heterologous, dissimilar|词性:形容词
词源: From Greek 'homologos' (agreeing, corresponding), from 'homos' (same) + 'logos' (ratio, proportion, relation, speech, word).
