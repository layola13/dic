creole: /ˈkriːoʊl/ | n. adj. |克里奥尔语；克里奥尔人（的）；混合的| A language that has developed from a mixture of different languages, especially a European language and the language of a non-European people.  Also, relating to creole people or their culture. |例句：Creole is spoken in many parts of the Caribbean.  克里奥尔语在加勒比海的许多地区使用。

ETYMOLOGY->
