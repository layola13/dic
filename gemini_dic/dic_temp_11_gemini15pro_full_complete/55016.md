disputer:/dɪˈspjuːtər/| n. | 争论者，辩论者 | A person who argues or debates a point. |  He was a skilled disputer, always ready with a counter-argument. 他是一个熟练的辩论者，总是准备好反驳的论点。

| 近义词：arguer, debater, contestant, opponent | 反义词: proponent, supporter, advocate | 词性: noun


disputed:/dɪˈspjuːtɪd/| adj. | 有争议的， disputed | Something that is subject to disagreement or debate. | The disputed territory has been a source of conflict for decades. 这片有争议的领土几十年来一直是冲突的根源。

| 近义词：controversial, debatable, contested | 反义词: undisputed, acknowledged, accepted | 词性: adjective


dispute:/dɪˈspjuːt/| n. | 争论，辩论 | A disagreement or argument. |  There was a dispute over the ownership of the land.  这块土地的所有权存在争议。
| vt. | 争论，辩论 | To argue about something. |  The two countries continue to dispute the border. 这两个国家继续就边界问题争论。
| 近义词：argument, debate, quarrel, controversy | 反义词: agreement, harmony, concord | 词性: noun, verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *disputer (Modern French disputer), from Latin disputare "discuss, examine, argue," from dis- "apart" + putare "to think, reckon, believe."
