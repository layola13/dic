beneficent:/bəˈnɛfɪs(ə)nt/
adjective
仁慈的；慈善的；有益的
Doing or producing good, especially acts of kindness or charity.
The beneficent ruler was loved by all his people.  仁慈的统治者受到所有人民的爱戴。
The charity received a beneficent donation.  慈善机构收到了一笔慷慨的捐赠。
Synonyms: kind, benevolent, generous, charitable, altruistic, philanthropic
Antonyms: malevolent, cruel, unkind, selfish, harmful
词性: 形容词
