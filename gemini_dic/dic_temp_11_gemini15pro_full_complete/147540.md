overpayment:/ˌoʊvərˈpeɪmənt/ | n. | 多付，过度支付 | The act of paying more than is due or required.  |  I received an overpayment from my employer and had to return the extra money. 我收到了雇主多付的工资，不得不把多余的钱退回去。 | Example: There was an overpayment on my account. 我的账户有多付的款项。 | Synonyms: excess payment, surplus payment | Antonyms: underpayment, shortfall | noun


overpay:/ˌoʊvərˈpeɪ/ | v. | 多付，支付过多 | To pay more than is due or required. | I think I overpaid for that car. 我觉得我那辆车买贵了。 | Example:  Don't overpay for groceries. 不要为杂货多付钱。 | Synonyms: overspend | Antonyms: underpay | verb
