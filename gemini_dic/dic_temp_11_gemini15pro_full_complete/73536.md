foresightless:/ˈfɔːrsaɪtləs/| adj.|缺乏远见的，目光短浅的|Lacking foresight; unable to anticipate future needs or consequences.|The foresightless decision to cut funding for education will have long-term negative consequences.  缺乏远见的削减教育经费的决定将产生长期的负面后果。|Synonyms: shortsighted, improvident, myopic, imprudent|Antonyms: farsighted, provident, prescient, prudent|adjective
