nondogmatic: /ˌnɒn.dɒɡˈmæt.ɪk/ /ˌnɑːn.dɑːɡˈmæt.ɪk/ | adj. | 非教条的；不武断的 | Not adhering rigidly to a set of beliefs or doctrines; open-minded and tolerant of different views. |  She has a nondogmatic approach to teaching. 她有一种非教条式的教学方法。 | The scientist presented his findings in a nondogmatic way, open to discussion and alternative interpretations.  这位科学家以非教条的方式陈述了他的发现，乐于讨论和接受不同的解释。 |  近义词: open-minded, tolerant, flexible, liberal, undogmatic | 反义词: dogmatic, doctrinaire, rigid, inflexible, authoritarian | 词性: adjective

nondogmatically: /ˌnɒn.dɒɡˈmæt.ɪ.kə.li/ /ˌnɑːn.dɑːɡˈmæt.ɪ.k.li/ | adv. | 非教条地；不武断地 | In a way that is not dogmatic; with an open mind and tolerance for different views. | She approached the issue nondogmatically, considering all perspectives. 她以非教条的方式处理这个问题，考虑了所有的观点。 | He nondogmatically presented his argument, acknowledging the validity of opposing viewpoints. 他以非教条的方式提出了他的论点，承认了相反观点的有效性。 | 近义词: open-mindedly, tolerantly, flexibly | 反义词: dogmatically, doctrinarily, rigidly | 词性: adverb
