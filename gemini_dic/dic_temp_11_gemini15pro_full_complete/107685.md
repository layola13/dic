lankier: /ˈlæŋkɪər/ | adj. | 更瘦长的，更瘦高的 | Comparative form of lanky: more lanky.  Someone who is lankier is tall and thin, and often moves awkwardly. | He was much lankier than I remembered. 他比我记忆中瘦长得多。|  Lankier and more awkward than ever, he tripped over the rug. 他比以往任何时候都更加瘦长笨拙，被地毯绊倒了。| 近义词: taller, thinner, ganglier, spindlier | 反义词: shorter, stockier, stouter | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From lanky + -er (comparative suffix).  Lanky itself likely originates from dialectal lank, meaning "slender," possibly related to Old Norse hlank ("tottering").
