ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hærfest (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *harbistaz, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱer-p- (“to pluck, harvest”). Cognate with Old Frisian hervest, Middle Dutch herfst (Dutch herfst), Old High German herbist (German Herbst), Old Norse haust.
