acculturation: /əˌkʌltʃəˈreɪʃən/
n.
文化适应; 同化
The process of cultural and psychological change that results following meeting between cultures.  This can result in changes to language, customs, values and world view.
New immigrants often struggle with acculturation. 新移民通常在文化适应方面很吃力。

Acculturation can be a difficult process for immigrants.  对于移民来说，文化适应可能是一个艰难的过程。


近义词: assimilation, adaptation, integration
反义词: isolation, segregation
词性: noun

Etymology:  From Late Latin *acculturatio, from Latin acculturare ("to cultivate, educate"), from ad- ("to") + cultura ("culture").
