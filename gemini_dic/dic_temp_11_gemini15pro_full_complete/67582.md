extrasensory:/ˌekstrəˈsɛnsəri/| adj.|超感官的，超感觉的|Beyond the normal senses; relating to or denoting perception apparently through other than the known senses.|She claimed to have extrasensory perception and could predict the future. 她声称拥有超感官知觉，可以预测未来。|例句：He believed he had extrasensory powers. 他相信自己有超感官能力。  Some people believe animals have extrasensory perception.  有些人相信动物有超感官知觉。|近义词：psychic, supernatural, clairvoyant|反义词：normal, ordinary|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From extra- + sensory. Extra- comes from Latin, meaning "outside of, beyond." Sensory comes from the Latin word "sensus," meaning "sense" or "feeling."
