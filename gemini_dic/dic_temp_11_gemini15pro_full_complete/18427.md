belts:/ˈbelts/| n.|腰带，皮带；传送带；地带|A strip of leather, cloth, or other material worn around the waist, or a similar band used to transmit motion or carry objects. |He tightened his belt.  他勒紧了腰带。|The conveyor belt carries the products to the packaging area.  传送带将产品运送到包装区域。|Synonyms: straps, bands, sashes|Antonyms: (For clothing belts) looseness|noun
