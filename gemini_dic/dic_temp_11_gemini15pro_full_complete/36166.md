coffeeroom:/ˈkɒfiˌruːm/ (US) /ˈkɒfiˌrʊm/ (UK)| n. |咖啡室；茶点室；休息室|A room in a hotel, office, etc., where coffee, tea, and other drinks are available, often with light snacks.|例句：The employees gather in the coffeeroom for their breaks. 员工们在休息室聚集休息。

例句：The hotel's coffeeroom serves complimentary tea and biscuits in the afternoon.  这家酒店的咖啡室下午提供免费的茶和饼干。|近义词：breakroom, tearoom, lounge, common room|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) office, workspace|词性:noun
