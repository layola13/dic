individualists:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlɪsts/
n.
个人主义者
People who believe in individualism, prioritizing individual rights, independence, and self-reliance.
Example:  Individualists often prefer to work alone rather than in teams.  个人主义者通常喜欢独自工作而不是团队合作。

Example:  Many entrepreneurs are individualists, driven by their own vision and goals. 许多企业家都是个人主义者，受自身愿景和目标的驱动。

近义词:nonconformists, mavericks, free spirits
反义词:conformists, collectivists
词性:noun
