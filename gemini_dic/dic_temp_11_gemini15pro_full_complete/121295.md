misfire:/ˌmɪsˈfaɪər/ | v. | 失灵，失败，达不到预期效果 | To fail to fire or explode properly (of a gun or missile); to fail to produce the intended result. | The engine misfired and the car stalled. 引擎失灵了，车子熄火了。|  The joke misfired and nobody laughed.  这个笑话没响，没人笑。 | synonyms: backfire, fail, flop, fizzle | antonyms: succeed, triumph, work | verb

misfire:/ˌmɪsˈfaɪər/ | n. | 失灵，失败 | An instance of misfiring. |  The misfire caused the car to stall. 失灵导致车子熄火了。 | The general election was a misfire for the smaller parties.  对于小党派来说，大选是一次失败。| synonyms: failure, flop | antonyms: success, triumph | noun
