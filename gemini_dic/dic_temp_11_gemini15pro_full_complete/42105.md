cottage:/ˈkɒtɪdʒ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːtɪdʒ/ (US)| n. |小屋，村舍|A small house, typically one in a rural or semi-rural location.|例句：They lived in a charming cottage by the sea. 他们住在海边一栋迷人的小屋里。

例句：The cottage had a thatched roof and a small garden. 这间小屋有一个茅草屋顶和一个小花园。

近义词：cabin, hut, house, lodge|反义词: mansion, palace |词性: 名词
