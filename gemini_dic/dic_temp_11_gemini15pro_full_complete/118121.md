menopoma:/mɛnəˈpoʊmə/ | n. | 美洲大鲵，地狱弯 salamander | A large aquatic salamander of the family Cryptobranchidae, native to eastern North America. |  The menopoma, also known as the hellbender, can grow to be quite large.  美洲大鲵，也被称为地狱弯，可以长得相当大。

The researchers studied the menopoma's unique respiratory system. 研究人员研究了美洲大鲵独特的呼吸系统。 | 近义词：hellbender, mudpuppy, waterdog | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *mēn* (moon) + *pous* (foot), referring to the animal's flattened, rounded toes. 源自希腊语 *mēn* (月亮) + *pous* (脚)，指的是这种动物扁平的圆形脚趾。
