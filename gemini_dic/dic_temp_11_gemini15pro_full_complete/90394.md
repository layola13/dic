howl:/haʊl/| n. v. |嚎叫，怒吼；呼啸|A long, loud, mournful cry or wailing sound, often associated with wolves or dogs, but also used to describe the sound of the wind, or a person expressing strong emotion such as grief, pain, or anger.|The wolves howled at the moon. 狼对着月亮嚎叫。|近义词：wail, cry, scream, roar, shriek|反义词:whisper, murmur|词性:名词，动词
howl:/haʊl/| v. |嚎叫，怒吼；呼啸|To emit a long, loud, mournful cry or wailing sound.|The wind howled through the trees. 风呼啸着穿过树林。|近义词：wail, cry, scream, roar, shriek|反义词:whisper, murmur|词性:动词
