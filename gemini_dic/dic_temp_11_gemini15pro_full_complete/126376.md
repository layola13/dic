myristin:/ˈmɪrɪstɪn/ | n. | 肉豆蔻酸甘油酯；十四烷酸甘油酯 | A white crystalline fat, the triglyceride of myristic acid, found in many vegetable fats and oils, especially nutmeg butter and coconut oil. | Myristin is a common ingredient in some cosmetics.  肉豆蔻酸甘油酯是一些化妆品的常见成分。| Myristin is found in high concentrations in nutmeg butter. 肉豆蔻酸甘油酯在肉豆蔻油中浓度很高。| 近义词: glyceryl trimyristate | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
