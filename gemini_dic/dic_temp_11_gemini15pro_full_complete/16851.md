basicerite: /ˌbeɪsɪˈsɛraɪt/
n.
基节突; 第二触角节基部
The second segment of the crustacean antenna; the segment to which the antennal scale is attached if present.
The basicerite of the shrimp is enlarged.  虾的基节突增大。
近义词:  basis, coxa (These are more general terms for basal segments)
反义词: None (There's no direct opposite for a specific anatomical part).
词性: noun

Etymology:
From basi- (base) + -cerite (from Greek keras, horn).
