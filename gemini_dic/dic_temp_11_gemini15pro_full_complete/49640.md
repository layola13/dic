demilitarises:/diːˈmɪlɪtəraɪzɪz/
verb
使非军事化;解除武装
To remove military forces, weapons, or fortifications from a place; to remove the emphasis on military matters from something.
The treaty demilitarises the border zone.  该条约使边境地区非军事化。
The government is trying to demilitarise the region. 政府正试图使该区域非军事化。
Synonyms: disarm, neutralize
Antonyms: militarize, arm
词性: 动词

adjective

词性: 形容词


demilitarisation:/diːˌmɪlɪtəraɪˈzeɪʃən/
noun
非军事化;解除武装
The action or process of removing military forces, weapons, or fortifications from a place; the action or process of removing the emphasis on military matters from something.
The demilitarisation of the region was a key part of the peace agreement. 该地区的非军事化是和平协议的关键部分。
The government is committed to the complete demilitarisation of the area. 政府致力于该地区的彻底非军事化。
Synonyms: disarmament, neutralization
Antonyms: militarization, armament
词性: 名词


词源:  de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + military + -ise/-ize (suffix forming verbs)
