luminant:/ˈluːmɪnənt/| adj. n.|发光的，发光体|Emitting light; luminous.  Something that produces light.|The fireflies were the only luminants in the dark forest. 萤火虫是黑暗森林中唯一的光源。|近义词：luminous, bright, shining, radiant|反义词：dark, dim, obscure|词性:adjective, noun
Etymology:  From Late Latin *luminant-, present participle stem of luminare "to light up," from Latin lumen "light."
