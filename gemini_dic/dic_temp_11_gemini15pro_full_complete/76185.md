fussiness:/ˈfʌsinəs/| n.| 过分讲究; 爱挑剔; 难伺候; 小题大做; 吹毛求疵|Excessive concern about minor details; difficult to please; tending to complain about things that are not important.|The child's fussiness about food worried his mother.  这个孩子对食物的挑剔让他的母亲很担心。

The designer's fussiness over the smallest details ensured a high-quality product.  设计师对最小细节的吹毛求疵确保了产品的高质量。|近义词：pickiness, fastidiousness, choosiness, finickiness|反义词：easygoingness, adaptability, flexibility|词性:noun
