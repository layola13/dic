counter-extension:/ˌkaʊntər ɪkˈstenʃən/ | n. | 反向伸展，对抗伸展 | The act of extending a limb or other body part in the opposite direction to another extension, often to provide stability or leverage.  It involves the simultaneous contraction of opposing muscle groups. |  The physical therapist instructed the patient to perform a counter-extension exercise for their back. 物理治疗师指导病人进行背部反向伸展练习。

The counter-extension of the legs helps to stabilize the body during certain exercises.  在某些练习中，腿的反向伸展有助于稳定身体。 | 近义词: counter-traction, opposition | 反义词: flexion | 词性: 名词
