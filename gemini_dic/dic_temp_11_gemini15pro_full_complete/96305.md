indigent:/ˈɪndɪdʒənt/| adj.|贫穷的，贫困的|Poor; needy; lacking the necessities of life.|Many indigent families rely on government assistance. 许多贫困家庭依靠政府援助。|The clinic provides free healthcare to indigent patients.  这家诊所为贫困病人提供免费医疗。|近义词：poor, impoverished, destitute, needy, penniless|反义词：wealthy, rich, affluent, prosperous|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *indigēns*, present participle of *indigēre* "to need, lack, be in want of," from *indu* "in, within" + *egēre* "to need."
