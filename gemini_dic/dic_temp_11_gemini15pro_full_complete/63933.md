epipodium:/ˌɛpɪˈpoʊdiəm/ | n. |外足，上足|The epipodium in mollusks refers to the part of the foot that lies above the metapodium (posterior part of the foot) and is often modified for various functions such as swimming or burrowing. 在软体动物中，外足指的是位于后足（足的后部）上方的足的一部分，通常被修改用于各种功能，例如游泳或挖洞。|The snail extended its epipodium to help it glide over the surface.  蜗牛伸出它的外足以帮助它在表面滑动。|近义词:propodium, foot (in a broader sense)|反义词: N/A| noun


epipodium:/ˌɛpɪˈpoʊdiəm/ | n. | (in plants) the upper portion of a leaf, including the blade and petiole|The epipodium is the main photosynthetic part of the leaf. 上足是叶子的主要光合作用部分。|The epipodium of this plant has a distinctive serrated edge. 这种植物的上足有明显的锯齿状边缘。|近义词: lamina, blade|反义词: N/A| noun
