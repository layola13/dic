cheeriness:/ˈtʃɪriːnɪs/| n.|快乐，高兴，兴高采烈|The quality of being cheerful; cheerfulness.|Her cheeriness was infectious, brightening the room as soon as she entered. 她一进来，房间就亮了起来，她的快乐很有感染力。|近义词：cheerfulness, gaiety, lightheartedness, merriment, joyfulness|反义词：gloom, sadness, dejection, melancholy, glumness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From cheery + -ness.  "Cheery" comes from Middle English *cheri, from Old North French cher, ultimately from Late Latin cara (face). The suffix "-ness" is a native English suffix forming nouns from adjectives, meaning "the state or quality of being."
