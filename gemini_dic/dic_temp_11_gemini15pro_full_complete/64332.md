eradicate:/ɪˈrædɪkeɪt/
verb
根除，消灭
To completely destroy or get rid of something, especially something bad.
Malaria has been eradicated in many countries.  许多国家已经根除了疟疾。

The government is trying to eradicate corruption. 政府正在努力消除腐败。

近义词: eliminate, abolish, exterminate, wipe out
反义词: cultivate, foster, promote, establish
词性: verb
