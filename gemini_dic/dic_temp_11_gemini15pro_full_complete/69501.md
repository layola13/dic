felinenesses:/fɪˈlaɪnɪsnɪsɪz/
|n.|猫的特性，猫科动物的特性|The qualities or characteristics of a feline, such as grace, agility, and independence.|Her movements displayed a remarkable felineness, a combination of grace and predatory stillness. 她展现出一种非凡的猫科动物的特性，优雅与掠食者的静止相结合。|近义词：feline qualities, catlike nature|反义词：canine qualities, doglike nature|词性:名词

词源:
From feline + -ness.  "Feline" comes from the Latin "felinus," meaning "of or pertaining to a cat," ultimately from "feles," meaning "cat."  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
