herb:/ɜːrb/ (US) /hɜːb/ (UK)| n.|药草；香草|A plant whose leaves, seeds, or flowers are used for flavouring food, medicine, or perfume.|She grows herbs in her garden. 她在她花园里种植香草。|Synonyms: spice, plant, botanical|Antonyms: synthetic, artificial|noun

herb:/ɜːrb/ (US) /hɜːb/ (UK)| n.|草本植物|Any seed-bearing plant that does not have a woody stem and dies down to the ground after flowering.|Parsley is an herb. 欧芹是一种草本植物。|Synonyms: herbage, vegetation|Antonyms: tree, shrub|noun
