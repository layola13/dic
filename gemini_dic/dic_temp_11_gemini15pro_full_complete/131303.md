noncertainty:/ˌnɒnˈsɜːrtənti/ | n. |不确定性，非确定性|The state of not being certain; lack of certainty.  It acknowledges the possibility of multiple outcomes or the limitations of knowledge in predicting a specific outcome.|Due to the noncertainty of the economic forecast, the company decided to postpone major investments. 由于经济预测的不确定性，该公司决定推迟重大投资。|Synonyms: uncertainty, ambiguity, doubt, vagueness|Antonyms: certainty, assurance, conviction|noun
