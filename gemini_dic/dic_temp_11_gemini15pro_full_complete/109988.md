libiatan: /ˌlɪbiˈɑːtən/ | n. | 利比亚坦; 巨兽; 邪恶势力| A sea monster mentioned in the Bible, often symbolizing chaos or evil. It is also used metaphorically to represent any large and powerful organization or force, especially one perceived as oppressive.  |例句：The corporation was a veritable libiatan, crushing smaller businesses in its path.  这家公司简直就是一个利维坦，摧毁了它前进道路上的小企业。

例句：The libiatan of bureaucracy can be frustrating to navigate. 官僚主义的巨兽让人难以驾驭。|近义词：monster, behemoth, leviathan |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
