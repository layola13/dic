margarodite:/ˌmɑːrɡəˈroʊdaɪt/ | n. |珍珠云母，水云母|A variety of muscovite, a hydrated phyllosilicate mineral of aluminum and potassium. It is characterized by its pearly luster and is often found in granite pegmatites.|Margarodite is a common mineral in some metamorphic rocks. 珍珠云母在一些变质岩中很常见。| The margarodite in this rock glitters beautifully in the sunlight.  这块岩石中的珍珠云母在阳光下闪闪发光。| 近义词: muscovite, mica | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific mineral) | 词性: noun
