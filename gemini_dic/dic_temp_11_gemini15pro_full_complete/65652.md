evaporation:/ɪˌvæpəˈreɪʃn/
n.|蒸发；汽化|The process by which a liquid changes into a gas or vapor. |The evaporation of water from the lake cools the surrounding air.  湖水蒸发使周围的空气冷却。|近义词：vaporization, dehydration|反义词：condensation, precipitation
词性: 名词
