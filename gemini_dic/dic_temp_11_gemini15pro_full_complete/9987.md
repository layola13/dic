aphorists:/ˈæfərɪsts/
n.
格言家；警句作者
a person who writes or speaks aphorisms.
The book is a collection of wisdom from various aphorists throughout history. 这本书收集了历史上各时期格言家的智慧。

近义词:maxim writers,  gnomic poets,  wise men, sages
反义词:prolix writers, verbose speakers
