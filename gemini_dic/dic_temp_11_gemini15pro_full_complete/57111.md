dour: /ˈdaʊər/ (US), /ˈdʊə(r)/ (UK) | adj. | 闷闷不乐的，阴沉的 | relentlessly severe, stern, or gloomy in manner or appearance. |  He has a dour personality. 他性格阴沉。|例句：The dour expression on his face made me uncomfortable. 他脸上阴沉的表情让我很不舒服。 | 近义词：sullen, gloomy, grim, stern | 反义词：cheerful, bright, happy | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Scots *dour, from Middle English *dour, from Old French *dur (“hard”), from Latin *dūrus (“hard”).  The adverb "dourly" is formed by adding the suffix "-ly" to the adjective "dour".
