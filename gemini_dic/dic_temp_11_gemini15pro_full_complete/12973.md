asthmatic: /æzˈmætɪk/ | adj. n. | 哮喘的；患哮喘的人 |  Suffering from or relating to asthma, a respiratory condition characterized by difficulty breathing, wheezing, coughing, and tightness in the chest. |  例句：He carries an inhaler because he's asthmatic.  他随身带着吸入器，因为他有哮喘。| 近义词：wheezy, breathless | 反义词: healthy | 词性: 形容词

asthmatic: /æzˈmætɪk/ | n. | 哮喘患者 | A person who suffers from asthma. | 例句： Many asthmatics find that their symptoms worsen in cold weather. 许多哮喘患者发现他们的症状在寒冷天气会加重。 | 近义词: sufferer | 反义词: (None -  Difficult to have a direct antonym for a person with a medical condition) | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Late Latin *asthmaticus, from Greek *asthmatikós, from asthma, meaning "panting, short-drawn breath."
