corticated:/ˈkɔːrtɪkeɪtɪd/| adj.|有皮层的，覆盖着的|Having or resembling a cortex or bark; covered with bark or a similar outer layer. |The corticated stems of the plant were rough to the touch. 这种植物的茎皮很粗糙。|近义词：barked, crusted, covered|反义词：decorticated, peeled|词性:adjective
词源:From Latin *corticātus past participle of corticāre to cover with bark, from cortex, cortic- bark.
