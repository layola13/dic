dannebrog:/ˈdænəˌbrɒɡ/ | n. |丹麦国旗|The national flag of Denmark, a white Scandinavian cross on a red background. It is one of the oldest flags in the world. |The Dannebrog flew proudly over the embassy. 丹麦国旗在使馆上空骄傲地飘扬。|Synonym: Danish flag |Antonym: N/A |noun
