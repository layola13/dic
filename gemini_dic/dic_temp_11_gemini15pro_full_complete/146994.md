词源:  "Overkill" originated during the Cold War, referring to the capacity to destroy a target many times over with nuclear weapons.  It later evolved to describe any excessive action or use of force beyond what is necessary.  “过度杀伤”一词起源于冷战时期，指的是用核武器多次摧毁目标的能力。后来演变为描述任何超过必要的过度行动或武力使用。
