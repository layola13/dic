noncompulsively:/ˌnɒnkəmˈpʌlsɪvli/ /ˌnɑːnkəmˈpʌlsɪvli/ | adv. | 非强迫性地，非冲动地 |  In a way that is not compulsive; without irresistible urges. | He checked his email noncompulsively, only a few times a day. 他并非强迫性地查看电子邮件，一天只查看几次。| Synonyms:  voluntarily, freely, willingly | Antonyms: compulsively, obsessively | 词性: 副词

noncompulsively:/ˌnɒnkəmˈpʌlsɪvli/ /ˌnɑːnkəmˈpʌlsɪvli/ | adv. | 非强迫性地，非冲动地 | Not driven by compulsion or obsession; done by choice. |  She ate a single piece of cake noncompulsively, enjoying the taste without feeling the need for more. 她非强迫性地吃了一块蛋糕，享受着味道，而没有感觉需要更多。 | Synonyms: moderately, temperately, restrainedly | Antonyms: compulsively, obsessively | 词性: 副词

词源:  Formed from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") + "compulsive" (meaning "resulting from or relating to an irresistible urge") + the adverbial suffix "-ly".
