invitiate: /ɪnˈvɪʃɪeɪt/
verb
邀请，请求
to invite, request
He was invited to attend the ceremony. 他被邀请参加典礼。
The organization invitiated applications for the position. 该组织征集该职位的申请。
近义词: invite, request, summon
反义词: dismiss, reject, decline
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *invitītāre, frequentative of Latin invitāre (“to invite”).
