congenerous:/kənˈdʒɛnərəs/ | adj. |同类的，同属的，性情相似的|Of the same kind or nature; kindred. Sharing the same ancestor, origin, or nature. Also, having similar tastes or interests. |例句：The congenerous animals exhibited similar behaviors. 同类的动物表现出相似的行为。|例句：They were congenerous spirits, sharing a passion for art and literature. 他们是性情相投的人，都热爱艺术和文学。|近义词：kindred, similar, like-minded, akin |反义词：dissimilar, different, unrelated |词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin *congener* "of the same kind," from *com-* "with, together" + *genus* "birth, race, kind."
