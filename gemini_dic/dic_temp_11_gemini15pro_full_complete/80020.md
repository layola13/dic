go-the-route:/ɡoʊ ðə ruːt/| idiom | 走全程；采取某种特定的做法 | To follow a particular course of action or process. |  He decided to go the route of starting his own business instead of working for someone else. 他决定自己创业，而不是为别人工作。

| Example: They decided to go the route of mediation instead of going to court. 他们决定采取调解的方式而不是上法庭。 | Synonyms: take the course, follow the path, pursue the approach, opt for, choose the way | Antonyms: deviate, stray, digress | idiom
