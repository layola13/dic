conchifera:/kɒŋˈkɪfərə/
noun
贝壳纲动物
A class of mollusks that includes those forms which have bivalve shells, such as clams, oysters, mussels, and scallops.  They are characterized by having a shell composed of two valves hinged together.
The giant clam is a member of the Conchifera.  巨大的蛤蜊是贝壳纲动物的一员。
bivalves, lamellibranchs
gastropods, cephalopods
n.
