overconsumption:/ˌōvər kənˈsʌmpʃən/
n.
过度消费，过量消耗
The act of consuming something to excess.
Overconsumption of sugar can lead to health problems.  糖的过度消费会导致健康问题。

Overconsumption of resources is a serious problem. 资源的过度消耗是一个严重的问题。

近义词: overindulgence, overuse, excess consumption
反义词: underconsumption, moderation, conservation
词性: noun
