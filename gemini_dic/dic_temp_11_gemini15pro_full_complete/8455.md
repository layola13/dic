antiapoplectic:/ˌæntiˌæpəˈplɛktɪk/
adjective
预防中风的；治疗中风的
Preventing or relieving apoplexy (stroke).
The doctor prescribed an antiapoplectic medication. 医生开了一种预防中风的药。
Near synonyms:  antistroke, neuroprotective
Antonyms:  stroke-inducing
词性: adjective
