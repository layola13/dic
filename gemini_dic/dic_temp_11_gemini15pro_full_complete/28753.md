carneous:/ˈkɑːrniəs/
adjective
肉色的；似肉的；肉质的
Of the color or appearance of flesh; fleshy.
The fungus had a thick, carneous cap. 这种真菌有一个厚厚的肉质菌盖。
The inside of the fruit was carneous and juicy. 水果的内部肉质多汁。

近义词: fleshy, meaty, pulpy
反义词: bony, thin, skeletal
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin carneus ("of flesh"), from caro, carn- ("flesh").
