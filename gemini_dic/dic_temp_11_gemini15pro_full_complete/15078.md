babirusa:/ˌbæbɪˈruːsə/| n.|鹿豚|A wild pig of Indonesia, with upward-curving tusks.|例句：The babirusa is native to Indonesia. 鹿豚原产于印度尼西亚。

例句：The babirusa's tusks can grow so long that they pierce its own skull. 鹿豚的獠牙可以长得非常长，以至于能刺穿自己的头骨。|近义词：pig, hog, boar|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
