belamy:/ˈbeləmi/ | n. | 好朋友，亲密伙伴 | A close friend or comrade.  Originally used among pirates and later among Freemasons. | Example 1:  He greeted his belamy with a hearty handshake. 他热情地和他亲密的朋友握手。

Example 2: The old pirates referred to each other as belamy. 老海盗们互称对方为亲密伙伴。 | Synonym: friend, mate, pal, comrade, companion | Antonym: enemy, foe | noun
