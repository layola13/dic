dominator:/ˈdɒmɪneɪtə(r)/ (UK) /ˈdɑːməneɪtər/ (US)| n.|支配者，统治者，控制者|A person or thing that dominates or controls another.|Rome was the dominator of the ancient world. 罗马是古代世界的统治者.|Synonyms: ruler, controller, master, lord, tyrant|Antonyms: subordinate, subject, follower|noun

dominate:/ˈdɒmɪneɪt/ (UK) /ˈdɑːməneɪt/ (US)| v.|支配，统治，控制|To have control or power over someone or something.|The company dominates the market. 这家公司控制着市场。|Synonyms: control, rule, govern, command|Antonyms: submit, obey, follow|verb
