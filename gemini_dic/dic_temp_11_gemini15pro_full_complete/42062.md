cotemporaneous:/kənˌtem.pəˈreɪ.ni.əs/
adjective
同时代的；同时发生的
existing or occurring in the same period of time
The two events were cotemporaneous, happening within the same week. 这两件事是同时发生的，发生在同一周内。

Cotemporaneous accounts of the battle differed widely. 对这场战斗的同时代记录差异很大。


近义词:concurrent, simultaneous, synchronous
反义词:non-concurrent, asynchronous

词性:adjective
