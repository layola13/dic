canonlike:/ˈkænənˌlaɪk/| adj.|类似经典的，符合传统的，权威性的|Resembling or characteristic of a canon; conforming to accepted standards or rules.|Her prose style is canonlike, reminiscent of the great Victorian novelists.|近义词：canonical, orthodox, traditional, authoritative|反义词：unorthodox, unconventional, heterodox|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From canon + -like.  "Canon" comes from the Greek "kanōn" meaning "rule" or "standard."
