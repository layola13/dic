livelily:/ˈlaɪvlɪli/| adv.|活泼地，充满活力地|In a lively manner; with animation and energy. |She danced livelily across the stage. 她充满活力地在舞台上跳舞。|The band played livelily, getting everyone on their feet. 乐队演奏得非常活泼，让每个人都站起来跳舞了。|近义词：energetically, vibrantly, animatedly|反义词：sluggishly, listlessly, apathetically|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From live + -ly.  "Live" comes from Old English *libban, from Proto-Germanic *libaną, from Proto-Indo-European *leip- (“to stick, adhere, remain”).  The suffix "-ly" comes from Old English -līce, from Proto-Germanic *-līkō, meaning "like, having the form or nature of."
