clause:/klɔːz/ (US) /klɔːz/ (UK)| n. |从句；条款；款项|A clause is a group of words that contains a subject and a predicate and functions as a part of a sentence. |例句：The contract contains a clause about payment.  合同中包含一个关于付款的条款。

例句：She added a clause to the will.  她在遗嘱中添加了一项条款。|近义词：provision, stipulation, section, article|反义词: N/A |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *clause, from Anglo-French clause, Old French clause (“close, end, conclusion of a legal document”), from Late Latin clausa, feminine past participle of Latin claudere (“to close, shut”).
