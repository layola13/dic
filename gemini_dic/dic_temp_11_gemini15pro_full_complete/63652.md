eozoonal:/ˌiːəˈzoʊnəl/ | adj. | 始生代的，伊始生代的 | Relating to or denoting Eozoon, a layered structure formerly thought to be a Precambrian fossil but now considered to be an inorganic mineral deposit. | Eozoonal structures were once thought to be evidence of early life. 伊始生代的结构曾被认为是早期生命的证据。 |  Eozoonal limestone was a subject of much debate in the 19th century. 伊始生代石灰岩是19世纪争论的焦点。 | archaic, primordial | modern, recent | adjective
