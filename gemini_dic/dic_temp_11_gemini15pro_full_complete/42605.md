counterprove:/ˌkaʊntərˈpruːv/
verb
反驳；提出反证
to prove the opposite of something that someone else has said is true
The defense lawyer attempted to counterprove the witness's testimony.  辩护律师试图反驳证人的证词。
Synonyms:  rebut, refute, disprove
Antonyms: prove, confirm, verify
词性: 动词
