occasioning:/əˈkeɪʒənɪŋ/
v.
引起，导致
causing or leading to something
The heavy rain occasioning the flood. 这场大雨导致了洪水。
近义词：causing, leading to, bringing about, producing, generating
反义词：preventing, hindering, stopping
