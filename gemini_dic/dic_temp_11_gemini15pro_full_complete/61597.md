emersed:/ɪˈmɜːrst/ | adj. | 浮出水面的；挺水植物的 | Growing above the surface of the water, especially of the leaves of an aquatic plant. | Example: The emersed leaves of the water lily floated gracefully on the pond's surface.  睡莲浮出水面的叶子优雅地漂浮在池塘表面。| Example:  Scientists studied the adaptation of emersed plants to drier conditions. 科学家研究了挺水植物对较干燥环境的适应。| 近义词：aerial, emergent, surface | 反义词：submerged, immersed | 词性: 形容词

emerge:/ɪˈmɜːrdʒ/ | v. | 浮现；出现 | To come out of something or out from behind something. | Example: The sun emerged from behind the clouds. 太阳从云层后面露出来。 | Example:  New evidence emerged during the investigation. 调查过程中出现了新的证据。| 近义词：appear, arise, come out | 反义词：disappear, vanish | 词性: 动词
