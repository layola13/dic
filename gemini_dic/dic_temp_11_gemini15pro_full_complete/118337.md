mericarp:/ˈmɛrɪˌkɑːrp/ | n. | 分果爿；小坚果 | One of the separate carpels of a schizocarp or cremocarp, as in the carrot or parsley family.  A dry, indehiscent, one-seeded fruit formed from one carpel of a compound ovary. | The mericarps of the plant split apart when ripe. 这种植物的成熟分果爿会裂开。| Synonym: carpel, segment, achene | Antonym: aggregate fruit | noun
