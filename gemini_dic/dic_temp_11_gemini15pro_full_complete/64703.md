escargatoire:/ɛs.kaʁ.ɡa.twaʁ/
n.
snail farm, snailery
A place where snails are raised for food.
Snails are a delicacy in French cuisine, often served in an escargatoire's restaurant.  蜗牛是法国菜中的一道美味佳肴，通常在蜗牛养殖场的餐厅供应。
The escargatoire also sold snail slime for cosmetic purposes.  这家蜗牛养殖场还出售蜗牛粘液用于化妆品用途。
近义词:snailery
反义词: N/A
词性:noun
