eco-geographical:/ˌiːkəʊˌdʒiːəˈɡræfɪk(ə)l/
adjective
生态地理的
Relating to the geographical distribution of organisms in relation to their environment.  The study of the relationships between organisms and their environment, considering geographical factors.
例句：Eco-geographical factors play a significant role in the distribution of species. 生态地理因素在物种分布中起着重要作用。

例句：The eco-geographical isolation of the island led to the evolution of unique species. 岛屿的生态地理隔离导致了独特物种的进化。

近义词：biogeographical, ecological
反义词：(No direct antonym, could consider "a-geographical" but it's not a standard word)
词性：adjective
