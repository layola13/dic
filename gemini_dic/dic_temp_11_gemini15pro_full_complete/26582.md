cabrerite:/kəˈbriːraɪt/| n. | 镁绿泥石 | A magnesium-rich clay mineral, typically green in color, belonging to the chlorite group. It is formed by the alteration of magnesium-rich rocks. | The cabrerite sample displayed a vibrant emerald green hue. 这块镁绿泥石样本呈现出鲜艳的翠绿色。| Cabrerite is often found in association with other secondary minerals. 镁绿泥石通常与其他次生矿物伴生。 | Synonym: chlorite (general term) | Antonym: N/A (lacks a direct opposite) | noun
