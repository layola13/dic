bumpiest:/ˈbʌmpiɪst/| adj.|最颠簸的|Superlative adjective of "bumpy," meaning having many bumps or uneven surfaces.|The bumpiest road I've ever driven on was in the countryside. 我开过最颠簸的路是在乡下。|The bumpiest ride of my life was on that old wooden rollercoaster.  我一生中最颠簸的旅程是在那座古老的木制过山车上。|近义词：roughest, unevenest, most irregular|反义词:smoothest, flattest|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "bump" (Middle English *bompe*, of uncertain origin) + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives meaning 'full of' or 'characterized by') + "-est" (superlative suffix).
