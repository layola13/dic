calenture:/ˈkæləntʃʊr/ /ˈkæləntjʊr/| n. |热病谵妄；思乡病|A fever with delirium; especially a tropical fever characterized by the delusion that the sea is green fields and the patient wants to leap into it. Also, an overwhelming desire.|例句：The sailor, suffering from calenture, imagined the ocean was a meadow and tried to jump overboard.  那名水手患了热病谵妄，把大海想象成一片草地，试图跳下船。

例句：Her calenture for home grew stronger with each passing day. 她对家乡的渴望与日俱增。|近义词：delirium, fever, homesickness, nostalgia|反义词: lucidity, sanity|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *calentura* "fever," from Latin *calentūra* "heat, fever," from *calēre* "to be warm."
