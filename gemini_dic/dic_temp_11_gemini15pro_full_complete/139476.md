obstinateness: /ˈɒbstɪnɪtnəs/ | n. | 固执，顽固 | The quality of being obstinate; stubbornness. | Her obstinateness made negotiation impossible. 她的固执使得谈判无法进行。| 近义词: stubbornness, pertinacity, intransigence, doggedness | 反义词: compliance, acquiescence, docility, amenability | 词性: 名词

obstinate: /ˈɒbstɪnət/ | adj. | 固执的，顽固的 | Stubbornly refusing to change one's opinion or chosen course of action, despite attempts to persuade one to do so. | He remained obstinate in his refusal to apologize. 他仍然固执地拒绝道歉。| 近义词: stubborn, headstrong, willful, unyielding | 反义词: compliant, acquiescent, docile, amenable | 词性: 形容词


词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French obstinat, from Latin obstinatus ‘resolved, steadfast’, past participle of obstinare, from ob- ‘against’ + stare ‘stand’.
