forefoot:/ˈfɔːrfʊt/| n. |前脚掌|The part of the foot that includes the metatarsals and phalanges (toes).|She landed awkwardly on her forefoot and sprained her ankle. 她笨拙地落在前脚掌上，扭伤了脚踝。|The dancer balanced gracefully on the forefoot. 舞蹈演员优雅地用前脚掌保持平衡。|近义词:toe, front part of the foot|反义词:heel|词性:noun
