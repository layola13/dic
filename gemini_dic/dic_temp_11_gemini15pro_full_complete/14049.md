austerer:/əˈstɪrər/ | adj. | 严厉的，简朴的，苦行的 |  Severe or strict in manner, attitude, or appearance. Practicing great self-denial. | He was known for his austere lifestyle, avoiding any luxury. 他以其简朴的生活方式而闻名，避免任何奢侈。|  The judge delivered his verdict in an austere tone. 法官以严厉的语气宣布了他的判决。 | synonyms: stern, strict, severe, ascetic, harsh, spartan, rigorous | antonyms: luxurious, lavish, indulgent, extravagant, lenient, flexible | adjective
