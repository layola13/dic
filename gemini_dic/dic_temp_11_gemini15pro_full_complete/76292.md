gabbers:/ˈɡæbərz/
n.
喋喋不休的人，饶舌者
People who talk excessively, especially about unimportant things.
The gabbers in the cafe made it difficult to concentrate. 咖啡馆里喋喋不休的人让我很难集中注意力。
The two gabbers spent hours discussing nothing of importance.  这两个饶舌者花了几个小时讨论无关紧要的事情。

近义词: chatterboxes, gossips
反义词: reticent people, quiet individuals
词性: noun (plural)
