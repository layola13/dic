khans:/kɑːnz/
| n. |可汗；汗国；（尤指中亚的）商队旅馆|A title given to rulers and military leaders in Central Asia, also referring to caravanserais, especially in Central Asia.|The Silk Road was dotted with khans where travelers could rest and trade. 丝绸之路上点缀着许多可汗，旅行者可以在那里休息和交易。|synonyms: rulers, leaders, chieftains, emirs, sultans, caravanserais, inns|antonyms: followers, subjects|noun

Etymology:
From Mongolian *qan*  meaning "ruler, lord, king," borrowed into Turkish and other languages.  Ultimately from Proto-Turkic *qaγan*.
