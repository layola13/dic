gruffest:/ˈɡrʌfɪst/ | adj. | 最粗暴的，最粗声粗气的 | Superlative form of gruff: most gruff.  It describes a voice or manner that is rough and unfriendly. |  He answered in his gruffest voice. 他用最粗暴的声音回答。|  The gruffest bouncer I'd ever seen guarded the door.  我见过的最粗暴的保镖守着门。|Synonyms: harshest, roughest, surliest, brusquest | Antonyms: gentlest, kindest, softest, sweetest | adjective
