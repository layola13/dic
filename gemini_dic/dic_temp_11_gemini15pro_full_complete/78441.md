get-wise-to:/ɡet ˈwaɪz tuː/| phrasal verb | 了解；意识到；识破；|to become aware of something that was previously hidden or secret, usually something dishonest|例句：The police eventually got wise to his scams. 警察最终识破了他的骗局。|例句：She got wise to their plan to cheat her out of her inheritance. 她识破了他们骗取她遗产的计划。|近义词：catch on to, figure out, realize, discover, become aware of|反义词：be unaware of, be oblivious to|词性: 短语动词
