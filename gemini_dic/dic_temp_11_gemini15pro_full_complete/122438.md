mobber:/ˈmɑːbər/ (US) /ˈmɒbə(r)/ (UK)| n. |暴徒，聚众闹事的人；网络暴民|A person who is part of a mob, especially one that is violent or disorderly; someone who participates in online harassment or bullying.|例句：The mobber threw a rock through the shop window. 暴徒向商店橱窗扔了一块石头。

例句：Online mobbers targeted the actress with hateful comments. 网络暴民用仇恨的评论攻击这位女演员。|近义词：rioter, hooligan, bully, harasser|反义词: bystander, onlooker |词性: noun
