decisive:/dɪˈsaɪsɪv/| adj.|决定性的；果断的；坚定的|Having the power or quality of deciding; putting an end to controversy; crucial or most important.|Her decisive action saved the company from bankruptcy. 她果断的行动挽救了公司免于破产。|近义词：conclusive, definitive, resolute, crucial|反义词：indecisive, hesitant, ambiguous|词性:形容词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin decisivus, from decidere ‘determine’ (see decide).
