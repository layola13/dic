decani:/dɪˈkeɪnaɪ/
n.
decan
十日，十分之一，十人长，院长，系主任
A decani is a period of ten days or a group of ten.  In astrology, it refers to one-third of an astrological sign, covering 10 degrees. It can also refer to an administrative official, like a dean.
The ancient Egyptians divided each of their zodiac signs into three decans.  埃及人将他们的每个星座分成三个十分之一。
