nonretiring:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈtaɪə.rɪŋ/
adjective
不停歇的，不退休的，不退却的
Not retiring or withdrawing; persistent; persevering.
He has a nonretiring nature, always busy with new projects. 他有着不停歇的天性，总是忙于新的项目。
The team showed a nonretiring spirit, fighting until the very end.  这支队伍展现了不屈不挠的精神，战斗到最后一刻。
近义词: persistent, persevering, tireless, indefatigable, relentless
反义词: retiring, withdrawing, timid, hesitant, yielding
词性: adjective
