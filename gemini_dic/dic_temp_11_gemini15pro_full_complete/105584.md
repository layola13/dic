kindlers:/ˈkɪndlɚz/| n. |点火者；煽动者；引火物|People who start fires, either literally or figuratively, by providing the initial spark or fuel.  Those who incite or provoke something, often negative like violence or strong emotions. Materials used to start a fire.|The kindlers used dry leaves and twigs to start the campfire. 点火者用干树叶和小树枝点燃了篝火。|Synonyms: incendiaries, instigators, fire starters, igniters|Antonyms: extinguishers, peacemakers|noun
