broken-reed:/ˈbroʊkən riːd/ |adj.|  脆弱的，靠不住的|Weak and unreliable; not to be depended on. |His promises were broken-reed, offering no real assurance.| 近义词:fragile, flimsy, unreliable | 反义词:strong, sturdy, dependable |adjective
