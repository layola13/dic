navys:/ˈneɪviz/| n. |海军（通常指美国海军）|  A colloquial and informal plural form of "navy," often used in American English to refer to the United States Navy.  It is grammatically less formal than "navies."| The navys are sending a fleet to the Pacific. 海军正在派遣一支舰队前往太平洋。|  The navys played a crucial role in the war. 海军在这场战争中扮演了至关重要的角色。|Synonym: naval forces, fleet,  (in US context) US Navy, USN| Antonym:  (loosely) enemy forces, opposing fleet| Noun
