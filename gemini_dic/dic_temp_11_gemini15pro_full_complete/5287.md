allosaurs:/ˈæləsɔːrz/
noun
异特龙（复数）
Large carnivorous dinosaurs of the Late Jurassic period.
Allosaurus was a fearsome predator. 异特龙是一种可怕的掠食者。
Synonyms: carnivore, predator
Antonyms: herbivore, prey
