overbide:/ˌoʊvərˈbaɪd/| v.|出价过高，过度约束|To bid more than is justified; to strain or overtax.|He overbid on the house and now regrets it. 他对那栋房子出价过高，现在后悔了。|The lawyer overbid his hand by demanding too much from the jury. 律师因向陪审团要求过多而弄巧成拙。|近义词：outbid, overestimate|反义词: underbid, underestimate|词性:动词

词源: From "over-" (excessively) + "bid" (to offer a price).
