galbanum:/ˈɡælbənəm/| n. |白松香，松脂|A gum resin with a strong, somewhat bitter odor, obtained from various Asian plants of the genus *Ferula*, used in perfumes and incense. |例句：The perfume had a distinctive note of galbanum. 这款香水有一种独特的松脂香味。

例句：Galbanum is often used as a base note in perfumes. 松脂常被用作香水的基调。|近义词：resin, gum|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From Late Latin *galbanum*, from Latin *galbanus*, from Ancient Greek *χαλβάνη* (khalbánē). Ultimately of Semitic origin.
