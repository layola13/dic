indiscrete:/ˌɪndɪˈskriːt/
adjective
不慎重的，轻率的；不加选择的，无差别的
Lacking in discretion; imprudent or unwise.  Not making or based on careful distinctions.
He made indiscrete remarks about his colleagues. 他对他的同事们说了些轻率的话。
indiscreet comments/laughter/questions 轻率的评论/笑声/问题
The information was passed on through indiscreet gossip. 这些信息是通过不慎重的闲话传出去的。
Synonyms: imprudent, unwise, incautious, careless, rash, impolitic, undiplomatic, tactless
Antonyms: discreet, prudent, cautious, careful, circumspect
