overlauding:/ˌōvərˈlɔːdɪŋ/ | v. | 过分称赞，吹捧过度 | Praising excessively; bestowing too much praise. | The critic was accused of overlauding the mediocre performance.  评论家被指责过分赞扬了这场平庸的表演。|  overpraising, exaggerating, gushing | understating, downplaying | verb
Etymology: From "over-" (excessive) + "laud" (praise).  "Laud" comes from Latin "laudare" (to praise).
