destituent:/ˈdɛstɪtjuːənt/
adjective
贫困的，缺乏的
lacking something needed or desirable
The destituent family had no food or shelter.  这贫困的家庭没有食物和住所。
近义词: impoverished, indigent, needy, poor
反义词: affluent, wealthy, rich
