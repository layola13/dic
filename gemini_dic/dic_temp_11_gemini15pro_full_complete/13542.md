attemperly:/əˈtɛmpərli/| adv.|适度地，温和地|Moderately; temperately.  It describes actions or qualities done in a balanced or measured way, avoiding extremes.|He spoke attemperly, carefully choosing his words to avoid causing offense.|近义词: moderately, temperately, mildly, gently|反义词: extremely, excessively, immoderately, violently|词性:副词

词源: From the verb "attemper," derived from the Latin "ad" (to) + "temperare" (to mix, moderate).
