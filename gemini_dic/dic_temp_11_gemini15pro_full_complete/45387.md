cup:/kʌp/| n. v.|杯子；奖杯；(一)杯；捧|A small, open container made of glass, plastic, or other material, typically having a handle and used for drinking from.  Can also refer to a trophy or the amount held by a cup.|例句：I drank a cup of coffee this morning. 我今天早上喝了一杯咖啡。|近义词：mug, glass, chalice, trophy, award|反义词: (none direct antonyms, perhaps 'bowl' if considering shape)|n. v.

cup:/kʌp/| v.|捧|To hold something in one's cupped hands.|例句：He cupped his hands around the flame to protect it from the wind. 他用手捧着火焰，保护它不被风吹灭。|近义词：cradle, hold|反义词: (none direct antonyms)|v.
