goal: /ɡoʊl/ (US) /ɡəʊl/ (UK) | n. | 目标，目的，球门，得分 |  Something that you are trying to do or achieve; an aim or objective. In sports, a frame or area into which a ball or puck must be sent in order to score. |  My goal is to finish this project by Friday. 我的目标是在周五前完成这个项目。 He scored the winning goal in the last minute of the game. 他在比赛的最后一分钟打进了制胜一球。 | synonyms: aim, objective, target, purpose, aspiration | antonyms: failure, loss, defeat | noun
