off-duty:/ˌɒfˈdjuːti/ (UK), /ˌɔːfˈduːti/ (US)| adj.|下班的，非值班的|Not working; having free time because you are not working.|The off-duty police officer saw the robbery and intervened. 下班的警官目睹了抢劫案并进行了干预。|Synonyms: off work, free, leisure, not working|Antonyms: on-duty, working, at work|adjective

off-duty:/ˌɒfˈdjuːti/ (UK), /ˌɔːfˈduːti/ (US)| n.|下班时间|Time when not at work.|He spends his off-duty relaxing with his family.  他的下班时间都用来和家人一起放松。|Synonyms: free time, leisure time|Antonyms: working hours, on-duty time|noun
