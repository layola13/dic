aestivation:/ˌes.tɪˈveɪ.ʃən/ | n. | 夏眠；度夏 |A state of dormancy or inactivity during the summer, especially in animals, to avoid the heat and drought.  It's the summer equivalent of hibernation. |  Many desert animals undergo aestivation to survive the scorching summer heat.  许多沙漠动物通过夏眠来熬过酷暑。|Synonyms: dormancy, inactivity, torpor | Antonyms: activity, wakefulness | noun
