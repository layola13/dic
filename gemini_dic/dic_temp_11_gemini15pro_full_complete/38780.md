conducivenesses:/kənˈduːsɪvnɪsɪz/
|n.|
|有利，有益，促进作用|
|The state of being conducive; the quality of contributing to a result.|
|The conducivenesses of the environment to learning are numerous, including a quiet study space and access to resources.|  这个环境对学习的诸多益处包括安静的学习空间和获取资源的途径。|
|The warm climate and fertile soil increased the conducivenesses of the region to agriculture.|  温暖的气候和肥沃的土壤增加了该地区对农业的有利条件。|
|conduciveness, favorableness, advantageousness|
|unfavorableness, disadvantageousness, unsuitableness|
|noun|
