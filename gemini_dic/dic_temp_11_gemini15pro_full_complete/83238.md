gymnasium: /dʒɪmˈneɪziəm/ | n. |体育馆，健身房|A large room with equipment for exercising and playing sports. |The school has a new gymnasium. 学校有一个新的体育馆。|The athletes trained in the gymnasium. 运动员在体育馆训练。|近义词: gym, fitness center, health club |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek γυμνάσιον (gumnásion) meaning "place for naked exercise," from γυμνός (gumnós, “naked”).  In ancient Greece, physical exercise was typically done naked.
