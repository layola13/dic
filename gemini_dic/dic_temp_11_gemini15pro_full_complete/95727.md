inconsequent:/ɪnˈkɒnsɪkwənt/ | adj. | 不一致的；不连贯的；不合逻辑的 | Not consistent or logical; illogical. | His arguments were inconsequent and unconvincing. 他的论点前后矛盾，没有说服力。 | Her behavior was often inconsequent with her stated beliefs. 她的行为经常与她声称的信仰不一致。| 近义词：inconsistent, illogical, incoherent | 反义词：consistent, coherent, logical | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Late Latin *inconsequēns, from in- ‘not’ + consequēns, present participle of consēqui ‘to follow’.
