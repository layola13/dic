noncivilians:/ˌnɒn.sɪˈvɪl.i.ənz/ /ˌnɑːn.səˈvɪl.jənz/
noun
平民以外的人；非平民；军事人员
people who are not members of the general public, especially members of the armed forces or police
例句：Noncivilians are not allowed on the military base. 非平民不得进入军事基地。

例句：The attack resulted in the deaths of both soldiers and noncivilians. 这次袭击导致士兵和平民均有伤亡。

近义词：military personnel, armed forces, combatants
反义词：civilians, public

词性:n.
