crumplier:/ˈkrʌmpliər/ | adj. | 更皱的，更蜷缩的 | More crumpled or wrinkled; more shrunken or withered.  | The old woman's face was crumplier than I remembered. 老妇人的脸比我记忆中的更加皱巴巴。 |  The leaves became crumplier as they dried. 叶子干了之后变得更加皱巴巴。 | 近义词: more wrinkled, more withered, more shriveled | 反义词: smoother, flatter | 词性: 形容词

crumpliest:/ˈkrʌmpliɪst/ | adj. | 最皱的，最蜷缩的 | Most crumpled or wrinkled; most shrunken or withered. |  Of all the apples, this one is the crumpliest. 所有苹果中，这个是最皱的。 | The crumpliest leaf fell from the tree. 最皱巴巴的叶子从树上掉了下来。 | 近义词: most wrinkled, most withered, most shriveled | 反义词: smoothest, flattest | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From crumple + -y/-ier/-iest.  "Crumple" comes from the Middle Dutch "krompen," meaning "to shrink" or "to contract."
