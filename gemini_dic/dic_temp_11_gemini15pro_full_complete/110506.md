ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *limbic (whence also Middle French lambic), from Medieval Latin limbicus, from Arabic الإنبيق (al-ʾinbīq, “the still”), from Greek ἄμβιξ (ámbix, “cup, beaker”).  Doublet of alembic.
