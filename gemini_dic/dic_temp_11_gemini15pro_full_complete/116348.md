masterwort:/ˈmæstərwɜːrt/| n. |独活;伞形科草本植物|A plant of the genus *Peucedanum*, especially *P. ostruthium* (common masterwort), formerly used medicinally.|Masterwort was once a popular medicinal herb, believed to cure everything from epilepsy to indigestion.|Synonyms: hog's fennel, imperatoria, ostruthium|Antonyms: N/A|noun

masterwort:/ˈmæstərwɜːrt/| n. |野芝麻;紫草科草本植物|Any of several North American plants of the genus *Astrantia*, having pink, white or purplish flowers.|The delicate pink flowers of the masterwort added a touch of elegance to the rock garden.|Synonyms: melanthium|Antonyms: N/A|noun
