lumps:/lʌmps/| n.|块，肿块；（尤指食物的）一小块；笨人|A lump is a small, compact mass of something.| I found a lump in my breast. 我在乳房里发现了一个肿块。|Synonyms: chunk, mass, piece, clod, bump |Antonyms: smoothness, flatness|noun


lumps:/lʌmps/| v. (lump something together)|把…归并在一起；把…混为一谈|To lump things together means to treat them as a single unit, often ignoring their differences.|They lumped all the expenses together. 他们把所有的费用都算在一起了。|Synonyms: combine, group, merge |Antonyms: separate, divide, distinguish|verb
