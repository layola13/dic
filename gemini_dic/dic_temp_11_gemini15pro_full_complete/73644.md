forfending:/fɔːrˈfɛndɪŋ/
adjective
禁止的，防止的
Preventing something from happening; averting.
God forfend that such a thing should happen! 上帝禁止这种事情发生！
近义词: preventing, averting, prohibiting, forbidding
反义词: causing, allowing, permitting
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Middle English *forfenden, from Old English forfēon (“to despise, disdain, abhor, renounce, refuse, forbid, prohibit”), from for- (“away, against, without”) + fēon (“to hate, abhor”). Cognate with Dutch verfoeien (“to detest”), German verfehlen (“to miss, fail, err”).
