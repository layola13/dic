outgaining:/ˌaʊtˈɡeɪnɪŋ/
verb
超过，胜过
Surpassing or exceeding in gains or earnings.
The company is outgaining its competitors in the market share.  这家公司在市场份额上超过了它的竞争对手。
New technologies are outgaining traditional industries in terms of growth. 新技术在增长方面超过了传统产业。
近义词: outperforming, exceeding, surpassing, beating
反义词: lagging behind, underperforming, trailing
词性: Verb (present participle)

outgain:/ˌaʊtˈɡeɪn/
verb
超过，胜过
To surpass or exceed in gains or earnings.
The company outgained its competitors last quarter. 这家公司上个季度超过了它的竞争对手。
Our team outgained theirs by a significant margin. 我们的团队以显著的优势超过了他们的团队。
近义词: outperform, exceed, surpass, beat
反义词: lag behind, underperform, trail
词性: Verb


词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding, surpassing") + "gain" (verb meaning "to obtain or acquire something desirable")
