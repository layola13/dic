inland:/ˈɪnlənd/ (US), /ˈɪnlænd/ (UK)| adj. adv. n.|内陆的，内地的；向内陆，在内地；内陆，内地|Situated away from the coast; towards or in the interior of a country.|We traveled inland to escape the coastal crowds. 我们前往内陆以避开沿海的人群。|近义词：interior, internal, midland|反义词：coastal, maritime|词性: adjective, adverb, noun

inland:/ˈɪnlənd/ (US), /ˈɪnlænd/ (UK)| adv.|向内陆，在内地|Towards or in the interior of a country.|They decided to move further inland. 他们决定搬到更内陆的地方。|近义词：inwards|反义词：outward, seaward|词性: adverb

inland:/ˈɪnlənd/ (US), /ˈɪnlænd/ (UK)| n.|内陆，内地|The interior part of a country.|The ship sailed from the coast to the inland. 船从海岸航行到内陆。|近义词：interior, hinterland|反义词：coast, shore|词性: noun
