overcompliance:/ˌoʊvərəmˈplaɪəns/ | n. | 过度遵从，过度合规 | The act of complying with rules, regulations, or requests to an excessive or unnecessary degree.  Going beyond what is required or expected in order to conform. |  The company's overcompliance with environmental regulations significantly increased their operating costs. 这家公司对环境法规的过度遵从大大增加了他们的运营成本。

The bank's overcompliance with anti-money laundering rules led to delays in processing legitimate transactions.  这家银行对反洗钱规定的过度遵守导致合法交易的处理延误。 | 近义词: over-regulation, over-scrupulousness | 反义词: noncompliance, undercompliance | 词性: 名词
