obvolute:/ˈɒbvəluːt/ | adj. | 包旋的，卷旋的 | (of a leaf or other plant part) rolled over and covering the part next to it; (of a shell) having each whorl overlapping the previous one. | The fern's new fronds unfurled, revealing tightly obvolute leaves.  这种蕨类植物的新叶展开，露出了紧紧包旋的叶子。| Synonym: convolute, involute | Antonym: revolute | adjective

obvolute:/ˈɒbvəluːt/ | n. | 包旋叶，卷旋体 | A leaf or other plant part that is obvolute; a shell having each whorl overlapping the previous one. | The obvolute of the young fern frond protected its delicate tissues. 幼蕨叶的包旋体保护着它娇嫩的组织。 | Synonym: coil, whorl | Antonym:  unfurling | noun
