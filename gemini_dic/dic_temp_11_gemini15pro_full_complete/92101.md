hyperrhythmic:/ˌhaɪpərˈrɪðmɪk/ | adj. | 心律过速的，节律过快的 | Characterized by an abnormally fast or irregular rhythm, especially of the heart. |  His hyperrhythmic heartbeat concerned the doctor. 他的心跳过快让医生很担心。 | The hyperrhythmic music made it hard to dance. 节奏过快的音乐让人难以跳舞。 | 近义词：tachycardic, arrhythmic | 反义词：bradycardic | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From hyper- + rhythmic.  "Hyper-" is a prefix derived from Greek meaning "over, above, excessive." "Rhythmic" comes from the Greek "rhythmos" meaning "measured motion, time, proportion."
