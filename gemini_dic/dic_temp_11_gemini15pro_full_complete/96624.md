indulges:/ɪnˈdʌldʒɪz/| v.|放纵，沉溺，纵容|allows oneself or another person to have or do something enjoyable, especially something that is considered bad for you.|He indulged his passion for fast cars. 他沉溺于对跑车的热爱。|近义词：pamper, spoil, gratify, humor|反义词：deny, restrict, abstain|词性：动词

indulge:/ɪnˈdʌldʒ/| v.|放纵，沉溺，纵容|allows oneself or another person to have or do something enjoyable, especially something that is considered bad for you.|She indulged in a long hot bath. 她尽情享受了一个长时间的热水澡。|近义词：pamper, spoil, gratify, humor|反义词：deny, restrict, abstain|词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *indulgier, from Latin indulgere ("to be kind or tender to, yield to, give way to, comply with, grant, concede"), from in- ("in, into") + dulcis ("sweet").
