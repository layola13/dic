geodetic:/ˌdʒiːəˈdɛtɪk/
adjective
大地测量的
Relating to geodesy, the science of measuring and representing the Earth's shape and size, including its gravitational field.
The geodetic survey provided precise measurements of the Earth's surface.  大地测量提供了地球表面的精确测量。
The satellite is equipped with geodetic instruments. 这颗卫星配备了大地测量仪器。

近义词: surveying, cartographic
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps inaccurate, imprecise)
词性: adjective
