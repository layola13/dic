brawlers:/ˈbrɔːlərz/
n.
争吵者，打架者
Those who engage in fights or quarrels.
The bar was full of brawlers, ready to start a fight at any moment. 酒吧里满是爱吵架的人，随时准备打架。
fighters, scrappers, combatants
pacifists, peacemakers
