excursioned:/ɪkˈskɜːrʒənd/
verb
进行远足，旅行
Having gone on an excursion; traveled.  This word is considered archaic or non-standard.  It's better to use "went on an excursion" or "took an excursion."
例句：They *went on an excursion* to the beach. 他们去海滩旅行。

例句：The family *took an excursion* to the mountains. 这家人去山上旅行。
近义词：journeyed, traveled, toured
反义词：stayed, remained
词性：v.
