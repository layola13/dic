disciple:/dɪˈsaɪpəl/
n.
弟子，信徒
a follower or student of a teacher, leader, or philosopher
Jesus had twelve disciples. 耶稣有十二个门徒。

近义词:follower, adherent, student, pupil
反义词:opponent, critic, adversary
词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *desciple*, from Latin discipulus (“pupil, student”), from discere (“to learn”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *di-sk- (“to point out, show, teach”).
