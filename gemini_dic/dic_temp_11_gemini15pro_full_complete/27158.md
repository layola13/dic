call the shots:/ˌkɔːl ðə ˈʃɑːts/
|idiomatic phrase|
|发号施令；做主；操纵|
|To be in the position of authority or control; to make the decisions.|
|The CEO calls the shots in this company.|
|首席执行官在这家公司里说了算。|

|He's been calling the shots for over a decade.|
|他已经掌权十多年了。|

|近义词：be in charge, be the boss, run the show, pull the strings, dominate, control|
|反义词：take orders, follow instructions, be subordinate|
|phrase|
