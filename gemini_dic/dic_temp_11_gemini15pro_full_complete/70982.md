fireside:/ˈfaɪərˌsaɪd/| n. adj.|炉边，火炉旁|The area around a fireplace, especially viewed as a place of warmth, comfort, and domesticity.|We spent a cozy evening by the fireside, sharing stories. 我们在炉边度过了一个舒适的夜晚，分享故事。|近义词：hearth, fireplace|反义词: outdoors|词性:名词, 形容词
