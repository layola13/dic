contingency:/kənˈtɪndʒənsi/ | n. | 意外事件，偶然事件；可能性；[逻]偶然性 | a future event or circumstance which is possible but cannot be predicted with certainty | We must consider all possible contingencies. 我们必须考虑所有可能发生的意外事件。| synonyms: possibility, eventuality, incident, happening, occurrence, emergency, exigency | antonyms: certainty, predictability | noun

contingencies:/kənˈtɪndʒənsiz/ | n. | 意外事件（contingency 的复数）| plural form of contingency |  They had made no allowance for contingencies. 他们没有考虑到意外事件。 | synonyms: possibilities, eventualities, incidents, happenings, occurrences, emergencies, exigencies | antonyms: certainties, predictabilities | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin contingentia, from Latin contingens, present participle of contingere "to touch, befall," from con- "together" + tangere "to touch."
