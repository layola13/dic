carefree:/ˈkeərˌfriː/| adj.|无忧无虑的，轻松愉快的|Free from care; without worry or responsibility.|She lived a carefree life, traveling the world with no worries. 她过着无忧无虑的生活，无忧无虑地环游世界。|近义词：lighthearted, untroubled, happy-go-lucky|反义词:worried, anxious, burdened|词性:形容词
Etymology: From care + free.  The word "care" in this context means worry or anxiety.  "Free" signifies being without something. Therefore, "carefree" literally means free from worry.
