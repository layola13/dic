nativistic:/ˌneɪtɪˈvɪstɪk/| adj.|本土主义的，排外主义的|Relating to or supporting nativism, a political policy of protecting the interests of native inhabitants against those of immigrants.|The politician's nativistic rhetoric fueled anti-immigrant sentiment. 这位政客的本土主义言论煽动了反移民情绪。| The group holds nativistic views and opposes further immigration.  这个团体持有本土主义观点，反对进一步的移民。|Synonyms: xenophobic, chauvinistic, isolationist|Antonyms: cosmopolitan, internationalist, inclusive|adjective

Etymology: From Latin *nativus* (native) + -istic (forming adjectives).
