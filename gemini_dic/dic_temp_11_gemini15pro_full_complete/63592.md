envisions:/ɪnˈvɪʒənz/
verb
展望，想象
To form a mental picture of something not yet existing; to conceive of as a possibility.
She envisions a future where everyone has access to clean water. 她 envisions 一个每个人都能获得清洁用水的未来。
The architect envisioned a soaring skyscraper that would dominate the skyline.  建筑师设想了一座高耸入云的摩天大楼，它将主宰天际线。
Synonyms: imagines, visualizes, pictures, contemplates, anticipates
Antonyms: ignores, disregards, overlooks
词性:动词
