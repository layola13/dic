hosts:/həʊsts/ (British) /hoʊsts/ (American)| n. v.| 主持人，东道主，许多，大量;主办，主持，招待|A person who receives or entertains other people as guests.|例句：The hosts of the party were very welcoming. 聚会的主人非常热情好客。|近义词：emcees, anchors, organizers, innkeepers|反义词:guests, visitors|词性:noun, verb

hosts:/həʊsts/ (British) /hoʊsts/ (American)| n. v.| 主机，众多;作为主人招待|A computer that provides services to other computers on a network.|例句：The hosts file on your computer can block access to certain websites. 你电脑上的主机文件可以阻止访问某些网站。|近义词：servers|反义词:clients|词性:noun, verb
