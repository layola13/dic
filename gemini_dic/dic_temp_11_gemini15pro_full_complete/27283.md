calumniously:/kəˈlʌmniəsli/
adverb
诽谤地；中伤地
in a manner that utters false and malicious statements about someone.
He was calumniously accused of theft. 他被诬告偷窃。

She calumniously spread rumors about her rival. 她恶意散播关于她对手的谣言。
近义词:falsely, slanderously, maliciously, libelously
反义词:truthfully, honestly, fairly
词性:adverb

词源:
From Late Latin *calumniōsus, from Latin calumnia (“false accusation, malicious prosecution, trickery”), from calvi (“to deceive”).
