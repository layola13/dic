induviae:/ɪnˈduːviiː/
noun
蜕皮; (昆虫的)蜕壳; (蛇的)蜕皮;  (尤指昆虫)脱落的皮,外皮
The cast-off outer covering of an animal, especially an insect, after molting.
The locust sheds its induviae. 蝗虫蜕皮。
Synonyms: exuviae, slough, molt, cast-off skin, shell
Antonyms:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 new skin 新皮肤)
