equites:/ˈɛkwɪˌtiːz/
n.
骑士; 骑兵; 古罗马骑士阶级
Roman cavalrymen, originally the wealthy citizens who could afford to serve as mounted soldiers. Later, it became a distinct social class ranking below the senatorial class but above the plebeians.
The equites played a significant role in Roman politics and commerce. 罗马骑士在罗马政治和商业中扮演着重要角色。
The equites formed a powerful voting bloc.  骑士阶层形成了一个强大的投票集团。
