divinest:/dɪˈvaɪnɪst/
adjective
最神圣的；最神圣的
Most divine; supremely good or beautiful.
例句：She has the divinest voice I've ever heard. 她拥有我听过的最神圣的声音。

例句：The sunset painted the sky in the divinest hues. 日落用最神圣的色彩描绘了天空。

近义词：holiest, sacred, blessed, sublime, heavenly
反义词：unholy, profane, wicked, earthly, mundane
词性：adjective

词源: From Middle English *divinest, superlative of *divin, from Old French devin (“divine”), from Latin dīvīnus (“divine”), from dīvus (“god”).
