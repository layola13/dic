motionist:/ˈmoʊʃənɪst/| n. |电影放映员；活动摄影师|A person who operates a motion-picture projector or camera, especially in the early days of cinema.| Example: The motionist carefully adjusted the projector to ensure a clear image.  这位电影放映员仔细地调整放映机以确保图像清晰。|Example:  The motionist captured the bustling city streets on film.  活动摄影师用胶片捕捉了繁华的城市街道。|Synonyms: projectionist, cinematographer (in some contexts), cameraman (in some contexts)|Antonyms: N/A|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "motion" (referring to moving pictures) + "-ist" (a suffix denoting a person who practices or is involved in something).  The word reflects the early days of cinema when the technology focused on capturing and displaying moving images.
