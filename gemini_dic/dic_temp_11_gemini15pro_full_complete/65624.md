evangelised:/ɪˈvændʒəlaɪzd/
verb
传福音；使皈依基督教；(为…)大力宣传；积极推广
to convert someone to Christianity; to preach the gospel to someone; to promote something with enthusiasm
例句：The missionaries evangelised the remote tribes.  传教士向偏远的部落传福音。

例句：He evangelised the benefits of a vegetarian diet. 他大力宣传素食的好处。

近义词：preached, proselytized, promoted, advocated
反义词：suppressed, discouraged
词性：verb
