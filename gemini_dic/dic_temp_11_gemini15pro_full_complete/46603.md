damask:/ˈdæməsk/| n. adj.|锦缎，大马士革锦缎；（尤指亚麻布或棉布上的）凸纹图案，提花图案|A rich, patterned fabric, typically woven in silk, linen, or cotton, with a raised design; also, the pattern itself.|The tablecloth was made of fine damask.  桌布是用上好的锦缎制成的。|She wore a dress of blue damask. 她穿着一件蓝色锦缎连衣裙。|近义词：brocade, tapestry, satin|反义词:muslin, calico|词性:名词, 形容词
damask:/ˈdæməsk/| v.|（尤指亚麻布或棉布上）织出凸纹图案，织出提花图案|To decorate (fabric) with a raised design.|The fabric was damasked with a floral pattern.  这种织物上织有花卉图案。|The craftsman damasked the linen with an intricate design.  工匠在亚麻布上织出了复杂的图案。|近义词：emboss, weave|反义词:N/A|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *damaske, from Old French damas, from Medieval Latin damascus, from Latin Damascēnus (“of Damascus”), from Damascus, the capital of Syria, where the fabric was originally made.
