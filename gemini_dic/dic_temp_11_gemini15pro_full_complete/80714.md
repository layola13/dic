gorka: /ˈɡɔːrkə/ | n. | 山地作战服， горка(俄语) | A type of camouflage uniform originally worn by Soviet and Russian Spetsnaz (special forces) units and mountain troops. It is designed for rugged terrain and provides good concealment in forested and mountainous environments. |  He wore a gorka suit on the hiking trip. 他在徒步旅行时穿了一套山地作战服。

The gorka is designed to be durable and practical. 山地作战服的设计经久耐用且实用。 | 近义词: camouflage uniform, combat uniform | 反义词: civilian clothes | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Russian горка (gorka), meaning "small mountain" or "hill".  The name likely refers to the suit's suitability for mountainous terrain.
