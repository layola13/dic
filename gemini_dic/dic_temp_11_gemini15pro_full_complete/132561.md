nondiaphanous:/ˌnɒndaɪˈæfənəs/
adjective
不透明的；不透光的
Not allowing light to pass through; opaque.
The curtains were thick and nondiaphanous, blocking out all sunlight.  厚厚的窗帘不透光，挡住了所有的阳光。
近义词:opaque, nontransparent, impervious to light
反义词:diaphanous, transparent, translucent
词性:adjective


Etymology:
From non- + diaphanous.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "diaphanous" comes from the Greek "diaphanēs" meaning "transparent, translucent."
