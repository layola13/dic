negotiated:/nɪˈɡoʊʃieɪtɪd/ |v. |协商的，谈判的|agreed upon or settled by discussion and compromise|The price was negotiated between the buyer and seller. 买卖双方协商了价格。|近义词：agreed upon, settled, arranged, hammered out|反义词：unresolved, disputed, contested|verb (past participle)

negotiate:/nɪˈɡoʊʃieɪt/ |v. |协商，谈判|to discuss something formally in order to make an agreement|We negotiated a deal with the supplier. 我们与供应商达成了协议。|近义词：bargain, discuss, confer, mediate|反义词：dictate, impose|verb
