nondisqualifying:/ˌnɒndɪsˈkwɒlɪfaɪɪŋ/
adjective
不取消资格的，不使丧失资格的
Not causing someone or something to no longer be suitable or eligible for something.
The judge ruled that the minor infraction was nondisqualifying. 法官裁定轻微违规并不取消资格。
Synonyms: acceptable, permissible, allowable
Antonyms: disqualifying, invalidating
词性: 形容词
