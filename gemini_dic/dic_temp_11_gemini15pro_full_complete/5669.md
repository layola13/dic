altostratus:/ˌæltəˈstrɑːtəs/ /ˌɑːltəˈstreɪtəs/ | n. |高层云|A gray or bluish sheet of clouds, often covering the entire sky, that usually produces little or no precipitation.  It is a mid-level cloud genus belonging to the stratiform physical category characterized by a generally uniform, flat, and featureless sheet-like appearance. |The altostratus clouds obscured the sun. 高层云遮蔽了太阳。| Synonym: cloud cover | Antonym: clear sky | noun

altostratus:/ˌæltəˈstrɑːtəs/ /ˌɑːltəˈstreɪtəs/| n. |高层云|A mid-level cloud, gray or bluish in color, that often covers the entire sky. It usually doesn't produce precipitation, but can sometimes cause light rain or snow.
