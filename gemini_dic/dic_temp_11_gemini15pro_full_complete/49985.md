denitration:/ˌdiːnaɪˈtreɪʃən/ | n. |脱硝，反硝化作用 |The process of removing nitrates or nitrites, especially from industrial waste water or exhaust gases to reduce pollution. |The denitration of flue gas is crucial for controlling air pollution.  烟气脱硝对于控制空气污染至关重要。| synonyms: nitrate removal, denitrification | antonyms: nitrification | noun


denitrate:/diːˈnaɪtreɪt/ | v. |脱硝，除去硝酸盐 |To remove nitrates or nitrites from. |The factory denitrates its wastewater before discharging it into the river.  这家工厂在将废水排放到河流之前对其进行脱硝处理。| synonyms: remove nitrates, reduce nitrates | antonyms: nitrate, nitrify | verb
