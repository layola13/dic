disloyalty:/dɪsˈlɔɪəlti/
n.
不忠;背叛;不忠的行为
The act of being disloyal; unfaithfulness.
His disloyalty to his friends was shocking. 他对朋友的不忠令人震惊。
近义词: treachery, betrayal, perfidy, infidelity
反义词: loyalty, fidelity, faithfulness
词性: 名词

disloyal:/dɪsˈlɔɪəl/
adj.
不忠的;背叛的;不忠诚的
Not loyal; faithless.
He was accused of being disloyal to the company. 他被指控对公司不忠。
近义词: treacherous, unfaithful, perfidious
反义词: loyal, faithful
词性: 形容词

词源:
From dis- (expressing negation) + loyal + -ty.  The word "loyal" comes from Old French "loial," ultimately from Latin "lēgālis" meaning "legal."
