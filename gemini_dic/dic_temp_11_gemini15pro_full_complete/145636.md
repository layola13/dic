overconsuming:/ˌoʊvər kənˈsuːmɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度消费的 | Consuming something, such as food or resources, in excess of what is needed or sustainable. |  She felt guilty about overconsuming during the holidays. 她对假期期间的过度消费感到内疚。 | overspending, excessive consumption | underconsuming, conserving | adjective

overconsume:/ˌoʊvər kənˈsuːm/ | v. | 过度消费 | To consume more than is necessary or desirable; to use up resources or goods excessively. |  We are overconsuming the Earth's resources at an alarming rate. 我们正在以惊人的速度过度消耗地球资源。 | overspend, overuse | underconsume, conserve | verb
