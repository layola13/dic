misdefined:/ˌmɪsdɪˈfaɪnd/
adjective
定义错误的；定义不当的
incorrectly or inadequately defined
The term "artificial intelligence" is often misdefined in popular culture.  人工智能这个术语在流行文化中经常被错误定义。
The problem was misdefined from the start, leading to incorrect solutions.  这个问题从一开始就定义错误，导致了不正确的解决方案。
近义词: misinterpreted, wrongly defined, inaccurately defined
反义词: well-defined, clearly defined, accurately defined
词性: adjective
词源: mis- (prefix meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + defined (past participle of "define")
