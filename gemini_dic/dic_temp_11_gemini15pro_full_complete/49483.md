delusive:/dɪˈluːsɪv/
|adj.|欺骗性的；虚妄的；错觉的|Giving a false or misleading impression.|
|例句：He had a delusive belief that he was a king. 他有一种错觉，认为自己是个国王。|
|例句：The calm before the storm proved to be delusive. 暴风雨前的平静被证明是虚假的。|
|近义词：misleading, deceptive, fallacious, spurious, illusory|
|反义词：accurate, truthful, genuine, real, factual|
|词性：Adjective|


词源:  From Latin *dēlūs-, from dē- 'down, away' + lūdō 'play, mock, deceive'.
