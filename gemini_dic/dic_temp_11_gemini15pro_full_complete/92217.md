hypertext:/ˈhaɪpɚˌtɛkst/ | n. | 超文本；超链接 | Text displayed on a computer display or other electronic devices with references (hyperlinks) to other text that the reader can immediately access, or where text can be revealed progressively at multiple levels of detail. | Example: Clicking on the hypertext word "history" might take you to another page defining the word. 点击超文本单词“历史”可能会带您到另一个定义该单词的页面。

Example:  Hypertext allows readers to navigate information non-sequentially. 超文本允许读者以非顺序方式浏览信息。 | Synonym:  hyperlink, hypermedia | Antonym: plain text, linear text | noun
