centroid:/ˈsɛntrɔɪd/| n.|质心，形心|The centroid of a triangle is the intersection of its medians. 三角形的形心是它中线的交点。|例句：The centroid of the data points was calculated to determine the average location. 数据点的质心被计算出来以确定平均位置。

The engineers used the centroid to balance the aircraft. 工程师们用质心来平衡飞机。|近义词：center of mass, center of gravity|反义词:|noun
