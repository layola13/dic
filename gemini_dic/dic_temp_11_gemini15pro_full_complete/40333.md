contrabass:/ˌkɑːntrəˈbeɪs/ /ˌkɒntrəˈbeɪs/| n.|低音提琴；低音号|A large stringed instrument of the violin family, the largest and lowest-pitched of the family,  played upright with a bow or by plucking the strings.|The contrabass provided a deep, resonant foundation for the orchestra. 低音提琴为管弦乐队提供了深沉、洪亮的基调。

The jazz musician plucked the strings of his contrabass. 这位爵士乐手拨动着他低音提琴的琴弦。|近义词：double bass, string bass|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps piccolo bass as a conceptual opposite)|词性:noun
