noncloistered:/nɒnˈklɔɪstəd/ | adj. | 世俗的，非隐居的 | Not cloistered; living and working among ordinary people rather than being secluded in a monastery or convent. |  Example:  She chose a noncloistered life dedicated to social work. 她选择了一种致力于社会工作的世俗生活。

Example:  The noncloistered nuns work in hospitals and schools. 这些非隐居的修女在医院和学校工作。 | Near Synonyms: secular, worldly, lay, unconfined | Antonyms: cloistered, monastic, secluded, withdrawn | adjective
