dollar:/ˈdɑːlər/ (US) /ˈdɒlə(r)/ (UK)| n.|美元|A basic monetary unit of the US, Canada, Australia, and several other countries; equal to 100 cents.|例句：The book costs ten dollars. 这本书售价十美元。|近义词：buck (informal), greenback (informal)|反义词: (No direct antonym, could use currencies of other countries e.g., euro, yen, pound)|noun
