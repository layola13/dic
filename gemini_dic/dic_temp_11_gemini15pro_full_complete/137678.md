nonuniversal:/ˌnɒnjuːnɪˈvɜːsəl/ | adj. | 非普遍的，非通用的 | Not applicable to all cases or situations; not general or universal. |  Einstein's theory of relativity showed that Newtonian physics, while a good approximation at low speeds, is nonuniversal and breaks down at relativistic speeds.  爱因斯坦的相对论表明，牛顿物理学虽然在低速情况下是一个很好的近似值，但并非普遍适用，在相对论速度下会失效。

| Example: The specific cultural customs of this tribe are nonuniversal and not found elsewhere. 这个部落特有的文化习俗并非普遍存在，在其他地方找不到。 | Synonym: particular, specific, local, restricted, limited | Antonym: universal, general, global, widespread, common | word type: adjective
