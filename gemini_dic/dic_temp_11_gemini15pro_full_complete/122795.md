moldered:/ˈmoʊldərd/| v.|腐烂，朽坏|To decay or crumble into dust; to slowly deteriorate due to dampness or neglect. |The old books moldered in the damp basement.  那些旧书在地下室的潮湿环境中腐烂了。|The abandoned house slowly moldered away. 这栋废弃的房子慢慢地腐朽了。|近义词：decay, rot, decompose, crumble|反义词:flourish, thrive|词性:动词

molder:/ˈmoʊldər/| v.|腐烂，朽坏|To decay or crumble into dust; to slowly deteriorate due to dampness or neglect. |The wood began to molder after being left in the rain. 木头被雨淋后开始腐烂。|Unused skills can molder over time. 不用的技能会随着时间的推移而荒废。|近义词：decay, rot, decompose, crumble|反义词:flourish, thrive|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *moldren, from Old English molde (“dust, earth”), from Proto-Germanic *maldō (“earth, ground”), from Proto-Indo-European *melh₂- (“to grind, crush”). Related to mill.
