cash-strapped:/ˈkæʃˌstræpt/ | adj. | 资金紧张的，缺钱的 | Experiencing financial difficulties; short of money. | The cash-strapped company had to lay off several employees.  资金紧张的公司不得不解雇几名员工。 | synonyms:  broke, impoverished, penniless, hard up | antonyms: wealthy, affluent, prosperous | adjective

Etymology:
"cash" (n.) -  from Old French "casse" (money box), ultimately from Latin "capsa" (box, case).
"strapped" (adj.) - from "strap" (v.) meaning to fasten with a strap, implying being tightly bound or restricted, in this case, financially.
