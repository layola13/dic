handsaws:/ˈhænsɔːz/
noun
手锯（复数）
A saw with a handle, operated using one hand.  A tool used for cutting wood.
He used a pair of handsaws to cut the planks. 他用一对/两把/几把手锯来锯木板。
synonyms: saws, hand saws
antonyms: power saws, electric saws
