matte: /mæt/ | n. | 无光泽的表面；无光照片；遮光幕 | A dull, non-glossy surface, especially in photography or painting. | The photograph has a matte finish. 这张照片是无光面的。 | synonyms: dull, flat, non-glossy | antonyms: glossy, shiny | noun

mattes: /mæts/ | n. | 无光泽的表面（matte的复数）；遮光幕（matte的复数）| Plural form of "matte," referring to multiple dull, non-glossy surfaces. |  The photographer used several mattes to control the lighting. 摄影师用了几个遮光幕来控制光线。| synonyms: dull finishes, flat surfaces, non-glossy surfaces | antonyms: glossy finishes, shiny surfaces | noun
