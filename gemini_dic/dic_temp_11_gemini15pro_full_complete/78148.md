geophysicist:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈfɪzɪsɪst/| n. |地球物理学家|A scientist who studies the physics of the Earth, including its structure, composition, and processes.|例句：The geophysicist studied the seismic data to understand the earthquake's origin.  这位地球物理学家研究了地震数据以了解地震的起源。

例句：A team of geophysicists is exploring the ocean floor for new energy sources. 一组地球物理学家正在探索海底寻找新的能源。|近义词：earth scientist, geoscientist|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From geo- (earth) + physicist.  "Geo-" comes from the Greek "gē" (earth). "Physicist" comes from "physics," ultimately from the Greek "physikos" (pertaining to nature).
