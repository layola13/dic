coterminous:/koʊˈtɜːrmɪnəs/| adj.|相接的，毗邻的；范围相同的|Having the same or coincident boundaries.  Sharing a common boundary; contiguous. Also, equal in scope or duration.|The two properties are coterminous, sharing a long fence line.|Synonyms: adjacent, adjoining, contiguous, abutting, bordering, neighboring, coextensive|Antonyms: distant, separate, nonadjacent|adjective
