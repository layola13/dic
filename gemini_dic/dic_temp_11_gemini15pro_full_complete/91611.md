hyperangelical:/ˌhaɪpərænˈdʒɛlɪkəl/
| adj. | 超天使的，极度天使般的 | Exceeding angelic; supremely angelic. |  The hyperangelical beings were described as radiating pure light and love. 这些超天使般的存在被描述为散发着纯洁的光和爱。|  The music was so ethereal, it sounded hyperangelical.  这音乐如此空灵，听起来像超天使的。 | 近义词：seraphic, celestial, divine | 反义词：demonic, infernal, hellish | 词性: adjective
词源: hyper- (Greek: "over, beyond") + angelical (from angel, ultimately from Greek "angelos" - messenger)
