emptying: /ˈɛmtiɪŋ/ (US), /ˈɛmtiɪŋ/ (UK)
n.|清空，倒空|The emptying of the tank took several hours. 油箱的清空花了几个小时。
emptying: /ˈɛmtiɪŋ/ (US), /ˈɛmtiɪŋ/ (UK)
v.|清空，倒空|She is emptying the dishwasher. 她正在清空洗碗机。
Etymology: From Old English *æmtigian, from æmtig "empty".
Synonyms: discharging, draining, evacuating
Antonyms: filling, loading, replenishing
Word Type: Gerund or present participle of "empty"
