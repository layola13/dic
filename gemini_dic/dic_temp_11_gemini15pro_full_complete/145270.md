overbooked: /ˌoʊvərˈbʊkt/ /ˌəʊvəˈbʊkt/
adjective
超额预订的
reserved or sold in excess of the available capacity.
The flight was overbooked, and some passengers had to be put on standby.  航班超额预订了，一些乘客不得不候补。
The hotel was overbooked for the holiday weekend.  这个酒店在假日周末超额预订了。
近义词: overcrowded, full
反义词: underbooked, available
词性: adjective

overbook: /ˌoʊvərˈbʊk/ /ˌəʊvəˈbʊkt/
verb
超额预订
to make too many reservations for (an airplane flight, hotel, restaurant, etc.).
The airline overbooked the flight by ten passengers.  航空公司超额预订了十名乘客。
The restaurant was overbooked for Valentine's Day.  这家餐厅在情人节那天超额预订了。
近义词: overcrowd
反义词: underbook
词性: verb
