kalonka: /kaˈlɔŋka/ | n. | 喇叭; (尤指)扩音器; 留声机 | A loudspeaker, especially one in a public address system or a gramophone. |  The music blared from the kalonka in the town square.  广场上的喇叭大声播放着音乐。| synonyms: loudspeaker, speaker, amplifier, megaphone, gramophone | antonyms: microphone | noun

Etymology:  Borrowed from Russian колонка (kolonka), diminutive of колонна (kolonna, “column”), from Italian colonna, ultimately from Latin columna (“column”).  The semantic shift occurred because early loudspeakers were often mounted on tall column-like structures.
