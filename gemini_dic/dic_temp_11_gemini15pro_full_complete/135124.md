nonoccupations:/ˌnɑːnˌɑːkjuˈpeɪʃənz/ | n. | 非职业，非工作 |  Activities or pursuits that are not considered a person's primary source of income or employment.  These could include hobbies, volunteer work, or leisure activities. | Many retirees find fulfillment in nonoccupations like gardening and traveling. 许多退休人员在园艺和旅行等非职业活动中找到了满足感。 | synonyms: hobbies, pastimes, leisure activities, avocations | antonyms: occupations, professions, jobs, careers | noun
