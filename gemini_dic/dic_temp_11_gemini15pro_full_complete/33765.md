circulation: /ˌsɜːrkjəˈleɪʃn/ (US) /ˌsɜːkjʊˈleɪʃn/ (UK)| n.|循环，流通，发行量|The movement of blood through the body.  The passing of something from one person or place to another. The number of copies of a newspaper or magazine sold.|
例句：The circulation of the newspaper has increased. 这份报纸的发行量增加了。

例句：Exercise improves blood circulation. 锻炼能促进血液循环。

近义词：distribution, flow, dissemination|
反义词：stagnation, suppression|
词性：noun
