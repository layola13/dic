astringently:/əˈstrɪndʒəntli/| adv.|涩地，严厉地|In an astringent manner; harshly or severely.  It refers to both a physical sensation of tightening or dryness and a metaphorical sense of severity or harshness.|He spoke astringently about the need for fiscal responsibility. 他严厉地谈到了财政责任的必要性。|Synonyms: harshly, severely, sternly, strictly|Antonyms: gently, kindly, mildly, softly|Adverb

astringent:/əˈstrɪndʒənt/| adj.|收敛的，涩的，严厉的|Having a tightening effect on living tissue; harsh or severe.|The astringent taste of the unripe fruit made her pucker her lips.  未成熟水果的涩味让她噘起了嘴。|Synonyms: sharp, biting, caustic, harsh|Antonyms: mild, gentle, soothing, sweet|Adjective
