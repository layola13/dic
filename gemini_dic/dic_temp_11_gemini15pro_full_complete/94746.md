improvidentness:/ɪmˈprɒvɪdəntnəs/| n.|缺乏远见,  浪费,  不顾将来|Lack of foresight or thrift; a failure to provide for the future.|His improvidentness led him to spend all his money on frivolous things, leaving him with nothing for emergencies. 他的缺乏远见让他把所有的钱都花在了琐碎的事情上，以至于没有钱应急。|近义词：shortsightedness, imprudence, recklessness, extravagance|反义词：foresight, prudence, thrift, providence|noun
