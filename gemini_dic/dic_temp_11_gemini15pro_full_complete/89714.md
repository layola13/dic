hoped:/hoʊpt/| v.|希望，期望|past tense and past participle of hope  表达过去曾经希望或期望过某事。|I hoped for a sunny day. 我希望有个晴天。|Synonyms: expected, wished, desired, anticipated|Antonyms: despaired, doubted|verb

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I hope for the best. = 我希望一切顺利。
He hopes to finish the project on time. = 他希望按时完成项目。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am hoping for a promotion. = 我正期望着升职。
They are hoping the weather improves. = 他们正期望着天气好转。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have hoped for this day for a long time. = 我很久以来一直期盼着这一天。
She has hoped to travel the world. = 她一直希望环游世界。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been hoping for a miracle. = 我一直期盼着奇迹出现。
He has been hoping to hear from her. = 他一直期盼着收到她的来信。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I hoped to see you there. = 我希望在那里见到你。
She hoped for a better outcome. = 她希望有一个更好的结果。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was hoping you could help me. = 我当时希望你能帮助我。
They were hoping for a peaceful resolution. = 他们当时希望和平解决。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had hoped to finish the book before my trip. = 我曾希望在旅行前看完这本书。
She had hoped for a different life. = 她曾希望过一种不一样的生活。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been hoping for a chance to talk to him. = 我一直希望有机会和他谈谈。
They had been hoping for a change in leadership. = 他们一直希望领导层能有所改变。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will hope for the best. = 我会抱最好的希望。 (不太常用, 通常用will expect 或 look forward to)
He will hope to see you soon. = 他会希望很快见到你。(不太常用, 通常用will expect 或 look forward to)


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be hoping for good news. = 我会期盼着好消息。(不太常用)
They will be hoping for a successful launch. = 他们会期盼着成功发射。(不太常用)


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next year, I will have hoped for many things. = 到明年，我会期盼过很多事情。(不自然)
By the time he arrives, she will have hoped he brought a gift. = 到他到达的时候，她会一直期盼着他带礼物来。(不自然)

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next month, I will have been hoping for a response for a year. = 到下个月，我就期盼回复一年了。(不自然)
By the time the project is finished, they will have been hoping for its success for years. = 到项目完成时，他们将期盼它成功多年。(不自然)

虚拟语气:
If I had hoped for more, I might have been disappointed. = 如果我期望更多，我可能会失望。
I wish I had hoped for something different. = 我希望我曾期望过不同的东西。

被动语态: (hoped 不能用于被动语态)


疑问句:
What had you hoped for? = 你曾经希望得到什么？
What were you hoping for? = 你当时希望得到什么？

否定句:
I didn't hope for much. = 我没抱多大希望。
She hadn't hoped for that outcome. = 她没料到会是那样的结果。



PHRASE-> (hope 一般不与what搭配使用，以下列出hope 的常用搭配)
hope for the best = 抱最好的希望
hope against hope = 抱着微弱的希望
in the hope that = 怀着...的希望
have high hopes = 抱有很高的期望
lose hope = 失去希望


注意事项:

将来时态中，hope 很少用进行时或完成时。 通常使用expect 或 look forward to 来表达对未来的期望。
