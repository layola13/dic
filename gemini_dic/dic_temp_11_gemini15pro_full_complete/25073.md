bubba:/ˈbʌbə/| n.|兄弟；老兄；老乡；（尤指美国南部）乡下人|A term of endearment, often used for a brother or close male friend, especially in the southern United States.  It can also refer to a good-old-boy type, sometimes implying a lack of sophistication.|Example 1: Hey, bubba, how's it going? 嘿，老兄，最近怎么样？

Example 2:  He's a real bubba, loves his pickup truck and sweet tea. 他是个典型的乡下人，喜欢他的皮卡车和甜茶。|近义词：buddy, pal, friend, mate, dude|反义词:enemy, foe|noun
