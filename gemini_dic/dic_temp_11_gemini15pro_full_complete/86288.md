heavy-handednesses:/ˌheviˈhændɪdnɪsɪz/| n. |笨拙；粗暴；专横|Her heavy-handednesses in managing the project led to several resignations.  她在管理项目中的专横导致了几人辞职。|近义词：clumsiness, brutality, oppressiveness, authoritarianism|反义词:tact, sensitivity, finesse, diplomacy|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From heavy-handed + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state, quality, or condition).  Heavy-handed itself comes from heavy (meaning "having great weight") + handed (meaning "having hands of a specified kind").  The figurative meaning of "clumsy, oppressive" developed from the literal sense of having heavy hands.
