meloplasty: /ˈmɛləˌplæsti/ | n. | 面部整形术，颊部成形术 | Surgical reconstruction of the cheek.  It often involves the use of skin grafts or implants to restore the cheek's shape and function. |  After the accident, he required meloplasty to repair the damage to his cheek. 事故发生后，他需要进行面部整形手术来修复脸颊的损伤。

The meloplasty significantly improved the patient's facial symmetry. 面部整形手术显著改善了患者的面部对称性。| 近义词:  cheek augmentation, facial reconstruction | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:  melo- (Greek mēlon, "cheek") + -plasty (Greek plastos, "molded, formed")
