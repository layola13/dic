erythroid:/ɪˈrɪθrɔɪd/
adjective
红细胞的，产生红细胞的
Relating to red blood cells or their development.
Erythroid precursors are found in the bone marrow.  红细胞前体存在于骨髓中。
The erythroid lineage gives rise to red blood cells. 红系谱系产生红细胞。
近义词: erythrocytic, hematogenic
反义词: None
