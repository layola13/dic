go-to-the-wall:/ˌɡəʊ tə ðə ˈwɔːl/ | idiom | 失败；破产；垮台 | To fail, especially in business; to be ruined financially.  |  The company went to the wall after the recession hit.  这家公司在经济衰退后倒闭了。 | synonyms: fail, collapse, go bankrupt, go under | antonyms: succeed, thrive, prosper | idiom
go-to-the-wall:/ˌɡəʊ tə ðə ˈwɔːl/ | idiom | 拼命；竭尽全力 | To make a desperate effort; to fight to the end. | He was willing to go to the wall to protect his family. 他愿意竭尽全力保护他的家人。 | synonyms: fight tooth and nail, give it one's all, go all out | antonyms: give up, surrender | idiom
