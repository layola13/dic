immensurability:/ɪnˌmɛnʃərəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可通约性，无法衡量
The state or quality of being immensurable; incapable of being measured or compared by a common standard.
The immensurability of human suffering makes it difficult to compare one person's experience with another's.  人类苦难的不可通约性使得很难将一个人的经历与另一个人的经历进行比较。
The artist explored the immensurability of the universe in his latest work.  这位艺术家在他最新的作品中探索了宇宙的不可估量性。
incommensurability, disproportion, disparity
commensurability, comparability
noun
