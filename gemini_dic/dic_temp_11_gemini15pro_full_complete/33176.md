chromium:/ˈkroʊmiəm/ | n. |铬|Chromium is a chemical element with the symbol Cr and atomic number 24. It is a steely-grey, lustrous, hard and brittle transition metal. Chromium is also the main additive in stainless steel, to which it adds anti-corrosive properties.|Chromium plating gives a shiny finish to car parts. 镀铬使汽车零件具有光亮的表面。|Synonyms: chrome |Antonyms: None|Noun
