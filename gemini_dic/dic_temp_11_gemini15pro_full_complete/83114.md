gustful: /ˈɡʌstfəl/ | adj. | 狂风的；突发的；一阵阵的 | Characterized by or occurring in gusts; windy.  Subject to sudden outbursts of passion or emotion. | The gustful winds whipped through the valley. 狂风席卷山谷。|  He was known for his gustful temper. 他以其暴躁的脾气而闻名。 | 近义词: windy, blustery, squally, tempestuous, impulsive | 反义词: calm, peaceful, steady | 词性: adjective

词源: From gust + -ful.  "Gust" comes from Old Norse "gustr" meaning a blast of wind.
