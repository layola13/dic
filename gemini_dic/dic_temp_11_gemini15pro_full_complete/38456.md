concessionist:/kənˈseʃənɪst/ | n. |妥协者；让步者|  Someone who is willing to make concessions or compromises, especially in political negotiations. | The concessionist agreed to drop the demand for a pay raise in exchange for better benefits. 妥协者同意放弃加薪的要求，以换取更好的福利。| The union leader, known as a concessionist, agreed to a smaller pay raise than originally demanded.  工会领袖，被认为是一个妥协者，同意了比最初要求的更低的加薪幅度。 |  negotiator, compromiser, appeaser | hardliner, intransigent, diehard | noun
