barratrous:/ˈbærətrəs/ | adj. | 诉讼教唆的；好争吵的；爱惹事的 | Habitually inciting lawsuits or quarrels.  Given to instigating and encouraging litigation, especially groundless suits. |  The barratrous lawyer was eventually disbarred for his unethical practices.  这个爱惹事的律师最终因其不道德的行为被取消了律师资格。 |  litigious, quarrelsome, contentious | peaceful, amicable, conciliatory | adjective

barratry: /ˈbærətri/ | n. | 诉讼教唆；唆使兴讼；海损 |  The illegal generation of lawsuits; vexatious litigation. In maritime law, any fraudulent or criminal act committed by the master or crew of a vessel that injures the owner of the vessel or its cargo. |  The lawyer was accused of barratry for encouraging frivolous lawsuits.  该律师因怂恿无意义的诉讼而被指控犯有诉讼教唆罪。 | litigation, lawsuit abuse |  cooperation,  mediation | noun
