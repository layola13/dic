microbiome: /ˌmaɪkroʊˈbaɪoʊm/ | n. | 微生物组，微生物群系 |  The collective genomes of the microorganisms (bacteria, fungi, viruses, etc.) that live in a particular environment, such as the human gut.  It refers to all the genetic material within a microbiota. | The gut microbiome plays a crucial role in human health. 肠道微生物组在人体健康中起着至关重要的作用。|  microbiota, microflora | macrobiome | noun
