hellbender:/ˈhɛlbɛndər/| n.|美洲大鲵，地狱弯腰者|A large, fully aquatic salamander (Cryptobranchus alleganiensis) of the eastern United States, having wrinkled skin and feathery gills retained throughout life.  It is also known as the "snot otter" or "devil dog".|例句：The hellbender is a fascinating creature found in clean, fast-flowing streams. 美洲大鲵是一种迷人的生物，生活在干净、流速快的溪流中。

例句：Hellbenders are considered an indicator species, reflecting the health of their aquatic environment.  美洲大鲵被认为是一种指示物种，反映了其水生环境的健康状况。|近义词：giant salamander, water dog|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
