ETYMOLOGY->

From four + footed.  From Middle English *fourfotyd*, from Old English *fēowerfōted*, from *fēower* (“four”) + *fōt* (“foot”) + *-ed*.  Compare Dutch *viervoetig*, German *vierfüßig*.
