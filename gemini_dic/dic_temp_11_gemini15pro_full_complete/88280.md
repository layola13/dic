hinterland:/ˈhɪntərlænd/ | n. | 内地，腹地，偏远地区 |A remote and undeveloped area lying beyond a coast or the banks of a river.  The area surrounding and served by a port or other center of population. | The farm is located in the hinterland of the province. 这个农场位于该省的内陆地区。 |  Example:  The explorers ventured deep into the uncharted hinterland. 探险家们深入到未知的腹地。


|近义词：backcountry, outback, bush, wilderness, interior | 反义词：foreland, coast, metropolis | 词性：名词
