mardi gras:/ˈmɑːrdi ɡrɑː/ (US), /ˈmɑːdi ɡrɑː/ (UK)| n.|狂欢节，忏悔星期二|A festive day celebrated in some countries on Shrove Tuesday, the day before Ash Wednesday, which marks the beginning of Lent. It typically involves parades, costumes, and feasting.|例句：New Orleans is famous for its Mardi Gras celebrations. 新奥尔良以其狂欢节庆祝活动而闻名。

例句：We went to a Mardi Gras parade last year. 我们去年去了一个狂欢节游行。

近义词：carnival, festival|反义词：Lent|词性：名词

mardi-gras:/ˈmɑːrdi ɡrɑː/ (US), /ˈmɑːdi ɡrɑː/ (UK)| adj.|狂欢节的|Relating to or characteristic of Mardi Gras.|例句：Mardi Gras beads are a popular souvenir. 狂欢节珠子是一种很受欢迎的纪念品。

例句：We wore Mardi Gras masks to the party. 我们戴着狂欢节面具去参加聚会。

近义词：carnival, festive|反义词：solemn|词性：形容词
