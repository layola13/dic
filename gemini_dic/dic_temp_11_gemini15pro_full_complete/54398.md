disimpassioned:/ˌdɪsɪmˈpæʃənd/
adjective
冷静的；不带感情的；不动感情的
Not influenced by strong emotion, and therefore able to think clearly.

例句：
She delivered a disimpassioned account of the accident.
她冷静地讲述了事故的经过。

例句：
The judge listened to the arguments and delivered a disimpassioned verdict.
法官听取了双方的辩论，并作出了冷静的判决。

近义词：
calm, composed, cool, detached, impartial, objective, unemotional

反义词：
emotional, passionate, fervent, ardent

词性
adjective


词源：
From dis- "not" + impassioned.  The word "impassioned" itself comes from "passion" with its roots in the Latin "pati" meaning "to suffer or endure."  The prefix "dis-" negates the meaning of passionate, resulting in "not passionate."
