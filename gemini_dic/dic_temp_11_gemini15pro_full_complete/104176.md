junket:/ˈdʒʌŋkɪt/ | n. | 旅行，郊游；(官员等的)公费旅游 | A trip, often one taken for pleasure by an official at public expense. | The senator went on a junket to Hawaii. 参议员到夏威夷公费旅游去了。|Synonyms: trip, excursion, outing, jaunt | Antonyms: work, business trip | noun

junket:/ˈdʒʌŋkɪt/ | vi. |  进行公费旅游；盛宴 | To go on a pleasure trip, especially one paid for with public funds; to feast. | The committee members junketted around Europe for a month. 委员会成员在欧洲公费旅游了一个月。| Synonyms: travel, tour, feast | Antonyms: work, stay home | verb
