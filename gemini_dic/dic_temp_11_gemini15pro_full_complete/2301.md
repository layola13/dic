adeptness:/əˈdɛptnəs/
n.
熟练，精通
Skillfulness and proficiency in a particular area.
Her adeptness at playing the piano impressed everyone. 她弹钢琴的熟练程度给大家留下了深刻的印象。
近义词：skill, proficiency, mastery, expertise
反义词：ineptness, clumsiness, awkwardness
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From adept + -ness.  Adept comes from Latin *adeptus*, past participle of *adipisci* "to attain," from *ad* "to" + *apisci* "to reach."
