kikoi:/kiˈkɔɪ/ (US) /kiˈkɔɪ/ (UK)| n. |基科伊；基科伊布|A large, brightly colored piece of cotton cloth, typically worn as a sarong or beach wrap, originating from the Swahili coast of East Africa. |She wrapped the kikoi around her waist as a sarong. 她把基科伊布裹在腰上当作沙滩巾。|近义词：sarong, beach wrap, pareo |反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly formal wear)|noun
