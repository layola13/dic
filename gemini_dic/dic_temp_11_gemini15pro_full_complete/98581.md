institutionally:/ˌɪnstɪˈtuːʃənəli/
adjective
体制地，制度上地
In a way that relates to or is part of a large and important organization, such as a government, university, or bank.
The problem is being addressed institutionally. 这个问题正在体制内得到解决。
The library is institutionally affiliated with the university.  该图书馆隶属于这所大学。

systematically, organizationally, formally
informally, individually
