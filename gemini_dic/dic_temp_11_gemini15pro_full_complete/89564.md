honorary:/ˈɑːnərəri/ (US), /ˈɒnərəri/ (UK)| adj.|荣誉的；名誉的|Conferred as an honour, without the usual requirements or duties.|She received an honorary doctorate from Harvard University. 她获得了哈佛大学的荣誉博士学位。|Example: He was an honorary member of the club. 他是该俱乐部的荣誉会员。|Synonyms: titular, nominal|Antonyms: earned, substantive|adjective

honourary:/ˈɑːnərəri/ (US), /ˈɒnərəri/ (UK)| n.|荣誉职位；荣誉称号|A title or position conferred as an honour, without the usual requirements or duties.  This usage is less common than the adjective form. |His honorary was revoked after the scandal. 他的荣誉称号在丑闻后被撤销了。|Example:  He accepted the honorary with gratitude. 他感激地接受了这个荣誉称号。|Synonyms: accolade, distinction|Antonyms: dishonor, disgrace|noun
