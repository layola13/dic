dentiloquist:/vɛnˈtrɪləkwɪst/| n.|腹语术表演者|A person who can speak or utter sounds so that they seem to come from somewhere else, especially with the mouth closed, as if from a dummy.|The ventriloquist made the dummy appear to be talking.|Synonym:  dummy operator|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "orator" in the sense of someone who speaks openly)|noun
