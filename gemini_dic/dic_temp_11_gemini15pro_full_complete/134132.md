nonimperious:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpɪəriəs/
adjective
非专横的，不傲慢的，谦逊的
Not domineering or overbearing; modest.
She spoke in a nonimperious tone, requesting rather than demanding assistance. 她以一种不专横的语气说话，请求而不是要求帮助。
He has a nonimperious leadership style, preferring collaboration to giving orders. 他的领导风格不专横，喜欢合作而不是发号施令。
Synonyms: humble, modest, unassuming, unpretentious
Antonyms: imperious, domineering, arrogant, haughty
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + imperious (domineering, overbearing).  The word "imperious" ultimately comes from the Latin "imperium" (command, power).
