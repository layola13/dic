helically:/ˈhelɪkəli/
adverb
螺旋形地；螺旋式地
in a helical shape or form; spirally.
The DNA molecule is helically structured.  DNA分子呈螺旋结构。
The vine wound helically around the tree. 藤蔓螺旋状地缠绕在树上。
Synonyms: spirally, corkscrew-like, coiled
Antonyms: straight, linear
词性: 副词
