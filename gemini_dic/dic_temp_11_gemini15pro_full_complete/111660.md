lochan:/ˈlɒxən/ (UK) /ˈlɑːxən/ (US)| n.|小湖；湖湾|A small lake, especially one in Scotland.|We paddled our canoe across the lochan. 我们用独木舟划过小湖。|近义词:lake, pond, tarn|反义词:ocean, sea|名词

词源:
From Scottish Gaelic *lochan, diminutive of loch (“lake”). Ultimately from Proto-Celtic *lokus (“pool, lake”).
