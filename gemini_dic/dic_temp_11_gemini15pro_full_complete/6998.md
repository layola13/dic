androgenic:/ˌæn.drəˈdʒɛn.ɪk/
adjective
雄激素的；产生雄性特征的
Relating to or tending to promote male characteristics.
Androgenic hormones are responsible for the development of male secondary sexual characteristics. 雄激素负责男性第二性征的发育。
Androgenic steroids can have masculinizing effects on women. 雄激素类固醇会对女性产生男性化的影响。
synonyms: masculinizing, virilizing
antonyms: estrogenic, feminizing
词性: adjective
