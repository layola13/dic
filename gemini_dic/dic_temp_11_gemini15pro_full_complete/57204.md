down-with:/ˌdaʊnˈwɪð/ | adj. | 打倒；反对；消灭 | Expressing opposition to or advocating the overthrow of someone or something. |  Down with the dictator! 打倒独裁者！| 近义词: abolish, overthrow, defeat, oppose | 反义词: support, uphold, promote | 词性: adjective (primarily used in slogans and chants)

down-with: |  |  |  Origin:  From the phrasal verb "down with," meaning to overthrow or suppress.  The hyphenated form is primarily used as an attributive adjective in slogans and chants. | Down with tyranny!  消灭暴政！| 近义词: against, opposed to | 反义词: for, in favor of | 词性: attributive adjective
