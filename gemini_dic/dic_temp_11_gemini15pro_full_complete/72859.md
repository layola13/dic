folkmoter:/ˈfoʊkməʊtər/| n. |民众大会，国民大会|An assembly of the people of a district or country, especially in Scandinavian countries, for open-air discussion of public affairs.  A large public meeting or assembly.|例句：The folkmoter voted to raise taxes to fund the new school. 国民大会投票决定提高税收以为新学校提供资金。

例句：The annual folkmoter was held in the town square. 年度国民大会在城镇广场举行。|近义词：assembly, meeting, gathering, congress, council|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *fólkmót, from fólk ("people") + mót ("meeting").  Cognate with Danish folkemøde.
