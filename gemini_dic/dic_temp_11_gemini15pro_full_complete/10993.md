arboreally:/ɑːrˈbɔːriəli/
adjective
树栖地；在树上
living in trees; of or relating to trees
例句：Arboreal animals, such as monkeys, spend most of their lives in trees. 树栖动物，例如猴子，一生中的大部分时间都在树上度过。

例句：The sloth is a slow-moving arboreal mammal. 树懒是一种行动缓慢的树栖哺乳动物。

近义词：tree-dwelling, arboreous
反义词：terrestrial, ground-dwelling
词性：adjective
