capricious:/kəˈprɪʃəs/ | adj. |反复无常的；任性的|Given to sudden and unaccountable changes of mood or behavior.|例句：The capricious weather made it difficult to plan a picnic. 反复无常的天气使得很难计划野餐。|例句：He's such a capricious boss, I never know what to expect. 他真是个反复无常的老板，我从来不知道他会做什么。|近义词：fickle, erratic, impulsive, whimsical, mercurial|反义词：consistent, steady, reliable, predictable|词性:形容词
词源:Late 16th century: from French capricieux, from Italian capriccioso, from capriccio ‘sudden start’ (see capriccio).
