cryometer:/kraɪˈɒmɪtər/ | n. |低温计，深冷温度计 | An instrument for measuring very low temperatures. |  A cryometer is used to measure the freezing point of liquids. 低温计用于测量液体的凝固点。|  The researchers used a cryometer to determine the exact temperature of the liquid nitrogen. 研究人员使用低温计来确定液氮的确切温度。| synonyms: thermometer (for low temperatures) | antonyms: N/A | noun
