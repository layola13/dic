guillemot:/ˈɡɪləmɒt/ (UK) /ˈɡɪləˌmɑːt/ (US)| n. |海鸠|A seabird of northern and Arctic waters, with black and white plumage and short wings. They are related to the auks.|例句：The cliffs were teeming with guillemots. 悬崖上到处都是海鸠。

例句：The guillemots dived into the sea to catch fish. 海鸠潜入海中捕鱼。|近义词：auk, murre|反义词: N/A (No opposite for a type of bird)|词性:noun
