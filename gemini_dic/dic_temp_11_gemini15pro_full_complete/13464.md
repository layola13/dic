atrocious:/əˈtroʊʃəs/
adjective
极坏的；糟糕的；残暴的
extremely wicked, cruel, or brutal; shockingly bad
The food in that restaurant was atrocious. 那家餐馆的饭菜糟透了。
近义词：terrible, awful, dreadful, appalling, heinous
反义词：excellent, wonderful, superb, magnificent
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *atrox*, from *āter* ("black, dark").  Originally meant "extremely dark, black," later developing the sense of "cruel, savage" and finally "horribly bad."
