ingathering:/ˈɪnɡæðərɪŋ/
n.|收获；聚集；收集|The ingathering of the crops is an important event in the farming calendar. 农作物的收获是农业日历上的一个重要事件。|
例句：The autumn ingathering brought a bountiful harvest. 秋季的收获带来了丰收。
例句：There was a large ingathering of people for the festival. 节日聚集了很多人。|近义词：harvest, gathering, collection, accumulation|反义词：dispersal, scattering|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *ingederinge, from Old English ingaderung (“gathering, assembly”), equivalent to in- +‎ gathering.
