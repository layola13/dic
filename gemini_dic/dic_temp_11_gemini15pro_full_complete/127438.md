nebbishly:/ˈnɛbɪʃli/| adv.|懦弱地，胆怯地|In a timid, nervous, or ineffectual manner; like a nebbish.|He nebbishly asked her to dance, his voice barely above a whisper. 他胆怯地邀请她跳舞，声音细若蚊蝇。|近义词:timidly, nervously, sheepishly|反义词:boldly, confidently, assertively|词性:副词

nebbish:/ˈnɛbɪʃ/| n.|懦夫，窝囊废|A timid, nervous, or ineffectual person.|He was such a nebbish, always apologizing for everything. 他真是个窝囊废，总是为所有事情道歉。|近义词:wimp, milquetoast, weakling|反义词: extrovert, go-getter|词性:名词

Etymology: From Yiddish נעבעך (nebekh), meaning "poor thing" or "unfortunately".
