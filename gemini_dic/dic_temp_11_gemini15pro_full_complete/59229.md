dysury:/ˈdɪsjʊri/ (US), /dɪsˈjʊəri/ (UK)| n.| 排尿困难，尿痛|Painful or difficult urination.|Example:  She experienced dysury after her surgery. 她手术后出现了排尿困难。

Example: The patient complained of dysury and frequency. 病人主诉排尿困难和尿频。|近义词:strangury, painful urination|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "easy urination" or absence of pain during urination)|词性: noun
