hyper-salinity:/ˌhaɪpər səˈlɪnɪti/ | n. |高盐度，超盐度 |  Excessive salinity; a concentration of salt in water or soil that is higher than normal. It can have negative impacts on aquatic life and agriculture. | The hyper-salinity of the Dead Sea makes it difficult for most organisms to survive. 死海的高盐度使大多数生物难以生存。

The hyper-salinity of the soil stunted the growth of the crops. 土壤的高盐度阻碍了农作物的生长。|近义词： high salinity, excessive salinity | 反义词：low salinity, hyposalinity |词性: 名词
