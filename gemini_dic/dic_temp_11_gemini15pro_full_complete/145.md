abased:/əˈbeɪst/| adj.| 卑微的，低下的，屈辱的|Brought low in rank, prestige, or esteem; humiliated.|例句：He felt abased after failing the exam. 他考试不及格后感到很卑微。|例句：The abased king was forced to beg for mercy.  被贬黜的国王被迫乞求怜悯。|近义词：humiliated, degraded, humbled, ashamed, mortified|反义词：exalted, proud, dignified, honored|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle French *abaser*, from *a-* (from Latin *ad-*, "to") + *baser* "to lower," from Late Latin *bassus* "low." Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰedʰ-*, "bottom, base."
