deign:/deɪn/
verb
屈尊，惠允
to do something that one considers to be beneath one's dignity; condescend.
The CEO rarely deigns to speak to her employees directly.  这位CEO很少屈尊直接和员工说话。
condescend, stoop, lower oneself
disdain, refuse, decline
