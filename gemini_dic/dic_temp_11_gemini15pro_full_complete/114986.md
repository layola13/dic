mand:/mænd/ | n. | 命令；指令；委托管理；托管 | A command or authorization to act in a particular way on behalf of another. | The king issued a mand to his knights to defend the realm. 国王向他的骑士们下达了保卫王国的命令。| synonym: command, order, instruction, decree, directive | antonym: request, plea, entreaty | noun

mand:/mænd/ | v. | 命令；指令；委托管理 | To command or authorize to act in a particular way on behalf of another. | He was mandated by the court to attend anger management classes. 法院命令他参加愤怒管理课程。| synonym: command, order, instruct, direct, commission | antonym: request, plead, entreat | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *mandāre*, present active infinitive of *mandō* ("I entrust, commit, order"). Related to *manus* ("hand") and *dō* ("I give"). The notion is of "giving something into someone's hand."
