buffoonish:/bəˈfuːnɪʃ/| adj.|滑稽可笑的；丑角般的|Resembling or characteristic of a buffoon; clownish.|His buffoonish behavior embarrassed his family. 他的滑稽行为让他的家人感到尴尬。|The comedian's buffoonish antics had the audience in stitches.  这位喜剧演员滑稽的动作让观众捧腹大笑。|近义词：clownish, ludicrous, silly, foolish, comical|反义词：serious, dignified, solemn, sober|词性:形容词
Etymology: From buffoon + -ish. Buffoon comes from Italian buffone "jester, clown," from buffa "jest, joke."
