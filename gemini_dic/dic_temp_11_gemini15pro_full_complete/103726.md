joshing:/ˈdʒoʊʃɪŋ/
verb
开玩笑，戏弄
Engaging in playful teasing or mockery.
例句：They were just joshing around, don't take it seriously. 他们只是在开玩笑，别当真。

例句：He was joshing me about my new haircut. 他拿我的新发型开玩笑。

近义词：teasing, kidding, joking, bantering
反义词：serious, sincere
词性：verb (动词)
