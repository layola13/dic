non-aqueous:/ˌnɒnˈeɪkwiəs/| adj.|非水性的，非水溶性的|Not containing water; not dissolved in or requiring the presence of water.|例句：Non-aqueous solvents are used in many industrial processes. 许多工业过程使用非水溶剂。|例句：The reaction takes place in a non-aqueous environment. 该反应发生在非水环境中。|近义词：anhydrous|反义词:aqueous|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From non- + aqueous.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "aqueous" relates to water.
