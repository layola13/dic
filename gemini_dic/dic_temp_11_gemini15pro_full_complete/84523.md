hamburgers:/ˈhæmbɜːrɡərz/ (US), /ˈhæmbɜːɡəz/ (UK)
n.
汉堡包
A ground beef patty, usually grilled, served on a bun or round roll and often garnished with various ingredients.
I ordered two hamburgers with cheese. 我点了两个芝士汉堡包。
近义词：burgers
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps a healthy salad could be considered a contrasting food choice.)
词性: noun (plural)


Etymology:
From Hamburg + -er + -s. Named after the German city of Hamburg, where a similar dish was popular.  The term initially referred to a patty made of ground beef, typically from Hamburg cows, but has evolved to encompass a wider range of variations.
