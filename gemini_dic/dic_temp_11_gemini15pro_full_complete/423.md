ablactation:/ˌæblækˈteɪʃən/
noun
断奶；戒奶
The cessation of breastfeeding.  The process of weaning a young mammal from its mother's milk.
The ablactation of the calf was completed successfully. 小牛的断奶顺利完成。
近义词: weaning, cessation of breastfeeding
反义词: lactation, breastfeeding
词性: noun

Etymology:
From Latin *ablactatio*, from ablactāre (“to wean”), from ab- (“from, away from”) + lactāre (“to suckle, give milk”).  Lactāre is from lac (“milk”).
