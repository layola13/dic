emmarble:/ɪmˈmɑːrbl/| vt.|使大理石化，使像大理石|To make something resemble marble in appearance or hardness.|They emmarbled the walls of the bathroom. 他们把浴室的墙壁弄得像大理石一样。|近义词：marbleize, petrify|反义词: soften|verb
