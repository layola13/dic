gromet:/ˈɡrɑːmɪt/ (US), /ˈɡrɒmɪt/ (UK)| n.|索环，金属孔眼，衬套|A ring or edge of metal, plastic, or rubber used to reinforce a hole in a sheet or panel and to prevent ropes, wires, or cables passing through it from fraying or chafing.|例句：The grommets in the sails prevented the ropes from tearing the canvas. 帆上的索环防止绳索撕裂帆布。

例句：He used a grommet to reinforce the hole for the electrical cord. 他用一个索环加固电线的孔。|近义词：eyelet, bushing, ring, washer|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "unreinforced hole")|词性:noun
