missupposing:/ˌmɪs.səˈpoʊ.zɪŋ/
verb
错误地假设；误以为
incorrectly supposing or assuming something
He missupposed that she would be happy to see him, but she wasn't. 他误以为她会很高兴见到他，但她并不高兴。
She missupposed his intentions and became angry with him. 她误解了他的意图，并生了他的气。
misunderstanding, misinterpreting, misconstruing
presuming, assuming correctly
verb
