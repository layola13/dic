booked-out:/bʊktˈaʊt/
adjective
客满的；预订一空的
fully reserved or booked; having no vacancies
例句：The hotel is booked-out for the weekend.  这家酒店周末客满了。

例句：The show was completely booked-out. 这场演出完全预订一空了。
近义词：sold-out, full, reserved
反义词：available, vacant
词性:adjective


booked-out:/bʊktˈaʊt/
verb
预订满
to reserve all available accommodations, tickets, etc.
例句：The concert was booked-out weeks in advance.  这场音乐会提前几周就预订满了。

例句：We booked-out the entire restaurant for the party. 我们为聚会预订了整个餐厅。
近义词：reserve, sell out
反义词：-
词性:verb (past participle used as adjective)
