act-out:/ˈækt aʊt/| phrasal verb | 表演出来；付诸行动；将（感情）表现出来 | To express (an emotion, fantasy, etc) by acting it out, as in a play. |  The children were encouraged to act out the story they had just heard. 孩子们被鼓励把刚刚听到的故事表演出来。

|近义词：perform, enact, express, demonstrate, portray|反义词：suppress, internalize|词性: 动词短语
