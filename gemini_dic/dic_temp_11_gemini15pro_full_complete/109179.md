legitimizations:/ləˌdʒɪtɪmaɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n. |合法化，正名，认可|The act of making something legitimate; the state of being legitimized.  The process of providing justification or sanction for something.| The legitimization of his claim to the throne took many years.  他王位继承权的合法化花了许多年。|近义词：justifications, validations, approvals|反义词：invalidations, condemnations|noun
