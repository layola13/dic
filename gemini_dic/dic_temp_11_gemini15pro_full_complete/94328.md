impeyan:/ˈɪmpiən/ | n. | 伊贝母鸡，喜马拉雅虹雉 | A large pheasant (Lophophorus impejanus) of the Himalayas, the male of which has brilliant iridescent plumage.  |  Example: The impeyan is the national bird of Nepal. 伊贝母鸡是尼泊尔的国鸟。 | Example:  We were lucky enough to spot a male impeyan displaying his vibrant feathers. 我们很幸运地看到一只雄性伊贝母鸡展示它鲜艳的羽毛。 | 近义词: Himalayan monal (更具体的名称), pheasant (更广泛的类别) | 反义词:  N/A (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑其他鸟类) | 词性: noun
