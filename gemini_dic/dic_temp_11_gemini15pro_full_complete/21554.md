bluenoser:/ˈbluːˌnoʊzər/| n.|蓝鼻子人，保守派，新斯科舍省人|A person from Nova Scotia, Canada, or, by extension, someone considered to be straitlaced or conservative.|He's a bluenoser from way back, always careful with his money. 他是个老派的蓝鼻子人，花钱总是很谨慎。|Synonyms: Nova Scotian, conservative, prude|Antonyms: liberal, radical|noun

bluenoser:/ˈbluːˌnoʊzər/| adj.|保守的，拘谨的|Conservative, straitlaced.|She has a rather bluenoser attitude towards modern art. 她对现代艺术的态度相当保守。|Synonyms: conservative, prudish, puritanical|Antonyms: liberal, permissive|adjective
