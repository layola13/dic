guilloched:/ˈɡɪləʃt/ | adj. |  雕花的，刻有细密花纹的 | Decorated with a very fine and intricate pattern of interwoven lines, typically produced by a machine. | The antique watch had a beautifully guilloched face. 这块古董手表有着精美雕花的表面。 | 近义词: engraved, etched, chased | 反义词: plain, smooth | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From French *guilloché*, past participle of *guillocher*, from the name of the inventor, Guillot, who perfected the engine used for the process.
