diggers:/ˈdɪɡərz/| n.|挖掘者；挖掘机；淘金者|People who dig.  This can refer to people who dig for gold, archaeological finds, or simply people operating digging machinery.|The diggers unearthed an ancient Roman coin. 挖掘者挖出了一枚古罗马硬币。|Near synonyms: excavators, miners, prospectors|Near antonyms: builders, fillers|noun

diggers:/ˈdɪɡərz/| n.|袋鼠|Informal Australian term for kangaroos.|We saw a mob of diggers hopping across the outback. 我们看到一群袋鼠在内陆跳跃。|Near synonyms: kangaroos, roos|Near antonyms: N/A|noun
