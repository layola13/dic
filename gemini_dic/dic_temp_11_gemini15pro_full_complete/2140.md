adaptorial: /ˌædəpˈtɔːriəl/ | adj. |  广告式编辑内容；软文；| An adaptorial is a form of advertising that is designed to look like editorial content. It is often used in magazines and newspapers, and it can be difficult to distinguish from genuine editorial content. | Example: The magazine ran an adaptorial about the new car. 这本杂志刊登了一篇关于这款新车的软文。
Example:  Many readers couldn't distinguish the advertorial from a genuine review. 很多读者无法区分这篇软文和真正的评论。| 近义词: advertorial, infomercial | 反义词: editorial | 词性: adjective
