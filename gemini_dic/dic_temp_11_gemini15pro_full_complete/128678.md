nicotinic:/ˌnɪkəˈtɪnɪk/ | adj. | 尼古丁的；烟碱的 | Relating to or resembling the action of nicotine.  It specifically refers to a type of acetylcholine receptor that is stimulated by nicotine. | Nicotinic receptors are found in the brain and are involved in addiction. 尼古丁受体存在于大脑中，并与成瘾有关。|  Nicotine exerts its effects by binding to nicotinic acetylcholine receptors. 尼古丁通过与烟碱型乙酰胆碱受体结合而发挥作用。 |  cholinergic, nicotinergic |  non-nicotinic | adjective
