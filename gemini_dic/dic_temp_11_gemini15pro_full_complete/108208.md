laudanine:/ˈlɔːdənɪn/ | n. |劳丹宁 | A crystalline alkaloid, C20H25NO4, found in opium. It is a narcotic analgesic. | Laudanine is one of the minor alkaloids found in opium. 劳丹宁是鸦片中的一种次要生物碱。 | Laudanine, while present in opium, is not as potent as morphine. 虽然劳丹宁存在于鸦片中，但它不如吗啡有效。 | 近义词: alkaloid, opiate | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

词源: From Latin *laudanum* (opium preparation), from Greek *ladanon* (a gum resin).
