otics:/ˈɑːtɪks/ | n. |学科, 学问|A branch of knowledge or study, often relating to a specific sense organ or system, especially hearing.  It's usually used in combination with a prefix or other word to create specific terms like "optometrics" (study of vision) or "acoustics" (study of sound).|Example:  He specializes in audiology and related otics. 他专门研究听力学和相关的学科。|Synonym: science, study, discipline|Antonym: ignorance|n.
