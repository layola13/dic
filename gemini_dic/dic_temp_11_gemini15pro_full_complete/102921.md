jaycees:/ˈdʒeɪsiːz/| n.|美国青年商会会员|Members of the United States Junior Chamber of Commerce (formerly the Junior Chamber International).  A leadership training and civic organization for young men and women.|例句：The Jaycees organized the town's annual Christmas parade. 美国青年商会组织了该镇一年一度的圣诞游行。|例句：She joined the Jaycees to develop her leadership skills. 她加入美国青年商会是为了培养她的领导能力。|近义词：young professionals, civic leaders|反义词: (No direct antonym.  Could consider "retirees" in a loose sense)|词性:noun
