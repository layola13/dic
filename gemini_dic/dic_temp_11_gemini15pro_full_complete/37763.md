compassioning:/kəmˈpæʃəniŋ/
adjective
富有同情心的；表示怜悯的
showing or feeling compassion
例句：Her compassioning nature led her to volunteer at the homeless shelter. 她富有同情心的天性使她自愿在流浪者收容所工作。

例句：He gave a compassioning look to the beggar. 他同情地看了乞丐一眼。
近义词：compassionate, sympathetic, empathetic, merciful
反义词：uncaring, callous, indifferent, apathetic
词性: adjective

词源: From the Late Latin *compassionare, from Latin compassio (“sympathy”)


注意事项:
Although "compassioning" is grammatically correct, "compassionate" is much more frequently used as an adjective.  "Compassioning" is less common and might sound slightly awkward in modern usage.  It's more common to see "compassioning" used as a verb in archaic texts or poetry.
