gyrator:/ˈdʒaɪreɪtər/ | n. | 旋转器，回转器；陀螺仪；电抗器 | A device, typically electrical, that exhibits gyroscopic properties or effects, or converts a signal from one form to another.  It is often used in circuits to simulate inductors or create non-reciprocal transfer functions. |  A gyrator can be used to simulate an inductor in a circuit.  一个旋转器可以用来在电路中模拟一个电感器。 | The gyrator stabilized the aircraft's navigation system.  旋转器稳定了飞机的导航系统。 | synonym: rotator, gyro, inductor simulator | antonym: N/A | noun
