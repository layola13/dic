claqueur:/klæˈkɜːr/
noun
喝彩员，拍手党
A person hired to applaud a performer or performance, especially to create a false impression of enthusiastic reception.
The claqueurs were instructed to cheer wildly after the aria.  喝彩员被指示在咏叹调之后热烈欢呼。
Synonyms: applauder, hired hand
Antonyms: critic, detractor
