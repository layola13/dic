desensitization:/diːˌsɛnsɪtaɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.|脱敏，减敏|The process of reducing sensitivity or reactivity to a stimulus, especially an allergen or emotional trigger.|Exposure therapy is a common method of desensitization for phobias. 暴露疗法是治疗恐惧症的一种常见脱敏方法。|
近义词:habituation, acclimatization, adaptation|反义词:sensitization|词性:名词

desensitization:/diːˌsɛnsɪtaɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.|脱敏，减敏|The reduction or elimination of allergic sensitivity.|Desensitization to certain allergens can be achieved through immunotherapy. 对某些过敏原的脱敏可以通过免疫疗法来实现。|
近义词:immunotherapy, hyposensitization|反义词:sensitization|词性:名词
