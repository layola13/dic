moho:/ˈmoʊhoʊ/| n. |(夏威夷)吸蜜鸟|The Moho are a group of extinct Hawaiian honeycreepers.|The ʻōʻō was a type of moho. ʻōʻō是一种吸蜜鸟。|Synonyms: Hawaiian honeycreeper|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
