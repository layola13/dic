helplessly:/ˈhɛlplɪsli/| adv.|无助地，绝望地|In a way that shows one is unable to act without help; in a hopeless manner. |She looked at him helplessly. 她无助地看着他。|Synonyms: powerlessly, weakly, hopelessly|Antonyms: capably, competently, effectively|adverb

helpless:/ˈhɛlplɪs/| adj.|无助的，无能的|Unable to manage independently or to act without help.|The baby birds were helpless without their mother. 没有妈妈，小鸟们很无助。|Synonyms: powerless, dependent, vulnerable|Antonyms: capable, independent, strong|adjective
