ascertaining:/ˌæsərˈteɪnɪŋ/
verb
查明，确定
the act of finding something out for certain; making sure of
例句：They are ascertaining the cause of the fire. 他们正在查明火灾原因。

例句：We are ascertaining the facts of the case. 我们正在确定案件的事实。

近义词：determining, establishing, verifying, confirming
反义词：guessing, supposing, assuming
词性：Verb (present participle of ascertain)


ascertain:/ˌæsərˈteɪn/
verb
查明，确定
find (something) out for certain; make sure of
例句：I ascertained that the train would be on time. 我确定火车会准时到达。

例句：The police are trying to ascertain the facts of the case. 警方正在努力查明案件的事实。

近义词：determine, establish, verify, confirm
反义词：guess, suppose, assume
词性：Verb

词源：From Old French *acertener, from a- (from Latin ad ‘to’) + certain ‘certain’
