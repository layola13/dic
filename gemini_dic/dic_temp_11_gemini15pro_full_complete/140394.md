oilstone:/ˈɔɪlˌstoʊn/| n.|油石|A fine-grained stone used for sharpening tools, especially knives and chisels, lubricated with oil.|He sharpened his chisel on the oilstone. 他在油石上磨他的凿子。|近义词：sharpening stone, whetstone|反义词: None|词性: noun
Etymology: From Old English *ælan stān (oil stone), from æle (oil) + stān (stone).
