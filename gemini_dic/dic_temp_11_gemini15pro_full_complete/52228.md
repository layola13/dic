diatonically:/ˌdaɪəˈtɒnɪkli/ /ˌdaɪəˈtɑːnɪkli/| adv.| 全音阶地，自然音阶地|  In a diatonic manner; using only notes belonging to a particular key without chromatic alteration.  Using only the seven notes of a standard major or minor scale.| The melody is diatonically simple. 这首旋律全音阶地简单。| The piece is written diatonically. 这首曲子是全音阶地写的。|Near Synonyms: stepwise, scale-wise|Antonyms: chromatically|词性:副词
