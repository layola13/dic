moonlike:/ˈmuːnlaɪk/ | adj. | 像月亮的；苍白的 | Resembling the moon, especially in being pale or luminous. | The landscape had a moonlike, ethereal quality.  这片风景有一种像月亮一样空灵的特质。| The actor's face was moonlike under the heavy makeup. 在浓妆之下，演员的脸像月亮一样苍白。 | 近义词: lunar, silvery, pale, ethereal | 反义词: sunlike, bright, colorful | 词性: adjective
