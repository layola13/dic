imbecilic:/ˌɪmbəˈsɪlɪk/ | adj. | 愚蠢的，低能的 |  Having or showing great foolishness or stupidity. Characterized by mental deficiency.  |  His imbecilic decision cost the company a fortune. 他的愚蠢决定让公司损失了一大笔钱。|  stupid, foolish, idiotic, moronic, asinine | intelligent, smart, bright, wise | adjective

imbecile:/ˈɪmbəˌsiːl/ | n. adj. | 低能者，愚蠢的 | A person of low intelligence.  Mentally deficient. |  He acted like an imbecile. 他表现得像个低能儿。 |  fool, idiot, moron, cretin | genius, intellectual | noun, adjective

词源:
From French imbécile, from Latin imbecillus “weak,” from in- “not” + baculum “staff, support.”  Originally meant physically weak, then mentally weak.
