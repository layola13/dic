abiding-by:/əˈbaɪdɪŋ baɪ/
|phrasal verb|遵守，遵循|The act of complying with rules, regulations, or agreements.|例句：They are abiding by the terms of the contract. 他们正在遵守合同条款。|近义词：complying with, adhering to, observing, following, respecting|反义词：violating, breaching, ignoring, disregarding|词性:动词短语

abide:/əˈbaɪd/
|verb|遵守，忍受|1. Accept or act in accordance with (a rule, decision, or recommendation). 2. Tolerate; endure.|词源：Old English ābīdan ‘remain, continue, endure,’ of Germanic origin; related to Dutch bijden and German beiden. |词性:动词

by:/baɪ/
|preposition|通过，依据|Identifying the agent performing an action.|词源：Old English bī, bi, be (preposition and adverb) ‘near, by, at, on, with, about,’ of Germanic origin.|词性:介词
