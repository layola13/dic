foregift:/ˈfɔːrɡɪft/
n.
预付款；定金；聘礼
A payment made in advance, especially as a deposit or installment.
He gave her a diamond ring as a foregift of his love. 他送给她一枚钻戒作为定情的信物。
synonyms: deposit, down payment, advance payment, earnest, handsel
antonyms: arrears, debt
