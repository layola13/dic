half-price:/ˌhɑːf ˈpraɪs/ /ˌhæf ˈpraɪs/| adj.|半价的|At half price, these shoes are a real bargain. 以半价出售，这些鞋真是便宜。|例句：These shoes are half-price today. 这些鞋子今天半价。|近义词：discounted, reduced|反义词：full-price, double-price|词性:形容词

half-price:/ˌhɑːf ˈpraɪs/ /ˌhæf ˈpraɪs/| adv.|半价地|Tickets are available half-price for students. 学生可以半价购买门票。|例句：The store is selling everything half-price. 这家商店所有商品半价出售。|近义词：at a discount, at a reduced price|反义词：at full price, at double price|词性:副词

half-price:/ˌhɑːf ˈpraɪs/ /ˌhæf ˈpraɪs/| n.|半价|Half-price is a great incentive to buy. 半价是一个很大的购买动力。|例句：The half-price sale attracted many customers. 半价促销吸引了许多顾客。|近义词：discount, reduction|反义词：full price, double price|词性:名词


词源: "half" comes from Old English "healf," meaning "side, part, portion." "Price" comes from Old French "pris," ultimately from Latin "pretium," meaning "value, worth, reward."
