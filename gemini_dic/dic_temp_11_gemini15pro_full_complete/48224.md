deconstructible:/ˌdiːkənˈstrʌktɪbl/
adjective
可解构的
able to be deconstructed; capable of being taken apart or analyzed into its constituent parts.
The building was designed to be easily deconstructible, allowing for the reuse of materials. 这栋建筑设计成易于解构，以便重复利用材料。
The argument is complex but deconstructible.  这个论点很复杂，但可以解构。
近义词:dismantlable, analyzable
反义词:non-deconstructible, indivisible
词性:adjective
词源:de- (prefix meaning "reverse" or "undo") + construct + -ible (suffix meaning "capable of being")  源自法语 déconstruire,  由 de- (down, from) + construire (to construct) 组成。
