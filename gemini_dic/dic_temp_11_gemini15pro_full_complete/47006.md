dashikis: /ˈdɑːʃiːkiːz/ (plural)  /ˈdɑːʃiːkiː/ (singular)
noun
达西基衫（一种宽松的，通常色彩鲜艳的非洲式套头衫）
A loose-fitting, brightly colored, pullover garment, usually with an ornate V-shaped collar, originating in West Africa.
He wore a vibrant dashiki to the festival. 他穿着鲜艳的达西基衫去参加节日。
She bought a beautiful dashiki from a local artisan. 她从当地一位工匠那里买了一件漂亮的达西基衫。
近义词:  kaftan, tunic, robe
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps) Western suit, formal attire
词性: n.
