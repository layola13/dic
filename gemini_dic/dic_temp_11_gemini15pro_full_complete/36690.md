collimation:/ˌkɒlɪˈmeɪʃn/ | n. | 准直，校准 |The act or process of making parallel; the state of being parallel, as rays of light.  Making rays of light or particles parallel. | The telescope requires collimation to produce sharp images. 这台望远镜需要准直才能产生清晰的图像。| synonyms: alignment, adjustment | antonyms: divergence, misalignment | noun

collimate:/ˈkɒlɪmeɪt/ | v. | 准直，校准 | To make (rays of light or particles) parallel. |  He collimated the laser beam before the experiment.  他在实验前准直了激光束。| synonyms: align, adjust, focus | antonyms: scatter, disperse | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "collineare" (to direct in a straight line), from "com-" (together) + "linea" (line).
