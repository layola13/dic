burdenous:/ˈbɜːrdənsəm/| adj.|繁重的，沉重的|Causing hardship or distress; troublesome.|Carrying such a heavy backpack all day is burdensome. 背着这么重的背包一整天很累人。|近义词：onerous, taxing, cumbersome|反义词：easy, light, simple|词性:形容词

词源:
From burden + -ous.  Ultimately from the Old French *burde, of Germanic origin.
