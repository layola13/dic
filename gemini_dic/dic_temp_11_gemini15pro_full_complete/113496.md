lystrosaurus:/ˌlɪstroʊˈsɔːrəs/ | n. |水龙兽| An extinct, pig-sized, herbivorous dicynodont therapsid reptile from the late Permian and early Triassic periods, notable for its survival of the Permian-Triassic extinction event. | Lystrosaurus fossils have been found in Antarctica, India, and South Africa. 水龙兽化石已在南极洲、印度和南非被发现。|  lystrosaurus is an example of a dicynodont. 水龙兽是二齿兽的一个例子。| 近义词:dicynodont (more general) | 反义词: N/A | noun
