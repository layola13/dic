distills:/dɪˈstɪlz/ | v. | 提炼；蒸馏；渗出；浓缩 | extracts the essential meaning or most important aspects of something; purifies a liquid by heating it so that it vaporizes, then cooling and condensing the vapor back into a liquid form. |  The writer distills years of research into a single volume.  这位作家将多年的研究成果浓缩成一本书。 | synonyms: extract, refine, concentrate, purify | antonyms: combine, mix, dilute, adulterate | verb
