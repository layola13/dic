feldspathic:/fɛldˈspæθɪk/ | adj. | 长石质的；似长石的 | Containing or resembling feldspar. | The geologist identified the rock as feldspathic. 地质学家将这块岩石鉴定为长石质的。| The soil in this region is feldspathic and rich in minerals. 该地区的土壤是长石质的，富含矿物质。 | 近义词:feldspathose | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-feldspathic) | 词性: adjective


词源: From German *feldspathisch*, from Feldspath (“feldspar”) +‎ -isch. Ultimately from Feld (“field”) + Spat (“spar”).
