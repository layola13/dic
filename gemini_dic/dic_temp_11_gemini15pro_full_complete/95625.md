incompatible:/ˌɪnkəmˈpætəbl/ | adj. | 不相容的；不兼容的；合不来的 | not able to exist or work together successfully | This software is incompatible with older operating systems.  这个软件与较旧的操作系统不兼容。| synonyms: conflicting, clashing, discordant, irreconcilable | antonyms: compatible, consistent, harmonious | adjective

incompatibility:/ˌɪnkəmˌpætɪˈbɪlɪti/ | n. | 不相容；不兼容；合不来 | the state of not being able to exist or work together successfully |  There's an incompatibility between our operating systems. 我们的操作系统之间存在不兼容性。| synonyms: conflict, clash, discord, irreconcilability | antonyms: compatibility, consistency, harmony | noun
