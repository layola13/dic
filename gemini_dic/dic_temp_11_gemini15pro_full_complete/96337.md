indigo:/ˈɪndɪɡoʊ/| n. adj.|靛蓝，靛蓝色|A dark blue dye obtained from plants, or the colour of this dye. |例句：She wore a dress of deep indigo. 她穿着一件深靛蓝色的连衣裙。|近义词：blue, sapphire|反义词:white, yellow|词性:名词，形容词

indigos:/ˈɪndɪɡoʊz/| n. |靛蓝染料，靛蓝色织物（indigo的复数形式）|The plural form of indigo, referring to items dyed with indigo or having an indigo color. |例句：The indigos were popular in the past. 过去靛蓝色织物很受欢迎。|近义词：blues, deep blues|反义词:whites, yellows|词性:名词
