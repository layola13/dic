examinations: /ɪɡˌzæmɪˈneɪʃənz/ | n. | 考试; 检查; 检验 |  A detailed inspection or investigation of something, typically to determine its nature, condition, or quality. Also, a formal test of a person's knowledge or proficiency in a particular subject or skill. | The students are worried about their final examinations. 学生们担心他们的期末考试。| synonyms: tests, assessments, inspections, checks, scrutinies | antonyms:  None (a true antonym is difficult to define; perhaps "oversight" in the sense of neglecting to examine) | noun
