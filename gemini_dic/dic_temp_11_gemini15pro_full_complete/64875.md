esquimalt:/ɛˈskwɪmɔːlt/
n.|埃斯奎莫尔特|A town in British Columbia, Canada, located on Vancouver Island. It is adjacent to Victoria, the provincial capital.  It was formerly a major naval base.|The Canadian Forces Base Esquimalt is still located there. 加拿大埃斯奎莫尔特军事基地仍然位于那里。|Victoria, Nanaimo|N/A|noun
Etymology: From the Lekwungen language, the language of local indigenous peoples. The precise meaning is debated, with suggestions ranging from "place of gradually shoaling water" to "a place where the camas bulbs are dug".
