crowd-pleasing:/ˈkraʊdˌpliːzɪŋ/ | adj. | 迎合大众的；讨好观众的 | Designed to gain popular approval, often at the expense of artistic merit. |  The movie was a crowd-pleasing romantic comedy. 这部电影是一部迎合大众的浪漫喜剧。 |  popular, commercial, entertaining |  highbrow, sophisticated, challenging | adjective

crowd-pleasing:/ˈkraʊdˌpliːzɪŋ/ | n. | 迎合大众的行为或事物 | Something designed to gain popular approval. | The band played a few crowd-pleasing covers. 乐队演奏了几首迎合大众的翻唱歌曲。 | popular entertainment, commercial success | niche appeal, artistic integrity | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "crowd" + "pleasing" (present participle of "please"). "Crowd" refers to a large number of people gathered together, and "please" means to cause enjoyment or satisfaction.
