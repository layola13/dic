dissolves:/dɪˈzɑːlvz/ (US), /dɪˈzɒlvz/ (UK)
verb
溶解，解散，消失
(of a solid) to become incorporated into a liquid so as to form a solution.  To end a partnership or official body. To fade away; disappear.
The sugar dissolves in water. 糖溶于水。
Parliament has been dissolved. 议会已经解散。
The image dissolved into the background. 图像渐渐消失在背景中。
近义词: melt, liquefy, disintegrate, disperse, fade
反义词: solidify, congeal, crystallize, materialize

词性: v.
词源: From Middle English *dissolven*, from Old French *dissoldre*, *dissoudre*, from Latin *dissolvere*, from *dis-* + *solvere* ("to loosen, release").
