imprison:/ɪmˈprɪzən/| vt.|监禁，关押|To confine someone in prison or as if in prison; to deprive of freedom of movement or action.|The government imprisoned the dissidents. 政府监禁了持不同政见者。|近义词：confine, jail, incarcerate, detain|反义词：release, liberate, free|verb

imprison:/ɪmˈprɪzən/| vt.|禁锢，束缚|To restrict severely, as by bonds or by force; to keep from freedom of action or expression.|His fear imprisoned him. 他的恐惧束缚了他。|近义词：restrict, constrain, trap|反义词：release, liberate, free|verb
