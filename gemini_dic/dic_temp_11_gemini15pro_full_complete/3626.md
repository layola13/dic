agency:/ˈeɪdʒənsi/| n.|代理，机构，力量，作用|a business or organization that provides a particular service, especially on behalf of other businesses or organizations.  The ability or capacity to act or exert power.|She found a job through an employment agency. 她通过一家职业介绍所找到了一份工作。|synonyms: organization, bureau, office, company, business, firm, establishment, concern, institute, service, department, branch, division, section, authority, power, control, influence, effect, action, operation|antonyms: inaction, powerlessness|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English agencie, from Old French agence, from Medieval Latin agentia (“action, operation, agency”), from Latin agō (“I do, I act, I drive”).
