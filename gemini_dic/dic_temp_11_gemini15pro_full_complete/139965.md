odontophorous:/ˌoʊdɒnˈtɒfərəs/
adjective
有齿的；具齿舌的
bearing teeth, specifically referring to the tooth-like structure on the radula (a rasping organ) of mollusks.
Snails are odontophorous creatures, using their radula to scrape food off surfaces.  蜗牛是有齿的生物，用它们的齿舌从表面刮取食物。
The odontophorous radula is a key feature of many mollusks. 齿舌是许多软体动物的关键特征。
近义词: toothed
反义词: toothless
词性: adjective
