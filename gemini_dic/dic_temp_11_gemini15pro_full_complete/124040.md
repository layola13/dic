一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃什么？
What does he do for a living? = 他以什么为生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? = 她正在做什么晚餐？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has he done to help? = 他做了些什么来帮忙？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has she been studying at university? = 她在大学里一直在学什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? = 我看到她的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had happened before the police arrived? = 警察到达之前发生了什么？
What had you eaten before you got sick? = 你生病之前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been working on before she quit her job? = 她辞职之前一直在做什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting tomorrow? = 明天会议上他们会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到今年年底你将完成什么？
What will they have finished by next week? = 到下周他们将完成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for the past ten years by 2033? = 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？
What would have happened if I hadn't been there? = 如果我不在那里，会发生什么？

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在对这个问题采取什么措施？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
There's nothing what I want. = 没有我想要的东西。
