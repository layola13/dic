nontolerated:/nɒnˈtɒləreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 不能容忍的，不被容忍的 | Not tolerated; unacceptable.  Something that is nontolerated is considered so unacceptable that it will not be allowed or endured. |  Discrimination is nontolerated in this company.  在这家公司，歧视是不能容忍的。|  unacceptable, inadmissible, prohibited, forbidden, disallowed | tolerated, permissible, acceptable, allowable, admissible | adjective
