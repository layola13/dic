mystifies:/ˈmɪstɪfaɪz/| v. | 使迷惑，使困惑 | To utterly bewilder or perplex someone; to make something difficult to understand. |例句：The magician's tricks mystified the audience. 魔术师的戏法使观众迷惑不解。

例句：The disappearance of the plane mystifies investigators. 飞机的消失使调查人员感到困惑。|近义词：bewilders, puzzles, confounds, perplexes |反义词：clarifies, explains, elucidates |词性：动词
