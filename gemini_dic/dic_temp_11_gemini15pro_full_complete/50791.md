descendant:/dɪˈsɛndənt/ | n. |后裔；子孙|A person who is related to someone who lived a long time ago; someone who is a later member of the same family.|例句：He is a descendant of George Washington. 他是乔治·华盛顿的后裔。

例句：Many of the town's residents are descendants of the original settlers.  这个镇上的许多居民都是最初定居者的后代。|近义词：offspring, heir, progeny, successor |反义词: ancestor, forebear |词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *descendant* (present participle of *descendre* "to descend"), from Latin *descendentem* (nominative *descendens*), present participle of *descendere* "to climb down, descend," from *de* "down" + *scandere* "to climb."
