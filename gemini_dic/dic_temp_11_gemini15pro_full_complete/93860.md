immobile: /ɪˈmoʊbəl/ (US), /ɪˈməʊbaɪl/ (UK) | adj. | 不动的，静止的；固定的 | Unable to move or be moved. |例句：The patient lay immobile in the bed. 病人一动不动地躺在床上。

例句：The car became immobile in the deep snow. 汽车在深雪中动弹不得。

近义词：motionless, static, fixed, stationary | 反义词：mobile, movable, active | 词性：形容词

immobile: /ɪˈmoʊbəl/ (US), /ɪˈməʊbaɪl/ (UK) | n. | 不动产 | Property, typically land or buildings, that cannot be moved.  | Example:  The family's wealth was primarily tied up in immobiles.  这个家庭的财富主要 tied up in 不动产。

Example: Investing in immobiles can be a good long-term strategy. 投资不动产可以是一个很好的长期策略。 | 近义词：real estate, property | 反义词：movable property, chattels | 词性：名词
