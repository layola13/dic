fringing:/ˈfrɪndʒɪŋ/| n. |边缘, 毛边, 流苏; 附加物; 次要部分|The act of forming a fringe or border.  A decorative border of threads left loose or formed into tassels or twists. An additional, less important, or secondary activity, aspect, or part of something.|The dress had fringing around the hem. 这件衣服的裙摆周围有流苏。|Synonyms: border, edge, rim, fringe, periphery|Antonyms: center, core, middle, interior|noun

fringing:/ˈfrɪndʒɪŋ/| adj. |边缘的，外围的|Located on or constituting a fringe; marginal. |The fringing reefs protect the island from erosion.  这些边缘的珊瑚礁保护岛屿免受侵蚀。|Synonyms: marginal, peripheral, outlying, bordering|Antonyms: central, core, inner, interior|adjective
