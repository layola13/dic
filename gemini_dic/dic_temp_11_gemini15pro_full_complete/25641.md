bunde:/ˈbʊndə/| n.| (German) bundle, package, bunch; (colloquial) a large amount; a lot; a mess; a shambles |A bunde of letters arrived this morning.  今天早上收到了一捆信。|Synonyms: bunch, collection, heap, lot, pile, stack|Antonyms: few, little, small amount|noun
bunde:/ˈbʊndə/| n.| (colloquial, often humorous) a person, especially a woman, perceived as old-fashioned, clumsy, or unattractive |She described her aunt as a real bunde. 她形容她姑姑是一个十足的老古董。|Synonyms: frump, dowdy|Antonyms: stylish, chic|noun
