ninjas:/ˈnɪndʒəz/| n.|忍者|A member of a feudal Japanese society of mercenary agents highly skilled in martial arts and stealth, who were hired for espionage and assassination.|The ninjas silently infiltrated the castle. 忍者悄悄地潜入了城堡。|近义词：assassin, spy, warrior|反义词:civilian, noncombatant|noun
