cheapskate:/ˈtʃiːpskeɪt/| n.|吝啬鬼，小气鬼|A person who is unwilling to spend money, especially on other people.|He's such a cheapskate, he never offers to buy a round of drinks. 他真是个吝啬鬼，从来不主动请客喝酒。|Synonyms: miser, tightwad, penny-pincher|Antonyms: spendthrift, philanthropist|Noun

cheapskate:/ˈtʃiːpskeɪt/| adj.|吝啬的，小气的|Unwilling to spend money, especially on other people.|That was a cheapskate thing to do.  那样做真小气。|Synonyms: stingy, miserly, parsimonious|Antonyms: generous, extravagant|Adjective
