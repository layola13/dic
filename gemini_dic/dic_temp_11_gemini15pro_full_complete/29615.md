categorized: /ˈkætəɡəˌraɪzd/ | v. |分类的，归类的 |  Past participle of *categorize*, meaning to put into a category or categories. | The books were categorized by genre. 这些书按类型分类。| classified, grouped, sorted | uncategorized, miscellaneous | Verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From *category* + *-ize*. *Category* comes from Late Latin *categoria*, from Greek *katēgoria* "accusation, prediction, category," from *katēgoros* "accuser, prosecutor," from *kata-* "down, against" + *agoreuein* "to speak in the assembly."
