nonbiodegradable:/ˌnɒnˌbaɪəʊdɪˈɡreɪdəbl/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˌbaɪoʊdɪˈɡreɪdəbl/
adjective
不可生物降解的
Not capable of being decomposed by bacteria or other living organisms.
Plastic bags are nonbiodegradable and contribute to environmental pollution.  塑料袋是不可生物降解的，会导致环境污染。
Many plastic products are nonbiodegradable, posing a threat to wildlife. 许多塑料制品不可生物降解，对野生动物构成威胁。

近义词:non-decomposable, nondegradable
反义词:biodegradable, decomposable
词性:adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  not, opposite of  (来自拉丁语 non)
bio- (prefix):  life, living organisms (来自希腊语 bios)
degradable (adjective):  able to be broken down (来自 degrade)
