It is not recognized by the scientific community. |  Example 1: Some people believe Nibiru will collide with Earth.  有些人相信尼比鲁会与地球相撞。

Example 2: The Nibiru cataclysm is a popular theme in science fiction. 尼比鲁灾难是科幻小说中的一个流行主题。 | Synonym: Planet X (sometimes used), doomsday planet | Antonym:  N/A (no antonym for a hypothetical planet) | noun
