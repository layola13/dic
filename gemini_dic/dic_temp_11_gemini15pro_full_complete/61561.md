emendation:/ˌiːmenˈdeɪʃn/ | n. | 校订，修订，改进 | An emendation is a correction or improvement made to a text or piece of writing. | The editor made several emendations to the manuscript before publication. 编辑在出版前对手稿做了几处修改。|  The latest edition of the book includes several important emendations. 最新版本的书包含了几个重要的修订。 | synonyms: correction, revision, amendment, improvement | antonyms: corruption, deterioration | noun
