hadith:/hæˈdiːθ/ | n. | 圣训，传述 | A collection of traditions containing sayings of the prophet Muhammad that, with the Koran, constitute the basis of Islamic law. | Example 1: The hadith emphasizes the importance of charity.  圣训强调慈善的重要性。

Example 2: Scholars study the hadith to understand Islamic teachings. 学者们研究圣训以理解伊斯兰教义。 | synonym: tradition, narration, record | antonym:  (No direct antonym exists, potentially "fabrication" if referring to a false hadith) | noun
