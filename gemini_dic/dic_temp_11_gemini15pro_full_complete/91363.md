hydrospeed:/ˈhaɪdrəʊspiːd/
n.
激流板运动;激流板
A white-water sport in which the participant is equipped with a buoyant board, a wetsuit, flippers, and a helmet and navigates a river current.
He went hydrospeeding down the rapids. 他乘着激流板冲下急流。
近义词:white-water rafting, riverboarding
反义词: (None - This is a specific sport, it doesn't have a direct opposite)
词性: noun
