enjoyer:/ɪnˈdʒɔɪər/| n.|享受者，爱好者|Someone who takes pleasure in something.|She's an enjoyer of fine wines. 她是一位美酒爱好者。|近义词：fan, enthusiast, aficionado|反义词：hater, detractor|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "enjoy" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting a person or thing that performs the action of the verb).  "Enjoy" comes from Old French "enjoir"  (to give joy to, delight), from "en-" (in) + "joir" (to rejoice), ultimately from Latin "gaudia" (joy).
