neurotomy:/njʊˈrɒtəmi/ | n. | 神经切断术 | The surgical division or dissection of a nerve. | Example 1: The patient underwent a neurotomy to relieve chronic pain. 病人接受了神经切断术来缓解慢性疼痛。

Example 2:  Neurotomy can be a last resort for intractable nerve pain.  神经切断术可以作为顽固性神经痛的最后手段。 | 近义词: nerve section | 反义词: neurorrhaphy | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Ancient Greek νεῦρον (neûron, “nerve, sinew”) +‎ -τομία (-tomía, “cutting”), from τέμνω (témnō, “I cut”).
