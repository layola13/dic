micropantograph: /ˈmaɪkroʊˈpæntəɡræf/ | n. | 微缩雕刻机，缩微仿形机 | A device that reduces drawings or engravings, often used for making very small copies of maps, illustrations, or other designs.  It works on the principle of a pantograph, but on a much smaller scale. |  The watchmaker used a micropantograph to engrave intricate details on the watch face.  钟表匠使用微缩雕刻机在表盘上雕刻复杂的细节。

The micropantograph allowed the artist to create miniature portraits with incredible precision.  微缩仿形机让艺术家能够以惊人的精度创作微型肖像。 | synonym:  reducing pantograph, micro-engraver | antonym: enlarging pantograph | noun
