caveat:/ˈkæviæt/ (US), /ˈkɑːviæt/ (UK)| n.|警告，告诫；限制条件|A warning or proviso of specific stipulations, conditions, or limitations.|例句：He agreed to the deal with the caveat that he would be paid in advance. 他同意这笔交易，但条件是他必须预先得到报酬。

例句：The offer of a free trial comes with a few caveats. 免费试用的提议有一些限制条件。


近义词：warning, proviso, admonition, caution, stipulation|反义词:None (No direct antonym)|词性:Noun
