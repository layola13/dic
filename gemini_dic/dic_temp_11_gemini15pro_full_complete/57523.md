dragooned:/drəˈɡuːnd/ | adj. |被强迫的 |forced into doing something against one's will (less common usage)| 例句：The dragooned recruits were unhappy. 被迫入伍的新兵很不高兴。| 近义词：coerced, compelled | 反义词：willing, voluntary |词性：形容词

词源：
