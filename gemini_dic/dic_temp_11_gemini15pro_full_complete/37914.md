complementer:/ˈkɑːmplɪˌmentər/ | n. |补语，补足语|A word or phrase that completes the meaning of a verb or adjective, often introducing a clause.  It links a verb or adjective to the clause that completes its meaning.  |例句： "That" is a complementizer in the sentence "I know that he is coming."  我知道他要来了。 |近义词：subordinator,  |反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:noun
