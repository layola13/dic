farcer:/ˈfɑːsə(r)/| n.|闹剧演员；拙劣的模仿者；粗劣的作家|A person who writes farces (a light dramatic work in which highly improbable plot situations, exaggerated characters, and often slapstick elements are used for humorous effect); a person who behaves in a foolish or exaggerated way, often to make people laugh.|例句：The farcer's over-the-top performance had the audience in stitches. 闹剧演员夸张的表演让观众捧腹大笑。

例句：He was a farcer, always making silly jokes and pulling pranks. 他是个爱开玩笑和恶作剧的滑稽人物。|近义词：clown, comedian, buffoon, jester|反义词：tragedian, serious writer|词性：名词
