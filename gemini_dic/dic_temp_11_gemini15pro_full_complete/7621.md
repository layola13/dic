annuitant:/əˈnjuːɪtənt/
n.|年金受益人；领受年金者|A person who receives an annuity.|The annuitant will receive monthly payments for the rest of his life.  这位年金受益人将在他的余生每月收到付款。|Synonym: pensioner, beneficiary|Antonym: payer, contributor
词性: 名词
