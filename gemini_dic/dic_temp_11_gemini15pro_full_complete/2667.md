adorning:/əˈdɔːrnɪŋ/
verb|装饰，装扮|Adding something decorative to something else; making something more beautiful or attractive.|She was adorning the Christmas tree with colorful ornaments. 她正在用彩色的装饰物装饰圣诞树。|decorating, embellishing, ornamenting|stripping, denuding|v.
adorning:/əˈdɔːrnɪŋ/
adjective|装饰的，装扮的|Serving as an adornment; decorative.|The adorning jewels made her look even more elegant. 装饰的珠宝使她看起来更加优雅。|decorative, ornamental, embellishing|plain, simple, unadorned|adj.
