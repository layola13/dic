fucivorous:/fjuːˈsɪvərəs/
adjective
食海藻的；以海藻为食的
Feeding on seaweed or wrack.  (Primarily referring to marine animals.)
例句：The fucivorous limpet grazes on the rocks at low tide.  食海藻的帽贝在退潮时在岩石上吃草。

例句：Fucivorous fish play an important role in maintaining the health of kelp forests. 食海藻的鱼类在维持海藻森林的健康方面发挥着重要作用。

近义词：algivorous, herbivorous (in a marine context)
反义词：carnivorous, omnivorous
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *fucus* ("seaweed, rock lichen") + *-vorus* ("-eating"), from *vorare* ("to devour").
