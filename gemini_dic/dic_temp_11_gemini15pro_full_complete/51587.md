devaluation:/ˌdiːvæljuˈeɪʃn/ | n. | 贬值；降低价值 |  The reduction or lessening of something's value, worth, or importance. |  The devaluation of the currency led to higher import prices. 货币贬值导致进口价格上涨。 | synonyms: depreciation, deflation, decline, reduction | antonyms: appreciation, inflation, increase, rise | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *devaluationem, from de- + valuationem,  meaning "reduction from value."  The "de-" prefix signifies "down, away from, off."  "Valuation" comes from "value."
