mistier:/ˈmɪstɪər/| adj.|更模糊的，更朦胧的|Covered with or obscured by mist, to a greater degree than something else that is also misty.  More misty.|例句：The mountain top was mistier this morning than yesterday. 今早山顶比昨天更加朦胧。

例句：With each passing hour, the view became mistier and more mysterious. 随着时间的流逝，景色变得越来越模糊和神秘。|近义词：foggier, hazier, murkier|反义词：clearer, brighter, sunnier|词性:形容词

词源: From mist + -y + -er.  Ultimately from Old English *mist "darkness, gloom."
