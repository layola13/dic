liberalizers:/ˈlɪbərəlaɪzərz/
noun
自由化者；放松管制者；开放者
People who promote or implement policies that reduce restrictions and promote free markets or individual liberties.
The government was praised by liberalizers for its deregulation of the banking industry. 政府因其对银行业放松管制而受到自由化者的赞扬。
advocates, reformers, progressives
conservatives, traditionalists
n.
