nectosack: /ˈnɛktoʊˌsæk/ | n. | 蜜囊，蜜胃 | A specialized pouch in the digestive system of some insects, such as bees and ants, used for storing and transporting nectar.| The bee filled its nectosack with nectar from the flower. 蜜蜂用花蜜装满了它的蜜囊。 | Synonyms: honey sac, crop | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "digestive tract" in a broader sense as it's a part of it but has a different function) | noun
