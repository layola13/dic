phonic:/ˈfɑːnɪk/ (US) /ˈfɒnɪk/ (UK)| adj.|语音的；声音的；发音的| relating to sound or to the organs or process of speech.  Pertaining to the nature of sound; acoustic.|例句：Phonic reading methods focus on sounding out words.  语音阅读法侧重于拼读单词。|例句：The phonic properties of the hall were excellent.  这个大厅的音响效果非常好。|近义词：acoustic, auditory, vocal|反义词: silent, mute|词性: adjective
