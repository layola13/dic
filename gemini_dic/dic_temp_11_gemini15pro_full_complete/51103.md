desperation:/ˌdɛspəˈreɪʃən/
n.
绝望，拼命
a state of despair, typically one that results in rash or extreme behavior.
He acted in desperation. 他是在绝望中采取行动的。

The family is now in desperation. 这个家庭现在陷入了绝望。

近义词:hopelessness, despair, distress
反义词:hope, calmness, composure
词性:名词


desperate:/ˈdɛspərɪt/
adj.
绝望的，拼命的
feeling, showing, or involving a hopeless sense that a situation is so bad as to be impossible to deal with.
He was desperate for a job. 他非常渴望得到一份工作。

They made a desperate attempt to escape. 他们拼命地试图逃跑。

近义词:frantic, hopeless, distressed
反义词:calm, hopeful, composed
词性:形容词


desperately:/ˈdɛspərɪtli/
adv.
拼命地，绝望地
in a way that shows despair.
She desperately wanted to see him again. 她非常想再见到他。

They desperately needed help. 他们迫切需要帮助。

近义词:frantically, hopelessly, urgently
反义词:calmly, hopefully, patiently
词性:副词
