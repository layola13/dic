florists:/ˈflɔːrɪsts/| n.|花商，花店老板|A person who sells and arranges flowers. |例句：The florists are busy preparing bouquets for Valentine's Day. 花商们正忙着准备情人节的花束。

例句：I ordered a beautiful arrangement from the local florists. 我在当地花店订购了一束漂亮的插花。

近义词：flower seller, flower arranger|反义词:|词性:noun

florist:/ˈflɔːrɪst/| n.|花卉栽培者，花卉研究者|A person who cultivates or studies flowers. |例句：The florist at the botanical garden is an expert on orchids. 植物园的花卉栽培者是兰花专家。

例句：The florist gave a lecture on the history of roses. 花卉研究者做了一个关于玫瑰历史的讲座。

近义词：horticulturist, gardener|反义词:|词性:noun
