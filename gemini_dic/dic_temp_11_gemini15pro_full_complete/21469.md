blowup:/ˈbloʊˌʌp/| n.| 爆炸；放大；发脾气；争吵|A sudden, often violent outburst of anger or a disagreement; an enlarged photograph or other image.|The political blowup over the scandal forced the prime minister to resign. 这场丑闻引起的政治风暴迫使首相辞职。|Synonyms: explosion, outburst, eruption, tantrum, quarrel, row, enlargement, magnification|Antonyms: calm, peace, agreement, reduction, shrinking|noun

blowup:/ˈbloʊˌʌp/| n.| 放大照片；放大图|An enlarged photograph or other image.|We had a blowup of the wedding photo made for our living room. 我们把结婚照放大挂在了客厅里。|Synonyms: enlargement, magnification, expansion|Antonyms: reduction, miniature|noun
