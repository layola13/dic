demiurgically:/ˌdɛmɪˈɜːrdʒɪk(ə)li/
adverb
创世般地；像造物主一样地
In a demiurgically creative manner, reminiscent of a divine creator bringing something into existence.
The artist shaped the clay demiurgically, as if breathing life into his creation.  艺术家以创世般的方式塑造粘土，仿佛在赋予他的作品生命。
The author demiurgically crafted a complex and intricate world in her novel.  作者在小说中以造物主般的方式创造了一个复杂而精细的世界。
近义词: creatively, innovatively, ingeniously
反义词: destructively, haphazardly
词性: adverb

词源:
From demiurge + -ically.  Demiurge comes from the Late Latin demiurgus, from Greek dēmiourgos "craftsman," from dēmios "public" + -ergos "working."
