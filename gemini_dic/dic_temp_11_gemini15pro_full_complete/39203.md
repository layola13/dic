congest:/kənˈdʒɛst/ | vt. | 拥塞，充塞 | To overcrowd or fill to excess; cause to become blocked.  | The heavy traffic congested the city streets. 交通拥堵阻塞了城市街道。|Synonyms: overcrowd, block, clog, jam | Antonyms: clear, unclog, expedite | verb

congested:/kənˈdʒɛstɪd/ | adj. | 拥挤的，堵塞的 | Overcrowded or blocked. | The congested roads made it difficult to get to work. 拥堵的道路使得上班变得困难。| Synonyms: overcrowded, blocked, jammed, packed | Antonyms: clear, open, unblocked, empty | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *congestus, past participle of congerere "to bring together, heap up," from com- "together" + gerere "to carry."
