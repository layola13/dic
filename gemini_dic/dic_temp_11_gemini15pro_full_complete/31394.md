chargeably:/ˈtʃɑːrdʒəbli/
adverb|可收费地，应收费地|In a way that can be charged or attributed to someone or something.  |The expenses are chargeably incurred by the company.  这些费用由公司承担。|Synonym: attributably, accountably |Antonym: unaccountably, irresponsibly
word origin|From "chargeable" + "-ly".  "Chargeable" comes from the Old French "chargable," from "charger" (to load, burden), from Late Latin "carricare" (to load a car), from "carrus" (car).
part of speech|adverb
