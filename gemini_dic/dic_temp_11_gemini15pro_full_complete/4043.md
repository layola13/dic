aide:/eɪd/| n.|助手，助理，副官|A person who acts as an assistant to another person, especially to someone important or senior.|The president's aide briefed the press. 总统的助手向新闻界做了简报。|Synonyms: assistant, helper, deputy|Antonyms: boss, superior|noun
