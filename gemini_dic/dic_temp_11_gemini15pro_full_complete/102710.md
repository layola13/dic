jangi:/ˈdʒɑːŋɡiː/
n.
战士；勇士；士兵 (Especially a soldier in the Iranian Revolutionary Guard)
A soldier, especially one in the Iranian Revolutionary Guard.
Example: The jangi fought bravely in the war.  这名战士在战争中英勇作战。

Example: He was a respected jangi among his comrades. 他是一位受战友尊敬的战士。

近义词:soldier, warrior, fighter, combatant
反义词:civilian, noncombatant
词性:名词
