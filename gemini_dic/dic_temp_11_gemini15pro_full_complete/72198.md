flops:/flɑːps/| n.|失败；惨败；彻底失败的人或事|A complete failure.|The play was a total flop.  这出戏彻底失败了。|Synonyms: failure, disaster, bomb|Antonyms: success, triumph|noun

flops:/flɑːps/| v. (flop的第三人称单数)|失败；重重地落下；扑通一声落下|To fall, sit, or lie down heavily and clumsily.|He flopped down onto the sofa, exhausted. 他筋疲力尽地倒在沙发上。|Synonyms: collapse, slump, tumble|Antonyms: rise, stand|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
Likely imitative of the sound of something falling heavily.
