capitulate:/kəˈpɪtʃʊleɪt/
verb
投降，屈服
Cease to resist an opponent or an unwelcome demand; surrender.
例句：The rebels were forced to capitulate. 叛军被迫投降。

例句：After a long siege, the city finally capitulated to the enemy.  经过长时间的围困，这座城市最终向敌人投降。


近义词：surrender, yield, submit, give in
反义词：resist, defy, oppose
词性：v.
