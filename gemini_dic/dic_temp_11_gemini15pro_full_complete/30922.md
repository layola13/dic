chainlike:/ˈtʃeɪnlaɪk/| adj.|链状的，像链条的|Resembling a chain in form or arrangement. |The bacteria formed a chainlike structure. 这些细菌形成了链状结构。|The climber used a chainlike series of ropes. 登山者使用了一系列像链条一样的绳索。|近义词：catenary, concatenated, linked|反义词: separate, detached|词性:形容词

词源: From "chain" + "-like." "Chain" comes from the Old French "chaine" (modern French "chaîne"), from the Latin "catena."  "-like" is a native English suffix meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of."
