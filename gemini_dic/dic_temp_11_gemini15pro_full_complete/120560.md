Miramax: /ˈmɪrəˌmæks/ | n. | 米拉麦克斯 | A former independent film production and distribution company known for producing and distributing critically acclaimed and commercially successful films. It was founded in 1977 by Bob and Harvey Weinstein.  | Miramax distributed many successful independent films, such as Pulp Fiction. 米拉麦克斯发行过许多成功的独立电影，例如《低俗小说》。 |  近义词:  independent film studio, production company | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


Etymology:
Miramax is a portmanteau of the first names of its founders, **Mira** (Max's mother) and **Max** (their father).
