half-madnesses:/ˌhæf ˈmædnɪsɪz/ | n. | 半疯狂，轻度疯狂 | States of being slightly or partially mad; instances of mild or temporary insanity.  It suggests a deviation from normalcy, but not full-blown madness. |  He experienced brief half-madnesses brought on by stress. 他因压力而经历了短暂的半疯狂状态。 |  The diaries detailed her descent into half-madnesses and strange obsessions.  日记详细记录了她陷入半疯狂和奇怪痴迷的过程。 |  delusions, eccentricities, irrationality | sanity, lucidity | noun
