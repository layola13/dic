flying-out:/ˈflaɪɪŋ aʊt/
n.
外出飞行；飞离；(尤指)飞往国外
The act of flying away from a place, especially to a foreign country.
例句：My flying-out is scheduled for tomorrow morning. 我的外出飞行安排在明天早上。

例句：Her flying-out was delayed due to bad weather. 由于天气不好，她飞往国外的航班延误了。

近义词：departure, flight, going abroad
反义词：arrival, flying-in, returning
词性：名词

flying-out:/ˈflaɪɪŋ aʊt/
adj.
飞出的；向外飞的
Moving or directed outwards from a central point.  (This usage is less common than the noun form.)
例句：The flying-out sparks from the bonfire were beautiful.  篝火飞溅出的火花很漂亮。(不太常用)

例句：The flying-out debris from the explosion caused significant damage. 爆炸飞出的碎片造成了重大损害。(不太常用)

近义词：outgoing, outward
反义词：incoming, inward
词性：形容词
