invited: /ɪnˈvaɪtɪd/
verb (past tense and past participle of "invite")|被邀请的，受邀的|Having received an invitation.|例句：I felt honored to be invited to the wedding. 我很荣幸被邀请参加婚礼。|近义词：summoned, called, asked|反义词：uninvited, excluded|词性:动词(过去式/过去分词)

invited: /ɪnˈvaɪtɪd/
adjective|被邀请的，受邀的|Asked formally or politely to be present or participate.|例句：The invited guests began to arrive. 受邀的客人们开始到达了。|近义词：welcomed, included|反义词：uninvited, excluded|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *invit, from Latin invītātus, past participle of invītāre (“to invite”), from in- (“in, on, upon”) + vocāre (“to call”).
