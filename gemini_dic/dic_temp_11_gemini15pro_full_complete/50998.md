desirousness:/dɪˈzaɪərəsnəs/
noun
渴望， desirousness
The state of having or showing strong desire.
His desirousness for power was evident in his every action. 他对权力的渴望体现在他的一举一动中。
近义词: desire, craving, longing, yearning, avidity
反义词: apathy, indifference, lack of interest
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From desirous + -ness.  Desirous comes from the Old French *desiros*  (modern French *désireux*), from the Latin *desiderosus*, from *desiderare* "to long for," from *de-* "from" + *siderari* "to consider, observe the stars," perhaps originally "to await a sign from the stars."
