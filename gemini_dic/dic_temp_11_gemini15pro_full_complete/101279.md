involute:/ˈɪnvəˌluːt/ /ˈɪnvəˌlʊt/ | v. |  使内卷；使缠绕；使复杂 |  to roll or curl inward; to make complex | The company's policies involuted over time, becoming increasingly difficult to understand.  公司的政策随着时间的推移变得越来越复杂，难以理解。 | 近义词：complicate, entangle, involve | 反义词：simplify, disentangle, clarify | 词性: verb
