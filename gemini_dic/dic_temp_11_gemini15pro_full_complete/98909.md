intensive:/ɪnˈtɛnsɪv/| adj.|集中的，密集的，加强的，深入的|concentrated on a single area or subject or into a short time; very thorough or vigorous.|例句：The course will involve ten weeks of intensive training. 这门课程需要十周的强化训练。|例句：She's been having intensive care since the accident. 事故发生后，她一直在接受重症监护。|近义词：concentrated, thorough, rigorous, exhaustive, demanding|反义词：superficial, extensive, casual|词性:形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin *intensivus, from Latin intendere ‘stretch out, strain’.
