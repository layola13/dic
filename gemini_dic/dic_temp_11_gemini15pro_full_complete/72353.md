flowering:/ˈflaʊərɪŋ/| adj. n.|开花的，开花；|The flowering of the cherry blossoms is a beautiful sight. 樱花盛开是一道美丽的风景。|例句：The flowering season is in spring. 开花季节在春天。

The early flowering of the plants was a surprise. 植物的早开花令人惊讶。|近义词：blooming, blossoming|反义词:non-flowering, fruitless|词性:adjective/noun

Etymology: From Middle English *flouring, *floring, from Old French florissant (present participle of florir), from Latin flōrēns (present participle of flōrēre, “to bloom, blossom, flower”), from flōs (“flower”).
