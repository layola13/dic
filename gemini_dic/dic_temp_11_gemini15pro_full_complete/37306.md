commands:/kəˈmændz/| n. /kəˈmænd/| v.| 命令，指令；掌握，控制|  An instruction, order, or signal. To have authority and control over; to order or direct.|  The general issued commands to his troops. 将军向他的部队下达了命令。 He commands a vast army. 他统率一支庞大的军队。|近义词：instructions, orders, directives, control, govern|反义词：obey, comply, follow, requests, pleas|词性:名词复数/动词
