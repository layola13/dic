metastases:/məˈtæstəsiːz/
|n.|转移；扩散；转移灶|The spread of a cancer or other disease from one organ or part to another not directly connected with it.  The plural form of metastasis.|The patient's cancer had already spread to the lungs, showing signs of metastases.  病人的癌症已经扩散到肺部，显示出转移的迹象。|spread, dissemination, diffusion|localization, confinement|noun
