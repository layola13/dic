forstraught:/fɔːrˈstrɔːt/
adjective
极度不安的，心烦意乱的
extremely agitated with distress and grief; distraught
例句：He was forstraught with worry when his daughter didn't come home. 当他的女儿没有回家时，他非常担心。

例句：She was utterly forstraught after the accident. 事故发生后，她完全心烦意乱。

近义词：distraught, frantic, agitated, distressed
反义词：calm, composed, serene
词性：adjective
