joni:/ˈdʒoʊni/ | n. | 乔尼（人名） |Joni is a given name, often a shortened version of Joan or Jonathan.  It can be used for both males and females, although it is more commonly associated with females.  It ultimately derives from the Hebrew name Yohanan, meaning "God is gracious". | Joni Mitchell is a famous Canadian singer-songwriter. 乔尼·米切尔是一位著名的加拿大创作歌手。| Joni went to the store to buy groceries. 乔尼去商店买杂货。| 近义词: (If considered a nickname) Joan, Jonathan | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
