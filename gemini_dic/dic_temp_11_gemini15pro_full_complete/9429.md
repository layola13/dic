antireactionary:/ˌæntiˌriˈækʃəˌneri/
adjective
反动的；反对反动派的
Opposing reaction or reactionaries; progressive.  Characterized by opposition to political or social reaction.
The antireactionary movement gained momentum after the oppressive regime was overthrown.  反动政权被推翻后，反动运动势头强劲。
The government implemented antireactionary policies to quell the growing unrest. 政府实施了反动政策以平息日益增长的动乱。
近义词:progressive, liberal, reformist, radical
反义词:reactionary, conservative, right-wing
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From anti- + reactionary.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to." "Reactionary" comes from "reaction," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "reactio," meaning "a driving back."
