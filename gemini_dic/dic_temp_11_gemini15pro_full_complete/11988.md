artha:/ˈɑːrtə/| n.|意义，目标，财富，利益|Meaning, object, wealth, prosperity.  From Sanskrit अर्थ (artha), meaning "purpose, meaning, cause, reason, motive, aim, object, sense, goal, subject matter, wealth, property, prosperity".|The artha of human life is often debated by philosophers.  人生的意义经常被哲学家们所争论。|Synonyms: meaning, purpose, aim, objective, wealth, prosperity|Antonyms: meaninglessness, purposelessness, poverty|noun
