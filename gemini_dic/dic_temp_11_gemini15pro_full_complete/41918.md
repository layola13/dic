cosmogony: /kɒzˈmɒɡəni/ (US) /kɑːzˈmɑːɡəni/ (UK)| n. |宇宙起源学；宇宙创生论|A theory or story about the origin and development of the universe. |例句：Ancient Egyptian cosmogony held that the world arose from a watery abyss. 古埃及的宇宙起源论认为世界是从水的深渊中产生的。

例句：Modern cosmogony is based on scientific observations and mathematical models. 现代宇宙起源论是基于科学观察和数学模型的。|近义词：creation myth, origin story, genesis|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


Etymology:
From Late Latin *cosmogōnia*, from Ancient Greek κοσμογονία (kosmogonía, “birth of the world”), from κόσμος (kósmos, “world, order, ornament”) + γονή (gonḗ, “generation, seed, offspring”).
