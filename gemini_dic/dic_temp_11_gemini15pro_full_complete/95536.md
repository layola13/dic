inclusivity:/ɪnˌkluːˈsɪvɪti/
n.|包容性，包容度|The state or quality of including people from all backgrounds and groups.|Our goal is to promote inclusivity and diversity in the workplace. 我们的目标是在工作场所促进包容性和多样性。|inclusion, acceptance, tolerance|exclusion, segregation|noun

inclusiveness:/ɪnˈkluːsɪvnəs/
n.|包容性，包容度|The quality of being inclusive.|The inclusiveness of the new policy was praised by many. 新政策的包容性受到了许多人的赞扬。|inclusivity, acceptance, tolerance|exclusion, segregation|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From include (v.) + -ive + -ity.  Ultimately from Latin includere "to shut in, enclose."
