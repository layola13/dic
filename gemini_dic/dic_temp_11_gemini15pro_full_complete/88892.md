hokiness:/ˈhoʊkinəs/| n.| 庸俗；老套；矫揉造作 | The movie's hokiness made it a cult classic.  这部电影的老套反而使它成为了一部邪典电影。| Examples: The hokiness of the dialogue made me cringe. 对话的矫揉造作让我感到尴尬。  The plot's hokiness was charming in a way.  故事情节的老套在某种程度上是很有魅力的。| 近义词：corniness, cheesiness, sentimentality | 反义词：sophistication, genuineness, authenticity | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From *hokey* (adjective) + *-ness* (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  *Hokey* is of uncertain origin, possibly from *hocus-pocus*, implying something contrived or artificial.
