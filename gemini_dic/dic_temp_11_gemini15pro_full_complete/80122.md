goblinesque:/ˌɡɒblɪnˈesk/ | adj. |像妖精的；丑陋怪诞的|Resembling or suggestive of a goblin; grotesquely ugly or evil-looking.|The gargoyle on the building had a goblinesque appearance.  建筑物上的滴水嘴兽有一种像妖精般的外观。| The twisted, gnarled branches of the tree gave it a goblinesque quality.  扭曲粗糙的树枝使它具有一种像妖精般的气质。| 近义词：grotesque, monstrous, hideous, deformed|反义词：beautiful, attractive, pleasant|词性：形容词
