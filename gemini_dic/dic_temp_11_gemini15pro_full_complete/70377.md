fifes:/faɪfs/
n.
横笛(fife的名词复数)
A small high-pitched flute, similar to a piccolo, used especially in military bands.
The fifes and drums played a marching tune. 横笛和鼓奏响了进行曲。
近义词:flutes, piccolos
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑低音乐器) tubas, bassoons
词性:noun

fifes:/faɪfs/
v.
吹横笛(fife的第三人称单数形式)
Plays a fife.
He fifes a merry tune. 他吹奏了一曲欢快的横笛曲。
近义词:plays, performs
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑停止演奏) stops, silences
词性:verb
