overfocuses:/ˌoʊvərˈfoʊkəsɪz/
| v.|过度关注|To concentrate on something too much, often to the exclusion of other important things.  The act of focusing excessively on one aspect or detail, often losing sight of the bigger picture.|He overfocuses on minor details and misses the main point. 他过度关注细枝末节，忽略了重点。|近义词: fixates, obsesses|反义词: neglects, ignores|词性:动词(第三人称单数)
