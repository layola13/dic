motor-scooterist:/ˈmoʊtər ˈskuːtərɪst/| n.|摩托车踏板车驾驶者|A person who rides a motor scooter.|He saw the motor-scooterist weaving through traffic. 他看到摩托车踏板车驾驶者在车流中穿梭。|The motor-scooterist parked their scooter by the curb.  摩托车踏板车驾驶者把他们的车停在路边。|近义词：scooter rider, scootering|反义词:pedestrian, driver|词性:noun


Etymology:
motor + scooter + -ist
* motor (n.): from Old French *motore*, from Latin *motor*, "mover," agent noun from *movēre*, "to move."
* scooter (n.): origin uncertain, possibly from *scoot* (v.) + *-er* (agent suffix), meaning "something that scoots."
* -ist (suffix): from Greek *-istēs*, forming agent nouns.
