overrudely: /ˌoʊvərˈruːdli/ | adv. | 过分粗鲁地，极其无礼地 | Excessively rude; in a very impolite manner. | He overrudely dismissed her concerns. 他极其粗鲁地驳回了她的担忧。| She overrudely interrupted the speaker. 她非常无礼地打断了演讲者。| 近义词：impolitely, disrespectfully, insolently | 反义词：politely, respectfully, courteously | 词性: 副词

词源:
"over-" (prefix) + "rudely" (adverb).  "Over-" indicates an excess or going beyond a limit. "Rudely" comes from the Old English "rūde," meaning rough or unrefined.
