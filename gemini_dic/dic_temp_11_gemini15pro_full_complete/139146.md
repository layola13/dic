obeyer:/əˈbeɪər/| n.|服从者，顺从者|Someone who obeys rules or commands.  A person who complies with orders or instructions.|例句：He is a strict obeyer of the law. 他是一个严格遵守法律的人。

例句：She's always been an obeyer of rules, unlike her rebellious brother.  她一直都很顺从规则，不像她那叛逆的哥哥。|近义词：conformist, follower, compliant person|反义词:rebel,  dissident, nonconformist|词性:noun
