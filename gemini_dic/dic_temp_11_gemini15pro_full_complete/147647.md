overpolitically:/ˌoʊvər pəˈlɪtɪkli/ | adv. | 过度政治化地 | Excessively or inappropriately focused on politics; motivated or influenced by political considerations to an excessive degree. |  The issue was discussed overpolitically, obscuring the real human impact.  这个问题被过度政治化地讨论，掩盖了真正的人的影响。 | 近义词: excessively political, hyperpolitical | 反义词: apolitical, non-political | 词性: 副词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "politically" (adverb form of "political")
