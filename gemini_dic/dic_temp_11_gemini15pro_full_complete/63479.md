entrusting:/ɪnˈtrʌstɪŋ/
verb
委托；信托；交付
the act of giving someone the responsibility of doing something or looking after something
例句：I am entrusting you with this important task. 我把这项重要的任务委托给你。

例句：He entrusted his savings to a financial advisor. 他把积蓄委托给了一位财务顾问。

近义词：assigning, delegating, committing, confiding
反义词：keeping, withholding, retaining
词性：动词 (present participle of entrust)
