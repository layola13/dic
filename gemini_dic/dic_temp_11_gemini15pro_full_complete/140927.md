ondogram:/ˈɒndəɡræm/ |n.| 声波图；振动图；波形图 |A visual representation of sound waves, often used to analyze speech or other acoustic phenomena. It shows the variations in amplitude and frequency of sound over time.| The ondogram revealed the subtle differences in pronunciation between the two speakers. 声波图显示了两个说话者之间发音的细微差别。| synonym: waveform, sonogram | antonym: N/A |noun
