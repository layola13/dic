ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *gnætt, from Proto-Germanic *gnatton.  Cognate with Middle Dutch gnatte, Old High German gnaz (German Gnitze).  Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European root *ghen- ("to gnaw, bite").
