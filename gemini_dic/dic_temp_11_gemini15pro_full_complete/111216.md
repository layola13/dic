lithophytous:/lɪˈθɒfɪtəs/
adjective
石生的；岩生的
Growing on rocks.  A lithophytous plant derives its nutrients from rainwater, dust, and decaying organic matter that accumulates around it.
The lithophytous orchids thrived on the cliff face. 石生兰花在悬崖面上茁壮生长。
lithophytic
saxicolous, rupestral, rock-dwelling
