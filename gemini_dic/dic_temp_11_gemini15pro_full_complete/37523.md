common-room:/ˈkɒmən ruːm/ (UK), /ˈkɑːmən ruːm/ (US)| n. |公共休息室，公共起居室|A room in a house, hotel, or institution, typically one shared by residents or guests. |例句：The students gathered in the common-room to watch TV. 学生们聚集在公共休息室看电视。|近义词：lounge, sitting room, recreation room|反义词:private room, bedroom|词性:名词

common-room:|词源:  From 'common' (shared by all) + 'room'.  The term originally referred to the dining hall in monasteries and colleges, where members of the community ate together.|
