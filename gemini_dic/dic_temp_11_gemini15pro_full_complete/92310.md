hypnotherapies:/ˌhɪpnəˈθɛrəpiz/
|n.|催眠疗法|Hypnotherapies are a range of treatments that use hypnosis to address various psychological and physical issues. 催眠疗法是一系列使用催眠来解决各种心理和生理问题的治疗方法。|Hypnotherapy can be used to treat anxiety, phobias, and addiction. 催眠疗法可以用来治疗焦虑、恐惧症和成瘾。|Hypnotherapy sessions can help people overcome their fears and achieve their goals. 催眠疗程可以帮助人们克服恐惧并实现目标。|Synonyms: hypnotic therapies, hypnotherapy treatments|Antonyms: conventional therapies, traditional medicine|Noun
