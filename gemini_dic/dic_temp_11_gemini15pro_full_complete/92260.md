hypervelocity:/ˌhaɪpərˈvɪləsəti/| n.|超高速，极高速|extremely high velocity|例句：The projectile traveled at hypervelocity.  弹丸以超高速行进。|近义词：ultrahigh speed, supersonic speed, hypersonic speed|反义词:low speed, subsonic speed|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- + velocity.  Hyper- is a prefix meaning "over, above, beyond," from Greek *hyper (over, beyond). Velocity comes from Old French velocite "swiftness, speed" from Latin velocitatem (nominative velocitas) "swiftness, speed" from velox (genitive velocis) "swift, quick."
