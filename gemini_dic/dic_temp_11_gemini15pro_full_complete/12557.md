aspirator:/ˈæspəreɪtər/ (US), /əˈspɪreɪtər/ (UK)| n.|抽吸器；吸尘器；注射器|A device that uses suction to remove fluids or gases.|例句：The doctor used an aspirator to remove the fluid from the patient's lung. 医生用抽吸器清除病人肺部的积液。

例句：She used an aspirator to clean the dust from the keyboard. 她用吸尘器清理键盘上的灰尘。|近义词：sucker, pump, vacuum|反义词:blower|noun
