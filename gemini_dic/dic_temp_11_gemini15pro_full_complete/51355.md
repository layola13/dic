detention:/dɪˈtɛnʃən/| n.|拘留，扣押，留堂|The act of detaining or the state of being detained; confinement.  The student received a detention for being late to class.|例句：His detention without trial caused an international outcry.  他未经审判就被拘留，引起了国际社会的强烈抗议。

例句：She served a two-month detention for her involvement in the protest. 她因参与抗议被拘留了两个月。|近义词：confinement, imprisonment, custody, arrest|反义词：release, freedom, liberation|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French detencion, from late Latin detentio(n-), from the verb detinere, from de- ‘away’ + tenere ‘to hold’.
