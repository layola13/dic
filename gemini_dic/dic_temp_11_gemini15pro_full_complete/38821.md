coneine:/ˈkoʊniːn/ | n. |毒芹碱; 锥碱|A poisonous alkaloid, C8H17N, derived from poison hemlock (Conium maculatum) and causing paralysis of motor nerve endings. |Coniine is a highly toxic substance. 毒芹碱是一种剧毒物质。|Coniine poisoning can be fatal. 毒芹碱中毒可能是致命的。|Synonyms: cicutine |Antonyms: antidote |noun
