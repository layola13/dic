knitchet: /ˈnɪtʃɪt/
v. (Rare and archaic form of "knit")
to knit
编织
She knitchet a beautiful scarf for her grandmother. 她为祖母编织了一条漂亮的围巾。
近义词: knit, crochet
反义词: unravel, unknit


Etymology:  From the verb "knit" + the diminutive suffix "-et".  The suffix "-et" is used to form nouns indicating small size or affection.
