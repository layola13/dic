mixtilinear:/ˌmɪkstɪˈlɪniər/| adj.|混合线的；直线和曲线混合的|Relating to or consisting of a mixture of straight and curved lines.|The architect's design incorporated mixtilinear elements, combining the starkness of straight lines with the softness of curves.  建筑师的设计融合了混合线元素，将直线的鲜明与曲线的柔和结合在一起。|Example 2:  The artist explored mixtilinear forms in her abstract paintings.  这位艺术家在她的抽象画中探索了混合线的形式。|近义词：curvilinear, nonlinear|反义词: rectilinear, straight|词性:adjective
