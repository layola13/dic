featheriest:/ˈfeðəriɪst/
adjective
最多羽毛的；最像羽毛的
covered with or made of feathers, especially to a greater degree than is usual or normal
例句：The featheriest boa I've ever seen. 我见过羽毛最多的围巾。

例句：The chick was the featheriest of the brood. 这只雏鸟是这窝中最毛茸茸的。

近义词：fluffiest, downiest, softest
反义词：baldest, smoothest, barest
词性：adjective
