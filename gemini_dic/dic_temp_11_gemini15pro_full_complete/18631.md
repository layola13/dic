benumbment:/bɪˈnʌm.mənt/
n.|麻木；麻痹；失去知觉|A state of physical or emotional numbness; a lack of feeling or responsiveness.|The trauma left her in a state of benumbment.|近义词：numbness, stupor, insensibility, paralysis|反义词：sensitivity, awareness, responsiveness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From be- (making a verb transitive or causative) + numb + -ment (forming nouns of action or result).  "Numb" traces back to Old English *numen, past participle of a verb *niman "to take," with the sense of "taken away" (in this case, sensation).
