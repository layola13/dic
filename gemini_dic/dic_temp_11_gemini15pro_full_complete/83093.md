gushingness:/ˈɡʌʃɪŋnɪs/
n.
滔滔不绝；热情洋溢；喷涌而出
The quality of being gushing; effusiveness.
Her gushingness about the new movie was contagious. 她对这部新电影的热情洋溢很有感染力。
Near Synonyms: effusiveness, exuberance, enthusiasm, ebullience
Antonyms: reticence, reserve, taciturnity
词性: 名词

gush:/ɡʌʃ/
v.
喷涌；涌出；滔滔不绝地说
To flow out suddenly and copiously; to speak effusively.
Water gushed from the broken pipe. 水从破裂的管道中喷涌而出。
She gushed about how wonderful the trip was. 她滔滔不绝地讲述这次旅行有多么精彩。
Near Synonyms: spout, pour, stream, effuse
Antonyms: trickle, drip, ooze
词性: 动词

gushing:/ˈɡʌʃɪŋ/
adj.
喷涌的；滔滔不绝的；热情洋溢的
Flowing out suddenly and copiously; speaking effusively.
The gushing waterfall was a beautiful sight. 奔涌的瀑布是一道美丽的风景。
Her gushing praise made him blush. 她热情洋溢的赞美让他脸红了。
Near Synonyms: effusive, exuberant, enthusiastic, ebullient
Antonyms: reserved, reticent, taciturn
词性: 形容词
