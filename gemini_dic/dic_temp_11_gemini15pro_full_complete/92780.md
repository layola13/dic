icicles:/ˈaɪsɪklz/ (US), /ˈaɪsɪkəlz/ (UK)
n.
冰柱, 垂冰
A pendent, tapering mass of ice formed by the freezing of dripping water.
The roof was adorned with long, glistening icicles. 屋顶上挂满了长长的、闪闪发光的冰柱。

近义词:none (While "ice formations" or "glaciers" could be related depending on context, there's no direct single-word synonym for icicle.)
