allopathist:/əˈlɒpəθɪst/
noun
顺势疗法医师，对抗疗法医师
A practitioner of allopathy; a physician who employs remedies that produce effects differing from those produced by the disease treated.
The allopathist prescribed antibiotics for the infection.  顺势疗法医师开了抗生素治疗感染。
synonyms: conventional doctor, physician, MD, doctor
antonyms: homeopath, naturopath
n.
