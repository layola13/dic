nonproletarians:/ˌnɒnˌprəʊləˈteəriənz/
n.
非无产阶级者；非无产阶级分子
people who are not members of the proletariat; those who do not earn their living by manual labor
The nonproletarians in the society included shopkeepers, merchants, and landowners.  这个社会的非无产阶级包括店主、商人和地主。
The party sought to appeal to both proletarians and nonproletarians. 该党试图同时吸引无产阶级和非无产阶级。
synonyms: bourgeoisie, middle class, upper class
antonyms: proletarians, working class
noun
