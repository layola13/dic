one-liners:/ˈwʌn ˌlaɪnərz/ | n. |  单行笑话; 简洁而机智的妙语 | A one-liner is a short joke or witty remark, typically only one sentence long. | He's known for his quick wit and hilarious one-liners. 他以机智和搞笑的单行笑话而闻名。|  quips, jokes, witticisms |  -| noun

one-liner:/ˈwʌn ˌlaɪnər/ | adj. |  单行的 |  Consisting of a single line; concise and to the point.  | Her one-liner response shut down the entire argument. 她简短的回应结束了整个争论。| concise, brief, succinct | verbose, lengthy | adjective
