incommunicative:/ˌɪnkəˈmjuːnɪkətɪv/
adj.
不爱说话的，不爱交际的
Not inclined to talk or communicate; unsociable.
例句：He was incommunicative after the argument.  他争吵后变得沉默寡言。

例句：She became incommunicative and withdrawn after the loss of her job.  失业后，她变得沉默寡言，不愿与人交往。

近义词：reserved, taciturn, unforthcoming, reticent, quiet
反义词：communicative, talkative, outgoing, sociable
词性：adjective
