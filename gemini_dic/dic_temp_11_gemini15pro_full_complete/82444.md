grumbles:/ˈɡrʌmbəlz/ (US), /ˈɡrʌmblz/ (UK)| n. vi. |抱怨，牢骚；嘟囔声|A low, rumbling, discontented sound; a complaint uttered in a low voice. |The old man grumbles about the weather every day.  这个老人每天都抱怨天气。

The dog grumbles when his food bowl is empty.  狗的食盆空了时，它就会发出咕噜声。|近义词：complaints, gripes, moans, murmurs, whines|反义词：praise, compliments, accolades|词性:名词，动词
