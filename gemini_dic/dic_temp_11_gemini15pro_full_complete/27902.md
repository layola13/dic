cantatory:/ˈkæntətɔːri/ |adj.|歌唱的；吟唱的；适合歌唱的|Relating to singing, chanting, or suitable for singing.|The cantatory style of the Gregorian chant filled the cathedral. 格里高利圣咏的吟唱风格充满了整个大教堂。|Synonyms: melodic, musical, lyrical, tuneful|Antonyms: unmusical, dissonant, discordant|adjective

cantatory:/ˈkæntətɔːri/ |n.|歌唱；歌曲|The act of singing or chanting, or a song.|The monk's cantatory was a beautiful expression of devotion.  僧侣的歌声是虔诚的美丽表达。|Synonyms: song, chant, hymn|Antonyms: silence, quiet|noun
