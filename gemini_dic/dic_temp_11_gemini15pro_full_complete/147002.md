overlabors:/ˌoʊvərˈleɪbərz/
verb
过度劳作，使…过度疲劳
works or exerts oneself excessively
The artist overlabored the details, making the painting cluttered and fussy.  这位艺术家过度描绘细节，使这幅画显得杂乱而繁琐。

overlabors:/ˌoʊvərˈleɪbərz/
verb
过分修饰，过分雕琢
elaborates or refines excessively
The writer overlabored the metaphor, making it convoluted and difficult to understand.  作者过度修饰了这个比喻，使其变得晦涩难懂。


Synonym: overwork, overexert, belabor, strain
Antonym: relax, rest, understate
