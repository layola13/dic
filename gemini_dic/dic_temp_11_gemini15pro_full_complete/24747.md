broodingly:/ˈbruːdɪŋli/| adv.|沉思地，闷闷不乐地|In a thoughtful, preoccupied, and often melancholic way, suggesting deep contemplation or worry.|He stared broodingly out the window at the rain. 他闷闷不乐地盯着窗外的大雨。|Synonyms: thoughtfully, pensively, moodily, morosely, sullenly|Antonyms: cheerfully, brightly, happily|词性:副词
