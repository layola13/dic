brainchild:/ˈbreɪntʃaɪld/| n.|创意，点子，脑力劳动的产物|An idea or invention that is the product of someone's creative effort. |例句：The project was the brainchild of the CEO. 这个项目是首席执行官的创意。

例句：This new marketing campaign is her brainchild. 这个新的营销活动是她的创意。

近义词：invention, creation, innovation, idea, concept|反义词:copy, imitation|词性:noun
