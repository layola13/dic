nonprofanity:/ˌnɒnprəˈfænɪti/ /ˌnɑːnprəˈfænɪti/ | n. | 非亵渎；非脏话；文明用语 | The absence of profanity; language that is not profane. |  This website enforces a strict policy of nonprofanity in all user-generated content.  这个网站对所有用户生成的内容都强制执行严格的非亵渎政策。

She was praised for her nonprofanity even in stressful situations. 即使在压力很大的情况下，她也因不说脏话而受到称赞。 | 近义词：decency, politeness, clean language | 反义词: profanity, vulgarity, obscenity | 词性: 名词

词源:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + profanity (from Latin profanus, meaning "outside the temple," hence "unholy" or "profane").
