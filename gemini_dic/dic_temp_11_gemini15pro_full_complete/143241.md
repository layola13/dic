outdebated:/ˌaʊtdɪˈbeɪtɪd/
verb
胜过辩论，辩论击败
defeated in debate; having had one's arguments successfully countered in a debate.
He was completely outdebated by his opponent. 他在辩论中被对手彻底击败了。
Synonyms: outargued, refuted, defeated
Antonyms: persuaded, convinced
词性：动词
