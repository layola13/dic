callose:/ˈkæləʊs/ (US) /ˈkæləʊz/ (UK)| n. |胼胝质；愈伤组织|A complex polysaccharide, a plant polymer whose deposition is associated with plasmodesmatal closure, wounding, and other stress responses.|Callose deposition is a common plant response to wounding.|近义词: wound gum|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider substances that promote cell wall degradation)|词性: Noun
