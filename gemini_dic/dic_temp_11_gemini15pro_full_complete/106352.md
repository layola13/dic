kokoshka:/kəˈkɒʃkə/| n.|科科施卡（奥地利表现主义画家）|Oskar Kokoschka (1886-1980) was an Austrian Expressionist painter, poet, and playwright.  He is best known for his intense portraits and landscapes, often depicting psychological states.|Kokoshka's portraits are known for their raw emotion. 科科施卡的肖像画以其原始的情感而闻名。|Near synonyms:  Expressionist, painter, artist|Near antonyms: N/A|Noun
