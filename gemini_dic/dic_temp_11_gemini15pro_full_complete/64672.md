ETYMOLOGY->
From French *escalope*, from Old French *escalope*, meaning "shell, husk, pod," later also "thin slice of meat." Ultimately from Germanic origin, related to Old High German *scalpa* "shell."  The sense of "scallop (shellfish)" arose from the resemblance of the meat to the shape of a scallop shell.
