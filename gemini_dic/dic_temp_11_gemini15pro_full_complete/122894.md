mollycoddling:/ˈmɒlɪˌkɒdlɪŋ/ (US) /ˈmɑːliˌkɑːdlɪŋ/| n.  v. (present participle)|娇惯；溺爱；过分呵护|The act of treating someone very indulgently or protectively, often to an excessive degree.|She resented her mother's mollycoddling. 她讨厌她母亲的溺爱。|近义词：pampering, coddling, spoiling, overprotecting|反义词:neglecting, ignoring|词性:名词/动词(现在分词)

mollycoddle:/ˈmɒlɪˌkɒdl/ (US) /ˈmɑːliˌkɑːdl/| vt. |娇惯；溺爱；过分呵护|To treat someone very indulgently or protectively, often to an excessive degree.|He was mollycoddled as a child. 他小时候被娇惯坏了。|近义词：pamper, coddle, spoil, overprotect|反义词:neglect, ignore|词性:动词

词源:  From *molly* (possibly a pet-form of *Mary*) + *coddle* (to treat tenderly).  The original meaning referred to an effeminate or weak man.
