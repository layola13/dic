elative:/ɪˈleɪtɪv/ | n. | 最高比较级 | The highest degree of comparison, especially in Semitic languages. | The elative form indicates the highest degree of a quality.  最高级形式表示质量的最高程度。 |  In Arabic, "akbar" is the elative form of "kabir," meaning "greatest."  在阿拉伯语中，"akbar"是"kabir"的最高级形式，意思是"最伟大的"。| 近义词: superlative | 反义词: positive (degree) | 词性: noun
