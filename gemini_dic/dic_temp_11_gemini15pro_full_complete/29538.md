catch:/kætʃ/ | v. n. | 接住，抓住；赶上；感染；理解；吸引 | To seize, grasp, or capture something, often in motion; to contract a disease; to reach in time (a train, bus, etc.); to understand something; to attract someone's attention. |
例句：
The dog caught the ball in mid-air.  狗在半空中接住了球。

He caught a cold last week. 他上周感冒了。

近义词：grab, seize, capture, contract, apprehend, comprehend, fascinate |
反义词：miss, release, lose, recover, repel |
词性：动词, 名词


catch:/kætʃ/ | n. | 捕获物；隐患；挂钩 | Something that is caught; a hidden problem or disadvantage; a fastening device. |
例句：
The fisherman had a good catch today. 渔夫今天的收获不错。

There's a catch to this offer. 这个提议有陷阱。

近义词：haul, snare, trap, snag, hitch |
反义词：release |
词性：名词



ETYMOLOGY->
From Old North French *cachier, from Vulgar Latin *captiāre, frequentative of Latin capere "to take, seize."
