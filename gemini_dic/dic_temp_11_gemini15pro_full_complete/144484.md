outthrow:/ˌaʊtˈθroʊ/
verb
投掷得比…更远；超过…的投掷距离
to throw something further than someone else
He can outthrow any other pitcher on the team. 他能比队里任何其他投手投得更远。
outdistance, surpass, exceed
fall short of
