commiseration:/kəˌmɪzəˈreɪʃn/
n.
同情，怜悯
feeling or expression of sympathy and sorrow for someone
例句：She offered her commiserations on his loss. 她对他的损失表示同情。

例句：He received many expressions of commiseration after his accident.  事故发生后，他收到了很多慰问。

近义词：sympathy, condolence, compassion
反义词：indifference, apathy
词性：noun


commiserate:/kəˈmɪzəreɪt/
v.
同情，怜悯
express or feel sympathy or pity for someone
例句：I commiserated with her over the loss of her job. 我对她的失业表示同情。

例句：She commiserated with him on his failure. 她对他的失败表示同情。


近义词：sympathize, condole
反义词：ignore, disregard
词性：verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *commiseratio, from Latin commiserari "to feel pity," from com- "with" + miserari "to pity," from miser "wretched, unfortunate."
