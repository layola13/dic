genealogists:/ˌdʒiːniˈælədʒɪsts/ | n. |系谱学家；家谱学家| A person who studies family history and ancestry. | Example 1: The genealogists traced the family's lineage back to the 17th century.  系谱学家将家族谱系追溯到17世纪。

Example 2: Many genealogists use online databases to research their family trees. 许多系谱学家使用在线数据库来研究他们的家谱。 | Near synonyms: family historian, ancestry researcher | Antonym:  N/A (no direct opposite) | Noun
