adsorber:/ædˈsɔːrbər/ | n. | 吸附器；吸附剂 | A substance or device that attracts and holds (adsorbs) other materials on its surface. | Activated carbon is a common adsorber used in water filters.  活性炭是水过滤器中常用的吸附剂。| The adsorber removed the impurities from the air stream.  吸附器去除了气流中的杂质。| synonyms: adsorbent, absorber | antonyms: desorber | noun

adsorb:/ædˈsɔːrb/ | v. | 吸附 | To hold (molecules of a gas, liquid, or dissolved substance) to a surface in a thin layer. | The charcoal adsorbed the toxins. 木炭吸附了毒素。 | The sponge adsorbed the spilled water. 海绵吸收了溢出的水。 | synonyms: attract, accumulate | antonyms: desorb, release | verb
