manoeuvrable:/məˈnuːvərəbl/ /məˈnuːvrəbəl/| adj.|易操纵的，灵活的|Easy to steer or manipulate; agile.|The small car is very manoeuvrable in city traffic. 这辆小汽车在城市交通中非常灵活。|The new fighter jet is highly manoeuvrable, capable of executing complex aerial maneuvers.  这架新型战斗机非常易于操纵，能够执行复杂的空中机动。|Synonyms: agile, nimble, maneuverable, flexible, controllable|Antonyms: clumsy, awkward, unwieldy|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *manœuvrable*, from *manœuvrer* (“to maneuver”), from *manœuvre* (“maneuver”). Ultimately from Latin *manu operare* (“to work by hand”).
