concubinal: /kɒnˈkjuːbɪnəl/ /kɑːnˈkjuːbɪnəl/
adjective
姘合的，妾侍的，同居的
Relating to a concubine or concubinage; characteristic of a concubine.
The historian detailed the king's concubinal relationships, highlighting the complex social dynamics of the royal court.  这位历史学家详细描述了国王的姘居关系，突出了宫廷复杂的社会动态。

The concubinal lifestyle, though accepted in some cultures, often led to power imbalances and social stigma for the women involved.  虽然姘居的生活方式在某些文化中被接受，但它常常导致权力失衡，并给涉及的女性带来社会污名。

近义词:  cohabiting, extramarital, adulterous
反义词:  marital, monogamous
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *concubinālis, from Latin concubīna (“concubine”) + -ālis.
