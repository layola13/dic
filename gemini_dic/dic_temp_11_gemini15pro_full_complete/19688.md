bilestone:/ˈbaɪlˌstoʊn/ (US), /ˈbaɪlˌstəʊn/ (UK)| n.|胆结石|A small, hard crystalline mass formed abnormally in the gallbladder or bile ducts from bile pigments, cholesterol, and calcium salts.|He had to have his gallbladder removed because of bilestones. 他因为胆结石不得不切除胆囊。|Synonyms: gallstone, calculus|Antonyms: None|noun
