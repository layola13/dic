jacksmith:/ˈdʒæksmɪθ/| n.|铁匠，锻工|A person who makes and repairs metal objects, especially horseshoes and other ironwork.|The jacksmith skillfully crafted a new set of horseshoes for the horse. 铁匠熟练地为马打造了一套新的马蹄铁。|近义词：blacksmith, smith, farrier|反义词:|noun
