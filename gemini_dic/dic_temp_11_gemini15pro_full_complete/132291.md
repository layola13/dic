nondefensively:/ˌnɒndɪˈfɛnsɪvli/
adv.|非防御地，不带防备地|In a way that is not defensive; without trying to protect oneself from criticism or attack.  Not intended or likely to be perceived as hostile or threatening.|She spoke nondefensively about her past mistakes. 她毫不避讳地谈论她过去的错误。|Synonym: openly, frankly, candidly|Antonym: defensively, guardedly|词性:副词
