misdescribed:/ˌmɪsdɪˈskraɪbd/
verb
错误描述，描述不当
past participle of misdescribe: to describe incorrectly or inaccurately.
The product was misdescribed in the advertisement.  该产品在广告中描述不当。
The witness misdescribed the suspect to the police. 目击者向警方错误地描述了嫌疑人。
Synonyms: misrepresented, distorted, falsified
Antonyms: accurately described, correctly portrayed
词性: 动词(过去分词)
