craftswomen:/ˈkrɑːftswɪmɪn/
n.
女工匠；手工艺女性
A woman who practices a craft, especially one that requires skilled work with the hands.
The craftswomen of the village are known for their beautiful pottery. 这个村庄的女工匠以她们美丽的陶器而闻名。
近义词：artisan, craftsperson, artist
反义词：(无直接反义词，可考虑 amateur 业余爱好者)
词性: 名词

craftswomen:/ˈkrɑːftswɪmɪn/
词源:  From craft + -s + women.  "Craft" comes from Old English cræft "strength, skill, power," and "women" comes from Old English wīfmann, from wīf "woman" + mann "man, person."  Originally, "man" was gender-neutral.
