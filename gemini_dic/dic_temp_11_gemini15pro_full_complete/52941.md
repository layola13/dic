dined-out:/daɪnd aʊt/
|Verb phrase (past tense)|外出用餐|The act of eating a meal at a restaurant or other establishment, rather than at home.  It emphasizes the act of going out specifically for a meal.|We dined out at a fancy Italian restaurant last night. 我们昨晚在一家高档意大利餐厅用餐。|ate out, went out to eat|cooked at home, ate in|Verb phrase
Etymology-> dine (from Old French disner "to dine") + out (adverb indicating location away from home)
