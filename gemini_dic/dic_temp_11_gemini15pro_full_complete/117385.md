medica:/ˈmɛdɪkə/| n.|医疗的，医学的 (通常用作Medicaid或Medicare的组成部分)|Medicaid is a joint federal and state program that helps with medical costs for some people with limited income and resources.  医疗补助计划是由联邦和州联合管理的项目，旨在帮助一些收入和资源有限的人支付医疗费用。|近义词：medical, therapeutic|反义词: non-medical|词性:形容词

medica (as a standalone word): This word isn't typically used on its own in English. It's the feminine form of the Latin adjective "medicus," meaning "healing" or "medical."  However, its primary usage is as part of the proper nouns "Medicaid" and "Medicare."
