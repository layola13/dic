beepers:/ˈbiːpərz/| n. |寻呼机；BP机|A small electronic device that you carry so that people can leave messages for you.  It makes a beeping sound when someone tries to contact you.|例句：He left a message on my beeper. 他在我的寻呼机上留言了。

例句：In the 1990s, beepers were a popular way to stay in touch. 在20世纪90年代，寻呼机是一种很流行的保持联系的方式。|近义词：pager|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps cellphone)|词性:noun
