obbligato:/ˌɒblɪˈɡɑːtəʊ/ (UK), /ˌɑːblɪˈɡɑːtoʊ/ (US)| adj. n.|必要的，不可缺少的；（音乐） obbligato part |(Adjective) Essential; indispensable.  (Noun) A musical part that is essential to a piece and cannot be omitted.  A prominent, independent accompanying melody played or sung above the main melody.|Example (Adjective): The chef explained that fresh herbs were obbligato to the dish's success.  厨师解释说，新鲜香草是这道菜成功的必要条件。

Example (Noun):  The violinist played a beautiful obbligato to the singer's melody. 小提琴手为歌手的旋律演奏了一段优美的 obbligato.|近义词:essential, indispensable, required, necessary, obligatory|反义词:optional, nonessential, unnecessary|词性:adjective, noun

词源: From Italian *obbligato*, past participle of *obbligare* "to oblige," from Latin *obligare* "to bind, obligate."
