flouriness:/ˈflaʊərinəs/
n.
粉状；面粉状
The flouriness of the cake was a sign that it was overmixed.  蛋糕的粉状表明它被过度混合了。
The potatoes had an unpleasant flouriness.  这些土豆有一种令人不快的粉状口感。
dryness, mealiness, powderiness
smoothness, moistness
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From floury + -ness.  Floury comes from flour, from Old French flor, from Latin flos ("flower"). The original sense referred to the finest part of a ground substance, like the "flower" of wheat.
