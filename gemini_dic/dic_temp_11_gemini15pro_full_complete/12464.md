asp's:/æsps/| n.|毒蛇; 蝮蛇; 坏人; 心狠手辣的人|A venomous snake, especially of the family Viperidae. Figuratively, a cruel or treacherous person.|The asp is a symbol of evil in many cultures. |在许多文化中，蝮蛇是邪恶的象征。|synonyms: viper, adder, serpent, villain, scoundrel|antonyms: hero, saint|noun
