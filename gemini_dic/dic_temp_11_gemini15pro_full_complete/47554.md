deauthorize:/diːˈɔːθəˌraɪz/
verb
取消授权，撤销授权
To remove the authorization or permission granted to someone or something.
The company deauthorized the third-party app to access user data.  该公司取消了第三方应用程序访问用户数据的授权。
After the security breach, all employees were deauthorized to access the confidential files.  安全漏洞事件发生后，所有员工都被取消了访问机密文件的授权。
近义词:revoke, withdraw, cancel, rescind
反义词:authorize, empower, grant
词性:v.


deauthorize:/diːˈɔːθəˌraɪz/
词源: de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + authorize (verb meaning "to give official permission or approval")
词性:v.
