downloaded:/ˈdaʊnˌloʊdəd/ (US) /ˈdaʊnˌləʊdɪd/ (UK)| v. | 下载的；已下载 | past participle of "download," meaning that something has been transferred from a computer network or the internet to a personal computer or device. | I have downloaded the latest software update. 我已经下载了最新的软件更新。| The downloaded file was corrupted. 下载的文件损坏了。 | Synonym: transferred, retrieved, copied | Antonym: uploaded | Verb (past participle)
