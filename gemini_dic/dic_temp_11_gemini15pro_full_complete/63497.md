entwining:/ɪnˈtwaɪnɪŋ/ | adj. v. | 缠绕的，交织的 | Twisting or winding together; interweaving. |  The entwining branches of the trees created a canopy overhead.  交错的树枝在头顶形成了一个树冠。 |  The vines were entwining around the fence. 藤蔓缠绕在篱笆上。 | 近义词：intertwining, interweaving, twisting, winding, braiding | 反义词：untangling, separating, unraveling | 词性: adjective, present participle of entwine
entwine:/ɪnˈtwaɪn/ | vt. vi. | 缠绕，交织 | To twist or twine together. |  She entwined her fingers with his. 她把自己的手指和他的手指缠绕在一起。 | The two lovers entwined their arms as they walked.  那对恋人挽着胳膊散步。 | 近义词：intertwine, interweave, twist, wind, braid | 反义词：untangle, separate, unravel


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *entwinen, from en- + twinen (“to twine”). Equivalent to en- + twine.
