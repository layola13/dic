nonforfeiture:/ˌnɑːnfɔːrˈfɪtʃər/ | n. | 不可丧失，(尤指人寿保险)不失效，不作废 |  A clause in an insurance policy that allows the insured to receive some benefits even if premiums are not paid. This can take the form of a reduced paid-up policy, extended term insurance, or a cash surrender value. | Example: The nonforfeiture clause protected her investment when she could no longer afford the premiums.  由于她无力继续支付保费，不失效条款保护了她的投资。 | Example:  Understanding the nonforfeiture options is crucial when choosing a life insurance policy.  在选择人寿保险时，了解不失效选项至关重要。 | Synonym: nonlapse, incontestability | Antonym: forfeiture, lapse | noun
