ablaqueation: /ˌæbləˈkweɪʃən/
| n. |  土壤剥离; 根部暴露 | The uncovering and exposure of roots of trees, especially for the purpose of applying medicaments. |  The gardener practiced ablaqueation to treat the diseased roots of the old oak tree. 园丁对老橡树的病根进行土壤剥离以进行治疗。| 近义词:  root exposure, uncovering | 反义词: covering, burying | 词性: noun
