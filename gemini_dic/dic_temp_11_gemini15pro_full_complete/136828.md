nonsigner:/ˌnɒnˈsaɪnər/ /ˌnɑːnˈsaɪnər/
noun
未签名者；拒签者
a person who has not signed a particular document or agreement.
例句：The nonsigners of the treaty were subject to sanctions.  那些没有签署条约的国家要受到制裁。

例句：The nonsigners argued that the agreement was unfair.  未签署者认为该协议不公平。

近义词：objector, dissenter, refusenik
反义词：signer, signatory
词性：n.
