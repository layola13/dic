emulsifies:/ɪˈmʌlsɪfaɪz/
verb
乳化
makes or becomes an emulsion; combines two liquids that usually don't mix.
The detergent emulsifies grease and oil.  这种洗涤剂能乳化油脂。

The liver produces bile, which emulsifies fats. 肝脏产生胆汁，胆汁乳化脂肪。

blend, mix, combine, homogenize
separate, divide
