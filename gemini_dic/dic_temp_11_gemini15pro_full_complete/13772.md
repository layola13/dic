audience:/ˈɔːdiəns/ (US) /ˈɔːdiəns/ (UK)| n.|观众；听众；读者；读者群；拥护者|A group of people who have gathered to watch or listen to something (a play, concert, somebody speaking, etc.) or are targeted by a TV program, book, etc. |The audience applauded the performers enthusiastically. 观众热情地为表演者鼓掌。|
The author wrote the book for a young adult audience. 作者为青少年读者写了这本书。|
Synonyms:  spectators, listeners, viewers, readership, following |Antonyms: performers, speakers, presenters|noun
