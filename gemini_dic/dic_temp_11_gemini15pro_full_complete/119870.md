milieu:/miːlˈjɜː/ /mɪlˈjuː/ |n.|环境，氛围；周围；出身背景|A person's social environment.  The setting or surroundings, especially social or cultural, of something. |She grew up in a privileged milieu. 她在一个优越的环境中长大。

|Synonyms: environment, atmosphere, setting, surroundings, background, context, ambience, climate, sphere, domain
|Antonyms:  None (It's difficult to have a direct antonym for "milieu.")
|Noun
