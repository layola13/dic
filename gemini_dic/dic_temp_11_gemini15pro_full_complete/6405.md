amphiprostyle:/ˈæmfɪprəˌstaɪl/| adj.|前后柱式的，两端有柱廊的|Having a portico at both ends, as of a temple.|The temple was built in the amphiprostyle, with a row of columns at both the front and back. 这座寺庙采用前后柱式建造，前后都有一排柱子。|近义词：double porticoed|反义词：in antis, peripteral|词性: adjective
