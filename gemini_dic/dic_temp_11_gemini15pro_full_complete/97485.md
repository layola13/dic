ingenuities:/ˌɪndʒəˈnjuːɪtiz/
n.|独创性，巧妙，精巧的装置|The project demonstrated the ingenuity of the engineers. 这个项目展示了工程师们的独创性。|
例句：The engineers displayed remarkable ingenuity in solving the problem. 工程师们在解决这个问题时展现了非凡的独创性。

例句：Her ingenuity allowed her to create a successful business from scratch. 她的聪明才智使她白手起家创建了一家成功的企业。

近义词：inventiveness, creativity, resourcefulness, cleverness
反义词：lack of imagination, incompetence
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

ingenuity:/ˌɪndʒəˈnjuːɪti/
n.|独创性，巧妙，精巧的装置|The system is a marvel of ingenuity. 该系统是独创性的奇迹。|
例句：We were impressed by the ingenuity of the design. 我们对设计的巧妙印象深刻。

例句：She showed great ingenuity in overcoming the obstacles.  她在克服障碍方面表现出了极大的聪明才智。

近义词：innovation, brilliance, skill, artistry
反义词：clumsiness, awkwardness
词性: 名词 (singular noun)


词源: Late Middle English: from Latin ingenuitas, from ingenuus ‘native, freeborn, noble, frank’ (see ingenuous). The original senses were ‘ingenuousness, nobility of character’, later ‘ingenuous disposition, frankness’.
