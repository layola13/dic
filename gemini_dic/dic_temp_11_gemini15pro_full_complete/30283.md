cementlike:/sɪˈmɛntlaɪk/
adjective
像水泥的，坚硬的
Resembling cement in appearance or consistency.
The mixture hardened into a cementlike mass.  这种混合物硬化成一种像水泥一样的块状物。
The mud had dried to a cementlike consistency. 泥土已经干成像水泥一样的稠度。
近义词:concrete-like, hard, solid, firm, rigid
反义词:soft, pliable, flexible
