molestful:/məˈlɛstfʊl/ | adj. | 骚扰的；恼人的；有害的 | Causing annoyance, disturbance, or harm.  Characterized by molesting or a disposition to molest. |  His molestful behavior towards his colleagues created a hostile work environment. 他对同事的骚扰行为制造了充满敌意的工作环境。 | The molestful noise from the construction site made it impossible to concentrate. 建筑工地恼人的噪音使人无法集中注意力。 | 近义词：annoying, harassing, troublesome, irritating, vexing | 反义词：soothing, calming, pleasant, agreeable | 词性: 形容词

词源: From molest + -ful.  "Molest" comes from Old French "molester"  and ultimately from Latin "molestare" meaning "to trouble, disturb, annoy."
