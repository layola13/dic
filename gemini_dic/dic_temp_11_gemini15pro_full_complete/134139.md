nonimplication:/ˌnɒnɪmplɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.|非蕴涵，不蕴含|The state of not implying something; lack of implication.  A logical relation such that a proposition does not entail another proposition.|The defendant's silence is not a nonimplication of guilt. 被告的沉默并不意味着他没有罪。|Synonyms: non-entailment, lack of implication|Antonyms: implication, entailment|noun
