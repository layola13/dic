kindnesses:/ˈkaɪndnɪsɪz/
n.
仁慈的行为；善举；好意
acts of generosity or compassion
She overwhelmed me with her kindnesses. 她对我的好意让我不知所措。

近义词:benevolence, generosity, charity, compassion, goodwill, favors, courtesies
反义词:cruelty, unkindness, meanness, hostility
词性:名词(复数)
