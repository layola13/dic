oubliette:/ˌuːbliˈet/ /ˌoʊbliˈet/| n.|地牢，土牢|A dungeon with an opening only at the top, originally a form of prison.|The prisoner was thrown into the oubliette and forgotten. 囚犯被扔进地牢，被人遗忘了。|Synonyms: dungeon, cell, pit, prison|Antonyms: freedom, liberty|noun
