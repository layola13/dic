disconformable:/ˌdɪskənˈfɔːrməbl/ | adj. |不整合的，不一致的 |  Describing a geological unconformity where horizontally parallel strata of sedimentary rock are deposited on tilted and eroded layers, producing an angular discordance with the older rocks below. | The geologist identified a disconformable contact between the limestone and the sandstone. 地质学家发现了石灰岩和砂岩之间的不整合接触面。| The disconformable layers revealed a period of uplift and erosion before the younger sediments were deposited. 不整合地层揭示了在新沉积物沉积之前一段抬升和侵蚀的时期。 | synonymous: nonconformable, discordant | antonymous: conformable, concordant | geology
