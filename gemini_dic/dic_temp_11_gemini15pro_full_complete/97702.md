inimical:/ɪˈnɪmɪk(ə)l/
adjective
有害的；不利的；敌意的
tending to obstruct or harm; unfriendly; hostile.
例句：Excessive bureaucracy is inimical to efficiency. 过多的官僚主义不利于效率。

例句：The policy was seen as inimical to the interests of small businesses. 这项政策被认为对小企业的利益有害。

近义词：hostile, antagonistic, adverse, harmful, detrimental, unfavorable, opposed
反义词：friendly, beneficial, favorable, helpful, advantageous
词性: adjective
