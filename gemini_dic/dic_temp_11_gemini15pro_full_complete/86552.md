helia:/ˈhiːliə/ | n. | 太阳,日光; 向日葵 |  A poetic term for the sun, or sunlight; also used to refer to sunflowers. | The poet described helia as the lifeblood of the earth. 诗人将日光描述为地球的生命线。| synonym: sun, sunlight, sunflower | antonym: darkness, night | noun
