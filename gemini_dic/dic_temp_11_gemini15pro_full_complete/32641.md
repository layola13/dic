chisellers:/ˈtʃɪzələrz/
n.
凿子使用者；骗子；吝啬鬼
People who use chisels, especially sculptors or stonemasons. Also, can refer to someone who cheats or swindles others, or someone who is very stingy with money.
The chisellers were hard at work on the stone facade. 凿子工人在石头立面上努力工作。
He's a real chiseler, always trying to get something for nothing. 他真是个骗子，总是想不劳而获。
近义词:swindlers, cheats, frauds, misers, tightwads
反义词: philanthropists, generous people
