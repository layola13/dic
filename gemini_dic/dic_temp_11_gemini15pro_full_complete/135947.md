nonpurposive:/ˌnɒnˈpɜːpəsɪv/ | adj. | 无目的的，非目的性的 | Lacking or not having a purpose; unintentional. |  He took a nonpurposive stroll through the park, simply enjoying the fresh air. 他漫无目的地在公园里散步，只是享受着新鲜的空气。| Near Synonyms: aimless, unintentional, random, undirected | Antonyms: purposeful, intentional, deliberate, directed | Word type: adjective

Etymology:
From non- + purposive.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "purposive" comes from "purpose" (from Old French porpos, meaning "aim, intention") + "-ive," a suffix forming adjectives.
