blini:/ˈbliːni/ /blɪˈniː/| n. |薄煎饼（尤指俄式或东欧的）|A thin pancake, especially one made with yeast and served with sour cream or caviar. |例句：The blini were served with smoked salmon and crème fraîche.  薄煎饼配熏鲑鱼和法式鲜奶油。

例句：She made delicious blini for breakfast. 她早餐做了美味的薄煎饼。

近义词：pancake, crepe |反义词: (无直接反义词)|词性:名词
