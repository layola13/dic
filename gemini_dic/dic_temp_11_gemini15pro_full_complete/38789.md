conduction:/kənˈdʌkʃən/ | n. |传导，导电，导热|The process by which heat or electricity is directly transmitted through a substance when there is a difference of temperature or of electrical potential between adjoining regions, without movement of the material.| Heat transfer by conduction can occur in solids, liquids, and gases. 热传导可以发生在固体、液体和气体中。|  Metals have high thermal conductivity. 金属具有高导热性。| synonyms: transmission, conveyance, transfer | antonyms: insulation, resistance | noun
