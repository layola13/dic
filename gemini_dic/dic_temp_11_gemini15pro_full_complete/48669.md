defer:/dɪˈfɜːr/ (US) /dɪˈfɜː(r)/ (UK)| v. |推迟，延期；遵从|To postpone something to a later time; to yield respectfully to another's opinion or wishes.|The committee deferred the decision until their next meeting. 委员会将决定推迟到下次会议。|synonyms: postpone, delay, adjourn, put off, shelve, table|antonyms: expedite, hasten, advance|verb

defer:/dɪˈfɜːr/ (US) /dɪˈfɜː(r)/ (UK)| n. |推迟，延期 (罕见用法)|The act of deferring or postponing.|The deferral of the decision caused much frustration.  决定的推迟引起了很多挫败感。|synonyms: postponement, delay, adjournment|antonyms: expedition, hastening, advancement|noun
