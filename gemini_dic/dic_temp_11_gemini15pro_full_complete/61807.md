emphysema:/ˌɛmfɪˈsiːmə/| n. |肺气肿|A condition in which the air sacs of the lungs are damaged and enlarged, causing breathlessness.|His emphysema made it difficult for him to climb stairs. 他的肺气肿使他爬楼梯很困难。|Synonyms: COPD (chronic obstructive pulmonary disease)|Antonyms: Healthy lung tissue|Noun

emphysema:/ˌɛmfɪˈsiːmə/| n. |肺气肿|A long-term, progressive disease of the lungs that primarily causes shortness of breath.|She was diagnosed with emphysema after years of smoking.  她吸烟多年后被诊断出患有肺气肿。|Synonyms: pulmonary emphysema|Antonyms: Healthy lung function|Noun
