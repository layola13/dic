cornamute: /ˈkɔːrnəˌmjuːt/ | n. |弱音器；闭塞音；（尤指圆号的）弱音器|A cone-shaped mute used in a brass instrument, especially a horn, to soften the tone.|The horn player inserted the cornamute to create a mellow sound. 圆号演奏者插入弱音器，以产生柔和的声音。|Synonyms: mute, damper, sordine|Antonyms: amplifier|noun
