on-screen:/ˈɒn skriːn/ (UK), /ˈɑːn skriːn/ (US)| adj. adv.|在屏幕上的，屏幕显示的|Displayed on a screen, such as a television, computer, or cinema screen.|The on-screen graphics were impressive.  屏幕上的图形令人印象深刻。|例句：The on-screen instructions were easy to follow. 屏幕上的说明很容易理解。|近义词：displayed, projected, digital|反义词：off-screen, printed|词性:形容词,副词
