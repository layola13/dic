gripsack: /ˈɡrɪpsæk/ | n. | 背包，旅行包 | A bag, often of canvas or other sturdy material, carried on the back or over the shoulder, used for carrying supplies or personal belongings, especially when traveling or hiking. |  He packed his gripsack with essentials for the weekend camping trip. 他把周末露营旅行的必需品装进了背包里。 |  The hiker's gripsack was heavy with food and water.  徒步旅行者的背包里装满了食物和水。 | Synonyms: backpack, rucksack, knapsack | Antonyms: suitcase, briefcase | noun
