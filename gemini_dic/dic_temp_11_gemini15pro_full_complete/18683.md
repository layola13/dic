bequeathments: /bɪˈkwiːðmənts/
| n. |遗产，遗赠物|Something that is bequeathed; a legacy.|He left generous bequeathments to his children in his will. 他在遗嘱中给他的孩子们留下了丰厚的遗产。|
|近义词：legacies, inheritances, bequests|
|反义词： (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用"acquisition" 获得) |
|词性: 名词|
|词源: From bequeath + -ment,  ultimately from Old English *becweðan "to declare, speak, utter; leave by will," from be- + cweðan "to say."|
