generalissimo:/ˌdʒɛnərəˈlɪsɪmoʊ/
n.
最高统帅，大元帅
The supreme commander of a combined military force, especially one who holds dictatorial powers.
Generalissimo Franco ruled Spain for decades.  佛朗哥大元帅统治了西班牙数十年。

The generalissimo ordered a full-scale attack. 最高统帅下令全面进攻。

近义词: commander-in-chief, supreme commander
反义词: subordinate, private
词性: noun
