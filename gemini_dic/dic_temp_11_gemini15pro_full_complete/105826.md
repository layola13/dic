kit-and-caboodle:/ˌkɪt ənd kəˈbuːdl/| n. | 全部，所有东西，一切 | everything; the whole lot.  It refers to all of one's belongings or the entirety of something. | He packed up his kit and caboodle and moved to California. 他收拾好全部家当，搬去了加利福尼亚。| synonyms: everything, the whole shebang, the whole nine yards, lock, stock, and barrel | antonyms: nothing, none | noun
