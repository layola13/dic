motivation:/ˌmoʊtɪˈveɪʃn/ | n. | 动机；积极性；动力 | The desire or willingness to do something; enthusiasm. |  Her motivation for learning Chinese is to travel to China. 她学习中文的动机是想去中国旅行。

| Example 2: Lack of motivation is a common problem for students. 缺乏动力是学生们普遍存在的问题。 | Synonyms: incentive, inspiration, drive, impetus | Antonyms: apathy, indifference, lethargy | noun
