obfuscate: /ˈɒbfəskeɪt/ (UK) /ˌɑːbfəˈskeɪt/ (US) | v. |  使模糊；使混乱；使困惑 | to make something less clear and harder to understand, especially intentionally | The government obfuscated the truth about the war. 政府模糊了战争的真相。|  The company's financial statements were deliberately obfuscated. 该公司的财务报表被故意混淆了。| synonyms: confuse, obscure, bewilder, mystify, conceal | antonyms: clarify, elucidate, explain, illuminate | verb
