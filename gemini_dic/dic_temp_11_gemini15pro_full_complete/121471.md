miskicked:/ˌmɪsˈkɪkt/
verb
踢偏，踢失误
kick something incorrectly or badly.
He miskicked the ball and it went out of bounds. 他把球踢偏了，球出界了。
近义词: mishit, flub
反义词: kick accurately, connect perfectly
词性: verb (past tense of miskick)
