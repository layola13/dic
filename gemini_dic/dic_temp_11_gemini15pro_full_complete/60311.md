eglomerate:/ɪˈɡlɒmərət/ | n. adj. | 聚集体，混合物；聚集的 | A confused or jumbled mass; a mixture of diverse elements. Formed by gathering into a rounded mass; clustered. |  The rock is an eglomerate of various minerals. 这块岩石是由各种矿物聚集而成的。 | 近义词: conglomeration, aggregate, mixture, composite | 反义词: element, component | n.

eglomerate:/ɪˈɡlɒmərət/ | adj. | 聚集的 | Formed by gathering into a rounded mass; clustered. | The eglomerate flowers formed a beautiful bouquet. 聚集的花朵形成了一束美丽的鲜花。 | 近义词: clustered, aggregated | 反义词: dispersed, scattered | adj.

词源: From Latin *eglōmerātus, past participle of *eglōmerāre "to wind into a ball," from e- "out" + glōmus "ball."
