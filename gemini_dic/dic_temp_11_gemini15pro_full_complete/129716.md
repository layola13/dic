non-elect:/ˌnɒn ɪˈlɛkt/ | adj. | 未当选的；非选举产生的 | Not elected to office. |  The non-elect candidate conceded defeat graciously. 未当选的候选人优雅地承认了失败。| The non-elect members of the committee have limited voting rights. 委员会中非选举产生的成员投票权有限。 | 近义词: unelected, defeated, unsuccessful | 反义词: elected, chosen, successful | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "elect" (from Latin "electus," past participle of "eligere," meaning "to choose out").
