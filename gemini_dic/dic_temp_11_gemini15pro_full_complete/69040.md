fatwa: /ˈfæt.wɑː/ (US), /ˈfæt.wə/ (UK)| n. |宗教裁决，法特瓦|A legal pronouncement in Islam, issued by a religious law specialist, on a specific issue.  A fatwa is not legally binding in most Muslim communities but carries significant moral weight.|The ayatollah issued a fatwa against the author.  阿亚图拉对这位作家发布了宗教裁决。|Synonyms: decree, edict, ruling, pronouncement|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym exists, possibly "reversal/repudiation" in context)|noun
