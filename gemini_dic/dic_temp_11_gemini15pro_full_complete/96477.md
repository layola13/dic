individuates:/ɪndɪˈvɪdʒueɪts/
verb
赋予个性；使个性化；区分；个体化
To distinguish from others; make individual or separate.
The artist individuates each character in the painting through distinct brushstrokes and color palettes.  艺术家通过独特的笔触和调色板使画作中的每个角色都具有个性。
Her unique style individuates her from other designers. 她独特的风格使她有别于其他设计师。
distinguishes, separates, differentiates, characterizes, singularizes
generalizes, conforms, assimilates
v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin individuatus, past participle of individuare "to make individual," from Latin individuus "indivisible," from in- "not" + dividuus "divisible," from dividere "to divide."
