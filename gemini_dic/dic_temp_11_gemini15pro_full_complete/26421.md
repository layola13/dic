byline: /ˈbaɪlaɪn/ | n. | 作者署名；（文章开头或结尾处的）作者姓名 | A line at the beginning or end of a piece of writing in a newspaper or magazine that gives the writer's name. | The article's byline identified the author as a freelance journalist. 这篇文章的署名表明作者是一位自由撰稿的记者。| by-line |  | noun

byline: /ˈbaɪlaɪn/ | n. |  稿件署名；演员阵容 | The list of performers or other people involved in a movie, play, or other production. | The film boasts an impressive byline, including several Oscar winners.  这部电影的演员阵容令人印象深刻，其中包括几位奥斯卡奖得主。| credits, cast list | | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "by" (indicating agency or authorship) + "line" (a row of written or printed words).  Essentially, the "line" by which the author is identified.
