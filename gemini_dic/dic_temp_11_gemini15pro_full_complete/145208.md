overbank: /ˈoʊvərˌbæŋk/ | adj. n.| 漫滩的，河漫滩；|  Relating to or occurring on a floodplain or overbank area, specifically when a river overflows its banks.  The overbank area is the land adjacent to a river that is covered with water when the river floods. | The overbank deposits were rich in silt. 漫滩沉积物富含淤泥。 | synonyms: floodplain, alluvial plain, riparian, flood-prone | antonyms: channel, riverbed | adjective, noun

overbank: /ˈoʊvərˌbæŋk/ | n.| 河漫滩 | The land adjacent to a river which is covered with water when the river overflows its banks. | The farmer lost his crops when the river flooded its overbank. 当河水漫过河漫滩时，农民的庄稼颗粒无收。| synonyms: floodplain, alluvial plain, riparian | antonyms: channel, riverbed | noun
