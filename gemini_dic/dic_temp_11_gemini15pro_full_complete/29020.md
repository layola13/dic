cartograph:/ˈkɑːrtəˌɡræf/ | n. | 地图；图表 | A map, especially one showing geographical features. |  Ancient cartographs often depicted mythical creatures. 古代地图上经常描绘神话生物。 | synonym: map, chart, plan | antonym: globe (arguably, as a 3D representation) | noun

cartographs:/ˈkɑːrtəˌɡræfs/ | n. | 地图（复数） | Maps, especially ones showing geographical features. |  The library has a collection of rare cartographs dating back to the 16th century.  这家图书馆收藏了可追溯到16世纪的珍稀地图。 | synonym: maps, charts, plans | antonym: globes (arguably) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French cartographe, from Greek khartēs "leaf of papyrus, chart" + -graphe "something written or drawn."
