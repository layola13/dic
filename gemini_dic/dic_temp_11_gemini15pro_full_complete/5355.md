allurements:/əˈlʊərmənts/
| n. |诱惑；魅力；吸引力|Something that allures; an attraction. | The allurements of city life drew her away from her small town. 城市生活的诱惑吸引她离开了小镇。|
| n. |诱惑物；诱饵|Temptations; enticements. | The salesman used various allurements to persuade customers to buy his products. 销售员用各种诱惑来说服顾客购买他的产品。
|近义词：attractions, temptations, enticements, charms, appeals|
|反义词：deterrents, repellents|
|词性：名词（复数）|
