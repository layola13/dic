dumbest:/ˈdʌməst/| adj.|最愚蠢的|Superlative form of dumb: most dumb.  It means lacking intelligence or good judgment; stupid.|That was the dumbest movie I've ever seen. 那是我看过的最愚蠢的电影。|Synonyms: stupidest, most foolish, silliest|Antonyms: smartest, brightest, wisest|adjective
Etymology->From Middle English *dombe, from Old English dumb (“mute, silent, speechless”), from Proto-Germanic *dumbaz (“dumb, stupid”).|
