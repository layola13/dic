dissenting:/dɪˈsɛntɪŋ/ | adj. | 异议的，不同意的 | holding or expressing opinions that are at variance with those commonly or officially held |  The judge wrote a dissenting opinion. 法官写了一份异议意见。| synonyms: disagreeing, opposing, objecting, differing, conflicting, contrary, at odds, at variance | antonyms: agreeing, concurring, assenting, approving, supporting | adjective


dissenting:/dɪˈsɛntɪŋ/ | n. | 异议，不同意见 (通常用复数形式，如 dissentings) | the expression or holding of opinions at variance with those previously, commonly, or officially held |  His dissentings from established church doctrine eventually led to his excommunication. 他对既定教会教义的异议最终导致他被逐出教会。| synonyms: disagreement, opposition, objection, difference, conflict, variance | antonyms: agreement, concurrence, assent, approval, support | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dissentire* "to differ in sentiment, disagree," from dis- "apart, away" + sentire "to feel, think."
