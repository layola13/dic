calymene:/ˈkælɪˌmiːn/| n.|卡利米尼三叶虫|A genus of extinct trilobites, common in the Ordovician and Silurian periods, characterized by a broad, flattened body and the ability to roll into a ball.|The museum displayed a well-preserved calymene fossil.  博物馆展出了一个保存完好的卡利米尼三叶虫化石。|Calymene are easily recognized by their distinctive head shields. 卡利米尼三叶虫很容易通过它们独特的头部护盾辨认出来。|Synonym: trilobite|Antonym: N/A|noun
