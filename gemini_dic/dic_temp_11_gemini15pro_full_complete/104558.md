kamshin:/ˈkɑːmʃɪn/ | n. | 热风，热浪 | A hot, dry wind, often dusty or sandy, that blows across deserts or semi-arid regions. | Example 1: The kamshin blew relentlessly across the desert, making travel difficult.  热风无情地吹过沙漠，使旅行变得困难。

Example 2: The villagers sought shelter from the scorching kamshin. 村民们寻找躲避酷热热风的庇护所。 | 近义词: sirocco, simoom, khamsin | 反义词: cool breeze, north wind | 词性: 名词

词源: From Arabic خماسين (khamsīn, “fifty”), because it typically blows for approximately fifty days in spring. 源自阿拉伯语خماسين (khamsīn，“五十”)，因为它通常在春天吹大约五十天。
