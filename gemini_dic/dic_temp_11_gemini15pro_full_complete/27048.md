calendaring:/ˈkælɪndərɪŋ/
n.
日程安排，日历制作
the process of scheduling appointments, meetings, and other events, typically using a calendar application.
例句：Calendaring software helps manage busy schedules. 日程安排软件有助于管理繁忙的日程。

例句：Effective calendaring is essential for project management.  有效的日程安排对于项目管理至关重要。

近义词：scheduling, appointment booking
反义词：(No direct antonym, could consider disorganization)
词性: noun


词源:  Derived from "calendar" (from Old French "calendier," ultimately from Latin "calendarium" - account book), with the suffix "-ing" forming a noun of action or process.
