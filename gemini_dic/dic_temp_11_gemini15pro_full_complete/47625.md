debilitant:/dɪˈbɪlɪtənt/| adj. n.|使人衰弱的；使人虚弱的；衰弱物；虚弱因素|Something that debilitates; causing weakness.|The debilitating effects of the illness kept her bedridden for months.  这种疾病带来的虚弱让她卧床数月。|近义词：weakening, enervating, enfeebling, incapacitating|反义词：strengthening, invigorating, fortifying|adjective
debilitant:/dɪˈbɪlɪtənt/| n.|衰弱物；虚弱因素|Something that debilitates; causing weakness.|Stress can be a major debilitant. 压力可能是一个主要的虚弱因素。|近义词：affliction, malady|反义词：tonic, restorative|noun
