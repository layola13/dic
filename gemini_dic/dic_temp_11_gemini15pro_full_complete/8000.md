anthophilous:/ænˈθɒfɪləs/
adjective
喜花的，由昆虫授粉的
Attracted to or pollinated by insects, especially bees.
例句：Anthophilous plants often have brightly colored petals. 喜花的植物通常有颜色鲜艳的花瓣。

例句：Many fruit trees are anthophilous, relying on bees for pollination. 许多果树都是喜花的，依靠蜜蜂授粉。
近义词：entomophilous
反义词：anemophilous, hydrophilous
词性: adjective
