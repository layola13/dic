nonsurgical:/ˌnɑːnˈsɜːrdʒɪkəl/
adjective
非手术的
Not involving surgery; performed or administered without surgery.
She opted for a nonsurgical cosmetic procedure. 她选择了一种非手术的美容方法。
The doctor recommended nonsurgical treatment for his knee pain. 医生建议对他膝盖疼痛进行非手术治疗。
近义词:medical, therapeutic, conservative
反义词:surgical, operative
词性:adjective

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + surgical (adjective meaning "relating to surgery")
