concealedness:/kənˈsiːldnəs/| n.|隐蔽性，隐藏状态|The concealedness of the weapon made it difficult to detect. 武器的隐蔽性使它难以被发现。|例句：The concealedness of his motives made him seem suspicious. 他动机的隐蔽性使他看起来很可疑。

conceal:/kənˈsiːl/| vt.|隐藏，隐瞒|He tried to conceal his disappointment. 他试图掩饰他的失望。|例句：She concealed the letter under her pillow. 她把信藏在枕头下。
