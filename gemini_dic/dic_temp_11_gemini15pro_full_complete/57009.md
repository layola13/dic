double-parking:/ˈdʌbəl ˈpɑːrkɪŋ/ | n. | 并排停车；双排停车 | Parking a vehicle beside another vehicle that is already parked at the curb, thus blocking it. |  He got a ticket for double-parking in front of the store. 他因为在商店前并排停车而收到罚单。

Double-parking is illegal in most cities. 在大多数城市，并排停车是违法的。 | 近义词: illegal parking | 反义词: legal parking | 词性: 名词
