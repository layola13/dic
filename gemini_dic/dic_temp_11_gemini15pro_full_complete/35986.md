codeshare:/ˈkoʊdʃɛr/| n. |代码共享；共用航班|An arrangement where two or more airlines share the same flight.|Example: I booked a codeshare flight on United, but it was actually operated by Lufthansa. 我预订了联合航空的代码共享航班，但实际上是由汉莎航空运营的.|Example:  The codeshare agreement allows passengers to earn miles on either airline. 代码共享协议允许乘客在任何一家航空公司赚取里程。|近义词：joint operation, alliance|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "independent operation")|词性:noun
