loved: /lʌvd/ | v. adj. | 爱过；被爱的 | past tense and past participle of love, indicating affection, fondness, or deep caring felt in the past. Can also be used as an adjective to describe something that was once loved or cherished. |  I loved the movie. 我很喜欢这部电影。| Synonyms: adored, cherished, treasured | Antonyms: hated, despised, loathed | verb, adjective
