advantageously:/ˌædvənˈteɪdʒəsli/
adverb
有利地，有效地
In a way that is advantageous; to someone's advantage.
She was advantageously positioned to observe the whole scene. 她所处的位置有利于观察整个场面。
Near Antonyms: disadvantageously, unfavorably, poorly, badly
Synonyms: profitably, beneficially, gainfully, usefully
词性: 副词
