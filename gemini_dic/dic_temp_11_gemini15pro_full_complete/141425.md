ophryon:/ˈɒfrɪɒn/ (US) /ˈɒfriˌɑn/| n. |眉间点|The point on the forehead midway between the eyebrows, just above the root of the nose.  It's an anthropological landmark used in measurements of the skull.|The anthropologist measured the distance between the ophryon and the glabella.|近义词: glabella (though slightly different in location)|反义词: N/A|noun
