margaritiferous:/ˌmɑːrɡərɪˈtɪfərəs/
adjective
产珍珠的；含珍珠的
Producing pearls; containing pearls.
例句：The margaritiferous oyster is highly valued for its pearls.  产珍珠的牡蛎因其珍珠而备受珍视。

例句：The divers searched the seabed for margaritiferous shells.  潜水员在海底搜寻含有珍珠的贝壳。

近义词：pearl-bearing, nacreous
反义词：barren, unproductive
词性: adjective
