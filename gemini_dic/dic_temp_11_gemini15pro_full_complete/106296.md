knur:/nɜːr/ (US), /nəːr/ (UK)| n.|节; 瘤; 疙瘩; 难点|A small rounded protuberance; a knot, knob, or lump, especially in wood.  A difficulty or complication.|The carpenter smoothed out the knurs in the wood before staining it. 木匠在染色前把木头上的节都磨光了。|近义词：knot, knob, lump, snag, difficulty, problem|反义词:smoothness, ease|词性:名词
