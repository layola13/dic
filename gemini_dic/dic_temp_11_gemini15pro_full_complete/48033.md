decivilize: /diːˈsɪvɪlaɪz/ | vt. | 使丧失文明，使野蛮化 | To cause someone or something to lose the qualities and behaviors considered civilized; to make barbarous or uncultured. |  The prolonged war has decivilized the entire region. 长期的战争使整个地区丧失了文明。

The constant exposure to violence has decivilized many young people. 持续接触暴力使许多年轻人变得野蛮。 | 近义词: barbarize, brutalize | 反义词: civilize, refine | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "reverse" or "remove") + civilize (from Latin civilis "relating to a citizen, civil, courteous").
