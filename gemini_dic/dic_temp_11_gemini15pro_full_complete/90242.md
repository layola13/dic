houseboated:/ˈhaʊsboʊtɪd/
verb
乘船屋旅行，居住在船屋上
spent time living or traveling in a houseboat
We houseboated on Lake Powell last summer.  去年夏天我们住在鲍威尔湖的船屋上。
They've houseboated down the river several times. 他们已经多次乘船屋沿河而下。
近义词: lived on a houseboat, cruised in a houseboat
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider) stayed in a hotel, camped
