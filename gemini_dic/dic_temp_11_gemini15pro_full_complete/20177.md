biracialism:/ˌbaɪˈreɪʃəlɪzəm/
n.|双种族制，种族融合|The concept of biracialism promotes the idea of a society where people of different racial backgrounds can coexist and intermarry. 双种族制的概念提倡一种不同种族背景的人们可以共存和通婚的社会。|例句：The United States is becoming an increasingly biracial society. 美国正在日益成为一个种族融合的社会。

biracialism:/ˌbaɪˈreɪʃəlɪzəm/
n.|双种族状态|Her biracialism has shaped her unique perspective on identity and culture. 她的双种族身份塑造了她对身份和文化的独特视角。|例句：He often explores themes of biracialism in his writing. 他经常在他的作品中探讨双种族主义的主题。|近义词：multiracialism, racial integration|反义词：racial segregation, racial purity|词性:noun
