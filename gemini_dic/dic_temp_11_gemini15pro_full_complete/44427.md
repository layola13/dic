cross-posted:/ˌkrɒsˈpəʊstɪd/ /ˌkrɑːsˈpoʊstɪd/ | adj. | 交叉发布的，跨版发布的 | Posted in more than one online forum or newsgroup. |  I cross-posted my question on several tech forums to get more responses. 我把我的问题交叉发布到几个技术论坛，以获得更多回复。

| Example: The job ad was cross-posted on LinkedIn and Indeed.  招聘广告在LinkedIn和Indeed上交叉发布。 | Synonym:  syndicated, reposted, duplicated | Antonym: original, unique | word property: adjective
