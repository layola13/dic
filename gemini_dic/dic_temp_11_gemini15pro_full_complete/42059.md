cote:/kəʊt/ (British) /koʊt/ (American) | n. |小屋，棚子；羊圈；鸽棚；海岸，山坡| A small shelter for animals, or a small, simple building used for a particular purpose; also a slope or side of a hill. |例句：The shepherd built a cote for his sheep. 牧羊人为他的羊建了一个羊圈。

例句：The vines grew on the sunny cote of the hill.  葡萄藤生长在阳光明媚的山坡上。|近义词：shed, hut, pen, slope, hillside |反义词:valley, plain |词性:noun


cote:/koʊt/ (American) /kɔːt/ (British) | n. |报价，估价；行情| A quotation; a price or value assigned to something. |例句：The cote for the repairs was higher than I expected. 修理的报价比我预期的要高。

例句：What's the cote on gold today?  今天黄金的行情是多少？ |近义词：quotation, quote, price, valuation |反义词: N/A |词性:noun


词源:  From Old French *cote ("hut, shed"), from Late Latin *cota ("hut"), of uncertain origin.  The financial sense comes from noting down prices on a tablet or board.
