disembrangle:/dɪsɪnˈtæŋɡl/ | v. | 理清，解开，使摆脱 | To separate things that have become tangled or mixed up. |  The firefighters worked to disembrangle the child from the wreckage. 消防队员努力将孩子从残骸中解救出来。

She carefully disembrangled the yarn. 她小心翼翼地解开了毛线。 | 近义词: untangle, unravel, extricate, separate | 反义词: entangle, tangle, complicate | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->

From dis- (expressing reversal) + entangle.  The prefix "dis-" indicates reversal or negation, and "entangle" means to twist or twine together.  Thus, "disembrangle" literally means to undo the entanglement.
