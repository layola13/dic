intaglio:/ɪnˈtæliːoʊ/ /ɪnˈtæljoʊ/
noun
雕刻,凹雕,阴雕,凹版印刷
A design incised or engraved into a surface.
The artist specialized in intaglio, creating intricate designs on gemstones.  这位艺术家擅长雕刻，在宝石上创作复杂的图案。
engraving, carving, etching
relief, cameo
n.

intaglio:/ɪnˈtæliːoʊ/ /ɪnˈtæljoʊ/
adjective
雕刻的,凹雕的,阴雕的
Cut or engraved into a surface.
The intaglio printing process creates a raised image on the paper.  凹版印刷工艺在纸上形成凸起的图像。
engraved, incised, carved
raised, embossed
adj.
