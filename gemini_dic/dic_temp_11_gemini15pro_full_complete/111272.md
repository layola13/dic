litre:/ˈliːtər/ (US) /ˈliːtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|升|A metric unit of capacity, formerly defined as the volume of one kilogram of water under standard conditions, now equal to one cubic decimeter (about 1.76 pints).|例句：The car has a 1.6 litre engine. 这辆车有一个1.6升的发动机。

|例句：I bought a two-litre bottle of soda. 我买了一瓶两升的汽水。|近义词：cubic decimeter|反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement) |词性:noun
