navel-gazing:/ˈneɪvəl ˌɡeɪzɪŋ/
noun
沉思；内省；自省
Excessive contemplation of one's own feelings and experiences, often to the exclusion of the outside world.  The act of spending too much time thinking about yourself and your own problems.
例句：He dismissed their complaints as mere navel-gazing.  他认为他们的抱怨只是无病呻吟。

例句：The article criticizes the navel-gazing of some modern artists. 这篇文章批评了一些现代艺术家的故弄玄虚。
近义词：introspection, self-absorption, soul-searching
反义词：extroversion, outward focus
词性:n.
