chain-reacts:/ˌtʃeɪn riˈækts/ | v. |连锁反应|To cause a series of reactions, each of which initiates the next. | The explosion chain-reacted, causing widespread damage. 爆炸引发了连锁反应，造成了大范围的破坏。|  The initial bank failure chain-reacted, leading to a financial crisis. 最初的银行倒闭引发了连锁反应，导致了一场金融危机。 | synonyms: cascades, snowballs, mushrooms, escalates | antonyms: stabilizes, halts, ceases | verb

chain-reaction:/ˌtʃeɪn riˈækʃən/ | n. |连锁反应|A series of reactions, each of which initiates the next. | The government's decision triggered a chain reaction of protests. 政府的决定引发了一系列的抗议活动。| The scientist explained the chain reaction that occurs in nuclear fission. 科学家解释了核裂变中发生的连锁反应。| synonyms: domino effect, cascade, snowball effect | antonyms: isolated incident, single event | noun
