commerciality:/kəˌmɜːrʃiˈælɪti/
noun
商业性，商业化
the quality of being suitable for commerce; the emphasis on profit-making or success in selling, sometimes at the expense of quality
例句：The increasing commerciality of Christmas has become a concern for many.  圣诞节越来越商业化，这已成为许多人的担忧。

例句：The film's commerciality ensured its widespread release. 这部电影的商业性确保了它的广泛发行。

近义词：marketability, merchantability
反义词：unprofitability, noncommercialism
词性: n.
