matriculate: /məˈtrɪkjʊleɪt/ | n. |  入学学生 | a student who has matriculated | The new matriculates were given a tour of the campus. 新入学的学生得到了校园参观。| synonyms: freshman, undergraduate, enrollee | antonyms: graduate, alumnus | noun
