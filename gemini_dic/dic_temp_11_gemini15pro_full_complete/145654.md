overcontractions:/ˌoʊvər.kənˈtrækʃənz/| n.|过度收缩|Excessive muscular contractions, often resulting in stiffness or pain.  It can refer to the shortening of muscles beyond their normal range, sometimes due to neurological conditions or overuse.|The athlete experienced overcontractions in his leg muscles after the marathon.|近义词:spasms, cramps, contractures|反义词:relaxation, extension|noun
