derring-doer:/ˈdɛrɪŋˈduːər/
noun
大胆的人；敢作敢为的人
A person who performs daring deeds; a brave adventurer.
例句：The derring-doer scaled the cliff face without ropes.  这个大胆的人不用绳子就爬上了悬崖峭壁。

例句：She was known throughout the land as a derring-doer of great renown. 她以是一位著名的敢作敢为的人而闻名全国。

近义词：daredevil, adventurer, swashbuckler
反义词：coward, timid person
词性：n.


Etymology:
From derring-do (daring action) + -er (agent suffix).  Derring-do itself comes from Middle English *daring do*, meaning "daring to do".
