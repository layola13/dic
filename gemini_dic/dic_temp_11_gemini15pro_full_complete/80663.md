gordian:/ˈɡɔːrdiən/ | adj. | 戈尔迪之结的；复杂的；难解的 | Relating to the Gordian knot, a knot tied by Gordius, king of Phrygia, which was said to be impossible to untie.  It came to symbolize an intractable problem, solved by bold or unconventional action. |  The gordian knot of the Middle East conflict seems impossible to unravel. 中东冲突这个戈尔迪之结似乎无法解开。|  intricate, complex, involved | simple, straightforward | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *Gordianus, from Latin Gordius, the name of the Phrygian king who tied the Gordian knot.
