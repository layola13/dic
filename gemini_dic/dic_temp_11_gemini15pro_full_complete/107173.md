laggardly:/ˈlæɡərdli/| adj.|缓慢的，迟缓的，落后的|Slow and reluctant; lagging.|例句：The laggardly pace of the project frustrated everyone.  这个项目缓慢的进度让每个人都很沮丧。

例句：He walked with a laggardly gait. 他走路步履缓慢。|近义词：slow, sluggish, tardy, dilatory|反义词：fast, swift, prompt, quick|词性:形容词

词源: From "laggard" + "-ly". "Laggard" comes from the Middle English "laggen," meaning "to stay behind."
