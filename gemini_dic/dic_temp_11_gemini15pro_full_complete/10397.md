appendixes:/əˈpɛndɪksiːz/
n.
附录，附件；阑尾
supplementary material at the end of a book, document, etc.; a small tube-shaped part attached to the large intestine
例句：The appendixes to the report contained valuable statistical data. 报告的附录包含了有价值的统计数据。

例句：She had her appendixes removed last year. 她去年切除了阑尾。
近义词：addendum, supplement, attachment, annex
反义词:preface, introduction
词性:名词
