multiplexer:/ˈmʌltɪˌplɛksər/ | n. | 多路复用器，多路器 | A device that selects one of several analog or digital input signals and forwards the selected input into a single line. |  A multiplexer is used to combine multiple signals onto a single transmission line.  多路复用器用于将多个信号组合到一条传输线上。 |  The network uses multiplexers to increase bandwidth efficiency. 该网络使用多路复用器来提高带宽效率。 | synonyms: mux, selector | antonyms: demultiplexer, demux | noun

multiplexers:/ˈmʌltɪˌplɛksərz/ | n. | 多路复用器，多路器（复数） | Plural form of multiplexer. Devices that select one of several analog or digital input signals and forwards the selected input into a single line.  | Multiplexers are crucial for telecommunications. 多路复用器对于电信至关重要。 |  These multiplexers handle a large volume of data. 这些多路复用器处理大量数据。 | synonyms: muxes, selectors | antonyms: demultiplexers, demuxes | noun
