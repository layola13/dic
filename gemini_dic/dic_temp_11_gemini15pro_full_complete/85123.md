hardheaded:/ˈhɑːrdˈhɛdɪd/| adj.|顽固的，固执的，倔强的|Stubbornly unyielding; unreasonably inflexible.  Practical and realistic; shrewd.|He's too hardheaded to admit he's wrong. 他太固执了，不肯承认自己错了。|Synonyms: stubborn, obstinate, unyielding, inflexible, headstrong, willful, pigheaded|Antonyms: compliant, amenable, docile, flexible, open-minded|adjective

hard-headed:/ˌhɑːrdˈhɛdɪd/| adj.|精明的，务实的|Having or showing shrewdness and good judgment, especially in money or business matters.|She's a hard-headed businesswoman who knows how to make a profit. 她是一位精明的女商人，知道如何盈利。|Synonyms: shrewd, astute, sharp, practical, realistic, pragmatic|Antonyms: naive, idealistic, impractical|adjective
