dangerous:/ˈdeɪndʒərəs/ | adj. | 危险的 | Involving or causing danger or risk; liable to hurt or harm. | That old bridge is dangerous to cross. 那座旧桥过起来很危险。 | The road is dangerous at night.  这条路夜间很危险。 | 近义词: perilous, hazardous, risky, unsafe | 反义词: safe, secure, harmless | 词性: 形容词 | 词源: From Old French *dangeros, from danger + -ous.
