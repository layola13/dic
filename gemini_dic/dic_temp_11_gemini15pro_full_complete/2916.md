advisements:/ədˈvaɪzmənts/
noun
建议；劝告；通知
recommendations or suggestions offered as guidance; notifications or announcements
The university sent out advisements about course registration. 大学发出了关于课程注册的通知。
synonyms: counsel, guidance, recommendations, notices, announcements
antonyms: dissuasion, discouragement
