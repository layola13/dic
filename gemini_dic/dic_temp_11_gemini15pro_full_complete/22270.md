bonell:/boʊˈnɛl/ | adj. |博内尔弹簧（床垫的一种）| A type of coil spring system used in mattresses, characterized by hourglass-shaped coils connected by helical wires. |  This mattress uses a bonell spring system for support. 这款床垫使用博内尔弹簧系统支撑。| synonym: coil spring, innerspring | antonym: pocket spring, memory foam | noun (attributive)

bonell:/boʊˈnɛl/ | n. |博内尔弹簧| A bonell spring unit within the mattress. | The bonell provides a firm feel. 这个博内尔弹簧提供了坚实的感觉。| synonym: coil spring unit, innerspring unit | antonym: pocket spring unit, memory foam core | noun
