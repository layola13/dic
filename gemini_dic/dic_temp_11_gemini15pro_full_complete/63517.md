enunciability:/ɪˌnʌnsiəˈbɪləti/
n.
可表达性，可陈述性
The enunciability of a concept refers to its capacity to be clearly and distinctly expressed in language.
The enunciability of complex scientific theories can be challenging for the general public.  复杂科学理论的可表达性对于普通大众来说可能具有挑战性。
expressibility, articulability, pronounceability
inenunciability, inexpressibility, inarticulacy
noun
