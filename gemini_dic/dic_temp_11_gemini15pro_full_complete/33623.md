cinder:/ˈsɪndər/| n.|煤渣，余烬，灰烬|A piece of partly burned coal, wood, or other material.  A small piece of ash or ember.|The blacksmith swept the cinders from his forge. 铁匠从他的熔炉里扫出煤渣。|Synonyms: ash, ember, residue, clinker|Antonyms: fuel, kindling|Noun
