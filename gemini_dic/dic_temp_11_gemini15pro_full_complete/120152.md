mincing:/ˈmɪnsɪŋ/| adj.| 装腔作势的；矫揉造作的；切碎的|affectedly dainty or delicate in manner or gait; cut into very small pieces|She spoke in a mincing voice. 她说话的声音矫揉造作。|近义词：affected, pretentious, dainty, delicate, chopped, ground|反义词：natural, unaffected, forthright, coarse|词性:形容词
mince:/mɪns/| v. n.| 切碎；碎步走；装腔作势地说|to cut into very small pieces; walk with short quick steps in an affected manner; speak affectedly or indirectly|Please mince the garlic. 请把大蒜切碎。|近义词：chop, grind, dice, strut, simper|反义词：stride, stomp|词性:动词/名词
