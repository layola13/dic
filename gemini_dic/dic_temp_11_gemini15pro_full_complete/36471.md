coldnesses:/ˈkoʊldnəsɪz/ /ˈkəʊldnəsɪz/
n.
寒冷；冷淡；冷漠
the state of being cold in temperature; lack of affection or warmth; unfriendliness
例句：The coldness of his stare made her shiver. 他冰冷的目光让她不寒而栗。

例句：There was a noticeable coldness in her voice. 她的声音里明显带着冷淡。

近义词：chilliness, frigidity, iciness, indifference, aloofness
反义词：warmth, friendliness, cordiality, affection
