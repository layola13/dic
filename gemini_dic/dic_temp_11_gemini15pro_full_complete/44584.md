croupier:/kruːpiˈeɪ/ /ˈkruːpiər/| n. |荷官；赌场发牌员|A croupier is someone who works in a casino, dealing cards or spinning a roulette wheel.|The croupier dealt the cards with a practiced hand. 荷官熟练地发了牌。|The croupier spun the roulette wheel and called out the winning number. 荷官旋转轮盘并喊出中奖号码。|近义词：dealer|反义词:player, gambler|词性:noun
