moca:/ˈmoʊkə/ (US), /ˈməʊkə/ (UK)| n.|摩卡咖啡，摩卡测试|A type of coffee drink made with espresso, chocolate syrup, steamed milk, and often topped with whipped cream. Also, a cognitive screening test.|例句：I ordered a moca latte with extra whipped cream. 我点了一杯摩卡拿铁，加了很多奶油。

例句：The doctor administered a MoCA test to assess her cognitive function. 医生对她进行了摩卡测试以评估她的认知功能。|近义词：mocha (alternative spelling), caffè mocha|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
