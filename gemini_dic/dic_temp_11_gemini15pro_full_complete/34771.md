climacteric: /klaɪˈmæk.tər.ɪk/ /klaɪˈmæk.tɚ.ɪk/ | n. adj. | 衰退期，更年期；转折点 |A period of significant change or decline, especially in a person's life or in the development of something.  Relating to such a period. |  She experienced some mood swings during her climacteric.  她在更年期经历了一些情绪波动。| synonyms: turning point, critical period, milestone, menopause (specifically for women) | antonyms:  stability, plateau, normalcy | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek klimaktēr (κλιμακτήρ)  "rung of a ladder," from klimakterikos (κλιμακτηρικός) "critical,"  from klīmax (κλῖμαξ) "ladder, staircase."  The original sense referred to critical points in life, as if climbing a ladder.
