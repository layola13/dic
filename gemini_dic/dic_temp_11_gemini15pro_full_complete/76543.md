勇敢的行为；殷勤的举动；骑士风度（gallantness的复数）

He was known for his gallantnesses toward women, always opening doors and offering compliments. 他以对女性殷勤的举动而闻名，总是开门并给予赞美。

Antonyms: cowardices, rudenesses, discourtesies
