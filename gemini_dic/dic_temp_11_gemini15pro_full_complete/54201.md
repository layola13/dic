disfashion:/dɪsˈfæʃən/
verb
使...过时，使...不流行
To cause something to become unfashionable.
例句：
The rise of casual wear has disfashioned formal suits for everyday use.
休闲服的兴起使得正式套装在日常生活中不再流行。

例句：
New technology quickly disfashions older gadgets.
新技术很快使旧的小玩意过时。

近义词：outmode, antiquate
反义词：fashion

词性:v.
