flaky:/ˈfleɪki/| adj.|薄片的，易剥落的；古怪的，靠不住的| characterized by breaking or separating easily into small thin pieces; unreliable, eccentric|The pastry was light and flaky. 这糕点又轻又酥。 He's a bit flaky, so I wouldn't rely on him too much.  他有点不靠谱，所以我不会太依赖他。|近义词：crumbly, unreliable, eccentric|反义词：stable, reliable, consistent|词性:形容词

flaky:/ˈfleɪki/| n.|怪人，不靠谱的人 (Informal)|An unreliable or eccentric person.|He's such a flaky, he always cancels plans at the last minute. 他真是个怪人，总是最后一刻取消计划。|近义词：eccentric, oddball|反义词：conformist, predictable|词性:名词
