diarrhea:/ˌdaɪəˈriːə/ (US), /ˌdaɪəˈrɪə/ (UK)| n. |腹泻|Frequent, loose, watery bowel movements.  The condition of having at least three loose or liquid bowel movements each day. It can be caused by a variety of things, including infections, food intolerances, and certain medications.|例句：He had a bad case of diarrhea after eating contaminated food. 他吃了受污染的食物后腹泻严重。

例句：The doctor diagnosed her with diarrhea and prescribed medication. 医生诊断她腹泻并开了药。|近义词：the runs, loose bowels, the trots|反义词: constipation|词性: noun
