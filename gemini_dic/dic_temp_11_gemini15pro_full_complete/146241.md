overexpressively:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspresivli/
| adv. |过度表达地，夸张地|Expressing something in an exaggerated or excessive manner.  Showing feelings too strongly.|She reacted overexpressively to the news, bursting into tears and then uncontrollable laughter.|近义词：excessively, dramatically, theatrically, flamboyantly|反义词：subtly, reservedly, understatedly|词性:副词
Etymology: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "expressive" (adjective meaning "serving to express or convey") + "-ly" (adverb suffix)
