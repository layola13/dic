mahayanistic:/ˌmɑːhɑːjəˈnɪstɪk/
adjective
大乘佛教的
Of or relating to Mahayana Buddhism, one of the two main existing branches of Buddhism and a religion developed in India. It is typified by idealization of the bodhisattva.
例句：Mahayana Buddhist philosophy emphasizes compassion and the bodhisattva ideal. 大乘佛教哲学强调慈悲和菩萨理想。

例句：The temple's architecture reflects Mahayana Buddhist influences. 这座寺庙的建筑反映了大乘佛教的影响。

近义词：Buddhist (更广泛), Mahayana (名词形式)
反义词：Hinayanistic, Theravada (指上座部佛教)
词性：adjective
