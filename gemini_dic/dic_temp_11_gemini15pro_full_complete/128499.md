newsreel: /ˈnjuːzriːl/ /ˈnuːzriːl/ | n. | 新闻影片，纪录片 | A short film of news events, formerly shown in cinemas/theaters. |  例句：We watched old newsreels of World War II. 我们观看了二战时期的老新闻片。

例句：Newsreels were a popular way to stay informed before television. 在电视出现之前，新闻影片是人们了解时事的一种流行方式。 | 近义词：documentary, newsfilm, footage | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From news + reel (n.) "a revolving cylinder on which film, wire, etc., is wound," from Old English *hreol or Old Norse hrull "roll, wheel"
