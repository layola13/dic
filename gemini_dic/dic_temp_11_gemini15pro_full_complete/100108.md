interpretability:/ɪnˌtɜːrprɪtəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可解释性，可理解性 | The quality of being easy to understand or explain.  The ability of a machine learning model to have its predictions understood by humans. | The interpretability of the model's results was crucial for gaining the stakeholders' trust.  模型结果的可解释性对于获得利益相关者的信任至关重要。 | synonyms: understandability, explainability, clarity, perspicuity | antonyms: opacity, incomprehensibility, obscurity | noun
