aguardiente:/ˌɑːɡwɑːrˈdjɛnteɪ/ (US), /ˌæɡwɑːrˈdjɛnteɪ/ (UK)| n. |烈酒；烧酒|An alcoholic beverage, typically a strong, clear brandy made from fermented sugarcane juice or other fruits, popular in Latin America and some parts of Europe.|I tried aguardiente for the first time in Colombia. 我在哥伦比亚第一次尝试了烧酒。|Synonyms: brandy, liquor, spirits|Antonyms: soft drink, juice|noun
