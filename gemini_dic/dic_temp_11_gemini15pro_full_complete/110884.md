lionlike: /ˈlaɪənˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 像狮子的；勇敢的；高贵的 | Resembling a lion in appearance, character, or behavior.  Having qualities typically associated with lions, such as courage, majesty, or fierceness. | The warrior had a lionlike mane of hair and a fearless demeanor.  这位战士有着像狮子一样的浓密鬃毛和无所畏惧的风度。|  The CEO delivered a lionlike roar of disapproval at the proposed merger. 首席执行官对拟议的合并发出了像狮子一样的怒吼，表示反对。 |  Near Synonyms: leonine, courageous, majestic, regal, fierce, bold | Near Antonyms: timid, cowardly, meek, humble, submissive | Adjective
