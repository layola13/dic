offing:/ˈɔːfɪŋ/
n.
远处，海面，即将发生
the more distant part of the sea that can be seen from the shore; something that is likely to happen soon
There are several new products in the offing.  几款新产品即将推出。
近义词:forthcoming, imminent, impending
反义词:distant, remote
词性:名词
