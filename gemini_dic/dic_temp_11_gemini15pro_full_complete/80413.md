gomer:/ˈɡoʊmər/ (US), /ˈɡɒmə(r)/ (UK)| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜；容易上当的人|A foolish or stupid person; someone easily duped.|"He's such a gomer, he fell for that old trick." 他真是个笨蛋，竟然相信了那个老把戏。|Synonyms: fool, idiot, dolt, simpleton, chump, sucker|Antonyms: genius, sage, mastermind|noun


gomer:/ˈɡoʊmər/ (US), /ˈɡɒmə(r)/ (UK)| n.|（美俚，尤指越战时期）步兵| (US slang, especially during the Vietnam War) an infantryman. |"The gomers were dug in, waiting for the attack." 步兵们挖好了战壕，等待着进攻。|Synonyms: grunt, infantryman, foot soldier|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps officer)|noun
