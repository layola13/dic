octyl:/ˈɒktɪl/ (UK), /ˈɑːktɪl/ (US)| adj. n.|辛基，八（个碳原子）的|Relating to or containing eight carbon atoms in a molecule, especially in the alkyl radical.  A univalent radical, C8H17, of the paraffin series.|Octyl acetate has a fruity odor.  乙酸辛酯有一种水果味。|Octyl alcohol is used in the manufacture of plasticizers. 辛醇用于制造增塑剂。|近义词:caprylic, octose|反义词:None. The term doesn't have a direct opposite.|词性:adjective, noun
