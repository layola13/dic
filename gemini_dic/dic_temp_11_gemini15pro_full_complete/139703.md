oceanographically: /ˌoʊʃənəˈɡræfɪkli/ | adv. | 海洋学地 | In a way that relates to oceanography, the study of the physical and biological aspects of the ocean. |  The data were analyzed oceanographically to understand the currents.  这些数据用海洋学的方法进行了分析，以了解洋流。| 近义词: marine, aquatic | 反义词: terrestrial | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
ocean + ography + ic + ally
ocean (n.): from Latin *ōceanus*, from Greek *ōkeanos*
-graphy (suffix):  from Greek *-graphia*, meaning "writing" or "description"
-ic (suffix): forms adjectives
-ally (suffix): forms adverbs
