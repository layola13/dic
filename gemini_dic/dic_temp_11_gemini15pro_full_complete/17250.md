bayatte:/baɪˈæt/| n.|巴亚特调式|A mode in Persian traditional music, similar to the Phrygian mode in Western music.  It evokes a sense of melancholy and introspection.|The musician played a beautiful piece in the bayatte mode.  音乐家演奏了一首优美的巴亚特调式曲子。|Near synonyms:  Phrygian mode (in Western music), Rast (another Persian mode)|Antonyms: None (Modes don't have direct opposites)|Noun
