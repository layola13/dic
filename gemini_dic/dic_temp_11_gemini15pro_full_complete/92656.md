iboga:/ɪˈboʊɡə/| n.|伊博加，伊波加|A shrub native to Central West Africa, *Tabernanthe iboga*, whose root bark contains the indole alkaloid ibogaine, used in some cultures for its purported medicinal and psychoactive properties.|The Bwiti religion uses iboga in their initiation ceremonies.  布威提宗教在其入会仪式中使用伊博加。|Synonyms:  iboga plant|Antonyms: None|Noun

ibogaine:/ɪˈboʊɡeɪn/| n.|伊博加因|An indole alkaloid derived from the iboga plant, used experimentally to treat drug addiction.|Ibogaine treatment is controversial due to potential side effects. 伊博加因治疗因潜在的副作用而存在争议。|Synonyms:  12-methoxyibogamine|Antonyms: None|Noun
