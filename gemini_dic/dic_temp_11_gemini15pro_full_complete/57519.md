dragoon:/drəˈɡuːn/| n. v. |龙骑兵；强迫；迫使|A soldier originally mounted on horseback but later also trained to fight on foot. To compel by coercion or threats. |例句：The dragoons were ordered to suppress the rebellion. 龙骑兵被命令镇压叛乱。

例句：He was dragooned into joining the committee. 他被迫加入了委员会。|近义词：coerce, compel, force, press, oblige|反义词：persuade, coax|词性:名词,动词
