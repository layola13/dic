devenustate: /diːˈvɛnəˌsteɪt/
verb
使失去魅力；使不雅；玷污
to deprive of beauty or grace; to mar or disfigure
The graffiti devenustated the ancient monument.  涂鸦玷污了这座古老的纪念碑。
近义词: mar, disfigure, spoil, blemish, deface
反义词: beautify, embellish, adorn, enhance
词性: verb

词源: From Latin *dēvēnustātus, past participle of *dēvēnustāre (“to make ungraceful”), from dē- (“from, away”) + vēnustās (“charm, grace, loveliness, beauty”), from Venus (“goddess of love and beauty”).
