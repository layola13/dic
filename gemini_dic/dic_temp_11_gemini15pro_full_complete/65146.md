ethmoid: /ˈɛθmɔɪd/ | adj. n. | 筛骨的；筛状的；筛骨 |  A bone forming part of the nose and separating the nasal cavity from the brain. It is lightweight and spongy. | The ethmoid bone is located at the roof of the nose, between the two orbits. 筛骨位于鼻腔的顶部，在两个眼眶之间。| The ethmoid sinuses are often involved in sinus infections. 筛窦经常会发生鼻窦感染。|  Synonyms:  (for the adjective) sieve-like, cribriform | Antonyms:  (no direct antonym for the anatomical term) | noun, adjective
