outworked:/ˌaʊtˈwɜːrkt/
verb
工作超过，比…更努力工作
To have worked harder than someone else.
例句：She outworked everyone on the team and deserved the promotion. 她比团队里的每个人都更努力工作，理应得到晋升。

例句：He felt outworked and underpaid. 他觉得自己工作过度，报酬过低。
synonym: outlabor, outstrip, surpass
antonym: underwork
词性: 动词
