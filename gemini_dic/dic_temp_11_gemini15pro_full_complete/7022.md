androsphinx:/ˌændroʊˈsfɪŋks/| n. | 男身人面狮身像 |A sphinx with the head of a man and the body of a lion.  It is a less common form compared to the traditional female sphinx. | The museum displayed a rare statue of an androsphinx. 博物馆展出了一尊罕见的男身人面狮身像。| The androsphinx guarded the entrance to the ancient city. 这座男身人面狮身像守护着古城的入口。| synonyms:  man-sphinx, male sphinx | antonyms:  gynecosphinx (female sphinx) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek *ἀνδρόσφιγξ (andrósphinx), from ἀνήρ (anḗr, “man”) + σφίγξ (sphígx, “sphinx”).
