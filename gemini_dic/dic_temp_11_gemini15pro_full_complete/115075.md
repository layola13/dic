maneuverability:/məˌnuːvərəˈbɪləti/ /məˌnuːv(ə)rəˈbɪlədi/| n.|机动性，操控性|The car's maneuverability is excellent in tight spaces. 这辆车在狭窄空间里的操控性非常好。|例句：The jet's maneuverability gave it an advantage in dogfights. 这架喷气式飞机的机动性使其在空战中占了优势。|近义词：agility, flexibility, mobility|反义词:clumsiness, awkwardness|词性:名词

maneuverability:/məˌnuːvərəˈbɪləti/ /məˌnuːv(ə)rəˈbɪlədi/| n.|可操作性|The software's maneuverability allows for complex tasks to be performed easily.  这款软件的可操作性使得复杂的任务可以轻松完成。 |例句：The ship's maneuverability was hampered by the strong currents.  强劲的水流阻碍了船的可操作性。|近义词：controllability, handiness|反义词:unwieldiness, rigidity|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From maneuver + -ability.  Maneuver comes from the French word "manœuvrer," ultimately from Latin "manu operari" meaning "to work by hand."
