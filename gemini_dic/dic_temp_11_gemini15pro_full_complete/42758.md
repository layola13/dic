country-and-western: /ˈkʌntri ən ˈwɛstən/ (US) /ˈkʌntri ən ˈwestən/ (UK)
| adj. n.|乡村音乐；西部乡村音乐|A genre of popular music originating in the rural southern US. It is characterized by simple harmonies and melodies, often accompanied by instruments such as the guitar, banjo, and fiddle, and its lyrics often deal with themes of love, loss, and everyday life.|I grew up listening to country-and-western music. 我听着乡村音乐长大。|Synonyms: country music, country,  western|Antonyms: pop, rock, classical|adjective, noun
