homothally:/ˌhɒməˈθælɪ/
adjective
同株配合的；同体受精的
(of some fungi and algae) Having male and female reproductive structures on the same thallus, and capable of self-fertilization.
Homothallic fungi can reproduce sexually by themselves.  同体受精的真菌可以自行进行有性生殖。
Some species of Chlamydomonas are homothallic. 一些衣藻物种是同体受精的。
近义词:self-fertile, monoecious
反义词:heterothallic
词性:adjective
