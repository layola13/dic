inhive: /ɪnˈhaɪv/
verb
使...入蜂巢; 引入; 嵌入
to cause to enter and remain within a hive; to bring in; to embed.

例句：
The beekeeper inhived the swarm. = 养蜂人把蜂群引入蜂巢。

The company inhived the new software into its existing system. =  公司将新软件嵌入到现有系统中。


近义词：
integrate, incorporate, embed, introduce

反义词：
extract, remove

词性
v.
