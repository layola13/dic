imperfections:/ˌɪmpərˈfekʃənz/
n.|缺陷，瑕疵，缺点|flaws, shortcomings, defects; things that are not perfect|The imperfections in the wood added to its rustic charm. 木材的瑕疵增添了它的乡村魅力。|synonyms: flaws, defects, blemishes, shortcomings|antonyms: perfections, strengths, virtues|noun

imperfection:/ˌɪmpərˈfekʃən/
n.|缺陷，瑕疵，缺点|a flaw, shortcoming, or defect; something that is not perfect|This diamond has a slight imperfection. 这颗钻石有一点小瑕疵。|synonyms: flaw, defect, blemish, shortcoming|antonyms: perfection, strength, virtue|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *imperfeccioun*, borrowed from Late Latin *imperfectiō*, *imperfectiōnem*, from Latin *imperfectus* ("incomplete, unfinished"), from *in-* ("not") + *perfectus* ("complete, finished").
