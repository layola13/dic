macrura:/məˈkrʊərə/| n. |长尾类，长尾亚目|A suborder of decapod crustaceans, including shrimps, prawns, lobsters, and crayfish, characterized by a long, well-developed abdomen.|Macrura are important commercially as a food source. 长尾类是重要的商业食物来源。|近义词:Decapoda, Natantia|反义词:Brachyura|词性:noun
