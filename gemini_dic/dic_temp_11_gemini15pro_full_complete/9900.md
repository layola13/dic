apatosaurus:/əˌpætəˈsɔːrəs/| n. |迷惑龙，雷龙|A very large herbivorous dinosaur of the Late Jurassic period, with a long neck and tail.|例句：The Apatosaurus was one of the largest land animals that ever lived. 迷龙是有史以来最大的陆地动物之一。

例句：Scientists have learned a lot about Apatosaurus from its fossilized bones. 科学家们从迷惑龙的骨骼化石中学到了很多东西。|近义词：Brontosaurus, dinosaur|反义词: None (It's a specific type of dinosaur, so it doesn't have a direct opposite.)|词性: noun
