non-fermentable:/ˌnɒn fɜːˈmɛntəbl/ | adj. | 不可发酵的；非发酵性的 | Not capable of undergoing fermentation. |  This bacterium is non-fermentable, meaning it cannot break down sugars into alcohol or acid.  这种细菌是不可发酵的，意味着它不能将糖分解成酒精或酸。| Example:  Non-fermentable substrates are often used in wastewater treatment. 不可发酵的基质常用于废水处理。| Synonyms: unfermentable | Antonyms: fermentable | Word type: Adjective

non-fermenter:/ˌnɒn fɜːˈmɛntər/ | n. | 非发酵菌；不可发酵物 | A substance or organism that cannot undergo fermentation. |  Pseudomonas aeruginosa is a common non-fermenter found in hospitals. 铜绿假单胞菌是一种常见的在医院发现的非发酵菌。| Example: The lab identified the bacteria as a non-fermenter. 实验室将该细菌鉴定为非发酵菌。| Synonyms: non-fermentable organism, unfermentable substance | Antonyms: fermenter, fermentable substance | Word type: Noun
