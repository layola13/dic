beer-bellied:/ˈbɪərˌbɛlid/| adj.|啤酒肚的，大腹便便的|Having a large, protruding belly, typically associated with excessive beer consumption.|He's a beer-bellied man in his fifties. 他是一个五十多岁的大腹便便的男人。|Synonyms: pot-bellied, paunchy, obese, corpulent|Antonyms: slim, thin, lean|adjective

Etymology:
Compound of "beer" + "bellied."  "Beer" comes from Old English "bēor," from Proto-Germanic "*beuzą." "Bellied" comes from Old English "-bellied," a suffix meaning "having a belly of a specified kind," from "bely" (belly).
