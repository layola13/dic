nonpermitted:/ˌnɒnpərˈmɪtɪd/ | adj. | 未经许可的，禁止的 | Not allowed; forbidden. | Entry to this area is nonpermitted without authorization. 未经授权，禁止进入该区域。| The company has a strict policy against nonpermitted software installations. 公司对未经许可的软件安装有严格的政策。 | 近义词：unauthorized, prohibited, forbidden, disallowed | 反义词：permitted, allowed, authorized | 词性: adjective

词源: "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "permitted" (past participle of the verb "permit," from Latin "permittere," meaning "to let through, allow").
