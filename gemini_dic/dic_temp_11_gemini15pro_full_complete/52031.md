dialectically:/ˌdaɪəˈlɛktɪkli/| adv.|辩证地；辩证法地|In a way that relates to the logical discussion of ideas and opinions.|Marx analyzed capitalism dialectically. 马克思辩证地分析了资本主义。|Example: The issue must be considered dialectically.  这个问题必须辩证地考虑。|Synonym: argumentatively, logically, rationally|Antonym: dogmatically, irrationally|adverb

词源:
From Late Latin *dialecticus*, from Ancient Greek διαλεκτικός (dialektikós) meaning "of conversation, argumentative".
