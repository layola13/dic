danceress:/ˈdænsərɪs/| n. |女舞者，女舞蹈家|A female dancer.  This term is now somewhat archaic and can be considered sexist.|例句：The danceress twirled gracefully across the stage.  女舞者优雅地在舞台上旋转。

例句：The famous danceress captivated the audience with her performance.  这位著名的女舞者以她的表演吸引了观众。|近义词：dancer, danseuse|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider audience, spectator)|词性:noun

词源: From Old French *dancereis, from dancier (to dance) + -eis (feminine suffix).  Related to "dance."
