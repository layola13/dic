locomotion:/ˌloʊkəˈmoʊʃən/| n. |运动;移动;位移|The act or power of moving from place to place.|Amoebas achieve locomotion by changing shape. 变形虫通过改变形状来移动。|The high cost of fuel has put a premium on fuel efficiency in all forms of locomotion. 高昂的燃料成本使得各种交通工具的燃料效率都非常重要。|近义词：movement, travel, motion|反义词：stasis, immobility|词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *locomotiōn-, from Latin locus (“place”) + motiōn- (“motion”).
