localizing:/ˈloʊkəˌlaɪzɪŋ/| v. | 本地化；局限化；确定…的位置 | Adapting a product or content to a specific locale or market. | The company is localizing its software for the Chinese market.  该公司正在将其软件本地化为中文版本。|synonyms: adapting, tailoring, adjusting, modifying, converting, translating| antonyms: globalizing, internationalizing| verb

localizing:/ˈləʊkəˌlaɪzɪŋ/| v. |  本地化；局限化；确定…的位置| Restricting something to a particular place. | The infection was localized to a small area. 感染被局限在一个小区域。|synonyms: confining, restricting, limiting| antonyms: spreading, expanding, diffusing| verb
