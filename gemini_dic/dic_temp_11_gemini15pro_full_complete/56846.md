dorsal:/ˈdɔːrsl/ | adj. | 背部的，背侧的 | Relating to the back or upper surface of an animal, plant, or organ. | The dorsal fin of a shark is easily recognizable.  鲨鱼的背鳍很容易辨认。| synonyms: back, upper | antonyms: ventral, underside | adjective
