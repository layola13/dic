nonequestrian:/ˌnɒnɪˈkwestriən/
adjective
非马术的；非骑马的
Not relating to horse riding or equestrianism.
She led a nonequestrian life, preferring books to horses. 她过着与马术无关的生活，更喜欢读书而不是骑马。
Nonequestrian activities were also offered at the ranch. 牧场也提供非马术活动。
Near synonyms: non-riding
Opposite: equestrian
词性: adjective
