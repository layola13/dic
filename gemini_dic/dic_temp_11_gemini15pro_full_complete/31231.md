chanterelle:/ˌʃæntəˈrɛl/ | n. |鸡油菌；鸡油菇 |An edible mushroom, typically golden or yellow in color, with a distinctive fruity aroma. |例句：The chef sautéed the chanterelles in butter and garlic.  厨师用黄油和大蒜煎了鸡油菌。

例句：We found a patch of chanterelles growing beneath the oak trees. 我们在橡树下发现了一片鸡油菌。 |近义词：girolle (especially in British English), golden chanterelle |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
