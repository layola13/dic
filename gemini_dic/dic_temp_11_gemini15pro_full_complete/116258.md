massacred:/ˈmæsəkərd/| v. |屠杀，残杀；彻底击败|To kill a large number of people indiscriminately and cruelly. |The army massacred the innocent villagers. 军队屠杀了无辜的村民。|Synonyms: slaughter, butcher, slay|Antonyms: protect, save|verb
massacre:/ˈmæsəkər/| n. |大屠杀，惨败|An indiscriminate and cruel killing of a large number of people. |The massacre of the civilians shocked the world. 对平民的屠杀震惊了世界。|Synonyms: slaughter, carnage, butchery|Antonyms: rescue, salvation|noun
