nonrailroads:/ˌnɒnˈreɪlrəʊdz/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈreɪlrəʊdz/ (UK)
noun
非铁路行业/企业
Businesses or industries that are not involved in the railroad industry.
The government is encouraging investment in nonrailroads to diversify the transportation sector. 政府正在鼓励对非铁路行业的投资，以实现交通运输行业的多元化。
The decline of railroads led to the growth of nonrailroads like trucking and air freight. 铁路的衰落导致了卡车运输和航空货运等非铁路行业的增长。
Synonyms: non-railway industries, other transportation sectors
Antonyms: railroads, railway companies
词性: noun
