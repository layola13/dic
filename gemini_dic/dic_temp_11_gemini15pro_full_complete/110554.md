limestones:/ˈlaɪmˌstoʊnz/
n.
石灰岩，石灰石
A sedimentary rock consisting mainly of calcium carbonate.
Limestones are commonly used in construction. 石灰岩通常用于建筑。
rock, calcium carbonate, sedimentary rock
-
noun
The White Cliffs of Dover are made of chalk, a type of limestone.  多佛白崖是由白垩构成，白垩是一种石灰岩。
stone, marble
granite
