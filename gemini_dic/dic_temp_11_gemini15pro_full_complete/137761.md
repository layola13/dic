nonvarieties:/ˌnɑːnvəˈraɪətiz/ | n. | 非变体，非品种 | Linguistic forms or features that are not considered standard or accepted varieties of a language.  These might include regional dialects, sociolects, or idiolects that deviate significantly from the established norm. |  Example: The researchers documented various nonvarieties of the language spoken in remote villages. 研究人员记录了偏远村庄使用的各种非标准语言变体。

Example:  Some linguists argue that nonvarieties are just as valid as standardized forms of communication. 一些语言学家认为，非标准变体与标准化的交际形式一样有效。 | synonyms: dialects, vernaculars, nonstandard forms | antonyms: standard varieties, standard language, mainstream dialects | noun
