foreshadowers:/fɔːrˈʃædəwərz/
n.
预兆者，预示者
people or things that foreshadow something; harbingers
The dark clouds were foreshadowers of the coming storm.  乌云预示着暴风雨即将到来。
近义词:harbingers, precursors, omens, portents
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider words like "aftermath," "consequences")
词性: noun (plural)

foreshadow:/fɔːrˈʃædoʊ/
v.
预示，预兆
to be a warning or indication of (a future event).
The author foreshadowed the tragic ending in the first chapter. 作者在第一章就预示了悲剧性的结局。
近义词:presage, augur, portend, prefigure
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: verb
