encompass:/ɪnˈkʌmpəs/ /ɛnˈkʌmpəs/| v.|包含，包围，环绕| To include comprehensively; contain.  To surround or enclose.| The course encompasses all aspects of business management. 这门课程涵盖了商业管理的各个方面.|Synonyms: include, contain, embrace, cover, comprise|Antonyms: exclude, omit, reject|verb

encompassed:/ɪnˈkʌmpəst/ /ɛnˈkʌmpəst/ |v.|(encompass的过去式和过去分词)包含，包围，环绕|Past tense and past participle of encompass.| The report encompassed all the relevant information.  这份报告包含了所有相关信息.|Synonyms: included, contained, embraced, covered, comprised|Antonyms: excluded, omitted, rejected|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French encompasser, from en- (“in”) + compass (“circle, circuit”). Ultimately from Latin in- + compassus.
