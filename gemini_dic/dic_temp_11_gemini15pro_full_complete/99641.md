interlapse:/ˌɪntərˈlæps/
n.
间隔；间歇；中断
A period of time between two events.
Several months interlapsed between his visits. 他两次访问间隔了几个月。
近义词: interval, pause, break, gap
反义词: continuation, continuance
词性: 名词

词源: From Latin interlapsus, past participle of interlabi ("to slip or glide between").  From inter- ("between") + labi ("to slip, glide").
