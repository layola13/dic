jungar:/ˈdʒʊŋɡɑːr/
noun|准噶尔（人，地区，或事物）|A member of a nomadic Mongol people formerly inhabiting the Dzungarian Basin, now the Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture, Xinjiang, China.  Also refers to the region itself, or anything related to the Dzungars.|The Jungar were a powerful force in Central Asia in the 17th and 18th centuries.|Synonyms:  Dzungar, Zunghar|Antonyms: N/A|n.
