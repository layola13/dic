dethronization:/diːˌθrəʊnɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
废黜；罢免；推翻
The act of removing a monarch or other ruler from power.
The dethronization of the king led to a period of instability. 国王的废黜导致了一段时期的动荡。
deposition, ousting, removal, overthrow
enthronement, coronation, accession
noun
