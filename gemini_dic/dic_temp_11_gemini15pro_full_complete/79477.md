globalisation:/ˌɡləʊbəlaɪˈzeɪʃn/ /ˌɡloʊbələˈzeɪʃn/| n.|全球化|The process by which businesses or other organizations develop international influence or start operating on an international scale.|例句：Globalization has led to increased interconnectedness between countries. 全球化导致各国之间联系日益紧密。

例句：The company benefited from globalization by expanding its market reach.  这家公司通过扩大市场范围从全球化中受益。|近义词：internationalization, mundialization|反义词:localization, regionalization|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From globalize + -ation.  Globalize is derived from global, ultimately from Latin globus ("sphere, round mass").
