mobbers:/ˈmɒbəz (UK), ˈmɑːbərz (US)| n.|暴徒，聚众闹事的人，网络暴民|People who participate in a mob, especially those involved in online harassment or bullying.  A group of people who attack or harass someone, either physically or online.|The mobbers harassed the celebrity online with hateful comments.|例句：The mobbers surrounded the car, shouting angrily. 暴徒们围住汽车，愤怒地叫喊着。

例句：Online mobbers can cause significant emotional distress to their victims. 网络暴民会给受害者造成严重的情感困扰。|近义词：bullies, harassers, perpetrators, attackers|反义词: protectors, defenders, supporters|词性:名词
