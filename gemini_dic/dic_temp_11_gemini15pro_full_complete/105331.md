kick back:/kɪk ˈbæk/| v.|轻松休息，(使)后座力，(使)反冲|to relax and enjoy oneself; (of a gun) recoil; to react strongly against something|例句：I'm going to kick back and watch some TV. 我要放松一下，看看电视。|近义词：relax, unwind, chill out, recoil, react|反义词:work, toil|动词
