augmented:/ɔːɡˈmɛntɪd/ | adj. | 增强的，扩充的 | made greater in size or value or strength or effectiveness; increased |  Example: The augmented reality game overlays digital images onto the real world.  增强现实游戏将数字图像叠加到现实世界中。 | Example:  He took an augmented version of the software on a trial basis. 他试用了增强版的软件。 | Synonyms: increased, enhanced, expanded, amplified, supplemented | Antonyms: decreased, diminished, reduced, lessened | adjective

augmented:/ɔːɡˈmɛntɪd/ | v. |  增加，增强（augment的过去式和过去分词） |  (past tense of augment) Made greater in size or value or strength or effectiveness; increased | Example: The team was augmented by several new players.  该团队增加了几个新队员。| Example: The army augmented their forces with additional troops. 军队增加了额外的部队来增强他们的力量。 | Synonyms: increased, enhanced, expanded, amplified, supplemented | Antonyms: decreased, diminished, reduced, lessened | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *augmenten*, from Old French augmenter, from Latin augmentāre (“to increase, enlarge”), from augmentum (“increase, growth”), from augēre (“to increase, enlarge”).
