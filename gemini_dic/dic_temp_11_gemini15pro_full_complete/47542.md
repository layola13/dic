deathly:/ˈdeθli/| adj.|死一般的，致命的，极度的|Resembling or suggestive of death; suggestive of ghosts or evil spirits; extreme.|例句：She wore a deathly pallor. 她脸色苍白得像死人一样。|例句：He was in deathly fear of being discovered. 他极其害怕被发现。|近义词：mortal, lethal, fatal, ghastly, pallid|反义词：lively, vital, living|词性:形容词

deathly:/ˈdeθli/| adv.|死一般地，极度地|In a deathly manner; extremely.|例句：The room was deathly silent. 房间里死一般的寂静。|例句：She was deathly afraid of spiders. 她非常害怕蜘蛛。|近义词：extremely, exceedingly|反义词：slightly, moderately|词性:副词
