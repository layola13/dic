embodies:/ɪmˈbɑːdiz/ /ɛmˈbɒdiz/| v.| 体现，使具体化，包含|represents in a clear and obvious way; be a very good example of a quality or idea; include or contain something|  This painting embodies the spirit of the Renaissance. 这幅画体现了文艺复兴的精神。

The constitution embodies the will of the people. 宪法体现了人民的意志。

|近义词：represent, personify, epitomize, incorporate, include|反义词：exclude, omit|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English: from Old French *embodier, based on Latin *inbodiare, from in- ‘into’ + body.
