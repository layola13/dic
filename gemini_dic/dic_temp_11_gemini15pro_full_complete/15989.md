bambino:/bæmˈbiːnoʊ/ (US), /bæmˈbiːnəʊ/ (UK)| n. |小孩，幼儿|A young child, especially a boy.  It is an Italian loanword.|例句：The bambino was playing with his toys.  这个小孩正在玩他的玩具。|例句：She held the bambino close to her chest. 她把小孩紧紧地抱在胸前。|近义词：child, toddler, infant, baby|反义词:adult, grown-up|词性:noun

词源: From Italian *bambino* "baby boy," diminutive of *bambo* "simpleton, naive person," probably from Latin *balbus* "stammering."
