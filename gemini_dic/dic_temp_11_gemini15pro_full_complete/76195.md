fustet:/ˈfʌstɪt/ | n. | 黄木，黄栌木，黄栌树 | A tropical tree (Cotinus coggygria) of the sumac family, the wood of which yields a yellow dye.  Also refers to the dye itself. |  The artisan used fustet to create a vibrant yellow hue on the silk. 工匠用黄栌木在丝绸上染出鲜艳的黄色。|  The fustet dye was prized for its rich golden color. 黄栌木染料因其丰富的金色而备受珍视。 | Synonyms: young fustic, Venetian sumac | Antonyms: N/A | noun
