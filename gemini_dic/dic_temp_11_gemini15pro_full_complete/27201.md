callosity:/kəˈlɒsəti/ /kəˈlɑːsəti/| n. |老茧；硬结；无情|An area of hard, thickened skin.  A hardened or thickened part of the skin.  Insensitivity to emotion or suffering.|例句：Years of playing the guitar had left calluses on his fingertips. 多年弹吉他使他的指尖起了老茧。

例句：He developed a callosity on his heel from wearing ill-fitting shoes.  他因为穿不合脚的鞋子，脚后跟长了老茧。|近义词：callus, induration, hardness, thickening|反义词: softness, tenderness|词性:noun
