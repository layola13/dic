overannotating: /ˌoʊvərˈænəˌteɪtɪŋ/
verb
过度注释
Adding excessive annotations, often to the point of obscuring the original text or data.
The researcher was overannotating the manuscript, making it difficult to read. 研究人员过度注释了手稿，使其难以阅读。
He overannotated the image with so many labels that it became cluttered. 他在图像上添加了太多标签，使其变得杂乱。
近义词: overdocumenting, overexplaining
反义词: underannotating

词性: verb (动词)
词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "annotate" (verb meaning "to add notes to a text or diagram explaining or commenting on it") + "-ing" (present participle suffix).  "Annotate" comes from the Latin "annotatus," past participle of "annotare," meaning "to mark," from "ad-" ("to") + "nota" ("mark").
