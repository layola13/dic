noncorrupter:/ˌnɒnkəˈrʌptər/ |n.| 不腐败的人；不受腐蚀的人 | A person who does not corrupt or become corrupted. |  He was known as a noncorrupter in a city rife with bribery. 他在一个贿赂盛行的城市里被认为是一个不腐败的人。 | The judge was a noncorrupter, refusing all bribes.  这位法官是一位廉洁的人，拒绝了所有的贿赂。 |  Synonyms:  incorruptible,  honest, upright | Antonyms: corrupter,  dishonest,  crooked | noun
