nonvital:/ˌnɒnˈvaɪtəl/ | /ˌnɑːnˈvaɪtl/ | adj. | 非重要的，不重要的 | Not essential to life; not necessary or important. |  The report detailed cuts to nonvital services. 报告详细说明了对非重要服务的削减。|  The company decided to outsource nonvital functions to reduce costs. 公司决定将非核心业务外包以降低成本。| 近义词: unimportant, nonessential, dispensable, secondary | 反义词: vital, essential, crucial, critical | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- + vital. "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "vital" comes from Latin "vitalis," meaning "of or belonging to life."
