malas:/ˈmɑːləs/| adj.|懒惰的，不情愿的；（马来语）坏的，邪恶的|Reluctant or disinclined to exert oneself; lazy. (Malay) bad; evil.|He felt malas about going to work. 他对去上班感到不情愿。|Synonyms: lazy, indolent, slothful, averse, unwilling|Antonyms: industrious, diligent, energetic, eager, willing|adjective


Etymology->
From Malay *malas*
