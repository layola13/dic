aikido:/aɪˈkiːdoʊ/ |n.|合气道|A Japanese martial art that emphasizes blending with an attacker's movements and redirecting their energy to neutralize the attack, rather than directly opposing force with force. | Aikido is a defensive martial art. 合气道是一种防御性武术。| Aikido practitioners learn to control their opponents without causing serious harm. 合气道练习者学习如何在不造成严重伤害的情况下控制对手。|Judo, Jujutsu, Karate|Wrestling, Boxing|noun
