agincourt:/ˈædʒɪnkɔːrt/ /ˈæʒɪnkɔːrt/| n. |阿金库尔; 阿金库尔战役|Agincourt is a commune in the Pas-de-Calais department in northern France.  It is particularly notable for the Battle of Agincourt, a major English victory in the Hundred Years' War, fought nearby on 25 October 1415.|例句：The Battle of Agincourt was a decisive victory for the English. 阿金库尔战役是英国的决定性胜利。
