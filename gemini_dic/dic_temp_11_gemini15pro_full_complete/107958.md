lascivious: /ləˈsɪviəs/ | adj. | 好色的；淫荡的；挑动情欲的 | Inclined to lustfulness; expressing or causing sexual desire. |  The lascivious lyrics of the song were considered offensive by many. 这首歌好色的歌词被许多人认为是冒犯性的。

The dancers' lascivious movements were intended to entice the audience. 舞者挑逗性的动作意在吸引观众。 | 近义词: lustful, lecherous, lewd, salacious | 反义词: chaste, modest, pure | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  From Late Latin *lasciviosus, from Latin lascivus (“wanton, playful, sportive”), from lascīvia (“wantonness”).
