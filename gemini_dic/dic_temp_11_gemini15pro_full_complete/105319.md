kibosh:/ˈkaɪbɒʃ/ (UK) /ˈkaɪbɑːʃ/ (US)| n.|禁止，阻止，压制|a sudden or decisive end to something|例句：The police put the kibosh on their plans for a late-night party. 警察阻止了他们深夜聚会的计划。

例句：She put the kibosh on any further discussion. 她禁止了任何进一步的讨论。

近义词：end, stop, halt, veto, prohibition|反义词:start, beginning, commencement|词性:名词
