agapae:/əˈɡɑːpeɪ/ | n. |  爱，盛宴 | Love, particularly the kind of selfless love described in the Bible; a love feast. |  Early Christians practiced agapae, sharing meals and strengthening their bonds. 早期基督徒实行爱筵，分享食物并加强彼此间的联系。|  Near Synonyms: charity, compassion, benevolence, philia, eros | Antonyms: hatred, animosity, indifference | noun

agape:/əˈɡɑːpeɪ/ | adj. |  (嘴巴)张得大大的，吃惊的 |  Wide open in surprise or wonder. |  He stood agape, staring at the incredible sight. 他目瞪口呆地站着，注视着那不可思议的景象。|  Near Synonyms: astonished, amazed, dumbfounded, awe-struck | Antonyms: unimpressed, bored, indifferent | adjective
