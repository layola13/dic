disclaimed:/dɪsˈkleɪmd/
|v.|否认；放弃；拒绝承认|To deny any claim to, connection with, or responsibility for; renounce.|He disclaimed all responsibility for the accident. 他否认对这起事故负有任何责任。|近义词：deny, renounce, repudiate|反义词：claim, acknowledge, accept|词性:动词
disclaimer:/dɪsˈkleɪmər/
|n.|否认；放弃；免责声明|A statement that denies something, especially responsibility.|The website included a disclaimer about the accuracy of its content. 该网站包含一项关于其内容准确性的免责声明。|近义词：denial, renunciation, waiver|反义词：acceptance, acknowledgment|词性:名词
