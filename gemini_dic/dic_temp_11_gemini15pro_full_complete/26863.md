cajolement:/kəˈdʒoʊlmənt/
n.|哄骗；劝诱；甜言蜜语|The salesman used cajolement to persuade her to buy the expensive car.  推销员用甜言蜜语劝她买那辆昂贵的汽车。|例句：He achieved his goal through cajolement and flattery. 他通过哄骗和奉承达到了目的。|近义词：persuasion, coaxing, wheedling, blandishment|反义词：coercion, force, intimidation|词性:名词

Etymology:
From French *cajolement*, from cajoler "to cajole," of uncertain origin. Possibly from Old North French *cajol*, variant of *gage*, "birdcage," hence "to chatter like a caged bird."
