glycoluric:/ˌɡlaɪkəˈljʊrɪk/
adjective
糖尿的；与尿中糖含量有关的
Relating to or characterized by the presence of sugar in the urine.
Glycoluric acid is found in the urine of mammals.  糖尿酸存在于哺乳动物的尿液中。

Glycoluric tests are important for diagnosing diabetes. 糖尿测试对于诊断糖尿病很重要。

synonym:glycosuric
antonym:aglycosuric
词性:adjective
