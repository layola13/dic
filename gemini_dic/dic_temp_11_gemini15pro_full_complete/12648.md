assecuration:/əˌʃʊəˈreɪʃən/
n.
保证；确信；断言
The act of assuring or the state of being assured; assurance.
He gave me his assecuration that the work would be completed on time. 他向我保证工作会按时完成。

近义词:assurance, guarantee, pledge, promise
反义词:denial, rejection, refusal
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *assecuratio, from Latin assecuratus, past participle of assecurare  "to make safe, secure," from ad- "to" + securus "safe, secure."
