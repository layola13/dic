overpolitical:/ˌoʊvərˈpəlɪtɪkəl/| adj.| 过度政治化的| Excessively concerned with or influenced by politics; too political.  Characterized by excessive involvement or emphasis on politics.|例句：The atmosphere at the office had become overpolitical, making it difficult to focus on work. 办公室的氛围变得过度政治化，让人难以专注于工作。|近义词：hyperpolitical, excessively political|反义词: apolitical, non-political|词性: adjective
