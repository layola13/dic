almighty:/ɔːlˈmaɪti/ | adj. | 全能的，万能的；非常强大的 | Having unlimited power; all-powerful.  | God is often referred to as almighty. 上帝常被称为全能的。

The CEO has almighty power in the company.  这位首席执行官在公司里拥有至高无上的权力。 | synonyms: omnipotent, all-powerful, supreme | antonyms: powerless, weak, impotent | adjective


almighty:/ɔːlˈmaɪti/ | n. |  上帝，万能的主 | God. (Often used as an exclamation.) |  Oh, Almighty! What have I done?  哦，上帝！我做了什么？ | synonyms: God, Lord, The Almighty | antonyms: N/A (No direct antonym for a name) | noun
