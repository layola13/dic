mosque:/mɒsk/ (UK), /mɑːsk/ (US)| n. |清真寺|A building used for Muslim worship.|We visited the grand mosque in Istanbul. 我们参观了伊斯坦布尔的宏伟清真寺。|The mosque is a place of peace and prayer.  清真寺是和平和祈祷的场所。|近义词：Islamic temple, masjid|反义词: (No direct antonym exists.  One might consider "church," "synagogue," or "temple" as places of worship for other religions, but they are not true opposites.)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *mosquee*, from Italian *moschea*, from Spanish *mezquita* "mosque," from Arabic *masjid*  "place of prostration (in prayer)," from *sajada* "to prostrate oneself, to bow down in worship".
