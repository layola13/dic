muesli: /ˈmjuːzli/ (UK), /ˈmuːsli/ (US)| n. | 木斯利，麦片粥 | A breakfast cereal consisting of rolled oats, nuts, dried fruit, and sometimes other ingredients, typically eaten with milk or yogurt. |  I like to eat muesli with berries and yogurt for breakfast. 我喜欢早餐吃木斯利配浆果和酸奶。 |  Example: She sprinkled some seeds on her muesli. 她往木斯利里撒了些种子。 | Synonym: granola, cereal | Antonym: N/A | noun
