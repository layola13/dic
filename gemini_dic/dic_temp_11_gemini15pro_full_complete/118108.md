meniver: /ˈmɛnɪvər/ | n. |灰鼠皮；用灰鼠皮做的衣服或饰物|The fine fur of a small, grey Eurasian squirrel, formerly used in trimming and lining garments.|The queen wore a robe of crimson velvet trimmed with meniver.|近义词:ermine, miniver |反义词: None |词性: Noun

Etymology:
From Old French *menu vair* (“small vair”), composed of menu (“small”) and vair (“a type of fur”).  Vair itself comes from Latin *varius* (“variegated, diverse”).
