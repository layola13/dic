misrefers:/ˌmɪsrɪˈfɜːrz/
verb
错误地提及；错误地引用
To cite or refer to incorrectly or inappropriately.
The lawyer misreferred to a key piece of evidence, weakening his case.  律师错误地引用了一个关键证据，削弱了他的论点。
misquote, miscite
correctly cite, accurately refer
verb
