obituarists:/əˈbɪtʃuːərɪsts/
n.
讣告作者，撰写讣告的人
writers of obituaries, often journalists employed by newspapers.
The New York Times employs several obituarists. 纽约时报雇佣了几位讣告作者。
journalist, writer, reporter, biographer, chronicler
