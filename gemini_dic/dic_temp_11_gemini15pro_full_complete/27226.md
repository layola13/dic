callyciflorous: /ˌkælɪsɪˈflɔːrəs/
adj.
杯状花冠的；合瓣花冠的
Having the petals of the corolla united into a tube or cup, as in the primrose.
The callyciflorous flower has petals fused together to form a cup or tube shape. 这种杯状花冠的花，其花瓣融合在一起形成杯状或管状。
Many members of the primrose family are callyciflorous.  报春花科的许多成员都是杯状花冠的。
近义词: gamopetalous, sympetalous
反义词: choripetalous, polypetalous
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin calyx (cup, calyx) + -florus (flowered), from flos (flower).
