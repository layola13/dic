jabberment:/ˈdʒæbərmənt/| n. |快而含糊不清的说话；喋喋不休|rapid, incoherent, and often meaningless talk; chatter.|She burst into a fit of jabberment, barely pausing for breath. 她突然开始喋喋不休地说个不停，几乎没有喘息的机会。|The salesman's jabberment made it difficult to understand the product's features. 推销员喋喋不休的讲话让人很难理解产品的功能。|近义词：babble, chatter, prattle, gibberish|反义词: silence, quiet|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From jabber (v.) + -ment.  "Jabber" comes from imitative origin, likely echoing the sound of rapid, indistinct speech.
