asylee:/əˌsaɪˈliː/
n.
寻求政治庇护者；难民
A person who is seeking or has been granted political asylum.
The asylee fled his war-torn country in search of safety.  这个寻求政治庇护者逃离了他饱受战争蹂躏的国家，以寻求安全。
The asylee was granted permission to stay in the country.  这位寻求政治庇护者获准留在该国。
近义词:refugee, exile
反义词:citizen, national
词性:名词
