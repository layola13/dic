nonhabitableness:/ˌnɒnhæbɪtəblnəs/
|n.|不适宜居住;  不宜居住性|The nonhabitableness of the planet made it impossible for humans to settle there.  这个星球不适宜居住，使得人类无法在那里定居。|
|英文解析: The state or quality of being unfit or unsuitable for habitation.|
|例句：The extreme temperatures contribute to the nonhabitableness of the desert. 极端的温度导致沙漠不适宜居住。|
|例句：The report detailed the nonhabitableness of the contaminated area. 报告详细说明了受污染地区不适宜居住的情况。|
|近义词：uninhabitability, inhospitability|
|反义词：habitability, suitability for living|
|词性: noun|
