abrasive:/əˈbreɪsɪv/ (US), /əˈbreɪsɪv/ (UK)
adjective|磨蚀的，研磨的；粗鲁的，伤人的|Tending to wear away by rubbing or scraping; Harsh and rough in manner.
例句：
The abrasive surface of the sandpaper smoothed the wood.
砂纸粗糙的表面使木头光滑。

He has a very abrasive personality.
他性格非常粗鲁。

近义词：rough, harsh, grating, caustic, grinding
反义词：smooth, gentle, kind, polite
词性|adjective


abrasive:/əˈbreɪsɪv/ (US), /əˈbreɪsɪv/ (UK)
noun|磨料|A substance used for grinding or polishing.
例句：
Diamond dust is an effective abrasive.
金刚石粉是一种有效的磨料。

They used a powerful abrasive to clean the rust off the metal.
他们用强力磨料清除金属上的锈迹。

近义词：grindstone, emery, sandpaper
反义词：polish, lubricant
词性|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin abradere "to scrape off," from ab- "away" + radere "to scrape."
