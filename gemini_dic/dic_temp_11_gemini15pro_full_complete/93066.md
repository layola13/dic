idolist: /ˈaɪdəlɪst/ | n. |偶像崇拜者；追星族 | A person who excessively admires or reveres an idol. | She's a real idolist, following every move of her favorite pop star. 她是一个真正的追星族，关注着她最喜欢的流行歌星的一举一动。 | 近义词: fan, devotee, admirer | 反义词: critic, detractor | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From idol + -ist.  "Idol" comes from the Old French "idole," ultimately from the Greek "eidolon" meaning "image" or "phantom."  The suffix "-ist" denotes a person who adheres to a particular doctrine or system or engages in a particular activity.
