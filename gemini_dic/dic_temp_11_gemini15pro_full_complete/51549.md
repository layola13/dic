deurbanization:/diːˌɜːrbənaɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
去城市化；逆城市化
The process of people moving from urban areas to rural areas.
The report discussed the causes and effects of deurbanization.  这份报告讨论了逆城市化的原因和影响。
Deurbanization is becoming a trend in some developed countries. 在一些发达国家，逆城市化正成为一种趋势。
近义词:ruralization, counter-urbanization
反义词:urbanization
词性:noun
