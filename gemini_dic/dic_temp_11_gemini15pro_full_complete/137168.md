nonsuccessful:/ˌnɒnsəkˈsɛsfəl/ | adj. | 不成功的，失败的 | Not achieving the desired outcome or result; failing to accomplish a goal or purpose. | The nonsuccessful launch of the product led to significant financial losses. 产品发布的失败导致了巨大的经济损失。| The team made a nonsuccessful attempt to climb the mountain. 团队攀登这座山的尝试失败了。 | 近义词: failed, unsuccessful, fruitless, abortive, unproductive | 反义词: successful, triumphant, prosperous, fruitful | 词性: adjective

词源:
From non- (not) + successful.  The prefix "non-" negates the meaning of "successful."  "Successful" ultimately comes from the Latin "succedere" (to succeed).
