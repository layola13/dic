guns: /ɡʌnz/
n.
枪支;炮;武器
firearms; weapons
The police found two guns in the car. 警方在车里发现了 two guns in the car. 警方在车里发现了两把枪。
近义词: firearms, weapons, armaments
反义词: peace


词性: 名词(plural)

词源: From Middle English *gunne, probably from a personal name, Gunnhildr, from Old Norse Gunnhildr, literally “war battle”.
