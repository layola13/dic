graptolitic:/ˌɡræptəˈlɪtɪk/| adj.|笔石的；似笔石的；含笔石的|Relating to, resembling, or containing graptolites, extinct marine colonial animals.|The rock formation was rich in graptolitic fossils. 这块岩层富含笔石化石。|The geologist discovered a new species of graptolitic organism.  这位地质学家发现了一个新的笔石生物物种。|近义词:fossiliferous (含化石的)|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "barren" in the context of fossil content) |词性:adjective

词源: From "graptolite" + "-ic".  "Graptolite" comes from Greek *graptos* (written, drawn) + *lithos* (stone), referring to their fossil appearance.
