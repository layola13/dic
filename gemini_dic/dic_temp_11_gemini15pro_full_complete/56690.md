doom-and-gloom:/ˈduːm ən ˈɡluːm/
noun
悲观沮丧；前景暗淡
A feeling or attitude of pessimism or despair; a prediction of a bleak or disastrous future.
The media's constant doom and gloom about the economy is depressing. 媒体对经济持续的悲观报道令人沮丧。
pessimism, despair, negativity
optimism, hope, positivity
