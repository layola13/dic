obtusity:/ɒbˈtjuːsɪti/ /ɑːbˈtuːsɪti/
n.
迟钝，愚钝；不尖，钝性
The state of being obtuse; dullness of mind or senses; insensitivity.
His obtusity made him unable to grasp the subtle nuances of the conversation. 他的迟钝使他无法理解对话中微妙的细微差别。
近义词：dullness, stupidity, insensitivity, obtuseness
反义词：acuteness, sharpness, intelligence, sensitivity
词性：名词

obtuse:/əbˈtjuːs/ /ɑːbˈtuːs/
adj.
钝的；迟钝的；不敏感的
Not sharp or pointed; slow to understand or perceive; insensitive.
He made an obtuse remark that offended everyone in the room. 他说了一句愚蠢的话，冒犯了房间里的每个人。
近义词：dull, stupid, insensitive
反义词：sharp, acute, intelligent, sensitive
词性：形容词
