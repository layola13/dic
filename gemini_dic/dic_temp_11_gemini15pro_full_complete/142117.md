oriflamme:/ˈɔːrɪˌflæm/ | n. | 旗帜，（尤指）军旗，象征 | A banner or standard, especially one associated with a particular cause or group.  It originally referred to the golden or orange-red banner of the French kings. | The king raised the oriflamme, signaling the start of the battle. 国王升起了军旗，标志着战斗的开始。 | The soldiers rallied around their oriflamme. 士兵们聚集在他们的军旗下。 | synonyms: banner, standard, pennant, flag | antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider) surrender, defeat | noun
