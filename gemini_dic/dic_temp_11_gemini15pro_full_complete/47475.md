deallocation:/ˌdiːæləˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |释放，解除分配|The deallocation of memory is crucial for preventing memory leaks. 内存的释放对于防止内存泄漏至关重要。|例句：The system performs automatic deallocation of unused memory. 系统自动释放未使用的内存。|近义词：release, freeing|反义词:allocation|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- + allocation.  "De-" is a prefix meaning "removal" or "reversal". "Allocation" comes from the Old French "alocation" meaning "assignment" or "apportionment".
