cooing:/ˈkuːɪŋ/ | n. | 咕咕声；柔声细语 | a soft murmuring sound like that of a dove |  The mother was cooing over her newborn baby. 母亲对着她刚出生的婴儿柔声细语。 | 近义词: murmuring, crooning | 反义词: shouting, yelling | 词性: 名词

cooing:/ˈkuːɪŋ/ | adj. | 柔声细语的 | making soft murmuring sounds | The cooing doves perched on the windowsill. 咕咕叫的鸽子栖息在窗台上。| 近义词: gentle, soft | 反义词: loud, harsh | 词性: 形容词
