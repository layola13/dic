bipolarities:/ˌbaɪpoʊˈlærɪtiz/ | n. | 双极性，两极分化 | The state of having two opposite poles or extremes.  It often refers to bipolar disorder in a psychological context, but can also describe situations with two opposing forces or viewpoints. |  The bipolarities of his personality made him difficult to understand. 他的性格的两极分化使他难以理解。| synonyms: dualities, extremes, oppositions | antonyms: similarities, uniformities | noun

bipolarities:/ˌbaɪpoʊˈlærɪtiz/ | n. | 两级，两极 | The existence of two contrasting or opposing aspects, forces, or tendencies. | The political bipolarities of the Cold War era shaped global relations. 冷战时期的政治两极格局塑造了全球关系。 | synonyms: dichotomies, polarities | antonyms: unities, harmonies | noun
