archiblastula:/ˌɑːrkɪˈblæstʃʊlə/ | n. | 原肠胚 | An embryonic stage in many animals, following the blastula and preceding the gastrula, characterized by a hollow sphere of cells with no clear differentiation of germ layers. |  The archiblastula is a crucial stage in embryonic development. 原肠胚是胚胎发育的关键阶段。 | The archiblastula develops into the gastrula. 原肠胚发育成原肠胚。 |  blastula, early embryo |  gastrula, late embryo | noun
