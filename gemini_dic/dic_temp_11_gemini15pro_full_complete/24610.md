broilingly:/ˈbrɔɪlɪŋli/
adv.
酷热地，灼热地
extremely hot, like something being broiled
The sun beat down broilingly. 太阳酷热地照射下来。

近义词: scorchingly, intensely hot, blazingly
反义词: coldly, freezingly
词性: 副词 (Adverb)
词源: From "broil" (to cook by exposure to direct, intense heat) + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverb suffix).  Ultimately from Old French "bruillir" (to burn, roast).
