affreighter:/əˈfreɪtər/
n.
包租人；租船人
A person or company that hires a ship to transport goods.
The affreighter is responsible for loading and unloading the cargo. 包租人负责装卸货物。
The company acted as an affreighter for the shipment of goods overseas. 这家公司担任了海外货物运输的包租人。
Synonym: charterer
Antonym: shipowner
