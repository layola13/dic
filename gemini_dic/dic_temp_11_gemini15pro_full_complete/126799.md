nanotechnology:/ˌnænəʊtekˈnɒlədʒi/ (British) /ˌnænəʊtekˈnɑːlədʒi/ (American)| n.|纳米科技|The science and technology of building devices, such as electronic circuits, from single atoms and molecules.|Nanotechnology has revolutionized materials science. 纳米技术彻底改变了材料科学。|近义词:nanoscience, nanotech|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "conventional technology" or "macro-technology")|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "nano-" (meaning one billionth) + "technology".  "Nano-" comes from the Greek word "nanos" meaning "dwarf".
