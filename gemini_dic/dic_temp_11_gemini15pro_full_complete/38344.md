conceives:/kənˈsiːvz/
verb
构想，设想；怀孕
form or devise (a plan or idea) in the mind. become pregnant with (a child).
She conceived the idea of starting her own business. 她萌生了自己创业的想法。
The project was conceived in 1990. 这个项目构想于1990年。
She conceived a child last year. 她去年怀了一个孩子。
The advertisement was cleverly conceived. 这则广告构思巧妙。

synonym: imagine, devise, formulate, visualize, think up, dream up; become pregnant with, gestate
antonym: misinterpret, misunderstand;
