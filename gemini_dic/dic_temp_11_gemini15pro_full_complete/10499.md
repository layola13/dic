appointable:/əˈpɔɪntəbl/
adjective
可任命的；可委派的
Suitable or qualified to be appointed to a position.
The position is appointable by the governor.  这个职位由州长任命。
The committee is seeking an appointable candidate for the vacant position. 委员会正在寻找一位可以被任命来填补空缺职位的候选人。
近义词: eligible, qualified, suitable
反义词: ineligible, unsuitable, unqualified
词性: adjective
词源: From appoint + -able.  来自动词appoint 加上形容词后缀 -able.
