cuspal:/ˈkʌspəl/| adj.|尖端的，交界的，关键的|Relating to a cusp, especially a cusp between two astrological signs.  It signifies a point of transition or change.|Her birthday is on the cusp of Gemini and Cancer, so she has traits of both. 她的生日在双子座和巨蟹座的交界点，所以她兼具两个星座的特质。|Synonyms: transitional, borderline, liminal|Antonyms: stable, unchanging|adjective
