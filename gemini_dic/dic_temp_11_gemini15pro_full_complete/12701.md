assertional:/əˈsɜːʃənl/
adjective
断言的；声明的
Of, relating to, or characterized by assertion.  Making a statement as if it were true.
The lawyer made an assertional statement about the defendant's guilt.  律师对被告的罪行作出了断言。
assertive, declarative, affirmative
unassertive, questioning, hesitant
adjective
