garland:/ˈɡɑːrlənd/ | n. | 花环，花冠，荣誉 | A wreath of flowers and leaves, worn on the head or hung as a decoration; an award or honour. | The bride wore a garland of white roses. 新娘戴着白色玫瑰花环。 | synonyms: wreath, chaplet, crown, festoon | antonyms: none | noun

garland:/ˈɡɑːrlənd/ | vt. | 用花环装饰 | To decorate with a garland. | They garlanded the winning horse. 他们用花环装饰了获胜的马。 | synonyms: decorate, adorn, festoon, wreathe | antonyms: strip, denude | verb
