molt:/moʊlt/ (US), /məʊlt/ (UK)| v. n. |蜕皮，换羽; 脱落; |To shed periodically part or all of a coat or an outer covering, such as feathers, cuticle, or skin, which is then replaced by a new growth.|The snake molts its skin. 蛇蜕皮。|近义词：shed, cast off, exuviate|反义词:grow, develop|词性:动词, 名词

molt:/moʊlt/ (US), /məʊlt/ (UK)| n. |蜕皮; 换羽; 脱落|The act or process of molting.|The bird is in its molt and can't fly. 这只鸟正在换羽，不能飞。|近义词：shedding, casting off, exuviation|反义词:growth, development|词性:名词
