diploid:/ˈdɪplɔɪd/| adj. n.|二倍体|Having two sets of chromosomes, one from each parent.  A cell or organism that is diploid.|例句：Humans are diploid organisms. 人类是二倍体生物。

例句：The diploid number of chromosomes in humans is 46. 人类的二倍体染色体数目是46。|近义词：double, twofold|反义词：haploid|词性：形容词, 名词
