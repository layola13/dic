jahve /ˈjɑːveɪ/ | n. |耶和华，上帝|A name of God, especially in the Old Testament, representing the Hebrew consonants YHWH.  It is considered by some to be the most sacred name of God and is traditionally not pronounced by Jews, being substituted with Adonai (Lord) or HaShem (The Name). |The Tetragrammaton, YHWH, is often rendered as "Jahve" in scholarly works.  耶和华这个名字，即YHWH，经常在学术著作中被翻译成“Jahve”。

God appeared to Moses and revealed his name as Jahve. 上帝向摩西显现，并告诉摩西他的名字是耶和华。|近义词：Jehovah, Yahweh|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
