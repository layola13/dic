grantor:/ˈɡræntər/ (US), /ˈɡrɑːntə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 授予者；让渡人；财产赠与人 |A grantor is a person or entity that transfers property or rights to another person or entity. |例句：The grantor transferred ownership of the land to the grantee.  授予者将土地所有权转让给了受让人。

例句：The grantor of the trust designated his daughter as the beneficiary.  信托授予人指定他的女儿为受益人。

近义词：donor, giver, assigner|反义词：grantee, recipient|词性:noun
