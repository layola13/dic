neophytic:/ˌniːəˈfɪtɪk/
adjective
新生的；新近引进的；缺乏经验的
Relating to or characteristic of a neophyte; new or recent in a profession or field of interest; inexperienced.
The neophytic writer struggled with plot development.  这位新手作家在情节发展方面遇到了困难。
The company's neophytic approach to marketing led to disappointing results.  这家公司在新市场营销方法上的缺乏经验导致了令人失望的结果。
近义词:new, recent, novice, inexperienced, fledgling, budding
反义词:experienced, veteran, seasoned, expert, skilled, mature
词性:adjective

neophyte:/ˈniːəfaɪt/
noun
新手；新入教者
A person who is new to a subject, skill, or belief.
He was a neophyte in the world of finance. 他是金融界的新手。
The neophytes were given a brief introduction to the company's history.  新员工们被简要介绍了公司的历史。
近义词:beginner, novice, learner, newcomer, rookie, initiate
反义词:expert, veteran, professional, master
词性:noun
