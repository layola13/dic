begin:/bɪˈɡɪn/| v.|开始，着手|To start or initiate something.|例句：The movie will begin soon. 电影很快就要开始了。|近义词：start, commence, initiate|反义词：end, finish, cease|词性:动词

began:/bɪˈɡæn/| v.|开始，着手（过去式）|The past tense form of "begin," indicating an action started in the past.|例句：They began the project last year. 他们去年开始了这个项目。|近义词：started, commenced, initiated|反义词：ended, finished, ceased|词性:动词

begun:/bɪˈɡʌn/| v.|开始，着手（过去分词）|The past participle of "begin," used in perfect tenses and passive voice.|例句：The project has begun. 项目已经开始了。|近义词：started, commenced, initiated|反义词：ended, finished, ceased|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *beginnen, from Old English beginnan, from Proto-Germanic *duginnan (“to begin, undertake”), equivalent to be- +‎ gin. Cognate with Dutch beginnen (“to begin”), German beginnen (“to begin”).

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
The concert begins at 8 pm.  音乐会晚上8点开始。
He begins his day with a cup of coffee. 他以一杯咖啡开始新的一天。


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
The band is beginning to play. 乐队开始演奏了。
He is beginning to understand the problem. 他开始理解这个问题了。


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
The rain has begun. 雨已经开始了。
The meeting has begun already. 会议已经开始了。


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
They have been beginning to plan their trip. 他们已经开始计划他们的旅行了。
She has been beginning to learn Spanish. 她已经开始学习西班牙语了。


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
The movie began at 7 pm. 电影晚上7点开始。
World War II began in 1939. 第二次世界大战始于1939年。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
The sun was beginning to set. 太阳开始落山了。
It was beginning to rain when we left. 我们离开时开始下雨了。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
The play had begun before we arrived.  在我们到达之前，戏剧已经开始了。
The game had begun by the time I got there. 我到那儿的时候比赛已经开始了。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
They had been beginning to suspect him. 他们已经开始怀疑他了。
She had been beginning to feel better. 她已经开始感觉好些了。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
The class will begin next week. 这门课下周开始。
The construction will begin in the spring. 施工将于春季开始。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
This time next week, I will be beginning my new job. 下周这个时候，我将开始我的新工作。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
