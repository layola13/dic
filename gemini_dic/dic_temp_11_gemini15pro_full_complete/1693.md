aconitic:/ˌækəˈnɪtɪk/| adj.|乌头酸的，与乌头酸有关的|Relating to or derived from aconitic acid, a crystalline unsaturated tricarboxylic acid found in plants such as monkshood and sugar cane.|Aconitic acid is an intermediate in the citric acid cycle.  乌头酸是柠檬酸循环的中间产物。|Aconitic acid is commercially important in the production of certain plastics.  乌头酸在某些塑料的生产中具有商业重要性。|Synonyms: propenetricarboxylic, |Antonyms: |adjective
