collating:/ˈkɒleɪtɪŋ/ (UK), /kəˈleɪtɪŋ/ (US)| v. |校对，整理，核对|The process of checking and arranging sheets of paper in the correct order.|例句：The secretary is collating the documents for the meeting. 秘书正在为会议整理文件。|例句：He spent hours collating data for his research paper. 他花了几个小时整理研究论文的数据。|近义词：assembling, gathering, organizing, arranging|反义词: scattering, disorganizing|词性:动词(present participle of collate)

collate:/kəˈleɪt/ (US), /ˈkɒleɪt/ (UK)| v. |校对，整理，核对|To collect and arrange (texts, information, or sets of figures) in proper order.|例句：The photocopier can collate and staple the documents automatically.  复印机可以自动整理和装订文件。|例句：She collated the responses from the survey. 她整理了调查问卷的回复。|近义词：assemble, gather, organize, arrange|反义词: scatter, disorganize|词性:动词
