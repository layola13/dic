consubstantialism:/ˌkɒnsəbˈstænʃəlɪzəm/ | n. | 同体性，同质性 | The doctrine that the Son is of the same substance as the Father.  This doctrine is central to Christian theology, especially in Trinitarianism. |  The Nicene Creed affirmed the consubstantiality of Christ with God the Father. 尼西亚信经确认了基督与圣父的同体性。|  The debate over consubstantialism led to a major schism in the early Church. 关于同体性的争论导致了早期教会的主要分裂。 |  coessentiality,  homousia | heterousia | noun
