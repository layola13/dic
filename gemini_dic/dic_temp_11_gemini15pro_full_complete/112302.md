lordful:/ˈlɔːrdfʊl/
adj.
像上帝的；有主权的；傲慢的；高贵的
Having the qualities of a lord; majestic, commanding, or arrogant.
The lordful king surveyed his domain.  这位威严的国王 surveying 他的领土。
The lordful manner in which she addressed her servants was offensive. 她用傲慢的态度对她的仆人说话，这很无礼。
近义词: majestic, stately, imperious, haughty
反义词: humble, meek, subservient
词性: 形容词


词源:  From "lord" + "-ful," a suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by."  来源于 "lord"（领主） + "-ful"（充满...的，具有...特性的后缀）。
