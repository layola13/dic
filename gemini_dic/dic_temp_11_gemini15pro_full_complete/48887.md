deframing:/diːˈfreɪmɪŋ/
|v.|去框架化；打破框架；重新构建|The process of challenging and changing limiting beliefs and assumptions, often culturally or socially constructed, that restrict one's perspectives and behaviors.  It involves examining the underlying structures that shape our understanding of the world and considering alternative ways of thinking and acting.|Therapy helped her in deframing her past experiences and moving forward.  治疗帮助她重新构建过去的经历并继续前进。|reframing, reconstructing, rethinking, challenging, deconstructing|framing, restricting, limiting|verb
