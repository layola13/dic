drizzle:/ˈdrɪzəl/ (US), /ˈdrɪzl/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|细雨，毛毛雨；下毛毛雨|A light, steady rain falling in very small drops.|例句：A light drizzle began to fall. 开始下起了毛毛雨。|近义词：sprinkle, mist|反义词：downpour, torrent|词性:名词,动词

drizzle:/ˈdrɪzəl/ (US), /ˈdrɪzl/ (UK)| vi. vt.|下毛毛雨；少量地洒|To rain lightly and steadily in very small drops; to sprinkle lightly.|例句：It drizzled all morning.  整个早上都在下毛毛雨。|近义词：sprinkle, mist|反义词：downpour, torrent|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *driselen, frequentative of drisle (“to fall in drops”), from Old English *drisol (“drizzle”), akin to Middle Low German drieseln, dreseln (“to drizzle”).
