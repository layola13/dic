departure:/dɪˈpɑːrtʃər/ (US) /dɪˈpɑːtʃə(r)/ (UK)| n.|出发，离开；背离，违反|An act of leaving a place, especially at the beginning of a journey.  The act of deviating from a usual or accepted course of action.|例句：Their departure was delayed by the storm. 他们的出发被暴风雨耽搁了。|近义词：exit, leaving, going away, egress|反义词：arrival, coming, return|noun
