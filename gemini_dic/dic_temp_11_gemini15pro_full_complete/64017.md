epitaphic:/ˌɛpɪˈtæfɪk/
adjective
墓志铭的；适合做墓志铭的；（尤指）哀悼的，追悼的
of, relating to, or resembling an epitaph; especially : suitable for or suggestive of an epitaph
The poem had an epitaphic tone, mourning the loss of a loved one. 这首诗带有墓志铭的意味，哀悼失去的亲人。
synonyms:memorial, commemorative, elegiac
antonyms: celebratory, joyous, festive
词性: adjective
