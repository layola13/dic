linchpin:/ˈlɪntʃpɪn/| n.|关键人物；核心；关键|A person or thing vital to an enterprise or organization.|例句：She is the linchpin of the whole team. 她是整个团队的关键人物。

例句：The central axle is the linchpin of the entire mechanism. 中心轴是整个机制的核心。|近义词：keystone, cornerstone, centerpiece, pivot, kingpin|反义词:nonessential, unimportant|词性:noun
