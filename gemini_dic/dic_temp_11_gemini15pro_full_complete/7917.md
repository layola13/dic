antepaschal:/ˌæntiˈpæskəl/
adjective
复活节前的, 四旬斋期的
Occurring or existing before Easter; pre-Paschal.
The antepaschal fast is traditionally observed by Christians. 基督徒传统上会遵守复活节前的斋戒。
近义词: pre-Easter, pre-Paschal
反义词: post-Paschal
词性: adjective

词源:  From Late Latin *antepaschālis, from Latin ante (“before”) + pāschālis (“Paschal”).
