mpeg:/ˈemˌpeg/| n. |动态图像专家组；MPEG 编码|Moving Picture Experts Group (a working group of ISO/IEC that develops standards for coded representation of moving pictures, audio, and their combination). A standard for compressing video signals.|例句：The video file is encoded in MPEG format.  这个视频文件是以MPEG格式编码的。
