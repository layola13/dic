citational:/saɪˈteɪʃənəl/
adj.
引用的，引证的
Of, relating to, or consisting of a citation or citations.
The professor made a citational reference to Shakespeare. 教授引用了莎士比亚的作品。
近义词:  quoted, referenced, allusive
反义词: original, uncited
词性: adjective
