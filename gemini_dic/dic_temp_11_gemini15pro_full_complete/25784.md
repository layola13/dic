bureaucrat: /ˈbjʊərəkræt/ (US) /ˈbjʊərəkræt/ (UK)| n. | 官僚 | An official in a government department, in particular one perceived as being concerned with procedural correctness at the expense of people's needs. | The bureaucrats insisted on following the rules to the letter, even though it meant delaying the aid to the disaster victims. 官僚们坚持严格遵守规则，即使这意味着推迟对灾民的援助。| Example 2:  The project was stalled by bureaucratic red tape.  这个项目因官僚主义的繁文缛节而停滞不前。 | Synonyms: official, administrator, civil servant | Antonyms: nonconformist, maverick, rebel | noun
