botryogen:/ˈbɒtriədʒən/ | n. |葡萄石|A hydrous magnesium iron sulfate mineral, MgFe3+(SO4)2(OH)·7H2O, occurring as reddish monoclinic crystals. It is typically found in arid regions as an alteration product of pyrite and other sulfide minerals.|Botryogen was identified in the mine tailings.|近义词: None (highly specific mineral name)|反义词: None|词性: noun
