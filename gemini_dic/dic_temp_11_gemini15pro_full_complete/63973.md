epispastic:/ˌɛpɪˈspæstɪk/ | adj. n. | 发疱的，起泡剂 |  Causing blisters; a blistering agent.  Derived from the Greek ἐπί (epi-) "upon" + σπάω (spaō) "to draw".  It refers to a substance that draws fluids to the skin's surface, creating a blister. |  Example: The physician applied an epispastic to draw out the infection.  医生涂抹了一种发疱剂来排出感染。| Synonyms: blistering, vesicant, vesicatory | Antonyms: soothing, demulcent | adjective
epispastic:/ˌɛpɪˈspæstɪk/ | n. | 发疱剂 | A medicine or agent that causes blistering. | Example:  The epispastic caused a painful blister.  这种发疱剂引起了一个疼痛的水泡。 | Synonyms: vesicant, vesicatory, blister agent | Antonyms:  demulcent, emollient | noun
