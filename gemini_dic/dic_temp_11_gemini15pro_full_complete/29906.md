cautioners:/ˈkɔːʃənərz/
n.
保证人，担保人
People who formally vouch for the good character or conduct of another, often in a legal or financial context.
The loan agreement required two cautioners to sign. 这份贷款协议需要两名担保人签字。
guarantors, sureties, sponsors
opponents, adversaries
noun
