embody:/ɪmˈbɒdi/ (UK) /ɪmˈbɑːdi/ (US)| v.|体现，具体表现，包含|to represent a quality or an idea exactly; to include as part of a system or whole|例句：She embodies the spirit of generosity. 她体现了慷慨的精神。

例句：The latest model embodies many new improvements.  最新款包含了许多新的改进。

近义词：represent, personify, incorporate, exemplify|反义词:exclude, omit|词性:动词
