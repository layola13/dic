butte:/bjuːt/| n. |孤峰，孤山，小丘|A butte is an isolated hill with steep sides and a flat top, similar to but smaller than a mesa.|We drove past a majestic butte rising from the plains. 我们开车经过平原上拔地而起的雄伟孤峰。|The butte offered a stunning view of the surrounding landscape.  这座孤峰提供了周围景观的壮丽景色。|Synonyms: mesa, hill, mound|Antonyms: valley, canyon, plain|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *butte*, meaning "small hill," ultimately from Vulgar Latin *butta*, "thicket, mound," itself of uncertain origin.
