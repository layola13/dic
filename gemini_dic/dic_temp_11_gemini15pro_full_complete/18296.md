belladonna:/ˌbeləˈdɒnə/ (US) /ˌbeləˈdɒnə/ (UK)| n. |颠茄，美女草|A perennial herbaceous plant (Atropa belladonna) with poisonous black berries and purplish-red flowers. The source of atropine and related alkaloids.|Belladonna is a highly toxic plant and should never be ingested. 颠茄是一种剧毒植物，绝对不能摄入。|She was poisoned with belladonna. 她被颠茄毒死了。|近义词：deadly nightshade|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider remedies for its effects, e.g., activated charcoal) |noun
