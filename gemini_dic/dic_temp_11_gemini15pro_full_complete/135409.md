nonperpendicularity:/ˌnɒnpɜːrpəndɪˌkjʊˈlærɪti/
noun
非垂直性；非正交性
The state or condition of not being perpendicular.  Lack of perpendicularity can cause structural instability.
The nonperpendicularity of the two lines caused the structure to lean.  两条线不垂直导致结构倾斜。
