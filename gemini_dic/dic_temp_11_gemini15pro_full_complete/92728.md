ichnology: /ɪkˈnɒlədʒi/ | n. |  遗迹学 | The study of trace fossils, such as footprints, burrows, and other evidence of animal activity. | Example 1: Ichnology provides valuable insights into the behavior of extinct animals.  遗迹学为了解已灭绝动物的行为提供了宝贵的见解。

Example 2: The ichnology of the site revealed a diverse range of dinosaur species.  该遗址的遗迹学揭示了各种各样的恐龙物种。 | Synonym:  trace fossil study | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps body fossil study) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek ikhnos (ἴχνος) "track, trace" + -logy (-λογία) "study of."
