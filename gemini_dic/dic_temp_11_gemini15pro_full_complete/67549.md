extraneous:/ɛkˈstreɪniəs/| adj.|外来的；无关的；非主要因素的|Not belonging to or part of something; irrelevant or unrelated to the subject being dealt with.|例句：Extraneous noise made it difficult to hear the speaker. 外来的噪音使人很难听清演讲者的声音。

例句：The extraneous information in the article made it confusing. 文章中无关的信息使它令人困惑。|近义词：irrelevant, foreign, external, outside, alien, peripheral|反义词：relevant, essential, intrinsic, inherent|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *extraneus, from Latin extrāneus "external, foreign," from extrā "outside," from ex "out of" + comparative suffix -ter (akin to Greek -teros, Sanskrit -tara).
