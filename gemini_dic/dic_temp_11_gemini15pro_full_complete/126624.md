nailer:/ˈneɪlər/| n.|钉枪；钉子工|A tool used to drive nails.  A person who drives nails (usually as a profession).|例句：He used a pneumatic nailer to build the fence. 他用气动钉枪建造了栅栏。

例句：The nailer finished the roof in record time.  那个钉子工以创纪录的速度完成了屋顶的建造。|近义词：nail gun, nail driver (for the tool); carpenter (for the person)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
