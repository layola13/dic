nonpliability:/ˌnɒnplaɪəˈbɪləti/
n.|不顺应性，固执，僵硬|The nonpliability of the material made it difficult to work with.  这种材料的不顺应性使得它很难加工。|例句：His nonpliability made negotiations difficult. 他的固执使得谈判很困难。  The nonpliability of the rules frustrated the employees. 规则的僵化使员工感到沮丧。|近义词：inflexibility, rigidity, stubbornness, obstinacy|反义词: flexibility, pliability, adaptability, compliance|词性:名词

nonpliant:/ˌnɒnˈplaɪənt/
adj.|不顺应的，固执的，僵硬的|The nonpliant branches of the old oak tree wouldn't bend.  老橡树不顺应的树枝不会弯曲。|例句：Her nonpliant attitude made it hard to reach a compromise. 她不顺应的态度使得很难达成妥协。 The nonpliant material cracked under pressure.  这种不顺应的材料在压力下破裂了。|近义词：inflexible, rigid, stubborn, obstinate|反义词: flexible, pliant, adaptable, compliant|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + pliant.  "Pliant" comes from Old French "pliant" (bending, folding), present participle of "plier" (to bend), from Latin "plicare" (to fold).
