overslowness:/ˌoʊvərˈsləʊnɪs/ | n. | 过于缓慢，迟缓 |  The overslowness of the process frustrated everyone involved. overslow:/ˌoʊvərˈsloʊ/ | adj. | 过于缓慢的，迟缓的 |  While less common than the noun form, "overslow" can function as an adjective.  It describes something that is excessively slow.  | The overslow pace of the project worried the stakeholders.  项目过于缓慢的进度让利益相关者感到担忧。 | synonyms: slow, sluggish, tardy | antonyms: fast, rapid, swift | adjective

Etymology:  "overslowness" is derived from the prefix "over-" (meaning "excessive" or "too much") and the adjective "slow," which has its roots in Old English "slaw."
