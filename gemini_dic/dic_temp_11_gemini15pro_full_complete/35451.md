co-unite:/ˌkoʊ juˈnaɪt/
verb
联合；合并
To unite or combine together; to join.
The two groups co-united to form a stronger organization.  两个团体联合起来组成了一个更强大的组织。
The different factions co-united to oppose the common enemy.  不同的派别联合起来反对共同的敌人。
Synonyms: combine, merge, join, unite, amalgamate
Antonyms: divide, separate, split, disunite
