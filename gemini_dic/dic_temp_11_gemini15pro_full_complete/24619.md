broke-off:/brəʊk ˈɒf/| adj.|中断的，破裂的|Ended abruptly; discontinued. |The negotiations broke off after several weeks.  谈判几周后中断了。|近义词：ended, discontinued, ceased, stopped, halted, aborted|反义词:continued, resumed, restarted|形容词
broke-off:/brəʊk ˈɒf/| v.|中断，折断，突然停止|To end abruptly; to discontinue. |They broke off their engagement. 他们解除了婚约。|近义词：ended, discontinued, ceased, stopped, halted, aborted|反义词:continued, resumed, restarted|动词

词源：From the phrasal verb "break off," which originates from the Old English "brecan" (to break).
