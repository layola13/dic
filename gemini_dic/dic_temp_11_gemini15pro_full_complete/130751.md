nonamendment:/ˌnɒnəˈmɛndmənt/ | n. | 非修正案；非修正条款 |  Something that is specifically not an amendment, often referring to a legislative action or provision that does not alter existing laws or regulations but clarifies, interprets, or implements them. | The judge ruled that the change was a nonamendment and therefore didn't require a full legislative vote. 法官裁定这项变更是非修正案，因此不需要进行全面的立法投票。

The committee clarified that the provision was a nonamendment designed to clarify the existing law, not change it. 委员会澄清，该条款是非修正案，旨在澄清现有法律，而非修改法律。| Near synonyms: clarification, interpretation, implementation | Antonyms: amendment, alteration, revision | noun
