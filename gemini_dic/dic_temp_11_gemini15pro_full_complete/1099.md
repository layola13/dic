accessariness:/əkˈsesərɪnɪs/
n.
附属品；次要性；从属性
The state or quality of being an accessory.  Being involved in a crime, but not as the main perpetrator.
His accessariness to the crime was proven by his presence at the scene. 他对犯罪的从属性由他在现场的事实得到证明。

The accessariness of the decorations did not detract from the beauty of the room. 装饰的次要性并没有减损房间的美感。
近义词: complicity, involvement, secondary, subordinate, nonessential
反义词: essentiality, primary, principal
词性: 名词

词源: From Late Latin *accessorius, from Latin accessus (“approach”).  The suffix -ness forms nouns meaning "the state or quality of being."
