debits:/ˈdebɪts/| n.|借方；借项；记入借方|An accounting entry that represents money flowing out of an account.  It increases asset, expense, and dividend accounts, and decreases liability, owner's equity, and revenue accounts. |例句：The debits and credits must balance. 借方和贷方必须平衡。

例句：She recorded the debits in the ledger.  她把借方记录在分类账上。|近义词：charges, expenses|反义词：credits|词性:noun


debit:/ˈdebɪt/| v.|借记；记入借方|To record a debit in an account.  It represents an outflow of funds or an increase in an asset, expense, or dividend account. |例句：Please debit my account for the purchase. 请从我的账户中扣除购买款项。

例句：The bank debited his account $50. 银行从他的账户中扣除了50美元。|近义词：charge|反义词：credit|词性:verb
