commercialists:/kəˈmɜːrʃəlɪsts/
|n.| 商业主义者；重商主义者；唯利是图的人|People who are primarily focused on profit and commercial success, often at the expense of ethical considerations or artistic integrity.|例句：The commercialists cared little about the environmental impact of their actions. 这些商人对他们行为对环境的影响漠不关心。

例句：The film was criticized by commercialists for not appealing to a wider audience. 这部电影因未能吸引更广泛的观众而受到商业主义者的批评。|近义词：businesspeople, profiteers, materialists|反义词：philanthropists, altruists|词性: noun
