iguanian:/ɪˈɡwɑːniən/| adj. n.|鬣蜥类的，鬣蜥亚目的；鬣蜥亚目动物|Relating to or denoting a suborder (Iguania) of lizards that includes iguanas, agamas, chameleons, etc., found mainly in warm regions.|The green iguana is a typical iguanian. 绿鬣蜥是典型的鬣蜥亚目动物。|The researchers studied the evolution of iguanian lizards. 研究人员研究了鬣蜥亚目蜥蜴的进化。|近义词:lizard-like|反义词:|adjective, noun
