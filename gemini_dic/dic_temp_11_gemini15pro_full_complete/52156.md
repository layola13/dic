diaphonic:/ˌdaɪəˈfɒnɪk/ /ˌdaɪəˈfɑːnɪk/| adj.|通音的，透声的|Relating to the transmission of sound through or by means of a material, especially a membrane.|The diaphonic properties of the material made it ideal for speaker cones. 这种材料的透声特性使它成为扬声器锥体的理想选择。|近义词:transmissive, resonant|反义词:opaque, soundproof|词性:形容词

diaphonic:/ˌdaɪəˈfɒnɪk/ /ˌdaɪəˈfɑːnɪk/| n.|透声体|A material or substance that transmits sound.|The researchers experimented with various diaphonics to find the best one for their device. 研究人员试验了各种透声体，以找到最适合他们设备的材料。|近义词:transmitter, resonator|反义词:insulator, sound barrier|词性:名词
