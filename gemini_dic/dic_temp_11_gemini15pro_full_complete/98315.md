insignia:/ɪnˈsɪɡniə/ | n. |徽章，标志，象征|A badge or distinguishing mark of military rank, office, or membership of an organization. |The president wore the insignia of his office. 总统佩戴着他职位的徽章。|  Example: The soldiers' uniforms bore the insignia of their regiment. 士兵的制服上带有他们所属团的徽章。 |Synonyms: emblem, symbol, badge, crest, logo|Antonyms: anonymity, obscurity|noun

insignia:/ɪnˈsɪɡniə/ | n. |(复数)标志，象征| Distinguishing marks or signs of anything. |These victories are the insignia of his skill. 这些胜利是他技能的标志。| Example: The company's logo is a prominent insignia on all of their products.  公司的标志在他们所有产品上都很显眼。|Synonyms: emblem, symbol, badge, crest, logo|Antonyms: anonymity, obscurity|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *insignia, plural of Latin *insigne "distinguishing mark," from neuter of insignis "remarkable, distinguished," from in- "in, on" + signum "mark, sign."
