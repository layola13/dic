neuropteris:/ˌnʊərɒptərɪs/
n.
神经蕨
An extinct genus of seed ferns that flourished in the Carboniferous period.
Neuropteris fossils are common in coal deposits. 神经蕨化石在煤矿床中很常见。
Neuropteris leaves are characterized by their distinctive venation pattern.  神经蕨叶子的特点是其独特的脉络图案。
Synonym: seed fern (general term)
Antonym: (No direct antonym, as it's an extinct genus. Could consider modern ferns as a distant contrast)
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek "neuron" (nerve) + "pteris" (fern), referring to the nerve-like venation pattern of the leaves.
