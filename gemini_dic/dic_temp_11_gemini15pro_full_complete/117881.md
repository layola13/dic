melodramatist:/ˈmɛlədrɑːmətɪst/| n. |情节剧作家；夸张的人|A writer of melodramas; a person who behaves in an exaggerated or overly dramatic way.|例句：The melodramatist filled his play with improbable plot twists and heightened emotions.  这位情节剧作家在他的戏剧中加入了各种不太可能的情节转折和夸张的情感。

例句：He's such a melodramatist, always making a big fuss over nothing. 他真是个爱小题大做的人，总是无事生非。|近义词：dramatist, playwright, overactor |反义词: realist, understated person |词性: noun
