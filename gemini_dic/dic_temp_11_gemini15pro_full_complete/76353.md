gadding:/ˈɡædɪŋ/ | n. |  闲逛，游荡 | The act of going from place to place in search of pleasure or amusement. | She spent her vacation gadding about Europe. 她假期都在欧洲四处游荡。| 近义词: roaming, wandering, gallivanting | 反义词: staying, remaining, settling | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *gadden*, from Old Norse *gaddr* (wandering, roving), from Proto-Germanic *gadją* (to go, wander).  Cognate with Old English *gad* (goad, spike), suggesting a sense of restless movement.
