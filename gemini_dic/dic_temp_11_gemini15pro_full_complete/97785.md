injudiciousness:/ˌɪndʒʊˈdɪʃəsnəs/
|n.|不明智；缺乏判断力|The state of being injudicious; lack of judgment or discretion.|His injudiciousness led to a costly mistake. 他的不明智导致了一个代价高昂的错误。|近义词：folly, imprudence, recklessness, indiscretion|反义词：wisdom, prudence, judiciousness, discretion|词性: 名词

injudicious:/ˌɪndʒʊˈdɪʃəs/
|adj.|不明智的；缺乏判断力的|Not showing good judgment; unwise.|It was an injudicious remark that offended many people. 这是一句不明智的话，冒犯了很多人。|近义词：unwise, imprudent, foolish, ill-advised|反义词：wise, prudent, judicious, sensible|词性: 形容词

词源:
Late 16th century: from in- ‘not’ + judicious + -ness.
