overemploys:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈplɔɪz/
verb
过度雇佣，人员过剩
employs too many people
The company overemploys staff and has to make redundancies every year.  这家公司人员过剩，每年都不得不裁员。
overstaff, overman
underemploy
v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + employ.  "Over-" signifies "excessively" or "too much". "Employ" means to give work to (someone) and pay them for it.
