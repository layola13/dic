euthanize:/ˈjuːθənaɪz/ | vt. | 安乐死，对…实施安乐死 | To kill (a person or animal) painlessly, especially to relieve suffering from an incurable illness. | The vet euthanized the injured dog. 兽医对受伤的狗实施了安乐死。 |  synonyms: put down, put to sleep | antonyms: resuscitate, revive | verb


euthanize:/ˈjuːθənaɪz/ | vt. |  使安然去世，使平静地死去| To end the life of someone suffering from a terminal illness or irreversible coma. | The family made the difficult decision to euthanize their terminally ill grandmother.  家人做出了艰难的决定，让身患绝症的祖母安乐死。 | synonyms: put out of one's misery | antonyms:  revive, bring back to life | verb
