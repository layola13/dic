dustlike:/ˈdʌstlaɪk/| adj.|像灰尘的，粉末状的|Resembling dust in fineness or consistency.|The desert floor was covered in a dustlike substance.|近义词：powdery, dusty, fine|反义词: coarse, granular|词性:形容词
Etymology: dust + like.  "Dust" comes from Old English *dust, from Proto-Germanic *dunstaz. "Like" comes from Old English *lic, from Proto-Germanic *lika-.
