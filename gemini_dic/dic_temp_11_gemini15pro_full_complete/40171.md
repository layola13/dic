contentedly:/kənˈtɛntɪdli/| adv.|满意地，心满意足地|in a contented manner; showing satisfaction|She smiled contentedly as she finished her book. 她看完书，心满意足地笑了。|近义词：happily, peacefully, cheerfully|反义词：discontentedly, unhappily, sadly|词性:副词

词源:
From content + -ed + -ly.  "Content" ultimately derives from Latin "contentus," the past participle of "continere" (to contain, hold together).  The suffix "-ed" forms the past participle (and sometimes the adjective form) of verbs.  The suffix "-ly" transforms adjectives into adverbs.
