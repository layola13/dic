gnathostegite:/ˈnæθəˌstaɪdʒaɪt/ | n. |颚足片，颚足外肢|A flattened, expanded, outer branch of a crustacean thoracic limb, especially one of the maxillipeds, often forming a platelike covering over the gills.|The gnathotegites of the crayfish protect its delicate gills.|Synonym: branchiostegal plate|Antonym: N/A|noun
