gamelans:/ˈɡæməlænz/ /ˈɡeɪmlɑːnz/
n.
甘美兰
A traditional Indonesian orchestra, typically including many bronze percussion instruments.
The sounds of the gamelans drifted through the open windows. 甘美兰的声音从敞开的窗户飘进来。

近义词:orchestra, ensemble
反义词:(无直接反义词，可以考虑soloist或individual musician)
词性:名词
