animaters:/ˈænɪˌmeɪtərz/ | n. | 动画师，动画制作者 | People who create moving images, especially in cartoons or computer graphics.  They bring characters and stories to life through animation. | The animaters worked tirelessly to finish the film on schedule. 动画师们不知疲倦地工作，以按时完成这部电影。 |  The studio hired several new animaters for the upcoming project. 工作室为即将到来的项目雇佣了几位新的动画师。 | 近义词: cartoonists, artists | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "inanimators" as a humorous opposite) | 词性: noun
