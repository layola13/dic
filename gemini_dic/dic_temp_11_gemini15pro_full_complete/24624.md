brokenhearted:/ˈbroʊkənˌhɑːrtɪd/| adj.|心碎的，伤心的|Overwhelmed by grief or disappointment, especially after the loss of love or a romantic relationship.|She was brokenhearted when her fiancé left her. 她未婚夫离开她时，她心碎了。|Synonyms: heartbroken, devastated, grief-stricken, inconsolable|Antonyms: joyful, happy, elated|adjective
