musk: /mʌsk/ | n. | 麝香; 麝香味; 浓烈的香味 | A strong-smelling reddish-brown substance secreted by a gland in the abdomen of the male musk deer, used in perfumery.  Also, any similar-smelling substance from other animals or plants. | The perfume had a strong musk scent.  这香水有股浓烈的麝香味。 | synonyms: scent, aroma, fragrance, perfume | antonyms: odor, stench | noun

musk: /mʌsk/ | n. | 麝香鹿 | The small deer that secretes musk. | Musk deer are endangered due to poaching. 麝香鹿因偷猎而濒临灭绝。 | synonyms: musk deer | antonyms: N/A | noun

musky: /ˈmʌski/ | adj. | 麝香的; 有麝香味的 | Having the scent of musk. | The room was filled with a musky aroma. 房间里充满了麝香味。 | synonyms: fragrant, aromatic, scented | antonyms: odorless, unscented | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *musc*, from Late Latin *muscus*, from Greek *μόσχος (moskhos)*, from Persian *مشک (mušk)*, ultimately from Sanskrit *मुष्क (muṣka)* meaning "testicle," referring to the shape of the gland.
