capitula:/kəˈpɪtʃʊlə/| n. |小花头，小头状花序|A small head, or capitulum, especially of a composite flower.  The capitula are arranged in corymbs.|The dandelion has a single capitulum, while sunflowers have many capitula forming a large head.|Near synonyms: head, capitulum, floret|Antonym: N/A|noun

capitula:/kəˈpɪtʃʊlə/| n. |(章，回)(capitulum 的复数)|Plural form of capitulum, referring to chapters or sections of a book or document, particularly in anatomical or botanical contexts. |The capitula of the textbook are organized thematically.|Near synonyms: chapters, sections, divisions|Antonym: N/A|noun
