nutritious:/njuːˈtrɪʃəs/| adj.|有营养的；营养丰富的|Providing the substances needed for growth and health.|例句：A nutritious diet is essential for good health.  营养丰富的饮食对健康至关重要。

例句：Leafy green vegetables are highly nutritious.  绿叶蔬菜营养价值很高。|近义词：nourishing, healthy, wholesome|反义词：unhealthy, unwholesome|词性:adjective
Etymology:  From Latin *nutritius, from nutritus, past participle of nutrire (“to nourish”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *sn̥-treh₂- (“to give suck, nourish”).
