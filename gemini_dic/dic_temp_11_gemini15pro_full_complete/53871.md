discontinuity:/ˌdɪskɒntɪˈnjuːɪti/ | n. |不连续性，中断，间断 |A break or interruption in the continuity of something.|The geological record shows several discontinuities, indicating periods of missing time. 地质记录显示了几个不连续性，表明存在缺失的时间段。|近义词：break, interruption, gap, hiatus|反义词：continuity, continuation|词性: 名词

discontinuities:/ˌdɪskɒntɪˈnjuːɪtiz/ | n. |不连续性，中断，间断（复数）|The plural form of discontinuity, referring to multiple breaks or interruptions.|The function has several discontinuities. 这个函数有几个不连续点。|近义词：breaks, interruptions, gaps, hiatuses|反义词：continuities, continuations|词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin discontinuitas, from discontinuus (“not continuous”), from dis- (“not”) + continuus (“continuous”).  The English word "discontinuity" first appeared in the late 16th century.
