collaborator:/kəˈlæbəreɪtər/| n. |合作者，协作者|A person who works jointly on an activity or project; an associate.|例句：She found a collaborator on the research project. 她为研究项目找到了一个合作者。|例句：He was a known collaborator with the enemy during the war.  他是战时与敌人合作的知名人士。|近义词：partner, associate, colleague, ally|反义词：opponent, adversary, enemy|词性:noun

Etymology->
From Late Latin *collaborator, from collaboratus, past participle of collaborare "to work together," from com- "together" + laborare "to work."
