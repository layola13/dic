giga:/ˈɡɪɡə/| adj. prefix| 十亿，千兆 |A prefix denoting a factor of one billion (10^9) in the International System of Units (SI).  It is used to form the names of units that are a billion times larger than the base unit. |A gigabyte is one billion bytes. 一千兆字节等于十亿字节。|近义词: billion |反义词: nano, micro, milli |词性: prefix


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *gigas* "giant".  Borrowed into the International System of Units in 1960.  Related to gigantic.  源自希腊语 *gigas* “巨人”。1960年引入国际单位制。 与 gigantic 相关。
