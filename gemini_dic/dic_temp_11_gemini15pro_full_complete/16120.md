bang-up:/ˈbæŋˌʌp/| adj.|一流的，极好的，非常棒的|Excellent; first-rate.  Originating from the notion of a "bang-up" job being done with great enthusiasm and effectiveness, possibly related to the forceful impact implied by "bang".|We had a bang-up time at the party. 我们在派对上玩得很开心。|Synonyms: excellent, superb, fantastic, wonderful, terrific, outstanding, marvelous, magnificent, brilliant, splendid|Antonyms: awful, terrible, dreadful, lousy, poor|adjective

bang-up:/ˈbæŋˌʌp/| n.|盛宴，丰盛的宴席|A lavish meal or feast.|They put on a real bang-up for his birthday.  他们为他举办了一个真正的盛宴。|Synonyms: feast, banquet, spread|Antonyms: snack, light meal|noun
