microbrewery:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌbruːəri/ /ˈmaɪkrəʊˌbruːəri/| n.|小型酿酒厂，精酿啤酒厂|A brewery that produces a limited amount of beer.  A small brewery, often independently owned, that produces specialty beers.|We visited a local microbrewery and sampled their latest IPA. 我们参观了一家当地的小型酿酒厂，品尝了他们最新的印度淡色艾尔啤酒。|Synonyms: craft brewery, brewpub|Antonyms: macrobrewery, large brewery|noun
