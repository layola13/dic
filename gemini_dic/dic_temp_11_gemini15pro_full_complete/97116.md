infields:/ˈɪnˌfiːldz/
n.
内场，内野
The infield is the area of a baseball field enclosed by the four bases.
内场是由四个垒围起来的棒球场区域。
The groundskeeper meticulously manicured the infield grass.
场地管理员精心修剪了内场的草坪。
近义词:infield dirt
反义词:outfield

词性:名词
