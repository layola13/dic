multitudinously:/ˌmʌltɪˈtjuːdɪnəsli/
| adv. | 众多地，大量地 | In a multitudinous manner; in great numbers. | The stars shone multitudinously in the clear night sky.  星星在晴朗的夜空中闪耀着。| 近义词: abundantly, copiously, profusely, numerously | 反义词: sparsely, scarcely, meagerly | 词性: 副词
multitude:/ˈmʌltɪtjuːd/
| n. | 大量，众多；群众 | A large number; a great quantity. | A multitude of people gathered in the square. 广场上聚集了很多人。| 近义词: crowd, throng, host, swarm | 反义词: few, handful | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Latin *multitudinōsus, from Latin multitūdō (“multitude”).
