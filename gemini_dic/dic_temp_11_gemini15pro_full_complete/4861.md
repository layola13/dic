alienating: /ˈeɪliəneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 疏远的，使人疏远的，离间的 | Causing someone to feel isolated or estranged. |  His cold behavior was alienating his friends. 他的冷漠行为使他的朋友们疏远了他。

The constant criticism was alienating her from her colleagues.  不断的批评使她与同事们疏远了。| 近义词：isolating, estranging, distancing | 反义词：uniting, connecting, endearing | 词性: 形容词

词源：From alienate + -ing.  Alienate comes from Late Latin alienatus, past participle of alienare "to estrange," from Latin alienus "of or belonging to another, foreign," from alius "other."
