constituency:/kənˈstɪtʃuənsi/| n.|选区；支持者；顾客群|A body of voters who elect a representative to a legislative body.|例句：The MP worked hard to address the concerns of his constituency.  这位议员努力解决了他选区民众的担忧。

例句：She has a large constituency of loyal fans. 她有一大批忠实的粉丝。|近义词：electorate, district, supporters, clientele|反义词: (No direct antonym)|Noun
