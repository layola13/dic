durio:/ˈdʊrioʊ/ | n. | 榴莲 | A large, oval, spiny fruit containing a creamy, pungent pulp. |  The durian is known as the "king of fruits" in Southeast Asia.  榴莲在东南亚被称为“水果之王”。

The smell of durian is quite overpowering. 榴莲的味道很浓烈。 | 近义词:  (no direct synonym, but could be grouped with other tropical fruits like jackfruit, mangosteen) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
