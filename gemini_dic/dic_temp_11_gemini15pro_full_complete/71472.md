flamineous:/fləˈmɪniəs/
adjective
火焰般的，火红的
Resembling flames; fiery; flaming.  Characterized by or consisting of flames.
The flamineous sunset painted the sky in vibrant hues of orange and red.  火焰般的日落将天空染成了鲜艳的橙色和红色。
synonyms: fiery, flaming, incandescent, ablaze
antonyms: dull, dark, extinguished
词性: 形容词
