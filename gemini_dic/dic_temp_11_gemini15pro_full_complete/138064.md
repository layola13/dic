normalisation:/ˌnɔːməlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ (UK) /ˌnɔːrmələˈzeɪʃən/ (US)| n.|标准化，正常化|The process of bringing or returning something to a normal condition or state.  The act of making something standard.  |例句：Data normalisation is crucial for efficient database management. 数据标准化对于有效的数据库管理至关重要。

|例句：The normalisation of relations between the two countries took years. 两国关系正常化花了数年时间。|近义词：standardization, regularization|反义词：deviation, abnormality|词性:noun
