overliterary:/ˌoʊvərˈlɪtərəri/ | adj. | 过于文学化的，咬文嚼字的 | Excessively literary; affecting a literary style beyond what is appropriate or natural.  Characterized by an excessive use of literary devices or allusions. | Her prose was dense and overliterary, making it difficult to follow. 她的散文艰涩难懂，过于咬文嚼字，让人难以理解。 |例句：The dialogue felt stiff and overliterary, not like how people actually speak.  对话感觉僵硬且过于文学化，不像人们实际的说话方式。 | 近义词：pretentious, affected, stilted, ornate | 反义词：natural, simple, straightforward, colloquial | 词性: 形容词
