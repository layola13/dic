bedmaker: /ˈbedmeɪkə(r)/ | n. | 寝室服务员，整理床铺的人 | A person employed to clean and tidy bedrooms, especially in a college or university. |  The bedmaker had already been and the room was tidy. 寝室服务员已经来过了，房间很整洁。|  The bedmaker comes in every morning except Sunday.  除了周日，寝室服务员每天早上都会来。|Synonyms: housekeeper, cleaner, maid | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "mess-maker") | Noun
