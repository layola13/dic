oily:/ˈɔɪli/ | adj. | 油腻的；含油的；油滑的 | Covered or saturated with oil; greasy. Also, resembling oil in appearance, texture, or manner. | The mechanic's hands were oily after working on the engine. 技工修完发动机后，手上油腻腻的。
