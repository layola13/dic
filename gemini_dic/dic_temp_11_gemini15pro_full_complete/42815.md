courageousness:/kəˈreɪdʒəsnəs/| n.|勇敢，勇气|The quality of being courageous; bravery.  The state or quality of mind or spirit that enables one to face danger, fear, or vicissitudes with self-possession, confidence, and resolution; bravery.|He showed great courageousness in the face of danger. 他在危险面前表现出极大的勇气。|Synonyms: bravery, valor, boldness, fearlessness, intrepidity, gallantry, heroism, fortitude, pluck, grit, mettle, nerve, backbone, spirit, audacity|Antonyms: cowardice, timidity, fearfulness, pusillanimity|noun
