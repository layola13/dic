delineation:/dɪˌlɪnɪˈeɪʃən/
n.|描绘，勾勒，轮廓|The artist's delineation of the human form was masterful.  这位艺术家对人体的描绘精湛无比。|例句：The report provides a clear delineation of the company's financial position.  该报告清晰地描绘了公司的财务状况。|近义词：description, depiction, portrayal, sketch, outline|反义词：obscuring, blurring|词性：名词

delineate:/dɪˈlɪnɪeɪt/
vt.|描绘，勾勒，阐明|He carefully delineated the plan to his colleagues. 他仔细地向同事们阐述了这个计划。|例句：The lawyer delineated the terms of the contract. 律师阐明了合同条款。|近义词：describe, depict, portray, outline, explain|反义词：obfuscate, confuse|词性：动词

delineature:/dɪˈlɪnɪətʃər/
n.|描绘，勾勒，轮廓|The delineature of the mountains against the sky was breathtaking. 山脉在天空中勾勒出的轮廓令人叹为观止。|例句：The architect's delineature of the building was precise and detailed.  建筑师对建筑物的描绘精确而详细。|近义词：delineation, depiction, portrayal, sketch, outline|反义词：obscuring, blurring|词性：名词


词源：From Latin *dēlineātus*, past participle of *dēlineāre* (“to sketch, outline”), from *dē-* (“from, down”) + *lineāre* (“to draw lines”), from *līnea* (“line”).
