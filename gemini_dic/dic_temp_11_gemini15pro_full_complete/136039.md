nonreasoner:/ˌnɒnˈriːzənər/ | /ˌnɑːnˈriːzənər/ | n. | 非理性者；不讲道理的人 | A person who does not or cannot reason; someone incapable of rational thought or logical argument. |  He dismissed his critics as mere nonreasoners, unwilling to engage in intellectual debate. 他把批评他的人斥为纯粹的不讲道理的人，不愿意参与智力辩论。| Near synonyms: irrational person, illogical person, unreasoning person | Antonyms: rational person, logician, thinker | Noun
