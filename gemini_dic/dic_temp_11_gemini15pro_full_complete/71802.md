flee:/fliː/
verb
逃跑，逃走
To run away from a place or situation of danger.
The family fled the burning building. 这家人逃离了燃烧的建筑物。
He was forced to flee the country. 他被迫逃离了这个国家。
synonyms: escape, run away, abscond
antonyms: face, confront, remain
v.

flee:/fliː/
noun
逃跑，逃离
The act of running away from danger.  (Less common as a noun)
Their flee from the city was hasty. 他们逃离这座城市很仓促。
synonyms: escape, flight
antonyms: arrival, return
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *flēon, from Proto-Germanic *fleuhaną, from Proto-Indo-European *pleuk-, meaning "to flow, flee."  Cognate with Dutch vlieden, German fliehen.
