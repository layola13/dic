flyblown:/ˈflaɪˌbloʊn/| adj.| 苍蝇产卵的；腐败的；污秽的|Infested with fly eggs or maggots; tainted or spoiled; sordid or corrupt.|The meat was flyblown and had to be thrown away. 肉上爬满了蛆，不得不扔掉。|近义词：tainted, spoiled, putrid, contaminated, rotten, decayed, maggoty|反义词：fresh, clean, pure|词性:形容词

词源:
From fly + blown, past participle of blow (in the sense "deposit eggs").  The original meaning referred to the eggs of flies being deposited on something.
