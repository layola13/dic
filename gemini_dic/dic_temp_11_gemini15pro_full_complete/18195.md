belatednesses:/bɪˈleɪtɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
迟来；延误；姗姗来迟
the state or quality of being belated
例句：The belatedness of the apology did little to soothe her anger.  迟来的道歉并没能平息她的怒火。

例句：He apologized for the belatedness of his response. 他为回复迟来而道歉。

近义词：lateness, tardiness, delay
反义词：promptness, timeliness
词性: 名词
