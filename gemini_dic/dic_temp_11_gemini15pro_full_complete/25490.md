bullfinches:/ˈbʊlfɪntʃɪz/
n.
红腹灰雀（复数）
A finch(雀科鸣鸟) with a stout bill(粗壮的喙) and, in the male, a reddish-pink breast(胸脯). Bullfinches are known for their ability to eat buds(芽) and seeds.
The bullfinches were feasting on the plum buds.  红腹灰雀正在享用梅花芽。
近义词:birds, finches
反义词:(无直接反义词，可考虑 predators 捕食者)
词性:noun

bullfinch:/ˈbʊlfɪntʃ/
n.
红腹灰雀（单数）
A finch with a stout bill and, in the male, a reddish-pink breast.
A bullfinch perched on the branch. 一只红腹灰雀栖息在树枝上。
近义词:bird, finch
反义词:(无直接反义词，可考虑 predator 捕食者)
词性:noun


词源:
From "bull" + "finch".  "Bull" likely refers to the bird's thick, bull-like neck and head, or possibly to its reddish color.  "Finch" denotes its membership in the finch family.
