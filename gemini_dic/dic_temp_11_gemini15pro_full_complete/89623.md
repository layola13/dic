hoofless:/ˈhuːflɪs/| adj.|无蹄的，没有蹄的|Lacking hooves; without hooves.  Relating to an animal that does not have hooves.|The hoofless animal left strange tracks in the mud. 这只无蹄的动物在泥里留下了奇怪的足迹。|Near synonyms:  unshod, claw-footed|Antonyms: hoofed|adjective
