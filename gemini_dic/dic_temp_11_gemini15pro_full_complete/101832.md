irrigate:/ˈɪrɪɡeɪt/ |v. |灌溉; 冲洗(伤口)|To supply water to (land or crops) in order to help growth, typically by means of channels.|The farmers irrigate their crops regularly to ensure a good harvest.  农民定期灌溉庄稼以确保丰收。| irrigate the wound with saline solution 用生理盐水冲洗伤口|synonyms: water, moisten, drench |antonyms: dehydrate, desiccate |verb
