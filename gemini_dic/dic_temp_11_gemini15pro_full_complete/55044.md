disquiets:/dɪsˈkwaɪəts/ | n. vt. |不安，焦虑；使不安，使焦虑|A feeling of anxiety or worry.|The news of the layoffs disquieted the employees. 裁员的消息让员工们感到不安。|Synonyms: worries, anxieties, concerns, troubles|Antonyms: calms, reassures, comforts|noun

disquiets:/dɪsˈkwaɪəts/ | vt. |使不安，使焦虑|To make someone feel anxious or worried.|The constant noise disquieted her. 持续不断的噪音让她感到不安。|Synonyms:  worries, disturbs, bothers, upsets|Antonyms: calms, soothes, reassures|verb
