ferricyanide:/ˌfɛriˈsaɪəˌnaɪd/ | n. | 铁氰化物 |A salt containing the anion [Fe(CN)6]3−.  It is commonly used as an oxidizing agent in organic chemistry and as a reagent in analytical chemistry.| Potassium ferricyanide was used to stain the tissue samples. 铁氰化钾被用来染色组织样本。| Synonyms: hexacyanoferrate(III), red prussiate of potash (for potassium ferricyanide)| Antonyms: ferrocyanide | noun

ferricyanate:/ˌfɛriˈsaɪəˌneɪt/ | n. | 铁氰酸盐; 铁氰酸根 | A salt or ester of ferricyanic acid. Often used interchangeably with ferricyanide. | The ferricyanate solution turned blue upon addition of ferrous ions.  加入亚铁离子后，铁氰酸盐溶液变成蓝色。| Synonyms: hexacyanoferrate(III) | Antonyms: ferrocyanate | noun
