asystole: /əˈsɪstəli/ (US), /əˈsɪstəli/ (UK)| n. |心搏停止；心脏停搏|The complete absence of electrical activity in the heart, resulting in cessation of effective circulation.|The patient went into asystole and could not be revived. 病人出现心搏停止，无法复苏。|Synonyms: cardiac arrest, cardiac standstill, heart stoppage|Antonyms: normal sinus rhythm|noun
