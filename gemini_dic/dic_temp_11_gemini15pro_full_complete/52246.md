dibble:/ˈdɪbəl/ | n. v. | 穴播器，移植铲; 用移植铲栽植 | A small hand tool used for making holes in the ground for planting seeds or seedlings.  To plant by making small holes in the ground with a dibble. | She used a dibble to plant the lettuce seedlings. 她用穴播器种莴苣苗。 | He dibbled the seeds into the prepared soil. 他用移植铲把种子种到准备好的土壤里。 | synonyms: dibber, planting tool, trowel | antonyms: (for the verb) unearth, uproot | noun, verb
