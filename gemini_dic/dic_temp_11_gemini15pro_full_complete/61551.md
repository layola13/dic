emcee:/ɛmˈsiː/| v. n.|担任司仪；司仪|To act as master of ceremonies at (an event).|例句：She emceed the awards ceremony with grace and humor. 她以优雅和幽默主持了颁奖典礼。

例句：He was asked to emcee the wedding reception. 他被邀请主持婚礼招待会。|近义词：host, presenter, moderator|反义词:audience, guest|词性:verb, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From M.C., abbreviation of Master of Ceremonies.
