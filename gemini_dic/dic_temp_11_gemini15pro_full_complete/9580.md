antisocial:/ˌæntiˈsoʊʃl/ | adj. | 反社会的；不擅社交的；不合群的 | Not sociable; not wanting the company of others.  | He was a lonely, antisocial boy. 他是一个孤独、不合群的男孩. | Synonyms: unsociable, withdrawn, reclusive, misanthropic | Antonyms: sociable, gregarious, outgoing | Word type: adjective

antisocial:/ˌæntiˈsəʊʃl/ | adj. | 反社会的；不擅社交的；不合群的 |  Harmful or disruptive to society. |  His antisocial behaviour is a menace to society. 他的反社会行为对社会构成威胁. | Synonyms: disruptive, hostile, destructive, criminal | Antonyms: prosocial, cooperative, constructive | Word type: adjective

Etymology:  From anti- "against" + social.
