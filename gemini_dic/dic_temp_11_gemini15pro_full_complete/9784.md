antrustion:/ænˈtrʌʃən/
n.
心腹；亲信；密友
A person entrusted with a position of authority or trust, especially a member of a ruler's bodyguard or personal following.
The king surrounded himself with an elite group of antrustions, each sworn to protect him with their lives. 国王身边围绕着一群精英心腹，每个人都发誓用生命保护他。

The antrustion proved his loyalty time and again, risking his life for the king.  这位心腹一次又一次地证明了他的忠诚，为国王冒着生命危险。

近义词: confidant, intimate, crony, associate
反义词: enemy, foe, adversary
词性: noun
