aldebaran:/ælˈdɛbərən/ | n. |毕宿五；金牛座α星|A bright, reddish star that is the brightest star in the constellation Taurus (the Bull).|例句：Aldebaran is easily visible in the night sky. 毕宿五在夜空中很容易看到。

例句：Ancient cultures often associated Aldebaran with royalty or divinity. 古代文化经常将毕宿五与皇室或神性联系起来。|近义词：Alpha Tauri (its Bayer designation), the Eye of the Bull|反义词:None (It's a specific star, not a concept with an opposite.)|词性:noun
