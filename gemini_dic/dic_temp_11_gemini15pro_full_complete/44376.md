cross-current:/ˈkrɒsˌkʌrənt/ (US) /ˈkrɒsˌkʌrənt/ (UK)| n.|交叉水流；反向潮流；逆流|A current flowing across another current, often at or near the surface.  A conflicting tendency or influence.|The ship had to contend with strong cross-currents. 这艘船不得不与强大的交叉水流作斗争。|Synonyms: counter-current, undercurrent, backwash|Antonyms: main current, mainstream|noun


cross-current:/ˈkrɒsˌkʌrənt/ (US) /ˈkrɒsˌkʌrənt/ (UK)| adj.|交叉的；反向的；逆流的|Running counter to the main current or trend.|Cross-current winds made sailing difficult. 交叉风使得航行困难。|Synonyms: conflicting, opposing, contrary|Antonyms: supporting, agreeing, conforming|adjective
