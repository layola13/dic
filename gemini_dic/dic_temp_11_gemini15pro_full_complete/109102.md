legerdemainist:/ˌlɛʒərdəˈmeɪnɪst/
n.
魔术师；手法敏捷的人
A person who performs legerdemain; a magician or sleight-of-hand artist.
The legerdemainist amazed the audience with his card tricks.  这位魔术师用他的纸牌戏法使观众惊叹。
Synonyms: magician, conjurer, illusionist, prestidigitator, trickster
Antonyms: -
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *leger de main* "light of hand," from *leger* "light" + *de* "of" + *main* "hand."
