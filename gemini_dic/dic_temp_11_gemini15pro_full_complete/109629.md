letters:/ˈletərz/ |n. |字母；信件| individual characters of the alphabet; written or printed communication | Example:  The word "cat" has three letters.  单词“cat”有三个字母。

Example: She received a bundle of letters from her admirers. 她收到了一捆来自仰慕者的信件。 | Synonyms: characters, alphabet, mail, correspondence | Antonyms: (For letters as alphabet) None. (For letters as mail)  silence | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *lettre*, from Latin *littera* (“letter of the alphabet”), originally *lintera* (“letter, character, literature”).  The Latin word is possibly of Etruscan origin.
