millimeter:/ˈmɪlɪmiːtər/| n.|毫米|A unit of length equal to one thousandth of a metre.|The screw is just a few millimeters long. 这颗螺丝只有几毫米长。|synonyms: mm|antonyms: kilometers, meters|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *millimètre*, from *milli-* (thousandth) + *mètre* (meter). Ultimately from Latin *mille* (thousand) and Greek *métron* (measure).
