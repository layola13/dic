defibrination:/diːˌfaɪbrɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. |去纤维蛋白作用；去纤维蛋白 |The removal of fibrin from the blood or from blood products. This can occur naturally in some medical conditions or be induced medically. |Disseminated intravascular coagulation can lead to defibrination.  弥散性血管内凝血会导致去纤维蛋白作用。

The doctor induced defibrination to prevent blood clots. 医生诱导去纤维蛋白作用以预防血凝块。 | 近义词: fibrinolysis | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider "coagulation" as an opposing process) | 词性: noun
