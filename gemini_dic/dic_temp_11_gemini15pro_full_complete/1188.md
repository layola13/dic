acclimatizes:/əˈklaɪməˌtaɪzɪz/
verb
适应(气候、环境)
to become used to a new climate or environment
The climbers needed a few days to acclimatize to the altitude.  登山者需要几天时间来适应海拔高度。
He acclimatized quickly to the hot weather. 他很快适应了炎热的天气。
accustom, adapt, adjust
unaccustom, disadapt
词性: 动词(verb)
