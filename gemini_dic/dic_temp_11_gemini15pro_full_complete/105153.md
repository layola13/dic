ketonic:/kiːˈtɒnɪk/ /kiˈtɑːnɪk/
adjective|酮的，含酮的|Relating to or containing a ketone, an organic compound containing a carbonyl group bonded to two carbon atoms.|The ketonic diet is a high-fat, moderate-protein, very-low-carbohydrate diet. 生酮饮食是一种高脂肪、适量蛋白质、极低碳水化合物的饮食。|近义词:keto, carbonyl|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, consider non-ketonic or carbohydrate-based)|词性:adjective
