aerolitic:/ˌɛəroʊˈlɪtɪk/
adjective
陨石的；由陨石组成的
Relating to or derived from an aerolite (a stony meteorite).
This aerolitic fragment was found in the desert. 这块陨石碎片是在沙漠中发现的。
The museum houses a large collection of aerolitic specimens.  这家博物馆收藏了大量的陨石标本。
近义词:meteoric, meteoritical
反义词:terrestrial
词性:adjective
