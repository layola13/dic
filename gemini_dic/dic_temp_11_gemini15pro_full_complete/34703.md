cleverer: /ˈklɛvərər/ (UK), /ˈklɛvərər/ (US)| adj.| 更聪明的 |Comparative form of "clever," meaning more intelligent or skillful.  It indicates a higher degree of cleverness than someone or something else.|She is cleverer than her brother at math. 她比她哥哥更擅长数学。| My new phone is cleverer than my old one; it can do so much more. 我的新手机比旧手机更智能；它可以做更多的事情。 | synonyms: brighter, smarter, more intelligent, sharper, more ingenious, more astute | antonyms: dumber, more stupid, less intelligent, duller |  comparative adjective
