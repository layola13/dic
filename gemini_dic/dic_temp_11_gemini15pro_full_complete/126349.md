myriadly:/ˈmɪriədli/| adv.|无数地，多种多样地|In a myriad of ways; in countless numbers.  Describes something occurring in a vast multitude of ways or instances.|例句：The stars shone myriadly in the night sky.  星星在夜空中闪耀着无数的光芒。

例句：The artist explored the theme myriadly through different mediums. 这位艺术家通过不同的媒介，以多种多样的方式探索了这个主题。|近义词：abundantly, profusely, copiously, innumerably|反义词: sparsely, scarcely|词性:副词

Etymology: From "myriad" + "-ly."  "Myriad" comes from the Greek "myrias," meaning "ten thousand."  Over time, it came to mean an indefinitely large number.
