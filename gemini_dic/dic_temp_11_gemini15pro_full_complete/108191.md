latticing:/ˈlætɪsɪŋ/| n.|格子，格栅，格子结构|A structure of crossed strips usually of wood or metal.|The latticing on the porch provided shade and privacy. 门廊上的格子提供了阴凉和隐私。|近义词：grid, trellis, latticework|反义词: solid, unbroken|noun

latticing:/ˈlætɪsɪŋ/| v.|构成格子，装格子|To furnish or construct with a lattice.|They are latticing the windows to keep the birds out. 他们在窗户上装格子以防止鸟儿飞进来。|近义词：crisscross, grid|反义词: uncover, open|verb
