junkie:/ˈdʒʌŋki/| n.|瘾君子；对…入迷的人|A person who is addicted to something, especially drugs.  A person with a compulsive interest in or enthusiasm for a particular activity.|He's a news junkie and reads several newspapers a day. 他是个新闻迷，每天都要读几份报纸。|近义词：addict, fiend, devotee (for non-drug contexts)|反义词:moderate user, casual user|词性:noun

junkies:/ˈdʒʌŋkiz/| n.|瘾君子们；对…入迷的人们|Plural form of "junkie," referring to multiple people who are addicted to something or have a compulsive interest in something.|The computer junkies were all huddled around the new server.  这些电脑迷都挤在新服务器周围。|近义词：addicts, fiends, devotees (for non-drug contexts)|反义词:moderate users, casual users|词性:noun
