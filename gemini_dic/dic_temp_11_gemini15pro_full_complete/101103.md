invert:/ˈɪnvɜːrt/ /ˈɪnvɝːt/ | n. | 倒置物，反向物 | something that is inverted | An invert sugar is used in many candies.  转化糖用于许多糖果中。| synonyms: inverse, reverse | antonyms: obverse | noun
