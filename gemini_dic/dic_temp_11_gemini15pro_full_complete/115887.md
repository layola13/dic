例句2：He's gotten himself into a real marmalade with his finances. 他的财务状况一团糟。|近义词：jam, preserve, mess, predicament |反义词:order, solution

ETYMOLOGY->
From Portuguese *marmelada "quince jam," from marmelo "quince," from Latin melimelum, from Greek melimēlon "honey apple." The sense of "mess" is a humorous extension.
