nonfixation:/ˌnɒn.fɪkˈseɪ.ʃən/ | n. | 非固定; 不固定化 | The nonfixation of nitrogen in the soil led to poor crop yields. 土壤中氮的非固定化导致作物产量低。| The study examined the nonfixation of atmospheric nitrogen by certain bacteria.  这项研究检测了某些细菌对大气中氮的非固定作用。 | 近义词: unfixedness, instability | 反义词: fixation, stabilization | 词性: 名词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + fixation (from Latin fixatio, meaning "a making fast")
