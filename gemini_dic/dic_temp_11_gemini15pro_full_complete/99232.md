intercombination:/ˌɪntərˌkɒmbɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. |相互结合，混合，（量子力学）禁戒跃迁 |A transition between terms of different multiplicity. |例句：The intercombination lines are much weaker than the allowed lines. 禁戒跃迁谱线比允许跃迁谱线弱得多。

例句：The phosphorescence arises from an intercombination transition. 磷光是由禁戒跃迁产生的。


近义词：transition, combination, mixture |反义词: separation |词性: noun
