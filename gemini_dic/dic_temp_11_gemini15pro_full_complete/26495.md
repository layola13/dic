cabalistically:/ˌkæbəˈlɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 神秘地；像犹太神秘哲学那样；隐晦地 | In a manner relating to or resembling Kabbalah; mystically; esoterically. | He spoke cabalistically about the hidden meanings in the text. 他神秘地谈论着文本中隐藏的含义。 | Example 2: The prophecy was delivered cabalistically, shrouded in symbolism and allegory.  这个预言是以神秘的方式传达的，笼罩在象征主义和寓言之中。| Synonyms: mystically, esoterically, occultly, secretly, cryptically | Antonyms: plainly, clearly, openly, transparently | Word Type: Adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cabalisticus, from cabala (“Kabbalah”), from Hebrew קַבָּלָה‎ (qabālā, “received tradition”), from קִבֵּל‎ (qibél, “to receive”).
