myology:/maɪˈɒlədʒi/
n.
肌肉学
The study of muscles, including their structure, function, and diseases.
Myology is a branch of anatomy. 肌肉学是解剖学的一个分支。
The myology of the human arm is complex. 人类手臂的肌肉学研究很复杂。

近义词:musculature
反义词:(None - No direct antonym for a field of study)
词性:noun
