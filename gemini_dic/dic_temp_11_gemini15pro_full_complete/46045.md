cyclic:/ˈsaɪklɪk/| adj.|循环的，周期的|Occurring in cycles; regularly repeated.|例句：The cyclic nature of the seasons. 季节的周期性循环。|近义词：recurring, periodic, repetitive|反义词:noncyclic, linear|adjective

cyclic:/ˈsaɪklɪk/| adj.|环状的|Having a ring structure (chemistry).|例句：A cyclic compound. 一种环状化合物。|近义词：ring-shaped, circular|反义词:acyclic, open-chain|adjective
