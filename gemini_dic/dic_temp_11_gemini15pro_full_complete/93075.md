idolographical:/ˌɪdiəˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
表意文字的;独特的
representing a thing or idea directly, not by letters or sounds; unique to an individual
例句：His handwriting is so idiosynchratic, almost idolographical, that only he can decipher it. 他的笔迹非常独特，几乎像表意文字一样，只有他自己才能辨认。

例句：The artist developed an idolographical language for his paintings, imbuing each symbol with personal meaning.  这位艺术家为他的画作开发了一种表意文字，赋予每个符号以个人意义。

近义词：symbolic, emblematic, pictorial, unique, individual
反义词：alphabetic, phonetic, common, general

词性: adjective
