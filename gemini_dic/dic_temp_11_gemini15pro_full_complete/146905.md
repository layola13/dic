overinstruction:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈstrʌkʃən/ | n. |过度指导，过度指示|The act of providing too much instruction, often leading to confusion or hindering learning.  It can involve giving excessively detailed directions, micromanaging tasks, or providing unnecessary explanations.|The overinstruction from the coach confused the players. 教练的过度指导让队员们感到困惑。|Synonyms: overteaching, micromanagement|Antonyms: underinstruction, neglect|noun
