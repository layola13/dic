indisputability:/ˌɪndɪˈspjuːtəˈbɪləti/ | n. |无可争辩性；不容置疑|The quality or state of being indisputable; undeniable certainty. |The indisputability of the evidence led to a swift conviction.  证据的无可争辩性导致了迅速定罪。|Synonyms: certainty, indubitableness, unquestionableness, irrefutability, incontestability |Antonyms: disputability, uncertainty, dubiety, questionableness |noun

indisputable:/ˌɪndɪˈspjuːtəbl/ | adj. |无可争辩的；不容置疑的|Not disputable; not open to question or doubt; undeniable. |His claim to the throne was indisputable. 他对王位的要求是无可争辩的。|Synonyms: undeniable, unquestionable, irrefutable, incontestable, indubitable |Antonyms: disputable, questionable, debatable, uncertain, doubtful |adjective
