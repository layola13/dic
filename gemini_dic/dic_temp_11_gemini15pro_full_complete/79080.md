glabrous:/ˈɡleɪbrəs/| adj.|光滑的，无毛的|Smooth; having a surface without hairs or projections.|例句：The leaves of the plant are glabrous and glossy. 这种植物的叶子光滑有光泽。|近义词：smooth, hairless, bald|反义词: hairy, pubescent, downy|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *glaber* (bald, smooth), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰladʰ- (bald, smooth).
