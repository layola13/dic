flang:/flændʒ/ | n. |法兰,凸缘;轮缘; |A projecting rim, edge, or flange, especially one used to strengthen an object, hold it in place, or attach it to something else. |The pipe has a flang at each end for connection.  管道两端都有法兰用于连接。
The flang of the wheel prevents it from derailing. 车轮的轮缘防止它脱轨。|近义词：rim, edge, lip, brim |反义词:center, core | noun
