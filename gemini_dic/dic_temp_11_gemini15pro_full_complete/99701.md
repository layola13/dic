interlocutor:/ˌɪntərˈlɑːkjətər/ (US), /ˌɪntəˈlɒkjʊtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|对话者，谈话者|A person who takes part in a dialogue or conversation.|例句：The interviewer and interviewee became interlocutors in a lively discussion.  面试官和受访者在一场活跃的讨论中成为了对话者。

例句：She found a sympathetic interlocutor in her therapist. 她从她的治疗师那里找到了一个善解人意的谈话对象。|近义词：conversationalist, speaker, dialogist, participant|反义词: listener, audience|词性: noun
