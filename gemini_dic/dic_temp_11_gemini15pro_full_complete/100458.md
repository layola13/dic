intertwiningly:/ˌɪntərˈtwaɪnɪŋli/
adverb
相互缠绕地，交织地
in a way that intertwines or interweaves; closely connected or interwoven.
The branches of the two trees were intertwiningly connected, forming a natural arch.  这两棵树的树枝相互交织，形成了一个天然的拱门。
The destinies of the two families were intertwiningly linked. 这两个家族的命运紧密相连。
Synonyms: inextricably, inseparably, interwovenly
Antonyms: separately, independently
词性: 副词
