blasphemer:/ˈblæsfɪmər/ | n. |亵渎神明者；渎神者|A person who commits blasphemy; someone who shows contempt or irreverence toward something considered sacred.|The blasphemer was condemned by the religious authorities.  亵渎神明者被宗教当局谴责。|He was accused of being a blasphemer for his controversial statements. 他因其有争议的言论被指控为亵渎神明者。| 近义词: heretic, infidel, nonbeliever | 反义词: believer, devotee, worshiper |词性: 名词(singular)
