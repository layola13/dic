individuate:/ɪnˌdɪvɪdʒueɪt/ |v. |个体化，赋予个性|To distinguish from others of the same kind; make individual or distinctive.|He tried to individuate each character in the novel.|近义词: differentiate, distinguish, singularize, personalize|反义词: generalize, categorize, homogenize|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin individuatus, past participle of individuare "to make individual," from Latin individuus "indivisible," from in- "not" + dividuus "divisible," from dividere "to divide."
