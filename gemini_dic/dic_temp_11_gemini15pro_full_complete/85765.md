hayam:/ˈhaɪæm/ | n. |鸡 (尤指家鸡) | A domesticated fowl kept for its eggs or meat, especially a young hen.  It is derived from Proto-Austronesian *qayam. |例句：The hayam pecked at the grains.  鸡啄食谷粒。

|例句：The farmer raised hayam for eggs. 农民养鸡是为了获取鸡蛋。 |近义词：chicken, fowl, hen (female), rooster/cock (male) |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
