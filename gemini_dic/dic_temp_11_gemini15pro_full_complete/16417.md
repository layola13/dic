barding:/ˈbɑːrdɪŋ/ | n. | 包裹脂肪，(烹饪)在瘦肉上裹脂肪 | The act of covering lean meat with fat before cooking to keep it moist, or the fat used for this purpose.  | The chef prepared the roast by barding it with bacon.  厨师用培根包裹烤肉来准备。| synonyms: larding, covering, wrapping | antonyms: exposing, uncovering | noun

barding:/ˈbɑːrdɪŋ/ | n. | (历史)马的披甲 |  Armor for a horse, typically consisting of metal plates or leather. | The knight's barding was intricately decorated with the family crest.  骑士的马甲上精心装饰着家族的徽章。| synonyms: armor, caparison, trappings | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "bareback" in a specific context) | noun
