cam:/kæm/| n.|凸轮，网络摄像头|A rotating or sliding piece, or projection, in a mechanical linkage, designed to impart a predetermined reciprocating motion to a follower by means of its constantly varying surface. |The camshaft rotates, causing the cams to push the valves open and closed. 凸轮轴旋转，使凸轮推动阀门打开和关闭。| cog, lobe| - |noun
cam:/kæm/| v.|装上凸轮，使用网络摄像头|To provide with a cam or cams. To film or photograph with a webcam.|He cammed the engine for better performance. 他为引擎装上凸轮以提高性能。| - | - |verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *kamme, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *kambō ("comb, crest"). Cognate with German Kamm ("comb").  The meaning "rotating machine part" is from 1578. The meaning "webcam" is a shortened form of "web camera," attested from 1995.
