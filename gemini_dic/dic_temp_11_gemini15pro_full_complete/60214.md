effluents:/ˈefluːənts/| n. |污水，废水；流出物|Liquid waste or sewage discharged into a river or the sea.|The factory was fined for releasing harmful effluents into the river.  这家工厂因向河流排放有害废水而被罚款。|synonyms: discharge, waste, sewage, outflow, emission|antonyms: inflow, intake|noun

effluents:/ˈefluːənts/| n. |流出物；散发物|Something that flows out, especially from the body or a container.|The volcano spewed forth molten rock and noxious effluents.  火山喷出了熔岩和有毒的散发物。|synonyms: emanation, exhalation, discharge|antonyms: absorption, intake|noun
