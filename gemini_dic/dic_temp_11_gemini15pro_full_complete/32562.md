chippy:/ˈtʃɪpi/| n.|木屑，碎片|A small thin piece of wood, or something similar, typically one that has been broken or chipped off from a larger piece.|例句：The carpenter swept up the chippies from the floor. 木匠把地上的木屑扫干净了。|近义词：splinter, shard, fragment|反义词: (no direct opposite)|词性:名词
