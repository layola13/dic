breastplow: /ˈbrestˌplaʊ/ | n. | 胸犁，雪犁式 |  A technique in skiing where the skier lies on their chest with their skis splayed out, using their body to slow down or stop. Often used in deep powder snow when a traditional stop is difficult or impossible.  | Example: He used a breastplow to slow himself down on the steep slope. 他在陡坡上用胸犁减速。

Example:  The breastplow is a useful technique for beginners to learn. 胸犁对初学者来说是一项有用的技巧。 | Synonym:  snowplow | Antonym: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "carving" as an opposite skiing technique) | Noun
