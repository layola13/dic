例句：The glueyness of the adhesive was perfect for the project.  这种粘合剂的粘性非常适合这个项目。|近义词：stickiness, adhesiveness, tackiness|反义词：smoothness, slipperiness|词性:名词

Etymology->
From glue + -y + -ness.  Glue traces back to Old French glu, from Late Latin glus, glūtis,  "glue."  The suffix -y forms adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "full of," while -ness forms nouns indicating a state or quality.
