carbonic:/kɑːrˈbɒnɪk/ | adj. |碳的，碳酸的|Relating to or containing carbon or carbon dioxide; of or relating to carbonic acid. |例句：Carbonic acid forms when carbon dioxide dissolves in water. 二氧化碳溶于水形成碳酸。|近义词：carbonated |反义词: None |词性: adjective

carbonic:/kɑːrˈbɒnɪk/ | n. |碳酸饮料|A carbonated drink. (This usage is less common) |例句：He ordered a carbonic at the bar. 他在酒吧点了一杯碳酸饮料。|近义词：soda, pop, soft drink |反义词: None |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *carbonique*, from carbone (“carbon”) + -ique (“-ic”).
