biding:/ˈbaɪdɪŋ/| adj.|等待的，期待的；居留的，逗留的| patiently waiting for an opportunity|例句：He's biding his time, waiting for the right moment to strike. 他在等待时机，等待合适的时机出击。

例句：She's biding in the countryside until the situation improves.  她在乡下待着，直到情况好转。|近义词：waiting, expectant, lingering, staying, residing|反义词：leaving, departing, impatient|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *biden, from Old English bīdan (“to abide, remain, wait, expect, experience, endure, obtain, occupy, dwell”), from Proto-Germanic *bīdaną (“to wait”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰeydʰ- (“to wait, expect”). Cognate with Scots bide (“to wait, stay, reside”), West Frisian bidde (“to wait”), Dutch beiden (“both”), German beiden (“both”), Old Norse bíða (“to wait, expect”).

注意事项:  “biding” 作为形容词，通常用于描述等待或居住的状态，很少用在其他语境中。  虽然“bide”作为动词也有“忍受”的意思，但“biding”作为形容词通常不表达这个意思。
