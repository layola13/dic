lisp:/lɪsp/| n. vi.| 咬舌，口齿不清；|A speech defect that involves pronouncing the sounds *s* and *z* as *th*.|She speaks with a lisp. 她说话口齿不清。|Synonym: slur, impediment|Antonym: clarity, articulation|noun
lisp:/lɪsp/| vi.| 口齿不清地说；|To speak with a lisp.|He lisped his way through the presentation. 他演讲时口齿不清。|Synonym: slur, mumble|Antonym: articulate, enunciate|verb
