declassify:/diːˈklæsɪfaɪ/| vt.|解密，降密|To remove the security classification from (a document or piece of information).|The government declassified the documents after 50 years. 政府在50年后解密了这些文件。|The CIA declassified some of its Cold War records. 中央情报局解密了它的一些冷战记录。|近义词：reveal, disclose, release|反义词：classify, conceal|词性：动词
declassification:/diːˌklæsɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/| n.|解密，降密|The act of declassifying something.|The declassification of these files proved the government's involvement. 这些文件的解密证明了政府的参与。|The declassification of the report led to public outcry. 报告的解密导致了公众的强烈抗议。|近义词：disclosure, revelation|反义词：classification, concealment|词性：名词
