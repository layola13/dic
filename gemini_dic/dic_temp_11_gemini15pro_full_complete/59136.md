dynamically: /daɪˈnæmɪkli/ | adv. | 动态地，充满活力地 | In a way that relates to energy or physical force, especially in a way that is continuously changing and developing. | The website content is dynamically generated.  网站内容是动态生成的。| dynamically allocated memory 动态分配的内存 | 近义词: actively, energetically, powerfully | 反义词: statically, passively | 词性: 副词
词源: From dynamic + -ally.  Ultimately from the Greek word "dunamikos," meaning "powerful."
