a-ok:/ˌeɪ oʊˈkeɪ/ | adj. | 非常好的，极好的 | Perfectly satisfactory; excellent.  |  Everything is a-ok with the project.  项目一切顺利。 |  近义词：excellent, fine, great, perfect, superb | 反义词：awful, terrible, bad, unsatisfactory | 词性: adjective
词源: A-OK originated in the US space program in the 1960s. It's thought to be a phonetic spelling of "all OK," emphasizing the completeness of the "OK."
