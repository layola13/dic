jonah:/ˈdʒoʊnə/| n.|约拿（男子名）; 约拿书（《圣经》旧约中的一卷）; 带来厄运的人|A biblical prophet who was swallowed by a large fish (or whale). Also used to refer to someone who is seen as bringing bad luck. |例句：He was seen as a jonah after the team lost every game he played. 他在球队输掉他参加的每场比赛后，被视为一个带来厄运的人。

例句：The Book of Jonah is a short but powerful story. 约拿书是一个简短而有力的故事。|近义词：jinx, curse|反义词:charm, blessing|名词
