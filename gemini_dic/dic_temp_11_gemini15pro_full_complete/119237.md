miaul:/mjaʊl/| v. n.| 猫叫；咪咪叫|To make the characteristic crying sound of a cat.  The sound itself can also be referred to as a miaul.|The kitten miauled pitifully. (小猫可怜地咪咪叫着。) The cat's constant miauling kept me awake all night. (猫不停地叫让我整夜没睡。)|近义词：meow, mew, purr (when content)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps hiss or growl)|verb, noun
