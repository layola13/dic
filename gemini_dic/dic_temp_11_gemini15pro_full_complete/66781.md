exotically:/ɪɡˈzɑːtɪkli/ /ɛɡˈzɒtɪkli/| adv.|异国情调地；奇特地|In a way that is unusual and exciting, because it is related to a foreign country.|She was dressed exotically in brightly coloured silks. 她穿着色彩鲜艳的丝绸，异国情调十足。|The food was exotically spiced.  食物用异国香料调味。|近义词：unusually, strangely, foreignly, bizarrely|反义词：ordinarily, commonly, typically|词性:副词

词源:
From exotic + -ally.  Exotic comes from the Late Latin exoticus, from the Greek exotikos "foreign," from exo "outside."
