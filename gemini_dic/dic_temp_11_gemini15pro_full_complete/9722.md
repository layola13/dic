antiuniversities:/ˌæntiˌjuːnɪˈvɜːrsətiz/
|n.|反大学|Institutions or movements that challenge or reject traditional academic norms and values, often emphasizing practical skills, alternative learning methods, or countercultural perspectives.|The rise of antiuniversities in the 1960s reflected a growing disillusionment with mainstream education. 20世纪60年代反大学的兴起反映了人们对主流教育日益增长的失望。|Alternatives to traditional universities, free schools, experimental colleges|Traditional universities, established institutions|noun
