monotessaron:/ˌmɒnəˈtɛsərɒn/ | n. | 四福音合参，四福音书 | A gospel harmony, or a continuous narrative of the life of Jesus Christ constructed from the four Gospels. |  Early Christians created monotessarons to provide a single, unified account of Jesus's life. 早期基督徒创造了四福音合参，以提供一个单一、统一的耶稣生平叙述。

The Diatessaron of Tatian is a well-known example of a monotessaron.  塔蒂安的《四福音合参》是著名的四福音合参的例子。 | 近义词: gospel harmony, diatessaron | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists as it refers to a specific type of text) | 词性: noun
