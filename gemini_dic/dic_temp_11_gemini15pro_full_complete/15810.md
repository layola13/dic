balkily:/ˈbɔːlkɪli/ | adv. | 倔强地；不愿地；不肯合作地 | In a manner that shows unwillingness or resistance; stubbornly or reluctantly. | The mule balkily refused to cross the stream. 骡子倔强地拒绝过河。|Synonyms: stubbornly, reluctantly, unwillingly, obdurately, perversely | Antonyms: willingly, readily, cooperatively, obediently | 词性: 副词
