noncorrespondingly:/ˌnɒnkɒrɪˈspɒndɪŋli/
adverb
不相对应地，不一致地
In a way that does not correspond or match.
The two graphs show noncorrespondingly fluctuating trends. 这两个图表显示出不相对应的波动趋势。
The numbers of registered voters and actual votes cast noncorrespondingly changed over the years. 登记选民的数量和实际投票数多年来变化不一致。
Synonym: differently, dissimilarly, discordantly
Antonym: correspondingly, similarly, concordantly
词性: 副词
