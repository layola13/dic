PHRASE->
crawling with something: 充满了...（通常指令人不快的东西） The room was crawling with ants. 房间里爬满了蚂蚁。
crawl back to someone:  低声下气地回到某人身边  He'll crawl back to her eventually. 他最终会低声下气地回到她身边。
make someone's skin crawl:  让某人感到毛骨悚然 The thought of spiders makes my skin crawl.  想到蜘蛛我就毛骨悚然。
be crawling up the walls:  非常焦虑不安 I've been stuck inside all day and I'm crawling up the walls. 我整天困在里面，都要疯了。
at a crawl:  以极慢的速度 The traffic was moving at a crawl.  车辆行驶得非常缓慢。
