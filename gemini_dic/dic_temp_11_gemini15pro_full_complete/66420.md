executory:/ɪɡˈzɛkjʊtɔːri/
adjective
待执行的；执行中的；遗产管理中的
A contract or agreement that is executory is one that has not yet been fully performed or completed.  It refers to something that is yet to be carried out or put into effect. It can also refer to the period of time during which an estate is being administered by an executor.
The executory contract stipulated the terms of the sale.  这份待执行的合同规定了销售条款。
The terms of the executory lease were favorable to the tenant. 租约的执行条款对承租人有利。
synonyms:  pending, unfulfilled, incomplete, prospective
antonyms:  executed, completed, fulfilled, performed
词性: 形容词
