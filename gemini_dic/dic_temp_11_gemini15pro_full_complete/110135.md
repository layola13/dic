lie:/laɪ/| v. |躺；位于；平放；说谎|To be in or assume a horizontal or resting position on a supporting surface.|The book lies on the table. 书在桌子上。|近义词：recline, rest, prevaricate (for "lie" meaning to tell a falsehood)|反义词:stand, rise, tell the truth|词性:动词

lie:/laɪ/| n. |谎言|A false statement made with deliberate intent to deceive; an intentional untruth.|He told a lie about where he had been. 他对他去过的地方撒了谎。|近义词：falsehood, untruth, fib|反义词:truth|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *lēogan "to lie, tell a falsehood," and licgan "to lie down, recline," both from Proto-Germanic *ligan (source also of Old Saxon ligan "to lie," Old Norse liggja, Old Frisian lidza, Middle Dutch liggen, Dutch liggen, Old High German ligen, German liegen "to lie, lie down"), from PIE root *legh- "to lie down, lay."
