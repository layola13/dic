landsat:/ˈlændsæt/| n. |陆地卫星|A series of U.S. satellites that scan the Earth's surface, used for environmental monitoring and resource management.|例句：Landsat images are used to monitor deforestation. 陆地卫星图像被用于监测森林砍伐。|例句：Scientists use Landsat data to study climate change. 科学家利用陆地卫星数据研究气候变化。|近义词：Earth observation satellite, remote sensing satellite|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "ground survey")|词性:noun


词源: "Landsat" is a portmanteau of "land" and "satellite."
