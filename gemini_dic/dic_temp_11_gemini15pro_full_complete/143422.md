outflee: /ˌaʊtˈfliː/
verb
逃走，逃脱，比…逃得更快
To flee from someone or something, especially faster or more successfully than someone else.
The refugees outfled the advancing army.  难民们逃脱了前进的军队。
The gazelle outfled the lion. 瞪羚比狮子跑得更快，逃脱了。
近义词: outrun, escape, flee
反义词: surrender, yield
词性: verb

词源:
From Old English *ūtflēon, from ūt (out) + flēon (to flee).  由古英语 *ūtflēon 构成，来自 ūt (out) + flēon (to flee)。
