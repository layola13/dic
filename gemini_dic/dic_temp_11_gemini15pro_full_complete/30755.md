cespitose:/ˈsespɪˌtoʊs/| adj.|丛生的，簇生的|Growing in dense tufts or clumps; forming a mat or turf.|例句：Cespitose grasses are common in prairie ecosystems. 丛生禾草在草原生态系统中很常见。

例句：The cespitose growth habit helps the plant conserve water. 丛生的生长习性有助于植物保持水分。|近义词：tufted, clumped, matted|反义词:scattered, dispersed|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *caespitosus, from caespes (“turf, sod”), from caedere (“to cut”).  The original sense referred to plants that grow in tufts that can be cut into sods.
