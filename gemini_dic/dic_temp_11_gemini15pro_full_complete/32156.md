chewiest:/ˈtʃuːiɪst/| adj.|最耐嚼的，最难嚼的|Superlative form of chewy; having the most resistance to chewing.|This is the chewiest gum I've ever had. 这是我吃过最难嚼的口香糖。|The steak was incredibly chewiest and difficult to swallow. 这块牛排非常难嚼，很难下咽。|近义词：toughest, firmest, gummiest|反义词：tenderest, softest|词性:形容词
词源: From *chew* + *-y* (suffix forming adjectives meaning 'having the quality of') + *-est* (suffix forming superlative adjectives).  The verb *chew* comes from Old English *cēowan*.
