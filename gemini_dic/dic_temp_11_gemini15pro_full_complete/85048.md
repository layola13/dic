harbours:/ˈhɑːrbərz/ (US) /ˈhɑːbəz/ (UK)| n. v.| 港口，海港；庇护；窝藏；怀有(想法或感情)|A place on the coast where ships may moor in shelter, especially one protected from rough water by walls, piers, or other artificial structures.
