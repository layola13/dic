chronogrammatist:/ˌkrɒnəˈɡræməˌtɪst/ | n. | 年代学家，年表学家 | A person skilled in or interested in chronograms (inscriptions in which certain letters, when interpreted as Roman numerals, express a date). |  John Aubrey, the 17th-century antiquarian, was a noted chronogrammatist.  约翰·奥布里，17世纪的古物研究者，是一位著名的年代学家。 |  chronographer, annalist |  - | noun
chronogram:/ˈkrɒnəɡræm/ | n. | 年代表铭，年代诗 | An inscription in which certain letters, when interpreted as Roman numerals, express a date. | The church door bore a chronogram commemorating its construction. 教堂的门上有一个年代表铭，纪念它的建造。 | inscription, epigraph | - | noun
