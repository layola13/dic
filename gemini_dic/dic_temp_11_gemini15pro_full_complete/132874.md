nonelaborating: /nɒnɪˈlæbəreɪtɪŋ/
adjective
不详述的，不阐述的
Not giving further details or explanation.
The witness gave a nonelaborating response, simply stating "I don't know."  证人没有详细说明，只是简单地说“我不知道”。
He offered a nonelaborating shrug in response to my question. 他对我的问题耸了耸肩，没有进一步解释。
近义词: terse, concise, brief, succinct, reticent
反义词: detailed, expansive, thorough, verbose, prolix
词性: adjective
