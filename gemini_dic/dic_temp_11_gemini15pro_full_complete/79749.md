glummy:/ˈɡlʌmi/ | adj. | 忧郁的，沮丧的，阴暗的 | Feeling or looking sad and without hope; dark or poorly lit. |  The weather was glummy and rainy. 天气阴沉多雨。|  The child looked glummy after losing his toy. 孩子丢了玩具后看起来很沮丧。| 近义词: depressed, dejected, gloomy, sad, dismal | 反义词: cheerful, happy, bright, sunny | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From gloom + -y. Ultimately from the Proto-Germanic *glūmaz (“to stare”).
