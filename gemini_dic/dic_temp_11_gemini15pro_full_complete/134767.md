nonmercantile:/ˌnɒnˈmɜːk(ə)ntaɪl/ |adj.|非商业的；非营利的|Not related to commerce or trade; not intended for profit.|例句：The museum is a nonmercantile organization dedicated to preserving local history.  这家博物馆是一个致力于保护当地历史的非营利组织。

例句：She pursued a nonmercantile career in social work. 她从事社会工作，这是一项非营利性事业。|近义词：nonprofit, noncommercial, charitable|反义词：commercial, mercantile, for-profit|词性：形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
mercantile (adjective): from Old French "mercantil,"  from Late Latin "mercantilis," meaning "relating to merchants or trade," from "mercans" (merchant), from "mercari" (to trade).
