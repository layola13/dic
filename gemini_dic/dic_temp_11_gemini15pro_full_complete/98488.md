installs:/ɪnˈstɔːlz/
verb
安装，设置
puts (something) in a position ready for use.
The technician installs the new software. 技术员安装了新软件。
近义词: sets up, implements, fits
反义词: uninstalls, removes
词性:v.


installs:/ɪnˈstɔːlz/
noun
安装，设置
the act of putting something in a position ready for use.
The installs took several hours to complete. 安装过程花了几个小时才完成。
近义词: setups, implementations
反义词: removals, uninstallations
词性:n.
