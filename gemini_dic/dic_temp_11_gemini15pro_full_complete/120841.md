misawards:/ˌmɪsəˈwɔːrdz/
noun
错误的奖励，不当的裁决
an award or judgment that is incorrect or unfair
The misawards in the competition caused a lot of controversy. 比赛中的错误裁决引起了很多争议。

misaward:/ˌmɪsəˈwɔːrd/
verb
错误地授予，不当地裁决
to give an award or judgment incorrectly or unfairly
The judges misawarded the prize to the wrong contestant. 评委把奖项错误地颁给了错误的参赛者。


近义词:  misjudge, mishandle, misallocate, err in awarding
反义词: award correctly, judge fairly, allocate properly
词性: noun (misawards), verb (misaward)
