freight:/freɪt/| n. vt.|货物，运费；运送|Goods carried by a large vehicle, ship, train, or aircraft; the charge for transporting something by freight.|The ship carries both passengers and freight. 这艘船既载客也载货。|We'll send your personal belongings by freight. 我们将用货运的方式寄送您的个人物品。|近义词：cargo, shipment, load|反义词: N/A|词性:名词, 动词
freight:/freɪt/| vt.|装货于；使充满|Load (a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle) with goods for transportation.|The train was freighted with coal. 火车装载了煤炭。|The plane was freighted with emergency supplies.  飞机装满了紧急物资。|近义词：load, lade|反义词:unload|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *freit, from freter "to hire, load," from Frankish *frahton "to load," from Proto-Germanic *frahtjaną "to load," from *frahtą "freight, load."
