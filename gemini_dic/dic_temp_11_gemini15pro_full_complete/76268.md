fuzzing:/ˈfʌzɪŋ/| n.|模糊测试|The process of finding security vulnerabilities in software by inputting random or unexpected data.|Example: Fuzzing is a crucial part of software testing to ensure its robustness. 模糊测试是软件测试中至关重要的一部分，以确保其健壮性。|Example:  The security team used fuzzing techniques to identify potential weaknesses in the system. 安全团队使用模糊测试技术来识别系统中潜在的弱点。|近义词：random testing, penetration testing|反义词:static analysis|词性:noun

词源: From "fuzz" (n.), meaning a mass of short, fine fibers, referring to the random and somewhat chaotic nature of the input data used.  The term "fuzz" in this context likely arose in the late 1980s at the University of Wisconsin-Madison.
