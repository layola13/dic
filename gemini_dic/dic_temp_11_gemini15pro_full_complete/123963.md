morintannic: /ˌmɒrɪnˈtænɪk/ | adj. | 鞣酸的，含有鞣酸的 | Relating to or containing tannic acid, a type of polyphenol found in plants, especially in galls, bark, and other woody tissues. It is used in tanning leather and dyeing, and also has medicinal properties. | The morintannic acid in the oak bark was used to tan the leather. 橡树皮中的鞣酸被用来鞣革。 | The wine had a strong, morintannic taste. 这酒有一种强烈的鞣酸味。|Synonyms: tannic, astringent | Antonyms: bland | word type: adjective
