clean house:/kliːn haʊs/ | v. | 打扫房子;清除;整顿;彻底改革 | to clean a house thoroughly; to get rid of unwanted personnel or things in an organization or group. |  例句：I spent all Saturday cleaning house. 我整个星期六都在打扫房子。| 近义词：tidy up, declutter, purge, reform | 反义词：mess up, clutter | 词性: 动词

clean house:/kliːn haʊs/ | n. | 大扫除;清除;整顿 | a thorough cleaning of a house; a purge of unwanted personnel or things in an organization or group. | 例句：The company is doing a clean house to reduce costs. 公司正在进行整顿以降低成本。| 近义词：purge, overhaul, shake-up | 反义词：disorganization, chaos | 词性: 名词
