bignonia:/bɪɡˈnoʊniə/| n. |紫葳，凌霄花|A climbing plant with showy trumpet-shaped flowers, typically of a reddish or orange hue.  It belongs to the family Bignoniaceae.|例句：The bignonia climbed up the trellis, its vibrant flowers a splash of color against the brick wall. 紫葳爬上了棚架，鲜艳的花朵在砖墙上构成了一抹亮丽的色彩。

例句：The fragrant bignonia perfumed the evening air.  芬芳的紫葳使夜晚的空气充满了香味。|近义词：trumpet vine, cross vine|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the Modern Latin genus name Bignonia, named by Joseph Pitton de Tournefort in honor of Abbé Jean-Paul Bignon (1662-1743), librarian to Louis XIV of France.
