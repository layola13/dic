liming:/ˈlaɪmɪŋ/| n.|石灰处理，涂白；鸟类换羽|The process of treating something with lime, or the white coating produced by this process; the period during which birds shed and replace their feathers.|The gardener was liming the soil to reduce its acidity. 园丁正在用石灰处理土壤以降低酸度。

The liming of the trees made them stand out against the dark background.  给树木涂白使它们在深色背景下显得格外醒目。|近义词:whitewashing, chalking|反义词:|n.
