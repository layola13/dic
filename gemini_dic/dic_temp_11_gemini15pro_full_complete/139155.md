obfuscater: /ˈɒbfʌskeɪtər/ (UK), /ˈɑːbfəskeɪtər/ (US) | n. |  混淆者；模糊处理程序 | A person or thing that obfuscates.  | The programmer used an obfuscater to protect the source code from reverse engineering.  程序员使用模糊处理程序来保护源代码不被逆向工程。 | Synonyms:  confuser, obscurer | Antonyms: clarifier | Noun
