craterous:/ˈkreɪtərəs/| adj.|多坑的，像陨石坑的|Resembling a crater or having many craters; pitted.|The moon's surface is craterous due to countless asteroid impacts. 月球表面由于无数次小行星撞击而布满坑洞。|例句：The landscape was craterous, pocked with holes of all sizes.  地貌坑坑洼洼，布满了大小不一的洞。

例句：The craterous terrain made driving difficult. 坑洼不平的地形使驾驶困难。|近义词：pitted, pockmarked, holey|反义词:smooth, flat|词性:形容词
