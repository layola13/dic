derogates:/dɪˈrɒɡeɪts/
v.| 贬低，损害，减损|
It derogates from their dignity to be treated like this.  被这样对待有损他们的尊严。
The new law derogates from women's rights.  新法律削弱了妇女的权利。
近义词:detract, diminish, belittle, disparage
反义词:enhance, elevate, praise, extol
词性:动词

derogate:/ˈdɛrəɡeɪt/
v.| 贬低，损害，减损|
His actions derogate from his reputation as an honest man. 他的行为损害了他诚实的名声。
Nothing could derogate from her beauty. 没有什么能减损她的美丽。
近义词:detract, diminish, belittle, disparage
反义词:enhance, elevate, praise, extol
词性:动词


词源:  From Latin *dērōgātus, past participle of dērōgāre (“repeal partially, restrict, detract from”), from dē- (“from, away”) + rogāre (“to ask, propose a law”).
