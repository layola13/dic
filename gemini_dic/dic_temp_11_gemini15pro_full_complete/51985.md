diag:/ˈdaɪæɡ/| n. |诊断，对角线|A test performed to identify the nature or cause of something, especially a medical condition.  Also short for diagonal.|The mechanic ran a diag on the car's engine.  |Synonyms: diagnosis, analysis, examination |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps oversight, neglect) |noun

diag:/daɪˈæɡənəl/ | adj. |对角线的|Joining two opposite corners of a square, rectangle, or other straight-sided shape. |The queen moves diagonally across the chessboard. |Synonyms:  slanting, oblique, transversal |Antonyms: horizontal, vertical |adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From the Greek "dia-" (through, across) + "gignoskein" (to know). Shortened form of "diagnosis" or "diagonal".
