aliseptal: /ˌælɪˈsɛptəl/
adjective
防腐的，抗菌的
Relating to or denoting a substance that prevents sepsis (the presence in tissues of harmful bacteria and their toxins, especially as a result of infection of a wound).  Aliseptal refers specifically to preventing sepsis.
A new aliseptal wound dressing was developed.  一种新的抗菌伤口敷料被研发出来。
Synonyms: antiseptic, antimicrobial, antibacterial
Antonyms: septic, infectious
词性: adjective
