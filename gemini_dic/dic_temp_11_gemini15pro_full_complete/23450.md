brady:/ˈbreɪdi/| adj.|缓慢的；迟钝的|slow, characterized by slowness, especially in movement or reaction.  Relating to or suffering from bradycardia (slow heart rate).|例句：The brady snail crawled slowly across the leaf.  这只行动迟缓的蜗牛慢慢地爬过树叶。

例句：The doctor diagnosed him with bradycardia, a condition characterized by a brady heartbeat. 医生诊断他患有心动过缓，这是一种以心跳缓慢为特征的疾病。|近义词：slow, sluggish, tardy, leisurely|反义词：fast, rapid, quick, swift|词性:形容词
词源: From Greek *bradys "slow," from PIE root *brad- "slow, dull."
