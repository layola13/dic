karabiner:/ˌkærəˈbiːnər/| n. | 登山扣，安全扣 | A metal loop with a spring-loaded gate used for connecting ropes, webbing, or other components in climbing, mountaineering, and other activities. | Climbers use karabiners to attach themselves to the safety rope. 登山者使用登山扣将自己连接到安全绳上。|  The karabiner held the rope securely. 登山扣牢牢地固定住了绳子。 |Synonyms: carabiner, snap link, quick link| Antonyms: None (no direct opposite for a piece of equipment)| noun
