couchee:/ˈkuːʃeɪ/
noun
睡衣晚会; 就寝时间
an evening party at which guests wear nightclothes or pajamas, or the time when someone goes to bed.
She invited her friends over for a sleepover/couchee. 她邀请朋友们来参加睡衣派对。
It was past my usual couchee.  已经过了我通常的就寝时间。
synonyms:  sleepover, bedtime, nightclothes
antonyms:  rising time, wake-up time
n.
