dexterities:/dɛkˈstɛrɪtiz/
n.
灵巧，熟练，敏捷
skill and ease in using the hands or body; mental quickness or cleverness
The surgeon's dexterities allowed him to perform the complex operation flawlessly.  外科医生的灵巧使他能够完美地完成复杂的手术。
近义词: skills, adroitness, agility, proficiency, aptitude
反义词: clumsiness, awkwardness, ineptness
词性: 名词(plural)
