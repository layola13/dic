captioner:/ˈkæpʃənər/ | n. | 字幕制作者，字幕员 | A person who writes captions for videos or television programs, especially for the deaf or hard-of-hearing. | Example: The captioner worked diligently to ensure the captions were accurate and synchronized with the dialogue. 字幕员努力工作以确保字幕准确并与对话同步。 | Example:  Thanks to the captioner, I was able to understand the foreign film. 多亏了字幕员，我才能够理解这部外国电影。 | Synonym: subtitler, transcriber | Antonym: N/A | Noun
