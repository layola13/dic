ghostship: /ˈɡoʊst ʃɪp/ (US) /ˈɡəʊst ʃɪp/ (UK)| n. |幽灵船，鬼船|A ship with no living crew aboard, often seen as a ghostly apparition.|The legend of the Flying Dutchman is a famous example of a ghostship. 飞翔的荷兰人号的传说是著名的幽灵船的例子。

The Mary Celeste was discovered as a ghostship, abandoned but undamaged. 玛丽·赛勒斯特号被发现时是一艘幽灵船，被遗弃但没有损坏。|近义词：derelict, phantom ship|反义词: crewed ship |词性: noun
