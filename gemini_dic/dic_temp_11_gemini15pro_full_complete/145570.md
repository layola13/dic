overcomplexity:/ˌōvərˌkəmˈpleksəti/ | n. | 过度复杂性；过于复杂 |The state of being excessively complex, often unnecessarily so.  It refers to a situation where something is made more complicated than it needs to be. | The overcomplexity of the system made it difficult to use.  这个系统的过度复杂性使其难以使用。| synonyms: intricacy, complication, elaborateness | antonyms: simplicity, straightforwardness | noun

overcomplex:/ˌoʊvərˌkɑːmˈplɛks/ | adj. | 过于复杂的；过于繁复的 | Excessively complex; more complicated than necessary. | The instructions were overcomplex and confusing.  这些说明过于复杂，令人困惑。| synonyms: intricate, complicated, elaborate | antonyms: simple, straightforward, easy | adjective
