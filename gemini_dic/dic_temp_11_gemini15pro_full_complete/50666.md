derived:/dɪˈraɪvd/ | adj. | 衍生的，派生的 | coming from or developed from something else |例句：The word "biology" is derived from Greek. “生物学”一词源于希腊语。|例句：Many English words are derived from Latin. 许多英语单词源自拉丁语。| 近义词: obtained, acquired, deduced, inferred | 反义词: original, primary | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Latin *derivatus, past participle of derivare (“to draw off, divert a stream”), from Latin de- (“from”) + rivus (“stream, brook”).
