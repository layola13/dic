fistulous:/ˈfɪstʃʊləs/
adjective
瘘管的；有瘘的
Characterized by the presence of a fistula, an abnormal passage or connection between two hollow spaces or organs.
The patient presented with a fistulous tract between the bowel and the skin.  病人出现肠道和皮肤之间的瘘管。
The surgeon repaired the fistulous opening in the bladder.  外科医生修复了膀胱上的瘘管开口。
Synonyms:  sinuous, winding, tortuous
Antonyms:  straight, unperforated, intact
词性: adjective
