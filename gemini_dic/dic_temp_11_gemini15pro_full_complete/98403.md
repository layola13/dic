insolvent: /ɪnˈsɒlvənt/ (UK) /ɪnˈsɑːlvənt/ (US)
adjective
无力偿还的；破产的
Unable to pay debts owed.
The company was declared insolvent after accumulating millions of dollars in debt.  这家公司在积累了数百万美元的债务后被宣布破产。
The insolvent debtor was forced to sell his assets.  无力偿债的债务人被迫出售其资产。

近义词: bankrupt, broke, ruined, penniless
反义词: solvent, affluent, wealthy
词性: adjective
