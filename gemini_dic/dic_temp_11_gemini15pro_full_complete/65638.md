evangelizes:/ɪˈvændʒəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
传福音；使皈依；宣传；推广
To preach the gospel to; to convert to Christianity; to advocate or promote something with enthusiasm.
He evangelizes to the poor in the inner city. 他在市中心的贫民区传福音。
Synonyms: proselytize, preach, promote, advocate
Antonyms: discourage, dissuade, oppose, suppress
词性: 动词
