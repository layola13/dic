garrett:/ˈɡærɪt/| n.|阁楼，小房间，顶楼房间|A small room, especially one at the top of a house.|He lived in a small garrett at the top of the building. 他住在楼顶的一个小阁楼里。|Synonyms: attic, loft, mansard|Antonyms: basement, cellar|noun

garret:/ˈɡærɪt/| n.|箭楼，守望楼 (古代)|A small tower on a wall or at the corner of a building, typically used in the past for defense.|The castle's garret offered a panoramic view of the surrounding countryside. 城堡的箭楼提供了周围乡村的全景。|Synonyms: turret, watchtower|Antonyms: N/A|noun
