outgrow:/ˌaʊtˈɡroʊ/| v. | 长大不再需要；长得比…大；发展得超过|To grow too large for (something, such as clothing); to grow faster or taller than (someone); to lose interest in or stop doing (something) as one gets older.|例句：The children have outgrown these clothes. 孩子们已经长得穿不下这些衣服了。

例句：He soon outgrew his older brother. 他很快就长得比他哥哥高了。


近义词：surpass, exceed, outstrip|反义词: shrink, diminish|词性:动词
