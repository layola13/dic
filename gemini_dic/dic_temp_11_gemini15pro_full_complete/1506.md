acetin:/ˈæsɪtɪn/ | n. |醋精；三醋酸甘油酯|A colorless, viscous, odorless liquid, C3H5(C2H3O2)3, the triacetate of glycerol, used as a solvent and plasticizer.|Acetin is commonly used as a solvent in the production of cosmetics.|近义词:glyceryl triacetate|反义词: None|词性:noun

Etymology: From acetic + -in.
