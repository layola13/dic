curatorship:/kjʊəˈreɪtərʃɪp/
n.
管理(权)；策展(工作)；监护(权)
The position or office of a curator; the act or process of curating.
She was awarded a prestigious curatorship at the museum. 她被授予了博物馆一个享有盛誉的策展职位。
The curatorship of the exhibition took months of planning.  展览的策展工作花费了数月时间进行规划。

近义词：administration, management, supervision, custodianship
反义词： (No direct antonym, but perhaps) neglect, abandonment
词性: noun
