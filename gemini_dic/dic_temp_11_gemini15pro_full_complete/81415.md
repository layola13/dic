grassroots:/ˈɡræsruːts/ /ˈɡræsrʊts/| adj. n.|草根；基层；|Relating to or involving the ordinary people in a society or organization, especially those not in positions of power.  Originating among or carried on by common people.|The grassroots campaign gained momentum quickly. 这场草根运动迅速获得了动力。|The party needs to strengthen its grassroots support. 该党需要加强其基层支持。|synonyms: rank and file, ordinary people, working class, common people, the masses, the populace, the public, the multitude |antonyms: elite, leadership, upper class|noun, adjective
