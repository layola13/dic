apophthegmatic:/ˌæpəˈθɛɡmætɪk/
| adj. |格言式的；警句的；简洁精辟的|Of or relating to an apophthegm; terse and pithy.|His apophthegmatic pronouncements were often quoted. 他的格言式言论常被引用。|
Synonyms: pithy, concise, succinct, terse, epigrammatic, sententious
|
Antonyms: verbose, prolix, rambling, diffuse
|adjective|
