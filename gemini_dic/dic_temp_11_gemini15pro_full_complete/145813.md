overdeep:/ˈoʊvərdiːp/
adjective
过深的，太深的
excessively deep; deeper than is usual or desirable
The lake is overdeep for swimming. 这个湖太深了，不适合游泳。
近义词: excessively deep, very deep, profound
反义词: shallow
词性: adjective


overdeep:/ˈoʊvərdiːp/
noun
过深之处
an excessively deep place
The overdeep in the ocean is mysterious. 海洋深处充满神秘。
近义词: abyss, chasm, depths
反义词: shoal
词性: noun
