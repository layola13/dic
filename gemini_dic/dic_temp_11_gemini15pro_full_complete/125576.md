multitrack:/ˈmʌltiˌtræk/ | adj. n.|多轨的；多声道录音；多轨录音带|A recording technique that uses multiple audio tracks to record different instruments or vocals separately, which can then be mixed together.|The band used multitrack recording to layer their instruments, creating a richer sound. 乐队使用多轨录音来叠加他们的乐器，创造更丰富的音效。|Synonyms: multichannel, polyphonic|Antonyms: monophonic, single-track|adjective, noun

multitrack:/ˈmʌltiˌtræk/ | n.|多轨录音带|A magnetic tape used for multitrack recording.|The engineer carefully spliced the multitrack tape. 工程师小心翼翼地拼接多轨录音带。|Synonyms: multichannel tape|Antonyms: single-track tape|noun
