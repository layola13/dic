glowingly:/ˈɡloʊɪŋli/
adverb
热情洋溢地；满面春风地
In a way that expresses great praise and enthusiasm.
She spoke glowingly of her trip to Italy. 她热情洋溢地讲述了她的意大利之旅。
Synonyms: enthusiastically, warmly, favorably, brightly
Antonyms: critically, negatively, dimly, coldly
词性: 副词
