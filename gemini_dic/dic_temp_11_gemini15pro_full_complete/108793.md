leather: /ˈleðər/ (US) /ˈleðə(r)/ (UK)| n. |皮革|Material made from the tanned hide of an animal.|例句：My new jacket is made of soft leather. 我的新夹克是用柔软的皮革制成的。

例句：He polished his leather shoes to a high shine. 他把他的皮鞋擦得锃亮。

近义词：hide, skin, pelt|反义词:synthetic leather, faux leather|词性:noun
