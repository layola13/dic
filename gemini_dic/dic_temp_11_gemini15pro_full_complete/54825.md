dispersible:/dɪˈspɜːrsəbl/ /dɪˈspɝːsəbl/
adjective
可分散的，可弥散的
Capable of being dispersed or scattered.
The powder is dispersible in water.  这种粉末可溶于水。
The seeds are light and dispersible by the wind. 这些种子很轻，可以随风飘散。
近义词:scatterable, diffusible, soluble, dissolvable
反义词:indispersible, insoluble
词性:adjective

disperse:/dɪˈspɜːrs/ /dɪˈspɝːs/
verb
分散，散开，消散
To distribute or spread over a wide area.
The crowd dispersed after the concert.  演唱会结束后，人群散开了。
Police dispersed the protesters with tear gas. 警察用催泪瓦斯驱散了抗议者。
近义词:scatter, dissipate, distribute
反义词:gather, collect, assemble
词性:verb

dispersion:/dɪˈspɜːrʒn/ /dɪˈspɝːʒn/
noun
分散，散布，弥散
The action or process of distributing things or people over a wide area.
The dispersion of seeds is essential for plant reproduction.  种子的散播对植物繁殖至关重要。
The prism caused a dispersion of light into a rainbow. 棱镜使光线散射成彩虹。
近义词:scattering, dissemination, distribution
反义词:concentration, gathering
词性:noun

词源: From Late Latin *dispersibilis, from Latin dispersus (past participle of dispergere 'to scatter'), from dis- 'apart' + spargere 'to scatter'.
