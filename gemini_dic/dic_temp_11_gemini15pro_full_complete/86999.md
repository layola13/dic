henogeny:/hɛˈnɒdʒəni/ | n. | 单性生殖 |  Asexual reproduction, specifically the development of an organism from a female gamete without fertilization. | Example: Some species of lizards reproduce through henogeny, allowing them to thrive even in the absence of males.  一些种类的蜥蜴通过单性生殖繁殖，即使没有雄性也能茁壮成长。 | Example: Henogeny is a fascinating adaptation that allows certain species to survive in isolated environments. 单性生殖是一种迷人的适应机制，允许某些物种在孤立的环境中生存。 | Synonym: parthenogenesis, asexual reproduction | Antonym: sexual reproduction | noun
