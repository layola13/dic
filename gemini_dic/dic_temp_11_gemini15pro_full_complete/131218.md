noncaking:/ˌnɒnˈkeɪkɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnˈkeɪkɪŋ/| adj.|不结块的，防结块的|Not forming lumps or sticking together; free-flowing.|This noncaking salt is perfect for salt shakers. 这种不结块的盐非常适合盐瓶使用。|The company developed a noncaking fertilizer for easier application. 这家公司开发了一种不结块的肥料，以便于施用。|近义词：free-flowing, loose, granular|反义词：caking, lumpy, clumping|词性:形容词

Etymology:
non- + caking

"non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of."
"caking" comes from the verb "cake," meaning to form a solid mass, often by drying or solidifying.
