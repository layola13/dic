neoplatonist:/ˌniːoʊˈpleɪtənɪst/
n.
新柏拉图主义者
A follower of Neoplatonism, a philosophical system originating in the 3rd century AD, which combined elements of Plato's philosophy with other mystical and religious ideas.
Plotinus is considered the founder of Neoplatonism, and many Neoplatonists followed his teachings.  普罗提诺被认为是新柏拉图主义的创始人，许多新柏拉图主义者都遵循他的教义。

The Neoplatonist believed in a single, supreme source of all being.  这位新柏拉图主义者相信所有存在都有一个单一的、至高无上的源头。

近义词:idealist, mystic
反义词:materialist, realist
词性:noun
