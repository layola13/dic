apathetical:/ˌæpəˈθɛtɪk(ə)l/
adj.
冷漠的，无热情的
Having or showing little or no emotion; indifferent.
He was apathetical to the suffering of others. 他对别人的苦难漠不关心。

近义词: indifferent, uncaring, unconcerned, impassive, passive, listless, lethargic, phlegmatic
反义词: enthusiastic, passionate, caring, concerned, empathetic, sympathetic
词性: adjective
