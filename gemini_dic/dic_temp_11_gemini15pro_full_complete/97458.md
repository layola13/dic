infusorial:/ˌɪnfjʊˈsɔːriəl/
adjective
注入的；浸泡的；草履虫的
Relating to or resembling infusions, especially infusions containing microscopic organisms.  Often used to describe organisms, especially protozoans, found in such infusions.
Infusorial earth is a good filtering agent. 硅藻土是一种良好的过滤剂。
Synonyms: microscopic, protozoan, unicellular
Antonyms: macroscopic, multicellular
词性: adjective
