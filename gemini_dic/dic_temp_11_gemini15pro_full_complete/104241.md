jurisprudent:/ˌdʒʊərɪsˈpruːdnt/| adj.|精通法律的；审慎的|Having or showing good judgment in matters of law; wise and careful in the way you make judgments.|例句：He was a wise and jurisprudent judge. 他是一位明智而审慎的法官。|例句：The jurisprudent lawyer carefully reviewed the contract.  这位精通法律的律师仔细审查了合同。|近义词：wise, judicious, sagacious, prudent, circumspect|反义词：imprudent, unwise, foolish, rash|词性:形容词

词源:
From Latin *iūrisprūdēns*, from *iūs* (“law, right”) + *prūdēns* (“wise, prudent”).
