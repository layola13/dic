nonblending:/nɒnˈblɛndɪŋ/ | adj. | 非混合的，不混合的 | Not blending; incapable of being blended.  Referring to something that does not mix or combine easily with other substances, or to a teaching method that doesn't integrate different subjects. | This paint is nonblending, so be careful not to overlap colors.  这种颜料是不混合的，所以小心不要让颜色重叠。| The school adopted a nonblending approach to teaching math and science. 这所学校采用了一种非混合式的数学和科学教学方法。 | immiscible, unmixable, separate | blending, miscible | adjective
