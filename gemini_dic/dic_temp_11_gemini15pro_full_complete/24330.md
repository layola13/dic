brim:/brɪm/| n. v.| 边缘，帽檐；充满 |The brim of a cup. 杯子的边缘。|The cup was filled to the brim. 杯子装满了。|近义词：edge, rim, brink, margin|反义词：center, middle|noun
brim:/brɪm/| v.| 充满，溢出 |Her eyes brimmed with tears. 她的眼里充满了泪水。|His heart brimmed with happiness. 他心中充满了幸福。|近义词：overflow, fill, teem|反义词：empty, drain|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *brimme, from Old English *brymm (“brim, bank, shore”), of uncertain origin. Cognate with Old Norse barmr (“edge, rim, lip”).
