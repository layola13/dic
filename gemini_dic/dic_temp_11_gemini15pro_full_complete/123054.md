monetarism:/ˈmʌnɪtərɪzəm/
n.|货币主义|A school of thought in economics that emphasizes the role of money supply in controlling inflation and economic growth.  It argues that governments should primarily focus on maintaining price stability through controlling the money supply.|Monetarism was a popular economic policy in the 1980s. 货币主义在20世纪80年代是一种流行的经济政策。|Synonyms: monetary policy, monetarism theory|Antonyms: Keynesianism, fiscal policy|noun
