outcompeting:/ˌaʊtkəmˈpiːtɪŋ/
verb
胜过，超越
defeating someone or something by being more successful or efficient
The company is outcompeting its rivals in the market. 该公司在市场上胜过其竞争对手。


outcompeting:/ˌaʊtkəmˈpiːtɪŋ/
v.
胜过，在竞争中击败
succeeding against someone or something by being better or more efficient
Online retailers are outcompeting traditional brick-and-mortar stores. 在线零售商正在胜过传统的实体店。


outcompeting:/ˌaʊtkəmˈpiːtɪŋ/
动词
胜过；在竞争中击败
to compete more successfully than someone or something else
Smaller companies are struggling to outcompete larger corporations. 小公司正努力在竞争中胜过大公司。



ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + competing, the present participle of compete (from Latin competere “to strive together”).


近义词: outdoing, surpassing, beating, defeating, triumphing over
反义词: losing to, being defeated by, succumbing to
