arraigners:/əˈreɪnərz/
n.
控告者，原告，责难者
A person or group of people who formally accuse someone of a crime in a court of law.
The arraigners presented compelling evidence against the defendant. 控告者提出了针对被告的有力证据。
The judge listened carefully to the arguments of the arraigners. 法官仔细听取了原告的论点。
Synonyms: accusers, prosecutors, indicters
Antonyms: defendants, accused, respondents
noun
