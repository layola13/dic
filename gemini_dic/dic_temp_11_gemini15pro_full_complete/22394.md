boogeyman:/ˈbʊɡimæn/ (US), /ˈbʊɡɪmæn/ (UK)| n. |妖怪，怪物；吓唬小孩的假想怪物|An imaginary monster used to frighten children.|例句：The children were afraid of the boogeyman hiding under their beds. 孩子们害怕躲在床下的妖怪。

例句：Don't be silly, the boogeyman isn't real. 别傻了，妖怪不是真的。|近义词：monster, goblin, ghoul|反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "hero" or "protector" in a narrative context) |词性: noun
