enravish:/ɪnˈrævɪʃ/| vt.|使狂喜；使陶醉；使入迷|To fill with delight, to enchant completely.|Her singing completely enravished the audience. 她的歌声使观众完全陶醉。|近义词：captivate, enchant, fascinate, enthrall|反义词：repel, disgust|词性:动词
Etymology: From Middle French *enraviss-, stem of enravir, from en- + ravir "to seize, ravish," from Latin rapere "to seize, carry off."
