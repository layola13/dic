avidly:/ˈævɪdli/
adverb
热切地；渴望地
With great eagerness and enthusiasm.
She avidly read every book she could find on the subject. 她热切地阅读了她能找到的关于这个主题的每一本书。
eagerly, enthusiastically, keenly
apathetically, indifferently
词性: 副词
