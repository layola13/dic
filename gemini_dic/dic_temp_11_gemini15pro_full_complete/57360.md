dox:/dɒks/ | n. | 文件; 档案; (尤指私人信息的)文件资料 | Documents, especially private information, released publicly. |  Hackers leaked the politician's dox online. 黑客在网上泄露了这位政客的文件。 | synonyms: files, documents, records, papers, data | antonyms:  N/A | noun

dox:/dɒks/ | v. | 公开(某人的)私人信息 | To publicly identify or publish private information about (someone), typically with malicious intent. | They doxed the whistleblower and published his address online. 他们曝光了举报人的身份，并在网上公布了他的地址。 | synonyms: expose, reveal, unmask, out | antonyms: conceal, hide, protect | verb
