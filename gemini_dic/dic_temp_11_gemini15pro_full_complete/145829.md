overdeliberateness:/ˌoʊvərdiˈlɪbərɪtnəs/
|n.| 过于深思熟虑，过度斟酌 |The overdeliberateness of his movements betrayed his nervousness. 他动作的过度斟酌暴露了他的紧张。| English Explanation: The excessive tendency to think carefully or consider something at length, often resulting in slowness or indecision. |例句：His overdeliberateness in making decisions often led to missed opportunities.  他做决定时过于深思熟虑，常常导致错失良机。

例句：Her overdeliberateness in choosing a restaurant annoyed her hungry friends.  她在选择餐厅时过度斟酌，惹恼了她饥肠辘辘的朋友们。

|近义词：hesitation, indecision, deliberation, cautiousness|反义词：impulsiveness, spontaneity, rashness|词性: 名词

词源:  From "over-" (excessive) + "deliberate" (to consider carefully) + "-ness" (noun-forming suffix).  来自 "over-"（过度） + "deliberate"（仔细考虑） + "-ness"（名词后缀）。
