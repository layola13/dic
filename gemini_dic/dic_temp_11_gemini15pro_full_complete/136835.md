nonsilicates:/ˌnɒnˈsɪlɪkeɪts/ | n. |非硅酸盐|Minerals that do not contain silicon and oxygen as their primary components.  They are a diverse group of minerals that include oxides, sulfides, halides, carbonates, phosphates, and native elements.|例句：Many valuable ores are nonsilicates, such as galena (lead sulfide). 许多有价值的矿石是非硅酸盐，例如方铅矿（硫化铅）。|例句：Nonsilicates play an important role in various geological processes. 非硅酸盐在各种地质过程中发挥着重要作用。|近义词：non-silicate minerals|反义词：silicates|词性：名词
