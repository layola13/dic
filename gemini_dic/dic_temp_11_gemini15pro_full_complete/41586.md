corporealness:/kɔːrˈpɔːriəlnəs/
n.
物质性；肉体性；形体性
The state or quality of being corporeal; having a physical body.
Her corporealness was undeniable; she was flesh and blood. 她的肉体存在是不可否认的；她是血肉之躯。
The painting captured the corporealness of the fruit, making it look almost edible. 这幅画捕捉到了水果的质感，使它看起来几乎可以食用。
近义词：physicality, materiality, tangibility
反义词：immateriality, spirituality, incorporeality
词性：noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *corporalnes*, from Old French *corporal*, from Latin *corporalis* (“of or belonging to the body”), from *corpus* (“body”).  The suffix *-ness* forms nouns expressing a state or quality.
