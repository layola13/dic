disaffection:/ˌdɪsəˈfɛkʃən/| n. |不满，不忠，疏远|A state of dissatisfaction or alienation, especially from authority or from a cause one previously supported.|The growing disaffection of the troops worried the general. 部队的日益不满令将军担忧。|Synonyms: alienation, disloyalty, estrangement, resentment, dissatisfaction|Antonyms: loyalty, affection, devotion|noun

disaffected:/ˌdɪsəˈfɛktɪd/| adj. |不满的，不忠的，疏远的|Feeling or showing dissatisfaction or alienation, especially from authority or from a cause one previously supported.|The disaffected workers went on strike. 不满的工人举行了罢工。|Synonyms: alienated, disloyal, estranged, resentful, dissatisfied|Antonyms: loyal, affectionate, devoted|adjective
