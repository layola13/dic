buttweld:/ˈbʌtwɛld/| n. v.|对焊，对接焊|A weld formed by joining two pieces of metal end to end.|The pipes were connected by a buttweld.|近义词：butt joint,  end weld|反义词: N/A|词性:名词, 动词

Etymology: "butt" (meaning "end") + "weld" (meaning "to join metal by heating")
