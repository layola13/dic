infinitesimally:/ˌɪnfɪnɪˈtɛsɪməli/| adv.| 极微地，无限小地 | Extremely small; so small as to be almost immeasurable. | The dose was increased infinitesimally. 剂量极微地增加了。| The car moved infinitesimally forward. 车子向前移动了极其微小的一段距离。| 近义词: microscopically, minutely, negligibly | 反义词: considerably, substantially, significantly | 词性: 副词

词源: From Late Latin *infinitesimalis, from infinit(us) "infinite" + -esimus (suffix forming ordinal numbers) + -alis (adjectival suffix).
