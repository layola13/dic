nonzealousness:/ˌnɑːnˈzɛləsnəs/
noun
缺乏热情，不热心
Lack of zeal or enthusiasm.
His nonzealousness towards the project worried his team.  他对这个项目缺乏热情，让他的团队很担心。
The nonzealousness of the crowd made the speaker feel discouraged.  观众的冷淡让演讲者感到气馁。
apathy, indifference, lack of enthusiasm
zeal, enthusiasm, fervor
