hydrobromic:/ˌhaɪdrəˈbroʊmɪk/| adj.|氢溴酸的；溴化的|Of or relating to hydrogen bromide.  Containing hydrogen and bromine.|Hydrobromic acid is a strong acid. 氢溴酸是一种强酸。|近义词:bromhydric|反义词: N/A|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From hydro- + bromic (relating to bromine).  "Hydro-" comes from Greek "hydōr" (water), referring to hydrogen's role in forming water. "Bromic" derives from "bromine," ultimately from Greek "brōmos" (stench), due to bromine's strong and unpleasant odor.
