kinds:/kaɪndz/
|n.|种类；类型|A category of things distinguished by some common characteristic or quality. |There are many different kinds of flowers in the garden. 花园里有许多不同种类的花。|近义词:types, sorts, varieties, classes|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用"uniformity"表示单一性)|词性:名词

kinds:/kaɪndz/
|n.|性质；本性|The essential quality or character of something. (通常用单数kind)|It's not in his kind to be rude.  他本性并不粗鲁。|近义词:nature, character, essence|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|词性:名词

Related to kin.
