fairiest:/ˈfeəriɪst/| adj.|最美丽的；最迷人的；最像仙女的|Superlative form of 'fairy'.  Meaning the most enchanting, magical, or delicate.|She had the fairest complexion of all the princesses. 她拥有所有公主中最白皙的肤色。|近义词：most beautiful, most enchanting, most magical|反义词：ugliest, plainest|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *fairest, from Old English fægerest, fægrost (“fairest”), superlative of fæger (“fair, beautiful, handsome; pleasant, agreeable; clean, pure”). Cognate with West Frisian fægerst, Dutch vaagst (“faintest”), German feist (“fat, plump”). More at fair.
