advisership:/ədˈvaɪzərʃɪp/
n.
顾问的职位；顾问身份
The position or role of being an advisor.
She accepted an advisership to the United Nations. 她接受了担任联合国顾问的职位。
He held an advisership with the government on economic policy. 他担任政府经济政策顾问。
Synonyms: advisory position, consultancy, consultant role, mentorship
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, consider: client, advisee, mentee)
noun
