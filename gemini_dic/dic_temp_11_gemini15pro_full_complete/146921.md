overintellectualizes: /ˌoʊvərˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
过度思考, 过于理性化
thinks about something in an excessively intellectual way, often to the point of obscuring or missing the simpler truth or practicality of a matter.
He overintellectualizes everything and misses the simple joys in life. 他对所有事情都过度思考，错过了生活中简单的快乐。
overthinks, analyzes excessively, intellectualizes too much
underthinks, simplifies
v.
