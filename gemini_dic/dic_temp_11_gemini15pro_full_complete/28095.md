/ˈkæpɪtl/ | /ˈkæpɪtəl/ | n. adj. | 首都，资本，大写字母 |  A city serving as the seat of government of a country, state, or province; wealth in the form of money or other assets owned by a person or organization or available for investment; (of a letter of the alphabet) large in size and of the form used to begin sentences and names. |  The capital of France is Paris. 法国的首都是巴黎。 | synonyms: chief city, metropolis, funds, resources, investment | antonyms: province, income, lowercase | noun

/ˈkæpɪtl/ | /ˈkæpɪtəl/ | adj. | 首都的，主要的，极好的 |  Relating to or being the seat of government; (of an offense or charge) liable to the death penalty; excellent; first-rate. | It was a capital idea to go to the beach. 去海滩是个极好的主意。 | synonyms: main, chief, principal, excellent, first-rate | antonyms: minor, secondary, unimportant, poor, terrible | adjective
