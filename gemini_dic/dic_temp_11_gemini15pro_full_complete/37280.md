commandeer:/ˌkɒmənˈdɪə(r)/ /ˌkɑːmənˈdɪr/ | vt. | 征用，强占 | To seize (something, especially a vehicle or building) for military or official use.| The army commandeered all the trucks to transport supplies. 军队征用了所有的卡车来运输物资。| synonyms: seize, confiscate, appropriate, expropriate | antonyms: return, release, relinquish | verb
