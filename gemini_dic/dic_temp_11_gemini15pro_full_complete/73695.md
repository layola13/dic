forgivers:/fərˈɡɪvərz/
n.
原谅者，宽恕者
People who forgive others; those who grant pardon or remission of an offense or debt.
The forgivers showed great compassion and understanding.  原谅者展现了极大的同情和理解。
Nelson Mandela is seen by many as one of history's greatest forgivers.  纳尔逊·曼德拉被许多人视为历史上最伟大的宽恕者之一。
近义词:pardoners, remitters
反义词:avengers, punishers
词性:名词

词源:
From "forgive" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Forgive" comes from Old English "forgiefan," meaning "to give up, remit (a debt, sin, etc.), pardon, grant."  来自“forgive”+“-er”（施动者后缀）。“Forgive”来自古英语“forgiefan”，意思是“放弃、免除（债务、罪过等）、原谅、授予”。
