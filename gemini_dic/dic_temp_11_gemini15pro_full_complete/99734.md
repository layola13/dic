intermarriage:/ˌɪntərˈmærɪdʒ/ | n. |通婚，异族结婚|Marriage between people of different social groups, races, or religions.|例句：Intermarriage between different religious groups has become more common. 不同宗教群体之间的通婚变得越来越普遍。

例句：The intermarriage rate between different ethnic groups is rising. 不同种族群体之间的通婚率正在上升。|近义词：miscegenation, cross-cultural marriage |反义词:endogamy |词性:noun
