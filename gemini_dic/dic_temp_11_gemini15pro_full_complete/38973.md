词源:
From "confinedness," which is derived from "confine" + "-ness."  "Confine" comes from Old French "confine" (border, limit), from Latin "confinis" (bordering, adjacent), from "com-" (together) + "finis" (end, limit).  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
