abate:/əˈbeɪt/ | v. | 减少，减轻，缓和 | To become less intense or widespread. | The storm began to abate.  暴风雨开始减弱。|  The pain in his leg abated after he took the medicine.  他服药后腿部的疼痛减轻了。 |  subside, diminish, lessen, decrease, wane | increase, intensify, escalate | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *abattre*, "to beat down," from *a-* (to) + *battre* (to beat).  The meaning shifted from "to beat down" to "to lessen in intensity."
