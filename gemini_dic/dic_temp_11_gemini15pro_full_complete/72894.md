词源:  From Middle English *folewing, *following, from Old English folgian (“to follow”), from Proto-Germanic *fulgijaną (“to follow”), from Proto-Indo-European *plēḱ- (“to plait, weave”). Cognate with Dutch volgen (“to follow”), German folgen (“to follow”), Swedish följa (“to follow”).
