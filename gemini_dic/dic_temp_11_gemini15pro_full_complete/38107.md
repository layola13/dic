compounders:/kəmˈpaʊndərz/ | n. |  复利者；调配者；混合者；加重者 |  A person or thing that compounds something, especially a pharmacist who prepares medicines, or a machine that mixes ingredients. | The compounders carefully measured the ingredients for the medicine. 调配师仔细测量了药物的成分。|  The market volatility was further compounded by the news.  市场波动因这一消息而进一步加剧。 | Synonyms: mixer, blender, combiner, aggravator | Antonyms: separator, divider, simplifier | noun

compounders:/kəmˈpaʊndərz/ | v. |  使恶化；使加重；混合；调配 | To make something worse; to combine; to mix. |  His financial problems were compounded by the loss of his job. 他的经济问题因失业而雪上加霜。 | The pharmacist compounded the medication according to the doctor's prescription. 药剂师根据医生的处方配制了药物。 | Synonyms: worsen, aggravate, exacerbate, combine, mix | Antonyms: alleviate, improve, simplify, separate | verb
