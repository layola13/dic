hawkishness:/ˈhɔːkɪʃnəs/| n.|强硬态度，鹰派作风|The president's hawkishness on foreign policy worried some voters. 总统在外交政策上的强硬态度让一些选民感到担忧。|例句：Her hawkishness on defense spending is well known. 她对国防开支的强硬态度是众所周知的。|近义词：aggressiveness, belligerence|反义词：dovishness, pacifism|词性：名词

Etymology:
From hawkish + -ness.  Hawkish itself comes from hawk (n.), referring to the bird of prey, which has been used metaphorically to describe those who favor aggressive or warlike policies.
