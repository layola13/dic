ETYMOLOGY->
Acronym derived from English as a Second Language.  "English" from Old English "Englisc,"  referring to the Angles, one of the Germanic peoples who settled in Britain.  "Second" from Middle English, from Old French "second," ultimately from Latin "secundus" meaning "following."  "Language" from Old French "langage," ultimately from Latin "lingua" meaning "tongue."
