dracanth:/drəˈkænθ/
n.
龙血树; 龙血树脂
A dracaena tree, especially Dracaena draco, which yields a reddish resin.  The resin itself is also called dracanth.
Example:  The apothecary used dracanth in his medicinal preparations. 药剂师在他的药剂中使用了龙血树脂。

Example: The dracanth tree is native to the Canary Islands. 龙血树原产于加那利群岛。
Synonym: dragon's blood
Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a specific tree resin.  Could consider antonyms for "resin" itself, like "solvent" in certain contexts, but this is a stretch.)
词性: 名词
