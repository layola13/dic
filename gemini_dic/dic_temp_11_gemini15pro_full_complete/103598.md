joiners:/ˈdʒɔɪnərz/
n.|连接者，细木工人|People who connect things or people, or skilled woodworkers who make furniture and other wooden items.|The joiners are busy assembling the new cabinets.  细木工们正忙着组装新橱柜。|synonyms: carpenters, woodworkers, assemblers, connectors|antonyms: dividers, separators|noun

joiner:/ˈdʒɔɪnər/
n.|连接者，细木工人|A person who connects things or people, or a skilled woodworker who makes furniture and other wooden items.|The joiner carefully crafted the wooden table.  细木工精心制作了木桌。|synonyms: carpenter, woodworker, assembler, connector|antonyms: divider, separator|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *joignour*, from Anglo-Norman *joignour*, from Old French *joindre* (to join), from Latin *iungere* (to join, yoke).
