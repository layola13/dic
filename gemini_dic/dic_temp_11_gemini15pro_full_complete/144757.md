over-communication:/ˌoʊvər kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度沟通，过度交流 | The act of communicating excessively, often to the point of redundancy or inefficiency. |  Example: Over-communication can sometimes lead to confusion rather than clarity. 过度沟通有时会导致混乱而不是清晰。| Synonym: oversharing, excessive communication | Antonym: under-communication, lack of communication | noun

over-communication:/ˌoʊvər kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度沟通，过度交流 |  The tendency to share too much information, often unnecessarily. | Example:  In an attempt to be transparent, the manager's over-communication overwhelmed the team. 为了保持透明，经理的过度沟通让团队不堪重负。 | Synonym: verbose communication, information overload | Antonym: conciseness, brevity | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"Over-" (prefix) + "communication"
"Over-" comes from Old English "ofer," meaning "above, beyond, across."
"Communication" comes from Latin "communicatio," meaning "sharing, imparting."
