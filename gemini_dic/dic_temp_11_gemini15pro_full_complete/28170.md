caponize:/ˈkeɪpəˌnaɪz/ | vt. | 阉割（雄性家禽，尤指公鸡），使失去活力 | To castrate (a male domestic fowl, especially a cockerel) or to deprive of vigor. |  The farmer caponized the roosters to make them fatter and more tender.  农民阉割了公鸡，使它们更肥更嫩。

|Synonyms: castrate, geld, neuter, emasculate|Antonyms: invigorate, strengthen, empower|verb|

caponize:/ˈkeɪpəˌnaɪz/ | n. | 阉割 | The act of castrating a male domestic fowl. | The caponize of the roosters is done early in their lives. 公鸡的阉割是在它们生命早期进行的。|Synonyms: castration, gelding, neutering, emasculation|Antonyms: invigoration, strengthening, empowerment|noun|
