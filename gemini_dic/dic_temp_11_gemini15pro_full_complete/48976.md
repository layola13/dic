degenerative:/dɪˈdʒen.ər.ə.tɪv/
adjective
退化的；变性的
Characterized by progressive deterioration and loss of function in the organs or tissues.
Her grandmother suffers from a degenerative disease. 她祖母患有退行性疾病。
deteriorating, worsening, declining
regenerative, restorative
