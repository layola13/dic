decampment:/dɪˈkæmpmənt/
n.
撤营；突然离去
The sudden decampment of the circus left the town feeling deserted.
马戏团的突然撤离让小镇感觉空荡荡的。
The family's decampment from the city to the countryside was a welcome change.
这家人从城市搬到乡村是一个受欢迎的变化。
departure, evacuation, exit
arrival, entrance
noun
