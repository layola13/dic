executant:/ˌɛksɪˈkjuːtənt/ | n. | 执行者；演奏者；表演者 | A person who carries out or performs something, especially a piece of music or other work of art. | The pianist is a highly skilled executant.  这位钢琴家是一位技艺高超的演奏者。| Synonyms: performer, player, musician, interpreter, artist | Antonyms: composer, creator, originator | noun

executant:/ˌɛksɪˈkjuːtənt/ | adj. | 执行的；演奏的 | Relating to or involving execution or performance. | Her executant skills on the violin are impressive. 她在小提琴演奏方面的技巧令人印象深刻。| Synonyms: performing, playing, interpretive | Antonyms: creative, composing | adjective
