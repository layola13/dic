outclassed: /ˌaʊtˈklɑːst/ /ˌaʊtˈklæst/
Verb|超越，胜过，远胜于|To be clearly superior to someone or something else in skill, quality, or achievement.|例句：The local team was completely outclassed by the visitors.  本地队被客队彻底击败了。|近义词：surpass, excel, outdo, outperform, eclipse|反义词:  underperform, lose to|词性:v.

outclassed: /ˌaʊtˈklɑːst/ /ˌaʊtˈklæst/
Adjective|被超越的，被胜过的|Clearly surpassed in skill, quality, or achievement.|例句：He felt completely outclassed by the other competitors.  他感觉自己完全被其他选手超越了。|近义词：outmatched, overshadowed|反义词: superior, dominant|词性:adj.
