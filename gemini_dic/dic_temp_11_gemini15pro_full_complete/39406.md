conker:/ˈkɒŋkə(r)/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋkər/ (US)| n.| 七叶树果实（用线穿起来玩的一种儿童游戏），斗栗子游戏|A hard, shiny, brown nut that grows on horse chestnut trees, often used by children in a game.  The game itself is also called conkers.|例句：
The children were playing conkers in the playground. 孩子们在操场上玩斗栗子游戏。

He won the conker championship. 他赢得了斗栗子比赛冠军。|近义词：horse chestnut, buckeye (US)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
