furlough:/ˈfɜːrləʊ/ (US) /ˈfɜːləʊ/ (UK)| n. v.|休假；暂时解雇|A period of time when an employee is told not to come to work, usually because there is not enough work, but they are still employed and are expected to return to work later.|Many companies were forced to furlough employees during the pandemic. 许多公司在大流行期间被迫让员工休假。| The company furloughed hundreds of workers.  公司让数百名工人休假了。|近义词：layoff, leave of absence|反义词：hire, recall|词性:名词,动词
