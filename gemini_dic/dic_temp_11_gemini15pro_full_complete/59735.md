eco-geographic:/ˌiːkoʊˌdʒiːəˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
生态地理的
Relating to both ecological and geographical factors, or the relationship between them.
The eco-geographic distribution of the species is complex.  该物种的生态地理分布很复杂。
Eco-geographic factors played a key role in the evolution of the species. 生态地理因素在该物种的进化中起到了关键作用。
近义词:biogeographic, ecological, geographical
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: adjective
