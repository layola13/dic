conceitedness:/kənˈsiːtɪdnəs/
n.
自负；骄傲自大
The state of being excessively proud and vain.
His conceitedness made it difficult for him to accept criticism. 他的自负让他难以接受批评。
conceit, vanity, arrogance, egotism, pride
humility, modesty, humbleness
noun
