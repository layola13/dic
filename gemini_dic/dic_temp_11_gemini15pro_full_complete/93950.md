immunosuppression:/ˌɪmjuːnoʊsəˈprɛʃən/ | n. |免疫抑制|The reduction or suppression of the body's immune response.  This can be intentionally induced with drugs, as in preparation for organ transplantation, or it can occur as a result of disease or other conditions.| Patients undergoing organ transplantation require immunosuppression to prevent rejection.  器官移植患者需要免疫抑制以防止排斥反应。|synonyms: immunodepression, immunosuppressive therapy | antonyms: immunostimulation, immunoenhancement | noun


immunosuppressions:/ˌɪmjuːnoʊsəˈprɛʃənz/ | n. |免疫抑制（复数）|Multiple instances of the reduction or suppression of the body's immune response.|  The various immunosuppressions used in the study had differing side effects.  该研究中使用的各种免疫抑制方法有不同的副作用。 |synonyms: immunodepressions | antonyms: immunostimulations | noun
