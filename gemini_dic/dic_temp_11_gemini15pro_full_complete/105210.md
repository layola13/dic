keynoters:/ˈkiːnoʊtərz/
n.
主题演讲者；主要发言人
People who deliver keynote speeches, often at conferences or conventions.  They set the main theme or tone of an event.
The keynoters at the technology conference inspired the attendees with their visions of the future.  技术大会上的主题演讲者用他们对未来的憧憬激励了与会者。
speakers, presenters, lecturers
listeners, audience
