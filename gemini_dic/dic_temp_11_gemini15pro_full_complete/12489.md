asperges:/əˈspɜːrdʒɪz/ /æˈspɜːrdʒɪz/ | n. |圣水掸；圣水礼；圣枝主日前的星期日 |A liturgical ceremony in which the congregation is sprinkled with holy water, especially on Palm Sunday.  A brush used for sprinkling holy water.  Palm Sunday or the Sunday before Palm Sunday. |The priest performed the asperges before Mass. 神父在弥撒前进行了圣水礼。|近义词：sprinkling, benediction |反义词: N/A |词性: Noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *asperges (plural), from Latin aspergere "to sprinkle," from ad- "to" + spargere "to scatter."
