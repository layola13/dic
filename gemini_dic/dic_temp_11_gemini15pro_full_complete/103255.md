jewelling:/ˈdʒuːəlɪŋ/
n.
珠宝镶嵌; 宝石装饰; 装宝石
The act or process of setting jewels in a piece of jewelry or other object.  Adding jewels to something for decoration.
The jewelling on the crown was breathtaking.  王冠上的珠宝镶嵌令人叹为观止。
近义词：adornment, ornamentation, decoration
反义词：plainness, simplicity
