duchess:/ˈdʌtʃɪs/| n.|公爵夫人；女公爵|A duchess is the wife or widow of a duke, or a woman holding a rank equivalent to duke in her own right. | The duchess presided over the charity gala. 公爵夫人主持了慈善晚会。|  The Duchess of Cambridge visited the hospital.  剑桥公爵夫人参观了医院。 |近义词：noblewoman, peeress|反义词: commoner|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *ducesse, from duc (“duke”) + -esse (feminine suffix), from Latin dux (“leader”) + -issa (feminine suffix).
