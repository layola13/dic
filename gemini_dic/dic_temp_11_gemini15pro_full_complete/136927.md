nonsovereign:/nɒnˈsɒvrɪn/ | adj. | 非主权的；非独立自主的 | Not possessing supreme or independent political power.  A nonsovereign entity is typically subject to the authority of another political entity. |  Puerto Rico is a nonsovereign territory of the United States. 波多黎各是美国的非主权领土。

The Basque Country in Spain has a nonsovereign status. 西班牙的巴斯克地区拥有非主权地位。| 近义词: dependent, subordinate, autonomous (sometimes) | 反义词: sovereign, independent | 词性: 形容词
