antiatheist:/ˌæntiˈeɪθiɪst/ | n. | 反无神论者；反对无神论的人 | Someone who opposes atheism.  An antiatheist may or may not believe in God, but they believe atheism is wrong or harmful. | He considers himself an antiatheist, arguing that atheism leads to moral decay. 他自认为是反无神论者，认为无神论导致道德沦丧。 |  Near synonyms: critic of atheism, opponent of atheism | Near antonyms: atheist, secularist | noun

antiatheist:/ˌæntiˈeɪθiɪst/ | adj. | 反无神论的 | Opposing or criticizing atheism. | He holds an antiatheist stance, believing that faith is essential for society. 他持有反无神论的立场，认为信仰对社会至关重要。 | Near synonyms:  anti-atheistic | Near antonyms: atheistic, secular | adjective
