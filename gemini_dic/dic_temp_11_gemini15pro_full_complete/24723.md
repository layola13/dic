bronzers:/ˈbrɑːnzərz/
n.
古铜色化妆品; 修容粉;
A powder, cream, or lotion applied to the skin to give it a tanned appearance.
She used bronzers to give her face a sun-kissed glow. 她用修容粉让她的脸看起来像是被阳光亲吻过一样。
synonyms: tanning lotion, sunless tanner, self-tanner
antonyms:  skin lightener, bleach (for skin), whitening cream
词性: 名词
