hypervigilant:/ˌhaɪpərˈvɪdʒɪlənt/| adj.|过度警觉的，高度警惕的|Characterized by an excessive or heightened state of alertness, often due to anxiety, paranoia, or trauma.  Being constantly on guard and scanning for potential threats, even when there is no real danger.|The soldier, after returning from war, remained hypervigilant, startled by loud noises.|近义词：watchful, wary, alert, vigilant, on guard|反义词：calm, relaxed, oblivious, unaware|词性:形容词

词源:
From hyper- + vigilant.  "Hyper-" is a prefix meaning "over, excessive," while "vigilant" comes from the Latin "vigilans," meaning "watchful, alert."
