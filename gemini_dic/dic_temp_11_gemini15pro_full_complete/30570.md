cerated:/ˈsɪəreɪtɪd/ /ˈsɛrəteɪtɪd/| adj.|锯齿状的，有锯齿的|Having a notched edge or margin, like a saw.|The leaf had a cerated edge. 这片叶子边缘呈锯齿状。|近义词：serrated, notched, toothed|反义词:smooth, even|词性:形容词
词源：From Latin *ceratus "covered with wax," from cera "wax," which became associated with "serrated" (from serra "saw") due to the similar shapes and sounds.
