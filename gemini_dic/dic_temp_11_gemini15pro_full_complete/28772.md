carnot:/kɑːrˈnoʊ/| adj. n.|卡诺的，卡诺循环|Relating to or denoting Nicolas Léonard Sadi Carnot (1796–1832), French physicist, or his work, especially the Carnot cycle.|The Carnot efficiency is the theoretical maximum efficiency that a heat engine can achieve during the conversion of heat into work, or conversely, the efficiency of a refrigeration system in creating a temperature difference by the application of work to the system. 卡诺效率是热机在将热量转化为功的过程中所能达到的理论最大效率，反之亦然，即制冷系统通过对系统施加功来产生温差的效率。|近义词:thermodynamic 热力学的|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective, noun
