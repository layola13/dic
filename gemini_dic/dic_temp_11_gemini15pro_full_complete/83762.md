hairbrained:/ˈhɛərbreɪnd/| adj.|轻率的；愚蠢的；不切实际的|Characterized by a lack of thought or good judgment; foolish and impractical.|He had a hairbrained scheme to get rich quickly. 他有一个轻率的快速致富计划。|例句：That's a hairbrained idea!  那真是一个愚蠢的主意！|近义词：foolish, silly, reckless, ill-conceived, harebrained|反义词：sensible, wise, prudent|词性:形容词
