barker:/ˈbɑːrkər/ | n. |叫卖者；狂吠的狗|A person who barks or a dog that barks frequently. | The carnival barker tried to lure people into the tent.  嘉年华的叫卖者试图引诱人们进入帐篷。| My neighbor's dog is a real barker; he never shuts up.  我邻居的狗真是个爱叫的家伙；他从来不停嘴。| Synonyms:  hawker, peddler, yeller (for people); yelper (for dogs) | Antonyms:  whisperer, murmurer (for people); quiet dog (for dogs)| noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *barkere*, from Old English *beorcere* "one who barks," agent noun from *beorcan* "to bark."
