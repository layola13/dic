axle's:/ˈæksəlz/
noun
车轴的
Possessive form of "axle," referring to something belonging to or associated with an axle.
The axle's grease needs to be replaced.  车轴的润滑脂需要更换。
One of the axle's bearings is worn out.  其中一个车轴的轴承磨损了。
Synonym: shaft's, spindle's
Antonym: (No direct antonym for a possessive form.  Consider antonyms for the base word "axle" such as "rim" or "wheel" but these don't directly relate to the possessive form)
