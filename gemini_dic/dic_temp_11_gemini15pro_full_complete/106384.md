conceal:/kənˈsiːl/| v. |隐藏，隐瞒|To keep something secret or hidden.|She tried to conceal her nervousness. 她试图掩饰自己的紧张。|He concealed the weapon under his coat. 他把武器藏在外套下面。|近义词：hide, disguise, mask, cover|反义词:reveal, expose, divulge|动词

concealment:/kənˈsiːlmənt/| n. |隐藏，隐瞒|The act of hiding or keeping something secret.|The concealment of evidence is a crime. 隐藏证据是犯罪。|The thief's concealment was clever, but the police eventually found him. 小偷的藏身之处很巧妙，但警察最终还是找到了他。|近义词：hiding, secrecy, disguise|反义词:disclosure, revelation|名词
