brambliest:/ˈbræmbliɪst/
adjective
最多荆棘的，最棘手的
covered with or characterized by a dense growth of brambles; most troublesome or difficult.
The brambliest path led to the hidden clearing.  最多荆棘的小路通向隐藏的空地。
The legal case was the brambliest he had ever encountered.  这件法律案件是他遇到的最棘手的案件。
近义词:thorniest, prickliest, most difficult
反义词:smoothest, easiest, simplest
词性:adjective
词源:From bramble + -y + -est.  Bramble comes from Old English bræmbel.
