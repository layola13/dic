detectors:/dɪˈtɛktərz/| n. |探测器，检测器|A device or instrument designed to detect the presence of something, such as light, radiation, or a change in condition.|The smoke detectors went off when the toast burned. 烤面包片烤糊时，烟雾探测器响了。|synonyms: sensors, monitors, scanners|antonyms: concealers, hiders|noun

detect:/dɪˈtɛkt/| v. |探测，检测，发现|To discover or identify the presence or existence of something.|The scientists detected a new planet. 科学家们发现了一颗新的行星。|synonyms: discover, find, identify, sense, perceive|antonyms: miss, overlook, conceal|verb
