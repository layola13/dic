chanceries:/ˈtʃɑːnsəriz/ /ˈtʃænsəriz/
n.
法院, 高等法院, 大法官法庭,（政府的）外交部门
A chancery is a court of equity, as distinguished from a common-law court.  It can also refer to the office or department of a chancellor or the building where such an office is located. Additionally, it can denote the administrative office of an embassy or consulate.
The case was heard in the chancery court.  该案件由衡平法院审理。
The embassy's chancery is located in the capital city. 大使馆的行政办公室位于首都。
近义词: court, tribunal, department, office
反义词: (No direct antonym, depends on the specific meaning used)
词性: 名词 (Noun)

chancery:/ˈtʃɑːnsəriz/ /ˈtʃænsəriz/
n.
档案室；档案馆
A chancery can also refer to a place where official documents or records are kept.
The ancient documents were discovered in the chancery.  这些古代文献是在档案馆里发现的。
The chancery holds records dating back centuries.  档案馆保存着几个世纪前的记录。
近义词: archive, record office, registry
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: 名词 (Noun)
