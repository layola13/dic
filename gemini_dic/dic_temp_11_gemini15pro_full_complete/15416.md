bactericide: /bækˈtɪərɪsaɪd/ | adj. | 杀菌的 | Having the capacity to kill bacteria. | The bactericidal properties of the cleaner make it ideal for hospital use.  这种清洁剂的杀菌特性使其非常适合医院使用。 | synonyms: disinfectant, antiseptic, germicide | antonyms: bacteriostatic | adjective
