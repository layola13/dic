heliostat:/ˈhiːliəˌstæt/ | n. |定日镜，日光反射镜|A device with a mirror that turns so as to keep reflecting sunlight toward a predetermined target.|The heliostat tracked the sun across the sky, focusing its rays onto the solar receiver. 定日镜跟踪天空中的太阳，将其光线聚焦到太阳能接收器上。|Synonyms: solar reflector, mirror, sun tracker|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
