cosponsored:/koʊˈspɑːnsərd/ |v. |共同赞助；联合主办|A bill cosponsored by several senators. |Several organizations cosponsored the conference.| jointly sponsored, supported | opposed |verb
Etymology-> From co- + sponsored, past participle of sponsor. "Sponsor" comes from the Old French "esponsor" and ultimately from the Latin "spondere" (to promise solemnly).
