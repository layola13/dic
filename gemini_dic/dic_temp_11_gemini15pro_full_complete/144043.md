outrighter:/ˈaʊtraɪtər/| n.|彻底的拥护者；完全的信徒；直言不讳的人|A person who speaks frankly and directly, or who supports a cause completely and without reservation.|He was an outrighter for animal rights, never hesitating to protest or speak his mind. 他是一位动物权利的彻底拥护者，从不犹豫抗议或表达自己的想法。|近义词：advocate, supporter, champion, proponent|反义词：opponent, adversary, critic|词性: 名词

outrighter:/ˈaʊtraɪtər/| adv.|彻底地；完全地；直率地|Completely; entirely; without reservation or qualification.|He denied the accusations outrightly. 他彻底否认了这些指控。|近义词：completely, totally, entirely, absolutely|反义词：partially, hesitantly|词性: 副词
