housemasters:/ˈhaʊsmɑːstərz/
n.
舍监，宿舍管理员
A teacher responsible for the welfare of students in a particular house at a boarding school.
The housemasters were responsible for ensuring the boys' safety and well-being. 舍监们负责确保男孩们的安全和健康。
Mr. Smith is a strict but fair housemaster. 史密斯先生是一位严格而公正的舍监。
synonyms: dorm supervisor, resident advisor, house parent
antonyms: student, pupil
