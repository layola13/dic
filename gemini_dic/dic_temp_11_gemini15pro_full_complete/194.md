sabbatical: /səˈbætɪkəl/ | n. adj. | 休假，公休假 | A period of paid leave granted to a university teacher or other worker for study or travel, traditionally one year for every seven years worked. |  She took a sabbatical to write her novel. 她休假去写小说了。|  He's on sabbatical this semester. 他这学期休假。| 近义词: leave, break, rest | 反义词: work | 词性: 名词，形容词
