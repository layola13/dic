avocado:/ˌævəˈkɑːdoʊ/ (US), /ˌævəˈkɑːdəʊ/ (UK)| n. |牛油果，鳄梨|A pear-shaped fruit with a rough leathery skin, smooth oily edible flesh, and a large stone.|I love avocado toast. 我喜欢牛油果吐司。|Synonyms: alligator pear|Antonyms: N/A|noun

avocado:/ˌævəˈkɑːdoʊ/ (US), /ˌævəˈkɑːdəʊ/ (UK)| adj. |牛油果色的，鳄梨色的|Having the color of an avocado; a light yellowish-green color.|She wore an avocado-colored dress. 她穿了一件牛油果色的连衣裙。|Synonyms:  pale green, chartreuse|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
