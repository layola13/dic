bushfighter:/ˈbʊʃˌfaɪtər/| n.| 丛林战士; 游击队员 |A soldier or guerrilla fighter who operates in wooded or jungle terrain.|  The bushfighters were skilled at camouflage and moving stealthily through the dense forest. 丛林战士擅长伪装和在茂密的森林中秘密行动。

|例句：The bushfighters knew the terrain intimately and used it to their advantage. 游击队员对地形了如指掌，并利用它来获得优势。

|例句：The film depicted the struggles of a group of bushfighters against a tyrannical regime. 这部电影描绘了一群丛林战士对抗暴虐政权的斗争。

|近义词：guerrilla, partisan, insurgent, rebel|反义词:regular soldier, government troop|词性:noun
