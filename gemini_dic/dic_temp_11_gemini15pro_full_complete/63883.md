epilogued:/ˈɛpɪlɔːɡd/
v.
为...写结语；以结语结束
provided with an epilogue; concluded.
The author epilogued his novel with a poignant reflection on the characters' fates. 作者在他的小说结尾处，对人物的命运进行了深刻的反思。
近义词: concluded, finished, ended
反义词:  began, commenced, initiated
词性: 动词

epilogue:/ˈɛpɪlɔːɡ/
n.
结语；收场白
a section or speech at the end of a book or play that serves as a comment on or a conclusion to what has happened.
The epilogue of the play tied up all the loose ends.  这部戏剧的结语把所有的未了结的事情都交代清楚了。
近义词: afterword, postscript, conclusion
反义词: prologue, preface, introduction
词性: 名词

词源: From Late Latin *epilogus, from Greek epilogos "conclusion of a speech," from epi- "upon, in addition" + logos "speech, word, discourse."
