asomatous:/ˌeɪˈsɒmətəs/
adjective
无形的；非物质的；无实体的
Lacking a material body; incorporeal.
The asomatous spirits floated through the graveyard. 无形的灵魂飘过墓地。
近义词:incorporeal, immaterial, disembodied, spiritual, ethereal
反义词:corporeal, material, physical, tangible, embodied
词性:adjective

Etymology:  From Greek *a- “not” + *sōma “body” + *-atos “having the character of”.
