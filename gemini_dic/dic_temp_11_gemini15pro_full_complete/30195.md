celestite: /ˈsɛlɪstaɪt/ /səˈlɛstaɪt/ | n. | 天青石 | A mineral consisting of strontium sulfate, typically pale blue in color.  It is often found in sedimentary rocks and is used as a source of strontium. | Celestite geodes are prized by collectors for their beautiful blue crystals. 天青石晶洞因其美丽的蓝色晶体而受到收藏家的珍视。| The museum has a stunning display of celestite from Madagascar.  博物馆展出了令人惊叹的来自马达加斯加的天青石。 | 近义词: strontium sulfate | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Latin <i>caelestis</i> "heavenly" + <i>-ite</i> (mineral suffix).  The name refers to the pale blue color often exhibited by the mineral.
