frequent:/ˈfriːkwənt/ (US) /ˈfriːkwənt/ (UK)| adj. |频繁的，经常的|Happening often; occurring repeatedly.|She is a frequent visitor to the library. 她经常去图书馆。|近义词: common, regular, usual, habitual, often |反义词: rare, infrequent, occasional, seldom |词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *frequentēs* (nominative *frequēns*), present active participle of *frequentāre* (“to visit often, resort to, haunt, crowd, people, frequent”), from *frequēns* (“crowded, numerous, frequent”), perhaps ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰerǵʰ-* (“high, elevated”).
