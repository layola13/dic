cineraria:/ˌsɪnəˈreəriə/ | n. | 瓜叶菊; 富贵菊; 千里光 | A plant of the daisy family, with bright daisy-like flowers, grown as a houseplant or for bedding. |  Cinerarias come in a wide variety of colors, from vibrant blues and purples to delicate pinks and whites. 瓜叶菊有各种各样的颜色，从鲜艳的蓝色和紫色到淡雅的粉色和白色。

The cineraria is a popular choice for adding a splash of color to winter gardens. 瓜叶菊是为冬季花园增添一抹色彩的热门选择。 | 近义词:Pericallis × hybrida (botanical name), florist's cineraria | 反义词: N/A | 词性:noun
