outgoing:/ˌaʊtˈɡoʊɪŋ/ | adj. | 外向的；友好的；即将离职的；流出的 |  Friendly and socially confident. Also describes something that is leaving a place or position. | She has a very outgoing personality. 她性格非常外向。| synonyms: sociable, extroverted, gregarious, affable | antonyms: shy, introverted, reserved, withdrawn | adjective

out-goer: /ˌaʊtˈɡoʊər/ | n. | 外出者；喜欢社交的人 | A person who regularly goes out to social events.  This word is less common than "outgoing person". |  He's a real out-goer and loves parties. 他是个真正的外出者，喜欢参加派对。 | synonyms: social butterfly, extrovert, party animal | antonyms: homebody, introvert, recluse | noun

词源： "out-goer" is derived from the verb "to go out" and the suffix "-er," which denotes someone who performs an action.  "Outgoing" is the present participle of the verb "to go out" used as an adjective.
