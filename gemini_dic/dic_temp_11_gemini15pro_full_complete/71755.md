flea-marketeer:/ˌfliːˈmɑːrkɪtɪər/
n.|跳蚤市场摊贩；在跳蚤市场卖东西的人|A person who sells goods at a flea market.
|例句：The flea-marketeer haggled with a customer over the price of an antique clock.  跳蚤市场摊贩与一位顾客就一个古董钟的价格讨价还价。

|例句：The experienced flea-marketeer always arrived early to secure the best spot.  经验丰富的跳蚤市场摊贩总是早早到达以确保最佳位置。
|近义词：vendor, seller, trader|反义词:buyer, customer|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
flea market + -eer
flea market: from French *marché aux puces* ‘market of the fleas’
-eer: suffix forming nouns denoting persons who do or are concerned with something
