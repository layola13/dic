deis:/deɪs/
noun|平台，祭坛，高台|A platform, especially one raised above the surrounding floor, as in a church or hall.|The speaker stood on the deis and addressed the crowd. 演讲者站在高台上向人群发表讲话。|platform, stage, podium|ground, floor|n.
Etymology: From Old French *deis*, from Latin *discus* "disk, quoit".
