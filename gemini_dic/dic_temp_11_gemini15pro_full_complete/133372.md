nonextraneous:/ˌnɒnɪkˈstreɪniəs/
adjective
非无关的;必要的;重要的
Not irrelevant or superfluous; essential; pertinent.
The lawyer argued that all the information presented was nonextraneous and vital to the case.  律师辩称，所有提供的信息都是必要的，对案件至关重要。
All nonextraneous personnel should leave the building immediately. 所有无关人员都应立即离开大楼。
近义词:essential, necessary, relevant, pertinent, germane
反义词:extraneous, irrelevant, unnecessary, superfluous
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From non- (not) + extraneous (from Latin extraneus, meaning "external, foreign").
