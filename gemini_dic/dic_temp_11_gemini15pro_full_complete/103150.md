jerrymandered:/ˈdʒɛriˌmændərd/ | adj. | 不公正划分的；为己党利益改划的 | Divided into election districts in a way that gives one political party an unfair advantage. |  The state's districts were obviously jerrymandered to favor the ruling party.  该州的选区明显地被不公正划分以利于执政党。 | gerrymandered districts have been accused of being racially motivated.  有人指责这些为己党利益改划的选区带有种族动机。| Synonyms: manipulated, rigged, biased | Antonyms: fair, impartial, unbiased | adjective
