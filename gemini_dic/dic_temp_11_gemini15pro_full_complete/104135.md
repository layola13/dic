junction:/ˈdʒʌŋkʃən/| n.|交叉路口，连接点，枢纽|A place where two or more things join.|例句：The accident happened at the junction of two major highways. 事故发生在两条主要高速公路的交叉路口。

例句：This is a critical junction in his life. 这是他人生中的一个关键时刻。|近义词：intersection, connection, joint, juncture|反义词:separation, division|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *junctionem, accusative of junctio (“a joining”), from jungere (“to join”).
