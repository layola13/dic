naphthalic:/næfˈθælɪk/
adjective
萘的；萘二甲酸的
Relating to or derived from naphthalene.
Naphthalic anhydride is an important intermediate in the production of certain dyes. 萘酐是生产某些染料的重要中间体。
Naphthalic acid is a dicarboxylic acid derived from naphthalene. 萘二甲酸是一种由萘衍生的二元羧酸。
近义词: naphthalene-based
反义词: (No direct antonym, could be something like "non-naphthalene-based")
词性: adjective
