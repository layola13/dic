makeless:/ˈmeɪklɪs/
adjective
无制作的；未完成的；未制造的
Not having been made; lacking form or shape.  Something that is makeless is not yet created or finished.
The sculptor stared at the makeless block of marble, envisioning the figure within.  雕塑家凝视着未成形的方形大理石，想象着里面的形象。
Synonyms: unformed, unfinished, incomplete, undeveloped
Antonyms: formed, finished, complete, developed
词性: adjective
