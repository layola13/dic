nonprogrammatic:/ˌnɒnproʊɡræˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 非程序化的，非编程的 | Not related to or controlled by a computer program; done manually or through traditional methods. |  Example: Nonprogrammatic advertising relies on manual insertion orders and negotiations.  非程序化广告依赖于手动插入订单和协商。 | Example: The museum curator preferred a nonprogrammatic approach to exhibition design, relying on intuition and experience.  博物馆馆长更喜欢非程序化的展览设计方法，依靠直觉和经验。 | Synonyms: manual, traditional, non-automated | Antonyms: programmatic, automated, algorithmic | adjective
