carunculate:/ˈkærəŋkjʊlət/| adj.|肉质突起的，有肉垂的|Covered in or having small, fleshy growths.  Characterized by caruncles.|The turkey's wattle is a carunculate appendage. 火鸡的肉垂是一个肉质突起的附属物。|Synonyms: fleshy, wattled|Antonyms: smooth, glabrous|adjective


caruncula:/kəˈrʌŋkjʊlə/| n.|肉垂，小肉瘤|A small, fleshy excrescence; a caruncle.|The caruncula at the corner of his eye was inflamed. 他眼角的小肉瘤发炎了.|Synonyms: caruncle, wattle|Antonyms: N/A|noun


caruncle:/ˈkærʌŋkəl/| n.|肉垂，小肉瘤|A small, fleshy outgrowth, typically red or pink, on the head or neck of a bird or other animal.|The rooster's caruncle was bright red. 公鸡的肉垂鲜红.|Synonyms: caruncula, wattle|Antonyms: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *caruncula*, diminutive of *caro* ("flesh").
