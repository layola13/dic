keep-ear-to-the-ground:/ˌkiːp jər ˈɪr tə ðə ˈɡraʊnd/
|idiom|
|密切关注事态发展; 注意周围的动静; 保持消息灵通|
|To pay close attention to current affairs and trends, especially in a particular field or area, in order to stay well-informed and anticipate changes.|
|例句：She keeps her ear to the ground so she knows what's happening in the fashion industry. 她密切关注时尚界的动态，所以她知道发生了什么。|
|例句：He's always kept his ear to the ground, which is how he got the inside scoop on the merger. 他一直密切关注着，这就是他如何获得合并内幕消息的。|
|近义词：stay informed, keep abreast of, monitor, follow closely|
|反义词：be out of touch, be uninformed, ignore|
|词性：idiom (习语)
