dared:/derd/
verb
敢于，胆敢
past tense of dare
He dared to ask her out. 他胆敢约她出去。
近义词: ventured, risked, challenged
反义词: hesitated, flinched, cowered
词性: verb (past tense)

dared:/derd/
adjective
被挑战的，被激将的
used after "to be" and followed by an infinitive with "to"
She was dared to jump off the bridge. 她被激将从桥上跳下去。
近义词: challenged, provoked
反义词: unchallenged
词性: adjective (past participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *durran, from Proto-Germanic *durzaną, from Proto-Indo-European *dhers- (“to be bold, dare”). Cognate with Old Saxon durran, Old Frisian durra, Old High German turran, German dürfen (“to be permitted, may”), Gothic ᚦ𐌰𐌹𐍂𐍃𐌰𐌽 (þairsan, “to dare”).
