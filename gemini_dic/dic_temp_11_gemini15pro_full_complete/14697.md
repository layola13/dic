awakening:/əˈweɪkənɪŋ/ | n. |苏醒，觉醒，认识|The act of waking up or becoming aware of something. |例句：The awakening of spring brought new life to the garden. 春天的到来给花园带来了新的生机。

例句：The political awakening of the people led to a revolution. 人民的政治觉醒导致了一场革命。|近义词：revival, resurgence, renewal, dawning|反义词：dormancy, slumber, stagnation|词性: 名词

awakenings:/əˈweɪkənɪŋz/ | n. |苏醒，觉醒，认识（复数）|Plural form of "awakening," referring to multiple instances of waking up or becoming aware. |例句：The book details the spiritual awakenings of several individuals. 这本书详细描述了几个人物的精神觉醒。


例句：The movie explores the awakenings of patients with a rare neurological disorder. 这部电影探讨了患有罕见神经系统疾病的患者的觉醒。 |近义词：revivals, resurgences, renewals, dawnings|反义词：dormancies, slumbers, stagnations|词性: 名词


词源: From Old English *awæcnung, verbal noun of awæcnian "to awaken," from a- (intensifying prefix) + wæcnan "to wake up."
