blurbed:/blɜːbd/ | v. |  宣传；(尤指)写推荐广告；吹捧 | To have publicized or promoted (a book, film, etc.) especially by writing a short description of it. | The author blurbed his own book on social media.  作者在社交媒体上宣传了他自己的书。|  The publisher blurbed the new novel as "a masterpiece." 出版商将这部新小说吹捧为“杰作”。 | synonyms: publicized, promoted, advertised, touted, hyped | antonyms: criticized, panned, condemned |verb
