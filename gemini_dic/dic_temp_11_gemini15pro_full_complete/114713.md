mallecho:/ˈmæliːkəʊ/ (US) /məˈliːkəʊ/ (UK)| n.|坏事，恶行，阴谋；(尤指)流言蜚语|A wicked deed, an evil action, a plot; especially, malicious gossip.  Shakespeare used it to mean "mischief."|例句：The tabloids were full of mallecho about the celebrity's personal life. 小报上充斥着关于这位名人私生活的流言蜚语。

例句：His political rivals spread mallecho to discredit him. 他的政敌散布流言蜚语败坏他的名声。


近义词：mischief, wrongdoing, evil, wickedness, calumny, slander|反义词:good deed, kindness, benevolence|词性:noun
