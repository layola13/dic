bogus:/ˈboʊɡəs/| adj.|假的，伪造的，冒牌的|Not genuine or true; fake.|例句：The company was built on a bogus investment scheme. 这家公司建立在一个虚假的投资计划之上。|例句：He presented bogus credentials to get the job. 他出示了伪造的证件以获得这份工作。|近义词：fake, counterfeit, spurious, sham, phony|反义词：genuine, authentic, real|词性:形容词
词源:Origin uncertain. Possibly from a counterfeiting machine known as a "bogus" in the US in the early 19th century.
