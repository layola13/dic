obstinacy: /ˈɒbstɪnəsi/ (UK) /ˈɑːbstɪnəsi/ (US) | n. | 固执，顽固，倔强 | The quality of being unreasonably determined to do something or to hold a particular belief, even when it is wrong or unwise. |  He showed his usual obstinacy, refusing to compromise. 他表现出他一贯的固执，拒绝妥协。

The child's obstinacy about eating vegetables frustrated his parents.  孩子对吃蔬菜的固执让他的父母很沮丧。

近义词: stubbornness, persistence, tenacity, inflexibility, rigidity
反义词: flexibility, compliance, acquiescence, docility
词性: noun
