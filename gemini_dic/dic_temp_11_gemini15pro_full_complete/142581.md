osteodentine:/ˌɒstiəʊˈdɛntɪn/ | n. | 骨质牙本质 | A type of dentine resembling bone in structure, containing blood vessels within the dentine tubules. | Osteodentine is found in the teeth of certain fish.  骨质牙本质存在于某些鱼类的牙齿中。|  Osteodentine is characterized by its vascular canals. 骨质牙本质的特征是其血管管道。 | synonym: vasodentine | antonym: orthodentine | noun
