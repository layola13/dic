cetologist:/siːˈtɒlədʒɪst/ /siːˈtɑːlədʒɪst/
n.
鲸类学家
A scientist who studies whales and other cetaceans.
Cetologists are studying the impact of pollution on whale populations. 鲸类学家正在研究污染对鲸鱼种群的影响。

Dr. Sylvia Earle is a renowned cetologist. 西尔维娅·厄尔博士是一位著名的鲸类学家.


Synonym:
whale scientist, marine biologist (more general)

Antonym:
None (lacks a direct opposite)

Word type:
Noun
