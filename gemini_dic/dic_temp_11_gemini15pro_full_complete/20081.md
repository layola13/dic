biorhythms:/ˈbaɪəʊˌrɪðəmz/ | n. |生物节律|  A cyclical pattern of physiological and psychological well-being, said to affect human behavior. |  Many people believe biorhythms can influence mood and performance. 很多人相信生物节律会影响情绪和表现。 | synonyms: biological rhythms, biological cycles | antonyms: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From bio- + rhythm. Bio- comes from Greek bios "life". Rhythm comes from Greek rhythmos "measured motion, time, proportion".
