nominalises:/ˈnɒmɪnəlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 名词化；名词化；将…变成名词 |  The process of turning a verb or adjective into a noun. For example, "failure" is a nominalisation of the verb "to fail". | The writer nominalises verbs frequently, giving the text a formal tone.  作者经常使用动词名词化，使文本显得正式。 |  nominalize, nounify | verbalize | verb
