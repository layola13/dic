maharishis:/ˌmɑːhəˈrɪʃiːz/
noun
大师们; 圣人们
A maharishi is a great Hindu sage or spiritual leader, especially one who has attained a state of enlightenment.
The maharishis gathered for the annual conference.  大师们聚集在一起参加年度会议。
The teachings of the maharishis have influenced generations. 圣人们的教诲影响了几代人。
Synonyms: sages, gurus, masters, ascetics
Antonyms: disciples, students, followers
词性:n.
