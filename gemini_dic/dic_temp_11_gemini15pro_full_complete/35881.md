cocoa:/ˈkoʊkoʊ/ (US) /ˈkəʊkəʊ/ (UK)| n. |可可树|The cacao tree; the seeds of this tree.|The cocoa beans are harvested from the cocoa tree. 可可豆是从可可树上收获的。|Synonyms: cacao tree|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms)|noun
