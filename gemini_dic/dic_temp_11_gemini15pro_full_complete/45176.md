cufic: /ˈkuːfɪk/| adj. n.|库法体的，库法体书法|An angular style of Arabic script, used primarily in decorative inscriptions.|The inscription on the mosque was written in elegant cufic script.  清真寺上的铭文是用优雅的库法体写成的。|Near synonyms: angular, geometric, stylized|Antonym: cursive|adjective, noun

cufic: /ˈkuːfɪk/| adj. n.|库法体的，库法体书法|Relating to Kufa, a city in Iraq, where this script originated.|Many early Qur'ans were written in cufic. 许多早期的古兰经是用库法体书写的。|Near synonyms:  Kufic script, early Arabic script|Antonym: modern Arabic script|adjective, noun
