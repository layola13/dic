ETYMOLOGY->
From Azerbaijan + -s (plural marker).  "Azerbaijan" itself derives from Atropates, a Persian satrap who ruled the region in the 4th century BC.  The name evolved through Middle Persian and Arabic forms before reaching its modern form.
