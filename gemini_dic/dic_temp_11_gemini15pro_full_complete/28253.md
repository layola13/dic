captains:/ˈkæptɪnz/| n. |船长，队长，领袖|A person who is in command of a ship, aircraft, or sports team.  The captain steered the ship through the storm. 船长驾驶着船穿过风暴。|近义词：leader, commander, chief, skipper (for ships) |反义词:subordinate, follower|noun
Etymology: From Old French capitaine, from Late Latin capitaneus (“chief”), from Latin caput (“head”).
