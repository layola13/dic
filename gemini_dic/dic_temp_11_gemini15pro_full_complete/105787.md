kirumbo:/kiˈrʊmboʊ/ | n. | 基伦布，一种东非的传统啤酒 |  A traditional East African beer, usually made from fermented millet or sorghum.  It can vary in strength and sweetness depending on the region and specific recipe. |  We enjoyed a refreshing kirumbo after a long day's work. 我们在一天辛苦的工作后享用了一杯清爽的基伦布。| synonyms: pombe (Swahili),  local brew, traditional beer | antonyms: soda, commercial beer | noun
