mardi: /ˈmɑːrdi/ (US), /ˈmɑːdi/ (UK) | n. | 星期二 |  French word for Tuesday. It comes from Latin "Martis dies," meaning "Mars's day." |  例句：I have a meeting on mardi. 我星期二有个会议。

例句：Mardi is the second day of the week in France. 在法国，星期二是每周的第二天。


近义词：Tuesday | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
