employees: /ɪmˈplɔɪiːz/
n.
雇员，员工
People who are employed by a particular company or organization.
The company has over 500 employees.  这家公司有超过500名员工。
All employees are required to attend the training session. 所有员工都必须参加培训课程。

近义词: workers, staff, personnel
反义词: employers

词性: 名词
