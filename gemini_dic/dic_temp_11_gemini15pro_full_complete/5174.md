alleviations:/əˌliːviˈeɪʃənz/
|n.| 减轻；缓和；缓解|The medicine provided some alleviation from the pain.  这种药减轻了一些疼痛。|
| |The government is working on alleviations for the housing crisis. 政府正在努力缓解住房危机。|
| |Synonyms: relief, ease, mitigation|
| |Antonyms: aggravation, intensification, exacerbation|
| |noun|

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *alleviacion*, from Old French *aleviacion*, from Late Latin *alleviātiō*, from Latin *alleviātus* (past participle of *alleviāre*, “to lighten, relieve”), from *ad-* (“to, towards”) + *levis* (“light”).
