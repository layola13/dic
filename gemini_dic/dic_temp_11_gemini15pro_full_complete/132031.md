noncorroboratory:/ˌnɑːnkəˈrɑːbəreɪtɔːri/
adjective
不证实的，未经证实的
Not providing confirmation or support; not corroborating.
The detective found noncorroboratory evidence at the crime scene.  侦探在犯罪现场发现了未经证实的证据。
His alibi was noncorroboratory, weakening his defense. 他的不在场证明未经证实，削弱了他的辩护。
Synonyms: unconfirmed, unsubstantiated, unsupported
Antonyms: corroborative, confirmatory, validating
