imperially:/ɪmˈpɪəriəli/| adv.|帝王般地，皇帝般地，帝国地|In a manner befitting or characteristic of an emperor or empire; in an imperial manner.  Relating to an empire or its ruler.|The ambassador spoke imperially, as if representing the entire world.  大使帝王般地说着话，仿佛代表着整个世界。|Synonym: majestically, royally, sovereignly|Antonym: humbly, submissively|Adverb
