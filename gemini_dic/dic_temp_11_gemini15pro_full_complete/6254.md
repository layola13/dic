amoebian:/əˈmiːbiən/| adj. n.|阿米巴的；变形虫似的；无定形的|Relating to or resembling an amoeba, especially in being shapeless or in constantly changing form.  Also, a single-celled organism of the genus Amoeba.|The amoebian creature moved slowly across the petri dish.  变形虫一样的生物缓慢地穿过培养皿。|The company's amoebian structure allows it to adapt quickly to market changes. 这家公司无定形的结构使其能够快速适应市场变化。|近义词：amorphous, shapeless, protean|反义词：fixed, rigid, structured|词性: adjective, noun
amoeba:/əˈmiːbə/| n.|阿米巴；变形虫|A single-celled animal that catches food and moves about by extending fingerlike projections of protoplasm. Amoebas are either free-living in damp habitats or parasitic.|The amoeba engulfed the bacteria. 变形虫吞噬了细菌。|Amoebas are simple organisms.  变形虫是简单的生物。|近义词：protozoan|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific organism)|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From New Latin *amoeba, from Greek amoibē "change, alteration," related to ameibein "to change."
