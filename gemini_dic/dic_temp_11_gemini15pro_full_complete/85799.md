hazarded:/ˈhæzərdɪd/| v.|冒险；大胆提出；冒昧地说|to say something, especially something that you are not sure about and might be wrong|He hazarded a guess that she was about 30. 他大胆猜测她大约30岁。|Synonyms: venture, risk, chance, gamble|Antonyms: protect, safeguard|verb

hazarded:/ˈhæzərdɪd/| adj.|冒风险的|risked, ventured|The mountaineers made a hazarded attempt to reach the summit. 登山者冒险尝试登顶。|Synonyms: risky, dangerous, perilous|Antonyms: safe, secure|adjective
