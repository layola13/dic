blazon:/ˈbleɪzən/ | v. n. |宣扬；纹章；炫耀；|To describe or depict heraldically; to display prominently or vividly; a coat of arms. |He blazoned his family's coat of arms on his car. 他把家族的盾形纹章装饰在他的车上。 |The company blazoned its success across the media.  公司在媒体上大肆宣扬它的成功。| synonyms: proclaim, announce, broadcast, publish, declare, exhibit, display | antonyms: conceal, hide, suppress | verb
blazon:/ˈbleɪzən/ | n. |纹章；炫耀；|A coat of arms; a showy display. |The knight's blazon was a golden lion on a red field.  骑士的纹章是红色底色上的金色狮子。|The celebrity's wedding was a blazon of wealth and extravagance.  这位名人的婚礼是对财富和奢华的炫耀。| synonyms: coat of arms, emblem, insignia, display, show | antonyms: concealment, hiding | noun
