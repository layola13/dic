hybrids:/ˈhaɪbrɪdz/| n.| 杂交种，混合物，混合动力车 |Something that is made by combining two different elements; a thing that has two different types of components performing essentially the same function; an offspring of two animals or plants of different breeds, varieties, species, or genera. |  The car is a hybrid, powered by both petrol and electricity. 这辆车是混合动力车，既可以用汽油也可以用电力驱动。

|近义词：crossbreeds, mixtures, blends, combinations|反义词：purebreds, unmixed|词性: noun


hybrid:/ˈhaɪbrɪd/| adj.| 混合的，杂交的 |Of mixed origin or composition; composed of different elements. | Many popular garden flowers are hybrids. 许多受欢迎的园艺花卉都是杂交品种。

|近义词：mixed, combined, blended, crossbred|反义词：pure, unmixed|词性: adjective
