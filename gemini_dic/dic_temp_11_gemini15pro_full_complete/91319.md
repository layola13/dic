hydropathist:/ˌhaɪdrəˈpæθɪst/| n. |水疗医师；水疗法信奉者|A person who practices or believes in hydropathy (the treatment of disease using water).|例句：The hydropathist recommended cold baths and compresses. 水疗医师建议洗冷水澡和冷敷。

例句：She consulted a hydropathist for her arthritis. 她去看水疗医师治疗关节炎。|近义词：hydrotherapist|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "allopath")|词性:noun
