immovableness:/ˌɪmuːvəbəlnəs/| n.|不动性；坚定；固执|The immovableness of his principles impressed everyone. 他的原则坚定不移，给大家留下了深刻的印象。|例句：The immovableness of the rock made it a perfect anchor.  这块岩石的固定性使它成为一个完美的锚。

例句：Her immovableness on the issue was frustrating. 她在这个问题上的固执令人沮丧。|近义词：stability, firmness, steadfastness, rigidity|反义词：mobility, flexibility, instability, wavering|词性:noun

immovable:/ɪˈmuːvəbl/| adj.|不能动的；固定的；坚定不移的|例句：The statue stood immovable in the town square.  雕像屹立在城镇广场上，纹丝不动。

例句：He was immovable in his decision to resign. 他辞职的决定坚定不移。|近义词：fixed, firm, steadfast, rigid|反义词：movable, mobile, flexible|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *immovablenesse, derived from immovaable + -nesse.  "Immovaable" comes from Old French immoüvable, from Late Latin immovābilis, from in- "not" + movābilis "movable,"  from movēre "to move."
