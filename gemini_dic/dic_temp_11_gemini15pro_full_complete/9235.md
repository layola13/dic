antiparticle:/ˈæntiˌpɑːrtɪkl/ | n. |反粒子|A subatomic particle that has the same mass as another particle, but opposite values for some other properties, such as electric charge.|Every particle of matter has a corresponding antiparticle. 每个物质粒子都有一个对应的反粒子。|Synonym: counterpart|Antonym: particle|noun
