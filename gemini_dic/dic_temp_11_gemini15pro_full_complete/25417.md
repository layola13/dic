bulimia: /buˈlɪmiə/ (US) /bjuːˈlɪmiə/ (UK)| n.|暴食症；贪食症|An eating disorder characterized by episodes of binge eating followed by inappropriate methods of weight control, such as self-induced vomiting, misuse of laxatives, fasting, or excessive exercise.|She struggled with bulimia for years before seeking help. 她与暴食症抗争多年才寻求帮助。|Synonyms: binge eating disorder, hyperphagia|Antonyms: anorexia|Noun


bulimia nervosa: /buˈlɪmiə nɜːrˈvoʊsə/ (US) /bjuːˈlɪmiə nɜːˈvəʊsə/ (UK)| n.|神经性贪食症|A specific type of bulimia, formally recognized as a mental disorder.|Bulimia nervosa can have serious health consequences. 神经性贪食症会对健康造成严重后果。|Synonyms: bulimia|Antonyms: anorexia nervosa|Noun
