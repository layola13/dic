handsprang:/ˈhændspræŋ/ | v. |手工编织（花边）|To create (lace) by a technique in which threads are twisted together by hand, without needles or bobbins. |She handsprang a delicate lace edging for the handkerchief. 她手工编织了一条精致的花边，用来装饰手帕。|The artisan handsprang a beautiful shawl using silk threads.  这位工匠用丝线手工编织了一条漂亮的披肩。| 近义词: make lace, braid, plait | 反义词: unravel, untwist | 词性: 动词

handsprings:/ˈhændsprɪŋz/ | n. |手翻，侧手翻（handspring的复数）|A gymnastic movement in which a person throws themselves forward or sideways onto their hands and then flips their legs over their head.| The gymnast performed a series of handsprings across the mat. 体操运动员在垫子上做了一连串的侧手翻。|  She impressed everyone with her perfect handsprings. 她完美的侧手翻给每个人都留下了深刻的印象。| 近义词: flip, somersault | 反义词:  (没有明确的反义词) | 词性: 名词
