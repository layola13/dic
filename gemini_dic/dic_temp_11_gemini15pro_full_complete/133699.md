nonfricative:/nɑːnˈfrɪkətɪv/| adj. n.|非摩擦音|A nonfricative is a speech sound, such as a vowel or nasal, produced without friction in the vocal tract.|The sounds /m/, /n/, and /ŋ/ are nonfricatives.|近义词:vowel, sonorant, approximant|反义词:fricative|词性:adjective, noun
