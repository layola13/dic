awesomest:/ˈɔːsəməst/| adj.|最棒的，最令人惊叹的|Superlative form of "awesome," meaning extremely impressive or daunting in a positive way.  It expresses the highest degree of awesomeness.|例句：That concert was the awesomest show I've ever seen! 那场音乐会是我看过的最棒的演出！|近义词：greatest, best, most amazing, most impressive|反义词：worst, terrible, awful|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "awesome," a combination of "awe" (from Old Norse "agi," meaning "fear" or "terror") and "-some" (a suffix meaning "causing" or "characterized by").  The "-est" suffix is a superlative ending.
