explosivenesses:/ɪkˌsploʊsɪvnəsɪz/| n. |爆炸性；爆发力；易爆炸性|The quality of being explosive; tendency to explode.  The state of being easily aroused to strong emotion or action.|The explosivenesses of the chemicals made them dangerous to transport. 这些化学品的爆炸性使得它们运输起来很危险。|Near synonyms: volatility, instability, inflammability|Near antonyms: stability, inertness|noun
