kindled:/ˈkɪndəld/| v. adj.|点燃的，激起的|past tense and past participle of kindle|The fire was kindled with small twigs. 火是用小树枝点燃的。|近义词：ignited, sparked, aroused, inspired|反义词:extinguished, quenched|词性:动词(过去式和过去分词), 形容词


Etymology->
From Middle English *kindlen, *kendlen, from Old Norse kynda (“to kindle, set fire to”), from Proto-Germanic *kundōną (“to set fire to”), from Proto-Indo-European *gʷʰendʰ- (“to kindle, ignite”). Cognate with Danish tænde (“to light, kindle”), Swedish tända (“to light, kindle”), Icelandic kveikja (“to kindle, ignite”).
