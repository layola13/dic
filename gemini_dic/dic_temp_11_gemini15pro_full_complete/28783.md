carolers: /ˈkærələrz/ /ˈkærələz/| n.|颂歌者；唱圣诞歌的人|A group of people who go from house to house singing carols, especially at Christmas.|例句：The carolers gathered on the town square and sang festive songs. 颂歌者聚集在城镇广场上唱着节日歌曲。

例句：We heard carolers singing in the snow outside our window. 我们听到窗外颂歌者在雪中唱歌。

近义词：singers, choristers|反义词:|词性:noun
