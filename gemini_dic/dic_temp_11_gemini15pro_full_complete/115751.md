marimbas:/məˈrɪmbəz/| n.|马林巴琴|A percussion instrument consisting of a set of wooden bars graduated in length to produce a musical scale, played with mallets.|The band had two marimbas, creating a rich, resonant sound. 乐队有两架马林巴琴，创造出丰富而洪亮的音响。|Synonyms: xylophone, vibraphone|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a musical instrument)|noun
