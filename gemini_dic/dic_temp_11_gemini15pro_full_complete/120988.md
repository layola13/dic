miscite: /ˌmɪsˈsaɪt/ | v. | 错误引用 | to quote incorrectly; to cite incorrectly |  He miscited the author in his essay. 他在他的论文中错误地引用了作者。

The lawyer miscited the legal precedent, weakening his case. 律师错误引用了法律先例，削弱了他的论证。| 近义词：misquote, misrepresent | 反义词：cite correctly, quote accurately | 词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (wrongly) + cite (to quote).  The prefix "mis-" comes from Old English "mis-" meaning "wrongly, badly, amiss."  "Cite" comes from Old French "citer" from Latin "citare" meaning "to summon, urge, call."
