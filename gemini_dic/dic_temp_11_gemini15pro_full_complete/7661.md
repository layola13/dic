anode:/ˈænəʊd/ (UK) /ˈænˌoʊd/ (US)| n.|阳极，正极|The positive electrode in an electrolytic cell, at which oxidation occurs.|Electrons flow from the anode to the cathode. 电子从阳极流向阴极。|The anode is positively charged. 阳极带正电。|Synonym: positive electrode, positive pole|Antonym: cathode, negative electrode|noun
