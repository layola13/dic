dollying:/ˈdɒlɪɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈdɑːliɪŋ/ (US)| n. v.|摄影移动拍摄，摄影移动拍摄（名词）；进行摄影移动拍摄（动词）|The act of moving a camera towards or away from a subject during filming to create a sense of motion or change in perspective.  This can be done on a dolly track or handheld.|The cameraman was dollying in on the actress's face. 摄影师用移动摄影车靠近女演员的脸部拍摄特写。|近义词：tracking, trucking, zooming|反义词: static shot|n./v.

dollying:/ˈdɒlɪɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈdɑːliɪŋ/ (US)| v.|进行摄影移动拍摄|Moving the camera towards or away from the subject on a dolly.|They were dollying back to reveal the full scale of the landscape. 他们用移动摄影车向后移动，以展现风景的全貌。|近义词：tracking, trucking|反义词: remaining stationary|v.
