campered:/ˈkæmpt/ /ˈkæmpərd/ | v. | 露营；扎营；(临时)驻扎 | To live temporarily in a tent or other temporary shelter, especially outdoors.  | We campered by the lake for a week. 我们在湖边露营了一个星期。

The refugees have campered near the border for months. 难民们在边境附近扎营已有数月。 | 近义词: bivouacked, tented, encamped | 反义词: resided, dwelt, lodged | 词性: 动词

campered:/ˈkæmpt/ /ˈkæmpərd/ | adj. | (informal)夸张的；做作的；装腔作势的 |  Exaggerated and theatrical in style or behaviour.  | He gave a very campered performance as the dame. 他扮演贵妇人的表演非常做作。

Her campered gestures made everyone laugh. 她夸张的姿势让每个人都笑了。 | 近义词: affected, theatrical, exaggerated, dramatic | 反义词: natural, unaffected, genuine | 词性: 形容词
