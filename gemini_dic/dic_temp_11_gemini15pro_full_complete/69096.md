fauves:/foʊvz/
|n.|野兽派画家；野兽派作品|Wild beasts; a term applied to a group of early 20th-century French artists whose works emphasized painterly qualities and strong color over the representational or realistic values retained by Impressionism.|The Fauves were known for their bold use of color. 野兽派以大胆运用色彩而闻名。|Synonyms: expressionists, modernists|Antonyms: traditionalists, classicists|Noun

fauve:/foʊv/
|n.|野兽；野兽派画家|Wild beast; a Fauvist artist.|Matisse was a leading fauve. 马蒂斯是一位主要的野兽派画家。|Synonyms: beast, wild animal; expressionist, modernist|Antonyms: tame animal; traditionalist, classicist|Noun
|adj.|野兽派的；野兽派的|Of or relating to Fauvism.|His fauve paintings are highly valued. 他的野兽派绘画作品非常珍贵。|Synonyms: expressionistic, modern|Antonyms: traditional, classical|Adjective
