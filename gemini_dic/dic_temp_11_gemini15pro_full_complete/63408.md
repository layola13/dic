entrained:/ɪnˈtreɪnd/ /ɛnˈtreɪnd/| v. adj.|夹带，携带；使…同步； entraîné (adj.) 受过训练的，训练有素的|To carry along with a stream or current; to become synchronized or coordinated with an external rhythm or influence.|The river entrained sediment downstream. 河流把沉积物向下游挟带。 The circadian rhythms of the travelers were quickly entrained to the new time zone. 旅行者的昼夜节律很快与新的时区同步。  The dancer was so entrained in the music, she didn't notice the audience. 舞者完全沉浸在音乐中，没有注意到观众。 The entrained soldier moved with precision and skill.  训练有素的士兵动作精准熟练。 |近义词：carry, transport, convey, synchronize, coordinate, trained, skilled|反义词:leave behind, deposit, unsynchronized, uncoordinated, untrained, unskilled|词性:动词，形容词

词源: From French entraîner, from en- + trainer ("to drag, draw").
