endlessly:/ˈɛndləsli/| adv.|无休止地，不断地|Without end; continuously.|The waves crashed endlessly against the shore. 海浪不断地拍打着海岸。|近义词:incessantly, perpetually, continuously, eternally|反义词:finitely, briefly, intermittently|词性:副词
词源: From "endless" + "-ly". "Endless" comes from Old English "endeleas," from "ende" (end) + "-leas" (without).
