longitudes:/ˈlɒŋɡɪtjuːdz/ (US) /ˈlɒŋɡɪtʃuːdz/ (UK)| n. |经度，经线|The longitude of a place is its angular distance east or west of a standard meridian. 一个地方的经度是它与标准子午线的东或西角距离。|例句：The ship's longitude was difficult to determine accurately. 这艘船的经度很难准确确定。|例句：Knowing the latitude and longitude is essential for navigation.  了解纬度和经度对于航行至关重要。|近义词：meridians |反义词:latitudes|词性:noun
