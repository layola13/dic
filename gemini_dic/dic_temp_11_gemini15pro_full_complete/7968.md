anthesis:/ˈænθɪsɪs/| n. |开花；开花期|The period during which a flower is fully open and functional.|The cherry blossoms reached their full anthesis in April. 樱花在四月盛开。|近义词：bloom, blossom, flowering|反义词: nonflowering, dormancy|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἄνθησις (ánthēsis, “flowering, blossoming”), from ἀνθέω (anthéō, “to bloom, blossom”), from ἄνθος (ánthos, “flower”).
