overmoralized: /ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrəlaɪzd/
| adj. | 过分道德化的，过分拘泥于道德的 | Excessively concerned with morality; having moral principles applied too strictly or rigidly.  Characterized by excessive moralizing. |  The book presents an overmoralized view of Victorian society. 这本书对维多利亚时代社会呈现了一种过分道德化的观点。 |  moralistic, puritanical, self-righteous |  amoral, immoral, unethical | adjective
