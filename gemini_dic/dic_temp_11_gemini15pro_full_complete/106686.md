kwɒm/ (British English), /kwɑːm/ (American English) | pron. | 什么 |  Used to ask for information about something.  Equivalent to "what" but considered nonstandard or dialectal, primarily found in some dialects of British English. |  "Kwam are you doing?" (What are you doing?) | Near synonyms: what | Antonyms: N/A | Pronoun

注意事项:

"kwam" is not standard English and its usage is generally limited to certain dialects. Using it in formal writing or speech is not recommended.  Using "what" is preferred in Standard English.
