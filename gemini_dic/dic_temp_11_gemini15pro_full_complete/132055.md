noncosmopolitanism:/ˌnɒnkɒzməˈpɒlɪtənɪzəm/
n.|非世界主义，非世界性|The state or quality of not being cosmopolitan; a lack of interest in or understanding of other cultures; parochialism.|His noncosmopolitanism was evident in his disdain for foreign films.|近义词：parochialism, insularity, provincialism|反义词：cosmopolitanism, internationalism|词性:名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + cosmopolitanism (from Greek kosmos "world" + politēs "citizen")
