dimwitted:/dɪmˈwɪtɪd/| adj.|愚笨的，傻的|Lacking intelligence; stupid.|例句：He made a dimwitted remark that embarrassed everyone. 他说了一句愚蠢的话，让每个人都很尴尬。

例句：The dimwitted thief left his fingerprints all over the scene.  这个愚蠢的窃贼在现场留下了他的指纹。|近义词：stupid, foolish, silly, obtuse, doltish, slow-witted|反义词：intelligent, smart, bright, clever, astute|词性:形容词

词源:
From dim (weak, faint) + witted (having wit).  The word suggests having a faint or weak wit.
