clatter:/ˈklætər/| v.  n.|发出咔嗒声，咔嗒声|to make a continuous rattling sound. A continuous rattling sound.|The pots and pans clattered in the kitchen. 锅碗瓢盆在厨房里叮当作响。|The carriage clattered over the cobblestones. 马车在鹅卵石路上发出咔嗒咔嗒的声音。|近义词：rattle, bang, clang|反义词:silence, quiet|词性:动词, 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *clateren, of imitative origin.  Related to Dutch klateren (“to clatter”), German klatern (“to climb, clamber”), and possibly also to Old Norse klatra (“to clatter, claw”).
