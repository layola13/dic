caecostomy:/siːkɒˈstɒmi/
noun
盲肠造口术
The surgical creation of an artificial opening into the cecum.
A caecostomy was performed to decompress the distended colon.  为减轻结肠膨胀的压力，进行了盲肠造口术。
Synonyms: cecostomy
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider procedures that close the opening)
词性: 名词
