betrothments:/bɪˈtrəʊðmənts/
n.
订婚，婚约
the act of promising to marry someone
例句：The betrothments were announced at the party. 订婚的消息在聚会上宣布了。

例句：Their betrothments were celebrated with a grand feast. 他们的订婚以盛宴庆祝。

近义词：engagements, promises of marriage
反义词：separations, breakups
词性: 名词
