nonutilitarians:/ˌnɒnjuːˌtɪlɪˈteəriənz/ | n. | 非功利主义者 | People who do not subscribe to the philosophy of utilitarianism. |  These nonutilitarians believe that some actions are inherently right or wrong, regardless of their consequences. 这些非功利主义者认为，有些行为本身是对或错的，无论其后果如何。|  近义词:deontologists, moral absolutists | 反义词: utilitarians | 词性: 名词

nonutilitarian:/ˌnɒnjuːˌtɪlɪˈteəriən/ | adj. | 非功利主义的 | Not relating to or characteristic of utilitarianism. | He took a nonutilitarian approach to the problem, focusing on fairness rather than efficiency. 他对这个问题采取了一种非功利主义的方法，关注公平而不是效率。 | 近义词: non-consequentialist | 反义词: utilitarian | 词性: 形容词
