get-the-air:/ɡet ði ɛr/ | phrasal verb | 被解雇；被抛弃；被拒绝；被播出；公开 | to be fired from a job; to be rejected by a romantic partner; to be broadcast; to be made public |  例句：He got the air after only three months on the job. 他工作了三个月就被解雇了。

例句：Her proposal for the new project got the air. 她的新项目提案被否决了。

近义词：be fired, be dismissed, be sacked, be let go, be dumped, be rejected, be broadcast, be aired | 反义词：be hired, be accepted | 词性：动词短语
