clientless:/ˈklaɪəntləs/
adjective
无客户端的
Without a client application; referring to software or systems that can be accessed directly through a web browser or other interface without needing to install specific software.
Many companies now offer clientless VPN solutions, allowing employees to connect securely to the company network from any device with an internet connection.  许多公司现在提供无客户端VPN解决方案，允许员工使用任何具有互联网连接的设备安全地连接到公司网络。
Clientless remote access is becoming increasingly popular due to its convenience.  由于其便利性，无客户端远程访问正变得越来越流行。
Near Synonyms: web-based, browser-based, thin-client
Antonyms: client-based, thick-client
word type: adjective
