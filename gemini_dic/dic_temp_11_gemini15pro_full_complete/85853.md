he-man:/ˈhiːmæn/| n.| 男子汉，壮汉|A strong, muscular man; a virile man.|例句：He's a real he-man, always lifting weights and working out. 他是个真正的男子汉，总是举重和锻炼。

例句：The movie portrays him as a he-man capable of incredible feats of strength. 这部电影把他描绘成一个能够完成惊人力量壮举的男子汉。|近义词：strongman, muscleman, tough guy|反义词:weakling, wimp|词性:noun

词源:Compound of *he* + *man*.  First recorded in the 1930s. Popularized in the 1980s by the *Masters of the Universe* franchise.
