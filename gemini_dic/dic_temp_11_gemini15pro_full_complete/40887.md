cookless:/ˈkʊklɪs/
adjective
无须烹饪的；无需厨师的
Lacking or not requiring a cook or cooking.
This apartment comes with a cookless kitchen, perfect for those who prefer not to cook.  这套公寓配有无需烹饪的厨房，非常适合那些不喜欢做饭的人。
The hotel offers cookless meal options for busy travelers.  这家酒店为忙碌的旅客提供无需烹饪的餐食选择。
Synonyms: ready-to-eat, pre-prepared, instant
Antonyms: home-cooked, freshly prepared
词性: 形容词
