bedsore:/ˈbedˌsɔːr/ (US) /ˈbedˌsɔː(r)/ (UK)| n. |褥疮，压疮|A sore caused by pressure against the skin, especially where the bones are close to the skin, as when a person has been lying in bed for a long time.|The patient developed a bedsore on his heel. 病人的脚后跟长了褥疮。|Synonym: pressure sore, decubitus ulcer|Antonym: healthy skin|noun

Etymology: bed + sore. From the fact that these sores often develop in bedridden patients.
