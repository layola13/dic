carpoolers:/ˈkɑːrˌpuːlərz/
noun
拼车的人
People who travel together to a destination, typically to work or school, in a single vehicle, sharing the costs and often taking turns driving.
例句：The carpoolers met at the park and ride. 拼车的人在停车换乘点集合。

例句：The company offers incentives to encourage carpoolers. 公司提供奖励措施来鼓励拼车。

近义词：ride-sharers, commuters
反义词：solo drivers

词性:noun
