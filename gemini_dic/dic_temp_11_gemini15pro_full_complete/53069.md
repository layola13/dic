diorthotic:/ˌdaɪɔːrˈθɑːtɪk/ | adj. |矫正的，矫形的 |Relating to or employed in the correction of deformities, especially of the skeletal system.  Designed to correct or improve a functional defect. | Example: Diorthotic shoes are often prescribed for children with flat feet. 矫形鞋通常被推荐给患有扁平足的儿童。

Example: The doctor recommended diorthotic braces for her scoliosis. 医生建议她使用矫形支架来治疗脊柱侧弯。| 近义词: corrective, orthopedic, orthotic | 反义词:  N/A (No direct antonym exists, perhaps 'deforming' could be considered loosely antonymous) | 词性: adjective


词源: From Greek *diorthōtikos*, from *diorthoun* "to straighten, set right," from *dia-* "through, across" + *orthoun* "to straighten," from *orthos* "straight, right."
