dna:/ˌdiː ɛn ˈeɪ/ /ˌdiː ɛnˈeɪ/ | n. |脱氧核糖核酸| deoxyribonucleic acid, a self-replicating material which is present in nearly all living organisms as the main constituent of chromosomes. It is the carrier of genetic information.| Scientists are studying the DNA of ancient humans. 科学家正在研究古代人类的DNA。|  The police used DNA evidence to identify the criminal. 警方使用DNA证据来识别罪犯。| synonyms: genetic code, genome | antonyms: None | noun
