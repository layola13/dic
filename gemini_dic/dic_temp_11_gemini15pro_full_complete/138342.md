nothingnesses:/ˈnʌθɪŋnɪsɪz/
n.
空虚；虚无；不存在的状态
The vast nothingness of space can be both awe-inspiring and terrifying. 浩瀚的宇宙空间既令人敬畏，又令人恐惧。
She felt a profound nothingness after her children left home. 孩子们离开家后，她感到深深的空虚。
Near synonyms: void, emptiness, nihility, oblivion
Antonyms: existence, being, somethingness
