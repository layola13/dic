blubbery: /ˈblʌbəri/ (US) /ˈblʌbəri/ (UK) | adj. | 肥胖的；鲸脂的；哭哭啼啼的 | Covered with or resembling blubber; fat and flabby; tearful. |  The walrus is a blubbery creature. 海象是一种肥胖的生物。 | Synonyms: obese, flabby, plump, chubby, tearful, weepy | Antonyms: thin, lean, muscular, stoic | adjective

blubbery: /ˈblʌbəri/ (US) /ˈblʌbəri/ (UK) | n. | 鲸脂 | The fat of whales and other sea mammals. |  The blubbery was used to make oil for lamps. 鲸脂被用来制作灯油。| Synonyms: blubber | Antonyms: N/A | noun


Etymology: From "blubber" (n.) + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives). "Blubber" comes from Middle English "blober," probably imitative of the sound of bubbling or splashing.
