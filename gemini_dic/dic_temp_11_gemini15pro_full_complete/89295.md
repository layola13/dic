homoeomorphous:/ˌhəʊmiəˈmɔːrfəs/ |adj.| 同形的，同胚的| Having the same form or shape; exhibiting homeomorphism.  Specifically in biology and crystallography, corresponding in form or structure but not necessarily in size. | The two crystals are homoeomorphous, despite their difference in size. 这两种晶体尽管大小不同，但形状相同。| synonyms: isomorphic, analogous, similar, resembling | antonyms: heteromorphic, dissimilar, different | adjective
