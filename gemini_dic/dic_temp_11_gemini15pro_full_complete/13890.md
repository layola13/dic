august:/ˈɔːɡəst/ (US) /ɔːˈɡʌst/ (UK)| adj. n.|威严的，庄重的；八月|Inspiring reverence or admiration; of supreme dignity or grandeur; majestic; the eighth month of the year, formerly Sextilis.|例句：The august presence of the queen filled the room. 女王的威严气势充满了整个房间。|近义词：majestic, dignified, stately, grand, noble, imposing|反义词：humble, lowly, common, ordinary|词性：形容词/名词

august:/ˈɔːɡəst/ (US) /ɔːˈɡʌst/ (UK)|n.| 八月 |The eighth month of the year, formerly Sextilis.|例句：My birthday is in August. 我的生日在八月。|近义词：N/A (as a month)|反义词：N/A (as a month)|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin Augustus ("consecrated, venerable"),  the title given to the first Roman Emperor, Octavian, in 27 BC. The month Sextilis was renamed in his honor.
