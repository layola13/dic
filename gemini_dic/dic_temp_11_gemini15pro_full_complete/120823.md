misattributions:/ˌmɪsætrɪˈbjuːʃənz/
n.
错误归因；误判
The incorrect assigning of something to a source or cause.
Several misattributions have been made about the authorship of this poem.  这首诗的作者被错误归因了好几次。
Misattributions of famous quotes are common online.  网上对名言的错误归因很常见。
近义词: misinterpretations, misidentifications, errors
反义词: correct attributions, accurate identifications
