arbor:/ˈɑːrbər/ (US) /ˈɑːbə(r)/ (UK)| n.|凉亭；藤架；树荫处|A shady garden recess, usually formed by trees or climbing plants trained over a framework.|We sat in the arbor, enjoying the shade. 我们坐在凉亭里，享受着树荫。|The arbor was covered in fragrant honeysuckle. 这座凉亭上覆盖着芬芳的金银花。|synonyms: bower, pergola, gazebo|antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "open space")|noun
