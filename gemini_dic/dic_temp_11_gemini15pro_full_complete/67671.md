extrusible:/ɪkˈstruːzɪbl/ | adj. | 可挤压的，可压出的，可挤出的 | Capable of being extruded; able to be shaped by forcing through a die. |  The toothpaste is extrusible from the tube.  牙膏可以从管中挤出来。

The soft clay was extrusible and could be molded into various shapes.  这种软粘土是可挤压的，可以塑造成各种形状。 | synonyms: moldable, shapeable, pliable, ductile | antonyms: rigid, inflexible, unyielding | adjective
