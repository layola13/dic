malengine:/mælˈɛndʒɪn/ | n. | 恶意引擎；恶意软件引擎 | A software engine designed to create or exploit vulnerabilities in computer systems for malicious purposes.  A system designed to automate the process of creating or deploying malware. |  Example 1: The hacker used a sophisticated malengine to infiltrate the company's network.  黑客使用了一个复杂的恶意引擎渗透到公司的网络。

Example 2:  Security researchers discovered a new malengine capable of generating polymorphic viruses. 安全研究人员发现了一个能够生成多态病毒的新恶意引擎。 | 近义词: malware generator, exploit kit, threat engine | 反义词: antivirus engine, security engine | 词性: noun
