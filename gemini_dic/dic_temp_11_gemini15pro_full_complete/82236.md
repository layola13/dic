grouch:/ɡraʊtʃ/
|n.|脾气坏的人；抱怨|He's always been a grouch in the morning. 他早上总是脾气不好。 |Don't be such a grouch! 别这么爱抱怨！|
|vi.|抱怨，发牢骚|He grouched about the cold weather. 他抱怨天气冷。|
例句：He's a grouch when he's hungry. 他饿的时候脾气很坏。
例句：She grouched about the long lines at the store. 她抱怨商店排队太长。
近义词：(n.) grumbler, complainer; (v.) complain, grumble
反义词：(n.) optimist; (v.) praise, compliment
词性: 名词，动词

词源：From the verb "grouch," which originated in the early 20th century, likely as an alteration of "grutch," meaning to complain or grumble.  "Grutch" has roots in Middle English "grucchen," from Old French "groucher."
