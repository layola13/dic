huckaback:/ˈhʌkəˌbæk/| n. adj.|粗糙的棉麻织物；用这种织物做的毛巾（或围裙等）|A type of strong absorbent cotton or linen fabric with a rough surface, used especially for towels and dishcloths.  Also used as an adjective to describe something made of this fabric.|She dried her hands on a huckaback towel. 她用一块粗棉布毛巾擦干了手。|近义词:terry cloth, waffle weave|反义词:silk, satin|词性:名词, 形容词

Etymology:
From Low German *hukkebak, perhaps from *hukke "back, hump" + bak "back," referring to the uneven texture of the fabric.
