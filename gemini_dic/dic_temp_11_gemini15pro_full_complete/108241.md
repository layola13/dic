laughs:/læfs/ | v. | 笑; 嘲笑 | An act of laughing; the sound of laughing.  | She laughs at all his jokes. 她听了他所有的笑话都笑了。 | synonyms: chuckles, giggles, roars, guffaws | antonyms: cries, weeps, sobs | verb


laughs:/læfs/ | n. | 笑声 | The sound produced by laughing. |  The room filled with laughs. 房间里充满了笑声。| synonyms: laughter, giggling, chuckling | antonyms: sobs, cries, weeping | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *laughen*, from Old English *hliehhan* (West Saxon), *hlihhan* (Anglian), from Proto-Germanic *klakjaną*, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱlegʰ-. Cognate with Dutch *lachen*, German *lachen*, Gothic *𐌷𐌻𐌰𐌷𐌾𐌰𐌽* (hlahan).
