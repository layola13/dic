extracted:/ɪkˈstræktɪd/ | adj. | 提取的，萃取的 |  Removed or taken out of something else.  Derived or obtained from a particular source. |  The oil is extracted from olives.  这种油是从橄榄中提取的。

The DNA was extracted from the blood sample. 从血液样本中提取了DNA。 | synonyms: removed, derived, obtained, separated, isolated | antonyms: inserted, added, combined, integrated | adjective
