banalities:/bəˈnælɪtiz/| n.|陈词滥调；平庸之语|A banality is a remark or statement that is very unoriginal and boring because it has been said many times before.|The speech was filled with banalities about the importance of hard work and dedication.|Synonyms: clichés, platitudes, truisms|Antonyms: originalities, novelties, insights|noun
