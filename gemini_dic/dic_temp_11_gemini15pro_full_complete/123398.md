monopersonal:/ˌmɒnəˈpɜːrsənəl/ | adj. | 单人的；一人公司的；仅限一人的 | Consisting of only one person; designed or intended for use by one person; of or relating to a company owned and run by a single person. |  This artist creates monopersonal performances designed for an audience of one.  这位艺术家创作了专为一位观众设计的单人表演。

The entrepreneur established a monopersonal limited liability company to manage his investments.  这位企业家成立了一家一人有限责任公司来管理他的投资。 | 近义词: single, sole, individual, one-man, one-person | 反义词: multi-person, group, collective | 词性: adjective

词源: From mono- (one) + personal.  "Mono-" comes from Greek "monos" meaning "alone, single." "Personal" comes from Latin "personalis," meaning "of a person."
