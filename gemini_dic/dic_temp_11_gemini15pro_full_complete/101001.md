invalidly:/ɪnˈvælɪdli/
adv.
无效地，错误地
in a way that is not valid or legal; wrongly.
He argued that the contract was invalidly drawn up. 他辩称该合同无效。
The election results were declared invalidly. 选举结果被宣布无效。
近义词:wrongly, illegitimately, unlawfully
反义词:validly, legitimately, lawfully
词性:副词


invalid:/ɪnˈvælɪd/
adj.
无效的；无根据的；站不住脚的
not legally or officially acceptable; not based on truth or reason; weak.
Your passport is invalid. 你的护照无效。
Her argument was invalid. 她的论点站不住脚。
近义词: void, null, illegitimate
反义词: valid, legitimate, sound
词性:形容词


valid:/ˈvælɪd/
adj.
有效的；有根据的；正当的
legally or officially acceptable; based on truth or reason; sound.
A valid passport is required for international travel. 国际旅行需要有效护照。
He made a valid point. 他提出了一个有根据的观点。
近义词: sound, legitimate, authentic
反义词: invalid, illegitimate, void
词性:形容词


词源:
From Late Latin invalidus "weak, infirm," from in- "not" + validus "strong"
