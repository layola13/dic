anhelation: /ˌæn.hɪˈleɪ.ʃən/ | n. | 喘息；渴望；热望 |  A state of breathlessness or panting, often as a result of exertion. Also, an eager yearning or aspiration. |  He reached the top of the hill in a state of anhelation. 他气喘吁吁地到达了山顶。  She felt an anhelation for a simpler life. 她渴望过上更简单的生活。 | 近义词: panting, gasping, yearning, longing, craving | 反义词: apathy, indifference | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Late Latin *anhelātiōn- (stem of anhelātiō), from Latin anhelāre  "to pant, gasp," from an- "toward" + halāre "to breathe."
