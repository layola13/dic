hypermutability:/ˌhaɪpərˌmjuːtəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 超突变性，高度突变性 | The increased rate of mutation, beyond the normal background rate, often observed in certain genetic contexts or environmental conditions.  It refers to an unusually high rate of genetic mutation. |  The hypermutability of certain viruses makes them difficult to treat.  某些病毒的超突变性使它们难以治疗。 |  hypermutation, high mutation rate | stability, immutability | noun
