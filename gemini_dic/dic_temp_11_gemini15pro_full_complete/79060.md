gives:/ɡɪvz/
verb
给予，赠送
third-person singular present tense form of give
He gives her a flower every day. 他每天都送她一朵花。
She gives her time to charity work. 她把时间奉献给慈善工作。
Synonyms: offers, presents, donates, bestows, contributes
Antonyms: takes, receives, withholds, denies
词性: 动词

词源:  Old English *giefan (attested only in the past participle giefen), from Proto-Germanic *gebaną (source also of Old Frisian jeva, Old Saxon geban, Old Norse gefa, Dutch geven, German geben, Gothic giban "to give"). Related to gift. The original past tense and past participle was gave, given.
