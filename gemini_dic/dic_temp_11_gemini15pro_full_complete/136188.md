nonrelatively:/nɒnˈrɛlətɪvli/ | adv. | 非相对地；绝对地 |  Not in a relative manner; absolutely.  It describes something considered in isolation, not in comparison to something else. | The speed of light is constant nonrelatively, meaning it doesn't change based on the observer's motion. 光速非相对地恒定，意味着它不会根据观察者的运动而改变。|  The cost of living is high nonrelatively, regardless of income levels.  无论收入水平如何，生活成本绝对很高。 |Synonyms: absolutely, independently, unconditionally | Antonyms: relatively, comparatively | 词性: 副词
