brog:/brɒɡ/| n. |粗革皮鞋；厚底鞋|A sturdy leather shoe, typically with ornamental perforated patterns.| He wore a pair of sturdy brogs for his hike in the countryside. 他穿着一双结实的粗革皮鞋在乡下远足。|Synonyms: oxford, shoe, boot|Antonyms: slipper, sandal|noun


brogue:/broʊɡ/| n. |口音；方言；土腔|A regional pronunciation of words, especially a strong Irish or Scottish accent.|His Irish brogue was charming. 他的爱尔兰口音很迷人。|Synonyms: accent, dialect, twang|Antonyms: standard pronunciation|noun
