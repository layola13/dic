andante:/ɑːnˈdɑːnteɪ/ /ænˈdænti/
adjective
缓慢的，行板的
moderately slow, at a walking pace.
The second movement is a beautiful andante. 第二乐章是一段优美的行板。
近义词:moderato, leisurely
反义词:allegro, presto
词性:adjective


andante:/ɑːnˈdɑːnteɪ/ /ænˈdænti/
adverb
缓慢地，行板地
in a moderately slow tempo, at a walking pace.
Play this passage andante.  这段要演奏得行板。
近义词:moderately, slowly
反义词:quickly, rapidly
词性:adverb


andante:/ɑːnˈdɑːnteɪ/ /ænˈdænti/
noun
行板
a musical direction indicating a moderately slow tempo.
The andante had a calming effect. 这段行板有镇 calming 定的效果。
近义词:moderato
反义词:allegro
词性:noun


词源: Italian, from *andare 'to walk'.
