nonperfection:/ˌnɒnpəˈfekʃən/ | n. | 不完美，缺陷 |The state or quality of being imperfect; imperfection.  |  Example: She embraced the nonperfection of her handmade pottery. 她欣然接受了自己手工陶器的不完美。 |  Example:  The artist found beauty in the nonperfection of nature.  这位艺术家在自然的不完美中发现了美。 | Synonyms: imperfection, flaw, defect, blemish | Antonyms: perfection, flawlessness, impeccability | noun
