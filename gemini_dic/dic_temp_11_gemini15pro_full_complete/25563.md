bum-around:/bʌm əˈraʊnd/
| v. |闲逛，游荡，无所事事|To spend time idly or without any specific purpose; to loaf or wander.|He bummed around Europe for a year after college. 他大学毕业后在欧洲闲逛了一年。|近义词：loaf, wander, drift, idle, hang around|反义词:work, hustle, be productive|词性:动词


bum-around:/bʌm əˈraʊnd/
| n. | 闲人，游民 | A person who bums around; a loafer or wanderer. | He's a bit of a bum-around, never settling down in one place. 他有点像个游民，从不在一个地方安定下来。 | 近义词：loafer, idler, wanderer, vagrant| 反义词:worker, busybody|词性:名词
