aerolithology:/ˌɛroʊlɪˈθɒlədʒi/ | n. | 空气石学；陨石学 | The study of meteorites, their composition, structure, and origin.  It encompasses the physical and chemical properties of meteorites, as well as their classification and relationship to other celestial bodies. |  Scientists specializing in aerolithology analyze meteorites to understand the early solar system.  科学家专门研究空气石学来了解早期太阳系。 |Synonyms: meteoritics | Antonyms: N/A | noun
