nonincentive:/ˌnɒnɪnˈsɛntɪv/ | adj. | 非激励性的，没有激励作用的 | Not providing any incentive or encouragement; discouraging. |  The nonincentive pay structure discouraged employees from working harder. 非激励性的薪酬结构让员工不愿更努力地工作。

The company's nonincentive policies led to low morale. 公司缺乏激励性的政策导致士气低落。 | 近义词:discouraging, demotivating, counterproductive | 反义词:incentivizing, motivating, encouraging | 词性:形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non "not"
incentive (noun/adjective): from Late Latin incentivus "inciting," from past participle stem of incendere "to kindle, set on fire," from in- "into, on" + candere "to shine, glow"
