amative: /ˈæmətɪv/ | adj. | 恋爱的，多情的 | Inclined to love; amorous. Showing or expressing love or sexual desire. |  She cast an amative glance at him across the crowded room. 她隔着拥挤的房间向他投去多情的目光。|  He wrote her amative poems filled with passion. 他给她写了充满激情的爱情诗。 | synonyms: amorous, affectionate, loving, passionate, romantic, erotic | antonyms: frigid, cold, indifferent, platonic | adjective

Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin *amativus, from Latin amare ‘to love’.
