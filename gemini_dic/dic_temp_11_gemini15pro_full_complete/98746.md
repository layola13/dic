insusceptibility:/ˌɪnsəˌsɛptəˈbɪləti/
n.
不易受影响;抗性;免疫性
The state or quality of being insusceptible; incapable of being affected, influenced, or harmed by something.
He showed an insusceptibility to the disease. 他对这种疾病表现出免疫力。
insensitivity, resistance, immunity
susceptibility, vulnerability
