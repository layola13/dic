banian: /ˈbænjən/ | n. | 印度榕树; （尤指印度的） 无花果树;  (宽松的) 背心 | A fig tree of southern Asia and Africa that sends out many roots that grow downwards from its branches and eventually reach the ground, forming additional trunks. Also, a loose sleeveless undershirt (chiefly in India).|  The banian tree provided welcome shade in the midday sun. 印度榕树在正午的阳光下提供了受欢迎的阴凉。| He wore a thin cotton banian under his shirt. 他在衬衫里穿着一件薄棉背心。 | synonyms: fig tree, banyan, undershirt | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a type of tree or clothing) | noun
