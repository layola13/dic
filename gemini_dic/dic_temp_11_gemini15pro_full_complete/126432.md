mysticalnesses:/ˈmɪstɪkəlnəsɪz/
n.
神秘性，神秘感
The quality or state of being mystical; mysteriousness.
The mysticalnesses of the ancient rituals captivated the anthropologist.  古代仪式的神秘性吸引了这位人类学家。
近义词: mysteriousness, otherworldliness, strangeness, enigma, spirituality
反义词: mundanity, ordinariness, commonness
词性: noun

词源:
From mystical + -ness. Mystical comes from Late Middle English mistik, from Old French mistique, from Late Latin mysticus, from Greek mystikos, from mystēs ‘initiate’, from myein ‘close the eyes or lips’.
