allergic:/əˈlɜːrdʒɪk/
adjective
过敏的；对…过敏的
Having a strong negative physical reaction to something.
例句：I'm allergic to peanuts. 我对花生过敏。

例句：She is allergic to cats and dogs. 她对猫和狗过敏。

近义词：hypersensitive, sensitive, susceptible
反义词：immune, resistant
词性：adjective

allergic:/əˈlɜːrdʒɪk/
adjective
过敏的；对…过敏的
Having a strong negative physical reaction to something.
词源：From French *allergique*, from allergie "allergy," coined 1906 by Viennese pediatrician Clemens von Pirquet (1874-1929) from Greek allos "other, different" + ergon "work."
例句：He's allergic to pollen. 他对花粉过敏。

例句：Many people are allergic to dust mites. 很多人对尘螨过敏。

近义词：hypersensitive, intolerant
反义词：tolerant, immune
词性：adjective
