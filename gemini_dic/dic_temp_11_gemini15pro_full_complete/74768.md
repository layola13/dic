freeze-dryer:/ˈfriːz ˌdraɪər/ (US), /ˈfriːz ˌdraɪə(r)/ (UK)| n.|冷冻干燥机，冻干机|A machine that removes water from a frozen substance by sublimation, typically used for preserving food or biological specimens.|The laboratory used a freeze-dryer to preserve the tissue samples.|近义词：lyophilizer|反义词:|名词

freeze-dry:/ˈfriːz ˌdraɪ/ (US), /ˈfriːz ˌdraɪ/ (UK)| v.|冷冻干燥|To preserve (something) by rapidly freezing it and then subjecting it to a high vacuum, causing the ice to change directly from a solid to a vapor without passing through a liquid phase.|They freeze-dried the coffee to preserve its flavor.|近义词：lyophilize|反义词:|动词
