overharvest:/ˌəʊvərˈhɑːrvɪst/ | v.  n. |过度捕捞，过度采伐| To harvest (a resource) to the point of diminishing returns; to harvest so heavily that a species’ population is depleted or a natural resource becomes scarce. | The overharvesting of fish stocks has led to a decline in the ocean's biodiversity. 过度捕捞鱼类资源导致海洋生物多样性下降。

The overharvest of timber has resulted in deforestation and soil erosion.  木材的过度采伐导致了森林砍伐和水土流失。 | 近义词：overexploit, deplete | 反义词: conserve, sustain | 词性: 动词，名词 |词源: over- + harvest
