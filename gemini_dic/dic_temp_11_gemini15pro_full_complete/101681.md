irreligiousnesses:/ɪˌrɪˈlɪdʒəsnɪsɪz/
n.
lack of religiousness; impiety
The survey highlighted the increasing irreligiousnesses of young people.  这项调查突出了年轻人越来越缺乏宗教信仰。
Etymology:  From irreligious + -ness.  "Irreligious" comes from "in-" (not) + "religious" (from Latin "religiosus").
Synonyms: impiety, ungodliness, secularism, atheism, nonbelief
Antonyms: piety, devoutness, religiousness, godliness
词性: Noun
