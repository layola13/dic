lessening:/ˈlesənɪŋ/| adj. |逐渐减少的，递减的|There's a lessening need for manual labor in many industries. 许多行业对手工劳动的需求正在减少。|例句：With lessening resources, we need to be more careful about our consumption. 随着资源的减少，我们需要更加谨慎地消费。|近义词：decreasing, diminishing, declining, dwindling|反义词:increasing, growing, expanding|词性:形容词

词源: From the verb "lessen," which comes from the Middle English "lessen," from Old English "læssan," meaning "to make less." Ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*lassojanan," related to "less."
