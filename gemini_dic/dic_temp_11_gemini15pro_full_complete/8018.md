anthracitic:/ˌænθrəˈsɪtɪk/
adjective
无烟煤的；像无烟煤的
Resembling anthracite coal in color or hardness.  Relating to or containing anthracite.
The anthracitic coal burned with a hot, blue flame. 这种无烟煤燃烧时发出炽热的蓝色火焰。
The region is known for its anthracitic deposits. 这个地区以其无烟煤矿床而闻名。
近义词:coal-black, ebony, jet-black
反义词:light, bright
词性:adjective
