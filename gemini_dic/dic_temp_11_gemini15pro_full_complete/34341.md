clasping: /ˈklɑːspɪŋ/ | adj. | 紧握的，抱住的 | Holding tightly; embracing. | She held the baby, clasping it tightly in her arms. 她抱着婴儿，紧紧地搂在怀里。 | The clasping roots of the ivy clung to the wall. 常春藤紧贴着墙壁。 | 近义词: gripping, holding, embracing, clutching | 反义词: releasing, letting go | 词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English *claspen, from Old English *clæpsen, frequentative of *clēofan "to cleave, adhere".  Related to cleave.
