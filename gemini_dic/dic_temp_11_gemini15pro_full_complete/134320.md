noninstitutions:/ˌnɑːnˌɪnstɪˈtuːʃənz/| n. |非机构; 非公共机构| Entities or groups that are not established, formal organizations like governments, corporations, or schools.  They often refer to households, private individuals, or informal groups.  |  Example:  Much of the nation's wealth is held by noninstitutions.  这个国家的很多财富都掌握在非机构手中。| Example: The survey collected data from both institutions and noninstitutions.  这项调查收集了来自机构和非机构的数据。 |Synonyms:  non-organizational, independent, private |Antonyms: institutions, organizations| noun
