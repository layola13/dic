ferret-out:/ˈferɪt aʊt/
verb
搜寻出，查获
to search out, discover, or bring to light, typically by persistent investigation
The reporter ferreted out the corruption scandal. 记者发现了腐败丑闻。
近义词: unearth, uncover, discover, expose, track down
反义词: conceal, hide, bury, cover up
词性: verb

词源:  From "ferret," originally a noun referring to a small, domesticated weasel used for hunting rabbits and rodents.  The verb "ferret" emerged from the animal's behavior of driving prey from burrows.  "Ferret out" is thus a phrasal verb emphasizing the thorough and persistent nature of the search.
