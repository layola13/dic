farthermore:/ˈfɑːrðərˌmɔːr/| adv.|此外，而且|Used to introduce a further point, especially one that strengthens a previous point.|He's lazy, and furthermore, he's dishonest. 他很懒惰，而且还不诚实。|Furthermore, the study found no link between the two groups. 此外，该研究发现两组之间没有联系。|近义词：moreover, in addition, besides, also|反义词:however, nevertheless|词性:副词

词源: From *further* + *more*.  *Further* itself traces back to Old English *furðor*, the comparative of *fore*.
