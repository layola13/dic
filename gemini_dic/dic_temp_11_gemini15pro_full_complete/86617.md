heliotypic:/ˌhiːliəˈtɪpɪk/
adjective
日光胶印的
Relating to or produced by a heliotype process, a photomechanical printing process that uses a gelatin relief to reproduce images.
A heliotypic reproduction of the original photograph was displayed in the gallery.  日光胶印复制的原始照片在画廊中展出。
The museum acquired a rare collection of heliotypic prints from the 19th century.  博物馆收藏了一批罕见的19世纪日光胶印版画。
synonyms: photomechanical, photogravure
antonyms: handcrafted, hand-drawn
