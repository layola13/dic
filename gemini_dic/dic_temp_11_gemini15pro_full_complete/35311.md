cnap: /knæp/
n.
a sharp, cracking sound.
a sudden sharp break or snapping sound.
The cnap of a twig broke the silence.  树枝的断裂声打破了寂静。
近义词：crack, snap, click
反义词:  None (lack of sound could be considered an antonym contextually)
词性: noun


cnap: /knæp/
v.
to break or snap with a sharp sound.
to cause something to break or snap with a sharp sound.
He cnapped the dry twig in two. 他把干树枝啪的一声折成两段。
近义词：break, snap, fracture
反义词: mend, repair
词性: verb


Etymology:  Likely of imitative origin, related to words like "snap," "crack," and "knock," which mimic the sounds they describe.
