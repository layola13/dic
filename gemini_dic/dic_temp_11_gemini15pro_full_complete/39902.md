constitutionist:/ˌkɒnstɪˈtjuːʃənɪst/ | n. | 宪政主义者；立宪主义者 | A person who strongly supports a particular constitution, or the idea of government based on a constitution. |  He was a staunch constitutionist, believing in the rule of law above all else. 他是一位坚定的宪政主义者，相信法律至上。

The constitutionist argued for strict adherence to the document's original intent.  这位宪政主义者主张严格遵守文件的初衷。| 近义词:constitutionalist | 反义词:dictator, authoritarian | 词性:noun

constitutionist:/ˌkɑːnstɪˈtuːʃənɪst/ | adj. | 宪政的；立宪的 | Relating to or advocating constitutionalism.  |  The group held constitutionist views, advocating for limited government and individual rights.  该团体持有宪政观点，主张有限政府和个人权利。

They proposed a constitutionist amendment to protect freedom of speech. 他们提出了一个宪政修正案来保护言论自由。 | 近义词:constitutional | 反义词:dictatorial, tyrannical | 词性:adjective
