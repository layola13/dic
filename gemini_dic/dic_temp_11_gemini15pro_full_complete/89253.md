hominid:/ˈhɒmɪnɪd/ (US) /ˈhɑːmɪnɪd/| n. |人科动物；原始人类|A primate of a family ( Hominidae ) that includes humans and their fossil ancestors.|例句：The discovery of the hominid skull provided crucial evidence for human evolution.  人科动物头骨的发现为人类进化提供了重要的证据。

例句：Scientists debate the precise classification of early hominids.  科学家们对早期人科动物的精确分类存在争议。|近义词：humanoid, hominin|反义词: (无明确的反义词)|词性: noun
