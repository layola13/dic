mcmanifest:/ˈmækˌmænɪfɛst/ | n. | 清单文件，资源清单文件 | A file, often named manifest.json, that describes the resources and metadata of a Minecraft mod or data pack. It is required for the game to load and understand the contents of the mod or data pack. | Example: The mcmanifest file specifies the mod's name, version, and dependencies.  清单文件指定了模组的名称、版本和依赖项。

Example: Without a valid mcmanifest, the game won't recognize the data pack. 没有有效的清单文件，游戏将无法识别数据包。 | 近义词: manifest, configuration file | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
