hearsay:/ˈhɪrseɪ/ | n. | 传闻，道听途说 | Information received from other people that one cannot adequately substantiate; rumor. | The court case was dismissed because the evidence was based on hearsay.  这起案件被驳回了，因为证据是基于传闻。 | Example: The rumor about the company merger was just hearsay. 关于公司合并的传言只是道听途说。 | Synonyms: rumor, gossip, report, tale | Antonyms: evidence, proof, fact | Noun
