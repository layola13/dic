commend:/kəˈmend/| v.|推荐；赞扬；把…托付给|To praise or approve of someone or something publicly.  To entrust someone or something to the care of someone else.|I commend her for her bravery. 我赞扬她的勇敢。|近义词：praise, applaud, endorse, entrust|反义词：criticize, condemn, denounce|verb

commendable:/kəˈmendəbl/| adj.|值得赞扬的；值得推荐的|Deserving praise and approval.|His dedication to the project is commendable. 他对这个项目的奉献精神值得称赞。|近义词：praiseworthy, laudable, admirable|反义词：reprehensible, blameworthy|adjective

commendation:/ˌkɒmenˈdeɪʃn/| n.|赞扬；推荐；奖状|The act of praising or expressing approval.  A formal statement of praise or approval.|He received a commendation for his bravery. 他因其勇敢而受到表彰。|近义词：praise, approval, acclaim|反义词：criticism, condemnation|noun
