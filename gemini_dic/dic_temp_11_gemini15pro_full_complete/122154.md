mistrust:/mɪsˈtrʌst/| n. vt.|不信任，怀疑；猜疑|Lack of trust or confidence.|She had a deep mistrust of politicians. 她对政客深感不信任。|近义词：suspicion, doubt, distrust|反义词:trust, confidence, faith|词性:名词, 动词

Etymology:
From mis- (expressing wrongness or lack) + trust.  The word originated in the late 16th century.
