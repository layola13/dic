antedates:/ˌæntiˈdeɪts/ | v. | 先于，早于 |  to be of an earlier date than something; precede in time | This building antedates the Norman Conquest.  这座建筑早于诺曼征服。| synonyms: precede, predate, preexist | antonyms: postdate, follow, succeed | verb

antedate:/ˈæntiˌdeɪt/ | n. | 先期日期；比实际日期早的日期 | a date assigned to an event or document earlier than the actual date of its occurrence or creation. | The antedate on the check was a mistake. 支票上的日期写早了，是个错误。| synonyms:  prior date | antonyms: postdate | noun
