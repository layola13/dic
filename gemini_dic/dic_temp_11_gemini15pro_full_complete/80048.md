goaf:/ɡoʊf/ (US), /ɡəʊf/ (UK)| n. |采空区，废矿坑|A goaf is the void left after coal, ore, or other minerals have been extracted from underground mines. |The collapse of the goaf caused a minor earthquake. 采空区的坍塌引发了轻微地震。

The old mine workings are now a dangerous goaf.  旧矿井现在是一个危险的采空区。|近义词：void, cavity, excavation|反义词:pillar, support|词性:名词
