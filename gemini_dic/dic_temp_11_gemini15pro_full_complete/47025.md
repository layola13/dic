dasymeter:/dæˈsɪmɪtər/ | n. | 密度计；气体比重计 | An instrument for determining the relative density or specific gravity of gases. | Example 1: The scientist used a dasymeter to measure the density of the unknown gas. 科学家用密度计测量未知气体的密度。

Example 2: The dasymeter showed a significant difference in the density of the two gases. 密度计显示两种气体的密度有显著差异。 | Synonym: densitometer, aerometer | Antonym: N/A | noun


Etymology:  From Greek *dasys* (dense, thick) + *-meter* (measurer).
