cabbalistic:/ˌkæbəˈlɪstɪk/| adj.|神秘的，玄奥的，犹太神秘哲学的|Relating to or characteristic of Jewish mysticism, esoteric, occult.|The old book was filled with cabbalistic symbols and diagrams.|近义词：esoteric, occult, mystical, arcane|反义词：mundane, common, ordinary|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *cabbalisticus*, from Hebrew קַבָּלָה (qabālā, “received tradition, reception”).
