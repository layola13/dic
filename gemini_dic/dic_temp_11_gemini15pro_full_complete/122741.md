moisteners: /ˈmɔɪsənərz/ | n. |润肤剂，保湿剂| Substances that attract and retain moisture, often used in skincare products to hydrate and soften the skin. |  Apply a rich moisturizer after showering to keep your skin hydrated. 洗澡后涂抹丰富的润肤霜，以保持皮肤水分。

She uses a facial moisturizer every day to prevent dryness. 她每天使用面部保湿霜以防止干燥。| 近义词: humectants, emollients, hydrators | 反义词: drying agents, desiccants | 词性: noun
