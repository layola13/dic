impetuousness:/ɪmˈpɛtʃʊəsnəs/| n. |冲动，鲁莽|The quality of being impetuous; rash action, impulsive behavior.  A tendency to act quickly and without thinking.|His impetuousness led him to make a hasty decision that he later regretted. 他的冲动让他做出了一个后来后悔的仓促决定。|Synonyms: rashness, impulsiveness, recklessness, hastiness |Antonyms: caution, prudence, deliberation, forethought|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *impetuositas, from Latin impetuosus (“violent, vehement”).  Ultimately from impetere (“to attack, assail”), from in- (“in, on, against”) + petere (“to seek, strive for, attack”).
