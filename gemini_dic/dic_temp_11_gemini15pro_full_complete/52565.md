diffusedness:/dɪˈfjuːzdnəs/ | n. |弥漫；扩散；漫射|The state of being spread out or scattered widely.|The diffusedness of the light made it difficult to see clearly. 光线的漫射使人看不清楚。|Synonyms: dispersion, dissemination, spread, pervasiveness|Antonyms: concentration, localization, focus|noun

diffusedness:/dɪˈfjuːzdnəs/ | n. |模糊；不明确|The lack of clarity or distinctness.|The diffusedness of his argument made it hard to follow. 他的论点模糊不清，难以理解。|Synonyms: vagueness, ambiguity, indistinctness|Antonyms: clarity, precision, sharpness|noun
