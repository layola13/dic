blindfolded:/ˈblaɪndˌfoʊldɪd/| adj.|蒙住眼睛的，被蒙住眼睛的|With one's eyes covered with a cloth or bandage.  Being unable to see because one's eyes are covered.|She walked a tightrope blindfolded. 她蒙着眼睛走钢丝。|Near synonyms: sightless, unseeing, eyeless|Antonyms: sighted, seeing|adjective

blindfolded:/ˈblaɪndˌfoʊldɪd/| v.|蒙住…的眼睛|To cover someone's eyes with a cloth or bandage.|The kidnappers blindfolded their hostage. 绑匪蒙住了人质的眼睛。|Near synonyms: cover, veil|Antonyms: uncover, unveil|verb
