ferulaceous:/ˌfɛrʊˈleɪʃəs/
adj.
像茴香的；有茴香味的；产茴香脂的
Resembling a ferula; having the odor of asafoetida; yielding asafoetida.
The ferulaceous scent filled the air.  空气中弥漫着茴香的气味。
Synonyms:  asafoetida-like, fennel-like
Antonyms:  odorless


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ferulāceus, from ferula (“giant fennel”) + -āceus.
