laterifolious:/ˌleɪtərɪˈfəʊliəs/
adjective
晚叶的；夏叶的
Producing leaves late in the season.  Characterized by the production of leaves late in the growing season.
The laterifolious tree didn't sprout leaves until mid-summer. 这棵晚叶树直到仲夏才长出叶子。
The laterifolious nature of the plant made it stand out in the early spring landscape.  这种植物晚叶的特性使它在早春的景观中显得格外突出。
近义词: late-leafing
反义词: early-leafing
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From Latin *lātifolius*, from *lātus* (broad, wide) + *folium* (leaf). However, the "lateri-" part suggests a modification influenced by "late," indicating the late appearance of leaves.
