inertness:/ɪˈnɜːrtnəs/
n.
惰性；迟钝；不活动
Lack of ability or tendency to move or react.
例句：The inertness of the gas makes it safe to handle. 这种气体的惰性使其可以安全处理。

例句：The political inertness of the population is a concern.  民众的政治冷漠令人担忧。

近义词：inactivity, passivity, sluggishness, inertia, quiescence
反义词：activity, reactivity, liveliness, dynamism
词性: 名词


inert:/ɪˈnɜːrt/
adj.
惰性的；迟钝的；不活动的
Lacking the ability or strength to move.
例句：The inert patient lay in bed.  不动的病人躺在床上。

例句：Helium is an inert gas. 氦气是一种惰性气体。

近义词：inactive, passive, motionless, immobile, quiescent
反义词：active, reactive, mobile, dynamic
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *inert- (stem of iners ‘idle, inactive’), from in- ‘not’ + ars, art- ‘skill, art’.
