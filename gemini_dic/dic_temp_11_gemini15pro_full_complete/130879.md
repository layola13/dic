nonaristocratically:/ˌnɑːnˌærɪstəˈkrætɪkli/ | adv. | 非贵族地，不拘泥于贵族礼仪地 | In a manner that is not characteristic of or suitable for an aristocrat. | She behaved nonaristocratically, mingling easily with people from all walks of life. 她举止不拘泥于贵族礼仪，很容易与各行各业的人打成一片。 |  近义词: informally, unpretentiously, simply | 反义词: aristocratically, formally, pompously | 词性: 副词

词源:
non- (prefix) + aristocratically
non- :  Latin origin, meaning "not"
aristocratically: derived from "aristocrat" (from Greek "aristokratia," meaning "rule of the best") + "-ic" + "-ally"
