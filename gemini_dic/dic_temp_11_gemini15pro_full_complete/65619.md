evangelicalness:/ˌiːvænˈdʒɛlɪkəlnəs/ | n. |福音主义, 福音派精神| The quality of being evangelical, marked by a strong emphasis on the teachings of the Gospels, especially the importance of personal conversion and the spreading of the Christian message. | Her evangelicalness was evident in her constant desire to share her faith. 她的福音派精神在她不断渴望分享她的信仰中显而易见。 | 近义词: piety, devoutness, religiousness | 反义词: irreligiousness, secularism | 词性: 名词

词源: From evangelical + -ness.  "Evangelical" comes from the Greek word "euangelion," meaning "good news" or "gospel."
