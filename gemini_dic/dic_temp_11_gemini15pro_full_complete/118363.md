meritmonger: /ˈmerɪtˌmʌŋɡər/
noun
邀功者，沽名钓誉者
Someone who boasts about their merits or achievements, often excessively and insincerely.
He was a meritmonger, always bragging about his accomplishments. 他是个邀功者，总是吹嘘自己的成就。
Synonyms: braggart, show-off, egotist, boaster
Antonyms: humble person, self-effacing person
n.
