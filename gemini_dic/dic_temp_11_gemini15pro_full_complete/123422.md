monopodial:/ˌmɒnəˈpəʊdiəl/ | adj. | 单轴的；总状分枝的 |  (of a plant) having a single main stem or axis that grows continuously from the tip, giving rise to lateral branches or flowers.  | Example: Monopodial orchids typically have a single, upright stem. 单轴兰花通常有一个单一的直立茎。

Example: The monopodial growth habit allows the plant to reach considerable heights. 单轴生长习性使植物能够达到相当的高度。 | 近义词: racemose | 反义词: sympodial | 词性: adjective
