apsidally:/æpˈsaɪdəli/
adjective
拱点地; 近拱点地
At the apsides of an orbit, a body is apsidally farthest or nearest to its primary.
在轨道的拱点，物体拱点地最远或最近于其主星。
The satellite reached its furthest point from Earth apsidally. 卫星拱点地到达了距离地球最远的点。
Near: periapsidally, apoapsidally
Far: non-apsidally (not commonly used)
