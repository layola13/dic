half-dram:/ˈhæf dræm/| n.|半打兰姆酒，少量（液体）|A small amount of liquid, especially liquor, equal to half a dram.  A dram is a unit of liquid volume. |He poured himself a half-dram of whiskey. 他给自己倒了半打兰姆酒的威士忌。|近义词：drop, dash, nip|反义词:gallon, liter|词性:名词
Etymology: "half" (Old English healf) + "dram" (Late Middle English dragme, from Old French dragme, ultimately from Greek drakhmē).
