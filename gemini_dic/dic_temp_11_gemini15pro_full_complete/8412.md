anti-tank:/ˈæntiˈtæŋk/ /ˈæntaɪtæŋk/| adj.|反坦克的，反坦克武器的|Designed to destroy or disable tanks.  Used to describe weapons, devices, or tactics specifically created to counter the threat of tanks.|例句：The army deployed anti-tank missiles. 部队部署了反坦克导弹。|近义词：tank-destroying, anti-armor|反义词: N/A|词性:形容词
anti-tank:/ˈæntiˈtæŋk/ /ˈæntaɪtæŋk/| n.|反坦克武器|A weapon designed to destroy tanks.|例句：The anti-tank was crucial in the defense. 反坦克武器在防御中至关重要。|近义词：tank-destroyer|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
"anti-" (prefix) from Greek "anti" meaning "against, opposite of".
"tank" from  the British military's use of the code word "tank" to disguise the development of armored vehicles during World War I.  Originally thought to be a contraction of "water tank", it was likely chosen for its resemblance to large metal tanks.
