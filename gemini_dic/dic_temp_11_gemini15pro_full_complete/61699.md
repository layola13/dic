emolumental:/ɪˈmɒljʊmənt(ə)l/| adj.|有报酬的，带来收益的|Relating to or providing emoluments; profitable.|Holding an emolumental office in the government can be quite lucrative. 在政府中担任有薪职位可能非常有利可图。|The position is not highly emolumental, but it offers valuable experience. 这个职位报酬不高，但能提供宝贵的经验。|近义词：profitable, lucrative, remunerative|反义词：unprofitable, unremunerative|词性:形容词
emolument:/ɪˈmɒljʊmənt/| n.|薪水，报酬，津贴|The compensation received for work or service, especially in a professional capacity; salary; fees; profits.|The emoluments of the CEO include a generous salary and stock options. 首席执行官的薪酬包括丰厚的薪水和股票期权。|The judge's emoluments are fixed by law. 法官的薪酬由法律规定。|近义词：salary, pay, compensation, remuneration|反义词：loss, expense|词性:名词
