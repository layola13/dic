dean:/diːn/| n.|院长，系主任；教务长|A dean is a senior official in a college or university who is responsible for a particular academic area or division. |The dean of the faculty announced the new curriculum.  学院院长宣布了新的课程安排。

The dean met with the students to discuss their concerns. 系主任与学生们见面讨论他们的担忧。|近义词：principal, headmaster, director|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
