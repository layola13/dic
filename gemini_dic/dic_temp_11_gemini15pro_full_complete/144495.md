outthwack:/ˌaʊtˈθwæk/
verb
彻底打败，痛击
To strike harder than someone else; to defeat decisively.
He outthwacked his opponent in the final round of the tournament. 他在锦标赛的最后一轮彻底击败了他的对手。
synonyms: trounce, thrash, vanquish, clobber, wallop
antonyms: lose to, be defeated by
词性:动词
