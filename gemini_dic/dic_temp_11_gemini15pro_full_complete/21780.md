bobbery:/ˈbɒbəri/ (UK), /ˈbɑːbəri/ (US)| n. |喧闹，骚动；争吵，吵闹；麻烦，困境|A noisy disturbance or commotion; a fuss or uproar; trouble or difficulty.|There was a bit of a bobbery in the pub last night. 昨晚酒吧里发生了一点骚乱。|近义词：commotion, uproar, disturbance, fuss, rumpus, row, fracas|反义词：peace, quiet, tranquility|词性:名词

Etymology:
From the 18th century, possibly from a dialectal word meaning "noise" or "confusion," of uncertain origin.  Some speculate a connection to "bob," meaning to move up and down quickly, suggesting a flurry of activity.
