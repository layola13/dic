conacre:/ˈkɑːnəkər/ (Irish English) /kəˈneɪkər/ (elsewhere)| n.|临时租地；短期租地(尤指为放牧或种植作物)|The practice of letting land for a single crop or grazing season.  Land let under such an agreement.|He took five acres of conacre to grow potatoes.|Synonyms: agistment, lease, rental, letting (especially in Ireland)|Antonyms: ownership, purchase|noun
