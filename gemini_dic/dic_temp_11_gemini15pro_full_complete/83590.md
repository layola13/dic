haemoglobin:/ˌhiːməˈɡlōbɪn/ (also hemoglobin) | n. |血红蛋白|A red protein responsible for transporting oxygen in the blood of vertebrates. It contains iron, to which the oxygen binds.|例句：Hemoglobin carries oxygen from the lungs to the tissues. 血红蛋白将氧气从肺部输送到组织。

例句：A low hemoglobin count can indicate anemia. 血红蛋白计数低可能表明贫血。|近义词：Hb (abbreviation)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
