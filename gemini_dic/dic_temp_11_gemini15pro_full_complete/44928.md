cryptogamian: /ˌkrɪptəˈɡeɪmiən/ | adj. n. | 隐花植物的；隐花植物 |A plant that reproduces by spores, without flowers or seeds.  Relating to or characteristic of a cryptogam. |  Example:  Ferns and mosses are cryptogamian plants.  蕨类植物和苔藓是隐花植物。 | Example: The botanist specialized in cryptogamian species. 这位植物学家专门研究隐花植物。| Synonym: spore-bearing, flowerless | Antonym:  phanerogam, flowering | adjective, noun
