kukuza:/kuˈkuːzɑː/ | v. |促进，发展，培养|To promote, develop, or nurture the growth of something, often referring to economic or social progress. It's primarily used in East Africa, particularly in Swahili. |例句：The government is working to kukuza tourism in the region. 政府正在努力发展该地区的旅游业。

例句：They are trying to kukuza a spirit of cooperation among the team members. 他们正在努力培养团队成员之间的合作精神。|近义词：develop, promote, foster, cultivate, nurture|反义词：hinder, obstruct, impede|词性:动词

PHRASE->
kukuza biashara = to promote business (促进商业)
kukuza uchumi = to develop the economy (发展经济)
kukuza elimu = to promote education (促进教育)
kukuza kilimo = to develop agriculture (发展农业)
kukuza afya = to promote health (促进健康)


词源:  From Swahili *kukuza*, from *ku-*, class 15 subject prefix + *-kuza*, causative extension of *-kua*, "to grow."
