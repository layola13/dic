nonzodiacal:/ˌnɒnzoʊˈdaɪək(ə)l/
adjective

Not of or relating to the zodiac; outside the zodiac.

例句：Nonzodiacal light is a faint, diffuse glow in the night sky. 非黄道光是在夜空中一种微弱、弥漫的光芒。
近义词：non-zodiacal
反义词：zodiacal
词性：adjective
