girdingly:/ˈɡɜːrdɪŋli/
adverb
准备战斗地，跃跃欲试地
In a manner suggestive of preparing for action, especially combat; eagerly, excitedly.
He girdingly accepted the challenge. 他跃跃欲试地接受了挑战。
Synonyms: eagerly, excitedly, readily, willingly, enthusiastically
Antonyms: reluctantly, hesitantly, unwillingly, apathetically
词性: 副词
