de-evolutions:/ˌdiːɛvəˈluːʃənz/
|n.|退化，逆进化|The process of returning to a less developed state; reverse evolution.  It implies a decline or deterioration from a more advanced or complex form.|Scientists debated the possibility of de-evolutions in certain species.  科学家们就某些物种退化的可能性进行了辩论。|
|Synonyms: regression, degeneration, decline, devolution, backsliding|
|Antonyms: evolution, progress, advancement, development|
|noun|
