microcephalous: /ˌmaɪkrəˈsɛfələs/ | adj. | 小头的，头小畸形的 | Having an abnormally small head and underdeveloped brain. |  The child was diagnosed as microcephalous shortly after birth.  这个孩子出生后不久就被诊断为小头畸形。 | Example:  Exposure to certain toxins during pregnancy can cause microcephalous babies. 怀孕期间接触某些毒素会导致婴儿小头畸形。 | Synonyms: microcephalic | Antonyms: macrocephalous | Adjective
