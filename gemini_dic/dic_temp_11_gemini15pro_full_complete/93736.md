immaterialism:/ɪməˈtɪəriəlɪzəm/
n.|非物质主义，唯心主义|The doctrine that material things have no objective existence but exist only as mental perceptions.  The belief that the spiritual world is more important than the physical world.|He was a strong proponent of immaterialism, believing that reality was essentially mental. 他是唯心主义的坚定拥护者，认为现实本质上是精神的。|Synonyms: idealism, spiritualism|Antonyms: materialism, physicalism|noun
