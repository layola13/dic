ogee:/ˈoʊdʒiː/
n.
S-shaped curve; molding with such a curve
A double curve, shaped like an S, consisting of a concave arc flowing into a convex arc.
The neckline of her dress had an elegant ogee curve.  她连衣裙的领口有一个优雅的S形曲线。
curve, wave, arch
straight, linear
noun
