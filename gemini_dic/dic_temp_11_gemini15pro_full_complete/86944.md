hemstitch:/ˈhɛmstɪtʃ/| n. v.| 抽纱；边缘缝纫；用抽纱法缝|A decorative stitch along a hem, made by drawing out several parallel threads and tying the remaining threads together in groups.|She hemstitched the edges of the handkerchief. 她用手绢的边缘抽了纱。|近义词：fagoting, drawn thread work|反义词: None|词性: noun, verb

hemstitch:/ˈhɛmstɪtʃ/| v.| 抽纱；边缘缝纫；用抽纱法缝|To decorate a hem by drawing out several parallel threads and tying the remaining threads together in groups.|She carefully hemstitched the tablecloth. 她仔细地给桌布抽了纱。|近义词：fagot, draw thread|反义词: None|词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->

From hem + stitch.  "Hem" comes from Old English "hemm" meaning "border, edge." "Stitch" comes from Old English "stician" meaning "to prick, pierce."
