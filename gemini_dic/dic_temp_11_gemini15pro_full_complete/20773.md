blanketed:/ˈblæŋkɪtɪd/
adj.|覆盖的，像毯子一样覆盖的|Covered completely, as if with a blanket.
The fields were blanketed with snow. |田野被雪覆盖。
近义词:covered, shrouded, enveloped
反义词:exposed, bare, uncovered
词性:形容词

词源: From the noun "blanket" + "-ed" (suffix forming past participles and adjectives).  Ultimately from Old North French "blankete," diminutive of "blanc" (white).
