arabicism:/ˈærəbɪzəm/ | n. | 阿拉伯语词汇; 阿拉伯风格; 阿拉伯文化的影响 | A word, expression, or other feature borrowed from Arabic.  A custom or trait characteristic of Arabs or Arab culture. The influence of Arab culture. | Many words in English are arabicisms, like "algebra" and "alcohol". 许多英语单词源自阿拉伯语，例如“代数”和“酒精”。 | synonym: Arabism, borrowing (linguistic) | antonym:  (No direct antonym, could consider words like "Anglicism" or "Gallicism" for borrowings from other cultures) | noun
