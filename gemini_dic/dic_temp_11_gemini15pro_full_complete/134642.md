nonmalignance:/ˌnɒnməˈlɪɡnəns/
n.|良性; 非恶性|The biopsy confirmed the nonmalignance of the tumor. 活检证实肿瘤为良性。|English Explanation: The state or quality of not being malignant; benignity.  It refers to a medical condition that is not cancerous and does not spread to other parts of the body.|例句：The doctor assured her of the nonmalignance of the lump. 医生向她保证肿块是良性的。

例句：Further tests confirmed the nonmalignance of the growth.  进一步的检查证实了增生是良性的。
|近义词：benignity, innocence|反义词:malignancy, cancerousness|词性:noun
