interrepellent:/ˌɪntərɪˈpɛlənt/
adjective
相互排斥的；互相排斥的
Causing or tending to cause avoidance between individuals of the same species, other than avoidance based on individual recognition or dominance relationships.  Relating to or denoting substances that cause such avoidance.
The researchers discovered an interrepellent pheromone in the ants, explaining their territorial behavior. 研究人员在蚂蚁身上发现了一种相互排斥的信息素，这解释了它们的领地行为。
Synonyms:  repulsive, deterrent
Antonyms: attractive, alluring
词性: adjective
