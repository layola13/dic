interposes:/ˌɪntərˈpoʊzɪz/
verb
插入，介入，干预
places or inserts between one thing and another.
The lawyer interposed an objection. 律师提出了反对意见。
近义词:intervenes, mediates, inserts, introduces
反义词:withdraws, removes, extracts
词性:v.

interposed:/ˌɪntərˈpoʊzd/
verb
插入，介入，干预 (过去式)
placed or inserted between one thing and another.
He interposed himself between the two fighters. 他挡在了两个打架的人中间。
近义词:intervened, mediated, inserted, introduced
反义词:withdrew, removed, extracted
词性:v.

interposing:/ˌɪntərˈpoʊzɪŋ/
verb
插入，介入，干预 (现在分词)
placing or inserting between one thing and another.
She is always interposing in other people's business. 她总是干涉别人的事。
近义词:intervening, mediating, inserting, introducing
反义词:withdrawing, removing, extracting
词性:v.


词源: From Latin interponere (to place between), from inter- (between) + ponere (to place).
