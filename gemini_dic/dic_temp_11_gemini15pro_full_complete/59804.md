ecraseur:/eɪˈkrɑːzər/ /ɪˈkrɑːzər/
noun
压榨器；勒除器；绞勒器
A surgical instrument, typically a wire loop, used to remove growths or tissues by tightening the loop around their base, causing them to be crushed and severed.
The surgeon used an ecraseur to remove the polyp.  外科医生用压榨器切除了息肉。
synonyms: snare, ligature, wire loop
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but conceptually: scalpel, laser - these cut rather than crush)
n.
