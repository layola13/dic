morris:/ˈmɔːrɪs/| n. |莫里斯舞，一种英国传统民间舞蹈|A traditional English folk dance involving men with blackened faces, bells, and sticks. |例句：The village held its annual morris dancing festival.  这个村庄举行了年度莫里斯舞节。

例句：The morris men danced through the streets. 莫里斯舞者在街上跳舞。


近义词：folk dance, jig |反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *morys, from Old French morisque (“Moorish dance”), from Spanish morisco (“Moorish”), ultimately from Late Latin Maurus (“Moor”). The dance was thought to be of Moorish origin, although this is now disputed.
