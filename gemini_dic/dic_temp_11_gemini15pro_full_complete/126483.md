mythopoeic:/ˌmɪθəʊˈpiːɪk/ | adj. |神话创作的，神话的| Relating to the creation of myths or having the qualities of a myth. |  The Lord of the Rings is a mythopoeic work of great power. 《指环王》是一部极具影响力的神话创作作品。 |  The artist's mythopoeic paintings depicted ancient gods and goddesses.  这位艺术家的神话绘画描绘了古代的神和女神。 | 近义词：mythical, legendary, mythological | 反义词：realistic, factual | 词性: adjective

mythopoeia: /ˌmɪθəʊˈpiːə/ | n. |神话创作，神话构建 | The creation of myths or the body of myths of a particular culture. | Tolkien's mythopoeia is incredibly detailed and immersive. 托尔金的神话创作非常详细，引人入胜。 |  The study of ancient Greek mythopoeia reveals much about their beliefs and values. 对古希腊神话创作的研究揭示了他们的信仰和价值观。| 近义词：mythmaking, mythology | 反义词：- |词性: noun
