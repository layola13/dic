larkishly:/ˈlɑːrkɪʃli/
adv.
轻浮地，嬉戏地
in a playful and mischievous way, like a lark
He grinned larkishly and winked. 他嬉皮笑脸地眨了眨眼。
近义词:playfully, mischievously, sportively
反义词:seriously, solemnly, gravely
词性:副词


lark:/lɑːrk/
n.
云雀
a small brown songbird
The lark sang sweetly in the morning air. 云雀在清晨的空气中甜美地歌唱。
n.
嬉戏，玩笑
a carefree, playful adventure or escapade
We went on a lark to the beach. 我们去海滩玩耍了一番。
vi.
嬉戏，玩耍
to play about; to have fun
The children were larking about in the garden. 孩子们在花园里嬉闹玩耍。
近义词:frolic, gambol, play, prank
反义词:work, toil, labor
词性:名词，动词


-ish:/-ɪʃ/
suffix
有点…的，像…的
somewhat like; having the quality of
He has a reddish beard. 他留着略带红色的胡须。
词性:后缀


词源:
From lark (n.) + -ish (suffix).  The adverbial form, larkishly, emerged later, building upon the playful connotations of the noun and verb forms of "lark."  The "-ish" suffix adds the sense of "in the manner of" or "like."
