disagree:/ˌdɪsəˈɡriː/ (US), /ˌdɪsəˈɡriː/ (UK)| vi.| 不同意，持异议 |To have a different opinion from someone else; to not agree.|  I disagree with his assessment of the situation. 我不同意他对情况的评估。

|近义词：differ, dissent, object, oppose|反义词：agree, concur, consent|词性:动词


disagreement:/ˌdɪsəˈɡriːmənt/ (US), /ˌdɪsəˈɡriːmənt/ (UK)| n.|分歧，意见不合|The state of having different opinions; a quarrel or dispute.| We had a disagreement about the best course of action. 我们对最佳行动方案存在分歧。

|近义词：conflict, difference, dispute, discord|反义词：agreement, accord, harmony, concord|词性:名词
