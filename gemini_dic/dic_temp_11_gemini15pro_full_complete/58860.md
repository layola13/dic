durabilities:/ˌdjʊərəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 耐久性，耐用性 | The plural form of durability, referring to the ability of multiple items or systems to withstand wear, pressure, or damage over time. |  The durabilities of these new tires are significantly improved compared to the older models. 这些新轮胎的耐久性比旧型号有了显著提高。|Synonyms: strengths, resiliences, lifetimes, endurances | Antonyms: fragilities, weaknesses | noun
