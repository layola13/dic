noncommiseratively:/ˌnɒnkəmɪzəˈreɪtɪvli/
adverb
不怜悯地，不同情地
without commiseration; showing no pity or sympathy.
He looked at the beggar noncommiseratively and walked away. 他毫无同情地看着乞丐，然后走开了。
近义词:unsympathetically, callously, heartlessly
反义词:sympathetically, compassionately
词性:副词
