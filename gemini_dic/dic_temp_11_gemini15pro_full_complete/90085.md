hostile:/ˈhɒstaɪl/ (UK) /ˈhɑːstaɪl/ (US)| adj.|敌对的，敌意的；不利的|Showing or feeling opposition or dislike; unfriendly.|例句：The local people were hostile to the idea of a new airport. 当地居民反对新建机场的想法。

例句：He gave me a hostile look. 他带着敌意地看了我一眼。

近义词：unfriendly, antagonistic, aggressive, inimical|反义词：friendly, amicable, welcoming, hospitable|词性：形容词

hostile:/ˈhɒstaɪl/ (UK) /ˈhɑːstaɪl/ (US)| n.|敌对者，敌方|An enemy or opponent.|例句：The two armies faced each other across the battlefield, hostiles ready to engage. 两军在战场上面对面，敌对双方准备交战。

例句：The natives, once considered hostiles, are now our allies.  曾经被认为是敌人的当地人现在是我们的盟友了。

近义词：enemy, opponent, foe, adversary|反义词：friend, ally, supporter|词性：名词
