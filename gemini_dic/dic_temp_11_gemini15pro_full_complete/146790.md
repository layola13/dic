overillustrated:/ˌoʊvərˈɪləstreɪtɪd/
adj.
过度插图的
Having too many illustrations.
The children's book was overillustrated, making it distracting rather than engaging.  这本童书插图过多，使其分散注意力而不是吸引人。
近义词:overdecorated, excessively illustrated
反义词:underillustrated, sparsely illustrated
词性:adjective


overillustrated:/ˌoʊvərˈɪləstreɪtɪd/
verb (past participle)
被过度插图说明的
Having been provided with too many illustrations.
The manuscript was considered overillustrated by the publisher and sent back for revisions.  这份稿件被出版商认为插图过多，并被退回修改。
近义词:overadorned, excessively illustrated
反义词:underillustrated, sparsely illustrated
词性:adjective (used as a past participle)
