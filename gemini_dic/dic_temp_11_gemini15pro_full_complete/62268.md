encyclopedia:/ɪnˌsaɪkləˈpiːdiə/ /ɛnˌsaɪkləˈpiːdiə/ | n. | 百科全书 | A book or set of books giving information on many subjects or on many aspects of one subject and typically arranged alphabetically. | I consulted an encyclopedia to learn more about the history of ancient Rome. 我查阅了一本百科全书，以了解更多关于古罗马的历史。| Example:  The online encyclopedia is a valuable resource for students. 在线百科全书对学生来说是一个宝贵的资源。 | Synonym: lexicon, reference book, compendium | Antonym: None | noun
