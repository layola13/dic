bursiform: /ˈbɜːsɪfɔːrm/
adjective
囊状的；钱包状的
Shaped like a pouch or purse.
The bursiform fruit of the plant hung heavy on the branch.  这种植物囊状的果实沉甸甸地挂在枝头。
The shepherd's purse flower has characteristic bursiform seed pods.  荠菜花有独特的囊状种子荚。
近义词：pouch-like, saccate
反义词: linear, elongated
词性: adjective
