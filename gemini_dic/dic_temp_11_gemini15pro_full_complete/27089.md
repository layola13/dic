calico:/ˈkælɪkoʊ/ (US), /ˈkælɪkəʊ/ (UK)| n. adj.|印花棉布，花斑猫|A plain-woven cotton cloth, typically with a bright floral pattern printed on one side.  Also used to describe a cat with a tri-color coat (typically white, black, and orange).|The dress was made of a cheerful calico print.|近义词:chintz, gingham (for fabric); tortoiseshell, tabby (for cat)|反义词:plain, solid (for fabric)|noun, adjective

calico:/ˈkælɪkoʊ/ (US), /ˈkælɪkəʊ/ (UK)| adj.|杂色的，花斑的|Having a spotted or parti-colored coat, typically of a cat.|The calico cat sat on the windowsill.|近义词:mottled, piebald|反义词:solid, monochrome|adjective
