dunning:/ˈdʌnɪŋ/| n. |催款，催债|The persistent dunning of debtors is a necessary part of running a business.  持续不断地向债务人催款是企业经营的必要组成部分。|例句：The company hired a collection agency to handle the dunning of overdue accounts.  这家公司聘请了一家收账机构来处理逾期账户的催款。

dunning:/ˈdʌnɪŋ/| adj. |催款的，催债的|Dunning letters are often sent as a final warning before legal action is taken. 催款信通常在采取法律行动之前作为最后警告发出。|例句：He received a dunning notice for the unpaid bill. 他收到了一份未付账单的催款通知。|近义词：pressing, harassing, badgering|反义词: forgiving, lenient|词性: adjective/noun

词源： From Middle English *dunnen, from dun (“dark-colored”), of uncertain origin.  The verb sense of "to demand payment" arose from the image of a debt collector, like a dark cloud, persistently hanging over the debtor.  名词意义上的“催款”源于讨债人像乌云一样持续笼罩债务人的形象。
