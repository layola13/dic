linoleic:/ˌlɪnəˈleɪɪk/ | adj. |亚油酸的；含亚油酸的 | An unsaturated fatty acid, C18H32O2, essential in the human diet.  It is a colorless oily liquid present in many vegetable oils, especially linseed oil and safflower oil. | Linoleic acid is an essential fatty acid. 亚油酸是一种必需脂肪酸。|  Linoleic acid is found in many vegetable oils. 亚油酸存在于许多植物油中。 |  近义词: polyunsaturated | 反义词: saturated | 词性: adjective
