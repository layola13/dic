incommunicating:/ˌɪnkəˈmjuːnɪkeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
不交流的，不沟通的
Not communicating with others; uncommunicative.
例句：He became increasingly incommunicating in his later years.  他晚年变得越来越不爱与人交流。

例句：The incommunicating nature of the group made collaboration difficult.  这个团体不沟通的性质使合作变得困难。

近义词：uncommunicative, taciturn, reserved, withdrawn
反义词：communicative, talkative, outgoing, sociable
词性: adjective
