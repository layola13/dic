out-greened:/ˌaʊtˈɡriːnd/
verb
超过...的绿色; 比...更环保
surpass someone in terms of environmental friendliness or ecological practices.  To be more environmentally conscious than someone else.
例句：The company out-greened its competitors by investing in renewable energy.  这家公司通过投资可再生能源在环保方面超过了竞争对手。

例句：She out-greened her neighbors by installing solar panels and growing her own vegetables. 她安装了太阳能电池板，自己种植蔬菜，比邻居们更环保。

近义词：outshine, surpass, eclipse in environmentalism
反义词：pollute more than, be less environmentally friendly than
词性：v.
