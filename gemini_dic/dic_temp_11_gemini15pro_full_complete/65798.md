evidence:/ˈevɪdəns/ (US) /ˈevɪd(ə)ns/ (UK)| n.|证据，证明，迹象|Something that furnishes proof.|The police found no evidence of a break-in. 警方没有发现入室盗窃的证据。|Synonyms: proof, testimony, indication|Antonyms: disproof, counter-evidence|noun
