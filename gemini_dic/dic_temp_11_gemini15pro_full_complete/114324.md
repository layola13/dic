maisonette:/ˌmeɪzəˈnɛt/ | n. | 小复式公寓；小型公寓 | A small apartment, often on two levels, within a larger building. |  Example 1: They live in a charming maisonette in the city center. 他们住在市中心一套迷人的小复式公寓里。

Example 2: The maisonette has a private entrance and a small balcony. 这套小型公寓有独立入口和一个小阳台。 | Near synonyms: apartment, flat, duplex | Antonym: house | noun
