medicommissure:/ˌmɛdɪkəˈmɪʃər/ | n. | 中缝 | A commissure of the brain, specifically the posterior commissure. | The medicommissure plays a role in the pupillary light reflex.  中缝在瞳孔对光反射中起作用。| Synonym: posterior commissure | Antonym: N/A | noun

medicommissure:/ˌmɛdɪkəˈmɪʃər/ | n. | 中脑水管 | The cerebral aqueduct, a passage connecting the third and fourth ventricles in the brain.  (This definition is less common but still used, sometimes leading to confusion with the posterior commissure). | Blockage of the medicommissure can lead to hydrocephalus. 中脑水管阻塞会导致脑积水。| Synonym: cerebral aqueduct, aqueduct of Sylvius | Antonym: N/A | noun
