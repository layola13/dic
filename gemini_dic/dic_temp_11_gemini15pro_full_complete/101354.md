ionicities:/ˌaɪɒˈnɪsɪtiz/ | n. |离子性，离子特性|The degree to which a substance is composed of ions.  It refers to the character of being ionic, relating to the formation of ions, or exhibiting the properties of ions.|The ionicities of the different compounds varied greatly. 不同化合物的离子性差异很大。|The high ionicity of the bond contributes to its strength.  这种键的高离子性有助于其强度。|Synonyms: ionic character, electrostatic character|Antonyms: covalency, covalent character|noun
