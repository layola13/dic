lithographically:/ˌlɪθəˈɡræfɪkli/
adjective
平版印刷地，光刻地
in a way that relates to or involves lithography
例句：The pattern is lithographically printed onto the silicon wafer.  这种图案是用平版印刷术印在硅片上的。

例句：Microchips are lithographically produced on silicon wafers. 微芯片是用光刻技术在硅片上生产的。

近义词：photolithographically
反义词：N/A
词性：adjective

Etymology:
From *litho- + -graphically*.  *Litho-* comes from Greek *lithos*, meaning "stone."  *-graphically* refers to the manner of writing or drawing.
