jantiness:/ˈdʒæntinəs/
noun
jauntiness (US)
快乐；轻松愉快；活泼
A quality of cheerful self-confidence; perkiness.
She walked with a certain jantiness in her step. 她走路的步伐轻快活泼。
Synonyms:  liveliness, perkiness, sprightliness, airiness, buoyancy, briskness, chirpiness, cheerfulness
Antonyms: dullness, lifelessness, gloominess
