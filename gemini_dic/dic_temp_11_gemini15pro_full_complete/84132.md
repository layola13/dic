half-Mexican:/ˌhæf ˈmɛksɪkən/
adj.
一半墨西哥血统的；墨西哥和其它血统混合的
A person who is of mixed Mexican and other (typically non-Mexican) ancestry.
My friend is half-Mexican; his mother is from Mexico City, and his father is American. 我的朋友有一半墨西哥血统；他母亲来自墨西哥城，他父亲是美国人。
She identifies as half-Mexican, embracing both her cultural heritages. 她认为自己有一半墨西哥血统，同时拥抱她的两种文化遗产。
近义词: biracial, multiracial, mixed-race
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) full-Mexican, single-race
词性: adjective
