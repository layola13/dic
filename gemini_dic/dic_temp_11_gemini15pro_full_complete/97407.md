infringe:/ɪnˈfrɪndʒ/ | v. | 侵犯，违反 | to act in a way that is against a law or right | Copying copyrighted material infringes on the copyright holder's rights.  复制受版权保护的材料侵犯了版权所有者的权利。| synonyms: violate, breach, contravene | antonyms: comply with, uphold, observe | verb

infringe:/ɪnˈfrɪndʒ/ | v. | 侵害，损害 | to limit or reduce someone's freedom or rights |  The new law infringes on our freedom of speech. 这项新法律侵犯了我们的言论自由。 | synonyms: encroach, impinge, trespass | antonyms: respect, protect | verb
