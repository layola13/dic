cadetships:/kəˈdɛtʃɪps/
n.
学员身份；见习军官职位；见习生职位
A cadetship is a period of training for someone who wants to become an officer in the armed forces or a merchant navy officer, or a period of training for a job in a particular industry.
He was awarded a cadetship in the merchant navy.  他获得了商船队的学员职位。
traineeship, apprenticeship, internship
n/a
noun
