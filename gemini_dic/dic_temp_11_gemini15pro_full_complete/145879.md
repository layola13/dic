overdesirously:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈzaɪərəsli/ | adv. | 过度渴望地，过分热切地 | Excessively or immoderately desirous; showing too much desire or eagerness.  It describes an action or feeling performed with an excessive amount of desire or eagerness.  It's formed by adding the prefix "over-" (meaning "excessive") to "desirously," which is the adverb form of "desirous" (meaning "having or characterized by desire"). | He overdesirously sought her approval. 他过分渴望得到她的认可。 |  The child overdesirously grabbed for the toy.  孩子过分热切地抓向玩具。| 近义词: excessively, immoderately, too eagerly, overzealously | 反义词: apathetically, indifferently, halfheartedly | 词性: 副词
