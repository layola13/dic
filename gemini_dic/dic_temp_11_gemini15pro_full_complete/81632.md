greed's:/ɡriːdz/| n.|贪婪,贪欲|Possessive form of greed, indicating something that belongs to or is caused by greed.  It refers to the consequences or manifestations of greed.|例句：Greed's grip on society is tightening.  贪婪对社会的控制越来越紧。

例句：The economic crisis was a direct result of greed's influence. 经济危机是贪婪影响的直接结果。|近义词：avarice's, covetousness's, rapacity's|反义词：generosity's, altruism's|词性:名词(possessive)
词源: From Old English *grǣdiġnes, from grǣdig "greedy".
