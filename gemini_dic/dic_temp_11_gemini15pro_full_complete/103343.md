jill:/dʒɪl/| n.|吉尔（女子名）|A female given name, a diminutive of Gillian, ultimately derived from Julian.  It can also refer to a girl or young woman in a general sense, though this usage is less common. |Jill is my best friend. 吉尔是我最好的朋友。| My neighbor Jill is a doctor. 我的邻居吉尔是一位医生。 |近义词：girl, lass (in a more informal or dialectal sense)|反义词:boy (if referring to Jill as a girl in general), man (if referring to Jill as a woman in general)|noun
