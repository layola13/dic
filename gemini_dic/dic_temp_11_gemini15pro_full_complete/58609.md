dullness:/ˈdʌlnəs/ | n. | 迟钝；沉闷；阴暗 | Lack of sharpness or brightness; lack of excitement or interest. | The dullness of the lecture made it difficult to stay awake. 这场讲座的沉闷让人昏昏欲睡。| synonyms: monotony, tediousness, boredom, flatness, vapidity | antonyms: brightness, excitement, vibrancy, sharpness, interest | noun

dullness:/ˈdʌlnəs/ | n. | 钝度 | The state of being blunt or not sharp. | The dullness of the knife made it difficult to cut the vegetables.  刀的钝度使切菜很困难。| synonyms: bluntness | antonyms: sharpness | noun
