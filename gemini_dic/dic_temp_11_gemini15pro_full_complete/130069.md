non-potential:/ˌnɒn pəˈtɛnʃəl/ | adj. | 非势的，非位的 | Not derived from or related to a potential function; not able to be expressed as a gradient of a scalar field. | The non-potential forces are responsible for the dissipation of energy in the system. 非势力是系统中能量耗散的原因。 | The magnetic field generated by a time-varying electric field is a non-potential field. 由时变电场产生的磁场是非势场。| 近义词: non-conservative, dissipative | 反义词: potential, conservative | 词性: adjective
