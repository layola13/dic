grittiness:/ˈɡrɪtɪnɪs/
n.
坚韧不拔，毅力，勇气，粗糙
The quality of being gritty; courage and resolve; strength of character.
Her grittiness helped her overcome many challenges.  她的毅力帮助她克服了许多挑战。
近义词: resilience, determination, toughness, resolve, fortitude, tenacity
反义词: weakness, fragility, timidity, cowardice
词性: noun

grit:/ɡrɪt/
n.
沙粒，勇气，毅力
Small, loose particles of stone or sand.  Courage and resolve; strength of character.
The road was covered in grit. 这条路覆盖着沙砾。
She showed true grit in the face of adversity. 面对逆境，她表现出了真正的勇气。
近义词: (for courage) determination, resolve, pluck, spirit, mettle
反义词: (for courage) cowardice, timidity, fearfulness
词性: noun

gritty:/ˈɡrɪti/
adj.
坚韧不拔的，有砂砾的，粗糙的
Containing or covered with grit; showing courage and resolve.
The story was a gritty portrayal of urban life.  这个故事是对城市生活的一种真实的写照。
He displayed a gritty determination to succeed. 他展现出一种坚韧不拔的决心要取得成功。
近义词: courageous, determined, resolute, tough, resilient, granular, coarse
反义词: smooth, refined, timid, cowardly
词性: adjective
