lecythis: /ləˈsɪθɪs/
n.
Lecythis tree; monkey pot tree; monkey pot nut
A genus of trees in the family Lecythidaceae, native to tropical South America.  These trees are known for their woody, urn-shaped fruits that contain edible nuts.
The lecythis tree produces a unique fruit called a monkey pot.

lecythis: /ləˈsɪθɪs/
n.
Monkey pot; monkey pot nut
The large, woody fruit of the lecythis tree, containing edible nuts.  The 'lid' of the fruit opens when ripe, allowing the nuts to fall out.
Monkeys are known to reach into the monkey pots for the nuts, sometimes getting their hands trapped.
