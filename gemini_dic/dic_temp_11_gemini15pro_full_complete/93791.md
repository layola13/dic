immerited:/ˌɪmɛrɪtɪd/ | adj. |退休的，荣誉退休的；享有退休金的|Having retired but allowed to retain an honorary title corresponding to the one held immediately before retirement.|Professor Jones is now immeritus professor of physics. 琼斯教授现在是物理学荣誉退休教授。|近义词:retired, emeritus|反义词:employed, working|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *immeritus*, from *in-* (not) + *meritus* (deserved, earned).  The word likely arose through confusion with *emeritus*.
