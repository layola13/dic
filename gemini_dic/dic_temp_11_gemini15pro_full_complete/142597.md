osteologic:/ˌɒstiəˈlɒdʒɪk/ | adj. | 骨学的 | Relating to the study of bones or the skeleton. |  Osteologic analysis revealed the individual's age and sex. 骨骼分析揭示了该个体的年龄和性别。|  The osteologic findings were consistent with the diagnosis. 骨骼检查结果与诊断一致。|  Synonyms: skeletal, bony | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider "non-skeletal") | adjective
