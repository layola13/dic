melanotype:/ˈmɛlənəˌtaɪp/
noun
黑蛋白照片，黑色素照片
A photographic process producing a black and white positive image on a non-transparent support.
Example: The melanotype was a popular photographic method in the 19th century.  黑蛋白照片是19世纪流行的摄影方法。

Example:  He found an old melanotype of his great-grandmother in the attic. 他在阁楼里发现了一张他曾祖母的旧黑蛋白照片。
Synonym: ferrotype, tintype
Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "color photograph" as a contrast)
n.
