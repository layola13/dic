elding: /ˈɛldɪŋ/
n.
焊接，熔接
The process of joining metal parts by heating their edges to the point of fusion.
The elding of the two pieces of metal created a strong bond.  两块金属的焊接形成了牢固的结合。
近义词: welding, fusing, joining
反义词: separating, detaching
词性: 名词


词源:
From Old Norse *eldr (“fire”) + -ing.  源自古诺尔斯语 *eldr（“火”） + -ing。
