contorted:/kənˈtɔːrtɪd/ | adj. | 扭曲的，歪曲的 | twisted or bent out of its normal shape. | The tree's branches were contorted by the strong winds. 树枝被强风吹得扭曲了。| His face was contorted in pain. 他的脸因痛苦而扭曲。| synonym: twisted, deformed, misshapen | antonym: straight, symmetrical | adjective
