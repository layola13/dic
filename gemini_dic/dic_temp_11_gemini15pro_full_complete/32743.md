chlorophylls: /ˈklɔːrəˌfɪlz/ | n. | 叶绿素 | Green pigments present in plants and algae that absorb light energy used in photosynthesis. |  Example: Chlorophylls are essential for photosynthesis. 叶绿素对光合作用至关重要。
 Example:  The chlorophylls in the leaves capture sunlight. 叶子中的叶绿素捕获阳光。| Synonym: pigment,  chlorophyll a, chlorophyll b | Antonym: None | Noun
