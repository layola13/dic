ceaselessness:/ˈsisləsnəs/
noun
不停, 不断, 无休止
The state of being ceaseless; continuity.  The quality of never stopping or ceasing.
The ceaselessness of the waves crashing on the shore was hypnotic. 海浪不断拍打海岸的声音令人着迷。
近义词: continuity, persistence, incessancy
反义词: intermission, interruption, cessation
词性: noun

Etymology: From ceaseless + -ness.  Ceaseless from cease + -less. Cease from Middle English cesen, from Anglo-French cesser, from Latin cessāre “to cease, stop, give over, leave off, abstain from, loiter, linger, tarry,” frequentative of cedere “to go, withdraw, yield.”
