cross-dressing: /ˌkrɒs ˈdrɛsɪŋ/ (UK) /ˌkrɔːs ˈdrɛsɪŋ/ (US)| n.| 穿异性服装；扮装；易装|The act of wearing clothing typically associated with the opposite sex.| He enjoys cross-dressing as a way to express himself. 他喜欢穿异性服装作为一种表达自己的方式。|Synonyms: transvestism| Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider "conforming to gender norms")| Noun

Etymology:
From 'cross' (meaning opposite or across) + 'dressing' (the act of putting on clothes).  The term gained popularity in the late 19th century.
