demephitize:/diːˈmɛfɪtaɪz/
verb
去除瘴气;净化空气;使无毒
to purify or rid of miasma or noxious influences; to make healthy
The city council implemented a plan to demephitize the polluted swamp. 市议会实施了一项净化被污染沼泽的计划。
The new air filtration system will demephitize the air in the building. 新的空气过滤系统将净化大楼内的空气。
近义词:purify, cleanse, sanitize, disinfect
反义词:pollute, contaminate, taint
词性:v.
