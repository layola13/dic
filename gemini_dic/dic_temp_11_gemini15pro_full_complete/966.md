acajou:/ˌækəˈʒuː/ (US), /ˌækəˈʒuː/ (UK)| n. |桃花心木，红木|A tropical American tree (Swietenia mahagoni) having hard reddish-brown wood used for furniture.| The desk is made of solid acajou. 这张桌子是用实心桃花心木做的。|Synonyms: mahogany, rosewood|Antonyms: softwood, pine|Noun
