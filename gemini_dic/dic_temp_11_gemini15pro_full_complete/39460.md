connexion:/kəˈnɛkʃən/ | n. | 连接，联系 |A link or association.| The police suspected a connexion between the two crimes. 警方怀疑这两起犯罪之间有联系. |近义词: connection, link, relationship, association |反义词: disconnection, separation | noun

connexion:/kəˈnɛkʃən/ | n. |亲戚关系;姻亲关系 | Family relationship; kinship.| He's a distant connexion of mine. 他是我的远房亲戚. |近义词: relation, relative, kinsman |反义词: stranger | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French connexion, from Latin connexiō (“a binding together”), from connectere (“to bind together”), from con- (“together”) + nectere (“to bind”).  Doublet of connection.  Primarily a British spelling.
