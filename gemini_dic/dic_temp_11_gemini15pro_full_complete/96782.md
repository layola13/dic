inequivalve:/ˌɪnɪˈkwɪvælv/
adjective
不等瓣的；两瓣壳不相等的
Having two valves or halves of a shell that are unequal in size or shape.
The oyster is an inequivalve mollusk. 牡蛎是一种不等瓣的软体动物。

inequivalve:/ˌɪnɪˈkwɪvælv/
noun
不等瓣壳的动物
An animal having two valves or halves of a shell that are unequal in size or shape.
Many scallops are inequivalves. 许多扇贝是不等瓣壳的动物。


近义词:asymmetrical, unequal
反义词:equivalve, equal
词性:adjective, noun

词源:in- (not) + equi- (equal) + valve (shell)
