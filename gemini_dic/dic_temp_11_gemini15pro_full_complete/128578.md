nicab:/nɪˈkɑːb/| n.|尼卡布（穆斯林妇女戴的面纱，只露出眼睛）|A veil worn by some Muslim women that covers the hair and face except for the eyes.|She wore a black nicab that concealed her features. 她戴着黑色的尼卡布，遮住了她的容貌。|近义词：veil, burqa, hijab|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用unveiled face or uncovered face表达相反的概念)|noun
