acned:/ˈæknid/| adj.|长粉刺的，满脸痘痘的|Covered with acne; having pimples.|例句：Her acned face made her self-conscious. 她满脸的痘痘让她感到很不自在。|例句：The teenager was embarrassed by his acned complexion.  这个青少年因为满脸痘痘而感到尴尬。|近义词：pimply, spotty, blemished|反义词：clear, flawless|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From acne + -ed.  Acne comes from a variant of Greek *akmē 'point, edge,' influenced by Greek akmai 'pimples.'
