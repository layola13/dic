death-warrant:/ˈdeθ ˌwɔːrənt/| n.|死刑执行令|A legal document authorizing the execution of a condemned person.  It is an official order for someone to be put to death.|The judge signed the death-warrant.|Synonym: execution order, death sentence|Antonym: pardon, reprieve|noun


death-warrant:/ˈdeθ ˌwɔːrənt/| n.|死亡通知书; 凶兆|Something that makes death or failure very likely or certain.|The latest drop in sales could be a death-warrant for the company.|Synonym: omen of doom, writing on the wall|Antonym: good omen, promise of success|noun
