melampyrite:/ˌmɛlæmˈpaɪraɪt/ | n. |红锌矿，红锌矿石|A rare mineral consisting of zinc oxide (ZnO). It is typically found in association with other zinc minerals, such as smithsonite and sphalerite.  It is usually red in color due to manganese impurities.|The geologist discovered a vein of melampyrite in the cave. 地质学家在山洞里发现了一条红锌矿脉。|Synonyms:  zincite (when pure ZnO)|Antonyms: None|noun
