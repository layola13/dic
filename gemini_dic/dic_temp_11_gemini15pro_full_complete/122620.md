modestness:/ˈmɑːdɪstɪs/ /ˈmɒdɪstɪs/| n. |谦虚，谦逊；适度，朴素|The quality of being unassuming or moderate in the estimation of one's abilities or achievements.  A lack of pretension.|She accepted the award with modesty. 她谦虚地接受了奖项。|Synonyms: humility, unpretentiousness, simplicity, meekness|Antonyms: arrogance, boastfulness, conceit, vanity|noun
