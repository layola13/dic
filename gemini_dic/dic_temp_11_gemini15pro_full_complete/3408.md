afore:/əˈfɔːr/| adv. conj. prep.|先前，以前，在…以前|Before.  Used chiefly in literary or folksy contexts.|The events mentioned afore happened last year. 上述事件发生在去年。|近义词：before, previously, earlier, formerly|反义词:after, afterward, later, subsequently|词性:副词, 连词, 介词

Etymology:
From Middle English *aforen, from Old English ætforan (“at the front, before”), from æt (“at”) + foran, fore (“before, in front”). Cognate with Scots afore (“before”), West Frisian foar (“for, before”), Dutch voor (“for, before”), Low German vör (“for, before”), German vor (“for, before”).

注意事项:
Afore is considered archaic or dialectal in modern English and is rarely used in everyday conversation.  It is more common in literature, particularly in historical novels or fantasy settings, and in some regional dialects.
