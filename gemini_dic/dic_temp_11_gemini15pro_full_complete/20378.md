bisulphite:/baɪˈsʌlfaɪt/ | n. |亚硫酸氢盐|A salt containing the HSO3− anion.  It is formed by the reaction of sulfur dioxide with a base in water.|Sodium bisulphite is often used as a food preservative. 亚硫酸氢钠常被用作食品防腐剂。|Synonyms: hydrogen sulfite|Antonyms: None|Noun
