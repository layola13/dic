nonincandescent:/ˌnɒnɪnkænˈdɛsənt/
adjective
非白炽的
Not incandescent; not emitting light as a result of being heated.
LED lights are nonincandescent light sources.  LED灯是非白炽光源。
The museum uses nonincandescent lighting to protect the artifacts.  博物馆使用非白炽灯光来保护文物。
Synonyms:  cool, fluorescent, LED
Antonyms: incandescent, glowing, burning
词性: 形容词
