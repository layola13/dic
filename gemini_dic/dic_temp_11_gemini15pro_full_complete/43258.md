cradleland:/ˈkreɪdlˌlænd/| n. |发祥地；摇篮|The place of origin of something; the place where something began or was nurtured. | Mesopotamia is considered the cradleland of civilization. 美索不达米亚被认为是文明的摇篮。|Synonyms: birthplace, origin, fountainhead, source, wellspring |Antonyms:  destination, end  |noun
