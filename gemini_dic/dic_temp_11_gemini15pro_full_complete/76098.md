furrowlike:/ˈfʌroʊlaɪk/| adj.|类似犁沟的，像犁沟的|Resembling a furrow; grooved.  A furrow is a long, narrow trench or groove, especially one made by a plow in the earth.|The old woman's face was covered in furrowlike wrinkles.|近义词：grooved, furrowed, channeled, wrinkled|反义词:smooth, flat|词性:形容词
词源: From 'furrow' + '-like'.  'Furrow' comes from Old English *furh, from Proto-Germanic *furhō, from Proto-Indo-European *perk-, meaning "to dig, scratch."
