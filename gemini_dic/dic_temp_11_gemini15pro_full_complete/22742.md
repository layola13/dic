borons: /ˈbɔːrɒnz/ | n. | 硼 | A metalloid chemical element with symbol B and atomic number 5. It is a trivalent nonmetal and is essential for plant growth.  It exists in several allotropes, including amorphous boron and crystalline boron. | Boron is used in fiberglass and semiconductors. 硼被用于玻璃纤维和半导体中。| Boron is added to steel to increase its hardness. 在钢中添加硼可以增加其硬度。| synonyms: metalloid | antonyms: metal, nonmetal | noun
