intertrochlear:/ˌɪntərˈtrɒkliə(r)/ (US) /ˌɪntərˈtrɑːkliər/| adj.|滑车间的，滑车神经间的|Located between the trochlea of the humerus (the bony prominence at the elbow) and another structure, typically referring to the nerve or the groove where it passes.  It pertains to the area between the trochlea, a grooved structure resembling a pulley, and another anatomical part.|

Example 1: The intertrochlear groove protects the ulnar nerve as it passes around the elbow.  滑车神经沟保护着尺神经绕过肘部。

Example 2: Intertrochlear neuritis can cause numbness and tingling in the ring and little fingers.  滑车神经炎会导致无名指和小指麻木和刺痛。
