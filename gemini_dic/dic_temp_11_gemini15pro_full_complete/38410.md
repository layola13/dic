concernedness:/kənˈsɜːrndnəs/ | n. |关心, 担忧, 挂念 |The state of being concerned; anxiety or solicitude. |She expressed concernedness about the rising crime rate. 她对不断上升的犯罪率表示担忧。|近义词: concern, worry, anxiety, solicitude |反义词: apathy, indifference, unconcern |词性: 名词

Etymology:
From concerned + -ness.  Concerned itself comes from the past participle of the verb concern, which traces back to the Old French concerner and ultimately the Latin concerner, meaning "to sift, separate, decide, concern."  The suffix -ness forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
