cheiroptera:/kaɪˈrɒptərə/ | n. |手翼目；蝙蝠|An order of mammals characterized by their ability to fly, commonly known as bats.  These mammals are the only ones capable of true, sustained flight. |例句：Cheiroptera are nocturnal animals. 蝙蝠是夜行动物。

例句：The study of cheiroptera is crucial for understanding the ecosystem. 对蝙蝠的研究对于理解生态系统至关重要。|近义词：bats|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
