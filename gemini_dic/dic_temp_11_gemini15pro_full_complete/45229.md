culottes:/kjuːˈlɒts/ /kuːˈlɑːts/| n. |裙裤；宽腿裤|Women's knee-length or calf-length trousers, cut wide so as to resemble a skirt.|She wore a pair of stylish culottes to the party. 她穿了一条时髦的裙裤去参加派对。|例句：The culottes were a popular fashion choice in the 1970s.  裙裤在20世纪70年代是很流行的时尚选择。|近义词：gauchos, palazzo pants, wide-leg trousers|反义词:skinny jeans, leggings|词性:noun
