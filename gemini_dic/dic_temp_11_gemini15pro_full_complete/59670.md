echinoidea:/ɪˌkaɪnɔɪˈdiːə/ | n. |海胆纲|A class of marine invertebrates, with a spherical or flattened shell covered in spines, belonging to the phylum Echinodermata.  They are commonly known as sea urchins.|例句：Echinoidea are found in all oceans, from shallow intertidal zones to deep-sea trenches. 海胆纲动物分布于所有海洋，从浅潮间带到深海海沟。

例句：The study of Echinoidea is crucial for understanding marine biodiversity. 研究海胆纲对于理解海洋生物多样性至关重要。|近义词：sea urchins|反义词:  (No direct antonym exists for a taxonomic classification)|词性:noun
