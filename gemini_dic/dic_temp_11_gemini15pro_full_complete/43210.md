crabwise:/ˈkræbwaɪz/| adv. adj.|横向地，蟹行地|Sideways; in the manner of a crab. |He edged crabwise along the narrow ledge. 他沿着狭窄的壁架横着身子挪动。|The crab moved crabwise across the sand.  螃蟹横着身子穿过沙滩。|近义词：sideways, laterally|反义词：forward, directly|词性:副词, 形容词

Etymology->
From crab +‎ -wise.  "Crab" refers to the crustacean known for its sideways movement, and "-wise" is a suffix meaning "in the manner of" or "in the direction of."
