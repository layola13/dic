deforestation:/ˌdiːˌfɔːrɪˈsteɪʃn/ | n. |森林砍伐；滥伐森林|The act of clearing a wide area of trees.|Deforestation is a serious environmental problem. 森林砍伐是一个严重的环境问题.|synonyms: logging, clearing, forest removal |antonyms: reforestation, afforestation |noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- + forest + -ation.  "De-" is a prefix meaning "remove" or "reduce". "Forest" refers to a large area covered chiefly with trees and undergrowth. "-ation" is a suffix that forms nouns of action or process.
