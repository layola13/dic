overhastiness:/ˌōvərˈhāstɪnəs/| n.| 过于匆忙，草率，轻率 |The overhastiness of his decision led to disastrous consequences. 他的草率决定导致了灾难性的后果。|English Explanation: Excessive haste; acting too quickly without sufficient thought or consideration. |例句：His overhastiness in selling the stock cost him a fortune. 他仓促出售股票让他损失了一大笔钱。|近义词：rashness, precipitateness, impetuosity|反义词：caution, deliberation, prudence|词性:noun
词源: From over- + hastiness, from hasty + -ness. Ultimately from Old Norse *hastr
