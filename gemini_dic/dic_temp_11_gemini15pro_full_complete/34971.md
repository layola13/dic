clodlike:/ˈklɒdlaɪk/ (US) /ˈklɑːdlaɪk/ (UK)| adj.|土块似的，笨拙的，愚钝的|Resembling a clod of earth; heavy, shapeless, and inert; also, stupid or insensitive.|He moved with a clodlike gait. 他走路的姿势笨拙，像块土疙瘩。|Synonyms: clumsy, awkward, lumpish, oafish, doltish|Antonyms: graceful, elegant, agile, nimble, astute|adjective
