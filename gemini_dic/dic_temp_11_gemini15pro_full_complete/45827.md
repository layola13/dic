customised:/ˈkʌstəmaɪzd/| adj.|定制的，个性化的|Made or altered to suit a particular person or task.  Modified according to a customer's individual requirements.|例句：She ordered a customised phone case with her initials. 她订购了一个刻有她姓名首字母的定制手机壳。|近义词：personalized, tailored, bespoke, individualised|反义词：standard, generic, mass-produced|词性:形容词
customise:/ˈkʌstəmaɪz/| vt.|定制，个性化|To modify something to suit a particular individual or task.|例句：The software can be customised to meet your specific needs.  这个软件可以根据你的具体需求进行定制。|近义词：personalize, tailor, adapt, modify|反义词：standardize|词性:动词
