(This tense doesn't apply to "begun" as it's a past participle.)


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
(This tense doesn't apply to "begun" as it's a past participle.)


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
They have begun the construction of the new bridge. 他们已经开始建造新的桥梁。
She has begun her studies at the university. 她已经开始在大学学习。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
They have been begun working on the project for months.  他们已经开始做这个项目好几个月了。（错误用法，begun不能用于进行时）


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
(This tense uses "began," not "begun.")


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
(This tense uses "was/were beginning," not "begun.")


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
By the time I arrived, the movie had begun. 在我到达之前，电影已经开始了。
He had begun to suspect something was wrong. 他开始怀疑有什么地方不对劲。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
(This tense uses "had been beginning," not "begun.")


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
(This tense uses "will begin," not "begun.")


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
(This tense uses "will be beginning," not "begun.")


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next week, they will have begun the renovations. 到下周，他们将已经开始装修。
She will have begun her new job by then. 到那时，她将已经开始她的新工作了。

将来完成进行时 (Future Continuous):
(This tense uses "will have been beginning," not "begun.")


虚拟语气:
If the game had begun earlier, we wouldn't have missed the first half. 如果比赛早点开始，我们就不会错过上半场了。
I wish they had begun the project sooner. 我希望他们早点开始这个项目。


被动语态:
(Begun is not typically used in the passive voice. "The project was begun" is grammatically correct but sounds awkward.  "The project was started" is preferred.)


疑问句:
Has the concert begun yet?  音乐会开始了吗？
Had the meeting begun before he arrived?  在他到达之前会议开始了吗？


否定句:
The project has not yet begun.  项目还没有开始。
The movie hadn't begun when we arrived.  我们到达时电影还没有开始。


词源: Old English *beginnan (attested only in past participle begunnen), from bi- "by" + *ginnan "to yawn, gape open, begin."

(Begun is not typically used in phrases. It's the past participle of "begin" and primarily used in perfect tenses.)
