hemlock:/ˈhɛmlɒk/ (UK), /ˈhɛmˌlɑːk/ (US)| n.|铁杉；毒芹；由毒芹制成的毒药|A poisonous plant with small white flowers, or a poisonous drink made from this plant.  Socrates was forced to drink hemlock. 苏格拉底被迫饮下毒芹汁。|Synonyms: poison, toxin|Antonyms: antidote, remedy|noun

hemlock:/ˈhɛmlɒk/ (UK), /ˈhɛmˌlɑːk/ (US)| n.|铁杉木|The wood of the hemlock tree, used in building and furniture. The cabin was built from hemlock logs. 小屋是用铁杉木搭建的。|Synonyms: timber, lumber|Antonyms: N/A|noun
