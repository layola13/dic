overinsolent: /ˌoʊvərˈɪnsələnt/
adjective
极其傲慢无礼的，过分傲慢的
Excessively insolent; arrogant to an extreme degree.
例句：His overinsolent behavior towards the teacher resulted in his suspension. 他对老师极其傲慢无礼的行为导致他被停学。

例句：The customer's overinsolent demands were met with a firm refusal from the manager.  顾客过分傲慢的要求遭到了经理的坚决拒绝。

近义词：insolent, arrogant, haughty, impudent, impertinent
反义词：humble, respectful, polite, courteous
词性：adjective
