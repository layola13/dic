annexment:/əˈnɛksmənt/
n.
附件；附加物；合并；吞并
the act of annexing something, especially territory.
例句：The annexment of the territory caused international outrage.  这片领土的吞并引起了国际社会的愤怒。

例句：The annexment of the smaller company increased the market share of the larger corporation.  对较小公司的吞并增加了较大公司的市场份额。

近义词：acquisition, incorporation, appropriation, seizure, takeover
反义词：cession, surrender, relinquishment
词性: 名词
