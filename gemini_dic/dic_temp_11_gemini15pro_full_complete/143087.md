outbrazens:/ˌaʊtˈbreɪzən/
verb
厚颜无耻地胜过；比…更无耻
To surpass or exceed in boldness or impudence.
He outbrazened his rivals by openly defying the rules. 他公然藐视规则，比他的对手更肆无忌惮。
Synonyms: outdare, outface, surpass in boldness
Antonyms: cower, recoil, shrink from
词性：动词
