freakishnesses:/ˈfriːkɪʃnɪsɪz/
noun
怪诞；奇异之处；古怪行为
The state or quality of being freakish; a collection of freakish qualities or occurrences.
His freakishnesses were tolerated because of his genius. 他的怪诞行为因为他的天才而被容忍。
synonyms: peculiarities, eccentricities, oddities, abnormalities, irregularities
antonyms: normalities, regularities, commonalities
n.
