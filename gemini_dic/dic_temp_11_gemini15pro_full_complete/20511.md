bivouacker:/ˈbɪvuækər/ | n. | 露营者；野营者 | A person who bivouacs, especially a soldier. | The bivouackers huddled around the fire for warmth. 露营者们挤在火堆旁取暖。| 近义词: camper, outdoorsman | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) indoorsperson | 词性: noun

bivouac:/ˈbɪvuæk/ | n. vi. | 露营；临时营地 | A temporary camp without tents or cover, used especially by soldiers or mountaineers. | The soldiers made bivouac for the night. 士兵们扎营过夜。|They bivouacked beside the river. 他们在河边露营。 | 近义词: camp, encampment | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) permanent lodging | 词性: noun, verb

词源: French bivouac, from Swiss German Biwacht, from bi by + Wacht watch.
