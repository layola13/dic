ceramic:/səˈræmɪk/| adj. n.|陶瓷的；陶瓷制品|Made of clay and hardened by heat.  A product made of ceramic material.|例句：The museum has a large collection of ancient ceramics.  博物馆收藏了大量的古代陶瓷制品。

例句：Ceramic tiles are often used in bathrooms and kitchens.  瓷砖常用于浴室和厨房。|近义词：pottery, earthenware, porcelain|反义词: - |词性: adjective, noun
