monoecism: /məˈniːsɪzəm/ | n. | 雌雄同株 | The condition of having both male and female reproductive organs in the same individual plant or animal. | Many cucurbits, like cucumbers and squash, exhibit monoecism.  许多葫芦科植物，如黄瓜和南瓜，都表现出雌雄同株的特性。 | Synonym: hermaphroditism (in animals), bisexual (in plants) | Antonym: dioecism | noun

monoecism: /məˈniːsɪzəm/ | n. | 雌雄同体 | The state of having both male and female reproductive organs in the same individual animal (less common usage than for plants). | Some snails demonstrate monoecism.  一些蜗牛表现出雌雄同体。| Synonym: hermaphroditism (in animals), bisexual (in plants) | Antonym: gonochorism | noun
