nontheatrically:/ˌnɑːnθiˈæt.rɪkli/
adv.
非戏剧性地，不在剧院上映地
Not in theaters; outside of the traditional theatrical context.
The film was released nontheatrically, going straight to DVD. 这部电影没有在影院上映，直接发行了DVD。
