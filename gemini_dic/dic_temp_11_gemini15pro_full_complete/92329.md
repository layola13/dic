hypnotize:/ˈhɪpnətaɪz/ /ˈhɪp.nə.taɪz/| vt.|催眠，使着迷|to put someone in a state of hypnosis; to fascinate someone|The magician hypnotized his audience with his dazzling performance. 魔术师以其令人眼花缭乱的表演催眠了观众。

The therapist hypnotized the patient to help them overcome their trauma.  治疗师催眠病人以帮助他们克服创伤。|近义词：mesmerize, entrance, captivate|反义词: awaken, arouse|词性: verb
词源: From French *hypnotiser*, from Greek *hypnōtizein* "put to sleep," from *hypnos* "sleep."
