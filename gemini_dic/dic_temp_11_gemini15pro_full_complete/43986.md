crimelessness:/ˈkraɪmləsnəs/
n.|无犯罪状态，没有犯罪|The state of being without crime. |The mayor boasted about the town's newfound crimelessness. 市长吹嘘该镇新近实现的无犯罪状态。|近义词:law-abidingness, peacefulness, security|反义词: criminality, lawlessness, disorder|词性:名词
Etymology: crime + -less + -ness.  From "crime" (meaning an illegal act) + the suffix "-less" (indicating absence) + the suffix "-ness" (forming a noun denoting a state or quality).
