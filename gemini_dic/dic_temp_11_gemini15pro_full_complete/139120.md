obediently:/oʊˈbiːdiəntli/| adv.|顺从地，服从地|In a manner that shows obedience; submissively.  Following instructions or rules willingly and without complaint.|The dog obediently sat when commanded. 狗狗在被命令时顺从地坐下。|近义词：submissively, compliantly, dutifully|反义词：disobediently, defiantly, rebelliously|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *obediently*, from Old French *obedientement*, from Latin *obediēns*, present participle of *obēdire* (“to obey”).
