berate:/bɪˈreɪt/| vt.|严厉责备，痛斥|To scold or criticize someone angrily and at length.|The coach berated the team for their poor performance. 教练严厉斥责了球队的糟糕表现。|Synonyms: scold, rebuke, reprimand, chide, chastise|Antonyms: praise, commend, compliment|verb

berattle:/bɪˈræt(ə)l/| vt.|1.发出格格声，2.使慌乱，使惊恐|1. To make a rattling sound. 2. To unsettle or confuse someone by intense criticism or questioning.|1. The old car berattled down the road. 老爷车发出格格声沿着道路行驶。 2. The lawyer's aggressive questioning berattled the witness. 律师咄咄逼人的质问使证人慌乱不堪。|Synonyms: rattle, clang, clatter (for sound); unsettle, fluster, perturb, disconcert (for unsettling)|Antonyms: soothe, calm, reassure (for unsettling)|verb
