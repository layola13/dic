encage:/ɪnˈkeɪdʒ/
verb
关入笼中；禁锢
To confine or imprison, especially in a cage.
The zookeepers encaged the lion. 动物园管理员把狮子关进了笼子。
The writer felt encaged by his fame. 这位作家感到自己的名声是一种禁锢。
Synonyms: imprison, confine, trap, restrict
Antonyms: free, liberate, release
词性:动词

encage:/ɪnˈkeɪdʒ/
noun
(罕见) 笼子，囚禁
The state of being encaged; confinement.
His encage was a gilded one, but still a cage. 他的囚笼是镀金的，但仍然是囚笼。
(This noun form is less common.)
Synonyms: confinement, imprisonment, captivity
Antonyms: freedom, liberty, release
词性:名词
