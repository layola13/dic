cleavingly:/ˈkliːvɪŋli/
adverb|紧贴地，粘着地，依附地|In a way that cleaves or adheres closely.|The ivy cleaved cleavingly to the wall. 常春藤紧紧地攀附在墙上。|closely, tightly, adhesively|loosely, separately
词性: 副词

词源:
From cleave (v.) + -ing + -ly.  The verb "cleave" has two contradictory meanings: to split apart and to adhere. "Cleavingly" derives from the second meaning.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *kliban, from Proto-Indo-European *gleib- “to stick, adhere.”
