nonfragility:/ˌnɒnfrəˈdʒɪləti/ | n. | 非脆弱性，反脆弱性 |  The quality of not being fragile; the ability to thrive and improve under stress and disorder.  It's not simply resilience, which means bouncing back to the original state, but the ability to become stronger in the face of adversity. |  The author's argument centers around the concept of nonfragility, showing how some systems actually benefit from shocks.  作者的论点围绕着非脆弱性的概念展开，展示了某些系统实际上是如何从冲击中受益的。

Antifragile systems demonstrate nonfragility by getting stronger under pressure. 反脆弱系统通过在压力下变得更强大来展示非脆弱性。 | 近义词: robustness, resilience, antifragility | 反义词: fragility, vulnerability, weakness | 词性: noun

Etymology:  Derived from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and "fragility" (from the Latin "fragilis" meaning "breakable").
