bubblinesses:/ˈbʌblɪnɪsɪz/
n.
活泼, 泡沫, 起泡
the quality of being bubbly; effervescence.
Her bubblinesses were infectious, brightening everyone's day. 她的活泼很有感染力，让每个人都开心起来。
The bubblinesses of the champagne added to the festive atmosphere. 香槟的泡沫增添了节日气氛。

近义词:effervescence, vivacity, exuberance, liveliness
反义词:flatness, dullness, apathy, lethargy
词性:noun (plural)

bubbly:/ˈbʌbli/
adj.
起泡的，活泼的
full of bubbles; effervescent.
The bubbly champagne tickled her nose. 起泡的香槟让她鼻子发痒。
She has a bubbly personality. 她性格活泼。

近义词:effervescent, sparkling, vivacious, lively
反义词:flat, still, dull, apathetic
词性:adjective

bubble:/ˈbʌbəl/
n.
气泡，泡沫
a thin sphere of liquid enclosing air or another gas.
The children were blowing bubbles in the park. 孩子们在公园里吹泡泡。

v.
冒泡，起泡
to form or produce bubbles.
The soup bubbled on the stove. 汤在炉子上冒泡。

近义词:(n.) orb, globe, sphere; (v.) fizz, effervesce
反义词:(v.) flatten, deflate
词性:noun, verb


词源:
From Middle English *bublen, from Old English *bublian, from Proto-Germanic *bublōną (“to bubble, swell”), from Proto-Indo-European *bew- (“to swell, blow”). Cognate with Dutch bubbelen (“to bubble”), German blubbern (“to bubble”), Swedish bubbla (“bubble”).
