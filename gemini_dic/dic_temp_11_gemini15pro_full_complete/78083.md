geoengineering:/ˌdʒiːoʊˌɛndʒɪˈnɪrɪŋ/
noun
地球工程
The deliberate large-scale manipulation of the planetary environment to counteract anthropogenic climate change.
Geoengineering is a controversial topic, with some scientists believing it could be a necessary tool to combat climate change, while others worry about its potential unintended consequences.  地球工程是一个有争议的话题，一些科学家认为它可能是应对气候变化的必要工具，而另一些科学家则担心其潜在的意外后果。
Geoengineering projects like solar radiation management are still in the early stages of research. 像太阳辐射管理这样的地球工程项目仍处于研究的早期阶段。
climate engineering, climate intervention,  planetary engineering
