baksheesh:/ˈbækʃiːʃ/ /bɑːkˈʃiːʃ/| n.|小费，赏钱，贿赂|A tip or gratuity, especially one given in some Asian and Middle Eastern countries. Can also refer to a bribe.|例句：He offered the official a small baksheesh to expedite the process. 他给官员一点小费以加快办理手续。

例句：Baksheesh is a common practice in some cultures.  在某些文化中，给小费是一种常见的做法。|近义词：tip, gratuity, bribe, kickback|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps penalty if referring to bribe)|词性:noun
