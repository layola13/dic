buffable:/ˈbʌfəbl/
adjective
可抛光的，可擦亮的
Capable of being buffed or polished to a smooth, shiny finish.
The car's paint is buffable, so minor scratches can be easily removed.  这辆车的油漆可以抛光，所以轻微的划痕很容易去除。
buffable leather shoes 可抛光的皮鞋
synonyms: polishable, burnishable
antonyms: unpolishable, non-buffable
词性: adjective
