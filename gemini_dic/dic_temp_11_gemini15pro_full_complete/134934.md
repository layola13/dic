nonmystical:/ˌnɑːnˈmɪstɪkəl/ | adj. | 非神秘的，不神秘的 | Not mystical; not involving or relating to mysticism; ordinary, commonplace. |  The explanation for the event was entirely nonmystical, involving a simple mechanical failure.  这个事件的解释完全是非神秘的，只涉及一个简单的机械故障。 | Examples of nonmystical experiences are brushing your teeth or walking the dog.  非神秘体验的例子包括刷牙或遛狗。| 近义词: mundane, ordinary, commonplace, prosaic, practical | 反义词: mystical, esoteric, spiritual, supernatural, occult | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  The word "nonmystical" is formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to the adjective "mystical".  "Mystical" derives from the Late Latin "mysticus," which in turn comes from the Greek "mystikos," relating to secret rites or mysteries.
