cheiloplasty: /ˈkaɪləʊplæsti/ (US) /ˈkaɪləʊplæsti/ (UK)| n. |唇整形术，唇成形术|Surgical repair or reconstruction of the lip.  It's a procedure used to correct deformities or defects of the lip, often due to birth defects like cleft lip or due to trauma.|She underwent cheiloplasty to correct her cleft lip. 她接受了唇裂修复手术。|Synonyms: lip surgery, lip reconstruction|Antonyms: N/A|noun
