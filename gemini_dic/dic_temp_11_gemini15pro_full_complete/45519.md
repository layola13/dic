curettement:/ˌkjʊrəˈtɑːʒmɒnt/ /ˌkjʊrəˈtɑːʒmənt/| n.|刮除术；刮宫术|The surgical procedure involving scraping or cleaning the walls of a cavity or other surface, such as the uterus.|She underwent a curettement after her miscarriage. 她流产后接受了刮宫手术。|Synonym: scraping, cleaning, debridement|Antonym: implantation|noun
