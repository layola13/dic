elastins: /ɪˈlæstɪnz/
n.
弹性蛋白
A protein forming the main constituent of elastic connective tissue; it is extensible, but returns to its original shape when the stretching force is removed.
Elastins give skin its elasticity. 弹性蛋白赋予皮肤弹性。
Elastins are essential for the proper functioning of lungs, arteries, and other tissues. 弹性蛋白对于肺、动脉和其他组织的正常功能至关重要。
synonyms:  collagen, fibrillin (related proteins)
antonyms: None (There isn't a direct antonym for a structural protein like elastin)
noun
