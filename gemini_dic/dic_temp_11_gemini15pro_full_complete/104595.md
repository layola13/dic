kantos:/ˈkæntoʊs/ | n. | 章節；篇章 | A major division of a long poem, such as an epic or narrative poem. |  The poem is divided into twelve cantos.  这首诗分为十二章。 | 近义词: chapter, section, book, part | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "whole" in some contexts) | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Italian *canto* ("song; chapter of a poem"), from Latin *cantus* ("song"), from *canō* ("I sing").
