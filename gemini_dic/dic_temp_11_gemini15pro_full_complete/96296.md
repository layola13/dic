indigene:/ˈɪndɪdʒiːn/ | n. |土著，原住民|A person who is indigenous to a particular place; a native.|例句：The government is working with indigenous groups to protect their land rights. 政府正在与原住民群体合作，以保护他们的土地权利。

例句：The indigenes of this island have a rich cultural heritage.  这个岛上的原住民拥有丰富的文化遗产。|近义词：native, aboriginal, original inhabitant|反义词：foreigner, immigrant, alien|词性: 名词

词源:
From Late Latin *indigenus (source of Old French indigene), from Latin indigena "sprung from the soil, native," from indu "in, within" + gignere "to beget."
