mapach:/ˈmɑːpɑːx/| n.| tablecloth; spread (especially a ritually used one)|A mapach is a cloth, often used in Jewish rituals, that covers a table or surface.  It can be plain or ornately decorated.|The mapach used for the Seder was beautifully embroidered.|Synonyms: cloth, covering, spread|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, possibly "bare" in the context of a surface) | noun
