chemiloon: /ˈkɛmɪluːn/
noun
化学气球/化学教学气球
A balloon used in chemistry demonstrations or experiments, often filled with gases like hydrogen or helium.  It may also refer to a balloon-like structure formed in a chemical reaction.
The chemiloon inflated rapidly as hydrogen gas was introduced. 化学气球随着氢气的引入而迅速膨胀。
The reaction created a chemiloon-like structure that floated briefly. 反应产生了一个类似化学气球的结构，短暂地漂浮着。
synonyms: gas balloon, reaction vessel (in some contexts)
antonyms: N/A
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
"chemiloon" is a blend of "chemistry" and "balloon." "Chemistry" ultimately derives from the Arabic word "al-kīmiyā'," referring to alchemy. "Balloon" comes from the Italian word "pallone," meaning "large ball."
