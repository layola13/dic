appointee:/əˌpɔɪnˈtiː/
n.
被任命者；受任者
a person who is appointed to a job or position
The new appointee will start work next week. 新任命的员工下周开始工作。

近义词: nominee, selectee, designee
反义词: appointer, nominator

词性: noun

词源: From the verb "appoint" + suffix "-ee", denoting the recipient of the action.  "Appoint" comes from Old French "apointier," from Latin "appunctuare," meaning "to add a point to," hence "to determine, fix, settle."
