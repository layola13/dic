fibrination:/ˌfaɪbrɪˈneɪʃən/
n.|纤维化，纤维蛋白形成|The process of forming fibrin, an insoluble protein that plays a key role in blood clotting.|Disseminated intravascular coagulation can lead to widespread fibrination. 弥散性血管内凝血可导致广泛的纤维蛋白形成。|
近义词：coagulation, clotting
反义词：fibrinolysis
词性: noun


fibrination:/ˌfaɪbrɪˈneɪʃən/
n.|纤维化，纤维蛋白沉积|The formation or deposition of fibrin, especially within the blood vessels.|Excessive fibrination can obstruct blood flow. 过多的纤维蛋白沉积会阻塞血流。|
近义词：fibrin deposition
反义词：N/A
词性: noun
