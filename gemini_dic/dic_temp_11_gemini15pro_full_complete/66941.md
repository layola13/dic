expensively:/ɪkˈspɛnsɪvli/
adverb
昂贵地
In a way that costs a lot of money.
She dresses expensively. 她穿着昂贵。
近义词:dearly, lavishly, richly
反义词:cheaply, economically, inexpensively
词性:adverb

词源: From expense + -ive + -ly.  Expense ultimately comes from Latin expendere "to weigh out, pay out."
