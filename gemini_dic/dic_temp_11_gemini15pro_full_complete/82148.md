groomish:/ˈɡruːmɪʃ/ | adj. | 整洁的，注重仪表的；像新郎的 |  Having the smart and neat appearance of a bridegroom; meticulously groomed.  | He looked very groomish in his new suit. 他穿着新西装，看起来非常整洁帅气。 |  例句：
He was looking quite groomish in his tuxedo. 他穿着燕尾服，看起来很精神。

He always maintains a groomish appearance. 他总是保持着整洁的仪表。

| 近义词：dapper, spruce, smart, tidy, neat | 反义词: scruffy, unkempt, disheveled, messy | 词性: adjective
