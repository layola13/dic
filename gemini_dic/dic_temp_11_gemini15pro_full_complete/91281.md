hydrographical:/ˌhaɪdrəˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
水文地理学的
relating to hydrography; the science of surveying and charting bodies of water, such as seas, lakes, and rivers.
The hydrographical survey revealed a previously unknown underwater mountain range.  水文地理调查揭示了一个以前不为人知的水下山脉。

The team conducted hydrographical studies to map the ocean floor.  该团队进行了水文研究以绘制海底地图。

synonym:  hydrographic, bathymetric
antonym:  N/A
词性: adjective
