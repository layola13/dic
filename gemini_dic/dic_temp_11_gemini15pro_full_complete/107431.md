lamper:/ˈlæmpər/ | n. |七鳃鳗; 吸血鬼 |A primitive eel-like fish with a sucker mouth, parasitic on other fish. |例句：The lamper attached itself to the salmon. 七鳃鳗附着在鲑鱼身上。

例句：Lampers are considered a delicacy in some cultures. 在某些文化中，七鳃鳗被认为是美味佳肴。|近义词：eel, hagfish |反义词: None |词性:noun


Etymology:
From Old English *lampreda, from Late Latin lampetra, from lampreda, from Greek lampetra, from *lampredein "to lick rocks," from *lambanein "to lick, lap" + *petra "rock."
