outwish:/ˌaʊtˈwɪʃ/
verb
胜过…的愿望；比…更希望
to wish more intensely than
例句：He outwished her for success. 他比她更渴望成功。

例句：She outwished everyone for the promotion. 她比任何人都更渴望升职。

近义词：outdesire, yearn more than
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider) be less desirous, be apathetic

词性：v.
