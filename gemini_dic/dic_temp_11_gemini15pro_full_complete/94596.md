imprecisely:/ɪmprɪˈsaɪsli/
adverb|不精确地，不准确地|not precisely; in a way that is not exact or accurate|The location of the treasure was imprecisely marked on the map. 宝藏的位置在地图上标记得不精确。|例句：He described the events imprecisely, leaving out important details. 他对事件的描述不准确，遗漏了重要的细节。

例句：She imprecisely estimated the cost of the project, leading to budget overruns. 她对项目成本的估算不准确，导致预算超支。

近义词：inaccurately, vaguely, loosely
反义词：precisely, accurately, exactly
词性：adverb
词源：im- (not) + precise + -ly
