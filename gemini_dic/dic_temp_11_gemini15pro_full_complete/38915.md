confessionalisms:/kənˌfɛʃənəˈlɪzəmz/
n.
忏悔主义，忏悔录体，自白体
Systems of belief or practice centered around confession, often within a religious context.  A focus on personal accounts of sin, faith, and spiritual experience.
The rise of confessionalisms in the 17th century led to an outpouring of personal religious narratives. 17世纪忏悔主义的兴起导致了个人宗教叙事的涌现。
The museum's exhibit explored the various confessionalisms of early American settlers.  博物馆的展览探索了早期美国定居者的各种忏悔主义。
近义词: confessions, testimonies, creeds
反义词: secularism, non-belief
