nonferventness:/ˌnɑːnˈfɜːrvəntnəs/| n.|缺乏热情，冷淡|Lack of fervor or enthusiasm; coolness of feeling.|His nonferventness about the project worried the team.  他对这个项目的冷淡让团队感到担忧。|The nonferventness of the audience was palpable.  观众的冷淡是显而易见的。|近义词：apathy, indifference, coldness, lukewarmness|反义词：fervor, enthusiasm, passion, zeal|词性:名词

Etymology:
From non- + fervent + -ness.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Fervent" comes from the Latin "fervere" meaning "to boil, glow." "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
