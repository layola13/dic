enthrill:/ɪnˈθrɪl/| vt.|使兴奋，使激动|To excite or thrill someone a great deal.|The roller coaster enthralled the children. 过山车让孩子们非常兴奋。|近义词：excite, stimulate, thrill, captivate, fascinate|反义词：bore, tire, weary|verb

词源:
From en- + thrill.  "Thrill" comes from Middle English "thrillen," from Old English "þryllan," meaning "to pierce," related to "þyrel" ("hole"). The sense of excitement likely developed from the physical sensation of a piercing or thrilling feeling.
