granite:/ˈɡrænɪt/ | n. |花岗岩 |A very hard, igneous rock, often used in buildings and monuments.  It is composed mainly of feldspar, quartz, and mica, and is typically grey or pink in color. | The kitchen countertops are made of polished granite. 厨房的台面是用抛光花岗岩做的。 | The monument was carved from a single block of granite. 这座纪念碑是由一整块花岗岩雕刻而成的。 | 近义词：stone, rock | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
