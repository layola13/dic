feel-good:/ˈfiːlɡʊd/| adj.|令人愉悦的； feel-good factor: 幸福感|Evoking pleasant feelings; promoting optimism and happiness.  Often used to describe films, music, or stories.  Sometimes used slightly disparagingly to imply a lack of depth or seriousness.|Watching a feel-good movie after a long day is always a good idea. 看完一天辛苦工作后，看一部令人愉悦的电影总是一个好主意。|Synonyms: heartwarming, uplifting, cheerful, positive|Antonyms: depressing, gloomy, disheartening|adjective

feel-good:/ˈfiːlɡʊd/| n.|令人愉悦的事物|Something that evokes pleasant feelings; promoting optimism and happiness.|That charity event was a real feel-good. 那个慈善活动真是令人愉悦。|Synonyms: mood booster, pick-me-up|Antonyms: downer|noun
