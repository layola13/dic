concetto:/kɒnˈtʃɛtoʊ/ (US), /kənˈtʃɛtəʊ/ (UK)| n.|概念，观念，构思; (音乐)协奏曲|A general notion or idea; a conception.  In music, a concerto is a composition for one or more solo instruments accompanied by an orchestra. |The architect's concetto for the new building was innovative and sustainable.  这位建筑师对新建筑的概念具有创新性和可持续性。

The pianist performed a breathtaking concetto by Mozart.  这位钢琴家演奏了莫扎特令人叹为观止的协奏曲。|近义词：concept, idea, notion, perception, design (in architecture), concerto (in music)|反义词:misconception, reality (sometimes), cacophony (opposite of concerto's harmony)|noun
