article:/ˈɑːrtɪkəl/ (US) /ˈɑːtɪkl/ (UK)| n.|文章；冠词；条款；物品|A piece of writing in a newspaper, magazine, or other publication; a particular item or object; a word (a, an, the) that identifies a noun as singular or plural, definite or indefinite.|

例句：I read an interesting article in the newspaper today. 我今天在报纸上读到一篇有趣的文章。

例句：This article of clothing is very expensive. 这件衣服很贵。


近义词：piece, item, story, report, essay|反义词: (For the "object" meaning) None; articles don't have direct opposites.|词性:noun


article:/ə, æn, ðə/| determiner (article)|冠词|A word used before a noun to show whether it is specific or general.  "The" is the definite article, "a" and "an" are indefinite articles.|

例句：The cat sat on the mat. 这只猫坐在垫子上。

例句：A cat sat on a mat. 一只猫坐在一个垫子上。


近义词： (No direct synonyms, as articles are a unique grammatical class)|反义词: None|词性:determiner (article)
