constance:/ˈkɒnstəns/ (UK), /ˈkɑːnstəns/ (US)| n. |坚定; 恒心; 忠贞|The quality of being faithful and dependable.|She showed great constance in her devotion to her family. 她对家庭的奉献表现出极大的忠贞。|近义词：fidelity, loyalty, steadfastness, perseverance|反义词：infidelity, disloyalty, fickleness|noun

词源:
From Old French *constance, from Latin constantia (“firmness, steadfastness”), from constans (“standing firm”), present participle of constare (“to stand firm”), from com- (“together”) + stare (“to stand”).
