kobotics:/koʊˈbɒtɪks/ | n. |  协作机器人技术；人机协作机器人技术 |  The study and development of collaborative robots, designed to work alongside humans in a shared workspace. | Kobotics is revolutionizing manufacturing processes by enabling closer human-robot collaboration. 协作机器人技术正在通过实现更紧密的人机协作来彻底改变制造流程。 |  collaborative robotics, human-robot interaction, cobots | N/A | noun
Etymology:  A portmanteau of "collaboration" and "robotics."
