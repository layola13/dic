neutrino:/njuːˈtriːnəʊ/ (US) /njuːˈtriːnəʊ/ (UK)| n. |中微子|A subatomic particle with no electrical charge and very little mass, which travels at almost the speed of light.|Scientists are studying neutrinos to learn more about the universe. 科学家们正在研究中微子以了解更多关于宇宙的信息。|Neutrinos are constantly passing through us without us even noticing. 中微子不断地穿过我们的身体，而我们甚至没有注意到。|Synonyms:  (No direct synonym, as it's a specific particle)  Related terms: lepton, fermion|Antonyms:  (No antonym for a fundamental particle)|noun
