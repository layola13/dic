depuratory:/dɪˈpjʊərətɔːri/ /dəˈpjʊrəˌtɔri/
adjective|净化的，清除的|Having the power to purify or cleanse, especially from bodily impurities.|Herbal teas are often touted for their depuratory properties.  草药茶常被吹捧具有净化特性。|Synonyms: purifying, cleansing, detoxifying|Antonyms: contaminating, polluting|adjective
depuratory:/dɪˈpjʊərətɔːri/ /dəˈpjʊrəˌtɔri/
noun|净化剂，清涤剂|A medicine or other substance that purges the body of impurities.|He took a strong depuratory to cleanse his system. 他服用了强效净化剂来清洁身体系统。|Synonyms: purgative, cleanser, detoxifier|Antonyms: contaminant, pollutant|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dēpūrātōrius*, from Latin dēpūrāre (“to cleanse, purify”), from dē- (“from, away”) + pūrāre (“to purify”).
