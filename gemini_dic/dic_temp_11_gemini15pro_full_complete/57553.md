drama: /ˈdrɑːmə/ (US) /ˈdrɑːmə/ (UK)| n.| 戏剧，戏剧艺术；剧本；激动人心的事件|A play for the theatre, radio, or television.  An exciting, emotional, or unexpected series of events or set of circumstances.|例句：The hospital drama continues with the arrival of a new patient.  医院的戏剧性事件随着一位新病人的到来而继续。|例句：Her life was full of drama. 她的人生充满了戏剧性。|近义词：play, theatre, spectacle, excitement|反义词：boredom, monotony|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *drama, from Ancient Greek δρᾶμα (drâma, “action, deed, play”), from δράω (dráō, “I do, act”).
