outbantering: /ˌaʊtˈbæntərɪŋ/
verb
胜过诙谐
Surpassing someone in witty repartee; exceeding in bantering.
He outbantered his rival in the debate, winning over the audience with his charm and wit. 他在辩论中以机智的玩笑胜过了对手，以他的魅力和智慧赢得了观众的支持。
She outbantered him with a quick and clever response. 她以快速而巧妙的回应胜过了他。
近义词: outwitting, outscoring, besting
反义词: losing to, being defeated by
词性: verb (动词)
