mangonist: /ˈmæŋɡənɪst/ | n. | 芒果爱好者；芒果专家 | A person who is especially fond of mangoes or knowledgeable about them. |  He's a true mangonist, able to identify different mango varieties by taste alone. 他是一位真正的芒果爱好者，能够单凭味道就能分辨出不同的芒果品种。 | Example 2: The mangonist gave a fascinating presentation on the history of mango cultivation. 这位芒果专家做了一个关于芒果种植历史的精彩演讲。 | Synonyms: mango enthusiast, mango connoisseur | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps someone who dislikes mangoes) | noun
