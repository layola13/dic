bewilderingly:/bɪˈwɪldərɪŋli/| adv.|令人困惑地，令人迷惑不解地|in a bewildering way, so as to cause confusion or disorientation.|The city streets were bewilderingly complex. 城市街道令人困惑地复杂。|Synonyms: confusingly, perplexingly, bafflingly|Antonyms: clearly, understandably|词性:副词

bewilder:/bɪˈwɪldər/| vt.|使迷惑，使不知所措|to confuse or puzzle someone|The sudden change in plans bewildered her. 计划的突然改变让她不知所措。|Synonyms: confuse, perplex, baffle|Antonyms: clarify, enlighten|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
