imperforata:/ɪmpərˈfɔːrɑːtə/
adjective
无孔的，闭锁的
lacking a normal opening; closed or not perforated.
The baby was born with an imperforate anus.  这个婴儿出生时肛门闭锁。
近义词:closed, atretic, occluded
反义词:perforated, open, patent
词性:adjective

imperforate:/ɪmˈpɜːrfəreɪt/
adjective
无孔的，闭锁的
lacking a normal opening; closed or not perforated.
The surgeon diagnosed an imperforate hymen.  外科医生诊断出处女膜闭锁。
近义词:closed, atretic, occluded
反义词:perforated, open, patent
词性:adjective


词源: From Latin *imperforatus, from in- (“not”) + perforatus (“perforated”), past participle of perforare (“to bore through”).
