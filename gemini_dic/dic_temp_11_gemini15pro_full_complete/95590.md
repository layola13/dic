incommensurably:/ɪnkəˈmɛnʃərəbli/
adjective
无法计量地，无比地，不能相比地
So large or great as to be beyond measurement or comparison.
The two projects are incommensurably different. 这两个项目有着天壤之别。
Her contribution to the project was incommensurably large. 她对这个项目的贡献巨大无比。
Synonyms: immensely, vastly, infinitely, boundlessly
Antonyms: measurably, comparably, finitely
词性: 形容词
