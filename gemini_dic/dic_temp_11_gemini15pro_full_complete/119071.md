metors:/ˈmiːtərz/| n.|流星|A meteor is a small body of matter from outer space that enters the earth's atmosphere, becoming incandescent as a result of friction and appearing as a streak of light.|We saw a meteor streak across the night sky. 我们看到一颗流星划过夜空。|近义词：shooting star, falling star|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, perhaps comet could be considered a contrasting celestial body)|noun
