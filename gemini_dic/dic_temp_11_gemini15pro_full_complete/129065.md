nitrocellulose:/ˌnaɪtroʊˈsɛljʊloʊs/| n.|硝化纤维素|A highly flammable substance made by treating cellulose with nitric acid, used in explosives and lacquers.|Nitrocellulose is a key ingredient in some types of gunpowder.|近义词：guncotton, flash paper|反义词: None|noun
