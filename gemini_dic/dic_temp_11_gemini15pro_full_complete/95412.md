incident:/ˈɪnsɪdənt/| n.|事件，事变|An incident is an event, especially one that is unusual, important, or violent.|There was an unfortunate incident on the train this morning. 今早火车上发生了一起不幸的事件。|Example: A serious incident occurred at the nuclear power plant.  核电站发生了一起严重事故。|Synonyms: event, occurrence, happening, episode|Antonyms: none|Noun

incident:/ˈɪnsɪdənt/| adj. |附带的，伴随的;易发生的，倾向于发生的|Likely to happen because of or connected with something else.|Skin cancer is more incident in fair-skinned people. 皮肤癌更容易发生在皮肤白皙的人身上。|Example:  The risks incident to the job are high. 这项工作附带的风险很高。|Synonyms: inherent, accompanying, related|Antonyms: separate, detached|Adjective
