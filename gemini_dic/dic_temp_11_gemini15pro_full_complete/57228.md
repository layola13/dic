downgraders:/ˈdaʊnˌɡreɪdərz/| n.|降级者，降低标准的人|People who reduce the quality, rank, or importance of something.  Those who advocate for or implement a downgrade.  |The downgraders were criticized for their pessimistic outlook on the company's future.  降级者因其对公司前景的悲观看法而受到批评。|Synonyms: detractors, critics, pessimists|Antonyms: upgraders, promoters, optimists|Noun

downgrader:/ˈdaʊnˌɡreɪdər/| n.|降级者，降低标准的人|A person who downgrades something. |The downgrader reduced the software version due to compatibility issues.  降级者由于兼容性问题降低了软件版本。|Synonyms: detractor, critic, pessimist|Antonyms: upgrader, promoter, optimist|Noun
