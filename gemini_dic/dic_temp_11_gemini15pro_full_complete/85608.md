haughty:/ˈhɔːti/| adj.|傲慢的，高傲的，自大的|Arrogantly superior and disdainful.|例句：She gave him a haughty look. 她傲慢地看了他一眼。|例句：His haughty manner made him unpopular. 他高傲的态度让他不受欢迎。|近义词：arrogant, disdainful, supercilious, proud, snobbish|反义词：humble, modest, unassuming|词性:形容词
词源: From Old French *hautif, *haltif  "high, proud, noble," from haut "high," from Latin altus.
