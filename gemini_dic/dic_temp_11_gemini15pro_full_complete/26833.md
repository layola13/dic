cahinca:/kæˈhɪŋkə/
noun
卡欣卡，一种药用植物
A South American shrub (Chiococca anguifuga) whose root is used as a diuretic and cathartic.  It's also known as "snowberry" or "David's root".
The cahinca plant is native to Brazil.  卡欣卡植物原产于巴西。
Cahinca root has been traditionally used to treat edema. 卡欣卡根传统上用于治疗水肿。
Synonyms:  snowberry, David's root
