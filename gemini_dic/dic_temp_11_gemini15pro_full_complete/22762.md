borrowing:/ˈbɒrəʊɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈbɑːroʊɪŋ/ (US)| n.| 借款，借用|The act of receiving something from someone with the intention of returning it.|例句：The library has a large selection of books available for borrowing.  图书馆有大量可供借阅的书籍。

例句：Excessive borrowing can lead to financial difficulties. 过度借贷会导致财务困难。

近义词：loan, debt|反义词: lending, loaning|词性:noun


borrowing:/ˈbɒrəʊɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈbɑːroʊɪŋ/ (US)| n.| 借用（词语）|Taking and using a word or expression from another language.|例句：The English language has a history of borrowing words from other languages. 英语有着从其他语言借用词汇的历史。

例句：The word "karaoke" is a borrowing from Japanese. “卡拉OK”一词是从日语借来的。


近义词：adoption, appropriation|反义词: None|词性:noun
