chaste:/tʃeɪst/| adj.|贞洁的，纯洁的，朴素的|abstaining from extramarital, or from all, sexual intercourse.  Not having any sexual nature or intention. Simple or restrained.|例句：She wore a chaste white dress. 她穿着一件朴素的白色连衣裙。|近义词：pure, virtuous, modest, decent, innocent|反义词：immodest, indecent, lewd, promiscuous|词性:形容词
chaste:/tʃeɪst/| n.|贞洁|The state or practice of refraining from extramarital, or especially from all, sexual activity.|例句：She valued her chaste above all else. 她珍视自己的贞洁胜过一切。|近义词：purity, virtue, modesty, decency, innocence|反义词：immodesty, indecency, lewdness, promiscuity|词性:名词
