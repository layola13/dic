multiculturalist: /ˌmʌltiˈkʌltʃərəlɪst/ | n. adj. | 多元文化主义者；多元文化的 | A person who believes in or supports multiculturalism; relating to or constituting several cultural or ethnic groups within a society. |  She considers herself a multiculturalist. 她认为自己是一个多元文化主义者。| synonyms: cosmopolitan, pluralist | antonyms: chauvinist, nativist | noun, adjective

multiculturalist: /ˌmʌltiˈkʌltʃərəlɪst/ | adj. | 多元文化的 | Relating to or constituting several cultural or ethnic groups within a society. | The city has a vibrant and multiculturalist atmosphere. 这座城市拥有充满活力和多元文化的氛围。| synonyms: diverse, pluralistic | antonyms: homogeneous, monolithic | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From multi- + cultural + -ist. Multi- means "many," cultural relates to "culture," and -ist denotes a person who adheres to a particular doctrine or system.
