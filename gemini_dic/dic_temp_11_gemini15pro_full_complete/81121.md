grammaticism: /ɡrəˈmætɪsɪzəm/ | n. |语法主义；语法癖；拘泥语法|A strong or excessive adherence to grammatical rules, often to the point of pedantry.  An instance of such adherence. | He was known for his grammaticism, correcting everyone's speech. 他以语法癖著称，总是纠正每个人的讲话。|  Her essay was marred by excessive grammaticism. 她的文章因过分拘泥语法而显得逊色。 |  pedantry, formalism | informality | noun
