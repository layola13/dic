humility:/hjuːˈmɪləti/ /hjuːˈmɪlɪti/| n. |谦逊，谦恭|The quality or state of not thinking you are better than other people; lack of pride.|She spoke with a humility that was unusual in a person of her wealth and position. 她说话的谦逊态度，在一个拥有她那样财富和地位的人身上是不常见的。|
近义词：modesty, humbleness|反义词：arrogance, pride|词性：名词

词源：
From Middle English *umilite, from Old French humilite, from Latin humilitas (“lowness, humility”), from humilis (“low, humble”), probably from humus (“ground, earth”).  最终源于拉丁语 humus（地面，土地）。
