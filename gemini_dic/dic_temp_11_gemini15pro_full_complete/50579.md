derail:/dɪˈreɪl/
verb
脱轨；阻挠；扰乱
To cause a train or other vehicle to leave its tracks. To obstruct the progress of something.
The train was derailed by a fallen tree. 火车被一棵倒下的树弄脱轨了。

The scandal threatened to derail his campaign.  这桩丑闻可能会破坏他的竞选活动。

近义词:obstruct, disrupt, hinder, thwart
反义词:advance, assist, facilitate, further
词性:verb

derail:/dɪˈreɪl/
noun
脱轨
The act of a train or other vehicle leaving its tracks.
The derailment caused significant delays. 脱轨造成了严重的延误。

近义词:divergence, deflection, deviation
反义词:continuation, progression
词性:noun
