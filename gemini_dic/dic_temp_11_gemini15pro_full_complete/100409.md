intertext:/ˌɪntərˈtɛkst/ | n. |互文性| The shaping of a text's meaning by another text. It can include an author’s borrowing and transformation of a prior text or to a reader’s referencing of one text in reading another.| The novel's intertextuality with classic mythology adds depth to its themes. 这部小说与经典神话的互文性增加了其主题的深度。|  allusion, echo, reference | originality, autonomy | noun
