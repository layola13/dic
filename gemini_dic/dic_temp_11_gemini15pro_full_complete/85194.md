hariali: /ˌhɑːriˈɑːli/ | adj. n.| 亮绿色的，翠绿色的; 一种翠绿色的颜色 |A bright, vibrant green color, typically associated with new vegetation or spring.  It can also refer to a dish made with green leafy vegetables. | The fields were a vibrant hariali after the monsoon.  田野在季风过后呈现出一片翠绿。|  The hariali chicken curry was delicious.  翠绿鸡肉咖喱非常美味。 | synonyms: verdant, emerald, jade, lush | antonyms: brown, arid, withered | adjective, noun
