outsallies:/ˈaʊtsæliz/| n.| 外围工事；外围阵地；外围防线；边远地区| A fortified position or outpost established outside or beyond the main defensive line. Also, remote or outlying districts of a city or region.| The army built outsallies around the castle to protect it from attack. 军队在城堡周围建造了外围工事以抵御攻击。|The outsallies of the city were underdeveloped and lacked basic amenities. 城市的边远地区欠发达，缺乏基本设施。| synonyms: outposts, ramparts, bulwarks, perimeters, outskirts, fringes | antonyms: center, core, interior, heartland | noun
