hypernormalnesses:/ˌhaɪpərˈnɔːrməlnəsəz/
|n.| 超常态 |A state of affairs where things appear superficially normal, but underlying conditions are extremely unstable or dysfunctional, often due to prolonged exposure to abnormal or extreme circumstances.|The citizens lived in a state of hypernormalness, outwardly compliant but inwardly seething with resentment. 市民们生活在一种超常态中，表面上顺从，内心却充满了怨恨。|例句：The dictator maintained a facade of hypernormalness through propaganda and public spectacles. 独裁者通过宣传和公共表演维持了一种超常态的表面现象。|例句：Despite the economic collapse, people tried to maintain a sense of hypernormalness by continuing their daily routines. 尽管经济崩溃，人们还是试图通过继续他们的日常生活来维持一种超常态的感觉。|近义词: artificiality, facade, pretense|反义词: normalcy, genuineness, authenticity|词性:名词
