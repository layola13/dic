overcanopy: /ˈoʊvərˌkænəpi/ | n. | 上层树冠，林冠上层 | The overcanopy consists of the tallest trees in a forest, emerging above the general canopy layer.  上层树冠由森林中最高的树木组成，出现在普通树冠层之上。| The monkeys swung effortlessly through the overcanopy. 猴子轻松地在林冠上层荡来荡去。 | 近义词: emergent layer, upper canopy | 反义词: understory, forest floor | 词性: 名词

overcanopy:  | adj. | 林冠上层的 | Overcanopy trees play a vital role in the forest ecosystem. 林冠上层的树木在森林生态系统中起着至关重要的作用。 | Researchers are studying the overcanopy biodiversity of the Amazon rainforest.  研究人员正在研究亚马逊雨林林冠上层的生物多样性。| 近义词: emergent,  upper canopy | 反义词: understory,  forest floor | 词性: 形容词


词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "above" or "excessive") + "canopy" (from Latin "canōpēum" meaning "mosquito net," later referring to a covering or shelter).  由表示“上方”或“过度”的前缀"over-"和来自拉丁语"canōpēum"（意为“蚊帐”，后来指覆盖物或遮蔽物）的"canopy"组成。
