antivivisectionist: /ˌæntiˌvɪvɪˈsɛkʃənɪst/ /ˌænˌtaɪˌvɪvəˈsɛkʃənɪst/
n.
反对活体解剖者，反活体解剖主义者
A person who opposes the practice of vivisection (performing operations on live animals for scientific research).
例句：The antivivisectionist protested outside the laboratory.  反活体解剖者在实验室外抗议。

例句：She became a passionate antivivisectionist after learning about the suffering of animals in research.  在了解到用于研究的动物所遭受的痛苦后，她成为了一名充满激情的反活体解剖主义者。

近义词：animal rights activist, animal welfare advocate
反义词:pro-vivisectionist (少用) , supporter of animal testing
词性:noun
