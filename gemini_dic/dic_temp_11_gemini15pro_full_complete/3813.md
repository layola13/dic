agnosticism:/æɡˈnɑːstɪsɪzəm/
|n.|不可知论|a philosophical belief that humans cannot know whether God exists or not|Many scientists find themselves drawn to agnosticism. 许多科学家发现自己被不可知论所吸引。|
|近义词：skepticism, doubt, unbelief|反义词：faith, belief, theism|词性：名词|

agnostic:/æɡˈnɑːstɪk/
|n.|不可知论者|a person who believes that nothing is known or can be known of the existence or nature of God or of anything beyond material phenomena|He described himself as an agnostic. 他自称是一个不可知论者。|
|近义词：skeptic, doubter|反义词：believer, theist|词性：名词|

agnostic:/æɡˈnɑːstɪk/
|adj.|不可知论的|relating to agnosticism|He has an agnostic view of religion. 他对宗教持不可知论的观点。|
|近义词：skeptical, doubting|反义词：believing, theistic|词性：形容词|
