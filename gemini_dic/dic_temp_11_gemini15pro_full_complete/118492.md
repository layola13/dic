mesmerizability:/ˈmɛzməˌraɪzəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可催眠性；易受催眠性 | The quality of being easily mesmerized; susceptibility to mesmerism. |  Her mesmerizability made her a perfect subject for the hypnotist's stage show.  她易受催眠的特质使她成为催眠师舞台表演的完美对象。 | Synonyms: hypnotizability, suggestibility | Antonyms: resistance, imperviousness | noun

mesmerize:/ˈmɛzməˌraɪz/ | vt. |  催眠；迷住；使着迷 | To hypnotize or fascinate someone. | The magician mesmerized the audience with his incredible illusions. 魔术师以其令人难以置信的幻觉迷住了观众。 | Synonyms: hypnotize, captivate, enthrall | Antonyms: bore, repel, disenchant | verb
