binocularities:/bɪˌnɒkjʊˈlærɪtiz/
n.
双眼视觉；用双筒望远镜观察
The act of seeing with both eyes, enabling perception of depth and distance; using binoculars for observation.
He used his powerful binocularities to scan the horizon. 他用他强大的双筒望远镜扫视地平线。
Near Synonyms: stereopsis, binocular vision
Antonyms: monocular vision
词性: 名词
