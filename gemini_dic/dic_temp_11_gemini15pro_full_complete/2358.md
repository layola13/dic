adiaphorism:/ˌeɪdiˈæfərɪzəm/ | n. | 中立主义；无关紧要的事；可有可无之事 |The doctrine that certain actions or matters, especially in religion, are morally neutral and neither commanded nor forbidden. adiaphoristic:/ˌeɪdiˌæfəˈrɪstɪk/ | adj. | 中立的；无关紧要的 |  Relating to adiaphorism; morally neutral. |  His adiaphoristic attitude towards the dress code annoyed his colleagues. 他对穿着规定的中立态度惹恼了他的同事。 |  The committee took an adiaphoristic stance on the issue. 委员会在这个问题上采取了中立的立场。 | synonyms: neutral, indifferent, unconcerned | antonyms: biased, prejudiced, partial | adjective
