detorsion: /diˈtɔːrʃən/ /diˈtɔrʒən/
n.
扭转复位；解扭
The act of untwisting or removing a torsion, especially of an organ or part of the body.
Detorsion of the testicle was performed emergently.  睾丸扭转复位手术紧急进行。
近义词: untwisting, unwinding
反义词: torsion
词性: 名词

detorsion: /diˈtɔːrʃən/ /diˈtɔrʒən/
v.
扭转复位；解扭
To untwist or unwind something, especially an organ or part of the body that has undergone torsion.
The surgeon detorsed the ovary. 外科医生将卵巢扭转复位。
近义词: untwist, unwind
反义词:  torse
词性: 动词
