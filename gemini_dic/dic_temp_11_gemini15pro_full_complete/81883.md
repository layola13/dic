grieves:/ɡriːvz/| v.| 悲伤，悲痛，哀悼|To feel or express deep sadness, especially due to someone's death.|She still grieves for her lost child. 她仍然为她失去的孩子感到悲伤。|近义词：mourns, sorrows, laments|反义词：rejoices, celebrates|词性:动词

grieves:/ɡriːvz/| n.| 悲伤，悲痛，哀悼 (罕用，通常用复数形式)|Deep sadness, especially due to someone's death.  (This noun form is less common than the verb.)|Her grieves were overwhelming. 她的悲伤难以承受。|近义词：sorrows, griefs|反义词：joys, celebrations|词性:名词
