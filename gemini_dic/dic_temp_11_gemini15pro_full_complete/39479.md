connoisseur:/ˌkɑːnəˈsɜːr/ (US) /ˌkɒnəˈsɜː(r)/ (UK)| n. |鉴赏家；行家|An expert judge in matters of taste, typically in the fine arts, food, or drink.  A person who has expert knowledge and keen discrimination, especially in the arts, or in matters of taste.|例句：He is a connoisseur of fine wines. 他是一位品酒行家。

例句：She is a connoisseur of modern art. 她是一位现代艺术鉴赏家。

近义词：expert, specialist, aficionado, buff|反义词：philistine, ignoramus|词性：noun
