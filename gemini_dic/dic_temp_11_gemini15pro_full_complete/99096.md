intercalary: /ɪnˈtɜːrkəleri/ (UK) /ɪnˈtɜːrkəˌleri/ (US)
| adj. | 闰的，插入的 |Inserted into the calendar, such as an extra day or month.  |例句：The intercalary day in a leap year keeps the calendar aligned with the solar year. 闰年中的闰日使日历与太阳年保持一致。|近义词：inserted, added, extra, interpolated |反义词: omitted, removed |词性: adjective

intercalary: /ɪnˈtɜːrkəleri/ (UK) /ɪnˈtɜːrkəˌleri/ (US)
| n. | 闰日; 闰月 |An intercalary day or month. |例句：February 29th is an intercalary day. 2月29日是闰日。|近义词：insertion, addition |反义词: omission, removal |词性: noun
