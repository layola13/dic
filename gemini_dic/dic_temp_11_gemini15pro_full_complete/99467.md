interfascicular:/ˌɪntərˈfæsɪkjʊlər/| adj.|束间的，束膜间的|Located between fascicles (bundles of muscle fibers or nerve fibers).|The interfascicular cambium is responsible for the increase in girth of the plant stem.  束间形成层负责植物茎的周长增加。|Interfascicular injection is a method of administering medication.|束间注射是一种给药方法。|近义词:interbundle|反义词:intrafascicular|词性:adjective
