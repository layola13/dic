mammillary:/ˈmæməˌlɛri/ (US), /məˈmɪləri/ (UK)| adj.|乳头的，乳头状的|Relating to or resembling a nipple or nipples; relating to the mammillary bodies (a pair of small round bodies, forming part of the hypothalamus, located on the undersurface of the brain).|The mammillary bodies are involved in memory. 乳头体与记忆有关。|The cactus has mammillary projections. 仙人掌有乳头状突起。|nipple-like, papillary|N/A|adjective
