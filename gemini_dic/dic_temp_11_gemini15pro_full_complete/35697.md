cobweb:/ˈkɒbwɛb/(UK) /ˈkɑːbwɛb/(US)| n.|蜘蛛网；蛛丝；陈腐的东西；混乱|A cobweb is a net of sticky silk threads made by a spider to catch insects. It can also refer to something old, neglected, or dusty, or a tangled and confusing situation.|例句：The old house was filled with cobwebs. 老房子里满是蜘蛛网。

例句：His mind felt like a cobweb of confused thoughts. 他的思绪像一团乱麻。|近义词：spiderweb, gossamer|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用clean, tidy)|词性: noun
