overfiltered:/ˌoʊvərˈfɪltərd/| adj.|过度过滤的|Filtered excessively, often referring to a photograph or image where the smoothing or blurring effect has been applied too heavily, resulting in a loss of detail and an artificial appearance.|The photo was so overfiltered it looked like a painting. 这张照片过度过滤了，看起来像一幅画。|Example: Her Instagram photos are so overfiltered that her skin looks unnaturally smooth. 她的Instagram照片过度美颜，以至于她的皮肤看起来很不自然地光滑。|Synonyms: over-processed, over-edited, airbrushed|Antonyms: unfiltered, natural, raw|adjective
