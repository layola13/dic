disheveled:/dɪˈʃevəld/| adj.|蓬乱的，凌乱的|Being in a hurry, her hair was disheveled and her clothes were rumpled. |例句：He looked disheveled, as if he'd just woken up. 他看起来衣冠不整，好像刚睡醒一样。

例句：The wind left her hair disheveled. 风吹乱了她的头发。

近义词：untidy, messy, rumpled, tousled|反义词：tidy, neat, orderly|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- + shevel, from Old French chevel "hair," from Latin capillus "hair."  The original meaning was "to deprive of hair," later evolving to "disarrange the hair," and finally to describe a generally untidy appearance.
