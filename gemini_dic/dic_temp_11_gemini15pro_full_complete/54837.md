dispiteous:/dɪˈspɪtiəs/
adjective
无情的，残酷的，无情的
Showing or causing a lack of pity or compassion; merciless.
例句：The dispiteous king ordered the execution of all the prisoners.  残忍的国王下令处死所有囚犯。

例句：The dispiteous winter storm left many people homeless.  无情的暴风雪使许多人无家可归。

近义词：ruthless, merciless, pitiless, cruel, inhumane
反义词：merciful, compassionate, kind, humane
词性：adjective
