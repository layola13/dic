cultivations:/ˌkʌltɪˈveɪʃənz/
|n.|耕作，栽培；培养；教化|The cultivations of the fields have yielded a bountiful harvest.|田地的耕作带来了丰收。|近义词:farming, agriculture, tillage, nurturing, development|反义词:neglect, abandonment|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin *cultivatio, from Latin cultivatus, past participle of cultivare "to cultivate," from cultus "care, cultivation," originally past participle of colere "to till, cultivate, inhabit."
