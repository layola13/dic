kidneywort:/ˈkɪdniwɜːrt/| n. |虎耳草，肾叶草|A plant of the genus *Saxifraga*, especially *Saxifraga stellaris*, typically growing in mountainous or rocky terrain.  It is known for its kidney-shaped leaves.|Kidneywort thrives in damp, shady crevices. 虎耳草在潮湿阴暗的缝隙中生长茂盛。| Kidneywort carpets the rocks beside the stream.  溪流边的岩石上覆盖着虎耳草。|Synonyms: saxifrage, rockfoil|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
