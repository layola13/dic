n.
青梅, 一种欧洲李子
A small, round, sweet greenish plum.
The greengage tree in my garden is laden with fruit. 我花园里的青梅树果实累累。

近义词: plum, gage
反义词: None (fruit doesn't have direct opposites)
词性: Noun


词源:  From the French "greengage," a misspelling of "reine-claude," meaning "Queen Claude," referring to Claude of France.
