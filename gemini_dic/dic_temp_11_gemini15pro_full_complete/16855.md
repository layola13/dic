basidiomycetes:/bəˌsɪdioʊˈmaɪsiːtiz/| n. |担子菌纲|A large and diverse class of fungi characterized by the production of sexual spores (basidiospores) on a specialized cell called a basidium.  These fungi include mushrooms, puffballs, rusts, and smuts.|例句：Basidiomycetes play a vital role in forest ecosystems. 担子菌在森林生态系统中起着至关重要的作用。

例句：Many basidiomycetes are edible, while others are poisonous. 许多担子菌可以食用，而另一些则有毒。|近义词：Basidiomycota|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From  *basidium* + *-mycetes*

*basidium*: from New Latin, diminutive of Greek *basis* "base."
*-mycetes*:  a taxonomic suffix denoting a class of fungi.
