aid's: /eɪdz/ | n. | 艾滋病; 获得性免疫缺陷综合症 | Acquired Immune Deficiency Syndrome, a disease in which there is a severe loss of the body's cellular immunity, greatly lowering the resistance to infection and malignancy. |  Example: AIDS is a serious global health issue. 艾滋病是一个严重的全球健康问题。 | Example:  Many organizations are working to find a cure for AIDS. 许多组织正在努力寻找治疗艾滋病的方法。 | Synonym: HIV infection, immunodeficiency | Antonym:  immunity, health | Noun
