misanthropically:/ˌmɪsənˈθrɒpɪkli/ /ˌmɪsənˈθrɑːpɪkli/ | adv. | 厌世地，遁世地，仇恨人类地 | In a way that shows a dislike of humankind. | He glared misanthropically at the noisy crowd. 他厌世地瞪着嘈杂的人群。|  The hermit lived misanthropically in the woods, avoiding all human contact.  这位隐士厌世地住在树林里，避免与所有人接触。 | 近义词：cynically, pessimistically, unsociably | 反义词：philanthropically, humanely, charitably | 词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From misanthropic + -ally.
Misanthropic: from Greek *misanthrōpos* "hating mankind," from *misein* "to hate" + *anthrōpos* "man."
