condenser:/kənˈdensər/ (US), /kənˈdɛnsə(r)/ (UK)| n.|冷凝器；电容器；聚光器|A device that cools a gas or vapor to a liquid state, or an electrical component that stores energy.|The condenser in the air conditioning unit needs cleaning. 空调机组中的冷凝器需要清洁。|synonyms: capacitor, cooler, chiller|antonyms: evaporator, heater|noun

condenser:/kənˈdensər/ (US), /kənˈdɛnsə(r)/ (UK)| n.|浓缩器；精简者|Something or someone that condenses something.|He acted as a condenser of information, summarizing the key points. 他充当了信息浓缩者的角色，总结了关键点。|synonyms: summarizer, abridger|antonyms: expander, elaborator|noun
