difference:/ˈdɪfrəns/ (US), /ˈdɪfrəns/ (UK)| n.| 差异，不同，区别|a point or way in which people or things are not the same|There's a big difference between knowing something and understanding it.  知道某事和理解某事之间有很大的区别。|synonyms: distinction, dissimilarity, discrepancy, variation|antonyms: similarity, likeness, resemblance|noun
