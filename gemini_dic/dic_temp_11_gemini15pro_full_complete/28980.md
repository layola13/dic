cartable: /kɑːrˈtɑːbl/ (UK), /kɑːrˈtæbl/ (US)
n.
school bag, satchel, book bag
A bag, typically made of leather or other sturdy material, used by schoolchildren to carry books and other school supplies.
例句：The child packed their cartable with books and pencils.  孩子把书和铅笔装进了书包。

例句：She slung her cartable over her shoulder and headed to school. 她把书包挎在肩上，朝学校走去。

近义词：schoolbag, satchel, backpack, book bag
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "briefcase" for an adult context)
词性: noun
