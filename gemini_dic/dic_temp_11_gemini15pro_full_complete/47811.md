decarbonizer:/diːˈkɑːrbənaɪzər/
noun
脱碳器；脱碳剂；去碳剂
A substance or device that removes carbon, especially from steel or iron; a person or thing that promotes decarbonization.
The steel company invested in a new decarbonizer to reduce its emissions. 这家钢铁公司投资了一种新的脱碳剂以减少排放。
The government is acting as a decarbonizer by promoting green energy. 政府通过推广绿色能源来充当脱碳者的角色。
synonyms: decarbonization agent, carbon remover
antonyms: carbon emitter, polluter
n.

decarbonize:/diːˈkɑːrbənaɪz/
verb
脱碳；去碳
To remove carbon from something, especially steel or iron; to reduce or eliminate carbon dioxide emissions.
The factory is working to decarbonize its operations by switching to renewable energy. 工厂正在努力通过改用可再生能源来实现其运营的脱碳。
Many countries are striving to decarbonize their economies to combat climate change. 许多国家正在努力使其经济脱碳以应对气候变化。
synonyms: decarbonise, reduce carbon emissions, eliminate carbon emissions
antonyms: increase carbon emissions, pollute
v.
