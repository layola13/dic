howl-down:/ˈhaʊl daʊn/
verb
压制；以吼叫声盖过…的声音；大声喊叫阻止…讲话
to prevent someone from speaking by shouting loudly to show disapproval
The speaker was howled down by the angry crowd.  演讲者被愤怒的人群的吼叫声所淹没。
shout down, drown out
allow to speak, listen to
v.
