grot:/ɡrɒt/ (UK) /ɡrɑːt/ (US)| n.| 洞穴，肮脏的地方|A natural or artificial cave. A dirty or unpleasant place. |例句：The teenagers had turned the basement into a grot.  青少年们把地下室变成了一个肮脏的地方。

例句：We explored the dark grot with our flashlights. 我们用手电筒探索了黑暗的洞穴。|近义词：cave, cavern, den, hovel |反义词: palace, mansion |词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French "grotte," from Italian "grotta," from Late Latin "grupta," variant of Latin "crypta," from Greek "kryptē" meaning "a vault, hidden place."  Ultimately from the Greek verb "kryptein" meaning "to hide."
