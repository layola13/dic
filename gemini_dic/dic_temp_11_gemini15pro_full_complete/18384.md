bellyflop:/ˈbeliˌflɑp/ (US) /ˈbeliˌflɒp/ (UK)| n. vi.| 肚皮入水；失败；惨败 |A fall or dive forward so that the abdomen hits a surface, typically water, flat. Also used figuratively to describe a failure or a poorly received performance. |He did a bellyflop into the pool. 他肚皮朝下跳进了泳池。 The movie was a total bellyflop. 这部电影彻底失败了。|近义词：flop, splash, bomb (informal), dud (informal)|反义词:success, triumph|词性:noun, verb

bellyflops:/ˈbeliˌflɑps/ (US) /ˈbeliˌflɒps/ (UK)| n. vi.| 肚皮入水（复数）；失败（复数）；惨败（复数） |Plural form of "bellyflop." Refers to multiple instances of falling or diving forward so that the abdomen hits a surface, or multiple failures. |He did three bellyflops in a row. 他连续三次肚皮入水。  The company has had a string of bellyflops lately. 这家公司最近经历了一连串的失败。|近义词：flops, splashes, bombs (informal), duds (informal)|反义词:successes, triumphs|词性:noun, verb
