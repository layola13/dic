equinumerant:/ˌekwɪˈnjuːmərənt/ /ˌikwɪˈnuːmərənt/
adjective
等数的; 基数相同的
Having the same number of elements.  Two sets are equinumerant if they have the same cardinality.
The two sets of marbles are equinumerant, each containing 12 marbles. 这两组弹珠数量相等，每组都有12颗。
These two finite sets are equinumerant. 这两个有限集的基数相同。
Synonym: equipotent, equal in number
Antonym: unequal in number
词性: adjective
