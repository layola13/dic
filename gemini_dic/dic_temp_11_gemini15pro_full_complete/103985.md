juggled:/ˈdʒʌɡəld/ | adj. | （数字）被篡改的 | Having been manipulated or altered, typically dishonestly. | The juggled figures made the company's profits look much higher than they actually were. 篡改后的数字使公司的利润看起来比实际高得多。 | Synonyms: falsified, manipulated, doctored | Antonyms: accurate, genuine, authentic | adjective
