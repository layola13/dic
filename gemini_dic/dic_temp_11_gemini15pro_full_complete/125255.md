multidivisional:/ˌmʌlti.dɪˈvɪʒ.ən.əl/
adjective
多分部的，多部门的
Relating to or denoting a company structure in which operations are grouped into semi-autonomous divisions based on product lines, geographical areas, or other criteria.
Many large corporations adopt a multidivisional structure to manage diverse business operations efficiently.  许多大公司采用多分部结构来有效管理不同的业务运营。
General Electric is a classic example of a multidivisional company.  通用电气是多分部公司的典型例子。
近义词:decentralized, divisionalized
反义词:centralized, unified
词性:adjective

Etymology:
"multi-" (prefix meaning "many") + "division" (from Latin "divisio," act of dividing) + "-al" (suffix forming adjectives)
