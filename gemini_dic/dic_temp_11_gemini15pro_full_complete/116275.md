massed:/mæst/| adj.|集中的，聚集的，大量的|gathered together in a large group;  collected or accumulated in large quantity|The massed choirs sang with impressive power.  聚集在一起的合唱团以令人印象深刻的力量演唱。|例句：The massed troops prepared for battle.  集结的军队准备战斗。|近义词：assembled, concentrated, clustered, grouped|反义词：scattered, dispersed, spread out|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the past participle of *mass*, from French *masse*, ultimately from Latin *massa* "lump, kneaded dough".
