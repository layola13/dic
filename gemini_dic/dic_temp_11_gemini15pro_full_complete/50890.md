desertification: /dɪˌzɜːtɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 荒漠化，沙漠化 | The process by which fertile land becomes desert, typically as a result of drought, deforestation, or inappropriate agriculture. |例句：Desertification is a serious environmental problem in many parts of the world.  荒漠化是世界许多地区的一个严重环境问题。

例句：The ongoing desertification of the region threatens the livelihoods of millions. 该地区持续的荒漠化威胁着数百万人的生计。|近义词：desertization, aridity, drought |反义词:  forestation, reclamation |词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *désertification*, from *désertifier* "to make into a desert," from desert + -fier (akin to -fy).  Ultimately from Latin *desertus* "abandoned, deserted."
