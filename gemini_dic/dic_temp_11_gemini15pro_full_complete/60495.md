eke out:/ik ˈaʊt/
verb
补充；勉强维持；延长
to make a supply of something such as food or money last longer by using very small amounts of it
We had to eke out our meager supplies. 我们不得不精打细算地用我们少得可怜的补给。

Synonyms: supplement, stretch, augment, boost
Antonyms: squander, waste, deplete
