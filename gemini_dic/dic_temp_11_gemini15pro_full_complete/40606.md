convellent:/kənˈvʌlsənt/| adj.|痉挛的，抽搐的；令人捧腹的|Producing or characterized by convulsions; causing fits of laughter.|The convellent sobs of the grieving mother echoed through the room. 悲伤的母亲一阵阵抽泣的声音在房间里回荡。|近义词：spasmodic, jerky, hilarious|反义词:steady, calm|adjective

convulse:/kənˈvʌls/| v.|使痉挛，使抽搐；使剧烈震动|To shake violently; to agitate.|The earthquake convulsed the city, destroying buildings and infrastructure. 地震使城市剧烈震动，摧毁了建筑物和基础设施。|近义词：shake, tremble, agitate|反义词:soothe, calm|verb

convulsion:/kənˈvʌlʃən/| n.|痉挛，抽搐；剧烈震动|A violent involuntary contraction of muscles; a violent disturbance.|The child's high fever brought on convulsions. 孩子的高烧引起了抽搐。|近义词：spasm, fit, upheaval|反义词:calm, stillness|noun


词源：From Latin *convulsus*, past participle of *convellere* "to pull violently," from *com-* "together" + *vellere* "to pluck, pull."
