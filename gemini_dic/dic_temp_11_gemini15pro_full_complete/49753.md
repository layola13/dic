demoisturizing:/ˌdiːmɔɪstʃəˌraɪzɪŋ/
adjective
去湿的，保湿的
removing moisture; hydrating.  This refers to the action of either removing excess moisture (as in a dehumidifier) or adding moisture to prevent dryness (as in a moisturizing cream).  It can be confusing as it seemingly encompasses two opposite actions.  Context is crucial to understanding the intended meaning.
This demoisturizing cream is perfect for dry skin.  这款保湿霜非常适合干性皮肤。
The demoisturizing effect of the air conditioner keeps the room comfortable. 空调的除湿效果使房间保持舒适。
近义词: hydrating, moisturizing (when adding moisture), drying, dehumidifying (when removing moisture)
反义词: humectant (when adding moisture) , humidifying (when removing moisture)
词性: adjective
