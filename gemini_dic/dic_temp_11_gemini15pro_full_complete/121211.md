miseducate:/ˌmɪsˈɛdʒʊkeɪt/
verb
误导;错误地教育
to teach (someone) incorrect information or give (someone) a wrong impression
The children were miseducated about the dangers of drugs. 孩子们受到了关于毒品危害的错误教育。
misinform, misguide, mislead
educate, inform, enlighten
