overlogicalities:/ˌoʊvərˌlɒdʒɪˈkælɪtiz/ | n. | 过分逻辑化；过度理性化 | The tendency to overthink and analyze situations to an excessive degree, often losing sight of practical considerations or emotional factors. |  His overlogicalities sometimes prevented him from seeing the simple solution. 他过度逻辑化的思考方式有时让他看不到简单的解决方法。 | 近义词：overthinking, overanalyzing, hairsplitting | 反义词：intuitiveness, instinct, impulsiveness | 词性: 名词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "logical" (adjective) + "-ity" (suffix forming abstract nouns)  "over-"（表示“过度”的前缀）+ "logical"（形容词）+ "-ity"（构成抽象名词的后缀）
