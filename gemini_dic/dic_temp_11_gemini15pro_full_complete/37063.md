combater:/kəmˈbætər/ (US), /ˈkɒmbætə(r)/ (UK)| n.|战士，战斗员，搏斗者|A person who fights, especially in a war or battle. |例句：The brave combaters defended their city against the invaders. 勇敢的战士们保卫他们的城市抵御侵略者。

例句：The skilled combatant moved with agility and precision.  熟练的战斗员动作敏捷而精准。|近义词：fighter, soldier, warrior, combatant|反义词：pacifist, civilian|词性: noun


combater (Portuguese):/kõ.baˈteɾ/ (Brazilian Portuguese)|v. |战斗，搏斗，抗击|To fight against someone or something. |例句：Os bombeiros combateram o incêndio por horas. 消防员与大火搏斗了几个小时。(The firefighters fought the fire for hours.)

例句：Eles combateram a corrupção com todas as suas forças. 他们竭尽全力打击腐败。(They fought corruption with all their might.)|近义词：lutar, brigar, enfrentar, resistir|反义词：ceder, render-se|词性: verb


词源：From Late Latin *combattāre, from Latin combattere "to fight together," from com- "together" + battuere "to beat."
