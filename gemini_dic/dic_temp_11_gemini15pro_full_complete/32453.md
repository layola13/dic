chimneyless:/ˈtʃɪmni.ləs/
adjective
没有烟囱的
lacking a chimney; without a chimney.
The chimneyless cabin relied on a wood stove vented through a pipe in the wall.  这间没有烟囱的小屋依靠一个通过墙上的管道排气的柴火炉。
The small, chimneyless cottage looked picturesque against the snowy backdrop.  在白雪皑皑的背景下，这座没有烟囱的小屋看起来风景如画。
Synonyms: ventless, stackless
Antonyms: chimneyed, with a chimney
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From chimney + -less.  "Chimney" comes from Old French "cheminée" meaning fireplace, ultimately from Latin "caminus" meaning furnace, forge. "-less" is a suffix meaning without.
