domineeringnesses:/ˌdɒmɪˈnɪərɪŋnɪsɪz/
n.
专横跋扈；盛气凌人
The state or quality of being domineering; overbearing behavior.
Her domineeringnesses made it difficult for anyone to offer suggestions. 她专横跋扈的态度让任何人都难以提出建议。
Synonyms: arrogance,authoritarianism, bossiness, high-handedness, imperiousness, overbearingness
Antonyms: humility, modesty, submissiveness
词性: 名词
