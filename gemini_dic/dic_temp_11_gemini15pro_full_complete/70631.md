filter:/ˈfɪltər/ (US), /ˈfɪltə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.| 过滤器，过滤，筛选 | A porous device for removing impurities or solid particles from a liquid or gas.  To remove impurities or solid particles from a liquid or gas using a filter. |例句：The coffee filter keeps the grounds out of the brewed coffee. 咖啡滤纸可以防止咖啡渣进入煮好的咖啡中。|近义词：sieve, strain, screen|反义词:pollute, contaminate|词性:名词, 动词

filter:/ˈfɪltər/ (US), /ˈfɪltə(r)/ (UK)| v.| 过滤，筛选 | To pass (something) through a filter to remove impurities or unwanted elements. |例句：She filtered the gravy to remove the lumps. 她过滤了肉汁去除了块状物。|近义词：purify, refine, sift|反义词: mix, combine|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Old French *filtre*, from Medieval Latin *filtrum*, probably from a West Germanic source akin to Old High German *filt*, "felt"
