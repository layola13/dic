accurately: /ˈækjərətli/ | adv. | 准确地，精确地 |  In a correct and exact way, without any mistakes. | He accurately predicted the outcome of the election. 他准确地预测了选举结果。| precisely, correctly, exactly | incorrectly, imprecisely | adverb

词源: From Late Latin *accuratus, past participle of accurare "to take care of," from Latin ad- "to" + cura "care."
