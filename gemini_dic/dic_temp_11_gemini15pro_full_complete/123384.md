monomorphic:/ˌmɒnəˈmɔːfɪk/ /ˌmɑːnəˈmɔːrfɪk/| adj. |单形的，单态的|Having a single form.  Existing in only one form, especially in only one crystalline form.|The population is monomorphic for this trait.  这个种群的这个性状是单态的。|近义词：uniform, homogenous|反义词: polymorphic, heteromorphic|词性:adjective
monomorphism:/ˌmɒnəˈmɔːfɪzəm/ /ˌmɑːnəˈmɔːrfɪzəm/| n. |单形性，单态性|The condition of being monomorphic. The existence of only one form, especially in only one crystalline form.|Monomorphism in this species is unusual.  这个物种的单形性是不寻常的。|近义词：uniformity, homogeneity|反义词: polymorphism, heteromorphism|词性:noun
