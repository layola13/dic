airfreight:/ˈɛərfreɪt/| n. |空运货物；空运费|The airfreight cost more than the goods themselves. 空运费比货物本身还贵。|例句：The airfreight arrived on time. 空运货物准时到达。

例句：The company specializes in international airfreight.  这家公司专门从事国际空运。


近义词：air cargo, air shipment, air transport|反义词: seafreight, land freight|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From air + freight.  "Air" referring to transport by aircraft, and "freight" meaning goods transported commercially.
