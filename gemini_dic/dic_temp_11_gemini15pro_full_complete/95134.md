inapplicably:/ˌɪnəˈplɪkəbli/
| adv.|不适用地，不切题地|Not able to be applied; irrelevant.|The law is inapplicably vague in this case.  在这个案例中，法律模糊不清，无法适用。|This theory is inapplicably complex for practical use. 这个理论过于复杂，不适用于实际应用。|近义词：irrelevantly, inappropriately, unsuitably|反义词: applicably, relevantly, appropriately|词性:副词
Etymology->
From in- (not) + applicable (capable of being applied) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  The root "apply" comes from Latin "applicare," meaning "to fold onto, attach."
