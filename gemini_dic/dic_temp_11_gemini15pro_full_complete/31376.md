charcoaled:/ˈtʃɑːrkoʊld/
adjective
烧成炭的，炭黑色的
burned or scorched so as to resemble charcoal
The steak was charcoaled on the outside but still rare inside. 牛排外面烧焦了，但里面仍然是生的。

近义词:burned, scorched, blackened
反义词:raw, uncooked
词性:adjective
