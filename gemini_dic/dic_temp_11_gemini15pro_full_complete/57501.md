draggy:/ˈdræɡi/| adj.|拖沓的，缓慢的，无聊的|Something that is draggy is slow, tedious, and boring.|The meeting was draggy and unproductive. 会议拖沓且没有成果。|The play's second act was a bit draggy. 这部戏的第二幕有点拖沓。|近义词：slow, tedious, boring, dull, monotonous|反义词：fast, exciting, interesting, lively, stimulating|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From drag + -y.  Meaning "slow and tedious" is from 1851.
