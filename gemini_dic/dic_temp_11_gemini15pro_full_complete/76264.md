fuzziest:/ˈfʌziɪst/| adj.|最模糊的，最失真的|The picture was so blurry, it was the fuzziest I'd ever seen.|The fuzziest memories are often the most precious. |近义词：blurriest, haziest, vaguest, indistinctest|反义词：clearest, sharpest, distinctest|词性:形容词
词源: From fuzzy + -est (superlative suffix). Fuzzy itself comes from fuzz, which likely originated as an imitative word.
