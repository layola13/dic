If he were smarter, he would elude capture.  如果他更聪明些，他就能逃脱追捕。
I wish I could elude this feeling of sadness.  我希望我能摆脱这种悲伤的感觉。


Passive Voice:
The true meaning of the poem is often eluded. 这首诗的真正含义常常难以捉摸。
The suspect was eluded by the police.  嫌疑人被警察逃脱了。（注意：elude通常不用于被动语态，这个例句略有不自然，更常见的表达是 "The police failed to catch the suspect."）

Negative Sentence:
