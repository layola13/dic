euphorias:/juːˈfɔːriəz/
n.
欣快症；狂喜；幸福感
A feeling or state of intense excitement and happiness.
She was in a state of euphoria after winning the lottery. 她中了彩票后欣喜若狂。
The euphoria of victory was short-lived. 胜利的喜悦是短暂的。
近义词:elation, joy, bliss, rapture, ecstasy
反义词:despair, misery, depression
词性:名词(plural)

euphoria:/juːˈfɔːriə/
n.
欣快症；狂喜；幸福感
A feeling or state of intense excitement and happiness.
He experienced a sense of euphoria after completing the marathon. 完成马拉松后，他感到欣喜若狂。
The drug induced a state of euphoria. 这种药物引起了一种欣快感。
近义词:elation, joy, bliss, rapture, ecstasy
反义词:despair, misery, depression
词性:名词(singular)

词源: From Greek *euphoria* "power of enduring easily," from *euphoros* "bearing well, healthy," from *eu* "well" + *pherein* "to bear."
