legalizations:/ˌliːɡəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/
noun
合法化；合法化的事物
The process of making something legal, or the act of giving legal status to something.
The legalization of marijuana is a controversial issue.  大麻合法化是一个有争议的问题。
The legalizations of gambling have led to an increase in tax revenue. 赌博合法化导致税收增加。
synonyms: legitimizations, authorizations, sanctions
antonyms: criminalizations, prohibitions, bans
