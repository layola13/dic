myelencephala:/ˌmaɪəlɛnˈsɛfələ/ | n. | 髓脑 |The myelencephalon is the posterior part of the rhombencephalon, developing into the medulla oblongata. 髓脑是菱脑的后部，发育成长髓。| The myelencephalon controls vital functions like breathing and heart rate. 髓脑控制呼吸和心率等重要功能。 | 近义词: medulla oblongata | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider higher brain regions like cerebrum) | 词性: noun

词源:
myel- (Greek: marrow, spinal cord) + encephalon (Greek: brain)
