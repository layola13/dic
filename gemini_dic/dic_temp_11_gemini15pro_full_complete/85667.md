hauynite:/ˈhɔɪnaɪt/ | n. | 蓝方石 | A tectosilicate mineral of the sodalite group, with endmember formula (Na,Ca)₄-₈Al₆Si₆(O,S)₂₄(SO₄,Cl)₁₋₂.  It is a feldspathoid and a member of the sodalite group.  Typically occurs as bright blue to bluish green, glassy crystals or granular masses in igneous rocks. | Hauynite is a relatively rare mineral found in certain volcanic rocks. 蓝方石是一种相对稀有的矿物，发现于某些火山岩中。 | Hauynite crystals can be a striking deep blue color. 蓝方石晶体可以呈现出醒目的深蓝色。 | Synonyms: haüyne | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
