orients:/ˈɔːriənts/ /ˈɔːrɪˌɛnts/ | v. | 使适应；确定方向；使朝向 |  To adjust to a new situation or environment; to find one's bearings; to direct towards something. | She orients herself by using the compass. 她用指南针确定方向。| synonyms: adapt, adjust, familiarize, acclimate, direct, position, align | antonyms: disorient, confuse, lose | verb

orients:/ˈɔːriənts/ /ˈɔːrɪˌɛnts/ | n. | 东方国家；东方 | Countries of the East, especially East Asia. |  Many ancient cultures flourished in the Orients. 许多古老的文化在东方繁荣昌盛。| synonyms: East, Asia, Far East | antonyms: West, Occident | noun

注意事项: 
Orients 作为名词通常用复数形式，指东方世界。
