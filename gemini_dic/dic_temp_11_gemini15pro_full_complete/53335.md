disacryl:/daɪˈsækrɪl/
noun
二丙烯酸酯，双丙烯酸酯
A type of acrylic monomer containing two acrylate groups, often used in the production of polymers and resins.  It is formed from two acrylic acid molecules.
Disacryl resins are commonly used in dental materials.  二丙烯酸酯树脂常用于牙科材料。
Disacryl is an important component in some adhesives. 双丙烯酸酯是某些粘合剂的重要成分。
