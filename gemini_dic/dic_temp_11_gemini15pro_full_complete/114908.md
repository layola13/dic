menace:/ˈmɛnɪs/ | n.  | 威胁，危险的人（或物） | Something that presents a danger or threat. | The escalating violence poses a serious menace to regional stability.  不断升级的暴力对地区稳定构成严重威胁。| synonyms: threat, danger, peril, hazard | antonyms: safety, security, protection | noun

menace:/ˈmɛnɪs/ | vt. | 威胁，恐吓 | To threaten, especially in a malignant or hostile manner. |  Rising floodwaters menaced the entire town.  上涨的洪水威胁着整个城镇。| synonyms: threaten, endanger, jeopardize, imperil | antonyms: protect, safeguard, defend | verb
