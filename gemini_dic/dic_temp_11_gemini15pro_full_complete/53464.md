disarmament:/dɪsˈɑːməmənt/
n.|裁军，解除武装|The process of reducing, limiting, or abolishing military forces and weapons.|例句：Nuclear disarmament is a crucial issue for global security. 核裁军是全球安全的一个关键问题。|近义词：demilitarization, arms reduction|反义词：armament, militarization|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (prefix meaning "reversal" or "removal") + armament (meaning "equipment with weapons").  Ultimately from Latin arma (weapons).
