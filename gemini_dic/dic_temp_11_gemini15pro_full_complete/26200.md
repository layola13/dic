butterball:/ˈbʌtərˌbɔːl/| n.|黄油球，胖墩，丰满的人（尤指小孩或女人），（尤指感恩节食用的）嫩肥火鸡|A plump, round person or animal, especially a young child or a turkey fattened for Thanksgiving. |例句：The baby was a real butterball, with chubby cheeks and rolls of fat.  这个婴儿真是个小胖墩，脸蛋胖乎乎的，一身的肥肉。

例句：We bought a big butterball turkey for Thanksgiving dinner. 我们买了一只肥嫩的大火鸡过感恩节。|近义词：chubby, plump, rotund, dumpy, turkey|反义词:thin, skinny, lean|词性:名词
