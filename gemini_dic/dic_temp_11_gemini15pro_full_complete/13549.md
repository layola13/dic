attempted:/əˈtɛmptɪd/| adj. |已尝试的，未遂的|Having been tried; attempted.  An action that someone has tried to do, but without success.|例句：The attempted robbery was foiled by the police. 警方挫败了这起抢劫未遂案。|近义词：tried, endeavored|反义词:successful, achieved|词性:形容词
attempted:/əˈtɛmptɪd/| v. |尝试|Past tense of "attempt," meaning to try to do something, especially something difficult.|例句：He attempted to climb Mount Everest. 他尝试攀登珠穆朗玛峰。|近义词：tried, endeavored|反义词:forbore, neglected|词性:动词


词源：From the Late Latin *attaminare*, meaning "to touch, lay hold of, attack," from Latin *ad* ("to") + *taminare* ("to touch"). This evolved into the Old French *attempter*, and eventually the English "attempt."
