exploratory:/ɪkˈsplɔːrətɔːri/
adjective
探索性的，考察的
Serving to explore or investigate.
例句：
The team embarked on an exploratory mission to Mars.
该团队开始了火星探索任务。

They conducted exploratory drilling in search of oil.
他们进行了石油勘探钻探。

近义词：investigative, probing, trial, experimental
反义词：conclusive, definitive

词性: adjective
