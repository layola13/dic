mitty: /ˈmɪti/ | n. | 空想家，白日梦者 | A Mitty is a person who spends more time daydreaming about unrealistic achievements than taking action to achieve them.  Derived from the character Walter Mitty in James Thurber's short story "The Secret Life of Walter Mitty". | He's a bit of a Mitty, always fantasizing about being a rock star. 他有点像个空想家，总是幻想成为摇滚明星。 |  dreamer, fantasist, visionary | realist, pragmatist | noun
