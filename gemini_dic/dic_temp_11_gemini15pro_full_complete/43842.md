crepon:/ˈkreɪpɒn/ (UK), /ˈkreɪpɑːn/ (US)| n.|绉布，泡泡纱|A fabric, typically of silk, wool, or cotton, with a crinkled or puckered surface, often heavier than crepe.|The curtains were made of a heavy crepon. 窗帘是用厚厚的绉布做的。|She chose a crepon blouse for its textured appearance. 她选择了一件绉布衬衫，因为它的质感外观。|synonyms: crepe, crinkle cloth|antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps smooth fabric)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
