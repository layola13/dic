noisiest:/ˈnɔɪziɪst/ | adj. | 最吵闹的 | Superlative form of noisy: most noisy.  It is used to compare three or more things/people. | The noisiest classroom was always Mrs. Smith's. 史密斯太太的教室总是最吵闹的。| The noisiest kid in the group was always getting into trouble.  小组里最吵闹的孩子总是惹麻烦。 |  loudest, rowdiest | quietest, calmest | adjective
