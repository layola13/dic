non-custodial:/ˌnɒn kʌˈstəʊdiəl/ (US) /ˌnɑːn kʌˈstoʊdiəl/| adj.|非监护权的，非保管的|Not having custody or guardianship, especially of a child.  Relating to financial assets that are held by a third party, not the owner.|例句：The father has non-custodial rights to his children. 父亲对他的孩子拥有非监护权。|近义词：independent, decentralized|反义词: custodial, guardianship|词性: adjective

non-custodial:/ˌnɒn kʌˈstəʊdiəl/ (US) /ˌnɑːn kʌˈstoʊdiəl/| n.|非监护权|The state of not having custody or guardianship, especially of a child.|例句：The mother was awarded full custody, leaving the father with non-custodial status. 母亲获得了全部监护权，父亲则处于非监护权状态。|近义词：non-possession|反义词: custody, guardianship|词性: noun
