overridden:/ˌoʊvərˈrɪdn/| adj. |被覆盖的，被重写的|Overridden means that a method in a subclass has been modified to provide a different implementation from the method in its superclass.  It's a key concept in object-oriented programming related to inheritance and polymorphism.|The method `toString()` is often overridden in Java classes.  `toString()` 方法在 Java 类中经常被重写。|Synonyms: superseded, replaced, overruled|Antonyms: upheld, preserved, original|Part of Speech: Adjective
