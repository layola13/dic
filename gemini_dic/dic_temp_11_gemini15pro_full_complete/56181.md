dodecaphonists:/ˌdoʊdəˈkɑːfənɪsts/
noun
十二音作曲家；十二音体系的拥护者
Composers or advocates of twelve-tone music, a compositional technique based on a series of all twelve notes of the chromatic scale.
Schoenberg was one of the most influential dodecaphonists.  勋伯格是最有影响力的十二音作曲家之一。
The dodecaphonists sought to break free from traditional tonality. 十二音作曲家试图摆脱传统的调性。
Synonyms: serialists, twelve-tone composers
Antonyms: tonal composers
