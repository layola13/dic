noninstructionally:/ˌnɒnɪnˈstrʌkʃənəli/ | adv. | 非教学地；非指导性地 |  In a manner that does not involve instruction or teaching.  It describes activities or contexts where formal teaching or guidance is absent. | The children were playing noninstructionally in the park. 孩子们在公园里自由玩耍，没有进行任何指导性的活动。 |  The museum offers noninstructional tours for visitors who prefer to explore at their own pace.  博物馆为喜欢自行探索的游客提供非指导性游览。| informally, freely, independently | didactically, pedagogically | adverb
