nonflexibilities:/ˌnɒnˌfleksɪˈbɪlɪtiz/ | n. | 不灵活性，僵化性 | Lack of flexibility; rigidities. The state or quality of being unable or unwilling to change or adapt. |  The company's nonflexibilities in adapting to the changing market led to its downfall.  这家公司对不断变化的市场缺乏适应性，导致了它的倒闭。 | inflexibilities, rigidities | flexibilities, adaptability | noun
