de-stratify: /diːˈstrætɪfaɪ/
verb
消除分层，使均匀
To eliminate or reduce the stratification of something; to make something uniform throughout.
The engineers designed a system to de-stratify the lake, preventing temperature and oxygen differences between layers. 工程师们设计了一个系统来消除湖泊的分层，防止层与层之间的温度和氧气差异。
The mixing process helps to de-stratify the air in the room.  混合过程有助于使房间内的空气均匀分布。
Synonyms: homogenize, mix, blend, integrate
Antonyms: stratify, layer, separate, segregate
词性: 动词

de-stratification: /diːˌstrætɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
noun
消除分层，均匀化
The process of eliminating or reducing stratification.
The de-stratification of the lake improved water quality.  湖泊分层的消除改善了水质。
De-stratification is essential for maintaining a healthy ecosystem in this type of lake. 在这种类型的湖泊中，消除分层对于维持健康的生态系统至关重要。
Synonyms: homogenization, mixing, blending, integration
Antonyms: stratification, layering, separation, segregation
词性: 名词
