carnallite:/kɑːrˈnəlaɪt/ | n. | 光卤石 | A mineral consisting of hydrated potassium magnesium chloride, used as a source of potassium. | Carnallite is an important source of potassium for fertilizers. 光卤石是肥料中钾的重要来源。| Carnallite deposits are often found alongside other evaporite minerals. 光卤石矿床通常与其他蒸发岩矿物一起发现。 | synonyms:  sylvite, kainite | antonyms: N/A | noun
