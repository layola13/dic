overgenerously: /ˌoʊvərˈdʒenərəsli/ | adv. | 过于慷慨地，过分大方地 |  in a way that is too generous; excessively generously | He tipped the waiter overgenerously. 他给服务员的小费太多了。| She donated overgenerously to the charity. 她向慈善机构捐款过分慷慨。 | 近义词: excessively, too generously, lavishly | 反义词: stingily, meanly, parsimoniously | 词性: 副词

词源:
From "over" (meaning excessively) + "generous" (from Latin "generosus" meaning of noble birth, magnanimous) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).
