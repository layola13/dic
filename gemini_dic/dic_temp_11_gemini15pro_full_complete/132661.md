nondisintegrating:/ˌnɒndɪˈsɪntɪˌɡreɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非崩解的，不分解的
Not breaking apart or disintegrating; remaining whole or intact.
The tablet is formulated to be nondisintegrating in the stomach. 这种药片被配制成在胃中不会崩解。
Near synonyms:  stable, durable, firm, solid, resistant, imperishable
Antonyms: disintegrating, crumbling, dissolving, decomposing
词性: adjective
