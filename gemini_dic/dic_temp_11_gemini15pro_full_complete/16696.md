barricader:/ˌbærɪˈkeɪdər/| n.|设路障者，用路障围堵的人|A person who builds or mans a barricade.|The barricaders held off the police for several hours. 路障设置者阻挡了警察几个小时。|Synonyms: defender, protector, resistor|Antonyms: attacker, aggressor|noun

barricade:/ˌbærɪˈkeɪd/| n. vt.|路障，障碍物；设路障，阻挡|A barrier hastily constructed, as in a street, to stop an enemy's advance; to obstruct or shut in with a barricade.|Protesters barricaded the street with overturned cars. 抗议者用掀翻的汽车设置路障封锁了街道.  They barricaded themselves in the building. 他们把自己关在大楼里，用障碍物挡住。|Synonyms: barrier, obstacle, blockade|Antonyms: opening, passage|noun, verb
