advancing:/ədˈvænsɪŋ/
verb (present participle) |前进的，发展的|moving forward, progressing|The advancing army captured the city. 前进的军队占领了这座城市。|progressing, developing, moving forward|retreating, receding
adjective |前进的，发展的|moving forward, progressing|The advancing technology is changing our lives. 先进的技术正在改变我们的生活。|progressive, developing, evolving|backward, declining, regressing


Etymology:
From Old French *avancer, from a- (from Latin ad-) + -vancer (from Latin abante "from before").
