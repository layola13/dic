obeyed:/əˈbeɪd/
verb
服从，遵守
complied with or followed commands, instructions, or rules.
The dog obeyed its owner's command to sit. 狗服从了主人的命令坐下了。
complied with, followed, adhered to, observed, kept
disobeyed, defied, ignored, disregarded
v.
