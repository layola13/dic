nonenlightened:/ˌnɒnɪnˈlaɪtnd/
adjective
未受启迪的，无知的
Not enlightened; ignorant or unaware of spiritual or intellectual understanding.
The nonenlightened masses were easily swayed by the charismatic leader's rhetoric. 未受启迪的民众很容易被这位有魅力的领导人的华丽辞藻所左右。
The nonenlightened view that women are less capable than men is still prevalent in some societies.  认为女性不如男性的无知观点在某些社会仍然盛行。
Synonyms: unenlightened, ignorant, unaware, benighted, unschooled
Antonyms: enlightened, aware, knowledgeable, informed, wise
词性: 形容词
