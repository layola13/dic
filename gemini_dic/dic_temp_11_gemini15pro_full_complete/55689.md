ditziness:/ˈdɪtzinəs/
noun
轻率；傻气；糊涂
A lack of seriousness and good judgment; silliness.
She giggled, a combination of nervousness and her usual ditziness. 她咯咯地笑着，既紧张又带着她惯有的傻气。
近义词：silliness, foolishness, flightiness, scatterbrainedness, airheadedness
反义词：seriousness, sensibleness, practicality, levelheadedness
词性:n.


ditzy:/ˈdɪtsi/
adjective
轻率的；傻气的；糊涂的
Silly and frivolous.
She's a bit ditzy and forgets things easily. 她有点傻乎乎的，很容易忘事。
近义词：silly, foolish, flighty, scatterbrained, airheaded
反义词：serious, sensible, practical, levelheaded
词性:adj.

词源:
From *ditz*, perhaps an alteration of *dits*, representing an earlier pronunciation of *digits*, rhyming slang for *wits*.
