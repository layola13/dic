aluminic: /ˌæljʊˈmɪnɪk/
adjective
铝的；含铝的
Relating to or containing aluminum, especially in the trivalent state.
Aluminum sulfate is an aluminic compound.  硫酸铝是一种铝化合物。
The soil is highly aluminic.  这种土壤含铝量很高。
Synonyms: aluminum, alumina, aluminous
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a chemical property.  Could consider antonyms for the metal itself, like non-metallic, or for properties conferred by aluminum, like ferrous (if contrasting with aluminum's lightness). )
词性: 形容词
