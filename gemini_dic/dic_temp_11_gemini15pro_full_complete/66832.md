expatiating:/ɪkˈspeɪʃieɪtɪŋ/
verb
详述，细说
speaking or writing about something at length or in detail
The professor expatiated on the history of the Roman Empire. 教授详细阐述了罗马帝国的历史。
expounding, elaborating, enlarging
concise, brief
v.
