feasible:/ˈfiːzɪbl/ /ˈfizəbl/| adj.|可行的，行得通的|Possible and practical to do easily or conveniently.|例句：It's not feasible to build a bridge here. 在这里建桥是不可行的。|例句：The project seems feasible with the current budget.  以目前的预算来看，这个项目似乎是可行的。|近义词：possible, practicable, viable, achievable|反义词：impossible, impracticable, unworkable|词性:形容词

词源:
From Old French faisable "that can be done," from faire "to do," from Latin facere "to make, do."
