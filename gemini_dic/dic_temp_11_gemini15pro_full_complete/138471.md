novelize: /ˈnɒvəlaɪz/ (UK) /ˈnɑːvəlaɪz/ (US)
verb
小说化；将…改编成小说
To adapt or convert (a story, play, film, etc.) into novel form.
The movie was novelized shortly after its release.  这部电影上映后不久就被改编成小说。
His screenplay was later novelized and became a bestseller. 他的剧本后来被改编成小说，成了畅销书。
近义词: adapt, fictionalize
反义词:  (没有明确的反义词)
词性: verb

novelization: /ˌnɒvəlaɪˈzeɪʃn/ (UK) /ˌnɑːvəlaɪˈzeɪʃn/ (US)
noun
小说化；改编小说
The act or process of novelizing something, or a novel that has been adapted from another form.
The novelization of the film was published last month.  这部电影的小说版上个月出版了。
He wrote the novelization of the popular TV series. 他创作了这部热门电视剧的小说版。
近义词: adaptation, version
反义词:  (没有明确的反义词)
词性: noun
