live-action:/ˈlaɪv ˈækʃ(ə)n/| adj.|真人版，实景拍摄的|A film or television programme that uses real actors and sets rather than animation or computer graphics.|例句：The new Disney movie is a live-action remake of the classic cartoon.  这部新的迪士尼电影是对经典动画的真人版翻拍。

例句：She prefers live-action films to animated ones. 她更喜欢真人电影而不是动画电影。|近义词：real-life, non-animated|反义词:animated, cartoon|词性:形容词
