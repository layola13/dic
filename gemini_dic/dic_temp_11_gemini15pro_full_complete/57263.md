downpayment:/ˈdaʊnˌpeɪmənt/| n. |首付款，定金|An initial payment made when something is bought on credit. The rest of the amount owed is paid in installments. |例句：We made a down payment of $10,000 on the house. 我们支付了1万美元的房屋首付款。

例句：A larger down payment will reduce your monthly mortgage payments.  更高的首付会减少你的每月按揭付款。|近义词：deposit, initial payment|反义词: balance due|词性:名词
