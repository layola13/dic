hyperconfidence:/ˌhaɪpərˈkɒnfɪdəns/ (US) /ˌhaɪpərˈkɒnfɪd(ə)ns/ (UK)| n.|过度自信；过分自信|Excessive confidence; an unrealistic belief in one's own abilities.|He displayed hyperconfidence in his ability to win the election, despite trailing in the polls. 他对赢得选举表现出过分的自信，尽管在民意调查中落后。|Synonyms: overconfidence, arrogance, hubris|Antonyms: self-doubt, insecurity|Noun
