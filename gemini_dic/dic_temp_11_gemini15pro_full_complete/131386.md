noncitable:/nɒnˈsaɪtəbl/ | adj. | 不可引用的，不能被引证的 | Not suitable or allowed to be cited as a source or authority.  | This preprint version of the article is noncitable. 这篇论文的预印本版本是不可引用的。 | The preliminary report is noncitable until it has been peer-reviewed.  这份初步报告在同行评审之前是不可引用的。 |  near synonyms: unquotable, unusable as evidence | citable, quotable | adjective
