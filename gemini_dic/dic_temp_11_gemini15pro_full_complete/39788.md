consonantly:/kənˈsɒnəntli/ /kənˈsɑːnəntli/| adv.|协调地，一致地，和谐地|In a consonant manner; harmoniously; agreeably.|The siblings, despite their differences, worked consonantly on the project.|近义词：harmoniously, consistently, accordantly, agreeably|反义词：discordantly, inconsistently, disharmoniously|词性:副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *consonantem (nominative consonans) "agreeing, harmonious," present participle of consonare "to sound together, harmonize," from assimilated form of com "with, together" (see con-) + sonare "to sound" (from PIE root *swen- "to sound").  The English adverb is recorded from the mid-15th century.
