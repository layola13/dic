anti-puritan:/ˌæntiˈpjʊərɪtən/
adjective
反清教徒的；反对清教主义的
opposed to or rejecting the beliefs and practices of Puritans, especially their strict moral code.
The anti-puritan movement gained traction during the Restoration period.  反清教徒运动在复辟时期获得了支持。
The play satirizes the anti-puritan sentiment of the time. 这部戏剧讽刺了当时的 反清教徒情绪。
近义词:libertine, freethinking
反义词:puritanical, pious
词性:adjective
