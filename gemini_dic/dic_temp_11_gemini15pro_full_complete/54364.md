dishorn:/diːˈhɔːrn/ /diːˈsɔːrn/
verb
去除角; 锯掉…的角
To deprive of horns; dehorn.
The farmer dishorned the cattle to prevent them from injuring each other.  农民锯掉了牛的角，以防止它们互相伤害。
The rancher dishorned the young calves. 牧场主去掉了小牛的角。

近义词: dehorn
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑"horn" 或 "antler")
词性: verb
