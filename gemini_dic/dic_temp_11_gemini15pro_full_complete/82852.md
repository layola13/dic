gull-like:/ˈɡʌlˌlaɪk/
adjective
像海鸥的
Resembling a gull, particularly in behavior or appearance.
The bird had a gull-like cry and a distinctive white patch on its wings.  这只鸟的叫声像海鸥，翅膀上有一块独特的白色斑块。
The robot had a gull-like wingspan, allowing it to soar effortlessly.  这个机器人有像海鸥一样的翼展，可以毫不费力地翱翔。
近义词:gullish
反义词:(No specific antonym)
词性:adjective
