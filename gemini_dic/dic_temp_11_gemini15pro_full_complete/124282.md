mothballed:/ˈmɔːθbɔːld/ | adj. | 封存的，搁置的 |  Something that is mothballed has been temporarily put out of use, but may be used again in the future.  It originally referred to storing something with mothballs to protect it from moths. | The government has mothballed a number of power plants. 政府已经封存了一些发电厂。 | The old ship was mothballed after years of service. 这艘老船在服役多年后被封存了。 |  decommissioned, inactive, stored, shelved | active, operational, in use | adjective
