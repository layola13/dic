jelling:/ˈdʒɛlɪŋ/| v. n.|凝胶化，胶凝，(想法、计划等)成形，变得清晰|The mixture is jelling nicely. 混合物正在很好地凝胶化。|近义词：congealing, solidifying, setting, taking shape, crystallizing|反义词:liquefying, melting, dissolving|词性:动词, 名词
Etymology: From "jell," a shortened form of "jelly," from Old French "gelee," ultimately from Latin "gelare" (to freeze).  The "-ing" suffix indicates the present participle form of the verb or the act of becoming a jelly-like substance.
