alighting:/əˈlaɪtɪŋ/
n.|下落，降落，下车|The alighting of the passengers from the train was orderly.  旅客们从火车上有序地下车。|
v.|下落，降落，下车|A butterfly alighted on a flower. 一只蝴蝶落在花上。|
近义词：landing, dismounting, disembarking|
反义词：taking off, ascending, embarking|
词性:  n. or present participle of alight (v.)


alight:/əˈlaɪt/
v.|点燃，着火；下车，落地|The bonfire was soon alight.  篝火很快就点燃了。|
The passengers alighted from the bus. 乘客们从公共汽车上下车。|
近义词：descend, land, disembark|
反义词：ascend, embark, board|
词性: v.


词源: From Middle English *alighten, from Old English *ālīhtan, from ā- + līhtan (“to alight, descend, dismount”), from Proto-Germanic *līhtaną (“to alight, descend”), from Proto-Indo-European *leyǵʰ- (“light, easy”).
