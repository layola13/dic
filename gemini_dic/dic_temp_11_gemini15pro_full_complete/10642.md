appropriateness:/əˈproʊpriɪtnəs/| n.|适当性，合适性，妥当性|The appropriateness of his behavior was questioned. 他的行为是否恰当受到了质疑。|例句：The appropriateness of her dress for the occasion was undeniable.  她的穿着对于这个场合来说是无可否认的得体。|例句：He questioned the appropriateness of the joke in such a formal setting. 他质疑在这个正式场合讲这个笑话是否合适。|近义词：suitability, fitness, propriety|反义词:inappropriateness, unsuitability, impropriety|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From appropriate (adj.) + -ness.  "Appropriate" comes from the Latin appropriatus, past participle of appropriare "to make one's own," from ad- "to" + proprius "one's own, special."
