interchoke:/ˌɪntərˈtʃoʊk/
verb
互相扼杀，互相抑制
to choke or obstruct each other; to become mutually obstructed
The weeds and flowers in the garden interchoked each other, competing for resources. 花园里的杂草和花卉互相扼杀，争夺资源。
The two vines interchoked as they grew, twisting around each other tightly.  两根藤蔓在生长过程中互相缠绕，紧紧地缠绕在一起。
Synonyms: strangle, stifle, suppress, obstruct
Antonyms: promote, encourage, nurture, facilitate
词性: 动词
