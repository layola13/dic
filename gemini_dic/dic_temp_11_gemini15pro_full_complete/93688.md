imitable:/ˈɪmɪtəbl/ | adj. | 可模仿的，值得模仿的 | Capable of being imitated; worthy of imitation. | Her style is imitable, but her voice is unique. 她的风格可以模仿，但她的声音是独一无二的。 |  His elegant prose style is imitable, but rarely imitated successfully. 他优雅的散文风格值得模仿，但很少有人能成功地模仿。 | 近义词:copyable, emulatable, reproducible | 反义词: inimitable, unique, original | 词性:形容词

imitate:/ˈɪmɪteɪt/ | v. | 模仿，仿效 | To copy the actions, appearance, mannerisms, etc., of someone or something. | Children often imitate the behavior of their parents. 孩子们经常模仿父母的行为。 |  He tried to imitate the style of the famous painter. 他试图模仿这位著名画家的风格。 | 近义词: copy, mimic, emulate | 反义词: originate, innovate | 词性:动词

词源: Late Middle English: from Latin imitabilis, from imitari ‘copy’.
