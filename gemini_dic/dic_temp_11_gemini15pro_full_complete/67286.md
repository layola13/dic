exscutellate: /ˌɛkskuːˈtɛleɪt/
| adj. |无小盾片的，没有盾状鳞片的|Lacking scutella or small shield-like scales.|The exscutellate leaf surface felt smooth to the touch.|近义词: glabrous, smooth|反义词: scaly, scutellate|词性:形容词
词源: ex- (Latin, out of, from) + scutella (Latin, small shield, diminutive of scutum, shield) + -ate (suffix forming adjectives)
