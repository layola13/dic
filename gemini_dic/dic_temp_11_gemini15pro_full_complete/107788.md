lapper:/ˈlæpər/| n.|拍打者；舔食者；（尤指赛车）重叠圈；波浪|Someone or something that laps (licks up liquid or washes against something repeatedly).  Also refers to a situation in racing where one competitor overtakes another who is a lap behind.|The dog was a noisy lapper, enjoying his water bowl. 这只狗是个吵闹的舔水者，很享受它的水碗。|The race car driver achieved a lapper on the final turn.  赛车手在最后一个弯道上套了对手一圈。|Synonyms: licker, overlapper (in racing context)|Antonyms: N/A|noun

lapper:/ˈlæpər/| n.|（尤指赛车）被套圈者|A racing competitor who is overtaken by another who is a lap ahead.|Two drivers became lappers in the demanding race. 在这场高要求的比赛中，有两名车手被套圈了。|Synonyms:  straggler, backmarker|Antonyms: leader, frontrunner|noun
