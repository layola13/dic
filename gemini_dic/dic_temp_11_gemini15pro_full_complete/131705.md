nonconducive:/ˌnɒnkənˈdjuːsɪv/ | adj. | 不利的，不适宜的 | Not favorable or helpful; tending to hinder or obstruct. |  The noisy environment was nonconducive to studying. 嘈杂的环境不利于学习。

The current political climate is nonconducive to foreign investment. 当前的政治气候不利于外国投资。

| 近义词：unfavorable, detrimental, adverse, unhelpful, hindering, obstructive | 反义词：conducive, favorable, helpful, beneficial, advantageous | 词性：adjective
