discontinuations: /ˌdɪskənˌtɪnjuˈeɪʃənz/
|n.| 中断，停止，终止 |The discontinuations of certain medications can cause withdrawal symptoms.  某些药物的停用会导致戒断症状。|
| |The frequent discontinuations of the power supply are causing problems for local businesses.  频繁的停电给当地企业带来了问题。|
|Synonym:| cessation, interruption, termination, suspension |
|Antonym:| continuation, resumption, commencement |
|词性:| noun (plural) |
|词源:| From Late Latin discontinuationem,  from discontinuare "to interrupt," from dis- "apart" + continuare "to continue."|
