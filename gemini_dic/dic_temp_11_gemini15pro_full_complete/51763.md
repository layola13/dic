devotees:/ˌdɛvəˈtiːz/ /ˌdiːvəʊˈtiːz/| n.|信徒，爱好者，热爱者|A devotee is a person who is very interested in and enthusiastic about someone or something.|The guru had many devotees who hung on his every word.  这位大师有很多信徒，他们对他说的每一个字都奉若圣旨。|Devotees of the sport spend hours practicing. 这项运动的爱好者们花费数小时练习。|synonyms: followers, admirers, fans, enthusiasts, aficionado|antonyms: critics, detractors, skeptics|noun
