ferric:/ˈferɪk/| adj.|铁的，含铁的|Containing or relating to iron, especially in its trivalent state (Fe³⁺).|例句：Ferric oxide is a common iron compound. 氧化铁是一种常见的铁化合物。|例句：The ferric chloride solution turned a deep red color. 氯化铁溶液变成了深红色。|近义词：iron(III), iron|反义词：ferrous|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *ferrique*, from Latin *ferrum* (iron).  The "-ic" suffix indicates a higher valence state.
