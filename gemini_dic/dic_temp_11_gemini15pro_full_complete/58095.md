drop-in:/ˈdrɒpɪn/ (UK) /ˈdrɑːpɪn/ (US)| adj. n.|即兴的，不速之客，开放接待时间|A drop-in event or facility is one that people can visit or use whenever they choose, without having to make an appointment.| 例句：The clinic has drop-in hours on Saturday mornings.  诊所周六上午有开放接待时间。|近义词：casual, informal, unplanned|反义词：scheduled, by appointment|词性:形容词/名词
