gybing:/ˈdʒaɪbɪŋ/
| n. | 转帆; 顺风换舷 | The act of changing the course of a sailing vessel downwind, such that the stern of the vessel passes through the wind. |  The gybing was executed perfectly, and the boat surged forward.  转帆执行得非常完美，船向前猛冲。| 近义词: jibing | 反义词: tacking | 词性: 名词

gybe: /dʒaɪb/
| v. | 转帆; 顺风换舷 | To change the course of a sailing vessel downwind, such that the stern of the vessel passes through the wind. | The captain ordered the crew to gybe the mainsail. 船长命令船员转动主帆。| 近义词: jibe | 反义词: tack | 词性: 动词
