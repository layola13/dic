consonous:/ˈkɒnsənəs/ /kənˈsoʊnəs/ | adj. | 一致的，和谐的；符合的 |  Agreeing; in agreement; harmonious; in harmony.  Characterized by agreement or harmony. | The team reached a consonous decision on the project.  团队就该项目达成了一致意见。

The community's consonous support for the new park was heartwarming. 社区对新公园的一致支持令人感到温暖。| 近义词：harmonious, accordant, agreeable, congruent, compatible, consistent | 反义词：discordant, dissonant, incongruous, incompatible, inconsistent | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *consonus, from Latin consonans, present participle of consonare (“to sound together, harmonize, agree”), from con- (“together”) + sonare (“to sound”).
