chelifer: /ˈkɛlɪfər/ | n. | 蝎子；伪蝎 | An arachnid of the order Pseudoscorpiones, also called a pseudoscorpion.  These small arthropods resemble scorpions but lack a stinging tail. | Example: The chelifer, though tiny, is a fierce predator of mites and other small invertebrates.  这只伪蝎虽然很小，却是螨虫和其他小型无脊椎动物的凶猛捕食者。

Example:  He discovered a chelifer hiding under a rock in his garden. 他在花园的岩石下发现了一只伪蝎。| 近义词: pseudoscorpion | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From New Latin *chēlīfer, from Latin chēla (“claw, chela”) + -fer (“-bearing”).  Ultimately from Ancient Greek χηλή (khēlḗ, “claw, hoof”).
