chaplaincy:/ˈtʃæplɪnsi/
n.
牧师的职位；牧师的管辖区；附属教堂
The office or position of a chaplain.  A place of worship or a body served by a chaplain.
The chaplaincy of the prison was vacant.  监狱的牧师职位空缺了。
近义词：chaplainship
反义词: (无直接反义词)
词性: 名词
