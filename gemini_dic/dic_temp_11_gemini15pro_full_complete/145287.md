overbraced: /ˌoʊvərˈbreɪst/
adjective
过度支撑的；过度加强的
having too many braces or supports; excessively strengthened or reinforced
The bridge was overbraced, making it unnecessarily heavy and expensive.  这座桥过度支撑，使其不必要地沉重和昂贵。
The design was overbraced, adding unnecessary complexity and cost.  该设计过度加强，增加了不必要的复杂性和成本。
Synonyms: overengineered, overbuilt, overdesigned
Antonyms: underbraced, weak, flimsy
词性: adjective
