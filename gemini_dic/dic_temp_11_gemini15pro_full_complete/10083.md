apocrypha: /əˈpɒkrɪfə/ (UK) /əˈpɑːkrɪfə/ (US)| n. |伪经；伪书；轶事；可疑的记载| Writings or reports not considered genuine or of doubtful authenticity. |  Many stories about his childhood are considered apocrypha.  关于他童年的许多故事都被认为是杜撰的。| synonyms: spurious writings, unauthenticated stories, doubtful accounts | antonyms: canonical scriptures, authentic texts | noun

apocryphal: /əˈpɒkrɪfəl/ (UK) /əˈpɑːkrɪfəl/ (US)| adj. |伪经的；伪造的；可疑的| Of doubtful authenticity, although widely circulated as being true.|  An apocryphal story about the president's childhood circulated on the internet.  一个关于总统童年轶事的可疑故事在互联网上传播开来。| synonyms: spurious, fabricated, fictitious, doubtful, questionable | antonyms: authentic, genuine, verifiable | adjective
