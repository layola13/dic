acrolects:/ˈækrəʊlɛkts/| n.|高层语言，标准语言|The acrolect is the most prestigious dialect of a language, often associated with education and formal settings.  高层语言是一种语言中最有声望的方言，通常与教育和正式场合相关。|Linguists study the differences between basilects, mesolects, and acrolects in a language. 语言学家研究一种语言中底层语言、中层语言和高层语言之间的差异。|Synonyms: standard language, prestige dialect|Antonyms: basilect, vernacular|noun
