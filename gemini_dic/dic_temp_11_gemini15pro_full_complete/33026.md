chorus: /ˈkɔːrəs/ (US) /ˈkɔːrəs/ (UK)| n. |合唱，副歌，齐声|A large organized group of singers, typically one that performs together regularly.  The part of a song that is repeated after each verse. A simultaneous utterance by many people. |The school chorus sang beautifully at the concert. 学校合唱团在音乐会上唱得优美。| synonyms: choir, refrain, ensemble, unison | antonyms: solo, individual | noun

chorus: /ˈkɔːrəs/ (US) /ˈkɔːrəs/ (UK)| v. |合唱，齐声说|To sing or say something simultaneously with others. |The crowd chorused their approval.  人群齐声表示赞同。| synonyms: chime in, echo, join in | antonyms:  be silent, whisper | verb
