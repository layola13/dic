gelignite:/ˈdʒɛlɪɡnaɪt/
noun
炸药，胶质炸药
A type of explosive made from nitroglycerine, wood pulp, and potassium or sodium nitrate.
The miners used gelignite to blast through the rock. 矿工们用炸药炸穿岩石。
Synonyms: dynamite, explosive, blasting gelatin
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a specific explosive.  One could consider "peace" or "safety" as thematic antonyms.)
