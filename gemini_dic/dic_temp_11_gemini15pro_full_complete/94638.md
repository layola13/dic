impressibility:/ɪmˌprɛsəˈbɪləti/
n.
可印性；易感性；敏感性
The quality of being easily impressed or influenced.
Her impressibility makes her susceptible to emotional manipulation. 她的易感性使她容易受到情绪操纵。
sensitivity, receptivity, responsiveness
resistance, insensitivity, impassivity
noun
