manchesterization:/ˌmæntʃɛstəraɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.|曼彻斯特化|The process of becoming industrialized and urbanized, often with negative connotations of overcrowding, pollution, and social problems, based on the experience of Manchester during the Industrial Revolution.|The rapid manchesterization of the region led to widespread poverty and disease. 该地区的快速曼彻斯特化导致了普遍的贫困和疾病。|industrialization, urbanization|ruralization, de-urbanization|noun
