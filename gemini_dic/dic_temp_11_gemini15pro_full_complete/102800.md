jarfuls:/ˈdʒɑːrfʊlz/| n.|几罐，若干罐|The recipe calls for three jarfuls of honey.  食谱需要三罐蜂蜜。|近义词：several jars, a few jars|反义词: a little, a small amount|noun


Etymology->

From "jar" (from Old French "jarre") + "-ful" (Old English "-full").  Meaning "the amount that fills a jar".
