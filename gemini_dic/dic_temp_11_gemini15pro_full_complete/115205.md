manifestational:/ˌmænɪfɛˈsteɪʃənəl/
|adj.|表明的，显示的|Relating to or in the nature of a manifestation; serving to manifest or reveal.|The artist's work was a manifestational expression of her inner turmoil. 艺术家的作品是她内心 turmoil 的外在表现。|
|近义词: expressive, demonstrative, revealing, indicative|反义词: hidden, concealed, latent, dormant|词性:形容词|

manifestation:/ˌmænɪfɛˈsteɪʃən/
|n.|表现，显示；显现|An event, action, or object that clearly shows or embodies something, especially a theory or an abstract idea.|The rash was a manifestation of an allergic reaction. 皮疹是过敏反应的一种表现。|
|近义词: display, demonstration, exhibition, expression|反义词: concealment, suppression, hiding|词性:名词|
