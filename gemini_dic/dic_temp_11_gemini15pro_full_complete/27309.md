calycular: /kəˈlɪkjʊlər/ | adj. | 杯状的;似萼的 | Resembling a calyx; cup-shaped.  Relating to or resembling a calyx, the outer whorl of floral parts, typically green and leaflike, enclosing the other floral parts in the bud. | The calycular bracts surround the base of the flower. 杯状苞片环绕着花朵的底部。| The plant has distinctive calycular appendages.  这种植物有独特的萼状附属物。| 近义词: cup-shaped, cup-like, calyx-like | 反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly) acalycular (without a calyx), non-calycular | 词性: 形容词
