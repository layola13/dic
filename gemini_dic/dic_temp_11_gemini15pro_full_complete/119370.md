microcosmic: /ˌmaɪkrəˈkɒzmɪk/ (US) /ˌmaɪkrəˈkɒzmɪk/ (UK)| adj.|微观的；缩影的|A microcosm is something that is seen as a small version of something much larger.|The village is a microcosm of the whole country.  这个村庄是整个国家的缩影。|Synonyms: miniature, epitome, model|Antonyms: macrocosmic, universal|Adjective
