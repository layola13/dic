muscle-bound:/ˈmʌsəlˌbaʊnd/
adjective
肌肉发达的；肌肉僵硬的
Having large, well-developed muscles that sometimes restrict movement.  A person who is muscle-bound may have difficulty with flexibility and range of motion.
He was so muscle-bound that he had trouble scratching his back. 他肌肉太发达了，以至于挠背都困难。
近义词: brawny, strapping, bulky
反义词: weak, flabby, atrophied
词性: adjective

muscle-bound:/ˈmʌsəlˌbaʊnd/
adjective
四肢僵硬的
(figurative) Inflexible; unwilling to change.
The company's muscle-bound management refused to adapt to the changing market.  这家公司僵化的管理层拒绝适应不断变化的市场。
近义词: rigid, inflexible, unyielding
反义词: flexible, adaptable, accommodating
词性: adjective
