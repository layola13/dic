galvanographic:/ˌɡæl.və.nəˈɡræf.ɪk/
adjective
电流描记的
Relating to or produced by galvanography, a technique of electrotyping.
Galvanographic techniques were used to create detailed images of the metal surface. 电流描记技术被用于创建金属表面的详细图像。
近义词:electrolytic, electrochemical
反义词:None
词性:adjective

galvanography:/ˌɡæl.vəˈnɒɡ.rə.fi/
noun
电流描记术
An electrotyping process for producing plates for printing.
Galvanography is a less common printing method today. 电流描记术是如今不太常见的印刷方法。
近义词:electrotyping
反义词:None
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *galvanographie*, from *galvano-* (from Italian *Galvani*,  referring to Luigi Galvani, Italian physician and physicist who studied animal electricity) + *-graphie* (from Greek *-graphia*, "writing").
