intercommune:/ˌɪntərˈkɒmjuːn/ /ˌɪntərˈkɑːmjuːn/
n.|市镇联合体；市镇联盟|An association or federation of communes, especially in France and Belgium. |The intercommune provides services like waste management and public transport.|近义词：federation, association, union|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps: individual commune, separate entity)|词性: noun
