extrinsical: /ɪkˈstrɪnzɪkəl/ /ekˈstrɪnzɪkəl/ | adj. | 外在的，外来的，非固有的 | Not inherent; external; coming from outside. |  Extrinsic motivation is driven by external rewards such as money, fame, grades, and praise. 外在动机是由金钱、名望、分数和赞扬等外部奖励驱动的。

Extrinsic factors, such as air temperature, can affect the experiment's results.  像气温这样的外部因素会影响实验结果。 | 近义词：external, outward, outer, superficial | 反义词：intrinsic, inherent, internal | 词性：形容词
