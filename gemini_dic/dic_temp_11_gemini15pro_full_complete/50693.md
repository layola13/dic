dermatopathic:/ˌdɜːrmətəˈpæθɪk/ | adj. |皮肤病的；由皮肤病引起的|Relating to or caused by skin disease.  Characterized by or affected with a skin disorder.|例句：Dermatopathic lymphadenopathy is a condition where lymph nodes become enlarged due to skin disease. 皮肤病性淋巴结病是一种由于皮肤病引起淋巴结肿大的疾病。

例句：The dermatopathic changes in his skin were a result of a severe allergic reaction. 他皮肤上的病变是由严重的过敏反应引起的。|近义词：cutaneous, dermal|反义词：None|词性：形容词

Etymology:
derma- + -pathic
derma-: from Greek *derma*, skin
-pathic: from Greek *-pathēs*, suffering from, affected by
