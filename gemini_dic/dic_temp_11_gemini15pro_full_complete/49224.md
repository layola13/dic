delegatees:/ˌdɛlɪˈɡiːz/| n. |代表，被委派者|People who have been delegated a task or responsibility.  Those to whom authority or power is delegated. |The delegatees from each department met to discuss the new policy. 每个部门的代表开会讨论新政策。| The conference welcomed delegatees from all over the world. 会议欢迎来自世界各地的代表。 |synonyms: representatives, envoys, commissioners, deputies | antonyms: delegators, assigners| noun
