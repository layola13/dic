hocus-pocus:/ˌhoʊkəs ˈpoʊkəs/| n. |戏法；胡言乱语；花招；骗人的把戏|Nonsense words used by magicians, or a trick or deception.|The magician waved his wand and muttered some hocus-pocus before pulling a rabbit out of the hat. 魔术师挥舞着魔杖，嘴里念叨着咒语，然后从帽子里变出一只兔子。|近义词：trickery, deception, sleight of hand, magic, conjuring|反义词:reality, truth, honesty|词性:noun

hocus-pocus:/ˌhoʊkəs ˈpoʊkəs/| v. |用戏法欺骗；施魔法|To deceive or trick someone using misleading words or actions.|He hocus-pocused his way into the position. 他用花言巧语骗取了这个职位。|近义词：trick, deceive, dupe, bamboozle, hoodwink|反义词:be honest, tell the truth|词性:verb
