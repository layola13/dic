nonmetrical:/nɒnˈmɛtrɪk(ə)l/ |adj.|非韵律的，非格律的，不符合韵律的|Not conforming to a regular metrical pattern; not metrical.|  The poem is intentionally nonmetrical, giving it a more natural, conversational feel. 这首诗故意不押韵，使其更自然，更具对话感。

The composer's nonmetrical rhythms were challenging for the orchestra to perform. 这位作曲家不符合韵律的节奏对乐团来说很难演奏。|Synonyms: arrhythmic, irregular, free verse (for poetry)|Antonyms: metrical, rhythmic, regular|adjective
