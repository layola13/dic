contorted:/kənˈtɔːrtɪd/ | adj. | 扭曲的，歪曲的 | twisted or bent out of its normal shape;  (of a face) twisted in pain or grimace |  His face was contorted in agony. 他的脸因痛苦而扭曲。| synonyms: twisted, deformed, warped, misshapen | antonyms: straight, unbent, normal, natural | adjective


contortion:/kənˈtɔːrʃn/ | n. | 扭曲，变形 | the act of twisting or deforming something out of its normal shape; a twisted or deformed state | The contortion of the metal was caused by the intense heat.  金属的扭曲是由高温引起的。| synonyms: twisting, warping, deformation, distortion | antonyms: straightening, smoothing, flattening | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *contortus*, past participle of contorquēre (“to twist, turn violently”), from con- (“together, with”) + torquēre (“to twist”).
