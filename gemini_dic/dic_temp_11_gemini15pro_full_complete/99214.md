intercohesion:/ˌɪntərkoʊˈhiːʒən/
n.
相互凝聚力；内部凝聚力
The intercohesion of the team was strong, leading them to victory.  团队内部的凝聚力很强， dẫn dắt họ đến chiến thắng。
The substance exhibited strong intercohesion, making it difficult to break apart. 这种物质表现出很强的内部凝聚力，使其难以分解。
近义词：unity, solidarity, cohesion, bond
反义词：division, disunity, fragmentation
词性: 名词

词源: "inter-" (between, among) + "cohesion" (the act or state of sticking together)
