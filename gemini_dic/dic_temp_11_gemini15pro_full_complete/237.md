abdicating:/ˈæbdɪˌkeɪtɪŋ/
verb
放弃；退位；逊位
present participle of abdicate, meaning to renounce or relinquish a throne, right, power, claim, responsibility, or the like, especially in a formal manner.
The king is abdicating his throne. 国王正在退位。
The CEO is abdicating his responsibilities. 首席执行官正在放弃他的责任。
Synonyms: relinquishing, renouncing, resigning, forsaking
Antonyms: assuming, seizing, usurping, accepting
