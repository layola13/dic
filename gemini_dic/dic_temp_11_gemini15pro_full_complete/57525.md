dragoon:/drəˈɡuːn/
v.|强迫，胁迫|To coerce or compel someone to do something.|The farmers were dragooned into providing food for the army. 农民被迫为军队提供食物。|coerce, compel, force, pressurize|persuade, encourage|verb
