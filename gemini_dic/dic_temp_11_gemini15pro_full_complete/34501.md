clean-cut:/ˈkliːnˌkʌt/| adj.|整洁的；轮廓分明的；干净利落的|Having a neat and tidy appearance; conventionally wholesome and respectable.  Often implies a conservative and traditional style.  |He was a clean-cut young man in a suit and tie. 他是一位穿着西装打领带的干净利落的年轻人。|近义词：tidy, neat, trim, well-groomed|反义词：scruffy, unkempt, messy|词性:形容词

clean-cut:/ˈkliːnˌkʌt/| adj.|清晰的；明确的；(尤指电影画面)剪辑干净的|Sharply defined; clear-cut; (especially of a film image) cleanly edited.  This meaning is less common than the appearance-related one. |The movie had a clean-cut, classic Hollywood style. 这部电影具有清晰的、经典的好莱坞风格。|近义词：clear, distinct, precise|反义词：blurred, fuzzy, indistinct|词性:形容词
