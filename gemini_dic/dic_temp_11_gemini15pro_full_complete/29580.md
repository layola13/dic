catechetical:/ˌkætɪˈkɛtɪkəl/
adjective
教义问答式的；教理问答的
Relating to the instruction of Christian doctrine by question and answer.
The priest led a catechetical session with the confirmation candidates.  神父带领坚信礼候选人进行了一场教义问答式的会议。
The textbook provided a catechetical approach to learning basic theological concepts. 这本教科书提供了一种教义问答式的方法来学习基本的神学概念。
Synonyms:  didactic, instructional, pedagogical
Antonyms:  n/a
词性: adjective
