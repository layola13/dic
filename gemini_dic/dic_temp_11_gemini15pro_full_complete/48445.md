deed:/diːd/| n.| 行为，行动；证书；契约| An action that is performed intentionally or consciously.  A legal document that is signed and delivered, especially one regarding the ownership of property or legal rights. |She performed a good deed by helping the elderly woman cross the street. 她帮助老妇人过马路，做了一件好事。| synonyms: act, action, feat, exploit, achievement, accomplishment | antonyms: inaction, inactivity | noun

deed:/diːd/| vt.|立契转让|To transfer (property) by deed. |He deeded the house to his son. 他立契约把房子转让给了儿子。| synonyms: convey, transfer | antonyms: retain, withhold | verb
