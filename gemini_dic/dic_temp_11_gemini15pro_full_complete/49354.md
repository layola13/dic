delinquent:/dɪˈlɪŋkwənt/| adj. n.|拖欠的，违法的；失职者，违法者|Failing in or neglectful of a duty or obligation; overdue in payment.|The delinquent account has been sent to a collection agency. 这个拖欠的账户已经被送到收账公司了。|近义词：overdue, remiss, negligent, culpable|反义词：responsible, punctual, prompt|adjective

delinquent:/dɪˈlɪŋkwənt/| n.|失职者，违法者|A person, especially a young one, who fails to do what is required by law or by duty, especially by failing to make a payment.|The social worker tried to help the juvenile delinquents. 社会工作者试图帮助这些青少年罪犯。|近义词：offender, criminal, culprit|反义词：law-abider, citizen|noun
