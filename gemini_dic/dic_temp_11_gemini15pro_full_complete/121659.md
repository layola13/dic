mispictured:/ˌmɪsˈpɪktʃərd/
| adj. |被错误描绘的，被歪曲的|Incorrectly or misleadingly depicted or represented.|The media mispictured the event, making it seem more violent than it actually was. 媒体歪曲了事件，使其看起来比实际情况更暴力。|例句：The artist felt mispictured by the critic's harsh review.  这位艺术家觉得评论家的严厉评论歪曲了他。

例句：The politician claimed to be mispictured in the documentary. 这位政客声称在纪录片中被歪曲了。|近义词：misrepresented, distorted, falsified|反义词：accurately portrayed, faithfully represented|词性:形容词
