engorge:/ɪnˈɡɔːrdʒ/ /ɛnˈɡɔːrdʒ/| v. |使充盈；狼吞虎咽；贪婪地吃|to fill something completely with blood; to eat a large amount of food quickly and greedily|The mosquito engorged itself with blood.  蚊子吸饱了血。

He engorged himself on the feast. 他在宴会上狼吞虎咽。|近义词：gorge, stuff, glut, satiate|反义词：starve, deprive|词性：动词

engorger:/ɪnˈɡɔːrdʒər/ /ɛnˈɡɔːrdʒər/| n. |充盈者；狼吞虎咽的人|one who engorges|The python, an engorger of large prey, swallowed the rabbit whole. 蟒蛇，一个吞食大型猎物的人，把兔子整个吞了下去。|近义词：glutton, gourmand|反义词：ascetic|词性：名词
