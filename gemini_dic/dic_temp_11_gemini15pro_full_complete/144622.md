outwinged:/ˌaʊtˈwɪŋd/
verb
胜过，超越（尤指在飞行速度或飞行距离方面）
surpass or outstrip in flight, especially in speed or distance.
The smaller plane easily outwinged the larger, heavier aircraft.  这架较小的飞机轻易地超越了那架更大、更重的飞机。
outdo, surpass, excel, outstrip, transcend
lag behind, fall behind
v.
