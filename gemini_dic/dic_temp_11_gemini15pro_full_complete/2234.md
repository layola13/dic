adduce:/əˈdjuːs/ | vt. | 引证；举出；提出 | to cite or offer as evidence or example | The lawyer adduced evidence that proved the defendant's innocence. 律师举证证明了被告的清白。| synonyms: cite, quote, present, offer, advance | antonyms: withhold, suppress | verb

adduce:/əˈdjuːs/ | vt. | 引用；引证；(为论点或声明)提供理由 | to give reasons for an opinion or suggestion |  He adduced several reasons for his resignation. 他为他的辞职提出了几个理由。 | synonyms: allege, assert, claim | antonyms: deny, refute | verb
