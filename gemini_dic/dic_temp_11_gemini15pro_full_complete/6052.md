amends:/əˈmendz/
noun
赔偿；补偿
compensation or reparation for a wrong or injury.
She made amends for her mistake by apologizing. 她通过道歉来弥补自己的错误。
synonyms: reparation, restitution, compensation, recompense
antonyms: penalty, punishment
n.
