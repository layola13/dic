modifier:/ˈmɒdɪfaɪər/ | adj. n. |修饰语，修饰词|A word, phrase, or clause that qualifies or describes another word or group of words. It limits or adds to the meaning of the word it modifies.|The word "very" in "very happy" is a modifier.  “very”在“very happy”中是修饰语。|Synonyms: qualifier, descriptor, attribute | Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider words like "subject," "head," or "nucleus" in a grammatical context) |词性: 形容词, 名词
