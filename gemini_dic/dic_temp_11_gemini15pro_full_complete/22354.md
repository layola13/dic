bony:/ˈboʊni/ (US) /ˈbəʊni/ (UK)| adj.|骨瘦如柴的；多骨的；似骨的|Composed of or resembling bone; very thin, so that bones show through the skin.|The old horse was bony and weak. 这匹老马骨瘦如柴，非常虚弱.|近义词：thin, gaunt, skeletal, emaciated|反义词: plump, fleshy, robust|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *bony*, from Old English *bānig* (“bony”), equivalent to *bān* (“bone”) + *-ig*. Cognate with German *beinicht*, *knochenig*.
