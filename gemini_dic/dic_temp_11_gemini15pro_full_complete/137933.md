nonvoters:/ˌnɒnˈvoʊtərz/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈvəʊtərz/ (UK)| n.|不投票者，未投票者|People who are eligible to vote but choose not to do so or are unable to do so.|例句：Nonvoters often feel disenfranchised from the political process.  不投票者常常感到被剥夺了政治权利。

例句：The number of nonvoters is increasing in many countries.  许多国家不投票的人数正在增加。|近义词：abstainers, non-participants|反义词:voters, electors|Noun
