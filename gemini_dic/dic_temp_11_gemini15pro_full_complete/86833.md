hematosis:/ˌhiːməˈtoʊsɪs/, /ˌhɛməˈtoʊsɪs/
noun
血液氧化；氧合作用
The process of oxygenation of the blood in the lungs or gills.  The transformation of venous blood into arterial blood.
Hematosis is essential for life as it provides oxygen to the body's tissues. 血液氧化是生命所必需的，因为它为身体组织提供氧气。
The efficiency of hematosis is reduced at high altitudes. 在高海拔地区，血液氧化的效率会降低。
Synonyms: oxygenation, aeration
Antonyms: deoxygenation, anoxia
