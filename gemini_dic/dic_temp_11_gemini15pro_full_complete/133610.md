nonfluidity:/ˌnɑːnfluˈɪdəti/
noun
非流动性; 非流质性
Lack of fluidity; the state of not being fluid.
The nonfluidity of the thick paint made it difficult to apply.  这浓稠的油漆不易流动，难以涂抹。
Near synonyms:  viscosity, thickness, stiffness, rigidity
Antonyms: fluidity, liquidity, flow
n.
