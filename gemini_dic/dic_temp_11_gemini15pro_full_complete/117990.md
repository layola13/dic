The memorialist submitted a petition to the government.  这位请愿人向政府提交了一份请愿书。 | 近义词: petitioner, memoirist, biographer | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English *memorialist*, from Medieval Latin *memoriālis* "pertaining to memory," from Latin *memoria* "memory" + *-ist*.
