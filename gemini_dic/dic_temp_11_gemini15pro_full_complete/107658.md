languishes:/ˈlæŋɡwɪʃɪz/
|v.|变得衰弱；失去活力；凋萎；受煎熬|To exist in an unpleasant or unwanted situation, often for a long time.|The economy languishes in recession. 经济在衰退中萎靡不振.|Synonyms: weaken, deteriorate, decline, fade, wilt, suffer|Antonyms: thrive, flourish, prosper|verb
