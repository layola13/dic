ethernet:/ˈiːθənɛt/ | n. | 以太网 | A system for connecting a number of computer systems to form a local area network, with data being transmitted over a single cable. |  Our office uses ethernet for its network connection. 我们的办公室使用以太网进行网络连接。 |  The university provides ethernet access in all dormitories.  这所大学在所有宿舍都提供以太网接入。 | 近义词: LAN (local area network) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider dial-up, Wi-Fi as alternatives) | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From ether (referring to the once-believed luminiferous aether, a medium thought to permeate all space) + net (short for network).
