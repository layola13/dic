open-mindedness:/ˌoʊpənˈmaɪndɪdnəs/ | n. | 开明; 心胸开阔 | The state of being receptive to new ideas or experiences; willingness to consider different perspectives. |  She demonstrated open-mindedness by listening to opposing viewpoints. 她通过倾听对立的观点展现了她的开明。| synonyms: receptiveness, tolerance, impartiality | antonyms: close-mindedness, intolerance, prejudice | noun

open-mindedness:/ˌoʊpənˈmaɪndɪdnəs/ | n. | 开放思想 |  A willingness to consider new ideas and perspectives. | He approached the problem with open-mindedness and a willingness to learn. 他以开放的思想和学习的意愿来处理这个问题。| synonyms: broad-mindedness, liberality | antonyms: narrow-mindedness, dogmatism | noun
