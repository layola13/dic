agro-climatic:/ˌæɡroʊklaɪˈmætɪk/ | adj. |农业气候的|Relating to the combined effects of climate and soil properties on agricultural production. |例句：The agro-climatic conditions in this region are suitable for growing grapes. 这个地区的农业气候条件适合种植葡萄。|例句：Farmers must adapt their practices to the specific agro-climatic conditions of their area.  农民必须根据他们所在地区的特定农业气候条件调整他们的耕作方式。|近义词：agro-ecological|反义词:|词性:adjective


agro-climatic:/ˌæɡroʊklaɪˈmætɪk/ | n. |农业气候学|The study of the combined effects of climate and soil properties on agricultural production. |例句：Agro-climatic studies can help predict crop yields. 农业气候研究可以帮助预测作物产量。|例句：Understanding agro-climatic zones is crucial for sustainable agriculture. 了解农业气候带对于可持续农业至关重要。|近义词：agro-ecology|反义词:|词性:noun
