overseeded:/ˌoʊvərˈsiːdɪd/| adj.| 过度播种的；草皮重播的|The lawn has been overseeded with ryegrass for winter color.  草坪上已经播种了黑麦草，以便在冬天保持绿色。| The golf course is overseeded annually to maintain a lush green surface year-round.  高尔夫球场每年都会进行草皮重播，以保持全年郁郁葱葱的绿色表面。| 近义词: resown, replanted | 反义词: sparsely seeded, underseeded | 词性: adjective

词源:  "overseed" comes from the prefix "over-" (meaning "excessively" or "too much") and the verb "seed" (meaning "to sow seeds").
