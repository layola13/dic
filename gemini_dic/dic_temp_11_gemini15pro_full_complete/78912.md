gipsiest:/ˈdʒɪpsiɪst/ | adj. | 最像吉普赛人的；最具吉普赛风格的 | Most characteristic of or resembling Gypsies or their way of life; most nomadic or free-spirited. |  She wore the gipsiest dress I'd ever seen, flowing and colorful. 她穿着我见过的最具吉普赛风格的连衣裙，飘逸而多彩。

| 近义词: nomadic, bohemian, free-spirited, unconventional, wandering | 反义词: settled, conventional, conformist | 词性: 形容词

gipsiest:/ˈdʒɪpsiɪst/ | adj. | 最浪漫的；最无拘无束的 | Most romantic or unrestrained; most evocative of a carefree, adventurous lifestyle. |  The music had a gipsiest melody, full of passion and longing. 这首音乐有着最浪漫的旋律，充满了激情和渴望。

| 近义词: romantic, adventurous, passionate, carefree, uninhibited | 反义词: restrained, inhibited, cautious, mundane | 词性: 形容词


词源：From Gypsy + -est (superlative suffix).  源自 Gypsy（吉普赛人） + -est（最高级后缀）。
