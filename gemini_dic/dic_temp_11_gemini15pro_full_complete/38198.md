compulsive:/kəmˈpʌlsɪv/| adj. n.|强迫性的，难以控制的；强迫症患者|Having a strong, often irresistible impulse to perform an act, especially one that is irrational or contrary to one's will.|He's a compulsive gambler. 他是个嗜赌成瘾的人。|近义词：obsessive, uncontrollable|反义词：controlled, voluntary|词性:形容词, 名词

compulsives:/kəmˈpʌlsɪvz/| n.|强迫行为者，强迫症患者（复数）|People who have compulsions, often associated with obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD).|The compulsives often feel a sense of relief after performing their rituals.  强迫症患者在完成他们的仪式后通常会感到轻松。|近义词：obsessives|反义词：None|词性:名词
