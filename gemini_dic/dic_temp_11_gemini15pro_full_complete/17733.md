bedegar:/ˈbɛdɪɡɑːr/| n.|蔷薇瘿/玫瑰瘿，蔷薇 Bedeguar 瘿|A mossy gall, often brightly coloured, found on wild roses, caused by a gall wasp. |The bedeguar is a common sight on wild rose bushes. 在野生玫瑰丛中，蔷薇瘿是常见的景象。|Synonym: rose gall, robin's pincushion|Antonym: (No direct antonym, could consider "healthy rose tissue")|Noun
