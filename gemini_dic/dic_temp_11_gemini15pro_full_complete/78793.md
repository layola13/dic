gilo:/ˈɡiːləʊ/| n.|灯笼辣椒，圭罗椒|A mild, sweet pepper, light green in color, popular in West African and Caribbean cuisine. It is often used in stews, soups, and sauces. |例句：We added gilo to the stew for a unique flavor. 我们在炖菜中加入了灯笼椒，以获得独特的风味。

例句：Gilo is a staple ingredient in many West African dishes. 灯笼椒是许多西非菜肴中的主要成分。|近义词：bell pepper, sweet pepper|反义词:chili pepper, hot pepper|词性:noun
