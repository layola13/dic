alkalamide: /ˌælkəˈlæmaɪd/ | n. | 生物碱酰胺 | A type of organic compound containing both an alkaloid and an amide group.  They are often found in plants and can have biological activity. |  This plant produces a variety of alkamides, some of which have shown anti-inflammatory properties. 这种植物会产生多种生物碱酰胺，其中一些具有抗炎特性。|Synonyms:  alkaloid amide (less common) | Antonyms:  N/A | noun
