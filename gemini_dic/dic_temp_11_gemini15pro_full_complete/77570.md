gearing-up:/ˈɡɪərɪŋ ʌp/|phrasal verb|准备；预备；加紧|The process of preparing or equipping oneself or something for a particular task or activity.  It implies an increase in activity or intensity in preparation for something.|The company is gearing up for a major expansion.  该公司正在为大规模扩张做准备。

The team is gearing up for the championship game.  该队正在为冠军赛做准备。|preparing, equipping, ramping up|winding down, slowing down|verb phrase


ETYMOLOGY->
"gear" (from Old Norse "gervi" meaning "equipment") + "up" (adverb indicating increase or intensification)
