monetizing:/ˈmʌnɪˌtaɪzɪŋ/
verb
货币化；盈利；变现
the process of converting or establishing something into legal tender.  The act of earning revenue from something.
The company is exploring ways of monetizing its user base.  这家公司正在探索使其用户群变现的方法。
commercializing, profiting, capitalizing
- (no direct opposite, could be considered the opposite of *losing money* or *incurring debt*)
v.
