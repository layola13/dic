bought:/bɔːt/
verb|买，购买|past tense and past participle of buy|I bought a new car. 我买了一辆新车。|purchased, acquired|sold|verb

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I buy groceries every week. 我每周都买菜。
He buys a newspaper every morning. 他每天早上都买报纸。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am buying a new dress for the party. 我正在为派对买一件新连衣裙。
They are buying a house in the suburbs. 他们在郊区买房子。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have bought a new laptop. 我买了一台新笔记本电脑。
She has bought a lot of books recently. 她最近买了好多书。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been buying organic vegetables lately. 我最近一直在买有机蔬菜。
He has been buying stocks in that company for years. 他多年来一直在买那家公司的股票。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I bought this book last year. 我去年买了这本书。
She bought him a gift for his birthday. 她给他买了一个生日礼物。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was buying groceries when the phone rang. 电话响的时候我正在买菜。
He was buying a ticket when the train arrived. 火车到达时他正在买票。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had already bought the tickets before you told me. 在你告诉我之前，我已经买了票。
She had bought the house before she got the job. 在她得到这份工作之前，她已经买了房子。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been buying books from that store for years before it closed down.  在那家书店关门之前，我已经在那里买书好几年了。
They had been buying up properties in the area for months before the market crashed. 在市场崩盘之前，他们已经在该地区购买房产好几个月了。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will buy a new phone next month. 我下个月要买一部新手机。
We will buy a bigger house when we have more money. 等我们有更多钱的时候，我们会买一更大的房子。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
This time next year, I will be buying Christmas presents. 明年这个时候，我将会在买圣诞礼物。
They will be buying a new car next week.  他们下周将买一辆新车。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By the end of the year, I will have bought a new car. 到年底，我将已经买了一辆新车。
By next summer, they will have bought a house. 到明年夏天，他们将已经买了一套房子。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next year, I will have been buying antiques for ten years. 到明年，我将已经购买古董十年了。
By the time he retires, he will have been buying stocks for over twenty years. 到他退休时，他将已经购买股票超过二十年了。

虚拟语气:
If I had enough money, I would have bought that car. 如果我有足够的钱，我就会买那辆车了。
I wish I had bought that painting when I had the chance. 我真希望我当初有机会的时候买了那幅画。


被动语态:
The house was bought by a young couple. 这所房子被一对年轻夫妇买下了。
The tickets were bought online.  这些票是在网上买的。

疑问句:
What did you buy yesterday? 你昨天买了什么？
Where did you buy that dress? 你在哪里买的这条裙子？

否定句:
I didn't buy anything yesterday. 我昨天什么都没买。
She hasn't bought a new car yet. 她还没有买新车。
