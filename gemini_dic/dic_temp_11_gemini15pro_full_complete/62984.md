enologists:/iːˈnɒlədʒɪsts/
n.
葡萄酒学家；酿酒学家
A person skilled in the science of winemaking.
The enologists at the winery carefully monitor the fermentation process.  这家酿酒厂的葡萄酒学家仔细监测发酵过程。
winemakers, vintners
