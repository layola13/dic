denotation:/ˌdiːnəʊˈteɪʃən/ | n. |指称意义；字面意义；表示|The denotation of a word is its literal meaning, distinct from its connotations. 一个词的指称意义是它的字面意义，与其内涵不同。|The denotation of "red" is the color itself, not the associated emotions. “红色”的指称意义是颜色本身，而不是与之相关的感情。|近义词:meaning, signification, sense, reference |反义词:connotation |词性:名词
