nonforensically:/ˌnɒnfəˈrɛnsɪkli/
adverb
非司法地，非取证地
In a manner not related to or suitable for legal proceedings or forensic analysis.
The evidence was gathered nonforensically, so it couldn't be used in court.  这些证据是非司法途径收集的，所以不能在法庭上使用。
The issue was discussed nonforensically, focusing on the human impact rather than legal ramifications.  这个问题被非司法地讨论，重点是人的影响而不是法律后果。
Synonym: informally, unofficially
Antonym: forensically
词性: 副词
