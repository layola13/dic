intermarketing:/ˌɪntərˈmɑːrkɪtɪŋ/
n.|内部营销|The marketing activities undertaken by a company to motivate its employees to perform their duties in a customer-focused manner, promoting customer satisfaction and loyalty.|例句：Intermarketing is essential for creating a customer-centric culture. 内部营销对于创建以客户为中心的文化至关重要。|例句：The company invested heavily in intermarketing training for its staff.  这家公司在其员工的内部营销培训上投入了巨资。|近义词：internal marketing, employee engagement|反义词:N/A|词性:名词

词源:
"inter-" (prefix meaning "between, among, mutually") + "marketing"
