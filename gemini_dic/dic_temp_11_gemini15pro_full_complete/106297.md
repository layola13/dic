knurl:/nɜːrl/| n. v. |滚花，压花；使有滚花|A small ridge or bead, typically one of a series, raised on a surface, especially to aid in gripping.  To make knurls on (a surface).|
The handle of the screwdriver was knurled for a better grip.  螺丝刀的手柄上有滚花，以便更好地握持。|
He carefully knurled the edges of the metal knob. 他小心翼翼地在金属旋钮的边缘压花。|
近义词：ridge, bead, corrugation, groove|
反义词：smoothness|
词性: noun, verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *knurle ("small knot, gnarl"),  related to knorren ("knot in wood").
