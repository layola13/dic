discerpible:/dɪˈsɜːrpɪbl/ | adj. | 可辨别的，可区分的 | Capable of being discerned or distinguished; perceptible. |  The subtle differences between the two wines were barely discerpible. 这两种葡萄酒之间的细微差别几乎难以分辨。|  The individual stars in the distant galaxy were not discerpible with the naked eye. 远处星系中的单个恒星用肉眼无法分辨。| synonyms: perceptible, distinguishable, noticeable, detectable, observable | antonyms: imperceptible, indistinguishable, unnoticeable, undetectable, invisible | adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *discerpibilis, from Latin discerpere "to tear apart, separate," from dis- "apart" + carpere "to pluck, gather."  The meaning shifted from "capable of being torn apart" to "capable of being distinguished" over time.
