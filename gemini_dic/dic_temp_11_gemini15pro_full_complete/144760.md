over-contraction:/ˌoʊvər kənˈtrækʃən/| n.|过度收缩|An excessive shortening, especially of a muscle or a word.|The over-contraction of muscles can lead to cramps. 过度收缩肌肉会导致抽筋。|Over-pronunciation, overextension|Under-contraction|noun
Etymology: From "over" + "contraction".  "Over" indicates excess, and "contraction" refers to the process of shortening or becoming smaller.  The word "contraction" itself ultimately derives from Latin "contrahere," meaning "to draw together."
