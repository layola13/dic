constructor's:/kənˈstrʌktərz/| n.|建造者的，建设者的| possessive form of constructor, meaning "belonging to a constructor" or "of a constructor".  A constructor, in programming, is a special method within a class used to create and initialize an object of that class. Therefore, "constructor's" refers to something that belongs to or is related to this special method. |The constructor's parameters define the initial state of the object. 构造函数的参数定义了对象的初始状态。

The constructor's code sets the initial values of the object's properties. 构造函数的代码设置对象属性的初始值。|近义词:builder's, maker's|反义词:destroyer's, demolisher's|词性:noun (possessive)
