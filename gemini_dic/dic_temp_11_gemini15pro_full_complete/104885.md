keepability:/ˌkiːpəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 保持能力；耐贮性；耐藏性 | The ability of something to remain in good condition for a period of time. | The keepability of fresh produce is an important factor in reducing food waste. 新鲜农产品的耐贮性是减少食物浪费的一个重要因素。|  The bread has good keepability.  这种面包的耐贮性很好。 | synonyms: shelf life, storage life, durability, longevity | antonyms: perishability, fragility | noun
