buttresses:/ˈbʌtrəsɪz/| n.|支撑物，扶壁|A structure built against a wall to support or strengthen it.  A source of defense or support.|The cathedral walls are supported by huge stone buttresses.  这座大教堂的墙壁由巨大的石质扶壁支撑。|synonyms: support, prop, reinforcement, brace, pillar|antonyms: weakness, undermine|noun

buttress:/ˈbʌtrəs/| vt.|支持，加强|To support or strengthen something.|The government's position was buttressed by public opinion. 政府的立场得到了公众舆论的支持。|synonyms: support, reinforce, strengthen, bolster, underpin|antonyms: weaken, undermine, impair|verb
