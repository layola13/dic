beslubber:/bɪˈslʌbər/
verb
弄脏，涂抹；诽谤，诋毁
to smear or stain; to slobber over; to daub; to calumniate.
He beslubbered his shirt with gravy. 他把衬衫上弄满了肉汁。
She beslubbered her rival's reputation with malicious gossip. 她用恶毒的流言蜚语诋毁对手的名誉。

近义词: smear, stain, daub, calumniate, slander, defame
反义词: clean, cleanse, purify, commend, praise

词性:v.
