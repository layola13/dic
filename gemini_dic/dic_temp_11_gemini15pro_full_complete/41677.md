correspondences:/ˌkɔːrəˈspɑːndənsɪz/ |n.|通信，信件，一致，符合|communication by exchanging letters with someone; similarity or analogy between things|例句：She kept up a lively correspondence with her family while she was abroad. 她旅居国外时，和家人保持着频繁的通信。

例句：There is a close correspondence between the two maps. 这两张地图非常吻合。|近义词：communication, letters, agreement, conformity, similarity, analogy|反义词：disagreement, disparity, difference|词性: 名词(plural)
