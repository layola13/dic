comparable:/ˈkɑːmpərəbl/ | adj. | 可比较的；比得上的 |  able to be compared or worthy of comparison | Her test scores were comparable to the best students in her class. 她的考试成绩可以与她班上最好的学生相媲美。 |  The two cars are comparable in price and performance. 这两款车在价格和性能上不相上下。| similar, akin, analogous, equivalent | incomparable, dissimilar, unlike | adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *comparabilis, from Latin comparare "to compare," from com- "together" + parare "to make ready, prepare."
