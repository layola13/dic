diabetical:/ˌdaɪəˈbetɪk(ə)l/
adjective
糖尿病的；与糖尿病有关的
Relating to or having diabetes.
My grandmother needs a special diabetical diet. 我的祖母需要特殊的糖尿病饮食。
Diabetics often need to monitor their blood sugar levels. 糖尿病患者经常需要监测他们的血糖水平。
近义词: diabetic
反义词: non-diabetic
词性: adjective
词源: From Greek *diabētēs* (diabetes) + *-ikos* (-ical).
