combo:/ˈkɒmboʊ/ (US), /ˈkɒmbəʊ/ (UK)| n.|组合，套餐，连击|A combination of things, especially when considered to be very effective or convenient.  A series of punches or blows delivered in rapid succession in boxing or other martial arts.|I ordered the combo meal with a burger, fries, and a drink. 我点了一份汉堡、薯条和饮料的套餐。|synonyms: combination, package, deal, set|antonyms: individual, separate|noun

combo:/ˈkɒmboʊ/ (US), /ˈkɒmbəʊ/ (UK)| adj. (informal)|组合的|Combined or offered in combination.|The combo ticket gets you into both museums. 这张联票可以让你进入两个博物馆。|synonyms: combined, joint, package|antonyms: individual, separate|adjective
