homodont:/ˈhɒməʊdɒnt/ (UK) /ˈhoʊməˌdɑːnt/ (US)| adj. n.|同齿型的；同齿动物|Having or being teeth that are all of the same type.  Referring to an animal possessing only one type of tooth, as in toothed whales.|Dolphins are homodont, possessing numerous similar teeth. 海豚是同齿动物，拥有许多相似的牙齿。|The paleontologist identified the fossil as a homodont reptile. 古生物学家将化石鉴定为同齿爬行动物。|近义词: isodont|反义词: heterodont|词性: adjective, noun

Etymology:  From Greek *homos* "same" + *odont-* (from *odous, odontos* "tooth").
