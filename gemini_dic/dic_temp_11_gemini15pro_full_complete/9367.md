antipyretic:/ˌæntipaɪˈrɛtɪk/| adj. n.|退热的，解热药|Reduces fever; an agent that reduces fever.|例句：Aspirin is an effective antipyretic. 阿司匹林是一种有效的退烧药。

例句：The doctor prescribed an antipyretic for her high fever. 医生给她开了退烧药来治疗高烧。|近义词：antifebrile, febrifuge|反义词:pyretic|词性:形容词, 名词
