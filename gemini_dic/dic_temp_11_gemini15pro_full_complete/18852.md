beseecher:/bɪˈsiːtʃər/
n.
恳求者；祈求者
Someone who beseeches; one who entreats or implores earnestly.
The beseecher knelt before the king, begging for mercy.  恳求者跪在国王面前，乞求怜悯。
The beseecher's voice was filled with desperation. 恳求者的声音充满了绝望。
supplicant, petitioner, implorer, pleader
opponent, adversary
