liberalise:/ˈlɪbərəlaɪz/ /ˈlɪbrəlaɪz/| v. |使自由化，放宽限制|To make something less strict or controlled, allowing more freedom.|The government decided to liberalise the laws regarding freedom of speech. 政府决定放宽有关言论自由的法律。|Synonyms: deregulate, loosen, relax, ease, free up|Antonyms: restrict, regulate, control, tighten, constrain|verb

liberalization:/ˌlɪbərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌlɪbrəlaɪˈzeɪʃn/| n. |自由化，放宽管制|The act or process of making something less strict or controlled.|The liberalization of trade policies has led to increased global commerce. 贸易政策的自由化导致全球贸易增长。|Synonyms: deregulation, relaxation, easing|Antonyms: restriction, regulation, control|noun
