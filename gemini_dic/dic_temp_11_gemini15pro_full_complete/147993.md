overriding:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪdɪŋ/| adj.|首要的，最重要的，压倒一切的；凌驾的|Having greater importance or influence than all other factors; dominant.|The overriding concern was the safety of the passengers. 最重要的考虑是乘客的安全。|Example:  His overriding ambition is to become a doctor.  他最大的抱负就是成为一名医生.|Synonyms: dominant, paramount, principal, chief, main, primary, foremost, predominant|Antonyms: secondary, subordinate, minor, insignificant|Adjective


overriding:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪd/| v.|否决，推翻，不理会；（自动装置）优先于，比…重要|To use one's authority to reject or cancel (a decision, view, etc.); to prevail over.|The chairman overrode the committee's objections. 主席否决了委员会的反对意见。|Example: The president overrode the decision of the committee. 总统否决了委员会的决定。|Synonyms: overrule, overturn, cancel, reverse, set aside, disregard, ignore, reject|Antonyms: uphold, sustain, confirm, endorse, ratify, approve|Verb
