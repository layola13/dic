opprobrium:/əˈproʊbriəm/
n.|耻辱，责难，咒骂|Harsh criticism or censure.|He faced opprobrium from his colleagues for his unethical behavior. 他因不道德的行为面临同事们的谴责。|synonyms: condemnation, censure, reproach, vilification|antonyms: commendation, praise, acclaim|noun
