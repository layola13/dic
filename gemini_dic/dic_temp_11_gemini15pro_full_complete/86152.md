heartlessnesses:/ˈhɑːrtlɪsnɪsɪz/
n.
无情；冷酷；残酷
The plural form of heartlessness, referring to multiple instances or acts of lacking compassion or empathy.
Her repeated heartlessnesses towards her family finally led to estrangement.她对家人的屡次冷酷无情最终导致了家庭关系的疏远。

Her heartlessnesses were evident in her refusal to help those in need. 她的无情表现在她拒绝帮助那些需要帮助的人。
近义词：cruelties, inhumanities, mercilessness
反义词：kindnesses, compassions, mercies
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From heartlessness + -es (plural suffix).  Heartlessness itself comes from heartless (lacking compassion) + -ness (suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality). Heartless derives from heart (the organ of emotion) + -less (suffix meaning "without").  Ultimately tracing back to Old English "heorte".
