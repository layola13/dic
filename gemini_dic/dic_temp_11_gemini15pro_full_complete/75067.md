frightless:/ˈfraɪtləs/| adj.|无畏的，不怕的|Not feeling or showing fright; fearless.|The frightless child happily petted the large dog.|近义词：fearless, undaunted, intrepid, brave, valiant|反义词：frightened, scared, fearful, timid, cowardly|词性:形容词
Etymology: frightless is derived from the noun "fright" (meaning fear or terror) + the suffix "-less" (meaning without). The word "fright" comes from the Middle English word "frighten," meaning to frighten.
