lopseed:/ˈlɑːpsiːd/| n. |歪斜的；不平衡的；不匀称的|A lopseed is something that is leaning or drooping to one side.  It can also refer to plants in the Phrymaceae family.|The sunflowers were lopseed from lack of water. 由于缺水，向日葵都歪了。|近义词：askew, asymmetrical, uneven, unbalanced, crooked, tilted|反义词:straight, symmetrical, balanced, even|noun
