arenilitic:/ˌærəˈnɪlɪtɪk/
adjective
砂屑的，由砂岩构成的
Composed of or containing sand; sandy.  Specifically, relating to or derived from sandstone.
The arenilitic rocks in this region are rich in fossils.  这个地区的砂屑岩富含化石。
Near synonyms:  sandy, arenaceous, psammitic
Antonyms:  clayey, argillaceous, pelitic
詞性: adjective
