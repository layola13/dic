anglicizing:/ˈæŋɡlɪsaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使英国化；英语化
The process of making something more English in form or character.
Many immigrants anglicized their names to fit in better. 许多移民将他们的名字英语化，以便更好地融入。
The spelling of the word has been anglicized over time.  这个词的拼写随着时间的推移已经被英语化了。
