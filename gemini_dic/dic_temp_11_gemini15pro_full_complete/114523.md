malaise:/mæˈleɪz/ /məˈleɪz/| n.|不舒服; 不适; 衰弱;  malaise (also a sense of malaise) a general feeling of being ill, or an uncomfortable feeling that something is wrong|例句：A general air of malaise settled over the country. 整个国家弥漫着一种普遍的不安气氛。

例句：She suffered from a general malaise and loss of appetite. 她浑身不适，没有食欲。|近义词：unease, discomfort, sickness, illness, weakness, lethargy|反义词:well-being, comfort, energy, health|词性:noun
