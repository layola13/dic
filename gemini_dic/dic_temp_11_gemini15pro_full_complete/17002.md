bat:/bæt/
n.|球棒；蝙蝠|A piece of wood used to hit a ball in games like baseball and cricket. Also, a nocturnal flying mammal.|He swung the bat and hit a home run. 他挥动球棒，击出了一个本垒打。|近义词:club (for sports), stick|反义词:(for animal) bird|Noun

bat:/bæt/
v.|挥动（尤指球棒）|To hit something with a bat.|He batted the ball over the fence. 他用球棒把球击过了篱笆。|近义词:hit, strike|反义词:miss|Verb
