clubhouse:/ˈklʌbˌhaʊs/ (US), /ˈklʌbˌhaʊs/ (UK)| n.|会所，俱乐部，棚屋|A building used for the activities of a club; a place where members of a club can meet and socialize. Also, a small shelter or shed, typically on a golf course or sports field.|例句：The golf clubhouse offers stunning views of the course.  高尔夫俱乐部会所的球场景观令人叹为观止。

例句：They met at the yacht clubhouse for drinks after sailing.  帆船运动结束后，他们在游艇俱乐部会所聚会喝酒。|近义词：club, center, lodge, hall|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
