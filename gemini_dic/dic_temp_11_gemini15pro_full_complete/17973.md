befriender:/bɪˈfrɛndər/
n.|施以友谊的人，帮助者|A person who befriends or supports someone, especially someone who is vulnerable or isolated. |She volunteered as a befriender to an elderly woman in the community.|近义词:helper, supporter, advocate, mentor|反义词:enemy, adversary, opponent|词性:名词

Etymology:
From befriend + -er.  "Befriend" comes from the Old English "befrēondan," meaning "to make a friend of, to associate oneself with."
