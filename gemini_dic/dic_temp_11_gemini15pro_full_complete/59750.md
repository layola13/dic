evolutionary: /ˌiːvəˈluːʃəneri/ | adj. | 进化的，演化的 | Relating to or denoting the process by which different kinds of living organisms are thought to have developed and diversified from earlier forms during the history of the earth. | Example: The evolutionary history of the whale is fascinating. 鲸鱼的进化史令人着迷。|  Example:  Evolutionary changes occur over long periods of time. 进化变化发生在很长一段时间内。 | Synonyms: developmental, phylogenetic, transformative | Antonyms: static, unchanging | adjective
