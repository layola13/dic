hint-at:/hɪnt ˈæt/ | phrasal verb |暗示，示意|To suggest something indirectly or subtly, without stating it explicitly. |He hinted at the possibility of a promotion. 他暗示了升职的可能性。

She hinted at her dissatisfaction with the current situation. 她暗示了她对现状的不满。|近义词：suggest, imply, intimate, allude to |反义词：state, declare, express explicitly |词性:动词短语
