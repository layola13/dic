nannyish:/ˈnæniɪʃ/| adj.|保姆似的，过度保护的，婆婆妈妈的|Resembling or characteristic of a nanny, especially in being overprotective or fussy.|例句：He's a bit nannyish with his children, always hovering around them. 他对他的孩子们有点像保姆，总是围着他们转。

例句：She gave me a nannyish lecture about wearing a scarf in the cold. 她像保姆一样教训我要在寒冷的天气里戴围巾。|近义词：overprotective, fussy, interfering, meddlesome|反义词：laid-back, relaxed, permissive|词性:形容词
