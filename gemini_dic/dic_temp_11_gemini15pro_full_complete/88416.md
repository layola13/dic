his-excellency:/hɪz ˈɛksələnsɪ/
noun
阁下；大使阁下
A title of honor for certain high officials, such as an ambassador or governor.
His Excellency the Ambassador of France will address the assembly.  法国大使阁下将在大会上致辞。
Synonyms: Your Excellency, His/Her Excellency
Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a title, but could consider terms like "commoner," "civilian," depending on context)
