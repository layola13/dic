distorters:/dɪˈstɔːrtərz/
n.|失真器；扭曲者；歪曲者|People who distort facts or information.  Those who misrepresent something, often for their own benefit. |The media can be powerful distorters of reality. 媒体可能是强大的现实扭曲者。|Synonyms: falsifiers, perverters, misrepresenters|Antonyms: clarifiers, rectifiers|noun

distort:/dɪˈstɔːrt/
v.|扭曲；变形；歪曲|To twist out of shape; to give a false or misleading account of; to misrepresent.|The loud music distorted the sound of her voice.  吵闹的音乐使她的声音失真了。|Synonyms: warp, deform, misrepresent|Antonyms: clarify, rectify|verb
