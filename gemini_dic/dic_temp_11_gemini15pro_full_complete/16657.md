barracuda:/ˌbærəˈkuːdə/ | n. |梭鱼；奸诈的人|A large, predatory marine fish found in tropical and subtropical waters, known for its elongated body, powerful jaws, and sharp teeth.  Figuratively, it can also refer to a ruthless or aggressive person. |例句：The barracuda darted through the coral reef. 梭鱼飞快地穿过珊瑚礁。

例句：He's a real barracuda in the business world. 在商界，他真是个狠角色。 |近义词：pike, predatory fish (literal); shark, cutthroat (figurative)|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "prey" in the literal sense, or "docile/gentle" in the figurative sense) |词性:noun
