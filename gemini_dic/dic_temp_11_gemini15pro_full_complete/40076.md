contagiously:/kənˈteɪdʒəsli/| adv.|传染地；感染力强地|In a way that spreads quickly from one person or thing to another.|Her enthusiasm spread contagiously through the crowd. 她的热情感染了人群。|近义词：infectiously, spreadingly|反义词: non-contagiously (非正式用法), uninfectiously|词性:副词

contagious:/kənˈteɪdʒəs/| adj.|传染性的；有感染力的|Able to be passed easily from one person to another.|The flu is a highly contagious virus. 流感是一种高度传染性的病毒。|近义词：infectious, catching|反义词: non-contagious|词性:形容词


词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin contagiosus, from Latin contingere ‘touch closely’ (see contingent).
