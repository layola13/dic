hindbrain:/ˈhaɪndbreɪn/ | n. | 后脑 | The part of the brain at the back of the skull, composed of the medulla oblongata, pons, and cerebellum.  It controls basic life functions such as breathing and heart rate. |  Damage to the hindbrain can be life-threatening.  后脑损伤可能危及生命。| synonyms: rhombencephalon | antonyms: forebrain | noun
