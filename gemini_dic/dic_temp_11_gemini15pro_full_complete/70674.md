finagle:/fɪˈneɪɡl/| vt.|欺骗；哄骗；搞到；(用计)弄到|to obtain something by indirect or involved means; to achieve something by trickery or devious methods|He finagled a free ticket to the concert. 他骗到了一张免费的演唱会门票。|近义词：wangle, manipulate, scheme, contrive|反义词: earn, deserve|词性:动词

finagle:/fɪˈneɪɡl/| n.|欺骗；哄骗；(用计)弄来的东西|an act of finagling; something obtained by finagling|That free ticket was a real finagle. 那张免费的票真是骗来的。|近义词：trick, ruse, scheme|反义词: honest acquisition|词性:名词
