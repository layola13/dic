mastectomies:/mæsˈtɛktəmiz/ | n. | 乳房切除术 | The surgical removal of one or both breasts, typically to treat or prevent breast cancer. |  After her diagnosis, she underwent a double mastectomy. 诊断出来后，她接受了双侧乳房切除术。

She opted for a preventative mastectomy due to her family history. 由于家族病史，她选择了预防性乳房切除术。 | 近义词: breast removal surgery | 反义词: breast reconstruction | 词性: 名词 (Plural)
