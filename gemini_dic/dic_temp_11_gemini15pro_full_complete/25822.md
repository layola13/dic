ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch burgemeester, from burge "citizen, burgher" + meester "master".  The element burge is from Proto-Germanic *burgiz, from *burgs "fortress, town."  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰerǵʰ- "high, elevated."
