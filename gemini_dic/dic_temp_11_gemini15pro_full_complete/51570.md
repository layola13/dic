deuteroscopy:/ˌdjutərˈɒskəpi/ | n. |氘镜检查；宫腔镜检查| Examination of the uterus using a deuterium light source.  It allows for better visualization of the endometrial lining. |  Example: The doctor recommended a deuteroscopy to investigate the cause of her abnormal bleeding. 医生建议进行氘镜检查以调查她异常出血的原因。

Example: Deuteroscopy is a valuable tool in diagnosing uterine abnormalities. 氘镜检查是诊断子宫异常的宝贵工具。| 近义词: hysteroscopy, endoscopy | 反义词:  None (No direct antonym for a medical procedure) | 词性: noun
