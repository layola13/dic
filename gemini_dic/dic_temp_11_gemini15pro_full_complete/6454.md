amplifies:/ˈæmplɪfaɪz/| v. |放大，增强|To increase the strength or intensity of something, especially sound or a signal.|The microphone amplifies the speaker's voice.  麦克风放大了演讲者的声音。|
The media amplified the public's fears about the virus. 媒体放大了公众对病毒的恐惧。|近义词：increase, boost, magnify, augment|反义词: reduce, diminish, decrease|词性:动词

amplified:/ˈæmplɪfaɪd/| adj. |放大的，增强的|Increased in strength or intensity.|The amplified music was too loud. 放大的音乐太吵了。|The amplified signal was clear and strong. 放大的信号清晰而强劲。|近义词：increased, boosted, magnified, augmented|反义词: reduced, diminished, decreased|词性:形容词

词源:
From Late Latin amplificāre, from Latin amplus (“large”) + -ficāre, a derivational suffix from facere (“to make, do”).
