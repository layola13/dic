一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I have a book. = 我有一本书。
She has a beautiful car. = 她有一辆漂亮的汽车。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am having dinner with my family. = 我正在和家人共进晚餐。
He is having a hard time understanding the concept. = 他很难理解这个概念。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have had this car for five years. = 我这辆车已经五年了。
They have had many arguments recently. = 他们最近有很多争论。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been having headaches all week. = 我整个星期都头疼。
She has been having trouble sleeping lately. = 她最近一直有睡眠问题。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I had a wonderful dream last night. = 我昨晚做了一个美梦。
He had a meeting with his boss yesterday. = 他昨天和他的老板开会了。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was having lunch when he called. = 他打电话时我正在吃午饭。
She was having a shower when the doorbell rang. = 门铃响的时候她正在洗澡。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had already had dinner before she arrived. = 她到达之前我已经吃过晚饭了。
He had had a bad day at work before the party. = 派对前他在工作中度过了糟糕的一天。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would be having a bigger party. = 如果我有更多钱，我会举办一个更大的派对。
I wish I were having a better time. = 我希望我玩得更开心些。


被动语态 (被动语态通常不用于"have"的现在分词形式，除非是"have something done"结构):
The car is being had repaired. (不自然) 车正在被修理。(更自然的说法是：The car is being repaired.)


疑问句:

否定句:
