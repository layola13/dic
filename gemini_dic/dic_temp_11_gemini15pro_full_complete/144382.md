outstreamed:/ˌaʊtˈstriːmd/
verb
超越，胜过（在流媒体方面）
To surpass or defeat someone in a competition or activity related to streaming media, especially in terms of viewership, popularity, or revenue.
Ninja outstreamed all other Twitch personalities last month.  忍者上个月在Twitch上超越了所有其他主播。
The new cooking show quickly outstreamed the established channels. 新的烹饪节目很快就超过了老牌频道。
近义词:outperform, surpass, exceed, beat, top
反义词:underperform, lose to
词性:v.
