bookcraft:/ˈbʊkkræft/| n.|书籍工艺;书籍制作;图书工艺;图书制作|The art or craft of making books, especially handcrafted or artistically designed books.  This can include aspects like binding, printing, papermaking, and illustration.|She learned bookcraft from a local artisan. 她跟一位当地的手艺人学习了书籍制作。|近义词：bookbinding, bookmaking|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps book destruction)|词性:noun
