farcify: /ˈfɑːrsɪfaɪ/
verb
填充；填塞；装满
to stuff or fill, typically with something excessive or unnecessary
The comedian farcified his routine with silly jokes.  这位喜剧演员在他的表演中塞满了愚蠢的笑话。
They farcified the turkey with stuffing. 他们把火鸡填满了馅料。
近义词: stuff, pack, cram, fill
反义词: empty, evacuate
词性: v.

词源：
"Farcify" comes from the Latin "farcire," meaning "to stuff."  The suffix "-fy" turns it into a verb meaning "to make stuffed" or "to stuff."  来源于拉丁语“farcire”，意思是“填充”。后缀“-fy”将其变成动词，意思是“使填充”或“填充”。
