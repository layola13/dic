delouses:/diːˈlaʊzɪz/
verb
除虱，去除虱子
Removes lice from.
The veterinarian delouses the stray kittens. 兽医为流浪小猫去除虱子。
近义词:cleanses, disinfects
反义词:infests
词性:v.
