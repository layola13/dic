beyond:/bɪˈjɒnd/(UK) /biˈjɑːnd/(US)| prep. adv. n.|超越，超出，除…之外| extending or situated farther away than; on the far side of; later than; past; exceeding; outside the range or sphere of|
例句：The house is beyond the river. 房子在河的另一边。
例句：This task is beyond my abilities. 这项任务超出了我的能力。

近义词：past, after, further, exceeding, outside, over|
反义词：within, inside, beneath, before|
词性:介词 (preposition), 副词 (adverb), 名词 (noun)
