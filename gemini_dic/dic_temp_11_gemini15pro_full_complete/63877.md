epilogical:/ˌɛpɪˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
结尾的；后记的；结语的
Relating to or resembling an epilogue; concluding.
The epilogical chapter tied up all the loose ends of the story.  结尾的章节把故事的所有未了结的线索都串联起来了。
The film's epilogical scene showed the main characters ten years later.  电影结尾的场景展现了十年后主要人物的生活。
近义词:concluding, final, closing
反义词: introductory, opening, preliminary
词性: adjective
