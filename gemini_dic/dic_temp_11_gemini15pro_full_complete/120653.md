mis-sorting:/ˌmɪsˈsɔːtɪŋ/
noun
错误分类，分类错误
The act of sorting incorrectly.
The mis-sorting of mail caused delays in delivery. 邮件的错误分类导致了递送延误。
Mis-sorting of waste can contaminate recyclable materials. 垃圾的错误分类会污染可回收材料。
