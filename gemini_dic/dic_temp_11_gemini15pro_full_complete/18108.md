beheads:/bɪˈhɛdz/
| vt.|斩首，砍头|To cut off the head of; decapitate. |The king beheaded his enemies. 国王将他的敌人斩首。|
| |execute, decapitate, guillotine| |v.
ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + head + -s.  "Be-" is a prefix meaning "thoroughly" or "completely".  "Head" comes from Old English "hēafod". The "-s" marks the third person singular present tense form of the verb.
