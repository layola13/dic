essaylet:/ˈɛseɪlɛt/
n.
小短文，短论，小品文
A short essay; a brief literary composition.
She penned a charming essaylet on the joys of gardening. 她写了一篇关于园艺乐趣的迷人小短文。
近义词：essay, article, piece, composition
反义词: novel, book, dissertation, thesis
词性: 名词
