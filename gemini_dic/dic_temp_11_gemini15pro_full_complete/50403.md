deposition:/ˌdɛpəˈzɪʃən/ | n. | 沉积；沉积物；证词；免职 | the process by which sediment settles out of the water or wind that is carrying it, or the sediment itself; a formal written statement made or used in a law court; the act of removing someone important from a powerful position |  The deposition of sand on the beach created a new sandbar. 沙子在海滩上的沉积形成了一个新的沙洲。 | synonyms: sediment, deposit, testimony, removal | antonyms: erosion, excavation, reinstatement | noun

deposition:/ˌdɛpəˈzɪʃən/ | n. | 宣誓作证；证言 |  a formal statement that someone who has promised to tell the truth makes so that the statement can be used in court | His deposition was crucial to the case. 他的证词对这个案子至关重要。| synonyms: testimony, affidavit, sworn statement | antonyms:  refutation, denial | noun
