accorder:/əˈkɔːrdər/ (UK), /əˈkɔːrdər/ (US)| v. |授予，给予；使一致，使符合|To grant or give something, such as power, status, or recognition; to make something consistent or compatible with something else.|The government accorded him full diplomatic status. 政府授予他正式外交地位。|Synonyms: grant, bestow, confer, award, give, concede, harmonize, reconcile|Antonyms: refuse, deny, withhold, disagree, clash|verb

accorder:/əˈkɔːrdər/ (UK), /əˈkɔːrdər/ (US)| n. |（尤指手风琴式的）键盘乐器|A keyboard instrument, especially one like an accordion.|He played a lively tune on the accorder. 他用手风琴演奏了一首轻快的曲子。|Synonyms: accordion, concertina|Antonyms: None|noun
