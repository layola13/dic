fatherlinesses:/ˈfɑːðərlɪnɪsɪz/
n.
父爱，慈父般的品质（复数）
The quality of being fatherly; paternal kindness.
His fatherlinesses were evident in the way he cared for his children. 他对孩子们的关爱体现了他的父爱。
The orphan craved the fatherlinesses he had never known.  这个孤儿渴望他从未感受过的父爱。
近义词：paternalism, fatherhood
反义词：neglect, indifference
词性：名词

Etymology:
From fatherly + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  "Fatherly" comes from "father" (Old English fæder) + "-ly" (Old English -līc, meaning "like"). "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, denoting a state or quality.  "-es" is a plural suffix.
