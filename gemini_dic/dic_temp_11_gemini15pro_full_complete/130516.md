nonaccommodations:/ˌnɑːnəkɑːməˈdeɪʃənz/
|n.|无处容身；缺乏住宿；不提供住宿；不迁就；不妥协|lack of accommodations; refusal to accommodate or compromise.|The refugees faced nonaccommodations in the overcrowded city. 难民们在这个拥挤的城市里无处容身。|Near Antonyms: accommodations, provisions|Antonyms: compromise, concession|noun

nonaccommodation:/ˌnɑːnəkɑːməˈdeɪʃən/
|n.|不适应；不调节|failure or refusal to adapt or adjust.|The nonaccommodation of the eye muscles caused blurry vision. 眼部肌肉的不适应导致视力模糊。|Near Antonyms: adaptation, adjustment|Antonyms: accommodation, compromise|noun
