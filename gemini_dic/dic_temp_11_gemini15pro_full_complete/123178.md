mono-cable:/ˈmɒnəʊˌkeɪbəl/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnoʊˌkeɪbəl/ (US)| n. |单缆; 单线缆|A single cable transmitting multiple signals, often used in aerial tramways or cable cars.|The new mono-cable system allows for a smoother ride. 新的单线缆系统可以让乘坐更加平稳。|The mono-cable gondola swiftly transported skiers up the mountain. 单线缆吊箱迅速地把滑雪者送上山顶。|Synonyms: single cable, single-line cable|Antonyms: bi-cable, double cable|noun
