non-productive:/ˌnɒn prəˈdʌktɪv/ /ˌnɑːn prəˈdʌktɪv/| adj.|非生产性的，徒劳的|Not producing or capable of producing; not yielding results or profits.|例句：The meeting was non-productive, we didn't make any decisions. 会议没有成果，我们没有做出任何决定。

例句：Spending hours on social media can be non-productive.  花几个小时在社交媒体上可能是没有效率的。|近义词：ineffective, inefficient, fruitless, barren, sterile, unproductive|反义词：productive, fruitful, effective, efficient|词性:形容词
