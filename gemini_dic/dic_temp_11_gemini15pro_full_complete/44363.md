cross-checking:/ˌkrɒsˈtʃɛkɪŋ/ | n. |交叉核对，交叉检验|The process of verifying information or data by comparing it with multiple sources or using different methods to ensure accuracy and consistency. | We performed cross-checking of the data to ensure its reliability. 我们对数据进行了交叉核对，以确保其可靠性。| cross-verification, double-checking, validation | omission, negligence | noun

cross-check:/ˌkrɒsˈtʃɛk/ | v. |交叉核对，交叉检验| To verify information or data by comparing it with multiple sources or using different methods. |  Please cross-check these figures against last year's results. 请将这些数字与去年的结果进行交叉核对。|verify, validate, confirm | ignore, disregard | verb
