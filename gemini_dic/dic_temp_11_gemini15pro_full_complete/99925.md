internee:/ɪnˈtɜːrniː/
n.
实习生，拘留者，被拘禁者
A person who is confined as a prisoner, especially for political or military reasons.  A person, such as a student or recent graduate, who works as an intern.
例句：The internees were held in a camp outside the city.  被拘禁者被关押在城外的营地里。

例句：The summer internee quickly learned the ropes of the business.  暑期实习生很快学会了这门行业的诀窍。
近义词：detainee, prisoner, captive, intern
反义词：guard, warden, captor
词性：noun

internee:/ɪnˈtɜːrniː/
n.
实习生，拘留者，被拘禁者
A trainee who works, sometimes without pay, at a trade or profession in order to gain practical experience.
例句：He worked as an internee at a local newspaper. 他在当地一家报社当实习生。

例句：She was an internee at a law firm before she qualified as a solicitor. 在获得律师资格之前，她在一家律师事务所实习。
近义词：trainee, apprentice, probationer
反义词：employer, supervisor, mentor
词性：noun
