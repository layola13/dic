metricities:/mɛˈtrɪsɪtiz/
n.
韵律学；诗体学；韵律规则；格律
The study or analysis of verse structure, especially in terms of meter, rhythm, and rhyme.
His poetry is technically brilliant, demonstrating a masterful command of metricities. 他的诗歌技艺精湛，展现了对韵律的娴熟掌握。
Synonyms: metrics, prosody, versification
Antonyms: prose
