answerableness:/ˈænsərəblənəs/
n.
责任性；应答性；可解答性
The state or quality of being answerable; accountability.
His answerableness for the company's debts made him anxious. 他对公司债务的责任让他感到焦虑。
近义词：accountability, responsibility, liability
反义词：irresponsibility, unaccountability
词性：名词

词源：From answerable + -ness.  Answerable traces back to Old French *responsable (unattested) and ultimately to Latin respondēre "to respond."
