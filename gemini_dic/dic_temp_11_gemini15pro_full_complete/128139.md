netiquette:/ˈnetɪkɛt/ /ˈnetɪket/| n. |网络礼仪|The set of conventional rules and polite behaviors that are considered acceptable when communicating over computer networks, especially the Internet.|Please observe proper netiquette when posting in online forums. 请在网上论坛发帖时遵守网络礼仪。|近义词：online etiquette, cyber etiquette|反义词: impropriety, rudeness|noun

etymology->
From *net* (network) + *etiquette*.  The term was coined in the early days of the internet, blending the words "network" and "etiquette."
