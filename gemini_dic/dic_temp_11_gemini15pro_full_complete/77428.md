gavotte:/ɡəˈvɒt/ /ɡəˈvɑːt/| n.|加沃特舞；加沃特舞曲|A medium-paced French folk dance, popular in the 17th and 18th centuries, or the music for this dance.  一种中速的法国民间舞蹈，流行于17和18世纪，或这种舞蹈的音乐。|The orchestra played a lively gavotte.  管弦乐队演奏了一首活泼的加沃特舞曲。|Synonym:dance, jig, reel|Antonym: stillness, rest|noun
