niching:/ˈnɪtʃɪŋ/
n.
利基化；针对特定细分市场
The process of identifying and targeting a specific, well-defined segment of the market.
Many businesses are now niching down to target specific customer groups. 许多企业现在正进行利基化，以瞄准特定的客户群体。
specialization, targeting
generalization, diversification
noun

niching:/ˈnɪtʃɪŋ/
v.
找到一个合适的利基市场
To establish a niche or specialized market.
The company successfully niched itself in the luxury travel market. 该公司成功地在奢侈旅游市场中找到了自己的利基。
specializing, targeting
generalizing, diversifying
verb
