belies:/bɪˈlaɪz/
verb
掩饰；证明…为虚假；与…不相符
to show something to be false, or to hide something such as an emotion
Her calm face belied the terror she felt. 她平静的脸庞掩饰了她内心的恐惧。
Synonyms: contradict, mask, disguise
Antonyms: reveal, expose, show
词性: 动词
