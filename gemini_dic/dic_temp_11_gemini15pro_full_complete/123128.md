monish:/ˈmɑːnɪʃ/ (US), /ˈmɒnɪʃ/ (UK)| v. | 劝告，警告，告诫 | To warn or advise someone about something, especially potential danger or a mistake. | The teacher monished the students to study hard for the upcoming exam. 老师告诫学生们要为即将到来的考试努力学习。

The doctor monished him against smoking. 医生劝他戒烟。

| 近义词：admonish, caution, warn, advise | 反义词：commend, praise, encourage | 词性: 动词
