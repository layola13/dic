grubworm:/ˈɡrʌbwɜːrm/ | n. |蛴螬，金虫 |The larva of a beetle, especially a scarab beetle, that lives in the soil and feeds on roots.  | The grubworms destroyed the roots of my tomato plants. 蛴螬毁坏了我的番茄植株的根部。| synonym: white grub, larva | antonym: adult beetle | noun

grubworm:/ˈɡrʌbwɜːrm/ | n. |吃货，贪吃的人 (informal) |A person who eats a lot; a glutton. | He's such a grubworm, he ate the entire pizza by himself. 他真是个吃货，他一个人吃掉了整个披萨。| synonym: glutton, gourmand | antonym: anorexic | noun
