mastitides:/mæˈstaɪtɪˌdiːz/
noun
乳腺炎（复数）
Inflammation of the mammary gland, typically due to bacterial infection.  It is most common in breastfeeding women.
The farmer was concerned about the high incidence of mastitides in his dairy herd.  这位农民很担心他的奶牛群中乳腺炎的高发病率。
Mastitis can be painful and uncomfortable. 乳腺炎会很痛苦和不舒服。
synonyms: mammary gland infection, inflammation of the breast,  mammitis
antonyms: breast health
n.
