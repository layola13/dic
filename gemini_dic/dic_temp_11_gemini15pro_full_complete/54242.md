disglory:/dɪsˈɡlɔːri/
noun
耻辱，不光彩
lack of glory; shame or disgrace.
His career ended in disglory after the scandal. 他的职业生涯在丑闻之后以耻辱告终。
近义词:shame, disgrace, ignominy, dishonor
反义词:glory, honor, fame, renown
词性:n.

disglory:/dɪsˈɡlɔːri/
verb
使丢脸；使蒙羞
to deprive of glory; to disgrace.
The team was disgloried by their defeat.  这支队伍因失败而蒙羞。
近义词:dishonor, shame, disgrace, discredit
反义词:honor, glorify, exalt
词性:v.
