extrudes:/ɪkˈstruːdz/| v. |挤出，压出，突出，伸出| to shape (a material such as plastic or metal) by forcing it through a die| The machine extrudes the plastic into long pipes.  这台机器将塑料挤压成长管。|近义词：expel, force out, push out, squeeze out|反义词: inject, insert, draw in|verb

extrusion:/ɪkˈstruːʒən/| n. |挤压，突出，伸出物| the act or process of extruding something| The extrusion of molten plastic created the desired shape.  熔融塑料的挤压创造了所需的形状。|近义词：protrusion, projection|反义词:  intromission, insertion |noun
