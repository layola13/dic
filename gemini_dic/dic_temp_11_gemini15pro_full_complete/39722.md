consigns:/kənˈsaɪnz/
verb
交付；委托；托运
to give, deliver, or transfer something to another's care or custody, typically for sale or safekeeping.
The artist consigned her paintings to the gallery.  这位艺术家把她的画交付给画廊。
The goods were consigned to the warehouse.  货物被托运到仓库。
commit, entrust, deliver
retain, keep, withhold
v.
