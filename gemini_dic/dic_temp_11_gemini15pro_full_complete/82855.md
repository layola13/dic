gullahs:/ˈɡʌləz/
n.
格拉人，格拉文化
A member of a Black people living mainly on the coast of South Carolina and Georgia, who preserve elements of African language and culture.
例句：The Gullahs have a rich tradition of storytelling. 格拉人有着丰富的讲故事传统。

例句：Gullah cuisine is known for its use of seafood and rice. 格拉菜肴以其使用海鲜和大米而闻名。

近义词：African Americans, Black Americans
