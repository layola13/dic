oompah-pah:/ˈuːmpɑː pɑː/
noun
咚咚啪;  oompah music
A rhythmic accompaniment to music or dancing, typically played on brass instruments, characterized by alternating bass notes and chords.
The oompah-pah of the tuba filled the beer tent.  低音号的咚咚啪声充满了啤酒帐篷。
Synonyms:  um-pah-pah,  oom-pah
Antonyms: None (This word describes a specific sound, making antonyms difficult to identify)
n.
