hyperbolist: /haɪˈpɜːrbəlɪst/ | n. | 夸张者；吹牛大王 | A person who uses hyperbole; a person who exaggerates. | He's such a hyperbolist, you can never believe a word he says. 他真是个夸张者，你说的话一个字也信不过。 | Synonyms: exaggerator, overstater | Antonyms: understater, minimizer | Noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *hyperbolistes*, from Ancient Greek ὑπερβολιστής (huperbolistēs, “one who throws beyond; one who exaggerates”), from ὑπερβάλλω (huperballō, “I throw beyond; I exaggerate”)
