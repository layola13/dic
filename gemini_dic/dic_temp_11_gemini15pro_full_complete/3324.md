affordability:/əˌfɔːrdəˈbɪləti/
n.|可负担性，负担能力|The affordability of housing is a major concern for many people.  房价的可负担性是许多人关心的主要问题。|
例句：The rising cost of college makes affordability a significant concern for students. 大学学费上涨使得学生们非常关注可负担性。

例句：The government is trying to improve the affordability of healthcare. 政府正在努力提高医疗保健的可负担性。
近义词：cheapness, reasonableness, inexpensiveness
反义词：expensiveness, unaffordability
词性：名词
