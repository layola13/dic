ossuarium:/ˌɒsjuˈɛəriəm/ /ˌɑːsjuˈɛəriəm/| n. |骨灰存放处；藏骨堂|A container or room in which the bones of dead people are placed.|The catacombs contained an ossuarium filled with the bones of early Christians.|Synonym: charnel house, bone house|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps cemetery as a place where bodies, not just bones, are interred)|Noun
