cloche:/klɒʃ/ (UK), /kloʊʃ/ (US)| n.|钟形罩，玻璃罩，女式钟形帽|A bell-shaped cover, typically made of glass or plastic, used to protect plants or food.  Also, a close-fitting woman's hat shaped like a bell.|The gardener placed a cloche over the delicate seedlings. 园丁在娇嫩的幼苗上盖上一个钟形罩。|Synonyms: bell jar, cover, dome, hat|Antonyms: (For hat) wide-brimmed hat|noun

cloche:/klɒʃ/ (UK), /kloʊʃ/ (US)|  n. | 钟形女帽 | A woman's close-fitting hat shaped like a bell, fashionable in the 1920s. |  She wore a stylish cloche hat that framed her face perfectly. 她戴着一顶时髦的钟形女帽，完美地衬托着她的脸型。 |  Synonyms:  bell hat, flapper hat | Antonyms: wide-brimmed hat | noun
