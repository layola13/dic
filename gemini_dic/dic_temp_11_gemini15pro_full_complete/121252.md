misesteem:/ˌmɪsɪˈstiːm/
verb
低估，轻视，误判
to underestimate or undervalue; to have a wrong opinion of
She misesteems her own abilities. 她低估了自己的能力。
misjudge, underestimate, undervalue
overestimate, overvalue
