idealize:/aɪˈdiːəlaɪz/ | v. |理想化；将…理想化|To regard or represent as perfect or better than in reality.|She idealizes her childhood. 她把她的童年理想化了。|例句：He tends to idealize his heroes. 他倾向于把他心目中的英雄理想化。

例句：The movie idealizes the lives of cowboys. 这部电影将牛仔的生活理想化了。


近义词：romanticize, glorify, exalt|反义词：vilify, disparage, denigrate|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *idéalisé*, past participle of *idéaliser*, from *idéal* (“ideal”) + *-iser*, equivalent to English *ideal* + *-ize*.
