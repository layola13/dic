distendedly:/dɪˈstɛndɪdli/ | adv. | 膨胀地，扩张地 | In a distended manner; swollen or stretched out.  Characterized by being abnormally enlarged or expanded. |  His stomach looked distendedly after the large meal.  他吃完大餐后肚子看起来鼓鼓的。| The balloon floated distendedly across the sky. 气球膨胀地飘过天空。 | 近义词：swollenly, bloatedly, expandedly | 反义词：contractedly, compressedly | 词性: 副词

distended:/dɪˈstɛndɪd/ | adj. | 膨胀的，扩张的 | Enlarged, expanded, or swollen beyond normal limits. | His abdomen was distended. 他的腹部肿胀。 | The veins in his forehead were distended. 他额头上的血管膨胀。| 近义词：swollen, bloated, expanded | 反义词：contracted, compressed | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *distendere, from dis- "apart, away" + tendere "to stretch".
