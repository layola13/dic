electrogenesis:/ɪˌlektrəˈdʒenɪsɪs/
n.
生物体发电;生物电的产生
the production of electrical activity in a living organism, especially by nerve and muscle cells.
Electrogenesis is essential for the functioning of the nervous system. 生物电的产生对于神经系统的功能至关重要。
The electric eel is a master of electrogenesis, capable of generating powerful shocks. 电鳗是生物发电的大师，能够产生强大的电击。
