hyalophane:/ˈhaɪəloʊfeɪn/ | n. |透明长石|A barium-rich feldspar mineral, (Ba,K)Al(Si,Al)3O8, that forms a solid solution series with celsian.  It is typically colorless to white and occurs in metamorphic rocks. |Hyalophane is a relatively rare mineral found in certain metamorphic deposits. 透明长石是一种相对稀有的矿物，存在于某些变质矿床中。|  The geologist identified the mineral as hyalophane due to its chemical composition.  地质学家根据其化学成分将这种矿物鉴定为透明长石。|Synonyms: celsian (partially)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
