neighboring:/ˈneɪbərɪŋ/| adj.|邻近的，附近的|Located next to or very near something else. |例句：We visited the neighboring town. 我们参观了邻近的城镇。|例句：Neighboring countries often share cultural similarities. 邻国通常有相似的文化。|近义词：adjacent, adjoining, nearby, bordering|反义词：distant, remote|词性：形容词

Etymology: From Middle English *neighborenge*, from Old English *nēahgebūr*, equivalent to *nēah* (near) + *gebūr* (dweller).  The suffix *-ing* transforms the noun into an adjective.
