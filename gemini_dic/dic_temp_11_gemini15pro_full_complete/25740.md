buoyed:/ˈbɔɪd/| v. |支持，鼓励，使振奋；（使）浮起|supported, encouraged, or made more cheerful; (cause to) float|例句：The team was buoyed by the arrival of their star player. 球队的明星球员到来让他们精神振奋。

例句：The life jacket buoyed her up in the water. 救生衣让她在水中漂浮起来。|近义词：encouraged, uplifted, heartened, sustained, floated, supported|反义词：discouraged, depressed, disheartened, sunk|词性: 动词 (past participle of buoy)

buoy:/ˈbuːi/| n. |浮标，航标；救生圈|a floating object anchored in water to mark a position or hazard; a lifebuoy|例句：The ship navigated using the buoys in the channel.  这艘船利用航道中的浮标航行。

例句：He threw a buoy to the struggling swimmer. 他向挣扎的游泳者扔了一个救生圈。|近义词：float, marker, beacon, life preserver, lifebelt|反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "anchor" in a metaphorical sense)|词性: 名词
