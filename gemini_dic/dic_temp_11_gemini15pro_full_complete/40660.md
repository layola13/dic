convergence:/kənˈvɜːrdʒəns/| n. |趋同，汇聚，融合|The process or state of converging or coming together.|例句：The convergence of the two rivers creates a fertile valley. 两条河流的汇合创造了一个肥沃的山谷。

例句：The convergence of media technologies has blurred the lines between traditional platforms. 媒体技术的融合模糊了传统平台之间的界限。|近义词：confluence, junction, meeting, union, merging,|反义词:divergence, separation,|词性:noun
