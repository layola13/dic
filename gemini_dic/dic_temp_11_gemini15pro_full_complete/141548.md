opposable:/əˈpəʊzəbl/ (US) /əˈpɑːzəbl/ (UK)| adj.|可相对的，对生的|Able to be placed opposite something else, especially a thumb that can be moved opposite the fingers of the same hand.|Humans have opposable thumbs, which allows them to grasp objects. 人类有可相对的拇指，这使他们能够抓握物体。|synonyms: prehensile, grasping|antonyms: non-opposable|adjective
