cordelle:/kɔːrˈdɛl/ | n. |纤绳，拉纤用的绳子|A rope, usually made of hemp, used for towing a boat or barge, especially on a river.|Example: The cordelle strained as the men hauled the barge upstream.  纤绳随着人们逆流拉动驳船而绷紧。

Example:  The cordelle snapped under the pressure of the strong current.  由于强劲水流的压力，纤绳断了。|近义词：towrope, towline, hawser|反义词: N/A|名词
