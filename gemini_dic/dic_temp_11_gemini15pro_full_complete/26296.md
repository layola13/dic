butyrate:/ˈbjuːtɪreɪt/ | n. | 丁酸盐 | A salt or ester of butyric acid.  Butyric acid is a four-carbon fatty acid. Butyrate is produced through the fermentation of dietary fiber by gut bacteria. | Sodium butyrate is often used as a supplement. 丁酸钠经常被用作补充剂。|  Butyrate is an important energy source for the cells lining the colon. 丁酸盐是结肠内壁细胞的重要能量来源。 |  synonyms: butanoate | antonyms: N/A | noun

butyric acid: /bjuːˈtɪrɪk ˈæsɪd/ | n. | 丁酸 | A short-chain fatty acid with a rancid smell found especially in butter. | Butyric acid gives vomit its characteristic odor. 丁酸赋予呕吐物其特有的气味。 | Butyric acid is produced by bacteria in the gut. 丁酸是由肠道内的细菌产生的。| synonyms: butanoic acid | antonyms: N/A | noun
