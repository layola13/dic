limewater:/ˈlaɪmˌwɔːtər/| n. |石灰水|A saturated solution of calcium hydroxide in water, having an alkaline reaction and used as an antacid and astringent.|The chemist mixed some limewater with the acidic solution.  化学家将一些石灰水与酸性溶液混合。|Limewater turns milky in the presence of carbon dioxide.  石灰水在二氧化碳存在下会变成乳白色。|近义词:calcium hydroxide solution|反义词: (No direct antonym for a chemical compound)|noun
