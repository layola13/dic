bawsin:/ˈbɔːsɪn/| adj.| 很棒的，极好的|A slang term meaning "awesome," "excellent," or "amazing," often used to describe something impressive or enjoyable.  Derived from "boss," meaning "excellent" in African American Vernacular English (AAVE).|That concert was bawsin! 那场音乐会真是太棒了！|近义词：awesome, fantastic, excellent, amazing, superb|反义词：terrible, awful, dreadful|adjective
