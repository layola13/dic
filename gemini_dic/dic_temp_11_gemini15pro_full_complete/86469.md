hegira:/hɪˈdʒaɪrə/ /ˈhedʒərə/
n.
逃亡，迁徙;尤指穆罕默德从麦加到麦地那的逃亡
an exodus or migration; especially the flight of Muhammad from Mecca to Medina in AD 622.
The prophet's hegira marked the beginning of the Muslim calendar.  先知的迁徙标志着穆斯林历法的开始。
近义词:flight, exodus, migration, escape
反义词:arrival, return, immigration
