babus:/ˈbɑːbuːs/ | n. | 巴布,老爷,先生 (主要用于印度)| A term of respect, often used in India, for a government official, clerk, or educated man.  Derived from Hindi/Urdu, ultimately from Persian. |  例句：The babus were slow to process the paperwork.  巴布们处理文件很慢。| 近义词：official, clerk, sir, mister | 反义词: (No direct antonym; perhaps "commoner" in a social context) | 词性: noun
