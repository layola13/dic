convexo:/kɔnˈvɛksoʊ/ (US), /kɒnˈvɛksoʊ/ (UK) | adj. | 凸的，凸面的 | Outwardly curved or rounded; the opposite of concave. | The lens of the eye is convexo.  眼睛的晶状体是凸面的。|  The mirror was convexo, distorting the reflection. 这面镜子是凸面的，使反射的影像变形。 |  近义词: bulging, protuberant, rounded | 反义词: concave, hollow | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin convexus, meaning "vaulted, arched, curved outward."
