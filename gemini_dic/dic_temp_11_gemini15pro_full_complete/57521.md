dragoonage:/drəˈɡuːnɪdʒ/
n.
强制征兵；强迫；迫害
The act of dragooning or the condition of being dragooned.  Coercion into military service or other forms of forced labor.  Oppressive or persecutory measures.
The government resorted to dragoonage to fill the ranks of its depleted army. 政府采取强制征兵来补充其兵力不足的军队。
The villagers lived in fear of dragoonage, knowing they could be forced into labor at any time. 村民们生活在对强制劳动的恐惧之中，知道他们随时可能被迫劳动。
synonyms: coercion, compulsion, impressment, forced enlistment, persecution, oppression
antonyms:  voluntarism, freedom, liberty
noun
