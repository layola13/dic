over-laboring:/ˌoʊvərˈleɪbərɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度劳累的，过于费力的 | working or studying too hard; putting too much effort into something |  He collapsed from over-laboring in the hot sun. 他因在烈日下过度劳累而晕倒了。|  She was criticized for over-laboring the point in her presentation.  她在演讲中因过度强调这一点而受到批评。 |  overworking, straining, toiling | relaxing, resting | adjective

over-labor:/ˌoʊvərˈleɪbər/ | v. | 过度劳累，过于费力 | to work or study too hard; to put too much effort into something | Don't over-labor the point; it's already clear. 不要在这个问题上过度费力；它已经很清楚了。 | He over-labored on the project and made himself sick. 他在这个项目上过度劳累，把自己弄病了。 | overwork, strain, toil | relax, rest | verb


词源: "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "labor" (from Old French "labour" meaning "work").
