erythric:/ɪˈrɪθrɪk/
adjective
赤藓糖醇的;红色的，赤色的
Of or relating to erythrose.  Pertaining to or containing salts of erythric acid.  Reddish; rosy.
Erythric acid is found in certain lichens. 赤藓酸存在于某些地衣中。
The erythric stage of sunburn is characterized by redness and pain. 晒伤的红斑期以发红和疼痛为特征。
近义词:red, reddish, rosy, rubicund
反义词:pale, pallid, wan
词性:adjective

词源: From French érythrique, from Ancient Greek ἐρυθρός (eruthrós, “red”).
