chauffer: /ˈʃoʊfər/ (US) /ˈʃəʊfə(r)/ (UK)| n. |司机；私人雇佣的汽车司机|A person employed to drive a private or hired car.|例句：The chauffeur opened the car door for her. 司机为她打开了车门。|例句：He works as a chauffeur for a wealthy family. 他在一个富裕家庭当司机。|近义词：driver, operator|反义词:passenger|词性:noun

chauffer: /ˈʃoʊfər/ (US) /ˈʃəʊfə(r)/ (UK)| v. |开车接送；当司机|To drive someone in a car, typically as a chauffeur.|例句：He chauffeured his boss to the airport. 他开车送他的老板去机场。|例句：She was chauffeured to the party in a limousine.  她坐着豪华轿车被送到派对现场。|近义词：drive, transport|反义词:ride|词性:verb
