harmaline:/ˈhɑːrməliːn/ | n. |骆驼蓬碱；哈尔明 | An indole alkaloid found in the seeds of *Peganum harmala* (Syrian rue) and other plants, used as a stimulant and hallucinogen. | Harmaline is a potent MAO inhibitor. 骆驼蓬碱是一种强效的单胺氧化酶抑制剂。

Harmaline can induce visual hallucinations. 哈尔明可以引起视觉幻觉。| 近义词: harmine | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

词源: From *harmala* + *-ine* (chemical suffix indicating an alkaloid).  The word *harmala* comes from the Arabic name for the plant, *harmal*.
