overlay:/ˈoʊvərleɪ/ (US) /ˈəʊvəleɪ/ (UK)| n. vt.|覆盖; 覆盖物; 叠加; 叠加层|A layer or covering of something. |The image had an overlay of swirling colors. 图像上覆盖着一层旋转的颜色。|近义词:covering, layer, film, coat, veneer|反义词:base, underlay|词性:名词, 动词

overlay:/ˈoʊvərleɪ/ (US) /ˈəʊvəleɪ/ (UK)| vt.|覆盖; 叠加|To cover something with a layer of something else. |She overlaid the wood with a coat of varnish. 她给木头涂了一层清漆。|近义词:cover, coat, layer, superimpose|反义词:expose, uncover|词性:动词
