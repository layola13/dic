frigatoon:/frɪɡəˈtuːn/
noun
快速帆船；轻型快速帆船
A fast-sailing ship; a light, fast vessel.
The pirate's frigatoon sliced through the waves. 海盗的快速帆船破浪前行。
synonyms:clipper, schooner, brigantine
antonyms:galleon, barge
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *frigate*, ultimately from Italian *fregata*.
