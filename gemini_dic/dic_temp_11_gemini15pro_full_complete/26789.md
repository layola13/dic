cafeteria:/ˌkæfəˈtɪəriə/ (US), /ˌkæfɪˈtɪəriə/ (UK)| n. |自助餐厅，食堂|A restaurant or dining room in a school, workplace, or other institution, where people serve themselves from a counter and pay before eating.|例句：The school cafeteria serves lunch and breakfast. 学校食堂供应午餐和早餐。

例句：The company cafeteria is always crowded at lunchtime. 公司食堂在午餐时间总是很拥挤。|近义词：canteen, dining hall, lunchroom|反义词: restaurant (A restaurant generally offers table service and a wider menu than a cafeteria.)|词性:noun
