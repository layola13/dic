comportable:/kəmˈpɔːrtəbl/ | adj. | 舒适的，合意的 | Providing or experiencing physical ease and relaxation; pleasant.  | The new sofa is very comfortable. 新沙发很舒服。|  The temperature in the room was comfortable. 房间里的温度很舒适。 | 近义词: agreeable, pleasant, cozy, snug | 反义词: uncomfortable, unpleasant, disagreeable | 词性: adjective
词源: From Old French confortable, from Late Latin confortabilis "strengthening," from Latin com- "together" + fortis "strong."
