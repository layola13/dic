divorced:/dɪˈvɔːrst/| adj.|离婚的，脱离的|No longer married; having had one's marriage legally dissolved. |例句：She is divorced from her husband of ten years. 她和她结婚十年的丈夫离婚了。

例句：He's been divorced for five years. 他离婚五年了。|近义词：separated, split up|反义词：married, wedded|词性：形容词

divorced:/dɪˈvɔːrst/| v.|离婚|past participle of divorce|例句：They divorced last year after twenty years of marriage.  他们结婚二十年后于去年离婚了。

例句：He divorced his wife after he discovered her affair. 他发现妻子外遇后和她离婚了。|近义词：split up, separate|反义词：marry, wed|词性：动词
