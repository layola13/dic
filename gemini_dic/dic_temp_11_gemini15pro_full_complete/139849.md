octostichous: /ɒkˈtɒstɪkəs/
adjective
八行诗的；排列成八行的
Arranged in eight rows or ranks, typically referring to leaves or other plant parts.
Example: The octostichous arrangement of the leaves gave the plant a unique appearance.  叶子八列的排列方式赋予了这种植物独特的外观。

Example:  The botanist noted the octostichous phyllotaxy of the newly discovered species.  植物学家注意到新发现的物种的叶序是八列的。
Synonym: eight-ranked
Antonym: (No direct antonym, but could use terms describing other arrangements like "distichous" (two-ranked) or "spiral")
词性: adjective
