abiogenous:/ˌeɪbaɪˈɒdʒɪnəs/
adjective
非生物成因的，非生物起源的
produced by non-living organisms or processes; not of biological origin.
例句：Abiogenous petroleum origin is a theory that petroleum is formed from inorganic matter. 非生物成因石油起源理论认为石油是由无机物形成的。

例句：Scientists are exploring the possibility of abiogenous methane on Mars. 科学家们正在探索火星上存在非生物成因甲烷的可能性。


近义词：inorganic, non-biological
反义词：biogenous, biological
词性: adjective
