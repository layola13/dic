dire:/ˈdaɪər/ (US), /ˈdaɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|可怕的，悲惨的，极端的|Causing or involving great fear or suffering; dreadful; extremely serious or urgent.|例句：The country is in dire need of food. 这个国家急需食物。|例句：They were in dire straits after the business failed. 生意失败后，他们陷入了困境。|近义词：terrible, awful, dreadful, catastrophic, desperate|反义词：good, excellent, fortunate, favorable|词性:形容词

词源：From Latin *dīrus* meaning "fearful, ill-omened."
