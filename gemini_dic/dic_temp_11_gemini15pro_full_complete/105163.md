kettler:/ˈkɛtlər/| n.|凯特勒（公司名）; 水壶|A German manufacturer of sporting goods, toys, and leisure products, known particularly for its exercise bikes.  Also, a less common spelling of "kettle" (a metal pot for boiling water).| I bought a Kettler exercise bike. 我买了一辆凯特勒健身自行车。|Synonym: (for the company) manufacturer, brand, company; (for the kettle meaning) kettle, pot, boiler|Antonym: (for the company) consumer, customer; (for the kettle meaning)  None (for an object)|noun
