ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- + questioning.  "Counter-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "in opposition to." "Questioning" is the noun form of the verb "question," which comes from the Old French word "questioner" and ultimately from the Latin "quaestio," meaning "a seeking, inquiry."
