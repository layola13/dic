koppie:/ˈkɒpi/ (UK), /ˈkɑːpi/ (US)|n.|小山丘，小圆山; (尤指南非的)岩石山丘|A koppie is a small, usually rounded hill, especially a rocky one in South Africa. |We climbed the koppie to get a better view of the valley. 我们爬上小山丘，以便更好地欣赏山谷的景色。|The koppie offered a strategic vantage point during the battle.  这座小圆山在战斗中提供了一个战略制高点。|Synonyms: hill, knoll, mound|Antonyms: valley, plain|noun
