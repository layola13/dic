november:/noʊˈvɛmbər/ (US) /nəʊˈvɛmbə(r)/ (UK)| n. |十一月|The eleventh month of the year, formerly the ninth.|
例句：My birthday is in November. 我的生日在十一月。

例句：November is often a cold and windy month. 十一月通常是寒冷多风的月份。

近义词：None (as it's a specific month)|反义词:None (as it's a specific month)|词性:noun

词源:From Old French *novembre*, from Latin November (mēnsis) “(month) of November”, from novem “nine”. Originally the ninth month in the Roman calendar.
