interlink:/ˌɪntərˈlɪŋk/ | v. n. |互相连接，互连；连环|To connect different parts of something together, or to be connected in this way.  The various systems are closely interlinked. |The global economy is increasingly interlinked.| 近义词: interconnect, link, connect, join | 反义词: separate, disconnect, isolate |词性: 动词，名词

interlink:/ˌɪntərˈlɪŋk/ | n. |互相连接，互连；连环|A connection or link between different parts of something. The interlink between the two companies is strong. |There are complex interlinks between the different parts of the ecosystem.| 近义词: connection, link, relationship | 反义词: separation, division |词性: 名词
