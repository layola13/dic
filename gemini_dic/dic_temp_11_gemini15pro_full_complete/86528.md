hectometer:/ˈhɛktəˌmiːtər/ (US), /ˈhɛktəʊˌmiːtər/ (UK)| n. |百米，公引|A unit of length in the metric system equal to 100 meters.|The race was a hectometer sprint. 这场比赛是一个百米冲刺。|The farm measured several hectometers in width.  这个农场有几百米宽。|Synonyms: hectometre (British spelling), 100 meters|Antonyms: millimeter, centimeter|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *hectomètre*, from *hecto-* (“hundred”) + *-mètre* (“meter”).  *Hecto-* comes from Greek *ἑκατόν* (hekatón, “hundred”). *-mètre* comes from Greek *μέτρον* (métron, “measure”).
