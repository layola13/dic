hindbrain:/ˈhaɪndbreɪn/| n. |后脑|The hindbrain, also called the rhombencephalon, is the lower part of the brainstem, comprising the cerebellum, pons, and medulla oblongata. It controls vital functions such as breathing and heart rate.|The hindbrain plays a crucial role in regulating basic life functions. 后脑在调节基本生命功能中起着至关重要的作用。

The doctor explained that damage to the hindbrain can be life-threatening. 医生解释说，后脑损伤可能危及生命。|近义词:rhombencephalon|反义词: (No direct antonym exists as it's an anatomical term) |词性:noun
