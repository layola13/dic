interrogatory:/ˌɪnˈtɛrəɡətɔːri/| adj. n.|提问的，疑问的；质问书|Of or relating to questioning; a question, especially one addressed to a witness or party in a legal proceeding.|The lawyer submitted a list of interrogatories to the defendant. 律师向被告提交了一系列质问书。|近义词：inquiring, questioning, query|反义词:declarative, affirmative|词性:adjective, noun
interrogatory:/ɪnˈtɛrəɡətərɪ/| n.|质问, 疑问句|A formal question, often in writing, as part of a legal process.|The interrogatories were designed to elicit key information from the witness. 这些质问旨在从证人那里获取关键信息。|近义词：question, inquiry, query|反义词:answer, reply|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *interrogātōrius*, from Latin interrogō (“I ask, question”), from inter (“between”) + rogō (“I ask”).
