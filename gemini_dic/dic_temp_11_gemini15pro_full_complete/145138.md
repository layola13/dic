overartificial:/ˌoʊvərˌɑːrtɪˈfɪʃəl/
adjective
过于人工化的；矫揉造作的
Excessively artificial; lacking naturalness or spontaneity.
例句：Her acting was overartificial and unconvincing. 她的演技过于做作，很不自然。

例句：The dialogue in the movie felt overartificial and stilted.  电影里的对话感觉过于矫揉造作，很不流畅。

近义词：contrived, affected, artificial, unnatural, stilted, forced
反义词：natural, genuine, authentic, spontaneous, unforced

词性: adjective
