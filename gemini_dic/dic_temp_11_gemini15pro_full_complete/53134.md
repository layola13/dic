diploma:/dɪˈploʊmə/ (US), /dɪˈpləʊmə/ (UK)| n. |文凭，毕业证书|A document conferring some honor, privilege, or power, especially a document certifying the successful completion of a course of study.|She finally received her high school diploma. 她终于拿到了高中文凭。|He earned a diploma in business administration. 他获得了工商管理文凭。|近义词：certificate, degree|反义词: N/A|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek δίπλωμα (díplōma, “folded paper, letter”), from διπλόω (diplóō, “I double, fold”), from δι- (di-, “two-”) + -πλόος (-plóos, “-fold”). Doubled or folded because it was originally a document written on two tablets of wax which were folded together.
