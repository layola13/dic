honchoed:/ˈhɑːntʃoʊd/ | v. | 担任头儿，指挥，负责 | acted as the honcho; took charge; supervised |  She honchoed the marketing campaign and it was a huge success. 她负责了这次营销活动，结果非常成功。

The team honchoed the project from start to finish.  团队从头到尾负责了这个项目。

| 近义词：managed, directed, supervised, led, headed, spearheaded, oversaw | 反义词：followed, obeyed, assisted | 词性: 动词


词源: From Japanese 班長 (hanchō, “squad leader”). Borrowed into American English during the Korean War.
