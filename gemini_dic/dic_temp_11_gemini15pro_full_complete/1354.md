accumulable: /əˈkjuːmjʊləbl/ | adj. | 可累积的，可积累的 | Capable of being accumulated; that can be collected or gathered together. |  Savings are accumulable over time. 随着时间的推移，积蓄是可以累积的。| The points are accumulable and can be redeemed for prizes. 积分是可以累积的，可以兑换奖品。 |近义词: collectible, gatherable, amassable | 反义词: non-cumulative, dissipating | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Late Latin *accumulabilis, from Latin accumulare "to heap up," from ad- "to" + cumulus "heap."
