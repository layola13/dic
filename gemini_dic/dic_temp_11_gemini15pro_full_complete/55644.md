ditheistical:/ˌdaɪθiːˈɪstɪk(ə)l/
adjective
二神论的
Believing in two gods, especially two equal and opposed gods.
The philosopher expounded a ditheistical worldview, arguing for the existence of two co-equal deities.  这位哲学家阐述了一种二神论的世界观，论证了两个平等神灵的存在。
Synonyms: dualistic
Antonyms: monotheistic, polytheistic
词性: adjective
