hyperaccurately:/ˌhaɪpərˈækjərətli/ | adv. | 超精确地，极其准确地 |  With extreme accuracy; exceeding normal accuracy.  It combines "hyper-" (meaning "over, excessive") with "accurately". | The robot arm placed the components hyperaccurately, with sub-millimeter precision.  机器人手臂超精确地放置了组件，精度达到亚毫米级。|  precisely, exactly, meticulously | loosely, approximately, roughly | adverb

Etymology->
From hyper- + accurately.  "Hyper-" comes from Greek "hyper" (over, beyond). "Accurately" comes from Latin "accuratus" (prepared with care), past participle of "accurare" (to take care of), from "ad-" (to) + "curare" (to take care).
