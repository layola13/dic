cocatalysts:/koʊˈkætəlɪsts/| n.|助催化剂|A substance that enhances the activity of a catalyst.|Titanium dioxide is often used as a cocatalyst in photocatalytic reactions. 二氧化钛常在光催化反应中用作助催化剂。|The cocatalyst improved the efficiency of the reaction.  助催化剂提高了反应效率。|Synonyms: promoter, accelerator|Antonyms: inhibitor, poisoner|Noun
