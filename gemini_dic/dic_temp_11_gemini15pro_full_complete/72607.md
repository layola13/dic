fly-tipping:/ˈflaɪˌtɪpɪŋ/
noun
非法倾倒垃圾
The illegal deposit of waste on land.
The council has launched a crackdown on fly-tipping. 市议会已经开始严厉打击非法倾倒垃圾的行为。
