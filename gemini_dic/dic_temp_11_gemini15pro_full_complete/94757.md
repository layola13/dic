improvise: /ˈɪmprəvaɪz/ (UK) /ˈɪmprəvɑɪz/ (US) | v. | 即兴创作，临时凑合 | To create or perform spontaneously or without preparation; to make something from whatever is available. |  The musicians improvised a bluesy tune.  音乐家们即兴演奏了一首布鲁斯曲调。

The chef had to improvise a meal with the limited ingredients available.  厨师不得不利用有限的食材即兴做一顿饭。| 近义词：extemporize, ad-lib, wing it, make do | 反义词：plan, prepare, rehearse | 词性: 动词
