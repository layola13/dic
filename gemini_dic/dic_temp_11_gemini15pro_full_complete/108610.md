leadening:/ˈlɛdənɪŋ/| adj.|沉重的，压抑的，令人沮丧的|Making something heavy, dull, or depressing.  This can refer to both physical weight and a more figurative sense of heaviness, like a leadening atmosphere.|The leadening humidity made it hard to breathe. 湿热难耐，让人难以呼吸。|The news of her death cast a leadening gloom over the entire family. 她去世的消息给整个家庭蒙上了一层阴影。|近义词：oppressive, burdensome, heavy, depressing, stultifying|反义词：inspiring, uplifting, invigorating, stimulating|词性:形容词

词源: From "leaden" + "-ing" suffix. "Leaden" comes from Old English "lēaden," meaning "made of lead." The "-ing" suffix transforms the adjective "leaden" into a present participle, functioning as an adjective.
