daydreaming: /ˈdeɪdriːmɪŋ/ | n. | 白日梦 | The act of indulging in fantasies or reveries while awake. |  She was caught daydreaming in class. 她上课时被抓到做白日梦了。

He often spends his afternoons daydreaming about traveling the world. 他经常在下午做白日梦，梦想着环游世界。 | 近义词：fantasy, reverie, musing | 反义词：concentration, focus | 词性: noun

daydream: /ˈdeɪdriːm/ | v. | 做白日梦 | To have pleasant but unrealistic thoughts. | I often daydream about winning the lottery. 我经常幻想中彩票。

She daydreamed of a life on a tropical island. 她幻想着在热带岛屿上的生活。 | 近义词：fantasize, imagine | 反义词：focus, concentrate | 词性: verb

Etymology: From "day" + "dream".  The word "dream" comes from Old English "drēam,"  meaning "joy, mirth, noisy merriment," later "a vision in sleep."
