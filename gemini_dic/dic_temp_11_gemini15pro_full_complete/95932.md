incrustment:/ɪnˈkrʌstmənt/ |n.|嵌入; 结壳; 包壳;|An incrustment of salt covered the rocks. 盐壳覆盖了岩石。|The ancient vase was covered with an incrustment of jewels. 这只古代花瓶上镶嵌着珠宝。|近义词:covering, coating, layer, crust, encrustation|反义词:exfoliation, stripping|名词

incrust:/ɪnˈkrʌst/ |vt.|使嵌入; 使结壳; 用…包壳;|The artist incrusted the box with semiprecious stones. 艺术家在盒子上镶嵌了半宝石。|Barnacles incrusted the ship's hull. 藤壶附着在船体上。|近义词:cover, coat, encrust|反义词:strip, uncover|动词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *incrustamentum*, from Latin incrustare "to cover with a crust," from in- "in, on" + crusta "crust, rind."
