luxembourgian:/ˌlʌksəmˈbɜːrɡiən/
adjective|卢森堡的；卢森堡人的；卢森堡语的|Relating to Luxembourg, its people, or their language.|The Luxembourgian economy is largely dependent on the financial sector.|近义词:Luxembourgish|反义词:|词性:adjective
luxembourgian:/ˌlʌksəmˈbɜːrɡiən/
noun|卢森堡人；卢森堡语|A person from Luxembourg or the Luxembourgish language.|Many Luxembourgians are multilingual.|近义词:Luxembourger|反义词:|词性:noun

词源: From Luxembourg + -ian.  Luxembourg itself comes from  Lucilinburhuc, meaning "little fortress".
