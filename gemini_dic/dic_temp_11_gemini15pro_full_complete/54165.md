disenthrall:/ˌdɪsɪnˈθrɔːl/ |v. |解放；使摆脱束缚|To free from enslavement, bondage, or oppression; liberate. |例句：The movement sought to disenthrall women from traditional social roles.  这场运动试图将妇女从传统的社会角色中解放出来。

例句：Education can disenthrall the mind from prejudice and superstition. 教育可以使心灵摆脱偏见和迷信。|近义词：liberate, free, release, emancipate|反义词：enslave, imprison, confine, restrain|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- + enthrall.  "Dis-" is a prefix meaning "apart," "asunder," "away," expressing negation, reversal, or deprivation. "Enthrall" comes from the Old French word "enthraler," meaning "to enslave."
