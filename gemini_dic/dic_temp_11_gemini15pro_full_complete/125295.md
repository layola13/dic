multifunction:/ˌmʌltiˈfʌŋkʃən/ | adj. | 多功能的 | Having or performing several or many functions. | This multifunction printer can copy, scan, and fax. 这台多功能打印机可以复印、扫描和传真。 | My new phone is multifunction - it's a camera, GPS, and music player all in one. 我的新手机是多功能的 - 它集相机、GPS 和音乐播放器于一身。 | 近义词:versatile, all-purpose, multipurpose | 反义词: single-function, specialized | 词性:形容词

Etymology:
From multi- (meaning "many") + function.  "Multi-" comes from Latin "multus" meaning "much, many." "Function" comes from Latin "functio(n-)"  meaning "performance, execution."
