deployable:/dɪˈplɔɪəbl/| adj.|可部署的，可展开的|Able to be put into use or action, typically of military equipment or software.|The new software is deployable on multiple platforms.  这款新软件可以在多个平台上部署。|例句：The army has several deployable units ready for action.  军队有几个可随时部署的作战单位。

例句：The software is easily deployable and requires minimal configuration.  这个软件很容易部署，只需要最少的配置。|近义词：usable, available, operational|反义词：undeployable, unusable|词性:adjective

词源: From deploy + -able. Deploy comes from Old French despleier, meaning "to unfold, unfurl."
