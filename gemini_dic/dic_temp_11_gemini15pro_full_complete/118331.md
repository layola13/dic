mergences:/ˈmɜːrdʒənz/
noun
合并，融合
The process of combining or merging multiple entities into one.
The company announced several mergences in the past year.  该公司在过去一年宣布了几次合并。
Mergences and acquisitions are common in the business world.  并购在商业世界中很常见。
synonyms: combinations, unions, amalgamations
antonyms: divisions, separations, splits
