amalgamate:/əˈmælɡəmeɪt/| v. n.|合并，混合|to combine or unite multiple entities into a single, larger whole, or to become combined or united in this way. |The two companies amalgamated to form a larger corporation. 这两家公司合并成了一家更大的公司。|The different groups amalgamated into one large crowd. 不同的小组合并成了一个大群体。|Synonyms: merge, combine, unite, consolidate, blend|Antonyms: separate, divide, split, disintegrate|verb

amalgamate:/əˈmælɡəmeɪt/| n.|混合物，汞合金|a mixture or blend of different elements, often referring to a metal alloy containing mercury.|The dentist used an amalgamate filling for the cavity. 牙医用汞合金填充了蛀牙。|The final product was an amalgamate of several different metals. 最终产品是几种不同金属的混合物。|Synonyms: mixture, blend, combination, compound|Antonyms: element, component, ingredient|noun
