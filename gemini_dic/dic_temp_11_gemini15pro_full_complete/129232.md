noctiluca:/ˌnɒktɪˈl(j)uːkə/ | n. | 夜光虫 | A free-swimming bioluminescent marine dinoflagellate that is responsible for the phenomenon known as "sea sparkle". | Example 1: The shimmering blue light in the bay at night was caused by a bloom of noctiluca. 夜晚海湾里闪烁的蓝光是由夜光虫大量繁殖引起的。

Example 2:  Scientists are studying the bioluminescence of noctiluca to understand its ecological role. 科学家们正在研究夜光虫的生物发光，以了解其生态作用。| 近义词: sea sparkle, dinoflagellate | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *nox, noct- (“night”) + *lūcēre (“to shine”).
