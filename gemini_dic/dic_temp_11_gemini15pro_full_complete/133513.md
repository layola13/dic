nonfestively:/nɒnˈfɛstɪvli/ | adv. | 非节日的，不喜庆的 |  In a manner that is not festive or celebratory; lacking the spirit or characteristics of a festival or holiday. | She decorated her apartment nonfestively, choosing muted colors and minimalist decor. 她以非节日的风格装饰了她的公寓，选择了柔和的色彩和极简主义的装饰。| The meeting was conducted nonfestively, with everyone focused on the serious business at hand. 会议以非节日的氛围进行，每个人都专注于手头的严肃事务。 | 近义词: somberly, seriously, plainly, dully | 反义词: festively, joyfully, merrily, cheerfully | 词性: 副词
词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + festive + -ly (adverbial suffix)
