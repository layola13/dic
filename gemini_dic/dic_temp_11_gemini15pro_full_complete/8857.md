antifemininely:/ˌæntiˈfɛmɪnɪnli/
| adv. | 不像女性地；以非女性的方式 | In a way that is not considered typical or appropriate for a woman; unwomanly. | She dressed antifemininely in loose trousers and a shapeless sweater. 她穿着不像女性的宽松裤子和一件不成形的毛衣。 | She behaved rather antifemininely, refusing to wear makeup or dresses. 她表现得相当不像女性，拒绝化妆或穿裙子。 | 近义词：unwomanly, mannishly | 反义词：femininely, womanly | 词性: 副词
