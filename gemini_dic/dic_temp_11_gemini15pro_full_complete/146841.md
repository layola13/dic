overindebtednesses:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈdɛtɪdnɪsɪz/ | n. | 过度负债（复数） | The state of owing more money than one can realistically repay.  It usually refers to a situation where an individual or company has accumulated so much debt that they are unable to meet their repayment obligations. | Many developing countries are struggling with overindebtednesses. 许多发展中国家正在努力应对过度负债问题。|  The global financial crisis exacerbated the overindebtednesses of several European nations. 全球金融危机加剧了几个欧洲国家的过度负债。 |  overleverage, excessive debt | solvency, financial stability | noun
