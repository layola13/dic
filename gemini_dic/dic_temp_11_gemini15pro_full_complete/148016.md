overripeness:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪpnəs/ | n. | 过熟，过度成熟 |The overripeness of the fruit made it mushy. 水果过熟使它变得糊状。| 近义词：overmaturity | 反义词: unripeness, immaturity | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From "overripe" + "-ness". "Overripe" is from "over-" (excessively) + "ripe" (mature).  "Ripe" comes from Old English "rīpe," of Germanic origin.
