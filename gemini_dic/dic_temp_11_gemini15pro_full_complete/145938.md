overdiscouraged:/ˌoʊvər dɪsˈkɜːrɪdʒd/
adjective
过度沮丧的，极其灰心丧气的
Excessively discouraged; very disheartened.
She felt overdiscouraged after failing the exam for the third time. 她第三次考试不及格后感到非常沮丧。
He was overdiscouraged by the constant rejections from publishers.  他不断被出版商拒绝，感到极其灰心。
Synonym: disheartened, demoralized, dejected, despondent
Antonym: encouraged, optimistic, hopeful, motivated
词性: adjective
