keepabilities:/ˌkiːpəˈbɪlətiz/| n.|可保存性，保持能力|The keepabilities of this product are excellent.  This refers to its ability to remain fresh and usable for an extended period.  这个产品的可保存性非常好。指的是它能长时间保持新鲜和可用。|例句：The keepabilities of these apples are surprisingly good, they've stayed fresh for weeks. 这些苹果的可保存性出奇地好，它们已经保持新鲜好几个星期了。

例句：Proper storage greatly enhances the keepabilities of many fruits and vegetables.  适当的储存可以大大提高许多水果和蔬菜的保存能力。|近义词：shelf life, storage life, durability, longevity|反义词:perishability, fragility|词性:noun
