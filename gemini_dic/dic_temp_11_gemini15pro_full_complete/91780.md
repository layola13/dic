hyperesthetes:/ˌhaɪpərˈɛsθiːts/| n. |感觉过敏者；感觉过度敏锐的人|A person with abnormally acute sensitivity to sensory stimuli, especially touch, pain, or sound.  They experience sensations more intensely than most people.|The hyperesthetes in the study experienced discomfort from clothing most people found comfortable.| 近义词: hypersensitive person, oversensitive person |反义词: hyposensitive person|noun
