oversauced:/ˌoʊvərˈsɔːst/
adjective
浇汁过多的；酱汁太多的
Covered with too much sauce.
The pasta was oversauced, making it too rich and heavy.  这意大利面酱汁太多了，太油腻厚重了。
The steak was perfectly cooked, but unfortunately oversauced. 牛排做得很好，可惜酱汁太多了。
近义词: drenched, smothered
反义词: undersauced, bland
词性: adjective
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "sauced" (past participle of "sauce," meaning "to add sauce to")
