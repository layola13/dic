anchor:/ˈæŋkər/ (US) /ˈæŋkə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|锚; 依靠; 主持; 抛锚; 固定|A heavy object attached to a ship or boat by a cable or chain and used to moor the vessel to the seabed; a source of stability or security; a person who presents a news program; to secure firmly; to be the main member of a team or group.|The ship dropped anchor in the bay. 这艘船在海湾抛锚了。|近义词：mooring, fastener, secure, host, mainstay|反义词：detach, loosen, drift|noun, verb
