orthography:/ɔːˈθɒɡrəfi/ /ɔːrˈθɑːɡrəfi/| n. |正字法；拼写法|The study of the letters and spelling of a language.  The way in which the words of a language are spelled.|例句：English orthography can be confusing. 英语的拼写规则有时会令人困惑。|近义词：spelling, writing|反义词:misspelling|词性:noun

orthographic:/ˌɔːθəˈɡræfɪk/ /ˌɔːrθəˈɡræfɪk/| adj. |正字法的；拼写的|Relating to or conforming to the rules of orthography.|例句：An orthographic error can change the meaning of a word. 拼写错误会改变单词的意思。|近义词：spelling|反义词: misspelling|词性:adjective
