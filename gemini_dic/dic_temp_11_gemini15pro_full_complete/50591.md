derange:/dɪˈreɪndʒ/ | vt. | 使精神错乱；扰乱；打乱 | to disturb the operation or arrangement of; throw into disorder |  The loud noise deranged his thoughts.  喧闹的声音扰乱了他的思绪。 | synonyms:  disorder, disrupt, unsettle, confuse, muddle | antonyms: organize, order, arrange, calm, soothe | verb

derangement:/dɪˈreɪndʒmənt/ | n. |  精神错乱；扰乱；混乱 | the act of deranging or the state of being deranged; disorder | The derangement of his affairs caused him much stress. 他事务的混乱给他带来了很大的压力。 | synonyms: disorder, disruption, confusion, chaos, muddle | antonyms: order, organization, arrangement, harmony, stability | noun
