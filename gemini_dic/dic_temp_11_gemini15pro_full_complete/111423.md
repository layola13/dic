lizard:/ˈlɪzərd/| n. |蜥蜴|A reptile that typically has a long body and tail, four legs, movable eyelids, and a rough, scaly, or spiny skin.|The lizard darted across the rock. 蜥蜴飞快地穿过岩石。|近义词：reptile, gecko, skink|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider mammal, bird as broader contrasting categories) |noun
