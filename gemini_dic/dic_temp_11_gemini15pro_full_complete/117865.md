melodics:/məˈlɑːdɪks/ | n. |旋律学; 旋律部分; 旋律; |The study of melodies, or the melodic aspects of music.  It can also refer to the melodic parts of a piece of music.|The guitarist's melodies were haunting and beautiful. 吉他手的旋律令人难忘而优美。|Synonyms: tunes, airs, melodies, themes|Antonyms: harmonies, rhythms | noun
