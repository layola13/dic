biblioteca:/ˌbiːbli.oʊˈteɪ.kə/ /ˌbɪb.li.əˈteɪ.kə/
n.|图书馆；藏书；图书室|A building or room containing collections of books, periodicals, and sometimes films and recorded music for people to read, borrow, or refer to.|She spent countless hours in the biblioteca, researching her thesis. 她花了无数个小时在图书馆研究她的论文。|library, archive|bookstore|noun
biblioteca:/ˌbiːbli.oʊˈteɪ.kə/ /ˌbɪb.li.əˈteɪ.kə/
n.|（尤指私人的）藏书；文库|A collection of books, especially one belonging to a private individual.|His personal biblioteca contained several first editions. 他的私人藏书包含几本初版书籍。|collection, library|lack of books|noun
