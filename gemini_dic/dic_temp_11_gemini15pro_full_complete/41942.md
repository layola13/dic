cosmopolitanisms:/ˌkɑːzməˈpɑːlɪtənɪzəmz/ | n. | 世界主义，世界性，国际主义 |  The ideology or practice of emphasizing the interconnectedness of all humans and promoting a sense of global citizenship, often transcending national or local allegiances.  It refers to the multiplicity and variety of ways in which cosmopolitanism is understood and practiced. |  Her writings reflect her cosmopolitanisms, drawing on diverse cultural and philosophical traditions. 她的作品反映了她的世界主义，借鉴了不同的文化和哲学传统。

The conference explored different cosmopolitanisms, from the Kantian ideal to contemporary global justice movements.  这次会议探讨了不同的世界主义，从康德的理想主义到当代的全球正义运动。 | 近义词：globalism, internationalism, multiculturalism | 反义词：nationalism, parochialism, isolationism | 词性: 名词
