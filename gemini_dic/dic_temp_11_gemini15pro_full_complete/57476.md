draftsmanly:/ˈdræftsmənli/
adj.
绘图员般的；精于制图的；技术娴熟的
Like a draftsman; skillful in drafting.
His draftsmanly precision was evident in the detailed blueprints. 他绘图员般的精确度在详细的蓝图中显而易见。
近义词：precise, meticulous, skillful, adept
反义词：clumsy, inept, unskilled
词性: adjective
词源: From draftsman + -ly.  Draftsman from draft (n.) + -sman. Ultimately from Old Norse *draga "to draw, pull, drag"
