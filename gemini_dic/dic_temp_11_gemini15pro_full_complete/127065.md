natals:/ˈneɪtəlz/| n.|出生，诞生日，星盘|A person's natal chart is a map of the positions of the planets at the moment of their birth. |例句：Her natals indicated a strong personality and a passion for travel. 她的星盘显示她个性强，热爱旅行。

例句：Astrologers often use natals to make predictions about a person's life. 占星家经常使用星盘来预测一个人的生活。|近义词：birth chart, horoscope|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
