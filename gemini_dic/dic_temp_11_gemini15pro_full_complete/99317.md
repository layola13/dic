intercrusted:/ˌɪntərˈkrʌstɪd/
adjective
镶嵌的，覆盖硬壳的
Covered with a hard crust; having a crust formed or deposited between layers.
The intercrusted jewels shimmered in the dim light. 镶嵌的珠宝在昏暗的光线下闪烁。
The geologist examined the intercrusted layers of rock. 地质学家检查了覆盖硬壳的岩层。
近义词:encrusted, inlaid
反义词:bare, uncovered
词性:adjective

词源:
From inter- + crust + -ed.  "Inter-" denotes "between" or "among," while "crust" refers to a hard outer layer.  "-ed" forms the past participle and adjective.
