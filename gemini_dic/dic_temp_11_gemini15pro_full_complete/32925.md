choosingly:/ˈtʃuːzɪŋli/
adverb
精挑细选地，慎重地
carefully and deliberately selecting only the best or most suitable
She dressed choosingly for the important interview. 她为这次重要的面试精心挑选了服装。
近义词:selectively, carefully, discriminatingly, judiciously
反义词:indiscriminately, randomly, carelessly
词性:副词


Etymology: From "choose" (Old English *cēosan) + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverb suffix).
