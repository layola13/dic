coessential:/ˌkəʊɪˈsɛnʃəl/| adj.|同质的，同本质的，共同的|Sharing the same essence or nature.  Being of the same substance or nature.|例句：The Father, Son, and Holy Spirit are coessential in Christian theology.  圣父、圣子和圣灵在基督教神学中是同质的。

例句：Love and compassion are coessential to a fulfilling life.  爱和同情对于充实的生活是同质的。|近义词：consubstantial, coequal, identical, equivalent|反义词：different, dissimilar, distinct, separate|词性: adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *coessentialis*, from *co-* (together) + *essentialis* (essential). The Latin *essentialis* comes from *essentia* (essence, being), which is derived from *esse* (to be).
