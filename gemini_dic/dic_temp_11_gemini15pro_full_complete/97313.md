informant:/ɪnˈfɔːrmənt/ | n. | 线人，告密者 | A person who gives information to another, typically to the police or a journalist. | The journalist refused to reveal the identity of his informant. 记者拒绝透露线人的身份。 |  The police received a tip-off from an informant. 警方收到线报。 | synonym: source, tipster, whistleblower | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "suspect" in some contexts) | noun
