aerophyte:/ˈɛəroʊfaɪt/| n. |气生植物；空气植物|A plant that obtains moisture and nutrients from the air and rain; it usually grows on another plant but is not parasitic, such as various orchids and bromeliads.|例句：The aerophyte clung to the branch, its roots dangling in the air.  这种气生植物附着在树枝上，根悬在空中。

例句：Orchids are a well-known example of an aerophyte.  兰花是气生植物的一个著名例子。|近义词：epiphyte|反义词: terrestrial plant|词性:noun
