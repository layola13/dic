miaows:/miˈaʊz/| n. |猫叫声|The sound a cat makes, similar to "meow" but implying multiple or repeated meows.  It's an informal and less common spelling. |The kitten miaows incessantly when it's hungry. 小猫饿的时候不停地喵喵叫。|近义词：meows, purrs, cries|反义词: (No direct antonym for a sound. Perhaps silence, quiet)|noun
