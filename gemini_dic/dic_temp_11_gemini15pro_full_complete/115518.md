maoism:/ˈmaʊɪzəm/| n. |毛泽东思想|The political ideology and practice developed by Mao Zedong, based on Marxism-Leninism but adapted to Chinese conditions. It emphasizes peasant revolution, guerrilla warfare, and the importance of continuous revolution.|例句：Maoism played a significant role in shaping modern China. 毛泽东思想对现代中国的形成起了重要作用。

例句：The Cultural Revolution was a direct result of Maoism. 文化大革命是毛泽东思想的直接结果。|近义词：Marxism-Leninism, Communism|反义词: Capitalism, Liberalism|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Mao Zedong (毛泽东), the former leader of the Chinese Communist Party, + -ism.
