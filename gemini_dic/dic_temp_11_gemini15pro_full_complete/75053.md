frightened:/ˈfraɪtnd/| adj.|害怕的，恐惧的|Feeling fear or alarm; terrified.|例句：The frightened child clung to his mother.  受惊的孩子紧紧抱住他的母亲。|例句：She was frightened by the sudden noise. 她被突如其来的噪音吓了一跳。|近义词：scared, terrified, afraid, alarmed|反义词：brave, courageous, unafraid|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *frightenen, *friȝtenen, from Old English *fryhtnian (“to frighten”), from Proto-Germanic *frihtjaną (“to frighten”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)bʰreyǵʰ- (“to tremble”). Cognate with West Frisian frygje (“to be afraid”), Dutch vrijten (“to make afraid”), German fright (“fear”).
