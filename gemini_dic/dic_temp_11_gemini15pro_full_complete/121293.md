misfiling: /ˌmɪsˈfaɪlɪŋ/
noun
错误归档
The act of filing something incorrectly, usually in the wrong place.
The misfiling of the documents caused a significant delay in the legal proceedings.  错误归档的文件导致法律程序严重延误。
misplacement, mismarking
correct filing
