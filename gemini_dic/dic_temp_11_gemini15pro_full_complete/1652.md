acknowledge:/əkˈnɒlɪdʒ/ (UK), /əkˈnɑːlɪdʒ/ (US)| vt.|承认；确认；答谢|To accept or admit the truth or existence of something.  To express gratitude or appreciation for something.|例句：He acknowledged his mistake. 他承认了自己的错误。|例句：She acknowledged the applause with a smile. 她微笑着回应掌声。|近义词：admit, recognize, accept, confirm|反义词：deny, reject, dispute|verb

acknowledge:/əkˈnɒlɪdʒ/ (UK), /əkˈnɑːlɪdʒ/ (US)| n.|承认；确认；答谢|The act of acknowledging something.|例句：His acknowledgement of the error came too late. 他对错误的承认来得太晚了。 |例句：The team received acknowledgement for their hard work.  团队因其辛勤工作而受到表彰。|近义词：admission, recognition, acceptance, confirmation|反义词：denial, rejection|noun
