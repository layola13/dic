nonsinkable:/ˌnɒnsɪŋkəbl/ /ˌnɑːnsɪŋkəbl/
adjective
不会沉没的；不可摧毁的
incapable of sinking; not able to be sunk.
The Titanic was deemed "practically unsinkable" before its maiden voyage.  泰坦尼克号在首航前被认为“几乎不会沉没”。
The company's reputation seemed nonsinkable despite the recent scandal. 尽管最近发生了丑闻，但该公司的声誉似乎坚不可摧。
synonyms: indestructible, buoyant, impervious, unsinkable
antonyms: sinkable, vulnerable, fragile
词性: adjective
