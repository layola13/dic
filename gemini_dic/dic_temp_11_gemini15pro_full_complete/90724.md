humiliant:/hjuːˈmɪliənt/| adj.|羞辱性的，丢脸的|Causing humiliation; humbling.  Something that causes a feeling of shame or loss of dignity.|His public reprimand was a humiliant experience. 他在公众面前受到斥责是一次丢脸的经历。|近义词：humiliating, shameful, degrading, mortifying|反义词：exalting, uplifting, honoring|词性:adjective
词源: From the Latin word "humiliare" meaning "to humble".  Formed by adding the suffix "-ant" to "humiliate," indicating something that causes humiliation.
