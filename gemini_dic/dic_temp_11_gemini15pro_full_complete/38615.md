concretional:/kɒnˈkriːʃənl/ /kɑːnˈkriːʃənl/
adjective
结核状的，凝固而成的
formed into a hard mass or concretion.
例句：The concretional limestone formations were fascinating to study.  这些结核状的石灰岩构造引人入胜。

例句：Concretional deposits are often found in caves.  在洞穴中经常发现凝固沉积物。
近义词：nodular, consolidated, hardened
反义词：loose, unconsolidated, dispersed
词性: adjective


concretion:/kɒnˈkriːʃən/ /kɑːnˈkriːʃən/
noun
凝固物，结石
a hard, solid mass formed by the local accumulation of matter, especially within the body or within a mass of loose material such as sediment.
例句：Kidney stones are a common type of concretion.  肾结石是一种常见的凝固物。

例句：The geologist examined the concretion found in the sedimentary rock. 地质学家检查了沉积岩中发现的凝固物。
近义词：calculus, nodule, deposit
反义词：solution, dispersion
词性: noun
