enliven:/ɪnˈlaɪvən/ | v. | 使活跃，使生动，使更有趣 |To make something more lively, interesting, or exciting. |例句：The music enlivened the party. 音乐使派对更加活跃。|例句：His jokes enlivened the otherwise dull lecture. 他的笑话让原本枯燥的讲座变得生动有趣。 |近义词：animate, invigorate, stimulate, cheer, brighten |反义词：dampen, dull, deaden |词性:动词
