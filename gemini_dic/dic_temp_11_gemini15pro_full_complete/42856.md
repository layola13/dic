courteousness:/ˈkɜːrtiəsnəs/ | n. | 礼貌；殷勤；彬彬有礼 | The staff treated all customers with courteousness.  工作人员对所有顾客都以礼相待。 |  Example:  His courteousness and charm made him a popular host. 他的礼貌和魅力使他成为受欢迎的主人。 | Synonyms: politeness, civility, respect, courtesy | Antonyms: rudeness, impoliteness, disrespect, discourtesy | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *curteisnes*, from Old French *corteisie* (modern French *courtoisie*), from *corteis* (“courteous”), from Vulgar Latin *cortis* (“court”), from Latin *cohors*, *cohort-* (“enclosed yard, retinue”).
