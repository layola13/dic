domableness:/ˈdoʊməbəlnəs/ /ˈdəʊməbəlnəs/
n.
驯服性，温顺，易驾驭
The quality of being easily tamed or domesticated.
The domableness of the horse made it easy to train. 这匹马的温顺使它很容易训练。
近义词：tameness, docility, manageability, tractability
反义词：wildness, ferocity, intractability, untamability
词性：名词

domableness: /ˈdoʊməbəlnəs/ /ˈdəʊməbəlnəs/
词源:  From "domable" + "-ness". "Domable" comes from the verb "dome" (meaning to tame or subdue), from Old French "domer" and ultimately from Latin "domare". The suffix "-ness" forms nouns indicating a state, quality, or condition.
