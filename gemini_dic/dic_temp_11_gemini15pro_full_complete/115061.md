maneater:/ˈmænˌiːtər/ | n. |食人者；危险的女人；妖妇|A person or animal that eats human flesh; a woman considered dangerously seductive or predatory. |例句：The shark was labeled a maneater after several attacks. 这条鲨鱼在几次袭击事件后被贴上了食人者的标签。
