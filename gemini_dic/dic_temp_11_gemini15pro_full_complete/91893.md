hyperkinetic:/ˌhaɪpərkaɪˈnɛtɪk/ | adj. |过度活跃的；运动过度的|Characterized by or affected with hyperkinesis, excessively or abnormally active.|例句：The hyperkinetic child ran around the classroom disrupting the other students. 这个过度活跃的孩子在教室里跑来跑去，打扰了其他学生。|例句：He has a hyperkinetic personality, always on the go. 他有着过度活跃的性格，总是闲不下来。|近义词：hyperactive, restless, energetic, frenetic|反义词：lethargic, sluggish, inactive, calm|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Greek *hyper* (“over, above”) + *kinētikos* (“kinetic, pertaining to motion”), from *kinein* (“to move”).
