bifacial:/baɪˈfeɪʃəl/
adjective
双面的；两面的
Having two faces or surfaces.
Bifacial solar panels generate electricity from both sides. 双面太阳能电池板两面都能发电。
double-sided, two-sided
single-sided, unilateral
