candidated:/ˈkændɪdeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 被提名的；被推荐的 |  Having been proposed as a candidate. While technically correct, it's rarely used and sounds archaic. "Nominated" or "put forward as a candidate" are more common and natural choices. | He was candidated for the position of mayor, but ultimately lost the election. 他被提名为市长候选人，但最终落选了。| Near Synonyms: nominated, proposed, suggested | Antonyms: rejected, disqualified | Word Type: Adjective
