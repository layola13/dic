intellectualism:/ˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəlɪzəm/| n. |智力主义，理智主义，知性主义|The theory that all knowledge is derived from reason, or that the exercise of the intellect is the supreme activity of the human mind.| He rejected intellectualism and embraced a more intuitive approach to art. 他摒弃了知性主义，转而接受一种更直观的艺术方法。|近义词：rationalism, cerebrality|反义词：emotionalism, anti-intellectualism|词性: noun

Etymology: Late 19th century: from intellectual + -ism.
