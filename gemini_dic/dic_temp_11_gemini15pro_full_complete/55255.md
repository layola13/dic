dissimulator:/dɪˈsɪmjʊleɪtər/| n.|伪装者，掩饰者，假装者|A person who dissimulates; one who conceals under a false appearance; one who pretends; a hypocrite.|He was a master dissimulator, hiding his true intentions behind a veil of politeness.|近义词：hypocrite, pretender, deceiver, fraud, impostor|反义词：honest person, truth-teller|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *dissimulātor*, from Latin dissimulō (“dissemble, disguise, conceal, ignore”) + -tor.
