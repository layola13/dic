ammoniac: /əˈmoʊniæk/ (US) /əˈməʊniæk/ (UK)| n. |氨; 氨水|A colorless, pungent gas composed of nitrogen and hydrogen (NH3), which is highly soluble in water and used in many industrial processes and cleaning products. Also refers to a solution of ammonia in water.|The strong smell of ammoniac filled the air. 空气中弥漫着浓烈的氨水味。|Synonyms: ammonia,  hartshorn|Antonyms: None|Noun

ammoniacal: /ˌæməˈnaɪək(ə)l/ (US) /ˌæməˈnaɪəkəl/ (UK)| adj. |氨的; 氨水的|Of, relating to, or containing ammonia.|The ammoniacal odor was overwhelming. 刺鼻的氨味令人难以忍受。|Synonyms:  ammonia-like|Antonyms: None|Adjective
