nonmetallurgical:/ˌnɒnˌmɛtəˈlɜːrdʒɪkəl/
adjective
非冶金的
Not related to or used in metallurgy (the science of extracting metals from their ores and modifying them for use).
The company specializes in nonmetallurgical applications of minerals.  这家公司专门研究矿物的非冶金应用。
The research focused on the nonmetallurgical uses of silicon.  这项研究的重点是硅的非冶金用途。
近义词:non-metallic
反义词:metallurgical
词性:adjective
