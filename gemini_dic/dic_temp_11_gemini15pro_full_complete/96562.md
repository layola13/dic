indraught:/ˈɪndrɑːft/| n.|吸入，吸气；汇流|An indraught of air.  一股气流吸入。|The indraught of air created by the fire. 火引起的空气吸入。|近义词：influx, intake, draft|反义词：outflow, exhaust|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- + draught.  "Draught" comes from the Old English "dragan" meaning "to draw" or "to pull."
