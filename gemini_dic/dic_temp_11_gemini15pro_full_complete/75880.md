fund:/fʌnd/ | n. | 基金；资金；现款；储备 | A sum of money saved or made available for a particular purpose. |  The company set up a fund to help victims of the disaster.  公司设立了一个基金来帮助灾难受害者。 | synonyms: capital, resources, assets, endowment, reserve | antonyms: debt, deficit | noun

fund:/fʌnd/ | vt. | 为…提供资金；拨款给 | To provide with money for a particular purpose. | The government is funding a new research project. 政府正在资助一个新的研究项目。| synonyms: finance, subsidize, back, support, endow | antonyms: defund, divest | verb
