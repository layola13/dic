disproven:/dɪsˈpruːvən/
Verb
已被证明是假的；已被反驳
refute or prove to be false
例句：The theory has been disproven by recent research.  这个理论已经被最近的研究证明是错误的。

例句：His claims were disproven by the evidence. 他的说法被证据驳倒了。

近义词：refuted, debunked, invalidated
反义词：proven, verified, substantiated
词性：Verb (Past Participle)

ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation) + proven (past participle of prove).  Ultimately from Latin probare (to test, approve).
