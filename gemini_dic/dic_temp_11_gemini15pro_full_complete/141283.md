ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *open, from Old English open, from Proto-Germanic *upanaz, from Proto-Indo-European *up- (“up, over”). Cognate with Dutch open, German offen, Gothic 𐌿𐍀𐌰𐌽 (upan).
