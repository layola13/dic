comblessnesses:/ˈkoʊmləsnəsɪz/
noun
梳子缺乏; 没有梳子的状态
The state of lacking combs or being without combs.
The comblessnesses of the travelers made it difficult to maintain their hair. 旅行者们没有梳子，难以打理头发。

The prolonged comblessnesses led to tangled and matted hair for many. 长期没有梳子导致许多人的头发缠结成块。
synonym: lack of combs, absence of combs
antonym: abundance of combs, plethora of combs
词性: 名词
