asseveratingly:/əˈsɛvəˌreɪtɪŋli/
adverb
断言地，肯定地
In a manner that expresses a positive or emphatic assertion.
He asseveratingly denied any involvement in the crime. 他断然否认参与了这起犯罪。
She asseveratingly declared her innocence. 她坚定地宣称自己是无辜的。
Near Synonyms: emphatically, positively, affirmatively, assertively, decidedly
Antonyms: hesitantly, tentatively, uncertainly, doubtfully, equivocally
Word Origin: From the verb "asseverate," from Latin "asseverare" (to assert earnestly), from "ad" (to) + "severus" (serious).
