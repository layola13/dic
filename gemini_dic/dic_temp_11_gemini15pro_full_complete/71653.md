flatterer:/ˈflætərər/ (US), /ˈflætərə(r)/ (UK)| n. |奉承者，谄媚者|Someone who praises insincerely, usually to gain a favor or advantage. |例句：He is a flatterer who always praises the boss. 他是个马屁精，总是称赞老板。

例句：She's such a flatterer, always telling people exactly what they want to hear. 她真是个奉承者，总是告诉人们他们想听的话。

近义词：sycophant, yes-man, brown-noser, toady |反义词:critic, detractor|词性:noun
