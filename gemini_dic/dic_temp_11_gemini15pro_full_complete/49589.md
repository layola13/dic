demeriting: /dɪˈmɛrɪtɪŋ/
verb
给予缺点; 削弱…的价值; 降低…的优点; 处罚
Reducing the value or merit of something; penalizing.
The team was demeriting for their unsportsmanlike conduct.  该队因其不道德的行为而受到处罚。
Demeriting a student for tardiness. 因为迟到而处罚学生。
Synonyms: penalizing, punishing, censuring, criticizing, downgrading
Antonyms: rewarding, praising, commending, promoting, upgrading
词性: 动词 (verb)
