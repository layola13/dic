congressing:/ˈkɒŋɡrɛsɪŋ/ | v. |  集会；开会；群集 |  Gathering or meeting, especially in a large group; assembling. | The butterflies were congressing around the blooming flowers. 蝴蝶聚集在盛开的花朵周围。 |  The students are congressing in the auditorium for the commencement ceremony. 学生们在礼堂集合参加毕业典礼。 |  assembling, gathering, convening |  dispersing, scattering | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English *congressen*, from Old French *congresse*, from Latin *congressus*, past participle of *congredi* "to meet," from *com-* (together) + *gradi* (to walk, to go).
