injuriously:/ɪnˈdʒʊəriəsli/
adverb
有害地；伤害地
in a way that harms or damages somebody/something
例句：His health was injuriously affected by the climate. 他的健康受到了气候的损害。

例句：The rumours affected her career injuriously. 这些谣言对她事业造成了有害影响。


近义词：harmfully, detrimentally, damagingly
反义词：beneficially, advantageously
词性：副词
