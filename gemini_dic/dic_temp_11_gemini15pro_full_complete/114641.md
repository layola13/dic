malexecution:/ˌmælɛksɪˈkjuːʃən/
|n.|错误执行，执行不当|The malexecution of the plan led to disastrous results. 该计划的执行不当导致了灾难性的后果。|The project's malexecution resulted in significant cost overruns.  这个项目的执行不当导致了严重的成本超支。|mismanagement, mishandling, misapplication| proper execution, correct implementation|noun
Etymology: From mal- (bad, ill) + execution.
