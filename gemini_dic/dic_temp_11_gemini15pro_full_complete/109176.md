legitimism:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmɪzəm/
n.
正统主义，合法主义
The belief that a monarch has a legitimate right to rule by virtue of birthright.  The political principle that the former ruling dynasty has the right to be restored.
The French legitimism supported the restoration of the Bourbons. 法国正统主义者支持波旁王朝的复辟。
synonyms: traditionalism, conservatism, orthodoxy
antonyms: revolutionism, radicalism, reformism
noun

legitimate:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmət/
adj.
合法的；正当的；合理的；正统的
A legitimate government. 合法政府
A legitimate claim. 合理的要求
synonyms: legal, lawful, valid, justifiable, rightful
antonyms: illegal, illegitimate, invalid, unjustified
adjective

legitimate:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmeɪt/
v.
使合法；使正当
To make legitimate; legitimize.
The government has legitimized the use of force. 政府已经将使用武力合法化。
synonyms: legalize, authorize, validate, justify
antonyms: illegalize, prohibit, invalidate
verb

legitimacy:/ləˈdʒɪtɪməsi/
n.
合法性；正统性
The quality or state of being legitimate.
The legitimacy of the government was questioned. 政府的合法性受到质疑。
synonyms: legality, lawfulness, validity, justifiability, rightfulness
antonyms: illegality, illegitimacy, invalidity
noun
