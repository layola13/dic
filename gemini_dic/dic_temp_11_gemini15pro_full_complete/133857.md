nongraphically:/ˌnɒnˈɡræfɪkli/ | adv. | 非图形地；非图解地 |  Not graphically; in a way that does not involve graphics or diagrams. |  He explained the concept nongraphically, using only words. 他只用文字非图解地解释了这个概念。 | Synonyms: verbally, orally, textually | Antonyms: graphically, visually | Adverb

Etymology:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "graphically" (from "graphic," ultimately from Greek "graphikos" meaning "of or for writing, drawing, painting").
