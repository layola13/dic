infusable:/ɪnˈfjuːzəbl/ | adj. | 可灌输的，可注入的 |  Capable of being infused; that can be introduced or instilled.  |  The tea is infusable with various herbs and spices.  这种茶可以加入各种香草和香料。 | Synonyms:  impregnatable, instill able,  injectable | Antonyms: non-infusable, impenetrable | adjective

infusable:/ɪnˈfjuːzəbl/ | adj. | 可浸泡的 |  Capable of being steeped in liquid to extract flavor or other properties. | The dried herbs are infusable and can be used to make tea. 这些干草药可以浸泡，用来泡茶。 | Synonyms: steepable, brewable | Antonyms: uninfusable | adjective
