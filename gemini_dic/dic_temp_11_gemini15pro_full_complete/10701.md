apricot: /ˈeɪprɪkɒt/ (UK) /ˈæprɪkɑːt/ (US)| n.|杏; 杏黄色; 杏树|A small, round, orange-yellow fruit with soft flesh and a single seed.|例句：I love the taste of fresh apricots. 我喜欢新鲜杏子的味道。

例句：The apricot jam is delicious. 杏酱很美味。

近义词：peach, nectarine|反义词:|词性:noun

apricot: /ˈeɪprɪkɒt/ (UK) /ˈæprɪkɑːt/ (US)| adj.|杏黄色的|Having a pale orange-yellow colour like that of an apricot.|例句：She wore an apricot dress. 她穿了一件杏黄色的连衣裙。

例句：The walls were painted a soft apricot color. 墙壁被漆成了柔和的杏黄色。
