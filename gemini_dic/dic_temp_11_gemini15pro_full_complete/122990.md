monandrous:/məˈnændrəs/
|adj.|
单雄蕊的；一雄蕊的；单夫的|
Having only one stamen or having stamens united into one body.  Also, having only one husband at a time.|
例句：Monandrous flowers are characteristic of certain plant families.  单雄蕊的花是某些植物科的特征。

例句：Some cultures historically practiced monandrous marriage systems. 一些文化在历史上实行一夫一妻制。
|
近义词：monandric|
反义词：polyandrous|
词性：adjective
