novices:/ˈnɒvɪsɪz/ (UK) /ˈnɑːvɪsɪz/ (US)| n.|新手，初学者|A person new to or inexperienced in a field or situation. |Many online games offer tutorials for novices. 许多网络游戏为新手提供教程。|
The novices struggled with the complex software. 新手们在使用复杂的软件时遇到了困难。|
Synonyms: beginner, learner, trainee, apprentice, neophyte
Antonyms: expert, professional, veteran, master
