interregnum:/ˌɪntərˈrɛɡnəm/| n. |空位期，过渡期，中断期|A period when normal government is suspended, especially between successive reigns or regimes.|The death of the king created an interregnum that lasted for several months. 国王的去世造成了持续数月的空位期。|Synonyms: interim, interval, hiatus, break|Antonyms: continuity, continuation|noun

interregnum:/ˌɪntərˈrɛɡnəm/| n. |空位期，过渡期，中断期|A pause or interruption in continuity.|There was a brief interregnum in the fighting, but it soon resumed.  战斗中出现了短暂的停顿，但很快就恢复了。|Synonyms: interim, interval, hiatus, break|Antonyms: continuity, continuation|noun
