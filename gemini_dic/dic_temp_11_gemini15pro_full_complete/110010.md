libraries:/ˈlaɪbrɛriz/ (US), /ˈlaɪbrəriz/ (UK)| n.|图书馆（复数）|A building or room containing collections of books, periodicals, and sometimes films and recorded music for people to read, borrow, or refer to.|例句：The city has several public libraries. 这个城市有几个公共图书馆。|例句：Many universities have extensive libraries. 许多大学都有大型图书馆。|近义词：archives, collections, depositories|反义词:|noun
