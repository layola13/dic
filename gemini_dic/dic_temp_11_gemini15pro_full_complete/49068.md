dehumidify:/ˌdiːhjuːˈmɪdɪfaɪ/
verb
除湿，去湿
To remove moisture or dampness from; make less humid.
The dehumidifier helps to dehumidify the air in the basement.  除湿机有助于去除地下室空气中的湿气。
The air conditioner also dehumidifies the air.  空调也能除湿。
dry, desiccate
humidify, moisten
词性:动词
