daymaid:/ˈdeɪmeɪd/
n.
女佣，白天做家务的女佣
A woman employed to do housework during the day.
The daymaid came in to clean the house every morning.  每天早上女佣都会来打扫房子。
近义词：housemaid, maid, charwoman, cleaner
反义词：(无直接反义词，可考虑 employer)
词性：名词


Etymology:
From "day" + "maid".  "Day" referring to daytime work, and "maid" meaning a female servant. 由表示白天工作的 "day" 和表示女仆的 "maid" 组成。
