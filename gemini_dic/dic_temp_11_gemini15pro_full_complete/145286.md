overbrace:/ˈoʊvərbreɪs/| vt. n.|在…上加括号；用括号连接|To connect or enclose with a brace or braces, typically for emphasis or in mathematical notation.  A brace or pair of braces placed over something.|He overbraced the equation to show which parts were to be multiplied first. 他用括号连接方程的一部分，以表明哪些部分要先进行乘法运算。|The author overbraced the key phrase in the sentence. 作者用括号标注了句子中的关键词组。|近义词: bracket, enclose, encompass|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 unbracket, open)|词性: verb, noun
