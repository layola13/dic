festoony:/ˈfestuːni/
adjective
花彩状的；饰以花彩的；节日气氛的
Decorated with or resembling festoons; festive.
The room was festoony with colorful balloons and ribbons. 房间里装饰着五彩缤纷的气球和彩带，充满了节日气氛。
The wedding reception was a festoony affair. 婚礼招待会充满了节日气氛。
Synonyms:festive, celebratory, decorated, adorned
Antonyms:plain, simple, undecorated
