obtrusivenesses:/əbˈtruːsɪvnəsɪz/
n.
强加于人;唐突;冒昧
The quality of being obtrusive; the act of obtruding.
Her constant obtrusivenesses eventually alienated her friends. 她不断的打扰最终疏远了她的朋友。
Synonyms: intrusions, impositions, interferences
Antonyms: discretion, reserve, tactfulness

obtrusive:/əbˈtruːsɪv/
adj.
扎眼的;突兀的;刺眼的;莽撞的;扰人的
Noticeable or prominent in an unwelcome or intrusive way.
The waiter was overly obtrusive. 服务员过于殷勤了。
Synonyms: intrusive, interfering, meddlesome
Antonyms: discreet, unobtrusive, inconspicuous
词性: 形容词
