bedouinism:/ˈbɛdwɪnɪzəm/
n.
贝都因人生活方式，游牧生活
The nomadic lifestyle or the characteristics of Bedouins.
The harsh conditions of the desert fostered a strong sense of bedouinism among the tribes.  沙漠的恶劣条件在部落中培养了强烈的贝都因主义意识。
nomadism, wandering
settled life, sedentary lifestyle
noun
