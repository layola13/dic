inapprehensive:/ˌɪnæprɪˈhɛnsɪv/| adj.|不担心的，不忧虑的|Not feeling or showing anxiety or apprehension; carefree.|例句：He seemed surprisingly inapprehensive about the upcoming exam. 他对即将到来的考试似乎出奇地不担心。|近义词：unconcerned, untroubled, carefree, relaxed|反义词：apprehensive, anxious, worried, nervous|词性:形容词
词源: Late 16th century: from in- (expressing negation) + apprehensive.
