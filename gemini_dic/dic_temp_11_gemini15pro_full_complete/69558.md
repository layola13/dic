fellowshiping:/ˈfɛləʊʃɪpɪŋ/ | n. | 交誼，友誼，夥伴關係 | The act of sharing fellowship; companionship. |  They enjoyed the fellowshiping with other members of the church. 他们享受与其他教会成员的友谊。| 近义词：companionship, camaraderie, fraternity, sorority | 反义词: isolation, loneliness | 词性:名词

词源: From "fellowship" + "-ing" suffix, indicating the act or process of.  "Fellowship" itself comes from Old English "fēolagscipe," meaning a sharing of money or property, from "fēolaga" (fellow) + "-scipe" (ship, state of being).
