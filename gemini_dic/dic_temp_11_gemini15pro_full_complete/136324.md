nonrequisitions:/ˌnɒnˌrekwiˈzɪʃənz/ (British), /ˌnɑːnˌrɛkwɪˈzɪʃənz/ (American)
noun
非征用物资；未经申请的物资
Items or materials not obtained through a formal requisition process.  These are supplies or equipment that were not officially requested but are available or acquired through other means.
The surplus equipment found in the warehouse was categorized as nonrequisitions.  仓库里发现的剩余设备被归类为非征用物资。
The team had to make do with nonrequisitions to complete the project.  团队不得不利用未经申请的物资来完成项目。
synonyms:  non-requested items, unrequested supplies, surplus materials, excess inventory
antonyms:  requisitions, requested items, ordered supplies
词性: noun
