immunodeficient: /ˌɪmjuːnəʊdɪˈfɪʃnt/ | adj. | 免疫缺陷的；免疫功能低下的 | Having an impaired immune system; lacking the normal ability to resist infection. |  People with immunodeficient conditions are more susceptible to infections. 免疫缺陷的人更容易受到感染。|  The child was immunodeficient and prone to illness.  这个孩子免疫缺陷，容易生病。 | Synonyms: immunocompromised, immunosuppressed | Antonyms: immunocompetent | adjective
