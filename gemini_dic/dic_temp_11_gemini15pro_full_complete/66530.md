exhaustingly:/ɪɡˈzɔːstɪŋli/| adv.|令人疲惫地，使人精疲力竭地|in a way that makes you feel extremely tired|The journey was exhaustingly long. 这趟旅程长得令人疲惫不堪。|近义词: tiringly, strenuously|反义词: refreshingly, invigoratingly|词性: 副词

Etymology:
From exhaust + -ing + -ly.  "Exhaust" comes from the Latin "exhaurire," meaning "to draw out completely," "to empty."
