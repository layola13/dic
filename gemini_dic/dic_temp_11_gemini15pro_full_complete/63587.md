envisages: /ɪnˈvɪzɪdʒɪz/ (British) /ɛnˈvɪzɪdʒɪz/ (American)| v. |设想，展望| to form a mental picture of something, especially something that may happen in the future |The report envisages that growth will slow down next year. 报告预计明年的增长将会放缓。|Synonyms: foresee, predict, anticipate, contemplate, visualize |Antonyms: disregard, ignore| Verb
