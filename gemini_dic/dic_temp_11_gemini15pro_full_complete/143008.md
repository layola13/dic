outbellowing:/ˌaʊtˈbɛloʊɪŋ/
verb
大声吼叫，胜过…的吼叫声
to bellow more loudly than someone or something else.
The bull outbellowed its rival. 公牛的吼叫声盖过了它的对手。
Synonyms: outshout, outroar, outyell
Antonyms: whisper, murmur
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + bellow.  "Out-" is a prefix meaning "exceeding," "surpassing," or "outside." "Bellow" comes from the Old English "bellan," meaning "to roar" or "to make a loud noise like a bull."
