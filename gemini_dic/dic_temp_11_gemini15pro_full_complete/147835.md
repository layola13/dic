overpunishing: /ˌoʊvərˈpʌnɪʃɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度惩罚的，惩罚过重的 | Punishing someone excessively or too harshly for a particular offense. | The school's policy on plagiarism was seen as overpunishing for first-time offenders.  学校的抄袭政策被认为对初犯者来说惩罚过重。 |  Excessive punishment, Draconian,  Too harsh, Overly severe | Lenient, Merciful, Moderate | adjective
