disconsolately:/dɪsˈkɒnsələtli/ /dɪsˈkɑːnsələtli/| adv.| 伤心欲绝地，沮丧地 | In a manner that expresses or shows dejection, sadness, or lack of comfort. | She walked disconsolately through the park after hearing the bad news. 她听到坏消息后，伤心地走过公园。|  He sat disconsolately in the corner, staring at the floor. 他沮丧地坐在角落里，盯着地板。| 近义词: dejectedly, sadly, mournfully, gloomily | 反义词: cheerfully, happily, joyfully | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From disconsolate + -ly.  Disconsolate from dis- "not" + consolatus, past participle of Latin consolari "to comfort, console," from com- "together" + solari "to soothe, console."
