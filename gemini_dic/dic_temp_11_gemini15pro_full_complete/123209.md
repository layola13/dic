monochronic:/ˌmɑːnəˈkrɑːnɪk/ | adj. | 单时制的，单线程的 | Characterized by doing one thing at a time and emphasizing schedules and deadlines.  Focusing on one task or activity at a time. |  She has a monochronic work style, preferring to focus on one task at a time until it's completed. 她有一个单时制的工作风格，喜欢一次专注于一项任务，直到完成。|  He's very monochronic and gets frustrated when interrupted. 他非常单线程，被打断时会感到沮丧。| 近义词：linear, sequential, single-tasking | 反义词: polychronic, multitasking | 词性: adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
