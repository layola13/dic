cesareans:/sɪˈzɛəriənz/| n. |剖腹产|Surgical delivery of a baby through an incision in the mother's abdomen and uterus.  A plural form of cesarean.|例句：Many cesareans are performed each year. 每年都有很多剖腹产手术。

例句：The hospital recorded a high number of cesareans last month. 上个月这家医院记录了大量的剖腹产手术。|近义词：C-section, cesarean section, caesarean section|反义词:vaginal birth, natural childbirth|词性:noun

cesarean:/sɪˈzɛəriən/| n. adj. |剖腹产|Surgical delivery of a baby through an incision in the mother's abdomen and uterus. Can also be used as an adjective pertaining to such a delivery.|例句：She had a cesarean. 她做了剖腹产。

例句：She had a cesarean delivery. 她进行了剖腹产。|近义词：C-section, cesarean section, caesarean section|反义词:vaginal birth, natural childbirth|词性:noun, adjective
