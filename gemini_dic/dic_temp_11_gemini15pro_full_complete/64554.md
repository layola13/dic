erred:/ɜːrd/ /ɝːd/
verb
犯错，出错
made a mistake; was incorrect
He erred in his calculations. 他在计算中犯了错误。
The court erred in its judgment. 法院的判决有误。
synonyms:mistook, blundered, slipped up, messed up, goofed, flubbed
antonyms:succeeded,  excelled, triumphed, aced
