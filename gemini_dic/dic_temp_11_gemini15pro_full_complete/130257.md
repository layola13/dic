non-statement:/ˌnɒnˈsteɪtmənt/ | n. | 非陈述; 非声明 |  Something that is not a statement, especially in logic or philosophy. A sentence that is not declarative and does not express a proposition that can be true or false. |  His utterance was a non-statement, offering no real information. 他的话等于什么都没说，没有提供任何实际信息。 | synonym:  non-assertion, non-declaration | antonym: statement, assertion | noun

non-statement:/ˌnɒnˈsteɪtmənt/ | adj. | 非陈述性的 | Not pertaining to or in the nature of a statement. | His non-statement reply left us wondering what he actually meant.  他模棱两可的回答让我们想知道他真正的意思。 | synonym: non-declarative | antonym: declarative, statement | adjective
