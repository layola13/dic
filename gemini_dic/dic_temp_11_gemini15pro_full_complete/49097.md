deiform:/ˈdiːɪfɔːrm/
adjective
神形的；神一样的
Having the form of a god; godlike.
例句：The deiform statue radiated an aura of power.  神形的雕像散发着力量的光环。

例句：Ancient Egyptians believed their pharaohs were deiform beings. 古埃及人相信他们的法老是神一样的存在。
近义词：godlike, divine, celestial
反义词：mortal, human, earthly
词性：adjective
词源：From Latin deus (“god”) + -formis (“-shaped, -formed”)
