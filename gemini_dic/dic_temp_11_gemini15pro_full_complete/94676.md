imprinter:/ɪmˈprɪntər/| n.|印记器，压印机，铭刻者|A device or person that imprints or stamps something.  A device that leaves a mark or impression on something, or someone who establishes a strong impression on someone else, especially early in life.|The imprinter stamped the company logo onto the leather.  压印机将公司标志印在了皮革上。

The orphaned goslings saw the farmer as their mother, truly an imprinter on their young lives. 这些失去父母的小鹅把农民当成了它们的母亲，这位农民真正在它们幼小的心灵上留下了深刻的印记。|近义词：marker, stamper, engraver|反义词:eraser|Noun
