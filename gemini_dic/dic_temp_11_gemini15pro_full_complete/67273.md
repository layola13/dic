exquisite:/ɛkˈskwɪzɪt/ | adj. | 精致的；精美的；敏锐的；剧烈的 | Characterized by refined taste and elegance; keenly sensitive or discerning; intensely felt. | The pain was exquisite. 疼痛剧烈。 | synonyms: refined, elegant, delicate, superb, acute, intense | antonyms: crude, coarse, dull, insensitive | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin exquisitus, past participle of exquirere "to search out, seek diligently," from ex- "out" + quaerere "to seek, ask."  The original sense was "carefully sought out," hence "choice, excellent."
