forecaster:/ˈfɔːrkæstər/ (US) /ˈfɔːkɑːstər/ (UK)| n. |预报员；预测者|A person who forecasts, especially weather conditions or economic trends. |The weather forecaster predicted heavy snow. 天气预报员预测会有大雪。|Synonyms: predictor, diviner, soothsayer, prophet, prognosticator|Antonyms:  None (Antonyms for the action of forecasting would be things like "miscalculate," "misjudge," but there isn't a direct antonym for the person doing the forecasting.)|noun
