new-ager:/ˈnjuː eɪdʒər/ /ˈnuː eɪdʒər/| n.|新时代人，新时代运动追随者|A person who follows New Age philosophies and practices, often involving spirituality, personal growth, and alternative medicine.|She's a new-ager who believes in crystals and reincarnation. 她是一个相信水晶和轮回的新时代人。|Synonyms: spiritual seeker, mystic, esotericist|Antonyms: traditionalist, conservative|Noun

new-age:/ˌnjuː ˈeɪdʒ/ /ˌnuː ˈeɪdʒ/| adj.|新时代的，新纪元的|Relating to or characteristic of the New Age movement.|She's into new-age music and meditation. 她喜欢新时代音乐和冥想。|Synonyms: alternative, holistic, unconventional|Antonyms: traditional, conventional, orthodox|Adjective
