dissension:/dɪˈsɛnʃən/ | n. | 争论，意见不合，分歧 |Disagreement that leads to discord.  A state of discord or disagreement. | There was much dissension among the committee members regarding the new proposal. 委员会成员对新提案存在很多分歧。| The dissension within the party threatened to split it. 党内的分歧有可能导致分裂。 | synonyms: disagreement, discord, strife, contention, conflict, dispute | antonyms: agreement, harmony, concord, unity | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dissēnsiō (“disagreement”), from Latin dissēnsus, perfect passive participle of dissentiō (“I disagree”), from dis- (“apart, away”) + sentiō (“I feel, think”).
