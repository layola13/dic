hominy:/ˈhɑːməni/ | n. |玉米粥，碾碎的玉米|A dish made from dried maize kernels that have been treated with an alkali, in a process called nixtamalization. |Example:  Hominy is a staple food in many cultures. 玉米粥是许多文化中的主食。

Example:  She made a hearty stew with hominy and beans. 她用玉米粥和豆子做了一道丰盛的炖菜。|近义词:grits, samp|反义词: None|词性:noun


hominies: /ˈhɑːməniz/ | n. |玉米粥（复数），碾碎的玉米（复数）|Plural form of hominy; a dish made from dried maize kernels that have been treated with an alkali. |Example:  Hominies are often served with chili. 玉米粥经常与辣椒一起食用。
