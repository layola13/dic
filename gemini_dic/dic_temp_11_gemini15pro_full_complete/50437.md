deprecate:/ˈdɛprɪkeɪt/| v. | 贬低，反对，不赞成 | express disapproval of; plead against; protest against |  The teacher deprecated the student's disruptive behavior. 老师不赞成这个学生扰乱课堂的行为。

The company deprecated the old software and encouraged users to upgrade. 该公司不建议使用旧软件，并鼓励用户升级。 | 近义词:criticize, condemn, disapprove, belittle | 反义词:praise, commend, approve, support | verb

deprecating:/ˈdɛprɪkeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 贬低的，反对的 | expressing disapproval; disapproving | He gave a deprecating look at my old car. 他用贬低的眼神看着我的旧车。

She made a deprecating gesture with her hand. 她用手做了一个表示反对的手势。 | 近义词:critical, disapproving, belittling | 反义词:praising, approving, complimentary | adjective

词源: From Late Latin *deprecatus, past participle of deprecari (“to avert by prayer, pray against, try to ward off”), from de- (“away from”) + precari (“to pray, beg”).
