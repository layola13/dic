autobiographic:/ˌɔːtəˌbaɪəˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. | 自传的，自传体的 | Relating to or characteristic of an autobiography; dealing with the writer's own life. |  Example: Her latest novel is surprisingly autobiographic. 她最新的小说出人意料地带有自传性质。 | Example: He gave an autobiographic account of his childhood. 他讲述了他童年的自传式经历。 | Synonyms: biographical, personal, memoiristic | Antonyms: fictional, invented | word type: adjective

autobiography:/ˌɔːtəbaɪˈɒɡrəfi/ | n. | 自传 | An account of a person's life written by that person. | Example:  He is working on his autobiography. 他正在撰写自传。 | Example: Her autobiography became a bestseller. 她的自传成了畅销书。 | Synonyms: memoirs, life story, personal narrative | Antonyms: biography | word type: noun
