ganister:/ˈɡænɪstər/| n.|硅质砂岩，硅石，耐火材料|A hard, close-grained siliceous sandstone, used in making refractory bricks.  It is typically cemented with secondary silica and very resistant to heat. |The furnace was lined with ganister.  熔炉衬有硅质砂岩。|Synonyms: quartzite, silica rock, firestone|Antonyms: N/A|noun

ganister:/ˈɡænɪstər/| n.|路面碎石|Small, broken stones used for road surfaces. (chiefly Northern England)|The road was mended with ganister. 这条路是用碎石修补的。|Synonyms: gravel, crushed stone, road metal|Antonyms: N/A|noun
