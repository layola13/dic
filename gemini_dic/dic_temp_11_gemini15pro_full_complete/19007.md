bestriding:/bɪˈstraɪdɪŋ/
verb
跨坐, 跨立, 骑, 统治, 遍布
extending across or over; sitting or standing with one leg on each side of.  Dominating, controlling, or extending over a wide area.
The Colossus of Rhodes bestrode the harbor entrance.  罗德岛巨像跨立在港口入口。
Napoleon bestrode Europe like a colossus. 拿破仑像巨人一样统治着欧洲。
bestride, straddle, mount, dominate, cover
