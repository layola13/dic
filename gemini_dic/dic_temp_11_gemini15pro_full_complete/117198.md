measurelessness:/ˈmɛʒərˌlɛsnɪs/| n. | 无限；无量；无度 | The state of being impossible to measure or boundless. |  The measurelessness of the universe is awe-inspiring. 宇宙的浩瀚令人敬畏。

The measurelessness of his ambition led to his downfall. 他无限的野心导致了他的垮台。 | 近义词：immensity, vastness, boundlessness, infinity | 反义词：finitude, limitedness, smallness | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From measureless + -ness.  Measureless from measure + -less.  Ultimately from Latin *metior.
