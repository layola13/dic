adjutage:/ˈædʒətɑːʒ/ /ˈædʒətɪdʒ/ | n. | 喷嘴；管嘴；放水管 | A tube attached to a container or pipe to control the flow of liquid. | The adjutage on the fire hose allowed for a concentrated stream of water. 消防水龙带上的喷嘴可以形成集中的水流。

The gardener adjusted the adjutage on the watering can to create a gentle spray. 园丁调整了洒水壶上的喷嘴，使其喷出柔和的水雾。 | synonym: nozzle, spout, jet, aperture | antonym: blockage, obstruction | noun
