bulkiness:/ˈbʌlkinɪs/
noun
体积庞大；笨重；庞大
The state or quality of being large and taking up a lot of space.
The bulkiness of the package made it difficult to carry.  这个包裹体积太大，很难搬运。
近义词:largeness, massiveness, size, volume, hugeness
反义词:compactness, smallness, slightness
词性:n.
