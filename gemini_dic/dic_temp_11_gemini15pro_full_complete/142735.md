otolithic:/ˌəʊtəˈlɪθɪk/ /ˌoʊtəˈlɪθɪk/ | adj. |耳石的，含耳石的|Relating to or containing otoliths (small crystals of calcium carbonate in the inner ear involved in balance and orientation).|The otolithic organs help us maintain balance.|近义词: vestibular |反义词: None|词性:形容词

otolith:/ˈəʊtəlɪθ/ /ˈoʊtəlɪθ/ | n. |耳石|A small crystal of calcium carbonate in the inner ear of vertebrates, involved in sensing gravity and acceleration.|The movement of otoliths stimulates hair cells, which send signals to the brain about the position of the head.|近义词: statoconium |反义词: None|词性:名词
