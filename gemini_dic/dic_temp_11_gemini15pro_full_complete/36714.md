collitigant:/kəˈlɪtɪɡənt/
n.
共同诉讼人，共同被告
One of two or more parties engaged together in a lawsuit.
The two collitigants finally reached a settlement.  这两个共同诉讼人最终达成了和解。
近义词:joint litigant, co-litigant, fellow litigant
反义词:opponent, adversary
词性:名词


Etymology:
From Latin com- (together) + litigant- (present participle stem of litigare, to go to law).
