overpolitic:/ˌoʊvərˈpɑːlətɪk/ | adj. | 过于政治化的，过分讲究策略的 | Excessively or overly concerned with political correctness, strategy, or maneuvering. Often implies a negative connotation, suggesting that someone is being manipulative or insincere. | The office environment had become overpolitic, with everyone carefully watching what they said. 办公室环境变得过于政治化，每个人都小心翼翼地注意着自己说的话。| The candidate's overpolitic speech failed to connect with voters.  这位候选人过于政治化的演讲未能与选民产生共鸣。 | 近义词: calculating, strategic, manipulative, shrewd, diplomatic | 反义词: candid, frank, straightforward, ingenuous, artless | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "politic" (adjective meaning "shrewd or prudent in practical matters; artful; cunning").  The word "politic" ultimately derives from the Greek "politikos" (of citizens or the state).
