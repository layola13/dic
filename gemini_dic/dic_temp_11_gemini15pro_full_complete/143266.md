outdistance:/ˌaʊtˈdɪstəns/
verb
超越，把…远远抛在后面
To go far ahead of someone or something in a race or competition.
The Kenyan runner easily outdistanced his rivals in the marathon. 肯尼亚选手在马拉松比赛中轻松地超越了他的对手。
He outdistanced the other runners. 他远远超过了其他赛跑者。
synonyms: outrun, outstrip, surpass, exceed
antonyms: lag behind, trail
词性:动词
