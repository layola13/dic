farse:/fɑːrs/| n.|闹剧；滑稽剧；胡说八道|A humorous play or film based on improbable situations, or a ludicrous or extravagant act or story.|The whole trial was a complete farse. 整个审判就是一场闹剧。|Synonyms:mockery, travesty, sham, pretense|Antonyms:reality, truth|noun

farse:/fɑːrs/| v.| (farsed, farsing) |填充；填塞（禽类等）|To stuff (poultry etc) before cooking.|The turkey was farsed with a chestnut stuffing. 火鸡里塞满了栗子馅。|Synonyms:stuff, fill|Antonyms:empty|verb
