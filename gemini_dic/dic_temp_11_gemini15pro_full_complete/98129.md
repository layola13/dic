insanely:/ɪnˈseɪnli/| adv.|疯狂地，极其地|In an insane manner; to an insane degree.  Meaning extremely or excessively.|例句：The music was insanely loud. 音乐吵得要命。|近义词：madly, crazily, extremely, ridiculously|反义词: sanely, rationally|词性:副词
词源: From insane + -ly.  Insane comes from Latin insanus "unsound," from in- "not" + sanus "sound, healthy."
