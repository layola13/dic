disked:/dɪskt/
|v. |制成磁盘；用磁盘存储；用圆盘犁耕地|To have stored something on a disk or to have plowed land using a disk harrow. |The farmer disked the field after the harvest. 农民在收获后用圆盘犁耕地。|Synonyms: plowed, tilled, cultivated |Antonyms:  (For plowing) unplowed, untilled|verb (past tense)


disked:/dɪskt/
|adj. |做成盘状的；装有磁盘的|Shaped like a disk; equipped with a disk. |The machine has a disked blade. 这台机器有一个盘状的刀片。|Synonyms: disc-shaped, circular, round|Antonyms: square, rectangular|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From disk + -ed.  "Disk" itself comes from Old Norse *diskr, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *diskaz.
