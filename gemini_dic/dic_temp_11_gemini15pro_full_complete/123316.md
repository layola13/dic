monogram:/ˈmɒnəɡræm/ (US) /ˈmɑːnəɡræm/| n. |字母组合，交织字母，花押字|A design consisting of two or more letters, typically the initials of a person's name, usually interwoven or combined in some artistic way, often used as a logo or personal mark.|She had her monogram embroidered on her handkerchiefs. 她的手帕上绣着她的名字缩写。|近义词:initial, cipher, insignia|反义词: (No direct antonym) |noun

monogram:/ˈmɒnəɡræm/ (US) /ˈmɑːnəɡræm/| v. |用字母组合图案装饰|To mark or decorate with a monogram.|The hotel monogrammed all its towels and linens.  这家酒店的所有毛巾和亚麻制品上都印有字母组合图案。|近义词:initial, personalize, customize|反义词: (No direct antonym) |verb
