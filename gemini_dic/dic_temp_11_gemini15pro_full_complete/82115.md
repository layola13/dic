grogging:/ˈɡrɒɡɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈɡrɑːɡɪŋ/ (US)| n.|痛饮烈酒; 豪饮; 狂饮|The act of drinking grog (an alcoholic beverage) heavily or excessively.  It often implies a social gathering involving such drinking.|例句：The sailors were grogging after a long voyage at sea.  水手们在海上航行很长一段时间后痛饮烈酒。

例句：The pirates were known for their nightly grogging sessions.  这些海盗以每晚的狂饮而闻名。|近义词：carousing, boozing, drinking, reveling|反义词：abstaining, temperance|词性:名词
