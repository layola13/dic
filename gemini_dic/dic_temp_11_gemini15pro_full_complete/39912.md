constrain:/kənˈstreɪn/| vt.|限制，约束，强迫|to limit or restrict someone or something|例句：The company is constrained by budget cuts.  公司受到预算削减的限制。

例句：She felt constrained to accept the offer. 她感到不得不接受这个提议。|近义词：restrict, limit, confine, compel, force|反义词：free, liberate, release|词性：动词

constrain:/kənˈstreɪn/| n.|限制，约束|something that limits or restricts something else (usually used in plural form: constraints)|例句：Financial constraints forced the company to lay off workers. 财政限制迫使公司解雇工人。

例句：Time constraints prevented us from finishing the project. 时间限制使我们无法完成项目。|近义词：restriction, limitation|反义词：freedom, liberty|词性：名词
