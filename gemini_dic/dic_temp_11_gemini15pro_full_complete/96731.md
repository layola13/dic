inelaborate:/ɪnɪˈlæbərət/ | adj. | 未经详细阐述的；不详尽的 | Not elaborated; not worked out in detail. | The plan was inelaborate and lacked specific details.  这个计划不够详尽，缺乏具体细节。| The instructions were inelaborate, leaving me confused. 说明书不够详尽，让我很困惑。 | 近义词: sketchy, vague, rudimentary, undeveloped | 反义词: detailed, elaborate, comprehensive, thorough | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From in- (not) + elaborate (worked out with great care and detail). Ultimately from Latin elaborare "to produce by labor," from e- "out" + laborare "to work."
