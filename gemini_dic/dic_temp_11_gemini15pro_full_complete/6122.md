词源: From Old French *ametiste, from Latin amethystus, from Ancient Greek ἀμέθυστος (améthustos, “not drunken”), from ἀ- (a-, “not”) + μεθύσκω (methúskō, “intoxicate”).  The ancient Greeks believed that amethysts could prevent intoxication.
