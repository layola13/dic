filial:/ˈfɪliəl/ (US), /ˈfɪlɪəl/ (UK)| adj.|子女的；孝顺的|Of or relating to a son or daughter; due from a son or daughter; denoting the generation or generations after the parental generation.|例句：She has a strong filial affection for her parents. 她对父母怀有深厚的孝心。

例句：Filial piety is highly valued in many cultures.  孝顺在许多文化中都受到高度重视。

近义词：dutiful, respectful, obedient|反义词：disobedient, disrespectful, unfilial|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *filial*, from Old French *filial*, from Latin *fīliālis* (“of or belonging to a son or daughter”), from *fīlius* (“son”) + *-ālis*.
