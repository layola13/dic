antisensuousnesses:/ˌæntiˈsɛnʃʊəsnɪsɪz/
|n.|反感性，禁欲主义|The state or quality of being opposed to or averse to sensuous pleasures or indulgence.|His antisensuousnesses manifested in his austere lifestyle and rejection of worldly pleasures. 他禁欲主义体现在他简朴的生活方式和对世俗享乐的拒绝。|Near synonyms: asceticism, austerity, abstinence|Antonyms: sensuality, hedonism|Noun

Etymology:

* anti- : Prefix meaning "against," "opposite of," from Greek *anti-*.
* sensuous : From late Middle English, from Old French *sensuous*, from Latin *sensus* "sense, feeling".
* -ness : Suffix forming nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
