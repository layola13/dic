accelerative:/əkˈsɛləˌreɪtɪv/
adjective
加速的，促进的
Increasing the speed of something; tending to accelerate.
The new policy had an accelerative effect on the economy. 新政策对经济起到了加速作用。
The accelerative power of the engine was impressive.  发动机的加速动力令人印象深刻。

近义词: accelerating, speed-up, boosting, quickening
反义词: decelerative, slowing, hindering, retarding
词性: adjective
