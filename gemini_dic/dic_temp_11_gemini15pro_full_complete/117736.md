melanistic:/ˌmɛləˈnɪstɪk/| adj.|黑色素的，黑化的|Having an unusually dark coloration due to an excess of melanin.|例句：Melanistic jaguars are often called black panthers.  黑化的美洲虎通常被称为黑豹。

例句：The melanistic form of the peppered moth became more common during the Industrial Revolution.  在工业革命期间，胡椒蛾的黑化形态变得更加普遍。|近义词：dark, black, pigmented|反义词：leucistic, albino|词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek μέλας (melas, “black, dark”) + -istic.
