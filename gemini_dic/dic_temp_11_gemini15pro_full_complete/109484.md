leprous:/ˈleprəs/ | adj. |麻风的；患麻风病的；像麻风病的；道德败坏的| Affected with, resembling, or characteristic of leprosy; morally corrupt or degraded. |例句：The leprous beggar sat by the roadside. 患麻风病的乞丐坐在路边。

例句：The building was in a leprous state of disrepair. 这座建筑处于一种像麻风病一样破败不堪的状态。

近义词：diseased, infected, tainted, corrupt | 反义词：healthy, pure, uncorrupted | 词性：adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *leprus*, from Old French *leprus*, from Latin *leprōsus*, from *lepra* (“leprosy”), from Ancient Greek *λέπρα* (*lépra*) (“leprosy”), from *λέπω* (*lépō*) (“to peel, scale”).
