enlivening:/ɪnˈlaɪvənɪŋ/
adjective
使人愉快的；使生动活泼的
making something more interesting or exciting
The music was quite enlivening.  这音乐非常令人愉快。
The speaker told some enlivening anecdotes. 演讲者讲了一些有趣的轶事。
近义词:invigorating, stimulating, exciting, animating
反义词:dulling, boring, tedious, monotonous
词性:adjective

词源: From enliven + -ing.  Enliven comes from the Old French enlivner, meaning "to give life to," from en- "in" + live "alive."
