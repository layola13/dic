gyppers:/ˈɡɪpərz/
noun
欺骗者；诈骗者
People who cheat or swindle others.
Those gyppers sold me a fake watch. 那些骗子卖给我一块假表。
近义词:cheaters, swindlers, fraudsters, con artists
反义词:honest people, trustworthy individuals
词性:noun (plural)


gypper:/ˈɡɪpər/
noun
欺骗者；诈骗者
A person who cheats or swindles others.
That gypper took all my money.  那个骗子拿走了我所有的钱。
近义词: cheat, swindler, fraudster, con artist
反义词: honest person, trustworthy individual
词性:noun


词源:
Likely derived from "gyp," which is possibly a shortened form of "gypsy," historically associated with deceitful practices.  This etymology is considered controversial due to its potentially derogatory nature towards the Roma people.  Another possible origin is from the Dutch word "gippen," meaning "to snatch."
