bosun: /ˈboʊsən/ (US), /ˈbəʊsn/ (UK)| n. | 水手长；甲板长 | A ship's officer in charge of the deck crew and responsible for the maintenance and handling of equipment. | The bosun directed the crew to raise the anchor. 水手长指挥船员起锚。| The bosun piped the crew to work. 水手长吹哨子让船员开始工作。 | synonyms: boatswain, deck officer, first mate | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps landlubber as a humorous contrast) | noun
