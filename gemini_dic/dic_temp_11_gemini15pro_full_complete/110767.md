linguatulida:/lɪŋˌɡwætʃʊˈlaɪdə/ | n. |舌形动物纲|A phylum of parasitic arthropods, commonly known as tongue worms, that infest the respiratory tracts of vertebrates. |例句：Linguatulida are obligate parasites. 舌形动物是专性寄生虫。

例句：The life cycle of Linguatulida is complex and involves intermediate hosts. 舌形动物的生命周期很复杂，涉及中间宿主。|近义词：Pentastomida (an older synonym)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *linguatus* (tongued) +  New Latin *-ida* (suffix forming names of animal classes and orders).  The name refers to the tongue-like shape of the adult parasite.
