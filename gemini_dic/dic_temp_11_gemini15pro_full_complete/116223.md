masons:/ˈmeɪsənz/| n.|石匠；共济会会员|A mason is a person skilled in cutting, shaping, and dressing stone for building.  A mason can also refer to a member of the Freemasons, a fraternal organization.|The masons were busy building the new cathedral. 石匠们正忙着建造新的大教堂。|近义词：bricklayer, stonemason, builder|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
