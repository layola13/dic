blotch:/blɑːtʃ/ /blɒtʃ/| n. v. |污点，斑点；弄脏|A mark or stain, typically one that is large and irregular in shape; to mark or stain with blotches.|The wall was covered in blotches of damp.  墙上满是潮湿的斑点。|He blotched the paper with ink. 他把墨水弄到纸上，留下污渍。|近义词：stain, spot, blemish, mark|反义词：cleanliness, purity|词性:名词, 动词
词源：From Middle English *blocche, from Old French bloche (“lump, block”), of uncertain origin.
