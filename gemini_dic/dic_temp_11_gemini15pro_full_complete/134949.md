nonnarcissisms:/ˌnɑːnˈɑːrsɪsɪzəmz/
n.|非自恋，缺乏自恋|The study focused on nonnarcissisms and its correlation with empathy. 这项研究的重点是非自恋及其与同理心的相关性。|
例句：Her nonnarcissism allows her to truly listen to others. 她的非自恋让她能够真正倾听他人。
例句：His nonnarcissism was refreshing in such a self-absorbed industry. 在这样一个以自我为中心的行业里，他的非自恋令人耳目一新。
近义词：humility, modesty, selflessness|反义词:narcissism, self-centeredness, egotism|词性:名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "narcissism" (from Narcissus, a figure in Greek mythology who fell in love with his own reflection).
