funker's:/ˈfʌŋkərz/ | n. | 无线电操作员的（东西）; (尤指) 无线电员的装备 |  Belonging to or associated with a radio operator, especially their equipment.  |  The soldier grabbed his funker's kit before heading out.  士兵在出发前抓起了他的无线电员装备。 | 近义词: radio operator's, signalman's | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词所有格


词源:
Derived from "funker," a slang term for a radio operator, which itself likely originated from the German word "Funker," meaning "radio operator." The apostrophe-s indicates possessive form.
