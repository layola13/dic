memorable:/ˈmɛmərəbl/ (US), /ˈmɛmrəbl/ (UK)| adj.|值得纪念的；难忘的|Worth remembering or easily remembered, especially because of being special or unusual.|That was a memorable day for me. 那对我来说是难忘的一天。|近义词：unforgettable, noteworthy, significant|反义词：forgettable, insignificant, unremarkable|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin memorabilis, from memorare ‘bring to mind’, from memor ‘mindful’.
