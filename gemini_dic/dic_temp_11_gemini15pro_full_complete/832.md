abstinently:/æbˈstɪnəntli/
adverb
禁欲地；节制地
In a manner characterized by abstaining, especially from alcohol.  Characterized by or showing abstinence, especially from alcohol.
He lived abstinently, avoiding all forms of indulgence.  他过着禁欲的生活，避免一切形式的放纵。
Near synonyms: temperately, moderately, soberly
Antonyms: intemperately, immoderately, excessively
词性: adverb (副词)
