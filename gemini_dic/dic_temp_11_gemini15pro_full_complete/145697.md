overcorrupts:/ˌoʊvərˈkərʌpts/
verb
过度腐蚀；极度败坏
To corrupt excessively or to an extreme degree.  It implies a level of corruption beyond the usual or expected.
The dictator overcorrupted the once-noble institution, turning it into a tool for his own personal gain.  独裁者极度败坏了这个曾经高尚的机构，把它变成了自己谋取私利的工具。

The constant influx of dark money has overcorrupted the political system, making it unresponsive to the needs of the people.  源源不断的黑金过度腐蚀了政治体系，使其对人民的需求毫无反应。

近义词:  debase, degrade, pervert, subvert
反义词:  purify, cleanse, uplift, ennoble
词性: verb
