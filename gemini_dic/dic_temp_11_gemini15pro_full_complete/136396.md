nonresuscitable:/ˌnɒnriːˈsʌsɪtəbl/ /ˌnɑːnriːˈsʌsɪtəbl/| adj.|不可复苏的，不进行心肺复苏的|Not able to be resuscitated; not appropriate for resuscitation efforts.  This usually refers to patients with a medical condition so severe that resuscitation attempts would be futile or unwanted.|The patient was deemed nonresuscitable due to their advanced stage of illness.|近义词：terminal, moribund, incurable|反义词:resuscitable, treatable|词性:adjective

nonresuscitable:/ˌnɒnriːˈsʌsɪtəbl/ /ˌnɑːnriːˈsʌsɪtəbl/| n.|不可复苏的病人|A patient deemed not appropriate for resuscitation efforts.|The hospital’s policy on nonresuscitables was clearly outlined.|近义词：terminal patient, moribund patient|反义词:patient eligible for resuscitation|词性:noun
