green-lit:/ˈɡriːnˌlɪt/ (US) /ˈɡriːnˌlɪt/ (UK)| adj. |批准的，获准进行的|To give permission for something to start, especially a movie or play.  It comes from the idea of a green light signaling "go".|The project was finally green-lit after months of waiting.  这个项目在数月的等待后终于获得批准。|近义词：approved, authorized, sanctioned|反义词：rejected, refused, vetoed|词性: 形容词

green-light:/ˈɡriːnˌlaɪt/ (US) /ˈɡriːnˌlaɪt/ (UK)| v. |批准，准许|To give permission for something to proceed.|The studio green-lit the sequel after the success of the first film.  第一部电影成功后，工作室批准了续集的拍摄。|近义词：approve, authorize, sanction|反义词：reject, refuse, veto|词性: 动词

词源:  Compound of "green" and "light," referencing the traffic signal indicating permission to proceed.
