banshee:/ˈbænʃiː/| n.|女妖，报丧女妖|A female spirit in Irish folklore whose wailing warns of impending death in a family.|The banshee's cries echoed through the valley. 女妖的哭声在山谷中回荡。|Synonyms: wraith, phantom, specter|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a supernatural being, could use "living being" or "mortal")|Noun
