club:/klʌb/| n. v.|俱乐部，社团，棍棒，球棒|A group of people who meet regularly for a shared interest, or the building they meet in.  To hit with a heavy object.|He joined the photography club. 他加入了摄影俱乐部。|She clubbed the attacker with a branch. 她用树枝击打了袭击者。|近义词：society, association, group, stick, bat|反义词: individual|noun, verb
