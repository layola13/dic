nonpurification:/nɒnˌpjʊərɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/
noun
非净化，未净化
The lack of purification; the state of being not purified.  This refers to something that has not undergone a process of cleansing or refining.
The nonpurification of the water supply led to an outbreak of illness.  未净化的供水导致了疾病的爆发。
The experiment demonstrated the effects of nonpurification on the sample.  该实验演示了未净化对样品的影响。
Near synonyms:  contamination, impurity, uncleanliness
Antonyms: purification, cleansing, refinement
n.
