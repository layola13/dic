bolis:/ˈboʊlis/| n. |冰棍，雪糕 (尤指墨西哥或中美洲)|A frozen dessert, typically made of fruit juice, water, and sugar, often sold by street vendors, especially in Mexico and Central America.|Example: The children cooled off with refreshing bolis on a hot summer day. 孩子们在炎热的夏日吃着清爽的冰棍来消暑。|Example:  I bought a mango boli from the street vendor. 我从街头小贩那里买了一根芒果冰棍。|近义词：popsicle, ice pop, paleta|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "hot soup" for contrast)|词性:noun
