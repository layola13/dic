gratifications: /ˌɡrætɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/
noun
满足感；令人满足的事物
The act of gratifying or the state of being gratified; satisfaction.
She found great gratification in helping others. 她从帮助别人中获得了极大的满足感。
Synonyms: satisfaction, pleasure, contentment, fulfillment, enjoyment
Antonyms: dissatisfaction, displeasure, discontentment, frustration
n.
