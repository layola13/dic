n.

The state of being fearless and resolute; intrepidity.
She showed remarkable dauntlessness in facing the challenge. 她面对挑战表现出非凡的勇气。
近义词: bravery, courage, fearlessness, intrepidity, boldness

词源: From dauntless + -ness.  Dauntless itself comes from the Old French *danter (“to subdue, tame”), ultimately from Latin domitare (“to tame”).
