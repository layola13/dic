burden:/ˈbɜːrdən/ (US) /ˈbɜːdn/ (UK)| n. vt.|负担，重担；负荷；责任；义务|A burden is a heavy load, often a difficult responsibility or duty.|She carried the burden of guilt for years. 她多年来背负着负罪感。|The donkey struggled under its heavy burden. 驴子在重担下挣扎。|近义词: load, weight, responsibility, obligation|反义词: relief, advantage, asset|名词,动词

burden:/ˈbɜːrdən/ (US) /ˈbɜːdn/ (UK)| vt.|使负担；加重压于；烦扰；劳累|To burden someone is to weigh them down with a heavy load, either physically or mentally.|He was burdened with debt. 他背负着债务。|I don't want to burden you with my problems. 我不想用我的问题来烦扰你。|近义词: load, weigh down, encumber, oppress|反义词: unburden, relieve, ease|动词
