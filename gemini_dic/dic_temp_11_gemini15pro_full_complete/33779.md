circumambient:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈæmbiənt/| adj.|周围的，环绕的|Surrounding; encompassing on all sides.  Derived from Latin "circum" (around) + "ambiens" (going around).|The circumambient air was thick with the smell of pine needles. 周围的空气中弥漫着松针的气味。|Synonyms: surrounding, encompassing, ambient, environing|Antonyms: central, internal|adjective
