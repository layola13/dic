elevatory: /ˈɛləvətɔːri/ (US), /ˈɛlɪvətəri/ (UK)
adjective
提升的；举起的；升高的
tending to elevate; elevating.  Relating to or producing elevation.
The elevatory muscles of the jaw allow us to chew. 颚部的提升肌让我们能够咀嚼。
The platform had an elevatory mechanism to raise heavy loads. 这个平台有一个升降机构来提升重物。
synonyms: raising, lifting, hoisting, elevating
antonyms: lowering, depressing, sinking
词性: 形容词
