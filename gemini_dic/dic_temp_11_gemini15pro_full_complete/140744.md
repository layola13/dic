omniscient:/ɒmˈnɪsiənt/ /ɑːmˈnɪʃiənt/| adj.|无所不知的，全知的|knowing everything|The narrator in the story is omniscient, able to access the thoughts and feelings of all the characters.|例句：The omniscient narrator knew the thoughts of every character. 全知的叙述者知道每个角色的想法。

例句：God is often described as omniscient and omnipotent. 上帝常被描述为无所不知、无所不能的。|近义词：all-knowing, all-seeing|反义词:ignorant, unknowing|词性:adjective

omniscience:/ɒmˈnɪsiəns/ /ɑːmˈnɪʃiəns/| n.|无所不知，全知|the state of knowing everything|The novel explores the theme of omniscience and its implications.|例句：The concept of divine omniscience is central to many religions. 神圣全知的概念是许多宗教的核心。

例句：The story's omniscience allows the reader to understand the motivations of all the characters. 故事的无所不知让读者能够理解所有角色的动机。|近义词：all-knowingness|反义词:ignorance, unawareness|词性:noun
