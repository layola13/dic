misassign:/ˌmɪsəˈsaɪn/ | vt. |错误地分配；指派错误|To assign incorrectly or to the wrong person or place. | The teacher misassigned the students to the wrong classes. 老师把学生错误地分到了班级。

The funds were misassigned due to a clerical error.  由于文书错误，资金被错误分配了。| 近义词: misallocate, misdirect | 反义词: assign correctly, allocate correctly | 词性: 动词
