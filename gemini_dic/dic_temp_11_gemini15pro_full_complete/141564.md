oppositenesses:/ˌɑːpəzɪtnɪsɪz/| n. |对立性，相反性|The oppositenesses of their views made it difficult to reach a compromise.  他们的观点截然相反，使得很难达成妥协。|例句：The painting explores the oppositenesses of light and darkness. 这幅画探索了光明与黑暗的对立。|近义词：contrasts, differences, polarities, dichotomies|反义词：similarities, likenesses|词性:名词

Etymology:
From opposite + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  "Opposite" comes from Old French "opposit," ultimately from Latin "oppositus," past participle of "opponere" (to place against).
