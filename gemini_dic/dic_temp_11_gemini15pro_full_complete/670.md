abruptly: /əˈbrʌptli/ (US), /əˈbrʌptli/ (UK) | adv. | 突然地，唐突地 |  Suddenly and unexpectedly; in a short and brusque manner. | The bus stopped abruptly, throwing the passengers forward. 公共汽车突然停下，乘客向前倾倒。

The meeting ended abruptly without any explanation. 会议突然结束，没有任何解释。


近义词: suddenly, unexpectedly, sharply, hastily

反义词: gradually, slowly, smoothly, gently

词性: adverb

词源: From the Latin word "abruptus," past participle of "abrumpere" (to break off).
