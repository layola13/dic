jumpsuit: /ˈdʒʌmpsuːt/ | n. | 连体衣，连身裤 | A one-piece garment of trousers and a bodice. | She wore a stylish denim jumpsuit. 她穿了一件时髦的牛仔连体裤。| 近义词: overall, playsuit, boiler suit | 反义词: (No direct antonym, consider separates like shirt and trousers) | noun

词源:  From jump + suit.  Originally designed for parachutists (jumping from planes).
