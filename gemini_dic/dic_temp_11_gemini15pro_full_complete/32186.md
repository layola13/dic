chiasmus: /kaɪˈæzməs/ | n. | 交错配列法，交错结构 | A rhetorical or literary figure in which words, grammatical constructions, or concepts are repeated in reverse order, in the same or a modified form. | Example 1: "Ask not what your country can do for you; ask what you can do for your country."  问问你的国家能为你做什么，问问你能为你的国家做什么。

Example 2: "Never let a fool kiss you or a kiss fool you."  永远不要让傻瓜吻你，也不要让吻愚弄你。 | synonym: antimetabole, inversion | antonym: N/A | noun
