northeastward:/ˌnɔːrθˈiːst.wərd/ | adv., adj.|向东北, 东北方的|Moving or situated in a direction between north and east. |The birds flew northeastward. 鸟儿向东北方向飞去。|近义词：northeasterly|反义词:southwestward|词性:副词，形容词
Etymology: From north + east + -ward (Old English -weard, meaning "toward").
