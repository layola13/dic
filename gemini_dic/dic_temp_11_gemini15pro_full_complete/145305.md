overbrief:/ˌoʊvərˈbriːf/
verb
过度介绍；过分详细地指示
To brief someone excessively, providing too much information or instruction, often to the point of confusion or inefficiency.
The lawyer overbriefed his client, causing him to become overwhelmed and unsure of his testimony. 律师对他的客户过度介绍案情，导致他不知所措，不确定自己的证词。
overinform, overinstruct
underbrief
v.
