duality:/duːˈælɪti/| n.|二元性，二象性，对偶性|The duality of human nature encompasses both good and evil. 人性的二元性包含善与恶。|例句：The novel explores the duality of love and hate. 这部小说探讨了爱与恨的二元性。

例句：Light exhibits a duality, behaving as both a wave and a particle. 光表现出二元性，既像波又像粒子。|近义词：dichotomy, polarity, ambivalence|反义词：unity, singularity|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin dualitas, from dualis "containing two," from duo "two."
