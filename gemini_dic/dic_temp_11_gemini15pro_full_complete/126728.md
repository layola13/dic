namoto:/nɑːˈmoʊtoʊ/ | n. |  合十礼，问候 | A traditional Japanese greeting, often accompanied by a slight bow.  It signifies respect and gratitude. | Example 1:  He greeted the monks with a deep namaste. 他深深地鞠躬向僧侣们致敬。

Example 2:  She offered a namaste at the end of the yoga class. 瑜伽课结束时，她行了一个合十礼。 | Synonym: greeting, bow, salutation | Antonym:  disrespect, insult | noun

注意事项:
“Namaste” 来源于梵语，虽然拼写类似，但和日语没有关系。它在西方世界通常与瑜伽练习联系在一起。
