folktales:/ˈfoʊkteɪlz/ | n. |民间故事；民间传说|Traditional stories passed down through generations, often with moral lessons or explanations of natural phenomena. |My grandmother used to tell me fascinating folktales before bedtime. 我的祖母过去常常在睡前给我讲迷人的民间故事。| Folktales often feature magical creatures and heroic deeds. 民间故事通常以神奇的生物和英雄事迹为特色。| synonyms: legends, myths, fables, lore | antonyms: history, nonfiction | noun
