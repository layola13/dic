amylate: /ˈæmɪleɪt/ | n. v. | 淀粉酶;  淀粉化 |  A digestive enzyme, found chiefly in saliva and pancreatic fluid, that converts starch and glycogen into simple sugars. To treat or convert with amylase. | Saliva contains amylase, which breaks down starch. 唾液含有淀粉酶，可以分解淀粉。 |  Amylase is used in the production of certain foods. 淀粉酶用于某些食品的生产。 |Synonyms: diastase, ptyalin | Antonyms: None | n.
amylate: /ˈæmɪleɪt/ | n. v. | 淀粉; 使淀粉化 | Starch. To convert into starch. |  The amylate content of the flour was high. 面粉的淀粉含量很高。 | The process amylates the grain. 这个过程使谷物淀粉化。 | Synonyms: starch, farina | Antonyms: None | v.
