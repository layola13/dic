animals:/ˈænɪməlz/ | n. | 动物 | Living creatures that are not plants, typically able to move independently and possessing specialized sense organs. |  例句：Many different animals live in the forest. 许多不同的动物生活在森林里。| 近义词：creatures, beasts, fauna | 反义词: plants | noun

animals:/ˈænɪməlz/ | n. | 兽性 | The animal instincts or qualities of human beings. | 例句：The animals in him took over, and he lashed out in anger. 他体内的兽性占据了上风，他愤怒地猛烈抨击。| 近义词：instincts, nature, savagery | 反义词: reason, intellect | noun
