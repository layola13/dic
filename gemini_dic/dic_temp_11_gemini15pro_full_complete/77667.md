gelidnesses:/dʒɪˈlɪdnɪsɪz/
|n.|极寒，冰冷|Extreme coldness; iciness. |The gelidnesses of the arctic winter made survival a constant struggle. 北极冬天的极度寒冷使得生存成为一场持续的斗争。|The prolonged gelidnesses of the ice age reshaped the landscape. 冰河时代持续的严寒重塑了地貌。|近义词: frigidities, icinesses, coldness|反义词: warmths, heats|词性:名词

词源: From gelid (extremely cold) + -ness (suffix forming nouns of quality or state).  Gelid ultimately comes from Latin *gelidus, from gelu "frost, ice."
