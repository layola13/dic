endorsee:/ɪnˌdɔːrˈsiː/ /ɪnˌdɔːrˈsiː/
n.
被背书人；受让人
The person or entity to whom a check, bill of exchange, or other negotiable instrument is endorsed and transferred.
The check was made payable to John, who then became the endorser when he signed it over to Mary, the endorsee. 这张支票的收款人是约翰，当他签字转让给玛丽时，他成为了背书人，玛丽成为了被背书人。
beneficiary, recipient, assignee
endorser, payer
noun
