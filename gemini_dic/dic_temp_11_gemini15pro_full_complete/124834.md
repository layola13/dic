muckiness:/ˈmʌkɪnɪs/
noun
污秽；泥泞；肮脏
The state of being mucky; dirtiness, filthiness, or stickiness.
The muckiness of the stable floor made it difficult to walk. 马厩地板的泥泞使得行走困难。
近义词:dirtiness, filthiness, grime, messiness, squalor
反义词:cleanliness, tidiness, neatness
词性:n.

muck:/mʌk/
noun, verb
n. 粪便；肥料；污物；淤泥；腐殖质
v. 清理粪便；施肥；弄脏
From Middle English *muk, from Old Norse myki (“dung, manure”), from Proto-Germanic *mukiz, *muką (“heap”), from Proto-Indo-European *meug- (“slippery, slimy”).
n. The stable was filled with muck. 马厩里充满了粪便.
v.  I had to muck out the stables before riding.  在骑马之前，我不得不清理马厩。
近义词:(n.) dirt, filth, grime, manure, mire; (v.) soil, dirty
反义词:(n.) cleanliness; (v.) clean
词性:n., v.

-iness:/ɪnɪs/
suffix
…的状态；…的性质
Forms nouns of quality or state from adjectives, such as happiness from happy.
The darkness of the room was unsettling. 房间的黑暗令人不安。
近义词: -ity, -ness
反义词: None
词性:suffix
