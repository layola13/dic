injury:/ˈɪndʒəri/ | n. | 伤害，损害；受伤处 | An act or instance that causes harm or damage.  | He suffered a serious injury in the car accident. 他在车祸中受了重伤。|  Synonyms: wound, harm, damage, trauma | Antonyms: healing, recovery | noun

injury:/ˈɪndʒəri/ | vt. | 伤害，损害 | To cause harm or damage to. | The scandal injured his reputation.  这桩丑闻损害了他的名誉。 | Synonyms: harm, damage, impair, hurt | Antonyms: heal, repair, benefit | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *injure* (Modern French injure "insult, offense, wrong"), from Latin injuria "wrong, injustice, insult," from in- "not, opposite of" + jus "right, law" (from PIE root *yewes- "law").  The meaning "physical harm" developed in Middle English.
