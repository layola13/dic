interclasping:/ˌɪntərˈklɑːspɪŋ/
adjective
互相扣紧的，交握的
Clasping or holding each other tightly; intertwined.
The lovers walked hand in hand, their fingers interclasping. 恋人们手牵着手走着，手指紧紧相扣。
近义词: intertwined, interlocked, interwoven
反义词: separated, unclasped
词性: adjective

interclasping:/ˌɪntərˈklɑːspɪŋ/
noun
互相扣紧，交握
The act of clasping or holding each other tightly.
The interclasping of their hands symbolized their unity.  他们双手的交握象征着他们的团结。
近义词: embrace, clasp, grip
反义词: release, separation
词性: noun

词源:  From inter- + clasping, present participle of clasp.
