megastructures:/ˈmɛɡəˌstrʌktʃərz/
|n.| 巨型建筑，巨型结构 |Extremely large artificial structures, often hypothetical, of immense size and scale, typically larger than a planet or star.| Scientists have theorized about megastructures like Dyson spheres that could harness the energy of a star. 科学家们对戴森球之类的巨型结构进行了理论研究，这种结构可以利用恒星的能量。

|例句：The concept of megastructures captures the imagination. 巨型建筑的概念激发了人们的想象力。|
|例句：Building megastructures would require advanced engineering and vast resources. 建造巨型建筑需要先进的工程技术和巨大的资源。|

|近义词：massive constructions, colossal structures, immense structures|
|反义词：microstructures, miniature structures|
|词性：Noun|
