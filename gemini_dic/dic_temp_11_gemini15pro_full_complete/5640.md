altimeters: /ˈæltɪˌmiːtərz/ | n. |高度计，测高仪|An instrument that measures altitude, especially of an aircraft above sea level or above ground level.|The altimeters in the cockpit showed the plane was rapidly losing altitude. 飞机驾驶舱里的高度计显示飞机正在迅速下降。|Synonyms:  hypsometer, altitude meter|Antonyms: depth gauge | noun
