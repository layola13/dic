chromophore:/ˈkrəʊməfɔːr/ /ˈkroʊməfɔːr/| n. |发色团，显色团|A chemical group that absorbs light and imparts color to a molecule.  A part of a molecule responsible for its color.|例句：The chromophore in chlorophyll absorbs light energy for photosynthesis. 叶绿素中的发色团吸收光能用于光合作用。

例句：Different chromophores absorb different wavelengths of light, resulting in different colors. 不同的发色团吸收不同波长的光，导致不同的颜色。|近义词：pigment, colorant|反义词:None|词性:noun


Etymology->
From Greek *khrōmophóros*  *khrôma* "color" + *-phoros* "-bearing," from *pherein* "to bear."
