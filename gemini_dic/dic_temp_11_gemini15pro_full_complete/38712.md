condignly:/kənˈdaɪnli/| adv.|适当地；应得地；罪有应得地|Deservedly; appropriately, especially of punishment.  Meaning the punishment fits the crime.|He was condignly punished for his crimes. 他因罪行受到应有的惩罚。|近义词：deservedly, justly, appropriately, fittingly|反义词：undeservedly, unjustly, inappropriately|词性: 副词

词源:
From Late Latin *condignus, from Latin con- ‘together with’ + dignus ‘worthy’.
