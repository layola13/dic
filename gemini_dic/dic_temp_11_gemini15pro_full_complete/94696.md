improbative:/ɪmˈprɒbətɪv/ | adj. | 无证据力的，不提供证据的 | Not tending to prove or disprove; not furnishing evidence. | The lawyer argued that the witness's testimony was improbative and should be disregarded. 律师辩称证人的证词没有证据力，应该忽略。|  The detective dismissed the improbative clue.  侦探排除了这条没有证据力的线索。 | Synonyms: inconclusive, irrelevant, uninformative | Antonyms: probative, conclusive, evidentiary | adjective
