gassiest:/ˈɡæsiɪst/
adjective
最多气的；充满气的
Containing or full of gas.  More gaseous than anything else.
例句：The gassiest planet in our solar system is Jupiter.  太阳系中气体最多的行星是木星。

例句：The room was the gassiest I'd ever been in, after someone lit a match near the leaky stove.  在有人在漏气的炉子附近点燃火柴后，房间里充满了气体，这是我从未经历过的。
近义词：airiest, fluffiest
反义词：densest, solidest
词性: adjective
