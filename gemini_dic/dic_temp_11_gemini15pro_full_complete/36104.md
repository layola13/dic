coequality:/ˌkoʊɪˈkwɒləti/ | n. | 平等，同等地位 | The principle of coequality emphasizes that all individuals, regardless of their background, should have the same rights and opportunities.  平等原则强调所有个人，无论其背景如何，都应享有相同的权利和机会。 | 例句：The coequality of the two partners was essential to the success of the business.  两位合作伙伴的平等地位对业务的成功至关重要。

例句：The constitution guarantees the coequality of all citizens before the law. 宪法保障所有公民在法律面前的平等。 | 近义词：equality, parity, equivalence | 反义词：inequality, disparity, inferiority | 词性: noun

Etymology: From co- (together, mutual) + equality (the state of being equal).
