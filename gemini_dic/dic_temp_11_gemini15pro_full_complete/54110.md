disembrace:/ˌdɪsɪmˈbreɪs/| v. |放弃，抛弃（信仰、事业等）|To abandon or reject (a belief, cause, or way of life).|例句：He disembraced his former religious beliefs. 他放弃了他以前的宗教信仰。

例句：She disembraced the corporate world to pursue her artistic passions. 她放弃了企业界去追求她的艺术热情。|近义词：abandon, reject, renounce, forsake, relinquish|反义词：embrace, adopt, espouse|词性:动词
