例句2：They played a few games of gammons. 他们玩了几局西洋双陆棋。

例句3：He's talking gammons! 他在胡说八道！|近义词：backgammon (for the game), ham, bacon (for the meat), nonsense, rubbish|反义词：sense (for nonsense/rubbish)|词性：名词
