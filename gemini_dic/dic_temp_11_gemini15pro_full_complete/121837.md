misremembering:/ˌmɪsrɪˈmɛmbərɪŋ/
|n.|记错，记不清|The act of remembering something incorrectly.|I have a misremembering of where I put my keys. 我记不清我把钥匙放哪儿了.|misrecollection, faulty memory|correct recollection, accurate memory|noun

misremember:/ˌmɪsrɪˈmɛmbər/
|v.|记错，记不清|To remember something incorrectly.|I misremembered the date of the meeting. 我记错了会议的日期.|misrecollect, misrecall|remember correctly, recall accurately|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- + remember.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Old English origin meaning "wrongly," "badly," or "incorrectly." "Remember" comes from Old French "remembrer," ultimately from Latin "rememorari" meaning "to call to mind."
