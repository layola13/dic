misfashioned:/ˌmɪsˈfæʃənd/
adjective
畸形的，拙劣的，式样不好的
badly or awkwardly made or shaped; deformed
例句：The dress was misfashioned and ill-fitting. 这件衣服做得很难看，而且不合身。

例句：He had a misfashioned limb due to a childhood injury. 由于童年受伤，他的一条腿畸形了。

近义词：deformed, malformed, misshapen, distorted, grotesque
反义词：well-formed, shapely, symmetrical, perfect

词性:adjective
