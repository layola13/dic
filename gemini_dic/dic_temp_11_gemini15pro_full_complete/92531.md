hypozoic:/ˌhaɪpəˈzoʊɪk/
adjective
下生命的，生命出现以前的
Relating to or denoting the period in the earth's history before the first appearance of life.
The hypozoic era predates the Archean era. 下生命代先于太古代。
Precambrian, lifeless
Living, biological
