convolution:/ˌkɒnvəˈluːʃn/ /ˌkɑːnvəˈluːʃn/| n. |卷积，盘绕，回旋，（尤指肠的）盘绕 |A convolution is a coiled shape or form. In mathematics, it's a function derived from two given functions by integration that expresses how the shape of one is modified by the other.|The intestines have many convolutions.|近义词：coil, twist, spiral, winding|反义词:straightening|词性:noun

convolution:/ˌkɒnvəˈluːʃn/ /ˌkɑːnvəˈluːʃn/| n. |（故事或论证的）复杂性，错综复杂| Something that is convoluted is extremely complex and difficult to follow.| The plot of the movie was so convoluted that I couldn't understand it.|近义词：complexity, intricacy, complication|反义词:simplicity|词性:noun
