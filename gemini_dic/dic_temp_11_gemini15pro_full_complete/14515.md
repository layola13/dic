aventine:/ˈævənˌtaɪn/ | n. | 阿文廷山，阿文廷 | One of the seven hills of Rome; also, a secluded or aristocratic place. |  The Aventine Hill offers stunning views of the city. 阿文廷山可以欣赏到城市的美景。| synonyms: hill, mount, elevation | antonyms: valley, depression | noun

aventine:/ˈævənˌtaɪn/ | adj. | 阿文廷的，隐蔽的，贵族的 |  He lived in an aventine neighborhood, far from the bustling city center. 他住在一个隐蔽的社区，远离喧嚣的市中心。 | synonyms: secluded, exclusive, aristocratic | antonyms: public, common, accessible | adjective
