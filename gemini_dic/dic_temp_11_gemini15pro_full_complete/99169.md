interchoking:/ˌɪntərˈtʃoʊkɪŋ/ | adj. |互相阻塞的，互相扼制的 |  The term "interchoking" describes a situation where two or more things are blocking or obstructing each other, often in a way that restricts flow or movement. It is formed from "inter-" (between) and "choking" (obstructing the flow of). It's a less common word, often used in technical contexts. | The interchoking vines made it impossible to pass through the forest. 互相阻塞的藤蔓使得穿过森林成为不可能。 |  Interfering, obstructing, hindering | Facilitating, aiding, assisting | adjective
