impeachable:/ɪmˈpiːtʃəbəl/ | adj. | 可弹劾的，可指责的 | Subject to impeachment; capable of being accused of a crime, especially a crime done while holding a public office.  | The president is impeachable for high crimes and misdemeanors. 总统因严重罪行和轻罪而可能遭到弹劾。 | The governor's actions were deemed impeachable. 州长的行为被认为是可以弹劾的。 |  Synonyms: accusable, indictable, chargeable | Antonyms: unimpeachable, blameless, irreproachable | word property: adjective
