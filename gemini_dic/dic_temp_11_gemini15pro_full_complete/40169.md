contentation:/ˌkɒntɛnˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 满足；满意；争论；争辩 | a state of satisfaction or happiness; the act of arguing or disputing |  He expressed his contentment with the outcome. 他对结果表示满意。 The two groups engaged in fierce contention over the land rights.  这两个团体就土地权进行了激烈的争论。 | 近义词：satisfaction, happiness, pleasure, contentment, dispute, argument, controversy | 反义词：dissatisfaction, unhappiness, displeasure, disagreement | 词性: 名词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French, or from late Latin contentatio(n-), from the verb contentare ‘make content’.
