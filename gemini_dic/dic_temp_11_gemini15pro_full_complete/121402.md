misidentified:/ˌmɪsɪˈdɛntɪfaɪd/ | v. | 误认，识别错误 | To incorrectly identify someone or something. | The police misidentified the suspect and arrested the wrong person. 警方误认了嫌疑人，逮捕了错误的人。| The bird was initially misidentified as a sparrow. 这只鸟最初被误认为是麻雀。 | 近义词: mislabeled, mistaken, misconstrued | 反义词: correctly identified, recognized | 词性: 动词

词源:
mis- (prefix): meaning "wrong" or "badly" (from Old English)
identify (verb): from Latin "identifico," meaning "to make identical," from "idem" (same) + "facio" (to make).
