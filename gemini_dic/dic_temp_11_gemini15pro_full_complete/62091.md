词源：
From Ancient Greek ἐγκέφαλος (enképhalos, “brain”), from ἐν (en, “in”) + κεφαλή (kephalḗ, “head”).


注意事项：
*  "Encephal-"  is a combining form used in many medical terms related to the brain.
* While "encephalous" exists, it is less commonly used than related terms like "cerebral" or "cranial."  "Encephala" (plural of encephalon) is more commonly used when referring to the physical brain itself.
* The pronunciation provided is a common American English pronunciation. British English pronunciation may vary slightly.
