compostela:/ˌkɒmpɒˈstɛlə/ | n. | 圣地亚哥-德孔波斯特拉；(前往该地的)朝圣证书 | A certificate of pilgrimage to Santiago de Compostela. | 例句：She proudly displayed her compostela after completing the Camino de Santiago. 她完成圣地亚哥朝圣之路后，自豪地展示了她的朝圣证书。

例句：He hoped to receive his compostela at the cathedral. 他希望在大教堂收到他的朝圣证书。| 近义词：certificate, credential | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *Compostella, from Vulgar Latin *Campus Stellae ("field of the star"), referring to a supposed miraculous appearance of Saint James's tomb.
