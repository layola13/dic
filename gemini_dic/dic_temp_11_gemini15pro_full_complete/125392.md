multinational: /ˌmʌltiˈnæʃənəl/ | adj. n. | 跨国的；跨国公司 | Operating in several countries. |  Example:  Many multinational corporations have their headquarters in the United States. 许多跨国公司总部设在美国。

Example:  She works for a multinational company. 她为一家跨国公司工作。


| 近义词: international, global, transnational, cosmopolitan | 反义词: domestic, national, local | 词性: 形容词, 名词 |

multinational: /ˌmʌltiˈnæʃənəl/ | n. | 跨国公司 | A company operating in several countries. | Example:  Several multinationals are vying for the contract. 几家跨国公司正在争夺这份合同。

Example:  The multinational announced plans to expand into new markets. 这家跨国公司宣布了进军新市场的计划。


| 近义词: conglomerate, corporation | 反义词: small business, local business | 词性: 名词 |
