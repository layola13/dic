disseminate:/dɪˈsɛmɪneɪt/
|v.|传播，散布，宣传|to spread or give out something, especially news, information, ideas, etc., to a lot of people|The internet allows us to disseminate information quickly and easily.  互联网使我们能够快速便捷地传播信息。|近义词：spread, distribute, circulate, propagate, broadcast|反义词：suppress, conceal, withhold|verb

Etymology:
From Latin disseminat- ‘sown, scattered’, from the verb disseminare, from dis- ‘widely’ + seminare ‘to sow’.
