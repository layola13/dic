CMYK: /ˌsiːemˈwaɪˈkeɪ/ | n. |  青色、品红色、黄色和黑色 (印刷四分色) |  A color model used in printing, based on the subtractive primary colors cyan, magenta, yellow, and key (black). | The brochure was printed using CMYK inks. 这本小册子是用CMYK油墨印刷的。| Synonym: process color | Antonym: RGB | Noun
