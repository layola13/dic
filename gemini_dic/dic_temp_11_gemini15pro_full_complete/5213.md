alignment: /əˈlaɪnmənt/ | n. | 对准，校准；排列；联盟，联合 | An arrangement in which two or more things are positioned in a straight line or parallel to each other.  The state of being aligned or aligned with something.  A position of agreement or alliance. | The wheels need alignment. 车轮需要校准。|  The countries formed an alignment against the common enemy. 这些国家结成了对抗共同敌人的联盟。 | synonyms: arrangement, positioning, adjustment, alliance, coalition | antonyms: misalignment, disagreement, disunity | noun
