cultus:/ˈkʌltəs/ | n. | 崇拜；祭祀；宗教仪式；教派 |A system of religious veneration and devotion directed toward a particular figure or object. | The ancient Romans had a complex cultus surrounding their gods. 古代罗马人围绕他们的神灵有一套复杂的宗教仪式。| 近义词: worship, reverence, veneration, adoration | 反义词: blasphemy, sacrilege, irreverence | noun

cultus:/ˈkʌltəs/ | adj. |耕作的，栽培的 | Cultivated; tilled. (Rarely used) | The cultus fields yielded a bountiful harvest. 耕作的田地获得了丰收。(很少使用) | 近义词: cultivated, tilled | 反义词: uncultivated, fallow | adjective
