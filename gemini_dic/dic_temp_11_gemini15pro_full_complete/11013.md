arborized:/ˈɑːrbəraɪzd/| adj.|树枝状的，分叉的|Resembling a tree in form or arrangement; branching.|The arborized pattern of the frost on the window was beautiful. 窗户上霜冻的树枝状图案很美。|Synonyms: branched, dendritic, ramified, tree-like|Antonyms: unbranched, linear, straight|adjective

arborize:/ˈɑːrbəraɪz/| v.|使成树枝状，分叉|To cause to resemble a tree in form; to branch out.|The surgeon arborized the blood vessels in the patient's leg.  外科医生使病人的腿部血管分叉。|Synonyms: branch, ramify, spread out|Antonyms: converge, consolidate|verb

词源: From Latin *arborizāre, from arbor "tree".
