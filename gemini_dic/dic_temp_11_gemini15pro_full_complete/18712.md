bereavements:/bɪˈriːvmənts/| n.|丧失亲友，丧亲之痛|The family suffered multiple bereavements in a short period. 这个家庭在短时间内经历了多次丧亲之痛。|例句：She struggled to cope with the bereavements of both her parents. 她难以承受失去双亲的痛苦。

The recent bereavements have left him feeling lost and alone.  最近的丧亲之痛让他感到失落和孤独。|近义词：losses, griefs, sorrows|反义词:joys, gains|词性:名词


Etymology:
From bereave + -ment.  Bereave comes from Old English *berēafian, "to deprive of," from be- + rēafian, "to rob, plunder."
