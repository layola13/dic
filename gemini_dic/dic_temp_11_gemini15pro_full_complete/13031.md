astrally:/ˈæstrəli/| adv.|星体投射地，精神上地|Existing or happening outside your body, as if your spirit has left it.  Relating to or emanating from the stars; stellar.|例句：She claimed to have visited him astrally. 她声称曾以星体投射的方式拜访过他。

例句：He felt astrally connected to the universe. 他感觉在精神上与宇宙相连。|近义词：spiritually, psychically, mentally|反义词：physically, corporeally|词性:副词
