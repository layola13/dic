diapasm: /ˈdaɪəˌpæzəm/ | n. |滞育; 休眠; |A period of suspended development in an insect, other invertebrate, or plant embryo, especially during unfavorable environmental conditions.| Many insects undergo diapause during the winter. 许多昆虫在冬天进行滞育。|Synonyms: dormancy, quiescence, inactivity|Antonyms: activity, growth, development|noun

diapasm: /ˈdaɪəˌpæzəm/ | n. |停滞期; 中止; |A temporary stop in a process.|The negotiations reached a diapause.  谈判陷入了停滞。|Synonyms: pause, hiatus, interruption|Antonyms: continuation, progression, advance|noun
