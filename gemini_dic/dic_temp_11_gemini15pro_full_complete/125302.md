multigenerous:/ˌmʌltiˈdʒɛnərəs/| adj.|多代同堂的，跨代的|Spanning or involving several generations.|例句：Multigenerous households are becoming more common due to rising housing costs. 由于住房成本上升，多代同堂的家庭变得越来越普遍。

例句：The family reunion was a multigenerous affair, with attendees ranging from great-grandparents to toddlers. 家庭聚会是一个跨代的活动，从曾祖父母到蹒跚学步的孩子都有参加。|近义词：intergenerational, cross-generational|反义词: N/A |词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "multi-" (meaning "many, multiple") + "generous" (relating to a generation).  The term is a relatively recent coinage, likely arising in the late 20th or early 21st century to describe the growing trend of multigenerational living.
