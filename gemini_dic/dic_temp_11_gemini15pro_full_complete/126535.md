nabobisms:/ˈneɪbɒbɪzəmz/ | n. | 奢华，炫耀，暴发户作风 |  Extravagant behavior or display of wealth, reminiscent of a nabob.  Derived from "nabob," referring to a person, especially a European, who made a fortune in India or elsewhere in the East, often associated with lavish spending. |  His nabobisms were evident in his collection of luxury cars and designer clothes. 他的奢靡作风体现在他收藏的豪车和名牌服装上。 |  Synonyms: extravagance, ostentation, lavishness, profligacy | Antonyms: frugality, modesty, simplicity | Noun
