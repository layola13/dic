helicopter:/ˈhelɪkɒptər/ (UK), /ˈhelɪkɑːptər/ (US)| n. |直升机|A type of aircraft that derives both lift and propulsion from one or more sets of horizontally revolving overhead rotors. It can take off and land vertically, hover, and fly forwards, backwards, and sideways.|The helicopter landed on the hospital roof. 直升机降落在医院屋顶上.| Two helicopters circled the stadium. 两架直升机在体育场上空盘旋。 |近义词：chopper, whirlybird|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

etymology: From French hélicoptère, from Greek helix (spiral) + pteron (wing).
