multicultural:/ˌmʌltiˈkʌltʃərəl/| adj.|多元文化的|Relating to or constituting several cultural or ethnic groups within a society.|例句：The city is a multicultural hub, attracting people from all over the world.  这座城市是一个多元文化中心，吸引着来自世界各地的人们。

例句：Multicultural education is becoming increasingly important in today's globalized world.  在当今全球化的世界中，多元文化教育变得越来越重要。|近义词：diverse, cosmopolitan, pluralistic|反义词：homogeneous, monocultural|词性:adjective


multiculturalism:/ˌmʌltiˈkʌltʃərəlɪzəm/| n.|多元文化主义|The presence of, or support for the presence of, several distinct cultural or ethnic groups within a society.|例句：Multiculturalism enriches our community by exposing us to different perspectives. 多元文化主义通过让我们接触不同的观点来丰富我们的社区。

例句：The government promotes multiculturalism through various programs and initiatives. 政府通过各种方案和倡议来促进多元文化主义。|近义词：diversity, pluralism|反义词：monoculturalism|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"multi-" (from Latin "multus" meaning "many, much") + "cultural" (from "culture")
