nonrefundability:/ˌnɒnriːˈfʌndəˈbɪləti/ /ˌnɑːnriːˈfʌndəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可退还性; 不可退款
The quality or state of being nonrefundable.  It refers to something, such as a payment or deposit, that cannot be returned.
The nonrefundability of the deposit was clearly stated in the contract.  押金的不可退还性在合同中已明确说明。
non-returnable, non-reimbursable
refundable, returnable
noun
