certificating:/sərˈtɪfɪkeɪtɪŋ/
|v.|证明；授予证书|The process of providing or receiving a certificate.  This word is less common than "certifying".|They are certificating the new software. 他们在为新软件提供认证。|近义词：certifying, licensing, accrediting|反义词:invalidating, revoking|verb


certificating:/sərˈtɪfɪkeɪtɪŋ/
|adj.|认证的；授予证书的|Relating to the process of certification. This usage is less common than the verb form.|The certificating authority issued the document. 认证机构签发了该文件。|近义词：certifying, licensing|反义词:uncertified|adjective
