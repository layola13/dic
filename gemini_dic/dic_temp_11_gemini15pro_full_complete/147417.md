overnourishes:/ˌoʊvərˈnɜːrɪʃɪz/
verb
过度营养；营养过剩
To provide with too much nourishment; to overfeed.
The child was so overnourished that he became obese.  这个孩子营养过剩，以至于变得肥胖。
He overnourished his plants, and they died. 他给植物施肥过多，结果植物死了。
overfeed, oversaturate
undernourish, starve
v.
