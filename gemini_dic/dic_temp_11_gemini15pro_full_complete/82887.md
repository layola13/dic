gulpiest:/ˈɡʌlpiɪst/| adj.|最容易上当受骗的，最轻信的| most gullible; most easily tricked or fooled.| He was the gulpiest person I'd ever met, believing every tall tale she spun. 他是我见过最容易上当受骗的人，她编的每一个荒诞故事他都相信。|近义词：naive, credulous, trusting|反义词: skeptical, cynical, suspicious|词性:形容词

词源:  From *gulp* (verb, "to swallow eagerly or greedily") + *-y* (adjective suffix) + *-est* (superlative suffix).  Essentially, someone who "swallows" anything they're told.
