antilibertarians:/ˌæntiˌlɪbərˈtɛəriənz/
|n.|反自由意志主义者|People who oppose libertarianism, a political philosophy that emphasizes individual liberty and limited government.| Antilibertarians believe that some government intervention is necessary for the common good. 反自由意志主义者认为，为了共同利益，某些政府干预是必要的。|近义词:statists, authoritarians|反义词:libertarians|词性:名词

antilibertarian:/ˌæntiˌlɪbərˈtɛəriən/
|adj.|反自由意志主义的|Opposed to libertarianism; advocating for more government intervention than libertarians.|  An antilibertarian stance often supports social programs and regulations. 反自由意志主义的立场通常支持社会项目和规章制度。|近义词:statist, authoritarian|反义词:libertarian|词性:形容词
