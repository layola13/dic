outrivalled:/ˌaʊtˈraɪv(ə)ld/| v. |胜过，超过|To surpass or defeat someone in competition or rivalry; to be superior to. |例句：She outrivalled all her competitors in the race. 她在比赛中胜过所有竞争对手。

例句：The new company quickly outrivalled the older, established businesses. 这家新公司迅速超过了老牌企业。

近义词：surpass, exceed, outdo, outstrip, eclipse, overshadow|反义词: be outrivalled by, lose to, be surpassed by|词性:动词
