antiguerrillas:/ˌæntiɡəˈrɪləz/
| n. |反游击队员|Soldiers or fighters specifically trained and equipped to combat guerrillas.|The antiguerrillas were deployed to the mountainous region to counter the rebel forces. 反游击队员被部署到山区以对抗叛军。|Synonyms: counterinsurgents, counterguerrillas|Antonyms: guerrillas, insurgents|noun

antiguerrilla:/ˌæntiɡəˈrɪlə/
| adj. |反游击的|Of or relating to operations or activities against guerrillas.|The army launched an antiguerrilla campaign to suppress the rebellion. 军队发动了一场反游击战役来镇压叛乱。|Synonyms: counterinsurgent, counterguerrilla|Antonyms: guerrilla, insurgent|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek "anti-")
guerrilla (noun): a member of a small independent group taking part in irregular fighting, typically against larger regular forces. (from Spanish "guerrilla," meaning "little war")
