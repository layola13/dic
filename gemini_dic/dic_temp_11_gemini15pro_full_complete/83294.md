gynandromorph:/ˌɡaɪˈnændrəˌmɔːrf/ | n. |雌雄嵌合体；雌雄同体|An organism, especially an insect, that displays both male and female characteristics in its physical form, due to the presence of both male and female tissues.|Example 1: The researchers discovered a gynandromorph butterfly with one wing displaying male coloration and the other female.|研究人员发现了一只雌雄嵌合体蝴蝶，它的一只翅膀呈现雄性颜色，另一只翅膀呈现雌性颜色。

Example 2:  Gynandromorphs are rare but provide valuable insights into sex determination and development.|雌雄嵌合体很罕见，但为了解性别决定和发育提供了宝贵的见解。|近义词:hermaphrodite, intersex|反义词:N/A|词性:名词

Etymology: From Greek *gunḗ* (γυνή) "woman" + *anḗr* (ἀνήρ) "man" + *-morph* (-μορφος) "form"
