cranium:/ˈkreɪniəm/| n. |头盖骨，颅骨|The cranium protects the brain.  颅骨保护大脑。|The skull, especially the part enclosing the brain. |例句：The blow to his cranium caused severe brain damage. 对他头盖骨的打击造成了严重的脑损伤。

例句：The doctor examined the patient's cranium for fractures. 医生检查了病人的颅骨是否有骨折。|近义词：skull|反义词: None|词性: noun
