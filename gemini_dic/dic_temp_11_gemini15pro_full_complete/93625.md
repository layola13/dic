imbitterment:/ɪmˈbɪtərmənt/ | n. | 苦恼，怨恨，辛酸 | The state of being made bitter or resentful.  It refers to a feeling of deep and lasting resentment or bitterness. |  The constant criticism led to a growing sense of imbitterment in the team.  不断的批评导致团队中越来越多的怨恨。|  resentment, bitterness, acrimony, rancor, animosity |  contentment, satisfaction, happiness, pleasure | noun
