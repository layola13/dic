adjutrix: /əˈdʒʌtrɪks/
n.
女助手，女性副官
a female assistant; a female adjutant
例句：The queen's adjutrix helped her manage her schedule. 女王的女助手帮她管理日程。

例句：She served as an adjutrix to the general during the war.  在战争期间，她担任将军的副官。

近义词：assistant, helper, aide, deputy
反义词：(No direct antonym, could consider "superior" or "leader" in certain contexts)
词性：noun
