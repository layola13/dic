fuliginosity:/ˌfjuːlɪdʒɪˈnɒsɪti/ /ˌfuːlɪdʒɪˈnɑːsəti/
n.
煤烟状；乌黑；熏黑
The state or quality of being fuliginous; sooty blackness.
The fuliginosity of the old chimney was evident in the thick layer of soot.  老烟囱的乌黑在厚厚的煤烟层中显而易见。
Near synonyms: sootiness, blackness, darkness, grime
Antonyms:  cleanliness, brightness, whiteness
noun

fuliginous:/fjuːˈlɪdʒɪnəs/ /fuːˈlɪdʒɪnəs/
adj.
煤烟状的；乌黑的；熏黑的
Resembling soot; sooty; dusky.
The fuliginous smoke from the fire filled the air.  大火产生的乌黑的烟雾弥漫在空气中。
Near synonyms: sooty, smoky, dark, dusky
Antonyms: clean, bright, clear
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fuliginosus (“sooty”), from fuligo (“soot”).
