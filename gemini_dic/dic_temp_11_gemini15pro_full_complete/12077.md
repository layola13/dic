artillerist:/ɑːrˈtɪlərɪst/
n.
炮兵；炮手
A person skilled in the use of artillery.
The artillerist aimed the cannon carefully before firing.  炮兵在开火前仔细瞄准大炮。

The skilled artillerist landed a direct hit on the enemy tank.  熟练的炮兵直接击中了敌人的坦克。

Synonyms: gunner, cannoneer
Antonyms: civilian, non-combatant
词性: 名词
