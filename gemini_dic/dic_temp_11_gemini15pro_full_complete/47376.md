deactuates:/diˈæktʃueɪts/
verb
使失效，使停止运作
to make something, especially a mechanism or electrical circuit, stop working
The pilot deactuated the autopilot system before landing.  飞行员在着陆前关闭了自动驾驶系统。
The security system was deactuated after the alarm was triggered. 警报触发后，安全系统被停用了。
deactivate, disable, turn off
activate, enable, turn on
