fremescent:/frəˈmɛsənt/
adjective
开始发怒的，暴怒的
beginning to be angry; furious
例句：A fremescent growl rumbled in the beast's throat. 野兽的喉咙里发出愤怒的低吼。

例句：Her fremescent glare dared anyone to challenge her. 她愤怒的目光让任何人都不敢挑战她。

近义词：furious, enraged, infuriated, incensed
反义词：calm, peaceful, tranquil
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *fremescens, present participle of *fremescere "to roar, rage," frequentative of fremere "to roar, murmur, rage."
