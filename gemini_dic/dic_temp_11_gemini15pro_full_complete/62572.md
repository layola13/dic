energumen:/ˈenərɡjumən/ | n. | 狂人；暴怒的人；受鼓动而狂热的人 | A person possessed by a spirit, or one who is extremely agitated and violent; a fanatic. | The preacher denounced the politician as a dangerous energumen, whipping up the crowd into a frenzy.  那个传教士谴责这个政客是一个危险的狂热分子，煽动人群陷入疯狂。| 近义词：fanatic, zealot, extremist, maniac | 反义词：moderate, pacifist | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Late Latin energumenus, from Greek energoumenos, present participle of energoumai "be worked on, be influenced, be possessed," passive of energō "set in motion, operate."  Ultimately from en- "in" + ergon "work."
