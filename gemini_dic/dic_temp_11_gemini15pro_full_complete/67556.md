extraordinaries:/ɪkˌstrɔːrdɪˈnɛriz/ | n. |非常项目，特别费用；意外开支| Items of expenditure or income arising from events or transactions that fall outside the ordinary course of business.  | The company's financial report included a section on extraordinaries, detailing unexpected costs related to the natural disaster. 公司的财务报告包含一个非常项目部分，详细说明了与自然灾害相关的意外成本。 | Example: The budget allows for unexpected extraordinaries. 预算包含了意外的额外开支。| Synonyms: contingencies, incidentals, exceptional items, one-off costs | Antonyms: ordinary expenses, recurring costs | noun
