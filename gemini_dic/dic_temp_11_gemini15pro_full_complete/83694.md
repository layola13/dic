haggle:/ˈhæɡl/| v. |讨价还价；争论；|To argue back and forth about a price, usually to get a lower price.|例句：We haggled with the street vendor over the price of the souvenirs. 我们和街边小贩就纪念品的价钱讨价还价。

例句：They spent hours haggling over the terms of the contract. 他们花了几个小时就合同条款进行讨价还价。|近义词：bargain, negotiate, dicker, barter|反义词:agree, concede|词性:动词
