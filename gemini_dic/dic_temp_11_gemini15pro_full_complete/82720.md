guidebookish: /ˈɡaɪdˌbʊkɪʃ/
adjective
像导游手册的；旅游指南式的
Resembling or characteristic of a guidebook, especially in being overly detailed, factual, or impersonal.  Often suggesting a lack of originality or personal experience.
Her prose is sometimes a little guidebookish, filled with dry facts and figures. 她的散文有时有点像导游手册，充满了枯燥的事实和数据。

Her travel blog was disappointingly guidebookish, offering no unique insights. 她的旅行博客令人失望地像导游手册，没有提供任何独特的见解。

近义词: formulaic, conventional, unoriginal, clichéd, pedestrian
反义词: original, creative, insightful, personal, subjective
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "guidebook" + "-ish."  "Guidebook" itself comes from "guide" + "book."  "Guide" ultimately traces back to the Old French word "guider," meaning "to guide" or "to show the way."
