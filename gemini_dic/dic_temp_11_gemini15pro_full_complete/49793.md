demonize: /ˈdiːmənaɪz/ | v. | 妖魔化；将…描绘成恶魔 | to represent as diabolically evil | The politician demonized his opponent. 这位政治家妖魔化了他的对手。 | Examples:  The media often demonizes certain groups in society. 媒体经常妖魔化社会中的某些群体。 They have been unfairly demonized by the press. 他们被媒体不公平地妖魔化了。 | Synonyms: vilify, slander, defame, denigrate | Antonyms: praise, extol, laud, celebrate | Verb

Etymology: From Late Latin *daemonizare, from Ancient Greek δαιμονίζω (daimonízō, “I am possessed by a demon”), from δαίμων (daímōn, “deity, demon”).
