meteoritically:/ˌmiːtiəˈrɪtɪkli/| adv.|迅速地，像流星般地|Rising very rapidly.|Her career rose meteorically. 她的事业飞速发展。|例句：His popularity rose meteorically. 他的人气迅速上升。|例句：The company's stock price increased meteorically. 该公司股价像火箭般上升。|近义词：rapidly, swiftly, quickly, dramatically|反义词：slowly, gradually, steadily|词性:副词

词源: From meteor + -ic + -al + -ly.  Ultimately from Greek *meteōros* "high in the air," from *meta* "above" + *aeirein* "to raise."
