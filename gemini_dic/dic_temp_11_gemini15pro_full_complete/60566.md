elasticated:/ɪˈlæstɪkeɪtɪd/
adjective
有松紧带的；弹性的
Having elastic added, or made of elastic.
This skirt is elasticated at the waist. 这条裙子的腰部是有松紧带的。
The socks have elasticated tops.  这些袜子的袜口有松紧带。
近义词:stretchy, elastic, flexible
反义词:rigid, stiff, inflexible
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From elastic + -ated.  "Elastic" comes from the Late Latin "elasticus",  meaning "able to be stretched," from the Greek "elastikos," from "elaunein," meaning "to drive, beat out." The suffix "-ated" forms adjectives and means "provided with" or "treated with."
