ciliiform:/ˈsɪliəfɔːrm/| adj.|纤毛状的，有纤毛的|Resembling cilia; having the form or appearance of cilia. |The ciliiform epithelium lines the respiratory tract.  纤毛状上皮排列在呼吸道。|The paramecium is a ciliiform protozoan. 草履虫是一种纤毛状原生动物。|近义词：ciliated, fimbriated|反义词: non-ciliated|adjective

Etymology->
From Latin *ciliiformis, from cilium (“eyelash, eyelid”) + -formis (“-shaped”).
