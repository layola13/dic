osteitis:/ˌɒstiˈaɪtɪs/ /ˌɑːstiˈaɪtɪs/
n.
骨炎
Inflammation of bone tissue.
The doctor diagnosed him with osteitis after examining the X-ray. 医生检查了X光片后诊断他患有骨炎。
Osteitis can cause significant pain and discomfort. 骨炎会引起明显的疼痛和不适。
