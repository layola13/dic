border:/ˈbɔːrdər/ (US) /ˈbɔːdə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.| 边界，国界；边，边缘；镶边 |A line separating two political or geographical areas, especially countries.| The two countries share a long border. 这两个国家共享一条漫长的边界。| synonym: boundary, edge, frontier, rim, margin | antonym: center, interior, middle | noun

border:/ˈbɔːrdər/ (US) /ˈbɔːdə(r)/ (UK)| v. | 与…接壤；接近；给…镶边 |To form a border to something or lie along its edge. | The garden is bordered by a hedge. 花园的边缘环绕着树篱。| synonym: adjoin, abut, bound, edge, fringe | antonym: separate, divide | verb
