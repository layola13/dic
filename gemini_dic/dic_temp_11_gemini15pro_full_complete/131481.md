noncoincidentally:/ˌnɑnkəʊˌɪnsɪˈdɛntəli/
| adv.|并非巧合地，毫不意外地|Not by coincidence; unsurprisingly.  It implies a strong connection between two events or facts.|Example: Noncoincidentally, the increase in crime coincided with a decrease in police funding. 犯罪率的上升与警察经费的减少同时发生，并非巧合。|Example: Noncoincidentally, her improved mood followed a week's vacation.  她的心情好转是在休假一周后发生的，这并非巧合。|Synonym: unsurprisingly, not accidentally, significantly, meaningfully|Antonym: coincidentally, accidentally, by chance|词性:副词
