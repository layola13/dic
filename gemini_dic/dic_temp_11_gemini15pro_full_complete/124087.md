morrowless:/ˈmɒrəʊləs/| adj.|没有明天的，短命的，转瞬即逝的|Without a morrow; having no tomorrow; doomed to die soon; ephemeral.  It describes something that is not expected to last beyond the present day or that will end very soon.  Often used in poetic or literary contexts.|The morrowless rose bloomed brightly, unaware of its impending demise.  没有明天的玫瑰开得灿烂，却不知自己即将凋零。

The butterfly, a morrowless creature, danced in the sunlight.  蝴蝶，这转瞬即逝的生物，在阳光下翩翩起舞。|近义词：ephemeral, transient, fleeting, short-lived|反义词:eternal, everlasting, permanent|词性:adjective
