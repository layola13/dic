overhomeliness:/ˌoʊvərˈhoʊmlɪnəs/| n.|过分的居家感，过分的朴素|A state of being excessively homely, to the point of being unattractive or unstylish.  It suggests a lack of sophistication or worldliness.|Her apartment's overhomeliness, with its crocheted doilies and faded floral wallpaper, made him uncomfortable.|近义词：dowdiness, frumpiness, plainness|反义词：chic, stylishness, elegance|词性: noun

Etymology: "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "homely" (adjective meaning "simple, plain, unpretentious") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives).  "Homely" itself comes from Old English "hāmlic" meaning "belonging to the home."
