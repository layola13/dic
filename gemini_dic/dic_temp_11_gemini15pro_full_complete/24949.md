brush-up:/ˈbrʌʃ ʌp/ (US) /ˈbrʌʃ ʌp/ (UK)| n. | 复习；温习；提高 | An act of improving one's skill or knowledge of something, especially after a period of not using it. | I need a brush-up on my Spanish before I go to Spain. 我去西班牙之前需要温习一下我的西班牙语。| 近义词: review, revision, refresher | 反义词: neglect, deterioration | 词性: noun
