eicosylene:/aɪˌkɒsəˈsiːliːn/| n. |二十碳烯|A long-chain hydrocarbon with the formula C20H40. It is an alkene with one double bond.  It can exist as several isomers depending on the location of the double bond. |Eicosylene is a component of some waxes.| 近义词: alkene, olefin | 反义词: alkane, paraffin |词性:noun
