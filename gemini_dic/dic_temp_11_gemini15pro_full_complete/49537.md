demarkation:/ˌdiːmɑːrˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 划界，分界，界限 | the action of fixing the boundary or limits of something |  The demarcation between the two countries was a river. 这两个国家的分界线是一条河流。 | demarcation line between North and South Korea | synonym: boundary, division, separation, delimitation | antonym: connection, union, integration | noun


demarkate:/ˈdiːmɑːrkeɪt/ | v. | 划界，标出界限 | set the boundaries or limits of |  The boundary between the two properties was demarcated by a fence. 两处房产的边界用栅栏划分。| demarcate the playing field | synonym: delimit, define, separate, distinguish | antonym: unite, combine, merge, blend | verb
