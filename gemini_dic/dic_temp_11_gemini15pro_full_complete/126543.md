nacre: /ˈneɪkər/ (US) /ˈnækrə/ (UK)| n. |珍珠母，珍珠层|A hard, iridescent substance forming the inner layer of certain mollusk shells, used for making buttons, jewelry, etc.|The abalone shell displayed a beautiful nacreous sheen.  鲍鱼壳展现出美丽的珍珠光泽。|Synonyms: mother-of-pearl, pearl oyster|Antonyms: None|Noun

nacreous: /ˈneɪkriəs/ (US) /ˈnækriəs/ (UK)| adj. |珍珠似的，有珍珠光泽的|Resembling nacre; iridescent.|The artist used nacreous paints to create a shimmering effect.  这位艺术家用珠光颜料来营造闪闪发光的效果。|Synonyms: pearly, iridescent, opalescent|Antonyms: dull, matte|Adjective
