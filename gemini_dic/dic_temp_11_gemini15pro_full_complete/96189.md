indeterminate:/ˌɪndɪˈtɜːrmɪnət/ /ˌɪndɪˈtɜːrmɪnɪt/| adj.|不确定的，不明确的；无限期的|Not exactly known, established, or defined.|The length of time they’ll be gone is indeterminate. 他们离开的时间长度不确定。|近义词：uncertain, indefinite, vague, ambiguous|反义词：definite, certain, precise, specific|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin indeterminatus, from in- ‘not’ + determinatus (see determinate).
