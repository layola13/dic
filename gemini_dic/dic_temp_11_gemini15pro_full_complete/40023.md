consumerism:/kənˈsjuːmərɪzəm/| n. |消费主义|The preoccupation of society with the acquisition of consumer goods.|例句：Consumerism is rampant in modern society. 消费主义在现代社会非常盛行。

例句：The holiday season often fuels consumerism. 假期季节通常会刺激消费主义。|近义词：materialism, commercialism|反义词:frugality, asceticism|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From consume + -ism.  "consume" comes from Latin *consumere "to use up, devour," from com- "together" + sumere "to take." "-ism" is a suffix forming nouns of action, practice, or doctrine, condition, state, etc.
