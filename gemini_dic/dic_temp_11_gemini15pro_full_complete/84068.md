half-heard:/ˌhɑːf ˈhɜːrd/ | adj. | 半听到的；听得不真切的 | Partially heard; not heard clearly or completely. |  I caught a half-heard conversation as I walked past the door. 我路过门口时听到一段断断续续的谈话。

| Example: The half-heard whispers added to the mystery of the old house.  断断续续的低语增加了老房子的神秘感。
| Synonym: overheard, glimpsed, snatches (of conversation), incomplete, fragmented
| Antonym:  clearly heard, distinct, audible, complete, understood
| Word Type: Adjective
