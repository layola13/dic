offends:/əˈfɛndz/
verb
冒犯，得罪，使…不悦
to cause someone to feel upset, annoyed, or resentful.
His remarks deeply offended many people. 他的话深深地冒犯了许多人。
近义词:insults, upsets, angers, annoys
反义词:pleases, delights, flatters
词性:v.

offense:/əˈfɛns/
noun
冒犯，得罪；进攻；犯罪
something that causes someone to feel upset, annoyed, or resentful; an attack; a crime.
His words were taken as a personal offense. 他的话被认为是人身攻击。
近义词:insult, affront, attack, crime
反义词:compliment, praise, defense
词性:n.

offending:/əˈfɛndɪŋ/
adjective
冒犯的；令人不快的；攻击性的
causing someone to feel upset, annoyed, or resentful.
The offending article was removed from the website. 这篇令人反感的文章已从网站上删除。
近义词:offensive, insulting, upsetting
反义词:pleasing, delightful, flattering
词性:adj.
