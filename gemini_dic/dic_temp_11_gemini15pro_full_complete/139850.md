octostyle: /ˈɒktoʊstaɪl/ | adj. | 八柱式的 | Having eight columns across the front, especially of a classical temple or building. | The architect designed an octostyle facade for the new museum. 建筑师为新博物馆设计了一个八柱式的正面。| Synonym: octastyle | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps tetrastyle - four columns) | adjective

octostyle: /ˈɒktoʊstaɪl/ | n. | 八柱式建筑 | A building or temple with eight columns across the front. | The Parthenon, while not strictly octostyle, is a well-known example of a classical temple. 帕特农神庙虽然并非严格意义上的八柱式，但却是古典神庙的一个著名例子。 | Synonym: octastyle building | Antonym: (No direct antonym) | noun
