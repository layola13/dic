intrench:/ɪnˈtrentʃ/ /ɪnˈtrɛntʃ/| v. | 牢固确立；使处于牢固地位；巩固；加强；(尤指军队)挖战壕；|To establish firmly; to fortify or strengthen (a position, belief, etc.).  The soldiers intrenched themselves in the forest. 士兵们在森林里挖战壕防守。|近义词：fortify, strengthen, reinforce, establish, embed, entrench|反义词: weaken, undermine, expose|词性:动词

Etymology:
From French "entrencher," from en- (in) + trenchier (to cut), from Old French trenchier, from Late Latin *trincare (to cut).
