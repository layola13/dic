hydrozoon:/ˌhaɪdrəˈzoʊən/ | n. | 水螅体 | An invertebrate animal belonging to the class Hydrozoa, which includes hydras, jellyfish, and Portuguese man-of-war.  These animals are typically marine and often colonial. |  The Portuguese man-of-war is a complex hydrozoon.  葡萄牙战舰是一种复杂的水螅体。|  Hydra, jellyfish, coral |  None | noun
