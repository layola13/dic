caravan: /ˈkærəvæn/ (US), /ˈkærəvæn/ (UK) | n. | 旅行队，大篷车；房车 | A group of people, especially traders or pilgrims, traveling together across a desert or through hostile territory. A vehicle equipped for living in, especially for holidays or camping. |  The caravan crossed the Sahara Desert. 旅行队穿越了撒哈拉沙漠。 | synonyms: convoy, procession, motor home, trailer, camper | antonyms:  stay, settlement | noun
