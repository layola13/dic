delirium:/dɪˈlɪriəm/ /dəˈlɪriəm/| n. |谵妄，神志不清，精神错乱|A state of confusion and disordered thinking that can occur during illness, high fever, or drug or alcohol withdrawal.  It's characterized by disorientation, hallucinations, and restlessness.|The patient experienced delirium due to the high fever.|近义词：confusion, madness, insanity, psychosis|反义词：lucidity, sanity, coherence|词性:noun

deliracy:/dɪˈlɪrəsi/ /dəˈlɪrəsi/| n. |谵妄状态，精神错乱|The state of being delirious; delirium.|His deliracy was brought on by a severe infection.|近义词：madness, insanity, derangement|反义词：sanity, lucidity, coherence|词性:noun
