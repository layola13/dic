interestednesses:/ˈɪntrɪstɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
利害关系; 利益
The state of having an interest in something.
Their interestednesses in the project were varied, ranging from financial gain to personal satisfaction. 他们对这个项目的利益各不相同，从经济利益到个人满足都有。
Synonyms: interests, stakes, concerns
Antonyms: apathy, indifference
