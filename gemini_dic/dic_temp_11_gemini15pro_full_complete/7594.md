announcing:/əˈnaʊnsɪŋ/
announcing|v.|宣布，宣告|The company is announcing its new product line next week.  公司下周将宣布其新的产品线。|例句：They are announcing the winners of the competition. 他们正在宣布比赛的获胜者。|近义词：declaring, proclaiming, reporting|反义词：concealing, hiding|词性:动词

announcing:/əˈnaʊnsɪŋ/
announcing|adj.|公告的，宣布的|The announcing angel appeared in a dream. 报喜的天使出现在梦中。|例句：The announcing committee will release the results soon. 公告委员会很快就会公布结果。|近义词：declarative, heralds|反义词：suppressed|词性:形容词
