herbals:/ˈɜːrbl̩z/ /ˈhɜːrbl̩z/
noun
草药；草本植物（herbal的复数）
Plants used for their medicinal properties, or products made from these plants.
She uses a lot of herbals in her cooking.  她在烹饪中使用很多草药。
Many cultures have long traditions of using herbals for healing. 许多文化都有使用草药治疗的悠久传统。
synonyms: botanicals, medicinal plants, herbs
antonyms: synthetics, pharmaceuticals
