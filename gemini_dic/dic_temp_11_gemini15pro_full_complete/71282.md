fixed-term:/ˌfɪkstˈtɜːrm/| adj.|固定期限的，定期限的|Having a fixed term or duration.|He has a fixed-term contract for two years. 他有一份两年的定期合同。|Synonyms: short-term, temporary|Antonyms: long-term, permanent|adjective

fixed-term:/ˌfɪkstˈtɜːrm/| n.|定期限|A period of time that is fixed.|The fixed-term of the lease is three years.  租约的期限是三年。|Synonyms: term, period|Antonyms:  indefinite period|noun
