discursive:/dɪˈskɜːrsɪv/ | adj. | 散漫的；离题的；推理的 |  passing aimlessly from one subject to another; digressive; proceeding by reasoning or argument rather than intuition. | The professor's lectures were notoriously discursive, often veering off on tangents. 教授的讲座出了名的散漫，经常偏离主题。

The book takes a discursive approach to the topic, exploring various related themes. 这本书对这个主题采取了散漫的探讨方式，探索了各种相关的主题。 | 近义词：rambling, digressive, meandering, diffuse, wandering | 反义词：concise, succinct, focused, direct, to the point | 词性: adjective
