grower:/ˈɡroʊər/| n.|种植者；栽培者；生长物|A grower is a person, animal, or plant that grows something, or increases in size or amount.  A grower can also refer to something that thrives in a particular environment.|He's a successful rose grower. 他是一位成功的玫瑰种植者。

The fast grower needs regular pruning.  这种快速生长的植物需要定期修剪。|Synonym: cultivator, farmer, producer|Antonym: consumer, buyer|noun
