absorption:/əbˈsɔːrpʃn/ /əbˈzɔːrpʃn/| n.|吸收; 专注; 合并; 消减|The absorption of water by the soil. 土壤对水的吸收。|synonyms: assimilation, incorporation, intake, uptake|antonyms: emission, excretion, release|noun

absorption:/əbˈsɔːrpʃn/ /əbˈzɔːrpʃn/| n.|全神贯注, 吸引|His absorption in his work.  他对工作的专注。|synonyms: immersion, preoccupation, engrossment|antonyms: distraction, inattention|noun

Etymology: From Late Latin absorptionem,  from Latin absorbere "to swallow up," from ab- "away" + sorbere "to suck up."
