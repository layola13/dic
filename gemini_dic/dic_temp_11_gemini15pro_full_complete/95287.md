incarcerating:/ɪnˈkɑːrsəreɪtɪŋ/
verb
监禁，关押
confining someone in a prison or jail.
The judge sentenced him to five years incarcerating. 法官判处他五年监禁。
They are incarcerating him for theft.  他们因盗窃罪将他监禁。
imprison, jail, confine, detain
release, free, liberate
v.
