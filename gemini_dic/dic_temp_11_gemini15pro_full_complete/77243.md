gastrolith:/ˈɡæstrəlɪθ/ | n. |胃石；砂囊石|A small stone swallowed by a bird or other animal and held in the gizzard to aid digestion.|Ostriches swallow gastroliths to help grind their food. 鸵鸟吞下胃石来帮助磨碎食物。|Synonym: gizzard stone|Antonym: N/A|noun
