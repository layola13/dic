archiving:/ˈɑːrkaɪvɪŋ/| n. |存档，归档|The process of storing or preserving data, documents, or other materials, often for historical or legal purposes.  The archiving of these old documents is a huge undertaking.|例句：The archiving of historical documents is essential for research.  将历史文献存档对于研究至关重要。

例句：Proper archiving ensures data integrity and accessibility.  正确的存档确保了数据的完整性和可访问性。|近义词：preservation, storage, filing|反义词:deletion, destruction|词性:noun (名词)

archiving:/ˈɑːrkaɪvɪŋ/| adj. |存档的|Relating to the process of archiving.  The archiving process is automated.|例句：The archiving system is highly secure. 该存档系统非常安全。

例句：Archiving software can help organize and manage large amounts of data. 存档软件可以帮助组织和管理大量数据。|近义词：preservative, storage|反义词:destructive|词性:adjective (形容词)


ETYMOLOGY->
From archive (n.) + -ing. "Archive" comes from the Old French "archif," meaning "records office," which ultimately derives from the Greek "arkheion," meaning "public building," from "arkhē," meaning "government."
