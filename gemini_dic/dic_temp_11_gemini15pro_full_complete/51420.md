deterring:/dɪˈtɜːrɪŋ/
| adj. |威慑的，制止的|Discouraging someone from doing something, typically by instilling doubt or fear of the consequences.|The high security fence is deterring thieves.  高高的安全栅栏阻止了小偷。|近义词：preventing, discouraging, inhibiting|反义词：encouraging, promoting|词性: adjective

deter:/dɪˈtɜːr/
| v. |威慑，阻止|To discourage someone from doing something, typically by instilling doubt or fear of the consequences.|The heavy fines should deter speeding.  高额罚款应该能阻止超速行驶。|近义词：prevent, discourage, inhibit|反义词：encourage, promote|词性: verb


Etymology:
From Middle English *deterren*, from Old French *deterrer*, from Latin *dēterrere*, from *dē* (“away from”) + *terrere* (“to frighten, terrify”).
