ascititious:/ˌæsɪˈtɪʃəs/
adj.
外来的，附加的；非内在的
Not inherent or innate; added from outside; supplemental.
The book contains many ascititious arguments to support its main thesis. 这本书包含许多外来的论点来支持其主要论点。
近义词：extrinsic, adventitious, supplementary, external, foreign
反义词：intrinsic, inherent, innate, essential, fundamental
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin ascitīcius (“adopted, taken in, assumed”), from asciscō (“I adopt, receive, associate, take to myself”), from ad- + scīscō (“I inquire, decree, vote for, approve, resolve, ordain”).
