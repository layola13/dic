effusion:/ɪˈfjuːʒən/ | n. | 流出；泻出；迸发；(感情的)流露 | An act of pouring forth; unrestrained expression of feeling. | The poet's effusion of grief over the loss of his friend was moving.  这位诗人对失去朋友的悲痛流露令人感动。 | Synonyms: outpouring, discharge, outburst, gush, overflow | Antonyms: suppression, repression, concealment | noun

effusion:/ɪˈfjuːʒən/ | n. | (气体或液体的)溢出，渗漏 | The escape of gas or liquid through a small opening. | The effusion of gas from the damaged pipe caused a fire. 损坏的管道气体泄漏引发了火灾。| Synonyms: leakage, seepage, escape | Antonyms: containment, blockage | noun
