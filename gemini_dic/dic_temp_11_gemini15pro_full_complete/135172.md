nonoptic:/nɒnˈɒptɪk/ | adj. | 非光学的 | Not involving or relating to optics or vision; not depending on light. | The device uses a nonoptic sensor to detect movement.  该设备使用非光学传感器来检测运动。| Example: The system employs nonoptic technology for data transfer.  系统采用非光学技术进行数据传输。 | Synonyms: non-optical, nonvisual | Antonyms: optic, optical, visual | adjective

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "optic" (from Greek "optikos" relating to sight).
