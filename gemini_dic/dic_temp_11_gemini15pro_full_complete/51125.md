despisingly:/dɪˈspaɪzɪŋli/
adverb
轻蔑地，鄙夷地
in a manner that shows that you have no respect for someone or something
He laughed despisingly at my efforts. 他轻蔑地嘲笑我的努力。
Synonyms: contemptuously, scornfully, disdainfully
Antonyms: respectfully, admiringly
词性: 副词
