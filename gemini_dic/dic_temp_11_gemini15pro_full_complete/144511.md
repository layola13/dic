outtraveling:/ˌaʊtˈtrævəlɪŋ/
verb
胜过旅行；比...更经常旅行
Surpassing someone in the amount or extent of travel.
He's outtraveling all his colleagues this year, visiting clients in over ten countries.  他今年的出差次数超过了所有同事，访问了十多个国家的客户。
近义词: outdo, surpass, exceed
反义词: lag behind, fall short
