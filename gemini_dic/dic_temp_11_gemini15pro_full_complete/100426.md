intertonic:/ˌɪntərˈtɒnɪk/ | adj. | 音调间的；介于两个主重音之间的 | Situated between two primary tonic accents.  Relating to the interval between two stressed syllables. | The intertonic syllables in this verse are very short. 这段诗歌中，两个重读音节之间的音节很短。|  medial, intermediate | accented, stressed | adjective
