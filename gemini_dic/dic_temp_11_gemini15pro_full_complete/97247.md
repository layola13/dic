inflexible:/ɪnˈflɛksəbl/ | adj. | 不灵活的，僵硬的，固执的 | Not able to be bent or changed; unwilling to change or compromise. |  The company's inflexible policy on refunds frustrated many customers.  这家公司不灵活的退款政策让很多顾客感到沮丧。

The inflexible rules made it difficult for the teachers to adapt to their students' needs. 僵化的规则使教师难以适应学生的需求。


近义词：rigid, stiff, unbending, stubborn, obstinate | 反义词：flexible, adaptable, pliable, yielding | 词性：形容词
