cassareep:/ˈkæsəriːp/| n. |木薯浓汁，苦木薯汁|A dark brown, slightly bitter syrup made by boiling down the juice of the bitter cassava root. It is used as a flavoring and coloring agent in Caribbean cuisine, particularly in pepperpot stew.|Cassareep adds a unique depth of flavor to the pepperpot. 木薯浓汁为辣椒锅增添了独特的风味。|Synonyms: None (unique product)|Antonyms: None|Noun
