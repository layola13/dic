obtruding:/əbˈtruːdɪŋ/
| adj. | 突出的，强加的，打扰的 |  pushing oneself or something else forward or into something when unwanted or uninvited; imposing oneself or one's opinions on others. |  His constant questions were obtruding on my privacy. 他不断地提问打扰了我的私人空间。 | synonyms: imposing, intruding, interfering, meddling | antonyms: withdrawing, receding, retiring, unobtrusive | adjective

obtrude:/əbˈtruːd/
| v. |  突出；强加；打扰 | to become noticeable in an unwelcome or intrusive way; to impose oneself or one's opinions on others. | Please don't obtrude on/upon private grief. 请不要打扰别人的悲伤。| synonyms: impose, intrude, interfere, meddle | antonyms: withdraw, recede, retire | verb
