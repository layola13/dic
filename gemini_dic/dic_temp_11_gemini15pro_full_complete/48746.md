definite:/ˈdefɪnət/ (US), /ˈdefɪnɪt/ (UK)| adj.|明确的，肯定的，限定的|Clearly stated or decided; not vague or doubtful.|例句：I need a definite answer by tomorrow. 我明天需要一个明确的答复。

例句：There's a definite improvement in his work. 他的工作有了明显的进步。

近义词：certain, specific, precise, explicit, clear, fixed|反义词：indefinite, vague, uncertain, ambiguous|词性:形容词
