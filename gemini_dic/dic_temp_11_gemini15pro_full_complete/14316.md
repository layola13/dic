automaticities:/ˌɔːtəməˈtɪsətiz/ | n. | 自动性，自动化，自动行为 | The ability to perform actions or tasks without conscious thought or effort.  The state of being automatic; involuntary or unconscious actions. |  Typing becomes one of the automaticities after a lot of practice.  大量练习后，打字成为一种自动化行为。 | Synonyms:  automatism, reflex, involuntary actions | Antonyms: deliberateness, conscious actions, voluntary actions | noun
