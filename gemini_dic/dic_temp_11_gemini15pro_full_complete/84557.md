hammerers:/ˈhæmərərz/| n.|锤击者，锻工|People who use hammers, especially as a profession or with great skill.  These people might shape metal, drive nails, or break rocks.|The hammerers worked tirelessly to forge the glowing metal. 锻工们不知疲倦地锻造着炽热的金属。|The rhythmic clang of the hammerers filled the air. 锤击者有节奏的叮当声充满了空气。|近义词：smiths, blacksmiths, metalworkers|反义词:(No direct antonym. Could be something like "office workers" for contrast)|Noun
