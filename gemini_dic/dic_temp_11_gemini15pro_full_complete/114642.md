maleyl:/ˈmeɪliːl/| adj.| 马来酰的，顺丁烯二酰的 | Relating to or containing the maleyl group, derived from maleic acid (顺丁烯二酸).  Specifically, it refers to the cis-form of the butenedioic group,  -OCCH=CHCO-. |  Maleyl chloride is a highly reactive compound. 马来酰氯是一种高活性化合物。| synonym:  cis-butenedioyl | antonym:  fumaryl (反丁烯二酰的) | affix
