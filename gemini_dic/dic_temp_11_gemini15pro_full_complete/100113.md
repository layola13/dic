interpretation:/ɪnˌtɜːrprɪˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 解释；说明；阐明；理解；表演；演绎 | An explanation or understanding of the meaning of something. |  Her interpretation of the poem was very moving. 她对这首诗的诠释非常感人。| synonyms: explanation, understanding, elucidation, rendition, performance | antonyms: misinterpretation, misunderstanding, distortion | noun

interpretation:/ɪnˌtɜːrprɪˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 口译 | The action of explaining the meaning of something. | She provided interpretation for the foreign visitors. 她为外国游客提供口译。| synonyms: translation, rendering, version | antonyms: - | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: interpretaten, via Old French from Latin interpretatio(n-), from the verb interpretari.
