bitt: /bɪt/
n.|小片，少量|A small piece or quantity of something.|He ate a bitt of the cake.|近义词:piece, morsel, fragment|反义词:lot, whole|词性:名词
词源: From Middle English *bitte*, from Old English *bita* (“piece bitten off, morsel, bit”), from Proto-Germanic *bitō* (“bite, piece bitten off”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰeyd- (“to split, cleave”). Cognate with Dutch *beet* (“bite, piece”), German *Bissen* (“bite, morsel”), Icelandic *biti* (“bite”).
