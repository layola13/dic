causerie:/ˈkɔːzəri/ (US), /ˈkɔːzəri/ (UK)| n. |非正式谈话，闲聊|An informal talk or discussion, often on a literary or artistic subject.  A light, informal conversation.|例句：We enjoyed a pleasant causerie after dinner about our travels.  我们晚饭后愉快地闲聊了我们的旅行。

例句：The professor's causeries on French literature were always popular. 教授关于法国文学的非正式谈话总是很受欢迎。|近义词：chat, conversation, discussion|反义词:lecture, debate, argument|词性:noun
