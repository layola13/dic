nonproliferation:/ˌnɒnprəˌlɪfəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 核不扩散，防扩散 |  The prevention of the spread of nuclear weapons and other weapons of mass destruction, especially to nations that do not already have them. | The treaty aims at the nonproliferation of nuclear weapons. 该条约旨在防止核武器扩散。 | 近义词：containment, limitation | 反义词：proliferation, expansion | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + proliferation.  Proliferation comes from Late Latin prōlīferātiōn- (stem of prōlīferātiō ), from prōlīferāre  "to bear offspring," from prōles "offspring" + -ferāre (akin to ferre "to bear").
