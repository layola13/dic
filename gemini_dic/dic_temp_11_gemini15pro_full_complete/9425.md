antirationality:/ˌæntiˌræʃəˈnælɪti/
|n.|反理性，非理性|The rejection of reason and logic, especially in favor of emotions, intuition, or tradition.  It emphasizes feeling over thinking and often distrusts scientific or empirical evidence.|His antirationality led him to believe in conspiracy theories despite overwhelming evidence to the contrary. 他对理性的反感使他相信阴谋论，尽管有压倒性的证据证明并非如此。|irrationality, unreasonableness, illogicality|rationality, reason, logic|noun
