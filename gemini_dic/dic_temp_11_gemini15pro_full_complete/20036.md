biomimicry:/ˌbaɪoʊˈmɪmɪkri/
n.
仿生学
The design and production of materials, structures, and systems that are modeled on biological entities and processes.
The bullet train's nose was inspired by the kingfisher's beak, a perfect example of biomimicry.
高铁车头的设计灵感来自翠鸟的喙，这是一个完美的仿生学例子。

Biomimicry offers sustainable solutions to human challenges by emulating nature's time-tested patterns and strategies.
仿生学通过模仿自然界经过时间考验的模式和策略，为人类的挑战提供可持续的解决方案。

近义词：biomimetics, bionics
反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially "artificial design" or "synthetic engineering")
词性: noun
