jibs:/dʒɪbz/| n.|三角帆的斜桁;心情,兴致(通常复数);|The jibs of the sailboat were taut in the wind. 帆船的斜桁在风中绷紧。 |He's got his jibs up today, I wonder why. 他今天心情很好，我想知道为什么。|近义词：spirits, mood, temper|反义词:depression, gloom|noun


jibs:/dʒɪbz/| v. (jibbed, jibbing)|(马)突然停下不肯前进;不愿做;|The horse jibbed at the jump. 马在跳跃时突然停了下来。|He jibbed at the idea of working overtime. 他不愿加班。|近义词：balk, resist, refuse|反义词:accept, comply|verb
