confervous:/kənˈfɜːrvəs/| adj.|紧张不安的，慌乱的|A blend of "confused" and "nervous," suggesting a state of being both disoriented and anxious.|例句：He felt confervous before his big presentation. 他在重要的演讲前感到紧张不安。

例句：She was so confervous she forgot her lines. 她太紧张了，忘了台词。|近义词：anxious, apprehensive, agitated, flustered|反义词：calm, composed, relaxed|词性:形容词

词源:  A portmanteau word combining "confused" and "nervous."  It's a relatively recent coinage and not yet recognized in standard dictionaries.
