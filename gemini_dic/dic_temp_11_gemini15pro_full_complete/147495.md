overornaments: /ˌoʊvərˈɔːrnəmənts/ | n. | 过度装饰; 过分装饰 | Excessive or superfluous ornamentation.  Something that is overly decorated, often to the point of gaudiness. |  The overornaments on the wedding cake made it look tacky. 婚礼蛋糕上的过度装饰让它看起来很俗气。| The architect felt the building's facade suffered from overornaments.  建筑师觉得大楼的正面装饰过度。 | synonyms: embellishments, frills, excesses, superfluities | antonyms: simplicity, minimalism, austerity | noun
