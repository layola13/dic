emollients:/ɪˈmɒliənts/ /ɪˈmɑːliənts/| n.|润肤剂，软化剂|Substances that soften and soothe the skin, often used in lotions and creams.  They reduce water loss and improve skin hydration.|This cream contains natural emollients to keep your skin soft.  这款面霜含有天然润肤剂，可保持肌肤柔软。|synonyms: moisturizers, softeners, lubricants|antonyms: irritants, astringents|noun


emollient:/ɪˈmɒliənt/ /ɪˈmɑːliənts/| adj.|润肤的，软化|Having the quality of softening or soothing the skin.|The emollient cream soothed her dry, cracked hands.  润肤霜舒缓了她干燥皲裂的双手。|synonyms: softening, soothing, moisturizing|antonyms: irritating, drying|adjective
