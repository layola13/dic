caginess:/ˈkeɪdʒinɪs/
n.
谨慎，小心，守口如瓶
The quality of being cautious, secretive, and unwilling to divulge information.
He answered questions with a certain caginess. 他回答问题时显得十分谨慎。
近义词: reticence, guardedness, secrecy, discretion
反义词: openness, frankness, candor, loquacity
词性: 名词

词源:  From "cagy" + "-ness". "Cagy" is of uncertain origin, possibly related to "cage," suggesting a guarded or restrained quality.
