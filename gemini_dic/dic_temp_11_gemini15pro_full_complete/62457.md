endorsible:/ɪnˈdɔːrsəbl/ | adj. | 可背书的，可转让的 | Able to be endorsed; transferable.  Specifically referring to a document, typically a check or bill of exchange, that can legally be transferred to another party by signing it. | This check is endorsible, so you can sign it over to me. 这张支票可以背书，所以你可以把它转让给我。|  The promissory note was endorsible and thus could be used as collateral.  这张期票是可以背书的，因此可以用作抵押品。 | transferable, negotiable | non-transferable, non-negotiable | adjective
