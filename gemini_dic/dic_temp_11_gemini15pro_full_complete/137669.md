nonunisons:/nɒnˈjuːnɪsənz/
noun
非齐奏，非同度音
A musical texture or passage consisting of two or more non-identical notes sounding simultaneously.
The composer frequently uses nonunisons to create a richer, more complex sound.  作曲家经常使用非齐奏来创造更丰富、更复杂的音响。
The piece moved from simple unison to complex nonunisons, creating a dramatic effect. 这首曲子从简单的齐奏过渡到复杂的非齐奏，营造出戏剧性的效果。
Synonyms: polyphony, counterpoint, harmony
Antonyms: unison, homophony
