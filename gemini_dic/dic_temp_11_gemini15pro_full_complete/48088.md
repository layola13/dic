declassifies:/diːˈklæsɪfaɪz/
verb
解密，撤销机密
To remove the security classification from (information or documents).
The government declassified the documents about the war. 政府解密了关于战争的文件。
The CIA declassified the files relating to the Kennedy assassination. 中央情报局解密了与肯尼迪遇刺案相关的文件。
synonyms: release, disclose, reveal, unveil, publish, divulge
antonyms: classify, conceal, hide, protect, restrict
词性: 动词
