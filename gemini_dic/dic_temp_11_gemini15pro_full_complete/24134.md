briar:/ˈbraɪər/ /ˈbraɪɚ/| n.|荆棘，野蔷薇|A thorny shrub or bush, especially a rose bush.|The path was overgrown with briars.|近义词：thorn, bramble, thicket|反义词:flower, blossom|词性:名词

briar:/ˈbraɪər/ /ˈbraɪɚ/| adj.(罕见)|荆棘丛生的，由荆棘制成的|Made of or covered with briars.|A briar pipe is made from the root of a briar bush.|近义词：thorny, prickly|反义词:smooth, soft|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English brere, from Old English brǣr (“briar, bramble”), from Proto-Germanic *brēzō (“thorn bush”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰars- (“thorn, point, bristle”). Cognate with Dutch braam (“bramble”), German Brombeere (“blackberry”), Icelandic brómber (“blackberry”).
