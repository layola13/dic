foussa: /ˈfuːsə/ (US), /ˈfuːsə/ (UK)| n.| fossa; 马岛獴|A carnivorous mammal endemic to Madagascar, related to the mongoose. It is the largest mammalian predator on the island and has a cat-like appearance.|The foussa is a unique predator found only in Madagascar. 马岛獴是一种独特的掠食动物，只在马达加斯加发现。|The foussa stalked a lemur through the dense forest. 马岛獴在茂密的森林里跟踪一只狐猴。|Synonyms:  Malagasy civet, fanaloka (related but distinct species)|Antonyms:  prey (in terms of ecological role)|noun
