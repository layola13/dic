getter-up:/ˈɡɛtərˌʌp/
noun
早起的人
A person who gets up early, especially habitually.
He's always been a getter-up, even on weekends. 他一直是个早起的人，即使在周末也是如此。
early bird, early riser
late sleeper, lie-abed
