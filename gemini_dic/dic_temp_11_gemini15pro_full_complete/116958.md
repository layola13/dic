mazunda:/məˈzʊndə/ | n. | 马宗达，马宗达语 |A Bantu language spoken primarily in Mozambique. It is also known as Sena or Chisena. | Many people in central Mozambique speak Mazunda.  许多莫桑比克中部的人说马宗达语。| Synonyms: Sena, Chisena | Antonyms: N/A | Noun


Etymology: From the name of a subgroup of the Shona people.
