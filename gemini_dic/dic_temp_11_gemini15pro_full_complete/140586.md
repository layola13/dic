olio:/ˈoʊlioʊ/ | n. |什锦；混合物；杂烩 | A miscellaneous collection of things. | Example: The book is an olio of poems, short stories, and essays. 这本书是诗歌、短篇小说和散文的混合体。

Example:  The market offered an olio of exotic fruits and vegetables. 市场提供了各种各样的异国水果和蔬菜。| 近义词: mixture, medley, assortment, hodgepodge | 反义词: uniformity, homogeneity | 词性: 名词

词源: From Italian *oglietto*, diminutive of *olio* "oil," referring to a Spanish dish of various stewed meats and vegetables, perhaps originally cooked in oil.
