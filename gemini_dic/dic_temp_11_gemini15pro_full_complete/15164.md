back the wrong horse:/ˌbæk ðə ˌrɒŋ ˈhɔːs/ | idiom | 支持输家；押错宝；选错人；判断失误 | To support the losing side or make a bad decision. | Example: He backed the wrong horse in the election and his candidate lost badly. 他在选举中押错了宝，他的候选人惨败。 | Example: Investing in that company was backing the wrong horse; they went bankrupt within a year. 投资那家公司是押错了宝；他们一年内就破产了。| Synonyms: miscalculate, make a bad judgment, choose poorly, bet on the loser, pick the wrong side, err in judgment, blunder | Antonyms: back the right horse, make a good decision, choose wisely, pick the winner, be successful, triumph | idiom
