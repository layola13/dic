forewomen:/ˈfɔːrwɪmɪn/ | n. | 女工头；女领班 |A woman who is in charge of a group of workers, especially in a factory. | The forewomen instructed the new employees on the factory procedures. 女工头指导新员工学习工厂的规章制度。| The forewomen ensured all safety protocols were followed on the assembly line.  女工头确保在装配线上所有安全规程都得到遵守。|Synonyms: supervisor, manager, overseer, superintendent | Antonyms: subordinate, employee, worker | noun
