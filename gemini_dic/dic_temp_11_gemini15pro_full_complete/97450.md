infusibleness:/ɪnˈfjuːzɪbəlnəs/
n.
不可熔性；难以灌输
The infusibleness of the metal makes it ideal for high-temperature applications.
这种金属的不可熔性使其成为高温应用的理想选择。
The infusibleness of his beliefs made him resistant to new ideas.
他根深蒂固的信念使他不易接受新思想。
incapability of being infused; difficult to instill or impart.
近义词:  resistance, imperviousness, obstinacy, rigidity
反义词:  malleability, flexibility, receptiveness, persuadability
词性: noun

Etymology:  From Late Latin *infusibilis + -ness.  *infusibilis from in- "not" + fusibilis "capable of being melted," from fundere "to pour, melt."
