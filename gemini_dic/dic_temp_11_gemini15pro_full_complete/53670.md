disciplelike:/dɪˈsaɪpəlˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 像弟子的；虔诚的；谦逊的 | Resembling or characteristic of a disciple; humbly obedient or devoted. |  He maintained a disciplelike devotion to his mentor.  他对他导师保持着弟子般的虔诚。 | 近义词: devoted, faithful, obedient, reverent, pious | 反义词: irreverent, disobedient, rebellious, impious | 词性: adjective
词源: disciple + like.  "Disciple" comes from the Old French word "disciple," which in turn comes from the Latin word "discipulus," meaning "pupil, learner." "Like" is an Old English word.
