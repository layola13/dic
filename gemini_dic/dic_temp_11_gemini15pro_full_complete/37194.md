comeliest:/ˈkʌmliɪst/| adj.|最美丽的，最漂亮的|Superlative adjective of comely; most comely.  Meaning most attractive in appearance; having a pleasing appearance; handsome.|She was by far the comeliest girl at the ball. 她是舞会上最漂亮的女孩。|Synonyms: prettiest, most beautiful, most attractive, fairest, most charming|Antonyms: ugliest, plainest, homeliest|adjective

comely:/ˈkʌmli/| adj.|美丽的，漂亮的|Attractive in appearance; having a pleasing appearance; handsome.|She was a comely young woman with a pleasant smile. 她是一位漂亮的年轻女子，面带愉悦的微笑。|Synonyms: pretty, beautiful, attractive, fair, charming|Antonyms: ugly, plain, homely|adjective
