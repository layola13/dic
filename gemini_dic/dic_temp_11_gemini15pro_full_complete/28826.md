carp:/kɑːrp/ (US) /kɑːp/ (UK)| n. vi.|鲤鱼；抱怨|A large freshwater fish.|例句：We caught a huge carp in the lake. 我们在湖里钓到了一条大鲤鱼。

例句：The lake is well-stocked with carp. 这个湖里有很多鲤鱼。|近义词：fish, koi (for ornamental carp)|反义词: None (carp refers to a specific type of fish, it doesn't have a direct opposite)|词性:noun, verb

carp:/kɑːrp/ (US) /kɑːp/ (UK)| vi.|抱怨；吹毛求疵|To complain or find fault, especially about petty matters.|例句：He's always carping about something. 他总是抱怨个不停。

例句：She carped about the service at the restaurant. 她对餐厅的服务很不满意。|近义词：complain, grumble, nitpick, cavil|反义词:praise, commend|词性:verb
