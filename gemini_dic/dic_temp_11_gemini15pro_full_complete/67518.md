extract:/ɪkˈstrækt/ | v. | 提取，提炼 | To remove or take out, often by force or effort. | 例句：The dentist extracted my wisdom tooth. 牙医拔掉了我的智齿。| 近义词：remove, withdraw | 反义词: insert, implant | 词性:动词
