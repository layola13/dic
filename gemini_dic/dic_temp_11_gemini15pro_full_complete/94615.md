impregnating:/ɪmˈprɛɡneɪtɪŋ/
|v.|使充满；使怀孕；浸渍；灌输|filling or permeating something thoroughly; making pregnant; saturating|The artist is impregnating the canvas with vibrant colors. 艺术家正在用鲜艳的色彩浸染画布。|
|v.|使充满；使怀孕；浸渍；灌输|The wood was impregnated with a preservative. 木材浸透了防腐剂。|
|近义词：permeating, saturating, infusing, imbuing, fertilizing|
|反义词：emptying, draining, removing|
|词性：动词 (present participle)|


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *impraegnare, from Latin impraegnare ("to make pregnant, impregnate"), from in- ("in, into") + praegnas ("pregnant").
