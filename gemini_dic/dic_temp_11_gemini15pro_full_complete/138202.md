noselessly:/ˈnoʊzləsli/| adv.|无鼻地，没有鼻子地|Without a nose; in a manner suggesting a lack of a nose.  It describes an action performed as if someone didn't have a nose, often figuratively to suggest a lack of sensitivity or awareness.|He sniffed noselessly at the perfume, unable to detect its delicate aroma.|近义词: insensitively, unperceptively, dully|反义词: sensitively, perceptively, keenly|词性:副词

Etymology:
"nose" (Old English nosu) + "-less" (Old English -lēas) + "-ly" (Old English -līce)
