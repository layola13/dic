erythematic:/ˌɛrɪθəˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 红斑的，充血的 | Relating to or characterized by erythema (redness of the skin). | The patient presented with an erythematic rash on her arms. 病人的手臂上出现了红斑疹。 | Synonyms: reddened, inflamed, flushed | Antonyms: pale, blanched | adjective

erythema:/ˌɛrɪˈθiːmə/ | n. | 红斑 | Redness of the skin caused by dilation of the capillaries. | The sunburn caused erythema on his shoulders. 晒伤导致他的肩膀出现红斑。 | Synonyms: redness, rash, inflammation | Antonyms: pallor, blanching | noun
