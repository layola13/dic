deobstruct:/ˌdiːəbˈstrʌkt/
verb
疏通，清除障碍
To remove an obstruction from; to clear.
The surgeon deobstructed the blocked artery.  外科医生疏通了阻塞的动脉。
The workers deobstructed the road after the landslide. 工人们在山体滑坡后清理了道路。
近义词: clear, unclog, unblock, open
反义词: obstruct, block, clog
词性: verb

词源:
From de- (expressing reversal) + obstruct.  来自de-（表示反转）+ obstruct（阻塞）。
