granularity:/ˌɡrænjuˈlærɪti/
n.
粒度；颗粒度
The level of detail in a set of data.
The database allows you to search with varying levels of granularity.  这个数据库允许你以不同的粒度进行搜索。
近义词：detail, precision, specificity
反义词：coarseness, generality
词性:名词

granular:/ˈɡrænjələr/
adj.
颗粒状的；粒状的
Resembling or consisting of granules.
The beach had fine, granular sand.  海滩上有细细的沙粒。
近义词：grainy, particulate
反义词：smooth, homogeneous
词性:形容词
