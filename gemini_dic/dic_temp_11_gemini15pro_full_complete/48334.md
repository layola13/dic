decriminalised: /diːˈkrɪmɪnəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 已非刑事化的；已合法化的 |  Something that is no longer considered a crime.  |  Possession of small amounts of cannabis has been decriminalised in some states. 在一些州，持有少量大麻已被非刑事化。 | legal, legitimate | criminal | adjective
