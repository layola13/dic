dwarfness:/ˈdwɔːrfnɪs/| n.|侏儒；矮小；矮化|The condition of being a dwarf; significantly below average height. |His dwarfness was caused by a genetic disorder. 他的侏儒症是由遗传疾病引起的。|Synonyms: short stature, stunted growth|Antonyms: tallness, gigantism|noun


dwarfness:/ˈdwɔːrfnɪs/| n.|矮小，矮化（指植物）|The condition of being abnormally small for its kind (referring to plants).|The dwarfness of the bonsai tree is achieved through careful pruning. 这棵盆景的矮小是通过仔细修剪实现的。|Synonyms: miniature, smallness|Antonyms: largeness, tallness|noun
