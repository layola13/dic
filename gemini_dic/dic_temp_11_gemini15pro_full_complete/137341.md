nontaxability:/ˌnɒnˌtæksəˈbɪləti/ /ˌnɑːnˌtæksəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可征税性, 免税
The quality or state of being exempt from taxation.
The nontaxability of municipal bonds makes them attractive to investors.  市政债券的免税性使其对投资者具有吸引力。
