dietitians:/ˌdaɪəˈtɪʃənz/
n.
营养师，营养学家
Experts in food and nutrition who advise people on healthy eating habits.
例句：The dietitians recommend eating a balanced diet. 营养师建议均衡饮食。

例句：Many hospitals employ dietitians to help patients with special dietary needs. 许多医院雇用营养师来帮助有特殊饮食需求的病人。

近义词：nutritionists
反义词: 无
