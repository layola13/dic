fascinator:/ˈfæsɪneɪtər/| n.|头饰，女帽，令人着迷的人或物|A fascinator is a headpiece, typically a decorative ornament attached to a band or clip, worn by women.  It can be made of feathers, beads, flowers, or other materials.  It can also refer to someone or something that fascinates.|She wore a beautiful fascinator to the wedding. 她戴了一个漂亮的头饰参加婚礼。|Synonym:headpiece, ornament, decoration|Antonym:plain, simple|Noun


fascinate:/ˈfæsɪneɪt/| v.|使着迷，强烈地吸引|To fascinate is to attract and hold the attention of someone by being extremely interesting, unusual, or beautiful.|The magician's tricks fascinated the children. 魔术师的戏法使孩子们着迷。|Synonym:captivate, enthrall, mesmerize|Antonym:bore, repel|Verb
