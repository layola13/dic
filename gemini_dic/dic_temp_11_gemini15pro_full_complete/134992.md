nonnavigably:/ˌnɒnˈnævɪɡəbli/
| adv. |不可通航地，无法航行地|In a way that is not navigable; in a way that cannot be navigated.  It refers to a body of water or a route that is impossible or extremely difficult to navigate by boat or ship due to obstacles, shallowness, or other factors.|The river was nonnavigably shallow due to the drought. 由于干旱，这条河浅得无法航行。|Near Antonyms: navigably|Far Antonyms: easily navigable
|adverb|
