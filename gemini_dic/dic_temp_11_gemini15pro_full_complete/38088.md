composting:/ˈkɒmpɒstɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmpɑːstɪŋ/ (US)| n.|堆肥，堆肥化|The process of decomposing organic matter (such as leaves and food scraps) to produce compost.|Example: Composting is a great way to reduce household waste and enrich your garden soil. 堆肥是减少家庭垃圾和丰富花园土壤的好方法。

Example:  He started composting kitchen scraps and yard waste. 他开始堆肥厨房垃圾和庭院废物。|近义词：decomposition, rotting|反义词: incineration, burning|词性:noun


compost:/ˈkɒmpɒst/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmpɑːst/ (US)| n. v.|堆肥，混合肥料；把…制成堆肥|Decomposed organic matter used as a fertilizer for growing plants. / To convert (organic matter) into compost.|Example:  The compost enriched the soil.  这种堆肥使土壤肥沃。

Example: We compost all our vegetable peelings. 我们把所有的蔬菜皮都做成堆肥。|近义词：fertilizer, manure|反义词: N/A|词性:noun, verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English *composte*, from Old French compost, from Latin compositus "put together," past participle of componere "to put together."
