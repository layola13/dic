lobbyisms:/ˈlɒbiɪzəmz/
n.
游说活动; 游说行为
The act or practice of lobbying; attempts to influence legislation.
Lobbyisms surrounding the new healthcare bill were intense.  围绕这项新的医疗保健法案的游说活动非常激烈。
近义词: lobbying, advocacy, influence-peddling
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑"grassroots activism" 草根行动主义)
词性: 名词


lobbying:/ˈlɒbiɪŋ/
n.
游说
The act of attempting to influence decisions made by officials in the government, most often legislators or members of regulatory agencies.
She works in lobbying for a pharmaceutical company. 她为一家制药公司从事游说工作。
近义词: advocacy, influence-peddling
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑"grassroots activism" 草根行动主义)
词性: 名词

词源:  From lobby + -ism.  "Lobby" originally referred to the anteroom of a legislative chamber where people could meet with legislators.
