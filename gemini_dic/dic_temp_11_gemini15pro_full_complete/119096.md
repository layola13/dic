metered:/ˈmiːtərd/ | adj. | 计量的，用计量器的 | Measured or controlled by a meter. | The taxi had a metered fare. 这辆出租车是计价收费的。| Synonyms: measured, calculated, gauged | Antonyms: unmetered, unlimited, free | adjective
