certifies:/ˈsɜːrtɪfaɪz/| v.|证明; 认证; 保证|confirms formally as true, accurate, or genuine; grants a certificate to (someone) attesting to their competence or expertise.|例句：The document certifies that he is a qualified doctor. 该文件证明他是一名合格的医生。|近义词：attests, validates, confirms, guarantees|反义词：denies, rejects, invalidates|词性:动词
Etymology: From Late Middle English *certifien, from Old French certifier, from Medieval Latin certificāre, from Latin certus (“certain”) + facere (“to make”).
