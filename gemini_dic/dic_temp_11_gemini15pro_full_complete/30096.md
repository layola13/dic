ceasing:/ˈsiːsɪŋ/
verb
停止，终止
The act of stopping or ending something.
The fighting is showing no sign of ceasing.  战斗没有停止的迹象。
Synonyms: stopping, halting, ending, terminating, concluding, finishing
Antonyms: beginning, starting, commencing, initiating, continuing, persisting
v.

ceasing:/ˈsiːsɪŋ/
adjective
停止的，终止的
Used to describe something that is stopping or coming to an end.
The ceasing of the rain was a welcome relief. 雨停了，真是令人欣慰。
Synonyms: ending, finishing, terminating, concluding, stopping, halting
Antonyms: beginning, starting, commencing, initiating, continuing, persisting
adj.
