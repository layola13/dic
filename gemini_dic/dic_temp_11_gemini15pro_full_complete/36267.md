cohabitant:/koʊˈhæbɪtənt/| n. |同居者，同住者|A person who lives with another, especially in a sexual relationship.|She and her cohabitant have been together for five years. 她和她的同居伴侣在一起五年了。|John and Mary are cohabitants. 约翰和玛丽是同居者。|近义词：roommate, housemate, partner, live-in partner|反义词：spouse (if legally married), stranger|词性：noun
