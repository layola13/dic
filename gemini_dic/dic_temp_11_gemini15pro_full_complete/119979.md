millerite:/ˈmɪləˌraɪt/| n. |米勒矿，硫镍矿|A rare nickel sulfide mineral (NiS) that crystallizes in the hexagonal system and is typically bronze-yellow in color. It is an important ore of nickel.|Millerite is sometimes found in radiating acicular crystals.|近义词:nickel sulfide, nickel ore|反义词:|名词
