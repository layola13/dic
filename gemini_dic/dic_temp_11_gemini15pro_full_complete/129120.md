niyak:/ˈniːæk/| n.|脂，油脂；（尤指动物的）脂肪|A fatty substance, especially in animal bodies.|The niyak from the seal was used to waterproof their boots. 海豹的脂肪被用来防水他们的靴子。|近义词：fat, grease, blubber|反义词:leanness|词性:名词

词源: From Inuktitut *niqak, meaning "fat, grease, blubber".
