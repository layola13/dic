counterreform:/ˌkaʊntər rɪˈfɔːrm/
n.
反改革，反宗教改革
A movement to counter the effects of the Reformation.
The Counter-Reformation led to the establishment of new religious orders. 反宗教改革导致了新的宗教秩序的建立。

The Counter-Reformation saw a resurgence of Catholic art and architecture. 反宗教改革时期见证了天主教艺术和建筑的复兴。

近义词:Counter-Reformation, Catholic Reformation
反义词:Reformation

词性:noun
