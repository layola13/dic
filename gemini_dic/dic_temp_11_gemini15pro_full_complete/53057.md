dioptric:/daɪˈɒptrɪk/| adj.|屈光度的，折射的|Relating to the refraction of light, especially through the eye or a lens.  Pertaining to the unit of refractive power of a lens, equal to the reciprocal of the focal length in meters.|The optician measured the dioptric power of my lenses.  验光师测量了我镜片的屈光度。|Synonyms: refractive, lenticular|Antonyms: non-refractive|adjective

dioptre:/daɪˈɒptər/| n.|屈光度|A unit of measurement of the refractive power of a lens, equal to the reciprocal of its focal length in meters.|My prescription is -2.5 dioptres. 我的处方是-2.5屈光度。|Synonyms: diopter (American spelling)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
