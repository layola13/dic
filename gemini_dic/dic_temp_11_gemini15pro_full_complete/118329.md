merged:/mɜːrdʒd/ | adj. | 合并的，融合的 | Combined or blended together, especially so as to become a single entity. | The two companies merged to form a larger corporation.  两家公司合并成了一家更大的公司。

The merged document incorporates all the changes. 合并后的文件包含了所有的修改。 | 近义词：combined, integrated, unified, amalgamated | 反义词：separate, divided, distinct | 词性:形容词

词源: From the verb "merge," which comes from Middle English mergen, from Old French mergier "to plunge, immerse," from Latin mergere "to dip, plunge, immerse."
