agit-prop:/ˈædʒɪtprɒp/ | n. | 煽动宣传 |  Political propaganda, especially disseminated through literature, drama, music, or other art forms, with the intent to agitate the masses. It's a portmanteau of the Russian words "agitatsiya" (agitation) and "propaganda." |  The play was a blatant piece of agit-prop designed to stir up revolutionary fervor. 这部戏剧是一部旨在激发革命热情的露骨煽动宣传作品。|  propaganda, indoctrination | censorship, objective reporting | noun

agit-prop:/ˈædʒɪtprɒp/ | adj. | 煽动宣传的 | Relating to or characteristic of agit-prop. | The government labeled the independent newspaper as "agit-prop" and shut it down. 政府将这家独立报纸贴上“煽动宣传”的标签并将其关闭。| propagandistic, inflammatory | neutral, unbiased | adjective
