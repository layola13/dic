absent: /ˈæbsənt/ (US), /ˈæbsənt/ (UK)| adj. n.|缺席的，不在的；缺乏的；心不在焉的；缺席者|Being away from a place or not present.|He was absent from school yesterday. 他昨天没来上学。|近义词：missing, away, lacking|反义词：present, attending|adjective

absent: /ˈæbsənt/ (US), /ˈæbsənt/ (UK)| n.|缺席者|A person who is not present.|The absents were noted in the register. 缺席者被记录在册。|近义词：nonattender|反义词：attendee|noun

absent: /æbˈsɛnt/ (US), /æbˈsɛnt/ (UK)| vt.|使缺席；使离开|To keep (oneself) away.|He absented himself from the meeting. 他没有出席会议。|近义词：withdraw|反义词：attend|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin absentem (nominative absens), present participle of abesse "be away, be absent," from ab "away, from" + esse "to be."
