burgee: /ˈbɜːrdʒiː/ (US), /ˈbɜːɡiː/ (UK)
n.
三角旗，小旗，燕尾旗
A small triangular flag flown from the masthead of a yacht or powerboat, often indicating a club affiliation or an award won.
The yacht club burgee snapped in the wind.  游艇俱乐部的三角旗在风中啪啪作响。
The captain proudly hoisted the winning regatta's burgee. 船长自豪地升起了赢得帆船赛的三角旗。
近义词: pennant, flag
反义词: (No direct antonym for a physical object. Could consider "absence of a burgee")
词性: noun
