overload:/ˌoʊvərˈloʊd/ |v. n.|超载，过载；负担过重|To load with too great a burden or cargo.  To overburden.| The truck was overloaded with goods.  卡车货物超载了。|近义词: overburden, overtax, overwhelm |反义词: underload|verb

overload:/ˌoʊvərˈloʊd/ |n.|超载，过载；负担过重|An excessive load or amount.|The electrical overload caused a power outage. 电力超载导致了停电。|近义词: excess, surplus, glut |反义词: shortage, deficit|noun
