neocortexes:/ˈniːoʊˈkɔːrtɛksiːz/ | n. | 新皮质（复数） | The outer layer of the cerebral hemispheres, responsible for higher-level cognitive functions such as language, reasoning, and conscious thought.  It is the most recently evolved part of the brain. | The human neocortexes are much larger than those of other mammals. 人类的新皮质比其他哺乳动物大得多。| The neocortexes of primates are highly developed, enabling complex social interactions. 灵长类动物的新皮质高度发达，能够进行复杂的社会互动。 |  cortex, cerebral cortex | None | Noun
