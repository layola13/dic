interbreeds:/ˌɪntərˈbriːdz/
verb
杂交繁殖
to breed animals or plants of different species or varieties together
例句：Some farmers interbreed different types of cattle to produce a more resilient breed.  一些农民将不同品种的牛杂交以培育出更具抵抗力的品种。

例句：Interbreeding different species of flowers can result in unique and beautiful hybrids.  杂交不同种类的花卉可以产生独特而美丽的杂交品种。
近义词：crossbreed, hybridize
反义词：purebreed, inbreed
词性：v.
