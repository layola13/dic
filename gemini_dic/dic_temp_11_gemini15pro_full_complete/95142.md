inapproachable:/ˌɪnəˈproʊtʃəbl/ | adj. |难以接近的；难以达到的；冷淡的 |Not easy to be sociable with or start a conversation with; unfriendly and not easy to talk to. |例句：The CEO was inapproachable, making employees hesitant to share their ideas.  首席执行官难以接近，这使得员工不愿分享他们的想法。

例句：The mountain peak, shrouded in mist, seemed inapproachable.  笼罩在薄雾中的山峰似乎难以攀登。|近义词：unfriendly, aloof, distant, remote, inaccessible |反义词：approachable, friendly, accessible, warm |词性：adjective

inaccessible:/ˌɪnækˈsesəbl/ | adj. |难以接近的；难以达到的；难理解的| Difficult or impossible to reach, obtain, or understand. |例句：The remote village was inaccessible during the winter months due to heavy snow.  由于大雪，冬天无法到达那个偏远的村庄。

例句：Some of the philosophical concepts were inaccessible to the average reader.  一些哲学概念对于普通读者来说难以理解。|近义词：unreachable, unattainable, remote |反义词：accessible, reachable, attainable |词性：adjective
