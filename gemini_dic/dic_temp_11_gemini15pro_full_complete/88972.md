holier:/ˈhəʊliər (UK), ˈhoʊliər (US)| adj.|更神圣的，更圣洁的|Comparative form of "holy," meaning more sacred or more devout.|例句：He felt a holier presence in the cathedral. 他在教堂里感受到了一种更神圣的存在。

例句：She considered her work a holier calling than any other profession. 她认为她的工作比任何其他职业都更神圣。

近义词：more sacred, more devout, more pious|反义词：less holy, more profane, more secular|词性:adjective
词源：From Old English *hāligrā, comparative of hālig (“holy”), from Proto-Germanic *hailagaz (“holy”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱoylos (“whole, healthy”).
