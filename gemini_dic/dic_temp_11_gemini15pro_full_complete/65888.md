evolved:/ɪˈvɒlvd/ | adj. | 进化的，演变的 | Having developed gradually from a simpler form. |例句：Birds are believed to have evolved from dinosaurs. 人们认为鸟类是从恐龙进化而来的。|例句：The company has evolved into a major player in the market.  这家公司已经发展成为市场上的主要参与者。| 近义词：developed, advanced, progressed | 反义词：primitive, rudimentary | 词性:形容词

evolved:/ɪˈvɒlvd/ | v. |进化，发展，演变 | To develop gradually, especially from a simple to a more complex form. |例句：The company has evolved from a small startup to a multinational corporation. 这家公司已经从一家小型初创企业发展成为一家跨国公司。|例句：Mammals evolved from reptiles millions of years ago.  哺乳动物是从数百万年前的爬行动物进化而来的。| 近义词：develop, progress, advance | 反义词：devolve, regress | 词性:动词

词源: From Latin *ēvolūtus, past participle of ēvolvere "to unroll, unfold, develop," from ex- "out" + volvere "to roll."
