imbitter:/ɪmˈbɪtər/ | v. | 使怨恨；使苦恼 | To make someone feel bitter or resentful. |  The constant criticism imbittered her against her boss.  不断的批评使她对老板怀恨在心。| 近义词: embitter, sour, alienate | 反义词: sweeten, gratify, please | verb

imbitter:/ɪmˈbɪtər/ | v. | 使变苦 | To make something taste bitter. | The unripe fruit imbittered the stew.  未成熟的水果使炖菜变苦了。| 近义词: sour | 反义词: sweeten | verb
