apostolicity:/ˌæpəstəˈlɪsɪti/| n. |使徒性，使徒统绪|The quality of being derived from or adhering to the teachings and practices of the apostles.|The church emphasizes its apostolicity, tracing its origins back to St. Peter. 教会强调其使徒性，将其起源追溯到圣彼得。|Synonyms: authenticity, orthodoxy|Antonyms: heresy, heterodoxy|noun
