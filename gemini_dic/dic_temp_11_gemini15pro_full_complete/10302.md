apparentness:/əˈpærəntnɪs/| n.|明顯;顯而易見;表面性|The apparentness of his guilt was shocking. 他的罪行昭然若揭，令人震惊。|The apparentness of the error made it easy to correct.  这个错误很明显，很容易纠正。|近义词：obviousness, clarity, visibility, conspicuousness|反义词：inconspicuousness, obscurity, subtlety|词性:名词

词源:
From apparent + -ness.  Apparent comes from the Latin *apparēns*, present participle of *apparēre* "to appear."
