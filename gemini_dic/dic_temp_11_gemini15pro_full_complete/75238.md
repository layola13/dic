frolicness:/ˈfrɑːlɪknəs/ /ˈfrɒlɪknəs/| n. |嬉戏；欢乐；嬉闹|The quality or state of being frolicsome; gaiety; merriment.|The children's frolicness filled the house with laughter. 孩子们的嬉闹让房子里充满了笑声。|近义词：playfulness, gaiety, merriment, mirth, jollity|反义词：seriousness, solemnity, gloominess|词性:名词

Etymology: from frolic (n. & v.) + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  Frolic itself comes from Middle Dutch *vrolijc (modern Dutch vrolijk, "merry"), from Proto-Germanic *frōlijaz ("glad, joyful").
