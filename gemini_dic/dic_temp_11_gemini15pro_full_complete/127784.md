neighborly:/ˈneɪbərli/| adj.|友好的，睦邻的|Showing a friendly and helpful attitude toward one's neighbors; sociable.|例句：She was always neighborly, offering help whenever she could. 她总是很友善，随时提供帮助。|例句：It's important to be neighborly and get along with the people who live around you.  与周围的人和睦相处是很重要的。|近义词：friendly, amicable, amiable, cordial, hospitable|反义词：unfriendly, hostile, inhospitable|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From neighbor + -ly.  "Neighbor" comes from Old English *nēahgebūr, meaning "near dweller," from *nēah (near) + gebūr (dweller).  The suffix "-ly" is used to form adjectives and means "like" or "characteristic of."
