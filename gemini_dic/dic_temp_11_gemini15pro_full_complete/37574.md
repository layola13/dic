communalist:/kəˈmjuːnəlɪst/
n.|宗派主义者；地方自治主义者；公社主义者|A person who strongly supports the interests of their own group or community, sometimes at the expense of others; a person who advocates communal living.|The clashes were blamed on communalist politicians stirring up hatred. 这些冲突被归咎于宗派主义政客煽动仇恨。|近义词：sectarian, partisan, separatist|反义词：universalist, integrationist|词性: 名词

communalist:/kəˈmjuːnəlɪst/
adj.|宗派的；地方自治的；公社主义的|Relating to or characteristic of communalism.|Communalist violence flared up again. 宗派暴力再次爆发。|近义词：sectarian, parochial|反义词：national, universal|词性: 形容词
