overripening:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪpənɪŋ/| n.|过度成熟，过熟|The overripening of the bananas made them mushy. 香蕉过熟使它们变得糊状。|例句：The overripening of the fruit caused it to spoil. 水果过熟导致它腐败。|近义词：overmaturity|反义词:underripening|名词

overripe:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪp/| adj.|过熟的，熟透的|The overripe tomatoes were too soft to use in a salad. 过熟的西红柿太软了，不能用来做沙拉。|例句：The overripe fruit attracted swarms of flies. 过熟的水果吸引了一大群苍蝇。|近义词：overmature|反义词:underripe|形容词
