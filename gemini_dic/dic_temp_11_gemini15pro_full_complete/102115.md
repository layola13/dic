isotonically:/ˌaɪsəˈtɒnɪkli/ /ˌaɪsəˈtɑːnɪkli/| adv.|等张地|In a way that has the same osmotic pressure as another solution, especially one in a cell or a body fluid. |The red blood cells were suspended isotonically. 红细胞等张悬浮。|Near synonyms: isosmotically | Opposite: hypertonically, hypotonically|Adverb
