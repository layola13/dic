breadlike:/ˈbredlaɪk/
adjective
像面包的；面包似的
Resembling bread in appearance, texture, or consistency.
The cloud was large and breadlike. 这朵云又大又像面包。
近义词:doughy, yeasty
反义词:crumbly, crusty
词性:adjective
