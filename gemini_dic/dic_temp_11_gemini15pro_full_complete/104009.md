juiceless:/ˈdʒuːsləs/| adj.|无汁的，干燥的，枯燥的|Lacking juice; dry.  Figuratively, lacking vitality or interest.|The juiceless steak was tough to chew. 那块无汁的牛排很难嚼。|近义词：dry, arid, insipid, dull|反义词: juicy, succulent, interesting|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From juice + -less.  "Juice" comes from Old French "jus."  "-less" is a suffix meaning "without."
