literalist: /ˈlɪtərəlɪst/ | n. | 字面主义者；拘泥字义者 | Someone who adheres strictly to the literal meaning of words or texts, often without considering context or metaphorical interpretations. |  The literalist interpretation of the poem missed its deeper meaning.  字面主义者对这首诗的解读忽略了它更深层的含义。 | synonyms: fundamentalist, literal interpreter, strict constructionist | antonyms:  figurativist, interpreter, free thinker | noun

literalist: /ˈlɪtərəlɪst/ | adj. | 字面的；字面意义上的 | Taking words in their usual or most basic sense without metaphor or allegory. |  He gave a very literalist interpretation of the biblical text. 他对圣经文本的解释非常字面化。 | synonyms: literal, verbatim, word-for-word | antonyms: figurative, metaphorical, symbolic | adjective
