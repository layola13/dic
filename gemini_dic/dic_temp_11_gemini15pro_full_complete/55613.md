disventure:/dɪsˈvɛntʃər/| n.|不幸；灾难；厄运|An unfortunate incident; a mishap.|例句：He met with several disventures on his journey. 他在旅途中遇到了几次不幸。|例句：The expedition was plagued by a series of disventures. 这次探险受到了一系列灾难的困扰。|近义词：mishap, misfortune, adversity, calamity|反义词：fortune, luck, success|词性:名词

词源:
From Old French *desaventure, from des- "away, from" + aventure "adventure".  The sense evolution is from "misadventure" to simply "misfortune".
