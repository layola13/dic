dewier:/ˈdjuːɪər/| adj.|更湿润的，露水更重的|Covered with or containing more dew.  More moist or damp.|例句：The grass was dewier this morning than yesterday. 今早的草比昨天更湿润。

例句：The dewier air made my skin feel softer.  更湿润的空气让我的皮肤感觉更柔软。|近义词：moister, damper, wetter|反义词：drier, less dewy|词性:形容词
词源: From dew + -ier (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *dawaz.
