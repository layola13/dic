beakless:/ˈbiːklɪs/| adj.|无喙的，没有喙的|Lacking a beak; having no beak.  A beakless bird is an anomaly.|The beakless chicken struggled to eat. 这只没有喙的小鸡很难进食。|beakless bird|beaked|adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From beak + -less.  "Beak" comes from Middle English *bek*, from Old North French *bec*, ultimately from Vulgar Latin *beccus*, of uncertain origin. "-less" is a suffix meaning "without" or "lacking," from Old English *-lēas*.
