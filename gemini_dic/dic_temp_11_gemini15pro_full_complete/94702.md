improgressive:/ɪm.prəˈɡrɛs.ɪv/| adj.|非进行式的，非持续性的|Not progressive; not capable of being used in a progressive tense.  It refers to verbs that describe states rather than actions and therefore cannot be used in continuous tenses.|The verb "know" is *improgressive*. You cannot say "I am knowing the answer."|近义词：stative, non-progressive|反义词: progressive, dynamic|词性: adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From *im-* (not) + *progressive*.  *Progressive* ultimately derives from Latin *progressus*, past participle of *progredi* (to go forward).


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I know the answer. = 我知道答案。
She loves music. = 她喜欢音乐。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Incorrect: I am knowing the answer.
Incorrect: She is loving music.  (While grammatically incorrect in standard English, this form is sometimes used for emphasis in informal contexts.)

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have known the answer for a long time. = 我很久以前就知道答案了。
She has loved music since she was a child. = 她从小就喜欢音乐。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
Incorrect: I have been knowing the answer.
Incorrect: She has been loving music.

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I knew the answer yesterday. = 我昨天就知道答案了。
She loved the concert. = 她很喜欢那场音乐会。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
Incorrect: I was knowing the answer.
Incorrect: She was loving the concert.

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had known the answer before the test. = 考试前我就知道答案了。
She had loved music before she started playing an instrument. = 她开始演奏乐器之前就喜欢音乐了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
Incorrect: I had been knowing the answer.
Incorrect: She had been loving music.


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will know the answer tomorrow. = 我明天就会知道答案了。
She will love the movie. = 她会喜欢这部电影的。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
Incorrect: I will be knowing the answer.
Incorrect: She will be loving the movie.

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I will have known the answer by then. = 到那时我就会知道答案了。
She will have loved music for twenty years by next year. = 到明年她喜欢音乐就二十年了。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
Incorrect: I will have been knowing the answer.
Incorrect: She will have been loving music.


虚拟语气:
If I knew the answer, I would tell you. = 如果我知道答案，我会告诉你的。
I wish I knew what to do. = 我希望我知道该怎么办。

被动语态:
(Improgressive verbs generally cannot be used in the passive voice.)

疑问句:
Do you know the answer? = 你知道答案吗？
Does she love music? = 她喜欢音乐吗？

否定句:
I don't know the answer. = 我不知道答案。
She doesn't love music. = 她不喜欢音乐。


PHRASE->
Not applicable. "Improgressive" is a grammatical term, not a word typically used in phrases.  It describes verbs, not forming phrases itself.
