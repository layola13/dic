informatively:/ɪnˈfɔːrmətɪvli/| adv. |信息丰富地，提供大量信息地|Providing useful or interesting information.|The guidebook described the city informatively, covering its history, culture, and attractions. 这本指南信息丰富地描述了这座城市，涵盖了它的历史、文化和景点。|近义词：instructively, helpfully, usefully, enlighteningly|反义词：uninformatively, meaninglessly, vaguely|词性:副词

词源: From informative + -ly.  "Informative" comes from the Latin "informare" meaning "to shape, form, train, instruct."
