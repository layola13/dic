auburn:/ˈɔːbərn/| adj.|赤褐色的，红棕色的|A reddish-brown color, typically of hair.|例句：Her auburn hair shimmered in the sunlight. 她的赤褐色头发在阳光下闪闪发光。|近义词：reddish-brown, chestnut, Titian|反义词:blonde, light brown|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *albourne, from Old French auborne (“whitish, blond”), from Late Latin alburnus (“off-white, pale”), from Latin albus (“white”).  The meaning shifted from "whitish" to "reddish-brown" perhaps influenced by Latin alburnum (“sapwood”).
