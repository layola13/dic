homolateral:/ˌhɒməˈlætərəl/ /ˌhoʊməˈlætərəl/| adj.|同侧的，同边的|Situated or occurring on the same side of the body.|The homolateral reflex causes the stimulated muscle to contract.|近义词：ipsilateral|反义词:contralateral|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *homolateralis*, from homo- (same) + lateralis (of the side).
