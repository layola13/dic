cruder:/ˈkruːdər/ | adj. | 更粗糙的，更原始的，更粗鲁的 |  Comparative form of crude: more crude.  Meaning more raw, unrefined, or undeveloped; also, more vulgar or offensive. | The tools they used were cruder than ours. 他们使用的工具比我们的更粗糙。|  The first version of the software was much cruder than the final product.  该软件的第一个版本比最终产品粗糙得多。 | synonyms: coarser, rougher, less refined, less sophisticated, more primitive, more basic, simpler,  ruder, more vulgar, more offensive | antonyms: finer, smoother, more refined, more sophisticated, more advanced, more complex, more polished, more polite, more courteous |adjective
