guesswork:/ˈɡɛswɜːrk/| n.|猜测；臆测|An activity in which you try to find an answer or make a decision without having enough information to be certain.|The police identified the thief through guesswork. 警方通过猜测确定了小偷的身份。|synonyms: conjecture, speculation, supposition, surmise|antonyms: certainty, knowledge, fact|noun
