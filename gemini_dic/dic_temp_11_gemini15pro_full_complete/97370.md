infralapsarian:/ˌɪnfrəlæpˈsɛəriən/ | adj. n.|次堕落论的，次堕落论者|Relating to the theological doctrine of infralapsarianism, which holds that God's decree of election occurred after the fall of humanity.|The infralapsarian view is often contrasted with supralapsarianism. 次堕落论观点经常与超堕落论对比。|He considered himself an infralapsarian. 他自认为是次堕落论者。|近义词:sublapsarian|反义词:supralapsarian|词性:adjective, noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *infralapsarius*, from infra- 'below' + lapsus 'fall', past participle of labi 'to slip, fall'.
