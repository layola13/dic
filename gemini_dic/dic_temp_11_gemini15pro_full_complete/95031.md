inaccurate:/ɪnˈækjərət/ | adj. | 不准确的，不精确的 | Not correct or exact; containing errors or mistakes. | The report was inaccurate and misleading.  这份报告不准确且具有误导性。|  The information he provided was inaccurate. 他提供的信息不准确。 | 近义词：wrong, incorrect, false, erroneous, imprecise | 反义词：accurate, correct, precise, exact | 词性：形容词

词源:  Late 16th century: from in- ‘not’ + accurate.
