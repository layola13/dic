overabundance:/ˌoʊvərəˈbʌndəns/| n.| 过剩，过量 | An excessive amount of something; a surplus. | The farmer had an overabundance of tomatoes this year and had to sell them at a discount. 今年农民的西红柿产量过剩，不得不打折出售。| synonyms: surplus, excess, profusion, plethora, glut | antonyms: scarcity, lack, deficiency, dearth | noun
