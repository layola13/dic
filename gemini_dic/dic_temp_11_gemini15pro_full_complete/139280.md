obliterate:/əˈblɪtəreɪt/
verb
消灭，抹去，彻底摧毁
To remove or destroy all traces of; do away with completely.
The bombing raid obliterated the small village. 轰炸袭击彻底摧毁了这个小村庄。
The heavy snowfall obliterated all signs of the path. 大雪掩盖了所有路径的痕迹。
synonyms:destroy, erase, annihilate, expunge, wipe out
antonyms:create, build, establish, preserve
