hilliness:/ˈhɪlɪnəs/
n.
丘陵起伏; 多丘陵
the quality of being hilly; the presence of many hills.
The hilliness of the terrain made cycling difficult.  这片地形的丘陵起伏使得骑自行车很困难。

flatness, levelness
