exploiters:/ɪkˈsplɔɪtərz/
n.|剥削者，利用者|People or entities that take unfair advantage of a situation or others for their own benefit. |The exploiters of child labor should be punished.  童工剥削者应该受到惩罚。|Synonyms: abusers, manipulators, profiteers|Antonyms: benefactors, philanthropists|noun

exploiters:/ɪkˈsplɔɪtərz/
n.|开发者，开拓者| (Less common) Those who make full use of resources or opportunities, especially in a business context. |The exploiters of new technologies often reap significant rewards. 新技术的开发者通常会获得丰厚的回报。|Synonyms: developers, pioneers, innovators|Antonyms: conservatives, laggards|noun
