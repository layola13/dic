baltia:/ˈbɔːltiə/ | n. |波罗的海地区; 波罗的地区|A region on the eastern coast of the Baltic Sea, historically inhabited by Baltic peoples.  It generally comprises the modern-day countries of Lithuania, Latvia, and Estonia, but sometimes also includes parts of surrounding countries like Poland, Russia, and Germany.|The amber found in Baltia is highly prized. 波罗的海地区的琥珀非常珍贵。|Near synonyms: Baltic States, Baltic countries|Antonym: N/A|noun


baltia:/ˈbɔːltiə/ | adj. |波罗的海的|Relating to the Baltic Sea or the Baltia region.|The Baltia climate is generally cool and damp. 波罗的海地区的气候普遍凉爽潮湿。|Near synonyms: Baltic|Antonym: N/A|adjective
