decility:/dɪˈsɪləti/
noun
顺从；温顺；服从
The quality of being docile; submissiveness; meekness.
Her decility made her an easy target for bullies. 她的温顺使她很容易成为欺凌的对象。
Synonyms: docility, meekness, submissiveness, obedience, compliance
Antonyms: defiance, rebelliousness, disobedience, insubordination
