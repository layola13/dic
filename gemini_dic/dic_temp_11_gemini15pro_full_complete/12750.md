assiduousness:/ˌæsɪˈdjuːəsnəs/| n.|勤奋，刻苦，孜孜不倦|The student's assiduousness in her studies resulted in excellent grades.  这个学生学习上的勤奋使她取得了优异的成绩。|近义词：diligence, industry, perseverance|反义词：laziness, indolence, negligence|词性:名词
Etymology: From Late Latin *assiduitas, from Latin assiduus (“sitting near, attending upon, constant, diligent”), from ad- + sedēre (“to sit”).
