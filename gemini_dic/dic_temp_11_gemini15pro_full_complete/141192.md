oomiak:/ˈuːmiˌæk/ | n. |尤皮克人或因纽特人的大型敞篷皮划艇|A large open skin boat used by the Yupik and Inuit peoples of the Arctic.  It is traditionally made of walrus or seal skins stretched over a wooden frame. |The family traveled across the icy waters in their oomiak.  这家人乘坐他们的尤米亚克皮划艇穿越冰冷的水域。|Synonyms: umiak, skin boat, open boat |Antonyms: kayak (smaller, closed-deck boat) | noun
