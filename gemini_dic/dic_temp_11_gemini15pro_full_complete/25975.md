bursae: /ˈbɜːrsiː/ (US), /ˈbɜːsiː/ (UK)| n. |滑囊|A small fluid-filled sac located between bones, tendons, muscles, and skin that reduces friction and cushions these structures during movement.|Bursitis is inflammation of the bursae.|Synonyms: sac, pouch|Antonyms: None|Noun

bursa: /ˈbɜːrsə/ (US), /ˈbɜːsə/ (UK)| n. |滑囊|Singular form of bursae.  Refers to a single fluid-filled sac.|The bursa in his knee was inflamed.|Synonyms: sac, pouch|Antonyms: None|Noun
