dertrotheca: /dɛrˈtrɒθɪkə/ | n. |  鞘翅；翅鞘 |  A sheath-like covering, specifically referring to the hardened forewings of beetles (Coleoptera) that protect the membranous hindwings used for flight. | The dertrotheca of the stag beetle was remarkably iridescent.  锹形虫的鞘翅非常有光泽。|  elytra, wing case |  None | Noun
