chaffwax:/ˈtʃæfˌwæks/
noun
麦蜡；蜂房渣滓蜡
A wax produced by bees and used in constructing honeycombs.
The beekeeper carefully collected the chaffwax to make candles. 养蜂人小心地收集蜂房渣滓蜡来制作蜡烛。
synonyms: beeswax, honeycomb wax
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but could consider synthetic waxes as a contrast)
