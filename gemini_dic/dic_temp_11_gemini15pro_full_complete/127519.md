necrobiotic:/ˌnɛkrəʊbaɪˈɒtɪk/| adj.|坏死的，变性的|Relating to or characterized by necrobiosis, a degenerative process preceding necrosis (death of living tissue).|Necrobiotic xanthogranuloma is a rare skin condition.  坏死性黄瘤是一种罕见的皮肤病。|The necrobiotic changes in the skin were evident. 皮肤的坏死性变化很明显。|近义词：degenerative, necrotizing|反义词：regenerative, restorative|词性:形容词

necrosis:/nəˈkroʊsɪs/| n.|坏死，组织坏死|The death of most or all of the cells in an organ or tissue due to disease, injury, or failure of the blood supply.|The doctor diagnosed the tissue as necrosis. 医生诊断该组织为坏死。|The severe burn caused necrosis of the skin. 严重的烧伤导致皮肤坏死。|近义词：gangrene, mortification|反义词：growth, regeneration|词性:名词
