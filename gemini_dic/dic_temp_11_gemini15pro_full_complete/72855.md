folklorist:/ˈfoʊklɔːrɪst/| n. |民俗学家|A scholar who studies folklore, which includes traditional customs, beliefs, stories, songs, and sayings of a people.|The folklorist spent years collecting folktales from remote villages.  这位民俗学家花了数年时间从偏远村庄收集民间故事。|Synonym:  ethnologist, anthropologist|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps one could consider someone who actively destroys or suppresses cultural traditions)|Noun
