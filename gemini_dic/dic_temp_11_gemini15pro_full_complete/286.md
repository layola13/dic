abelite:/ˈeɪbəˌlaɪt/
n.
阿贝力特人;阿贝力特(一种炸药)
A member of a religious sect founded in the 18th century by Abiezer Coppe;  A type of explosive.
John, an abelite, believed in spiritual perfection achievable in earthly life. 约翰，一个阿贝力特人，相信尘世生活中可以达到精神上的完美。
The abelite explosive was used in mining operations. 阿贝力特炸药用于采矿作业。
