expound:/ɪkˈspaʊnd/| v. |阐述，解释，详细说明|to explain or talk about something in detail|The professor expounded on the theory of relativity. 教授详细阐述了相对论。|近义词：explain, elaborate, clarify, interpret|反义词：summarize, condense, obscure|词性:动词
词源: From Latin *exponere*  "to put forth, set forth, explain," from *ex* "out" + *ponere* "to put, place."
