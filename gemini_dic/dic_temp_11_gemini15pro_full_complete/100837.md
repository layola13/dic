introgression:/ˌɪntrəˈɡrɛʃən/ | n. |基因渗入，渐渗杂交|The transfer of genetic information from one species to another as a result of hybridization between them and repeated backcrossing. |Example 1: Evidence of introgression from Neanderthals into the modern human genome has been found.  基因渗入的证据已经从尼安德特人到现代人类基因组中被发现。

Example 2: Introgression can play a significant role in the evolution of new species. 渐渗杂交可以在新物种的进化中发挥重要作用。|Synonyms: gene flow, hybridization, genetic admixture|Antonyms: genetic isolation|noun
