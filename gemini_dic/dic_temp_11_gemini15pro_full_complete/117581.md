megalomania: /ˌmɛɡələˈmeɪniə/ | n. | 夸大妄想症；极度权力欲 | A mental disorder marked by delusions of grandeur, power, wealth, or omnipotence.  | He suffers from megalomania and believes he is the king of the world. 他患有夸大妄想症，认为自己是世界之王。 | Example:  His megalomania led him to believe he could single-handedly solve the world's problems. 他的夸大妄想症让他相信他可以单枪匹马地解决世界上的所有问题。 | Synonym: grandiosity, narcissism, egomania | Antonym: humility, modesty | noun


Etymology: From Greek megalo- (large, great) + -mania (madness, obsession).
