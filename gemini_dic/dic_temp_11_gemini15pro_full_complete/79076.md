glabrate:/ˈɡleɪbreɪt/| adj.|近乎无毛的，渐脱落的|Becoming glabrous or nearly so; losing hairs with age or maturity.  The plant's leaves are glabrate, smooth to the touch.|The stems of this species are initially hairy but become glabrate with age. 这种植物的茎最初是有毛的，但随着年龄的增长会变得近乎无毛。

The glabrate surface of the fruit makes it easier to clean.  这种水果近乎无毛的表面使它更容易清洗。|近义词：glabrous, smooth|反义词: hairy, pubescent|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin glaber (smooth, hairless) + -ate (suffix forming adjectives).
