malacoderm:/ˌmæləˈkəʊdəːm/ | n. |软甲虫|An insect of the Malacodermata, a now-obsolete group of beetles having soft elytra.  This group included fireflies, glowworms, and soldier beetles. |Fireflies are a type of malacoderm. 萤火虫是一种软甲虫。| The museum's collection includes several rare specimens of malacoderms.  博物馆的收藏包括几个稀有的软甲虫标本。 | synonym: soft-bodied beetle,  rove beetle (loosely) | antonym: hard-bodied beetle (general contrast) | noun
