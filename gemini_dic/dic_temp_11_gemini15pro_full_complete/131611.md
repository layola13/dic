noncompliances:/ˌnɑːnkəmˈplaɪənsɪz/
n.
不符合，违规行为
Failure or refusal to comply with a rule, regulation, or request.
The company was fined for multiple noncompliances with environmental regulations.  这家公司因多次违反环境法规而被罚款。
