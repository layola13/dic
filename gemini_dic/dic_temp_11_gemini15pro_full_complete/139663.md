occupations:/ˌɑːkjəˈpeɪʃənz/ | n. |职业，工作|A job or profession.|例句：Various occupations require different skill sets. 不同的职业需要不同的技能。|例句：He listed his occupation as "writer." 他把自己的职业列为“作家”。|近义词：professions, jobs, careers, trades, vocations |反义词: unemployment, idleness, leisure |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *occupacioun, from Old French occupation, from Latin occupātiō (“a seizing, taking possession of”), from occupō (“I seize, take possession of”), from ob- (“towards”) + capiō (“I take, seize”).
