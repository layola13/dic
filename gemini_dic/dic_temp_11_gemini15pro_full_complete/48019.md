decision-making:/dɪˈsɪʒən ˌmeɪkɪŋ/ |n.|决策； تصمیم گیری|The process of making choices by identifying a decision, considering alternatives, gathering information, and assessing alternative resolutions.|The company's decision-making process is very efficient.  这家公司的决策过程非常高效。| synonyms:  choice, selection, determination| antonyms: indecision, vacillation |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From decision + making. Decision comes from the Late Middle English *decisioun*, from Old French *decision*, from Latin *dēcisiō*, *dēcisiōn*, from *dēcīdere* "to decide, determine," from *de-* "off, down" + *caedere* "to cut." Making comes from Middle English *maken*, from Old English *macian* "to make, form, build, compose, create."
