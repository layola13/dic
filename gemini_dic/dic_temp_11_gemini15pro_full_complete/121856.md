misrepresented:/ˌmɪsrɛprɪˈzɛntɪd/
|v.|被歪曲地；被错误地描述地|Past participle of misrepresent|The company's financial situation was seriously misrepresented in the report. 该公司的财务状况在报告中被严重歪曲了。|Synonyms: distorted, falsified, misstated|Antonyms: accurately represented, truthfully depicted|verb

misrepresent:/ˌmɪsrɛprɪˈzɛnt/
|v.|歪曲；不如实地叙述|To present or describe (someone or something) in a false or misleading way|He claimed that the newspaper had misrepresented his views. 他声称报纸歪曲了他的观点。|Synonyms: distort, falsify, misstate|Antonyms: represent accurately, portray truthfully|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- + represent.  "Mis-" is a prefix meaning "wrong" or "badly." "Represent" comes from the Latin "repraesentare," meaning "to present again, to exhibit, to portray."
