backwoods:/ˈbækˌwʊdz/| adj. |偏僻的，落后的|characteristic of remote, sparsely populated areas; unsophisticated, rustic|例句：The family lived in a backwoods cabin with no electricity. 这家人住在偏僻的小木屋里，没有电。|例句：She had a backwoods accent that was hard to understand. 她操着一口很难理解的乡下口音。|近义词：rural, remote, isolated, provincial|反义词:urban, sophisticated, modern|词性:形容词

词源: From back + wood, referring to the wooded areas far from settled regions.
