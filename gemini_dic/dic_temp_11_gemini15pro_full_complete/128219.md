neurochord:/ˈnjʊərəˌkɔːrd/ | n. |神经索|A rudimentary nervous system found in the embryos of chordates, developing into the spinal cord and brain in vertebrates.  It is a defining characteristic of the phylum Chordata. |Example 1: The neurochord is a key feature in the development of the central nervous system. 神经索是中枢神经系统发育的关键特征。

Example 2:  Damage to the neurochord in the embryonic stage can have severe consequences.  胚胎期神经索的损伤会导致严重的后果。|近义词:notochord (in early development), nerve cord, spinal cord (in later development)|反义词: None (It's a specific anatomical structure)|词性:noun
