equalized: /ˈiːkwəlaɪzd/ | v. | 使均等，使相等 | to make equal; to become equal | The government tried to equalize the distribution of wealth. 政府试图使财富分配均等。| The scores were equalized in the final minutes of the game. 比分在比赛的最后几分钟被扳平了。|  synonyms: balance, level, even, match | antonyms:  differentiate, unbalance, vary | verb

equalized: /ˈiːkwəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 均等的，相等的 | made equal; balanced | The two teams were equalized at halftime.  两队在半场时比分相同。|  The equalized distribution of resources ensures fairness. 均衡的资源分配确保了公平。| synonyms: balanced, level, even, uniform | antonyms: unequal, uneven, imbalanced, disparate | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *equallen*, from Old French *egaller*, from Latin *aequālis* (“equal”), from *aequus* (“level, even, equal”).
