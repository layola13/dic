nonhabitably:/ˌnɒnˈhæbɪtəbli/
adjective
不适宜居住地
not suitable for living in
例句：The planet was deemed nonhabitably due to its toxic atmosphere.  这个星球由于其有毒的大气层被认为不适宜居住。

例句：The extreme temperatures make the desert nonhabitably for most life forms. 极端的温度使得沙漠对大多数生命形式来说不适宜居住。

近义词：uninhabitable, unlivable
反义词：habitable, livable
词性: adjective

词源:
From non- + habitable + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," "habitable" comes from the Latin "habitabilis" meaning "fit to be lived in," and "-ly" is a suffix that forms adverbs from adjectives.
