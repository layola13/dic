joyriding: /ˈdʒɔɪraɪdɪŋ/
n.
偷车兜风，驾驶取乐
The act of stealing a car and driving it recklessly for pleasure.
例句：The teenagers were arrested for joyriding in a stolen sports car.  这些青少年因偷窃跑车兜风而被捕。

例句：Joyriding is a serious crime that can result in injury or death.  偷车兜风是一种严重的犯罪行为，可能导致伤亡。

近义词：car theft, grand theft auto
反义词：(No direct antonym, but consider "lawful driving" or "responsible driving")
词性: noun
