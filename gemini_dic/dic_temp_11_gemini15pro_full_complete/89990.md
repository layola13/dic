horsinesses:/ˈhɔːrsɪnɪsɪz/
noun
爱好骑马；对马的喜爱；马性；马的习性

She displayed her horsinesses by constantly talking about her riding lessons and her favorite breeds. 她不断地谈论她的骑马课和她最喜欢的品种，展现了她对马的喜爱。

Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider using disinterest in horses)
