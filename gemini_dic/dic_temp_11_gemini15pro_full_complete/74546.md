frats:/fræts/
n.
兄弟会；大学生联谊会
Fraternities, often referred to as frats, are social organizations at colleges and universities.
例句：He joined one of the frats on campus.  他加入了校园里的一个兄弟会。

例句：The frats are known for their parties.  这些兄弟会以他们的派对而闻名。
近义词：fraternities
反义词:sororities
词性:n. (noun)
