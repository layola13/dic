antidiarrheals:/ˌæntiˌdaɪəˈriːəlz/| n.|止泻药|Medications used to treat diarrhea.|The doctor prescribed antidiarrheals to help control her symptoms. 医生开了止泻药来帮助控制她的症状。|Synonyms: antidiarrheal agents, antidiarrheal medications|Antonyms: laxatives, purgatives|noun
antidiarrheal:/ˌæntiˌdaɪəˈriːəl/| adj.|止泻的|Having the property of preventing or relieving diarrhea.|He was given antidiarrheal medicine. 他服用了止泻药。|Synonyms: antidiarrhetic|Antonyms: laxative, purgative|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + diarrheal.  "Anti-" comes from Greek "anti" meaning "against, opposite of." "Diarrheal" comes from "diarrhea," which ultimately derives from Greek "diarrhoia," from "diarerein"  meaning "to flow through," from "dia" ("through") + "rhein" ("to flow").
