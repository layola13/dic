nickelodeon:/ˌnɪkəlˈoʊdiən/| n.|五分钱自动点唱机；早期电影院；儿童电视频道|A nickelodeon was originally a type of early movie theater where admission cost five cents (a nickel).  Later, it became the name of a children's television channel. |例句：We went to the nickelodeon to see a silent film. 我们去五分钱电影院看了一部无声电影。

例句：My kids love watching cartoons on Nickelodeon. 我的孩子们喜欢在尼克儿童频道看动画片。|近义词：cinema, movie theater, picture house (for early movie theaters);  children's television network (for the TV channel)|反义词: None|词性:noun
