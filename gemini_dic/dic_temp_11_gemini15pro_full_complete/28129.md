capite:/ˈkæpɪteɪ/
n.
head, chapter, heading
A heading or chapter, especially in a legal or formal document.  Derived from the Latin word "caput" meaning "head".
The lawyer referred to the specific capite in the legal document.  律师提到了法律文件中具体的章节。
The capite of this chapter deals with contract law.  本章的标题涉及合同法。
synonym: heading, chapter, section, title
antonym:  (No direct antonym for a structural element like "capite")
noun
