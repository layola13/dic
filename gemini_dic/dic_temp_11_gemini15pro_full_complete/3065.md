aerolith: /ˈɛərəlɪθ/| n.|陨石|A stony meteorite. A meteor that survives passage through the earth's atmosphere and reaches the ground. |例句：The aerolith crashed into a field, creating a small crater. 这颗陨石坠落在一块田野里，形成了一个小陨石坑。

例句：Scientists studied the aerolith to learn more about the early solar system. 科学家们研究这颗陨石以了解更多关于早期太阳系的信息。|近义词：meteorite, meteoroid|反义词: None|词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek ἀήρ (aḗr, “air”) + λίθος (líthos, “stone”).
