coordinateness:/koʊˈɔːrdɪnɪtnəs/| n.|协调性，配合性|The coordinateness of the dancers' movements was breathtaking. 舞蹈演员动作的协调性令人叹为观止。|例句：The project's success depends on the coordinateness of all the teams involved.  这个项目的成功取决于所有参与团队的协调性。

例句：The coordinateness of their efforts ensured the smooth running of the event. 他们的努力协调一致，确保了活动的顺利进行。

|近义词：harmony, coherence, synchronization, integration|反义词:discordance, disharmony, incoordination|词性:名词


Etymology->

From coordinate + -ness.  "Coordinate" comes from Late Latin  *coōrdinātus, past participle of coōrdināre "to arrange, regulate," from com- "together" + ōrdināre "to ordain, arrange."
