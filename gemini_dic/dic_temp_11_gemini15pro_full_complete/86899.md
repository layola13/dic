hemistichal:/ˌhemɪˈstɪkəl/
adjective
半行的，半诗行的
Of, relating to, or constituting a hemistich.  A hemistich is half a line of verse.
The poem used hemistichal rhymes throughout. 这首诗通篇使用了半诗行韵。
Near synonyms: half-line, half-verse
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider "full-line" or "complete verse")
词性: adjective
