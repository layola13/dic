glimmerless:/ˈɡlɪmərləs/| adj.|无微光的，黯淡的|Lacking any glimmer or faint light; completely dark or dull.|The glimmerless night made it difficult to see the path.  无微光的夜晚使得很难看清道路。|例句：The cave was glimmerless and silent.  洞穴里一片漆黑，寂静无声。
|近义词：dark, dim, gloomy, murky|反义词：bright, shining, luminous|词性:形容词

glimmer:/ˈɡlɪmər/| n. vi.|微光，闪烁；隐约出现|A faint or wavering light; to shine faintly or unsteadily.|A glimmer of hope appeared on the horizon.  一丝希望出现在地平线上。|例句：The candle glimmered in the window.  蜡烛在窗子里闪烁着微光。|近义词：flicker, gleam, shimmer|反义词：darkness, gloom|词性:名词，动词
