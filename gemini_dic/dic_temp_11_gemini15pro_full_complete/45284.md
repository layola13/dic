culturation:/ˌkʌltʃəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 文化适应; 文化修养; 陶冶 | The process by which a person or group acquires the culture of another group, often in the context of immigration or colonization. |  Example 1: The culturation of immigrants is a complex process involving adaptation to new customs and values.  移民的文化适应是一个复杂的过程，包括适应新的习俗和价值观。

Example 2:  Her research focused on the culturation of indigenous peoples in the Americas. 她的研究集中于美洲土著人民的文化适应。 | 近义词: acculturation, assimilation, adaptation | 反义词: deculturation, alienation | 词性: 名词
