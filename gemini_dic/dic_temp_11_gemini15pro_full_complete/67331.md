|n.|即兴表演者，即兴演说者|A person who performs or speaks without prior preparation.  Someone who improvises, particularly in speech or music.|He was a skilled extemporizer, able to deliver a compelling speech on any topic.  他是一位熟练的即兴演说者，能够就任何话题发表引人入胜的演讲。|Synonyms: improviser, ad-libber, off-the-cuffer|Antonyms: planner, preparer|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the Latin "ex tempore" meaning "out of the time/moment", meaning "at the time," "immediately," "without preparation."  The word evolved into "extemporize" (verb) and then "extemporizer" (noun).
