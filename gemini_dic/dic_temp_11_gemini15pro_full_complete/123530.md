monotreme:/ˈmɒnəˌtriːm/ (US) /ˈmɑːnəˌtriːm/| n. |单孔目动物|An egg-laying mammal, such as the echidna or platypus, characterized by a single opening (cloaca) for the passage of eggs, sperm, and excrement.|The platypus is a fascinating monotreme found in Australia.  鸭嘴兽是在澳大利亚发现的一种迷人的单孔目动物。|
|Synonyms: prototherian (more technical)|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym; could consider placental mammal or marsupial as contrasting groups) | noun |
