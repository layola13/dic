distilled:/dɪˈstɪld/ | adj. | 蒸馏的；提炼的；精炼的 | Purified or concentrated by distillation; refined or extracted.  |  例句：The chemist analyzed the distilled water.  化学家分析了蒸馏水。 | 近义词：purified, refined, concentrated, extracted | 反义词：unrefined, crude, raw | 词性:形容词
distill:/dɪˈstɪl/ | v. | 蒸馏；提炼；渗出；浓缩 | Purify (a liquid) by heating it so that it vaporizes, then cooling and condensing the vapor and collecting the resulting liquid; extract the essential meaning or most important aspects of. | 例句：They distill whisky from malted barley. 他们用麦芽大麦酿造威士忌。 | 近义词：purify, refine, extract, concentrate | 反义词：dilute, adulterate | 词性:动词
