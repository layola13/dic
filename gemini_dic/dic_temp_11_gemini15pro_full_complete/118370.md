meritoriousnesses:/ˌmɛrɪˈtɔːriəsnɪsɪz/
noun
功绩；优点；价值
The state or quality of being meritorious; desert.  The sum of merits.
His meritoriousnesses in the field of science earned him widespread acclaim.  他在科学领域的功绩为他赢得了广泛赞誉。
Synonyms: merits, virtues, excellences, achievements, accomplishments
Antonyms: flaws, shortcomings, defects, faults
词性: 名词
