gazans:/ɡəˈzɑːnz/
n.
加沙人
People who live in or originate from Gaza.
The gazans faced many hardships during the conflict.  加沙人民在冲突期间面临许多困难。
The UN provided aid to the gazans. 联合国向加沙人民提供了援助。
近义词:Palestinians
反义词:Israelis
词性:noun
