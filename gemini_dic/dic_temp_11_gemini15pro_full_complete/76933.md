gaper:/ˈɡeɪpər/| n.| 呆呆地看的人；张口结舌的人；容易上当的人 |Someone who stares open-mouthed, typically in amazement or wonder; a gullible person. |例句：The tourists were gapers, staring in awe at the ancient ruins.  游客们都看呆了，敬畏地凝视着古老的废墟。

例句：He's such a gaper, he'll believe anything you tell him. 他真是个容易上当的人，你说什么他都信。|近义词：onlooker, spectator, gawker, simpleton, dupe |反义词: cynic, skeptic |词性: noun
