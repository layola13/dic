galvanize:/ˈɡælvənaɪz/| vt.|激励；刺激；电镀；镀锌|To stimulate or excite as if by electric shock; to coat (iron or steel) with zinc.|The coach's speech galvanized the team into action. 教练的讲话激励了队员们行动起来。

The iron railings were galvanized to prevent rust. 铁栏杆镀锌以防生锈。|近义词：stimulate, excite, arouse, electrify, energize, invigorate, jolt, shock, startle|反义词：calm, soothe, pacify, lull, tranquilize|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *galvaniser*, from Italian *galvanizzare*, from Luigi Galvani (1737-1798), Italian physician and physicist, who discovered the effect of electricity on muscles + *-izzare*.
