anaesthetization:/ˌænɪsˌθɛtɪˈzeɪʃən/
|n.|麻醉；麻醉状态|The anaesthetization was successful and the patient felt no pain. 麻醉成功了，病人没有感到疼痛。|English Explanation: The process of inducing anesthesia, a state of temporary loss of sensation or awareness.|例句：The anaesthetization took longer than expected. 麻醉的时间比预期的要长。


The anaesthetization was necessary for the surgical procedure.  麻醉对于外科手术是必要的。

|近义词：anesthesia|反义词:|词性:noun



词源: From Greek "anaisthesia," meaning "lack of sensation," from "an-" ("not") + "aisthesis" ("sensation").
