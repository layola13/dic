acrogen:/ˈækrədʒən/| n. |顶生植物；顶端生长的植物|A plant that grows from the apex, such as a fern or a moss.|Ferns are acrogens, meaning their growth occurs at the tip of the stem.  蕨类植物是顶生植物，这意味着它们的生长发生在茎的顶端。|Synonyms:  acrocarpous plant|Antonyms:  pleurocarpous plant|Noun
