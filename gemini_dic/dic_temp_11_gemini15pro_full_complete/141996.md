organise:/ˈɔːɡənaɪz/ (British) /ˈɔːrɡənaɪz/ (American)| v. |组织，安排|To arrange or put in order.  To make arrangements or preparations for.|例句：I need to organise my notes before the exam. 我需要在考试前整理我的笔记。|近义词：arrange, coordinate, manage, plan, structure|反义词:disorganise, mess up|verb

organise:/ˈɔːɡənaɪz/ (British) /ˈɔːrɡənaɪz/ (American)| v. |组织，成立|To form (a group or activity).|例句：They organised a protest against the new law. 他们组织了一场反对新法律的抗议活动。|近义词：establish, found, set up|反义词:disband, dismantle|verb
