coupling:/ˈkʌplɪŋ/ | n. | 耦合，连接，联轴器 |The act of joining or connecting two or more things together. It can refer to a physical connection, like a mechanical coupling, or a more abstract connection, like the coupling of two events. |The coupling between the two train cars broke. 两节火车车厢之间的联轴器断了。

The economic coupling between the two countries has strengthened in recent years.  近年来两国之间的经济联系加强了。| synonyms: connection, joining, union, link, linkage, bond, tie | antonyms: separation, disconnection, decoupling | noun


coupling:/ˈkʌplɪŋ/ | n. | 配对，结合 | The pairing or combining of two things, often implying a close relationship. | The coupling of music and dance creates a powerful artistic experience. 音乐和舞蹈的结合创造了强大的艺术体验。| synonyms: pairing, combination, union, match | antonyms: separation, division | noun
