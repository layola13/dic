advocate:/ˈædvəkət/ (US), /ˈædvəkeɪt/ (UK)| n. vt.| 提倡者，拥护者；辩护律师；倡导，拥护|A person who publicly supports or recommends a particular cause or policy.  To publicly recommend or support.|例句：She is a strong advocate for animal rights. 她是一位坚定的动物权利倡导者。|近义词：supporter, proponent, champion, backer|反义词：opponent, antagonist, critic|n.
