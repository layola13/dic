overingeniously: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʒiːniəsli/
adjective
过于巧妙地; 过于精巧地
in a way that is too clever and complicated
Her argument, though overingeniously constructed, ultimately failed to convince the jury. 她的论证虽然构建得过于精巧，但最终未能说服陪审团。
Synonyms: excessively cleverly, too elaborately, over-subtlely
Antonyms: simply, plainly, artlessly
词性: 副词 (adverb)
