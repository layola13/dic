lungi:/ˈlʌŋɡiː/| n.|隆基，纱笼，围裙|A garment consisting of a length of cotton or silk cloth wrapped around the waist or hips, traditionally worn by men in India, Sri Lanka, Bangladesh, Myanmar, and other parts of Southeast Asia.|He wore a brightly coloured lungi.|Synonyms: sarong, dhoti, mundu|Antonyms: trousers, pants|noun

lungi:/ˈlʌŋɡiː/| n.|隆基|A long piece of cloth worn wrapped around the waist, traditionally by men in South Asia.|He tied his lungi securely before wading into the river.|Synonyms: sarong, dhoti, mundu|Antonyms: trousers, pants|noun
