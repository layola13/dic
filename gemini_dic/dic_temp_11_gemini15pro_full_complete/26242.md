butterscotches:/ˈbʌtərˌskɒtʃɪz/ (UK) /ˈbʌtərˌskɑːtʃɪz/ (US)| n. |奶油糖果（复数）|A type of hard, brittle toffee made with butter, brown sugar, and sometimes vanilla.  Derived from "butter" and "scotch," likely referring to the process of scoring or cutting the candy into pieces.|
