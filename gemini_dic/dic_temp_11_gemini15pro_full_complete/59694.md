echopractic:/ˌekəˈpræktɪk/
adjective
回声定位的，回声实践的
Relating to or involving the use of echolocation, especially by animals such as bats and dolphins.  This word is not commonly used and is more of a technical term.
Bats use echopractic navigation to fly in the dark.  蝙蝠使用回声定位导航在黑暗中飞行。
Echolocating bats emit calls and listen for the returning echoes to build a sonic map of their surroundings. 回声定位蝙蝠发出叫声并聆听返回的回声，以构建周围环境的声波图。
