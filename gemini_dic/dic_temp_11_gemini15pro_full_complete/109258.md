lemma's:/ˈlɛməz/ | n. | 词元; 词根; 引理 | The lemma of "went" is "go".  "went"的词元是"go"。 | Etymology: From Late Latin lemma, from Greek lēmma "something received, a gift, gain, also premise, assumption," from lambanein "to take." | Example:  The dictionary entry lists the lemma followed by its various inflected forms.  词典条目列出了词元及其各种变形形式。

Example:  In linguistics, a lemma is the canonical form of a word. 在语言学中，词元是一个单词的规范形式。 | Synonyms: headword, base form, root | Antonyms: inflection, derivative | noun
