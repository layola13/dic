consultatively:/kənˈsʌltətɪvli/
adverb| 咨询地，协商地|In a way that involves consulting with others; by seeking advice or discussion.|The decision was made consultatively, involving all stakeholders. 这个决定是通过咨询所有利益相关者做出的。|例句：The project was managed consultatively, with regular meetings between the team and the client.  该项目以协商的方式进行管理，团队和客户之间定期举行会议。

例句：She approached the problem consultatively, seeking input from her colleagues before making a decision. 她以协商的方式处理这个问题，在做出决定之前征求了同事的意见。|近义词：collaboratively, cooperatively, jointly|反义词：autocratically, dictatorially, unilaterally|词性:副词
