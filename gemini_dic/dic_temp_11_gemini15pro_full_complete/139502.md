obstructive:/əbˈstrʌktɪv/| adj.|阻塞的，阻碍的，妨碍的|Causing or tending to cause something to be obstructed.|The obstructive tactics of the opposition party delayed the passage of the bill. 反对党的阻挠策略拖延了法案的通过.|Synonyms: hindering, impeding, blocking, deterrent|Antonyms: helpful, assisting, facilitating, aiding|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *obstructus*, past participle of obstruere (“to build in front of, block up, obstruct”), from ob (“against, in front of, towards”) + struere (“to pile, build”).
