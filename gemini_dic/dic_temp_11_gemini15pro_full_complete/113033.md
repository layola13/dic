illuminate:/ɪˈluːmɪneɪt/
verb
照亮，阐明
to light up or make clear
例句：The streets were illuminated with festive lights. 节日的灯光照亮了街道。

例句：The article illuminated the complex issue of climate change. 这篇文章阐明了气候变化的复杂问题。
近义词：light, brighten, clarify, elucidate
反义词：darken, obscure, confuse
词性：v.

illuminate:/ɪˈluːmɪneɪt/
adjective
被照亮的
lit up
例句：The illuminated manuscript was a beautiful work of art.  这本被照亮的稿件是一件美丽的艺术品。

例句：The illuminated building stood out against the night sky. 被照亮的建筑物在夜空中格外显眼。
近义词：bright, lit
反义词：dark, unlit
词性：adj. (less common)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin illuminatus, past participle of illuminare "to light up, enlighten," from Latin in- "in" + luminare "to light up," from lumen (genitive luminis) "light."
