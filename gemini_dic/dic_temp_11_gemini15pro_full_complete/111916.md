loiteringly:/ˈlɔɪtərɪŋli/ | adv. | 闲逛地，游荡地 | In a manner that involves lingering or dawdling; slowly and aimlessly. |  He walked loiteringly through the park, enjoying the sunshine. 他在公园里闲逛，享受着阳光。| Synonyms: lazily, idly, slowly, leisurely | Antonyms: quickly, briskly, hurriedly | adverb

loiter:/ˈlɔɪtər/ | v. | 闲逛，徘徊 | To stand or wait around idly or without apparent purpose. | They loitered in the street corner. 他们在街角闲逛。| Synonyms: linger, dawdle, hang around | Antonyms: hurry, hasten, rush | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *loteren "to swing, dangle," of uncertain origin.
