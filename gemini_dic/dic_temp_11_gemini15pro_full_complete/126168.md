mutt:/mʌt/| n. |杂种狗，笨蛋|A dog of mixed breed.  Informally, it can also refer to a foolish or incompetent person.|例句：That mutt of mine ate my homework!  我的那只笨狗吃了我的作业！

例句：He's such a mutt, he locked his keys in the car again. 他真是个笨蛋，又把钥匙锁在车里了。|近义词：mongrel, cur, crossbreed (for dogs); fool, idiot (for people)|反义词:purebred (for dogs); genius (for people)|词性:noun
