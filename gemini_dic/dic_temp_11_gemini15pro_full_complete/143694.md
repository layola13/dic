outlearn:/ˌaʊtˈlɜːrn/
verb
超过…的学习速度；学得比…快
To surpass someone in learning; to learn faster or more effectively than someone else.
例句：She outlearned all her classmates in math. 她数学学得比所有同学都快。

例句：He quickly outlearned his mentor and became a leading expert in the field. 他很快在学习上超过了他的导师，成为了该领域的顶尖专家。

近义词：surpass, excel, outstrip
反义词：lag behind, fall behind
词性：v.
