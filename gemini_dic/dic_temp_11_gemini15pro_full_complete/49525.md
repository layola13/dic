demands:/dɪˈmændz/
| n. | 要求，需求 |  The demands of the job are very high. 这份工作的要求很高。 |  requests, needs, requirements | concessions, compromises | noun
demands:/dɪˈmændz/
| v. | 要求，需要 |  He demanded an explanation. 他要求一个解释。 |  request, require, insist on | waive, concede | verb

Etymology:
From Old French *demander, from Latin dēmāndāre (“to entrust, commit, enjoin, order”), from dē (“from, down, away”) + māndāre (“to entrust, order”).
