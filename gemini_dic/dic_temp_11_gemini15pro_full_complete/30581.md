Figuratively used to describe something fiercely protective, vigilant, or hellish.|The cerberean security system made it nearly impossible to breach the facility.  这个地狱看门狗般的安全系统使得几乎不可能突破这个设施。|Synonyms: vigilant, watchful, fierce, relentless, hellish, infernal|Antonyms: lenient, forgiving, gentle, welcoming|adjective
