comal: /koʊˈmɑːl/
n.
煎饼锅，烤盘 (单数)
A flat griddle, typically made of clay or metal, used in Mexican cooking for making tortillas and other foods.
The *comal* is heated over an open flame. 煎饼锅在明火上加热。

Etymology: From Spanish *comal*, from Nahuatl *comalli*.

Synonyms: griddle, hotplate
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "oven" as a contrasting cooking method)
Noun
