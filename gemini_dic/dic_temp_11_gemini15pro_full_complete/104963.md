kemler: /ˈkɛmlər/ | n. | Kemler Code |  A four-digit number displayed on placards affixed to tanks and other containers used for the transport of hazardous materials. It identifies the material being transported.  | The truck carrying gasoline had a "1203" Kemler Code. 这辆载着汽油的卡车有一个“1203”的Kemler代码。 | near synonyms: HAZMAT code, UN Number | antonym:  N/A | noun
