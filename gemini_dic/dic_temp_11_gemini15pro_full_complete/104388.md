kabbalistical:/ˌkæbəlˈɪstɪkəl/
adj.
卡巴拉的，神秘的，玄奥的
Relating to or characteristic of the Kabbalah, a system of Jewish mystical interpretation of the Bible.  Esoteric; occult.
The rabbi's kabbalistical teachings were understood by only a few.  拉比关于卡巴拉的教义只有少数人能够理解。

The ancient manuscript contained kabbalistical symbols. 这份古老的手稿包含卡巴拉符号。
近义词:mystical, esoteric, occult, arcane
反义词:mundane, common, ordinary
词性:adjective


词源:
From Late Latin *Cabbala, from Hebrew qabbālā (“received tradition”), from qibbel (“to receive, accept”).
