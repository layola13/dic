bothers:/ˈbɑːðərz/ (US), /ˈbɒðəz/ (UK)| v. |打扰，烦扰；使不安；使恼火| To cause somebody trouble or worry, especially slightly.|  The noise from the street bothers me when I'm trying to sleep. 街上的噪音在我试图睡觉时打扰我。| synonyms: annoy, disturb, trouble, irritate | antonyms: soothe, comfort, please | verb

bothers:/ˈbɑːðərz/ (US), /ˈbɒðəz/ (UK)| n. |麻烦，困难；烦心事|Something that causes trouble or worry.|It's not worth the bother of cooking for just myself. 只为自己做饭不值得麻烦。| synonyms: trouble, difficulty, inconvenience, hassle | antonyms: ease, convenience, comfort | noun
