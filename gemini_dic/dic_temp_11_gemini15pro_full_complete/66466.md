exequial: /ɪkˈsiːkwiəl/ /ɛkˈsiːkwiəl/
adjective
丧葬的，与葬礼有关的
Relating to a funeral or burial rites.
例句：The exequial music was somber and moving.  葬礼音乐凄凉而感人。

例句：Exequial services were held at the local church. 葬礼在当地教堂举行。

近义词：funereal, mortuary, funeral
反义词: celebratory, festive
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *exsequialis, from Latin exsequiae (“funeral procession, obsequies”), from exsequi (“to follow to the grave, perform funeral rites”), from ex- (“out”) + sequi (“to follow”).
