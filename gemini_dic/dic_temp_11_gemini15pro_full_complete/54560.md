dislocatory:/ˌdɪsləˈkeɪtəri/
adjective
脱臼的，导致错位的；扰乱的
causing dislocation; disruptive.
The dislocatory effects of the war on the economy were substantial. 战争对经济的破坏性影响是巨大的。
近义词: disruptive, disturbing, unsettling
反义词: stabilizing, ordering, constructive
词性: adjective

dislocatory:/ˌdɪsləˈkeɪtəri/
noun
错位剂
A dislocating agent. (Rarely used)
The doctor injected a dislocatory to help reset the joint.  医生注射了一种错位剂来帮助复位关节。(罕用)
近义词: (For verb form "dislocate") luxate
反义词: (For verb form "dislocate") reduce, relocate
词性: noun (rarely used)


Etymology:
From dislocate + -ory.  "Dislocate" comes from the Latin "dislocare," meaning "to displace," from "dis-" (apart, asunder) + "locare" (to place).  The suffix "-ory" forms adjectives meaning "relating to" or "serving for."
