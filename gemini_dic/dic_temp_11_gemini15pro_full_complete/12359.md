ashiness:/ˈæʃɪnəs/| n. |灰白；苍白；灰烬状态|The ashiness of her face betrayed her illness. 她苍白的脸色暴露了她的病情。|例句：The ashiness of his skin was alarming. 他的皮肤灰白得令人担忧。  Her face had an unhealthy ashiness. 她的脸色呈现出一种不健康的灰白。|近义词：paleness, pallor, wanness|反义词: ruddiness, flush, rosiness|词性:名词

词源: From ashy + -ness.  From ash + -y. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *askō.
