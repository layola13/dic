boundary:/ˈbaʊndri/ (US), /ˈbaʊndəri/ (UK)| n.|边界，界限，分界线|A line that marks the limits of an area; a dividing line.|The river forms the boundary between the two countries. 这条河是两国之间的分界线。|Synonyms: border, limit, edge, frontier|Antonyms: center, interior, inside|noun

boundary:/ˈbaʊndri/ (US), /ˈbaʊndəri/ (UK)| adj. |边界的，界限的 (rare usage, mostly found in technical contexts)|Relating to or forming a boundary.|Boundary disputes are common in densely populated areas. 边界纠纷在人口稠密的地区很常见。|Synonyms: limiting, bordering|Antonyms: central, internal, interior|adjective
