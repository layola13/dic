airstrips:/ˈɛərstrɪps/| n. |简易机场；简易跑道|Airstrips are landing strips used for taking off and landing aircraft, typically smaller or less developed than a standard airport runway.  |例句：The small plane landed on the remote airstrip. 这架小型飞机降落在了偏远的简易机场上。

例句：The airstrip was barely long enough for the plane to take off. 这条简易跑道勉强够飞机起飞。|近义词：landing strip, runway, airfield|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

Etymology->
From air + strip.  "Strip" in this context refers to a long, narrow piece of land.
