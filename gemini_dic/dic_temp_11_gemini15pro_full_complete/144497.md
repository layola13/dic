outtinkled:/ˌaʊtˈtɪŋkəld/
verb
发出比...更清脆的叮当声
surpassed in tinkling; made a clearer, higher-pitched tinkling sound than something else.
The tiny silver bells outtinkled the larger brass ones. 小银铃发出的叮当声比大铜铃更清脆。
近义词:out-ring, outsound
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "muffled," "dampened")
词性: verb
