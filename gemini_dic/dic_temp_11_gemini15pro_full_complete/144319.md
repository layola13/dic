outsprints:/ˌaʊtˈsprɪnts/
|v.|胜过，比…跑得快|He outsprinted the other runners to win the race. 他比其他选手跑得更快，赢得了比赛。|例句：She outsprinted her rivals in the final stretch. 她在最后冲刺阶段超过了她的对手。|近义词：outruns, surpasses, beats|反义词：loses to, is outpaced by|词性:动词

outsprints:/ˌaʊtˈsprɪnts/
|n.|超越，冲刺胜利|His outsprint in the final seconds secured the victory. 他在最后几秒的冲刺确保了胜利。|例句：The outsprint to the finish line was thrilling. 到终点线的冲刺令人激动。|近义词：sprint, dash, burst|反义词：lag, fall behind|词性:名词
