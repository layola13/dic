drinkableness:/ˈdrɪŋkəbəlnəs/
n.|适饮性，可饮用性|The drinkableness of the water is questionable.  这种水的适饮性值得怀疑。|例句：The drinkableness of well water depends on its purity. 井水的适饮性取决于其纯净度。|近义词：potability, palatability|反义词：undrinkableness, unpotability|词性:名词
Etymology: drinkable + -ness. From "drinkable" (suitable for drinking), derived from "drink" (verb) + "-able" (suffix indicating capability or suitability). The suffix "-ness" forms nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
