nonclogging:/ˌnɑːnˈklɑːɡɪŋ/
adjective
防堵塞的，不易堵塞的
Designed or formulated to prevent clogging or blockage.
This paint uses a nonclogging formula.  这种油漆采用了防堵塞配方。
The nozzle is nonclogging, ensuring smooth application.  这个喷嘴是防堵塞的，确保喷涂顺畅。
近义词: clog-free, blockage-resistant, free-flowing
反义词: clogging, blocking
词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not" or "without"
clog (verb): to block or become blocked
-ing (suffix): forming an adjective from a verb
