ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ascian (attested in the Northumbrian dialect as ascian, and in West Saxon as axian), from Proto-Germanic *aiskōjaną (compare West Frisian aske "to ask", Dutch vragen "to ask", German heischen "to demand"), from Proto-Indo-European *ais- "to seek, desire".
