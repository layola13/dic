gill-less:/ˈɡɪlləs/| adj.|无鳃的|Lacking gills; having no gills.  This term is used to describe organisms, especially aquatic animals, that do not possess gills for respiration.|例句：The gill-less tadpole struggled to breathe in the water. 这只没有鳃的蝌蚪在水中呼吸困难。

例句：Scientists discovered a new species of gill-less salamander in a cave. 科学家在一个山洞里发现了一种新的无鳃蝾螈。|近义词：abranchiate|反义词：branchiate|词性: adjective
