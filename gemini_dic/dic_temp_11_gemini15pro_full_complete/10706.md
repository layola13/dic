apriorism:/ˌeɪ praɪˈɔːrɪzəm/ | n. | 先验论 | The theory that knowledge is based on prior reasoning or principles, rather than on experience. |  Example: His argument was based on pure apriorism, with no regard for empirical evidence. 他的论点纯粹基于先验论，完全不考虑经验性证据。

Example:  Apriorism can be a useful tool in philosophical debates, but it shouldn't replace empirical observation. 先验论在哲学辩论中可能是一个有用的工具，但不应该取代经验观察。 | 近义词: rationalism, innatism | 反义词: empiricism | 词性: noun
