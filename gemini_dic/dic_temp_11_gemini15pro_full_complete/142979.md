outbanter: /ˌaʊtˈbæntər/
verb
胜过(某人)的玩笑;以玩笑胜过
To surpass someone in banter; to outdo someone in witty or playful conversation.
He tried to outbanter her, but her quick wit was no match for him. 他试图在玩笑上胜过她，但她机智的反应让他望尘莫及。
verb
以俏皮话击败
To defeat or best someone by bantering.
She outbantered him in every argument. 在每一次争论中，她都用俏皮话击败了他。
近义词: outwit, outperform, surpass
反义词: be outdone, lose to
