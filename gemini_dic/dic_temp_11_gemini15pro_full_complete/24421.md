briskly: /ˈbrɪskli/ | adv. | 轻快地，活泼地， briskly means in a quick, energetic way. | She walked briskly down the street. 她轻快地走在街上。| He rubbed his hands together briskly. 他快速地搓了搓手。|近义词：quickly, rapidly, energetically, swiftly | 反义词：slowly, sluggishly | 词性: 副词

brisk: /brɪsk/ | adj. | 轻快的，活泼的， brisk means quick and energetic. |  The brisk wind blew the leaves off the trees.  轻快的风把树叶吹落了。 | Business was brisk at the market.  市场上的生意很红火。 | 近义词：quick, active, energetic, lively | 反义词：slow, sluggish, dull | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *brisk, from Middle French *brisque “nimble, lively”, probably from Italian *brisco “vigorous, lively” (compare Italian brusco “tart, rough”). Possibly related to Old Occitan *briscar “to break, shatter”, referring to the abrupt movements of a lively person.
