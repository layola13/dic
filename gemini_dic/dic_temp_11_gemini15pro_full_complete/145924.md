overdirect:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈrɛkt/ |v.|过度指导，过分指挥|To direct or manage with excessive control or attention to detail, often stifling creativity or initiative.|The manager overdirected his team, leading to resentment and a lack of innovation.  经理过度指导他的团队，导致怨恨和缺乏创新。|The micromanaging boss overdirected every task, leaving his employees feeling demoralized.  事无巨细的老板对每一项任务都过度指导，让他的员工感到 demoralized.|近义词: micromanage, overmanage,oversupervise|反义词: empower, delegate|词性:动词

Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "direct" (verb meaning "to manage or guide")
