dextroglucose:/ˌdɛkstroʊˈɡluːkoʊs/
|n.| 葡萄糖，右旋糖 |A form of glucose that rotates polarized light to the right.  It is the naturally occurring form of glucose and the primary source of energy for living organisms.|Dextroglucose is the main sugar found in the blood. 葡萄糖是血液中的主要糖分。|Synonyms: glucose, D-glucose|Antonyms: L-glucose|noun
