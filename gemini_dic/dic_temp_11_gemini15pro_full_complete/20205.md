birder:/ˈbɜːrdər/ (US), /ˈbɜːdə/ (UK)| n.|观鸟者，鸟类爱好者|A person who engages in the activity of observing and identifying birds, often as a hobby.|She's a keen birder and spends her weekends in the woods with her binoculars. 她是一位热衷的观鸟者，周末都带着双筒望远镜在树林里度过。|Near synonyms: birdwatcher, ornithologist (more formal/scientific)|Antonym: N/A (no direct opposite)|Noun
