bromal:/ˈbroʊməl/| n.|三溴乙醛; 溴醛|A sedative and hypnotic drug, CBr3CHO, formerly used to treat insomnia and epilepsy, now largely obsolete due to its toxicity.  A colorless, oily liquid with a pungent odor.|Bromal is a toxic substance and should not be used without medical supervision.  溴醛是一种有毒物质，不应在没有医疗监督的情况下使用。|Synonym: tribromoacetaldehyde|Antonym: N/A|noun
