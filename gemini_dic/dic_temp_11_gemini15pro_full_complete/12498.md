asperity:/əˈspɛrɪti/ /æˈspɛrɪti/| n. |粗糙；严酷；刻薄|Harshness of tone or manner.|例句：The asperity of her voice startled him. 她尖刻的声音吓了他一跳。

例句：He spoke with asperity to the waiter who had spilled his drink. 他粗暴地对那个把饮料洒在他身上的服务员说话。

近义词：harshness, roughness, severity, acrimony, sharpness|反义词:smoothness, gentleness, mildness, kindness|词性:noun
