cholesterols: /kəˈlɛstəˌrɔːlz/ /kəˈlɛstəˌrɑːlz/
n.
胆固醇
A waxy, fat-like substance that's found in all the cells in your body.

High levels of cholesterols can increase your risk of heart disease. 高胆固醇会增加你患心脏病的风险。


Cholesterol is essential for good health, but high levels can be harmful. 胆固醇对健康至关重要，但高水平的胆固醇可能是有害的。


synonym: sterols
antonym:  (No direct antonym for a substance, but perhaps "lipid-lowering agents" in a medical context)
词性: noun
