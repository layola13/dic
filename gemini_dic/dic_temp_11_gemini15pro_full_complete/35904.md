cocounsel:/ˌkoʊˈkaʊnsəl/
verb|共同辩护，共同担任律师|To act as a joint lawyer with another on a case.|The two lawyers cocounseled on the high-profile case.|collaborate, cooperate, joint counsel|oppose, contend against|v.

cocounsel:/ˌkoʊˈkaʊnsəl/
noun|共同律师，合作律师|A lawyer who works with another lawyer on a case.|She served as cocounsel for the defendant.|joint counsel, co-counsel, collaborating attorney|opposing counsel, adversary|n.
