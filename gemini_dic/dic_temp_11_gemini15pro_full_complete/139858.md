octuple:/ˈɒktjʊpəl/ (US) /ˈɒktəpəl/ (UK)| adj. n. v.|八倍的，八重的；八倍(数)；使成八倍|Eight times as great or as many.|The bacteria octupled in a matter of hours. 这种细菌在几个小时内数量增加了八倍。|近义词：eightfold|反义词: (no direct antonym, consider fractions like one-eighth)|词性: adjective, noun, verb

octuple:/ˈɒktjʊpəl/ (US) /ˈɒktəpəl/ (UK)| n.|八倍(数)|The number eight times greater than another.|The octuple of 2 is 16. 2的八倍是16。|近义词：eightfold|反义词: (no direct antonym, consider fractions like one-eighth)|词性: noun

octuple:/ˈɒktjʊpəl/ (US) /ˈɒktəpəl/ (UK)| v.|使成八倍；乘以八|To multiply by eight.|He octupled his investment in just two years. 他在短短两年内就让自己的投资翻了八倍。|近义词：multiply by eight|反义词: (no direct antonym, consider dividing by eight)|词性: verb


USAGE->

词源：From Late Latin octuplus, from Latin octo "eight" + -plus "-fold".

用法说明：Octuple can function as an adjective, a noun, or a verb, all relating to the concept of eight. As an adjective, it describes something eight times greater or consisting of eight parts. As a noun, it refers to a quantity eight times greater than another.  As a verb, it means to multiply by eight. It is less commonly used than "eight times" or "eightfold".
