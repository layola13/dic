nonbroodings:/ˌnɒnˈbruːdɪŋz/
n.
非沉思期，非孵化期
Periods of time when an animal, typically a bird, is not engaged in brooding (incubating eggs or caring for young).
The scientist observed the bird's nonbroodings to understand its foraging patterns.  科学家观察了这只鸟的非沉思期，以了解它的觅食模式。
The nonbroodings of the penguins allowed them time to replenish their energy reserves.  企鹅的非孵化期让它们有时间补充能量储备。
近义词: non-incubating periods,  rest periods
反义词: brooding periods, incubation periods
词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "brooding" (present participle of "brood," from Old English "brōd," meaning "a brood of young birds").
