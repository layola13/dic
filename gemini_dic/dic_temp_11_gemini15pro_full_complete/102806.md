jargoneers:/ˈdʒɑːrɡəˌnɪərz/
n.
行话使用者；术语使用者；喜欢用行话的人
People who habitually use jargon, often excessively or to obscure meaning.
The jargoneers in the marketing department made it impossible for anyone outside the team to understand their presentations.  市场部那些满口行话的人使得团队以外的任何人都无法理解他们的演示。
Synonyms: specialists, experts, insiders, professionals, technicians
Antonyms: laypeople, amateurs, novices, generalists
noun
