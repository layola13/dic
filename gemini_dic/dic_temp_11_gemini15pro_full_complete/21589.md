bluff:/blʌf/| n. |悬崖，峭壁；虚张声势；恐吓|A high, steep bank or cliff.|The hikers stood at the edge of the bluff, overlooking the ocean.  徒步旅行者站在悬崖边上，俯瞰大海。|cliff, precipice, escarpment|slope, incline|noun
bluff:/blʌf/| v. |虚张声势，吓唬；蒙骗|To deceive someone by pretending to be stronger or more confident than one really is.|He bluffed his way through the poker game, even though he had a terrible hand.  尽管他的牌很差，他还是虚张声势地完成了扑克游戏。|deceive, mislead, trick|be honest, tell the truth|verb
