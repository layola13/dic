leery:/ˈlɪəri/| adj.| 猜疑的，怀疑的，谨慎的|Suspicious; wary; distrustful.  Characterized by caution and suspicion, especially due to past experiences or a perceived threat.|例句：I'm leery of investing in that company. 我对投资那家公司持谨慎态度。|例句：She was leery of his promises. 她对他的承诺表示怀疑。|近义词：suspicious, wary, distrustful, cautious, skeptical|反义词：trusting, gullible, naive|词性:形容词
