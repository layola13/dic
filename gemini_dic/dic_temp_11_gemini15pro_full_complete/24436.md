bristliness:/ˈbrɪsəlnəs/ | n. | 毛发竖立,易怒,性情暴躁 | The state or quality of being bristly; covered with short, stiff hairs.  A prickly or irritable disposition. |  The bristliness of the cactus made it difficult to handle. 仙人掌的毛刺使得它难以处理。 His bristliness made it hard to approach him with any criticism. 他的易怒使得很难对他提出任何批评。 | synonyms: prickliness, spikiness, irascibility, irritability, cantankerousness | antonyms: smoothness, softness, gentleness, affability, amiability | noun
