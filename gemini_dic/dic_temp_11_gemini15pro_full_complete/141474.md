opinionatedly: /əˈpɪnjəneɪtɪdli/ | adv. | 固执己见地，武断地 | In a way that shows you have strong opinions that you are not willing to change. | He opinionatedly insisted that he was right. 他固执地坚持认为他是对的。 |  She opinionatedly declared her preference for classical music. 她武断地宣称自己喜欢古典音乐。 | 近义词: dogmatically, stubbornly, assertively | 反义词: open-mindedly, receptively | 词性: 副词

词源: From the adjective "opinionated," which comes from "opinion" (from Old French "opinion," ultimately from Latin "opinio") + "-ated" (a suffix forming adjectives meaning "characterized by"). The adverbial suffix "-ly" is added to form "opinionatedly."
