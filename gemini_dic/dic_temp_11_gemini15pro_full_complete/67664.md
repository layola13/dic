extrudability:/ɪkˌstruːdəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可挤出性，挤出性能 | The property of a material that allows it to be extruded, referring to the ease with which it can be forced through a die or other opening to form a particular shape. | The extrudability of the plastic was tested before mass production.  这种塑料的可挤出性在量产前进行了测试。| Synonyms: extrusion capacity, flowability | Antonyms: non-extrudability, rigidity | noun
