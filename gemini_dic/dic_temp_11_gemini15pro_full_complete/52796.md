diligent:/ˈdɪlɪdʒənt/ | adj. | 勤奋的，勤勉的 | Showing care and conscientiousness in one's work or duties. | She is a diligent student, always completing her homework on time. 她是一个勤奋的学生，总是按时完成作业。 |  近义词: hard-working, industrious, assiduous | 反义词: lazy, indolent, negligent | 词性: 形容词
词源: From Latin *diligens*, present participle of *diligere* "to love, esteem, value highly, select," from *dis-* "apart" (see dis-) + *legere* "to choose, gather, select, read."
