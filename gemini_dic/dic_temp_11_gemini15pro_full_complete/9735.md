antivenom:/ˈæntivɛnəm/| n.|抗蛇毒血清|A medicine used to counteract the effects of venom from a snake or other venomous animal.  It contains antibodies that neutralize the venom's toxins.|The doctor quickly administered the antivenom after the snake bite. 医生在被蛇咬后迅速注射了抗蛇毒血清。|Synonyms: antivenin, antitoxin|Antonyms: venom, poison|noun
