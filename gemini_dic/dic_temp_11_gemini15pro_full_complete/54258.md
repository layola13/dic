词源: From Middle English *disgracen*, from Anglo-Norman *descrescer*, from Old French *descreistre* "to decrease, diminish," from Latin *decrescere*, from *de-* "down" + *crescere* "to grow."  The sense shifted from "diminish in rank" to "bring shame upon."
