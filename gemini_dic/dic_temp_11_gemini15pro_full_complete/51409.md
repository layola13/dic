determinists:/dɪˈtɜːmɪnɪsts/ | n. | 决定论者 | People who believe in determinism, the philosophy that all events are predetermined by prior causes. | Determinists argue that free will is an illusion. 决定论者认为自由意志是一种幻觉。|Synonyms: fatalists, necessitarians | Antonyms: libertarians, indeterminists | noun
