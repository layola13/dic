oppugn: /əˈpjuːn/ | v. | 质疑，反对，挑战 | To call into question; to challenge as false or wrong. | The lawyer oppugned the witness's testimony. 律师质疑证人的证词。|  He oppugned the new policy, arguing it would harm the economy. 他反对这项新政策，认为它会损害经济。 | 近义词: challenge, question, dispute, contest, gainsay | 反义词: support, uphold, endorse, defend | 词性: 动词

词源: From Latin oppugnare "to fight against, attack," from ob "against" + pugnare "to fight."
