overcharitably:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃærɪtəbli/
adverb
过于仁慈地，过分宽厚地
in a way that is too kind and forgiving, especially when someone does not deserve it
He judged her overcharitably, overlooking her obvious flaws. 他过分仁慈地评价她，忽略了她明显的缺点。
Synonyms: too kindly, excessively generously, overindulgently
Antonyms: uncharitably, harshly, severely
词性: 副词
