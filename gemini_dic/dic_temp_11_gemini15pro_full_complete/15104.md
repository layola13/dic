bacciferous:/ˌbækˈsɪfərəs/
adjective
浆果的；多浆果的
Bearing or producing berries.
The bacciferous shrub was laden with ripe, juicy berries. 这种结浆果的灌木上挂满了成熟多汁的浆果。
The botanist identified the plant as a bacciferous species. 植物学家将这种植物鉴定为一种浆果植物。
近义词: berry-bearing, berry-producing
反义词: non-bacciferous


词性: adjective
