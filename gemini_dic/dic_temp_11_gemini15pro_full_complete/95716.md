inconnexedly:/ˌɪnkəˈnektɪdli/
adverb
不连贯地，无联系地
without connection or coherence; disjointedly.
He spoke inconnexedly, jumping from one topic to another. 他说话语无伦次，从一个话题跳到另一个话题。
Synonyms: disjointedly, ramblingly, incoherently, disconnectedly
Antonyms: coherently, connectedly, logically
词性: 副词
