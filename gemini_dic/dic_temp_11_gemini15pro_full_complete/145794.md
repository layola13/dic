overdecadence:/ˌoʊvərˈdɛkədəns/| n. |过度颓废，极度堕落|Excessive decadence; a state of extreme moral or cultural decline marked by excessive indulgence in pleasure or luxury.|The Roman Empire's overdecadence ultimately led to its downfall. 罗马帝国的极度颓废最终导致了它的灭亡。|例句：The city's overdecadence was evident in its opulent parties and lavish spending.  这座城市的过度颓废体现在其奢华的派对和挥霍无度的开销上。

例句：Historians often cite overdecadence as a contributing factor to the decline of civilizations. 历史学家经常将过度颓废列为文明衰落的一个促成因素。|近义词：corruption, depravity, dissipation, debauchery|反义词:  austerity, moderation,  discipline|词性: noun
