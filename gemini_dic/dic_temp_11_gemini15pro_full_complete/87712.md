hexastichic:/ˌhɛksəˈstɪkɪk/
n.
六行诗
A poem or stanza of six lines.
The poet experimented with the hexastichic form, finding it a concise yet expressive medium.  这位诗人尝试了六行诗体，发现它是一种简洁而富有表现力的媒介。
近义词:sestet, six-line stanza
反义词:(None - describes a form, not a quality)
词性:noun
