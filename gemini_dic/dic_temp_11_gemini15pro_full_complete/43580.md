creakingly:/ˈkriːkɪŋli/ | adv. | 吱吱作响地；嘎吱嘎吱地 | In a way that produces a creaking sound. | The door opened creakingly. 门吱吱作响地打开了。| Synonyms: squeakily, gratingly | Antonyms: silently, smoothly | adverb

creakingly:/ˈkriːkɪŋli/ | adv. | 吱吱作响地；嘎吱嘎吱地 |  The stairs creaked ominously as she climbed them. 她爬楼梯时，楼梯发出不祥的吱吱声。 | Synonyms: groaningly, raspingly | Antonyms: quietly, noiselessly | adverb

Etymology: From the verb "creak" + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverb suffix).  "Creak" likely originates from imitative roots.
