decriminalise: /ˌdiːˈkrɪmɪnəlaɪz/ /ˌdiːˈkrɪmənəˌlaɪz/ | vt. | 使非刑事化，将…合法化 | To remove or reduce the criminal penalties associated with something. |  Many countries are considering decriminalising the possession of small amounts of marijuana. 许多国家正在考虑将持有少量大麻合法化。

The government decriminalised certain minor offences. 政府将某些轻微罪行非刑事化。 | 近义词: legalize, legitimize | 反义词: criminalize | 词性: 动词

decriminalisation: /ˌdiːkrɪmɪnəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌdiːkrɪmənəˌlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 非刑事化，合法化 | The act of making something legal that was previously a crime. | The decriminalisation of drugs is a controversial issue.  毒品合法化是一个有争议的问题。

The decriminalisation of homosexuality led to greater social acceptance. 同性恋合法化带来了更大的社会包容。 | 近义词: legalization, legitimization | 反义词: criminalization | 词性: 名词
