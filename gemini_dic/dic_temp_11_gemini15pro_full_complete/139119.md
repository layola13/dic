obediential:/ˌoʊbiːˈdɛnʃəl/
adjective
顺从的；服从的
Relating to or expressive of obedience; willing to obey.
She displayed an obediential attitude towards her superiors. 她对上司表现出顺从的态度。
The child's obediential behavior was praised by the teacher.  这孩子顺从的行为受到了老师的表扬。

synonyms: obedient, compliant, submissive, docile
antonyms: disobedient, rebellious, defiant, insubordinate
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *obedientialis, from Latin oboedient- (stem of oboediens present participle of oboedire ‘obey’) + -ialis.
