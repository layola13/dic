baffler:/ˈbæflər/ | n. | 迷惑的东西，难题，抑制器 | Something that baffles; a puzzle or a perplexing problem.  Also, a device used to deflect or regulate the flow of air, gases, or sound. | The complex riddle proved to be a real baffler for the contestants.  这个复杂的谜语对参赛者来说是一个真正的难题。|The engine's baffler reduced the noise considerably.  发动机的消音器大大降低了噪音。| synonym: puzzle, enigma, riddle, conundrum, silencer, muffler | antonym: solution, answer, explanation, amplifier | noun

baffle:/ˈbæfl/ | vt. | 使困惑，难倒，阻碍 | To confuse or perplex someone; to frustrate or thwart someone's plans or efforts.  Also, to check or deflect the flow of (air, light, sound, etc.), especially by means of baffles. | The magician's tricks baffled the audience.  魔术师的戏法使观众困惑不解。 | The high winds baffled their attempts to launch the boat.  强风阻碍了他们下水。 | synonym: confuse, perplex, bewilder, frustrate, thwart, impede, obstruct, deflect | antonym: clarify, explain, enlighten, aid, assist, facilitate, amplify | verb
