giftednesses:/ˈɡɪftɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
天赋，才华（复数）
The state of being gifted; exceptional talent or natural ability.
Her giftednesses in music and art were evident from a young age. 她从小就在音乐和艺术方面展现出非凡的天赋。
The program caters to children with diverse giftednesses. 这个项目面向具有不同天赋的儿童。
synonyms: talents, abilities, aptitudes, endowments, capabilities
antonyms: inabilities, inadequacies, weaknesses
