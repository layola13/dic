It refers to the rejection of a neutral stance, often involving taking sides in a conflict. |  The country's antineutralities drew them into the war.  这个国家的反中立政策使他们卷入了战争。|  The president's strong antineutralities signaled a shift in foreign policy. 总统强烈的反中立立场标志着外交政策的转变。 | synonyms:  belligerence, interventionism, partisanship | antonyms: neutrality, nonintervention, impartiality | noun
