insist:/ɪnˈsɪst/| v. |坚持，坚决要求|To demand something forcefully, not accepting refusal.|She insisted on paying for dinner. 她坚持要付晚餐的钱。|近义词:persist, persevere, maintain|反义词:yield, concede|词性:动词
insistence:/ɪnˈsɪstəns/| n. |坚持，坚决主张|The act of insisting on something.|His insistence on punctuality was well known.  他对守时的坚持是众所周知的。|近义词:persistence, perseverance|反义词:acquiescence|词性:名词
insistent:/ɪnˈsɪstənt/| adj. |坚持的，持续的|Continuing to do something even though it is difficult or other people oppose it.|The insistent ringing of the phone woke me up.  持续不断的电话铃声把我吵醒了。|近义词:persistent, unrelenting|反义词:yielding|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin insistere "to persist, dwell upon," literally "to stand upon," from in- "on, upon" + sistere "to stand, set, place," from PIE root *sta- "to stand, make or be firm."
