gyro:/ˈdʒaɪroʊ/ (US) /ˈdʒaɪərəʊ/ (UK)| n.|旋转陀螺，陀螺仪，希腊烤肉|A spinning wheel or disc mounted so that its axis can change direction freely, used to stabilize or control the movement of a ship, aircraft, missile, etc. Also, a Greek dish of meat cooked on a vertical rotisserie.|The ship's gyro helped it stay on course during the storm. 这艘船的陀螺仪帮助它在风暴中保持航向。|The gyro was sliced thin and served in pita bread. 希腊烤肉被切成薄片，夹在皮塔饼里。|synonyms:  rotator, stabilizer, kebab, shawarma|antonyms: instability|noun
