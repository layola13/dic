maharanis:/ˌmɑːhəˈrɑːniz/
n.
女王；王妃；女土邦主
(especially in India) a maharaja's wife or widow.  A female ruler in India.
The maharanis of Jaipur were known for their beauty and intelligence.  斋浦尔的王妃们以她们的美貌和智慧而闻名。
The maharani addressed her subjects with grace and dignity. 女王优雅而庄严地向她的臣民发表讲话。
近义词：queen, princess, ruler
反义词: commoner, subject
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi महारानी (mahārānī), from Sanskrit महाराज्ञी (mahārājñī), feminine of महाराजन् (mahārājan, “great king”).  Ultimately from महा (mahā, “great”) + राजन् (rājan, “king”).
