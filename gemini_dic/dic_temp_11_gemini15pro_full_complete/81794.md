grenadier:/ˌɡrɛnəˈdɪr/ | n. | 手榴弹兵；掷弹兵 | A soldier, originally one specially trained to throw grenades. |  例句：The grenadier hurled the grenade towards the enemy bunker. 掷弹兵向敌人的碉堡投掷了手榴弹。

例句：The grenadiers formed a special unit within the army. 掷弹兵在军队中组成了一支特种部队。| 近义词：soldier, trooper | 反义词:civilian | 词性:noun
