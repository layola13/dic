athletical: /æθˈlɛtɪkəl/
adjective
运动的，强壮的
Relating to athletes, athletics, or physical strength and skill.
She has a natural athletical ability. 她有天生的运动才能。
He has an athletical build. 他有运动员的体格。
近义词: athletic, sporty, muscular, strong, vigorous
反义词: weak, frail, feeble
词性: adjective


athletical: /æθˈlɛtɪkəl/
adjective
体格健美的
Having a strong and well-proportioned physique.
He looked very athletical in his new tennis outfit. 他穿着新的网球服看起来很健美。
She admired his athletical physique. 她羡慕他健美的体格。
近义词: muscular, well-built, fit, toned
反义词: unfit, out of shape, flabby
词性: adjective


词源: From Late Latin *athleticalis, from Latin athleta + -ical.  Ultimately from Greek athletes, "contestant in games."
