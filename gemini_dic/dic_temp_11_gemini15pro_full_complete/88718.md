hobgoblin:/ˈhɒbˌɡɒblɪn/ (US: /ˈhɑːbˌɡɑːblɪn/)| n.|妖怪；恶作剧的小妖精；令人烦恼或恐惧的事物|A mischievous and often malicious sprite or goblin. A source of fear or annoyance.|例句：The children were afraid of the hobgoblin in the attic. 孩子们害怕阁楼里的妖怪。|例句：Taxes have become the hobgoblin of the middle class. 税收已经成为中产阶级的梦魇。|近义词：goblin, bogeyman, bugbear|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider using words like "comfort", "reassurance")|词性:noun
