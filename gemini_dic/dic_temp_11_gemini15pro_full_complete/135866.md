nonprotestations:/ˌnɒnˌprɒtɛˈsteɪʃənz/
n.
lack of protests; absence of objections
The government's new policy was met with nonprotestations from the public, suggesting a general acceptance, or perhaps apathy.
His nonprotestations about the extra work implied he was willing to do it.
Synonyms: acquiescence, acceptance, compliance, resignation
Antonyms: objections, protests, dissent, resistance
