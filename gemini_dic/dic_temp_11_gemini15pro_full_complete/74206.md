four-sided:/ˈfɔːrˈsaɪdɪd/ | adj. | 四边的，有四边的 | Having four sides. |  例句：A square is a four-sided figure. 正方形是一个四边形。

例句：The building has a four-sided courtyard. 这栋建筑有一个四边形的庭院。

近义词：quadrilateral, tetragonal | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From "four" + "sided".  "Four" comes from Old English "feower", from Proto-Germanic "*fedwor", from Proto-Indo-European "*kwetwor-". "Sided" comes from Old English "sīde" meaning "side," from Proto-Germanic "*sīdō", also from Proto-Indo-European.
