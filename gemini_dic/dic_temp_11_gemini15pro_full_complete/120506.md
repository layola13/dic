minute:/ˈmɪnɪt/ (US), /ˈmɪnɪt/ (UK)| n. adj.|分钟，片刻，会议记录；微小的，详细的|A minute is a unit of time equal to 60 seconds.  A minute can also refer to a very short period of time or something extremely small.|例句：It takes only a minute to walk there.  走到那里只需一分钟。|近义词：moment, instant, second, tiny, minuscule|反义词：hour, large, huge|词性:名词，形容词

minute:/maɪˈnjuːt/ (US), /maɪˈnjuːt/ (UK)| adj.|微小的，详细的|Something minute is extremely small and difficult to see.|例句：The insect was so minute that I could barely see it. 这只昆虫太小了，我几乎看不见它。|近义词：tiny, minuscule, microscopic|反义词：large, huge, massive|词性:形容词

词源：From Old French *minute (modern French minute), from Late Latin *minuta (hora), meaning "small (hour)," from Latin minutus, past participle of minuere "to lessen, diminish."


注意事项:
*  minute 作名词时，重音在第一个音节；作形容词时，重音在第二个音节。
* minute 作形容词时，通常用来描述大小，而不是时间。
