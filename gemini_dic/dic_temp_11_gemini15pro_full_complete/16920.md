basmati:/bɑːsˈmɑːtiː/ /bɑːzˈmɑːtiː/
n.
巴斯马蒂米
A long-grain aromatic rice grown in the foothills of the Himalayas, prized for its delicate flavor and fragrance.
This recipe calls for basmati rice.  这个食谱需要巴斯马蒂米。

n.
巴斯马蒂大米
A type of long-grain rice traditionally grown in India and Pakistan.
Basmati rice is often served with curry. 巴斯马蒂大米经常与咖喱一起食用。

近义词:long-grain rice, fragrant rice
反义词:(No direct antonym, could consider short-grain rice for contrast)
词性:noun
