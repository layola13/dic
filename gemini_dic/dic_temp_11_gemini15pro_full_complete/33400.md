churchgoings:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃɡəʊɪŋz/
noun
经常去教堂；参加礼拜的人们
The act of attending church regularly, or the people who regularly attend church.
例句：Churchgoings have decreased in recent years. 近年来，去教堂做礼拜的人数有所减少。

例句：Sunday churchgoings are an important part of their lives.  星期天去教堂是他们生活中重要的一部分。

近义词：attendance, worshippers, congregation
反义词：non-attendance, absenteeism
词性:noun
