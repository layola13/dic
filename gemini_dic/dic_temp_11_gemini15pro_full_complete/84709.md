handicap:/ˈhændikæp/| n. v. |障碍，不利条件；残疾；给…设置不利条件|A circumstance that makes progress or success more difficult.|His shyness was a handicap in his job. 他的害羞是他工作中的一个障碍。|近义词：disadvantage, impediment, obstacle, disability|反义词：advantage, benefit|词性:名词，动词
handicap:/ˈhændikæp/| v. |妨碍，阻碍；使残疾|To place at a disadvantage.|The lack of funding handicapped the project. 缺乏资金阻碍了这个项目。|近义词：hinder, impede, obstruct|反义词：aid, assist, help|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From hand in cap, an old game of chance where forfeits were held in a cap.  Originally referred to a system of equalizing chances in a contest, later evolved to mean a disadvantage.
