arundineous:/ˌærənˈdɪniəs/| adj.|芦苇的；似芦苇的；长满芦苇的|Of, relating to, or resembling reeds; growing among reeds. |例句：The arundineous swamp provided shelter for many birds.  芦苇丛生的沼泽地为许多鸟类提供了庇护所。

例句：Arundineous plants thrive in wet environments.  芦苇类植物在潮湿的环境中茁壮成长。|近义词：reedy, reedlike|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "barren" for the opposite of a reed-filled environment) |词性:adjective
