arcadias:/ɑːrˈkeɪdiəz/ | n. | 世外桃源；田园牧歌式的理想境界 | A region or scene of simple pleasure and quiet. Often used in a literary or idealized sense to represent a utopia or pastoral paradise. |  He dreamed of retiring to a peaceful arcadia in the countryside. 他梦想着退休后到乡下的一个宁静的世外桃源。| synonyms: utopia, paradise, Eden, idyll | antonyms: dystopia, hell, inferno | noun
