overargue:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːrɡjuː/
v.
过度争论，争论过久
to argue for too long or too forcefully
He overargued his point and ended up alienating the jury.  他过度争论自己的观点，最终疏远了陪审团。
近义词：overstate, belabor
反义词: concede, yield
词性:动词

overargue:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːrɡjuː/
v.
驳倒，通过争论战胜
to defeat in argument
She overargued the opposing counsel and won the case. 她驳倒了对方律师，赢得了这场官司。
近义词：outargue, refute
反义词: lose an argument
词性:动词
