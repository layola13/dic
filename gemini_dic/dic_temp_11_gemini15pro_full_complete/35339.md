co-dependency:/ˌkoʊdɪˈpɛndənsi/| n. |相互依赖，共依存|A relationship where one person is controlled or manipulated by another who is affected by an addiction or disorder.|Her co-dependency on her alcoholic husband was apparent. 她对她酗酒丈夫的依赖显而易见。|近义词：interdependence, reliance|反义词：independence, autonomy|词性:名词

co-dependent:/ˌkoʊdɪˈpɛndənt/| adj. |相互依赖的，共依存的|Relating to or exhibiting co-dependency.|He recognized his co-dependent behavior patterns. 他意识到了自己相互依赖的行为模式。|近义词：dependent, enabling|反义词：independent, self-sufficient|词性:形容词
