exogamy:/ɛkˈsɒɡəmi/ /ɛkˈsɑːɡəmi/
n.
异族通婚，外婚制
The practice of marrying outside one's own group, tribe, or social unit.
Exogamy is practiced in many cultures to strengthen alliances between groups.  异族通婚在许多文化中都有实践，以加强群体之间的联盟。
The tribe's strict rules of exogamy prevented members from marrying within their own clan.  该部落严格的外婚制规则禁止成员在本族内部通婚。
近义词: intermarriage
反义词: endogamy
词性: noun
