exprobration:/ˌɛksproʊˈbreɪʃən/| n.|责备，斥责，谴责|The act of reproaching; an expression of disapproval or disappointment.|His exprobration of her behavior was harsh and unforgiving. 他对她的行为进行了严厉无情的谴责。|近义词：reproach, rebuke, censure, condemnation|反义词:praise, commendation|noun
词源: From Latin *exprobratio*, from exprobrare ‘to reproach’, from ex- ‘out, forth’ + probrare ‘to find fault with’.
