daytime:/ˈdeɪtaɪm/| n.|白天，日间|The daytime is for work and the nighttime is for sleep. 白天是工作的时间，夜晚是睡觉的时间。|Daytime temperatures can be very high in the summer. 夏天白天的温度会很高。|近义词：daylight, sunlight hours|反义词：nighttime, night|词性:名词

Etymology: From Old English *dægtīma, from dæg "day" + tīma "time".
