innocence:/ˈɪnəsəns/| n.|清白，无辜，天真，单纯|The state of being free from sin or moral wrong; lack of knowledge or experience.|例句：The jury believed in his innocence. 陪审团相信他是无辜的。

例句：She has the innocence of a child. 她有着孩子般的天真。|近义词：purity, guiltlessness, naivety, simplicity|反义词：guilt, culpability, experience, sophistication|词性:名词
