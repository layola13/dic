cormorants:/ˈkɔːrmərənts/ (US), /ˈkɔːmərənts/ (UK)| n. |鸬鹚|Large, black seabirds with long necks and hooked bills, known for their diving skills and ability to catch fish.|Cormorants were often used by fishermen to help catch fish.  鸬鹚经常被渔民用来帮助捕鱼。|Synonyms:shag, sea raven|Antonyms:None|Noun

cormorant:/ˈkɔːrmərənt/ (US), /ˈkɔːmərənts/ (UK)| adj. |贪婪的，狼吞虎咽的|(Figurative) Greedy or ravenous.|He had a cormorant appetite after the hike.  徒步旅行后，他的胃口大开。|Synonyms:voracious, gluttonous|Antonyms:abstemious, temperate|Adjective
