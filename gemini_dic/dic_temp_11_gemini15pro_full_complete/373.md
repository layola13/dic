abietite:/ˈeɪbiɪtaɪt/| n. |枞酸|A resin acid derived from rosin, specifically from the species Abies alba (silver fir). It is a crystalline solid, typically white or yellowish, and is used in various applications such as varnishes, adhesives, and printing inks.|Abietite is a component of some natural resins used in varnish production.|近义词：rosin acid, abietic acid|反义词: None|词性:noun
