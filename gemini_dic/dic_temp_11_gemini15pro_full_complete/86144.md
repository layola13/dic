heartiest:/ˈhɑːrtiɪst/| adj.|最衷心的，最热烈的，最诚挚的|Showing warm and heartfelt affection; enthusiastic.|例句：He gave me the heartiest welcome I've ever received. 他给了我我所受到过的最热烈的欢迎。

例句：She offered her heartiest congratulations on my success. 她对我取得的成功表示最衷心的祝贺。|近义词：warmest, sincerest, most enthusiastic|反义词：coldest, insincere, apathetic|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *hertiest, superlative of herty, from Old English heorte (“heart”) + -y. Cognate with German herzlich.
