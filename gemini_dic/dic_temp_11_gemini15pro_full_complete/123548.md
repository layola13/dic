monozygosity:/ˌmɒnəʊzaɪˈɡɒsɪti/ /ˌmɑːnəʊzaɪˈɡɑːsɪti/| n.|同卵性，单卵性|The state of being monozygotic; derived from a single fertilized ovum, as identical twins.|Monozygosity is a key factor in twin studies. 同卵性是双胞胎研究的一个关键因素。|近义词：identical twinning|反义词：dizygosity|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From mono- (one) + zyg- (yoke, zygote) + -ity (state, quality).  "Mono-" comes from Greek "monos" (single, alone). "Zyg-" comes from Greek "zygon" (yoke, pair).  "-ity" is a suffix forming abstract nouns from adjectives, often denoting a state or quality.
