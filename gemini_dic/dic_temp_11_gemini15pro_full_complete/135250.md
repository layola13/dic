nonpantheistical:/ˌnɒnpænθiˈɪstɪkəl/
adjective
非泛神论的
Not pantheistic; characterized by the belief that God is distinct from the universe.
His nonpantheistical worldview led him to believe in a creator separate from creation. 他非泛神论的世界观使他相信造物主是独立于造物的。
Near Synonyms: non-pantheistic, non-panentheistic
Antonyms: pantheistic, panentheistic
词性: adjective
