adipose:/ˈædɪˌpoʊs/| adj.|脂肪的；肥胖的|Relating to animal fat; fatty.  Containing or composed of animal fat.|Example: Adipose tissue is a specialized connective tissue that stores energy in the form of fat. 脂肪组织是一种特殊的结缔组织，以脂肪的形式储存能量。

Example: People with excessive adipose tissue are at higher risk for certain health problems. 脂肪组织过多的人患某些健康问题的风险更高。|近义词：fatty, obese, corpulent|反义词:lean, thin|词性:adjective
adiposes:/ˈædɪˌpoʊsɪz/| n.|脂肪组织| (Often treated as plural) Adipose tissue; body fat.|Example:  Excess adiposes can lead to health complications. 过多的脂肪会导致健康并发症。

Example: The surgeon carefully removed the adiposes surrounding the organ.  外科医生小心地移除了器官周围的脂肪组织。|近义词：fat, fat deposits, lipid stores|反义词:muscle tissue|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adipōsus, from adeps (“fat, lard”).
