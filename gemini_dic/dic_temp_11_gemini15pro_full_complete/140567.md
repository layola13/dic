oligarchization:/ˌɒlɪɡɑːkɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 寡头政治化，寡头统治化 | The process by which a small group of individuals or entities gain control over a country's resources and political system. |  The oligarchization of Russia's economy after the fall of the Soviet Union resulted in vast wealth disparities.  俄罗斯在苏联解体后经济的寡头政治化导致了巨大的贫富差距。


The increasing oligarchization of media ownership raises concerns about freedom of the press. 媒体所有权日益寡头化，引发了人们对新闻自由的担忧。 | 近义词: concentration of power, plutocracy | 反义词: democratization | 词性: 名词


oligarchic:/ˌɒlɪˈɡɑːkɪk/ | adj. | 寡头政治的 | Relating to or characteristic of an oligarchy. | The country suffers under an oligarchic regime. 这个国家遭受寡头政治的统治。


The oligarchic structure of the company gave a small group of executives immense power.  该公司的寡头结构赋予了一小群高管巨大的权力。 | 近义词: plutocratic | 反义词: democratic | 词性: 形容词
