disoxidation:/ˌdɪsˌɒksɪˈdeɪʃən/
noun
脱氧，还原
The process of removing oxygen from a compound.
The disoxidation of iron ore is a crucial step in steel production.  铁矿石的脱氧是钢铁生产的关键步骤。

The disoxidation of the metal was achieved using a reducing agent.  这种金属的脱氧是通过使用还原剂来实现的。

近义词: reduction, deoxidation
反义词: oxidation
词性: noun
