gangsterism:/ˈɡæŋstərɪzəm/
n.
歹徒行为；黑帮活动
The practice of gangsters; organized crime.
The city was plagued by gangsterism during the Prohibition era.  这个城市在禁酒时期饱受黑帮活动的困扰。
criminality, racketeering, lawlessness
lawfulness, peacefulness
