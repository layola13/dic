angustation:/ˌæŋɡʌˈsteɪʃən/| n.|痛苦，苦恼，狭窄|Extreme distress or anxiety; a feeling of constriction or tightness.|She felt a deep angustation upon hearing the news of her friend's death. 她听到朋友去世的消息后感到非常痛苦。|Synonyms: anxiety, distress, anguish, torment, suffering|Antonyms: comfort, relief, solace, ease, calmness|noun

angustation:/ˌæŋɡʌˈsteɪʃən/| n.|狭窄|The state of being narrow or constricted.|The angustation of the blood vessels can lead to serious health problems. 血管狭窄会导致严重的健康问题。|Synonyms: constriction, narrowing, tightness|Antonyms: widening, expansion, broadening|noun
