girders: /ˈɡɜːrdərz/ (UK) /ˈɡɜːrdərz/ (US) | n. | 大梁，桁梁 | A large iron or steel beam or compound structure used as a main horizontal support in a building or structure, such as a bridge or ship. | The bridge's girders groaned under the weight of the traffic. 桥上的大梁在交通的重压下发出呻吟。| The steel girders support the roof. 钢梁支撑着屋顶。 | synonyms: beams, supports, joists | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "foundation" in a conceptual sense as it supports from below rather than horizontally) | noun
