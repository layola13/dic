nonurbanites:/ˌnɒnˈɜːrbənaɪts/
n.
非城市居民；乡村居民
People who do not live in urban areas.
Many nonurbanites enjoy the peace and quiet of rural life. 许多非城市居民享受乡村生活的宁静。
Near Synonyms:  rural dwellers, country folk, rustics
Antonyms: urbanites, city dwellers
noun
