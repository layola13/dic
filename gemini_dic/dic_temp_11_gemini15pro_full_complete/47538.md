deathlike:/ˈdɛθˌlaɪk/| adj.|死一般的，像死人一样的|Resembling death; as pale and still as a corpse.  |Her face was deathlike after the ordeal.  她经历了这场磨难后，脸色苍白得像死人一样。|Near Synonyms: deathly, pale, ashen, lifeless, inanimate, corpselike|Antonyms: lively, animated, vibrant, flushed, rosy|adjective
