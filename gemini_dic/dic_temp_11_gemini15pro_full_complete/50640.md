derision:/dɪˈrɪʒən/| n. |嘲笑，嘲弄，奚落|Contemptuous ridicule or mockery.|The politician's proposal was met with derision from the public.  这位政客的提议遭到了公众的嘲笑。|
The comedian's act was filled with derision aimed at politicians.  这位喜剧演员的表演充满了对政客的嘲弄。|
Synonyms: mockery, ridicule, scorn, contempt, disdain|Antonyms: respect, admiration, praise, approval|noun
