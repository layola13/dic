humic:/ˈhjuːmɪk/| adj.|腐殖质的|Of or relating to humus, a dark-brown or black organic substance found in soil, formed by the decomposition of plant and animal matter.|The humic acids in the soil improve its fertility.|近义词：organic, earthy|反义词：inorganic|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *humicus*, from humus "earth, ground, soil".
