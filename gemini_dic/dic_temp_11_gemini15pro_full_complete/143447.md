outform:/ˌaʊtˈfɔːrm/| v.|胜过，优于，表现超过|To perform better than someone or something.|例句：He easily outformed his rivals in the race. 他在比赛中轻松胜过对手。|例句：The company consistently outforms its competitors in terms of innovation.  这家公司在创新方面一直优于竞争对手。|近义词：surpass, outperform, exceed, excel, outstrip|反义词：underperform, lag behind|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + form.  "Out-" acts as a prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing.  "Form" refers to performance or shape.
