adjustmental:/əˈdʒʌstmənt(ə)l/
adjective
调整性的；适应性的
Relating to or characterized by adjustment, especially psychological adjustment.  This word is not commonly used.
He seemed to be going through an adjustmental phase after the divorce.  他似乎在离婚后经历了一个适应阶段。
近义词: adaptive, accommodating
反义词: maladaptive, inflexible
词性: adjective

adjustmental:/əˈdʒʌstmənt(ə)l/
noun
(罕见) 调整；适应
The process or result of adjusting, especially psychologically.  This noun form is even less common than the adjective.
The therapist helped her through the difficult adjustmental.  治疗师帮助她度过了艰难的适应期。
近义词: adaptation, accommodation
反义词: maladaptation
词性: noun
