antisynod:/ˌæntaɪˈsɪnəd/ | n. |反对教会会议者；反对教会会议的团体|A person or group who opposes a church synod or council. | The antisynod protested the council's decision on same-sex marriage. 反对教会会议者抗议教会关于同性婚姻的决定。

The antisynod published a pamphlet outlining their objections. 反对教会会议的团体发表了一本小册子，概述了他们的反对意见。 |Synonyms: dissenter, opponent, protestor | Antonyms: supporter, advocate | Noun


Etymology:  From anti- + synod.  "Anti-" is a Greek prefix meaning "against" or "opposite." "Synod" comes from the Greek "synodos," meaning "meeting" or "assembly."
