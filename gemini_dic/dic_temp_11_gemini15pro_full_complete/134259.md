noninflammablenesses:/ˌnɒnɪnˈflæməbəlɪsnɪsɪz/
n.
不易燃性；非易燃性
the quality of not being easily set on fire.
The noninflammablenesses of the new materials ensured the safety of the building. 这些新材料的不易燃性确保了建筑物的安全。
Near synonyms: fire resistance, incombustibility
Antonyms: flammability, inflammability
