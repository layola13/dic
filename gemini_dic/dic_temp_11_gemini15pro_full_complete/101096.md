inverse:/ɪnˈvɜːrs/ | adj. n.| 相反的，倒转的；反面；倒数 | something that is the opposite or reverse of something else | The inverse of multiplication is division. 乘法的逆运算是除法。 | synonyms: reverse, opposite, contrary, reciprocal | antonyms: direct, same, similar | adjective

inverse:/ˈɪnvɜːrs/ | n.| 相反，倒转；倒数 | something that is the opposite or reverse of something else | We can calculate the inverse of the matrix. 我们可以计算矩阵的逆矩阵。 | synonyms: reverse, opposite, contrary, reciprocal | antonyms: direct, same, similar | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *invers, from Latin inversus, past participle of invertere "to turn upside down, turn about, invert," from in- "in" + vertere "to turn."
