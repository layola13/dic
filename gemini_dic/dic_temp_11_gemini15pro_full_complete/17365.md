beadier:/ˈbiːdiər/| adj.|饰有更多珠子的；珠子般的；更圆的|Covered with more beads; resembling beads; more rounded.|The dress was even beadier than I had imagined. 这条裙子比我想象的还要珠光宝气。|近义词：pearly, beaded|反义词:plain, unadorned|词性:形容词
词源:From bead + -ier (comparative suffix). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *baudiz.
