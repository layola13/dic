indecisively:/ˌɪndɪˈsaɪsɪvli/| adv.|优柔寡断地，犹豫不决地|In a manner that shows an inability to make decisions quickly and effectively.|He paced the room indecisively, unable to choose between the two options. 他犹豫不决地在房间里踱来踱去，无法在两个选项之间做出选择。|Synonyms: hesitantly, tentatively, irresolutely, doubtfully|Antonyms: decisively, resolutely, firmly, confidently|词性:副词
