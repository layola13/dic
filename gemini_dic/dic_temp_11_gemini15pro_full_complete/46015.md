Etymology: From Ancient Greek κυβερνητική (kubernētikḗ, “art of steering, art of governing”) +‎ -ics.  The term "cybernetics" was coined by Norbert Wiener in his 1948 book *Cybernetics: Or Control and Communication in the Animal and the Machine*.
