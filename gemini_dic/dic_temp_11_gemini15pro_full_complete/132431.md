nondependencies:/ˌnɒndɪˈpɛndənsiz/ | n. | 非依赖项; 无关项 |  Lack of dependence; things or factors that are not essential or do not influence something else. | The project's success hinges on key resources, not nondependencies like office supplies. 这个项目的成功取决于关键资源，而不是像办公用品这样的非依赖项。|  The report focused on core dependencies and omitted nondependencies. 该报告侧重于核心依赖项，而忽略了非依赖项。 | synonyms: irrelevancies, extraneous factors | antonyms: dependencies, prerequisites | noun
