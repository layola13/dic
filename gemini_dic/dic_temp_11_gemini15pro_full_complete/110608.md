limonite:/ˈlaɪməˌnaɪt/| n. |褐铁矿|A common iron oxide mineral, usually occurring as earthy masses and coatings, often yellow-brown to dark brown in color, and consisting of a mixture of hydrated iron oxides, especially goethite. It is an important iron ore.|The prospector discovered a large deposit of limonite.|近义词：goethite, bog iron ore|反义词: None|词性: Noun
