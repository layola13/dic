clumsier:/ˈklʌmziər/| adj.|更笨拙的|Comparative form of clumsy: more clumsy.  Meaning more awkward in movement or handling things.|He became clumsier as he got older.  他年纪越大越笨手笨脚。|Near Synonyms: more awkward, more ungainly, more inept|Antonyms: more graceful, more dexterous, more adroit|adjective

clumsy:/ˈklʌmzi/| adj.|笨拙的|Awkward in movement or handling things.|She felt clumsy in her new high heels. 她穿着新高跟鞋感到笨手笨脚。|Near Synonyms: awkward, ungainly, inept|Antonyms: graceful, dexterous, adroit|adjective
