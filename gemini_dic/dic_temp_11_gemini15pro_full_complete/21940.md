bogginess:/ˈbɒɡɪnəs/ (US) /ˈbɒɡɪnɪs/ (UK)| n.|沼泽性；泥泞；潮湿|The bogginess of the ground made it difficult to walk. 地面的泥泞使得行走困难。|The trail's bogginess increased after the heavy rain.  暴雨过后，小径变得更加泥泞。|近义词：marshiness, swampiness, wetness, sogginess|反义词：dryness, firmness, aridity|词性:名词

boggy:/ˈbɒɡi/ (US) /ˈbɒɡɪ/ (UK)| adj.|沼泽的；泥泞的|The boggy ground made it difficult to walk. 沼泽地使行走困难。|The trail was boggy after the heavy rain.  暴雨过后，小径变得泥泞不堪。|近义词：marshy, swampy, wet, soggy|反义词：dry, firm, arid|词性:形容词

词源: From bog + -iness.  "Bog" comes from the Irish or Scottish Gaelic word "bogach," meaning "soft, wet ground."
