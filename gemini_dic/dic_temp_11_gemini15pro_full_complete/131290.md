noncensoriousness:/ˌnɒnˌsensɔːrɪəsnəs/
|n.| 不苛求；宽容；不加评判 |The state of being noncensorious; tolerance and acceptance of others' views and behaviors without criticism or judgement. |Her noncensoriousness made her a popular confidante. 她不爱评判别人的性格使她成为一个受欢迎的知心朋友。| 近义词: tolerance, acceptance, open-mindedness, forbearance, liberality |反义词: censoriousness, judgmentalness, criticalness, intolerance
|词性: 名词
