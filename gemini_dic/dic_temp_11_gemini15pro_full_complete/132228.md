nondeceiving:/ˌnɒndɪˈsiːvɪŋ/
adj.
不欺骗的；真实的
Not deceiving; truthful and genuine.
The advertisement provided a nondeceiving representation of the product.  这则广告如实地展现了该产品。
The witness gave a nondeceiving account of the incident.  目击者对事件进行了真实的描述。
近义词:honest, truthful, genuine, sincere, candid
反义词:deceiving, misleading, dishonest, untruthful, deceptive
词性:形容词

Etymology:
From non- + deceiving.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "deceiving" comes from the verb "deceive," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "dēcipere," meaning "to ensnare, beguile, or cheat."
