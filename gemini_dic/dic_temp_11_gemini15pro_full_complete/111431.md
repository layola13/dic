llama:/ˈlɑːmə/ (US) /ˈlɑːmə/ (UK)| n.|美洲驼|A domesticated pack animal of the camel family found in the Andes, valued for its soft woolly fleece.|The llama carried a heavy load across the mountains. 美洲驼背着沉重的货物翻越了群山。|近义词:alpaca, guanaco|反义词:None (Llamas have no direct antonyms.  You could contrast them with other pack animals like horses or donkeys, but those aren't opposites.)|noun
