credibly: /ˈkrɛdəbli/ (US), /ˈkrɛdɪbli/ (UK) | adv. | 可信地，可靠地 |  in a way that makes people believe something |  She spoke credibly about the challenges facing the company. 她令人信服地讲述了公司面临的挑战。| synonym: convincingly, believably, plausibly | antonym: unbelievably, implausibly, doubtfully | word type: adverb

Etymology: From "credible" + "-ly". "Credible" comes from the Late Latin "credibilis" meaning "worthy of belief", from "credere" meaning "to believe".
