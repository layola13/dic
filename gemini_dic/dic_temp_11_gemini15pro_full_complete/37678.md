compadre:/kəmˈpɑːdreɪ/ /kəmˈpɑːdri/ | n. |朋友；伙伴；教父；(尤指拉美国家的)密友|A close friend, especially one's best man or child's godfather.  It often implies a strong bond of friendship and mutual respect. |例句：He went fishing with his compadre. 他和他的好友去钓鱼了。

例句：He asked his compadre to be his son's godfather. 他请他的好友做他儿子的教父。

近义词：friend, pal, buddy, mate, amigo |反义词: enemy, foe |词性: noun
