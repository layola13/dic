elevator:/ˈɛləˌveɪtər (American), ˈɛlɪˌveɪtə(r) (British)/| n.|电梯，升降机|A platform or cage that is moved up and down in a shaft to transport people or goods to different levels in a building.|The elevator took us to the tenth floor. 电梯把我们带到了十楼。|近义词：lift, hoist|反义词:stairs, escalator|noun
