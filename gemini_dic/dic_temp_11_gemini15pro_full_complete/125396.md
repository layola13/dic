multinomial:/ˌmʌltiˈnoʊmiəl/ | adj. n. | 多项式的；多项式 |  (adj.) Relating to or consisting of several terms; (n.) An algebraic expression consisting of the sum of two or more terms. |  Example (adj.):  A multinomial distribution is used in statistics.  多项式分布用于统计学。

Example (n.):  2x² + 3xy + 4y² is a multinomial. 2x² + 3xy + 4y² 是一个多项式。 | Synonym (adj.): polynomial | Antonym (adj.): monomial, binomial |  adjective/noun

multinomial:/ˌmʌltiˈnoʊmiəl/ | n. | 多项式 | An algebraic expression consisting of the sum of two or more terms. | Example:  The multinomial theorem is a generalization of the binomial theorem. 多项式定理是二项式定理的推广。 | Synonym: polynomial | Antonym: monomial, binomial | noun
