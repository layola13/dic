demonstrated:/ˈdɛmənˌstreɪtɪd/| v. |证明的，已演示的|shown or explained something clearly, especially by giving practical examples|She demonstrated how to use the new software. 她演示了如何使用这款新软件。|近义词：shown, exhibited, displayed, proven, established|反义词：refuted, disproven|verb (past tense)

demonstrate:/ˈdɛmənˌstreɪt/| v. |证明，演示，示威|to show or make something clear, especially by giving practical examples; to take part in a public meeting or march, usually as a protest|The teacher demonstrated the experiment to the class. 老师向全班演示了这个实验。|近义词：show, exhibit, display, prove, establish|反义词：refute, disprove|verb

demonstration:/ˌdɛmənˈstreɪʃən/| n. |示范，演示，示威|a practical exhibition and explanation of how something works or is performed; a public meeting or march protesting against something or expressing views on a political issue|The salesman gave a demonstration of the new vacuum cleaner. 售货员演示了这款新的吸尘器。|近义词：display, exhibition, presentation, protest, march|反义词：concealment|noun
