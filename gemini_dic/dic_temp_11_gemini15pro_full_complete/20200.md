birdcages:/ˈbɜːrdˌkeɪdʒɪz/
n.
鸟笼
A cage for keeping birds in.
The birdcages hung in the porch, filled with colorful finches.  鸟笼挂在门廊里，里面满是五颜六色的雀类。
The canary sang sweetly from its birdcage. 金丝雀在它的鸟笼里甜蜜地歌唱着。
