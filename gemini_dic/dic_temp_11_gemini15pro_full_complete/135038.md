nihilist: /ˈnaɪəˌlɪst/ (US), /ˈnɪɪlɪst/ (UK)| n. |虚无主义者；  |A person who believes that life is meaningless and rejects all religious and moral principles. |例句：He became a nihilist after witnessing the horrors of war.  他目睹了战争的恐怖后变成了一个虚无主义者。

|例句：The nihilist argued that all values are baseless and that nothing has real existence. 虚无主义者认为所有价值观都是毫无根据的，没有任何东西是真正存在的。

|近义词：pessimist, cynic, skeptic |反义词: optimist, believer |词性: noun


nonnihilists: /ˌnɒnˈnaɪəˌlɪsts/ (UK), /ˌnɑːnˈnaɪəˌlɪsts/ (US) | n. |非虚无主义者；|People who do *not* believe that life is meaningless and do *not* reject all religious and moral principles.  They generally believe in some form of meaning or purpose in life. |例句：Nonnihilists find meaning in relationships, work, or spiritual beliefs. 非虚无主义者在人际关系、工作或精神信仰中找到意义。

|例句：Unlike nihilists, nonnihilists strive to find purpose and value in their lives.  与虚无主义者不同，非虚无主义者努力在生活中寻找目标和价值。

|近义词: optimist, believer, humanist |反义词: nihilist |词性: noun
