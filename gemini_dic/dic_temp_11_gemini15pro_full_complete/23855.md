breads:/bredz/
n.
面包(复数，非标准用法)
Plural form of "bread," though its use is generally considered non-standard.  "Bread" is typically used as a mass noun and doesn't require pluralization.  "Breads" can sometimes be used to refer to different kinds or types of bread.
She baked several different breads for the festival. 她为节日烤了几种不同的面包。
Near Synonyms: loaves, rolls, buns
Antonyms: None (Bread doesn't typically have an antonym)
