nonbigoted:/ˌnɑːnˈbɪɡətɪd/
adjective
没有偏见的; 不抱偏见的
Not prejudiced; tolerant or showing tolerance towards different opinions, beliefs, or lifestyles.
She raised her children to be nonbigoted and accepting of everyone. 她教育她的孩子们不要抱有偏见，要接受每个人。
Synonyms: tolerant, unprejudiced, open-minded, impartial, fair, unbiased, equitable, just
Antonyms: bigoted, prejudiced, biased, intolerant, narrow-minded, discriminatory

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  Expressing negation or absence. From Latin *non < PIE *ne "not".
bigoted (adjective):  Having or revealing an obstinate belief in the superiority of one's own opinions and a prejudiced intolerance of the opinions of others.  From bigot (noun), of uncertain origin. Possibly from Middle Dutch *bij God "by God."
