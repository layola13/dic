gelatiniferous:/ˌdʒɛləˈtɪnɪfərəs/
adjective
含胶质的；产胶质的
Containing or yielding gelatin.
The gelatiniferous substance coated the petri dish.  培养皿上覆盖着一层胶状物质。
The scientist studied the gelatiniferous properties of the seaweed.  科学家研究了海藻的胶质特性。
Synonyms:gelatinous, gummy, viscous, jelly-like
Antonyms:dry, non-viscous, solid
词性:adjective
