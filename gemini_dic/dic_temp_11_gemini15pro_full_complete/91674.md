hypercivilized:/ˌhaɪpərˈsɪvəlaɪzd/
adjective
超级文明的
Excessively civilized; having a civilization that is perceived as excessively refined, complex, or artificial, often implying decadence or a loss of vital force.
例句：The hypercivilized society, though technologically advanced, had lost touch with nature.  这个超级文明的社会虽然科技发达，却失去了与自然的联系。

例句：Their hypercivilized customs seemed absurdly formal and restrictive to the outsider.  在外人看来，他们超级文明的习俗显得荒唐地正式和拘束。
近义词：overcivilized, sophisticated, decadent
反义词：primitive, unsophisticated, barbaric
词性: adjective

词源: hyper- (prefix meaning "over, excessive") + civilized
