individuation:/ɪndɪˌvɪdʒuˈeɪʃən/| n. |个体化，个性化|The process of becoming an individual, a separate and distinct being. The development of a person's unique personality and sense of self.|例句：Jung believed that individuation is the central process of human development. 荣格认为个体化是人类发展的核心过程。

例句：Her art is an expression of her individuation. 她的艺术是她个性化的表达。|近义词：differentiation, specialization, development, growth|反义词：conformity, standardization, uniformity|词性: noun
