effacement:/ɪˈfeɪsmənt/
n.
消除；抹去；淡忘
the act of erasing or removing something; the state of being erased or removed
例句：The effacement of the inscription made it impossible to date the tombstone.  碑文的消除使得无法确定墓碑的年代。

例句：Years of erosion led to the effacement of the ancient carvings. 多年的侵蚀导致古代雕刻被抹去。
近义词：obliteration, erasure, deletion, removal
反义词：preservation, restoration, enhancement
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *effacement*, from *effacer* "to erase," from *ex-* "out" + *face* "face."
