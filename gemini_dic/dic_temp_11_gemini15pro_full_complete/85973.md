heads-up:/ˈhɛdz ˌʌp/ (US) /ˈhedz ˌʌp/ (UK)| n.| 预先通知，提醒；小心|A heads-up is a warning or a piece of advice given to someone to prepare them for something that is going to happen.|Just a heads-up, the boss will be in early tomorrow.  提前告诉你一声，老板明天会很早来。|synonyms: warning, alert, notification| antonyms: surprise, ambush| noun

heads-up:/ˈhɛdz ˌʌp/ (US) /ˈhedz ˌʌp/ (UK)| adj. | 警惕的，留心的|Characterized by alertness and attentiveness.|He gave me a heads-up look. 他给了我一个警惕的眼神。|synonyms: alert, watchful, vigilant| antonyms: inattentive, careless, oblivious| adjective
