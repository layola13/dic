caked:/keɪkt/ | adj. | 覆盖着的，结块的，厚厚的 | Covered with a thick, dried layer of something; encrusted. |  The car was caked with mud after driving through the field. 汽车开过田野后沾满了泥。| 近义词: crusted, coated, covered, encrusted | 反义词: clean, clear | 词性:形容词

词源: From the verb "cake," meaning to form into a hard mass. 源自动词"cake"，意思是形成硬块。
