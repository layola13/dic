inter-service:/ˌɪntərˈsɜːrvɪs/
adjective
跨部门的，部门间的，军种间的
Existing or occurring between different branches of the armed services.
例句：Inter-service rivalry can sometimes hinder joint operations.  部门间的竞争有时会阻碍联合行动。

例句：The inter-service training program was designed to improve cooperation.  跨部门培训项目旨在加强合作。

近义词：interdepartmental, joint, combined, collaborative
反义词：intraservice, within-service
