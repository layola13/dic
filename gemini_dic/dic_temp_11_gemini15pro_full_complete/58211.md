drug's:/drʌɡz/| n.|药物的；毒品的| possessive form of drug, meaning "belonging to a drug" or "of a drug".  It indicates ownership or association.|The drug's side effects were severe.  这种药物的副作用很严重。|Synonyms: medicine's, medication's, narcotic's (if referring to illicit drugs) |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a possessive. Could consider "natural remedy's" for a contrasting concept)|noun (possessive)
