hyperthermal:/ˌhaɪpərˈθɜːrməl/ | adj. | 过热的，超热的 | Relating to or characterized by temperatures considerably above normal. | Example 1: Hyperthermal vents on the ocean floor support unique ecosystems. 海底过热喷口支持独特的生态系统。

Example 2:  The hyperthermal environment of the engine requires specialized lubricants. 发动机过热的环境需要专门的润滑剂。 | Near synonyms: superheated, overheated | Antonyms: hypothermal, cold | word type: adjective
