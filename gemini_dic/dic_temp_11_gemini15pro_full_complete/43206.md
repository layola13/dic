crabgrasses:/ˈkræbˌɡræsɪz/
n.
马唐属植物;蟋蟀草
Any of various weedy grasses of the genus Digitaria, especially D. sanguinalis (large crabgrass or hairy crabgrass), having long, creeping stems that root at the nodes. These grasses are common lawn weeds in warm regions.
Crabgrasses are a common problem in lawns during the summer months.  马唐在夏季的草坪上是个常见问题。
The gardener spent all afternoon trying to remove crabgrasses from his flowerbeds. 园丁花了整个下午试图从他的花坛里清除马唐。
近义词:weed, grass
反义词:flower, desirable plant
词性:noun
