insnarer:/ɪnˈsnɛərər/
n.
诱捕者；陷害者
Someone or something that insnares.
The poacher was an insnarer of wild animals.  这个偷猎者是一个野生动物的诱捕者。
近义词：trapper, entrapper, ensnarer
反义词：liberator, rescuer
词性：名词

insnare:/ɪnˈsnɛər/
vt.
诱捕；使陷入圈套；使入迷
To catch in or as in a snare; trap. To involve in difficulties or complications from which it is difficult to escape. To lure into a dangerous situation
The spider insnared the fly in its web. 蜘蛛用网诱捕了苍蝇。
His charm insnared her. 他的魅力使她着迷。
近义词：trap, entrap, ensnare, catch, tangle
反义词：free, release, liberate
词性：动词

词源：
From in- + snare.  "snare" 来自古英语 "sneare," 意为 "noose, trap"（套索，陷阱）。 "in-" 是一个前缀，表示 "in, into, on, upon"（在...里面，进入，在...上面）。
