glim:/ɡlɪm/ | n. |微光，闪光；少许，一点点|A faint or wavering light; a small amount or glimpse of something.|例句：I saw a glim of hope in his eyes. 我在他的眼中看到了一丝希望。

例句：There's not a glim of truth in what he said. 他说的话没有一点是真的。|近义词：gleam, glimmer, flicker, shimmer, spark|反义词：darkness, gloom|词性：名词
