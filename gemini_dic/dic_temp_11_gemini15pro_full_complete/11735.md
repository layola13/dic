aromatherapists:/əˌrəʊməˈθerəpɪsts/| n.|芳香治疗师|A person who practices aromatherapy, using essential oils and other aromatic compounds for therapeutic purposes.|例句：The aromatherapists at the spa offer a variety of massage treatments.  这家水疗中心的芳香治疗师提供各种按摩疗法。

例句：I consulted with several aromatherapists before choosing the right essential oils for my needs.  在选择适合我需要的精油之前，我咨询了几位芳香治疗师。|近义词：massage therapist, holistic practitioner|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) conventional doctor, physician|词性:Noun (plural)

ETYMOLOGY->
aroma + therapy + -ist
aroma: from Greek arōma ‘spice’
therapy: from Greek therapeia ‘curing, service done to the sick’
-ist: a suffix forming agent nouns, denoting a person who practices or is concerned with something, or holds certain principles, etc.
