abdominally:/æbˈdɑːmɪnəli/
adverb
腹部地；经腹部地
In or through the abdomen.
The patient was treated abdominally.  病人经腹部接受了治疗。
The surgeon made an incision abdominally.  外科医生在腹部做了一个切口。
Synonym:  intra-abdominally
Antonym:  externally
