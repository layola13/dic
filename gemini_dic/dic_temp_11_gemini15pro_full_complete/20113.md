biotically:/baɪˈɒtɪkli/
adverb
生物地；通过生物作用
Relating to living organisms; by biological means.
The soil was biotically enriched by the addition of compost.  土壤通过添加堆肥而生物富集。
The ecosystem was biotically diverse.  这个生态系统生物种类繁多。
Synonym: organically, naturally
Antonym: abiotically, chemically
词性: 副词
