licentiate: /laɪˈsɛnʃɪət/ /laɪˈsɛnʃiət/ | n.|  学位获得者；领有执照者|A person who has been granted a license, especially one to practice a profession, or who has passed the examinations for a degree but has not yet received it.  | The licentiate in medicine was allowed to practice under the supervision of a fully qualified doctor.  这位医学学位获得者获准在一位完全合格的医生的监督下行医。| synonym:  graduate, diploma holder, qualified professional | antonym: amateur, unqualified | noun

licentiates: /laɪˈsɛnʃɪəts/ /laɪˈsɛnʃiəts/ | n.| 学位获得者；领有执照者（复数）| Plural form of "licentiate." |  The licentiates gathered for the commencement ceremony.  学位获得者们聚集在一起参加毕业典礼。 | synonym: graduates, diploma holders, qualified professionals | antonym: amateurs, unqualified | noun
