barracked:/ˈbærəkt/ | v. |起哄，嘲笑；喝倒彩 |to shout loudly at someone to show disapproval or to try to make them make a mistake, especially at a sporting event|The fans barracked the referee throughout the match. 球迷们整场比赛都对裁判喝倒彩。|Synonyms: jeer, heckle, taunt |Antonyms: cheer, applaud, support |verb

barracked:/ˈbærəkt/ | adj. |被关押的；被拘禁的|(of soldiers) living in barracks|The barracked troops were awaiting deployment.  驻扎在兵营里的部队正在等待部署。|Synonyms: confined, quartered, garrisoned |Antonyms: free, liberated, released |adjective
