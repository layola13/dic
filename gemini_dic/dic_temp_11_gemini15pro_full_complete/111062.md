listenable:/ˈlɪsənəbl/| adj.|可倾听的，可以一听的|Easy or enjoyable to listen to.|The podcast is very listenable, even for people who aren't usually interested in the topic.  这个播客很容易听，即使是对这个话题通常不感兴趣的人来说也是如此。|Synonyms: audible, agreeable, pleasant, enjoyable|Antonyms: inaudible, unpleasant, grating|adjective
