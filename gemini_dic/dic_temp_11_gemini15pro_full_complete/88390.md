hirelings:/ˈhaɪərliŋz/
n.
雇佣兵，受雇者，唯利是图的人
People employed to do menial work, especially those regarded as mercenary or unscrupulous.
The king surrounded himself with hirelings and sycophants. 国王身边都是些唯利是图的雇佣兵和马屁精。

The factory owner hired some hirelings to break the strike.  工厂主雇了一些人来破坏罢工。

mercenaries, hacks, employees
loyalists, volunteers, devotees
noun
