nonexceptionally:/ˌnɒnɪkˈsɛpʃənəli/ | adv. |并非例外地，平常地| Not exceptionally; in an ordinary or usual way. | The food was nonexceptionally bland. 这食物很一般，没什么味道。 | He performed nonexceptionally on the test. 他的考试成绩很普通。 | 近义词: ordinarily, typically, usually, commonly | 反义词: exceptionally, remarkably, unusually | 词性: 副词

词源:  "non-" (not) + "exceptionally" (in an exceptional manner).  "Exceptionally" comes from "exception" (from Latin "exceptio," meaning "a taking out, exception"), ultimately from "excipere" (to take out, except).
