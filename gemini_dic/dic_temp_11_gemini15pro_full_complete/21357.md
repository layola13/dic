blore:/blɔːr/| n.| 喧闹声，咆哮声，号角声 |A loud, resonant sound, like that of a trumpet or horn. |The blore of the trumpets announced the king's arrival.  国王的到来由嘹亮的号角声宣告。|近义词：blast, roar, blare |反义词: whisper, murmur |名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bloren, from Old Norse *blása (“to blow”). Cognate with Icelandic blása (“to blow”).
