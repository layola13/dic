delusionary:/dɪˈluːʒənəri/
adjective
中文翻译：妄想的；错觉的
英文解析：Based on or having the nature of a delusion; mistaken or unfounded.
例句：He had delusionary beliefs about being a secret agent. 他错觉地认为自己是一个特工。

例句：Her delusionary state made it difficult for her to function in everyday life. 她妄想的状态使她难以在日常生活中正常运作。

近义词：deluded, mistaken, unrealistic, illusory, fanciful, false
反义词：realistic, rational, sane, sound, lucid
词性：adjective


delusion:/dɪˈluːʒən/
noun
中文翻译：妄想；错觉
英文解析：An idiosyncratic belief or impression that is firmly maintained despite being contradicted by what is generally accepted as reality or rational argument, typically a symptom of mental disorder.
例句：He suffered from delusions of grandeur. 他患有夸大妄想症。

例句：The patient experienced delusions of persecution. 病人经历了被迫害妄想。

近义词：hallucination, illusion, misconception, fallacy, fantasy
反义词：reality, truth, fact
词性：noun
