moistly:/ˈmɔɪstli/ | adv. |潮湿地；湿润地| In a moist manner; slightly wet. | The cake was moistly baked and delicious. 蛋糕烤得湿润可口。| 近义词: damply, humidly | 反义词: dryly, aridly | 词性: 副词

词源: From moist + -ly.  "Moist" comes from Old French "moiste," ultimately from Latin "musteus," meaning "new, fresh, young."
