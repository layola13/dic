other-worldly:/ˌʌðərˈwɜːldli/ | adj. | 超凡脱俗的；空灵的；非尘世的 | Relating to or characteristic of another world, especially a spiritual or supernatural one; ethereal. |  She had an other-worldly beauty. 她有一种超凡脱俗的美。 | The music had an other-worldly quality. 这音乐有一种空灵的特质。 | 近义词: unearthly, supernatural, ethereal, spiritual, transcendental | 反义词: mundane, earthly, worldly, terrestrial | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From other + worldly.  "Worldly" originally meant "of this world," so "other-worldly" literally means "of another world."
