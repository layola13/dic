arundinaceous:/ˌærənˌdɪˈneɪʃəs/
adjective
芦苇的；像芦苇的
Resembling or consisting of reeds or canes.  Relating to or characteristic of reeds.
The arundinaceous stems swayed gently in the breeze.  芦苇般的茎秆在微风中轻轻摇曳。
The marsh was filled with tall, arundinaceous grasses.  沼泽里长满了高大、像芦苇一样的草。
近义词:reedy, cany
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider woody, ligneous)
词性:adjective
