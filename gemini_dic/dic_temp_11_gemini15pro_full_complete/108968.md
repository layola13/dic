leester: /ˈliːstər/ | n. |  新手，菜鸟 (especially in online gaming) | A leester is a newbie or unskilled player, often used derogatorily in online gaming communities. |  例句：He's such a leester, he keeps getting killed. 他真是个菜鸟，总是被杀。

例句：Don't be so harsh on him, he's just a leester. 别对他这么苛刻，他只是个新手。

近义词：noob, newbie, beginner | 反义词：expert, pro, veteran | 词性：名词
