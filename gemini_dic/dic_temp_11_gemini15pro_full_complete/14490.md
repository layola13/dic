avauntour:/əˌvɔːnˈtʊər/ | n. | 冒险家; 骑士; (旧) 冒险事业 | An adventurer; a knight; (archaic) an adventurous undertaking. | He was a true avauntour, always seeking new lands and challenges. 他是一位真正的冒险家，总是寻求新的土地和挑战。 | The avauntour embarked on a perilous journey across the mountains.  这位冒险家开始了一段穿越群山的危险旅程。 | 近义词: adventurer, explorer, knight errant | 反义词: coward, stay-at-home | 词性: 名词

词源:

From Old French *avanturier, from avanturer "to venture," from avant "forward," from Latin abante "from before."
