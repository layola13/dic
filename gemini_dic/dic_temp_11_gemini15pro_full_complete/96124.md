indell:/ɪnˈdɛl/ | n. |山谷，洼地; (苏格兰)低洼的平地| A small valley or hollow; (Scottish) low-lying land. |The farmer grazed his sheep in the indell.| 近义词:valley, dale, glen, hollow|反义词:hill, mountain| 词性:名词

词源: From Scots, variant of *dale*. Related to Old English *dæl* (“valley”).
