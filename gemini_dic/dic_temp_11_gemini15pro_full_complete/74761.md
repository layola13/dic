freezability:/ˌfriːzəˈbɪləti/
n.|可冻性，冷冻能力|The freezability of food depends on its water content.  食物的可冻性取决于其含水量。|例句：The freezability of this fruit is excellent.  这种水果的冷冻性能非常好。

例句：The researchers tested the freezability of different types of meat. 研究人员测试了不同类型肉类的冷冻能力。
|近义词：freezing capacity,  freezing potential|反义词: resistance to freezing, thawability|词性: 名词
