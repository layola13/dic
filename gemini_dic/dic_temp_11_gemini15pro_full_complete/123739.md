moonstone:/ˈmuːnˌstoʊn/| n.|月光石|A semiprecious stone that exhibits a milky bluish or grayish sheen, especially as a result of its internal structure causing light to be scattered.  It is a variety of feldspar.|She wore a moonstone pendant. 她戴着一条月光石吊坠。|Synonyms:adularia, orthoclase|Antonyms: N/A|noun
