interwrapping:/ˌɪntərˈræpɪŋ/
n.
相互包裹，隔层包装，衬垫物
The act or process of wrapping objects individually and then wrapping them together.  The material used for wrapping between items.
The delicate china was protected by interwrapping.  这些易碎的瓷器用衬垫物保护着。
近义词: padding, cushioning, packing, wrapping
反义词: unwrapping
词性: 名词

interwrap:/ˌɪntərˈræp/
v.
相互包裹，隔层包装
To wrap between items, to wrap individually and then together.
He interwrapped the glasses with tissue paper to prevent them from breaking. 他用薄纸将玻璃杯隔层包装，以防止它们破碎。
近义词: pad, cushion, pack, wrap
反义词: unwrap
词性: 动词


词源:
From inter- + wrap.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among."
