haematosin:/ˌhiːməˈtoʊsɪn/ /ˌheməˈtoʊsɪn/ | n. | 血红素 | A red pigment in blood, specifically the iron-containing nonprotein part of hemoglobin. | Example: Haematosin is responsible for the red color of blood. 血红素是血液呈现红色的原因。

Example:  The scientist isolated the haematosin from the blood sample.  科学家从血液样本中分离出血红素。 | 近义词: heme, hematin | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *haîma* (blood) + *-sin* (a suffix forming nouns).
