goodby:/ɡʊdˈbaɪ/ /ɡʊdˈbaɪ/ | int. n.|再见，告别|A farewell remark.|例句：Goodbye, see you later. 再见，回头见。

近义词：farewell, adieu, so long|反义词: hello, welcome|词性: interjection, noun

词源: Contraction of "God be with ye" (God be with you).
