geography:/dʒiˈɒɡrəfi/ /dʒiˈɑːɡrəfi/| n.|地理，地理学|The study of the physical features of the earth and its atmosphere, and of human activity as it affects and is affected by these, including the distribution of populations and resources, land use, and industries.|例句：She is majoring in geography. 她主修地理。|例句：The geography of this region is very diverse. 这个地区的地理环境非常多样。|近义词：topography, terrain|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

etymology->
From Old French geographie and Latin geographia, from Greek geographia  "description of the earth's surface," from geo- "earth" + -graphia "description, writing."
