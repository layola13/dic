militiamen:/mɪˈlɪʃəmən/ | n. | 民兵，义勇军 | A member of a militia, a group of civilians trained as soldiers but not part of the regular army. |  The militiamen defended their village against the bandits. 民兵保卫他们的村庄抵抗土匪。|  The governor called up the militiamen to help control the riots. 州长召集民兵来帮助控制骚乱。| synonyms: reservist, volunteer soldier, territorial | antonyms: regular soldier, professional soldier | noun
