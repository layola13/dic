divan:/daɪˈvæn/| n.|长沙发，卧榻；(东方国家)议事厅，接待室|A long low sofa without arms or a back, typically placed against a wall.  A council chamber or reception room in the Middle East.|She stretched out on the divan, enjoying the afternoon sun. 她躺在卧榻上，享受着午后的阳光。 The sultan received his visitors in the divan. 苏丹在接待室接见了他的访客。|近义词：sofa, couch, settee|反义词: (无明确反义词)|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *divan*, from Turkish *divan*, from Persian *dīvān*, originally meaning "list, register," later "royal court, council, office," and then "sofa."
