hydrophobia:/ˌhaɪdrəˈfoʊbiə/ | n. | 恐水症；狂犬病 | An abnormal and persistent dread of water.  A morbid fear of water. Also means rabies. |  Example 1:  His hydrophobia prevented him from even taking a bath. 他有恐水症，甚至不敢洗澡。

Example 2:  Rabies, also known as hydrophobia, is a deadly viral disease. 狂犬病，也称为恐水症，是一种致命的病毒性疾病。 | 近义词: aquaphobia | 反义词: aquaphilia | 词性: noun
