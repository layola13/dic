approximates:/əˈprɑːksɪmeɪts/
verb
接近，近似
comes close or near to something in quality, quantity, or manner.
The total cost approximates $1 million.  总成本接近100万美元。
synonyms: approaches, nears, resembles
antonyms: differs, diverges, contrasts
词性: 动词

approximate:/əˈprɑːksɪmət/
adjective
近似的，大约的
almost correct or exact, but not completely so.
The approximate cost is $100. 大约的成本是100美元。
synonyms: rough, estimated, near
antonyms: exact, precise, accurate
词性: 形容词

approximation:/əˌprɑːksɪˈmeɪʃn/
noun
近似值，近似
a value or quantity that is nearly but not exactly correct.
This is only a rough approximation. 这只是一个粗略的近似值。
synonyms: estimate, guess, nearness
antonyms: precision, exactness, accuracy
词性: 名词


词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin approximatus, past participle of approximare ‘to approach’, from ad- ‘to’ + proximare ‘come near’, from proximus ‘nearest’.
