eophytic:/ˌiːoʊˈfɪtɪk/
adj.
始生植物的;在演化早期阶段出现的
Relating to or characteristic of an eophyte; a plant that appeared early in evolutionary history.
Eophytic flora colonized the bare volcanic rock. 始生植物群落覆盖了光秃秃的火山岩。
近义词:primitive, early
反义词:recent, modern
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From eo- (dawn, early) + -phytic (relating to plants).  The prefix "eo-" comes from Greek "ēōs" meaning "dawn" or "early." The suffix "-phytic" comes from Greek "phyton" meaning "plant."
