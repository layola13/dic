objectified:/əbˈdʒɛktɪfaɪd/
adj.
被物化的；被客体化的
treated as an object, especially a sexual object; deprived of human qualities or individuality.
She felt objectified by the photographer's gaze. 她感觉被摄影师的目光物化了。
近义词:dehumanized, depersonalized
反义词:humanized, personalized
词性:形容词


objectify:/əbˈdʒɛktɪfaɪ/
vt.
使客观化；使具体化；物化
to treat someone as an object, especially a sexual object; to express something in a concrete form.
The advertising industry often objectifies women. 广告行业经常物化女性。
近义词:dehumanize, depersonalize, materialize
反义词:humanize, personalize
词性:动词
