diffracting:/dɪˈfræktɪŋ/ | v. | 衍射，绕射 | The light was diffracting through the prism. 光线正在透过棱镜衍射。|  The sound waves were diffracting around the corner. 声波正绕着角落衍射。| synonyms: scattering, dispersing, spreading | antonyms: focusing, concentrating | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *diffractus*, past participle of *diffringere*  "to break apart," from *dis-* "apart" + *frangere* "to break."  The scientific sense emerged in the 17th century.
