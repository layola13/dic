assemblers:/əˈsɛmblərz/ | n. | 汇编器；汇编程序；装配工 | A program that translates assembly language into machine code.  People who put together the parts of something. | Example: The assemblers translated the code into a format the computer could understand. 汇编器将代码转换成计算机可以理解的格式。

Example: The assemblers on the factory floor worked quickly and efficiently. 工厂里的装配工工作迅速高效。 | 近义词: compilers, builders, constructors | 反义词: disassemblers, deconstructors | 词性: noun
