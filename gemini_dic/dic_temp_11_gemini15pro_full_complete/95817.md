incorporate: /ɪnˈkɔːrpəreɪt/ (US) /ɪnˈkɔːpəreɪt/ (UK)
| vt. | 包含，吸收；体现；注册成立 | To include something as part of something larger; to combine something into a single entity; to form a legal corporation. | The new car design incorporates all the latest safety features. 这款新车的设计包含了所有最新的安全特性。| include, integrate, combine, absorb | exclude, separate, remove | verb

incorporate: /ɪnˈkɔːrpəreɪt/ (US) /ɪnˈkɔːpəreɪt/ (UK)
| adj. | 合并的；一体化的 | Formed into a legal corporation. |  The newly incorporated company began trading on the stock market. 这家新成立的公司开始在股票市场上交易。 | integrated, combined, united | separate, individual | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin incorporatus, past participle of incorporare "to embody," from in- "in" + corporare "to form into a body," from corpus "body."
