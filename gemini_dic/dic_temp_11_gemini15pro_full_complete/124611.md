Etymology:
From "mousy" + "-ness" + "-'s". "Mousy" comes from "mouse," referring to the quiet and timid nature often associated with mice.  "-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition. "-'s" is the possessive suffix.
