coordination:/koʊˌɔːrdɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 协调，调和 |The act of organizing different parts or people so that they work together effectively. |The coordination of the dancers was impeccable. 舞蹈演员们的配合天衣无缝。|  近义词：cooperation, collaboration, harmonization, synchronization | 反义词：discord, conflict, disorganization | 词性: 名词

coordination:/kəʊˌɔːdɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 协调，调和 |The ability to move different parts of your body smoothly and efficiently. |Her hand-eye coordination is excellent. 她的手眼协调能力非常好。 | 近义词：dexterity, agility, skill | 反义词：clumsiness, awkwardness | 词性: 名词
