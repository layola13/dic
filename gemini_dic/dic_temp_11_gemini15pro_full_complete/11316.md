arduousnesses:/ˈɑːrdʒuːəsnəsɪz/
n.
艰难险阻；艰辛
The quality or state of being arduous; difficulty.
The arduousnesses of the climb left him exhausted.  攀登的艰辛使他筋疲力尽。
difficulty, hardship, laboriousness, strenuousness, toil
ease, facility, simplicity
