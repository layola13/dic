lukewarmly:/ˈluːkwɔːrmli/ | adv. | 不冷不热地；不热心地；冷淡地 |  In a manner that lacks enthusiasm or conviction; indifferently.  Showing little enthusiasm or interest. | She greeted the proposal lukewarmly. 她对这个提议反应冷淡。|  He lukewarmly applauded the performance. 他对演出敷衍地鼓了鼓掌。 | 近义词: apathetically, indifferently, halfheartedly | 反义词: enthusiastically, eagerly, warmly | 词性: 副词

词源: From lukewarm + -ly.  Lukewarm itself comes from Middle English lewke, from Old Norse hlýr ("warm") + -k, a frequentative suffix.
