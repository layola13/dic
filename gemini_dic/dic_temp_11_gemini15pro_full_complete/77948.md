genotypes:/ˈdʒiːnəˌtaɪps/ | n. |基因型|The genetic constitution of an individual organism.|Scientists compared the genotypes of the two species. 科学家比较了这两个物种的基因型。| synonyms: genetic makeup, genome | antonyms: phenotype | noun

genotype:/ˈdʒiːnəˌtaɪp/ | n. |基因型|The genetic makeup of an organism or group of organisms with reference to a single trait, set of traits, or an entire complex of traits.|The plant's genotype determines its resistance to disease.  这种植物的基因型决定了它对疾病的抵抗力。| synonyms: genetic constitution, genome | antonyms: phenotype | noun
