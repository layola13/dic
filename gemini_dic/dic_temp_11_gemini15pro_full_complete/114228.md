mail-bombing:/ˈmeɪlˌbɒmɪŋ/| n.|邮件炸弹|The act of sending a massive amount of emails to a specific email address, often with the intent to overwhelm the server or the recipient's inbox.|The activist group was accused of mail-bombing the senator's office.|近义词:email bombing, spamming, cyberattack|反义词:None|词性:名词

Etymology:
"mail" (n.) - from Old French "male" (bag, wallet), ultimately from Frankish "malha" (bag).
"bombing" (n.) - from "bomb" + "-ing" (present participle suffix). "bomb" from Italian "bomba" (large hollow ball filled with explosives), ultimately from Latin "bombus" (a deep hollow sound).
