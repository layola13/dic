automatics:/ˌɔːtəˈmætɪks/ | n. | 自动装置；自动机械；自动武器 |Automatic devices or machines; firearms that reload automatically. |The factory relies heavily on automatics for its production. 这家工厂的生产严重依赖自动化设备。|  The soldier carried automatics into battle. 士兵携带自动武器投入战斗。 | synonyms: automated systems, robots, automatic weapons, machine guns | antonyms: manual devices, hand tools | noun

automatic:/ˌɔːtəˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 自动的；无意识的；必然的 | Done or occurring spontaneously, without conscious thought or intention; operating mechanically;  inevitable. |The automatic doors opened as we approached. 当我们走近时，自动门打开了。 |  Breathing is an automatic function of the body. 呼吸是身体的自动功能。|His failure was automatic after he stopped studying. 他停止学习后，失败是必然的。 | synonyms: involuntary, instinctive, mechanical, inevitable, spontaneous | antonyms: manual, deliberate, voluntary, conscious | adjective

automatically:/ˌɔːtəˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 自动地；无意识地；必然地 | In a mechanical or involuntary way; without conscious thought or intention; inevitably. | The lights turned on automatically when it got dark. 天黑时，灯自动亮了。| I automatically reached for my phone when I heard the notification. 我听到通知后，无意识地伸手去拿手机。 | synonyms: instinctively, involuntarily, mechanically, spontaneously | antonyms: manually, deliberately, consciously | adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Greek word "automatos" meaning "self-acting,"  from "autos" meaning "self" + "matos" meaning "thinking, willing."
