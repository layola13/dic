intrinsic:/ɪnˈtrɪnzɪk/ /ɪnˈtrɪnsɪk/| adj.|固有的，内在的，本质的| Belonging naturally; essential.| Curiosity is intrinsic to human beings. 好奇心是人类固有的。|近义词：inherent, innate, essential, inborn, natural|反义词：extrinsic, external, acquired|词性:形容词

词源:
From Late Latin *intrinsecus (“inwardly”), from Latin intro (“within”) + secus (“beside, along”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *sekʷ- (“to follow”).
