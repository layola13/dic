ergometer:/ˈɜːrɡɒmɪtər/ (UK) /ˈɜːrɡɑːmɪtər/ (US)| n. |测力计；功率计；划船器|An instrument for measuring the amount of work performed (as by muscles). A device, such as a rowing machine or stationary bicycle, used for ergometric testing or exercise.|

例句：
The athlete trained on the ergometer to improve his endurance.  运动员在功率计上训练以提高耐力。

例句：
The doctor used an ergometer to assess the patient's cardiovascular health. 医生使用测力计来评估病人的心血管健康。


近义词：dynamometer, exercise bike, rowing machine|
反义词: None (No direct antonym for a measuring instrument)|
词性: noun|

词源:  From Greek *ergon* (work) + *metron* (measure).|
