manograph:/ˈmænəɡræf/ | n. |压力图;压力记录器;单张印刷品|An instrument for recording variations in pressure, especially blood pressure. Also, a printed sheet or pamphlet.|The doctor used a manograph to monitor the patient's blood pressure during surgery. 医生在手术期间用压力图监测病人的血压。|近义词：sphygmograph, pressure gauge |反义词: None |词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *manos* (rare, thin) + *-graphos* (written, recorded)
