antitechnologies:/ˌæntiˌtɛkˈnɒlədʒiz/ | n. | 反技术；抵制技术；对抗技术 | Technologies that are perceived as harmful, undesirable, or that seek to counteract the negative effects of other technologies. |  Example: Some consider social media to be an antitechnology due to its potential for privacy violations and spread of misinformation.  一些人认为社交媒体是一种反技术，因为它可能侵犯隐私和传播错误信息。

Example:  The development of antitechnologies like ad blockers is a direct response to the pervasiveness of online advertising.  广告拦截器等反技术的发展是对在线广告泛滥的直接回应。| Near synonyms: counter-technologies, opposing technologies | Antonym: technologies | noun
