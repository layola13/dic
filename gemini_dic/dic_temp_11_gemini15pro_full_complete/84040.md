half-fascinatingly:/ˌhæfˈfæsɪneɪtɪŋli/
half-fascinatingly| adv.|略微令人着迷地；半有趣地|In a manner that is partially or moderately fascinating; not fully captivating but holding some interest.|The documentary was half-fascinatingly about the mating rituals of deep-sea creatures. 这部纪录片略微有趣地讲述了深海生物的交配仪式。|half-interestingly, moderately engaging, mildly captivating|tediously, boringly, dully
half-fascinatingly|词性|副词

"half-" (Old English healf) + "fascinatingly" (from "fascinate" (Latin fascinare "to bewitch") + "-ing" + "-ly")
