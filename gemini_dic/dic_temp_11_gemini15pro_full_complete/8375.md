anti-racing:/ˈæntiˈreɪsɪŋ/
adjective
反赛车的，反对赛车的
Opposed to racing, especially horse racing or dog racing.
The anti-racing group protested outside the racetrack.  反赛车组织在赛马场外抗议。
The politician voiced his anti-racing views.  这位政治家表达了他反对赛车的观点。
近义词: against racing
反义词: pro-racing
词性: adjective


anti-racing:/ˈæntiˈreɪsɪŋ/
noun
反赛车运动
The movement or campaign against racing, especially horse racing or dog racing.
Anti-racing has gained momentum in recent years.  近年来，反赛车运动势头强劲。
There was a surge of support for anti-racing after the documentary aired.  纪录片播出后，对反赛车运动的支持激增。
近义词: opposition to racing
反义词: support for racing
词性: noun


词源:
"anti-" (prefix) + "racing" (noun)

"anti-" comes from Greek "anti" meaning "against, opposite of."
"racing" comes from the verb "race," which has its roots in Old Norse "rasa" meaning "to run, to race."
