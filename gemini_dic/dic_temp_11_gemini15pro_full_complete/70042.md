fetterer:/ˈfɛtərər/| n.|束缚者，限制者|Someone or something that restricts or restrains.|The high cost of living is a fetterer to many young people's dreams. 高昂的生活成本束缚着许多年轻人的梦想。|例句：Debt can be a real fetterer on your ambitions. 债务会严重束缚你的抱负。

例句：The company's strict rules are a fetterer to innovation. 公司严格的规定束缚了创新。

|近义词：restrainer, hinderer, limiter|反义词: liberator, emancipator|词性:noun
