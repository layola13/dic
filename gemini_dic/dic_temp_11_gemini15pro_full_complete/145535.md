overcommending:/ˌoʊvər kəˈmɛndɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度赞扬的；过分推荐的 | Praising or recommending something excessively, beyond what is warranted or deserved. |  He was criticized for overcommending a mediocre restaurant owned by his friend. 他因为过分推荐他朋友开的平庸餐馆而受到批评。

The marketing campaign was accused of overcommending the product's capabilities.  这个营销活动被指责夸大了产品的功能。 | 近义词：overpraising, exaggerating, overselling | 反义词：understating, downplaying | 词性: adjective
