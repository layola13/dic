nonostentation:/ˌnɒnˌɒstɛnˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 不炫耀，不夸示 |  Lack of ostentation; avoidance of showy display.  The state or quality of being modest and unassuming. |  She lived a life of nonostentation, preferring simple clothes and a modest home. 她过着不炫耀的生活，喜欢简单的衣服和朴素的家。| synonyms: modesty, unpretentiousness, humility, simplicity, understatement | antonyms: ostentation, showiness, flashiness, pretentiousness, extravagance | noun
