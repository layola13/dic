attache:/əˈtæʃeɪ/ | n. | 专员；随员；大使馆馆员 | A person who is assigned to the diplomatic staff of an ambassador or other high-ranking official.  |  The military attache provided intelligence reports to the ambassador. 军事专员向大使提供了情报报告。|  Example: The cultural attaché arranged for the visiting artists to meet local musicians. 文化专员安排来访艺术家与当地音乐家见面。 | synonym:  aide, assistant, diplomat, official, representative | antonym:  civilian (in some contexts) | noun

attache case:/əˈtæʃeɪ keɪs/ | n. | 公文包 | A small, rectangular briefcase, typically made of leather or other sturdy material, used for carrying documents and other business essentials. | He carried his important papers in an attache case. 他把重要文件放在公文包里。| Example: The lawyer opened his attache case and pulled out the contract. 律师打开公文包，拿出了合同。 | synonym: briefcase, portfolio | antonym: backpack, satchel (less formal alternatives) | noun
