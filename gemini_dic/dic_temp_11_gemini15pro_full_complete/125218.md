multicorneal:/ˌmʌltiˈkɔːrniəl/| adj.|多角膜的|Having multiple corneas.  It describes a structure, typically an eye, possessing more than one cornea.|A multicorneal eye structure is found in some insects.|Near Synonyms: multi-corneal, polycorneal|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "single-corneal" could be considered)|adjective
