evangelises:/ɪˈvændʒəlaɪzɪz/
verb
传福音；传播福音；劝人信教
To preach the gospel; to convert or seek to convert to Christianity.
The missionary evangelises in remote villages.  传教士在偏远村庄传福音。
He evangelises to the poor and needy. 他向穷人和需要帮助的人传福音。
Synonyms:  preach, proselytize, convert
Antonyms:  discourage, dissuade, secularize
词性: 动词
