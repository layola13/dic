cross-referring:/ˌkrɒs rɪˈfɜːrɪŋ/ | adj. | 交叉引用的，相互参照的 | Referring to another text or part of a text.  It creates connections within a document or between different documents. |  This section is cross-referring to the information in Chapter 3.  这一节交叉引用了第三章的信息。

The footnotes cross-referring to other sources are helpful.  脚注中交叉引用其他资料很有帮助。 | synonyms: interreferencing, correlating | antonyms: unrelated, independent | adjective
