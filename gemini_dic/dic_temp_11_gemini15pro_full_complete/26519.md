cabbaging:/ˈkæbɪdʒɪŋ/
|n.| 偷窃（尤指小东西）;  收集零碎物品; 贪污小额款项 | The act of stealing, especially in small amounts; collecting scraps or odds and ends; pilfering small sums of money.| He was accused of cabbaging office supplies. 他被指控偷窃办公室用品。 | synonyms: pilfering, pinching, filching, purloining, swiping, appropriating | antonyms: donating, giving, returning | noun

cabbaging:/ˈkæbɪdʒɪŋ/
|v.| 偷窃（尤指小东西）;  收集零碎物品; 贪污小额款项 | To steal, especially in small amounts; to collect scraps or odds and ends; to pilfer small sums of money.| He was caught cabbaging stamps from the mailroom. 他在邮件室偷邮票时被抓住了。| synonyms: pilfer, pinch, filch, purloin, swipe, appropriate | antonyms: donate, give, return | verb
