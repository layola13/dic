bravoing:/ˈbrɑːvoʊɪŋ/
v.
喝彩；大声叫好
The act of shouting "Bravo!" to express appreciation or approval, especially for a performer.
The audience was bravoing the singer after her incredible performance.  观众在她精彩的表演后为她喝彩。
近义词: applauding, cheering, acclaiming
反义词: booing, jeering, hissing
词性: 动词 (verb)
