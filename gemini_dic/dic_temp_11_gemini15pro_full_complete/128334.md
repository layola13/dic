neuterly:/ˈnjuːtərli/
adv.
中性地；不带偏见地
In a neutral manner; without bias or favoritism.
The judge listened neuterly to both sides of the argument.  法官不带偏见地听取了双方的辩论。
He reported the incident neuterly, without expressing his own opinion. 他中立地报道了这起事件，没有表达自己的意见。
近义词: impartially, neutrally, objectively
反义词: biasedly, subjectively, partially
词性: 副词

neuter:/ˈnjuːtər/
n.
中性；中性词；无性动物；阉割动物
A thing that is neutral or neutered; a word that is neither masculine nor feminine; a castrated animal.
In some languages, nouns have neuter gender.  在一些语言中，名词有中性。
The vet neutered the cat to prevent unwanted kittens. 兽医阉割了这只猫以防止不必要的幼崽出生。
近义词: asexual, unsexed
反义词: male, female
词性: 名词

neuter:/ˈnjuːtər/
adj.
中性的；无性的
Neither masculine nor feminine in gender; having no or undeveloped sexual organs.
"It" is a neuter pronoun in English. “It”在英语中是一个中性代词。
Some plants are neuter, having neither male nor female reproductive organs. 一些植物是无性的，既没有雄性也没有雌性生殖器官。
近义词: asexual, sexless, neutral
反义词: male, female, sexual
词性: 形容词

neuter:/ˈnjuːtər/
vt.
阉割；使中性化
To castrate or spay (an animal); to make neutral or ineffective.
They decided to neuter their dog. 他们决定阉割他们的狗。
The amendment effectively neutered the law. 这项修正案实际上使该法律失效。
近义词: castrate, spay, emasculate
反义词: empower, strengthen
词性: 动词

词源: From Latin *neuter (neither), from ne- (not) + uter (which of two).
