blimp-out:/ˈblɪmp aʊt/| n.|暴饮暴食; 放纵;  (尤指)大吃垃圾食品|A sudden indulgence in a large amount of food, especially junk food.  It often implies a loss of control and excessive consumption.|例句：I had a major blimp-out last night and ate an entire pizza. 昨晚我大吃特吃，吃了一整个披萨。

例句：After a stressful week at work, she had a blimp-out on ice cream and cookies.  在经历了紧张的一周工作后，她放纵自己吃冰淇淋和饼干。|近义词：pig out, binge, gorge, indulge|反义词:diet, abstain|词性:名词

词源:  "blimp" originally referred to a small, non-rigid airship. The connection to overeating likely comes from the image of a blimp expanding with air, similar to a stomach expanding with food. This metaphorical usage likely emerged in the mid-20th century.
