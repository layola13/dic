nos:/nəʊz/| pron.|我们（us的宾格）|The object form of "we". Used after a preposition or as the object of a verb.|He gave the book to nos. 他把书给了我们。|近义词:us|反义词:them|词性:代词

Etymology->
From Latin *nōs, accusative and ablative plural of *ego ("I"). Cognate with Italian noi, Spanish nos, French nous.
