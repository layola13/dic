ecologies:/ɪˈkɒlədʒiz/ /iˈkɑːlədʒiz/ | n. |生态，生态学，生态环境|The study of the relationships between organisms and their environment.  The complex web of interactions between living things and their surroundings, including both the physical environment and other organisms.|

例句：
The ecologies of the rainforest are incredibly diverse.
雨林的生态环境极其多样。

The construction project threatened the delicate ecologies of the coastal region.
该建设项目威胁到沿海地区脆弱的生态环境。


近义词：ecosystems, environments, habitats
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *écologie*, from Greek *oikos* "house, dwelling" + *-logia* "study of".
