abducting:/əbˈdʌktɪŋ/
verb
绑架；劫持
the action of taking someone away illegally by force or deception; kidnapping
The police are investigating the abducting of a young child. 警方正在调查一起幼童绑架案。
Synonyms: kidnapping, seizing, snatching, capturing
Antonyms: releasing, freeing, liberating
词性: 动词 (present participle)

abduct:/əbˈdʌkt/

Antonyms: release, free, liberate
