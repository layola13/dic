filmstrip: /ˈfɪlmˌstrɪp/ | n. | 电影胶片；幻灯片 | A length of film containing a sequence of images, especially one designed for viewing as slides projected on a screen. | We watched a filmstrip about the solar system in science class. 我们在科学课上看了一部关于太阳系的幻灯片。 | The museum has a collection of old filmstrips showing life in the 1920s.  这家博物馆收藏了一些展示 1920 年代生活的旧幻灯片。 | synonyms: slide, transparency, film | antonyms: movie, video | noun
