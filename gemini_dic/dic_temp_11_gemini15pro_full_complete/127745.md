negotiators:/nɪˈɡoʊʃieɪtərz/
n.
谈判者，协商者
A person who negotiates, typically on behalf of a group or organization.
The negotiators worked late into the night to reach an agreement. 谈判人员工作到深夜才达成协议。

Synonyms: mediator, arbitrator, intermediary, representative, envoy, delegate, agent, broker
Antonyms: opponent, adversary
