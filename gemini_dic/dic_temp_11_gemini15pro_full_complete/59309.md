earbashing:/ˈɪəˌbæʃɪŋ/ | n. | 痛骂，唠叨，喋喋不休 | A prolonged and often tedious scolding or lecture. |  My boss gave me a real earbashing for being late. 我的老板因为我迟到狠狠地训斥了我一顿。

| 近义词：scolding, lecture, harangue, tirade, diatribe | 反义词：praise, commendation | 词性: 名词


earbashing:/ˈɪəˌbæʃɪŋ/ | v. | 痛骂，唠叨，喋喋不休 | To talk to someone incessantly and tediously, especially to scold or complain. | He earbashed me all the way home about the football game. 他一路上都在跟我唠叨那场足球比赛。

| 近义词：scold, lecture, harangue, rant | 反义词：praise, commend | 词性: 动词
