bonsai:/ˈbɒnsaɪ/ (UK) /ˈbɑːnsaɪ/ (US)| n. |盆景|A miniature tree or shrub grown in a pot, typically by dwarfing techniques.|He has a beautiful collection of bonsai trees. 他收藏了很多美丽的盆景。|Synonyms: miniature tree, dwarf tree|Antonyms: giant tree, redwood|Noun

bonsai:/ˈbɒnsaɪ/ (UK) /ˈbɑːnsaɪ/ (US)| adj. |盆景的|Relating to or characteristic of bonsai.|She attended a bonsai workshop to learn the art. 她参加了一个盆景研讨会学习这门艺术。|Synonyms: miniature, dwarfed|Antonyms: giant, oversized|Adjective
