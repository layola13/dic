jugglingly:/ˈdʒʌɡlɪŋli/
adverb
熟练地；巧妙地；像变戏法一样地
in a juggling manner; skillfully and adeptly, as if by juggling.
He jugglingly managed his multiple responsibilities. 他巧妙地处理了他的多重职责。
She jugglingly balanced work and family life. 她巧妙地平衡了工作和家庭生活。
近义词: deftly, skillfully, adroitly, nimbly
反义词: clumsily, awkwardly, ineptly
词性: adverb

词源: From "juggling" (present participle of "juggle") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  The word "juggle" ultimately comes from Old French "jogler" meaning "to entertain by performing tricks."
