coprosperity:/ˌkoʊprɑːˈspɛrəti/ /ˌkəʊprɒˈspɛrɪti/| n.|共同繁荣，共荣|A slogan used by the Japanese Empire to justify its expansionist policies in Asia, implying mutual benefit but often masking exploitation and domination.|Japan's wartime goal of a "Greater East Asia Co-Prosperity Sphere" proved to be a deceptive promise. 日本战时的“大东亚共荣圈”目标最终被证明是一个骗人的承诺。|Synonyms: collaboration, partnership, alliance|Antonyms: conflict, antagonism, opposition|noun

coprosper:/ˌkoʊprɑːˈspɛr/ /ˌkəʊprɒˈspɛr/| v.|共同繁荣，共荣|To prosper together, usually implying a mutually beneficial relationship.|The two companies aimed to coprosper through joint ventures.  两家公司旨在通过合资企业共同繁荣。|Synonyms: flourish together, thrive together, succeed together|Antonyms: decline together, fail together, struggle together|verb
