korma:/ˈkɔːrmə/ (UK) /ˈkɔːrmɑː/ (US)| n.|咖喱菜，科尔玛咖喱|A mild curry dish originating in India, usually made with yogurt, nuts, and spices, often served with meat (especially chicken or lamb) or vegetables.|例句：We had a delicious chicken korma for dinner. 我们晚饭吃了美味的鸡肉咖喱。

例句：The restaurant's lamb korma is their signature dish. 这家餐厅的羊肉咖喱是他们的招牌菜。|近义词：curry, stew|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps something like "bland dish")|词性:noun
