catilinarian:/ˌkætɪlɪˈnɛəriən/
adjective
卡提林式的；阴谋叛乱的；煽动性的
of or relating to Catiline or his conspiracy.  Characterized by conspiracy and rebellion.
Cicero delivered his famous Catilinarian orations against the conspirator. 西塞罗发表了他著名的反对阴谋者卡提林的演说。
Synonyms:  seditious, rebellious, treasonous, subversive, insurrectionary
Antonyms: loyal, patriotic, faithful, law-abiding
词性: adjective
