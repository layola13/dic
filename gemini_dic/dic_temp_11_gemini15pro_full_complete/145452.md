overcircumspect:/ˈoʊvərˈsɜːrkəmspekt/| adj.|过度谨慎的，过分小心的|Excessively cautious and careful; too concerned with avoiding potential risks or mistakes.|She was so overcircumspect in her business dealings that she missed several lucrative opportunities. 她由于在商业交易中过于谨慎小心，错失了几个赚钱的好机会。|
The lawyer's overcircumspect approach to the case ensured that no detail was overlooked, but it also prolonged the proceedings considerably.  这位律师对案件过度谨慎的处理方式确保了没有遗漏任何细节，但也大大延长了诉讼过程。|近义词：cautious, wary, prudent, discreet, meticulous|反义词：reckless, impulsive, careless, rash, impetuous|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *over* (over, excessively) + *circumspectus* (circumspect, cautious), from *circumspicere* (to look around), from *circum* (around) + *specere* (to look).
