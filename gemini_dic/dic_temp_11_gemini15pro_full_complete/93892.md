immortalist: /ɪˌmɔːrtəlɪst/
n.
不朽者；追求长生不老的人
A person who believes in or seeks immortality.
He considered himself an immortalist, dedicated to finding the secret of eternal life. 他认为自己是一个不朽者，致力于寻找永生的秘密。
近义词: longevity seeker, life extensionist
反义词: mortal
词性: 名词
