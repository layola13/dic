haecceity:/ˌhɛksiːɪti/ /ˌheɪksiːɪti/ | n. | 此性；殊性；个体性 | The property of being a unique and individual thing; thisness.  It refers to the aspects of a thing that make it *this* particular thing and not something else. | This painting has a certain haecceity that makes it stand out from the others. 这幅画具有一种特殊的此性，使其从其他画作中脱颖而出. | synonyms: thisness, individuality, quiddity | antonyms: universality, generality | noun
