hetaera:/hɪˈtaɪrə/ /hiːˈtɪərə/
n.
高级妓女；艺妓
a highly educated and skilled courtesan in ancient Greece, often accomplished in music, poetry, and conversation.
Aspasia was a renowned hetaera in ancient Athens.  阿斯帕西娅是古雅典著名的艺妓。
The hetaera entertained the men with her wit and charm.  这位艺妓以她的机智和魅力款待了男人们。
近义词:courtesan, concubine
反义词:wife, matron
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek  ἑταίρα (hetaíra, “female companion”), from ἑταῖρος (hetaîros, “companion, comrade”).
