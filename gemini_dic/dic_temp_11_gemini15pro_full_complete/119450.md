micromanage: /ˌmaɪkrəʊˈmænɪdʒ/ (US), /ˌmaɪkrəʊˈmænɪdʒ/ (UK)| v. | 微观管理；事无巨细地管理 | To control every small detail of (someone's work or activities); to manage with excessive attention to minor details. |  The new boss micromanages everything, which makes it hard for us to get any work done. 新老板事无巨细地管理一切，这让我们很难完成任何工作。

My previous manager micromanaged me constantly, checking up on every single task I completed. 我以前的经理总是对我进行微观管理，检查我完成的每一项任务。


|Synonyms: overmanage, oversee excessively, control closely|Antonyms: delegate, empower, trust|verb
