bipinnate:/baɪˈpɪneɪt/
adjective
二回羽状的
(of a compound leaf) having leaflets arranged on each side of a secondary rib branching from the main rib.  Twice pinnate.
The fern has bipinnate fronds.  这种蕨类植物有二回羽状的叶子。
bipinnatifid, doubly pinnate
pinnate
adjective
