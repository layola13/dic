inherits:/ɪnˈhɛrɪts/ /ɪnˈherɪts/| v. |继承，遗传|To receive (property, a title, etc.) from one's ancestors or predecessors by legal succession or will. To derive (a quality, characteristic, or predisposition) genetically from one's parents or ancestors.|She inherited a large fortune from her grandfather. 她从祖父那里继承了一大笔财产。

He inherited his father's musical talent. 他遗传了父亲的音乐天赋。|近义词：receive, obtain, acquire, derive|反义词:forfeit, lose|词性:动词
