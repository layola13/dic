bladderlike:/ˈblædərlaɪk/| adj.|像膀胱的；囊状的|Resembling a bladder in form or function; thin-walled and inflatable.|The plant has bladderlike leaves that trap insects.  这种植物有囊状的叶子来捕捉昆虫。|近义词：saclike, pouchlike, vesicular|反义词:solid, compact|词性:形容词

Etymology:
bladder (n.) from Old English blædre "bladder, blister, pouch," from Proto-Germanic *bladro (source also of Old Norse blaðra, Old High German blātara "bladder"), from PIE root *bhled- "to blow, swell."
-like (suffix) from Old English -lic, from Proto-Germanic *-lika (source also of Old Norse -ligr, Old Saxon -lik, Old Frisian -lik, Dutch -lijk, Old High German -lih, German -lich, Gothic -leiks), from PIE *-likos.
