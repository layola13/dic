nondissidents:/ˌnɒnˈdɪsɪdənts/ | n. |非持不同政见者|People who do not publicly disagree with the government or ruling power.  Those who conform to the established political system or ideology.|例句：The government's propaganda was aimed at reassuring the nondissidents. 政府的宣传旨在安抚那些不持不同政见的人。

例句：The nondissidents remained silent during the political upheaval. 在政治动乱期间，非持不同政见者保持沉默。|近义词：conformists, loyalists, adherents|反义词：dissidents, rebels, protestors|词性:名词
