apothegm: /ˈæpəθɛm/ | n. | 格言，箴言，警句 | A short, pithy, instructive saying. |  Example:  "Know thyself" is a famous apothegm.  “认识你自己”是一句著名的格言。

Example: He punctuated his speech with frequent apothegms. 他在演讲中不时穿插一些格言。 | 近义词: maxim, adage, proverb, saying | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *apothegma, from Greek *apophthégma "a thing spoken forth," from apophthéngesthai "to speak out," from apo- "forth" + phthéngesthai "to utter."
