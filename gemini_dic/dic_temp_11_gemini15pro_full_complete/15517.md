bafflingly:/ˈbæflɪŋli/ | adv. | 令人困惑地，难以理解地 | in a manner that is baffling; in a way that is difficult to understand |  The magician bafflingly made the rabbit disappear.  魔术师令人困惑地把兔子变没了。 |  confusingly, perplexingly, mystifyingly | clearly, understandably | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From baffle + -ly. Baffle (v.) comes from Middle English baffelen "to disgrace, insult," of uncertain origin. The modern sense of "to perplex, frustrate" developed in the late 16th century.
