nimbly:/ˈnɪmbli/| adv.|敏捷地，灵活地|Moving quickly and lightly.|The gymnast nimbly performed a backflip. 体操运动员敏捷地做了一个后空翻。|近义词：agilely, deftly, quickly, swiftly|反义词：clumsily, awkwardly, slowly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From Middle English *nimbely, from nimble + -ly.  Nimble itself comes from Old English *nimele, meaning "quick to grasp," related to the verb niman "to take."
