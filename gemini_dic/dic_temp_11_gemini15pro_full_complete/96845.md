inexact:/ˌɪnɪɡˈzækt/
adjective
不精确的，不准确的
not quite accurate or correct
例句：The measurements were inexact, leading to errors in the calculations. 测量结果不精确，导致计算出错。
例句：His memory of the event was inexact. 他对这件事的记忆不准确。
近义词：inaccurate, imprecise, approximate
反义词：exact, precise, accurate
词性: adjective

词源:
From in- "not" + exact.  The "in-" prefix comes from Latin, meaning "not, opposite of, without".  "Exact" also comes from Latin, from *exactus, past participle of exigere "to drive out, demand, enforce, weigh, measure," from ex- "out" + agere "to drive, lead, act, do."
