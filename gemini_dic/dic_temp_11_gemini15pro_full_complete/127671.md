nefariously:/nɪˈfɛəriəsli/ | adv. | 邪恶地，穷凶极恶地 | in a wicked or criminal manner. |  He nefariously plotted to overthrow the government. 他阴险地密谋推翻政府。| wickedly, evilly, villainously | righteously, virtuously | adverb

Etymology->
From Latin *nefarius* ("wicked, impious, abominable"), from *nefas* ("wrong, sin, crime against divine law") + *-arius*.
