misfit:/ˈmɪsfɪt/| n. adj.|不适应环境的人，不称职的人；不合适的|A person who is not suited to a particular job or situation, or who is not accepted by a particular group of people.  Someone who is different and doesn't fit in with other people.|He was a social misfit in high school. 他在高中时是一个不合群的人。|近义词：nonconformist, maverick, outsider, eccentric|反义词: conformist, insider|词性:名词，形容词

misfit:/ˈmɪsfɪt/| adj.|不合适的，不配套的|Something that is not suitable for a particular purpose or situation.|These shoes are a misfit; they're too big.  这双鞋不合脚，太大了。|近义词：unsuitable, inappropriate, ill-fitting|反义词: suitable, appropriate, fitting|词性:形容词
