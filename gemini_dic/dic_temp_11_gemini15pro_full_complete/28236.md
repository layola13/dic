capsulary:/ˈkæpsjʊləri/ | adj. | 胶囊状的；摘要的 |  Relating to or resembling a capsule; containing the essential features of something concisely. | The doctor prescribed capsulary medication. 医生开了胶囊药物。| The report provided a capsulary overview of the project.  这份报告提供了该项目的概要概述。 |  concise, summarized, brief, compact | lengthy, detailed, extensive | adjective

capsular:/ˈkæpsjʊlər/ | adj. | 胶囊的；包膜的 | Of, relating to, or resembling a capsule, especially a biological capsule. | The capsular ligament surrounds the joint.  关节囊韧带环绕关节。|  The bacteria had a thick capsular layer. 这种细菌有一层厚厚的荚膜层。| enveloping, enclosing, sheathing | exposed, bare | adjective


Etymology:  From Late Latin *capsularis*, from Latin capsula "small box," diminutive of capsa "box."
