menhir: /ˈmenhɪr/ | n. | 巨石柱，立石 | A large, upright, pre-historic stone, typically single and roughly shaped, but sometimes part of a group or forming an alignment.  A single upright megalith. |  Example: The archaeologists discovered a circle of menhirs on the moor.  考古学家在荒野上发现了一圈巨石柱。

Example: The menhir stood silhouetted against the setting sun. 巨石柱在落日的映衬下轮廓分明。| 近义词: megalith, standing stone | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
