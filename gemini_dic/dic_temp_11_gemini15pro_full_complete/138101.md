normless:/ˈnɔːmlɪs/
adjective
无规范的；无法无天的；没有标准的
Lacking norms or standards of behavior; unregulated or uncontrolled.
The movie depicted a normless society where violence and corruption were rampant.  这部电影描绘了一个暴力和腐败猖獗的无法无天的社会。
The collapse of the government led to a normless state, where crime was rampant. 政府的垮台导致了一个无法无天的状态，犯罪猖獗。
synonyms: lawless, unruly, chaotic, anarchic, unregulated
antonyms: regulated, orderly, lawful, controlled, principled
词性: 形容词
