conquistadors:/kɒŋˈkɪstədɔːz/ (UK), /kɑːŋˈkɪstədɔːrz/ (US)| n. |征服者；侵略者|A conqueror, especially one of the Spanish conquerors of Mexico and Peru in the 16th century.|例句：The conquistadors plundered the Inca empire for gold.  征服者掠夺了印加帝国的黄金。
