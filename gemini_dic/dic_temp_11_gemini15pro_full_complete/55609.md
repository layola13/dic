disvalue:/dɪsˈvæljuː/
|v.|贬低；轻视；降低…的价值|To consider or treat something as having little or no value.|He disvalued her contributions to the project.|近义词：devalue, depreciate, belittle, underestimate|反义词:value, appreciate, overestimate|verb

disvalue:/dɪsˈvæljuː/
|n.|贬值；低估|The state of being considered unimportant or worthless.|The disvalue placed on manual labor is unfair.|近义词：devaluation, depreciation|反义词:value, appreciation|noun
