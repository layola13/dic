ischury:/ˈɪskjʊri/| n.|排尿困难，尿潴留|Inability to urinate; retention of urine.|The patient presented with ischury and abdominal distension. 病人表现为排尿困难和腹部胀气。|The ischury was caused by an enlarged prostate. 排尿困难是由前列腺肥大引起的。|近义词：anuria, urinary retention|反义词: diuresis, polyuria|词性:noun

词源: From Greek  *ἰσχουρία* (iskhouria), from *ἴσχω* (iskhō, “I hold back, restrain”) + *οὖρον* (ouron, “urine”).
