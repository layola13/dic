contraposition:/ˌkɑːntrəpəˈzɪʃən/
n.
换位,换质位,逻辑学对置
The inference by contraposition is logically valid.
通过换位推理在逻辑上是有效的。
The contraposition of "If A, then B" is "If not B, then not A".
“如果A，那么B”的换位是“如果不是B，那么不是A”。
近义词: transposition, conversion
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps "obversion")
词性: noun
