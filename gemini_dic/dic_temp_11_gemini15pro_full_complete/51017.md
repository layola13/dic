deskills:/diːˈskɪlz/
verb
使…失去技能；降低…的技能
to reduce the level of skill required to do a job, especially by introducing technology
Automation has deskilled many factory jobs. 自动化使许多工厂工作失去了技能。
近义词: downgrade, dequalify
反义词: upskill, train
词性:v.
