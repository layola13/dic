drab-bed:/dræbˈbɛd/
adjective
单调乏味的；穿着邋遢的
Dull and monotonous in appearance; dressed in drab clothing.
The refugees huddled together in their drab-bed clothes.  难民们穿着邋遢的衣服挤在一起。
The room was drab-bed and depressing. 房间单调乏味，令人沮丧。
近义词: dingy, dull, dreary, monochrome, colorless
反义词: bright, colorful, vibrant, cheerful
词性: adjective

drab:/dræb/
adjective
单调乏味的；土褐色的
Dull and monotonous; of a dull light brown color.
She wore a drab brown coat. 她穿着一件土褐色的单调外套。
The landscape was drab and uninteresting.  景色单调乏味，毫无趣味。
近义词: dull, dreary, monotonous, colorless, lackluster
反义词: bright, vibrant, colorful, exciting, stimulating
词性: adjective


词源:
From Middle English *drabbe, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old Norse drabba "dregs," or to Middle Low German drab "mud, mire." The original sense was "of a dull light brown color," and the meaning "monotonous, dull" developed later.
