framed:/freɪmd/
v.|陷害，诬陷；给…装框|Past participle of frame.  The picture was framed in gold. 这幅画镶嵌在金框里。|近义词:incriminated, set up, encompassed, bordered|反义词:exonerated, cleared|词性:动词(过去分词)

framed:/freɪmd/
adj.|有构架的；有框的；被陷害的|Having a frame or frames.  A framed picture. 一幅带框的画。|近义词:bordered, enclosed, outlined, incriminated|反义词:frameless, open|词性:形容词
