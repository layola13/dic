maxer:/ˈmæksər/| n. |最大化者，追求极致者|Someone who strives to maximize something, often in the context of gaming or other hobbies, by achieving the highest possible stats, levels, or results.  They often dedicate significant time and effort to optimize their performance and reach the absolute limit of what is possible. |He's a real maxer, always trying to get the best gear and highest stats in the game.|近义词: optimizer, min-maxer, powergamer|反义词: casual (player), newbie|Noun
