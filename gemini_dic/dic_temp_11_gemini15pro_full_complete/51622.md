development:/dɪˈvɛləpmənt/ | n. | 发展，开发，发育，进展 |The process of developing or being developed. |例句：The development of new technologies is crucial for progress. 新技术的开发对进步至关重要。

|例句：Child development is a fascinating area of study. 儿童发展是一个引人入胜的研究领域。|近义词：growth, progress, evolution, advancement |反义词：decline, stagnation, regression |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *développement*, from *développer* "to unwrap, unfold," from *des-* "out" + *voloper* "to wrap."
