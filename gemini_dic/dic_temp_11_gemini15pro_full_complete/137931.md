nonvolubly:/ˌnɒnˈvɒljʊbli/ | adv. | 不健谈地，沉默寡言地 | In a manner that is not talkative or fluent.  Characterized by reticence or few words. | He answered nonvolubly, offering only a curt nod. 他沉默寡言地回答，只简短地点了点头。|  She listened nonvolubly, absorbing every word. 她静静地听着，把每个字都记在心里。 | 近义词: taciturnly, quietly, silently, reservedly | 反义词: volubly, loquaciously, talkatively | 词性: 副词

词源:
From non- + voluble + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "voluble" comes from the Latin "volubilis," meaning "easily turning" or "fluent."  The suffix "-ly" transforms the adjective "voluble" into the adverb "nonvolubly."
