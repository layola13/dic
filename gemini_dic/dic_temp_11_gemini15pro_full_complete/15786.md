PHRASE->

baling wire: 打包线/铁丝  Farmers use baling wire to secure the bales of hay. 农民用打包线/铁丝来固定干草包。
baling machine: 打包机  The baling machine makes the process much faster. 打包机使这个过程更快。
baling twine: 打包绳  Baling twine is another material used to tie bales. 打包绳是另一种用来捆扎的材料。
baling press: 打包压机 The baling press compresses the material into compact bales. 打包压机将材料压缩成紧凑的包。
baling needle: 打包针  A baling needle is used to thread twine through bales. 打包针用于将绳子穿过草捆。
