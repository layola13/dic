clade:/kleɪd/| n.|进化枝，支系|A group of organisms believed to have evolved from a common ancestor, according to the principles of cladistics.|例句：Birds, dinosaurs, and crocodiles form a clade called Archosauria. 鸟类、恐龙和鳄鱼构成了一个名为祖龙的进化枝。

例句：The clade of mammals includes all animals that have hair and mammary glands. 哺乳动物的进化枝包括所有有毛发和乳腺的动物。|近义词：monophyletic group, taxon|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially polyphyletic group or paraphyletic group depending on context) |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek κλάδος (kládos, “branch”).
