dexter:/ˈdɛkstər/| adj.|灵巧的，熟练的，惯用右手的|On the right hand side; skillful and adroit.  Relating to or located on the right side of the body. |He's very dexter with a needle and thread. 他针线活很灵巧。|近义词：skillful, adroit, adept, proficient, right-handed|反义词: clumsy, awkward, left-handed, gauche|词性:形容词

词源:
From Latin *dexter, meaning "right, favorable, skillful."  The Latin word is related to the Greek δεξιός (dexios), also meaning "right." The connection to "skillful" likely arose because the right hand is traditionally considered the dominant hand for most people.
