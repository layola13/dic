ghawazi:/ɣɑːˈwɑːziː/ | n. | Ghawazee舞者（埃及的流浪舞蹈演员）|  A class of primarily female itinerant dancers and entertainers in Egypt. They were known for their alluring dances and music, and often faced social stigma and marginalization. | Example: The ghawazi dancers captivated the audience with their vibrant performance.  埃及加瓦济舞者以她们充满活力的表演吸引了观众。 | Example:  The portrayal of ghawazi in 19th-century paintings often romanticized their lifestyle. 19世纪绘画中对加瓦济舞者的描绘通常将其生活方式浪漫化了。| 近义词:  dancers, entertainers | 反义词:  None (缺乏明确的反义词) | 词性: noun
