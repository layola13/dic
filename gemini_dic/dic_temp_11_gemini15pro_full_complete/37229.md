comfortabilities:/ˈkʌmfərtəˈbɪlɪtiz/
n.
舒适设施；安逸；舒适
material comforts that make life easier and more pleasant.
The hotel offers all modern comforts and amenities.  这家酒店提供所有现代化的舒适设施和便利设施。
amenities, conveniences, luxuries
discomforts, inconveniences, hardships
noun
