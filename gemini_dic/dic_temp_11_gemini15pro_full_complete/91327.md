hydrophobic:/ˌhaɪdrəˈfəʊbɪk/ (US) /ˌhaɪdrəˈfoʊbɪk/| adj.|疏水的，憎水的|Tending to repel or fail to mix with water.|Oil is hydrophobic and does not mix with water. 油是疏水的，不会与水混合。|The lotus leaf's hydrophobic surface causes water to bead up and roll off. 荷叶的疏水表面使水珠凝结并滚落。|近义词:water-repellent, nonpolar|反义词:hydrophilic, water-loving|词性:adjective
词源:  From Greek *hydro- ‘water’ + -phobic (from Greek phobos ‘fear’).
