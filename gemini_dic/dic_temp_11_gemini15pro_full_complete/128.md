abandonee:/əˌbændəˈniː/
noun
被遗弃者; 受托人放弃权利者
a person who has been abandoned or deserted.
The abandonee was left with nothing but the clothes on her back.  这位被遗弃者除了身上的衣服外一无所有。
The insurance company became the abandonee of the wrecked ship.  保险公司成为了这艘失事船只的受托放弃者。
synonyms: deserted person, forsaken individual, derelict
antonyms: guardian, protector
