diffidentnesses:/ˈdɪfɪdən(t)nɪsɪz/
n.
缺乏自信；胆怯；羞怯
lack of self-confidence; shyness; timidity
例句：His diffidentnesses prevented him from speaking up in class. 他的胆怯使他不敢在课堂上发言。
例句：She overcame her diffidentnesses and gave a confident presentation. 她克服了胆怯，做了一个自信的演讲。
近义词：shyness, timidity, hesitancy, insecurity
反义词：confidence, boldness, assertiveness
词性：名词

词源：From diffident + -ness.  Diffident comes from the Latin diffīdens, diffīdent-  present participle of diffīdere to distrust, from dis- + fīdere to trust.
