brae:/breɪ/
noun
山坡，斜坡
A slope or hillside, especially a steep bank or hillside.
The sheep grazed peacefully on the brae. 羊群在山坡上安静地吃草。
近义词:slope, hillside, incline, bank
反义词:valley, plain, flat
