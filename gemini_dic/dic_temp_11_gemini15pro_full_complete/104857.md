keel:/kiːl/| n. v. |龙骨；船底；倾覆；晕倒|The ship's keel was damaged in the storm. 这艘船的龙骨在风暴中受损了。|近义词：bottom, hull |反义词:  (No direct antonym for the physical object "keel."  "Capsize" could be considered an antonym for the verb meaning "to overturn.") | noun

keel:/kiːl/| v. |倾覆；晕倒；冷却|The boat keeled over in the strong wind.  这艘船在强风中倾覆了。|近义词：capsize, faint, collapse|反义词:right, recover| verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *cēol (attested only in compounds like *cēolþæt "ship's bottom"), from Proto-Germanic *kēlaz (“ship's bottom, keel”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰélh₁- (“ship's bottom”). Cognate with Old Norse kjǫlr (“keel”), Dutch kiel (“keel”), German Kiel (“keel”), Latin calamus (“reed”).
