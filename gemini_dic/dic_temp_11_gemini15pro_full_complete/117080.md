meadowlarks:/ˈmedəʊlɑːk/ (UK) /ˈmedolɑːrk/ (US)| n. |云雀；草地鹨|A North American songbird of grasslands and meadows, typically with a yellow breast and a black V-shaped mark on the throat.  They are known for their beautiful, flute-like songs.| The meadowlarks filled the air with their cheerful melodies. 草地鹨用它们欢快的旋律充满了天空。

The western meadowlark's song is a complex series of whistles and gurgles. 西部草地鹨的歌声是一系列复杂的哨音和咯咯声。|近义词：lark, songbird|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific bird species)|词性:noun
