nonevolving:/ˌnɒnɪˈvɒlvɪŋ/ | adj. | 非进化性的，不进化的 | Not evolving; not undergoing development or change. | The coelacanth, a "living fossil," is often described as a nonevolving species.  腔棘鱼，一种“活化石”，常被描述为一种不进化的物种。|  例句：Some creationists believe in nonevolving species. 一些神创论者相信物种是不进化的。 | 近义词：static, unchanging, fixed, stagnant | 反义词：evolving, developing, changing, dynamic | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (not) + "evolving" (present participle of "evolve").  "Evolve" comes from the Latin "evolvere" meaning "to unroll, unfold, develop".
