declassifiable:/diːˈklæsɪfaɪəbl/
adjective
可解密的
able to be declassified (made no longer secret or confidential).
The documents were declassifiable after fifty years. 五十年后这些文件可以解密。
近义词:releasable, publishable
反义词:classified, confidential
词性:adjective

declassify:/diːˈklæsɪfaɪ/
verb
解密
to officially declare (information or documents) no longer secret.
The government declassified the wartime files. 政府解密了战时文件。
近义词:release, publish
反义词:classify, conceal
词性:verb

classify:/ˈklæsɪfaɪ/
verb
分类
arrange (a group of people or things) in classes or categories according to shared characteristics.
Biologists classify animals into different groups. 生物学家将动物分成不同的类别。
近义词:categorize, sort
反义词:randomize, disorganize
词性:verb
