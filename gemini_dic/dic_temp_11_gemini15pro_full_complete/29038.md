cartoons: /kɑːrˈtuːnz/ | n. |卡通片，动画片|A film or television programme made using characters and images that are drawn rather than real, especially one for children.|例句：My children love watching cartoons on Saturday mornings. 我的孩子们喜欢在星期六早上看动画片。

例句：The newspaper publishes political cartoons every day. 这家报纸每天都刊登政治漫画。


近义词：animation, animated films|反义词: documentaries, live-action films |词性: noun
