ordered:/ˈɔːrdərd/| adj.|有序的，整齐的，排列好的| Arranged or organized in a particular way; commissioned; bespoke. |例句：The books were ordered alphabetically by author. 这些书按作者姓名字母顺序排列。|例句：She ordered a new dress for the wedding. 她为婚礼订购了一件新连衣裙。|近义词：arranged, organized, systematic, neat, tidy, commissioned, bespoke|反义词：disordered, chaotic, messy, random|词性:形容词

ordered:/ˈɔːdərd/| v. (past tense and past participle of order)|命令，订购，点餐| To give an authoritative command to do something; to request (something) to be made, supplied, or served.|例句：The doctor ordered a complete rest. 医生嘱咐要完全休息。|例句：I ordered a pizza for dinner. 我点了一个披萨作为晚餐。|近义词：command, instruct, direct, request, purchase|反义词：countermand, cancel|词性:动词
