cyclostome: /ˈsaɪkləˌstoʊm/ (US) /ˈsaɪkləstəʊm/ (UK)| n. |圆口纲动物|A primitive jawless vertebrate animal of the class Cyclostomata, such as a lamprey or hagfish.|Lampreys and hagfish are cyclostomes. 七鳃鳗和盲鳗是圆口纲动物。|近义词:jawless fish|反义词:gnathostome (有颌类动物)|词性:noun

词源: From Greek *kúklos* (circle) + *stóma* (mouth).
