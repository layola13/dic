hurraying:/həˈreɪɪŋ/
| v.|欢呼，喝彩|The crowd was hurraying as the team scored the winning goal. |人群在球队攻入制胜球时欢呼雀跃。|近义词：cheering, applauding, acclaiming|反义词: booing, jeering|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From hurrah + -ing.  Hurrah itself is an alteration of huzzah, of uncertain origin, perhaps imitative.

PHRASE->
Not commonly used in phrases.  Hurraying is generally used on its own. It is primarily the action of saying "hurray!" repeatedly. Therefore, phrases using the -ing form are less common.
