cryptographies:/krɪpˈtɒɡrəfiz/
n.
密码学; 密码术
The study and practice of techniques for secure communication in the presence of third parties called adversaries.  This involves creating written or generated codes that allow information to be kept secret.
Modern cryptographies is heavily based on mathematical theory and computer science practice. 现代密码学很大程度上基于数学理论和计算机科学实践。
The development of quantum computers poses a significant threat to current cryptographies. 量子计算机的发展对当前的密码学构成了重大威胁。
近义词: cryptography, encryption, coding
反义词:  plain text, decipherment
词性: noun (plural)


cryptography:/krɪpˈtɒɡrəfi/
n.
密码学; 密码术
The study and practice of techniques for secure communication in the presence of third parties called adversaries. This involves creating written or generated codes that allow information to be kept secret.
The Enigma machine was a complex device used in cryptography during World War II.  恩尼格玛机是二战期间用于密码学的一种复杂设备。
Public-key cryptography is fundamental to the security of online transactions. 公钥密码学是网络交易安全的基础。
近义词: cryptographies, encryption, coding
反义词: plain text, decipherment
词性: noun (singular)
