dutied:/ˈdjuːtid/ /ˈduːtid/| adj.|尽职的，负有责任的|Assigned a task or responsibility; obligated.  Having a duty or obligation. |例句：The dutied officer remained at his post. 尽职的官员坚守岗位。|例句：She felt dutied to report the incident. 她觉得有责任报告这起事件。|近义词：obligated, responsible, bound, required|反义词：unobligated, free, exempt|词性:形容词

词源: From "duty" + "-ed."  "Duty" comes from Old French "deu," meaning "due," ultimately from Latin "debitum," meaning "something owed."
