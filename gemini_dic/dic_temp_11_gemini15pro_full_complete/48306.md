decrepitation:/dɪˌkrɛpɪˈteɪʃən/
n.|爆裂；劈啪声； decrepit 的名词形式|The decrepitation of the old house was unsettling. 老房子的爆裂声令人不安。|近义词：crackling, popping, splitting|反义词：silence, quiet|词性: 名词
Etymology: From Late Latin *decrepitātiō, from Latin decrepitāre (“to crackle”), frequentative of crepitāre (“to rattle, crackle”).
