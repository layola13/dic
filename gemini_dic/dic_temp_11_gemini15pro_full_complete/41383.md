cormophytes:/ˈkɔːrməˌfaɪts/| n. |维管植物；茎叶植物|Plants that possess a differentiated plant body, with roots, stems, and leaves; vascular plants.|Cormophytes are the dominant form of plant life on land. 维管植物是陆地上主要的植物生命形式。| Ferns and flowering plants are cormophytes. 蕨类植物和开花植物都是维管植物。|近义词：vascular plants, tracheophytes|反义词：thallophytes (叶状体植物), bryophytes (苔藓植物)|词性：noun
