headily:/ˈhɛdəli/| adv.|轻率地，鲁莽地；令人兴奋地；强烈地|In a rash or reckless manner; excitedly; intensely. |He rushed headily into the burning building. 他轻率地冲进了燃烧的建筑。|Synonyms: rashly, recklessly, impetuously, precipitately, hastily|Antonyms: cautiously, carefully, prudently, deliberately|adverb

headily:/ˈhɛdəli/| adv.|令人陶醉地；浓烈地|In an intoxicating way; strongly, powerfully (of a scent or flavour). |The wine smelled headily of blackberries. 这酒散发出浓郁的黑莓香味。|Synonyms: intoxicatingly, powerfully, strongly, intensely|Antonyms: mildly, faintly, subtly|adverb
