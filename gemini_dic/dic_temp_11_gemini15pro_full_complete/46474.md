daimio:/ˈdaɪmioʊ/| n.|大名, 藩主|A feudal lord in Japan, especially one of the more powerful territorial lords ruling before the Meiji Restoration.|The daimio controlled vast estates and commanded armies of samurai.  大名控制着广阔的土地，并指挥着武士军队。|The shogun granted land to the daimio. 将军把土地赐给了大名。|近义词：feudal lord, magnate|反义词: peasant, commoner|noun
