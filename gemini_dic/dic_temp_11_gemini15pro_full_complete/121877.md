misruler:/mɪsˈruːlər/| n. |暴君；管理不善的统治者|A person who governs badly or unjustly.  A tyrant or an incompetent leader.|例句：The misruler was overthrown by the people. 人民推翻了暴君。

例句：The misruler's policies led to widespread famine. 暴君的政策导致了大范围的饥荒。|近义词：tyrant, dictator, despot, autocrat|反义词：benevolent ruler, just ruler|词性: noun
