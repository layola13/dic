overnurses:/ˌoʊvərˈnɜːrs/ | n. |过度护理；过分护理；护理过度|Excessive or unnecessary nursing care, often provided with good intentions but potentially hindering patient independence and recovery.  It can manifest in doing things for patients that they are capable of doing themselves. |The patient's family was accused of overnursing, preventing him from regaining his autonomy. 病人的家人被指责过度护理，阻止他恢复自主能力。

The overnursing led to muscle atrophy and a decline in the patient's overall well-being. 过度护理导致病人肌肉萎缩和整体健康状况下降。|近义词: overcare,  over-nursing |反义词: neglect, understaffing |词性: noun
