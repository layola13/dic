nonreprehensibly:/ˌnɒnˌrɛprɪˈhɛnsɪbli/
adverb
无可非议地，无懈可击地
in a way that is not reprehensible; blamelessly.
His conduct throughout the entire affair was nonreprehensibly noble. 他在整件事情中的行为无可非议地高尚。
近义词:blamelessly, impeccably, faultlessly, irreproachably
反义词:reprehensibly, culpably, blameworthy
