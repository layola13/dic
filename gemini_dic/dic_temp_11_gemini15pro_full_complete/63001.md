词源：enormous + -ness
enormous (adj.)  "huge, vast," 1530s, from Latin enormis "irregular, unusual, exceeding proper measure," from e- "out of" (see ex-) + norma "rule, pattern" (see norm).  The -ous is a later addition. Related: Enormously; enormousness.
