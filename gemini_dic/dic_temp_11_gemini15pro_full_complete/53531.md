disaventure:/dɪsədˈvɛntʃər/
noun
不幸；灾难
An unfortunate mishap; a misadventure.
例句：He met with a series of disaventures on his journey. 他在旅途中遇到了一系列的不幸。

例句：The expedition was plagued by disaventures from the start. 探险队从一开始就灾难不断。

近义词：misfortune, mishap, adversity, calamity
反义词：fortune, luck, success
词性：n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *desaventure, from des- "away, from" + aventure "adventure".  The prefix des- implies a negative or unfavorable sense, turning "adventure" into "misadventure".
