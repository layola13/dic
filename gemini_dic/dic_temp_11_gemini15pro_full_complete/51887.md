一般现在时 (Simple Present):
The dog barks every morning.  狗每天早上都叫。
The sun rises in the east. 太阳从东方升起。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
The baby is crying. 婴儿在哭。
The leaves are falling from the trees. 树叶正在从树上落下。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
The snow has melted. 雪已经融化了。
I have read the book. 我已经读过这本书了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
The rain has been falling all day. 雨下了一整天。
They have been playing games for hours. 他们玩游戏已经好几个小时了。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
The phone rang. 电话响了。
The train arrived late. 火车晚点了。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
The wind was blowing hard. 风刮得很厉害。
I was watching TV when the power went out. 停电的时候我在看电视。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
The movie had already started when we arrived. 我们到达时电影已经开始了。
She had finished her homework before dinner. 她晚饭前就完成了作业。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
They had been waiting for an hour before the bus came. 他们在公共汽车来之前已经等了一个小时。
He had been working on the project for months. 他已经在这个项目上工作了好几个月了。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
The concert will start at 8 pm. 音乐会晚上8点开始。
I will go to the store tomorrow. 我明天去商店。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
This time next week, I will be lying on the beach. 下周的这个时候，我将躺在沙滩上。
They will be discussing the issue at the meeting. 他们将在会议上讨论这个问题。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next year, I will have finished my degree. 到明年，我将完成我的学位。
She will have written the report by Friday. 到周五，她将完成报告。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next month, I will have been living here for two years. 到下个月，我将在这里住了两年。
They will have been traveling for six months by the time they return. 到他们回来的时候，他们将已经旅行了六个月。

注意事项:

词源：源自古英语 se, seo, þæt，分别对应阳性、阴性、中性的定冠词。随着时间的推移，这些形式融合成了现代英语的 "the"。
