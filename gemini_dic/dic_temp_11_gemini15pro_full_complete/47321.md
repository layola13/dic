de-objectification:/diːˌɒbdʒɛktɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 去物化，非客体化 | The process of ceasing to treat someone as an object and instead recognizing their full humanity and subjectivity. |  Example 1: The feminist movement has long fought for the de-objectification of women in media. 女权运动长期以来一直在争取媒体对女性的去物化。

Example 2:  De-objectification of marginalized groups is crucial for achieving social justice.  对边缘群体的去物化对于实现社会正义至关重要。 |  Near Synonyms: humanization, subjectification, empowerment | Antonyms: objectification, commodification, depersonalization | Noun
