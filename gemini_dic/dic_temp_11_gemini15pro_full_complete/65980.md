exalt:/ɪɡˈzɔːlt/ /ɪɡˈzɑːlt/| vt.| 高举；提升；赞扬；使兴奋 |To raise in rank, power, or character; to elevate by praise or in estimation.|The king exalted his loyal knight to a higher position. 国王将忠诚的骑士提升到更高的职位。|近义词：praise, elevate, glorify, laud, extol|反义词：debase, degrade, humble, lower|verb

exalt:/ɪɡˈzɔːlt/ /ɪɡˈzɑːlt/| adj.(obsolete, rare) |高贵的；高尚的|Elevated in rank, character, or quality.|He possessed an exalt spirit, always striving for the best.  他拥有一种高尚的精神，总是力求做到最好。（这个形容词用法已过时，很少使用）|近义词：noble, lofty, elevated|反义词：base, low, ignoble|adjective
