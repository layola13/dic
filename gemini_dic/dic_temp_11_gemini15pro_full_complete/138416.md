noumenon:/ˈnuːmɪnɒn/ (UK), /ˈnuːməˌnɑːn/ (US)| n.| 本体；物自体 |A thing as it is in itself, as distinct from a phenomenon.  An object of purely intellectual intuition, not knowable through the senses. |Kant's theory is concerned with the distinction between the phenomenon and the noumenon.  康德的理论关注的是现象和本体之间的区别。|近义词:thing-in-itself, absolute|反义词:phenomenon|词性:noun

noumena:/ˈnuːmɪnə/ (UK), /ˈnuːmənə/ (US)| n.| 本体；物自体（noumenon的复数形式）|Plural of noumenon. Things as they are in themselves, as distinct from phenomena. |He explored the relationship between noumena and phenomena in his philosophical writings. 他在他的哲学著作中探讨了本体和现象之间的关系。|近义词:things-in-themselves, absolutes|反义词:phenomena|词性:noun
