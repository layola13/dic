chondrigenous:/ˌkɒndrɪˈdʒɛnəs/
adjective
软骨生成的，软骨来源的
Formed or derived from cartilage.
Example: Chondrigenous tumors are tumors that originate from cartilage cells.  软骨源性肿瘤是源自软骨细胞的肿瘤。

Example: The chondrigenous layer gives rise to the skeletal system. 软骨生成层形成了骨骼系统。

synonym: cartilaginous
antonym: non-cartilaginous, non-chondrogenic
词性: adjective
