cerebricity: /ˌsɛrɪˈbrɪsɪti/
noun
机智，睿智
Mental acuity or quickness of wit.
Example: His cerebricity was evident in his witty remarks and sharp observations. 他的机智体现在他妙语连珠的评论和敏锐的观察中。

Example: The professor's lectures were known for their cerebricity and challenging concepts.  这位教授的讲座以其睿智和富有挑战性的概念而闻名。

Synonym: intelligence, wit, acumen, sharpness, brilliance
Antonym: stupidity, dullness, obtuseness

Etymology: From Latin *cerebricitas*, from cerebrum ("brain").
