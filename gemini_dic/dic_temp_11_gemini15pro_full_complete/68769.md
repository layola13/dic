farro:/ˈfæroʊ/ (US), /ˈfærəʊ/ (UK)| n. | 法罗（一种小麦）| An ancient grain, a type of hulled wheat, similar to spelt, with a nutty flavor and chewy texture. |  I made a salad with farro, roasted vegetables, and feta cheese.  我做了一份沙拉，里面有法罗小麦、烤蔬菜和羊乳酪。

| Synonym: spelt, emmer, wheat berries | Antonym: (No direct antonym for a type of grain) | noun
