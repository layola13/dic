musard:/ˈmʌsərd/ | n. | 闲逛；游荡；懒散的人 | A leisurely stroll or promenade; a person who strolls. | He spent the afternoon in musard, wandering aimlessly through the park. 他下午都在公园里闲逛，漫无目的地游荡。| Synonyms: saunter, stroll, amble, loiter | Antonyms: hasten, hurry, rush | noun

musard:/ˈmʌsərd/ | v. | 闲逛；游荡 | To stroll or saunter leisurely. |  They musarded along the riverbank, enjoying the cool breeze. 他们在河边闲逛，享受着凉爽的微风。| Synonyms: saunter, stroll, amble, loiter | Antonyms: hasten, hurry, rush | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *muser* "to muse, dream," from Old French *muser* "to gape, stare, loiter," of uncertain origin.
