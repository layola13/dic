debunks:/diːˈbʌŋks/| v. | 揭穿；揭露；驳斥；证明…是假的 | exposes the falseness of (a myth, idea, or belief). | The scientist debunks the myth that eating carrots improves eyesight.  这位科学家揭穿了吃胡萝卜可以改善视力的说法。 | synonyms: exposes, disproves, refutes, discredits | antonyms: confirms, validates, substantiates, proves | verb

debunking:/diːˈbʌŋkɪŋ/| n. | 揭穿；揭露；驳斥 | the act of exposing the falseness of something | The debunking of the conspiracy theory was widely reported. 对阴谋论的揭穿被广泛报道。 | synonyms: exposure, refutation, disproof | antonyms: confirmation, validation, substantiation | noun
