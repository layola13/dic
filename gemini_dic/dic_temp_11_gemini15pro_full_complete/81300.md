granule:/ˈɡrænjʊl/ (US), /ˈɡrænjuːl/ (UK)| n. |颗粒，微粒|A small, compact particle of a substance.|The dry fertilizer comes in granules. 这种干肥料是颗粒状的。|Synonyms: grain, particle, speck, bit|Antonyms: lump, mass, bulk|noun

granule:/ˈɡrænjʊl/ (US), /ˈɡrænjuːl/ (UK)| n. |(天文学)米粒组织|A small granular marking on the surface of the sun.|Solar granules are caused by convection currents of plasma within the Sun's convective zone. 太阳米粒组织是由太阳对流层内等离子体的对流引起的。|Synonyms: grain|Antonyms: N/A|noun
