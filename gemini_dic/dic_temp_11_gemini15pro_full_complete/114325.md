maisonettes:/ˌmeɪzəˈnets/ | n. | 小型公寓 | A small apartment, typically one of several in a larger building and often on two storeys. |  Example 1:  They live in a maisonette near the park. 他们住在公园附近的一套小型公寓里。

Example 2: The maisonette has its own private entrance. 这套小型公寓有自己的私人入口。 | Synonym: apartment, flat, duplex | Antonym: house, mansion | noun
