coordain:/koʊˈɔːrdɪn/ /kəʊˈɔːdɪn/ | v. | 协调；配合 | to organize different things or people so that they work together effectively |  The departments need to coordain their efforts. 各个部门需要协调他们的工作。 |  The dancers coordained their movements perfectly. 舞者们的动作配合得天衣无缝。 | 近义词：harmonize, synchronize, integrate | 反义词: conflict, clash | 词性: 动词

词源: From Late Latin *coordinare, from Latin co- + ordinare "to arrange, set in order."
