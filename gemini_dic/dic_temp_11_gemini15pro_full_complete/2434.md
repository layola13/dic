adjudicates:/əˈdʒuːdɪkeɪts/| v. |裁决，判决，宣判|To formally judge and settle a dispute or contest; to act as judge in a matter.|The judge adjudicates the case. 法官判决了这个案件。|近义词：judge, decide, determine, arbitrate, rule, settle|反义词：defer, postpone, delay|词性:动词
词源: From Late Latin *adjudicare, from Latin ad- 'to' + judicare 'to judge'.
