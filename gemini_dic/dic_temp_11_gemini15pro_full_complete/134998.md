nonnebulously:/nɒnˈnɛbjʊləsli/
adjective
清晰地，不含糊地
clearly and definitely, not vaguely or unclearly
The instructions were written nonnebulously, leaving no room for misinterpretation. 指示写得很清楚，没有误解的余地。
The contract terms were nonnebulously defined, ensuring all parties understood their obligations.  合同条款定义明确，确保所有各方都理解他们的义务。
synonyms: clearly, distinctly, explicitly, precisely, unequivocally
antonyms: vaguely, nebulously, ambiguously, obscurely, indefinitely
词性: adjective
