origami:/ˌɔːrɪˈɡɑːmi/ /ˌɔːrɪˈɡɑːmi/
n.
折纸；折纸艺术
The Japanese art of folding paper into decorative shapes and figures.
例句：She made a beautiful crane using origami. 她用折纸做了一只漂亮的鹤。

例句：Origami is a traditional Japanese art form. 折纸是日本传统艺术形式。

近义词：paper folding
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps "paper tearing" or "paper crumpling")
词性：noun
