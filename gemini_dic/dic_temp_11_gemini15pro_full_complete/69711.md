fenor:/ˈfɛnɔːr/ | n. | 泥炭沼泽；泥炭地；沼泽地 | A low-lying tract of land, usually with acid peat and characteristic flora (such as sphagnum moss and heath), and often a habitat for wildlife. |  The rare butterfly thrives in the fenor.  这种稀有的蝴蝶在泥炭沼泽中繁衍生息。 | Synonyms: bog, marsh, swamp, mire | Antonyms: upland, hill | noun
