optometry: /ɒpˈtɒmɪtri/ (UK), /ɑːpˈtɑːmətri/ (US) | n. | 验光；验光学 | The science and practice of examining the eyes, prescribing corrective lenses, and detecting eye diseases. |  She studied optometry at university. 她大学学习验光专业。|  Synonym: eye care | Antonym: N/A | noun

optometries: /ɒpˈtɒmɪtriz/ (UK), /ɑːpˈtɑːmətriz/ (US) | n. | 验光(复数，罕用) |  Multiple practices or fields of optometry.  This word is rarely used in standard English, as "optometry" itself usually encompasses all its sub-fields. |  The different optometries practiced around the world vary in their regulations. 世界各地实行的验光方法规章制度各不相同。(例句略有不自然，因该词本身不常用) | Synonym:  (As a plural, it has no direct synonym) | Antonym: N/A | noun

Etymology: From French *optométrie*, from Greek *ὀπτός* (optos, "seen, visible") + *-metry* (measuring).
