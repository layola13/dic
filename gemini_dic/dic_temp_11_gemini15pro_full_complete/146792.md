overillustrating:/ˌōvərˈɪləˌstreɪtɪŋ/
|v.|过度图示；过度插图|Adding too many illustrations to a text, often to the point of redundancy or distraction.  The act of illustrating something excessively.|The textbook was overillustrating, making it difficult to focus on the actual content. 这本教科书插图过多，让人难以专注于实际内容。|Over-illustrating can sometimes detract from the message. 过多的插图有时会分散读者对信息的注意力。|overdoing the illustrations, using too many pictures|under-illustrating|verb
