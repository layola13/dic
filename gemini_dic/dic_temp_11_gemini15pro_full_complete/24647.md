broking:/ˈbrəʊkɪŋ/| n.|经纪；中间人业务；代理业|The act of acting as a broker; the business of a broker.  The arrangement of sales, agreements, etc. between two or more parties.|He made his fortune in stock broking. 他在股票经纪业发了财。|Synonyms: brokerage, intermediation, agency|Antonyms: direct dealing,  proprietary trading|noun
