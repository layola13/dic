ne'er-do-wells:/ˌnerduːˈwɛlz/ | n. | 浪荡子；游手好闲的人；不务正业的人 | A person who is habitually idle and irresponsible. |  例句：The town was plagued by a gang of ne'er-do-wells.  这个小镇饱受一群游手好闲的人的困扰。

例句：He was known as a ne'er-do-well, always borrowing money and never paying it back.  他以游手好闲而闻名，总是借钱却从不偿还。

近义词：loafer, idler, good-for-nothing, wastrel | 反义词：hard worker, diligent person | 词性：名词
