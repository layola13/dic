lenslet:/ˈlɛnzlət/| n.|小透镜，微透镜|A small lens, typically one of an array used in devices such as integral photography and Shack-Hartmann wavefront sensors. |Each lenslet in the array forms a tiny image of the scene.|Synonyms: microlens|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but one might consider "macrolens" in some contexts) |noun
