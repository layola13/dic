kitted:/ˈkɪtɪd/
adjective
配备齐全的，装备齐全的
equipped or supplied with the necessary items, especially for a particular activity or purpose.
The expedition was fully kitted out with the latest equipment. 探险队配备了最新的设备。
Synonyms: equipped, outfitted, geared up, supplied
Antonyms: unequipped, unprepared, ill-equipped
词性: adjective

kitted:/ˈkɪtɪd/
verb
kit 的过去分词和过去式
past participle and past tense of "kit" - to supply with a kit
They kitted us out with everything we needed for the camping trip. 他们为我们露营旅行提供了所需的一切。
Synonyms: equipped, outfitted, supplied
Antonyms: unequipped, unprepared
词性: verb
