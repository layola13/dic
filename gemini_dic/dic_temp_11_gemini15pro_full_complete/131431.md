noncloser:/ˈnɒnˌkləʊzər/ (US) /ˈnɑːnˌkloʊzər/| n.| 未完成者；未结束者；（尤指销售）无法完成交易的人|Someone who fails to close a deal, especially in sales. |The sales team identified several nonclosers and offered them additional training. 销售团队找出了一些无法完成交易的人，并为他们提供了额外的培训。|  He was labeled a noncloser after failing to secure several key contracts.  在未能 secured 几份关键合同后，他被贴上了“无法完成交易的人”的标签。| near synonyms:  underperformer, unsuccessful salesperson | antonym: closer, dealmaker | noun
