micro-encapsulation:/ˌmaɪkroʊɪnˌkæpsəˈleɪʃən/ | n. |微胶囊化，微囊化|The process of enclosing small particles of a substance within a protective coating or shell.|Flavorings are often micro-encapsulated to protect them from degradation.|Synonyms: microencapsulation, encapsulation|Antonyms: release, decapsulation|Noun
