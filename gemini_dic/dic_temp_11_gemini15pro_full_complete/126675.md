namaste:/ˈnʌməsteɪ/ /nəmɑːˈsteɪ/| int. n.|你好；致敬；问候；告别|A traditional Hindu greeting, originating from Sanskrit, meaning "I bow to the divine in you." It is used as a respectful greeting, farewell, or gesture of reverence.|Example 1:  Upon entering the yoga studio, she greeted the instructor with a "Namaste."  进入瑜伽馆后，她向教练问候“你好”。

Example 2:  He concluded his speech with a heartfelt "Namaste." 他以衷心的“Namaste”结束了演讲。|Synonym: hello, greetings|Antonym: (No direct antonym, as it's a greeting)|Interjection, Noun
