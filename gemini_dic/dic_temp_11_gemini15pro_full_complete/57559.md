dramaticism:/drəˈmætɪsɪzəm/
n.
戏剧性；戏剧化；戏剧主义
A philosophy of language and human relations that views all human interaction as a kind of drama.
Kenneth Burke's theory of dramaticism suggests that all communication can be analyzed as a performance. 肯尼思·伯克的戏剧主义理论认为，所有交流都可以被分析为一种表演。
近义词:dramatics, theatricality
反义词:understatement, simplicity
