almandine:/ˈælməndaɪn/ | n. |铁铝榴石; 紫牙乌 |A deep red variety of garnet, used as a gemstone. |例句：The ring featured a stunning almandine gemstone. 这枚戒指上镶嵌着一颗令人惊艳的铁铝榴石宝石。

例句：Almandine is a common mineral in metamorphic rocks. 铁铝榴石是变质岩中常见的矿物。|近义词：garnet, carbuncle |反义词: N/A |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *alamandine (modern French alabandine), from Latin alabandicus (lapis) (“(stone) of Alabanda”), from Alabanda, a town in Caria, Asia Minor, where the mineral was mined. Doublet of alabandine.
