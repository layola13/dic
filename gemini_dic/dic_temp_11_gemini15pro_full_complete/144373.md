outstinks:/ˌaʊtˈstɪŋks/
verb
比...更臭，胜过...的臭味
to smell worse than something or someone else.
例句：The durian outstinks the garbage.  榴莲比垃圾更臭。

例句：His gym socks outstink anything I've ever smelled. 他的健身房袜子比我闻过的任何东西都臭。
近义词：overpowers, overwhelms (in terms of smell)
反义词：is out-smelled by, smells better than
词性：verb
