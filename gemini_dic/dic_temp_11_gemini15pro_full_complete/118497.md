mesmerized:/ˈmezməraɪzd/| adj.|着迷的，被迷住的|Completely fascinated or spellbound; hypnotized.|例句：I was mesmerized by the dancer's graceful movements. 我被舞者优雅的动作迷住了。

例句：The audience was mesmerized by the magician's performance. 观众被魔术师的表演迷住了。|近义词：captivated, enthralled, fascinated, hypnotized|反义词：bored, disinterested, unimpressed|词性:形容词

词源: From Franz Mesmer, an Austrian physician who popularized hypnotism (then called "mesmerism").  源自弗朗茨·梅斯梅尔，一位推广催眠术（当时被称为“梅斯梅尔主义”）的奥地利医生。
