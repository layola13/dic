goswami:/ɡɒˈswɑːmi/ | n. | 古鲁，尊者，大师 | A Hindu religious teacher, especially one who is a leader of a particular sect.  |  例句：The goswami offered blessings to his followers.  古鲁向他的追随者们祝福。

例句：She sought guidance from a renowned goswami. 她向一位著名的古鲁寻求指导。 | 近义词：guru, teacher, master, swami, preceptor | 反义词：disciple, student, follower | 词性：noun

词源: From Sanskrit *gosvāmin*, meaning "master of cows, lord of senses".  It is a compound of *go* ("cow, senses") and *svāmin* ("master, lord, owner"). Originally referring to someone who had mastered their senses, it evolved to mean a religious teacher or leader.
