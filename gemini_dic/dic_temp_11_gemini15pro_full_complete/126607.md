nahuatl:/ˈnɑːwɑːtəl/ | n. | 纳瓦特尔语 | A group of languages of the Uto-Aztecan language family, spoken by about 1.5 million Nahua peoples, mostly in Central Mexico. |  Many place names in Mexico, such as Acapulco and Oaxaca, are derived from Nahuatl.  许多墨西哥的地名，例如阿卡普尔科和瓦哈卡，都源自纳瓦特尔语。| synonyms:  Aztec, Mexican | antonyms: N/A | noun
