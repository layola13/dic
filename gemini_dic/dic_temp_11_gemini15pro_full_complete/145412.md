overcentralizations: /ˌoʊvərˌsɛntrələˈzeɪʃənz/
|n.|过度集中化|The act of centralizing power or authority excessively, often resulting in inefficiency and inflexibility.|Excessive overcentralizations of political power can stifle innovation and local initiatives. 过度的政治权力集中会扼杀创新和地方积极性。 The company's overcentralizations of decision-making led to delays and frustration among employees. 公司决策的过度集中导致员工的延误和沮丧。|近义词: concentration, consolidation|反义词: decentralization, distribution|词性: 名词

overcentralize: /ˌoʊvərˌsɛntrəlaɪz/
|v.|过度集中|To centralize to an excessive degree.|The government overcentralized control of the economy, stifling private enterprise. 政府过度集中控制经济，扼杀了私营企业。 The new CEO overcentralized decision-making authority, alienating department heads. 新任首席执行官过度集中决策权，疏远了各部门主管。|近义词: concentrate, consolidate|反义词: decentralize, distribute|词性: 动词

centralization: /ˌsɛntrələˈzeɪʃən/
|n.|集中化|The process of bringing something under the control of a central authority.|The centralization of power in the hands of the few can lead to tyranny.  权力集中在少数人手中会导致暴政。 The company underwent a period of centralization to streamline operations. 公司经历了一段时间的集中化管理，以精简运营。|近义词: consolidation, unification|反义词: decentralization, distribution|词性: 名词
