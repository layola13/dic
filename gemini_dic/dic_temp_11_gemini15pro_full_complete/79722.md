gluelike:/ˈɡluːlaɪk/| adj.|胶状的，像胶一样的|Resembling glue in stickiness or consistency.|The substance had a gluelike consistency, making it difficult to remove.|近义词：sticky, adhesive, gummy, viscous|反义词：slippery, smooth|词性:形容词

词源: glue + like.  "Glue" comes from Old French "glu" (birdlime), ultimately from Late Latin "glus" (glue). "Like" comes from Old English "lic" (similar).
