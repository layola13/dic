leaflets:/ˈliːflɪts/| n.|传单；小叶；散页印刷品|A printed sheet of paper, sometimes folded, containing information or advertising and usually distributed free.|They were handing out leaflets about the upcoming election. 他们正在分发有关即将举行的选举的传单。|synonyms: pamphlets, brochures, flyers, handbills|antonyms: book, volume|noun

leaflets:/ˈliːflɪts/| n.|小叶|A small or young leaf, especially one of a compound leaf.|The leaflets of the fern were delicate and intricate. 蕨类植物的小叶精致而复杂。|synonyms:foliole, pinna, pinnule|antonyms: branch, limb|noun
