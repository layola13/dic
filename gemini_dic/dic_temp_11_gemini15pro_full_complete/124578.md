mourningly:/ˈmɔːrnɪŋli/ | adv. | 悲哀地，哀悼地 | In a way that expresses sorrow or grief for someone who has died. | She looked at the photograph mourningly. 她悲痛地看着照片。|  She spoke mourningly of her lost youth. 她悲伤地谈起她逝去的青春。 | 近义词：sadly, sorrowfully, grievously | 反义词：joyfully, happily | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From mourning + -ly.  "Mourning" comes from the Old English *murnung, meaning "sorrow, grief, lamentation."
