denudation:/ˌdiːnjuːˈdeɪʃn/ | n. |剥蚀；裸露；脱衣；剥夺 | the process of stripping something of its covering, especially land of vegetation |  The denudation of the forest by excessive logging led to severe soil erosion. 过度砍伐导致森林剥蚀，造成严重的水土流失。| synonyms: stripping, baring, uncovering, erosion, deforestation | antonyms: afforestation, reforestation, covering, accretion | noun

denude:/dɪˈnjuːd/ | vt. |使裸露；剥夺；剥蚀 | to strip something of its covering, especially land of vegetation | The hillside was denuded of trees by the fire. 山坡上的树木被大火烧光了。| synonyms: strip, bare, uncover, expose, deprive | antonyms: cover, clothe, protect, enrich | verb
