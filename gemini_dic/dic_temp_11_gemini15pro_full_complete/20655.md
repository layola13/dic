blackshirt: /ˈblækʃɜːrt/ (UK) /ˈblækʃɜːrt/ (US) | n. | 黑衫党（成员）；黑衫 |  A member of a fascist organization, especially a supporter of Mussolini or one of his followers.  |  Example: The blackshirts marched through Rome. 黑衫党穿过罗马游行。

Example:  He feared the rise of the blackshirts in Italy. 他害怕意大利黑衫党的崛起。


近义词: fascist, Nazi | 反义词:  anti-fascist | 词性: noun
