housemen's:/ˈhaʊsmənz/
n.
住院医生的（东西）
belonging to or associated with housemen (junior doctors in a hospital).
The housemen's quarters were cramped and basic. 住院医生的宿舍狭小简陋。
近义词: interns', residents'
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "consultants'")
词性: 名词所有格
