chiliad:/ˈkɪliæd/| n. |千年，一千年，千人，大量|A period of one thousand years; a group of one thousand.  A great number or a vast quantity (often used hyperbolically).|例句：The chiliad is coming to an end. 千年就要结束了。

例句：Chiliads of stars twinkled in the night sky.  无数星星在夜空中闪烁。|近义词：millennium, thousand|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑少量 few, handful)|词性:名词
