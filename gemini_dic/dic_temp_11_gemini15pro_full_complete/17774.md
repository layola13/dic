bedlike: /ˈbedlaɪk/
adjective
像床的；适于做床的
Resembling a bed; suitable for use as a bed.
The wide, flat rock formed a bedlike surface.  这块宽阔平坦的岩石形成了一个像床一样的表面。
The sofa was so soft and bedlike, I fell asleep almost instantly.  沙发又软又像床，我几乎立刻就睡着了。
近义词: bed-shaped, couchlike, sofa-like
反义词: chairlike, upright

词性: adjective
