no-fault:/ˌnoʊ ˈfɔːlt/| adj.| 无过错的，不论过失的|  Relating to or denoting a system of insurance or compensation in which benefits are payable regardless of who was to blame for an accident.  No blame is assigned in a no-fault accident. |  "They have a no-fault divorce law in that state."  他们在那个州有无过错离婚法。| Synonym: blameless, guiltless | Antonym: culpable, at-fault | adjective

no-fault:/ˌnoʊ ˈfɔːlt/| n.| 无过错保险| A system of insurance in which a party is compensated by their own insurance company regardless of fault.  No-fault insurance often simplifies the claims process. | "She filed a claim under her no-fault insurance policy." 她在她的无过失保险单下提出索赔。 | Synonym: no-fault insurance, personal injury protection (PIP) | Antonym: at-fault insurance | noun
