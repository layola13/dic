lettered:/ˈlɛtərd/
adjective
有文字的；有字母的；受过教育的；识字的
Marked with letters; educated; literate.

The lettered stones marked the graves of the fallen soldiers.  刻有文字的石头标记着阵亡士兵的坟墓。

He was a lettered man, well-versed in literature and philosophy. 他是一位博学的人，精通文学和哲学。

Synonyms: literate, educated, learned
Antonyms: illiterate, uneducated, ignorant
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lettered*, from Old English *geletterod*, past participle of *geletterian* (“to mark with letters, to teach letters”), equivalent to letter + -ed. Cognate with Dutch *geletterd*, German *gelettert*.
