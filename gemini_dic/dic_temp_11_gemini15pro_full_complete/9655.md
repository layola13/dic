antiswedish:/ˌæntiˈswiːdɪʃ/
adjective
反瑞典的；敌视瑞典的
opposed to, hostile to, or prejudiced against Sweden or its people.
例句：His antiswedish sentiments were evident in his writings. 他的反瑞典情绪在他的作品中显而易见。

例句：The antiswedish propaganda spread quickly throughout the region. 反瑞典的宣传迅速在整个地区传播开来。

近义词：anti-Swedish, Swedophobic
反义词：pro-Swedish, Swedophile
词性: adjective
