diuretically:/ˌdaɪjʊˈrɛtɪkli/
adv.
利尿地
In a way that promotes the formation and excretion of urine.
This medication acts diuretically, helping to remove excess fluid from the body. 这种药物具有利尿作用，有助于排出体内多余的液体。
Synonyms:  urinative
Antonyms:  antidiuretic
词性: 副词
