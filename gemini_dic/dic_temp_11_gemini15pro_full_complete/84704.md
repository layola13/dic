handicapped:/ˈhændɪkæpt/ /ˈhændɪkæptɪd/ | n. |残疾人|A person with a physical or mental disability. |例句：Facilities for the handicapped are improving. 残疾人设施正在改善。|近义词：disabled person, person with a disability|反义词：able-bodied person|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From hand in cap, originally referring to a form of gambling or begging in which participants placed forfeits in a cap and drew lots to determine the winner or loser.  Later, it came to refer to something that disadvantaged a person.

注意事项:
