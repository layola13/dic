javelins: /ˈdʒævəlɪnz/ (US), /ˈdʒævəlɪnz/ (UK) | n. | 标枪（复数） | Plural form of "javelin," referring to multiple light spears thrown in athletic contests. | The javelins landed far down the field. 标枪落在了场地远处。 | synonyms: spears, lances | antonyms: shields, armors | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *javeline, diminutive of javelot "javelin," from Vulgar Latin *gabalus, of uncertain origin, perhaps from Gaulish.
