bewilderment:/bɪˈwɪldərmənt/| n. |困惑，茫然，不知所措|A state of being confused and puzzled; a feeling of complete loss or disorientation.|She stared at the complex instructions in complete bewilderment. 她完全茫然地盯着复杂的说明书。|The sudden change left him in a state of bewilderment. 突如其来的变化让他不知所措。|近义词：confusion, perplexity, disorientation, puzzlement|反义词：clarity, understanding, comprehension|词性:名词
