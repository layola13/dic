oversoftening:/ˌoʊvərˈsɔːfnɪŋ/ /ˌoʊvərˈsɒfnɪŋ/ | n. |过度软化；过度弱化|The process of making something excessively soft, often referring to sounds in phonetics, or policies in economics/politics.| The oversoftening of consonants is a common feature of this dialect.  辅音的过度软化是这种方言的一个共同特征。| 近义词：weakening, softening, lessening | 反义词：hardening, strengthening, toughening |词性: 名词
