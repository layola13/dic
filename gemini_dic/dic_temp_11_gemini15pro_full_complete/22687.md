bordrag:/ˈbɔːrdraːɡ/| n.| 餐费，膳食费，伙食费 |A charge for meals; board.  It covers the cost of providing regular meals. |Example 1: The monthly bordrag at the student dormitory was quite reasonable.  学生宿舍每月的餐费相当合理。

Example 2:  The hotel quoted a price that included bordrag.  酒店报价包含了餐费。|Synonyms: board, meal charge, meal expenses|Antonyms:  gratuity (小费, this is an opposite in terms of payment related to dining but not a direct opposite), discount (折扣)|noun
