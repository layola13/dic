dionysus:/daɪ.əˈnaɪ.səs/
proper noun
狄俄尼索斯
The Greek god of wine, vegetation, pleasure, festivity, madness and wild frenzy.
Dionysus is often depicted with a thyrsus and grapes. 狄俄尼索斯通常被描绘成手持酒神杖和葡萄的样子。
Dionysus was the son of Zeus and Semele. 狄俄尼索斯是宙斯和塞墨勒的儿子。
synonym:Bacchus, Liber
antonym:None (deities typically lack direct antonyms)
词性:名词

dionisian:/daɪ.əˈnɪ.zi.ən/
adjective
酒神的；狂欢的；放纵的
Related to or characteristic of Dionysus, the Greek god of wine and revelry.  
The dionysian festival was filled with music and dancing. 酒神节充满了音乐和舞蹈。
The atmosphere at the concert was dionysian.  音乐会上的气氛非常狂热。
synonym:bacchanalian, orgiastic, unrestrained
antonym:ascetic, restrained, inhibited
词性:形容词
