imprescriptible:/ˌɪmprɪˈskrɪptəbl/
adjective
不可剥夺的；不可废除的；不受时效限制的
Not subject to prescription; incapable of being lost or annulled by lapse of time or failure to enforce.
The Declaration of Independence speaks of imprescriptible rights to life, liberty, and the pursuit of happiness.  《独立宣言》阐述了生命权、自由权和追求幸福的不可剥夺的权利。
Human rights are imprescriptible. 人权是不可剥夺的。
近义词: inalienable, unassailable, absolute
反义词: forfeitable, revocable
词性: adjective

词源:
From French *imprescriptible*, from Latin *impraescriptibilis*, from *in-* (not) + *praescript-* (prescribed) + *-ibilis* (able to be). Ultimately from Latin *praescribere* (to prescribe).
