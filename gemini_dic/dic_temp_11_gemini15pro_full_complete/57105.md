doum:/duːm/
n.|杜姆棕榈|A type of palm tree (Hyphaene thebaica) native to North Africa and Arabia, bearing edible fruit.|The doum palm produces a sweet, fibrous fruit that is a staple food in some regions.  杜姆棕榈产出一种甜的、纤维状的水果，在一些地区是主食。|近义词:palm, gingerbread palm|反义词:|词性:noun
doum:/duːm/
n.|杜姆棕榈果|The edible fruit of the doum palm.|Doum fruits are often sold in markets across North Africa.  杜姆果实在北非各地的市场上都有出售。|近义词:doom fruit|反义词:|词性:noun
