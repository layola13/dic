arrowy:/ˈæroʊi/
adjective
像箭的; 箭状的; 快速的
Resembling an arrow in shape, speed, or direction.
The bird had an arrowy flight.  这只鸟飞行迅速如箭。
swift, rapid, darting
slow, sluggish
