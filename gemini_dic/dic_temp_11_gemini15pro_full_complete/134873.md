nonmonists:/ˌnɒnˈmɒnɪsts/ | n. | 非一元论者 | People who do not subscribe to monism, a philosophical view that a variety of existing things can be explained in terms of a single reality or substance.  |  Nonmonists believe that reality is composed of more than one fundamental substance. 非一元论者认为现实是由不止一种基本物质构成的。 | Synonyms: pluralists, dualists | Antonyms: monists | Noun
