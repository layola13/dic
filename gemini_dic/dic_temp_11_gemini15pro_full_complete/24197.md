bridegrooms:/ˈbraɪdɡruːmz/
n.
新郎（复数）
a man who is about to get married or has just got married.
例句：The bridegrooms were all dressed in tuxedos. 新郎们都穿着燕尾服。

例句：The bridegrooms nervously awaited the arrival of their brides. 新郎们紧张地等待着新娘们的到来。

近义词：grooms, husbands (after marriage)
反义词：brides
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *brȳdguma, from brȳd "bride" + guma "man."
