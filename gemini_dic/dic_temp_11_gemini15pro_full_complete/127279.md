naves:/neɪvz/ | n. |教堂中殿；(尤指大型教堂的)中殿，主堂；(工厂或仓库的)中央过道|The central part of a church building, intended for the congregation, extending from the narthex to the chancel. Also, a large hall or central part of an industrial building.|The soaring naves of the cathedral were breathtaking. 大教堂高耸的中殿令人叹为观止。|Synonyms: aisle, sanctuary, hall, bay |Antonyms:  transept, apse|noun

naves:/neɪvz/ | n. |(nave 的复数)|Plural form of nave.|The naves of both cathedrals were remarkably similar. 两座教堂的中殿都非常相似。|Synonyms: aisles, sanctuaries, halls |Antonyms: transepts, apses|noun
