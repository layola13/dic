douay:/ˈduːeɪ/ |adj. n.| 杜埃版（圣经）; 杜埃（法国北部城市）| An edition of the Vulgate Bible, along with extensive annotations,  translated into English at Douai, France, in the early 17th century.  Also refers to the city of Douai itself in northern France.|  The Douay-Rheims Bible is still preferred by some traditional Catholics.  杜埃-兰斯圣经仍然受到一些传统天主教徒的青睐。|近义词: Rheims-Douay, Vulgate|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific Bible translation)
词性: adjective, noun
