copalm:/ˈkoʊpɑːm/ |n.|柯巴树脂，柯巴脂|A resin obtained from tropical trees, used in varnishes.|The artist used copalm in his varnish to give it a rich, glossy finish. 艺术家在他的清漆中使用了柯巴脂，使其具有丰富的光泽。|近义词:resin, amber|反义词: None|词性:名词

Etymology: From Spanish *copal*, from Nahuatl *copalli* meaning "incense", "resin".
