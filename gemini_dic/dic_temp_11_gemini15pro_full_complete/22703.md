boredoms:/ˈbɔːrdəmz/
n.
无聊，厌倦（复数）
The state of being weary and restless through lack of interest.
She suffered from terrible boredoms during the long winter months. 她漫长的冬日饱受无聊的煎熬。
近义词：ennui, tedium, monotony
反义词：interest, excitement, engagement
词性：名词


Etymology:
From bore + -dom.  "Bore" traces back to Old English *borian "to pierce, perforate," related to Dutch boren "to bore." "-dom" is a suffix forming nouns of state, condition, or domain.
