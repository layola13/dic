convertibility:/kənˌvɜːrtɪˈbɪləti/
n.|可兑换性，可变换性|The quality of being able to be converted from one form to another, especially currency to another currency or asset. |The convertibility of the currency is crucial for international trade. 该货币的可兑换性对于国际贸易至关重要。|exchangeability, interchangeability|inconvertibility|noun
