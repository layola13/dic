mustinesses:/ˈmʌstinɪsɪz/
noun
霉味；霉臭；陈腐
the quality of smelling stale, damp, and moldy.
The mustinesses of the old books filled the library.  图书馆里弥漫着旧书的霉味。
The various mustinesses emanating from the basement were overwhelming. 从地下室散发出的各种霉味令人难以忍受。

近义词:moldiness, dampness, fustiness, staleness
反义词:freshness, cleanness

词性:noun (plural)

词源: From musty + -ness + -es.  Musty comes from Middle English *musti, from Old French moisi "moldy," ultimately from Latin mucidus.
