brawned:/brɔːnd/
adjective
强壮的，肌肉发达的
(of a person) having strong muscles; muscular
Example:  The brawned lumberjack easily lifted the heavy log.  强壮的伐木工人轻松地举起了沉重的原木。

Example: She admired his brawned physique. 她欣赏他强壮的体格。

Synonym: muscular, brawny, strong, powerful, burly, strapping
Antonym: weak, feeble, frail, delicate, puny

Etymology: From *brawn* (muscular strength; muscles) + *-ed* (forming adjectives from nouns). The word "brawn" comes from Old French *braon*, meaning "fleshy part of the body, buttock, ham," probably from a Germanic source.
