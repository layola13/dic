nondecorousness:/nɒndɪˈkɔːrəsnəs/
noun
不体面; 不雅;失礼
lack of decorousness; impropriety.  A state or quality of being unseemly or inappropriate in behavior or appearance.
Her nondecorousness at the funeral was shocking. 她葬礼上的失礼行为令人震惊。
Near Synonyms: impropriety, indecorum, unseemliness, indecency, immodesty
Antonyms: decorum, propriety, decency, seemliness, modesty
词性: noun
