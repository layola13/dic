gyroidal:/ˈdʒaɪrɔɪdəl/
adjective
螺旋状的，环形的
Resembling a gyroid, a surface that has a twisting, saddle-like shape.  It is a member of the minimal surface family.
The bone structure of the inner ear is gyroidal.  内耳的骨骼结构是螺旋状的。

The newly discovered butterfly species exhibits a gyroidal pattern on its wings.  新发现的蝴蝶物种在其翅膀上展现出螺旋状的图案。

spiral, helical, twisting, convoluted
flat, planar, straight

词性: adjective
