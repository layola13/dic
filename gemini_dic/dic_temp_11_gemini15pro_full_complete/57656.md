drawbench: /ˈdrɔːbɛntʃ/ | n. | 拉丝机，拉拔机 | A machine for drawing metal into wire or rod. |  The wire is pulled through a series of progressively smaller dies on the drawbench. 拉丝机上，金属丝被拉过一系列逐渐变小的模具。

The factory uses a powerful drawbench to produce steel cables.  这家工厂使用一台强力拉丝机来生产钢缆。 | synonyms: wire-drawing machine, drawing machine | antonyms: N/A | noun
