anticommercialist:/ˌænti kəˈmɜːrʃəlɪst/| n. adj.|反商业主义者，反商业主义的|Someone who opposes commercialism or the emphasis on profit and material values.|Example (n.): He's a staunch anticommercialist, refusing to buy anything advertised on television. 他是一位坚定的反商业主义者，拒绝购买任何在电视上做广告的东西。

Example (adj.):  She took an anticommercialist stance, advocating for locally sourced and ethically produced goods. 她采取了反商业主义的立场，提倡本地采购和符合伦理道德的商品。|近义词：anticonsumerist, nonmaterialist|反义词:materialist, capitalist, consumerist|词性: noun, adjective
