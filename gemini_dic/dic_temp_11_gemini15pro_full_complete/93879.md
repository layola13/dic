immolators:/ˈɪməˌleɪtərz/
n.
祭祀者；自焚者
A person who kills as a sacrificial victim, especially by fire.
The immolators prepared the sacrifice for the ancient ritual. 祭祀者为古老的仪式准备祭品。

The monk was one of the immolators protesting the war.  这位僧侣是抗议战争的自焚者之一。
synonyms: sacrificers, victims (when self-immolating)
antonyms: rescuers, saviors
noun
