lockstep:/ˈlɑːkˌstɛp/ /ˈlɒkˌstɛp/| n. adv. adj.| 锁定步调，同步，一致|  A state of rigid synchronization or conformity, where actions or processes are tightly coupled and proceed at the same pace.  Origin: Early 20th century, from 'lock' (fasten) + 'step' (a single movement of the foot in walking).|The two companies are in lockstep on this issue. 这两家公司在这个问题上步调一致。|近义词：synchronization, unison, harmony, conformity, alignment|反义词:discord, disagreement, disunity, independence|noun

lockstep:/ˈlɑːkˌstɛp/ /ˈlɒkˌstɛp/| adv. adj.|  同步地，一致地| In a synchronized or coordinated manner. |The dancers moved lockstep across the floor. 舞者们在舞池中同步移动。|近义词：synchronously, together, jointly, uniformly|反义词:independently, separately, divergently|adverb

lockstep:/ˈlɑːkˌstɛp/ /ˈlɒkˌstɛp/| adj.|  同步的，一致的| Characterized by synchronization or conformity. |Their lockstep approach to the problem stifled creativity. 他们在解决这个问题上的步调一致扼杀了创造力。|近义词：synchronized, coordinated, unified, harmonious, aligned|反义词:independent, separate, divergent, disunified|adjective
