maggot:/ˈmæɡət/| n. |蛆，幼虫|The decaying meat was infested with maggots. 腐烂的肉里爬满了蛆。|例句：The fisherman used maggots as bait. 渔夫用蛆做诱饵。  The sight of maggots crawling on the garbage made her stomach churn. 看到蛆虫在垃圾上爬来爬去，她感到胃里一阵翻腾。|近义词：larva, grub|反义词: None|词性: Noun
Etymology: From Old English *maðætte, a diminutive of maða "maggot," from Proto-Germanic *maþōn (source also of Old Norse maðkr, Old High German mado, German Made "maggot"), from Proto-Indo-European *mōth- "worm, moth."
