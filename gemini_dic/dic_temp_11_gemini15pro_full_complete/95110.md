inaniloquous:/ˌɪnəˈnɪləkwəs/ | adj. |空洞的，废话连篇的 |Speaking or writing without substance or meaning; characterized by empty or useless chatter.  |  The politician's inaniloquous speech left the audience bored and confused. 这位政客空洞的演讲让听众感到无聊和困惑。 |  He delivered an inaniloquous presentation, full of jargon and lacking any real insights. 他做了一个空洞的演讲，充满了术语，缺乏真正的见解。 |  Synonyms:  empty, vacuous, vapid, inane, pointless | Antonyms: meaningful, substantive, concise, pithy | adjective
