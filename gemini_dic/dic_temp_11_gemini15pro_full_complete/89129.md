homebound:/ˈhoʊmbaʊnd/| adj.|在家的，不能外出的|Confined to one's home, typically due to illness, injury, or old age.  Unable to leave the house.|例句：She was homebound for several weeks after the surgery.  手术后，她在家待了几个星期。

例句：The heavy snowstorm made many people homebound. 暴风雪使许多人无法出门。|近义词：housebound, confined, restricted, shut-in|反义词:outgoing, mobile, active|词性:adjective
Etymology:  "home" + "bound" (past participle of "bind").  "Bound" in this sense means restricted or obligated.
