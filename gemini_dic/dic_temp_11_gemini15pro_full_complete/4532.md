alburnous:/ælˈbɜːrnəs/
adjective
白色的；白木质的；边材的
Relating to, or resembling, alburnum; white; pale.  Specifically, designating the outer, lighter-colored portion of a tree trunk, consisting of sapwood.
The alburnous layer of the tree was clearly visible beneath the bark. 树干的白木质层在树皮下清晰可见。
Synonyms: sapwood, white, pale, light-colored
Antonyms: heartwood, dark, duramen
词性: 形容词
