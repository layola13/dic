enged: /ɪnˈɡeɪdʒd/ | adj. | 已订婚的；忙碌的；从事…的 | betrothed; busy; occupied |  She is engaged to be married. 她已订婚。 | 近义词: betrothed, promised, busy, occupied, involved | 反义词: unengaged, free, idle, uninvolved | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Old French *engager (Modern French engager) "to pledge, pawn; bind by oath; enlist," from en- "in" + gage "pledge."
