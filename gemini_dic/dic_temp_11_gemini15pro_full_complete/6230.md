amnestying: /æmˈnɛstiɪŋ/
verb
赦免;大赦
The act of granting an amnesty, which is an official pardon for people who have been convicted of political offenses.
The government is amnestying all political prisoners. 政府正在赦免所有政治犯。
近义词: pardoning, forgiving, absolving
反义词: condemning, punishing, prosecuting
词性: verb

amnesty: /ˈæmnɪsti/
noun
赦免;大赦
An official pardon for people who have been convicted of political offenses.
The government granted an amnesty to all political prisoners. 政府对所有政治犯给予了赦免。
近义词: pardon, forgiveness, absolution
反义词: condemnation, punishment, prosecution
词性: noun

词源: From French amnistie, from Latin amnestia, from Greek amnēstiā "forgetfulness, oblivion," from amnēstos "forgotten, not remembered"
