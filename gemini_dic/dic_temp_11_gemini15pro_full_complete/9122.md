antimusic: /ˌæntiˈmjuːzɪk/ | n. | 反音乐; 非音乐 | Music that is deliberately unpleasant or challenging to traditional notions of music.  It often incorporates elements of noise, dissonance, and unconventional instrumentation. |  Example: The performance artist's piece was labeled as antimusic due to its jarring sounds and lack of melody.  这位行为艺术家的作品因其刺耳的声音和缺乏旋律而被标记为反音乐。

Example:  Some critics considered John Cage's 4'33" to be a form of antimusic, challenging the very definition of music. 一些评论家认为约翰·凯奇的《4分33秒》是一种反音乐形式，挑战了音乐的定义本身。 | 近义词: noise music, experimental music | 反义词: music, traditional music | 词性: noun
