outbuild:/ˈaʊtbɪld/
verb|加建；在…外面建造|to build something outside of and adjoining a main building|They outbuilt a shed onto the side of their house. 他们在房子旁边加建了一个棚子。|
近义词:extend, add to
反义词:demolish, dismantle
词性:verb

outbuild:/ˈaʊtbɪld/
noun|外屋；附属建筑|a building such as a shed or stable that is built separate from but near to a house |The farm has several outbuildings including a barn and a workshop.  这个农场有几个附属建筑，包括一个谷仓和一个车间。|
近义词:annex, outhouse
反义词:main building
词性:noun
