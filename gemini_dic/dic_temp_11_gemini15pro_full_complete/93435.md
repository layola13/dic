illuminating:/ɪˈluːmɪneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
启发的；照明的
making something clearer or easier to understand; shining light on something
The lecture was illuminating; it helped me understand the complex theory.  这个讲座很有启发性，它帮助我理解了这个复杂的理论。
The streetlights were illuminating the path ahead.  路灯照亮了前方的道路。

近义词: enlightening, informative, revealing, clarifying, bright
反义词: confusing, obscure, unclear, dim, dark
词性: adjective


词源:  From Latin illuminare "to light up, brighten," from in- "in" + luminare "to light," from lumen "light."
