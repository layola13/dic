at all events:/æt ɔːl ɪˈvents/ | adv. | 无论如何，不管怎样 | In any case; whatever happens; at least.  Means something will happen regardless of other factors. | I may be late, but at all events I will be there by tomorrow.  我可能会迟到，但无论如何，我明天都会到那里。|例句：The hotel was awful, but at all events the food was good. 这家酒店很糟糕，但至少食物还不错。 | 近义词: anyway, anyhow, in any case, at any rate | 反义词: specifically, particularly | 词性: 副词
