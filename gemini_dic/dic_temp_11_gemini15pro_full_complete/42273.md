counter-compony:/ˌkaʊntərˈkʌmpəni/ | n. | 反纹章; 逆纹章; 对纹; | A heraldic bearing in reversed colours from another. An exact replica of a coat of arms but with the colours reversed. | The duke bore a counter-compony of the royal arms. 公爵的纹章是皇室纹章的反纹章。 | Synonyms: reversed arms, inverted arms | Antonyms: standard arms | noun

counter-compony:/ˌkaʊntərˈkʌmpəni/ | adj. | 对分的; 格子形的 | Divided or checkered in alternate colours, especially in heraldry. | The shield was counter-compony or and azure. 盾牌是金色和天蓝色相间的格子图案。 | Synonyms: checkered, chequered, tessellated | Antonyms: plain, solid | adjective
