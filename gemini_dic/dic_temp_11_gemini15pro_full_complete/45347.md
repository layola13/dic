cumulonimbus:/ˌkjuːmələʊˈnɪmbəs/ | n. |积雨云|A dense, towering vertical cloud characterized by a dark, flat base and a distinctive anvil-shaped top, often accompanied by heavy precipitation, lightning, and thunder.| Example 1: The cumulonimbus clouds gathered quickly, signaling an approaching thunderstorm. 积雨云迅速聚集，预示着即将到来的雷暴。

Example 2: Pilots avoid flying through cumulonimbus clouds due to the risk of severe turbulence. 飞行员会避免飞穿过积雨云，因为存在严重湍流的风险。| 近义词: thundercloud, storm cloud | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific cloud type) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin cumulus (“heap, accumulation”) + nimbus (“rain cloud, storm cloud”).
