danburite:/ˈdænbəˌraɪt/| n. |丹砂石|A colorless, pale yellow, or pink mineral, calcium borosilicate, CaB2Si2O8, occurring in orthorhombic crystals.|Danburite is prized by collectors for its clarity and delicate colors. 丹砂石因其清澈度和柔和的色彩而受到收藏家的珍视。|
|synonyms: borosilicate (general term, not a true synonym)|antonyms: N/A (Minerals don't typically have antonyms)|noun
