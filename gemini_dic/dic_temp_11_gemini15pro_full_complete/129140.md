no-brainers:/ˌnoʊˈbreɪnərz/ | n. | 轻而易举的事；不费脑筋的事 | Something that is very easy to do or understand; something that requires little or no thought. | Choosing to go to the beach on a hot day is a no-brainer. 在炎热的天气选择去海滩是件不费脑筋的事。 | Synonyms: cinch, piece of cake, breeze, child's play, easy peasy | Antonyms: challenge, difficult task, puzzle, conundrum | noun

no-brainer:/ˌnoʊˈbreɪnər/ | n. | 明摆着的事；显而易见的事 | Something that is obvious or self-evident. |  It's a no-brainer that you should apologize after being rude.  很显然，你应该在粗鲁之后道歉。| Synonyms: obvious choice, clear decision, no-brainer | Antonyms: dilemma, quandary, tough decision | noun
