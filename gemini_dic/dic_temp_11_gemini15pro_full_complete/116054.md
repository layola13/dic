ETYMOLOGY->
From *martial* (adjective) + *-ness* (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  *Martial* comes from Old French *martial*, from Latin *martialis* "of Mars," from *Mars*, the Roman god of war. The suffix *-ness* is a native English suffix used to form nouns from adjectives.
