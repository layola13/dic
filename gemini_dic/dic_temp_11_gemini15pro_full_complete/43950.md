cribellum:/krɪˈbɛləm/
n.
筛器
A spinning organ of certain spiders that produces bands of extremely fine silk used to make the hackled bands of the web.
The cribellum of the spider is used to produce a special type of silk.  蜘蛛的筛器用来产生一种特殊的丝。
