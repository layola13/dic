gruffing:/ˈɡrʌfɪŋ/| adj.|粗声粗气的；生硬的|Speaking in a rough, low voice, often sounding unfriendly or impatient.|He answered her question in a gruffing tone. 他粗声粗气地回答了她的问题。|近义词：curt, brusque, surly, gruff|反义词：gentle, polite, courteous|词性:形容词

词源: From the verb "gruff" + "-ing" (present participle suffix).  "Gruff" comes from Middle English *grof* and is likely imitative in origin, echoing a rough, low sound.
