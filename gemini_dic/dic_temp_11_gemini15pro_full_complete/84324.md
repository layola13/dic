halfback:/ˈhæfˌbæk/ (US), /ˈhɑːfˌbæk/ (UK)| n.|跑锋 (橄榄球)|A player positioned behind the quarterback in American football, primarily responsible for carrying the ball and receiving passes.|The halfback broke through the line for a touchdown. 跑锋突破防线达阵得分。|
The halfback caught a short pass and ran for a first down.  跑锋接住一个短传，跑出了一个首攻。|
近义词: running back, tailback|反义词: lineman|词性: 名词
