nonaccredited:/ˌnɒnəˈkrɛdɪtɪd/
adjective
未经认证的，未经认可的
Not having official approval or recognition from an authorized agency or organization.
The online university offered nonaccredited courses, so employers didn't recognize the degrees. 这所网络大学提供未经认证的课程，所以雇主不认可用其获得的学位。
This diploma is from a nonaccredited institution and might not be accepted by some employers.  这个文凭来自未经认证的机构，一些雇主可能不接受。
近义词:unaccredited, uncertified, unauthorized, unapproved
反义词:accredited, certified, authorized, approved
词性:adjective

词源:
non- (prefix): meaning "not"  来自拉丁语 non
accredited (adjective):  from accredit + -ed.  来自拉丁语 ad- (to) + credere (to believe).
