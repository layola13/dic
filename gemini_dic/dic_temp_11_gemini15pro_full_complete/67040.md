explanatoriness:/ɪkˌsplænəˈtɔːrɪnɪs/ | n. | 解释性；说明性 | The quality of being explanatory; the extent to which something explains or makes something clear. | The explanatoriness of the diagram helped students understand the complex process. 这张图的解释性帮助学生理解了这个复杂的过程。| 近义词：clarity, lucidity, intelligibility | 反义词：vagueness, obscurity, ambiguity | 词性: noun
词源: From explanatory + -ness.  "Explanatory" comes from the Late Latin *explanatorius, from explanare "to explain," from ex- "out" + planare "to make level, flatten," from planus "flat, level, plain."
