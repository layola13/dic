industriousness:/ɪnˈdʌstriəsnəs/| n. |勤奋，勤勉|The quality of being industrious; persistent and hard-working.|Her industriousness and dedication led to her success. 她的勤奋和奉献精神导致了她的成功。|近义词：diligence, assiduity, application|反义词：laziness, indolence, sloth|词性:名词

Etymology:
From late Middle English: from industrious + -ness.  Industrious comes from late Latin industrius ‘diligent’, from Latin industria ‘diligence’.
