hock: /hɑːk/ (US) /hɒk/ (UK)| n. v.|跗关节;典当;债务;窘境|The part of an animal's hind leg between the knee and the fetlock, or the joint in this part.  Also, to pawn something, or the state of being in debt or difficulty.|例句：The horse injured its hock. 这匹马伤了跗关节。|近义词：pawn, pledge (v.); joint, ankle (n.); difficulty, trouble (n.)|反义词: redeem (v.)
词性: 名词; 动词

hock: /hɑːk/ (US) /hɒk/ (UK)| v.|典当;抵押|To pawn or pledge something as security for a loan.|例句：He had to hock his watch to pay the rent. 他不得不典当他的手表来付房租。|近义词：pawn, pledge|反义词: redeem
