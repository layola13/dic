easy-going:/ˌiːziˈɡəʊɪŋ/ /ˌiːziˈɡoʊɪŋ/| adj.|随和的，悠闲的|Relaxed and tolerant in approach or manner.|He's an easy-going guy who gets along with everyone. 他是一个随和的家伙，和每个人都相处得很好。|My dad is very easy-going and rarely gets angry. 我的爸爸非常随和，很少生气。|近义词：relaxed, laid-back, carefree, tolerant|反义词：uptight, tense, anxious, demanding|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From easy + going, meaning "tolerant in disposition, not easily ruffled".  First recorded in the late 18th century.
