liquorish: /ˈlɪkərɪʃ/ (US), /ˈlɪkərɪʃ/ (UK)| adj.|贪吃的，嗜甜的；渴望的，贪恋的|Having a strong desire or craving for something, especially sweet things; fond of sweet liquors.|She has a liquorish tooth and can never resist chocolate. 她嗜甜，无法抗拒巧克力。|Synonyms: greedy, gluttonous, avaricious, covetous, desirous|Antonyms: abstemious, temperate, indifferent|adjective

liquorice (British English): /ˈlɪkərɪs/ (UK),  /ˈlɪkərɪʃ/ (US) (also spelled licorice in American English)| n.|甘草；由甘草制成的糖果|A black confection made from the root of the liquorice plant.|I love the taste of liquorice. 我喜欢甘草的味道。|Synonyms: licorice (US)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
