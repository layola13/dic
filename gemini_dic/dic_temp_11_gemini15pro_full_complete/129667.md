non-delegate:/ˌnɒnˈdɛlɪɡət/ | adj. | 非代表的，非授权的 | Not having the status of a delegate; not delegated or appointed to represent another.  |  Only non-delegate members can vote on this issue. 只有非代表成员可以对此问题进行投票。 |  The non-delegate attendees were primarily observers. 非代表的与会者主要是观察员。 | 近义词: unrepresented, non-representative | 反义词: delegate, representative | 词性: adjective

词源:
From "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "delegate" (from Latin "delegatus," past participle of "delegare" - to send, assign).
