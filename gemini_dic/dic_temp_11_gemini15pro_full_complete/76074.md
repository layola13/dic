furo:/ˈfjʊəroʊ/| n. |浴缸，澡盆|A large, usually deep, tub used for bathing. |例句：She relaxed in the furo after a long day. 她忙碌了一天后在浴缸里放松。

例句：The furo was filled with hot water and bubbles. 浴缸里充满了热水和泡泡。|近义词：bathtub, bath, tub|反义词:shower|词性:名词

词源: From Japanese 風呂 (furo), meaning "bath".
