featurelessly: /ˈfiːtʃərləsli/
adjective
无特征地；平淡无奇地
lacking distinguishing features or characteristics; bland and unremarkable
The landscape stretched before them, featurelessly flat and monotonous.  这片景色在他们面前延伸，平坦单调，毫无特色。
The walls were painted a featureless beige. 墙壁被涂成了毫无特色的米黄色。
Synonyms: bland, monotonous, nondescript, characterless, plain, unexceptional, unremarkable, undistinguished
Antonyms: distinctive, striking, remarkable, prominent, noticeable, unique, individual
词性: 形容词
