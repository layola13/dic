diurnally:/daɪˈɜːnəli/
adv.
每天地，白天活动的
Happening or active during the daytime.
例句：
The flowers open diurnally and close at night. 这些花白天开放，晚上闭合。

Bats are not diurnally active, preferring to fly at night.  蝙蝠不是白天活动的，更喜欢在夜间飞行。

近义词：daily, every day, during the day
反义词：nocturnally, nightly
词性：副词


词源：
From Late Latin *diurnalis, from Latin diurnus (“daily”), from dies (“day”).
