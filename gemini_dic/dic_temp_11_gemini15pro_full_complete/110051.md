licensure:/ˈlaɪsəntʃər/ |n.|许可，执照|The state board grants licensure to qualified applicants.  州委员会授予合格申请人执照。|例句：Doctors must obtain licensure before they can practice medicine. 医生必须获得执照才能行医。

例句：The licensure requirements vary from state to state. 各州的执照要求各不相同。

近义词：certification, authorization, accreditation|反义词:prohibition, forbiddance|词性:noun
