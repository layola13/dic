codfish: /ˈkɒdfɪʃ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːdfɪʃ/ (US)| n.|鳕鱼|A fish of the North Atlantic, important as a food fish.  It has a greenish-grey back, white sides and belly, and three dorsal fins.|Codfish is a popular dish in many countries. 鳕鱼在许多国家都是一道受欢迎的菜肴。|近义词：haddock, pollock|反义词: None (It's a specific type of fish, not a concept with an opposite.)|词性: noun

词源:
From Middle English *coddefisshe, *codlyng, from Middle Dutch kabeljauw, kabbeljauw, from Old Norse kabeljá, kabaljá (“cod”).  Compare also Old English codd (“bag, husk, cod”). The second element is from fish.
