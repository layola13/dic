heptone:/ˈheptəʊn/ (UK) /ˈheptˌoʊn/ (US)| n. |庚酮; 七碳酮|A ketone containing seven carbon atoms.  Specifically, it often refers to 2-heptanone.|

例句：
The fruity aroma of the cheese is due in part to the presence of 2-heptanone.
这种奶酪的水果香味部分归功于 2-庚酮的存在。

The lab synthesized various heptones for use in fragrance development.
该实验室合成了各种庚酮，用于开发香水。

近义词：ketone (general term), dipropyl ketone (specifically for 4-heptanone)|
