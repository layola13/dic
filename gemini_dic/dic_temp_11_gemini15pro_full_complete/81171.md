grandeeships:/ˌɡrænˈdiːʃɪps/
noun
显贵的地位；高官的职位
The position or status of a grandee, especially collectively.  This word emphasizes the collective power and influence of high-ranking officials.
The grandeeships of the Spanish court held considerable sway over the king. 西班牙宫廷的显贵们对国王有相当大的影响力。
Synonyms: nobility, aristocracy, elite, high society
Antonyms: commoners, peasantry, proletariat
词性: 名词
