dextronic:/dɛkˈstrɒnɪk/ | adj. | 右旋的，与右旋糖有关的 | Relating to or designating dextrose or other dextrorotatory substances. |  Dextrose is a dextronic sugar. 葡萄糖是一种右旋糖。|  Dextronic substances rotate polarized light to the right. 右旋物质使偏振光向右旋转。 | synonyms: dextrorotatory, right-handed (in terms of optical activity) | antonyms: levorotatory, left-handed (in terms of optical activity) | adjective
