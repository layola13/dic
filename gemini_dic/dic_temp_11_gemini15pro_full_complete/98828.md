intelligences:/ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒənsɪz/| n.|智力，智能；情报；理解力|The capacity for learning, reasoning, understanding, and similar mental activities; aptitude in grasping truths and meanings.|Multiple intelligences theory suggests that people have different kinds of intelligences. 多元智能理论认为人们拥有不同类型的智能。|synonyms: intellect, mind, reason, understanding, brains, wit, wisdom,  |antonyms: stupidity, ignorance|noun
