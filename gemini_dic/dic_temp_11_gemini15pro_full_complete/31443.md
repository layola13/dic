charityless: /ˈtʃærɪtilɪs/ | adj. | 无情的，缺乏仁慈的 | Lacking charity; uncharitable. Showing no compassion or kindness.  |  He gave a charityless response to the beggar's plea. 他对乞丐的请求作出了无情的回应。 | The charityless king ignored the suffering of his people.  无情的国王无视了他人民的苦难。| 近义词: uncharitable, merciless, heartless, unkind, cruel | 反义词: charitable, kind, compassionate, merciful | 词性: adjective

词源:  charity + -less. "charity" comes from Old French "charité"  and ultimately from Latin "caritas" meaning "dearness, high price, affection, love, benevolence." The suffix "-less" is of Old English origin, meaning "without." Thus, "charityless" literally means "without charity."
