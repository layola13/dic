hypermetrical:/ˌhaɪpərˈmetrɪkl/
adjective
超度量的，超过标准格律的
Relating to or involving hypermeter, which is a metrical irregularity in verse where a line has an extra syllable beyond the expected number.  It goes beyond the standard metrical pattern.
This poem employs a hypermetrical line to create a sense of overflowing emotion. 这首诗运用超度量诗行来营造一种情感 overflowing 的感觉。
The hypermetrical syllable in the final line adds a subtle emphasis. 最后一行中超出的音节增添了微妙的强调。
Near Synonyms: irregular, unusual, non-standard, anomalous
Antonyms: regular, standard, metrical, typical
word type: adjective
