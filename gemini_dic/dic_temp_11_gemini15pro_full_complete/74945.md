freudians:/ˈfrɔɪdiənz/
n.|弗洛伊德主义者；精神分析学家|Followers of or believers in the theories of Sigmund Freud, especially relating to psychoanalysis.|例句：Many Freudians believe that dreams reveal unconscious desires. 许多弗洛伊德主义者认为梦揭示了潜意识的欲望。

例句：The Freudians interpreted his slip of the tongue as a repressed desire. 弗洛伊德主义者将他的口误解释为被压抑的欲望。
|近义词：psychoanalysts, analysts|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑不同学派的学者，例如 Jungians)|词性: 名词
