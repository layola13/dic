gomorrah:/ɡəˈmɔːrə/| n.|所多玛（罪恶之城）|A city mentioned in the Book of Genesis, destroyed by God along with Sodom because of its wickedness.  It is often used figuratively to refer to a place of immorality and wickedness.|The city became a byword for wickedness, a modern-day Gomorrah.|近义词：Sodom, den of iniquity, sink of iniquity|反义词:utopia, paradise|名词
