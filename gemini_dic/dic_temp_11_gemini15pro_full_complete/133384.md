nonexultantly: /ˌnɒnɪɡˈzʌltəntli/
adverb
非兴高采烈地，不欢欣鼓舞地
in a way that does not express or show exultation; without exultation.
He accepted the award nonexultantly, acknowledging the contributions of his team.  他以不喜形于色的姿态接受了奖项，并肯定了团队的贡献。
The news was received nonexultantly, as everyone knew the hard work that lay ahead.  这个消息并没有受到热烈的欢迎，因为每个人都知道未来的艰巨工作。
近义词: calmly, soberly, quietly, reservedly
反义词: exultantly, triumphantly, joyfully
词性: adverb

词源:
non- (prefix):  not; absence of.  来源于拉丁语 non.
exultant (adjective): feeling or expressing great triumph or happiness. 来源于拉丁语 exultant-, exultans, present participle of exultare 'leap up, rejoice'.
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives.
