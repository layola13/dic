bifilar:/baɪˈfaɪlər/| adj.|双线的，双丝的|Having two threads or filaments.  Relating to a measuring instrument that uses two threads or wires.|The bifilar suspension is commonly used in galvanometers.|近义词：double-threaded, two-wired|反义词: single-threaded, single-wired|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin "bi-" (two) + "filum" (thread).
