on an even keel:/ɒn ən ˈiːvən kiːl/
adjective|平稳的，均衡的，稳定的|Being on an even keel means being in a calm, stable, and balanced state, especially after a period of difficulty or uncertainty.|例句：After a turbulent few months, the company is finally back on an even keel. 经历了几个月的动荡之后，公司终于恢复了平稳。|近义词：stable, balanced, steady, calm, composed|反义词:unstable, unbalanced, turbulent, chaotic, erratic
词性|adjective
