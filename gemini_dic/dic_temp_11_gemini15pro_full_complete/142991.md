outbarters:/ˌaʊtˈbɑːrtərz/
| n. |以物易物胜过他人者；在交易中胜过他人的人|People who outperform others in bartering or trading.  Those who are skilled at exchanging goods and services and get a better deal than others.| The shrewd merchant outbartered all his competitors at the market.  这位精明的商人胜过了市场上所有的竞争对手。|Synonyms:  outtrader, superior negotiator|Antonyms:  poor negotiator, unskilled trader|noun

outbarter:/ˌaʊtˈbɑːrtər/
| v. |在以物易物中胜过；在交易中胜过 |To barter or trade more effectively than someone else; to get a better deal than another person in an exchange.| She outbartered him, getting two chickens for a single goat. 她胜过他，用一只山羊换了两只鸡。|Synonyms: outtrade, surpass in trading|Antonyms: be outtraded, be worsted in a deal|verb
