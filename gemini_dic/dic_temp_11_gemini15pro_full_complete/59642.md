ecgonine:/ˈekɡəˌnaɪn/ | n. | 芽子碱; 莨菪烷-3-羧酸-2-β-醇 |A crystalline tropane alkaloid that is obtained by hydrolysis of cocaine or by synthesis. It is used medicinally as a topical anesthetic. | Ecgonine is a precursor in the synthesis of cocaine. 芽子碱是可卡因合成中的前体。| Ecgonine itself is not psychoactive, but it can be converted into cocaine. 芽子碱本身不具有精神活性，但它可以转化为可卡因。 | synonyms:  tropane derivative | antonyms: N/A | noun
