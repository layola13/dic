avenaceous:/ˌeɪvɪˈneɪʃəs/
adj.
燕麦似的，像燕麦的
Relating to, resembling, or characteristic of oats.
The field was covered in an avenaceous crop, swaying gently in the breeze. 这片田野覆盖着像燕麦一样的作物，在微风中轻轻摇曳。
Synonyms: oat-like, graminaceous
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but could use terms like "unlike oats," "dissimilar to oats")
word type: adjective
