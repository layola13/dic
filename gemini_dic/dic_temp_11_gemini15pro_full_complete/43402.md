crankless:/ˈkræŋklɪs/
adjective
无曲柄的，无摇柄的
Lacking a crank or handle; operated without a crank.
The crankless engine design simplifies the mechanism and reduces maintenance.  无曲柄的发动机设计简化了机构并减少了维护。
The new bike features a crankless pedal system.  这辆新自行车采用无曲柄踏板系统。
Near synonyms: direct-drive, handleless, leverless
Antonyms: cranked, handled
