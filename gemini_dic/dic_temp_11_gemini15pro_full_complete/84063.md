half-Greek:/ˌhɑːf ˈɡriːk/ | adj. | 半希腊的；混血希腊的 | Partly of Greek descent or origin; having one parent of Greek origin and one of another. | Her father was English and her mother Greek, so she was half-Greek. 她的父亲是英国人，母亲是希腊人，所以她是混血希腊人。| Example: He considered himself half-Greek, having grown up in Athens despite his American father. 他认为自己是半个希腊人，尽管他的父亲是美国人，但他在雅典长大。| Synonyms: half-blood, mixed-race (in broader context) | Antonyms: pure-blooded, full-blooded | Adjective
