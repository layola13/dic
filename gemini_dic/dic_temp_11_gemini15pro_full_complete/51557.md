deuterium:/djuːˈtɪəriəm/ | n. |氘，重氢|A stable isotope of hydrogen with a nucleus of one proton and one neutron, used especially in nuclear fusion.|Deuterium is twice as heavy as ordinary hydrogen. 氘是普通氢的两倍重。| Deuterium is used in nuclear fusion reactions. 氘用于核聚变反应。 |Synonyms: heavy hydrogen | Antonyms: protium (ordinary hydrogen)| noun
