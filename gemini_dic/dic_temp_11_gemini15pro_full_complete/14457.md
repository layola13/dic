availablenesses:/əˌveɪləˈbɪlɪtɪz/| n. |可用性（复数）|The state of being available; the fact that something can be obtained or used.  It is generally considered awkward and overly formal.  "Availability" is preferred.|Due to the availablenesses of resources, the project was completed ahead of schedule. (Generally considered awkward – better to use "availability") 由于资源的可用性，项目提前完成。|Near synonyms: availability, accessibilities  (using the plural of accessibility is similarly awkward)|Antonym: unavailability |noun
