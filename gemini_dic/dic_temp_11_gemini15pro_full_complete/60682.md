electrician:/ɪˌlekˈtrɪʃən/ | n. |电工；电气技师|A skilled worker who installs and maintains electrical systems.|例句：The electrician wired the new house. 电工给新房子布线了。

例句：We had to call an electrician to fix the faulty wiring. 我们不得不叫一个电工来修理故障线路。

近义词：electrical worker, wireman|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a profession)|词性: Noun
