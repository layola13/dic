crudely:/ˈkruːdli/ /ˈkruːdli/| adv.|粗糙地；粗鲁地；简陋地|In a raw or unrefined way; roughly; without skill or finesse.  Showing a lack of culture or refinement; offensively coarse or rude.|The painting was crudely executed. 这幅画画得很粗糙。|Synonyms: roughly, coarsely, clumsily, awkwardly, ineptly, artlessly, unskilfully, inexpertly|Antonyms: finely, delicately, skillfully, expertly, elegantly, refinedly|词性:副词

crudely:/ˈkruːdli/ /ˈkruːdli/| adv.|粗暴地；粗俗地|In a way that is offensively rude or insensitive.|He spoke crudely about her appearance. 他粗鲁地谈论她的外貌。|Synonyms: rudely, offensively, coarsely, vulgarly, indecently, obscenely|Antonyms: politely, respectfully, courteously, graciously, tactfully, discreetly|词性:副词
