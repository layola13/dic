nonrepellent:/ˌnɑːnrɪˈpɛlənt/ | adj. | 不驱避的，非驱避性的 |  Not repelling or driving away, especially of insecticides or pesticides that do not deter insects or pests from the treated area but kill them upon contact or ingestion. | The researchers developed a nonrepellent insecticide to control the termite infestation. 研究人员开发了一种非驱避性杀虫剂来控制白蚁的侵扰。 | The farmer used a nonrepellent spray on his crops. 农民在他的作物上喷洒了非驱避性喷雾。 | synonyms: nondeterrent, undetectable | antonyms: repellent, deterrent | adjective
