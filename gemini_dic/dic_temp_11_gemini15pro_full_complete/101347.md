iolite:/ˈaɪəlaɪt/ | n. |堇青石;二碘石|A transparent gemstone, a variety of cordierite, typically violet-blue in color. |The iolite in her ring shimmered in the sunlight. 她的戒指上的堇青石在阳光下闪闪发光。|Synonyms: cordierite, dichroite |Antonyms: N/A |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek "ion" (violet) + "lithos" (stone).  源于希腊语 "ion"（紫罗兰色） + "lithos"（石头）。
