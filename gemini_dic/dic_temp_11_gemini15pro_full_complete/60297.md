eggplant:/ˈɛɡˌplænt/ (US) /ˈɛɡˌplɑːnt/ (UK)| n. |茄子|A large, oval, purple fruit, eaten as a vegetable. It is botanically a berry.|例句：I added eggplant to the stew. 我在炖菜里加了茄子。

例句：The eggplant parmigiana was delicious. 茄子帕尔马干酪很美味。


|近义词：aubergine (chiefly British)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
