/ˈhɪər/ /ˈhɪr/ | determiner, pronoun | 这，这里，此处;  (用于谈话或写作中指已提到或将要提到的人或事物) |This refers to something close to the speaker or writer, either physically or in context. It can be a determiner before a noun or a pronoun on its own.| Here is my book.  这是我的书。 | synonyms: this, these | antonyms: there, those | Determiner

/ˈhɪər/ /ˈhɪr/ | adverb | 这里，在这里;  (用于谈话中表示某人或某物在说话人所在的地方) |This adverb indicates location, specifically where the speaker is. | Come here!  过来这里！| synonyms: hither | antonyms: hence, thence, away | Adverb
