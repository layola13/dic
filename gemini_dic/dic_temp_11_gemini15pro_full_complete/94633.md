impressed:/ɪmˈprɛst/ | adj. |印象深刻的；钦佩的| Feeling admiration or respect for someone or something.|  例句：I was very impressed with her presentation. 我对她的演讲印象非常深刻。| 近义词：admiring, awed, moved, touched | 反义词：unimpressed, indifferent, apathetic | 词性:形容词

impress:/ɪmˈprɛs/ | v. |使印象深刻；使铭记| To have a strong effect on someone's mind or feelings.| 例句：He impressed me with his knowledge. 他的知识给我留下了深刻的印象。 | 近义词：affect, influence, move, sway | 反义词：bore, disappoint |词性:动词

词源: From Old French *imprester, from Latin imprimere "to press into, imprint," from in- "in" + premere "to press."
