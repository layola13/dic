interceder:/ˌɪntərˈsiːdər/| n.|调解人，说情者，仲裁者|Someone who intervenes on behalf of another, especially by pleading.  A person who acts as a mediator or peacemaker.|例句：She acted as an interceder between the warring factions. 她充当了交战派别之间的调解人。

例句：The lawyer served as an interceder for his client, asking the judge for leniency.  律师为他的客户求情，请求法官宽大处理。|近义词：mediator, advocate, negotiator, intermediary|反义词：opponent, adversary|词性: Noun
