bubbled:/ˈbʌbəld/| v. |冒泡；发出冒泡的声音；洋溢着（某种感情）；|The champagne bubbled in the glass. 香槟在杯子里冒泡。|近义词：fizzed, effervesced, frothed, overflowed (with emotion)|反义词:flattened, subsided|词性:动词

Etymology: From Middle English *bublen, from Old English *bublian, frequentative of *būgan (“to bend, bow, stoop”)
