digitally:/ˈdɪdʒɪtəli/
adjective
数字式地；数位地
in a way that involves or relates to digital technology and methods.
例句：The information is stored digitally. 信息以数字方式存储。

例句：The image is digitally enhanced. 图像经过数字增强。

synonym: electronically, computationally, cybernetically
antonym: analogically, manually

词性: adjective
