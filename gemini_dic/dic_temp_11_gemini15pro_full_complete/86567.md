helicoidal:/ˌhɛlɪˈkɔɪdəl/| adj.|螺旋形的，螺旋状的|Having the form or shape of a helix; spiral.|The DNA molecule has a helicoidal structure. DNA分子具有螺旋结构。|spiral, helical, corkscrew|straight, linear|adjective

helicoid:/ˈhɛlɪkɔɪd/| n.|螺旋面，螺旋体|A surface generated by a straight line moving in such a way that every point on the line traces a helix.|The architect designed a roof in the shape of a helicoid. 建筑师设计了一个螺旋形的屋顶。|spiral, helix|plane, flat|noun
