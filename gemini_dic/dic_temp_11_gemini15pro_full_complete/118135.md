menschy:/ˈmen(t)ʃi/| adj.|正直的；高尚的；体面的；有情有义的|Having admirable human qualities; honorable, decent.  A mensch is someone who is considered to be a person of integrity and honor.|He did the menschy thing and returned the lost wallet with all the cash inside. 他做了件高尚的事，把丢失的钱包连同里面的所有现金都归还了。|近义词：decent, honorable, upright, noble, kind, compassionate|反义词：dishonorable, ignoble, mean, selfish, cruel|词性:形容词

词源:
From Yiddish מענטש‎ (mentsh), from Middle High German mensch (human being), from Old High German mennisco (human being), from Proto-Germanic *manniskō (human being), diminutive of *mann- (man). Cognate with German Mensch (human being).
