caron:/ˈkærən/ | n. | 变音符号，抑扬符 | A diacritical mark (ˇ) placed over certain letters (especially vowels) to indicate a pronunciation different from that of the unmarked letter. |  The letter "š" has a caron. 字母"š"有一个抑扬符。| diacritical mark, accent |  | noun
