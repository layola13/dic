clough:/klʌf/ | n. |峡谷，溪谷；山谷中的陡坡 |A deep, narrow valley, especially one with steep sides.| We hiked through the clough, admiring the towering cliffs. 我们徒步穿越峡谷，欣赏高耸的悬崖。|近义词：ravine, gorge, gully, canyon |反义词:plain, plateau |词性:名词

clough:/klʌf/ | n. |（尤指英格兰北部）排水沟，涵洞 |A culvert or drain, especially in northern England. |The clough overflowed after the heavy rain. 大雨过后，涵洞溢出来了。|近义词：culvert, drain, ditch |反义词:  (没有明确的反义词) |词性:名词

词源: From Old English *clōh (attested only in place names), from Proto-Germanic *klōhaz, meaning "cliff, ravine".  Related to Dutch kloof ("cleft, ravine").
