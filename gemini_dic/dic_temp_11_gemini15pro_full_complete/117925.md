melts:/mɛlts/ | v. | 融化，熔化；溶解；消散；软化；感动 | (of a solid) becomes liquid by heat. To become softened or gentle. | The snow melts quickly in the sun. 雪在阳光下融化得很快。| synonyms: liquefies, dissolves, thaws, softens | antonyms: freezes, solidifies, hardens | verb

melt:/mɛlt/ | n. | 融化；熔化的东西 | the process of becoming liquid by heat. | The spring melt caused the river to flood. 春天的融雪导致河流泛滥。| synonyms: thawing, liquefaction, dissolving | antonyms: freezing, solidification | noun
