non-aggression:/ˌnɒn əˈɡrɛʃən/ (US) /ˌnɑːn əˈɡrɛʃən/ (UK)| n.|非侵略；不侵略；互不侵犯|The state of not attacking or being attacked, or the policy of not attacking other countries or people. |The two countries signed a non-aggression pact. 这两个国家签署了互不侵犯条约。


The UN promotes non-aggression between nations. 联合国促进各国间的互不侵犯。


近义词：peace, neutrality, pacifism|反义词:aggression, hostility, belligerence|词性:名词
