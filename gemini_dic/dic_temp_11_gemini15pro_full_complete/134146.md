nonimpositions:/ˌnɒnɪmpəˈzɪʃənz/
n.
nonimposition (的复数)
lack of burdens or obligations; refraining from imposing.
The treaty promised nonimpositions of new taxes on the citizens. 该条约承诺不会对公民征收新税。
近义词: exemptions, freedoms, liberties
反义词: impositions, obligations, burdens
词性: 名词
