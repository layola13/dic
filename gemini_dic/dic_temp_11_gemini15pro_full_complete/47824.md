decategorization:/diːˌkætɪɡərɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |去类别化，非分类化 |The process of removing or blurring the boundaries between categories. It involves challenging traditional classifications and promoting more fluid and inclusive understandings.|  The decategorization of mental illness aims to reduce stigma by focusing on individual experiences rather than diagnostic labels. 去类别化精神疾病旨在通过关注个人经历而不是诊断标签来减少污名。

Decategorization can lead to more innovative solutions by breaking down traditional silos. 去类别化可以通过打破传统的孤岛来促成更具创新性的解决方案。| 近义词: de-classification,  blurring, integration | 反义词: categorization, classification, labeling | 词性: noun
