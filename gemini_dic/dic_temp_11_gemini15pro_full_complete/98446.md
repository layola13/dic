insphere:/ɪnˈsfɪər/| n.|内切球，内球|A sphere that is tangent to every face of a polyhedron.  A sphere inscribed in a polyhedron.|The insphere of a cube touches each of the cube's six faces.  立方体的内切球与其六个面相切。|近义词: inscribed sphere|反义词: circumsphere|词性: noun
