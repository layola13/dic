catchall:/ˈkætʃɔːl/ (US), /ˈkætʃɔːl/ (UK)| n. adj.|包罗万象的; 全面性的; 杂物箱; 备用方案|A category or solution designed to include everything not specifically provided for; something designed to be inclusive or comprehensive. |例句：The "miscellaneous" category is a catchall for items that don't fit anywhere else. “杂项”类别是一个包罗不包括在其他任何地方的项目的类别。

例句：He uses his junk drawer as a catchall for loose screws, buttons, and other small items. 他用他的杂物抽屉作为散落的螺丝、纽扣和其他小物件的收集处。|近义词：general, comprehensive, inclusive, blanket, umbrella|反义词：specific, limited, restricted|词性: 名词, 形容词
