debatement:/dɪˈbeɪtmənt/ | n. | 辩论，争论 |  The act or process of debating; a discussion or argument. | The debatement over the new policy lasted for hours.  新政策的辩论持续了几个小时。 | synonyms: argument, discussion, dispute, controversy, contention | antonyms: agreement, accord, harmony, consensus | noun

debatement:/dɪˈbeɪtmənt/ | n. | 考虑，思考 | The act of thinking about or considering something carefully.  | After much debatement, she decided to accept the offer. 经过仔细考虑后，她决定接受这个提议。 | synonyms: consideration, deliberation, reflection, contemplation | antonyms: impulsiveness, recklessness | noun
