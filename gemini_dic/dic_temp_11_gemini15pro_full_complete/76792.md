gaming:/ˈɡeɪmɪŋ/| n.|游戏; 游戏产业; 赌博|The act of playing computer or video games, often competitively.  Also refers to the industry surrounding video games.|He spends hours on gaming every day. 他每天花几个小时玩游戏。|近义词:gambling (赌博), playing (玩耍)|反义词:working (工作), studying (学习)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "game" (玩游戏), which originates from Old English "gamen" (game, sport, amusement), ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*gamaną" (joy, amusement).
