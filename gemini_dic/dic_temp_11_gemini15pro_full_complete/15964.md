balsamine:/ˈbɔːlsəmɪn/ | n. | 凤仙花; 水金凤 | A flowering plant, typically with brightly colored flowers, belonging to the genus *Impatiens*.  The name comes from its seed pods, which burst open when touched, scattering the seeds. | The balsamine in my garden are blooming beautifully. 我的花园里的凤仙花开得很漂亮。| The Himalayan balsam is considered an invasive species in some areas. 喜马拉雅凤仙花在某些地区被认为是入侵物种。 | synonyms: impatiens, touch-me-not | antonyms: N/A | noun

balsam:/ˈbɔːlsəm/ | n. | 香脂; 慰藉物 | A fragrant, resinous substance obtained from certain trees and used in perfumes, medicines, etc. Also, anything soothing or restorative. | The balsam had a sweet, piney scent.  这种香脂有甜甜的松香味。 |  His kind words were a balsam to her wounded spirit. 他的亲切话语抚慰了她受伤的心灵。| synonyms: balm, solace, comfort | antonyms: irritant, aggravation | noun
