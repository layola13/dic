nonevasions:/ˌnɒnɪˈveɪʒənz/ | n. | 不逃避，不回避 |  The act of not avoiding or evading something, especially a difficult or unpleasant issue or question.  It implies a direct and honest approach. This word is likely a neologism formed by adding the prefix "non-" to "evasions."  "Evasion" itself comes from the Late Latin "evasionem," meaning "escape." |  Her nonevasions on the sensitive topic were refreshing. 她对敏感话题的直言不讳令人耳目一新。 |  directness, honesty, candor, forthrightness | evasion, avoidance, prevarication | noun
