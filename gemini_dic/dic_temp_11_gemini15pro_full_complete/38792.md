conductive:/kənˈdʌktɪv/| adj.|有益的，促成的|Tending to promote or assist; helpful.|The quiet atmosphere of the library is conducive to study. 图书馆安静的氛围有利于学习。|Synonyms: helpful, favorable, beneficial|Antonyms: hindering, detrimental, unfavorable|Adjective
