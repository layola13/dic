detasseled:/ˌdiːˈtæsəld/
|v. |去雄; 除去雄蕊;|To remove the tassels from (a plant, especially corn) to prevent self-pollination and promote cross-pollination.|The workers detasseled the corn plants by hand. 工人们手工去除了玉米植株的雄蕊。|Synonyms: emasculate, unsex |Antonyms: pollinate|Verb


detasseling:/ˌdiːˈtæsəlɪŋ/
|n. |去雄; 除去雄蕊(的行为);|The act of removing tassels from a plant, especially corn.|Detasseling is a common practice in hybrid seed corn production. 去雄是杂交玉米种子生产中的常见做法。|Synonyms: emasculation |Antonyms: pollination|Noun
