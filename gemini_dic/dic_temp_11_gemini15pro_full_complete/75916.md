funereal:/fjuːˈnɪəriəl/ /fəˈnɪriəl/| adj.|丧葬的；适合葬礼的；悲哀的；阴沉的|Suggestive of a funeral, especially in being dark, gloomy, or mournful.|例句：The music was slow and funereal.  音乐缓慢而悲哀。

例句：His funereal expression made everyone uncomfortable. 他那副悲伤的表情让每个人都感到不舒服。|近义词：mournful, somber, gloomy, doleful|反义词：joyful, cheerful, celebratory, festive|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *funerālis, from Latin *funerārius (“of or belonging to a funeral”), from *funus (“funeral rites, funeral procession, burial”).
