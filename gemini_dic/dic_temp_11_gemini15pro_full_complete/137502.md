nontraceably:/ˌnɒnˈtreɪsəbli/ | adv. | 不可追踪地 | In a way that cannot be traced or tracked. | The money was nontraceably transferred to an offshore account. 这笔钱被不可追踪地转移到一个离岸账户。 | The thief entered nontraceably, leaving no fingerprints or forced entry marks. 小偷神不知鬼不觉地进来了，没有留下指纹或强行进入的痕迹。 | synonym: anonymously, secretly, invisibly | antonym: detectably, visibly, traceably | adverb

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"
traceable (adjective): from "trace" (verb) + "-able" (suffix)
"trace" comes from Old French "tracier" meaning "to draw, sketch, track"
