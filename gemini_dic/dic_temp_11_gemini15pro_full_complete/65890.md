evolvements:/ɪˈvɒlvmənts/
n.
参与，卷入；发展，演变
the act or process of developing; the act of becoming involved in something
例句：Her evolvements in the project were numerous and varied. 她在这个项目中的参与是多方面的。

例句：The evolvements of the story are quite captivating.  这个故事的发展相当引人入胜。

近义词：involvement, development, evolution, progression
反义词：stagnation, regression, withdrawal
词性: 名词
