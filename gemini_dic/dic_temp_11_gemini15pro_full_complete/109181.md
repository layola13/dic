legitimized:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmaɪzd/
v. | 使合法化，使正当化 | To make something legal or acceptable; to justify something. | The government legitimized the use of force. 政府将使用武力合法化。 | legalized, validated, justified, authorized |  illegalized, outlawed, condemned, prohibited | verb

legitimate:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmət/
adj. | 合法的，正当的 | Conforming to the law or to rules; justifiable. |  He had a legitimate reason for being late. 他迟到是有正当理由的。 | legal, valid, lawful, justifiable | illegal, invalid, unlawful, unjustifiable | adjective

legitimacy:/ləˈdʒɪtɪməsi/
n. | 合法性，正当性 | The quality or state of being legitimate. |  The legitimacy of the election results is being questioned. 选举结果的合法性受到质疑。 | legality, validity, lawfulness | illegality, invalidity, unlawfulness | noun
