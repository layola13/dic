coarsish:/ˈkɔːrsɪʃ/| adj.|粗糙的，粗俗的|Somewhat coarse; slightly rough or crude in texture, appearance, or manner.|The fabric felt coarsish against her skin.  这种布料贴着她的皮肤感觉粗糙。|近义词：rough, crude, coarse, unrefined|反义词：smooth, fine, refined, delicate|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From coarse + -ish.  Coarse from Middle English cors, from Old French cors, from Latin cursus, past participle of currere "to run."  The suffix -ish means "somewhat" or "rather."
