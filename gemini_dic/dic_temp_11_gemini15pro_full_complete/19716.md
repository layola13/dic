biliousnesses:/ˈbɪliəsnəsɪz/
n.
恶心，呕吐; 脾气暴躁
a state of nausea and vomiting, or of irritability and bad temper.
He suffered from biliousnesses after eating too much rich food. 他吃了太多油腻的食物后感到恶心。
She was prone to biliousnesses when she was tired. 她疲倦的时候容易发脾气。
Synonyms: nausea, vomiting, queasiness, sickness, irritability, irascibility, bad temper, grumpiness
Antonyms: calmness, good humor, cheerfulness
noun


Etymology:
From bilious + -ness.  "Bilious" comes from Late Latin biliosus "pertaining to bile," from Latin bilis "bile." The connection to bad temper arises from the ancient belief that an excess of bile caused irritability.
