complicant:/kəmˈplaɪənt/ | adj. | 顺从的，服从的；依从的；合规的 | disposed to agree with others or obey rules; acquiescent | The company expects its employees to be compliant with all regulations.  这家公司希望其员工遵守所有规章制度。 |  The compliant child always did what he was told.  这个顺从的孩子总是按照吩咐去做。| 近义词: obedient, submissive, yielding, docile | 反义词: rebellious, defiant, noncompliant, resistant | 词性: adjective

complicant:/kəmˈpleɪnənt/ | n. |  申诉人；投诉人；原告 | a person who makes a formal complaint about something, especially to a court | The complainant alleged that the company had discriminated against her. 申诉人声称该公司歧视她。| The complainant filed a lawsuit against the defendant. 原告对被告提起诉讼。| 近义词: plaintiff, accuser, petitioner | 反义词: defendant, respondent | 词性: noun
