junglist:/ˈdʒʌŋɡlɪst/| n.|丛林音乐爱好者；丛林音乐家|A person who is a fan of or makes jungle music, a genre of electronic music characterized by fast breakbeats.|例句：The junglist danced energetically to the fast-paced music.  这位丛林音乐爱好者随着快节奏的音乐精力充沛地跳舞。

例句：Many junglists appreciate the complex rhythms and layered sounds of the genre. 许多丛林音乐爱好者欣赏这种音乐类型的复杂节奏和多层次的声音。|近义词：raver, electronic music fan|反义词:(No direct opposite. Could consider fans of opposing music genres)|词性:noun
