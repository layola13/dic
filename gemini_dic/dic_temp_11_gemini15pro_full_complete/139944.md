odontalgia:/ˌoʊdɑːnˈtælʤə/ | n. | 牙痛 | Toothache; pain in a tooth. |例句：He suffered from severe odontalgia. 他患有严重的牙痛。| 例句: The odontalgia kept her awake all night. 牙痛让她整夜无法入睡。 | 近义词：toothache | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 "dental health" 牙齿健康) | 词性: noun

词源:  From Modern Latin *odontalgia, from Ancient Greek ὀδονταλγία (odontalgía), from ὀδούς (odoús, “tooth”) + ἄλγος (álgos, “pain”).
