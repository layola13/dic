multidisciplinary:/ˌmʌltiˈdɪsəplɪnəri/
|adj.|多学科的，跨学科的|Combining or involving several academic disciplines or professional specializations.|例句：The university offers a multidisciplinary program in environmental studies. 这所大学提供环境研究的多学科课程。

例句：This research project requires a multidisciplinary approach, involving experts from various fields. 这个研究项目需要多学科方法，涉及各个领域的专家。|近义词：interdisciplinary, cross-disciplinary|反义词：unidisciplinary, specialized|词性: adjective
