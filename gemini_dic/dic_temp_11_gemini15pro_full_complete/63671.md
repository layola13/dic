eparterial:/ˌɛpɑːrˈtɪəriəl/
adjective
动脉上的；位于动脉上的
Situated on or above an artery.
The eparterial bronchus is a branch of the right main bronchus that passes above the pulmonary artery. 右上叶支气管是右主支气管的一个分支，它经过肺动脉的上方。
Synonyms: supra-arterial
Antonyms: subarterial, infra-arterial
词性: adjective
