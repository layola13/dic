irking:/ˈɜːrkɪŋ/| adj.|令人恼火的，使人烦躁的|Annoying; irritating.  Something that is irking is causing slight annoyance or irritation.|Her constant complaining was irking him. 她不停的抱怨让他很恼火。|近义词：annoying, irritating, vexing, bothersome|反义词：soothing, calming, pleasing, delightful|adjective
