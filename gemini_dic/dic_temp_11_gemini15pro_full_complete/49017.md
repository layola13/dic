degradingness:/dɪˈɡreɪdɪŋnɪs/ | n. | 贬低; 堕落; 降格 | The state or quality of being degrading; humiliation. |  The degradingness of the situation made her cry.  这种情况的侮辱性让她哭了。 | 近义词: humiliation, debasement, degradation | 反义词: elevation, ennoblement | 词性: 名词

词源: From degrading + -ness.  Degrading comes from the Latin degradare, meaning "to step down."
