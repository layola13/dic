compels:/kəmˈpɛlz/ | v. |引起，激起（某种感情）| to evoke a particular feeling or reaction |Her performance compelled the audience to give her a standing ovation. 她的表演博得了观众的起立鼓掌。|近义词：elicit, evoke, provoke |反义词: suppress, stifle |verb
