overbriefs: /ˌoʊvərˈbriːfs/
verb
过度指示；提供过多的指示
To instruct or inform someone excessively, often to the point of confusion or inefficiency.
The lawyer overbriefed his client, causing him to become overwhelmed and unsure of how to proceed. 律师过度地指导了他的客户，导致他不知所措，不知道该如何进行。
The director overbriefed the actors, stifling their creativity and making the performance feel rigid. 导演过度地指导了演员，扼杀了他们的创造力，使表演显得僵硬。
近义词: overinstruct, overcoach
反义词: underbrief, underinstruct
词性: verb
