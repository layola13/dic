laryngotome: /ˌlærɪŋˈɡɒtəʊm/ (UK), /ˌlærɪŋˈɡɑːtətoʊm/ (US)
noun
喉刀
A surgical instrument used to make an incision into the larynx, typically during an emergency tracheotomy.
The surgeon used a laryngotome to quickly open the patient's airway.
Synonyms: tracheotome
Antonyms: None (It's a specific surgical instrument, no direct opposite.)
词性: noun
