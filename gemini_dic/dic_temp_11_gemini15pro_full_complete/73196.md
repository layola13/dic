force-feeding:/fɔːrs ˈfiːdɪŋ/ | n. | 强行喂食 | The act of forcing someone to eat, often against their will. |  The farmer resorted to force-feeding the geese to produce foie gras.  农民为了生产鹅肝酱而强行给鹅喂食。| Synonyms: gavage, forced feeding | Antonyms: voluntary eating, self-feeding | noun

force-feed:/fɔːrs ˈfiːd/ | vt. | 强行喂食 | To make someone eat, typically against their will. |  The nurses had to force-feed the patient who refused to eat.  护士们不得不强行给拒绝进食的病人喂食。| Synonyms: stuff, cram, gorge | Antonyms: allow to eat freely, respect appetite | verb
