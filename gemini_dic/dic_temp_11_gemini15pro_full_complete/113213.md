lusciousness:/ˈlʌʃəsnəs/ | n. |美味，甘美，丰饶，性感|The state of being luscious; deliciousness, richness, or sensuality. |The lusciousness of the ripe peaches was irresistible.  成熟桃子的美味让人无法抗拒。|近义词：deliciousness, richness, sweetness, succulence, luxuriance |反义词：blandness, dryness, insipidity| noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From luscious + -ness.  Luscious comes from Middle English *luscious, from Old French *lucius "delicious," perhaps from a lost Vulgar Latin word *luxuriosus, from Latin luxuriosus "luxurious, abundant," from luxuria "luxury, abundance."
