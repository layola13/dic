colonializes:/kəˈloʊnɪˌlaɪzɪz/
|v.|殖民化，将…变成殖民地|to establish a colony in; to settle; to take control of a place or people and make it a colony. |European powers colonialized much of Africa in the 19th century. 欧洲列强在19世纪殖民了非洲的大部分地区。|Synonyms: conquer, settle, occupy, subjugate |Antonyms: liberate, free, decolonize|verb
