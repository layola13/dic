bandage:/ˈbændɪdʒ/| n. vt.|绷带，包扎|A strip of material used to bind up a wound; the act of applying such a strip. |He wrapped a bandage around his injured ankle. 他在受伤的脚踝上缠了一条绷带。  She bandaged his arm expertly. 她熟练地包扎了他的手臂。|近义词：dressing, wrap|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性: Noun, Verb
