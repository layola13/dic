fangot:/ˈfæŋɡɒt/| n.|  钱币（尤指旧时印度、巴基斯坦等地使用的钱币）; 少量钱; |A fangot was a unit of currency, especially a gold coin, used in parts of India and Pakistan.  It was also used to refer to a small amount of money. |He paid a few fangots for the trinket. 他花了几个方戈特买那个小饰品。|Synonyms: coin, bit, trifle |Antonyms: fortune, wealth |noun
