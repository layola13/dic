bipennated:/baɪˈpɛneɪtɪd/
adjective
双羽状的；二回羽状的
Having leaflets or pinnae arranged on each side of a common axis, like a feather.  Resembling a feather with barbs arranged on either side of the shaft.
The bipennated muscle allows for greater force production.  这块双羽状肌肉可以产生更大的力量。
The fern's bipennated fronds created a delicate, lacy pattern.  这种蕨类植物的双羽状叶子构成了精致的花边图案。
synonyms: bipinnate, twice-pinnate
antonyms: simple, unbranched
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *bipennatus*  (having two wings/feathers), from *bi-* (two) + *pennatus* (feathered, winged),  from *penna* (feather, wing).
