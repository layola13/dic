blackjacked:/ˌblækˈdʒækt/ | v. |敲诈；以武力威胁；胁迫|To extort or coerce someone, often using threats or violence.| He felt blackjacked into accepting the deal. 他感觉自己被迫接受了这笔交易。| The store owner was blackjacked by a local gang.  店主被当地一个帮派敲诈了。| synonyms: coerce, extort, intimidate, force, compel | antonyms: persuade, encourage, coax | verb

blackjacked:/ˌblækˈdʒækt/ | adj. |被敲诈的；被胁迫的|Feeling or being extorted or coerced, often through threats or violence.|He felt utterly blackjacked after they threatened to reveal his secret.  他们威胁要揭露他的秘密后，他感到完全被胁迫了。| The blackjacked businessman had no choice but to pay the ransom. 被敲诈的商人别无选择，只能支付赎金。| synonyms: coerced, extorted, intimidated, forced, compelled | antonyms: willing, voluntary, unforced | adjective
