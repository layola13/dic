maunderer:/ˈmɔːndərər/ (US) /ˈmɔːndərə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 闲聊者；絮叨者；漫步者 | Someone who talks in a rambling and often vague or complaining way.  A person who wanders aimlessly. | He was a known maunderer, often losing his train of thought mid-sentence. 他是个出了名的絮叨者，经常说到一半就忘了自己要说什么。

The old maunderer spent his days wandering through the park.  这位絮叨的老人整天在公园里闲逛。| 近义词: rambler, driveler, chatterer, wanderer | 反义词:  concise speaker, listener | 词性: noun
