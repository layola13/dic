jessant:/ˈdʒɛsənt/
adjective
突出; 冲出; 生长出
springing out; issuing forth
The shield bears three fleurs-de-lis jessant-de-lis.  盾牌上有三朵突出百合花的百合花饰。
Synonym:  issuing, springing, emerging
Antonym:  receding, withdrawing
词性: 形容词
