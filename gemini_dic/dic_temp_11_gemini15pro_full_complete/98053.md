inputer:/ˈɪnpʊtər/| n.|输入者，输入器|A person or device that enters data or information into a computer or other system. |Example: The inputer typed the data into the spreadsheet. 输入者将数据输入到电子表格中。|Example: The barcode scanner acts as an inputer for the inventory system. 条形码扫描器充当库存系统的输入器。|近义词:encoder, keyer, typist|反义词:outputer|noun
