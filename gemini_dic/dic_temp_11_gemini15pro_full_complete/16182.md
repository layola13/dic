bankrolling: /ˈbæŋkˌroʊlɪŋ/
|v.|资助，为…提供资金|The millionaire is bankrolling the new theatre.  这位百万富翁正在资助新剧院。|英文解析: To provide the money needed for something, especially a large project or venture.|例句：He bankrolled the entire expedition. 他资助了整个探险。|例句：The project is being bankrolled by a wealthy investor. 这个项目由一位富有的投资者资助。|近义词：financing, funding, sponsoring, subsidizing|反义词：defunding|词性:动词

bankroll: /ˈbæŋkˌroʊl/
|n.|资金，资助|The project requires a substantial bankroll. 这个项目需要大量的资金。|英文解析: The money needed to fund a project or venture.|例句：They lost their entire bankroll in the stock market crash. 他们在股市崩盘中损失了全部资金。|例句：She secured a bankroll for her startup from a venture capital firm. 她从一家风险投资公司获得了创业资金。|近义词：funds, capital, financing, stake|反义词：debt|词性:名词
