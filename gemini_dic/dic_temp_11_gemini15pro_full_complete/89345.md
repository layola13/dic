homological:/ˌhɒməˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ /ˌhoʊməˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/| adj.|同源的，同调的|Relating to or exhibiting homology; having the same relative position, value, or structure.|例句：The homological structure of the two proteins suggests a common ancestor. 这两种蛋白质的同源结构表明它们有一个共同的祖先。|近义词：analogous, similar, corresponding|反义词：heterologous, dissimilar, unrelated|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Greek 'homologos' (agreeing, corresponding) + '-ical' (suffix forming adjectives). 'Homologos' is formed from 'homos' (same) + 'logos' (word, reason, proportion).
