hamburger:/ˈhæmbɜːrɡər/ (US) /ˈhæmbɜːɡə(r)/ (UK)| n. |汉堡包|A sandwich consisting of a cooked patty of ground meat, usually beef, placed in a sliced bun or bread roll. Various condiments such as lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and cheese may be added.| I ate a hamburger for lunch. 我午饭吃了个汉堡包。|近义词：burger, patty|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


hamburger:/ˈhæmbɜːrɡər/ (US) /ˈhæmbɜːɡə(r)/ (UK)| adj. (less common) |汉堡肉的，与汉堡有关的|Relating to or containing hamburger meat.|  hamburger steak 汉堡牛排|近义词：ground beef (for the meat itself)|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective
