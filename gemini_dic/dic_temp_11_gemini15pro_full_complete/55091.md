disrespectful:/ˌdɪsrɪˈspektfəl/| adj.|不尊重的，失礼的|Showing a lack of respect or courtesy.|例句：His disrespectful behavior towards his elders was unacceptable. 他对长辈的不尊重行为是不可接受的。

例句：It's disrespectful to interrupt someone while they are speaking. 打断别人说话是不礼貌的。|近义词：rude, impolite, insolent, impertinent|反义词：respectful, polite, courteous|词性:形容词
