doodles:/ˈduːdlz/| n.|涂鸦，胡写乱画|Meaning: absentminded scribbles or drawings, often done while preoccupied with something else.  These can be simple shapes, patterns, or more elaborate designs.|例句：He covered the margins of his notebook with doodles. 他在笔记本的空白处画满了涂鸦。

例句：She doodles during meetings to help her concentrate.  她在开会时涂鸦以帮助自己集中注意力。|近义词：scribbles, sketches, drawings|反义词:masterpiece, artwork|词性:noun (plural)
