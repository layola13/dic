hydrophobias:/ˌhaɪdrəˈfoʊbiəz/
n.
狂犬病；恐水症；憎水性
An abnormal or unnatural dread of water.  A morbid dread of water. Also, rabies.
Example 1:  The dog exhibited classic symptoms of hydrophobias, including foaming at the mouth and an aversion to water. 这只狗表现出狂犬病的典型症状，包括口吐白沫和厌水。

Example 2:  His hydrophobias prevented him from even taking a bath. 他的恐水症让他甚至无法洗澡。
Synonym:  aquaphobia, rabies (for the disease)
Antonym:  hydrophilia (for the chemical property)
词性: 名词 (plural of hydrophobia)
