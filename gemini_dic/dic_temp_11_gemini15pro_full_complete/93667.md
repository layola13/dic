imbuing:/ɪmˈbjuːɪŋ/ | v. | 灌输，使充满 | to inspire or permeate with a feeling or quality |  He imbued his followers with confidence. 他使他的追随者充满了信心。 |  instilling, infusing, permeating, impregnating | draining, divesting | verb

imbued:/ɪmˈbjuːd/ | adj. | 充满...的 | saturated, filled | The speech was imbued with a sense of hope. 演讲充满了希望。| infused, permeated, steeped | devoid, lacking | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *imbuen*, from Old French *embuer*, from *en-* (in) + *buer* (to drink, soak), from Latin *bibere* (to drink).
