bunkum:/ˈbʌŋkəm/
bunkums:/ˈbʌŋkəmz/
noun
废话，胡说，空话
Nonsense; empty talk.
That politician's speech was pure bunkum.  那个政客的演讲纯属胡扯。
synonyms: nonsense, rubbish, claptrap, hogwash, balderdash
antonyms: truth, fact, reality
词性: 名词
