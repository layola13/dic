denotive:/dɪˈnəʊtɪv/| adj.|指示的，表示的，外延的|Expressing or signifying something clearly and directly; relating to denotation (the literal or primary meaning of a word, in contrast to the feelings or ideas that the word suggests).|The word "home" has a denotive meaning of a place where one lives, but it also has connotative meanings of comfort and family.|近义词：indicative, signifying, expressive, explicit|反义词：connotative, suggestive, implicit|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Late Latin *dēnōtātīvus, from Latin dēnōtāre (“to mark, indicate”), from dē- (“down, completely”) + notāre (“to mark”).
