cauterizes:/ˈkɔːtəraɪzɪz/
verb
烧灼；烙
burns (a wound or snakebite) with a hot iron or chemical to stop bleeding and prevent infection.
The doctor cauterized the wound to stop the bleeding. 医生烧灼伤口以止血。
cauterize, sear, burn
heal, soothe
v.
