obstupefaction:/ˌɒbstjuːpɪˈfækʃən/ | n. | 昏迷；麻木；惊愕 | The news of his sudden death left us in a state of obstupefaction.  他突然去世的消息让我们感到震惊和麻木。|  A feeling of profound obstupefaction overcame her as she realized the extent of the damage. 当她意识到损失的程度时，一种深深的惊愕感涌上了她的心头。 | 近义词：stupor, daze, bewilderment, astonishment | 反义词：alertness, awareness, consciousness | 词性: 名词

词源:  Late 16th century: from French *obstupéfaction*, from Latin obstupēfact- ‘stupefied’, from the verb obstupēscere, from ob- ‘against’ + stupēscere ‘be amazed’.
