outcreeped:/aʊtˈkriːpt/
verb
蔓延出，悄悄地出现
To creep out or emerge slowly and stealthily.
The shadows outcreeped from the corners of the room as the sun began to set.  随着太阳开始落山，阴影从房间的角落里悄悄地蔓延出来。
The vines had outcreeped across the entire wall. 藤蔓已经悄悄地爬满了整面墙。
近义词: spread, extended, emerged
反义词: receded, retreated, withdrawn
词性: verb
