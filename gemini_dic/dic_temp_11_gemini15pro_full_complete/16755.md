baru: /ˈbaruː/ (Indonesian)
词性: adjective
中文翻译: 新的,最近的,刚刚
英文解析: new, recent, just
例句:
Saya membeli mobil baru. = I bought a new car.

Rumah ini baru dibangun. = This house was just built.


近义词:  fresh, novel, modern, latest
反义词: old, ancient, outdated, used
词性: adjective
