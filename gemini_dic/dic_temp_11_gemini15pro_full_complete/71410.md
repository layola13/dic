flail:/fleɪl/| n. v. |连枷，打谷机；乱动，挥动|A tool for threshing grain, consisting of a wooden staff with a short, heavy stick swinging from it. To wave or swing wildly.|The farmer used a flail to separate the wheat from the chaff. 农民用连枷把小麦和谷壳分开。|He flailed his arms wildly, trying to keep his balance. 他疯狂地挥舞着手臂，试图保持平衡。|近义词：thrash, beat, swing, wave|反义词:control, steady|名词, 动词

flail:/fleɪl/| v. |(of a person or their limbs) move or swing wildly.|To move or swing your arms or legs in a wild and uncontrolled way.|The drowning man flailed desperately in the water. 溺水的人在水中拼命挣扎。|  She flailed her arms, trying to swat the bee. 她挥舞着手臂，试图拍打蜜蜂。|近义词：thrash, struggle, flounder|反义词:control, be still|动词
