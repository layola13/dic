icterus:/ˈɪktərəs/
noun
黄疸
a medical condition in which the skin and the whites of the eyes become yellow because of increased levels of bile in the blood
The patient presented with fever and icterus.  病人出现发烧和黄疸症状。

synonym
jaundice
antonym
(No direct antonym for a medical condition.  One could consider "healthy liver function" a contrasting state.)
