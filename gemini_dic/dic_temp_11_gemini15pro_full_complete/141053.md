only-so-much:/ˈoʊnli ˈsoʊ ˈmʌtʃ/ | adj. phrase | 有限的，只有这么多的 |  Used to emphasize that there is a limited amount of something, especially time, money, or patience.  This phrase suggests a restriction or limitation on a resource or capacity. It often implies that despite efforts or desires, there's a cap on what's available or achievable. | I only have so much time to dedicate to this project. 我只有这么多时间可以投入到这个项目中。

| There's only so much money we can spend. 我们只有这么多钱可以花。 | Synonyms: limited, restricted, finite, a certain amount | Antonyms: unlimited, infinite, boundless | phrase
