externalized:/ɪkˈstɜːrnəlaɪzd/ /ɛkˈstɝːnəˌlaɪzd/| adj. v. (past participle)|外化的，体现出来的|To express (a thought or feeling) outwardly:|She externalized her anger by yelling. 她通过大喊大叫来发泄她的愤怒。|externalise (British), express, manifest, vent|internalize, repress|adjective, verb
Etymology->
From externalize + -ed, from external + -ize. Ultimately from Latin externus.
