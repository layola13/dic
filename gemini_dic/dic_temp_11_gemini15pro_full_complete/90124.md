It often implies a casual and intimate gathering.|We had a great hot-tubbing session last night with some friends. 昨晚我们和一些朋友在热水浴缸里度过了愉快的时光。|Synonyms: soaking, jacuzziing, chilling in a hot tub|Antonyms: N/A|noun
