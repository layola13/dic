indignantly:/ɪnˈdɪɡnəntli/| adv.|愤慨地，义愤填膺地|In a manner expressing strong displeasure at something considered unjust, offensive, insulting, or base.|She indignantly denied the accusations. 她愤慨地否认了这些指控。|Synonyms: angrily, furiously, wrathfully|Antonyms: calmly, patiently, placidly|adverb

indignant:/ɪnˈdɪɡnənt/| adj.|愤慨的，义愤填膺的|Feeling or showing anger or annoyance at what is perceived as unfair treatment.|He was indignant at the suggestion that he had cheated. 他对有人暗示他作弊感到愤慨。|Synonyms: angry, annoyed, resentful|Antonyms: pleased, happy, content|adjective
