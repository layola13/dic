blackfin:/ˈblækˌfɪn/| n. |黑鳍鱼；黑鳍|A type of fish characterized by its dark fins.  A dark-finned tuna, especially *Thunnus atlanticus*, found in warm seas.|例句：The fishermen caught a large blackfin tuna. 渔民捕获了一条大型黑鳍金枪鱼。

例句：Blackfin tuna are prized for their firm, flavorful flesh. 黑鳍金枪鱼因其肉质紧实、味道鲜美而备受推崇。|近义词：tuna, bonito|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific fish species) |词性:noun
