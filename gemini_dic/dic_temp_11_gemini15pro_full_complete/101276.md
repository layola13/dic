involuntariness:/ɪnˌvɒlənˈtɛrɪnɪs/ | n. |非自愿性，无意识 |The state or quality of being involuntary; done without will or conscious control. |His hand trembled with involuntariness. 他的手不由自主地颤抖。|Synonyms: automaticity, reflex, spontaneity, instinct |Antonyms: voluntariness, volition, conscious control |noun
