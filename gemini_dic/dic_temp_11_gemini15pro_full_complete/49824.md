demonstration:/ˌdemənˈstreɪʃn/ | n. | 示范，演示；游行示威；证明；表露 | a display or explanation of how something works or is done; a public march or gathering to express political views; proof; an outward expression of a quality or feeling |  The teacher gave a demonstration of the experiment. 老师演示了这个实验。| synonyms: display, exhibition, presentation, protest, march, proof, evidence | antonyms: concealment, suppression | noun

demonstration:/ˌdemənˈstreɪʃn/ | n. |示威;游行抗议 | a public meeting or march at which people show that they are protesting against or supporting somebody/something |  They held a demonstration against the new law. 他们举行示威反对新法律。| synonyms: protest, march, rally | antonyms: support, approval | noun
