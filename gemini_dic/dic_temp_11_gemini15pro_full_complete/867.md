abstruse:/əbˈstruːs/ /æbˈstruːs/| adj.|深奥的，难懂的|Difficult to understand; obscure.|例句：The professor's lectures were so abstruse that many students struggled to follow them. 教授的讲座太深奥了，很多学生都听不懂。

例句：The philosophical concepts were abstruse and difficult for the average person to grasp. 这些哲学概念深奥难懂，普通人难以理解。|近义词：obscure, esoteric, recondite, complex, complicated|反义词：clear, simple, lucid, easy, straightforward|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *abstrusus, past participle of abstrūdere (“to conceal, hide”), from ab- (“away from”) + trūdere (“to thrust, push”).
