obstructionist:/əbˈstrʌkʃənɪst/
n.|妨碍者，阻挠者|a person who deliberately obstructs or delays progress, especially in business or politics. |The senator gained a reputation as an obstructionist, constantly blocking legislation proposed by the opposing party.  这位参议员因不断阻挠反对党提出的立法而获得了阻挠者的名声。|opponent, antagonist, blocker|supporter, advocate|noun

obstructionist:/əbˈstrʌkʃənɪst/
adj.|阻挠的，妨碍的|characterized by obstruction or delaying tactics. |Their obstructionist tactics stalled the project for months. 他们的阻挠策略使项目停滞了数月。|hindering, delaying, impeding|helpful, cooperative|adjective
