malashaganay:/ˌmæləˈʃɑːɡənˌeɪ/ | n. | 玛拉沙甘尼（菲律宾一种硬木，用于制作家具和乐器）|  A type of hardwood tree native to the Philippines, used for making furniture and musical instruments. Its scientific name is *Shorea negrosensis*. | This guitar is made of malashaganay. 这把吉他是用玛拉沙甘尼木做的。| synonym: Philippine mahogany, red lauan | antonym: N/A | noun
