nontribal:/ˌnɑːnˈtraɪbəl/
adjective
非部落的，非部族的
Not belonging to or characteristic of a tribe or tribes.
The archaeologist specialized in nontribal societies of the ancient world.  这位考古学家专门研究古代世界的非部落社会。
The museum's collection includes nontribal artifacts from various periods.  博物馆的藏品包括来自不同时期的非部落文物。
synonyms: non-tribal (variant), civilized, modern
antonyms: tribal, indigenous, aboriginal
