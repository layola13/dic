outsider:/ˌaʊtˈsaɪdər/ | n. |局外人；外行；圈外人| a person who does not belong to a particular group |  He felt like an outsider at the exclusive club. 他在那家高级俱乐部里感觉像个局外人。| synonyms: stranger, alien, nonmember | antonyms: insider, member, native | noun

outsider:/ˌaʊtˈsaɪdər/ | n. |（尤指赛马或赛狗比赛中）不被看好者；冷门选手|  a person or animal that is not expected to win a competition, especially a horse or dog in a race | The outsider surprisingly won the race.  这匹不被看好的马竟然赢得了比赛。| synonyms: underdog, long shot, dark horse | antonyms: favorite, front-runner | noun
