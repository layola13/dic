counterinstitutions:/ˈkaʊntərˌɪnstɪˈtuːʃənz/ | n. | 对抗性机构，反建制机构 | Organizations that are formed in opposition to existing institutions, often with the aim of challenging or replacing them. They typically offer alternative ways of organizing social, economic, or political life. |  Example: The activists established counterinstitutions to provide social services the government failed to deliver.  活动家们建立了对抗性机构，以提供政府未能提供的社会服务。

Example:  Many historical counterinstitutions, like alternative schools and free clinics, eventually became mainstream. 许多历史上的对抗性机构，如另类学校和免费诊所，最终成为主流。 | Near Synonyms: alternative institutions, oppositional organizations, parallel structures | Antonyms: mainstream institutions, established institutions | Noun
