finely:/ˈfaɪnli/| adv.|精细地，细致地，微小地，美好地|In a fine manner; minutely; exquisitely; beautifully.  It describes something done with great care, precision, or refinement.|The fabric was finely woven. 这块布料织得很精细。|近义词：delicately, exquisitely, precisely, subtly|反义词：coarsely, roughly, crudely|词性:副词
词源: From Middle English *finely, derived from fine + -ly.  Ultimately from Latin *finis* (end, boundary, limit).
