masterwork:/ˈmæstərˌwɜːrk/ (US), /ˈmɑːstəˌwɜːk/ (UK)| n.|杰作；名著|A piece of work, such as painting, film, book or music, of outstanding artistry and craftsmanship.|例句：The Sistine Chapel ceiling is widely considered to be Michelangelo's masterwork. 西斯廷教堂的天花板被广泛认为是米开朗琪罗的杰作。

例句：This symphony is regarded as the composer's masterwork. 这部交响曲被认为是这位作曲家的杰作。|近义词：masterpiece, classic, chef-d'œuvre, magnum opus|反义词:failure, flop, disaster|词性:名词
