incorruptibility:/ˌɪnkəˌrʌptɪˈbɪləti/| n.| 不可腐败性，廉洁，正直|The quality of being honest and having moral principles that cannot be corrupted.  It refers to a steadfast adherence to principles and resistance to bribery or other forms of unethical influence.|His incorruptibility made him an ideal candidate for the position. 他的廉洁使他成为该职位的理想人选。|Synonyms: honesty, integrity, probity, rectitude, uprightness|Antonyms: corruption, dishonesty, venality, unscrupulousness|noun
