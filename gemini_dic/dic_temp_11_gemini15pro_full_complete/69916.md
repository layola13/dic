ferulic:/fəˈruːlɪk/
adjective
阿魏酸的;  阿魏酸
Relating to or derived from ferulic acid, a crystalline acid present in various plants.
Ferulic acid is a potent antioxidant found in many plant-based foods. 阿魏酸是一种强效抗氧化剂，存在于许多植物性食物中。
Ferulic acid, found in plant cell walls, plays a role in their rigidity.  在植物细胞壁中发现的阿魏酸在其硬度中起作用。
近义词: caffeic, coumaric (related phenolic acids)
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a chemical compound)
词性: adjective
