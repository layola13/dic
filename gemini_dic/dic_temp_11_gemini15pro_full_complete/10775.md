aquaplaner:/ˈækwəˌpleɪnər/ (US), /ˈækwəˌpleɪnə/ (UK)| n. |水上滑行，滑水板; (车辆) 水漂现象|A vehicle aquaplanes when a layer of water builds up between the tires and the road surface, leading to a loss of traction.|The car aquaplaned on the wet road and spun out of control. 汽车在湿滑的路面上打滑，失控旋转。|近义词：hydroplane|反义词: N/A|名词

aquaplane:/ˈækwəˌpleɪn/ (US), /ˈækwəˌpleɪn/ (UK)| vi. |进行水上滑行，(车辆) 水漂|To slide uncontrollably on a wet surface, as a vehicle does when its tires lose contact with the road due to a layer of water.|The car aquaplaned across the flooded street. 汽车在积水的街道上打滑。|近义词：hydroplane|反义词: N/A|动词
