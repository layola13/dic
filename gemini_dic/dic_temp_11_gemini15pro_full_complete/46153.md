cynanche:/sɪˈnæŋki/ | n. | 肉苁蓉 | A parasitic plant of the genus *Cistanche*, native to arid and semi-arid regions of North Africa, the Middle East, and Asia, used in traditional Chinese medicine.  |  Cistanche deserticola, a species of cynanche, is known for its medicinal properties. 肉苁蓉的一种，荒漠肉苁蓉，以其药用价值而闻名。 | synonyms: desert hyacinth, broomrape | antonyms: N/A (no direct opposite for a plant) | noun
