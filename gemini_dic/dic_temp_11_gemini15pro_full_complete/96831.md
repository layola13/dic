inestimable:/ɪnˈɛstɪməbl/ | adj. | 无价的，无法估量的 | too valuable to be estimated or calculated |  Her knowledge and experience proved inestimable to the project. 她的知识和经验对这个项目来说是无价之宝。

Her contributions to the team were inestimable. 她对团队的贡献是无法估量的。

invaluable, priceless, precious | worthless, cheap | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English *inestimable, from Old French inestimable, from Latin inestimabilis, from in- ‘not’ + aestimare ‘to estimate, value’.
