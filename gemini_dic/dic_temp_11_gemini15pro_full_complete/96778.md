inequitate:/ɪnˈɛkwɪteɪt/
verb
不公平对待；使不平等
to treat someone unfairly or unequally
The current system inequitably distributes resources.  当前的系统不公平地分配资源。
The law inequitably favors the wealthy.  法律不公平地偏袒富人。
近义词:discriminate against, treat unfairly,  wrong, oppress
反义词:treat fairly, be just to, equalize
词性:v.

词源: From Latin *inaequitatus, from in- + aequitas (“equity”).
