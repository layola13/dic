cedars:/ˈsiːdərz/| n.|雪松（cedar的复数形式）|Plural form of cedar, referring to multiple cedar trees.|The cedars of Lebanon are famous for their size and longevity. 黎巴嫩的雪松以其大小和长寿而闻名。|近义词：conifers, evergreens|反义词:(无直接反义词，可以考虑deciduous trees 落叶树)|词性:名词

词源:
From Middle English *ceder, *cedre, from Old French cedre, from Latin cedrus, from Ancient Greek κέδρος (kédros).
