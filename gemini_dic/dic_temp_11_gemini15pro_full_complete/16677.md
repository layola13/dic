barrelling:/ˈbærəlɪŋ/ (US), /ˈbærəlɪŋ/ (UK)| v. |快速移动，冲，飞奔|Moving at high speed, typically in a noisy or uncontrolled way.  |The car was barrelling down the highway. 汽车在高速公路上飞驰。|近义词：rushing, speeding, hurtling, careening|反义词：crawling, creeping|词性:动词

barrelling:/ˈbærəlɪŋ/ (US), /ˈbærəlɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |快速移动，冲，飞奔 (名词形式较少用)|The act of moving at high speed, typically in a noisy or uncontrolled way. |The barrelling of the truck towards the intersection caused the accident. 卡车向十字路口飞奔导致了事故。|近义词：rush, dash, sprint|反义词：crawl, creep|词性:名词
