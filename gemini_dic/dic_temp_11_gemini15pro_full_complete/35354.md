co-guardian:/ˌkoʊˈɡɑːrdiən/| n.|共同监护人|A person who shares the legal responsibility for the care and upbringing of a child with another person.|例句：The child's co-guardians are her mother and grandmother.  这个孩子的共同监护人是她的母亲和祖母。

例句：The co-guardians disagreed on the child's education. 共同监护人在孩子的教育问题上意见不一致。|近义词：joint guardian, shared guardian|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
