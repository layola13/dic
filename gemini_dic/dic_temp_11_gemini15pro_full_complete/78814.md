gimcrack:/ˈɡɪmkræk/ | n. | 小玩意儿；华而不实的东西 | A showy but useless or worthless object. |  The shelves were filled with gimcracks and souvenirs. 架子上摆满了小玩意儿和纪念品。 |  Synonyms: trinket, novelty, bauble, gewgaw, trifle | Antonyms: valuable, essential, necessary | noun

gimcrack:/ˈɡɪmkræk/ | adj. | 华而不实的；便宜的 | Showy but cheap and poorly made. |  He wore a gimcrack watch encrusted with fake jewels. 他戴着一块镶嵌着假珠宝的华而不实的手表。 | Synonyms: tawdry, shoddy, cheap, flimsy | Antonyms: durable, sturdy, valuable, quality | adjective
