offensive: /əˈfɛnsɪv/ (American) /əˈfɛnsɪv/ (British)| adj.| 冒犯的；无礼的；攻击性的|Causing someone to feel resentful, upset, or annoyed; actively aggressive.|
His comments were deeply offensive to many people. 他的评论让很多人深感不快。

She launched an offensive against the government's policies. 她对政府的政策发起了攻击。


offensive: /əˈfɛnsɪv/ (American) /əˈfɛnsɪv/ (British)| n.| 进攻；攻势|An attacking military campaign.|
The army launched a major offensive against the enemy. 军队对敌人发动了大规模进攻。

They took the offensive and began to push the enemy back. 他们采取攻势，开始将敌人击退。


近义词: insulting, rude, abusive, aggressive, obnoxious, distasteful|
反义词: inoffensive, polite, respectful, defensive|
词性: adjective, noun|
词源: From Old French *offensif, from Latin offensivus (“striking against”), from offendere (“to strike against, offend”).
