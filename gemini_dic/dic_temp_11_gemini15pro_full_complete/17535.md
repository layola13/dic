beaten:/ˈbiːtn/ (US), /ˈbiːtən/ (UK)| adj.|被打败的，筋疲力尽的，(道路)常有人走的|Defeated, exhausted, (of a path) frequently used.|The beaten army retreated.  败军撤退了。|The travelers followed the beaten path through the forest.  旅行者沿着森林里常有人走的小路前进。|近义词：defeated, conquered, exhausted, tired, worn out, well-trodden|反义词：victorious, undefeated, energetic, fresh|词性:形容词

詞源->
From the Old English *bēaten*, past participle of *bēatan* ("to beat"). Cognate with Old Frisian *bēten*, Old Saxon *gislagen*, Old High German *gibizzan*, Gothic *gabugan*. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *bauaną* (“to beat, strike”).
