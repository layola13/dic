acromegaly: /ˌækrəˈmɛɡəli/ | n. | 肢端肥大症 | A hormonal disorder that develops when the pituitary gland produces too much growth hormone during adulthood. |  He was diagnosed with acromegaly after experiencing persistent headaches and enlarged hands and feet. 他在经历了持续的头痛和手脚增大的症状后被诊断出患有肢端肥大症。| Synonym: gigantism (in children), hypersomatotropism | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, could consider dwarfism as a contrasting condition, though not directly opposite) | noun
