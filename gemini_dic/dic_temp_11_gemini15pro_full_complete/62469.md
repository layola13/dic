endoscopy:/enˈdɒskəpi/ /ˌɛnˈdɑːskəpi/| n. |内窥镜检查|The examination of the interior of a body cavity or hollow organ using an endoscope.|The doctor recommended an endoscopy to investigate the cause of his stomach pain. 医生建议做内窥镜检查以查明他胃痛的原因。|近义词: laparoscopy (for abdominal cavity), colonoscopy (for colon), bronchoscopy (for lungs), cystoscopy (for bladder) |反义词:  N/A |词性: noun

endoscope:/ˈɛndəˌskop/ | n. |内窥镜|A thin, flexible tube with a light and camera attached, used to examine the inside of the body.|The endoscope was inserted through his mouth to examine his esophagus. 内窥镜通过他的嘴插入以检查他的食道。| 近义词: fiberscope |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

endoscopic:/ˌɛndəˈskɑːpɪk/ | adj. |内窥镜的|Relating to or performed using an endoscope.|The patient underwent an endoscopic procedure to remove a polyp. 病人接受了内窥镜手术切除息肉。|近义词: minimally invasive |反义词: open (surgery) |词性: adjective
