flop-down:/ˈflɒpˌdaʊn/ (US) /ˈflɒpˌdaʊn/ (UK)| adj.|可折叠的，可放下的|Designed to be folded down, typically for storage or to create more space.  A flop-down seat or table is one that can be folded down against a wall or other surface when not in use.|The camper van had a handy flop-down table.|Synonyms: fold-down, drop-down, collapsible|Antonyms: fixed, permanent|adjective

flop-down:/ˈflɒpˌdaʊn/ (US) /ˈflɒpˌdaʊn/ (UK)| n.|可折叠的物品（尤指座椅或桌子）|A seat or table that can be folded down.|The flop-down served as both a seat and a bed.|Synonyms: fold-down, drop-down|Antonyms: fixed seat, fixed table|noun
