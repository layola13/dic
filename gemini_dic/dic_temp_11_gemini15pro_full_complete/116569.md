matins:/ˈmætɪnz/| n. |晨祷，早课|A service of morning prayer, especially in the Anglican Church.|例句：The monks gathered in the chapel for matins.  僧侣们聚集在小教堂里做晨祷。

例句：She always attended matins when she was staying at the convent. 当她住在修道院时，她总是参加晨祷。|近义词：morning prayer, lauds|反义词: vespers, evensong|词性:noun
