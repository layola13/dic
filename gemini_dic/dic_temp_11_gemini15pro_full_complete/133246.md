nonexemplary:/ˌnɑːnɪɡˈzɛmpləri/ | adj. | 非典范的；非示范性的；不值得效仿的 | Not serving as a good example; not exemplary. |  This nonexemplary behavior led to his dismissal.  这种不值得效仿的行为导致他被解雇。 |  The nonexemplary product was quickly recalled.  这个不合格的产品很快被召回了。 | 近义词: atypical, unusual, substandard, poor, inadequate | 反义词: exemplary, typical, model, ideal, perfect | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (not) + "exemplary" (serving as a desirable model).  "Exemplary" comes from the Latin "exemplum" (example).
