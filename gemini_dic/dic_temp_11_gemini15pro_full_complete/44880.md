crybaby: /ˈkraɪˌbeɪbi/ | n. | 爱哭鬼，哭包 |  A person, especially a child, who cries easily or complains a lot. |  Example 1: Stop being such a crybaby and pull yourself together!  别像个爱哭鬼一样，振作起来！

Example 2:  He's a real crybaby; he cries over everything. 他真是个哭包，什么事都哭。 | 近义词: whiner, complainer, sook (Australian/NZ) | 反义词: stoic, tough guy/girl | 词性: 名词
