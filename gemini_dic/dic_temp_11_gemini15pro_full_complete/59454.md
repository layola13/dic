earwig:/ˈɪərˌwɪɡ/ (US), /ˈɪəwɪɡ/ (UK)| n.|蠼螋; 耳夹|A small, slender insect with a pair of forceps-like pincers at the end of its abdomen.  It is a common misconception that they crawl into people's ears.|例句：I found an earwig in the garden. 我在花园里发现了一只蠼螋。

|例句：She screamed when she saw an earwig. 她看到一只蠼螋时尖叫起来。|近义词：pincher bug|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
