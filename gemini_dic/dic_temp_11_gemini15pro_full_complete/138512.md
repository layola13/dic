now-now:/naʊ naʊ/ | adv. | 現在，立刻; 馬上; (尤指對兒童)別鬧了，乖 | right now; at this very moment; (used especially to a child) be good; stop crying |  Now-now, don't cry, it'll be alright.  別哭別哭，會沒事的。 | 近义词: immediately, instantly, right away | 反义词: later, eventually | 词性: 副词
