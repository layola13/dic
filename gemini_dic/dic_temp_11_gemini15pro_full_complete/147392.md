overnicely:/ˌoʊvərˈnaɪsli/| adv.|过分客气地，过分殷勤地|Excessively nicely; too politely or solicitously.  It implies an insincere or excessive display of niceness, often to the point of being irritating or suspicious.|He greeted us overnicely, making us wonder what he was hiding. 他对我们过分客气，让我们怀疑他隐瞒了什么。|近义词：excessively, too politely, overly solicitously|反义词：rudely, curtly, abruptly|词性:副词
词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessively") + nicely (adverb from nice).  "Over-"  has Germanic origins, related to Old English "ofer," while "nice" ultimately comes from Latin "nescius" (ignorant).
