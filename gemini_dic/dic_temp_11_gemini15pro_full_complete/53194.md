dipteral: /ˈdɪptərəl/ | adj. | 双列柱式的；有双排柱子的 | Having two rows of columns.  A dipteral temple has a double colonnade surrounding the naos. | The Parthenon, though not strictly dipteral, exhibits a similar grandeur.  帕特农神庙虽然并非严格意义上的双列柱式，但也展现了类似的宏伟气势。| The architect designed a dipteral temple, inspired by ancient Greek architecture.  这位建筑师设计了一座双列柱式神庙，其灵感来自古希腊建筑。 |  peripteral, double colonnaded | single colonnaded | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *dipteros* "having two wings," from *di-* "two" + *pteron* "wing."  The architectural term arises from the resemblance of the double row of columns to two wings.
