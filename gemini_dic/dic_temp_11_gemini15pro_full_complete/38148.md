compressing:/kəmˈprɛsɪŋ/ | v. |压缩，压紧| reducing the size or volume of something by applying pressure |  The machine is compressing the metal into sheets. 这台机器正在把金属压缩成薄片。 | synonyms: squeezing, squashing, compacting | antonyms: expanding, stretching, enlarging | verb (present participle)

compressing:/kəmˈprɛsɪŋ/ | adj. |压缩的| being in the process of being compressed | The compressing air creates heat. 被压缩的空气会产生热量。 | synonyms: squeezed, constricted | antonyms: expanded, decompressed | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *compressāre*, present active infinitive of *compressus*, past participle of *comprimere* ("to press together"), from *com-* ("together") + *premere* ("to press").
