ontologism:/ɒnˈtɒlədʒɪzəm/
n.
本体论主义
The philosophical theory that we have direct intuitive knowledge of God's existence.
Ontologism asserts that God is immediately known to the human intellect. 本体论主义断言，上帝是人类理智可以直接认识的。
The priest's teachings leaned heavily on ontologism, much to the chagrin of his more empirically minded colleagues.  这位牧师的教义严重依赖本体论主义，这让他那些更注重经验的同事非常懊恼。
近义词:idealism, transcendentalism
反义词:empiricism, materialism
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *ontologisme*, from *ontologie* ("ontology") + *-isme* ("-ism").  *Ontologie* from Ancient Greek ὄντος (óntos, “of being, of that which is”) (genitive singular of ὤν (ṓn, “being”)) + -λογία (-logía, “study of”).
