adversities: /ədˈvɜːrsətiz/ | n. | 逆境，困境，不幸 | difficulties; misfortune.  A state of hardship or affliction; a condition of misfortune or difficulty. |  He overcame many adversities in his life. 他一生克服了许多逆境。| synonyms: hardships, misfortunes, trials, tribulations, setbacks, difficulties | antonyms: prosperity, success, advantages, blessings | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *adversite, from Old French adversite, from Latin adversitās, from adversus (“turned against, opposite, hostile, unfavorable”), from ad (“to, towards”) + versus (“turned”), past participle of vertere (“to turn”). Doublet of adversity.
