outquibble:/ˌaʊtˈkwɪbəl/
verb
胜过诡辩；以更巧妙的诡辩驳倒
to surpass or defeat in quibbling; to argue or raise objections to a point in a petty or frivolous manner, especially to outdo another in such argumentation.
He outquibbled his opponent in the debate, winning the judges over with his clever wordplay. 他在辩论中以巧妙的文字游戏胜过了对手，赢得了评委的青睐。
The lawyer outquibbled the witness, confusing him with technicalities. 律师用专业术语驳倒了证人，使他感到困惑。
近义词: outwit, outsmart, outmaneuver
反义词: concede, yield
