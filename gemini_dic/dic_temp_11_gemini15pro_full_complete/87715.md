hexateuch:/ˈhɛksətjuːk/
n.
摩西五经（加上约书亚记）,The first six books of the Old Testament (Genesis, Exodus, Leviticus, Numbers, Deuteronomy, and Joshua).
The Hexateuch narrates the story of creation and the early history of Israel. 摩西六经讲述了创世记和以色列的早期历史。
Scholars debate the authorship and composition of the Hexateuch. 学者们对摩西六经的作者和构成进行了辩论。
近义词:Pentateuch, Torah
反义词:None
词性:Noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek ἑξάτευχος (hexáteukhos, “six-volumed”), from ἕξ (héx, “six”) + τεῦχος (teûkhos, “tool, implement, book, volume”).
