homeopathies:/ˌhoʊmiˈɑːpəθiz/ | n. |顺同疗法|A system of alternative medicine based on the principle of "like cures like," where substances that cause symptoms in healthy people are believed to cure similar symptoms in sick people, often using highly diluted preparations.|Many people turn to homeopathies for treating allergies.| 近义词:alternative medicine, complementary medicine|反义词:conventional medicine, allopathic medicine|词性:名词

Etymology: From homeo- + -pathy, from Greek homoios "similar" + pathos "suffering, feeling."
