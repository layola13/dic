malet:/ˈmælɪt/ | n. | 木槌; 小槌 | A mallet is a type of hammer, usually made of wood, rubber, or plastic, used for striking a surface without causing damage. | He used a rubber mallet to assemble the furniture. 他用橡胶槌组装家具。|  The carpenter used a mallet to tap the chisel. 木匠用木槌敲击凿子。|Synonyms: hammer, beetle, maul | Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a tool. Could consider words like "hand" if referring to direct touch) | Noun
