gossip:/ˈɡɒsɪp/ /ˈɡɑːsɪpɪŋ/| n. v.|闲话，八卦|Casual or unconstrained conversation or reports about other people, typically involving details of an intimate or scandalous nature.|She enjoys a good gossip with her friends. 她喜欢和朋友们聊八卦。|近义词：rumor, scandal, hearsay|反义词:truth, fact|词性:noun, verb
