blockaders:/blɒˈkeɪdəz/| n.|封锁者，阻塞者|People or forces that establish a blockade.  Those who prevent entry or exit of a place, typically by sea or land, often during a war or conflict.|例句：The blockaders prevented any ships from entering the port. 封锁者阻止任何船只进入港口。

例句：The blockaders were eventually forced to withdraw. 封锁者最终被迫撤退。|近义词：besiegers, obstructers|反义词: liberators, rescuers|词性:noun
