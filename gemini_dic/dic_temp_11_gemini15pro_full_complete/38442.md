concertmaster:/ˈkɑːnsərtˌmaɪstər/ (US) /ˈkɒnsətˌmaɪstə(r)/ (UK)| n. |首席小提琴手；乐队首席|The concertmaster is the principal first violinist in an orchestra and is responsible for leading the other violinists. | The concertmaster tuned the orchestra before the concert began. 首席小提琴手在音乐会开始前为乐队调音。

The concertmaster took a bow after the orchestra's impressive performance.  乐队精彩的演出结束后，首席小提琴手鞠了一躬。| 近义词：leader, principal violinist, first chair | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun


Etymology: From German *Konzertmeister*, from *Konzert* (concert) + *Meister* (master).
