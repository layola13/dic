homageable:/ˈhɒmədʒəbəl/ | adj. |值得致敬的，应受崇敬的| Deserving of homage or tribute; worthy of respect and admiration. |例句：His long and distinguished career made him homageable. 他漫长而杰出的职业生涯使他值得敬佩。

例句：The homageable veteran was honored with a parade.  这位值得尊敬的老兵受到了游行的致敬。|近义词：respectable, admirable, venerable, esteemed|反义词：disreputable, contemptible, despicable|词性: adjective

词源: From French *homaigeable*, from Old French *homage* (homage) + *-able* (-able). Ultimately from Latin *homō* (man).
