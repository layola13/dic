consulships:/ˈkɒnsəlʃɪps/ /ˈkɑːnsəlʃɪps/
n.
领事职位；领事任期
The term or office of a consul.  A consul's jurisdiction or area of authority.
His consulships in several countries gave him a broad perspective on international relations. 他在几个国家的领事任期使他对国际关系有了广阔的视野。
The consulship in Shanghai was a prestigious posting.  驻上海领事馆是一个享有盛誉的职位。
近义词: consulate, consulship
反义词: (No direct antonym for a position. Perhaps, lack of representation?)
词性: noun
