improvabilities:/ɪmˌpruːvəˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. |可改进性；可改善性|The improvabilities of the system are being assessed.  这个系统的可改进性正在被评估。|例句：The report highlighted the improvabilities of the current manufacturing process.  报告强调了当前制造工艺的可改进性。

The improvabilities of the education system are a key focus for the government. 教育系统的可改进性是政府关注的重点。|近义词：potentialities, possibilities, capacities|反义词: limitations, weaknesses|词性:noun
词源: From "improvable" + "-ity" + "-es" (plural). "Improvable" comes from "improve" (from Old French "emprouwer," meaning "to make a profit, to set to advantage") + "-able" (suffix indicating capability).
