dollardom: /ˈdɒlərdəm/ (US) /ˈdɒlədəm/ (UK) | n. | 金钱至上；拜金主义 | The state or condition of being dominated or influenced by money or material wealth.  It refers to a social system or environment where money is the primary motivator and measure of success. | The pervasive dollardom of Hollywood can be disheartening for aspiring artists. 好莱坞盛行的拜金主义可能会让有抱负的艺术家感到沮丧。

The relentless pursuit of dollardom often leads to unhappiness.  对金钱至上的不懈追求往往导致不快乐。| 近义词: materialism, consumerism, money-worship | 反义词: altruism, philanthropy, selflessness | 词性: 名词
