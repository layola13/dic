crybaby:/ˈkraɪˌbeɪbi/| n.|爱哭鬼，哭鼻子的人|A person, especially a child, who cries easily or complains a lot.|例句：He's such a crybaby! 他真是个爱哭鬼！

例句：Stop being such a crybaby and deal with it. 别再像个爱哭鬼一样，去处理它。|近义词：whiner, complainer|反义词：stoic|词性：名词

crybabied:/ˈkraɪˌbeɪbid/| adj.|爱哭的，爱抱怨的|Given to crying or complaining frequently, like a crybaby.|例句：He’s so crybabied; he complains about everything. 他真是爱哭鬼，什么都抱怨。

例句：She gets crybabied when things don't go her way. 当事情不如她意时，她就变得爱哭鼻子。|近义词：whiny, complaining|反义词：stoic, resilient|词性：形容词


词源:  A compound word from "cry" and "baby".  Relatively modern, likely emerging in the early to mid-20th century.
