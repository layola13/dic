bilker:/ˈbɪlkər/ | n. | 骗子，赖账的人 | Someone who cheats or swindles others, especially by avoiding payment of debts. |  The bilker skipped town after racking up thousands of dollars in unpaid bills.  这个骗子在累积了数千美元的未付账单后逃离了小镇。|Synonyms: cheat, swindler, defrauder, dodger | Antonyms: honest person, payer | noun

bilker:/ˈbɪlkər/ | n. | 逃票者 | Someone who evades paying fares on public transport. | The transit authority is cracking down on bilkers who jump the turnstiles. 交通管理局正在严厉打击跳过旋转栅门的逃票者。| Synonyms: fare-dodger, fare-evader | Antonyms: paying passenger | noun
