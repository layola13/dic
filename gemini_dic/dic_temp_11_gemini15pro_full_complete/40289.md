continues: /kənˈtɪnjuːz/ (British) /kənˈtɪnjuːz/ (American)
verb
继续，持续
go on without stopping
The rain continued all day. 雨下了一整天。

continues: /kənˈtɪnjuːz/
adjective
连续的，持续的
uninterrupted in time; extending or carrying on without interruption.
This is a continuation of the previous discussion. 这是先前讨论的延续。
