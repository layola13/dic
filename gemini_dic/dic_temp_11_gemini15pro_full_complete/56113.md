doctrinaires:/ˈdɒktrɪnɛːz/ |ˈdɑːktrɪnɛːrz/| n.| 教条主义者；空谈理论家 |A person who tries to apply theories or principles in a rigid, inflexible way, often ignoring practical considerations or the complexities of real-world situations.  |These doctrinaires clung stubbornly to their outdated economic theories, despite clear evidence of their failure. 这些教条主义者固执地坚持他们过时的经济理论，尽管有明显的证据表明这些理论已经失败了。

The doctrinaires refused to compromise, insisting on strict adherence to their principles, even if it meant harming the people they claimed to represent. 这些教条主义者拒绝妥协，坚持严格遵守他们的原则，即使这意味着会伤害他们声称代表的人民。 |Synonyms: dogmatist, ideologue, theorist, pedant |Antonyms: pragmatist, realist, empiricist |Noun
