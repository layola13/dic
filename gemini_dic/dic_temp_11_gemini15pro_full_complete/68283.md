falafel:/fəˈlɑːfəl/ (US), /fəˈlæfəl/ (UK)| n.| 炸鹰嘴豆丸子，沙拉三明治|Deep-fried balls or patties made from ground chickpeas or fava beans. Often served in pita bread with various toppings and sauces.|例句：I had a delicious falafel sandwich for lunch. 我午餐吃了一个美味的炸鹰嘴豆丸子三明治。|例句：Falafel is a popular street food in the Middle East. 炸鹰嘴豆丸子是中东地区流行的街头小吃。|近义词：chickpea fritters, vegetable patties|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider meat-based counterparts like meatballs)|词性:noun
