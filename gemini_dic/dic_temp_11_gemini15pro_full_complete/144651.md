outwrangling:/ˌaʊtˈræŋɡlɪŋ/
verb
胜过争吵，在争吵中胜过
outdoing someone in wrangling; surpassing in wrangling ability.
He outwrangled his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中胜过了他的对手。
近义词: outarguing, outdebating, besting
反义词: losing to, being defeated by
词性: v.

outwrangling:/ˌaʊtˈræŋɡlɪŋ/
noun
胜过争吵的行为
the act of outdoing someone in wrangling.
His outwrangling of the other negotiators secured a favorable deal. 他胜过其他谈判者，从而确保了一项有利的协议。
近义词: victory in debate, triumph in argument
反义词: defeat in debate, loss in argument
词性: n.
