aftergrowth:/ˈæftərˌɡroʊθ/
noun
再生, 新长出的草木, 后续增长
The new grass appearing after mowing or grazing.  A second growth of plants in the same season. A later consequence or result.
The farmer waited for the aftergrowth to appear before letting his cattle graze again. 农民等着再生草长出来才让他的牛再次吃草。
The aftergrowth of the fire was a stronger, more resilient forest. 火灾后的再生森林更加强壮，更有韧性。
synonyms: regrowth, second growth, aftermath, resurgence
antonyms: decline, decay, deterioration
