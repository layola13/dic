clavichordists:/ˈklævɪˌkɔːrdɪsts/
noun
古钢琴演奏家，击弦古钢琴演奏者
People who play the clavichord, a European stringed keyboard instrument.
The clavichordists gathered for a competition. 古钢琴演奏家们聚集在一起参加比赛。
Clavichordists are known for their delicate touch.  古钢琴演奏家以其细腻的触感而闻名。
synonyms: keyboard players, musicians
antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-musicians)
