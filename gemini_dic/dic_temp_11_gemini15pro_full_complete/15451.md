badam:/bəˈdɑːm/ | n. | 杏仁 | An edible, oval nut with a hard shell, the seed of the almond tree.  From Persian بادام (bâdâm) ultimately from Proto-Iranian *bādāma-. |  She sprinkled slivered badam over the rice pudding. 她把杏仁片撒在米饭布丁上。 |  Example: Badam milk is a popular drink in India. 印度杏仁奶是一种很受欢迎的饮品。 |Synonyms: almond | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
