nonregenerating:/ˌnɒnriːˈdʒɛnəreɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 非再生性的，不能再生的 | Not able to regenerate or be regenerated; incapable of renewal or restoration. | The nonregenerating tissue in the spinal cord makes recovery from serious injuries difficult. 脊髓中的非再生组织使从严重损伤中恢复变得困难。|  The heart is largely composed of nonregenerating muscle. 心脏主要由不能再生的肌肉组成。 | 近义词:nonrenewable, irreparable, incurable | 反义词:regenerating, renewable, repairable | 词性:形容词

词源:
non- (prefix): not, opposite of  (来自拉丁语 non)
regenerating (present participle of regenerate): to cause to be born again; to bring into renewed existence; to reform spiritually or morally; to restore to a better or pristine state. (来自拉丁语 regeneratus, 过去分词 regenerare,  re- + generare to beget)
