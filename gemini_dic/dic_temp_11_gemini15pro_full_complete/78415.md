get lost:/ɡet lɒst/ (British) /ɡet lɔːst/ (American)| phrasal verb | 滚开；迷路 | to go away or leave, especially because someone is annoying you; to become lost or unable to find your way | Example: "Get lost!" she shouted angrily. “滚开！”她生气地喊道。 | Example: We got lost in the woods. 我们在树林里迷路了。 | Synonyms: go away, scram, disappear, become lost | Antonyms: stay, be found, show up | verb phrase
