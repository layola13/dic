aristulate:/əˈrɪstjʊleɪt/
adjective
具小芒的; 具短尖的
Having short, stiff, bristle-like hairs or projections.
The aristulate leaf margins help to prevent water loss. 这些具小芒的叶缘有助于防止水分流失。
近义词: barbed, bristly, spiny
反义词: smooth, glabrous
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *aristulatus, diminutive of *aristatus, from arista (“awn, beard of grain”), probably from a Proto-Indo-European root meaning “point, bristle.”
