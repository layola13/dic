decked:/dekt/
adjective
装饰的，打扮的
Adorned or decorated, especially elaborately or festively.
The hall was decked with Christmas decorations.  大厅装饰着圣诞装饰。
The ship was decked with flags for the regatta.  这艘船为了帆船赛而装饰着旗帜。
adorned, decorated, ornamented, embellished
plain, bare, undecorated
adjective
