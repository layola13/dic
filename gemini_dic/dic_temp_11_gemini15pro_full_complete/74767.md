freeze-dry:/ˈfriːz draɪ/ |v. | 冷冻干燥 | To preserve (food) by freezing it rapidly and then subjecting it to a high vacuum that removes ice by sublimation. | The company freeze-dries the coffee to preserve its flavor.  这家公司将咖啡冷冻干燥以保存其风味。| synonyms: lyophilize | antonyms: hydrate | verb

freeze-dry:/ˈfriːz draɪ/ |adj. | 冷冻干燥的 | Preserved by freeze-drying. |  Freeze-dried food is lightweight and easy to store. 冷冻干燥的食物重量轻，易于储存。| synonyms: lyophilized | antonyms: hydrated | adjective
