erasabilities:/ɪˌreɪzəˈbɪlətiz/
noun
可擦除性；可抹去性
The quality of being erasable.  The ability to be erased or removed.
The erasabilities of pencil marks make them ideal for sketching. 铅笔痕迹的可擦除性使它们成为素描的理想选择。
Modern whiteboards offer excellent erasabilities. 现代白板具有出色的可擦除性。
erasability, effaceability
permanence, indelibility
