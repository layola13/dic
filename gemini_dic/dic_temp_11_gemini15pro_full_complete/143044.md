outbluffs:/ˌaʊtˈblʌfs/
verb
以虚张声势胜过；比…更会虚张声势
to defeat someone by bluffing more successfully than they do
He outbluffed his opponents and won the hand. 他以虚张声势胜过对手，赢得了这一局。
近义词: outsmart, outwit, outmaneuver
反义词: concede, yield

词性:v.
