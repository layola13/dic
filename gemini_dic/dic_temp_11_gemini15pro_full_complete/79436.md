glitch's:/ɡlɪtʃɪz/| n.|小故障；小毛病；小差错|A glitch is a small problem or fault in a system or machine, especially a computer.|The computer program had a glitch that caused it to crash.|近义词：bug, error, malfunction, problem, defect|反义词：perfection, flawlessness|词性:名词 (plural of glitch)
Etymology:  Origin uncertain, possibly from Yiddish *glitsh ‘slippery place,’ or German glitschen ‘to slip.’  First recorded in the early 1960s, originally in the context of spaceflight.
