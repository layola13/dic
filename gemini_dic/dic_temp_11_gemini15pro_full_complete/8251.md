anti-infectives:/ˌæntiɪnˈfɛktɪvz/| n.|抗感染药|Medicines or agents that combat infections, typically by killing or inhibiting the growth of microorganisms such as bacteria, viruses, fungi, or parasites.|The doctor prescribed anti-infectives to treat the patient's pneumonia. 医生开了抗感染药来治疗病人的肺炎。|近义词：antimicrobials, antibiotics (for bacterial infections), antivirals (for viral infections), antifungals (for fungal infections), antiparasitics (for parasitic infections)|反义词:  None (no direct opposite, but immunosuppressants have the opposite effect of weakening the body's ability to fight infections)|词性:Noun
