disroof:/dɪsˈruːf/
verb
揭开屋顶，拆除屋顶
To remove the roof from (a building).
The hurricane disroofed several houses in the coastal area.  飓风掀翻了沿海地区的几座房屋的屋顶。
The fire disroofed the ancient temple. 大火烧毁了这座古老寺庙的屋顶。
synonyms: unroof, uncover
antonyms: roof, cover
