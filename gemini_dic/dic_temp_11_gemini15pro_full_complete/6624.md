anaemics:/əˈniːmɪks/ | n. | 贫血患者 | People suffering from anaemia.  Those who have a deficiency of red blood cells or haemoglobin in the blood. | The doctor is treating a group of anaemics. 医生正在治疗一群贫血患者。|  There is a support group specifically for anaemics to share their experiences. 有一个专门为贫血患者设立的支持小组，供他们分享经验。 | 近义词: anaemia sufferers | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

anaemic:/əˈniːmɪk/ | adj. | 贫血的；缺乏活力的；苍白的 | Suffering from anaemia; lacking in colour, vitality, or vigour. |  She looked anaemic after her long illness. 她久病之后看起来很苍白。| The play was an anaemic production, lacking energy and passion. 这部戏剧制作得很苍白无力，缺乏活力和激情。| 近义词: pale, wan, pallid, lifeless | 反义词: robust, healthy, vigorous | 词性: 形容词
