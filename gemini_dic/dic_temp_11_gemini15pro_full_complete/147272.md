overmining:/ˈoʊvərmaɪnɪŋ/
n.
过度开采
The act of extracting minerals from a mine at a rate that is not sustainable, leading to depletion of resources or environmental damage.
Overmining has led to the collapse of several mountains. 过度开采导致了几座山体的坍塌。
Overexploitation, excessive mining
Undermining, sustainable mining
noun
