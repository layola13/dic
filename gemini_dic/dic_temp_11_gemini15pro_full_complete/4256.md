airship:/ˈɛərˌʃɪp/ | n. |飞艇；空中飞船|A large aircraft without wings, filled with a gas lighter than air and propelled by engines.  It is steered by rudders. | The airship drifted silently across the sky. 飞艇静静地飘过天空。|  The Hindenburg was a famous, but ill-fated, airship.  兴登堡号是一艘著名的但命运多舛的飞艇。|Synonyms: dirigible, blimp | Antonyms: airplane, helicopter | noun
