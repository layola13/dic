nontransposing:/nɒnˌtrænˈspəʊzɪŋ/
adjective
非转置的
Not transposing; specifically (of a musical instrument) producing tones at the written pitch.  A nontransposing instrument plays the note as written on the score.
The clarinet is a transposing instrument, while the piano is nontransposing.  单簧管是移调乐器，而钢琴是非移调乐器。
A flute is a nontransposing instrument, meaning that when a musician plays a C, the listener hears a C.  长笛是一种非移调乐器，这意味着当音乐家演奏C音时，听众听到的也是C音。
