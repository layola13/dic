jump-cutting:/ˈdʒʌmp kʌtɪŋ/
n.
跳切
An abrupt transition from one scene to another, appearing as if a section of the film has been removed, often used to create a sense of discontinuity or urgency.
The director used jump-cutting to create a fast-paced, energetic feel. 导演使用跳切来营造快节奏、充满活力的感觉。

jump-cutting:/ˈdʒʌmp kʌtɪŋ/
v.
进行跳切
To edit a film using jump cuts.
The editor jump-cut between the two scenes to heighten the tension. 剪辑师在两个场景之间进行跳切以增强紧张感。


近义词: jump cut, cutting, montage
反义词: smooth transition, continuous shot
词性: 名词, 动词
