aboriginalities:/ˌæbəˌrɪdʒɪˈnælɪtiz/
n.
古怪；怪癖；原始状态
qualities that are considered strange or unusual, especially qualities relating to early stages of development
例句：He delighted in the aboriginalities of the local culture. 他对当地文化的怪癖感到欣喜。

例句：The museum's collection showcases the aboriginalities of the ancient civilization.  博物馆的藏品展示了古代文明的原始状态。

近义词：peculiarities, eccentricities, oddities, quirks
反义词：normalities, commonalities, conventions


词性: 名词 (plural noun)
词源: From aboriginality + -ies (plural suffix). Aboriginality comes from aboriginal (originally inhabiting a place) + -ity (suffix forming abstract nouns).  The root word is ab origine (Latin) meaning "from the beginning."
