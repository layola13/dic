livable:/ˈlɪvəbl/ (US) /ˈlɪvəbəl/ (UK)| adj.|适宜居住的；可以忍受的|Suitable for living in; tolerable.|例句：This city is very livable due to its low crime rate and excellent public transportation.  这个城市由于犯罪率低和公共交通发达，非常宜居。

例句：The noise from the construction site made the apartment barely livable. 建筑工地的噪音使得公寓几乎无法居住。|近义词：habitable, comfortable, pleasant, agreeable|反义词：uninhabitable, unbearable, unpleasant|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From live + -able.  "live" comes from Old English libban, meaning "to live."  "-able" is a suffix of French origin meaning "capable of being."
