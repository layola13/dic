hydrometeorology:/ˌhaɪdroʊˌmiːtiəˈrɑːlədʒi/ | n. | 水文气象学 | The study of the atmospheric processes that affect the Earth's water resources, including precipitation, evaporation, and runoff. | Hydrometeorology plays a crucial role in flood forecasting. 水文气象学在洪水预报中起着至关重要的作用。|  Hydroclimate, water science | Meteorology, climatology | noun
