outsider:/ˌaʊtˈsaɪdər/| n.|局外人，外人，外行|a person who does not belong to a particular group|He felt like an outsider in the group. 他觉得自己在这个群体中像个局外人。|synonyms: stranger, alien, nonmember|antonyms: insider, member|noun

outsider:/ˌaʊtˈsaɪdər/| adj.|局外的，外来的|coming from outside a place, group, or organization|The outsider's perspective can be valuable.  外人的视角可能很有价值。|synonyms: external, foreign, alien|antonyms: internal, domestic, native|adjective
