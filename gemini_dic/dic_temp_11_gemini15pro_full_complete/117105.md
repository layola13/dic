mealiness:/ˈmiːlinəs/| n.|粉状；质地疏松；乏味|A texture characterized by being dry, crumbly, and soft, often due to overcooking or a high starch content.  It can also refer to a lack of vibrancy or interest.|The mealiness of the overcooked potatoes ruined the dish.  煮过头的土豆的粉状毁了这道菜。|Synonyms: dryness, crumbliness, softness, blandness, staleness|Antonyms: moistness, firmness, crispness, flavorfulness, freshness|noun

mealiness:/ˈmiːlinəs/| n.|乏味；平淡|A lack of vibrancy, interest, or excitement.|The mealiness of the presentation made it difficult to stay awake.  演讲的乏味让人难以保持清醒。|Synonyms: blandness, vapidity, dullness, tedium|Antonyms: vibrancy, excitement, interest,  engaging|noun
