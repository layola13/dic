montages:/ˈmɒntɑːʒɪz/ (UK), /mɑːnˈtɑːʒɪz/ (US)| n.|蒙太奇，剪辑|A montage is a series of short shots or images used to tell a story or convey a particular emotion or idea, often in film or video. It can also refer to a composite picture made by combining several separate pictures.|The film used a montage of old photographs to show the passage of time.|synonyms: compilation, collage, medley, mix, blend|antonyms: separation, division|noun
