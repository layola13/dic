moover:/ˈmuːvər/| n.|推动者，搬运工，搬家公司|Someone or something that moves or facilitates movement.  A person or company that helps people relocate their belongings to a new home or office. |例句：The moovers arrived early in the morning. 搬家公司一大早就到了。

例句：He's a real moover and shaker in the industry. 他是这个行业的真正推动者。|近义词：mover, transporter, relocator, carrier|反义词: stayer, settler|词性:noun
