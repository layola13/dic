mishit:/ˌmɪsˈhɪt/| n. vt.|击球失误，失误；错误;|A shot, especially in golf or tennis, that does not connect correctly with the ball.  A person whose skills or personality are not suited to a particular job or other role. |He mishit the ball and it went wide. 他击球失误，球偏出去了。|近义词：miscue, blunder, flub|反义词:perfect shot, success|词性:名词, 动词
