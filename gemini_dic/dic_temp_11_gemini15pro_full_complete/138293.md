notation:/noʊˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 符号；记号；注释；标记 | A system of signs or symbols used to represent information or instructions. |  Musical notation is a system of symbols used to represent musical sounds.  音乐符号是用来表示音乐声音的一套符号系统。

| Example: The notation used in the mathematical formula was confusing.  这个数学公式中使用的符号令人困惑。 | Synonyms: symbol, sign, mark, representation | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider "misinterpretation", "obfuscation") | Noun

notation:/noʊˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 注解；批注 | A note or comment added to a text or diagram to explain or clarify something. | The editor made several notations in the margins of the manuscript. 编辑在稿件的页边空白处做了几处注解。

| Example:  Her notations on the map helped us find the hidden treasure. 她在地图上的注释帮助我们找到了隐藏的宝藏。 | Synonyms: annotation, comment, note, remark | Antonyms: (No direct antonym) | Noun
