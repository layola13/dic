orientative:/ˌɔːriˈentətɪv/ | /ˌɔːrˈiɛntətɪv/ | adj. | 指导性的，指示性的，试探性的，大致的 | Serving to orient; indicative of a general direction or position; provisional or approximate. | This budget is only orientative; the final figures may be different.  这个预算只是指示性的；最终数字可能会有所不同。 | The meeting provided an orientative framework for future collaborations. 这次会议为未来的合作提供了一个指导性框架。 | 近义词: indicative, approximate, preliminary, provisional | 反义词: definitive, final, conclusive | 词性: adjective

词源: From French *orientatif*, from orienter (“to orient”) + -atif. Ultimately from Latin oriēns (“rising”).
