moorish:/ˈmʊərɪʃ/ | adj. | 摩尔人的；摩尔式的；有摩尔人风格的 | Relating to the Moors, a Muslim people of mixed Berber and Arab descent who ruled Spain and other parts of North Africa and Europe for several centuries.  Characterized by the style of architecture and decoration developed by the Moors. |  The Alhambra palace is a stunning example of Moorish architecture. 阿尔罕布拉宫是摩尔式建筑的绝佳典范。 The intricate tilework is a typical Moorish design. 错综复杂的瓷砖作品是典型的摩尔式设计。 | Synonyms: Islamic, Arabic, Moresque | Antonyms: European, Western | adjective
