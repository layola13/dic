encystment:/ɪnˈsɪstmənt/ /ɛnˈsɪstmənt/| n. |包囊形成，被膜包裹|The process of forming or becoming enclosed in a cyst.  The encystment of the parasite protects it from harsh environmental conditions.|The amoeba underwent encystment to survive the drought.  变形虫通过形成包囊来度过干旱。|近义词： encapsulation, enclosure |反义词: excystment |noun
