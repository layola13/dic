imploding:/ɪmˈploʊdɪŋ/
verb
内爆；崩溃；剧烈收缩
collapsing inward violently; breaking down or failing suddenly and completely
The star imploded at the end of its life cycle. 这颗恒星在其生命周期结束时发生了内爆。
The company's stock price imploded after the scandal.  丑闻曝光后，这家公司的股价暴跌。
近义词: collapsing, crumbling, disintegrating, failing
反义词: exploding, expanding, growing
词性: verb

implosion:/ɪmˈploʊʒən/
noun
内爆；崩溃；剧烈收缩
the act of collapsing inward violently; a sudden and complete failure or breakdown
The implosion of the housing market caused a financial crisis. 房地产市场的崩溃引发了金融危机。
The building's implosion was carefully controlled.  这栋建筑的爆破是经过精心控制的。
近义词: collapse, downfall, failure
反义词: explosion, expansion, growth
词性: noun

词源: From Latin implodere, from in- 'in' + plaudere 'to clap.'  来自于拉丁语 implodere，由 in- “在...里面” + plaudere “拍手” 组成。
