nonornamentally:/ˌnɒnˌɔːrnəˈmentəli/
adverb
非装饰性地，朴素地
Without ornamentation; plainly.
She dressed nonornamentally for the funeral. 她穿着朴素地参加了葬礼。
近义词: plainly, simply, unadornedly
反义词: ornately, decoratively, elaborately
词性: 副词
