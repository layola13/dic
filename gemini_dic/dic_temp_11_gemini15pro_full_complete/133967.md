nonheritably:/ˌnɒnˈhɛrɪtəbli/
adv.
不可遗传地
in a way that cannot be passed on from parents to children through genes.
Exposure to radiation can cause nonheritably altered genes. 辐射暴露会导致基因发生不可遗传的改变。
近义词: non-genetically
反义词: heritably
词性: 副词
