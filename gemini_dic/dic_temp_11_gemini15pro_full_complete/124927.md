mudwort:/ˈmʌdwɜːrt/| n. |泥滩草，泥生草|A type of plant that grows in mud or wet ground, specifically *Limosella aquatica*.|例句：The mudwort thrived in the marshy area. 泥滩草在沼泽地带茁壮成长。

例句：We found mudwort growing along the edge of the pond. 我们在池塘边发现了泥滩草。|近义词：waterwort|反义词: (No direct antonym as it's a specific plant name.  Could consider "xerophyte" (旱生植物) as a broad contrasting concept.) |词性:noun
