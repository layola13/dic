laconia:/ləˈkəʊniə/ | n. |  简洁, 简洁的言语; 拉哥尼亚(古希腊城邦)|  A style of speech or writing characterized by extreme conciseness or terseness, often to the point of abruptness; also, the ancient Greek region of Laconia, known for its inhabitants' brief and pithy speech. |  His laconia was legendary; he could convey volumes with a single look.  他惜字如金是出了名的，一个眼神就能表达千言万语。|  conciseness, brevity, terseness | verbosity, prolixity | noun

laconic:/ləˈkɒnɪk/ | adj. |  简洁的, 简洁的言语 | Using very few words; expressing much in few words. |  Her laconic reply was simply, "No." 她简洁地回答道：“不”。  His laconic style made him a popular newspaper columnist.  他简洁的文风使他成为一位受欢迎的报纸专栏作家。| concise, brief, terse, succinct | verbose, prolix, loquacious | adjective
