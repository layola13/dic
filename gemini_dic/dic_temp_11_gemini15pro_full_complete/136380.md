nonrestitutions:/ˌnɑːnˌrɛstɪˈtuːʃənz/
n.
未归还，未赔偿
the failure to return something or to make amends for a loss or injury
The museum faced criticism for the nonrestitution of looted artifacts.  博物馆因未归还被掠夺的文物而面临批评。
The nonrestitution of the stolen jewels angered the victim. 被盗珠宝未归还激怒了受害者。
non-return, non-payment, withholding
restitution, return, compensation
noun
