leash:/liːʃ/
noun|皮带，束缚|A strap or cord used to restrain an animal, typically a dog.|The dog strained at its leash, eager to chase the squirrel. 狗拉着皮带，急于追逐松鼠。|lead, tether, rope|freedom, liberty|n.

leash:/liːʃ/
verb|用皮带拴住，控制，束缚|To restrain or control with or as if with a leash.|She leashed the dog before taking it for a walk.  她在遛狗之前给它拴上了皮带。|restrain, control, curb|unleash, free, release|v.
