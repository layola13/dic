anticlassicalist:/ˌæntiˈklæsɪkəlɪst/| n. adj.|反古典主义者，反古典主义的|Someone who opposes or rejects classicism, especially in art, literature, or music.  It can also describe something that goes against classical principles.|The artist was known as an anticlassicalist, preferring abstract forms over traditional representation. 这位艺术家以反古典主义而闻名，他更喜欢抽象形式而不是传统表现。|近义词：modernist, avant-garde, iconoclast|反义词：classicist, traditionalist|词性: noun, adjective

anticlassicalist:/ˌæntiˈklæsɪkəlɪst/| adj.|反古典主义的|Characterized by opposition to or rejection of classicism.  It describes art, literature, music, or any other work that deviates from classical principles.|Her anticlassicalist compositions challenged the established norms of musical structure. 她的反古典主义作品挑战了既定的音乐结构规范。|近义词：modernistic, avant-garde, iconoclastic|反义词：classical, traditional|词性: adjective
