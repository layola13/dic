insolation:/ˌɪnsəˈleɪʃn/ | n. | 日晒，日照；太阳辐射 |  The amount of solar radiation reaching a given area.  Exposure to the sun's rays. | The researchers measured the insolation at different latitudes. 研究人员测量了不同纬度的日照量。

The high levels of insolation in the desert contribute to the extreme temperatures. 沙漠中高水平的日照导致了极端的气温。| 近义词: solar radiation, sunshine, sunlight | 反义词: shade, darkness | 词性: noun

词源: From Latin *insolatio(n-), from insolatus, past participle of insolare "to expose to the sun," from in- "in, on" + sol "sun."
