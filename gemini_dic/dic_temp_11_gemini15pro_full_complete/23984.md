breathalyser:/ˈbrɛθəlaɪzər/ (UK), /ˈbrɛθəlaɪzər/ (US)| n. |呼气酒精测试仪|A device used by police for measuring the amount of alcohol in a driver's breath.|The police officer asked him to blow into the breathalyser. 警察让他对着呼气酒精测试仪吹气。|Synonyms: breathalyzer (US spelling), Alcotest|Antonyms: None|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "breath" + "analyser/analyzer".  "Analyse/analyze" comes from French "analyser," from Late Latin "analisare," from Greek "analyein"  meaning "to loosen, dissolve, resolve."
