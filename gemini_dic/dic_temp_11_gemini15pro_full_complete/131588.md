noncompetent:/nɒnˈkɒmpɪtənt/ /nɑːnˈkɑːmpɪtənt/| adj.|不称职的，不胜任的|Not having the necessary skills or knowledge to do something successfully; lacking ability.|The noncompetent worker was fired after a series of mistakes.  这个不称职的工人在犯了一系列错误后被解雇了。|Synonyms: incapable, unskilled, inept, unqualified|Antonyms: competent, capable, skilled, qualified|adjective
