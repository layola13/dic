hydropathical:/ˌhaɪdrəˈpæθɪkəl/
adjective
水疗法的；用水治疗的
Relating to hydropathy, the treatment of disease using water.
Example: The doctor recommended a hydropathical treatment for her arthritis. 医生建议用水疗法治疗她的关节炎。
Example:  The hydropathical establishment offered various treatments including cold plunges and steam baths. 这家水疗机构提供各种疗法，包括冷水浴和蒸汽浴。
Synonym: hydrotherapeutic
Antonym: allopathic (although not a perfect antonym, it represents a contrasting approach to medicine)
词性: adjective
