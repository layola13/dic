nonpersuasiveness:/ˌnɒnpərˈsweɪsɪvnəs/ /ˌnɑːnpərˈsweɪsɪvnəs/
noun
无说服力；不具说服力
The nonpersuasiveness of his argument led to the proposal being rejected.  他的论点缺乏说服力，导致提案被否决。
The report's nonpersuasiveness stemmed from a lack of concrete evidence.  这份报告没有说服力是因为缺乏具体的证据。
unconvincingness, ineffectuality, impotence
persuasiveness, effectiveness, potency
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + persuasiveness.  Persuasiveness from persuade + -ness. Persuade from Latin persuadēre "to prevail upon, convince," from per- "thoroughly" + suadēre "to advise, urge."
