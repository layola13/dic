alive:/əˈlaɪv/ | adj. | 活着的，在世的；有活力的，活跃的 | Living, not dead; full of energy and enthusiasm. | The fish we caught was still alive. 我们抓到的鱼还活着。| He felt more alive than he had in years. 他感觉比过去几年更有活力。 | 近义词：living, existing, lively, vibrant, energetic | 反义词：dead, deceased, lifeless, inert | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From Old English *on līfe "in life," from Proto-Germanic *liban "to live."  The "a-" represents a reduced form of "on."
