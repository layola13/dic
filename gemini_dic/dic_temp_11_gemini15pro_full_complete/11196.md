architecting:/ˈɑːrkɪtɛktɪŋ/ |v. (present participle)|设计；架构；构筑|The process of designing and planning the structure and components of a system, especially a computer system or software application.  It involves making high-level design choices that address non-functional requirements such as reliability, scalability, performance, and security.|We are architecting a new cloud-based platform. 我们正在构建一个新的基于云的平台.|designing, planning, structuring, engineering| dismantling, destroying|verb
