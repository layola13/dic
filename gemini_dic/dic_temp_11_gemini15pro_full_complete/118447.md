mescaline:/ˈmɛskəlɪn/ | n. | 墨斯卡灵，仙人球毒碱 | A hallucinogenic alkaloid, C₁₁H₁₇NO₃, obtained from the peyote cactus and other cacti.  It is used in Native American religious ceremonies and has also been studied for its potential therapeutic effects, but its recreational use is illegal in most countries due to its powerful hallucinogenic properties. |  Mescaline is a powerful hallucinogen that can cause profound alterations in perception and consciousness. 墨斯卡灵是一种强效致幻剂，会导致感知和意识的深刻改变。

The effects of mescaline can last for up to 12 hours. 墨斯卡灵的效果可以持续长达12小时。 | 近义词: hallucinogen, psychedelic | 反义词: None (lacks a direct antonym; could consider "sobering agent" but it's not a precise opposite) | 词性: noun
