antireaction:/ˌæntiɹiˈækʃən/| n.|反作用，反动，对抗反应|An antireaction is an action in the opposite direction or manner.  It's a response against something, often a change or innovation.| The antireaction to the new tax law was swift and fierce. 新税法的反作用迅速而强烈。|The government faced a strong antireaction to its proposed reforms. 政府面临着对其拟议改革的强烈反作用。|近义词：reaction, backlash, resistance, opposition|反义词: support, agreement|词性:noun
