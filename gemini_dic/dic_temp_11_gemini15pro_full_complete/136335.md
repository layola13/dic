nonreserves: /ˌnɑːn rɪˈzɜːrvz/ /ˌnɒn rɪˈzɜːvz/ | n. | 非储备 | Assets or resources that are not held in reserve or set aside for a specific purpose.  Generally referring to a portion of a bank's assets. |  The bank's nonreserves were invested in various securities.  这家银行的非储备资产投资于各种证券。 | Synonyms: non-reserve assets, available assets | Antonyms: reserves, reserve assets | Noun
