nondisparity:/ˌnɒndɪˈspærəti/ | n. |无差异，平等 | Absence of disparity; equality or similarity. | The government is working towards nondisparity in pay between men and women. 政府正在努力实现男女薪酬平等。|  The study revealed nondisparity in educational outcomes between the two groups.  这项研究显示两组之间的教育成果没有差异。 | synonyms: equality, parity, uniformity, similarity | antonyms: disparity, inequality, difference, variation | noun
