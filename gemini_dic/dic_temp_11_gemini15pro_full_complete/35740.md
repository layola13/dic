cochairs:/ˈkoʊtʃɛrz/| n.|共同主席，联合主席|Two individuals sharing the responsibility and authority of chairing a committee, meeting, or organization.|例句：The cochairs of the committee will lead the meeting. 委员会的两位共同主席将主持会议。

例句：The conference had two cochairs, a biologist and a physicist.  这次会议有两名共同主席，一位是生物学家，一位是物理学家。

|近义词：co-chairs, joint chairs, co-leaders|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps subordinate)|词性:noun
