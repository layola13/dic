cottonmouth:/ˈkɑːtnˌmaʊθ/ /ˈkɒtnˌmaʊθ/| n.|水蝮蛇，棉口蛇； (非正式) 坏脾气的人|A venomous semiaquatic pit viper (Agkistrodon piscivorus) of the southeastern U.S., having a whitish interior of the mouth.  It is also sometimes used informally to refer to a person with a bad temper or who speaks harshly.|例句1：The cottonmouth is a dangerous snake found in swamps.  棉口蛇是一种在沼泽地发现的危险蛇类。

例句2：He was called a cottonmouth for his constant complaining. 他因为不停地抱怨而被称为“坏脾气的人”。|近义词：water moccasin, viper, grump, curmudgeon|反义词: (for the snake) harmless snake, (for the person) amiable person|词性:noun
