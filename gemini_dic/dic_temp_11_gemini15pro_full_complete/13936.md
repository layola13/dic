词源:
From Middle English *aunte, ante, from Old French ante, from Latin amita (“father's sister”), from Proto-Italic *amitā, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂emH- (“mother's brother; uncle”). Cognate with Hindi आंटी (āṇṭī). Displaced native Old English fæderan swester (“father's sister”) and modrigan swester (“mother's sister”).
