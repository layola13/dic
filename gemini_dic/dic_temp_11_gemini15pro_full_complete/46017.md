ETYMOLOGY->
"Cyberpunk" is a portmanteau of "cybernetics" and "punk."  "Cybernetics" comes from the Greek "κυβερνήτης" (kybernētēs), meaning "pilot" or "governor," referring to the science of communication and control systems in both machines and living things. "Punk," originally meaning "worthless person" or "rotten wood," evolved to describe a rebellious subculture and music genre. The -’s is the standard English possessive suffix.
