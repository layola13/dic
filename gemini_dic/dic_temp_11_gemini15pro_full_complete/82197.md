grossly:/ˈɡroʊsli/| adv.|严重地，非常地，粗俗地|in a very obvious and unacceptable manner; excessively; vulgarly|例句：The report grossly exaggerated the extent of the problem. 报告严重夸大了问题的严重程度。|近义词：excessively, extremely, flagrantly, significantly, substantially|反义词：slightly, mildly, subtly, minimally|词性:副词

grossly:/ˈɡroʊsli/| adv.|总共，大体上|in total|例句：The company made £5 million grossly last year.  这家公司去年总共赚了500万英镑。 (This meaning is less common.)|近义词：overall, in all, altogether|反义词：net|词性:副词
