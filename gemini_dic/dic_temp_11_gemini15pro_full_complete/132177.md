noncyclical:/ˌnɒnˈsɪklɪkəl/ /ˌnɑːnˈsaɪklɪkəl/
adjective
非周期性的，非循环的
Not subject to regular fluctuations or cycles; not cyclical.
例句：
The demand for healthcare services is generally considered noncyclical.
医疗保健服务的需求通常被认为是非周期性的。

Noncyclical stocks are often seen as a safe haven during economic downturns.
非周期性股票在经济低迷时期通常被视为避风港。


近义词：stable, consistent, steady
反义词：cyclical, volatile
