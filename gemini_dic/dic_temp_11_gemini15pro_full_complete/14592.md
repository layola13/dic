avidness:/ˈævɪdnəs/| n. |渴望，热切，贪婪|An intense desire or enthusiasm for something.|He read the book with avidness, eager to absorb every word.|近义词：eagerness, keenness, enthusiasm, zeal|反义词：apathy, indifference, reluctance|词性:名词

Etymology: From the adjective "avid," from Latin "avidus" (greedy, eager, desirous), from "avēre" (to desire eagerly).
