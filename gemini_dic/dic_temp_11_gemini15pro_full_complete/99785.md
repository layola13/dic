interment:/ɪnˈtɜːrmənt/ | n. |埋葬；安葬|The act of burying a dead person. |例句：The interment of the king was a grand ceremony. 国王的葬礼是一场盛大的仪式。

例句：The family held a private interment for their loved one. 家人为他们所爱的人举行了私人葬礼。


近义词：burial, inhumation |反义词:exhumation |词性:名词

词源: From Old French enterrement, from enterrer "to bury," from in- "in" + terre "earth."
