insnare:/ɪnˈsneər/ /ɪnˈsnɛr/| vt.|诱捕，使陷入圈套|To catch or trap someone or something, often by trickery or deceit.|The hunter insnared the rabbit in a net. 猎人用网诱捕了兔子。|The con man insnared several elderly people in his elaborate scheme. 骗子用他精心设计的诡计诱骗了几位老人。|近义词：trap, catch, entangle, ensnare|反义词：free, liberate, release|词性:动词

词源:
From in- + snare.  "In-" is a prefix meaning "in" or "into." "Snare" comes from the Old English word "sner" meaning "noose" or "trap."
