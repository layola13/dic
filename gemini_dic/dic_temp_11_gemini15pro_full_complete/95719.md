inconsecutiveness:/ˌɪnkənˌsɛkjʊˈtɪvnəs/
|n.|不连续性，不连贯性|The inconsecutiveness of the plot made the movie hard to follow.  情节的不连贯性使得这部电影难以理解。|The report suffered from inconsecutiveness and lacked a clear narrative thread.  这份报告缺乏连贯性，缺乏清晰的叙事主线。|近义词：discontinuity, disjunction, brokenness|反义词：continuity, coherence, connectedness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + consecutive (following in order) + -ness (noun suffix forming abstract nouns from adjectives).  Consecutive comes from Latin *consecut-, past participle stem of consequi "to follow closely," from com- "together" + sequi "to follow."
