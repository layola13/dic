nonfinites:/nɒnˈfaɪnaɪts/ | n. | 非限定动词 |  Verbs that are not marked for tense or person. They include infinitives, participles, and gerunds. |  Reading is my hobby.  阅读是我的爱好。 |  infinitives, participles, gerunds | finite verbs | noun

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
She enjoys reading books. = 她喜欢读书。
He prefers swimming in the ocean. = 他更喜欢在大海里游泳。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am considering taking a vacation. = 我正在考虑休假。
They are planning on attending the concert. = 他们正计划参加音乐会。


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Having finished his work, he went home. = 完成工作后，他回家了。
She has enjoyed living in the city. = 她一直很喜欢住在城市里。


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
Having been working for ten hours, she was exhausted. = 连续工作了十个小时，她筋疲力尽。
He has been considering changing jobs. = 他一直在考虑换工作。


一般过去时 (Simple Past):

She decided to take a walk. = 她决定去散步。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was considering taking a vacation last year. = 我去年正在考虑休假。
They were planning on attending the concert before it was cancelled. = 在音乐会被取消之前，他们正计划参加。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
Having finished his homework, he went to bed. = 完成作业后，他上床睡觉了。
She had considered moving to a new city. = 她曾考虑过搬到一个新的城市。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will consider taking a vacation next year. = 我明年会考虑休假。
They will be delighted to hear the news. = 他们听到这个消息会很高兴。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be considering my options next week. = 我下周将考虑我的选择。
They will be planning the party next month. = 他们下个月将计划聚会。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next year, I will have finished writing my book. = 到明年，我将完成我的书的写作。
By next month, they will have completed the project. = 到下个月，他们将完成这个项目。



将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):

疑问句:

否定句:
