negligibility:/ˌnɛɡlɪˈdʒɪbəlɪti/| n.|可忽略性，微不足道|The negligibility of the side effects made the drug a viable option.  这种药物的副作用可以忽略不计，使其成为一个可行的选择。|The error was deemed of such negligibility that it could be safely ignored.  这个错误被认为是微不足道的，可以安全地忽略。|近义词：insignificance, unimportance, triviality|反义词：significance, importance, consequence|词性:noun

negligible:/ˈnɛɡlɪdʒəbl/ | adj.|微不足道的，可忽略不计的|The cost was negligible compared to the potential benefits.  与潜在的收益相比，成本微不足道。|The damage to the car was negligible. 汽车的损坏微不足道。|近义词：insignificant, trivial, unimportant|反义词：significant, important, considerable|词性:adjective
