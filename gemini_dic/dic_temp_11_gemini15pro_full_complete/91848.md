hyperhypocrisies:/ˌhaɪpərˌhɪpˈɑːkrəsiːz/
|n.|超级虚伪/极度伪善|The act of engaging in extreme hypocrisy, often involving layers of deception and self-righteousness.|
|例句：The politician's hyperhypocrisies were evident in his public pronouncements, which contradicted his private actions. 这位政客的极度伪善在他的公开声明中显而易见，这些声明与他的私人行为相矛盾。|
|例句：The hyperhypocrisies of the organization were exposed by the whistleblower, revealing a culture of corruption and deceit.  该组织的极度伪善被举报人揭露，暴露了一种腐败和欺骗的文化。|
|近义词：duplicity, hypocrisy, sanctimony, two-facedness, cant, deceit, fraud, sham|
|反义词：honesty, sincerity, integrity, genuineness|
|词性: 名词|
