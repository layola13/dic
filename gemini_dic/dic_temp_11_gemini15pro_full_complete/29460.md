catalytic:/kætəˈlɪtɪk/| adj.|催化的；促进的|Relating to or involving the action of a catalyst.|例句：Enzymes are catalytic proteins that speed up biochemical reactions. 酶是催化蛋白质，加速生化反应。

例句：The government's intervention had a catalytic effect on the peace talks. 政府的干预对和平谈判起了催化作用。|近义词：facilitative, stimulative, accelerating|反义词：inhibitory, hindering|词性:形容词

词源: From French *catalytique*, from Greek *katalutikos*, from *kataluein* "to dissolve," from *kata-* "down" + *luein* "to loosen."
