dynasty: /ˈdaɪnəsti/ (US) /ˈdɪnəsti/ (UK)| n. | 王朝；朝代 | A sequence of rulers from the same family, stock, or group.  A period of time during which a single family reigns. | The Ming dynasty ruled China for centuries.  明朝统治了中国几个世纪。

The Kennedy dynasty has had a profound impact on American politics.  肯尼迪王朝对美国政治产生了深远的影响。 | synonyms: reign, empire, house, lineage | antonyms: anarchy, disorder | noun
