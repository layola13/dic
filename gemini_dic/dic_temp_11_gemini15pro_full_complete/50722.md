derogant:/ˈdɛrəɡənt/ | adj. | 贬低的，诋毁的 | Showing a critical or disrespectful attitude. |  
例句：He made some derogant remarks about her abilities. 他对她的能力发表了一些贬低的言论。

例句：Her derogant tone offended many people at the meeting. 她轻蔑的语气冒犯了会议上的许多人。


近义词：disparaging, belittling, demeaning, derogatory | 反义词：complimentary, praising, laudatory | 词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->

From Late Latin *dērogāns, present participle of dērogāre (“to repeal partly, detract from”), from dē- (“away, from”) + rogāre (“to ask, propose a law”).
