at full speed:/æt fʊl spiːd/
|adv.|全速地；以最高速度；|Moving or operating as fast as possible.|The car raced down the track at full speed. 汽车全速在赛道上飞驰。|Synonyms: top speed, full tilt, flat out|Antonyms: slowly, gradually|adverb

at:/æt, ət/
|prep.|在，于；向，朝；达，计；忙于；|Expressing location or arrival in a particular place or position.|The meeting is at 3 pm. 会议在下午3点举行。|Synonyms: in, on, by, near|Antonyms: from, away from|preposition

full:/fʊl/
|adj.|满的；充满的；完全的；丰满的；|Containing or holding as much or as many as possible; having no empty space.|The theater was full. 剧院坐满了人。|Synonyms: complete, filled, packed, crammed|Antonyms: empty, vacant|adjective

speed:/spiːd/
|n. v.|速度；快速；昌盛；|The rate at which someone or something is able to move or operate.|The speed limit is 60 mph. 限速是每小时60英里。|Synonyms: velocity, pace, rapidity, swiftness|Antonyms: slowness|noun, verb
