misanalyzing:/ˌmɪsˈænəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
错误分析；分析不当
Analyzing something incorrectly or drawing the wrong conclusions from an analysis.
He was misanalyzing the data and came to the wrong conclusion. 他错误地分析了数据，得出了错误的结论。
