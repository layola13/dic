fruiterer:/ˈfruːtərər/| n.|水果商；水果店老板|A person who sells fruit, especially one who owns or works in a shop selling fruit.|例句：The fruiterer arranged the mangoes attractively in his stall. 水果商把芒果诱人地摆放在他的摊位上。

例句：She bought a basket of strawberries from the local fruiterer. 她从当地的水果商那里买了一篮草莓。|近义词：greengrocer, fruit seller, vendor|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 buyer, customer)|词性: noun
