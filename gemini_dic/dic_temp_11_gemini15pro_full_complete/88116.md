hijacker:/ˈhaɪdʒækər/| n.|劫持者，劫机者|A person who hijacks a vehicle, especially an aircraft.|The hijackers threatened to kill the passengers if their demands were not met. 劫机者威胁说，如果他们的要求得不到满足，就杀死乘客。|Synonyms: pirate, bandit, outlaw|Antonyms: passenger, crew, hostage|noun

hijacker:/ˈhaɪdʒækər/| n.|劫持者，强占者|Someone who seizes control of a vehicle or aircraft by force.|The hijackers forced the pilot to change course. 劫持者强迫飞行员改变航线。|Synonyms: abductor, usurper, commandeerer|Antonyms: rescuer, liberator|noun
