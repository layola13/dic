isosporic:/ˌaɪsoʊˈspɔːrɪk/
adjective
同型孢子的
Producing spores of one kind only, which are not differentiated by sex.
Ferns are isosporic plants.  蕨类植物是同型孢子植物。
Isosporic ferns produce spores that develop into bisexual gametophytes. 同型孢子蕨类产生能发育成双性配子体的孢子。
近义词:homosporous
反义词:heterosporous
词性:adjective
