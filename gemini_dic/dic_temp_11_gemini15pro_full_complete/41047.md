copiers:/ˈkɒpiəz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpiərz/ (US)| n. |复印机；复印者|Machines or people that make copies of documents or other materials.|例句：The office has two new copiers. 办公室有两台新的复印机。

例句：The copiers are located down the hall. 复印机在大厅的尽头。|近义词：photocopiers, duplicators, reproduction machines|反义词: original (referring to the source document) |词性:noun
