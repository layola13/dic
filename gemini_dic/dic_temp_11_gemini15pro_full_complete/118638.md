messiness:/ˈmesɪnəs/| n.|杂乱，凌乱，脏乱|The messiness of the room appalled her. 房间的凌乱让她感到震惊。|例句：The messiness of his desk reflected the messiness of his mind. 他的办公桌的凌乱反映了他思绪的混乱。

例句：She apologized for the messiness of the kitchen after the party.  派对结束后，她为厨房的脏乱道歉。|近义词：disorder, untidiness, clutter, chaos|反义词：tidiness, neatness, order|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From messy + -ness.  "Messy" comes from the Old English word "mese," meaning a portion of food.  Over time, it evolved to mean a dirty or disordered state.
