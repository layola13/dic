一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking in the kitchen? = 他在厨房里做什么菜？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? = 她在商店买了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been studying for the past few months? = 过去几个月他一直在学习什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last weekend? = 你上周末做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the wedding? = 她去婚礼穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you eaten before you got sick? = 你生病之前吃了什么？
What had they decided before the meeting started? = 会议开始前他们决定了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been working on before she quit her job? = 她辞职之前一直在做什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will happen if we don't leave now? = 如果我们现在不走会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next year? = 明年这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be presenting at the conference? = 他们在会议上将要展示什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the age of 30? = 到30岁时你将取得什么成就？
What will have changed in the next ten years? = 未来十年会发生什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for the past five years by then? = 到那时你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？

虚拟语气:

What would have happened if I hadn't been there? = 如果我不在那里会发生什么？

被动语态:

疑问句:

What are your plans for the summer? = 你夏天的计划是什么？

否定句:
