disanoint:/ˌdɪsəˈnɔɪnt/ | vt. | 取消…的资格；废黜；使失望 | To remove from a position of privilege or power; to deprive of official standing.  Also, to disappoint. | The king was disanointed and banished from the kingdom. 国王被废黜并被驱逐出王国。

The failure of the project disanointed the team. 项目的失败使团队感到失望。 | 近义词: dethrone, dismiss, remove, disappoint | 反义词: appoint, elect, inaugurate, encourage | 词性: 动词
