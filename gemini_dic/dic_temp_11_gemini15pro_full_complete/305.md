aberrational: /ˌæbəˈreɪʃənəl/
adjective
异常的，反常的
deviating from what is normal or expected; atypical
His aberrant behavior led to his dismissal from the company. 他的异常行为导致他被公司解雇。
abnormal, atypical, deviant, irregular, anomalous
normal, typical, usual, standard, regular
adjective
