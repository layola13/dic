nonobsessiveness:/ˌnɒnəbˈsɛsɪvnəs/ | n. | 非痴迷;  不沉迷 | The state of not being obsessed with something or someone.  A healthy level of detachment or balance in one's interests and pursuits. | Her nonobsessiveness with her appearance was refreshing. 她对自身外表的不在意令人耳目一新。 |  detachment, balance, sanity, reasonableness | obsession, fixation, preoccupation | noun
non-obsessive:/ˌnɒnəbˈsɛsɪv/ | adj. | 非痴迷的; 不沉迷的 | Not excessively preoccupied with or attached to something or someone. | He has a non-obsessive approach to fitness. 他对健身的态度并不痴迷。 |  balanced, reasonable, moderate, healthy | obsessive, fixated, compulsive | adjective
