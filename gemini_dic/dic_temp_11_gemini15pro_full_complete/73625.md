foreworld: /ˈfɔːrwɜːrld/ | n. | 前世，前生；古代世界 |  The concept of a foreworld suggests a previous life or a world that existed before the present one.  It often carries a sense of mystery and ancient wisdom. |  Legends speak of a foreworld where magic was commonplace. 传说中有一个魔法盛行的前世。 He believed his dreams were glimpses into a foreworld. 他相信他的梦是瞥见了前世。 | synonyms: previous life, past life, former world, ancient world | antonyms: afterlife, future world | noun
