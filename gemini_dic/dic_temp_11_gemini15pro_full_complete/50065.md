densimeter:/dɛnˈsɪmɪtər/
n.
密度计
An instrument for measuring the density or relative density of a substance.
The brewer used a densimeter to check the specific gravity of the wort.  酿酒师用密度计测量麦芽汁的比重。
hydrometer, areometer
