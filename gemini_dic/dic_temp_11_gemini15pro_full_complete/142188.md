ornamenters:/ˈɔːrnəˌmɛntərz/ | n. | 装饰者，装潢者 |  People who ornament or decorate something.  Those who add embellishments or adornments. | The ornamenters spent hours meticulously decorating the Christmas tree. 圣诞装饰者花了几个小时精心装饰圣诞树。 | The ornamenters skillfully embellished the palace walls with intricate carvings. 装饰者巧妙地用复杂的雕刻装饰了宫殿的墙壁。 |  decorators, embellishers, adorners | plain makers, strippers | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From ornament + -er (agent suffix).  "Ornament" itself comes from Old French "ornement" and ultimately from Latin "ornare" (to adorn, equip).
