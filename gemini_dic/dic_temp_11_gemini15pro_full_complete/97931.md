innominable:/ɪˈnɒmɪnəbl/ /ɪˈnɑːmɪnəbl/
adjective
不可名状的，难以形容的
Not to be named; unnamable; too sacred or awful to be named.  It can also refer to something so bad or objectionable that it's difficult to even describe.
He committed innominable acts of cruelty. 他犯下了难以名状的残暴罪行。

The family was forced to endure innominable suffering. 这个家庭被迫忍受难以言喻的痛苦。


近义词:unnameable, unspeakable, ineffable, undescribable
反义词:nameable, describable
词性:adjective
