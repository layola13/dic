cobnut: /ˈkɒbnʌt/ (UK), /ˈkoʊbnʌt/ (US) | n. |榛子;欧洲榛的坚果 |A round, hard-shelled nut, the fruit of the hazel tree. |例句：I love the taste of fresh cobnuts in the autumn. 我喜欢秋天新鲜榛子的味道。

例句：The squirrels were busy burying cobnuts for the winter. 松鼠们正忙着为冬天埋藏榛子。|近义词：hazelnut, filbert |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *cobnut*, from cob (meaning "round object" or "head, top") + nut.  The "cob" part likely refers to the shape of the nut and its husk.
