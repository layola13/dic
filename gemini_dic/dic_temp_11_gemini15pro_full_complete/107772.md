lapidify:/ləˈpɪdɪfaɪ/ /ˈlæpɪdɪˌfaɪ/
verb
使石化；使硬化
to turn into stone; to petrify or make hard like stone.
The minerals slowly lapidified over millions of years, forming the stunning cave formations. 数百万年来，矿物质慢慢石化，形成了令人惊叹的洞穴构造。
The intense heat lapidified the clay.  高温使粘土硬化。

近义词: petrify, fossilize, harden
反义词: soften, liquefy
词性: verb
