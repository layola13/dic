aniline: /ˈænɪlɪn/ (US), /ˈænɪliːn/ (UK) | n. |苯胺|A colorless oily liquid, C6H5NH2, derived from benzene, used in making dyes, explosives, and plastics. | Aniline is a key ingredient in the production of many dyes. 苯胺是生产许多染料的关键成分。| Aniline is toxic and should be handled with care. 苯胺有毒，应小心处理。| 近义词: aminobenzene, phenylamine | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
