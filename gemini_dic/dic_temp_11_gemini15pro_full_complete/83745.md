hailshot:/ˈheɪlʃɒt/ (UK) /ˈheɪlʃɑːt/ (US)| n.|雹弹; 霰弹; 猛烈的攻击;|A sudden and intense barrage, like a hailstorm, especially of criticism, questions, or missiles.  It can refer to a literal shower of hail or a metaphorical one. | The speaker faced a hailshot of questions from the angry crowd. 演讲者面临着来自愤怒人群的连珠炮般的提问。

The enemy launched a hailshot of missiles at the city. 敌人向城市发射了一连串导弹。|近义词：barrage, volley, onslaught, bombardment|反义词:trickle, dribble|noun
