immodesties: /ɪməˈdɛstiz/ | n. |  不端庄的行为；不谦虚的行为；失礼的行为| lack of modesty; improper behavior or speech; indecency |  Her immodesties shocked the conservative community.  她的不端庄行为震惊了保守的社区。|  The comedian's immodesties on stage were met with mixed reactions.  这位喜剧演员在舞台上的不雅言行引来了褒贬不一的反应。 | 近义词: improprieties, indecencies, indecorum | 反义词: modesty, decorum, propriety | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *immodestie (modern French immodestie), from Late Latin immodestia, from im- (not) + modestia (modesty). Ultimately from Latin modus (measure, manner).
