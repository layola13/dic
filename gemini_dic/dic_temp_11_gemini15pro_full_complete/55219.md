disseverance:/dɪˈsɛvərəns/
n.
断绝，分离，决裂
The disseverance of the two countries led to years of conflict.  这两个国家的决裂导致了多年的冲突。
The disseverance of church and state is a fundamental principle of democracy. 政教分离是民主的基本原则。
近义词：separation, division, rupture, disjunction
反义词：union, connection, junction
词性：名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *disseverantia, from disseverant- (stem of disseverans), present participle of disseverare "to separate, sever," from dis- "apart" + severare "to separate, sever," from severus "serious, strict, severe."
