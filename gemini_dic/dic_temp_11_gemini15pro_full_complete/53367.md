disaffiliation:/dɪsˌəˌfɪliˈeɪʃən/
n.|脱离，断绝关系|The act of formally withdrawing from an organization, affiliation, or group. |The union voted for disaffiliation from the national organization. 工会投票决定脱离国家组织。|

反义词:affiliation, association, union, connection
词性:名词
