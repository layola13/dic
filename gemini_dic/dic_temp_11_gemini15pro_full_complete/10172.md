apophysis:/əˈpɒfɪsɪs/
noun
骨突; (矿物)附加物; (植物)侧枝
A natural outgrowth or projection from a bone or other part of an organism, or a mineral outgrowth on a rock.
The doctor pointed to the apophysis on the X-ray, explaining it was a bony outgrowth. 医生指着X光片上的骨突，解释说那是一个骨质增生。
The apophysis on the quartz crystal made it a unique specimen. 石英晶体上的附加物使它成为一个独特的标本。
outgrowth, projection, process, protuberance
