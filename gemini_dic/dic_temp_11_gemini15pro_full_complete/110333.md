lightfastness:/ˈlaɪtˌfæstnəs/
n.
耐光性，不褪色性
The ability of a pigment or dye to resist fading or discoloration when exposed to light.
This watercolor paint boasts excellent lightfastness, ensuring your artwork remains vibrant for years.  这种水彩颜料拥有极佳的耐光性，确保您的作品多年保持鲜艳。
The artist used paints with high lightfastness to prevent the colors from fading.  这位艺术家使用了具有高耐光性的颜料，以防止颜色褪色。
近义词：colorfastness, fade resistance, permanence
反义词：fugitiveness, instability (of color)
