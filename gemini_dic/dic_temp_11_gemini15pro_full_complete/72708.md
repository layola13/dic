focusable:/ˈfoʊkəsəbəl/| adj.|可聚焦的，可获得焦点的|Able to be focused on; able to receive focus.  This term is often used in the context of computer interfaces, referring to elements that can be selected by the user, typically with a keyboard or other input device.|The button on the webpage is focusable, allowing users to select it using the Tab key.|近义词：selectable, accessible|反义词:unfocusable, inaccessible|词性:adjective
