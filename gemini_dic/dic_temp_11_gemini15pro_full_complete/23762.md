bravo: /ˈbrɑːvoʊ/ (US) /ˈbrɑːvəʊ/ (UK) | int. n. | 好！/喝彩；亡命之徒 | An exclamation of praise or approval, especially at a performance. Also, a desperate criminal, especially one who is a member of a gang. | The audience shouted "Bravo!" after the performance. 演出结束后，观众高呼“好！” |  近义词: well done, excellent, hurrah, bandit, outlaw | 反义词: boo, terrible | 词性: interjection, noun
