felicitous:/fɪˈlɪsɪtəs/
adj.
恰当的；贴切的；幸福的
well chosen or suited to the circumstances.  Marked by happiness or good fortune.
a felicitous phrase  一个贴切的短语
a felicitous occasion 一个幸福的场合
近义词：apt, suitable, appropriate, fitting, happy, fortunate, lucky
反义词：inappropriate, unsuitable, unfitting, unhappy, unfortunate, unlucky
词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *felicitosus (lucky, happy, fruitful), from felix (lucky, happy, fruitful) + -osus (suffix forming adjectives meaning 'full of,' 'prone to,' or 'characterized by').  The English word felicitousness is derived from the adjective felicitous by adding the suffix -ness, which forms nouns denoting a state or quality.
