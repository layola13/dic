maestro:/ˈmaɪˌestroʊ/ (US) /ˈmaɪstrəʊ/ (UK)| n.|大师；名家；乐队指挥|A distinguished musician, especially a conductor, composer, or teacher. | The maestro led the orchestra with passion and precision.  大师充满激情和精准地指挥着乐队。|  The renowned maestro composed some of the most beautiful music of our time.  这位著名的音乐大师创作了我们这个时代一些最优美的音乐。 |Synonyms: virtuoso, expert, master, conductor, composer| Antonyms: amateur, beginner, novice| noun
