mate:/meɪt/ /mæt/| n. v.|朋友，伙伴；配偶；(动物)配偶；(用作称呼)老兄，老弟；使…交配|Etymology:  From Middle Low German *māte (“companion, table companion”), from Proto-Germanic *ga-mōtō (“assembly person”), from *mōtō (“meeting”). Cognate with Dutch maat (“mate, fellow, gauge”), German Maat (“mate, fellow”), Swedish mått (“measure”).|

例句：
He's my best mate. = 他是我最好的朋友。

She met her mate at university. = 她在大学里遇到了她的伴侣。


近义词：friend, pal, buddy, partner, companion, spouse|
反义词：enemy, foe, opponent|
词性：名词|


mate:/meɪt/ /mæt/| v. |(使)交配|Etymology:  From Middle Low German *māte (“companion, table companion”), from Proto-Germanic *ga-mōtō (“assembly person”), from *mōtō (“meeting”). Cognate with Dutch maat (“mate, fellow, gauge”), German Maat (“mate, fellow”), Swedish mått (“measure”).  The verb sense developed from the noun, referring to animals being paired for breeding.|

例句：
The farmer mated his prize bull with several cows. = 农民把他最好的公牛和几头母牛交配。

Pandas are notoriously difficult to mate in captivity. = 熊猫在圈养环境下很难交配，这是众所周知的。


近义词：breed, pair, couple|
反义词：separate|
词性：动词|
