bodhisattva:/ˌboʊdiˈsɑːtvə/ /ˌbɒdiˈsʌtvə/| n.|菩萨|A person who is able to reach nirvana but delays doing so out of compassion in order to save suffering beings.|例句：The bodhisattva vowed to help all beings achieve enlightenment.  菩萨发愿帮助一切众生觉悟。

例句：She is revered as a bodhisattva for her selfless service to others. 她因无私奉献他人而被尊为菩萨。|近义词：saint, enlightened being|反义词:demon, evil spirit|词性:noun
