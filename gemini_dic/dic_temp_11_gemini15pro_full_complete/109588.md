lethalness:/ˈliːθəlnəs/| n. |致命性，致死率|The lethalness of the virus is a major concern. 这种病毒的致命性是一个主要问题。|例句：The lethalness of the new strain is significantly higher.  这种新毒株的致命性要高得多。

The researchers are studying the lethalness of different doses of the drug. 研究人员正在研究不同剂量药物的致命性。|近义词：fatality, deadliness, mortality|反义词:harmlessness, safety|词性:名词

词源: From lethal + -ness.  Lethal comes from Late Latin *lēthālis, from Latin lēthum "death."
