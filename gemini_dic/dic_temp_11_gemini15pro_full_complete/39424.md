connect:/kəˈnɛkt/ (US), /kəˈnɛkt/ (UK)| v. n. adj.|连接，联系，联络|to join or link two or more things together; to establish communication or a relationship|例句：The two cities are connected by a highway. 这两个城市由一条高速公路连接。|近义词：join, link, unite, associate, relate|反义词:disconnect, separate, detach|词性:verb

connect:/kəˈnɛkt/ (US), /kəˈnɛkt/ (UK)| n.|连接，联系，联络|a relationship or link between things or people|例句：There is a strong connect between the two families. 这两个家庭之间有很强的联系。|近义词：link, relationship, association, tie, bond|反义词:separation, division|词性:noun

connect:/kəˈnɛkt/ (US), /kəˈnɛkt/ (UK)| adj.|连接的，关联的|joined or linked together|例句：The rooms are connect. 这些房间是相连的。|近义词：joined, linked, related, associated|反义词:disconnected, separate, unrelated|词性:adjective
