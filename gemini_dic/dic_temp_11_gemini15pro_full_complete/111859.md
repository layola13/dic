logined:/ˈlɒɡɪnd/ (US) /ˈlɒɡɪnd/ (UK)| v. |已登录|This word is non-standard. The standard past tense and past participle of "login" (which itself can be considered non-standard, with "log in" being preferred) is "logged in". It signifies the action of having successfully authenticated oneself to a computer system or online service.|Example (using correct form): I logged in to my email account. 我登录了我的邮箱账户。|近义词：signed in, authenticated|反义词:logged out, signed out|词性:Verb (non-standard)
