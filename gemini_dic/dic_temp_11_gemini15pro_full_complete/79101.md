glad-handing:/ˈɡlædˌhændɪŋ/
noun
热情握手；虚情假意的寒暄；拉拢人心
The act of shaking hands with someone in an overly friendly and enthusiastic way, often in order to gain their favor or support.
The politician worked the room, glad-handing everyone in sight.  这位政客在房间里来回走动，对每个人都热情握手。
Glad-handing is common practice at political rallies. 在政治集会上，虚情假意的寒暄是常见的做法。
Synonyms: schmoozing, hobnobbing, backslapping
Antonyms: aloofness, coldness, indifference
