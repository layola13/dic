amalgam:/əˈmælɡəm/| n.|汞合金；混合物|A mixture or blend of different elements.|The band's music is an amalgam of jazz, rock, and blues.  这个乐队的音乐融合了爵士、摇滚和蓝调。|例句：The city is an amalgam of old and new buildings. 这座城市是新旧建筑的混合体。|近义词：mixture, blend, combination, fusion|反义词：separation, division|名词

amalgamate:/əˈmælɡəmeɪt/| v.|合并；混合|To combine or unite to form one organization or structure.|The two companies amalgamated to form a larger corporation.  两家公司合并成了一家更大的公司。|例句：Several small political parties amalgamated to form a single larger party.  几个小政党合并成了一个更大的政党。|近义词：merge, combine, unite, consolidate|反义词：separate, divide|动词
