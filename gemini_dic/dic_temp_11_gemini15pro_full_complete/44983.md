crystallizability:/ˌkrɪstələˈzaɪzəbɪləti/ | n. | 结晶性，结晶能力 | The ability of a substance to form crystals.  The crystallizability of this chemical compound is high under controlled conditions. | 例句：The slow cooling process enhances the crystallizability of the solution.  缓慢的冷却过程增强了溶液的结晶性。

例句：The crystallizability of sugar is a key factor in candy making. 糖的结晶性是糖果制作的关键因素。

近义词：crystallization propensity, tendency to crystallize | 反义词: amorphousness, non-crystallizability | 词性: noun
