immodestly:/ɪˈmɑːdɪstli/ | adv. | 不谦虚地，不客气地 | In a way that lacks modesty; boastfully. | He immodestly boasted about his achievements. 他不谦虚地吹嘘自己的成就。| She spoke immodestly of her own talents. 她不谦虚地谈论自己的才能。 | 近义词: boastfully, arrogantly, presumptuously | 反义词: modestly, humbly, unassumingly | 词性: 副词

immodest:/ɪˈmɑːdɪst/ | adj. | 不谦虚的，不客气的 | Having or showing a lack of modesty. | It was immodest of him to brag about his wealth. 他吹嘘自己的财富是不谦虚的。 |  She wore an immodest dress to the party. 她穿着一件不庄重的裙子去参加聚会。| 近义词: boastful, arrogant, presumptuous | 反义词: modest, humble, unassuming | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Late Middle English: from im- ‘not’ + modest + -ly.
