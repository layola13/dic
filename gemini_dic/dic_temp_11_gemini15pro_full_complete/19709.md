bilinguals:/baɪˈlɪŋɡwəlz/| n.|双语者|People who can speak two languages fluently. | Many bilinguals find it easier to learn a third language. 许多双语者发现学习第三语言更容易。| synonyms: polyglots, multilinguals | antonyms: monolinguals | noun

bilingual:/baɪˈlɪŋɡwəl/| adj.|双语的|Able to speak two languages fluently. | She grew up in a bilingual household, speaking both English and Spanish. 她在一个双语家庭长大，会说英语和西班牙语。| synonyms: multilingual | antonyms: monolingual | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From bi- (two) + lingual (relating to language).  Ultimately from Latin *bi- (two) and lingua (tongue, language).
