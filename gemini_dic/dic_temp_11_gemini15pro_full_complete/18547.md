beneficing:/ˈbɛnɪfɪsɪŋ/| v. |  授予圣职；使受益 |present participle of benefice.  The act of providing someone with a church living. | The bishop was beneficing several young priests. 主教正在授予几个年轻的牧师圣职。|近义词: endowing, enriching, benefiting |反义词: harming, depriving |词性: Verb (present participle)

benefice: /ˈbɛnɪfɪs/ | n. | 圣俸，有俸圣职；封地 | A church office providing a regular income, or the income itself.  Historically, it could also refer to a fief or estate. | He was appointed to the benefice of St. Michael's Church. 他被任命到圣迈克尔教堂的圣职。 | 近义词: living, incumbency, pastorate, fief | 反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider secular employment) | 词性: Noun

词源:
From Old French *benefice*, from Latin *beneficium* "benefit, kindness, favor," from *bene* "well" + *facere* "to do."
