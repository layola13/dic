muriform:/ˈmjʊərɪfɔːrm/
adjective
多室的，隔成许多小格的，砖墙状的
Resembling a brick wall; divided into many small compartments by intersecting walls.
The fungus has muriform spores.  这种真菌具有多室孢子。
The artist created a muriform sculpture using small, interconnected blocks.  这位艺术家用小的、相互连接的块创作了一个多室雕塑。

近义词: multicellular, compartmentalized, locular
反义词: unicellular, non-compartmentalized


词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *mūrīformis (“wall-shaped”), from mūrus (“wall”) + -formis.
