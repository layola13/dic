aphoristic:/ˌæfəˈrɪstɪk/
adj.
格言式的；警句的；简洁的
terse and witty and like a maxim; concise
Her aphoristic writing style was admired by many. 她的格言式写作风格受到许多人的赞赏。

His speech was peppered with aphoristic comments. 他的演讲充满了格言式的评论。

近义词: pithy, concise, epigrammatic, succinct, terse
反义词: prolix, verbose, rambling, lengthy, diffuse
词性: adjective
