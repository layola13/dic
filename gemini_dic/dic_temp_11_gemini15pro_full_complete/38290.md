concave:/ˈkɒnkeɪv/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnkeɪv/ (US)| adj.|凹的，凹面的|Curving inwards, like the inside of a bowl.|The mirror was concave, distorting the reflection.  这面镜子是凹的，使反射的影像变形。|The satellite dish has a concave surface. 卫星天线有一个凹面。|近义词：hollow, depressed, sunken|反义词:convex, bulging|词性:形容词

concave:/ˈkɒnkeɪv/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnkeɪv/ (US)| n.|凹面，凹形|A surface that curves inwards.|The concave of the lens focuses the light.  透镜的凹面使光线聚焦。|The artist used the concave of his hand to mix the paints.  艺术家用手心（凹面）来混合颜料。|近义词：hollow, depression|反义词:convexity, bulge|词性:名词
