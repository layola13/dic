apprenticeship: /əˈprɛntɪsʃɪp/
n.
学徒期，学徒身份
A period of time working as an apprentice.
He served his apprenticeship as a carpenter.  他做过木工学徒。

She completed a three-year apprenticeship in graphic design. 她完成了三年的平面设计学徒期。


近义词: traineeship, internship, apprenticeship
反义词: mastery, expertise


词性: noun

词源: From Middle English *aprentis-ship, equivalent to apprentice +‎ -ship.
