ilvaite:/ˈɪlvaɪt/| n. |伊瓦石，黑柱石|A black, brittle mineral, a sorosilicate of calcium, iron, and manganese, CaFe2+2Fe3+Si2O7O(OH).  It occurs as prismatic crystals in metamorphic rocks.|The ilvaite crystals were well-formed and lustrous. 伊瓦石晶体形态完整，光泽度好。|Synonyms: yenite, lievrite|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
