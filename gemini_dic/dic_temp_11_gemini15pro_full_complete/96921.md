inexposure:/ɪnɪkˈspəʊʒə(r)/ /ɪnɪkˈspoʊʒər/ |n.|缺乏经验，未接触|lack of exposure or experience|例句：His inexposure to the harsh realities of life made him naive. 他缺乏对生活残酷现实的接触，这使他很天真。

例句：Her inexposure to different cultures limited her perspective. 她缺乏对不同文化的接触，限制了她的视野。|近义词：unfamiliarity, naivete, ignorance|反义词：experience, familiarity, sophistication|词性:名词

词源:  "in-" (not) + "exposure" (the state of being exposed to something).  The prefix "in-" negates the meaning of "exposure."
