congeal:/kənˈdʒiːl/
verb
凝固，冻结
(of a liquid or soft substance) become thick or solid, especially as a result of cooling.
The blood had congealed into a thick, dark mass. 血液已经凝结成一团浓稠的黑色物质。
solidify, thicken, harden, set, clot, coagulate
melt, liquefy
词性:动词
