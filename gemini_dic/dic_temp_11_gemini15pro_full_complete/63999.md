epistolet:/ɪˈpɪstəl/ | n. | 书信；长信；（尤指）使徒书信 | A letter, especially a formal or didactic one; often used in reference to the letters of the apostles in the New Testament. | Example 1:  He penned a lengthy epistole to his friend detailing his travels. 他给他的朋友写了一封长信，详细描述了他的旅行。

Example 2: The pastor's sermon was based on an epistole of Paul. 牧师的布道是基于保罗的一封使徒书信。 | Synonym: letter, missive, communication | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but consider) conversation, dialogue | Noun
