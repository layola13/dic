fair-traders:/ˈfeərˌtreɪdərz/
|n.|公平贸易者|People who support and/or participate in fair trade practices, often by buying goods produced under fair trade conditions.  They believe in ensuring that producers in developing countries receive a fair price for their products.|Many fair-traders choose to buy coffee that is certified as fair trade. 许多公平贸易者选择购买获得公平贸易认证的咖啡。|
|Consumers who actively support fair trade principles are considered fair-traders.  积极支持公平贸易原则的消费者被视为公平贸易者。|
|Synonyms: ethical consumers, responsible shoppers|Antonyms:  exploiters (although not a direct antonym, it represents the opposite principle)|Noun|
