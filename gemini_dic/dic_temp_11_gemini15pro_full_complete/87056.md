heptagonal:/hɛpˈtæɡənəl/
adjective
七边形的; 七角形的
Having seven sides and seven angles.
A heptagonal building is being constructed in the city center.  市中心正在建造一座七边形的建筑。
The playground is heptagonal in shape.  操场是七边形的。
synonym:septagonal
antonym:circular, triangular, square, pentagonal, hexagonal, octagonal, etc. (any shape other than heptagonal)
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hepta- (seven) + -gōnos (angled), from gōnia (angle, corner)
