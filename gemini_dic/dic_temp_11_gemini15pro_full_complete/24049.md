breezing:/ˈbriːzɪŋ/| v. |轻松地行动，轻而易举地完成|Moving or accomplishing something easily and quickly. |She breezed through the exam. 她轻松地通过了考试。|He breezed into the room, full of confidence. 他充满自信地轻快地走进房间。|近义词：gliding, coasting, sailing,  |反义词:struggling, toiling|verb

breezy:/ˈbriːzi/| adj. |轻松愉快的，有微风的|Cheerful and relaxed; slightly windy.|She had a breezy manner. 她态度轻松愉快。|It was a breezy afternoon. 那是一个有微风的下午。|近义词：airy, light, carefree, windy|反义词:gloomy, serious, still, calm|adjective
