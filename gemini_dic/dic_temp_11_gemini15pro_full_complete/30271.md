cement:/sɪˈment/ | n. vt. | 水泥；胶泥；粘合剂；巩固，加强 | A powdery substance made with calcined lime and clay. It is mixed with water to form mortar or mixed with sand, gravel, and water to make concrete. |  The workers mixed the cement and sand. 工人们把水泥和沙子混合在一起。|  They cemented the bricks together.  他们用水泥把砖块粘合在一起。 | synonyms: concrete, mortar, glue, bond, consolidate, strengthen | antonyms: weaken, separate, dissolve | noun/verb

cement:/sɪˈment/ | v. | 巩固，加强 | To make something stronger, more certain, etc. | The agreement cemented the relationship between the two countries.  这项协议巩固了两国之间的关系。 | synonyms: solidify, strengthen, fortify, reinforce | antonyms: weaken, undermine, impair | verb
