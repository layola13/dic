disbelieve:/ˌdɪsbɪˈliːv/
verb
不信；怀疑
To refuse to believe; to not accept as true.
She disbelieved his story about winning the lottery. 她不相信他中了彩票的故事。
近义词：doubt, question, mistrust
反义词：believe, trust, accept
词性：v.

Etymology:
From dis- (expressing negation) + believe (from Old English belīefan "to believe").
