divulge:/dɪˈvʌldʒ/ | v. | 泄露，透露 | To make known (private or sensitive information). |  He refused to divulge the source of his information. 他拒绝透露消息来源。

The company was reluctant to divulge its financial details. 该公司不愿透露其财务细节。

| 近义词: reveal, disclose, unveil, expose | 反义词: conceal, hide, suppress | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin divulgare "to make public, publish," from dis- "widely" + vulgare "to make common, publish," related to vulgus "the common people."
