downtroddennesses:/ˌdaʊnˈtrɒdn̩nɪsɪz/ | n. | 被压迫的状态，受欺凌的状态 |  The state of being downtrodden; oppression and degradation.  | The downtroddennesses of the working class in the 19th century were appalling. 19世纪工人阶级的受压迫状态令人震惊。The persistent downtroddennesses of certain communities require urgent attention.  某些社群持续受压迫的状态需要紧急关注。 | 近义词: oppression, subjugation, suppression, repression | 反义词: empowerment, liberation, freedom | 词性: 名词


词源:  From downtrodden + -ness.  Downtrodden itself comes from down + trodden, the past participle of tread.
