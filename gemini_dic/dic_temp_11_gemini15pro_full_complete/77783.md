generalising:/ˈdʒɛnərəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
概括，归纳
The process of forming a general statement or concept by inferring from specific cases.
Scientists are generalising from their research to form a new theory. 科学家们正在从他们的研究中进行概括，以形成一个新的理论。
He is generalising from his own experience. 他正在从他自己的经验中进行概括。
generalize, summarizing, abstracting
specifying, particularizing
v.
