overfulfilling: /ˌōvərfulˈfɪlɪŋ/
adjective
过度完成的，超额完成的
Going above and beyond in fulfilling a requirement or expectation.
The team overfulfilled their sales target by 20%.  该团队超额完成了20%的销售目标。
He overfulfilled his duties and impressed his boss. 他超额完成了他的职责，给他的老板留下了深刻的印象。
近义词: exceeding, surpassing, overachieving
反义词: underfulfilling, underachieving
词性: adjective

overfulfill: /ˌōvərfulˈfɪl/
verb
过度完成，超额完成
To fulfill something to a greater extent than required or expected.
The factory overfulfilled its production quota for the quarter.  这家工厂超额完成了本季度的生产配额。
She consistently overfulfills her responsibilities. 她总是超额完成她的职责。
近义词: exceed, surpass, overachieve
反义词: underfulfill, underachieve
词性: verb


词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "fulfill" (verb meaning "to carry out or bring to completion")
