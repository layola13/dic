absolutistic:/ˌæbsəˈluːtɪstɪk/
adjective
专制的，绝对主义的
Exercising or favoring absolute power; autocratic.  Characterized by belief in absolute principles.
例句：The king ruled with an absolutistic hand, brooking no dissent. 国王以专制的手段统治，不容许任何异议。

例句：Her absolutistic approach to parenting left her children with little autonomy. 她专制的育儿方式使得她的孩子们几乎没有自主权。

近义词：autocratic, dictatorial, tyrannical, despotic
反义词：democratic, liberal, constitutional
词性：adjective


词源：
From absolute + -ist + -ic.  Ultimately from Latin absolūtus, past participle of absolvere "to absolve, acquit, finish, complete," from ab- "off, away from" + solvere "to loosen, detach, solve, pay."
