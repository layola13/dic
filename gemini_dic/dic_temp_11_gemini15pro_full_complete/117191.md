measured:/ˈmeʒərd/ | adj. | 慎重的，有分寸的；精确的，仔细测量的 | Carefully considered or controlled; determined with precision |  She spoke in a measured tone, choosing her words carefully. 她说话的语气很谨慎，字斟句酌。


The tailor took measured steps to ensure the suit fit perfectly. 裁缝采取精确的步骤以确保西装完美合身。 | 近义词: careful, deliberate, precise, calculated | 反义词: rash, impulsive, careless, inaccurate | 词性: 形容词


词源: From Middle English *mesuren, from Old French mesurer, from Latin mēnsūrāre (“to measure”), from mēnsūra (“measure”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *mē- (“to measure”).
