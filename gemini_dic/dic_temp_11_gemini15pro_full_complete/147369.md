overneglected: /ˌoʊvər nɪˈɡlɛktɪd/
adjective
过度忽视的; 严重疏忽的
Severely neglected; not given enough care or attention.
The overneglected garden was overgrown with weeds.  过度被忽视的花园里杂草丛生。
The overneglected children showed signs of emotional distress.  被严重忽视的孩子们表现出情绪困扰的迹象。
Synonym: disregarded, ignored, overlooked
Antonym: cherished, nurtured, cared for
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From over- + neglected.  "Over-" acts as an intensifier, meaning "excessively" or "too much."  "Neglected" comes from the Latin word "neglectus," the past participle of "neglegere," meaning "to disregard" or "to neglect."
