balaclava:/ˌbæləˈklɑːvə/ | n. |巴拉克拉瓦帽，面罩|A close-fitting garment covering the whole head and neck except for parts of the face, typically made of wool.|The soldiers wore balaclavas to protect their faces from the cold. 士兵们戴着巴拉克拉瓦帽以保护他们的脸不受冻。|Synonyms: ski mask, face mask|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps uncovered face)|noun

balaclavas:/ˌbæləˈklɑːvəz/ | n. |巴拉克拉瓦帽，面罩（复数）|Plural form of balaclava, referring to more than one such garment.|The robbers wore balaclavas to conceal their identities.  劫匪们戴着巴拉克拉瓦帽来掩盖他们的身份。|Synonyms: ski masks, face masks|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|noun
