analogue's:/ˈænəlɒɡz/ |n. |类似物；模拟信号|Something that is similar or comparable to something else; a signal that represents data as a continuous variable.| This drug is an analogue of morphine. 这种药物是吗啡的类似物。| synonyms: counterpart, equivalent, parallel | antonyms: digital, original | noun

analogue's:/ˈænəlɒɡz/ |adj. |模拟的| Relating to or using signals or information represented by a continuously variable physical quantity such as spatial position or voltage.|  The analogue's clock face showed the time clearly.  这个模拟钟的钟面清楚地显示了时间。| synonyms: nondigital | antonyms: digital | adjective
