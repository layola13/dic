dwindle:/ˈdwɪndl/| v. |减少，缩小，衰落|To become smaller and smaller; to decrease gradually in size, amount, or strength. |例句：The town's population has dwindled to a mere few hundred.  这个城镇的人口已经减少到只有几百人。

例句：With each passing year, the number of wild tigers dwindles.  随着时间的推移，野生老虎的数量逐年减少。|近义词：decrease, diminish, shrink, decline|反义词:increase, grow, expand|词性:动词
