ossivorous:/ɒˈsɪvərəs/
adjective
食骨的；以骨为食的
Feeding on bone.
Hyena are ossivorous animals.  鬣狗是食骨动物。
Ossivorous insects help break down carcasses. 食骨昆虫有助于分解尸体。
近义词: bone-eating
反义词: herbivorous, carnivorous (in the strict sense, as these don't primarily eat bone)
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *oss-, os "bone" + -vorous "eating".
