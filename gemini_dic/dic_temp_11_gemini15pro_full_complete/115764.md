marinate:/ˈmærɪneɪt/ |v. |腌制|To soak food in a marinade, a seasoned liquid, before cooking. |例句：I marinated the chicken in a mixture of soy sauce, ginger, and garlic. 我把鸡肉浸泡在酱油、姜和大蒜的混合物中腌制。

例句：She marinated the tofu for several hours before grilling it. 她把豆腐腌制了几个小时才开始烤。|近义词：soak, steep|反义词:|词性:动词

marinate:/ˈmærɪneɪt/ |n. |腌泡汁|A seasoned liquid in which food is marinated. |例句：The chef prepared a special marinate for the fish.  厨师为鱼准备了特殊的腌泡汁。

例句：This steak marinate is delicious and easy to make.  这款牛排腌泡汁美味且易于制作。|近义词：marinade |反义词:|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *mariner*, "to pickle in seawater," from *marin*, "sea," ultimately from Latin *marinus*, also meaning "of the sea".
