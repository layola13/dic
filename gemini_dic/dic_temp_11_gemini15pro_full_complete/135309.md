nonpaternally:/ˌnɒnpəˈtɜːnəli/
adverb
非父系地，非经父系遗传地
Not inherited or derived from the father.
The trait is transmitted nonpaternally.  这个特征是非父系遗传的。
Mitochondrial DNA is inherited nonpaternally. 线粒体DNA是母系遗传的。
Synonym: maternally
Antonym: paternally
词性: 副词
