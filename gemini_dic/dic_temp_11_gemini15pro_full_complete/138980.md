nylons:/ˈnaɪlɒnz/ (UK) /ˈnaɪlɑːnz/ (US)| n.|尼龙, 尼龙制品|A tough, lightweight synthetic fiber or fabric, used especially in clothing and to make ropes and bristles.|She wore a pair of sheer nylons.|近义词:polyamide, synthetic fiber|反义词:natural fiber|词性:noun

词源:
The word "nylon" is a genericized trademark coined by DuPont.  While its exact origin is debated, it's thought to be a modification of "no-run" (referring to stockings) or a completely fabricated word.  It became a generic term for polyamides.
