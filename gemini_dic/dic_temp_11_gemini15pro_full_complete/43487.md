crater:/ˈkreɪtər/| n.|火山口；陨石坑；弹坑|A bowl-shaped depression in the ground or on the surface of a planet or moon, typically caused by the impact of a meteorite or by a volcanic eruption.|The meteor left a massive crater in the Arizona desert. 这颗流星在亚利桑那沙漠留下了一个巨大的陨石坑。|近义词：cavity, hole, pit, depression|反义词: mound, hill|词性:名词
cratered:/ˈkreɪtərd/ | adj. |布满坑洞的；有坑的|Marked with craters.|The moon's surface is heavily cratered. 月球表面布满了坑洞。|近义词：pitted, pocked|反义词: smooth, flat|词性:形容词
