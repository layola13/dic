newsreader: /ˈnjuːzˌriːdər/ (British English), /ˈnuːzˌriːdər/ (American English)
| n. |新闻播报员；新闻主播|A person who reads the news on television or radio.|The newsreader announced the breaking news with a serious tone.  新闻播报员以严肃的语气播报了突发新闻。|例句：The newsreader delivered the news clearly and concisely. 新闻播报员清晰简洁地播报了新闻。|近义词：anchor, announcer, broadcaster, journalist, reporter|反义词: listener, viewer |noun
