mess-about:/ˈmes əˈbaʊt/ | noun | 胡闹，闲逛 | an act of messing about; playful or frivolous activity |  Their afternoon mess-about resulted in a broken window.  他们下午的胡闹导致窗户破裂。| synonyms: horseplay, tomfoolery | antonyms: serious work, diligent effort | noun
