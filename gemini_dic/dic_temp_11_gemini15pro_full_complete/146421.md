overfoolishness:/ˌoʊvərˈfuːlɪʃnəs/
|n.|过度愚蠢，极端的愚蠢|The act or state of being excessively foolish.|His overfoolishness led him to make a series of bad decisions. 他的过度愚蠢导致他做出了一系列糟糕的决定。|例句：Her overfoolishness cost her the job. 她的过度愚蠢让她丢了工作。|近义词：stupidity, folly, idiocy|反义词：wisdom, prudence, sagacity|词性：名词

词源：
"over" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi) + "foolish" (Middle English folisch, from Old English folisc) + "-ness" (Old English -nes(s), a suffix forming nouns of quality or state)
