hyperinflationary:/ˌhaɪpərɪnˈfleɪʃəneri/
adjective
恶性通货膨胀的
marked by or causing hyperinflation; relating to runaway inflation.
例句：The country's economy is in a hyperinflationary spiral.  这个国家的经济正处于恶性通货膨胀的螺旋式上升中。

例句：Hyperinflationary periods often lead to social unrest. 恶性通货膨胀时期往往会导致社会动荡。

近义词：inflationary, runaway
反义词：deflationary
词性：adjective


词源:
From hyper- + inflationary.  "Hyper-" is a prefix meaning "over, beyond, excessive," from Greek hyper "over, above, beyond." "Inflationary" relates to inflation, which comes from Late Latin inflatio(n-), from inflare "to blow into, inflate," from in- "in" + flare "to blow."
