extend:/ɪkˈstend/ /ɛkˈstɛnd/| v.|延长，延伸，扩展，提供，给予|To make something longer or larger in space or time; to offer or give something such as help, thanks, or an invitation.|The company extended the deadline by a week. 公司将截止日期延长了一周。|近义词：expand, lengthen, prolong, stretch, offer, give|反义词:shorten, contract, limit, reduce|词性:动词

extend:/ɪkˈstend/ /ɛkˈstɛnd/| n.|延长，延伸，扩展|An act of extending something; the area over which something extends.|The extend of the damage was not yet known.  损害的程度尚不清楚。|近义词：expansion, length, reach, range|反义词:limitation, restriction|词性:名词 (较少用)
