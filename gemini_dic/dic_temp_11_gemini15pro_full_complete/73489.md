forerunner:/ˈfɔːrˌrʌnər/ (US); /ˈfɔːˌrʌnə(r)/ (UK)| n.|先驱，前兆，预兆|A person or thing that comes before another of the same kind; a predecessor.|The Model T Ford was a forerunner of modern automobiles. 福特T型车是现代汽车的先驱。|precursor, predecessor, herald, harbinger|follower, successor|noun


forerunner:/ˈfɔːrˌrʌnər/ (US); /ˈfɔːˌrʌnə(r)/ (UK)| n.|前兆，预报|A sign or warning of something to come.|Dark clouds are often a forerunner of a storm.  乌云通常是暴风雨的前兆。|omen, portent, sign, indication|aftermath, consequence|noun
