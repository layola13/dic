lumpsucker:/ˈlʌmpˌsʌkər/| n. | 圆鳍鱼，䲢鱼 |A type of fish with a sucker on its underside that it uses to attach itself to rocks or other surfaces.  It is also known as the lumpfish.| The lumpsucker clung to the rock in the strong current.  圆鳍鱼在强流中紧紧地贴在岩石上。| synonyms: lumpfish, sea hen | antonyms: N/A | noun

lumpsucker:/ˈlʌmpˌsʌkər/| n. |笨拙的人；懒汉| (informal) A clumsy or inactive person.| He called his brother a lumpsucker for refusing to help with the chores. 他称他弟弟为懒汉，因为他拒绝帮忙做家务。| synonyms: slowpoke, sluggard | antonyms: go-getter, dynamo | noun
