docusoap:/ˈdɒkjuːˌsəʊp/ | n. |纪实肥皂剧；真人秀肥皂剧 | A television genre that combines documentary-style footage with the melodramatic elements of soap operas, often focusing on the lives of ordinary people.  |  Example:  The show "Laguna Beach" is a classic example of a docusoap. 拉古纳海滩是纪实肥皂剧的经典例子。

Example:  Docusoaps often blur the line between reality and fiction. 纪实肥皂剧经常模糊了现实和虚构的界限。 | 近义词: reality show, reality TV | 反义词: drama, fiction | 词性: noun
