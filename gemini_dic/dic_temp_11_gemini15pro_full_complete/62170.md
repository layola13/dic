encoding:/ɪnˈkəʊdɪŋ/ /ɛnˈkoʊdɪŋ/ | n. | 编码，代码 | The process of converting information from one form to another.  | Example: The encoding of the message was complex and difficult to decipher.  这条信息的编码很复杂，难以破译。

Example:  Character encoding is crucial for displaying text correctly on websites. 字符编码对于在网站上正确显示文本至关重要。 | Synonyms: coding, ciphering | Antonyms: decoding, deciphering | Noun
