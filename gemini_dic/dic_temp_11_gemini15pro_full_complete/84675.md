handcuffed:/ˈhændkʌft/ | adj. | 戴上手铐的；被束缚的 | Being restrained with handcuffs. | The police officer led the handcuffed suspect to the car. 警察把戴着手铐的嫌疑人带到了车上。|  The handcuffed prisoner was escorted to the courtroom. 戴着手铐的囚犯被押送到法庭。 | 近义词: shackled, manacled, restrained | 反义词: free, unrestrained | 词性:形容词

Etymology: From "handcuff" (noun and verb) + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Handcuff" itself is a compound of "hand" and "cuff" (originally referring to a cuff on a sleeve).
