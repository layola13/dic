erotesis: /ɪrəˈtiːsɪs/ /ɛrəˈtiːsɪs/ | n. | 反问; 提问 | A rhetorical question implying strong affirmation or denial of its own terms. |  "Is the Pope Catholic?" is an erotesis.  “教皇是天主教徒吗？”这是一个反问句。| synonyms: rhetorical question, interrogation, query | antonyms: statement, declaration, assertion | noun
