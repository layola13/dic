didymium:/dɪˈdɪmiəm/
noun
镨钕混合物;双线光谱元素(旧称，现已知为镨和钕的混合物)
A mixture of praseodymium and neodymium, formerly regarded as an element.
Didymium was historically used in glassmaking to color glass. 镨钕混合物过去曾用于玻璃制造中给玻璃着色。
Didymium goggles are used by glassblowers. 吹玻璃的工人使用镨钕护目镜。
Synonyms: none (as it's a specific mixture)
Antonyms: none
