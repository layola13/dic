larceny:/ˈlɑːrsəni/ | n. | 盗窃罪，偷窃 | The unlawful taking and carrying away of personal property with intent to deprive the rightful owner of it permanently. |  He was charged with grand larceny. 他被指控犯有重大盗窃罪。

larceny:/ˈlɑːrsəni/ | n. | 盗窃物 | The stolen goods themselves. | The police recovered the larceny. 警方找回了赃物。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *larcin, from Latin latrocinium "robbery," from latro "robber, bandit."

SYNONYMS->
theft, stealing, robbery, pilfering, purloining

ANTONYMS->
returning, restoring, giving

PART OF SPEECH->
noun
