impermeable:/ɪmˈpɜːrmiəbl/| adj.|不可渗透的；不透水的|Not allowing fluid to pass through.|The jacket is made of an impermeable material, keeping you dry in the rain. 这件夹克是用不透水材料制成的，让你在雨中保持干燥。|Synonyms: waterproof, watertight, impervious, impassable|Antonyms: permeable, porous, penetrable|adjective
