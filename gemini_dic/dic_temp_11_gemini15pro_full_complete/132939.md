nonembryonic:/ˌnɑːnˈɛmbriˈɑːnɪk/
adjective
非胚胎的
Not relating to, derived from, or characteristic of an embryo.
Nonembryonic stem cells are derived from adult tissues.  非胚胎干细胞来源于成体组织。
These nonembryonic stem cells show promise in treating various diseases. 这些非胚胎干细胞在治疗各种疾病方面显示出希望。
Synonyms:  postembryonic, adult
Antonyms:  embryonic
