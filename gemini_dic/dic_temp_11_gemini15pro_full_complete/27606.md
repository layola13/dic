cancellate: /ˈkænsəleɪt/ | v. |取消，删除，划掉| To cross out, delete, or invalidate something, often by marking it with lines. | The editor cancellated the inappropriate paragraph. 编辑删除了不合适的段落。|  He cancellated his subscription to the magazine. 他取消了杂志订阅。 | 近义词: cancel, cross out, delete, obliterate, expunge | 反义词: approve, confirm, validate, reinstate | 词性: 动词

词源: From Late Latin cancellatus, past participle of cancellare "to make like a lattice," from Latin cancelli "lattice."  The original meaning referred to crossing out writing with intersecting lines, resembling a lattice.
