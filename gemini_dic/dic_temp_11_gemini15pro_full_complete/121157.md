misdeveloping:/ˌmɪsdɪˈvɛləpɪŋ/
adjective
发育不良的，发展不正常的
Developing incorrectly or abnormally.
例句：The misdeveloping brain can lead to a range of cognitive impairments. 发育不良的大脑会导致一系列的认知障碍。

例句：The report highlighted the problem of misdeveloping economies in the region.  报告强调了该区域经济发展不正常的问题。

近义词：maldeveloping, underdeveloped, stunted
反义词：developing, thriving, flourishing
词性：adjective


Etymology:
mis- + developing

mis-:  Old English *mis-, from Proto-Germanic *missa- "wrongly, amiss," from PIE root *mei- (1) "to change, go, move."  Meaning "badly, wrongly" in English from c. 1200.

developing:  from develop + -ing.  Develop comes from Old French desveloper "unwrap, unfold, reveal," from des- "un-, out" (from Latin dis-) + voloper "to wrap," of uncertain origin.  The meaning "grow, expand" in English is first recorded 1755.
