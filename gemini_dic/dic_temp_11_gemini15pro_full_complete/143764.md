outmastered:/ˌaʊtˈmæstərd/
verb
胜过，技高一筹
surpassed in skill or expertise, defeated by greater skill
He was completely outmastered by his opponent in the chess match. 他在象棋比赛中被对手彻底击败。
近义词: outmaneuvered, outsmarted, defeated, conquered, vanquished
反义词: defeated, lost to
