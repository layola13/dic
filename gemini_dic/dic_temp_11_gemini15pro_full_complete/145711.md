overcovetous: /ˌoʊvərˈkʌvɪtəs/ | adj. | 贪得无厌的，过度贪婪的 | Excessively covetous; desiring more than one's share or needs.  Marked by inordinate desire for wealth or possessions or for another's possessions. | He was an overcovetous man, always wanting more than he had. 他是一个贪得无厌的人，总是想要更多。| Synonyms: greedy, avaricious, grasping, rapacious | Antonyms: generous, selfless, altruistic | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *overcoveitous*, from *over-* + *coveitous*, from Old French *coveitos*, from Latin *cupidus* ("desirous, eager, passionate"), from *cupere* ("to desire").
