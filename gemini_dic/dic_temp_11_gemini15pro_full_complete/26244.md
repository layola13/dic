butterweight:/ˈbʌtərˌweɪt/| n.|蝇量级，奶油重量|A weight class in boxing and other combat sports, typically between 108 and 112 pounds (49 and 51 kilograms).  Also, can refer to something light as butter.|He's a natural butterweight, never exceeding 110 pounds. 他天生就是个蝇量级，体重从未超过110磅。|Synonym: flyweight|Antonym: heavyweight|noun
