countersuit:/ˈkaʊntərˌsuːt/
n.|反诉，反请求|A lawsuit filed in response to an initial lawsuit.|The company filed a countersuit against its former employee for breach of contract.  该公司对其前雇员提起反诉，指控其违反合同。|Example: After being sued for damages, the defendant filed a countersuit.  在被起诉赔偿损失后，被告提出了反诉。|Synonyms: counterclaim, cross-action, cross-claim|Antonyms: lawsuit, claim|noun
