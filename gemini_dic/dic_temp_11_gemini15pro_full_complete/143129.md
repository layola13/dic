outcarolled: /ˌaʊtkəˈrɒld/ /ˌaʊtkəˈrɑːld/ | v. | (以歌声)胜过，超过 | To sing more beautifully or loudly than. | The nightingale outcarolled all the other birds in the forest. 夜莺的歌声胜过了森林里所有其他的鸟。|  She outcarolled her rivals in the singing competition. 她在歌唱比赛中以歌声胜过对手。 | 近义词：outsing, surpass, excel | 反义词：be outsung | 词性：动词

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning exceeding) + "carol" (verb meaning to sing joyfully).  源自古法语 "caroler"，最终源自晚期拉丁语 "carolare"，意为环舞歌曲。
