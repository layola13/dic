carcinoma:/kɑːrsɪˈnoʊmə/ /kɑːsɪˈnəʊmə/| n. |癌；癌肿|A malignant tumor formed from epithelial cells.  It is a type of cancer that starts in the tissues that line the inner or outer surfaces of the body. |例句：The biopsy revealed a squamous cell carcinoma.  活检显示为鳞状细胞癌。

例句：Lung carcinoma is a leading cause of cancer deaths. 肺癌是癌症死亡的主要原因之一。|近义词：cancer, malignancy, tumor, neoplasm|反义词: (没有医学上的反义词，可以考虑用“health”或“wellness”表示健康状态)|词性:noun
