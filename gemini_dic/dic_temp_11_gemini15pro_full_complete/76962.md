garbanzo:/ɡɑːrˈbɑːnzoʊ/ (US), /ɡɑːˈbænsoʊ/ (UK)| n. |鹰嘴豆|A round, light-brown legume used widely in Middle Eastern and Mediterranean cooking.  It is also called a chickpea.|例句：I added garbanzos to the salad for extra protein. 我在沙拉里加了鹰嘴豆以补充蛋白质。

例句：Hummus is made from blended garbanzos, tahini, lemon juice, and garlic.  鹰嘴豆泥是由混合鹰嘴豆、芝麻酱、柠檬汁和大蒜制成的。|近义词：chickpea|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
