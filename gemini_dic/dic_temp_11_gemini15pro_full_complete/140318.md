ogre:/ˈoʊɡər/ | n. | 食人魔，怪物，丑陋的人 | A cruel or terrifying person, especially one perceived as menacing or powerful; a hideous giant of folklore, typically having a large head and body and known for its savagery. |  The ogre in the fairy tale locked the princess in a tower.  童话故事里的食人魔把公主锁在塔里。

|  He's such an ogre to his employees.  他对他的员工真是个暴君。 | Synonyms: monster, giant, brute, fiend | Antonyms: hero, angel, saint | noun
