overprecisely:/ˌoʊvərprɪˈsaɪsli/ | adv. | 过于精确地，过分精确地 |  Defined as "in an excessively precise manner," indicating a level of detail or accuracy that is unnecessary or even counterproductive. | He described the incident overprecisely, making it sound more complicated than it was. 他过于精确地描述了这件事，使它听起来比实际情况更复杂。 | The instructions were written overprecisely, confusing the user with unnecessary details. 说明书写得过于精确，用不必要的细节使用户感到困惑。 | 近义词: too precisely, excessively precisely, meticulously, fastidiously | 反义词: vaguely, imprecisely, loosely | 词性: 副词

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively, too much") + "precisely" (adverb form of "precise")
"precise" comes from the Latin "praecisus," past participle of "praecidere" ("to cut off in front"), from "prae-" ("before") + "caedere" ("to cut").
