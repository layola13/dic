outfoxing:/ˌaʊtˈfɒksɪŋ/
verb
以智取胜，胜过，比…更狡猾
defeating someone by being more clever or cunning than they are
The company outfoxed its competitors by launching a new product earlier than expected. 这家公司通过比预期更早地推出新产品，智胜了竞争对手。
近义词: outsmarting, outmaneuvering, tricking
反义词: being outwitted, being outsmarted
