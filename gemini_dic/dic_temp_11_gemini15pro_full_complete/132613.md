nondirigible:/ˌnɒndɪˈrɪdʒəbl/ /ˌnɑːndɪˈrɪdʒəbl/
adjective
不可操纵的；非硬式飞艇
not able to be steered or controlled; (of an airship) not rigid
The nondirigible balloon drifted aimlessly across the sky.  这个不可操纵的气球漫无目的地飘过天空。
The Hindenburg was a dirigible, while many earlier airships were nondirigible.  兴登堡号是可操纵的飞艇，而许多早期的飞艇是不可操纵的。
synonyms: uncontrollable, unsteerable, unmanageable
antonyms: dirigible, controllable, steerable
词性: adjective
