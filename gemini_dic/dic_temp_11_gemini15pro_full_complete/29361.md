castrating: /ˈkæstreɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 阉割的，去势的 | The act of removing the testicles or ovaries, rendering a male or female animal or human incapable of reproduction. Also used figuratively to describe the suppression of power, vitality, or creativity. |  The farmer was castrating the young bulls.  农民正在阉割小公牛。 | Synonyms:  emasculating, neutering, gelding | Antonyms:  fertilizing, empowering | Verb form: castrate
