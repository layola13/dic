emotive:/ɪˈmoʊtɪv/ (US), /ɪˈməʊtɪv/ (UK)| adj.|引起情绪的；表达感情的；感性的|Arousing or able to arouse intense feeling.  Relating to the emotions.|例句：The music was highly emotive, bringing tears to many listeners' eyes. 这首音乐非常感人，让很多听众都流下了眼泪。

例句：His emotive speech roused the crowd to action. 他充满感情的演讲激励了群众行动起来。|近义词：emotional, affecting, moving, poignant, touching|反义词：unemotional, impassive, apathetic|词性:形容词

词源: Late 19th century: from French *émotif, -ive, from émouvoir ‘to move, stir’, from Latin emovere (see emotion).
