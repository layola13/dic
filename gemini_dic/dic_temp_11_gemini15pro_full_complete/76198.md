fustianist:/ˈfʌstiənɪst/| n.|夸夸其谈的人；浮夸的文风作者|A person who writes or speaks in a pompous or inflated style, characterized by bombast and insincerity.  A fustianist often uses elaborate language to disguise a lack of substance.|例句：The critic dismissed the author as a mere fustianist, full of sound and fury, signifying nothing. 评论家认为这位作者只是一个夸夸其谈的人，充满了喧嚣和愤怒，没有任何意义。

例句：His speeches were filled with fustian, leaving the audience unimpressed and bored. 他的演讲充满了浮夸之词，让观众毫无印象，感到无聊。|近义词：bombast, braggart, rhetorician|反义词：minimalist, conciseness writer|词性:noun
