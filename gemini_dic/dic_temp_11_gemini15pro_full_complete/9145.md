antinationalists:/ˌæntiˈnæʃənəlɪsts/
|n.|反民族主义者|People who oppose nationalism or patriotism, especially those who believe that nationalism is harmful or dangerous.|The antinationalists argued that patriotism was a dangerous force that led to war and conflict.|近义词：cosmopolitans, internationalists, globalists|反义词：nationalists, patriots|noun
