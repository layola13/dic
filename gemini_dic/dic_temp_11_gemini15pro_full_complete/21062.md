blindfold:/ˈblaɪndˌfoʊld/| n. v.|眼罩；蒙眼布；|A piece of cloth tied over someone's eyes to prevent them from seeing.|The kidnappers put a blindfold on the hostage. 绑匪给 hostage 戴上了眼罩。|近义词：blind, eyemask, mask|反义词:uncover, expose|词性:名词, 动词

blindfold:/ˌblaɪndˈfoʊld/| v.|蒙住…的眼睛；使…看不见|To cover someone's eyes with a blindfold.|They blindfolded the prisoners before leading them out. 他们把犯人的眼睛蒙上，然后带他们出去。|近义词：blind, cover, obscure|反义词:uncover, expose|词性:动词

词源：From "blind" (unable to see) + "fold" (a layer of cloth). 字面意思是“遮住眼睛的布”。
