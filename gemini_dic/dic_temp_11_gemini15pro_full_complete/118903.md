metatungstic:/ˌmɛtəˈtʌŋstɪk/ | adj. |偏钨酸的；含偏钨酸的| Relating to or containing metatungstic acid (H₂W₁₂O₄₀), a complex acid formed from tungstic acid. |Metatungstic acid is a strong acid used in various chemical processes. 偏钨酸是一种强酸，用于各种化学过程中。|  Metatungstic acid is a powerful catalyst. 偏钨酸是一种强效催化剂。 |近义词: polytungstic|反义词: N/A|词性: 形容词

Etymology:
meta- (Greek):  "after," "changed," "transformed," indicating a change in form or structure.
tungstic (from tungsten):  Relating to the element tungsten.
