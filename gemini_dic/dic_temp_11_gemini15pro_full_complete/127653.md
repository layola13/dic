neel:/niːl/| n. adj.|靛蓝，深蓝色;给…染上靛蓝色|A deep blue color, like that of a clear night sky.  Also used as a verb meaning to color something with this dye. |The artist used neel to create a dramatic nightscape. 艺术家用靛蓝创作了一幅引人注目的夜景图。|近义词：indigo, sapphire, azure|反义词：white, yellow|词性:名词, 形容词
词源: From Hindi नील (nīl), from Sanskrit नील (nīla), meaning "dark blue," related to Latin *niger ("black").
