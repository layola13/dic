coniferin:/kəˈnɪfərɪn/
noun
松柏苷
A glucoside found in the cambial sap of coniferous trees, which yields coniferyl alcohol on hydrolysis.
Coniferin is extracted from the sap of conifer trees.  松柏苷是从针叶树的汁液中提取的。
Coniferin plays a role in lignin biosynthesis in these trees.  松柏苷在这些树木的木质素生物合成中发挥作用。
