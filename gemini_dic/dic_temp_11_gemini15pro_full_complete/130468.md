nonabstention:/ˌnɒnæbˈstɛnʃən/
n.
不弃权，不 abstention
The act of not abstaining; participation, especially in voting.
The country's nonabstention in the vote signaled its commitment to the issue.  这个国家在投票中的不弃权表明了其对这个问题的承诺。
Near synonyms: participation, involvement, engagement
Antonyms: abstention, nonparticipation, withdrawal
noun
