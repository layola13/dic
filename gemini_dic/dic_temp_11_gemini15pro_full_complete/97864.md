inlock:/ˈɪnlɒk/| n. v.|联锁, 互相扣住|A mechanical system where parts are interlocked so that the operation of one affects others.|The gears are in inlock, so turning one will turn the others.|近义词：interlock, engage, mesh|反义词:disengage, separate|词性:名词, 动词

Etymology: From "in-" (expressing inclusion or involvement) + "lock" (to fasten or secure).
