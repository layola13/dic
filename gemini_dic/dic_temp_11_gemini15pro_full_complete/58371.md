dualisms:/ˈduːəlɪzəmz/
noun
二元论；二元对立
The division of something conceptually into two opposed or contrasted aspects, or the state of being so divided.
The Cartesian dualism of mind and matter.  笛卡尔的物质和精神二元论。
Synonyms: dichotomy, duality, polarity, opposition, antithesis
Antonyms: monism, unity, singularity
