mortality:/mɔːrˈtælɪti/ |n.|死亡率；必死性；死亡人数|The mortality rate from lung cancer is increasing. 肺癌的死亡率正在上升。|The doctor explained the mortality of the disease. 医生解释了这种疾病的死亡率。|近义词：death rate, fatality, lethality|反义词：immortality, survival|词性：名词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin mortalitas, from Latin mortalis ‘mortal’.
