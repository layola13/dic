medals:/ˈmedlz/
n.
奖章，勋章
A small, flat piece of metal bearing an inscription or design, given as an award or commemoration.
She won three gold medals at the Olympics. 她在奥运会上赢得了三枚金牌。
近义词: awards, honors, decorations
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑disgrace耻辱)
