animalic:/ˌænɪˈmælɪk/
adj.
动物性的; 似动物的; 有动物气味的
Relating to or characteristic of animals; having the smell or taste of an animal.
The cheese had a pungent, almost animalic aroma.  这种奶酪有一种刺鼻的，近乎动物的气味。
animalistic, beastly, feral
human, humane
词性: 形容词
