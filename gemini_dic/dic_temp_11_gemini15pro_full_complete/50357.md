depone:/dɪˈpoʊn/| v. |作证；宣誓作证；郑重声明|to give evidence, especially in a court of law; to state something formally and under oath|The witness was called to depone about what she had seen.  证人被传唤来宣誓作证她所看到的情况。

He was asked to depone his signature. 他被要求确认他的签名。|近义词：testify, swear, attest, declare, affirm|反义词: recant, deny|词性:动词

词源: From Latin *dēpōnere*, from *dē* (“down, away from”) + *pōnere* (“to put, place”).  源自拉丁语 *dēpōnere*，由 *dē*（“向下，远离”）+ *pōnere*（“放置”）组成。
