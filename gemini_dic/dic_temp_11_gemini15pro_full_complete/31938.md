chelate:/ˈkiːleɪt/ /ˈkɛˌleɪt/ | n. v. adj.|螯合物；螯合；螯合的|A chelate is a chemical compound in which a metal ion is bonded to a ligand in a cyclic structure.  As a verb, it means to combine with a metal to form a chelate. As an adjective, it describes something that forms or is formed by chelation.|

例句：
The chelate was stable in acidic conditions.
这种螯合物在酸性条件下是稳定的。

EDTA is a common chelating agent.
EDTA是一种常见的螯合剂。

The reaction produced a chelate complex.
该反应产生了一种螯合物。
