demotivated:/ˌdiːˈmoʊtɪveɪtɪd/
adjective
失去动力的；泄气的
Having lost motivation; discouraged.
The team was demotivated after losing several games in a row. 球队在连续输了几场比赛后失去了动力。
Synonyms: discouraged, disheartened, dispirited, demoralized
Antonyms: motivated, inspired, enthusiastic
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "reduce" or "reverse") + motivated (past participle of motivate, from motive + -ate). The word "motive" comes from Old French motif, ultimately from Latin motus ("motion").
