mountaineer:/ˌmaʊntnˈɪr/ | n. | 登山者；山地居民 | A person who climbs mountains, especially as a sport. |  She is a skilled mountaineer and has climbed many of the world's highest peaks. 她是一位经验丰富的登山者，攀登过世界上许多高峰。

mountaineer:/ˌmaʊntnˈɪr/ | v. | 登山；攀登 | To climb mountains, especially as a sport. | They plan to mountaineer in the Himalayas next summer.  他们计划明年夏天去喜马拉雅山脉登山。


Etymology: From mountain + -eer (agent suffix, meaning "one who does something"). Ultimately from Latin *montanea (mountainous region), from mons (mountain).

Synonyms: climber, alpinist, hillwalker

Antonyms: lowlander, plainsman
