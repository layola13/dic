nonlibrary:/nɒnˈlaɪbrəri/ /nɑːnˈlaɪbrɛri/ | adj. | 非图书馆的；非藏书的 | Not related to or part of a library; not characteristic of a library. |  The nonlibrary resources include online databases and archives.  非图书馆资源包括在线数据库和档案馆。| Synonyms: non-bibliothecal | Antonyms: library | adjective


nonlibrary:/nɒnˈlaɪbrəri/ /nɑːnˈlaɪbrɛri/ | n. | 非图书馆机构/场所 |  A place or institution that is not a library. (This usage is less common.) | The community center serves as a nonlibrary for local residents. 社区中心充当当地居民的非图书馆场所。 | Synonyms:  (depending on context) community center, archive, resource center | Antonyms: library | noun
