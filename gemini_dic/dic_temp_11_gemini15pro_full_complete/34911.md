clitellum:/klaɪˈtɛləm/
n.
环带，生殖带
A thickened glandular section of the body wall in earthworms and leeches, that secretes a viscid sac in which the eggs are deposited.
Earthworms have a clitellum which is readily visible as a saddle-shaped swelling. 蚯蚓有一个环带，很容易被看作是马鞍状的突起。
The clitellum secretes a cocoon for the eggs.  环带分泌一个茧来保护卵。
synonym: girdle, band
antonym: None (lack of a clitellum would be described, not named)
