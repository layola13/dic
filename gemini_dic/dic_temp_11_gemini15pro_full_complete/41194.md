copywriting:/ˈkɑːpiˌraɪtɪŋ/ | n. |文案写作；广告文字撰写|The act or occupation of writing text for the purpose of advertising or other forms of marketing.  | The company hired a freelance copywriter to create compelling copy for their new product launch.  这家公司聘请了一位自由撰稿人为他们的新产品发布撰写引人注目的文案。| synonyms: advertising writing, marketing writing, content creation | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but could be contrasted with)  technical writing, journalistic writing | noun
