bismite:/ˈbɪzmaɪt/| n. |铋华;氧化铋|A naturally occurring mineral form of bismuth trioxide (Bi2O3).  It is a secondary mineral, often found in the oxidized zones of bismuth-bearing deposits.|Bismuthinite alters to bismite in the presence of oxygen.  在氧气存在的情况下，辉铋矿会转变为铋华。|Synonyms: bismuth ochre, bismuth oxide|Antonyms: N/A|noun
