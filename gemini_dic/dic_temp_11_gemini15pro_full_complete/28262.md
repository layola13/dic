captionless:/ˈkæpʃənləs/
adjective
无标题的；没有字幕的
lacking a caption; without a descriptive title or explanation.
The photo was captionless, so I didn't know who was in it. 这张照片没有标题，所以我不知道里面是谁。
synonyms: untitled, unlabeled, undescribed
antonyms: captioned, titled, labeled
词性: 形容词


词源:  "caption" (标题) + "-less" (无)
