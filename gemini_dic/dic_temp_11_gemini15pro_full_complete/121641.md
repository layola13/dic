misperceives:/ˌmɪspərˈsiːvz/
verb
误解，错误地理解
interprets incorrectly; understands wrongly
He misperceives my silence as agreement. 他把我的沉默误解为同意。
misunderstands, misinterprets, misconstrues
correctly perceives, understands correctly
词性:动词
