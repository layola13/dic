eschew:/ɪsˈtʃuː/
verb
避开，回避
deliberately avoid using; abstain from.
He eschewed publicity and avoided interviews. 他回避公众，拒绝采访。
synonyms: abstain from, refrain from, avoid, shun, steer clear of, give up, forgo, forswear, renounce, relinquish, reject, spurn, decline, refuse, repudiate, disavow
antonyms: embrace, welcome, accept, indulge in
词性: 动词
