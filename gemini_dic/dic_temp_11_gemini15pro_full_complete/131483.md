noncoking:/ˌnɒnˈkəʊkɪŋ/ | /ˌnɑːnˈkoʊkɪŋ/ | adj. | 非焦化的，非炼焦的 |  Not capable of being converted into coke.  Relating to coal that does not readily form coke when heated in the absence of air. |  The power plant uses noncoking coal.  这家发电厂使用非焦煤。|  non-coking, non-caking | coking, caking | adjective
