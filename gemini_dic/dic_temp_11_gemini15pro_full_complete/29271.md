cassowaries:/ˈkæsəˌwɛriz/ | n. |食火鸡；鹤鸵|Large, flightless birds native to New Guinea and northeastern Australia, known for their powerful legs and sharp claws.| Cassowaries are dangerous birds and should not be approached. 食火鸡很危险，不应该靠近它们。|  Cassowaries roamed the rainforest floor, searching for fallen fruit. 食火鸡在雨林地面漫步，寻找落下的水果。 |近义词:ratites (泛指走禽)|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider small birds, flying birds) |Noun
