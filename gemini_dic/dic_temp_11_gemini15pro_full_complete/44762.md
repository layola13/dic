crummiest:/ˈkrʌmiɪst/| adj.|最糟糕的，最差的|Superlative form of crumby: worst, of the poorest quality.  It implies something is unpleasant, dirty, or of low quality.|The hotel room was the crummiest I've ever stayed in.  这间酒店的房间是我住过的最差的。|The food at the party was absolutely crummiest.  派对上的食物糟透了。|近义词：worst, lousiest, poorest, awful, terrible|反义词：best, greatest, finest|词性:形容词
