despicable:/ˈdɛspɪkəbl/ /dɪˈspɪkəbl/| adj.|卑鄙的；可鄙的；令人厌恶的|Deserving hatred and contempt.|例句：His behavior was despicable. 他的行为卑鄙龌龊。

例句：It was a despicable act of betrayal. 这是一个卑鄙的背叛行为。

近义词：contemptible, vile, abhorrent, loathsome, odious|反义词：admirable, commendable, praiseworthy|词性：形容词
