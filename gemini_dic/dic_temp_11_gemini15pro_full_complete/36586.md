collateralness:/kəˈlætərəlnəs/ | n. | 抵押品性质；附属品性质；担保物性质 | The quality or state of being collateral; the condition of serving as security or guarantee for a debt. | The bank questioned the collateralness of the property offered as security for the loan. 银行对作为贷款抵押品的房产的抵押品性质提出了质疑。 |  近义词：security, guarantee, pledge, surety | 反义词：unsecured, unguaranteed | 词性: noun

collateral:/kəˈlætərəl/ | adj. | 附属的；旁系的；间接的；担保的；抵押的 |  Something pledged as security for repayment of a loan, to be forfeited in the event of a default. Also, secondary or indirect. | He used his house as collateral for the loan. 他用房子作抵押贷款。 | 近义词：secondary, ancillary, subsidiary, auxiliary, supporting, supplementary, incidental, attendant, concomitant, peripheral, fringe, marginal, tangential, oblique, indirect, related, connected, associated, accompanying | 反义词：direct, primary, principal, main, central, core, key, essential, fundamental, vital, crucial, important, significant, major, leading, chief, paramount, predominant, dominant | 词性: adjective
