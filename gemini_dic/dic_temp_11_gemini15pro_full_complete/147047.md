overlayer:/ˈoʊvərˌleɪər/| n.|覆盖层，表层|A layer or coating over something else. |The thin overlayer of gold protected the silver underneath. 薄薄的黄金覆盖层保护了下面的银。|synonyms: coating, film, layer, sheet, veneer|antonyms: substrate, underlayer|noun

overlayer:/ˈoʊvərˌleɪər/| n.|涂层|A thin layer of material spread over a surface.|The cake had a delicious chocolate overlayer. 蛋糕上有一层美味的巧克力涂层。|synonyms: coating, icing, frosting, glaze|antonyms: base, foundation|noun
