hypotheses:/haɪˈpɑːθəsiːz/
noun
假设，猜想
A supposition or proposed explanation made on the basis of limited evidence as a starting point for further investigation.
Many scientists begin their research by formulating a hypothesis. 许多科学家通过提出假设开始他们的研究。
hypotheses (plural)
n.
假设，猜想
Several hypotheses for the origin of the universe have been proposed.  关于宇宙起源的几个假设已经被提出。
synonyms: theories, suppositions, assumptions, conjectures, speculations
antonyms: proofs, facts, certainties
词性: 名词(plural)
