nonbeliefs:/ˌnɒnbɪˈliːfs/
noun
不信; 怀疑; 缺乏信仰
Lack of belief or beliefs, especially religious ones.
His nonbeliefs caused him to question the existence of God.  他的不信让他质疑上帝的存在。

Her nonbeliefs extended to all forms of organized religion. 她的不信延伸到所有形式的有组织宗教。

Synonym:disbelief, skepticism, unbelief, incredulity
Antonym:belief, faith, conviction, credence
词性: 名词
