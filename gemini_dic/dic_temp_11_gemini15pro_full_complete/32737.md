chlorometry:/klɔːˈrɒmɪtri/ | n. | 氯量测定法；氯量滴定法 |The determination of the amount of chlorine in a compound, especially by titration with a standard solution of sodium thiosulfate. | Chlorometry is used to determine the chlorine content in water. 氯量测定法用于测定水中氯的含量。 |  synonyms: chlorimetric analysis | antonyms: N/A | noun
