overphilosophized: /ˌoʊvərˌfɪləˈsɒfaɪzd/ | adj. | 过度哲学化的，过度思辨的 |  Having thought about something too much, especially in a way that is not practical or useful.  It implies that the subject has been analyzed or intellectualized to an excessive and often unproductive degree. | The issue of free will has been overphilosophized by academics.  自由意志的问题已经被学者们过度思辨了。 | The meaning of the poem has been overphilosophized. 这首诗的意义被过度解读了。 |  overanalyzed, overintellectualized | practical, pragmatic | adjective
