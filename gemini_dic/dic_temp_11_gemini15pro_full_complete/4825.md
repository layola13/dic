alias:/ˈeɪliəs/| n.|别名，化名，假名|An alias is a false name, especially one used by a criminal.|He was living under an alias. 他化名生活。|synonyms: pseudonym, assumed name, false name, nom de guerre, nom de plume, incognito, stage name|antonyms: real name|noun

alias:/ˈeɪliəs/| vt.|给...起别名|To give someone or something an alias.|The program allows you to alias commands to make them easier to type. 这个程序允许你给命令设置别名，使它们更容易输入。|synonyms: rename, retitle, call, dub, christen|antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider "reveal true identity")|verb
