laemmergeyer:/ˈlæmərdʒaɪər/ (US), /ˈlæmərdʒaɪə/ (UK)| n.|胡兀鹫|A large Old World vulture (Gypaetus barbatus) with feathered head and neck, inhabiting mountainous regions of Africa, Asia, and southern Europe. It is known for its habit of dropping bones onto rocks to break them open and access the marrow.|例句：The laemmergeyer soared high above the mountain peaks. 胡兀鹫在山峰上空翱翔。

例句：The laemmergeyer is a unique bird of prey. 胡兀鹫是一种独特的猛禽。

近义词：bearded vulture, bone-breaker|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
