nonretrievable:/ˌnɒnrɪˈtriːvəbl/ /ˌnɑːnrɪˈtriːvəbl/
adj.
不可检索的；无法恢复的
Not able to be retrieved or recovered.
The data on the damaged hard drive is nonretrievable.  损坏的硬盘上的数据无法恢复。
The lost spaceship is nonretrievable.  丢失的宇宙飞船无法找回。
近义词:irrecoverable, lost, unrecoverable, irretrievable
反义词:retrievable, recoverable
词性:adjective

nonretrievable:/ˌnɒnrɪˈtriːvəbl/ /ˌnɑːnrɪˈtriːvəbl/
n.
不可检索的东西
Something that cannot be retrieved or recovered.
This classified document is marked as a nonretrievable. 这份机密文件被标记为不可检索。
The sunken treasure became a nonretrievable after the storm.  暴风雨过后，沉没的宝藏变成了无法找回的东西。
近义词:loss
反义词:find
词性:noun (rarely used)
