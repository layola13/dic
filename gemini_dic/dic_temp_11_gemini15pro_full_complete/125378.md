multimillionairess:/ˌmʌltiˌmɪljəˈnɛəres/ | n. | 女千万富翁，女巨富 | A woman who has a net worth of several million dollars (or other currency).  |例句：The multimillionairess donated a large sum to the charity.  这位女千万富翁向慈善机构捐赠了一大笔钱。

例句：She became a multimillionairess through her successful tech startup. 她通过成功的科技创业成为了千万富翁。 | 近义词：tycoon, magnate, heiress | 反义词：pauper | 词性: noun
