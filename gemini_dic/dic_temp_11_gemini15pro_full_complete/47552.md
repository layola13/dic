deauthorization:/diːˌɔːθəraɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |撤销授权，取消授权 | The act of withdrawing authorization or permission.  The process of officially revoking someone's authority or right to do something. |  The deauthorization of the software prevented further use.  软件的撤销授权阻止了进一步的使用。| revocation, withdrawal, cancellation | authorization, empowerment | noun
