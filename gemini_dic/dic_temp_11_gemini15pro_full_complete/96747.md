ineloquently:/ɪnˈɛləkwəntli/
adv.
不善言辞地，笨拙地
Lacking eloquence; in a way that is not fluent, graceful, or persuasive.
He stammered ineloquently, unable to express his feelings. 他结结巴巴地说不出话来，无法表达自己的感受。
ineloquently delivered speech.  一篇表达笨拙的演讲。

近义词:inarticulately, clumsily, awkwardly, falteringly
反义词:eloquently, articulately, fluently, persuasively
词性:副词
