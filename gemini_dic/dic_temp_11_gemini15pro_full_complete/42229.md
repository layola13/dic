count-coup: /ˌkaʊnt ˈkuː/ | n. | 击中敌人（但不杀死）| The act of striking an opponent in battle, especially by touching them with a coup stick, as a demonstration of bravery and skill, without necessarily killing or seriously injuring them. This practice was common among Plains Indians tribes. |  He counted coup on the enemy warrior with his coup stick. 他用击敌棒击中了敌方战士，以此记功。| Synonyms:  touch, strike, tap, graze | Antonyms: kill, murder, slaughter | noun

count-coup: /ˌkaʊnt ˈkuː/ | v. | 击中敌人（但不杀死）(用作动词较少见) | To perform the act of counting coup. | The warrior counted coup on three enemies in the battle.  这名战士在战斗中击中了三个敌人并以此记功。| Synonyms:  touch, strike, tap, graze | Antonyms: kill, murder, slaughter | verb
