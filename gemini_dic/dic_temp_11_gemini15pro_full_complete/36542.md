collage:/kəˈlɑːʒ/ | n. | 拼贴画；拼贴艺术；混合物 | A picture or design created by sticking photographs, pieces of paper, cloth, etc. onto a surface; a collection or combination of various things. |  She made a collage of holiday photos. 她用假日照片做了一幅拼贴画。|  The room was a collage of different styles. 房间是不同风格的混合体。| synonym: montage, assemblage, compilation | antonym: uniformity, simplicity | noun
