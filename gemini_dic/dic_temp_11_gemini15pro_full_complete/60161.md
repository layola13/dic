effeminate: /ɪˈfɛmɪnət/ | adj. |女人气的，柔弱的| Having or showing characteristics regarded as typical of a woman; unmanly. | He had an effeminate manner that some people found off-putting.  他有一种女人气的举止，让一些人觉得反感。| 近义词: womanish, unmanly, sissy | 反义词: masculine, manly, virile |词性: 形容词

effeminates: /ɪˈfɛmɪneɪts/ | vt. |使柔弱，使女人气| To make someone or something effeminate. |  Constant exposure to romantic novels seemed to effeminate him.  长期接触言情小说似乎使他变得柔弱。 | 近义词: weaken, emasculate | 反义词: strengthen, masculinize |词性: 动词
