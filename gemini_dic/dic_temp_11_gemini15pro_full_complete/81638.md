greedless:/ˈɡriːdlɪs/| adj.|无贪欲的，不贪婪的|Lacking greed; not greedy; content.|She lived a simple, greedless life, finding joy in the small things.|近义词：generous, unselfish, content, satisfied|反义词：greedy, avaricious, covetous|词性:形容词
词源: greed + -less.  Greed comes from Old English *grǣd, from Proto-Germanic *grēdiz.  The suffix -less is also from Old English -lēas.
