一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I eat breakfast now. 我现在吃早餐。
She reads the book now. 她现在正在读那本书。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am eating now. 我现在正在吃。
He is reading the newspaper now. 他现在正在读报纸。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have finished the work now. 我现在已经完成了工作。
She has eaten the cake now. 她现在已经吃了蛋糕。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been working now. 我现在一直在工作。
He has been reading now. 他现在一直在读书。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I ate dinner then, not now. 我那时吃了晚饭，不是现在。
She read the letter then, not now. 她那时读了信，不是现在。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was eating when you called, but not now.  我打电话时正在吃饭，但现在不是。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

She had read the book by now. 她现在之前已经读完了那本书。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will eat later, not now. 我晚点吃，不是现在。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
