flogging:/ˈflɒɡɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈflɑːɡɪŋ/ (US)| n. |鞭笞，鞭打|The act of beating someone with a whip or stick as punishment or torture. |The slave received a brutal flogging for trying to escape.  奴隶因试图逃跑而遭到残酷的鞭打。| 近义词：whipping, beating, lashing, caning | 反义词: praising, rewarding | noun

flogging:/ˈflɒɡɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈflɑːɡɪŋ/ (US)| v. |鞭笞，鞭打; (informal) 竭力推销 |The act of beating someone with a whip or stick as punishment or torture;  trying very hard to sell something. |He was flogging his old car. 他在竭力推销他的旧车。| 近义词：whipping, beating, lashing, caning, peddling, hawking | 反义词: praising, rewarding | verb
