alchemies:/ˈælkɪmiz/
n.
炼金术；神秘的变化；魔力
A seemingly magical process of transformation, creation, or combination.  The plural form of "alchemy".
The alchemies of nature transformed the caterpillar into a butterfly.  大自然的魔力将毛毛虫变成了蝴蝶。

The market's strange alchemies turned debt into assets.  市场的神奇变化将债务变成了资产。

近义词:magic, sorcery, witchcraft, wizardry
反义词:science, reality
词性:名词
