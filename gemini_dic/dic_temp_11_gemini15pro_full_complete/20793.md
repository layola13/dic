blare: /blɛər/ | v. n. | 刺耳地大声鸣响；发出刺耳的声音；响亮的声音 | to make a loud, harsh sound | The trumpets blared as the procession entered the church.  喇叭声随着队伍进入教堂而响起。 |  The siren blared, warning of the approaching tornado. 警报器刺耳地响起，警告龙卷风即将来临。 | 近义词: blast, roar, boom | 反义词: whisper, murmur | 词性：动词，名词

blare: /blɛər/ | n. | 刺耳的声音；响亮的声音 | a loud, harsh sound | The blare of the car horn startled the pedestrians. 汽车喇叭的刺耳声音吓了行人一跳。| The constant blare of the television made it difficult to concentrate. 电视不断发出的响亮声音使人难以集中注意力。 | 近义词: blast, roar, boom | 反义词: whisper, murmur | 词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *blaren, of uncertain origin. Possibly imitative.
