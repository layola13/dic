blunge: /blʌndʒ/ | v. | 猛冲，跳进，投入 | To leap or plunge clumsily or excitedly into something, often a liquid. | He blunged into the cold water. 他一头扎进了冰冷的水里。 | She blunged into the conversation without knowing what it was about. 她一头扎进谈话中，却不知道他们在谈论什么。 | 近义词: plunge, dive, jump, leap | 反义词: emerge, rise | verb

Etymology:  Probably a blend of *blunk* (an obsolete word meaning ‘to swell’ or ‘to blink’) and *plunge*.
