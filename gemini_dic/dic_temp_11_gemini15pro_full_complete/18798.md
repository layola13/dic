berry-like:/ˈberiˌlaɪk/
adjective
像浆果的；形似浆果的
Resembling a berry in appearance, size, or shape.
The plant has small, berry-like fruits.  这种植物有小的、像浆果一样的果实。
The candy was a bright red, berry-like shape.  这颗糖果是鲜红色的，形状像浆果。
近义词: berry-shaped, bacciform
反义词: (No specific antonym, depends on context)
词性: adjective
