monkishness:/ˈmʌŋkɪʃnəs/
n.
僧侣气质；修道士风度；禁欲主义
The quality or state of being monkish; resembling or suggesting a monk, especially in devotion, austerity, or seclusion.
He retreated to the mountains, seeking a life of monkishness and contemplation.  他隐居到山中，寻求一种僧侣般的生活和沉思。
Near synonyms: asceticism, austerity, monasticism, piety, religiousness
Antonyms: worldliness, hedonism, materialism
noun
