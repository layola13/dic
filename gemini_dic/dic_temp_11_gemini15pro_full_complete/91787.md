hyperexcited:/ˌhaɪpərɪkˈsaɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过度兴奋的，极度激动的 |  Characterized by excessive excitement; extremely agitated and enthusiastic.  | The children were hyperexcited about going to the amusement park. 孩子们对去游乐园感到非常兴奋。 |  The fans were hyperexcited after their team won the championship. 球迷们在他们的球队赢得冠军后欣喜若狂。 | synonyms: overexcited, overstimulated, thrilled, exhilarated | antonyms: calm, composed, relaxed, tranquil | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- (prefix meaning "over, excessive") + excited (past participle of excite, from Latin excitare "to rouse, stir up").
