foreseer:/fɔːrˈsiːər/ | n. | 预言家，先知 | A person who can foresee the future. |  例句：The ancient foreseer predicted the fall of the empire. 古代的预言家预言了帝国的衰落。

例句：She was known as a foreseer of trends in the fashion industry.  她以预测时尚界趋势而闻名。| 近义词：prophet, seer, diviner, oracle | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

foresee:/fɔːrˈsiː/ | v. | 预见，预知 | To see or know something that will happen in the future. | 例句：He foresaw the difficulties the project would encounter. 他预见到了项目将会遇到的困难。

例句：Nobody could have foreseen the sudden change in the market. 没有人能够预见到市场的突然变化。| 近义词：predict, anticipate, envision, forecast | 反义词: N/A | 词性: verb
