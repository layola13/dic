nontumultuousness:/ˌnɒn.tjuːˈmʌl.tʃu.əs.nəs/ |n.| 平静，宁静 |The nontumultuousness of the countryside was a welcome change from the city's hustle and bustle.  乡村的宁静与城市的喧嚣相比，是一个受欢迎的变化。| Example: The nontumultuousness of the lake at dawn was incredibly peaceful. 黎明时分的湖面异常平静。|Synonyms: tranquility, calmness, peacefulness, serenity, quietude |Antonyms: turbulence, tumult, chaos, disorder, commotion |noun

Etymology:
From non- (not) + tumultuous (noisy and disorderly) + -ness (forming nouns denoting a state or quality).  Ultimately from Latin tumultus ("commotion, uproar").
