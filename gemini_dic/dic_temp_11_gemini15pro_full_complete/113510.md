ma'am:/mæm/ | n. | 女士；夫人 | A polite term of address for a woman.  Used especially in American English. | Excuse me, ma'am, do you know where the restroom is?  对不起，女士，你知道洗手间在哪里吗？ | 近义词: madam, lady, Mrs. | 反义词: sir, mister | noun
