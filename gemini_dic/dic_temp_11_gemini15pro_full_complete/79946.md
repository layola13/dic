GNP:/ˌdʒiː ɛn ˈpiː/ | n. |国民生产总值 (Gross National Product)|The total value of goods and services produced by a country's residents, regardless of their location.| The GNP of the country increased by 2% last year.  这个国家的国民生产总值去年增长了2%。|Synonyms: gross national income, GNI | Antonyms:  N/A | noun
