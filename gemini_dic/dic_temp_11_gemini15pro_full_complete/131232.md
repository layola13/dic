noncapitalists:/nɒnˈkæpɪtəlɪsts/
noun
非资本家
People who are not capitalists, meaning they do not own or control significant means of production and do not primarily derive their income from capital investments.
Noncapitalists often rely on wages or salaries for their livelihood.  非资本家通常依靠工资或薪水为生。
Many noncapitalists work in service industries. 许多非资本家在服务行业工作。
Synonym:workers, laborers, proletariat
Antonym:capitalists, bourgeoisie
