inclusively:/ɪnˈkluːsɪvli/
adverb
包含地，包括在内地
including everyone or everything; all-encompassing.
The price is $12.99, inclusively of sales tax. 价格是12.99美元，包含销售税。
inclusively of shipping 运费包括在内
近义词: comprehensively, totally, fully, wholly, entirely, globally
反义词: exclusively, except for
词性: 副词
