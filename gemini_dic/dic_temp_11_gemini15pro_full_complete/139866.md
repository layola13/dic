inoculate:/ɪˈnɑːkjʊleɪt/ /ɪˈnɒkjʊleɪt/
verb
接种，嫁接；灌输(思想、感情等)
To introduce something, such as a microorganism, serum, or vaccine, into an organism in order to create immunity to a disease. Also, to introduce an idea or feeling into someone's mind.
The doctor inoculated the children against measles. 医生给孩子们接种了麻疹疫苗。
He tried to inoculate his students with a love of literature. 他试图将对文学的热爱灌输给他的学生。
近义词: vaccinate, immunize, inject, implant, introduce, infuse
反义词: infect, contaminate
词性: verb

inoculation:/ɪˌnɑːkjuˈleɪʃən/ /ɪˌnɒkjuˈleɪʃən/
noun
接种，预防注射；(思想、感情等的)灌输
The act or process of inoculating or of being inoculated. Also, the introduction of an idea or feeling into someone's mind.
Annual influenza inoculation is recommended for the elderly.  建议老年人每年接种流感疫苗。
His constant inoculation of fear made his children timid. 他不断地灌输恐惧，使他的孩子们胆怯。
近义词: vaccination, immunization, injection, implant, introduction, infusion
反义词: infection, contamination
词性: noun
