inducement:/ɪnˈdjuːsmənt/ | n. | 引诱；诱因；动机 |Something that persuades someone to do something. |例句：The government offers tax inducements to encourage savings. 政府提供税收优惠以鼓励储蓄。

例句：They offered him a large salary as an inducement to change jobs.  他们提供给他一份高薪作为跳槽的诱饵。

近义词：incentive, motivation, encouragement, enticement, lure | 反义词：deterrent, discouragement | 词性：名词

词源：Late Middle English: from induce + -ment.
