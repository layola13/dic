caftaned:/ˈkæftænd/| adj.|穿着长袍的；罩袍似的|Wearing a caftan; resembling a caftan.|She looked elegant in her caftaned attire. 她穿着长袍，看起来很优雅。|The model wore a brightly colored, caftaned dress.  模特穿着一件颜色鲜艳的长袍式连衣裙。|近义词：robed, gowned|反义词: undressed|词性:形容词
词源: From caftan + -ed.  Ultimately from Persian خفتان (xaftān).
