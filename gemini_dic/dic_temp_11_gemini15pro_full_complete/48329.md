decriers:/dɪˈkraɪərz/
n.
批评者，诋毁者
People who publicly denounce or criticize something or someone.
The new policy has many detractors and decriers. 这项新政策有很多批评者和诋毁者。
近义词: critics, detractors, denouncers, attackers
反义词: supporters, proponents, advocates, defenders
词性: 名词

词源:
From the verb "decry," which comes from Old French "descrier" meaning "to cry against, proclaim," and ultimately from Latin "discrīdere" meaning "to separate by judgment, distinguish."
