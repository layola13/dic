amortise:/ˈæmərtaɪz/ /əˈmɔːrtaɪz/| vt.|摊销，分期偿还|To gradually reduce a debt or cost by making regular payments over a period of time.|The company is amortising its debt over a ten-year period.  该公司正在十年内分期偿还债务。|

The cost of new equipment was amortised over five years. 新设备的成本在五年内摊销。|

Synonyms: depreciate, write off, expunge|Antonyms: appreciate, increase|verb
