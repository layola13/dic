noncompromising:/nɒnˈkɒmprəmaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
不妥协的，不让步的
Unwilling to compromise or make concessions.
例句：He has a noncompromising attitude towards quality. 他对质量的态度毫不妥协。

例句：She was known for her noncompromising stance on ethical issues. 她以在道德问题上毫不妥协的立场而闻名。

近义词：inflexible, unyielding, rigid, firm, resolute, steadfast
反义词：compromising, flexible, accommodating, yielding

词性: adjective
词源:  "non-" (not) + "compromising" (from "compromise," from Late Latin compromissum, meaning "mutual promise")
