house-proud:/ˈhaʊsˌpraʊd/| adj.|为房子感到骄傲的，喜欢炫耀自己房子的| Excessively concerned with keeping one's house clean and tidy and showing it off to visitors.  过分在意保持房子干净整洁，并向访客炫耀。|例句：She's very house-proud and always has her home looking immaculate. 她非常以自己的房子为荣，总是把家里弄得一尘不染。

例句：My mother is house-proud and loves to entertain guests. 我妈妈以她的房子为荣，喜欢招待客人。|近义词：housewifely, domesticated|反义词:slovenly, messy|词性:adjective
