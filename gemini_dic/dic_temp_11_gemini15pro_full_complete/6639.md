anaesthetised:/əˈniːsθətaɪzd/ | adj. | 麻醉的，失去知觉的 |  Deprived of feeling or awareness, typically by the administration of an anesthetic.  The adjective form of "anaesthetize." | The patient was anaesthetised before the surgery. 病人在手术前被麻醉了。| The anaesthetised limb was completely numb. 被麻醉的肢体完全麻木了。 | 近义词: numb, insensible, unconscious | 反义词: conscious, aware, sensitive | 词性: adjective | 词源: From Greek "anaisthetos" meaning "without sensation."
