disillusionary:/ˌdɪsɪˈluːʒənəri/ | adj. | 令人失望的，使幻想破灭的 | Causing disillusionment; tending to destroy illusions or ideals. |  The disillusionary experience of war left him cynical. 战争令人失望的经历让他变得愤世嫉俗。| synonyms: disappointing, disheartening, discouraging | antonyms: encouraging, inspiring, uplifting | adjective

disillusion:/ˌdɪsɪˈluːʒən/ | n. | 幻灭，失望 | A feeling of disappointment resulting from the discovery that something is not as good as one believed it to be. | He experienced a profound disillusion with politics. 他对政治彻底失望了。| synonyms: disappointment, disenchantment | antonyms: hope, optimism | noun
