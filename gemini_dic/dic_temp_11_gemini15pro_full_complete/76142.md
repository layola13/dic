fuscous:/ˈfʌskəs/| adj.|暗褐色的；深灰色的；昏暗的|Of a dark brownish-gray color; dusky.|The fuscous clouds hinted at impending rain. 暗褐色的云预示着即将下雨。|近义词：dusky, murky, somber, dark|反义词：bright, vibrant, light|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fuscus (“dark, brown”), perhaps related to Sanskrit धूम (dhūma, “smoke”).
