expect: /ɪkˈspekt/ (UK) /ɪkˈspɛkt/ (US) | v. | 预期，期望；要求；认为；猜想 | to think or believe something will happen, or someone will arrive | I expect to finish the work by Friday. 我预计在周五前完成工作。 | anticipate, await, foresee | doubt | verb

expect: /ɪkˈspekt/ (UK) /ɪkˈspɛkt/ (US) | n. | 期望，预期 | something expected; expectation | The expect of the company is to increase sales.  公司的期望是提高销售额。| anticipation, expectation | doubt | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin exspectāre (“to look out for, await, expect”), from ex- (“out”) + spectāre (“to look”).
