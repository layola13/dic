busboys:/ˈbʌs.bɔɪz/ (US), /ˈbʌs.bɔɪz/ (UK)| n.|餐馆勤杂工，餐桌清理员|A busboy is a person who works in a restaurant clearing tables, setting tables, and doing other tasks.|The busboys quickly cleared the dishes after the guests left. 客人们离开后，餐馆勤杂工迅速清理了餐具。|The busboy refilled our water glasses. 餐馆勤杂工帮我们把水杯加满了。|近义词：busser, dining room attendant|反义词: waiter (服务员 - 侧重点不同，busboy侧重清理，waiter侧重服务)|词性:名词
