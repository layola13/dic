expectedness:/ɪkˈspɛktɪdnəs/| n.|预期性，预料之中|The expectedness of the outcome made the game less exciting.  结果在意料之中，使得比赛不那么精彩了。|例句：The expectedness of his reaction surprised no one.  他的反应在意料之中，没有人感到惊讶。

The lack of expectedness made the twist ending all the more shocking.  缺乏预期性使得反转结局更加令人震惊。|近义词：predictability, foreseenness, anticipation|反义词:unexpectedness, surprise, shock|词性:noun

词源: From "expected" + "-ness".  "Expected" comes from the Latin "expectare" (to look out for, await).  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
