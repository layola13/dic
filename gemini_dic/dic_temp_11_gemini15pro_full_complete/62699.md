engineer: /ˌɛndʒɪˈnɪr/ (British) /ˌɛndʒəˈnɪr/ (American)| n. v.|工程师；设计；策划|A person trained and skilled in the design, construction, and use of engines or machines, or in any of various branches of engineering.  To skillfully arrange for something to happen.|例句：He is a software engineer. 他是一名软件工程师。|例句：She engineered her son's escape from prison. 她策划了她儿子越狱。|近义词：architect, designer, planner, constructor|反义词: (n.) layman, amateur, (v.) obstruct, hinder|词性: noun, verb
