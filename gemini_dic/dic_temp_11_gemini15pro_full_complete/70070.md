feudality:/fjuːˈdælɪti/
n.
封建制度；封建性
The system of feudalism, or the feudal spirit.
封建制度或封建精神。

Feudality is a social system in which land is held in exchange for service or labour.
封建制度是一种以服务或劳动换取土地的社会制度。

Synonym: feudalism, vassalage, manorialism
Antonym: democracy, republic
