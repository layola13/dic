despair:/dɪˈspɛər/ | n. vi.| 绝望，失望；令人绝望的人或事 | a state of having lost all hope| He sank into despair after failing the exam. 他考试不及格后陷入了绝望。|synonyms: hopelessness, despondency, desperation | antonyms: hope, optimism | noun

despairs:/dɪˈspɛərz/ | v. (third person singular simple present) | 绝望，丧失希望 | loses or abandons all hope| He despairs of ever finding a job. 他对找到工作感到绝望。| synonyms: loses hope, gives up | antonyms: hopes, expects | verb
