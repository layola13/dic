naiad:/ˈnaɪæd/ | n. | 水仙女；水生植物 | A nymph inhabiting rivers, springs, or other bodies of fresh water.  A type of aquatic plant. | The naiad danced gracefully in the crystal-clear stream.  水仙女在清澈的小溪中翩翩起舞。|  The pond was teeming with naiads.  池塘里满是水生植物。 | synonyms: nymph, water nymph, water sprite, mermaid, aquatic plant | antonyms:  (no direct antonym, could consider terrestrial plant for the plant sense) | noun
