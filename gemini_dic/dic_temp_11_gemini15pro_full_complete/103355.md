jimcrack:/ˈdʒɪmkræk/
noun
小玩意儿；廉价的小装饰品；华而不实的东西
A showy but flimsy or worthless trifle.
The shelves were filled with jimcracks and souvenirs.  架子上摆满了小玩意儿和纪念品。
synonyms: trinket, knick-knack, bauble, gewgaw, novelty, trifle
antonyms: valuable, treasure, masterpiece
