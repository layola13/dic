minimality:/ˌmɪnɪˈmælɪti/| n.|最小性，极简性|The state or quality of being minimal; the fact of involving or requiring the smallest possible amount of something.|The artist achieved a striking effect through the minimality of his design. 艺术家通过设计的极简性实现了惊人的效果。|minimalism, simplicity|maximalism, complexity|noun

minimality:/ˌmɪnɪˈmælɪti/| n.|最小化，最低限度|The principle or practice of reducing something to the smallest possible amount or degree.|The company's pursuit of minimality in packaging led to significant cost savings. 公司对包装最小化的追求带来了显著的成本节约。|reduction, simplification|excess, extravagance|noun
