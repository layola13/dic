cataclysmic:/ˌkætəˈklɪzmɪk/| adj.|灾难性的，剧变的|Relating to or denoting a violent upheaval or sudden change, especially one of a social or political nature.|The cataclysmic earthquake devastated the city. 这场灾难性的地震摧毁了这座城市.|近义词：disastrous, catastrophic, devastating, calamitous|反义词:fortunate, beneficial, advantageous|词性:形容词
词源: From Greek *kataklysmos* "deluge, flood," from *kataklyzein* "to inundate, deluge," from *kata-* "down" + *klyzein* "to wash, dash over."
