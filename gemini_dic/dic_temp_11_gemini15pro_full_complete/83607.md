haematosac: /ˌhiːmætəˈsæk/ (also hematosac) /ˌhemætəˈsæk/
noun
血囊，血袋
A sac containing blood, especially an effusion of blood.  More technically, a localized collection of extravasated blood that is usually clotted.

The surgeon carefully drained the haematosac that had formed near the patient's liver.  外科医生小心翼翼地排出了在病人肝脏附近形成的血囊。

The haematosac caused by the injury continued to swell.  由受伤引起的血囊持续肿胀。


Synonym:  blood sac, hematoma, hematic cyst
Antonym: (No direct antonym, but consider terms relating to blood deficiency like anemia, hypovolemia)
词性: noun
