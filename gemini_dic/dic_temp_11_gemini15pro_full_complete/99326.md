intercurled:/ˌɪntərˈkɜːrld/
adjective
相互缠绕的，卷曲在一起的
curled or wound together; intertwined.
Her hair was a mass of intercurled, dark ringlets. 她的头发是一团相互缠绕的黑色卷发。
The vines were intercurled around the trellis. 藤蔓相互缠绕在格子上。
近义词: intertwined, interlaced, entwined, tangled
反义词: straight, uncurled, untangled
词性: adjective

词源:
From inter- + curled, past participle of curl.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among." "Curl" comes from the Old North French word "curler," meaning "to curl or bend."
