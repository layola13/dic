mortgager:/ˈmɔːrɡɪdʒər/| n. |抵押人，按揭人|A person who mortgages property; the borrower in a mortgage transaction.  Someone who gives a mortgage to a lender as security for a loan. |Example 1: The mortgager agreed to repay the loan within 25 years. 抵押人同意在25年内偿还贷款。

Example 2:  The bank required the mortgager to provide proof of income. 银行要求抵押人提供收入证明。|Synonym: borrower, debtor |Antonym: mortgagee, lender |noun
