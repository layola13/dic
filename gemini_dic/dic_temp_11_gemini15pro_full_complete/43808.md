crenelled:/ˈkrɛnəld/ (US), /ˈkrɛnəld/ (UK)| adj.|城垛状的，锯齿状的|Having battlements or loopholes, typically in a castle wall; indented with regular, square notches.|The crenelled towers of the castle loomed against the sky. 城堡锯齿状的塔楼映衬着天空。|近义词：battlemented, notched, indented, serrated|反义词:smooth, even|词性:形容词
词源:From Old French *crenelé, past participle of creneler, from cren, crenel ‘notch’, from a Vulgar Latin word *crenellus, diminutive of Latin crena ‘notch’.
