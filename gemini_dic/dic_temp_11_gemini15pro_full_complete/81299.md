granulation:/ˌɡrænjʊˈleɪʃən/ | n. |颗粒化，造粒，肉芽组织|The act or process of forming granules or grains. In medicine, it refers to the formation of small, rounded, fleshy masses (granulation tissue) in wounds as part of the healing process.|Sugar undergoes granulation to produce the familiar crystals. 糖经过造粒过程形成了我们熟悉的晶体。|Synonyms: crystallization, atomization|Antonyms: pulverization, dissolution|noun
