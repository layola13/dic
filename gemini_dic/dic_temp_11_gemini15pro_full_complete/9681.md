antitheft:/ˌæntiˈθɛft/
adjective
防盗的
Designed to prevent theft.
This car has an antitheft device. 这辆车有防盗装置。
近义词：theft-resistant, burglarproof
反义词：N/A
词性: adjective

antitheft:/ˌæntiˈθɛft/
noun
防盗装置
A device or measure designed to prevent theft.
The store installed a new antitheft system. 这家商店安装了新的防盗系统。
近义词：security system, alarm system
反义词：N/A
词性: noun


Etymology:
From anti- + theft.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to," derived from Greek.  "Theft" comes from Old English "þēofþ," meaning "the act of stealing."
