fascicularly:/fæˈsɪkjʊləli/
| adv. |成束地，簇生地|In botany, it describes the arrangement of leaves, flowers, or other plant parts growing in bundles or clusters.|The nerve fibers were arranged fascicularly.  神经纤维成束排列。|近义词：in bundles, in clusters, grouped together|反义词:singly, individually, separately|词性:副词
Etymology: From Latin "fasciculus" (small bundle) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).
