例句：The franken-software, patched together from various open-source projects, was unstable.  这个由各种开源项目拼凑而成的弗兰肯式软件很不稳定。|近义词：hybrid, composite, patchwork, cobbled together|反义词：natural, organic, seamless|词性：形容词
