bloodshot:/ˈblʌdʃɒt/ (US) /ˈblʌdʃɒt/ (UK)| adj.|充血的，布满血丝的|Having blood vessels that are visible and appear red, typically because of lack of sleep, irritation, or excessive alcohol consumption.|例句：His eyes were bloodshot from lack of sleep. 他的眼睛因为缺乏睡眠而布满血丝。|例句：The wind made her eyes bloodshot. 风吹得她眼睛充血。|近义词：red-rimmed, inflamed, injected|反义词:clear, white|词性:形容词

bloodshot:/ˈblʌdʃɒt/ (US) /ˈblʌdʃɒt/ (UK)| n.|充血|The condition of having bloodshot eyes.|例句：The bloodshot in his eyes was alarming. 他眼睛的充血状态令人担忧。 |例句：He woke up with terrible bloodshot. 他醒来时眼睛严重充血。|近义词：redness, inflammation|反义词: clarity|词性:名词 (较少使用)
