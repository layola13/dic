jazzy:/ˈdʒæzi/| adj.|爵士乐的，活泼的，花哨的|Characteristic of jazz music; lively, stylish, and exciting.|例句：The band played a jazzy tune. 乐队演奏了一首活泼的爵士乐曲。|近义词：stylish, lively, upbeat, vibrant, snazzy|反义词：dull, boring, bland, conservative, subdued|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "jazz" + "-y". "Jazz" originated in early 20th century African American communities in New Orleans, USA.  The exact origin is uncertain, but it's thought to be influenced by West African and European musical traditions.
