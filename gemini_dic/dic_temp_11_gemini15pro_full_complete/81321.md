grapevine:/ˈɡreɪpvaɪn/ | n. | 葡萄藤; 小道消息; | An informal person-to-person means of circulating information or gossip.  A climbing plant that produces grapes. | The news spread through the grapevine. 消息通过小道消息传播开来。 The grapes hang heavy on the vine. 葡萄藤上挂满了沉甸甸的葡萄。 | 近义词：rumor mill, word of mouth, vine | 反义词: official channel | 词性: 名词
