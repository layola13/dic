crinkliest:/ˈkrɪŋkliɪst/| adj.|最皱的|Most wrinkled; having the most crinkles or wrinkles.|The crinkliest leaves on the tree were the oldest. 树上最皱的叶子是最老的.|Synonyms: most wrinkled, most creased|Antonyms: smoothest, flattest|Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->

From crinkle + -y + -est.  "Crinkle" comes from the Middle Dutch "krinkelen," meaning "to bend, turn, twist."  The "-y" suffix transforms the verb into an adjective, and "-est" designates the superlative form.
