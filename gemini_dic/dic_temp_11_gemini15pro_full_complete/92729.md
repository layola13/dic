ichnological:/ɪk.nəˈlɒdʒ.ɪ.kəl/
adjective
遗迹学的；关于遗迹学的
Relating to ichnology, the study of trace fossils.
Ichnological evidence suggests dinosaurs roamed this area.  遗迹学证据表明恐龙曾在该地区漫游。
The team conducted an ichnological study of the area.  团队对该地区进行了遗迹学研究。
近义词:  trace fossil, biogenic
反义词:  abiogenic
词性:  adjective

ichnology:/ɪkˈnɒlədʒi/
noun
遗迹学
The study of trace fossils, such as footprints, burrows, and other evidence of animal activity.
Ichnology provides valuable insights into the behavior of extinct animals.  遗迹学为了解已灭绝动物的行为提供了宝贵的见解。
The museum has a fascinating exhibit on ichnology.  博物馆有一个关于遗迹学的精彩展览。
近义词:  paleoichnology
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider fields studying body fossils)
词性:  noun
