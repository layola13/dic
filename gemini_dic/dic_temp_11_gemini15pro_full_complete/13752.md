auctioning:/ˈɔːkʃ(ə)nɪŋ/
verb
拍卖;竞卖
the process of selling something at an auction.
They were auctioning off their furniture. 他们正在拍卖他们的家具。
synonyms: selling, vending, trading
antonyms: buying, purchasing, acquiring
v.
