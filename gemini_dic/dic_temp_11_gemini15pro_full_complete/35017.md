clonk:/klɒŋk/ (UK), /klɑːŋk/ (US)| n. v. |发出沉闷的金属撞击声（名词）;  使发出沉闷的金属撞击声（动词）|A dull, heavy sound, as of metal striking metal. (noun); To strike something so as to produce a dull, heavy metallic sound.
