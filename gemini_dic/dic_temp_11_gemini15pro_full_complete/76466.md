gaiterless:/ˈɡeɪtərlɪs/
adjective
无绑腿的；没穿绑腿的
Without gaiters.  Lacking gaiters; not wearing gaiters.
The hiker went gaiterless through the muddy trail, much to the detriment of his socks.  这个徒步旅行者没穿绑腿就走过泥泞的小路，结果他的袜子遭殃了。
