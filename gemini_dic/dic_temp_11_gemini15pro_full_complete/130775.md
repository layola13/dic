词源:

"non-" (prefix):  from Latin "non" meaning "not".
"anarchistic": from "anarchy" + "-istic" (suffix).  "Anarchy" comes from Greek "anarkhia," meaning "without a ruler," from "an-" (without) + "arkhos" (ruler).  "-istic" forms adjectives meaning "relating to" or "characteristic of."
