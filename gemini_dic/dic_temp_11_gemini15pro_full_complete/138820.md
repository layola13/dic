nuncio:/ˈnʌnʃioʊ/ (US), /ˈnʌnsɪəʊ/ (UK)
n.
教皇使节；外交使节
A diplomatic representative of the Pope.
The Pope's nuncio to the United Nations addressed the General Assembly.  教皇驻联合国使节向大会发表了讲话。

The nuncio delivered a message from the Vatican.  使节传达了梵蒂冈的讯息。

近义词: ambassador, envoy, legate
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑receiver-接收者，或opponent-反对者, 但语境差异很大)
词性: noun
