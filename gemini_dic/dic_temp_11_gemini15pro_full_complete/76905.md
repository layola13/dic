ganocephalous:/ˌɡænoʊˈsɛfələs/| adj.| 头盖骨发育不全的；脑发育不全的| Having a skull with defective ossification, especially in the back part, and usually with a malformed brain.  A condition characterized by incomplete development of the skull and brain, often leading to severe neurological deficits.| The ganocephalous infant required specialized medical care.  这个头盖骨发育不全的婴儿需要专门的医疗护理。| Synonym: microcephalic | Antonym: normocephalic|adjective
