nondeforming:/ˌnɒndɪˈfɔːmɪŋ/ | adj. |不变形的，非变形的| Not changing shape; resistant to deformation. | The bridge was built with nondeforming steel girders. 这座桥是用不变形的钢梁建造的。| The nondeforming nature of the material makes it ideal for this application. 这种材料的不变形特性使其非常适合这种应用。| 近义词：rigid, inflexible, unyielding | 反义词：deforming, flexible, malleable | 词性: adjective

词源: "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "deforming" (present participle of "deform," from Latin "deformare," meaning "to disfigure, distort").
