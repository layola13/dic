heavy-handed:/ˈhɛviˈhændɪd/| adj.|笨手笨脚的；高压的；严厉的|clumsy or insensitive; using too much force or authority|例句：The police were accused of being heavy-handed in their treatment of the protesters. 警方被指控在对待抗议者时手段强硬。

例句：His heavy-handed approach to management alienated many employees. 他高压的管理方式疏远了许多员工。|近义词：clumsy, awkward, oppressive, authoritarian, dictatorial|反义词：delicate, subtle, tactful, gentle, lenient|词性:形容词

heavy-handed:/ˈhɛviˈhændɪd/| adj.|笨手笨脚的；高压的；严厉的|lacking subtlety and finesse; overly forceful or oppressive|例句：The government's heavy-handed response to the crisis only made things worse. 政府对危机笨拙的反应只会使情况变得更糟。

例句：She's a bit heavy-handed with the spices when she cooks. 她做饭时香料放得有点重。|近义词：overbearing, ham-fisted, iron-fisted|反义词：light-handed, subtle, nuanced|词性:形容词
