imbower:/ɪmˈbaʊər/ | n. | (古语) 树荫处；凉亭 | A shady place; a bower. |  They found solace in the quiet imbower. 他们在安静的树荫下找到了慰藉。 | Synonyms: arbor, bower, pergola | Antonyms: open area, field | Noun
