nosology: /noʊˈsɑːlədʒi/ /nɒˈsɒlədʒi/ | n. | 疾病分类学 | The branch of medical science that deals with the classification of diseases. |  Nosology helps doctors understand the relationships between different illnesses. 疾病分类学帮助医生了解不同疾病之间的关系。

| Example: The nosology of mental disorders is constantly evolving. 精神疾病的分类学在不断发展。

| Example:  Advances in genetics have significantly impacted the nosology of certain cancers. 基因学的进步极大地影响了某些癌症的分类学。

| Synonym: classification, taxonomy, systematization | Antonym: N/A | Noun
