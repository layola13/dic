double-booked:/ˌdʌblˈbʊkt/ | adj. | 双重预定的，重复预订的 |  Reserved for two different people or purposes at the same time. |  I'm so sorry, but I seem to be double-booked for that evening.  我很抱歉，但那天晚上我好像被重复预订了。| Synonyms: overbooked, oversubscribed | Antonyms: available, free | adjective

double-booked:/ˌdʌblˈbʊkt/ | v. | 双重预定，重复预订 | To reserve (something) for two different people or purposes at the same time. | The restaurant double-booked our table and we had to wait for an hour. 这家餐厅把我们的桌子重复预订了，我们不得不等了一个小时。 | Synonyms: overbook | Antonyms: cancel | verb
