deformational:/ˌdiːfɔːrˈmeɪʃənəl/
adjective
变形的，形变的
Relating to or characterized by deformation; changing the shape or form of something.
The deformational processes involved in mountain building are complex.  山脉形成过程中涉及的变形过程非常复杂。

The artist captured the deformational qualities of the clay.  艺术家捕捉到了粘土的变形特质。

synonym: distorted, warped, misshapen, contorted
antonym:  unaltered, unchanged, intact, perfect
词性: adjective
