blitz:/blɪts/| n. v.|闪电战，突袭，闪击|A sudden, overwhelming military attack.|The German blitz of London in 1940 caused widespread destruction.  1940年德国对伦敦的闪电战造成了大范围的破坏。|近义词：attack, assault, onslaught|反义词:defense, retreat|词性:名词,动词

blitz:/blɪts/| v.|快速完成，集中力量攻击|To do something very quickly and intensively.|We blitzed the house and got it clean in an hour. 我们突击打扫了房子，一个小时就打扫干净了。|近义词：rush, speed up|反义词:delay, procrastinate|词性:动词
