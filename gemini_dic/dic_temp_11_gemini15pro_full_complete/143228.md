outdanced: /ˌaʊtˈdænst/ /ˌaʊtˈdɑːnst/
verb
超越…的舞蹈技艺；在舞蹈比赛中胜过
To surpass someone in dancing; to perform better than someone else in a dance competition.
He outdanced everyone at the party. 他在派对上跳舞跳赢了所有人。
She outdanced her competitors and won the trophy. 她比对手跳得更好，赢得了奖杯。
Synonyms: outperformed, surpassed, beat
Antonyms: lost to, was outdanced by
词性: 动词
