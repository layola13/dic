eschewal:/ɪsˈtʃuːəl/
n.
回避；躲避
the act of deliberately avoiding or abstaining from something
例句：His eschewal of alcohol stemmed from a troubled past.  他因过去的不幸经历而戒酒。

例句：The company's eschewal of traditional marketing methods led to its success. 该公司对传统营销方法的摒弃促成了它的成功。

近义词：avoidance, abstinence, shunning, forbearance
反义词：indulgence, embrace, acceptance
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *escheuen, from Old French eschever, from Frankish *skiuhan, from Proto-Germanic *skeuhwijaną (“to shun, avoid”). Cognate with Old High German sciuhen (“to shy away, avoid”), Dutch schuwen (“to shun, avoid”), German scheuen (“to shy, be afraid of”).
