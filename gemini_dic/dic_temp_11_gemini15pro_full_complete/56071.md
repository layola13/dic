The ship incurred heavy dockage fees. 这艘船产生了高昂的码头费。

The farmer was upset about the dockage for impurities in his grain. 农民对谷物中杂质的扣除量感到不安。

The dockage provided ample space for several large vessels.  这个码头为几艘大型船只提供了充足的空间。|

dockage:/ˈdɑːkɪdʒ/ | n. | 码头费，停泊费；扣除量；码头|The act or process of deducting a certain amount or percentage from a commodity for impurities, moisture, etc. A deduction from the gross weight of a commodity.|
The dockage on the wheat shipment was significant due to excessive moisture. 小麦运输的扣除量很大，原因是水分过多。|
近义词：deduction, reduction|
反义词：addition, increase|
词性：noun


词源: From the verb "dock," ultimately from Middle Dutch "docke."
