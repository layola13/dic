overannotate:/ˌoʊvərˈænəˌteɪt/| v. |过度注释，注释过多|To add excessive or unnecessary annotations to something, often to the point of obscuring the original meaning.|The professor overannotated the student's essay, making it difficult to read. 教授在学生的文章上做了过多的注释，使得文章难以阅读。|He overannotated the document, adding so many comments that it became confusing. 他在文档上添加了过多的注释，以至于让人感到困惑。|overexplain, overcomment|underannotate|verb
