implacentalia:/ˌɪmpləsɛnˈteɪliə/
n.
无胎盘类
A subclass of mammals that lack a placenta, including monotremes and marsupials.
The implacentalia are a diverse group of mammals with unique reproductive strategies. 无胎盘类是一组多样化的哺乳动物，具有独特的繁殖策略。
Echidnas and kangaroos are examples of implacentalia.  针鼹和袋鼠是无胎盘类的例子。
近义词:Metatheria, Prototheria
反义词:Placentalia, Eutheria
词性:noun
