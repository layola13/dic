distinguishers:/dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃərz/| n. |区别物，区分物|Objects or features that help to differentiate between things. |These small details serve as distinguishers between the two models. 这些小细节是区分这两个型号的区别物。|Synonyms: differentiators, separators,  contrasts|Antonyms: unifiers, similarities|noun
