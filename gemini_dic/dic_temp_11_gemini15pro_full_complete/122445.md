mobcap:/ˈmɒbkæp/ | n. | 生物数量上限；群体上限；怪物数量上限 | A limit on the number of mobs (typically hostile creatures) that can spawn in a particular area of a video game.  | Example: The server admin lowered the mobcap to reduce lag. 服务器管理员降低了怪物数量上限以减少延迟。| Example: The mobcap in this zone is too high, making it difficult to explore. 这个区域的怪物数量上限太高，导致难以探索。 | Synonym: mob limit, spawn limit | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but conceptually: unlimited spawns) | noun
