nordicism:/ˈnɔːrdɪˌsɪzəm/ | n. | 北欧主义；北欧中心论 | The belief that people of northern European descent, especially Scandinavians, are inherently superior to other races and that Nordic traits are essential for the progress of civilization.  It often emphasizes characteristics like fairness of skin, blond hair, and blue eyes. |  The professor's lecture debunked the outdated theories of Nordicism.  教授的讲座驳斥了过时的北欧中心论理论。

Nordicism was a popular ideology in early 20th-century Europe. 北欧主义是20世纪初欧洲的一种流行意识形态。 | 近义词: racism, racial superiority, ethnocentrism | 反义词: racial equality, multiculturalism | 词性: noun
