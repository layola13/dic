opinative:/əˈpɪnjətɪv/ | adj. | 固执己见的；武断的；爱发表意见的 | Holding stubbornly to one's own opinion, especially without evidence or regard for other opinions; dogmatic; inclined to express opinions freely. |  He was an opinative man, not easily swayed by others' views. 他是个固执己见的人，不容易被别人的观点左右。| synonyms: dogmatic, opinionated, assertive, insistent, peremptory | antonyms: open-minded, receptive, flexible, acquiescent | adjective
