entombs:/ɪnˈtuːmz/
verb
埋葬，安葬
To place (a dead body) in a tomb. To enclose or trap within or as if within a tomb.
The avalanche entombed the skiers. 雪崩掩埋了滑雪者。
The miners were entombed alive. 矿工被活埋了。
bury, inter, inhume
exhume, unearth
