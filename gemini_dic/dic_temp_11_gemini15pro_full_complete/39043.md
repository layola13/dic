confluent:/ˈkɒnfluːənt/ /ˈkɑːnfluːənt/| adj.|汇合的，融合的，合流的|Flowing together or merging into one another. |The two rivers are confluent at this point. 这两条河流在此处汇合。|Synonyms: merging, converging, joining, uniting|Antonyms: diverging, separating, branching|adjective

confluence:/ˈkɒnfluːəns/ /ˈkɑːnfluːəns/| n.|汇合，融合，合流|The junction of two rivers, especially rivers of approximately equal width. Also, a coming or flowing together, merging. |The confluence of the two rivers created a fertile valley. 两条河流的汇合创造了一个肥沃的山谷。|Synonyms: junction, convergence, meeting point, union|Antonyms: divergence, separation, branching|noun
