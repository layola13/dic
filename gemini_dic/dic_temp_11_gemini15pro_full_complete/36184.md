cofinanced:/ˌkoʊˈfaɪnænst/ | adj. | 共同出资的；共同融资的 | Financed by two or more parties. | The project was cofinanced by the government and several private investors. 这个项目由政府和几个私人投资者共同出资。|The bridge construction was cofinanced by the state and federal governments. 这座桥的建设由州政府和联邦政府共同出资。| synonyms: jointly funded, jointly financed | antonyms: solely funded, independently financed | adjective

cofinance:/ˌkoʊˈfaɪnæns/ | v. | 共同出资；共同融资 | To finance something together with one or more other parties. | The two companies decided to cofinance the research project. 这两家公司决定共同资助这个研究项目。| Several European countries cofinanced the development of the new aircraft.  几个欧洲国家共同资助了这种新型飞机的开发。| synonyms: jointly fund, jointly finance | antonyms: solely fund, independently finance | verb
