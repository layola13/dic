galvanises: /ˈɡælvənaɪzɪz/
verb
激励；刺激；震动；镀锌
to shock or excite someone into taking action.  To coat (iron or steel) with a protective layer of zinc.
The news galvanized them into action. 这消息激励他们行动起来。
The steel is galvanized to prevent rust. 钢铁经过镀锌以防生锈。
近义词: stimulate, energize, excite, rouse, spur, jolt, shock, electrify, arouse, animate, invigorate,  plate (with zinc)
反义词: demotivate, discourage, depress, soothe, calm
词性: verb (动词)

galvanise: /ˈɡælvənaɪz/ (British English)
verb
激励；刺激；震动；镀锌
to shock or excite someone into taking action.  To coat (iron or steel) with a protective layer of zinc.
The President's speech galvanized the nation. 总统的讲话激励了全国人民。
The railings were galvanized to protect them from the elements. 栏杆经过镀锌以防止其受自然因素的影响。
近义词: stimulate, energize, excite, rouse, spur, jolt, shock, electrify, arouse, animate, invigorate, plate (with zinc)
反义词: demotivate, discourage, depress, soothe, calm
词性: verb (动词)


词源: From French *galvaniser*, from  Italian *galvanizzare* "to galvanize," named after Luigi Galvani (1737-1798), Italian physiologist.
