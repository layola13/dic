enlightened:/ɪnˈlaɪtnd/| adj.|开明的；启发的；有见识的；|Having or showing a rational, modern, and well-informed outlook.|例句：The Dalai Lama is seen as an enlightened leader. 达赖喇嘛被视为一位开明的领袖。|例句：She felt enlightened after her yoga retreat.  瑜伽静修之后她感觉受到了启迪。|近义词：informed, aware, educated, knowledgeable, wise|反义词：ignorant, unenlightened, prejudiced, biased|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From en- + lightenen, from light + -en.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *leukhtam.
