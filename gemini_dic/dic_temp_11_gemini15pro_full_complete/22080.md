bollen:/ˈbɒlən/ (UK) /ˈboʊlən/ (US)| adj.|肿胀的，膨胀的|Swollen, inflated, enlarged, distended.  Referring to something increased in size due to internal pressure, often from fluid or gas.|The bee sting made his hand bollen. 蜜蜂蜇了他的手，使他的手肿了起来。|The river was bollen with floodwater. 河水因洪水而暴涨。|近义词:swollen, inflated, distended, enlarged, puffy, turgid|反义词:shrunken, deflated, contracted|词性:形容词
词源: Middle English *bollen*, from Old Norse *bolginn*, past participle of *bolgna* "to swell," related to Old English *belgan* "to swell with anger."
