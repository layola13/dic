elliptic:/ɪˈlɪptɪk/ | adj. | 椭圆的；省略的 | Having the shape of an ellipse; of or relating to ellipsis. |  The moon's orbit is elliptic. 月球的轨道是椭圆的。| synonyms: oval, oblong | antonyms: circular, round | adjective

elliptic:/ɪˈlɪptɪk/ | adj. | 省略的 |  (of speech) using or involving ellipsis. | His elliptic style can be hard to follow. 他省略的风格很难理解。 | synonyms: concise, terse | antonyms: verbose, prolix | adjective
