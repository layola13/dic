blanketeer:/ˌblæŋkɪˈtɪər/
n.
提供毛毯的人；分发毛毯的志愿者
A person who distributes blankets, especially to the homeless or disaster victims.
The blanketeers were out in force last night, providing warmth to those sleeping rough in the freezing temperatures. 昨晚，许多志愿者在严寒中为露宿街头的人分发毛毯。
近义词:volunteer, aid worker
反义词: N/A
词性:noun

blanketeer:/ˌblæŋkɪˈtɪər/
v.
向…提供毛毯
To provide blankets for someone, especially the homeless or disaster victims.
The charity blanketed the refugees with warm blankets.  慈善机构为难民提供了温暖的毛毯。
近义词:cover, provide
反义词: uncover, deprive
词性:verb
