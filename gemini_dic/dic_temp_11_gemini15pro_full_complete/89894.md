horridness:/ˈhɒrɪdnəs/ (US) /ˈhɒrɪdnɪs/ (UK)| n.|恐怖；可怕；令人厌恶|The horridness of the crime scene shocked even the seasoned detectives. 犯罪现场的恐怖景象即使是经验丰富的侦探也感到震惊。|The horridness of the smell made me gag.  这可怕的气味让我作呕。|近义词：awfulness, dreadfulness, ghastliness, terribleness|反义词：pleasantness, beauty, delightfulness|词性:名词
Etymology: From horrid + -ness.  Horrid comes from the Latin word "horridus," meaning "bristling," "rough," or "dreadful."
