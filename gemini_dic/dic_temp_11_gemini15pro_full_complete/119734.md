midward:/ˈmɪdwərd/| adj.|向中间的，朝向中心的|Directed or situated toward the middle.|The midward drift of the continents. 大陆向中间的漂移。|近义词：central, middle, medial|反义词:outward, peripheral|词性:形容词

Etymology:  From mid + -ward.  "Mid" meaning middle and "-ward" indicating direction.
