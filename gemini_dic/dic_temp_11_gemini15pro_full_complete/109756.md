leviers: /ˈliːvərz/ | n. | 杠杆；手段；施压方式 |  A rigid bar resting on a pivot, used to help move a heavy or firmly fixed load with one end when pressure is applied to the other. Also, a means of accomplishing; a tool for achieving something. |  Example 1:  They used a lever to lift the heavy rock.  他们用杠杆举起了沉重的岩石。

Example 2:  Economic sanctions are a powerful lever for influencing foreign policy. 经济制裁是影响外交政策的有力杠杆。 | Synonyms: tool, instrument, mechanism, device, handle, crowbar | Antonyms: hindrance, obstacle, impediment | noun
