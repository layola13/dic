channellers:/ˈtʃænələrz/
n.
通灵者，灵媒
People who claim to communicate with spirits or other non-physical entities.
Many channelers claim to receive messages from deceased loved ones. 许多通灵者声称收到来自已故亲人的信息。
mediums, psychics
sceptics, doubters
noun
