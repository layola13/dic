lengthens:/ˈlɛŋθənz/
verb
加长，延长，变长
makes or becomes longer.
The days lengthen in the spring. 春天白天变长。
近义词:extends, prolongs, stretches
反义词:shortens, contracts, reduces
词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lengthnen, from Old English *lenȝþnian (unattested), from lenȝþu (“length”) +‎ -nian (suffix forming verbs). Equivalent to length +‎ -en. Cognate with Dutch lengen (“to lengthen”), German längen (“to lengthen”), Swedish länga (“to long for”).
