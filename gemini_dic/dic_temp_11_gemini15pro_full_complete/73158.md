forbearant:/fɔːrˈbɛərənt/ | adj. | 宽容的；忍耐的；克制的 | Patient and tolerant; refraining from retaliation or punishment. | Example 1: Despite the constant interruptions, the teacher remained forbearant with her students. 尽管不断被打断，老师仍然对她的学生们保持耐心。

Example 2:  Her forbearant nature allowed her to forgive his thoughtless remarks. 她宽容的性格让她原谅了他轻率的言论。 | 近义词: patient, tolerant, forgiving, lenient, indulgent | 反义词: impatient, intolerant, unforgiving, vindictive | 词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English *forberand, present participle of forberen, from Old English forberan (“to forbear, refrain, endure, tolerate”), from for- + beran (“to bear”).
