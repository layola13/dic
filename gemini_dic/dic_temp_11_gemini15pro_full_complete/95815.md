incorporality:/ˌɪnkɔːrˈpɔːriˈælɪti/
n.
非物质性，无形性
The state of not having a physical body or form; immateriality.
The incorporality of ghosts makes them difficult to study. 鬼魂的非物质性使得它们难以研究。
Near Synonyms: immateriality, intangibility, bodilessness, formlessness, spirituality, ethereality
Antonyms: corporeality, materiality, physicality, tangibility
