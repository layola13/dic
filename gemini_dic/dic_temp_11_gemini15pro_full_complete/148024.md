overroasting:/ˌoʊvərˈroʊstɪŋ/ | n. | 过度烘烤 | The act of roasting something for too long, resulting in dryness and loss of flavor. | The overroasting of the chicken made it dry and tough. 过度烘烤使得鸡肉又干又硬。| overcooking, burning | undercooking | noun

overroast:/ˌoʊvərˈroʊst/ | vt. | 过度烘烤 | To roast something for too long, resulting in dryness and loss of flavor. | I overroasted the vegetables, and they were burnt. 我把蔬菜烤过头了，烤焦了。| overcook, burn | undercook | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "roast" (from Old French "rostir," ultimately from Latin "torrere" - to parch).
