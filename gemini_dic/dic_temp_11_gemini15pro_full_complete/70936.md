firecrackers:/ˈfaɪərkrækərz/ (US), /ˈfaɪəkrækəz/ (UK)| n. |爆竹，鞭炮|Small explosive devices primarily used for producing a loud noise and bright flashes, especially during celebrations.|例句：Children love to light firecrackers during the Spring Festival. 孩子们喜欢在春节期间燃放鞭炮。

例句：The sound of firecrackers filled the air on New Year's Eve. 除夕夜，鞭炮声响彻天空。

近义词：fireworks, bangers, crackers|反义词: None|词性: Noun
