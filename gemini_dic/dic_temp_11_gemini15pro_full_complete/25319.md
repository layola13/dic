bugler's:/ˈbjuːɡlərz/| n. |号兵的，吹号手的|Something that belongs to or is associated with a bugler.|The bugler's trumpet gleamed in the sunlight.  号兵的喇叭在阳光下闪闪发光。|近义词：trumpeter's|反义词: N/A|词性:名词所有格

Etymology:
From "bugler" + "'s" (possessive suffix). "Bugler" comes from the Old French "bugle" (wild ox), later referring to a hunting horn made from ox horn, and eventually to the person who plays the bugle.
