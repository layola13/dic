coevolution:/ˌkoʊˌɛvəˈluːʃən/ |n.| 共同进化 |The joint evolution of two or more interacting species, each of which evolves in response to selection pressures imposed by the other.  |The coevolution of flowering plants and their pollinators is a classic example of mutualistic symbiosis.  开花植物与其传粉者的共同进化是互惠共生的典型例子。

The relationship between predator and prey is often described as an example of coevolution. 捕食者与猎物之间的关系通常被描述为共同进化的一个例子。| 近义词: coadaptation, reciprocal adaptation | 反义词: independent evolution | 词性: 名词
