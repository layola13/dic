geekiest:/ˈɡiːkiɪst/ | adj. | 最怪咖的，最书呆子气的 |  Superlative form of geeky: most geeky.  Describes someone who is extremely enthusiastic about and knowledgeable about a particular subject, especially one considered obscure or technical, often to the point of being socially awkward. | He has the geekiest collection of vintage computers I've ever seen. 他收藏的古董电脑是我见过的最怪的。 |  nerdiest, dorkiest | coolest, most popular | adjective
