encradle:/ɪnˈkreɪdl/| vt.| 拥抱；抚育；珍藏；安置|To hold or cherish as if in a cradle; to shelter or protect.|例句：The mother encradleed her newborn baby in her arms. 母亲把新生儿抱在怀里。|例句：The valley is encradleed by majestic mountains. 山谷被雄伟的山脉环绕。|近义词：cherish, embrace, nurture, protect, shelter|反义词：abandon, expose, neglect|词性：动词

词源:

From en- + cradle.  "En-" is a prefix meaning "to put into" or "to cause to be in".  "Cradle" refers to a baby's bed or a place of origin.
