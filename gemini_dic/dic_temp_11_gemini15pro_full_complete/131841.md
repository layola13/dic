nonconspiring:/ˌnɑːnkənˈspaɪrɪŋ/
adjective
未串通的，没有共谋的
Not engaged in a conspiracy; not plotting or scheming secretly with others.
The jury found the defendant nonconspiring in the robbery. 陪审团认定被告没有参与抢劫的共谋。
The two witnesses provided nonconspiring testimonies. 两名证人的证词没有串通。
Synonyms: innocent, uninvolved, independent
Antonyms: conspiring, colluding, complicit
词性: 形容词
