contradance: /ˈkɒntrəˌdɑːns/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntrəˌdæns/ (US)
| n. |集体舞，方块舞|A folk dance in which couples form two facing lines and perform a sequence of movements with different partners. |The town held a contradance in the town square.  小镇在广场上举办了一场集体舞。|近义词：square dance, folk dance|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "solo dance")|noun
