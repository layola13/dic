grande:/ˈɡrɑːndeɪ/ (US), /ˈɡrændi/ (UK)| adj. n.|大的，特大的 (adj.)；大杯 (n.)|From Italian *grande* "large, great," from Latin *grandis* "big, great."  It refers to a large size, especially of a drink. As a noun, it specifically refers to a large size beverage, commonly coffee.  As an adjective, while less common in English than in Italian or Spanish, it still carries the meaning of "large" or "grand."|例句：I'll have a grande latte, please.  我要一杯大杯拿铁。|近义词：large, big, huge, massive (adj.); venti (Starbucks size) (n.)|反义词:small, little, tiny, petite (adj.); tall, short (coffee sizes) (n.)|词性:adjective, noun
