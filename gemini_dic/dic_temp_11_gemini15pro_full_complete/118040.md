menace:/ˈmenəs/ | n. | 威胁，恐吓  | A person or thing that is likely to cause harm; a threat or danger. |  He is a menace to society. 他是对社会的威胁。| Synonym: threat, danger, peril | Antonym: safety, security, protection | 词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *menacen, from Anglo-Norman manacer, menacer, from Late Latin mināciārī (“to threaten”), present active infinitive of minācior, from Latin minax (“threatening”), from minae (“threats”).
