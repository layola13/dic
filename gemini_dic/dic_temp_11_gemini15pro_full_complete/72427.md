fluid:/ˈfluːɪd/(US), /ˈfluːɪd/(UK)| adj. n.|流动的，流体的；易变的，不固定的；流畅的|A substance that has no fixed shape and yields easily to external pressure; a gas or (especially) a liquid.|例句：Water is a fluid. 水是流体。

The situation remains fluid. 局势依然不稳定。|近义词：liquid, gaseous, flowing, smooth, adaptable|反义词：solid, rigid, inflexible, fixed|词性:形容词,名词

fluid:/ˈfluːɪd/(US), /ˈfluːɪd/(UK)| n.|流体|A substance that has no fixed shape and yields easily to external pressure; a gas or (especially) a liquid.|例句：The body is composed of various fluids. 人体由各种液体组成。

Hydraulic fluid is used in many machines. 液压油用于许多机器。|近义词：liquid, gas|反义词：solid|词性:名词

From Late Middle English: from late Latin fluidus, from Latin fluere ‘to flow’.
