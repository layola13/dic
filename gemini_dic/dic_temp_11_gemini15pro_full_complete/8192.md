anti-domestic:/ˌænti-dəˈmɛstɪk/
adjective
反家庭的；反对家庭生活的；不适于家庭生活的
Opposed to or unsuitable for domestic life; rejecting traditional family values or structures.
She had a very anti-domestic attitude, preferring to travel and work rather than settle down and raise a family. 她有非常反家庭的态度，宁愿旅行和工作，也不愿安定下来，养家糊口。
His anti-domestic lifestyle meant he ate out most nights and rarely did any housework. 他反家庭的生活方式意味着他大多数晚上都在外面吃饭，很少做家务。
Near Synonyms: non-domestic, undomesticated, unconventional
Antonyms: domestic, home-loving, traditional, conventional
