outstrips:/ˌaʊtˈstrɪps/
verb
超过，胜过
to go faster or further than someone or something else in a race or competition, or to be more successful
例句：Demand is outstripping supply. 需求超过了供应。

例句：The company's profits have outstripped all expectations.  公司的利润超出了所有预期。

近义词：surpasses, exceeds, transcends, overtakes, outdoes
反义词：lags behind, falls short of, trails
词性：v.
