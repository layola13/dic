oomph:/ʊmf/ | n. |活力，吸引力，魅力 |Energy, vigor, appeal; sex appeal.| Her dress added a touch of oomph to her appearance. 她的裙子为她的外表增添了一丝魅力。| 近义词: energy, vigor, pizzazz, charisma, appeal | 反义词: dullness, apathy | 词性: 名词

词源:  Likely imitative of a sudden expulsion of breath, suggesting sudden exertion or impact.  Possibly influenced by German _umpf_ (interjection expressing exertion or impact).
