flesher:/ˈflɛʃər/ | n. |屠夫；剔骨工；肉商|A person who prepares animal carcasses for market, especially one who removes the bones from meat. | The flesher skillfully prepared the cuts of beef for the butcher shop.  剔骨工人熟练地为肉店准备牛肉块。|  The flesher worked quickly to process the deer carcass.  剔骨工人迅速地处理鹿的尸体。|Synonyms: butcher, meat cutter | Antonyms: vegetarian, vegan | noun
