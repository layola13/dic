deforcement:/dɪˈfɔːrsmənt/| n.|阻碍；阻止|The act of preventing someone from doing something they are legally entitled to do.|The deforcement of his right to vote angered him.  他被阻止行使投票权，这让他很生气。|Synonyms: obstruction, hindrance, prevention|Antonyms: assistance, facilitation|noun
