hypergrammatically:/ˌhaɪpərɡrəˈmætɪkli/| adv.|过度语法地，过分注重语法的|Excessively or overly concerned with grammar; adhering to grammatical rules with extreme rigidity. |She analyzed the sentence hypergrammatically, dissecting every clause and identifying every part of speech.| 近义词: pedantically, meticulously, scrupulously | 反义词: loosely, carelessly, informally |词性:副词
