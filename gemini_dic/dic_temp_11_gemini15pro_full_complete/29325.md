castigations:/ˌkæstɪˈɡeɪʃənz/
noun
惩罚，责骂，苛评
Severe criticisms or punishments.
The teacher's castigations were feared by all the students. 老师的责骂让所有学生都感到害怕。
synonyms: reprimands, rebukes, criticisms, punishments, scoldings, chastisements
antonyms: praise, commendation, accolades, rewards
n.

castigation:/ˌkæstɪˈɡeɪʃən/
noun
惩罚，责骂，苛评
Severe criticism or punishment.
His latest novel received harsh castigation from the critics.  他的最新小说受到了评论家严厉的批评。
synonyms: reprimand, rebuke, criticism, punishment, scolding, chastisement
antonyms: praise, commendation, accolade, reward
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin castigātiō (“correction, reproof”), from Latin castigō (“I correct, chastise, punish”), from castus (“pure, chaste”) + agō (“I do, drive”).
