buddying-up:/ˈbʌdiɪŋ ʌp/ | n./phrasal verb |结伴，搭档，建立伙伴关系 |The act of forming a partnership or close working relationship with someone, often for mutual benefit or support.  It implies a sense of camaraderie and collaboration. | Example: The company encourages buddying-up between senior and junior employees for mentorship. 公司鼓励资深员工和初级员工结成伙伴关系，进行指导。

Example:  Buddying-up with a native speaker is a great way to improve your language skills. 与母语人士结伴学习是提高语言技能的好方法。| 近义词: partnering, teaming up, collaborating, joining forces | 反义词: going solo, working independently | verb phrase
