misdemeanant: /ˌmɪsdɪˈmiːnənt/ | n. | 轻罪犯；行为不检点者 | A person convicted of a misdemeanor (a less serious crime than a felony). |  The misdemeanant was sentenced to community service.  轻罪犯被判处社区服务。 | Synonym: offender, culprit, criminal (for broader context) | Antonym: law-abiding citizen | noun

misdemeanor: /ˌmɪsdɪˈmiːnər/ | n. | 轻罪 | A minor wrongdoing; a nonindictable offense, less serious than a felony. |  Jaywalking is considered a misdemeanor in many jurisdictions. 乱穿马路在许多司法管辖区被认为是轻罪。| Synonym: petty offense, infraction, violation | Antonym: felony | noun
