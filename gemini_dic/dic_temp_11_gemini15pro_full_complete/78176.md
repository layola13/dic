georgical:/dʒɔːˈdʒɪk(ə)l/
adjective
农事的；田园诗的
Relating to agriculture or rural life; descriptive of or relating to a type of poetry that deals with agriculture or rural subjects.
Virgil's Georgics are poems about agriculture. 维吉尔的《农事诗》是关于农业的诗歌。
近义词:agricultural, rural, pastoral, agrarian
反义词:urban, industrial
词性:adjective
