invertebrate:/ɪnˈvɜːrtɪbrət/ /ɪnˈvɜːrtəbrɪt/| n. adj.|无脊椎动物；无脊椎动物的|Animals without a backbone, such as insects, spiders, worms, and jellyfish are invertebrates.|例句：Invertebrates make up the vast majority of animal species.  无脊椎动物占动物物种的绝大多数。

例句：Many invertebrates live in the ocean. 许多无脊椎动物生活在海洋中。

|近义词：spineless creature|反义词:vertebrate|词性:名词, 形容词
