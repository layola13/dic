feel-for:/ˈfiːl fɔːr/ | v. |同情；体谅；理解；摸索；寻找|To have sympathy and understanding for someone; to try to find something by touch.|例句：I really feel for her, losing her job like that. 我真的很同情她，像那样失去了工作。|例句：He fumbled in the dark, feeling for the light switch. 他在黑暗中摸索，寻找电灯开关。|近义词：sympathize with, empathize with, grope for|反义词：ignore, disregard|词性:动词
