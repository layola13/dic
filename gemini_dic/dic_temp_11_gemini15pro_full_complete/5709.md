alums:/əˈlʌmz/
noun
校友（复数）
Plural of alum, short for alumnus (male) or alumna (female).  A former student of a particular school, college, or university.
We invited all the alums back for the school's 100th anniversary. 我们邀请了所有的校友回校参加学校的百年校庆。
The alums donated a new library to the school. 校友们向学校捐赠了一个新图书馆。
synonyms: graduates, former students
antonyms: current students, freshmen
