heterosporous:/ˌhɛtərəˈspɔːrəs/
adjective
异孢子的
Producing two types of spores differing in size and sex potential.
Example: Heterosporous plants, such as ferns, produce both megaspores and microspores.  异孢子植物，例如蕨类植物，会产生大孢子和微孢子。
Example:  The evolution of heterospory was a key step in the development of seed plants. 异孢子的进化是种子植物发展中的关键一步。
Synonym: anisosporous
Antonym: homosporous
词性: adjective
