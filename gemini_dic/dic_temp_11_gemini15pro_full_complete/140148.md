offcuts:/ˈɔːfkʌts/
n.
残料，边角料，零碎
pieces of material that are left over after the main part has been cut
The carpenter used the offcuts of wood to make a small shelf. 木匠用木头的边角料做了一个小架子。
scraps, remnants, leftovers, trimmings
--- (no clear antonym)
noun


offcuts:/ˈɔːfkʌts/
n.
(广播、电视或电影节目的)剪辑片段，节选
short extracts from broadcasts, films, or recordings
The documentary included offcuts from interviews with the president.  这部纪录片包括了对总统采访的剪辑片段。
clips, excerpts, snippets
--- (no clear antonym)
