defectious:/dɪˈfɛkʃəs/
adjective
有缺陷的；不完美的；叛逃的
deserting a cause or organization, especially in a time of crisis.
例句：The defectious soldier revealed classified information to the enemy.  叛逃的士兵向敌人泄露了机密信息。

例句：The product was recalled due to a defectious design. 该产品因设计缺陷而被召回。
近义词：disloyal, traitorous, flawed, imperfect, defective
反义词：loyal, faithful, perfect, flawless
词性：adjective
词源：From Latin dēfectiō (“revolt, desertion”), from dēficiō (“I revolt, desert, fail”).
