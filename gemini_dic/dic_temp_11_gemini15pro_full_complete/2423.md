adjournment:/əˈdʒɜːnmənt/| n.|休会，延期，休庭|An adjournment is a temporary postponement or suspension of a meeting, legal case, or other formal proceeding. |The judge announced an adjournment until the following week. 法官宣布休庭到下周。|Synonyms: postponement, deferment, recess, suspension|Antonyms: commencement, resumption, continuation|Noun
