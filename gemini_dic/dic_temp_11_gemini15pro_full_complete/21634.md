blurriest:/ˈblɜːriɪst/| adj.|最模糊的|Superlative form of blurry; most blurry.  Having the least clarity or distinctness. |The photo was the blurriest of the bunch, making it impossible to identify the subject. 这张照片是所有照片中最模糊的，无法识别拍摄对象。| The CCTV footage was so blurriest that we couldn't make out the license plate number. 监控录像太模糊了，我们看不清车牌号。|近义词：haziest, fuzziest, indistinctest, vaguest|反义词：clearest, sharpest, distinctest|词性:形容词

词源:
From blur + -y + -est.  "Blur" comes from Middle English blure, perhaps related to bleren "to bleat," suggesting a wavering or indistinct sound, and later applied to vision.
