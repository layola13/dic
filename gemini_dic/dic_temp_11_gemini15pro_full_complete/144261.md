outsoaring:/ˌaʊtˈsɔːrɪŋ/
|v.|超越，胜过|Surpassing or exceeding in flight or altitude; figuratively, surpassing or exceeding in any achievement or quality.|The eagle was outsoaring all the other birds.  这只鹰翱翔的高度超过了所有其他鸟类。|例句：Her talent outsoared all expectations. 她的才华超出了所有人的预期。|近义词：surpassing, exceeding, excelling, transcending|反义词: lagging, trailing, falling behind|词性:动词
