amphicarpic:/æmfɪˈkɑːrpɪk/ | adj. | 两型果的，异果性的 | Producing fruit of two different forms, especially aerial and subterranean fruit, often differing in morphology and means of dispersal. | Peanuts are an example of an amphicarpic plant, producing both above-ground and below-ground pods. 花生是两型果植物的一个例子，既能在地上结荚，也能在地下结荚。| Synonym: heterocarpic | Antonym: homocarpic | adjective

amphicarpous:/æmfɪˈkɑːrpəs/ | adj. | 两型果的，异果性的 |  Producing fruit of two different forms, especially aerial and subterranean fruit, often differing in morphology and means of dispersal. | Some species of *Trifolium* are amphicarpous. 一些三叶草属的物种是两型果的。 | Synonym: heterocarpic | Antonym: homocarpic | adjective
