grasscutter:/ˈɡræsˌkʌtər/ | n. | 草食动物，割草机 | An animal that feeds on grass; a machine used for cutting grass. | The grasscutter was busy in the field. 割草机在田野里忙碌着。 | The farmer uses a grasscutter to maintain his lawn. 农夫用割草机维护他的草坪。 | 近义词：herbivore, mower, lawnmower | 反义词: carnivore | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "grass" + "cutter". "Grass" comes from Old English "græs," and "cutter" comes from "cut" which originates from Old English "cyttan."
