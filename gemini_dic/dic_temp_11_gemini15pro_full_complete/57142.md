例句：The movie was mastered in Dolby Vision, giving it a more realistic look. 这部电影采用杜比视界进行母带制作，使其看起来更逼真。|近义词：HDR10, HDR10+, HLG|反义词:SDR (Standard Dynamic Range)|词性:名词
