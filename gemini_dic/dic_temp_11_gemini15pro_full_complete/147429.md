overnutrition:/ˌoʊvərnuˈtrɪʃən/| n. |营养过剩，营养过度|The condition of receiving more nutrients than the body needs, which can lead to health problems such as obesity and heart disease.| Excessive consumption of fats and sugars can lead to overnutrition. 过量摄入脂肪和糖会导致营养过剩。| 近义词：hypernutrition, overalimentation | 反义词: malnutrition, undernutrition | 词性: noun
