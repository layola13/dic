gorging:/ˈɡɔːrdʒɪŋ/ /ˈɡɔːr-/| n. |狼吞虎咽；暴食；塞饱|The act of eating a large amount of food greedily.|He felt sick after a weekend of gorging on junk food. 他周末狂吃垃圾食品后感到恶心。|近义词：stuffing, cramming, overeating, bingeing|反义词:dieting, fasting|noun

gorge:/ɡɔːrdʒ/ /ɡɔːr-/| v. |狼吞虎咽；塞饱；充斥|To eat a large amount of food greedily; to fill something completely.|He gorged himself on pizza. 他狼吞虎咽地吃了披萨。|近义词：stuff, cram, overeat, binge|反义词:starve, diet|verb
