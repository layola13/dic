near-misses:/nɪr ˈmɪsɪz/| n. |侥幸脱险，差一点发生的意外|An event or situation that comes close to being an accident or disaster, but is narrowly avoided.|The pilot reported several near-misses with other aircraft during the flight. 飞行员报告说在飞行过程中有几次险些与其他飞机相撞。|Near-misses can be valuable learning experiences. 这些险些发生的意外可以成为宝贵的学习经验。|close calls, near accidents|hits, collisions|noun
