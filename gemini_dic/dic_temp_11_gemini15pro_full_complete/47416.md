deadlining: /ˈdɛdlaɪnɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
设定截止日期;  处理最后期限相关事宜
The act of setting deadlines, or working towards a deadline.
We're deadlining the project for next Friday.  我们正在为项目设定下周五的截止日期。
They were deadlining the tasks for the next sprint.  他们正在为下一个冲刺阶段的任务设定截止日期。
近义词: scheduling, prioritizing
反义词: procrastinating

词性: verb (present participle)
