microcyst: /ˈmaɪkrəʊsɪst/ | n. | 微囊，小囊肿 | A small cyst, often microscopic.  A cyst is a closed sac or cavity, having a distinct membrane and developing abnormally in a body cavity or structure. Microcysts are usually filled with fluid or semisolid material. |  Microscopic examination revealed numerous microcysts in the tissue sample.  显微镜检查显示组织样本中存在大量微囊。|  cyst, vesicle | tumor, mass | noun
