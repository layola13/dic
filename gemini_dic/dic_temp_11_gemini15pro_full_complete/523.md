abomasus:/ˌæbəˈmeɪsəs/ | n. | 皱胃 | The fourth and final stomach compartment in ruminants such as cattle and sheep. | The abomasus is responsible for the final stage of digestion in ruminants. 皱胃负责反刍动物的最后消化阶段。| Synonym:  rennet stomach | Antonym: None | Noun
