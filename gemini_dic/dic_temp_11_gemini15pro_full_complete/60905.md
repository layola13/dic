elenctic:/ɪˈlɛŋktɪk/
adjective
驳斥的，反驳的
Refuting or capable of refuting; convicting of error.  Serving to confute or disprove.
Elenctic arguments are designed to expose fallacies and inconsistencies in reasoning.  驳斥性论证旨在揭露推理中的谬误和不一致之处。
Elenctic questioning can help clarify complex issues.  驳斥性提问可以帮助澄清复杂的问题。
近义词:refuting, disproving, confuting
反义词:supporting, confirming, proving
词性:adjective

词源: From Ancient Greek ἐλεγκτικός (elenktikós, “refuting”), from ἐλέγχω (elénkhō, “I examine, cross-examine, convict, refute”).
