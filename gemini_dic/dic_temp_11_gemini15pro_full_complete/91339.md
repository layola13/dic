hydropically:/haɪˈdrɒpɪkli/| adv.|水肿地，水肿性地|Characterized by or exhibiting dropsy; swollen with fluid.|The patient's legs were hydropically distended. 病人的腿水肿膨胀。|近义词：edematous, swollen|反义词: shrunk, withered|词性:副词

词源: From Greek *hydrops* (ὕδωρ water + ὤψ face, appearance) + -ical + -ly.  Originally referring to a condition characterized by watery swelling, especially of the face.
