junking:/ˈdʒʌŋkɪŋ/
n.|抛弃，丢弃，处理掉|The act of discarding something considered useless or unwanted.  The process of getting rid of old or unwanted items.|They are junking their old car. 他们正在处理掉他们的旧车。|近义词：discarding, scrapping, dumping, ditching|反义词：keeping, saving, hoarding|词性:名词

junking:/ˈdʒʌŋkɪŋ/
v.|丢弃，抛弃，处理掉 (gerund or present participle of "junk")|The act of discarding something considered useless or unwanted. The process of getting rid of old or unwanted items.|He's junking all his old clothes. 他正在丢弃所有旧衣服。|近义词：discarding, scrapping, dumping, ditching|反义词：keeping, saving, hoarding|词性:动词

词源: From the verb "junk," which originated in the 15th century as a nautical term referring to old rope or cables.  It later expanded to mean discarded items in general.
