festschrift: /ˈfɛstʃrɪft/ | n. |  纪念文集； Festschrift | A collection of essays or articles written by colleagues and admirers to honor a scholar, often on a special anniversary such as retirement. | Example 1: A festschrift was published in honor of Professor Smith's 70th birthday.  史密斯教授70岁生日时，出版了一本纪念文集。

Example 2:  She contributed an article to the festschrift for her former mentor. 她为她以前的导师的纪念文集撰写了一篇文章。| Synonym:  commemorative volume, tribute, homage | Antonym: N/A | noun
