fictionally:/ˈfɪkʃənəli/| adv.|虚构地，小说式地|In a fictional way; as if invented for a novel or story.  It describes something created from imagination, not based on fact.|He spoke fictionally about his adventures. 他虚构地讲述了他的冒险经历。|Synonyms: imaginatively, inventively, unrealistically, fictitiously|Antonyms: factually, truly, realistically, actually|词性:副词
