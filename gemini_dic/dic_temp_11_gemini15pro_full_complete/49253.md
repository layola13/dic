deleterious:/ˌdɛləˈtɪəriəs/
adjective
有害的；有毒的
causing harm or damage
例句：The chemical has deleterious effects on the environment. 这种化学物质对环境有有害影响。

例句：Smoking is deleterious to your health. 吸烟有害健康。

近义词：harmful, injurious, detrimental, damaging, noxious, pernicious
反义词：beneficial, advantageous, helpful, salutary

词性：adjective
