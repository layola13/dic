amuna:/əˈmuːnə/ | n. | 阿穆纳（古埃及的创造神）| Amuna is a variant spelling of Amun, the ancient Egyptian god of creation.  He was particularly associated with Thebes, where he was worshipped as the "King of the Gods". Later, he was combined with the sun god Ra to become Amun-Ra. |  The temple of Amuna was magnificent.  阿穆纳神庙雄伟壮丽。| Synonym: Amun, Amen, Amon | Antonym: None | noun

amuna:/əˈmuːnə/ | adj. | 阿蒙的；与阿蒙神有关的 (罕见用法) | Relating to or associated with the Egyptian god Amun. | The Amuna priesthood was very powerful.  阿蒙神职人员非常有权势。 | Synonym: Amun's | Antonym: None | adjective
