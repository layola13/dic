avuncularity:/əˌvʌŋˈkjʊˈlærəti/
|n.|叔伯般的慈爱；像伯父（或叔父、舅父、姑父）般地|Kindness and generosity, especially of an older man towards a younger one, like that of a benevolent uncle.|He exuded an avuncularity that made everyone feel comfortable. 他展现出一种叔伯般的慈爱，让每个人都感到自在。|Synonyms: benevolence, paternalism, condescension|Antonyms: hostility, animosity, indifference|noun
