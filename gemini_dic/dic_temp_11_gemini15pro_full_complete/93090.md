idyll:/ˈɪdɪl/ /ˈaɪdl/ | n. |田园诗; 牧歌; 浪漫插曲|A short description of a peaceful or romantic scene, especially in the countryside.|例句：The book evokes an idyllic pastoral scene. 这本书描绘了一幅田园牧歌式的画面。

例句：Their honeymoon was a brief idyll. 他们的蜜月是一段短暂的浪漫插曲。|近义词：pastoral, romance, poem, scene|反义词：ordeal, hardship|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *idylle*, from Latin *idyllīon*, from Greek *eidúllion* (“little picture, short poem”), diminutive of *eidos* (“form, shape”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *weyd- (“to see”).
