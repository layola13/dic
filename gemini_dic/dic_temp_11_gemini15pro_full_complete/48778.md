deflationism:/dɪˈfleɪʃənɪzəm/ | n. | 通货紧缩主义 |  The economic belief that a general decline in prices (deflation) is desirable or beneficial, often advocating policies to reduce the money supply to achieve this. | The government's deflationism policies led to a recession. 政府的通货紧缩政策导致了经济衰退。|  The central bank adopted a policy of deflationism to combat rising inflation. 中央银行采取了通货紧缩政策来对抗不断上升的通货膨胀。 | synonyms: monetary contraction, tight money policy | antonyms: inflationism, reflation | noun
