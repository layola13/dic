antistress:/ˌæntiˈstrɛs/| adj. n.|抗压的，减压的；抗压物，减压方法|Something that is antistress is intended to help people become less stressed.  It can also be used as a noun to refer to something that relieves stress.|I bought an antistress coloring book. 我买了一本抗压涂色书。|近义词：relaxing, calming, soothing, stress-relieving|反义词: stressful, anxiety-inducing|词性: adjective, noun
anti-stress (hyphenated form):/ˌæntiˈstrɛs/| adj.|抗压的，减压的|Same as "antistress," but with a hyphen. This form is less common but still acceptable.|This anti-stress tea really helped me relax. 这款抗压茶真的帮助我放松了。|近义词：relaxing, calming, soothing, stress-relieving|反义词: stressful, anxiety-inducing|词性: adjective

Etymology:  "anti-" (prefix of Greek origin meaning "against, opposite of") + "stress" (from Old French "estresse" meaning "narrowness, oppression").
