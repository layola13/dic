cartoned:/ˈkɑːrtənd/ | adj. | 装在纸盒里的；盒装的 | Packed in a carton; boxed. | The milk is cartoned for easy transport. 牛奶装在纸盒里以便运输。| The juice was cartoned and ready for shipping. 果汁已装箱并准备发货。 | 近义词：boxed, packaged | 反义词: unpackaged, loose | 词性:形容词
词源: carton (n.) + -ed (suffix forming adjectives)  From French carton, from Italian cartone, from carta "paper," from Latin charta. The suffix -ed is used to form adjectives from nouns, often indicating that something is "provided with" or "characterized by" the noun.
