bouffe: /buːf/ (US), /buːf/ (UK) | n. | 食物；伙食（尤指粗劣的） | Food, especially of a rough or simple kind. |  The bouffe at the camp was barely edible.  营地的食物几乎不能吃。

The restaurant served surprisingly good bouffe. 这家餐馆提供的食物出乎意料的好。 | 近义词：grub, chow, food, victuals | 反义词: gourmet food, delicacy | 词性: noun


词源:
From French "bouffer" (to puff, to stuff, to eat greedily). Ultimately from Latin "bufo" (toad), perhaps influenced by the puffed-up appearance of a toad.
