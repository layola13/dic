nonfattening:/ˌnɑːnˈfætənɪŋ/ /ˌnɒnˈfætənɪŋ/| adj.|不增肥的，低脂肪的|Containing little or no fat, and therefore unlikely to cause weight gain.  Food marketed as "nonfattening" is often low in calories as well.|This yogurt is nonfattening, so you can enjoy it without worrying about your weight.  这种酸奶不增肥，所以你可以尽情享用，不用担心体重。|Low-fat, lean, diet, light|Fattening, high-calorie, rich|adjective
