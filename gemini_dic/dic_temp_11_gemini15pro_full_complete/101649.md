irrefutably:/ɪrɪˈfjuːtəbli/
adverb
无可辩驳地
in a way that cannot be refuted or disproved.
His guilt was irrefutably established by the evidence. 他的罪行被证据无可辩驳地确立了。
Synonyms: indisputably, undeniably, incontrovertibly
Antonyms: disputably, deniably, questionably
词性: 副词
