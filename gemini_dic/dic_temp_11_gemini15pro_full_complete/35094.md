clothespress: /ˈkloʊzˌprɛs/ (US) /ˈkləʊðzˌprɛs/ (UK)| n.|衣橱，衣柜|A large cupboard or wardrobe for storing clothes.|She kept her winter coats in the clothespress.|近义词：wardrobe, closet, armoire|反义词: N/A|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "clothes" + "press".  "Clothes" comes from Old English "clāþas". "Press" comes from Old French "presse" and ultimately from Latin "premere" (to press).
