dun:/dʌn/
adjective|暗褐色的，灰褐色的|of a dull grayish-brown color.|The horse was a dun color.|近义词:  grayish-brown, mousey, drab|反义词: bright, colorful|词性:形容词

dun:/dʌn/
noun|讨债人|a person, especially a creditor or debt collector, who persistently makes demands.|The dun was relentless in his pursuit of the debt.|近义词: debt collector, creditor|反义词: debtor, borrower|词性:名词

dun:/dʌn/
verb|催促(还债)|make persistent demands on (someone), especially for payment of a debt.|He was being dunned by creditors.|近义词: pester, hound, harass|反义词: pay, forgive|词性:动词

词源:
From Middle English *dunnen*, *dunnyn*, of uncertain origin; perhaps related to Old English *dunn* (“dark, dusky”).
