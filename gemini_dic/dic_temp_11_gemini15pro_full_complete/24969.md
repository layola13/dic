bruskest:/brʌskɪst/ | adj. | 最粗暴的，最唐突的 |  Superlative form of "brusk," meaning abrupt or offhand in speech or manner.  It describes someone who is extremely short and unfriendly in their way of communicating. |  He gave the bruskest reply I've ever heard. 他给出了我听过的最粗鲁的回答。

The receptionist gave me the bruskest look and turned away.  接待员给了我一个极其粗鲁的眼神，然后转身走开了。| 近义词: rudest, curtest, abruptest, sharpest | 反义词: kindest, gentlest, politest, most courteous | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *brusque*, from Italian *brusco* "sour, harsh, tart," perhaps of Gaulish origin.
