ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *landesman, from Old English *landesmann (“countryman, compatriot”), equivalent to land +‎ -s +‎ man. Cognate with Dutch landsman (“compatriot, fellow countryman”), German Landsmann (“compatriot, fellow countryman”).
