avaricious: /ˌævəˈrɪʃəs/ | adj. | 贪婪的，贪得无厌的 |  Driven by an insatiable desire for wealth or material possessions. | The avaricious landlord raised the rent every year.  这个贪婪的房东每年都提高租金。 |Synonyms: acquisitive, mercenary | Antonyms: selfless, philanthropic | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *avaricidus, from Latin avaritia (“avarice”) + -idus.  Ultimately from Latin avere ("to crave").
