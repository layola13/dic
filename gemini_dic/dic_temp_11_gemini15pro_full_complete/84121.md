half-mad:/ˌhɑːf ˈmæd/ (US), /ˌhɑːf ˈmæd/ (UK)| adj.| 半疯的；精神错乱的；狂热的| Partially insane; exhibiting eccentric or irrational behavior. Driven by excessive enthusiasm or passion.| He had a half-mad gleam in his eyes as he described his plan. 他描述他的计划时，眼中闪着疯狂的光芒。|  The fans were half-mad with excitement after their team won the championship.  球队赢得冠军后，球迷们欣喜若狂。|近义词：crazy, insane, deranged, fanatic, obsessed|反义词：sane, rational, lucid|词性:形容词
