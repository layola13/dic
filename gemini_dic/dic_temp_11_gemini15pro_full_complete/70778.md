fingal:/ˈfɪŋɡəl/| n. |芬戈尔洞;  (苏格兰或爱尔兰传说中的)巨人|A sea cave, especially a large one. Also, a giant in Irish and Scottish Gaelic folklore. |Example:  We visited Fingal's Cave on Staffa. 我们参观了斯塔法岛上的芬戈尔洞。|Example:  In legend, Fingal was a powerful giant.  在传说中，芬戈尔是一位强大的巨人。|近义词：cave, cavern, giant |反义词:  dwarf|noun
