laces:/ˈleɪsɪz/ /leɪsəz/
n.|鞋带，系带，花边，饰带|A string or cord used to tie or fasten shoes or other garments. Also, decorative edging made of fabric or thread.|She tied her laces tightly before the race. 她赛跑前把鞋带系紧了。|近义词：cords, strings, ribbons, trims|反义词: (For the "tie" meaning) buckles, zippers, Velcro|词性:noun


laces:/ˈleɪsɪz/ /leɪsəz/
v.|系带，扎紧，穿带子|To fasten or tie something with laces.|He laced his boots quickly. 他迅速系好靴子上的带子。|近义词：tie, fasten, secure|反义词:untie, loosen, unfasten|词性:verb
