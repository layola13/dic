exotheca:/ˈeksoʊθiːkə/
noun
外鞘，外壳 (指某些腹足类动物的壳口外部的延伸部分)
An exotheca is a shelf-like extension of the shell aperture in some gastropods.
The snail's exotheca provided additional protection for its soft body.  这只蜗牛的外壳延伸部分为它的柔软身体提供了额外的保护。
The paleontologist identified the fossil by its distinctive exotheca.  古生物学家通过其独特的外壳延伸部分识别了这块化石。
近义词: shell extension, outer lip
反义词: (无明确的反义词)
词性: n.

词源:
From Greek *exo- (outside) + *thēkē (case, box).
