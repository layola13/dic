impressionistic:/ɪmˈprɛʃənˈɪstɪk/| adj.|印象派的；给人印象的；印象主义的|Based on subjective reactions presented unsystematically.  Relating to or characteristic of Impressionism, an artistic style that seeks to capture a momentary feel, or impression, of the piece they are drawing.|The painting was impressionistic, capturing the feeling of a summer afternoon. 这幅画是印象派的，捕捉了夏日午后的感觉。|近义词：suggestive, evocative, atmospheric|反义词：realistic, detailed, precise|词性:形容词
