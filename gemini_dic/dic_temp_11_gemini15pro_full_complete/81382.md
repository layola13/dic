grasper:/ˈɡræspər/ (US), /ˈɡrɑːspər/ (UK)| n.| 抓取器；掌握者；理解者 |A tool or device used for grasping or gripping something.  Someone who grasps or understands something quickly. |The surgeon used a grasper to retrieve the small object.  外科医生用抓取器取回小物件。 He's a quick grasper of complex ideas. 他能很快理解复杂的概念。 |近义词：gripper, holder, catcher, grabber, comprehender|反义词：releaser|noun
