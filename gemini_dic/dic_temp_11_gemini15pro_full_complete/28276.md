captivator:/ˈkæptɪveɪtər/| n. |迷住人的人；令人着迷的事物|Someone or something that captivates; a charmer.|Her beauty and charm made her a captivator of hearts. 她的美貌和魅力使她成为迷倒众生的人。|近义词：charmer, enchanter, fascinator, mesmerizer|反义词：bore, repellent|noun

captivating:/ˈkæptɪveɪtɪŋ/| adj. |迷人的；有魅力的|Attracting and holding the interest and attention of.|The captivating performance left the audience speechless.  引人入胜的表演让观众哑口无言。|近义词：enthralling, fascinating, mesmerizing|反义词：boring, dull, tedious, uninteresting|adjective
