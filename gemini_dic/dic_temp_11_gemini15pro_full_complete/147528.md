overpassing:/ˌoʊvərˈpæːsɪŋ/ | n. | 超车，高架桥，立交桥 | The act of passing over something, especially a vehicle overtaking another.  Also, a structure like a bridge that carries one road or railroad over another. |  The overpassing maneuver was executed perfectly. 超车动作执行得很完美。 The overpassing bridge provided a safe way to cross the highway. 立交桥提供了一种安全的过马路的方式。 | 近义词: overtaking, flyover, bridge | 反义词: underpassing | 词性: 名词

overpassing:/ˌoʊvərˈpæːsɪŋ/ | adj. | 超越的，经过的 |  Going over or surpassing something. | The overpassing clouds obscured the sun.  飘过的云遮住了太阳。| 近义词: surpassing, exceeding | 反义词:  falling short of | 词性: 形容词


词源: From the verb "overpass," which combines "over" (meaning "above" or "across") and "pass" (meaning "to go").
