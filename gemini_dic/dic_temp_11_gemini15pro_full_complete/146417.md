overfondly: /ˌoʊvərˈfɑːndli/ | adv. | 过于喜爱地，溺爱地 | Showing excessive fondness or affection. | She looked at him overfondly, making him uncomfortable. 她过于喜爱地看着他，让他感到不舒服。|  He patted the dog overfondly. 他溺爱地拍了拍那只狗。 | 近义词: excessively fondly, dotingly | 反义词: coldly, indifferently | 词性: 副词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "fondly" (adverb from "fond," meaning "having an affection or liking for").  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "fondly."
