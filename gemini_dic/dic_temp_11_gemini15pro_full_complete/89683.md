hooray:/hʊˈreɪ/ /hʊˈreɪ/ | int. n. | 好哇！万岁！欢呼声 | An exclamation of joy or approval. | Example: Hooray! We won the game! 好哇！我们赢了比赛！| Example:  The crowd erupted in hoorays.  人群爆发出欢呼声。 | 近义词: cheers, yahoo, hurrah | 反义词: boo, hiss | 词性: interjection, noun | 词源: From the phrase "huzzah," a sailor's cheer, possibly influenced by Dutch "hoera."
