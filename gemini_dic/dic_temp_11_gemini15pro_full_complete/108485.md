layabout:/ˈleɪəbaʊt/| n.|懒汉，游荡者|A person who habitually avoids work or exertion; an idler.|He's a layabout who never does a day's work. 他是个懒汉，从不工作。|Synonyms: loafer, idler, slacker, good-for-nothing, ne'er-do-well|Antonyms: hard worker, workaholic, diligent person|noun
