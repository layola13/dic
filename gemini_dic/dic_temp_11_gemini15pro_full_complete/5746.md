alzando:/ælˈtsɑːndoʊ/ /ælˈzændoʊ/
| adj. | 提升的，举起的 | Raised; lifted.  Describes a note or passage played with increasing volume and intensity.  Less common usage than the adverb form. |  The alzando notes added drama to the piece.  提升的音符为乐曲增添了戏剧性。 | 近义词: crescendoing, swelling | 反义词: diminuendoing, fading | 形容词
