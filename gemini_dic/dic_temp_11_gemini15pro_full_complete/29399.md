catacaustic:/ˌkætəˈkɔːstɪk/
noun
焦散线；焦散面
A curve or surface formed by the focusing of light rays or other radiation reflected or refracted by another curve or surface.
The catacaustic curve created by the sunlight reflecting off the rippling water was mesmerizing.
阳光照射在波光粼粼的水面上形成的焦散线令人着迷。
Synonyms: caustic curve, caustic surface
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, consider diffuse reflection or scattering)
