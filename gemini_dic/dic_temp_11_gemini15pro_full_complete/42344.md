counteractive:/ˌkaʊntərˈæktɪv/
adjective
抵消的，对抗性的
Acting against something so as to cancel or reduce its effect.
The government has taken counteractive measures to combat inflation. 政府已采取对抗性措施来应对通货膨胀。
The medicine had a counteractive effect on the poison.  这种药物对毒药有抵消作用。

近义词:neutralizing, remedial, corrective, antidotal
反义词:aggravating, exacerbating
词性:adjective
