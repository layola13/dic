interpervasivenesses:/ˌɪntərˌpɜːrˈveɪsɪvnəsɪz/
|n.|互相渗透，普遍性|The state or quality of being interpervasive; the extent to which something is interpervasive.  This concept highlights the interconnectedness and mutual influence of different elements within a system or across systems.  It suggests a deep and thorough integration, where influences are reciprocal and widespread.|The interpervasivenesses of digital technologies in modern life are undeniable.  数字技术在现代生活中的相互渗透是不可否认的。|The interpervasivenesses of global trade have impacted cultures worldwide. 全球贸易的相互渗透影响了世界各地的文化。 |pervasiveness, diffusion, prevalence|localization, isolation|noun
