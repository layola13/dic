avoiding:/əˈvɔɪdɪŋ/
verb (present participle)|避免，回避|The act of keeping away from something or someone. |He is avoiding eye contact. 他正在避免眼神接触。|dodging, eluding, evading, shunning|confronting, encountering|v.
Etymology: From Middle English *avoiden*, from Old French *esvuidier* "to empty out, make void," from *es-* (out) + *vuidier* "to empty."
