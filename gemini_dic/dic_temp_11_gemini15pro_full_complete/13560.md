attend-upon:/əˈtɛnd əˈpɒn/ | phrasal verb | 伺候；照料；护理 | To provide service to someone, especially someone important or of high social status. | The servants attended upon the king.  仆人们伺候国王。| 近义词: wait on, serve, minister to | 反义词: neglect, ignore | verb

attend-upon:/əˈtɛnd əˈpɒn/ | phrasal verb | 处理；关注 |  To deal with something or give attention to it. | I will attend upon this matter as soon as possible. 我会尽快处理这件事。 | 近义词: address, deal with, handle | 反义词: disregard, postpone | verb
