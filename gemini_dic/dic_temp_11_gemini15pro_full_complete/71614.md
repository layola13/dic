flatbread:/ˈflætˌbred/ | n. | 薄饼，扁平面包 | A thin, flat, often unleavened bread. | We had some delicious flatbread with hummus. 我们吃了一些美味的鹰嘴豆泥配薄饼。| synonyms: pita, naan, tortilla, lavash | antonyms: loaf (a general antonym for a shape) | noun

Etymology: From *flat* (adjective) + *bread* (noun).  The word "flat" comes from Old English *flæt*, meaning "level, horizontal." "Bread" comes from Old English *bread*.
