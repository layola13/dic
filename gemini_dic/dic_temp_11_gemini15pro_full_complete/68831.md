fascism:/ˈfæʃɪzəm/
n.
法西斯主义
A political system headed by a dictator that calls for extreme nationalism and racism and no tolerance of opposition.
Mussolini's Italy was the first example of a fascist state.  墨索里尼统治下的意大利是法西斯国家的第一个例子。

近义词:authoritarianism, totalitarianism, dictatorship
反义词:democracy, liberalism, socialism
词性:noun


Etymology:
From Italian *fascismo*, from *fascio* (“bundle, fasces”), ultimately from Latin *fascis* (“bundle of rods”).  The fasces were a Roman symbol of authority, consisting of a bundle of rods tied together around an axe, and were adopted by Mussolini's political movement.
