ammonites: /ˈæməˌnaɪts/ | n. | 菊石 | An extinct group of cephalopod molluscs with a coiled, chambered shell, related to the modern nautilus. |  We found some ammonites fossils on the beach. 我们在海滩上发现了一些菊石化石。 |  fossils, cephalopods |  N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *Ammonis cornū* "horn of Ammon", because their coiled shells resembled the ram's horns of the Egyptian god Ammon.
