olden:/ˈoʊldən/| adj.|古老的，旧时的|Of or belonging to a remote period in the past; ancient.|The knights of olden times were brave and noble. 旧时的骑士勇敢而高贵。|近义词：ancient, old, bygone, former|反义词：modern, new, contemporary|词性:形容词

词源: From Old English *ealdan (unattested) meaning "to grow old," from Proto-Germanic *aldan, from Proto-Indo-European *al- "to grow, nourish." The "-en" suffix transforms the verb (or a related noun "old") into an adjective.
