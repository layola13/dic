colloquist:/ˈkɒləkwɪst/ (US) /ˈkɒləkwɪst/ (UK)| n.|腹语表演者；和动物说话的人|A person who performs ventriloquism, the art of speaking without moving one's lips, often making it appear that a puppet or dummy is speaking.|The colloquist made the dummy appear to sing and dance. 腹语表演者让木偶看起来像在唱歌跳舞。|Synonyms: ventriloquist|Antonyms: None|noun

colloquial:/kəˈloʊkwiəl/ (US) /kəˈləʊkwiəl/ (UK)| adj.|口语的，通俗的|Used in ordinary or familiar conversation; not formal or literary.|Her colloquial language made her speech relatable to the audience. 她通俗的语言使她的演讲更容易引起观众的共鸣。|"It's raining cats and dogs" is a colloquial expression. “倾盆大雨”是一个口语表达。|Synonyms: informal, conversational, chatty|Antonyms: formal, literary|adjective
