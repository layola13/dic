inerrably:/ɪˈnɛrəbli/
adverb
必然地，无可避免地
in a way that cannot be avoided or altered.
The events of that day led inerrably to war. 那一天的事件不可避免地导致了战争。
Synonyms: inevitably, unavoidably, necessarily
Antonyms: avoidably, preventably
词性: 副词
