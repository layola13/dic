outreason:/ˌaʊtˈriːzn/
verb
胜过；以理服人
To surpass in reasoning; to overcome by argument.
He outreasons his opponents with his logic. 他用逻辑胜过了对手。
Synonyms: outargue, defeat, conquer
Antonyms: lose to, be defeated by
词性: 动词
