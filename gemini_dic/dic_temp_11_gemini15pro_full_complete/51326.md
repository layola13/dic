detasselling:/diːˈtæsəlɪŋ/
n.| 去雄; 除雄;|The process of removing the tassel from a corn plant.  This prevents self-pollination and allows for controlled cross-pollination.|Detasselling is a common summer job for teenagers in the Corn Belt. 在玉米带，去雄是青少年常见的暑期工作。|Synonyms:  tassel removal, emasculation|Antonyms: pollination|noun
