magisteriality:/ˌmædʒɪˈstɪəriˈælɪti/| n. |权威性，威严，专横 |The quality of being magisterial; authoritative, dignified, and commanding. |  His magisteriality was evident in the way he addressed the crowd. 他的威严在他对人群讲话的方式中显而易见。|  authority, dignity, command, loftiness, grandeur | subservience, humility, meekness | noun

magisterial:/ˌmædʒɪˈstɪəriəl/| adj. |权威的，威严的，专横的 |Having or showing great authority; befitting a master; domineering. |He delivered a magisterial performance. 他进行了权威的表演。|authoritative, commanding, dignified, imperious, lordly |humble, meek, modest, unassuming | adjective
