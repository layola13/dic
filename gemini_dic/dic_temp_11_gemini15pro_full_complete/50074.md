dentalium:/dɛnˈteɪliəm/ | n. | 象牙贝 | The tooth shell or tusk shell, a marine mollusk of the class Scaphopoda. Its shell is tubular, open at both ends, and slightly curved, resembling a small elephant tusk. |  Archaeologists found dentalium shells used as beads in the ancient burial site. 考古学家在古代墓地发现了用作珠子的象牙贝壳。| Synonyms: tooth shell, tusk shell | Antonyms: None | Noun
