locality:/loʊˈkælɪti/ /ləʊˈkælɪti/| n.|地点，位置；地区， locality|The police are investigating the locality of the crime. 警方正在调查犯罪地点。|近义词：area, region, vicinity, neighborhood, district, place, spot, location|反义词:ubiquity, universality|noun

locality:/loʊˈkælɪti/ /ləʊˈkælɪti/| n.|局部性；地方性|The locality of the custom makes it unique.  这种习俗的地方性使它独一无二。|近义词：provincialism|反义词:globalism, universality|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *localitas, from Latin localis (“local”), from locus (“place”).
