leak:/liːk/| n. v.|泄漏，漏洞|An act or instance of leaking; an escape of something through a hole or crack.  The release of secret or private information.|The pipe has a leak. 管道漏水。  He leaked the story to the press. 他把这个故事泄露给了媒体。|近义词：drip, escape, disclosure, spill|反义词: seal, closure, concealment|词性:名词, 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *leka ("to leak, drip"), from Proto-Germanic *lekaną ("to drip, leak, run").

leak out: 泄露出去  The secret leaked out despite their best efforts. 尽管他们尽了最大努力，秘密还是泄露了出去。

plug a leak:  堵住漏洞  They tried to plug the leak with a rag. 他们试图用破布堵住漏洞。
