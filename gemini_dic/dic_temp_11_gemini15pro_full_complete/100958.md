n.
习惯,适应;有效;生效
the act or process of inuring; the state of being inured
例句：The inurement to violence in video games desensitizes players to real-world violence.  电子游戏中的暴力习惯使玩家对现实世界的暴力麻木。

例句：His inurement to criticism made him a strong leader.他对批评的适应使他成为了一位强有力的领导者。
近义词：habituation, acclimation, adaptation
反义词：sensitivity, vulnerability
词性：名词
