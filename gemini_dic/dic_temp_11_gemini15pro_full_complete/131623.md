noncomposites:/ˌnɒnˈkɒmpəzɪts/ | n. |非合数；质数| Numbers that are not composites are called primes or noncomposites.  非合数被称为质数或非合数。| Seven is a noncomposite number. 七是一个非合数。| 近义词: primes | 反义词: composites | 词性: noun

noncomposite:/ˌnɒnˈkɒmpəzɪt/ | adj. |非复合的；非合成的|A noncomposite material is made of a single substance. 一种非复合材料是由单一物质制成的。 | This structure is made of a noncomposite material. 这个结构是由一种非复合材料制成的。| 近义词: simple, basic | 反义词: composite, compound | 词性: adjective
