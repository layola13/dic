inertnesses:/ɪˈnɜːrtnɪsɪz/
n.
惰性；迟钝；无活动
lack of ability or willingness to move or act.
The inertnesses of the noble gases make them useful in certain applications. 惰性气体的惰性使它们在某些应用中很有用。

The chemical inertnesses of the material make it suitable for food packaging. 这种材料的化学惰性使其适用于食品包装。
近义词: inactivity, passivity, sluggishness, inertia
反义词: activity, energy, liveliness

词性: 名词 (plural of inertness)

词源:
From inert + -ness.  Inert comes from the Latin word "iners," meaning "inactive" or "idle."  The suffix "-ness" is used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
