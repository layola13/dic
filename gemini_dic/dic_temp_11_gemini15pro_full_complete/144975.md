overaccentuating: /ˌoʊvərækˈsɛntʃueɪtɪŋ/
verb
过分强调；过分突出
placing too much emphasis or importance on something.
She tends to overaccentuate the negative aspects of any situation. 她倾向于过分强调任何情况的消极方面。
The media overaccentuated the violence at the protest. 媒体过分强调了抗议活动中的暴力行为。
overemphasizing, overstressing
understating, downplaying
v.

词源：
From over- + accentuate, from accent + -uate.  "Over-"  means "excessively" or "too much". "Accentuate" means to make something more noticeable or prominent.  Therefore, "overaccentuating" means emphasizing something to an excessive degree.
