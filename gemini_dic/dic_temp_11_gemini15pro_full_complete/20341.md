bismarckian:/bɪzˈmɑːrkiən/
adjective
俾斯麦的；铁血宰相式的；强权政治的
Relating to Otto von Bismarck, the first Chancellor of Germany, or his policies, especially his use of Realpolitik and strong centralized government.
Bismarckian policies often involved manipulation and the use of force. 俾斯麦的政策通常涉及操纵和使用武力。
His bismarckian approach to diplomacy alienated many of his allies. 他俾斯麦式的外交手段疏远了他的许多盟友。
Synonyms: authoritarian, autocratic, dictatorial, despotic, tyrannical
Antonyms: democratic, liberal, egalitarian
词性: adjective
