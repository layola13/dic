counterfort:/ˈkaʊntərfɔːrt/ (US), /ˈkaʊntəfɔːt/ (UK)| n.|支撑结构，扶壁；支持，防御工事|A structure built against a wall to strengthen it, especially one at right angles projecting from it; something that gives support or strength in a difficult situation.|例句：The cathedral walls are supported by massive counterforts.  大教堂的墙壁由巨大的扶壁支撑。

例句：Her family was her counterfort during the difficult time.  在她困难时期，她的家人是她的支柱。


近义词：buttress, support, reinforcement, bulwark|反义词:weakness, vulnerability|词性:noun
