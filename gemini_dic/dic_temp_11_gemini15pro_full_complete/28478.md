carcavelhos:/kɑːrkəˈvɛljuʃ/ |n. (plural)|棺材，灵柩|(Portuguese) Coffins; sarcophagi.  A term primarily used in Portuguese, referring to coffins or, more archaically, sarcophagi.  It emphasizes the age and potentially decayed state of the coffin.|The old church crypt was filled with crumbling carcavelhos.|近义词:coffins, sarcophagi, caskets|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


Etymology: From Portuguese *carcavelho* (singular), ultimately from Latin *carcass-*. Related to "carcass".
