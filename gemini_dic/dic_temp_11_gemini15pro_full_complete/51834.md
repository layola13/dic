dewless:/ˈdjuːləs/| adj.|无露水的，干燥的|Without dew; dry.  Derived from "dew" (moisture condensed from the atmosphere) + "-less" (suffix indicating absence).|The dewless morning suggested a hot day ahead.  无露水的早晨预示着一天的炎热。|Example: The desert landscape was dewless and barren.  沙漠景观干燥而荒芜。|Synonyms: arid, dry, parched|Antonyms: dewy, moist, damp|adjective
