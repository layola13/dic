inflammabilities:/ɪnˌflæməˈbɪlətiz/| n. |易燃性；可燃性|The quality of being easily set on fire.  This refers to how easily something catches fire and how intensely it burns. |The inflammabilities of different materials are crucial considerations in fire safety regulations. 不同材料的易燃性是消防安全法规中的关键考虑因素。| flammability, combustibility |incombustibility, fire resistance|noun
