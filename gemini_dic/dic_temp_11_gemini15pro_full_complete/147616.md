overplenteousness:/ˌoʊvərˈplɛntɪəsnəs/
n.|过度丰富，过剩|The overplenteousness of food at the buffet was overwhelming.  自助餐上食物的过度丰富令人不知所措。|
Etymology: From over + plenteous + -ness.  "Over" indicates excess, "plenteous" means abundant, and "-ness" forms a noun indicating a state or quality.  由 over + plenteous + -ness 组成。“Over”表示过度，“plenteous”表示丰富，“-ness”构成表示状态或性质的名词。
Example: The farmer rejoiced at the overplenteousness of his harvest this year.  农民为今年丰收的景象感到欣喜。
Synonyms: abundance, superfluity, plethora, profusion, excess
Antonyms: scarcity, lack, dearth, deficiency, paucity
