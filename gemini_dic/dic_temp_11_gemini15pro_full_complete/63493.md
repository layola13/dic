entwined:/ɪnˈtwaɪnd/ | adj. | 缠绕的，交织的 | Closely twisted or interwoven together. |  The lovers sat with their fingers entwined. 恋人们手指交缠地坐着。| synonyms: intertwined, interwoven, interlaced, tangled, knotted | antonyms: separated, disentangled, unraveled | adjective

entwine:/ɪnˈtwaɪn/ | v. |  缠绕，交织| To twist or twine together. | The ivy entwined itself around the oak tree. 常春藤缠绕在橡树上。 | synonyms: interweave, interlace, braid, plait | antonyms: untwine, disentangle, unravel | verb
