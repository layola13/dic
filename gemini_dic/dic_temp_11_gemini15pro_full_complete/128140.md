netizen: /ˈnetɪzən/ (US), /ˈnetɪzn/ (UK)| n. |网民|A user of the internet, especially a habitual or avid one.|例句：Netizens quickly shared the news on social media. 网民们很快在社交媒体上分享了这个消息。

例句：Many netizens expressed their opinions in the online forum. 许多网民在网上论坛表达了他们的意见。

近义词：internet user, online user, cybernaut|反义词: None (no direct opposite)|词性: Noun
