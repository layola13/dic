nylgau:/ˈnɪlɡaʊ/ | n. | 蓝牛，印度羚 | A large, dark-gray antelope (Boselaphus tragocamelus) of India. It has short horns, a tufted mane, and a humped back. |  例句：The nylgau grazed peacefully in the meadow.  蓝牛在草地上平静地吃草。

例句：We saw a herd of nylgau while on safari.  我们在旅行途中看到了一群蓝牛。 | 近义词：blue bull, nilgai | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Hindi नीलगाय (nīlgāy), from नील (nīl, “blue”) + गाय (gāy, “cow”).
