farer:/ˈfɛərər/ | n. | 旅行者；航海者；远行者 | A person who travels, especially one who travels a long distance or habitually. |  Example: He was a seasoned farer, having journeyed across many lands. 他是一位经验丰富的旅行者，曾游历过许多地方。

Example:  The ancient mariners were skilled farers, navigating by the stars. 古代水手是熟练的航海者，靠星星导航。 | 近义词: traveler, voyager, journeyer, adventurer | 反义词: stay-at-home, resident | 词性: 名词
