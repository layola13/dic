crabeater:/ˈkræbiːtər/| n.|食蟹动物；食蟹海豹|An animal that eats crabs; specifically, the crabeater seal (Lobodon carcinophaga), which, despite its name, primarily feeds on krill.|The crabeater seal is surprisingly agile in the water. 食蟹海豹在水中出奇地敏捷。| The documentary showed a crabeater seal filtering krill from the water.  这部纪录片展示了一只食蟹海豹从水中过滤磷虾。|synonyms:  seal, predator (in a broad sense)|antonyms: prey (in a broad sense)|noun
