nonlimiting:/ˌnɒnˈlɪmɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非限制性的
Not limiting or intended to limit; illustrative and not exhaustive.
This list is nonlimiting, meaning there may be other options available.  这个清单是非限制性的，意味着可能还有其他选项可用。
The examples provided are nonlimiting and are intended to illustrate the invention. 所提供的例子是非限制性的，旨在说明本发明。
近义词: inclusive, illustrative, non-exhaustive
反义词: limiting, restrictive, exhaustive
词性: adjective
