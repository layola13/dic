kloof:/kluːf/
n.|峡谷，深谷|A deep ravine or gorge, typically one with steep sides.|We hiked through the kloof, marveling at the towering rock formations. 我们徒步穿越峡谷，惊叹于高耸的岩层。|近义词：ravine, gorge, canyon, chasm|反义词:peak, summit|名词
词源: From Afrikaans kloof, from Middle Dutch clove, clift, related to cleave.
