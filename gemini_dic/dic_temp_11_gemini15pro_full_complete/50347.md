depolarize:/ˌdiːˈpoʊləraɪz/ /ˌdeɪˈpəʊləraɪz/ | v. | 去极化，使去极化 | To reduce or eliminate the difference in electrical potential across a cell membrane, especially of a nerve or muscle cell, making it less negative inside. | The drug depolarizes the cell membrane.  这种药物使细胞膜去极化。

The electrical stimulation depolarized the neuron. 电刺激使神经元去极化。 | 近义词: neutralize, counteract | 反义词: polarize, hyperpolarize | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- + polarize, from polar + -ize.  "Polarize" comes from French polariser, from pole (referring to the poles of a magnet or battery).
