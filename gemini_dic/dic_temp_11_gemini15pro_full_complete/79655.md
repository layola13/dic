glossolalia:/ˌɡlɒsəˈleɪliə/ (US) /ˌɡlɒsəˈleɪliə/ (UK)| n. |  方言|Speaking in tongues, especially in religious worship; unintelligible speech that resembles speaking in an unknown language.|She experienced glossolalia during the religious service. 她(他)在宗教仪式中经历了方言(说方言)。|The preacher's glossolalia amazed the congregation.  牧师的方言令会众惊叹。|Synonyms: speaking in tongues, xenoglossia|Antonyms: articulate speech, clear speech|noun
