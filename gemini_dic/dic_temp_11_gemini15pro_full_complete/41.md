a-team:/ˈeɪ tiːm/| n.|A队；精英团队|A group of highly skilled individuals assembled for a particular purpose, often implying exceptional ability and effectiveness.  Originally referring to the television series "The A-Team".|The company assembled an a-team of lawyers to handle the merger. 公司组建了一支精英律师团队来处理并购事宜。| The project requires an a-team to ensure its success.  这个项目需要一个精英团队来确保其成功。|synonyms: elite team, crack team, top team, dream team|antonyms: b-team, mediocre team, incompetent team|noun
