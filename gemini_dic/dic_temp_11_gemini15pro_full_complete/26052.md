bushido:/ˌbuːˈʃiːdoʊ/ /ˌbʊˈʃiːdoʊ/
noun
武士道
The code of honour and morals developed by the Japanese samurai.
例句：Bushido emphasized loyalty, martial arts mastery, and honor unto death. 武士道强调忠诚、武艺精湛和视死如归。

例句：The samurai adhered strictly to the tenets of bushido. 武士们严格遵守武士道的信条。

近义词：code, chivalry, ethos
反义词：dishonor, cowardice

词性: n.
