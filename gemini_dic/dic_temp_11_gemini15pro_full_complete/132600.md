nondiligently:/ˌnɒnˈdɪlɪdʒəntli/ | adv. | 不勤奋地，不努力地 | Not diligently; in a way that lacks care, effort, or attention to detail.  | He completed the task nondiligently, resulting in several errors. 他不勤奋地完成了任务，导致了几个错误。 |  He nondiligently tossed his clothes on the floor. 他漫不经心地把衣服扔在地板上。 | Synonyms: carelessly, lazily, negligently, perfunctorily | Antonyms: diligently, carefully, assiduously, meticulously | adverb

Etymology:
* non- :  a prefix of Latin origin meaning "not," freely used as an English formative, especially of adjectives and adverbs.
* diligently:  from Late Latin *diligent- , from Latin diligens "attentive, careful, painstaking," present participle of diligere "to value highly, cherish, love; single out, select," from dis- "apart" (see dis-) + legere "to choose, gather, read" (see lecture (n.)).
