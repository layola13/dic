interchained:/ˌɪntərˈtʃeɪnd/
adjective
相互连接的，互相联系的
linked together; connected by a chain, either literally or figuratively
The climbers were interchained for safety as they ascended the mountain. 登山者在爬山时为了安全起见互相连接在一起。
interconnected, intertwined, linked
separate, detached, independent
词性: 形容词
