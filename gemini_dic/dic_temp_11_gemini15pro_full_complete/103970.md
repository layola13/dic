jugal:/ˈdʒuːɡəl/| adj. n.|颧骨的，轭的，一对的；轭骨|Relating to the cheek or zygomatic bone.  A jugal bone forms part of the cheek or zygoma.|The bird has a prominent jugal stripe. 这只鸟有明显的颧骨条纹。|The jugal bones are important for the structure of the skull. 轭骨对头骨的结构很重要。|synonyms: zygomatic, cheek|antonyms: N/A|adjective, noun
