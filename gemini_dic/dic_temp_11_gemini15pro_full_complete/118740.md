metalanguage:/ˈmetəlˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ/ /ˈmetlˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ/ | n. |元语言|A language used to describe or talk about another language (the object language).  A metalanguage may be identical to the object language, or it may be different. |Linguists use metalanguage to describe the grammar of a language. 语言学家使用元语言来描述一种语言的语法。| synonyms: meta-language| antonyms: object language | noun
