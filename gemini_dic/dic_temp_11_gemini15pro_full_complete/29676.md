cathedrallike:/kəˈθiːdrəlˌlaɪk/
adjective
像大教堂一样的
Resembling or suggestive of a cathedral, especially in grandeur, vastness, or loftiness.
The cavern had a cathedrallike ceiling, inspiring awe in all who entered. 洞穴的天花板像大教堂一样，让所有进入的人都感到敬畏。
The trees in the redwood forest formed a cathedrallike canopy. 红杉林中的树木形成了一个像大教堂一样的树冠。
Synonyms: majestic, imposing, grand, awe-inspiring, monumental
Antonyms: small, cramped, humble, modest, insignificant
词性: 形容词
