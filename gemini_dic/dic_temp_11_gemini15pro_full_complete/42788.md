couple:/ˈkʌpəl/ (US) /ˈkʌp(ə)l/ (UK)| n. v. |一对；几个；连接|A pair of people or things; a small number of things; to join or connect|The couple celebrated their 50th anniversary. 这对夫妇庆祝了他们结婚50周年。|Synonyms: pair, duo, few, several|Antonyms: many, numerous, single|noun

couple:/ˈkʌpəl/ (US) /ˈkʌp(ə)l/ (UK)| v. |连接；结合|To join or connect two or more things together|They coupled the train cars together. 他们把火车车厢连接在一起。|Synonyms: join, connect, link, attach|Antonyms: separate, disconnect, detach|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *cople, from Latin copula ("a link, tie, band"), related to copulare ("to join, connect").
