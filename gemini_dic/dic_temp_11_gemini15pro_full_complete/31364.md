charart:/ˈtʃɑːrɑːrt/| n.|图表；字符艺术|A visual representation of data, often using symbols or characters to create an image.  A form of art using characters to create pictures.|例句：The charart clearly showed the sales trends over the past year.  图表清楚地显示了过去一年的销售趋势。

例句：He created a beautiful charart of a dragon using keyboard symbols. 他用键盘符号创作了一幅美丽的龙的字符艺术。|近义词：chart, graph, diagram, ASCII art|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
