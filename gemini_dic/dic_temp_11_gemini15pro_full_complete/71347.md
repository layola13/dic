flaccidity:/flækˈsɪdəti/ /fləˈsɪdəti/
n.|松弛，无力|The flaccidity of the muscles was a symptom of the disease. 肌肉的松弛是这种疾病的症状。|
例句：The flaccidity of the sails in the still air was unsettling.  无风的空气中，帆的松弛令人不安。

例句：The doctor noted the flaccidity of the patient's limbs. 医生注意到病人四肢的无力。

近义词：limpness, looseness, laxity
反义词：firmness, tightness, rigidity
词性：名词


flaccid:/ˈflæksɪd/ /ˈflæsɪd/
adj.|松弛的，无力的|The flowers were flaccid from lack of water. 由于缺水，花朵都蔫了。|
例句：The flaccid balloon lay deflated on the floor. 松弛的气球瘪瘪地躺在地板上。

例句：His handshake was surprisingly flaccid. 他的握手出奇地无力。

近义词：limp, drooping, soft
反义词：firm, taut, rigid
词性：形容词
