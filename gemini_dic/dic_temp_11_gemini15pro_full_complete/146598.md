overgrieve: /ˌoʊvərˈɡriːv/
verb
过度悲伤，过分悲痛
To grieve excessively or for too long a period.
She overgrieved the loss of her pet hamster, refusing to leave her room for days. 她因失去宠物仓鼠而过度悲伤，好几天不愿离开房间。
He overgrieved his father’s death for years.  他为父亲的去世悲痛了多年。
近义词: mourn excessively, grieve too much
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider) recover, heal
词性: verb
