orangemen:/ˈɔːrɪndʒmən/| n.|奥兰治人|A member of the Orange Order, a Protestant fraternal organization originating in Ireland.|例句：The Orangemen marched through the streets. 奥兰治人走过街道。

例句：Many Orangemen celebrate the Twelfth of July. 许多奥兰治人庆祝七月十二日。

近义词：Protestant, Unionist|反义词: Catholic, Nationalist|词性:noun
