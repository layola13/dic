nonrevivals:/ˌnɑːnriˈvaɪvəlz/
|n.| 未复兴的事物；未再次流行的事物 |Things that have not been revived or brought back into popularity.| The museum's exhibition on nonrevivals showcased items that never regained their former popularity.  博物馆关于未复兴事物的展览展示了那些从未恢复昔日流行度的物品。| 近义词:  obscurities, has-beens, relics | 反义词: revivals, comebacks, renaissances | 词性: 名词


nonrevivals:/ˌnɑːnriˈvaɪvəlz/
|n.| 未复兴的演出；未再次上演的剧目 |Performances or productions that have not been revived or restaged.|  The theater company decided against nonrevivals and opted for a fresh, new play. 剧团决定不采用未复兴的剧目，而是选择了一部全新的戏剧。 | 近义词: forgotten works, neglected pieces | 反义词: revivals, reprises | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
"non-" (prefix): from Latin "non" meaning "not"
"revival": from Late Middle English "revivalle," from Anglo-French and Old French "revivre" meaning "to return to life," from Latin "revivere" composed of "re-" (again) + "vivere" (to live)
