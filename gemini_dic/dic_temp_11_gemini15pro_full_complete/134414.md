noninvincibilities:/ˌnɒnɪnvɪnsɪˈbɪlɪtiz/
|n.| 不可战胜性；非无敌性 |The myth of their noninvincibility was shattered. |他们的不可战胜的神话被打破了。|
|近义词:|vulnerability, weakness, fallibility|
|反义词:|invincibility, invulnerability, impenetrability|
|词性:|noun (plural)|
|词源:|From non- + invincibility.  "Invincibility" comes from Late Middle English: from Old French invincible, from Latin invincibilis, from in- ‘not’ + vincibilis (from vincere ‘conquer’).|
