anti-macho:/ˈæntiˈmɑːtʃoʊ/| adj.|反男子气概的，反对大男子主义的|Opposed to or rejecting traditional masculine behavior and values, often seen as aggressive, dominant, or insensitive.|He has a very anti-macho attitude and always tries to share the housework equally.| 近义词:feminist, progressive, egalitarian| 反义词:macho, chauvinist, patriarchal|词性:形容词

anti-macho:/ˈæntiˈmɑːtʃoʊ/| n.|反大男子主义者|A person who opposes or rejects traditional masculine behavior and values.|He considers himself an anti-macho and actively challenges gender stereotypes.| 近义词:feminist, egalitarian| 反义词:macho, chauvinist|词性:名词
