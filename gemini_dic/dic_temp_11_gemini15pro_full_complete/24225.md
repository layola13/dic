bridges:/ˈbrɪdʒɪz/| n.|桥梁；桥牌；纽带；桥接器|A structure carrying a road, path, railway, etc. across a river, road, or other obstacle.  Also, a card game; something that connects two things; a device connecting two networks.|The old stone bridge spanned the river.  古老的石桥横跨河流。|近义词：overpass, viaduct, link, connection|反义词:division, separation|noun
