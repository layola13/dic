词源：
The origin of "blob" is uncertain, but it's thought to be imitative, perhaps echoing the sound of something soft and sticky dropping.  "Blob"的起源不确定，但它被认为是拟声词，可能模仿了某种柔软粘稠的东西掉落的声音。
