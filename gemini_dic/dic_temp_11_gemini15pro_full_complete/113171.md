lurcher:/ˈlɜːrtʃər/ (UK) /ˈlɜːrtʃər/ (US)|n.|杂种猎犬，流浪狗|A dog, especially a sighthound, resulting from crossing a purebred dog with a Greyhound, often a crossbred dog used for hunting rabbits and other small game.|例句：The lurcher stealthily pursued the rabbit through the field. 那只杂种猎犬悄悄地穿过田野追逐兔子。

例句：My neighbour's lurcher is a mix of Greyhound and Collie. 我邻居的杂种猎犬是灰狗和牧羊犬的混种。|近义词：mongrel, crossbreed, mutt|反义词:pedigree, purebred|词性:noun
