conveyer:/kənˈveɪər/ | n. | 输送机；传送带；运送者 |A machine that moves objects from one place to another, often using a belt or chain.  A person or thing that carries or transmits something.| The factory uses a conveyer belt to move products along the assembly line. 工厂使用传送带沿着装配线移动产品。| synonyms: transporter, carrier, conveyor belt | antonyms: receiver, collector | noun

convey:/kənˈveɪ/ | vt. | 传达；表达；运送 | To communicate a message, feeling, or idea. To transport or carry something from one place to another. |  His music conveys a sense of peace. 他的音乐传达了一种平静的感觉。 The train conveyed passengers from London to Paris. 火车将乘客从伦敦运送到巴黎。 | synonyms: communicate, transmit, transport | antonyms: withhold, suppress, receive | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English conveien,  from Anglo-French conveier, convoyer, from Vulgar Latin *conviare, from Latin com- + via “way”.
