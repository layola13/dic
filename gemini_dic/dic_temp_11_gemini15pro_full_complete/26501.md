caballers:/ˌkæbəˈlɪərz/ | n. | 骑士; 绅士;  (尤指)  骑马的斗牛士 |  A gallant or chivalrous man, especially one serving on horseback. Originally, a horseman.  Derived from Spanish "caballero" (horseman, knight). |  The caballers rode their horses with skill and grace.  骑士们骑着马，技艺娴熟，风度翩翩。 |  The caballers tipped their hats to the ladies.  骑士们向女士们脱帽致敬。 | Synonyms: knights, gentlemen, horsemen | Antonyms: villains, boors | noun
