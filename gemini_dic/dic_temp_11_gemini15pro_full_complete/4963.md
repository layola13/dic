alkalescent:/ˌælkəˈlɛsənt/
adjective
微碱性的; 趋于碱性的
Slightly alkaline; becoming alkaline.
The soil in this area is alkalescent, which is good for certain types of plants.  这个地区的土壤呈弱碱性，适合某些类型的植物生长。
The solution turned alkalescent after the addition of the base. 添加碱后溶液变成弱碱性。
近义词: alkaline, basic
反义词: acidic
词性: adjective

词源: From alkali + -escent (suffix forming adjectives meaning "becoming" or "beginning to be").  来自 alkali（碱）+ -escent（后缀，表示“正在变成”或“开始变得”）。
