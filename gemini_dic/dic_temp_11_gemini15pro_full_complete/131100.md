nonbibulousness:/ˌnɒnbɪˈbjʊləsnəs/
noun
不饮酒；戒酒；滴酒不沾
The state of not drinking alcohol; abstinence from alcoholic beverages.
His nonbibulousness made him an outlier in the fraternity. 他不饮酒的习惯让他在兄弟会中显得格格不入。
近义词:abstinence, sobriety, temperance
反义词:alcoholism, drunkenness, inebriation
词性:n.

Etymology:
From non- (not) + bibulous (given to drinking) + -ness (state of being).  Bibulous comes from Latin bibulus "drinking, fond of drink," from bibere "to drink."
