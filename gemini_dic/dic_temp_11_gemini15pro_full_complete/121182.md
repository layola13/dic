misdistinguish:/ˌmɪsdɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃ/| vt.|误认，区别错误|To incorrectly identify or perceive something as different from what it actually is.|例句：I misdistinguished her voice on the phone. 我在电话里误认了她的声音。|近义词：misidentify, mistake, confuse|反义词:distinguish, recognize|词性:动词
Etymology: mis- (Old English) + distinguish (from Old French distinguer, from Latin distinguere)
