misperception:/ˌmɪspərˈsepʃn/ | n. |误解，错误的认识|A view or opinion that is incorrect because based on faulty thinking or understanding. |  Example 1: There's a common misperception that all vegans are unhealthy.  中文：有一种普遍的误解，认为所有素食主义者都不健康。

Example 2:  His misperception of her intentions led to a huge argument. 中文：他对她的意图的误解导致了一场激烈的争吵。 | 近义词：misunderstanding, misinterpretation, fallacy, delusion | 反义词：accurate perception, correct understanding | 词性: noun
