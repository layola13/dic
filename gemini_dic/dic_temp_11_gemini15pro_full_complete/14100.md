auteurists:/ˌɔːtəˈrɪsts/
n.
作者论者，导演论者
Film critics who believe in the auteur theory, which emphasizes the director as the primary creative force behind a film.
Many auteurists champion the work of directors like Alfred Hitchcock and Quentin Tarantino.
许多作者论者拥护像阿尔弗雷德·希区柯克和昆汀·塔伦蒂诺这样的导演的作品。
Synonyms:cinephiles, film buffs
Antonyms:N/A
词性:名词
