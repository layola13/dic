nonamputations:/ˌnɒnˌæmpjuˈteɪʃənz/
noun
非截肢，未截肢（手术）
The study focused on limb salvage techniques and successful nonamputations. 这项研究的重点是保肢技术和成功的非截肢手术。
nonamputations refers to instances where a limb is saved and amputation is avoided. 非截肢指的是保住肢体避免截肢的情况。
例句：
The surgeon specialized in nonamputations, prioritizing limb preservation.  这位外科医生专门从事非截肢手术，优先考虑保肢。

The advancements in microsurgery have led to an increase in successful nonamputations.显微外科的进步导致成功的非截肢手术有所增加。

近义词：limb salvage, limb preservation
反义词：amputations


词性: noun (名词)


词源:
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "amputation" (from Latin "amputare" - to cut around, prune)
