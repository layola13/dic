de-licensed:/diːˈlaɪsənst/
adjective
被取消执照的，被吊销执照的
deprived of a license, especially a professional license.
The de-licensed doctor could no longer legally practice medicine.  这位被吊销执照的医生不能再合法行医了。
The bar was de-licensed after repeated violations of health codes.  这家酒吧因反复违反卫生条例而被吊销了执照。
近义词: unlicensed, unauthorized, disqualified
反义词: licensed, authorized, certified
词性: adjective
词源: "de-" (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + "licensed" (past participle of "license")
