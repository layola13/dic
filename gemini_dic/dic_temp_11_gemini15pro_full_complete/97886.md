innavigable:/ɪnˈnævɪɡəbl/
adjective
不可通航的，无法航行的
Not navigable; incapable of being navigated or traversed by ships or boats.
The river is innavigable due to the low water level. 这条河由于水位低而无法通航。
The dense weeds made the stream innavigable. 茂密的杂草使溪流无法航行。
近义词: impassable, unnavigable, non-navigable
反义词: navigable, passable
词性: adjective

词源:
Late Middle English: from Old French innavegable, from in- ‘not’ + navigable.
