gladiatorial:/ˌɡlædiəˈtɔːriəl/ | adj. |  角斗士的；像角斗士的；适合角斗士的 | Relating to gladiators or their combats; resembling or characteristic of gladiators or their combats. | The gladiatorial combat was brutal and bloody. 角斗士的战斗残酷而血腥。 | The Colosseum was built for gladiatorial games.  罗马斗兽场是为角斗士比赛而建造的。 | 近义词: combative, martial | 反义词: peaceful, pacifistic | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *gladiātōrius*, from gladiātor (“gladiator”) + -ius (adjective suffix).
