agblo: /ˈæɡbloʊ/ | n. | 阿格博（西非加纳埃维语中的一种主食，由玉米和木薯制成）| Agblo is a staple food in Ewe cuisine of Ghana, West Africa, made from corn and cassava.  It is similar to fufu but has a slightly different texture. | Example: Agblo is often served with a variety of soups and stews. 阿格博通常与各种汤和炖菜一起食用。| Example:  She prepared a delicious agblo for dinner. 她为晚餐准备了美味的阿格博。 | 近义词: fufu (in terms of function and general category), kenkey (another Ghanaian staple) | 反义词: None (no direct opposite for a specific food item) | 词性: noun
