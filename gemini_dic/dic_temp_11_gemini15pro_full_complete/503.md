ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English abidyng, aboding, from Old English ābīding (“delay, abiding, remaining”), verbal noun from ābīdan (“abide, wait, remain, continue, endure, last”), from a- (“on, in, at, to”) + bīdan (“bide, wait, remain”).  Related to German abwarten (“wait out, outwait”) and Dutch afwachten (“wait out”).
