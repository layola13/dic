antimonic:/ˌæntɪˈmɒnɪk/ | adj. |锑的，五价锑的 | Relating to or containing antimony, especially in its +5 oxidation state. | Antimonic acid is a weak acid. 锑酸是一种弱酸。|Antimonic oxide is a yellowish powder. 五氧化二锑是一种淡黄色粉末。| 近义词: stibic | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but one might consider compounds of antimony in lower oxidation states, e.g., antimonous) |词性: adjective
