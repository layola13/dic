arbitrates:/ˈɑːrbɪtreɪts/| v.|仲裁，公断|acts as a judge in a dispute; mediates.|例句：The union arbitrates disputes between management and the workers. 工会仲裁管理层和工人之间的纠纷。

例句：An independent body arbitrates in the event of a disagreement. 如果发生分歧，将由一个独立机构进行仲裁。|近义词：mediates, judges, adjudicates, settles, resolves|反义词：disputes, quarrels, fights|词性:动词

词源: From Late Latin *arbitratus*, past participle of arbitrari (“to judge, examine, witness”), from arbiter (“judge, umpire”).
