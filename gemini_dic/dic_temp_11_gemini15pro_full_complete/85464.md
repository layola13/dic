haskalah:/hɑːsˈkɑːlɑː/| n.|启蒙运动（犹太教）|The Jewish Enlightenment, a late 18th- and 19th-century intellectual movement among the Jews of Central and Eastern Europe, emphasizing reason, education, and assimilation into European society.|The Haskalah encouraged Jews to study secular subjects and integrate into broader society. 启蒙运动鼓励犹太人学习世俗科目并融入更广泛的社会。|近义词:Enlightenment, Renaissance|反义词:Hasidism|词性:名词
