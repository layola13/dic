anno:/ˈænoʊ/
noun
year (in dates)
Used in dates, especially in formal contexts.  It is an abbreviation of the Latin word "anno," which means "in the year."
The painting dates from anno 1820. 这幅画可以追溯到1820年。
Synonyms: year
Antonyms: N/A
词性: noun (abbreviation)
