accessally: /ækˈsɛsəli/ | adv. | 附加地，顺便地 |  In a way that is connected to or part of something else, but not essential. | He mentioned the issue accessally, almost as an afterthought. 他顺便提到了这个问题，几乎像是事后才想到的。 | 近义词: incidentally, by the way, additionally | 反义词: primarily, chiefly, mainly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From "accessary" (now usually spelled "accessory") + "-ly".  Ultimately from Latin "accessus", meaning "approach".
