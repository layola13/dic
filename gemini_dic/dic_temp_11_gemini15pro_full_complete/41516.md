corolline: /ˈkɒrəlaɪn/ (UK) /ˈkɔːrəlaɪn/ (US)| adj.| 花冠的；似花冠的；合瓣花的| Relating to, resembling, or having the form of a corolla (the petals of a flower).| The corolline tube was a vibrant shade of pink. 花冠管是鲜艳的粉红色。|  The botanist examined the corolline structure of the newly discovered flower. 植物学家检查了新发现的花的花冠结构。| 近义词: petaloid, gamopetalous | 反义词: apetalous | 词性: adjective

corolline: /ˈkɒrəlaɪn/ (UK) /ˈkɔːrəlaɪn/ (US)| n.| 合瓣花植物| A plant having gamopetalous flowers.|  Corollines are common in many gardens. 合瓣花植物在许多花园中很常见。| Many corollines are known for their vibrant colors. 许多合瓣花植物以其鲜艳的颜色而闻名。|近义词: gamopetalous plant| 反义词: polypetalous plant | 词性: noun
