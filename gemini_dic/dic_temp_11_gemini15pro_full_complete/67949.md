facias:/ˈfeɪʃəz/ /ˈfeɪʃiːz/| n. |面板，饰带|(plural of fascia) The broad flat surface over a shop front or below a cornice, typically one bearing a name or an advertisement.  It also refers to a thin sheath of fibrous tissue enclosing a muscle or other organ.|The shop facias were all decorated for the holidays. 商店的招牌都为节日装饰一新。|近义词:front, facade, covering|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps interior)|noun
