marara:/məˈrɑːrə/| n.|苦味，苦涩；（尤指）树皮的苦味|A bitter taste, especially of tree bark.|The marara of the quinine was overpowering. 金鸡纳树皮的苦味太强烈了。|近义词：bitterness, acridity, sourness|反义词：sweetness|词性：名词
词源：Likely of African origin, possibly Bantu.  Not found in major English dictionaries, but used in some regional dialects, particularly in parts of East Africa, to describe the bitter taste of certain barks or plants.  Not enough information is available to provide a definitive etymology.
