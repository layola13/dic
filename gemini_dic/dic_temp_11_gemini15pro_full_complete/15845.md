ballistic:/bəˈlɪstɪk/| adj.|弹道的，发射的；勃然大怒的|Relating to the science of projectiles and firearms; extremely and suddenly angry.|例句：The ballistic missile was launched from a submarine. 这枚弹道导弹是从潜艇发射的。

例句：He went ballistic when he heard the news. 他听到这个消息后勃然大怒。|近义词：furious, enraged, irate, livid|反义词：calm, peaceful, tranquil|词性:adjective

ballistics:/bəˈlɪstɪks/| n.|弹道学|The science of projectiles and firearms.|例句：Ballistics is a complex field of study. 弹道学是一门复杂的学科。

例句：The police used ballistics to match the bullet to the gun. 警方利用弹道学将子弹与枪支匹配。|近义词：projectiles|反义词:None|词性:noun
