lightens:/ˈlaɪtnz/| v.|（使）变亮，（使）变浅，（使）轻松|The sky lightens before dawn.  天空在黎明前变亮。|近义词：brightens, illuminates, eases, relieves|反义词：darkens, dims, burdens|词性:动词

Etymology: From Middle English *lightnen, from Old English līhtan (“to illuminate, shine, give light, kindle, set on fire”), from Proto-Germanic *liuhtijaną (“to light”), from Proto-Indo-European *lewk- (“light, bright”).  The meaning "to make or become less burdensome or oppressive" developed from the notion of making something less heavy.
