begrudge:/bɪˈɡrʌdʒ/
verb
吝惜；妒忌
to feel unhappy about spending money on something or giving something to someone:
例句：I don't begrudge her the success she's had. 我并不妒忌她所取得的成功。

例句：No one begrudges a fair wage for hard work. 没人会吝啬对辛勤工作的合理报酬。

近义词：envy, resent, grudge
反义词：grant, give, allow
词性：v.
