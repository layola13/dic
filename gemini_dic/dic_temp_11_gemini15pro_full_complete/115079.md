maneuverer:/məˈnuːvərər/ /məˈnuːvərər/
n.|操纵者，调动者|A person who skillfully maneuvers or directs something, especially a vehicle or a plan.|The experienced maneuverer expertly parked the large truck in the tight space.|Synonym: operator, manipulator, strategist, tactician|Antonym: novice, beginner|Noun

maneuver:/məˈnuːvər/ /məˈnuːvər/
n.|策略，调动，演习|A movement or series of moves requiring skill and care.|The troops performed a complex maneuver in the field.|Synonym: tactic, strategy, operation, ploy|Antonym: inaction, standstill|Noun

maneuver:/məˈnuːvər/ /məˈnuːvər/
v.|操纵，调动， maneuvering, maneuvered, maneuvers|To move skillfully or carefully.|She maneuvered the car through the heavy traffic.|Synonym: manipulate, navigate, steer, guide|Antonym: neglect, abandon|Verb
