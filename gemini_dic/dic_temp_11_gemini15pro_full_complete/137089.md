nonstudiousnesses:/ˌnɑːnˈstuːdiəsnəsɪz/
n.
lack of studiousness; the state of not being studious
The nonstudiousnesses of the students worried the teacher.  学生们不爱学习让老师很担心。
lack of diligence in study;  不勤奋学习
His nonstudiousnesses led to poor grades. 他不勤奋学习导致成绩很差。
Synonyms: indolence, laziness, apathy
Antonyms: studiousness, diligence, assiduity
noun
