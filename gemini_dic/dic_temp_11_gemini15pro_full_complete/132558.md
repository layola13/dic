nondialectic:/ˌnɒndaɪəˈlɛktɪk/
adjective
非辩证的
Not characteristic of or using dialectic; not proceeding by the methods of dialectic.
His approach to the problem was surprisingly nondialectic, focusing on a single perspective rather than exploring opposing viewpoints.  他处理这个问题的方式令人惊讶地是非辩证的，只关注单一视角，而不是探讨对立的观点。
The nondialectic presentation simply laid out the facts without engaging in a discussion of their implications.  非辩证的陈述只是简单地列出了事实，而没有讨论其含义。
近义词:undisputed, unargued, linear
反义词:dialectic, argumentative


Etymology:
From non- + dialectic.  Non- meaning "not" and dialectic referring to a method of argument.
