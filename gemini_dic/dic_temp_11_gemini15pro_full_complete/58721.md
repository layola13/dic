duncical: /ˈdʌŋsɪk(ə)l/
adjective
愚蠢的，笨拙的
Resembling or suggestive of a dunce; stupid and awkward.
He made a duncical remark that embarrassed everyone in the room. 他说了一句愚蠢的话，让房间里的每个人都很尴尬。
The duncical clown tripped over his own feet.  那个笨手笨脚的小丑被自己的脚绊倒了。
近义词: stupid, foolish, clumsy, awkward, inept
反义词: intelligent, smart, clever, graceful, adept
词性: adjective
词源: From "dunce" + "-ical."  "Dunce" originally referred to followers of John Duns Scotus, a 13th-century philosopher whose teachings later fell out of favor and were considered outdated and foolish.
