orthocone:/ˈɔːrθəkəʊn/ | n. |直角石|A straight, conical fossil shell of a cephalopod mollusk of the extinct order Orthocerida, common in Paleozoic rocks. |The museum displayed a large orthocone fossil. 博物馆展出了一块大型直角石化石。|Near synonyms:  cephalopod, nautiloid |Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider extant cephalopods like squid or octopus as conceptually opposite due to their drastically different shell morphology) | noun
