loess:/ləʊs/, /les/ | n. |黄土|A loosely compacted yellowish-gray deposit of windblown sediment of which extensive deposits occur, e.g., in eastern China and the American Midwest.|The fertile loess plateau of China is home to ancient civilizations. 中国肥沃的黄土高原是古代文明的摇篮。|Synonyms: silt, dust, sediment|Antonyms: bedrock, rock|noun
