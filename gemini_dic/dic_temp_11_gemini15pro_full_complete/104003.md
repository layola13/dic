jugulars:/ˈdʒʌɡjʊlərz/| n.|颈静脉|The jugulars are major veins in the neck that carry blood from the head back to the heart.  颈静脉是颈部主要的静脉，将血液从头部输送回心脏。|The predator went straight for the jugulars of its prey. 捕食者直接攻击猎物的颈静脉。|Near synonyms: neck veins, major veins|Antonym: artery|noun
