disintegrators:/dɪsˈɪntɪɡreɪtərz/
|n.|分解器，瓦解器|A machine or device that disintegrates something.  Something that causes disintegration.  Someone who breaks things down or destroys them. |The disintegrators reduced the rock to powder.  分解器将岩石变成了粉末。|近义词:pulverizers, crushers, destroyers|反义词: integrators, constructors, builders|词性:名词(复数)

disintegrator:/dɪsˈɪntɪɡreɪtər/
|n.|分解器，瓦解器|A machine or device that disintegrates something.  Something that causes disintegration.  Someone who breaks things down or destroys them. |The scientist invented a new disintegrator for recycling plastic.  科学家发明了一种新的塑料回收分解器。|近义词:pulverizer, crusher, destroyer|反义词:integrator, constructor, builder|词性:名词(单数)


ETYMOLOGY->
From disintegrate + -or. Disintegrate comes from dis- (apart) + integrate (to make whole). Integrate comes from Latin integratus, past participle of integrare (to make whole), from integer (whole).
