naenia:/ˈniːniə/ /ˈneɪniə/
noun
挽歌；葬歌；哀歌
A funeral song or lament; a dirge.
The naenia echoed through the ancient halls. 挽歌在古老的大厅里回荡。
The mourners sang a sorrowful naenia. 哀悼者唱着悲伤的挽歌。
近义词:dirge, lament, elegy, requiem
反义词:paean, hymn
词性:n.
