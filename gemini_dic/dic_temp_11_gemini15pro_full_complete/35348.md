co-exist:/ˌkoʊɪɡˈzɪst/| n. |共存|The state of living together in peace.| Peaceful co-exist is the goal of many international organizations. 和平共处是许多国际组织的目标。|Synonyms: coexistence, living together, sharing space, |Antonyms: conflict, discord, antagonism|Noun
