conglomeration:/kənˌɡlɒməˈreɪʃn/ /kənˌɡlɑːməˈreɪʃn/| n. |聚集，集合；混合物；企业集团|A large collection of different things or parts that are put or grouped together.  A large company formed by joining together different firms.|The city is a conglomeration of various ethnic groups.  这座城市是各种族群的聚居地。|synonyms:  aggregation, collection, accumulation, assemblage, cluster, mix, composite, combination|antonyms: separation, division, dispersion|noun

conglomerate:/kənˈɡlɒmərət/ /kənˈɡlɑːmərət/| n. |砾岩；企业集团|A rock consisting of rounded fragments cemented together;  a company formed by merging several different businesses.|The conglomerate owns businesses in multiple sectors. 这个企业集团拥有多个行业的业务。|synonyms:  corporation, combine, multinational|antonyms: subsidiary, small business|noun


conglomerate:/kənˈɡlɒməreɪt/ /kənˈɡlɑːməreɪt/| v. |聚集，集合|To gather into a rounded mass or ball; to collect or form into a mass or group.|Smaller companies conglomerated to form a larger corporation.  较小的公司合并成一个更大的公司。|synonyms:  amalgamate, consolidate, merge, unite|antonyms: separate, divide, disperse|verb
