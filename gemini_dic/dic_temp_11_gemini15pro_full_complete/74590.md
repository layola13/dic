freaked:/friːkt/ | adj. | 吓坏了的，非常激动的，兴奋的 | Feeling extremely surprised, upset, or excited, often in a negative way. freaked out:/friːkt aʊt/ | v. | 吓坏了，非常激动，兴奋 | To become extremely surprised, upset, or excited, often in a negative way. | The loud noise freaked me out. 巨大的噪音把我吓坏了。| synonyms: panic, lose control, go crazy, get excited | antonyms: calm down, relax, compose oneself | verb
