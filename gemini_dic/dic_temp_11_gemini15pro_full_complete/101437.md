iras:/ˈaɪræs/ (IPA pronunciation may vary depending on dialect; this is an approximation)| n.|爱尔兰共和军 (Irish Republican Army)|The Irish Republican Army (IRA) was a paramilitary organization seeking to end British rule in Northern Ireland.|The IRA's campaign of violence lasted for decades. 爱尔兰共和军的暴力活动持续了数十年。|Synonyms:  Provisional IRA, PIRA (referring to the Provisional Irish Republican Army, a specific faction), Republican movement (broader term)|Antonyms:  British security forces, Loyalist paramilitaries (groups opposing the IRA)|noun
