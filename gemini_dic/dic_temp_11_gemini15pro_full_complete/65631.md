ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ēvangelista, from Ancient Greek εὐαγγελιστής (euangelistḗs, “bringer of good news”), from εὐαγγέλιον (euangélion, “good news, gospel”), from εὖ (eû, “well”) + ἀγγέλλω (angéllō, “I announce”).
