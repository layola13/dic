attire:/əˈtaɪər/| n. vt.| 服装，盛装；穿着| Clothes, especially fine or formal ones.  The act of dressing someone in particular clothes.|  例句：He was in formal attire. 他穿着正式服装。|  例句：The queen's attire was magnificent. 女后的服装华丽至极。| 近义词：clothes, garments, apparel, outfit| 反义词:undress, nudity| 词性:名词, 动词

attire:/əˈtaɪər/| vt.| 穿着，打扮| To dress someone in particular clothes, especially fine or formal ones.| 例句：She was attired in a long flowing gown. 她穿着一件飘逸的长礼服。| 例句：He attired himself in his finest suit for the wedding. 他为婚礼穿上了他最好的西装。| 近义词：dress, clothe, garb| 反义词: undress, disrobe| 词性:动词
