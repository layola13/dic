disinvests:/ˌdɪsɪnˈvɛsts/
|v.|撤资，减少投资|To withdraw investment from (something).|The company disinvested from its failing subsidiaries.  公司从其亏损的子公司撤资。|The government disinvested in the railway sector. 政府减少了对铁路部门的投资。|synonyms: divest, withdraw, liquidate|antonyms: invest, fund, finance
|verb|
