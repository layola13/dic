discourteously: /dɪsˈkɜːrtiəsli/ /dɪsˈkɝːtiəsli/
adjective
粗鲁地，无礼地
in a rude and impolite manner.
He discourteously interrupted the speaker mid-sentence. 他粗鲁地在演讲者说到一半时打断了他。
The waiter discourteously ignored our requests. 服务员无礼地忽视了我们的要求。

近义词: rudely, impolitely, uncivilly, disrespectfully
反义词: politely, courteously, respectfully, civilly
词性: adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation) + courteous + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Courteous ultimately derives from the Old French "corteis" meaning "courteous," which itself comes from "cort" meaning "court."  Therefore, the word originally referred to behavior befitting a royal court.
