aerobiologist:/ˌɛroʊbaɪˈɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 空气生物学家 | A scientist who studies airborne organisms, such as pollen, spores, and microorganisms, and their effects on the environment and living beings. |  Aerobiologists monitor pollen counts to help allergy sufferers. 空气生物学家监测花粉计数以帮助过敏患者。|  The aerobiologist collected air samples to identify the source of the mold.  空气生物学家收集空气样本以识别霉菌的来源。 |  palynologist (花粉学家), microbiologist (微生物学家) |  N/A | noun
