atheists:/ˈeɪθiɪsts/
n.
无神论者
People who do not believe in the existence of God or any gods.
Many atheists are also humanists. 许多无神论者也是人道主义者。
近义词:nonbelievers, infidels, skeptics
反义词:believers, theists
词性:名词

Etymology:
From French *athée* (noun) and *athéiste* (adjective), from Greek *atheos* "without god," from *a-"without" + *theos* "god."
