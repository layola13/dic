arseniate: /ˈɑːrsənɪt/ (US), /ˈɑːsənɪt/ (UK)| n.|砷酸盐|A salt or ester of arsenic acid. Arsenic acid is a chemical compound with the formula H3AsO4. Arsenates are salts or esters of this acid containing the arsenate anion AsO43−.  |Sodium arsenate is used as a herbicide. 砷酸钠用作除草剂。|Synonyms: arsenical (more general, can refer to any arsenic compound)|Antonyms: None (no direct chemical opposite)|noun

arseniate: /ˈɑːrsənɪt/ (US), /ˈɑːsənɪt/ (UK)| adj.|砷酸的|Containing arsenate.|Arsenate contamination of groundwater is a serious environmental problem.  地下水中砷酸盐污染是一个严重的环境问题。|Synonyms: arsenical (more general)|Antonyms: None (no direct chemical opposite)|adjective
