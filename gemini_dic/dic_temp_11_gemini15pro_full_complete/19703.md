biliment:/ˈbɪlɪmənt/| n. | 装饰; 服饰; |Something that adorns or embellishes, especially an article of clothing or equipment. |The knight's biliment included a plumed helmet and a shining breastplate. 骑士的服饰包括一顶有羽毛的头盔和一件闪亮的胸甲。| The queen's biliment was magnificent, sparkling with jewels. 女王的服饰华丽非凡，闪耀着珠宝的光芒。|近义词：adornment, decoration, embellishment, ornament, finery, garb, attire|反义词：plainness, simplicity|词性:noun

词源:
From Middle French biliment, from bilier (“to equip, adorn”), from Old French abillier.
