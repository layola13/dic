hale:/heɪl/| adj.|健康的，健壮的；(尤指老年人)矍铄的；(向人打招呼时用语)你好|free from defect, disease, or infirmity; also : retaining exceptional health and vigor : ROBUST |例句：He is still hale and hearty at 80. 他80岁了仍然矍铄健壮。

例句：They greeted him with a hale "Good morning!" 他们精神抖擞地向他问候“早上好！”

近义词：healthy, robust, sound, well, hearty|反义词:infirm, frail, sickly, weak, feeble|词性:形容词
