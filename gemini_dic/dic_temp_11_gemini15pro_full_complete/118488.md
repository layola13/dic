mesmerises:/ˈmezməraɪzɪz/
verb
迷住，使着迷
to hold the attention of (someone) to the exclusion of all else or as if by magic
Her performance mesmerised the audience. 她的表演迷住了观众。
hypnotizes, captivates, fascinates
bores, repels
v.
