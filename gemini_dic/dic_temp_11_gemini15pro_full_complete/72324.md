floutingly:/ˈflaʊtɪŋli/| adv.|轻蔑地，公然违抗地|In an openly disrespectful or contemptuous manner; showing disregard for rules or conventions.|He floutingly ignored the dress code and wore jeans to the formal event. 他公然无视着装规定，穿着牛仔裤出席了正式活动。|Synonyms: disdainfully, contemptuously, scornfully, defiantly|Antonyms: respectfully, obediently, submissively|词性: 副词
