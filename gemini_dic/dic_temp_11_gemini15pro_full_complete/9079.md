antimiscegenation:/ˌæntiˌmɪsɪdʒɪˈneɪʃən/
n.
反对不同种族通婚
laws prohibiting interracial marriage or sexual relations
例句：Antimiscegenation laws were common in the United States until the mid-20th century.  美国在20世纪中期以前普遍存在反对不同种族通婚的法律。

例句：The fight against antimiscegenation laws was a crucial part of the civil rights movement.  反对禁止不同种族通婚法律的斗争是民权运动的重要组成部分。

近义词：racial segregation, racial discrimination
反义词：integration, racial equality
词性：名词
