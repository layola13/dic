digressional: /daɪˈɡrɛʃənl/ /dɪˈɡrɛʃənl/
adjective
离题的；枝节的
straying from the main subject; departing from the principal line of argument, narrative, or thought.
The professor's lectures were often digressional, full of anecdotes and personal stories. 教授的讲座经常离题，充满了奇闻轶事和个人故事。
His book contains too many digressional chapters. 他的书里包含太多离题的章节。
近义词: tangential, rambling, discursive, meandering
反义词: focused, concise, relevant, pertinent
词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *digressio, from Latin digressus, past participle of digredi, from di- "aside" + gradi "to walk, step, go".
