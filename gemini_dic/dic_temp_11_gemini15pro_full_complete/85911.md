heading:/ˈhedɪŋ/ | n. | 标题，方向 | A title at the head of a page or section of a book. | The heading of the chapter is "Introduction".  这一章的标题是“引言”。 | title, caption |  footer | noun

heading:/ˈhedɪŋ/ | n. | 航向，方位 | The direction in which a vehicle, especially an aircraft or ship, is travelling or pointing. | The ship's heading was due north. 船的航向是正北。 | direction, course, bearing |  | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *heding, *hedding, from Old English hēafodung (“a beheading”), from hēafod (“head”).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *haubiþą (“head”).
