crueller:/ˈkruːələr/ (US) /ˈkrʊələr/ (UK)| adj.|更残酷的，更无情的|More cruel; more heartless and unkind.|例句：He was a crueller master than his predecessor. 他比他的前任是一个更残酷的主人。

例句：The crueller reality is that many people will lose their jobs. 更残酷的现实是许多人将会失业。

近义词：more heartless, more ruthless, more merciless|反义词:kinder, gentler, more compassionate|词性:形容词

cruel:/ˈkruːəl/ (US) /ˈkruːəl/ (UK)| adj.|残酷的，无情的|Wilfully causing pain or suffering to others, or feeling no concern about it.|例句：He is cruel to animals. 他对动物很残忍。

例句：It was a cruel joke. 这是一个残酷的玩笑。

近义词：heartless, ruthless, merciless|反义词:kind, gentle, compassionate|词性:形容词
