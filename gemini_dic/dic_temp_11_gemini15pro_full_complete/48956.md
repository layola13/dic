degeled:/dɪˈɡɛld/ | adj. |  剥皮的；去壳的；去皮的 | Removed of a shell, husk, or rind. |  The degeled sunflower seeds are ready to be roasted.  剥了壳的葵花籽可以烤了。 | 近义词: shelled, husked, peeled, skinned | 反义词: unpeeled, unshelled, unhusked | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Likely derived from the verb "degel," a back-formation from "degelling,"  meaning to remove the gel-like coating or outer layer of something. The root "gel" refers to a jelly-like substance.
