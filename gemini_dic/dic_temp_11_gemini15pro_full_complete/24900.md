bruisingly:/ˈbruːzɪŋli/
adverb|残酷地，激烈地，令人沮丧地|In a way that inflicts or suggests the pain of a bruise, whether physical or emotional.  It implies a harsh, impactful, and often discouraging effect.|The election was bruisingly close, leaving both candidates exhausted and uncertain of the outcome.  选举结果势均力敌，双方候选人都筋疲力尽，对结果也毫无把握。|Synonyms: harshly, severely, intensely, painfully, crushingly|Antonyms: gently, kindly, mildly, softly|词性: 副词

bruisingly:/ˈbruːzɪŋli/
adverb|带有淤伤的，有瘀斑的 (较少用)|Marked by bruises.|His face was bruisingly swollen after the fight.  打架后他的脸肿得青一块紫一块的。（这个用法相对少见，更多用于描述外观）|Synonyms: black-and-blue, discolored|Antonyms: unblemished, clear|词性: 副词
