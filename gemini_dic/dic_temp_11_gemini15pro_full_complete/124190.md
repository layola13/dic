moschatel:/ˈmɒskətɛl/ | n. |麝香草|A small perennial plant (Adoxa moschatellina) of the family Adoxaceae, having pale green flowers with a musky scent.| Moschatel often grows in damp, shady places. 麝香草通常生长在潮湿阴暗的地方。|Synonyms:  townhall clock, muskroot|Antonyms: N/A| noun
