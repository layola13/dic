morbid:/ˈmɔːrbɪd/ (US), /ˈmɔːbɪd/ (UK)| adj.| 病态的，不正常的；（对死亡等）着迷的|Characterized by or appealing to an abnormal and unhealthy interest in disturbing and unpleasant subjects, especially death and disease. | She has a morbid fascination with true crime. 她对真实犯罪有着病态的迷恋。| synonyms: macabre, gruesome, unhealthy, ghoulish | antonyms: healthy, wholesome, cheerful | adjective

morbidity:/mɔːrˈbɪdɪti/ (US), /mɔːˈbɪdɪti/ (UK)| n.| 病态；发病率|The condition of being diseased; the rate of disease in a population. | The morbidity rate from the virus was surprisingly low. 这种病毒的发病率出奇地低。| synonyms: prevalence, incidence, sickness | antonyms: health, wellness | noun
