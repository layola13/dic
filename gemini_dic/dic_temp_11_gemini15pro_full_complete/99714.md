interlotting:/ˌɪntərˈlɒtɪŋ/ | n. |相互分配，交错分配，穿插|The interlotting of land among different owners can lead to disputes. 不同业主之间的土地相互分配会导致纠纷。|例句：The interlotting of the farmlands made irrigation difficult.  农田的交错分配使得灌溉变得困难。

例句：The complex interlotting of responsibilities between departments caused confusion. 各部门之间复杂的职责交错分配造成了混乱。|近义词：interlacing, intertwining, interweaving, integration|反义词：separation, division|词性:名词

Etymology:
From inter- + lotting (from lot, meaning "a portion or share").  The prefix "inter-" signifies "between" or "among." Thus, the word literally refers to the act of allotting or distributing something between or among different entities.
