chickory: /ˈtʃɪkəri/ (US), /ˈtʃɪkəri/ (UK)| n.| 菊苣; 菊苣根| A blue-flowered plant whose leaves are eaten in salads and whose root is roasted and ground to be used as a coffee substitute or additive.  It can also refer to the root itself. |例句：
I added some roasted chickory to my coffee. 我在我的咖啡里加了一些烘焙菊苣。

The salad contained young chickory leaves. 沙拉里有嫩菊苣叶。

|近义词：endive (especially for the leafy vegetable), coffee substitute|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
