downthrow:/ˈdaʊnθroʊ/| n.|下落；下降；断层下降盘|A downthrow is the side of a fault that moves downwards relative to the other side.|The earthquake caused a significant downthrow, creating a new valley.|近义词：downcast, downslip, subsidence|反义词：upthrow, uplift|noun

downthrow:/ˈdaʊnθroʊ/| v.|向下扔；使下落|To throw or cast something downwards.|He downthrew the ball with considerable force.|近义词：cast down, throw down|反义词：throw up, uplift|verb
