attractiveness:/əˈtræktɪvnəs/
n.|吸引力，魅力|The attractiveness of the offer was undeniable. 这个提议的吸引力是不可否认的。|例句：The city's attractiveness lies in its vibrant cultural scene. 这座城市的魅力在于其充满活力的文化景象。|近义词：appeal, charm, allure, magnetism|反义词：unattractiveness, repulsiveness|词性:名词

Etymology: From attract + -ive + -ness. Attract comes from the Latin *attrahere*, from *ad-* "to" + *trahere* "to draw".
