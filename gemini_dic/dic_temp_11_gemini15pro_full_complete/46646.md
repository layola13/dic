damnific:/dæmˈnɪfɪk/
adjective
损害的，造成损失的
causing damage or loss; injurious
The damnific effects of the storm were felt throughout the region.  这场风暴的破坏性影响波及了整个地区。
近义词: damaging, harmful, detrimental, injurious, prejudicial
反义词: beneficial, advantageous, helpful
词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin damnificus, from Latin damnificare “to cause loss or harm,” from damnum “loss, damage” + -ficare (akin to facere “to make, do”).
