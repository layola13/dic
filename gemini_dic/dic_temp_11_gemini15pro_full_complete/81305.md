granulose: /ˈɡrænjuːləʊs/ (UK) /ˈɡrænjuːloʊs/ (US)
adjective
颗粒状的；粒状的
Consisting of or appearing to consist of granules.
The soil is dry and granulose. 土壤干燥且呈颗粒状。
The surface of the moon is granulose. 月球表面是颗粒状的。
近义词: granular, grainy
反义词: smooth, fine
词性: adjective

granulose: /ˈɡrænjuːləʊs/ (UK) /ˈɡrænjuːloʊs/ (US)
noun
颗粒层
The layer of small follicular cells surrounding the oocyte in a Graafian follicle.
The granulosa produces estrogen. 颗粒层产生雌激素。
Damage to the granulosa can affect fertility.  颗粒层的损伤会影响生育能力。
近义词: membrana granulosa
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
