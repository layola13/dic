migrative: /ˈmaɪɡrətɪv/ | adj. |迁徙的，迁移的|Relating to, or characterized by migration. |The scientists tracked the migrative patterns of the monarch butterflies. 科学家们追踪了帝王蝶的迁徙模式。| The salmon begin their migrative journey upstream. 鲑鱼开始它们向上游的迁徙之旅。|近义词：migratory, nomadic, wandering|反义词：settled, stationary, resident|词性:形容词
词源：From Latin *migrat- , the past participle stem of migrare "to change one's residence, depart from a place," from migrus "about to depart."
