monocotyledonous:/ˌmɒnəʊˌkɒtɪˈliːdənəs/ | adj. | 单子叶的 | Having only one cotyledon (seed leaf).  A characteristic of a major group of flowering plants. |  Corn is a monocotyledonous plant. 玉米是一种单子叶植物。|  Monocot, monocotyledon | Dicotyledonous | adjective
