acequia:/əˈseɪkiə/ /ɑːˈseɪkiə/
n.
灌溉渠，沟渠
A ditch or canal used for irrigation, especially in the southwestern US.
The acequia system is vital for agriculture in New Mexico.  新墨西哥州的灌溉渠系统对农业至关重要。
The farmers share the water from the acequia.  农民们共享来自灌溉渠的水。
近义词:canal, ditch, irrigation channel
反义词:None
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *acequia*, from Arabic *as-saqiya* (الساقية), meaning "the water-carrier," ultimately from *sāqā* (سقى), meaning "to give to drink."
