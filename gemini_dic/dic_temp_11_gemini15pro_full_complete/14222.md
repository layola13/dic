autoclave:/ˈɔːtəˌkleɪv/ (US) /ˈɔːtəʊˌkleɪv/ (UK)| n. v. |高压灭菌器；高压灭菌|A strong, heated container used for chemical reactions and other processes using high pressures and temperatures, especially for sterilizing medical equipment. |例句：The surgical instruments were sterilized in the autoclave. 手术器械在高压灭菌器中消过毒了。

|例句：We autoclaved the lab equipment before the experiment. 我们在实验前用高压锅对实验室设备进行了灭菌。|近义词：sterilizer |反义词: - |词性: noun, verb
