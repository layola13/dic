largemouth:/ˈlɑːrdʒˌmaʊθ/ (US), /ˈlɑːdʒˌmaʊθ/ (UK)| n. adj.|大嘴鲈鱼; 大嘴巴的|A fish (Micropterus salmoides) known for its large mouth, also used as an adjective to describe something having a large mouth.|例句：The largemouth bass is a popular game fish. 大嘴鲈鱼是一种受欢迎的游钓鱼。|近义词：bass, wide-mouthed|反义词:small-mouthed|词性:名词, 形容词

Etymology:
From large + mouth.  The term "largemouth" specifically refers to the species Micropterus salmoides, first described scientifically in 1802. The name directly reflects the fish's prominent physical characteristic.
