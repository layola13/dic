hump:/hʌmp/| n. v. |驼峰，隆起；弓起；努力；|A rounded protuberance on the back of a camel or other animal.  To exert oneself vigorously; to carry (something heavy) with difficulty. |The camel has two humps. 这只骆驼有两个驼峰。 He humped the sack of potatoes onto his shoulder. 他把那袋土豆扛到肩上。|近义词：bulge, protuberance, lump, hunch (n.); strain, toil, slog (v.)|反义词:dip, depression, hollow (n.); relax, rest (v.)|词性:名词,动词
Etymology: From Middle English *homp*, probably of imitative origin.
