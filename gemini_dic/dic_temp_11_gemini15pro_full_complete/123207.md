monochromy: /məˈnɒkrəmi/ /məˈnɑːkrəmi/ | n. | 单色; 单色画法 | The quality or state of being monochromatic. The use of a single color or shades of a single color. | The artist chose monochromy for his latest series of portraits, using only shades of blue.  这位艺术家为他最新的肖像系列选择了单色，只使用了蓝色阴影。| 近义词: monochrome | 反义词: polychromy | 词性: 名词

monochromatic: /ˌmɒnəkrəˈmætɪk/ /ˌmɑːnəkrəˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 单色的; 单频的 | Having or consisting of one color or shades of one color. | The room was decorated in a monochromatic scheme of grey. 房间以灰色单色方案装饰。| 近义词: one-color, single-colored | 反义词: polychromatic, multicolored | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *μονόχρωμος (monochromos) meaning "one-colored," from *μόνος (monos) meaning "alone, single" and *χρῶμα (chroma) meaning "color."
