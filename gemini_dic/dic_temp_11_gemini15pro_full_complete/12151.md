aruspice: /əˈrʌspaɪs/ | n. | 占卜官；预言家 | A Roman priest who practiced divination by inspecting the entrails of sacrificial animals. |  The aruspice examined the liver of the sacrificed ox to predict the outcome of the battle. 占卜官检查了献祭的牛的肝脏来预测战斗的结果。 |  soothsayer, diviner, prophet, oracle |  - | noun
