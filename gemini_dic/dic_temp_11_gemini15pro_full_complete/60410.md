eighteenth:/ˌeɪˈtiːnθ/ /ˌeɪˈtiːnθ/
adjective
第十八
Being the ordinal number for 18.
例句：This is her eighteenth birthday. 这是她的十八岁生日。

例句：It was the eighteenth time he'd asked me the same question. 这是他第十八次问我同样的问题了。

近义词：18th
反义词：(no direct antonym for ordinal numbers)
词性：adjective
