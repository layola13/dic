nonhumanized:/ˌnɒnˈhjuːmənaɪzd/ | /ˌnɑːnˈhjuːmənaɪzd/ | adj. | 非人化的，未人化的 | Not given human characteristics or form; not adapted for human use. | The study focused on the effects of the virus on nonhumanized mice.  这项研究集中于病毒对非人化小鼠的影响。 |  The software provides a nonhumanized interface. 该软件提供了一个非人化的界面。 | 近义词: inanimate, non-anthropomorphic | 反义词: humanized, anthropomorphic | 词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"  来源于拉丁语 non
humanize (verb): meaning "to make or become human or more human"  来源于晚期拉丁语 humanizare,  从 humanus (human) 演变而来
-ed (suffix):  forming the past participle of verbs, and often adjectives
