fuggier:/ˈfʌɡiər/ | adj. | 更闷热潮湿的，更加令人窒息的 | More humid and stuffy, more suffocating. |  It's much fuggier in here than it was outside. 这里比外面闷热潮湿多了。| 近义词：stuffier, muggier, sultrier | 反义词：cooler, fresher, airier | 词性: 形容词

fuggier:/ˈfʌɡiər/ | adj. | 更模糊的，更难理解的 | More vague, harder to understand. | The situation is even fuggier now than it was before. 现在的情况比以前更令人困惑了。| 近义词：vaguer, hazier, murkier | 反义词：clearer, more distinct, more precise | 词性: 形容词


词源:  "fuggy" 的比较级，源自 "fug," 指代一种浓厚的，令人窒息的空气。
