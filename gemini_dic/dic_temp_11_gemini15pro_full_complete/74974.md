fricative:/ˈfrɪkətɪv/ | n. | 摩擦音 | A consonant sound, such as /f/ or /v/, produced by forcing air through a narrow channel made by placing two articulators close together. |  The sounds /f/, /v/, /θ/, and /ð/ are fricatives. /f/、/v/、/θ/和/ð/都是摩擦音。 |  近义词:spirant | 反义词:plosive, stop | 词性:noun

fricatives:/ˈfrɪkətɪvz/ | n. | 摩擦音 | Plural form of "fricative". Refers to multiple consonant sounds produced by forcing air through a narrow channel. | The English language has several fricatives, including /f/, /v/, /θ/, /ð/, /s/, /z/, /ʃ/, /ʒ/, and /h/.  英语有几个摩擦音，包括/f/、/v/、/θ/、/ð/、/s/、/z/、/ʃ/、/ʒ/和/h/。| 近义词:spirants | 反义词:plosives, stops | 词性:noun
