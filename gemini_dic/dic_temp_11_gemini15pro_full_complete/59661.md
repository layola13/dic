echinital: /ɪˈkaɪnɪtəl/ | adj. | 海胆的；像海胆的 | Relating to or resembling a sea urchin. |  The pottery shard was decorated with an echinital pattern.  这块陶器碎片装饰有海胆图案。 |  Synonyms: echinoid | Antonyms:  N/A | adjective

echinital: /ɪˈkaɪnɪtəl/ | n. | 海胆类动物 | Any of various echinoderms of the class Echinoidea, having a typically globular body enclosed in a shell of interlocking plates covered with movable spines. | The echinital was found washed up on the beach after the storm.  暴风雨后，人们发现这只海胆被冲上了海滩。 | Synonyms: sea urchin, echinoid | Antonyms: N/A | noun
