diffraction:/dɪˈfrækʃən/| n. |衍射|The spreading of waves (light, sound, etc.) as they pass through an opening or around an obstacle.|例句：The diffraction of light through a prism creates a rainbow. 光线通过棱镜的衍射形成了彩虹。

例句：The diffraction pattern of the crystal revealed its atomic structure. 晶体的衍射图样揭示了它的原子结构。|近义词：scattering, dispersion|反义词: interference|词性: 名词
