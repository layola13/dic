detonatability:/ˌdiːtɒnəˈtɪləti/ | n. | 爆炸性，起爆性 | The quality of being detonatable; the ease with which something can be detonated. | The detonatability of the new explosive was significantly higher than that of conventional dynamite.  这种新型炸药的爆炸性明显高于传统的炸药。| Synonyms: explosiveness, explosibility | Antonyms: stability, inertness | noun


detonate:/ˈdetəneɪt/ | v. | 爆炸，引爆 | Explode or cause to explode. | The bomb detonated prematurely, causing significant damage. 炸弹过早爆炸，造成重大损失。| Synonyms: explode, blow up | Antonyms: defuse, disarm | verb
