bulldogs:/ˈbʊldɔːɡz/| n.|斗牛犬（复数）|A breed of dog with a short nose, a wrinkled face, and a stocky build.  It is known for its courage and tenacity.  It is the plural form of "bulldog".|The bulldogs were playing in the park. 斗牛犬们在公园里玩耍。|The Yale bulldogs are a famous university sports team.  耶鲁大学的斗牛犬队是一支著名的大学运动队。|近义词：dogs, canines|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific breed)|noun
