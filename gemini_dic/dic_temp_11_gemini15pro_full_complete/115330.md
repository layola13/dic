mannitol:/ˈmænɪˌtɔːl/ (US), /ˈmænɪtɒl/ (UK)| n.|甘露醇|A white crystalline alcohol, C6H14O6, occurring naturally in many plants and used medically as a diuretic and in the manufacture of resins, artificial ivory, and explosives.|Mannitol is used to reduce swelling in the brain. 甘露醇用于减轻脑肿胀。|Synonyms: hexitol, manna sugar|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a chemical substance. Could consider substances with opposite effects, like antidiuretics.)|Noun
