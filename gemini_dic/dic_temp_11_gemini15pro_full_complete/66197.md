excise:/ɪkˈsaɪz/ | vt. | 切除; 删去 | To remove completely, especially by cutting or surgery. | The surgeon excised the tumor. 外科医生切除了肿瘤。| synonyms: remove, cut out, extract | antonyms: insert, implant, add | verb
