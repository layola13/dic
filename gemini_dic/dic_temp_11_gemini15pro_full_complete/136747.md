nonsensuousness:/ˌnɒnsenˈsjuːəsnəs/ |n.|无感觉；非感官性；抽象性|The nonsensuousness of abstract art can be challenging for some viewers. 抽象艺术的非感官性对一些观众来说可能具有挑战性。|The philosopher explored the nonsensuousness of pure thought. 这位哲学家探索了纯粹思想的非感官性。|近义词: abstractness, incorporeality, immateriality|反义词: concreteness, physicality, tangibility|词性:noun

Etymology: non- (not) + sensuous (related to the senses) + -ness (suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality)
