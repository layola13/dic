overnationalization: /ˌoʊvərˌnæʃənələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 超国家化，超国家主义 | The transfer of power from national governments to international bodies.  The process by which national governments delegate some of their sovereignty to supranational institutions. | The overnationalization of economic policy in the European Union has led to both benefits and drawbacks for member states. 欧盟经济政策的超国家化给成员国带来了好处和坏处。|  supranationalism, integration, globalization | nationalism, localization | noun
