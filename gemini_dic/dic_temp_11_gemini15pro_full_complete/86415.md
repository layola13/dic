heeders:/ˈhiːdərz/
n.
注意者；听从者；服从者
Those who pay attention and obey are heeders.
例句：The heeders of the warning stayed indoors during the storm.  听从警告的人在暴风雨期间待在室内。

例句：Wise heeders of financial advice often prosper.  明智的 heeders of financial advice often prosper. 睿智的理财建议听从者通常会兴旺发达。

近义词：listeners, followers, observers
反义词：ignorers, rebels, dissenters
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "heed" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting a person or thing that performs the action of the verb).  "Heed" comes from Old English "hēdan," meaning "to take care, pay attention to."
