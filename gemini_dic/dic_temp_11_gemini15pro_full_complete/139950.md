odontocete:/ˌoʊdɒntoʊˈsiːt/ | n. | 齿鲸 |A toothed whale, belonging to the suborder Odontoceti.  They are characterized by having teeth, a single blowhole, and the ability to use echolocation. | Example: Dolphins and porpoises are examples of odontocetes. 海豚和鼠海豚是齿鲸的例子。| Synonym: toothed whale | Antonym: baleen whale | noun
