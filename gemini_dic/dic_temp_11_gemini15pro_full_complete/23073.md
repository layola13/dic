boundarylessness:/ˈbaʊndri.ləsnəs/
n.|无边界性；无界限；无疆界|The state of lacking clearly defined boundaries or limits, especially in organizations, referring to the permeability of boundaries between departments, functions, or roles.|例句：Boundarylessness in the workplace can foster collaboration but also lead to role confusion. 工作场所的无边界性可以促进合作，但也可能导致角色混淆。

例句：The company embraced boundarylessness to encourage innovation across departments.  公司拥抱无边界性，以鼓励跨部门创新。

近义词：fluidity, openness, permeability, limitlessness
反义词：boundedness, restriction, limitation
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
boundary (from Old North French bounde, "limit") + -less (suffix meaning "without") + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)
