fallaciousness:/fəˈleɪʃəsnəs/ |n.|谬误, 虚假|The fallaciousness of his argument was quickly exposed. 他的论证的谬误很快就被揭露了。|例句：The fallaciousness of the claim was evident in the lack of supporting data.  这种说法的谬误性在缺乏支持数据的情况下显而易见。

The fallaciousness of their reasoning led them to the wrong conclusion.  他们推理的谬误导致他们得出了错误的结论。|近义词：falsity, invalidity, unsoundness, deceptiveness|反义词：truthfulness, validity, soundness, accuracy|词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From fallacious + -ness.  "Fallacious" comes from Late Latin *fallaciosus, from Latin fallax "deceptive," from fallere "to deceive, trip, lead into error."  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
