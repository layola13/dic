demonstrators:/ˈdɛmənˌstreɪtərz/
noun
示威者，游行者
People who participate in a public demonstration to express their opinions or support for a cause.
Thousands of demonstrators marched through the streets. 数千名示威者走上街头游行。
protesters, marchers, participants
opponents, bystanders
n.
