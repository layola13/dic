keeve:/kiːv/| n. |大桶，发酵桶|A large vat, tub, or vessel, especially one used for fermenting or storing liquids.|The keeve was filled with fermenting grapes for the winemaking process.  大桶里装满了用来酿酒的发酵葡萄。|近义词：vat, tub, cask, barrel|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps container with a specific, smaller capacity like "jug")|noun
