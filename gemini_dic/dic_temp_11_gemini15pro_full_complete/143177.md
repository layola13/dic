outclimbs:/ˌaʊtˈklaɪmz/
| v. | 胜过攀登; 爬得比…更高 | surpasses in climbing; climbs higher or faster than | The young mountaineer outclimbs all the others in the expedition.  年轻的登山家在探险中胜过所有其他人。|
| v. | 比…爬得更高 | Climbs higher than |  The ivy outclimbs the garden wall. 常春藤爬过了花园墙。 |
| synonym: surpass, exceed, outdo, top, beat |
| antonym: lag behind, trail |
| verb |
