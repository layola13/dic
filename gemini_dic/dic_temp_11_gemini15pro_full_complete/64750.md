escript:/ˈiːskrɪpt/ | n. |脚本，电子脚本|A script, especially one for a movie, play, or broadcast, often in electronic form.|The escript for the film was leaked online. 这部电影的电子脚本在网上泄露了。|近义词:script, screenplay|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 improvisation 即兴创作) |词性:名词


词源: e- (electronic) + script.  'e-' prefix denotes electronic, as in e-mail. 'script' comes from Latin 'scriptum', meaning 'something written'.
