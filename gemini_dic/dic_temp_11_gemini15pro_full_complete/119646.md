middlebrow:/ˈmɪdəlˌbraʊ/ | adj. n. | 中庸的；中等文化水平的；中庸之人 | Appealing to or typical of people with conventional, middle-class tastes in literature, music, art, etc.; not highly intellectual or cultured, but not lowbrow either.  | Example: He dismissed the novel as middlebrow sentimentalism. 他认为这部小说是庸俗的感伤主义。

Example:  She enjoys reading middlebrow novels and watching popular television dramas. 她喜欢读一些中等文化水平的小说，看一些流行的电视剧。 | 近义词: conventional, mainstream, unpretentious | 反义词: highbrow, lowbrow, intellectual, sophisticated | 词性: adjective, noun
