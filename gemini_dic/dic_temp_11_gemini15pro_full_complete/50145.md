denumbered:/diːˈnʌmbərd/
adjective
减少数量的；被剥夺公民权的
deprived of civil rights (often as a punishment); reduced in number.
The denumbered population struggled to regain their rights.  被剥夺公民权的人们努力争取恢复他们的权利。
The denumbered ranks of the army struggled to hold the line.  数量减少的军队艰难地坚守阵线。
synonyms:diminished, reduced, depleted, lessened
antonyms:increased, augmented, enlarged, expanded
