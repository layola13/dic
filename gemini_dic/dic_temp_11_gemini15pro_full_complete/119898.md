militarize:/ˈmɪlɪtəraɪz/| v. |军事化|To equip or train for war; to adapt for military use; to give a military character to.|例句：The government decided to militarize the border region after the attack. 政府决定在袭击后将边境地区军事化。

例句：The small island nation began to militarize, concerned about its neighbor's growing military power.  这个岛国开始军事化，担心邻国日益增长的军事实力。|近义词：arm, fortify, strengthen|反义词:demilitarize, disarm, pacify|词性:动词

词源: From military + -ize.  "Military" comes from Latin "militaris" meaning "of soldiers, of warfare". "-ize" is a suffix that forms verbs meaning "to make or become".
