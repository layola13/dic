blow-dry: /ˈbloʊˌdraɪ/ (American) /ˈbləʊˌdraɪ/ (British)| n. v.|吹干，吹发|The act of drying something, especially hair, with a blow-dryer.  As a noun, it refers to the styled hair resulting from a blow-dry.| I gave my hair a quick blow-dry. 我快速地吹干了头发。|近义词:drying, coiffure (n.)|反义词:wetting, soaking (v.)|词性:名词/动词

词源:  A compound word formed from "blow" (referring to the air propelled by the dryer) and "dry."  Relatively recent, emerging in the mid-20th century with the popularization of handheld hair dryers.
