disconcertion:/ˌdɪskənˈsɜːrʃən/ /ˌdɪskənˈsərʃən/
|n.|不安；慌张；混乱|A feeling of worry and slight embarrassment|The sudden cancellation of the meeting caused some disconcertion among the participants. 会议突然取消，使与会者有些不安。|近义词：unease, anxiety, perturbation, confusion|反义词：calm, composure, assurance|词性:名词

disconcerting:/ˌdɪskənˈsɜːrʃən/ /ˌdɪskənˈsərʃən/
|adj.|令人不安的；令人尴尬的；令人困惑的|Causing a feeling of worry and slight embarrassment|His silence was disconcerting. 他的沉默令人不安。|近义词：unsettling, disturbing, troubling, confusing|反义词：reassuring, comforting, calming|词性:形容词
