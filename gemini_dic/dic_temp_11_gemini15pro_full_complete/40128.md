contemplatist:/ˈkɒntəmplətɪst/ (US) /ˈkɑːntəmpleɪtɪst/| n.|沉思者；冥想者；静观者|A person who spends much time in contemplation or meditation; one given to contemplation.|He was a contemplatist, preferring solitude and reflection to the bustle of city life. 他是一个沉思者，喜欢孤独和反思，胜过城市的喧嚣。|近义词：meditator, thinker, ponderer, reflector|反义词：activist, doer|词性:noun

contemplative:/ˈkɒntəmplətɪv/ (US) /ˈkɑːntəmpleɪtɪv/| adj.|沉思的；冥想的；静观的|Characterized by or given to contemplation; thoughtful.|She had a contemplative nature, often lost in her own thoughts. 她生性沉思，常常沉浸在自己的思绪中。|近义词：thoughtful, meditative, reflective, pensive|反义词：active, impulsive|词性:adjective

词源: From Late Latin *contemplativus*, from Latin contemplatus (past participle of contemplari "to contemplate").
