miscreed:/ˌmɪsˈkriːd/
n.
异端邪说；错误的信念
A belief that differs from established doctrine.
例句：His miscreed caused him to be ostracized from the community. 他的异端邪说导致他被逐出社区。

例句：The cult followed a dangerous miscreed.  这个邪教信奉着危险的异端邪说。

近义词：heresy, fallacy, heterodoxy
反义词：orthodoxy, dogma
