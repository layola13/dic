imbecilitate:/ɪmˈbɛsɪleɪt/| vt.|使衰弱，使愚钝|To make someone or something weak or ineffective, especially mentally.|The drug imbecilitated him to the point where he could barely speak. 这种药使他变得虚弱，几乎说不出话来。|Synonyms: weaken, enfeeble, debilitate, incapacitate|Antonyms: strengthen, invigorate, empower|Verb
imbecility:/ˌɪmbɪˈsɪlɪti/| n.|愚蠢，低能|The state of being imbecile; stupidity or weakness of mind.|His imbecility was evident in his poor decision-making. 他的愚蠢体现在他糟糕的决策上。|Synonyms: stupidity, foolishness, idiocy, weakness|Antonyms: intelligence, wisdom, brilliance|Noun
