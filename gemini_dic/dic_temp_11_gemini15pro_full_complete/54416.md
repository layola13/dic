disinclose:/dɪsɪnˈkloʊz/ | v. | 揭露，透露 | To reveal or disclose something previously hidden or secret. |  He disinclosed the secret to his best friend. 他向他最好的朋友透露了这个秘密。

The journalist disinclosed the corruption within the government.  这位记者揭露了政府内部的腐败。 | 近义词: reveal, disclose, divulge, unveil, expose | 反义词: conceal, hide, suppress | 词性: 动词


词源:  From dis- (expressing reversal) + enclose.  "Dis-" 表示相反或否定，"enclose" 表示包围或封闭。因此，"disinclose" 的字面意思是“打开封闭”，引申为揭露或透露。
