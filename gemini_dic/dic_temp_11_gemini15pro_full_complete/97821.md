inkiness:/ˈɪŋkɪnɪs/
noun
墨黑; 漆黑
The state or quality of being inky; blackness.
The inkiness of the night sky was breathtaking. 夜空的墨黑令人叹为观止。
synonyms: blackness, darkness, obscurity
antonyms: brightness, lightness, paleness
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From inky + -ness.  Inky from ink + -y. Ink from Old French "enque," from Late Latin "encaustum," from Greek "enkauston" meaning "burnt in," referring to a pigment fixed by heat.
