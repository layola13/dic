hybridize:/ˈhaɪbrɪdaɪz/ /ˈhaɪbrəˌdaɪz/| v. |杂交，混合|To crossbreed or combine different varieties of plants or animals to create a hybrid; to blend different elements or styles.|Scientists have hybridized the two species of plant to create a new variety. 科学家们将这两种植物杂交，创造了一个新品种。

The band's music hybridizes elements of rock and jazz.  这个乐队的音乐融合了摇滚和爵士的元素。|近义词：crossbreed, interbreed, blend, merge, combine|反义词：separate, isolate, purify|词性:动词

hybridization:/ˌhaɪbrɪdəˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌhaɪbrɪdɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |杂交，混合|The act or process of hybridizing.|The hybridization of different cultures can lead to new and exciting forms of art. 不同文化的融合可以带来令人兴奋的新艺术形式。

The successful hybridization of the two plant species resulted in a more disease-resistant variety.  两种植物的成功杂交产生了一个更抗病的品种。|近义词：crossbreeding, interbreeding, blending, merging, combination|反义词：separation, isolation, purification|词性:名词
