hoodedness:/ˈhʊdɪdnəs/
n.
戴兜帽的状态；隐蔽性；神秘性
The state of being hooded; the quality of being covered or concealed, often suggesting mystery or secrecy.
The hoodedness of the figure added to the sense of mystery.  这个身影戴着兜帽，增添了神秘感。
The hoodedness of the falcon's eyes made it difficult to tell where it was looking. 猎鹰眼睛的遮蔽性使人很难判断它在看哪里。
secrecy, concealment, mystery
openness, clarity, visibility
noun
