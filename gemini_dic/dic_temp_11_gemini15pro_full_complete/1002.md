acatalectic:/ˌeɪkætəˈlɛktɪk/| adj.|完整无缺的，抑扬格五音步诗行完整的|Having the complete number of syllables in a line of verse; (of a line of verse) having the metrical length appropriate to the meter in which it is written.|The poem is written in perfect acatalectic tetrameter.|近义词：complete, full, entire, whole|反义词:catalectic, incomplete|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Greek *akatalēktos*, from *a-* (not) + *katalēktikos* (stopping short, catalectic), from *katalēgein* (to stop, leave off).
