antisceptical:/ˌæntiˈskeptɪkəl/
adjective
反怀疑论的；不怀疑的
opposed to or tending to refute skepticism; not skeptical
He maintained an antisceptical stance throughout the debate, firmly believing in the evidence presented. 他在整个辩论中保持着反怀疑的态度，坚信所提出的证据。
She had an antisceptical view of the new scientific theory, readily accepting its premises. 她对新的科学理论持不怀疑的态度，很容易接受它的前提。
近义词:credulous, trusting, unquestioning
反义词:sceptical, doubting, questioning
词性:adjective
