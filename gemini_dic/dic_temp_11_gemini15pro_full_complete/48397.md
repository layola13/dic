dedesignations:/ˌdiːˌdɛzɪɡˈneɪʃənz/ | n. | 取消指定；撤销名称 | The dedesignations of the protected areas led to increased development. 取消对保护区的指定导致了开发的增加。|  The president announced the dedesignations of several national monuments. 总统宣布取消几个国家纪念地的称号。 | synonyms: removals, revocations, annulments | antonyms: designations, appointments, nominations | noun

Etymology:  "de-" (prefix meaning "removal, reversal") + "designation" (from Latin "designatio," meaning "a marking out, appointment").  The prefix "de-" negates the action of "designation," resulting in the meaning of "removal of a designation."
