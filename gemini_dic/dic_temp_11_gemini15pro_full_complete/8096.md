anthropophagy:/ˌænθrəˈpɒfədʒi/
n.
食人，同类相食
The practice of eating human flesh by human beings.
Cannibalism is a taboo in almost every culture.  食人在几乎所有文化中都是禁忌。
The Donner Party resorted to anthropophagy to survive the harsh winter. 唐纳党为了在严冬中生存下去， resorted to anthropophagy。
近义词:cannibalism
反义词:N/A
词性:noun


anthropophagous:/ˌænθrəˈpɒfəɡəs/
adj.
食人的
Relating to or characterized by anthropophagy.
Some ancient tribes were believed to have been anthropophagous.  一些古代部落被认为是食人的。
The anthropophagous practices of the tribe shocked the explorers.  这个部落的食人习俗震惊了探险家们。
近义词:cannibalistic
反义词:N/A
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek  *ánthrōpos* (human being) + *phagein* (to eat).
