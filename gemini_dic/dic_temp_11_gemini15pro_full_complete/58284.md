drupaceous:/druːˈpeɪʃəs/
|adj.|核果的；似核果的|Relating to, or resembling a drupe, a fleshy fruit with thin skin and a central stone containing the seed, like a peach, plum, or cherry.|Peaches, plums, and cherries are drupaceous fruits. 桃子、李子和樱桃都是核果。|The tree bears small, drupaceous fruits. 这棵树结小核果。|近义词：stone-fruited|反义词:|词性:形容词
