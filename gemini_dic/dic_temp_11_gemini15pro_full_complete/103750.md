jouk:/dʒuːk/ | v. | 躲避，闪避，弯腰|To stoop down quickly, especially to avoid being hit or seen. | He jouked to avoid the low-hanging branch. 他弯腰躲过了低垂的树枝。|近义词：duck, dodge, stoop, crouch |反义词: stand, expose |词性:动词

词源:  From Scots *jouk*, of uncertain origin, perhaps related to Old Norse *júka* (“to bend, bow”).
