bathygraphic:/ˌbæθɪˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
水深测量的；海底地形的
relating to the measurement of the depths of oceans or other large bodies of water.
The bathygraphic survey revealed a previously unknown trench.  水深测量揭示了一条以前不为人知的沟壑。

The expedition used bathygraphic data to create a detailed map of the seabed.  探险队利用水深数据绘制了详细的海底地图。
近义词:hydrographic
反义词: (No common antonym)
词性:adjective
