fumidity:/fjuːˈmɪdɪti/
n.|潮湿，湿气|The fumidity of the tropical rainforest was almost unbearable.  热带雨林的潮湿几乎令人难以忍受。|The high fumidity made the air feel thick and heavy. 高湿度使空气感觉又闷又重。|近义词：dampness, humidity, moisture|反义词：aridity, dryness|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *fumiditas, from Latin fumidus (“smoky, steaming”), from fumus (“smoke, steam”).  来自晚期拉丁语 *fumiditas，源自拉丁语 fumidus（“烟雾弥漫的，冒着蒸汽的”），来自 fumus（“烟，蒸汽”）。
