overloup:/ˌoʊvərˈluːp/ /ˌəʊvəˈluːp/ | n. v. | 重叠，交叠；重复 | An overlap is a shared area or part between two or more things.  As a verb, it means to partially cover something or extend over something. |
The shingles on the roof should overlap. 屋顶上的瓦片应该重叠。
There are some overlaps between the two projects. 这两个项目之间有一些重叠的部分。
Their responsibilities overlap. 他们的职责重叠。
The two events overlap. 这两个事件有重叠的时间。
| 近义词: intersection, convergence, coincide, duplicate, repeat | 反义词: diverge, separate | 词性: 名词, 动词


Etymology: "over" + "lap" (Old English *læppa, related to "lobe") meaning a loose flap of something
