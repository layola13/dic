acidity:/əˈsɪdəti/ | n. |酸度，酸性|The acidity of the soil is too high for most plants. 土壤的酸度对大多数植物来说太高了。|The wine has a pleasant acidity. 这酒的酸度适中。|近义词：sourness, tartness|反义词: alkalinity, basicity|noun

acidity:/əˈsɪdəti/ | n. |胃酸过多；胃灼热|He suffers from acidity after eating spicy food. 他吃了辛辣食物后会胃酸过多。|She took an antacid to relieve her acidity. 她服用了抗酸剂来缓解胃灼热。|近义词：heartburn, indigestion|反义词: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *aciditas, from Latin acidus (“sour, sharp”), from acēre (“to be sour”).
