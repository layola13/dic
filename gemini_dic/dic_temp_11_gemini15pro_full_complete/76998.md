gardenlike: /ˈɡɑːrdənˌlaɪk/ | adj. |像花园的，花园似的 | Resembling a garden; having the qualities of a garden, such as being pleasant, orderly, or productive. |  The small town had a gardenlike atmosphere, with flowers blooming everywhere.  这个小镇有一种花园般的气氛，到处鲜花盛开。| synonyms:  gardenesque,  picturesque,  pleasant,  orderly,  lush,  verdant | antonyms:  barren,  desolate,  unpleasant,  chaotic,  overgrown | adjective


Etymology:
From "garden" + "-like"
