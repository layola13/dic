albino:/ælˈbaɪnoʊ/ /ælˈbiːnəʊ/
|n.|白化病患者；白化体|A person or animal with albinism.|例句：The albino rabbit stood out against the dark grass. 白化兔在深色的草地上很显眼。

例句：Researchers are studying the genetic causes of albinism in humans. 研究人员正在研究人类白化病的遗传原因。
|近义词：none (for the condition itself, though specific animals/plants might have similar appearances)|反义词：none|词性：noun


albinic:/ælˈbɪnɪk/
|adj.|白化的|Of, relating to, or affected with albinism.|例句：The albinic deer was a rare sight. 白化的鹿是一种罕见的景象。

例句：The scientist studied the albinic plants to understand their lack of pigment. 科学家研究了白化植物以了解它们缺乏色素的原因。
|近义词：leucistic, amelanistic|反义词：pigmented|词性：adjective


albinism:/ˈælbɪˌnɪzəm/
|n.|白化病|A genetic condition characterized by a deficiency or absence of pigment in the skin, hair, and eyes.|例句：Albinism affects people of all races. 白化病影响所有种族的人。

例句：People with albinism are often sensitive to sunlight. 白化病患者通常对阳光敏感。
|近义词：achromasia, achromatosis|反义词：none|词性：noun


词源: From the Portuguese *albino*, from *albo* "white", ultimately from Latin *albus* "white".
