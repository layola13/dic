noncommodious:/ˌnɒnkəˈməʊdiəs/
adjective
不宽敞的，狭小的
lacking sufficient space; not commodious.
The noncommodious apartment felt cramped with all our furniture.  这套不宽敞的公寓放了我们所有的家具后感觉很拥挤。
The attic room was noncommodious and poorly lit. 阁楼房间狭小，光线也不好。
近义词: cramped, confined, small, restricted, tight
反义词: commodious, spacious, roomy, ample
