noncredibleness:/ˌnɒnˌkrɛdəˈbɪlnəs/
n.
不可信；不足信
The noncredibleness of his story made it difficult to believe. 他故事的不可信性让人难以相信。
The noncredibleness of the witness's testimony led to the acquittal of the defendant.  证人证词的不可信导致被告被判无罪。
近义词：unbelievability, implausibility, improbability
反义词：credibility, believability, plausibility
词性：名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + credible + -ness (suffix forming nouns of state or quality)
From Latin non- "not" + credibilis "worthy of belief," from credere "to believe."
