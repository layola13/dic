burganet:/ˈbɜːrɡənɪt/| n. |轻盔，铁帽|A light helmet, usually of plate iron, often worn under a heavier helmet.  It typically protected the head and neck and sometimes the face. |The knight wore a burganet under his great helm. 骑士在大头盔下戴着轻盔。|Synonyms: sallet, bascinet, helmet |Antonyms: None (lack of a specific antonym for a type of helmet) |Noun
