被动语态:
This tense doesn't apply directly to the possessive pronoun "notre."  However, it can be used with verbs in the passive voice:  Notre voiture a été réparée. (Our car has been repaired.)

PHRASE->

These phrases don't typically incorporate "notre" directly as it's a possessive pronoun. However, here are some examples showing how it might be used within a larger phrase:

1.  Dans notre cas... (In our case...)
2.  Pour notre part... (For our part...)
3.  Selon notre opinion... (In our opinion...)
4.  À notre avis... (In our view...)
5.  De notre côté... (On our side...)


注意事项:

"Notre" is specifically used before masculine plural nouns.  The feminine singular is "notre," the masculine singular is "notre," and the feminine plural is "nos."  "Nos" is also used before plural nouns beginning with a vowel or silent 'h'.
