boardrooms:/ˈbɔːrdruːmz/ /ˈbɔːrdrʊmz/
n.
董事会会议室；高级管理层
A room where a company's board of directors meets.
The CEO made the announcement in the boardroom. 首席执行官在董事会议室宣布了这一消息。

The boardrooms of corporate America. 美国企业的董事会会议室。


近义词：meeting rooms, conference rooms
反义词：（无直接反义词）
词性：名词(plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From board + room.  "Board" in this sense refers to a group of directors or advisors.  "Room" refers to the space where they meet.
