grapple's:/ˈɡræpəlz/ | n. | 抓钩; 抓住; 扭打; | This is the grapple's hook. 这是抓钩的钩子。| 近义词:grip, grasp, wrestle | 反义词:release, let go | 词性:名词 (Noun), 更确切地说是名词所有格


ETYMOLOGY->

From Middle French *grappeler*, frequentative of *graper*, from Frankish *krappon* (“to seize, grasp”), from Proto-Germanic *krappōną* (“to seize, grasp, hook”), from Proto-Indo-European *gerbʰ- (“to scratch, scrape, carve”).  Cognate with Old High German *chrappon*, *krapfon* (“to seize, grasp”), Dutch *krabben* (“to scratch, claw, scrape”), German *krabben* (“to scratch, claw, scrape”), English *crab*.
