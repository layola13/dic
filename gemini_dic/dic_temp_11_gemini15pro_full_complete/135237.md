nonpalatal:/nɒnˈpælətəl/ | adj. | 非腭音的 |  Not relating to or produced by the palate; not palatal.  A nonpalatal sound is made without the body of the tongue touching the hard palate. | The sound /t/ in "ten" is nonpalatal.  /t/ 在 "ten" 中是非腭音的。| The phoneme /k/ can be either palatal or nonpalatal depending on the following vowel. /k/ 这个音素根据后面的元音可以是腭音的也可以是非腭音的。 |  velar, non-palatalized | palatal | adjective
