goniatite:/ˈɡoʊniəˌtaɪt/ | n. | 戈尼亞特菊石 | An extinct marine mollusk of the order Goniatitida, related to the ammonites, that existed from the Devonian to the Permian periods and had a shell with simple, angular sutures. |  Example 1: The goniatite fossil provided evidence of the ancient sea that once covered the region.  这块戈尼亞特菊石化石证明了这片区域曾经被一片古老的海洋覆盖。

Example 2:  Goniatites are valuable index fossils for dating Devonian and Carboniferous rocks. 戈尼亞特菊石是确定泥盆纪和石炭纪岩石年代的珍贵标准化石。 | 近义词: ammonoid (广义的菊石),  cephalopod (头足类动物) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


goniatitic:/ˌɡoʊniəˈtɪtɪk/ | adj. | 戈尼亞特菊石的 | Relating to or characteristic of goniatites. | Example 1: The goniatitic sutures were clearly visible on the well-preserved fossil.  保存完好的化石上清晰可见戈尼亞特菊石式的缝合线。

Example 2: The paleontologist identified the fossil as a goniatitic ammonoid.  古生物学家将这块化石鉴定为一种戈尼亞特菊石类的菊石。| 近义词: ammonoidal | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin *Goniatites,  from Ancient Greek γωνία (gōnía, “angle”) + -ites.  The name refers to the angular shape of the sutures on the shell.
