electrometry:/ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒmətri/ | n. |静电测量；电量测量|The science of measuring electrical quantities. It involves the use of instruments to measure voltage, current, resistance, and other electrical properties.| Electrometry is crucial for understanding the behavior of electrical circuits. 静电测量对于理解电路的行为至关重要。|Synonyms: electrical measurement, electrostatic measurement|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
