battlement: /ˈbæt(ə)lmənt/ | n. | 城垛；防卫墙 | A parapet at the top of a wall, usually notched or crenellated for defense. |  The castle's battlements offered a commanding view of the surrounding countryside. 城堡的城垛可以俯瞰周围的乡村。| Synonyms: parapet, rampart, fortification | Antonyms:  opening, breach | noun

battlement: /ˈbæt(ə)lmənt/ | vt. | 用城垛加强；设防 | To furnish with battlements. | They battlemented the walls of the fort for added protection. 他们在堡垒的墙上设置了城垛以增强防御。| Synonyms: fortify, strengthen, protect | Antonyms: weaken, expose | verb
