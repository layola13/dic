bistre:/ˈbɪstər/| n. adj.|灰褐色；用木炭制成的颜料|A brown pigment extracted from soot; a yellowish-brown or grayish-brown color.|The artist used bistre to create a moody atmosphere in the drawing. 艺术家用灰褐色颜料在画中营造了一种忧郁的氛围。|近义词：brown, sepia, umber|反义词：white, vibrant colors|词性:名词, 形容词

Etymology: From French *bistre*, from the town of Bistre, Moldova, where the pigment was originally produced.
