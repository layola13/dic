oboe:/ˈoʊboʊ/ (US), /ˈəʊbəʊ/ (UK)| n. |双簧管|A woodwind instrument with a double reed and a conical bore, producing a penetrating tone.|The oboe solo in the second movement was particularly moving. 第二乐章的双簧管独奏尤其动人。|Synonyms:hautboy (archaic)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
