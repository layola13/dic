nonimperiously:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpɪəriəsli/
adverb
非专横地，不傲慢地
Not in an imperious manner; without arrogance or a commanding tone.
He nonimperiously suggested a different approach to the problem. 他不傲慢地提出了解决问题的不同方法。
She nonimperiously directed the team, earning their respect. 她以非专横的方式指导团队，赢得了他们的尊重。
近义词: humbly, modestly, unassumingly
反义词: imperiously, arrogantly, domineeringly
词性: 副词
