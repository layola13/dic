estuary:/ˈestʃʊəri/ (US), /ˈestʃʊəri/ (UK)| n. |河口，江口|A partly enclosed coastal body of water with one or more rivers or streams flowing into it, and with a free connection to the open sea.  A transition zone between river environments and maritime environments.|The Thames estuary is home to a variety of bird species. 泰晤士河口是多种鸟类的家园。|synonyms: inlet, firth, bay, sound|antonyms: headwater, source, upstream|noun
