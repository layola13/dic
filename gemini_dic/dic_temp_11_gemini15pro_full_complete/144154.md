outshined:/ˌaʊtˈʃaɪnd/
verb
胜过，使黯然失色
surpassed in achievement or excellence; eclipsed.
Her performance outshined all the others. 她的表演胜过所有其他人。
outshone:/ˌaʊtˈʃoʊn/
verb

surpassed in achievement or excellence; eclipsed.

antonym:pale, dim, fade
