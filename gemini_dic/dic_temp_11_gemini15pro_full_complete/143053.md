outbluster: /aʊtˈblʌstər/
verb
恐吓，威吓；以大声压过
to overcome or attempt to overcome by blustering; to roar down.
Example: He tried to outbluster his opponent during the debate. 他试图在辩论中以气势压倒对手。

Example: The wind outblustered the speaker's voice. 风声盖过了演讲者的声音。

近义词: intimidate, cow, browbeat, bully, hector
反义词: cajole, coax, persuade, soothe
词性: verb
