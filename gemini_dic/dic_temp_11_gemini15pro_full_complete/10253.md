apotheosis:/əˌpɑːθiˈoʊsɪs/ /əˌpɒθiˈəʊsɪs/| n.|神化，典范，巅峰|The apotheosis of Romanticism was the poetry of Keats and Shelley.  浪漫主义的巅峰是济慈和雪莱的诗歌。| elevation, culmination, peak, climax |decline, downfall, nadir|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin apotheōsis, from Ancient Greek ἀποθέωσις (apotheōsis, “deification”), from ἀποθεόω (apotheóō, “I deify”), from ἀπό (apó, “from, away”) + θεός (theós, “god”).
