baa'd:/bɑːd/ | adj. | 绵羊叫声的；像绵羊叫的 |  Making a sound like a sheep. It imitates the bleating sound a sheep makes. | The lamb baa'd loudly for its mother. 小羊羔大声地咩咩叫着找妈妈。| 近义词: bleating, blatting | 反义词: silent, quiet | 词性:形容词

词源:  Onomatopoeic, imitating the sound a sheep makes.
