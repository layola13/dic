counter-motion:/ˈkaʊntərˈmoʊʃən/ | n. | 反向运动，对抗性动作 | An opposing or offsetting motion; a motion made in opposition to another. |  The swimmer used a counter-motion with her arms to stay afloat. 游泳者用手臂的反向运动来保持漂浮。

The lawyer filed a counter-motion to the plaintiff's request. 律师对原告的请求提出了反诉。 | 近义词：countermove, reaction, opposition | 反义词: motion, action | 词性: noun

counter-motion:/ˈkaʊntərˈmoʊʃən/ | n. | 对抗性议案 | A motion made in opposition to another, especially in a legislative or legal setting. | The defense presented a counter-motion to dismiss the case. 辩方提出驳回此案的反诉。

The city council voted on the counter-motion to postpone the development project. 市议会投票表决了推迟开发项目的反议案。| 近义词：counterproposal, counterclaim | 反义词: proposal, motion | 词性: noun
