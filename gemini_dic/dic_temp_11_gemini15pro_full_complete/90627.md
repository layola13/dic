humanist:/ˈhjuːmənɪst/| n. adj.| 人文主义者，人道主义者；人文主义的|A humanist is someone who believes in humanism.  A humanist approach emphasizes human reason and values.|例句：She is a well-known humanist and advocate for social justice. 她是一位著名的人文主义者和社会正义的倡导者。|近义词：humanitarian, philosopher, freethinker|反义词:bigot, fanatic|词性:noun, adjective

humanist:/ˈhjuːmənɪst/|adj.|人文主义的|Relating to or supporting the principles of humanism.|例句：He took a humanist approach to education, focusing on the individual student's needs. 他采取了一种人文主义的教育方法，关注每个学生的 individual needs。|近义词：humanistic|反义词:spiritual, religious|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: via French *humaniste* from late Latin *humanista*, from *humanitas* 'human nature, culture' (see humanity).
