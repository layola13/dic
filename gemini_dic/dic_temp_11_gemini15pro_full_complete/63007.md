enpatron:/ɛnˈpeɪtrən/ |v. |以恩主身份对待；以赞助人身份对待|To act as a patron to; to support or sponsor. |He enpatroned the young artist, providing him with a studio and materials.| 近义词: sponsor, support, back, champion | 反义词: obstruct, hinder, oppose |词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From en- (meaning "to put into" or "to cause to be") + patron (meaning "protector" or "supporter").  The word "patron" ultimately comes from the Latin "pater," meaning "father."
