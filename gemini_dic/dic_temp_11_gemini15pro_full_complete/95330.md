incensation:/ˌɪnsɛnˈseɪʃən/ | n. |焚香；香气；激怒|The act of burning incense, especially as part of a religious ceremony. Also, the perfume or smoke produced by burning incense. Can also refer to a feeling of extreme anger or exasperation.|例句：The incensation filled the temple with a mystical aroma. 寺庙里充满了神秘的香气。

例句：His constant criticism was an incensation to her. 他不断的批评让她感到非常恼火。|近义词：fumigation, aroma, perfume, rage, fury|反义词:calm, peace|Noun
