erstwhile:/ˈɜːrstˌwaɪl/ | adj. | 从前的，以前的 | Former; previously. |  The erstwhile friends had a falling out. 从前的那些朋友闹翻了。|  例句：The erstwhile champion was defeated in the first round.  从前的冠军在第一轮就被击败了。 | 近义词：former, previous, onetime, past, ex- | 反义词：present, current, future | 词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *erstwhile, equivalent to erst (“formerly”) + while (“time”). Cognate with German einstweilen (“meanwhile”).
