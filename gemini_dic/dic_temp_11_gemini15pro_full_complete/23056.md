bouncier: /ˈbaʊnsiər/ (US), /ˈbaʊnsɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更有弹性的，更活泼的|Comparative form of bouncy: more bouncy.  Something that is bouncier has more spring or resilience.|The new mattress is much bouncier than the old one.  新床垫比旧床垫更有弹性。|The children were bouncier after their nap. 孩子们午睡后更有活力。|Near synonyms: springier, more resilient, livelier, more energetic|Near antonyms: less bouncy, stiffer, less resilient, less energetic|adjective
