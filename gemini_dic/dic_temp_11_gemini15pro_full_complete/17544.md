beatification:/ˌbiːætɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 福佑；宣福礼 | The official act of the Catholic Church of declaring a deceased person blessed (beatified) and thus worthy of limited veneration.  This is a step on the path to canonization (sainthood). | The beatification of Mother Teresa was a momentous occasion for Catholics around the world. 特蕾莎修女的宣福礼对于全世界的信徒来说都是一个重要的时刻。|  canonization, declaration of blessedness |  excommunication, condemnation | noun
