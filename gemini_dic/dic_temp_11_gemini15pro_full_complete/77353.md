gaudish:/ˈɡɔːdɪʃ/| adj.|俗丽的；华而不实的；炫耀的|Excessively showy or bright in a tasteless way; ostentatious.|例句：She wore a gaudish dress to the party. 她穿了一件俗丽的裙子去参加聚会。

例句：The decorations were a bit too gaudish for my taste.  对我来说，这些装饰有点过于华而不实了。|近义词：garish, flashy, showy, ostentatious, flamboyant|反义词：understated, subtle, elegant, refined, simple|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *gawdyssh, from Anglo-Norman gaudis, plural of gaudi (“joy, rejoicing, ornament”), from Latin gaudium (“joy, gladness”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *gāu- (“to rejoice”).
