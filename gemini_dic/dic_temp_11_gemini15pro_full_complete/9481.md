antiritual:/ˌæntiˈrɪtʃuəl/
adj.|反仪式的，非传统的|Opposed to or lacking rituals; unconventional or unorthodox.|Her wedding was deliberately antiritual, a simple ceremony on a beach.|近义词:unconventional, unorthodox, informal, nonconformist, iconoclastic|反义词:ritualistic, traditional, conventional, orthodox, ceremonial|词性:形容词

antiritual:/ˌæntiˈrɪtʃuəl/
n.|反仪式，反传统行为|An act or practice that is opposed to or rejects traditional rituals.|His antiritual involved burning effigies of authority figures.|近义词:rebellion, dissent, protest, defiance|反义词:ritual, ceremony, observance, tradition|词性:名词
