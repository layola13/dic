ding:/dɪŋ/
n.|叮当声，敲钟声|a ringing sound, like a bell|The ding of the doorbell announced a visitor. 门铃叮当一声，宣告有客人来访。|近义词: chime, clang, ring|反义词: silence|词性:名词

ding:/dɪŋ/
vt.|（反复）强调，灌输|to repeat or emphasize something persistently|He kept dinging into me the importance of hard work. 他不停地向我灌输努力工作的重要性。|近义词:  emphasize, reiterate, repeat, inculcate |反义词: ignore, neglect |词性:动词
