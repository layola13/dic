hyperdevelopmental:/ˌhaɪpərˌdɪˌveləpˈmentl/
adjective
过度发展的，超常发展的
Characterized by accelerated or excessive development, often beyond what is considered typical or normal.
例句：The child showed hyperdevelopmental traits in language acquisition.  这个孩子在语言习得方面表现出超常发展的特征。

例句：Researchers are studying the hyperdevelopmental patterns observed in some gifted children. 研究人员正在研究在一些天才儿童身上观察到的超常发展模式。
近义词：accelerated, advanced, precocious
反义词：delayed, underdeveloped, retarded
词性: adjective

Etymology:
hyper- (prefix): from Greek, meaning "over, above, beyond"
developmental (adjective): from develop (verb), ultimately from Old French *desveloper, "to unwrap, unfold"
