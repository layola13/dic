enticer:/ɪnˈtaɪsər/ | n. |诱惑者；引诱者|Someone who entices or lures another person to do something, often something immoral or illegal.|例句：The enticer promised riches and fame.  诱惑者许诺了财富和名望。

例句：The police were able to catch the enticer who had been preying on young girls. 警察抓住了那个一直在捕猎年轻女孩的诱惑者。|近义词：tempter, seducer, lure, bait|反义词：dissuader, preventer|词性:noun
