decursive:/dɪˈkɜːrsɪv/
adjective
推论的；演绎的；递归的
Proceeding by reasoning from premises to conclusions.  Derived or deduced from something known or assumed; discursive.
The philosopher presented a decursive argument for the existence of God. 哲学家提出了一个演绎上帝存在的论证。
Synonyms: deductive, inferential, discursive, reasoned
Antonyms: inductive, intuitive
词性: 形容词
