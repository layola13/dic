deathlessness:/ˈdeθləsnəs/
n.
不死，不朽
The state or quality of being deathless; immortality.  
His poems speak of the deathlessness of love. 他的诗歌歌颂了爱情的永恒。
Synonyms: immortality, eternity, permanence, everlastingness
Antonyms: mortality, finiteness, temporality
