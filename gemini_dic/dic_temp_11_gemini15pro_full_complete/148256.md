oversleeps:/ˌoʊvərˈsliːps/ | v. | 睡过头 | Sleeps longer than intended, especially past the time one planned to wake up. | He often oversleeps on weekends and misses breakfast. 他周末经常睡过头，错过早餐。 |  I oversleep and was late for work. 我睡过头了，上班迟到了。 | 近义词：sleep in, sleep late | 反义词：wake up early, get up early | 词性: 动词
