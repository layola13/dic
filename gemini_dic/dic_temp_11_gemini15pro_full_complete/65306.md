eudialyte:/juːˈdaɪəlaɪt/ | n. | 硅锆石 |A relatively rare silicate mineral containing sodium, calcium, cerium, iron, manganese, yttrium, and zirconium.  | Eudialyte is a sought-after mineral by collectors. 硅锆石是收藏家追捧的矿物。| Eudialyte can be found in alkaline igneous rocks. 硅锆石可以在碱性火成岩中找到。|  Synonyms: None (highly specific mineral) | Antonyms: N/A | noun
