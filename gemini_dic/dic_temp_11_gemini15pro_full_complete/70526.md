filialness:/ˈfɪliəlness/ | n. | 孝顺，孝心 | The quality of being respectful and obedient to one's parents. |  Her filialness was evident in the way she cared for her aging mother. 她对年迈母亲的照顾方式体现了她的孝顺。|  She demonstrated great filialness by always putting her family's needs before her own. 她总是把家庭的需求放在自己的需求之前，展现了极大的孝顺。| Synonyms: piety, devotion, obedience | Antonyms: disrespect, disobedience | noun
