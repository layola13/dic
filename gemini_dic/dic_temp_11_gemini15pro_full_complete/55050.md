disquisitionist:/ˌdɪskwɪˈzɪʃənɪst/
n.
专题论文作者；长篇大论者
A person who writes or delivers long, formal speeches or treatises, often on a scholarly subject.
The disquisitionist delivered a three-hour lecture on the history of philosophy.  专题论文作者就哲学史发表了长达三个小时的演讲。
Synonyms:  scholar, academic, intellectual, theorist, pundit
Antonyms:  dilettante, amateur
词性: noun
