fiendishness:/ˈfiːndɪʃnəs/
n.
极度的邪恶; 恶魔般的残酷; 凶残
The fiendishness of the crime shocked the community.  这起罪行的凶残震惊了整个社区。
Extreme wickedness or cruelty; devilishness.
The dictator's fiendishness was evident in his treatment of political prisoners.  独裁者对政治犯的待遇显露出了他的凶残。
近义词：cruelty, brutality, savagery, wickedness, malevolence
反义词：benevolence, kindness, compassion
词性：名词

词源:
From fiend + -ishness.  来自fiend（恶魔）+ -ishness（名词后缀，表示性质或状态）。
