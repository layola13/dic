excretory:/ˈɛkskrɪtɔːri/ (US), /ɪkˈskriːtəri/ (UK)
adjective|排泄的；排泄器官的|Relating to or functioning in excretion, the process of eliminating waste matter from the body.|The excretory system removes waste products from the body.  排泄系统排出体内的废物。|The kidneys play a vital role in the excretory process. 肾脏在排泄过程中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词:eliminative, secretory|反义词:retentive|词性:adjective
