monologist:/ˈmɒnəlɒdʒɪst/ (US) /ˈmɑːnəlɑːdʒɪst/| n.|独白者；进行独白表演的人|A person who delivers a monologue. |The comedian's act consisted of a series of hilarious monologues.  这位喜剧演员的表演由一系列滑稽的独白组成。|近义词：solo performer, speaker, reciter|反义词：dialogist, conversationalist|词性: noun

monologue:/ˈmɒnəlɒɡ/ (US) /ˈmɑːnəlɔːɡ/| n.|独白；独白表演|A long speech by one person in a play or film, or as part of a theatrical or broadcast programme. |Hamlet's famous "To be or not to be" speech is a classic monologue. 哈姆雷特的著名演讲“生存还是毁灭”是一段经典的独白。|近义词：soliloquy, speech, address|反义词：dialogue, conversation|词性: noun
