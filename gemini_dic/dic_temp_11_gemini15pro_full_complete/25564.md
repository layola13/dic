bum-someone-out:/bʌm ˈsʌmwʌn aʊt/
|phrasal verb|
|使某人沮丧，使某人扫兴|
|To make someone feel sad, disappointed, or depressed.|
|The bad news really bummed me out.|坏消息真的让我很沮丧。|
|The rainy weather bummed out the children, who had planned a picnic.|下雨的天气让孩子们很失望，他们本来计划去野餐。|
|depress, disappoint, sadden, upset|
|cheer up, elate, excite|
|verb phrase|
