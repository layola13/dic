ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hwæt, nominative and accusative neuter singular of hwā (“who”). Cognate with Scots quhat (“what”), West Frisian wat (“what”), Dutch wat (“what”), Low German wat (“what”), German was (“what”), Danish hvad (“what”), Swedish vad (“what”), Icelandic hvað (“what”), Gothic ƕa (“what”).
