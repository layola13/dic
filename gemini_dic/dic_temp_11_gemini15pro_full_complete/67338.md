extendable:/ɪkˈstendəbl/| adj.|可扩展的，可延长的|Able to be extended or made longer.|This table has extendable legs, making it suitable for larger gatherings.|近义词:expandable, adjustable, lengthenable|反义词:fixed, non-adjustable, rigid|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From extend + -able.  "Extend" comes from the Latin "extendere," meaning "to stretch out."  "-able" is a suffix meaning "capable of being."
