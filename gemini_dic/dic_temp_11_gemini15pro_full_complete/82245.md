groundage:/ˈɡraʊndɪdʒ/| n.|停泊费；地租|A charge for allowing a ship to remain at a wharf or dock; a charge for parking a vehicle.  Also, a rent or charge for the use of land. |The shipowner had to pay a hefty groundage fee for the extended stay at the port. 船主不得不为在港口延长的停留支付高额的停泊费。

The farmer charged a small groundage for allowing campers to stay on his land.  这位农民对允许露营者留在他的土地上收取少量地租。|近义词：dockage, wharfage, rent, land charge|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
