nonreciprocating:/ˌnɒnˌrɛsɪˈprɒkeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 非往复的，单向的 | Not reciprocating; not returning or corresponding to something done or given. | The pump uses a nonreciprocating mechanism.  这种泵使用非往复式机构。| The feeling was nonreciprocating; she loved him, but he didn't feel the same. 这种感觉是不对等的；她爱他，但他没有同样的感觉。 | Synonyms: unidirectional, one-way | Antonyms: reciprocating, two-way | adjective
