die-standing-up:/ˌdaɪ ˈstændɪŋ ˈʌp/
adj.
顽强不屈的；至死不渝的；宁死不屈的
Remaining active and engaged until the very end of one's life; refusing to give up or compromise one's principles.
He was a die-standing-up union man, fighting for workers' rights until his last breath. 他是一位宁死不屈的工会会员，直到最后一口气都在为工人权利而战。
Synonyms: unyielding, resolute, steadfast, tenacious
Antonyms: yielding, submissive, compliant
词性:形容词
