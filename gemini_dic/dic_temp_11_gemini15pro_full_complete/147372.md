overneglectfulness:/ˌoʊvər nɪˈɡlɛktfʊlnəs/ | n. | 过度疏忽，极度忽略 |The state of neglecting something or someone excessively.  A serious lack of attention or care. |  Her overneglectfulness of her garden led to a complete overgrowth of weeds. 她对花园的过度疏忽导致杂草丛生。|  negligence, disregard, inattention | care, attention, diligence | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
over- (prefix): From Old English *ofer, meaning "above," "across," "beyond."
neglect (verb/noun): From Latin neglectus, past participle of neglegere "disregard," from neg- "not" + legere "to gather, choose, read."
-ful (suffix):  Forms adjectives meaning "full of" or "characterized by."
-ness (suffix): Forms nouns from adjectives, denoting a state, quality, or condition.
