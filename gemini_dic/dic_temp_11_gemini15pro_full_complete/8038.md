anthropocentricities:/ˌænθrəpəʊˌsɛnˈtrɪsɪtiz/ | n. | 人类中心主义 | The belief that human beings are the most important entity in the universe.  It considers human values and experiences as the central and most significant. |  Modern society often displays anthropocentricities, prioritizing human needs over the health of the planet. 现代社会经常表现出人类中心主义，将人类需求置于地球健康之上。

The anthropocentricities inherent in some philosophical viewpoints can lead to environmental degradation. 某些哲学观点中固有的人类中心主义会导致环境恶化。 | 近义词: anthropocentrism, humanocentrism | 反义词: biocentrism, ecocentrism | 词性: 名词(plural)

词源:
From anthropocentrism + -ities (suffix forming plural nouns).  Anthropocentrism comes from Ancient Greek *ánthrōpos* (human being) + *kéntron* (center).
