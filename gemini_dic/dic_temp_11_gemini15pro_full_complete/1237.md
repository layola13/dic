accomplice: /əˈkɑːmplɪs/ /əˈkʌmplɪs/ | n. | 共犯，帮凶 | A person who helps another person commit a crime or do something wrong. |  He was arrested as an accomplice to murder. 他因作为谋杀案的帮凶而被捕。

The thief and his accomplices were caught by the police. 小偷和他的同伙被警察抓住了。

近义词: partner in crime, accessory, collaborator, abettor | 反义词: opponent, adversary, enemy | 词性: noun
