cut-to-the-chase:/kʌt tə ðə tʃeɪs/
idiom
开门见山；直奔主题；切入正题
To get to the point without wasting time on unnecessary details or preliminaries.
"We're running out of time, so let's cut to the chase." 我们时间不多了，所以让我们开门见山吧。
synonyms: get to the point, come to the point, get down to business, get down to brass tacks, skip the preliminaries
antonyms: beat around the bush, talk in circles, be vague
