artless:/ˈɑːrtlɪs/
adjective
自然的；不做作的；朴实的
Lacking art, skill, or finesse; natural and unartificial.  Free from guile or craft; ingenuous.
She gave an artless account of what had happened. 她朴实地讲述了事情的经过。
synonyms:natural, simple, innocent, naive, guileless, ingenuous, unpretentious, unsophisticated
antonyms: artful, cunning, sophisticated, pretentious
