bothy: /ˈbɒθi/ (UK), /ˈboʊθi/ (US) | n. | 小屋，棚屋 | A small hut or cottage, especially one used by farmworkers or for shelter on hills or mountains. | We stayed in a bothy overnight while hiking in the Scottish Highlands. 我们在苏格兰高地徒步旅行时在一个小屋里过夜。|  cabin, hut, shed | house, mansion | noun

Etymology: From Scottish Gaelic *bothan*, meaning "hut," related to Irish *botán*.
