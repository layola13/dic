kaizens:/kaɪˈzɛnz/| n. |改进; 改善; (尤指商业管理中的)持续改进，改善|A Japanese business philosophy of continuous improvement.|The company implemented several kaizens to improve efficiency. 公司实施了几项改善措施以提高效率。|Synonyms: improvements, enhancements, betterments|Antonyms: deteriorations, declines, regressions|noun
