catch-up:/ˈkætʃʌp/ (US), /ˈkætʃʌp/ (UK)| n.| 补上, 赶上; 追赶; 弥补落后; (增进友谊的)叙旧; 番茄酱|A meeting to discuss the progress of a project and catch up on any missed deadlines. 一次会议，讨论项目进展，并赶上任何错过的截止日期。|近义词：update, review, ketchup (for tomato ketchup)|反义词:fall behind|noun


catch-up:/ˈkætʃʌp/ (US), /ˈkætʃʌp/ (UK)| adj.| 追赶的; 弥补落后的|I have a lot of catch-up work to do after my vacation.  我度假回来后有很多工作要补。|近义词：remedial, supplementary|反义词:advanced, ahead|adjective
