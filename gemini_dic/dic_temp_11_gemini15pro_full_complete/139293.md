oblivious:/əˈblɪviəs/ | adj. | 遗忘的，健忘的；不注意的，不在意的 | Not aware of or not concerned about what is happening around one. |  She was completely oblivious to the stares of the other passengers. 她完全没有注意到其他乘客的目光。 | oblivious to the noise | He was oblivious to the danger he was in. 他没有意识到自己身处险境。 | 近义词：unaware, unmindful, ignorant, insensible | 反义词：aware, conscious, mindful | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *oblivius, from oblivīscī (“to forget”), from ob- (“over, upon”) + -līvīscī, related to līvor (“bluish color”).  The sense development is from "forgetful" to "unmindful".
