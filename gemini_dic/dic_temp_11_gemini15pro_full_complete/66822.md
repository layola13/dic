expansively:/ɪkˈspæn.sɪv.li/
adv.
膨胀地，广阔地，开阔地
in a way that covers a large area or includes many things
He smiled expansively. 他笑容满面。
The company is planning to expand expansively into new markets.  该公司正计划大举拓展新市场。
近义词: broadly, widely, extensively
反义词: narrowly, restrictively, limitedly
词性:副词

expansive:/ɪkˈspæn.sɪv/
adj.
广阔的，浩瀚的；广泛的；健谈的，开朗的
covering a large area; including many things; (of a person) showing feelings openly and enthusiastically
There was an expansive view from the hotel balcony. 从酒店的阳台上可以看到广阔的景色。
He was in an expansive mood after winning the award. 获奖后，他心情舒畅。
近义词: broad, wide, extensive, comprehensive, outgoing, communicative
反义词: narrow, restricted, limited, reserved, introverted
词性:形容词
