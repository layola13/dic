blub:/blʌb/
n.|哭泣，呜咽；气泡；错误|A sound made when crying or sobbing; a bubble; a mistake or blunder.|He let out a loud blub when he realized he'd lost his toy. 当他意识到自己弄丢了玩具时，他大声哭了起来。|近义词：sob, weep, cry, bubble, blunder|反义词:laugh, rejoice|词性:名词

blub:/blʌb/
v.|哭泣，呜咽；发出汩汩声|To cry or sob; to make a bubbling sound.|The baby blubbed quietly in its crib. 婴儿在婴儿床里小声地呜咽着。|近义词：sob, weep, cry, gurgle|反义词:laugh, rejoice|词性:动词
