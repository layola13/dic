enthymematic:/ˌɛnθaɪˈmɛmætɪk/ | adj. |  省略推理的，隐含前提的 | Based on an enthymeme; containing an enthymeme. Characterized by argumentation based on enthymemes, where one or more premises are implied rather than explicitly stated. |  Her argument was enthymematic, relying on shared cultural assumptions. 她的论证是省略推理的，依赖于共同的文化假设。 |  The politician's speech was highly enthymematic, appealing to the audience's patriotism without explicitly stating it. 这位政治家的演讲非常具有隐含前提的性质，它诉诸听众的爱国主义，却没有明确地陈述出来。 |  implicit, tacit, elliptical | explicit, detailed, comprehensive | adjective
