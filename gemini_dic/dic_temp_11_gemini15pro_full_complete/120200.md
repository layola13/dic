miner:/ˈmaɪnər/ (US), /ˈmaɪnə(r)/ (UK)| n.|矿工；未成年人|A person who works in a mine, extracting minerals; a person under the legal age of majority.|The miners worked long hours in dangerous conditions. 矿工们在危险的环境中长时间工作。|Synonyms: digger, pitman, collier (for coal miners)|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps employer, executive)|Noun
