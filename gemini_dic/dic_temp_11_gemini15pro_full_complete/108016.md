last-minute:/ˌlæst ˈmɪnɪt/ /ˌlɑːst ˈmɪnət/| adj.|最后一刻的；临时的；仓促的|done, made, or occurring at the last possible moment before something happens|例句：We had to make some last-minute changes to the plan. 我们不得不对计划做一些最后的修改。|近义词：last-second, eleventh-hour, down-to-the-wire|反义词：prearranged, planned, advance|词性:形容词
