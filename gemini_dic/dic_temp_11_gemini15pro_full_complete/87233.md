heresiography:/ˈhɛrɪsiˈɒɡrəfi/ | n. |异端学说，异端邪说研究|The study of heresies, especially their origins, beliefs, and influence.|His new book delves into the heresiography of the early Church.  他的新书深入研究了早期教会的异端学说。|近义词：heterodoxy, unorthodoxy|反义词：orthodoxy|词性: 名词

Etymology: From Ancient Greek αἵρεσις (haíresis, “choice, sect, heresy”) + -graphy.
