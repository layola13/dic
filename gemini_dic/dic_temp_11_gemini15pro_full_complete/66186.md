excheator:/ɪkˈskiːtər/| n.|遗产估价员；（旧时）税务官|An official formerly appointed in each English county to look after the property of deceased persons who had died intestate or whose heirs were minors.  旧时英国各郡指派的一名官员，负责照看无遗嘱死亡者或继承人为未成年人的财产。|The excheator was responsible for valuing the estate. 遗产估价员负责估价遗产。|Synonyms: administrator, executor, official|Antonyms: heir, inheritor|noun
