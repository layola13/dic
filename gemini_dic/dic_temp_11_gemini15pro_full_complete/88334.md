hippocratic:/ˌhɪpəˈkrætɪk/| adj.|希波克拉底的；医德的；冷静的|Of or relating to Hippocrates, the Greek physician, or his medical principles.  It also refers to a calm demeanor, especially in the face of pain or death, attributed to Hippocrates.|The doctor took the Hippocratic oath. 医生宣誓了希波克拉底誓言。|Synonyms: medical, clinical, physicianly|Antonyms: unprofessional, unethical|adjective

hippocratic:/ˌhɪpəˈkrætɪk/| n.|希波克拉底学派的人|A follower of Hippocrates or his medical principles.|He was a true Hippocratic, dedicated to the well-being of his patients. 他是一位真正的希波克拉底学派的人，致力于病人的福祉。|Synonyms: physician, doctor, healer|Antonyms: charlatan, quack|noun
