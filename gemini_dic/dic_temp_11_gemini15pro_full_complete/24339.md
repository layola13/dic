brimless:/ˈbrɪmlɪs/| adj.|无边的，没有帽檐的|Lacking a brim; without a brim, as of a hat.|She wore a brimless hat to the beach. 她戴着一顶无檐帽去了海滩。|近义词:unbrimmed|反义词:brimmed|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From brim + -less.  "Brim" comes from Old English "brymm" meaning edge, rim, brink.  "-less" is a suffix meaning without.
