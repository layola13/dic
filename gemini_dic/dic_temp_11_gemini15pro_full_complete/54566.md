dislodging:/dɪsˈlɒdʒɪŋ/
verb
使移位；取出；逐出
removing something from a fixed position
The earthquake dislodged several boulders from the cliff face. 地震使几块巨石从悬崖面上移位。
remove, displace, shift, extract
fix, secure, embed, lodge
v.
