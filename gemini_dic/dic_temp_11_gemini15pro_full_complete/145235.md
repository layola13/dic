overbills:/ˌoʊvərˈbɪlz/| v. n.|过高收费，超额账单|To charge someone more than the correct amount.|The contractor overbilled us for the renovations.  承包商对我们的装修费用多收了钱。|Example:  The restaurant overbilled us by $20.  这家餐馆多收了我们20美元。|Synonyms: overcharge, inflate|Antonyms: undercharge, discount|verb

overbills:/ˌoʊvərˈbɪlz/| n.|超额账单|A bill that is higher than it should be.|We received an overbill for our electricity usage. 我们收到了电费的超额账单。|Example: The overbills from the phone company were a recurring problem. 这家电话公司的超额账单是一个反复出现的问题。|Synonyms: excessive bill, inflated bill|Antonyms: underbill, discounted bill|noun
