aplanatic:/ˌeɪpləˈnætɪk/ | adj. | 消球差的，不晕的 |  Free from spherical aberration and coma; Corrected for both spherical and coma aberrations. An aplanatic lens or mirror forms a sharp, undistorted image. | An aplanatic lens is essential for high-quality microscopy.  消球差透镜对于高质量显微镜至关重要。 |  achromatic, corrected |  distorted, aberrated | adjective
