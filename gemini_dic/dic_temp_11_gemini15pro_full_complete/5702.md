alumish:/əˈlʌmɪʃ/
adjective
像明矾的；略带涩味的；含明矾的
Resembling or containing alum; slightly astringent.
The water tasted slightly alumish.  这水尝起来有点像明矾。
The alumish taste of the unripe fruit made her pucker.  未成熟水果的涩味让她皱起了眉头。
Near synonyms: astringent, tart, metallic
Near antonyms: sweet, bland
词性: 形容词
