oecology:/iːˈkɒlədʒi/ | n. | 生态学 | The study of the relationships between organisms and their environment. | Ecology plays a crucial role in understanding the natural world. 生态学在理解自然界中起着至关重要的作用。|  例句：The oecology of the rainforest is incredibly complex. 雨林的生态极其复杂。| 近义词: ecology | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From German Ökologie, from Greek oikos (house, dwelling) + -logie (-logy).
