diphygenic:/ˌdaɪfaɪˈdʒɛnɪk/
adjective
双源的，二源发生的
Originating from two sources or lines of descent.
Diphygenic inheritance involves genetic material from two distinct sources. 双源遗传涉及来自两个不同来源的遗传物质。
The diphygenic theory suggests that the human race arose from two separate ancestral populations. 双源论认为人类起源于两个独立的祖先群体。
近义词:bigenetic, dual origin
反义词:monophyletic, single origin
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *di- "two" + phylon "race, tribe" + -genic.
