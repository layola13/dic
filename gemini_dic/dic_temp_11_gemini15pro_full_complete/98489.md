instalment:/ɪnˈstɔːlmənt/ (US) /ɪnˈstɔːlmənt/ (UK)| n. |分期付款；部分；（分期连载的）一期|A payment made as one of a series for something bought on credit; one of several parts of a story, movie, or television program that is published or broadcast at different times.|She paid for the car in monthly instalments. 她按月分期付款买下了这辆车。|The first instalment of the trilogy will be released next month. 三部曲的第一部将于下个月上映。|synonyms: payment, portion, part, installment (US), chapter|antonyms: lump sum, whole|noun
