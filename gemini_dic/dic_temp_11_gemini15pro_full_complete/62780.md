engraving:/ɪnˈɡreɪvɪŋ/
n.|雕刻,雕刻品|The act or art of cutting designs, letters, etc., into a hard surface, or the design produced by this process. |The museum has a wonderful collection of  18th-century engravings.  博物馆收藏了大量18世纪的雕刻作品。|近义词：carving, etching, inscription|反义词: - |noun


engraver:/ɪnˈɡreɪvər/
n.|雕刻师|A person who engraves.|The engraver carefully etched the design onto the metal plate.  雕刻师小心翼翼地将图案蚀刻在金属板上。|近义词：carver, etcher|反义词: - |noun


engrave:/ɪnˈɡreɪv/
v.|雕刻|To cut or carve (letters, designs, etc.) into a hard surface.|He engraved his name on the inside of the ring. 他把他的名字刻在了戒指内侧。|近义词：carve, etch, inscribe|反义词: erase, efface |verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French engraver, from en- "in" + graver "to carve," from Frankish *graban "to dig, carve."
