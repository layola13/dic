antioxygenating:/ˌæntiˈɒksɪdʒəneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
抗氧化的
Acting to inhibit oxidation.
Antioxygenating agents are added to some foods to prevent spoilage.  抗氧化剂被添加到一些食物中以防止腐败变质。
The cream boasts antioxygenating properties that protect the skin from free radicals.  这款面霜具有抗氧化特性，可以保护皮肤免受自由基的侵害。
synonyms: antioxidant, anti-oxidative
antonyms: oxidizing, pro-oxidant
词性: adjective
