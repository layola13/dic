guild:/ɡɪld/| n. |协会，行会，同业公会|An association of people with similar interests or pursuits, especially merchants or artisans, formed for mutual aid and protection.|例句：The carpenters' guild held a meeting to discuss wages. 木匠行会召开会议讨论工资问题。

例句：The merchants' guild controlled trade in the city. 商人行会控制着城市的贸易。|近义词：association, union, society, league, fraternity|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 non-members, individuals 等表示非行会成员的概念) |词性:noun
