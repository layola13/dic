caved-in:/keɪvdˈɪn/
adjective
塌陷的，坍塌的
having fallen in or down; collapsed.
The roof of the old mine caved in.  旧矿的屋顶塌陷了。
The tunnel caved in after the earthquake. 地震后隧道塌陷了。
collapsed, sunken
intact, standing
adjective

caved-in:/keɪvdˈɪn/
verb
塌陷，坍塌
to fall in or down; collapse.
The roof of the barn caved in under the weight of the snow. 谷仓的屋顶在雪的重压下塌陷了。
The sides of the excavation caved in. 挖掘的侧面塌陷了。
collapse, crumble
hold up, stand firm
