flamingo:/fləˈmɪŋɡoʊ/ (US) /fləˈmɪŋɡəʊ/ (UK)| n.|火烈鸟，红鹳|A large wading bird with pink or scarlet plumage, long legs, and a long neck, native to tropical and subtropical regions.|We saw a flock of flamingos wading in the shallows. 我们看到一群火烈鸟在浅滩涉水。|近义词：None (Specific bird)|反义词:None|Noun

flamingo:/fləˈmɪŋɡoʊ/ (US) /fləˈmɪŋɡəʊ/ (UK)| adj.|火烈鸟粉色的，艳丽的|Of a vibrant pink or reddish orange color resembling that of a flamingo's plumage.|She wore a flamingo pink dress. 她穿着一件火烈鸟粉色的连衣裙。|近义词：pink, scarlet, coral|反义词:dull, muted|Adjective
