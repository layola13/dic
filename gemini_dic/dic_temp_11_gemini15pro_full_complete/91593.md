hyperaesthete:/ˌhaɪpərˈɛsθiːt/
noun
唯美主义者；过度敏感的人
A person who is excessively sensitive to and appreciative of beauty, especially in art and literature.
例句：The hyperaesthete recoiled at the gaudy decorations.  这位唯美主义者对俗丽的装饰感到厌恶。

例句：He was known as a hyperaesthete, obsessed with the finest fabrics and perfumes. 他以唯美主义者而闻名，痴迷于最精美的布料和香水。

近义词：aesthete, connoisseur, epicure
反义词：philistine, barbarian
