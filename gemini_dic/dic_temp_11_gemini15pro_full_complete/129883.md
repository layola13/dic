non-intervene:/ˌnɑːnˌɪntərˈviːn/| v. |不干预，不介入|To refrain from interfering or involving oneself in the affairs of others. |The United Nations decided to non-intervene in the conflict.| 近义词: abstain, refrain, forbear |反义词: intervene, interfere, meddle |动词

non-intervention:/ˌnɑːnˌɪntərˈvenʃən/| n. |不干预，不介入|The policy or practice of not intervening or interfering, especially in the affairs of other countries.|The country adopted a policy of non-intervention in foreign disputes.| 近义词: noninterference, abstention |反义词: intervention, interference |名词


词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + intervene (from Latin intervenire, meaning "to come between")
