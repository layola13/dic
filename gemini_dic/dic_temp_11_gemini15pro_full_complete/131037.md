nonautonomously:/ˌnɒnɔːˈtɒnəməsli/ | adv. | 非自主地，不自律地 | Not operating independently; under the control or influence of something else. | The robot arm moved nonautonomously, guided by the technician's controls.  该机械臂非自主移动，由技术人员的控制器引导。 | Example 2: The heartbeat, while typically nonautonomous in response to the nervous system, can sometimes exhibit autonomous activity. 心跳虽然通常非自主地响应神经系统，但有时会表现出自律性活动。 |  Synonyms: dependently, externally, passively | Antonyms: autonomously, independently, actively | 词性: 副词
