mewlers:/ˈmjuːlərz/| n. |喵喵叫的人;爱哭的人|People who mew, especially habitually or excessively. This can refer to both literal mewing like a cat and figurative mewing like a crying child.|The mewlers in the back of the classroom disrupted the lesson. 教室后面喵喵叫的人扰乱了课堂秩序。|近义词：criers, whiners, complainers|反义词:stoics, tough guys|noun
