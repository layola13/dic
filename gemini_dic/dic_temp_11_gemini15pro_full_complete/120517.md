minutia:/maɪˈnjuːʃə/ /maɪˈnuːʃə/ | n. | 细节，细枝末节，琐事 | A minute or minor detail; a trifling or insignificant matter. |  The contract was full of legal minutiae.  合同充满了法律上的细枝末节。


| The detective carefully examined every minutia of the crime scene.  侦探仔细检查了犯罪现场的每一个细节。 | synonyms: detail, particular, nicety, triviality | antonyms: generality, whole, entirety | noun
