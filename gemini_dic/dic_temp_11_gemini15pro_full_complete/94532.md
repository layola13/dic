imposition:/ˌɪmpəˈzɪʃən/ | n. |强加；负担；税款；不合理的要求 |  the act of imposing something (such as a tax, rule, or punishment)  |  The government imposed a new tax on imported goods. 政府对进口商品征收了新的税。| synonyms: burden, levy, onus, demand | antonyms: concession, privilege, boon, exemption | noun


imposition:/ˌɪmpəˈzɪʃən/ | n. |打扰，麻烦 | Something that is imposed; an unwelcome demand or burden. | I hope it's not too much of an imposition to ask you to pick me up from the airport.  希望让你去机场接我不是太麻烦你。| synonyms: intrusion, nuisance, bother, inconvenience | antonyms: convenience, help, assistance, aid | noun
