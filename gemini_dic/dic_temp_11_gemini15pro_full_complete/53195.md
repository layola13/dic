dipteran:/ˈdɪptərən/| n. |双翅目昆虫|A two-winged fly; an insect of the order Diptera, such as a housefly, mosquito, gnat, or midge.|例句：The mosquito is a common dipteran that transmits diseases. 蚊子是一种常见的传播疾病的双翅目昆虫。

例句：Dipterans are a diverse group of insects found worldwide. 双翅目昆虫种类繁多，遍布世界各地。|近义词：fly, two-winged insect|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

dipterous:/ˈdɪptərəs/| adj. |双翅目的|Having two wings, or belonging to the order Diptera.|例句：The dipterous insects include flies and mosquitoes. 双翅目昆虫包括苍蝇和蚊子。

例句：Dipterous larvae are often found in decaying organic matter. 双翅目昆虫的幼虫通常存在于腐烂的有机物中。|近义词：two-winged|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective
