overmeddling:/ˌoʊvərˈmedlɪŋ/| adj.| 过度干预的，多管闲事的 | Excessively interfering in the affairs of others. |  She has an overmeddling mother who tries to control every aspect of her life. 她有一个爱管闲事的母亲，试图控制她生活的方方面面。|  He resented his neighbor's overmeddling advice. 他讨厌邻居多管闲事的建议。| 近义词: interfering, intrusive, meddlesome | 反义词: respectful, unintrusive, noninterfering | 词性: adjective

词源: From "over" (excessive) + "meddle" (to interfere).  "Meddle" comes from Old French "medler" (to mix, mingle), ultimately from Latin "miscere" (to mix).
