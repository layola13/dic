junkyards: /ˈdʒʌŋkˌjɑːrdz/
n.
废品场；旧车堆放场
A place where junk, especially old vehicles, is collected, stored, and sometimes dismantled or recycled.
The old car was towed to the junkyard.  这辆旧车被拖到了废品场。
He found some spare parts for his truck at the junkyard. 他在废品场找到了一些卡车备件。

近义词: scrapyard, salvage yard
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与其功能相反的地方，例如) showroom, dealership
词性: 名词
