griller:/ˈɡrɪlər/| n.|烧烤架，烧烤的人|A person or device that grills food.  A griller can be a person who cooks food on a grill or the appliance itself, like a George Foreman griller.|He's a skilled griller, always getting the perfect char marks on the steak. 他是一位熟练的烧烤师，总能在牛排上烤出完美的焦痕。

The griller wasn't working properly, so we had to cook the burgers inside. 烧烤架坏了，所以我们不得不在室内做汉堡。|近义词：barbecue, grill, broiler|反义词: (No direct antonym.  Perhaps "oven user" if focusing on method, or "raw foodist" if focusing on outcome)|词性:noun
