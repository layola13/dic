counterdistinct:/ˌkaʊntər dɪˈstɪŋkt/ | adj. |  对立区别的，截然不同的 |  Distinguished by contrast; contrasting.  Meaning something is clearly different or separate from something else, often highlighting the opposition or difference between them. | The two approaches are counterdistinct, one focusing on theory and the other on practice. 这两种方法截然不同，一种侧重于理论，另一种侧重于实践。 | The company's culture is counterdistinct from its competitors, emphasizing collaboration over competition.  这家公司的文化与其竞争对手截然不同，强调合作而不是竞争。 | 近义词: contrasting, different, distinct, dissimilar, opposite, opposed | 反义词: similar, alike, identical | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "counter-" (prefix meaning "against" or "opposite") + "distinct" (adjective meaning "clearly different or separate").
