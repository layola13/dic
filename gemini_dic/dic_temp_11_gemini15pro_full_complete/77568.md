gearheads:/ˈɡɪərˌhɛdz/| n.|汽车迷，机械迷|A person who is enthusiastic about machinery, especially cars or motorcycles.  They are often knowledgeable about how these machines work and enjoy working on them.|例句：The gearheads spent all weekend tinkering with their cars.  汽车迷们整个周末都在摆弄他们的汽车。

例句：My brother is a real gearhead; he can take apart and rebuild an engine in his sleep. 我弟弟是个十足的机械迷，他闭着眼睛都能拆装发动机。|近义词：motorhead, car enthusiast, petrolhead, mechanic, tinkerer|反义词:Luddite|Noun
