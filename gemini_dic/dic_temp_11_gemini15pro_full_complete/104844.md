keelhaul:/ˈkiːlhɔːl/| v.| 龙骨吊，酷刑折磨 |To drag a person under the keel of a ship as punishment or torture.  This involved tying the person to a rope, throwing them overboard, and dragging them under the ship's keel. |The captain threatened to keelhaul any man caught stealing. 船长威胁要对任何被抓到偷窃的人处以龙骨吊。|Synonyms: flog, scourge, punish, torture|Antonyms: reward, praise|verb

keelhauling:/ˈkiːlhɔːlɪŋ/| n.| 龙骨吊，酷刑折磨 |The act of keelhauling. |Keelhauling was a brutal form of punishment. 龙骨吊是一种残酷的惩罚形式。|Synonyms: flogging, scourging, punishment, torture|Antonyms: reward, praise|noun
