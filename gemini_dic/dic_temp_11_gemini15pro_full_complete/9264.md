antipatriots:/ˌæntiˈpeɪtriəts/ | n. | 反爱国者，不爱国者 |  A person who does not love or support their own country, and may even actively oppose its interests. |  The antipatriots protested the war. 反爱国者抗议战争。 |  The group of antipatriots actively sought to undermine the government.  这群反爱国者积极地试图破坏政府。 | Near synonyms: traitor, dissident, rebel, fifth columnist | Antonyms: patriot, nationalist, loyalist | Noun
