hemin:/ˈhiːmɪn/| n.|血红素|A reddish-brown crystalline derivative of hemoglobin.|Hemoglobin is converted to hemin when red blood cells are destroyed.  血红蛋白在红细胞被破坏时会转化为血红素。| The presence of hemin can be used in forensic testing. 血红素的存在可以用于法医测试。|Synonyms: ferriheme, ferric protoporphyrin IX chloride|Antonyms: N/A|noun
