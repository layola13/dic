nonlegislatively:/ˌnɒnˈlɛdʒɪslətɪvli/
adverb
非立法地，非通过立法
In a manner that does not involve legislation or the making of laws.
The issue was resolved nonlegislatively, through negotiations between the parties.  这个问题是通过双方协商以非立法的方式解决的。
The board decided to handle the matter nonlegislatively. 董事会决定以非立法的方式处理此事。
近义词:informally, privately
反义词:legislatively
