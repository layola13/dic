gift:/ɡɪft/ | n. | 礼物；天赋；才能 | Something given willingly to someone without payment; a natural ability or talent. | She gave him a birthday gift. 她送给他一份生日礼物。 |  He has a gift for music. 他有音乐天赋。 | synonyms: present, donation, talent, aptitude | antonyms:  curse,  detriment | noun


gift:/ɡɪft/ | vt. | 赠送；赋予 | To bestow a gift upon someone; to endow with a talent or quality. | He gifted her a necklace. 他送给她一条项链。 | Nature gifted her with a beautiful voice. 大自然赋予了她美妙的歌喉。 | synonyms: bestow, donate, endow, present | antonyms: take, deprive | verb
