balanoglossus:/ˌbælənəˈɡlɒsəs/ /ˌbælənəˈɡlɔːsəs/
noun|柱头虫，肠鳃纲动物|A marine invertebrate of the class Enteropneusta, having a wormlike body with a proboscis, collar, and trunk.  These animals are considered to be closely related to chordates.|Balanoglossus is a fascinating creature that provides insights into the evolution of chordates.|近义词:acorn worm, tongue worm|反义词:None|词性:n.

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek βάλανος (bálanos, “acorn”) + γλῶσσα (glôssa, “tongue”)
