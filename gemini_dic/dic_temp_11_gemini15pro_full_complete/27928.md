cantilever:/ˈkæntilɪvər/ /ˈkæntilɛvər/| n. v.|悬臂，悬臂梁；外伸梁；|A cantilever is a long piece of wood, metal, etc., that sticks out from a wall or other structure to support something above it.|The balcony is supported by concrete cantilevers.|近义词:overhang, projection, jetty|反义词:support, base|noun
cantilever:/ˈkæntilɪvər/ /ˈkæntilɛvər/| v.|使成悬臂式；|To build something with a cantilever.|The roof is cantilevered over the terrace.|近义词:extend, project, overhang|反义词:support, underpin|verb
