gulfiest: /ˈɡʌlfiɪst/
adjective
最容易上当的；最轻信的
Most gullible; most easily fooled or cheated.
例句：He's the gulfiest person I know; he'll believe anything. 他是我认识的最容易上当的人；他什么都信。

例句：She was gulfiest of the group and fell for the prank immediately.  她是这群人中最容易上当的，立刻就上了当。

近义词：naive, credulous, trusting
反义词：skeptical, cynical, suspicious
词性：adjective

词源：
From "gullible" + "-est" (superlative suffix). "Gullible" likely comes from "gull" (verb, meaning "to swallow," as in swallowing a lie) + "-ible" (suffix meaning "capable of being").  The verb "gull" comes from a now obsolete word "gulle" of uncertain origin.
