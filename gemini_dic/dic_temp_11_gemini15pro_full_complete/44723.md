cruise:/kruːz/| n. vi. vt.|巡航，游弋；漫游，闲逛；|A journey on a large ship for pleasure, during which you visit several places.|We went on a cruise to the Caribbean. 我们去加勒比海进行了邮轮旅行。|近义词：voyage, tour, sail, trip|反义词:stay, remain|noun, verb

cruise:/kruːz/| vi. vt.|以平稳的速度行驶；轻松前进；|To travel at a moderate speed.|The car cruised along the highway. 汽车在高速公路上平稳行驶。|近义词：coast, drift, glide|反义词:race, speed|verb
