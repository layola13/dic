bouillabaisse:/ˌbuːjəˈbeɪs/| n. |法式海鲜汤|A rich, spicy stew or soup made with various kinds of fish, shellfish, vegetables, and seasonings, typically flavored with saffron and served with a rouille. |例句：The bouillabaisse was rich and flavorful, a true taste of the Mediterranean. 这道法式海鲜汤浓郁而美味，是真正的地中海风味。

例句：We ordered the bouillabaisse as a special treat. 我们特意点了一份法式海鲜汤。|近义词：fish stew, seafood stew|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific dish) |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From Provençal *bolhabaissa*, from bolhir ("to boil") and abaissar ("to lower the heat").  The process of making the stew involves boiling the ingredients and then reducing the heat to simmer.
