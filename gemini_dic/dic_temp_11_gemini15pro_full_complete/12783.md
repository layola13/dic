assimilating:/əˈsɪməleɪtɪŋ/
|v.|吸收; 同化; 消化|The process of absorbing or integrating something into a larger whole, such as a culture, society, or group. It can also refer to the process of digesting food or information.|They are assimilating into the local culture. 他们正在融入当地文化。|Synonyms: integrating, incorporating, absorbing, adapting, adjusting|Antonyms: rejecting, excluding, segregating|verb
