disagreement:/ˌdɪsəˈɡriːmənt/| n. |不一致，分歧，争论|A lack of consensus or harmony; a difference of opinion.|例句：There was a strong disagreement between the two parties about the terms of the contract. 双方在合同条款上存在严重分歧。

例句：The committee was plagued by disagreement over the proposed budget cuts. 委员会因拟议的预算削减而争论不休。|近义词：discord, dispute, contention, conflict, difference, variance|反义词：agreement, accord, harmony, concord, consensus|词性：名词
