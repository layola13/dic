hoker:/ˈhɔːkər/ | n. |小贩，叫卖小贩 | A person who sells goods, typically food or small items, from a stall or cart in a public place. | Example 1: The hawkers in the night market offered a wide variety of delicious street food. 夜市上的小贩提供了各种各样美味的街头小吃。

Example 2:  The streets were filled with the cries of hawkers selling their wares. 街上到处都是叫卖小贩的叫卖声。 |Synonyms: vendor, peddler, street vendor, trader | Antonyms: buyer, customer | noun
