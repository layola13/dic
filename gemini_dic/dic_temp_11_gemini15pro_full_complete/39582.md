conscriptive:/kənˈskrɪptɪv/
adjective
强制征兵的; 征兵的
relating to or involving conscription
例句：The government introduced conscriptive service during the war. 政府在战争期间实行了义务兵役制。

例句：Conscriptive policies are often controversial.  征兵政策经常引起争议。

近义词：draft, compulsory
反义词：voluntary
词性：adjective
