gnus:/nuːz/ /njuːz/ | n. | 角马；牛羚 | A large African antelope with a head like an ox, short mane, long tail, and curved horns. | 例句：A herd of gnus grazed peacefully on the savanna. 一群角马在草原上平静地吃草。

例句：The gnus migrated across the plains in search of water. 角马为了寻找水源而穿越平原迁徙。| 近义词：wildebeest, antelope | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for an animal) | 词性：名词
