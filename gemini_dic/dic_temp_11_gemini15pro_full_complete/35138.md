cloudy:/ˈklaʊdi/ (US) /ˈklaʊdi/ (UK)| adj.|多云的，阴天的|Covered with clouds; overcast.|例句：It's a cloudy day today. 今天是阴天。|例句：The sky is cloudy and grey. 天空多云且灰蒙蒙的。|近义词：overcast, hazy, dull|反义词：clear, sunny, bright|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *cloudi, from Old English clūdig (“cloudy”), equivalent to cloud +‎ -y. Cognate with Middle Low German klōdich (“cloudy”).
