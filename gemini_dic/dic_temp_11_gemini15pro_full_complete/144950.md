overabstemiousnesses:/ˌoʊvəræbˈstiːmiəsnəsəz/
|n.|过度节制；极度禁欲|The overabstemiousnesses of the monks surprised the visitors. 修道士们极度的禁欲让游客们感到惊讶。|Excessive abstinence, especially in the consumption of food or alcohol.  Overabstemiousness can lead to health problems. 过度节制，尤其是在食物或酒精的摄入方面。过度节制会导致健康问题。|例句：Their overabstemiousnesses led to malnutrition. 他们的过度节制导致了营养不良。

|例句：Such overabstemiousnesses are rarely seen in modern society. 这种过度节制在现代社会很少见。
|近义词：asceticism, austerity, self-denial|反义词：gluttony, indulgence, excess|词性：名词


词源：
From over- + abstemiousness + -es.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much." "Abstemiousness" comes from the Latin word "abstemius," meaning "abstaining from wine."  The suffix "-es" creates the plural form.
