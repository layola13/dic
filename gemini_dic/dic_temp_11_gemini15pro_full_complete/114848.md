mammifer:/ˈmæməfər/| n.|哺乳动物|A warm-blooded vertebrate animal of a class that is distinguished by the possession of mammary glands, which in females produce milk for nourishing (giving suck to) their young, and that are typically covered with hair. |例句：The blue whale is the largest mammifer. 蓝鲸是最大的哺乳动物。

例句：Humans, dogs, and cats are all examples of mammifers. 人类、狗和猫都是哺乳动物的例子。|近义词：mammal|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially reptile, bird, amphibian, fish depending on context)|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *mammifère*, from Latin *mammifer* "breast-bearing," from *mamma* "breast, udder" + *-fer* "bearing."
