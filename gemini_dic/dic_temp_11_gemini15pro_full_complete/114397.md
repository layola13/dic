make away with:/meɪk əˈweɪ wɪð/
|phrasal verb|
|偷走；携...潜逃；杀死；|
|To steal something and escape with it; to kill someone.|
|The thieves made away with the jewels.| 小贼们偷走了珠宝。
|The murderer made away with his victim.| 凶手杀死了受害者。
|近义词: steal, abscond, pilfer, filch, murder, eliminate, dispatch|
|反义词: return, give back, surrender|
|词性: 短语动词|

make away with:/meɪk əˈweɪ wɪð/
|phrasal verb|
|挥霍；浪费；|
|To squander or waste something, especially money or time.|
|He made away with his inheritance in a matter of months.| 他几个月内就挥霍光了遗产。
|She made away with the whole afternoon watching TV.| 她看了一下午电视，浪费了整个下午。
|近义词: squander, waste, fritter away, dissipate|
|反义词: save, conserve, hoard|
|词性: 短语动词|
