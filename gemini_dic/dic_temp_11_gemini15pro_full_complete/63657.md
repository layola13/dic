epag: /ˈiːpæɡ/ | n. | Electronic Publication Aggregate |  An electronic publication aggregate (EPAG) is a collection of digital publications, often related by subject or publisher, offered as a single subscription or purchase. | Example: The university library subscribed to several EPAGs covering various academic disciplines. 大学图书馆订阅了几个涵盖各种学科的电子出版物集。 | Example:  Accessing research papers through the EPAG is much more convenient than searching individual journals. 通过电子出版物集访问研究论文比搜索单个期刊方便得多。 | 近义词: digital library, online database, e-journal collection | 反义词: print collection, physical library | 词性: noun
