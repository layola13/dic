overdifferentiations: /ˌoʊvərˌdɪfəˌrenʃiˈeɪʃənz/ | n. | 过度区分，过度差异化 | The act of making too many distinctions or subdivisions, often unnecessarily or to an excessive degree, resulting in complexity and potentially obscuring underlying similarities. |  Excessive market segmentation can lead to overdifferentiations of products, confusing consumers and increasing production costs. 过度的市场细分会导致产品过度差异化，使消费者感到困惑，并增加生产成本。

The overdifferentiations of tasks within the team created unnecessary barriers to collaboration and slowed down progress. 团队内部任务的过度区分造成了不必要的合作障碍，减缓了进度。 | 近义词: oversubdivision, overcategorization, overclassification | 反义词: simplification, generalization, standardization | 词性: 名词
