cruisers:/ˈkruːzərz/ (US), /ˈkruːzəz/ (UK)| n.|巡洋舰；巡逻车；漫游者|Large, fast warships. Also, a person or thing that cruises, such as a police car patrolling an area or someone travelling for pleasure.|The navy deployed several cruisers to the region. 海军向该区域部署了几艘巡洋舰。|The police cruisers patrolled the streets. 警车在街道上巡逻。|synonyms: warships, patrol cars, travelers, rovers|antonyms: stay-at-homes, settlers|noun
