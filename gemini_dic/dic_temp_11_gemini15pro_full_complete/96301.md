indigenous-to:/ɪnˈdɪdʒənəs tuː/
adjective
原产于，土生土长的
originating or occurring naturally in a particular place; native.
The kangaroo is indigenous to Australia.  袋鼠原产于澳大利亚。
This plant is indigenous to the tropics. 这种植物原产于热带地区。
native, aboriginal, endemic
introduced, foreign, exotic
adjective
