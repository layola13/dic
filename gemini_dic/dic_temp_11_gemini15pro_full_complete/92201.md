词源:
From "hyper-" (meaning "excessive" or "over") + "suggestible" (capable of being influenced by suggestion) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  来源于"hyper-"（意为“过度”或“超过”）+ "suggestible"（易受暗示的影响）+ "-ly"（副词后缀）。
