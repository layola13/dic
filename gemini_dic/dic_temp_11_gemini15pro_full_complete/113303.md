luthern:/ˈluːθərn/ | adj. n.| 路德教的；路德教派的人|Relating to Martin Luther, his theology, or the churches that follow his teachings.  A follower of Martin Luther's teachings; a member of a Lutheran church. |例句：He was raised in the Lutheran faith. 他是在路德教信仰中长大的。| 近义词：Protestant 新教的 | 反义词：Catholic 天主教的 | 词性：形容词/名词
Etymology: From Martin Luther's name + -an.  Ultimately from the Germanic name *Liuthar, meaning "people's army".
