bursa:/ˈbɜːrsə/ (US) /ˈbɜːsə/ (UK)| n.|滑囊|A small fluid-filled sac lined with synovial membrane that provides cushioning between bones and tendons and/or muscles around a joint.|Bursitis is inflammation of a bursa.|近义词:sac, pouch|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun

bursas:/ˈbɜːrsiː/ (US) /ˈbɜːsiː/ (UK)| n.|滑囊（复数）|Plural form of bursa, referring to multiple small fluid-filled sacs that reduce friction between moving parts in the body.|He had pain in several bursas in his knee.|近义词: sacs, pouches|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
