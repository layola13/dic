manipulation:/məˌnɪpjuˈleɪʃn/ | n. | 操纵，控制，处理 | the action of manipulating something in a skillful manner; skillful treatment of something by hand | The magician's manipulation of the cards was impressive. 魔术师对纸牌的操控令人印象深刻。 | synonyms: control, influence, maneuvering, exploitation | antonyms: incompetence, clumsiness | noun

manipulate:/məˈnɪpjuleɪt/ | vt. | 操纵，控制，处理 | handle or control (a tool, mechanism, etc.), typically in a skillful manner; control or influence (a person or situation) cleverly, unfairly, or unscrupulously | He manipulated the joystick to control the game. 他操纵游戏杆来控制游戏。| synonyms: control, influence, operate, exploit | antonyms: release, free, liberate | verb
