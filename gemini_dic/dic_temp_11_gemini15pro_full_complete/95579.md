incomer's:/ˈɪnkʌməz/
noun
新来者的（所有格）
belonging to or relating to a newcomer or someone who has recently arrived in a place.
The incomer's guide to the city was very helpful. 这座城市的新来者指南非常有用。
近义词:newcomer's, arrival's
反义词:resident's, local's
词性:n.(名词所有格)

incomer:/ˈɪnkʌmə(r)/
noun
新来者；新居民
a person who has recently arrived in a place, especially to live there.
The village welcomed the incomers with open arms. 村民们张开双臂欢迎新来的人。
近义词:newcomer, arrival, settler
反义词:resident, local, native
词性:n.(名词)
