lenticula:/lɛnˈtɪkjʊlə/ | n. |晶状体, 小透镜, 扁豆 |A small lens-shaped or lentil-shaped body.  Specifically, the crystalline lens of the eye. Also, a freckle or lenticular spot on the skin. | The doctor examined the patient's lenticula. 医生检查了病人的晶状体。| The lenticula of the camera was scratched. 相机的镜头被刮花了。 | synonyms: lens, crystalline lens, freckle, lentigo | antonyms: N/A | noun
