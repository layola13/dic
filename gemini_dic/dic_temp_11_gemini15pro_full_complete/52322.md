dickinsonia:/ˌdɪkɪnˈsoʊniə/ | n. |狄更逊水母|An extinct genus of basal animal that lived during the late Ediacaran period in the Precambrian era, known from fossils found worldwide.|Example: Dickinsonia fossils offer crucial clues about early animal life. 狄更逊水母化石为早期动物生命提供了重要线索。

Example:  The classification of Dickinsonia is still debated among scientists.  科学家们仍在争论狄更逊水母的分类。|近义词: Ediacaran biota (a broader term), Charnia (a similar Ediacaran organism)|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
