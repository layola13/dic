mollycoddles:/ˈmɒlɪˌkɒdəlz/ (British) /ˈmɑːliˌkɑːdəlz/ (American)| n. |娇生惯养的人；被溺爱的人|A person who is pampered or treated indulgently.|He was raised as a mollycoddle, unable to cope with the harsh realities of life. 他被娇生惯养长大，无法应对生活的残酷现实。|She mollycoddled her youngest child. 她溺爱她最小的孩子。|Synonyms: softie, weakling, milksop|Antonyms: tough guy, hard-ass|Noun

mollycoddle:/ˈmɒlɪˌkɒdəl/ (British) /ˈmɑːliˌkɑːdəl/ (American)| v. |娇惯；溺爱|To pamper or treat indulgently.|Don't mollycoddle him, he needs to learn to stand on his own two feet.  别溺爱他，他需要学会自立。|She mollycoddled her dog with expensive treats and toys. 她用昂贵的零食和玩具娇惯她的狗。|Synonyms: pamper, coddle, spoil, indulge|Antonyms: neglect, ignore, discipline|Verb
