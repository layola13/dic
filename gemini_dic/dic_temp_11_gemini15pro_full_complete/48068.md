declarative:/dɪˈklærətɪv/| adj.|声明的，陈述的，说明的|Serving to declare, state, or make known; explanatory. A declarative sentence makes a statement.|The witness made a declarative statement to the police.  证人向警方作了陈述。|近义词：assertive, affirmative, explicit|反义词：interrogative, imperative, exclamatory|词性:adjective
词源:Late Middle English: from late Latin declarativus, from declarare ‘make clear’ (see declare).

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Declarative sentences are commonly used in the simple present tense.
陈述句通常用于一般现在时。

He eats an apple every day.
他每天吃一个苹果。

She works at the library.
她在图书馆工作。
