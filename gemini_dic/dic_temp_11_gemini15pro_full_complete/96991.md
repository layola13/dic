infantilized: /ɪnˈfæn.tɪ.laɪzd/ |v. |幼稚化；像对待幼儿般对待；|to treat (someone) as if they were a child| He felt infantilized by his parents' constant hovering. 他觉得父母的不断盘旋让他感到幼稚化。|  She resented being infantilized by her older siblings. 她讨厌被哥哥姐姐们当小孩对待。|近义词: patronize, condescend, coddle|反义词: empower, respect, mature|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From infant (n.) + -ize (suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or become like") + -ed (past participle suffix).
