mentorships:/ˈmɛntərʃɪps/ | n. | 师徒关系；指导；辅导 | A relationship in which a more experienced or knowledgeable person helps guide a less experienced or knowledgeable person. | The company's mentorship program pairs senior employees with new hires.  该公司指导计划将高级员工与新员工配对。| Synonyms: guidance, tutoring, coaching, training | Antonyms: neglect, abandonment | noun


mentorships:/ˈmɛntərʃɪps/ | n. |  指导项目；导师制 |  A system or program that involves mentorship. |  The university offers several mentorships for students interested in different career paths.  该大学为对不同职业道路感兴趣的学生提供多种指导项目。| Synonyms: guidance programs, training programs, development programs | Antonyms:  N/A (lack of a program isn't a direct antonym) | noun
