collocation:/ˌkɑːləˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 搭配；词语搭配；组合 |The words "strong" and "tea" are a common collocation. “浓”和“茶”是一个常见的搭配。 | Example:  "Heavy rain" is a common collocation, while "strong rain" sounds unnatural. “大雨”是一个常见的搭配，而“强雨”听起来不自然。

Example:  Learning collocations is essential for fluent English. 学习搭配对于流利的英语至关重要。 |Synonyms: combination, pairing, association | Antonyms: separation, disassociation | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "collocāre" (to place together),  from "com-" (together) + "locāre" (to place).
