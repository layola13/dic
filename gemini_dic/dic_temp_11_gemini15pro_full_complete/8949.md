antihumanist:/ˌæntiˈhjuːmənɪst/
n.|反人文主义者|A person who opposes humanism. |The antihumanist argued that focusing solely on human potential ignored the importance of spiritual values.|近义词:anti-humanitarian|反义词:humanist|词性:名词

antihumanist:/ˌæntiˈhjuːmənɪst/
adj.|反人文主义的|Opposed to or rejecting humanism; characterized by a belief that human reason and values are insufficient or even harmful. |The author's antihumanist philosophy was evident in his bleak portrayal of society.|近义词:misanthropic, cynical|反义词:humanistic, anthropocentric|词性:形容词
