meddlers:/ˈmedlər/ (US) /ˈmedlə(r)/ (UK)| n.|爱管闲事的人，干涉者|A person who interferes in something that is not their concern.|例句：The neighbors are such meddlers, always gossiping about everyone's business. 邻居们真是爱管闲事，总是对每个人的事说三道四。

例句：He resented the meddlers who tried to interfere in his personal life. 他讨厌那些干涉他私生活的人。|近义词：busybodies, interferers, intruders|反义词:mind their own business|词性:noun
