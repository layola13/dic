ground:/ɡraʊnd/ | n.  v. | 地面，土地；基础；立场；根据；范围 | The book fell to the ground. 书掉到了地上。 |  近义词：earth, floor, basis, reason | 反义词：sky, air | 词性: 名词, 动词

ground:/ɡraʊnd/ | v. |  使搁浅；使停飞；罚（孩子）不准出门；打基础 | The ship was grounded on a sandbar. 船搁浅在沙洲上。 | 近义词：base, establish | 反义词：launch, unsettle | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *grund, from Proto-Germanic *grunduz, from Proto-Indo-European *ghrēn- (“to rub, grind”). Cognate with West Frisian grûn, Dutch grond, German Grund, Danish grund.
