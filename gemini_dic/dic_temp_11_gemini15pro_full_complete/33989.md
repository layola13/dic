citations:/saɪˈteɪʃənz/| n.|引用；引证；表彰；传票|A citation is a quotation from or reference to a book, paper, or author, especially in a scholarly work.  A citation is also an official document that praises a person for doing something brave or useful. It can also refer to a summons to appear in court.|The essay contained numerous citations from ancient texts. 这篇文章引用了许多古代文献。|synonyms: quotation, reference, mention, commendation, award, summons|antonyms: omission, exclusion|noun
