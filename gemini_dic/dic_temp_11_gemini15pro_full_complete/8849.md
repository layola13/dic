antifashionable:/ˌæntiˈfæʃənəbl/
adjective
过时的，不时髦的，不流行的
Not fashionable; out of fashion.
Her anti-fashionable clothes made her stand out from the crowd.  她那过时的衣服让她在人群中显得格格不入。
He deliberately cultivated an anti-fashionable image. 他故意塑造了一个不时尚的形象。
近义词: unfashionable, outdated, outmoded, old-fashioned
反义词: fashionable, stylish, trendy, chic
词性: adjective

词源:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to  反对，相反
fashionable (adjective): conforming to the current fashion  符合当前时尚的
