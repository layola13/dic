disseminative:/dɪˈsɛmɪnətɪv/
adjective
传播的，散布的
tending to disseminate; scattering widely.
The internet has a disseminative effect on information. 互联网对信息传播具有广泛的影响。
The disseminative power of social media is undeniable. 社交媒体的传播力不可否认。
近义词: spreading, distributive, dispersive
反义词: restrictive, suppressive, limiting
词性: adjective
