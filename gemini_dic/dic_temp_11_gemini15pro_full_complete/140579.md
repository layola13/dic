oligomyold:/ˌɒlɪɡəˈmaɪɔɪld/ | adj. | 少肌的，肌肉发育不良的 |  Having diminished or deficient muscular development.  Derived from "oligo-" (meaning "few" or "deficient") and "myo-" (relating to muscle) and "-old" (a suffix indicating a resemblance or condition). | Example: The child was diagnosed with an oligomyold condition, explaining his weakness. 这个孩子被诊断出患有少肌症，这解释了他的虚弱。 | Synonym: hypotonic, asthenic | Antonym: muscular, robust | Adjective
