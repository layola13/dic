circumflex:/ˈsɜːrkəmfleks/ | n. |抑扬符，音调符号 |A mark (^) placed over a vowel to indicate pronunciation, length, or a contraction. It's used in some languages, but less common in English. |The word "crèche" sometimes has a circumflex over the "e".  这个单词“crèche”有时在“e”上有一个抑扬符。|近义词:caret, diacritic|反义词:None|词性:noun

circumflex:/ˈsɜːrkəmfleks/ | adj. |抑扬的，弯曲的 |Bent or curved, typically in a downward and then upward curve.  |He drew a circumflex line on the paper. 他在纸上画了一条弯曲的线。|近义词:curved, arched|反义词:straight|词性:adjective
