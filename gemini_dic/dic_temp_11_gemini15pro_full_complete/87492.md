heterocarpous:/ˌhɛtərəˈkɑːrpəs/| adj.|异果的，多型果的|Producing different types of fruit.  A plant that bears two or more morphologically different kinds of fruits.|Example:  The heterocarpous plant produced both spiny and smooth fruits. 这种异果植物既结有带刺的果实，也结有光滑的果实。|Example: Some species of *Bidens* are heterocarpous, having outer achenes with barbs for dispersal and inner achenes without barbs that remain within the flower head. 一些鬼针草种类是异果的，外层瘦果带刺以便传播，内层瘦果无刺留在花序中。|近义词:polycarpic|反义词:homocarpous|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From hetero- (different) + -carpous (fruit)
From Greek héteros (different) + karpós (fruit)
