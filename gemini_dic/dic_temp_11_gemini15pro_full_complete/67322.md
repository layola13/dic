extempore:/ɪkˈstɛmpəri/ /ɛkˈstɛmpəri/| adj. adv.|即兴的，临时的|Done or spoken without preparation; impromptu.|He gave an extempore speech at the wedding. 他在婚礼上发表了即兴演讲。|近义词：impromptu, offhand, ad-lib|反义词：prepared, rehearsed|词性: adjective, adverb

extempore:/ɪkˈstɛmpəri/ /ɛkˈstɛmpəri/| n.|即兴的演讲或表演|An extempore speech or performance.|Her extempore was surprisingly eloquent. 她的即兴演讲出奇地雄辩。|近义词：improvisation|反义词：prepared speech, rehearsed performance|词性: noun

词源: From Latin *ex tempore* "out of the time," from *ex* "out of" + *tempore* "time," ablative singular of *tempus*.
