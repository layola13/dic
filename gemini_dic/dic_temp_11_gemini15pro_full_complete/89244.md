homily:/ˈhɒməli/ (US) /ˈhɑːməli/| n. |布道；说教|A religious discourse that gives moral guidance.  A sermon.|The priest gave a moving homily about forgiveness. 牧师做了一次关于宽恕的感人布道。|Synonyms: sermon, lecture, discourse, preaching|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "diatribe" for a harshly critical speech)|noun
