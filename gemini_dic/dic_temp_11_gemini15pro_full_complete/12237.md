ascariasis:/ˌæskəˈraɪəsɪs/
|n.|蛔虫病|An infection of the intestines with parasitic roundworms of the genus *Ascaris*, especially *Ascaris lumbricoides*.|Ascariasis is a common intestinal parasitic infection in tropical and subtropical regions. 蛔虫病是热带和亚热带地区常见的肠道寄生虫感染。|
|近义词:roundworm infection, helminthiasis|反义词:None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From New Latin *Ascaris* (genus name) + *-iasis* (suffix denoting a diseased condition).  The genus name *Ascaris* comes from the Greek word *askaris*, meaning "intestinal worm."
