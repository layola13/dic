ichthyosis:/ˌɪkθiˈoʊsɪs/| n. |鱼鳞癣|a group of skin disorders characterized by dry, thickened, scaly skin resembling fish scales.  It's caused by genetic mutations that affect the way skin cells grow and shed.| My uncle suffers from ichthyosis and has to apply special lotions daily. 我的叔叔患有鱼鳞癣，必须每天涂抹特殊的乳液。|  Synonyms: xeroderma, fish-skin disease | Antonyms:  None (This is a medical condition; there isn't a direct opposite.) | noun
