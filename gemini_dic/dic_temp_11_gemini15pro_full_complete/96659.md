industrialized:/ɪnˈdʌstriəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 工业化的 | Having undergone industrialization; developed industries.  Characterized by or resulting from the widespread development of industries. |  例句：The industrialized nations of the world must work together to address climate change. 世界上工业化的国家必须共同努力应对气候变化。| 近义词：developed, advanced, modernized | 反义词：undeveloped, agrarian, pre-industrial | 词性: adjective

industrialized:/ɪnˈdʌstriəlaɪzd/ | v. | 工业化 | To make industrial; convert to industrialism.  To develop industries in (a country or region) on a wide scale. | 例句：Many countries in Asia industrialized rapidly in the 20th century.  许多亚洲国家在20世纪迅速工业化。 | 近义词：develop, modernize, industrialize | 反义词：ruralize, deindustrialize | 词性: verb (past participle)
