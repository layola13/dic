interfactional:/ˌɪntərfækʃənəl/
adjective
派系间的；派系斗争的
Occurring between or involving different factions within a group or organization.
The interfactional rivalry within the party threatened to tear it apart.  党内派系间的竞争有可能使其分裂。
Synonyms: internecine, internal, sectarian
Antonyms: unified, harmonious
