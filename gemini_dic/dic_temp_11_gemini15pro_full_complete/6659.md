anagnorisis:/ˌænəɡˈnɒrɪsɪs/
n.
顿悟；发现；识别；认识
A moment in a play or other work when a character makes a critical discovery.
例句：The play's anagnorisis occurs when the protagonist realizes he's been betrayed by his best friend.  这出戏的顿悟时刻发生在主角意识到自己被最好的朋友背叛的时候。

例句：The anagnorisis is a crucial element of many tragedies. 顿悟是许多悲剧的关键因素。

近义词：recognition, discovery, realization, epiphany
反义词：ignorance, obliviousness
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ἀναγνώρισις* (anagnōrisis, “recognition”), from *ἀναγιγνώσκω* (anagignōskō, “I recognize”), from *ἀνά* (ana, “up”) + *γιγνώσκω* (gignōskō, “I know, perceive”).
