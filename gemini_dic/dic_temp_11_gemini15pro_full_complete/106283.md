knuckled:/ˈnʌkəld/| adj.|指节的，用指关节的；屈服的，被迫的|Having knuckles; done with the knuckles; yielded or forced into submission.|He gave a knuckled rap on the door. 他用指关节敲了敲门。|Synonyms:  joint,  yielding,  submissive,  reluctant,  grudging|Antonyms:  unyielding,  resistant,  defiant,  obstinate|adjective
