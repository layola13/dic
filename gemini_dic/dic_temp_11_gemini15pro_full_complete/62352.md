endest:/ˈendɪst/
verb
结束，终止 (archaic second-person singular present form of "end")
You endest the game too soon. 你过早地结束了游戏。
近义词: finish, conclude, terminate
反义词: begin, start, commence
词性: verb (动词)

endest:/ˈendɪst/
Etymology: From Middle English *endest, from Old English *endast, second-person singular present indicative of endian (“to end”).
英文解析:  Archaic second-person singular present form of the verb "end."  It means "you end" and is no longer used in modern English.  It would have been used in a similar way to how we now use "you end" in the simple present tense.
例句：Thou endest the day with a prayer. 你以祈祷结束了一天。
例句：If thou endest thy journey here, thou shalt find peace. 如果你在这里结束你的旅程，你将找到平静。
近义词: finishest, concludest, terminatest (all archaic)
反义词: beginnest, startest, commencest (all archaic)
词性: verb
