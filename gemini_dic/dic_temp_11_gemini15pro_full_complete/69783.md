fermenting:/fərˈmentɪŋ/
v.
发酵;酝酿
the chemical breakdown of a substance by bacteria, yeasts, or other microorganisms, typically involving effervescence and the giving off of heat.
The grapes are fermenting in the vats. 葡萄正在大桶里发酵。
近义词: brewing, decomposing, festering
反义词: stabilizing
词性: 动词(present participle)


ferment:/ˈfɜːrment/
n.
发酵;骚动;激动
a state of great excitement, agitation, or turbulence.
The country was in a state of political ferment.  这个国家处于政治动荡之中。
近义词: agitation, unrest, turmoil
反义词: calm, peace, tranquility
词性: 名词


ferment:/fərˈment/
v.
(使)发酵;(使)激动
undergo fermentation.
The wine is fermenting in the cellar. 酒正在地窖里发酵。
近义词: brew, decompose, fester
反义词: stabilize
词性: 动词


词源: From Old French fermenter, from Latin fermentāre “to leaven, cause to rise,” from fermentum “leaven, yeast.”
