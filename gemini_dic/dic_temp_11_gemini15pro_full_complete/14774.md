awl:/ɔːl/
n.
锥子;尖钻
A small pointed tool used for piercing holes, especially in leather.
He used an awl to make holes in the leather belt. 他用锥子在皮带上钻孔。
近义词:bradawl, piercer, punch
反义词:None
词性:名词


awls:/ɔːlz/
n.
锥子(awl的复数)
Plural form of awl.
He keeps a set of awls in his workshop. 他在车间里放着一套锥子。
近义词:bradawl, piercer, punch
反义词:None
词性:名词
