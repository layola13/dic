miry:/ˈmaɪəri/ | adj. | 泥泞的；泥潭似的 | Covered with deep, sticky mud. | The car got stuck in the miry road. 汽车陷在了泥泞的道路上。 | The hikers struggled through the miry field.  徒步旅行者艰难地穿过泥泞的田野。 | 近义词: muddy, swampy, boggy | 反义词: dry, firm, solid | 词性: 形容词
词源: From Middle English *mirie, from Old English *myrig, from Proto-Germanic *muziją (“mud, mire”). Cognate with Old Frisian mire (“mire”), Middle Dutch mīre (“mire”), Dutch mier (“ant”), German Moder (“mould, decay”).
