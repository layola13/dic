durango:/dʊˈræŋɡoʊ/ /djʊˈræŋɡoʊ/
n.|杜兰戈|A city in southwestern Colorado, USA, and the seat of La Plata County. Also refers to a type of SUV manufactured by Dodge. |We drove through Durango on our road trip. 我们在公路旅行中开车穿过杜兰戈。|Synonyms: (for the city) town, municipality; (for the car) SUV, vehicle|Antonyms: (for the city) countryside, rural area; (for the car) N/A|Noun
