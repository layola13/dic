demilitarizes:/diːˈmɪlɪtəˌraɪzɪz/
verb
使非军事化；解除武装
To remove military forces, weapons, or fortifications from a place or region.
The treaty demilitarized the border zone.  该条约使边境地区非军事化。
The government demilitarized the island after the war. 战后，政府解除了该岛的武装。
Synonym: disarm, neutralize
Antonym: militarize, fortify
词性: 动词
