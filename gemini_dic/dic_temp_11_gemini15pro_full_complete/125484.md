multiproduct:/ˌmʌltiˈprɒdʌkt/ (US) /ˌmʌltiˈprɒdʌkt/ (UK)| adj.|多产品的，多种产品的|Producing or selling multiple products.|例句：The company is a multiproduct firm with offerings in various sectors. 这家公司是一家多产品公司，在各个领域都有产品。|近义词：diversified, varied|反义词：single-product, specialized|词性:形容词

multiproduct:/ˌmʌltiˈprɒdʌkt/ (US) /ˌmʌltiˈprɒdʌkt/ (UK)| n.|多种产品|Multiple products offered by a company.|例句：The company's multiproduct portfolio caters to a wide range of customers.  这家公司的多产品组合满足了广泛的客户需求。|近义词：product line, product mix|反义词：single product|词性:名词
