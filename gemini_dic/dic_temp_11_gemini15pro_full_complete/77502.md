gazillion:/ɡəˈzɪljən/| n. | 极多; 无数; 巨量 | An informal word for a very large, indeterminate number. | There were a gazillion people at the concert last night. 昨晚的音乐会人山人海。| 近义词：zillion, myriad, countless, innumerable | 反义词: few, little | 词性: 名词

词源:  A humorous blend of American English 'gaz' (influenced by 'googol', a large number) and the suffix '-illion' (as in million, billion, trillion), creating a playful exaggeration.
