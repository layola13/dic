myological:/ˌmaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
肌学的；肌肉系统的
Relating to the study of muscles or the muscular system.
The myological structure of the human arm is incredibly complex.  人的手臂的肌肉结构非常复杂。
Myological research has led to a better understanding of how muscles work. 肌学研究使人们更好地了解肌肉是如何工作的。
Near Synonyms:muscular
Antonyms: None specific.
