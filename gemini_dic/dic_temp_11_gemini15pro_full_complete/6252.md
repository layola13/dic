amoeba:/əˈmiːbə/ (US) /əˈmiːbə/ (UK)| n. |变形虫；阿米巴|A single-celled animal that catches food and moves about by extending fingerlike projections of protoplasm.  A microscopic single-celled organism which has a nucleus and cytoplasm and constantly changes shape.|
Amoebas are simple, single-celled organisms. 变形虫是简单的单细胞生物。

Amoebas can be found in freshwater and soil. 变形虫可以在淡水和土壤中找到。
|近义词：protozoan, microorganism|反义词: (No direct antonym for a biological organism) |noun


amoebae:/əˈmiːbiː/ (US) /əˈmiːbiː/ (UK)| n. |变形虫（amoeba的复数形式）|Plural form of amoeba, referring to multiple single-celled animals that catch food and move about by extending fingerlike projections of protoplasm.|
The amoebae were observed under the microscope.  在显微镜下观察到变形虫。

Several different species of amoebae were present in the sample. 样本中存在几种不同种类的变形虫。
|近义词：protozoa, microorganisms|反义词: (No direct antonym for a biological organism) |noun
