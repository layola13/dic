militarily:/ˌmɪlɪˈtɛrɪli/| adv.|军事上地，从军事角度|In terms of strategy or military action.  Relating to the armed forces or to soldiers, arms, or war.|例句：The country is militarily strong. 这个国家军事上很强大。|近义词：strategically, martially|反义词:peacefully, diplomatically|词性:副词
词源: From military + -ly.  "Military" comes from Latin "militaris" meaning "of soldiers, of warfare".
