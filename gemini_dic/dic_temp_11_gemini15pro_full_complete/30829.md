cha-chaing:/tʃɑːˈtʃeɪŋ/ | interj. | 钱币叮当声，收银机的声音 |  The sound of coins jingling, often used to represent the sound of a cash register or money being exchanged.  |  The cashier rang up the sale, and I heard a satisfying "cha-chaing!" 收银员敲响了销售额，我听到了令人满意的“叮当”声！ | 近义词：clink, jingle, ka-ching | 反义词: (无明确反义词) | 词性:感叹词
词源:  拟声词，模仿硬币碰撞的声音。
