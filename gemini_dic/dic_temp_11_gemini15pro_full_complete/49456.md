deltohedron:/ˌdɛltəˈhiːdrən/
noun
三角多面体
A polyhedron whose faces are all congruent equilateral triangles.
A deltohedron is a polyhedron whose faces are congruent equilateral triangles.  三角多面体是一个所有面都是全等等边三角形的凸多面体。
Deltohedra are a class of Johnson solids. 三角多面体是约翰逊多面体的一类。
Synonym:  triangular polyhedron, Johnson solid (some deltohedra are Johnson solids)
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-deltohedron")
