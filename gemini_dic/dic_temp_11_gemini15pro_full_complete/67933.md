faceting:/ˈfæsɪtɪŋ/ /fæˈsɛtɪŋ/ | n. |刻面，琢面；分面；方面|The process of cutting facets into a gemstone to enhance its brilliance.  The act of considering something from multiple angles or perspectives.|

例句：
The faceting of the diamond gave it exceptional sparkle.
钻石的琢面赋予了它非凡的光彩。

The article explored the different facets of the problem.
这篇文章探讨了问题的不同方面。


近义词：aspect, side, phase, angle|
反义词: whole, entirety |
词性: noun|
