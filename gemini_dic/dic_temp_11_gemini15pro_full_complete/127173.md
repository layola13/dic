naturality:/ˌnætʃəˈrælɪti/| n.|自然性；自然状态；不做作；自然大方|The actor's naturality on stage was impressive.  这位演员在舞台上的自然大方令人印象深刻。|例句：She spoke with great naturality, as if she were talking to an old friend. 她说话非常自然，就好像在跟老朋友聊天。

She delivered her lines with convincing naturality. 她以令人信服的自然方式说出了她的台词。|近义词：spontaneity, ease, unaffectedness, artlessness|反义词：artificiality, affectation, stiffness, formality|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *naturalitas, from Latin naturalis (“natural”) + -itas (“-ity”). Equivalent to natural +‎ -ity.
