nonservile:/nɒnˈsɜːrvaɪl/ | adj. | 非奴性的，不卑躬屈膝的 | Not servile; not submissive or fawning.  Characterized by independence and self-respect. |  He maintained a nonservile attitude even when speaking to the king.  他即使在与国王交谈时也保持着不卑躬屈膝的态度。| Synonyms: independent, self-respecting, dignified, assertive, autonomous | Antonyms: servile, submissive, fawning, obsequious, subservient | adjective

Etymology:  From non- + servile.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "servile" comes from the Latin word "servilis," meaning "of a slave."
