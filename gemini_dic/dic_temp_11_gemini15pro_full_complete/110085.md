lichenology:/laɪkəˈnɒlədʒi/ |n.|地衣学|The study of lichens, a composite organism that arises from algae or cyanobacteria living among filaments of multiple fungi in a symbiotic relationship.|He is a specialist in lichenology and has published several papers on the topic. 他是地衣学专家，并在该主题上发表了多篇论文。| 近义词:  phycology (藻类学, 范围更广)|反义词: None|词性: noun
