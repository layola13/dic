actionably:/ˈækʃənəbli/
adverb
可操作地，切实可行地
in a way that can be acted on; practically
The report provided actionably insights. 这份报告提供了可操作的见解。
The information was presented actionably.  这些信息以可操作的方式呈现。
近义词: practically, feasibly, viably
反义词: impractically,  unfeasibly,  inactionably
词性: adverb

词源:  From "action" + "-able" + "-ly".  "Action" comes from the Latin "actio", meaning "a doing, act". "-able" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being". "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.
