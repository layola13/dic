hermitisms:/ˈhɜːmɪtɪzəmz/
noun
隐居生活; 隐士的言行; 隐士的习惯; 遁世主义
The practice of living as a hermit; the doctrines or habits of hermits.
His hermitisms kept him from having many friends.  他的隐居生活使他没有什么朋友。
近义词：seclusion, isolation, solitude
反义词：sociability, gregariousness
词性:n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *heremitismus, from Latin eremita (“hermit”), from Ancient Greek ἐρημίτης (erēmítēs, “desert-dweller”), from ἔρημος (érēmos, “desert, lonely, solitary”).
