inspections:/ɪnˈspɛkʃənz/
n.
检查；视察
The act of looking at something carefully in order to learn more about it or find flaws.
The building passed all safety inspections.  这栋建筑通过了所有安全检查。
examinations, reviews, surveys
negligence, disregard
noun
