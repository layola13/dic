immurations:/ˌɪmjʊˈreɪʃənz/| n. |禁闭，监禁，围墙|The act of immuring or the state of being immured; confinement within walls, especially as a form of imprisonment or burial.|The novel describes the protagonist's immuration in a dark, damp cell. 这部小说描述了主人公被禁闭在一个黑暗潮湿的牢房里。|近义词：confinement, imprisonment, incarceration, internment|反义词: liberation, freedom, release|词性:名词

词源:  From Late Latin *immurationem,  accusative singular of *immuratio (“walling in”), from Latin immuratus, past participle of immurare (“to wall in”), from in- + murare (“to wall”).
