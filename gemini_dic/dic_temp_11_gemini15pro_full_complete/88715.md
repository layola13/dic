hobbyist's:/ˈhɒbiɪsts/ (UK) /ˈhɑːbiɪsts/ (US)| n.| 爱好者的（东西）| Something that belongs to a hobbyist.  It is the possessive form of "hobbyist". | This is the hobbyist's workshop. 这是那位爱好者的工作室。| Example: The hobbyist's collection of stamps was impressive.  这位集邮爱好者的收藏令人印象深刻。|Synonyms: amateur's, enthusiast's|Antonyms: professional's|Possessive noun
