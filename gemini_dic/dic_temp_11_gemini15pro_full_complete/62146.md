enclitically:/ɪnˈklɪtɪkli/ | adv. | 附着地，贴着地 | In a way that is enclitic (attached to the preceding word).  A word or particle is attached phonetically to the word that precedes it. | The pronoun is attached enclitically to the verb.  这个代词附着在动词后面。| N/A | N/A | adverb
