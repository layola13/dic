claimants:/ˈkleɪmənts/| n.|索赔人，申请人|A person who makes a claim, especially in a legal context. |The insurance company is processing the claims of the claimants. 保险公司正在处理索赔人的索赔。|
claimants:/ˈkleɪmənts/| n.|索赔人，申请人|Someone who asserts a right or title to something. |The claimants to the throne engaged in a bitter feud. 王位争夺者陷入了激烈的争斗。|
claimants:/ˈkleɪmənts/| n.|索赔人，申请人|People applying for something, such as benefits or asylum. |The number of claimants for unemployment benefits has risen sharply. 失业救济金申请人数急剧上升。|

近义词: applicants, petitioners, candidates
反义词: defendants, respondents

词源: From the Old French "claimant," present participle of "claimer" meaning "to call, call out, summon," from Latin "clamare" to cry out, shout.
