embeddedness:/ɪmˈbɛdɪdnəs/ | n. |嵌入性；根植性；内嵌性|The state of being embedded within a larger context, especially social or cultural.  Being firmly and deeply ingrained or integrated into something. |The embeddedness of traditional values in the community makes change difficult.  这个社区传统价值观的根深蒂固使得改变很困难。|近义词：integration, rootedness, incorporation |反义词: detachment, isolation |词性: noun

词源: From the verb "embed" + suffix "-ness". "Embed" comes from Middle English "embetten," from "em-" (in) + "bed" (bed).
