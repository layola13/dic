distributionist:/ˌdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃənɪst/
|n.| 分配主义者；平均主义者
|A person who advocates distributionism, a socio-economic ideology that emphasizes widespread ownership of productive assets.|
|例句：The distributionist argued for policies that would empower small businesses and local communities.  分配主义者主张推行赋能小型企业和地方社区的政策。

|例句：She's a staunch distributionist who believes in a more equitable distribution of wealth. 她是一位坚定的分配主义者，相信更公平的财富分配。
|近义词：egalitarian, leveler
|反义词：elitist, monopolist
|词性: noun
