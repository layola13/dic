farewells:/ˌfɛərˈwɛlz/| n.|告别，告别辞；送别，欢送会|an act of saying goodbye; words spoken when leaving someone|We said our farewells and boarded the plane. 我们告别后登上了飞机。|synonyms: goodbyes, leave-takings, departures|antonyms: greetings, welcomes|noun

farewell:/ˌfɛərˈwɛl/| int. adj.|再见，告别；告别的|used to express good wishes on parting; of or relating to a farewell|Farewell, my friend! 再见，我的朋友！|synonyms: goodbye, adieu, so long|antonyms: hello, welcome|interjection, adjective
