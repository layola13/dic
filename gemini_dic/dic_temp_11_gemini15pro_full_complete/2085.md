adagial:/ædˈeɪdʒiəl/
| adj.|谚语的；格言式的|Of, relating to, or resembling an adage; proverbial.|His adagial pronouncements became tiresome. 他那些格言式的言论变得令人厌烦。|近义词：proverbial, aphoristic, axiomatic|反义词：uncommon, rare|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *adagialis, from Latin adagium (“adage, proverb”), from ad- + aio (“I say”).
