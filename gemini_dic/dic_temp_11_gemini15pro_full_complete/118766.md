metalliform:/məˈtælɪfɔːrm/
adjective
金属状的;像金属的
Resembling metal; having the appearance of metal.
The mineral has a metalliform luster.  这种矿物具有金属般的光泽。
metallic, metal-like
nonmetallic, non-metal-like
adjective
