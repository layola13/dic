liminess:/ˈlaɪminəs/| n.|粘性；石灰性；含石灰|The liminess of the soil made it difficult to work with. 土壤的粘性使得它难以耕作。|例句：The liminess of the water is due to the high calcium carbonate content.  水的粘性是由于高碳酸钙含量造成的。

The liminess of the clay made it ideal for pottery.  这种粘土的石灰性使它成为制作陶器的理想材料。|近义词：stickiness, viscosity, chalkiness|反义词：smoothness, fluidity|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From lime + -ness.  Lime itself comes from Old English *līm, from Proto-Germanic *līmaz, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *lei- (“to smear, stick”).
