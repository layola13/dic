idealised:/aɪˈdiːəlaɪzd/ | adj. |理想化的；完美的|Regarded or presented as perfect or better than in reality.|She had an idealised view of marriage. 她对婚姻抱有理想化的看法。|Synonyms: romanticized, glorified, exalted, perfect, utopian|Antonyms: realistic, practical, pragmatic, flawed, imperfect|adjective

idealised:/aɪˈdiːəlaɪzd/ | v. |理想化；将…理想化|To regard or represent as perfect or better than in reality.|He idealised his childhood. 他将自己的童年理想化了。|Synonyms: romanticize, glorify, exalt|Antonyms:  debase, degrade, diminish|verb
