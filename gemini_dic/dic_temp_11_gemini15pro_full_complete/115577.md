maraschino:/ˌmærəˈskiːnoʊ/ /ˌmærəˈʃiːnəʊ/
|n.|
|蜜饯樱桃; 马拉斯金樱桃利口酒|
|A maraschino cherry is a preserved, sweetened cherry, typically dyed bright red, used as a garnish in cocktails and desserts. Maraschino also refers to the liqueur in which the cherries are preserved.|
|Example 1: The bartender added a maraschino cherry to my Manhattan.  调酒师在我的曼哈顿鸡尾酒里加了一颗蜜饯樱桃。

Example 2: She loved the maraschino cherries on top of her sundae. 她喜欢圣代上面的蜜饯樱桃。|
|Synonyms: candied cherry, glace cherry|
|Antonyms: fresh cherry|
|Noun|
