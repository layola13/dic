over-assertive:/ˌoʊvər əˈsɜːrtɪv/| adj.|过分自信的；过于武断的；咄咄逼人的|Excessively assertive; too confident and forceful in one's opinions and actions, often to the point of being aggressive or disrespectful.|She was so over-assertive in the meeting that she alienated her colleagues. 她在会议上过于强势，疏远了她的同事。|Synonyms: pushy, aggressive, domineering, overbearing, presumptuous|Antonyms: timid, shy, reserved, unassuming, modest|Adjective
