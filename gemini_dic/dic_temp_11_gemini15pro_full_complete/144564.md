outwalloping: /ˌaʊtˈwɑːləpɪŋ/
verb
彻底击败，重创
defeating decisively; trouncing
The home team outwalloped the visitors 10-0.  主队以10比0大胜客队。
synonyms: thrash, trounce, rout, crush, clobber
antonyms: lose to, be defeated by
