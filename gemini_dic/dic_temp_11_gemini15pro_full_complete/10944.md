araucarian:/ˌærɔːˈkɛəriən/ | adj. |南洋杉的，属于南洋杉科的|Relating to or belonging to the Araucariaceae family of coniferous trees, characterized by their stiff, scale-like or needle-like leaves and often symmetrical branching. |Example: The araucarian forests of South America are home to a diverse range of plant and animal life. 南美洲的南洋杉林是各种动植物的家园。

Example: The monkey puzzle tree is a well-known araucarian species. 智利南洋杉是一种著名的南洋杉科树种。 | 近义词: coniferous (针叶的) | 反义词: deciduous (落叶的) | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Modern Latin *Araucaria*, the genus name, from Araucanía, a region of Chile where these trees are common.
