incapables:/ɪnˈkeɪpəblz/
n.
无能的人；不能胜任的人
People who lack the ability or qualification to do something.
The project was hampered by a team of incapables.  这个项目被一组无能的人阻碍了。
The company is riddled with incapables.  这家公司充满了无能之辈。
synonyms: incompetents, inefficients
antonyms: experts, professionals
词性: 名词(plural)
