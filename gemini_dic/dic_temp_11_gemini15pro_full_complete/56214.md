dodoisms:/ˈdoʊdoʊɪzəmz/
|n.|渡渡鸟式的做法；过时的、不切实际的想法或行为|The politician's dodoisms cost him the election. 这位政客的过时想法让他输掉了选举。|Obsolete practices, outdated beliefs, anachronisms|His insistence on using carrier pigeons was pure dodoism. 他坚持使用信鸽完全是过时的做法。|近义词: anachronisms, archaisms, outdated beliefs|反义词: modernism, innovation, contemporary ideas|词性:名词

词源:  Derived from "dodo," the extinct flightless bird, symbolic of something outdated or extinct. 源自“渡渡鸟”，一种已灭绝的不会飞的鸟，象征着过时或灭绝的事物。
