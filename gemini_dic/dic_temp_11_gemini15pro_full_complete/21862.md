bodkin:/ˈbɒdkɪn/ (US) /ˈbɑːdkɪn/ (UK)| n.|锥子；发簪；钝头针；粗针|A sharp, pointed instrument, usually of metal or bone, used for piercing holes in leather, cloth, etc., or historically, as a dagger.
