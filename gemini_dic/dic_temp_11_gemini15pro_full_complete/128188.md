neuralinker:/ˈnjʊrəlɪŋkər/ | n. | 神经连接器；神经链路器 | A device or system that connects to the nervous system, typically to record or stimulate neural activity.  It often refers specifically to the company Neuralink Corporation and its brain-computer interface technology. |  Elon Musk's company, Neuralink, is developing a neuralinker to help people with paralysis. 伊隆·马斯克的公司Neuralink正在开发一种神经连接器来帮助瘫痪的人。| synonym: brain-computer interface, neuroprosthetic, neural implant | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "natural nervous system") | noun
