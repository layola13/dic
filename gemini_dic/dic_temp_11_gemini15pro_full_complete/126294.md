myochrome:/ˈmaɪəkroʊm/| n. |肌红蛋白|A red protein containing heme, which carries and stores oxygen in muscle cells. |Myoglobin is responsible for the red color of meat. 肌红蛋白是肉呈现红色的原因。| Myoglobin levels can rise in the blood after muscle injury.  肌肉损伤后血液中肌红蛋白水平会升高。|近义词:myohaemoglobin|反义词:|n.
