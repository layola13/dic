hygrine:/ˈhaɪɡriːn/
noun
莨菪烷类生物碱; 海古林; 海桥林
A pyrrolidine alkaloid found in coca leaves. It is a oily liquid.
Hygrine is found in coca leaves.  莨菪碱存在于古柯叶中。
Hygrine is a precursor to other tropane alkaloids.  莨菪烷是其他莨菪烷类生物碱的前体。
alkaloid, tropane derivative
