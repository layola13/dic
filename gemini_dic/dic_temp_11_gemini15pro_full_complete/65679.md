evened-out:/ˈiːvəndˈaʊt/| adj.| 平均的，均衡的，平稳的| Made smooth or uniform, balanced, or stabilized. | The road was evened-out after the construction.  道路施工后变得平坦了。| The stock market has evened-out after the recent volatility.  股票市场在最近的波动后趋于平稳。 | 近义词: balanced, stable, uniform, level, smooth | 反义词: uneven, irregular, volatile, fluctuating | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From the verb "even out," which is formed from the adjective "even" (meaning level or smooth) and the adverb/particle "out."  The word "even" itself has Old English roots (efen).
