bode:/boʊd/| v.|预示，预兆|To indicate something, typically something that will happen in the future.|例句：The dark clouds bode ill for our picnic. 乌云预示着我们的野餐会很不顺利。

例句：This does not bode well for the future of the company. 这对公司的前途可不是好兆头。|近义词：foretell, presage, augur, portend|反义词: N/A|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Old English *bodian "to announce, proclaim, foretell," from Proto-Germanic *bodjanan "to announce," from *bodo "messenger," related to bid.
