cutwork:/ˈkʌtwɜːrk/| n.|镂空制品；镂空细工|Openwork embroidery or needlework. A form of needlework in which portions of the fabric are cut away and the remaining fabric is embellished with embroidery, creating a lacy effect.|The tablecloth was edged with delicate cutwork.|近义词：lacework, openwork, embroidery|反义词: solid fabric|noun
