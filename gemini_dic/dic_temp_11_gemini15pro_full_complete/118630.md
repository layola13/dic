messiah:/məˈsaɪə/| n.|救世主，弥赛亚|A leader or savior who is expected to deliver people from difficulty or suffering.  A title given to Jesus Christ.|例句：He is considered by many to be the messiah.  他被许多人认为是救世主。

例句：They waited for the coming of the messiah. 他们等待着救世主的降临。|近义词：savior, redeemer, deliverer|反义词: oppressor, tyrant|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin Messias, from Greek Messīas, from Aramaic mšīḥā, from Hebrew māšīaḥ  "anointed,"  past participle of māšaḥ "to anoint."
