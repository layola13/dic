overbusily:/ˌoʊvərˈbɪzɪli/
adverb|过于忙碌地，过度忙碌地|Engaging in activity to an excessive degree; too busily.|She overbusily prepared for the party, exhausting herself in the process. 她为派对准备得过于忙碌，把自己累坏了。|Example: He overbusily flitted from task to task, accomplishing little. 他忙得团团转，却没完成多少事。|Synonyms: excessively, too busily, hyperactively|Antonyms: lazily, idly, calmly|词性: 副词
