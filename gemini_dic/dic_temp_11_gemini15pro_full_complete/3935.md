agritourists:/ˈæɡrɪˌtʊərists/
|n.|农业游客|Tourists who visit farms or agricultural areas for recreational or educational purposes, often engaging in activities like picking fruit, visiting wineries, or learning about farming practices.|Many agritourists enjoy the experience of picking their own apples. 许多农业游客喜欢自己采摘苹果的体验。|Synonyms: farm tourists, rural tourists|Antonyms: urban tourists|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
agri- (from Latin ager, "field") + tourist
