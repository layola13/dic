azimuth: /ˈæzɪməθ/ (US), /ˈæzɪmʌθ/ (UK) | n. | 方位角，方位 |  The horizontal angle or direction of a compass bearing. It is measured clockwise from north around to the point in question. | The azimuth of the sunrise was 60 degrees.  日出的方位角是60度。 | synonyms: bearing, direction, heading | antonyms: N/A | noun

azimuth: /ˈæzɪməθ/ (US), /ˈæzɪmʌθ/ (UK) | n. | 方位，方位角 |  The angle between the vertical plane containing a celestial body and the plane of the meridian. (Astronomy) |  Astronomers use azimuth and altitude to locate celestial objects. 天文学家用方位角和高度来定位天体。 | synonyms: bearing, direction, heading | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *azimut, from Arabic السمت (as-samt, “the direction, the way, the path”).
