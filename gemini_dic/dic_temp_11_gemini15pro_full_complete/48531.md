deface:/dɪˈfeɪs/| vt.|污损，毁坏(外观)，丑化|To mar the surface or appearance of; vandalize.|Vandals defaced the statue with graffiti. 暴徒们用涂鸦污损了雕像。|近义词：mar, spoil, damage, vandalize, disfigure|反义词:beautify, enhance, improve|verb

deface:/dɪˈfeɪs/| vt.|使丢脸，使蒙羞|To bring shame or disgrace upon.|His actions defaced the family name. 他的行为让家族蒙羞。|近义词：disgrace, shame, dishonor|反义词:honor, glorify|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "desfacer"  (to disfigure, deface), from "des-" (dis-) + "face" (face).  Ultimately from Latin "facies" (face).
