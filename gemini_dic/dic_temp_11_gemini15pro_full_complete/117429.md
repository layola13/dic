mediocrity:/ˌmiːdiˈɒkrəti/ | n. | 平庸；平凡；庸俗 |  The state or quality of being mediocre; of moderate to low quality, value, ability, or performance.  Lack of distinction or excellence. | His acting career descended into mediocrity after his initial success.  他最初的成功之后，他的演艺事业就变得平庸了。| The restaurant's food was disappointingly mediocre. 这家餐馆的食物令人失望地平庸。 | synonyms: ordinariness, averageness, commonplaceness, banality | antonyms: excellence, brilliance, superiority, distinction | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin mediocritas ("middleness"), from Latin medius ("middle").
