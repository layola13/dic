Genghis:/ˈɡɛŋɡɪs/| n. |成吉思汗|Genghis Khan, born Temüjin, was the founder and first Great Khan of the Mongol Empire, which became the largest contiguous land empire in history after his death.  He came to power by uniting many of the nomadic tribes of Northeast Asia. |Example: Genghis Khan led the Mongols to conquer vast swathes of Asia. 成吉思汗带领蒙古人征服了亚洲的广阔地域。

Example: The legacy of Genghis Khan is still debated today. 成吉思汗的遗产至今仍有争议。 |近义词: Temüjin (his birth name)|反义词: N/A|词性: 名词
