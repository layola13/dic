intentive:/ɪnˈtɛntɪv/| adj.|专注的，聚精会神的，专心的|Characterized by close and careful attention.  Giving careful attention to something or someone.|例句：The students were very attentive during the lecture. 学生们在讲座期间非常专注。|例句：She was an attentive listener. 她是一位专注的倾听者。|近义词：attentive, observant, heedful, mindful|反义词：inattentive, distracted, unmindful|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *intentīvus*, from Latin intentus (“stretched out, strained, intent”), past participle of intendō (“stretch out, strain, extend”).
