/faɪl/ | n. | 文件; 档案; 卷宗; 锉刀 | A collection of data or information stored under a specific name on a computer or other electronic medium; a folder or box for holding loose papers that are typically related to a particular subject or person; a metal tool with a rough surface, typically used for shaping or smoothing wood or metal. |  I saved the file to my computer. 我把文件保存到我的电脑里了。 | synonyms: document, record, data, folder, portfolio | antonyms:  (For the tool meaning) smooth, polish | noun

/faɪl/ | v. | 提交; 提出(申请等); 把…归档; (军队)排成纵队行进 | To submit (a legal document or charge) to be considered by a court; to place (a document) in a designated place for safekeeping; to walk in a single line or in several lines behind one another. |  She filed a complaint against her former employer. 她对她的前雇主提出了诉讼。 | synonyms: submit, lodge, register, queue, march | antonyms: withdraw, retrieve, scatter | verb
