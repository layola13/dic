bilingual:/baɪˈlɪŋɡwəl/| adj. n.|双语的，能说两种语言的；双语使用者|Speaking two languages fluently; able to communicate effectively in two languages. |She is bilingual in English and Spanish. 她精通英语和西班牙语。|Near Synonyms: multilingual, polyglot|Antonym: monolingual|adjective, noun

bilingual:/baɪˈlɪŋɡwəl/| n.|双语使用者|A person who speaks two languages fluently. |Many bilinguals work as translators. 许多双语者从事翻译工作。|Near Synonyms: multilingual, polyglot|Antonym: monolingual|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bi- (two) + lingual (relating to language). Ultimately from Latin *bi- (two) and lingua (tongue, language).
