creme:/krɛm/ | n. | 奶油，乳霜 | A thick, smooth substance, typically pale yellow or white, made from milk or cream and used in cooking or as a topping for desserts. | The cake was decorated with whipped creme. 蛋糕上装饰着鲜奶油。| synonyms: cream, custard | antonyms: - | noun

creme:/krɛm/ | adj. | 奶油色的，乳白色的 | Having the pale yellow or white color of cream. | She wore a creme dress to the wedding. 她穿了一件奶油色的连衣裙参加婚礼。 | synonyms: cream, off-white, ivory | antonyms: black, dark | adjective
