highlighted:/ˈhaɪˌlaɪtɪd/ | adj. | 突出显示的；强调的；醒目的 |  Made prominent or emphasized, often with color or other visual markers.  Derived from "highlight," meaning to emphasize or make prominent.  The "ed" suffix transforms the verb into an adjective describing something that *has been* highlighted. |  The highlighted text in the document was important.  文档中突出显示的文本很重要。

The keywords were highlighted in yellow.  关键词用黄色突出显示。| 近义词: emphasized, marked, stressed, prominent | 反义词: obscure, hidden, subdued, unremarkable | 词性: adjective
