disassembled:/ˌdɪsəˈsɛmbəld/ | adj. | 拆卸的，分解的 | Taken apart; separated into pieces. | The mechanic disassembled the engine to find the problem. 技工拆卸了发动机以查找问题。| The disassembled toy lay scattered on the floor.  拆卸的玩具散落在地板上。|  synonyms: dismantled, taken apart, broken down,  deconstructed | antonyms: assembled, constructed, put together | adjective


disassemble:/ˌdɪsəˈsɛmbəl/ | v. | 拆卸，分解 | To take apart; to separate something into its constituent parts. |  He disassembled the clock to clean it. 他拆卸了钟表进行清洁。 | She carefully disassembled the computer. 她小心地拆卸了电脑。 | synonyms: dismantle, take apart, break down, deconstruct | antonyms: assemble, construct, put together | verb

Etymology:
dis- (prefix) + assemble.  "Dis-" is a Latin prefix meaning "apart," "asunder," "reversal," or "negation."  "Assemble" comes from Old French "assembler," meaning "to bring together."
