entermete:/ˌɛntərˈmiːt/ | n. |娱乐表演，间奏曲，加演|A short dramatic entertainment performed between the acts of a play or other performance.|The entermete between acts was a juggling performance.  幕间表演是杂耍。|Synonyms: interlude, divertissement, intermission entertainment |Antonyms: main act, principal performance |noun


entermete:/ˌɛntərˈmiːt/ | v. |娱乐，招待|To entertain or amuse, especially between the acts of a play or other performance.|The clown entermeted the audience during the intermission.  小丑在幕间休息时娱乐观众。|Synonyms: amuse, entertain, divert |Antonyms: bore, tire, weary |verb
