entender:/entɛnˈdeɾ/ (Spanish), /ˌɛntɛnˈdeɪ/ (anglicized pronunciation)| v. |理解，明白，懂得|To grasp the meaning of something; to comprehend.|例句：No entiendo lo que dices. 我不明白你说什么。|例句：¿Entiendes español? 你懂西班牙语吗？|近义词：comprender, entenderse|反义词:malinterpretar, desconocer|词性:动词
Etymology: From Old Spanish entender, from Latin intendere (“to stretch out, turn one’s attention to, aim at, intend”), from in- (“in, into, on, upon, towards, against”) + tendere (“to stretch, extend”).
