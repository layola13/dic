multihomer:/ˌmʌltiˈhoʊmər/ | n. | 多宿主 | A device or system configured with multiple network interfaces, each with its own IP address, allowing it to communicate on multiple networks simultaneously. |  A multihomer server can provide redundancy and increase bandwidth.  多宿主服务器可以提供冗余并增加带宽。 | synonyms: multihomed device/host | antonyms: single-homed device/host | noun


multihome:/ˌmʌltiˈhoʊm/ | v. |  进行多宿主配置 | To configure a device or system with multiple network interfaces and IP addresses, allowing it to communicate on multiple networks simultaneously.  | The company multihomed its servers to improve reliability. 公司对服务器进行多宿主配置以提高可靠性。 | synonyms: configure for multihoming | antonyms: configure for single-homing | verb
