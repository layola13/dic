conferree:/ˌkɒnfəˈriː/ /ˌkɑːnfəˈriː/
noun
与会者；受赠者
A person who takes part in a conference or meeting.  A person to whom something is conferred, such as an honorary degree.
The conferrees were asked to submit their feedback after the workshop. 研讨会结束后，与会者被要求提交反馈意见。
The university conferred honorary degrees upon several distinguished conferrees. 大学向几位杰出的受赠者授予了荣誉学位。
Synonyms: participant, attendee, recipient, awardee
Antonyms:  absentee, non-participant
