offsetting:/ˌɒfˈsɛtɪŋ/ /ˌɔːfˈsɛtɪŋ/ | adj. n. | 抵消的，补偿的；抵消，补偿 | Balancing one influence against an opposing influence; a consideration or amount that diminishes or balances the effect of an opposite one. | The company's losses were offsetting by gains in other areas. 该公司的亏损被其他领域的收益所抵消。| Synonyms: compensating, counteracting, balancing | Antonyms: increasing, exacerbating | adjective/noun

offset:/ˈɒfset/ /ˈɔːfˌsɛt/ | vt. n. | 抵消，补偿；偏移量，分支 | To counterbalance, compensate for; something that counterbalances or compensates for something else. | The benefits of the new policy offset the disadvantages. 新政策的好处抵消了其缺点。 | Synonyms: counterbalance, compensate, neutralize | Antonyms: enhance, exacerbate | verb/noun
