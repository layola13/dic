multibuy:/ˈmʌltiˌbaɪ/| n.|多件购买，批量购买|A purchase of multiple items of the same product, often at a discounted price.  It encourages consumers to buy more of a product at one time.|The supermarket had a multibuy offer on toilet paper - buy three packs for the price of two.|Near synonyms: bulk buy, multi-pack, quantity discount|Antonym: single purchase|noun
