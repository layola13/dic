localing: /ˈloʊkəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
本地化；局部化
the process of adapting a product or content to a specific locale or market
Example: The company is localizing its software for the Chinese market.  该公司正在将其软件本地化为中文版本。

Example:  They are localizing the website content to appeal to a wider audience.  他们正在本地化网站内容以吸引更广泛的受众。

近义词: adapting, translating, modifying
反义词: globalizing, internationalizing
词性: verb (动名词形式)
