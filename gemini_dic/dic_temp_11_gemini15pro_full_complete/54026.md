discursiveness:/dɪˈskɜːsɪvnəs/
noun
离题性；散漫性；东拉西扯
The quality of being discursive; digressing from one subject to another.
His writing suffers from excessive discursiveness. 他的写作过于散漫，显得冗长。
近义词:rambling, digression, prolixity
反义词:conciseness, brevity, succinctness
词性:n.

discursive:/dɪˈskɜːsɪv/
adjective
散漫的；离题的；东拉西扯的
Passing aimlessly from one subject to another; rambling.
The speaker's discursive style made it hard to follow his argument. 演讲者散漫的风格让人很难跟上他的论点。
近义词:rambling, digressive, meandering
反义词:concise, focused, succinct
词性:adj.
