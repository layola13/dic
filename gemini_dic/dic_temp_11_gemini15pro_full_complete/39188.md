congenialities:/kənˌdʒiːniˈælɪtiz/
n.
适合性；意气相投； congeniality的复数
The warm congenialities of the gathering made everyone feel welcome.  这次聚会的热情友好让每个人都感到宾至如归。
They discovered a surprising number of congenialities in their tastes. 他们在品味上发现了惊人数量的相似之处。
Synonyms: affinities, compatibilities, harmonies, rapport, accord
Antonyms: disagreements, clashes, conflicts, discord, antipathies
Noun


congeniality:/kənˌdʒiːniˈælɪti/
n.
意气相投；适合性；同质性；合宜性
She and her roommate share a deep congeniality of spirit. 她和她的室友精神上十分契合。
The congeniality of the atmosphere put him at ease. 舒适的氛围让他感到放松。
Synonyms: affinity, compatibility, harmony, rapport, accord
Antonyms: disagreement, clash, conflict, discord, antipathy
Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin congenialitās, from Latin congeniālis (“of the same kind, kindred”) + -itās. Equivalent to congenial + -ity.
