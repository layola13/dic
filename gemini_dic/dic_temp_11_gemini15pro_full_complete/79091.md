glaciered:/ˈɡleɪʃərd/ | adj. | 冰川覆盖的；冰川作用形成的 | Covered or affected by glaciers.  |  The glaciered peaks of the Himalayas are breathtaking. 喜马拉雅山冰川覆盖的山峰令人叹为观止。|  The valley was glaciered during the last ice age.  这个山谷在上一个冰河时代被冰川覆盖。 | icy, glacial |  arid, barren | adjective

词源: From glacier + -ed.  源自 glacier（冰川） + -ed（形容词后缀，表示“被…覆盖的”）。
