lilac:/ˈlaɪlək/ (US), /ˈlaɪlək/ (UK)| n. adj.|丁香花，淡紫色|A shrub or small tree that produces fragrant, purplish flowers. Also, the pale purple color of these flowers.|The lilac bushes in my garden are in full bloom.  我花园里的丁香花盛开了。|近义词：lavender, violet, mauve|反义词:(color) orange, yellow|noun, adjective
