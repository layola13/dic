intertwist:/ˌɪntərˈtwɪst/ | v. | 交织，缠绕，盘绕 | To twist or twine together; interweave. | The vines intertwisted around the tree trunk. 藤蔓缠绕在树干上。|  The plot lines of the novel intertwisted in a complex and intriguing way. 小说的情节以一种复杂而有趣的方式交织在一起。 | 近义词: interweave, entwine, interlace, braid | 反义词: untwist, disentangle, separate | 词性: 动词

词源:
From inter- + twist.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," and "twist" refers to the act of winding or turning something.
