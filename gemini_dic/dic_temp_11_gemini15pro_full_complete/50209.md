department:/dɪˈpɑːrtmənt/ /dəˈpɑːrtmənt/ | n. |部门，系，科|A specialized division of a large organization. |The sales department is having a meeting. 销售部正在开会。| 近义词: division, section, branch, unit |反义词: whole, entirety | noun

department:/dɪˈpɑːrtmənt/ /dəˈpɑːrtmənt/ | n. |（尤指大学的）系|One of the major sections of a university. |She's a professor in the history department. 她是历史系的一名教授。| 近义词: faculty, school |反义词: university (as a whole) | noun

department:/dɪˈpɑːrtmənt/ /dəˈpɑːrtmənt/ | n. |（法国等的）省|A major territorial unit in France and some other countries. |The department of Loire is known for its wines.  卢瓦尔省以其葡萄酒而闻名。| 近义词: province, region |反义词: country (as a whole) | noun
