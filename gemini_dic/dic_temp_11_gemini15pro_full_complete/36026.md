codirecting:/kəʊdaɪˈrɛktɪŋ/
Verb
共同执导，共同指导
The film was codirected by two talented filmmakers. 这部电影由两位才华横溢的电影制作人共同执导。
They are codirecting a new play for the festival. 他们正在为节日共同指导一部新戏。
co-directing (hyphenated form also acceptable)
共同指导, 联合导演
The process of directing something together with another person or persons.  Two directors codirecting a film often have complementary skills. 两个共同执导电影的导演通常具有互补的技能。
She is codirecting the research project with her colleague. 她和她的同事共同指导这个研究项目。
collaborating, co-managing, joint directing, sharing direction
sole directing, directing alone, independently directing
