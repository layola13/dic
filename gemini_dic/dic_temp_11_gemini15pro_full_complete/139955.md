odontoid:/ˈɒdənˌtɔɪd/| adj. n.|齿状的，齿突|Resembling a tooth; tooth-shaped.  Specifically refers to the dens, a tooth-like projection from the second cervical vertebra (axis).|The odontoid process is crucial for head rotation.|近义词：tooth-shaped, dentoid|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider terms like "smooth" or "flat" when referring to shape)|adjective, noun
