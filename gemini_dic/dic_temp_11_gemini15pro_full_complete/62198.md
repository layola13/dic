encounter:/ɪnˈkaʊntər/ (US) /ɛnˈkaʊntə(r)/ (UK)| v.|遇见，邂逅；与…冲突，与…交战|To meet someone or something unexpectedly. |The hikers encountered a bear in the woods.  徒步旅行者在树林里遇到了一只熊。|近义词：meet, come across, experience, face, confront|反义词:avoid, miss, evade|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *encontrer*, from *en-* (in, into) + *contrer* (to meet, oppose). Ultimately from Latin *contra* (against).
