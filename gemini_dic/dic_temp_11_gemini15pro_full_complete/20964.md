blemishing:/ˈblɛmɪʃɪŋ/
n.|瑕疵，缺点，污点|An imperfection that mars or spoils the appearance of something.|The scandal was a blemishing on his career. 这桩丑闻是他职业生涯中的一个污点。|近义词：defect, flaw, imperfection, fault, mark, spot, stain|反义词：perfection, asset, virtue|词性：名词

blemish:/ˈblɛmɪʃ/
vt.|损害，玷污|To spoil the appearance or quality of something.|The accusations blemished his reputation. 这些指控损害了他的名誉。|近义词：mar, spoil, tarnish, damage, impair|反义词：enhance, improve, perfect|词性：动词
