deia:/ˈdeɪə/| n. |命运，天意，神谕|A divinely ordained fate or destiny; an oracle or prophecy. |The deia of the oracle foretold a great victory. 神谕预言了一场伟大的胜利。|近义词：fate, destiny, oracle, prophecy|反义词:free will, choice|noun

deia:/ˈdeɪə/| n. |女神|A female divinity or goddess. (Archaic, poetic)|The deia of the forest protected the animals. 森林女神保护着动物们。|近义词：goddess, divinity|反义词:god, male deity|noun


词源: From Ancient Greek δαίμων (daímōn, “deity, divine power, fate”).
