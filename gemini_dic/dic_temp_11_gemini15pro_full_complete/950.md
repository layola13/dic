academical:/ˌækəˈdemɪkl/ | adj. | 学术的；学院的；理论的 | Relating to a college or university, or to academic studies; theoretical. |  He has a brilliant academical record. 他有着辉煌的学术记录。|  The question is purely academical. 这个问题纯粹是理论上的。| 近义词：scholarly, academic, theoretical | 反义词：practical, applied, non-academic | 词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin academicus, from Greek akadēmikos, from akadēmeia ‘Academy’.
