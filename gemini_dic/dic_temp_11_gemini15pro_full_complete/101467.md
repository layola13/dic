irgun:/ɪrˈɡuːn/| n.|伊尔贡, 国家军事组织 (以色列)|A Zionist paramilitary organization that operated in Mandate Palestine between 1931 and 1948.|The Irgun was a key player in the struggle for Israeli independence. 伊尔贡是以色列独立斗争中的关键角色。|The Irgun carried out several controversial operations. 伊尔贡执行了几次有争议的行动。|synonyms:  Haganah (partially), Lehi, Etzel (alternative name)|antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps organizations opposing Zionist goals, e.g., certain Arab groups during that period)|noun
