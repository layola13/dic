brownier:/ˈbraʊniər/| adj.|更棕色的，更褐色的|Comparative form of brown.  Used to describe something that has a deeper or richer brown color than something else.|This brownie is brownier than the one I baked yesterday.  这个布朗尼比我昨天烤的顏色更深。|近义词：darker, richer|反义词:lighter, paler|词性:形容词

词源:  From brown + -er (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from Old English brūn.
