medicornu: /ˌmɛdɪˈkɔːrnjuː/
n.|羊角；羊角状物|A horn-like anatomical structure, specifically the hippocampus minor, a projection into the posterior horn of the lateral ventricle of the brain.|The medicornu is located in the brain. 羊角位于大脑中。|synonyms: hippocampus minor, calcar avis|antonyms: N/A|noun
