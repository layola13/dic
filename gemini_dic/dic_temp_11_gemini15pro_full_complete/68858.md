fashions:/ˈfæʃənz/
noun
时尚，潮流
the popular or latest style of clothing, hair, decoration, or behaviour
例句：She's always interested in the latest fashions. 她总是对最新的时尚感兴趣。

例句：Fashions change quickly these days. 如今时尚变化很快。

近义词：trends, styles, modes
反义词：tradition, old-fashionedness
词性：noun


fashions:/ˈfæʃənz/
verb
塑造，制作
to shape or make something, usually with your hands
例句：He fashioned a boat out of a piece of wood. 他用一块木头做了一艘船。

例句：The artist fashioned the clay into a beautiful sculpture. 艺术家把黏土塑造成一个美丽的雕塑。

近义词：create, make, form
反义词：destroy, ruin
词性：verb
