local's: /ˈloʊkəlz/| n.|当地人的；本地人的|Something that belongs to or is associated with the local people of a particular area.  The possessive form of "local".|例句：The local's favorite bar was always crowded. 当地人最喜欢的酒吧总是很拥挤。

例句：This restaurant is a local's secret; tourists rarely find it.  这家餐馆是当地人的秘密，游客很少找到它。|近义词：inhabitant's, resident's, native's|反义词：foreigner's, tourist's|词性: 名词所有格
