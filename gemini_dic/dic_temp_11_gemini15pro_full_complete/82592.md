guidance:/ˈɡaɪdns/| n.|指导，引导，咨询|The students benefited greatly from the teacher's guidance. 学生们从老师的指导中受益匪浅。|Synonyms: direction, advice, counsel, support, leadership|Antonyms: misguidance, neglect|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *guidonce "guidance, conduct," from guider "to guide," from Frankish *witan "to show the way," from Proto-Germanic *witanan, from PIE root *weid- "to see."
