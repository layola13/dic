olympian:/əˈlɪmpiən/ | n. adj. | 奥林匹克运动员；奥林匹克的；庄严的，崇高的 |  A person who participates in the Olympic Games; relating to the Olympic Games or their ideals of athleticism and sportsmanship; majestic and godlike in manner. |  例句：She's a true Olympian, dedicated to her sport. 她是一位真正的奥林匹克运动员，全身心投入到她的运动中。| 近义词：athlete, competitor, participant, majestic, grand, august | 反义词：undignified, unimpressive | 词性: 名词, 形容词

olympian:/əˈlɪmpiən/ | adj. | 奥林匹克的；庄严的，崇高的 | Relating to the Olympic Games or their ideals of athleticism and sportsmanship; majestic and godlike in manner. | 例句：The opening ceremony had an Olympian grandeur. 开幕式具有奥林匹克的庄严气派。| 近义词：majestic, grand, august, stately | 反义词：undignified, unimpressive, humble | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek "Ὀλύμπιος" (Olýmpios), meaning "of Olympus," the mythical home of the Greek gods.  The word was later adopted to refer to participants in the Olympic Games, held in ancient Greece at Olympia.
