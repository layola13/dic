bailiffship: /ˈbeɪlɪfʃɪp/ | n. | 法警的职位；法警的管辖区域 | The office or jurisdiction of a bailiff.  A bailiff is a legal officer responsible for enforcing court orders. |  He inherited the bailiffship from his father. 他从他父亲那里继承了法警的职位。 | The bailiffship covers a wide rural area.  这个法警的管辖区域覆盖了广阔的农村地区。 |近义词: bailiwick | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps lack of jurisdiction) | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *baillifshipe*, equivalent to bailiff +‎ -ship.  Ultimately from Old French *baillif* ("guardian, administrator").
