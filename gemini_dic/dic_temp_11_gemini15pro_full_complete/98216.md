insecure:/ˌɪnsɪˈkjʊr/ /ˌɪn.səˈkjʊr/ | adj. |不安全的，不牢靠的；缺乏信心的，不自信的|Not confident or assured; uncertain and anxious.|例句：She felt insecure about her ability to do the job. 她对自己的工作能力感到不自信。|近义词：anxious, apprehensive, uncertain, doubtful, timid, shy|反义词：secure, confident, self-assured|词性:形容词

insecure:/ˌɪnsɪˈkjʊr/ /ˌɪn.səˈkjʊr/ | n. |不安全感|Lack of confidence or assurance.|例句：Her insecurity made her reluctant to speak up. 她的不安全感使她不愿开口。|近义词：anxiety, apprehension, uncertainty, self-doubt|反义词：security, confidence, self-assurance|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + secure.  The word "secure" ultimately comes from Latin "securus" meaning "free from care."
