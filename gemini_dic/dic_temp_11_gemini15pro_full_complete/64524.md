eroticists:/ɪˈrɒtɪsɪsts/
n.
色情作家；好色之徒
A person who is interested in or writes about erotic things.
The eroticists of the 18th century often used pseudonyms to protect their identities.  18世纪的色情作家经常使用假名来保护自己的身份。
The book was written by a notorious eroticists. 这本书是由一位臭名昭著的色情作家写的。
近义词: sensualists, hedonists
反义词:  puritans, ascetics
词性: noun
