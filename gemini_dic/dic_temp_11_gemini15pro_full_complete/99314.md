intercross:/ˌɪntərˈkrɒs/ (US) /ˌɪntərˈkrɔːs/ (UK)| n. v.|杂交, 交配, 混血儿|The act of crossing different breeds or varieties of animals or plants.|Scientists intercrossed different strains of mice to study the inheritance of certain traits. 科学家们将不同品系的老鼠杂交以研究某些性状的遗传。|近义词：crossbreed, hybridize|反义词: inbreed|词性: 名词, 动词
