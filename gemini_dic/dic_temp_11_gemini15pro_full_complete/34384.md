classifier: /ˈklæsɪfaɪər/ (US), /ˈklæsɪfaɪə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 分类器，分类者 | A person or thing that classifies or sorts things into categories. | Example: The librarian is a classifier of books. 图书管理员是书籍的分类者。

Example:  A decision tree is a common type of classifier in machine learning.  决策树是机器学习中一种常见的分类器。|Synonyms: categorizer, sorter, organizer | Antonyms: mixer, jumbler, disorganizer | Noun
