evolutions:/ˌɛvəˈluːʃənz/
n.
演变，进化；发展，进展
The process by which different kinds of living organisms are thought to have developed and diversified from earlier forms during the history of the earth.  The gradual development of something, especially from a simple to a more complex form.
例句：The evolution of the internet has changed the way we communicate. 互联网的演变改变了我们交流的方式。
例句：Darwin's theory of evolution revolutionized biology. 达尔文的进化论彻底改变了生物学。
近义词：development, progress, advancement, growth, transformation
反义词：regression, stagnation, deterioration, devolution
词性：名词

Etymology:
From Latin *ēvolūtiōn- (stem of ēvolūtiō), from ēvolvere "to unroll, unfold, develop," from ex- "out" + volvere "to roll"
