demote:/dɪˈmoʊt/ (US), /diːˈməʊt/ (UK)| v. |降级，降职|To lower in rank or position; to relegate to a lower grade or class.|He was demoted from manager to assistant manager. 他被从经理降职为助理经理.|The soldier was demoted for disobeying orders.  这名士兵因不服从命令而被降级。|synonyms: downgrade, relegate, lower, reduce|antonyms: promote, upgrade, elevate, advance|verb
