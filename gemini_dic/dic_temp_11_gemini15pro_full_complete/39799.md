consortia:/kənˈsɔːrʃə/ /kənˈsɔːrtiə/
|n.|财团，联合企业，联盟|A consortium is an association of two or more individuals, companies, organizations or governments (or any combination of these entities) with the objective of participating in a common activity or pooling their resources for achieving a common goal.| Several European consortia are bidding for the contract.  几个欧洲财团正在竞标这份合同。|Synonyms: association, federation, alliance, coalition, syndicate, partnership|Antonyms: individual, single, separate|noun
