nonfragileness:/ˌnɒnfrəˈdʒɪlnəs/
|n.|反脆弱性，抗脆性
|The quality of benefiting from disorder and uncertainty; the ability to thrive and improve in response to stressors, shocks, volatility, noise, mistakes, faults, attacks, or failures.|
|Example 1: The nonfragileness of the system allows it to adapt and grow stronger in the face of unexpected challenges.  系统的反脆弱性使其能够适应并从意想不到的挑战中变得更加强大。

Example 2:  He argued for building nonfragileness into our institutions and systems. 他主张将反脆弱性构建到我们的机构和系统中。|
|Synonyms: resilience, robustness, antifragility|
|Antonyms: fragility, vulnerability, weakness|
|noun|
