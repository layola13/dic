aphoristical:/ˌæfəˈrɪstɪkəl/
adj.
格言式的；警句式的
terse and witty, like an aphorism; expressed concisely and forcefully
His aphoristical style of writing made his essays memorable. 他格言式的写作风格使他的文章令人难忘。
近义词：pithy, concise, epigrammatic, sententious
反义词：prolix, verbose, rambling
