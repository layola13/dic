disanchor:/dɪsˈæŋkər/
verb
解锚；使不再固定；使不安定
To loosen or release from an anchor; to detach or free from a fixed position.
The ship disanchored at dawn and set sail.  黎明时分，船解锚起航。
The storm disanchored the buoy.  暴风雨使浮标脱离了固定位置。
近义词: detach, unmoor, loosen, free, release
反义词: anchor, secure, fasten, moor
词性:v.
