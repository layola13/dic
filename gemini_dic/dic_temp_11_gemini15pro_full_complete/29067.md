carve: /kɑːrv/ (British) /kɑːrv/ (American)
verb
雕刻，刻; 切，割
To cut into a surface, typically wood or stone, to create a three-dimensional form or design.  Also, to cut slices from a larger piece of food, especially meat.
He carved a beautiful statue from a single block of marble. 他用一整块大理石雕刻了一座美丽的雕像。

The chef carved the turkey expertly.  厨师熟练地切着火鸡。

近义词: sculpt, chisel, engrave, slice, whittle
反义词: construct, build, assemble
词性: verb
