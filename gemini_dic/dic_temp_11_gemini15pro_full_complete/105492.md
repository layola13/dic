kilned:/kɪlnd/ | adj. | 烧过的，窑烧的 | Heated in a kiln; baked, fired, or dried in a kiln. |  The bricks were kilned at a high temperature.  这些砖是在高温下烧制的。| synonyms: baked, fired, roasted | antonyms: raw, unbaked, green | adjective

kiln:/kɪln/ | n. | 窑 | An oven for burning, baking, or drying, especially one used for firing pottery, bricks, or lime. |  The pottery is fired in a large kiln.  陶器是在一个大窑里烧制的。| synonyms: oven, furnace, roaster | antonyms:  (no direct antonym, perhaps "cooler") | noun
