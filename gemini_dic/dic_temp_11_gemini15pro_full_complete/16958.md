bassoonists:/bəˈsuːnɪsts/
noun
巴松管演奏家；巴松管吹奏者
A person who plays the bassoon, a double-reed woodwind instrument.
The bassoonists tuned their instruments before the concert began.  巴松管演奏家们在音乐会开始前调音了他们的乐器。
The orchestra had two talented bassoonists.  这个管弦乐队有两个才华横溢的巴松管演奏家。
synonyms:bassoon player
antonyms:N/A
