apprenticeage:/əˈprɛntɪsɪdʒ/ |n.| 学徒期，学徒身份|The period of time during which someone is an apprentice.  The state or condition of being an apprentice.|He served his apprenticeage as a carpenter. 他当木匠学徒期满。|Synonyms: apprenticeship, traineeship, internship|Antonyms: mastery, expertise|noun
