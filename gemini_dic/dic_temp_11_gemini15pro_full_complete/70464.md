figurine:/ˌfɪɡjəˈriːn/| n.|小雕像，小塑像|A small statue or model of a person, animal, or other subject, typically made of china, plastic, or metal.|She collects porcelain figurines of birds.|近义词：statuette, model, miniature|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑giant sculpture 巨型雕塑)|noun
