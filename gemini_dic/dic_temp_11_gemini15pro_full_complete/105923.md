kjoekken:/ˈkøkən/ | n. | kitchen | A room or area where food is prepared and cooked.  From Danish *køkken*, from Middle Low German *köken*, ultimately from Latin *coquina*. |  She bakes bread in her kjoekken every Sunday. 她每个星期天都在她的厨房里烤面包。 |  The kjoekken is small but well-equipped. 厨房虽小，但设备齐全。 | synonyms: galley, cookhouse | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical space.
