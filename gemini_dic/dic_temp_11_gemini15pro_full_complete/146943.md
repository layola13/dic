noun
过度关心，过分热心
Excessive interest or concern, often to an inappropriate degree.
His overinterestednesses in his neighbor's affairs caused tension. 他对邻居事务的过分关心导致了紧张关系。
Overinterestedness in a child's life can stifle their independence. 对孩子生活过分的关心会扼杀他们的独立性。
Synonyms: nosiness, intrusiveness, meddlesomeness
Antonyms: indifference, apathy, detachment
