nondepreciating:/ˌnɒndɪˈpriːʃieɪtɪŋ/
adjective
不贬值的
Not decreasing in value over time.
Gold is often considered a nondepreciating asset. 黄金通常被认为是不贬值的资产。
The collector hoped his rare stamps would prove to be a nondepreciating investment.  这位收藏家希望他珍稀的邮票最终能成为一项不贬值的投资。
近义词:appreciating, inflation-proof
反义词:depreciating, declining

词性:adjective
