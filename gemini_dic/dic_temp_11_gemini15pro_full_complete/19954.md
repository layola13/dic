biodynamical:/ˌbaɪoʊdaɪˈnæmɪkəl/
adjective
生物动力学的
Relating to or characterized by biodynamics, a system of farming that treats the farm as a unified and self-sustaining ecosystem.
Biodynamic agriculture views the farm as a self-regulating ecosystem. 生物动力农业将农场视为一个自我调节的生态系统。
The vineyard uses biodynamical practices, such as burying cow horns filled with manure.  这个葡萄园采用生物动力法，例如将装满肥料的牛角埋在地里。
Near synonyms: organic, ecological, sustainable
Antonym: conventional
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
bio- + dynamical
bio-: from Greek bios "life"
dynamical: from Greek dynamis "power"
