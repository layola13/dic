lamb:/læm/| n. |羔羊，小羊；羔羊肉|A young sheep, typically less than one year old. The meat of a young sheep.|The lamb followed its mother. 小羊跟着它的妈妈。|近义词：sheep, ewe (female), ram (male)|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 predator 捕食者)|noun

lamb:/læm/| v. |产羊羔|To give birth to a lamb.|The ewe lambed this morning. 母羊今天早上产下羊羔。|近义词：bear, deliver|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *lamb, from Proto-Germanic *lambaz, from Proto-Indo-European *lembʰ-. Cognate with Dutch lam, German Lamm, Old Norse lamb.
