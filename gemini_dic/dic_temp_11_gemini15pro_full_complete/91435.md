hygienic:/haɪˈdʒiːnɪk/ |adj.|卫生的，清洁的|Clean and conducive to health.|例句：It's important to keep the kitchen hygienic. 保持厨房卫生很重要。

例句：Hygienic conditions are essential in a hospital. 医院里的卫生条件至关重要。|近义词：clean, sanitary, sterile, antiseptic|反义词：unhygienic, dirty, insanitary|词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *hygiénique* or directly from Greek *hygieinē* (feminine of *hygieinós* 'relating to health'), from *hygieia* 'health'. Ultimately from the name of the Greek goddess of health, Hygieia (Ὑγιεία).
