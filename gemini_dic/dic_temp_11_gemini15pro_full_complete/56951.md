dotting:/ˈdɒtɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈdɑːtɪŋ/ (US)| adj. | 散布的; 点缀的 | Appearing in scattered or isolated instances. |  Dotting the landscape were small villages.  散布在景色中是些小村庄。 | synonyms: scattered, sporadic, interspersed | antonyms: concentrated, clustered | adjective
