claudicant: /ˈklɔːdɪkənt/| adj.|跛行的，蹒跚的|Limping; halting.  Referring to a person or animal that limps.|The claudicant dog struggled to keep up with its owner. 那只跛脚的狗努力跟上它的主人。|近义词：limping, halting, hobbled, lame|反义词:agile, nimble, sound|adjective

claudication: /ˌklɔːdɪˈkeɪʃən/| n.|跛行，间歇性跛行|The condition of limping; intermittent claudication refers to pain in the leg muscles that occurs during exercise and is relieved by rest, typically caused by obstruction of the arteries.|His claudication made it difficult for him to walk long distances. 他的跛行使他难以行走很长的距离。|近义词：limping, lameness|反义词:mobility, soundness|noun
