dissemblers:/dɪˈsɛmblərz/
n.
伪装者，掩饰者
People who conceal their true motives, feelings, or beliefs.
The courtroom was full of dissemblers, each with their own carefully crafted lies.  法庭上满是伪装者，每个人都精心编造了谎言。
He was a master of dissembling, hiding his true intentions behind a charming facade. 他是个伪装大师，用迷人的外表掩盖他真正的意图。
synonyms: liars, deceivers, hypocrites, pretenders
antonyms: truth-tellers, honest people
