kpd:/keɪpiːˈdiː/| abbreviation | Key Performance Driver | Key Performance Driver is a factor that has a direct impact on the success of a specific aspect of a business. |  Example: Increasing customer satisfaction is a key performance driver for our company's growth. 提高客户满意度是我们公司增长的关键绩效驱动因素。 | Example:  The kpd for this project is the number of units sold. 这个项目的关键绩效驱动因素是销售的单位数量。 | Synonym: key performance indicator (KPI), key success factor (KSF) | Antonym: N/A | noun
