acropolitan:/ˌækrəˈpɒlɪtən/
adjective
居住在城堡里的，城堡的
Relating to or inhabiting a citadel, especially the Acropolis in Athens.
The acropolitan ruins offered a breathtaking view of the city.  城堡遗址提供了令人叹为观止的城市景观。
Near Antonyms:rural, suburban
Etymology:  From Greek *akropolis* (ἀκρόπολις), from *akros* (ἄκρος, "highest, topmost") + *polis* (πόλις, "city").  词源：来自希腊语 *akropolis* (ἀκρόπολις)，由 *akros* (ἄκρος，“最高的，最上面的”) + *polis* (πόλις，“城市”) 组成。
