hortyard: /ˈɔːrtjɑːrd/ (UK) /ˈɔːrtjɑːrd/ (US)| n. |果园，果树园|An enclosed piece of land devoted to the cultivation of fruit trees.|例句：The apple hortyards were in full bloom. 苹果园里鲜花盛开。
