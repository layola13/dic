expansion:/ɪkˈspænʃən/ | n. | 扩张，膨胀，扩展；扩大，发展 | the act of increasing (something) in size or volume or quantity or scope | The company is undergoing a period of rapid expansion.  这家公司正处于快速扩张时期。| synonyms: growth, enlargement, extension, development | antonyms: contraction, reduction, shrinking | noun

expansion:/ɪkˈspænʃən/ | n. | 阐述；详述 | a detailed explanation or discussion of a subject |  His book includes an expansion of his earlier theories. 他的书包含了他早期理论的详细阐述。 | synonyms: elaboration, exposition, amplification | antonyms: abbreviation, condensation, summary | noun
