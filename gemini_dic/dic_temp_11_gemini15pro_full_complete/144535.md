outvie:/ˌaʊtˈvaɪ/
verb
超越，胜过
To surpass or excel someone in achievement or skill.
He outvied his rivals in the competition. 他在比赛中胜过对手。
She strives to outvie her classmates in every subject. 她努力在每门学科上都超过她的同学。

近义词: surpass, excel, exceed, outdo, outstrip, beat, defeat, conquer, triumph over
反义词: lose to, be defeated by, fall behind

词性: verb

词源: From Middle English *outvien, from out- + vien (“to strive, contend”), from Old French enviier (“to vie with, compete with”).
