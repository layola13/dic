nuncius: /ˈnʌnʃɪəs/ | n. | 使者，信使，传令官；消息，音讯 | A messenger; one who announces or reports something. | The emperor sent a nuncius to deliver the message. 皇帝派了一名信使去传递消息。 | synonyms: messenger, envoy, emissary, herald, courier | antonyms: recipient, receiver | noun

nuncius: /ˈnʌnʃɪəs/ | n. | message, news, announcement |  A piece of news or information. This meaning is less common than the "messenger" meaning. | The nuncius of the king's victory spread quickly. 国王胜利的消息迅速传播开来。 | synonyms: message, news, tidings, report, announcement | antonyms: silence, suppression | noun
