miscuts: /ˈmɪskʌts/
noun
错误切割; 切错; 切坏的部分
Incorrectly cut pieces or parts, often referring to paper, fabric, or other materials.
The miscuts in the fabric made it unusable for the project.  这些布料的错误裁剪使得它无法用于该项目。
The printer produced several miscuts, wasting a lot of paper.  打印机印出了几张切坏的纸，浪费了很多纸。
Synonyms: misprints, errors, mistakes, flaws, defects, imperfections
Antonyms: perfect cuts, accurate cuts, precise cuts
