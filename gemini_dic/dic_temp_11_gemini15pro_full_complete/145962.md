overdistorted: /ˌoʊvərˈdɪstɔːrtɪd/ | adj. | 过度扭曲的，过度失真的 | Excessively distorted; altered to an excessive degree, especially so as to misrepresent. | The image was so overdistorted it was barely recognizable. 这张图片过度失真，几乎无法辨认。 | The sound was overdistorted, making it unpleasant to listen to.  声音过度失真，听起来很不舒服。| synonyms: exaggerated, warped, misshapen, contorted | antonyms: accurate, faithful, undistorted | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "distorted" (past participle of "distort," from Latin "distorquere," meaning "to twist awry").
