olivine:/ˈɒlɪˌviːn/ (US) /ˈɒlɪvɪn/ (UK)| n. |橄榄石|A magnesium iron silicate mineral ((Mg, Fe)₂SiO₄) that is a major constituent of basalt and peridotite. It is typically olive-green in color, hence its name. |Olivine is a common mineral in Earth's mantle. 橄榄石是地幔中常见的矿物。

Olivine is used in some refractories. 橄榄石用于某些耐火材料。|近义词：chrysolite (宝石级橄榄石), peridot (宝石级橄榄石)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


olivine:/ˈɒlɪˌviːn/ (US) /ˈɒlɪvɪn/ (UK)| adj. |橄榄石的，橄榄绿色的|Made of or containing olivine; having the color of olivine. |The beach sand was olivine-rich, giving it a greenish hue.  沙滩富含橄榄石，呈现出绿色色调。

The olivine crystals sparkled in the sunlight. 橄榄石晶体在阳光下闪闪发光。|近义词：olive-green, olive-drab|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective (less common)
