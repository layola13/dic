moratoriums:/ˌmɔːrəˈtɔːriəmz/ | n. | 暂停，延期，中止 | A temporary prohibition of an activity. | The government imposed a moratorium on nuclear testing. 政府宣布暂停核试验。|  The bank declared a moratorium on loan repayments for businesses affected by the flood.  银行宣布对受洪水影响的企业暂停偿还贷款。| synonyms: suspension, postponement, delay, halt, standstill | antonyms: continuation, resumption, progression | noun
