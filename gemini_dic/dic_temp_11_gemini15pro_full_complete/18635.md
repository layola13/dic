benzamide:/ˈbɛnzəmaɪd/ | n. |苯甲酰胺|An amide of benzoic acid, C₇H₇NO, used in the synthesis of various organic compounds. |Benzamide is a common starting material in organic synthesis. 苯甲酰胺是有机合成中常用的起始原料。| Benzamide is prepared by reacting benzoyl chloride with ammonia. 苯甲酰胺是通过苯甲酰氯与氨反应制备的。 |Synonyms:  benzoylamide| Antonyms: None | noun
