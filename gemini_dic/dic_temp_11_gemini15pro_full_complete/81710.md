greenish:/ˈɡriːnɪʃ/| adj.|浅绿色的，略带绿色的|Somewhat green.  A color that is between green and another color, like yellow or blue, but mostly green.|The water in the lagoon had a greenish tinge.  泻湖里的水略带绿色。|近义词：greeny, olive, viridescent|反义词: reddish, bluish, yellowish|词性:形容词
Etymology: From green + -ish.  The suffix "-ish" is used to form adjectives meaning "somewhat" or "slightly".
