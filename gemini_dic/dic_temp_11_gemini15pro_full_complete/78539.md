ghostings:/ˈɡoʊstɪŋz/
n.
故意冷落，玩失踪 (尤指在恋爱或其他亲密关系中)
The act of ending a personal relationship with someone by suddenly and without explanation withdrawing from all communication.
She was upset by his ghostings. 她被他玩失踪的行为弄得很伤心。
近义词：ignoring, ditching, disappearing, cutting off contact
反义词：communicating, maintaining contact, being present
词性: 名词 (Noun)


ghosting:/ˈɡoʊstɪŋ/
n.
故意冷落，玩失踪 (尤指在恋爱或其他亲密关系中)
The act of ending a personal relationship with someone by suddenly and without explanation withdrawing from all communication.
He's a serial ghoster; he's done this to several women. 他是一个惯犯，对好几个女人都做过这种事。
近义词：ignoring, ditching, disappearing, cutting off contact
反义词：communicating, maintaining contact, being present
词性: 名词 (Noun)


词源:  来源于动词 "ghost" (像鬼魂一样消失) 的现在分词形式，加上复数后缀 -s，表示多次或多人的这种行为。
