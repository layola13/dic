chrysalize:/ˈkrɪsəlaɪz/| v. | 蛹化；蜕变；进入休眠状态 |To enter a transformative state, like a chrysalis, often implying a period of seclusion or inactivity before emerging in a changed form. | The caterpillar began to chrysalize, spinning a silken cocoon around itself. 毛毛虫开始化蛹，吐丝结茧把自己裹起来。

The writer chrysalized in his cabin for months, working on his novel.  这位作家在他的小屋里蛰伏了几个月，创作他的小说。 | 近义词: metamorphose, transform, evolve | 反义词: regress, stagnate, deteriorate | 词性: 动词
