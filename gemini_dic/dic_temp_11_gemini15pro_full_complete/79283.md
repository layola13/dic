glaucometer:/ɡlɔːˈkɒmɪtər/ | n. | 青光眼计，眼压计 | An instrument used to measure intraocular pressure (IOP), the fluid pressure inside the eye, which is important in the diagnosis and management of glaucoma. | Example 1: The ophthalmologist used a glaucometer to check the patient's IOP. 眼科医生使用青光眼计检查病人的眼压。

Example 2: Regular checkups with a glaucometer are essential for people with a family history of glaucoma.  对于有青光眼家族史的人来说，定期使用青光眼计检查至关重要。 | 近义词: tonometer | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists) | 词性: noun

Etymology: glaucoma + -meter.  "Glaucoma" comes from Greek glaukōma, meaning "cataract of the eye, opacity of the crystalline lens," from glaukos "gleaming, silvery," also "bluish-green, gray,"  + -ōma, noun suffix. "-meter" comes from Greek metron "measure."
