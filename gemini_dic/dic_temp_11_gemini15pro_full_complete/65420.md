euphorbium:/juːˈfɔːrbiəm/
n.
大戟胶；一种有毒的树脂状物质
A gum resin obtained from various Old World spurges of the genus *Euphorbia*, formerly used as a purgative.
The doctor prescribed euphorbium to purge the patient's system, a practice now considered dangerous due to its toxicity.  医生给病人开了大戟胶来净化他的系统，这种做法现在被认为是危险的，因为它有毒性。
Synonyms: spurge, gum resin
