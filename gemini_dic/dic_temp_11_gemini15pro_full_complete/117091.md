meager:/ˈmiːɡər/ (US), /ˈmiːɡə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|贫乏的，不足的，瘦弱的|Lacking in quantity or quality; deficient.  Also, thin or emaciated.|The meager rations were barely enough to sustain the refugees.  这些少得可怜的口粮几乎不足以维持难民的生活。|synonyms: scanty, sparse, paltry, insufficient, inadequate, skimpy, lean, thin, emaciated|antonyms: abundant, plentiful, ample, copious, substantial, generous, plump, stout|adjective
