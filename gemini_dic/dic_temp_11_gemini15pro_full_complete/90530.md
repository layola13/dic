huffed:/hʌft/
verb
生气地说；气呼呼地说
said something in an annoyed or offended way, typically after a disagreement.
He huffed and puffed as he climbed the stairs. 他气喘吁吁地爬上楼梯。
近义词: snorted, puffed, sulked, complained
反义词: agreed, accepted, approved, acquiesced
词性: verb

huffed:/hʌft/
adjective
气恼的；生气的
annoyed or offended.
"She gave a huffed reply." 她气呼呼地回答。
近义词: annoyed, irritated, offended, disgruntled
反义词: pleased, happy, delighted, content
词性: adjective
