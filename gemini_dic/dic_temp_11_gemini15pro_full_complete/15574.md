Baha'ism: /bəˈhɑːɪɪzəm/ /bɑːˈhɑːɪɪzəm/
noun
巴哈伊信仰
A monotheistic religion emphasizing the spiritual unity of all humankind.
Baha'ism is a relatively new religion. 巴哈伊信仰是一个相对较新的宗教。
The Baha'i faith promotes unity among all people. 巴哈伊信仰提倡所有人类的团结。
synonyms: Baha'i Faith, Baha'i
antonyms: None (religions don't typically have direct antonyms)
