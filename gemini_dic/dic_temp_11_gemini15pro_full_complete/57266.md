downplay:/ˌdaʊnˈpleɪ/| v. |淡化，轻描淡写，贬低|To minimize the significance or importance of something; to represent something as less important than it actually is.|The government tried to downplay the seriousness of the economic crisis. 政府试图淡化经济危机的严重性。|Synonyms: minimize, understate, de-emphasize, soft-pedal|Antonyms: exaggerate, overstate, emphasize, play up|verb
downplay:/ˌdaʊnˈpleɪ/| v. |淡化，轻描淡写，贬低|He downplayed his role in the project, even though he did most of the work.  他淡化了他在项目中的作用，即使他做了大部分的工作。|Synonyms: minimize, understate, de-emphasize, soft-pedal|Antonyms: exaggerate, overstate, emphasize, play up|verb
