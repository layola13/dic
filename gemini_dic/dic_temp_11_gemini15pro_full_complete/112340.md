lorgnette:/lɔːrˈnjɛt/ | n. | 长柄眼镜；观剧眼镜 | A pair of eyeglasses or opera glasses with a handle. | She peered at him through her lorgnette. 她用长柄眼镜打量着他。 |  She raised her lorgnette to get a better look at the painting. 她举起观剧眼镜，想更好地看看这幅画。 | 近义词: opera glasses, eyeglasses | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French, diminutive of lorgne (“squinting, sidelong glance”), from lorgner (“to squint, look sideways”).
