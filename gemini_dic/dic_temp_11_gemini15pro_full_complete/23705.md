brasser:/brɑːˈseɪ/ /ˈbræsər/ | v. | 抛光；擦亮；磨光；(尤指)给(黄铜或铜器)抛光；使闪亮；|To polish (especially brass or copper) by rubbing; to make something shiny.|例句：He spent hours brassing the antique candlesticks. 他花了几个小时擦拭古董烛台。

例句：The doorknobs were regularly brassed to maintain their shine. 门把手定期抛光以保持光泽。|近义词：polish, buff, shine, burnish |反义词:dull, tarnish|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From brass + -er (agent suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *brasa-.
