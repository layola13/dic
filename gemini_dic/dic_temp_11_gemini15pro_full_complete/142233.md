orogenies:/ɔːˈrɒdʒəniz/ | n. | 造山运动；造山期 |A process in which a section of the earth's crust is folded and deformed by lateral compression to form a mountain range. | The Himalayas are the result of ongoing orogenies. 喜马拉雅山脉是持续造山运动的结果。| The Alps were formed during the Alpine orogeny. 阿尔卑斯山脉是在阿尔卑斯造山运动期间形成的。 |  synonyms: mountain building, tectonic uplift | antonyms: erosion, weathering | noun
