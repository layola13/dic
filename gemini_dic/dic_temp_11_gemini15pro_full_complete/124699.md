ETYMOLOGY->

From Old English *māwan*, from Proto-Germanic *mēaną* (to mow, reap), from Proto-Indo-European *meh₁- (to mow, reap). Cognate with Dutch *maaien*, German *mähen*, Old Norse *meja*.  The noun form "mowe" is archaic and rarely used.
