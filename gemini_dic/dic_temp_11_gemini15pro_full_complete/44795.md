crunchy:/ˈkrʌn.tʃi/ /ˈkrʌn.tʃi/| adj.|脆的，嘎吱作响的|Making a sharp sound when bitten or crushed.  Firm and crisp. |The apple was crisp and crunchy.  这个苹果又脆又嘎吱响。|Synonyms: crisp, brittle, firm|Antonyms: soft, soggy, chewy|adjective

crunchy:/ˈkrʌn.tʃi/ /ˈkrʌn.tʃi/| n.|松脆的食物|Something crunchy, typically a food.|(informal) Granola is a favorite crunchy.  格兰诺拉麦片是一种受欢迎的松脆食品。|Synonyms: crisp, snack|Antonyms: mush, pap|noun
