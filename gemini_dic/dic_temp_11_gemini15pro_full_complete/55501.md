distrainable:/dɪˈstreɪnəbəl/
adjective
可扣押的
Subject to distraint; liable to be seized to satisfy a debt.
The landlord can seize distrained goods if the tenant doesn't pay the rent. 如果租户不支付租金，房东可以扣押货物。
Synonyms: seizable, confiscatable, attachable
Antonyms: unseizable, exempt
