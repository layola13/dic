obelion:/əʊˈbɛlɪən/ /oʊˈbiːliən/ | n. | 额顶孔 |The obelion is a landmark on the human skull located on the sagittal suture, at the level of the parietal foramina.  额顶孔是人类颅骨上的一个标志，位于矢状缝上，与顶孔水平。| The surgeon carefully located the obelion before starting the procedure.  外科医生在开始手术前仔细地定位了额顶孔。 | Synonym:  vertex (in a general sense), lambda (another cranial landmark) | Antonym:  N/A (anatomical term, no direct opposite) | noun
