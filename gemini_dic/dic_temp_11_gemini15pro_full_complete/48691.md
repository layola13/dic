defiance:/dɪˈfaɪəns/| n.|反抗；违抗；挑战；蔑视|open resistance; bold disobedience.|She stared at him in defiance. 她以蔑视的态度盯着他。|近义词：resistance, opposition, rebellion, disobedience|反义词：obedience, submission, compliance|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *defiance*, from *defier* "to defy, challenge," from Latin *diffidare* "to distrust, despair of, renounce," from *dis-* "apart, away" + *fidare* "to trust," from *fides* "faith, trust."
