deluge's:/ˈdeljuːdʒɪz/| n.|大洪水的，泛滥的|This refers to something belonging to or associated with a deluge, a great flood or overwhelming rush of something.|The deluge's impact on the town was devastating.  这场大洪水对小镇的影响是毁灭性的。|近义词：flood's, inundation's|反义词: trickle's, drought's|词性:名词所有格


Etymology:
From Middle English *deluge*, from Old French *deluge*, from Latin *diluvium* (“flood, inundation”), from *diluere* (“to wash away”), from *dis-* (“apart, away”) + *luere* (“to wash”).
