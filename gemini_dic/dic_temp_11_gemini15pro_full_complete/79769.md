gluteus: /ˈɡluːtiəs/ /ˈɡluːtiːəs/ | n. | 臀肌 | Any of the three large muscles in each buttock that move the thigh. |  The gluteus maximus is the largest muscle in the human body.  臀大肌是人体最大的肌肉。

The gluteus medius helps stabilize the pelvis during walking. 臀中肌有助于在行走时稳定骨盆。 |  Synonyms: buttock muscles, glutes | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a muscle group, could consider antagonist muscles like hip flexors) | Noun
