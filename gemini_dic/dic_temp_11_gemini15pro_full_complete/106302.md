一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does he do for a living? 他做什么工作？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? 她晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she accomplished this year? 她今年完成了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has she been studying at university?  她一直在大学里学什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had they discussed before the decision was made? 做决定之前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I arrived? 我来之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been working on before she changed jobs?  她换工作前一直在做什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? 你下周末要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? 他们下次会议将讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will they have built by next summer?  到明年夏天他们将建成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做什么十年了？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业的时候，她将已经学习什么多久了？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
What would she say if she knew the truth? 如果她知道真相她会说什么？

被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the summer? 你暑假的计划是什么？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. 我不知道该做什么。
She doesn't understand what you mean. 她不明白你的意思。
