epiphytical:/ˌɛpɪˈfɪtɪkəl/ | adj. | 附生的，附生植物的 | Growing on another plant non-parasitically, using it for support but not for nutrients. |  Many orchids are epiphytical, clinging to the branches of trees. 很多兰花是附生的，依附在树枝上。 | Example: The epiphytical fern thrived in the humid rainforest. 这种附生蕨类植物在潮湿的雨林中茁壮成长。| Synonyms: epiphytic, air-plant | Antonyms: terrestrial, rooted | adjective
