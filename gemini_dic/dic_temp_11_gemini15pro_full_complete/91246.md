hydrodynamics:/ˌhaɪdrəʊdaɪˈnæmɪks/ | n. | 流体力学 | The branch of physics that deals with the motion of fluids (liquids and gases) and the forces acting on them. | Example 1: The hydrodynamics of the ship's hull were carefully studied to minimize drag. 船体的流体力学经过仔细研究以最大程度地减少阻力。

Example 2:  Understanding hydrodynamics is crucial for designing efficient pipelines. 了解流体力学对于设计高效的管道至关重要。 | Near Synonyms: fluid mechanics, fluid dynamics | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
