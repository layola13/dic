off-site:/ˌɒfˈsaɪt/ /ˌɔːfˈsaɪt/| adj. adv.|场外的，非现场的；异地|Away from the main site or premises; at a different location.|例句：The company holds off-site meetings every quarter. 公司每个季度都会举行异地会议。|近义词：external, remote|反义词：on-site|词性：形容词/副词

off-site:/ˌɒfˈsaɪt/ /ˌɔːfˈsaɪt/| n.|场外，外部场所|A place away from the main site or premises.|例句：The team building activity took place at an off-site.  团队建设活动在一个外部场所举行。|近义词：external location, remote location|反义词：on-site location|词性：名词

词源:  "off" (meaning away from) + "site" (meaning location)
