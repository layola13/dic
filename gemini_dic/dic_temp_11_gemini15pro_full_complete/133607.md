nonfluentnesses:/ˌnɒnˈfluːənsɪznɪsɪz/
n.
不流利，不流畅
lack of fluency in speech, often characterized by hesitations, repetitions, or fillers.
His nonfluentnesses during the presentation made him appear nervous.  他在演讲中的不流畅让他显得紧张。
近义词: hesitancies, dysfluencies, stammering
反义词: fluency, eloquence
词性: noun
