allograph:/ˈæləɡræf/ | n. |異體字；同音异形字|A variant form of a letter or other grapheme.  A different way of writing the same letter or character. |例句：The handwritten letter "a" can have many different allographs depending on the writer's style. 手写字母“a”根据书写者的风格可以有很多不同的异体字。

例句：In different fonts, the letter "g" can appear as two distinct allographs. 在不同的字体中，字母“g”可以显示为两种不同的异体字。|近义词：variant, glyph, grapheme |反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially "standard form") |词性:noun
