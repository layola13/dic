mashing:/ˈmæʃɪŋ/ | n. | 捣碎，压碎 | The act of crushing something into a soft pulp. |  The mashing of the potatoes took longer than expected. 土豆捣碎的时间比预期的要长。 | crushing, pulverizing, grinding |  - | noun

mashing:/ˈmæʃɪŋ/ | adj. | 用于捣碎的; 捣碎似的 |  Relating to or used for mashing.  | He used a mashing tool to prepare the guacamole. 他用了一个捣碎工具来准备鳄梨酱。 | crushing, grinding | - | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mashen, from Old English *mascian, of uncertain origin.  Related to Dutch meischen (“to mix”) and German mischen (“to mix”).
