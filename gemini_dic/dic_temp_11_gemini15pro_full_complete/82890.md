gulpingly:/ˈɡʌlpɪŋli/ | adv. | 吞咽地，大口地 | In a manner characterized by gulping, typically large amounts of liquid or food.  | He drank the water gulpingly after his run. 他跑完步后大口地喝水。 |  She gulpingly ate the entire pizza. 她狼吞虎咽地吃完了整个披萨。 |  greedily, ravenously, voraciously | slowly, daintily | 词性: 副词

词源: From "gulp" (verb) + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Gulp" originates from Middle Dutch "gulpen" or Low German "gulpen," both imitative of the sound.
