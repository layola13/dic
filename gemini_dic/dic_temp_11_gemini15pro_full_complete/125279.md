multifetation: /ˌmʌltɪfɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 多胎妊娠；多胎分娩 | The development of multiple fetuses in the uterus; the birth of multiple offspring. |  The doctor confirmed the multifetation after performing an ultrasound. 医生做完超声检查后确认了多胎妊娠。 |  She had a successful multifetation and gave birth to triplets. 她成功地进行了多胎分娩，生下了三胞胎。 | synonyms: multiple birth, multiple pregnancy | antonyms: single birth | noun
