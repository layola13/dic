lashing's:/ˈlæʃɪŋz/ | n. | 捆绑；鞭打；责骂；大量 |The act of tying something securely with a rope or cord; a whipping or flogging; a harsh scolding; a large amount.|
The sailor secured the cargo with strong lashings. 水手用结实的绳索捆绑货物。

The prisoner received a brutal lashing. 囚犯受到了残酷的鞭打。

He gave the boys a lashing for breaking the window. 他因男孩们打破窗户而责骂了他们。

We had lashings of food at the party. 派对上有大量的食物。|
近义词：binding, tying, whipping, flogging, scolding, abundance, plenty |
反义词：unbinding, loosening, praise, scarcity |
词性：名词
