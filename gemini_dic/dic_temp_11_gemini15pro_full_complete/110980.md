liquidator:/ˈlɪkwɪdeɪtər/| n.|清算人，善后处理人|A person appointed to wind up the affairs of a company or other organization, especially one that is insolvent.  |The liquidators are selling the company's assets to pay off its debts.|Synonyms: receiver, administrator, trustee|Antonyms: founder, establisher|noun
