cuticle:/ˈkjuːtɪkl/ /ˈkjuːt̬ɪkəl/| n. |角质层，表皮|A protective layer covering the epidermis of a plant, or the thin skin at the base of a fingernail or toenail.|The manicurist pushed back my cuticles. 美甲师把我的角质层往后推。|Synonyms: epidermis, skin|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym)|noun

cuticle:/ˈkjuːtɪkl/ /ˈkjuːt̬ɪkəl/| n. |(昆虫的)表皮|The outer layer of an insect's exoskeleton.|The insect's cuticle protects it from predators. 昆虫的表皮保护它免受捕食者的侵害。|Synonyms: exoskeleton, shell|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym)|noun
