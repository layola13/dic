aproned:/ˈeɪprənd/| adj.|穿着围裙的|Wearing an apron; covered or protected by an apron.|The aproned chef expertly flipped the omelet.  穿着围裙的厨师熟练地翻转着煎蛋卷。|例句：The aproned baker kneaded the dough. 穿着围裙的面包师揉着面团。|近义词：covered, protected|反义词：uncovered, exposed|词性：形容词
Etymology->
From apron + -ed.  Apron comes from Old French naperon, meaning "small tablecloth," which was misinterpreted as an apron and became "a napron," eventually shortening to "apron."
