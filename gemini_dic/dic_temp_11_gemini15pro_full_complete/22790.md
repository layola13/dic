bosen:/ˈboʊzən/ | adj. | 厌倦的，无聊的 | Feeling weary and impatient because one is unoccupied or lacks interest in one's current activity. | I'm bosen with this game. 我对这个游戏感到厌倦。| 近义词: bored, tired, weary, fed up | 反义词: interested, excited, enthusiastic | 词性:形容词
词源:  Likely a variant or misspelling of "bored," past participle of the verb "bore."  The word "bore" comes from the Old English "borian," meaning "to pierce, perforate," which later developed the figurative sense of "to weary by tediousness."
