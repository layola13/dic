outlandish:/aʊtˈlændɪʃ/ | adj. |古怪的；奇异的；异国情调的 | Looking or sounding strange or unfamiliar. |  She wore an outlandish outfit to the party. 她穿着一套古怪的服装去参加聚会。| Synonyms: bizarre, eccentric, peculiar | Antonyms: conventional, ordinary, normal | adjective
