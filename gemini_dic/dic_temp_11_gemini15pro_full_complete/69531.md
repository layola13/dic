felled:/feld/
| v. |砍倒，打倒，击倒|To cut down a tree or other large plant. To knock someone to the ground.|The storm felled several large trees in the park.  暴风雨刮倒了公园里的几棵大树。|近义词：cut down, knock down, topple, prostrate|反义词: plant, erect, raise
|verb|


felled:/feld/
| adj. |被砍倒的|Cut down; knocked down.|The felled trees blocked the road. 被砍倒的树木挡住了道路。|近义词：downed, prostrated|反义词: standing, upright
|adjective|


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *fellan (attested only in past participle feallen), from Proto-Germanic *fallaną ("to cause to fall, fell"), from Proto-Indo-European *pol- ("to fall"). Cognate with Old Saxon fellian, Old Frisian fella, Dutch vellen, German fällen, Old Norse fella. Related to fall.
