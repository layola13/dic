effeteness:/ɪˈfɛktnəs/| n.|柔弱；衰弱；颓废|The state of being effete; loss of vitality, strength, or effectiveness; over-refinement and decadence.|The effeteness of the ruling class led to the downfall of the empire. 统治阶级的颓废导致了帝国的衰落。|近义词：weakness, feebleness, decadence, enervation|反义词：strength, vigor, robustness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *effētus (“exhausted, worn out”), past participle of effētiō (“exhaustion”), from ex- (“out”) + fētus (“breeding, bearing young”), from perfect passive participle of Latin feō (“I bear young”). Equivalent to e- +‎ fetus.
