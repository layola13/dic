aecidium:/iːˈsɪdiəm/| n. |锈子器|A cup-shaped fruiting body of some rust fungi (order Uredinales), containing chains of aeciospores.|The aecidium releases aeciospores which infect the alternate host.|近义词:cup, fruiting body|反义词:None|词性:noun
Etymology: From Latin *aecidium*, from Ancient Greek *αἰκίδιον* (aikídion, “small wound, sore”), diminutive of *αἰκία* (aikía, “injury, insult”).
