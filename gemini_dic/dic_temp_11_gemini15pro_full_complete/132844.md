noneffervescent:/ˌnɒnˌɛfəˈvɛsənt/ | adj. | 无泡的；沉闷的；不兴奋的 | Not effervescent; without bubbles.  Figuratively, lacking liveliness or enthusiasm. | The champagne was surprisingly noneffervescent, falling flat on the palate.  这香槟出奇地没有气泡，喝起来很平淡。| Synonyms: flat, still, dull, insipid, vapid | Antonyms: effervescent, bubbly, sparkling, lively, vivacious | word property: adjective
