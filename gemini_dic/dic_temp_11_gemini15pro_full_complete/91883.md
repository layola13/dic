hyperintensely:/ˌhaɪpərɪnˈtɛnsli/| adv.|高强度地，非常强烈地|With an unusually high intensity; extremely.|The lesion appeared hyperintensely on the T2-weighted MRI.  病灶在T2加权核磁共振成像上呈高信号。|Near synonyms:  markedly, intensely, exceedingly, exceptionally|Near antonyms: mildly, faintly, subtly|Word Type: Adverb

Etymology: From 'hyper-' (prefix meaning "over, above, excessive") + 'intense' (adjective meaning "of extreme force, degree, or strength") + '-ly' (adverb suffix). The word 'intense' ultimately derives from the Latin 'intendere' meaning "to stretch out, strain."
