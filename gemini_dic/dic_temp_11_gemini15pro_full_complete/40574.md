contusion:/kənˈtjuːʒən/ | n. | 挫伤；青肿 | An injury in which the skin is not broken but there is bruising underneath. |  He suffered a severe contusion on his leg in the accident. 他在事故中腿部严重挫伤。

| Synonym: bruise, injury | Antonym: healing, recovery | Noun


contuse:/kənˈtjuːz/ | v. |  挫伤；使青肿 | To injure a part of the body without breaking the skin. | The fall contused his shoulder. 摔倒使他肩膀挫伤。

| Synonym: bruise, injure | Antonym: heal, cure | Verb
