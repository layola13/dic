Synonym: apathy, indifference, unconcern
Antonym: curiosity, inquisitiveness, interest
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + curiosity.  Curiosity comes from the Latin curiosus "careful, inquisitive," from cura "care."
