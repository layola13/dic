ballyhooing:/ˌbæliˈhuːɪŋ/
noun
大肆宣传；吹嘘
Excessive or clamorous advertising or publicity.
The company's ballyhooing of their new product was excessive and annoying.  这家公司对他们新产品的大肆宣传过分又令人讨厌。
hype, promotion, publicity, fanfare
understatement, downplaying
n.

ballyhoo:/ˈbælihuː/
noun, verb
喧闹；大肆宣传；（对…）大肆宣传
A noisy or blatant attempt to attract customers or attention. To promote or publicize (something) noisily or blatantly.
The circus came to town with much ballyhoo. 马戏团带着大肆宣传来到镇上。
The company ballyhooed its new product launch. 这家公司大肆宣传了其新产品的发布。
hype, promotion, publicity, fanfare
understatement, downplaying
n., v.
