balsamation:/bɔːlˈseɪʃn/ /bɑːlˈseɪʃn/
n.
防腐处理；尸体 embalming
The balsamation of the body was carried out according to ancient Egyptian rituals.  根据古埃及的仪式对尸体进行了防腐处理。
The process of balsamation helps preserve the body for viewing. 防腐过程有助于保存尸体以便瞻仰。
embalming, preservation
decay, decomposition
noun
