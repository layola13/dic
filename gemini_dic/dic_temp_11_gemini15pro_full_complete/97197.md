inflaming:/ɪnˈfleɪmɪŋ/
v. |使激怒；使加剧；使发炎|Arousing or intensifying strong feelings, especially anger or excitement; causing inflammation.|Her inflammatory rhetoric only inflamed the already tense situation. 她的煽动性言论只会加剧本已紧张的局势。|aggravating, exacerbating, provoking|soothing, calming|verb

inflamed:/ɪnˈfleɪmd/
adj. |发炎的；激动的|Reddened, swollen, and painful as a result of infection or injury; aroused to strong emotion, especially anger.|The inflamed wound needed immediate medical attention. 发炎的伤口需要立即就医。|irritated, aggravated, swollen|soothed, calmed|adjective
