broodinesses:/ˈbruːdinɪsɪz/
n.
孵蛋状态；沉思状态；闷闷不乐的状态
The state of being broody, inclined to sit on eggs to hatch them, or preoccupied and withdrawn.
The hen's broodinesses lasted for several weeks.  这只母鸡的孵蛋状态持续了几个星期。
Her recent broodinesses have her friends worried. 她最近的闷闷不乐让她的朋友们很担心。
近义词: pensiveness, thoughtfulness, contemplativeness, moodiness, sullenness
反义词: cheerfulness, happiness, sociability
