finenesses:/ˈfaɪnɪsnɪsɪz/
n.
精细,细致,纯度,细微之处
plural of fineness
The finenesses of the silk threads were remarkable.  这些丝线的精细程度令人惊叹。
近义词:delicacies, subtleties, refinements, precisions
反义词:coarsenesses, crudenesses, imprecisions
词性:名词(plural)

fineness:/ˈfaɪn.nɪs/
n.
精细,细致,纯度,细微之处
The fineness of the fabric made it expensive. 这块布料的精细使得它很昂贵。
近义词:delicacy, subtlety, refinement, precision
反义词:coarseness, crudity, imprecision
词性:名词


词源: From Middle English *fynesse, from Old French finesse (“fineness, delicacy, purity”), from fin (“fine”).
