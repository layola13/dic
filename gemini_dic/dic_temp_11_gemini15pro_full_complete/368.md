abietene:/ˈeɪbiəˌtiːn/ | n. |松香烯；枞烯| A colorless, oily hydrocarbon, C19H30, obtained from Canada balsam and other conifer resins. It is a tricyclic diterpene. |  Abietene is used as a solvent in some industrial applications. 松香烯在一些工业应用中用作溶剂。|  Abietene is a component of some essential oils derived from conifers. 松香烯是某些源自针叶树的精油的成分。 | Synonyms:  dextropimaric acid, pimaric acid | Antonyms: N/A | noun
