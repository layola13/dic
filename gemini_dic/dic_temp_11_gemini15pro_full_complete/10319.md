appealed:/əˈpiːld/ | v. | 上诉；呼吁；吸引；引起兴趣 |  To make a serious or urgent request, typically to the public. To be attractive or interesting.| The defendant appealed against the sentence.  被告对判决提出上诉。| synonyms: attract, charm, fascinate, interest, please | antonyms: repel, disgust, offend | verb

appealed:/əˈpiːld/ | adj. | 有吸引力的 (主要用于被动语态，例如 be appealed to) | Appealing is attractive or interesting.  | I wasn't appealed to by his suggestion. 我对他的建议不感兴趣。 | synonyms: attractive, charming, engaging | antonyms: unappealing, unattractive, repellent | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *apeler, from Latin appellare "to accost, address, call upon, appeal to," from ad- "to" + pellere "to push, drive."
