lapidists:/ˈlæpɪdɪsts/
n.
宝石匠；宝石专家；宝石雕刻师
A person who cuts, polishes, or engraves precious stones.
例句：The lapidists carefully examined the raw diamonds. 宝石匠仔细检查了未加工的钻石。

例句： Skilled lapidists can transform rough stones into dazzling jewels.  熟练的宝石匠可以将粗糙的石头变成耀眼的珠宝。

近义词：gemologist, jeweler
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps amateur or novice in the context of skill)
词性: noun
