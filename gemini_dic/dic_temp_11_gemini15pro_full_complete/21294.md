bloodshed:/ˈblʌdʃɛd/| n. | 流血, 殺戮, 屠殺 |The act of killing people, especially in large numbers.  |The war resulted in much bloodshed. 这场战争导致了大量的流血事件。| 近义词: slaughter, carnage, massacre, butchery | 反义词: peace, truce | 词性: noun

Etymology: Old English *blōdsċed, from blōd 'blood' + sċed(d) 'shedding'.
