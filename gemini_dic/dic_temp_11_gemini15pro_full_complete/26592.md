cacaemia:/kækˈiːmiə/
n.
恶病质，败血症
The presence of faecal matter in the blood.  A serious condition often associated with severe bowel problems.
Example: The patient presented with symptoms suggesting cacaemia, prompting urgent medical investigation. 病人出现的症状表明存在败血症，促使紧急医疗调查。

Example:  Cacaemia is a rare but life-threatening complication of intestinal perforation. 败血症是一种罕见的但危及生命的肠穿孔并发症。
Synonym:  copraemia, stercoraemia
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but a state of health would be the opposite condition)
词性: noun
