gavelled:/ˈɡævəld/ | v. | 用小锤敲击（尤指法官），宣布（裁决）| The judge gavelled the court to order. 法官敲响法槌宣布开庭。| The chairman gavelled the meeting to a close. 主席敲槌宣布会议结束。 | 近义词: adjourned, concluded, finalized | 反义词: opened, commenced | 词性: 动词

词源: From "gavel" (n.), a small mallet used by a judge or auctioneer, from Old Norse *kafla "short stick, piece of wood." The verb form "gavel" meaning "to strike with a gavel" emerged later.
