nonsignifications:/ˌnɒnsɪɡnɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 无意义的事物；无关紧要的事物 |  Lack of meaning or importance; trivialities.  It refers to things that are unimportant, irrelevant, or without meaning. | The politician dismissed the criticisms as mere nonsignifications. 这位政治家把批评斥为无稽之谈。 | synonyms: trivialities, insignificances, frivolities, bagatelles, nonsense | antonyms: significances, importances, essentials, necessities | noun
