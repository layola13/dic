disinvolve:/ˌdɪsɪnˈvɒlv/ |v. | 使脱离；使不参与；不再介入 | To detach or separate oneself or someone else from involvement in something. |  He tried to disinvolve himself from the family argument. 他试图不介入家庭争吵。

The company is disinvolving itself from the project. 公司正在退出这个项目。


|近义词：detach, withdraw, extricate, disentangle |反义词：involve, engage, participate |词性：动词
