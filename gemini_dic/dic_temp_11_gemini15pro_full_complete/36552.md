collagists:/ˈkɒlɑːʒɪsts/
n.
拼贴艺术家
Artists who create collages.
The exhibition showcased the works of several talented collagists.  这个展览展示了几位才华横溢的拼贴艺术家的作品。
Collagists often use a variety of materials in their work. 拼贴艺术家经常在他们的作品中使用各种材料。
Synonym: collage makers, collage artists
Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "traditional painters" if contrasting styles)
