dissuaders:/dɪˈsweɪdərz/
n.
劝阻者；威慑物；制止物
People or things that discourage someone from doing something.
Speed bumps are effective dissuaders against speeding in residential areas.  减速带在居民区是有效的防止超速的措施。
The heavy fines are strong dissuaders against illegal parking.  高额罚款是对违章停车的有力威慑。
Synonyms: deterrents, discouragements, impediments, obstacles
Antonyms: encouragements, inducements, incentives
