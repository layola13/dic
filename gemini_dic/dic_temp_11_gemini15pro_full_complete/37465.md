commissionate:/kəˈmɪʃənət/| adj.|委任的；持有委任状的|Appointed to a position of authority by a commission or formal authorization. |The commissionate officer was in charge of the operation.  这位受委任的官员负责这次行动。|Synonym: authorized, appointed, commissioned|Antonym: unauthorized, unappointed|adjective

commissionate:/kəˈmɪʃəneɪt/| v.|委任；授予…委任状|To appoint or authorize someone to a position of authority, often in the military or government. |They commissionated him as a captain in the army. 他们委任他为陆军上尉。|Synonym: appoint, authorize, commission|Antonym: dismiss, discharge|verb
