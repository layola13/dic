elucidating:/ɪˈluːsɪdeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |  阐明的，解释清楚的 | making something clear; explaining something |  His elucidating comments helped everyone understand the complex issue. 他的解释性评论帮助每个人理解了这个复杂的问题。 | The professor's elucidating lecture clarified the confusing topic. 教授清晰的讲解阐明了这个令人困惑的话题。 | 近义词: clarifying, illuminating, explicating, illustrative | 反义词: confusing, obscuring, mystifying | 词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *elucidatus*, past participle of *elucidare* "to make clear, explain," from *e-* "out" + *lucidus* "clear, bright."
