jehovistic:/ˌdʒɛhəˈvɪstɪk/
adjective
耶和华主义的；耶和华见证人派的
Relating to or characteristic of Jehovah's Witnesses or their beliefs.
Jehovistic publications are readily available online.  耶和华见证人派的出版物很容易在网上找到。
Their jehovistic beliefs prevent them from accepting blood transfusions.  他们的耶和华见证人信仰阻止他们接受输血。
近义词:Jehovah's Witness
反义词:None
词性:adjective
词源:From "Jehovah" (a name of God) + "-istic" (forming adjectives meaning 'relating to').  来自“Jehovah”（上帝的名字）+“-istic”（构成意为“与...相关的”的形容词）。
