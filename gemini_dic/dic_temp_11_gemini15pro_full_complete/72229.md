floribunda:/ˌflɔːrɪˈbʌndə/
noun
丰花月季，多花月季
A modern shrub rose derived from hybrid tea and polyantha roses, typically having clusters of numerous, rather small flowers.
This floribunda rose produces masses of pink blooms throughout the summer. 这种丰花月季整个夏天都会开出大量的粉色花朵。

近义词: polyantha rose, cluster rose
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps single-flowered rose)
词性: noun
