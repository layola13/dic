constatives:/ˈkɒn.stə.tɪvz/ /ˈkɑːn.stə.tɪvz/| n.|述事句，陈述句|Statements that describe or assert something, contrasted with performatives which perform an action in the saying. Constatives can be true or false.|例句： "The cat sat on the mat" is a constative. “猫坐在垫子上”是一个陈述句。|近义词：statements, assertions, declarations|反义词:performatives|词性:名词
