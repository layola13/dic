megaseme:/ˈmɛɡəˌsim/ | n. |超级攻|A term used in fanfiction and online communities to describe a fictional character, typically male, who is extremely dominant, confident, and often sexually aggressive.  It's an amplified version of "seme," a term borrowed from Japanese yaoi and boys' love (BL) culture referring to the dominant partner in a romantic relationship between two men.  "Mega" implies an extreme level of these dominant traits. | He wrote a fanfiction featuring a megaseme character. 他写了一篇以超级攻角色为主角的同人小说。|  Near synonyms: alpha, dominant, seme  | Antonym: uke (submissive partner) | noun
