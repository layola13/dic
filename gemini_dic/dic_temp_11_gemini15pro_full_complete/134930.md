nonmutual:/ˌnɒnˈmjuːtʃuəl/ /ˌnɑːnˈmjuːtʃuəl/| adj.|非相互的，单方面的|Not shared, felt, or done by both sides equally; not reciprocal.|例句：They had a nonmutual understanding of the agreement. 他们对协议的理解并非相互的。|近义词：one-sided, unilateral, unreciprocated|反义词：mutual, reciprocal|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From non- + mutual.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "mutual" comes from the Latin "mutuus," meaning "lent, borrowed, reciprocal."  Thus, "nonmutual" literally means "not reciprocal."
