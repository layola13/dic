marjorams:/ˈmɑːrdʒərəmz/
n.
墨角兰（植物）; 墨角兰（调味品）
A fragrant Mediterranean herb (Origanum majorana) of the mint family, used as a culinary herb. It is also used in some perfumes and medicines.
The marjorams in the garden are thriving this year.  今年花园里的墨角兰长得很好。

近义词：oregano, sweet marjoram
反义词：(None -  no direct opposite for a herb)
词性：noun
