ledger:/ˈlɛdʒər/| n.|分类账，账簿|A book or other collection of financial accounts of a particular type. |例句：The company's ledger showed a significant loss this quarter. 这家公司的账簿显示本季度亏损严重。

例句：He meticulously recorded every transaction in the ledger. 他一丝不苟地在账簿中记录每一笔交易。|近义词：account book, record book, register|反义词: None|Noun
