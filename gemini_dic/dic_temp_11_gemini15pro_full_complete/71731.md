flawlessness:/ˈflɔːləsnəs/| n.|完美无瑕，完美无缺|The flawlessness of her performance amazed the audience. 她的完美表演令观众惊叹。|Synonyms: perfection, impeccability, immaculateness|Antonyms: imperfection, defectiveness, faultiness|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From flawless + -ness.  Flawless from flaw + -less. Flaw (Old Norse *flaga "slab of stone, layer of rock") + -less (Old English -lēas).
