desecraters:/ˈdɛsɪˌkreɪtərz/
n.
亵渎者，污损圣物者
Those who treat sacred things with disrespect; people who violate or profane something holy or highly regarded.
The desecraters of the ancient temple were condemned by all.  那些亵渎古庙的人受到了所有人的谴责。
Synonyms:  profaners, violators, defilers, vandals
Antonyms:  reverers, protectors, guardians
词性: 名词
