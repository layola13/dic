firewall:/ˈfaɪərwɔːl/ (US) /ˈfaɪəwɔːl/ (UK)| n.|防火墙|A security system that controls the flow of information between networks, such as the internet and a private network.|Example 1: The company installed a firewall to protect its data from hackers. 公司安装了防火墙来保护其数据免受黑客攻击。

Example 2:  A strong firewall is essential for online security.  强大的防火墙对于在线安全至关重要。|Synonym:  security barrier, network protection|Antonym:  open access|Noun
