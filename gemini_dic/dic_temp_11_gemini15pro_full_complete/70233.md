fickle:/ˈfɪkəl/| adj.|善变的，反复无常的|Likely to change, especially due to caprice, irresolution, or instability; casually changeable.|The weather here is notoriously fickle.  这里的天气是出了名的变化无常。|She's so fickle - she changes her mind every day about what she wants to do. 她真是善变——她每天都想做不同的事情。|近义词：capricious, erratic, mercurial, volatile, changeable|反义词：constant, steadfast, steady, loyal|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *fikel*, from Old English *ficol* (“deceitful, treacherous”), from Proto-Germanic *fikilaz (“changeable, fickle”).  Related to Old English *fāc* (“fraud, guile, treachery”).
