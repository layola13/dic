dimensionless:/daɪˈmɛnʃənləs/| adj.|无量纲的，无维的|Not having a physical dimension, such as length, mass, or time; being a pure number.|The coefficient of friction is a dimensionless quantity. 摩擦系数是一个无量纲量。|Example:  The Mach number, a ratio of speeds, is dimensionless. 马赫数，速度的比值，是无量纲的。|synonyms: non-dimensional, scalar|antonyms: dimensional|adjective
