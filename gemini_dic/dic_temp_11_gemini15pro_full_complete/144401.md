outstrip:/ˌaʊtˈstrɪp/
verb
超过，胜过
to go faster or farther than someone or something else
例句：The sales of the new product quickly outstripped those of the old one.  新产品的销量很快超过了旧产品。

例句：She soon outstripped her rivals in the race.  她在比赛中很快就超过了她的对手。

近义词：surpass, exceed, outrun, outdo
反义词：lag behind, fall behind
词性：v.


outstrip:/ˌaʊtˈstrɪp/
词源：From Middle English *owtstrippen, from out- + strippen (“to strip”), equivalent to out- + strip.
英文解析：To surpass, exceed, or leave behind in any achievement or quality; to excel.
中文翻译：超过，胜过
