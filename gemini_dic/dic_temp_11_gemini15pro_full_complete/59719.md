eclipsable:/ɪˈklɪpsəbl/| adj.|可蚀的，可遮蔽的|Capable of being eclipsed; obscuring or overshadowing.|The star is eclipsable by its larger companion. 这颗恒星会被其较大的伴星遮蔽。|Synonyms: obscure, overshadow, veil, hide|Antonyms: reveal, expose, illuminate|adjective

eclipsable:/ɪˈklɪpsəbl/| adj.|可胜过的，可超越的|Capable of being surpassed or exceeded.|His performance was eclipsable by hers. 他的表现可以被她超越。|Synonyms: surpassable, exceedable, outshinable|Antonyms: unsurpassable, matchless|adjective
