Etymology:

* **non-**:  a prefix meaning "not," from Latin *non*.
* **depravity**: from Late Latin *depravitat-*, *depravitas*, from Latin *depravare* "to corrupt, distort," from *de-* "from, away" + *pravus* "crooked, perverse."
