criticism:/ˈkrɪtɪsɪzəm/| n.|批评，批判；评论|the act of expressing disapproval of someone or something and opinions about their faults or bad qualities; a serious examination and judgment of something|The film received harsh criticism. 这部电影受到了严厉的批评。|synonyms: censure, condemnation, disapproval, denunciation, stricture, reproof, rebuke, reprimand, admonishment, castigation, lambasting, panning, slating|antonyms: praise, commendation, acclaim, applause|noun
