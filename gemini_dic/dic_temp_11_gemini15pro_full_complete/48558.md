defame:/dɪˈfeɪm/ | vt. | 诽谤，中伤 | to harm someone's reputation by saying or writing bad things about them that are not true | The article defamed the politician, falsely accusing him of corruption. 这篇文章诽谤了这位政治家，错误地指责他腐败。 | synonyms: slander, libel, malign, vilify | antonyms: praise, commend, extol | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *diffamer, from Latin diffamare (“to spread abroad, publish, divulge; to defame”), from dis- (“apart, in different directions”) + fama (“fame, report, rumor”).
