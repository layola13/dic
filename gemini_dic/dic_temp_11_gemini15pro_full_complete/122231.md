misworshipped:/ˌmɪsˈwɜːrʃɪpt/
verb
错误地崇拜；崇拜了不应崇拜之物
To worship wrongly or improperly; to offer reverence to something that is not worthy of it.
The cult misworshipped a golden idol.  该邪教错误地崇拜一个金色的偶像。
He felt he had misworshipped false ideals for too long. 他感觉自己崇拜虚假理想太久了。
Near Antonyms:  revere, venerate, worship correctly
Antonyms: despise, abhor
