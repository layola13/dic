blackcurrant:/ˌblækˈkʌrənt/ | n. | 黑醋栗，黑加仑 |A small, dark purple berry with a tart flavor, grown on a shrub of the same name. Also refers to the shrub itself. |  I love the tart flavor of blackcurrant jam. 我喜欢黑醋栗果酱的酸味。

Blackcurrants are rich in Vitamin C. 黑醋栗富含维生素C。 | 近义词: cassis | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a fruit) | 词性: noun
