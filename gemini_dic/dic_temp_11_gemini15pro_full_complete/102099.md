isosporous:/ˌaɪsoʊˈspɔːrəs/
adjective
同形孢子的
producing spores of one kind only, that are not differentiated by sex.
Ferns are isosporous plants.  蕨类植物是同形孢子植物。
Isosporous ferns produce spores that develop into bisexual gametophytes. 同型孢子的蕨类植物产生的孢子会发育为双性配子体。
homosporous
heterosporous
adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From iso- (equal) + -sporous (spore-bearing).
