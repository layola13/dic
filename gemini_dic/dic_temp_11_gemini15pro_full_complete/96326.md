indignancy:/ɪnˈdɪɡnənsi/| n. |愤慨，义愤，愤怒|A feeling of anger or annoyance provoked by what is perceived as unfair treatment.|例句：She expressed her indignance at being unfairly dismissed. 她对被不公平解雇表示愤慨。

例句：His indignance at the accusation was palpable. 他对指控的愤慨显而易见。

近义词：resentment, anger, outrage, fury|反义词:calmness, composure, acceptance|词性:noun
