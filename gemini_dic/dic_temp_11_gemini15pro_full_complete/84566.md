hammerlock:/ˈhæmərˌlɑːk/| n.|锤锁，压制|A wrestling hold in which an opponent's arm is twisted behind the back and held there.|The wrestler applied a hammerlock to his opponent.  摔跤选手对他的对手使用了锤锁。|Synonyms: armlock, hold|Antonyms: release, freedom|noun

hammerlock:/ˈhæmərˌlɑːk/| vt.|用锤锁制服|To hold (someone) in a hammerlock.|He hammerlocked the thief until the police arrived. 他用锤锁控制住了小偷，直到警察到来。|Synonyms: subdue, restrain|Antonyms: release, free|verb
