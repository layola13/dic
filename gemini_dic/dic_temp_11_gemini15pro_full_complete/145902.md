overdiffuses:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈfjuːzɪz/
verb
过度扩散，过度传播
To spread or disperse something too widely or excessively.
The marketing team overdiffused their resources across too many platforms. 营销团队在太多平台上过度分散了他们的资源。
Social media can overdiffuse information, making it difficult to find credible sources. 社交媒体可能会过度传播信息，从而难以找到可靠的来源。

concentrate, focus
