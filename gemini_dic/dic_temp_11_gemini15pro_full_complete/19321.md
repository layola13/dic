bibless:/ˈbaɪbləs/
adjective
没有圣经的；不信奉圣经的
Lacking a Bible; not adhering to the Bible.
The missionary encountered a bibless tribe in the remote jungle.  传教士在偏远的丛林中遇到了一个没有圣经的部落。
The bibless man claimed to have his own moral code. 这个不信奉圣经的人声称他有自己的道德准则。
Synonyms: scriptureless, irreligious, secular
Antonyms: scriptural, religious, pious
词性: 形容词
