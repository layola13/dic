fallout:/ˈfɔːlaʊt/ (US) /ˈfɔːlaʊt/ (UK)| n.|放射性尘埃；后果；余波|The radioactive dust that is in the air after a nuclear explosion. The unpleasant results or effects of an action or event.|例句：The political fallout of the scandal was considerable. 这桩丑闻造成的政治后果相当严重。

例句：The fallout from the Chernobyl disaster continues to affect the region. 切尔诺贝利灾难的余波继续影响着该地区。|近义词：aftermath, consequences, repercussions, side effects|反义词:cause, origin, source|词性:名词
