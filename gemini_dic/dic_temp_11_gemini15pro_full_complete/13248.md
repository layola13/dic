eat into:/iːt ˈɪntuː/ | phrasal verb | 侵蚀，消耗，削弱 | To gradually reduce the amount of something, especially money or time. | The rising cost of living is eating into our savings. 生活成本的上升正在消耗我们的积蓄。| Synonyms: erode, diminish, deplete, consume, use up | Antonyms: increase, augment, enhance | verb
