anomophyllous:/əˌnɒməˈfɪləs/
| adj. | 异形叶的 | Having leaves of different forms on the same plant or stem. | Some aquatic plants are anomophyllous, with submerged leaves differing in shape from floating or aerial leaves.  一些水生植物是异形叶的，其沉水叶的形状与漂浮叶或气生叶不同。| Example 2: The anomophyllous nature of the plant made it difficult to classify. 这种植物的异形叶特性使其难以分类。 | Near synonyms: heterophyllous, polymorphic | Antonym: homophyllous | word type:adjective
