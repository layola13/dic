humorous:/ˈhjuːmərəs/ (US) /ˈhjuːmərəs/ (UK)| adj.|幽默的，滑稽的|Causing laughter and amusement; funny.|He told a humorous story that made everyone laugh. 他讲了一个幽默的故事，把大家都逗笑了。|近义词：funny, comical, amusing, witty, droll|反义词：serious, solemn, grave|词性:形容词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin humorosus, from Latin humor ‘moisture’.  The original sense was ‘moist, damp’,  later ‘capricious, whimsical’, then ‘amusing’.
