nonslaveholding:/ˌnɒnˈsleɪvhəʊldɪŋ/ | adj. | 非蓄奴的；不持有奴隶的 | Not owning or holding slaves.  Relating to individuals or groups who opposed slavery, especially in the pre-Civil War United States. | The nonslaveholding states were increasingly at odds with the slaveholding South.  非蓄奴州与蓄奴的南方日益对立。|  The growth of nonslaveholding sentiment in the North contributed to the tensions leading up to the Civil War. 北方非蓄奴情绪的增长加剧了导致内战的紧张局势。| 近义词:abolitionist, antislavery | 反义词:slaveholding, pro-slavery | 词性:形容词
