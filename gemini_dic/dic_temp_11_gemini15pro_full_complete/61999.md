enameled:/ɪˈnæməld/ /ɪˈnæmld/ | adj.| 搪瓷的；涂珐琅的；光滑的 | coated or decorated with enamel; smooth and shiny like enamel |  The artist created an enameled box with intricate designs. 艺术家创作了一个有着复杂图案的搪瓷盒子。| The car had an enameled finish that gleamed in the sunlight. 汽车的搪瓷漆面在阳光下闪闪发光。 | 近义词：glazed, lacquered, varnished | 反义词：dull, matte | 词性: adjective

enamel:/ɪˈnæməl/ /ɪˈnæml/ | n. vt.| 珐琅；搪瓷；釉质；指甲油 | a glassy substance, usually opaque, applied by fusion to the surface of metal, pottery, etc., as an ornament or for protection; the hard, glossy surface of a tooth | She wore a beautiful enamel brooch. 她戴着一个漂亮的珐琅胸针。 |  They enameled the copper pot for durability. 他们给铜锅涂上搪瓷以提高耐用性。| 近义词：glaze, lacquer, varnish | 反义词：None | 词性: noun, verb

词源:
From Old French *esmaler (Modern French émailler), from esmail ("enamel"), from Frankish *smalt, from Proto-Germanic *smeltaną (“to smelt”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)mel- (“to melt, soften”). Related to smelt.
