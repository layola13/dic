natant:/ˈneɪtənt/| adj.|漂浮的，游泳的|Swimming or floating on the surface of a liquid; buoyant.|例句：The natant leaves of the water lily. 睡莲漂浮的叶子。|例句：Natant insects skimmed the surface of the pond. 水生昆虫掠过池塘水面。|近义词：floating, buoyant, swimming|反义词：submerged, sunken|词性:形容词
natant:|/ˈneɪtənt/| n.|漂浮物|Something that floats on the surface of a liquid.|例句：Various natants were observed on the surface of the polluted water. 在污染的水面上观察到各种漂浮物。|例句：The natants included leaves, twigs, and dead insects. 漂浮物包括树叶、树枝和死昆虫。|近义词：flotsam, debris|反义词：sediment|词性:名词
