diner:/ˈdaɪnər/ (US), /ˈdaɪnə/ (UK)| n.|用餐者；小餐馆|A person who eats dinner; a small, informal restaurant, especially one along a highway, often resembling a railroad car. |例句：The diner ordered the steak. 用餐者点了牛排。

例句：We stopped at a diner for breakfast on our road trip. 我们在公路旅行中停在一个小餐馆吃早餐。|近义词：eater, restaurant, cafe, luncheonette|反义词: None|词性:Noun


diner:/ˈdaɪnər/ (US), /ˈdaɪnə/ (UK)| n.|餐车式餐厅|A restaurant, originally resembling a railroad dining car, usually inexpensive and serving simple food.|例句：The diner was bustling with activity. 这家餐车式餐厅熙熙攘攘。

例句：They serve great burgers at that diner. 那家餐车式餐厅的汉堡很棒。|近义词：restaurant, cafe, greasy spoon|反义词: None|词性:Noun
