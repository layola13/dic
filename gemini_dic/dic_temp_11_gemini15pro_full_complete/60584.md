elaterite:/ɪˈlætəraɪt/
n.
弹性沥青，弹性地沥青，沥青橡胶
A naturally occurring, elastic, asphalt-like mineral resembling India rubber.
Elaterite is sometimes used as a sealant. 弹性沥青有时用作密封剂。
Elaterite was used in early waterproofing materials. 弹性沥青曾用于早期的防水材料。
Synonyms: mineral caoutchouc, elastic bitumen
Antonyms: None (It's a specific mineral, not a concept with an opposite.)
