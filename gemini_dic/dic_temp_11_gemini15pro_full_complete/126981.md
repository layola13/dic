narwhal:/ˈnɑːrˌwɑːl/ (US), /ˈnɑːwəl/ (UK)| n. |独角鲸|A medium-sized toothed whale (Monodon monoceros) that possesses a large "tusk" from a protruding canine tooth, mostly found in Arctic waters.|The narwhal's tusk can grow up to 10 feet long. 独角鲸的长牙可以长到10英尺长。

Narwhals are found in the Arctic Ocean. 独角鲸生活在北冰洋。|近义词：unicorn whale (非正式), toothed whale|反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用"other whales"或特定种类的鲸鱼)|词性:noun
