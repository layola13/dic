comminute: /ˈkɒmɪnjuːt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmɪnjuːt/ (US)
verb
粉碎，磨碎，细分
To reduce to minute particles or fragments; pulverize.
The machine comminuted the rocks into gravel.  这台机器把石头磨碎成了砾石。
The pharmacist comminuted the tablets into a fine powder for the infant. 药剂师将药片磨成细粉给婴儿服用。

近义词: crush, grind, pulverize, fragment
反义词: combine, consolidate, unify
词性: verb
