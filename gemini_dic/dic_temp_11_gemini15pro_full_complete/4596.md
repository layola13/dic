alcoran:/ˈælkərɑːn/ /ˈælkɔːrɑːn/
n.
可兰经
The sacred book of Islam; believed by Muslims to contain the revelations of God to Muhammad.
He read a passage from the alcoran. 他读了一段古兰经。
近义词:Quran, Koran
反义词:None
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French "alcoran," from Spanish "alcorán," from Arabic "al-Qur'ān"  meaning "the recitation,"  referring to its being recited aloud.
