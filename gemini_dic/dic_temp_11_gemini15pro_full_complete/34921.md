注意事项:

"cllr" is an abbreviation and should generally be avoided in formal writing.  Use the full word "councilor" or "councillor" instead.  While "cllr" can also refer to a lawyer, it's much less common in that context.  The word "counselor" (US) or "counsellor" (UK) is more appropriate.
