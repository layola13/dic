metred:/ˈmiːtəd/ (UK) /ˈmitərd/ (US)| adj.|有韵律的，格律的，用韵律写的；计量的|Having a metrical structure, composed in metre; measured.|The poem was carefully metered and rhymed.|近义词：rhythmical, measured, poetic|反义词:prosaic, unmetrical|词性:形容词

词源: From "metre" + "-ed."  "Metre" comes from the Old French "metre" and ultimately from the Greek "metron" meaning "measure."
