exothermic:/ˌeksoʊˈθɜːrmɪk/| adj.|放热的|A process or chemical reaction characterized by or causing the liberation or giving off of heat.|The burning of wood is an exothermic reaction.|近义词：exergonic, heat-releasing, heat-producing|反义词:endothermic, heat-absorbing|adjective
