misdescribe:/ˌmɪsdɪˈskraɪb/
verb
错误地描述；描述不当
to describe something wrongly or inaccurately
He misdescribed the suspect as being much taller than he actually was. 他错误地描述了嫌疑人，说他比实际身高要高得多。
misrepresent, mischaracterize
describe accurately, depict correctly
词性:动词
