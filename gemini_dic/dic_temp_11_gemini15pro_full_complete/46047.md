cyclically: /ˈsaɪklɪkli/ /ˈsɪklɪkli/ | adv. | 循环地，周期性地 | In a manner that occurs in cycles; repeatedly returning to the same starting point. | The seasons change cyclically. 季节循环变化。

The economy cyclically experiences periods of growth and recession. 经济周期性地经历增长和衰退时期。| 近义词: periodically, regularly, recurrently | 反义词: randomly, irregularly, sporadically | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From "cycle" + "-ic" + "-al" + "-ly". "Cycle" comes from the Greek "kyklos" meaning "circle, wheel, cycle."
