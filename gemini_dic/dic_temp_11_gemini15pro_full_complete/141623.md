optician:/ɒpˈtɪʃən/ /ɑpˈtɪʃən/| n.|眼镜商；验光师|A person who makes or sells eyeglasses.  制作或销售眼镜的人。|例句：I went to the optician to get my eyes tested. 我去验光师那里检查眼睛。

例句：The optician adjusted the frames of my glasses. 眼镜商调整了我的眼镜框。

|近义词：eye doctor, optometrist|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


optician:/ɒpˈtɪʃən/ /ɑpˈtɪʃən/| n.|配镜师；光学家|Someone who is skilled in the science that deals with sight and light. 精通处理视觉和光学的科学的人。|例句：The optician explained the different types of lenses available. 配镜师解释了可用的不同类型的镜片。

例句：My optician recommended contact lenses for my astigmatism. 我的配镜师建议我戴隐形眼镜来矫正散光。|近义词：eye doctor, optometrist|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
