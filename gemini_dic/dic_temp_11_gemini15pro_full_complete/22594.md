bootmaker:/ˈbuːtˌmeɪkə(r)/ | n. | 靴匠；制靴工人 | A person who makes boots. | The bootmaker crafted a beautiful pair of leather boots.  这位靴匠制作了一双漂亮的皮靴。 |  shoemaker, cobbler |  customer | noun
Etymology: From "boot" + "maker". "Boot" comes from Old English "bōt" and "maker" comes from Old English "maca" + "-er".
