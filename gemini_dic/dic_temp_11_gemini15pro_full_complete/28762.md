carnallite:/kɑːrˈnəˌlaɪt/ | n. | 光卤石；钾镁盐 | A mineral consisting of hydrated potassium magnesium chloride, KCl.MgCl2.6H2O, typically occurring as pink or white granular masses. It is used as a source of potassium and magnesium. | Carnallite is an important source of potassium for fertilizers. 光卤石是肥料中钾的重要来源。 | Synonym: potash salt | Antonym: N/A | noun
