landlouper:/ˈlændˌluːpər/| n.|陆地人；旱鸭子；不习惯航海的人|Someone who lives inland and is unfamiliar with the sea or ships. |例句：He's a real landlouper and gets seasick very easily. 他是个十足的旱鸭子，很容易晕船。

例句：Being a landlouper, she was amazed by the vastness of the ocean.  作为一个不习惯航海的人，她对大海的浩瀚感到惊叹。|近义词：landlubber|反义词:seafarer, sailor|noun
