adventitia:/ˌædvenˈtɪʃə/ | n. | 外膜 | The outermost connective tissue covering of an organ or vessel.  It is located external to the muscular layer. | The surgeon carefully dissected the adventitia from the artery.  外科医生小心地将外膜从动脉上剥离。| The inflammation spread to the adventitia of the esophagus. 炎症蔓延到食道的外面。 | synonyms:  outer layer, external coat, serosa (in some cases) | antonyms: intima (innermost layer) | noun
