chastening: /ˈtʃeɪsənɪŋ/ /ˈtʃæsnɪŋ/
adjective
使人悔悟的；使人收敛的；磨炼人的；惩罚性的
Having a restraining or humbling effect.  Bringing about a feeling of subdued remorse.

The experience was chastening for the young athlete, making him realize he wasn't invincible.  这次经历让这位年轻运动员得到了教训，让他意识到自己并非不可战胜。


The team suffered a chastening defeat in the final.  这支队伍在决赛中遭遇了一场惨痛的失利。

近义词: humbling, sobering, subduing, disciplining, corrective
反义词: encouraging, emboldening, exhilarating


词性: adjective
