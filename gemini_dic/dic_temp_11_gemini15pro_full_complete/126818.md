naped:/neɪpt/| adj.| 颈背有…颜色的，有…颜色披毛的|Having a nape of a specified kind or color.  Referring to the back of the neck.|The yellow-naped amazon is a popular pet bird. 黄颈亚马逊鹦鹉是一种受欢迎的宠物鸟。|近义词：naped, |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "bare-necked" in some contexts) |词性: adjective

词源: From *nape* + *-ed*.  *Nape* comes from Old English *hnaepp*, meaning "back of the neck," related to Old Norse *hnakkr*.
