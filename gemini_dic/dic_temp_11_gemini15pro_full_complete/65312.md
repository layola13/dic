eudoxian:/juːˈdɒksiən/ | adj. | 欧多克索斯的，与欧多克索斯相关的；(尤指)与欧多克索斯的天文学体系相关的 | Relating to Eudoxus of Cnidus (a Greek astronomer, mathematician, physician, scholar and student of Plato) or his theories, especially his system of astronomy. | Eudoxian astronomy posited a series of concentric spheres to explain the motions of celestial bodies. 欧多克索斯的天文学假设了一系列同心球体来解释天体的运动。|  近义词:  geocentric (having or relating to the Earth as center) | 反义词: heliocentric (having or relating to the sun as center)| 词性: adjective

eudoxian:/juːˈdɒksiən/ | n. |  欧多克索斯的追随者；信仰欧多克索斯学说的人| A follower or adherent of Eudoxus or his doctrines. | The eudoxians believed in a geocentric model of the universe. 欧多克索斯的追随者相信宇宙的地心模型。| 近义词: disciple, follower | 反义词: opponent, critic | 词性: noun
