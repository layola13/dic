gougeshell:/ˈɡaʊdʒʃɛl/
n.
凿贝壳；从贝壳中凿取
The act of gouging or carving a shell, or the piece removed by such action.  
He found a beautiful gougeshell on the beach, likely made by a native artisan. 他在海滩上发现了一个美丽的凿贝壳，可能是由当地工匠制作的。
carving, engraving, etching

noun
