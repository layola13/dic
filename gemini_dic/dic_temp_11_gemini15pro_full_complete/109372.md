lensings:/ˈlɛnzɪŋz/| n. |透镜效应; 引力透镜效应|The effect of light bending around massive objects, creating distorted or multiple images of distant objects.  This phenomenon is predicted by Einstein's theory of general relativity. |Astronomers observed multiple lensings of the distant quasar. 天文学家观察到了遥远类星体的多重透镜效应。

The gravitational lensings allowed them to study the early universe. 引力透镜效应使他们能够研究早期宇宙。|近义词:gravitational lensing, lens effect|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
