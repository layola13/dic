Implies greater wealth, status, or generosity associated with land ownership.|He became lairdlier as his estates grew.  他随着地产的增长变得更像地主了。|The lairdlier of the two brothers hosted lavish parties.  两兄弟中更富有的那个举办了奢华的派对。|Synonyms: wealthier, richer, more generous, grander|Antonyms: poorer, less generous, humbler|adjective
