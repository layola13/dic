outsnoring: /ˌaʊtˈsnɔːrɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的鼾声；鼾声盖过…
outsnoring someone means snoring louder than them.
He was outsnoring his wife. 他鼾声比妻子大。
近义词: snoring louder than, drowning out the snores of
反义词: being outsnores by
词性: verb
