impanelling:/ɪmˈpænəlɪŋ/
n.
入选陪审团; 遴选陪审团
The act of selecting and swearing in a jury.
The impanelling of the jury took several hours.  遴选陪审团花了几个小时。
synonyms:  jury selection, empaneling, empanelment
antonyms:  discharge (of a jury)


impanel:/ɪmˈpænəl/
v.
组成陪审团; 挑选陪审团
To enroll and swear in (a jury).
The judge impanelled a jury of twelve people. 法官挑选了12人组成的陪审团。
synonyms:  empanel, swear in
antonyms:  discharge
