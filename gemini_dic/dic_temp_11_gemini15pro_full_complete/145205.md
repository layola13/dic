overballasted:/ˌoʊvərˈbæləstɪd/
adjective
压载过重的，装载过重的
Having too much ballast.  A ship that is overballasted sits too low in the water and is less stable.
The ship was overballasted and struggled in the heavy seas. 这艘船压载过重，在汹涌的海浪中艰难航行。
The small yacht was overballasted and nearly capsized. 这艘小游艇压载过重，差点倾覆。

近义词: overloaded, overladen
反义词: underballasted

词性: adjective
