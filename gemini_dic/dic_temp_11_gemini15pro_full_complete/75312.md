frontman: /ˈfrʌntmæn/ (US) /ˈfrʌntmæn/ (UK)| n. |主唱，领唱；(乐队、组织、运动等的)发言人，代表|The lead singer and often the public face of a band or other group.  He acts as the spokesperson and often interacts with the audience.|例句：The frontman of the band is known for his energetic stage presence.  乐队的主唱以其充满活力的舞台表现而闻名。

例句：He's the frontman for a local environmental group. 他是当地一个环保组织的发言人。|近义词：lead singer, vocalist, spokesperson, representative|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
