concessive:/kənˈsesɪv/| adj.|让步的；妥协的|Making a concession to avoid an argument, acknowledging a point against your argument while maintaining your main point.| Although I disagree with his methods, I concede that his results are impressive. 虽然我不同意他的方法，但我承认他的结果令人印象深刻。|近义词：yielding, accommodating, granting|反义词：intransigent, unyielding|词性:形容词
concessive:/kənˈsesɪv/| n.|让步；让步状语从句|A clause or phrase that expresses an idea that contrasts with the main idea of the sentence but does not invalidate it.| Although she was tired, she continued working. 虽然她很累了，但她继续工作。|近义词：concession|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider words related to insistence like "demand")|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *concessivus, from Latin concessus, past participle of concedere "to yield, concede," from com- "together" (see com-) + cedere "to go, yield" (see cede).
