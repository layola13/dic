landscape: /ˈlændskeɪp/ | n. v. |风景，景色；景观；地形；山水画；（计算机屏幕的）横向显示；美化（环境）；从事景观美化工作 |A section of countryside or land, often considered in terms of its aesthetic appeal.  To improve the appearance of an area of land by adding trees, plants, etc. |The landscape was dotted with small farms.  景色中点缀着一个个小农场。| 近义词：scenery, view, vista, panorama | 反义词：seascape | 词性: 名词, 动词

landscaped: /ˈlændskeɪpt/ | adj. | 园林绿化的; 美化了的 | Designed and planted with trees, shrubs, etc., to enhance its appearance. | The hotel has beautifully landscaped grounds.  这家酒店拥有美丽的园林绿化场地。| 近义词：gardened, cultivated | 反义词：barren, desolate | 词性: 形容词

landscaping: /ˈlændskeɪpɪŋ/ | n. | 园林绿化；景观美化 | The process or art of designing and planting trees, shrubs, etc., to enhance the appearance of an area of land. | The landscaping of the park was done by a professional company.  公园的景观美化是由一家专业公司完成的。| 近义词：gardening, horticulture | 反义词： - | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Dutch *landschap, from land (land) + -schap (ship, -scape). Cognate with German Landschaft. Originally referred to a painting depicting a view of natural inland scenery.
