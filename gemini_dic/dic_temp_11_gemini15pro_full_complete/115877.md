marlins:/ˈmɑːrlɪnz/ /ˈmɑːrlɪns/
|n.|马林鱼；旗鱼（marlin的复数）|A type of large, saltwater game fish known for their long, pointed bills and powerful bodies. They are found in tropical and subtropical waters around the world. |The Marlins are playing the Dodgers tonight.|近义词：sailfish, spearfish|反义词: (no direct antonym, perhaps small fish like minnows)|noun
