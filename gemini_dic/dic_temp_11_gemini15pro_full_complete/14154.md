authorizers:/ˈɔːθəˌraɪzərz/
|n.|授权者，批准者|People or entities who give permission or approval for something to happen.  Those who grant authorization.|The authorizers of the project finally signed the contract. 项目的授权者最终签署了合同。|Synonyms: approvers, sanctioners, ratifiers|Antonyms: objectors, dissenters|noun
