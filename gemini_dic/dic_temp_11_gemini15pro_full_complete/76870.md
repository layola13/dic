ganglion: /ˈɡæŋɡlɪən/ (US) /ˈɡæŋɡlɪən/ (UK)| n. |神经节；腱鞘囊肿|A mass of nerve tissue containing numerous cell bodies typically linked by synapses, and often forming a swelling on a nerve fiber.|例句：The doctor diagnosed the lump on my wrist as a ganglion.  医生诊断我手腕上的肿块是腱鞘囊肿。

例句：Ganglions are clusters of nerve cell bodies found in the peripheral nervous system. 神经节是存在于周围神经系统中的神经细胞体簇。

近义词：node, cluster|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ganglia: /ˈɡæŋɡliə/ (US) /ˈɡæŋɡlɪə/ (UK)| n. |神经节（ganglion 的复数形式）|The plural form of ganglion, referring to multiple masses of nerve tissue.|例句：The surgeon carefully removed the ganglia that were pressing on the spinal cord.  外科医生小心地移除了压迫脊髓的神经节。

例句：The ganglia in the autonomic nervous system control involuntary functions like heart rate and digestion. 自主神经系统中的神经节控制着心率和消化等非自主功能。
