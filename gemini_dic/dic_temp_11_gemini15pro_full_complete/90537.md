huffing:/ˈhʌfɪŋ/ | n. | 喷气, (吸入毒品后)兴奋, 吹嘘 | the act of breathing out loudly, especially when annoyed or out of breath; inhaling fumes from substances such as glue or paint thinner for a hallucinatory effect; bragging |  例句：The huffing and puffing of the old steam train could be heard for miles.  老式蒸汽火车呼哧呼哧的声音几英里外都能听到。

例句：The police caught teenagers huffing paint thinner in the park. 警察在公园里抓住了吸食油漆稀释剂的青少年。| 近义词：puffing, panting, bragging, boasting | 反义词: inhaling (in a normal way) | 词性: noun


huffing:/ˈhʌfɪŋ/ | adj. | 气喘吁吁的, 吹嘘的 | breathing heavily; bragging |  例句：He arrived at the top of the hill, red-faced and huffing. 他到达山顶时，脸涨得通红，气喘吁吁。

例句：She was huffing about her latest achievement. 她正在吹嘘她最近的成就。| 近义词：puffing, panting, bragging, boasting | 反义词: quiet, modest | 词性: adjective


词源: From the verb "huff," which imitates the sound of heavy breathing. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *hupjaną.
