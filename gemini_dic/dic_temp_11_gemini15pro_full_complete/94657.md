impressive:/ɪmˈprɛsɪv/| adj.|令人印象深刻的；感人的|Making a strong or vivid impression; producing a feeling of admiration or respect.|The sheer size of the building was impressive.  这座建筑的规模令人印象深刻。| synonyms: striking, remarkable, extraordinary, memorable, impactful| antonyms: unimpressive, unremarkable, ordinary, forgettable, mediocre|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *impressivus, from Latin impress-, past participle stem of imprimere "to press into, imprint," from in- "in" + premere "to press."
