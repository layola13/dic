nonpatently:/ˌnɒnˈpeɪtəntlɪ/
adverb
不明显地，非专利地
not obviously; not readily apparent
The flaws in the system were nonpatently obvious, requiring careful investigation to uncover.  系统中的缺陷并不明显，需要仔细调查才能发现。
The invention was nonpatently disclosed in a prior art publication, making it ineligible for patent protection. 这项发明在先前的出版物中未被明显地公开，因此不符合专利保护的资格。
Synonyms: subtly, inconspicuously, indirectly
Antonyms: obviously, patently, clearly
词性: 副词
