obligational:/ˌɑːblɪˈɡeɪʃənl/
adjective
义务的；强制性的
Relating to or arising from an obligation; binding.
例句：He felt an obligational duty to help his family. 他感到有义务帮助他的家人。

例句：The company has obligational responsibilities to its shareholders. 公司对股东负有强制性的责任。

近义词：mandatory, compulsory, binding, required
反义词：optional, voluntary
词性: adjective
