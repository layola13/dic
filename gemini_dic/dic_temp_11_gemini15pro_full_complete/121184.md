misdivide:/ˌmɪsdɪˈvaɪd/
verb
错误地划分；不正确地分割
To divide incorrectly, especially in a mathematical calculation.
The student misdivided the numbers in the equation and got the wrong answer.  这个学生在等式中错误地划分了数字，得到了错误的答案。
The clerk misdivided the bill amongst the diners.  服务员错误地划分了用餐者的账单。
近义词: miscalculate, blunder
反义词: divide correctly, calculate accurately
词性: verb
