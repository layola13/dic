ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *gromette, diminutive of gromet (“curb bit of a horse”), from Old Norse gríma (“mask, visor”), from Proto-Germanic *grimō, from Proto-Indo-European *ghrei- (“to rub, grind”).  The original sense was of a ring attached to a bridle.
