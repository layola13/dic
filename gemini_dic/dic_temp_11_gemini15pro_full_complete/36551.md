collagist:/ˈkɒləʒɪst/ (UK) /kəˈlɑːʒɪst/ (US)| n. |拼贴艺术家|An artist who creates collages, artworks made by assembling different materials such as photographs, fabric, and paper. |Hannah Höch was a prominent German collagist in the Dada movement. 汉娜·霍克是达达运动中一位杰出的德国拼贴艺术家。|
Collage artists often incorporate found objects into their work. 拼贴艺术家经常将现成物品融入到他们的作品中。|
Synonyms: collage maker, assemblage artist|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider minimalist artist) | noun
