leisure:/ˈliːʒər/ /ˈleʒər/| n.|休闲，空闲，闲暇|Free time not taken up with work or duties.  Time free from the demands of work or duty, when one can rest, relax, and pursue activities of personal interest.| I spend my leisure reading and gardening. 我把我的闲暇时间花在阅读和园艺上。| synonyms: free time, spare time, downtime, relaxation, recreation | antonyms: work, labor, toil, business| noun
