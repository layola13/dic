crumpled:/ˈkrʌmpəld/| adj.| 皱的，弄皱的| crushed, wrinkled, compressed and no longer smooth. |The paper was crumpled and torn. 这张纸又皱又破。|近义词：wrinkled, creased, rumpled|反义词：smooth, ironed|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the verb "crumple," which originates from the Middle English "crumplyn" or "cromplen," frequentative of "crump," meaning "to curl up," probably of imitative origin.
