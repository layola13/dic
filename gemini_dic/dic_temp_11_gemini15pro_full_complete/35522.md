coagulability:/kəʊˌæɡjʊləˈbɪləti/ | n. | 凝固性；凝血性 | The ability of a liquid, especially blood, to change to a solid or semi-solid state. | The doctor tested the patient's blood coagulability before surgery. 医生在手术前测试了病人的血液凝固性。|  Synonym: clotting ability, congealment | Antonym: fluidity, incoagulability | noun
