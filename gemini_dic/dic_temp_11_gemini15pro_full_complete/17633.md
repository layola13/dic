becalmed:/bɪˈkɑːmd/
adjective
静止的；停滞的；(尤指帆船)因无风而无法前进的
Rendered motionless or still, especially of a sailing ship, by lack of wind.
The ship was becalmed in the doldrums. 这艘船因无风而在赤道无风带停滞不前。
近义词:stranded, stuck, motionless, still
反义词:moving, sailing, progressing
词性:adjective

becalmed:/bɪˈkɑːmd/
verb
使静止；使停滞；(尤指帆船)因无风而无法前进
To render motionless or still, especially of a sailing ship, by lack of wind.
The sudden absence of wind becalmed the sailboat. 突然没有了风，帆船无法航行。
近义词:immobilize, strand, trap
反义词:propel, move
词性:verb
