nestlings:/ˈnestlɪŋz/
n.
雏鸟；刚孵出的幼鸟
Young birds that have recently hatched and are still in the nest.
The nestlings chirped loudly, begging for food from their mother. 雏鸟大声啁啾，向它们的母亲乞求食物。
The storm destroyed the nest and killed the nestlings.  暴风雨摧毁了鸟巢，杀死了雏鸟。
近义词：chicks, hatchlings
反义词: adult birds, fledglings
词性: 名词
