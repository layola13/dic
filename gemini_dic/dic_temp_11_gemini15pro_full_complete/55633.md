ditching:/ˈdɪtʃɪŋ/ (US), /ˈdɪtʃɪŋ/ (UK)
|n. |抛弃，丢弃；(飞机)紧急迫降|The act of abandoning something or someone.  Also, the emergency landing of an aircraft on water.|
|例句：The ditching of the plane in the Hudson River was a miracle. 飞机在哈德逊河上的紧急迫降是一个奇迹。|
|近义词：abandoning, dumping, deserting, discarding, jettisoning|
|反义词：keeping, retaining, saving, rescuing|
|词性：名词|

ditching:/ˈdɪtʃɪŋ/ (US), /ˈdɪtʃɪŋ/ (UK)
