breakfastless: /ˈbrɛkfəstlɪs/
adjective
没有吃早餐的; 未吃早餐的
Without having eaten breakfast.
He started his breakfastless day with a cup of black coffee. 他没有吃早餐，以一杯黑咖啡开始了新的一天。
She often went to school breakfastless due to oversleeping.  由于睡过头，她经常不吃早餐就去上学。
近义词:  hungry, unfed
反义词:  fed, full
词性: adjective

词源: breakfast + -less
"breakfast" (早餐) + "-less" (无，没有)
