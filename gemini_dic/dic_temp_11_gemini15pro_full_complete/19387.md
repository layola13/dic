bicameralism:/baɪˈkæmərəlɪzəm/
|n.|两院制|The principle or system of having two legislative or parliamentary chambers.|The United States operates under bicameralism, with a Senate and a House of Representatives. 美国实行两院制，由参议院和众议院组成。|The UK Parliament is also bicameral, consisting of the House of Commons and the House of Lords. 英国议会也是两院制，由下议院和上议院组成。|bicameral system, two-chamber system|unicameralism|noun
