outstayed:/ˌaʊtˈsteɪd/
verb
停留过久，逗留过长
To stay longer than the permitted or expected time.
He outstayed his visa by three months. 他签证过期三个月了。
近义词: overstay, linger, remain
反义词: leave, depart
词性: verb

outstayed:/ˌaʊtˈsteɪd/
verb
超过…的期限
To stay beyond the time limit of something.
She outstayed her welcome at the party. 她在聚会上待得太久了，让人感到不愉快。
近义词: exceed, surpass
反义词: observe, respect
词性: verb
