hyperemotionalities:/ˌhaɪpərɪˌmoʊʃənˈælɪtiz/ | n. |过度情绪化；过度情感化 |  A tendency to experience and express emotions with excessive intensity and frequency.  This can involve a wide range of emotions, including happiness, sadness, anger, and fear. | She struggled with hyperemotionalities after the trauma, swinging from intense joy to deep despair. 她遭受创伤后一直在与过度情绪化作斗争，从极度喜悦到深深的绝望。 |  Hypersensitivity, oversensitivity, emotional lability, mood swings | Emotional stability, composure,  emotional regulation | noun
