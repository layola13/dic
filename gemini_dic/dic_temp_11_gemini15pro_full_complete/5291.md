allotheism:/ˈæləθiːɪzəm/
n.
异神崇拜；崇拜异教神
The belief in or worship of other gods.
He was accused of allotheism for worshipping a deity not recognized by the state religion.  他因崇拜国家宗教不承认的神而被指控为异神崇拜。
Allotheism is often contrasted with monotheism. 异神崇拜常与一神论作对比。
近义词：polytheism, paganism
反义词：monotheism
词性：noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἄλλος (állos, “other”) + θεός (theós, “god”) + -ism.
