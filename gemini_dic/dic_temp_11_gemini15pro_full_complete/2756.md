adulterously:/əˈdʌltərəsli/
adverb
通奸地，不忠地
In a manner that involves adultery; unfaithfully to one's spouse.
He behaved adulterously, betraying his wife's trust.  他通奸地行事，背叛了他妻子的信任。
She admitted to having acted adulterously during her marriage. 她承认在婚姻期间有不忠的行为。
Synonym: unfaithfully, disloyally
Antonym: faithfully, loyally
词性: adverb
