discordant:/dɪˈskɔːrdənt/ | adj. |不和谐的，不一致的 | disagreeing or incongruous | The discordant notes of the untuned piano grated on my ears.  走调的钢琴发出不和谐的音符，刺耳难听。|近义词：inharmonious, conflicting, clashing, jarring |反义词：harmonious, agreeable, concordant | adjective
