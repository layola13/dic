gratefullest:/ˈɡreɪtfəlɪst/
adjective
最感激的
Superlative adjective of grateful; most grateful.  This form is considered nonstandard or informal by many.  It's better to use "most grateful".
I am the gratefullest person alive for your help. (Informal) 我对你提供的帮助感激不尽。
She was the gratefullest of all the recipients. (Informal) 在所有受赠者中，她是最感激的。
近义词:most grateful, thankfullest (also nonstandard)
反义词:ungrateful, unappreciative
词性:adjective
