irreplevisable:/ˌɪrɪˈplɛvɪsəbl/
adjective
不能归还的；无法弥补的
Not able to be replevied (returned to the owner after being unlawfully taken), especially in law; not able to be recovered or replaced.
The stolen artwork was deemed irreplevisable, leaving the museum with a permanent void in its collection.  这件被盗的艺术品被认为是无法归还的，给博物馆的收藏留下了永久的空白。
He made an irreplevisable contribution to the field of medicine. 他对医学领域做出了不可磨灭的贡献。
synonym:irrecoverable, irreplaceable, lost
antonym:recoverable, replaceable
词性:adjective
