muflon:/ˈmʌflən/ (US), /ˈmʌflɒn/ (UK)| n.|欧洲盘羊，摩弗伦羊|A wild sheep (Ovis musimon or O. orientalis) of Corsica and Sardinia, the ancestor of the domestic sheep. It has short, reddish-brown hair and large, curved horns in the male.|The muflon is a beautiful and elusive creature.|近义词:wild sheep, mouflon|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially "domestic sheep")|词性:noun
