overmilks:/ˌoʊvərˈmɪlks/| v. |过度挤奶；榨取；|To extract or demand too much from (someone or something), like milking a cow excessively. | He overmilked the project, trying to squeeze every last penny out of the budget. 他过度榨取这个项目，试图从预算中榨取每一分钱。

The company overmilks its employees, expecting them to work long hours for little pay.  这家公司过度压榨员工，期望他们长时间工作却只给很少的工资。|近义词：exploit, overuse, overwork|反义词: underuse, neglect|词性:动词
