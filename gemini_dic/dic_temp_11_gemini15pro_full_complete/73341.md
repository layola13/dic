forefend:/fɔːrˈfɛnd/ | v. | 预防，阻止，避免 | To avert, prevent, or ward off (something evil or unpleasant). |  May God forefend such a tragedy. 愿上帝阻止这样的悲剧发生。| 近义词: prevent, avert, obviate, preclude | 反义词: cause, incite, allow, permit | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From Middle English *forefenden*, from Old English *forfēon* (“to despise, abhor, renounce, refuse, prevent, prohibit, protect against”), from *for-* (“for, against”) + *fēon* (“to hate, despise, shun”). Equivalent to *for-* +‎ *fend*.
