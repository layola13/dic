assaulting:/əˈsɔːltɪŋ/
|v.|攻击，袭击|Attacking someone physically or verbally. |The man was arrested for assaulting a police officer.  该男子因袭击一名警察而被捕。|
|近义词：attacking, battering, abusing|反义词：defending, protecting|Verb|


assaulting:/əˈsɔːltɪŋ/

|近义词：offensive, aggressive|反义词：defensive, protective|Adjective|
