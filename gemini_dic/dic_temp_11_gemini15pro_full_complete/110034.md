licencees:/ˌlaɪsənˈsiːz/
n.
被许可人，获授权者
Individuals or entities granted a license to do something.
The licensees are responsible for complying with the terms of the agreement.  被许可人有责任遵守协议条款。
All licensees are required to attend the annual training. 所有被许可人必须参加年度培训。
synonyms: permit holders, franchisees, grantees
antonyms: licensors, grantors
