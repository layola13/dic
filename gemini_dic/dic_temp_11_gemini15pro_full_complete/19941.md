biodegradation:/ˌbaɪoʊˌdeɡrəˈdeɪʃən/ | n. | 生物降解 | The process by which organic substances are broken down into simpler substances by the action of living organisms, especially bacteria. | Example 1: The biodegradation of plastic is a slow process. 塑料的生物降解是一个缓慢的过程。

Example 2:  Scientists are researching new methods to enhance biodegradation of pollutants. 科学家们正在研究增强污染物生物降解的新方法。 | 近义词: decomposition, biotransformation | 反义词: biosynthesis | 词性: 名词
