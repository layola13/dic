nonagglomerative:/ˌnɒnəˈɡlɒmərətɪv/
adjective
非凝聚性的，非结块的
Not agglomerative; not clustering or aggregating into a mass.
The mixture was nonagglomerative, remaining as a fine powder.  这种混合物是非凝聚性的，仍然是细粉末。
The scientist developed a nonagglomerative coating for the seeds. 科学家为种子开发了一种非凝聚性涂层。

synonym: non-clustering, non-aggregating, dispersed, separate
antonym: agglomerative, clustering, aggregating, clumping, caking
词性: 形容词
