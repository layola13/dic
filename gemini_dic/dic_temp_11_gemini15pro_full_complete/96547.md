indopacific:/ˌɪndoʊpəˈsɪfɪk/| adj. n.| 印度洋-太平洋的，印度洋-太平洋地区|Relating to or denoting the region comprising the Indian and Pacific Oceans and the landmasses that border them.  The Indopacific is a biogeographic region of Earth's oceans comprising the tropical waters of the Indian Ocean, the western and central Pacific Ocean, and the seas connecting the two in the general area of Indonesia.|例句：The Indopacific region is known for its biodiversity. 印度洋-太平洋地区以其生物多样性而闻名。

例句：The Indopacific strategy focuses on maritime security.  印度洋-太平洋战略侧重于海上安全。|近义词：Asia-Pacific, Indo-West Pacific|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:adjective, noun
