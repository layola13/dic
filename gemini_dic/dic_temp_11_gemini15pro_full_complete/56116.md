doctrinalism: /ˈdɒktrɪnəlɪzəm/ /ˈdɑːktrɪnəlɪzəm/
n.
教条主义; 教义至上; 教条化
Adherence to doctrines or the tendency to emphasize doctrine over experience or practice.  The belief that doctrines are the most important aspect of a religion or ideology.
例句：The party's slide into doctrinalism alienated many of its supporters. 该党滑向教条主义疏远了许多支持者。

例句：His doctrinalism prevented him from seeing the practical implications of his ideas. 他的教条主义使他看不到自己想法的实际意义。

近义词：dogmatism, rigidity, orthodoxy
反义词：pragmatism, flexibility, heterodoxy
词性: noun
