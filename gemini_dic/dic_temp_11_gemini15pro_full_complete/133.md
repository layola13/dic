abandonment:/əˈbændənmənt/| n. |放弃，抛弃，遗弃|The act of leaving someone or something, or of ending or stopping something, usually forever.|The child was traumatized by the abandonment of his parents.  这个孩子被他父母的抛弃深深地伤害了。|近义词：desertion, relinquishment, forsaking|反义词: adoption, acceptance, retention|noun
