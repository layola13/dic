antiquarian:/ˌæn.tɪˈkweə.ri.ən/
antiquarian: n.| 古董商；收藏家；古物研究者|A person who studies or collects antiques or antiquities.| He was a renowned antiquarian with a vast collection of Roman artifacts. 他是一位著名的古董收藏家，收藏了大量的罗马文物。|近义词: collector, connoisseur, archaeologist |反义词: modernist|词性:名词

antiquarian:/ˌæn.tɪˈkweə.ri.ən/
antiquarian: adj.| 古董的；古文物研究的；好古的|Relating to or dealing in antiques or antiquities.| She had an antiquarian interest in medieval manuscripts. 她对中世纪手稿有浓厚的古文物研究兴趣。|近义词: ancient, antique, archaic |反义词: modern, contemporary|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *antiquarius*, from Latin antiquus (“old, ancient”).
