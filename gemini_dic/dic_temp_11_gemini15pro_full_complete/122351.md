mixolydian:/ˌmɪksəˈlɪdiən/
adjective|混合利底亚式的|Relating to or denoting a musical mode or scale in which the dominant is a major chord, giving a characteristically bright or restless quality.|The guitarist played a mixolydian riff that sounded both familiar and exotic.|近义词:dominant seventh, major|反义词:minor|词性:adjective
noun|混合利底亚调式|A musical mode or scale in which the dominant is a major chord.|The song is in G mixolydian.|近义词:dominant seventh scale, major mode|反义词:minor mode|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *mixolydios, from mixo- (mixed) + Lydian (a Greek musical mode). The Mixolydian mode combines elements of both major and minor scales.
