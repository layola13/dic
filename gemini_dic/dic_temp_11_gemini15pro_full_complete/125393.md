multinationals:/ˌmʌltiˈnæʃənəlz/ | n. | 跨国公司 | Companies that operate in multiple countries. |  Many multinationals have their headquarters in developed countries. 许多跨国公司将其总部设在发达国家。

| Example: Multinationals play a significant role in the global economy. 跨国公司在全球经济中扮演着重要角色。 | Synonym: global corporations, transnational corporations | Antonym: domestic companies, local businesses | Noun


multinational:/ˌmʌltiˈnæʃənəl/ | adj. | 跨国的 |  Involving or operating in several nations. | Many multinational corporations have offices around the world. 许多跨国公司在世界各地设有办事处。

| Example: The company has a multinational workforce. 这家公司拥有跨国员工队伍。 | Synonym: international, global, transnational | Antonym: national, domestic, local | Adjective
