flowerier:/ˈflaʊəriər/ (US), /ˈflaʊəriə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更加华丽的，更加词藻丰富的|More flowery means more elaborate or ornate in language, often excessively so.  It implies a use of decorative or embellished language, often to the point of being overly elaborate or artificial.|Her writing style became flowerier as she grew older. 她的写作风格随着年龄的增长变得更加华丽。|The politician's speech was too flowerier for the average voter to understand.  这位政治家的演讲对于普通选民来说过于华丽，难以理解。|近义词：ornate, elaborate, embellished, flamboyant|反义词：plain, simple, unadorned, austere|词性:形容词
