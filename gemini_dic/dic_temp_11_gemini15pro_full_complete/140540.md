oleosity:/ˌoʊliˈɑːsəti/
n.
油性；油腻；油脂
the property of being oily or greasy
The oleosity of the skin can contribute to acne.  皮肤的油性会导致痤疮。
Synonyms: oiliness, greasiness, fattiness
Antonyms: dryness
