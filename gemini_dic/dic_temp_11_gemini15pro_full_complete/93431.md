illuminate:/ɪˈluːmɪneɪt/ | vt.| 照亮，阐明，启发 | To light up or make clear; to provide understanding or insight. | The streetlights illuminated the dark alley. 路灯照亮了黑暗的小巷。 |  The professor's lecture illuminated the complex topic. 教授的讲座阐明了这个复杂的话题。 | synonyms: brighten, clarify, enlighten | antonyms: darken, obscure, confuse | verb

illuminate:/ɪˈluːmɪneɪtɪd/ | adj.| 被照亮的，受启发的 | Lit up; intellectually or spiritually enlightened. | The illuminated manuscript was a beautiful work of art.  这本被照亮的稿件是一件美丽的艺术品。 | His face was illuminated with joy. 他的脸上洋溢着喜悦。 | synonyms: bright, radiant, enlightened | antonyms: dark, dim, unenlightened | adjective
