anonaceous:/ˌænəˈnoʊʃəs/| adj.|番荔枝科的，属于番荔枝科的|Relating to or belonging to the Annonaceae family (custard-apple family) of plants.|The cherimoya is an anomaceous fruit. 刺果番荔枝是一种番荔枝科水果。|Synonyms: custard apple family, Annonaceae|Antonyms: N/A|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin *Annonaceus, derived from Annona (the type genus of the family) + -aceus (suffix forming adjectives of resemblance).  Annona itself comes from Taíno (a Caribbean language) anón, meaning "custard apple."
