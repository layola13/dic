have-a-word:/hæv ə wɜːrd/ (British) /hæv ə wɝːd/ (American)
|phrasal verb|
|与…谈谈；训斥；警告|
|To have a brief, usually serious or critical, conversation with someone.|
|I need to have a word with you about your performance. 我需要和你谈谈你的表现。|

|I had a word with him about his behavior. 我警告了他关于他的行为。|
|speak to, talk to, chat with (informal), discuss with, reprimand, scold, warn, caution|
|avoid, ignore|
|phrasal verb|
