jetted:/ˈdʒɛtɪd/
verb
喷射而出，快速移动
to travel or move by jet
例句：He jetted off to Paris for the weekend. 他周末乘飞机去了巴黎。

例句：The water jetted out of the pipe. 水从管子里喷射而出。
travelled quickly, dashed, sped, zipped
crawled, lingered, stayed
v.
