backchannel:/ˈbækˌtʃænl/ | n. | 幕后沟通；私下交流；辅助频道 | A secondary or covert route of communication, often used for discreet signaling or feedback. |  The speaker received encouraging nods and smiles from the audience through backchannel communication. 演讲者通过幕后沟通收到了观众鼓励的点头和微笑。


| The diplomats used a backchannel to communicate secretly with their counterparts. 外交官们利用辅助频道与对方秘密沟通。 | synonyms: side channel, private communication, behind-the-scenes communication | antonyms: open communication, public discourse | noun


backchannel:/ˈbækˌtʃænl/ | adj. | 幕后的；私下的 | Relating to or using a secondary or covert route of communication. | The president's aides used a backchannel method to reach out to the opposition leader. 总统的助手们用幕后方法联系了反对派领导人。



|  The backchannel negotiations helped pave the way for a peace agreement. 幕后谈判为和平协议铺平了道路。 | synonyms: behind-the-scenes, private, covert | antonyms: open, public, overt | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
"back channel" originally referred to a secondary audio channel in recording studios used for communication between engineers and musicians. This meaning later expanded to encompass any secondary or covert communication channel.
