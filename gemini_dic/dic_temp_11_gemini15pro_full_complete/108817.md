leave-out:/ˈliːv aʊt/| n.|遗漏，省略; 被遗漏的人(或物)|An act of omitting or neglecting to include something or someone. | The accidental leave-out of a key ingredient ruined the cake. 意外遗漏了一个关键成分，毁了蛋糕。|近义词：omission, exclusion, oversight|反义词:inclusion, addition|noun
leave-out:/ˈliːv aʊt/| adj.|被遗漏的，省略的|Omitted or excluded.|He felt left-out when his friends went to the party without him.  当他的朋友们不带他去参加聚会时，他感到被冷落了。|近义词：omitted, excluded|反义词:included|adjective
