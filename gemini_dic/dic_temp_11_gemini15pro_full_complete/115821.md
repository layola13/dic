market: /ˈmɑːrkɪt/ (US) /ˈmɑːkɪt/ (UK) | n. v. | 市场，集市；行情；销路；推销，营销 | A place where people buy and sell goods; the demand for a particular product or service. |  We went to the farmer's market for fresh produce. 我们去农贸市场买新鲜农产品。| synonyms: marketplace, bazaar, emporium, exchange | antonyms:  monopoly | noun

market: /ˈmɑːrkɪt/ (US) /ˈmɑːkɪt/ (UK)| v. | 推销；营销 | To promote or sell a product or service. | They marketed the new phone as being incredibly user-friendly.  他们把这款新手机宣传成非常用户友好。| synonyms: promote, advertise, sell, merchandise | antonyms: withhold | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *market, from Latin mercatus "trade, market," past participle of mercari "to trade, traffic," from merx "merchandise."
