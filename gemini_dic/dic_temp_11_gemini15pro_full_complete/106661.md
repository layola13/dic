kushites:/ˈkuːʃaɪts/| n.|库施人，古实人|People of ancient Kush, a powerful kingdom located south of Egypt in Nubia.  They were known for their advanced civilization, including ironworking, trade, and architecture.  Sometimes also refers more broadly to people from the Horn of Africa.|The Kushites built impressive pyramids and temples. 库施人建造了令人印象深刻的金字塔和寺庙。|The Kushites were skilled traders and controlled important trade routes.  库施人是熟练的商人，控制着重要的贸易路线。|Nubians, Ethiopians|Egyptians (sometimes considered rivals)|noun
