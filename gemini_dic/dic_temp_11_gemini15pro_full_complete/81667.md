green-manuring: /ˌɡriːn məˈnjʊərɪŋ/ | n. | 绿肥，绿肥种植 | The practice of plowing under or otherwise incorporating green vegetation into the soil to improve its physical structure and fertility. |  We used green-manuring to enrich the soil before planting the vegetables. 我们在种植蔬菜之前用绿肥来肥沃土壤。| synonyms: cover cropping, green manure cropping | antonyms: chemical fertilization | noun

green-manure: /ˌɡriːn məˈnjʊər/ | n. | 绿肥 | A crop grown specifically to be plowed under while green or soon after flowering to improve the soil. |  Rye is often used as a green-manure. 黑麦通常用作绿肥。 | synonyms: cover crop | antonyms: chemical fertilizer | noun
