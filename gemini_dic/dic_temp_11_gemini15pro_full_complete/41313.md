corecipients:/ˌkəʊrɪˈsɪpiənts/ | n. | 共同接收者；共同收件人 | People who receive something together, such as an email or a letter. | Example: The corecipients of the award were all smiles.  该奖项的共同获奖者都笑了。

Example: All corecipients on this email will be notified of any replies. 此电子邮件的所有共同收件人都会收到回复通知。 | Synonyms: joint recipients, fellow recipients | Antonyms: sender, originator | Noun
