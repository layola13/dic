caryophyllous: /ˌkærɪəˈfɪləs/ | adj. |  具瓣状花冠的；石竹科植物的 | Having petals separate and distinct from one another, as in flowers of the pink family (Caryophyllaceae). | Example: The caryophyllous flower had five distinct petals. 这朵瓣状花冠的花有五片清晰的花瓣。

Example:  Many caryophyllous plants are popular garden flowers. 许多石竹科植物是受欢迎的园艺花卉。 | 近义词: polypetalous | 反义词: gamopetalous | 词性: adjective
