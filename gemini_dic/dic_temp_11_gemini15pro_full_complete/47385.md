deadbolt:/ˈdɛdbəʊlt/ (US) /ˈdɛdbɒlt/ (UK)| n.|门闩；插销锁|A lock bolt that is moved by turning a knob or key without the action of a spring.  A strong lock that is often used in addition to a regular door lock.|例句：I slid the deadbolt into place. 我把插销锁上。

例句：Make sure the deadbolt is engaged before you leave. 离开前确保插销锁已锁好。|近义词：lock, bolt|反义词:unlock|词性:noun
