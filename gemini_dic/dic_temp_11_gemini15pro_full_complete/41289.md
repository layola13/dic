cordite:/ˈkɔːrdaɪt/| n.|线状无烟火药|A smokeless explosive made chiefly of nitrocellulose, nitroglycerin, and petroleum jelly.|The cordite exploded with a deafening roar. 线状无烟火药爆发出震耳欲聋的巨响。|近义词:gunpowder, explosive|反义词:inert material|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From cordite,  a trademark from cord (string, rope) + -ite.  Late 19th century: from the cord-like appearance of the explosive.
