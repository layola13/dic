in-the-end:/ɪn ðiː ɛnd/
|adv.|最终，最后|Finally, ultimately, eventually. After everything has happened or been considered.|例句：In the end, they decided to go to the beach. 最后，他们决定去海滩。|近义词：finally, eventually, ultimately, at last|反义词:initially, at first|词性:副词
in-the-end:/ɪn ðiː ɛnd/
|n.|最后，结局|The final outcome or result.|例句：The in-the-end of the story was surprising. 故事的结局令人惊讶。|近义词：conclusion, outcome, result|反义词:beginning, start|词性:名词
