nucamentaceous:/ˌnjuːkəmɛnˈteɪʃəs/| adj.|坚果状的，似坚果的|Bearing or resembling a nucament; relating to plants that bear nucaments (a dry fruit consisting of many small nuts).|例句：The tree's nucamentaceous fruits littered the ground. 这棵树的坚果状果实散落在地上。

例句：The botanist identified the plant as nucamentaceous due to its distinct fruit structure.  植物学家根据其独特的果实结构将该植物鉴定为坚果状的。|近义词：nut-bearing, nutlike|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly smooth, fleshy depending on the context)|词性:adjective
