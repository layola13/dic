mockeries:/ˈmɑːkəriːz/ |n.|嘲弄，嘲笑，模仿|An act of mocking or making fun of someone or something; a poor, inadequate, or contemptible imitation.|The play was a mockery of justice. 这部戏是对正义的嘲弄。|synonyms: derision, ridicule, scorn, contempt, imitation, travesty|antonyms: respect, admiration, praise, reverence|noun
