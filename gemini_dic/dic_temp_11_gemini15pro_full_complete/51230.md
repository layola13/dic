destroyer:/dɪˈstrɔɪər/| n. |驱逐舰；破坏者|A warship designed to destroy other ships, especially submarines.  A person or thing that destroys something.|The destroyer escorted the aircraft carrier. 这艘驱逐舰护送航空母舰。/ The hurricane was a destroyer of homes and businesses.  飓风摧毁了房屋和企业。|近义词：warship, demolisher, ruiner|反义词：creator, builder|词性：名词

词源: From destroy + -er.  Ultimately from Latin destruere.
