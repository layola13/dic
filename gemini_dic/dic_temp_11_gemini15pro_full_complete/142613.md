osteopathy: /ˌɒstiˈɒpəθi/ (UK) /ˌɑːstiˈɑːpəθi/ (US)| n. |整骨疗法|A system of medicine based on the belief that the body is able to heal itself and that musculoskeletal problems can be addressed through manipulation and massage of the skeleton and tissues.|She's undergoing osteopathy for her back pain. 她正在接受整骨疗法治疗背痛。|近义词: chiropractic, manipulative therapy|反义词: conventional medicine|词性: noun

词源: From osteo- + -pathy.  Osteo- comes from Greek *ostéon* meaning "bone," and -pathy comes from Greek *-pátheia* meaning "suffering" or "disease," ultimately from *páthos* "suffering, feeling, experience."
