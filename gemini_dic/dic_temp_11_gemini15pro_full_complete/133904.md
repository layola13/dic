/nɒnˈɡʌtərəl/ | adj. | 非喉音的；非咽喉音的 | Not produced in the throat; not guttural.  A nonguttural sound is made without constriction in the throat. | The sound of "m" is nonguttural.  "m"的发音是非喉音的。| The singer's voice was clear and nonguttural.  这位歌手的声音清晰且非喉音。 | 近义词: smooth, clear, euphonious | 反义词: guttural, harsh, raspy | 词性: adjective

词源:  "Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "guttural" (from Latin "guttur," meaning "throat").
