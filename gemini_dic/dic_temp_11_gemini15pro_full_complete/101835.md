irruptible:/ɪˈrʌptɪbl/
adjective
不可中断的；不能闯入的；不容侵犯的
Not capable of being interrupted or disturbed; not subject to irruption or invasion.
The irruptible flow of time. 时间不可阻挡的流动。
Near Synonyms:  inviolable, uninterruptible, continuous, constant, incessant
Antonyms: interruptible, breakable, divisible
词性: 形容词
