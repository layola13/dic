bulla:/ˈbʊlə/ (US), /ˈbʌlə/ (UK)| n. |大泡，水疱；印玺；教皇诏书；中世纪教皇或皇帝颁发的用铅封加盖的诏书或特权证书|A rounded prominence; a bubble-like cavity; a blister; a seal, especially a leaden seal attached to papal documents|The doctor diagnosed the skin irritation as a bulla.|近义词：blister, vesicle, bleb, seal|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "depression" for the physical sense)|noun

bulla:/ˈbʊlə/ (US), /ˈbʌlə/ (UK)| n. |（考古）粘土圆形或球形封泥|A hollow clay sphere or envelope used in ancient Mesopotamia as a container for tokens representing trade goods|Archaeologists unearthed several bullae containing ancient tokens.|近义词：seal, envelope|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
