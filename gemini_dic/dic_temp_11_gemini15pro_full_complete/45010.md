csa:/ˌsiː es ˈeɪ/ | n. | Community Supported Agriculture (社区支持农业) | A system in which consumers buy shares of a farm's harvest in advance and receive a portion of the produce throughout the growing season.  | Example:  Joining a CSA is a great way to get fresh, local produce.  加入社区支持农业是一个获得新鲜本地农产品的好方法。

Example:  The CSA delivers boxes of vegetables every week during the summer.  社区支持农业在夏季每周都会配送蔬菜箱。 | 近义词: farm share, local food program | 反义词: supermarket, industrial agriculture | 词性: noun
