fragmentation:/ˌfræɡmənˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 碎片化，分裂，破碎 | The process of breaking something into smaller parts. | The fragmentation of the Roman Empire led to numerous smaller states.  罗马帝国的碎片化导致了许多更小的国家的出现。|  disintegration, division, breaking apart | unification, integration | noun

fragmentation:/ˌfræɡmənˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 破裂，片段化（尤指计算机文件）|  In computer science, the storing of a file in several separate areas of memory. |  Disk fragmentation can slow down your computer. 磁盘碎片会降低你的计算机速度。| scattering, dispersal | consolidation, unification | noun
