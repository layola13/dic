fulgurating:/ˈfʌlɡjʊˌreɪtɪŋ/
adjective
闪电般的，闪光的，(医学)电灼的，(尤指疼痛)剧烈的
occurring suddenly and intensely, like a flash of lightning; (medical) destroying tissue by means of a high-frequency electric current; (especially of pain) sudden and severe.
The patient experienced fulgurating pain in his chest. 病人感到胸口一阵剧烈的疼痛。
近义词:sudden, intense, searing, flashing, piercing, lancinating
反义词:mild, dull, chronic, gradual
词性:adjective

fulgurate:/ˈfʌlɡjʊˌreɪt/
verb
(使)像闪电般闪过，(医学)电灼(组织)
to flash like lightning; (medical) to destroy (tissue) by means of a high-frequency electric current
The meteor fulgurated across the night sky. 流星划过夜空。
近义词:flash, blaze, gleam
反义词:dim, fade
词性:verb

词源: From Latin fulguratus, past participle of fulgurare (“to flash like lightning”), from fulgur (“lightning”).
