acquaintable:/əˈkweɪntəbl/
adjective
易于结识的；容易相处的
Easy to get to know; approachable.
例句：She has a very acquaintable personality. 她性格随和，很容易结识。

例句：He seemed quite acquaintable, so I went over and introduced myself.  他看起来很容易相处，于是我走过去做了自我介绍。

近义词：approachable, affable, sociable, friendly, amiable
反义词：unapproachable, aloof, standoffish, unfriendly
词性：adjective

词源：From acquaint + -able.  来自acquaint（使熟悉） + -able（可…的）。
