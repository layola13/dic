divestiture:/daɪˈvɛstɪtʃər/ |n.|剥夺，出售，放弃|The company is undergoing a divestiture of its non-core assets.  该公司正在剥离其非核心资产。|Synonyms: disposal, sale, shedding|Antonyms: acquisition, purchase, investment|noun
divest:/daɪˈvɛst/ |vt.|剥夺，使脱去，出售|The court divested him of all his property. 法院剥夺了他所有的财产。|Synonyms: deprive, strip, rid|Antonyms: invest, endow, furnish|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *divestitura, from Latin divestire "to undress," from dis- "off, away" + vestire "to clothe."  Meaning shifted from "undressing" to "stripping of property" in the 17th century.
