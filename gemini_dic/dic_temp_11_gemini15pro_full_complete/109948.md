liberalizations:/ˌlɪbərələˈzeɪʃənz/
|n.|自由化，放宽管制|The act of making something less strict or controlled; the removal or loosening of restrictions.|例句：The government has introduced a series of liberalizations in the financial sector. 政府在金融领域实施了一系列自由化政策。

例句：The liberalizations of trade policies have led to increased global commerce. 贸易政策的自由化导致了全球贸易的增长。

|近义词：deregulation, easing, relaxation, opening up|
|反义词：restrictions, tightening, control, regulation|
|词性：名词|
