arenga:/əˈrɛŋɡə/ | n. | 糖棕; 糖棕树;  (糖棕的) 树液; 激动人心的演说;  | A type of palm tree (Arenga pinnata) native to tropical Asia, cultivated for its sap which is used to make sugar, wine, and vinegar. Also refers to the sap itself, and figuratively, to a stirring or rousing speech. |  The arenga tree is a valuable source of sugar in some parts of Asia.  糖棕树在亚洲一些地区是重要的糖源。

The politician delivered a fiery arenga to the crowd. 这位政治家向人群发表了激动人心的演说。 | 近义词：harangue, exhortation, address, palm,  | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
