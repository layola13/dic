arthroderm:/ˈɑːrθrəˌdɜːrm/| n. |节肢动物的表皮，外骨骼|The arthroderm of insects is composed of chitin. 昆虫的外骨骼是由几丁质组成的。|例句：The hard arthroderm protects the insect. 坚硬的外骨骼保护着昆虫。

例句：The arthroderm is shed during molting.  外骨骼在蜕皮过程中脱落。

|近义词：exoskeleton, cuticle|反义词:endoskeleton|词性:noun
