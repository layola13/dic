melted:/ˈmɛltɪd/| adj.| 融化的，熔化的|Something that has changed from a solid to a liquid state through heat. |The melted butter soaked into the bread. 融化的黄油浸入了面包。|近义词：liquefied, molten, dissolved|反义词:frozen, solid|词性:形容词
melt:/mɛlt/| v. n.| 融化，熔化；溶解；消散；|To change from a solid to a liquid state through heat; to disappear gradually.|The snow melted quickly in the sunshine. 雪在阳光下迅速融化了。|近义词：liquefy, dissolve, thaw|反义词:freeze, solidify|词性:动词,名词

词源: From Old English *meltan, from Proto-Germanic *meltaną, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)melt- "to make soft, melt".
