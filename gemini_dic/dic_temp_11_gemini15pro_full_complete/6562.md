an-arm-and-a-leg:/ən ˌɑːrm ənd ə ˈleɡ/ | idiom |代价高昂，非常昂贵 |  This idiom means that something is very expensive. It implies that you would have to give up a valuable body part to afford it, obviously an exaggeration used for emphasis.| That car cost me an arm and a leg. 那辆车花了我一大笔钱。|  expensive, costly, exorbitant, pricey | cheap, inexpensive, affordable | idiom
