overmuch:/ˌōvərˈmʌtʃ/| adj. adv.| 过多的，过分的；过度的|Too much; excessively.  It emphasizes an excessive amount beyond what is necessary or desirable.|He worried overmuch about his health. 他过分担心自己的健康。|近义词：excessive, undue, immoderate, exorbitant|反义词: moderate, reasonable, insufficient|词性:形容词, 副词
词源: From Old English *ofermycel, from ofer (over) + mycel (much).
