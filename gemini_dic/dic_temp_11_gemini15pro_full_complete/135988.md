nonraisable:/nɒnˈreɪzəbl/ | adj. | 不可提升的，无法提出的 |  Describes an exception in programming that cannot be handled or propagated further up the call stack. It typically occurs when an exception is raised in a context where it cannot be caught or processed, such as during garbage collection or during the finalization of objects. |  The error was nonraisable, so the program terminated abruptly.  这个错误是不可提升的，所以程序突然终止了。 |  unhandleable, uncatchable, untrappable |  handleable, catchable, trappable | adjective
