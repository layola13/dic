henori: /hɛˈnɔːri/
noun
henori (a Japanese term)
Henori refers to a unique Japanese aesthetic concept that describes the beauty of things that are bent or twisted, highlighting the charm in imperfection and asymmetry. It often applies to natural forms like branches or paths, but can also extend to other objects.
例句：The bonsai's henori added to its character. 这盆栽的扭曲形态增添了它的韵味。

The winding mountain path had a certain henori that made the hike more interesting. 蜿蜒的山路有一种独特的扭曲美，使徒步旅行更加有趣。


近义词：asymmetry, irregularity, crookedness, curvature
反义词：symmetry, straightness, regularity
词性: n.
