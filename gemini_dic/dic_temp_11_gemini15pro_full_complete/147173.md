overlubricating: /ˌoʊvərˈluːbrɪkeɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度润滑
Applying excessive lubricant, resulting in potential issues like slippage or attracting dirt.
The mechanic warned against overlubricating the chain, as it could attract dirt and cause it to wear out faster.  这名机械师警告不要过度润滑链条，因为它可能会吸附灰尘并导致其更快磨损。
Overlubricating the engine can lead to decreased performance.  过度润滑发动机可能导致性能下降。
近义词: overgreasing
反义词: underlubricating
