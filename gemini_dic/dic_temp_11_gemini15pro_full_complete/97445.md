infuser:/ɪnˈfjuːzər/| n.| 浸泡器；注入器|A device in which tea leaves or herbs are steeped in hot water to make a drink.|例句：I use an infuser to make loose leaf tea. 我用浸泡器泡散叶茶。

例句：She added some fruit to the water infuser for a refreshing drink.  她往浸泡器里加了些水果，做了一杯清爽的饮料。|近义词：tea strainer, tea ball, diffuser|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
