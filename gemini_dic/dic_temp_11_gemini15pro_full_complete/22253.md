bondswoman: /ˈbɒndzwʊmən/ (UK) /ˈbɑːndzwʊmən/ (US) | n. | 女奴；女契约佣工 | A woman who is legally bound to service without wages, typically as a punishment or to pay off a debt. |  In the 18th century, many bondswomen worked as domestic servants in wealthy households.  在18世纪，许多女契约佣工在富裕的家庭中当佣人。| The historical novel described the hardships endured by bondswomen in the colonies. 这部历史小说描述了殖民地女奴所遭受的苦难。 | 近义词: slave, servant, captive | 反义词: freeman, freewoman | 词性: noun
