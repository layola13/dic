oversacrificially:/ˌoʊvərˌsækrɪˈfɪʃəli/
| adv. | 过度牺牲地，过分牺牲地 |  In a manner that involves excessive or unnecessary sacrifice.  It implies going beyond what is reasonable or healthy in terms of giving up one's own needs, desires, or well-being for the sake of others or a cause. | She worked oversacrificially for her family, neglecting her own health. 她为了家人过度牺牲，忽略了自己的健康。|  Excessively, unduly, immoderately, unnecessarily | Selfishly, moderately | adverb
