anchoress:/ˈæŋkərɪs/| n. |女隐士，遁世修行的女子|A woman who withdraws from the world for religious reasons and lives in seclusion, often enclosed in a small cell attached to a church.|例句：The anchoress devoted her life to prayer and contemplation.  这位女隐士将自己的一生奉献给了祈祷和沉思。

例句：Julian of Norwich was a famous anchoress.  诺里奇的朱利安是一位著名的女隐士。|近义词：hermit, recluse, ascetic|反义词:|noun
