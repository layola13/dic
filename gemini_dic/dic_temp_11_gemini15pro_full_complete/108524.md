laywomen:/ˈleɪˌwɪmɪn/ /ˈleɪˌwʊmɪn/
noun
外行女子；俗女子；非专业人士（尤指女性）
A laywoman is a woman who is not trained, qualified, or experienced in a particular subject or profession.
The medical procedure was explained to the laywomen in simple terms.  这个医疗程序用简单的术语向外行妇女解释。
The laywomen on the jury struggled to understand the complex legal arguments. 陪审团中的外行妇女难以理解复杂的法律论证。
synonyms: non-professional, amateur, non-expert, outsider
antonyms: professional, expert, specialist
