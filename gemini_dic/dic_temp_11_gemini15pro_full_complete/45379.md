cunningly:/ˈkʌnɪŋli/| adv.|狡猾地，诡诈地|In a clever and deceitful way, typically in order to achieve a particular aim.|The thief cunningly disguised himself as a security guard. 小偷狡猾地把自己伪装成保安。|近义词：slyly, craftily, artfully, deviously|反义词：honestly, openly, frankly|词性:副词
Etymology: From Middle English *cunningeli*, from Old English *cunnunglice* (“skillfully, knowingly”), equivalent to *cunning* + *-ly*.
