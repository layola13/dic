moniliform:/ˈmɒnɪlɪfɔːm/ /ˈmɑːnɪlɪfɔːrm/| adj.|念珠形的，串珠状的|Resembling a string of beads; constricted at regular intervals. |The bacteria formed moniliform chains. 这些细菌形成了念珠状的链。|The necklace was moniliform, with small pearls strung together. 这条项链是念珠形的，由一颗颗小珍珠串成。|近义词：beaded, chain-like, segmented|反义词:continuous, unsegmented|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *monile* (necklace) + *-formis* (having the form of).  *Monile* is related to *monere* (to warn, advise), perhaps originally referring to amulets worn as necklaces.
