come-true:/ˌkʌmˈtruː/ | adj. | 实现的，成真的 |  Something that comes true happens in reality after being hoped for or wished for. |  Her dream of becoming a doctor came true. 她成为医生的梦想实现了。| synonyms: realized, fulfilled, actualized | antonyms: unrealized, unfulfilled | adjective

come-true:/ˌkʌmˈtruː/ | n. | 实现，梦想成真 |  An instance of something becoming reality after being hoped for or wished for.  | The lottery win was a dream come true.  赢得彩票真是梦想成真。 | synonyms: realization, fulfillment, actualization | antonyms: failure, disappointment | noun
