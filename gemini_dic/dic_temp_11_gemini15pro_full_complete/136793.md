nonsetting:/nɒnˈsɛtɪŋ/ | adj. | 不凝固的，不沉淀的 | Not solidifying or hardening; remaining fluid or in suspension. | The paint was specifically designed to be nonsetting, allowing for easy cleanup.  这种油漆专门设计成不凝固的，便于清理。|  Example 2: The nonsetting adhesive is ideal for temporary applications.  这种不凝固的粘合剂非常适合临时应用。 | 近义词: noncoagulating, nonhardening | 反义词: setting, hardening, coagulating | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "setting" (present participle of "set," meaning to solidify or harden)
