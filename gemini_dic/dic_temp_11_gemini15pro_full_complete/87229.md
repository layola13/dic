heresiarch:/ˈhɛrɪsiɑːrk/ | n. |异端首领，异教创始人|A leader or founder of a heresy or heretical sect.  A person who originates or is the chief propagator of a heresy. |例句：The heresiarch was excommunicated from the church. 异端首领被逐出教会。

例句：The heresiarch's teachings were deemed dangerous and subversive.  异端首领的教义被认为是危险和颠覆性的。 |近义词：heretic, schismatic, dissenter, nonconformist |反义词：orthodox, conformist |词性: noun
