harmonist:/ˈhɑːrmənɪst/ | n. | 和声歌手；作曲家；协调者；调音师 | A person who sings or plays harmony, a composer, someone who brings different elements into agreement, or someone who tunes musical instruments. |例句：The harmonist blended his voice beautifully with the lead singer. 和声歌手的声音与主唱完美融合。

例句：Bach was a master harmonist, creating complex and beautiful musical textures. 巴赫是一位和声大师，创作了复杂而优美的音乐织体。| 近义词：musician, composer, singer, coordinator, tuner | 反义词: soloist, discordant
