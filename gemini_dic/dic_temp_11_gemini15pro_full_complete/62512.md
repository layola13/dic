endozoa:/ˌɛndoʊˈzoʊə/
n.
内寄生动物
Internal parasites, specifically animals that live inside the body of their host.
The biologist studied the endozoan parasites infecting the fish.  生物学家研究了感染鱼类的内寄生动物。
近义词:internal parasites, endoparasites
反义词:ectozoa, ectoparasites
词性:名词


Etymology:
From  endo- (within) + -zoa (animals).  Ultimately from Greek *éndon (within) +  *zôion (animal).
