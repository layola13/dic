jacuzzis:/dʒəˈkuːziz/| n. |按摩浴缸，极可意浴缸（Jacuzzi 的复数）|A large bath with underwater jets that massage the body. It is a trademarked name.|例句：They relaxed in the hotel's jacuzzis after a long day of sightseeing.  他们观光了一整天后在酒店的按摩浴缸里放松身心。

例句：The resort boasts private jacuzzis on every balcony.  这个度假村的每个阳台上都设有私人按摩浴缸。|近义词：hot tubs, whirlpools, spas|反义词:bathtubs|词性:名词
