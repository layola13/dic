nihilistic: /ˌnaɪɪˈlɪstɪk/ /ˌniːɪˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
虚无主义的；否定一切的
Rejecting all religious and moral principles in the belief that life is meaningless.
例句：
He held a nihilistic view of the world.
他持有世界是虚无的观点。

His nihilistic attitude made him difficult to work with.
他虚无主义的态度让他很难共事。


近义词：pessimistic, cynical, negative
反义词：optimistic, positive, hopeful
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *nihiliste*, from Latin *nihil* ("nothing") + *-ist*.
