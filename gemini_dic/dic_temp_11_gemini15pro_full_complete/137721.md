nonvacuousness:/ˌnɑːnvəˈkjuːəsnəs/| n. |非空性，非平凡性|The state or quality of not being vacuous; having substance or meaning; not empty or trivial.  This often refers to a mathematical or logical statement that is not trivially true.|The theorem asserts the nonvacuousness of a certain set.  该定理断言某个集合的非空性。|近义词：substance, significance, meaningfulness, importance|反义词: vacuity, emptiness, triviality|noun
