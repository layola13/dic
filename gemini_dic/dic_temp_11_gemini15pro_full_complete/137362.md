nonteachers:/ˌnɒnˈtiːtʃəz/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈtiːtʃərz/ (US)
noun
非教师；非教学人员
people who are not teachers; people who do not work in the teaching profession.
The school employs a number of nonteachers, including administrative staff and maintenance workers. 学校雇佣了许多非教师，包括行政人员和维修工人。
Nonteachers often have a different perspective on education. 非教师对教育经常有不同的看法。
synonyms: non-teaching staff, non-faculty, non-academic staff
antonyms: teachers, educators, instructors
词性: 名词
