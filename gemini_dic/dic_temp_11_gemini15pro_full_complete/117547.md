megabits:/ˈmɛɡəˌbɪts/| n. |兆比特|A unit of information equal to one million bits, used to measure data transfer rates or storage capacity.  It is commonly used in the context of internet speeds, network bandwidth, and file sizes.|The internet connection offers speeds up to 100 megabits per second.  该互联网连接提供高达每秒 100 兆比特的速度。|Synonyms: Mbps|Antonyms: kilobits, bits|noun
