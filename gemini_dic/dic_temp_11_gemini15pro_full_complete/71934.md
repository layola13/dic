flickering:/ˈflɪkərɪŋ/| adj.|闪烁的，摇曳的，忽隐忽现的|burning unsteadily; shining with a light that is not steady|The flickering candlelight created a spooky atmosphere. 闪烁的烛光营造了一种诡异的气氛。|Synonyms: glimmering, shimmering, twinkling, flashing|Antonyms: steady, constant, fixed|adjective

flicker:/ˈflɪkər/| v. n.|闪烁，摇曳，忽隐忽现|to burn or shine unsteadily; to appear briefly|The candle flickered in the wind.  蜡烛在风中摇曳。|Synonyms: glimmer, shimmer, twinkle, flash|Antonyms: stay, remain, hold|verb, noun
