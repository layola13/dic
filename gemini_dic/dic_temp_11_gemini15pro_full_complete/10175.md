apoplectically:/ˌæpəˈplɛktɪkli/| adv.|愤怒得几乎要发作；极度愤怒地|In a manner suggesting apoplexy; with extreme anger or rage.  Characterized by or exhibiting apoplexy.  |He shouted apoplectically at the referee's decision. 他对裁判的判决暴跳如雷。|近义词：furiously, enragedly, irately|反义词：calmly, peacefully|词性:副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *apoplecticus, from Ancient Greek ἀποπληκτικός (apoplēktikós, “struck down”), from ἀποπλήσσω (apoplēssō, “disable by a stroke”), from ἀπό (apó, “away from”) + πλήσσω (plēssō, “strike”).
