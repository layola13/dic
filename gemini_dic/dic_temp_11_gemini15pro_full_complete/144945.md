overabstaining:/ˌoʊvərəbˈsteɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度节制;  |  Practicing excessive abstinence, especially from alcohol or other potentially pleasurable substances.  It implies going beyond moderation and potentially having negative consequences.  |  His overabstaining from all social activities made him seem isolated. 他过度节制所有社交活动，使他看起来很孤立。|  Overabstaining from sugar can lead to cravings and binge eating. 过度戒糖会导致渴望和暴饮暴食。 | Near synonyms: over-restricting, overly abstemious | Antonyms: indulging, overindulging | word property: Adjective
