idealic:/aɪˈdiːlɪk/ | adj. | 田园诗般的，理想的 | Extremely pleasant, beautiful, or peaceful. |  例句：We spent an idyllic week in the countryside. 我们在乡下度过了一个田园诗般的一周。| 近义词：peaceful, picturesque, utopian, serene | 反义词：bleak, unpleasant, dystopian | 词性: adjective

idealic:/aɪˈdiːlɪk/ | adj. | 田园诗般的，理想的 |  Suggestive of an idyll; charmingly simple and serene. | 例句：The island offered an idyllic setting for their honeymoon.  这个岛屿为他们的蜜月旅行提供了一个田园诗般的环境。| 近义词：tranquil, pastoral, charming | 反义词：realistic, harsh, grim | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *idylicus, from Greek eidyllion "short poem describing rustic life," diminutive of eidos "form, shape."  The meaning shifted from "pertaining to a short poem" to "pleasingly simple" and then to "charmingly natural and peaceful."
