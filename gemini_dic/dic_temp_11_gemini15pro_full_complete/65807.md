evidentially:/ˌevɪˈdenʃəli/| adv.|显然地，明显地；根据证据显示|Based on evidence; apparently.|例句：Evidentially, he was lying. 显然，他在撒谎。|近义词：apparently, obviously, clearly, seemingly, demonstrably|反义词：doubtfully, questionably|词性:副词
词源: From Late Latin *evidentialiter, from Latin evidens "evident."
