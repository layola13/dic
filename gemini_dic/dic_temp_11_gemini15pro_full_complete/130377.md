non-usurious:/ˌnɒn juˈʒʊəriəs/ | adj. | 非高利贷的 | Not charging excessive or exorbitant interest; not involving usury. |  The loan agreement was carefully structured to be non-usurious and comply with all applicable laws. 这份贷款协议经过精心设计，以确保其非高利贷性质并符合所有适用法律。

| Example:  He offered a non-usurious loan to his friend to help him start a business. 他向他的朋友提供了一笔非高利贷的贷款，以帮助他创业。| Synonym: fair, reasonable, moderate, ethical | Antonym: usurious, exorbitant, predatory | adjective
