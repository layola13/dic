nisi:/ˈnaɪsaɪ/| conj.|除非, 若不|A conjunction meaning "unless" or "except." It introduces a condition.  It is primarily used in formal or legal contexts.|例句：The contract is binding nisi you provide written notice of cancellation within 30 days.  合同具有约束力，除非您在 30 天内提供书面取消通知。

例句：Nisi prius, the case will be dismissed. 若非如此，此案将被驳回。|近义词：unless, except|反义词: if|词性: conjunction
