frenetical:/frəˈnɛtɪkəl/| adj.|疯狂的，狂热的|Marked by frenzy; frantic.  Characterized by excessive excitement or agitation.|The fans were frenetical after their team won the championship. 球迷们在他们球队赢得冠军后欣喜若狂。|近义词：frantic, frenzied, frantic, wild, delirious|反义词：calm, composed, tranquil|词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin freneticus, from Greek phrenitikos, from phrenitis ‘frenzy’.
