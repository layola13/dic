gasiform:/ˈɡæsɪfɔːrm/| adj.|气态的，气体的|Having the form or nature of gas; gaseous.|例句：The substance exists in a gasiform state at room temperature.  这种物质在室温下以气态存在。|例句：Scientists studied the gasiform emissions from the volcano. 科学家们研究了火山的气态排放物。|近义词：gaseous, aeriform|反义词：solid, liquid|词性:形容词
词源：From Latin *gasiformis, from gas (“gas”) + -formis (“-form”). Coined by Jöns Jacob Berzelius.
