antisepalous:/ˌæntiˈsɛpələs/
adjective
对萼的；与萼片对生的
Positioned opposite the sepals.  The petals of a flower are antisepalous if they are positioned directly opposite the sepals.
Example: The small, white flowers had antisepalous petals, a key characteristic for identification. 小小的白花有对萼的花瓣，这是识别的关键特征。

Example:  The botanist noted the antisepalous arrangement of the petals in his field journal. 植物学家在他的野外日记中记录了花瓣的对萼排列。
