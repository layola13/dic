bryozoa:/ˌbraɪəˈzoʊə/ | n. |苔藓虫；苔藓动物 |  Aquatic invertebrate animals that form colonies.  They are also known as moss animals. | Bryozoa are often found encrusting rocks and seaweed. 苔藓虫经常被发现包裹在岩石和海藻上。|  Bryozoan colonies can create intricate structures. 苔藓虫群体可以形成复杂的结构。 | synonyms: moss animals, Ectoprocta | antonyms: N/A | noun
