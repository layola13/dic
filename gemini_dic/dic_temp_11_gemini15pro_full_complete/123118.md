monicker:/ˈmɑːnɪkər/ /ˈmɒnɪkə(r)/| n. |绰号，别名|A name, especially an informal or descriptive one.  A familiar or humorous name given to a person or thing instead of or as well as the real name.|His monicker is "Speedy". 他的绰号是“飞毛腿”。|近义词：nickname, alias, sobriquet, handle|反义词:formal name, legal name|词性:noun


monicker:|词源|From the Shelta word *munik*, meaning "name".  Shelta is a language spoken by Irish Travellers. It is thought to have originated as a cant, or secret language, designed to obscure communication from outsiders.|"Monicker" entered mainstream English usage in the 19th century, likely through contact with Irish Travellers.|词性:noun
