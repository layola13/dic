baneberry:/ˈbeɪnˌberi/ | n. | 毒莓 | A poisonous berry, especially of the genus Actaea. |  The baneberry plant, though attractive, produces highly toxic berries. 毒莓植物虽然吸引人，但会结出剧毒的浆果。

The child was warned to stay away from the baneberry bushes.  孩子被警告要远离毒莓灌木丛。| 近义词：poison berry,  nightshade | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a poisonous berry, perhaps a safe/edible berry) blueberry, strawberry | 词性: noun
