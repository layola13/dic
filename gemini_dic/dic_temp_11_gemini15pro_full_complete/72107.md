flocking:/ˈflɒkɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈflɑːkɪŋ/ (US)| n. | 群集，成群结队，植绒，（墙纸的）绒毛 | The act of gathering in a flock; a material used to create a velvety texture. | Birds are flocking together for the winter migration. 鸟类正聚集在一起准备冬季迁徙. The wallpaper has a delicate flocking pattern. 墙纸上有一种精致的植绒图案。 |  近义词：gathering, congregating, swarming, aggregation, fuzz, pile | 反义词：dispersing, scattering | 词性：名词

flocking:/ˈflɒkɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈflɑːkɪŋ/ (US)| adj. | 成群的，群集的 |  Occurring in flocks; covered with flocking. | There are flocking birds in the sky. 天空中有成群的鸟。 | 近义词：gregarious, swarming | 反义词：solitary, dispersed | 词性：形容词 (较少使用)


词源: From Middle English *flokken, frequentative of *floken  "to fly in a flock," from Old English *floccan "to flock," of uncertain origin. Related to flock (n.).
