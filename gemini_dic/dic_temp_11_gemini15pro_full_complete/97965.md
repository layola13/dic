innutrition:/ˌɪnjuˈtrɪʃn/ | n. |营养不良|A state of poor nutrition; inadequate nourishment.|Malnutrition and innutrition are serious problems in developing countries. 营养不良和营养不足在发展中国家是严重的问题。| 近义词: malnutrition, undernutrition | 反义词: overnutrition | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From in- (not) + nutrition.  The prefix "in-" acts as a negative, indicating a lack of or deficiency in nutrition.
