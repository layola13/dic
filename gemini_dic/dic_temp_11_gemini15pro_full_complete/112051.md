longbows:/ˈlɒŋboʊz/ (UK) /ˈlɔːŋboʊz/ (US)| n. |长弓|A powerful type of bow about six feet long, formerly used in England for war and hunting.  A longbow requires considerable strength to draw.|The English archers were renowned for their skill with the longbow.  英国弓箭手以其精湛的长弓技艺而闻名。|Near synonyms: bow, war bow|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
