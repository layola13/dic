injudiciously:/ˌɪndʒʊˈdɪʃəsli/
adverb
不明智地；不审慎地
in a way that shows bad judgment; unwisely
He injudiciously revealed confidential information. 他不明智地泄露了机密信息。
Synonyms: unwisely, imprudently, foolishly, rashly, carelessly
Antonyms: wisely, prudently, judiciously, carefully, thoughtfully
詞性: adverb (副詞)
