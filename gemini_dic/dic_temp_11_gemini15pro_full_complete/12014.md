arthropodan:/ˈɑːrθrəˌpɒdən/ (US) /ˈɑːθrəpɒdən/ (UK)| n. |节肢动物|An invertebrate animal of the phylum Arthropoda, such as an insect, spider, or crustacean, characterized by a segmented body and jointed appendages.|例句：The lobster is an arthropodan with a hard exoskeleton. 龙虾是一种具有坚硬外骨骼的节肢动物。

例句：Arthropods make up the largest phylum in the animal kingdom. 节肢动物构成了动物界中最大的门。|近义词：invertebrate, arthropod |反义词: vertebrate|词性:noun


arthropodan:/ˌɑːrθrəˈpɒdən/ (US) /ˌɑːθrəˈpɒdən/ (UK)| adj. |节肢动物的|Relating to or characteristic of arthropods.|例句：The arthropodan exoskeleton provides protection and support. 节肢动物的外骨骼提供保护和支撑。

例句：Arthropodan populations are declining worldwide due to habitat loss. 全球节肢动物的数量由于栖息地丧失正在下降。|近义词：invertebrate, arthropod |反义词: vertebrate|词性:adjective
