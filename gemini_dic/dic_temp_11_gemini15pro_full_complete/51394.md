determine:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪn/ (US), /dɪˈtɜːmɪn/ (UK)| v. | 决定；确定；查明；裁决 | to officially decide something, especially because of evidence or facts |  The tests will help the doctors determine what treatment to use. 这些测试将帮助医生确定使用何种治疗方法。| decide, establish, ascertain | waver, hesitate | verb

determine:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪn/ (US), /dɪˈtɜːmɪn/ (UK)| adj. | 坚决的；果断的 | having a strong desire to do something, so that you will not let anyone stop you | He was determined to win the race. 他决心要赢得比赛。| resolute, firm, unwavering | hesitant, indecisive | adjective
