nonphagocytic:/nɒnˌfæɡəˈsɪtɪk/ | adj. | 非吞噬的 | Not capable of phagocytosis, the process by which cells engulf and digest foreign particles or microorganisms. |  Langerhans cells are nonphagocytic dendritic cells of the epidermis.  朗格汉斯细胞是表皮的非吞噬性树突状细胞。|  Nonphagocytic cells play a crucial role in immune regulation. 非吞噬细胞在免疫调节中起着至关重要的作用。 | 近义词: non-ingesting | 反义词: phagocytic | 词性: adjective
