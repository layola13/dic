bisie:/ˈbɪziː/
adjective
忙碌的；繁忙的
occupied, especially with work or other activity.
例句：I've been very bisie lately with work.  我最近工作很忙。

例句：She had a bisie day filled with meetings. 她今天开了一整天的会，很忙。

近义词：busy, engaged, occupied, swamped
反义词：idle, free, unoccupied
词性:adjective
