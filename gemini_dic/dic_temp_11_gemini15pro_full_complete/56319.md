dogma:/ˈdɒɡmə/ (UK) /ˈdɔːɡmə/ (US)| n. |教条，信条， dogma|A principle or set of principles laid down by an authority as incontrovertibly true. |Example 1:  The dogma of the divine right of kings.  君权神授的信条。

Example 2:  Challenging religious dogma.  挑战宗教教条。|近义词：doctrine, tenet, creed, belief|反义词：heterodoxy, unbelief, skepticism|词性: noun
