companionship:/kəmˈpæn.jən.ʃɪp/| n.|友谊；陪伴；伴侣关系|The state of being with someone and enjoying their company.  The comfort and support provided by a companion.|I really value her companionship. 我很珍惜她的陪伴。|Synonyms: fellowship, friendship, camaraderie, society, company|Antonyms: loneliness, isolation, solitude|noun
