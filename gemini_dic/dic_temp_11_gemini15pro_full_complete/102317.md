ivory: /ˈaɪvəri/ (US) /ˈaɪvəri/ (UK)| n. adj.| 象牙；象牙色；乳白色|A hard, white material made from the tusks and teeth of elephants and other animals.| The piano keys were made of ivory.  钢琴键是象牙做的。| 近义词: bone, cream, off-white | 反义词: black, ebony | 词性: noun, adjective

ivory: /ˈaɪvəri/ (US) /ˈaɪvəri/ (UK)| adj.| 象牙色的；乳白色的|Having the creamy white color of ivory.| She wore an ivory dress to the wedding. 她穿着一件象牙色的连衣裙参加婚礼。| 近义词: cream, off-white | 反义词: black, ebony | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French ivoire, from Late Latin eboreus, from Latin ebur ("ivory").
