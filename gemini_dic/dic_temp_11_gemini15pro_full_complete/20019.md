biomagnetism:/ˌbaɪoʊˈmæɡnɪtɪzəm/
n.|生物磁学|The study of magnetic fields produced by living organisms.  It encompasses both the measurement of these fields and their effects on biological systems.|Researchers are exploring the potential of biomagnetism in medical diagnostics.|近义词: magnetobiology|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
