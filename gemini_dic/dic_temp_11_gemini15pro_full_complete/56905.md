dot-comer:/ˈdɑːtˌkʌmər/ |n.|网络公司创业者；网络新贵|A person who became wealthy through involvement with the Internet, especially during the dot-com boom of the late 1990s.|Many dot-comers lost their fortunes when the bubble burst. 许多网络新贵在泡沫破灭时赔光了家产。|Synonyms: internet entrepreneur, web pioneer|Antonyms: traditional businessman|Noun

dot-comer:/ˈdɑːtˌkʌmər/ |n.|网络公司；网络泡沫时期成立的公司|A company that does most of its business on the internet, especially one formed during the dot-com boom of the late 1990s. |The dot-comer went bankrupt after the market crash. 这家网络公司在市场崩盘后破产了。|Synonyms: internet company, online business|Antonyms: brick-and-mortar business|Noun
