biventral:/baɪˈventrəl/
adjective
双腹的；有两个腹部的
Having two bellies or two belly-like parts.
The digastric muscle is a biventral muscle located in the neck.  双腹肌位于颈部，是一块双腹肌。
The frog's biventral muscles help it to jump.  青蛙的双腹肌帮助它跳跃。

近义词:digastric
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider single-bellied if such a term exists in the specific context)
