bellied:/ˈbelɪd/| adj.|大肚子的，鼓起的|Having a belly of a specified kind.  Characterized by a prominent belly.|例句：A pot-bellied pig. 一只大肚猪。

例句：A red-bellied woodpecker. 一只红腹啄木鸟。|近义词：protruding, bulging, swollen|反义词:flat, hollow|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From 'belly' + '-ed'.  'Belly' comes from Old English 'belg', meaning "bag, pouch, belly."  The suffix '-ed' is used to form adjectives meaning "having, characterized by."
