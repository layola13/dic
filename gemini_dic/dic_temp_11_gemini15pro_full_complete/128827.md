nightshade:/ˈnaɪtʃeɪd/| n. |茄科植物；颠茄；夜来香|A plant of the Solanaceae family, typically having poisonous berries. Many common vegetables, such as tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers, belong to this family.  Some nightshades are highly toxic. |The deadly nightshade is a highly poisonous plant.  颠茄是一种剧毒植物。|Synonyms: belladonna, atropa,  solanaceous plant|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a plant family.  Could consider edible plants as a loose antonym in some contexts.)|noun

nightshade:/ˈnaɪtʃeɪd/| adj. |茄科的|Belonging to or relating to the nightshade family of plants (Solanaceae).|These nightshade vegetables are a staple in many cuisines. 这些茄科蔬菜是许多菜肴的主食。|Synonyms: solanaceous|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a plant family adjective.)|adjective
