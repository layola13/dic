antienvironmentalists:/ˌæntiɪnˌvaɪrənˈmɛntəlɪsts/
n.
反环保主义者
People who oppose environmentalism.  Those who actively or passively resist efforts to protect the environment.
Antienvironmentalists often argue that environmental regulations hinder economic growth. 反环保主义者经常争辩说，环境法规阻碍了经济增长。
Regulations are often seen as burdensome by antienvironmentalists, who believe they stifle innovation and job creation.  法规经常被反环保主义者视为负担，他们认为这些法规扼杀了创新和就业创造。
近义词:conservationist, preservationist
反义词:environmentalist, ecologist
词性:noun
