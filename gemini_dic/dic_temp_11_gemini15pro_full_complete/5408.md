almoravid: /ˌælməˈrɑːvɪd/ /ˌælməˈrævɪd/ | n. adj.| 阿尔摩拉维德王朝（的）|  A member of a Berber Muslim dynasty that ruled in North Africa and Spain in the 11th and 12th centuries.  Also used as an adjective to describe things relating to this dynasty. | The Almoravids conquered much of Spain. 阿尔摩拉维德王朝征服了西班牙的大部分地区。|  Synonym:  Murabitun (Arabic name)| Antonym: None (specific historical dynasty, no direct opposite) | Noun, Adjective
