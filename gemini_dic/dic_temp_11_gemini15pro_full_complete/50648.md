deritualized:/diːˈrɪtʃuəlaɪzd/
adj.
去仪式化的，非仪式化的
Deprived of ritual; made secular or informal.  Removed from a traditional or ceremonial context.
The wedding ceremony was deritualized, focusing on personal vows rather than traditional religious practices.  这场婚礼仪式被去仪式化了，侧重于个人的誓言而不是传统的宗教仪式。
The once sacred dance has become deritualized and is now performed as a form of entertainment.  曾经神圣的舞蹈已经变得世俗化，现在作为一种娱乐形式表演。
secularized, informal, unceremonious
ritualized, formal, ceremonial
adjective
