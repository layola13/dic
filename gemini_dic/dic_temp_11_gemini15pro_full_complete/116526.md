mathemagicians:/ˌmæθəməˈdʒɪʃənz/| n. |数学魔术师|A person who is exceptionally skilled in mathematics, often combining mathematical prowess with a flair for presentation or problem-solving that seems almost magical.  It's a blend of "mathematician" and "magician".|例句：The mathemagicians wowed the audience with their lightning-fast calculations. 这些数学魔术师以其闪电般的计算速度让观众惊叹不已。

例句：He's a true mathemagician, able to solve complex equations in his head. 他是一位真正的数学魔术师，能够用心算解出复杂的方程式。|近义词：mathematical prodigy, math whiz, number cruncher|反义词:mathematically challenged|词性:noun
