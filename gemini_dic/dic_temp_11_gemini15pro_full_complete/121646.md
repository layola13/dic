misperformance:/ˌmɪspərˈfɔːrməns/| n.| 差强人意的表现，不佳表现，失职|The misperformance of the company led to a drop in stock prices.  这家公司的不佳表现导致股价下跌。|例句：The actor's misperformance in the play disappointed the audience.  这位演员在剧中的糟糕表现令观众失望。|近义词：failure, underperformance, non-performance|反义词：success, achievement, good performance|词性:名词

Etymology:
From mis- + performance.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "bad, wrong, or wrongly."  "Performance" comes from the Old French "performir" and ultimately from the Latin "performare," meaning "to complete, to carry out."
