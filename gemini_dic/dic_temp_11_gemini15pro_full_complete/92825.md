iconological:/ˌaɪkənəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
图像学的；图像象征的
Relating to the study of the meaning of images and symbols, especially in art.
Iconological interpretation goes beyond simple description and considers the cultural, social, and historical context of the artwork. 图像学解释不仅仅是简单的描述，还考虑了艺术品的文化、社会和历史背景。
The iconological analysis of the painting revealed hidden meanings related to the artist's personal life. 这幅画的图像学分析揭示了与艺术家个人生活相关的隐藏含义。
近义词:symbolic, emblematic
反义词:literal
词性:adjective
