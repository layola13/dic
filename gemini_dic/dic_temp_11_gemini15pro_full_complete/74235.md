fowler:/ˈfaʊlər/| n.|捕鸟人；猎鸟者|A person who hunts or traps wildfowl.|The fowler used a decoy to attract the ducks. 捕鸟人用诱饵来吸引鸭子。|近义词：birdcatcher, huntsman|反义词:game warden, conservationist|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English fowler, from Old English fuglere, from fugol (fowl) + -ere (suffix denoting an agent). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *fuglaz.
