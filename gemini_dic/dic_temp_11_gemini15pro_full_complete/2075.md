acyl:/ˈeɪsɪl/ | adj. n.| 酰基 | An acyl group is a functional group derived from a carboxylic acid by the removal of the hydroxyl group (-OH).  It's represented by the general formula RCO-, where R represents an alkyl or aryl group.|  Acetyl chloride is a common acylating agent. 氯乙酰是一种常见的酰化剂。|  近义词: carbonyl, carboxyl | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective, noun


Etymology->
From  acetic + -yl.  Ultimately from Latin *acetum "vinegar"
