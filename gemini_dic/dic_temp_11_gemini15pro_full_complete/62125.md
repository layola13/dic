enchylemma:/ɛnˈkaɪlɛmə/ | n. |乳糜管内膜|The thin membrane lining the lymphatic vessels of the small intestine that absorb fats. |  Chylomicrons are formed within the intestinal cells and then enter the enchylemma. 乳糜微粒在肠细胞内形成，然后进入乳糜管内膜。| 近义词: endothelium (in a broader sense) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | noun
