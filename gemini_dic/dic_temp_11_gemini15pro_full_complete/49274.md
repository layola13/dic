deliberates:/dɪˈlɪbəreɪts/
|v.|仔细考虑，商议|Thinks about or discusses something carefully.|The jury deliberated for three days before reaching a verdict. 陪审团商议了三天，才作出裁决。|consider, contemplate, ponder, discuss|rush, hasten
词性:动词

deliberate:/dɪˈlɪbəreɪt/
|adj.|故意的，深思熟虑的|Done consciously and intentionally; carefully thought out.|The attack was a deliberate act of terrorism. 这次袭击是一次蓄意的恐怖主义行为。|intentional, purposeful, conscious|accidental, unintentional
词性:形容词


词源: From Latin  *dēlīberāre*  "to weigh in one's mind, consider," from *de-* "thoroughly" + *lībrare* "to weigh,"  from *lībra* "a balance, scales."
