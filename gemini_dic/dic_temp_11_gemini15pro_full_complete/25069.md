bub:/bʌb/| n.|哥们儿，伙计，小子 (亲昵的称呼)|A term of endearment, often used for a young boy or close friend.  It can also be a shortened form of "buddy".|Example: Hey there, bub, how's it going?  嘿，哥们儿，最近怎么样？ |Example:  That little bub is growing up so fast! 那小子长得真快！|Synonym: buddy, pal, dude, fella, chap|Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a term of endearment)|Noun
