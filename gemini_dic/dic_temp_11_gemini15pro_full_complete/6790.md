anaphroditic:/ˌænæfrəˈdɪtɪk/ | adj. | 性冷淡的，无性欲的 | Lacking sexual desire or interest; asexual.  Relating to or characteristic of anaphrodisia. |  Some medications can have anaphroditic side effects.  一些药物可能有性冷淡的副作用。 | The patient reported experiencing anaphroditic symptoms after starting the new medication.  病人报告说开始服用新药后出现了性冷淡的症状。 | 近义词: asexual, non-libidinous, frigid, impotent | 反义词: libidinous, lustful, passionate | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀναφροδισία (anaphrodisía, “lack of sexual desire”), from ἀν- (an-, “without”) +  Ἀφροδίτη (Aphrodítē, “Aphrodite, goddess of love”).
