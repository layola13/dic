ecclesiologist:/ɪˌkliːziˈɒlədʒɪst/
n.
教会学家，教堂建筑学家
A specialist in the study of church buildings and their history.
The ecclesiologist meticulously documented the architectural details of medieval cathedrals.  教会学家一丝不苟地记录了中世纪大教堂的建筑细节。
The noted ecclesiologist gave a lecture on the symbolism of stained glass windows.  这位著名的教会学家做了一场关于彩色玻璃窗象征意义的讲座。
Synonym: church historian, architectural historian
Antonym: (No direct antonym, but could consider someone who destroys or disregards churches)
