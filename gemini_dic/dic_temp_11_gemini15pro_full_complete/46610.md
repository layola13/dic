damewort:/ˈdeɪmwɜːrt/ | n. | 母菊, 戴菊 | A Eurasian plant (Matricaria recutita syn. Chrysanthemum parthenium) of the daisy family, having strongly scented foliage and small white-rayed flower heads, formerly used medicinally. Also called feverfew. |  The damewort was once believed to have magical properties.  戴菊曾经被认为具有神奇的功效。 | synonyms: feverfew, featherfew,  matricaria | antonyms: N/A | noun

damewort:/ˈdeɪmwɜːrt/ | n. | 壮阳草 |  A plant believed to have aphrodisiac properties. This meaning is archaic and less common. | In ancient folklore, damewort was sometimes used as a love potion ingredient.  在古代民间传说中，壮阳草有时被用作爱情魔药的成分。| synonyms:  aphrodisiac (in this context, though not a plant name) | antonyms:  N/A | noun
