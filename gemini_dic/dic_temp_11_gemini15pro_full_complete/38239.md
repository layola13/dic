computerising:/kəmˈpjuːtəraɪzɪŋ/
verb
计算机化
The process of equipping a system or organisation with computers, especially to automate operations.
Many companies are computerising their accounts departments. 许多公司正在将其会计部门计算机化。
The library is in the process of computerising its catalogue. 图书馆正在将其目录计算机化。
近义词: automating, digitizing, electronicizing
反义词: manual
