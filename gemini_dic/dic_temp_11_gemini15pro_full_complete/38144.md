compressibility:/kəmˌprɛsɪˈbɪlɪti/
n.|可压缩性|The quality of being compressible; the extent to which something can be compressed.|The compressibility of air allows it to be stored in tanks for scuba diving. 空气的可压缩性使其能够存储在水肺潜水罐中。|compactability, density|incompressibility, rigidity|noun


compress:/kəmˈprɛs/
v.|压缩，压紧|To press or squeeze something together so that it becomes smaller.|He compressed the files into a zip folder. 他将文件压缩成一个zip文件夹。|squeeze, condense, compact|expand, enlarge, loosen|verb


compressed:/kəmˈprɛst/
adj.|压缩的|Reduced in volume by pressure.|Compressed air is used in many industrial applications. 压缩空气用于许多工业应用。|compact, condensed, squeezed|expanded, enlarged, loose|adjective
