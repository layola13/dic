lepal: /ˈsiːpəl/ | n. | 萼片 | A leaf-like structure that forms part of the calyx of a flower, enclosing the petals and other parts before the flower opens. | The sepals of the rose were a deep crimson. 玫瑰的萼片是深红色的。| The lepal protected the developing bud. 萼片保护着发育中的花蕾。| synonym: calyx segment | antonym: petal | noun
