binoxalate:/baɪˈnɒksəleɪt/
n.
草酸氢盐；草酸氢根
a salt of oxalic acid containing the anion  HC₂O₄⁻ or the anion C₂O₄²⁻
Potassium binoxalate is used in cleaning products. 草酸氢钾用于清洁产品中。

近义词:hydrogen oxalate
反义词: (No direct antonym for a chemical compound)
词性:noun
