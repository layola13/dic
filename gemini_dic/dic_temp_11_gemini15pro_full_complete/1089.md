acceptingnesses:/əkˈsɛptɪŋnɪsɪz/
n.
接纳; 接受; 包容; 宽容
The state or quality of being accepting; willingness to tolerate or embrace different perspectives, behaviors, or situations.
Her acceptingness of his flaws was remarkable. 她对他的缺点的包容令人钦佩。
近义词:tolerance, acceptance, open-mindedness, receptiveness
反义词:intolerance, rejection, narrow-mindedness, resistance
词性:名词

词源:  From "accepting" + "-ness."  "Accepting" comes from the Latin "acceptare" (to receive willingly).  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
