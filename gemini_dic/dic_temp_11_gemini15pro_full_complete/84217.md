half-rueful:/ˌhɑːfˈruːfəl/
adjective
半懊悔的，略带悔意的
Feeling or expressing slight regret or remorse, often tinged with amusement or irony.
He gave a half-rueful, half-amused smile. 他露出了半懊悔半 amused 的微笑。
Near Synonyms:  slightly regretful, faintly remorseful, apologetic, sheepish, contrite
Near Antonyms:  unrepentant, remorseless, unapologetic, shameless


ETYMOLOGY->
From "half" + "rueful".  "Half" is from Old English *healfh. "Rueful" is from Middle English reufull, from reuwen "to rue," from Old English hreowan "to feel sorrow or regret."
