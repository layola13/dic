cross-pollinated:/krɒsˈpɒləˌneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 异花授粉的 | Fertilized by pollen from a different plant of the same species, or from a plant of a different species. | The apple trees were cross-pollinated with a variety of crabapple. 这些苹果树与各种海棠杂交授粉。|  The farmer cross-pollinated the corn with a new strain. 农民用新品种给玉米进行异花授粉。 | Synonyms: hybridized, crossbred, interbred | Antonyms: self-pollinated, inbred | adjective


cross-pollinate:/krɒsˈpɒləˌneɪt/ | v. | 异花授粉 | To fertilize a plant with pollen from a different plant of the same species, or from a plant of a different species. | Bees cross-pollinate flowers as they collect nectar. 蜜蜂在采集花蜜时会给花进行异花授粉。| The gardener cross-pollinated the roses to create a new variety. 园丁对玫瑰进行异花授粉以创造新品种。 | Synonyms: hybridize, crossbreed, interbreed | Antonyms: self-pollinate, inbreed | verb

Etymology:  From "cross-" + "pollinate". "Cross-" signifies "across" or "between", and "pollinate" refers to the transfer of pollen.  因此，“cross-pollinate”表示花粉在不同植物之间的转移。
