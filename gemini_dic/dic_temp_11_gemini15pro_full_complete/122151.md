mistrials:/ˌmɪsˈtraɪəlz/
n.
无效审判；流审
A trial that is not considered valid because of a mistake in the proceedings.
The judge declared a mistrial after the jury could not reach a verdict. 法官因陪审团无法达成裁决而宣布审判无效。
The case ended in a mistrial due to juror misconduct.  由于陪审员行为不当，案件以无效审判告终。
synonyms: aborted trial, invalid trial, hung jury
antonyms: valid trial, successful trial
noun
