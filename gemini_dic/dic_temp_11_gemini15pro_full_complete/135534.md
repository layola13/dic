nonpliancy:/nɒnˈplaɪənsi/
n.|不顺从，不服从；顽固|The nonpliancy of the material made it difficult to work with. 这种材料不服帖，很难加工。|English Explanation:  Lack of pliancy; inflexibility; refusal to yield or comply.|例句：His nonpliancy ultimately cost him his job. 他的不服从最终让他丢了工作。

例句：The government was frustrated by the nonpliancy of the rebels. 政府对叛军的顽固感到沮丧。

近义词：inflexibility, rigidity, stubbornness, obstinacy, recalcitrance
反义词：pliancy, flexibility, compliance, obedience, docility
词性: noun
