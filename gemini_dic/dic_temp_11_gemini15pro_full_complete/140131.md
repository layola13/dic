off-the-ground:/ˌɒf ðə ˈɡraʊnd/ /ˌɔːf ðə ˈɡraʊnd/
adjective
成功的；运转正常的；进行中的
Started and making progress; operating successfully.
The project finally got off the ground after months of planning.  这个项目在数月的规划后终于启动了。
The business is off the ground and making a profit.  生意已经步入正轨，并且开始盈利了。
Synonym: underway, launched, operational, active, functioning, running, in progress, in motion
Antonym: stalled, grounded, failed, inactive, defunct, stagnant
词性: 形容词
