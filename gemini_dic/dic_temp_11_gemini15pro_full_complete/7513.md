anneal:/əˈniːl/| vt. |使退火，使韧化；使变软|To heat (metal or glass) and allow it to cool slowly, in order to remove internal stresses and toughen it. |The glass is annealed to make it stronger.  玻璃经过退火处理以增强强度。

The metal was annealed before being shaped.  金属在成形前进行了退火处理。|近义词：temper, toughen, harden|反义词: weaken, soften|词性: verb
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *anælan, from *an- "on" + *ælan "to burn, bake," related to æled "fire."  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *ailan "to burn."
