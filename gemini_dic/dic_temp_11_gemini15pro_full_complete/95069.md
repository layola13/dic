inadvertency:/ˌɪnədˈvɜːrtnsi/ | n. |疏忽，无心之过|An oversight or unintentional act or omission. |  I deleted the file by inadvertency. 我无意中删除了该文件。| Synonyms: oversight, blunder, slip, lapse, error, mistake | Antonyms: care, attention, deliberation, mindfulness | noun

inadvertent:/ˌɪnədˈvɜːrtnt/ | adj. |非故意的，无心的|Not resulting from or achieved through deliberate planning. |  An inadvertent remark caused offense. 一句无心的话引起了冒犯。| Synonyms: unintentional, accidental, unintended, unplanned, unpremeditated | Antonyms: deliberate, intentional, planned, premeditated | adjective
