mongrelly: /ˈmʌŋɡrəli/ /ˈmɑːŋɡrəli/
| adj. | 杂种的，混血的；卑劣的，低劣的 | of mixed breed or origin; of low quality or character; base | The mongrelly dog was a mix of several breeds. 这只杂种狗是几个品种的混合。| The politician's mongrelly tactics disgusted many voters.  这位政客卑劣的策略令许多选民感到厌恶。 | 近义词: hybrid, mixed, base, vile, despicable | 反义词: purebred, noble, virtuous | 词性: 形容词

mongrel: /ˈmʌŋɡrəl/ /ˈmɑːŋɡrəl/
| n. | 杂种，混血儿 | an animal, especially a dog, of mixed breed |  The little mongrel was full of energy. 这只小杂种狗精力充沛。 | 近义词: hybrid, crossbreed | 反义词: purebred | 词性: 名词

词源：From Old French *mongrel, from *mordrelier "to bite, gnaw," from Latin mordere "to bite."  Originally referred to animals that bite or snap, then to dogs of mixed breed, and later extended to anything of mixed origin or low quality.
