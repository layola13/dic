orichalcum:/ˌɔːrɪˈkælkəm/ | n. | 奥利哈钢，山铜 | A legendary metal, mentioned in ancient writings, often identified with brass or a similar alloy.  It is described as a reddish or gold-colored metal, valued highly and sometimes considered more precious than gold. In some contexts, it is associated with Atlantis. | The ancient texts describe the palace of Atlantis as being adorned with orichalcum. 古代文献描述亚特兰蒂斯的宫殿装饰着奥利哈钢。

The orichalcum shimmered in the sunlight, its brilliance surpassing even that of gold.  阳光下，山铜闪闪发光，其光彩甚至超过了黄金。 | 近义词: brass, bronze | 反义词: None (as it is a legendary metal) | 词性: noun
