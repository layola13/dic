coinciding: /koʊɪnˈsaɪdɪŋ/ | adj. | 一致的，同时发生的 | happening at the same time; corresponding exactly | The two events were coinciding, adding to the drama of the day.  这两个事件同时发生，增加了当天的戏剧性。 | synonyms: concurrent, simultaneous, synchronous | antonyms: asynchronous, non-concurrent, separate | adjective

coincidence: /koʊˈɪnsɪdəns/ | n. | 巧合，一致 | a remarkable concurrence of events or circumstances without apparent causal connection | It was pure coincidence that we met at the airport. 我们在机场相遇纯属巧合。 | synonyms: accident, chance, fluke | antonyms: intention, plan | noun

coincide: /ˌkoʊɪnˈsaɪd/ | v. |  一致，同时发生 | occur at or during the same time |  My birthday coincides with Christmas. 我的生日和圣诞节是同一天。 | synonyms: concur, correspond, synchronize | antonyms: differ, diverge | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *coincidere*, from co- "together" + incidere "to fall into, happen".
