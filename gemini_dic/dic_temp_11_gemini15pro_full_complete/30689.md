certifiable:/ˈsɜːrtɪfaɪəbl/ /ˈsɝːtəˌfaɪəbəl/| adj.|可证明的；可确认的；（非正式）疯狂的；精神错乱的|Officially recognized as such or meeting certain standards; (informal) insane or crazy.|例句：He's a certifiable genius. 他是个名副其实的天才。

例句：His behavior was so bizarre it was almost certifiable. 他的行为如此怪异，几乎可以认定他疯了。|近义词：proven, demonstrable, verifiable, authentic, genuine; crazy, insane, mad, lunatic|反义词：unproven, questionable; sane, rational|词性:形容词
词源: From certify + -able. Certify comes from Late Latin certificare "to make certain," from Latin certus "settled, sure" + facere "to make."
