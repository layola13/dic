hogan:/ˈhoʊɡɑːn/ |n.|霍根（纳瓦霍人的传统民居）|A hogan is a traditional Navajo dwelling.|Example 1: The family gathered in their hogan for a ceremony.  这家人聚集在他们的霍根里举行仪式。

Example 2:  Many hogans are still built using traditional methods. 许多霍根仍然使用传统方法建造。|Synonym: dwelling, house, home|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "modern building")|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Navajo *hooghan*, meaning "home," "house," or "place of dwelling."
