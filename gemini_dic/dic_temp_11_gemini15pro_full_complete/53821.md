disconnecters:/ˌdɪskəˈnɛktərz/
|n.|断路器，隔离开关|A device used to isolate a part of an electrical circuit.|The maintenance crew used disconnecters to isolate the faulty section of the power line. 维修人员使用隔离开关隔离了电力线故障部分。|
|近义词：isolators, switches|反义词：connectors|词性：名词|

disconnecters:/ˌdɪskəˈnɛktərz/
|n.|断开连接的人或物|People or things that cause disconnection or separation.|Social media can be a disconnector of real-life relationships. 社交媒体有时会断开现实生活中的关系。|
|近义词：separators, dividers|反义词：connectors, uniters|词性：名词|
