dialogging:/ˈdaɪəˌlɒɡɪŋ/ (US) /ˈdaɪəˌlɒɡɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|对话; 对话交流|The process of engaging in a dialogue or conversation.  The act of communicating with others through spoken or written exchange.|They spent hours dialogging about their future plans. 他们花了几个小时讨论他们的未来计划。|synonyms: conversing, communicating, talking, discussing|antonyms: silence, monologue|noun
