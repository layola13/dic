hellos:/həˈloʊz/ /hɛˈləʊz/ | n. | 你好（复数，非正式） |  Plural form of "hello," sometimes used informally.  Often used playfully or when addressing multiple people at once, though "hello" itself can be used for both singular and plural. | Hello everyone! 大家好！| Hellos from everyone back home!  来自家乡所有人的问候！| 近义词：greetings, hi (plural), hey (plural) | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a greeting) | noun
