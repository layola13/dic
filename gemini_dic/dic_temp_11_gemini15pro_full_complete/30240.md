cellulated:/ˈsɛljʊˌleɪtɪd/
adjective
细胞状的；蜂窝状的
Composed of small compartments or cells.  Having a cellular structure.
The artist created a cellulated sculpture, reminiscent of a honeycomb.  这位艺术家创作了一个蜂窝状的雕塑，让人联想起蜂窝。
The microscopic image revealed a cellulated pattern in the tissue sample. 显微图像显示组织样本中呈蜂窝状。
近义词: cellular, alveolate, honeycombed
反义词: noncellular, acellular
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *cellulatus, from cella (“cell”) + -atus (suffix forming adjectives).
