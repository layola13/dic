jubilee:/ˈdʒuːbɪliː/
n.
禧年，周年纪念，欢乐的节日
A period of celebration or rejoicing, especially one marking a special anniversary.
The town celebrated its bicentennial jubilee with a parade and fireworks.  这个小镇以游行和烟火庆祝其二百周年纪念。
The Queen's Golden Jubilee was marked with nationwide celebrations. 女王的登基五十周年庆典举国欢腾。
celebration, anniversary, festival
hardship, sorrow
noun
