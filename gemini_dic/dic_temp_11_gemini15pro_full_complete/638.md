/əˈbriːvieɪt/ | vt. | 缩写，简写 | Shorten (a word, phrase, or text). | Example:  "Street" is often abbreviated to "St." “Street”通常缩写为“St.” | Example:  The word "corporation" can be abbreviated to "corp."  “Corporation”可以缩写为“corp.” | Synonyms: shorten, condense, curtail | Antonyms: expand, lengthen | Verb
