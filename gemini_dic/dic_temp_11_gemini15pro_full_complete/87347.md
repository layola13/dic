heroical:/hɪˈrəʊɪk(ə)l/
adjective
英雄的；英勇的
Suitable for or characteristic of a hero; exhibiting or marked by courage and daring.
例句：He told a heroical tale of adventure. 他讲述了一个英勇的冒险故事。

例句：Her heroical actions saved many lives. 她英勇的行为拯救了许多生命。

近义词：heroic, valiant, brave, courageous, daring, bold
反义词：cowardly, timid, fearful
词性：adjective


词源：
Late Middle English: from late Latin heroicalis, from Latin heroicus (see heroic).
