denominationalist:/dɪˌnɒmɪˈneɪʃənəlɪst/
n.
宗派主义者；教派主义者
A person who strongly supports the beliefs and practices of a particular religious denomination and often opposes cooperation with other denominations.
Denominationalists often prioritize their own sect's doctrines over broader Christian unity.  宗派主义者往往将自己教派的教义置于更广泛的基督教团结之上。
The denominationalist refused to participate in the interfaith service.  这位宗派主义者拒绝参加跨宗教的礼拜。
近义词:sectarian, partisan
反义词:ecumenist, non-denominational
词性:noun
