knowingnesses:/ˈnoʊɪŋnəsəz/
n.
认知, 知识, 见识 (plural)
States or qualities of being knowledgeable; awareness or understanding of something.
例句：His knowingnesses in various fields impressed everyone. 他在各个领域的见识给每个人都留下了深刻印象。

例句：She demonstrated surprising knowingnesses about ancient history. 她展示了对古代历史令人惊讶的知识。

近义词：knowledge, awareness, understanding, wisdom, expertise, insight
反义词：ignorance, unawareness, naivety
词性：名词(plural)

词源: From "knowingness," derived from "knowing" (present participle of "know"), with the suffix "-ness" indicating a state or quality. "Know" traces back to Old English "cnāwan."
