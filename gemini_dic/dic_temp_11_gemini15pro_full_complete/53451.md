disapprovable:/ˌdɪsəˈpruːvəbl/ | adj. | 可非难的，不值得赞同的 | Something that is disapprovable is considered to be wrong or bad and deserving of disapproval.  | Her behavior was highly disapprovable. 她的行为非常不值得赞同。 | The committee deemed the proposal disapprovable due to its lack of feasibility. 委员会认为该提案因缺乏可行性而不值得赞同。 | 近义词: objectionable, reprehensible, censurable | 反义词: commendable, laudable, praiseworthy | 词性: adjective

disapprove:/ˌdɪsəˈpruːv/ | v. | 不赞成，反对 | To have or express an unfavorable opinion of. |  I disapprove of his methods. 我不赞成他的方法。 |  She disapproved of the way he treated his staff. 她不赞成他对待员工的方式。 | 近义词: object to, deprecate, condemn | 反义词: approve, endorse, commend | 词性: verb
