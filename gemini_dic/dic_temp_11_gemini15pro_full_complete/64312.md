equivocatingly:/ɪˈkwɪvəkeɪtɪŋli/
adv.
含糊其辞地，模棱两可地
in a way that is equivocating; using unclear language to avoid committing oneself or giving a direct answer
He answered equivocatingly, hoping to avoid a fight. 他含糊其辞地回答，希望避免争吵。
equivocally, ambiguously, evasively
directly, frankly, straightforwardly
词性: 副词
