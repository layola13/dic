aramean:/ˌærəˈmiːən/ | adj. n.| 亚拉姆的，亚拉姆人 |Relating to Aram, an ancient region in southwestern Asia, or its language or people.  A native or inhabitant of Aram. |例句：Aramaean was the lingua franca of the Near East for centuries.  亚拉姆语几个世纪以来一直是近东地区的通用语言。

例句：He studied ancient Aramaean inscriptions. 他研究古代亚拉姆铭文。 |近义词：Syrian (in some contexts), Semitic |反义词: (No direct antonym, could be non-Aramaean, non-Semitic) |词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From Latin *Aramaeus*, from Greek *Ἀραμαῖος (Aramaîos)*, ultimately from Akkadian *Aramu*.
