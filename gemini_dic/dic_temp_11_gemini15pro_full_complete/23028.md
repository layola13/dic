boule:/buːl/| n.|法式圆面包，滚球，金属球|A round loaf of bread, typically French. Also refers to a metal ball used in the game of pétanque.|例句：The bakery is known for its crusty boules. 这家面包店以其硬皮圆面包而闻名。
