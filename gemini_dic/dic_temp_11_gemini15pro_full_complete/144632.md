outwitted:/ˌaʊtˈwɪtɪd/
verb
以智取胜，智胜
defeated by using greater intelligence or cunning.
The chess player outwitted his opponent with a clever move.  这位棋手以一个巧妙的棋步智胜了对手。
outmaneuver, outsmart, outfox
be outwitted by
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From outwit + -ed.  From out- + wit.
