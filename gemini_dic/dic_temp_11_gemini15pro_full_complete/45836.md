customizers:/ˈkʌstəˌmaɪzərz/
n.
定制者，自定义者
People or companies who customize products or services to meet individual needs.
Many car manufacturers offer online tools for customizers to design their perfect vehicle. 许多汽车制造商都提供在线工具，供定制者设计他们理想的车辆。
The website allows customizers to personalize their experience. 该网站允许定制者个性化他们的体验。
