multigenerational:/ˌmʌltiˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃənəl/
adjective
多代的；几代人的
Spanning or encompassing several generations.
例句：
The family reunion was a multigenerational affair, with attendees ranging from great-grandparents to toddlers.
家庭聚会是一个多代人的盛事，从曾祖父母到蹒跚学步的孩子都有参加。

例句：
Multigenerational households are becoming increasingly common due to economic pressures and changing social norms.
由于经济压力和社会规范的变化，多代同堂的家庭越来越普遍。

近义词：intergenerational, cross-generational
反义词：single-generation
词性: adjective
