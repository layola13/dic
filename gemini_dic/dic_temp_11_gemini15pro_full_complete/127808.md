neither-fish-nor-fowl:/ˈnaɪðər fɪʃ nɔːr faʊl/ | adj. | 不伦不类；非驴非马；四不像 | Something that is neither fish nor fowl does not fit easily into any category and is therefore difficult to define or deal with. |  This new type of vehicle is neither fish nor fowl – it's too big to be a car, but too small to be a van. 这种新型车辆不伦不类——它太大而不能算作汽车，但又太小而不能算作货车。|  ambiguous, anomalous, indeterminate, nondescript, unclassifiable | clear-cut, definite, distinct, specific, typical | adjective

neither-fish-nor-fowl:/ˈnaɪðər fɪʃ nɔːr faʊl/ | n. | 不伦不类的人或事物 | A person or thing that does not fit easily into any category and is therefore difficult to define or deal with. | The committee's proposal is neither fish nor fowl – it doesn't address the real problems, but it's not completely useless either. 委员会的提案不伦不类，既没有解决实际问题，但也不是完全没用。|  oddity, anomaly, misfit |  conformist, archetype, prototype | noun
