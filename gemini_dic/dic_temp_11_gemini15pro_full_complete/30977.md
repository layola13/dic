chalcographist: /ˌkælkəˈɡræfɪst/ | n. | 铜版雕刻师 | An engraver, especially one who engraves on copper or other metal plates for printing. |例句：The chalcographist meticulously etched the design onto the copper plate. 铜版雕刻师一丝不苟地将图案蚀刻到铜板上。

例句：The museum exhibited a collection of prints by a renowned 18th-century chalcographist.  博物馆展出了一位著名的18世纪铜版雕刻师的版画收藏。|近义词：engraver, etcher |反义词: None |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *chalcographe*, from Greek *khalkographos*, from *khalkos* "copper" + *graphein* "to write".
