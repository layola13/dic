decoyer:/dɪˈkɔɪər/ | n. |诱饵者，诱捕者|Someone or something that decoys or lures another into a trap or dangerous situation. | The hunters used a mechanical decoyer to attract ducks. 猎人使用机械诱饵来吸引鸭子。| 近义词: lure, bait, trap, ensnare | 反义词: rescuer, savior | noun

词源:
From the verb "decoy," which comes from the Old French word "decoi" meaning "a lure or bait."  Ultimately, it traces back to the Vulgar Latin "*de- + cauda" (tail), suggesting the original meaning referred to a tail used as a lure in hunting.
