emeu:/ˈiːmjuː/ (US), /ˈiːmjuː/ (UK)| n.|鸸鹋|A large, flightless Australian bird (Dromaius novaehollandiae) similar to an ostrich, but smaller and with three toes on each foot. It is the second-largest living bird by height, after its ratite relative, the ostrich.|例句：The emeu is native to Australia. 鸸鹋原产于澳大利亚。|例句：We saw a family of emeus while driving through the outback. 我们开车穿过内陆时看到了一群鸸鹋。|近义词：ostrich, rhea, cassowary|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific animal)|词性:noun
