cold-store:/ˈkəʊld stɔːr/ (British) /ˈkoʊld stɔːr/ (American)| n.|冷藏库，冷库|A building or room kept cold for storing food or other perishable goods.|The supermarket chain has a large cold-store for its frozen products. 超市连锁店有一个大型冷库用于存放冷冻产品。|Synonyms: cold storage, freezer, refrigerator|Antonyms: oven, furnace|noun

cold-store:/ˈkəʊld stɔːr/ (British) /ˈkoʊld stɔːr/ (American)| v.|冷藏，冷冻|To store something in a cold-store.|They cold-store the meat to keep it fresh. 他们把肉冷藏以保鲜。|Synonyms: refrigerate, freeze|Antonyms: heat, warm|verb
