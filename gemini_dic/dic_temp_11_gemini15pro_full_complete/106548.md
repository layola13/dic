kronen:/ˈkrəʊnən/| n. |克朗（捷克、丹麦、挪威、瑞典等国的货币单位）|The plural of krone, the basic monetary unit of several countries, including Denmark, Norway, and Sweden.  It is also formerly used in Austria-Hungary, Czechoslovakia, Estonia, Iceland. |He paid 100 kronen for the book. 他花了100克朗买了这本书。|近义词：currency, money|反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of currency)|noun
