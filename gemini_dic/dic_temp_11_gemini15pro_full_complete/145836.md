overdelicious:/ˌōvər dɪˈlɪʃəs/
adjective
极其美味的；非常可口的
Exceedingly delicious; more than just delicious.
The chocolate cake was overdelicious; I couldn't stop eating it.  这块巧克力蛋糕极其美味；我停不下来。
This freshly baked bread is overdelicious with butter.  这块刚出炉的面包配黄油非常可口。
近义词:scrumptious, delectable, exquisite, heavenly
反义词:disgusting, unappetizing, revolting, nasty
词性:adjective

词源:
"Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively, too much") + "delicious" (from Old French "delicieux," meaning "delightful, delicate")
