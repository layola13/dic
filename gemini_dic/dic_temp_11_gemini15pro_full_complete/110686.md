lineage:/ˈlɪniɪdʒ/| n.|血统，世系，家系|A sequence of parents and offspring or a continuous line of descent; ancestry.|She's very proud of her royal lineage. 她对她的皇室血统感到非常自豪。|The lineage of the royal family can be traced back centuries. 这个皇室家族的血统可以追溯到几个世纪以前。|近义词：ancestry, descent, genealogy, pedigree, heritage|反义词: n/a|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *ligneage*, from ligne ("line"), from Latin līnea ("line, string").
