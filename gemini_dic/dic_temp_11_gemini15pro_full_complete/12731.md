assetless:/ˈæsɛtlɪs/
adjective
无资产的；没有财产的
Having no assets; lacking possessions of value.
The assetless company was forced to declare bankruptcy.  这家没有资产的公司被迫宣布破产。
The young man found himself assetless after a series of bad investments.  这个年轻人在一系列糟糕的投资之后发现自己一无所有。
synonyms: impecunious, impoverished, indigent, destitute, penniless
antonyms: wealthy, affluent, rich, prosperous
word type: adjective
