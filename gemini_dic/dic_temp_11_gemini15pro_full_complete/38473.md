conchie:/ˈkɒntʃi/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntʃi/ (US)| n.|拒服兵役者，尤其指出于宗教或道德原因者|A person who refuses to perform military service, especially on religious or moral grounds.|"He was branded a conchie and ostracized by the community." 他被贴上拒服兵役者的标签，并被社区排斥。|Synonyms: conscientious objector, pacifist, peacemaker|Antonyms: warmonger, hawk, soldier|noun

conchie:/ˈkɒntʃi/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntʃi/ (US)| n.|贝壳，海螺|Seashell, conch|"He found a beautiful conchie on the beach." 他在沙滩上发现了一个漂亮的海螺。|Synonyms: shell, conch|Antonyms: None|noun
