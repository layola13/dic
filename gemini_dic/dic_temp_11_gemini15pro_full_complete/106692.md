kwh:/ˈkeɪˌdʌbəl.juːˈeɪtʃ/ | n. | 千瓦时 | A unit of energy equal to one kilowatt of power used for one hour.  It is commonly used to measure electricity consumption. | Our household uses about 500 kWh of electricity per month. 我们家每月用电量约为 500 千瓦时。| synonym: kilowatt-hour | antonym: N/A | noun
