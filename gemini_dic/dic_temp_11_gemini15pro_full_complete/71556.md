flashboard:/ˈflæʃˌbɔːrd/ | n. | 水闸的活动挡板; 闪光信号装置 | A board or a series of boards placed on the top of a dam or weir to regulate the flow and level of water.  A device that produces flashes of light for signaling. | The flashboard helped control the water level during the flood.  水闸的活动挡板在洪水期间帮助控制了水位。 | The photographer used a flashboard to illuminate the dark scene. 摄影师使用闪光信号装置照亮了黑暗的场景。 | synonyms: spillway, weir, flash lamp, strobe light | antonyms: dam, levee | noun
