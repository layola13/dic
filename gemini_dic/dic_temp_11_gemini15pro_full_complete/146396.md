overfluent:/ˌoʊvərˈfluːənt/ | adj. | 过于流利的，说话太快的 | Excessively fluent; speaking too rapidly and smoothly, often at the expense of clarity or content. | He was so overfluent that it was hard to understand his point. 他说话太快太流利了，以至于很难理解他的意思。| The presenter's overfluent delivery made it difficult for the audience to absorb the information. 演讲者过于流利的表达方式使观众难以吸收信息。 | 近义词: glib, voluble, verbose | 反义词: hesitant, halting, inarticulate |词性: 形容词
