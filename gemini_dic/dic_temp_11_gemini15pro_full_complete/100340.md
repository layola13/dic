intersqueeze:/ˌɪntərˈskwiːz/
verb
插入挤压;  
To squeeze or insert between two other things.
He intersqueezed the book between two larger volumes on the shelf. 他把那本书挤插在书架上两本更大的书之间。

verb
(比喻) 挤出(时间);  
To manage to fit something in between other activities or commitments.
She intersqueezed a quick visit to her parents between meetings.  她在会议间隙挤出时间去看望了父母。

近义词:insert, squeeze, wedge, cram, fit in
反义词:remove, extract, take out
词性:v.
