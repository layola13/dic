deceptory:/dɪˈsɛptəri/
adjective
欺骗性的; 旨在欺骗的
intended to deceive; misleading.
Her deceptory smile hid her true intentions. 她那欺骗性的笑容掩盖了她真实的意图。
近义词:misleading, deceptive, fallacious, fraudulent
反义词:honest, truthful, genuine, sincere
词性:adjective

词源:  From Latin *dēceptōrius, from dēcipere (“to deceive”).  Ultimately from de- (“down, away”) + capere (“to take”).
