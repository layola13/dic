mocking:/ˈmɒkɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈmɑːkɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|嘲笑的，嘲弄的|Making fun of someone or something in a cruel or harsh way.  It can also describe something that appears to mimic or imitate something else in a way that suggests ridicule.|He gave a mocking laugh. 他发出一声嘲笑。|Synonyms: derisive, scornful, sarcastic, sardonic|Antonyms: respectful, admiring, complimentary|adjective


mocking:/ˈmɒkɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈmɑːkɪŋ/ (US)| n.|嘲笑，嘲弄|The act of mocking someone or something.|The mocking of his efforts made him angry. 对他努力的嘲笑使他生气。|Synonyms: derision, scorn, ridicule|Antonyms: praise, admiration, respect|noun
