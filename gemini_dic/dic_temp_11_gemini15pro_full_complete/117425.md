medievals:/ˌmediˈiːvəlz/ | adj. n. | 中世纪的；中世纪的人或事物 | Relating to the Middle Ages (roughly AD 500-1500); people or things from the Middle Ages | The castle's architecture was distinctly medieval. 这座城堡的建筑风格明显是中世纪的。 |  Medieval manuscripts are fascinating historical sources. 中世纪的手稿是引人入胜的历史资料。| 近义词: ancient, archaic | 反义词: modern, contemporary | 词性: 形容词/名词

词源: From Old French *medieval* (modern French *médiéval*), from Latin *medius* (“middle”) + *aevum* (“age”).
