album: /ˈælbəm/ (US), /ˈælbəm/ (UK) | n. |相册，专辑 | A blank book for the insertion of photographs, stamps, or pictures, or a bound collection of such items; a long-playing record. | I keep all my travel photos in an album.  我把所有的旅行照片都放在相册里。| Synonyms: collection, compilation, record, anthology | Antonyms: single, individual | noun
