musks:/mʌsks/| n.|麝香；麝香香水；类似麝香的气味|A strong-smelling reddish-brown substance which is produced by a male deer (= a type of animal with horns) and used in perfumes. Also, perfumes that smell of musk, or any similar smell.|The air was thick with the scent of musks and incense. 空气中弥漫着麝香和熏香的气味。|Synonyms: fragrances, scents, aromas, perfumes|Antonyms: odor, stench|Noun

musks:/mʌsks/| v. (third-person singular present tense musks, present participle musking, simple past and past participle musked)|使充满麝香味；掩盖（不好的气味）|(often passive) To perfume or scent with musk; to cover up (a bad smell).|The room was musked with a heavy, sweet fragrance. 房间里充满了浓郁的甜香味。|Synonyms: perfume, scent, aromatize|Antonyms: deodorize|Verb
