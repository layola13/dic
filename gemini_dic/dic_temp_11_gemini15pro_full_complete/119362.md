microcomputer:/ˈmaɪkroʊkəmˌpjuːtər/| n.|微型计算机；微机|A small computer that contains a microprocessor as its central processing unit. |例句：I bought a new microcomputer for my home office. 我为我的家庭办公室买了一台新的微型计算机。

例句：Microcomputers revolutionized the way people work and communicate. 微型计算机彻底改变了人们工作和交流的方式。|近义词：PC, personal computer, desktop, laptop|反义词:mainframe, supercomputer|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From micro- + computer.  "Micro-" comes from the Greek "mikrós" meaning "small". "Computer" originally referred to a person who performed calculations, but evolved to mean a machine for performing calculations.
