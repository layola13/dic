featherlet:/ˈfeðərlɪt/| n. |小羽毛，绒羽|A small, delicate feather, often downy.  A tiny feather, especially one from a young bird. |The baby bird was covered in soft featherlets.  这只雏鸟身上覆盖着柔软的绒羽。

The pillow was filled with goose featherlets.  这个枕头里填充的是鹅绒。|近义词：down, plumelet, plumule|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider: quill, shaft - referring to the hard parts of a feather)|Noun
