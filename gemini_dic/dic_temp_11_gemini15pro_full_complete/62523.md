endurance:/ɪnˈdjʊərəns/ /ɪnˈdʊrəns/| n. |忍耐力，耐力，持久力|The ability to withstand hardship, adversity, or stress for a prolonged period.|She showed remarkable endurance throughout the race. 她整个比赛中都表现出了非凡的耐力。|近义词: stamina, resilience, fortitude, perseverance|反义词: weakness, fragility, frailty|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *enduraunce*, from Anglo-Norman *endurance*,  from Old French *endurance*, from endurer (to endure), from Latin *indūrāre*  "to harden, make hard, inure" from *in-* "in, into" + *dūrāre* "to harden, last, continue" from *dūrus* "hard".
