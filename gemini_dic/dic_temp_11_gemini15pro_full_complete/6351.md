amphiaster:/ˈæmfiˌæstər/| n.|双星体|A star-shaped structure formed during cell division, consisting of two asters connected by a spindle.  It is involved in the separation of chromosomes.|The amphiaster plays a crucial role in mitosis. 双星体在有丝分裂中起着至关重要的作用。|The amphiaster is visible under a microscope during cell division.  在细胞分裂过程中，可以在显微镜下观察到双星体。|synonyms:  mitotic spindle, spindle apparatus|antonyms: N/A|noun
