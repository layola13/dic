knock-downs: /ˈnɒkˌdaʊnz/ (British) /ˈnɑːkˌdaʊnz/ (American)
knock-downs: n.|击倒，击落，拆卸的部件|A knock-down is an instance of knocking someone or something down, especially in boxing.  It can also refer to something (like furniture) designed for easy assembly from prefabricated parts.|The boxer scored three knock-downs in the first round.  拳击手在第一回合击倒对手三次。|
knock-downs: |These knock-down shelves are easy to assemble. 这些易拆装的架子很容易组装。|Synonyms: knockouts, takedowns|Antonyms: victories, wins|Noun
