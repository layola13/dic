opalesce:/ˌoʊpəˈlɛs/ | v. | 发出乳白光；闪现乳白色 | To exhibit a play of colors like that of an opal; to become or appear milky or iridescent. | The stone opalesced with a fiery glow. 这块石头闪耀着火热的光芒。| synonyms: shimmer, glisten, gleam, iridescence | antonyms: dull, darken | verb


opalescence:/ˌoʊpəˈlɛsəns/ | n. | 乳白光；乳光 | The state or quality of exhibiting a play of colors like that of an opal. | The opalescence of the pearl was breathtaking. 珍珠的乳白色光彩夺目。| synonyms: shimmer, iridescence, gleam | antonyms: dullness, darkness | noun


opalescent:/ˌoʊpəˈlɛsənt/ | adj. | 乳白色的；闪现乳白色光的 | Exhibiting a play of colors like that of an opal. | The sky was an opalescent blue. 天空呈现乳白色蓝色。| synonyms: shimmering, iridescent, pearly | antonyms: dull, dark | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *opalescent*, from *opale* (opal) + *-escent* (suffix indicating becoming or beginning to be).  Ultimately from Sanskrit *upala* (precious stone, gem).
