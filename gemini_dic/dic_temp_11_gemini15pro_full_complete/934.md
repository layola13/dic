abyss's:/əˈbɪsɪz/| n.|深渊的；深不见底的|Something that belongs to or is associated with an abyss.  It refers to a possessive form of "abyss".|The abyss's depths are unknown.  这深渊的深度无人知晓。|近义词：chasm's, void's, gulf's|反义词:summit's, peak's|词性:noun (possessive)

abyss:/əˈbɪs/| n.|深渊，无底洞；地狱|A deep, immeasurable space, gulf, or cavity; vast chasm. Can also be used figuratively to describe a state of extreme misery or despair.|She stared into the abyss. 她凝视着深渊。|近义词：chasm, void, gulf|反义词:summit, peak|词性:noun
