intuitiveness:/ɪnˌtuːɪtɪvnəs/ | n. | 直觉性；直观性 | The quality of being easily understood or operated without explicit instruction.  | The intuitiveness of the software makes it easy to use.  这款软件的直观性使其易于使用。 | synonyms:  user-friendliness, simplicity, accessibility, clarity | antonyms: complexity, difficulty, obscurity | noun
