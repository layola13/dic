overmilking:/ˌoʊvərˈmɪlkɪŋ/| n. |过度挤奶；过度利用；榨取|The act of milking a cow or other animal excessively, or of exploiting something or someone beyond reasonable limits.|The overmilking of the company's resources led to its eventual downfall. 公司资源的过度利用导致了它最终的垮台。|Overmilking of the dairy cows resulted in a decrease in milk production. 对奶牛的过度挤奶导致了产奶量的下降。|近义词：overexploiting, overuse, abuse|反义词: underuse, conservation|noun
