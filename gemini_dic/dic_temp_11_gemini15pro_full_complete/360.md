abiding:/əˈbaɪdɪŋ/
adjective
持久的，永久的
lasting or continuing for a long time
例句：He has an abiding love for his hometown.  他对家乡有着永恒的爱。

例句：Her abiding faith helped her through difficult times. 她坚定的信念帮助她度过了难关。

近义词：enduring, lasting, permanent, persistent
反义词：temporary, transient, fleeting, ephemeral
词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English abiden, from Old English ābīdan (“to abide, wait, await, remain, continue, last, endure, experience”), from ā- (“on, in, at, to”) + bīdan (“to bide, wait, remain, continue, dwell, inhabit, occupy, possess”). Cognate with Old Frisian abīda (“to expect, hope for, wait”), Old Saxon abīdan, Old High German abītan (“to wait, remain”), Old Norse bíða (“to wait, await”).
