moblogger:/ˈmoʊblɒɡər/ (US) /ˈməʊblɒɡə(r)/ (UK)| n. |手机博客写手，移动博客作者|A person who creates and maintains a blog using a mobile device, such as a smartphone or tablet.|例句：The mobloggers posted live updates from the conference. 手机博客写手们在会议现场发布了实时更新。

|例句：Mobloggers often share photos and short videos of their daily lives. 手机博客写手经常分享他们日常生活的照片和短视频。|近义词：blogger, mobile journalist, citizen journalist|反义词: None|词性: Noun
