bedchamber: /ˈbɛdˌtʃeɪmbər/ (US) /ˈbɛdˌtʃeɪmbə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 卧室，寝室 | A room used primarily for sleeping. |  The king retired to his bedchamber. 国王退居到他的寝室。| Synonym: bedroom, chamber, sleeping room | Antonym: living room, dining room | Noun

bedchamber: /ˈbɛdˌtʃeɪmbər/ (US) /ˈbɛdˌtʃeɪmbə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 内室 | A private room, especially one used by a sovereign or high-ranking person. |  Access to the queen's bedchamber was strictly limited.  进入女王内室的权限受到严格限制。| Synonym: inner chamber, private room | Antonym: public room, hall | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English bedchambre, from Old English beddchamber, a compound of bedd (bed) and chamber (room).
