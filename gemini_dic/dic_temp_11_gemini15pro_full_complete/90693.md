humdrum:/ˈhʌmdrʌm/| adj. |单调的，乏味的，无聊的|Lacking excitement or variety; dull; monotonous.|例句：He lived a humdrum existence, working at the same job for 30 years. 他过着单调乏味的生活，在同一个岗位上工作了30年。|例句：The humdrum routine of housework bored her.  家务的单调乏味让她感到厌倦。|近义词：monotonous, dull, tedious, boring, mundane|反义词：exciting, interesting, stimulating, thrilling|词性:形容词
词源: From the reduplication of *hum* (onomatopoeic, expressing boredom or monotony) + *drum*, perhaps influenced by words like *thrum* (to make a continuous rhythmic humming sound).  最初是由拟声词 *hum*（表达无聊或单调）的重复构成，加上 *drum*，可能受到 *thrum*（发出连续有节奏的嗡嗡声）等词的影响。
