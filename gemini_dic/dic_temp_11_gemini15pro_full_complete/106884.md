labyrinthiform: /ˌlæbəˈrɪnθɪfɔːrm/ (US) /ˌlæbəˈrɪnθɪfɔːm/ (UK)| adj.| 迷路的，迷宫似的；具有迷路构造的| Resembling a labyrinth; intricate and confusing.| The fish's labyrinthiform organ allows it to breathe air. 这种鱼的迷器器官可以让它呼吸空气。|  near synonyms: labyrinthine, mazy, winding, tortuous | near antonyms: straight, direct, simple | adjective

labyrinthiform: /ˌlæbəˈrɪnθɪfɔːrm/ (US) /ˌlæbəˈrɪnθɪfɔːm/ (UK)| n.| 迷路鱼| A fish of the order Labyrinthiformes, characterized by a labyrinthiform organ that allows them to breathe air. |  The Betta fish is a common type of labyrinthiform.  斗鱼是一种常见的迷路鱼。| near synonyms: anabantoid, climbing perch | antonyms: N/A | noun
