melisma:/məˈlɪzmə/ (US), /ˈmelɪzmə/ (UK)| n. |滑音，装饰音|A melisma is a musical technique where a single syllable of text is sung over multiple notes.  It is often used to create a sense of ornamentation or expressiveness in vocal music.|The soprano's melisma on the final note was breathtaking. 女高音在最后一个音符上的滑音令人叹为观止。

The gospel singer used melismas extensively throughout the song. 福音歌手在整首歌中广泛使用了滑音。|近义词：vocal run, flourish, ornamentation|反义词:monotone|词性:noun
