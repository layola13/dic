interbreed:/ˌɪntərˈbriːd/
verb
杂交，异种交配
to breed or cause to breed with members of another breed or group
例句：Wolves rarely interbreed with coyotes.  狼很少与郊狼杂交。

例句：The two species of birds occasionally interbreed. 这两种鸟类偶尔会杂交。

近义词：crossbreed, hybridize
反义词：inbreed, purebreed
词性: v.
