contributary: /kənˈtrɪbjʊtəri/ (US), /kənˈtrɪbjʊtəri/ (UK)| n. adj.|支流；贡献的|A stream or river that flows into a larger stream or river; contributing to a larger whole.|The Missouri River is a major tributary of the Mississippi River. 密苏里河是密西西比河的主要支流。|近义词：affluent, feeder, branch|反义词:mainstream, distributary|词性:名词

contributary: /kənˈtrɪbjʊtəri/ (US), /kənˈtrɪbjʊtəri/ (UK)| adj.|贡献的；辅助的|Playing a part in bringing something about.|Stress is a contributory factor in many illnesses. 压力是许多疾病的诱因。|近义词：contributing, conducive, instrumental|反义词:hindering, obstructive|词性:形容词
