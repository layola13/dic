nonheritable:/ˌnɒnˈhɛrɪtəbl/ | adj. | 不可遗传的，非遗传的 | Not able to be inherited or passed on from parent to offspring. | Example:  Eye color is heritable, but learned skills like playing the piano are nonheritable. 眼睛的颜色是可遗传的，但像弹钢琴这样的学习技能是不可遗传的。

Example:  Most cancers are nonheritable, arising from environmental factors or random mutations. 大多数癌症是不可遗传的，是由环境因素或随机突变引起的。


| Synonym: non-inheritable, acquired | Antonym: heritable, genetic, inherited | 词性: 形容词
