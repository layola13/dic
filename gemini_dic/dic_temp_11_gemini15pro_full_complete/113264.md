lustrousnesses:/ˈlʌstrəsnəsɪz/
n.
光泽；光彩；辉煌
the state or quality of being lustrous; gloss or sheen.
Her hair had a healthy lustrousness. 她的头发很有光泽。
Synonyms: sheen, gloss, shine, luster, brilliance, radiance, luminosity, gleam, shimmer, sparkle
Antonyms: dullness, flatness, drabness, lackluster
