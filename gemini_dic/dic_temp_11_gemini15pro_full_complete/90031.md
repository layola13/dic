hosiery:/ˈhoʊʒəri/ (US), /ˈhəʊʒəri/ (UK)| n. |袜类，针织品| articles of clothing (such as socks, stockings, and tights) that fit closely over the feet and legs | She bought new hosiery for the winter. 她买了新的冬季袜类。|Synonyms: stockings, socks, tights, legwear | Antonyms: None (Clothing doesn't typically have direct antonyms) | Noun


hosier:/ˈhoʊʒər/ (US), /ˈhəʊʒər/ (UK)| n. |袜商| a person who deals in hosiery | The hosier had a wide variety of socks and stockings.  那个袜商有各种各样的袜子和长袜。| Synonyms: haberdasher | Antonyms: None | Noun
