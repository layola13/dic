overcloying: /ˌoʊvərˈklɔɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 过于甜腻的，腻烦的，令人感到过分的 | Excessively sweet, rich, or sentimental, to the point of causing disgust or boredom.  Derived from "cloy," meaning to cause disgust or aversion through excess. | The overcloying sweetness of the dessert made me feel sick.  这道甜点过于甜腻，让我感到恶心。| The romance novel was so overcloying that I couldn't finish it. 这部爱情小说太腻了，我读不完。 | 近义词: saccharine, cloying, sickly, treacly | 反义词:  subtle, understated, dry, austere | 词性: 形容词
