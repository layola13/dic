aeromechanics: /ˌɛroʊmɪˈkænɪks/ | n. |空气力学;航空力学 |The study of the forces and motion of gases, especially air, and the effects of these forces and motion on bodies in the gas.  It encompasses both aerodynamics and the mechanics of gases.| Aeromechanics plays a crucial role in aircraft design. 空气力学在飞机设计中起着至关重要的作用。|Synonyms: aerodynamics, gas dynamics |Antonyms: N/A | Noun
