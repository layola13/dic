aesthetic:/ɛsˈθɛtɪk/ (US) /iːsˈθetɪk/ (UK)| adj. n.|美的，美学的；审美的；美感；美学|Concerned with beauty or the appreciation of beauty.|The building has a pleasing aesthetic quality. 这栋建筑有一种令人愉悦的美感。|近义词:artistic, beautiful, creative|反义词:ugly, unattractive|词性:形容词, 名词

aesthetic:/ɛsˈθɛtɪk/ (US) /iːsˈθetɪk/ (UK)| n.|美感；美学|A set of principles underlying the work of a particular artist or artistic movement.|The minimalist aesthetic is very popular in interior design these days. 极简主义美学如今在室内设计中非常流行。|近义词:beauty, taste, style|反义词:ugliness, vulgarity|词性:名词
