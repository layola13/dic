heftily:/ˈhevɪli/| adv.|沉重地；大量地；有力地；昂贵地|In a hefty manner; with great weight, force, or amount.  It can also refer to a high price.|例句：He slammed the door heftily. 他重重地摔上了门。|近义词：heavily, substantially, considerably, expensively|反义词：lightly, slightly, insignificantly, cheaply|词性:副词
词源: From *hefty* + *-ly*.  *Hefty* likely originated from *heft*, meaning "weight," which is of uncertain origin.
