inermis:/ɪˈnɜːrmɪs/
adjective
无刺的， unarmed, without thorns or spines, defenseless
Latin origin: in- (not) + arma (arms) meaning "without arms/weapons," referring to plants lacking thorns or spines.
The rose cultivar 'Rosa banksiae var. inermis' is a popular choice for gardeners because it is thornless.  罗莎蔷薇的无刺变种“罗莎蔷薇无刺变种”因其无刺而成为园丁们的热门选择。
Acacia inermis is a species of acacia that lacks thorns. 无刺金合欢是一种没有刺的金合欢树种。
近义词:unarmed, thornless, spineless
反义词:armed, thorny, spiny
词性:adjective
