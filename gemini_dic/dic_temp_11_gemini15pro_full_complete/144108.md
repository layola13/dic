outscold:/ˌaʊtˈskoʊld/
verb
责骂，胜过…的责骂
to scold someone more loudly or for a longer time than someone else does
She outscolded him by a mile. 她把他骂得狗血淋头。
近义词:berate, rebuke, reprimand, chide
反义词:praise, commend
词性:verb
