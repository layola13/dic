/hæd/ | auxiliary v. | 有(have的过去式), 经历过 | Had is the past tense and past participle of the verb "have." It can be used as an auxiliary verb or a main verb. | I had a great time yesterday. 我昨天玩得很开心. | synonyms: possessed, owned, experienced | antonyms: lacked, needed | verb

/hæd/ | auxiliary v. | 已经 | Had is used to form the past perfect tense. | They had already eaten when I arrived.  当我到达时，他们已经吃过了。| synonyms: completed, finished | antonyms: hadn't, didn't have | verb

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I have a book. = 我有一本书。
She has a cat. = 她有一只猫。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am having a good time. = 我正玩得开心。
She is having lunch. = 她正在吃午饭。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have had a busy day. = 我今天很忙。
She has had enough. = 她受够了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been having trouble sleeping. = 我一直有睡眠问题。
He has been having a great time traveling. = 他旅行玩得很开心。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I had a wonderful dream last night. = 我昨晚做了一个美梦。
She had a meeting yesterday. = 她昨天有个会议。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was having dinner when the phone rang. = 电话响的时候我正在吃晚饭。
She was having a shower when the power went out. = 停电时她正在洗澡。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had already finished my work before he arrived. = 在他到达之前我已经完成了我的工作。
They had left the party by the time we got there. = 当我们到达时，他们已经离开了派对。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been working on the project for weeks when it was cancelled. = 我一直在做这个项目好几个星期了，然后它被取消了。
She had been studying English for years before she moved to the UK. = 在她搬到英国之前，她已经学习英语多年了。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will have more time next week. = 我下周会有更多时间。
She will have a party for her birthday. = 她会为她的生日举办一个派对。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
This time next week, I will be having a holiday in Spain. = 下周的这个时候，我将在西班牙度假。
This time tomorrow, she will be having a meeting with her boss. = 明天这个时候，她将和她的老板开会。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next year, I will have had this car for five years. = 到明年，我将拥有这辆车五年了。
By the end of the month, she will have had her driver's license for ten years. = 到月底，她将拥有驾照十年了。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next month, I will have been having driving lessons for six months. = 到下个月，我将学习驾驶课程六个月了。
By the end of the year, he will have been working at this company for two years. = 到年底，他将在这家公司工作两年了。


虚拟语气:
If I had known, I would have told you. = 如果我知道了，我就会告诉你。
I wish I had gone to the party. = 我希望我去了那个派对。

被动语态:
The car had been driven for thousands of miles. = 这辆车已经被开了几千英里了。
The house had been built in the 19th century. = 这栋房子建于19世纪。

疑问句:
Had you seen the movie before? = 你以前看过这部电影吗？
Had she finished her homework? = 她完成作业了吗？

否定句:
I had not seen him before. = 我以前没见过他。
She had not finished her work. = 她没有完成她的工作。
