bowdlerizer:/ˈboʊdləˌraɪzər/| n.|删改者，净化者|A person who bowdlerizes texts, removing or changing parts considered vulgar or offensive.|The bowdlerizer removed all references to sex and violence from the novel.|Synonyms: censor, expurgator, editor|Antonyms: none|noun

bowdlerize:/ˈboʊdləˌraɪz/| v.|删改，净化|To remove or change parts of a text considered vulgar or offensive.|The publisher bowdlerized the novel before releasing it to a younger audience.|Synonyms: censor, expurgate, edit|Antonyms: none|verb
