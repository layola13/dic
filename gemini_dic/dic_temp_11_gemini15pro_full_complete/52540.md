diffidentness:/ˈdɪfɪdəntnəs/| n. |缺乏自信，胆怯，羞怯|The state or quality of being diffident; lack of self-confidence; shyness; hesitancy.|His diffidentness prevented him from speaking up in class. 他的胆怯使他不敢在课堂上发言。|近义词：shyness, timidity, hesitancy, insecurity, self-doubt|反义词：confidence, assertiveness, boldness, self-assurance|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *diffīdentia (“distrust, lack of confidence”), from Latin diffīdō (“I distrust”), from dis- + fīdō (“I trust”).
