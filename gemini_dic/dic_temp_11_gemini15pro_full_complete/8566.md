anticipations:/ænˌtɪsɪˈpeɪʃənz/
n.
预期，期望，预料
The act of anticipating or the state of being anticipated; expectation or prediction.
We waited with great anticipation for the concert to begin. 我们满怀期待地等待音乐会开始。
