colonial: /kəˈloʊniəl/ | adj. | 殖民的；殖民地的 | Relating to, characteristic of, or possessing colonies. |  The British Empire had many colonial possessions around the world.  大英帝国在世界各地拥有许多殖民地。

| Example: The colonial architecture of the city reflects its history. 这座城市的殖民时期建筑反映了它的历史。| Synonyms: imperial, dependent, subject | Antonyms: independent, sovereign | word type: adjective
