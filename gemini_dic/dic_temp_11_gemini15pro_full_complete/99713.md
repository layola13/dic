interlotted:/ˌɪntərˈlɒtɪd/ | adj. | 相互分配的，交错的 |  Divided into lots and assigned to different owners or uses, often in a complex or interwoven pattern.  Implies a close relationship or mixture between the different parts. | The farmers interlotted the land along the river, each receiving a narrow strip with access to the water. 农民们沿着河相互分配土地，每个人都得到一条可以通往水边的狭长地带。 | The interlotted fields of crops created a colorful tapestry across the valley. 交错的农田在山谷中构成了一幅色彩缤纷的织锦。| intermingled, interspersed, interwoven | segregated, separated | adjective
