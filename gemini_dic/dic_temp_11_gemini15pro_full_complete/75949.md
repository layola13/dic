funicle:/ˈfjuːnɪkəl/| n. |珠柄; 胎座; 脐带|A small cord, especially the stalk that attaches an ovule or seed to the placenta in a plant.|The funicle of the seed is short and thick.  种子的珠柄短而粗。|Synonyms: stalk, filament, cord|Antonyms: N/A|noun


funicle:/ˈfjuːnɪkəl/| n. |输精管|A slender cord or band, especially the spermatic cord.|Damage to the funicle can affect fertility.  输精管损伤会影响生育能力。|Synonyms: spermatic cord, vas deferens|Antonyms: N/A|noun
