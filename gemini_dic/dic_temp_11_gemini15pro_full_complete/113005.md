lumberingly:/ˈlʌmbərɪŋli/
| adv. |笨拙地，缓慢地|Moving in a slow, heavy, and awkward way.  Characterized by clumsiness and lack of grace.|The elephant lumberingly made its way through the dense forest. 大象笨拙地穿过茂密的森林。|近义词：clumsily, awkwardly, ponderously, heavily|反义词：gracefully, nimbly, swiftly, deftly|词性:副词
