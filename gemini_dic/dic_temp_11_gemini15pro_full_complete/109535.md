lessees:/ˌlesiːz/
noun
承租人，租赁者
A person or organization that holds a lease.
The lessees are responsible for maintaining the property. 承租人负责维护房产。

The lessees agreed to the terms of the lease. 承租人同意了租赁条款。


Synonym: tenant, renter
Antonym: lessor, landlord
n.
