come-to-a-head:/kʌm tuː ə hɛd/
|phrasal verb|
|达到紧急关头；到达顶点|
|To reach a crisis or critical point; to culminate.|
|The situation came to a head when the workers went on strike.|
|局势在工人罢工时达到了顶点。|

|The argument came to a head when she stormed out of the room.|
|当她冲出房间时，争论达到了白热化。|

|climax, culminate, peak, reach a crisis|
|subside, abate, diminish, lessen|
|verb phrase|
