lights: /laɪts/ | n. | 灯，灯光；光源；照明设备 |  The source of illumination, either natural or artificial. | The city lights twinkled below. 城市的灯光在下面闪烁。| synonyms: illumination, lamps, luminescence | antonyms: darkness, shadows | noun

lights: /laɪts/ | v. | 点燃；照亮；(使)容光焕发 | To ignite or illuminate something; to cause something to become bright or cheerful. | She lit the candles on the birthday cake. 她在生日蛋糕上点燃了蜡烛。 His face lit up when he saw her. 他看到她时，脸上容光焕发。 | synonyms: ignite, illuminate, brighten | antonyms: extinguish, darken, dim | verb
