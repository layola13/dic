individualizer:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlaɪzər/ | n. |个性化者；个体化者| A person or thing that individualizes something or someone.  A person who focuses on or emphasizes the individual. |例句：The teacher acted as an individualizer, tailoring the curriculum to each student's needs.  老师扮演了个性化者的角色，根据每个学生的需要调整课程。|近义词：customizer, personalizer |反义词: standardizer, homogenizer |词性: noun

individualize:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlaɪz/ | v. |使个性化；使个体化| To make something or someone individual; to give something or someone a distinct character. |例句：The company individualizes its products to meet customer specifications.  公司根据客户的规格个性化其产品。|近义词：customize, personalize |反义词: standardize, homogenize |词性: verb

individualized:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlaɪzd/ | adj. |个性化的；个体化的| Made individual or distinctive. |例句：The students received individualized attention from the tutor. 学生们得到了导师的个性化关注。|近义词：customized, personalized |反义词: standardized, homogenized |词性: adjective

Etymology: From individual + -ize, ultimately from Latin *individuus "indivisible" + -ize.
