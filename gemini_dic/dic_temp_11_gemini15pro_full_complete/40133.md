contemplatives:/kənˈtɛmplətɪvz/ | n. |沉思者；冥想者；修行者| People who engage in contemplation; those dedicated to a life of meditation and reflection. |  The monastery was home to a community of contemplatives.  这个修道院是一群修行者的家园。|  The contemplatives spent hours in silent prayer. 这些沉思者花了数小时默默祈祷。| 近义词:meditators, thinkers, monks, nuns, ascetics | 反义词: activists, doers, extroverts | noun

contemplative:/kənˈtɛmplətɪv/ | adj. |沉思的；冥想的| Expressing or involving prolonged thought. | She had a contemplative look on her face. 她脸上带着沉思的表情。| He spent a contemplative afternoon by the river. 他在河边度过了一个沉思的下午。| 近义词: thoughtful, reflective, meditative, pensive | 反义词:  rash, impulsive, unthinking | adjective
