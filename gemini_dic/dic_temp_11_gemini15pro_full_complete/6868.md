anatropal:/əˈnætrəpəl/
adjective
倒生的，反向的
(of an ovule) inverted so that the micropyle is turned towards the funiculus and the chalaza is at the opposite end.
例句：The anatropal ovule is the most common type found in flowering plants.  倒生胚珠是被子植物中最常见的类型。

例句：The botanist identified the ovule as anatropal due to its inverted orientation.  植物学家根据胚珠的倒置方向将其鉴定为倒生的。
近义词：inverted, resupinate
反义词：orthotropous, atropous
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ἀνάτροπος (anátropos, “turned upside down”) from *ἀνατρέπω (anatrépō, “I turn upside down”) from *ἀνά (aná, “up”) + *τρέπω (trépo, “I turn”)
