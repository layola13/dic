outbrawl:/ˌaʊtˈbrɔːl/
verb
大声叫嚷胜过；在争吵中胜过
to shout louder than someone else in a quarrel or fight; to defeat someone in a quarrel or fight
例句：He outbrawled his opponent and won the argument. 他在争论中以高声压倒了对手，赢得了胜利。

例句：The two politicians outbrawled each other on live television.  两位政客在电视直播中互相叫嚷。

近义词：outshout, outargue, defeat, vanquish
反义词：lose, surrender, yield
词性: v.
