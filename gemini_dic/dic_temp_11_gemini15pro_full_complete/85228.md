harlequinesque:/ˌhɑːrləkwɪˈnɛsk/ | adj. |滑稽的，小丑般的，五颜六色的，杂乱的 |  Relating to or resembling a harlequin; characterized by bright colors, contrasting patterns, or a flamboyant, often comic style. |  The harlequinesque costumes were a riot of color and pattern. 小丑般的服装色彩斑斓，图案各异。 | The party had a harlequinesque atmosphere, with people in bizarre costumes and loud music. 派对的气氛很滑稽，人们穿着奇装异服，音乐震耳欲聋。 | 近义词:  clownish, comical, flamboyant, multicolored, motley | 反义词:  drab, monochrome, serious, somber | 词性: adjective

词源: From Harlequin, a character in commedia dell'arte, known for his checkered costume.  源自意大利即兴喜剧中的角色“丑角”，以其方格图案的服装而闻名。
