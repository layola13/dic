locating:/ˈloʊkeɪtɪŋ/ /ˈləʊkeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 定位，找出…的位置 |  The app is designed for locating nearby restaurants.  这个应用程序是用来定位附近餐馆的。| Synonyms: finding, pinpointing, positioning, situating | Antonyms: losing, misplacing | verb

locating:/ˈloʊkeɪtɪŋ/ /ˈləʊkeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 定位的 |  The locating device helped us find the lost hiker.  这个定位装置帮助我们找到了迷路的徒步旅行者。| Synonyms: positioning, placement | Antonyms: N/A | adjective

Etymology: From the verb "locate," which comes from the Late Latin "locatus," past participle of "locare" (to place), from "locus" (place).
