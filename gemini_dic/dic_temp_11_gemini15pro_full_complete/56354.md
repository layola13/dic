doi:/dɔɪ/ | n. |数字对象标识符 (Digital Object Identifier)| A persistent identifier that provides a unique and permanent link to a digital object, such as a journal article, dataset, or book chapter. | Example:  The DOI for this article is 10.1000/182. | Synonym:  Persistent identifier, handle | Antonym: N/A | noun
