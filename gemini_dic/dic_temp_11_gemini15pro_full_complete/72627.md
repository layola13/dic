flyings:/ˈflaɪɪŋz/| n.|飞行物，飞行，飞行训练|Usually considered incorrect; the correct form is typically "flights" or "flying" depending on the context.  "Flyings" might be found in older texts or dialects, or used informally to refer to a collection of things that fly.|"The flyings of birds are a marvel of nature." (This example is somewhat archaic and would be better phrased as "The flight of birds...")|近义词：flights, flying|反义词:landings, grounding|词性:名词 (usually considered incorrect)
