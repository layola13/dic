artillerymen:/ɑːrˈtɪlərimən/ | n. |炮兵，炮手|Soldiers who operate artillery (large-caliber guns).|The artillerymen fired a barrage at the enemy positions. 炮兵向敌人的阵地发射了弹幕。|Synonyms: gunner, cannoneer|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, possibly civilian)|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From artillery + man. Artillery comes from Old French artillerie, meaning "equipment, preparation," ultimately from Late Latin artillator, meaning "one who prepares or equips."
