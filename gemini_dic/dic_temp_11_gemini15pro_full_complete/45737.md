例句：The curviness of the woman's figure was accentuated by the dress.  这件衣服突出了这位女士身材的曲线美。


|近义词：roundness, shapeliness|反义词:straightness, flatness|词性:名词


Etymology->
From curvy + -ness.  "Curvy" itself comes from "curve" with the suffix "-y" indicating a quality or characteristic.  The word "curve" ultimately derives from the Latin "curvus" meaning "bent" or "curved."
