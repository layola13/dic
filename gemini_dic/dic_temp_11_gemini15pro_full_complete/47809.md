decarbonize:/diːˈkɑːrbənaɪz/ | v. |脱碳，减少碳排放| To reduce or eliminate carbon dioxide emissions. |  Many countries are trying to decarbonize their economies. 许多国家正试图减少其经济体的碳排放。

The company has pledged to decarbonize its operations by 2050. 该公司承诺到2050年实现运营脱碳。 | synonyms: decarbonise, reduce emissions, go green | antonyms: increase emissions, pollute | verb
