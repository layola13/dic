consolatorily:/ˌkɒnsəˈleɪtərɪli/ | adv. |安慰地，慰藉地|In a way that offers comfort or solace.  |She patted his hand consolatorily. 她安慰地拍了拍他的手。|例句：He smiled consolatorily at her. 他安慰地对她笑了笑。|近义词：comfortingly, soothingly, reassuringly|反义词：harshly, cruelly, unsympathetically|词性:副词

consolatory:/kənˈsɒlət(ə)ri/ | adj. |安慰的，慰藉的|Providing comfort or solace. |She offered him a few consolatory words. 她给了他几句安慰的话。|例句：The letter contained a consolatory message. 这封信包含了一条安慰的信息。|近义词：comforting, soothing, reassuring|反义词：distressing, upsetting, disheartening|词性:形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin consolatorius, from Latin consolator ‘comforter’, from consolari (see console).
