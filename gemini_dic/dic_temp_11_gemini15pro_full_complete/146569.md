overgrassed:/ˌōvərˈɡræst/ | adj. |过度放牧的；牧草过多的| Covered with too much grass; excessively grazed. | The field was overgrassed, and the cattle couldn't find enough fresh shoots to eat.  这块田地过度放牧，牛找不到足够的新芽吃。 |  The pasture was so overgrassed that the sheep couldn't find any clover. 这片牧场草长得太茂盛了，羊找不到三叶草。| 近义词: overgrazed | 反义词: undergrazed | 词性: 形容词
