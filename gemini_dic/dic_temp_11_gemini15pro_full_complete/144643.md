outworks:/ˈaʊtwɜːrks/
noun
外围工事，防御工事
The external fortifications of a place, especially a castle or walled city.
The castle's outworks were breached by the enemy's cannons. 城堡的外围工事被敌人的大炮攻破了。
synonyms: fortifications, defenses, ramparts, bulwarks
antonyms: interior, inside, citadel
