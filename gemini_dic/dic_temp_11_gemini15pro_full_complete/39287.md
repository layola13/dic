congruously:/kənˈɡruːəsli/ | adv. | 一致地，适合地，和谐地 | In a way that is in agreement, harmony, or correspondence; appropriately or suitably. |  The furniture was congruously arranged, creating a harmonious atmosphere. 家具摆放得井然有序，营造出和谐的氛围。 |  She dressed congruously for the occasion, choosing a simple yet elegant gown. 她穿着得体，选择了一件简洁而优雅的礼服。 | 近义词: appropriately, suitably, fittingly, harmoniously, consistently | 反义词: inappropriately, unsuitably, discordantly, inconsistently | 词性: 副词

congruent:/ˈkɒŋɡruːənt/ | adj. | 一致的，适合的，全等的 | In agreement, harmony, or correspondence; identical in form. |  Her actions were congruent with her words, demonstrating her sincerity. 她的行动与言行一致，表明了她的真诚。 | The two triangles are congruent, having the same size and shape. 这两个三角形全等，大小和形状相同。 | 近义词: corresponding, matching, consistent, identical, similar | 反义词: incongruent, inconsistent, dissimilar, different | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *congruentem (nominative congruens), present participle of congruere "to come together, agree, coincide," from com "with, together" (see com-) + -gruere, related to grex (genitive gregis) "flock, herd."
