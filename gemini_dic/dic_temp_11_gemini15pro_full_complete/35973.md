codeless:/ˈkoʊdlɪs/| adj.|无代码的，无需编码的|Relating to or denoting methods of software development that do not require traditional programming skills.  It allows individuals to create applications and automate tasks without writing code, typically through visual interfaces and drag-and-drop components.|Example: Codeless platforms empower citizen developers to build business applications. 无代码平台使平民开发者能够构建业务应用程序。|Example: This codeless platform allows me to create apps quickly. 这个无代码平台让我可以快速创建应用程序。|近义词:no-code, visual programming|反义词:coded, programmatic|词性:形容词
