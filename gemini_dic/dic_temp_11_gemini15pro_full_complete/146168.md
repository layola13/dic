overestimation:/ˌoʊvərˌɛstɪˈmeɪʃən/| n. |高估，过高估计|An act of estimating something to be larger, better, or more important than it really is.|The company's overestimation of demand led to a surplus of inventory.  公司对需求的高估导致库存过剩。|Overvaluation, exaggeration|Underestimation|Noun
