detriment:/ˈdetrɪmənt/ | n. | 损害，伤害，不利 |Something that causes damage, harm, or loss. |  Exposure to the sun can be a detriment to your skin.  暴露在阳光下可能会对你的皮肤有害。|Synonyms: harm, damage, injury, loss, disadvantage | Antonyms: benefit, advantage, gain, improvement | noun

detriments:/ˈdetrɪmənts/ | n. | 损害，伤害，不利（detriment 的复数形式）| The plural form of detriment, referring to multiple factors that cause harm, damage, or loss. | Smoking and excessive drinking are detriments to one's health. 吸烟和酗酒对健康有害。 | Synonyms: harms, damages, injuries, losses, disadvantages | Antonyms: benefits, advantages, gains, improvements | noun
