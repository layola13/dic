interunions:/ˌɪntərˈjuːnjənz/
noun
工会间组织，跨工会组织
An organization or collaboration involving multiple labor unions.
The interunions council met to discuss the proposed legislation. 工会间理事会开会讨论了拟议的立法。
近义词:labor federation, trade union association
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "independent union")
