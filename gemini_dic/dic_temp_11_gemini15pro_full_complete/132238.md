nondeciduous:/ˌnɒndɪˈsɪdʒuəs/
adjective
非落叶的
Not shedding leaves seasonally; evergreen.
The nondeciduous trees remained green throughout the winter.  非落叶树整个冬天都保持绿色。
The nondeciduous forest provides year-round shelter for animals. 非落叶林为动物提供全年的庇护所。
evergreen, persistent
