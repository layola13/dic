ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + perceptible + -ness.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not." Perceptible comes from the Latin percept-, past participle stem of percipere "to perceive," from per- "thoroughly" + capere "to take, seize."  -ness is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, meaning "state or quality of being."
