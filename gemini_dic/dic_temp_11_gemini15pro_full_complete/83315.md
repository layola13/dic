gynophore:/ˈɡaɪnəfɔːr/
n.
雌蕊柄；子房柄
A stalk bearing the ovary of certain flowers above the level of insertion of the other floral parts, as in the passionflower.
The passionflower's gynophore elevates the ovary above the petals and sepals.  西番莲的雌蕊柄将子房抬高到花瓣和萼片之上。

The gynophore of the caper bush is a distinctive feature.  刺山柑的雌蕊柄是一个显著特征。
近义词: stipe, stalk
反义词:  (No direct antonym,  could consider parts like petal, sepal as conceptually opposite in location)
词性: noun
