get-along:/ɡɛt əˈlɒŋ/ (UK) /ɡɛt əˈlɔːŋ/ (US)| phrasal verb |相处融洽，进展|To have a harmonious or friendly relationship with someone; to progress or manage in a particular situation.|例句：They get along very well. 他们相处得很好。|近义词：get on, agree, harmonize|反义词: clash, disagree, quarrel|词性:动词短语

get-along:/ɡɛt əˈlɒŋ/ (UK) /ɡɛt əˈlɔːŋ/ (US)| n. |相处, 友好关系|A state of harmonious or friendly relations.|例句：They have a good get-along. 他们的关系很好。|近义词：relationship, rapport, understanding|反义词: conflict, discord, friction|词性:名词 (较少用)
