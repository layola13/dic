liaison:/liˈeɪzɒn/ (UK), /liˈeɪzɑːn/ (US)| n.|联络，联系；联络人；私通|A close working relationship between people or organizations.|例句：He acted as a liaison between the two departments. 他担任两个部门之间的联络人。

例句：She maintained a close liaison with the local community. 她与当地社区保持着密切的联系。|近义词：link, connection, contact, intermediary|反义词:separation, division|noun
