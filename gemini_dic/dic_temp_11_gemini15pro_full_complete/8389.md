anti-russians:/ˌæntiˈrʌʃənz/| n. |反俄人士；仇俄者|People who are opposed to or hostile towards Russia, its government, or its people.  It can encompass individuals, groups, or even policies that express antagonism towards Russia.|例句：The anti-Russians protested the invasion. 反俄人士抗议入侵。

例句：The government implemented anti-Russian sanctions. 政府实施了反俄制裁。|近义词：Russia-haters, Russia-phobes|反义词：Russophiles, pro-Russians|词性:名词
