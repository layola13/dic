elogy: /ˈelədʒi/ | n. | 颂词，悼词；赞美 | A speech or piece of writing that praises someone or something highly, typically someone who has just died. |  He delivered a moving eulogy at his father's funeral. 他在父亲的葬礼上发表了感人的悼词。

The book is a eulogy to the natural beauty of the countryside. 这本书是对乡村自然美景的赞歌。 | 近义词: tribute, panegyric, encomium, accolade | 反义词: criticism, condemnation, denunciation | 词性: noun

词源: From Middle French *eloge*, from Latin *elogium* "inscription, brief saying," from Greek *eulogia* "praise," from *euloges*, "speaking well of," from *eu* "well" + *legein* "to speak."
