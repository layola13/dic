mollus:/ˈmɑːləs/ /ˈmɒləs/| n. |软体动物|Any of a large phylum (Mollusca) of invertebrate animals (such as snails, clams, or squids) with a soft unsegmented body usually enclosed in a shell. |Examples:  The octopus is a highly intelligent mollusc. 章鱼是一种非常聪明的软体动物。


The giant squid is one of the largest molluscs in the world.  大王乌贼是世界上最大的软体动物之一。|近义词:invertebrate, shellfish|反义词:vertebrate|词性:名词
