outgroup:/ˈaʊtɡruːp/
n.
外群体，外围群体
a social group with which an individual does not identify
例句：
They view members of the outgroup with suspicion.
他们用怀疑的眼光看待外群体成员。

Newcomers to the community are often treated as an outgroup.
新来者经常被当作外围群体。


近义词:
outsiders, non-members
反义词:
ingroup
