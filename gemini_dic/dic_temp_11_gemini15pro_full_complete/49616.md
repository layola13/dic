demi-volte:/ˌdɛmiˈvɔːlt/| n.|半回转，半圈|A half turn made by a horse.|The rider executed a perfect demi-volte.|近义词：half-turn|反义词:full turn|词性:noun
Etymology: From French demi-volte, from demi- "half" + volte "turn". Ultimately from Latin dimidius "half" and volvere "to turn".
