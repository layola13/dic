mickey-mousing:/ˈmɪkiˌmaʊsɪŋ/
noun
米老鼠式配乐，亦称“照搬动作”
A film technique that synchronizes the accompanying music with the actions on screen, often for comic effect.
The cartoon used mickey-mousing to emphasize the character's clumsy movements.  这部动画片用米老鼠式配乐来强调角色笨拙的动作。
synchronization, mirroring
counterpoint, dissonance
n.
