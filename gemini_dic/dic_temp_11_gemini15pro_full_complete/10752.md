apyretic:/ˌeɪpaɪˈrɛtɪk/ | adj. | 无热的，退热的 | Without fever; afebrile.  | The patient is now apyretic after taking the medication. 病人服药后已退烧。 |  近义词: afebrile, feverless | 反义词: febrile, feverish | adjective

Etymology:  From a- (without) + pyretic (feverish).  Ultimately from Greek *pyr* (fire).
