filariasis:/ˌfɪləˈraɪəsɪs/ | n. | 丝虫病 | A tropical disease caused by parasitic filarial worms, transmitted by mosquitoes or other blood-sucking insects.  It can lead to elephantiasis. |  Filariasis is a major public health problem in many tropical countries. 丝虫病是许多热带国家的主要公共卫生问题。| Synonym:  elephantiasis (in its advanced stage)| Antonym:  None (diseases don't typically have antonyms) | noun
