ETYMOLOGY->
From "light" (referring to color) + "coloured" (past participle of "colour," meaning to add color to). "Light" ultimately derives from Proto-Germanic *leukhtam, meaning "bright, shining."  "Colour" comes from Old French colour, ultimately from Latin color.
