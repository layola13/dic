outhustle:/ˌaʊtˈhʌsəl/ | v. | 胜过，比…更努力 | To outperform or surpass someone through greater effort, energy, or determination. |  The small startup outhustled the larger corporation to win the contract. 这家小型初创公司比更大的公司更努力，赢得了合同。

| Example 1: She outhustled her competitors and landed the top sales spot. 她比竞争对手更努力，获得了最佳销售业绩。

| Example 2:  He knew he wasn't as talented, but he planned to outhustle everyone else on the team. 他知道自己没有那么有才华，但他计划比团队中的其他人都更努力。


| 近义词: outwork, outperform, surpass | 反义词:  be outdone, be surpassed | 词性: 动词
