hippish:/ˈhɪpɪʃ/| adj.|嬉皮士的，嬉皮风格的|Resembling or characteristic of a hippie, especially in dress and lifestyle; unconventional and anti-establishment.|She had a hippish style, with long flowing skirts and flowers in her hair. 她有一种嬉皮士的风格，穿着飘逸的长裙，头发上插着花。|Example: He wore hippish clothes and listened to psychedelic rock. 他穿着嬉皮士风格的衣服，听着迷幻摇滚。|Synonyms: bohemian, unconventional, countercultural, alternative|Antonyms: conventional, traditional, mainstream, conservative|adjective
