nonpersecuting:/ˌnɒnpɜːrsɪˈkjuːtɪŋ/ | adj. | 不迫害的，宽容的 | Not persecuting; tolerant or accepting of others, especially those of different beliefs or backgrounds. |  The government claimed to be nonpersecuting, but its actions told a different story. 政府声称不迫害，但其行动却讲述了不同的故事。

The nonpersecuting nature of the community attracted people from all walks of life. 这个社区宽容的性质吸引了来自各行各业的人。 | 近义词: tolerant, accepting, open-minded, liberal | 反义词: persecuting, discriminatory, bigoted, intolerant | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "persecuting" (present participle of "persecute," from Latin *persequi* "to pursue, follow persistently," from *per-* "thoroughly" + *sequi* "to follow").
