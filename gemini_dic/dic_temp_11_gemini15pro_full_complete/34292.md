clarified:/ˈklærɪfaɪd/| adj.| 已澄清的，已阐明的|Made clear or easier to understand.|The company clarified its position on the merger. 公司澄清了其对合并的立场。|近义词：explained, elucidated, simplified|反义词：confused, obscured, complicated|词性:形容词
clarify:/ˈklærɪfaɪ/| vt.| 澄清，阐明|To make (a statement or situation) less confused and more clearly comprehensible.|Could you clarify your earlier remarks? 你能澄清一下你之前的言论吗？|近义词：explain, elucidate, simplify|反义词：confuse, obscure, complicate|词性:动词

词源: From Late Latin clarificare "to make clear," from Latin clarus "clear" + -ficare  (a verb-forming suffix).
