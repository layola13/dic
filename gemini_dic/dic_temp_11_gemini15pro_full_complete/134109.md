nonimmune:/ˌnɒnɪˈmjuːn/ /ˌnɑːnɪˈmjuːn/| adj.|非免疫的，无免疫力的|Not immune; lacking immunity.| People who are nonimmune to measles can get the disease.  非免疫性麻疹的人可能会感染这种疾病。| The nonimmune child was at risk of contracting the illness.  这个没有免疫力的孩子有感染这种疾病的风险。|近义词：susceptible, vulnerable|反义词：immune, resistant|词性:形容词

词源: non- + immune

non-:  prefix meaning "not"

immune: from Latin immunis "exempt, free, not paying a share," from in- "not" (from PIE root *en "in") + munis "performing services, obligated," related to moenia "city walls"
