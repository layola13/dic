outplot:/ˈaʊtplɑːt/
verb
密谋胜过，策划击败
to create a better or more effective plot than someone else, especially in a story or play
The villain was outplotted by the hero in the end.  最终，反派被英雄的计谋击败了。
outmaneuver, outwit, outsmart
