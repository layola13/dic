cacostomia:/ˌkækəˈstoʊmiə/| n. |口臭，恶臭|A condition characterized by foul-smelling breath.|Example: The patient complained of cacostomia, which was later attributed to poor dental hygiene. 病人抱怨口臭，后来发现是由于口腔卫生不良引起的。

Example:  The doctor investigated the cause of the patient's cacostomia. 医生调查了病人患有口臭的原因。|近义词：halitosis, bad breath|反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "fresh breath")|词性:noun
