gutters:/ˈɡʌtərz/
n.
排水沟；槽；檐沟
channels at the eaves of a roof for carrying off rainwater.
Rainwater flows along the gutters and into the downpipes. 雨水沿着排水沟流入落水管。
channels; troughs; drains
ridges; peaks

词性: 名词 (plural)


gutters:/ˈɡʌtərz/
n.
(保龄球)球沟
the two grooves on either side of a bowling lane.
The ball went straight into the gutters. 球直接滚进了球沟。
troughs; channels
center of the lane

词性: 名词 (plural)


gutters:/ˈɡʌtərz/
n.
贫民窟；社会底层
the lowest or most degraded section of society.
He rose up from the gutters to become a successful businessman. 他从社会底层崛起，成为了一名成功的商人。
slums; underworld
high society; elite

词性: 名词 (plural)


词源: From Middle English *gotere, *gutere, from Old French goutiere (“gutter, channel”), from goutte (“drop”), from Vulgar Latin *gutta, from Latin gutta (“drop”).
