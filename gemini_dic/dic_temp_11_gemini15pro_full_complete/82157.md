groot:/ɡruːt/| n.|格鲁特 (树人格鲁特)|A sentient, tree-like creature from Planet X, Groot is known for his limited vocabulary, primarily uttering the phrase "I am Groot." This phrase, however, conveys a wide range of emotions and meanings depending on the inflection and context.|例句：Groot sacrificed himself to save the Guardians of the Galaxy. 格鲁特牺牲自己来拯救银河护卫队。

例句：Groot danced to music while the others cleaned up. 格鲁特在其他人打扫的时候随着音乐跳舞。|近义词：tree-like being, flora colossus|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-sentient plant")|词性:noun
