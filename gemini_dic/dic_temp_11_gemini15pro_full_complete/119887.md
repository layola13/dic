militarised:/ˈmɪlɪtəraɪzd/
adjective
军事化的
Equipped for or adapted to warfare; given a military character.
The region has become increasingly militarised in recent years.  近年来，该地区越来越军事化。
The government militarised the police force. 政府将警察部队军事化。
militarized:/ˈmɪlətəˌraɪzd/
adjective
军事化的
Equipped for or adapted to warfare; given a military character.
The border has been heavily militarized due to the ongoing conflict. 由于持续的冲突，边界已被高度军事化。
The school was militarized and used as a base during the war.  战争期间，学校被军事化并用作基地。
近义词: armed, fortified, weaponized
反义词: demilitarized, civilianized
词性: adjective
词源:  From military + -ize.  由 military（军事的） + -ize（使…化）构成。
