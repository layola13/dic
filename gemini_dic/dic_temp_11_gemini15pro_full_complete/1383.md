accusable:/əˈkjuːzəbl/| adj.|可指责的，可控告的|Subject to or deserving of accusation; blameworthy.|He was accusable of negligence. 他因疏忽而受到指责。|近义词：blameworthy, culpable, guilty, indictable|反义词：innocent, blameless|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *accusabilis, from Latin accusare (“to accuse”) + -abilis (suffix forming adjectives meaning "able to be," "worthy of being," "capable of being").  The Latin accusare is derived from ad (preposition meaning "to," "toward," "at") + causa ("cause," "reason," "case," "lawsuit").
