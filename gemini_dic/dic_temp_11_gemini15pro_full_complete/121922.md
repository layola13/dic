misshapen:/ˌmɪsˈʃeɪpən/
adjective
畸形的，奇形怪状的
badly shaped; deformed.
The tree had a misshapen trunk due to a disease.  这棵树的树干因为疾病而变得畸形。
The potter discarded the misshapen vase.  陶工丢弃了那个畸形的瓶子。
近义词:deformed, distorted, malformed
反义词:shapely, well-formed
词性:adjective

词源:
From mis- + shapen, past participle of shape.  由mis-（表示“坏的”、“错误的”）+ shape（形状）的过去分词shapen构成。
