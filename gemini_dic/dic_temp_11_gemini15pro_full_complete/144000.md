outreached:/ˌaʊtˈriːtʃt/
verb
超越，伸出，超出…的范围
surpassed, exceeded the limit of.
The company outreached its sales target for the quarter.  这家公司超过了本季度的销售目标。

verb
接触，联系（尤指帮助弱势群体）
contact, connect with (especially to help vulnerable groups)
The charity outreached to the homeless population in the city.  该慈善机构联系了该市的无家可归者。

近义词: exceed, surpass, extend, reach out
反义词: fall short, fail to reach

词性:v.
