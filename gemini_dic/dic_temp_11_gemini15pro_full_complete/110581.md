limited-government:/ˈlɪmɪtɪd ˈɡʌvərnmənt/
noun
有限政府
A political system in which the government's power is restricted by law, usually in a written constitution.
The United States is an example of a limited government, where the Constitution outlines the powers of the federal government and protects individual rights.  美国是一个有限政府的例子，宪法概述了联邦政府的权力并保护个人权利。
A limited government ensures individual liberties are protected. 有限政府确保个人自由得到保护。
Synonyms: constitutional government, restricted government
Antonyms: unlimited government, authoritarian government, totalitarian government
