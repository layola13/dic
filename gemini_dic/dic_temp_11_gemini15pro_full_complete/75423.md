frugivorous:/fruːˈdʒɪvərəs/
adjective
食果的
Feeding primarily or exclusively on fruit.
例句：Many bats are frugivorous, playing a vital role in seed dispersal. 许多蝙蝠以水果为食，在种子传播中起着至关重要的作用。

例句：The frugivorous monkey feasted on the ripe mangoes. 这只食果猴尽情享用成熟的芒果。

近义词：fruit-eating
反义词：carnivorous, herbivorous
词性: adjective


词源: From Latin *frugivorus*, from *frux* (genitive *fruges*) "fruit" + *-vorus* "-eating," from *vorare* "to devour."
