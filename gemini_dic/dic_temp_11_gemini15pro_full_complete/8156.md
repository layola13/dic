anti-creational:/ˌænti-kriˈeɪʃənəl/
| adj. | 反创造的，反对创造的 | Opposing or hindering creation, typically in a philosophical or religious context.  It describes something that goes against the act or process of bringing something into existence. |  His anti-creational philosophy saw the universe as inherently flawed. 他的反创造哲学认为宇宙本质上是有缺陷的。

|  The cult's anti-creational beliefs led them to destructive practices.  该邪教的反创造信仰导致他们进行破坏性活动。 | Near Synonyms: nihilistic, destructive, iconoclastic | Antonyms: creative, generative, constructive | adjective
