mucific:/mjuːˈsɪfɪk/
adjective
产生粘液的；粘液性的
Producing or secreting mucus; mucous.
The mucific glands in the frog's skin secrete a sticky substance.  青蛙皮肤上的粘液腺分泌一种粘稠的物质。
The snail left a mucific trail across the leaf. 蜗牛在叶子上留下了一条粘液的痕迹。
近义词: mucous, slimy, viscous
反义词: dry, non-mucous
