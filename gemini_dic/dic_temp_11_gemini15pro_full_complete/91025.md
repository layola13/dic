hushfully:/ˈhʌʃfʊli/ | adv. | 轻轻地，安静地 | In a hushed and quiet manner; silently.  | She hushfully closed the door behind her. 她轻轻地关上了身后的门。 |  He hushfully tiptoed into the baby's room. 他静静地踮着脚尖走进婴儿房。 | quietly, softly, silently | loudly, noisily | adverb

词源:
From "hush" (verb meaning "to become or make silent") + "-fully" (adverbial suffix). "Hush" itself is likely of imitative origin, echoing the sound used to quiet someone.
