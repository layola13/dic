inducing:/ɪnˈdjuːsɪŋ/ | adj. | 诱导的，导致的 | Causing or leading to something.  |
The inducing agent caused the cells to differentiate.  诱导剂导致细胞分化。

The smell of freshly baked bread was inducing hunger.  新鲜出炉面包的香味让人感到饥饿。

| 近义词: causing, stimulating, prompting, triggering | 反义词: preventing, inhibiting, suppressing | 词性:  形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *inducens, present participle of inducere "to lead into, bring in, introduce," from in- "in" + ducere "to lead."
