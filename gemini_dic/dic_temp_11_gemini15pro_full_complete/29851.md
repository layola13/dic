causativeness:/ˌkɑːzəˈtɪvnəs/| n. | 使役性，致使性 |The property of a verb or construction that indicates that a subject causes someone or something else to do something.  It often involves causing a change of state or an action.| The causativeness of the verb "make" is clearly seen in the sentence "He made her cry." 他让她哭了. |近义词：causativity |反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "inertness" or "passivity" in certain contexts) |词性: noun
