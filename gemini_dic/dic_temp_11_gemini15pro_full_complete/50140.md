denuder:/dɪˈnjuːdər/ | n. | 除尘器，脱除器 | A device used to remove gases or aerosols from a gas stream. | The denuder efficiently removed ammonia from the air sample. 这台除尘器有效地清除了空气样本中的氨。| The denuder is crucial for accurate measurement of ambient air pollutants.  除尘器对于精确测量环境空气污染物至关重要。 | synonyms: scrubber, purifier, filter | antonyms: polluter, contaminant | noun

denude:/dɪˈnjuːd/ | vt. | 使裸露，剥夺 | To strip bare, deprive. | The harsh winds denuded the trees of their leaves.  狂风把树上的叶子都吹光了。| The land was denuded of its topsoil by heavy rains.  暴雨冲走了土地的表层土壤。 | synonyms: strip, bare, divest, deprive | antonyms: cover, clothe, enrich | verb
