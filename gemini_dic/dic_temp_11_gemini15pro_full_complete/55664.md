ditransitivity:/ˌdaɪtrænˈsɪtɪvɪti/ | n. | 双及物性，双宾语性 | The property of a verb that takes both a direct object and an indirect object.  | The verb "give" exhibits ditransitivity.  动词“give”展现了双及物性。 |  ditransitiveness | intransitivity | noun
