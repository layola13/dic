adjoining:/əˈdʒɔɪnɪŋ/
adjective
邻近的，毗邻的
next to or joined with something else
例句：We reserved adjoining rooms in the hotel. 我们在酒店预订了相邻的房间。

例句：The adjoining land belongs to the local council.  毗邻的土地属于当地议会。

近义词：adjacent, neighboring, contiguous
反义词：separate, detached, distant
词性: adjective
