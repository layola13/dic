field-tested:/ˈfiːldˌtestɪd/
adjective
经过实地测试的；经过现场检验的
Having been tested in real-world conditions; proven effective through practical use.
The army is equipped with field-tested weapons. 军队配备了经过实地测试的武器。
The software has been field-tested for six months before its official release.  这款软件在正式发布前经过了六个月的实地测试。
近义词:proven, tried, battle-tested, validated
反义词:untested, unproven, theoretical
词性:adjective
