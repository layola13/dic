flawedness:/ˈflɔːdnɪs/| n.|缺陷，瑕疵|The flawedness of the plan was apparent from the start. 这个计划的缺陷从一开始就很明显。|例句：The flawedness of the diamond reduced its value significantly.  这颗钻石的瑕疵大大降低了它的价值。

例句：Despite its flawedness, the film was still enjoyable. 尽管有缺陷，这部电影仍然很有趣。|近义词：defect, imperfection, fault, blemish, shortcoming|反义词：perfection, flawlessness, impeccability|词性:名词

词源:  From flawed + -ness.  "Flawed" comes from the Old Norse *flaga, meaning "slab of stone, layer of rock." The original sense likely referred to a crack or imperfection in a stone.
