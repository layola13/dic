nonstretchable:/ˌnɒnˈstrɛtʃəbl/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈstretʃəbl/ (UK)| adj.|不可伸展的，无弹性的|Not able to be stretched or made longer or wider.|The fabric is nonstretchable, so it's important to buy the right size.  这种布料没有弹性，所以买合适的尺寸很重要。|例句：The material is nonstretchable, which makes it unsuitable for activewear.  这种材料不可伸展，因此不适合做运动服。

例句：Nonstretchable bandages can restrict blood flow if applied too tightly.  如果绑得太紧，无弹性绷带会限制血液流动。|近义词：inflexible, rigid, inelastic|反义词:stretchable, elastic, flexible|词性:adjective
