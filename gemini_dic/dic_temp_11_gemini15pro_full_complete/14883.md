ayesha:/ɑːˈjeɪʃə/ /əˈjeɪʃə/| n. |艾莎|A female given name, of Arabic origin, meaning "alive and well" or "full of life". It is a variant transliteration of the Arabic name عائشة (ʻĀ'ishah), the name of the Prophet Muhammad's third wife.| My friend Ayesha is a talented artist. 我的朋友艾莎是一位才华横溢的艺术家.|近义词: Aisha,  |反义词: N/A|noun
