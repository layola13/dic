coauthored:/ˌkoʊˈɔːθərd/| v. |共同创作，合著|A work coauthored by two prominent scientists. 由两位杰出科学家合著的作品。|例句：The book was coauthored by two experts in the field. 这本书是由该领域的两位专家合著的。

例句：They coauthored a research paper on climate change. 他们合著了一篇关于气候变化的研究论文。|近义词：jointly authored, co-written|反义词:solely authored|词性:动词
