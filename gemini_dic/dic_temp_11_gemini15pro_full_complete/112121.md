longtime:/ˈlɔːŋtaɪm/ /ˈlɑːŋtaɪm/| adj.| 长久的，长期的|A longtime friend is someone you have known for a long time.  一个长久的朋友是指你认识了很长时间的人。|例句：He is a longtime friend of mine. 他是我的老朋友。|近义词：long-standing, long-term|反义词：recent, short-term|词性:形容词

longtime:/ˈlɔːŋtaɪm/ /ˈlɑːŋtaɪm/| n.| 很长一段时间|Longtime can refer to a considerable duration. Longtime可以指相当长的一段时间。|例句:  It had been a longtime since they last met.  他们上次见面已经是好久以前的事了。|近义词：ages,  a long while|反义词：recently, a short while|词性:名词
