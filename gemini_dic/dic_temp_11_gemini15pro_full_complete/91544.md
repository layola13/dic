hyper-aggressive:/ˌhaɪpərəˈɡrɛsɪv/
adjective
极具攻击性的；过度侵略性的
Excessively aggressive; showing or characterized by extreme or excessive aggressiveness.
His hyper-aggressive driving style led to several accidents. 他的极具攻击性的驾驶风格导致了几起事故。
The company pursued a hyper-aggressive marketing strategy. 这家公司采取了极具攻击性的营销策略。
Synonyms: belligerent, combative, hostile, pugnacious, truculent
Antonyms: passive, peaceful, docile, submissive
词性: 形容词
