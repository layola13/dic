enveloping:/ɪnˈvɛləpɪŋ/
adjective
包围的，笼罩的
surrounding and enclosing something completely.
The enveloping darkness made it hard to see.  包围的黑暗使人难以视物。
The child felt safe and secure in her mother's enveloping hug.  孩子在母亲的拥抱中感到安全和安心。
近义词: surrounding, encompassing, enfolding
反义词: exposing, revealing
词性: adjective

enveloping:/ɪnˈvɛləpɪŋ/
verb
包住；裹住；覆盖
to wrap up, cover, or surround completely.
The fog enveloped the city.  雾笼罩着城市。
She enveloped the baby in a soft blanket. 她用柔软的毯子裹住婴儿。
近义词: surround, cover, enshroud
反义词: uncover, expose
词性: verb (present participle of envelop)


词源: From Old French *enveloper, from en- (“in”) + voloper (“to wrap”).
