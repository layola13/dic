lorcha:/ˈlɔːrtʃə/| n.|艀，帆船(尤指中国及东南亚一带的)|A light sailing vessel used in China and the waters of southeastern Asia, typically with a junk rig, often European rigged in later times, and sometimes propelled by oars.|The lorcha sailed smoothly through the calm waters of the bay.  这艘艀平稳地航行在平静的海湾水面上。|Near synonyms: junk, sampan, barge|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
