bitcoins:/ˈbɪtkɔɪnz/| n. |比特币|A decentralized digital currency, without a central bank or single administrator, that can be sent from user to user on the peer-to-peer bitcoin network without the need for intermediaries.|I invested some money in bitcoins a few years ago. 我几年前投资了一些比特币。|近义词：cryptocurrency, digital currency|反义词: physical currency, fiat currency|词性:noun
