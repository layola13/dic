opposed to or hostile towards aliens or foreigners.
The politician's antialien rhetoric stirred up xenophobic sentiments in the crowd.  这位政客的反外星人言论激起了人群中的仇外情绪。
The antialien group protested the new immigration policy.  反外星人团体抗议新的移民政策。
近义词:xenophobic, nativist, anti-immigration
反义词:pro-immigration, welcoming, inclusive
词性:adjective

antialien:/ˌæntiˈeɪliən/
noun
排外者；反对外星人者
a person who is opposed to or hostile towards aliens or foreigners.
The antialiens rallied against the proposed sanctuary city.  排外者聚集起来反对拟议的庇护城市。
He was labeled an antialien for his views on immigration. 他因为对移民的看法而被贴上排外者的标签。
近义词:xenophobe, nativist
反义词:internationalist, cosmopolitan
词性:noun
