empty-nesters:/ˈɛmpti ˈnɛstərz/
n.
空巢老人
Parents whose children have grown up and left home.
例句：The empty-nesters traveled extensively now that their children were grown.  这对空巢老人在孩子们长大后开始了广泛的旅行。

例句：The empty-nesters found themselves with more free time and less responsibility.  空巢老人发现自己有了更多的空闲时间和更少的责任。

近义词：retired couple, older adults
反义词:family with children
