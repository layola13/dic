denotational:/ˌdiːnəʊˈteɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 指示的，指称的，表意的 | Relating to or concerned with the meaning of something, especially in a precise or formal way. | The denotational semantics of a programming language describes the meaning of its constructs in terms of mathematical objects.  这种编程语言的指称语义用数学对象描述其结构的含义。|  The word "home" has a denotational meaning of a dwelling place. “家”这个词的指示意义是指住所。 | synonyms: indicative, signifying, representative | antonyms: connotative, suggestive, implied | word type: adjective
