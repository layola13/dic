melaninlike:/ˈmɛlənɪnˌlaɪk/
adjective
类似黑色素的
Resembling melanin in properties or appearance.
Melaninlike pigments were observed in the fungal culture.  在真菌培养物中观察到类似黑色素的色素。
The synthetic compound exhibited melaninlike properties.  这种合成化合物表现出类似黑色素的特性。
similar, analogous, resembling
dissimilar, different
词性:形容词
