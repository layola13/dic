Deloitte:/dəˈlɔɪt/| n.|德勤|A multinational professional services network.  One of the "Big Four" accounting organizations and the largest professional services network in the world by revenue and number of professionals. It provides audit, tax, consulting, enterprise risk and financial advisory services to public and private clients spanning multiple industries.|Deloitte audited the company's financial statements. 德勤审计了该公司的财务报表。|Near Synonyms:  Ernst & Young (EY), KPMG, PricewaterhouseCoopers (PwC) (as they are all Big Four firms)|Antonyms: N/A (Difficult to have a direct antonym for a proper noun)|noun
