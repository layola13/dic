adventive:/ædˈvɛntɪv/ | adj. n. | 外来的，非本地的；外来物种 | Introduced from another region and not fully established or naturalized; a plant or animal that has been introduced to a region where it is not native. |
例句：The adventive weed quickly spread through the garden. 这种外来的杂草迅速蔓延到整个花园。
例句：Many adventive species are now considered invasive pests. 许多外来物种现在被认为是入侵性有害生物。
| 近义词：introduced, foreign, exotic, non-native, non-indigenous | 反义词：native, indigenous, endemic | 词性: 形容词, 名词

adventive:/ædˈvɛntɪv/ | n. | 外来物种 | An organism that has been introduced to a region where it is not native. |
例句：The island's ecosystem was disrupted by the introduction of several adventives.  这个岛的生态系统被几种外来物种的引入所破坏。
例句：Researchers are studying the impact of adventives on local biodiversity. 研究人员正在研究外来物种对当地生物多样性的影响。
| 近义词：introduced species, alien species, exotic species | 反义词：native species, indigenous species | 词性: 名词


词源: From Latin *adventivus, from adventus (“arrival”), from advenire (“to arrive”), from ad- (“to”) + venire (“to come”).
