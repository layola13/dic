docetae:/doʊˈsiːtiː/| n.|幻影说信徒；基督幻影说信徒|A group of early Christians who believed that Jesus only seemed to have a physical body and that his sufferings were only apparent.|The Docetae believed Jesus' crucifixion was an illusion. 幻影说信徒认为耶稣被钉十字架只是一种幻象。|Near Synonyms: Gnostics (some Gnostics held Docetic views)|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "physicalists" in a philosophical sense)|Noun
