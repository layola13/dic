compactedness:/kəmˈpæktɪdnəs/ |n.|紧密度，紧凑性，密实度|The compactedness of the soil makes it difficult for plants to grow. 土壤的紧实度使得植物难以生长。|例句：The compactedness of the snow made it ideal for building a snowman.  雪的密实度使它非常适合堆雪人。

例句：The engineer tested the compactedness of the concrete. 工程师测试了混凝土的密实度。|近义词：density, solidity, firmness|反义词：looseness, porosity, sponginess|词性: 名词

compaction:/kəmˈpækʃn/ |n.|压实，紧缩，压缩|Compaction of the soil can be caused by heavy machinery. 重型机械会导致土壤压实。|例句：Soil compaction can reduce crop yields. 土壤压实会降低作物产量。

例句：The compaction of the data reduced the file size. 数据压缩减小了文件大小。|近义词：compression, consolidation, condensation|反义词：expansion, loosening, decompression|词性: 名词

compact:/kəmˈpækt/ |adj.  n. v.|紧凑的，紧密的；小型汽车；使紧密，压缩|The compact design of the apartment made the most of the limited space. 公寓紧凑的设计最大限度地利用了有限的空间。|例句：She prefers a compact car for city driving. 她喜欢开小型车在城市里行驶。

例句：He compacted the trash into the bin. 他把垃圾压实到垃圾桶里。|近义词：(adj.) dense, small, concise; (v.) compress, condense; (n.) agreement|反义词：(adj.) large, spacious, loose; (v.) expand, loosen|词性: 形容词，名词，动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin compactus, past participle of compingere "to fasten together," from com- "together" + pangere "to fix, fasten."
