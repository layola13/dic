imposes:/ɪmˈpoʊzɪz/
verb
强制实行，施加，强加于
forces something on someone, such as a rule, tax, punishment, or one's presence or opinion.
The government imposes taxes on cigarettes. 政府对香烟征税。
He imposes his will on others. 他把自己的意志强加于人。
Synonyms: levy, inflict, enforce, dictate
Antonyms: remove, lift, abolish
词性:动词
