backstop:/ˈbækˌstɒp/ (US) /ˈbækˌstɒp/ (UK)| n. v.|支撑; 后盾;  保障; 阻止球飞出场外; |A backstop is something that provides support or protection in case of failure or difficulty. backstop:/ˈbækˌstɒp/ (US) /ˈbækˌstɒp/ (UK)| v.|支撑; 作为…的后盾; 阻止…恶化|The government backstopped the failing bank. 政府为这家倒闭的银行提供了支持。|They are working to backstop the spread of the disease. 他们正在努力阻止疾病的传播。|近义词: support, reinforce, bolster, underpin|反义词: undermine, weaken|词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "back" + "stop," literally something that stops something from going back further.  Originally a screen or fence behind a baseball field.
