obstacle:/ˈɑːbstəkəl/ (US) /ˈɒbstəkəl/ (UK)| n.|障碍，阻碍，妨碍|Something that blocks or hinders progress.|例句：The biggest obstacle to their plan was lack of money.  他们计划的最大障碍是缺钱。

例句：Heavy snow proved a major obstacle to traffic. 大雪成了交通的主要障碍。


近义词：barrier, hindrance, impediment, obstruction|反义词：aid, assistance, help|词性: 名词
