abstractednesses: /æbˈstræktɪdnɪsɪz/
|n.| 抽象性；心不在焉的状态 |The state of being abstracted; preoccupation with something other than one's immediate surroundings.|His abstractedness made him oblivious to the conversation around him. 他心不在焉，没有注意到周围的谈话。|近义词：abstraction, preoccupation, absent-mindedness|反义词：attention, alertness, presence of mind|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From abstracted + -ness. Abstracted itself comes from the Latin abstractus, the past participle of abstrahere "to draw away," from abs- "away" + trahere "to draw."
