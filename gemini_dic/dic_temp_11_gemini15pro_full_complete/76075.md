furoin:/ˈfjʊrɔɪn/ | n. |糠醛|A yellowish crystalline solid, C10H8O4, formed by the action of alkali on benzaldehyde.|Furoin is an intermediate in the synthesis of certain pharmaceuticals.| 近义词: (As a benzoin analogue) benzoin | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From fur(an) + -oin (as in benzoin).  The structure of furoin is analogous to benzoin, but with furan rings replacing the benzene rings.
