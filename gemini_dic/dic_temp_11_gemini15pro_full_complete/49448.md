Delsarte:/dɛlˈsɑːrt/ | n. adj.| 德尔萨特体系；德尔萨特的 |A system of dramatic expression, based on codified gestures and body movements, developed by the French musician and teacher François Delsarte (1811–71). Also used as an adjective relating to this system or its founder. | He studied the Delsarte system of acting. 他学习了德尔萨特表演体系。 | 近义词: expressionism, method acting | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps naturalism) | 词性: noun, adjective

Delsartian:/dɛlˈsɑːrʃən/ | adj.| 德尔萨特体系的；德尔萨特的 | Relating to François Delsarte or his system of dramatic expression. | Her Delsartian gestures seemed exaggerated. 她的德尔萨特式手势显得夸张。| 近义词: expressionistic, stylized | 反义词: naturalistic, unstudied | 词性: adjective


Etymology: Named after François Delsarte (1811-1871), a French singer and teacher who developed the system.
