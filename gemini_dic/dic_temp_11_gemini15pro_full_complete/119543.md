microseme: /ˈmaɪkrəʊsiːm/ | n. | 微义; 微小语义成分 | A microseme is a minimal unit of meaning, a very small semantic feature.  It is often used in componential analysis of word meaning. | The difference between "walk" and "stroll" can be explained by microsemes related to pace and purpose. 走路和散步的区别可以用与步速和目的相关的微义来解释。|  Microsemes help linguists analyze the subtle distinctions between seemingly synonymous words. 微义帮助语言学家分析看似同义词之间的细微差别。 |  Near Synonyms: semantic feature, semantic component | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym exists, perhaps "macroscopic meaning" in a contrasting context) | Noun
