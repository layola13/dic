conjurers:/ˈkʌndʒərərz/ (US), /ˈkʌndʒərəz/ (UK)| n. |魔术师，巫师|A person who performs magic tricks; someone who seems to make things appear or disappear as if by magic.  A person who practices conjuration.|The conjurers amazed the audience with their illusions. 魔术师用他们的幻觉使观众惊叹。|Synonyms: magician, illusionist, sorcerer, wizard|Antonyms: None (A direct antonym doesn't exist, perhaps "ordinary person" or "non-magician")|Noun
