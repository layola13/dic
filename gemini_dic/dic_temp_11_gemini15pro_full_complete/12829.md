associate:/əˈsoʊʃieɪt/ (US), /əˈsəʊsieɪt/ (UK)| n. v. adj.|伙伴，同事；联想；联合的|A person united with another or others in an enterprise, interest, or common purpose.  To connect (someone or something) with something else in one's mind.|例句：He is a business associate of mine. 他是我的生意伙伴。|近义词：partner, colleague, comrade, connect, link|反义词：enemy, opponent, foe, disconnect|词性:n.
