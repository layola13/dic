defloration:/ˌdiːfləˈreɪʃn/
n.
丧失贞洁，破处，摘花
The act of taking away someone's virginity, especially a woman's.  It also refers to the taking of flowers or the removal of blossoms.
The novel described the defloration of a young innocent girl. 这部小说描述了一个年轻纯洁女孩的失贞。
devirginization, loss of virginity, maidenhead loss
