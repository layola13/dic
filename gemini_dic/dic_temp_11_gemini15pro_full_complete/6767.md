anamorphoscope:/ˌænəˈmɔːrfəˌskoʊp/| n. |变形镜，变形观看器|An optical device used to view distorted images, especially anamorphs, which appear in their proper proportions when viewed from a particular angle or reflected from a curved mirror. |The artist used an anamorphoscope to create a hidden image in his painting.  这位艺术家使用变形镜在他的画作中创造了一个隐藏的图像。|Synonym:  distorting mirror, anamorphic viewer |Antonym:  N/A |noun
