discommender:/ˌdɪskəˈmɛndər/ | n. | 不推荐者；反对者 |  Someone who discourages or advises against something. |  The project's discommender cited budget concerns as the primary reason for halting its development.  项目反对者指出预算问题是停止开发的主要原因。|  opponent, critic, detractor |  proponent, supporter, advocate | noun
