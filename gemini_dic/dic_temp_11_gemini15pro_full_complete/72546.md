fluters:/ˈfluːtərz/| n.|长笛演奏者；装有凹槽的工具|People who play the flute are called fluters.  吹长笛的人被称为长笛演奏者。|Flute players, musicians|---|n.
Etymology: From flute (n.) + -er (suffix denoting an agent or one who performs an action).  Ultimately from Old French "flaüte" and related to Old Provençal "flaüt," probably of imitative origin.
