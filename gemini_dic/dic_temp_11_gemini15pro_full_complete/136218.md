nonremedially:/ˌnɑːnrɪˈmiːdiəli/
adverb
非补救地；无需补救地
in a way that does not involve remediation
The student passed the exam nonremedially.  这个学生无需补救就通过了考试。
Students who demonstrate proficiency will be placed nonremedially.  展现出能力的学生将被非补救地安置。
Synonym: directly, straightforwardly
Antonym: remedially

Etymology:
non- (prefix): not
remedial (adjective): giving or intended as a remedy or cure
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs from adjectives
