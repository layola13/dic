legally:/ˈliːɡəli/| adv.|合法地，法律上|In a legal manner; according to law.  Conforming to the law.|例句：He is legally blind. 他法律上被认定为失明。|例句：The contract is legally binding. 这份合同具有法律约束力。|近义词：lawfully, legitimately|反义词：illegally, unlawfully|词性:副词
词源: From Middle English *legalli, from Old French legal, from Latin lēgālis (“legal”), from lēx (“law”).
