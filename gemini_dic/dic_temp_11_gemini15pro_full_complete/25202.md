buddleia:/ˈbʌdliə/ | n. |醉鱼草; 大叶醉鱼草|A shrub with clusters of fragrant flowers that attract butterflies.  A genus (Buddleja) of shrubs or trees of the family Scrophulariaceae.|例句：The buddleia bush in my garden is covered in butterflies. 我花园里的醉鱼草丛上满是蝴蝶。

例句：The buddleia's fragrant flowers attract many pollinators. 醉鱼草芬芳的花朵吸引了许多传粉者。|近义词：butterfly bush|反义词:|noun
