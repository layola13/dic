bibcock:/ˈbɪbkɒk/ (UK) /ˈbɪbkɑːk/ (US)| n. |户外水龙头；室外龙头|An outdoor faucet or tap, typically with a nozzle bent downwards, used for drawing water from a pipe or barrel.|例句：The bibcock on the side of the house was leaking.  房子侧面的室外水龙头漏水了。

例句：He attached a hose to the bibcock to water the garden. 他把一根软管接到室外水龙头上浇花园。|近义词：outdoor faucet, outside tap, sillcock, hose bibb|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
