dephosphorization:/diːˌfɒsfəraɪˈzeɪʃən/ /diːˌfɑːsfəraɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
脱磷；去磷
The process of removing phosphorus from a substance, especially from molten iron in steelmaking.
Steelmaking involves dephosphorization to improve the quality of the steel.  炼钢涉及脱磷以提高钢的质量。
dephosphoration, removal of phosphorus
phosphorization, addition of phosphorus
noun
