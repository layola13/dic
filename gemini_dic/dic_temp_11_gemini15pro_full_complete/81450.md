gratinate:/ˈɡrætɪneɪt/ /ˈɡrædəˌneɪt/| v. |烤成焦黄色的，焗|To cook until browned and crisp on top, often with a topping of breadcrumbs and cheese.|例句：She gratinated the potatoes until they were golden brown. 她把土豆烤至金黄色。

例句：The dish was gratinated with a mixture of Parmesan and breadcrumbs. 这道菜上面撒了帕玛森奶酪和面包屑的混合物烤至焦黄。|近义词：brown, bake, broil, toast|反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑：undercook, burn  指烹飪程度的反面)|词性:动词

gratin:/ˈɡrætɪn/ /ˈɡrætn/| n. |烤奶酪食品，焗菜|A dish with a browned or crusted top, often using breadcrumbs and cheese.|例句：The potato gratin was creamy and delicious. 土豆焗菜口感柔滑，美味可口。

例句：For dinner, we had a vegetable gratin.  晚餐我们吃了一道蔬菜焗菜。|近义词：casserole |反义词: (没有明确的反义词) |词性:名词
