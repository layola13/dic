inter-agents:/ˌɪntərˈeɪdʒənts/ |adj.| 代理间，智能体间 |Describing interactions or relationships between multiple agents, especially in the context of artificial intelligence or distributed systems.  It signifies communication, cooperation, or competition among independent entities working towards individual or shared goals.|  Example: Inter-agent communication is crucial for effective teamwork in multi-agent systems.  智能体间的通信对于多智能体系统中的有效团队合作至关重要。|Example: The researchers studied inter-agent competition in a simulated market environment.  研究人员在模拟市场环境中研究了智能体间的竞争。| 近义词:multi-agent, distributed| 反义词: single-agent, centralized |词性:形容词
