cultirostral:/kʌltɪˈrɒstrəl/
adjective
耕作的喙；用于耕作的喙状物
Having a beak or beak-like structure adapted for tilling or cultivating the soil.
Cultirostral birds use their beaks to dig for insects and seeds in the ground.  耕作的鸟类用它们的喙在地上挖掘昆虫和种子。
The extinct dinosaur had a cultirostral snout, possibly used for rooting up plants.  这种已灭绝的恐龙有一个用于耕作的口鼻部，可能用于挖掘植物。
近义词:fossorial
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider words like "non-fossorial," "aerial")
词性:adjective
