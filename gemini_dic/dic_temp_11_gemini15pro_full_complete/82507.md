gryphaea:/ɡrɪˈfiːə/| n. |鬼爪蛤，牡蛎的一种|An extinct genus of oysters, common fossils in Jurassic and Cretaceous rocks, characterized by a curved, beak-like shell.  These fossils are often called "devil's toenails".|例句：The gryphaea fossils were embedded in the cliff face. 鬼爪蛤化石嵌在悬崖面上。

例句：He found a large gryphaea while walking along the beach. 他在沿着海滩散步时发现了一个很大的鬼爪蛤化石。|近义词：oyster, fossil|反义词: N/A (extinct organism)|词性:noun
