disarrange:/ˌdɪsəˈreɪndʒ/
verb
扰乱，弄乱
To put out of order; to disturb the arrangement of.
The wind disarranged her hair. 风吹乱了她的头发。
近义词: disorder, disorganize, disturb, mess up, ruffle
反义词: arrange, organize, order, tidy
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing reversal) + arrange (from Old French arengier, from a- (to) + rengier (to range, rank)).
