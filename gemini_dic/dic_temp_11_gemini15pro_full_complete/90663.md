humble:/ˈhʌmbəl/ (US) /ˈhʌmbl/ (UK)| adj. v. |谦逊的；简陋的；低下的；使谦卑| having or showing a modest or low estimate of one's own importance. | He gave a humble apology for his behavior. 他对自己的行为表示了谦卑的歉意。|  Near Synonyms: modest, unassuming, meek, unpretentious | Antonyms: arrogant, haughty, proud, conceited | adjective

humble:/ˈhʌmbəl/ (US) /ˈhʌmbl/ (UK)| v. |使谦卑；使感到卑微；降低…的地位| lower (someone) in dignity or importance. | He was humbled by the experience.  这段经历使他变得谦卑了。|Near Synonyms: humiliate, abase, demean, subdue | Antonyms: exalt, elevate, aggrandize, promote | verb
