It affects chemicals in the brain and nerves that contribute to hyperactivity and impulse control.|例句：He was prescribed Adderall for his ADHD.  他被开了阿得拉尔治疗他的多动症。

例句：Adderall can be addictive if misused.  如果滥用，阿得拉尔可能会上瘾。|近义词：amphetamine, dextroamphetamine, stimulant|反义词:depressant, sedative|词性:noun
