brickmaker:/ˈbrɪkˌmeɪkər/| n.|制砖工人，砖匠|A person who makes bricks.  A worker who makes bricks, usually by hand or with simple machinery.|The brickmaker skillfully molded the clay.  这个制砖工人熟练地塑造粘土。|Synonyms: bricklayer, brick-maker|Antonyms: None|noun

Etymology:  From 'brick' + 'maker'. 'Brick' comes from Middle Dutch 'bricke', and 'maker' comes from Old English 'maca', meaning "one who makes."
