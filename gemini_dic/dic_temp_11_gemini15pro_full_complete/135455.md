nonperturbing:/ˌnɒnpərˈtɜːbɪŋ/
adjective
非扰动的，不引起扰动的
Not causing a perturbation or disturbance; having no perturbing effect.
The probe was designed to be nonperturbing so that it wouldn't affect the delicate ecosystem.  这个探测器被设计成非扰动的，这样它就不会影响脆弱的生态系统。
The ideal measurement tool is nonperturbing and provides accurate data. 理想的测量工具是非扰动的，并提供准确的数据。

synonyms: non-disruptive, unintrusive, noninterfering
antonyms: disruptive, perturbing, interfering
词性: adjective
