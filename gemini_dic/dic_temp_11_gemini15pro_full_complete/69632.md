feminization:/ˌfɛmɪnɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌfɛməˌnɪˈzeɪʃən/| n.|女性化|The process of becoming feminine or being made feminine.  This can refer to biological, social, or cultural changes.|例句：The feminization of poverty is a serious issue. 贫困女性化是一个严重的问题。

例句：He noticed the feminization of men's fashion. 他注意到男装的女性化趋势。|近义词：effeminacy, emasculation|反义词：masculinization|词性:noun
