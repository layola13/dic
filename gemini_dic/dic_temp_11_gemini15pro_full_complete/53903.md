discounted:/dɪˈskaʊntɪd/| adj.|打折的，贴现的|reduced in price|例句：The store offered discounted prices on all items.  这家商店所有商品都打折出售。

例句：I bought a discounted ticket to the concert. 我买了一张打折的音乐会门票。|近义词：reduced, cheap, sale, bargain|反义词：full-price, expensive|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the verb "discount," which comes from the Old French "desconter" meaning "to count off, deduct."  Ultimately from Latin "dis-" (apart, away) + "computare" (to count).
