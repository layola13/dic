|英文解析|The state or quality of being nonceremonious; informal; abrupt; blunt.|
|例句|His nonceremoniousness offended the guests. 他的无礼冒犯了客人。|
|例句|The nonceremoniousness of the event made it feel more like a casual gathering.  这次活动的不拘礼节让它感觉更像一个随意的聚会。|
|近义词|rudeness, abruptness, informality, bluntness, curtness, brusqueness|
|反义词|politeness, courtesy, formality, ceremony, decorum|
|词性|noun|
|词源|From nonceremonious + -ness.  Nonceremonious comes from non- + ceremonious. Ultimately from Latin caerimonia "religious rite".|
