mass-produce:/ˌmæs prəˈduːs/ |v.| 大量生产；批量生产 | To manufacture (a product) in very large quantities, especially by machinery. | Factories now mass-produce cars. 工厂现在大量生产汽车。|  Many companies mass-produce toys for the holiday season. 许多公司为假期大量生产玩具。 | 近义词: mass-manufacture, churn out | 反义词: handcraft, custom-make | 词性: verb

mass-produce:/ˌmæs prəˈduːs/ |adj.| 大量生产的 | Produced in very large quantities, especially by machinery. | Mass-produced goods are often cheaper than handcrafted items.  大量生产的商品通常比手工制品便宜。 | Mass-produced furniture is readily available. 大量生产的家具很容易买到。| 近义词: factory-made | 反义词: handcrafted, bespoke | 词性: adjective (less common)
