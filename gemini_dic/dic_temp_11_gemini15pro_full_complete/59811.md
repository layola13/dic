ecstatically:/ɪkˈstætɪkli/| adv.|狂喜地，欣喜若狂地|In a state of ecstasy; overwhelmingly joyful.|She danced ecstatically when she heard the good news. 她听到这个好消息后欣喜若狂地跳了起来。|例句：He grinned ecstatically at the news. 他听到这个消息后欣喜若狂地咧嘴笑了。|近义词：joyfully, rapturously, blissfully|反义词：miserably, sorrowfully|词性:副词
词源: From ecstasy + -ally. Ecstasy from Late Latin extasis, from Greek ekstasis, from existanai "displace, put out of place," from ex- "out" + histanai "to cause to stand."
