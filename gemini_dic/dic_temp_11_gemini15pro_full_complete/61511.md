embrown:/ɪmˈbraʊn/| vt.|使成褐色，使变棕色；染成褐色；使变暗|To make brown or darker in color; to stain brown.|The setting sun embrowned the hills. 落日使山丘变成了褐色。|近义词：brown, darken, tan|反义词: lighten, whiten|动词

embrown:/ɪmˈbraʊn/| vt.|（比喻）使蒙上阴影，玷污|To darken or stain figuratively; to tarnish.|The scandal embrowned his reputation. 这桩丑闻玷污了他的名声。|近义词：tarnish, sully, taint|反义词: purify, cleanse|动词
