go-in-the-tank:/ɡoʊ ɪn ðə tæŋk/ | idiom | 放弃；故意输掉；故意表现差  | To deliberately perform poorly or lose, especially in a competition or performance.  Originates from boxing, where a fighter would pretend to be knocked out by falling into the water tank that was sometimes ringside. | The boxer seemed to go in the tank in the third round.  这位拳击手在第三回合似乎故意输掉了比赛。 |  The team went in the tank after their star player was injured.  在他们的明星球员受伤后，球队就故意输球了。 | Near Synonyms: throw a game, take a dive, fake it | Antonyms:  try one's best, give it one's all | idiom
