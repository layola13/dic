habit-forming:/ˈhæbɪtˌfɔːrmɪŋ/ | adj. | 形成习惯的；容易上瘾的 |  Creating a habit; addictive.  Something that is habit-forming is likely to cause addiction or a strong habit. | Smoking is a habit-forming activity. 吸烟是一种容易上瘾的行为。|  The daily use of this medication can be habit-forming. 每天服用这种药物可能会形成习惯。| 近义词: addictive, compelling | 反义词: non-addictive, harmless | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "habit" (from Old French "habit," from Latin "habitus" meaning "condition, appearance, dress") + "forming" (present participle of "form," from Old French "former," from Latin "formare" meaning "to shape, fashion").
