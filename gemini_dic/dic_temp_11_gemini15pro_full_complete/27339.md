camber:/ˈkæmbər/ (US), /ˈkæmbə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|倾斜，拱起；使…拱起|A slightly convex or arched shape of a road or other surface.  The action of giving such a shape to something.|The road has a slight camber to allow rainwater to run off. 这条路略微拱起以便雨水流走。|近义词：curve, arch, slope, incline|反义词：dip, sag|noun
camber:/ˈkæmbər/ (US), /ˈkæmbə(r)/ (UK)| v.|使…拱起；使…倾斜|To cause something to have a slightly convex or arched shape.|They cambered the road to improve drainage. 他们把路拱起来以改善排水。|近义词：curve, arch, slope, incline|反义词：flatten|verb
