antistrophic:/ˌæntiˈstrɒfɪk/ | adj. | 反对称的，非对称的 |  Relating to or designating the second stanza of a Greek choral ode, differing in meter from the strophe and forming a balanced pair with it; more generally, not corresponding or symmetrical. | The antistrophic movement in the piece created a sense of imbalance, yet also a dynamic tension.  这首曲子中反对称的乐章营造了一种不平衡感，但也带来了一种动态张力。|  asymmetrical, unsymmetrical, irregular | symmetrical, balanced, regular | adjective
