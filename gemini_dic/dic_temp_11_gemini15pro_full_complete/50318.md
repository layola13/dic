deplorableness:/dɪˈplɔːrəbəlnəs/
n.
可悲; 悲惨; 凄惨
The state or quality of being deplorable; wretchedness.
The deplorableness of the situation moved her to tears.  这种局面的悲惨让她流下了眼泪。
近义词：patheticness, wretchedness, misery, sadness
反义词：excellence, goodness, merit, wonderfulness
词性：名词

deplorable:/dɪˈplɔːrəbl/
adj.
可悲的; 悲惨的; 凄惨的
Deserving strong condemnation; shockingly bad in quality.
The living conditions in the refugee camp were deplorable.  难民营的生活条件极其恶劣。
近义词：appalling, awful, dreadful, terrible
反义词：admirable, excellent, commendable, praiseworthy
词性：形容词

词源：
From late Middle English: from Old French deplorable, from deplorer ‘deplore’, from Latin deplorare (from de- ‘expressing thoroughness’ + plorare ‘weep, bewail’).
