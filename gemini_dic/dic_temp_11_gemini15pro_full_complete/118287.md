mercilessness:/ˈmɜːrsɪləsnəs/| n. |无情；残忍；冷酷|The mercilessness of the dictator's regime was evident in the widespread human rights abuses.  独裁者政权的残忍无情体现在普遍存在的侵犯人权行为中。|  The mercilessness of the winter storm left many people stranded. 冬季暴风雪的无情使得许多人滞留。|近义词：cruelty, ruthlessness, inhumanity, pitilessness|反义词：mercy, compassion, kindness, pity|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From merciless + -ness.  Merciless comes from Middle English *mercilees, from Old French merci + -less. Ultimately from Latin merces "wages, price, reward" and related to the verb merēre "to earn, deserve".
