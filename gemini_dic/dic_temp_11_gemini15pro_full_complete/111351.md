liven:/ˈlaɪvən/| v. |使活跃，使生动，使更有趣|To make something more lively, interesting, or exciting.|The band livened up the party.  乐队使派对活跃起来。|The comedian livened up the atmosphere with his jokes.  喜剧演员用他的笑话活跃了气氛。|enliven, animate, brighten|dull, dampen|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *liven, from Old English libban (“to live”), from Proto-Germanic *libjaną (“to live”), from Proto-Indo-European *leip- (“to stick, adhere, remain”).  The sense of "to make lively" developed later.
