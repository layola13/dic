balkier:/ˈbɔːlkɪər/ (US), /ˈbɔːlkɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更不愿合作的，更固执的|More unwilling to cooperate or comply; more stubborn or obstinate.  Derived from the verb "balk," meaning to refuse to proceed or to hesitate.|The balkier horse refused to jump the fence. 那匹更倔强的马拒绝跳过栅栏。|Synonyms: more stubborn, more obstinate, more uncooperative, more recalcitrant|Antonyms: more cooperative, more willing, more compliant|adjective

balkiest:/ˈbɔːlkɪɪst/ (US), /ˈbɔːlkɪɪst/ (UK)| adj.|最不愿合作的，最固执的|Most unwilling to cooperate or comply; most stubborn or obstinate.|That's the balkiest mule I've ever seen. 那是我见过最倔的骡子。|Synonyms: most stubborn, most obstinate, most uncooperative, most recalcitrant|Antonyms: most cooperative, most willing, most compliant|adjective
