dismayingly:/dɪsˈmeɪɪŋli/
adverb
令人沮丧地
in a way that causes dismay; alarmingly
The results of the survey were dismayingly low.  调查结果低得令人沮丧。
近义词: alarmingly, worryingly, distressingly, disappointingly
反义词: encouragingly, hearteningly, reassuringly
词性: adverb

dismay:/dɪsˈmeɪ/
noun
沮丧，灰心；惊慌
a feeling of unhappiness and disappointment
To my dismay, I realized I had left my wallet at home. 令我沮丧的是，我意识到我把钱包忘在家里了。
verb
使沮丧；使惊慌
to cause (someone) to feel discouraged or disappointed
The news dismayed us all.  这个消息使我们所有人都感到沮丧。
词性: noun, verb
词源: From Old French *desmayer (“to lose one's power, to faint”), from Vulgar Latin *dismaius (“powerless, faint”), from Latin dis- + *maius (“power”), from magis (“more”).
