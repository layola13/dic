incitation:/ˌɪnsɪˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 煽动，鼓动 | The act of encouraging or urging someone to do something, especially something violent or unlawful. |  His fiery speech was seen as an incitation to riot. 他的激昂的演讲被视为煽动骚乱。|  The propaganda was a blatant incitation to violence. 这宣传是公然的煽动暴力。 | incitement, instigation, provocation | dissuasion, deterrence | noun
