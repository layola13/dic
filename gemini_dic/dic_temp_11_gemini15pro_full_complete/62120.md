enchisel:/ɪnˈtʃɪzəl/
|v. |雕刻，凿刻|To carve or cut into a hard material, such as stone or wood, using a chisel.|He enchiseled his name into the wood. 他把他的名字刻在木头上。|
|v. |铭记|To firmly fix something in someone's mind or memory.|The experience was enchiseled in her memory. 这段经历深深地铭刻在她的记忆中。|
|n. |凿子|A metal tool with a sharp, beveled edge used for carving or cutting hard materials.|The sculptor used a variety of enchisels to create the statue. 雕塑家用了各种各样的凿子来创作雕像。|
近义词:carve, engrave, sculpt, chisel
反义词:erase, obliterate
词性:verb, noun
词源:From en- + chisel.  "Chisel" comes from Old French "cisel," from Vulgar Latin "cisellum," diminutive of Latin "caesulum,"  from "caedere" meaning "to cut."
