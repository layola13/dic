boilable:/ˈbɔɪləbl/| adj.|可煮的，耐煮的|Something that is boilable can be boiled without being damaged.|These potatoes are boilable. 这些土豆可以煮。|例句：Is this fabric boilable?  这种布料可以煮吗？|近义词：cookable|反义词:non-boilable|词性:形容词
词源: From boil + -able.
