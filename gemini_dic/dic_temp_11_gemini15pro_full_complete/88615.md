hiya:/ˈhaɪjə/ (US), /ˈhaɪə/ (UK)| int.|你好，嗨|An informal greeting, similar to "hi" or "hey". It's often used in casual conversations, especially online or in text messages.|Hiya! How are you doing? 你好！你怎么样？|近义词：hi, hey, hello|反义词:goodbye, farewell|感叹词

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Hiya, what's your favorite color? 你好，你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
Hiya, do you come here often? 你好，你经常来这里吗？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Hiya, what are you working on? 你好，你在忙什么？
Hiya, is she still sleeping? 你好，她还在睡觉吗？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Hiya, have you finished your homework yet? 你好，你完成作业了吗？
Hiya, has he ever been to Paris? 你好，他去过巴黎吗？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
Hiya, what have you been doing all afternoon? 你好，你整个下午都在做什么？
Hiya, has she been studying English for a long time? 你好，她学英语很久了吗？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
Hiya, did you see the game last night? 你好，你昨晚看比赛了吗？
Hiya, where did you go yesterday? 你好，你昨天去哪里了？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
Hiya, what were you doing when I called? 你好，我打电话时你在做什么？
Hiya, was she cooking dinner when you arrived? 你好，你到的时候她正在做饭吗？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
Hiya, had you already eaten when she arrived? 你好，她到的时候你已经吃过了吗？
Hiya, had they finished the project before the deadline? 你好，他们在截止日期前完成项目了吗？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
Hiya, had you been waiting long before I got here? 你好，我来之前你等很久了吗？
Hiya, had they been working on that project for months? 你好，他们做那个项目已经好几个月了吗？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
Hiya, will you come to the party tomorrow? 你好，你明天会来参加派对吗？
Hiya, what will you do next weekend? 你好，你下周末要做什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
Hiya, will you be working tomorrow afternoon? 你好，你明天下午要工作吗？
Hiya, what will they be doing this time next week? 你好，下周这个时候他们会在做什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
Hiya, will you have finished the report by Friday? 你好，你周五前会完成报告吗？
Hiya, will they have left by the time we arrive? 你好，我们到达时他们会已经离开了吗？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
Hiya, will you have been living here for ten years by next year? 你好，到明年你住在这里就十年了吗？
Hiya, will she have been studying French for five years by then? 你好，到那时她学法语就五年了吗？
