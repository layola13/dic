misappraising:/ˌmɪsəˈpreɪzɪŋ/
verb
错误评估；估价过低；低估
Incorrectly assessing or valuing something; undervaluing.
He was misappraising the situation and thus made a poor decision. 他错误地评估了情况，因此做出了糟糕的决定。
The company misappraised the value of the property, leading to a significant financial loss.  这家公司错误地估价了这处房产，导致了重大的经济损失。
synonyms: undervaluing, underestimating, misjudging, miscalculating
antonyms: overvaluing, overestimating, correctly assessing
