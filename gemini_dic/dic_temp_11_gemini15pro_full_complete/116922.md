mayorships:/ˈmeɪərʃɪps/
n.
市长职位；市长任期
the office or term of office of a mayor.
She held two consecutive mayorships. 她连任了两届市长。
近义词: mayoralty
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "citizenship" in a broad sense)
词性: Noun
