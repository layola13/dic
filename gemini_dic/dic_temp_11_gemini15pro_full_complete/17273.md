bays:/beɪz/
n.
海湾，月桂树枝，隔间
A body of water forming an indentation of the shoreline, larger than a cove but smaller than a gulf.  A recess in a wall, especially one containing a window.  An honorary garland or crown bestowed as a prize for victory.
The ship anchored in the bay.  这艘船停泊在海湾里。
The bay window offered a panoramic view of the city.  飘窗提供了城市的全景。
The poet was crowned with bays for his epic poem.  这位诗人因其史诗而戴上了桂冠。
近义词:coves, inlets, gulfs, recesses, compartments
反义词:headlands, promontories
