medjidieh: /məˈdʒiːdiə/ | n. | 麦吉迪勋章（奥斯曼帝国的一种勋章）| An order of knighthood instituted in 1851 by the Turkish sultan Abdülmecid I. | He was awarded the Medjidieh for his bravery in battle. 他因在战斗中的英勇而被授予麦吉迪勋章。 | 近义词: decoration, medal, order | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑dishonor, disgrace) | 词性: noun

Etymology: Named after Abdülmecid I (1823-1861), Sultan of the Ottoman Empire.
