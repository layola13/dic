incites:/ɪnˈsaɪts/| v. |煽动，激起|encourages or stirs up violent or unlawful behavior|His speech incited the crowd to riot. 他的演讲煽动了群众暴乱。|近义词：provoke, instigate, agitate, stir up, foment|反义词:calm, soothe, pacify, quell|词性:动词
Etymology: From Late Middle English inciten, from Old French enciter, from Latin incitāre (“to urge on, encourage, rouse”), frequentative of inciēre (“to set in motion, begin”), from in- (“in, on”) + ciēre (“to set in motion, stir, rouse”).
