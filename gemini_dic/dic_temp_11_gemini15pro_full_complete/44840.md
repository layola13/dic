crustaceologist:/krʌˈsteɪʃənɒlədʒɪst/ (US) /krʌˈsteɪʃənɒlədʒɪst/ (UK)| n.| 甲壳动物学家 |A scientist who studies crustaceans, a large group of arthropods including crabs, lobsters, shrimps, and barnacles.|例句：The crustaceologist spent years studying the migration patterns of lobsters.  这位甲壳动物学家花了数年时间研究龙虾的迁徙模式。

例句：My uncle is a crustaceologist, so he knows everything about crabs.  我叔叔是一位甲壳动物学家，所以他对螃蟹了如指掌。

近义词：marine biologist, zoologist|反义词:  (No direct antonym)|词性: Noun
