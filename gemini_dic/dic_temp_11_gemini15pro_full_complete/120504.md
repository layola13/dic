minute:/ˈmɪnɪt/| n. adj.|分钟，片刻，一会儿；微小的，详细的|A minute is a unit of time equal to 60 seconds.  A minute can also refer to a short period of time, or something very small. |例句：It only takes a minute.  这只需要一分钟。|近义词：moment, instant, second, tiny, small, minuscule|反义词：hour, large, huge|词性:名词，形容词
minute:/maɪˈnjuːt/| adj.|微小的，详细的|Something minute is extremely small and difficult to see. Minute can also mean very detailed and precise.|例句：The details were so minute that they were easily overlooked. 细节如此微小，很容易被忽略。|近义词：tiny, microscopic, precise, detailed|反义词：huge, massive, general|词性:形容词
