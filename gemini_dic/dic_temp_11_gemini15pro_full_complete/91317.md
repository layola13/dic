hydropathic:/ˌhaɪdrəˈpæθɪk/
adjective
水疗法的；与水疗法有关的
Relating to hydropathy, the treatment of disease using water.
例句：
He went to a hydropathic establishment for his rheumatism. 他去了一家水疗机构治疗风湿病。

The hydropathic doctor prescribed cold baths and compresses. 水疗医生开了冷水浴和冷敷的处方。


近义词：hydrotherapeutic
反义词： (No direct antonym, but possibly allopathic)
词性：adjective
