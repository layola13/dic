embellishes:/ɪmˈbɛlɪʃɪz/
verb
装饰；润色；渲染
To make something more attractive or interesting by adding details or decorations.
She embellished her story with a few dramatic details. 她用一些戏剧性的细节润色了她的故事。
He embellished the Christmas cake with colourful icing. 他用五颜六色的糖霜装饰圣诞蛋糕。
Synonyms: adorn, decorate, enhance, ornament, beautify
Antonyms: simplify, strip, understate
词性:动词
