gleamed:/ɡliːmd/| v. |流露出，显露出(喜悦、兴奋等)|to show a particular emotion or quality|His eyes gleamed with amusement. 他的眼里闪着愉悦的光芒。| radiated, expressed, showed|hid, concealed|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *glēamian, frequentative of glēaman (“to gleam”), from Proto-Germanic *glēmijaną (“to gleam”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰel- (“to shine, gleam”). Cognate with Dutch glimmen (“to gleam, glitter, shine”), German glimmen (“to glow, glimmer”), Swedish glimma (“to gleam, glitter”).
