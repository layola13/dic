ephemeral: /ɪˈfɛmərəl/ | adj. |短暂的; 朝生暮死的| Lasting for a very short time. | The beauty of the cherry blossoms is ephemeral.  樱花的美丽是短暂的。 | Near Synonyms: transient, fleeting, short-lived | Antonyms: enduring, permanent, lasting | Adjective
