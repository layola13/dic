inheritable:/ɪnˈhɛrɪtəbl/
adjective
可继承的，可遗传的
able to be inherited
例句：Eye color is an inheritable trait. 眼睛的颜色是可遗传的特征。

例句：This house is inheritable property. 这房子是可继承的财产。


近义词：hereditary, genetic, transmissible
反义词：acquired, non-heritable
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *hereditabilis, from Latin heres (“heir”) + -abilis (suffix forming adjectives meaning “capable of being, worthy of being”).
