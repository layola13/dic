nonclinically:/nɒnˈklɪnɪkli/
adverb
非临床地
Not related to the treatment of patients; outside of a clinical setting.
The drug was tested nonclinically in a laboratory setting.  这种药物在实验室环境下进行了非临床测试。
The research was conducted nonclinically using animal models.  这项研究是用动物模型非临床进行的。
近义词: preclinically, experimentally
反义词: clinically
词性: adverb
