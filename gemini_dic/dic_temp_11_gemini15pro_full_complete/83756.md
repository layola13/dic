hair-raising:/ˈhɛːˌreɪzɪŋ/
adjective
令人毛骨悚然的，惊险的
causing excitement and fear; terrifying
例句：It was a hair-raising journey across the mountains in a blizzard. 在暴风雪中穿越山脉是一次惊险的旅程。
例句：The rollercoaster was a hair-raising experience. 过山车是一次令人毛骨悚然的经历。
近义词：terrifying, frightening, chilling, scary
反义词：calming, soothing, relaxing
词性: adjective
