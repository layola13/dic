deregulators:/diːˈreɡjʊleɪtərz/
n.
放松管制者；解除管制者
People who advocate for or implement the removal of regulations, typically in a particular industry or sector.  Those who support deregulation often argue it stimulates economic growth by reducing costs for businesses.
The deregulators argued that their policies would boost the economy. 放松管制者认为他们的政策会促进经济增长。

The airline industry saw significant changes after the deregulators removed many restrictions. 航空业在放松管制者取消了许多限制后发生了重大变化。

近义词:liberalizers
反义词:regulators
词性:noun (plural)
