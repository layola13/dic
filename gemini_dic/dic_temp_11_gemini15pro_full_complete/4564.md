alcheringas: /ˌælˈtʃɛrɪŋɡəs/ | n. |  祭祀用具，尤指澳大利亚土著居民使用的扁平椭圆形木板 | A sacred object, especially a flat, oval piece of wood, used in Aboriginal Australian ceremonies. |  The alcheringa is central to many Aboriginal Australian creation myths.  这块alcheringa是许多澳大利亚土著创世神话的核心。 |  synonyms: sacred object, ritual object, ceremonial object | antonyms: profane object, secular object | noun
