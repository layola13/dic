ombrometer:/ɒmˈbrɒmɪtər/ /ɑːmˈbrɑːmɪtər/
noun
雨量计，测雨计
An instrument for measuring the amount of rainfall.
The ombrometer showed that we had received two inches of rain last night. 昨晚的雨量计显示我们这里降了两英寸的雨。
Synonyms: rain gauge, pluviometer, udometer
Antonyms: None (measuring instrument - no direct opposite)
n.
