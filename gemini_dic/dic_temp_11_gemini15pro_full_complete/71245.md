fitting:/ˈfɪtɪŋ/ | adj. n. |合适的，恰当的；装配，配件 |suitable; the act of putting something in place; a small part or accessory | The dress was a fitting tribute to her elegance. 这件衣服是对她优雅气质的恰当赞美。 | synonyms: appropriate, suitable, apt, proper | antonyms: inappropriate, unsuitable, improper | adjective

fitting:/ˈfɪtɪŋ/ | n. |装配，安装；试穿，试衣 |the act of putting something in place; a session where clothes are tried on |  The fitting of the new windows took several hours. 新窗户的安装花了几个小时。 | synonyms: installation, assembly | antonyms: removal, dismantling | noun
