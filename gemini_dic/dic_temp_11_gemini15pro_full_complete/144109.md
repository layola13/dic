outscolded:/ˌaʊtˈskəʊldɪd/
verb
责骂得比...厉害
Having surpassed someone in scolding; having scolded more vehemently or effectively than another.
She outscolded her neighbor in the argument over the fence. 她在那场关于篱笆的争论中把邻居骂得狗血淋头。
近义词: outcried, outargued
反义词: praised, commended
词性: verb

近义词: rebuked, reprimanded
