frankforter's:/ˈfræŋkfɔːrtərz/
noun
弗兰克福特人的（东西）; 属于弗兰克福特的人的（东西）
Something that belongs to or is associated with a person or people from Frankfurt.
This is one of Frankforter's books.  这是弗兰克福特人的一本书。
近义词:Frankfurter’s possessions, Frankfurt resident's belongings
反义词: N/A
词性: possessive noun (所有格名词)

Etymology:
Derived from "Frankforter," which itself comes from "Frankfurt" (the name of a city in Germany) + "-er" (a suffix denoting a person from a place).  The "-'s" indicates possession. 来自“Frankforter”，它本身来自“Frankfurt”（德国一座城市的名称）+“-er”（表示来自某个地方的人的后缀）。 “-'s”表示所属关系。
