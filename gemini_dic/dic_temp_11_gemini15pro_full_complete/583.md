abounds:/əˈbaʊndz/
verb
大量存在；充满；富于
exists or occurs in large numbers or amounts.
Fish abound in the lake.  湖里鱼很多。
近义词:teems, proliferates, is plentiful, is numerous
反义词:lacks, is scarce, is deficient
词性:v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French abunder, from Latin abundare "to overflow, abound," from ab- "from, away from" + undare "to surge, overflow," from unda "a wave."
