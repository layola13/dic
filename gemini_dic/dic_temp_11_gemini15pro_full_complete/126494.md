myxine:/ˈmɪksaɪn/| n. |盲鳗|A primitive eel-like jawless marine vertebrate of the class Myxini, also called hagfish. It has a cartilaginous skull and a sucking mouth with teeth-like structures.|Myxine glutinosa is a common species of hagfish.|近义词：hagfish|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially other fish classes like Chondrichthyes (cartilaginous fishes) or Osteichthyes (bony fishes) could be considered broadly contrasting groups.)|词性:noun
