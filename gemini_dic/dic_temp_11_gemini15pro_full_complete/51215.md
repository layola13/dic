destituting:/ˈdɛstɪˌtjuːtɪŋ/| v.|使贫困，使穷困潦倒|Making someone destitute; depriving someone of all resources or means of subsistence.|The economic crisis is destituting many families.|近义词：impoverishing, pauperizing, ruining|反义词：enriching, supporting, providing|verb

destitute:/ˈdɛstɪˌtuːt/| adj.|贫困的，穷困潦倒的|Without the basic necessities of life; lacking in resources or means of subsistence.|The earthquake left many people destitute.|近义词：impoverished, penniless, needy|反义词：wealthy, affluent, prosperous|adjective
