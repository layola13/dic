hexameric:/ˌhɛksəˈmɛrɪk/
adjective
六聚体的，六元的
Composed of six monomers.  A hexameric protein is made up of six subunits.
The virus has a hexameric capsid structure.  这种病毒具有六聚体衣壳结构。
The enzyme forms a hexameric complex for optimal activity.  该酶形成六聚体复合物以实现最佳活性。
