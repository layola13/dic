nonmeasurableness:/ˌnɒnˈmeʒərəblənəs/ | n. | 不可测性；无法测量 | The nonmeasurableness of certain quantum phenomena makes them difficult to study.  某些量子现象的不可测性使它们难以研究。 | non-measurability, immeasurability, unquantifiability | measurability, quantifiability | noun

Etymology:
non- (Latin prefix meaning "not") + measurable + -ness (suffix forming nouns of state or quality)
From "measurable," which comes from "measure" (from Old French "mesure," ultimately from Latin "mensura")
