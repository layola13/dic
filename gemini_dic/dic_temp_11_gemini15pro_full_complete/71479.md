flammability:/ˌflæməˈbɪləti/ | n. | 易燃性；可燃性 | The flammability of the material makes it unsuitable for use near open flames.  这种材料的可燃性使其不适合在明火附近使用。 | flammability is the ability of a substance to burn or ignite, causing fire or combustion.  易燃性是指物质燃烧或点燃，引起火灾或燃烧的能力。| Example 1: The flammability of dry leaves makes them a fire hazard. 干树叶的易燃性使它们成为火灾隐患。

Example 2:  The flammability of the fabric was a major concern for the designers.  这种织物的易燃性是设计师们主要关注的问题。 | synonyms: combustibility, inflammability | antonyms: non-flammability, fire resistance | noun
