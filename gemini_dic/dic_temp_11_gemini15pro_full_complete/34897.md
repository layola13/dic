cliqued:/klɪkt/| adj.|小集团的，派系的|Forming or belonging to a small exclusive group of people.|例句：The popular girls formed a cliqued group and rarely spoke to anyone outside their circle. 受欢迎的女孩们形成了一个小集团，很少与圈外人说话。

例句：He felt excluded because all the other students seemed to be part of a cliqued group. 他感到被排斥，因为其他学生似乎都属于一个小集团。|近义词：exclusive, clannish, insular|反义词：inclusive, open, welcoming|词性:形容词
Etymology: From French *cliquer*, "to click, to clink," from the sound made by clicking glasses together, suggesting a close-knit group.  Ultimately from Middle Dutch *clicken*.|词源: 来自法语 *cliquer*，“点击，叮当”，来自碰杯发出的声音，暗示一个紧密联系的群体。最终来自中古荷兰语 *clicken*。
