actualizing: /ˈæktʃuəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
实现，实行
Making something real or putting it into action.
She is actualizing her dream of becoming a doctor. 她正在实现她成为一名医生的梦想。
He is actualizing his potential. 他正在实现他的潜能。
synonyms: realizing, fulfilling, achieving, manifesting
antonyms: neglecting, hindering, suppressing
v.

Etymology:  From Late Latin actualis "active, practical" (from Latin actus, past participle of agere "to do, drive, lead, act") + -ize.
