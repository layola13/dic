bibacity: /ˌbɪbˈæsɪti/
noun
嗜书癖; 书迷; 读书狂
An excessive fondness for reading books; bookishness.
He displays an incredible bibacity, often reading several books a week. 他表现出惊人的嗜书癖，经常一周读几本书。
Bibliomania, bibliophilism
Apathy towards reading

词性: noun
