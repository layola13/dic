gerfalcon:/ˈdʒɜːrfɔːlkən/ |ˈdʒɜːrˌfɔːlkən/| n. |矛隼，白隼|A large, powerful falcon (Falco rusticolus) found in arctic regions. It is the largest falcon species.|例句：The gerfalcon soared high above the snowy peaks. 矛隼在雪峰上翱翔。|例句：The falconer trained the gerfalcon to hunt ptarmigan.  驯鹰人训练矛隼猎捕雷鸟。|近义词：gyrfalcon, falcon|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑prey猎物)|词性:noun
