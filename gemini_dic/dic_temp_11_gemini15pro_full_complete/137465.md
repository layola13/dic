nontitular:/ˌnɒnˈtɪtjʊlə(r)/
adj.
非名义上的，非头衔的
Not having a title; holding a position or office but lacking the usual or proper title.
He held a nontitular leadership role in the organization. 他在该组织中担任非名义上的领导角色。
Near Synonyms:  unofficial, nominal, de facto
Antonyms: titular, official
词性: 形容词
