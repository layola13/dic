overfocusing:/ˌoʊvərˈfoʊkəsɪŋ/ | n. | 过度专注 | The act of concentrating excessively on one aspect or detail while neglecting others. |  He lost the game due to his overfocusing on a single tactic. 他因过度专注于单一战术而输掉了比赛。

Overfocusing on minor details can prevent you from seeing the bigger picture.  过度关注细枝末节会让你看不到全局。 | 近义词：obsessing, fixating | 反义词: distraction, inattention | 词性: 名词 (derived from verb "focus")
