hedonism:/ˈhiːdənɪzəm/ |n.|享乐主义；快乐论|The pursuit of pleasure; sensual self-indulgence.|例句：Their hedonism was legendary. 他们的享乐主义是传奇性的。|例句：He embraced a life of hedonism, indulging in every pleasure imaginable. 他拥抱享乐主义的生活，沉溺于各种可以想象的快乐。|近义词：pleasure-seeking, indulgence, sensualism, epicureanism|反义词：asceticism, self-denial, abstinence|词性: 名词

hedonisms:/ˈhiːdənɪzəmz/ |n.|享乐主义（复数）|Plural form of hedonism, referring to multiple instances or types of  the pursuit of pleasure.|例句：Different cultures have different hedonisms. 不同的文化有不同的享乐主义。|例句：The hedonisms of the wealthy elite often contrast sharply with the struggles of the poor. 富裕精英的享乐主义往往与穷人的挣扎形成鲜明对比。|近义词：pleasures, indulgences|反义词：self-denials, abstinences|词性: 名词
