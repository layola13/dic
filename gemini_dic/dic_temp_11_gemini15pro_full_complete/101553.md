ironlike:/ˈaɪərnˌlaɪk/
adjective
像铁一样的；坚硬的；冷酷的
Resembling iron in hardness, strength, or coldness.
The soldier had an ironlike grip on his rifle.  这个士兵紧紧地握着他的步枪。
His expression was ironlike and unyielding. 他的表情像铁一样冷酷而坚毅。
近义词:iron-hard, steely, rigid, inflexible, unyielding
反义词:flexible, soft, yielding, gentle, compassionate
词性:adjective

词源:
From iron +‎ -like.
