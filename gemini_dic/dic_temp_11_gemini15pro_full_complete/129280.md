nodosous:/ˈnɒdəsəs, ˈnoʊ-/| adj.|多节的；有结节的|Having or characterized by small rounded swellings or protuberances called nodes.|The ginger root was nodosous and gnarled.|近义词：knobby, gnarled, lumpy, bumpy|反义词:smooth, even|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Latin *nōdōsus*, from *nōdus* ("knot") + *-ōsus* (suffix forming adjectives of fullness or possession).
