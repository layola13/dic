outwandered:/ˌaʊtˈwɒndəd/ /ˌaʊtˈwɑːndərd/
|v.|漫游，漂泊(过去分词)|Having roamed or wandered extensively, especially beyond usual limits.|The explorers had outwandered their supplies and were forced to return. 探险者们漫游得太远，耗尽了补给，被迫返回。|Synonyms: roamed, rambled, strayed, traversed|Antonyms: stayed, remained, settled|verb (past participle)

outwandered:/ˌaʊtˈwɒndəd/ /ˌaʊtˈwɑːndərd/
|v.|超过…的漫游范围(过去分词)|Having wandered further than someone or something else.|She had outwandered the other hikers and found herself alone in the wilderness. 她比其他徒步旅行者走得更远，发现自己独自一人在荒野中。|Synonyms: surpassed, exceeded, outstripped|Antonyms: lagged behind, trailed|verb (past participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From "out-" (prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing) + "wander" (from Old English wandrian, meaning "to wander").
