elaterin:/ɪˈlætərɪn/| n.|葫芦素A; 泻根素|A crystalline compound obtained from elaterium, used as a purgative.|Elaterin is a potent component of elaterium. 泻根素是泻根中的一种有效成分。|Synonyms: cucurbitacin A, purgative|Antonyms: None|noun
