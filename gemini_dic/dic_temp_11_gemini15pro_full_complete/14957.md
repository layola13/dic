b-lists:/ˈbiː lɪsts/ | n. | B级名单；次要名单；不太重要的名单 |  A list of people or things considered to be of secondary importance.  This often implies they are less desirable, less popular, or of lower quality than those on an "A-list." |  The restaurant wasn't our first choice; it was more of a b-list option. 这家餐馆不是我们的首选，更像是一个备选方案。| synonyms: second string, also-rans, backups | antonyms: A-list, top-tier, elite | noun
