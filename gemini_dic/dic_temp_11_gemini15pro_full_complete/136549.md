nonsaccharins:/ˌnɑːnˈsækərɪnz/ | n. | 非糖甜味剂 | Artificial sweeteners that are not sugars.  Substances used to sweeten food and beverages that are not carbohydrates and do not contribute calories. | Example: Many diet sodas use nonsaccharins to reduce sugar content. 许多低糖汽水使用非糖甜味剂来减少糖分。| Example:  Nonsaccharins are often preferred by people trying to lose weight. 非糖甜味剂通常受到试图减肥的人们的青睐。|Synonyms: artificial sweeteners, sugar substitutes | Antonyms: sugars, saccharins | noun
