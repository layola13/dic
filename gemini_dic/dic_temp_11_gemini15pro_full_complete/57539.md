drainer:/ˈdreɪnər/| n.|排水器，滤器，沥水器|A device used to remove excess liquid from something.|The drainer in the sink is clogged. 水槽里的滤水器堵塞了.|近义词:colander, strainer, filter|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) dam, block|词性:noun
Etymology: From drain + -er (agent suffix). Ultimately from Old English *drēahnian, "to become dry."
