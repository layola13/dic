folkloristics:/ˌfəʊklɔːˈrɪstɪks/ /ˌfoʊklɔːˈrɪstɪks/
n.
民俗学
The study of folklore, its artistic forms, and its cultural significance.
Folkloristics examines the traditions, customs, and beliefs of different cultures. 民俗学考察不同文化的传统、习俗和信仰。
Folklorists use various methods to collect and analyze folklore. 民俗学家使用各种方法来收集和分析民俗。
近义词：folklore study
反义词：（无直接反义词）
词性：noun
