nonfluenter:/nɒnˈfluːəntər/ /nɑːnˈfluːəntər/ | adj. | 不流利的，不熟练的 | Not fluent; lacking fluency, especially in speaking a language. |  He's a nonfluenter speaker of English. 他英语说得不流利。| 近义词：halting, hesitant, faltering, stumbling | 反义词：fluent, eloquent, articulate | 词性: 形容词
