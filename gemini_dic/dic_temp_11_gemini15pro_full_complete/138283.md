notarize: /ˈnəʊtəraɪz/ (UK) /ˈnoʊtəˌraɪz/ (US)| vt.|公证，证明|to officially certify a document or signature|Example: I had to get my affidavit notarized before submitting it to the court. 我必须在将我的宣誓书提交法院之前进行公证。

Example:  She notarized the contract to make it legally binding. 她对合同进行了公证，使其具有法律约束力。|近义词：certify, authenticate, validate|反义词:invalidate|词性:动词
