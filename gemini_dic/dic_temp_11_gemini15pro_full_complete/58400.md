dubious:/ˈduːbiəs/ (US), /ˈdʒuːbiəs/ (UK)| adj.|可疑的，不确定的，犹豫不决的|Hesitating or doubting; not to be relied upon; suspect.|例句：I was dubious about his promises. 我对他的承诺表示怀疑。|近义词：doubtful, uncertain, skeptical, suspicious|反义词：certain, sure, confident|词性:形容词
dubious:/ˈduːbiəs/ (US), /ˈdʒuːbiəs/ (UK)| adj.|半信半疑的|Feeling doubt or hesitation.|例句：She gave me a dubious look. 她给了我一个怀疑的眼神。|近义词：uncertain, hesitant, unsure|反义词：convinced, certain, sure|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dubius*, from duo "two" + habere "to hold, consider".  The original sense was "wavering between two options," hence "uncertain."
