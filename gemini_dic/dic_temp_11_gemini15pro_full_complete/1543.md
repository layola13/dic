achene:/əˈkiːn/ | n. | 瘦果 | A small, dry, one-seeded fruit that does not open to release the seed. | The dandelion's seeds are dispersed by wind-blown achenes. 蒲公英的种子是由风吹散的瘦果传播的。|  The sunflower produces achenes that we eat as seeds. 向日葵产生瘦果，我们把它当作种子吃。 | synonym: seed vessel, fruit | antonym:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific botanical term) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *akène*, from Ancient Greek *ἀ- (a-)*  "without" + *χαίνειν (khaínein)* "to gape, yawn."  Referring to the fruit not opening.
