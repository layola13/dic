dislocative:/dɪsˈlɒkətɪv/ | adj.|错位的，脱臼的，移位的|Relating to or causing dislocation, especially of a joint; displaced from its proper place or context.  A grammatical case indicating motion from.|例句：The dislocative case marks motion away from a place.  离格标记了从一个地方离开的动作。

例句：The injury resulted in a dislocative fracture of the shoulder.  这次受伤导致了肩膀骨折脱位。|近义词：displaced, out of place, out of joint|反义词：in place, located, situated|词性: adjective
