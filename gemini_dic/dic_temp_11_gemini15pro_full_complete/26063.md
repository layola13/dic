bushlike: /ˈbʊʃˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 像灌木的；灌木丛生的 | Resembling a bush in appearance or form; growing in a dense, tangled way like a bush. | The plant had a bushlike habit, spreading outwards rather than upwards.  这种植物呈灌木状，向外扩展而不是向上生长。 | The trail was overgrown with bushlike vegetation.  这条小径上长满了灌木状的植物。 | 近义词: shrubby, bushy | 反义词: treelike, vinelike | 词性: 形容词

词源: From "bush" + "-like" (suffix indicating resemblance).  来自 "bush"（灌木） + "-like"（表示相似的后缀）。
