Mainz: /maɪnts/ | n. | 美因茨（德国城市）| A city in western Germany, the capital of the state of Rhineland-Palatinate.  It is located on the Rhine River. | Mainz is known for its Gutenberg Museum. 美因茨以其古腾堡博物馆而闻名。| Synonym:  (no direct synonym for a city name, perhaps a similar-sized German city like Wiesbaden) | Antonym: N/A | noun


Etymology: From Middle High German *Meintz, from Old High German *Maguntiacum, from Latin Moguntiacum, from Celtic *Magontiacum, meaning "marketplace of Magos" (a Celtic personal name).
