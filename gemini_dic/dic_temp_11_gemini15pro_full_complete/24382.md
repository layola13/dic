brininesses:/ˈbreɪninɪsɪz/
noun
咸度；盐分；海水的咸味
the quality or degree of being briny; saltiness.
The brininesses of the different sea salts varied considerably.  不同海盐的咸度差异很大。
The chef adjusted the brininesses of the broth by adding more water. 厨师通过加水调整了肉汤的咸度。
synonyms: saltiness, salinity, brackishness
antonyms: freshness, sweetness
n.
