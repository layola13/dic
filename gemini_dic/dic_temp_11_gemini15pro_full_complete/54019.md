discumber:/dɪsˈkʌmbər/
|v.| 解除…的负担；使摆脱困难；使脱离妨碍|To free from something that burdens or hinders; relieve.|He discumbered himself of his heavy coat. 他脱掉了他的厚外套。|近义词: disburden, relieve, rid, free|反义词: encumber, burden, hinder|动词

discumber:/dɪsˈkʌmbər/
|v.| 清除…的障碍；使畅通；疏通|To clear from obstruction; make free passage through.|They discumbered the road of fallen trees. 他们清除了路上倒下的树木。|近义词: clear, remove, eliminate|反义词: obstruct, block, hinder|动词
