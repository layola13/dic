lacanian:/ləˈkɑːniən/
adjective|拉康式的，拉康主义的|Relating to the theories of the French psychoanalyst Jacques Lacan.|Her lacanian interpretation of the film was fascinating. 她对这部电影的拉康式解读非常引人入胜。|近义词:psychoanalytic|反义词:None|词性:adjective

lacanian:/ləˈkɑːniən/
noun|拉康主义者，拉康学派的人|A follower or advocate of the theories of Jacques Lacan.|Many lacanians believe that the unconscious is structured like a language. 许多拉康主义者认为无意识像语言一样构成。|近义词:psychoanalyst|反义词:None|词性:noun
