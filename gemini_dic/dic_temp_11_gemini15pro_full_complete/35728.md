coccus:/ˈkɒkəs/ (US) /ˈkɑːkəs/| n. |球菌|A spherical or roughly spherical bacterium.|例句：The infection was caused by a staphylococcus.  这种感染是由葡萄球菌引起的。|例句：Streptococcus pneumoniae is a common cause of pneumonia. 肺炎链球菌是肺炎的常见病因。|近义词：bacterium, microbe, germ|反义词: (No direct antonym for a shape.  Could consider viruses or multicellular organisms as conceptually opposite.)|词性:noun
