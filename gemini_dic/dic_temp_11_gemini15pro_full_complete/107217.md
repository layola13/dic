laid-in:/ˌleɪd ˈɪn/
adjective
嵌入的，插页的
inserted, especially of illustrations in a book.
The book features several laid-in color plates. 这本书有几张插页彩图。
近义词: inserted, tipped-in, inset
反义词: integral, bound-in
词性: adjective

laid-in:/ˌleɪd ˈɪn/
adjective
(of paper) having a slightly textured surface.
(指纸张)表面略有纹理的
I prefer laid-in paper for drawing. 我喜欢用有纹理的纸画画。
近义词: textured, slightly rough
反义词: smooth, wove
词性: adjective
