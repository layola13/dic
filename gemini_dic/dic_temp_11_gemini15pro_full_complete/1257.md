accordant:/əˈkɔːrdənt/
accordant| adj.|一致的，和谐的，相符的|Agreeing or compatible; harmonious.|Her actions were accordant with her words. 她的行为与她的话一致。|近义词：harmonious, consistent, consonant, agreeable, compatible|反义词：discordant, conflicting, incompatible, inconsistent|词性:形容词
accordant:| n.|一致,和谐|Agreement; harmony. (This usage is less common.)|The accordant of their voices was beautiful. 他们的声音和谐优美。|近义词：harmony, agreement, concord|反义词：discord, conflict, disagreement|词性:名词 (罕见)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *accordaunt*, from Old French *accordant*, present participle of *accorder* (“to agree”), from Vulgar Latin *accordāre*, from Latin *ad* (“to”) + *cor, cord-* (“heart”).
