lexis:/ˈlɛksɪs/| n.|词汇，词语|The lexis of a language is its vocabulary.  一种语言的词汇就是它的词汇量。|例句：The lexis of legal documents can be difficult to understand. 法律文件的词汇可能难以理解。

例句：His lexis is quite sophisticated for his age.  以他的年龄来说，他的词汇量相当丰富。|近义词：vocabulary, lexicon|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "silence" or "inarticulacy" in specific contexts)|noun
