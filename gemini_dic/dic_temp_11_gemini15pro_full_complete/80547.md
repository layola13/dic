goodnesses:/ˈɡʊdnɪsɪz/
noun
善良，美德，仁慈
the qualities of being good, kind, helpful, etc.
She was a woman of many goodnesses. 她是一位有很多美德的女性。
virtues, merits, kindness
badnesses, evils, wickedness
词性: 名词
