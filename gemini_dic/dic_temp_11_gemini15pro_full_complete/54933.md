dispositively:/dɪˈspɑːzɪtɪvli/
adverb
最终地；决定性地；最终处理地
settling a matter finally or conclusively
The question was dispositively answered in the latest report.  这份最新报告最终解答了这个问题。
The judge dispositively ruled against the defendant. 法官最终裁决被告败诉。
Synonym: conclusively, decisively, definitively
Antonym: inconclusively, indecisively, tentatively
词性: 副词
