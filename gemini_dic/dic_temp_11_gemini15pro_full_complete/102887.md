javelinier:/ˌdʒævəlɪˈnɪər/ | n. | 标枪运动员，掷标枪者 | A person who throws a javelin, especially in competition. |  The javelinier launched the spear with impressive force.  标枪运动员以惊人的力量投掷了标枪。|  javelin thrower, spear thrower |  archer (someone who uses a bow and arrow, a different projectile weapon) | noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *javelinier*, from *javeline* ("javelin"). Ultimately from Frankish *gapi* (“spear”).
