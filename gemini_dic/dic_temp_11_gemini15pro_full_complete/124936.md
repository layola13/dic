muezzin:/muːˈɛzɪn/ | n. | 宣礼员，唤礼员 | A man who calls Muslims to prayer from the minaret of a mosque. |  The muezzin's call to prayer echoed through the city. 宣礼员的唤礼声在城市中回荡。|  Synonym: crier, caller | Antonym: None | noun

Etymology: From Arabic  muʾadhdhin,  active participle of  ʾadhina  "to proclaim, give the call to prayer."
