brusque:/brʌsk/ | adj. | 唐突的，鲁莽的，简慢的 | Abrupt or offhand in speech or manner. |例句：His brusque manner offended many people. 他的粗鲁态度冒犯了很多人。|例句：She gave a brusque reply and turned away. 她简慢地回答后就转身离开了。|近义词：abrupt, curt, blunt, short, rude |反义词：polite, courteous, gracious, tactful |词性:形容词
词源: From French *brusque*, from Italian *brusco* "sour, harsh-tasting", from Late Latin *bruscus* "butcher's broom" (a shrub with stiff twigs). The semantic development is from the sense of "rough, harsh" as applied to the taste of certain plants to the sense of "rough, harsh" as applied to behavior.
