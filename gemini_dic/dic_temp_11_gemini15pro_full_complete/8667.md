anticonsumerism:/ˌæntiˈkənˈsjuːmərɪzəm/
|n.|反消费主义|Opposition to consumerism, the equation of personal happiness with the purchase and consumption of material possessions.|Example: Anticonsumerism is a growing movement that encourages people to buy less and live more sustainably. 反消费主义是一项日益壮大的运动，鼓励人们少买东西，更可持续地生活。
Example:  She embraces anticonsumerism by mending her clothes instead of buying new ones. 她通过修补衣服而不是买新衣服来拥抱反消费主义。|近义词:anti-materialism, simple living, minimalism|反义词:consumerism, materialism|词性:noun
