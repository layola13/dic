dunman:/ˈdʌnmən/| n.|苦工; 负重者; 讨债人|a person employed in demanding payment of debts; a debt collector. | He worked as a dunman for a loan shark. 他在一个放高利贷的人手下当讨债人。|Synonyms: debt collector, bailiff|Antonyms: debtor, borrower|noun

dunman:/ˈdʌnmən/| n.|苦力; 搬运工|a person who does hard or heavy manual labor; a drudge. |The dunman hauled sacks of grain all day. 这个苦力整天都在搬运粮袋。|Synonyms: laborer, navvy,  workman|Antonyms: employer, boss|noun
