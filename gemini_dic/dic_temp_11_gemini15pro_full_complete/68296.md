falconer:/ˈfɔːlkənər/ | n. | 猎鹰人；驯鹰师 | A person who keeps, trains, and hunts with falcons. |  The falconer released the bird into the sky. 驯鹰师将鸟放飞到空中。|  The falconer's glove protected his hand from the bird's talons. 驯鹰师的手套保护他的手免受鸟爪的伤害。 |  Synonyms: hawker, austringer | Antonyms: None | Noun
