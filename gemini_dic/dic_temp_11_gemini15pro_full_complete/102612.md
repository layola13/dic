jails:/dʒeɪlz/ | n. |监狱，监牢|A place for the confinement of people accused or convicted of a crime. |The thief was sent to jail. 小偷被送进了监狱。|synonyms: prisons, penitentiaries, lockups |antonyms: freedom, liberty |noun

jails:/dʒeɪlz/ | v. |监禁，拘留|To confine someone in jail. |They jailed him for theft.  他们把他关进监狱，因为他偷窃。|synonyms: imprison, incarcerate, confine |antonyms: release, liberate, free |verb
