misclaiming:/ˌmɪsˈkleɪmɪŋ/
verb
错误地声称；不正当地要求
Making a false or incorrect claim.
The company was accused of misclaiming expenses.  这家公司被指控虚报费用。
He was misclaiming ownership of the land. 他不正当地声称拥有这块土地。
Synonyms: misrepresenting, falsifying, fabricating, distorting
Antonyms: verifying, confirming, substantiating
v.
