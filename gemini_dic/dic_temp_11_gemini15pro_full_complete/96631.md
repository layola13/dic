indument:/ɪnˈdjuːmənt/| n. |（植物表面的）毛被，绒毛，被覆物 |A covering of hairs or scales on a plant. |The fern's indument is composed of fine, silky hairs.  这种蕨类植物的毛被是由细滑的绒毛组成的。|Example: The silvery indument on the underside of the leaves helps to reflect sunlight.  叶子下面的银色绒毛有助于反射阳光。|Synonyms: covering, coating, vesture, pubescence |Antonyms: glabrousness (the state of being hairless)|noun
