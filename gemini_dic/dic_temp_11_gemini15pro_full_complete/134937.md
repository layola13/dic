nonmysticalnesses:/ˌnɒnˈmɪstɪkəlnəsɪz/
noun
非神秘性；缺乏神秘感
The nonmysticalnesses of science sometimes disappoints those seeking magical explanations.  科学的非神秘性有时会让那些寻求神奇解释的人失望。
The author emphasized the nonmysticalnesses of everyday life. 作者强调了日常生活的非神秘性。
Near Synonyms: mundanity, ordinariness, pragmatism, practicality
Antonyms: mysticism, spirituality, enigma, otherworldliness
