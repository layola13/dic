mamaluke: /ˈmæməˌluːk/ | n. | 马穆鲁克，奴隶兵 | A member of a former military caste originally composed of slave soldiers from various ethnic backgrounds, notably Turkic, Circassian, and Georgian, who rose to become powerful rulers in Egypt and other parts of the Islamic world. |  The Mamluks ruled Egypt for centuries. 马穆鲁克统治埃及数个世纪。|  The Mamluke cavalry was renowned for its skill. 马穆鲁克骑兵以其技艺而闻名。| synonyms: slave soldier, warrior, mercenary | antonyms: civilian, citizen | noun
