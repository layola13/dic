censorships:/ˈsɛnsərʃɪps/
n.
审查制度，审查行为
The act of censoring; the practice of officially examining books, movies, etc., and suppressing unacceptable parts.
The government imposed strict censorships on the media. 政府对媒体实施了严格的审查制度。
censorship, suppression, restriction
freedom, liberty
noun
