culminating:/ˈkʌlmɪneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
达到顶点的，最终的
reaching the highest point of development; being the climax or point of highest development.
The festival culminated in a spectacular fireworks display.  这个节日以一场壮观的烟火表演达到高潮。
The investigation culminated in the arrest of several suspects. 调查最终导致了几名嫌疑人的被捕。

近义词:
climaxing, peaking, concluding
反义词:
beginning, starting, initiating
词性
adjective
