grandee:/ɡrænˈdiː/
n.
大人物，权贵，贵族
A person of high rank or eminence, especially a Spanish or Portuguese nobleman.
The grandees of the city gathered for the annual gala.  这座城市的大人物们聚集在一起参加年度庆典。
nobles, aristocrats, magnates
commoners, peasants
