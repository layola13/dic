heightening:/ˈhaɪtnɪŋ/| n. v.|加剧，增强，提高|An increase in intensity, strength, or effect.|The crisis is heightening tensions between the two countries. 这场危机加剧了两国之间的紧张关系。|近义词：increasing, intensifying, escalating, amplifying|反义词:decreasing, lessening, diminishing|词性:noun, verb (present participle of heighten)
Etymology: From Middle English heighten, from height + -en.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *hauhiþō.
