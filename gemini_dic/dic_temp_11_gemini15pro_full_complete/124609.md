mousily:/ˈmaʊsɪli/| adv.|悄悄地，蹑手蹑脚地|In a quiet and timid manner, like a mouse. |She mousily crept into the room, trying not to wake anyone. 她蹑手蹑脚地溜进房间，尽量不吵醒任何人。|例句：He mousily entered the library, hoping not to disturb anyone. 他悄悄地走进图书馆，希望不要打扰任何人。|近义词：quietly, stealthily, softly, timidly|反义词：loudly, boisterously, boldly|词性: 副词

词源:
From mouse + -ly.  The suffix "-ly" is commonly used to form adverbs from adjectives.  In this case, it transforms the noun "mouse" into an adverb describing an action done in the manner of a mouse.
