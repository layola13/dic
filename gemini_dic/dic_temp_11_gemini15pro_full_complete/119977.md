millerian:/mɪˈliːriən/
adjective
米勒管的；米勒氏的
Relating to the Müllerian ducts, embryonic structures that develop into the female reproductive tract in mammals.
The Müllerian ducts are paired tubes in the embryo that give rise to the female reproductive tract.
Müllerian agenesis is a condition where the Müllerian ducts fail to develop properly.
synonym:paramesonephric
antonym:Wolffian
词性:adjective
