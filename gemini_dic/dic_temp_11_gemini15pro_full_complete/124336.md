mothiest:/ˈmɔːθiɪst/
adjective
满是蛀虫的；陈腐的
full of moths; stale and outdated, especially of something stored away and neglected.
The attic was dark and mothiest, filled with forgotten treasures.  阁楼里阴暗潮湿，满是蛀虫，堆满了被遗忘的宝贝。
The old books in the library were mothiest and smelled of dust. 图书馆里的旧书满是蛀虫，散发着灰尘的气味。
近义词:musty, fusty, stale
反义词:fresh, new, modern
词性:adjective

词源:
From moth + -y + -est.  来自moth（蛾） + -y（形容词后缀）+ -est（最高级后缀）。
