meroistic:/ˌmɛrəˈɪstɪk/ | adj. | 部分的，不完全的; （昆虫）指卵只形成部分身体的 | Relating to or characterized by merogony, a type of development in which the egg forms only part of the body of the offspring. |  The meroistic ovariole is characteristic of most higher insects. 高等昆虫的特征是具有部分卵巢管。|  Meroistic cleavage is a type of incomplete cleavage. 部分卵裂是一种不完全卵裂。| 近义词: partial, incomplete | 反义词: complete, holoblastic | 词性: adjective
