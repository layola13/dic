ETYMOLOGY->
From *mollusc* + *-oid* + *-al*.  Mollusc comes from the French *mollusque*, which in turn is from the Latin *molluscus* "soft," from *mollis* "soft".  The suffix *-oid* means "resembling" or "having the form of", and the suffix *-al* forms adjectives.
