appetize:/ˈæpɪtaɪz/| v.|刺激食欲，开胃|to stimulate one's appetite.|The smell of baking bread always appetize me. 烤面包的味道总是让我食欲大增。|近义词:tempt, whet|反义词:satiate, stuff|verb

appetizer:/ˈæpɪtaɪzər/| n.|开胃菜，开胃食品|a small dish of food or a drink taken before a meal or the main course of a meal to stimulate the appetite.|We had shrimp cocktail as an appetizer. 我们吃了虾仁鸡尾酒作为开胃菜。|近义词:starter, hors d'oeuvre|反义词:dessert, main course|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *appetiser, from Latin appetītus (“desire, appetite”), past participle of appetō (“I strive after, desire”), from ad (“to”) + petō (“I seek, strive for”).
