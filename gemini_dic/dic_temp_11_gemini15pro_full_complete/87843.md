hide-and-seek:/ˈhaɪd ən siːk/ | n. |捉迷藏|A children's game in which one player closes their eyes for a short period while the others hide, and then tries to find them.|The children were playing hide-and-seek in the park. 孩子们正在公园里玩捉迷藏。|Synonyms: hide-and-go-seek |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "being found" or "being in plain sight")|noun
