auto-infective:/ˌɔːtəʊɪnˈfɛktɪv/
adjective
自身感染的
Capable of reinfecting oneself.  The infection originates from within the body itself.
The doctor explained that the patient's condition was auto-infective, meaning the infection was spreading from one part of his body to another. 医生解释说，病人的病情是自身感染的，这意味着感染正从他身体的一个部位蔓延到另一个部位。
The auto-infective nature of the disease made it difficult to treat. 这种疾病的自身感染性使其难以治疗。

近义词:self-infecting, reinfecting
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "non-contagious" or "immunized" for related concepts)
词性:adjective
