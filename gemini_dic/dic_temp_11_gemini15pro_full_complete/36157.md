cofeatures:/koʊˈfiːtʃərz/| n.|协同特征，共同特征|A set of features that are used together to describe or classify something. These features often complement each other and provide a more complete picture than any single feature alone.|These cofeautures are useful in distinguishing between different types of cancer. 这些协同特征有助于区分不同类型的癌症。|synonyms: covariables, concomitant variables, related features|antonyms: unrelated features, independent features|noun
