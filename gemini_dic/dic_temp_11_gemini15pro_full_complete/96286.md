indifferent:/ɪnˈdɪfərənt/| adj.|漠不关心的，无动于衷的；无关紧要的|Having no particular interest or sympathy; unconcerned.|例句：He seemed indifferent to the news. 他对这个消息似乎漠不关心。|近义词：apathetic, uninterested, unconcerned|反义词：interested, concerned, enthusiastic|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from Old French indifferent, from Latin indifferentem, from in- ‘not’ + differentem (present participle of differre ‘differ’).
