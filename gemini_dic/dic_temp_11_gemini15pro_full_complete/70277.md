fiddlewood:/ˈfɪdlˌwʊd/| n. |小提琴木，美国七叶树|A type of tree (Citharexylum spinosum) native to the West Indies and South America, known for its fragrant flowers and sometimes used for making musical instruments.  It is also a common name for the American linden (Tilia americana).|例句：The fiddlewood tree in the garden is in full bloom. 花园里的小提琴木正在盛开。

例句：Fiddlewood is sometimes used in cabinetmaking. 小提琴木有时用于制作橱柜。|近义词：Jamaican fiddlewood, Florida fiddlewood, American linden|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
