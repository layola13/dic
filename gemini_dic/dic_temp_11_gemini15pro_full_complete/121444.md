misinterpretable:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtɜːrprɪtəbl/
adjective
可误解的
Able to be misinterpreted; open to misinterpretation.
His comment was misinterpretable and caused a lot of confusion. 他的评论容易被误解，引起了很多困惑。
The instructions were poorly written and thus misinterpretable.  这些说明写得不好，因此容易被误解。
Synonyms: ambiguous, misleading, equivocal, vague
Antonyms: clear, unambiguous, precise, explicit
词性: adjective
