hoariness:/ˈhɔːrɪnəs/ /ˈhorɪnəs/| n. |灰白；白霜；古老|The hoariness of the ancient stones spoke of centuries of weathering.  古老的石头上的灰白色表明了几个世纪的风化。|例句：The hoariness of his beard gave him a venerable appearance. 他的白胡子使他显得德高望重。

例句：The hoariness of the winter landscape was breathtaking. 冬日景色的银装素裹令人叹为观止。|近义词：whiteness, grayness, antiquity, age|反义词：youthfulness, newness|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *horinesse*, from Old English *hārignes*, from *hār* (“hoary”) + *-nes* (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *hairaz* (“gray, hoary”).
