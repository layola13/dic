boozier:/ˈbuːziər/
adjective
更醉的；更热闹的
more boozy; more alcoholic; more convivial and lively due to alcohol consumption.
The party got boozier as the night went on.  派对随着夜幕降临变得更加热闹喧嚣。
The second bar was boozier than the first. 第二家酒吧比第一家更醉人。
Near Synonyms:  drunker, more intoxicated, livelier, rowdier
Antonyms:  soberer, quieter, calmer
