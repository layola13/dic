ellipse:/ɪˈlɪps/ | n. |椭圆，椭圆形|A regular oval shape, traced by a point moving in a plane so that the sum of its distances from two other points (the foci) is constant; a closed plane curve resulting from the intersection of a cone and a plane that does not intersect the base. |The Earth's orbit around the sun is an ellipse. 地球绕太阳的轨道是一个椭圆。|synonyms: oval, ellipsoid |antonyms: circle, sphere |noun

ellipse:/ɪˈlɪps/ | v. |省略，删节|To omit or shorten (something).|He ellipsed the less important details from his report. 他从报告中省略了不太重要的细节。|synonyms: omit, shorten, abbreviate, condense |antonyms: expand, lengthen, amplify |verb
