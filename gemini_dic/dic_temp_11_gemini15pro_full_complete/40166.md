contentment:/kənˈtɛntmənt/| n. |满足；满意；知足|A state of happiness and satisfaction.|She found contentment in her simple life. 她从简朴的生活中找到了满足感。|例句：He expressed his contentment with the outcome. 他对结果表示满意。|近义词：satisfaction, gratification, fulfillment, happiness, pleasure|反义词：discontent, dissatisfaction, unhappiness, restlessness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Old French contentement, from contenter (“to content”), from Latin contentus (“contained, content”), past participle of continēre (“to contain, hold together”), from com- (“together”) + tenēre (“to hold”).
