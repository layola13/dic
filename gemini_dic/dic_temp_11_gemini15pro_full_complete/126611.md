naiades:/ˈneɪ.ə.diːz/ /ˈnaɪ.ə.diːz/
noun
仙女, 水泽仙女
Nymphs of freshwater, especially of springs, rivers, lakes, and fountains, in Greek mythology.
The naiades of the spring granted wishes to those who offered them gifts.  春天里的水泽仙女会实现给她们供奉礼物的人的愿望。
近义词:nymphs, sprites, water spirits
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与之对立的神话生物，例如) demons, monsters
词性:n.
