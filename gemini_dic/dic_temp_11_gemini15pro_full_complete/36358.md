coincide: /ˌkoʊɪnˈsaɪd/ | v. | 巧合；一致；同时发生 | happened at the same time or in the same place; corresponded exactly | The two reports coincided in their conclusions. 这两份报告的结论一致。| Our views coincide on this matter. 我们在这个问题上的观点一致。 | synonyms: concur, correspond, agree, match | antonyms: differ, diverge, clash | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *coincidere, from co- (“together”) + incidere (“to fall into, happen”), from in- (“in, on”) + cadere (“to fall”).
