caponiere:/ˌkæpəˈnɪər/ | n. |碉堡，掩体，有顶盖的通道|A passageway protected from enemy fire, typically one built across a ditch or moat.  A caponiere is a type of fortification structure.|The caponiere provided safe passage across the moat.|Synonym: covered way, gallery, passage, tunnel |Antonym: open ground| noun
