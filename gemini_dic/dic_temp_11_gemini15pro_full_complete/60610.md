eldercare:/ˈeldərˌker/ | n. |老年护理，老年人照顾 |The demand for eldercare services is increasing as the population ages.  对老年护理服务的需求随着人口老龄化而增加。|  Eldercare can involve a range of services, from assisted living to in-home care. 老年护理可以包括一系列服务，从辅助生活到家庭护理。| senior care, geriatric care | child care,  youth care | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From elder + care.  "Elder" comes from Old English "ieldra," comparative of "eald" (old). "Care" comes from Old English "caru," meaning anxiety, sorrow, grief, but later broadened to include the sense of looking after someone.
