deadpanners:/ˈdɛdˌpænərz/
noun
不动声色的人；面无表情的人
A person who delivers jokes or funny stories with a completely straight face, without showing any emotion.
The comedian is a master of deadpan humor.  这位喜剧演员是不动声色式幽默的大师。
Synonyms: poker-face, straight-faced comedian
Antonyms: expressive, emotional
n.


deadpan:/ˈdɛdˌpæn/
adjective
不动声色的；面无表情的
Delivered or performed without showing any emotion, especially when telling jokes.
His deadpan delivery made the joke even funnier.  他面无表情的讲述让笑话更有趣了。
Synonyms: expressionless, impassive
Antonyms: expressive, animated
adj.


deadpan:/ˈdɛdˌpæn/
verb
面无表情地说；不动声色地表演
To deliver something, especially a joke, without showing any emotion.
He deadpanned the punchline, and the audience erupted in laughter. 他面无表情地说出了妙语，观众哄堂大笑。
Synonyms: deliver flatly, say without emotion
Antonyms: emote, express
v.
