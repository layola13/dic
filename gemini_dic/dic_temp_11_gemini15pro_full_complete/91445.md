hygrology:/haɪˈɡrɒlədʒi/ | n. | 水文学；湿度学 | The study of the distribution and movement of moisture in the atmosphere, soil, and underlying rocks. |  Hygrology is crucial for understanding the water cycle. 水文学对于理解水循环至关重要。|  The hygrology of this region is complex due to the varied terrain. 由于地形多样，该地区的水文学很复杂。| synonyms: hydrometry, hydrology | antonyms: N/A | noun
