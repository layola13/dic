deeply:/ˈdiːpli/| adv.|深深地，强烈地，深刻地|In a deep or profound way; intensely.|She was deeply moved by his words. 她被他深深地感动了。|近义词：profoundly, intensely, seriously, extremely|反义词：superficially, slightly, mildly|词性:副词
Etymology: From Middle English *depliche, from Old English *dēoplīċe (“deeply”), equivalent to deep +‎ -ly. Cognate with Dutch diepelijk (“deeply”), German tieflich (“deeply”).
