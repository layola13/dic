misclaims:/ˌmɪsˈkleɪmz/

misclaim:/ˌmɪsˈkleɪm/
v.|错误地声称; 谎称; 提出虚假索赔|To make a false or inaccurate claim. |He misclaimed the expenses incurred on the trip. | 近义词:misrepresent, falsify, distort| 反义词:verify, confirm, corroborate|词性:动词
