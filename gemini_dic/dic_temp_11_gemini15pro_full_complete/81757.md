greety: /ˈɡriːti/ | adj. | 贪婪的，贪心的 |  Excessively or inordinately desirous of wealth, profit, or possessions; avaricious. | He was known for his greety nature, always wanting more. 他以贪婪的本性而闻名，总是想要更多。 |  The greety landlord raised the rent again.  贪婪的房东又提高了租金。| 近义词: greedy, avaricious, covetous | 反义词: generous, charitable | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Middle English *greti, from Old English grǣdig.  Cognate with Old Frisian grēdeg (“greedy”), Old Saxon grādag (“greedy”), Old High German grātag (“greedy”), Old Norse gráðugr (“greedy, eager”). Related to greed.
