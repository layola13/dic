crickets:/ˈkrɪkɪts/ | n. |蟋蟀; 寂静; 零反应|A leaping, orthopterous insect, of the family Gryllidae, that makes a chirping noise by rubbing its forewings together. Also can refer to a complete lack of sound or response. |The crickets chirped loudly in the night. 夜晚蟋蟀的叫声很大。


The proposal was met with crickets.  这个提议无人回应。| 近义词：silence, quiet, hush |反义词：noise, clamor, response | 词性：名词


crickets: /ˈkrɪkɪts/ |int. |一片寂静|Used to indicate an awkward silence, especially after telling a joke or making a statement that is not well-received. |He told a joke, but all he got were crickets. 他讲了个笑话，但是无人回应。|近义词：nothing, zip, zilch |反义词：laughter, applause, cheers | 词性：感叹词
