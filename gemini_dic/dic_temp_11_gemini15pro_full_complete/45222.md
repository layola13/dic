culminant:/ˈkʌlmɪnənt/| adj.|最高的，顶点的， culminating 的现在分词形式|Reaching the highest point or degree; climaxing.|例句：The culminant point of the mountain offers a breathtaking view. 山的最高点提供了一个令人叹为观止的景色。

例句：The culminant moment of the ceremony was the exchange of vows.  典礼的高潮时刻是交换誓言。|近义词：climactic, peak, apex, summit, crowning|反义词：nadir, lowest, base, anticlimactic|词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin culminant- (stem of culminans, present participle of culminare “to crown, culminate”), from culmen “summit, top”
