cabining:/ˈkæbɪnɪŋ/
noun
限制；囚禁；小屋生活
The act of confining or restricting someone to a cabin or small space; the lifestyle associated with living in a cabin.
He disliked the cabining feeling of the small apartment. 他不喜欢这间小公寓带来的囚禁感。
confinement, restriction, seclusion
freedom, liberation
n.

cabining:/ˈkæbɪnɪŋ/
verb (present participle of cabin)
住在小屋里；限制；囚禁
Living in a cabin; confining or restricting someone.
He is cabining in the woods for the summer. 他夏天住在森林里的小屋中。
confining, restricting, secluding
freeing, liberating
v.
