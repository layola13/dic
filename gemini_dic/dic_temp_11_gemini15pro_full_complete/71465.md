flameproofing:/ˈfleɪmpruːfɪŋ/
noun
防火处理；阻燃处理
The process of treating materials with chemicals to make them resistant to catching fire.
The curtains had undergone flameproofing treatment.  窗帘经过了防火处理。
近义词:fireproofing, fire-retardant treatment
反义词:combustibility, flammability
词性:n.

flameproof:/ˈfleɪmpruːf/
adjective
防火的；阻燃的
Treated with chemicals to make resistant to catching fire.
These overalls are flameproof. 这些工作服是防火的。
近义词:fireproof, fire-retardant
反义词:flammable, combustible
词性:adj.

flameproof:/ˈfleɪmpruːf/
verb
使…防火；使…阻燃
To treat (a material) with chemicals to make it resistant to catching fire.
They flameproofed the children's pajamas. 他们对孩子们的睡衣进行了防火处理。
近义词:fireproof
反义词:ignite
词性:v.
