homestays: /ˈhoʊmsteɪz/ (US) /ˈhəʊmsteɪz/ (UK)| n. |寄宿家庭，民宿|A period of time during which a visitor, especially a student, lives with a family in a foreign country, as part of a programme of cultural exchange.|例句：I arranged a homestay for two weeks in Japan. 我在日本安排了两周的寄宿家庭。

例句：Homestays offer a more immersive cultural experience than hotels.  寄宿家庭比酒店提供更沉浸式的文化体验。

|近义词：lodgings, accommodation, billet |反义词:hotel|词性:名词
