connotation's:/ˌkɑːnəˈteɪʃənz/
n.
含义;言外之意;内涵
An idea or feeling that a word invokes in addition to its literal or primary meaning.
The word "cheap" has negative connotations of poor quality. “便宜”一词带有质量差的负面含义。
connotations: nuance, implication, undertone
