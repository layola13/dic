canoe-like:/kəˈnuːˌlaɪk/
adj.
像独木舟的，类似独木舟的
Resembling a canoe in shape or form.
The narrow boat was canoe-like in its design.  这条窄窄的船在设计上很像独木舟。
The aircraft had a long, canoe-like fuselage. 这架飞机有一个细长的、像独木舟一样的机身。

近义词:canoe-shaped, elongated, narrow
反义词:wide, broad
