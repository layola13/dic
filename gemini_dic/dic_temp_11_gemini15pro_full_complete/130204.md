non-segmental:/nɒn-sɛɡˈmɛntl/ /nɑːn-sɛɡˈmɛntl/ | adj. | 非音段的，超音段的 |  Relating to features of pronunciation (such as pitch, stress, and intonation) that extend over more than one sound segment in an utterance, rather than to individual sounds. |  Intonation is a non-segmental feature of pronunciation. 语调是发音的非音段特征。|  suprasegmental | segmental | adjective
