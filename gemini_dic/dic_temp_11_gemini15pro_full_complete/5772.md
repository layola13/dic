amalgamative:/əˈmælɡəˌmeɪtɪv/
adjective
合并的; 融合的
Tending to unite or combine; uniting.  Relating to or involving amalgamation.
例句：The new company was formed through an amalgamative process involving several smaller firms. 这家新公司是通过整合几家小公司合并而成的。

例句：The artist's style is amalgamative, blending elements of cubism and surrealism.  这位艺术家的风格是融合性的，混合了立体主义和超现实主义的元素。

近义词：combining, unifying, merging, blending, integrating
反义词：separating, dividing, disintegrating
词性：adjective


amalgam:/əˈmælɡəm/
noun
混合物; 合金
A mixture or blend of different elements. A metal, such as silver, mixed with mercury, used by dentists to fill cavities in teeth.
例句：The report was an amalgam of facts, opinions, and speculation. 这份报告是事实、观点和推测的混合体。

例句：The dentist used a silver amalgam to fill the cavity. 牙医用银汞合金来填补蛀牙。

近义词：mixture, blend, combination, compound, composite
反义词：element, component
