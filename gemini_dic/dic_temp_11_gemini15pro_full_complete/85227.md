harlequinade:/ˌhɑːrləkwɪˈneɪd/ |n.|滑稽戏；闹剧；丑角的滑稽表演|A theatrical performance based on the character of Harlequin, typically including mime, slapstick comedy, and extravagant costumes. |The harlequinade was a popular form of entertainment in the 18th century. 这出滑稽戏是18世纪一种流行的娱乐形式。

The children put on a lively harlequinade in the garden. 孩子们在花园里上演了一出活泼的滑稽戏。|近义词:burlesque, farce, slapstick, comedy|反义词:tragedy, drama|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *harlequinade*, from Italian *arlequinata*, from *Arlecchino* "Harlequin," a character in commedia dell'arte. Ultimately of Germanic origin.
