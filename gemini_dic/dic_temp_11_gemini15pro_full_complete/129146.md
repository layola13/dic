no-frills:/ˌnoʊˈfrɪlz/| adj.|朴实无华的；经济实惠的；简洁的| lacking extraneous features, especially ones considered unnecessary or superfluous; basic; simple. | We stayed in a no-frills hotel. 我们住在一个朴实无华的旅馆。| The airline offers a no-frills service at a lower price.  这家航空公司以较低的价格提供简洁的服务。|Synonyms: basic, simple, economical, budget, inexpensive, unadorned, plain| Antonyms: luxurious, extravagant, fancy, elaborate| adjective
