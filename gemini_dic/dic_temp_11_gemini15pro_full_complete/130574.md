nonactivities:/ˌnɒnækˈtɪvətiz/| n. |非活动；不活动；闲置状态|A state of inactivity or lack of activities.  Things that are not considered activities, often referring to passive pursuits or time spent not doing much.|例句：He spends most of his weekends engaged in nonactivities like watching TV. 他大部分周末都花在看电视之类的非活动上。

例句：The survey asked participants to list their daily activities and nonactivities.  这项调查要求参与者列出他们每天的活动和非活动。|近义词：inactivity, idleness, leisure, relaxation|反义词:activities, action, work, busyness|词性:noun
