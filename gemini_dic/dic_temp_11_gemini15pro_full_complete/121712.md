misprinted:/ˌmɪsˈprɪntɪd/
adjective
印错的，印刷错误的
incorrectly printed
The book was misprinted and had several errors. 这本书印错了，有好几个错误。
近义词:misspelt, wrongly printed
反义词:correctly printed
词性:adjective
