barraged:/bəˈrɑːʒd/
verb
猛烈攻击，连续炮击，（用问题、批评等）围攻
To attack continuously with bombs, bullets, or other missiles; to bombard someone with questions, criticisms, or complaints.
The troops were barraged with enemy fire. 部队遭到敌军炮火猛烈攻击。
Reporters barraged the president with questions about the scandal. 记者们就这起丑闻向总统连珠炮地发问。
近义词:bombarded, peppered, assailed, inundated
反义词:protected, shielded, defended
词性:verb

词源: From French *barrager*, from *barre* "bar," originally referring to a barrier of artillery fire.
