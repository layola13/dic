gymnastically:/dʒɪmˈnæstɪkli/ | adv. | 体操式地；优美地；灵活地 | In a manner resembling or characteristic of gymnastics; with agility and flexibility. | She moved gymnastically across the stage. 她像体操运动员一样灵活地在舞台上移动。| The cat leaped gymnastically onto the fence.  猫像体操运动员一样灵活地跳上了篱笆。 | 近义词: athletically, nimbly, agilely, gracefully | 反义词: clumsily, awkwardly | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From gymnastic + -ally.  "Gymnastic" ultimately derives from the Greek "gymnos" (naked), as athletes in ancient Greece competed naked.
