kakuro:/kəˈkuroʊ/ |n.|数和字谜; 加库罗; カックロ|A kind of logic puzzle involving numbers and sums.  It is similar to crossword puzzles, but with numbers instead of letters. The objective is to fill all empty squares using numbers from 1 to 9 so the sum of each horizontal block equals the clue on its left, and the sum of each vertical block equals the clue on its top.| I spent all afternoon trying to solve a difficult kakuro puzzle. 我花了一下午的时间试图解开一个很难的数和字谜。|Synonym: cross-sum, number puzzle|Antonym: (No direct antonym exists, perhaps "word puzzle" as a contrasting category)|noun
