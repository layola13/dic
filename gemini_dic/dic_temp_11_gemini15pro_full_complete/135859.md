|n.|不繁荣，不景气（的状态）
|The nonprosperousnesses of the region were largely due to the ongoing drought.|该地区的不景气很大程度上是由于持续的干旱造成的。
|The report detailed the nonprosperousnesses of several key industries.|该报告详细说明了几个关键行业的不景气状况。
|近义词: poverty, hardship, adversity, destitution, impoverishment|
|反义词: prosperity, affluence, wealth, success, fortune|
|词性: noun|


Etymology:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "prosperous" (from Latin "prosperus" meaning "favorable, fortunate") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality)
