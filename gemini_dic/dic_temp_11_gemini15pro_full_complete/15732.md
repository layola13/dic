balbuties:/bɔːlˈbjuːʃiːz/ /bælˈbjuːʃiːz/
noun
结巴，口吃;  言语不清
stammering, stuttering; unclear speech
The child's balbuties made it difficult to understand what he was saying.  这孩子的口吃使得很难听懂他在说什么。
Synonyms: stammering, stuttering, faltering, hesitancy, halting speech
Antonyms: fluency, eloquence, articulation
词性: 名词 (n.)

balbuties:/bɔːlˈbjuːʃiːz/ /bælˈbjuːʃiːz/
verb
结结巴巴地说;  言语不清地说
to speak with a stammer or stutter; to speak unclearly
He balbutied an apology, his face red with embarrassment. 他结结巴巴地道歉，脸涨得通红。
Synonyms: stammer, stutter, falter, hesitate, halt
Antonyms: articulate, enunciate, pronounce clearly
词性: 动词 (v.)
