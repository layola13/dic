anthropologically:/ˌænθrəpəˈlɒdʒɪkli/
adv.
人类学地，从人类学的角度
From the perspective of anthropology; in a way that relates to the study of humankind.
Anthropologically speaking, the discovery of these tools is significant. 从人类学的角度来看，这些工具的发现意义重大。
The study examined the issue anthropologically. 这项研究从人类学的角度探讨了这个问题。
近义词:ethnographically, culturally
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider using phrases like "non-anthropologically" or specifying a different perspective like "biologically")
词性: adverb
