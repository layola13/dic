free-floater:/ˈfriː ˈfloʊtər/ | n. | 自由浮动者，无固定职业者，自由职业者 | A person or thing that is not tied to a particular place, job, or group; someone who is unemployed and not actively seeking employment. |  Example 1:  He's a free-floater, moving from job to job without a clear career path.  他是一个自由职业者，没有明确的职业道路，工作换来换去。

Example 2: The company has a few free-floaters who are called upon for special projects. 这家公司有一些自由职业者，他们被召集来做特殊项目。 | Synonyms: independent contractor, freelancer, temporary worker, consultant | Antonyms: permanent employee, full-time employee | noun
