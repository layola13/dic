meanwhiles:/ˈmiːnˌ(h)waɪlz/| adv.|与此同时，在此期间|In the meantime; during the intervening time.|The storm raged outside; meanwhile, inside, the family huddled around the fire.  外面暴风雨肆虐；与此同时，在屋内，一家人围坐在火炉旁。|Synonyms: meantime, in the meantime, at the same time, concurrently, simultaneously|Antonyms: afterward, later, subsequently|adverb
