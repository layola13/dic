governableness:/ˈɡʌvərnəbəlnəs/
n.
可 управляемость, 可统治性
The state or quality of being governable; the capacity to be governed.
The governableness of a nation depends on the rule of law. 一个国家的可治理性取决于法治。
The new leader questioned the governableness of the rebellious province.  新领导人质疑叛乱省份的可治理性。

manageability, controllability, tractability
ungovernableness, intractability
