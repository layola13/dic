hearable:/ˈhɪərəbl/| adj.|听得见的，可听见的|Something that is hearable can be heard.  It refers to sounds that are loud enough or clear enough to be perceived by the human ear.|The music was barely hearable over the noise of the traffic.  音乐在交通噪音中几乎听不见。|Synonyms: audible, perceptible, discernible|Antonyms: inaudible, imperceptible, faint|Adjective
