organophosphate:/ɔːrˌɡænəˈfɒsfeɪt/ |n.|有机磷酸酯；有机磷化合物|An organophosphate (sometimes abbreviated OP) is the general name for esters of phosphoric acid. Many of these are highly toxic, especially the nerve agents.  They are used widely as insecticides, herbicides, and nerve agents.|Organophosphates are commonly used in agriculture to control pests. 有机磷酸酯常用于农业害虫控制。|Exposure to organophosphates can cause serious health problems. 接触有机磷酸酯会导致严重的健康问题。|近义词:phosphoric acid ester,  OP compound|反义词: (No direct antonym, but one might consider "non-toxic pesticide" in specific contexts)|词性: noun
