ideogrammatic:/ˌaɪdiəɡrəˈmætɪk/ | adj. |表意文字的; 象形文字的| Relating to or expressed by ideograms (a written character symbolizing the idea of a thing without indicating the sounds used to say it). |  Chinese characters are ideogrammatic, representing ideas rather than sounds. 汉字是表意的，代表的是意义而不是声音。

The ancient Egyptian hieroglyphs were also largely ideogrammatic. 古埃及的象形文字也主要是表意的。| 近义词: symbolic, pictorial | 反义词: phonetic, alphabetic | 词性: adjective

词源:  From ideogram + -atic.  "Ideogram" comes from  Greek *idéa* (idea) + *grámma* (something written).
