auncel:/ˈɔːnsəl/| n.|盎司（旧时重量单位，相当于十六分之一磅）|A unit of weight, especially for precious metals, equal to one sixteenth of a pound. |The gold chain weighed three auncel. 这条金链重三盎司（旧制）。|Synonyms: ounce, dram|Antonyms: ton, pound|noun
