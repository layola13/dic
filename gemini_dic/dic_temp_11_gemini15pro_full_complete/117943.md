membrane:/ˈmɛmbreɪn/ (US), /ˈmɛmbreɪn/ (UK)| n.|膜，薄膜|A thin, pliable sheet or layer of animal or vegetable tissue, serving to line an organ, connect parts, etc.|The cell membrane controls what enters and leaves the cell. 细胞膜控制物质进出细胞。|synonyms: film, layer, sheet, skin|antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "thick wall" in some contexts)|noun
