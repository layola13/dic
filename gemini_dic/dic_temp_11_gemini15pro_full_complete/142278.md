orrery: /ˈɔːrəri/ (US), /ˈɒrəri/ (UK)
n.
太阳系仪，行星仪
A mechanical model of the solar system, or of the sun, earth, and moon, used to represent their relative positions and motions.
The museum has a beautiful orrery on display.  博物馆展出了一架精美的太阳系仪。
The orrery clearly demonstrated the planets' orbits.  太阳系仪清楚地展示了行星的轨道。
synonyms: planetarium (when referring to a large, immersive display), astronomical model
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but one could consider "static display" or "illustration" as contrasting concepts.)
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Charles Boyle, 4th Earl of Orrery, who commissioned the construction of one in the early 18th century.
