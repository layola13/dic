overgrieved:/ˌoʊvərˈɡriːvd/
adjective
过度悲伤的
Excessively or inappropriately grieved; overwhelmed by grief.  Experiencing more grief than is considered normal or healthy.
She was overgrieved by the loss of her pet hamster, refusing to leave her room for days. 她因为失去宠物仓鼠而过度悲伤，好几天不愿离开房间。
He felt overgrieved, almost paralyzed by the news of his friend's sudden death.  他感到过度悲伤，几乎被朋友突然去世的消息惊呆了。
近义词: distraught, inconsolable, overwhelmed with grief
反义词: unmoved, indifferent, apathetic
词性: adjective

词源:
"over-" (prefix) + "grieved" (past participle of "grieve")
"over-"  表示 "过度"，"过分"
"grieve" 来自古法语 "grever"，意为 "使沉重，使悲伤"，最终源于拉丁语 "gravis"，意为 "沉重的"。
