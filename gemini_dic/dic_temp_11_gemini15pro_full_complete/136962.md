nonspectacular:/ˌnɒnspekˈtækjʊlə(r)/ | /ˌnɑːnspekˈtækjələr/ | adj. | 平凡的；不引人注目的；不壮观的 | Not spectacular; ordinary or unimpressive. |  The scenery was pleasant but nonspectacular.  风景宜人，但并不壮观。|  The team's performance was rather nonspectacular. 团队的表现相当平庸。 | 近义词: ordinary, commonplace, unimpressive, mundane, pedestrian | 反义词: spectacular, impressive, remarkable, extraordinary | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non "not."
spectacular (adjective): From Latin spectaculum "a show, spectacle," from spectare "to look at, watch, behold," frequentative of specere "to look at."
