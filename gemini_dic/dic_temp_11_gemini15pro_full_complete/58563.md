dukhobors:/duːkəˈbɔːrz/
|n.|杜霍波尔派教徒|A member of a Christian sect originating in Russia, characterized by pacifism and communal living.|The Dukhobors migrated to Canada in the late 19th and early 20th centuries. 杜霍波尔派教徒在19世纪末和20世纪初移民到加拿大。|Synonyms: spirit wrestlers (an early name), pacifists|Antonyms: militants|noun
