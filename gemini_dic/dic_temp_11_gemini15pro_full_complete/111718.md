locomotion:/ˌloʊkəˈmoʊʃən/| n. |移动; 运动; 位移|The act or power of moving from place to place.|Amoebas achieve locomotion by changing their shape. 变形虫通过改变形状来移动。|movement, travel, motion|stasis, immobility|noun

locomotions:/ˌloʊkəˈmoʊʃənz/| n. |(各种)运动方式; 位移方式|Plural form of locomotion.|Different animals have evolved various locomotions to suit their environments. 不同的动物进化出各种运动方式以适应它们的环境。|movements, travels, motions|stagnations, immobilities|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English: from late Latin *locomotion-, from locomot- ‘moved from its place’, from the verb locomovere, from locus ‘place’ + movere ‘to move’.
