glutenous:/ˈɡluːtənəs/ | adj. | 糯米的；黏性的 | Containing or resembling gluten; sticky or gummy. | The mochi ice cream has a wonderfully glutenous texture.  麻糬冰淇淋有着很棒的糯米质地。| sticky, gummy, glutinous, viscous | non-sticky, dry, crumbly | adjective

glutenous:/ˈɡluːtənəs/ | adj. | (informal) 贪吃的；嘴馋的 | Excessively fond of food; greedy. | He has a glutenous appetite for sweets. 他对甜食有着贪婪的胃口。 | greedy, gluttonous, voracious | abstemious, moderate | adjective
