divinely:/dɪˈvaɪnli/| adv.|神圣地；极其地；非常好地|In a way that relates to or comes from God or a god; extremely well; wonderfully|例句：She sang divinely. 她唱得非常好。|近义词：wonderfully, beautifully, exquisitely, superbly, heavenly, marvelously|反义词：poorly, awfully, terribly|词性:副词
词源: From Middle English *divinely, from Old French divinement, from Late Latin dīvīnālis, from Latin dīvīnus (“divine”) + -ment (“-ly”).
