cross-trainers: /ˈkrɒsˌtreɪnəz/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsˌtreɪnərz/ (US)| n. |交叉训练鞋；综合训练鞋|Athletic shoes designed for a variety of activities, rather than specializing in one, such as running or tennis.|例句：I bought a new pair of cross-trainers for the gym. 我买了一双新的综合训练鞋去健身房。

例句：Cross-trainers provide good support for lateral movement. 交叉训练鞋为横向运动提供了良好的支撑。

近义词：training shoes, athletic shoes, sneakers|反义词:|词性:noun


cross-trainer: /ˈkrɒsˌtreɪnər/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsˌtreɪnər/ (US)| n. |交叉训练者；综合训练者|A person who engages in cross-training.|例句：He's a dedicated cross-trainer, incorporating swimming, cycling, and running into his routine. 他是一位专注的交叉训练者，将游泳、骑自行车和跑步纳入他的日常训练。

例句：Cross-trainers often have a lower risk of injury. 交叉训练者通常受伤的风险较低。

近义词：athlete, sportsman, sportswoman|反义词:specialist|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"cross-" + "trainer"
"cross-"  meaning across, combined, or involving different activities.
"trainer" refers to a shoe designed for athletic training.
