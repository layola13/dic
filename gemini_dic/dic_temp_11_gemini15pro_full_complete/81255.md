granita:/ɡrəˈniːtə/
n.
冰沙，刨冰
A coarse, granular ice similar to sorbet, but made with sugar, water, and various flavorings, such as fruit juice, coffee, or nuts.
We enjoyed a refreshing lemon granita after dinner. 我们晚餐后享用了清爽的柠檬冰沙。
The cafe offered a variety of granitas, including almond and coffee. 这家咖啡馆提供各种各样的冰沙，包括杏仁和咖啡口味的。
synonyms: sorbet, shaved ice, Italian ice
antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider "hot beverage")
noun
