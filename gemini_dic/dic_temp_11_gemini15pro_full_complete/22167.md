bombast:/ˈbɒmbæst/ (UK) /ˈbɑːmbæst/ (US)| n.|夸大的言辞；浮夸的语言|High-sounding language with little meaning, used to impress people.|His speech was full of bombast, but lacked substance. 他的演讲充满了夸夸其谈，但缺乏实质内容。|近义词：bluster, rhetoric, hyperbole|反义词：understatement, simplicity|词性: noun

bombastic:/bɒmˈbæstɪk/ (UK) /bɑːmˈbæstɪk/ (US)| adj.|夸大的；言辞浮夸的|Using high-sounding but meaningless language.|The politician's bombastic pronouncements failed to impress the voters.  这位政客夸夸其谈的声明未能打动选民。|近义词：inflated, pompous, overblown|反义词：understated, simple, modest|词性: adjective
