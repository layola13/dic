nonthirstiest:/ˌnɒnˈθɜːstiɪst/
adjective
最不渴的
Not the most thirsty; less thirsty than others.
He was the nonthirstiest person in the marathon, having just drunk a gallon of water. 他是马拉松比赛中最不渴的人，因为他刚刚喝了一加仑水。
Near synonyms: less thirsty, not as thirsty
Antonyms: thirstiest, most thirsty
word class: adjective
