nonfrictions:/ˌnɑːnˈfrɪkʃənz/| n.|无摩擦，无摩擦力|absence of friction; a hypothetical situation where there is no resistance to motion between surfaces.|The engineer designed a system with near nonfrictions to minimize energy loss.|近义词：smoothness, lubrication|反义词:friction, resistance|词性:名词

nonfriction:/ˌnɑːnˈfrɪkʃən/| adj.|无摩擦的|characterized by the absence of friction.|The nonfriction surface allowed the object to glide effortlessly.|近义词：frictionless, smooth|反义词:frictional, rough|词性:形容词

词源: non- + friction.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "absence of," and "friction" refers to the resistance that one surface or object encounters when moving over another.
