abating:/əˈbeɪtɪŋ/
verb
减轻，减少，缓和
becoming less strong or intense
The storm is abating.  暴风雨正在减弱。
The pain in his leg was slowly abating. 他腿上的疼痛慢慢减轻了。
subsiding, lessening, decreasing, diminishing, waning
increasing, intensifying, escalating, aggravating
v.
