combustive:/kəmˈbʌstɪv/| adj.|燃烧的；易燃的；易激动的|Relating to or causing combustion; easily ignited; easily roused to excitement or anger.|例句：The combustive material caught fire easily. 易燃材料很容易着火。|近义词：flammable, incendiary, explosive, inflammable|反义词：nonflammable, fire-resistant|词性:形容词
combustion:/kəmˈbʌʃtʃən/| n.|燃烧；氧化；骚动|The process of burning something; rapid chemical combination of a substance with oxygen, involving the production of heat and light.|例句：The combustion of fossil fuels releases harmful gases into the atmosphere. 化石燃料的燃烧向大气中释放有害气体。|近义词：burning, ignition, oxidation|反义词：extinguishment|词性:名词
