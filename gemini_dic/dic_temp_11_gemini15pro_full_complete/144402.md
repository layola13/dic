outstrive: /ˌaʊtˈstraɪv/
verb
胜过，超越
to strive harder than; surpass in striving.
He outstrove all his rivals to win the championship. 他胜过所有对手，赢得了冠军。
The team outstrove their opponents in the final minutes of the game.  该队在比赛的最后几分钟超越了对手。
近义词: outdo, surpass, excel, outstrip
反义词: fail, lose, surrender
词性: verb


词源:
From out- + strive.  Out- is a prefix meaning "beyond," "more than," or "outside." Strive comes from the Old French word "estriver," meaning "to contend" or "to struggle."
