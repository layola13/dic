neuroprosthetics:/ˌnʊəroʊprɑːˈθɛtɪks/ | n. | 神经义肢；神经修复术 |  A field of biomedical engineering concerned with developing neural implants that restore or supplement lost motor, sensory, or cognitive functions. | Researchers are making great strides in the field of neuroprosthetics, offering hope to those with paralysis.  研究人员在神经义肢领域取得了巨大进展，为瘫痪患者带来了希望。 |  Synonyms: neural implants, brain-computer interfaces, bionic limbs | Antonyms:  N/A (no direct antonym) | noun
