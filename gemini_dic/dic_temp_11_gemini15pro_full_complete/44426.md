cross-post:/ˈkrɒsˌpəʊst/ (British) /ˈkrɔːsˌpoʊst/ (American)| v. n.|交叉发布，跨平台发布|To publish the same piece of content in multiple online locations, such as different social media platforms or forums.|I cross-posted my article on Facebook and Twitter to reach a wider audience. 我把我的文章交叉发布在脸书和推特上，以接触更广泛的受众。|近义词：share, repost, syndicate|反义词: (No direct antonym)|verb, noun

PHRASE->
cross-post to = 交叉发布到 (Cross-post this article to Facebook. 把这篇文章交叉发布到脸书。)
cross-post from = 从...交叉发布 (I cross-posted this from my blog. 我从我的博客交叉发布了这篇文章。)
cross-post something on = 在...上交叉发布 (She cross-posted the photo on Instagram. 她在Instagram上交叉发布了这张照片。)
automatic cross-posting = 自动交叉发布 (He set up automatic cross-posting between his Twitter and Facebook accounts. 他设置了推特和脸书账户之间的自动交叉发布。)
cross-post a link = 交叉发布链接 (I will cross-post a link to the article on my social media. 我将在我的社交媒体上交叉发布文章的链接。)
