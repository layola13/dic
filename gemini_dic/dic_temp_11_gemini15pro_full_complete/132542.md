nondevotionally:/ˌnɒndɪˈvəʊʃənəli/| adv.|不虔诚地，不热诚地|Without devotion or piety; in a way that lacks religious fervor or commitment.  It also describes actions done without dedication or enthusiasm.|He attended church nondevotionally, his mind wandering during the sermon.  他心不在焉地参加了教堂礼拜，在布道过程中思绪飘忽。|Synonyms: irreverently, perfunctorily, half-heartedly, apathetically|Antonyms: devoutly, piously, reverently, zealously|词性:副词
