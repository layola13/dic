misnumbers:/mɪsˈnʌmbərz/

misnumber:/ˌmɪsˈnʌmbər/
| n. |错误的编号|An incorrect number.|There was a misnumber on the invoice. 发票上的编号有误。|近义词：error, mistake|反义词: correct number|名词

词源：mis- (prefix meaning "wrong" or "badly") + number (verb meaning "to give a number to")
