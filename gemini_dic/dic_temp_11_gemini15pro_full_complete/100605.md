intimateness:/ˈɪntɪmɪtnəs/| n.|亲密，亲昵，密切|The intimacy of their friendship was evident. 他们的友谊非常亲密。|例句：There was an intimacy between them that went beyond friendship. 他们之间有一种超越友谊的亲密关系。

例句：She cherished the intimacy of their late-night conversations. 她珍惜他们深夜交谈的亲密时光。

近义词：closeness, familiarity, nearness|反义词: distance, remoteness, formality|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin intimitas, from intimus ‘inmost’.
