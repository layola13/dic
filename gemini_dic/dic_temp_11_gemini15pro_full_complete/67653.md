extroversions: /ˌɛkstrəˈvɜːrʒənz/ /ˌɛkstroʊˈvɜːrʒənz/
noun
外向性，外向性格
The tendency or characteristic of being socially outgoing and deriving energy from interacting with others.  The plural form indicates multiple instances or types of this trait.
Her extroversions made her a natural leader. 她的外向性格使她自然而然地成为了一名领导者。
Social gatherings amplified her extroversions. 社交聚会放大了她的外向性格。
synonyms: outgoingness, sociability, gregariousness
antonyms: introversion, shyness, reserve
