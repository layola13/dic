decollate:/diːˈkɒleɪt/
verb
斩首，砍头
To sever the head from the body; behead.
The executioner decollated the prisoner.  刽子手将囚犯斩首。
近义词: behead, decapitate
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 "spare" (饶恕) 或 "preserve" (保存))
词性: verb

decollate:/diːˈkɒleɪt/
adjective
(植物学) 无头的，去顶的
(of a plant, shell, etc.) lacking an apex or tip
The decollate snail has a broken or eroded spire.  这只断头蜗牛的螺旋部破损或被侵蚀了。
近义词: truncated, headless
反义词: complete, whole
词性: adjective
