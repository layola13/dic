gaucho:/ˈɡaʊtʃoʊ/ (US), /ˈɡaʊtʃəʊ/ (UK)| n.| 牛仔，牧人（尤指南美潘帕斯草原上的）|A skilled horseman, adept at herding cattle, particularly in the pampas of South America.  A gaucho's life is often romanticized as one of freedom and adventure.|The gaucho skillfully lassoed the runaway calf.|近义词：cowboy, herdsman, horseman|反义词:city dweller|noun
