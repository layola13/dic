manbote:/ˈmænˌboʊt/| n.|人命赔偿金；赎人费|A payment made to the family of a murdered person, especially in Anglo-Saxon law, as compensation for the loss of life.|The manbote was a substantial sum, reflecting the value placed on human life in that society.|近义词：wergild, blood money, compensation|反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps 'punishment' as the opposite consequence for the perpetrator)|名词
