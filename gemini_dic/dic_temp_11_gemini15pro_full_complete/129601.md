non-compoundable:/ˌnɒn kəmˈpaʊndəbl/ (US) /ˌnɒn kəmˈpaʊndəbl/ (UK)| adj.|不可复合的，不能和解的|Not able to be settled by compromise or agreement, especially of a legal offense.|例句：The offense was deemed non-compoundable, so the case proceeded to trial.  这起违法行为被认为是不可和解的，因此案件进入了审判程序。

例句：Certain traffic violations, like drunk driving, are non-compoundable.  某些交通违规行为，例如酒后驾驶，是不可和解的。|近义词：uncompromising, irreconcilable, inflexible|反义词：compoundable, negotiable, flexible|词性:形容词
