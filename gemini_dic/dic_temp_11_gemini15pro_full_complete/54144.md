disengagement's:/dɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənts/
n.|脱离，解脱，撤离|The process of withdrawing from involvement or connection.  It refers to the act of becoming free or detached from something.|
例句：The disengagement's impact on the local economy was significant.  脱离对当地经济的影响是巨大的。

例句：The military announced its disengagement's from the conflict zone.  军方宣布脱离冲突地区。
近义词：separation, withdrawal, detachment, extrication
反义词：engagement, involvement, connection
词性: 名词 (Noun)

ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (not, opposite of) + engagement.  "Engagement" comes from Old French "engager," meaning to pledge or promise.
