carolling: /ˈkærəlɪŋ/ (US) /ˈkærəlɪŋ/ (UK)
n.
圣诞颂歌演唱
The act of singing carols, especially door-to-door during the Christmas season.
例句：We went carolling around the neighborhood last night. 昨晚我们到附近社区唱圣诞颂歌。
例句：The sound of carolling filled the air. 圣诞颂歌的声音弥漫在空气中。
近义词：carol singing, Christmas carolling
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps silence or quiet)
词性：名词
