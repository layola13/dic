feasted:/ˈfiːstɪd/| v.| 宴请；尽情享受；饱餐 |  enjoyed a large meal; celebrated with a large meal. | We feasted on roast turkey and all the trimmings. 我们享用了烤火鸡和各种配菜。|  celebrated, dined, indulged, revelled | starved, abstained | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English festen, from Old French fester (“to attach, fix, fasten”), from Late Latin festare (“to fasten, fix”), from Latin festus (“festive, solemn, joyful”).  The semantic shift is from "holding a celebration" to "partaking in a feast".
