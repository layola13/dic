half-ingeniously:/ˌhɑːf ɪnˈdʒiːniəsli/
| adv. | 半巧妙地，略带几分精巧地 |  In a way that is somewhat clever or resourceful, but not entirely. | He solved the problem half-ingeniously, using a method that was effective but not particularly elegant. 他半巧妙地解决了这个问题，使用的方法有效但不特别优雅。| synonyms: partly cleverly, somewhat inventively | antonyms: clumsily, awkwardly | adverb

Etymology:
"half-" (Old English healf) + "ingeniously" (from "ingenious," ultimately from Latin "ingeniosus," meaning "gifted with genius")
