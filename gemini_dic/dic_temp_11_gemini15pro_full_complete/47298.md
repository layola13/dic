de-ices:/diːˈaɪsɪz/
verb
除冰
removes ice from something.
The airport crew de-ices the planes before takeoff in winter.  机场工作人员在冬天起飞前给飞机除冰。
The captain de-iced the ship's deck. 船长给船的甲板除冰。
Synonym: thaw, defrost
Antonym: freeze, ice up
v.

de-icer:/diːˈaɪsər/
noun
除冰器；除冰剂
a substance or device used to remove ice.
The pilot sprayed de-icer on the wings. 飞行员在机翼上喷洒了除冰剂。
They used a de-icer to clear the windshield. 他们用除冰器清理挡风玻璃。
Synonym: antifreeze, thawing agent
Antonym: freezing agent
n.
