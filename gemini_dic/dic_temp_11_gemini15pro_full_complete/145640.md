overcontentedly:/ˌoʊvər.kənˈtɛn.tɪd.li/
adverb
过于满足地，非常心满意足地
excessively contentedly; in a manner expressing extreme satisfaction or happiness.
She sighed overcontentedly after finishing her meal. 她吃完饭后心满意足地叹了口气。
Near synonyms: blissfully, ecstatically, radiantly
Antonyms: discontentedly, unhappily, miserably
词性: 副词
