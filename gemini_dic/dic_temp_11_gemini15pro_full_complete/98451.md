inspirationist: /ˌɪnspəˈreɪʃənɪst/
n.
灵感主义者；默示论者
A person who believes in inspiration, especially divine inspiration.  Someone who believes that the Bible is literally the word of God, dictated by the Holy Spirit.
The preacher, a staunch inspirationist, believed every word of the scripture was divinely inspired.  这位传教士是一位坚定的灵感主义者，他相信圣经中的每一个字都是神灵启示的。
synonyms: fundamentalist, literalist, inerrantist
antonyms: skeptic, rationalist, freethinker
noun
