imperturbablenesses:/ˌɪmpərˌtɜːrbəˈbɪlnɪsɪz/
|n.|沉着冷静；泰然自若；不易被打扰的状态|The state of being imperturbable; calmness and composure, especially in difficult situations.  This noun refers to the quality of being extremely calm and collected, not easily upset or disturbed.|Her imperturbableness in the face of danger was remarkable.  她在危险面前的沉着冷静令人敬佩。|Synonyms: composure, calmness, serenity, tranquility, equanimity|Antonyms: anxiety, nervousness, agitation, distress, excitability|noun
