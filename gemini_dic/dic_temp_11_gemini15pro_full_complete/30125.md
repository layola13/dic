ceding:/ˈsiːdɪŋ/
v.|割让，放弃，屈服|The act of surrendering or yielding something, such as territory, rights, or control.|例句：The treaty involved the ceding of several provinces.  该条约涉及几个省的割让。|例句：He was ceding his authority to his successor. 他正在将他的权力移交给他的继任者。|近义词：surrendering, relinquishing, yielding, granting|反义词：retaining, keeping, acquiring, seizing|词性:动词
