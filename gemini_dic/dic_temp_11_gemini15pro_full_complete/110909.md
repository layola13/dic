lipides:/ˈlɪpɪdz/ /ˈlaɪpɪdz/| n. |脂质|A group of naturally occurring molecules that include fats, waxes, sterols, fat-soluble vitamins (such as vitamins A, D, E, and K), monoglycerides, diglycerides, triglycerides, phospholipids, and others.|Lipids are essential for cell structure and function. 脂质对细胞结构和功能至关重要。|
Lipids store energy in the body. 脂质在体内储存能量。|近义词：fats, oils|反义词:|noun
