gastronomical:/ˌɡæstrəˈnɒmɪkəl/ | adj. | 烹飪的；美食的；讲究饮食的 | Relating to the art or science of good eating. |  The restaurant offered a truly gastronomical experience. 这家餐厅提供了一次真正的美食体验。 |  culinary, epicurean | bland, unappetizing | adjective

gastronomically:/ˌɡæstrəˈnɒmɪkli/ | adv. | 美食地；烹飪地 | In a way that relates to the art or science of good eating. | The meal was gastronomically prepared and presented. 这顿饭的烹饪和呈现都非常讲究。 |  deliciously, exquisitely | plainly, simply | adverb
