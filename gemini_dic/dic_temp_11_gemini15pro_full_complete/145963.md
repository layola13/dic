overdistorting:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈstɔːrtɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度扭曲的，过度失真的 | Exaggerating or misrepresenting something to a degree that it is significantly altered from its true form or meaning. |
The media was accused of overdistorting the facts of the case to create a more sensational story. 媒体被指责为了制造更耸人听闻的故事而过度歪曲了案件的事实。

The artist's overdistorting self-portrait reflected his inner turmoil. 艺术家过度扭曲的自画像反映了他内心的混乱。

| 近义词：exaggerating, misrepresenting, twisting, warping | 反义词：accurately representing, faithfully depicting | 词性：形容词
