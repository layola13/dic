intercarriers:/ˌɪntərˈkæriərz/ | n. | 互相交换；相互运输；（尤指电信）互联互通 | The agreement facilitates intercarriers between different telecommunication networks.  该协议促进了不同电信网络之间的互联互通。 |  Intercarrier interference can cause signal degradation. 载波间干扰会导致信号衰减。 | synonyms: interconnection, interoperability, exchange | antonyms: isolation, separation | noun
