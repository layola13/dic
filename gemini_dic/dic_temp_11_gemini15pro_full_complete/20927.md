bleary-eyed:/ˈblɪəri aɪd/| adj.|睡眼惺忪的，视线模糊的|Having eyes that look tired and watery, as from lack of sleep or excessive crying.|例句：He stumbled into the kitchen, bleary-eyed and disoriented. 他跌跌撞撞地走进厨房，睡眼惺忪，迷迷糊糊。|例句：She was bleary-eyed after studying all night for the exam. 她整夜复习考试，眼睛都模糊了。|近义词：tired-eyed, sleepy, exhausted|反义词：alert, wide-awake|词性:形容词

詞源: From bleary +‎ -eyed.  "Bleary" itself comes from Middle English *bleren "to make dim," probably from Old Norse *blæra "to blow, bellow," referring to the effect of wind or smoke on the eyes.
