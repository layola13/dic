bindingly:/ˈbaɪndɪŋli/
adverb
有约束力地；强制性地
in a way that legally or morally obligates someone to do something
The agreement is bindingly upon both parties.  该协议对双方都具有约束力。
The contract was bindingly signed by all parties. 合同已由各方正式签署。
近义词: obligatorily, compulsorily, mandatorily
反义词: optionally, non-bindingly
词性: adverb
词源: From "binding" + "-ly." "Binding" comes from the Old English "bindan," meaning "to tie, fasten."
