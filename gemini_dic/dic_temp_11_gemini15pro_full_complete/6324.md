amounts:/əˈmaʊnts/ | v. |  总计，等于 | To add up to; to be equivalent to in significance, effect, or value. |  His silence amounts to an admission of guilt. 他的沉默等于承认有罪。| synonyms: equals, totals, adds up to | antonyms: differs, deviates | verb
