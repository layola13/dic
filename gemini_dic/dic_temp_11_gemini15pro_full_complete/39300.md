conico:/ˈkɒnɪkəʊ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnɪkoʊ/ (US)| adj.|圆锥形的; 圆锥体的|Having the shape of a cone; conical.|The volcano has a perfectly conico shape. 这座火山拥有完美的圆锥形。| The ice cream was served in a conico wafer. 冰淇淋盛放在锥形威化饼里。|近义词：cone-shaped, conical|反义词: flat, planar|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *conicus*, from Greek *κωνικός* (kōnikos), from *κῶνος* (kōnos, “cone”).
