monthly:/ˈmʌnθli/ (US), /ˈmʌnθli/ (UK)| adj. adv. n.|每月的，每月一次地，月刊|Happening, done, or produced every month.  It can function as an adjective, adverb, or noun.|
She makes monthly payments on her car loan. 她每月偿还汽车贷款。
The magazine is published monthly. 这本杂志每月出版。
|近义词：every month, each month|反义词:annually, yearly|词性:adjective, adverb, noun


monthly:/ˈmʌnθli/ (US), /ˈmʌnθli/ (UK)| n.|月刊|A magazine or other publication that is issued every month.|
She subscribes to several monthlies. 她订阅了几种月刊。
This monthly is full of interesting articles. 这本月刊里有很多有趣的文章。
|近义词：magazine, periodical|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

词源:
From Old English *mōnaþlīċ (adjective), from mōnaþ "month" + -līċ "-ly."  The word "month" ultimately comes from Proto-Germanic *mēnōþs, related to the word "moon."
