disimbitter:/dɪsɪmˈbɪtər/
verb
消除怨恨；使不再苦涩
To free from bitterness; to sweeten.
The therapist helped to disimbitter the man after his difficult divorce.  治疗师帮助这名男子在艰难的离婚后消除了怨恨。
He tried to disimbitter the coffee with sugar. 他试图用糖来减少咖啡的苦味。
近义词: soothe, mollify, appease, conciliate, placate
反义词: embitter, aggravate, exacerbate
词性:v.

Etymology:
dis- (prefix meaning "reversal or removal") + imbitter (to make bitter)
