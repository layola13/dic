empugn:/ɪmˈpjuːn/| vt.|质疑，攻击，抨击|To challenge the truth, validity, or honesty of a statement or motive; to call into question.|The lawyer empugned the witness's testimony, suggesting it was fabricated. 律师质疑证人的证词，暗示它是伪造的。|例句：His motives were empugned by his political rivals. 他的动机受到了政治对手的质疑。

例句：The newspaper article empugned the integrity of the mayor. 这篇报纸文章质疑市长的正直。

近义词：challenge, question, dispute, attack, impugn, contradict|反义词:support, defend, uphold, endorse, confirm|词性:verb
