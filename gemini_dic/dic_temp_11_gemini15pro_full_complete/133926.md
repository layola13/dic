nonhallucinatory:/ˌnɒnhəˈluːsɪnətɔːri/
adjective
非幻觉的; 不产生幻觉的
Not relating to or involving hallucinations; not causing hallucinations.
The patient experienced nonhallucinatory visions, more like vivid daydreams. 患者经历了非幻觉的视觉体验，更像是生动的白日梦。
She had nonhallucinatory flashbacks to the trauma. 她对创伤有非幻觉的闪回。
Synonyms: non-psychotic, lucid, clearheaded, sane
Antonyms: hallucinatory, psychotic, delusional
