chasten:/ˈtʃeɪsən/| vt.| 磨炼；惩罚；抑制；使变温和 |to correct by punishment or suffering : to restrain or subdue|例句：She was chastened by her failure. 她的失败使她受到了磨炼。|近义词：discipline, punish, subdue, humble|反义词: praise, commend, reward|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English chasten, chastien, from Old French chastier, chastier, from Late Latin castigāre, from Latin castīgāre (“to purify, correct, chastise”), from castus (“pure, chaste”) + agere (“to drive, lead, act”).
