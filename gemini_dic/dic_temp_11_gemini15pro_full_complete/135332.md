nonpelagic:/ˌnɒnpɪˈlædʒɪk/ | adj. | 非远洋的，非深海的 | Not living or occurring in the open ocean; relating to or inhabiting shallow or coastal waters. |  Nonpelagic fish species tend to stay closer to the shore. 非远洋鱼类倾向于靠近海岸。 | Nearshore, coastal, littoral | Pelagic, oceanic | adjective
Etymology-> From non- + pelagic.  "Pelagic" comes from the Greek word "pelagikos" meaning "of the open sea," ultimately from "pelagos" meaning "sea."
