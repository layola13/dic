attenuate:/əˈtɛnjuːeɪt/ | v. | 减少，减弱，衰减 | to reduce the force, effect, or value of something | The drug attenuates the effects of the virus.  这种药物减弱了病毒的作用。| synonyms: weaken, diminish, lessen, reduce, decrease | antonyms: strengthen, intensify, increase, amplify | verb

attenuated:/əˈtɛnjuːeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 减少的，减弱的，衰减的 | weakened or reduced in force, intensity, or effect |  The attenuated virus is used in the vaccine.  减毒病毒用于疫苗中。| synonyms: weakened, diminished, lessened, reduced, decreased | antonyms: strengthened, intensified, increased, amplified | adjective
