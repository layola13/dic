centuriator:/ˌsɛntʃʊˈrieɪtər/ | n. | 百夫长，百人队长 | A leader of a century, a military unit of about 100 soldiers in ancient Rome. |  The centuriator led his men bravely into battle. 百夫长勇敢地率领他的士兵投入战斗。| The centuriator was responsible for the training and discipline of his century. 百夫长负责训练和纪律他的百人队。 | Synonyms: commander, captain, officer | Antonyms: soldier, private | noun
