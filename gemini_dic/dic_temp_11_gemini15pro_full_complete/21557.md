blueprinted:/ˈbluːˌprɪntɪd/ | adj. |  蓝图绘制的，按计划进行的 | Planned or designed in detail, often referring to something that is based on a blueprint or a detailed plan.  | The architect blueprinted the entire building before construction began. 建筑师在施工开始前绘制了整栋建筑的蓝图。| synonyms: planned, designed, charted, outlined, mapped out | antonyms: unplanned, improvised, extemporaneous | adjective

blueprinted:/ˈbluːˌprɪntɪd/ | v. |  制定蓝图，详细计划 | To make a detailed plan or blueprint of something. | The committee blueprinted a strategy for the next five years. 委员会为未来五年制定了战略蓝图。 | synonyms: plan, design, chart, outline, map out | antonyms: improvise, extemporize | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "blueprint" + "-ed." "Blueprint" originally referred to a photographic print of a technical drawing, typically in white lines on a blue background, used for architectural and engineering plans.
