verb
使惊奇，使惊叹
third-person singular simple present indicative form of amaze

反义词: bores, disappoints

amaze:/əˈmeɪz/
verb
使惊奇，使惊叹

近义词: astonish, surprise, astound
反义词: bore, disappoint
