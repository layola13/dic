cajolers:/kəˈdʒoʊlərz/| n. |哄骗者，劝诱者，甜言蜜语者|People who use flattery and gentle persuasion to get what they want.  Those who coax or wheedle others.|The salesman was a master of cajolers, convincing people to buy things they didn't need.  这个销售员是个哄骗高手，能说服人们买他们不需要的东西。|近义词：flatterers, wheedlers, coaxers|反义词:  critics, dissenters|Noun
