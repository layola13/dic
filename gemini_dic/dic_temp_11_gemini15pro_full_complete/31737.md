cheapnesses:/ˈtʃiːpnəsɪz/
n.
廉价；低劣；卑鄙
the state or quality of being cheap, or the fact of something being low in price or quality.  It can also refer to a mean or ungenerous character.
The cheapnesses of the materials used in the construction led to the building's rapid deterioration.  这些建筑材料的低劣导致了建筑物的快速老化。
Synonyms:  inexpensiveness, affordability, meanness, stinginess
Antonyms:  expensiveness, costliness, generosity, lavishness
