apprehension:/ˌæprɪˈhɛnʃən/| n.|忧虑，恐惧；逮捕；理解|Anxiety or fear that something bad or unpleasant will happen. The act of arresting someone.|例句：He felt a growing apprehension as the day of the exam approached. 考试临近，他感到越来越焦虑。

例句：The police officer made the apprehension without incident. 警察顺利地进行了逮捕。|近义词：anxiety, fear, dread, worry, concern, trepidation, angst, misgiving, foreboding, premonition, suspicion, arrest, capture, seizure, detention|反义词：confidence, composure, calmness, tranquility, security, release|词性:名词
