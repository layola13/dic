feudalization:/ˌfjuːdəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.|封建化|The process by which a society becomes feudal, characterized by the decentralization of power and the development of a hierarchical system based on land ownership and personal loyalty.|The feudalization of Europe took centuries. 欧洲的封建化历经数个世纪。|近义词：decentralization, fragmentation|反义词：centralization, unification|词性:名词

Etymology:
From French *féodalisation*, from *féodal* (“feudal”) + *-isation*.  Ultimately from Medieval Latin *feodum* (“fief”).
