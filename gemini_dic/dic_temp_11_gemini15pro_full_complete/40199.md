contest:/ˈkɒntest/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntest/ (US)| n. vt.| 比赛，竞赛；争夺，竞争|A contest is a competition or struggle between rivals.|The two teams held a contest to see who could raise the most money. 这两支队伍举行了一场比赛，看谁筹集的资金最多。|synonyms: competition, match, game, tournament|antonyms: agreement, harmony, peace|noun

contest:/kənˈtest/ (UK) /kənˈtest/ (US)| vt.| 争辩，质疑；争夺，竞争|To contest something means to dispute or challenge it.|The lawyer contested the evidence presented in court. 律师对法庭上提出的证据提出了质疑。|synonyms: challenge, dispute, oppose, question|antonyms: accept, concede, surrender|verb
