like-hell:/laɪk ˈhɛl/| adv.| 绝对不；才怪；极其，非常（用于强调）|  Used to emphasize a negative statement, or to express strong disbelief. Also used to emphasize an adjective or adverb. |"Like hell I'm going to apologize!"  我绝对不会道歉！| 近义词:certainly not, no way, absolutely not, definitely not, not at all, not a bit, by no means, on no account, under no circumstances| 反义词: definitely, absolutely, certainly |词性: 副词

like-hell (as an adjective): /laɪk ˈhɛl/| adj. |  艰难的；糟糕的；非常的，极度的（非正式）|  Used informally to emphasize how difficult, bad, or extreme something is. | "It was a like-hell week at work."  这周工作真是糟糕透了。 | 近义词: terrible, awful, extremely difficult, very bad | 反义词: easy, wonderful, great | 词性: 形容词
