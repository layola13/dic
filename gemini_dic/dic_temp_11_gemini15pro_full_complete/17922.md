beeswaxing:/ˈbiːzwæksɪŋ/
noun
上蜡；涂蜂蜡；抛光；打光
the act of polishing or waxing something with beeswax
例句：The beeswaxing of the antique furniture restored its shine.  给古董家具上蜂蜡使其恢复了光泽。

例句：He spent the afternoon beeswaxing his surfboard. 他一下午都在给他的冲浪板打蜡。

近义词：polishing, waxing, buffing
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps consider "dulling" or "roughening")
词性: n.
