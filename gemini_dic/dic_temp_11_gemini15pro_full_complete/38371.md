conceptacle:/kənˈsɛptəkəl/ | n. | 容器，囊 | A specialized cavity, flask-shaped or spherical, containing reproductive organs, as in certain algae and fungi. |  The alga releases its gametes from the conceptacle. 海藻从囊中释放配子。| synonym: cavity, chamber, receptacle | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "exterior" in a specific context) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *conceptaculum, from Latin conceptāre  "to conceive," frequentative of concipere "to take in, conceive," from com- "together" + capere "to take."
