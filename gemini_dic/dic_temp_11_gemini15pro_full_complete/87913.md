hieroglyphs:/ˈhaɪərəˌɡlɪfs/| n.|象形文字|A system of writing using picture-like symbols, most famously used in ancient Egypt.|Example 1: The walls of the tomb were covered in hieroglyphs.  墓穴的墙壁上覆盖着象形文字。

Example 2:  Deciphering the hieroglyphs helped us understand ancient Egyptian culture.  破译象形文字帮助我们了解古埃及文化。|Synonyms:  symbols, characters, ideograms|Antonyms: letters, alphabet|noun
