androdiecious:/ænˌdroʊˈdaɪʃəs/
adjective
雄全异株的
Having male flowers on one plant and hermaphrodite flowers on another.
例句：Androdiecious plants have evolved a unique reproductive strategy. 雄全异株植物进化出了一种独特的繁殖策略。

例句：The androdiecious nature of this species makes it an interesting subject for botanical research.  这个物种的雄全异株特性使它成为植物学研究的一个有趣课题。
近义词:polygamous (in a broader sense)
反义词:dioecious, monoecious
