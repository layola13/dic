cyclopic:/saɪˈklɒpɪk/ (US) /saɪˈkloʊpɪk/| adj.|独眼的；巨大的，庞大的|Relating to a Cyclops (a mythical one-eyed giant); of immense size, like that of a Cyclops.|例句：The cyclopic walls of the fortress were impressive.  堡垒巨大的城墙令人印象深刻。

例句：He possessed a cyclopic strength that amazed everyone. 他拥有惊人的力量，令所有人叹为观止。|近义词：gigantic, enormous, colossal, immense, huge|反义词：tiny, small, miniature, minuscule|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Late Latin *Cyclopicus*, from Latin Cyclops, from Ancient Greek Κύκλωψ (Kúklōps, “Cyclops”), from κύκλος (kúklos, “circle, wheel, eye”) + ὤψ (ṓps, “eye, face”).
