limelighted:/ˈlaɪmˌlaɪtɪd/| adj.|聚光灯下的，受公众瞩目的|Having been placed in or brought to the center of public attention.|The actress was limelighted after her Oscar-winning performance.  这位女演员在获得奥斯卡奖后备受瞩目。|例句：The young musician was limelighted after his performance at the festival.  这位年轻的音乐家在音乐节上的表演后备受瞩目。

例句：She enjoyed being limelighted and sought the attention of the media. 她喜欢成为公众关注的焦点，并寻求媒体的关注。|近义词：famous, celebrated, prominent, renowned|反义词：obscure, unknown, anonymous|词性：形容词
