leucophyllous:/ˌluːkəˈfɪləs/| adj.|白叶的，淡色的|Having white or pale-colored leaves.|Example: Some desert plants are leucophyllous as an adaptation to reflect sunlight and reduce water loss.  一些沙漠植物的叶子是白色的，以此来反射阳光减少水分流失。

Example: The leucophyllous nature of the plant made it stand out in the lush green forest.  这种植物叶子的淡白色使它在郁郁葱葱的绿色森林中显得格外突出。|近义词：pale, light-colored, achlorophyllous|反义词:chlorophyllous, green|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *leukos* (white) + *phyllon* (leaf)
