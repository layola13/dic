orienters:/ˈɔːriˌɛntərz/ /ˈɔːriˌɛntɚz/
n.
定向者，导向者
People who help others find their way or adjust to a new situation.
The camp counselors acted as orienters for the new arrivals, showing them around and explaining the rules.  营地辅导员充当新来者的向导，带他们四处参观并解释规则。

The orienters helped the new students find their classrooms.  向导帮助新生找到他们的教室。
近义词: guides, advisors, mentors, instructors
反义词: disorienters, misleaders
词性: noun
