kaisers:/ˈkaɪzərz/
n.
皇帝（复数）
Emperors (plural form of kaiser).  It refers to the emperors of Germany or Austria, especially those who ruled before World War I.
The kaisers of Germany held significant power. 德国的皇帝们掌握着巨大的权力。
World War I saw the end of the kaisers' reign. 第一次世界大战见证了皇帝统治的结束。
emperors, monarchs, rulers
dictators, presidents
noun
