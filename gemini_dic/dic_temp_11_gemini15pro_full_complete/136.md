abandons:/əˈbændənz/
verb
放弃，抛弃
ceases to support or look after (someone); desert.
He abandoned his wife and children. 他抛弃了他的妻子和孩子。
Synonyms: desert, forsake, leave, quit
Antonyms: support, maintain, keep, cherish
词性: 动词

abandons:/əˈbændənz/
verb
离弃，丢弃
give up completely (a course of action, a practice, or a way of thinking).
The government abandoned its policy of price controls. 政府放弃了价格管制政策。
Synonyms: relinquish, renounce, forgo, surrender
Antonyms: retain, keep, continue, pursue
词性: 动词
