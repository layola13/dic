averted: /əˈvɜːrtɪd/ /əˈvɝːtɪd/
verb
避免；防止；转移
prevented something unpleasant or bad from happening
例句：A disaster was narrowly averted. 一场灾难勉强避免了。
例句：He averted his eyes from the terrible sight. 他把目光从可怕的景象上移开。
近义词：prevent, avoid, preclude, obviate, forestall
反义词：cause, incite, provoke, allow
词性：v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *avertere*, from *ab* ("away, from") + *vertere* ("to turn").
