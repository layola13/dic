out-top:/ˈaʊtˈtɒp/ | adj. | 超顶的，超越顶部的 | Surpassing the top; exceeding in height or prominence. | The out-top branches of the redwood tree swayed in the wind.  高大的红杉树顶部的树枝在风中摇曳。 | Example 2: The skyscraper's out-top antenna made it the tallest structure in the city. 摩天大楼顶部的天线使它成为这座城市最高的建筑。 | 近义词: surpassing, exceeding, towering | 反义词: below, underneath, shorter | 词性: adjective

词源: "out-" (prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing) + "top" (noun referring to the highest point).
