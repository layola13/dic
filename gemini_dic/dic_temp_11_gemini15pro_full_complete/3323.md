affordabilities:/əˌfɔːrdəˈbɪlətiz/
noun
可负担性；负担能力
the quality of being affordable; the fact that something is affordable.  The plural form emphasizes the multiple factors or aspects contributing to affordability.
Rising house prices affect the affordabilities of homes for first-time buyers.  不断上涨的房价影响了首次购房者的购房能力。
synonyms: affordability, cheapness, inexpensiveness
antonyms:  expensiveness, unaffordability, costliness
n.
