aggravator:/ˈæɡrəveɪtər/| n. |加重者；恶化者；加剧因素|Something that makes a problem, situation, or condition worse.|Stress is a common aggravator of skin conditions. 压力是皮肤病的常见加剧因素。|Synonyms: irritant, intensifier, exacerbator|Antonyms:  alleviator, mitigator, ameliorator|noun
