adventuring:/ədˈvɛntʃərɪŋ/| n. |冒险，探险|The act of going on an adventure; engaging in exciting or unusual experiences.| They spent their summer adventuring in the mountains. 他们在山上度过了充满冒险的夏天。|近义词: exploring, roaming, wandering|反义词: staying, resting|词性: 名词 (gerund, present participle of 'adventure')
adventuring:/ədˈvɛntʃərɪŋ/| adj. |爱冒险的，喜欢探险的| Inclined to adventure; adventurous.|  He has an adventuring spirit. 他有冒险精神。|近义词: adventurous, daring, bold|反义词: cautious, timid, fearful|词性: 形容词 (present participle of 'adventure')
