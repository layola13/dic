needles:/ˈniːdlz/| n.|针，指针，针状物|A small, slender, pointed piece of metal with a hole at one end for thread, used in sewing; or anything resembling this shape. |The tailor used needles to sew the button back on.  裁缝用针把扣子缝了回去。

The compass needles pointed north. 指南针指向北方。|Synonyms: pins, spikes, spines|Antonyms: blunt objects|Noun
