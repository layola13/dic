gypsum: /ˈdʒɪpsəm/ | n. | 石膏 | A soft white or gray mineral consisting of hydrated calcium sulfate. It is used to make plaster of Paris and fertilizers, and occurs in various forms including alabaster. | The walls were made of gypsum.  墙是用石膏做的。| Gypsum is commonly used in construction. 石膏通常用于建筑。 | synonyms: plaster, plaster of Paris, anhydrite, alabaster | antonyms: N/A | noun

gypseous: /ˈdʒɪpsiəs/ | adj. | 含石膏的，石膏质的 | Relating to, resembling, or containing gypsum. | The soil in this area is gypseous.  这个地区的土壤是含石膏的。| The gypseous rocks were easily eroded.  这些石膏质的岩石很容易被侵蚀。 | synonyms: chalky, calcareous | antonyms: N/A | adjective
