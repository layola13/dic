overmonopolized:/ˌoʊvərˌməˈnɑːpəˌlaɪzd/
adjective
过度垄断的
Having a state of excessive control by a single entity or group, beyond the point of a typical monopoly.  It implies a negative consequence due to the extreme level of control.
The overmonopolized pharmaceutical industry leads to inflated drug prices.  制药行业过度垄断导致药品价格虚高。
The overmonopolized media landscape stifled independent voices. 过度垄断的媒体环境扼杀了独立的声音。
Synonym: excessively monopolized, hypermonopolized
Antonym: competitive, diversified, decentralized
词性: adjective
