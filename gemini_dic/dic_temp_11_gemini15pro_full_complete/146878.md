overinformed:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈfɔːrmd/
adjective
过度知情的，信息过载的
Having or receiving too much information, often to the point of confusion or inaction.
The overinformed consumer can suffer from analysis paralysis and struggle to make a decision.  过度知情的消费者可能会患上分析瘫痪，难以做出决定。
He felt overinformed and overwhelmed by the sheer volume of data. 他感觉信息过载，被海量的数据压得喘不过气。
近义词: overloaded, saturated, bombarded
反义词: uninformed, ignorant, unaware
词性: adjective
