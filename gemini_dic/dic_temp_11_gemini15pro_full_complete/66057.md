exasperation:/ɪɡˌzæspəˈreɪʃn/| n. |恼怒，愤怒，激怒|A feeling of intense irritation or annoyance.|Her constant lateness drove him to exasperation.|近义词：irritation, annoyance, frustration, anger|反义词:patience, calmness, tolerance|词性:noun
Etymology: From Late Latin exasperatio, from Latin exasperare "to roughen, irritate," from ex "out" + asper "rough."
