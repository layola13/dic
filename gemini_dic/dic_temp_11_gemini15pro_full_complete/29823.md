cauliflowers:/ˈkɔːlɪˌflaʊərz/| n. |花椰菜；菜花|A vegetable with a large head of white florets. It is a member of the cabbage family.|例句：I like to roast cauliflowers with olive oil and spices. 我喜欢用橄榄油和香料烤花椰菜。

例句：Cauliflowers are a good source of vitamin C. 花椰菜是维生素C的良好来源。

近义词：broccoli |反义词:None|词性:noun
