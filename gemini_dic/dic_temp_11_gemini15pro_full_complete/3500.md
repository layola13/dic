afterpains:/ˈæftərpeɪnz/| n.|产后痛，分娩后阵痛|Cramping abdominal pain experienced by women following childbirth, caused by uterine contractions as the uterus returns to its normal size.|She experienced severe afterpains after the birth of her twins. 她生完双胞胎后经历了严重的产后痛。|近义词：cramps, uterine contractions|反义词: comfort, ease|名词
