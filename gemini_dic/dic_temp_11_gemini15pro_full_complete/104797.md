kbang:/ˈkeɪbæŋ/| interj.|巨响，砰的一声|A loud explosive sound, often used in comics or informal writing to represent such a sound.|Example: The dynamite went kbang! 炸药砰的一声爆炸了！|Example:  With a kbang, the door slammed shut. 砰的一声，门猛地关上了。|近义词：bang, boom, crash, wham|反义词: (No direct antonym for a sound effect)|词性: Interjection

词源:  Onomatopoeic, meaning it imitates the sound it represents. Likely a variant of "bang."
