effable:/ˈɛfəbl/| adj.|可表达的，能说出的|Able to be expressed in words.  Something that can be described or talked about.|Her joy was so profound, so effable, that she could barely contain it. 她的快乐如此深刻，如此可以表达，以至于她几乎无法抑制。|Synonyms: expressible, utterable, communicable|Antonyms: ineffable, indescribable, unutterable|Adjective
