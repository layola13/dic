bulbose:/ˈbʌlbəs/| adj.|球根状的；膨胀的；球状的|Resembling a bulb, especially in roundness and swelling.  Having a bulb or bulbs.|The plant has a bulbose root system. 这种植物有球根状的根系。 The onion has a bulbose shape. 洋葱呈球状。|近义词：bulbous, swollen, rounded, globular|反义词：slender, thin, narrow|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *bulbosus, from bulbus (“bulb”) + -osus (suffix forming adjectives indicating fullness or abundance). Ultimately from Ancient Greek βολβός (bolbós, “bulb, onion”).
