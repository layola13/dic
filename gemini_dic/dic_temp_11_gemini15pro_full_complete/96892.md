inexpediently:/ˌɪnɪkˈspiːdiəntli/
adverb
不明智地，不适当地
in a way that is not practical or suitable for a particular purpose.
He acted inexpediently by ignoring the advice of his lawyer. 他无视律师的建议，行事不明智。
Synonyms: unwisely, imprudently, foolishly, impractically
Antonyms: wisely, prudently, judiciously, practically
词性: 副词
