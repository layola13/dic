feather-like:/ˈfeðərˌlaɪk/| adj.|像羽毛的，轻如羽毛的|Resembling a feather, especially in lightness. |The dancer's movements were feather-like and graceful. 舞蹈演员的动作轻盈优雅，宛如羽毛。|The material was surprisingly strong despite its feather-like texture. 这种材料尽管质地轻如羽毛，却异常结实。|近义词：feathery, light, airy, delicate|反义词：heavy, dense, thick|词性:形容词

词源:
From feather + -like.  "Feather" comes from Old English "feðer," from Proto-Germanic "*fethrō,"  related to Old High German "fedara." "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
