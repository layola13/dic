hexachord:/ˈhɛksəkɔːrd/| n. |六音列，六度音阶|A series of six notes, especially any of the six natural diatonic notes contained within a perfect fourth or fifth, forming the basis of the system of solmization. |The medieval hexachord was a fundamental element of musical theory. 中世纪的六音列是音乐理论的基本要素。| The Guidonian hand was a mnemonic device used to teach singers the hexachords.  圭多手势是一种用来教歌手六音列的记忆法。 |scale, gamut| antonym: N/A (lack of a direct opposite concept)|noun
