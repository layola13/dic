bulkily:/ˈbʌlkɪli/| adv.|笨重地，庞大地|in a bulky way; taking up much space|The package was bulkily wrapped and difficult to carry.  这个包裹包装得很笨重，很难携带。|The furniture was bulkily arranged, making the room feel small.  家具摆放得很笨重，使房间感觉很小。|近义词：clumsily, awkwardly, heavily|反义词：compactly, neatly, lightly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From bulk + -ily.  Bulk meaning large size or mass comes from Middle English bulke, of Scandinavian origin.  The suffix -ily forms adverbs from adjectives.
