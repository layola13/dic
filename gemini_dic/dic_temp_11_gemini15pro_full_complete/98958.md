inter-European:/ˌɪntərˌjʊərəˈpiːən/
adjective
欧洲内部的，欧洲国家之间的
Relating to or involving relations between the countries of Europe.
The inter-European trade has been significantly impacted by the new regulations. 欧洲内部贸易受到了新规定的显著影响。
Inter-European cooperation is crucial for tackling climate change.  欧洲国家间的合作对于应对气候变化至关重要。
近义词: intra-European, continental
反义词: extra-European, intercontinental
词性: adjective


Etymology:
"inter-" (Latin) meaning "between, among" + "European" (from Europe)
