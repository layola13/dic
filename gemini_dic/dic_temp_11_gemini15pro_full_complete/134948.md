nonnarcissism:/nɒnˈnɑːsɪsɪzəm/
n.|非自恋|The state or quality of not being narcissistic; lack of narcissism.  Absence of excessive self-love or self-absorption.|Her nonnarcissism was refreshing in a world obsessed with selfies.|近义词:humility, modesty, selflessness|反义词:narcissism, egotism, self-centeredness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + narcissism.  "Narcissism" comes from the Greek myth of Narcissus, who fell in love with his own reflection.
