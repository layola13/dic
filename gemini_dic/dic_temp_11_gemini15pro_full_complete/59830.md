ectocyst:/ˈɛktoʊsɪst/| n. |外囊|The outer layer or covering of certain organisms, especially the gelatinous covering of some colonial or solitary invertebrates.|The ectocyst of the bryozoan was damaged.  苔藓虫的外囊受损了。|近义词: outer layer, covering, sheath|反义词: endocyst|名词
