例句：It's important to create a judgeless space for people to share their experiences.  创造一个让人们分享经验的无评判空间是很重要的。

|近义词：nonjudgmental, accepting, unbiased, impartial, tolerant|反义词:judgmental, critical, censorious|词性:形容词
