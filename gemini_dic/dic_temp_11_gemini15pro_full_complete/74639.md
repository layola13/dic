free-enterprising: /ˌfriː ˈɛntərˌpraɪzɪŋ/
adjective
自由创业的；有进取心的；富于创业精神的
Characterized by initiative and willingness to undertake new ventures and businesses; entrepreneurial.
She was a free-enterprising woman who started her own business. 她是一位富有创业精神的女性，创办了自己的企业。
He has a free-enterprising spirit and is always looking for new opportunities. 他有进取心，总是寻找新的机会。
近义词: entrepreneurial, enterprising, ambitious, go-getting, resourceful
反义词: unambitious, uninventive, lazy, passive, timid
词性: adjective

词源:
"free" (meaning unrestricted, independent) + "enterprising" (from "enterprise," meaning a project or undertaking, especially one that is difficult or requires effort).  The term "enterprise" ultimately derives from Old French "entreprendre" (to undertake).
