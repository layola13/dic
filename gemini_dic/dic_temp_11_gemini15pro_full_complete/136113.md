nonreducibility:/ˌnɑːnˌrɪˌduːsəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可约性，不可化简性 | The quality or state of being nonreducible; inability to be reduced to a simpler form.  | The nonreducibility of consciousness to physical processes is a central tenet of some philosophical schools.  意识的不可约性到物理过程是一些哲学流派的核心原则。| Synonyms: irreducibility, indecomposability | Antonyms: reducibility, decomposability | noun

nonreducible:/ˌnɑːnˌrɪˈduːsəbəl/ | adj. | 不可约的，不可化简的 | Not able to be reduced to a simpler form. |  The problem proved to be nonreducible, requiring a novel approach. 这个问题被证明是不可约的，需要一种新的方法。 |  Synonyms: irreducible, indecomposable | Antonyms: reducible, decomposable | adjective
