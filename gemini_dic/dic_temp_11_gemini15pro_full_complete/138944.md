nuttinesses:/ˈnʌtinəsɪz/
n.
古怪行为；愚蠢行为；疯狂
The state of being nutty; craziness or eccentricity.
His latest nuttinesses include wearing a rubber chicken on his head. 他最近的古怪行为包括在头上戴一只橡胶鸡。
Synonyms: eccentricities, follies, absurdities, craziness, silliness
Antonyms: sanity, reasonableness, sensibleness
词性: 名词
