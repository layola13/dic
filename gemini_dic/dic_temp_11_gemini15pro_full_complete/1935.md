actinophorous:/ˌæktɪˈnɒfərəs/
adjective
放射状的；有射线的
Having rays or ray-like parts.
例句：The actinophorous structure of the coral was clearly visible under the microscope. 在显微镜下，珊瑚的放射状结构清晰可见。

例句：Actinophorous organisms, like starfish, often exhibit radial symmetry. 像海星这样的放射状生物通常呈现径向对称。

近义词：radial, radiate, stellate
反义词：non-radial

词性
adjective
