neighborhood:/ˈneɪbərˌhʊd/ (US) /ˈneɪbəhʊd/ (UK)| n.|邻近地区；街区；社区|A district or area, often with reference to its character or inhabitants.  |Example: I grew up in a friendly neighborhood. 我在一个友好的街区长大。

|Example:  The neighborhood has changed a lot in recent years.  这个街区近年来变化很大。
|近义词：community, area, district, vicinity, locality|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "isolation" or "remoteness" in a figurative sense) |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *nēahgebūrscipe ("neighborhood, vicinity"), from nēahgebūr ("neighbor") +‎ -scipe ("-ship").
