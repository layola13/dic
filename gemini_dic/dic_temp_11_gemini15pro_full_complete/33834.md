circumlocutionary:/ˌsɜːrkəmloʊˈkjuːʃəneri/ /ˌsɜːrkəmləˈkjuːʃənəri/| adj.|迂回的，绕圈子的|Speaking in a circumlocutionary manner, she avoided directly answering the question.  以一种迂回的方式说话，她避免了直接回答问题。|She gave a circumlocutionary explanation, never quite getting to the point. 她给了一个迂回的解释，从来没有说到点子上。|近义词：indirect, evasive, periphrastic|反义词:direct, straightforward, concise|词性:adjective
