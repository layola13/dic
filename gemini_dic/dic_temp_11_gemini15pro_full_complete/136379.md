nonrestitution:/ˌnɒnrɛstɪˈtjuːʃən/ | /ˌnɑːnrɛstɪˈtuːʃən/ | n. | 非恢复; 不归还; 不赔偿 | The nonrestitution of the stolen artifacts angered the museum curator.  博物馆馆长对被盗文物未归还感到愤怒。 |  The judge ordered the nonrestitution of the property. 法官下令不归还财产。 | 近义词: nonpayment, nonreturn, withholding | 反义词: restitution, restoration, return | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From non- (not) + restitution (from Latin restitutio, meaning "restoration").
