enamelists:/ɪˈnæməlɪsts/
n.
珐琅匠；搪瓷工人；珐琅艺术家
A person who applies enamel to metal, glass, or pottery.
The enamelists carefully fired the jewelry in the kiln. 珐琅匠小心翼翼地在窑中烧制珠宝。

近义词:  enameller,  artisan, craftsman
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider someone who removes or destroys enamel)
