behave:/bɪˈheɪv/| vi. vt.|表现，行为，举止|To act in a particular way, especially in a social situation; to function or operate in a certain way.|She always behaves well in public.  她在公共场合总是举止得体。|The car behaved badly on the icy road. 这辆车在结冰的路上表现很差。|近义词：act, conduct oneself, perform|反义词:misbehave|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English behaven, from be- + haven (“to have, hold, keep”).  More at be-, have.
