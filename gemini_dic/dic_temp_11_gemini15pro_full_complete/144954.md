overabstracts:/ˌoʊvərˈæbˌstrækts/ | v. | 过度抽象化 | To think about or describe something in a way that is too general and theoretical, leaving out important details and making it difficult to understand. | The philosopher overabstracts the concept of love, making it seem detached from real-life experiences. 这位哲学家过度抽象了爱的概念，使其看起来脱离了现实生活经验。 | The theory overabstracts the complex social dynamics at play, ignoring individual agency. 这个理论过度抽象了复杂的社会动态，忽略了个人能动性。 |  近义词: overgeneralizes, oversimplifies, theorizes | 反义词: specifies, details, concretizes | 词性: 动词
