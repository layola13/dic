motives:/ˈmoʊtɪvz/ (US) /ˈməʊtɪvz/ (UK)| n. |动机，目的|Something that causes a person to act in a particular way; a reason for doing something.|例句：Her motives for leaving were never clear. 她离开的动机一直不清楚。

例句：The police are still trying to establish a motive for the murder. 警方仍在试图确定谋杀的动机。|近义词：reasons, intentions, aims, objectives, goals, purposes|反义词：deterrents|词性：名词
