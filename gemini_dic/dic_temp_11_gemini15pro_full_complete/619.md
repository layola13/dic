abranchial:/əˈbræŋkiəl/
adjective
无鳃的；非鳃的
Relating to or denoting structures that resemble gills but are not true gills, or to the region of the body where gills would be expected.
Axolotls have abranchial lungs and can breathe air at the surface.  蝾螈有无鳃肺，可以在水面呼吸空气。
The abranchial structures on the larva are thought to be sensory organs.  幼虫身上的无鳃结构被认为是感觉器官。
近义词: agill
反义词: branchial
词性: adjective
