jujutsu:/dʒuːˈdʒʊtsuː/ /dʒuːˈdʒɪtsuː/
noun
柔术
A Japanese martial art and method of close combat using holds, throws, and other grappling techniques to subdue or disable an opponent.
He's a black belt in jujutsu. 他是柔术黑带。
近义词:jiu-jitsu, judo
反义词: (No direct antonym for a martial art, perhaps consider concepts like pacifism or non-violence)
词性:noun
