miserhood:/ˈmaɪzərˌhʊd/| n.|吝啬，守财奴|The state of being a miser; extreme stinginess.|He lived a life of miserhood, hoarding his wealth and denying himself even basic comforts. 他过着守财奴般的生活，囤积财富，甚至连基本的舒适都舍不得享受。|Near Synonyms: miserliness, avarice, parsimony, penuriousness|Near Antonyms: generosity, liberality, munificence|noun
