impoverished: /ɪmˈpɒvərɪʃt/ | adj. | 贫困的，赤贫的；贫瘠的，枯竭的 | Reduced to poverty; (of a country, area, etc.) having few resources; depleted. |  Many impoverished families struggle to afford basic necessities. 许多贫困家庭难以负担基本生活必需品。| The impoverished soil yielded a meager harvest.  贫瘠的土地只产出了微薄的收成。 | synonyms: poor, destitute, indigent, needy, deprived, penniless | antonyms: rich, wealthy, affluent, prosperous, fertile | word type: adjective

impoverish: /ɪmˈpɒvərɪʃ/ | v. | 使贫穷；使贫瘠；使枯竭 | Make (a person or area) poor; exhaust the strength or vitality of. | The war impoverished the country. 战争使这个国家变得贫穷。| Excessive farming can impoverish the soil. 过度耕作会使土壤贫瘠。| synonyms: deplete, exhaust, drain, bankrupt, ruin | antonyms: enrich, enhance, improve, strengthen | word type: verb
