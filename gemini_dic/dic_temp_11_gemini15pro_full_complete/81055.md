grail:/ɡreɪl/| n.|圣杯；渴望得到但难以得到的东西|Something that is eagerly pursued or sought after; a desired object or goal that is difficult to attain.|The Holy Grail is a legendary object in Arthurian literature. 圣杯是亚瑟王文学中的传奇物品。He spent his life searching for the grail of perfect happiness. 他一生都在追求完美幸福的圣杯。|近义词：quest, objective, prize|反义词：none|词性:名词

词源: From Old French *graal* or *greal*, probably from Late Latin *gradale*, meaning "a dish or platter".  Originally referred to a shallow dish or platter, later associated with the cup used by Christ at the Last Supper and believed to possess miraculous powers.
