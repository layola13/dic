adj.|肥胖的，油腻的|The cat was so fat it could barely move.  这只猫太胖了，几乎动不了。
fat:/fæt/
adj.|丰厚的，大量的|He received a fat bonus this year. 他今年得到了丰厚的奖金。
fat:/fæt/
v.|养肥，使肥胖|Farmers fatten their pigs for market.  农民养肥猪以供出售。
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *fætt (attested only in compounds like *fætt-swīn 'fat swine'), from Proto-Germanic *faitaz (source also of Old Frisian fet, Middle Dutch vet, Dutch vet, Old High German feizit 'fat', German feist 'obese, well-fed', Old Norse feitr), from Proto-Indo-European *peyH- 'to swell, be fat'.
SYNONYMS->
plump, chubby, obese, overweight, greasy, oily, rich, substantial
ANTONYMS->
thin, lean, skinny, slender, meager, scanty
