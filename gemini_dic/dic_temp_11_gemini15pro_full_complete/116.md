abalienate:/əˈbeɪliəneɪt/
verb
疏远；让渡
to cause someone to feel that they are different and not part of a group or community; to give up the legal right to own something
例句：His comments have alienated many former supporters. 他的言论疏远了许多以前的拥护者。

例句：He abalienated all his property to his son. 他把所有财产都让给了他的儿子。
近义词：estrange, alienate, transfer, convey
反义词：reconcile, unite, retain
词性：v.
