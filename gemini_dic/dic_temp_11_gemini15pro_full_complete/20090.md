bioshock:/ˈbaɪoʊʃɑːk/| n. |生物冲击|A portmanteau of "biological" and "shock," referring to the debilitating effects of rapid pressure changes on the human body, particularly in underwater environments, and also the title of a popular video game series.|The divers experienced bioshock when they ascended too quickly. 潜水员上升过快，经历了生物冲击。|近义词：decompression sickness, the bends|反义词: N/A|名词
