building's: /ˈbɪldɪŋz/| n. |建筑物的；大楼的|  It is the possessive form of "building," meaning something that belongs to or is associated with a building. | The building's foundation is very strong.  这栋建筑的地基非常坚固。| synonyms: edifice's, structure's | antonyms: N/A | Possessive Noun

词源: From Middle English *bilding, from Old English *bylding, verbal noun of byldan (“to build”). Cognate with West Frisian bilding (“building”).
