formicid:/fɔːrˈmɪsɪd/
| adj. n.|蚁，蚁类的；蚁酸的|Relating to or resembling ants; characteristic of ants. Also, pertaining to formic acid.  Derived from Latin *formīca* "ant".|Ants are formicid insects. 蚂蚁是蚁类昆虫。|The sting of a fire ant delivers a painful formicid injection. 火蚁的叮咬会带来痛苦的蚁酸注射。|近义词：ant, ant-like|反义词:|adjective, noun
