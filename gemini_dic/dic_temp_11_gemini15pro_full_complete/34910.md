cliquisms:/ˈkliːkɪzəmz/
n.
小团体用语；派系语言
Phrases or expressions used within a particular clique or social group, often excluding outsiders.
Example: The teenagers used cliquisms that their parents couldn't understand. 这些青少年使用他们的父母无法理解的小团体用语。
Example:  The office had its own set of cliquisms, making it difficult for new employees to integrate.  办公室里有一套自己的行话，这使得新员工很难融入。
Synonyms: jargon, slang, argot
Antonyms: standard language, formal language
