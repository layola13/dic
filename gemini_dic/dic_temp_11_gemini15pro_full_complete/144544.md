outvoted:/ˌaʊtˈvoʊtɪd/
verb
被投票否决；投票击败
defeated in a vote
例句：The chairman was outvoted by the board. 董事长被董事会投票否决了。

例句：The proposal was outvoted by a narrow margin. 这项提议以微弱的差距被否决了。
近义词：defeated, overruled
反义词：approved, supported
词性：Verb (Past Participle)

outvote:/ˌaʊtˈvoʊt/
verb
投票击败；以票数胜过
defeat (someone) in a vote
例句：The government was outvoted in the House of Commons. 政府在下议院投票中被击败了。

例句：They outvoted their rivals by a large majority. 他们以压倒性多数票击败了对手。
近义词：defeat, overrule
反义词：lose to
词性：Verb
