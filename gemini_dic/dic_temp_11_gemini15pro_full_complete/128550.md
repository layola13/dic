NHP:/ˈen eɪtʃ piː/| abbreviation |非人灵长类动物（Nonhuman Primate）|NHP is an abbreviation that stands for Nonhuman Primate, referring to any primate that is not a human.  It is commonly used in scientific research, particularly in fields like medicine and biology.|Researchers use NHPs to study various diseases. 研究人员使用非人灵长类动物来研究各种疾病。|Synonym: nonhuman primate|Antonym: human|abbreviation
