onion-domed:/ˈʌnjənˌdoʊmd/
adjective
洋葱形的，圆顶形的
Having a dome shaped like an onion, typically used in architecture, especially relating to Eastern European or Islamic styles.
The onion-domed churches of Russia are iconic. 俄罗斯的洋葱形圆顶教堂是标志性的。
The photograph captured the beauty of the onion-domed Taj Mahal at sunset.  这张照片捕捉到了日落时分洋葱形圆顶泰姬陵的美丽。
近义词: bulbous, domed
反义词: flat, pointed
词性: adjective

onion:/ˈʌnjən/
noun
洋葱
A pungent edible bulb, used as a vegetable.
I always cry when I chop onions. 我切洋葱的时候总是哭。
The recipe calls for one diced onion.  这个食谱需要一个切丁的洋葱。
近义词: scallion, shallot (for certain types)
反义词: (No direct antonym for the vegetable itself)
词性: noun

dome:/doʊm/
noun
圆顶
A rounded vault forming the roof of a building or structure, typically with a circular base.
The dome of the cathedral was covered in gold leaf. 大教堂的圆顶覆盖着金箔。
The planetarium has a large dome for projecting images of the night sky.  天文馆有一个巨大的圆顶，用于投射夜空图像。
近义词: cupola, vault
反义词: base, foundation
词性: noun
