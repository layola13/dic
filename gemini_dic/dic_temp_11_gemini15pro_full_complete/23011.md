bouillabaisse:/ˌbuːjəˈbeɪs/ | n. | 法式浓鱼汤 | A rich, traditional Provençal fish stew flavored with fennel, saffron, garlic, and orange peel. |  We had a delicious bouillabaisse at the seaside restaurant. 我们在海边的餐厅吃了一顿美味的法式浓鱼汤。| synonyms: fish stew, seafood stew | antonyms: None | noun

bouillabaisse:/ˌbuːjəˈbeɪs/ | n. |  杂烩；大杂烩 |  A mixture of various things. (Figurative) | The play was a bouillabaisse of different theatrical styles.  这出戏是各种戏剧风格的大杂烩。 | synonyms: medley, hodgepodge, potpourri | antonyms: None | noun
