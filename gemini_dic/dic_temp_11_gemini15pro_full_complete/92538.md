hypsometry:/hɪpˈsɒmətri/ | n. |测高学，测高法|The study of the measurement of land elevation.  It often refers to the determination of the heights of mountains or the depths of the ocean floor.|例句：Hypsometry plays a crucial role in understanding the topography of a region. 测高学在理解一个地区的地形方面起着至关重要的作用。

例句：The hypsometry of the Himalayas is a complex subject. 喜马拉雅山的测高法是一个复杂的课题。|近义词：altimetry, elevation measurement|反义词:bathymetry (measurement of depths)|词性:noun
