inns:/ɪnz/
noun
小旅馆，客栈
A small hotel or pub, typically one in the country, providing accommodation, food, and drink.
We stayed in a cozy little inn in the Cotswolds. 我们在科茨沃尔德的一家舒适的小旅馆住了下来。
synonyms: hotel, pub, tavern, lodge
antonyms: (none specific - could consider large hotel, resort)
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *inn, from Proto-Germanic *innam (compare West Frisian yn, Dutch in, German Inne "inside, within"), from Proto-Indo-European *en "in".
