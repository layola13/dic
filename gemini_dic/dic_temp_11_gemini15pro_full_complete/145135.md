overarticulates: /ˌoʊvərɑːrˈtɪkjuleɪts/ /ˌəʊvərɑːˈtɪkjʊleɪts/
verb
过度清晰地发音；过分清晰地吐字
pronounces words more distinctly than necessary, often exaggerating sounds or stressing syllables unnecessarily.
She overarticulates her words when she's nervous, making her sound a bit artificial.  她紧张的时候会过度清晰地发音，听起来有点不自然。
The actor overarticulated his lines, which made the character seem pompous. 这位演员的台词发音过于清晰，使角色显得有些浮夸。
近义词: enunciates excessively, overpronounces
反义词: mumbles, slurs
词性: verb

overarticulate: /ˌoʊvərɑːrˈtɪkjuleɪt/ /ˌəʊvərɑːˈtɪkjʊleɪt/
adjective
发音过分清晰的
characterized by overarticulation
His overarticulate speech pattern made it difficult to understand him. 他过分清晰的发音方式让人难以理解他。
The newsreader's overarticulate delivery sounded unnatural.  新闻播音员过分清晰的播报听起来很不自然。
近义词: excessively enunciated, overpronounced
反义词: mumbled, slurred
词性: adjective

overarticulation: /ˌoʊvərɑːrˌtɪkjʊˈleɪʃən/ /ˌəʊvərɑːˌtɪkjʊˈleɪʃən/
noun
过度清晰的发音；过分清晰的吐字
the act of overarticulating
Her overarticulation was distracting during the presentation.  她在演讲中过分清晰的发音让人分心。
Overarticulation can sometimes impede communication. 过分清晰地发音有时会妨碍交流。
近义词: excessive enunciation, overpronunciation
反义词: mumbling, slurring
词性: noun


词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "articulate" (from Latin "articulatus," past participle of "articulare" meaning "to utter distinctly")
