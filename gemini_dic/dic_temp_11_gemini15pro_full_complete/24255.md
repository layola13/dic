briefings: /ˈbriːfɪŋz/
n.
简报；简报会；情况介绍会
A meeting for giving information or instructions.
The president receives daily briefings from the intelligence services. 总统每天都会收到情报部门的简报。
synonyms: meeting, conference, session, seminar, discussion, consultation
antonyms: debriefing

briefings: /ˈbriːfɪŋz/
n.
指示；命令；任务
Instructions or orders given to someone.
The team leader gave us our briefings before the mission. 团队领导在任务开始前给我们下达了指示。
synonyms: instructions, orders, directives, commands
antonyms: None


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "brief" (to instruct or inform concisely), which comes from the Old French word "brief" meaning "short, concise." Ultimately derived from the Latin word "brevis" meaning "short."
