metastasizes:/məˈtæstəˌsaɪzɪz/
verb
转移；扩散
(of a cancer) spread to other sites in the body by metastasis.
The cancer metastasized to his lungs. 癌症转移到他的肺部。
Synonyms: spread, disseminate, proliferate, propagate
Antonyms: localize, contain
词性: verb
