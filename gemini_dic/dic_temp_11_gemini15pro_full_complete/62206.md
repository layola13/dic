encourage:/ɪnˈkɜːrɪdʒ/ /ɪnˈkɝːɪdʒ/
verb
鼓励，激励
To give support, confidence, or hope to (someone).
The teacher encouraged her students to ask questions. 老师鼓励学生提问。
inspire, motivate, support
discourage, hinder, deter
词性:动词

encouraged:/ɪnˈkɜːrɪdʒd/ /ɪnˈkɝːɪdʒd/
adjective
受到鼓舞的，被鼓励的
Feeling or showing confidence and hope.
I felt encouraged by the positive feedback I received.  我收到的积极反馈让我感到鼓舞。
inspired, motivated, optimistic
discouraged, disheartened, pessimistic
词性:形容词


词源:
From Old French *encoragier (recorded as encoragier), from en- "in" + corage "courage," ultimately from Latin cor "heart."
