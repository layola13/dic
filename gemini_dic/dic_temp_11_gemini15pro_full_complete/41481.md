cornmuse:/kɔːrnˈmjuːz/ | n. |  玉米女神; 农业女神; 丰收女神 | A mythical goddess associated with corn and harvest, often depicted as a beautiful woman adorned with corn stalks and ears of corn. This word is a neologism, blending "corn" and "muse." |  The artist painted a cornmuse, her golden hair intertwined with ripe ears of maize. 艺术家画了一位玉米女神，她金色的头发与成熟的玉米穗交织在一起。| 近义词: Ceres, Demeter | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词
