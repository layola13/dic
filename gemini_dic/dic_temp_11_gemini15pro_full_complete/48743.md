definers:/dɪˈfaɪnərz/| n.|限定词|Words that introduce and specify a noun or noun phrase, such as articles (a, an, the), demonstratives (this, that, these, those), possessives (my, your, his, her, its, our, their), quantifiers (some, any, many, few, several, all, both, each, every), and numbers.|The definers "the" and "a" specify which book is being referred to. 限定词“the”和“a”指明了指的是哪本书。|Synonyms: determiners, modifiers|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
