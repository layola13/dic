half-anglicized:/ˌhæf ˈæŋɡləsaɪzd/
adj.
半英化的
Partly adapted to English ways or customs; partially Anglicized.  A word or phrase borrowed from another language and adapted to English pronunciation, spelling, or grammar.
例句：The restaurant's menu featured a half-anglicized version of the traditional dish.  这家餐厅的菜单提供了一种半英化的传统菜肴。

例句：His name, originally from his native language, was half-anglicized for easier pronunciation.  他的名字，原本来自他的母语，为了更容易发音而被半英化了。
近义词：semi-Anglicized, partly Anglicized
反义词：fully Anglicized, completely Anglicized
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
half- (Old English healf) + Anglicized (from Anglicize, from Anglo- + -ize)
