nonpreparation:/ˌnɒnprɛpəˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 无准备，缺乏准备 | Lack of preparation; the state of being unprepared. |  His nonpreparation for the exam resulted in a failing grade.  他考试没准备导致不及格。

The team's nonpreparation was evident in their poor performance. 球队的缺乏准备在他们糟糕的表现中显而易见。 | synonyms: unpreparedness, lack of preparation | antonyms: preparation, readiness | noun
