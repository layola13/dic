livings:/ˈlɪvɪŋz/
noun
生计, 收入, 生活费用
means of earning money; livelihood.  Often used in the plural.
They earned their livings as farmers. 他们以务农为生。
近义词: livelihood, sustenance, income, means
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) expenses, debts
词性: noun
