compatriotisms: /kəmˌpeɪtriˈɒtɪzəmz/ | n. | 同胞情谊，爱国心（复数）|  The feeling of loyalty and affection that people have for their country, especially when they are abroad, or for the people who share their national identity.  It often manifests in a sense of shared history, culture, and destiny. |  Expatriate communities often exhibit strong compatriotisms, especially during times of national crisis.  海外侨民社区经常表现出强烈的同胞情谊，尤其是在国家危机时期。

Compatriotisms were evident in the support offered to refugees fleeing the conflict. 在为逃离冲突的难民提供的支持中，同胞情谊显而易见。 | 近义词： patriotism, nationalism, national pride | 反义词： xenophobia, treachery, disloyalty | 词性: 名词
