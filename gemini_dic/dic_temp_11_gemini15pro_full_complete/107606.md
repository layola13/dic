landstorm:/ˈlændstɔːrm/ (US), /ˈlændstɔːm/ (UK)| n. |沙尘暴|A strong wind that carries sand and dust over a large area.|The landstorm made it difficult to see the road ahead. 沙尘暴使得很难看清前方的道路。|近义词：dust storm, sandstorm|反义词:calm, clear weather|词性:名词
Etymology:  From 'land' + 'storm',  both of Germanic origin. 'Land' refers to the ground, and 'storm' refers to a violent disturbance of the atmosphere.
