destructibles:/dɪˈstrʌktɪblz/
n.
可破坏物；易毁坏物
Objects or things that can be destroyed.
The game features many destructibles, like crates and barrels.  这个游戏有很多可以破坏的物品，比如板条箱和桶。
The building was filled with highly flammable destructibles. 这栋建筑物里充满了高度易燃的物品。
synonyms: breakables, fragile items, perishables
antonyms: indestructibles, durables, permanents
noun
