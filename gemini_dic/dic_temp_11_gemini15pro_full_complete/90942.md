hurlers:/ˈhɜːrlərz/| n.|投掷者，掷球手|People who hurl something, often in sports like baseball or cricket.  Those who throw with great force.|The hurlers on both teams were exceptionally skilled.  两队的投掷者都非常熟练。|Synonyms: pitchers, throwers, chuckers|Antonyms: catchers, receivers|noun
