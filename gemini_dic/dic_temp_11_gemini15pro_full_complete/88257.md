hingeless:/ˈhɪndʒləs/| adj.|无铰链的，无枢纽的|Lacking a hinge or hinges; not hinged.  Something that is hingeless doesn't have a hinge, the movable joint that connects two parts and allows them to swing open and closed.|The hingeless laptop opened flat like a tablet. 这台无铰链的笔记本电脑可以像平板电脑一样平放打开。|近义词:seamless, unjointed, fixed|反义词:hinged, jointed|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From hinge + -less.  "Hinge" comes from Middle English "henge," from Old English "henge" meaning "hinge, hook, band." The suffix "-less" means "without" or "lacking."
