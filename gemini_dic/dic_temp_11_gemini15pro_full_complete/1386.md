accusant:/əˈkjuːzənt/| n.|原告，控告者，指控者|A person who brings a criminal charge against someone else. |The accuser pointed at the defendant and identified him as the assailant. 原告指着被告，指认他就是袭击者。|The accuser gave a detailed account of the incident. 控告者详细地叙述了事件的经过。|近义词：accuser, plaintiff, complainant|反义词：defendant, accused|noun
