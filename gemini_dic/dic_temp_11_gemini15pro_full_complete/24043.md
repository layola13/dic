breezeways:/ˈbriːzweɪz/ | n. | 有顶棚的通道，凉棚 | A covered passage, often open on the sides, connecting two buildings or leading from a building to a parking area or other outdoor space. |  The breezeway between the house and garage provided shelter from the rain.  房子和车库之间的有顶棚通道提供了避雨的地方。| Synonyms: passageway, walkway, corridor, arcade | Antonyms: enclosed space, room | noun

breezeways:/ˈbriːzweɪz/ | n. | 凉风习习的地方 | A place where a breeze often blows. | The breezeway offered a welcome respite from the heat. 凉风习习的地方提供了一个受欢迎的避暑场所。 | Synonyms: airy spot, breezy spot | Antonyms: stuffy place, windless area | noun
