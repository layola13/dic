monopolists:/məˈnɒpəlɪsts/ /məˈnɑːpəlɪsts/| n. |垄断者；独占者|A person or company that has a monopoly.  A monopolist controls the supply or trade in a commodity or service.|The monopolists controlled the price of oil.|Synonyms:  monopolies, trusts, cartels, magnates, tycoons|Antonyms: competitors, rivals|Noun
