nomothetic:/ˌnɑːməˈθɛtɪk/ | adj. | 法则性的，普遍适用的 | Relating to the discovery of general laws or principles.  | Nomothetic research aims to establish general laws and principles that apply to a wide range of individuals or situations.  法则性研究旨在建立适用于广泛个人或情况的一般规律和原则。 | 近义词: general, universal, law-based | 反义词: idiographic, particularistic | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Greek *nomothetes* "lawgiver," from *nomos* "law" + *thetes* "one who establishes."
