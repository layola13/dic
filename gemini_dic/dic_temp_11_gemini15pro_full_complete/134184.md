noninclinational:/ˌnɒnɪnklɪˈneɪʃənəl/
adjective
非倾向性的，没有倾向的
Not having or showing an inclination or preference; unbiased.
Her noninclinational stance on the controversial issue surprised everyone. 她在这个有争议的问题上不带倾向性的立场让每个人都感到惊讶。
The judge maintained a noninclinational demeanor throughout the trial.  法官在整个审判过程中保持着不带倾向性的态度。
近义词:neutral, impartial, unbiased, objective
反义词:biased, prejudiced, partial, subjective
词性:adjective
词源:non- (prefix meaning "not") + inclination (noun meaning "a tendency to do something") + -al (suffix forming adjectives)
