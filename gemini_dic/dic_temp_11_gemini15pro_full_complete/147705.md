overpreoccupied: /ˌoʊvərpriˈɑːkjəpaɪd/
adjective
过度专注的，过度担忧的
Excessively preoccupied; having one's attention completely absorbed by something, often to the exclusion of other important matters.  This implies a negative connotation, suggesting that the preoccupation is unhealthy or excessive.
He was overpreoccupied with his work and neglected his family. 他过度专注于工作而忽略了家人。

She's been overpreoccupied with her health lately. 她最近一直过度担忧自己的健康。

近义词: obsessed, fixated, consumed, engrossed
反义词: carefree, unworried, detached, indifferent
词性: adjective
词源: "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "preoccupied" (from Latin "praeoccupare" - to seize beforehand, occupy in advance)
