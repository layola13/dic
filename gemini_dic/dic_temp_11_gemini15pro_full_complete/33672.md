cingle:/ˈsɪŋɡəl/| n.|肚带；带状物；环带；(尤指马的) 腹带|A strap or girdle, especially one used to girth an animal, such as a horse. |The horse's cingle was too tight.  马的肚带太紧了。|近义词：girth, strap, band|反义词: N/A|名词

cingle:/ˈsɪŋɡəl/| v.|用带捆绑；用带束住|To fasten or bind with a cingle or girth. |He cinkled the saddle onto the horse. 他用肚带把马鞍固定在马上。|近义词：girth, fasten, secure|反义词:unfasten, loosen|动词
