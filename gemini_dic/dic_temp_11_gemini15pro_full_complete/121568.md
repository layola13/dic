misnavigated:/ˌmɪsnævɪɡeɪtɪd/
verb
错误导航，导航错误
navigated incorrectly; followed a wrong course or route.
The ship misnavigated and ended up in the wrong port. 这艘船导航错误，最终到达了错误的港口。
The driver misnavigated and got lost. 司机导航错误，迷路了。
近义词:lost, strayed, went astray, got lost, took a wrong turn
反义词:found the way, arrived correctly, navigated correctly
词性:verb
