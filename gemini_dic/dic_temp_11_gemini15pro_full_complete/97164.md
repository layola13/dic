infinitesimal: /ˌɪnfɪnɪˈtɛsɪməl/ | adj. n.| 极小的，无限小的；无穷小量 | An infinitesimal quantity or difference is so small that it is impossible to measure or calculate. |  The difference between the two values is infinitesimal. 这两个值之间的差异微乎其微。| synonyms: minute, tiny, microscopic, negligible | antonyms: huge, enormous, vast, immense | adjective, noun

infinitesimals: /ˌɪnfɪnɪˈtɛsɪməlz/ | n. | 无穷小；极小量 |  Infinitesimals are quantities that are considered to be infinitely small. | Calculus deals with infinitesimals. 微积分处理无穷小量。| synonyms: minute quantities, infinitely small values | antonyms: large quantities, substantial amounts | noun
