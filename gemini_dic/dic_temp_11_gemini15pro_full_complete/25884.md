burlesquers:/bərˈlɛskərz/
noun
滑稽剧演员（复数）
Performers who create and perform burlesques, a type of variety entertainment traditionally including ribald comedy, song, and dance.
The burlesquers donned elaborate costumes and exaggerated makeup. 滑稽剧演员们穿上了精致的服装，化着夸张的妆容。
The burlesquers poked fun at current events and popular culture. 滑稽剧演员们拿时事和流行文化开玩笑。
Synonyms: parodists, satirists, mimics
Antonyms: tragedians, dramatists
