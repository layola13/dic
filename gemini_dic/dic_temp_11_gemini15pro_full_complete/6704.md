allantoidea:/ˌælənˈtɔɪdiə/| n.| 尿囊膜；尿囊|A membrane that develops from the hindgut in embryos of reptiles, birds, and mammals, and plays an important role in the development of the placenta and umbilical cord in mammals.|The allantoidea is vital for gas exchange and waste removal in developing embryos.|近义词:allantois|反义词: None|noun

allantois:/əˈlæntɔɪs/| n.| 尿囊|The fetal membrane lying below the chorion in many vertebrates, formed as an outgrowth of the embryo's gut. In mammals, it forms part of the placenta.|The allantois helps to remove waste products from the developing embryo.|近义词:allantoidea|反义词: None|noun
