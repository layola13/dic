gbits:/ˈɡɪbɪts/| n. | 零碎信息；小道消息；花边新闻 | Small pieces of information, gossip, or trivial news. | Example: I heard some gbits about the new project, but nothing official. 我听到了一些关于新项目的小道消息，但没有官方消息。

Example:  She enjoys sharing gbits of celebrity gossip with her friends. 她喜欢和朋友们分享名人的八卦新闻。


|近义词：snippets, tidbits, scraps, fragments|反义词:  whole, complete |词性: noun|


ETYMOLOGY->
Likely a blend of "gibble-gabble" (meaning idle talk or chatter) and "bits" (small pieces).  While not definitively confirmed, this etymology aligns with the word's meaning.
