agricultural:/ˌæɡrɪˈkʌltʃərəl/
adjective|农业的，农用的|Relating to farming or agriculture. |例句：The agricultural industry is vital to the economy. 农业对经济至关重要。|例句：They use agricultural machinery to harvest crops. 他们使用农业机械收割庄稼。|近义词：farming, agrarian|反义词：industrial, urban|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *agriculturālis, from Latin agricultura (“agriculture”) + -ālis (adjective suffix).  The Latin agricultura is from ager (“field”) + cultūra (“cultivation”).
