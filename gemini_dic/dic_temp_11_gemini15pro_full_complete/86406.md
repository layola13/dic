hedonists:/ˈhiːdənɪsts/
n.
享乐主义者
People who believe that pleasure is the most important thing in life.
The ancient Epicureans were sometimes considered hedonists.  古代的伊壁鸠鲁主义者有时被认为是享乐主义者。
He denounced the hedonists of the modern world. 他谴责现代世界的享乐主义者。
近义词:sybarites, voluptuaries, sensualists
反义词:ascetics, puritans
