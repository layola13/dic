outblossomed:/ˌaʊtˈblɒsəmd/ |v. |胜过；开花更繁茂|To surpass in blossoming; to blossom more profusely than another. |The cherry tree outblossomed all the others in the orchard this year. 今年果园里的樱桃树比其他树都开花茂盛。|近义词：surpass, excel, outdo, outstrip |反义词：lag behind, fall short of |词性: 动词

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding, surpassing") + "blossom" (verb meaning "to produce flowers").  The prefix intensifies the meaning of the base verb.
