garlanded:/ˈɡɑːrləndɪd/
adjective
戴着花环的，用花环装饰的
Adorned or decorated with garlands.  A garland is a wreath or chain of flowers, leaves, or other material worn around the head or neck or used for decoration.
The bride walked down the aisle, garlanded with roses.  新娘走在过道上，戴着玫瑰花环。
The victorious athletes were garlanded with laurel wreaths. 凯旋的运动员们戴着月桂花环。
Synonyms: wreathed, festooned, adorned, decorated
Antonyms: bare, undecorated, plain
词性: adjective
