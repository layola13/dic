firings:/ˈfaɪərɪŋz/| n. |解雇，开火，烧制|The company announced a new round of firings.  公司宣布了新一轮的裁员。|例句：The firings were a result of the company's restructuring. 这些解雇是公司重组的结果。|近义词：dismissals, layoffs, terminations|反义词:hirings, employments|noun

firings:/ˈfaɪərɪŋz/| n. |射击，发射|The firings of the artillery were deafening.  炮兵的射击震耳欲聋。|例句：The firings ceased after a few minutes.  射击几分钟后停止了。|近义词：shots, discharges|反义词: - |noun


Etymology: From the verb "fire" which comes from Old English "fyr"  meaning fire, and the suffix "-ing" which indicates a process or action.
