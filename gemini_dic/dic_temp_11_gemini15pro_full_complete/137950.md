nonweaknesses:/nɒnˈwiːknɪsɪz/ |n. | 非弱点；优势；长处 |  The absence of weaknesses; often used to emphasize strengths or positive attributes.  This word is not standard English and is considered informal. It's formed by adding the prefix "non-" to "weaknesses".  It's often used in business or self-improvement contexts. |  Instead of focusing on nonweaknesses, let's address the actual problems.与其关注非弱点，不如解决实际问题。|Synonyms: strengths, advantages, assets, forte, virtues | Antonyms: weaknesses, flaws, shortcomings, vulnerabilities, defects | noun
