gestatory:/ˈdʒɛstətɔːri/
adjective
怀孕的，妊娠的
Relating to pregnancy or gestation.
The gestatory period of a horse is about 11 months.  马的妊娠期约为11个月。
She experienced various complications during the gestatory process.  她在妊娠过程中经历了各种并发症。
近义词: pregnant, gravid
反义词: non-pregnant, barren
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *gestatorius, from gestatus, past participle of gestare "to bear, carry," frequentative of gerere "to carry, bear."
