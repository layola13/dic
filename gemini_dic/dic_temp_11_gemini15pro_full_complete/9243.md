antipathetical:/ˌæntɪpəˈθɛtɪk(ə)l/
adjective
相反的，对立的，格格不入的
Showing or expressing strong opposition or dislike. Having a natural aversion.
例句：His views are antipathetical to mine. 他的观点与我的观点截然相反。

例句：The two approaches are antipathetical. 这两种方法是对立的。

近义词：opposed, hostile, averse, contrary, conflicting, incompatible
反义词：sympathetic, similar, compatible, harmonious
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *antipatheticus, from Ancient Greek ἀντιπαθητικός (antipathētikós, “having antipathy”), from ἀντιπάθεια (antipátheia, “antipathy”). Equivalent to antipathy +‎ -ical.
