noncollusion:/ˌnɑːnkəˈluːʒən/
noun
不串通，不勾结
The absence of an agreement, especially an illegal or secret one, between two or more people or companies.
The investigation found no evidence of noncollusion between the two companies.  调查未发现两家公司之间有不串通的证据。
The government stressed the noncollusion in the bidding process. 政府强调了招标过程中的不串通行为。
Synonyms: independence, autonomy, noncooperation
Antonyms: collusion, conspiracy, collaboration
n.
