means-tested:/ˈmiːnzˌtɛstɪd/ | adj. | 经过经济状况调查的；根据经济情况审核的 |  Relating to a system in which financial support from the government is given only to people who have incomes below a particular level. |  Medicaid is a means-tested program for low-income individuals.  医疗补助是针对低收入人群的一项经经济状况调查的计划。|  Eligibility for food stamps is means-tested.  食品券的资格需要经过经济状况调查。|近义词: income-based, needs-based | 反义词: universal | 词性: adjective
