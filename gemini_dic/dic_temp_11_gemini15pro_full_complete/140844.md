on-the-boards:/ˌɑːn ðə ˈbɔːrdz/
adjective
计划中的，筹备中的，正在进行的
Being planned or developed; in progress.
The company has several new products on the boards.  这家公司有几个新产品正在筹备中。
The theater has a new play on the boards for next season.  剧院正在筹备一部新戏，准备下一季上演。
Synonyms: planned, underway, in progress, in the works, in the pipeline, developing, forthcoming
Antonyms: completed, finished, abandoned, shelved, canceled
词性: adjective
