melicratory:/ˌmɛlɪˈkreɪtəri/ | adj. | 流蜜的；甜蜜的；像蜂蜜的 | Relating to or resembling honey; sweet. | The melicratory scent of the blossoms filled the air.  花朵的蜜香弥漫在空气中。 |  近义词: honeyed, sweet, nectarous | 反义词: bitter, sour | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *melicratus, from Greek melikratos (μελίκρατος), from meli (μέλι) "honey" + kerannynai (κεράννυμι) "to mix."
