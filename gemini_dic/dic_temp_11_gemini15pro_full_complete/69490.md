felicitate: /fəˈlɪsɪteɪt/ | vt. | 祝贺，庆祝 | To express congratulations to someone; to compliment upon a happy event. |  I felicitated her on her recent promotion. 我祝贺她最近升职了。

|  The community felicitated the mayor on his successful first term. 社区祝贺市长第一任期圆满成功。

| 近义词: congratulate, commend, compliment | 反义词: condole, commiserate | 词性: verb (动词)

felicity: /fəˈlɪsɪti/ | n. | 幸福；恰当，贴切；巧妙 |  Great happiness; bliss; appropriateness; aptness. | She spoke with great felicity and charm. 她谈吐优雅迷人。

| The felicity of the phrasing made the poem memorable.  措辞的贴切使这首诗令人难忘。

| 近义词: happiness, bliss, aptness, appropriateness | 反义词: unhappiness, misery, inappropriateness | 词性: noun (名词)

felicitous: /fəˈlɪsɪtəs/ | adj. |  恰当的；贴切的；幸福的 | Well-chosen or suited to the circumstances; marked by happiness or good fortune. | Her felicitous remark eased the tension in the room. 她恰如其分的评论缓和了房间里的紧张气氛。

| It was a felicitous occasion for the whole family. 对整个家庭来说，这是一个幸福的时刻。

| 近义词: apt, suitable, appropriate, fortunate, happy | 反义词: inappropriate, unsuitable, unfortunate, unhappy | 词性: adjective (形容词)
