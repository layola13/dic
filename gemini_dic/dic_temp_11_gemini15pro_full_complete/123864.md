morani:/məˈrɑːni/| n. |莫拉尼人，马赛武士|A young Maasai warrior, typically between the ages of 15 and 30, who has undergone initiation and is responsible for protecting the community's livestock.|例句：The morani stood guard, spears in hand, watching over the cattle. 莫拉尼人手持长矛站岗，守护着牛群。

例句：The morani, adorned in colorful beads and ochre, performed a traditional dance.  盛装打扮的莫拉尼人，用彩珠和赭土装饰着自己，表演着传统舞蹈。|近义词：warrior, soldier |反义词: civilian, elder |词性:noun
