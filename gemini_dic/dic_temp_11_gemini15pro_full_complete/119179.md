mezzanine: /ˈmezənɪn/ (US) /ˈmezəniːn/ (UK)| n. | 夹层; 中层楼;  |A low story between two others in a building, typically between the ground and first floors.| The office has a mezzanine floor for extra storage space. 办公室有一个夹层，用来存放额外的物品。|  The restaurant had a mezzanine level overlooking the main dining area.  这家餐厅有一个可以俯瞰主要用餐区的夹层。| synonyms: balcony, loft, gallery | antonyms: basement, cellar | noun

mezzanine: /ˈmezənɪn/ (US) /ˈmezəniːn/ (UK)| adj. | 夹层的; 中层楼的; |Relating to or denoting a mezzanine floor.| Mezzanine financing is a hybrid of debt and equity financing. 夹层融资是债务融资和股权融资的混合体。|  The mezzanine level provided additional seating for the performance.  夹层提供了额外的座位供表演使用。| synonyms: intermediate, middle | antonyms: top, bottom | adjective
