defectionist:/dɪˈfɛkʃənɪst/
n.|叛逃者，变节者|A person who abandons a country, cause, party, or religion for another.|The defectionist was granted asylum in a neighboring country.|Synonyms: deserter, renegade, traitor, turncoat|Antonyms: loyalist, patriot|noun

defectionist:/dɪˈfɛkʃənɪst/
adj.|变节的，叛逃的|Relating to or characteristic of a defectionist.|His defectionist tendencies became apparent when he started criticizing the party.|Synonyms: traitorous, disloyal, renegade|Antonyms: loyal, faithful|adjective
