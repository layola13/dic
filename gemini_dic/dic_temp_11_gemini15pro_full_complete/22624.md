boozy:/ˈbuːzi/| adj.|微醉的，嗜酒的|Given to or marked by the consumption of alcohol; intoxicated.|例句：He had a boozy lunch with his colleagues. 他和同事们一起吃了一顿酒足饭饱的午餐。

例句：The atmosphere in the pub was boozy and cheerful. 酒馆里的气氛既醉人又愉快。|近义词：drunk, tipsy, intoxicated, inebriated|反义词:sober, temperate|词性:adjective
