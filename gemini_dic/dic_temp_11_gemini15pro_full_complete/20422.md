bitless:/ˈbɪtlɪs/| adj.|无嚼子的，不用嚼子的|Without a bit (the metal mouthpiece of a bridle).|The stable offers bitless riding lessons for those who prefer a gentler approach.  这个马厩为喜欢更温和方法的人提供无嚼子的骑马课程。|Example 2:  She transitioned her horse to a bitless bridle and noticed a significant improvement in his relaxation. 她把她的马换成了无嚼子的马勒，注意到它的放松程度有了显著改善。|Synonyms: bridleless (though this implies no bridle at all, not just the absence of a bit)|Antonyms: bitted|adjective
