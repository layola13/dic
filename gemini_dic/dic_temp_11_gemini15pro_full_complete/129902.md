non-Israelites:/nɒnˈɪzreɪlaɪts/ | n. |非以色列人|People who are not Israelites, i.e., not descendants of Jacob (also called Israel) and therefore not members of the twelve tribes of Israel.  Refers to any person or group of people outside of the ancient Israelite community. |The Canaanites, Philistines, and Egyptians were all non-Israelites.  迦南人、非利士人和埃及人都是非以色列人。|The laws regarding sacrifices were different for Israelites and non-Israelites.  关于祭祀的律法，以色列人和非以色列人有所不同。|  Gentiles, foreigners|Israelites|noun
