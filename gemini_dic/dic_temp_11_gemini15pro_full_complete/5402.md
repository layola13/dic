almanac: /ˈɔːlmənæk/ (US), /ˈɔːlmənæk/ (UK)| n. |年鉴；历书|An annual publication containing information such as weather forecasts, astronomical data, tide tables, and other tabular information often arranged according to the calendar.|I checked the almanac to see the best time to plant tomatoes.|Synonyms: calendar, yearbook, annual, register|Antonyms: None|Noun
