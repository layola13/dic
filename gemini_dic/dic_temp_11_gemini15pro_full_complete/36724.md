collocative:/ˈkɒləkeɪtɪv/ | adj. | 搭配的，组合的 | Relating to or concerned with collocation.  |  A collocative meaning is the meaning a word acquires in the company of certain other words. 搭配意义是一个词在与某些其他词连用时获得的意义。 | Collocative restrictions on grammar are an important part of linguistic competence. 语法上的搭配限制是语言能力的重要组成部分。| synonymous:  combinatory, related | antonymous: unrelated, independent | adjective
