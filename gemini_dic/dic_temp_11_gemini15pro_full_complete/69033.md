fatuity:/fəˈtjuːɪti/ /fəˈtuːɪti/| n. |愚蠢；愚昧；愚蠢的行为；愚蠢的想法|lack of good sense; foolishness; a foolish act or idea|His infatuation with her led to many acts of fatuity. 他对她的迷恋导致了许多愚蠢的行为。|Synonyms: foolishness, stupidity, silliness, absurdity, idiocy|Antonyms: wisdom, intelligence, sagacity, prudence, sense|noun

fatuous:/ˈfætʃuəs/ /ˈfætuəs/| adj. |愚蠢的；愚昧的|silly and pointless|a fatuous grin  愚蠢的笑容|Synonyms: silly, foolish, stupid, inane, vacuous|Antonyms: sensible, intelligent, wise, smart, astute|adjective
