bogusly:/ˈboʊɡəsli/
adverb
虚假地，伪造地
in a bogus manner; falsely.
He bogusly claimed to be a doctor. 他谎称自己是医生。
She bogusly signed her father's name on the check.  她在支票上伪造了她父亲的签名。

近义词:fraudulently, falsely, dishonestly, deceptively, spuriously
反义词:genuinely, truly, honestly, authentically
词性:adverb

词源: From bogus + -ly. Bogus is of uncertain origin, possibly from a counterfeiting machine called a "bogus."
