outtricking:/ˌaʊtˈtrɪkɪŋ/
verb
以智取胜，胜过；瞒骗；巧妙地胜过
The act of tricking or deceiving someone more cleverly than they trick or deceive you.
He succeeded by outtricking his opponents. 他靠智胜对手而获得成功。
Synonyms: outsmarting, outwitting, deceiving, cheating, defrauding
Antonyms: being outsmarted, being outwitted, being deceived
词性: 动词
