distant:/ˈdɪstənt/ (US), /ˈdɪstənt/ (UK)| adj.|遥远的；冷漠的；疏远的；不友好的|Far away in space or time; not friendly; reserved or aloof.|例句：The distant mountains were capped with snow. 遥远的山峰被白雪覆盖。|近义词：remote, far, faraway, aloof, cold|反义词：near, nearby, close, friendly, warm|词性:形容词

distant:/ˈdɪstənt/ (US), /ˈdɪstənt/ (UK)| n.|远处的事物；[乐]远关系调|Something that is far away; a remote key.| 例句:  The ship appeared as a distant speck on the horizon. 那艘船像一个远处的斑点出现在地平线上。|近义词：horizon, far distance |反义词:foreground, proximity|词性:名词
