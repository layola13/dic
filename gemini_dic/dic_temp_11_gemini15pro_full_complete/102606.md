jailhouse:/ˈdʒeɪlˌhaʊs/ | n. |监狱；拘留所|A jail; a building for the confinement of persons held in lawful custody, especially while awaiting trial or serving a short sentence.|He spent two years in the jailhouse for theft. 他因为盗窃在监狱里待了两年。|Synonyms: jail, prison, lockup, penitentiary|Antonyms: freedom, liberty|noun
