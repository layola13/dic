interosculant:/ˌɪntərˈɒskjʊlənt/| adj.|相互接吻的；密切相关的；中间类型的|Connecting or blending into one another; intermediate in character between two groups, species, etc.|例句：The interosculant forms between the two species made it difficult to classify them. 这两个物种之间的中间类型使得难以对它们进行分类。

例句：The artist created an interosculant pattern, blending various colors seamlessly. 艺术家创造了一个相互融合的图案，将各种颜色无缝地融合在一起。|近义词：intermediate, transitional, blending, merging|反义词：distinct, separate, discrete|词性:adjective
Etymology->
From Latin *interosculans*, present participle of *interosculari* (“to kiss between, kiss mutually”), from *inter-* (“between”) + *osculari* (“to kiss”), from *osculum* (“kiss, little mouth”).
