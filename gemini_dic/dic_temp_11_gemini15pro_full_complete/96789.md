inergetic:/ˌɪnərˈdʒɛtɪk/
adjective
缺乏能量的; 惰性的
Lacking energy; inert.
He felt inergetic and unable to motivate himself to exercise. 他感到浑身无力，无法激励自己去锻炼。
近义词:inactive, lethargic, sluggish, inert, passive
反义词:energetic, active, dynamic, vigorous
词性:adjective

词源:
From Latin *inergeticus*, from iners (“idle, inactive”).  The English word was coined in the 19th century.
