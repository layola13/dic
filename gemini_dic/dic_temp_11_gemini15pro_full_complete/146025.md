overdramatizes:/ˌoʊvərˈdræməˌtaɪzɪz/
verb
过分戏剧化；夸大其词
To exaggerate the emotional or dramatic significance of something.
She overdramatizes everything that happens to her, turning minor setbacks into major tragedies. 她把她身上发生的每件事都过分戏剧化，把小挫折变成大悲剧。
overstates, exaggerates, embellishes
understates, downplays, minimizes
v.
