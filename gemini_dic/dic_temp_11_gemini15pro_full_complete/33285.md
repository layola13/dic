USAGE->
Simple Present:
What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? = 他晚饭在做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? = 她在商店买了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do last night? = 你昨晚做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? = 她对这部电影说了些什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? = 她参加聚会穿了什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you went to bed? = 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? = 她生病前吃了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I called? = 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 她换专业之前一直在学什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will she study in college? = 她会在大学里学什么？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be wearing to the wedding? = 她会穿什么去参加婚礼？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the age of 30? = 到30岁时你将取得什么成就？
What will she have learned by the end of the course? = 到课程结束时她将学到什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying for by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？


Subjunctive Mood:
If I knew what he wanted, I would give it to him. = 如果我知道他想要什么，我就会给他。
I wish I knew what the answer was. = 我希望我知道答案是什么。


Passive Voice:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being built next to the library? = 图书馆旁边在建什么？


Interrogative Sentence:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？


Negative Sentence:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
He doesn't understand what I'm saying. = 他不明白我在说什么。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What the hell! = 搞什么鬼！
What if it rains? = 如果下雨怎么办？
What for? = 为了什么？
No matter what happens, I'll be there. = 无论发生什么，我都会在那里。
