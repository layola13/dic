opinionable:/əˈpɪnjənəbl/| adj.|可提出意见的，可评论的|Open to personal opinion; debatable.  Allowing or susceptible to personal opinion or interpretation.|Her taste in music is highly opinionable. 她对音乐的品味是很主观的。|近义词：debatable, disputable, arguable, contestable|反义词：undisputable, incontestable, irrefutable|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From opinion + -able.  Meaning "capable of being held as an opinion" is from the late 19th century.
