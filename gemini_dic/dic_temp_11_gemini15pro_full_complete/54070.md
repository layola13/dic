ETYMOLOGY->
From disease + -ful + -ness.  Disease (from Old French *desaise "discomfort, uneasiness," from des- "opposite of, away" + aise "ease") meaning "illness, sickness." The suffix "-ful" means "full of" or "characterized by," and "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives.
