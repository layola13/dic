deposit:/dɪˈpɒzɪt/ (British), /dəˈpɑːzɪt/ (American)| n. vt.| 存款；押金；沉淀物；存放；使沉积|A deposit is a sum of money which is part of the full price of something, paid when you agree to buy it. The rest of the price is paid later.  A deposit is also a sum of money that is paid into a bank account. / To deposit something means to put or leave it in a particular place.|例句：I put down a deposit on a new car. 我付了新车的定金。

She deposited the check in her account. 她把支票存入了她的账户。|近义词：payment, down payment, installment, sediment, precipitate|反义词：withdrawal, refund|词性：名词, 动词
