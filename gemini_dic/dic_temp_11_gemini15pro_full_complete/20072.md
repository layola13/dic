biorhythmic:/ˌbaɪoʊˈrɪðmɪk/
adjective
生物节律的
Relating to or influenced by biorhythms, which are cyclical biological processes.
The athlete believed his biorhythmic cycles affected his performance.  运动员相信他的生物节律周期会影响他的表现。
Synonyms:  cyclical, rhythmic, periodic
Antonyms:  arrhythmic, non-cyclical
词性: adjective

biorhythm:/ˈbaɪoʊˌrɪðəm/
noun
生物节律
A cyclical biological process, such as the sleep-wake cycle.
He attributed his mood swings to his biorhythm. 他将他的情绪波动归因于他的生物节律。
Synonyms:  cycle, rhythm
Antonyms:  arrhythmia
词性: noun
