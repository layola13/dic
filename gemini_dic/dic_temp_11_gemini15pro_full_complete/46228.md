cystocarp:/ˈsɪstəʊkɑːrp/ (US) /ˈsɪstəkɑːp/ (UK)| n. |囊果|A fruiting structure formed after fertilization in certain red algae, consisting of carposporangia surrounded by a pericarp.|The cystocarp of this red alga is particularly prominent.  这种红藻的囊果特别突出。|The researchers studied the development of the cystocarp in different species of red algae. 研究人员研究了不同种类红藻中囊果的发育。|近义词:carposporophyte (in some contexts) |反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Ancient Greek *κύστις (kýstis, “bladder, pouch”) + *καρπός (karpós, “fruit”).
