chafflike:/ˈtʃæflaɪk/| adj.|像谷壳的；轻薄的；无价值的|Resembling chaff; light and worthless.  Something that is chafflike is similar to the dry, scaly protective casings of seeds of cereal grain, often considered worthless.|The old woman's bones were chafflike and brittle. 老妇人的骨头像谷壳一样易碎。|近义词：worthless, flimsy, insubstantial, trifling|反义词：valuable, substantial, significant|词性:形容词
