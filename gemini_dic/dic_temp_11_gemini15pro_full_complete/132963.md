nonempiricisms:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpɪrɪsɪzəmz/
|n.|非经验主义|Theories based on nonempiricisms are often criticized for lacking practical grounding. 基于非经验主义的理论经常因缺乏实践基础而受到批评。|
|英文解析:  Plural form of nonempiricism.  Referring to doctrines or theories not based on experience or observation.|
|例句：His philosophical stance was grounded in nonempiricisms, leading him to reject scientific findings. 他的哲学立场基于非经验主义，导致他拒绝科学发现。|

|例句：Debates about nonempiricisms often center on the role of intuition and reason in acquiring knowledge. 关于非经验主义的辩论通常集中在直觉和理性在获取知识中的作用。|
