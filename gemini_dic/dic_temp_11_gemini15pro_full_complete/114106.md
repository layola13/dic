magnolia: /mæɡˈnoʊliə/ (US) /mæɡˈnəʊliə/ (UK)| n. |木兰，木兰花；乳白色|A tree or shrub with large, fragrant flowers, typically white, pink, or purple.  The flowers often appear before the leaves in spring.  The wood of the magnolia tree is also sometimes used in furniture making.|例句：The magnolia tree in my yard is in full bloom.  我家院子里的木兰树盛开了。

例句：She wore a dress the color of a magnolia blossom. 她穿着一件木兰花色的连衣裙。|近义词：blossom, flower|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps decay, wilt for the flower itself) |词性: noun
