breathless:/ˈbreθləs/| adj.|气喘吁吁的，屏息的，激动的|Lacking breath, especially because of exertion or excitement. Also, extremely exciting or thrilling.|After running up the stairs, I was breathless. 跑上楼梯后，我气喘吁吁。|The breathless pace of the movie kept me on the edge of my seat. 电影令人窒息的节奏让我一直处于紧张状态。|Synonyms: winded, gasping, panting, out of breath, amazed, awestruck|Antonyms: calm, composed, relaxed|adjective
