brown-bag:/ˈbraʊn bæɡ/ | n. |自带午餐；自带午餐的聚会；|A lunch carried to work or school, typically in a brown paper bag.  A meeting or seminar held during lunchtime where attendees bring their own lunches. |例句：I brown-bag it most days to save money. 我大多数日子都自带午餐以省钱。| 近义词：packed lunch, sack lunch | 反义词: dine out, eat out | noun

brown-bag:/ˈbraʊn bæɡ/ | v. |自带午餐；|To bring one's own lunch to work or school. | 例句：She brown-bags it every day. 她每天都自带午餐。 | 近义词：pack a lunch, bring a lunch | 反义词: dine out, eat out | verb
