meeters:/ˈmiːtərz/
noun
聚会者，开会者，见面者
People who meet for a particular purpose.
The meeters gathered in the conference room.  聚会者聚集在会议室里。
The online meeters discussed the new project. 在线会议的参与者讨论了新项目。
synonyms: attendees, participants, gatherers
antonyms: absentees, non-participants
n.
