monosymmetrical:/ˌmɒnəsɪˈmɛtrɪkəl/ /ˌmɑːnəsɪˈmɛtrɪkəl/

A monosymmetrical cross-section has only one axis of symmetry. | The bridge has a monosymmetrical cross-section. 这座桥的横截面是单对称的。| The building features a monosymmetrical facade. 这座建筑的特点是单对称的正面。 | asymmetrical, unsymmetrical | symmetrical, bisymmetrical | adjective
