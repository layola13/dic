outjumping:/ˌaʊtˈdʒʌmpɪŋ/
verb
超越跳跃，跳得比…更远/高
The act of jumping further or higher than someone or something else.
Michael Jordan was famous for outjumping his opponents. 迈克尔·乔丹以跳得比对手更高而闻名。
outleap, surpass, exceed
