banderillero:/ˌbændərɪˈljɛroʊ/ | n. | 斗牛士助手，花镖手 | A bullfighter's assistant whose role is to plant banderillas (barbed darts) into the bull's shoulders. |  The banderillero skillfully placed the banderillas into the bull's shoulders. 斗牛士助手熟练地将花镖插进了公牛的肩膀。| Synonyms:  matador's assistant,  torero's assistant | Antonyms:  bull, matador (in a sense, as they are opposing figures in the bullfight)| noun
