demonstrations:/ˌdemənˈstreɪʃənz/
n.
示威游行；证明；演示
a public meeting or march protesting against something or expressing views on a political issue; the act of showing someone how to do something or how something works.
例句：The demonstrations against the war continued for weeks.  反战示威持续了数周。

例句：The teacher gave a demonstration of the new software. 老师演示了新软件。

近义词：protests, rallies, marches, exhibitions, displays
反义词：suppression, concealment

词性: 名词
