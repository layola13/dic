hexacapsular:/ˌhɛksəˈkæpsjʊlər/
adjective
六囊的；有六个蒴的
Having six capsules or compartments.
The hexacapsular fruit splits open to release its seeds.  这种六囊的果实裂开释放种子。
近义词: six-capsuled
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider describing something with a different number of capsules, e.g., unicapsular, bicapsular)
词性: adjective
