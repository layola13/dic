egal:/ˈiːɡəl/ | adj. | 平等的，相等的 | Relating to or believing in the principle that all people are equal and deserve equal rights and opportunities. |  Example:  All people are egal before the law.  所有人在法律面前一律平等。

Example:  She advocated for a more egal society.  她倡导一个更平等的社会。 | Synonyms: equal, equitable, just, fair, impartial, even-handed | Antonyms: unequal, inequitable, unjust, unfair, biased, prejudiced | adjective
