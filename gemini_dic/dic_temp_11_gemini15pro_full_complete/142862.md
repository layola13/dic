out-jumps:/ˌaʊtˈdʒʌmps/
verb
超越，跳得比...远/高
To jump further or higher than.
He out-jumped all his competitors in the high jump. 他在跳高比赛中跳得比所有对手都高。
surpasses, exceeds, beats
lags behind, falls short of
词性:动词

out-jumps:/ˌaʊtˈdʒʌmps/
noun
超越，跳得比...远/高的动作
The act of jumping further or higher than someone or something.
His out-jump secured him the gold medal. 他的超越让他获得了金牌。
leap, bound
fall, stumble
词性:名词
