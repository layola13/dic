haplostemonous:/ˌhæpləˈstɛmənəs/
adjective
单轮雄蕊的，单体雄蕊的
Having only one whorl of stamens.  A flower is haplostemonous when its stamens are arranged in a single whorl and are equal in number to the petals or sepals.
The flower of *Primula vulgaris* is haplostemonous.  报春花的雄蕊是单轮的。
synonym: isostemonous (sometimes used when stamens equal petals in number)
antonym: diplostemonous, obdiplostemonous (having two whorls of stamens)
词性: adjective
