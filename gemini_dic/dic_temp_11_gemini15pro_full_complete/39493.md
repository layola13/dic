connoting:/kəˈnəʊtɪŋ/| v.|暗示，意味着，隐含|Implying or suggesting an idea or feeling in addition to the literal or primary meaning.|Her silence connoted disapproval. 她的沉默暗示着不赞成。|Synonyms: suggesting, implying, intimating|Antonyms: denoting, stating explicitly|Verb

connotation:/ˌkɒnəˈteɪʃn/| n.|内涵，含义，隐含意义|An idea or feeling that a word invokes in addition to its literal or primary meaning.|The word "home" has connotations of warmth and comfort. “家”这个词包含着温暖和舒适的含义。|Synonyms: implication, nuance, undertone|Antonyms: denotation, literal meaning|Noun
