desirable:/dɪˈzaɪərəbl/| adj.|可取的，值得拥有的，令人满意的|Something desirable is worth having or doing, or is likely to give one satisfaction.|A desirable residence in a quiet neighborhood. 在一个安静的街区拥有一个令人满意的住所。|近义词：attractive, appealing, worthwhile, preferable|反义词：undesirable, objectionable, unpleasant|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Middle English *desirable*, from Old French *desirable*, from *desirer* (“to desire”) + *-able*, from Latin *dēsīderābilis* (“desirable”), from *dēsīderō* (“long for, wish for”) + *-bilis* (“-able”).
