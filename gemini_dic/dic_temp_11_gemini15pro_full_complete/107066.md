ETYMOLOGY->
From lace + -y.  来自 lace（蕾丝）+ -y（形容词后缀）。  The word "lace" comes from Old French "las,"  "lace, snare, net," from Vulgar Latin *laceum, from Latin "laqueus" "noose, snare, trap."  “lace”一词来源于古法语“las”，“蕾丝，陷阱，网”，来自通俗拉丁语*laceum，来自拉丁语“laqueus”“套索，陷阱，圈套”。
