giggler:/ˈɡɪɡlər/| n.|爱傻笑的人，咯咯笑的人|A person who giggles frequently.|She's such a giggler, always laughing about something. 她真是个爱傻笑的人，总是咯咯地笑个不停。|Example:  He's a bit of a giggler, isn't he? 他有点爱傻笑，不是吗？|Synonyms: laugher, chuckler|Antonyms: grouch, complainer|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "giggle" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting a person or thing that performs the action of the verb).  "Giggle" itself likely originates from a Scandinavian source, related to Old Norse "gikla" (to tickle).
