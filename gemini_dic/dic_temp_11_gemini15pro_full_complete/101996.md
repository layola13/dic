isocitrate:/ˌaɪsoʊˈsɪtreɪt/ | n. |异柠檬酸|A salt or ester of isocitric acid.  It is an intermediate in the citric acid cycle (Krebs cycle).|Isocitrate is oxidized to alpha-ketoglutarate in the Krebs cycle. 异柠檬酸在三羧酸循环中被氧化成α-酮戊二酸。|

Isocitrate dehydrogenase catalyzes the oxidative decarboxylation of isocitrate. 异柠檬酸脱氢酶催化异柠檬酸的氧化脱羧。|Synonym: isocitric acid|Antonym: None (as it's a specific chemical compound)|Noun
