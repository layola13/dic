amenorrhoea: /əˌmenəˈriːə/ (UK) /əˌmenəˈriːə/ (US)| n. |闭经；无月经|Absence of menstruation.|Example:  The athlete suffered from amenorrhoea due to excessive training.  这位运动员由于过度训练而闭经。

Example: Amenorrhoea can be a symptom of various underlying health conditions. 闭经可能是各种潜在健康问题的症状。|Synonym: amenia, absence of menstruation|Antonym: menstruation, menses|noun
