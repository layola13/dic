extravasation: /ɪkˌstrævəˈseɪʃən/ | n. | 渗出；外渗；溢出 | The leakage of blood, lymph, or other fluid, such as an anticancer drug, from a blood vessel or lymph vessel into the surrounding tissues. |  The patient experienced extravasation of the chemotherapy drug, causing tissue damage. 病人的化疗药物外渗，导致了组织损伤。| Example: The nurse monitored the IV site carefully to prevent extravasation of the medication. 护士仔细监测静脉注射部位，以防止药物外渗。 | Synonyms: leakage, seepage, effusion | Antonyms: containment, retention | noun

extravasate: /ɪkˈstrævəseɪt/ | v. | （液体）渗出，漏出 | (of fluids) escape from a vessel into the tissues. | The contrast dye extravasated from the vein during the CT scan.  造影剂在CT扫描期间从静脉渗出。 | Example:  The blood extravasated from the ruptured artery. 血液从破裂的动脉中渗出。| Synonyms: leak, seep, ooze | Antonyms: contain, retain | verb
