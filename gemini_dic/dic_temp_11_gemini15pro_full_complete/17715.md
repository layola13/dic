bedclothes: /ˈbedkloʊðz/ (US) /ˈbedkləʊðz/ (UK)
n.
床上用品；寝具
Items such as sheets, blankets, and quilts used on a bed.
She carefully folded the bedclothes and put them away. 她仔细地叠好床上用品并将它们收起来。
The bedclothes were damp from the humidity. 床上用品因为潮湿而有些湿。
近义词: bedding, linens
反义词:  (No direct antonym)
词性: noun
