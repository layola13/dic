medler:/ˈmedlər/| n.|爱管闲事的人，干预者|A person who meddles; an interferer in other people's affairs.|例句：He's such a medler, always sticking his nose in other people's business. 他真是个爱管闲事的人，总是把鼻子伸到别人的事情里。

例句：The neighbors considered her a medler because she was always offering unsolicited advice. 邻居们认为她是个爱管闲事的人，因为她总是主动提供不必要的建议。|近义词：busybody, interferer, intruder, interloper|反义词:mind your own business|词性:noun
