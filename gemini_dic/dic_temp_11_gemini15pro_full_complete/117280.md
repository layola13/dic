mechanizer:/ˈmɛkəˌnaɪzər/
n.|机械化者，机械化部队|A person or thing that mechanizes something. Someone who introduces or uses machines to replace manual labor or animal power, or a military unit equipped with mechanized equipment.|The farmer, a true mechanizer, used tractors and harvesters for all his work.  这位农民是一位真正的机械化者，他所有的工作都使用拖拉机和收割机。|mechanist, modernizer|traditionalist
