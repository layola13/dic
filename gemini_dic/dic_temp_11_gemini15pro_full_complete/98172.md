inscriptible:/ɪnˈskrɪptɪbl/
adjective
可刻写的，可铭记的
Able to be inscribed or written on.  Suitable for inscription.
The smooth surface of the monument was perfectly inscriptible. 这座纪念碑光滑的表面非常适合刻字。
inscribable
non-inscriptible
adjective
