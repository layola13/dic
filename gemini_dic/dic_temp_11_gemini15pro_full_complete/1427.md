ace-it:/ˈeɪs ɪt/| v.|完美完成；取得成功；出色地完成|To succeed or perform exceptionally well, especially on a test or other challenging task.|例句：I aced it! 我成功了！

例句：She studied hard and aced the exam. 她努力学习，并在考试中取得了优异的成绩。|近义词：succeed, triumph, excel, nail it, pass with flying colors|反义词:fail, flunk, bomb|词性:动词
