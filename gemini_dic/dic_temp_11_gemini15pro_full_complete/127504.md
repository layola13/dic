necklike:/ˈnɛklaɪk/
adjective
像脖子的；颈状的
Resembling a neck in shape or form.
The vase had a long, necklike base.  这个花瓶有一个细长的、像脖子一样的底座。
The narrow, necklike passage led to a hidden chamber. 这条狭窄的、像脖子一样的通道通向一个隐藏的房间。
近义词:cervical, columnar
反义词: N/A
词性:adjective

Etymology: neck + -like.  "Neck" comes from Old English *hēafod-necca "head-neck," from Proto-Germanic *knakkaz "neck." "-like" is a suffix meaning "resembling, having the characteristics of," from Old English -līc.
