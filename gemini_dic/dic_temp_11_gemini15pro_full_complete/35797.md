cockered:/ˈkɑːkərd/ (US) /ˈkɒkəd/ (UK)| adj.|被溺爱的，娇惯的|Pampered, indulged, treated with excessive leniency.|例句：The cockered child refused to eat his vegetables. 这个被宠坏的孩子拒绝吃蔬菜。

例句：He was a cockered young man, used to getting his own way. 他是一个娇生惯养的年轻人，习惯于随心所欲。

近义词：pampered, spoiled, indulged, babied|反义词：disciplined, neglected, independent|词性:形容词

cocker:/ˈkɑːkər/ (US) /ˈkɒkər/ (UK)| v.|溺爱，娇惯|To pamper, indulge, or treat with excessive leniency.|例句：She cockered her dog with treats and toys. 她用零食和玩具溺爱她的狗。

例句：Growing up, he was cockered by his grandparents. 他从小就被祖父母宠着长大。

近义词：pamper, spoil, indulge, baby|反义词：discipline, neglect|词性:动词


词源: From the verb "cock" (to pamper, indulge), from "cock" (a fighting rooster), referring to the special care given to fighting cocks.
