nonsubtle:/ˌnɒnˈsʌt(ə)l/ | adj. | 不微妙的，明显的，露骨的 | Not subtle; obvious, blatant, or heavy-handed. |  Her nonsubtle hint about needing a new car was hard to ignore. 她毫不掩饰地暗示需要一辆新车，让人难以忽视。 | The politician's nonsubtle attempts to win votes backfired. 这位政客露骨的拉票举动适得其反。 | 近义词：obvious, blatant, overt, transparent, conspicuous, unconcealed | 反义词：subtle, nuanced, understated, delicate, discreet | 词性：形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non.
subtle (adjective): From Old French sutil, from Latin subtilis "fine, delicate, thin," from sub "under" + tela "web, warp."
