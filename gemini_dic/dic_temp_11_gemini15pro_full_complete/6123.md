ametropia:/ˌæmɪˈtroʊpiə/
n.
屈光不正
A defect in the eye that prevents light from focusing properly on the retina, resulting in blurred vision.
She was diagnosed with ametropia and prescribed corrective lenses. 她被诊断出患有屈光不正，并被开了矫正镜片。
Myopia, hyperopia, astigmatism
Emmetropia
