chairing:/ˈtʃeərɪŋ/| v. n.|主持，担任主席|The act of presiding over a meeting or committee.  It can also refer to the position of chairperson.|例句：She is chairing the meeting today. 她今天主持会议。

例句：He enjoyed chairing the committee. 他很喜欢担任委员会主席。|近义词：presiding, moderating, leading, heading|反义词:attending, participating|词性:动词/名词

chair:/tʃer/ |n. v.| 椅子，主持|A seat for one person, typically with a back and four legs.  As a verb, it means to preside over a meeting.|词源: From Old French *chaiere (Modern French chaise), from Latin cathedra "seat."|例句：He sat in the chair. 他坐在椅子上。

例句：He will chair the meeting next week. 他下周将主持会议。|近义词：(n.) seat, stool, bench (v.) preside, lead, moderate|反义词: (n.) floor, standing (v.) attend, participate|词性:名词/动词
