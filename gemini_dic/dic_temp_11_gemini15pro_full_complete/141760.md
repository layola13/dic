orangutan:/ɔːˈræŋuːtæn/ (US), /ɔːˈræŋʊˌtæn/ (UK)| n. |猩猩|A large, arboreal ape with long reddish-brown hair, long arms, and no tail, native to Borneo and Sumatra.|例句：Orangutans are critically endangered due to habitat loss. 猩猩由于栖息地丧失而极度濒危。

例句：The orangutan swung effortlessly through the trees.  这只猩猩毫不费力地在树间荡来荡去。

近义词：ape, primate|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a specific animal)|词性: noun
