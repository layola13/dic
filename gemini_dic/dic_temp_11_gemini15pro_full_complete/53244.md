directorship:/dəˈrɛktərˌʃɪp, daɪˈrɛktərˌʃɪp/
n.
董事职位；董事身份；董事任期
the position of being a director of a company
She holds a directorship on the board of several large companies. 她担任几家大公司的董事。

近义词: board membership,  seat on the board, directorship
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider)  subordinate position,  employee status
词性: noun
