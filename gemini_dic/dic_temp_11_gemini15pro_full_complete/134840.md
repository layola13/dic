nonmobile:/nɒnˈməʊbaɪl/ /nɑnˈmoʊbaɪl/
adjective
非移动的，不流动的
Not able to move or be moved easily; immobile.
The patient was nonmobile after the surgery.  手术后病人无法移动。
The nonmobile sand dunes eventually formed solid rock formations.  不流动的沙丘最终形成了坚固的岩层。
近义词: immobile, fixed, stationary, static
反义词: mobile, movable, portable
