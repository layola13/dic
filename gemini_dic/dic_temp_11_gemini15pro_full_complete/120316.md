minidrama: /ˈmɪniˌdrɑːmə/ | n. | 小型戏剧，短剧 | A short dramatic piece, often televised.  A short, often emotionally charged incident or series of incidents, resembling a play in its intensity. | Example 1:  They showed a minidrama about bullying on the school's TV channel. 他们在学校的电视频道上播放了一个关于欺凌的短剧。

Example 2:  Her breakup with her boyfriend turned into a public minidrama. 她和男朋友的分手变成了一场公开的小闹剧。 | synonym: short play, vignette, sketch | antonym: epic, full-length play | noun
