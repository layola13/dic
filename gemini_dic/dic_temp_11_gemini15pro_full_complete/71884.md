fleur:/flɜːr/ (US) /flɜː(r)/ (UK)| n. |花; 花状装饰; 精华; 最佳部分|A flower, especially one that is used as decoration or as a symbol.  It can also refer to the finest or most select part of something.|

例句：
The chef garnished the dish with a delicate fleur.  厨师用精致的花朵装饰了这道菜。

She is the fleur of the acting community.  她是演艺界的精英。


近义词：flower, blossom, bloom, pick, cream, elite|
反义词: dregs, sediment|
词性: noun|

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *flour*, *flor*, from Latin *flōs* (genitive *flōris*), meaning "flower," related to *florēre* "to blossom."
