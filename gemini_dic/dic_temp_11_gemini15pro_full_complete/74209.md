foursquare:/ˈfɔːrskwɛər/ /ˈfɔːrˌskwer/| adj. adv.|正直的，坚定的；公开地，直率地|Solidly and firmly; in a frank and honest manner.|He stood foursquare against the proposal. 他坚决反对这项提议. | The mayor stated his position foursquare. 市长公开表明了自己的立场。|Synonyms: forthright, straightforward, direct, honest, upright, resolute, firm, steadfast, staunch |Antonyms: devious, evasive, indirect, dishonest, wavering, irresolute| adjective/adverb


foursquare:/ˈfɔːrskwɛər/ /ˈfɔːrˌskwer/| n.|广场，正方形|A square or rectangular open space in a town, typically one with buildings surrounding it.  Also, a square or rectangle.|The market was held in the town foursquare. 集市在城镇广场举行。| Children drew foursquares on the pavement with chalk. 孩子们用粉笔在人行道上画了几个方块。|Synonyms: plaza, square, quadrangle, rectangle|Antonyms: N/A| noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *foursquare*, equivalent to four + square.  The word literally refers to something having four sides and four right angles.  The figurative meaning of "honest" or "straightforward" likely developed from the idea of something being solid and stable like a square.
