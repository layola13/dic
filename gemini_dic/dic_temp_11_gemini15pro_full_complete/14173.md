auto-complete:/ˌɔːtəʊkəmˈpliːt/ /ˌɑːtəʊkəmˈpliːt/ | n. & adj. | 自动完成，自动补全 | A feature in software that predicts the rest of a word or phrase as you type. |  The auto-complete function saves me a lot of typing. 自动完成功能为我节省了很多打字时间。 | synonyms: predictive text, autosuggest | antonyms: manual entry | noun

auto-complete:/ˌɔːtəʊkəmˈpliːt/ /ˌɑːtəʊkəmˈpliːt/ | adj. | 自动完成的，自动补全的 |  Relating to or involving a feature that predicts the rest of a word or phrase as you type. |  I rely heavily on the auto-complete feature in my email program. 我非常依赖电子邮件程序中的自动完成功能。| synonyms: predictive, autosuggestive | antonyms: manual | adjective


Etymology: auto- (from Greek "self") + complete (from Latin "completus," past participle of "complere" meaning "to fill up")
