antiquarianize:/ˌæn.tɪˈkwer.i.ə.naɪz/
verb
使古色古香；仿古；研究古物
To make something appear antique or old-fashioned, or to study antiquities.
He antiquarianized his study with old books and maps. 他用旧书和地图把他的书房装饰得古色古香。

She enjoys antiquarianizing, spending hours in dusty old bookshops. 她喜欢研究古物，在尘土飞扬的旧书店里一待就是几个小时。

近义词: archaize, antique
反义词: modernize
词性: verb
