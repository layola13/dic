caressing:/kəˈrɛsɪŋ/
v.
爱抚；抚摸；
touching or stroking gently and affectionately.
She was caressing the soft fur of the kitten. 她正在轻抚小猫柔软的皮毛。
fondling, stroking, petting
hitting, striking
