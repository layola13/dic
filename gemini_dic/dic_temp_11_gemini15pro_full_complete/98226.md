inseminate:/ɪnˈsɛmɪneɪt/
verb
使受精；使授粉；灌输
To introduce semen into the vagina or uterus of a female animal or plant, or to introduce pollen to the stigma of a flower, in order to cause fertilization and reproduction; to implant or introduce (something, such as an idea) into the mind.
The farmer inseminated the cows artificially.  农民给母牛人工授精。
The wind inseminated the flowers. 风给花授粉。
His writings are inseminated with radical ideas. 他的作品充满了激进的思想。
synonyms: impregnate, fertilize, pollinate, implant, infuse
antonyms: sterilize, barren
