loiterer:/ˈlɔɪtərər/ (US), /ˈlɔɪtərə(r)/ (UK)| n. |闲逛者，游荡者|A person who stands or waits around idly or suspiciously without apparent purpose.|The police arrested the loiterer who had been hanging around the bank for hours. 警察逮捕了在银行附近闲逛数小时的游荡者。|近义词：loafer, idler, lingerer, dawdler|反义词：worker, hustler|词性: 名词
