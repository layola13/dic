anti-creationist:/ˌænti kriˈeɪʃənɪst/
n./adj.|反创造论者/反创造论的|Someone who opposes creationism, the belief that the universe and living organisms originate from specific acts of divine creation, as in the biblical account, rather than by natural processes such as evolution.|Richard Dawkins is a well-known anti-creationist. 理查德·道金斯是一位著名的反创造论者。|anti-evolutionist|creationist,pro-creationist|noun/adjective

anti-creationist:/ˌænti kriˈeɪʃənɪst/
n./adj.|反创造论的|Opposing or disagreeing with the belief that the universe and living organisms originate from specific acts of divine creation, as in the biblical account, rather than by natural processes such as evolution.|The anti-creationist movement advocates for the teaching of evolution in schools. 反创造论运动倡导在学校教授进化论。|anti-evolutionist|creationist,pro-creationist|noun/adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix): from Greek anti- "against, opposite of"
creationist: from creation (from Latin creationem, "a creating, founding") + -ist (suffix forming agent nouns)
