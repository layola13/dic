necrological:/ˌnɛkrəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
讣告的；死亡的；悼念的
Relating to or constituting an obituary or obituaries.  A necrological notice is a formal announcement, especially in a newspaper, of someone's death.
The newspaper published a necrological notice for the deceased mayor.  该报纸刊登了已故市长的讣告。
The historian specialized in necrological research of 18th-century figures.  这位历史学家专门研究18世纪人物的讣告。
近义词:obituary, funereal, mortuary
反义词:eulogistic, celebratory
词性:adjective

词源: From Greek nekros "dead body" + -logy "study of" + -ical "relating to."
