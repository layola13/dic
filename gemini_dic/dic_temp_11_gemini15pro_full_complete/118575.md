mesosphere:/ˈmɛsoʊsfɪr/ | n. | 中间层 | The region of the earth's atmosphere above the stratosphere and below the thermosphere, between about 50 and 85 kilometers in altitude. | The mesosphere is where most meteors burn up upon entering the Earth's atmosphere.  中间层是大多数流星进入地球大气层后燃烧的地方。| synonyms: None (It's a specific scientific term) | antonyms: None (It's a specific scientific term) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From  meso-  +‎ -sphere. Meso- comes from Greek μέσος (mésos, “middle”). -sphere comes from Greek σφαῖρα (sphaîra, “sphere, globe”).
