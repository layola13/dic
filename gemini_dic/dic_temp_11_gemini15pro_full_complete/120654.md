mis-spaced:/ˌmɪsˈspeɪst/| adj.| 间距错误的，排列不当的|incorrectly spaced, especially with an unsuitable gap between words or letters. |The document was mis-spaced and difficult to read.  这份文件间距错误，难以阅读。|The title was mis-spaced on the cover. 封面上的标题间距排列不当。|近义词：incorrectly spaced, badly spaced|反义词：correctly spaced, properly spaced|词性:形容词
