deforester:/diːˈfɒrɪstər/ /diːˈfɔːrɪstər/| n.| 砍伐森林者; 破坏森林者|A person who clears forests, especially for logging or agriculture.|The deforester was fined heavily for illegal logging.  这个砍伐森林的人因非法砍伐而被处以巨额罚款。|Synonyms: logger, clear-cutter|Antonyms: conservationist, environmentalist|noun
