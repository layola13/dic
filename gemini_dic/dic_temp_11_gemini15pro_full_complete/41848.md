corymbiferous:/ˌkɔːrɪmˈbɪfərəs/
adjective
伞房花序的
Bearing corymbs; producing flowers in corymbs.  A corymb is a flat-topped or convex inflorescence in which the outer flowers open first.
The corymbiferous plant attracted many bees.  伞房花序的植物吸引了许多蜜蜂。

The botanist identified the specimen as a corymbiferous species.  植物学家将该标本鉴定为伞房花序物种。
synonym: corymbose
antonym: (No direct antonym, as it describes a specific inflorescence type.  One might consider terms like "racemose" or "paniculate" to describe other inflorescence types, but they aren't true opposites.)
词性: adjective
