mangrove:/ˈmæŋɡroʊv/ (US) /ˈmæŋɡrəʊv/ (UK)| n. |红树林；红树|A tree or shrub that grows in tropical coastal swamps, having numerous tangled roots that grow above ground and form dense thickets.|例句：The mangroves protect the coastline from erosion. 红树林保护海岸线免受侵蚀。

例句：Many species of fish breed in the mangroves. 许多种类的鱼在红树林中繁殖。|近义词：tidal forest, mangrove swamp|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可用内陆森林 inland forest 表示对比)|词性: 名词
