ellipsoidal:/ɪˌlɪpˈsɔɪdəl/ | adj. |椭球的，椭圆形的 |Having the shape of an ellipsoid; resembling an ellipse. |例句：The Earth is not perfectly spherical, but rather ellipsoidal. 地球并非完美的球形，而是椭球形的。|例句：The watermelon had an ellipsoidal shape. 这个西瓜是椭球形的。| 近义词：elliptic, oval, ovate | 反义词：spherical, round |词性：形容词

ellipsoid:/ɪˈlɪpsɔɪd/ | n. |椭球体，椭球面 |A three-dimensional shape resembling a stretched or compressed sphere. |例句：The rugby ball is shaped like a prolate ellipsoid. 橄榄球的形状像一个长椭球体。|例句：In mathematics, an ellipsoid is a quadric surface. 在数学中，椭球体是一个二次曲面。| 近义词：spheroid | 反义词：sphere |词性：名词
