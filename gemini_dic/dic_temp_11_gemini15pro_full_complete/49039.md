degust:/dɪˈɡʌst/| v. |品尝，鉴赏（尤指美食或美酒）|To taste something carefully, especially food or wine, in order to appreciate its qualities.|We degusted several fine wines at the vineyard. 我们在葡萄园品尝了几种优质葡萄酒。|近义词：savor, relish, sample|反义词：gulp, bolt|词性:动词
Etymology: From French _déguster_, from Latin _degustare_ "to taste," from _de-_"down" + _gustare_ "to taste."
