durst:/dɜːrst/| v.| 敢, 胆敢 |A past tense form of *dare*, primarily used in literary or archaic contexts. It expresses having the courage or boldness to do something.  |He durst not speak against the king. 他不敢反驳国王。|近义词：dared, ventured, risked|反义词：feared, hesitated, cowered|动词
