outplacement:/aʊtˈpleɪsmənt/
n.
失业安置，转职安置
The process of helping redundant employees find new jobs, often with the assistance of a specialist consultancy.
The company offered outplacement services to all the employees affected by the restructuring.  这家公司向所有受重组影响的员工提供转职安置服务。
近义词:career transition services, job placement assistance
反义词: recruitment, hiring
词性: 名词
