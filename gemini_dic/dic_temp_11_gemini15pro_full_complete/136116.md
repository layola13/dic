nonreducing:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈdjuː.sɪŋ/ |adj.| 非还原性的 | Not able to reduce another substance; not undergoing or causing reduction, especially not reacting with Fehling's solution or Tollens' reagent.| Sucrose is a nonreducing sugar. | 蔗糖是一种非还原性糖。|  Nonreducing disaccharides like sucrose do not react with Benedict's reagent.  | 像蔗糖这样的非还原性二糖不与本尼迪克特试剂反应。 | 近义词: non-oxidizing | 反义词: reducing | 词性: 形容词
