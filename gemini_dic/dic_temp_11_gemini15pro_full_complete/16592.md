baroque: /bəˈroʊk/ | adj. n. | 巴洛克式的；巴洛克风格；奇形怪状的；(宝石)形状不规则的 |  (adj.) extravagantly ornate, complex, or bizarre, especially in the arts; (n.) the baroque style or period in art, music, or architecture | The palace is a masterpiece of baroque architecture. 这座宫殿是巴洛克建筑的杰作。| 近义词: ornate, elaborate, flamboyant, extravagant | 反义词: simple, plain, austere | 词性: adjective, noun
barocco: /bəˈrɒkəʊ/ (British) | adj. n. | 巴洛克式的；巴洛克风格；奇形怪状的；(宝石)形状不规则的 | (adj.) extravagantly ornate, complex, or bizarre, especially in the arts; (n.) the baroque style or period in art, music, or architecture |  The music is characterized by its baroque ornamentation.  这首音乐的特点是巴洛克式的装饰。| 近义词: ornate, elaborate, flamboyant, extravagant | 反义词: simple, plain, austere | 词性: adjective, noun

词源: From French *baroque* "irregularly shaped pearl," from Portuguese *barroco* "irregular pearl," probably from Vulgar Latin *barrocus*.
