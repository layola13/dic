countereducational:/ˌkaʊntərˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃənəl/
| adj. |反教育的，阻碍教育的|Going against the principles of education or hindering the process of learning.|例句：The constant distractions from social media can be countereducational for students. 社交媒体持续不断的干扰对学生可能起反教育作用。

例句：The government's decision to cut funding for schools was seen as a countereducational move. 政府削减学校经费的决定被视为一项反教育的举措。|近义词：anti-educational, uneducational|反义词:educational, pro-educational|词性:形容词
