linesman: /ˈlaɪnzmən/ (US) /ˈlaɪnzmən/ (UK)| n. |边线裁判；线路工人；线务员|A person who assists a referee in a sport such as football, tennis, or cricket, especially by deciding whether the ball has gone out of play;  A person who installs or repairs electrical power lines.|The linesman raised his flag for offside. 边线裁判举旗示意越位。|The linesman worked through the night to restore power after the storm. 线务员彻夜工作以在暴风雨后恢复电力。|Synonyms: assistant referee, umpire (in tennis), touch judge (in rugby), lineman (US), linesperson|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
