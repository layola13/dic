controvert:/ˈkɒntrəvɜːt/ (US) /ˈkɑːntrəvɜːrt/| v. |反驳，驳斥；争论|To argue against; dispute or deny. |例句：The lawyer controverted the witness's testimony. 律师反驳了证人的证词。

例句：Scientists controvert the theory of evolution. 科学家们对进化论提出了质疑。


近义词：dispute, challenge, oppose, rebut, deny|反义词: confirm, support, corroborate|词性:动词
