bridezilla:/braɪdˈzɪlə/| n.|新娘zilla，指新娘在婚礼筹备过程中表现得控制欲极强、脾气暴躁、要求苛刻。|A bridezilla is a woman whose behavior in planning the details of her wedding is considered overbearing or obsessive.|例句：She turned into a complete bridezilla during the wedding planning.  她在婚礼筹备期间变成了一个十足的泼妇新娘。

例句：The documentary follows several bridezillas as they plan their extravagant weddings.  这部纪录片追踪了几位泼妇新娘筹划她们奢华婚礼的过程。|近义词：diva, tyrant, perfectionist|反义词: easygoing bride, relaxed bride|词性:noun
