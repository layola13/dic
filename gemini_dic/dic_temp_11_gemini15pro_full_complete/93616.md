imbibe:/ɪmˈbaɪb/ | v. | 吸收，吸取; 喝，饮 | To absorb or assimilate something, especially information or ideas; to drink. |  He imbibed the culture of the new country. 他吸收了这个新国家的文化。

She imbibed vast quantities of coffee. 她喝了大量的咖啡。

| 近义词：absorb, assimilate, drink, consume, ingest | 反义词：excrete, expel | 词性：动词
