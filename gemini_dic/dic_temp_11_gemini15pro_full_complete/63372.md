entomophilous:/ˌɛntəˈmɒfɪləs/ | adj. | 虫媒的 | Pollinated by insects; insect-pollinated. |  Many brightly colored flowers are entomophilous, attracting bees and butterflies. 许多颜色鲜艳的花是虫媒的，吸引蜜蜂和蝴蝶。|  The orchid's intricate structure suggests it is entomophilous, relying on a specific insect for pollination.  这朵兰花的复杂结构表明它是虫媒的，依靠特定的昆虫进行授粉。 |  insect-pollinated | anemophilous (wind-pollinated), hydrophilous (water-pollinated) | adjective
