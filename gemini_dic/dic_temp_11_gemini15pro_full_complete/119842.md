mildewing:/ˈmɪlduɪŋ/| n. |发霉，长霉|The process of becoming covered with mildew.  The act of mildew growing on something.|The dampness in the basement caused mildewing on the walls.  地下室的潮湿导致墙壁发霉。|近义词：molding, rotting, decaying|反义词:drying, preserving|词性:noun


mildew:/ˈmɪlduː/| n. vi. |霉，霉菌；使发霉，使长霉|A superficial usually whitish growth produced on organic matter or living plants by fungi.  To become covered with mildew.|The old books were covered in mildew.  这些旧书上长满了霉。|近义词：mold, fungus|反义词: - |词性:noun, intransitive verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *mildēaw, from mil "honey" + dēaw "dew".  Originally referred to a honeydew-like substance.
