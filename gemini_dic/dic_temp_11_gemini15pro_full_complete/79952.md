go-about:/ɡəʊ əˈbaʊt/ | noun | 行为；举止 | a way of behaving or doing something | Example: I don't like his go-about. 我不喜欢他的举止。 | Synonyms: demeanor, conduct, behavior | Antonyms: N/A | noun
