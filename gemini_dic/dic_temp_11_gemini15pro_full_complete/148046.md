overruling:/ˌoʊvərˈruːlɪŋ/ | adj. v. | 推翻，否决，驳回 |  rejecting a decision or argument, especially one made by someone with less authority | The judge's decision was overruling. 法官的决定被推翻了。| The Supreme Court overruled the lower court's decision. 最高法院否决了下级法院的裁决。 | synonyms: overriding, rejecting, canceling, revoking | antonyms: upholding, sustaining, confirming, supporting | verb

overruling:/ˌoʊvərˈruːlɪŋ/ | adj. | 至高无上的，支配一切的|  having superior power and influence |  The overruling factor in their decision was the cost.  他们决定的首要因素是成本。 |  His overruling ambition was to become president. 他最大的野心就是成为总统。 | synonyms: dominant, paramount, preponderant, supreme | antonyms: subordinate, secondary, minor, insignificant | adjective
