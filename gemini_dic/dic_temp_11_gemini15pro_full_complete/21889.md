bodychecking:/ˈbɒdiˌtʃɛkɪŋ/ /ˈbɑːdiˌtʃɛkɪŋ/
noun
身体冲撞，身体对抗
The act of using one's body to make physical contact with an opponent, typically in ice hockey or other contact sports, to impede their progress or gain possession of the puck or ball.
Bodychecking is a key part of hockey.  身体冲撞是曲棍球的关键部分。
The coach emphasized the importance of legal bodychecking. 教练强调了合法身体冲撞的重要性。
checking, hitting, contact
obstruction, illegal check
n.
