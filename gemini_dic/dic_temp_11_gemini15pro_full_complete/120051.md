millponds:/ˈmɪlpɑːndz/
n.
池塘；平静的水面
A body of water, typically a pond, created by damming a stream for the purpose of powering a watermill.  Often used metaphorically to describe a very calm and still surface of water.
The lake was as smooth as a millpond.  湖面像磨坊池塘一样平静。
近义词:ponds, lakes
反义词:rapids, currents
词性:名词
