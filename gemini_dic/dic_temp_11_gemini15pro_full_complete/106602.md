kudzu:/ˈkʊdzuː/
n.
葛根，葛藤
A fast-growing East Asian climbing plant with reddish-purple flowers, used as a fodder crop and for erosion control.
The kudzu had grown over the abandoned car.  葛藤已经爬满了那辆废弃的汽车。

近义词:vine, creeper
反义词:(没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与其作用相反的植物，例如：ground cover, grass)
词性:noun
