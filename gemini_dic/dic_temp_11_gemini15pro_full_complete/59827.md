ectobronchium:/ˌɛktoʊˈbrɒŋkiəm/
n.
外鳃
a gill projecting from the body wall, as in some larval amphibians and certain aquatic insects.
The larval salamander uses its ectobronchia for respiration. 幼体蝾螈用它的外鳃呼吸。

The axolotl retains its ectobronchia throughout its life.  美西螈在其一生中都保留着它的外鳃。
near synonym: external gill
antonym: internal gill
noun
