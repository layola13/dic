deuteropathic:/ˌdjuːtərəˈpæθɪk/
adjective
继发性病变的，继发性疾病的
Relating to or affected by deuteropathy, a disease or condition that is a consequence of another disease.
Deuteropathic conditions can arise from various primary illnesses. 继发性疾病可能由各种原发性疾病引起。
The patient's rash was deemed deuteropathic, a result of his underlying autoimmune disorder. 病人的皮疹被认为是继发性的，是他潜在的自身免疫性疾病的结果。
近义词: secondary, consequential
反义词: primary, idiopathic
词性: adjective
