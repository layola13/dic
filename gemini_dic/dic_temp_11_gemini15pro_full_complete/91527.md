hyosternal:/haɪoʊˈstɜːrnl/| adj.| 胸骨的，连接胸骨和肋骨的|Relating to the hyosternum (the anterior segment of the sternum in some animals, particularly birds).  It connects the sternum and ribs.|The hyosternal apparatus plays a crucial role in avian respiration. 胸骨器械在鸟类呼吸中起着至关重要的作用。|Near Synonyms: sternal, costosternal|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|adjective
