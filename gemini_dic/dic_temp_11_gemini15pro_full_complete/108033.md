lasts:/læsts/ /lɑːsts/
verb
持续；持久
continues for a period of time

The meeting lasts two hours.
会议持续两个小时。

The battery lasts a long time.
这块电池很耐用。

Synonyms: endures, persists, remains, continues
Antonyms: finishes, ends, ceases, stops
词性: 动词(verb)
