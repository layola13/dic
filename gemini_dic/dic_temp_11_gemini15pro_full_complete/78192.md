geosystems:/ˈdʒiːoʊˌsɪstəmz/
n.
地球系统
Earth systems; the interconnected and interacting physical, chemical, and biological processes that operate on Earth.
The study of geosystems helps us understand how the Earth works.  地球系统的研究帮助我们理解地球是如何运作的。
Scientists use computer models to simulate complex geosystems. 科学家用计算机模型来模拟复杂的地球系统。
近义词：Earth systems, environmental systems
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


Etymology:
geo-  from Greek gaia, gē "earth, land"
-systems from Greek sustēma "organized whole, a whole compounded of parts"
