melaconite:/məˈlækəˌnaɪt/| n. |黑铜矿|A black mineral consisting of copper(II) oxide, CuO.  It is a secondary mineral formed by the weathering of other copper minerals. |Melaconite is a valuable ore of copper.|近义词：tenorite (a crystalline form of the same chemical compound)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From Greek *μέλας* (mélas, “black”) + *-ίτης* (-ítēs, adjectival suffix forming names of minerals).
