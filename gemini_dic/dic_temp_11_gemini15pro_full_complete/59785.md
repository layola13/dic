ecotok: /ˈiːkəʊtɒk/ (British English) /ˈiːkoʊtɑːk/ (American English) | n. | 生态象征; 生态标记 | A symbol or label indicating that a product or service is environmentally friendly or sustainable.  |  This product carries the ecotok, certifying its low environmental impact.  这个产品带有生态标记，证明其对环境的影响很小。 |  近义词: ecolabel, green label, environmental certification | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider) unsustainable label, conventional label | 词性: noun

词源:  A blend of "ecological" and "token".  由"ecological"和"token"混合而成。
