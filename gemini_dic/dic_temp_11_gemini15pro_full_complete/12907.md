assuredly:/əˈʃʊrɪdli/| adv.|无疑地，确实地|Without a doubt; certainly.|He will assuredly win the election. 他肯定会赢得选举。|近义词:certainly, definitely, undoubtedly, indeed, truly|反义词:doubtfully, questionably|词性:副词
词源: From Middle English *assuredly, *asuredly, equivalent to assured +‎ -ly.
