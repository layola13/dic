arquebuse:/ˈɑːrkɪbʌs/ | n. | 火绳枪，钩銃 | An early type of portable gun supported on a tripod or a forked rest.  A long-barreled firearm, the predecessor of the musket. |  The arquebuse was a significant advancement in firearms technology.  火绳枪是枪械技术的一项重大进步。

The soldiers were armed with arquebuses and pikes. 士兵们装备了火绳枪和长矛。 | 近义词: musket, handgun, firearm | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps bow and arrow or sword) | 词性: noun
