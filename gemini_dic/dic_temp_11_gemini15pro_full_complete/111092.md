litanies:/ˈlɪtəniz/ /ˈlɪtniːz/| n. |连祷；冗长而枯燥的陈述|a series of petitions for use in church services or processions, usually recited by the clergy and responded to in a recurring formula by the people. Also, a tedious recital or repetitive series.|The priest recited the litanies. 神父诵读了连祷文。|The constant litany of complaints was wearing her down.  不断重复的抱怨让她疲惫不堪。| synonyms: prayer, supplication, invocation, petition, chant, recital, enumeration, repetition|antonyms: celebration, praise, commendation|noun
