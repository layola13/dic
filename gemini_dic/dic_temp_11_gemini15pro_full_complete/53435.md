disappointedly: /ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪntɪdli/ /ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪntɪdli/ | adv. | 失望地 | In a manner expressing disappointment; sadly. | She sighed disappointedly and turned away. 她失望地叹了口气，转过身去。| He shook his head disappointedly when he heard the news.  听到这个消息，他失望地摇了摇头。 | 近义词：sadly, glumly, dejectedly | 反义词: happily, gladly, joyfully | 词性: 副词

词源: From the adjective "disappointed" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Disappointed" comes from the verb "disappoint," which in turn comes from the Old French "desapointer," meaning "to undo an appointment."
