desert:/ˈdɛzərt/ (US) /ˈdɛzət/ (UK)| n. |沙漠；荒地| A barren or desolate area, especially one with little or no vegetation.|The Sahara is the largest hot desert in the world.  撒哈拉沙漠是世界上最大的热沙漠。

desert:/dɪˈzɜːrt/ (US) /dɪˈzɜːt/ (UK)| vt. |抛弃；遗弃；擅离（职守）| To abandon or leave someone or something.|He deserted his wife and children. 他抛弃了他的妻儿。

desert:/ˈdɛzərt/ (US) /ˈdɛzət/ (UK)| adj. |荒芜的；无人居住的|Barren or desolate; uninhabited.|The desert landscape stretched for miles. 荒凉的景色绵延数英里。


近义词：(n.) wasteland, wilderness; (v.) abandon, forsake; (adj.) barren, desolate
反义词：(n.) oasis, paradise; (v.) support, stay; (adj.) fertile, lush
词性: n., v., adj.
