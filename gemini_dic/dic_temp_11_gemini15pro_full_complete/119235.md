miasmically:/maɪˈæzmɪkli/ | adv. |瘴气般地；腐臭地；有害地|Affecting or affected by miasma; spreading or being spread in a way resembling the spread of miasma.|Rumors of corruption spread miasmically through the government. 腐败的谣言像瘴气一样在政府中蔓延开来。|Synonym: pervasively, insidiously, virally | Antonym: cleanly, purely | 词性: 副词
