nongarrulously:/ˌnɒnˈɡærʊləsli/ | adv. |  不饶舌地，简洁地 | In a way that is not garrulous; concisely and quietly. |  He explained the situation nongarrulously, sticking to the facts. 他简洁地解释了情况，只说了事实。| Synonym: concisely, succinctly, briefly, laconically | Antonym: garrulously, loquaciously, verbosely | 词性: 副词
