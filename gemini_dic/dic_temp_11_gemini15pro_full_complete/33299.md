chubby:/ˈtʃʌbi/
adj.
胖乎乎的，圆胖的
Plump and rounded, especially in a child or young person.
He had chubby cheeks and a cheerful smile. 他有着胖乎乎的脸颊和开朗的笑容。
近义词: plump, portly, rotund
反义词: thin, slender, lean
词性: 形容词

词源:
From 'chub' (a type of fish known for its rounded body) + '-y' (suffix forming adjectives). 最初来源于chub（一种以圆形身体而闻名的鱼），加上后缀-y构成形容词。
