leave someone in the lurch:/liːv sʌmwʌn ɪn ðə lɜːrtʃ/ | idiom |  把某人置于困境；抛弃某人 | To abandon someone in a difficult situation without help. |  He left me in the lurch when he quit the project without any notice. 他在没有通知的情况下退出了项目，把我置于困境。| Synonyms: abandon, desert, forsake | Antonyms: support, assist, help | idiom

leave someone in the lurch:/liːv sʌmwʌn ɪn ðə lɜːrtʃ/ | idiom |  使某人失望；失约于某人 | To disappoint someone by failing to fulfill a commitment or promise.  | She promised to help me move, but she left me in the lurch at the last minute. 她答应帮我搬家，但在最后一刻却失约了。| Synonyms: disappoint, fail, let down | Antonyms: keep one's word, follow through | idiom
