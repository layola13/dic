overassumes:/ˌoʊvərəˈsuːmz/
verb
过度假设，过分揣测
To assume too much; to make presumptions beyond what is warranted by the evidence or situation.
He overassumes her willingness to help, she's actually quite busy. 他过分假设她愿意帮忙，她实际上很忙。
The report overassumes the impact of the new policy.  这份报告高估了新政策的影响。
presumes, presupposes, surmises
underestimates, undervalues
v.
