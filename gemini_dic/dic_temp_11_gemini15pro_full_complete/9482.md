antiritualism:/ˌæntiˈrɪtʃuəlɪzəm/
n.
反仪式主义，仪式反对论
Opposition or hostility to rituals, especially in religion.
The rise of antiritualism in the Protestant Reformation led to simplified church services.  新教改革中反仪式主义的兴起导致了教堂仪式的简化。
The author explored the themes of antiritualism and individualism in modern society.  作者探讨了现代社会中反仪式主义和个人主义的主题。
近义词:iconoclasm, irreverence, secularism
反义词:ritualism, formalism, traditionalism
词性:noun
