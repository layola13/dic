nod:/nɑːd/ (US) /nɒd/ (UK)| v. n.|点头，打盹，首肯|To lower and raise one's head briefly, especially in greeting, assent, or drowsiness.|He nodded in agreement. 他点头表示同意。|近义词：bow, incline, doze|反义词：shake (one's head)|verb/noun
