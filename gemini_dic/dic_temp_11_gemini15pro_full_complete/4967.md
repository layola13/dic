alkalify: /ˈælkəlaɪfaɪ/ | v. | 碱化；使成碱性 | To make something alkaline, or more alkaline. |  The chemist alkalified the solution by adding a base. 化学家通过添加碱来碱化溶液。

The process alkalifies the soil, making it more suitable for certain plants. 这个过程碱化了土壤，使其更适合某些植物生长。 | 近义词: neutralize, basify | 反义词: acidify | 词性: 动词
