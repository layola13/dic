marmoreally:/mɑːrˈmɔːriəli/ | adv. | 大理石般地，冷冰冰地 | In a manner resembling marble, especially in coldness, smoothness, or hardness; figuratively, impassively. |  She stared marmoreally at the news of her brother's death, her face betraying no emotion. 她大理石般地盯着哥哥的死讯，脸上没有流露出任何情绪。|  例句：His face was marmoreally smooth. 他的脸像大理石一样光滑。 | 近义词：impassively, stonily, coldly | 反义词：emotionally, warmly, expressively | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Latin "marmor" (marble) + "-eally" (adverbial suffix).
