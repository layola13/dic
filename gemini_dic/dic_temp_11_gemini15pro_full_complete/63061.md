enrol:/ɪnˈroʊl/ (US) /ɪnˈrɒl/ (UK)|v. |注册，登记，入学|To officially register as a member of an institution or a student on a course.|She enrolled in the photography course. 她报名参加了摄影课程。|近义词：register, sign up, join|反义词：withdraw, unregister|verb

enrolment:/ɪnˈroʊlmənt/ (US) /ɪnˈrɒlmənt/ (UK)|n. |注册，登记，入学|The act of enrolling or being enrolled.|Enrolment for the new term begins in September. 新学期的注册从九月开始。|近义词：registration, admission|反义词：withdrawal|noun
