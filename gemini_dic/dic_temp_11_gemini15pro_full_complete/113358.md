USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she study at university? 她在大学学习什么专业？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she accomplished this year? 她今年完成了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading these days? 他这些天一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? 她对这部电影说了些什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 开会的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left? 你离开之前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? 她生病之前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I arrived? 我来之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? 她转专业之前一直在学习什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? 你下周末要做什么？
What will she study in graduate school? 她研究生院要学习什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time tomorrow? 明天这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be presenting at the conference?  她将在会议上展示什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will she have written by the deadline?  到截止日期前她将写完什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时，她将已经学习了多久什么专业？
