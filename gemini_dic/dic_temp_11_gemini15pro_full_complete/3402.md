afloat:/əˈfloʊt/| adj.|漂浮的， afloat的;在船上;免于经济困难|Being afloat means being on the surface of the water or other liquid and not sinking. It can also mean having enough money to pay debts.|The boat stayed afloat despite the storm. 这艘船尽管遇到风暴，仍然漂浮着。|The company is struggling to stay afloat.  这家公司正在努力维持下去。|近义词：floating, buoyant, solvent|反义词: sunk, submerged, bankrupt|词性:形容词
Etymology: a- (on) + float (to rest on the surface of a liquid). From Old English *afloten, from *a- (on) + flotian (to float).
