dibranchiate:/ˌdaɪˈbræŋkiət/

dibranchiate:/ˌdaɪˈbræŋkiət/
dibranchiate: n. |[动]二鳃类动物|A cephalopod mollusk of the subclass Dibranchiata, characterized by having two gills.|The dibranchiate rapidly changed color to camouflage itself.  这只二鳃类动物迅速改变颜色进行伪装。|Synonyms: cephalopod|Antonyms: tetrabranchiate|noun
