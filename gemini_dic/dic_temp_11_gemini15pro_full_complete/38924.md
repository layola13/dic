confessor:/kənˈfɛsər/ | n. |忏悔者；告解神父；坦白者|a person who confesses their sins to a priest, especially in the Roman Catholic Church; someone who admits to having done something wrong. |例句：The confessor listened patiently to the penitent. 忏悔神父耐心地听着忏悔者。

例句：He was a confessor to many of the town's secrets. 他是镇上许多秘密的知情者。|近义词：penitent, admitter, acknowledger |反义词：denier, liar |词性：noun

confess:/kənˈfɛs/ | v. |承认；坦白；忏悔；供认|admit or state that one has committed a crime or is at fault in some way; (in the Roman Catholic Church) declare one's sins to a priest formally in order to receive absolution. |例句：I confess I ate the last piece of cake. 我承认我吃了最后一块蛋糕。

例句：She confessed her sins to the priest. 她向神父忏悔她的罪过。|近义词：admit, acknowledge, own up, divulge |反义词：deny, conceal |词性：verb
