asphalting:/ˈæsˌfɔːltɪŋ/
noun
沥青铺设; 铺沥青
the process of covering a surface with asphalt
The asphalting of the driveway was completed last week.  车道的沥青铺设工作上周完成了。

近义词: paving, surfacing, tarmac(king)(英式)
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 excavation (挖掘) 或 deconstruction (拆除))
词性:  n. (noun)


asphalt:/ˈæsˌfɔːlt/
noun/verb
沥青/用沥青铺(路)
a dark, sticky substance used for making roads/ to cover with asphalt
The road was made of asphalt. 这条路是用沥青铺成的。
They are asphalting the road. 他们在用沥青铺路。

近义词:  bitumen, tar, tarmac
反义词: (名词形式无明确反义词，动词形式无明确反义词)
词性: n. (noun), v. (verb)
