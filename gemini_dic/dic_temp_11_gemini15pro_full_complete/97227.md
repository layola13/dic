inflations:/ɪnˈfleɪʃənz/| n.|通货膨胀（复数）|A general increase in prices and fall in the purchasing value of money.|例句：The government is trying to control inflations. 政府正在努力控制通货膨胀。|近义词：price hikes, rising prices|反义词:deflation, disinflation|词性:名词

Etymology: From Late Latin *inflatio (“a blowing up”), from Latin inflare (“to blow into, inflate”), from in- (“in, into”) + flare (“to blow”).
