beseechingly:/bɪˈsiːtʃɪŋli/
adverb
恳求地；哀求地
In a way that expresses urgent pleading.
She looked at him beseechingly. 她恳求地看着他。
近义词:pleadingly, imploringly, supplicatingly
反义词:demandingly, assertively
词性:adverb

Etymology: From beseech + -ing + -ly.  Beseech comes from Middle English bisechen, from Old English besēcan "to seek, implore, beseech," from be- "completely" + sēcan "to seek."
