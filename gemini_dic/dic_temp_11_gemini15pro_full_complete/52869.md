diminisher:/dɪˈmɪnɪʃər/
n.|减少者，减弱者|A person or thing that diminishes something.|The new law acted as a diminisher of individual freedom.  这项新法律减少了个人自由。|reducer, decreaser|augmenter, increaser|noun


diminish:/dɪˈmɪnɪʃ/
v.|减少，减小，降低|Make or become less.|The pain will gradually diminish. 疼痛会逐渐减轻。|reduce, decrease, lessen|increase, augment|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *diminuer*, from Latin diminuere "to lessen, diminish," from dis- "apart, away" + minuere "to lessen."
