nonpervasive:/ˌnɒn.pɜːˈveɪ.sɪv/ | adj. | 非普遍的，非渗透性的 | Not spreading throughout all parts of something; not pervasive. | The impact of the new policy was nonpervasive, affecting only a small segment of the population. 新政策的影响并不普遍，只影响了一小部分人口。

The disease, thankfully, proved to be nonpervasive and easily contained. 谢天谢地，这种疾病被证明不普遍，很容易控制。 | 近义词：localized, limited, contained, restricted, isolated | 反义词：pervasive, widespread, ubiquitous, rampant, prevalent | 词性: 形容词
