bpi:/biː piː ˈaɪ/ /biː piː ˈaɪ/ | n. | 每英寸位数；每英寸点数 | bits per inch; dots per inch, a measure of image resolution.  It refers to the number of dots or pixels that can be printed or displayed in a linear inch.  Higher BPI means higher resolution and better image quality. | The scanner has a bpi of 600. 这台扫描仪的分辨率为每英寸600点。 | synonyms: resolution, dpi (dots per inch) | antonyms: N/A | noun
