calabozo:/ˌkæləˈboʊzoʊ/ (US), /ˌkæləˈbɒzəʊ/ (UK)| n. |地牢，牢房|A dungeon or jail cell, especially one that is dark, damp, and unpleasant.|He was thrown into a dark and dank calabozo. 他被扔进了一个阴暗潮湿的地牢。|The calabozo was infested with rats.  牢房里老鼠横行。|synonyms: dungeon, jail, cell, prison, lockup|antonyms: freedom, liberty|noun
