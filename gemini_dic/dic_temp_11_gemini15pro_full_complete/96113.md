indelibility:/ˌɪndɪˈlɪbɪlɪti/
n.
不可磨灭性，难以消除
The quality or state of being indelible.  The indelibility of a memory.
The indelibility of the ink made it perfect for signing important documents. 这墨水不可磨灭，非常适合用来签署重要文件。

Near Synonyms: permanence, immutability, durability
Antonyms: ephemerality, transience, impermanence
Noun


indelible:/ɪnˈdɛlɪbəl/
adj.
不可磨灭的，擦不掉的
Not able to be erased or removed; permanent.  An indelible mark.
Her childhood experiences left an indelible mark on her personality. 她童年的经历在她的人格上留下了不可磨灭的印记。
The ink stain on my shirt is indelible. 我衬衫上的墨渍洗不掉了。

Near Synonyms: permanent, lasting, enduring
Antonyms: erasable, temporary, fleeting
Adjective
