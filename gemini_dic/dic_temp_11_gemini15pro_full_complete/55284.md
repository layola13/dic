dissocial:/dɪˈsoʊʃəl/
adjective
反社会的
lacking in social skills or graces; unwilling or unable to associate in a normal or friendly way with others; averse to society.
He displayed dissocial behavior, refusing to cooperate with his classmates. 他表现出反社会行为，拒绝与同学合作。
Dissocial personalities can struggle to form meaningful relationships.  反社会人格的人难以建立有意义的人际关系。
近义词：antisocial, asocial, misanthropic, unsociable
反义词：social, sociable, gregarious, outgoing
词性：adj.

Etymology: From dis- "apart, away" + social.  The prefix dis- adds a negative or reversing force to the word "social".
