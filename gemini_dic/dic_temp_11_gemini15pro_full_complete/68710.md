farfalle:/fɑːrˈfɑːleɪ/ /ˌfɑːrˈfɑːli/
noun
蝴蝶形意大利面；蝴蝶结面；领结面
A type of pasta shaped like bow ties or butterflies.
She made a delicious pasta dish with farfalle and pesto. 她做了一道美味的蝴蝶面配香蒜酱。
synonyms: bow tie pasta, butterfly pasta
antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a type of pasta)
n.
