decipherer:/dɪˈsaɪfərər/
noun
解密者，破译者
A person who deciphers something, especially a code or cipher.
The decipherer worked tirelessly to break the enemy's code.  解密者不知疲倦地工作以破解敌人的密码。
近义词:decoder, interpreter, analyst, cryptanalyst
反义词:encoder, encrypter
词性:n.

decipher:/dɪˈsaɪfər/
verb
解密，破译
To succeed in understanding, interpreting, or identifying something.
She managed to decipher the handwriting on the old letter. 她设法辨认出旧信上的笔迹。
近义词:decode, interpret, solve, translate, crack
反义词:encode, encrypt, obscure
词性:v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *deschifrer, from des- (reversal prefix) + chifrer (to encrypt, to write in cipher).  Ultimately from Arabic ṣifr (zero, empty, code).
