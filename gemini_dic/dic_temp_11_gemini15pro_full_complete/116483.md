materiality:/məˌtɪəriˈælɪti/ | n. | 重要性；实质性；物质性 |The quality of being important or relevant, especially in accounting or law, relating to a matter's significance in influencing the decisions of users of financial information or the outcome of a legal case. |  The materiality of the evidence will determine the verdict.  证据的重要性将决定判决结果。|  significance, importance, relevance, consequence | immateriality, insignificance, irrelevance | noun
