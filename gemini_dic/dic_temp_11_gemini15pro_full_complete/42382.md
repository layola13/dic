counterblast:/ˈkaʊntərbæst/ /ˈkaʊntərbɑːst/| n.|反击，猛烈抨击|A forceful reply or attack, especially a verbal one, against an earlier attack.|His book was a counterblast to the government's policies. 他的书是对政府政策的猛烈抨击。|Synonyms: retort, rebuttal, riposte, rejoinder|Antonyms: agreement, concession, acquiescence|noun
counterblast:|/ˈkaʊntərbæst/ /ˈkaʊntərbɑːst/| n.|反驳；抗辩|a strong reply or reaction against something|The article was a counterblast to earlier criticism.|Synonyms: retort, rebuttal, riposte|Antonyms: agreement, concession|noun
