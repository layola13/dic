dispelled:/dɪˈspɛld/
verb
驱散，消除
to make something, especially a feeling or belief, go away
The sun soon dispelled the mist. 太阳很快驱散了薄雾。
近义词:banish, eliminate, disperse, scatter
反义词:gather, collect, assemble
词性:v.

词源:
From Latin dispellere, from dis- + pellere "to drive."
