nonvenously:/nɒnˈviːnəsli/
adjective
非静脉注射地
Not administered or occurring through a vein; not intravenous.
The medication was administered nonvenously, through an intramuscular injection.  药物不是通过静脉注射，而是通过肌肉注射给药的。
The doctor opted for a nonvenous method of drug delivery due to the patient's fragile veins. 由于病人血管脆弱，医生选择了一种非静脉给药的方法。
近义词: extravenously, intramuscularly
反义词: intravenously
词性: adjective
