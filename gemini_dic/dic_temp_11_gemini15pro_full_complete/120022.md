millionaires:/ˌmɪljəˈnɛərz/ | n. | 百万富翁（复数） | A person or people whose assets are worth at least one million units of a particular currency, such as dollars or pounds. |  Many millionaires live in this neighborhood. 许多百万富翁住在这个社区。| The millionaires invest heavily in the stock market. 这些百万富翁在股市投入巨资。 | synonyms: tycoons, magnates, wealthy individuals | antonyms: paupers, the poor, the impoverished | noun
