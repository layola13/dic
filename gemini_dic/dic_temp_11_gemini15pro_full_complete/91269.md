hydrogenated:/ˈhaɪdrədʒəneɪtɪd/| adj.|氢化的|Treated or combined with hydrogen.  The process of adding hydrogen to (something, especially an unsaturated fat).|Hydrogenated vegetable oil is often used in processed foods. 氢化植物油常用于加工食品。|saturated, hydrogenized|unsaturated|adjective
Etymology->
From hydrogen + -ate (verb-forming suffix) + -ed (past participle suffix).  Ultimately from Greek *hudōr* (water) and *genes* (forming).
