adeptly:/ˈædəptli/| adv.|熟练地，巧妙地|In a skillful and expert manner, demonstrating proficiency and expertise.|She adeptly handled the difficult situation. 她巧妙地处理了这个棘手的局面。|近义词：skillfully, expertly, proficiently, deftly|反义词：clumsily, awkwardly, ineptly|词性:副词
词源: From the adjective "adept" + "-ly". "Adept" comes from the Latin "adeptus," past participle of "adipisci" (to attain, achieve).
