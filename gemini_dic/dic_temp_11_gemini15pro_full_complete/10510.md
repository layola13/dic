appoints:/əˈpɔɪnts/| v. |任命，委派，约定，指定|assigns a job or role to someone; designates a time or place|The president appoints the cabinet members. 总统任命内阁成员。|近义词：designate, name, assign, elect, choose|反义词:dismiss, fire, remove|verb

appoints:/əˈpɔɪnts/| v. |确定，指定（时间、地点）|sets or fixes a time or place|Let's appoint a time to meet next week. 我们约定下周见面的时间。|近义词：set, fix, schedule, determine|反义词:cancel, postpone|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *apointer (Modern French appointer), from Latin appunctare "to add a point to," from ad- "to" + punctum "point."  The original sense was "to add points to," hence "to settle, determine."
