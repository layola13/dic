hematite:/ˈhiːməˌtaɪt/ /ˈheməˌtaɪt/| n.|赤铁矿|A black or reddish-black mineral consisting of ferric oxide (Fe2O3), the principal ore of iron.|The hematite deposits were a valuable resource for the burgeoning iron industry.|Synonyms: iron ore, red iron ore, kidney ore, specular iron, specularite|Antonyms: N/A (Hematite is a specific mineral, not a concept with a direct opposite.)|noun
