illicium:/ɪˈlɪsiəm/| n. |八角属；八角茴香|A genus of evergreen shrubs and trees (family Schisandraceae, the star anise family), comprising about 40 species native to Southeast Asia and the southeastern United States, some of which yield the spice star anise. |Star anise is derived from the fruit of Illicium verum. 八角是从八角茴香的果实中提取的。| The illicit trade in Illicium verum is a growing problem. 八角茴香的非法贸易是一个日益严重的问题。|Synonyms: star anise, anise tree|Antonyms: None|Noun
