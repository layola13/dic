indrench:/ɪnˈdrentʃ/| vt.|浸透，使湿透|To wet thoroughly; soak.|The sudden downpour indrenched us to the skin. 突如其来的倾盆大雨把我们淋得浑身湿透。|近义词：drench, soak, saturate|反义词:dry|词性:动词

词源:  From in- + drench.  "Drench" comes from the Old English *drencan, from Proto-Germanic *drinkaną (“to cause to drink, give to drink, drown”).  The prefix "in-" intensifies the meaning.
