indolent: /ˈɪndələnt/ (US) /ˈɪndələnt/ (UK)| adj.|懒惰的；好逸恶劳的；不活跃的|Wanting to avoid activity or exertion; lazy.|He was an indolent young man who had never done a day's work in his life. 他是一个懒惰的年轻人，一生中从未工作过一天。|Synonyms: lazy, idle, slothful, shiftless, lethargic|Antonyms: industrious, diligent, energetic, active|adjective

indolence: /ˈɪndələns/ (US) /ˈɪndələns/ (UK)| n.|懒惰；怠惰|Avoidance of activity or exertion.|Her indolence prevented her from achieving her goals. 她的懒惰使她无法实现自己的目标。|Synonyms: laziness, idleness, sloth, shiftlessness, lethargy|Antonyms: industry, diligence, energy, activity|noun
