overexpanded:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspændɪd/
adjective
过度扩张的
Having expanded or been expanded too much.  Expanding beyond safe or reasonable limits.
The company overexpanded during the boom years and now faces bankruptcy.  这家公司在繁荣时期过度扩张，现在面临破产。
overextended, inflated, bloated
contracted, reduced, consolidated
词性: 形容词
