虚拟语气:
Si nostra casa pulchra esset, felices essemus. = If our house were beautiful, we would be happy.
Utinam nostra casa pulchra esset. =  I wish our house were beautiful.


被动语态:
Nostra casa aedificata est. = Our house was built.
Nostra lingua docetur. = Our language is being taught.


疑问句:
Ubi est nostra casa? = Where is our house?
Quid est nostra lingua? = What is our language?


否定句:
Nostra casa non est magna. = Our house is not large.
Nostra familia non est parva. = Our family is not small.


PHRASE->
(Since "nostra" is a Latin word, standard English phrases don't apply.  Here are some Latin phrases using "noster/nostra/nostrum")

Nostra culpa. = Our fault.
Noster Deus. = Our God.
Pro nostro rege. = For our king.
In nostra patria. = In our country.
Nostra vita. = Our life.


注意事项:

The word "nostra" itself doesn't have separate American and British pronunciations as it's a Latin word.  However, the pronunciation can vary slightly depending on the individual.

The provided examples are adapted to demonstrate usage, as some tenses don't exist in classical Latin.  Latin grammar differs significantly from English.
