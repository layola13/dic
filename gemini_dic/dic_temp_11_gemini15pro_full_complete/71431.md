flakily:/ˈfleɪkɪli/
adverb|成片地，成鳞状地；薄弱地；古怪地；飘忽不定地|in a way that resembles flakes; in a flaky manner; erratically, unreliably|The paint was peeling flakily from the wall. 油漆成片地从墙上剥落。|近义词：erratically, inconsistently, capriciously, unreliably|反义词:reliably, consistently, steadily|词性:adverb

flakily:/ˈfleɪkɪli/
adverb|薄弱地；古怪地|in a flaky manner; erratically, unreliably|He behaved rather flakily throughout the meeting.  他在整个会议期间表现得相当古怪。|近义词：erratically, inconsistently, capriciously, unreliably|反义词:reliably, consistently, steadily|词性:adverb

flakily:/ˈfleɪkɪli/
adverb|飘忽不定地；古怪地|in a flaky manner; erratically, unreliably|She's been acting very flakily lately, canceling plans at the last minute. 她最近表现得很飘忽不定，经常在最后一刻取消计划。|近义词：erratically, inconsistently, capriciously, unreliably|反义词:reliably, consistently, steadily|词性:adverb


词源: From flake + -y.  源自flake（薄片，碎片）+ -y（形容词后缀，表示“具有...性质的”）。 最初指物质上的成片状，后来引申为行为或性格上的不稳定和不可靠。
