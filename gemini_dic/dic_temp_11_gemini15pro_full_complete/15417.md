bacterin:/ˈbæktərɪn/
n.
bacterial vaccine；vaccine prepared from killed bacteria
细菌疫苗；灭活细菌疫苗
A bacterin is a vaccine prepared from killed bacteria. 细菌疫苗是一种由灭活细菌制成的疫苗。
Farmers use bacterins to protect their livestock from various diseases. 农民使用细菌疫苗来保护他们的牲畜免受各种疾病的侵害。
近义词：vaccine, immunogen
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider: placebo, immunosuppressant)
词性: noun
