maigre:/ˈmeɪɡər/ (US), /ˈmɛːɡrə/ (UK)| adj.|瘦的；贫瘠的；不足的|Thin, lean; lacking in richness or fullness; inadequate.|例句：The refugees survived on meagre rations.  难民们靠着微薄的口粮生存。

例句：The soil in this area is too meagre to support much plant life.  这个地区的土壤太贫瘠，无法支持很多植物生长。|近义词：thin, lean, spare, scanty, meager (US), insufficient|反义词：fat, plump, obese, rich, abundant, ample|词性:形容词
词源: From Old French *maigre, from Latin macer ("lean, thin").
