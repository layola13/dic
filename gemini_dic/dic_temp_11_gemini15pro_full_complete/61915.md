empyreumatize:/ɪmˌpaɪriˈuːmətaɪz/
v.
使升华；精炼；使精神化；使上天堂
To refine or purify, especially to a celestial or spiritual level; to make empyreal.
The alchemist attempted to empyreumatize the base metals, hoping to transform them into gold and achieve spiritual enlightenment.  炼金术士试图将贱金属升华，希望将它们转化为黄金并获得精神上的启迪。

The mystic sought to empyreumatize his soul through meditation and prayer.  这位神秘主义者试图通过冥想和祈祷来净化他的灵魂。
近义词:sublimate, refine, purify, exalt
反义词:debase, corrupt, degrade
词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin empyreumatizāre, from Late Greek empyreumatizein,  from empyreuma ("highest heaven; the empyrean") + -ize.  Ultimately from Ancient Greek empyros ("fiery, in fire").
