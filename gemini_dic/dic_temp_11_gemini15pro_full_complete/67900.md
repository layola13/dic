facadism:/fəˈsɑːdɪzəm/| n.|立面主义，门面主义|The practice of preserving only the facade of a building while demolishing the rest, often for new construction behind or around it.  保留建筑物正面而拆除其余部分的做法，通常是为了在其后方或周围进行新的建筑。|例句：The developers were accused of facadism when they demolished the historic building but kept its front wall.  开发商被指责为门面主义，因为他们拆除了这座历史建筑，但保留了它的前墙。

例句：Facadism is a controversial practice, with some arguing that it preserves a sense of history while others see it as a superficial gesture. 立面主义是一种有争议的做法，一些人认为它保留了历史感，而另一些人则认为这是一种肤浅的姿态。|近义词：facade retention, preservationism (in a limited sense)|反义词：complete demolition, total reconstruction|词性:noun
