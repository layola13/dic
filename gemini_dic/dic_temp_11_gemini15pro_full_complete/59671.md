echinozoa:/ˌekɪnoʊˈzoʊə/ | n. | 棘皮动物门 | A major group of invertebrate animals, characterized by their radial symmetry and spiny skin. This phylum includes sea urchins, sand dollars, sea cucumbers, and starfish. |  Example 1: Echinoderms, members of the phylum Echinodermata, and Hemichordates belong to the superphylum Echinodermata.  棘皮动物，棘皮动物门的成员，和半索动物属于棘皮动物总门。

Example 2:  Scientists are studying the regenerative abilities of some echinozoa. 科学家正在研究一些棘皮动物的再生能力。 | 近义词: echinoderm | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


Etymology:
From Greek *ekhinos* ("hedgehog, sea urchin") + *zōion* ("animal").
