empyreuma:/ˌɛmpaɪˈruːmə/ | n. |焦臭；焦油味 |A burnt smell or taste, typically from organic matter.| The empyreuma of the burnt toast permeated the kitchen. 烤焦面包的焦臭味弥漫了整个厨房。|  The fire left a lingering empyreuma in the house. 火灾过后，房子里留下了挥之不去的焦臭味。 | Synonyms: burnt smell, char, scorch | Antonyms: fragrance, perfume | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *empyreumaticus, from Ancient Greek ἐμπυρεύματος (empyreúmatos), genitive singular of ἐμπύρευμα (empýreuma, “product of fire, burnt matter”), from ἐμπυρεύω (empyreúō, “I set on fire”), from ἐν (en, “in”) + πῦρ (pûr, “fire”).
