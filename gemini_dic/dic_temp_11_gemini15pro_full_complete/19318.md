bible-bashing:/ˈbaɪblˌbæʃɪŋ/
| n. |圣经抨击；以宗教名义进行的攻击|The act of aggressively or excessively quoting or citing the Bible to support one's views, often in a judgmental or self-righteous way.  It implies a lack of nuance and understanding of the text and is often used to condemn or criticize others.|
|例句：He was known for his bible-bashing sermons that alienated many parishioners. 他因其抨击圣经的布道而疏远了许多教区居民。|
|例句：She accused the politician of bible-bashing to gain votes. 她指责这位政客为了赢得选票而抨击圣经。|
|近义词：dogmatic, moralizing, sanctimonious, preachy|
|反义词：tolerant, open-minded, accepting|
|词性：名词|
