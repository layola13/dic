noncancerous: /ˌnɒnˈkænsərəs/ (British) /ˌnɑːnˈkænsərəs/ (American)
| adj. |非癌性的；良性的|Not cancerous; benign.|The lump was found to be noncancerous.  这个肿块被发现是良性的。|
The mole was thankfully noncancerous.  谢天谢地，这颗痣是良性的。
|近义词：benign, nonmalignant|反义词：cancerous, malignant|词性: 形容词
Etymology: non- + cancerous.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not".  "Cancerous" relates to cancer, a disease caused by uncontrolled cell growth.
