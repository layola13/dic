jib:/dʒɪb/| n. v.|三角帆，船首三角帆；(起重机的)吊臂；拒绝做；不配合|A triangular sail set forward of the foremast of a sailing vessel. The projecting arm of a crane. To refuse to do something; balk.|The ship's jib caught the wind and filled.|近义词：sail, boom, crane, balk, refuse|反义词: cooperate, comply|n. v.

jib:/dʒɪb/| v.| (马)突然停下不肯前进；(人)不愿做；不合作|To refuse to proceed or cooperate.|The horse jibbed at the jump.|近义词： balk, resist, recoil|反义词: cooperate, comply|v.
