n'gana:/ˈŋɑːnə/ | n. | 非洲锥虫病；昏睡病 | A disease of cattle, horses, and other livestock in Africa, caused by trypanosomes and transmitted by the tsetse fly. |  The cattle were weakened by n'gana.  这些牛被非洲锥虫病削弱了。 | Synonyms: trypanosomiasis, sleeping sickness | Antonyms: health, wellness | noun

n'gana:/ˈŋɑːnə/ | n. | 懒散，无精打采 | A state of lethargy or inactivity.  (Less common usage) |  He seemed to have a perpetual n'gana. 他似乎永远无精打采。 | Synonyms: lethargy, lassitude, inertia, torpor  | Antonyms: energy, vigor, activity | noun
