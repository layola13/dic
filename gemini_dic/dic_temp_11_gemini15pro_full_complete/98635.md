insubjection:/ˌɪnsəbˈdʒɛkʃən/ | n. |  屈服; 臣服; 隶属 | The state of being subjected to or under the dominion or control of another. |  The tribes lived in insubjection to the Roman Empire. 这些部落臣服于罗马帝国。 | 近义词: subservience, subordination, subjection, submission | 反义词: dominance, supremacy, control, rebellion | 词性: 名词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin *insubjectio(n-), from the verb insubjectare, from in- ‘into’ + subicere ‘place under’.
