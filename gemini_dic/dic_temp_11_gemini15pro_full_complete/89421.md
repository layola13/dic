homotypy:/həˈmɒtɪpi/ | n. |同型性；相似性|Similarity of structure, especially as a result of descent from a common ancestor.|The homotypy of the two species suggests a shared evolutionary history. 这两个物种的同型性表明它们有共同的进化史。|Example 1: The homotypy between the wings of bats and birds is an example of convergent evolution.  蝙蝠和鸟类翅膀的同型性是趋同进化的一个例子。

Example 2:  Despite their homotypy, the two plant species have distinct genetic profiles. 尽管这两种植物具有同型性，但它们的基因图谱却截然不同。| 近义词: similarity, resemblance, analogy | 反义词: heterotypy, dissimilarity | 词性: noun
