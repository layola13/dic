nonpriestlier:/ˌnɒnˈpriːstliər/
adjective
更不像神职人员的，更缺乏神职人员气质的
Less priestlier; less resembling or characteristic of a priest.
He adopted a nonpriestlier tone in his sermon, focusing on practical advice rather than theological dogma. 他在布道中采取了不太像神职人员的语气，侧重于实用建议而不是神学教条。
Near Synonyms: less priestly, more secular, less clerical, more worldly
Antonyms: more priestly, more clerical, more devout, more religious
词性: adjective
