embryonal:/ˌɛmbrɪˈɑːnəl/ | adj. |胚胎的；萌芽期的|Relating to or characteristic of an embryo; rudimentary. |The embryonal cells are rapidly dividing. 胚胎细胞正在迅速分裂。|The project is still in its embryonal stages.  这个项目还处于萌芽阶段。| 近义词: embryonic, rudimentary, nascent, incipient |反义词: developed, mature |词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *embryonalis, from Latin embryo + -alis.  Ultimately from Ancient Greek ἔμβρυον (émbruon, “embryo”).
