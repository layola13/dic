amaurosis:/ˌæmɔːˈroʊsɪs/| n. |失明，黑蒙|Blindness, especially that occurring without apparent lesion of the eye; partial or complete loss of vision.|例句：The patient experienced sudden amaurosis after the accident.  病人事故后突然失明。

例句：The doctor diagnosed the patient with amaurosis fugax. 医生诊断病人患有短暂性黑蒙。|近义词：blindness, vision loss|反义词:sight|词性:名词
