acanthocarpous:/əˌkænθəˈkɑːrpəs/
adj.
具刺果的；有刺状果实的
Bearing fruit covered with spines or prickles.
Example:  The acanthocarpous plant was difficult to handle without gloves.  这种具刺果的植物不戴手套很难处理。

Example: Researchers discovered a new species of acanthocarpous tree in the rainforest. 研究人员在雨林中发现了一种新的具刺果树种。
近义词: prickly, spiny, thorny
反义词: smooth
词性: adjective


词源:
From Greek *akantha* (thorn, spine) + *karpos* (fruit)
