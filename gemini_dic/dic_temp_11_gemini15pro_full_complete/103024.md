jejuneness:/dʒɪˈdʒuːnnəs/
n.
空洞；贫乏；枯燥无味
A state of being meager, uninteresting, or unsubstantial; aridity.
The jejuneness of the desert landscape stretched for miles.  沙漠的空旷荒凉绵延数英里。
The speaker's jejuneness left the audience bored and restless. 演讲者的空洞乏味让观众感到无聊和不安。
近义词: aridity, barrenness, emptiness, vapidity, dullness
反义词: richness, abundance, fertility, interest, excitement
词性: noun


jejune:/dʒɪˈdʒuːn/
adj.
空洞的；贫乏的；枯燥无味的
Naive, simplistic, and superficial. Lacking in nutritive value; not nourishing.
His jejune arguments failed to impress the committee. 他幼稚肤浅的论点未能打动委员会。
The jejune diet of rice and beans left him feeling weak.  只有米饭和豆子的贫乏饮食让他感到虚弱。
近义词: naive, simplistic, superficial, insubstantial, vapid
反义词: mature, sophisticated, profound, substantial, stimulating
词性: adjective
