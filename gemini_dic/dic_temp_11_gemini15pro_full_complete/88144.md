hilbert: /ˈhɪlbərt/ | n. | 希尔伯特 |  German mathematician recognized as one of the most influential and universal mathematicians of the 19th and early 20th centuries.  He invented or developed a broad range of fundamental ideas in many areas, including invariant theory, the axiomatization of geometry,  Hilbert space (a key concept in functional analysis), and the foundations of mathematics. | David Hilbert formulated 23 unsolved problems in mathematics that deeply influenced the direction of mathematical research in the 20th century. 戴维·希尔伯特提出了23个未解决的数学问题，这些问题深刻地影响了20世纪数学研究的方向。

| The Hilbert Hotel is a thought experiment illustrating a counterintuitive property of infinite sets. 希尔伯特旅馆是一个思想实验，它阐释了无限集合的一个反直觉的性质。 | 近义词: mathematician | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
