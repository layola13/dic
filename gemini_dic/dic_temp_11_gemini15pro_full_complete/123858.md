moraller:/məˈrælər/| n.|士气鼓舞者，激励士气的人|Someone who boosts the morale of others. |The coach acted as a moraller for the team after their tough loss. 教练在球队惨败后充当了鼓舞士气的人。|Synonyms: motivator, inspirer, cheerleader|Antonyms: demoralizer, discourager|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From morale (n.) + -er (suffix forming agent nouns).  Morale itself comes from French, ultimately from Latin *moralis, relating to character or custom.
