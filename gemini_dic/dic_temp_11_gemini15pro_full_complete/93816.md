immigrants:/ˈɪmɪɡrənts/| n. |移民；侨民|People who have come to a new country to live there permanently.|Many immigrants come to this country seeking better opportunities. 许多移民来到这个国家寻求更好的机会。|The city has a large immigrant population. 这个城市有大量的移民人口。|近义词：newcomers, settlers, migrants|反义词：emigrants, natives|Noun


Etymology:
From Late Middle English: from Latin *immigrant- ‘immigrating’, from the verb immigrare, from in- ‘into’ + migrare ‘to migrate’.
