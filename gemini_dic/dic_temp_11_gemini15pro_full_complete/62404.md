endogen:/ˈɛndədʒən/ |n.|内源性物质，内生变量|A substance or factor that originates within a system, especially a living organism or cell, or a model of economic growth. |  Endogens are internal factors that stimulate economic growth. 内生因素是刺激经济增长的内部因素。| The model uses endogens to predict future market behavior.  这个模型使用内生变量来预测未来的市场行为。| synonym: internal factor, intrinsic variable | antonym: exogenous, external factor | noun
