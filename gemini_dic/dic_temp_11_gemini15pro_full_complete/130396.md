non-violent:/ˌnɒnˈvaɪələnt/ /ˌnɑːnˈvaɪələnt/| adj.|非暴力的|Not involving violence; peaceful.|例句：They staged a non-violent protest. 他们举行了非暴力抗议。|近义词：peaceful, pacifist, nonaggressive|反义词:violent, aggressive|词性:形容词
Etymology: non- (prefix meaning "not") + violent (from Latin violentus, meaning "vehement, boisterous, furious")
