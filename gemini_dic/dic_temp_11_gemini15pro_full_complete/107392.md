laminability:/ˌlæmɪnəˈbɪləti/
n.
可层压性，可叠片性
The quality or state of being laminable; capable of being laminated.
This material has excellent laminability, making it ideal for creating layered structures.  这种材料具有优异的可层压性，使其成为创建层状结构的理想选择。
laminableness
non-laminability
noun
