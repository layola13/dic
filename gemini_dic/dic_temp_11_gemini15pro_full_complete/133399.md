nonfactors:/ˈnɒnˌfæk.təz/ /ˈnɑːnˌfæk.tɚz/| n. |非因素；无关因素|Something that does not contribute to or influence a particular result or situation. |The weather was a nonfactor in the game, as it was played indoors.  天气对比赛没有影响，因为比赛是在室内进行的。|
nonfactors:/ˈnɒnˌfæk.təz/ /ˈnɑːnˌfæk.tɚz/| n. |非因素；无关因素|His lack of experience was a nonfactor in his success; his natural talent carried him through.  他缺乏经验对他成功来说无关紧要；他的天赋使他成功。|
nonfactors:/ˈnɒnˌfæk.təz/ /ˈnɑːnˌfæk.tɚz/| n. |非因素；无关因素|Etymology:  "non-" (not) + "factor" (something that contributes to a result).  词源：“non-”（不）+“factor”（促成结果的事物）。|近义词: irrelevancies, unimportant details|反义词: factors, key elements|词性:noun
