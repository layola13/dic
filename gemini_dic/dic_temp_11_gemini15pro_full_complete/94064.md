impassible:/ɪmˈpæsəbl/ /ɪmˈpæsɪbl/| adj.|无动于衷的，冷漠的，不为所动的；（道路、河流等）不能通行的|Not capable of feeling or suffering; incapable of being affected or disturbed; also, incapable of being passed, traversed, or crossed.|His face remained impassible throughout the trial.  他的表情在整个审判过程中都无动于衷。|近义词：stoic, unemotional, apathetic, indifferent, emotionless, inscrutable, impenetrable; (for places) inaccessible, impassable|反义词：emotional, sensitive, passionate, expressive; (for places) accessible, passable|词性:形容词

impassible:/ɪmˈpæsəbl/ /ɪmˈpæsɪbl/| adj.|不能通行的，无法通过的|Impossible to travel on or over.|The road became impassible after the heavy rain. 大雨过后，这条路变得无法通行。|近义词：inaccessible, impassable|反义词：accessible, passable|词性:形容词
