chafing:/ˈtʃeɪfɪŋ/ (US), /ˈtʃeɪfɪŋ/ (UK)| n. v. adj.|擦伤，摩擦， раздражение (Russian)|The soreness or irritation caused by rubbing against something.|The tight shoes caused chafing on his heels.  紧的鞋子导致他的脚后跟擦伤。|近义词：abrasion, irritation, rubbing, friction|反义词:soothing, comfort|词性:Noun (primarily), Verb, Adjective

chafe:/tʃeɪf/ (US), /tʃeɪf/ (UK)| v.|擦伤，摩擦, раздражать (Russian)|To become sore or irritated from rubbing.|The rough fabric chafed her skin. 粗糙的布料擦伤了她的皮肤。|近义词：irritate, abrade, graze|反义词:soothe, comfort|词性:Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *chaufen, from Old North French *chaufer "to warm, heat," from Vulgar Latin *calefare "to make warm," from Latin calefacere, from calēre "to be warm" + facere "to make." The sense shifted from "to warm by rubbing" to "to make sore by rubbing."
