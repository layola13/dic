deescalate:/ˌdiːˈɛskəleɪt/| v. |缓和，降级，降低|To reduce the intensity or seriousness of a conflict or situation.|The police tried to deescalate the situation by talking to the protesters. 警察试图通过与抗议者对话来缓和局势。| The company decided to deescalate its involvement in the project due to budget cuts. 由于预算削减，公司决定减少对该项目的参与。 |synonyms: reduce, lessen, diminish, mitigate, alleviate, moderate|antonyms: escalate, intensify, aggravate, exacerbate|verb

deescalation:/ˌdiːˌɛskəˈleɪʃən/| n. |缓和，降级，降低|The act of reducing the intensity or seriousness of a conflict or situation.|The deescalation of the conflict was welcomed by all parties. 冲突的缓和受到了各方的欢迎。|  Successful de-escalation requires careful communication and empathy.  成功的降级需要谨慎的沟通和同理心。|synonyms: reduction, lessening, diminution, mitigation, alleviation, moderation|antonyms: escalation, intensification, aggravation, exacerbation|noun
