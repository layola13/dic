come-hitherness:/kʌmˈhɪðərnəs/| n.|诱惑；性感；挑逗性|The photograph conveyed a distinct sense of come-hitherness.  这张照片传达出一种明显的诱惑感。|The dress had a certain come-hitherness that made heads turn.  这件衣服有一种特别的诱惑力，让人忍不住回头看。|近义词：allure, seductiveness, provocativeness|反义词：modesty, demureness|词性:名词

Etymology:  From "come hither," meaning "come here," implying a beckoning or inviting gesture.  "Come hither" dates back to the 14th century. The suffix "-ness" transforms the phrase into a noun denoting a quality or state.
