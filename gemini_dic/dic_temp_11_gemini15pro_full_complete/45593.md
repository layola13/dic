current: /ˈkɜːrənt/ (UK) /ˈkɜːrənt/ (US) | adj. n. | 当前的，现在的；潮流，水流 |  flowing or happening now; a flow of water, air, or electricity | The current situation is very complex. 目前的形势非常复杂。| synonyms: contemporary, present, prevailing | antonyms: past, former, outdated | adjective

current: /ˈkɜːrənt/ (UK) /ˈkɜːrənt/ (US) | n. | 潮流，水流；电流 | a flow of water, air, or electricity | The strong current carried the boat downstream.  强劲的水流把船带向下游。| synonyms: stream, flow, tide | antonyms: stillness, stagnation | noun

Etymology: From Latin "currens," present participle of "currere" (to run).
