nonaffecting:/ˌnɒnəˈfɛktɪŋ/
adjective
不影响的; 无作用的
Not producing any significant or noticeable effect; not causing a change or alteration.
The new law is nonaffecting to current retirees.  这项新法律对目前的退休人员没有影响。
The software update was nonaffecting, causing no noticeable changes.  这个软件更新没有作用，没有引起任何明显的改变。
近义词:  inert, neutral, ineffective
反义词:  affecting, influential, impactful
词性: adjective
词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + affecting (present participle of "affect," from Latin *affectare, frequentative of afficere "to influence," from ad- "to" + facere "to do, make")
