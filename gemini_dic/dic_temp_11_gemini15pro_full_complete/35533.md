coagulatory:/kəʊˈæɡjʊlətəri/ /koʊˈæɡjələtɔːri/| adj.|凝血的，促凝的|Relating to or promoting coagulation; tending to clot.|The doctor prescribed coagulatory medicine to stop the bleeding. 医生开了促凝血药物来止血。|Synonyms: clotting, hemostatic|Antonyms: anticoagulant|adjective

coagulate:/kəʊˈæɡjʊleɪt/ /koʊˈæɡjəleɪt/| v.|凝结，凝固| (of a fluid, especially blood) change to a solid or semisolid state.|The blood began to coagulate around the wound.  血液开始在伤口周围凝结。|Synonyms: clot, thicken, congeal|Antonyms: liquefy, thin|verb
