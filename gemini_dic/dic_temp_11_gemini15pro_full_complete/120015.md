millinet:/ˌmɪlɪˈnɛt/ | n. | 米利内特（一种细密的网状织物） | A fine, strong, usually cotton or silk plain-woven fabric used for dresses, curtains, and mosquito nets. |  She wore a dress of white millinet. 她穿着一件白色米利内特连衣裙。|  The windows were covered with millinet curtains. 窗户上挂着米利内特窗帘。| 近义词: netting, mesh, tulle | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "coarse fabric") | 词性: noun
