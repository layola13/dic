gazunder:/ɡæˈzʌndə(r)/
n.
压低出价；临时压价
The practice of lowering an offer on a property just before the exchange of contracts.
例句：The seller was gazundered just days before completion. 卖家在交易完成前几天被压价了。

例句：Gazundering is a common practice in a falling property market. 在房地产市场下跌的情况下，压低出价是一种常见的做法。
近义词：downward pressure, price reduction
反义词：gazumping
词性: 名词
