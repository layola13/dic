clambered:/ˈklæmbərd/| v.| 攀登，爬|climb with difficulty, using both hands and feet|例句：They clambered over the rocks. 他们攀爬过岩石。|例句：The children clambered up the steep hill. 孩子们爬上了陡峭的山坡。|近义词：climb, scramble, ascend|反义词:descend|词性:动词

词源:
From Middle English *clambren, frequentative of climben (“to climb”).
