acceding:/əkˈsiːdɪŋ/
v.
同意，加入
agreeing to a demand, request, or treaty
The company president is acceding to the employees' demands for better benefits. 公司总裁同意了员工提出的改善福利的要求。
synonyms: agreeing, consenting, complying, acquiescing
antonyms: refusing, rejecting, denying, opposing
verb

acceding:/əkˈsiːdɪŋ/
v.
就任，就职 (尤指高级职位)
coming into a position of authority or a title, especially a high office
The new king is acceding to the throne next month. 新国王将于下个月登基。
synonyms: assuming, taking on, undertaking
antonyms: resigning, abdicating, relinquishing
verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin accedere (“to approach, go to, arrive at, be added to”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + cedere (“to go, yield”).
