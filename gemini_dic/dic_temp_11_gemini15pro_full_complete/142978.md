outbalance:/ˌaʊtˈbæləns/
verb
超过，胜过
to be greater or more important than something else
The advantages outbalance the disadvantages. 利大于弊。
The benefits greatly outbalance the risks. 好处远远大于风险。
outweigh, override, surpass, exceed
counterbalance, offset
v.
