all-in-one:/ˌɔːl ɪn ˈwʌn/ | adj. n. |一体式的，多功能的；一体机|A single product that combines multiple functions. |  I bought an all-in-one printer that can also scan and copy. 我买了一台一体机打印机，它还可以扫描和复印。|Synonyms: multifunctional, integrated, combined | Antonyms: separate, individual | Adjective/Noun

all-in-ones:/ˌɔːl ɪn ˈwʌnz/ | n. |一体机（复数）|Plural form of "all-in-one," referring to multiple such devices. |  Many offices now use all-in-ones instead of separate printers, scanners, and fax machines. 许多办公室现在使用一体机，而不是单独的打印机、扫描仪和传真机。| Synonyms: multifunctional devices, integrated units | Antonyms: separate devices, individual units | Noun
