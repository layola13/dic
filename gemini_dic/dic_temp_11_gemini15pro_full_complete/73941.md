forthcomingnesses:/ˌfɔːθˈkʌmɪŋnəsəz/
n.
即将来临；坦率，直爽
The quality or state of being forthcoming.  Forthcomingness can refer to the willingness to provide information or to be helpful and cooperative. It can also describe something that is readily available or about to happen.
His forthcomingness about his past impressed the interviewers. 他对过去的坦诚给面试官留下了深刻印象。

The forthcomingness of the new product launch has everyone excited.  新产品发布的临近让每个人都很兴奋。

近义词: openness, candor, frankness, directness, availability, imminence
反义词: reticence, evasiveness, secrecy, unavailability

词性: noun
