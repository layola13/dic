nonqualifications:/ˌnɒnˌkwɒlɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/
|n.|不合格；不胜任；没有资格|Lack of necessary skills, experience, or credentials for a particular job or task.  The state of not meeting required standards or conditions. |His lack of formal qualifications was a barrier to his promotion.|Synonyms: inadequacy, deficiency, unsuitability, incompetence, ineligibility|Antonyms: qualifications, competence, suitability, eligibility, adequacy
|noun|
