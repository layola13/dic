creamest:/ˈkriːmiɪst/ | adj. | 最奶油味的，最像奶油的 | Superlative form of creamy: most creamy.  Describes something that has the highest degree of a smooth, rich texture and flavor resembling cream. |  The creamest ice cream I've ever tasted was in Italy. 我尝过最奶油味的冰淇淋是在意大利。|  The bakery makes the creamest cheesecake in town. 这家面包店做全城最奶油味的芝士蛋糕。| Near Synonyms: richest, smoothest, silkiest | Antonyms: thinnest, runniest, watery |  Adjective
