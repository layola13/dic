multimodality:/ˌmʌltiˌmoʊˈdælɪti/ | n. |多模态，多形态|The use of multiple modes (such as text, images, audio, and video) to communicate or represent information.  It refers to the integration of different modes of communication or representation.| Example: The museum exhibit employed multimodality, combining artifacts, interactive displays, and audio recordings to tell the story of the city.  博物馆展览采用多模态，结合文物、互动展示和录音来讲述城市的故事。| Synonym: multimedia, multimodal communication | Antonym: unimodal, single-mode | noun
