dirtiest:/ˈdɜːrtiɪst/ | adj. | 最脏的 | Superlative form of dirty; most dirty. |  The dirtiest room I've ever seen was in that abandoned house. 我见过的最脏的房间就在那栋废弃的房子里。| The playground was the dirtiest after the rain. 雨后操场是最脏的。 | 近义词：filthiest, foulest, grubbiest | 反义词：cleanest, purest, tidiest | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *dirtieste*, superlative of *dirti*, from Old English *dyriht*, *dryge* (“excrement, dung”), of uncertain origin.  Related to Old Norse *drit* (“excrement”).  The adjectival sense developed from the noun, referring to something soiled with excrement.
