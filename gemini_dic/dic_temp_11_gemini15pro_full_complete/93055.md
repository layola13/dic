idolatrize:/aɪˈdɑːlətraɪz/ | vt. | 崇拜；奉为偶像；盲目崇拜 | To admire, revere, or love someone or something excessively or to a degree that approaches worship. |  She idolizes her older brother. 她把她哥哥奉为偶像。

He idolized the famous actress. 他盲目崇拜那位著名女演员。

近义词: worship, adore, venerate, exalt, glorify | 反义词: despise, abhor, loathe, condemn | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *idolatrare, from Latin idololatria "idolatry," from Greek eidōlolatreia, from eidōlon "image, idol" + latreia "worship, service."
