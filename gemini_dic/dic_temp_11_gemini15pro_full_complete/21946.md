PHRASE->
mind-boggling = 令人难以置信的
It's mind-boggling how much information the internet holds.  互联网存储的信息量之大令人难以置信。
what boggles the mind is... = 令人费解的是……
What boggles the mind is how anyone could believe such a thing.  令人费解的是，怎么会有人相信这种事。
boggle at = 对...感到吃惊；对...犹豫不决
Don't boggle at the price, it's worth it.  不要对价格犹豫不决，它物有所值。
boggle (someone's) mind = 使（某人）感到困惑
The sheer number of stars in the galaxy boggles my mind.  银河系中星星的数量之多让我感到困惑。
it boggles the imagination = 难以想象
It boggles the imagination to think of the vastness of space.  难以想象太空的浩瀚。
