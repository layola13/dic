hypercatharses:/ˌhaɪpərˈkəθɑːrsiːz/
| n. |过度宣泄，极度净化|An intense emotional release or purification, going beyond typical catharsis.|She experienced hypercatharsis after finally confronting her abuser. 她终于面对了施暴者后，经历了极度的感情宣泄。|近义词：catharsis, purgation, cleansing|反义词: repression, suppression|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek *ὑπέρ (hupér, “over, above”) + κάθαρσις (kátharsis, “cleansing, purification”).
