defencing:/dɪˈfɛnsɪŋ/
n.|防御；防护；辩护|The act of defending something or someone.|The defencing of the castle was impressive. 城堡的防御令人印象深刻。|近义词: protecting, guarding, shielding|反义词: attacking, assaulting|词性:名词

defencing:/dɪˈfɛnsɪŋ/
v.|防御；防护；辩护|(present participle of 'defence') The action of protecting from or resisting attack.|They are defencing their territory against invaders. 他们正在保卫自己的领土免受侵略者的侵害。|近义词: protecting, guarding, shielding|反义词: attacking, assaulting|词性:动词
