carbureted:/ˈkɑːrbjəreɪtɪd/ /ˈkɑːrbjʊreɪtɪd/
adjective
汽化的；用化油器装备的
equipped with a carburetor; mixed with a volatile liquid to increase the explosive power.
My old car has a carbureted engine. 我的旧车有一个化油器发动机。
The mixture is carbureted with gasoline. 混合物用汽油汽化。
近义词:fueled, gasified
反义词:fuel-injected
词性:adjective
