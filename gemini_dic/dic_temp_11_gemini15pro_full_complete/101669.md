irrelevances:/ɪˈrɛləvənsɪz/
|n.| 无关紧要的事物；不相关的事物 |Something that is not relevant or important to the matter at hand.|  The report was filled with irrelevances and lacked focus on the key issues.  这份报告充满了无关紧要的内容，缺乏对关键问题的关注。| synonyms: trivialities, inconsequentialities,  inapposite, extraneous matters| antonyms:  relevancies, pertinences | noun


Etymology: From irrelevant +‎ -ance.  Irrelevant comes from in- (“not”) +‎ relevant. Relevant from Latin  *relevant- (“raising up, lifting”), present participle of *relevāre, from re- +‎ levāre (“to lift, lighten”).
