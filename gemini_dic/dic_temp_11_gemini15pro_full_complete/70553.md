filigree:/ˈfɪlɪɡriː/
n.|金银丝细工; (尤指金属的) 透雕细工; 精致的装饰|Ornate silver filigree decorated the handle of the dagger.  华丽的银丝装饰着匕首的手柄。|近义词: ornamentation, tracery, arabesque|反义词: plainness, simplicity|词性:名词

filigree:/ˈfɪlɪɡriː/
v.|用金银丝细工装饰; 精心制作|The gold box was filigreed with tiny flowers. 金盒子上用细小的花朵图案进行了金银丝细工装饰。|近义词: adorn, decorate, embellish|反义词: simplify, strip|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *filigraine*, from Italian *filigrana*, from *filo* (“thread”) + *grana* (“grain”).
