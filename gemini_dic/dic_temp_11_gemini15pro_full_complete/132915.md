nonelite:/ˌnɒnɪˈliːt/
adjective
非精英的；平民的
Not belonging to an elite; not of the highest quality or status.
The nonelite members felt excluded from the decision-making process.  非精英成员感觉被排除在决策过程之外。
The university offers scholarships to attract nonelite students.  这所大学提供奖学金以吸引非精英学生。
近义词:ordinary, common, average, non-elite
反义词:elite, privileged, exclusive
词性:adjective

词源:
Prefix "non-" (not) + "elite" (the choice part or best of anything considered collectively)
