growingly: /ˈɡroʊɪŋli/ | adv. | 越来越；日益 | Increasingly; to an increasing extent. | The situation is becoming growingly complex.  情况变得越来越复杂。

The problem is growingly serious. 这个问题日益严重。 | 近义词: increasingly, progressively, more and more | 反义词: decreasingly, less and less | 词性: 副词

词源: From "growing" + "-ly". "Growing" comes from the Old English "growan," meaning "to grow." The suffix "-ly" forms adverbs from adjectives.
