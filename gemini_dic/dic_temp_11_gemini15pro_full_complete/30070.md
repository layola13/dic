CD-RW (Compact Disc-ReWritable) 可重写光盘
A CD-RW is a type of compact disc that can be written to, erased, and rewritten multiple times. CD-RW是一种可以写入、擦除和多次重写的紧凑型光盘。
I used a CD-RW to back up my important files. 我使用CD-RW备份了我的重要文件。
近义词:CD-R, DVD-RW, USB drive
反义词:CD-ROM
词性:名词
