hypermagically:/ˌhaɪpərˈmædʒɪkli/ | adv. | 超魔般地，极其神奇地 | In a way that surpasses ordinary magic; with extraordinary magical power. | The door hypermagically opened, revealing a hidden chamber. 门超魔般地打开了，露出一个隐藏的房间。| The potion hypermagically healed his wounds in seconds.  这药水神奇地几秒钟就治好了他的伤口。 | 近义词：miraculously, magically, enchantingly | 反义词：ordinarily, normally | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
hyper- (prefix): from Greek, meaning "over, beyond, excessive"
magical: from Old French magique, from Latin magicus, from Greek magikos, relating to magi (wise men, magicians).
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives
