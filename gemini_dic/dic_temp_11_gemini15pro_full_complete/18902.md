besmirchers:/bɪˈsmɜːrtʃərz/
n.
诽谤者，污蔑者
People who besmirch someone's reputation.  Those who tarnish or discredit another's name or character.
The besmirchers were spreading lies about the candidate. 诽谤者正在散布关于候选人的谎言。
Synonyms: slanderers, defamers, vilifiers, detractors
Antonyms:  supporters, defenders, champions
词性: 名词 (Noun)


besmirch:/bɪˈsmɜːrtʃ/
vt.
玷污，诽谤，损害(名誉)
To damage the reputation of (someone or something) by saying or suggesting that they are bad or wrong.
The scandal besmirched the reputation of the entire company.  这桩丑闻玷污了整个公司的声誉。
Synonyms:  sully, tarnish, smear, taint, disgrace
Antonyms:  honor, praise, extol, laud
词性: 动词 (Verb)


ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + smirch.  "Smirch" comes from Middle English *smerten, of uncertain origin, probably from Old Norse *smyrja "to smear."  The prefix "be-" intensifies the meaning.
