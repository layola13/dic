abasing:/əˈbeɪsɪŋ/| adj. | 降低身份的；贬低的；屈辱的 |  Humiliating or degrading; lowering in rank, dignity, or esteem. | His abasing apology made him feel even smaller. 他那低声下气的道歉让他感觉更加渺小。| 近义词:humiliating, degrading, humbling, shameful | 反义词:exalting, elevating, ennobling | 词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *abasen, from Old French abaissier, from a- (from Latin ad-) + baissier (to lower), from Vulgar Latin *bassiare, from Latin bassus (low).
