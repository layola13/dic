drudge:/drʌdʒ/
n.|苦工，做单调乏味工作的人|a person who does hard, menial, or tedious work|She felt like a drudge, constantly cleaning and cooking for her family.|近义词:toiler, laborer, slave, menial|反义词:idler, loafer|词性:名词

drudge:/drʌdʒ/
v.|做苦工，辛勤工作|to work hard and persistently at tedious tasks|He drudged away at his homework all weekend.|近义词:toil, slave, grind|反义词:rest, relax|词性:动词
