hippocrates:/hɪˈpɒkrəˌtiːz/ | n. | 希波克拉底 |  A Greek physician of the Classical period, traditionally regarded as the father of medicine. | Hippocrates is considered the father of Western medicine. 希波克拉底被认为是西方医学之父。|  Galen,  Avicenna | N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin Hippocratēs, from Ancient Greek Ἱπποκράτης (Hippokrátēs).  Ultimately derived from ἵππος (híppos, “horse”) + κρατέω (kratéō, “to rule, be strong”).  Thus, "horse master" or "strong horse".
