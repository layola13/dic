altogether:/ˌɔːltəˈɡeðər/| adv.|总共，完全地|Completely; entirely; in all.|例句：There were ten people altogether. 总共有十个人。|近义词：entirely, completely, totally, wholly, utterly|反义词:partly, partially|词性:副词
词源：From Old English *eall togædere, from eall "all" + togædere "together."
