morro:/ˈmɔːroʊ/ (US), /ˈmɒrəʊ/ (UK)| n. |小山, 圆丘; (尤指突出于水面的) 小岛, 岬|A rounded hill or promontory, especially one overlooking the sea.|We watched the sunset from the morro. 我们从小山上观看了日落。|近义词：hill, knoll, promontory, bluff|反义词:valley, plain|noun

morro:/ˈmɔːroʊ/ (US), /ˈmɒrəʊ/ (UK)| n. |(古巴) 城堡| (in Cuba) a castle or fort|The Morro Castle in Havana is a famous landmark. 哈瓦那的莫罗城堡是一个著名的地标。|近义词：castle, fort, fortress|反义词:N/A|noun
