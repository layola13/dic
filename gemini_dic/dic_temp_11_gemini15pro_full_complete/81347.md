graphitic:/ɡrəˈfɪtɪk/
adjective
石墨的，石墨质的
Relating to or containing graphite; made of graphite.
The electrode is made of a graphitic material.  该电极由石墨材料制成。
The graphitic layers slide easily against each other. 石墨层很容易相互滑动。
Synonyms: carbonaceous, graphite-like
Antonyms: non-graphitic
词性: adjective
