anticonservative:/ˌæntikənˈsɜːrvətɪv/
adjective
反保守主义的；非保守的
Opposed to traditional values and beliefs, especially in politics or religion.
The student group held an anticonservative rally to protest the new policies. 学生团体举行了一场反保守主义的集会，抗议新的政策。
Her anticonservative views often clashed with her family's traditional beliefs. 她反保守的观点经常与她家庭的传统观念发生冲突。
Near Synonyms: liberal, progressive, radical, reformist, revolutionary
Antonyms: conservative, traditional, orthodox, reactionary
