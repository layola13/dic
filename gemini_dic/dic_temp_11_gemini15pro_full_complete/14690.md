awakenable:/əˈweɪkənəbl/| adj.|可唤醒的，可激发的|Capable of being awakened or roused.|The volcano is awakenable after centuries of dormancy. 这座火山在休眠了几个世纪后可以被唤醒。|The CEO's speech was awakenable, inspiring the employees to work harder.  CEO的演讲很有感染力，激励员工更加努力工作。|近义词：rousable, arousable, excitable|反义词：unrousable, dormant, lethargic|词性:形容词

awaken:/əˈweɪkən/| v.|唤醒，觉醒|To rouse from sleep; to become aware of something.|She was awakened by a loud noise. 她被一声巨响吵醒。|His interest in art was awakened by a visit to the museum. 他对艺术的兴趣是被一次博物馆参观激发的。|近义词：rouse, arouse, stir, waken|反义词：sleep, slumber, doze|词性:动词
