octoradiated: /ˌɒk.təˈreɪ.di.eɪ.tɪd/ | adj. | 八辐的，有八条射线的 | Having eight rays or arms radiating from a central point. |例句：The octoradiated coral displayed its vibrant colors under the ocean's surface. 八辐珊瑚在海洋表面下展现出鲜艳的色彩。

例句：Octoradiated symmetry is a common feature of some marine invertebrates.  八辐对称是一些海洋无脊椎动物的共同特征。|近义词：eight-rayed, radial |反义词:  N/A |词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Latin *octo- "eight" + radiatus "rayed," past participle of radiare "to shine, radiate."
