cocultures:/ˈkoʊkʌltʃərz/
noun
共同培养，共培养物
The simultaneous cultivation of two or more different species or strains of organisms together.
Researchers are studying the cocultures of bacteria and fungi to understand their interactions. 研究人员正在研究细菌和真菌的共培养，以了解它们的相互作用。
co-cultivation, mixed culture
monoculture
