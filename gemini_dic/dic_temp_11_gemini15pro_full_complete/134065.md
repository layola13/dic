nonignominiously: /ˌnɒnɪɡnəˈmɪniəsli/ /ˌnɑːnɪɡnəˈmɪniəsli/
| adv. | 不丢脸地，不耻辱地，光荣地 |  In a way that is not ignominious; honorably or creditably.  | He retired nonignominiously after a long and successful career. 他在漫长而成功的职业生涯后光荣退休。 |例句：She fought nonignominiously for her rights. 她为自己的权利进行了不屈不挠的斗争。 | 近义词：honorably, creditably, respectably, gloriously | 反义词：ignominiously, shamefully, disgracefully, dishonorably | 词性: 副词


Etymology:
From non- (not) + ignominious (disgraceful) + -ly (adverb suffix).  The root of "ignominious" comes from Latin "in-" (not) + "nomen" (name, reputation) + "-ous" (adjective suffix).
