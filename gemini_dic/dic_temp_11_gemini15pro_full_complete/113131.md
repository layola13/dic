lungworm:/ˈlʌŋwɜːrm/| n. | 肺线虫 |A parasitic worm that infects the lungs of animals, especially mammals. |例句：The farmer wormed his pigs to protect them from lungworm.  这位农场主给他的猪驱虫以防止肺线虫感染。

例句：Lungworm can cause serious respiratory problems in animals. 肺线虫会导致动物严重的呼吸问题。|近义词：parasite, nematode|反义词: None (no direct opposite for a specific parasite)|词性: noun
