eventlessly: /ɪˈvɛntləsli/
adjective
平静地；平淡无奇地
Happening without any significant or memorable events.
The day passed eventlessly.  这一天平淡无奇地过去了。
The meeting proceeded eventlessly. 会议平淡无奇地进行着。
Synonyms: uneventfully, monotonously, dully, plainly
Antonyms: excitingly, dramatically, interestingly
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From event + -less + -ly.  Event comes from Latin "eventus" meaning "outcome, occurrence."  The suffix "-less" indicates absence, and "-ly" forms an adverb.
