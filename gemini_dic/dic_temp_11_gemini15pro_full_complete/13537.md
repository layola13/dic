attemper:/əˈtɛmpər/| vt.|缓和；调和；使适应|To moderate or make milder; to adapt or accustom.|The king attempted to attemper the anger of his subjects. 国王试图缓和臣民的愤怒。|近义词：moderate, mitigate, temper, soften, adjust, acclimatize|反义词：aggravate, exacerbate, intensify|verb

attemper:/əˈtɛmpər/| vt.|调温；使温度适中|To bring to a suitable temperature.|The chemist attempered the solution to precisely 70 degrees Celsius.  化学家将溶液的温度精确地调到70摄氏度。|近义词：regulate, adjust|反义词：--|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *attemperare, from Latin ad- (“to”) + temperare (“to moderate, mix”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *temH- (“to cut, divide”).
