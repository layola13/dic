introductor:/ˌɪntrəˈdʌktər/| n.|介绍人，引荐人，引言，导论|A person who introduces another person to a group or gathering; something that serves as a preface or introduction.|The chairman acted as the introductor for the guest speaker. 主席担任了客座演讲者的介绍人。|synonyms: presenter, usher, preface, foreword, prologue|antonyms: concluder, epilogue|noun


introducer:/ˌɪntrəˈdjuːsər/| n.|介绍人，引进者|Someone who introduces something new, such as a product or idea.|He is known as the introducer of new farming techniques in the region. 他是该地区新农业技术的引进者。|synonyms: initiator, pioneer, innovator|antonyms: follower, imitator|noun
