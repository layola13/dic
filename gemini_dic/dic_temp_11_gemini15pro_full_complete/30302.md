cenogamy:/sɪˈnɒɡəmi/| n.|群婚制，群婚|The practice or custom of group marriage.  A social system in which marriage is permitted between any male and any female of the group.|Cenogamy was practiced in some ancient societies.  某些古代社会实行群婚制。|group marriage, communal marriage|monogamy, polygamy|noun
Etymology:  From Greek *koinos* "common" + *gamos* "marriage".
