effervescent:/ˌɛfərˈvɛsənt/ | adj. | 兴奋的；冒泡的；活泼的 | Vivacious and enthusiastic; giving off bubbles; fizzy. |  The effervescent champagne bubbled in the glass. 香槟在杯中冒着泡泡。| Synonyms: bubbly, sparkling, lively, vivacious | Antonyms: flat, dull, lifeless | adjective

effervesce:/ˌɛfərˈvɛs/ | v. | 冒泡；兴奋；活跃 | To give off bubbles; to show liveliness or exhilaration. | The soda effervesced when I opened the bottle.  我打开瓶子时，苏打水冒泡了。| Synonyms: fizz, bubble, sparkle | Antonyms: flatten, subside | verb

Etymology: From Late Latin *effervescens, present participle of effervescere "to boil over, ferment," from ex- "out" + fervescere "to begin to boil, grow hot," from fervere "to boil, glow."
