incrementally:/ˌɪŋkrəˈmentəli/| adv.|逐渐地，递增地|in a way that increases in small amounts; gradually|The project will be implemented incrementally, starting with the most critical components.|近义词：gradually, progressively, steadily|反义词: suddenly, abruptly, all at once|词性:副词

词源: From increment + -al + -ly. Increment comes from Latin incrementum "growth, increase," from past participle stem of increscere "to grow in or upon" (see increase).
