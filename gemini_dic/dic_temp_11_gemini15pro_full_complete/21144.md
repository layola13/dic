blitzer:/ˈblɪtsər/| n.|超速者，测速雷达，突击队员|A person who drives faster than the speed limit; a device used to detect speeding vehicles; a member of a blitz team (especially in sports).|The police blitzer caught him going 15 miles over the speed limit. 警察的测速雷达测到他超速15英里。|Synonyms: speeder, speedster (for a person), radar gun, speed trap (for a device)|Antonyms: slowpoke, laggard (for a person)|noun
