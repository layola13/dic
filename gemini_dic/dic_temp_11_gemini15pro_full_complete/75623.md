fuggy:/ˈfʌɡi/| adj.|闷热的；雾茫茫的；浑浊不清的|Characterized by fog or mist; thick with or like fog; stuffy.|The room was fuggy with cigarette smoke. 房间里满是香烟味，令人窒息。|近义词：misty, hazy, foggy, stuffy, close|反义词：clear, fresh, airy|词性:形容词
词源: From obsolete *fug "thick air," probably from Middle Low German *vug "damp, warm atmosphere in a closed room."  Related to Norwegian fug "close, stuffy air," Swedish fug "sultry, close air."
