corp:/kɔːrp/ | n. | 公司，企业 |  Short for "corporation," referring to a large company or group of companies authorized to act as a single entity and recognized as such in law. |  Example: The corp announced a merger with a rival company. 这家公司宣布与竞争对手合并。 | Synonym: corporation, company, firm, business, enterprise | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but consider "individual," "sole proprietor," "freelancer") | noun
