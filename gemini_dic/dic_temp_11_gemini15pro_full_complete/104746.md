kavass:/kəˈvæs/ | n. | 卫士，侍卫，门房 | An armed attendant or guard, especially in Turkey and other parts of the former Ottoman Empire. |  The kavass stood impassively at the entrance to the embassy. 大使馆门口的卫士面无表情地站着。| synonyms: guard, attendant, doorkeeper, watchman | antonyms:  none (lack of a direct opposite, could consider "intruder" or "attacker" in certain contexts) | noun

kavass:/kəˈvæs/ | n. | (历史) 特使，信使 | (historical) A messenger or courier, especially one employed by a consulate or embassy. | The kavass delivered the important document to the consulate. 信使将重要文件送到了领事馆。| synonyms: messenger, courier, envoy | antonyms: recipient, receiver | noun
