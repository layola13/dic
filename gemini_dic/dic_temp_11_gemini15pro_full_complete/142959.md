outages:/ˈaʊtɪdʒɪz/
| n. |停电；断电；中断；故障|A period when a power supply or other service is not available or when equipment is out of order.|The power outages affected thousands of homes.  停电影响了数千户家庭。|Synonyms:  blackouts, power cuts, failures, disruptions, interruptions|Antonyms:  connections, restorations, operations|Noun
