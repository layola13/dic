interoperative:/ˌɪntərˈɒpərətɪv/ | adj. |可共同操作的，可互操作的|Able to operate or work together; compatible.  Relating to or involving cooperation between different groups, especially in surgery.|The software is interoperative with all major operating systems. 该软件可与所有主要操作系统互操作。|The interoperative procedure involved specialists from multiple disciplines. 这个跨学科手术需要多个专业的专家参与。|近义词：compatible, interoperable, collaborative|反义词：incompatible, non-interoperable|词性:形容词
词源: inter- (between) + operative (functioning)
