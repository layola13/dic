nonarguments:/ˌnɒnˈɑːɡjʊmənts/ | n. | 非论证，非论据 | Statements or points that do not logically support a conclusion or claim.  They might be irrelevant, emotionally charged, or fallacious. |  Example: His personal attacks were nonarguments and didn't address the real issue.  他的人身攻击并非论证，也没有解决真正的问题。


Example:  Citing her popularity as a reason to support her policies is a nonargument.  以她的受欢迎程度作为支持她政策的理由并非论证。


| 近义词: fallacies, irrelevancies, distractions | 反义词: arguments, evidence, proof | 词性: noun
