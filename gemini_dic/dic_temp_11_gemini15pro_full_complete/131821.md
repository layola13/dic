nonconservational:/ˌnɑːnkɑːnsərˈveɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 非守恒的，非保守的 | Not conserving or tending to conserve something, especially energy or mass.  Relating to a force field in which work done in moving a particle between two points depends on the path taken. | The process is nonconservational, meaning that energy is lost as heat. 这个过程是非守恒的，意味着能量以热量的形式损失掉了。| Near synonyms: dissipative, non-conservative | Near antonyms: conservative, preservational | adjective

nonconservational:/ˌnɑːnkɑːnsərˈveɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 非守恒的，非保守的 |  In physics, a nonconservative force is one for which the work done in moving a particle between two points depends on the path taken. Friction is a classic example of a nonconservative force. | The friction force acting on the sliding block is nonconservational. 作用在滑动块上的摩擦力是非守恒的。 | Near synonyms: dissipative, non-conservative | Near antonyms: conservative, preservational | adjective
