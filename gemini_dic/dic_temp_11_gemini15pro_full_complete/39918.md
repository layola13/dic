constraining:/kənˈstreɪnɪŋ/
| adj. |限制的，约束的|Limiting or restricting something, or someone's actions or behaviour.|High taxes are constraining economic growth. 高税收正在限制经济增长.|近义词：restricting, limiting, inhibiting, confining|反义词：liberating, freeing, enabling|词性:形容词
词源：From the verb "constrain," which comes from Old French "constraindre," from Latin "constringere," meaning "to bind tightly together."
