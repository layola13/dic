frontignac: /ˌfrɒntiˈnjæk/ (UK) /ˌfrʌntɪˈnjæk/ (US)| n. | 白麝香葡萄（用于酿造甜葡萄酒）;由白麝香葡萄酿造的甜葡萄酒|A white grape variety used to make sweet wines, especially in the Loire Valley and Bordeaux regions of France; a sweet white wine made from this grape.|例句：This Frontignac has a lovely aroma of apricots.  这种白麝香葡萄酒有可爱的杏子香气。

例句：We enjoyed a bottle of chilled Frontignac with dessert. 我们享用了一瓶冰镇的白麝香葡萄酒配甜点。|近义词：Muscat, sweet wine|反义词: dry wine|词性:noun
