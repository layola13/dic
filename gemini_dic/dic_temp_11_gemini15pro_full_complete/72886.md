follow-up:/ˈfɒləʊ ʌp/ (UK) /ˈfɑːloʊ ʌp/ (US)| n. adj.|后续行动，跟进；补充；续篇；后续报道|A further action or thing that continues something that has already started or been done. |例句：I have a follow-up appointment with the doctor next week. 我下周要和医生进行一次复诊。|近义词：continuation, sequel, consequence|反义词：precursor, beginning|词性:名词

follow-up:/ˈfɒləʊ ʌp/ (UK) /ˈfɑːloʊ ʌp/ (US)| v.|跟进；补充；采取后续行动|To continue or add to something that has already started or been done. |例句：The police are following up on several leads. 警察正在跟进几条线索。|近义词：pursue, continue, investigate|反义词：abandon, neglect|词性:动词
