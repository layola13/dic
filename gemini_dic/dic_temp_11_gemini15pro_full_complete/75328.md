frostbite:/ˈfrɒstbʌɪt/ (UK) /ˈfrɔːstbʌɪt/ (US)| n.|冻伤；冻疮|Injury to body tissues caused by exposure to extreme cold.|He got frostbite on his fingers after being lost in the snow. 他在雪中迷路后手指冻伤了。|Near synonyms: freezing, chilblains|Opposite: burn, scald|noun


frostbite:/ˈfrɒstbʌɪt/ (UK) /ˈfrɔːstbʌɪt/ (US)| v.|使…冻伤|To injure body tissues by exposure to extreme cold.|His toes were frostbitten after spending hours in the freezing temperatures. 他的脚趾在冰点下待了几个小时后冻伤了。|Near synonyms: freeze|Opposite: burn, scald|verb
