freedman:/ˈfriːdmən/| n. |自由民，被解放的奴隶|A person, especially a man, who has been freed from slavery.|例句：The freedmen rejoiced at their newfound liberty. 自由民为他们新获得的自由而欢欣鼓舞。

例句：Many freedmen struggled to find work after the Civil War. 内战后，许多自由民难以找到工作。|近义词：emancipated slave, liberated slave|反义词:slave|词性:名词

词源:  freed + man.  "freed" 来自古英语 frēo,  意思是 "free" (自由的). "man" 来自古英语 mann, 意思是 "man, person" (男人，人).  因此，freedman 字面意思是“被解放的人”。
