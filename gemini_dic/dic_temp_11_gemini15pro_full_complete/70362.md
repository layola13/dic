fiercest:/ˈfɪərsɪst/| adj.|最凶猛的，最激烈的|superlative adjective of fierce;  meaning most aggressive, violently intense, or strong.|The fiercest battles were fought in the north. 最激烈的战斗发生在北方。|The competition was fiercest in the final round. 决赛中的竞争最为激烈。|近义词：most aggressive, most intense, most violent, strongest|反义词：gentlest, mildest, calmest|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *fers, from Anglo-Norman fers, fierce, from Old French fers, fiers (“wild, cruel, proud”), from Latin ferus (“wild, untamed”).
