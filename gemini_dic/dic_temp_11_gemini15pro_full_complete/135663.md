nonpreferentialism:/ˌnɒnprɛfərˈɛnʃəlɪzəm/
noun
非优惠主义
The policy or practice of not granting preferential treatment to any particular group or country, especially in trade.
Many developing countries argue against nonpreferentialism, believing it hinders their economic growth. 许多发展中国家反对非优惠主义，认为它阻碍了它们的经济增长。
non-discrimination, impartiality
preferentialism, favoritism
