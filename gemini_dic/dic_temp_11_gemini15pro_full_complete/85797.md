hazard:/ˈhæzərd/ (US), /ˈhæzəd/ (UK)| n. vt.|危险，危害；冒险，尝试|A danger or risk.|例句：The icy roads were a hazard to drivers. 结冰的道路对司机来说是一种危险。|近义词：danger, risk, peril, menace|反义词:safety, security|noun
hazard:/ˈhæzərd/ (US), /ˈhæzəd/ (UK)| vt.|冒险，尝试；使遭受危险|To expose to danger or risk.|例句：He decided to hazard a guess. 他决定冒险猜测一下。|近义词：risk, venture, gamble|反义词:protect, safeguard|verb
