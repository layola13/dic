de-intensifying:/diːɪnˈtɛnsɪfaɪɪŋ/
adjective
降低强度的，缓和的
Reducing the intensity or strength of something; making something less extreme.
The diplomat worked on de-intensifying the conflict between the two nations.  外交官致力于缓和两国之间的冲突。

The new medication is de-intensifying the side effects of the treatment.  这种新药正在减轻治疗的副作用。

Synonyms: mitigating, moderating, lessening, alleviating, softening
Antonyms: intensifying, escalating, aggravating, exacerbating
词性: adjective
