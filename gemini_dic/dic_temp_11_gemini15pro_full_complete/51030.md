desman:/ˈdɛzmən/ /ˈdɛsmən/| n. | 麝鼠 | A small semiaquatic, insect-eating mammal of the family Talpidae, native to parts of Europe and Asia. It has a long, sensitive snout and webbed feet. |  The Pyrenean desman is an endangered species. 比利牛斯麝鼠是一个濒临灭绝的物种。 |  The Russian desman has a larger body size than the Pyrenean desman. 俄罗斯麝鼠的体型比比利牛斯麝鼠大。 | near synonyms: mole, shrew | antonyms: (No direct antonyms for an animal name.
