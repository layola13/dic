forewit:/ˈfɔːrˌwɪt/ | n. |远见，预见，先见之明| The ability to anticipate and prepare for the future; foresight. | He showed great forewit in investing his money wisely. 他在投资方面展现了非凡的远见卓识。| 近义词：foresight, prescience, prudence | 反义词：shortsightedness, imprudence | 词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *forewit*, from Old English *forewīt* ("foreknowledge, foresight, prudence"), from *fore- ("before") + *wīt* ("knowledge, understanding"). Cognate with Old Frisian *forewit* ("foreknowledge"), Dutch *voorwit* ("foresight"), Old High German *forawizzi* ("foreknowledge").
