disassembles:/ˌdɪsəˈsɛmbəlz/
|v.|拆卸，分解|To take something apart; to separate something into its component parts.|He disassembled the engine to find the problem. 他拆卸了引擎以找出问题所在。|The technician disassembled the computer to replace the hard drive. 技术员拆卸了电脑以更换硬盘。|synonyms: dismantle, take apart, break down, deconstruct|antonyms: assemble, construct, put together, build|verb

disassemble:/ˌdɪsəˈsɛmbəlz/
|v.|反汇编|To translate machine code into assembly language.|The programmer disassembled the program to understand its workings. 程序员反汇编程序来理解其工作原理。|She disassembled the malware to analyze its behavior. 她反汇编了恶意软件来分析其行为。|synonyms: reverse engineer, decode|antonyms: compile, assemble|verb
