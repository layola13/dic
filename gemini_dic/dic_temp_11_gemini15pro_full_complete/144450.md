outswindling:/ˌaʊtsˈwɪndlɪŋ/
verb
诈骗; 比...更会诈骗
The act of swindling or defrauding someone, especially by surpassing them in their own methods of deception.
He was accused of outswindling even the most experienced con men. 他被指控甚至比最有经验的骗子还要会骗人。
近义词: outsmarting, outmaneuvering, defrauding, cheating
反义词: being honest, being truthful
