brave: /breɪv/ | adj. | 勇敢的，无畏的 | Showing courage; not afraid. |  He was a brave soldier who fought for his country. 他是一位勇敢的战士，为国家而战。

She showed brave resistance against the bully. 她勇敢地反抗了恶霸。

近义词: courageous, valiant, fearless, bold, daring | 反义词: cowardly, timid, fearful

词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English *brav, from Old French *brave (“excellent, worthy”), from Italian *bravo (“wild, savage, brave”), from Latin *barbarus (“foreign, barbarian”).
