bilges:/ˈbɪldʒɪz/
noun
舱底积水，污水
the lowest internal part of the hull of a ship, where water collects.
The bilges were full of oily water. 船舱底部积满了油污水。
synonyms:bottom, sump
antonyms:top, peak
noun

bilge:/bɪldʒ/
noun
舱底
the lowest internal part of the hull of a ship.
The bilge pump failed, and the boat began to sink.  舱底泵坏了，船开始下沉。
synonyms:bottom, sump
antonyms:top, peak
noun

bilge:/bɪldʒ/
verb
（船）破裂；（船底）出现裂缝
(of a ship's hull) to be damaged and leak.
The ship bilged on the rocks. 船在岩石上撞出了裂缝。
synonyms:rupture, split
antonyms:repair, mend
verb
