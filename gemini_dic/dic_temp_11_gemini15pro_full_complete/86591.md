heliographer:/ˌhiːliˈɒɡrəfər/ |n.| 日光通信员，日光摄影师 |A person who operates a heliograph, a device used for sending signals using reflected sunlight. |例句：The heliographer signaled the approaching ships. 日光通信员向驶近的船只发出信号。

例句：During the war, heliographers played a crucial role in communication. 战争期间，日光通信员在通讯中起到了至关重要的作用。|近义词：signalman, signaler |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

Etymology:
From Greek *hēlios* "sun" + *-graphos* "writer"
