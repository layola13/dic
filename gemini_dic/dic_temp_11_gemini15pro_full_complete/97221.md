inflates:/ɪnˈfleɪts/
verb
使充气；使膨胀；抬高（价格）；鼓吹；使得意
To fill with air or gas so that it becomes larger; to increase the size or amount of something artificially; to increase a price, etc. beyond its real value.
The hot air balloon slowly inflates.  热气球慢慢膨胀起来。
He inflated his life jacket and jumped into the sea. 他给救生衣充气，然后跳入大海。
Synonyms: expand, swell, pump up, enlarge, exaggerate, boost
Antonyms: deflate, shrink, contract, reduce, diminish
词性: 动词
