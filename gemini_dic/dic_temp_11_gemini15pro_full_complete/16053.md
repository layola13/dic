bandicoot:/ˈbændɪkuːt/| n.|袋狸|A bandicoot is a small marsupial native to Australia and New Guinea, characterized by its pointed head, long ears, and strong claws.|例句：The bandicoot scurried through the undergrowth. 袋狸窜过灌木丛。|近义词：marsupial,  bilby|反义词: None (no direct opposite for a specific animal)|词性:noun

Etymology: From Telugu *pandikokku (పందికొక్కు), from pandi (పంది, “pig”) + kokku (కొక్కు, “fowl”).
