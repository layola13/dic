cossacks: /ˈkɒsæks/ (UK) /ˈkɑːsæks/ (US) | n. | 哥萨克人 |  A member of a people of Southern Russia and Ukraine, noted for their horsemanship and military skill.  Historically, they formed semi-independent communities, often serving as mercenaries or irregular troops. |  The Cossacks were famed for their fierce independence. 哥萨克人以其强烈的独立性而闻名。

Cossack cavalry played a crucial role in several wars. 哥萨克骑兵在几场战争中发挥了至关重要的作用。 | 近义词: horsemen, warriors | 反义词: civilians | 词性: 名词
