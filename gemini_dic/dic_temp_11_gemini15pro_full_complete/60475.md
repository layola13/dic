ejecta:/ɪˈdʒɛktə/
n.
喷出物；排出物；射出物
Matter thrown out from a volcano or crater.
例句：The ejecta from the volcano included ash, rock, and lava. 火山的喷出物包括火山灰、岩石和熔岩。

例句：The impact of the meteor scattered ejecta across a wide area.  陨石的撞击将喷出物散布到很广的区域。

近义词：debris, discharge, emission
反义词: intake


词性: 名词

词源:  From Latin ējectus, past participle of ēicere "to throw out," from ex- "out" + jacere "to throw."
