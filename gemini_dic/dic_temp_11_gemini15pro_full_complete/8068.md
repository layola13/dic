anthropomorphically:/ˌænθrəpəˈmɔːrfɪkli/ | adv. | 拟人地，人格化地 |  In a way that gives human characteristics to non-human things, such as animals or objects. | The cartoon anthropomorphically portrays a dog walking on two legs and talking.  这部动画片将一只狗拟人化地描绘成用两条腿走路和说话。| 近义词: humanly, personally | 反义词: inhumanly, impersonally | 词性: 副词

anthropomorphism: /ˌænθrəpəˈmɔːrfɪzəm/ | n. | 拟人化，人格化 | The attribution of human characteristics or behavior to a god, animal, or object. |  The story relies heavily on anthropomorphism to make the animals relatable.  这个故事非常依赖拟人化来使动物更具亲和力。| 近义词: personification | 反义词: objectification | 词性: 名词

词源: From the Greek ἄνθρωπος (ánthrōpos, “human being”) + μορφή (morphḗ, “form”).
