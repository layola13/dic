nonperishables:/ˌnɑːnˈpɛrɪʃəbəlz/
n.|不易腐烂的食品|Food that doesn't spoil easily, such as canned goods, dried pasta, and rice.|We stocked up on nonperishables before the hurricane. 我们在飓风来临前储备了不易腐烂的食品。|Synonyms: non-perishable goods, shelf-stable foods, staples|Antonyms: perishables, fresh foods
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

nonperishable:/ˌnɑːnˈpɛrɪʃəbəl/
adj.|不易腐烂的|Not subject to decay or spoilage.|Canned goods are nonperishable. 罐头食品不易腐烂。|Synonyms: long-lasting, durable, shelf-stable|Antonyms: perishable, decomposable
词性: 形容词
