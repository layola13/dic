orchestrated:/ˈɔːrkɪstreɪtɪd/
adjective
精心策划的，精心安排的
carefully arranged or planned, like the different parts of a musical performance.
The CEO orchestrated a complex merger between the two companies. 首席执行官精心策划了两家公司之间复杂的合并。
The surprise party was perfectly orchestrated.  这个惊喜派对安排得非常完美。
近义词:arranged, planned, organized, coordinated, stage-managed
反义词:improvised, unplanned, spontaneous
词性:adjective

orchestrate:/ˈɔːrkɪstreɪt/
verb
精心策划，精心安排
arrange or plan (something) carefully and in detail.
He orchestrated a complex series of events. 他精心策划了一系列复杂的事件。
The politician orchestrated a campaign to win the election.  这位政治家精心策划了一场竞选活动以赢得选举。
近义词:arrange, plan, organize, coordinate, stage-manage
反义词:improvise
词性:verb
