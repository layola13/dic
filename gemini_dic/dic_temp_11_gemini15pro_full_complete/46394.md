daemon:/ˈdiːmən/ /ˈdeɪmən/| n.|守护进程，精灵，后台程序|A daemon is a computer program that runs as a background process, rather than being under the direct control of an interactive user.|The email server runs as a daemon, constantly checking for new messages.|Synonyms: background process, service, spirit, guardian|Antonyms: foreground process|noun
