assessorship:/əˈsesərʃɪp/
n.
估价员的职位；估价员的资格
The position or qualification of an assessor.
He held the assessorship for five years. 他担任估价员五年了。
近义词：appraiser, valuer, evaluator
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From assessor + -ship.  "Assessor" comes from the Late Latin "assessor" meaning "one who sits beside," from "assessus," past participle of "assidere"  (to sit beside), from "ad" (to, at) + "sedere" (to sit).  The suffix "-ship" denotes a state, condition, or quality.
