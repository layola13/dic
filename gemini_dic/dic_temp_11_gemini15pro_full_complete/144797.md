over-focused:/ˈoʊvərˈfoʊkəst/ | adj. | 过于关注的，过度集中的 | Concentrating too much on one particular thing, often to the detriment of others. | She was so over-focused on the details that she missed the bigger picture. 她过于关注细节，反而忽略了全局。|  He became over-focused on his work and neglected his family. 他变得过度专注于工作，而忽略了家人。 |近义词: obsessive, fixated, preoccupied |反义词: unfocused, distracted, inattentive | 词性: 形容词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "focused" (past participle of "focus")
