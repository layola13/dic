inevitabilities:/ɪnˌevɪtəˈbɪlətiz/
n.
不可避免性；必然性；必然发生的事
The state or quality of being inevitable; something that is inevitable.
Climate change and its inevitabilities are a growing concern. 气候变化及其不可避免性日益受到关注。

The inevitabilities of aging are something we all must face.  衰老的必然性是我们所有人都必须面对的。

近义词:certainties, necessities
反义词:uncertainties, possibilities
词性:名词(复数)

词源:
From inevitable + -ity + -es.  Inevitability comes from the Late Middle English word "inevitabilite," borrowed from the Old French "inevitabilité," ultimately deriving from the Latin "inevitabilis" (unavoidable).
