misdiagnosed: /ˌmɪsdaɪəɡˈnoʊzd/ | adj. | 误诊的 | Incorrectly diagnosed; given the wrong diagnosis. |  The patient was initially misdiagnosed with the flu, but later tests revealed a more serious condition.  病人最初被误诊为流感，但后来的检查发现了一种更严重的疾病。|  Misdiagnosed as having a common cold, she was actually suffering from pneumonia. 她被误诊为普通感冒，实际上患有肺炎。|Synonyms: wrongly diagnosed, incorrectly diagnosed | Antonyms: correctly diagnosed, accurately diagnosed | adjective
