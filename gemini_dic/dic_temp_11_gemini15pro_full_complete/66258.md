exclusionism:/ɪksˈkluːʒənɪzəm/
n.
排外主义，排斥主义
The practice of excluding a person or group from a place, group, or privilege.
The country's exclusionism towards immigrants is a contentious issue.  这个国家对移民的排外主义是一个有争议的问题。
The club's exclusionism prevents many people from joining.  俱乐部的排外主义让很多人无法加入。
近义词：discrimination, segregation, intolerance, bigotry
反义词：inclusion, acceptance, tolerance
词性：名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin exclūsiōn- (stem of exclūsiō ), from Latin exclūdō (“I shut out, exclude”),  from ex- (“out”) + claudō (“I shut, close”).  + -ism.
