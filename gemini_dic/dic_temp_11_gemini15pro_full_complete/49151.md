dejection:/dɪˈdʒɛkʃən/| n.|沮丧，颓丧|A state of low spirits caused by loss or disappointment. |She felt a deep sense of dejection after failing the exam. 她考试不及格后感到非常沮丧。|近义词：despondency, gloom, melancholy, sadness|反义词:elation, joy, happiness|词性:名词

dejected:/dɪˈdʒɛktɪd/| adj.|沮丧的，灰心的|Sad and depressed; dispirited. |He looked dejected after his team lost the game.  他的球队输了比赛后，他看起来很沮丧。|近义词：downcast, disheartened, discouraged, sad|反义词:cheerful, happy, joyful|词性:形容词
