odiously:/ˈoʊdiəsli/ | adv. | 可憎地，讨厌地 | in a way that is extremely unpleasant or offensive |  The villain acted odiously, twirling his mustache and laughing maniacally.  这个反派的行为令人憎恶，他捻着胡子疯狂地大笑。| synonyms: hatefully, abominably, detestably, repulsively | antonyms: pleasantly, agreeably, delightfully, charmingly | adverb

odious:/ˈoʊdiəs/ | adj. | 可憎的，讨厌的 | extremely unpleasant; repulsive | The odious smell of burning rubber filled the air. 燃烧橡胶的难闻气味弥漫在空气中。 | synonyms: hateful, abominable, detestable, repulsive | antonyms: pleasant, agreeable, delightful, charming | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *odios*, from Latin *odiosus* (“hateful, offensive”), from *odium* (“hatred”).
