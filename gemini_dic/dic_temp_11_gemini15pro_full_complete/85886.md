headcount:/ˈhɛdˌkaʊnt/ | n. | 点名，清点人数 | the act of counting the number of people present |  They did a headcount to make sure everyone was on the bus. 他们清点人数以确保每个人都在车上。 | synonyms: roll call, tally, count | antonyms:  (No direct antonym) | noun
