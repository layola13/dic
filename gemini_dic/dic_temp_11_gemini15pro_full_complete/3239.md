affiche:/æˈfiːʃ/ /əˈfiːʃ/ | n. | 海报；招贴 | A large printed notice or advertisement, often illustrated, that is displayed in a public place. | Example 1: I saw an affiche for a concert in the park. 我在公园里看到一张音乐会的海报。

Example 2: The affiche advertised a new film.  这张海报宣传一部新电影。 | synonym: poster, placard, bill | antonym: N/A | noun
