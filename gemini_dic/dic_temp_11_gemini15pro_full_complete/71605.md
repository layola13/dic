flat-panel:/ˈflætˌpænəl/| adj. n.|平板的；平板显示器|A flat and thin display screen, as opposed to a traditional cathode ray tube (CRT) display.|例句：He bought a flat-panel TV for his living room. 他为他的客厅买了一台平板电视。

例句：Flat-panel displays are much lighter and thinner than CRTs.  平板显示器比CRT显示器轻薄得多。|近义词：flat-screen, thin-film|反义词:CRT (cathode ray tube)|词性:adjective, noun


flat-panel:/ˈflætˌpænəl/| n.|平板显示器|A flat and thin display screen used in televisions, computer monitors, and other electronic devices.|例句：The new flat-panel is energy efficient.  新的平板显示器很节能。

例句：She prefers a flat-panel monitor for her graphic design work. 她喜欢用平板显示器进行图形设计工作。|近义词：flat-screen display, LCD, LED|反义词:CRT monitor|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "flat" + "panel". "Flat" meaning having a level surface, and "panel" referring to a distinct section or division, typically rectangular.
