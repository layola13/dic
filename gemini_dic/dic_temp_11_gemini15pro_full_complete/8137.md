anti-capitalist:/ˌæntiˈkæpɪtəlɪst/
| adj. n.|反资本主义的；反资本主义者|Opposed to capitalism.  Characterized by opposition to or hostility toward capitalism.|
The anti-capitalist protesters marched through the city. 反资本主义抗议者在城市中游行。
She wrote an anti-capitalist manifesto. 她写了一份反资本主义宣言。
|近义词：socialist, communist, Marxist, radical, left-wing|
|反义词：capitalist, pro-capitalist, conservative, right-wing|
|词性: adjective, noun|


anti-capitalist:/ˌæntiˈkæpɪtəlɪst/
| n.|反资本主义者|A person who opposes capitalism.|
He's a well-known anti-capitalist. 他是一位著名的反资本主义者。
