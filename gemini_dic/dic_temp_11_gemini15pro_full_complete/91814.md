hyperflexions:/ˌhaɪpərˈflɛkʃənz/ | n. |过度屈曲，过度屈伸|Excessive bending or flexing of a joint, often beyond its normal range of motion.  It can be a symptom of certain neurological conditions.|The patient exhibited hyperflexions of the knee joint.|近义词:hyperextension (sometimes, depending on context), overflexion|反义词:rigidity, stiffness|词性:noun
