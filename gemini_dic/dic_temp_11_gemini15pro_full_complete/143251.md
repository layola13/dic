outdesigns:/ˌaʊt dɪˈzaɪnz/
verb
胜过…的设计；比…设计得更好
surpasses in design; designs better than
Her new dress completely outdesigns anything I've ever made. 她的新裙子比我做过的任何裙子设计得都好。
近义词: outmatches, surpasses, betters
反义词:  is outdesigned by, is surpassed by
词性: verb
