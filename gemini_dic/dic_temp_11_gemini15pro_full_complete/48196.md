decompression:/ˌdiːkəmˈpreʃn/
n.|减压，解压|The act or process of reducing or relieving pressure.|Divers must undergo decompression after deep dives. 潜水员深潜后必须进行减压。|近义词:relaxation, release|反义词:compression, pressurization|词性:名词
decompress:/ˌdiːkəmˈpres/
v.|减压，解压|To reduce or relieve pressure; to expand or cause to expand after compression.|The pilot decompressed the cabin before landing. 飞行员在着陆前降低了机舱压力。|近义词:relax, unwind|反义词:compress, pressurize|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "removal, reversal") + compression (from Latin compressio, meaning "a pressing together").
