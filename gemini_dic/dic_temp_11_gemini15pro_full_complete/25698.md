bunkhouses:/ˈbʌŋkhaʊzɪz/
n.
临时宿舍，集体宿舍
A simple building providing sleeping quarters for a number of people, typically workers.
The bunkhouses were spartan, with only basic beds and a communal washroom. 这些临时宿舍很简陋，只有基本的床位和一个公共洗手间。

The migrant workers lived in bunkhouses provided by the farm.  这些移民工人住在农场提供的集体宿舍里。

dormitory, barracks, hostel
quarters, apartments, homes
