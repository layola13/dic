equipensate: /ɪˈkwɪpənˌseɪt/
verb
补偿；赔偿
to give someone something, usually money, for something that they have lost or suffered
例句：The company will equipensate employees for any losses they incurred. 公司将补偿员工所遭受的任何损失。

例句：The government promised to equipensate farmers for the damage caused by the floods. 政府承诺赔偿农民因洪水造成的损失。

近义词：compensate, recompense, indemnify
反义词：penalize, fine
词性：v.
