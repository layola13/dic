anientise: /əˈnaɪənˌtaɪz/
verb
消灭，歼灭
To reduce to nothing; to annihilate.
The dictator sought to anientise all opposition.  独裁者试图消灭所有反对派。
The new weapon could anientise entire cities.  这种新武器可以摧毁整个城市。

近义词: annihilate, obliterate, destroy, eradicate, extinguish
反义词: create, build, establish, foster

词性: verb (动词)

词源: From French *anéantir*, from *a-* (to) + *néant* (nothing). Related to *nihil*.
