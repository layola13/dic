nonpolarity:/ˌnɑːnpəˈlærəti/ | n. | 非极性 | The property of a molecule having no dipole moment because of the symmetrical distribution of its electrical charge. | Oil and water don't mix because of the nonpolarity of oil molecules. 油和水不相溶，是因为油分子的非极性。| synonyms: hydrophobicity, lipophilicity | antonyms: polarity, hydrophilicity | noun

nonpolar:/ˌnɑːnˈpoʊlər/ | adj. | 非极性的 | Not having a dipole moment; lacking polarity. | Nonpolar molecules are typically symmetrical. 非极性分子通常是对称的。| synonyms: hydrophobic, lipophilic | antonyms: polar, hydrophilic | adjective
