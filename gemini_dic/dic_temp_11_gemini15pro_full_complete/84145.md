half-numb:/ˌhɑːf ˈnʌm/ | adj. | 半麻木的；感觉迟钝的 | Partly numb; having reduced sensation. |  My fingers were half-numb from the cold. 我的手指冻得半麻木了。

The anesthetic made his jaw half-numb. 麻醉剂使他的下巴半麻木了。 | 近义词：partially numb, semi-numb | 反义词：sensitive, responsive | 词性: 形容词
