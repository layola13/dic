conservable:/kənˈsɜːrvəbl/| adj.|可保存的，可保护的|Capable of being conserved or preserved; able to be maintained or kept in existence without loss or decay.|The historical documents are conservable with proper storage techniques.  这些历史文献通过适当的储存技术可以保存下来。|The archaeologist believes the ancient pottery is conservable.  考古学家认为这些古代陶器可以保存下来。|近义词：preservable, maintainable, protectable|反义词：perishable, unsustainable, destructible|词性:形容词
词源: From "conserve" + "-able". "Conserve" comes from Latin "conservare," from "con-" (together) + "servare" (to keep, guard).
