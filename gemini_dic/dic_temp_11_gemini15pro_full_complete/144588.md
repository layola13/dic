outwaved:/ˌaʊtˈweɪvd/
verb
超过，胜过（在挥手致意方面）
surpassed someone in waving; waved more vigorously or for a longer period than someone else.
The enthusiastic fans outwaved the departing celebrities.  热情的粉丝们向离去的名人挥手致意，胜过了他们。
The children outwaved their parents as the train pulled away. 火车开动时，孩子们向他们的父母挥手告别，挥手的力度超过了父母。
近义词:outdo, surpass, excel
反义词: be outdone, lose to
