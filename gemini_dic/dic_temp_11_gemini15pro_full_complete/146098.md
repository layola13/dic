overegg:/ˌoʊvərˈeɡ/
verb
过分渲染；过分装饰；加太多鸡蛋(于食物中)
to spoil something by elaborating it too much; to add too many eggs to something
The movie was overegged with special effects. 这部电影的特效太过了。
He overegged the custard and it curdled. 他在蛋奶冻里加了太多鸡蛋，结果凝结了。
overdo, exaggerate, embellish
understate, downplay
v.
