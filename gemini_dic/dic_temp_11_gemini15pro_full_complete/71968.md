flimflammer:/ˈflɪmˌflæmər/| n.|骗子，江湖郎中|A person who deceives others, especially by telling lies or using tricks.|He's a flimflammer, don't trust a word he says. 他是个骗子，别相信他说的任何一句话。|Synonyms: con artist, swindler, cheat, fraudster, trickster|Antonyms: honest person, truthful person|noun
Etymology: From the early 19th century, of uncertain origin, perhaps imitative.
