The histogeny of this particular plant species is complex and not fully understood.  这种特殊植物的组织发生很复杂，尚未完全被理解。 | 近义词:  development, morphogenesis | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider "degeneration" or "atrophy" in certain contexts) | 词性: noun
