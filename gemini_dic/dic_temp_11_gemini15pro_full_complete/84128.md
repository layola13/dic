half-masticated:/ˌhæf ˈmæstɪkeɪtɪd/
adjective
半咀嚼的，未完全咀嚼的
partly chewed; not fully masticated.
The dog spit out a half-masticated piece of meat. 狗吐出了一块未完全咀嚼的肉。
The baby's half-masticated food was all over her face. 婴儿的半咀嚼食物弄得她满脸都是。
Near Synonyms: partly chewed, incompletely chewed, undigested
Antonyms: fully chewed, masticated, digested
词性: 形容词
