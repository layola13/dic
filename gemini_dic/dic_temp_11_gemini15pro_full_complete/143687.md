outlaying:/ˌaʊtˈleɪɪŋ/
adjective
外围的，边远的
Situated on the edge or periphery of something.
The outlying areas of the city are less developed.  城市的边远地区不太发达。
The outlying islands were sparsely populated. 外围岛屿人口稀少。

近义词:peripheral, remote, distant, outer
反义词:central, inner, interior
词性:adjective


outlaying:/ˈaʊtleɪɪŋ/
noun
支出，花费 (通常指大量的)
Expenditure, especially a large one.
The company's outlaying on research and development has increased significantly. 公司在研发方面的支出大幅增加。
The initial outlaying for the project was estimated at $10 million.  该项目的初期支出估计为1000万美元。

近义词:expenditure, spending, outlay
反义词:income, revenue
词性:noun


outlaying:/ˈaʊtleɪɪŋ/
verb (present participle of outlay)
支出，花费
Spending (money).
The government is outlaying substantial funds on infrastructure projects. 政府正在基础设施项目上投入大量资金。
They are outlaying a fortune on their new home. 他们正在为他们的新家花费一大笔钱。

近义词:spending, expending, disbursing
反义词:saving, hoarding
词性:verb


词源: From the verb "outlay," which combines "out" (meaning "outside" or "beyond") and "lay" (meaning "to put or place").  The original meaning was "to expend," and the adjectival form developed to describe something situated outside or beyond a central area.
