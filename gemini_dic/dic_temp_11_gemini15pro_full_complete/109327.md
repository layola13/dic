lengthiest:/ˈlɛŋθiɪst/| adj.|最长的，持续时间最久的|Having the greatest length in space or time.  It is the superlative form of "lengthy".|The lengthiest speech I've ever heard lasted for over three hours. 我听过的最长的演讲持续了三个多小时。

The Amazon River is the lengthiest river in South America. 亚马逊河是南美洲最长的河流。|近义词：longest, most protracted, most prolonged|反义词：shortest, briefest, quickest|词性:形容词
词源: From length + -y + -est.  Ultimately from the Proto-Germanic *langaz.
