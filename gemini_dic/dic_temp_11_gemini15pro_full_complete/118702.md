metacinnabar:/ˌmɛtəˈsɪnəˌbɑːr/
noun
黑辰砂，β-硫化汞
A black, tetragonal crystalline form of mercury sulfide, HgS, found as a mineral.
Metacinnabar is a less common form of mercury sulfide than cinnabar. 黑辰砂是一种比辰砂少见的硫化汞形式。
Metacinnabar is often found in association with other mercury minerals. 黑辰砂经常与其他汞矿物伴生。
Synonym:  black cinnabar,  HgS (chemical formula)
Antonym:  N/A (lacks a direct opposite mineral)
