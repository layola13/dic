antitheses:/ænˈtɪθəsiːz/
n.
对照，对立； antithesis的复数
The poem explores the antithesis between good and evil. 这首诗探讨了善与恶的对立。
His actions are the antithesis of what he says he believes. 他的行为与他所说的信仰截然相反。
Synonyms: contrast, opposition, contradiction
Antonyms: similarity, resemblance, agreement
noun
