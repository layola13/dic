graciousness:/ˈɡreɪʃəsnəs/| n. |仁慈，和蔼，优雅|The quality of being kind, polite, and generous, especially to someone of lower social status.  A pleasant or attractive quality or feature.|She accepted his apology with graciousness. 她优雅地接受了他的道歉。|Synonyms: courtesy, kindness, generosity, politeness, charm, elegance|Antonyms: rudeness, unkindness, meanness, impoliteness, awkwardness|noun
