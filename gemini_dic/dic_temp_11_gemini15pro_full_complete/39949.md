constructive:/kənˈstrʌktɪv/
adjective
建设性的；有益的
Serving a useful purpose; tending to build up.
The meeting was very constructive, and we made a lot of progress.  这次会议非常有建设性，我们取得了很大进展。

The criticism he offered was constructive and helped me improve my work. 他提出的批评很有建设性，帮助我改进了我的工作。

helpful, useful, productive, positive
destructive, negative, harmful, unproductive
词性: 形容词
