PHRASE->
There are not many common set phrases with "outbelching". It's more of a descriptive verb used in specific contexts.  However, some related phrases could be:

*  a violent outbelching of lava  (猛烈的熔岩喷发)
*  the constant outbelching of smoke (持续不断的烟雾喷出)
*  an outbelching of fumes  (烟雾的喷出)
* a sudden outbelching of gas (气体的突然喷出)
* the outbelching of noxious gases (有害气体的喷出)



词源： out- (prefix meaning "outward") + belch (verb meaning "to expel gas noisily from the stomach through the mouth")
