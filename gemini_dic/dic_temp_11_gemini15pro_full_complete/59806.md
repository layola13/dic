ecrus:/ˈiːkruːz/ | n. |  淡褐色，略带灰黄的白色；淡褐色的布料| A light grayish yellow-brown color; a cloth of this color.  |The curtains were made of a heavy ecrus linen. 窗帘是用厚重的淡褐色亚麻布做的。 | 近义词: beige, cream, off-white | 反义词: black, dark brown | 词性: 名词

词源: From French *écru*, past participle of *écrure*, meaning 'unbleached'.  Ultimately from Dutch *kru*, 'curly, wrinkled'.
