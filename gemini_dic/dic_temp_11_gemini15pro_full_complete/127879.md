neoconservatives:/ˌniːoʊkənˈsɜːrvətɪvz/
| n. |新保守主义者|A group of people, especially in the US, who advocate a return to traditional moral values, who are strongly anti-communist, and who favour the use of military intervention in international affairs.|The neoconservatives pushed for the invasion of Iraq. 新保守主义者推动了入侵伊拉克。|近义词：hawks, right-wingers|反义词：liberals, doves|Noun
