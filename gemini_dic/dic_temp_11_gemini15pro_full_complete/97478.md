ingenious:/ɪnˈdʒiːniəs/| adj.|有独创性的；巧妙的；心灵手巧的| (of a person) clever, original, and inventive.|(of a machine or idea) cleverly and originally devised and well suited to its purpose.|例句：He was ingenious enough to overcome the limited resources. 他足智多谋，能够克服资源有限的困难。

例句：It was an ingenious solution to a seemingly impossible problem. 这是一个巧妙地解决了一个看似不可能的问题的方案。|近义词：clever, inventive, innovative, original, resourceful, bright, brilliant, creative, inspired, talented|反义词：unimaginative, uninspired, unoriginal, dull, stupid|词性:形容词

词源:
Late Middle English: from late Latin ingeniosus ‘gifted with genius’, from Latin ingenium ‘natural capacity’.
