degenerous:/dɪˈdʒɛnərəs/
adjective
堕落的，退化的，变质的
Showing a decline in quality or standards, especially morally or socially; having low moral standards.
例句：He was accused of degenerous behavior. 他被指控行为堕落。
例句：The film portrayed a degenerous society obsessed with wealth and power.  这部电影描绘了一个沉迷于财富和权力的堕落社会。

近义词：corrupt, depraved, immoral, decadent, degraded, base, vile
反义词：noble, virtuous, upright, moral, righteous
词性：adjective

词源：Late Middle English: from late Latin degeneratus ‘fallen from its kind’, past participle of degenerare, from de- ‘down, away’ + genus, gener- ‘stock, kind’.
