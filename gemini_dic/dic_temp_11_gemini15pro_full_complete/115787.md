marionettist:/ˌmærɪəˈnɛtɪst/ | n. |木偶操纵者；傀儡师|A person who operates marionettes.  A puppeteer, especially one who controls marionettes by strings attached to a control. |The marionettist skillfully manipulated the strings, bringing the wooden figures to life. 木偶操纵者熟练地操纵着绳子，使木偶栩栩如生。| 近义词: puppeteer | 反义词: audience, spectator | 词性: noun
