fibsters:/ˈfɪbstərz/ | n. | 谎言者; 编造故事的人 |  A person who tells fibs, or small lies.  Derived from "fib," meaning a small, often trivial lie. |  He's known as a bit of a fibster among his friends, always embellishing his stories. 他在他的朋友中被认为是一个爱说小谎的人，总是喜欢添油加醋。| 近义词: liar, prevaricator, storyteller | 反义词: truth-teller, honest person | 词性: 名词
