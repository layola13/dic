nontransferences:/ˌnɒntrænsˈfɜːrənsɪz/
noun
非转移;非转让;非转账
The absence of transference, especially in a psychoanalytic context, where feelings, desires, and expectations from past relationships are not projected onto the therapist.
Psychotherapy explored the patient's nontransferences and current emotional state.  这心理治疗探讨了病人的非移情状态和当前的情绪状态。
The lawyer ensured the nontransference of the property until all legal matters were settled.  律师确保在所有法律事宜解决之前，该房产不会被转让。

近义词: non-projection, neutrality
反义词: transference, projection
词性: noun (plural)

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "transference" (from Latin "transferre" - "to carry across").
