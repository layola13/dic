lithotomist:/lɪˈθɒtəmɪst/ | n. | 碎石医生；切石术医生 | A surgeon who performs lithotomy (the surgical removal of stones, especially from the urinary tract). |  The lithotomist skillfully removed the bladder stone. 碎石医生熟练地取出了膀胱结石。 |  surgeon, urologist | (No common antonym) | noun

lithotomy:/lɪˈθɒtəmi/ | n. | 切石术；膀胱切开术 |  The surgical removal of a calculus (stone), especially from the bladder, kidney, or urinary tract. | In the past, lithotomy was a painful and risky procedure. 过去，切石术是一项痛苦且危险的手术。 | surgery, operation | (No common antonym) | noun
