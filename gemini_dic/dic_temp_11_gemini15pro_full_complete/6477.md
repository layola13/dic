amputate: /ˈæmpjəˌteɪt/ | v. | 截肢；切断 | To surgically remove a limb or other body part. | The doctors had to amputate his arm to save his life. 医生不得不截肢他的手臂以挽救他的生命。| Synonyms: sever, excise, remove | Antonyms: attach, connect, join | verb
