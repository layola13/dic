combiner:/kəmˈbaɪnər/ (US), /kəmˈbaɪnə(r)/ (UK)| n.|联合收割机; 联合机; 结合者; 连接器; 结合体|A machine that harvests grain crops, such as wheat, corn, oats, rye, barley, etc. It typically performs reaping, threshing, and winnowing in a single process.|The farmer used a new combiner to harvest his wheat crop.|近义词：harvester, reaper-thresher, reaper-binder|反义词:separator, divider|词性:noun
