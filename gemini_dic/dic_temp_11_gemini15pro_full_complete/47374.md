deactuate:/diːˈæk.tʃu.eɪt/
verb
使失效，解除，停用
To make something, especially a mechanism or device, inactive or ineffective.
The robot's arm was deactuated after the assembly process.  机器人在组装过程后，其手臂被停用了。
The safety system will deactuate the engine in case of a collision. 如果发生碰撞，安全系统将使发动机失效。
synonyms: deactivate, disable, neutralize
antonyms: activate, enable
词性: 动词
