nonprecipitative:/ˌnɒnprɪˈsɪpɪteɪtɪv/
adjective
非降水的
Not involving or characterized by precipitation (rain, snow, sleet, or hail).
The fog was dense and nonprecipitative. 这雾很浓，没有降水。
近义词:dry, rainless
反义词:precipitative, rainy
词性:adjective

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + precipitative (from precipitate, meaning "to cause to separate from a solution or suspension")
