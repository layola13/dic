ccing:/ˈsiːsiːɪŋ/ |v. |抄送，将...列入邮件抄送名单|The act of including someone in an email's CC field.  It keeps them informed, even if they are not the primary recipient.|I'm ccing the manager on this email so they're aware of the situation. 我正在将此电子邮件抄送给经理，以便他们了解情况。| synonyms: copying, including, informing | antonyms: excluding, omitting | verb
