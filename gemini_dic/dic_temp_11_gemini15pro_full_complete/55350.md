distagram: /ˈdɪstəɡræm/ | n. | 直方图，距离图 | A graphical representation of the distribution of a set of data. It is similar to a histogram, but the main difference is that a distogram uses the actual data values for the x-axis, while a histogram uses ranges of values (bins). It can also refer to a graph showing the distances between objects. | Example 1: The distogram showed that most of the students scored between 70 and 80 on the test. 直方图显示大多数学生在考试中得分在 70 到 80 分之间。

Example 2: The researchers used a distogram to visualize the distances between different cities. 研究人员使用距离图来可视化不同城市之间的距离。 | Near Synonyms: histogram, frequency distribution, bar chart | Antonyms: N/A | Word Type: Noun
