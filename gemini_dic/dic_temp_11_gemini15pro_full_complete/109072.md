legatary:/ˈlɛɡətɛri/ | n. | 受遗赠人；遗产继承人 | A person to whom a legacy is bequeathed. | Example:  She was named as the legatary in her uncle's will. 她被指定为她叔叔遗嘱中的遗产继承人。

Example: The legatary received a substantial sum of money. 这位遗产继承人得到了一大笔钱。| 近义词: heir, inheritor, beneficiary | 反义词: testator, bequeather | 词性: noun
