mesosperm:/ˈmɛzəʊspɜːm/ | n. | 中胚层 | The middle layer of the three primary germ layers of an embryo, from which arises the connective tissues, muscles, vascular system, peritoneum, and excretory and reproductive organs. | The mesosperm gives rise to the circulatory system in vertebrates.  中胚层在脊椎动物中形成循环系统。|  The scientist studied the development of the mesosperm in the chick embryo. 科学家研究了鸡胚胎中中胚层的发育。| mesoblast | ectoderm, endoderm | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek μέσος (mésos, “middle”) + σπέρμα (spérma, “seed”).
