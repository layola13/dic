funkyer:/ˈfʌŋkiər/ | adj. | 更时髦的，更别致的 | More funky; having a stronger funky quality.  This describes something that has more of the characteristics associated with funk music, often implying a strong rhythm, groove, and soulful quality. It can also be used more generally to describe something that is unconventional, stylish, and cool. |  Her new apartment is even funkier than her old one. 她的新公寓比旧公寓更时髦。 |  Synonyms: groovier, hipper, cooler, trendier, more stylish, more chic | Antonyms: blander, duller, more conventional, more traditional, less stylish | adjective
