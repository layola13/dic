disconnect:/ˌdɪskəˈnekt/ | n. | 断开，分离，不连接|A lack of connection or communication. |There's a disconnect between what he says and what he does. 他的言行不一。|近义词：separation, gap, discrepancy|反义词：connection, link, unity|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation, reversal) + connect (from Latin connectere, from com- "together" + nectere "to bind").
