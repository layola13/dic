okra: /ˈoʊkrə/ (US), /ˈɒkrə/ (UK)| n.|秋葵|A tall annual plant (Abelmoschus esculentus) of the mallow family, native to the Old World tropics, cultivated for its edible green pods.|
例句：I love fried okra. 我喜欢炸秋葵。

例句：Okra is a popular ingredient in Southern cooking. 秋葵是南方烹饪中很受欢迎的食材。

近义词：gumbo, lady's finger|
