hard-working:/ˌhɑːrd ˈwɜːrkɪŋ/| adj.|勤奋的，努力工作的|Characterized by diligence and perseverance in work or other endeavors.  It describes someone who puts in considerable effort and dedication to their tasks.|She is a hard-working student and always gets good grades. 她是一个勤奋的学生，总是取得好成绩。|近义词：diligent, industrious, assiduous|反义词：lazy, indolent, slothful|词性:形容词
词源:  Compound of "hard" (meaning strenuous or requiring effort) and "working" (the present participle of "work").  "Hard" ultimately derives from Proto-Germanic *harduz, while "work" comes from Proto-Germanic *werkan.
