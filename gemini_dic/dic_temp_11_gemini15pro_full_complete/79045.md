give-way-to:/ɡɪv ˈweɪ tuː/| phrasal verb |让位于；被…取代；转变为| to be replaced by something; to change into something |The anger gave way to a dull ache. 愤怒变成了隐隐作痛。|近义词：succumb to, yield to, give in to, be replaced by|反义词: resist, withstand|词性:短语动词

give-way-to:/ɡɪv ˈweɪ tuː/| phrasal verb |让路| to allow other vehicles or pedestrians to go first, especially at a junction |You must give way to traffic coming from the right. 你必须让路给从右边来的车辆。|近义词：yield to, make way for|反义词: take precedence over|词性:短语动词
