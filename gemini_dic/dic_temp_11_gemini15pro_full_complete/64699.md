escapological:/ˌeskəpəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
逃避主义的;逃避现实的
relating to or providing a means of escape, especially from routine or boredom.
例句：
He enjoys reading escapological fiction to take his mind off his troubles.
他喜欢读逃避现实的小说来忘却烦恼。

例句：
The film provides a welcome dose of escapological entertainment.
这部电影提供了一剂受欢迎的逃避现实的娱乐。

近义词： escapist, diversionary, escapism
反义词： realistic, practical, pragmatic
词性: adjective
