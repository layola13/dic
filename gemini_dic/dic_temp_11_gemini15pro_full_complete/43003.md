cowardice:/ˈkaʊərdɪs/| n.|懦弱，胆怯|Lack of courage to face danger, difficulty, opposition, pain, etc.|His cowardice prevented him from speaking up. 他的懦弱使他不敢开口说话。|近义词:fearfulness, timidity, pusillanimity|反义词:bravery, courage, valor|词性:名词

Etymology: From Old French *coardise, from coart (Modern French couard), from coue (Modern French queue) "tail," from Latin cauda "tail."  The image is of an animal with its tail between its legs, running away in fear.
