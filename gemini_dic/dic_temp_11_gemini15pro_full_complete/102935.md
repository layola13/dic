jazel: /ˈdʒæzəl/
noun
吟唱；一种抒情诗体
A jazel is a short lyrical poem, usually expressing love or praise.
She wrote a beautiful jazel for her beloved. 她为她的爱人写了一首美丽的抒情诗。
synonyms: lyric, poem, verse, ode, song
antonyms: prose, story, novel
