outsuck:/ˌaʊtˈsʌk/ | v. | 比...更糟糕，胜过(通常指在负面方面) | To be worse than, to surpass in a negative way.  This word is often used informally and can be considered slang. |  He thought his day was bad, but his friend's situation outsucked his by a mile. 他觉得他的一天很糟糕，但他的朋友的处境比他糟糕得多。|  Their new phone outsucks the previous model with its terrible battery life. 他们的新手机电池续航能力很差，比之前的型号更糟糕。 | 近义词: outdo (in a negative way), surpass (negatively), be worse than | 反义词: outperform, excel, be better than | 词性:动词

词源:  "Out-" (prefix meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "suck" (verb meaning to be bad or unpleasant).  It's a relatively recent colloquial formation.
