colourer:/ˈkʌlərə(r)/| n.|着色师，涂色的人|A person who colours something, especially an artist or someone who works in a printing or dyeing industry. |例句：The colourer carefully blended the shades of blue. 着色师小心地混合了各种蓝色调。

例句：My daughter is a budding colourer, always filling her sketchbook with vibrant drawings. 我女儿是一位崭露头角的着色师，总是用充满活力的图画填满她的速写本。


近义词：artist, painter, decorator, dyer|反义词:|词性:noun

colourer:/ˈkʌlərər/| n.|着色器|A substance used to add colour to something. This meaning is less common than the person who colours. |例句：This food colourer is made from natural ingredients.  这种食用色素是由天然成分制成的。

例句：They used a special colourer to enhance the vibrancy of the fabric. 他们使用了一种特殊的着色剂来增强织物的鲜艳度。

近义词：dye, pigment, tint, colorant|反义词:|词性:noun


词源: From the verb "colour" (meaning to add colour to something), with the suffix "-er" (denoting a person who performs an action). Ultimately derived from the Old French word "color" and the Latin word "color".
