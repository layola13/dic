faithfulness:/ˈfeɪθfəlnəs/ | n. | 精确性，准确性 | Accuracy in details; exact correspondence with the original. | The faithfulness of the translation to the original text was impressive.  译文对原文的准确性令人印象深刻。| accuracy, precision, exactness | inaccuracy, imprecision, distortion | noun
