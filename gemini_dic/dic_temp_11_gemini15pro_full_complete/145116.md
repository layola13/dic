overarch:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːrtʃ/| v. n.| 覆盖；成为…的拱顶；构成…的总体框架；| To span or extend over like an arch; to dominate or embrace inclusively.|  A large bridge overarches the river.  一座大桥横跨这条河。| 近义词：span, encompass, cover, dominate, embrace | 反义词: underlie, be subordinate to | 词性: 动词, 名词

overarch:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːrtʃɪŋ/| adj.| 主要的；支配一切的；包罗万象的；| Dominating; overarching principles 支配性原则| The overarching theme of the conference was sustainability. 这次会议的首要主题是可持续性。| 近义词：dominant, principal, overall, comprehensive | 反义词: subordinate, secondary, minor | 词性: 形容词
