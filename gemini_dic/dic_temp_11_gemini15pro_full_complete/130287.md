non-survival:/ˌnɒn səˈvaɪvəl/ | n. | 非生存，未能生存，无法生存 | The non-survival of the seedlings was due to lack of water.  幼苗的非生存是由于缺水造成的。|  The museum highlighted the non-survival of many traditional crafts.  博物馆着重强调了许多传统工艺的失传。 | 近义词: demise, extinction, failure to survive | 反义词: survival, persistence | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix) from Latin *non "not"
survival (noun) from Old French survivre "to outlive," from sur- "over" + vivre "to live," from Latin vivere
