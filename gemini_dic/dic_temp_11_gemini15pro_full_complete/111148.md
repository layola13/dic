lithely:/ˈlaɪðli/| adv.|轻盈地，灵活地|Moving and bending with ease and grace; nimbly.|The dancer moved lithely across the stage. 舞蹈演员轻盈地穿过舞台。|近义词：gracefully, nimbly, agilely, elegantly|反义词：clumsily, awkwardly, stiffly|词性:副词
Etymology: From lithe + -ly. Lithe comes from Old English *liðe "gentle, mild, meek," from Proto-Germanic *linþjaz (source also of Old Saxon lithi "gentle, pleasant," Old High German lindi "lenient, soft," German lind "mild, soft"), from PIE root *lento- "flexible."
