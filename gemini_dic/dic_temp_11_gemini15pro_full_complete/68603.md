fantasizers:/ˈfæntəsaɪzərz/
n.
幻想家；空想家；沉溺于幻想的人
People who indulge in fantasies; daydreamers.
例句：Fantasizers often create elaborate worlds in their minds. 幻想家经常在脑海中创造精致的世界。

例句：He was known as one of the great fantasizers of his time. 他以他那个时代伟大的幻想家之一而闻名。

近义词：dreamers, visionaries, idealists
反义词：realists, pragmatists
词性：名词(plural)


fantasize:/ˈfæntəsaɪz/
v.
幻想；想像；做白日梦
To indulge in fantasies or daydreams.
例句：She often fantasized about winning the lottery. 她经常幻想中彩票。

例句：He fantasized about traveling the world. 他幻想着环游世界。

近义词：daydream, imagine, dream
反义词：face reality
词性：动词
