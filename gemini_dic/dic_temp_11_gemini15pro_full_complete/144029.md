outreproducing:/ˌaʊtriːprəˈduːsɪŋ/
verb
过度繁殖，生育过多
Reproducing at a rate that exceeds the carrying capacity of the environment.
The rabbits are outreproducing their predators, leading to an imbalance in the ecosystem. 兔子繁殖速度超过了捕食者的承受能力，导致生态系统失衡。
