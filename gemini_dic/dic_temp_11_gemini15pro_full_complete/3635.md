agent:/ˈeɪdʒənt/| n.|代理人，代理商；特工；药剂|A person who acts on behalf of another person or group.|The travel agent booked our flights. 旅行社代理预定了我们的航班。|synonyms: representative, deputy, broker, operative|antonyms: principal, client|noun

agent:/ˈeɪdʒənt/| adj.|代理的；有效的|Acting or having the power to act.|The cleaning agent removed the stain.  这种清洁剂去除了污渍。|synonyms: active, effective, operative|antonyms: inactive, ineffective|adjective
