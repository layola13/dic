half-liberally:/ˌhæf ˈlɪbərəli/ | adv. | 半自由地，不完全自由地 |  Done in a way that is only partly liberal or generous; not completely free or open-minded.  This word is less common and usually implies a degree of reluctance or restriction in the liberality. It suggests a hesitant or partial embrace of liberal principles or practices. | He gave half-liberally to the charity, donating a smaller amount than he could afford. 他对慈善机构的捐赠并不慷慨，捐赠的金额比他所能承受的要少。

He interpreted the rules half-liberally, allowing some exceptions but not others. 他对规则的解释并不完全自由，允许一些例外情况，但不允许其他例外情况。 | 近义词: partially liberally, somewhat liberally, not quite liberally | 反义词: liberally, generously, freely, completely liberally | 词性: 副词
