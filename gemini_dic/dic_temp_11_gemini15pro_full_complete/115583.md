marathon: /ˈmærəθɒn/ (UK) /ˈmærəˌθɑːn/ (US) | n. | 马拉松,  耐力比赛,  持久考验 | A long-distance running race of 26 miles 385 yards (42.195 kilometres). Also used figuratively to describe any long and arduous undertaking. |  She ran her first marathon last year. 她去年跑了她的第一个马拉松。| synonyms: endurance race, long haul | antonyms: sprint, dash | noun


marathon: /ˈmærəθɒn/ (UK) /ˈmærəˌθɑːn/ (US) | adj. | 马拉松式的, 持久性的 | Lasting a long time; requiring great endurance. | They had a marathon meeting that lasted for eight hours. 他们开了长达八个小时的马拉松式会议。| synonyms: prolonged, extended | antonyms: short, brief | adjective
