encroaches:/ɪnˈkroʊtʃɪz/ | v. |侵入，侵占，蚕食|intrudes gradually upon the rights or possessions of another; advance beyond proper limits.|例句：The city encroaches further and further into the surrounding countryside. 城市越来越侵占周围的乡村。

例句：The factory is encroaching on valuable farmland. 工厂正在侵占宝贵的农田。|近义词：intrude, infringe, impinge, trespass|反义词：retreat, withdraw|词性：动词
