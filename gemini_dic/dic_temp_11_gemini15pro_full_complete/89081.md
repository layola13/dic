holour:/ˈhɒlər/ (British English), /ˈhɑːlər/ (American English)| n.|喧闹，吵闹；大声喊叫|A loud confused noise, especially one caused by a lot of people shouting.|The children were making a terrible holour in the garden. 孩子们在花园里吵闹得厉害。|近义词：clamor, din, racket, uproar|反义词：silence, quiet|noun
