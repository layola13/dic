adjustability:/əˌdʒʌstəˈbɪləti/| n.|可调节性，可调整性|The adjustability of the car seat makes it suitable for children of all ages.  汽车座椅的可调节性使其适合所有年龄的儿童。|近义词：flexibility, adaptability, modifiability|反义词：inflexibility, rigidity|词性:noun

Etymology->
From adjust + -ability.  Adjust comes from Old French ajuster "to arrange, put in order," from à "to" + juste "right, proper."
