fader:/ˈfeɪdər/ (US), /ˈfeɪdə(r)/ (UK)| n.|衰减器，推子；渐隐装置|A device, typically a knob or slider, used to gradually increase or decrease the volume or level of an audio or video signal.  |The DJ used the fader to smoothly transition between two songs. DJ用推子平滑地在两首歌之间过渡。 |synonyms: attenuator, control, dimmer, slider|antonyms: amplifier, booster|noun
