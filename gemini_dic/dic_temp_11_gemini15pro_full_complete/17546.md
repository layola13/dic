beatified:/bɪˈætɪfaɪd/
adjective
被宣福的；幸福的
Declared by the Catholic Church to have attained the blessedness of heaven and thus worthy of public veneration; supremely happy.
Mother Teresa was beatified in 2003.  特蕾莎修女于2003年被宣福。
She had a beatified smile on her face. 她脸上洋溢着幸福的微笑。
synonyms:blessed, saintly, holy, blissful, rapturous
antonyms:cursed, damned, wretched, miserable
word class:adjective
