baselessness:/ˈbeɪsləsnəs/
n.
无根据，无基础
Lack of foundation or justification.
The baselessness of the rumor was soon revealed.  这个谣言的无根据很快就被揭露了。
近义词：groundlessness, unsubstantiatedness, invalidity
反义词：validity, soundness, foundation
词性：名词
