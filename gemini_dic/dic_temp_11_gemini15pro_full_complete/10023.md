apiculturists:/ˈeɪpɪkʌltʃərɪsts/
n.
养蜂人
People who keep bees, especially for commercial purposes.
例句：The apiculturists carefully inspected the hives for signs of disease. 养蜂人仔细检查蜂箱是否有疾病迹象。

例句：Local apiculturists are concerned about the declining bee population. 当地的养蜂人担心蜜蜂数量的减少。

近义词：beekeeper, apiarist
反义词: N/A
词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin apis "bee" + cultura "cultivation"  + -ist "one who does".
