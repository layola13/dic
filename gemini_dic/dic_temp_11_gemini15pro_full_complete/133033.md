nonenunciatory:/ˌnɒnɪˈnʌnsiətɔːri/
adjective
非陈述的，非宣言的
Not declarative or assertive; not expressing a proposition or judgment.
His response was a nonenunciatory shrug. 他的回应只是耸了耸肩，没有明确表态。
The artist's work is nonenunciatory, leaving interpretation to the viewer.  这位艺术家的作品是非陈述性的，将解释留给观众。
近义词:implicit, suggestive, allusive
反义词:explicit, declarative, enunciatory
词性:adjective

词源:
From non- (not) + enunciatory (relating to enunciation or pronouncement), from Latin enunciatus, past participle of enunciare (to pronounce, declare), from e- (out) + nuntiare (to announce).
