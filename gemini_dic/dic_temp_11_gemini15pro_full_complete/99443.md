interestedly:/ˈɪntrəstɪdli/ | adv. | 感兴趣地；饶有兴趣地 | In a way that shows one's interest. | She listened interestedly to his story. 她饶有兴趣地听着他的故事。 |  近义词: attentively, keenly, eagerly | 反义词: apathetically, indifferently | 词性: 副词

词源: From "interested" (adjective) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Interested" comes from the past participle of the verb "interest," which ultimately derives from the Latin "interesse" meaning "to be between," hence "to concern," "to be of importance."
