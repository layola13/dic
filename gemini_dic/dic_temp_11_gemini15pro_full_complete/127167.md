naturalism:/ˈnætʃrəlɪzəm/| n.|自然主义；自然写实主义；博物学|A belief that only natural (as opposed to supernatural or spiritual) laws and forces operate in the world;  A style and theory of representation based on the accurate depiction of detail.|例句：Naturalism in art rejects idealized portrayals in favor of realistic representation.  艺术中的自然主义拒绝理想化的描绘，而倾向于现实的描绘。

例句：The author's naturalism extended to detailed descriptions of even the most unpleasant aspects of life.  作者的自然主义延伸到对生活中即使是最不愉快的方面的详细描述。|近义词：realism, materialism|反义词：idealism, spiritualism|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *naturalisme*, from *naturel* "natural" (from Latin *naturalis*) + *-isme* "-ism."
