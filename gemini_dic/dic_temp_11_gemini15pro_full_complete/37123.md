combustious:/kəmˈbʌstʃəs/ | adj. | 易燃的；易激动的；暴躁的 |  Likely to catch fire; easily ignited. Also, easily roused to excitement or anger; irascible. | The combustious nature of the chemicals made them dangerous to store near heat. 这些化学品的易燃性使它们在靠近热源储存时很危险。| The combustious politician was known for his fiery speeches.  这位易怒的政治家以其激烈的演讲而闻名。 | Synonyms: flammable, inflammable, incendiary, volatile, fiery, irascible | Antonyms: nonflammable, fire-resistant, calm, placid | adjective


Etymology:
From Late Latin *combustiosus, from Latin combustus (past participle of comburere "to burn up"), from com- "completely" + burere "to burn."
