döner:/ˈdɜːnər/ (US), /ˈdɒnər/ (UK)| n.|土耳其烤肉|A Turkish dish consisting of seasoned meat (usually lamb, beef, or chicken) stacked on a vertical rotisserie and shaved off as it cooks. Served in a pita or other bread with vegetables and sauce.|I had a delicious döner kebab for lunch. 我午饭吃了一个美味的土耳其烤肉卷。|近义词：gyro, shawarma|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
