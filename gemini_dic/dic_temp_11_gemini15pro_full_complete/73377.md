foregut:/ˈfɔːɡʌt/ | n. |前肠|The anterior part of the alimentary canal from the esophagus to the duodenum, including the stomach.  It develops from the foregut in the embryo.|The esophagus, stomach, and part of the duodenum develop from the embryonic foregut. |食道、胃和十二指肠的一部分是由胚胎前肠发育而来的。|synonyms: anterior gut, proenteron | antonyms: hindgut | noun
