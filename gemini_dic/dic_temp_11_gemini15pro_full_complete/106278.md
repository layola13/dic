knoxvillite:/ˈnɒksvɪlaɪt/| n.|诺克斯维尔石|A rare chromium-bearing mineral found near Knoxville, Tennessee, USA.  It is a hydrated sodium calcium chromium sulfate mineral.|Knoxvillite is often found alongside other chromium minerals. 诺克斯维尔石经常与其他铬矿物一起被发现。|Synonym: none (very specific mineral)|Antonym: none (not applicable)|noun
