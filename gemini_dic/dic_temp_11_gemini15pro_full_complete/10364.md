appellatory:/əˈpɛləˌtɔːri/ | adj. | 上诉的，称呼的 | Relating to or having the power of appeal; of or relating to a name or title. | The appellatory process can be lengthy and complex. 上诉过程可能漫长而复杂。The defendant challenged the appellatory jurisdiction of the court.  被告对法院的上诉管辖权提出了质疑。| 近义词：appealable, nominal, titular | 反义词：non-appealable | 词性: adjective

appellatory:/əˈpɛləˌtɔːri/ | n. | 名称，称呼 | A name or title. |  His appellatory, "The Great," was well deserved.  他的称号“大帝”当之无愧。 The official appellatory for the position is "Chief Executive Officer."  该职位的正式名称是“首席执行官”。 | 近义词：name, title, designation | 反义词：None | 词性: noun
