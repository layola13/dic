camphretic:/kæmˈfrɛtɪk/
adjective
樟脑的；似樟脑的
Relating to or containing camphor; having the properties of camphor.
Camphor has a strong, pungent, camphretic odor. 樟脑有强烈的、刺鼻的、樟脑的气味。
The ointment had a distinct camphretic smell.  这种药膏有一种明显的樟脑味。
近义词:camphoraceous
反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider words like "odorless," "bland," or specific opposing scents like "floral" depending on the context)
词性: adjective
