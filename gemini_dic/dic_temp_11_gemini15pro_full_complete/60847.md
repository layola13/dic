electrotonus:/ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒtənəs/ /ɪˌlɛkˈtroʊtənəs/| n. |电紧张|A change in the electrical potential of a nerve or muscle cell in response to a subthreshold stimulus, which does not produce an action potential but alters the excitability of the cell.|Electrotonus can be observed in nerve fibers when a subthreshold current is applied. 电紧张可以在神经纤维施加低于阈值的电流时观察到。|Near synonyms: electrotonic potential, passive potential|Antonyms: action potential|Noun
