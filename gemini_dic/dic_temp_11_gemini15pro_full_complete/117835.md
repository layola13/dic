melligo:/məˈlaɪɡoʊ/ /məˈliːɡəʊ/
noun
白癜风
a skin condition that causes the loss of pigment (coloring) resulting in irregular pale patches of skin.
Melligo is a common skin problem that affects people of all races. 白癜风是一种常见的皮肤问题，影响所有种族的人。
The dermatologist diagnosed her skin condition as melligo. 皮肤科医生诊断她的皮肤病为白癜风。
近义词:leukoderma, vitiligo
反义词: N/A
词性:n.

Etymology:
From Latin *melligo, from mel (honey) +‎ -igo (a suffix forming nouns denoting diseases, from the same PIE root as Greek -ικός).  The connection with honey likely refers to the color of the affected skin or perhaps a perceived sweetness of the urine of sufferers.
