disinhabit:/ˌdɪsɪnˈhæbɪt/
verb
使无人居住，撤离居民
To cause (a place) to become uninhabited; depopulate.
The war disinhabited the village. 战争使这个村庄无人居住。
近义词：depopulate, evacuate
反义词：populate, inhabit
词性：v.
