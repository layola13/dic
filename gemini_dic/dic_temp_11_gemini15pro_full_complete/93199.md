ignorantist:/ˈɪɡnərəntɪst/| n. |无知者，愚昧者|A person who is willfully ignorant or who actively opposes enlightenment or intellectualism.  Someone who cultivates ignorance, especially as a way of life. |He's such an ignorantist; he refuses to believe anything that challenges his worldview. 他真是个无知者；他拒绝相信任何挑战他世界观的东西。

He proudly declared himself an ignorantist, content in his narrow-minded beliefs. 他自豪地宣称自己是一个无知者，满足于他狭隘的信仰。|近义词：bigot, philistine, know-nothing|反义词：intellectual, scholar, polymath|词性: Noun
