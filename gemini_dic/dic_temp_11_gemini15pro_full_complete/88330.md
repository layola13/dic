hippocampus:/ˌhɪpəˈkæmpəs/ | n. |海马体；海马 |A complex neural structure (shaped like a seahorse) consisting of gray matter and located on the floor of each lateral ventricle of the brain. It is involved in the consolidation of information from short-term memory to long-term memory and spatial navigation.| The hippocampus plays a crucial role in forming new memories. 海马体在形成新记忆方面起着至关重要的作用。 | The hippocampus is essential for spatial navigation. 海马体对空间导航至关重要。| synonyms: hippocampal formation | antonyms: N/A | noun
