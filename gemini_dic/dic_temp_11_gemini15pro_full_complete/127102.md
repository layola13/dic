nationalists:/ˈnæʃənəlɪsts/
词性:n.
中文翻译:民族主义者；国家主义者
英文解析:A person who strongly identifies with their own nation and vigorously supports its interests, especially to the exclusion or detriment of the interests of other nations.
例句：The nationalists rallied in the city square, waving flags and chanting slogans. 民族主义者聚集在城市广场，挥舞着旗帜，高呼口号。

例句：Some nationalists believe their country is superior to all others. 一些民族主义者认为他们的国家比所有其他国家都优越。


近义词:patriots, chauvinists
反义词:cosmopolitans, internationalists


词源:From nation + -ist. Late 18th century.
