interacademic:/ˌɪntərækəˈdɛmɪk/
adjective
校际间的，学院间的
Existing or occurring between different academic institutions or schools.
The interacademic sports competition fostered friendly rivalry between the schools. 校际运动比赛促进了学校之间的友好竞争。

The interacademic debate tournament drew participants from across the state.  校际辩论赛吸引了来自全州的参赛者。

Near Synonyms: intercollegiate, interschool
Antonyms: intra-academic, within-school
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- (prefix meaning "between") + academic.  "Inter-" comes from Latin, and "academic" ultimately derives from Greek "Akadēmeia," the name of Plato's school near Athens.
