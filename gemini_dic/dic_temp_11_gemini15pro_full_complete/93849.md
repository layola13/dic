immobilising:/ɪˈmoʊbəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使不动；固定；使不能动弹
Making something or someone unable to move.
The doctor immobilised his broken leg with a cast. 医生用石膏固定了他断掉的腿。
immobilize, restricting, confining
mobilizing, freeing
v.
