graduate: /ˈɡrædʒʊɪt/ (US) /ˈɡrædʒuət/ (UK)| n. vi. vt. adj.| 毕业生；研究生；毕业；刻度|A person who has successfully completed a course of study at a school, college, or university.  To successfully complete a course of study.  Marked with degrees or units of measurement.|
She's a graduate of Harvard University. 她毕业于哈佛大学。


He graduated from Yale with a degree in history. 他毕业于耶鲁大学，获得了历史学学位。


The beaker is graduated in milliliters. 烧杯上刻有毫升刻度。


A recent college graduate.  一位刚毕业的大学生


近义词：alumnus, alumna (n.), complete, finish (v.)|
反义词: undergraduate (n.), commence (v.)|
词性: noun, verb, adjective


词源：From Latin graduatus, past participle of graduari ‘to walk, take a degree’, from gradus ‘step, degree’.


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
She graduates next year. 她明年毕业。
He graduates with honors. 他以优异的成绩毕业。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
He is graduating this spring. 他今年春天毕业。
They are graduating from high school. 他们即将从高中毕业。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
She has graduated from college. 她已经大学毕业了。
He has graduated with a degree in physics. 他已经获得了物理学学位。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
She has been graduating with honors. 她一直以优异的成绩毕业。 (This tense is less common with "graduate" in this context.  It implies an ongoing process of achieving honors.)
They have been graduating from the same school for generations.  几代人都从同一所学校毕业。（这种情况比较少见，表示毕业是一个持续的过程）


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He graduated last year. 他去年毕业了。
She graduated from Oxford in 2020. 她2020年从牛津大学毕业。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was graduating when the pandemic hit.  疫情爆发时我正在毕业。
She was graduating from medical school when she received the news. 她接到消息时正从医学院毕业。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
He had graduated before he started his own business. 他创业前就已经毕业了。
She had graduated by the time the job offer came in.  工作机会来的时候她已经毕业了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
She had been graduating with honors until her final semester. 她直到最后一学期一直都以优异的成绩毕业。(Again, less common.)
They had been graduating from that program for years until it was discontinued.  多年来他们一直从那个项目毕业，直到它被停止。（这种情况比较少见）


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will graduate next spring. 我明年春天毕业。
They will graduate in June. 他们将在六月毕业。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be graduating this time next year. 明年这个时候我将要毕业。
She will be graduating from law school next May. 明年五月她将从法学院毕业。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next summer, I will have graduated. 到明年夏天，我将已经毕业了。
He will have graduated before he turns 25.  他25岁之前就将已经毕业了。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By the time he retires, he will have been graduating students for over 40 years. 到他退休的时候，他将已经毕业了40多年的学生。（这种情况比较少见）
By next year, she will have been graduating students from her art class for a decade. 到明年，她的艺术班将已经毕业十年了。



虚拟语气:
If I were to graduate tomorrow, I would celebrate. 如果我明天毕业，我会庆祝。
I wish I had graduated sooner. 我希望我早点毕业。

被动语态:
The students were graduated last week. 学生们上周毕业了。 (Less common, "graduated" is usually intransitive)
Degrees are graduated according to merit. 学位根据成绩评定等级。


疑问句:
When did you graduate? 你什么时候毕业的？
Where did she graduate from? 她从哪里毕业的？

否定句:
I didn't graduate from college. 我没有大学毕业。
She hasn't graduated yet. 她还没有毕业。
