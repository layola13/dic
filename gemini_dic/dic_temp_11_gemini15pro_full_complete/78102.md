geographies:/dʒiˈɒɡrəfiz/
n.
地理学；地理环境；地理特征
The study of the physical features of the earth and its atmosphere, and of human activity as it affects and is affected by these, including the distribution of populations and resources, land use, and industries.
The book discusses the geographies of several different countries.  这本书讨论了几个不同国家的地理环境。
近义词: topography, terrain, landscapes
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑抽象的概念，例如"uniformity" 均匀性)
词性: 名词


geographies:/dʒiˈɒɡrəfiz/
n.
地理位置分布
The geographical distribution of something.
The geographies of poverty and wealth are often closely linked. 贫困和财富的地理位置分布通常密切相关。
近义词: distribution, location, spread
反义词: (没有直接的反义词)
词性: 名词
