carbonations:/ˌkɑːrbəˈneɪʃənz/
|n.|碳酸化作用；充气；起泡|The process of dissolving carbon dioxide in a liquid, often under pressure, to create carbonated beverages.  This can also refer to the fizzy bubbles produced.|
例句：The carbonations in the soda made it refreshing. 苏打水中的气泡让它很清爽。

例句：He prefers beverages with minimal carbonations. 他更喜欢气泡少的饮料。
|近义词：fizz, effervescence, bubbling|
|反义词：flatness|
|词性：名词|
