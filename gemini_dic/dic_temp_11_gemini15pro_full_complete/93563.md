imagings:/ˈɪmɪdʒɪŋz/
n.
成像，影像，图像
the process of producing images, or the images themselves
例句：Medical imagings, such as X-rays and MRI scans, are crucial for diagnosis.  医学影像，例如 X 光和 MRI 扫描，对诊断至关重要。

例句：The latest satellite imagings reveal the extent of the deforestation. 最新的卫星图像揭示了森林砍伐的程度。

近义词：imaging, imagery, pictures, photographs, visualizations
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "obscuring," "concealing")
词性: noun (plural)
