consolation:/ˌkɒnsəˈleɪʃn/ (US) /ˌkɑːnsəˈleɪʃn/| n.|安慰，慰藉，令人安慰的事物|Something that provides comfort or solace in a time of grief or disappointment.|Her kind words were a great consolation to me. 她友善的话语对我来说是莫大的安慰。|近义词：comfort, solace, relief|反义词: distress, sorrow, grief|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *consolacioun*, from Old French *consolacion*, from Latin *cōnsōlātiō*, from *cōnsōlārī* ("to console"), from *con-* (with, together) + *sōlārī* (to comfort, soothe).
