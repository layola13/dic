interlocked:/ˌɪntərˈlɒkt/ (UK), /ˌɪntərˈlɑːkt/ (US)| adj.|互相连接的，扣住的|Joined or connected together firmly.|The fingers of his hands were interlocked. 他的双手十指交叉扣在一起。|近义词：linked, intertwined, connected, joined|反义词:separate, detached|形容词

interlock:/ˌɪntərˈlɒk/ (UK), /ˌɪntərˈlɑːkt/ (US)| v.|（使）连锁，（使）紧密连接|To fit or be fitted together firmly.|The cogwheels interlock to drive the machine. 齿轮相互啮合以驱动机器。|近义词：link, connect, join, mesh|反义词:separate, disconnect|动词

interlock:/ˌɪntərˈlɒk/ (UK), /ˌɪntərˈlɑːkt/ (US)| n.|连锁装置，联锁装置|A device or system that interlocks things.|The safety interlock prevents the machine from operating if the guard is removed. 安全联锁装置防止机器在防护装置被移除时运行。|近义词：connection, link, joint|反义词:separation|名词
