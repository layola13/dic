curiousest:/ˈkjʊə.ri.ə.st/ | adj. | 最好奇的 |  This is a non-standard superlative form of "curious." While grammatically incorrect, it might be used informally for emphasis.  The standard superlative is "most curious." |  "She had the curiousest look on her face." 她脸上露出了最奇怪的表情。(Informal) | 近义词: most curious, inquisitive, inquiring | 反义词: indifferent, apathetic | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *curios, borrowed from Old French curios “careful, solicitous; inquisitive,” from Latin cūriōsus “careful, diligent, inquisitive,” from cūra “care, concern.”
