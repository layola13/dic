nays:/neɪz/
n.
拒绝，反对票
a negative vote or voter
The nays have it.  反对票占多数。
近义词: rejections, refusals, vetoes, dissents
反义词: ayes, assents, approvals
词性: 名词(plural noun)

nays:/neɪz/
v.
(third-person singular simple present nays, present participle naying, simple past and past participle nayed)
投反对票，拒绝
to vote no; to oppose or reject
He nayed the proposal. 他对这项提议投了反对票。
近义词: reject, refuse, oppose, veto, dissent
反义词: approve, assent, accept
词性: 动词(verb)
