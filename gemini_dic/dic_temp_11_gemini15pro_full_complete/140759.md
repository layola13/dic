omnivore:/ˈɒmnɪvɔːr/ (UK), /ˈɑːmnɪvɔːr/ (US)| n.|杂食动物；无所不读的人|An animal or person that eats food of both plant and animal origin.|Pigs are omnivores, eating both plants and animals. 猪是杂食动物，既吃植物也吃动物。|Humans are omnivores. 人类是杂食动物。|近义词:generalist, all-eater|反义词:herbivore, carnivore|词性:noun

omnivorous:/ɒmˈnɪvərəs/ (UK), /ɑːmˈnɪvərəs/ (US)| adj.|杂食性的；无所不读的|Eating both plants and animals.|Bears are omnivorous mammals. 熊是杂食性哺乳动物。|She has omnivorous reading habits, devouring everything from comics to classics. 她有无所不读的阅读习惯，从漫画到经典著作什么都看。|近义词:all-devouring, polyphagous|反义词:herbivorous, carnivorous|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin omnis, meaning "all," and vorare, meaning "to devour."
