cycad: /ˈsaɪkæd/ | n. | 苏铁 |  A palmlike plant of tropical and subtropical regions, bearing large male or female cones. Cycads are gymnosperms, meaning their seeds are not enclosed in an ovary. |  The botanical garden had a fascinating collection of cycads. 这个植物园里收藏了各种各样迷人的苏铁。 | synonyms:  palm-fern, sago palm | antonyms: N/A | noun
