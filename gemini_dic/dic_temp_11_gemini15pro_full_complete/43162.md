cpc:/ˌsiːpiːˈsiː/ | n. | Cost Per Click (每次点击费用) | Cost Per Click is an online advertising revenue model where the advertiser pays the publisher for each click on an ad. | Example 1: The CPC for this keyword is very high, so we need to optimize our campaign. 这个关键词的每次点击费用很高，所以我们需要优化我们的广告系列。

Example 2:  We're seeing a good return on investment with a low CPC.  由于每次点击费用低，我们的投资回报率很高。 | synonym: pay-per-click (PPC), cost per clickthrough | antonym: CPM (Cost Per Mille/Thousand), CPA (Cost Per Acquisition) | abbreviation
