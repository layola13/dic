connecting:/kəˈnɛktɪŋ/ | adj. | 连接的，联系的 | joining two or more things together; relating two or more things | The connecting flight was delayed.  转机航班延误了。 | related, linking, joining | disconnecting, separating | adjective


connecting:/kəˈnɛktɪŋ/ | n. | 连接，联系 | the act of joining or the state of being joined | The connecting of the two cities by the new highway has boosted trade. 新建的高速公路连接了两个城市，促进了贸易。| connection, link, junction | disconnection, separation | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English *connecten*, from Latin *connectere* ("to bind together"), from *com-* ("together") + *nectere* ("to tie, bind").
