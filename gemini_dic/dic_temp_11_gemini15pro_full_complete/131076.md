nonbeauty:/ˌnɑːnˈbjuːti/ | n. | 缺乏美感；不美的事物；| Lack of beauty; something that is not beautiful. | The landfill site was a scene of utter nonbeauty. 垃圾填埋场是一个完全没有美感的地方。| The nonbeauty of the industrial area contrasted sharply with the natural beauty of the countryside. 工业区的缺乏美感与乡村的自然美形成了鲜明的对比。 | 近义词：ugliness, unattractiveness, plainness | 反义词：beauty, attractiveness, loveliness | 词性：名词

词源:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + beauty
