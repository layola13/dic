miscatalogue:/ˌmɪskætəlɒɡ/ | /ˌmɪskætəlɑːɡ/ | v. | 错误分类 | To catalogue incorrectly; to put into the wrong category. |  The librarian miscategorised the new biography as fiction. 图书管理员把新的传记错分到小说类去了。

The museum miscatalogued the ancient artifact, placing it in the wrong historical period. 博物馆将这件古代文物错误分类，将其放置在错误的历史时期。 | misclassify, misfile | categorize correctly, classify correctly | verb
