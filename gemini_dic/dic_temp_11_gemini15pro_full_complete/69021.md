fattiest:/ˈfætiɪst/
adjective
最肥的
containing the most fat; highest in fat content.
The fattiest cuts of meat are often the most flavorful.  最肥的肉块通常是最美味的。
This is the fattiest burger I've ever seen. 这是我见过的最肥的汉堡。
Synonyms:  richest, oiliest, greasiest, plumpest
Antonyms: leanest, skinniest, thinnest
词性: adjective
