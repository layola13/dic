out-passes:/ˈaʊtˌpæsɪz/
noun
外出许可；出境通行证；（尤指囚犯的）离监许可
permission to leave a place, especially a prison, for a short period
The prisoner was granted an out-pass to attend his mother's funeral.  这名囚犯获准外出参加他母亲的葬礼。
passes, permits, leaves
confinements, restrictions
n.

out-passes:/ˈaʊtˌpæsɪz/
verb
给予（某人）外出许可；批准…外出
to grant someone permission to leave a place
The warden out-passed the prisoner for the weekend. 典狱长批准囚犯周末外出。
grants leave, authorizes exit
denies leave, restricts exit
v.
