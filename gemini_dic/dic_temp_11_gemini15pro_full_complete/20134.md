bipectinate:/baɪˈpɛktɪneɪt/
adjective
双栉形的，两侧梳齿状的
Having two margins with comb-like projections.  Resembling a feather with projections on both sides of the axis.
The bipectinate antenna of the moth is highly sensitive to pheromones.  这只蛾子的双栉形触角对信息素高度敏感。

The leaves of some ferns are bipectinate. 一些蕨类植物的叶子是双栉形的。
pectinate, double-combed
non-bipectinate
adjective
