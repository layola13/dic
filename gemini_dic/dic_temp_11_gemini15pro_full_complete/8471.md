antiasthmatic: /ˌæntiæsˈmætɪk/ | adj. n. | 抗哮喘的；抗哮喘药 |  Relating to a drug or treatment that relieves or prevents asthma. antiasthmatic: /ˌæntiæsˈmætɪk/ | n. | 抗哮喘药 | A medication that relieves or prevents asthma. | Example:  She carries her antiasthmatic with her at all times. 她随时带着她的抗哮喘药。| Synonyms: asthma medication | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym) | Noun
