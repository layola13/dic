nonenvironmental:/ˌnɑːnɪnˌvaɪrənˈmɛntəl/
adjective
非环境的，与环境无关的
Not related to or caused by the environment; not concerning the natural world around us.
The report focused on nonenvironmental factors contributing to the decline in bee populations, such as parasites and diseases.  这份报告集中讨论了导致蜜蜂数量下降的非环境因素，例如寄生虫和疾病。
The company's nonenvironmental performance, including financial stability and employee satisfaction, also needs to be considered.  公司的非环境绩效，包括财务稳定性和员工满意度，也需要考虑。
近义词: non-ecological
反义词: environmental
词性: adjective
