granularity:/ˌɡrænjuˈlærəti/ | n. | 粒度；颗粒度；精细度 | The level of detail in a set of data or information.  It refers to the size of the smallest distinguishable unit or level of detail considered.  A finer granularity implies a more detailed view, while a coarser granularity implies a more summarized view. | We need to analyze the data at a finer granularity to understand the underlying trends.  我们需要更细粒度地分析数据以了解潜在趋势。

The project requires data with high granularity.  这个项目需要高粒度的数据。 |  detail, precision, resolution, specificity  | generality, vagueness, ambiguity | noun
