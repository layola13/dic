epiphytal:/ˌɛpɪˈfaɪtəl/ | adj. | 附生的，附生植物的 | (of a plant) growing on another plant but not parasitic, deriving moisture and nutrients from the air and rain. |  Many orchids are epiphytal, attaching to tree branches. 许多兰花是附生的，附着在树枝上。 | Epiphytal ferns thrive in the humid rainforest canopy.  附生蕨类植物在潮湿的雨林冠层中茁壮成长。| 近义词:  aerophyte | 反义词: terrestrial, parasitic | 词性: adjective

epiphyte: /ˈɛpɪˌfaɪt/ | n. | 附生植物 | a plant that grows on another plant but does not derive nourishment from it. | Orchids and bromeliads are examples of epiphytes. 兰花和凤梨是附生植物的例子。 | The tree was covered in epiphytes, creating a miniature jungle in its branches. 这棵树上覆盖着附生植物，在它的枝杈上形成了一个微型丛林。| 近义词: air plant | 反义词: terrestrial plant | 词性: noun
