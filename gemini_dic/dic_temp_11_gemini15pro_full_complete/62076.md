encephalic:/ɛnˌsɛˈfælɪk/ | adj. | 脑的；脑内的 | Of or relating to the brain; located within the skull. | The doctor diagnosed an encephalic disorder. 医生诊断出一种脑部疾病。| The encephalic region showed signs of trauma. 脑部区域显示出外伤的迹象。 | 近义词: cerebral, cranial | 反义词: peripheral | 词性: adjective

词源: From Greek enkephalos "brain," from en- "in" + kephalē "head."
