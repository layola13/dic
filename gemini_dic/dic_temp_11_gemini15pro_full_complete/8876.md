antifogging:/ˌæntiˈfɒɡɪŋ/ /ˌæntiˈfɑːɡɪŋ/ | adj. | 防雾的，防结雾的 | Preventing the formation of condensation, such as on a surface like glass or plastic. |  The antifogging spray works well on my bathroom mirror.  浴室镜上的防雾喷雾效果很好。| synonyms:  non-fogging, anti-mist, demisting | antonyms: fogging, misting | adjective

antifog:/ˌæntiˈfɒɡ/ /ˌæntiˈfɑːɡ/ | n. | 防雾剂 | A substance or coating that prevents fogging. | I applied antifog to my goggles before diving. 我在潜水前在护目镜上涂了防雾剂。| synonyms: anti-mist, demister | antonyms:  N/A | noun


词源: anti- (prefix meaning "against") + fog (noun) + -ing (suffix forming present participle or adjective)
