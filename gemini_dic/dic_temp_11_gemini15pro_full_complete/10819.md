aquiline:/ˈækwɪlaɪn/ /ˈækwəlaɪn/| adj.|鹰的，似鹰的；钩状的|Curved like an eagle's beak; hooked.|He had a prominent, aquiline nose. 他有一个突出的鹰钩鼻。|近义词：hooked, curved, beaklike|反义词:straight, flat|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *aquilīnus* (“of or belonging to an eagle”), from *aquila* (“eagle”).
