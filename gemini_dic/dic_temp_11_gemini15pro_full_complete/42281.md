counter-espionage:/ˈkaʊntər ˈɛspɪənɑːʒ/ | n. |反间谍活动| The systematic use of spies to acquire information about the plans and activities, especially of a foreign power or a business competitor, and the use of other agents to thwart such activities.  It involves detecting and neutralizing the espionage activities of others. |  The government invested heavily in counter-espionage during the Cold War. 政府在冷战期间大力投资反间谍活动。|  The agency's counter-espionage efforts were successful in preventing the leak of classified information.  该机构的反间谍工作成功地防止了机密信息的泄露。 | 近义词: counterintelligence | 反义词: espionage | 词性: 名词
