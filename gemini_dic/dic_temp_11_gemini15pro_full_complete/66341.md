exculpation:/ˌɛkskʌlˈpeɪʃən/
n.
免罪；辩解；开脱
the act of clearing someone of blame or guilt
The lawyer presented evidence for his client's exculpation.  律师出示了证明其委托人无罪的证据。

The discovery of new evidence led to his exculpation. 新证据的发现使他得以免罪。

近义词:acquittal, absolution, exoneration, vindication
反义词:incrimination, conviction, accusation
词性:noun
