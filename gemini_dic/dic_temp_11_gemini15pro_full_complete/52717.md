digynian: /dɪˈdʒɪniən/ | adj. | 二雌蕊的；两心皮的 |  Having two carpels or pistils (female reproductive organs in a flower).  Referring to a flower having two carpels. This term is primarily used in botany. | The digynian flower has two distinct styles.  这朵二雌蕊的花有两根明显的花柱。| The digynian plant produced fewer seeds than the tetrastigmatic one. 这株二雌蕊植物比四柱头的植物产生的种子少。 | bicarpellary | - | adjective
