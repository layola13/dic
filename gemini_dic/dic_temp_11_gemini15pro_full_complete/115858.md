/ˈmɑːrkʌp/ | v. | 标高；加价 | The store marked up the price of the imported goods.  这家商店提高了进口商品的价格。| increase, raise | decrease, lower | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From "mark up," a phrasal verb meaning to increase the price of something. "Mark" in this context refers to making a written note or indication, while "up" indicates an increase.
