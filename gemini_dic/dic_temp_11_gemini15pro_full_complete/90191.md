hotter:/ˈhɑːtər (US) /ˈhɒtər (UK)| adj.|更热的，更辣的|Comparative form of "hot."  Indicates a higher degree of heat or spiciness.|It's hotter today than yesterday. 今天比昨天更热。|The chili is hotter than I expected.  这辣椒比我想象的要辣。|近义词：warmer, spicier|反义词:cooler, milder|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *hottere, comparative of *hot, from Old English hāttra, comparative of hāt (“hot”), from Proto-Germanic *haitaz, from Proto-Indo-European *kaytos. Cognate with West Frisian hjitter (“hotter”), Dutch heter (“hotter”), German heißer (“hotter”).
