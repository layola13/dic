creep:/kriːp/| v. n.|爬行，匍匐；缓慢地行进；（植物）蔓延；毛骨悚然的感觉|To move slowly and quietly, especially in order to avoid being noticed; a slow, steady movement or progress; a feeling of fear and disgust.|例句：The spider crept slowly along the wall. 蜘蛛沿着墙慢慢爬行。|近义词：crawl, slither, inch, sneak, tiptoe|反义词：sprint, run, dash|词性: verb, noun
creep:/kriːp/| n.|讨厌鬼，谄媚者|Someone who is unpleasant and makes you feel uncomfortable.|例句：He's such a creep! 他真是个讨厌鬼！|近义词：weirdo, weirdo,  jerk|反义词:|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *crēopan, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch kruipen and German kriechen.
