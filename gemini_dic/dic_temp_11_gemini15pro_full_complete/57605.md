drapet:/ˈdreɪpɪt/| adj.|披着垂布的，悬挂着的|Covered with or draped in cloth; hanging loosely or gracefully.|例句：The windows were drapet in velvet curtains. 窗户上挂着天鹅绒窗帘。|近义词：draped, hung, suspended|反义词：undecorated, bare|词性:形容词

drapet:/ˈdreɪpɪt/| n.|垂布，帷幔|A cloth hanging or covering.|例句：The drapet around the bed created a cozy atmosphere. 床周围的帷幔营造了一种舒适的氛围。|近义词：drapery, curtain, hanging|反义词：None|词性:名词
