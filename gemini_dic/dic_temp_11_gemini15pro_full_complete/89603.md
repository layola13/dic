hoodoo:/ˈhuːduː/ | n. | 厄运；不祥之物；骗局；巫毒教 | A jinx; something that brings bad luck. Also, a fraud or trickery. In some contexts, it refers to a form of African American folk magic. | Example 1:  There's a hoodoo on this house; nothing ever goes right here. 这房子好像中了邪，做什么都不顺。

Example 2: The salesman's promises turned out to be a hoodoo; the product didn't work as advertised.  推销员的承诺结果是个骗局；产品不像广告宣传的那样有效。| 近义词: jinx, curse, bad luck, charm, hex,  swindle, trickery | 反义词: blessing, good luck, talisman | 词性: 名词

hoodoo:/ˈhuːduː/ | vt. | 使倒霉；施魔法于 | To bring bad luck to; to bewitch. | Example 1:  I think someone hoodooed my car; it keeps breaking down. 我觉得有人诅咒了我的车，它总是坏。

Example 2: She believes her rival hoodooed her business to make it fail. 她认为她的对手用巫术诅咒了她的生意，使其失败。| 近义词: curse, hex, jinx, bewitch | 反义词: bless | 词性: 动词
