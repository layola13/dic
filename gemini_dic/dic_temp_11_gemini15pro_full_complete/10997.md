arborescent:/ˌɑːrbəˈrɛsənt/ | adj. | 树状的，像树的 | Resembling a tree in growth or appearance; branching like a tree. |  The fungus had an arborescent structure.  这种真菌具有树状结构。| 近义词: treelike, dendritic, branching, ramified | 反义词: shrubby, bushy | 词性: 形容词
Etymology: From Latin *arborescent-, present participle stem of arborescere "to become a tree," from arbor "tree."
