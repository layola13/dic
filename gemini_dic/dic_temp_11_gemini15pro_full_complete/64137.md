equably:/ˈikwəbli/| adv.|平静地；均匀地；公平地|In a calm, steady, and even manner; also, impartially.|He distributed the workload equably among his team. 他将工作量平均分配给他的团队。|例句：The profits were divided equably among the partners. 利润在合伙人之间公平分配。|例句：She spoke equably, despite the stressful situation. 尽管处境紧张，她还是平静地说话。|近义词：calmly, evenly, fairly, justly, uniformly|反义词: unevenly, unfairly, unjustly, disproportionately|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin aequabiliter, from Latin aequabilis (“equable, uniform”), from aequus (“equal, level, just”).
