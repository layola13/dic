midheaven: /ˈmɪdˌhɛvn/ | n. | 中天；天顶；事业的顶峰 | The point of the ecliptic that is directly overhead at a particular location and time.  In astrology, it represents career, reputation, and public image. |  Her Midheaven is in Leo, suggesting a career in the entertainment industry. 她的中天在狮子座，暗示着在娱乐业的职业生涯。

例句：

His Midheaven is in Capricorn, signifying ambition and a drive for success. 他的中天在摩羯座，象征着雄心壮志和对成功的渴望。

Astrologers believe the Midheaven can reveal your ultimate potential and public standing.  占星家认为中天可以揭示你最终的潜力和公众地位。


近义词: zenith, culmination, apex | 反义词: nadir, bottom | 词性: noun
