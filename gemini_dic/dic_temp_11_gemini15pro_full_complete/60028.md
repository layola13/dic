edt:/iːdiːˈtiː/ | abbreviation | 东部夏令时间 (Eastern Daylight Time) | Eastern Daylight Time, a time zone used in North America during the summer months, equivalent to UTC-4. | Example: The meeting is scheduled for 9:00 AM EDT. 会议安排在东部夏令时间上午9点。 | Synonym: EDT | Antonym: EST (Eastern Standard Time) | abbreviation

edt:/ˈɛdɪt/ | v. | 编辑 | To prepare written material for publication by correcting, condensing, or otherwise modifying it. | Example: She edits manuscripts for a publishing house.  她为一家出版社编辑稿件。
Example: He spent hours editing his photos before posting them online. 他花了好几个小时编辑照片才把它们上传到网上。| Synonym: revise, amend, correct, alter, modify | Antonym:  write, compose | verb
