cablelike:/ˈkeɪbl̩laɪk/
adjective
像电缆的，缆状的
Resembling a cable in form or function.
The climber used a cablelike rope to ascend the cliff. 攀登者使用了一条像电缆一样的绳子攀登悬崖。
The vine grew thick and cablelike. 藤蔓长得又粗又像缆绳。
近义词: ropelike, cordlike, wirelike
反义词: thin, slender, delicate
词性: adjective

词源: cable + like.  "Cable" comes from Old North French "cable," ultimately from Late Latin "capulum" (rope, halter).  "-like" is a native English suffix meaning "resembling."
