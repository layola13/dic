co-favourite:/kəʊˈfeɪvərɪt/
n.
共同的最受欢迎的人/物
One of two or more things or people equally favored to win or succeed.
Manchester United and Chelsea are co-favourites to win the league. 曼联和切尔西是共同的联赛夺冠热门。
joint favourite, equal favourite
underdog, outsider
noun
