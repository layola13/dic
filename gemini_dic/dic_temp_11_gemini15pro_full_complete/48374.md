decury:/ˈdɛkjəri/| n.|十人组，十户联保|A group of ten, especially a group of ten families in the ancient Roman system of frankpledge, responsible for each other's good behavior.|The decury system was used to maintain order in Roman society.|近义词:group, band, company|反义词:individual|词性:名词

Etymology: From Old French *decurie*, from Latin *decuria* ("a group of ten"), from *decem* ("ten").
