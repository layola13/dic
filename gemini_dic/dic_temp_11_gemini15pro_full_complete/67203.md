expressionists:/ɪkˈspreʃənɪsts/
n.
表现主义者
Artists, writers, or musicians who practice expressionism.  A style that seeks to express emotional experience rather than impressions of the external world.
The expressionists sought to convey the anguish of the human condition. 表现主义者试图表达人类境况的痛苦。
近义词:modernists, avant-garde
反义词:traditionalists, classicists
词性:Noun
