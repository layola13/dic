deadlatch:/ˈdɛdlætʃ/| n.|插销；自动锁；|A lock that is engaged automatically when a door is closed and requires a key or a handle to retract the latch bolt. |例句：The deadlatch on the front door is broken. 前门的插销坏了。

例句：Make sure the deadlatch is engaged when you leave. 你离开时确保插销已锁上。|近义词：latch, lock, bolt|反义词: unlock |n.
