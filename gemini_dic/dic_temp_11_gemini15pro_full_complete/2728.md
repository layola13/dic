adularia:/ˌædʒʊˈlɛəriə/| n. |冰长石，透长石|A transparent to translucent variety of orthoclase feldspar, sometimes having an opalescent or pearly luster. It is used as a gemstone, especially when exhibiting adularescence.|The ring featured a cabochon of adularia with a beautiful blue sheen. 这枚戒指的特点是一块带有美丽蓝色光泽的凸圆形冰长石。|Synonyms: moonstone, orthoclase|Antonyms: N/A|noun
