cuisinier:/kwiːziːˈnjeɪ/ | n. |厨师，厨子 | A professional cook, especially the head chef in a restaurant or hotel.  A cuisinier is a skilled culinary professional, responsible for creating and preparing dishes.  |  The cuisinier prepared a magnificent seven-course meal.  这位厨师准备了一顿丰盛的七道菜。|  Synonyms: chef, cook | Antonyms: N/A | noun
