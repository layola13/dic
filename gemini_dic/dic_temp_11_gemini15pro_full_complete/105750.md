kipskin:/ˈkɪpskɪn/| n.|小牛皮，小山羊皮|A soft, pliable leather made from the skin of a young or small animal, typically a calf or kid.|例句：She bought a pair of expensive kipskin gloves. 她买了一副昂贵的小牛皮手套。

例句：Kipskin is often used for making high-quality bookbindings. 小牛皮常用于制作高质量的书籍装订。|近义词：calfskin, kidskin, goatskin|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "synthetic leather")|词性:noun
