jaggedest:/ˈdʒæɡɪdɪst/
adjective
最锯齿状的，最参差不齐的
superlative adjective of jagged; having the most deeply indented edge; most uneven and rough.
The jaggedest peaks of the mountains were shrouded in mist.  群山中最为锯齿状的山峰笼罩在薄雾中。
The coastline was jaggedest where the cliffs met the sea. 海岸线在悬崖与大海交汇处最为参差不齐。
近义词:roughest, most uneven, most serrated, most ragged
反义词:smoothest, flattest, evenest
词性:adjective
词源:From *jagged* + *-est*. *Jagged* from *jag*, of uncertain origin, perhaps related to *shake*.
