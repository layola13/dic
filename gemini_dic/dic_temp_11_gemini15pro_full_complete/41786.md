corsair:/ˈkɔːrseər/ (US), /ˈkɔːsɛː/ (UK)| n. |海盗，私掠船|A pirate, especially one operating in the Mediterranean Sea during the 16th-18th centuries, often operating with the sanction of a government to attack enemy shipping.|例句：The corsair plundered the merchant ship, taking all its valuable cargo.  海盗洗劫了商船，夺走了所有珍贵的货物。

例句：Tales of daring corsairs filled the taverns along the coast.  沿海酒馆里充满了关于勇敢海盗的故事。

近义词：pirate, buccaneer, privateer|反义词:merchant, trader|词性:noun
