faineant:/ˈfeɪnɪənt/ | adj. n.| 懒惰的，懒汉 | An idle or lazy person; one who shirks work or responsibility. | He was such a faineant that he couldn't even be bothered to clean his own room. 他如此懒惰，甚至懒得打扫自己的房间。 | 近义词: lazy, indolent, slothful | 反义词: industrious, diligent, energetic | 词性: adjective, noun | 词源: From French *fainéant*, from *fait néant* "doing nothing," literally "does nothing."
