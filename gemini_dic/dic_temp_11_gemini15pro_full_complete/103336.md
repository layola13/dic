jigsawn:/ˈdʒɪɡˌsɔːn/
adjective
镂花的；用竖锯锯成的
cut with a jigsaw; having an irregular outline suggestive of having been cut with a jigsaw.
The jigsawn edges of the wooden puzzle pieces fit together perfectly.  这些木制拼图的镂空边缘完美地契合在一起。
The carpenter created a beautiful jigsawn frame for the mirror. 木匠为镜子制作了一个漂亮的镂花框架。
synonym: fretted, serrated, notched
antonym: smooth, straight, even
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From jig + saw + -n.  "Jig" refers to a type of reciprocating saw, and "sawn" is the past participle of "saw."  Therefore, "jigsawn" literally means "cut with a jigsaw."
