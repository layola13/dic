honestly:/ˈɑnɪstli/ /ˈɒnɪstli/| adv.|真诚地，坦率地，老实说|Used to emphasize the truth or sincerity of a statement.  It can also be used to express one's true feelings or opinions. |Honestly, I don't know what to do. 老实说，我不知道该怎么办。|Synonyms: truthfully, frankly, sincerely, genuinely|Antonyms: dishonestly, falsely, deceptively|Adverb
