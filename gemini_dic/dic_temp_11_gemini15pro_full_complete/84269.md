half-sunk:/ˌhɑːf ˈsʌŋk/ |adj.|半沉的; 快沉的; 衰败的; 衰落的|Partially submerged, especially in water or mud; also used figuratively to describe something in a state of decline or decay.|The half-sunk boat was a haunting reminder of the storm. 那艘半沉的船是对风暴挥之不去的记忆。|The half-sunk village struggled for survival after the flood. 洪水过后，这个衰败的村庄挣扎着生存。|Near synonyms: subsiding, sinking, foundering, declining, decaying|Near antonyms: afloat, buoyant, thriving, flourishing|adjective
