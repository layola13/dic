dribber:/ˈdrɪbər/| n. |运球者；口水滴落的人；<非正式>笨蛋|A person who dribbles, especially in sports like basketball.  A person who drools or lets saliva drip from their mouth.  Informally, a foolish or inept person.|例句：The basketball dribbler skillfully maneuvered past the defender.  这个篮球运球者巧妙地绕过了防守队员。

例句：The baby was a dribbler, constantly needing a bib.  这个婴儿是个流口水的小家伙，总是需要围兜。|近义词：drooler, driveller (for drooling), player (in sports), fool, idiot (informal)|反义词: N/A|词性: Noun


dribble:/ˈdrɪbl/ |v. n.|运球；滴下；流口水；胡说| (v.) To propel (a ball) by repeated light bounces or kicks. To fall or flow in drops or an unsteady stream. To let saliva flow from the mouth uncontrollably. To talk foolishly or incoherently. (n.) The act or an instance of dribbling.|例句：He dribbled the basketball down the court. 他运球沿着球场前进。

例句：Rain dribbled down the windowpane. 雨水顺着窗玻璃滴下来。|近义词： trickle, drip, drool, babble|反义词:gush, pour|词性: Verb, Noun
