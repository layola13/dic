mischief:/ˈmɪstʃɪf/ (US), /ˈmɪstʃiːf/ (UK)| n.|恶作剧，淘气，损害，祸害|Playful but often annoying or harmful behavior; trouble or damage caused by something or someone.|例句：The children were full of mischief. 孩子们非常淘气。

例句：Their mischief caused a lot of damage. 他们的恶作剧造成了很大的损失。

近义词：pranks, naughtiness, trouble, harm, damage|反义词:obedience, goodness|词性:名词
