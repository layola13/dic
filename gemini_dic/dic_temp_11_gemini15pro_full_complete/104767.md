kayf: /keɪf/
n.
欣福感；舒适感；享受；快感
A feeling of contentment or well-being; enjoyment or pleasure.
例句：He was in a state of kayf after the massage. 他按摩后感到非常舒服。

例句：The kayf of victory was short-lived. 胜利的喜悦是短暂的。
近义词：euphoria, bliss, pleasure, delight, joy
反义词：discomfort, pain, misery, distress
词性: 名词

词源: From Arabic كيف (kayf), meaning "how," but also used as an adverb meaning "well," "comfortably," and hence as a noun denoting a state of well-being.
