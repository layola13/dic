dewretting:/diːˈrɛtɪŋ/
n.
沤麻；脱胶
The process of removing the gummy substance from plant fibers, such as flax or hemp, to prepare them for spinning and weaving.
Dewretting is a crucial step in the production of linen.  沤麻是亚麻生产的关键步骤。
The farmer used a traditional dewretting method for his hemp crop.  这位农民用传统的沤麻方法处理他的大麻作物。
retting, decortication
