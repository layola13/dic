disoxidate:/diːˈsɒksɪdeɪt/ | v. |脱氧，还原|To remove oxygen from a compound; to reduce.|The chemist disoxidated the copper oxide to obtain pure copper.  化学家对氧化铜进行脱氧以获得纯铜。|Example:  The process disoxidates the metal ore. 这个过程使金属矿石脱氧。|Synonyms: reduce, deoxidize|Antonyms: oxidize, oxygenate|verb
