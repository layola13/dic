bellower:/ˈbeləʊər/ (US), /ˈbɛləʊə(r)/ (UK)| n.|大声吼叫的人；咆哮者|A person who bellows; someone who shouts loudly and deeply.|The bellower on the street corner was preaching about the end of the world. 街角那个大声吼叫的人正在宣讲世界末日。|Synonyms: shouter, roarer, screamer, yeller|Antonyms: whisperer, murmurer|noun

bellow:/ˈbeləʊ/ (US), /ˈbɛləʊ/ (UK)| v.|大声吼叫；咆哮|To shout in a deep, loud voice.|The sergeant bellowed orders at the recruits. 军士对着新兵大声吼叫着命令。|The bull bellowed in pain. 公牛痛苦地吼叫着。|Synonyms: roar, yell, shout, scream|Antonyms: whisper, murmur|verb

Etymology: From Middle English *belwen*, of imitative origin.
