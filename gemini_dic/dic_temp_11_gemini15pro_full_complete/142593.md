osteography: /ˌɒstiˈɒɡrəfi/ /ˌɑːstiˈɑːɡrəfi/
n.
骨骼学
The scientific description of the bones.  The study of the structure and function of the skeleton.
例句：The osteography of the dinosaur revealed much about its locomotion.  恐龙的骨骼学揭示了它的运动方式。

例句：His research in osteography contributed significantly to our understanding of ancient civilizations. 他的骨骼学研究极大地促进了我们对古代文明的理解。

近义词：skeletal anatomy
反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, but one could consider "soft tissue anatomy" as a contrasting field of study)
词性: Noun
