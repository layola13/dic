botanical:/bəˈtænɪkəl/
adjective
植物的；植物学的
Relating to plants; of or pertaining to botany.
The botanical garden has a wide variety of flowers. 这个植物园有各种各样的花。
Near Antonyms: zoological
Antonyms: nonbotanical

botanical:/bəˈtænɪkəl/
noun
植物性药材
A substance obtained from a plant and used as a medicine or drug.
This botanical is known for its healing properties.  这种植物药材以其治疗特性而闻名。
Near Synonyms: herbal remedy, plant-based medicine
