beerier:/ˈbɪəriər/ | adj. | 更像啤酒味的；啤酒味更浓的 | Having a stronger flavor or aroma of beer. |  This beer is beerier than the one I had last week.  这啤酒比我上周喝的啤酒味更浓。|  more beery, stronger-tasting | less beery, milder-tasting | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From beer + -y + -er (comparative suffix).  The suffix "-y" turns the noun "beer" into an adjective "beery," meaning "having the characteristics of beer."  The suffix "-er" then creates the comparative form "beerier," indicating a greater degree of beeriness.
