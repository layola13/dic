escheat:/ɪsˈtʃiːt/
n. vi. vt.|充公；归属；归还权|The property escheated to the state because there were no legal heirs.|由于没有合法继承人，财产充公给了国家。|近义词: forfeit, confiscate|反义词: inherit, grant|词性:名词, 动词

escheat:/ɪsˈtʃiːt/
v. |（因无人继承而）归属，归还|The land will escheat to the government if the owner dies without a will.|如果所有者没有遗嘱而去世，土地将归政府所有。|近义词: revert, lapse|反义词: inherit, grant|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French eschete "that which falls to one," from escheoir "to fall to, devolve," from Latin excadere "to fall out, fall away, slip away," from ex- "out" + cadere "to fall."
