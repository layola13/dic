isotopic:/ˌaɪsəˈtɒpɪk/ /ˌaɪsəˈtɑːpɪk/| adj.|同位素的|Relating to or containing isotopes, atoms of the same element with the same number of protons but different numbers of neutrons.|Uranium has several isotopic forms. 铀有几种同位素形式。|近义词：isomeric|反义词:|形容词
isotopic:/ˌaɪsəˈtɒpɪk/ /ˌaɪsəˈtɑːpɪk/| n.|同位素|An isotope of an element.|Carbon-14 is an isotopic form of carbon. 碳-14是碳的一种同位素形式。|近义词：isotope|反义词:|名词
