disloyalists:/dɪsˈlɔɪəlɪsts/
noun
不忠者，变节者
People who are not loyal, especially to their country or a political cause.
During the American Revolution, the disloyalists faced persecution from their patriot neighbors.  在美国独立战争期间，不忠诚者面临着来自爱国邻居的迫害。
Many disloyalists fled to Canada after the war.  战争结束后，许多不忠诚者逃往加拿大。
synonyms: traitors, defectors, renegades, turncoats
antonyms: loyalists, patriots
