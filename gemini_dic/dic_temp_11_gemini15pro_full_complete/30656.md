cernuous:/ˈsɜːrnjuːəs/| adj.|低垂的，下垂的|Drooping; hanging down, usually of plants or plant parts.  Characterized by a drooping or nodding posture.|The cernuous blossoms of the willow tree swayed gently in the breeze. 垂柳低垂的花朵在微风中轻轻摇曳。|近义词：drooping, pendulous, nodding|反义词：erect, upright|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Latin *cernuus*, from *cernere* "to distinguish, separate, sift, decide, decree".  The connection lies in the idea of something separated or set apart, perhaps suggesting the way a drooping flower is separated from its stem.
