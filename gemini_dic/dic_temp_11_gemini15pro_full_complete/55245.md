dissimilitude:/ˌdɪsɪˈmɪlɪtjuːd/
n.
lack of resemblance; difference; dissimilarity
The dissimilitude between the twins was striking, despite their identical genes. 这对双胞胎之间的差异很明显，尽管他们的基因相同。
The dissimilitude between their political views caused a rift in their friendship. 他们政治观点的差异导致了他们友谊的破裂。
近义词: difference, dissimilarity, unlikeness, disparity, divergence
反义词: similarity, resemblance, likeness, similitude
