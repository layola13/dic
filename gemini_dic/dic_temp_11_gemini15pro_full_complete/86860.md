hemiglyph:/ˈhɛmɪɡlɪf/
noun
半形符号，半象形文字
A character that is only partially ideographic; half a glyph.  It represents a sound value but gives some indication of meaning.
The ancient Egyptian writing system used hemiglyphs to represent certain sounds.  古埃及文字使用半形符号来表示某些声音。
