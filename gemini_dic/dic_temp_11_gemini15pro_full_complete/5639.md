altimeter:/ˈæltɪmiːtər/ | n. |高度计；测高仪|An instrument that measures altitude, especially an aircraft's height above sea level. |The pilot checked the altimeter to confirm their cruising altitude. 飞行员检查了高度计以确认他们的巡航高度。|Synonyms:  hypsometer,  altitude meter |Antonyms: depth gauge |noun
