headlessnesses:/ˈhɛdləsnəsɪz/
n.
无头状态; 无领导状态; 无组织状态
The state of being headless, lacking a leader, or lacking organization.
The company descended into headlessnesses after the CEO resigned unexpectedly.  公司在首席执行官意外辞职后陷入了群龙无首的状态。
The recent headlessnesses in the government has led to widespread instability.  政府最近的无领导状态导致了普遍的不稳定。
近义词:disorganization, disorder, chaos, anarchy, leaderlessness
反义词:organization, order, structure, leadership
