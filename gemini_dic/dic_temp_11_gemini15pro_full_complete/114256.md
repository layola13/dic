maimedly:/ˈmeɪmdli/| adv.|残缺地，伤残地|In a manner that suggests being maimed or disabled.  Describes an action performed with difficulty or incompleteness due to injury.|He walked maimedly across the room, his leg still stiff from the accident. 他拖着因事故而僵硬的腿，一瘸一拐地走过房间。|近义词：crippledly, disabledly, haltingly|反义词:soundly, healthily|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "maim" (v.) + "-ed" (suffix forming past participles and adjectives) + "-ly" (suffix forming adverbs).  "Maim" comes from Old French "mahaignier," probably of Germanic origin.
