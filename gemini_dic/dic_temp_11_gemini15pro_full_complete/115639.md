marcionite:/ˈmɑːrʃəˌnaɪt/| n. adj.| 马吉安派信徒，马吉安派的|A follower of Marcion, a second-century Christian theologian who rejected the Hebrew Bible and much of the New Testament.  A belief or practice characteristic of Marcion or his followers. |例句：He was denounced as a Marcionite for his rejection of the Old Testament. 他因拒绝接受旧约而被谴责为马吉安派信徒。

例句：Marcionite beliefs were considered heretical by the early Church. 马吉安派的信仰被早期教会视为异端。|近义词：heretic, gnostic|反义词：orthodox|词性: 名词，形容词
