misauthorizing:/ˌmɪsˈɔːθəˌraɪzɪŋ/
verb
未经授权，错误授权
To authorize wrongly or improperly; to give incorrect or unauthorized permission.
The bank clerk misauthorized the transaction, leading to a significant loss of funds.  银行职员错误地授权了这笔交易，导致了大量的资金损失。
He misauthorized the release of confidential information. 他错误授权泄露了机密信息。
Near Synonyms: misapprove, disallow, prohibit, forbid
Antonyms: authorize, permit, approve, sanction
词性:动词
