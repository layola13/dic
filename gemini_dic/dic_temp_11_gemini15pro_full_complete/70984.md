firestarter:/ˈfaɪərstɑːrtər/| n.|纵火犯；煽动者；导火索；引火物|A person or thing that starts fires, literally or figuratively.  A person who deliberately sets fires or incites unrest. Something that ignites easily or that causes a larger fire to start. |例句：The police are looking for the firestarter who caused the blaze. 警方正在寻找引起这场大火的纵火犯。

例句：His inflammatory speech acted as a firestarter for the riot. 他煽动性的演讲成了这场骚乱的导火索。|近义词：arsonist, incendiary, instigator, catalyst, tinder|反义词：peacemaker, mediator, extinguisher|词性:noun
