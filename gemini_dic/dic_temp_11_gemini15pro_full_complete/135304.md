nonpatentability:/ˌnɒnpæt(ə)ntəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可专利性 | The state or quality of not being patentable; inability to be patented. | The nonpatentability of abstract ideas is a cornerstone of patent law.  抽象概念的不可专利性是专利法的基石。| Synonyms: unpatentability | Antonyms: patentability | noun
