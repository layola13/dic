flacherie:/flæʃˈriː/ | n. | 幼虫腐败病；空头病 | A bacterial disease of silkworms and other insect larvae, characterized by the rapid liquefaction of the body tissues.  |  The silkworm farmer lost his entire crop due to flacherie. 养蚕农由于幼虫腐败病损失了全部的蚕. |  Synonyms: wilt disease, bacterial infection | Antonyms: health, well-being | noun
