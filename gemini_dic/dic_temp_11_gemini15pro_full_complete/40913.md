cooled-down:/kuːld daʊn/
adjective
冷却的；平静的；镇定的
having become less hot, excited, or angry
例句：
The cooled-down lava formed new land. 冷却后的熔岩形成了新的陆地。

I had a cooled-down cup of coffee. 我喝了一杯冷却了的咖啡。


近义词：calm, composed, tranquil, serene, placid, peaceful
反义词：heated, agitated, excited, furious
