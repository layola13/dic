hit-list:/ˈhɪt lɪst/ | n. | 暗杀名单；打击目标名单 | A list of people or things intended to be eliminated, attacked, or dealt with. | The dictator kept a hit-list of his political enemies. 独裁者保留了一份他的政敌的暗杀名单。

The company has a hit-list of poorly performing stores that it plans to close. 这家公司有一份业绩不佳的门店名单，计划关闭它们。| synonyms: blacklist, target list, death list | antonyms: safe list, whitelist | noun
