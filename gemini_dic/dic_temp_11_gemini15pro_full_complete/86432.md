heeler:/ˈhiːlər/| n.|牧牛犬；紧跟不舍的人；帮手|A dog, especially an Australian cattle dog, trained to nip at the heels of cattle to herd them. Also, a person who follows closely or persistently, or a helper.|例句：The experienced heeler quickly gathered the stray cattle.  经验丰富的牧牛犬迅速地将走散的牛聚集起来。

例句：He was known as a tireless heeler, always ready to lend a hand. 他以不知疲倦的帮手而闻名，总是乐于助人。|近义词：herder, helper, assistant, follower|反义词:leader, opponent|词性:noun


heeler:/ˈhiːlər/| n.|修理鞋跟的人|A person who repairs heels of shoes.|例句：The heeler skillfully replaced the worn-out heel.  修鞋匠熟练地更换了磨损的鞋跟。

例句：I took my boots to the heeler to get them resoled. 我把靴子拿到修鞋匠那里去换鞋底。|近义词：cobbler, shoemaker|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
