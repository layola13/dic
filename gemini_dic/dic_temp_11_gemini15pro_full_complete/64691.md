escapers:/ɪˈskeɪpərz/
|n.|逃脱者；逃避者|People who escape from a place or situation. Those who manage to get away from confinement, danger, or an unpleasant reality.|The escapers from the prison were quickly apprehended.  从监狱里逃出来的人很快就被抓住了。|Synonyms: escapees, refugees, runaways|Antonyms: captives, prisoners|noun


escapers:/ɪˈskeɪpərz/
|n.|逃避现实者|People who seek distraction or relief from unpleasant realities, often through fantasy, entertainment, or other activities.|He's an escaper who immerses himself in video games to avoid dealing with his problems. 他是一个逃避现实的人，沉浸在电子游戏中以避免处理他的问题。|Synonyms: daydreamers, fantasists|Antonyms: realists, pragmatists|noun
