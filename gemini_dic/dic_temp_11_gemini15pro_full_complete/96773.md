inequilobate:/ɪnˌiːkwɪˈloʊbeɪt/
|adj.|不等裂的；不等基部的|Having lobes that are unequal or dissimilar, especially referring to the basal lobes of a leaf or other organ.|The botanist identified the plant by its inequilobate leaves.  植物学家通过其不等裂的叶子识别了这种植物。|Example: The inequilobate shape of the leaf was a key characteristic for identification.  叶子的不等基部形状是鉴定的关键特征。|Synonyms: asymmetrical, uneven, unequal|Antonyms: symmetrical, even, equal|adjective

inequilateral:/ɪnˌiːkwɪˈlætərəl/
|adj.|不等边的|Having unequal sides.|The inequilateral triangle has sides of different lengths. 这个不等边三角形的边长不同。|Example:  The artist used inequilateral shapes to create a dynamic composition. 艺术家使用不等边形状来创造动态的构图。|Synonyms: asymmetrical, uneven|Antonyms: equilateral, symmetrical|adjective
