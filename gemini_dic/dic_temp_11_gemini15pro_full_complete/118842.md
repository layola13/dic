metaphorist: /ˈmɛtəfərɪst/ /ˈmɛtəfɔːrɪst/
n.
隐喻家；善用隐喻者
A person who uses metaphors, especially habitually or skillfully.
Shakespeare was a master metaphorist.  莎士比亚是一位隐喻大师。
Synonyms: figure of speech user, allegorist, symbolist
Antonyms: literalist
词性: noun
