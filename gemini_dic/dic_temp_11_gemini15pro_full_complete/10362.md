appellativenesses: /əˌpɛlətɪvnɪsɪz/ | n. | 名词性；通称性 | The quality of being appellative; the use of appellatives or common nouns instead of proper nouns.  It refers to the general nature of a word or phrase, rather than a specific instance. |  The appellativenesses of the term "hero" makes it applicable to many individuals. “英雄”一词的通称性使其适用于许多人。 |  generality, commonness | specificity, particularity | noun
