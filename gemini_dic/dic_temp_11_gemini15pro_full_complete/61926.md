emulating:/ˈɛmjʊleɪtɪŋ/
verb| 模仿；效仿；仿真 | Imitating or trying to equal or surpass someone or something admired. | He was emulating the style of his favorite painter. 他在模仿他最喜欢的画家的风格。| synonyms: imitating, copying, mimicking, mirroring, following,  aping | antonyms:  neglecting, ignoring, disregarding | v.

emulate:/ˈɛmjʊleɪt/
verb| 模仿；效仿；仿真 | Imitating or trying to equal or surpass someone or something admired. |  She tried to emulate her older sister's success. 她试图效仿她姐姐的成功。 | synonyms: imitate, copy, mimic, mirror, follow, ape | antonyms: neglect, ignore, disregard | v.

emulation:/ˌɛmjʊˈleɪʃən/
noun| 模仿；效仿；仿真 |  The effort to match or surpass a person or achievement, typically by imitation. | The new software is designed in emulation of popular programs.  这款新软件的设计是为了模仿流行的程序。| synonyms: imitation, copying, mimicking, mirroring, following | antonyms: originality, individuality | n.
