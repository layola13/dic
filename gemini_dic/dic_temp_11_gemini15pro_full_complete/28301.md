capybara:/ˌkæpiˈbɑːrə/ (US), /ˌkæpɪˈbɑːrə/ (UK)| n. |水豚|A large South American rodent with partially webbed feet, a short tail, and coarse, bristly hair.|Capybaras are the largest rodents in the world. 水豚是世界上最大的啮齿动物。|Synonym:hydrochoerus|Antonym: N/A|Noun


capybaras:/ˌkæpiˈbɑːrəz/ (US), /ˌkæpɪˈbɑːrəz/ (UK)| n. |水豚（复数）|Plural form of capybara, referring to more than one capybara.|We saw a group of capybaras by the river. 我们在河边看到一群水豚。|Synonym:hydrochoeruses|Antonym: N/A|Noun
