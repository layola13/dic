heartbreak:/ˈhɑːrtbreɪk/ (US) /ˈhɑːtbreɪk/ (UK)| n.|心碎，极度悲伤|Overwhelming sadness, crushing grief, typically caused by romantic loss or other emotional trauma.|She suffered terrible heartbreak after her divorce. 她离婚后遭受了极大的痛苦。|synonyms: heartache, sorrow, grief, anguish, agony, devastation, desolation|antonyms: joy, happiness, elation|noun

heartbreak:/ˈhɑːrtbreɪk/ (US) /ˈhɑːtbreɪk/ (UK)| n.|令人心碎的事|Something that causes overwhelming sadness or grief.|The loss of their child was a heartbreak they never recovered from. 失去孩子是他们永远无法恢复的伤痛。|synonyms: tragedy, catastrophe, disaster, blow|antonyms: blessing, miracle, godsend|noun
