litmus:/ˈlɪtməs/| n.|石蕊|A dye obtained from certain lichens that changes color according to the pH of the solution it is in, used as an indicator of acidity or alkalinity.|The scientist dipped litmus paper into the solution. 科学家将石蕊试纸浸入溶液中。|Synonym: indicator, reagent|Antonym: N/A|noun

litmus test:/ˈlɪtməs tɛst/| n.|石蕊试验，试金石|A test that uses litmus paper to determine the acidity or alkalinity of a solution; figuratively, a decisive test that reveals the true character or worth of something.|The election served as a litmus test of public opinion on the issue.  这次选举是对公众在这个问题上意见的试金石。|Synonym: acid test, barometer|Antonym: N/A|noun
