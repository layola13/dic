corneule:/kɔːrˈnjuːl/ | n. | 角质层细胞; 角膜小体 | A specialized flattened and keratinized cell of the stratum corneum, the outermost layer of the epidermis.  These cells are constantly shed and replaced. | The corneules form a protective barrier against environmental insults. 角质层细胞形成保护屏障，抵御环境侵害。 |  Corneules are essential for maintaining skin hydration. 角质层细胞对于维持皮肤水分至关重要。 | synonyms: horny cell, squame | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider basal cell, which is a cell from the deepest layer of the epidermis) | noun
