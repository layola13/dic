interconnectedness:/ˌɪntərkəˈnektɪdnəs/ |n.|相互关联，相互联系|The interconnectedness of global financial markets means a crisis in one country can quickly spread to others. 全球金融市场的相互关联意味着一个国家的危机可以迅速蔓延到其他国家。|例句：The interconnectedness of ecosystems is a key factor in maintaining biodiversity. 生态系统的相互关联性是维持生物多样性的关键因素。

例句：The growing interconnectedness of the world through the internet has both advantages and disadvantages. 互联网使世界越来越紧密地联系在一起，既有好处也有坏处。

近义词：interdependence, interrelationship, linkage, network, nexus|反义词:isolation, separation, disconnection|词性:noun

interconnect:/ˌɪntərkəˈnekt/ |v.|相互连接，相互关联|The different parts of the system interconnect to form a complex whole. 系统的不同部分相互连接，形成一个复杂的整体。|例句：The highways interconnect all the major cities. 高速公路连接了所有主要城市。

例句：The neurons in the brain interconnect to form complex neural networks. 大脑中的神经元相互连接形成复杂的神经网络。

近义词：link, connect, join, network|反义词:separate, disconnect, isolate|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- (prefix meaning "between, among, mutually") + connect + -ed + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality). "Connect" comes from the Latin "connectere," meaning "to bind together."
