embrue:/ɪmˈbruː/ | vt. | 沾染，染上（通常指鲜血或暴力）; 使浸透 | To stain or drench (usually with blood or violence); to steep or imbue | The battlefield was embrued with the blood of fallen soldiers. 战场被倒下的士兵的鲜血染红了。| 近义词: stain, drench, soak, saturate, imbue | 反义词: cleanse, purify | 词性: 动词

embrue:/ɪmˈbruː/ | vt. | 使充满（某种强烈的情感或品质）| To fill or pervade with a strong emotion or quality | His heart was embrued with grief. 他的内心充满了悲伤。| 近义词: fill, pervade, imbue, infuse | 反义词: drain, empty | 词性: 动词
