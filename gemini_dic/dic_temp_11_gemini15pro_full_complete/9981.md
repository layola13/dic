aphorismic:/ˌæfəˈrɪzmɪk/
adj.
格言式的；警句式的
terse and witty, like an aphorism; expressed in the form of an aphorism.
His aphorismic style makes his writing memorable. 他的格言式风格使他的作品令人难忘。
concise, pithy, epigrammatic, sententious
prolix, verbose, rambling
adjective
