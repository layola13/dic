heathenize:/ˈhiːðənaɪz/| vt.|使异教化，使不信教，使世俗化|To make heathen; to convert to heathenism or to make like a heathen in character or morals.|Missionaries worried about heathenizing influences on the native population. 传教士担心异教的影响会作用于当地居民。|近义词：paganize, secularize|反义词：Christianize, evangelize|词性:动词
ETYMOLOGY->
From heathen + -ize.  Heathen comes from Old English *hæþen "not Christian or Jewish," probably originally "dwelling on the heath" (where people remained unconverted after the Christianization of towns and villages).
