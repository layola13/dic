asterioidea: /ˌæstəriˈɔɪdiə/ | n. |海星纲|A taxonomic class of echinoderms that includes starfish.  They are characterized by a star-shaped body with five or more arms radiating from a central disc.|例句：Asterioidea are commonly found in tide pools. 海星纲动物常见于潮池中。

例句：The diversity of Asterioidea is remarkable. 海星纲的多样性非常显著。|近义词：starfish, sea star |反义词: N/A |词性:noun

Etymology: From Ancient Greek ἀστήρ (astḗr, “star”) + -o- + -idea.
