beefing:/ˈbiːfɪŋ/
verb
抱怨；发牢骚；争吵
complaining, grumbling, or arguing, especially persistently
例句：They were beefing about the long lines at the airport. 他们在抱怨机场排长队。
例句：He's always beefing about something. 他总是抱怨个不停。
近义词：complaining, grumbling, whining, arguing, quarreling
反义词：praising, complimenting
词性：v.
