orthodoxness:/ˈɔːrθədɑːksnəs/ |n.|正统性，正教性|The state of adhering to generally accepted or established doctrines, especially in religion; conventionality.|His orthodoxness in religious matters was well known. 他在宗教事务上的正统性是众所周知的。|近义词:conformity, conventionality, traditionalism|反义词:heterodoxy, unorthodoxy, unconventionality|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From orthodox + -ness.  "Orthodox" comes from the Late Latin "orthodoxus," from the Greek "orthodoxos," meaning "right-believing."  "Orthos" means "straight," "right," "correct," and "doxa" means "opinion" or "belief."  The suffix "-ness" is used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
