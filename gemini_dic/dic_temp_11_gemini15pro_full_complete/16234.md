banquette:/bæŋˈkɛt/ |n.|长椅，宴会|A long upholstered bench, often built into a wall, especially one used for seating in a restaurant.  A feast or banquet.|例句：The banquette in the corner provided a cozy spot for dining.  角落里的长椅提供了一个舒适的用餐场所。

例句：The king held a lavish banquette to celebrate his victory. 国王举行了盛大的宴会来庆祝他的胜利。|近义词：bench, sofa, settee, feast|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "famine" in the context of the "feast" meaning) |词性: noun
