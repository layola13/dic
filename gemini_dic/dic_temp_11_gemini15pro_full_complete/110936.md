lippiest:/ˈlɪpiɪst/
adjective
最爱发牢骚的，最爱抱怨的
Having a tendency to complain; whining or fretful.
例句：He was the lippiest child in the class, always complaining about something. 他是班上最爱发牢骚的孩子，总是抱怨这抱怨那。

例句：She's become so lippiest lately, it's hard to be around her. 她最近变得太爱抱怨了，很难和她相处。

近义词：complaining, whining, fretful, querulous
反义词：cheerful, content, happy, satisfied
词性: adjective

词源:  "Lippy" comes from "lip," referring to the movement of the lips when complaining or talking back.  The "-iest" suffix is a superlative ending, meaning "most."  Therefore, "lippiest" means "most lippy" or "most prone to complaining."
