outflying:/ˌaʊtˈflaɪɪŋ/
verb
胜过飞行；飞得比…远；飞得比…更久
surpassing in flight; flying farther or longer than
The young falcon was outflying its parent.  这只年轻的猎鹰飞得比它的父母更远。
近义词: outsoaring, excelling in flight
反义词: lagging, trailing in flight
词性: v.


outflying:/ˌaʊtˈflaɪɪŋ/
adjective
胜过飞行的；飞得比…远；飞得比…更久的
surpassing in flight; flying farther or longer than
His outflying skills were impressive. 他的飞行技巧令人印象深刻。

反义词: inferior in flight
词性: adj.
