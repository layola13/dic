archlute:/ˈɑːrtʃluːt/ | n. | 大键琴 | A large lute with additional bass strings, popular in the 17th century.  It's a plucked string instrument, larger than a typical lute. | The archlute produced a rich, resonant sound, favored for solo and continuo playing.  大键琴发出丰富而洪亮的声音，很适合独奏和持续演奏。| synonyms: chitarrone, theorbo | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but perhaps instruments with very different sounds, like a trumpet or drum) | noun
