overlanguaged:/ˌoʊvərˈlæŋɡwɪdʒd/
adjective
过分修饰的，语言冗赘的
Characterized by excessive or elaborate language; using more words than necessary.
The academic paper was dense and overlanguaged, making it difficult to understand the core argument. 这篇学术论文语言冗长且过于修饰，难以理解其核心论点。
The overlanguaged legal document was impenetrable to the average person. 这份措辞过度的法律文件对普通人来说难以理解。
近义词: verbose, prolix, wordy
反义词: concise, succinct, terse
词性: adjective
