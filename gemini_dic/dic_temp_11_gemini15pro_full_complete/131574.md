noncommutativity:/ˌnɒnkɒmjuːtəˈtɪvɪti/ /ˌnɑːnkɑːmjuːtəˈtɪvɪti/| n. |非交换性|The property of an operation where changing the order of the operands changes the result.  It means that  a * b does not equal b * a. |Matrix multiplication is a classic example of noncommutativity. 矩阵乘法是非交换性的典型例子。

The noncommutativity of quantum operators is a fundamental concept in quantum mechanics.  量子算符的非交换性是量子力学中的一个基本概念。|近义词:non-abelianity|反义词:commutativity|词性:noun

noncommutative:/ˌnɒnkɒmjuːtəˈtɪv/ /ˌnɑːnkɑːmjuːtəˈtɪv/| adj. |非交换的|Not commutative; referring to an operation where the order of the operands matters. |Matrix multiplication is noncommutative. 矩阵乘法是非交换的。

Rotation in three dimensions is noncommutative.  三维旋转是非交换的。|近义词:non-abelian|反义词:commutative|词性:adjective
