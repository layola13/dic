nonremunerative: /ˌnɒnriˈmjuːnərətɪv/ (British) /ˌnɑːnriˈmjuːnərətɪv/ (American)
|adj.|无报酬的，不赚钱的|Not providing any reward, especially financial.|Volunteering is a nonremunerative activity, but it is personally rewarding.  志愿工作是无报酬的活动，但在个人方面很有意义。|She took a nonremunerative position with the charity. 她在这个慈善机构担任了一个没有报酬的职位。|近义词: unpaid, unrewarded, voluntary|反义词: paid, remunerative, profitable|词性: adjective

nonremunerative:
|词源|From non- + remunerative.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "remunerative" means providing payment or reward.|
