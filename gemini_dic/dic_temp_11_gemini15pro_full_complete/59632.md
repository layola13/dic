ecclesiasticize:/ɪˌkliːziˈæstɪsaɪz/
v.
使教会化；使具有教会性质；用教会语言表达
To give a clerical or ecclesiastical character to; to imbue with the spirit, or invest with the forms, of the church; to convert to ecclesiastical uses.
The government attempted to ecclesiasticize the education system. 政府试图将教育系统教会化。
The writer ecclesiasticized the language of his novel.  这位作家用教会语言来写他的小说。
近义词: clericalize, spiritualize
反义词: secularize
词性: verb (动词)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ecclesiasticus + -ize.  *ecclesiasticus derives from Latin ecclesiasticus, from Ancient Greek ἐκκλησιαστικός (ekklēsiastikós, “of or belonging to the church”), from ἐκκλησία (ekklēsía, “assembly, especially a religious one; church”) + -ικός (-ikós, adjective-forming suffix).
