debility:/dɪˈbɪlɪti/
n.
虚弱，衰弱
physical weakness, especially as a result of illness
The illness left him with a general debility.  这种疾病使他身体十分虚弱。

近义词: weakness, frailty, infirmity, feebleness
反义词: strength, vigor, robustness


词源:
From Late Latin dēbilitas, from Latin dēbilis “weak,” from dē- “from, away” + habilis “able.”
