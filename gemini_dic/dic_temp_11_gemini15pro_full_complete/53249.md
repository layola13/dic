directrix:/daɪˈrektrɪks/ /dəˈrektrɪks/
n.
准线
A fixed line used in describing a curve or surface, especially a conic section.  The distance from any point on the curve/surface to the directrix bears a constant ratio to the distance from the same point to the focus (for a conic section).
The directrix of the parabola is parallel to the x-axis.  这条抛物线的准线平行于x轴。

directrix:/daɪˈrektrɪks/ /dəˈrektrɪks/
noun
准线
A fixed line used in the generation of a conic section.
The parabola is the locus of points equidistant from the focus and the directrix. 抛物线是与焦点和准线等距的点的轨迹。


近义词: (No direct synonym, but related terms) focal line, generating line
反义词: None
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *directrix (feminine of *director), from Latin dirigere "to direct, guide," from dis- "apart" + regere "to guide, rule."
