credits-to:/ˈkrɛdɪts tuː/| prepositional phrase | 归功于；感谢 | Used to acknowledge the person or entity responsible for something, especially in online content.  Indicates attribution or source. | Example:  This amazing artwork - credits to the talented @JaneDoe.  这幅惊人的艺术作品 - 感谢才华横溢的@JaneDoe。 | Synonym:  attribution to, courtesy of, thanks to | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but conceptually opposite could be "stolen from," "plagiarized from") | phrase
