outhires:/ˌaʊtˈhaɪərz/
|v.|外包；对外招聘|The company outhires its IT services. 这家公司将其IT服务外包。|
|例句：The company outhires its customer service to a call center in India. 这家公司将其客户服务外包给印度的一个呼叫中心。|
|例句：Many businesses outhire tasks like data entry and payroll processing. 许多企业将数据录入和工资处理等任务外包。|
|近义词：outsources, contracts out, subcontracts|
|反义词：insources|
|词性：动词|
