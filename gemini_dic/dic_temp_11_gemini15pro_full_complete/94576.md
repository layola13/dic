impoverishes: /ɪmˈpɒvərɪʃɪz/ /ɪmˈpɑːvərɪʃɪz/
| vt. | 使贫穷；使枯竭；使贫瘠 | To make poor; to deprive of strength, richness, or fertility. |  The ongoing drought has impoverished the soil, making it difficult to grow crops.  持续的干旱使土壤贫瘠，难以种植作物。 | depletes, exhausts, drains | enriches, enhances, improves | verb
