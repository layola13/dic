monsoonal:/mɒnˈsuːnl/ /mɑːnˈsuːnl/| adj.|季风的，受季风影响的|Relating to or caused by monsoons; affected by seasonal winds. |The region experiences monsoonal rains every summer.  该地区每年夏天都会经历季风雨。|近义词：rainy, wet, seasonal|反义词：dry, arid|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From monsoon + -al.  Monsoon comes from Portuguese monção, from Arabic mawsim 'season', related to wasama 'to mark, brand'.
