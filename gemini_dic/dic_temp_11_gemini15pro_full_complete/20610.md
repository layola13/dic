blackheads:/ˈblækˌhedz/| n.|黑头|A small, dark bump on the skin, formed by a blockage of a pore.  A blackhead is a plug of sebum (oil) and dead skin cells that has oxidized (reacted with oxygen) and turned dark. |例句：She has a lot of blackheads on her nose. 她鼻子上有很多黑头。

例句：He used a special cleanser to remove blackheads. 他用了一种特殊的洗面奶去黑头。|近义词：comedones, pimples|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider clear skin)|词性:noun
