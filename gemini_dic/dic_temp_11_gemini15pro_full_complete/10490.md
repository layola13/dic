appliqueing /əˈplɪkeɪɪŋ/
verb
缝贴，贴花，运用
The act of applying or sewing a piece of fabric onto another as decoration.
She is appliqueing flowers onto the quilt. 她正在把花贴到被子上。
近义词: attaching, affixing, sewing on
反义词: removing, detaching
词性: verb
