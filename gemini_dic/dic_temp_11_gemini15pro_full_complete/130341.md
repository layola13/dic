non-transparency:/ˌnɒn trænsˈpærənsi/ | n. | 不透明性，缺乏透明度 |  The lack of transparency in the government's dealings has led to public distrust.  This refers to a situation or process that is not open, clear, or easily understood. It implies a lack of access to information or a deliberate attempt to obscure information. |  The non-transparency of the company's financial records made investors nervous.  公司财务记录的不透明让投资者感到不安。| Synonyms: opacity, concealment, secrecy | Antonyms: transparency, openness, clarity | noun

non-transparent:/ˌnɒn trænsˈpærənt/ | adj. | 不透明的，不清晰的 | Not allowing light to pass through; not easily understood or open to public scrutiny. | The frosted glass is non-transparent, offering privacy to the bathroom. 磨砂玻璃不透明，为浴室提供了私密性。|  The non-transparent decision-making process angered the employees.  不透明的决策过程激怒了员工。 | Synonyms: opaque, obscure, unclear | Antonyms: transparent, clear, open | adjective
