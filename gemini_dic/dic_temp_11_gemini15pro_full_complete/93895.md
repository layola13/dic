immortalizable:/ɪˌmɔːrtəˈlaɪzəbl/
adjective
可使不朽的，可使永生的
Capable of being made immortal; able to be preserved or remembered forever.
His courageous acts were immortalized in a epic poem. 他的英勇事迹在一首史诗中被不朽化。
immortalizing, memorable, enduring
forgettable, transient
adjective
