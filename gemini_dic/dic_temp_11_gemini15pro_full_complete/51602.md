devastatingly:/ˈdevəsteɪtɪŋli/
adverb
毁灭性地；极度地
in a way that causes a lot of damage or destruction; extremely
The hurricane devastated the island devastatingly.  飓风毁灭性地摧毁了这个岛屿。
近义词: disastrously, catastrophically, ruinously
反义词: slightly, mildly, beneficially
词性: adverb

devastate:/ˈdevəsteɪt/
verb
摧毁；毁灭
destroy or ruin
The fire devastated the forest. 大火摧毁了森林。
近义词: destroy, ruin, wreck
反义词: build, create, repair
词性: verb

devastation:/ˌdevəˈsteɪʃn/
noun
毁灭；破坏
great destruction or damage
The earthquake caused widespread devastation. 地震造成了大范围的破坏。
近义词: destruction, ruin, wreckage
反义词: construction, creation, repair
词性: noun
