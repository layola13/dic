benchmark: /ˈbentʃ.mɑːrk/ (US) /ˈbentʃ.mɑːk/ (UK)| n.|基准；标准；水准线|A standard or point of reference against which things may be compared or assessed.|The company's profits this year exceeded the benchmark set last year.  这家公司今年的利润超过了去年设定的基准。|synonyms: standard, criterion, gauge, yardstick, touchstone|antonyms:  deviation, anomaly|noun

benchmark: /ˈbentʃ.mɑːrk/ (US) /ˈbentʃ.mɑːk/ (UK)| vt.|以…为基准；评估；衡量|Evaluate or check (something) by comparison with a standard.|We need to benchmark our performance against our competitors. 我们需要以竞争对手为基准来评估我们的业绩。|synonyms: evaluate, assess, measure, gauge|antonyms: disregard, ignore|verb
