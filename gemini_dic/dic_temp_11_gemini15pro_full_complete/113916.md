词源:  "Madwort" comes from the Old English "maedwyrt", where "maed" meant "meadow" and "wyrt" meant "plant" or "herb".  The name likely refers to the plant's habitat.  The association with madness is unclear, possibly related to traditional beliefs about its medicinal properties.
