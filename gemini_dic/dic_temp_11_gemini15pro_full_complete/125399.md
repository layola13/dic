multinuclear:/ˌmʌltiˈnjuːkliər/ | adj. | 多核的 | Having more than one nucleus.  This term is often used in biology and chemistry to describe cells or molecules with multiple nuclei. |  A multinuclear cell is a eukaryotic cell that has more than one nucleus per cell. 多核细胞是一种真核细胞，每个细胞中有多个细胞核。

The researchers used multinuclear NMR spectroscopy to study the structure of the compound. 研究人员使用多核核磁共振波谱来研究化合物的结构。 |  polynuclear | mononuclear, uninuclear | adjective
