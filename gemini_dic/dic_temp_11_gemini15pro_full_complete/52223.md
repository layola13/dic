diatomic:/ˌdaɪəˈtɒmɪk/ (US) /ˌdaɪəˈtɑːmɪk/ (UK)| adj.|双原子的|Consisting of two atoms.|Oxygen is a diatomic molecule. 氧气是双原子分子.|近义词:bimolecular|反义词:monatomic|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From di- (two) + -atomic (relating to atoms).  "Di-" comes from Greek "di-" meaning "twice, double". "-atomic" comes from Greek "atomos" meaning "indivisible".
