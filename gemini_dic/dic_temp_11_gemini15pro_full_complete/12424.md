askance:/əˈskæns/ /əˈskɑːns/| adv.|斜视地，怀疑地，不信任地|With a side-glance; with suspicion, mistrust, or disapproval.|He looked askance at the stranger's offer of help. 他怀疑地斜视着陌生人提供的帮助。|Synonyms: distrustfully, suspiciously, skeptically|Antonyms: trustingly, confidently|adverb

askance:/əˈskæns/ /əˈskɑːns/| adj.(古用法，罕见)|斜的|Sideways; oblique.|An askance glance. 斜视的一瞥 (这个用法很罕见).|Synonyms: oblique, sideways|Antonyms: straight, direct|adjective
