cyclotrons:/ˈsaɪklətrɒnz/ (US) /ˈsaɪklətrɒns/ (UK)| n. |回旋加速器|A type of particle accelerator in which charged particles are accelerated outwards from the center along a spiral path. |例句：The cyclotron is a powerful tool for research in nuclear physics. 回旋加速器是核物理研究的有力工具。

例句：Scientists use cyclotrons to create radioactive isotopes for medical purposes. 科学家使用回旋加速器制造用于医疗目的的放射性同位素。|近义词：particle accelerator 粒子加速器|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
