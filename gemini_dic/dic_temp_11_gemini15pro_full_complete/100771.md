intraverbal:/ˌɪntrəˈvɜːrbl/
| adj.| 言内行为的; 言内操作的| relating to verbal behavior that is controlled by other verbal behavior rather than by stimuli in the immediate environment.  In other words, it's a response to another verbal stimulus. |"The child's ability to answer 'four' when asked 'what is two plus two?' is an example of intraverbal behavior."  孩子被问到“二加二等于几？”时能够回答“四”是言内行为的一个例子。|近义词：verbal, linguistic|反义词: nonverbal|词性:adjective
