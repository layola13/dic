annalists:/ˈænəlɪsts/
n.
编年史家，年代史编者
A person who writes annals; a historian, especially one who records events year by year.
The annalists recorded the king's reign in meticulous detail.  编年史家一丝不苟地记录了国王的统治。
The annalists of the monastery kept detailed records of local events. 修院的编年史家详细记录了当地发生的事件。

近义词: chroniclers, historians
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "fiction writer" or "fabulist" as a contrasting concept)
词性: noun
