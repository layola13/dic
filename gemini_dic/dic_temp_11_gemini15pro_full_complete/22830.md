bosset:/ˈbɒsɪt/| n.|小老板；小主管；小包工头|A person in charge of a small group of workers, often used informally or dismissively. | He acts like a little bosset, ordering everyone around. 他表现得像个小包工头，对每个人颐指气使。|近义词：foreman, supervisor, overseer |反义词: worker, subordinate| noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French "bossette," a diminutive of "bosse" (boss, hump), implying a small protuberance or a minor authority figure. Ultimately from Frankish *bōs (a blow, swelling).
