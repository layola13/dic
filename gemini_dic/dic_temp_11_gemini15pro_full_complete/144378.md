outstorming:/ˈaʊtˌstɔːrmɪŋ/
n.
头脑风暴的一种变体，专注于挑战和质疑现有想法
A variation of brainstorming that focuses on challenging and questioning existing ideas.
The team engaged in an outstorming session to identify weaknesses in their current marketing strategy.  团队进行了一次“反向头脑风暴”，以找出他们当前营销策略中的弱点。
近义词：reverse brainstorming, critical thinking, devil's advocacy
反义词：brainstorming, ideation
