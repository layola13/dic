aquarius: /əˈkwɛəriəs/ | n. |水瓶座；宝瓶座| a constellation of the zodiac which is represented by a Water Bearer; the eleventh sign of the zodiac, relating to the period from about January 20 to February 18. |例句：Her zodiac sign is Aquarius. 她的星座是水瓶座。

|例句：Aquarius is an air sign. 水瓶座是风象星座。

|近义词：Water Bearer |反义词: (No direct antonym for a zodiac sign) |词性：noun
