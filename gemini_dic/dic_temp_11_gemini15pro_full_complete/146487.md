overgamble: /ˌoʊvərˈɡæmbəl/ | v. | 过度赌博，赌博成瘾 | To gamble excessively, often to the point of financial or personal harm.  | He overgambled and lost his life savings. 他过度赌博，输掉了毕生积蓄。 | She had a tendency to overgamble when stressed.  她压力大的时候容易赌博过度。 | 近义词: gamble excessively, compulsive gambling | 反义词: gamble responsibly, abstain from gambling | 词性: 动词
