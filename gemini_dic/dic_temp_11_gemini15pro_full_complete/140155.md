offended: /əˈfɛndɪd/ | adj. |  offended; resentful; feeling hurt | Feeling upset or hurt by someone's words or actions. |  He was deeply offended by her sarcastic remarks. 他对她的讽刺言论感到非常生气。

She felt offended that he hadn't called her back.  她因为他没有回电话而感到生气。

近义词: hurt, upset, insulted, aggrieved, resentful
反义词: pleased, delighted, flattered

词性: adjective
