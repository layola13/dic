contexts:/ˈkɒn.teksts/ | n. | 上下文；背景；环境 | The meaning of a word can change depending on its context.  一个词的意思会根据它的上下文而改变。 | Example: You have misinterpreted my words; you have taken them out of context. 你曲解了我的话；你断章取义了。 | Synonyms: background, circumstances, situation, setting | Antonyms: isolation, detachment | noun

/kənˈtekstʃu.ə.laɪz/ | v. | 把…置于背景中考虑；将…置于语境中 | To understand the novel, you need to contextualize it within the historical period in which it was written. 要理解这本小说，你需要把它放在它写作的历史时期来考虑。 | Example:  The professor contextualized the poem by discussing the poet's life. 教授通过讨论诗人的生平来解释这首诗的背景。 | Synonyms: place, situate, locate, position | Antonyms: decontextualize, abstract | verb
