nonincumbent:/ˌnɒnɪnˈkʌmbənt/ | adj. | 非现任的；不负有责任的 | Not holding an office or position currently; not subject to a duty or responsibility. |  The nonincumbent candidate surprised everyone by winning the election.  非现任候选人赢得了选举，让所有人大吃一惊。

The nonincumbent members were invited to observe the proceedings. 非现任成员被邀请旁听会议。 | 近义词: challenging, opposing | 反义词: incumbent | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From non- + incumbent.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "incumbent" comes from the Latin "incumbentem" (present participle of "incumbere"), meaning "to lie or lean upon," hence "to rest as a duty or obligation upon."
