consecutiveness:/kənˌsekjətɪvnəs/| n. |连续性，连贯性|The state of following continuously or without interruption.|The consecutiveness of the victories boosted the team's morale.  这些胜利的连续性鼓舞了球队的士气。|近义词：continuity, succession, sequence|反义词: discontinuity, interruption|词性:名词
Etymology: From consecutive + -ness. Consecutive from Latin *consecut-, past participle stem of consequi "to follow closely," from com- "together" (see com-) + sequi "to follow" (see sequel).
