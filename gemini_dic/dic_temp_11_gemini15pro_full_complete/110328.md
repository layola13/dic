lightering:/ˈlaɪtərɪŋ/
n.
减载;驳运
The act of transferring part of the cargo of a ship to another vessel, typically a barge or smaller ship, in order to reduce the ship's draft, enabling it to navigate shallower waters or to achieve a desired trim.
The ship underwent lightering operations before entering the port. 这艘船在进港前进行了减载作业。
offloading, unloading
loading
