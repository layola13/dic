cynegetics:/ˌsɪnəˈdʒɛtɪks/ | n. |狩猎学|The study or art of hunting.|He was a scholar of cynegetics, knowledgeable about all aspects of the chase.|Synonyms: hunting, venery|Antonyms: conservation, preservation|noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *cynegetica, neuter plural of cynegeticus (“of or pertaining to hunting”), from Ancient Greek κυνηγετικός (kunēgetikós, “of or pertaining to hunting”), from κυνηγέτης (kunēgétēs, “hunter”), from κύων (kúōn, “dog”) + ἄγω (ágō, “I lead”).
