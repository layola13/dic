optimisms: /ˈɒptɪmɪzəmz/ | n. |乐观主义，乐观情绪|The feeling of being hopeful about the future or the success of something; a tendency to expect good things to happen.|  She maintained her optimism despite the difficult circumstances. 她即使在困境中也保持乐观。|Synonyms: hopefulness, positivism, sanguinity| Antonyms: pessimism, negativity| noun


Etymology:
From Late Latin *optimismus, from Latin optimus (“best”).
