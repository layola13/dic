aplacental:/ˌeɪpləˈsɛntəl/
adjective
无胎盘的
Lacking a placenta; said of mammals of the infraclass Metatheria (marsupials) and Prototheria (monotremes), which do not develop a placenta during gestation.
Marsupials are aplacental mammals.  有袋动物是无胎盘哺乳动物。
Kangaroos are aplacental. 袋鼠是无胎盘的。
近义词: nonplacental
反义词: placental
词性: adjective
