nervimotor:/ˌnɜːrvɪˈmoʊtər/ (US), /ˌnɜːvɪˈməʊtə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|神经运动的|Relating to both nerves and muscles; concerning the activation of muscles by nerves.|The nervimotor system is responsible for coordinating movement. 神经运动系统负责协调运动。|The doctor tested the patient's nervimotor function. 医生检查了病人的神经运动功能。|近义词:neuromuscular|反义词: None|词性:形容词

词源: nervi- (from Latin nervus, meaning "nerve") + motor (from Latin mōtor, meaning "mover")
