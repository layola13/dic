herderite:/ˈhɜːrdəˌraɪt/ | n. | 羟基磷灰石 | A rare mineral, a fluophosphate of beryllium and calcium, found in crystals, usually yellowish or greenish-white. | Herderite is a sought-after mineral by collectors due to its rarity and beauty. 羟基磷灰石是一种受收藏家追捧的矿物，因为它稀有且美丽。| Synonyms: none (as it's a specific mineral name) | Antonyms: none | Noun
