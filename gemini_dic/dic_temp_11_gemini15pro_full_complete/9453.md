antireflectiveness:/ˌæntiˌrɪˈflɛktɪvnəs/
|n.|抗反射性，防反光性|The quality of being antireflective, meaning it reduces the reflection of light.  This is achieved by applying a thin coating to a surface, such as a lens or screen.|The antireflectiveness of the coating improved the image quality.|近义词:non-reflectivity, non-reflectance|反义词:reflectiveness, reflectivity|noun
