anodes:/ˈænəʊdz/ (UK) /ˈænˌoʊdz/ (US)| n. |阳极，正极|The positive electrode in an electrolytic cell or electron tube.  An electrode through which the conventional current enters into a polarized electrical device.|

例句：
Electrons flow from the cathode to the anode.
电子从阴极流向阳极。

The anode in a battery is the positive terminal.
电池中的阳极是正极。


近义词：positive electrode, positive pole|
反义词: cathode, negative electrode, negative pole|
词性: noun
