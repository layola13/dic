inspirative: /ɪnˈspaɪrətɪv/
adjective
鼓舞人心的；启发灵感的
tending to inspire or elevate
Her speech was truly inspirative, motivating many to pursue their dreams. 她的演讲 truly 鼓舞人心，激励了许多人去追求他们的梦想。
His paintings are very inspirative to me. 他的画作对我来说非常有启发性。
近义词: inspiring, motivational, uplifting
反义词: uninspiring, demotivating
词性: adjective
