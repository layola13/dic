immarcescible:/ˌɪmɑːrˈsɛsəbəl/
adjective
不凋谢的，不枯萎的
Not subject to decay; unfading.
The immarcescible beauty of her spirit captivated everyone. 她不朽的精神之美深深吸引了每个人。
Artificial flowers are immarcescible, unlike real ones.  人造花不像真花那样会凋谢，它们是不凋谢的。
近义词: unfading, everlasting, permanent, enduring, imperishable
反义词: fading, decaying, perishable, transient, ephemeral
词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin *immarcescibilis, from in- "not" + marcescere "to wither, droop, fade"  + -ibilis "-ible".
