akimbo:/əˈkɪmboʊ/| adj.|叉腰的，两手叉腰的|With hands on the hips and elbows bent outward.  Characterized by having the hands on the hips and elbows turned outward.|She stood with her arms akimbo, waiting impatiently. 她叉着腰不耐烦地站着。|Near synonyms:  hands-on-hips, askance, braced | Antonyms: straight, down|Adjective
