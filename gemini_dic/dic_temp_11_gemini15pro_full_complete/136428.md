nonretroactivity:/ˌnɒnˌrɛtrəʊækˈtɪvɪti/ /ˌnɑːnˌrɛtroʊækˈtɪvɪti/| n.| 非追溯性；不溯既往|The law has nonretroactivity, meaning it doesn't apply to past actions. 这项法律不溯既往，意味着它不适用于过去的行为。| The principle of nonretroactivity ensures that new laws do not punish actions that were legal when they were committed.  非追溯性原则确保新法律不会惩罚在实施时合法的行为。|retroactivity|non-applicability|noun
nonretroactive:/ˌnɒnˌrɛtrəʊˈæktɪv/ /ˌnɑːnˌrɛtroʊˈæktɪv/| adj.| 非追溯的；不溯既往的|The new tax law is nonretroactive. 新的税法不溯既往。| This policy is nonretroactive, so it only applies from today onwards. 这项政策不溯既往，因此仅适用于从今天开始。|retroactive|prospective|adjective
