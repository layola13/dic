humdinger:/ˈhʌmdɪŋər/ | n. | 惊人之作；极好的东西；出色的人 | something excellent or remarkable; an outstanding person | That new sports car is a real humdinger!  那辆新的跑车真是棒极了！|  She's a humdinger of a singer. 她是一位非常出色的歌手。 | 近义词: marvel, wonder, gem, knockout | 反义词: dud, flop | 词性: 名词

词源:  The word "humdinger" likely originated in the late 19th or early 20th century in American English. Its etymology is uncertain, but it may be related to "hum," suggesting something that makes a strong impression, and "dinger," a slang term for something excellent, possibly derived from baseball.
