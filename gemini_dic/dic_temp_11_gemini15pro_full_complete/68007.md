facto:/ˈfæktəʊ/ | adv. | 实际上，事实上 | in fact; actually.  Used to state what is really the case. | The trip was, in facto, a disaster.  这趟旅行实际上是一场灾难。| synonym: actually, effectively, practically, virtually | antonym: theoretically, hypothetically | adverb

facto:/ˈfæktoʊ/ | adj. | 事实的，实际的 (通常只用于短语 de facto) | Existing in fact, whether legally recognized or not. |  Their leader holds de facto control of the country.  他们的领导人实际上控制着这个国家。| synonym: actual, real, existing | antonym: theoretical, nominal, de jure | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin factō, ablative of factum ("deed, act, fact"), from past participle of facere ("to do, make").
