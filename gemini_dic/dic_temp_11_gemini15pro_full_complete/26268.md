buttonhole:/ˈbʌtnˌhoʊl/ (US), /ˈbʌtnˌhəʊl/ (UK)| n. v.|纽扣孔；扣眼；（用扣眼）固定；强留（某人）交谈|A small slit in a piece of clothing, designed to receive a button, used for fastening.  As a verb, it means to detain someone in conversation. |例句：The buttonhole on my coat is too small for this button. 我的外套上的扣眼对于这颗纽扣来说太小了。

例句：He buttonholed me as I was leaving the meeting. 我正要离开会议时，他把我拦住聊了起来。|近义词：(n.) eyelet, slit; (v.) accost, detain|反义词: N/A|词性:名词, 动词
