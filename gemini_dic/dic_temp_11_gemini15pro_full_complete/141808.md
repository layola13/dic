orbital:/ˈɔːrbɪtl/| n. adj.|轨道;眼眶的|A region of space around the nucleus of an atom where an electron is likely to be found.  Also refers to the path of a celestial body around another.|Electrons occupy orbitals around the atom's nucleus. 电子占据原子核周围的轨道。|The satellite entered a stable orbit around Earth. 卫星进入地球周围的稳定轨道。|Synonym: path, trajectory, circuit|Antonym: stationary, fixed|noun

orbital:/ˈɔːrbɪtl/| adj.|眼眶的;轨道的|Relating to the eye socket or an orbit.|The orbital bone was fractured in the accident. 事故中眼眶骨骨折了。|The spacecraft made an orbital flight around the moon.  航天器绕月球进行了一次轨道飞行。|Synonym: ocular, periorbital|Antonym: N/A|adjective
