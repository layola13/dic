bioplast:/ˈbaɪoʊplæst/| n. |生物塑料|A type of plastic made from renewable biomass sources, such as vegetable fats and oils, corn starch, or microbiota. |Bioplast is becoming a popular alternative to traditional plastics. 生物塑料正在成为传统塑料的流行替代品。|Bioplastics are derived from renewable resources, offering a more sustainable option. 生物塑料来源于可再生资源，提供更可持续的选择。| 近义词:biodegradable plastic, compostable plastic|反义词:conventional plastic, petroleum-based plastic| noun
