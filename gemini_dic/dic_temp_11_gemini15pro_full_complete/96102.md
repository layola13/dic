indefinite:/ɪnˈdɛfɪnɪt/| adj.|不定的；无限的；模糊的|Not clearly defined or determined; not precise or explicit; vague.|例句：He has been gone for an indefinite period. 他已经离开了一段不确定的时间。|例句：The project has been delayed for an indefinite period.  这个项目已经被无限期推迟了。|近义词：vague, uncertain, imprecise|反义词：definite, precise, specific|词性:形容词

indefinite:/ɪnˈdɛfɪnɪt/| n.|不定词；不定式|An indefinite word, phrase, or pronoun.|例句： "Someone" is an indefinite pronoun. “有人”是一个不定代词。|例句：Use an indefinite article before a singular countable noun. 在单数可数名词前使用不定冠词。|近义词：undefined|反义词：defined|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *indefinitus, from in- "not" + definitus "defined," past participle of definire "to limit, define."
