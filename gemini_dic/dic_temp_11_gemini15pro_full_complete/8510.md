antiburglary:/ˌæntiˈbɜːrɡləri/| adj.|防盗的，防窃的|Designed to prevent or deter burglary.  Relating to or intended to prevent burglaries.|例句：They installed an antiburglary system in their new house. 他们在新房子里安装了防盗系统。|近义词：theft-proof, burglar-resistant, security|反义词:  None (The opposite concept would be something that *facilitates* burglary, for which there isn't a common single word.)|词性: adjective
Etymology: "anti-" (prefix meaning "against") + "burglary" (noun meaning "the act of illegally entering a building with intent to commit a crime, especially theft")
