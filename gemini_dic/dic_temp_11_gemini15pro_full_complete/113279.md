lutein:/ˈluːtiːn/ | n. | 叶黄素 | A yellow carotenoid pigment found in the retina of the eye, egg yolk, and many yellow and orange fruits and vegetables. It is thought to protect the eye from damage caused by sunlight. |  Lutein is beneficial for eye health. 叶黄素对眼睛健康有益。|  Foods rich in lutein can help prevent macular degeneration. 富含叶黄素的食物可以帮助预防黄斑变性。 |  zeaxanthin, carotenoid |  N/A | noun
