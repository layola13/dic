aquila:/ˈækwɪlə/| n.|天鹰座；鹰|A constellation in the northern sky, representing an eagle.  It is one of the 48 constellations listed by the 2nd-century astronomer Ptolemy.|例句：Aquila is easily recognizable by its bright star Altair. 天鹰座很容易通过其明亮的恒星牛郎星辨认出来。

例句：The constellation Aquila is located near the celestial equator. 天鹰座位于天赤道附近。|近义词：eagle, bird (in a figurative sense)|反义词: (No direct antonym for a constellation)|词性:noun
