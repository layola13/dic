irefulness:/ˈaɪərfəlnəs/
n.
愤怒,暴怒
The state of being full of ire; extreme anger.
His irefulness was evident in his clenched fists and reddened face. 他的愤怒从他紧握的拳头和涨红的脸上一览无遗。
近义词:fury, rage, wrath, anger
反义词:calmness, peacefulness, tranquility
词性:名词

Etymology:
From ireful + -ness.  "Ire" comes from the Old French "ire," ultimately from the Latin "ira" meaning "anger."  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns expressing a state or quality.
