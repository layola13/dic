nonillustratively:/ˌnɒnɪˈlʌstrətɪvli/
adverb
非举例说明地，无例证地
Failing to illustrate; without illustration or example.
The author nonillustratively explained the complex theory, leaving the students confused. 作者没有举例说明就解释了这个复杂的理论，让学生们感到困惑。
The speaker nonillustratively described the process, making it difficult to understand. 演讲者没有举例说明地描述了这个过程，使其难以理解。
Synonyms: abstractly, generally, vaguely
Antonyms: concretely, specifically, illustratively
词性: 副词
