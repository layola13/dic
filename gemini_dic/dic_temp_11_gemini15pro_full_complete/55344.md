dissymmetry:/dɪˈsɪmɪtri/ |n.| 不对称，不对称性 |Lack of symmetry or absence of bilateral symmetry; the absence of or the deviation from symmetry.  It differs from asymmetry, which denotes a complete absence of symmetry. Dissymmetry is a deviation from perfect symmetry. | The crystal exhibits dissymmetry in its structure.  这种晶体结构呈现不对称性。| Near synonyms: asymmetry,  anisotropy, imbalance | Antonym: symmetry | Noun

dissymmetric:/ˌdɪsɪˈmetrɪk/ |adj.| 不对称的 |Not symmetrical; exhibiting dissymmetry; characterized by lack of symmetry. | The dissymmetric arrangement of the atoms contributed to the unusual properties of the molecule. 原子的不对称排列导致了分子的异常特性。| Near synonyms: asymmetrical,  anisotropic, unbalanced | Antonym: symmetrical | Adjective
