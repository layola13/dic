invalided:/ɪnˈvælɪdɪd/
adjective
因病退役的，因伤退役的
Disabled and discharged from military service.
He was invalided out of the army after being injured in battle. 他在战斗中受伤后因病退伍。
近义词:disabled, incapacitated
反义词:healthy, able-bodied
词性:adjective

词源: From the verb "invalidate" + "-ed" suffix.  Ultimately from Latin "invalidus" meaning "weak, infirm".
