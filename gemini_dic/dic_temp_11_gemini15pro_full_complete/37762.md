compassioned:/kəmˈpæʃənd/| adj.|充满同情心的，悲悯的|Moved by deep sympathy and sorrow for another who is stricken by misfortune; filled with pity.|She cast a compassioned glance at the beggar. 她对乞丐投以充满怜悯的目光。|近义词：sympathetic, empathetic, merciful, pitying|反义词：uncaring, apathetic, indifferent, callous|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from Old French compassionné, past participle of compassionner, from compassion.
