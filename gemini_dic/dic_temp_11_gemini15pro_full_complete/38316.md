concealment:/kənˈsiːlmənt/ | n. | 掩蔽，遮蔽物 | something that conceals | The dense foliage provided excellent concealment for the animals. 茂密的树叶为动物们提供了极佳的掩蔽。| synonyms: cover, screen, shelter | antonyms: exposure, openness | noun
