embalming:/ɪmˈbɑːmɪŋ/ /ɛmˈbɑːmɪŋ/
noun
防腐，(尸体)保存
the process of treating a dead body with chemicals to prevent it from decaying
例句：The embalming process preserved the body for the viewing.  防腐过程保存了尸体以供瞻仰。

例句：Embalming is a common practice in many cultures. 在许多文化中，防腐是一种常见的做法。

近义词：preservation, mummification
反义词：decomposition, decay
词性：noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *embaumen, from Old French embaumer, from en- + baumer "to balm," from Late Latin balsamum "balsam," from Greek balsamon "balsam, fragrant ointment."
