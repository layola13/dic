inspirers:/ɪnˈspaɪərərz/
n.
鼓舞人心的人; 激励者
People who inspire others; motivators.
例句：The inspirers of the new generation are often found online.  新一代的激励者通常在网上被发现。

例句：Her inspirers were her parents and teachers. 她的激励者是她的父母和老师。

近义词：motivators, role models, influencers
反义词：discouragers, deterrents
