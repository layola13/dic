fellows:/ˈfeləʊz/ (UK), /ˈfeloʊz/ (US)| n.|伙伴，同事，会员，家伙们|A group of people sharing a common interest, activity, or profession; comrades; members of a society or college.|例句：The fellows at the club welcomed the new member. 俱乐部里的会员们欢迎新成员。

例句：My fellows at work are very supportive. 我的同事们都很支持我。

近义词：friends, colleagues, associates, companions, members|反义词：enemies, opponents, adversaries|词性：名词(plural)

fellow:/ˈfeləʊ/ (UK), /ˈfeloʊ/ (US)| n.|伙伴，同事，会员，家伙|A person sharing a common interest, activity, or profession; a comrade; a member of a society or college.|例句：He's a good fellow. 他是个好人。

例句：She was elected a fellow of the Royal Society. 她被选为皇家学会会员。
