cease-fire: /ˈsiːsfaɪər/ | n. |停火; 停火协议| a temporary stopping of fighting, typically one during which peace talks take place |  A cease-fire was declared at midnight. 午夜宣布停火。| synonyms: truce, armistice | antonyms: war, hostilities | noun

cease-fire: /ˈsiːsfaɪər/ | v. |停火| to stop firing weapons | Both sides have agreed to cease fire. 双方同意停火。| synonyms: halt, stop | antonyms: attack, continue | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "cease" (from Old French "cesser", from Latin "cessare" "to stop") + "fire" (from Old English "fyr").
