outdueling: /ˌaʊtˈduːəlɪŋ/ /ˌaʊtˈdʊəlɪŋ/
|v.|战胜，在决斗中胜过|Defeating someone in a duel or competition, especially by being more skillful or clever.|He outdueled his opponent in the final round of the chess tournament. 他在国际象棋锦标赛的最后一轮战胜了对手。|Synonyms: outmaneuver, outsmart, defeat, conquer|Antonyms: lose to, be defeated by|verb

outdueling: /ˌaʊtˈduːəlɪŋ/ /ˌaʊtˈdʊəlɪŋ/
|n.|决斗胜利，战胜|The act of outdueling someone.|His outdueling of the champion was unexpected. 他战胜冠军的举动出乎意料。|Synonyms: victory, triumph|Antonyms: defeat, loss|noun
