coaly:/ˈkoʊli/| adj.|煤的；像煤的；含煤的|Resembling coal in color, texture, or composition.|The air was thick and coaly from the nearby factory. 空气中弥漫着附近工厂排出的浓浓煤烟味。|近义词：coal-black, carboniferous, sooty|反义词：bright, clean|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "coal" + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "full of").  "Coal" comes from Old English "col," which in turn comes from Proto-Germanic "*kula(n)," ultimately from Proto-Indo-European "*g(e)u-lo-" (glowing ember).
