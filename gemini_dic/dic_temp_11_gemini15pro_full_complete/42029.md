costlier:/ˈkɒstliər/ (UK) /ˈkɑːstliər/ (US)| adj.|更昂贵的，更代价高的|Comparative form of costly: more costly.  Indicates something is more expensive or comes at a greater price than something else.|例句：This new phone is costlier than my old one.  这款新手机比我的旧手机更贵。|例句：A costlier education doesn't always guarantee a better job. 更昂贵的教育并不总是能保证更好的工作。|近义词：more expensive, pricier, dearer|反义词：cheaper, less expensive, more affordable|词性:形容词
