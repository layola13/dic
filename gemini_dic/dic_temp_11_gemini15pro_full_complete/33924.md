cirrhiferous: /sɪˈrɪfərəs/
adjective
卷须的，有卷须的
Bearing cirri or tendrils.  Having curly or threadlike appendages.
Example: The cirrhiferous vine climbed the trellis.  卷须状的藤蔓爬上了棚架。
Example: The deep-sea creature had cirrhiferous appendages around its mouth.  这种深海生物的嘴周围有卷须状的附属物。
Near synonyms: tendrilled, cirrose, cirrate
Antonym: smooth, glabrous
词性: adjective
