jumped:/dʒʌmpt/ | v. | 跳跃; 猛增 | past tense of "jump" -  performed the action of jumping. |  She jumped over the fence. 她跳过了栅栏。| leap, bound, hop | crawl, walk | verb

Etymology->
From Middle English jumpen, from Old French jumper (“to leap, jolt, to gambol”), of uncertain origin, perhaps imitative.
