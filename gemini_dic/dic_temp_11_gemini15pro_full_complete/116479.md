materialistic:/məˌtɪəriəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
物质主义的；拜金的
Excessively concerned with material possessions; money-oriented.
She's so materialistic; all she cares about are designer clothes and expensive cars. 她太物质了；她只在乎名牌服装和豪车。
He's become very materialistic since he got his promotion. 他升职后变得非常物质化。
近义词: acquisitive, greedy, mercenary, grasping
反义词: spiritual, altruistic, ascetic
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From material + -istic.  "material" comes from Old French "materiel" and ultimately from Latin "materialis" (relating to matter).  "-istic" is a suffix used to form adjectives meaning "relating to" or "characterized by".
