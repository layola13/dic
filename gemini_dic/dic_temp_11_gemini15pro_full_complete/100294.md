interests:/ˈɪntrəsts/ (US), /ˈɪntrests/ (UK)| n.|兴趣，利益，利息|Something that concerns, involves, draws the attention of, or excites the curiosity of a person.|She has many interests, including reading and playing the piano. 她有很多兴趣爱好，包括阅读和弹钢琴。|近义词：hobbies, passions, benefits, stakes|反义词：disinterests, apathy|Noun

词源：From Anglo-French *interesse, from Medieval Latin interest, from Latin interesse “to be between, to make a difference, to concern, to be of importance,” from inter- “between” + esse “to be.”
