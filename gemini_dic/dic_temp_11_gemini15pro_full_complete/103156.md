jerseyans:/ˈdʒɜːziənz/
noun
泽西岛人；泽西牛
people from Jersey, one of the Channel Islands. Also, a breed of dairy cattle originally from Jersey.
Many Jerseyans are bilingual, speaking both English and French. 许多泽西岛人都会说两种语言，英语和法语。
The Jerseyans are known for their dairy farming. 泽西岛人以他们的奶牛养殖而闻名。
synonyms: islanders, Channel Islanders (for people) Jersey cattle (for cows)
antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a nationality or breed of cattle)
n.
