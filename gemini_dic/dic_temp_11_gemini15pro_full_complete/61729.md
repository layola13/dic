emotively:/ɪˈmoʊtɪvli/| adv.| 感情上地，情绪激动地 | Expressing strong feelings; in a way that shows emotion. | She spoke emotively about her childhood. 她充满感情地谈论了自己的童年。 | The musician played the piece emotively, bringing tears to the audience's eyes.  这位音乐家充满感情地演奏了这首曲子，使观众热泪盈眶。| 近义词：emotionally, feelingly, passionately | 反义词：apathetically, indifferently | 词性: 副词

词源:  From the adjective "emotive" + "-ly."  "Emotive" comes from "emotion" + "-ive."  Ultimately from the Latin "emovere" meaning "to move out, remove, agitate."
