ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + patriarch.  "anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to". "Patriarch" comes from Greek "patriarkhēs" meaning "head of a family or tribe," from "patēr" (father) + "arkhein" (to rule).
