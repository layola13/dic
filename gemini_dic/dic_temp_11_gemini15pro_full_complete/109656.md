leucitic:/luːˈsɪtɪk/| adj.|白榴的，富含白榴石的|Containing leucite, a white or grayish feldspathoid mineral.  A rock is described as leucitic if it contains a significant amount of leucite.|The lava flow was determined to be leucitic after analysis.  熔岩流经分析后被确定为白榴质的。|
The leucitic rock formed distinctive crystals. 白榴岩形成了独特的晶体。|Synonyms: feldspathoidal|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-leucitic)|adjective
