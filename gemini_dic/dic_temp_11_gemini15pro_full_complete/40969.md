coopery:/ˈkuːpəri/
n.
制桶业; 桶匠工艺; 制桶厂;
The art or occupation of making barrels, casks, and other similar wooden vessels.
He learned coopery from his father. 他从他父亲那里学习了制桶工艺。
近义词: barrel-making, cask-making
反义词:  (无直接的反义词)
词性: 名词


Etymology:
From Middle English *couperie, from Old French couperie (“cooperage”), from coupere (“cooper”), from cupe (“cask, tub”) (from Latin cupa (“tub, cask, vat”)).
