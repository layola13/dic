couching:/ˈkaʊtʃɪŋ/
couching: n. |表达方式，措辞；躺卧；缝纫；针刺术|The author's couching of the story in mystical terms made it more appealing. 作者用神秘的措辞表达这个故事，使它更具吸引力。|Synonyms: phrasing, wording, expressing, formulating |Antonyms: misrepresenting, distorting| noun
couching:/ˈkaʊtʃɪŋ/
couching: v. |表达，措辞；躺卧；缝纫；实施针刺术|He couched his refusal in polite terms. 他用礼貌的措辞表达了他的拒绝。|Synonyms: phrase, word, express, formulate |Antonyms: misrepresent, distort| verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French coucher "to lay down," from Latin collocare "to place, arrange," from com- "together" + locare "to place." The meaning shifted from "laying down" to "expressing" in the sense of "laying down words."  The medical meaning relates to the "laying down" of a cataract during surgery.
