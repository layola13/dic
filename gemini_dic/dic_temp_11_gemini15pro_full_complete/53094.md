diphthongation:/ˌdɪfθɒŋˈɡeɪʃən/ /ˌdɪpθɑːŋˈɡeɪʃən/
n.
双元音化；元音变为双元音
The process by which a single vowel sound changes into a diphthong.
The Great Vowel Shift in English involved a significant amount of diphthongation.  英语元音大推移包含大量的双元音化。
The diphthongation of the vowel in "house" is a characteristic of certain dialects. "house" 中元音的双元音化是某些方言的特征。
