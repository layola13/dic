nullipore:/ˈnʌlɪˌpɔːr/
n.
多孔石灰岩;多孔珊瑚;珊瑚藻
A rock or fossil composed of the calcareous skeletons of minute marine organisms.  A type of coral or coral-like alga.
Nullipores are important reef-building organisms in tropical waters.  珊瑚藻是热带水域重要的造礁生物。
Synonyms: coral, calcareous alga, rhodolith
Antonyms: N/A (No single antonym for a geological formation)
