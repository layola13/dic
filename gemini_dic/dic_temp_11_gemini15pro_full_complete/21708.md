boarding:/ˈbɔːrdɪŋ/ (UK), /ˈbɔːrdɪŋ/ (US)| n. |登机; 上船; 寄宿; 用木板覆盖|The act of getting onto a plane, ship, or other vehicle, or the provision of meals and lodging.|例句：Boarding will begin in 30 minutes.  登机将在30分钟后开始。|近义词：embarkation, entering|反义词:disembarkation, leaving|noun

boarding:/ˈbɔːrdɪŋ/ (UK), /ˈbɔːrdɪŋ/ (US)| adj. |寄宿的|Providing meals and lodging.|例句：She sent her children to boarding school. 她把她的孩子送到寄宿学校。|近义词：residential|反义词:day (school)|adjective
