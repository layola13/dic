multitool: /ˈmʌltiˌtuːl/ (US) /ˈmʌltiˌtuːl/ (UK)| n. |多功能工具|A hand tool that combines several individual functions in a single unit.  It typically includes a knife blade, pliers, screwdrivers, and other tools.|He always carries a multitool on his camping trips. 他在露营旅行中总是随身携带一把多功能工具。|Synonyms: multi-purpose tool, pocket tool|Antonyms: single-purpose tool|noun
