hydrosulphuret:/ˌhaɪdrəʊsʌlˈfjʊərɛt/ /ˌhaɪdroʊsʌlˈfjʊəˌrɛt/
noun
硫氢化物, 氢硫化物
A compound containing the HS− ion.  A sulfide containing an excess of hydrogen sulfide.  An old term for hydrosulfide.
The hydrosulphuret of ammonia is a useful reagent in chemical analysis. 硫氢化铵是化学分析中一种有用的试剂。
synonyms: hydrosulfide, bisulfide
antonyms: None (lack of a direct chemical antonym)
