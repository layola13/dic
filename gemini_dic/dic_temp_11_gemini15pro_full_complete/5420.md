almightily:/ɔːlˈmaɪtɪli/ | adv. |全能地，非常，极其| With great power; in a manner showing great power. | The wind blew almightily, tearing at the trees.  风刮得非常猛烈，撕扯着树木。|  The champion fought almightily to defend his title. 这位冠军奋力卫冕。 |Synonyms: powerfully, strongly, mightily, extremely, immensely | Antonyms: weakly, feebly, gently | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From "all" + "mighty" + "-ly". "All" intensifies the meaning of "mighty", which comes from Old English "mihtig" meaning "powerful" or "strong". The suffix "-ly" turns the adjective "mighty" into an adverb.
