depleter:/dɪˈpliːtər/| n.|消耗者，损耗者|Someone or something that depletes a resource or supply.  It reduces the amount of something available.|The ozone layer has been significantly affected by depleters such as chlorofluorocarbons (CFCs). 臭氧层受到了氯氟烃 (CFCs) 等消耗物质的显著影响。|synonyms: consumer, user, reducer, drainer|antonyms: replenisher, restorer, supplier, producer|noun


deplete:/dɪˈpliːt/| vt.|耗尽，消耗|To reduce the amount of something, especially a resource or supply.  It implies a significant decrease, often to a dangerously low level.|Overfishing has severely depleted fish stocks in the region. 过度捕捞严重耗尽了该地区的鱼类资源。|synonyms: exhaust, drain, reduce, diminish|antonyms: replenish, restore, increase, augment|verb
