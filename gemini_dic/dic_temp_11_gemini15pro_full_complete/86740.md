helotry:/ˈhɛlətri/| n.|奴役，农奴身份，奴隶状态|The system of helotry kept the peasants in a state of perpetual poverty and dependence.  这种农奴制度使农民长期处于贫困和依赖的状态。|Helotry was abolished in the 19th century.  农奴制在19世纪被废除了。|近义词：slavery, servitude, bondage|反义词：freedom, liberty|词性: 名词

Etymology: From Late Latin *helotrīa, from Late Greek ἡλωτεία (hēlōteía), from  Ἕλωτες (Hélōtes, “Helots”), the enslaved population of Laconia and Messenia. Ultimately from the verb ἑλεῖν (heleîn, “to capture”).
