nicheing:/ˈniːʃɪŋ/
n.|利基化，针对利基市场|The process of identifying and exploiting a niche market.  The act of creating, adapting to, or filling a niche.|
例句：Nicheing allows small businesses to compete with larger corporations. 利基化使得小企业能够与大公司竞争。

例句：The company's success is due to its skillful nicheing strategy.  这家公司的成功归功于其巧妙的利基化战略。
近义词：specialization, targeting, segmenting|
反义词:generalization, mass marketing|
词性:名词

nicheing:/ˈniːʃɪŋ/
v.|利基化，针对利基市场|Present participle of "niche," meaning to place or position in a niche; to establish a specialized market.|
例句：They are nicheing their product to appeal to a specific demographic. 他们正在将他们的产品利基化，以吸引特定的人群。

例句：The company is nicheing down to focus on a smaller, more profitable segment.  这家公司正在缩小利基市场，专注于更小、更有利可图的细分市场。
近义词：specializing, targeting, focusing|
反义词:generalizing, broadening|
词性:动词


词源: From niche + -ing.  Ultimately from Old French nichier "to make a nest."
