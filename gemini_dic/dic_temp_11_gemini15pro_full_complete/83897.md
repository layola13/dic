Example 2:  There's a growing community of half-Asian individuals sharing their experiences online.  越来越多的半亚裔群体在网上分享他们的经历。 | Synonym: biracial, mixed-race, multiracial | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but could consider) full-blooded, single-race |Etymology:  "half" (Old English healf) + "Asian" (from Asia, ultimately from Greek Asía) | word type: adjective
