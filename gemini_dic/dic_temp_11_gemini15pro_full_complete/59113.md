dylan:/ˈdɪlən/| n. |迪伦|A Welsh given name meaning "son of the sea" or "born from the ocean". It is derived from the Welsh elements "dy" meaning "great" and "llanw" meaning "tide" or "flow". It gained popularity due to the poet Dylan Thomas.|例句：Dylan is a popular name in Wales. 迪伦在威尔士是个很受欢迎的名字。
