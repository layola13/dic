dhansak: /ˈdænsæk/ | n. | 印度香辣炖菜；达萨克(一种印度菜肴，用扁豆、蔬菜和肉类炖制而成) | A spicy Indian stew made with lentils, vegetables, and meat, often lamb or mutton, flavored with a blend of spices. It often includes pineapple or other fruits for a sweet and sour flavor. |  We had a delicious dhansak for dinner last night. 昨晚我们晚餐吃了一顿美味的达萨克。|  Synonyms: curry, stew | Antonyms: None | Noun
