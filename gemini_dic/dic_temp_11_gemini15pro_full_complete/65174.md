ethnolinguistics:/ˌɛθnoʊlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪks/| n.|民族语言学|The study of the relationship between language and culture, and how they mutually influence each other.  It explores how language reflects and shapes cultural values, beliefs, and practices.|Ethnolinguistics investigates the ways language influences social structure. 民族语言学研究语言如何影响社会结构。

Ethnolinguistic research often focuses on indigenous communities. 民族语言学研究通常关注土著群体。|近义词：anthropological linguistics, sociolinguistics|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From ethno- (meaning "race, people, culture") + linguistics (the study of language).  "Ethno-" comes from the Greek word "ethnos" meaning "nation, people, race".
