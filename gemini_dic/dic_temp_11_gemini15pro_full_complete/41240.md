coramandel:/ˌkɒrəˈmændl/ | n. |科罗曼德尔海岸；科罗曼德尔木 |The Coromandel Coast is a southeastern coastal region of India. 科罗曼德尔海岸是印度东南沿海的一个地区。| The cabinet was made of beautiful coramandel wood.  这个橱柜是用美丽的科罗曼德尔木制成的。| Synonyms: Coromandel Coast (for the region), ebony, rosewood (for the wood) | Antonyms: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the Tamil கரமண்டலம் (karamanţalam), meaning "black sand shore".  It originally referred to the coast of southeastern India, and later to the hard, dark, decorative wood from trees of that region.
