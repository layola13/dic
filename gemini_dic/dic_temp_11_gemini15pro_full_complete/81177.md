rendezvous:/ˈrɑːndeɪvuː/ /ˈrɒndeɪvuː/
n.|约会，会合；会合地点|A prearranged meeting at a certain time and place.|We have a rendezvous at 7 p.m. 我们晚上7点有个约会。|近义词：meeting, appointment, gathering|反义词：separation, parting
v.|会合，约会|To meet at a prearranged time and place.|Let's rendezvous at the cafe. 我们在咖啡馆碰面吧。|近义词：assemble, meet, gather|反义词：separate, disperse
词性：名词，动词
