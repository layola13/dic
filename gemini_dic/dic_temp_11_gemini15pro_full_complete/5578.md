altarist: /ˈɔːltərɪst/
noun
祭坛侍奉者，圣器保管员
A person who serves at the altar, especially one who takes care of the sacred vessels and vestments.
The altarist prepared the chalice and paten for the Eucharist.  祭坛侍奉者为圣餐准备了圣杯和圣盘。
The altarist carefully placed the candles on the altar.  圣器保管员小心翼翼地把蜡烛放在祭坛上。
Synonyms: acolyte, server
Antonyms: None
