deviousness:/ˈdiːviəsnəs/
noun
狡猾；阴险；迂回
the quality of being devious; indirectness or deceitfulness.
Her deviousness was evident in the way she manipulated the situation to her advantage. 她的狡猾体现在她操纵局势为自己谋利的方式上。
近义词: cunning, slyness, craftiness, guile, trickery
反义词: honesty, straightforwardness, forthrightness, sincerity
词性: noun

devious:/ˈdiːviəs/
adjective
狡猾的；阴险的；迂回的
showing a skillful use of underhanded tactics to achieve goals.
He took a devious route to avoid the traffic. 他走了一条迂回的路线以避开交通堵塞。
近义词: cunning, sly, crafty, guileful, tricky
反义词: honest, straightforward, direct, frank
词性: adjective
