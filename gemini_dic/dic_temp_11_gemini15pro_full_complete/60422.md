eightier:/ˈeɪtɪər/| adjective|八十多岁的，八十年代的|Of or relating to the eighties (years, people born then, etc.).|例句：She's in her eightier years. 她八十多岁了。

例句：The music of the eightier was very distinct. 八十年代的音乐非常独特。|近义词：octogenarian (for people)|反义词: (No direct antonym; could use younger, earlier depending on context)|词性: adjective

词源:  From the cardinal number eighty + the suffix -ier, which forms adjectives indicating a decade.  Ultimately from Old English *eahta + tig (eighty).
