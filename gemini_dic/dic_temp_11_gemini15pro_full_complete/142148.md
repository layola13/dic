oriol:/ˈɔːriəl/ (US), /ˈɒriəl/ (UK)| n.|金莺，黄鹂|A bright-colored songbird, typically with yellow and black plumage.|例句：The oriole built its nest high in the elm tree. 金莺在榆树高处筑巢。

例句：We saw a beautiful oriole flitting among the blossoms. 我们看到一只美丽的黄鹂在花丛中飞舞。

近义词：songbird, blackbird|反义词:|n.
