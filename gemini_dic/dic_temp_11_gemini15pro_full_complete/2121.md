adaptations:/ˌædæpˈteɪʃənz/
n.
改编；适应；改写本
the action or process of adapting or being adapted.
Polar bears have several adaptations that help them survive in the Arctic. 北极熊有几种适应性特征，帮助它们在北极生存。

The movie is an adaptation of a popular novel. 这部电影是由一部受欢迎的小说改编的。
近义词: modifications, adjustments, alterations, conversions
反义词: originals
