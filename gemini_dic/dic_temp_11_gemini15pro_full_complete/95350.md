incentivizes:/ɪnˈsɛntɪvaɪzɪz/ | v. | 激励；刺激 | To motivate or encourage someone to do something, typically by providing a reward or avoiding a penalty. | The bonus structure incentivizes employees to work harder. 奖金制度激励员工更加努力工作。|  The new law incentivizes companies to reduce their carbon emissions. 新法律鼓励公司减少碳排放。 | synonyms: motivates, encourages, stimulates, prompts | antonyms: discourages, deters, hinders | verb
