archaic:/ɑːrˈkeɪɪk/ | adj. | 古代的；过时的；陈旧的 | very old or old-fashioned |例句：The company's computers are archaic.  这家公司的电脑很老旧。

例句：'Thou art' is an archaic form of 'you are'. “Thou art”是“you are”的古体。| 近义词：ancient, obsolete, outdated, antiquated | 反义词：modern, contemporary, current | 词性: adjective

Etymology->
From Late Latin *archaicus, from Ancient Greek ἀρχαϊκός (arkhaïkós, “ancient, old-fashioned, primitive”), from ἀρχαῖος (arkhaîos, “ancient, from the beginning”), from ἀρχή (arkhḗ, “beginning, origin, first principle”).
