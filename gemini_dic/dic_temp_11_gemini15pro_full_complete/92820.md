iconographist: /ˌaɪkənˈɒɡrəfɪst/ (US) /ˌaɪkəˈnɒɡrəfɪst/ (UK)
n.
图像学家；图像志学者；圣像画家
A specialist in the study and interpretation of iconography (the symbolic meaning of images and artistic representations).
The iconographist explained the hidden meanings in the painting. 图像学家解释了这幅画的隐藏含义。

The iconographist identified the figures in the ancient mosaic. 图像学家辨认出了古代马赛克画中的人物。

近义词: art historian, symbologist, image analyst
反义词:  (No direct antonym; could consider someone who *disregards* symbolic meaning)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *iconographe*, from *icono-* (from Greek *eikōn* "image") + *-graphe* (from Greek *graphē* "writing, description").
