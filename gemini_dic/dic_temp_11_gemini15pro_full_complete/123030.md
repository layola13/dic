mondayishness:/ˈmʌndeɪɪʃnɪs/
noun
星期一综合症；星期一状态
The feeling of weariness, gloom, or lack of enthusiasm that often accompanies the return to work or school after the weekend.
I'm suffering from a severe case of mondayishness today. 我今天星期一综合症很严重。
近义词: Monday blues, post-weekend blues
反义词: weekend euphoria, Friday feeling
词性: noun
