consignors:/kənˈsaɪnərz/
n.
发货人；委托人；寄售人
People or companies who deliver goods to another party for sale.  Those who entrust something to another's care, typically for sale or safekeeping.
The consignors shipped their goods to the auction house.  发货人将货物运送到拍卖行。
Many local farmers are consignors at the farmer's market. 许多当地农民是农贸市场的寄售者。
synonyms: shippers, vendors, sellers, depositors
antonyms: consignees, buyers, recipients
noun
