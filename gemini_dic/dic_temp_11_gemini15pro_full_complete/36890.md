colorfulness:/ˈkʌlərfəlnəs/| n. |色彩鲜艳；丰富多彩|The state or quality of being full of bright colors or a variety of colors.|The colorfulness of the coral reef amazed the divers. 珊瑚礁的缤纷色彩使潜水员惊叹不已。|近义词：vibrancy, brilliance, vividness|反义词:dullness, drabness|词性:noun
Etymology: color + -ful + -ness.  From "color" (meaning hue or tint), with the suffix "-ful" added to indicate abundance or possession of a quality, and finally the suffix "-ness" to create a noun denoting the state or quality of being colorful.
