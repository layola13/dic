词源： From French clausure, from Latin clausūra (“a closing, a shutting up”), from clausus, perfect passive participle of claudere (“to close, to shut”).

注意事项: Clausure is less common than "closure" in general usage.  While they share etymological roots and similar meanings, "closure" is the preferred term in most contexts. Clausure is more likely to be found in formal or legal settings.
