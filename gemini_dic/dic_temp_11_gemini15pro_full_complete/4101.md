aimers:/ˈeɪmərz/
noun
瞄准者; 致力于…的人
People who aim at something; those who strive for a particular goal.
The aimers on the rifle range were all highly skilled.  步枪射击场上的瞄准者都非常熟练。
synonyms: aspirants, strivers, contenders
antonyms: quitters, non-participants
n.
