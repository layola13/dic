arm-twisted:/ɑːrm-ˈtwɪstɪd/
adjective
被迫的，被胁迫的
persuaded to do something against one's will, by means of coercion or pressure.
He felt arm-twisted into signing the contract. 他感觉被迫签了合同。
Synonyms: coerced, pressured, forced, compelled
Antonyms: willing, voluntary
词性: 形容词
