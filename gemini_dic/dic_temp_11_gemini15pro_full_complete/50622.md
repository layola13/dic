derelict:/ˈdɛrəlɪkt/
noun
被遗弃的人; 被遗弃的船
a person without a home, job, or property.
a ship abandoned at sea.
The city shelters are full of derelicts. 城市的收容所里到处都是流浪汉。
The derelict ship drifted aimlessly in the ocean. 被遗弃的船只在海上漫无目的地漂流。
Synonyms: vagrant, bum, tramp; wreck
Antonyms: employed person, homeowner; functioning ship
