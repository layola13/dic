ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + elegant.  "Over-" acts as a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much." "Elegant" comes from the French "élégant," ultimately derived from the Latin "eligere" meaning "to choose" or "select," implying a careful selection of style and refinement.
