overhappy:/ˌoʊvərˈhæpi/| adj.|过于快乐的，欣喜若狂的|Excessively happy; extremely joyful.|She was overhappy to hear the good news. 她听到这个好消息欣喜若狂。|He was overhappy about winning the lottery. 他中了彩票高兴得不得了。|近义词：ecstatic, overjoyed, thrilled|反义词：sad, unhappy, miserable|词性:形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "happy" (adjective).  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "happy" to indicate an excessive degree of happiness.
