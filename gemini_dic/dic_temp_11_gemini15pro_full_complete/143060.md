outborn:/ˈaʊtbɔːrn/
|adj.|天生的，生来的|Existing from birth; innate or congenital.|例句：Her musical talent is outborn. 她的音乐天赋是天生的。|近义词：innate, congenital, natural, inherent|反义词：acquired, learned|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ūtborn, from ūt (out) + geboren (born).  Cognate with Dutch uitgeboren and German ausgeboren.
