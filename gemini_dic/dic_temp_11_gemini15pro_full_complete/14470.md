avalanching:/ˈævəlɑːntʃɪŋ/ | v. | 崩塌，像雪崩一样迅速增加；涌现 | A sudden, overwhelming rush or accumulation of something; increasing rapidly like an avalanche. | The number of complaints about the product is avalanching.  产品投诉的数量像雪崩一样迅速增加。| Synonyms: surging, escalating, mushrooming | Antonyms: dwindling, decreasing, diminishing | verb

avalanche:/ˈævəlɑːnʃ/ | n. | 雪崩；大量涌现的事物 | A large mass of snow, ice, and rocks sliding down a mountainside; a sudden overwhelming amount of something. | The avalanche buried the village. 雪崩掩埋了村庄。| An avalanche of emails flooded his inbox. 大量的电子邮件涌入他的收件箱。| Synonyms: landslide, deluge, flood, torrent | Antonyms: trickle, dribble | noun
