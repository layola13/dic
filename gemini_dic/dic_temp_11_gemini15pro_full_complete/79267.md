glasswort:/ˈɡlæswɜːrt/ | n. | 海蓬子，碱蓬 | A succulent, salty plant that grows in salt marshes and is sometimes used as a vegetable or for making soda ash. |Glasswort thrives in salty coastal areas. 海蓬子在含盐的沿海地区茁壮生长。| Glasswort can be pickled and eaten as a salty snack. 海蓬子可以腌制后作为咸味小吃食用。 |Synonyms: samphire, saltwort| Antonyms: N/A | Noun
