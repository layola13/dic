Example 2:  He was a dedicated newwaver, collecting every vinyl record released by his favorite bands. 他是一个忠实的新浪潮乐迷，收集了他最喜欢的乐队发行的每一张黑胶唱片。 |Synonym: new wave musician, new wave fan, post-punk artist|Antonym: traditionalist, conservative (in terms of musical taste)|Noun
