broadwayites: /ˈbrɔːdweɪˌaɪts/
noun
百老汇人；百老汇的常客；百老汇的演员或工作人员
People associated with Broadway theater, including actors, producers, stagehands, and frequent audience members.
Broadwayites often gather at Sardi's after a show. 百老汇人经常在一场演出结束后聚集在萨迪餐厅。
The seasoned broadwayites knew all the backstage gossip.  经验丰富的百老汇人知道所有后台的八卦。
近义词: theater people, Broadway enthusiasts
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps)  non-theatergoers
