circumflexion:/ˈsɜːrkəmfleksən/ | n. |抑扬符，音调符号 | A mark (^) placed over a vowel to indicate pronunciation, contraction, or omission of a letter.  It can also refer to the rising and falling intonation of speech. | Example 1: The word "crèche" has a circumflexion over the "e".  单词 "crèche" 中字母 "e" 上面有一个抑扬符。 | Example 2:  His speech was marked by a dramatic circumflexion, rising and falling with the emotion of his story.  他的演讲以戏剧性的抑扬顿挫为标志，随着故事的情感起伏而起伏。 | Synonym:  diacritic, accent | Antonym: (No direct antonym) | noun

circumflex:/ˈsɜːrkəmfleks/ | adj. |抑扬的，弯曲的 | Bent or curved; marked with a circumflex. | Example 1: A circumflex accent.  抑扬重音。 | Example 2:  The bird had a circumflex wing.  这只鸟的翅膀是弯曲的。 | Synonym: curved, arched | Antonym: straight | adjective
