dent's:/dɛnts/| n.|凹痕；减少；削弱；（数量、程度上）下降；（价格）折扣|A dent is a hollow in the surface of something, usually caused by a blow. It can also refer to a reduction in amount or degree, or a discount in price.|例句：The car had a dent in the fender. 汽车的挡泥板上有个凹痕。|近义词：depression, hollow, indentation, reduction, decrease, discount|反义词:increase, rise, bulge, protrusion|词性:名词(通常作为所有格形式出现)

词源: From Old English *dynt, from Proto-Germanic *duntiz ("blow, stroke"), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰen- ("to hit, push, thrust").
