carse:/kɑːrs/| n. |低洼肥沃的冲积地；(苏格兰)低洼而肥沃的土地，通常靠近河流|A fertile, low-lying area of alluvial land, typically by a river (especially in Scotland).|例句：The carse lands along the river were ideal for farming. 河边的低洼肥沃土地是耕种的理想之地。

例句：The farmers of the carse grew abundant crops. 低洼肥沃土地上的农民种植了大量的农作物。


近义词：lowland, valley, plain, meadow|反义词: highland, upland|词性:noun

词源: From Scots <i>cars</i>, <i>kerse</i>, probably from a North Germanic source akin to Old Norse <i>kjarr</i> (“marsh, brushwood”). Cognate with Icelandic <i>kjarr</i> (“brushwood, copse”), Faroese <i>kjarr</i> (“copse”), Norwegian <i>kjerr</i> (“brushwood, copse”).
