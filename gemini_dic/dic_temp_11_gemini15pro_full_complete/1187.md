acclimatizers: /əˈklaɪməˌtaɪzərz/
noun
适应者；驯化者
People or organisms that acclimatize, or adjust to new climates or environments.
The acclimatizers introduced new species of plants to the region.  这些适应者向该地区引进了新的植物品种。
Mountaineers are often skilled acclimatizers, adapting to high altitudes.  登山者通常是熟练的适应环境者，能够适应高海拔地区。
synonyms: adapters, adjusters
antonyms: non-adapters
