ankylosaurs:/ˈæŋkɪləˌsɔːrz/ | n. | 甲龙 |  A group of armored dinosaurs, herbivores, characterized by bony plates and spikes covering their bodies, and often having a club-like tail. | Ankylosaurs were heavily armored and could defend themselves against predators. 甲龙 heavily armored，可以抵御捕食者。| synonyms: armored dinosaurs, thyreophorans | antonyms: (no direct antonym, perhaps lightly armored dinosaurs, though not a taxonomic group) | noun


ankylosaur:/ˈæŋkɪləˌsɔːr/ | n. | 甲龙 | A single member of the ankylosaur group.  | The museum displayed a complete ankylosaur skeleton. 博物馆展出了一具完整的甲龙骨架。| synonyms: armored dinosaur, thyreophoran | antonyms: (no direct antonym) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ankulos*  meaning "bent" or "curved" (referring to the stiffening of the back) + *sauros* meaning "lizard".
