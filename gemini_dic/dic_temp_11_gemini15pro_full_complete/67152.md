exposes:/ɪkˈspoʊzɪz/| v.|暴露，揭露，使曝光|Third-person singular present tense form of the verb "expose."  It means to uncover, reveal, or make something visible that was previously hidden or unknown.|The documentary exposes the corruption within the company. 这部纪录片揭露了公司内部的腐败。|Synonyms: reveals, uncovers, discloses|Antonyms: conceals, hides, protects|verb

expose:/ɪkˈspoʊz/| v.|暴露，揭露，使曝光|To uncover, reveal, or make something visible that was previously hidden or unknown.  Can also mean to leave something unprotected from the elements or to introduce someone to a new experience or idea.|She exposed the truth about the politician's lies. 她揭露了这位政客谎言的真相。|Synonyms: reveals, uncovers, discloses|Antonyms: conceals, hides, protects|verb

expose:/ɪkˈspoʊz/| n.|暴露，揭露，揭发|The act of exposing something or the state of being exposed. Less commonly used as a noun than a verb.|The expose of the scandal ruined his career.  这起丑闻的曝光毁了他的事业。|Synonyms: revelation, disclosure, uncovering|Antonyms: concealment, cover-up, protection|noun
