clincher:/ˈklɪntʃər/| n.|定论；敲定；决定性因素；钳子；尖嘴钳|A fact, argument, or event that settles a matter conclusively.|Her testimony provided the clincher that proved he was guilty. 她的证词提供了证明他有罪的决定性证据。|synonyms: deciding factor, conclusive argument, key point, nail in the coffin|antonyms: inconclusive evidence, weak argument|noun


clincher:/ˈklɪntʃər/| v.|敲弯钉尖；用钳子夹住；敲弯（钉尖）使其固定；最终确定；（非正式）拥抱|To settle (an argument, agreement, etc.) decisively.|The new evidence clinched the case for the prosecution. 新的证据最终确定了控方的案件。|synonyms: settle, conclude, decide, secure, confirm|antonyms: unsettle, reopen, question|verb
