fungiform:/ˈfʌŋdʒɪfɔːrm/ | adj. | 菌状的，蘑菇形的 | Resembling a fungus in shape; specifically, designating one of the papillae on the upper surface of the tongue. | The fungiform papillae are scattered over the surface of the tongue and are mushroom-shaped. 舌背上的菌状乳头呈蘑菇状散布。| synonymous: mushroom-shaped, club-shaped | antonymous: filiform | adjective

fungiform:/ˈfʌŋdʒɪfɔːrm/ | n. | 菌状体 | A structure shaped like a fungus. | The taste buds are located on the fungiform papillae. 味蕾位于菌状乳头上。 | synonymous: mushroom body | antonymous: N/A | noun
