indoline: /ˈɪndəˌlin/ /ˈɪndoʊlin/ | n. | 吲哚啉 | A heterocyclic organic compound with the formula C8H9N. It is a bicyclic structure, consisting of a six-membered benzene ring fused to a five-membered nitrogen-containing pyrrole ring.  It is the reduced form of indole. | Indoline is a common structural motif in many alkaloids and pharmaceuticals. | synonym: 2,3-dihydroindole | antonym: N/A | noun
