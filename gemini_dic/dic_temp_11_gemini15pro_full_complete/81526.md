gravitated:/ˈɡrævɪteɪtɪd/| v.|被吸引；受吸引移动|To move towards something, as if by an invisible force. |例句：People naturally gravitated towards the charismatic speaker. 人们自然而然地被这位富有魅力的演说家所吸引。

例句：The conversation gravitated toward politics.  谈话不知不觉地转到了政治话题。|近义词：moved, drifted, inclined, tended|反义词：repelled, avoided|词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *gravitatus*, past participle of *gravitare* "to weigh down, press heavily," from *gravis* "heavy."
