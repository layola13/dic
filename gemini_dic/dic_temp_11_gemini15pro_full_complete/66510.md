exfoliative:/ɛksˈfoʊliˌeɪtɪv/
adjective
脱落的；剥落的
Characterized by or tending to cause exfoliation (the shedding of something in scales or layers).
Exfoliative dermatitis is a severe inflammatory skin reaction.  剥脱性皮炎是一种严重的炎症性皮肤反应。
Exfoliative cytology involves the microscopic examination of cells shed from body surfaces.  剥脱细胞学涉及对从体表脱落的细胞进行显微镜检查。
Synonyms: peeling, shedding, scaling, flaking
Antonyms: adherent, attached
词性: adjective
