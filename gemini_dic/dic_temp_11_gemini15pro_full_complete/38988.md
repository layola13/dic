confirmation:/ˌkɒnfəˈmeɪʃən/ (UK) /ˌkɑːnfərˈmeɪʃən/ (US)| n.|确认，证实，证明|The act of confirming something or the state of being confirmed.  A statement or piece of evidence that confirms something.|We are awaiting confirmation of the test results. 我们正在等待测试结果的确认。|Synonyms: verification, validation, corroboration, affirmation|Antonyms: denial, refutation, contradiction|noun
