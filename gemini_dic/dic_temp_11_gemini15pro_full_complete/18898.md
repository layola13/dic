besmearer:/bɪˈsmɪrər/
n.
涂抹者；诽谤者
One who besmears something or someone.  A person who smears something on a surface or who smears someone's reputation.
The besmearer of the wall with graffiti was caught on camera.  在墙上涂鸦的人被摄像头拍到了。
The politician denounced his opponent as a besmearer of his good name.  这位政客谴责他的对手玷污了他的好名声。
smearer, dauber, slanderer, defamer
