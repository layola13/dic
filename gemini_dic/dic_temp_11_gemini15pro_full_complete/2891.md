advertisers:/ˈædvərtaɪzərz/
n.
广告商，广告客户
People or companies that pay to advertise their products or services.
Large advertisers are often keen to sponsor popular television programmes.  大型广告商往往热衷于赞助受欢迎的电视节目。
synonyms: sponsors, promoters, marketers
antonyms: consumers, audience, viewers
noun
