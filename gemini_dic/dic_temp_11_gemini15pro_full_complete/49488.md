delve:/dɛlv/| v. |探究，钻研，深入研究|To research something thoroughly; to dig deeply into a subject. |例句：The historian delved into the archives, searching for clues. 这位历史学家深入档案馆，寻找线索。

例句：She delved into her handbag for her keys. 她把手伸进手提包里找钥匙。|近义词：explore, investigate, probe, examine, research|反义词: skim, ignore, neglect|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *delven*, from Old English *delfan* (“to dig, delve”), from Proto-Germanic *delbaną* (“to dig”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰelbʰ- (“to dig, bury”). Cognate with Dutch *delven* (“to dig”), German *delben* (“to dig a ditch, to mine”), Icelandic *delfa* (“to dig, delve”).
