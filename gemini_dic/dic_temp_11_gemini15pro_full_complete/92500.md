hypothesis:/haɪˈpɒθɪsɪs/ (US) /haɪˈpɑːθəsɪs/ (UK)| n. |假设，假说|A proposed explanation for a phenomenon that can be tested through further investigation.|Scientists are testing the hypothesis that the universe is constantly expanding. 科学家们正在验证宇宙不断膨胀的假说。|The hypothesis that stress causes ulcers has been widely studied. 压力导致溃疡的假说已被广泛研究。|近义词：theory, assumption, supposition, conjecture|反义词：fact, proof, reality|词性:noun

Etymology: From Late Latin hypothesis, from Ancient Greek ὑπόθεσις (hupóthesis, “a placing under, foundation, base, supposition”), from ὑποτίθημι (hupotíthēmi, “I place under”), from ὑπό (hupó, “under”) + τίθημι (títhēmi, “I put, place”).
