mini-condo:/ˈmɪni ˈkɑːndoʊ/ (US) /ˈmɪni ˈkɒndoʊ/ (UK)| n. |小型公寓|A small condominium, typically less spacious than a standard condo unit.  It often caters to single occupants or couples and may be more affordable.|She just bought a mini-condo in the city center, perfect for her busy lifestyle. 她刚在市中心买了一套小型公寓，非常适合她忙碌的生活方式。|Example:  The mini-condo was perfect for a student.  这个小型公寓对学生来说很完美。|Synonym: studio apartment, small apartment, efficiency apartment|Antonym: mansion, large house|Noun
