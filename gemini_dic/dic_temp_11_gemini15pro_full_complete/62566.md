energize:/ˈɛnədʒaɪz/ /ˈenərdʒaɪz/ | v. |  使充满活力，激励；供给…能量 | To give energy or vigor to; to invigorate. |  The coach's speech energized the team before the big game. 教练的讲话在比赛前激励了球队。

The company is looking for ways to energize its workforce.  这家公司正在寻找激励员工的方法。| 近义词：invigorate, stimulate, revitalize, empower | 反义词：enervate, weaken, exhaust, drain | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *energizare, from Ancient Greek ἐνέργεια (enérgeia, “activity, operation”), from ἐν (en, “in, at”) + ἔργον (érgon, “work”).
