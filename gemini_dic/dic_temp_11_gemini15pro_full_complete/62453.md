endorsement:/ɪnˈdɔːrsmənt/ | n. | 赞同，支持；背书；代言 | An act of giving public approval or support to someone or something.  A signature on a check or bill of exchange that makes it payable to someone else. |  The product has received endorsements from several celebrities.  这个产品得到了几位名人的代言。 He needed to get the check endorsed. 他需要将支票背书。 | 近义词: approval, support, backing, sanction, commendation, recommendation, testimonial | 反义词: disapproval, opposition, condemnation | 词性: 名词

词源: Late Middle English: from endorse + -ment.
