crash-out:/ˈkræʃ aʊt/| phrasal verb | 突然退出；（尤指比赛）出局；（因醉酒或累极）倒头便睡 | To suddenly exit or be eliminated from something, especially a competition; to fall asleep suddenly because of exhaustion or intoxication. | England crashed out of the World Cup. 英格兰队在世界杯中出局了。 | Synonyms: lose, withdraw, exit, drop out, conk out | Antonyms: win, succeed, enter, join | verb

crash-out:/ˈkræʃ aʊt/| noun |  突然退出；意外出局；（因醉酒或累极）昏睡 | A sudden exit or elimination, especially from a competition; a sudden sleep due to exhaustion or intoxication. | His crash-out from the tournament was unexpected. 他在锦标赛中的意外出局是出乎意料的。| Synonyms: elimination, withdrawal, exit | Antonyms: entry, victory, success | noun
