carrageen:/ˌkærəˈɡiːn/ | n. | 角叉菜；角叉菜胶 | A red seaweed (Chondrus crispus) of the family Gigartinaceae, the source of carrageenan.  |  Carrageen is commonly used in food production as a thickening agent. 角叉菜通常在食品生产中用作增稠剂。 | synonyms: Irish moss, carrageenan | antonyms: None | noun

carrageen:/ˌkærəˈɡiːn/ | n. | 角叉菜胶 | A gummy substance obtained from carrageen, used as a thickening agent, stabilizer, or emulsifier in food products and other industrial applications. |  Ice cream often contains carrageen to improve its texture. 冰淇淋通常含有角叉菜胶以改善其质地。| synonyms: carrageenan, Irish moss gel | antonyms: None | noun
