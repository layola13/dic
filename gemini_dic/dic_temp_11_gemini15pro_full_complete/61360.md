embezzle: /ɪmˈbɛzəl/ | v. | 盗用；挪用；贪污 | To illegally take money that you are responsible for, usually from a company or organization. |  He was caught embezzling funds from the charity. 他被发现盗用慈善机构的资金。

The accountant embezzled thousands of dollars from his clients. 会计师从他的客户那里贪污了数千美元。

| 近义词: misappropriate, pilfer, defraud, steal | 反义词: donate, contribute | 词性: verb
