anthologizes:/ænˈθɒlədʒaɪzɪz/
verb
选集化；将…编成选集
includes in an anthology; makes into an anthology.
The professor anthologizes the best student essays at the end of each semester.  教授在每个学期末都会将最好的学生论文编成选集。
anthologizes the works of contemporary poets. 将当代诗人的作品编成选集。
synonyms: compile, collect, gather
antonyms: disperse, scatter
verb
