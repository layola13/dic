novator:/ˈnoʊveɪtər/| n.|革新者，创新者|A person who introduces new methods, ideas, or products.|例句：He was a true novator in the field of medicine. 他是医学领域的真正革新者。

例句：The company is looking for novators who can drive change and innovation.  这家公司正在寻找能够推动变革和创新的革新者。|近义词：innovator, pioneer, inventor, reformer|反义词:conservative, traditionalist|词性:noun
Etymology: From Latin *novator*, from *novare* "to make new, renew, alter," from *novus* "new."
