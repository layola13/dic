anthozoa:/ˌænθəˈzoʊə/ | n. | 珊瑚虫纲 |A large class of marine invertebrates which comprises the sea anemones and corals. These animals have a cylindrical body and a central mouth surrounded by tentacles.|  Coral reefs are built by anthozoans. 珊瑚礁是由珊瑚虫纲动物建造的。| 近义词: corals, sea anemones | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

Etymology: From Ancient Greek ἄνθος (ánthos, “flower”) + ζῷον (zôion, “animal”).
