nontractions:/ˌnɑːnˈtrækʃənz/ | n. | 非收缩形式 |  Words written out in full, without contractions (e.g., "cannot" instead of "can't"). |  Many formal style guides recommend using nontractions in academic writing.  许多正式的风格指南建议在学术写作中使用非缩略形式。| synonyms: full forms, expanded forms | antonyms: contractions | noun
