directer:/dɪˈrɛktər/ /daɪˈrɛktər/
n.|导演；指导者；主管；处长；（公司）董事|Someone who supervises or controls a film, play, or other artistic work.  Also, a person who manages or guides the affairs of a business or organization.|The director yelled "cut!" after the scene. 导演在拍摄完这个场景后喊了“卡！”|synonyms: manager, supervisor, leader, administrator, executive|antonyms: subordinate, follower|noun

directer:/dɪˈrɛktər/ /daɪˈrɛktər/
n.|（公司）董事；理事|A member of the board of directors of a company or organization.|She's a director at a major advertising agency. 她是一家大型广告公司的董事。|synonyms: board member, trustee, governor|antonyms: employee, staff|noun
