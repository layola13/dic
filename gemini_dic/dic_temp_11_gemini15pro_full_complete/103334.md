jigsawed:/ˈdʒɪɡˌsɔːd/ | adj. | 拼图式的，参差不齐的 | Resembling a jigsaw puzzle, often in a complex or intricate way; assembled from various pieces. | The coastline was jigsawed with inlets and bays. 海岸线像拼图一样由小港湾和海湾组成。 | The detective pieced together the jigsawed fragments of evidence. 侦探将零散的证据拼凑在一起。 | 近义词: fragmented, interlocking, pieced together | 反义词: smooth, continuous | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From jigsaw + -ed.  "Jigsaw" itself originates from the tool used to create such puzzles.
