interpretive:/ɪnˈtɜːrprətɪv/ | adj. | 解释的；说明的；阐释的 | Relating to or providing an interpretation. |  The museum offers interpretive exhibits about the history of the region.  博物馆提供关于该地区历史的解说性展览。| Synonyms: explanatory, expository, elucidative, illustrative | Antonyms: uninterpretive, ambiguous, obscure | adjective

interpretive:/ɪnˈtɜːrprətɪv/ | adj. | （艺术表演方面）基于个人理解的；演绎的 | Based on personal interpretation, especially in artistic performance. | Her interpretive dance was a powerful expression of grief. 她的演绎性舞蹈是对悲伤的有力表达。| Synonyms: expressive, creative, individualistic, subjective | Antonyms: literal, objective, factual | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English: from late Latin *interpretativus, from interpretat- ‘interpreted’, from the verb interpretari.
