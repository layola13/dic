acidifiable:/əˈsɪdɪfaɪəbl/
adjective
可酸化的
Capable of being made acidic.
The solution is acidifiable by adding a few drops of hydrochloric acid.  该溶液可以通过添加几滴盐酸来酸化。
acidifying, acidulous
alkaline, basic
adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From acidify + -able.  Acidify comes from acid + -ify. Acid ultimately derives from Latin acidus ("sour, sharp").
