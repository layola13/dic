immovably:/ɪˈmuːvəbli/| adv.|不可移动地，坚定地|in a way that cannot be moved|The statue stood immovably in the square. 这座雕像一动不动地矗立在广场上.|近义词：fixedly, firmly, steadfastly|反义词: movably, loosely|词性:副词
Etymology: From "immovable" + "-ly."  "Immovable" comes from the Old French "immovable," from the Latin "immovabilis," from "in-" (not) + "movabilis" (movable), from "movere" (to move).
