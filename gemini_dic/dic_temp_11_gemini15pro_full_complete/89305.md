homoeothermal:/ˌhoʊmioʊˈθɜːrməl/ /ˌhɒmiəʊˈθɜːməl/| adj.|恒温的，温血的|Maintaining a constant body temperature despite changes in the surrounding environment.  Able to regulate body temperature internally.|例句：Humans are homoeothermal animals. 人类是恒温动物。|近义词：endothermic, warm-blooded|反义词：poikilothermic, cold-blooded|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Greek *homoio* (similar, like) + *thermē* (heat)
