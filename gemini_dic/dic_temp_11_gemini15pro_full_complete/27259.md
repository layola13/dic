calorimetry:/ˌkæləˈrɪmətri/| n. |量热法，量热学|The science of measuring heat changes that accompany chemical reactions and physical changes.|例句：The experiment involved the use of calorimetry to determine the heat capacity of the substance.  这个实验包括使用量热法来确定物质的热容。

例句：Calorimetry plays a crucial role in understanding the energy content of foods. 量热法在理解食物的能量含量方面起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：thermal analysis, heat measurement|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

calorimetric:/ˌkælərɪˈmetrɪk/| adj. |量热法的，量热学的|Relating to calorimetry or measured by a calorimeter.|例句：Calorimetric measurements were taken to determine the energy released during combustion.  进行了量热测量以确定燃烧过程中释放的能量。

例句：The researchers used a calorimetric bomb to study the reaction. 研究人员使用量热弹来研究反应。|近义词：thermal|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective

calorimeter:/ˌkæləˈrɪmɪtər/| n. |量热器，热量计|An apparatus for measuring the heat generated or absorbed during a chemical or physical change.|例句：The calorimeter measured the heat released by the burning fuel. 量热器测量了燃烧燃料释放的热量。

例句：A bomb calorimeter is used to determine the heat of combustion of substances.  炸弹量热计用于测定物质的燃烧热。|近义词：heat meter|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
