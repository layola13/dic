dunness:/ˈdʌnɪs/
noun
昏暗；阴暗；晦暗
The state or quality of being dun or dark in color.
The dunness of the November sky made me feel melancholic. 十一月天空的阴暗让我感到忧郁。
近义词:dimness, darkness, gloominess
反义词:brightness, lightness, radiance
词性:名词

dunness:/ˈdʌnɪs/
noun
单调；沉闷
Lack of brightness or liveliness; monotony.
The dunness of the landscape was relieved only by the occasional tree.  单调的景致只有偶尔出现的树木才能缓解。
近义词:drabness, dullness, monotony
反义词: vibrancy, excitement, colorfulness
词性:名词


词源：From *dun* (adjective) + *-ness* (suffix forming nouns of quality or state). *Dun* (meaning brownish-gray) is of uncertain origin, possibly from Old English *dunn*.
