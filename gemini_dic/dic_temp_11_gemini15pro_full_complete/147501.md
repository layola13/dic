overpackaged:/ˌoʊvərˈpækɪdʒd/
adjective
过度包装的
Wrapped in more layers of packaging than necessary.
The doll was overpackaged in a huge box filled with plastic and styrofoam. 这个娃娃过度包装，装在一个装满塑料和泡沫聚苯乙烯的大盒子里。
This product is ridiculously overpackaged – it's bad for the environment.  这个产品包装得太多了，对环境有害。
近义词: excessively packaged
反义词: minimally packaged
词性: adjective
