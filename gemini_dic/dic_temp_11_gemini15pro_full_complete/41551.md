corozo:/kəˈroʊzoʊ/
n.
Corozo nut; Ivory palm; Vegetable ivory
A type of palm tree native to South America, whose hard white seeds are used as a substitute for ivory.  The seed itself is also called corozo.
Corozo nuts are often used to make buttons.
Corozo buttons are a sustainable alternative to plastic.
synonym: tagua nut, ivory nut, vegetable ivory
antonym: plastic, animal ivory
noun
