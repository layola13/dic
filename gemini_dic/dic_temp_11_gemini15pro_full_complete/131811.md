nonconsequence:/ˌnɒnˈkɒnsɪkwəns/ | n. | 无关紧要，不重要；没有逻辑后果 | Lack of consequence or importance; something that doesn't follow logically. |  The typo was a nonconsequence and didn't change the meaning of the sentence.  这个拼写错误无关紧要，并没有改变句子的意思。 | synonyms: insignificance, irrelevance, triviality | antonyms: consequence, importance, significance | noun
