horizontalize:/ˌhɔːrɪˈzɒntəlaɪz/ | v. |使水平,使横向,使世俗化|To make something horizontal or to spread something out over a wider area; to make something more focused on worldly matters rather than spiritual ones. |The architect decided to horizontalize the roofline to create a more modern look.  建筑师决定将屋顶线水平化，以营造更现代的外观。

The company decided to horizontalize its management structure, giving more autonomy to individual departments. 公司决定将其管理结构横向化，赋予各个部门更大的自主权。|Synonyms: flatten, level, broaden, secularize|Antonyms: verticalize, steepen, narrow, spiritualize|verb
