artlessnesses:/ˈɑːrtləsnəsɪz/
n.
天真烂漫，单纯，自然，不矫揉造作
The quality or state of being artless; naturalness; simplicity; unpretentiousness.
Her artlessnesses charmed everyone she met. 她的天真烂漫迷住了她遇到的每一个人。
synonyms: innocence, naiveté, ingenuousness, simplicity, candor, guilelessness
antonyms: artifice, cunning, guile, sophistication, worldliness
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From artless + -ness.  Artless itself comes from art + -less.  "Art" in this context refers to skill or cunning, particularly in deception.  Therefore, "artless" means without such skill or cunning, and "artlessness" is the noun form of this adjective.
