braze:/breɪz/| v. | 钎焊；使坚强；使硬化 | To join two pieces of metal together using a brazing alloy that melts at a lower temperature than the metals being joined; to make someone strong and confident, especially by making them experience difficult or unpleasant things.| The pipes were brazed together. 这些管道被钎焊在一起了。|  The experience brazed her. 这段经历使她变得坚强。 | synonyms: solder, weld, fuse, harden, toughen | antonyms: separate, weaken, soften | verb

braze:/breɪz/| n. | 钎焊；黄铜焊料 | The act or process of brazing; a brazing alloy. | The braze held the two pieces of metal together securely.  这钎焊接合牢固地将两块金属连接在一起。| synonyms: solder, weld, joint | antonyms: separation | noun
