murexide:/ˈmjʊrɪksaɪd/ | n. |紫脲酸铵; 骨螺紫; |An ammonium salt of purpuric acid, used as an indicator in titrations of calcium and other metal ions, and as a dye. |Murexide develops a deep red-violet color in the presence of calcium ions.  紫脲酸铵在钙离子存在下会呈现深红紫色。

Murexide was historically obtained from mollusks, giving it the name related to "murex," a genus of sea snails.  紫脲酸铵历史上是从软体动物中提取的，因此它的名字与海螺属“murex”有关。|近义词: ammonium purpurate |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
