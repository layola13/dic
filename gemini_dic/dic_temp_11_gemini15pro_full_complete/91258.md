hydrofluosilicate:/ˌhaɪdroʊˌfluːoʊˈsɪlɪkeɪt/
noun
氟硅酸盐
A salt of hydrofluosilicic acid, containing the SiF₆²⁻ anion.
Sodium hydrofluosilicate is used in water fluoridation. 氟硅酸钠用于水氟化。
