cognatenesses:/ˌkɑːɡˈneɪtnəsnɪsɪz/ | n. |同源性；同源词的关系| The cognatenesses between these languages are striking. 这些语言之间的同源性令人吃惊。|例句：The cognatenesses of the Romance languages are readily apparent.  罗曼语族的同源性显而易见。

The high degree of cognatenesses between Spanish and Portuguese makes them mutually intelligible to a large extent.  西班牙语和葡萄牙语之间高度的同源性使得它们在很大程度上可以互通。| 近义词：kinship, affinity, relationship | 反义词：dissimilarity, difference | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From cognate + -ness. Cognate comes from Latin cognātus ("born together, related by blood"), from com- ("together") + nātus ("born").
