journalization:/ˌdʒɜːrnələɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |  记日记；记账；日志记录 | The process of recording transactions in a journal.  记录交易到日记账的过程。| Example: The journalization of the sales transaction was completed accurately.  销售交易的记账已准确完成。| Example: Proper journalization is crucial for accurate financial reporting.  正确的日记账记录对于准确的财务报告至关重要。 |Synonyms: recording, entry, logging | Antonyms: deletion, erasure | noun

journalize:/ˈdʒɜːrnəlaɪz/ | v. |  把…记入日记；把…记入分类账 | To record transactions in a journal.  将交易记录到日记账中。| Example: The accountant journalized the daily transactions. 会计记录了每日交易。| Example:  She meticulously journalized every expense. 她一丝不苟地记录了每一笔开支。 |Synonyms: record, enter, log | Antonyms: delete, erase | verb
