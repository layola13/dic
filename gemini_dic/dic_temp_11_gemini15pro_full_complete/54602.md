dismast:/dɪsˈmɑːst/ | vt. | 卸下桅杆；使失去桅杆；摧毁 | To remove the mast(s) from (a ship); to deprive of a mast or masts, especially by breakage in a storm or collision; to disable or cripple. | The ship was dismasted in the storm. 这艘船在暴风雨中失去了桅杆。 | Synonyms: disable, cripple, wreck | Antonyms: rig, equip | verb

dismast:/dɪsˈmɑːst/ | n. | 卸下桅杆的行为；失去桅杆的状态 | The act of dismasting or the state of being dismasted.  | The dismast of the ship left the crew stranded. 船失去桅杆使船员滞留。| Synonyms: disabling, crippling, wrecking | Antonyms: rigging, equipping | noun
