over-animated:/ˌoʊvərˈænɪmeɪtɪd/
adjective
过度活跃的，过于兴奋的
Excessively animated; showing too much excitement or enthusiasm in one's gestures and mannerisms.
Her over-animated performance felt forced and unnatural. 她过于活跃的表演让人感觉很做作，不自然。

The over-animated puppy was bouncing off the walls.  这只过度兴奋的小狗在墙上乱蹦乱跳。


近义词:  excitable, hyperactive, overenthusiastic, boisterous
反义词: calm, subdued, restrained, quiet
词性: adjective
