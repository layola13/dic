methide:/ˈmɛθaɪd/| n. |甲基负离子|A negatively charged CH3- ion, also known as a carbanion. It is a highly reactive species.|Example 1: Methide ions are important intermediates in organic synthesis. 甲基负离子是有机合成中的重要中间体。

Example 2: The stability of the methide ion can be influenced by neighboring functional groups. 甲基负离子的稳定性会受到相邻官能团的影响。|近义词:methyl anion, carbanion|反义词:carbocation|词性:noun
