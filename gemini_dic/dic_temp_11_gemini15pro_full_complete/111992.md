loneliness:/ˈləʊnlɪnəs/ | n. | 孤独，寂寞 | The feeling of being alone and unhappy because you have no friends or do not have anyone to talk to. |  She suffered from intense loneliness after her husband died. 她丈夫去世后，她感到非常孤独。| synonyms: isolation, solitude | antonyms: togetherness, companionship | noun
