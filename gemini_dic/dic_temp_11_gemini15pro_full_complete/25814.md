burghbote:/ˈbɜːrɡboʊt/
n.
城镇修缮义务; 城堡维护税
An archaic term referring to a contribution or tax levied on inhabitants of a borough for the maintenance and repair of its fortifications, especially walls.
The burghbote was an essential part of maintaining the town's defenses.  城镇修缮税是维护城镇防御工事的重要组成部分。

The king imposed a hefty burghbote on the townspeople after the siege. 国王在围攻之后向市民征收了沉重的城堡维护税。

近义词:scot, levy, tax
反义词:exemption, remission
词性:noun
