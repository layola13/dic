caterpillar:/ˈkætərˌpɪlər/| n. |毛毛虫，履带|The larva of a butterfly or moth, having a segmented wormlike body with three pairs of true legs and several pairs of fleshy prolegs.|The caterpillar munched on a leaf. 毛毛虫啃食着一片叶子。|近义词：larva, worm|反义词:butterfly, moth|noun
