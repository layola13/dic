footfault:/ˈfʊtfɔːlt/| n.|脚误，脚部犯规|A fault in serving or receiving the ball in tennis, volleyball, or racquetball, made by touching the boundary line with any part of the foot before striking the ball.  It results in the loss of the point.|The player was penalized for a footfault.  这名球员因为脚误被罚分。|近义词：service fault, violation|反义词: legal serve|词性:noun
