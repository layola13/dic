concordanced:/kənˈkɔːrdənst/ | adj. |  和谐的，一致的，相符的 | Arranged in agreement or harmony; brought into concord.  | The testimonies of the witnesses were concordanced. 目击者的证词是一致的。 | Example: The different versions of the story were concordanced to create a cohesive narrative.  不同版本的故事被协调一致，以创造一个连贯的叙述。 | Synonyms: harmonious, consistent, agreeing, congruent, corresponding | Antonyms: discordant, inconsistent, disagreeing, incongruent, conflicting | word property: adjective
