illegal:/ɪˈliːɡl/ (US), /ɪˈliːɡəl/ (UK)| adj.|非法的，违法的|Contrary to or forbidden by law, especially criminal law.|例句：It is illegal to drive without a license.  无证驾驶是违法的。|近义词：unlawful, illicit, prohibited|反义词:legal, lawful|词性:形容词
