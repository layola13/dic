insatiability:/ˌɪnseɪʃiəˈbɪləti/ |n.|贪得无厌，不知足|The insatiability of human desire is a recurring theme in literature. 人类的欲望永无止境是文学中反复出现的主题。|例句：His insatiability for power led to his downfall. 他对权力的贪得无厌导致了他的垮台。

insatiability:/ˌɪnseɪʃiəˈbɪləti/ |n.|无法满足，永不满足|The insatiability of her curiosity drove her to explore the world. 她永不满足的好奇心驱使她去探索世界。|近义词：greed, avarice, rapacity, voracity|反义词：contentment, satisfaction|词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin insatiabilitās, from Latin insatiabilis (insatiable) + -itās (-ity).  The Latin insatiabilis comes from in- (not) + satiabilis (capable of being satisfied), from satiāre (to satisfy). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *sa- (to satisfy).
