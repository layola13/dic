inceration:/ɪnsəˈreɪʃn/| n.|焚烧，烧成灰烬|The incineration of waste is a common method of disposal. 废物焚烧是一种常见的处理方法。|例句：The city opted for incineration of its waste rather than landfill.  这个城市选择焚烧垃圾而不是填埋。

例句：The incineration of medical waste must be carefully controlled to prevent the release of harmful substances. 医疗废物的焚烧必须小心控制，以防止有害物质的释放。

近义词：combustion, burning|反义词: preservation|词性:名词
