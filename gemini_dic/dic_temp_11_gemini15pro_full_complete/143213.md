outcrowd: /ˈaʊtkraʊd/
noun
不受欢迎的人群；边缘人群
The group of people who are not accepted as part of the main social group.
She always felt like she was part of the outcrowd in high school.  她在高中时总是觉得自己被排挤在外。
They were treated as the outcrowd because of their unconventional beliefs. 由于他们非传统的信仰，他们被视为边缘人群。
synonyms: outsiders, misfits, nonconformists
antonyms: incrowd, popular people
