gazundering:/ɡəˈzʌndərɪŋ/ | n. | 压低房价; 临时压价 | The act of lowering an offer on a house purchase at the last minute, often just before contracts are exchanged, usually in the hope that the seller will accept a lower price to avoid the sale falling through. |  The seller was furious about the gazundering, but accepted the lower offer as they needed to move quickly. 卖家对临时压价感到愤怒，但由于他们需要快速搬家，还是接受了较低的报价。 |  The buyer tried gazundering, hoping to save a few thousand pounds. 买家试图压低房价，希望能省下几千英镑。 | 近义词: last-minute price reduction, lowballing | 反义词: gazumping | 词性: 名词
