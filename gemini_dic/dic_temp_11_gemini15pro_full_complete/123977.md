mormo:/ˈmɔːrmoʊ/| n.|梦魇，女鬼，妖怪|A female specter or phantom, especially one appearing in nightmares; a bugbear.|She was haunted by mormos in her sleep. 她晚上睡觉被梦魇缠身。|近义词：nightmare, phantom, specter, ghost, hobgoblin, bugbear|反义词:comfort, peace|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *mormo, *mormōnis, likely related to Greek μορμώ (mormō), a frightful she-monster used to frighten children.  Ultimately of uncertain origin, perhaps imitative of a frightening sound.
