heathclad:/ˈheθklæd/| adj.|石楠覆盖的，覆盖着石楠的|Covered with heath; clad in heath. |The heathclad hills were beautiful in the late summer light. 夏末的阳光下，石楠覆盖的山丘很美。|The heathclad slopes provided a haven for wildlife. 石楠覆盖的山坡为野生动物提供了庇护所。|近义词：heathy, moorland|反义词: barren, treeless|词性:形容词
词源: heath (n.) + clad (adj.)

heath:  from Old English *hāþ, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch heide and German Heide.
clad: from Old English clāþ ‘cloth, garment’, of Germanic origin; related to cloth.
