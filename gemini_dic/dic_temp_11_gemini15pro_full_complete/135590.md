nonporousness:/ˌnɒnpɔːrəsnəs/
n.|无孔性，不透气性|The nonporousness of the material makes it ideal for containing liquids.  这种材料的无孔性使其成为盛放液体的理想选择。|The nonporousness of the rock prevented water from seeping through.  岩石的无孔性阻止了水渗透。|impermeability, impenetrability|porosity, permeability|noun
nonporous:/ˌnɒnˈpɔːrəs/
adj.|无孔的，不透气的|A nonporous surface is easy to clean. 无孔表面易于清洁。|This container is nonporous and will not leak. 这个容器是无孔的，不会漏。|impermeable, impenetrable|porous, permeable|adjective
