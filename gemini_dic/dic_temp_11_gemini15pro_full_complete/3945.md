agrochemical:/ˈæɡroʊˌkɛmɪkəl/ | n. | 农用化学品；农业化学制品 |A chemical product used in agriculture, such as a pesticide, herbicide, or fertilizer. | The overuse of agrochemicals can have detrimental effects on the environment. 过度使用农用化学品会对环境产生不利影响。|  The farmer sprayed his crops with an agrochemical to protect them from insects. 农民给他的作物喷洒了一种农用化学品以防止昆虫侵害。 |Synonyms: pesticide, herbicide, fertilizer, insecticide, fungicide | Antonyms: organic fertilizer, biopesticide | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From agro- (combining form meaning "agriculture," from Latin ager "field") + chemical.
