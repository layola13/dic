nonfrighteningly:/nɒnˈfraɪtənɪŋli/ | adv. | 不吓人地，不令人恐惧地 | In a way that does not cause fear or fright. | The clown approached the children nonfrighteningly, with a gentle smile. 小丑带着温和的微笑，不吓人地走向孩子们。| The spider crawled across the floor nonfrighteningly, almost gracefully.  蜘蛛不吓人地、几乎是优雅地爬过地板。| 近义词: calmly, gently, soothingly | 反义词: terrifyingly, frighteningly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
frightening: from the verb "frighten," from Old English *fryhtnian "to frighten," from Proto-Germanic *furhtjanan, from the root *furkhtaz "afraid."
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives
