bipartisan:/ˌbaɪˈpɑːrtɪzən/| adj.|两党的；得到两党支持的|Involving or supported by two political parties, especially the two major parties.|The bill received bipartisan support in Congress. 这项法案获得了国会两党的支持.|Synonyms: nonpartisan, inter-party, cross-party, joint, united, collaborative|Antonyms: partisan, one-sided, factional|adjective
