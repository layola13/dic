drosophilist:/droʊˈsɒfɪlɪst/| n. |果蝇研究者；果蝇学家|A biologist who specializes in the study of Drosophila, especially Drosophila melanogaster (the common fruit fly).|例句：The drosophilist spent years studying the genetic mutations in fruit flies.  这位果蝇学家花了数年时间研究果蝇的基因突变。

例句：Many drosophilists believe that their research can provide valuable insights into human genetics. 许多果蝇学家认为他们的研究可以为人类遗传学提供宝贵的见解。|近义词：geneticist, biologist|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Drosophila (the genus of fruit flies) + -ist (a suffix forming agent nouns).  Drosophila comes from Greek δρόσος (drosos, “dew”) + φίλος (philos, “loving”).
