keenest:/ˈkiːnɪst/| adj.|最敏锐的，最热心的|Superlative form of keen: having or showing eagerness or enthusiasm.|He has the keenest mind in the class. 他是班上头脑最敏锐的学生。|近义词：sharpest, most enthusiastic, most eager|反义词：dullest, most apathetic|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English kene, from Old English cēne (“bold, brave, sharp”), from Proto-Germanic *kōniz (“brave, clever”). Cognate with Dutch keen (“sharp, spicy”), German kühn (“bold, daring”). The original sense was “sharp” (of blades, etc.), which later developed the figurative senses “sharp-witted” and “eager.”
