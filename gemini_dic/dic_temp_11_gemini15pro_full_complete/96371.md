indiscerptibility:/ˌɪndɪsɜːnɪptɪˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可分辨性；不可区分性 | The state or quality of being indistinguishable from something else. | The indiscerptibility of the twins made it difficult to tell them apart. 这对双胞胎难以区分，使得很难把他们分开。 | 近义词: indistinguishability, sameness, uniformity | 反义词: distinguishability, difference, distinctness | 词性: 名词

indiscerptible:/ˌɪndɪsɜːnɪptɪbl/ | adj. | 不可分辨的；不可区分的 | Not able to be perceived as different or distinct. | The two shades of blue were almost indiscerptible. 这两种蓝色几乎无法区分。| 近义词: indistinguishable, identical, similar | 反义词: distinguishable, different, distinct | 词性: 形容词


词源:
From Late Latin *indiscerptibilis, from in- 'not' + discerptibilis 'separable', from discerpere 'to separate'. Ultimately from the Latin roots dis- "apart" and carpere "to pluck, harvest".
