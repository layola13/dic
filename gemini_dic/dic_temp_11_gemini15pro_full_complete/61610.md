emeute:/eɪˈmjut/ /eɪˈmuːt/| n. |暴乱，骚乱|A sudden outburst of popular disorder; a riot.|例句：The emeute quickly spread through the city. 骚乱迅速蔓延到整个城市。

例句：The government struggled to contain the emeute. 政府努力控制骚乱。|近义词：riot, uprising, revolt, insurrection, disturbance|反义词：peace, calm, order, tranquility|词性:noun

词源: From French émeute, from Old French esmeute "riot, uprising," literally "a moving out," from esmovoir "to stir up, excite," from Latin emovere "to move out, remove, agitate," from ex- "out" + movere "to move."
