hot-swappable:/ˌhɒt ˈswɒpəbl/ /ˌhɑːt ˈswɑːpəbl/ | adj. | 可热插拔的 | Able to be removed and replaced while the system is running, without interrupting its operation. | This server features hot-swappable hard drives for maximum uptime.  这台服务器配备了可热插拔硬盘，以实现最大的正常运行时间。|  The new battery is hot-swappable, so you can change it without turning off the device. 新电池是可热插拔的，因此您无需关闭设备即可更换电池。 | interchangeable, replaceable, plug-and-play | non-hot-swappable, fixed | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
"hot" (referring to live electrical circuits) + "swap" (to exchange) + "-able" (suffix indicating capability).  The term gained prominence with the rise of computer technology.
