demolishes:/dɪˈmɒlɪʃɪz/
verb
摧毁，拆除
completely destroys a building, structure, system, idea, etc.
The old factory was demolished to make way for a new shopping mall.  旧工厂被拆除，以便为新的购物中心腾出空间。
The hurricane demolished several houses along the coast.  飓风摧毁了沿海的几栋房屋。
Synonym: destroy, raze, wreck, tear down
Antonym: build, construct, erect
词性: 动词
