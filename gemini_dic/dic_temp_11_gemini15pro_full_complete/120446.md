minnows:/ˈmɪnoʊz/ (US), /ˈmɪnəʊz/ (UK)| n. |米诺鱼；小鱼；无足轻重的人|Small freshwater fish of the carp family, often used as bait.  Figuratively, it can also refer to insignificant or unimportant people. |例句：The minnows darted through the shallows.  小鱼在浅滩中穿梭。

例句：Compared to the industry giants, his company was just a minnow.  与行业巨头相比，他的公司不过是小鱼小虾。|近义词：tidlers, small fry, fry|反义词：giants, behemoths|词性:名词
