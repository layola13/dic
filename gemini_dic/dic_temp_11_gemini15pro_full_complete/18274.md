belittlingly:/bɪˈlɪtlɪŋli/
adverb
轻蔑地，贬低地
in a way that makes someone or something seem unimportant
He belittlingly referred to their efforts as "child's play." 他轻蔑地将他们的努力称为“儿戏”。

近义词:condescendingly, disdainfully, scornfully
反义词:respectfully, admiringly
词性:adverb
