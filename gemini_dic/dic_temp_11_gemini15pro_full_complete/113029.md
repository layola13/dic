luminances:/ˈluːmɪnənsɪz/
n.
亮度（复数）
The intensity of light emitted from a surface per unit area in a given direction.
The luminances of the different areas of the screen were carefully calibrated.  屏幕不同区域的亮度都经过了仔细的校准。
synonyms: brightnesses, intensities
antonyms: darknesses, dimnesses
noun
