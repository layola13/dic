nonrelativeness:/nɒnˌrɛlətɪvnəs/| n.|非相对性，非关联性|The nonrelativeness of the two events makes it difficult to establish a causal link.  这两个事件的非关联性使得很难建立因果关系。|This theory emphasizes the nonrelativeness of moral values within a specific cultural context.  该理论强调了道德价值观在特定文化背景下的非相对性。|近义词：absoluteness, independence|反义词：relativity, relatedness|词性:noun
Etymology: non- + relative + -ness.  From "non-" (not) + "relative" (depending on or determined by comparison) + "-ness" (forming nouns expressing state or quality).
