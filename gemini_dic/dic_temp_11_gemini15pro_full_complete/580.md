abounder:/əˈbaʊndər/
noun
富于…的人；盛产…的地方
One that abounds or is abundant, especially a place that teems with something specified.  A person or place that has a lot of something.
Fish are abounders in this lake.  这个湖里鱼很多。
近义词: producer, supplier, generator
反义词: consumer, user, lack
