overdrawing:/ˌoʊvərˈdrɔːɪŋ/ | n. |透支；超额提取|The act of withdrawing more money from a bank account than is available.| He received a notice from the bank about an overdrawing on his account. 他收到了银行关于他账户透支的通知。| 近义词: overdraft | 反义词: deposit | 词性: 名词

overdrawing:/ˌoʊvərˈdrɔːɪŋ/ | v. |透支；超额提取|The action of taking out more money than is available in a bank account.|She was careful not to overdrawing her checking account. 她小心翼翼地避免透支她的支票账户。| 近义词: overdraft | 反义词: deposit | 词性: 动词
