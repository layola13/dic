nonchalkier:/ˌnɑːnˈtʃɔːkiər/
adjective
更不白垩的，chalky的反义词比较级
less chalky; having a smoother, less powdery texture.
The new paint is nonchalkier than the old one. 新油漆比旧油漆更不白垩。
The surface of this ceramic tile is nonchalkier and easier to clean.  这个瓷砖的表面更不白垩，更容易清洁。
Near synonyms: smoother, less powdery, less chalky, firmer, denser
Antonyms: chalkier, more chalky, powdery, crumbly
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "chalky" + "-er" (comparative suffix). "Chalky" comes from "chalk" (Old English *cealc, from Latin calx "lime, limestone").
