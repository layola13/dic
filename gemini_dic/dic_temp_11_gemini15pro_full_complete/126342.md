myotomy:/maɪˈɒtəmi/ /maɪˈɑːtəmi/
noun
肌切开术
Surgical incision into a muscle.
The patient underwent a myotomy to relieve the muscle spasms in his esophagus.  病人接受了肌切开术以缓解食道肌肉痉挛。
Near Antonyms:  myoplasty (muscle repair)
Antonyms:  myorrhaphy (muscle suture)
n.
