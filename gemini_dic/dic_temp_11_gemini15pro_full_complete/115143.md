mangroves:/ˈmæŋɡroʊvz/| n.|红树林|A mangrove is a shrub or small tree that grows in coastal saline or brackish water, usually having prop roots—roots that grow from the trunk or branches and extend down into the water or mud.|The mangroves provide a habitat for a variety of marine life.|近义词：mangrove swamp, tidal forest|反义词:|noun
