canola:/kəˈnoʊlə/| n. |菜籽油；油菜籽|A type of oilseed rape developed in Canada, yielding seeds that produce a valuable edible oil low in erucic acid.  It's widely used in cooking and food processing.|Canola oil is a popular choice for frying. 菜籽油是油炸的常用选择。

Canola seeds are crushed to extract the oil. 油菜籽被压碎以提取油。|近义词：rapeseed oil (though not technically identical), vegetable oil|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider saturated fats like butter or lard as culinary opposites)|词性:noun
