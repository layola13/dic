in respect of:/ɪn rɪˈspɛkt əv/
preposition
关于；就…而言
Concerning; regarding.
例句：In respect of your request, we have decided to grant it. 关于你的请求，我们已经决定批准。
例句：The two groups were similar in respect of age and sex. 这两组在年龄和性别方面相似。
近义词：regarding, concerning, with regard to, relating to, as regards, with respect to, in relation to
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以根据语境选择表示相反关系的词)
词性:介词 (preposition)
