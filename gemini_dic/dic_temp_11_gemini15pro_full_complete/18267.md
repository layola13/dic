belittle:/bɪˈlɪt(ə)l/
Verb
贬低，轻视
to make someone or something seem unimportant
例句：He belittled her efforts to get the project finished on time. 他贬低了她为按时完成项目所做的努力。

例句：The critic belittled the artist's work, calling it amateurish.  评论家贬低了这位艺术家的作品，称其为业余水平。

近义词：disparage, denigrate, minimize, underestimate, deprecate, deride
反义词：praise, commend, extol, laud, compliment, aggrandize
词性:v.
