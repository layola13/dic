ok'ing:/oʊˈkeɪɪŋ/
verb
批准，认可，同意
The manager is ok'ing the budget proposal. 经理正在批准预算方案。
He's ok'ing the plans for the new building. 他正在批准新建筑的计划。
近义词:approving, authorizing, endorsing, ratifying, sanctioning, validating
反义词:disapproving, rejecting, refusing, vetoing
词性:动词

Etymology:  Derived from "OK" + "-ing" (present participle suffix). "OK" itself has a disputed etymology, with one prominent theory suggesting it originated as an abbreviation of "oll korrect," a humorous misspelling of "all correct" in the 19th century.
