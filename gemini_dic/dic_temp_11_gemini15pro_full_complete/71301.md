fixtureless:/ˈfɪkstʃərlɪs/
adjective
无固定装置的; 没有固定装置的; 没有固定比赛的
lacking fixtures, especially permanent fixtures.  Often used in the context of sports, meaning without a pre-arranged match or opponent.
The team found themselves fixtureless for the weekend after their opponents forfeited the game.  该队在对手弃权后发现自己周末没有比赛了。
The apartment was fixtureless and bare, with no light fittings or kitchen appliances. 这套公寓没有固定装置，光秃秃的，没有灯具或厨房用具。
Synonyms: unattached, loose, free, unfixed
Antonyms: fixed, attached, secured
词性: 形容词
