hayya:/ˈhæjə/| int.|来吧，走吧，快点|An Arabic word used as an exclamation to encourage or urge someone to come, go, or hurry.|Hayya! Let's go to the beach! 来吧！我们去海滩吧！|近义词：come on, let's go, hurry up|反义词:wait, stay|interjection

注意事项:
Hayya is primarily used in spoken Arabic and may not be as common in formal written contexts.  While the examples provided illustrate grammatical usage, the word's interjectory nature makes its use in some tenses less common.  It's generally used as a standalone exclamation to encourage action.
