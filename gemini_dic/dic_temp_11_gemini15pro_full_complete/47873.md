decelerated: /diːˈsɛləˌreɪtɪd/
verb (past tense)|减速的，减速了的|Reduced speed; slowed down.|The car decelerated smoothly as it approached the traffic light. 汽车在接近交通信号灯时平稳减速。|
近义词: slowed, reduced speed, braked, retarded
反义词: accelerated, speeded up, increased speed
词性:  Verb (past participle)

decelerated: /diːˈsɛləˌreɪtɪd/
adjective|减速的，减速了的|Having reduced speed; slowed down.|The decelerated growth of the economy is a cause for concern.  经济增长放缓令人担忧。|近义词: slow, slower, reduced, diminished
反义词: accelerated, fast, faster, increased
词性: Adjective

词源: From the Latin word "celer" meaning "swift," with the prefix "de-" indicating reversal or removal, and the suffix "-ate" forming a verb. The "-ed" ending signifies the past tense or past participle form.
