gruel:/ˈɡruːəl/| n. |稀粥，麦片粥|A thin liquid food of oatmeal or other meal boiled in milk or water.|The patients were fed a thin gruel.|近义词：porridge, oatmeal|反义词: feast, banquet|词性:noun
gruels:/ˈɡruːəlz/| n. |稀粥，麦片粥（复数）|A thin liquid food of oatmeal or other meal boiled in milk or water (plural form).|The gruels were served lukewarm.|近义词：porridges, oatmeals|反义词: feasts, banquets|词性:noun

词源: From Middle English *gruel, from Anglo-Norman gruel, greuel, from Old North French gruel, greuel (“gruel, coarse meal”), from Late Latin grūtellum, diminutive of grutum (“groats, coarse meal”), from Proto-Germanic *grutą (“groats, crushed grain”). Cognate with Old English grūt (“groats, coarse meal”).
