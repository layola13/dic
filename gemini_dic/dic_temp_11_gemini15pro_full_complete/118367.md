meritorious:/ˌmerɪˈtɔːriəs/
adjective
值得称赞的；有功的
Deserving reward or praise.
Her meritorious service to the community was recognized with an award. 她对社区的杰出服务获得了表彰。
synonyms: praiseworthy, commendable, laudable, deserving
antonyms: blameworthy, reprehensible, discreditable
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *meritorius, from Latin meritum (“reward, merit”), from merēre (“to earn, deserve”).
