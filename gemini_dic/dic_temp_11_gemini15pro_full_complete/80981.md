grad:/ɡræd/| n.|毕业生|A person who has received a degree or diploma on completing a course of study, especially from a college or university.|She's a recent grad of Harvard University. 她最近刚从哈佛大学毕业。|synonyms: graduate, alumnus, alumna|antonyms: undergraduate, student|noun


grad:/ɡræd/| adj.|毕业的|Relating to or characteristic of a graduate or graduation.|The grad student is working on her thesis. 这位研究生正在写她的论文。|synonyms: graduate|antonyms: undergraduate|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
Shortening of graduate, from Latin graduatus, past participle of graduari "to take steps, go, proceed," from gradus "step, pace."
