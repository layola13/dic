megascope: /ˈmɛɡəskoʊp/ | n. | 放大镜；幻灯机；投影仪 | A device for projecting magnified images onto a screen, especially one used for viewing photographs or other opaque objects.  | Example 1: The megascope projected the tiny insect onto the wall, revealing its intricate details.  投影仪将小昆虫投射到墙上，展现出其复杂的细节。

Example 2: Before digital projectors, megascopes were commonly used in classrooms to show slides and photographs. 在数字投影仪出现之前，幻灯机通常用于教室里展示幻灯片和照片。 | 近义词: projector, episcope, magic lantern | 反义词: microscope | 词性: noun
