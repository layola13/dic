non-hydraulic:/ˌnɒn haɪˈdrɔːlɪk/ |adj.|非液压的|Not operated by the pressure of a liquid, especially not operated by water or oil. |The car has non-hydraulic brakes. 这辆车有非液压刹车。|The door uses a non-hydraulic closing mechanism. 这扇门使用非液压关闭装置。|近义词:mechanical, non-powered|反义词:hydraulic|词性:形容词

词源:
"non-" (prefix): From Latin *non*, meaning "not".
"hydraulic": From Greek "hydraulikos" meaning "water organ," later referring to devices operated by water pressure.
