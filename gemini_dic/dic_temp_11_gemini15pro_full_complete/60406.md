eighteen-wheeler:/ˌeɪˈtiːn ˈwiːlər/| n. |十八轮卡车；牵引式挂车|A large truck with eighteen wheels (ten on the tractor and eight on the trailer), used for transporting goods.  It's a common sight on highways across America. |Example 1: The eighteen-wheeler jackknifed on the icy road, blocking traffic for hours. 十八轮卡车在结冰的道路上打滑，阻塞交通数小时。

Example 2:  The company relies on a fleet of eighteen-wheelers to deliver its products nationwide. 该公司依靠一支十八轮卡车车队在全国范围内运送其产品。|近义词：semi-trailer truck, tractor-trailer, big rig|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "small truck" or "delivery van" in certain contexts) |词性:noun
