deferred:/dɪˈfɜːrd/| adj.|延期了的，推迟了的|Postponed or delayed to a later time or date.|例句：The deferred payment plan allows customers to buy now and pay later.  延期付款计划允许顾客现在购买，以后付款。

例句：His deferred gratification led to greater success in the long run. 他延迟满足的习惯从长远来看带来了更大的成功。|近义词：postponed, delayed, shelved, tabled|反义词：immediate, prompt, advanced, expedited|词性:形容词

/dɪˈfɜːr/| vt. vi.|推迟，延期，遵从|To postpone or delay (an action or event) to a later time or date. To put off (something) to a later time.|例句：They deferred the decision until next week. 他们把决定推迟到下周。

例句：I defer to your judgment on this matter.  在这个问题上我遵从你的判断。|近义词：postpone, delay, put off|反义词：expedite, hasten, advance|词性:动词

词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French differer, from Latin differre ‘carry apart, postpone’, from dis- ‘apart’ + ferre ‘carry’.


注意事项:  "deferred" 主要用作形容词，表示“延期了的，推迟了的”。 作为动词时，过去式和过去分词形式为 "deferred"。
