nonbeneficently:/ˌnɒnbɛnɪˈfɪʃəntli/
adverb|非善意地，无益地|In a way that is not beneficial or helpful; harmfully.|He acted nonbeneficently by withholding crucial information.|Synonym: harmfully, detrimentally, malevolently|Antonym: beneficially, helpfully, kindly
Etymology: non- (not) + beneficent (doing good) + -ly (adverbial suffix)
