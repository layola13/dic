orchard: /ˈɔːrtʃərd/ (UK), /ˈɔːrtʃərd/ (US)| n. |果园；果树园|An enclosed area of land planted with fruit trees.|We visited an apple orchard in the fall. 我们秋天参观了一个苹果园。|
例句：
The orchard was filled with the sweet scent of ripe apples. 果园里充满了成熟苹果的甜香。

The family owns a peach orchard in Georgia. 这家人在佐治亚州拥有一个桃园。

近义词：grove, plantation|反义词: - |词性: noun
