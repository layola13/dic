half-clearly:/ˌhæfˈklɪrli/| adv.|半清晰地，不太清楚地|Expressing something that is communicated or understood only partially or vaguely.  It combines "half" indicating partialness and "clearly" indicating clarity.|The instructions were half-clearly explained, leading to confusion.  说明书解释得不太清楚，导致了混乱。|Near synonyms: vaguely, obscurely, partially, indistinctly|Near antonyms: clearly, distinctly, explicitly, unequivocally|adverb
