gaseousness:/ˈɡæsiəsnəs/
n.
胀气；气态
The state or quality of being gaseous; the presence of gas in the stomach or intestines.
He complained of bloating and gaseousness after eating beans.  他抱怨吃完豆子后腹胀和胀气。
flatulence, gas, bloating, wind
solidity
