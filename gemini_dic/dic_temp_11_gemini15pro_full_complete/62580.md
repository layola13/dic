enervative:/ˈɛnərveɪtɪv/ | adj. |使衰弱的；使失去活力的|Causing someone to feel drained of energy or vitality.  Weakening or debilitating.|The enervative climate made it difficult to work.  这种令人衰弱的气候使工作变得困难。|Synonyms: weakening, debilitating, devitalizing, exhausting, sapping, draining |Antonyms: invigorating, energizing, strengthening, revitalizing|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ēnervātivus, from ēnervātus (past participle of ēnervāre “to weaken, enervate”), from ex- “out” + nervus “sinew, nerve.”
