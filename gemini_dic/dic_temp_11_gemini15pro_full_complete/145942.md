overdiscouraging:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈskɜːrɪdʒɪŋ/ | adj. | 极其令人沮丧的 | Excessively discouraging; so discouraging as to be overwhelming or daunting. | The constant rejections were overdiscouraging, and she almost gave up on her dream.  不断的拒绝让她极其沮丧，她几乎放弃了梦想。|Synonyms: disheartening, demoralizing, dispiriting | Antonyms: encouraging, inspiring, uplifting | word property: adjective

overdiscouraging:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈskɜːrɪdʒɪŋ/ | adj. | 极其令人气馁的 |  Making someone lose enthusiasm or hope to a very large degree. |  The overdiscouraging news about the economy made him reconsider his investment plans. 关于经济的极其令人气馁的消息让他重新考虑了他的投资计划。 | Synonyms: debilitating, undermining, daunting | Antonyms:  motivating, heartening, invigorating | word property: adjective
