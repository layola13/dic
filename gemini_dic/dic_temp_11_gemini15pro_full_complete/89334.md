homogony:/həˈmɒɡəni/ /həˈmɑːɡəni/| n. |同配生殖；同性婚配|Reproduction involving the union of gametes that are similar in form and size.  The state of having one spouse at a time.|例句：Homogony is common in some species of algae.  某些种类的藻类普遍存在同配生殖。

例句：Homogony is the legal norm in most Western countries. 在大多数西方国家，一夫一妻制是法律规范。|近义词：monogamy|反义词：polygamy, heterogamy|词性: noun
