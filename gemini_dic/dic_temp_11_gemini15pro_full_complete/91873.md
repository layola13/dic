hyperinsulinaemic:/ˌhaɪpərɪnsjʊlɪˈniːmɪk/ | adj. | 高胰岛素血症的 | Characterized by excess levels of insulin in the blood. |  Hyperinsulinaemic states can lead to hypoglycemia. 高胰岛素血症可导致低血糖。 | synonym: hyperinsulinemic | antonym: hypoinsulinemic | adjective

hyperinsulinemia:/ˌhaɪpərɪnsjʊlɪˈniːmiə/ | n. | 高胰岛素血症 | A condition marked by excessive levels of insulin circulating in the blood. | Hyperinsulinemia is often associated with insulin resistance. 高胰岛素血症常与胰岛素抵抗相关。 | synonym: hyperinsulinism | antonym: hypoinsulinemia | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
hyper- (Greek):  meaning "over," "above," "excessive"
insulin- (Latin insulinum): derived from "insula" (island), referring to the islets of Langerhans in the pancreas where insulin is produced.
-emic (Greek -aemia, Latin -emia):  a suffix meaning "blood condition."
