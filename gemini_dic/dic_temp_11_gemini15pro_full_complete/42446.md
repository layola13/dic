counterdeployments:/ˌkaʊntər dɪˈplɔɪmənts/ | n. |反部署，对抗部署| The act of deploying forces or resources in response to an opponent's deployment, typically to neutralize or counter their threat. |  The general ordered counterdeployments to address the enemy's flanking maneuver. 将军下令反部署以应对敌人的侧翼机动。|Synonyms: countermeasures, countermoves | Antonyms: deployments | noun
