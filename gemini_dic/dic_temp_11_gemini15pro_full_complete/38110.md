compounds:/ˈkɒmpaʊndz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmpaʊndz/ (US)| n. |化合物；混合物；复合词；用围墙围起的场地|A compound is a substance formed when two or more chemical elements are chemically bonded together.|Water is a compound of hydrogen and oxygen. 水是氢和氧的化合物。|synonyms: mixture, blend, composite, complex |antonyms: element, component, ingredient|noun
compounds:/kəmˈpaʊnd/ (UK) /kəmˈpaʊnd/ (US)| vt. |合成；混合；加重；和解|To combine or mix two or more things.|Stress compounds the risk of heart attack. 压力会加重心脏病发作的风险。|synonyms: combine, mix, blend, aggravate, worsen |antonyms: separate, divide, simplify, alleviate, improve|verb
