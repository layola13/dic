lemuriform:/ˌlɛmjʊrɪˈfɔːrm/ | adj. n. |狐猴形的；狐猴亚目的| Relating to or resembling lemurs; belonging to the primate suborder Lemuriformes. | Example: The aye-aye, despite its unusual appearance, is a lemuriform primate.  指猴，尽管它外表奇特，是一种狐猴形灵长动物。

Example: Lemuriform primates are found only on Madagascar and nearby islands. 狐猴形灵长类动物只在马达加斯加和附近的岛屿上发现。| 近义词: lemur-like | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps anthropoid) | 词性: adjective, noun
