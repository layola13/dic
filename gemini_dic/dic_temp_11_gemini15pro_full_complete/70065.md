feudal:/ˈfjuːdl/ | adj. | 封建的 | Relating to a social system that existed in Europe during the Middle Ages, in which people worked and fought for a lord in exchange for land and protection. | The feudal system was dominant in Europe for centuries.  欧洲的封建制度盛行了几个世纪。 |  The king granted lands to his loyal knights under the feudal system. 国王在封建制度下将土地赐予他忠诚的骑士。 |  medieval, aristocratic | democratic, modern | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *feodal, *feudal, from Medieval Latin feudalis, from feudum "fief".
