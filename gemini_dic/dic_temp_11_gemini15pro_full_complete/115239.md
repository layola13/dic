manilio:/məˈnɪlioʊ/| n. |马尼拉麻, 马尼拉绳|A strong fiber obtained from the leaves of the abacá plant (Musa textilis), native to the Philippines, used for making rope, matting, etc.  It's often called Manila hemp.|The sailors used manilio to secure the sails. 水手们用马尼拉麻固定帆。|近义词：abaca, Manila hemp|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps synthetic fibers)|noun
