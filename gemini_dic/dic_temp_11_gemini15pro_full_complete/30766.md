cessions:/ˈseʃənz/ | n. |割让，转让；放弃，屈服|The treaty involved the cession of territory from one country to another. 该条约涉及一个国家向另一个国家割让领土。|例句：The cession of Hong Kong to China marked a significant historical event. 香港割让给中国标志着一个重大的历史事件。

例句：He finally agreed to the cession of his parental rights. 他最终同意放弃他的父母权利。|近义词：surrender, relinquishment, transfer, concession |反义词：acquisition, retention|词性:名词
