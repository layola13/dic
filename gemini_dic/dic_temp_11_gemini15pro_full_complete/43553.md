crazed:/kreɪzd/| adj.|疯狂的，狂热的，着迷的|Driven insane; wildly insane; mentally deranged.  Also, intensely enthusiastic.|The crazed fans screamed for their idol.  那些疯狂的粉丝为他们的偶像尖叫。|Synonyms: insane, mad, demented, lunatic, fanatical, obsessed|Antonyms: sane, rational, calm, composed|adjective

craze:/kreɪz/| n.|狂热，风尚|An enthusiasm for a particular activity or object which appears suddenly and achieves widespread but short-lived popularity.|There was a craze for hula hoops in the 1950s.  20世纪50年代曾出现过一阵呼啦圈热。|Synonyms: fad, trend, fashion, vogue|Antonyms: dislike, aversion|noun
