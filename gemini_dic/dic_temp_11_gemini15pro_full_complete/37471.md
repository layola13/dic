commissionnaire:/kəˌmɪʃəˈnɛər/ | n. |门卫，行李搬运工，跑腿的人|A person employed by a hotel or other establishment to perform errands, especially those involving going outside, such as carrying luggage, delivering messages, or calling taxis.| The commissionnaire opened the door for us.  门卫为我们开了门。 |Synonyms: porter, doorman, attendant| Antonyms: guest, visitor | noun

commissionnaire:/kəˌmɪʃəˈnɛər/ | n. |（尤指法国或比利时的）委托商，代理商|An agent, especially in France or Belgium, who buys and sells goods or acts as a broker.| The commissionnaire handled the sale of the antique furniture. 代理商处理了古董家具的销售。|Synonyms: agent, broker, representative| Antonyms: client, customer | noun
