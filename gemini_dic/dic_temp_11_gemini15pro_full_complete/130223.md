non-serial:/ˌnɒnˈsɪəriəl/ | adj. | 非连续的，非系列的 | Not occurring in a series or sequence; not related to other things in a predictable or regular pattern. |  The artist's paintings were non-serial, each a unique exploration of color and form.  这位艺术家的绘画是非系列的，每一幅都是对色彩和形式的独特探索。

The symptoms appeared in a non-serial fashion, making diagnosis difficult. 这些症状以非连续的方式出现，使诊断变得困难。 | synonyms: discontinuous, sporadic, irregular, random, unconnected | antonyms: serial, sequential, consecutive, continuous, ordered | adjective
