diplomat: /ˈdɪpləmæt/ (US) /ˈdɪpləmæt/ (UK)| n. |外交官|An official representing a country abroad.|The diplomat negotiated a peace treaty.  这位外交官谈判了一项和平条约。|
近义词: envoy, ambassador, consul, negotiator|反义词: warmonger|词性: 名词

diplomat: /ˈdɪpləmæt/ (US) /ˈdɪpləmæt/ (UK)| adj. (less common) |外交的|Relating to diplomacy or diplomats.|He took a diplomatic approach to the sensitive issue. 他对这个敏感问题采取了外交手段。|近义词: tactful, politic|反义词: undiplomatic, blunt|词性: 形容词
