deltaic:/dɛlˈteɪɪk/
adjective
三角洲的；河流三角洲的
Of, relating to, or like a delta.
The deltaic deposits are rich in nutrients. 三角洲沉积物营养丰富。
The Mississippi deltaic plain is slowly sinking. 密西西比三角洲平原正在缓慢下沉。
近义词: alluvial, estuarine
反义词: (无明确的反义词)
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From delta + -ic.  "Delta" comes from the Greek letter Δ (delta), which resembles the triangular shape of a river delta.
