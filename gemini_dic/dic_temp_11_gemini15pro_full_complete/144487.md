outthrows:/ˌaʊtˈθroʊz/
verb
投掷得比…更远；比…投掷次数更多
throws further or more times than
The pitcher outthrew his opponent, striking out ten batters.  这位投手比他的对手投得更好，三振了十名击球手。
近义词: surpasses, exceeds, outperforms
反义词: underperforms, falls short of
词性: verb
