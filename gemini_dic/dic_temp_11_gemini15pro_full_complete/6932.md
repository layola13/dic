anconal:/æŋˈkoʊnl/| adj.|肘的，尺骨的|Relating to the elbow, specifically the point of the elbow.  It refers to the region of the arm related to the olecranon (the bony tip of the elbow).|The anconal fossa is a triangular depression on the posterior surface of the humerus, superior to the trochlea.  肘窝是肱骨后表面，滑车上方的一个三角形凹陷。

The patient experienced anconal pain after falling on his outstretched arm. 病人在摔倒时手臂伸出后感到肘部疼痛。|Near Synonyms:  cubital, olecranal|Antonym:  None (lack of a direct opposite for anatomical location)|Adjective
