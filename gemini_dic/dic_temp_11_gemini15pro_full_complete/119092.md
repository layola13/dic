metrics:/ˈmɛtrɪks/| n.|指标，度量标准|A set of measurements that quantify results.  These measurements are collected and analyzed to track performance, identify trends, and make informed decisions.|The company uses key performance metrics to evaluate employee productivity.|Synonyms: measures, indicators, gauges, criteria|Antonyms: estimations, guesses, approximations|noun
