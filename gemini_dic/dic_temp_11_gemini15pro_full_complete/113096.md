luncheon-meats:/ˈlʌntʃən miːts/ | n. |午餐肉|Pre-prepared or preserved meats, typically sliced and served cold or hot, often in sandwiches. These meats are processed and convenient for quick meals. |例句：I made a sandwich with luncheon meats and cheese. 我用午餐肉和奶酪做了个三明治。

例句：Luncheon meats are a popular choice for picnics. 午餐肉是野餐的热门选择。|近义词：cold cuts, deli meats, processed meats|反义词:fresh meat|词性:名词
