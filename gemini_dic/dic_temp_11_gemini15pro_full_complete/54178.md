disequilibriums:/ˌdɪsɪkwɪˈlɪbriəmz/
|n.|失衡，不平衡|A state of imbalance or instability.|The current disequilibrium in the market is causing price fluctuations. 目前市场的不平衡正在引起价格波动。|
|近义词：imbalances, instabilities|反义词：balances, equilibriums|词性：名词|
|词源：From dis- (not) + equilibrium (balance).|
