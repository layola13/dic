casern:/ˈkæzərn/ (US), /ˈkɑːzən/ (UK)| n. |兵营，营房|A building or group of buildings used to house soldiers.|The soldiers returned to their casern after a long day of training. 士兵们经过一天的训练后回到了他们的营房。|Example: The old casern was converted into apartments.  这座旧兵营被改建成了公寓。|Synonyms: barracks, quarters, garrison|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "home" or "civilian residence" in a loose sense)|noun
