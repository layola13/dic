illustriousness:/ɪˈlʌstriəsnəs/
n.
杰出；卓越；辉煌
The state or quality of being illustrious; eminence; distinction.
His illustriousness is recognized throughout the world.  他的杰出举世闻名。
近义词: eminence, distinction, renown, fame, celebrity
反义词: obscurity, anonymity, insignificance
词性: 名词

词源: From illustrious + -ness.  Illustrious comes from Late Latin illustris "bright, shining," from Latin illustrare "light up, make bright," from in- "in" + lustrare "make bright," related to lustrum "purification, atonement," originally "a purification by sacrifice every five years."
