noncompetitive:/ˌnɑːnkəmˈpɛtɪtɪv/
adjective
非竞争性的；非竞争的
not involving or based on competition
The grant is noncompetitive and available to all eligible organizations.  这个拨款是非竞争性的，所有符合条件的组织都可以申请。
Some enzymes exhibit noncompetitive inhibition. 一些酶表现出非竞争性抑制。
Synonyms: non-contending, cooperative, collaborative
Antonyms: competitive, vying, rivalrous
词性: adjective
