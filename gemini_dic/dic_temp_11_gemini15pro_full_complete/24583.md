brocket:/ˈbrɒkɪt/ (UK) /ˈbrɑːkɪt/ (US)| n.|幼鹿，一岁以下的雄赤鹿或黇鹿|A brocket is a young male deer, typically less than a year old, especially a red deer or fallow deer.|The hunter spotted a small brocket grazing near the edge of the forest. 猎人发现一只小雄鹿在森林边缘吃草。|The young brocket stayed close to its mother. 小雄鹿紧紧地跟着它的妈妈。|Synonyms: fawn, yearling, calf|Antonyms: stag, hart|noun

brocket:/ˈbrɒkɪt/ (UK) /ˈbrɑːkɪt/ (US)| n.|支架，托架|A support or bracket.|The shelf was held up by a metal brocket. 架子由一个金属支架支撑着。|The camera was mounted on a brocket attached to the wall. 相机安装在固定在墙上的支架上。|Synonyms: bracket, support, brace|Antonyms: None|noun
