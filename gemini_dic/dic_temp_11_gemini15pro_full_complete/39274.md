congressman:/ˈkɑːŋɡrəsmən/ (US) /ˈkɒŋɡrəsmən/ (UK)| n. |国会议员（男性）|A male member of a congress, especially the US Congress.  A female member is usually called a congresswoman. |例句：The congressman voted against the bill.  这位国会议员投票反对该法案。

例句：He wrote a letter to his congressman to complain about the new tax law. 他写信给他的国会议员抱怨新的税法。

近义词：representative, legislator, delegate|反义词:citizen, constituent|词性:noun
