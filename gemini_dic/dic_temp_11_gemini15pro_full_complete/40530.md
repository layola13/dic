controlling-interests:/kənˈtrəʊlɪŋ ˈɪntrəsts/ | n. | 控股权；控制性股权 | Ownership of enough voting stock in a company to control its policies and management. |  Example: The company sought to acquire a controlling interest in its main competitor. 该公司试图获得其主要竞争对手的控股权。

Example:  He maintains a controlling interest in several major corporations. 他在几家大公司中保持着控股权。 | Synonyms: majority stake, controlling stake, dominant interest | Antonyms: minority stake, non-controlling interest | noun
