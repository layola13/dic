lampoonists:/læmˈpuːnɪsts/
n.
讽刺作家；嘲弄者
People who publicly criticize someone or something by using ridicule, irony, or sarcasm.
The lampoonists mercilessly mocked the politician's hypocrisy. 讽刺作家无情地嘲笑了这位政客的虚伪。
The magazine is known for its biting satire and its talented lampoonists. 这本杂志以其尖刻的讽刺和才华横溢的讽刺作家而闻名。
synonyms: satirists, mockers, caricaturists, parodists
antonyms: admirers, flatterers, eulogists
noun
