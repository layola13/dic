lutherism:/ˈluːθərɪzəm/
n.
路德教, 路德宗
The doctrines of Martin Luther.
例句：Lutherism is a major branch of Protestant Christianity. 路德教是基督教新教的一个主要分支。

例句：The spread of Lutherism significantly impacted the religious landscape of Europe. 路德教的传播极大地影响了欧洲的宗教格局。

近义词：Lutheranism, Protestantism
反义词：Catholicism
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Martin Luther + -ism.  Named after Martin Luther (1483-1546), a German professor of theology, composer, priest, monk, and a seminal figure in the Protestant Reformation.
