betraying:/bɪˈtreɪɪŋ/
verb
背叛；出卖；辜负；(不经意地)显露
exposing (a feeling or quality) unintentionally
He betrayed his nervousness by laughing loudly. 他因紧张而大笑不止。

例句：
He was accused of betraying his country.
他被指控背叛了他的国家。

By betraying his friends, he lost their trust.
由于背叛了他的朋友，他失去了他们的信任。


近义词：
deceiving, misleading, double-crossing, backstabbing, revealing, divulging, exposing

反义词：
supporting, aiding, helping, protecting, concealing, hiding

词性：
v.  动词 (present participle of betray)
