granted:/ˈɡrɑːntɪd/ /ˈɡrænɪd/| adj. v.|被授予的；理所当然的；|It is taken for granted that everyone knows this. 大家都理所当然地认为每个人都知道这一点。|近义词：given, awarded, assumed|反义词：denied, refused|词性:形容词/动词

granted:/ˈɡrɑːntɪd/ /ˈɡrænɪd/| v.|授予；同意；准予|He was granted permission to leave early. 他获准提前离开。|近义词：allow, permit, give|反义词：deny, refuse, withhold|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *granter (Modern French garantir "to guarantee, warrant"), from Late Latin *credentare "to entrust, commit," from Latin credere "to believe, trust."  The sense shifted from "entrust" to "give, concede."
