gentrification: /ˌdʒɛntrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 中产阶级化；士绅化 | The process of renovating and improving a house or district so that it conforms to middle-class taste. This often involves displacing lower-income residents due to rising property values and costs of living. |  Example 1: The gentrification of the neighborhood has led to a sharp increase in rents. 这个街区的士绅化导致租金急剧上涨。

Example 2:  Many residents are protesting the gentrification that is pushing them out of their homes. 许多居民抗议将他们赶出家园的士绅化。| 近义词: urban renewal, upgrading, revitalization | 反义词: urban decay, blight | 词性: noun
