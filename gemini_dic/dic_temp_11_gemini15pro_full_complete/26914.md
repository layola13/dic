calamine:/ˈkæləˌmaɪn/
noun
炉甘石；菱锌矿
A pink powder consisting of zinc oxide mixed with a small amount of ferric oxide, used in skin lotions and ointments.
Calamine lotion is often used to treat itching. 炉甘石洗剂常用于治疗瘙痒。
近义词:zinc oxide
反义词: (No direct antonym for a mineral)
词性: noun
