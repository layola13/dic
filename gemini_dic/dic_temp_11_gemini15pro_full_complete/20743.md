blanch:/blɑːntʃ/ /blæntʃ/| v. |使变白；漂白；使(脸色)变苍白；用沸水烫(蔬菜) |to make white or pale by extracting color; to scald or parboil in water or steam| The almonds were blanched to remove their skins. 杏仁被烫过以去除外皮。

The sight of blood blanched her cheeks.  看到血，她的脸变得苍白。|近义词：whiten, pale, bleach, scald, parboil |反义词: darken, color, flush |词性:动词

blanched:/blɑːntʃt/ /blæntʃt/| adj. |变白的；苍白的| having been made white or pale|  He had a blanched, frightened look on his face. 他脸上带着苍白而恐惧的表情。 |近义词：white, pale, bleached|反义词: colored, flushed, tanned |词性:形容词
