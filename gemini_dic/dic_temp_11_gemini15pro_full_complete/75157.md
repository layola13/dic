fritterers:/ˈfrɪtərərz/
n.
挥霍者，浪费者
People who fritter away their time or money.
Those fritterers spent their inheritance on frivolous purchases. 那些挥霍者把他们的遗产花在了无用的东西上。
spendthrifts, wasters, squanderers
savers, economizers
noun
