nonviscid:/nɒnˈvɪskɪd/ | adj. | 无粘性的，不粘稠的 | Having little or no viscosity; not viscous.  A nonviscid fluid flows easily. | The lubricant was nonviscid and flowed easily through the engine. 润滑油无粘性，很容易流过发动机。 | Near synonyms: inviscid, fluid, mobile | Antonyms: viscous, sticky, thick | adjective
