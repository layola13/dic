bright:/braɪt/| adj.|明亮的，聪明的，鲜艳的|Shining strongly; intelligent and quick-witted; vivid in color.|The sun is very bright today. 今天阳光明媚。|近义词：shiny, intelligent, vivid, brilliant, radiant, luminous, smart, clever|反义词：dull, dim, dark, stupid, gloomy|词性:形容词
bright:/braɪt/| n.|光亮，光明|The quality of being full of light; shining.|The bright of the moon lit up the path. 月光照亮了小路。|近义词：light, shine, radiance|反义词：darkness, dimness|词性:名词
bright:/braɪt/| adv.|明亮地，欢快地|In a bright manner; cheerfully.|The fire burned brightly. 火烧得很旺。|近义词：brightly, cheerfully, merrily|反义词：dimly, gloomily|词性:副词

词源: From Old English *beorht (not attested), from Proto-Germanic *berhtaz, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰerǵʰ- (“to shine, white, bright”). Cognate with Dutch brecht (“brittle”), German berchten (“to brighten”), Swedish bjärt (“bright”).
