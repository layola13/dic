bukar:/ˈbuːkɑːr/ | n. |  山羊（尤指雄性，用于祭祀或食用）| A male goat, especially one kept for sacrifice or slaughter. |  The villagers sacrificed a bukar to appease the gods. 村民们献祭了一只公羊来安抚神灵。| Synonyms: billy goat, he-goat | Antonyms: ewe, nanny goat | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Malay *bukar*, ultimately from Proto-Austronesian *buqel*.  Cognates include Tagalog *bukál*.
