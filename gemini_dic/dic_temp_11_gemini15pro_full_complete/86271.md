heaves:/hiːvz/ | n. | 呕吐; 举起; 喘息; 胀起 | the act of lifting or raising something heavy with great effort; a strong outward breath; a rhythmic rising and falling | The ship heaves on the waves. 船在波涛中起伏。 | 近义词: lifts, raises, hoists, pumps, pants, gasps | 反义词: drops, lowers, falls | noun

heaves:/hiːvz/ | v. | 举起; 呕吐; (用力地)扔; 喘息 | to lift or haul (something heavy) with great effort; to throw (something heavy); to breathe in and out (with obvious effort); to rise and fall rhythmically | He heaved the anchor up. 他用力把锚拉了起来。 | 近义词: lifts, raises, hoists, throws, hurls, pants, gasps | 反义词: drops, lowers, catches | verb
