overpoweringness:/ˌoʊvərˈpaʊərɪŋnɪs/
n.
压倒性；不可抗拒性；极度强大
The state or quality of being overpowering; overwhelming force or strength.
The overpoweringness of her grief made it difficult to speak.  她极度的悲伤让她难以开口。
Near Synonyms: intensity, strength, power, force, dominance
Antonyms: weakness, feebleness, powerlessness
noun


overpowering:/ˌoʊvərˈpaʊərɪŋ/
adj.
压倒性的；不可抗拒的；极度强大的
Having great power or force; overwhelming.
The heat was overpowering.  酷热难耐。
Near Synonyms: overwhelming, intense, strong, potent, formidable
Antonyms: weak, mild, gentle
adjective
