fishbowl:/ˈfɪʃboʊl/ (US) /ˈfɪʃbəʊl/ (UK)| n. |鱼缸；金鱼缸；像鱼缸一样的环境|A transparent container, typically rounded, filled with water and used to keep fish.|The goldfish swam lazily around the fishbowl. 金鱼懒洋洋地在鱼缸里游来游去。|近义词：aquarium, tank|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "open water")|noun
