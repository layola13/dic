bicolligate:/baɪˈkɒlɪɡeɪt/| adj.|双重的，双叶的|Having two leaves, leaflets, or parts.|The bicolligate fern has fronds divided into two leaflets.|近义词：bifoliate, double|反义词: simple, single|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Latin *bi-*, "two" + *colligare*, "to bind together," from *com-*, "together" + *ligare*, "to tie, bind."  Hence, "bound together in two parts."
