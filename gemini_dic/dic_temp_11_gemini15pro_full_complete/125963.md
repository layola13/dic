musicologists:/ˌmjuːzɪˈkɒlədʒɪsts/
n.
音乐学家
Experts in the field of music, who study its history, theory, and cultural context.
Musicologists often specialize in a particular period, genre, or composer.  音乐学家通常专攻某个特定时期、流派或作曲家。
The musicologists debated the authenticity of the newly discovered manuscript.  音乐学家们对新发现的手稿的真伪进行了辩论。
近义词：music scholars, music historians
反义词: N/A
词性: noun

Etymology:
From music + -ology (study of) + -ist (one who studies).  源自 music（音乐）+ -ology（…学）+ -ist（…学家）。
