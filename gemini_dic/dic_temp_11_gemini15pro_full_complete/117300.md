meconium:/mɪˈkoʊniəm/ /məˈkəʊnɪəm/
n.
胎粪
A dark green sticky substance forming the first feces of a newborn infant.
The nurse checked the baby's diaper for meconium.  护士检查了婴儿的尿布，看看有没有胎粪。

The baby passed meconium shortly after birth. 婴儿出生后不久就排出了胎粪。


近义词:
first stool,  newborn stool

反义词:
(No direct antonym, as it's a unique substance)


词性:
noun
