lumbers:/ˈlʌmbərz/ | n. | 木材；废旧木材（lumber 的复数）| timber; pieces of wood that have been cut for use | The lumbers were stacked high in the yard. 木材堆放在院子里很高。| synonyms: timber, wood | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for physical lumber) | noun
