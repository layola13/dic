california:/ˌkælɪˈfɔːrniə/ /ˌkæləˈfɔːrnjə/| n. |加利福尼亚州；加利福尼亚(简称加州)|A state in the western United States, on the Pacific coast. |例句：California is known for its beautiful beaches. 加利福尼亚以其美丽的海滩而闻名。

例句：I'm planning a trip to California next summer. 我计划明年夏天去加利福尼亚旅行。|近义词：Golden State(昵称)|反义词: None.|词性:名词

词源: The name California is believed to have come from a 16th-century Spanish romance novel, *Las Sergas de Esplandián*, by Garci Rodríguez de Montalvo.  The novel describes a fictional island called California, ruled by Queen Calafia.
