outskates:/ˌaʊtˈskeɪts/
verb
(比…更擅长滑冰)out-skate
He easily outskates all the other players on the team. 他轻松地超越了队里所有其他队员的滑冰技术。
outskate someone in something 在某方面胜过某人
She outskated her rivals in the competition. 她在比赛中滑冰技术胜过对手。
近义词:surpass, excel, outdo, outperform
反义词:lose to, be inferior to
词性:v.
