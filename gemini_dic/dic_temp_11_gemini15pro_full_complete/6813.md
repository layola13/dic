anarchistic:/ˌænərˈkɪstɪk/ | adj. |无政府主义的；无政府状态的 | Relating to or supporting anarchy; lacking any principle of organization or control. |  The group held anarchistic views, believing in the abolition of all government.  该团体持有无政府主义观点，相信应该废除所有政府。 | synonyms: rebellious, revolutionary, dissident, lawless, ungovernable | antonyms:  obedient, compliant, law-abiding, authoritarian, governmental | adjective


anarchist:/ˈænərkɪst/ | n. |无政府主义者 | A person who believes in or tries to bring about anarchy. | The anarchist threw a Molotov cocktail at the police car.  那个无政府主义者向警车投掷了燃烧瓶。 | synonyms:  rebel, revolutionary, insurgent, dissident | antonyms: conformist, traditionalist, conservative | noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From French *anarchiste*, from Ancient Greek *anarkhos* (without a ruler) *an-* (without) + *arkhos* (ruler).
