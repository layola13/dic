boothale:/ˈbuːθeɪl/| n.|摊位啤酒，庆祝啤酒|Ale sold at a booth, especially at a fair or festival.  Historically, it referred to ale brewed for a specific event or celebration.|The boothale at the county fair was surprisingly good.  县集市上的摊位啤酒出奇的好喝。|近义词：ale, beer|反义词:None|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Old English *bōþealu, from bōþ (booth) + ealu (ale).
