hematinic:/ˌhɛməˈtɪnɪk/ /ˌhiːməˈtɪnɪk/
noun
补血药；补血剂
A medicine or other substance that increases the number of red blood cells or the amount of hemoglobin in the blood.
The doctor prescribed a hematinic to treat her anemia. 医生给她开了补血药来治疗她的贫血。
synonyms: antianemic,  erythropoietic
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but consider words related to causing anemia, such as "hemolytic")
adjective
补血的
Increasing the number of red blood cells or the amount of hemoglobin in the blood.
Iron is a hematinic mineral. 铁是一种补血矿物质。
synonyms: antianemic, erythropoietic
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but consider words related to causing anemia, such as "hemolytic")
