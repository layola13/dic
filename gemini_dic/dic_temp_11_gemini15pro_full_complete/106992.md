lacquerer:/ˈlækərər/ | n. | 漆工；涂漆工人 | A person who applies lacquer. | The lacquerer carefully applied several coats to the wooden box.  漆工小心地在木盒上涂了好几层漆。| 近义词: varnisher, finisher | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "remover" in the context of removing lacquer) | noun

lacquer:/ˈlækər/ | vt. | 涂漆；上漆 | To coat with lacquer. | He lacquered the table to a high gloss. 他把桌子涂得光亮亮的。| 近义词: varnish, paint | 反义词: strip, remove | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French "laquier," from Portuguese "lacre," ultimately from Arabic "lakk" (lac, resin).  The word traces back to the resinous substance used in traditional lacquering.
