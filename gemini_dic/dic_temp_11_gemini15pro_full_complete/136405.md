nonretainments:/ˌnɑːnriˈteɪnmənts/ | n. | 未保留款项，扣留款 | Amounts of money that are withheld from payments, typically in construction contracts, until a project is completed satisfactorily. | The contractor is frustrated by the high percentage of nonretainments. 承包商对高比例的未保留款感到沮丧。|  The nonretainments will be released upon final inspection.  未保留款项将在最终验收后发放。 | synonyms: retentions (opposite meaning - context-dependent), holdbacks | antonyms: payments, disbursements | noun
