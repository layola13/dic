harpoon:/hɑːrˈpuːn/ | n. vt. | 鱼叉; 用鱼叉捕 | A barbed spear-like missile attached to a rope, used for hunting large fish or whales.  To strike, catch, or kill with a harpoon. | The whaler hurled the harpoon at the massive whale. 捕鲸人将鱼叉掷向巨大的鲸鱼。 | The fisherman harpooned a large tuna. 渔夫用鱼叉叉住了一条大金枪鱼。 | 近义词:spear, lance | 反义词: None | noun, verb
