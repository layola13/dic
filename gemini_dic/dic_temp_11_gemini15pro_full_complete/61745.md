empanelment:/ɪmˈpænəlmənt/ | n. | 组成陪审团；遴选陪审团；陪审团名单 | The act of selecting and swearing in a jury.  The list of jurors so selected. | The empanelment of the jury took several hours.  组成陪审团花了好几个小时。

The lawyer challenged the empanelment of the jury due to a perceived bias.  律师因察觉到偏见而对陪审团的组成提出了质疑。 | 近义词: jury selection, impaneling | 反义词: dismissal of jury | 词性: 名词
