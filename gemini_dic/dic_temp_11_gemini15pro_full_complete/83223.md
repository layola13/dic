gybe:/dʒaɪb/ | v. n. | 转帆；转向 | To change the course of a sailboat by swinging the stern through the wind, causing the boom to shift from one side to the other.  | The captain ordered the crew to gybe as they approached the buoy. 船长命令船员们在接近浮标时转帆。 |  The yacht gybed gracefully in the strong wind.  帆船在强风中优雅地转向。| 近义词: jibe | 反义词: tack | 词性: 动词，名词
