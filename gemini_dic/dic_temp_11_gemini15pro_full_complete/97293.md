influxively: /ɪnˈflʌksɪvli/ | adv. | 流入地，涌入地 | In a manner characterized by influx; flowing or pouring in.  This word is not commonly used and might be considered non-standard or even archaic. |  The tourists influxively entered the city for the festival. 游客们涌入城市参加节日庆典。|  The water influxively poured into the basin after the dam broke.  大坝决堤后，水涌入盆地。 | 近义词: floodingly, torrentially, copiously | 反义词: gradually, slowly, tricklingly | 词性: 副词

词源:  Derived from "influx" (n.), which comes from Latin "influxus," past participle of "influere" ("to flow in"), from "in-" ("in") + "fluere" ("to flow").
