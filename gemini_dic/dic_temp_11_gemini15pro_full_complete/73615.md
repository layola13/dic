forewaste:/ˈfɔːrweɪst/
n.
损耗，浪费，破坏；(尤指)森林的过伐滥伐
destruction or waste, especially of woodlands.
The forewaste of the Amazon rainforest is a global concern. 亚马逊雨林的过度砍伐是一个全球性的问题。
The king lamented the forewaste of his kingdom's natural resources. 国王对王国自然资源的浪费感到悲痛。
近义词：destruction, waste, depletion, ruin, devastation
反义词：conservation, preservation, protection
