battlefront: /ˈbætlˌfrʌnt/ | n. | 战线，斗争前线 |The area where fighting takes place, especially in a war. | The soldiers were sent to the battlefront. 士兵们被派往了前线。|  The battlefront shifted constantly during the war. 战争期间战线不断变化。 |synonyms: frontline, front line, firing line, front, theater (of war), theater of operations | antonyms: rear, base | noun

battlefront: /ˈbætlˌfrʌnt/ | n. | (比喻)斗争的重要领域 |An important area of activity where there is a lot of conflict and disagreement. | Education has become a key battlefront in the fight for social justice. 教育已成为争取社会正义斗争的关键领域。| The company is on the battlefront of technological innovation. 该公司处于技术创新斗争的前沿。 | synonyms:  arena, field, sphere, domain | antonyms: peace, harmony, agreement | noun
