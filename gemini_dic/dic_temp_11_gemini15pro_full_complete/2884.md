advertisable:/ˌædvərˈtaɪzəbl/
adjective
可宣传的；适于做广告的
Suitable for being advertised.
This new product is highly advertisable due to its unique features.  这款新产品由于其独特的特性非常适合做广告。
The company's ethical practices make them easily advertisable. 该公司的道德规范使其易于宣传。
synonyms: promotable, marketable, publicizable
antonyms: unmarketable, unpromotable
词性: adjective
