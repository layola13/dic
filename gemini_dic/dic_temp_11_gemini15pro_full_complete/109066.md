legalizes:/ˈliːɡəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使合法化；将…合法化
To make something legal; to give legal status to something.
The government legalized the sale of marijuana. 政府将大麻销售合法化。
synonyms: legitimizes, authorizes, sanctions
antonyms: prohibits, bans, criminalizes
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From legal + -ize.  Legal comes from Old French legal, from Latin legalis "of or pertaining to law," from lex (genitive legis) "law."  The suffix -ize forms verbs meaning "to make or cause to be," from French -iser, from Greek -izein.
