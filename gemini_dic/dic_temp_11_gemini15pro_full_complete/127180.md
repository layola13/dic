naturalizes:/ˈnætʃrəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
使归化；使入籍；移植；使适应
grants citizenship to (a foreigner).  To introduce and establish (a plant or animal) in a new region or environment. To adopt (something foreign) as one's own.
Many immigrants have naturalized in recent years. 近年来，许多移民已经入籍。
The plant has naturalized in this area. 这种植物已经在这个地区适应了环境。
近义词:acclimatize, adapt, assimilate, integrate
反义词:alienate, expatriate
词性:v.
