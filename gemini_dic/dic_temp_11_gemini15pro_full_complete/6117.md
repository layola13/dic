ametabolous:/ˌeɪmɪˈtæbələs/
adj.
无变态的；不变态的
Undergoing no metamorphosis; developing without marked changes in form between the immature stages and the adult.
Silverfish are ametabolous insects.  衣鱼是不变态昆虫。
近义词:ametabolic
反义词:metabolic, holometabolous
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Greek *a* (not) + *metabolē* (change) + *-ous* (adjective suffix).
