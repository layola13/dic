interfiltrated: /ˌɪntərˈfɪltreɪtɪd/

interfiltrated: /ˌɪntərˈfɪltreɪtɪd/
|adj. |渗透的，渗入的|having been permeated or mixed with something else|The interfiltrated troops caused confusion in the enemy ranks. 渗透的部队在敌军队伍中造成了混乱。|Synonyms: permeated, infiltrated, penetrated, pervaded|Antonyms: separated, segregated|adjective
