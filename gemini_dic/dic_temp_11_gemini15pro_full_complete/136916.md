nonsoluble:/nɒnˈsɒljʊbl/ /nɑːnˈsɑːljəbl/| adj.|不溶的，不能溶解的|Not capable of being dissolved in a liquid.|例句：Sand is nonsoluble in water. 沙子不溶于水。|例句：This chemical compound is nonsoluble in most organic solvents. 这种化合物在大多数有机溶剂中不溶。|近义词：insoluble|反义词：soluble|词性:形容词
Etymology->
From non- + soluble, from Latin solubilis, from solvere "to loosen, dissolve."
