acropodium: /ˌækrəˈpoʊdiəm/
noun
肢端；远端
The terminal part of a limb (hand or foot).
The delicate acropodium of the jerboa allows it to navigate the shifting sands.  跳鼠精巧的肢端使它能够在流沙中穿行。
Synonyms: extremity, appendage
Antonyms: proximal part, base
