inextirpable:/ˌɪnɪkˈstɜːpəbl/
adjective
无法根除的；难以消灭的
not capable of being extirpated or destroyed; ingrained
例句：His hatred for his brother was deep and seemingly inextirpable. 他对兄弟的仇恨根深蒂固，似乎无法消除。

例句：The weeds in my garden are inextirpable; no matter how many I pull, they always come back. 我花园里的杂草无法根除；无论我拔掉多少，它们总是会再长出来。

近义词：ineradicable, ingrained, deep-rooted, persistent, tenacious
反义词：extirpable, eradicable, removable, superficial
词性：adjective
