inhospitably:/ɪnˈhɑːspɪtəbli/ | adv. | 不友好地，冷淡地，荒凉地 |  in a way that is not welcoming or generous, especially to guests; in a barren and desolate way. | The remote island, battered by constant storms, seemed inhospitably bleak.  这个偏远的岛屿，不断受到暴风雨的袭击，显得荒凉凄凉。|  The host treated his guests inhospitably, offering them nothing to eat or drink.  主人对他的客人很不友好，既不给他们吃也不给他们喝。|  unwelcomingly, unkindly, coldly, bleakly, barrenly | welcomingly, warmly, hospitably, kindly, generously | adverb
