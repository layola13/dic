outpeoples:/ˌaʊtˈpiːpəlz/
|v.| 超越，胜过（尤指在人数或力量上）|
|To surpass or exceed in number or power, especially of a population.|
|The invaders outpeopled the native inhabitants and eventually took over the land. 入侵者的人口超过了当地居民，最终占领了这片土地。|
|The small country was easily outpeopled by its larger neighbors. 这个小国的人口很容易被其较大的邻国超越。|
|surpass, exceed, outnumber, outstrip|
|be outnumbered, be surpassed|
|verb|
