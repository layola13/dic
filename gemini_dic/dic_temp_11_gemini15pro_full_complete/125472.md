multiplitarian:/ˌmʌltɪplɪˈtɛəriən/
| adj. n.|多种的，多样的；坚持多种观点的人|Relating to or characterized by multiplicity; adhering to multiple viewpoints or perspectives.|例句：The multiplitarian nature of the problem makes it difficult to solve.  这个问题的多样性使得它难以解决。

例句：He considers himself a multiplitarian, embracing both scientific and spiritual explanations. 他认为自己是一个坚持多种观点的人，同时接受科学和精神层面的解释。|近义词：diverse, varied, multifaceted, pluralistic|反义词：uniform, homogeneous, singular|词性:形容词, 名词


Etymology:
From "multiple" (ultimately from Latin "multus," meaning "much") + "-itarian" (a suffix denoting adherence to a particular doctrine or system).
