clupeoid:/ˈkluːpiɔɪd/| adj. n.|鲱科动物的，鲱科鱼|Relating to or resembling a herring; a fish of the herring family.|例句：The clupeoid fishes are an important food source worldwide. 鲱科鱼类是世界范围内的重要食物来源。

例句：Sardines and anchovies are clupeoid fish. 沙丁鱼和凤尾鱼都是鲱科鱼类。|近义词：herring-like, |反义词: (No direct antonym, could use terms like "non-clupeoid" or names of specific fish families that are not clupeoids like "salmonid") |词性：adjective, noun


Etymology:
From  *clupea*  "herring" (from Latin *clupea*, related to Greek *klypeos* "shield") + *-oid* "resembling".
