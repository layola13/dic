huffingly:/ˈhʌfɪŋli/| adv.|气呼呼地，愤愤地|In a manner characterized by huffs; with annoyance or indignation.  Expressing displeasure or impatience through forceful exhalations of breath.|She stomped off huffingly after being told she couldn't have dessert. 她被告知不能吃甜点后，气呼呼地跺着脚走了。|Synonyms: angrily, resentfully, indignantly, sullenly, petulantly|Antonyms: cheerfully, happily, pleasantly, contentedly|Adverb
