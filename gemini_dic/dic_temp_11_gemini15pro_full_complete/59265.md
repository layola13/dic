eachwhere: /ˈiːtʃˌweər/ | adv. | 各处，到处 |  Not a standard English word. A blend of "each" and "everywhere," intending to mean "in every place."  Consider using "everywhere" instead. | Example:  (Incorrect usage) Eachwhere I looked, there were flowers.  (Correct usage) Everywhere I looked, there were flowers. 各处我都看到了鲜花。| 近义词: everywhere, throughout | 反义词: nowhere | 词性: 副词
