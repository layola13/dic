outcheat:/ˌaʊtˈtʃiːt/
verb
以欺骗胜过
to cheat more or better than someone else
He outcheated his opponent in the poker game. 他在扑克游戏中比对手更会作弊。
近义词: outsmart, outwit, outmaneuver
反义词: be outcheated, lose to cheating
