miscasualty:/ˌmɪsˈkæʒuəlti/
n.|误伤，意外伤亡|An instance of accidental injury or death, especially in war.  A person injured or killed accidentally, especially in war.|The report detailed the tragic miscasualty of civilians during the bombing raid.  这份报告详细描述了轰炸袭击中平民的悲惨意外伤亡。|Near synonyms: accidental death, unintended loss, collateral damage, friendly fire (in military context)|Near antonyms: intentional killing, targeted attack
词性: noun
