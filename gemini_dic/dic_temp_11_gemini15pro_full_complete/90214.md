houndstooth:/ˈhaʊndzˌtuːθ/ /ˈhaʊndzˌtuːθ/
noun|千鸟格，犬牙纹|A duotone textile pattern characterized by broken checks or abstract four-pointed shapes, often in black and white, resembling a canine tooth.|She wore a houndstooth jacket. 她穿了一件千鸟格外套。|Synonyms: dogtooth, checks|Antonyms: solid color|n.

houndstooth:/ˈhaʊndzˌtuːθ/ /ˈhaʊndzˌtuːθ/
adjective|千鸟格的，犬牙纹的|Made of or patterned with houndstooth.|He has a houndstooth blazer. 他有一件千鸟格的西装外套。|Synonyms: dogtooth, checked|Antonyms: solid-colored|adj.
