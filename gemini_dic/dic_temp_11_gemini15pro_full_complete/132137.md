noncultivability:/ˌnɒnkʌltɪvəˈbɪləti/ |n.| 不可培养性 |The noncultivability of many microorganisms presents a challenge for researchers. 许多微生物的不可培养性给研究人员带来了挑战。| This bacterium's noncultivability makes it difficult to study in the lab.  这种细菌的不可培养性使其难以在实验室中研究。 |近义词: unculturability|反义词: cultivability|词性: 名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
cultivable (adjective): from cultivate + -able
cultivate (verb): from Latin cultivare "to till, cultivate," from cultus "care, cultivation," past participle of colere "to till, cultivate, inhabit"
-ability (suffix): from French -abilité, from Latin -abilitatem (nominative -abilitas), from -abilis "-able"
