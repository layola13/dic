life-and-death:/ˈlaɪf ən ˈdɛθ/
adj.
生死攸关的，极其重要的
A matter of life and death; vitally important.
The soldier faced a life-and-death situation on the battlefield.  这个士兵在战场上面临着生死攸关的局面。
The surgery was a life-and-death operation.  这个手术是一场生死攸关的手术。
近义词:crucial, critical, vital, essential
反义词:trivial, unimportant, insignificant
词性:adjective
词源:Compound of 'life' and 'death', both with Germanic roots.  由“life”和“death”两个词复合而成，两者都源于日耳曼语。
