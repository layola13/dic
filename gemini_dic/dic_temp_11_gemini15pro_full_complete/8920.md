antiguerrilla:/ˌæntiɡəˈrɪlə/ | adj. | 反游击队的 |  Of or relating to measures or activities designed to counter guerrilla warfare. | The army launched an antiguerrilla campaign in the mountains. 军队在山区发动了反游击战役。| synonyms: counterinsurgency, counterguerrilla | antonyms: guerrilla, insurgent | adjective

antiguerrilla:/ˌæntiɡəˈrɪlə/ | n. | 反游击队员 | A person or force engaged in antiguerrilla warfare. | The antiguerrillas were trained in jungle combat. 反游击队员接受过丛林战训练。| synonyms: counterinsurgent | antonyms: guerrilla, insurgent | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix) : against, opposed to (from Greek anti-)
guerrilla (noun) : a member of a small independent group fighting against a larger regular force (from Spanish guerrilla, diminutive of guerra "war")
