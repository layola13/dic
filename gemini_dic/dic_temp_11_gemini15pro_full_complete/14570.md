aviary:/ˈeɪviəri/ (US), /ˈeɪvɪəri/ (UK)| n.|鸟舍，鸟类饲养场|A place, such as a large cage, building, or enclosure, where birds are kept.|The zoo's aviary housed a variety of exotic birds.  动物园的鸟舍里饲养着各种各样的珍奇鸟类。

The aviary was filled with the songs of colorful birds.  鸟舍里充满了五彩缤纷的鸟儿的歌声。

|近义词：birdhouse, cage, enclosure|反义词:|noun
