crusts:/krʌsts/
n.
硬皮;外壳;面包皮
The hard outer surface of something, especially bread.
The children left the crusts of their sandwiches. 孩子们剩下了三明治的硬皮。
rind, shell, covering
center, inside
noun

crusts:/krʌsts/
n.
(地球的)地壳
The outermost layer of the Earth.
The Earth's crust is constantly shifting. 地球的地壳不断变化。
tectonic plates, lithosphere
core, mantle
noun
