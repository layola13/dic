arborous:/ˈɑːrbərəs/| adj.|树木繁茂的，多树的|Relating to, resembling, or inhabiting trees; wooded.  Characterized by trees.|例句：The arborous landscape was a haven for wildlife.  树木繁茂的景观是野生动物的避难所。

例句：They built their cabin in an arborous setting. 他们在树木繁茂的环境中建造了他们的小屋。|近义词：wooded, sylvan, forested|反义词：barren, treeless|词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *arborōsus*, derived from *arbor* ("tree").
