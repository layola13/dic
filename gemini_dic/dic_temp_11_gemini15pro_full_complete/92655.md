iBoat:/ˈaɪboʊt/
noun|充气船，橡皮艇|An inflatable boat, often made of PVC or Hypalon, typically powered by an outboard motor and used for recreational purposes.|I took my iBoat out on the lake for some fishing. 我带着我的充气船去湖里钓鱼了。|Synonyms: inflatable boat, dinghy, raft|Antonyms: ship, yacht|n.
