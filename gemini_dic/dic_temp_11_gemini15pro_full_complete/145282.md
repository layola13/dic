overbounteously: /ˌoʊvərˈbaʊntiəsli/
adverb
过度慷慨地；过分丰富地
in an excessively generous or abundant manner.
He overbounteously showered her with gifts. 他过分慷慨地送给她很多礼物。
近义词: lavishly, profusely, excessively, extravagantly
反义词: sparingly, meagerly, stingily
词性: adverb

overbounteous: /ˌoʊvərˈbaʊntiəs/
adjective
过度慷慨的；过分丰富的
excessively generous or abundant.
The overbounteous harvest filled the barns to overflowing.  丰收的收成使粮仓堆得满满的。
近义词: lavish, profuse, excessive, extravagant
反义词: sparing, meager, stingy
词性: adjective

词源:  From over + bounteous, from Middle English *bountevous, from bounte + -ous.  Ultimately from Old French bonté.
