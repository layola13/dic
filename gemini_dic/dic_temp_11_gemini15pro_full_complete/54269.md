disgregation:/dɪsˌɡrɪˈɡeɪʃən/
n.
分解，瓦解，崩溃
the process of breaking into separate parts or fragments; disintegration
The disgregation of the Roman Empire took centuries. 罗马帝国的瓦解历经数个世纪。
近义词：disintegration, breakdown, decomposition, crumbling
反义词：integration, unification, consolidation
词性：名词

词源：From Latin disgregationem, accusative of disgregatio (“separation”), from disgrego (“separate, disperse”), from dis- (“apart”) + grego (“gather, collect”).
