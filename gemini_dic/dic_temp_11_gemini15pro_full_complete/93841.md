immitigably:/ɪˈmɪtɪɡəbli/
adjective
无法缓和地，不可调和地
not capable of being mitigated, appeased, or softened.
His immitigable anger led to the destruction of the village. 他无法缓和的愤怒导致了村庄的毁灭。
Near synonyms: relentless, implacable, unyielding, inexorable
Near antonyms: mitigable, appeasable, placable, yielding
