occamy:/ˈɒkəmi/| n.|奥卡姆剃刀原则; 节俭原则| A problem-solving principle that, when presented with competing hypothetical answers to a problem, one should select the answer that makes the fewest assumptions.  It's often paraphrased as "the simplest explanation is usually the best."|Example: Scientists often apply Occam's razor when developing theories.  科学家们在发展理论时经常应用奥卡姆剃刀原则。

Example:  He used Occam's razor to eliminate the least likely explanation for the strange phenomenon. 他用奥卡姆剃刀原则排除了对这种奇怪现象最不可能的解释。|近义词：parsimony, simplicity, law of parsimony, principle of parsimony|反义词: complexity, elaboration|词性:noun
