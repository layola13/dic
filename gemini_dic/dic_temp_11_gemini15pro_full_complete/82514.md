gsm:/dʒiːɛsˈɛm/ | n. | 全球移动通信系统（Global System for Mobile communications） | A globally accepted standard for digital cellular communication.  |  This phone operates on the GSM network.  这款手机在GSM网络上运行。 | synonyms: mobile network, cellular network | antonyms:  (GSM doesn't have a direct antonym, as it's a specific technology.  Perhaps "landline" could be considered a contrasting technology.) | noun
