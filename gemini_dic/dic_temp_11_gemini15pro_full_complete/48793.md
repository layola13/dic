deflector:/dɪˈflɛktər/| n.|偏转器，导流板|a device that deflects something|The car's deflector prevented the rocks from hitting the windshield. 汽车的导流板防止石头撞到挡风玻璃上。|synonyms: shield, guard, baffle|antonyms: conduit, channel|noun

deflect:/dɪˈflɛkt/| v.|使转向，使偏斜|to cause something to change direction|The goalie deflected the puck with his stick. 守门员用球杆挡开了冰球。|synonyms: divert, deviate, avert|antonyms: attract, focus, concentrate|verb
