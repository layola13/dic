is:/ɪz/| v.|是|The third person singular present tense form of *be*.  Used to indicate existence, identity, or state of being.|He is a doctor. 他是一位医生.|近义词：exists, represents|反义词:isn't, are, am|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *is*, from Proto-Germanic *isti*, from Proto-Indo-European *esti*. Cognates include German *ist*, Dutch *is*, and Latin *est*.

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
He is tall. 他很高。
She is a student. 她是一名学生。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
He is playing basketball. 他正在打篮球。
She is reading a book. 她正在读书。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
He is gone. 他已经走了。（相当于He has gone.)
The work is finished. 工作已经完成了。(相当于The work has been finished.)


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
He is tired because he has been working all day.  他很累，因为他工作了一整天。（is tired 描述目前状态，has been working 解释原因）
The ground is wet; it has been raining. 地面是湿的；一直在下雨。(is wet 描述目前状态，has been raining 解释原因）

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He was happy. 他很高兴。
She was at home. 她在家里。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
He was walking home when it started to rain. 他正走回家，这时开始下雨了。
She was cooking dinner when the phone rang. 她正在做饭时，电话响了。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
He had already left when I arrived. 我到的时候他已经离开了。
She had finished her homework before dinner.  她在晚饭前已经完成了作业。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
He had been working for ten hours before he finally went to bed. 他连续工作了十个小时才终于上床睡觉。
She had been studying English for two years before she moved to London. 在她搬到伦敦之前，她已经学习了两年英语。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
He will be there tomorrow. 他明天会在那里。
She will be happy to see you. 她会很高兴见到你。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
He will be working tomorrow. 他明天要工作。
She will be traveling next week. 她下周要去旅行。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
He will have finished the project by next month.  他将在下个月之前完成这个项目。
She will have graduated by the time you get back. 你回来的时候她就已经毕业了。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next year, he will have been living in London for five years. 到明年，他将在伦敦生活五年了。
By the time she finishes, she will have been working on this painting for over a year.  到她完成的时候，她将在这幅画上工作一年多了。


虚拟语气:
If he were here, he would know what to do. 如果他在这里，他会知道该怎么做。
I wish she were here. 我希望她在这里。

被动语态:
The letter is written by John. 这封信是约翰写的。
The house is being built.  房子正在建造中。


疑问句:
Is he a doctor? 他是医生吗？
Is she happy? 她快乐吗？


否定句:
He is not a doctor. 他不是医生。
She is not happy. 她不快乐。
