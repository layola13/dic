defray:/dɪˈfreɪ/ | vt. |支付，承担费用| To pay the cost or expenses of something. | The company will defray all your travel expenses.  公司将承担你所有的旅行费用。|  The scholarship will defray the cost of tuition. 奖学金将支付学费。| synonyms: pay, cover, bear, meet, settle | antonyms: charge, bill, invoice | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *desfraier, from des- (apart, away) + fraier (to rub, break).  The original sense was "to break up expenses," implying to distribute the cost among several parties. This evolved into the modern meaning of simply paying the expenses.
