bankless:/ˈbæŋkləs/| adj.|无银行的；去中心化的；不受银行控制的|Operating or existing without reliance on banks or traditional financial institutions.  Describes systems, individuals, or organizations that function independently of banks.|Example: The bankless movement advocates for a decentralized financial system. 去中心化金融运动倡导一种无银行的金融体系。

Example:  He envisioned a bankless future where individuals have full control over their assets. 他设想了一个无银行的未来，个人可以完全控制自己的资产。|近义词：decentralized, autonomous, independent|反义词: centralized, traditional, banked|词性: 形容词
