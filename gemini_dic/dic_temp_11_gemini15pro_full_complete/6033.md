amenability:/əˌmiːnəˈbɪləti/
n.
顺从；服从；通情达理
the quality of being open to suggestion or persuasion; willingness to cooperate.
The government expressed its amenability to the proposed reforms. 政府表示愿意接受提议的改革。
synonyms: receptiveness, openness, willingness, compliance, tractability
antonyms: unresponsiveness, resistance, unwillingness, defiance, intractability
noun
