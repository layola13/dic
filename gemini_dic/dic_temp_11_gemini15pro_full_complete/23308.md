boybands:/ˈbɔɪbændz/| n. |男子乐队，男孩乐队|A group of young male singers who play pop music, usually aimed at a teenage audience.|The 1990s saw a surge in popularity of boybands like Take That and Backstreet Boys.  90年代见证了像Take That和Backstreet Boys这样的男子乐队的流行浪潮。|Synonyms: boy groups, pop groups|Antonyms: girl bands, girl groups|Noun
