noncredentialed: /ˌnɑːnkrɪˈdɛnʃəld/ | adj. | 未经认证的，无资格证书的 |  Lacking official credentials or certification, especially in a professional field.  |  Many noncredentialed teachers work in private schools. 许多未经认证的教师在私立学校工作。 |  The clinic relies heavily on noncredentialed staff. 这家诊所严重依赖没有资格证书的员工。 |  uncertified, unqualified | certified, licensed | adjective

Etymology:
non- (prefix): meaning "not"
credentialed (adjective): from "credential" (noun), ultimately from Latin "credentia" meaning "belief, trust".
