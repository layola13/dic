hardware:/ˈhɑːrdwɛər/| n.|硬件|The physical components of a computer or other electronic system.|Example:  The computer's hardware is outdated. 这台电脑的硬件过时了。
Example: We need to upgrade our hardware to run the new software. 我们需要升级硬件来运行新软件。|Synonyms: equipment, apparatus, machinery|Antonyms: software|noun
