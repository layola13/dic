non-fluency:/nɒnˈfluːənsi/ /nɑːnˈfluːənsi/| n. |不流利，不流畅|Lack of fluency or smoothness, especially in speech.  The inability to express oneself easily and articulately.|His non-fluency in English made it difficult for him to communicate. 他英语不流利，很难与人交流。|Synonyms: hesitancy, stammering, faltering, halting|Antonyms: fluency, eloquence, articulateness|noun
