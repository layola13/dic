hypoactive:/ˌhaɪpoʊˈæktɪv/| adj.|活动减退的；机能减退的|Showing abnormally diminished activity; less active than normal.  Relating to or characterized by hypoactivity.  |The doctor diagnosed her with a hypoactive thyroid. 医生诊断她甲状腺功能减退。|The child's hypoactive behavior concerned the teachers.  这孩子不活跃的行为让老师们很担心。|近义词：underactive, sluggish, inert, passive|反义词：hyperactive, overactive, energetic|词性:adjective
词源: hypo- (Greek: under, below) + active
