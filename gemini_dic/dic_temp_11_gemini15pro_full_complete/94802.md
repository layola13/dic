impulsed:/ɪmˈpʌlst/ | v. | 驱使，推动 | Driven or urged by a sudden strong feeling or desire. |  He felt impulsed to help the stranded motorist. 他感到一阵冲动，想去帮助那个被困的驾车者。| synonyms: driven, compelled, urged, motivated, prompted | antonyms: hesitant, reluctant, restrained, inhibited | verb

impulsed:/ɪmˈpʌlst/ | adj. | 冲动的 |  Actuated or swayed by impulse; capricious or whimsical.  |  She made an impulsed decision to quit her job. 她一时冲动决定辞掉工作。 | synonyms: impulsive, spontaneous, rash, hasty | antonyms: planned, deliberate, considered, thoughtful | adjective
