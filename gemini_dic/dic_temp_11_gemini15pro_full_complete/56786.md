dopplerite:/ˈdɒpləˌraɪt/ | n. | 多普勒泥炭 | A dark brown or black, jelly-like organic mineraloid found in peat bogs, formed by the partial decomposition of plant matter in anaerobic conditions. It is named after Christian Doppler, an Austrian physicist. | Dopplerite was discovered in a peat bog near Vienna.  多普勒泥炭是在维也纳附近的一个泥炭沼泽中发现的。

Dopplerite is a valuable resource for studying past environmental conditions. 多普勒泥炭是研究过去环境条件的宝贵资源。 | Near synonyms: humic acid, peat, gyttja | Antonyms:  N/A (lacks a direct opposite) | noun
