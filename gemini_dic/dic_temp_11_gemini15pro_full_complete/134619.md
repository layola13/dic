nonlyrical:/nɒnˈlɪrɪkəl/ | adj. | 非抒情的；没有歌词的 | Not lyrical; lacking lyrics or poetic beauty.  | The play had long, nonlyrical passages that challenged the audience. 这部戏剧有很长的、非抒情的段落，这对观众来说是一个挑战。|  The film's score was nonlyrical, relying more on atmosphere than melody.  这部电影的配乐是非抒情的，更依赖于氛围而不是旋律。 | 近义词: unlyrical, prosaic, unpoetic | 反义词: lyrical, poetic, melodic | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + lyrical (adjective meaning "expressing the writer's emotions in an imaginative and beautiful way").  来源于non-（前缀，意为“不”）+ lyrical（形容词，意为“以富有想象力和美感的方式表达作者的情感”）。
