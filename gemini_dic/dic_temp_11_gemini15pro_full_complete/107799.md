lapse:/læps/
n.|失效，终止；流逝；过失，失误|A temporary lapse of concentration caused the accident. 一时的注意力不集中导致了事故。
lapse:/læps/
vi.|失效，终止；流逝；陷入（某种状态）；衰退|His membership in the club has lapsed. 他的俱乐部会员资格已经失效。
lapse:/læps/
n.|（时间）流逝，间隔|After a lapse of several years, they met again.  几年之后，他们再次相遇。
lapse:/læps/
n.|过失，失误|It was a minor lapse in judgment. 这只是个小小的判断失误。
lapse:/læps/
vi.|衰退|He lapsed into unconsciousness. 他陷入了昏迷。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin lapsus (“a slipping, sliding, gliding, falling, flowing”), past participle of labi (“to slip, slide, glide, fall, sink, decline”).

SYNONYMS->
(n.) expiration, expiry, termination, cessation, discontinuation, slip, error, mistake, oversight, decline
(v.) expire, terminate, cease, discontinue, decline, deteriorate, regress, slip, err

ANTONYMS->
(n.) continuation, renewal, persistence, correctness, accuracy
(v.) continue, renew, persist, improve, progress
