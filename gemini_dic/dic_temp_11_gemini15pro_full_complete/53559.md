disbars:/dɪsˈbɑːrz/
v.|取消律师资格|To expel a lawyer from the bar, revoking their license to practice law.|The state bar association disbarred him for misconduct. 州律师协会因他的不当行为取消了他的律师资格。|Synonyms: expel, debar, suspend|Antonyms: admit, reinstate|Verb

disbars:/dɪsˈbɑːrz/
v.|禁止，阻止|To prohibit or prevent someone from doing something, especially officially.|The judge disbarred him from holding public office. 法官禁止他担任公职。|Synonyms: prohibit, forbid, prevent|Antonyms: allow, permit|Verb
