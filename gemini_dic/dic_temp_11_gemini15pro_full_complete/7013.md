andromeda:/ænˈdrɒmɪdə/ | n. |仙女座，仙女座星系|A constellation in the northern hemisphere between Cassiopeia and Pegasus, containing the Andromeda Galaxy.  It represents Andromeda, daughter of Cepheus and Cassiopeia, in Greek mythology. |例句：The Andromeda Galaxy is the closest major galaxy to our own Milky Way galaxy. 仙女座星系是离我们银河系最近的大星系。

例句：Andromeda is a beautiful constellation visible in the autumn sky. 仙女座是秋季夜空中可见的美丽星座。 | 近义词:  (For the galaxy) M31 | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun
