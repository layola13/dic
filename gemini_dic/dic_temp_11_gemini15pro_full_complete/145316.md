overbroad:/ˌoʊvərˈbrɔːd/| adj.| 过于宽泛的，过于笼统的|Too broad or inclusive; not sufficiently specific or limited.|The law was deemed overbroad and unconstitutional because it restricted free speech too much. 这项法律被认为过于宽泛，违反宪法，因为它过度限制了言论自由。|近义词：too broad, too general, sweeping, excessive, immoderate|反义词：narrow, specific, limited, restricted|词性:形容词
词源: "over-" (过度) + "broad" (宽泛的)
