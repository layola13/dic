impala:/ɪmˈpɑːlə/ | n. |黑斑羚|A medium-sized antelope found in eastern Africa, known for its graceful leaps.|例句：The impala leaped effortlessly over the bush. 黑斑羚轻而易举地跳过了灌木丛。

例句：A herd of impala grazed peacefully in the savanna. 一群黑斑羚在热带草原上平静地吃草。|近义词：antelope, gazelle|反义词: predator (捕食者, 广义上的反义词) |词性: noun
