journalese:/ˈdʒɜːrnəˌliːz/ |n.|新闻腔，报刊体|A style of writing typical of newspapers and magazines, often characterized by clichés, sensationalism, and a superficial treatment of complex issues.|The article was full of journalese, making it difficult to understand the real story. 这篇文章充满了新闻腔，让人很难理解真正的情况。|近义词：newspeak, tabloidese|反义词: literary style, academic writing|词性: noun

Etymology:  From journal + -ese (a suffix forming nouns denoting languages or styles of language).
