oreoselin: /ˌɔːriˈoʊsɪlɪn/ | n. | 奥利奥西林 | A semisynthetic penicillin antibiotic derived from methicillin and used especially against penicillinase-producing staphylococci.  | Example:  The doctor prescribed oreoselin to treat the resistant staphylococcal infection. 医生开了奥利奥西林来治疗耐药的葡萄球菌感染。| Example: Oreoselin is effective against some strains of bacteria. 奥利奥西林对某些菌株有效。 | Synonym: methicillin, oxacillin | Antonym: N/A | noun
