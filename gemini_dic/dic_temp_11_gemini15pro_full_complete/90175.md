hotplate:/ˈhɒtpleɪt/ (UK) /ˈhɑːtpleɪt/ (US)| n.|电热板，加热板|A flat, electrically heated surface used for cooking or warming food.|I used a hotplate to warm up my lunch. 我用电热板加热了我的午餐。|Synonyms: heating plate, warming plate, electric burner, electric stove|Antonyms: refrigerator, freezer|noun
