biy: /baɪ/ | pronoun | 买（非标准拼写，正确拼写为buy） | To obtain something by paying money for it. |  例句：I want to biy a new car. (Non-standard) 我想买一辆新车。 (This is incorrect spelling; use "buy") | 近义词：purchase, acquire, obtain | 反义词：sell, dispose of | 词性：verb

注意事项:
