hotbedding:/ˈhɒtˌbɛdɪŋ/ | n. |  办公桌轮用；轮用办公位；|The practice of sharing desks or workstations between different workers who use them at different times. |  Many companies are adopting hotbedding to save on office space. 许多公司正在采用办公桌轮用制来节省办公空间。 | hot-desking, desk-sharing | fixed desks, assigned seating | noun

hotbedding:/ˈhɑːtˌbɛdɪŋ/ | n. |  温床；滋生地；|A situation that favors the development of something, especially something undesirable. | The overcrowded prison became a hotbedding of disease. 拥挤的监狱成了疾病的滋生地。 | breeding ground, seedbed, incubator | deterrent, impediment | noun
