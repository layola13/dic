maximizer:/ˈmæksɪmaɪzər/| n.|最大化者，力求完美者|A person who strives to make the best possible decision in every situation, often to the point of excessive deliberation and analysis.  A maximizer tends to feel less satisfied with their choices than a satisficer.|She's a maximizer, always researching every option before buying anything, even a toothbrush.|近义词：optimizer, perfectionist|反义词: satisficer|词性:noun

Etymology:  From the verb "maximize," which comes from "maximum" + "-ize."  "Maximum" originates from the Latin "maximus" (greatest).
