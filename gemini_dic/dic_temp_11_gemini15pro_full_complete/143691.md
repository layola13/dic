outleaping:/ˌaʊtˈliːpɪŋ/
verb
超越，跳得比…远/高
Surpassing or exceeding something by leaping.  Going beyond in a sudden burst of activity or progress.
The athlete outleaped his competitors, securing the gold medal.  这位运动员跳得比他的对手远，获得了金牌。
New technologies are constantly outleaping the old ones.  新技术不断超越旧技术。
Synonyms: surpassing, exceeding, outdoing, outstripping
Antonyms: lagging, trailing, falling behind
词性: Verb
