furnace:/ˈfɜːrnɪs/ (US) /ˈfɜːnɪs/ (UK)| n.|火炉，熔炉|An enclosed structure in which heat is produced, as for heating a house or for industrial processes.  A furnace can be used for heating homes or for melting metals.|The furnace broke down in the middle of winter.  冬天的时候，火炉坏了。|The blacksmith heated the metal in the furnace.  铁匠在熔炉里加热金属。|synonyms: heater, oven, kiln, incinerator|antonyms: refrigerator, freezer|noun
