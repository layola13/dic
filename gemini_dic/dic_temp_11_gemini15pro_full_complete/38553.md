conclusions:/kənˈkluːʒənz/| n.|结论，推论|The conclusions of the study were surprising. 这项研究的结论令人惊讶。|例句：The committee reached several important conclusions. 委员会得出几个重要结论。|例句：Based on the available evidence, the detective drew his conclusions about the case. 根据现有证据，侦探得出了关于此案的结论。|近义词：results, findings, deductions, inferences|反义词：beginnings, premises, assumptions|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *conclusion, from Old French conclusion, from Latin conclūsiō (“a shutting up, a closing”), from conclūdō (“I shut up, enclose, conclude”), from con- (“together”) + claudō (“I shut, close”).
