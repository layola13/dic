dirged:/dɜːrdʒd/
verb (past tense)|悲歌；哀悼；|having been sung or lamented in a dirge|The fallen soldiers were dirged by their comrades.  战友们为牺牲的战士唱起了挽歌。|近义词：mourned, lamented, grieved|反义词：celebrated, rejoiced
etymology: From dirge (n.) + -ed (past tense suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *dirige*, imperative of *dirigere* "to direct," referring to the Office of the Dead.
