blues:/bluːz/
n.|布鲁斯；忧郁；沮丧|A genre of music originating in African American communities in the United States. It typically has a slow tempo and a melancholic mood.|She listened to the blues to soothe her broken heart. 她听着布鲁斯音乐来抚慰她破碎的心。|synonyms: melancholy, sadness, depression, dejection|antonyms: happiness, joy, elation, cheerfulness|noun

blues:/bluːz/
n.|蓝色|A color intermediate between green and violet.|The sky was a brilliant shade of blues. 天空是一片灿烂的蓝色。|synonyms: azure, cerulean, sapphire, indigo|antonyms: yellow, red, green|noun
