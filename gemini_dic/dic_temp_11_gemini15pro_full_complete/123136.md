monists:/ˈmɒnɪsts/ (US) /ˈmoʊnɪsts/| n. |一元论者|A person who believes in monism, the doctrine that reality is ultimately constituted by a single kind of substance or principle.| Spinoza was a prominent monist, believing that mind and body are aspects of a single substance.|近义词: Unitarian, nondualist|反义词: dualist, pluralist|词性:noun
