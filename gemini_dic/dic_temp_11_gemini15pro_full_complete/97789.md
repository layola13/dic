injunction's:/ɪnˈdʒʌŋkʃənz/
n.|禁令的，指令的|The possessive form of "injunction," meaning belonging to or associated with an injunction.  It refers to something that is a quality, characteristic, or possession of an injunction.|The judge's injunction's wording was very specific. 法官的禁令措辞非常具体。|Synonyms:  order's, decree's, command's|Antonyms: permission's, allowance's
词性: 名词所有格
