fashionables:/ˈfæʃənəblz/
n.
时髦人士；时尚物品
People or things that are fashionable, especially clothes.
The fashionables were all dressed in the latest styles.  时髦人士都穿着最新款式的服装。
The boutique caters to fashionables. 这家精品店迎合时尚人士的需求。
synonyms: trendsetters, stylish people, chic people
antonyms: unfashionable people, outdated people
