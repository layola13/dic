darwinites:/ˈdɑːrwɪnaɪts/
n.
达尔文主义者；达尔文信徒
A believer in Darwin's theory of evolution by natural selection.
Darwinites believe that natural selection is the driving force of evolution. 达尔文主义者认为自然选择是进化的驱动力。
The conference attracted many darwinites from around the world.  这次会议吸引了来自世界各地的许多达尔文主义者。
近义词：evolutionists,  proponents of Darwinism
反义词：creationists, anti-evolutionists
词性: noun
