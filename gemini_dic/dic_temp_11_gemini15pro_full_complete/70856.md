fink:/fɪŋk/| n.|告密者，卑鄙的人|A person, especially a labor union member, who acts as an informer or strikebreaker, or who fails to join a labor union; a contemptible person.|The other workers called him a fink for crossing the picket line.|Synonym:informer, rat, snitch, scab, betrayer|Antonym:loyalist, comrade|noun


fink:/fɪŋk/| vi.|告密，背叛|To inform on someone; to betray someone.|He finked on his friends to the police.|Synonym:inform, betray, rat, snitch|Antonym:protect, defend|verb
