evacuate: /ɪˈvækjueɪt/ (US), /ɪˈvækjʊeɪt/ (UK)
|v.|撤离，疏散|To remove someone from a dangerous place.|The police evacuated the building after the bomb threat. 警方在炸弹威胁后疏散了大楼。|The city was evacuated because of the approaching hurricane. 由于飓风逼近，城市被疏散了。|近义词:clear, remove, withdraw|反义词:occupy, inhabit|词性:动词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin evacuatus, past participle of evacuare "to empty out," from ex- "out" + vacuus "empty."
