disreputability:/dɪsˌrepjətəˈbɪləti/
n.
不名誉，声名狼藉
the state of having a bad reputation
The disreputability of the neighborhood made people hesitant to move there.  这个街区的声名狼藉让人们不愿搬到那里。

synonym: disgrace, infamy, notoriety, ill repute
antonym: respectability, repute, honor, prestige
词性: noun
