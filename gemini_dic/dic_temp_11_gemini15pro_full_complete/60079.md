eek:/iːk/
interjection|呀！; 哎呀！; 哇！|(Expressing surprise, alarm, or dismay.) 表示惊讶、惊恐或沮丧。|Eek! A spider! 呀！一只蜘蛛！|近义词:yikes, gasp, oh dear|反义词:(No direct antonym, but could be considered opposite of expressions of calmness like "phew" or "thank goodness")|词性:interjection
