organoleptic:/ˌɔːrɡənəˈlɛptɪk/ |adj.|感官的，通过感官感知的|Relating to or involving the use of the senses, especially taste or smell.  Perceived by the senses. |The wine has complex organoleptic qualities. 这款酒具有复杂的感官品质。|Synonyms: sensory, perceptible, gustatory (taste), olfactory (smell)|Antonyms: imperceptible, non-sensory|adjective

organoleptic:/ˌɔːrɡənəˈlɛptɪk/ |n.|感官属性|A property perceived by the senses.|The chef carefully balanced the organoleptics of the dish. 厨师仔细地平衡了这道菜的感官属性。|Synonyms: sensory property, sensory attribute|Antonyms: N/A|noun
