blamer:/ˈbleɪmər/| n. |指责者，责备者|Someone who blames other people for things. |例句：He's always been a blamer, never taking responsibility for his own actions. 他总是指责别人，从不为自己的行为负责。

例句：Don't be a blamer; try to understand the situation before accusing someone. 不要总是责备别人；在指责别人之前先试着了解情况。|近义词：accuser, critic, faultfinder |反义词:  forgiver, pardoner |词性:noun
