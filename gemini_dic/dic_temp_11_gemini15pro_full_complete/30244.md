cellulite: /ˈseljʊlaɪt/| n.|脂肪团，橘皮组织|localized accumulations of fat that push against the connective tissue beneath a person's skin, causing the surface of the skin to appear bumpy, like the peel of an orange. |Example 1:  She's been exercising to try to get rid of her cellulite. 她一直在锻炼以试图摆脱脂肪团。

Example 2:  Many women are self-conscious about cellulite on their thighs. 很多女性对大腿上的橘皮组织感到难为情。|Synonym:  orange-peel skin, cottage cheese skin|Antonym:  smooth skin|Noun
