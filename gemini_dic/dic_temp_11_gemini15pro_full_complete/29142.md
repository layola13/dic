caseloads:/ˈkeɪsloʊdz/
noun
案件量；个案量
The number of cases handled by a professional, such as a social worker, lawyer, or doctor.
Social workers often have heavy caseloads. 社工的案件量通常很大。
The lawyer struggled to manage his increasing caseload.  这位律师努力应对不断增加的案件量。
synonyms: workload, case number, client load
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "light workload" or "few cases")
n.
