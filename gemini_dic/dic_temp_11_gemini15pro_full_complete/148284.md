oversoaps:/ˌoʊvərˈsoʊps/| n.|肥皂剧|Overly dramatic and sentimental television or radio serials, typically focusing on domestic situations and involving a large cast of characters.|Her grandmother loved watching oversoaps.|近义词：soap opera, melodrama|反义词: documentary, news program|词性:名词

Etymology:  "Over-" (excessive) + "soaps" (originally referring to soap companies that sponsored these programs).  The term originated in the 1930s when many of these daytime serials were sponsored by soap manufacturers.
