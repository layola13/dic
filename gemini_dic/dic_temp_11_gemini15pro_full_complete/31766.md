check-off:/ˈtʃɛk ɔːf/| n. |核对；勾选；检查表；清点|An act of checking or verifying something, often by marking it on a list.  A list of items to be checked or verified.| I made a check-off list for packing my suitcase. 我制作了一个装箱单来打包我的行李箱。|synonyms: verification, confirmation, check, tick-off|antonyms: disregard, neglect|noun
check off:/ˈtʃɛk ɔːf/| vt. |核对；勾选；清点|To mark an item on a list as having been completed or verified.|I checked off all the items on my grocery list. 我把我购物清单上的所有东西都勾选了。|synonyms: tick off, mark off, check, verify|antonyms: miss, overlook|verb
