intractable:/ɪnˈtræktəbl/ | adj. | 难处理的，棘手的 |  Not easily managed, controlled, or solved; stubborn.  Derived from Latin "in-" (not) + "tractabilis" (manageable), from "tractare" (to handle).| The intractable problem of poverty continues to plague many countries. 贫困这一棘手的问题继续困扰着许多国家。| The negotiations reached an impasse due to the intractable nature of the dispute. 由于争议的棘手性质，谈判陷入了僵局。 |  stubborn, obstinate, unyielding, difficult, challenging | manageable, docile, compliant, amenable | adjective
