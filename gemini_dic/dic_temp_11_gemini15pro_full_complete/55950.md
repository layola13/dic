djereed: /dʒəˈriːd/
n.
Jerid, jereed
A wooden javelin used in equestrian sports, especially in the Middle East and North Africa.
The horsemen charged at each other, hurling djereeds with great force.  骑兵们互相冲锋，用力投掷杰里德。
近义词: javelin, lance
