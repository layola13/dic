coffee-table:/ˈkɒfi ˌteɪbəl/ (UK) /ˈkɑːfi ˌteɪbəl/ (US)| n. |咖啡桌|A low table, typically placed in front of a sofa, used for resting drinks, magazines, etc.|We put the coffee and biscuits on the coffee table. 我们把咖啡和饼干放在咖啡桌上。|Synonyms: cocktail table, occasional table|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps dining table)|noun
