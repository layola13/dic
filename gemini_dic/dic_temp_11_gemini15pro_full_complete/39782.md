consonantally:/ˌkɑːnsəˈnæntəli/
adv.
辅音地；就辅音而言
Relating to or consisting of consonants; in a manner characterized by consonants.
The two words are consonantally similar but differ in their vowels. 这两个单词辅音相似，但元音不同。
近义词: phonetically, phonologically
反义词: vocally
