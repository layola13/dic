farewelling:/ˌfɛəˈwɛlɪŋ/ | n. | 告别，辞行 |  The act of saying goodbye or a farewell gathering. |  They held a farewelling for their departing colleague. 他们为即将离职的同事举行了一场告别会。 | synonyms: goodbye, farewell, send-off | antonyms: greeting, welcome | noun

farewelling:/ˌfɛəˈwɛlɪŋ/ | adj. | 告别的，辞行的 |  Relating to a farewell. |  His farewelling speech was moving. 他的告别演讲很感人。| synonyms: valedictory, parting | antonyms: welcoming, introductory | adjective
