occluded:/əˈkluːdɪd/
adjective
闭塞的，堵塞的
blocked or closed so that nothing can pass through
The artery was occluded by a blood clot. 这条动脉被血凝块堵塞了。
Synonyms: blocked, obstructed, closed
Antonyms: open, clear, unobstructed
词性: 形容词


occluded:/əˈkluːdɪd/
verb
(牙齿)咬合的
(of teeth) close together in the normal way
Her teeth are perfectly occluded. 她的牙齿咬合得很好。
Synonyms: close, shut
Antonyms: open
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin occludere (to shut up, close, obstruct), from ob- (against, towards) + claudere (to shut, close).
