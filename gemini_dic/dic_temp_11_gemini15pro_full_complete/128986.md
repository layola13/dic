nippiest:/ˈnɪpiɪst/
adjective
最寒冷的，最刺骨的
Most cold and biting; chilling.
The nippiest wind I've ever felt blew across the frozen lake. 我感觉过的最刺骨的风吹过结冰的湖面。
近义词:coldest, chilliest, frostiest, biting, raw
反义词:warmest, hottest, balmiest
词性:adjective
词源:From nip (v.) + -y + -est.  "Nip" comes from Old English "hnipan" meaning "to pinch, bite, or nip."
