nonspectrally:/nɒnˈspɛktrəli/
adjective
非光谱地
Not related to or determined by a spectrum; not involving or based on the analysis of light or other electromagnetic radiation.
This material absorbs light nonspectrally, meaning the absorption is not dependent on the wavelength of the light.  这种材料非光谱地吸收光，这意味着吸收不依赖于光的波长。
The artist uses nonspectral colors in his paintings, creating unique visual effects. 这位艺术家在他的画作中使用非光谱颜色，创造出独特的视觉效果。
近义词: non-spectrally (alternative spelling), achromatically (related to absence of color)
反义词: spectrally
词性: adjective
