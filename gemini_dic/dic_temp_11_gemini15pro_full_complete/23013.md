bouillon:/ˈbuːjɒn/ (UK), /ˈbuːljɑːn/ (US)| n.| 肉汤，清汤 |A clear, seasoned broth made by simmering meat or vegetables or both. |She added bouillon cubes to the boiling water to make a quick soup. 她往沸水里加了浓汤块，做了一道速食汤。

The chef simmered the bones for hours to create a rich bouillon.  厨师将骨头炖了几个小时，熬制出浓郁的肉汤。|近义词：broth, stock, consommé|反义词: - |noun
