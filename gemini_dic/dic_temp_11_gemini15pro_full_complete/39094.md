confrere:/ˈkɒnfrɛər/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnfrɛər/ (US)| n. |同事；同行；同僚|A fellow member of a profession, society, or other group.|例句：He lunched with his confreres at the club. 他和他的同事在俱乐部共进午餐。

例句：The journalist enjoyed meeting with his confreres at the annual conference. 这名记者喜欢在年度会议上与同行们见面。

近义词：colleague, associate, peer, fellow|反义词: opponent, rival, adversary |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French confrère, from Old French confrere, from Late Latin confrāter, from Latin com- + frāter “brother.”  Literally, "fellow brother."
