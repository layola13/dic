over-righteous:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪtʃəs/| adj.|自以为是的，伪善的| Excessively or hypocritically righteous; acting as if morally superior to others.  Characterized by claiming to be morally superior to others, often in a way that is excessive or insincere.| He gave me an over-righteous lecture about the dangers of smoking, even though he used to smoke two packs a day. 他自以为是地教训我吸烟的危害，尽管他以前一天抽两包。|  sanctimonious, self-righteous, holier-than-thou| tolerant, humble, open-minded| adjective
