jurist:/ˈdʒʊərɪst/| n.| 法学家；法律专家|A jurist is an expert in law, especially one who is a judge, lawyer, or legal scholar. |例句：The jurist specialized in constitutional law.  这位法学家专攻宪法。

例句：She is a respected jurist with years of experience. 她是一位经验丰富的受人尊敬的法学家。|近义词：lawyer, attorney, legal expert, legal scholar|反义词: layman|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin iurista "lawyer," from Latin ius "law" (see just (adj.)).
