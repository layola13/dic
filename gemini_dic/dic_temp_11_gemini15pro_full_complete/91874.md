hyperintellectual:/ˌhaɪpərˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəl/
|adj.|过度理智的，过分强调智力的|Excessively intellectual; overly concerned with intellectual matters.|He was seen as hyperintellectual, more interested in theories than in practical application. 他被认为是过度理智的，对理论比对实际应用更感兴趣。|She had a hyperintellectual approach to literature, dissecting every text with painstaking detail. 她对文学采取了一种过度理智的方法，对每篇文章都进行了极其细致的剖析。|近义词：cerebral, highbrow, intellectualistic|反义词：unintellectual, simpleminded, pragmatic|词性: adjective
