cogitating:/ˈkɒdʒɪteɪtɪŋ/ /ˈkɑːdʒɪteɪtɪŋ/
verb
深思熟虑；仔细考虑
thinking deeply about something; meditating
She sat alone, cogitating on the problem. 她独自坐着，仔细思考这个问题。
近义词: pondering, contemplating, meditating, reflecting, musing
反义词: ignoring, neglecting, disregarding
词性: verb (present participle)
