debtee:/ˌdɛbˈtiː/
n.
债务人，欠债者
A person or organization that owes money to another.
The debtee is struggling to repay the loan.  债务人正在努力偿还贷款。
The company became a debtee after taking out a large loan.  这家公司在获得一大笔贷款后成为了债务人。
Synonyms: debtor, borrower, owing party
Antonyms: creditor, lender
