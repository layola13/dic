acclimatization:/əˌklaɪmətaɪˈzeɪʃən/ |əˌklaɪmətiˈzeɪʃən/
n.|适应，驯化|The process of becoming accustomed to a new climate or environment.|The climbers spent a few days at base camp for acclimatization to the altitude. 登山者们在大本营待了几天以适应海拔高度。|
acclimatization:|n.|适应，习惯|The acclimatization period can be difficult, but it's crucial for long-term health. 适应期可能很困难，但对长期健康至关重要。|
synonym:adaptation, adjustment, habituation
antonym:maladaptation

acclimatizations:/əˌklaɪmətaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ |əˌklaɪmətiˈzeɪʃənz/
n.|适应，驯化（复数）|The plural form of acclimatization, referring to multiple instances of becoming accustomed to new climates or environments.|The team underwent several acclimatizations before attempting the summit. 团队在尝试登顶之前进行了多次适应。|
acclimatizations:|n.|适应，习惯（复数）|Successful acclimatizations are essential for species survival in changing environments.  成功的适应对于物种在变化的环境中的生存至关重要。|
synonym:adaptations, adjustments, habituations
antonym:maladaptations


ETYMOLOGY->
From French acclimatation, from acclimater (to acclimatize), from à (to) + climat (climate), from Latin clima (region, slope).
