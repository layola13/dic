maltiest:/ˈmɔːltiɪst/| adj.|最麦芽味的，麦芽味最浓的|Having the most malt flavor; superlative of "malty".|This is the maltiest beer I've ever tasted. 这是我喝过麦芽味最浓的啤酒。|The maltiest loaf of bread had a deep, rich flavor. 麦芽味最浓的面包有着浓郁醇厚的味道。|近义词：malty, rich, flavorful|反义词: bland, tasteless|词性:形容词
Etymology: malt + -y + -est.  "Malt" comes from Old English "mealt,"  ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*malt-," meaning "melted." The "-y" suffix forms adjectives indicating "having the quality of" or "full of," while "-est" is the superlative suffix.
