insular:/ˈɪnsjələr/ | adj. | 狭隘的；岛屿的 | Relating to or characteristic of an island; narrow-minded or uninterested in people or ideas from outside one's own experience. | The islanders had an insular culture, resistant to outside influences. 岛民们有一种狭隘的文化，抵制外来影响。|  The community's insular nature made it difficult for newcomers to integrate. 这个社群的封闭性使新来的人难以融入。 | synonyms: narrow-minded, parochial, provincial, isolated, detached | antonyms: cosmopolitan, broad-minded, open-minded, worldly | word type: adjective

insular:/ˈɪnsjələr/ | n. | 岛民 | An inhabitant of an island. |  Many islanders rely on fishing for their livelihood. 许多岛民依靠捕鱼为生。 | He is an insular, born and raised on a remote island. 他是一个岛民，在一个偏远的岛屿上出生和长大。 | synonyms: islander | antonyms: mainlander, continental | word type: noun
