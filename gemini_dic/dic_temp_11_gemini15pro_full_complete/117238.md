mebibyte:/ˈmebɪbaɪt/| n.|兆字节（MiB，等于2的20次方字节）|A mebibyte is a unit of information or computer storage equal to 2^20 bytes, or 1,048,576 bytes.  It is often confused with a megabyte (MB), which is 1,000,000 bytes.| My hard drive has a capacity of 100 mebibytes. 我的硬盘容量为100兆字节。|近义词：megabyte (often used interchangeably, though technically different)|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider smaller units like kilobyte, byte, bit)|词性:noun
