misconception:/ˌmɪskənˈsɛpʃən/| n.|误解，错误观念|A view or opinion that is incorrect because based on faulty thinking or understanding.|Many people have misconceptions about the dangers of flying. 很多人对飞行的危险抱有误解。|Synonyms: fallacy, delusion, misapprehension, misunderstanding|Antonyms: understanding, comprehension, truth, accuracy|noun


misconception:/ˌmɪskənˈsɛpʃən/| n.|误解，错误观念|An idea that is wrong because it is based on a failure to understand a situation.|It's a common misconception that older workers cannot learn new skills.  认为老员工学不会新技能是一种普遍的误解。|Synonyms: misbelief, error, misinterpretation|Antonyms: correct understanding, accurate perception, fact|noun
