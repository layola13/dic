noninsulator:/ˌnɑːnˈɪnsəleɪtər/ | n. | 非绝缘体 | A substance that does not prevent the flow of electricity or heat.  A material that readily conducts heat or electricity. |  The metal casing acted as a noninsulator, transferring heat quickly. 金属外壳充当了非绝缘体，迅速传递热量。| Copper is a noninsulator and is used in electrical wiring. 铜是非绝缘体，用于电线。 | conductor,  non-dielectric | insulator, dielectric | noun
