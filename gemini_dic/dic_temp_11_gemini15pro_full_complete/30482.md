centrists:/ˈsɛntrɪsts/| n.|中间派，温和派|A person who holds moderate political views, advocating a balance between or avoiding extreme left-wing and right-wing positions.|例句：Centrists often play a key role in forming coalition governments. 中间派通常在组建联合政府中扮演关键角色。

例句：The party's centrists are trying to appeal to a wider range of voters. 该党的中间派正试图吸引更广泛的选民。|近义词：moderates, middle-of-the-roaders|反义词:extremists, radicals|词性:noun

Etymology:
From French *centriste*, from centre (“center”) + -iste.  Ultimately from Latin *centrum* (“center”).
