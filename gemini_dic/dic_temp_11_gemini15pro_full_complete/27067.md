calflike:/ˈkɑːflaɪk/| adj.|像小牛的，小牛般的|Resembling or characteristic of a calf, especially in being awkwardly young or having slender legs.|例句：She had calflike eyes, large and innocent. 她有着小牛般的眼睛，大而天真。|例句：His calflike legs struggled to keep up with the older boys. 他那细长的腿努力跟上大男孩们的步伐。|近义词：delicate, slender, fawnlike|反义词：thick, sturdy, mature|词性:形容词

词源:
calf (n.) + -like (suffix)

"calf" 来自古英语 "cealf,"  原始日耳曼语 "*kalbaz."
"-like"  来自古英语 "-lic," 表示 "having the form or qualities of."
