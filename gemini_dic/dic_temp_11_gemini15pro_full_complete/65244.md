etui:/eɪˈtwiː/ /ˈetwi/
noun
小盒，套，容器 (尤指针线盒，化妆盒等)
A small case for holding needles, cosmetics, etc.
She kept her sewing needles in a beautiful silver etui. 她把缝衣针放在一个漂亮的银盒里。
近义词: case, container, holder, receptacle
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "bulk storage" for a contrasting concept)
词性: noun
