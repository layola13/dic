numerable:/ˈnjuːmərəbl/ /ˈnuːmərəbl/| adj.|可数的，能计算的|Able to be counted; countable.|例句：The stars in the sky are not numerable.  天上的星星数不清。|例句：The grains of sand on the beach are innumerable, but theoretically numerable. 海滩上的沙粒无数，但理论上是可数的。|近义词：countable, calculable|反义词：innumerable, countless|词性:形容词

词源:
From Late Latin *numerabilis, from Latin numerare "to count, number," from numerus "number."
