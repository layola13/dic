birthdom:/ˈbɜːrθdəm/
n.
出生地，诞生地；出身
The land of one's birth; native land.  A person's origin or descent.
He returned to his birthdom after many years abroad.  他多年在国外后回到了他的出生地。
She was proud of her royal birthdom. 她为她的皇室出身感到骄傲。
Synonyms: birthplace, homeland, native land, origin, descent, ancestry
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider) adopted home, foreign land
