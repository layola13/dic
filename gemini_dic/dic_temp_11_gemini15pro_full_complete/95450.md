incisional:/ɪnˈsɪʒənl/| adj.|切开的，切口的|Relating to or made by an incision (a surgical cut).|The surgeon made a precise incisional cut. 外科医生做了一个精确的切口。|The incisional wound healed quickly. 切口愈合得很快。|近义词：cutting, surgical, incisive|反义词: blunt, non-invasive|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin *incisionalis, from Latin incisio (incision), from incidere (to cut into).
