nationalistic: /ˌnæʃənəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 国家主义的；民族主义的 | Showing strong patriotism, sometimes to an extreme degree; excessively or fanatically devoted to one's own nation. | The politician's nationalistic rhetoric stirred up strong feelings in the crowd.  这位政客的民族主义言论激起了民众的强烈情绪。|  patriotic, chauvinistic | cosmopolitan, internationalist | adjective


Etymology:  From nation + -al + -istic.  "Nation" ultimately derives from the Latin "natio" (birth, race, kind).
