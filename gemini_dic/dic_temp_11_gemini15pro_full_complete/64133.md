equability:/ˌiːkwəˈbɪləti/
n.| 平静；稳定；（尤指气候的）温和；均匀|the quality of being steady and even; especially, the evenness of a person's temperament or the evenness and mildness of climate|The region is known for the equability of its climate. 这个地区以其气候温和而闻名。|近义词：stability, calmness, composure, evenness, uniformity|反义词：instability, volatility, variability, unevenness|词性:名词

equable:/ˈekwəbəl/
adj.| 平静的；稳定的；（尤指气候的）温和的；均匀的|not easily disturbed or angered; calm and even-tempered;  free from extremes; mild; or uniform|He has a remarkably equable temperament. 他性情非常平和。|近义词：stable, calm, composed, even, uniform, steady, temperate, mild|反义词：unstable, volatile, variable, uneven, erratic, turbulent, extreme|词性:形容词
