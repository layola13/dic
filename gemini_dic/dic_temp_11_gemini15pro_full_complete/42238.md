countable:/ˈkaʊntəbl/ | adj. | 可数的 | Able to be counted; not infinite. |  Apples are countable nouns. 苹果是可数名词。| Countable resources are finite. 可数资源是有限的。 | numerable, calculable | uncountable, innumerable | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From count + -able.  Count ultimately derives from Latin computāre "to count, sum up, reckon," from com- "together" + putāre "to prune, cleanse, reckon."
