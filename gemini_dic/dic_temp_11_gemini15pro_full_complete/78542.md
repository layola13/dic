ghostless:/ˈɡoʊstləs/| adj.|无鬼的，没有鬼魂的|Free from ghosts; not haunted.  Lacking in spirit or vitality; lifeless.|The old house, once rumored to be haunted, now stands ghostless and empty.  这栋老房子，曾经传闻闹鬼，现在空空如也，没有鬼魂。|近义词：unhaunted, spiritless, lifeless|反义词：haunted, ghostly, spirited|词性:形容词

Etymology:  "ghostless" is formed by combining the noun "ghost" with the suffix "-less," which means "without" or "lacking."  "Ghost" comes from Old English "gast," meaning "spirit," "soul," or "breath."
