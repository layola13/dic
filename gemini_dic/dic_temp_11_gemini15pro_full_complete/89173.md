homeopathist:/ˌhoʊmiˈɒpəθɪst/| n. |顺势疗法医师; 顺势疗法医生|A practitioner of homeopathy, a system of alternative medicine.|例句：I consulted a homeopathist for my allergies. 我咨询了一位顺势疗法医师治疗我的过敏症。|例句：The homeopathist prescribed a remedy made from diluted plant extracts. 顺势疗法医师开了一种用稀释植物提取物制成的药物。|近义词：alternative medicine practitioner, naturopath|反义词:conventional doctor, allopathic physician|词性:noun

Etymology:
From homeopathy + -ist. Homeopathy from  Greek homoios  "similar" + -pathy  "suffering"
