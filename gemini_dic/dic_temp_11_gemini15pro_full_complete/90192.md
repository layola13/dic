hottest:/ˈhɒtɪst/ (British) /ˈhɑːtɪst/ (American)| adj.|最热的|Superlative form of "hot."  Means having the highest temperature; most sexually attractive.|例句：This is the hottest summer on record. 这是有记录以来最热的夏天。

例句：He's one of the hottest actors in Hollywood right now. 他是现在好莱坞最红的男演员之一。|近义词：warmest, burning, scorching, sizzling, sweltering|反义词：coldest, coolest, chilliest|词性:形容词 (adjective)
