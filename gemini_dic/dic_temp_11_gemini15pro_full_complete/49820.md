demonstratedly:/ˌdemənˈstreɪtɪdli/
adverb
明显地；可论证地；确凿地
clearly; obviously; in a way that can be proved
His guilt was demonstratedly clear. 他的罪行显然是清楚的。
demonstratedly superior 明显优越的
synonyms: evidently, manifestly, patently, obviously, plainly, clearly, visibly, discernibly, noticeably, perceptibly, distinctly, unmistakably, undeniably
antonyms: vaguely, unclearly, doubtfully
词性: 副词
