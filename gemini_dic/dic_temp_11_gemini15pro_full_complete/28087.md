capillation:/ˌkæpɪˈleɪʃən/ | n. |按人头收费；人头费；人口税| A system of payment in which a doctor or hospital is paid a fixed amount for each patient treated, regardless of the actual services provided. | Example 1: The clinic operates on a capitation basis, receiving a set fee per patient registered.  诊所按人头收费运营，每个注册病人收取固定费用。

Example 2: Capitation payments can incentivize healthcare providers to focus on preventative care. 按人头付费可以激励医疗保健提供者专注于预防性护理。 |Synonyms: per capita payment, head fee | Antonyms: fee-for-service | noun
