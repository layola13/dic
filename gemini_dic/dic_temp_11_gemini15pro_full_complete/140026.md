oenanthic:/iːˈnænθɪk/ | adj. | 葡萄酒的，酒香的 | Relating to or characteristic of wine, especially its aroma.  Specifically, referring to the bouquet of aged wine, often described as "nutty," "sherried," or having notes of dried fruit. |  The oenanthic aromas of the vintage port filled the room.  陈年波特酒的酒香弥漫了整个房间。| Synonyms: vinous, winy | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "odorless," "flat" in describing wine) | adjective
