anoint:/əˈnɔɪnt/| vt.|涂油，施以涂油礼，圣化|To rub or sprinkle with oil or an oily liquid, typically as part of a religious ceremony. It can also mean to consecrate or ordain someone to a position.|The priest anointed the king's forehead with holy oil.|consecrate, ordain, bless|desecrate, profane|verb

anoint:/əˈnɔɪnt/| vt.|指定，选定（尤指领导人）|To choose someone for a particular job or position, often one of leadership. This usage is more metaphorical and doesn't involve literal oil.|He was anointed as the company's next CEO.|designate, appoint, choose|dismiss, reject|verb
