coarsely:/ˈkɔːrsli/| adv.|粗糙地；粗俗地；粗略地|In a rough or harsh manner; lacking refinement or delicacy.|The fabric was coarsely woven. 这块布料织得很粗糙。|roughly, crudely, rudely|finely, delicately, smoothly|adverb
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *corsly*, from *cors* (“coarse”) + *-ly*, from Old English *cearse* (“not soft or fine, harsh, rough”) + *-līce*.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *karsus*.
