ornithosauria:/ˌɔːrnɪθəˈsɔːriə/ | n. |鸟掌翼龙类，翼龙目 | An extinct order of flying reptiles, also known as pterosaurs. They lived during most of the Mesozoic Era. | Example: Ornithosauria were the first vertebrates to evolve powered flight. 鸟掌翼龙类是第一批进化出动力飞行的脊椎动物。

Example:  The largest Ornithosauria had wingspans exceeding 30 feet.  最大的鸟掌翼龙类翼展超过30英尺。 | 近义词: pterosaur | 反义词: None | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
