misrule:/ˌmɪsˈruːl/| n. v. | 暴政，乱政；对…施暴政 |  The act or process of ruling badly; government by incompetent or dishonest people. |  The country suffered years of misrule under the dictator. 这个国家在独裁者的统治下遭受了多年的暴政。 | synonyms: tyranny, despotism, oppression | antonyms: good government, democracy, justice | noun

misrule:/ˌmɪsˈruːl/| v. |  To govern badly or unjustly. | The king misruled the country, leading to widespread poverty and unrest. 国王对国家施暴政，导致普遍的贫困和动乱。 | synonyms: tyrannize, oppress, dominate | antonyms: govern well, lead justly, rule fairly | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (badly) + rule (to govern).  The prefix "mis-" comes from Old English "mis-" meaning "wrongly, badly, astray." "Rule" comes from Old French "riule" and ultimately from Latin "regula" meaning "straight stick, ruler, pattern."
