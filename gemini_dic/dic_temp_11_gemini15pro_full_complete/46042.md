cycled:/ˈsaɪkəld/
verb
骑自行车；循环
past tense of cycle.  Meaning: rode a bicycle, or moved through a sequence.
I cycled to work this morning. 我今天早上骑自行车上班。
The washing machine cycled through its wash, rinse, and spin functions. 洗衣机循环完成了它的洗涤、漂洗和甩干功能。
近义词:biked, rode
反义词:walked, drove
词性:v.


cycle:/ˈsaɪkəl/
noun, verb
循环；周期；自行车；骑自行车
n. a series of events that are regularly repeated in the same order.  v. ride a bicycle.
The life cycle of a butterfly is fascinating. 蝴蝶的生命周期很奇妙。
She cycles to school every day. 她每天骑自行车上学。
近义词:(n.) period, phase, sequence; (v.) bike, ride
反义词:(v.) walk, drive
词性:n., v.
