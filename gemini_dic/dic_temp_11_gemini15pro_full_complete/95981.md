incumbrance:/ɪnˈkʌmbrəns/ | n. | 负担，累赘，妨碍物 |Something that impedes or is burdensome; a hindrance. |例句：The mortgage was a huge incumbrance on their finances. 这笔抵押贷款对他们的财务状况是一个巨大的负担。|例句：She considered her elderly parents more of a blessing than an incumbrance. 她认为她年迈的父母与其说是负担，不如说是福气。|近义词：burden, obstacle, impediment, hindrance, obstruction |反义词:aid, assistance, help |词性:noun

incumbrance:/ɪnˈkʌmbrəns/ | n. |（尤指法律或财务方面的）义务，责任 | A legal or financial obligation. |例句：He had to sell the property to discharge the incumbrance. 他不得不卖掉房产来偿清债务。|例句：There were several incumbrances on the property title. 房产所有权上有几项责任。|近义词：liability, obligation, charge, debt |反义词:asset |词性:noun
