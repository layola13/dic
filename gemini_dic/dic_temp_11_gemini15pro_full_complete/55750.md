divergent:/daɪˈvɜːrdʒənt/ /dɪˈvɝːdʒənt/| adj.|分歧的，不同的，相异的|tending to be different or develop in different directions|例句：The two species followed divergent evolutionary paths. 这两个物种沿着不同的进化路径发展。|例句：Their opinions on the matter were widely divergent. 他们在这个问题上的意见分歧很大。|近义词：different, varying, dissimilar, disparate, contrasting|反义词：convergent, similar, alike, uniform|词性:形容词

divergence:/daɪˈvɜːrdʒəns/ /dɪˈvɝːdʒəns/| n.|分歧，差异，背离|the process or state of diverging|例句：The divergence of opinion within the group led to its eventual split.  团队内部的意见分歧最终导致了分裂。|例句：There's a significant divergence between the two reports. 这两份报告之间存在显著差异。|近义词：difference, disagreement, disparity, deviation|反义词：convergence, agreement, similarity, unity|词性:名词

diverge:/daɪˈvɜːrdʒ/ /dɪˈvɝːdʒ/| v.|分歧，偏离，相异|to separate and go in different directions|例句：The paths diverged in the woods.  小路在树林里分岔了。|例句：After college, their career paths diverged dramatically. 大学毕业后，他们的职业道路截然不同。|近义词：separate, deviate, branch off, differentiate|反义词：converge, meet, unite, intersect|词性:动词
