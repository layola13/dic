bolt-ons:/ˈboʊltˌɑːnz/ | n. | 附加组件，附加装置 | Something that is added to a larger existing thing, often a product or service, to make it more attractive or useful. |  The car comes with several bolt-ons, including a sunroof and heated seats. 这辆车带有一些附加组件，包括天窗和加热座椅。

The software company offers a variety of bolt-ons, such as additional storage and technical support. 这家软件公司提供各种附加组件，例如额外的存储空间和技术支持。 | 近义词: add-ons, accessories, extras | 反义词: core, essential | 词性: 名词

bolt-on:/ˈboʊltˌɑːn/ | adj. | 附加的，可附加的 |  Able to be bolted on or attached to something else. |  The company offers a bolt-on GPS system for its trucks. 这家公司为其卡车提供附加的GPS系统。

They decided to purchase a bolt-on warranty for their new appliance. 他们决定为他们的新电器购买附加保修。 | 近义词: attachable, add-on, supplementary | 反义词: built-in, integrated, integral | 词性: 形容词
