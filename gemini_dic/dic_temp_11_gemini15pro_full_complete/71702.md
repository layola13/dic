flavored:/ˈfleɪvərd/| adj.|有味道的，加味了的|Having a particular flavor; flavored with a particular substance.|例句：I like flavored yogurt. 我喜欢加味酸奶。|例句：She ordered a flavored coffee. 她点了一杯加味咖啡。|近义词：seasoned, spiced, tasty, aromatic|反义词：unflavored, bland, tasteless|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From flavor + -ed.  "Flavor" comes from Old French *flaur, from Latin *flātor "a blowing, blast, breath," from flāre "to blow." The semantic shift occurred via the notion of "exhalation" and "scent."
