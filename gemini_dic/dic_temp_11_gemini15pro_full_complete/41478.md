cornloft:/ˈkɔːrnˌlɒft/ /ˈkɔːrnˌlɑːft/| n. | 谷仓；玉米仓 |A room or space, typically located above a stable or barn, used for storing corn (especially ears of corn).|The farmer stored the harvest in the cornloft. 农民把收成储存在玉米仓里。|Synonyms: granary, crib|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps silo (for a different type of storage))|noun
