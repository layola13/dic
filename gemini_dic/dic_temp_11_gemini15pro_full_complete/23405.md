brachyural: /brækɪˈjʊərəl/
adjective
短尾的
Having a short tail, especially of decapod crustaceans having a short abdomen tucked under the thorax.  Relating to or characteristic of brachyuran crabs.
Brachyural crabs are known for their broad carapace. 短尾蟹以其宽阔的甲壳而闻名。
The brachyural morphology allows them to scuttle sideways. 短尾的形态使它们能够横向移动。
Synonyms: short-tailed
Antonyms: long-tailed
词性: adjective
