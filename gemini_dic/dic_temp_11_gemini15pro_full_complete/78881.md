ginkgo:/ˈɡɪŋkoʊ/ (US), /ˈɡɪŋkɡəʊ/ (UK)| n.|银杏（树）|A large Chinese tree with fan-shaped leaves and foul-smelling yellowish fruit.|例句：The ginkgo tree outside my window turns golden in the fall. 我窗外那棵银杏树在秋天变成了金黄色。|例句：Ginkgo biloba extract is believed by some to improve memory.  有些人认为银杏叶提取物可以提高记忆力。|近义词：maidenhair tree|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
