moe:/moʊ/| n. |萌|A Japanese term used to describe feelings of affection, adoration, devotion, and excitement, primarily towards characters in anime, manga, and video games. It can also be used to describe the characters themselves.|She has a real moe for that anime character.|近义词:kawaii, adorable|反义词:kirai (dislike)|词性:noun
