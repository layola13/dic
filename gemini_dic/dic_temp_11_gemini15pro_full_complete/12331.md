aseptics: /eɪˈsɛptɪks/ | n. |无菌法；无菌技术；防腐剂|The practice and procedures involved in preventing contamination by microorganisms; substances that prevent infection.|Hospitals employ strict aseptics to minimize the risk of infection. 医院采用严格的无菌技术，以尽量减少感染的风险。| The aseptics used during surgery are crucial. 手术中使用的无菌技术至关重要。| synonyms: sterile techniques, antiseptics | antonyms: sepsis, contamination | noun


aseptic: /eɪˈsɛptɪk/ | adj. |无菌的；防腐的；冷漠的|Free from contamination caused by harmful bacteria, viruses, or other microorganisms; preventing infection; lacking emotion or warmth.|The operating room must be aseptic. 手术室必须是无菌的。|  He spoke in an aseptic tone, devoid of any emotion. 他用一种冷漠的语气说话，没有任何感情。| synonyms: sterile, germ-free, antiseptic, impassive | antonyms: septic, contaminated, emotional | adjective
