humite:/ˈhjuːmɪt/ | n. | 硅镁石 | A mineral of the humite group, a magnesium iron silicate hydroxide fluoride.  A nesosilicate of magnesium with varying proportions of hydroxyl and fluorine. | Example 1: The geologist discovered a rare sample of humite in the volcanic rock.  地质学家在火山岩中发现了一种罕见的硅镁石样本。

Example 2: Humite is often found in association with other metamorphic minerals. 硅镁石经常与其他变质矿物伴生。 | Synonym: chondrodite (similar mineral), clinohumite | Antonym: N/A (Minerals don't typically have antonyms) | noun
