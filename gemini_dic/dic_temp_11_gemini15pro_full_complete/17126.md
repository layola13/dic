batteries:/ˈbætəriz/ /ˈbætəriːz/| n.|电池|A device that stores and provides electrical power.  Multiple battery units.|例句：The batteries in my remote control are dead. 我的遥控器里的电池没电了。

例句：I need new batteries for my flashlight. 我的手电筒需要新电池。


近义词：cells, power sources|反义词:|词性:noun (plural)


batteries:/ˈbætəriz/ /ˈbætəriːz/| n.|一组砲台；砲兵连|A group of heavy guns used in warfare, especially artillery. |例句：The batteries bombarded the enemy positions. 砲兵连轰炸了敌人的阵地。

例句：The batteries were positioned on the hill overlooking the valley. 炮兵阵地设在俯瞰山谷的小山上。


近义词：artillery, ordnance|反义词:|词性:noun (plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From French "batterie" (a beating, battery), from "battre" (to beat), from Latin "battuere" (to beat).  Originally referred to a group of cannons and later extended to a group of similar items, including electrical cells.
