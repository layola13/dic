nonpertinencies:/ˌnɑːnpɜːrtnənsɪz/
| n. | 无关紧要的事物；不相干的事物 |Irrelevant or unrelated matters; things that do not pertain to the subject at hand.  Derived from "non" (not) + "pertinent" (relevant) + "-cies" (plural suffix).|The judge asked the lawyer to refrain from introducing nonpertinencies into the case.  法官要求律师不要在案件中引入无关紧要的事情。|Synonyms: irrelevancies, digressions, extraneous matters|Antonyms: relevancies, pertinent information|noun

nonpertinency:/ˌnɑːnpɜːrtnənsi/
| n. | 无关紧要；不相干 |The state of being irrelevant or unrelated to the subject at hand.|The nonpertinency of his comments was apparent to everyone in the room. 他评论的无关紧要是房间里每个人都显而易见的。|Synonyms: irrelevance, extraneousness|Antonyms: relevance, pertinence|noun
