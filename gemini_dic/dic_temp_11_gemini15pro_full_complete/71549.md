flash-in-the-pan:/ˌflæʃ ɪn ðə ˈpæn/ | n. |昙花一现的人或事；一时成功|Something or someone that achieves sudden success or popularity but quickly fades away.| The band's first album was a huge hit, but they turned out to be a flash in the pan.  这个乐队的首张专辑非常成功，但结果证明他们只是昙花一现。| 近义词:one-hit wonder, passing fad, short-lived success | 反义词:lasting success, enduring achievement | 词性:名词

flash-in-the-pan:/ˌflæʃ ɪn ðə ˈpæn/ | adj. (Less common) |昙花一现的|Characterized by sudden success or popularity that quickly fades away.| Their flash-in-the-pan fame didn't last long. 他们昙花一现的名声并没有持续多久。 | 近义词:short-lived, ephemeral, transient | 反义词:lasting, enduring, persistent | 词性:形容词
