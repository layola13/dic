outhasten:/ˌaʊtˈheɪsən/
verb
超过…速度；赶在…之前
to hasten more than or to get ahead of in doing something.
They outhastened the other runners and won the race. 他们超过了其他选手，赢得了比赛。
outhasten the sunrise  比太阳起得还早
synonyms: outstrip, surpass, outpace, exceed
antonyms: lag behind, fall behind
词性: 动词
