bicycle: /ˈbaɪsɪkəl/ (US) /ˈbaɪsɪkl/ (UK) | n. |自行车|A vehicle with two wheels, one behind the other, propelled by pedals and steered with handlebars. |例句：I ride my bicycle to work every day. 我每天骑自行车上班。

例句：The bicycle is a popular mode of transportation. 自行车是一种流行的交通方式。

近义词：bike, cycle |反义词: car, bus |词性: noun


bike: /baɪk/ | n. |自行车；摩托车| Short for bicycle or motorbike. |例句：I'll take my bike to the park. 我会骑自行车去公园。

例句：He just bought a new bike. 他刚买了一辆新自行车。

近义词：bicycle, cycle, motorcycle (for motorbike) |反义词: car, bus |词性: noun
