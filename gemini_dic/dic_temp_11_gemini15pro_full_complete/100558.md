interwishes: /ˌɪntərˈwɪʃɪz/
| n. |相互的愿望，共同的愿望|Mutual desires or wishes shared between individuals or groups.  It expresses a sense of reciprocal hoping or aspiration.|The interwishes of the two nations for peace led to a treaty.|近义词：shared desires, mutual aspirations, common goals|反义词: conflicting desires, opposing wishes|词性:名词

词源:  "Inter-" (prefix) meaning "between, among, mutually" + "wishes" (noun) meaning "desires, hopes." This word is a neologism, meaning it's a recently coined word and not yet established in standard dictionaries.  It's formed by combining existing morphemes in a novel way.
