oceanography:/ˌoʊʃəˈnɑːɡrəfi/ | n. |海洋学|The science that deals with the physical and biological aspects of the oceans.|例句：She studied oceanography at university. 她在大学学习海洋学。

例句：Oceanography helps us understand the complex interactions within marine ecosystems. 海洋学帮助我们了解海洋生态系统中复杂的相互作用。|近义词：marine science, oceanology|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ὠκεανός (ōkeanós, “ocean”) + -γραφία (-graphía, “description, writing”).
