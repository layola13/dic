courtier:/ˈkɔːrtɪə(r)/ /ˈkɔːrtiər/| n.|朝臣；廷臣；奉承者|A person who attends a royal court as a companion or advisor to the king or queen.  A person who tries to please someone in order to gain an advantage.|例句：The courtier bowed low before the king. 朝臣在国王面前深深鞠了一躬。

例句：He was a skilled courtier, adept at navigating the complexities of court life. 他是一位老练的朝臣，善于应对复杂的宫廷生活。|近义词：attendant, sycophant, flatterer|反义词：rebel, dissident|词性:noun
