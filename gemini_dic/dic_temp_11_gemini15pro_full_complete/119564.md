microtome:/ˈmaɪkrətoʊm/| n.|切片机|An instrument for cutting extremely thin sections of material, especially for microscopic examination.|The microtome is essential for preparing tissue samples for pathological analysis. 切片机对于制备用于病理分析的组织样本至关重要。|近义词:sectioning instrument|反义词:None|名词

Etymology->
From micro- (small) + -tome (cutting instrument).  The word element "-tome" comes from the Greek "tomos," meaning "cut" or "slice."
