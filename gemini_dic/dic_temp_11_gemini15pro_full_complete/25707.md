bunnier:/ˈbʌniər/| adj.|更好的，更棒的|Comparative form of "bunny," meaning more bunny-like. This is a slang/informal term and rarely used.  It implies something is cuter, more adorable, or more appealing in a way similar to a bunny. |That stuffed animal is even bunnier than the last one. 那个毛绒玩具比上一个更像兔子/更可爱。|近义词：cuter, sweeter, more adorable|反义词：uglier, less appealing|词性: 形容词
