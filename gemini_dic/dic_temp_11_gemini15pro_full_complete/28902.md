carrells:/kəˈrɛlz/| n.|卡雷尔式学习桌；小单间|A small individual study area, typically enclosed by low partitions, designed to provide privacy and minimize distractions for users, especially in libraries.|The library has numerous carrells for students to study in. 图书馆里有许多卡雷尔式学习桌供学生学习。|近义词：study cubicle, study desk, carrel|反义词:open study area|词性:名词
