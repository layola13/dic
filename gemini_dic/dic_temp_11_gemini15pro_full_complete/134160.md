nonimputable:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpjuːtəbl/
adjective
不可归咎的，不可归罪的
Not capable of being imputed or attributed; not chargeable.
His actions were deemed nonimputable due to his mental state. 由于他的精神状态，他的行为被认为是不可归咎的。
The accident was nonimputable to the driver.  这起事故不能归咎于司机。
近义词: unattributable, non-ascribable, inexcusable
反义词: imputable, attributable, culpable
词性: adjective

词源:
From non- + imputable, from Late Latin imputabilis, from imputare “to reckon, ascribe,” from in- “into” + putare “to think, reckon.”
