diskindness:/dɪsˈkaɪndnɪs/
n.|不友善，刻薄，冷酷|Lack of kindness; cruelty. |She remembered his unkindness and refused to speak to him. 她记得他的刻薄，拒绝和他说话。|He showed great unkindness to his former friends. 他对以前的朋友非常冷酷无情。|近义词：cruelty, harshness, meanness, ruthlessness, unkindness|反义词：kindness, compassion, benevolence, generosity, sympathy|词性: 名词

词源:
From dis- (expressing negation) + kindness.  The prefix "dis-" comes from Latin and means "apart," "asunder," "away," "utterly," or having a privative, negative, or reversing force. "Kindness" comes from Middle English kindenes, from kind + -ness.
