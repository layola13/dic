curatorial:/kjʊərəˈtɔːriəl/
adjective|策展的；馆长的|Relating to the selection and organization of a collection, especially in a museum or art gallery.  Pertaining to a curator or their duties.|The museum's curatorial team is highly respected.  博物馆的策展团队备受尊敬。|近义词：museological, exhibitionary|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "uncurated")
词性: adjective
