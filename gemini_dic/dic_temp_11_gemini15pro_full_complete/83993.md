half-defiant:/ˌhæf dɪˈfaɪənt/
| adj. | 半挑衅的；略带反抗的；半不服气的 | Showing a mixture of defiance and some other emotion, such as fear or uncertainty.  It suggests a hesitant or incomplete rebellion. |  He gave a half-defiant, half-pleading look. 他带着半挑衅半恳求的眼神。 | Near synonyms: partially defiant, somewhat defiant, slightly rebellious | Near antonyms: submissive, compliant, obedient | adjective

half-defiant:/ˌhæf dɪˈfaɪənt/
| adj. | 半挑衅的；略带反抗的；半不服气的 |  Implying a subdued or restrained defiance; not fully or openly rebellious.  It often suggests a struggle between wanting to resist and feeling constrained. | She lifted her chin in a half-defiant gesture. 她略带挑衅地扬起了下巴。 | Near synonyms:  half-hearted defiance, subdued defiance, restrained rebellion | Near antonyms:  fully compliant, completely obedient, totally submissive | adjective
