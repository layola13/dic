ETYMOLOGY->
From French *hippodrome*, from Latin *hippodromus*, from Ancient Greek *ἱππόδρομος* (hippódromos, “racecourse for horses, horse-racing”), from *ἵππος* (híppos, “horse”) + *δρόμος* (drómos, “course, running, race”).
