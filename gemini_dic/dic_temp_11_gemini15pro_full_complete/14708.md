awardable:/əˈwɔːrdəbl/
adjective
可授予的，可奖励的
Suitable for being given as an award.
The scholarship is awardable to students who demonstrate both academic excellence and financial need.  这笔奖学金可授予那些在学业上表现优异且有经济需要的学生。
The prize is awardable to anyone who completes the challenge.  任何完成挑战的人都可以获得该奖项。
Synonyms: grantable, conferrable
Antonyms: unawardable, ineligible
词性: 形容词
