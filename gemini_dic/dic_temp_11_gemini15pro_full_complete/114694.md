malinger:/məˈlɪŋɡər/| v. |装病；逃避责任|To pretend to be ill or incapacitated in order to avoid work or duty.|He malingered for weeks, claiming a back injury. 他装病几周，声称背部受伤。|例句：The soldier malingered to avoid going into battle.  这个士兵装病以逃避上战场。

例句：He was suspected of malingering to get out of military service. 他被怀疑装病以逃避兵役。|近义词：shirk, feign, fake, dodge, goldbrick|反义词：persevere, work, labor|词性:动词
