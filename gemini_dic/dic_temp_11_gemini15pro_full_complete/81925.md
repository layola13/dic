grillroom: /ˈɡrɪlˌruːm/ | n. | 烧烤室，烤肉餐厅 | A room, especially in a restaurant, containing a grill for cooking food. |  We had dinner in the hotel's grillroom last night. 昨晚我们在这家酒店的烤肉餐厅吃了晚饭。

The grillroom specializes in steaks and seafood. 这家烤肉餐厅专营牛排和海鲜。 | 近义词：steakhouse, barbecue restaurant | 反义词: None | 词性: 名词


词源: grill (n.) + room (n.)

"grill" 源自古法语 "griller"，意为 "to broil, toast"，最终源自拉丁语 "craticula"，意为 "small gridiron".

"room" 源自古英语 "rūm,"，意为 "space, place."
