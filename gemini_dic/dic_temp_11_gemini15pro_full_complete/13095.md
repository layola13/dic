astronomical: /ˌæstrəˈnɒmɪkəl/ (US) /ˌæstrəˈnɒmɪkl/ (UK)| adj.|天文数字的，庞大的|Relating to astronomy or the vastness of space; extremely large. |例句：The cost of the project was astronomical.  这个项目的成本天文数字。|近义词：huge, immense, vast, enormous, colossal|反义词：tiny, minuscule, small, insignificant|词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin *astronomicus, from Ancient Greek ἀστρονομικός (astronomikós, “astronomical”), from ἄστρον (ástron, “star”) + νόμος (nómos, “law, custom”).
