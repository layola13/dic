audio-lingual:/ˌɔːdiəʊˈlɪŋɡwəl/ | adj. |听说法的，视听法的| Relating to or involving the use of both hearing and speaking in the learning of a language. |The audio-lingual method emphasizes repetition and drills. 视听教学法强调重复和练习。| 近义词: aural-oral | 反义词: written, visual |词性: 形容词
