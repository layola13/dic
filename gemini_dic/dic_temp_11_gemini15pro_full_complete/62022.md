enargite:/ˈɛnɑːrdʒaɪt/| n. | 辉砷铜矿 |A metallic, grayish-black mineral, Cu3AsS4, an important ore of copper. | Enargite is often found in association with other copper sulfide minerals. 辉砷铜矿经常与其他硫化铜矿物伴生。|Synonyms: luzonite (a polymorph), famatinite |Antonyms: None (specific mineral, no direct opposite) |noun
