animatronicses:/ˌænɪməˈtrɒnɪksɪz/ |n.|机电一体化装置;电子动画装置|The technology of making robots that look and act like living creatures, typically used in film and amusement parks.|The animatronicses in the new dinosaur exhibit were incredibly realistic. 新恐龙展览中的电子动画装置非常逼真。|近义词:robotics, automation|反义词:None|词性:名词

animatronic:/ˌænɪməˈtrɒnɪk/ |adj.|机电一体化的;电子动画的|Relating to animatronics.|The animatronic dinosaurs roared and moved their heads. 电子动画恐龙咆哮着并移动着它们的头部。|近义词:robotic, automated|反义词:None|词性:形容词

animatronic:/ˌænɪməˈtrɒnɪk/ |n.|机电一体化装置;电子动画装置(单数)|A robot that looks and acts like a living creature, typically used in film and amusement parks.|The animatronic on display was a life-sized replica of a Tyrannosaurus Rex.  展出的电子动画装置是霸王龙的真人大小的复制品。|近义词:robot, automaton|反义词:None|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From animate (赋予生命) + electronics (电子学). Coined by Walt Disney Imagineer Bob Gurr.
