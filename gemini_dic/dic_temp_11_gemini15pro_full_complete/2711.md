adsorbability:/ˌædzɔːrbəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 吸附性；吸附能力 | The adsorbability of a material refers to its ability to attract and hold other substances on its surface. | The activated carbon's high adsorbability makes it effective in water filtration.  活性炭的高吸附性使其在水过滤中有效。 | synonyms: adsorption capacity, sorption capacity | antonyms: desorbability | noun
