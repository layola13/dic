hematophilia:/ˌhiːməˈfɪliə/ /ˌheməˈfɪliə/| n. |血友病|A medical condition, typically inherited, in which the blood doesn't clot normally, leading to excessive bleeding from even minor injuries.|Hemophilia is a serious genetic disorder. 血友病是一种严重的遗传性疾病。|My cousin has hemophilia and has to be careful to avoid injuries. 我的表弟患有血友病，必须小心避免受伤。|Synonyms: bleeding disorder, coagulopathy|Antonyms: normal blood clotting|noun
