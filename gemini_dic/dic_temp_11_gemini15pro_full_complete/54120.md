disenchanted:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtʃæntɪd/
adjective
失望的，不再抱有幻想的
Freed from illusion or enchantment; disappointed.
She became disenchanted with politics and left the party. 她对政治感到失望，离开了该党。
The movie is about a disenchanted princess who leaves her fairytale life.  这部电影讲述的是一位不再抱有幻想的公主离开了她的童话生活。
近义词: disillusioned, disappointed, jaded
反义词: enchanted, charmed, delighted
词性: adjective

词源:
From dis- (expressing negation) + enchanted (past participle of enchant).  "Dis-" 表示否定，"enchanted" 是 "enchant"（使着迷）的过去分词。
