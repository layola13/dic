nonmystic:/ˌnɑːnˈmɪstɪk/
adjective
非神秘的;不神秘主义的
Not mystical; not related to or characterized by mysticism.
He offered a nonmystic interpretation of the event. 他对这一事件提出了非神秘主义的解释。

近义词:mundane, earthly, secular, practical, realistic
反义词:mystic, mystical, spiritual, esoteric, occult
词性:adjective
