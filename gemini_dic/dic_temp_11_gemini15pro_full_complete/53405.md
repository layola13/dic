disambiguated:/dɪsˌæmˈbɪɡjueɪtɪd/ | adj. | 消除了歧义的，明确的 |  Having removed ambiguity or uncertainty; made clear. | The meaning of the sentence was disambiguated by the context. 这句话的意思通过上下文消除了歧义。| clarified, specified, distinct, clear, unequivocal | ambiguous, vague, unclear, equivocal | adjective

disambiguate:/dɪsˌæmˈbɪɡjueɪt/ | v. | 消除歧义，使明确 | To remove uncertainty of meaning from; make clear. |  The writer disambiguated the passage by adding a footnote. 作者通过添加脚注消除了这段文字的歧义。 | clarify, specify, clear up, explain, define | confuse, obscure, complicate | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (not, opposite of) + ambiguous (having more than one possible meaning) + -ate (verb suffix) + -ed (past participle suffix). Ultimately from Latin ambigere (to wander, be uncertain).
