ginnery:/ˈdʒɪnəri/
n.
轧棉厂，轧棉机，杜松子酒厂
A place where cotton is ginned, or a machine for ginning cotton.  Also, a distillery for gin.
The old ginnery stood silent, a testament to a bygone era.  那座旧轧棉厂静静地矗立着，见证着过去的时代。
The ginnery produced both cotton fiber and cottonseed.  轧棉厂生产棉纤维和棉籽。
Synonym: cotton gin, distillery
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "cotton field" for the cotton processing meaning)
