aesthetical: /ɛsˈθɛtɪkəl/ /iːsˈθetɪkl/ | adj. | 美学的；审美的；有美感的 | Relating to aesthetics; concerned with beauty or the appreciation of beauty. |  The aesthetical appeal of the painting was undeniable. 这幅画的美学魅力不可否认。| Synonyms: artistic, beautiful, elegant, tasteful | Antonyms: ugly, unattractive, displeasing | adjective

aesthetics: /ɛsˈθɛtɪks/ /iːsˈθetɪks/ | n. | 美学 |  A set of principles concerned with the nature and appreciation of beauty, especially in art. | The course covered the aesthetics of the Renaissance period. 这门课程涵盖了文艺复兴时期的美学。| Synonyms: philosophy of art, theory of beauty | Antonyms: N/A | noun
