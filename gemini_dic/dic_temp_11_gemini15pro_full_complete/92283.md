hyphen:/ˈhaɪfən/ | n. |连字符，连接号 |A punctuation mark (-) used to join words or parts of words. |Example: The word "well-being" uses a hyphen.  单词“well-being”使用了连字符。|Synonyms: dash |Antonyms: N/A | noun

hyphen:/ˈhaɪfən/ | v. |用连字符连接 |To connect (words or parts of words) with a hyphen. |Example:  They hyphenated the two words to form a compound adjective.  他们用连字符连接这两个词，构成一个复合形容词。|Synonyms: connect, join |Antonyms: separate, divide | verb
