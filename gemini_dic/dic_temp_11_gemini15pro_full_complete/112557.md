loveliest:/ˈlʌvliɪst/| adj.|最可爱的，最美丽的|Superlative form of lovely; most lovely.  Meaning the most beautiful or attractive.|例句：She wore the loveliest dress to the party. 她穿着最漂亮的裙子去参加了聚会。|近义词：prettiest, most beautiful, most charming|反义词：ugliest, least attractive|词性:形容词

loveliest:/ˈlʌvliɪst/| adj.|最令人愉快的|Superlative form of lovely; most delightful. Meaning the most pleasing or enjoyable.|例句：It was the loveliest evening we've had in a long time. 这是我们很久以来最美好的一个夜晚。|近义词：most pleasant, most delightful, most enjoyable|反义词：most unpleasant, least enjoyable|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *loveliest, superlative of lovely, from Old English luflic (“lovely”).
