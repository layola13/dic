doctoral: /ˈdɑːktərəl/ (US) /ˈdɒktərəl/ (UK)| adj.| 博士的；博士学位的|Of, relating to, or designed to lead to a doctorate.  A doctoral program is a program of study leading to a doctorate.|She's working on her doctoral dissertation. 她正在撰写她的博士论文。|近义词: postgraduate|反义词: undergraduate|词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *doctoralis*, from Latin doctor (“teacher, learned man”) +‎ -alis.
