acalycine: /ˌækəˈlaɪsaɪn/ | adj. | 无花萼的；无萼片的 | Lacking a calyx; having no sepals. |  The acalycine flower was unusual and caught the botanist's attention.  这朵无萼片的花很不寻常，引起了植物学家的注意。

The acalycine nature of this plant makes it difficult to classify.  这种植物无花萼的特性使其难以分类。 | 近义词: asepalous | 反义词: sepalous | 词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *a-* "without" + *kalyx* "cup of a flower, calyx."
