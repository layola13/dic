europeanize:/juːrəˈpɪənʌɪz/ |v. |欧洲化|To make something more European in character or style; to influence something with European ideas, customs, or institutions.|Many countries in the world have been europeanized through trade and cultural exchange. 世界上许多国家通过贸易和文化交流实现了欧洲化。|The government's policies are aimed at europeanizing the education system. 政府的政策旨在使教育系统欧洲化。|westernize, modernize|localize, traditionalize|Verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Europe + -ize.  "Europe" ultimately derives from Greek "Eurṓpē," of uncertain etymology.  "-ize" is a suffix forming verbs with the sense "to make or become (like), to treat with, to subject to."
