exorcizes:/ˈɛksɔːrsaɪzɪz/
|v.|驱魔，驱除(坏事)，|to expel (an evil spirit) from a person or place by means of a religious ritual.|The priest exorcizes the demons from the possessed man.|驱魔人将恶魔从被附身的人身上驱除出去。|
|v.|消除，去除(不好的想法、感觉)，|to rid oneself or another of a troublesome or unwelcome feeling, memory, or influence.|She tried to exorcize the memories of the accident.|她试图消除事故的记忆。|
|近义词:|expel, drive out, cast out, banish, purge|
|反义词:|invoke, summon, conjure|
|词性:|verb (动词)
