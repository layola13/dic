hayloft:/ˈheɪlɒft/ (US) /ˈheɪlɒft/ (UK)| n. |干草棚，草料棚|A storage space within a barn or similar agricultural building, typically located above the ground floor, used to hold hay or straw for animal feed.|The children loved playing in the hayloft during the summer holidays. 孩子们喜欢在暑假期间在干草棚里玩耍。|
例句：
The farmer stored the hay in the hayloft for the winter. 农民把干草储存在干草棚里过冬。

The cat jumped down from the hayloft onto a pile of straw. 猫从干草棚上跳到一堆稻草上。

近义词：barn loft, hay mow |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
