emerged:/ɪˈmɜːrdʒd/
verb
出现，浮现，暴露
come out into view, as from concealment
After hours of deliberation, a solution finally emerged. 经过数小时的审议，终于找到了解决方案。
The facts of the case emerged slowly.  案件的真相慢慢地浮出水面。
appear, arise, come out, come up, develop
disappear, fade, vanish
v.

被动语态:

The details were gradually revealed. 细节逐渐被揭露出来。 (Correct passive voice alternative)
