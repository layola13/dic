degu:/ˈdeɪɡuː/
n.
degu
A small rodent native to Chile, also known as the common degu or Octodon degus.
Degus are social animals and live in burrows. 八齿鼠是一种原产于智利的啮齿动物，也称为普通八齿鼠。八齿鼠是群居动物，生活在地洞里。
Degus make excellent pets. 八齿鼠是很好的宠物。
近义词: rodent, cavy
