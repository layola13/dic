conjoiner:/kənˈdʒɔɪnər/| n.|连接器，连接体|A conjoiner is something that joins or connects things together. |The conjoiner in this electrical circuit is faulty.  这个电路中的连接器有问题。|近义词：connector, coupler, link, joiner|反义词:separator, divider|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From conjoin + -er.  Conjoin comes from Latin conjungere "to join together," from com- "together" + jungere "to join."
