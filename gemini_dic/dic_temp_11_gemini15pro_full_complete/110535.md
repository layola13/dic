limed:/laɪmd/
verb|涂上石灰，用石灰处理|To treat or cover with lime.|The farmer limed the soil to reduce its acidity. 农民用石灰处理土壤以降低酸度。|近义词：whitewash, plaster|反义词: acidify
adjective|涂过石灰的，用石灰处理过的|Covered or treated with lime.|The walls of the old house were limed. 老房子的墙壁涂了石灰。|近义词：whitewashed, plastered|反义词: untreated


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *līmian, from līm (“lime”).  The word "lime" itself ultimately comes from Proto-Germanic *lēmaz,  from Proto-Indo-European *ley-, meaning "to smear, stick."
