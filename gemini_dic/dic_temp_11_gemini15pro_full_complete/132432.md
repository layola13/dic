nondependency:/ˌnɒndɪˈpɛndənsi/ | n. | 非依赖性，独立性 |  The state or quality of not being dependent; independence.  | The company emphasized the nondependency of its new software, highlighting its ability to function offline.  该公司强调其新软件的独立性，突出其离线运行的能力。 | Example: The country declared its nondependency from foreign aid. 这个国家宣布不再依赖外国援助。 | Synonyms: independence, self-reliance, autonomy, self-sufficiency | Antonyms: dependence, reliance, subjection | noun
