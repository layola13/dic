melty:/ˈmɛlti/| adj.|融化的，熔化的，快融化的|Starting to melt; becoming liquid.  Almost melted, soft, and gooey. |The ice cream was melty in the hot sun. 冰淇淋在烈日下融化了。|The cheese on the pizza was perfectly melty and stringy. 比萨上的奶酪融化得恰到好处，而且可以拉丝。|近义词：molten, thawing, dissolving, soft, gooey|反义词:frozen, solid, hard|词性:形容词
Etymology: From melt + -y.  "Melt" comes from Old English "melten," of Germanic origin. The suffix "-y" is commonly used to form adjectives indicating a state or condition.
