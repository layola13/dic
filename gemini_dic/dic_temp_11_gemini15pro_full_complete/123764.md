moorings:/ˈmʊərɪŋz/
n.
停泊处；系泊设备
The ship was secured to its moorings.  这艘船被固定在它的停泊处。
The boat dragged its moorings in the storm.  在暴风雨中，船的系泊设备被拖走了。
Place where a boat or ship is moored.
The yacht was safe in its moorings.  游艇在它的泊位上很安全。
synonyms: anchorages, berth, dock, pier, wharf
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider "drifting," "adrift")


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *morynge, from Old Norse *mǫring, from Proto-Germanic *mōrjaną (“to moor”), from Proto-Indo-European *mer- (“sea, lake”). Related to moor (v.).
