amblyopia:/ˌæmbliˈoʊpiə/ | n. |弱视|A condition in which the vision in one eye is reduced because the eye and the brain are not working together properly.  It is sometimes called lazy eye.|Example: Early detection and treatment of amblyopia are crucial to prevent permanent vision loss. 早期发现和治疗弱视对于预防永久性视力丧失至关重要。

Example:  The child was diagnosed with amblyopia and prescribed an eye patch.  这个孩子被诊断出患有弱视，并被规定要戴眼罩。|近义词：lazy eye,  hypovision|反义词: normal vision|词性:noun
