The word "effossion" doesn't exist in standard English dictionaries.  It's likely a misspelling of "effusion."  Here's the analysis for "effusion":

effusion: /ɪˈfjuːʒən/ | n. | 流出；涌出；倾泻；(感情的)流露，迸发 | An instance of giving off something such as a liquid, light, or smell.  Also, an unrestrained expression of feeling. |  Example 1: The effusion of blood from the wound was alarming. 伤口处涌出的鲜血令人惊恐。

Example 2:  Her poem was an effusion of grief after her mother's death.  她的诗是她母亲去世后悲伤的流露。 | 近义词: outpouring, discharge, gush, overflow | 反义词: suppression, concealment | 词性: noun
