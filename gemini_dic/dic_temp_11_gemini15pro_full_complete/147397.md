overnighted:/ˌoʊvərˈnaɪtɪd/| v. adj.|过夜的，连夜快递的|To send something by overnight delivery; to stay somewhere overnight.|例句：I overnighted the package to ensure it arrived on time. 我连夜快递了包裹以确保它准时到达。

例句：We overnighted in a small hotel on our way to the coast. 我们在去海岸的路上在一个小旅馆过夜。|近义词：shipped overnight, express delivered, stayed overnight|反义词:delayed, held, stayed during the day|词性:动词/形容词
overnight:/ˌoʊvərˈnaɪt/| adv. adj.|一夜之间，突然，过夜的|Happening suddenly or quickly; during the night.|例句：His success happened almost overnight. 他的成功几乎是一夜之间发生的。

例句：She took an overnight flight to London. 她乘坐夜间航班去了伦敦。|近义词：suddenly, quickly, instantly, during the night|反义词:gradually, slowly, during the day|词性:副词/形容词

词源: "Over" + "night". "Over" meaning "during the course of," and "night" referring to the period from sunset to sunrise.  The verb form evolved from the adjective and adverbial uses.
