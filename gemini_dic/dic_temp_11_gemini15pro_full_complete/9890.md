apathetic:/ˌæpəˈθɛtɪk/ | adj. | 冷漠的，无动于衷的 | Having or showing little or no emotion; not interested or concerned; indifferent. | He was apathetic about the upcoming election, not even bothering to register to vote. 他对即将到来的选举漠不关心，甚至懒得登记投票。|  She remained apathetic despite our pleas for help. 尽管我们恳求帮助，她仍然无动于衷。 | 近义词: indifferent, uninterested, unconcerned, listless | 反义词: enthusiastic, passionate, interested, concerned | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Greek *apathēs* "without feeling," from *a-* "without" + *pathos* "feeling."
