immunise:/ˈɪmjʊnaɪz/ (UK) /ˈɪmjəˌnaɪz/ (US)| vt.|免疫，使免疫|To make someone immune to a disease, usually by giving them a vaccine.|例句：The doctor immunised the children against measles. 医生给孩子们接种了麻疹疫苗。|例句：They are immunising all the cattle against anthrax. 他们正在给所有牛接种炭疽疫苗。|近义词：vaccinate, inoculate|反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 expose (暴露于))|词性:动词
