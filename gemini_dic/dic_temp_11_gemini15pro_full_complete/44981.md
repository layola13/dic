crystallite:/ˈkrɪstəˌlaɪt/| n. |微晶，晶粒|A small crystal, especially one of an imperfectly formed crystal found in a crystalline substance.|The properties of the material are determined by the size and orientation of the crystallites.|Synonyms: grain, microcrystal|Antonyms: amorphous solid|noun
