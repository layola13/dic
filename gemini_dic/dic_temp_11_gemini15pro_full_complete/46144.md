cymophane:/ˈsaɪməˌfeɪn/ | n. | 金绿宝石（猫眼石的一种） | A variety of chrysoberyl that exhibits chatoyancy (a silky sheen and a bright line of reflected light).  | The cymophane ring caught the light beautifully. 这枚金绿宝石戒指漂亮地捕捉了光线。 |  The jeweler showed her a stunning cymophane necklace.  珠宝商给她看了一条令人惊艳的金绿宝石项链。 | 近义词:cat's-eye, chrysoberyl | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French *cymophane*, from Ancient Greek κύμα (kúma, “wave”) + φαίνω (phaínō, “to appear, shine”)
