interstreet:/ˈɪntərˌstriːt/
adjective
街道间的；街坊的
Situated between streets; relating to the area between streets.
The interstreet rivalry was intense. 街坊间的竞争很激烈。
近义词: between streets, neighborhood
反义词: none
词性: adjective

词源:  "inter-" (prefix meaning "between") + "street"
