bummer:/ˈbʌmər/| n.|令人失望的事；扫兴的事|A disappointing or unpleasant situation or experience. |例句：It was a real bummer that the concert was cancelled.  音乐会被取消真是太扫兴了。

例句：Losing my wallet was a major bummer. 丢了钱包真是件倒霉事。


近义词：disappointment, let-down, drag, downer|反义词:joy, pleasure|词性:noun


词源:
Likely from German “Bummler” (loafer, idler), suggesting a sense of inactivity or frustration.  It gained popularity in American slang during the 1960s.
