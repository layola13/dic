kvetchers:/ˈkvɛtʃərz/ | n. | 抱怨者，爱发牢骚的人 | People who complain frequently, often about minor issues.  They habitually express dissatisfaction or grumble. |  The kvetchers in the office were constantly complaining about the temperature. 办公室里那些爱抱怨的人总是抱怨温度。|  My grandma is a real kvetchers, always finding something to gripe about. 我奶奶真是个爱抱怨的人，总是能找到一些事情来抱怨。 | complainers, whiners, gripers |  optimists, enthusiasts | noun
