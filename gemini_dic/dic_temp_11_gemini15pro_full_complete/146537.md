overglamourized: /ˌoʊvərˈɡlæmərʌɪzd/ | adj. | 过分美化的，过分渲染的 |  Presented as more glamorous than is true or appropriate.  Making something seem more exciting and attractive than it really is. |  The movie overglamourized the life of a rock star, showing only the parties and the fame.  这部电影过分美化了摇滚明星的生活，只展现了派对和名声。 |  romanticized, idealized, exaggerated, embellished | understated, realistic, down-to-earth | adjective
