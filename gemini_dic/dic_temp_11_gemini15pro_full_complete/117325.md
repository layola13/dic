meddlesome: /ˈmɛdəlsəm/ | adj. | 爱管闲事的，好干预的 | Given to meddling; interfering. |  She's a meddlesome old woman, always poking her nose into other people's business. 她是个爱管闲事的老太太，总是干涉别人的事情。

|例句：He's a meddlesome type who can't resist offering unsolicited advice. 他是个爱管闲事的人，总是忍不住提供不请自来的建议。
|例句：His meddlesome nature often caused friction with his colleagues. 他爱管闲事的性格经常与同事产生摩擦。

|近义词：interfering, intrusive, nosy, officious |反义词：respectful, unintrusive, minding one's own business |词性: adjective
