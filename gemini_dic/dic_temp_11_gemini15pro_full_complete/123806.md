mopish:/ˈmoʊpɪʃ/| adj.|闷闷不乐的；沮丧的；抑郁的|Gloomy, dejected, and listless.|He sat in the corner, looking mopish after his team lost the game. 他坐在角落里，球队输了比赛后显得闷闷不乐。|She's been quite mopish ever since her best friend moved away. 自从她最好的朋友搬走后，她一直很沮丧。|近义词：glum, downcast, sullen, depressed|反义词：cheerful, happy, jovial|词性:形容词
词源:From mop (n.) + -ish.  Originally in the sense "dull, spiritless" (1530s), from mop (n.1) "grimace," perhaps on the notion of having a drooping countenance. The sense evolved to "dejected, gloomy" (1570s).
