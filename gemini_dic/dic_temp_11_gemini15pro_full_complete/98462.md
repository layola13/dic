inspiring:/ɪnˈspaɪərɪŋ/ | adj. | 鼓舞人心的；启发灵感的 | giving you new ideas and making you feel you want to do something | Her inspiring speech motivated the team to work harder. 她鼓舞人心的演讲激励团队更加努力工作。 |  The inspiring story of his success encouraged many young people.  他成功的励志故事鼓舞了许多年轻人。| 近义词：motivating, encouraging, uplifting | 反义词：discouraging, disheartening | 词性:形容词


Etymology:
From Middle English *inspiren*, from Old French *inspirer*, from Latin *īn-spīrāre* (“to breathe or blow into”), from *in* (“in, into”) + *spīrāre* (“to breathe”).
