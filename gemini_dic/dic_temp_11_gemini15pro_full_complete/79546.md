glol:/ɡlɒl/ | interj. |  (网络用语)大笑，狂笑 | Expressing loud laughter, similar to "LOL" (laughing out loud) | Example: "I just saw a cat wearing a tiny hat, glol!" 我刚看到一只戴着小帽子的猫，笑死我了！| 近义词: LOL, ROFL, LMAO | 反义词:  (无明确的反义词，可以考虑 sad, upset) | 词性: 感叹词


Etymology: "Glol" is likely a variation of "LOL," possibly influenced by other internet slang or by the sound of laughter. Its precise origin is uncertain.
