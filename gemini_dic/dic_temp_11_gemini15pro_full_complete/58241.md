drum-out:/drʌm ˈaʊt/| v.| 驱逐；开除；赶走| To expel someone from a group or organization, usually in a public and humiliating way.  It often involves a formal ceremony, such as a military drumbeat accompanying the person's departure.| The disgraced soldier was drummed out of the regiment.  这名蒙羞的士兵被赶出了军团。| synonyms: expel, dismiss, discharge, cashier, oust | antonyms: admit, reinstate, welcome| verb

drum-out:/drʌm ˈaʊt/| n.| 驱逐；开除；赶走| The act of expelling someone in a public and disgraceful manner.|  His drum-out from the army was a disgrace to his family. 他被军队开除对他的家庭来说是一种耻辱。| synonyms: expulsion, dismissal, discharge | antonyms: admittance, reinstatement| noun
