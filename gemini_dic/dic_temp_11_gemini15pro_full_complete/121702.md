mispredicted:/ˌmɪsprɪˈdɪktɪd/
Verb
错误预测的
Incorrectly predicted; having made a wrong prediction about.
The analyst mispredicted the stock market's performance. 分析师错误地预测了股市的走势。
Near Synonyms: miscalculated, misjudged, misforecasted
Antonyms: correctly predicted, accurately forecasted
词性: 动词
