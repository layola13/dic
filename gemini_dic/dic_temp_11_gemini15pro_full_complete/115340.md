manoeuvrability:/məˌnuːvərəˈbɪləti/ /məˌnuːvrəˈbɪlədi/| n.|机动性；可操作性；操控性|The manoeuvrability of the aircraft was impressive. 这架飞机的机动性令人印象深刻。|例句：The new car boasts excellent manoeuvrability in tight spaces.  这辆新车以其在狭窄空间内的出色操控性而自豪。

例句：The tank's manoeuvrability was severely hampered by the rough terrain.  崎岖的地形严重阻碍了坦克的机动性。|近义词：agility, flexibility, mobility|反义词:clumsiness, rigidity, inflexibility|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *manœuvrabilité*, from *manœuvrable* (“manoeuvrable”) + *-ité* (-ity).  *manœuvrable* from *manœuvrer* (“to manoeuvre”) + *-able* (-able). Ultimately from Latin *manu operare* (“to work by hand”).
