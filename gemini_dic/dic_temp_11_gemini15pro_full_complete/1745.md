acquiet:/əˈkwaɪɪt/ | v. | 免除责任；使平静；履行（义务） | to free someone from a criminal charge by a verdict of not guilty; to conduct oneself in a specified way |  The jury acquitted the defendant of all charges. 陪审团宣判被告所有罪名不成立。 | synonyms: absolve, exonerate, clear, vindicate | antonyms: convict, condemn, incriminate | verb

acquiet:/əˈkwaɪɪt/ | v. |  使平静；抚慰；使安心 | to make someone calmer or less worried | He tried to acquit himself of his guilt by confessing. 他试图通过忏悔来解除自己的负罪感。 | synonyms: soothe, calm, pacify, appease | antonyms: agitate, upset, disturb | verb
