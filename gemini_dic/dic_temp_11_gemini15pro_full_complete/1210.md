accommodates: /əˈkɒmədeɪts/ (UK), /əˈkɑːmədeɪts/ (US)
|v.|容纳；提供住宿；适应；顾及|provides lodging or sufficient space for; adapts to; considers and satisfies the needs of|
The hotel accommodates up to 500 guests.  这家酒店最多可容纳500位客人。

The new design accommodates people with disabilities. 新的设计考虑到了残疾人的需要。

|近义词: house, lodge, fit, suit, adapt, adjust|
|反义词: exclude, reject, refuse|
