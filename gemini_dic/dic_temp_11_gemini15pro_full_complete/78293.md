germinating:/ˈdʒɜːrmɪneɪtɪŋ/| adj. v.|发芽的，萌芽的；处于萌芽状态的|beginning to grow and put out shoots after a period of dormancy; (of an idea, plan, etc.) beginning to develop|例句：The seeds are germinating. 种子正在发芽。|近义词：sprouting, budding, developing, growing|反义词：dormant, inactive, withering, decaying|词性:形容词

germinate:/ˈdʒɜːrmɪneɪt/| v.|发芽，萌芽；开始发展| (of a seed or spore) begin to grow and put out shoots after a period of dormancy; (of an idea, plan, etc.) begin to develop|例句：The beans will germinate quickly if soaked overnight. 豆子如果浸泡一夜会很快发芽。|近义词：sprout, bud, develop, grow|反义词：wither, decay, die|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English: from Latin germinat- ‘budded’, from the verb germinare, from germen ‘bud, sprout’.
