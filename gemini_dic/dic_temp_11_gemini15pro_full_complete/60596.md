elbowboard: /ˈɛlboʊˌbɔːrd/
noun
扶手板，肘托
A board or rest for the elbow.  A small writing desk with a sloping top and often a drawer, designed to be rested on the lap.
He used an elbowboard to steady his hand while writing. 他用扶手板稳定手写字。
She balanced the elbowboard on her knees and began to sketch. 她把扶手板放在膝盖上开始画素描。
Synonyms: armrest, writing slope, lap desk
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider) table, desk
词性: 名词
