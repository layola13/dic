gravies: /ˈɡreɪviz/
n.
肉汁; 肉汤; 调味汁
A thick sauce made from the juices of cooked meat, often flavored with onion, herbs, or wine.
The roast beef was served with rich brown gravy. 烤牛肉配上了浓郁的棕色肉汁。

近义词: sauce, jus
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 thin broth 清汤)
词性: 名词

gravy: /ˈɡreɪvi/
n.
意外之财; 非法所得; 轻松得来的钱
Money easily acquired.
They thought they could make some easy gravy from the deal. 他们认为他们可以从这笔交易中轻松获利。

近义词: windfall, bonus, easy money
反义词: loss, debt
词性: 名词
