conflictual: /kənˈflɪktʃuəl/ | adj. | 冲突的，矛盾的 | Characterized by conflict; involving or causing conflict. |  The conflictual relationship between the two countries has been ongoing for decades. 这两个国家之间冲突不断的关系已经持续了几十年了。

The team's conflictual dynamic hindered their progress. 团队中矛盾的动态阻碍了他们的进步。

| 近义词: antagonistic, contentious, hostile, discordant | 反义词: harmonious, peaceful, cooperative, agreeable | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *conflictuel*, from conflict + -uel (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Latin conflictus ("striking together, collision, conflict").
