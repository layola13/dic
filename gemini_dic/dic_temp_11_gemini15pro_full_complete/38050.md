compose:/kəmˈpəʊz/ (UK), /kəmˈpoʊz/ (US)| vt. & vi.|组成，构成；创作；使平静|To form by putting together; to create a musical, literary, or artistic work; to calm or quiet.|

例句：
He composed a symphony. 他创作了一部交响曲。

She composed herself before entering the room.  她在进入房间前让自己平静下来。


近义词：create, write, form, constitute, assemble, calm, soothe|
反义词：decompose, destroy, agitate, upset|
词性：verb|

compose:/kəmˈpəʊz/ (UK), /kəmˈpoʊz/ (US)| vt. & vi.|组成，构成；创作；使平静|Made up of;  created; calmed or quieted (when used as a past participle - composed).  This adjectival usage is less common than the verb form.|

例句：
The committee is composed of ten members. 委员会由十名成员组成。

He appeared calm and composed. 他显得平静而沉着。


近义词：(For "made up of") comprising, consisting of; (for "created") written, created; (for "calmed") tranquil, serene|
反义词：(For "made up of")  N/A; (for "created") unoriginal, derivative; (for "calmed") agitated, disturbed|
词性：verb (primarily), can function adjectivally as a past participle|
