fanon:/ˈfænən/| n.|同人设定，二次创作设定|A body of literature and/or art created by fans of a fictional universe, which expands the fictional universe and/or changes elements within the existing fictional universe. It is a derivative work, not considered official by the copyright holders of the original work.|例句：The fanon surrounding this character is extensive, with many fans creating their own backstories and relationships for them.  这个角色的同人设定非常广泛，许多粉丝为他们创造了自己的背景故事和人际关系。

例句：While the show never explicitly stated it, it's become accepted fanon that the two characters are siblings. 虽然这部剧从未明确说明，但这两个人物是兄弟姐妹的关系已经成为了被广泛接受的同人设定。|近义词：headcanon, fan-created lore|反义词：canon|词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From fan + canon.  "Fan" is shortened from "fanatic". "Canon" refers to the official, authoritative body of work.
