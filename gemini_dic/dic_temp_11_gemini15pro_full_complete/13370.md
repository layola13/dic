atmosphere:/ˈætməsfɪr/ (US) /ˈætməsfɪə/ (UK)| n.| 大气; 气氛; 环境; 空气|The Earth's atmosphere is a layer of gases surrounding our planet. 地球的大气层是环绕我们星球的气体层。|例句：The atmosphere in the cafe was warm and friendly. 咖啡馆里的气氛温暖而友好。|近义词：ambiance, aura, climate, mood, environment|反义词：vacuum|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *atmosphère*, from Ancient Greek *ἀτμός* (atmós, “vapor, steam”) + *σφαῖρα* (sphaîra, “sphere”).
