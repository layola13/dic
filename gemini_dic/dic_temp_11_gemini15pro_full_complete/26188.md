butlers:/ˈbʌtlərz/
n.
男管家（复数）
Male domestic workers, typically the chief manservant of a large household, responsible for supervising other staff, receiving guests, and attending to the needs of the employer.
The butlers served the guests champagne. 男管家们为客人们上了香槟。
近义词：manservants, stewards
反义词：maids, housekeepers
词性：名词
