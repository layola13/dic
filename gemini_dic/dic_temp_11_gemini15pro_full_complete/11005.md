arboriculture:/ˈɑːrbərɪkʌltʃər/
noun
树木栽培；树艺
The cultivation of trees and shrubs, especially for ornamental purposes.
例句：He studied arboriculture and became an expert in tree care. 他学习了树木栽培，成为树木护理专家。

例句：The city's arboriculture program has helped to beautify the streets.  城市的树木栽培计划帮助美化了街道。

近义词：tree cultivation, silviculture, forestry
反义词: N/A
词性: n.

Etymology: From Latin *arbor* (tree) + *cultura* (cultivation).
