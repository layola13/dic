词源:  Likely an alliterative elaboration of Greek *κύκλος (kúklos, “circle”). The Klan was originally called the "Kuklos Clan".  可能源自希腊语 *κύκλος (kúklos, “圆圈”) 的押头韵衍生词。三K党最初被称为“Kuklos Clan”。
