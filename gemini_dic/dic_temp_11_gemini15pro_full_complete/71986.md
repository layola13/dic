flinch:/flɪntʃ/ | vi. | 畏缩，退缩， flinch 通常指由于害怕疼痛或不愉快的事情而做出的快速、不由自主的退缩动作。| The horse flinched when the whip cracked. 马在鞭子抽打时畏缩了。| synonyms: wince, shrink, recoil, cower | antonyms:  brave, face, confront | verb

flinches:/ˈflɪntʃɪz/ | v. (third-person singular simple present) | 畏缩，退缩 | He flinches every time he hears a loud noise. 他每次听到 loud noise 都會畏縮。 | synonyms: winces, shrinks, recoils, cowers | antonyms: braves, faces, confronts | verb
