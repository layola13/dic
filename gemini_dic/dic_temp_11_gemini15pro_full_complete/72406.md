fluential: /fluˈɛnʃəl/
adjective
有影响力的；有势力的
Having the power to influence someone or something; effective.
Her influential work has inspired many young artists. 她的影响力很大的作品激励了许多年轻艺术家。

近义词: powerful, impactful, dominant, effective
反义词: powerless, ineffective, insignificant

词性: adjective
词源:  From Latin *fluentia, from fluens, present participle of fluere "to flow."  The original meaning related to the flowing or spreading of influence.
