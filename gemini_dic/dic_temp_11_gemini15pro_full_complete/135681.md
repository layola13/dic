nonprescribed:/ˌnɑːnprɪˈskraɪbd/ | /ˌnɒnprɪˈskraɪbd/
adjective
未开处方的，非处方药的
Not prescribed by a doctor or other medical professional.
Many nonprescribed medications are available at your local pharmacy.  许多非处方药在您当地的药店都可以买到。
Over-the-counter pain relievers are nonprescribed.  非处方止痛药无需处方。
synonym: over-the-counter, nonprescription
antonym: prescribed, prescription
词性: adjective
