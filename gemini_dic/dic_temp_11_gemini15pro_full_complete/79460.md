glitzing:/ˈɡlɪtsɪŋ/ /ˈɡlɪtsɪŋ/
verb
给…加上亮片;使闪耀
making something shiny or sparkly, often with glitter; giving something a glamorous and exciting appearance.
She was glitzing up her dress for the party. 她正在为派对装点她的裙子，加上了亮片。
The designer glitzed up the shoes with sequins.  设计师用亮片装饰鞋子，使之闪耀。
近义词:  sparkling, shimmering, glittering, glamorizing, bedazzling
反义词:  dulling, toning down, simplifying
词性: verb
