geothermally:/ˌdʒiːəˈθɜːrməli/ | adv. | 地热地；利用地热 | Using the heat from the Earth's interior. |  Iceland generates much of its electricity geothermally. 冰岛的大部分电力都是地热发电的。|  Synonym: volcanically, thermally | Antonym: conventionally | Part of Speech: Adverb

geothermal:/ˌdʒiːəˈθɜːrməl/ | adj. | 地热的 | Relating to or produced by the internal heat of the earth. | Geothermal energy is a renewable resource. 地热能是一种可再生资源。| Synonym: volcanic | Antonym: conventional | Part of Speech: Adjective
