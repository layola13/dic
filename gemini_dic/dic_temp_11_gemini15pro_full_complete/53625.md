discernable:/dɪˈsɜːrnəbl/ /dɪˈsɝːnəbl/| adj.|可辨别的，可识别的|Perceptible; distinguishable; noticeable. discern:/dɪˈsɜːrn/ /dɪˈsɝːn/| v.|辨别，识别|To perceive or recognize something, typically something that is not obvious.|I could just discern a figure in the darkness. 我只能依稀辨认出黑暗中的一个人影。|Synonyms: perceive, recognize, detect, distinguish, make out, spot, notice|Antonyms: overlook, miss|verb
