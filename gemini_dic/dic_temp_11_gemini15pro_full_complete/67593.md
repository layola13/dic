distraught:/dɪˈstrɔːt/
adjective
极度痛苦的，心烦意乱的
Extremely worried, nervous, or upset.
She was distraught with grief after her husband's death. 她丈夫去世后，她悲痛欲绝。
近义词: distressed, agitated, frantic, upset
反义词: calm, composed, serene
词性: adjective

/dɪˈstrɔːt/
adjective
极度痛苦的，心烦意乱的
So upset and worried that you cannot think clearly.
He was utterly distraught over the loss of his job. 他对失去工作感到十分沮丧。
近义词: desperate, hysterical, overcome
反义词: calm, collected, composed
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *distraught*, past participle of *distraughen*, *distracten* “to distract,” from Latin *distractus*, past participle of *distrahere* "to pull apart, divide, distract," from *dis-* "apart" + *trahere* "to draw, pull".
