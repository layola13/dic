bastion:/ˈbæstʃən/ | n. |堡垒，防御工事；(比喻)捍卫者，堡垒|a projecting part of a fortification built at an angle to the line of a wall, so as to allow defensive fire in several directions. (A fortified place.)  A person, group, or principle strongly defending or upholding something. |例句：The old castle stood as a bastion against the invaders.  这座古老的城堡像堡垒一样抵御侵略者。

例句：He was the last bastion of freedom of speech in that country. 他是那个国家言论自由的最后堡垒。|近义词：fortress, stronghold, bulwark, citadel, rampart, defender, advocate, champion |反义词: vulnerability, weakness |词性:noun
