indispensability:/ˌɪndɪspɛnsəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可或缺，必需性 | The quality of being absolutely necessary or essential. |  Her indispensability to the team was evident when they lost the match without her. 她对团队的不可或缺性在他们没有她输掉比赛时就显而易见了。| synonyms:  essentiality, necessity, cruciality, vitalness, importance  | antonyms: dispensability, nonessentiality, unimportance, triviality | noun
