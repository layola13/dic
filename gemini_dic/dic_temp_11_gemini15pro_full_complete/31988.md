chemotherapeutic:/ˌkiːmoʊˌθɛrəˈpjuːtɪk/ | adj. | 化疗的；化学疗法的 | Relating to or used in chemotherapy; a drug used in chemotherapy. |  Chemotherapeutic drugs are used to treat cancer. 化疗药物用于治疗癌症。| The doctor prescribed a chemotherapeutic regimen for the patient. 医生为病人开了化疗方案。|Synonyms: antineoplastic, cytotoxic | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider "palliative" or "supportive" in certain contexts) | adjective


chemotherapeutic: /ˌkiːmoʊˌθɛrəˈpjuːtɪk/ | n. | 化疗药物 | A drug used in chemotherapy. | The new chemotherapeutic showed promising results in clinical trials. 这种新的化疗药物在临床试验中显示出良好的效果。| Several chemotherapeutics were combined to target the specific cancer cells. 几种化疗药物被联合使用以靶向特定的癌细胞。 | Synonyms:  antineoplastic agent, cytotoxic drug | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym) | noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From "chemo-" (chemical) + "therapeutic" (having healing power).  "Chemo-" derives from Greek "khemeia" (alchemy), and "therapeutic" derives from Greek "therapeutikos" (healing).
