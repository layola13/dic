geissler:/ˈɡaɪslər/ | n. | 盖斯勒管 | A type of gas-discharge tube used to demonstrate the principles of electrical glow discharge. | The physics teacher used a Geissler tube to show the students how neon glows. 物理老师用盖斯勒管向学生展示氖气是如何发光的。 | 近义词: discharge tube, vacuum tube | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists) | 词性: noun

Etymology: Named after Heinrich Geissler (1814-1879), a German glassblower and physicist who invented it. 源于德国玻璃吹制工和物理学家海因里希·盖斯勒(1814-1879)的名字，他发明了这种管子。
