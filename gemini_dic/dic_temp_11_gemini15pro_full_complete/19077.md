betrayals:/bɪˈtreɪəlz/| n.|背叛，辜负|The act of betraying someone or something; an act of disloyalty or treachery. |His betrayals of his friends shocked everyone. 他的背叛让他的朋友们都震惊了。|近义词：treacheries, disloyalties, perfidies|反义词：loyalties, fidelities|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *betrayal, from Anglo-French betrayal, from Old French *betraial, from *betrair (“to betray”), from be- + trair (“to hand over, deliver”), from Latin trādere (“to hand over, deliver”).
