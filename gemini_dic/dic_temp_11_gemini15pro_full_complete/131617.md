noncomposer:/ˌnɒnkəmˈpəʊzər/ /ˌnɑːnkəmˈpoʊzər/
noun
非作曲家
a person who does not compose music
He's a talented musician, but a noncomposer; he focuses on performance. 他是一位才华横溢的音乐家，但不是作曲家；他专注于演奏。
non-musician, performer
composer, songwriter
