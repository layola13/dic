disengage:/ˌdɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒ/ | v. |脱离，解开，退出，摆脱 |To release or detach (something); to become free from something. |  The soldiers disengaged from the enemy. 士兵们脱离了敌人。

The gears disengaged, causing the car to stall. 齿轮脱开，导致汽车熄火。

| 近义词：detach, disconnect, withdraw, extricate, release | 反义词：engage, connect, attach, involve | 词性: 动词
