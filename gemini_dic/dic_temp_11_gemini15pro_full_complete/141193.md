oompah:/ˈuːmpɑː/ | adj. |  （尤指德国铜管乐队音乐）节奏强劲的 | (especially of German band music) having a strong rhythm | The oompah band played a lively tune.  这支铜管乐队演奏了一首活泼的曲子。 | Synonym: rhythmic, upbeat | Antonym: mellow, slow | adjective
