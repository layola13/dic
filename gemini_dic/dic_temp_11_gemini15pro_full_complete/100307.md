intersite:/ˈɪntərˌsaɪt/| adj.|站点间的，不同站点间的|Existing or occurring between different sites. |例句：Intersite communication is crucial for a distributed team. 不同站点间的沟通对分布式团队至关重要。|例句：The intersite data transfer was slow due to network congestion. 由于网络拥堵，站点间的数据传输很慢。|近义词：between sites, multi-site|反义词：intrasite, onsite|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From inter- (prefix meaning "between") + site (noun meaning "a place").
