haematolin:/ˌhiːməˈtoʊlɪn/ | n. |苏木精，苏木色素| A dark red crystalline compound obtained from the heartwood of the logwood tree (Haematoxylum campechianum), used as a biological stain and dye.  It stains cell nuclei blue-violet. | The pathologist used haematoxylin and eosin to stain the tissue sample. 病理学家用苏木精和伊红来染色组织样本。| Synonyms: hematoxylin | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
