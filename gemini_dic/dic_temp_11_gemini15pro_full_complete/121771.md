misraise:/ˌmɪsˈreɪz/
verb
错误地加注;加注过低
to bet less than the required minimum amount in poker.  In some contexts, it can also mean to raise inappropriately or at the wrong time.
He misraised, putting in only $10 when the minimum bet was $20. 他加注错误，最低赌注是 20 美元时只下了 10 美元。

He misraised pre-flop with a weak hand. 他在翻牌前用一手弱牌错误地加注。
misdeal, underbet
overbet, overraise
