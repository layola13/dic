nymphiparous: /ˌnɪmfɪˈpærəs/
adjective
蛹生的；若虫产的
(of an insect) giving birth to nymphs or pupae, rather than eggs.  Developing in or attached to the parent.
例句：
The tsetse fly is a nymphiparous insect.  采采蝇是一种蛹生昆虫。

The ked is a nymphiparous parasite of sheep. 羊虱蝇是一种蛹生的绵羊寄生虫。

近义词: pupiparous
反义词: oviparous (卵生的)
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From Ancient Greek νύμφη (númphē, “bride, nymph, young woman”) +‎ -parous.
