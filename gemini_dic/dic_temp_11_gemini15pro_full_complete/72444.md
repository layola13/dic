fluked:/fluːkt/
adjective
侥幸的；意外的
Achieved or accomplished by chance rather than skill or planning.
He fluked a victory in the final seconds of the game. 他在比赛的最后几秒侥幸获胜。
Synonyms: lucky, fortunate, accidental, serendipitous
Antonyms: planned, intentional, deliberate
词性:形容词


fluked:/fluːkt/
verb
(fluke的过去式和过去分词)
侥幸成功；意外做到
To achieve something by luck or chance.
I fluked the exam. 我侥幸通过了考试。
Synonyms: chance, luck into, stumble upon
Antonyms: earn, achieve through effort
词性:动词
