disputation:/ˌdɪspjuˈteɪʃən/| n.|争论，辩论，论战|A formal academic debate or argument.|The students engaged in a lively disputation about the merits of different philosophical theories. 学生们就不同哲学理论的优点进行了激烈的辩论。|synonyms: argument, debate, controversy, contention, quarrel |antonyms: agreement, harmony, concord, accord|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French disputacion, from Latin disputatio, from disputare ‘discuss, argue’.
