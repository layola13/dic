forestallers:/fɔːrˈstɔːlərz/
| n. | 预防者，抢先者 | People or things that forestall something; those who prevent something from happening by acting first. | The forestallers secured the best seats in the theater before anyone else arrived.  剧院的抢先者在其他人到达之前就获得了最好的座位。 |  近义词：preventers, anticipators | 反义词: followers, reactors | noun


forestall:/fɔːrˈstɔːl/
| vt. | 预防，阻止，先发制人 | To prevent or obstruct (an anticipated event or action) by taking action ahead of time. |  They forestalled the riot by increasing police presence. 他们通过增加警力来防止骚乱。 | 近义词：prevent, avert, preclude, obviate | 反义词: allow, permit, encourage | verb
