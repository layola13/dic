expanding:/ɪkˈspændɪŋ/ | adj. | 扩张的，扩大的 | Increasing in size, range, or amount. | The company is expanding its operations into new markets. 公司正在将业务扩展到新的市场。|  The expanding universe is a fascinating concept. 不断膨胀的宇宙是一个引人入胜的概念。 | 近义词: growing, increasing, enlarging, widening, developing, stretching, spreading | 反义词: shrinking, contracting, decreasing, diminishing | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Late Latin *expandens, present participle of expandere "to spread out," from ex- "out" + pandere "to spread."
