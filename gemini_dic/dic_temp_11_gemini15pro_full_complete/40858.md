cooingly:/ˈkuːɪŋli/| adv.|温柔地；亲昵地；|In a soft, gentle, and loving way, often used to describe the sound of doves or the way someone speaks to a loved one.|She cooingly whispered sweet nothings in his ear. 她在他耳边柔声细语地说着情话。|近义词：tenderly, lovingly, affectionately, softly|反义词：harshly, coldly, roughly|词性:副词

词源: From coo (verb) + -ing (present participle suffix) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from the imitative verb "coo."
