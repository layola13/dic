fanaticalnesses:/fəˈnætɪkəlnəsɪz/
n.
狂热；极端；入迷
The state or quality of being fanatical; excessive enthusiasm or zeal.
His fanaticalnesses about cleanliness borders on obsessive-compulsive disorder. 他对清洁的狂热近乎强迫症。
近义词: fanaticism, extremism, zealotry
反义词: apathy, indifference, moderation
词性: 名词

fanatical:/fəˈnætɪkəl/
adj.
狂热的；极端的
Characterized by excessive enthusiasm and often intense uncritical devotion or zeal.
He's fanatical about football. 他对足球很狂热。
近义词: obsessive, zealous, fervent
反义词: moderate, apathetic, indifferent
词性: 形容词

fanatic:/fəˈnætɪk/
n.
狂热者；极端分子
A person filled with excessive and single-minded enthusiasm for something, especially religion or politics.
He's a real sports fanatic. 他是一个真正的体育迷。
近义词: zealot, extremist, enthusiast
反义词: moderate
词性: 名词


词源:
From Late Latin *fanaticus "inspired by a deity, frenzied," from Latin fanum "temple," related to fas "divine law."  The sense evolution was from "inspired by a god" to "possessed, frantic, mad."  The noun fanatic is from 1550s, and the adjective fanatical is from 1640s. The plural noun "fanaticalnesses" is less common than the singular "fanaticism."
