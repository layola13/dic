homocategoric:/ˌhɒməʊˌkætəˈɡɒrɪk/ | adj. | 同类的，同范畴的 | Belonging to the same category or class.  Sharing the same categorical properties or characteristics. | Example 1: The two species are homocategoric, sharing similar genetic makeup and ecological niches. 这两个物种是同类的，具有相似的基因组成和生态位。

Example 2: The linguist argued that the two dialects were homocategoric, despite minor differences in pronunciation.  这位语言学家认为，尽管发音略有不同，但这两种方言是同类的。| 近义词: congeneric, cognate, similar | 反义词: heterocategoric, different, dissimilar | 词性: adjective
