outsighing:/ˌaʊtˈsaɪɪŋ/
verb
叹息着出去; 叹息着离开
The act of sighing while going out or leaving.
She was outsighing the room, clearly upset. 她叹息着离开了房间，显然很沮丧。
He outsighed his way through the door. 他叹息着走出了门。
Synonyms: sighing, lamenting, grieving
Antonyms: rejoicing, celebrating
