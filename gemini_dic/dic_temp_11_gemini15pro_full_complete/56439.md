dolls:/dɑːlz/ (US), /dɒlz/ (UK) | n. |玩偶，玩具娃娃|A small figure of a human being, especially one used as a child's toy.|She loved playing with her dolls as a child. 她小时候喜欢玩她的洋娃娃。|toys, figurines, action figures | - | noun

Etymology:
From Old French *dole, hypocoristic of the name *Doll, a pet form of Dorothy. Ultimately from Greek Δωροθέα (Dōrothéa), meaning "gift of God."
