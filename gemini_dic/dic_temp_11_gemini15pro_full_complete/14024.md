auscultate:/ˈɔːskəlˌteɪt/| v. |听诊|To listen to sounds within the body, typically with a stethoscope, as a method of diagnosis.|The doctor auscultated the patient's chest. 医生听诊了病人的胸部。|Synonyms: listen, examine, monitor|Antonyms: ignore, disregard|verb

auscultation:/ˌɔːskəlˈteɪʃən/| n. |听诊|The act of listening to sounds within the body, typically with a stethoscope, as a method of diagnosis.|Auscultation of the heart revealed a murmur. 心脏听诊发现杂音。|Synonyms: listening, examination, monitoring|Antonyms: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *auscultāre*  "to listen to," frequentative of *auscultare* "to listen," from *auris* "ear."
