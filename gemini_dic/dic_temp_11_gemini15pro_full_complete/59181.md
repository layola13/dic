dyslexic:/dɪsˈlɛksɪk/ | adj. |诵读困难的；患诵读困难症的| Relating to or affected by dyslexia; having unusual difficulty with reading and spelling. | Example: He is dyslexic and struggles with reading. 他有阅读障碍，阅读很吃力。

Example:  Schools are now much more aware of the needs of dyslexic children. 学校现在更加关注诵读困难儿童的需求。


dyslexia: /dɪsˈlɛksiə/ | n. |阅读障碍；诵读困难|A general term for disorders that involve difficulty in learning to read or interpret words, letters, and other symbols, but that do not affect general intelligence.| Example:  Her son has dyslexia. 她儿子有阅读障碍。

Example: Early diagnosis of dyslexia is crucial for effective intervention.  早期诊断阅读障碍对于有效干预至关重要。


近义词: learning-disabled | 反义词: n/a | 词性: adjective (dyslexic), noun (dyslexia)
