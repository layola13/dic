edified:/ˈɛdɪfaɪd/
adjective
受到教化的；道德/精神上得到提升的
Morally or intellectually improved.
例句：I felt edified after attending the lecture on philosophy.  参加完哲学讲座后，我感到受到了教化。

例句：She was edified by the stories of the saints. 她被圣徒的故事所启迪。

近义词：enlightened, uplifted, inspired, improved
反义词：degraded, corrupted, demoralized
词性：adjective

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *edifien*, from Old French *edifier*, from Latin *aedificāre* (“to build”), from *aedēs* (“house, temple”) + *facere* (“to make, do”).  The original sense of "to build" later shifted to "to build up morally or intellectually."
