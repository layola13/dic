outstruck: /ˌaʊtˈstrʌk/
词性: verb (past tense of outstrike)
中文翻译: 比...打得更厉害; 超越;胜过 (主要用于棒球)
英文解析: To strike out (a batter) more times in a game than another pitcher.  It also carries a more general meaning of exceeding or surpassing someone in a competitive action, particularly one involving striking or hitting.
例句:
Johnson outstruck Martinez, fanning 12 batters. 约翰逊比马丁内斯的三振次数更多，三振了12名击球手。

The young prodigy outstruck his mentor in the final round of the competition.  这位年轻的神童在比赛的最后一轮胜过了他的导师。

近义词: outpitched, surpassed, exceeded, bested, triumphed over
反义词:  was outstruck by, lost to, was surpassed by
词性: verb
