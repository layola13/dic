capitalization: /ˌkæpɪtələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 大写；资本化；资本总额 | The act or process of capitalizing.  The use of capital letters. | Example: Correct capitalization is important in formal writing. 正确使用大写字母在正式写作中很重要。

Example: The capitalization of the company increased significantly after the IPO. 公司的资本总额在首次公开募股后显著增加。

近义词: uppercasing | 反义词: lowercasing | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *capitalisatio, from Latin capitalis ("of the head," hence "chief, principal"), from caput ("head").
