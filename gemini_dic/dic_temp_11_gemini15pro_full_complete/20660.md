blacksmithing:/ˈblækˌsmɪθɪŋ/
n.|铁匠工艺；锻造|The art or craft of a blacksmith.  The process of forging and shaping metal, especially iron, using a forge, hammer, and anvil.| Blacksmithing is a traditional craft that requires skill and strength. 铁匠工艺是一项需要技巧和力量的传统工艺。| 近义词:forging, metalworking|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

Etymology:
From black + smith + -ing.  "Black" likely refers to the black color of the metal scale formed during heating and working, or possibly the soot from the forge. "Smith" comes from Old English "smiþ," meaning "worker in metal." "-ing" is a suffix forming a noun of action.
