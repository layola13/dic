otologist:/ˌoʊtɒlədʒɪst/ | n. |耳科专家；耳科医生 | A doctor who specializes in the ear and its diseases. |  例句：The otologist diagnosed an ear infection. 耳科医生诊断出耳朵感染。

例句：I was referred to an otologist for my hearing loss.  因为我的听力损失，我被转诊到了一位耳科医生那里。

近义词：ear specialist, ear doctor | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From oto- + -logist.  "Oto-" comes from the Greek word "ous" (ὠτός), meaning "ear." "-logist" signifies "one who studies" or "specialist."
