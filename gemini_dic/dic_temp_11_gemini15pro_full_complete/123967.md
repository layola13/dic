morisco:/məˈrɪskoʊ/| adj. n.|摩尔人的，摩尔式艺术（或建筑）的；摩里斯科人|Relating to the Moors or their culture, especially after their expulsion from Spain.|The intricate patterns on the tiles are characteristic of Morisco art.  这些瓷砖上复杂的图案是摩尔式艺术的特征。|The Moriscos were forced to convert to Christianity. 摩里斯科人被迫皈依基督教。|近义词：Moorish, Moresque|反义词:Christian|词性:形容词, 名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *morisco*, from *moro* ("Moor"), from Latin *Maurus* ("inhabitant of Mauretania"), ultimately from Greek *Mauros*.
