noddle:/ˈnɒdl/ (British) /ˈnɑːdl/ (American)| n.| 头；脑袋；理解力|The act of nodding one's head slightly. Often used informally or humorously to refer to the head or mind.|He gave a slight noddle of agreement. 他微微点头表示同意。|Synonyms: head, noggin, pate, bean (informal)|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms for the physical head. For "mind" or "intellect," antonyms could be ignorance, stupidity.)|noun


noddle:/ˈnɒdl/ (British) /ˈnɑːdl/ (American)| v.| 点（头）；思考|To move one's head slightly up and down, typically to indicate agreement, understanding, or greeting; to think or consider something.|She nodded her head in agreement. 她点头表示同意。  He sat nodding over his book. 他坐着边点头边看书。|Synonyms: nod, bow, incline (head), ponder, consider|Antonyms: shake (head), disagree, reject|verb
