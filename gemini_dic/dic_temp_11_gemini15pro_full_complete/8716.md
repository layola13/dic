anticultures:/ˈæntiˌkʌltʃərz/
noun
反文化，对抗文化
Groups or movements that actively oppose or reject mainstream culture and its values.
The punk rock movement was seen as one of the most prominent anticultures of the 20th century.  20世纪的朋克摇滚运动被视为最著名的反文化之一。
Anticultures often challenge societal norms and propose alternative ways of living. 反文化通常挑战社会规范并提出其他的生活方式。
Synonyms: counterculture, subculture
Antonyms: mainstream culture, dominant culture
