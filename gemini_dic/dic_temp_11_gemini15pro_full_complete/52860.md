dimetric:/daɪˈmɛtrɪk/
adjective
二聚体的；等轴的
Having two similar parts or occurring in two forms; relating to or characterized by a crystal system having three axes of equal length, two of which intersect at oblique angles and the third of which is perpendicular to the plane of the other two.
The dimetric projection is often used in technical drawings.  二测投影常用于技术图纸。
近义词: dimerous, isomorphic (in some contexts)
反义词: trimetric, isometric
词性: adjective


dimetric:/daɪˈmɛtrɪk/
noun
二聚体
A chemical compound composed of two identical or similar subunits or monomers.
This reaction results in the formation of a dimetric.  这个反应导致了二聚体的形成。
近义词: dimer
反义词: monomer, polymer
词性: noun
