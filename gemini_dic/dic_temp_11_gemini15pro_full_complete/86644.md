hellbred:/ˈhɛlˌbrɛd/| adj.|地狱之子；地狱般的；凶恶的|Bred in hell; fiendishly evil or cruel.  Having a cruel or wicked nature, as if originating in hell.|例句：The hellbred monster terrorized the village.  地狱之子般的怪物恐吓着整个村庄。|近义词：fiendish, demonic, infernal, hellish, devilish|反义词：angelic, divine, heavenly|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "hell" + "bred" (past participle of "breed").  Meaning "bred or produced in hell".
