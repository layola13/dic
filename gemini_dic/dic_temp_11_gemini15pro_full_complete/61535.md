embryonic:/ˌɛmbrɪˈɒnɪk/ /ˌɛmbriˈɑːnɪk/| adj.|胚胎的，萌芽期的，未成熟的|Relating to an embryo; undeveloped, rudimentary.|例句：The project is still in its embryonic stage.  这个项目仍处于萌芽阶段。|例句：The embryonic development of the bird was fascinating to observe. 观察鸟类的胚胎发育过程非常吸引人。|近义词：undeveloped, rudimentary, nascent, incipient|反义词：developed, mature, advanced|词性:形容词

embryo:/ˈɛmbriəʊ/ /ˈɛmbrioʊ/| n.|胚胎，萌芽|An unborn or unhatched offspring in the process of development, especially in the early stages; a thing at a rudimentary or early stage of development.|例句：The embryo develops inside the mother's womb. 胚胎在母体子宫内发育。|例句：The idea was still just an embryo. 这个想法还只是一个萌芽。|近义词：germ, seed, beginning|反义词：adult, mature|词性:名词
