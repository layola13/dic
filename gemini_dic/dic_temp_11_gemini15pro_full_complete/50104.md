dentile:/ˈdɛnˌtaɪl/
noun
齿状装饰，齿饰
A small block, typically rectangular, used as a repeating ornament in a cornice, resembling teeth.
The dentile molding added a touch of elegance to the room.  齿饰线脚为房间增添了一丝优雅。
synonyms: tooth-like projection, dentil, toothing
antonyms: (No direct antonym, possibly "smooth" if referring to the opposite architectural style)
