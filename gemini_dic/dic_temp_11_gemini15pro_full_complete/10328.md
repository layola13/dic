appear:/əˈpɪr/ (US), /əˈpɪə/ (UK)| vi. |出现，显现，看来，似乎|to become visible or noticeable; to seem|The ship appeared on the horizon. 这艘船出现在地平线上。|近义词：emerge, show, seem, look|反义词:disappear, vanish|verb

appear:/əˈpɪr/ (US), /əˈpɪə/ (UK)| vi. |出版，发表|to be published|His new novel will appear next month. 他的新小说下个月出版。|近义词：publish, release|反义词: - |verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *apareir, *aparoir, from Latin apparēre "to appear, become visible, come in sight," from ad- "to" + parēre "to come in sight, be visible, obey, show obedience" (from PIE root *pere- (1) "to produce, procure").
