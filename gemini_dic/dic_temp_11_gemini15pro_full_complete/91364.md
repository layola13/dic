hydrosphere:/ˈhaɪdrəsfɪr/ | n. | 水圈 | All the waters on the earth's surface, such as lakes and seas, and sometimes including water over the earth's surface, such as clouds. | The Earth's hydrosphere is constantly changing due to evaporation and precipitation. 地球的水圈由于蒸发和降水而不断变化。| The hydrosphere plays a critical role in regulating Earth's temperature. 水圈在地球温度调节中起着至关重要的作用。| 近义词:water sphere | 反义词:lithosphere, atmosphere | 词性:noun
