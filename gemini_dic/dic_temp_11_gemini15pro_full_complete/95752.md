inconsonance:/ˌɪnkənˈsɒnəns/ /ˌɪnkənˈsɑːnəns/| n. |不和谐，不一致，不协调|lack of harmony or agreement; discord|The architect felt the new extension was in inconsonance with the original building's style. 建筑师觉得新的扩建部分与原建筑的风格不协调。|His aggressive behavior is in inconsonance with his usually calm demeanor. 他好斗的行为与他平常冷静的举止不一致。|近义词：discord, disharmony, conflict, disagreement, clash|反义词：consonance, harmony, agreement, accord, concord|词性:名词
