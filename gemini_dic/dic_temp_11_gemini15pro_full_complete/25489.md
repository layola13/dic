bullfinch:/ˈbʊlfɪntʃ/ | n. |红腹灰雀 |A Eurasian finch (Pyrrhula pyrrhula) with a short thick bill and gray and black plumage with a white rump and wing bars. The male has a rose-red breast and cheeks. |例句：The bullfinch perched on the branch and began to sing. 红腹灰雀栖息在树枝上开始唱歌。|例句：We saw a bullfinch cracking seeds in the garden. 我们看到一只红腹灰雀在花园里嗑种子。| 近义词：finch, bird | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific bird) | 词性:noun
