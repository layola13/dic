cosponsorships:/koʊˈspɑːnsərʃɪps/
n.
共同赞助，联合赞助
The act of sponsoring something together with one or more other individuals or organizations.
The conference benefited greatly from the cosponsorships of several local businesses.  这次会议得益于几家当地企业的共同赞助。
The event was a success thanks to the cosponsorships of several organizations.  由于几个组织的共同赞助，这次活动取得了成功。

Synonyms: joint sponsorships, shared sponsorships, collaborative sponsorships
Antonyms: sole sponsorship, independent sponsorship
