devolutionists:/ˌdiːvəˈluːʃənɪsts/
n.
权力下放主义者；地方分权主义者
People who advocate for devolution, the transfer of power from a central government to regional or local governments.
The devolutionists argued for greater autonomy for Scotland.  权力下放主义者主张苏格兰拥有更大的自治权。
Devolutionists believe that local governments are better equipped to handle local issues. 地方分权主义者认为地方政府更有能力处理地方问题。
近义词:federalists, autonomists
反义词:centralists, unionists
词性:noun
