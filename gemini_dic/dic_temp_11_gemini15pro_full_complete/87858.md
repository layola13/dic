hideously:/ˈhɪdiəsli/ | adv. |可怕地；丑陋地；讨厌地|in a hideous manner; terribly; extremely unpleasantly|例句：The building was hideously ugly. 这栋楼丑得可怕。|例句：She was hideously embarrassed. 她尴尬极了。|近义词：frightfully, awfully, terribly, grotesquely, repulsively|反义词：beautifully, attractively, pleasantly|词性:副词
词源: From Middle English *hideousli, from hideous +‎ -ly.
