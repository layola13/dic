camels:/ˈkæməlz/| n. |骆驼|A large, long-necked ungulate mammal of arid regions of Africa and Asia, having either one or two humps on its back and adapted to desert conditions. It is used as a beast of burden and for providing milk, meat, and wool.|We saw a caravan of camels crossing the desert. 我们看到一队骆驼穿越沙漠。|
synonyms: dromedary (single-humped camel), Bactrian camel (two-humped camel)|antonyms: N/A|noun
