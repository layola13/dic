infinitesimal:/ˌɪnfɪnɪˈtɛsɪməl/| n. adj.|无穷小；极小；无限小|An infinitesimal is an indefinitely small quantity; a value approaching zero.|The effect of the change was infinitesimal. 变化的影响微乎其微。|synonyms: minute, tiny, microscopic, negligible|antonyms: huge, enormous, vast, immense|noun, adjective
