foliate:/ˈfoʊliˌeɪt/ | adj. v. | 叶状的；生叶的；使成叶状；加叶饰 | Having leaves; producing leaves; decorated with leaf-like ornamentation. |  The artist foliated the sculpture with intricate bronze leaves. 艺术家在雕塑上加上了复杂的青铜叶子装饰。 | Synonyms: leafy, verdant, frondose | Antonyms: leafless, bare, barren | Adjective

foliate:/ˈfoʊliˌeɪt/ | v. | 生叶； foliated, foliating | To produce leaves; to decorate with leaf-like ornaments.  | The tree foliated in the spring.  这棵树在春天长出了叶子。 | Synonyms: leaf, sprout, burgeon | Antonyms: defoliate, shed | Verb

From Late Latin *foliātus, past participle of foliāre (“to produce leaves”), from Latin folium (“leaf”).
