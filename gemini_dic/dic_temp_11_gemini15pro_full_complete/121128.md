misdeeming:/ˌmɪsdɪˈmiːmɪŋ/
|n.|判断错误；误判|The act of judging wrongly or having a wrong opinion about something.|His misdeeming of her character led to a great misunderstanding. 他对她性格的误判导致了很大的误会。|近义词：misjudgment, misinterpretation, misconception|反义词：correct judgment, accurate assessment|词性:名词
Etymology: From Middle English *misdemen*, from Old English *misdēman*, from *mis-* (wrongly) + *dēman* (to judge).
