incubator:/ˈɪŋkjʊbeɪtər/| n. |孵化器；保育箱；创业孵化器|An apparatus used to hatch eggs or grow microorganisms under controlled conditions.  A company that helps startup companies to develop by providing services such as management training or office space.|The hospital has a special incubator for premature babies.  这家医院有一个专门的早产儿保育箱。|Synonyms: hatchery, breeder, nurturer |Antonyms:  destroyer, extinguisher|noun

incubator:/ˈɪŋkjʊbeɪtər/| n. |培养皿;温床|An environment that encourages the development of something, especially something new or unusual|Silicon Valley is an incubator for tech startups. 硅谷是科技创业公司的温床。|Synonyms: breeding ground, hotbed, seedbed|Antonyms: impediment, obstacle|noun
