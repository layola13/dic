conciliabule:/kənˈsɪliəˌbjuːl/ /kənˈsɪliəˌbuːl/
noun
秘密会议，密谋
a secret meeting of a group of people for discussion or planning, especially for political or illegal purposes.
The conspirators held a conciliabule in the dead of night to plot their next move.  密谋者在夜深人静时举行了一次秘密会议，策划他们的下一步行动。
synonyms: cabal, conspiracy, intrigue, plot
antonyms: open forum, public meeting
词性: 名词
