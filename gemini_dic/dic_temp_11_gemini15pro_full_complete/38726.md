conditionality:/ˌkɒndɪʃəˈnælɪti/ | n. | 条件性; 附带条件 | The conditionality of the loan requires the recipient to implement certain reforms.  这笔贷款的条件是接受者必须实施某些改革。 | Example: The agreement's conditionality includes strict environmental safeguards.  该协议的条件包括严格的环境保障措施。| Synonym: stipulation, provision, requirement, contingency | Antonym: unconditionality, absoluteness | noun

conditionality:/ˌkɑːndɪʃəˈnælɪti/ | n. | 条件性; 附带条件 | The IMF often imposes conditionality on loans to developing countries. 国际货币基金组织经常对发展中国家的贷款附加条件。 | Example:  The aid comes with strict conditionalities attached. 这项援助附带严格的条件。 | Synonym: prerequisite, string, rider, proviso | Antonym: unconditionality,  absoluteness | noun
