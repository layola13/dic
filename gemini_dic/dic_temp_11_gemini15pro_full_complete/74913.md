fret-away:/frɛt əˈweɪ/
verb
消磨；焦虑不安地度过
to gradually wear something away, especially by rubbing or gnawing. To cause someone to be constantly worried or anxious, leading to physical or emotional decline.
She fretted away the hours worrying about her son. 她焦虑不安地度过了几个小时，担心着她的儿子。
The constant dripping of water had fretted away the stone.  不断滴落的水把石头磨损了。
synonyms: worry, erode, wear away, consume, corrode
antonyms: soothe, calm, build up, strengthen, comfort
词性: 动词
