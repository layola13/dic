acceptablenesses:/əkˌsɛptəˈbɪlnəsɪz/
n.
可接受性（复数）
the quality or state of being acceptable; the extent to which something is acceptable
例句：The acceptablenesses of the different proposals were debated. 不同提案的可接受性受到了辩论。

例句：We are evaluating the acceptablenesses of various solutions. 我们正在评估各种解决方案的可接受性。


近义词：acceptability, suitability, appropriateness
反义词：unacceptability, unsuitability, inappropriateness
词性: 名词
