habitualize:/həˈbɪtʃuəlaɪz/
verb
使...成为习惯；习惯于
to make something a habit; to become used to something
He tried to habitualize the practice of writing every day. 他试图养成每天写作的习惯。
She had habitualized herself to waking up early. 她已经习惯了早起。
synonyms: accustom, inure
antonyms: break, discontinue
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From habit + -ual + -ize.  From Latin habitus (“condition, appearance, dress, bearing, character”).
