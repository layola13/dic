coast's:/koʊsts/
noun
海岸的
Possessive form of "coast," indicating ownership or belonging related to a coast.
The coast's beauty captivated the tourists. 海岸的美丽吸引了游客。
The coast's erosion is a serious problem. 海岸的侵蚀是一个严重的问题。
Synonym: shoreline's, seaside's
Antonym: inland's
