bacteriology:/ˌbækˌtɪəriˈɒlədʒi/ /ˌbækˌtɪriˈɑːlədʒi/
noun
细菌学
The study of bacteria, especially in relation to medicine and agriculture.
Bacteriology is a crucial field in understanding and combating infectious diseases. 细菌学是理解和对抗传染病的关键领域。
The hospital's bacteriology lab is investigating the outbreak. 医院的细菌学实验室正在调查疫情爆发。
