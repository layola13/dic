hetic: /ˈhɛtɪk/ | adj. | 忙乱的，紧张的 | Characterized by intense activity, confusion, or excitement. |  The atmosphere in the emergency room was hectic. 急诊室里气氛很紧张。


The days leading up to the wedding were hectic. 婚礼前的日子非常忙碌。


近义词: frantic, frenzied, chaotic, turbulent
反义词: calm, peaceful, tranquil, serene
词性: adjective


词源:  From Ancient Greek ἕκτικός (hektikós, “habitual, customary”), from ἕξις (héxis, “state, condition, habit”).  Originally referred to a continuous fever associated with tuberculosis, later broadened to describe any state of agitated activity.
