languor:/ˈlæŋɡər/ | n. | 慵懒，倦怠，无力 |A state of physical weakness or listlessness. |  She felt a pleasant languor steal over her as she lay in the sun. 她躺在阳光下感到一阵惬意的慵懒袭来。

|Synonyms: lethargy, lassitude, listlessness, inertia, torpor, sluggishness|Antonyms: energy, vigor, vitality, liveliness, activity|Noun
