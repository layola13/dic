epigraph: /ˈepɪɡræf/ | n. |题词；碑文；引语|A short quotation or saying at the beginning of a book or chapter, intended to suggest its theme.|The epigraph to the novel was a line from Shakespeare. 这部小说的题词是一句莎士比亚的名言。|Synonyms: quotation, inscription, motto|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms exist for epigraph)|noun
