bayoneted:/ˌbeɪəˈnɛtɪd/
verb
用刺刀刺；用刺刀杀死
stabbed or killed with a bayonet
The soldier bayoneted the enemy.  这个士兵用刺刀刺杀了敌人。

近义词:stabbed, pierced, impaled
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "spared," "protected")
词性:Verb (past tense and past participle)


bayonet:/ˈbeɪənɪt/
noun
刺刀
a blade that can be attached to a rifle for use in hand-to-hand fighting
The soldier fixed his bayonet to his rifle. 士兵把刺刀装到步枪上。
近义词:blade, knife
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "sheath" if referring to the absence of a bayonet)
词性:Noun


词源: From French *baïonnette*, from Bayonne, a city in southwestern France, where bayonets are said to have been first made.
