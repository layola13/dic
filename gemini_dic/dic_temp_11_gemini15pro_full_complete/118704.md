metacism:/ˈmɛtəˌsɪzəm/ | n. | 浊音化，齿音化 | A change in the pronunciation of a consonant, especially the change of a non-m sound to an m sound. | The word "thumb" exhibits metacism, evolving from the Old English "þūma."  拇指这个词展现了浊音化，从古英语的"þūma"演变而来。|  The development of metacism can be observed in several Indo-European languages.  在一些印欧语系语言中可以观察到浊音化的发展。 |  consonant mutation, sound change |  - | noun
