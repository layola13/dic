half-breed:/ˈhæfˌbriːd/
noun
混血儿；杂种
A person whose parents are of different races, especially of European and Native American ancestry.  This term is now considered offensive.
He was shunned by both communities for being a half-breed. 他因为是混血儿而被两个群体排斥。
近义词:mixed-race, biracial (preferred), multiracial
反义词:purebred (in animal breeding)
词性:noun

half-breed:/ˈhæfˌbriːd/
adjective
混血的；杂种的
Of mixed race or breed. This term is now considered offensive.
The half-breed dog was unusually intelligent. 这只混血狗异常聪明。
近义词:mixed-breed, crossbred (for animals)
反义词:purebred (in animal breeding)
词性:adjective
