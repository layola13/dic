nonmovably:/nɒnˈmuːvəbli/
adverb
不可移动地
in a way that cannot be moved
The statue was fixed nonmovably to the pedestal. 这座雕像被牢牢地固定在底座上。
The equipment was nonmovably attached to the floor.  这设备被固定在地板上，无法移动。
Synonyms: immovably, fixedly, permanently, securely
Antonyms: movably, portably, temporarily

Etymology:
non- (prefix) + movable + -ly (suffix)
"non-"  means "not"
"movable" means "capable of being moved"
"-ly" forms adverbs
