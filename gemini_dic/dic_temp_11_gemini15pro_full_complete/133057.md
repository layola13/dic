Her back pain was nonepisodical, a constant dull ache that never fully went away. 她的背痛是持续性的，一种从未完全消失的持续钝痛。
The surveillance recorded a nonepisodical presence of the suspect in the area, suggesting he lived there. 监控录像显示嫌疑人在该区域持续出现，表明他住在那里。

反义词: episodic, intermittent, occasional, sporadic
词性: adjective
