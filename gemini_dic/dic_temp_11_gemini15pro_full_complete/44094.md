cripplingly:/ˈkrɪplɪŋli/
adverb
严重地；造成严重后果地
in a way that makes something very difficult to deal with or makes it fail
The country's economy has been cripplingly affected by the pandemic. 这个国家的经济受到了疫情的严重影响。
The small business was cripplingly in debt.  这家小企业负债累累。

近义词:severely, devastatingly, drastically
反义词:slightly, mildly, insignificantly
词性:adverb
