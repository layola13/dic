lazes:/ˈleɪzɪz/| v.| 偷懒，懒散度日 |spends time idly or relaxing; acts lazily. |He lazes around the house all day. 他整天在家无所事事。|She lazes in the sun. 她在阳光下懒洋洋地躺着。|近义词：lounges, loafs, relaxes, idles |反义词: works, hustles, toils |词性:动词

词源:
From the adjective "lazy" + "-s" (third-person singular present tense verb ending).  "Lazy" comes from Middle English *lazi*, from Old English *læs*, meaning "weak, slow, feeble."
