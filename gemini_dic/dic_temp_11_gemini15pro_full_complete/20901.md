bleached:/bliːtʃt/| adj.|漂白的，褪色的|Having had the color removed or reduced by a chemical process.|The bleached bones lay scattered across the desert. 漂白的骨头散落在沙漠中。|近义词：whitened, faded, pale|反义词：dyed, colored|词性:形容词
词源: From bleach (v.) + -ed.  The verb "bleach" comes from Old English *blǣcan, from Proto-Germanic *blaikijaną "to whiten, make pale," related to Old Norse bleikja "to bleach," and ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bhleg- "to shine, flash, burn."
