convivially:/kənˈvɪviəli/ | adv. | 欢乐地；友好地； convivial的副词形式 |  in a convivial way; characterized by or befitting a feast;  sociable | We spent a convivial evening with our new neighbors, sharing stories and laughter. 我们和新邻居一起度过了一个欢乐的夜晚，分享故事和欢笑。 | The atmosphere at the party was very convivial. 派对上的气氛非常友好。 |  cheerfully, merrily, jovially, genially, sociably | unsociably, gloomily | adverb

convivial: /kənˈvɪviəl/ | adj. | 欢乐的；友好的；适于宴会的 | friendly, lively, and enjoyable; fond of feasting, drinking, and good company |  We enjoyed a convivial atmosphere at the pub. 我们在酒吧里享受了欢乐的气氛。 | The restaurant has a convivial atmosphere. 这家餐厅的氛围很友好。|  jolly, cheerful, merry, festive, sociable | unfriendly, unsociable, gloomy, solitary | adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin convivialis, from Latin convivium (“a feast, banquet”), from com- (“together”) + vivere (“to live”).
