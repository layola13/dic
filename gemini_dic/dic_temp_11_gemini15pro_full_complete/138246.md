nostrum:/ˈnɒstrəm/ (US) /ˈnɑːstrəm/| n.| 秘方，万能药，（尤指）骗人的药，妙计；|a medicine, especially one that is not considered effective, prepared by an unqualified person; a scheme or remedy for bringing about some social or political reform or improvement|The politician offered the same old nostrum about the economy needing less government intervention.  这位政客提出了关于经济需要减少政府干预的老一套说辞。|Synonyms: panacea, cure-all, remedy, elixir, potion|Antonyms: poison, toxin|noun
