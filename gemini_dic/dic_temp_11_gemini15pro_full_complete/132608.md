nondiplomatic: /ˌnɒndɪpləˈmætɪk/ (UK) /ˌnɑːndɪpləˈmætɪk/ (US) | adj. | 不外交的，粗鲁的 | Not showing tact or skill in dealing with people; blunt or rude.  | His nondiplomatic approach offended many people. 他不圆滑的方式冒犯了很多人。 | Her nondiplomatic language caused a rift between the two families. 她不外交的语言导致了两个家庭之间的裂痕。 | 近义词: blunt, rude, indelicate, undiplomatic, tactless | 反义词: diplomatic, tactful, polite | 词性: 形容词


词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "diplomatic" (from French "diplomatique," ultimately from Greek "diploma" meaning "folded document").  "Nondiplomatic" is formed by adding the negative prefix "non-" to the adjective "diplomatic."  The adverbial form "nondiplomatically" is created by adding the suffix "-ly."
