bridestake: /ˈbraɪdˌsteɪk/ | n. |  新娘的彩礼，嫁妆 | A payment or gift given by a bride's family to the groom or his family as part of a marriage agreement. This practice is largely outdated in most Western cultures. |  In some cultures, a bridestake was a common part of the marriage negotiations. 在某些文化中，新娘彩礼是婚姻谈判的常见部分。| dowry, marriage portion |  - | noun
