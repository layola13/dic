intervention:/ˌɪntərˈvenʃən/ | n. | 干预，介入，干涉 | The government's intervention in the economy was controversial. 政府对经济的干预备受争议. |  The surgical intervention saved the patient's life.  外科手术的介入挽救了病人的生命。| Synonyms: interference, mediation, involvement | Antonyms: noninterference, inaction | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English intervencioun, from Late Latin interventionem, accusative singular of interventio (“a coming between, intervention”), from Latin intervenire (“to come between, intervene”), from inter- (“between”) + venire (“to come”).
