alphabetizers:/ˈælfəbəˌtaɪzərz/ | n. | 字母排序者，按字母顺序排列的人或物 | People or things that alphabetize. | The alphabetizers worked diligently to organize the extensive archive.  字母排序者们努力工作，整理了大量的档案。| The library hired alphabetizers to arrange the books. 图书馆雇佣了字母排序员来整理书籍。 |  synonyms:  sorters, organizers, arrangers | antonyms: disorganizers | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From alphabetize + -er.  "Alphabetize" comes from "alphabet" + "-ize".  "Alphabet" ultimately derives from the first two letters of the Greek alphabet, "alpha" and "beta".
