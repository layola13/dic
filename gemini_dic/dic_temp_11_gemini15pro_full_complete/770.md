absolve:/əbˈzɒlv/, /əbˈsɑːlv/| vt.|赦免，免除，宣告…无罪|To free from guilt or blame or their consequences; to pardon or forgive. |The priest absolved him of his sins.  神父赦免了他的罪过。

The court absolved her of all responsibility for the accident. 法院宣告她对这起事故不承担任何责任。|近义词：exonerate, acquit, pardon, forgive|反义词：condemn, convict, incriminate|verb
