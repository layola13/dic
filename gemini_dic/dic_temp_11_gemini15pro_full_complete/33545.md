cierge:/kɔːnˈsjerʒ/ | n. | 礼宾员，门房 | A hotel employee who assists guests with various tasks, such as making reservations, arranging tours, and providing information. |  Example: The concierge helped us book a table at the best restaurant in town.  礼宾员帮我们预订了城里最好的餐厅的位子。

Example:  I asked the concierge for directions to the museum. 我向门房询问去博物馆的方向。| Synonym: doorman, porter, attendant | Antonym: guest | noun
