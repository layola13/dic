nonpoliticization:/ˌnɒnˌpəlɪtɪsaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌnɑːnˌpəlɪtɪsaɪˈzeɪʃən/
noun
非政治化
The act or process of making something nonpolitical, or the state of being nonpolitical.
The nonpoliticization of humanitarian aid is crucial.  人道主义援助的非政治化至关重要。
The committee urged the nonpoliticization of the judicial system. 委员会敦促司法系统非政治化。
