nonstimulable:/ˌnɒnˈstɪmjʊləbl/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈstɪmjələbəl/ (US)| adj.|无刺激性的，不可刺激的|Not capable of being stimulated or excited; unresponsive to stimuli.|The patient's nonstimulable nerves caused concern among the medical team. 病人的神经无法被刺激，这引起了医疗团队的担忧。|The growth was found to be nonstimulable by growth factors.  人们发现这种生长不受生长因子的刺激。|近义词：unresponsive, inert, insensitive|反义词：stimulable, responsive, sensitive|词性:形容词
词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + stimulable (adjective from stimulate + -able suffix)
