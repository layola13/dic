dean: /diːn/
n.|院长，系主任；教务长|A dean is a senior official in a college or university who is responsible for a particular academic division, such as a faculty or a school. |The dean of the medical school announced a new research initiative. 医学院院长宣布了一项新的研究计划。|synonyms: principal, headmaster, headmistress, director|antonyms: student, pupil|noun
