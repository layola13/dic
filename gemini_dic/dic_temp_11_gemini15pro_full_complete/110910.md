The cell membrane has a lipidic bilayer. 细胞膜有一个脂质双分子层。
The researcher studied the lipidic content of the sample. 研究人员研究了样品的脂质含量。
近义词: fatty, lipoid
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly hydrophilic)
