atari:/əˈtɑːri/ | n. | 雅达利（公司名）;  (游戏中)击中目标; | A pioneering video game company. Also refers to a successful hit in a game. |  He worked at Atari during the golden age of arcade games. 他在街机游戏的黄金时代在雅达利工作。| Synonym: hit, strike | Antonym: miss | noun

atari:/əˈtɑːri/ | n. | 雅达利（日本围棋术语，指下一步棋即将被吃掉的状态）|  A term in the game of Go indicating a stone or group of stones about to be captured. | The opponent's atari put him in a difficult position. 对手的雅达利使他陷入了困境。| Synonym: almost captured | Antonym: safe | noun
