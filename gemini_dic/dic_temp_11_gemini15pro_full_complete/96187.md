indestructibly:/ˌɪndɪˈstrʌktɪbli/
adverb
不可摧毁地；牢不可破地
in a way that cannot be destroyed
The data is encrypted and stored indestructibly. 数据经过加密并以不可摧毁的方式存储。
The two nations are indestructibly linked by history. 这两个国家有着牢不可破的历史联系。
Synonym:permanently, enduringly, lastingly
Antonym:destructively, temporarily, precariously
词性: 副词
