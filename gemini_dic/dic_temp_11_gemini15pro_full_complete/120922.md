miscatalogues:/ˌmɪsˈkætəlɒɡz/ | n. | 错误分类，错误编目 | The librarian discovered several miscatalogues in the non-fiction section. 图书管理员在非小说类书籍区发现了几个错误的分类。| The museum's recent miscatalogues have led to confusion among researchers.  博物馆最近的错误编目给研究人员带来了困惑。 | 近义词: misclassifications, errors, blunders | 反义词: correct classifications, accurate entries | 词性: 名词

miscatalogue:/ˌmɪsˈkætəlɒɡ/ | vt. | 错误分类，错误编目 |  The assistant miscatalogued several important documents. 助理错误地归档了几份重要文件。 |  She miscatalogued the book under "fiction" instead of "biography." 她把这本书错误地归类到“小说”而不是“传记”类。 | 近义词: misclassify, misfile, mislabel | 反义词: classify correctly, file correctly, label correctly | 词性: 动词

词源: "mis-" (prefix meaning "wrong" or "badly") + "catalogue" (from Greek *katálogos* meaning "list").
