nonphonetically:/ˌnɒnfəˈnetɪkli/ | adv. | 非语音地；不按语音规则地 | Not based on the sounds of words; not according to phonetic principles. |  The word was spelled nonphonetically.  这个单词的拼写不符合语音规则。 | synonym: unphonetically | antonym: phonetically | adverb

Etymology:  From non- + phonetically.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "phonetically" relates to the study of speech sounds.
