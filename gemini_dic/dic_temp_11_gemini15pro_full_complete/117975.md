memorability:/ˌmɛmərəˈbɪləti/| n.| 记忆度，难忘性|The memorability of the event was enhanced by the beautiful music.  这次活动的难忘性因优美的音乐而增强。|Example: The song's simple melody contributes to its memorability. 这首歌简单的旋律使其更易于记忆。

Example: The president's speech lacked memorability.  总统的演讲缺乏记忆点。|Synonyms:  unforgettable nature,  recallability, retention |Antonyms: forgetfulness, unmemorableness |noun
