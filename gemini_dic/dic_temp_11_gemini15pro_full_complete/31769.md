check-out:/ˈtʃɛkˌaʊt/ (US), /ˈtʃekˌaʊt/ (UK)| n.|结账；结账柜台；退房；(从图书馆)借出(书籍等)|The process of paying for goods or services before leaving a store or hotel, or the place where this is done.  Also, the act of borrowing something from a library. |例句：The hotel check-out time is 11 a.m.  酒店退房时间是上午11点。


check-out:/ˈtʃɛkˌaʊt/ (US), /ˈtʃekˌaʊt/ (UK)| n.|检查；检验|An examination or inspection of something. |例句：The mechanic gave the car a thorough check-out. 技工对汽车进行了彻底的检查。



check-outs (plural):/ˈtʃɛkˌaʊts/ (US), /ˈtʃekˌaʊts/ (UK)| n.|结账；结账柜台；退房；(从图书馆)借出(书籍等)|Plural form of "check-out."|例句：There were long lines at all the check-outs. 所有收银台都排起了长队。


近义词:checkout (alternative spelling), departure (for hotels), payment, examination, inspection
反义词:check-in, arrival
词性:noun
