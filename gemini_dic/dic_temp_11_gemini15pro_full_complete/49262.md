deli:/ˈdɛli/| n.|熟食店，熟食|A store selling prepared foods, such as cheeses, salads, and cooked meats, ready to be eaten.|例句：I went to the deli to get a sandwich for lunch. 我去熟食店买了个三明治当午餐。|例句：This deli makes the best pastrami on rye. 这家熟食店的黑麦熏牛肉是最好吃的。|近义词：delicatessen|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑restaurant if 指的是提供现做食物的餐厅) |词性:noun
