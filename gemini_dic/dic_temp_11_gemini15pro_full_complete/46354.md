dactyliomancy:/ˌdæktɪliˈɒmænsi/
n.
指环占卜，指环法
Divination using a suspended ring, especially over a chart or map.
The dactyliomancy revealed the location of the hidden treasure. 指环占卜揭示了宝藏的隐藏地点。
近义词：divination, fortune-telling
反义词:  (无明确的反义词)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *daktylios* (ring) + *manteia* (divination).
