appropriacy:/əˈproʊpriəsi/ |n.|适宜性；得体性|The appropriacy of his remarks was questioned. 他的言论是否得体受到了质疑。|Example:  The teacher questioned the appropriacy of wearing shorts to school. 老师质疑穿短裤上学是否合适。

Example:  She demonstrated appropriacy in her choice of words for the formal occasion.  她在正式场合的措辞展现了得体性。|近义词：suitability, fitness, appropriateness|反义词：inappropriateness, unsuitability|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From appropriate (adj.) + -acy.  "Appropriate" comes from the Latin appropriatus, past participle of appropriare "to make one's own," from ad- "to" + proprius "one's own."
