lisne: /ˈlɪsən/ (This word is likely a misspelling of "listen") | v. | 倾听，仔细听 | To pay attention to a sound; to try to hear something. | Example: Listen carefully to the instructions. 仔细听指示。

Example: Please listen to me. 请听我说。

| 近义词: hear, heed, pay attention to | 反义词: ignore, disregard | 词性: verb
