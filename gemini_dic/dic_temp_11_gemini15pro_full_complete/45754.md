cushiness:/ˈkʊʃɪnɪs/
n.
舒适；安逸；轻松
the quality of being very comfortable, especially in a way that makes it easy to avoid working hard
The cushiness of his job allowed him to travel extensively. 他工作轻松，可以四处旅行。

近义词:comfort, ease, luxury, softness
反义词:discomfort, hardship, difficulty

词性:名词

词源: 源自形容词cushy (舒适的)，加上名词后缀-ness构成。 Cushy可能源自印度语gádī (垫子)。
