congenial:/kənˈdʒiːniəl/ | adj. | 意气相投的；合意的；适宜的 | (of a person) pleasant because of having similar tastes and interests; (of a thing) pleasant or agreeable because suited to one's taste or inclination |  We spent a congenial evening with our new neighbors. 我们和新邻居度过了一个愉快的夜晚。| synonyms: agreeable, pleasant, compatible, sympathetic, kindred, like-minded | antonyms: uncongenial, disagreeable, incompatible, hostile | adjective

congenial:/kənˈdʒiːniəl/ | adj. | (指环境)适宜的，宜人的 |  a congenial working environment 舒适的工作环境 | synonyms: suitable, agreeable, pleasant, favorable | antonyms: unsuitable, disagreeable, unpleasant, unfavorable | adjective
