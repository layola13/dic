collectivizing:/kəˈlɛktɪvaɪzɪŋ/
verb
集体化
The act of organizing something, typically agriculture or industry, on the basis of collective ownership or control.
The government was focused on collectivizing farms. 政府致力于集体化农场。
Stalin's policy of collectivizing agriculture resulted in widespread famine. 斯大林的农业集体化政策导致了 widespread 的饥荒。
