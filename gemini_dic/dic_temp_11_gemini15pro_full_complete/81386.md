graspingness:/ˈɡræspɪŋnɪs/
n.
贪婪，贪心
The state of being grasping or avaricious; greediness.
His graspingness for power alienated his allies. 他对权力的贪婪疏远了他的盟友。
Synonyms: avarice, greed, cupidity, rapacity
Antonyms: generosity, altruism, benevolence
