crawfishing:/ˈkrɔːfɪʃɪŋ/
n.
退缩，食言，出尔反尔
The act of backing out of a commitment or agreement, similar to a crawfish moving backward.
He promised to help, but then he went crawfishing. 他承诺要帮忙，但后来他食言了。
近义词:backpedaling, reneging, withdrawing
反义词:committing, following through
词性:名词

crawfishing:/ˈkrɔːfɪʃɪŋ/
vi.
退缩，食言，出尔反尔
To back out of a commitment or agreement.
After boasting about his plans, he crawfished when it came time to act.  在吹嘘了自己的计划之后，当行动的时候他却退缩了。
近义词:backpedal, renege, withdraw
反义词:commit, follow through
词性:动词
