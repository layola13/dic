balsamical: /bɔːlˈsæmɪkəl/ | adj. | 香脂的，有镇痛作用的，安慰性的 | Having the qualities of balm; soothing, healing, restorative.  | The balsamical scent of the pine trees filled the air. 松树的香脂气味弥漫在空气中。 |  The lotion had a balsamical effect on my sunburned skin.  这款乳液对我的晒伤皮肤有舒缓作用。 | 近义词: soothing, healing, restorative, lenitive, anodyne | 反义词: irritating, exacerbating | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin balsamicalis, from balsamum (“balsam”) + -icalis.  Ultimately from Greek βάλσαμον (balsamon).
