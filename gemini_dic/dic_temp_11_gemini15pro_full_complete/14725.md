awayday:/əˈweɪdeɪ/
noun
外出日；远足日；（尤指）球队客场比赛日
A day trip, especially one taken for leisure or to watch a sporting event away from home.
We're going on an awayday to the seaside. 我们要去海边一日游。
The team lost badly on their awayday.  该队在客场比赛中惨败。
近义词:outing, excursion, trip, day trip
反义词:homestand (主要用于体育比赛)
词性:n.
