interictal:/ˌɪntərˈɪktəl/
adjective
发作间歇期的，痫发作间歇的
Occurring between seizures or attacks, especially of epilepsy.
例句：Interictal EEG showed no epileptiform activity. 发作间歇期脑电图未显示癫痫样放电。

例句：Interictal discharges are brief bursts of abnormal electrical activity in the brain that occur between seizures. 发作间歇期放电是大脑中发生的异常电活动的短暂爆发，发生在癫痫发作之间。

近义词：interseizure
反义词：ictal


词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin *inter- (between) + ictus (a blow, stroke), related to epilepsy seizures being seen as "strokes" or attacks.
