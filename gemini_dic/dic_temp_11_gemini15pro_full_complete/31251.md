chanukkah:/ˈhɑːnəkə/ /ˈkɑːnəkə/| n.|光明节|An eight-day Jewish holiday commemorating the rededication of the Second Temple in Jerusalem at the time of the Maccabean Revolt against the Seleucid Empire.|We celebrate Chanukkah by lighting candles on the menorah each night. 我们通过每晚在烛台上点燃蜡烛来庆祝光明节。|近义词：Festival of Lights|反义词:None|词性:noun
