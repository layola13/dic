chain-reacted:/ˌtʃeɪn riˈæktɪd/
adjective
链式反应的
Caused by or involving a chain reaction, a series of events where each event causes the next.
The chain-reacted explosion destroyed the entire building. 链式反应的爆炸摧毁了整栋楼。
The chain-reacted events led to a market crash.  链式反应的事件导致了市场崩盘。
Synonyms: cascading, domino effect
Antonyms: isolated, independent
词性: adjective
