conchylaceous:/ˌkɒŋkɪˈleɪʃəs/| adj.|贝壳状的，似贝壳的|Resembling a shell, especially in form or texture. Having a shell-like structure or appearance.|The fossilized remains were conchylaceous, indicating a marine environment. 这些化石遗骸呈贝壳状，表明是海洋环境。|Synonyms: shell-like, shelly|Antonyms: shell-less|adjective
