gremlin: /ˈɡrem.lɪn/ (US), /ˈɡrem.lɪn/ (UK)| n.|小精灵；捣蛋鬼；（机械故障的）神秘原因|A gremlin is an imaginary mischievous creature that is blamed for mechanical problems or malfunctions. |The car trouble was blamed on gremlins in the engine. 汽车故障被归咎于发动机里的小精灵。|Synonyms: imp, sprite, goblin, elf|Antonyms: angel|Noun
