ETYMOLOGY->
From Dutch *Sint Nikolaas* (Saint Nicholas), via Pennsylvania German *Pelznickel* (literally, "fur Nicholas", referring to a fur-clad gift-bringer figure). The name *Santa Claus* evolved from this.
