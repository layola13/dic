frankenstein: /ˈfræŋkənˌstaɪn/ | n. |科学怪人；弗兰肯斯坦；弗兰肯斯坦的怪物|A scientist or inventor who creates something that ultimately destroys or harms him.  A creation that becomes destructive or harmful to its creator. |例句：He was hailed as a modern frankenstein for creating the virus. 他因创造了这种病毒而被称为现代弗兰肯斯坦。

例句：The social media platform became a frankenstein, spreading misinformation and hate speech beyond the control of its creators.  这个社交媒体平台变成了一个弗兰肯斯坦，传播着虚假信息和仇恨言论，超出了其创造者的控制。|近义词：monster, creation, abomination|反义词: creator, savior |词性: noun
