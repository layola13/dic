meterless:/ˈmiːtərləs/
adj.
无韵律的，没有格律的
Lacking regular meter or rhythm, especially in poetry.
The poem was striking for its meterless, free-flowing style. 这首诗以其无韵律、自由流畅的风格引人注目。
Synonyms: arrhythmic, free verse, unmetrical
Antonyms: metrical, rhythmic
词性: 形容词
