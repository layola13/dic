musomania:/ˌmjuːzoʊˈmeɪniə/ | n. | 狂热于音乐；音乐迷恋症 | An excessive or abnormal fondness for music. | He suffers from musomania; he can't go a day without listening to music for hours. 他患有音乐迷恋症；他一天不听几个小时的音乐就受不了。 | 近义词: melomania | 反义词: misomusy | 词性: noun
