crooned:/kruːnd/
verb
低吟; 哼唱; 轻柔地唱
to sing or hum in a soft, sentimental voice.
The singer crooned a romantic ballad. 歌手低吟浅唱了一首浪漫的民谣。

He crooned the baby to sleep. 他哼着歌哄宝宝入睡。


近义词: hummed, sang softly, murmured, warbled
反义词: shouted, yelled, screamed
词性: verb
