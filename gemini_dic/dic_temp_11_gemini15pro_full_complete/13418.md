atonable:/əˈtoʊnəbl/
adjective
可赎罪的; 可弥补的
Capable of being atoned for; expiable.
His sins are atonable. 他的罪过是可以赎回的。

atonable:/əˈtoʊnəbl/
adjective
可和解的
Capable of being atoned; reconcilable.
The dispute between the two families is atonable.  两个家族之间的争端是可以和解的。


近义词:excusable, forgivable, reparable
反义词:unpardonable, unforgivable, inexpiable
词性:adjective
