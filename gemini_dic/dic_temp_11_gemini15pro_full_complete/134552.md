nonliteralnesses:/ˌnɒnˈlɪtərəlnəsɪz/
|n.|非字面性；引申义；比喻意义|The state or quality of not being literal; figurative or metaphorical meaning.|The poet’s use of nonliteralnesses added depth and complexity to her work. 诗人对非字面意义的使用为她的作品增添了深度和复杂性。|synonyms:  figurativeness, metaphoricalness, symbolism, allegory|antonyms: literalness, literality|noun

nonliteralness:/ˌnɒnˈlɪtərəlnəs/
|n.|非字面性；引申义；比喻意义|The state or quality of not being literal; figurative or metaphorical meaning.|The comedian's jokes relied heavily on nonliteralness.  这位喜剧演员的笑话很大程度上依赖于非字面意义。|synonyms:  figurativeness, metaphoricalness, symbolism, allegory|antonyms: literalness, literality|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix): not
literal (adjective): taking words in their usual or most basic sense without metaphor or allegory. From Latin *litteralis* "of or belonging to letters," from *littera/litera* "letter."
-ness (suffix): forms nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
