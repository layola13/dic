nematodes:/ˈnɛməˌtoʊdz/ (US) /ˈnɛməˌtəʊdz/ (UK)| n. |线虫|Roundworms, a diverse phylum of unsegmented, usually cylindrical worms. They are found in a wide variety of environments, including soil, freshwater, and marine habitats, and some are parasitic.|Plant-parasitic nematodes can cause significant damage to crops. 植物寄生线虫会对农作物造成重大损害。

Many nematodes are beneficial to the soil ecosystem. 许多线虫对土壤生态系统有益。|近义词：roundworms, eelworms|反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific type of organism)|Noun
