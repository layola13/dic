go-getting:/ˈɡəʊˌɡetɪŋ/
adjective
积极进取的；有事业心的；努力争取成功的
Energetic and ambitious, especially in pursuing career advancement or success.
He's a real go-getting young executive. 他是一位真正积极进取的年轻主管。
近义词:ambitious, driven, enterprising, resourceful
反义词:lazy, passive, unambitious
词性:adjective

go-getter:/ˈɡəʊˌɡetər/
noun
积极进取的人；有事业心的人；努力争取成功的人
A person who is energetic, determined, and successful, especially in their career.
She's a real go-getter and I'm sure she'll do well in the new job. 她是一个真正积极进取的人，我相信她会在新工作中做得很好。
近义词:achiever, high-flyer, hustler
反义词:slacker, underachiever
词性:noun

词源：
"Go-getter" emerged in the early 20th century, combining the verb "go" with "getter," implying someone who actively pursues and obtains what they want.  "Go-getting" is the adjective form of this noun.
