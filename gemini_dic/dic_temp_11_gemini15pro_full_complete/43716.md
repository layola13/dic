creds:/krɛdz/ | n. | 资格，信用，信誉；凭证 | credentials的缩写，指证明一个人有资格做某事的文件或信息，例如学历、经验等。|  Example:  She had the creds to get the job. 她有获得这份工作的资格。| Synonyms: qualifications, credentials, certification | Antonyms: disqualification | noun

/krɛdz/ | n. | 可信度，可靠性 | 指一个人或事物被认为是真实可靠的程度。 | Example: The witness's creds were questioned.  证人的可信度受到质疑。| Synonyms: credibility, reliability, trustworthiness | Antonyms: unreliability, untrustworthiness | noun
