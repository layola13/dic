censor:/ˈsensər/ | n. |审查员，检查员| An official who examines books, films, news, etc., to remove anything considered offensive or harmful. | The censor cut several scenes from the movie. 审查员从电影中剪掉了几个场景。 |  近义词: examiner, inspector, reviewer | 反义词:  N/A | noun
censor:/ˈsensər/ | vt. |审查，检查| To examine books, films, news, etc., to remove anything considered offensive or harmful. | The government censored all news reports coming out of the war zone. 政府审查了所有来自战区的新闻报道。 | 近义词: expurgate, suppress, redact | 反义词: permit, allow, publish | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "censere" (to assess, judge, or value).  Originally referred to Roman censors, officials who conducted the census and supervised public morals.
