loadstone:/ˈloʊdˌstoʊn/| n. |天然磁石，磁铁矿；吸引人的东西|A piece of magnetite or other naturally magnetized mineral, able to attract iron.  Something that attracts strongly.|例句：The lodestone attracted the iron filings. 天然磁石吸引了铁屑。

例句：Her charisma acted as a lodestone, drawing people to her. 她的魅力像磁石一样吸引着人们。|近义词：magnet, attraction|反义词: repellent|词性:noun
