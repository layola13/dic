four-ply:/ˈfɔːrˈplaɪ/| adj.|四层的；四股的|Made of four layers or strands twisted together.  Composed of four separate strands or layers, often used to describe fabrics, yarn, or rope.|例句：She bought some four-ply wool for knitting. 她买了一些四股的羊毛来织毛衣。

例句：Four-ply tires are more durable than two-ply tires. 四层轮胎比两层轮胎更耐用。|近义词：quadruple, fourfold|反义词:single-ply, two-ply|词性:形容词
four-ply:/ˈfɔːrˈplaɪ/| n.|四层织物；四股线|A fabric or yarn made of four strands or layers.|例句：This four-ply is perfect for making warm sweaters.  这种四层织物非常适合制作保暖毛衣。

例句：The rope was made of strong four-ply. 这根绳子是由结实的四股线制成的。|近义词：four-fold yarn, quadruple thread|反义词:single-ply yarn|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "four" + "ply" (meaning a layer or fold). "Ply" comes from the Old French "plier" (to fold), ultimately from Latin "plicare" (to fold).
