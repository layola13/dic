causational:/kɔːˈzeɪʃənl/
adjective
因果的; 引起…的
relating to or acting as a cause
例句：The report explored the causational link between smoking and lung cancer.  这份报告探讨了吸烟和肺癌之间的因果关系。

例句：Causational factors in the spread of the disease are still being investigated.  该疾病传播的致病因素仍在调查中。

近义词：causal, causative
反义词：resultant, consequential
词性 adjective
