outfacing:/ˌaʊtˈfeɪsɪŋ/
adjective
面向外的，外向的
directed or facing outwards.
The outfacing windows offered a stunning view of the city. 面向外的窗户提供了令人惊叹的城市景观。
The building has large outfacing windows. 这栋建筑有很大的外向窗户。
synonym: outward-facing, exterior
antonym: inward-facing, interior
词性: adjective
