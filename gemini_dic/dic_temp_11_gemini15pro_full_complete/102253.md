iteratively:/ˈɪtərətɪvli/ | adv. | 迭代地，重复地 |  In a manner that involves repeating a process or set of instructions multiple times, typically with slight modifications or adjustments at each step, in order to achieve a desired outcome. |  The software iteratively refines the image until it reaches the desired clarity.  软件迭代地改进图像，直到达到所需的清晰度。 |  repeatedly, recurrently, cyclically |  non-iteratively, once | adverb
