oosporic: /ˌoʊəˈspɔːrɪk/ | adj. | 卵孢子的，由卵孢子产生的 | Relating to or formed from an oospore, a thick-walled sexual spore that develops from a fertilized oosphere. |  Oosporic reproduction is common in certain algae and fungi. 卵孢子繁殖在某些藻类和真菌中很常见。|  The oosporic walls are thick and resistant. 卵孢子壁厚而有抵抗力。| synonyms: oosporogenous | antonyms: N/A | adjective
