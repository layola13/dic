myeloidin:/maɪˈɛlɔɪdɪn/| n. |类髓素，髓鞘质|A scleroprotein found in the elastic fibers of ligaments and in the skeletal supporting structures of some invertebrates.|Myeloidin is a key component in the shell of certain cephalopods. 类髓素是某些头足类动物外壳中的关键成分。|Myeloidin provides structural support. 髓鞘质提供结构支撑。|近义词：scleroprotein|反义词: None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *myelos* (marrow) + *-oid* (like) + *-in* (suffix forming names of proteins)
