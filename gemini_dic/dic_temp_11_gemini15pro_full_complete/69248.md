feastful:/ˈfiːstfʊl/| adj.|盛宴的；丰富的；欢乐的|Characterized by or resembling a feast; abundant; joyous.|We had a feastful Christmas dinner. 我们享用了丰盛的圣诞晚餐。|近义词：festive, abundant, plentiful, copious|反义词：meager, scanty, sparse|词性:形容词
Etymology: From 'feast' (from Old French feste, ultimately from Latin festa) + '-ful' (a suffix meaning "full of," "characterized by").
