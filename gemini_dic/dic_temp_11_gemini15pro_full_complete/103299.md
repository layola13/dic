jibe:/dʒaɪb/| v. n.|嘲讽，嘲弄；与…一致，符合；(航海)转向|To make a taunting or sarcastic remark; to be in agreement or accord; (nautical) to shift a sail from one side of a vessel to the other.|Example 1: His jibes about my cooking skills were hurtful. 他对我厨艺的嘲讽很伤人。

Example 2:  The two witnesses' stories didn't jibe.  两个证人的说法不一致。|近义词：taunt, mock, sneer, agree, correspond|反义词: compliment, praise, disagree, clash|词性:动词, 名词


jibes:/dʒaɪbz/| n.|嘲讽，嘲弄|Plural form of "jibe," meaning taunting or sarcastic remarks.|Example 1:  His constant jibes made her uncomfortable. 他不断的嘲讽让她感到不舒服。

Example 2:  She ignored his jibes and continued with her presentation. 她无视他的嘲讽，继续她的演讲。|近义词：taunts, mocks, sneers|反义词: compliments, praises|词性:名词

词源:
From Middle English *geben, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old Norse geipa (“to gape, shout”).
