isopodiform:/ˈaɪsəʊpɒdɪfɔːm/| adj.|等足类形的，等足目形的|Resembling or related to isopods; having the form or characteristics of an isopod.  Isopodiform crustaceans are characterized by their flattened, segmented bodies and seven pairs of legs.|The newly discovered species had a distinctly isopodiform body.  这种新发现的物种有一个明显的等足类体型。|近义词:isopod-like|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps something like "unlike an isopod")|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From "isopod" + "-iform."  "Isopod" comes from the Greek "isos" (equal) + "pous" (foot), referring to their similar legs.  "-iform" is a suffix meaning "having the form of."
