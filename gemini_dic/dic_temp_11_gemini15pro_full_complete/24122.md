brews:/bruːz/
verb|酿造，酝酿，沏，煮，调制|The coffee brews slowly. 咖啡煮得很慢。|English Explanation:  Third-person singular simple present form of "brew."  To make (beer, tea, coffee, etc.) by soaking, boiling, or infusing in liquid.  Can also mean to plot or contrive something.|例句：He brews his own beer. 他自己酿啤酒。

例句：Trouble is brewing. 麻烦正在酝酿。|近义词：ferment, concoct, prepare, steep, infuse|反义词:N/A
词性:v.

brew:/bruːz/
noun|酿造的饮料，混合物|This is a strong brew. 这是一种浓烈的饮料。|English Explanation: A beverage made by brewing, especially tea or coffee. Can also refer to a mixture of various things.|例句：He offered me a hot brew. 他给了我一杯热饮。

例句：A nasty brew of chemicals. 一种令人作呕的化学混合物。|近义词：concoction, mixture, beverage|反义词:N/A
词性:n.


词源:
From Old English *brēowan "to brew," of Germanic origin; related to Dutch brouwen and German brauen.
