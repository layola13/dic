asarone:/ˈæsəroʊn/ | n. |α-细辛醚; β-细辛醚 |A fragrant liquid compound found in certain plants, notably calamus and asarum.  It exists as two isomers, α-asarone and β-asarone.  |Asarone is a psychoactive chemical found in the calamus plant.  α-细辛醚是一种在菖蒲属植物中发现的精神活性化学物质。| Asarone is known to have insecticidal properties.  β-细辛醚已知具有杀虫特性。|Synonym:  (for effect) psychoactive compound|Antonym:  (generally) inactive compound|Noun

asarone:/ˈæsəroʊn/ | n. |细辛酮; 细辛醚 |A crystalline ketone C12H16O3 obtained from asarum oil. |The asarone found in the essential oil of Acorus calamus is primarily β-asarone.  在菖蒲精油中发现的细辛酮主要是β-细辛酮。|Excessive consumption of asarone can be toxic.  过量食用细辛酮可能会有毒。|Synonym: (for chemical structure) phenylpropanoid|Antonym: (generally) non-toxic compound|Noun
