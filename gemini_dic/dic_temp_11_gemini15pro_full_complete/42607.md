counterquestioned:/ˌkaʊntərˈkwɛstʃənd/ | v. |反问，提出质疑|To respond to a question with another question, often to challenge or evade the original inquiry.|  The witness counterquestioned the lawyer's attempt to lead him. 证人反问了律师引导他的企图。| 近义词：challenge, question back, retort, rebut | 反义词: answer, respond, reply | 词性: 动词

counterquestion:/ˌkaʊntərˈkwɛstʃən/ | n. |反问，质疑|A question asked in response to another question.| The lawyer's counterquestion threw the witness off balance.  律师的反问使证人措手不及。| 近义词：retort, rebuttal, riposte | 反义词: answer, response, reply | 词性: 名词

He counterquestioned her about her whereabouts on the night of the crime. 他盘问了她在犯罪当晚的行踪。
She counterquestioned him sharply, demanding proof. 她严厉地反问他，要求他拿出证据。
He counterquestioned the premise of the argument. 他质疑了论证的前提。
She skillfully counterquestioned the witness, exposing inconsistencies in his testimony. 她巧妙地反问证人，揭露了他证词中的矛盾之处。
