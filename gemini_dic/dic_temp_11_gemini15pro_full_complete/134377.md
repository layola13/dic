noninterpretable:/ˌnɒnɪnˈtɜːprɪtəbl/ | /ˌnɑːnɪnˈtɜːrprɪtəbl/ | adj. | 不可解释的，无法诠释的 | Not able to be interpreted or understood; incomprehensible. | The results of the experiment were noninterpretable due to a flaw in the methodology. 实验结果由于方法上的缺陷而无法解释。| The artist's abstract painting was noninterpretable to most viewers.  这位艺术家的抽象画对大多数观众来说难以理解。 | 近义词：incomprehensible, unintelligible, inexplicable, unfathomable | 反义词：interpretable, understandable, comprehensible, clear | 词性：形容词

词源：non- (prefix meaning "not") + interpretable (from interpret, from Latin interpretari "to explain, expound," from interpres "agent, translator").
