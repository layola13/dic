by-passes:/ˈbaɪˌpæsɪz/
n. |旁路，绕行道；分流术|A road that passes around a town or other congested area. A surgical operation in which a diseased or blocked part of a blood vessel is bypassed by a graft.|The new by-pass avoids the town centre.  这条新的绕行道避开了市中心。|synonyms: detour, alternative route, shunt|antonyms: direct route, main road|noun
by-passes:/ˈbaɪˌpæsɪz/
v. |绕过，避开；忽视|Go past or around (a place or obstacle). Ignore (a rule, procedure, or person).|He bypassed the committee and went straight to the president. 他绕过了委员会，直接去找了总裁。|synonyms: circumvent, avoid, evade, ignore, disregard|antonyms: confront, address, acknowledge|verb
