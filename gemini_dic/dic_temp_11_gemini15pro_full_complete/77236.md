gastroenteritis:/ˌɡæstroʊˌentəˈraɪtɪs/
n.
肠胃炎
Inflammation of the stomach and intestines, typically resulting from bacterial toxins or viral infection and causing vomiting and diarrhea.
She was diagnosed with gastroenteritis after experiencing severe abdominal pain and vomiting. 她经历了严重的腹痛和呕吐后被诊断为肠胃炎。
The contaminated food caused an outbreak of gastroenteritis among the wedding guests. 受污染的食物导致婚礼宾客中爆发了肠胃炎。
近义词: stomach flu, belly bug
反义词: (No direct antonym for a medical condition)
