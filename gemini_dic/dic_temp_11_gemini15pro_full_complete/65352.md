eulogistic: /ˌjuːləˈdʒɪstɪk/ | adj. | 赞美的，颂扬的 | Praising someone or something very highly.  Expressing or containing eulogy. |  The eulogistic speech moved the audience to tears.  颂扬性的演讲使观众感动得流下了眼泪。 |  laudatory, complimentary, praising | critical, derogatory | adjective

eulogy: /ˈjuːlədʒi/ | n. | 颂词，悼词 | A speech or piece of writing that praises someone or something highly, typically someone who has just died. | He delivered a moving eulogy at his father's funeral. 他在父亲的葬礼上致了感人的悼词。 |  tribute, panegyric, encomium | criticism, condemnation | noun
