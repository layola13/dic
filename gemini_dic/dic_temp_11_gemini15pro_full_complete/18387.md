bellying:/ˈbelɪɪŋ/
bellying: v. |膨胀，鼓起；(使)突出|The wind bellied the sails. 风鼓起了船帆。|
bellying: n. |膨胀，鼓起|The bellying of the sails indicated a strong wind. 船帆的鼓起表明风很大。|
bellying: adj.| 鼓起的，膨胀的 | The bellying clouds threatened rain. 鼓起的云预示着要下雨。|
Etymology: From belly + -ing.  "Belly" comes from Old English "belg," meaning "bag, skin."|
Synonyms: swelling, bulging, protruding, expanding|
Antonyms: contracting, shrinking, deflating, flattening|
Part of Speech: Verb (primarily), Noun (less common), Adjective (rarely used)|
