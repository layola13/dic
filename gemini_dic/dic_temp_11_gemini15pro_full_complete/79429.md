glister:/ˈɡlɪstər/ (US), /ˈɡlɪstə(r)/ (UK)| v. n.| 闪耀，闪烁；光彩；灿烂|To shine with a soft reflected light; sparkle.| The dew glistened on the grass. 露珠在草地上闪闪发光。|近义词：glitter, shimmer, sparkle, gleam, shine|反义词：dull, fade|词性:动词,名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *glisteren, *glisteren, from Old English *glisterian, *glistrian (unattested), from Proto-West Germanic *glistrōn, from Proto-Germanic *glistrijaną (“to glitter, glisten”). Cognate with West Frisian glisterje (“to glitter”), Dutch glinsteren (“to glitter”), German glitzern (“to glitter”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *(s)leyd- (“smooth, slippery”).
