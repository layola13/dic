competitress: /kəmˈpɛtɪtrɪs/ | n. | 女竞争者，女对手 | A woman who competes; a female rival. |  She was a fierce competitress in the business world.  她在商界是一位强劲的竞争对手。| 近义词: competitor, rival, opponent | 反义词: ally, teammate, partner | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *competitour, from Old French competiteur, from Latin competitor, agent noun from competere "to strive together," from com- "together" + petere "to seek, strive after." The -ess suffix denotes a female.
