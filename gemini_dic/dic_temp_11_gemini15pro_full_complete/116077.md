martite: 赤铁矿
martite: A form of hematite (Fe2O3) pseudomorphously replacing magnetite (Fe3O4), usually having the octahedral form of magnetite.  It forms through the oxidation of magnetite.
martite: Martite is a common mineral found in banded iron formations.
martite: 近义词: hematite, iron oxide
