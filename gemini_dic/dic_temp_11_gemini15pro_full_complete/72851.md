folkething:/ˈfɔːlkəθɪŋ/ | n. | 国民议会，丹麦议会 |The Folkething is the lower house of the Danish Parliament.  丹麦国民议会是丹麦议会的下议院。| The bill was passed by the Folkething. 该法案已由国民议会通过。| 近义词: parliament, legislature, congress | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑用上议院 upper house) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Danish *folkething*, from *folk* (“people”) + *ting* (“assembly, parliament”).  源自丹麦语 *folkething*，由 *folk*（“人民”）和 *ting*（“集会，议会”）组成。
