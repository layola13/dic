eriach:/ˈɛriːæk/ | n. | 赔偿;赎金;补偿;赎价 |A payment made to compensate for loss or injury, especially to atone for wrongdoing; a ransom. | The eriach demanded for the kidnapped prince was exorbitant.  被绑架王子的赎金要求过高。|  synonyms: compensation, restitution, redress, reparation, atonement, ransom | antonyms: penalty, forfeiture, fine | noun
