musics:/ˈmjuːzɪks/| n. |音乐（非标准用法，通常用 music）| While technically a plural form, "musics" is rarely used and considered non-standard.  "Music" is the commonly accepted term.  Some argue "musics" can refer to different genres or styles of music, but this usage is not widely accepted. |  (Generally avoid using "musics")  *She enjoys many different kinds of music.* | 近义词：music, melodies, tunes, compositions | 反义词: (None for music, perhaps noise or silence) | 词性: Noun
