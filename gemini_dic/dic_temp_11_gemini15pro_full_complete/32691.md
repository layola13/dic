chloasma:/kləʊˈæzmə/ /kloʊˈæzmə/
noun
黄褐斑，妊娠斑
A pigmentary skin discoloration, usually of a tan or dark brown color, occurring in irregular patches most often on the face.
She developed chloasma during her pregnancy.  她在怀孕期间出现了黄褐斑。

Chloasma is often triggered by hormonal changes. 黄褐斑通常由荷尔蒙变化引起。

Synonym:melasma, hyperpigmentation
Antonym:hypopigmentation
n.
