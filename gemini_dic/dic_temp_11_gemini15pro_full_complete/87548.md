heteromerous:/ˌhɛtərˈɒmərəs/
| adj. |
异部的，异构的 |
Having unequal parts or elements, especially having the parts of a flower unequal in number.  Specifically in botany, having a different number of parts in successive whorls (e.g., a flower with five sepals, five petals, ten stamens, and five carpels). |
Example 1:  The heteromerous flower structure is a key characteristic of some plant families.  异部花结构是一些植物科的关键特征。

Example 2:  Botanists studied the heteromerous arrangement of the floral organs.  植物学家研究了花器官的异构排列。|
Near synonyms:  anisomerous, asymmetrical |
Antonyms:  isomerous, symmetrical |
