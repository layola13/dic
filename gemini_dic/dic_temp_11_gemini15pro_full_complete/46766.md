danger:/ˈdeɪndʒər/ | n. | 危险，风险，威胁 |Exposure to radiation is a danger to health. 暴露于辐射下对健康有害。|  The soldier was in grave danger.  这名士兵处于极大的危险之中。| 近义词：peril, hazard, risk, threat | 反义词：safety, security | 词性: 名词

词源: From Old French *dangier "power, jurisdiction, peril" (itself from Late Latin *dominarium "power of a lord," from Latin dominus "lord"), from a prehistoric Germanic source akin to Old English dom "judgment, doom." The meaning shifted from "power to harm" to "liability to harm."
