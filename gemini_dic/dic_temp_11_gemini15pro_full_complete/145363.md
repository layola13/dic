overburnt:/ˌoʊvərˈbɜːrnt/| adj.|烧过头的，烤焦的|Burned or cooked for too long, resulting in a scorched or bitter taste.|The overburnt toast tasted like charcoal. 烤焦的吐司尝起来像木炭。|Synonyms: scorched, burnt, charred|Antonyms: undercooked, raw|adjective

overburnt:/ˌoʊvərˈbɜːrnt/| adj.|过度燃烧的|Burned excessively, often used to describe materials like fuel or chemicals.|The overburnt engine emitted thick black smoke. 过度燃烧的引擎排放出浓浓的黑烟。|Synonyms: incinerated, consumed|Antonyms: unburnt, intact|adjective
