assigners:/əˈsaɪnərz/
|n.|分配者，让与人，转让人|People who assign something, typically a right or property, to someone else.  They are the ones who transfer ownership or responsibility.|The assigners of the patent transferred all rights to the company.|近义词：transferors, grantors|反义词: assignees, recipients|词性:noun
