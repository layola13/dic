nostalgia:/nɑˈstælʒə/ /nɒˈstældʒə/| n. |乡愁；怀旧；念旧|A sentimental longing or wistful affection for the past, typically for a period or place with happy personal associations.|例句：A wave of nostalgia swept over her when she saw her childhood home. 当她看到 childhood home. 当她看到童年的家时，一阵怀旧之情涌上心头。

例句：He felt a pang of nostalgia for his college days. 他对大学时光感到一阵怀念。|近义词：homesickness, longing, yearning, reminiscence|反义词: apathy, indifference|词性:noun
