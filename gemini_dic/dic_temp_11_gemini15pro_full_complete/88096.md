hightail:/ˈhaɪteɪl/| v. | 迅速离开，逃走 | To leave or depart quickly, often in a panicked or hurried manner. |  The robbers hightailed it out of the bank when the alarm went off. 银行警报响起时，劫匪迅速逃离了银行。

The cat hightailed it across the road when it saw the dog.  那只猫看到狗后，飞快地跑过了马路。| 近义词：flee, escape, run away, bolt, scram | 反义词：arrive, approach, stay |词性: 动词
