overserved:/ˌoʊvərˈsɜːrvd/| adj.| 过量供应的，招待过分的| Having been served more alcohol than is good for one.  Being provided with more of something, especially food or drink, than is necessary or wanted.| The guests felt slightly overserved at the wedding reception. 婚礼招待会上，客人们觉得酒水供应得有点过多了。| The restaurant overserved us with appetizers. 这家餐厅给我们上了太多的开胃菜。|近义词：overfed, glutted, satiated, sated|反义词: underserved, deprived|词性:形容词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "served" (past participle of "serve")  "Over-" 源于古英语 "ofer"，表示“在...之上，超过”。 "serve" 源于拉丁语 "servire"，表示“服务，服侍”。
