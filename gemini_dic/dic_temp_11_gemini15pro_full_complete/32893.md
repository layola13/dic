chondrometer:/kɒnˈdrɒmɪtər/
noun
软骨测量器
An instrument for measuring the thickness of cartilage.
The surgeon used a chondrometer to accurately assess the cartilage damage in the patient's knee.  外科医生使用软骨测量器精确评估患者膝盖软骨的损伤程度。
Synonyms:  cartilage gauge
Antonyms: N/A
