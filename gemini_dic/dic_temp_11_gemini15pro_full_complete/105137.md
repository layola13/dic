ketamines:/ˈkiːtəˌmiːnz/ | n. |氯胺酮|A synthetic compound used as an anesthetic and analgesic drug, and also recreationally as a hallucinogen.  It acts primarily as an NMDA receptor antagonist.|The patient was given ketamines before the surgery. 病人在手术前服用了氯胺酮。|Synonyms: Ketalar, Special K|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific drug, could consider stimulants as a functional opposite in some contexts)|noun
