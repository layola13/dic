cloddy:/ˈklɒdi/ (UK) /ˈklɑːdi/ (US)| adj.|土块状的；结块的|Covered in or full of clods of earth; lumpy.|The field was cloddy after the heavy rain. 雨后田地里满是土块。|The tractor left cloddy furrows in its wake. 拖拉机过后留下了块状的犁沟。|近义词：lumpy, bumpy, chunky|反义词:smooth, fine|词性:形容词

词源: From clod + -y.  "Clod" comes from Old English *clod-, related to Middle Dutch klotte and Old High German klotto, all of Germanic origin.
