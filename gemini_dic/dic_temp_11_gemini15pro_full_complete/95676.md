inconcinnity:/ˌɪnkənˈsɪnɪti/
n.
不协调，不和谐，不一致
lack of harmony or suitability; incongruity
The inconcinnity of her bright pink dress with the somber funeral setting was jarring.  她那件亮粉色的连衣裙与肃穆的葬礼气氛格格不入，很不协调。
近义词：disharmony, incongruity, incompatibility, discordance
反义词：harmony, congruity, agreement, consistency
词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *inconcinnitas, from Latin inconcinnus "unbecoming, inelegant, awkward," from in- "not" + concinnus "elegant, neat, skillfully put together."
