classified: /ˈklæsɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 机密的，保密的；分类的 | Arranged in classes or categories.  Designated as officially secret; confidential. |  例句：The documents were classified as top secret.  这些文件被列为绝密。| 近义词：confidential, secret, restricted | 反义词：public, open, unclassified | 词性: adjective

classified: /ˈklæsɪfaɪd/ | v. (past tense and past participle classified) | 分类，归类 | To arrange (something) in classes or categories. | 例句：The books were classified by subject.  这些书按主题分类。| 近义词：categorize, sort, arrange | 反义词：randomize, disorganize | 词性: verb
