obligee:/ˌɒblɪˈdʒiː/ /ˌɑːblɪˈdʒiː/
noun
债权人；受惠者
The person to whom another is obligated or bound; the person in whose favor an obligation exists.
The bank is the obligee in the mortgage agreement.  银行是抵押协议中的债权人。
The charity was the obligee of a large donation.  该慈善机构是一大笔捐款的受益者。
creditor, beneficiary
debtor, obligor
n.
