monologuing:/məˈnɒləɡɪŋ/ /məˈnɑːləɡɪŋ/| v.|自言自语；发表长篇大论|The actor was monologuing about his past.  这位演员正在自言自语地讲述他的过去。|近义词：soliloquizing, ranting, rambling|反义词: conversing, dialoguing|词性:动词
词源: From monologue + -ing.  Monologue comes from Greek monos "alone" + legein "to speak".
