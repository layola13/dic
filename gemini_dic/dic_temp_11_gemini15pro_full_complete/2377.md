adipic:/əˈdɪpɪk/| adj.| 己二酸的；肥胖的|Relating to or derived from fat; fatty.  Specifically, relating to or designating a dibasic acid, C6H10O4, obtained by the oxidation of fats or by synthesis, and used especially in the manufacture of nylon and other polymers.|Adipic acid is a key component in the production of nylon. 己二酸是尼龙生产中的关键成分。|Synonyms: fatty, obese (for the less common meaning)|Antonyms: lean, thin (for the less common meaning)|adjective
