calcographic:/ˌkælkoʊˈɡræfɪk/| adj.|铜版雕刻的；铜版印刷的|Relating to or produced by calcography, a printmaking technique.  It refers specifically to intaglio printing, where the image is incised into a metal plate.|The museum has a fascinating collection of calcographic prints.  博物馆收藏了引人入胜的铜版画。|Synonyms: intaglio, engraved|Antonyms: relief, raised|adjective

calcography:/kælˈkɒɡrəfi/| n.|铜版雕刻术；铜版印刷术|The art or process of engraving on copper or other metal plates for printing. Also refers to the prints produced by this method.|Calcography was a popular method of reproducing artwork in the 18th century. 18世纪，铜版雕刻是复制艺术品的一种流行方法。|Synonyms: engraving, etching|Antonyms: lithography, woodcut|noun
