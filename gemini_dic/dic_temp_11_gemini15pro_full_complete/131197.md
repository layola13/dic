nonburdensomeness:/ˌnɒnbɜːrdənsəmənəs/ |adj.| 无负担; 不麻烦; 不构成负担 |The principle of nonburdensomeness in medical ethics emphasizes that treatment should not create an undue burden on the patient.  医学伦理中的无负担原则强调治疗不应给患者造成过度的负担。|  例句：The hospice emphasized nonburdensomeness in their care philosophy, prioritizing the patient's comfort and quality of life.  临终关怀机构在其护理理念中强调无负担，优先考虑患者的舒适度和生活质量。

| 例句：  Her family ensured her nonburdensomeness during her final days by providing constant support and care. 她的家人通过持续的支持和照顾，确保她在最后的日子里没有负担。 | 近义词:  easiness, lightness, effortlessness, undemanding | 反义词: burdensomeness, onerousness, difficulty, demanding | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "burden" (noun meaning "a load, something difficult to bear") + "-some" (suffix forming adjectives) + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)
