chasteners:/ˈtʃeɪsənərz/
n.
惩罚者；磨炼者；使人改正错误的人或事物
People or things that correct or discipline someone.
The difficulties he faced were chasteners that ultimately made him a stronger person.  他面临的困难是磨炼他的人格，最终使他成为一个更坚强的人。

近义词: disciplinarians, correctors, reformers
反义词: encouragers, supporters

词性: 名词 (plural)

词源: From the verb "chasten," from Old French "chastier," from Latin "castigare" (to punish, correct).
