interlocution:/ˌɪntərloʊˈkjuːʃən/| n. |对话，交谈|An instance of speaking or conversing with someone; a conversation.|例句：The play consisted of a series of witty interlocutions between the two main characters. 这部戏剧由两个主要人物之间一系列机智的对话组成。

例句：The president engaged in a brief interlocution with reporters before boarding the plane. 总统在登机前与记者进行了简短的交谈。|近义词：conversation, dialogue, exchange, discourse|反义词:silence, monologue|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin interlocutio, from Latin interloqui (“to speak between, interrupt”), from inter- (“between”) + loqui (“to speak”).
