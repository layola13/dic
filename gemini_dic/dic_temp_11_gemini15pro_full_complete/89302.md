homoeostasis:/ˌhoʊmiːoʊˈsteɪsɪs/| n. |体内平衡，恒定性|The tendency of a system, especially the physiological system of higher animals, to maintain internal stability, owing to the coordinated response of its parts to any situation or stimulus that would tend to disturb its normal condition or function.|The body's temperature regulation is a good example of homoeostasis. 人体的温度调节是体内平衡的一个很好的例子。|近义词：equilibrium, balance, stability|反义词:imbalance, instability|名词
