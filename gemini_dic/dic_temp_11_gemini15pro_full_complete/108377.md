law-abiding:/ˈlɔː əˈbaɪdɪŋ/
adjective
守法的
Obeying and respecting the law.
She is a law-abiding citizen. 她是一位守法的公民。
law-respecting, compliant, well-behaved
lawless, criminal, delinquent
