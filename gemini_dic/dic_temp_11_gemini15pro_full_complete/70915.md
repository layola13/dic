fireball:/ˈfaɪərbɔːl/ (US), /ˈfaɪəbɔːl/ (UK)| n.|火球；流星；烈酒|A bright meteor or a ball of fire.  A highly alcoholic drink.|例句：The meteor streaked across the sky like a fireball.  流星像火球一样划过天空。

例句：He ordered a fireball shot at the bar. 他在酒吧点了一杯火球烈酒。|近义词：meteor, meteoroid, bolide, comet, shooting star (for the astronomical sense); liquor, spirits (for the alcoholic drink)|反义词: (No direct antonym.  One could consider "ice" for the temperature contrast, or "weak drink" for the alcoholic contrast depending on the context.)|词性:noun
