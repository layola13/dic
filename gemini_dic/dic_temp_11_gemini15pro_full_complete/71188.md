fistic:/ˈfɪstɪk/| adj. n.|拳击的；斗殴；| relating to boxing or fighting with the fists. |例句：He settled the argument in a fistic manner. 他用拳击的方式解决了争端。

例句：The two boys engaged in a brief fistic exchange. 两个男孩进行了一场短暂的拳击较量。|近义词：pugilistic, boxing, fighting|反义词:peaceful, nonviolent|词性:adjective, noun
fisticuffs:/ˈfɪstɪˌkʌfs/| n.|斗殴，拳斗；|a fight with fists. |例句：The two men engaged in a round of fisticuffs. 两个人互相扭打起来。


例句：The argument escalated into fisticuffs. 争论升级成了拳打脚踢。 |近义词：brawl, fight, scuffle|反义词:peace, truce|词性:noun

词源: From fist + -ic, influenced by cuff.
