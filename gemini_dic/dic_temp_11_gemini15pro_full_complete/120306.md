minicity:/ˈmɪnɪsɪti/
noun
迷你城市；小型城市
A small city, often planned and built with specific features, sometimes as a model or experiment in urban development.  It can also refer to a scaled-down version of a city, like a theme park attraction.
The architect envisioned a minicity powered entirely by renewable energy.  这位建筑师设想了一个完全由可再生能源提供动力的迷你城市。
The children built a minicity out of LEGOs. 孩子们用乐高积木搭建了一个迷你城市。
Synonyms: small town, microcity, model city, miniature city
Antonyms: metropolis, megacity
