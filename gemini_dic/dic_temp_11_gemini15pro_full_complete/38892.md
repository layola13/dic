conferring:/kənˈfɜːrɪŋ/
verb
授予，赋予; 商谈，商议
present participle of confer
例句：
The university is conferring an honorary degree on her.
这所大学将授予她荣誉学位。

They spent hours conferring with each other.
他们花了几个小时互相协商。

近义词：
granting, bestowing, awarding (授予)
discussing, consulting, deliberating (商谈)

反义词：
revoking, withdrawing (授予的反义词)
disagreeing, arguing (商谈的反义词)

词性
v.
