dramatical:/drəˈmætɪkl/| adj.|戏剧的；引人注目的；夸张的|Relating to drama or the performance of drama; striking in appearance or effect; exaggerated.|例句：She made a dramatic entrance, sweeping into the room in a long red gown. 她穿着鲜红色的长袍大步走进房间，出场方式十分引人注目。

例句：He gave a dramatic reading of the poem. 他声情并茂地朗诵了这首诗。|近义词：theatrical, exaggerated, striking, impressive|反义词：understated, subtle, low-key|词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin dramaticus, from Greek dramatikos, from drama (see drama).
