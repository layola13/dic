grillers:/ˈɡrɪlərz/| n.|烧烤架，烧烤的人|People who grill food, or appliances used for grilling.  Those grillers make delicious BBQ.|例句：The grillers were busy preparing food for the picnic. 烧烤的人们正忙着准备野餐的食物。

例句：We bought new grillers for our backyard barbecue. 我们为后院烧烤买了新的烧烤架。|近义词：barbecue chefs, grill cooks, barbecue grills, grills|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "oven users" or "microwave users" in a specific context)
词性:名词(plural)

词源: From the verb "to grill," which originates from the Old French word "griller," meaning "to broil."
