foreshortening:/fɔːrˈʃɔːrtnɪŋ/ /fɔrˈʃɔːrtnɪŋ/
n.
透视缩短（法）
The technique of depicting an object or human figure in a picture so that it appears shorter than its actual length, as an effect of perspective or the angle of vision.
The artist used foreshortening to make the outstretched arm appear to project towards the viewer. 艺术家用透视缩短法使伸出的手臂看起来像是朝向观众。

The foreshortening in this painting makes the figures look dramatically elongated.  这幅画中的透视缩短法使人物看起来戏剧性地拉长了。

perspective, contraction
elongation
noun
