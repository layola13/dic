buzzingly:/ˈbʌzɪŋli/| adv.|嗡嗡地，嘈杂地|In a manner that produces a buzzing sound.  Describes an atmosphere filled with a low, continuous humming sound, often suggestive of activity or excitement.|例句：The bees were buzzingly busy around the hive. 蜜蜂在蜂巢周围嗡嗡地忙碌着。|例句：The room was buzzingly alive with conversation. 房间里充满了嗡嗡的谈话声，热闹非凡。|近义词：hummingly, droningly, vibratingly|反义词:quietly, silently|词性:副词

词源:
From buzz + -ing + -ly.  "Buzz" itself likely originates from imitative roots, reflecting the sound it describes.
