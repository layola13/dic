aardon: /ˈɑːrdən/ | n. | 热心，热情 | Ardor; eagerness; zeal.  A strong feeling of enthusiasm or passion. |  He approached the task with aardon. 他满怀热情地对待这项任务。 | 近义词: zeal, fervor, passion, enthusiasm | 反义词: apathy, indifference, coldness | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *arden, from Anglo-Norman arder, from Old French ardoir (“to burn”), from Latin ardēre (“to burn, be on fire”).  Related to ardent.
