malleably:/ˈmæliəbli/ | adv. | 可锻地，可延展地，易适应地 |  In a malleable manner; capable of being shaped or formed, easily influenced or adaptable. | The clay was malleably shaped into a vase.  这块黏土被塑造成一个花瓶。| 近义词: pliably, flexibly, adaptably | 反义词: inflexibly, rigidly, unadaptably | 词性: 副词

词源: From Late Latin *malleabilis, from Latin malleus "hammer" + -abilis "-able."
