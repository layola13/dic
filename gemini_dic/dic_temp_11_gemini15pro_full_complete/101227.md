invitatory:/ɪnˈvɪtətɔːri/ /ɪnˈvaɪtətɔːri/
adjective|邀请的；祈祷的；开始的|Serving to invite; pertaining to an invitation; introductory.  Relating to or of the nature of an invitation, especially to a religious service or to prayer.  Used to introduce or begin something.|The bishop delivered the invitatory remarks before the service began. 主教在仪式开始前发表了邀请致辞。|
近义词: introductory, opening, preliminary, inviting
反义词: concluding, closing, valedictory
词性: adjective

invitatory:/ɪnˈvɪtətɔːri/ /ɪnˈvaɪtətɔːri/
noun|邀请；祷文；开场白|An invitation, especially to a religious service or to prayer.  A psalm or antiphon sung or recited at the beginning of matins, especially in the Anglican church.|The invitatory to the evening prayer was a beautiful chant. 晚祷的祷文是一段优美的圣歌。|
近义词: invitation, summons, call, opening
反义词: dismissal, farewell
词性: noun
