overhaughtiness:/ˌoʊvərˈhɔːtinəs/| n.|傲慢，自大，盛气凌人|The state of being excessively proud and arrogant, considering oneself superior to others.|Her overhaughtiness alienated her from her colleagues. 她的傲慢使她疏远了同事。|近义词：arrogance, haughtiness, pride, superciliousness, disdain|反义词：humility, modesty, humbleness|词性:名词

词源:
From over + haughtiness.  Haughtiness comes from Middle English *hauteinesse*, from Old French *hautaineté*, from *hautain* "haughty," from Vulgar Latin *altanus* "high," from Latin *altus*.
