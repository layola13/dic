appreciatingly:/əˈpriːʃieɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 感激地，欣赏地 | In a way that shows gratitude or pleasure; with appreciation. | She smiled appreciatively at his compliment. 她感激地对他赞美的话报以微笑。| Synonyms: gratefully, thankfully, admiringly | Antonyms: ungratefully, unappreciatively | adverb

appreciate:/əˈpriːʃieɪt/ | v. | 欣赏；感激；理解；增值 | 1. Recognize the full worth of. 2. Be grateful for (something). 3. Understand (a situation) fully; grasp the full implications of. 4. Rise in value or price. | I appreciate your help. 我感谢你的帮助. | Synonyms: value, cherish, acknowledge, admire, be grateful for, be thankful for, understand, realize, recognize, comprehend. | Antonyms: depreciate,  criticize, condemn, undervalue, dislike, resent. | verb
