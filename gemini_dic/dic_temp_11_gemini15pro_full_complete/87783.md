hibernate:/ˈhaɪbərneɪt/ (US), /ˈhaɪbəneɪt/ (UK)| v. |冬眠，蛰伏，过冬|To spend the winter in a dormant state; to remain inactive, especially during the winter.|Bears hibernate during the winter. 熊在冬天冬眠。|The software hibernates to save power.  这个软件会休眠以节省电源。|近义词：sleep, slumber, overwinter|反义词: awaken, revive|词性:动词
