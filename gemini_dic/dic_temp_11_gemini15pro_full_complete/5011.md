all-better:/ɔːlˈbɛtər/
| adj. |痊愈的，好了的|Informal term meaning recovered from an illness or injury.  Used especially with children.|例句：Mommy kissed my boo-boo and now it's all-better. 妈妈亲了我的伤口，现在好了。|例句：Don't worry, your scraped knee will be all-better soon. 别担心，你擦伤的膝盖很快就会好的。|近义词：well, recovered, healed, cured|反义词:sick, ill, injured, hurt|词性:adjective
词源:Compound of "all" and "better".  "All" in this context implies completely, and "better" refers to an improved state of health.
