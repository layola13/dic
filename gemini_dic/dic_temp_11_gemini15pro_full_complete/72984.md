foolify:/ˈfuːlɪfaɪ/| vt.|愚弄，欺骗|To make someone look or feel foolish; to trick or deceive someone.|He foolified her with his ridiculous stories. 他用他荒谬的故事愚弄了她。|近义词：deceive, trick, dupe, hoodwink|反义词: enlighten, educate|verb

foolify:/ˈfuːlɪfaɪ/| vt.|使…显得愚蠢|To cause someone to appear foolish or silly.|Her attempt to lie only foolified her further. 她试图撒谎，结果让自己显得更加愚蠢。|近义词：discredit, embarrass|反义词:dignify, honor|verb
