confessions: /kənˈfɛʃənz/ (US), /kənˈfɛʃnz/ (UK)| n.| 忏悔; 坦白; 供认; 招供; (对信仰、感情等的) 表白; | a formal statement admitting that one is guilty of a crime:  He made a full confession to the police. 他向警方作了完整的供述。|例句：His confessions shocked the nation. 他的忏悔震惊了全国。
例句：The priest heard the confessions of his parishioners. 神父聆听了教区居民的忏悔。
|近义词：admissions, acknowledgements, disclosures, avowals|反义词：denials, concealments|词性:名词
