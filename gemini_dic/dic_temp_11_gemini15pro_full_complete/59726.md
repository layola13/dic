ecliptic: /ɪˈklɪptɪk/ | n. | 黄道 | The apparent path of the Sun on the celestial sphere as seen from the Earth.  It is the plane of the Earth's orbit around the Sun. | The planets in our solar system all orbit roughly on the same plane as Earth, close to the ecliptic.  我们的太阳系中的行星都大致在地球所在的平面上运行，靠近黄道。 | The moon's orbit is tilted with respect to the ecliptic. 月球轨道相对于黄道是倾斜的。 | celestial sphere, apparent path of the sun |  | noun
