clingers:/ˈklɪŋərz/| n.|依附者，紧抓不放的人（或物）; 黏着物|People or things that cling; something that adheres tightly.|The wet clothes were clingers and uncomfortable against his skin. 湿衣服紧紧贴在他的皮肤上，让他感到不舒服。|近义词：adherents, followers, hangers-on|反义词:leavers, deserters|noun

clingers:/ˈklɪŋərz/| n.|（尤指电影或电视节目中的）续集，额外内容|sequels, especially in films or television programmes.|The movie had two clingers that were not as good as the original. 这部电影有两个续集，都不如原版好。|近义词：sequels, follow-ups, continuations|反义词:prequels, originals|noun
