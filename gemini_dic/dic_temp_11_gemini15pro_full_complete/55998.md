do-a-number-on:/duː ə ˈnʌmbər ɑːn/
verb phrase
严重损害；使…遭受重创
To inflict serious damage or harm on someone or something, either physically or emotionally.
The hurricane did a number on the coastal towns.  飓风对沿海城镇造成了严重破坏。
Stress can really do a number on your health. 压力真的会严重损害你的健康。
近义词: damage, harm, injure, wreck, ruin, devastate
反义词: repair, fix, heal, improve
词性: verb phrase
