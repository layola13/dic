gloriously:/ˈɡlɔːriəsli/ /ˈɡlɔːr.i.əs.li/ | adv. | 光荣地；辉煌地；壮丽地 | in a glorious manner; splendidly | The sun set gloriously. 太阳辉煌地落下了。| Synonym: magnificently, splendidly, triumphantly | Antonym: ignominiously, shamefully, disgracefully |词性:副词

词源:
From Middle English *gloriously, from Old French glorieusement, from Latin gloriōsus (“glorious”) + -ment.  Ultimately from Latin glōria (“glory”).
