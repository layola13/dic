bulbiferous:/ˌbʌlˈbɪfərəs/
adjective
生球茎的，产生球茎的
Bearing or producing bulbs.
例句：
The bulbiferous plant propagated quickly through its numerous bulbs.  这种生球茎的植物通过其众多的球茎迅速繁殖。

Some lilies are bulbiferous, developing small bulbs in their leaf axils. 一些百合是生球茎的，在它们的叶腋处会长出小鳞茎。

近义词：
bulbing
proliferous

反义词：
(No direct antonym, but one could consider)
sterile


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *bulbifer*, from bulbus (“bulb”) + -fer (“bearing”).
