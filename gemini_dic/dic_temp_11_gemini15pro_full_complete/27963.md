cantoris:/kænˈtɔːrɪs/| adj.|左侧的，唱诗班席的|Of or pertaining to the north side of a church, especially the north side of the choir.  It is derived from the Latin word "cantor," meaning "singer."  Traditionally, the cantoris side was where the main body of the choir sat. |The cantoris side of the cathedral housed the organ.  大教堂的左侧安放着管风琴。|近义词：north, left (in a church context)|反义词：decani|词性:形容词
