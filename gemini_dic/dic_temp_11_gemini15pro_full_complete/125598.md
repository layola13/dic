multivagous:/ˌmʌltɪˈveɪɡəs/| adj.|多食性的，杂食性的|Feeding on a wide variety of food sources.|例句：Multivagous insects can be beneficial in controlling pest populations. 多食性昆虫在控制害虫数量方面是有益的。

例句：The multivagous diet of the bear allows it to thrive in various environments. 熊的多食性饮食使它能够在不同的环境中茁壮成长。|近义词：polyphagous, omnivorous|反义词：monophagous, oligophagous|词性: 形容词

词源: multi- (many) + -vagous (wandering, roving in search of food)  From Latin *multivagus, from multus (many) + vagus (wandering).
