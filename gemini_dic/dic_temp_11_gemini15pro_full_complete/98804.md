integumentation:/ɪnˌtɛɡjʊmɛnˈteɪʃən/
n.
外皮形成，被覆，覆盖
the process of forming or providing a natural covering, like skin, shell, or rind.
The integumentation of the seed protects the embryo within. 种子的外皮保护着里面的胚胎。
covering, skin, rind, shell
uncovering, exposure

词性: noun
