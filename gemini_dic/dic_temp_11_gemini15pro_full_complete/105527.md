kilovoltage:/ˌkɪləˈvəʊltɪdʒ/ /ˌkɪləˈvoʊltɪdʒ/| n.|千伏(特)|The kilovoltage of an x-ray machine determines the penetrating power of the x-rays.  X光机的千伏数决定了X射线的穿透力。|
kilovoltage:|(electrical) expressed in kilovolts.|The radiograph was taken at a kilovoltage of 120. 这张射线照片是在120千伏的电压下拍摄的。|
kilovoltage:|近义词: voltage, potential difference|反义词: (no direct antonym, perhaps low voltage)|名词
kilovoltage:|词源: kilo- (thousand) + voltage|
