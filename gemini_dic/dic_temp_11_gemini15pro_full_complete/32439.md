chimerical:/kɪˈmɛrɪkəl/ /kaɪˈmɛrɪkəl/
adjective
空想的；虚幻的；妄想的
Existing only as an idea, and not likely to become real.  Relating to or being a chimera.
例句：His plans to build a castle were entirely chimerical. 他建造城堡的计划完全是异想天开。

例句：She chased a chimerical dream of becoming a famous actress. 她追逐着成为著名女演员的虚幻梦想。

近义词：illusory, fanciful, imaginary, unreal, visionary, quixotic
反义词：realistic, practical, achievable, attainable, real
词性: adjective
