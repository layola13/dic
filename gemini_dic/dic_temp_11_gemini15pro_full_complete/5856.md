amazonian:/ˌæməˈzoʊniən/| adj. n.|亚马逊的；女战士|Relating to or characteristic of the Amazon River or its basin; resembling an Amazon.  A tall, strong, masculine woman. |例句：She's an amazonian woman, both physically and mentally. 她是一位内外兼修的女强人。

例句：The amazonian rainforest is a vast and diverse ecosystem. 亚马逊雨林是一个广阔而多样的生态系统。|近义词：powerful, strong, immense (for rainforest), warrior-like |反义词:weak, small, delicate|adjective, noun


amazonian:/ˌæməˈzoʊniən/| n.|亚马逊人；女战士|A member of a legendary race of female warriors believed by the ancient Greeks to exist in Scythia or elsewhere along the shores of the Black Sea.  A tall, strong, masculine woman. |例句：She competed like a true Amazonian. 她像个真正的女战士一样比赛。

例句：In Greek mythology, Amazonians were fierce warriors. 在希腊神话中，亚马逊人是凶猛的战士。|近义词：warrior, fighter |反义词:civilian, pacifist|noun
