hippyish:/ˈhɪpiɪʃ/| adj.|嬉皮士风格的，嬉皮士气的|Resembling or characteristic of a hippy, especially in dress, lifestyle, or attitudes.  Having an unconventional or carefree appearance or attitude reminiscent of the hippie movement.|She had a hippyish look with her long flowing skirt and beaded necklace. 她穿着飘逸的长裙，戴着串珠项链，看起来很有嬉皮士风格。|Synonyms: bohemian, unconventional, free-spirited, alternative, countercultural|Antonyms: conventional, traditional, conformist, mainstream|Adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From hippy + -ish.  "Hippy" originated in the 1960s as a term for members of the counterculture movement who embraced peace, love, and unconventional lifestyles. The suffix "-ish" is added to adjectives or nouns to form adjectives indicating resemblance or characteristic of.
