analogues:/ˈænəlɒɡz/ (UK) /ˈænəlɔːɡz/ (US)| n.|类似物；相似体；对等物|Something that is similar or comparable to something else.|Researchers are developing analogues of human hormones.|近义词：equivalents, counterparts, parallels|反义词：opposites, dissimilars|词性:noun (plural)


analogue:/ˈænəlɒɡ/ (UK) /ˈænəlɔːɡ/ (US)| n. adj.|类似物；相似体；对等物；模拟的|Something that is similar or comparable to something else; relating to or using signals or information represented by a continuously variable physical quantity such as spatial position or voltage.|The research uses an animal model as an analogue for human depression. / This is an analogue recording, not digital.|近义词：equivalent, counterpart, parallel, similar|反义词：opposite, dissimilar, digital|词性:noun, adjective


词源: From the Greek word "analogos" meaning "proportionate".
