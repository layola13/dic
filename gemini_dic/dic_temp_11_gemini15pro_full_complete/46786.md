danglers:/ˈdæŋɡlərz/
n.
悬挂物；悬荡物
something that hangs or dangles
The crystal danglers on the chandelier shimmered in the light. 枝形吊灯上的水晶垂饰在灯光下闪闪发光。

Dangling participles are a common grammatical error.  悬垂分词是一种常见的语法错误。

dangling modifiers
悬垂修饰语
The modifier "drenched in sweat" dangles because it doesn't clearly modify the subject of the sentence.  修饰语“汗流浃背”是悬垂的，因为它没有明确地修饰句子的主语。

近义词:pendants, ornaments
无明确反义词
词性:名词 (plural)
