iconic:/aɪˈkɒnɪk/ (US) /aɪˈkɑːnɪk/| adj.|标志性的；象征性的；偶像的|Relating to or of the nature of an icon; widely recognized and representative of a particular person, place, or thing.|The Eiffel Tower is an iconic landmark in Paris. 埃菲尔铁塔是巴黎的标志性地标。|synonyms: symbolic, representative, emblematic, classic|antonyms: obscure, unknown, atypical|adjective

iconic:/aɪˈkɒnɪk/ (US) /aɪˈkɑːnɪk/| adj.|标志性的；象征性的；偶像的|Widely recognized and admired, especially for distinctive qualities.|Marilyn Monroe is an iconic figure in American culture. 玛丽莲·梦露是美国文化中的标志性人物。|synonyms: legendary, famous, celebrated|antonyms: unremarkable, ordinary, obscure|adjective
