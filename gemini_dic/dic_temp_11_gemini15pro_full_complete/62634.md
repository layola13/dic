enforcive:/ɪnˈfɔːrsɪv/ | adj. |强制的，强行的| Having the power to compel obedience or compliance.  Relating to enforcement.  Although "enforcive" is grammatically correct, it's rarely used. "Enforceable" is much more common. | The government passed an enforcive law regarding pollution control. 政府通过了一项关于污染控制的强制性法律。|  The new regulations are enforcive immediately. 新规章立即生效。| Synonyms: compulsory, mandatory, obligatory, binding | Antonyms: optional, voluntary, discretionary | Word type: Adjective
