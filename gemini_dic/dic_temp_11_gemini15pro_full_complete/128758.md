niggle:/ˈnɪɡəl/| v.|烦恼；困扰；挑剔；吹毛求疵|To worry or irritate someone slightly, or to cause slight but persistent discomfort, physically or mentally.|The constant dripping of the faucet niggled at her. 水龙头不停地滴水让她很烦。|Synonyms: bother, irritate, annoy, vex, irk, pester, trouble|Antonyms: soothe, comfort, please|verb

niggle:/ˈnɪɡəl/| n.|轻微的抱怨或批评；小问题；小麻烦|A slight worry or criticism, or a small problem or difficulty.|He had a few niggles about the contract. 他对合同有一些小问题。|Synonyms: quibble, complaint, criticism, objection, grievance|Antonyms: praise, compliment|noun
