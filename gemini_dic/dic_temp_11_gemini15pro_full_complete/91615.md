hyperarousal:/ˌhaɪpərəˈraʊzl/ | n. |过度 возбуждение|A state of increased physiological and psychological tension characterized by heightened sensitivity to stimuli,  difficulty relaxing, and exaggerated responses to perceived threats.| After the accident, she experienced hyperarousal, including difficulty sleeping and being easily startled. 事故发生后，她经历了过度兴奋，包括难以入睡和容易受惊吓。| 近义词: hypersensitivity, overstimulation | 反义词: calmness, relaxation | 词性: noun

hyperarousal:/ˌhaɪpərəˈraʊzl/ | adj. |过度兴奋的|Characterized by hyperarousal; excessively or abnormally alert and responsive to stimuli.| The hyperarousal symptoms made it difficult for him to concentrate. 过度兴奋的症状使他难以集中注意力。| 近义词: overstimulated, hypersensitive | 反义词: calm, relaxed | 词性: adjective


词源:  "Hyper-" (Greek: over, above) + "arousal" (English: the act of arousing or being aroused)
