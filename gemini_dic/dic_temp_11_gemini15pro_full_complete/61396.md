emblematist: /ˈembləmətɪst/
noun
纹章学家，纹章设计者
A person who designs or studies emblems or coats of arms.
The emblematist carefully researched the family history before designing their crest.  纹章学家在设计他们的家徽之前仔细研究了家族史。

The museum hired an emblematist to catalog its collection of medieval seals.  博物馆聘请了一位纹章学家来编目其中世纪印章的收藏。
近义词: heraldist, armorist
反义词: N/A
词性: n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From emblem + -ist.  Emblem from French emblème, from Latin emblema, from Greek émblema "inlaid ornament," from embállein "to throw in, insert."
