cataphracted: /ˈkætəˌfræktɪd/
adjective
披甲的，重甲的
Covered with armor or scales; heavily armored.
The cataphracted knight rode into battle.  披甲的骑士骑马出战。
The ancient Roman army utilized cataphracted cavalry. 古罗马军队使用重甲骑兵。
近义词: armored, mailed, shielded
反义词: unarmored, unprotected
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *cataphractus, from Greek κατάφρακτος (kataphraktos, “completely enclosed, clad in mail”), from κατά (kata, “down, completely”) + φρακτός (phraktos, “enclosed, protected”), from φράσσω (phrassō, “I enclose, protect”).
