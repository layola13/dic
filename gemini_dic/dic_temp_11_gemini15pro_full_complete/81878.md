grieve:/ɡriːv/| v.|悲伤，悲痛，感到悲伤|To feel intense sorrow or distress, especially at the death of someone or the loss of something.|She grieved deeply for her lost child. 她为失去的孩子感到深深的悲痛。|Synonyms: mourn, sorrow, lament|Antonyms: rejoice, celebrate|verb

grieve:/ɡriːv/| n.(罕见)|悲伤，悲痛|Deep sorrow, especially that caused by someone's death.|His heart was filled with grieve. 他的心中充满了悲伤。|Synonyms: sorrow, grief, sadness|Antonyms: joy, happiness|noun
