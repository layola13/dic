nonexpedient:/ˌnɑːnɪkˈspiːdiənt/| adj.|不明智的，不合适的，不方便的|Not advisable or suitable; not practical or convenient.  It implies that something is disadvantageous or unwise under the current circumstances, often because it would be ineffective or counterproductive.|例句：It was deemed nonexpedient to raise taxes at that time. 当时认为加税是不明智的。

例句：Given the current economic climate, expanding the business would be nonexpedient. 鉴于目前的经济环境，扩大业务是不合适的。|近义词：inadvisable, inappropriate, unsuitable, impractical, unwise, impolitic|反义词: advisable, appropriate, suitable, practical, wise, expedient|词性:adjective
