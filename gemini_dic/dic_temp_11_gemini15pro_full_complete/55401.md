distensive:/dɪˈstɛnsɪv/
adjective
膨胀的，扩张的
causing or tending to cause distension.
例句：The distensive effects of gas in the intestines can cause discomfort.  肠道内气体的膨胀作用会引起不适。

例句：A distensive force was applied to the balloon.  对气球施加了膨胀力。

近义词：expansive, swelling, dilating
反义词：contractive, constrictive, compressing
词性: adjective
