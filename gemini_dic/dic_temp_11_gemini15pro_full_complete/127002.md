nasalizes:/ˈneɪzəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使鼻音化；用鼻音发音
To make a sound nasal by lowering the soft palate so that air passes through the nose.
He nasalizes his vowels. 他用鼻音发他的元音。
近义词: nasalise
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "oralize")
词性: verb


nasalizing:/ˈneɪzəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使鼻音化；用鼻音发音 (present participle)
Making a sound nasal by lowering the soft palate so that air passes through the nose.
She is nasalizing the "n" sound.  她正在用鼻音发出“n”音。
近义词: nasalising
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: verb


nasalized:/ˈneɪzəlaɪzd/
adjective, verb (past tense)
鼻音化的；用鼻音发音的
Pronounced with air passing through the nose. / Made a sound nasal.
The singer's voice was highly nasalized.  这位歌手的声音鼻音很重。 / The singer nasalized the final vowel. 歌手用鼻音发出了最后的元音。
近义词: nasalised
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: adjective, verb
