conceding:/kənˈsiːdɪŋ/
verb
承认；让步
admitting or yielding something
He is conceding defeat. 他正在承认失败。
The government conceded to the protestors' demands. 政府对抗议者的要求做出了让步。
synonyms: admitting, acknowledging, granting, yielding, accepting
antonyms: denying, refusing, rejecting
v.
