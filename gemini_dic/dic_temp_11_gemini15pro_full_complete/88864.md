hoisin:/ˈhɔɪsɪn/ (US), /ˈhɔɪsɪn/ (UK)| n. |海鲜酱，海仙酱|A thick, dark, reddish-brown sauce made from soybeans, sugar, vinegar, garlic, and spices, commonly used in Chinese cooking.|例句：I marinated the pork in hoisin sauce before grilling. 我在烤猪肉之前先用海鲜酱腌制。

例句：The duck was served with pancakes and hoisin sauce.  这鸭肉配薄饼和海鲜酱一起食用。

|近义词：Chinese barbecue sauce, plum sauce|反义词: None|词性: Noun
