galaxy: /ˈɡæləksi/ (US) /ˈɡæləksi/ (UK)| n.|星系，银河系|A system of millions or billions of stars, together with gas and dust, held together by gravitational attraction.|Our galaxy, the Milky Way, contains billions of stars. 我们的星系，银河系，包含数十亿颗恒星。|The Andromeda Galaxy is the closest major galaxy to our own. 仙女座星系是我们最近的主要星系。|近义词：cosmos, universe|反义词: (none - a galaxy is a fundamental astronomical structure with no direct opposite)|词性: noun
