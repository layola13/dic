decompresses:/ˌdiːkəmˈprɛsɪz/
| v. |减压，解压|To release from pressure, literally or figuratively.  To return to normal atmospheric pressure after being subjected to lower pressure, as in deep-sea diving or space travel. To relax and relieve stress. |After a long day at work, she decompresses by listening to music. 下班后，她通过听音乐来放松减压。|Synonyms: relax, unwind, de-stress, expand|Antonyms: compress, stress, tense|verb
