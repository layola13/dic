carriageway:/ˈkærɪdʒweɪ/
n.
车行道，行车道
The part of a road intended for vehicles, not pedestrians.
Drive carefully on the carriageway.  小心在行车道上驾驶。
road, roadway, highway, street, thoroughfare
pavement, sidewalk, footpath
