couchless:/ˈkaʊtʃləs/
adjective
无沙发可躺的; 没有沙发的
Without a couch; lacking a couch.  This word describes a state of being without a couch, either temporarily or permanently.
I've been couchless since I moved, and my back is killing me.  我自从搬家后就一直没有沙发，我的背疼死了。
My apartment is so small, it's practically couchless. 我的公寓太小了，几乎放不下沙发。
近义词:sofa-less
反义词:furnished (with a couch)
词性:adjective


couchless:/ˈkaʊtʃləs/
adjective
(figuratively) Homeless; without a permanent residence. 比喻：无家可归的；没有固定住所的
This describes a state of homelessness, using "couch" to represent a stable home environment.
He's been couchless for months, staying with friends whenever he can. 他已经无家可归好几个月了，尽可能地和朋友们住在一起。
The economic downturn has left many people couchless and struggling to survive. 经济衰退使许多人无家可归，难以生存。
近义词:homeless, displaced
反义词:housed, sheltered
词性:adjective


词源:
"Couch" (from Old French "coucher," meaning "to lie down") + "-less" (a suffix indicating absence or lack).  "Couch" (来自古法语 "coucher"，意思是“躺下”) + "-less"（一个表示缺失或缺乏的后缀）。
