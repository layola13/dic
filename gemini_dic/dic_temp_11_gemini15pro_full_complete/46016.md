cyberpunk: /ˈsaɪbərˌpʌŋk/ | n. adj.| 网络朋克 | A subgenre of science fiction set in a dystopian future characterized by advanced technology and social decay.  Often features a blend of high tech and low life, with themes of cybernetics, artificial intelligence, and corporate control. | The movie "Blade Runner" is a classic example of cyberpunk.  《银翼杀手》是网络朋克的经典例子。 |  Cyberpunk 2077 is a popular video game. 《赛博朋克2077》是一款很受欢迎的电子游戏。 |  近义词: futuristic, dystopian | 反义词: utopian | 词性: 名词, 形容词
