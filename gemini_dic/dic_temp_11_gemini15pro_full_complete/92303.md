hypnocyst: /ˈhɪpnəsɪst/
n.
催眠状态；催眠包囊;催眠囊肿
A state resembling sleep induced by hypnosis. A cyst-like structure containing dormant embryos, as in certain invertebrates.
The patient was in a deep hypnocyst.  病人处于深度催眠状态。
The bryozoan's hypnocyst protects its embryos during unfavorable conditions.  苔藓虫的催眠囊肿在不利条件下保护其胚胎。
近义词: trance, hypnotic state
反义词: wakefulness, alertness
词性: noun


Etymology:
From hypno- (Greek hypnos, "sleep") + cyst (Greek kystis, "bladder, pouch").
