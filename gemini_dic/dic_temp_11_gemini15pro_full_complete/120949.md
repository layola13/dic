mischanceful:/mɪsˈtʃænsfl/
adjective
不幸的;充满危险的
Unlucky; characterized by misfortune.
It was a mischanceful journey, plagued by delays and bad weather. 这是一次不幸的旅程，充满了延误和恶劣天气。
The mischanceful expedition met with disaster in the mountains.  这支不幸的探险队在山里遭遇了灾难。
近义词:unlucky, unfortunate, ill-fated, disastrous
反义词:lucky, fortunate, auspicious


词性:adjective

词源:  From mis- + chance + -ful.  "Mis-" is a prefix indicating something bad or wrong. "Chance" refers to luck or fortune (often bad). "-ful" is a suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by."  Therefore, "mischanceful" literally means "full of bad luck."
