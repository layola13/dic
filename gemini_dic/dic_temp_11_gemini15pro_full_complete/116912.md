mayor:/ˈmeɪər/ | n. | 市长 | The chief executive officer of a city or town. | The mayor announced a new initiative to improve public transportation. 市长宣布了一项改善公共交通的新举措。 | 近义词: city manager, chief magistrate | 反义词: (无直接反义词) | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Old French *maire, from Latin *major, comparative of magnus "great".
