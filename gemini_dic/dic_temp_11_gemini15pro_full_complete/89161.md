homeling:/ˈhoʊmlɪŋ/| n.|归巢，归家; 归巢的动物|A homing instinct or the act of returning home.  The word emphasizes the process of returning home, often guided by instinct.|The homing pigeon made its way back to the loft.  这只信鸽回到了鸽舍。|近义词： homecoming, return|反义词: departure, leaving|词性:名词

词源: "home" + "-ling" (a suffix denoting smallness or affection, but in this case, it indicates the act or process).  From "home" meaning one's residence, and the suffix "-ling".
