locum: /ˈləʊkəm/ (British), /ˈloʊkəm/ (American) | n. | 代替者；替补者；临时医生 | a person who stands in temporarily for someone else; a doctor who stands in temporarily for another doctor | Dr. Smith is away on holiday, so we have a locum covering his patients. 史密斯医生休假去了，所以我们请了一位替补医生来照顾他的病人。| synonyms: substitute, replacement, temporary, fill-in | antonyms: permanent, full-time | noun

locum: /ˈləʊkəm/ (British), /ˈloʊkəm/ (American) | adj. | 代理的；替补的 | acting as a temporary substitute |  She's working as a locum GP. 她在做代理全科医生。 | synonyms: acting, temporary, substitute, interim | antonyms: permanent, full-time | adjective
