estuarine:/ˈɛstʃʊəˌraɪn/ | adj. | 河口的，江口的 | Relating to or formed in an estuary, the tidal mouth of a large river, where the tide meets the stream. | The estuarine environment is rich in biodiversity.  河口环境生物多样性丰富。|  The researchers are studying the estuarine ecosystem. 研究人员正在研究河口生态系统。 | 近义词: coastal, littoral | 反义词: pelagic, oceanic | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *aestuarium* "tidal part of a shore, marsh," from Latin *aestus* "tide, heat, surge, boiling."
