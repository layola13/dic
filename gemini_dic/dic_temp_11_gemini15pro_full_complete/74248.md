foxhole:/ˈfɑːkshəʊl/ (UK) /ˈfɑːksˌhoʊl/ (US)| n. |散兵坑，掩体|A hole dug in the ground as a shelter for one or two soldiers from enemy gunfire or shelling.|The soldiers huddled in their foxhole, waiting for the barrage to end. 士兵们挤在散兵坑里，等待炮火停止。

The sniper fired from his concealed foxhole. 狙击手从他隐蔽的散兵坑里开火。|近义词：trench, dugout, bunker, earthwork|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "exposed position")|词性:noun
