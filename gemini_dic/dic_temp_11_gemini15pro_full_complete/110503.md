limbas:/ˈlɪmbəs/ | n. | 边缘，界限；地狱边境 |  A region on the border of hell or heaven | The souls of the unbaptized were thought to reside in Limbo. | 近义词: edge, fringe, periphery, brink | 反义词: center, core | 词性: 名词

词源: From Latin limbus “border, fringe, hem”.

例句：
The project was in limbo for months.
这个项目搁置了好几个月。

His career was in limbo while he recovered from surgery.
在他手术恢复期间，他的事业处于停滞状态。
