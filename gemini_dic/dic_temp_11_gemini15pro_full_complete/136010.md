nonrationally:/ˌnɒnˈræʃənəli/
adverb
非理性地
Not based on, guided by, or employing reason.
He acted nonrationally, driven by pure emotion.  他非理性地行事，完全受情绪驱使。
She made a nonrationally decision to quit her job without another offer. 她做出了一个不理智的决定，在没有其他工作机会的情况下辞掉了工作。
Synonyms: irrationally, unreasonably, illogically
Antonyms: rationally, reasonably, logically
词性: 副词
