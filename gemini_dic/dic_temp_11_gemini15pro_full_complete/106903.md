laceration:/ˌlæsəˈreɪʃn/| n. | 伤口；撕裂；（感情上的）伤害 | A rough tear or cut in the flesh; a wound; a painful emotional experience. | The surgeon stitched the deep laceration on his leg.  外科医生缝合了他腿上的深度撕裂伤。

His harsh words caused a deep laceration in their friendship. 他尖刻的话语深深地伤害了他们的友谊。| 近义词: cut, gash, wound, injury, trauma | 反义词: healing, recovery | 词性: 名词


词源:  From Latin lacerare "to tear to pieces, mangle," from lacer "torn, mangled."
