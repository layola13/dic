overambitious: /ˌoʊvəræmˈbɪʃəs/ | adj. | 过于雄心勃勃的；野心过大的 | Having or showing too much ambition. |  He had slightly overambitious plans for his small company. 他为他那家小公司制定了有点过于雄心勃勃的计划。 | overzealous, aspiring, ambitious | modest, unambitious | adjective

Etymology:
From over- + ambitious.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," "too much," or "beyond." "Ambitious" comes from the Latin word "ambitio," meaning "a going around," particularly to solicit votes.  This evolved to mean a strong desire for advancement or success.
