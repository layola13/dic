overaccelerate:/ˌoʊvərəkˈsɛləˌreɪt/
verb
过度加速；加速过快
To accelerate beyond a safe or desirable limit; to accelerate too quickly.
The driver overaccelerated and lost control of the vehicle. 司机过度加速，失去了对车辆的控制。
The economy overaccelerated, leading to inflation. 经济加速过快，导致了通货膨胀。
synonyms: accelerate excessively, speed up too much
antonyms: decelerate, slow down
词性: 动词
