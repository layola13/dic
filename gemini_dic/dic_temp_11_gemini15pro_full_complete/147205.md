overmanaged:/ˌoʊvərˈmænɪdʒd/ | adj. | 过度管理的；管理过度的 |  Managed excessively or inefficiently, often with too much control and interference.  | The company became overmanaged and inflexible, leading to a decline in innovation.  这家公司变得管理过度，缺乏灵活性，导致创新能力下降。| The project was overmanaged, with too many committees and approvals required.  这个项目管理过度，需要太多的委员会和批准。 | overadministered, overcontrolled | undermanaged, loosely managed | adjective
