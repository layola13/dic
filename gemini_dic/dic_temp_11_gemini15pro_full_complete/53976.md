discrete:/dɪˈskriːt/| adj.|离散的，分立的，不连续的|Individually separate and distinct; not continuous.|例句：The process involves a series of discrete steps. 这个过程包括一系列独立的步骤。|例句：Data is transmitted as discrete packets. 数据以离散的数据包的形式传输。|近义词：separate, distinct, individual, discontinuous|反义词：continuous, connected, merged|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin discretus, past participle of discernere "to separate, distinguish," from dis- "apart" + cernere "to sift, distinguish."
