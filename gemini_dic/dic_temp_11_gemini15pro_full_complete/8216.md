Example: Her anti-freudianism led her to explore alternative therapeutic approaches. 她的反弗洛伊德主义促使她探索其他治疗方法。 | 近义词: Freudian dissent,  opposition to psychoanalysis | 反义词: Freudianism, psychoanalysis | 词性: noun
