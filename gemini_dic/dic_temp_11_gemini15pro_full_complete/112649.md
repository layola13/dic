lowbred:/ˈloʊbrɛd/| adj.|低劣的，粗野的，出身卑贱的|Of low or common origin; not of good breeding; vulgar; coarse.  Having qualities (such as rudeness or coarseness) associated with people of low social status.|He showed his lowbred nature by spitting on the floor. 他在地板上吐痰，显露出他粗野的本性。|Synonyms: vulgar, coarse, unrefined, ill-bred, baseborn, plebeian|Antonyms: well-bred, refined, cultivated, aristocratic, noble|adjective
