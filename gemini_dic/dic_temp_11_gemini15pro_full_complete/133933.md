nonharmonic:/ˌnɒnhɑːrˈmɒnɪk/ /ˌnɑːnhɑːrˈmɑːnɪk/| adj.|非谐波的，不和谐的|Not harmonic; lacking harmony; discordant.|The nonharmonic sounds grated on my ears.  非和谐的声音刺激着我的耳朵。|The engine made a loud, nonharmonic noise. 引擎发出巨大的、不和谐的噪音。|近义词：inharmonic, discordant, dissonant|反义词：harmonic, consonant, harmonious|词性:形容词
Etymology: non- + harmonic.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "harmonic" refers to sounds that are pleasing and in agreement.
