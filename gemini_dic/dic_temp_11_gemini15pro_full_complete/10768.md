aqualung:/ˈækwəlʌŋ/ | n. | 水肺;水中呼吸器 | A device used for breathing underwater, consisting of a tank of compressed air strapped to the diver's back and connected by a hose to a mouthpiece. |  He explored the coral reef with an aqualung. 他用水肺探索了珊瑚礁。 |  scuba, diving apparatus |  -- | noun
