distressful:/dɪˈstrɛsfʊl/
adjective
令人痛苦的；令人忧虑的
Causing or characterized by emotional or physical distress.
The news of her illness was deeply distressful. 她生病的消息令人非常痛苦。
It was a distressful time for everyone involved. 对所有相关人员来说，那都是一段令人痛苦的时期。
近义词: upsetting, worrying, distressing, agonizing, harrowing
反义词: comforting, soothing, calming, reassuring
词性: adjective
