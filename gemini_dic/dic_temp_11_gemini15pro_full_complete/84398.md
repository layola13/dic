hallmark:/ˈhɔːlmɑːrk/ | n. |  品质证明；特征 | An official mark stamped on gold, silver, or platinum articles in England, guaranteeing their standard of purity. A distinctive characteristic, trait, or feature. |  Simplicity is a hallmark of this design. 简洁是这个设计的标志。 This exhibition bears all the hallmarks of a masterpiece.  这次展览具有所有杰作的特征。 | synonyms: characteristic, feature, trait, trademark, indication, sign, stamp, badge | antonyms: anomaly, exception | noun
