extradite:/ˈɛkstrəˌdaɪt/| vt.|引渡|To hand over (a person accused or convicted of a crime) to the jurisdiction of another country, state, etc.|The government refused to extradite the suspect to the United States. 政府拒绝将嫌疑犯引渡到美国。|Example 2: The country has agreed to extradite the fugitive back to his homeland.  这个国家同意将逃犯引渡回他的祖国。|Synonyms: deport, hand over, surrender|Antonyms: protect, harbor, shield|verb

PHRASE->
extradite to (引渡到): The suspect was extradited to the United States. 嫌疑人被引渡到美国。
extradite from (从...引渡): The suspect was extradited from Canada. 嫌疑人从加拿大被引渡。
extradition treaty (引渡条约): The two countries have an extradition treaty. 这两个国家之间有引渡条约。
extradition request (引渡请求): The government submitted an extradition request. 政府提交了引渡请求。
fight extradition (对抗引渡): The suspect is fighting extradition. 嫌疑人正在对抗引渡。
