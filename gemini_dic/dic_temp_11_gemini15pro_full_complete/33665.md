cinerescent:/ˌsɪnəˈrɛsənt/| adj.|灰白色的；变成灰白色的；灰烬状的|Covered with or resembling ashes; ashen.  Relating to, or containing ashes.  Of the color of ashes.|例句：The cinerescent moon hung low in the sky. 灰白色的月亮低垂在天空中。|例句：The cinerescent landscape after the fire was a chilling reminder of the destruction. 火灾后灰烬般的景象令人不寒而栗，提醒着人们这场破坏。|近义词：ashen, ash-colored, grayish, dusty|反义词：colorful, vibrant|词性:形容词
cineritious:/ˌsɪnəˈrɪʃəs/| adj.|灰白色的；灰烬状的|Similar to cinerescent, meaning ash-colored or resembling ashes.|例句：The cineritious clouds hinted at impending rain. 灰白色的云暗示着即将到来的雨。|例句：His cineritious complexion suggested he hadn't slept well. 他灰白的脸色表明他没睡好。|近义词：cinerescent, ashen, ash-grey|反义词：colorful, vibrant|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cinerescentem*, present participle of *cinerescere* "to turn to ashes," from *cinis* "ashes" + *-escere* (inceptive suffix).
