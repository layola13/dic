n.|礼节；正式手续；俗套|Conventional usages of behavior or etiquette; required or customary procedures; something done simply for form's sake.|We had to observe all the formalities of a traditional wedding. 我们必须遵守传统婚礼的所有礼节。|近义词：ceremonies, rituals, procedures, conventions, etiquette|反义词：informalities, casualness|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *formalité*, from Late Latin *formālitās*, from Latin *formālis* ("formal"), ultimately from *forma* ("form, shape").
