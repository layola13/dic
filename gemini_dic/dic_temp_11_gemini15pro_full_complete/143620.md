outjet:/ˈaʊtˌdʒɛt/
n.|喷出, 射出|The outjet of water from the fountain was spectacular. 喷泉喷出的水流蔚为壮观。|例句：The outjet of lava from the volcano was a terrifying sight. 火山喷出的熔岩是一个可怕的景象。|近义词：ejection, expulsion, discharge, emission|反义词：injection, intake, absorption|词性:名词

Etymology:  "out-" (prefix meaning "outward, from within") + "jet" (from Old French "jetter" - to throw, cast, gush) - implying a forceful outward projection.
