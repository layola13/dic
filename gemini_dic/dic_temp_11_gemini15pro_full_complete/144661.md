outwriggle: /ˌaʊtˈrɪɡəl/
词性: verb
中文翻译: 扭动着逃脱；比…更会扭动
英文解析: To escape or extricate oneself by wriggling; to wriggle more or better than.  It combines the prefix "out-" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) with the verb "wriggle" (meaning to twist and turn the body with quick movements).
例句：The fish outwriggled the fisherman's grasp and escaped back into the river.  鱼扭动着身体逃脱了渔夫的抓捕，回到了河里。

例句：The magician's assistant outwriggled the ropes and freed herself from the locked chest. 魔术师的助手扭动着身体挣脱了绳索，从锁着的箱子里逃了出来。

近义词: squirm, wiggle, writhe, escape
反义词: be captured, be trapped
