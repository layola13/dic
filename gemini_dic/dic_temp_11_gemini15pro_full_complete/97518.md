noun
嗉囊，食管下端的膨大部分
The ingluvies is a part of the bird's digestive system, an expanded portion of the esophagus used to store food temporarily.
Birds use their ingluvies to store food before it passes to the gizzard. 鸟类用嗉囊来储存食物，然后再将其输送到砂囊。
