boorish:/ˈbʊərɪʃ/ (US), /ˈbɔːrɪʃ/ (UK)| adj.|粗鲁的，粗野的，缺乏教养的|Resembling or characteristic of a boor; rude and insensitive; ill-mannered.  Lacking refinement or grace.|He displayed boorish behavior by loudly interrupting the conversation. 他粗鲁地打断谈话，表现得十分粗野。|Synonyms: rude, uncivil, uncouth, vulgar, loutish, churlish, crass, insensitive, ill-mannered, unrefined|Antonyms: refined, polite, courteous, sophisticated, cultured, gracious, civil|adjective
