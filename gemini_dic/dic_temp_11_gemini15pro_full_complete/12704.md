assertively:/əˈsɜːrtɪvli/
adverb|断然地，坚定地|In a manner that is confident and forceful, expressing one's opinions or desires strongly.|She assertively defended her position in the meeting. 她在会议上坚定地捍卫了自己的立场。|近义词：confidently, firmly, decisively|反义词：hesitantly, timidly, passively|词性:副词
词源: From assert + -ive + -ly.  Assert comes from Latin asserere "claim, maintain, affirm," from ad- "to" + serere "join, connect."
