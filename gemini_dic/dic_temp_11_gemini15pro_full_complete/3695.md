aggregateness:/ˈæɡrɪɡɪtnəs/| n.|集合性，总体性|The aggregateness of the data allows for a comprehensive analysis. 数据的总体性允许进行全面的分析。|The aggregateness of the individual scores determined the team's ranking. 个人分数的集合性决定了团队的排名。|近义词：totality, entirety, wholeness|反义词:individuality, separateness|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From aggregate + -ness.  Aggregate comes from Latin aggregatus, past participle of aggregare "to add to," from ad- "to" + greg- "flock, herd."  The suffix -ness forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
