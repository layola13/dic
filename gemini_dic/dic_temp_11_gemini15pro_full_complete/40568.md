contumelious:/ˌkɒntjʊˈmiːliəs/ /ˌkɑːntjʊˈmiːliəs/| adj.|傲慢无礼的，辱骂的|Insolently abusive and humiliating; scornful and insulting.|例句：His contumelious remarks about her family were deeply offensive. 他对她家人的无礼言论令人深感不快。

例句：The critic's contumelious review of the play angered the director.  评论家对这部戏剧的傲慢评论激怒了导演。


近义词：insolent, abusive, insulting, scornful, disdainful, contemptuous|反义词: respectful, polite, courteous, deferential|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin contumeliosus, from Latin contumelia ("abuse, insult, affront"),  ultimately from con- ("with, together") + tumēre ("to swell").  The original sense was "causing to swell," referring to the swelling of pride or anger.
