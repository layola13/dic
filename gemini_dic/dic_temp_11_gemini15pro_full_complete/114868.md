mammonish:/ˈmæmənɪʃ/
adjective
贪婪的；拜金的
Characterized by a devotion to material wealth; greedy.
He lived a mammonish lifestyle, always chasing the next big deal. 他过着拜金的生活，总是追逐着下一笔大买卖。
近义词: greedy, avaricious, materialistic, mercenary
反义词: generous, altruistic, charitable
词性: adjective

词源: From "Mammon," a personification of wealth regarded as an evil influence or false object of worship and devotion. Ultimately from Aramaic māmonā "wealth, riches."
