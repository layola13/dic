inexpressibly:/ˌɪnɪkˈsprɛsəbli/
adverb
无法形容地，难以表达地
Not capable of being expressed; too great or extreme to be expressed in words.
Her beauty was inexpressibly lovely. 她的美丽难以言喻。
Synonyms: indescribably, unutterably, ineffably
Antonyms: expressibly, describably
词性: 副词
