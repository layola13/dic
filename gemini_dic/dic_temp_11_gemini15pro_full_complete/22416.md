book-learned: /ˈbʊk lɜːrnd/ | adj. | 书本知识的，从书本上学来的 | Learned from books rather than experience; academic and not practical. | He was book-learned but lacked practical experience. 他学识渊博，但缺乏实践经验。|例句：His book-learned knowledge wasn't helpful in the real world. 他从书本上学来的知识在现实世界中并没有什么用。

例句：She had a book-learned understanding of economics, but had never worked in business. 她对经济学有书本上的理解，但从未在商界工作过。

近义词: academic, theoretical, scholarly | 反义词: practical, experienced, hands-on | 词性: adjective
