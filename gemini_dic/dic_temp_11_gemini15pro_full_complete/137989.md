noobs:/nuːbz/ | n. |菜鸟，新手| A term used to refer to inexperienced or unskilled players in a game or activity.  Derived from "newbie".  Often used derogatorily. | Those noobs kept dying in the first level.  那些菜鸟在第一关一直死。| 近义词: newbies, beginners, rookies | 反义词: experts, veterans, pros | noun
