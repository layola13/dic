munify:/ˈmjuːnɪfaɪ/| v. |筑防御工事，加强防御|To fortify, strengthen, or protect, especially with walls or fortifications.|The city council voted to munify the city center against potential attacks. 市议会投票决定加强市中心的防御以应对潜在的袭击。| The general ordered his troops to munify the hilltop. 将军命令他的部队加强山顶的防御工事。|近义词: fortify, strengthen, reinforce, secure, protect|反义词: weaken, undermine, expose|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *munificare “to fortify,” from Latin munis “wall” + -ficare (from facere “to make”).  Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European root *mey- “to build, construct.”
