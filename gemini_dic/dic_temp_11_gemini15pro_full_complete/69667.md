fencing:/ˈfɛnsɪŋ/ | n. | 击剑; 围栏; 剑术 | The act or sport of fighting with swords, especially foils, épées, or sabres, according to a set of rules. Also refers to the fences or other barriers enclosing an area. | He took up fencing in college.  他大学时开始学习击剑。 | The property was surrounded by a high fencing. 这处房产周围环绕着高高的围栏。 | 近义词: swordsmanship, barrier, enclosure | 反义词: openness | noun

fencing:/ˈfɛnsɪŋ/ | adj. | 围栏的; 击剑的 |  Relating to the sport of fencing or to fences. |  He’s a member of the fencing club. 他是击剑俱乐部的成员。| The fencing material was made of strong wood. 围栏材料是用坚固的木材制成的。 | 近义词: enclosing, barrier | 反义词: open | adjective
