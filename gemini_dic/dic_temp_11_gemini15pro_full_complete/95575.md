incombustible:/ˌɪnkəmˈbʌstəbl/
adjective
不可燃的; 不燃烧的
not capable of catching fire and burning
The firefighters' suits are made of incombustible materials. 消防员的制服是由不可燃材料制成的。
This building is constructed with incombustible bricks. 这栋建筑是用不燃砖建造的。
near synonyms: fireproof, fire-resistant, nonflammable, non-inflammable
antonyms: combustible, flammable, inflammable
adjective
