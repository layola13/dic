aut:/ɔːt/ | n. | increase, rise |  A rise or increase; especially : a gradual increase in force, intensity, or magnitude.  Derived from the Latin "auctus," the past participle of "augere" (to increase).  No longer commonly used as a standalone word in modern English.  It primarily survives in compounds like "auto-" (self) and as a root in words like "augment." |  (No longer used in modern English sentences in this form.) | 近义词: increase, rise, growth | 反义词: decrease, decline, fall | 词性: noun (obsolete)
