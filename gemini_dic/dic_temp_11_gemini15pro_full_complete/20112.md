biotic:/baɪˈɑːtɪk/
adjective
生物的；有关生命的
Relating to living organisms.
例句：The biotic factors in an ecosystem include plants, animals, and microorganisms. 生态系统中的生物因素包括植物、动物和微生物。

例句：Biotic pressures, such as competition for resources, influence the evolution of species.  生物压力，例如资源竞争，影响物种的进化。

近义词：living, organic
反义词：abiotic
词性：adjective
