dispensable:/dɪˈspɛnsəbl/
adj.
可有可无的；非必要的
Many people consider a landline phone dispensable in the age of cell phones.
在手机时代，许多人认为座机电话是可有可无的。
The assistant proved to be dispensable after the manager learned to use the new software.
在经理学会使用新软件后，助手被证明是可有可无的。
近义词: unnecessary, redundant, expendable
反义词: indispensable, essential, necessary
词性: adjective

词源: From dispense + -able.  Dispense comes from Old French dispenser, from Latin dispensare "weigh out, distribute, administer," from dis- "apart" + pensare "weigh, pay."
