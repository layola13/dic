bilingual:/baɪˈlɪŋɡwəl/ | adj. n.| 双语的，能说两种语言的；双语者 | Speaking two languages fluently. Able to communicate effectively in two languages. | She is bilingual in English and Spanish. 她精通英语和西班牙语。| synonyms: multilingual, fluent in two languages | antonyms: monolingual | adjective, noun
词源: From Latin "bi-" (two) + "lingua" (tongue, language).
