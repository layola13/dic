coursings:/ˈkɔːrsɪŋz/
n.
快速流动；奔流；追逐；(猎犬)追捕野兔的活动
the swift movement of something, especially liquid; the sport of hunting hares with greyhounds
The coursing of blood through his veins quickened.  他的血液在血管里奔流得更快了。
The greyhounds thrilled to the coursing of the hare.  猎犬兴奋地追逐野兔。
近义词:flows, rushes, chases, pursuits
反义词:stagnation, stillness
词性:名词
