hogans:/ˈhoʊɡɑːnz/
n.
霍根式圆顶棚屋（尤指美国西南部纳瓦霍人传统的圆顶棚屋）
A traditional Navajo dwelling, usually made of logs and earth.
The Navajo family lived in a hogan on their reservation.  纳瓦霍一家住在他们保留地上的一个霍根式圆顶棚屋里。
dwellings, houses, shelters
