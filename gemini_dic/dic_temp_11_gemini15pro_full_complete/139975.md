odorating:/ˈoʊdəreɪtɪŋ/
verb
加味；加臭；使有气味
Imparting an odor to, especially a detectable odor to something naturally odorless.
The company is odorating the natural gas so that leaks can be detected. 这家公司正在给天然气加臭，以便可以检测到泄漏。
近义词:  perfuming, scenting, aromatizing
反义词: deodorizing
词性: v.


odorating:/ˈoʊdəreɪtɪŋ/
adjective
加味的；加臭的
Having the quality of adding an odor.
The odorating agent is added to the gas supply. 加臭剂被添加到气体供应中。
近义词: perfuming, scenting, aromatizing
反义词: deodorizing
词性: adj.
