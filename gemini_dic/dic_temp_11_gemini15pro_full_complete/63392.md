entotic:/ɛnˈtɒtɪk/ | adj. | 内耳的，耳内的 |  Relating to sounds produced within the ear, as opposed to sounds coming from outside the body. | Example 1: The doctor determined that the ringing in her ears was entotic, not caused by an external source. 医生确定她耳鸣是内耳引起的，而不是外部原因造成的。

Example 2: Entotic tinnitus can be caused by muscle spasms or blood flow near the ear. 内耳性耳鸣可能是由肌肉痉挛或耳朵附近的血液流动引起的。 | Near synonyms: internal aural, intra-aural | Antonyms: external aural, extra-aural | word property: adjective
