cheesier:/ˈtʃiːziər/| adj.|更俗气的，更做作的，更劣质的|Comparative form of cheesy: more cheesy.  Having qualities of something cheap, tacky, or sentimental in an exaggerated way.|That romantic comedy was cheesier than I expected. 那部浪漫喜剧比我想象的还要俗气。|近义词：corny, tacky, schmaltzy|反义词：sophisticated, refined, tasteful|词性:形容词

cheesier:/ˈtʃiːziər/| adj.|更浓郁的奶酪味的|Comparative form of cheesy: more cheesy.  Having a stronger cheese flavor.|This new cheddar is cheesier than the one we usually buy.  这种新的切达干酪比我们通常买的奶酪味更浓。|近义词：richer, stronger|反义词：milder, blander|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From cheese + -y (suffix forming adjectives).  The comparative form, cheesier, is formed by adding -er.  Ultimately from Latin *caseus (cheese).
