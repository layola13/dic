breviped:/ˈbrɛvɪpɛd/| adj. n.|短腿的，短腿动物|Having short legs; a short-legged animal.  Derived from Latin "brevis" (short) and "pes" (foot).|例句：The corgi is a naturally breviped breed.  柯基犬是一种天生短腿的品种。

例句：The breviped lizard scurried across the sand. 短腿蜥蜴快速穿过沙地。|近义词：short-legged|反义词:long-legged|词性:形容词, 名词
