nonequable:/nɒnˈiːkwəb(ə)l/
adjective
不均匀的，不稳定的，易变的
Not uniform or consistent; uneven or changeable.
The nonequable climate made farming difficult.  不稳定的气候使耕作变得困难。
The artist's nonequable temperament was reflected in his work.  这位艺术家反复无常的性情反映在他的作品中。
近义词:variable, uneven, fluctuating, erratic, unstable, inconsistent, irregular
反义词:stable, consistent, uniform, steady, even, regular
词性:adjective

词源:
From non- + equable (from Latin aequabilis, from aequare "to make equal," from aequus "equal").  由non- + equable（源自拉丁语aequabilis，来自aequare“使相等”，来自aequus“相等”）组成。
