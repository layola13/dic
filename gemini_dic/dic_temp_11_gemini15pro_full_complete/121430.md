misinstruct:/ˌmɪsɪnˈstrʌkt/
verb
错误地指导，误导
to instruct wrongly or improperly; to give incorrect information to.
The guide misinstructed us about the trail, and we got lost.  导游错误地指示了我们路线，我们迷路了。
misguide, mislead
inform correctly, guide correctly
词性:动词
