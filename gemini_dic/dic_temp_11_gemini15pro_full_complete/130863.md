nonarchitect:/ˌnɒnˈɑːrkɪtekt/ | n. | 非建筑师；非建筑专业人士 | A person who is not an architect, either by profession or by training. |  He's a nonarchitect, but he has a good eye for design. 他不是建筑师，但很有设计眼光。 | Example: The nonarchitect struggled to understand the blueprints.  这位非建筑专业人士很难理解这些蓝图。 | Synonyms: layman, amateur, nonprofessional | Antonyms: architect, professional, expert | noun
