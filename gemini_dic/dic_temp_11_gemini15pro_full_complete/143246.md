outdelivering:/ˌaʊt.dɪˈlɪv.ər.ɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
超越交付；在交付方面胜过
Surpassing in delivery or performance, especially in a business context.  It signifies exceeding expectations in providing goods or services.
Our company is outdelivering the competition in terms of customer satisfaction. 我们公司在客户满意度方面超越了竞争对手。
The team is outdelivering its targets this quarter.  这个团队在本季度超额完成了目标。
Synonyms: outperforming, exceeding, surpassing
Antonyms: underperforming, lagging, failing
