molding:/ˈmoʊldɪŋ/| n. |  装饰线条，造型，铸件 |A shaped strip of wood, plastic, or other material used for decoration or finishing, especially around doors, windows, or the top or bottom of walls.  Also, the process of shaping something in a mold. |  The molding around the window was intricately carved.  窗户周围的装饰线条雕刻精美。| synonyms: trim, cornice, border, frame | antonyms: plainness, simplicity | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *moldyng, from Old French moler (to grind),  ultimately from Latin molere (to grind). The sense shifted from "grinding" to "shaping" and eventually to the decorative strips used in architecture and other crafts.
