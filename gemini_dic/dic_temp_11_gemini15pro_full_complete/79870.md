gnarliest:/ˈnɑːrliɪst/
adjective
最棒的，最酷的，最险峻的，最棘手的
(slang) Extremely impressive, cool, difficult, or challenging.
That wave was the gnarliest I've ever surfed. 那个浪是我冲浪以来遇到的最险的。
synonyms: awesome, amazing, rad, wicked, sick, challenging, difficult, tough
antonyms: easy, simple, straightforward, unimpressive, boring
词性: adjective
