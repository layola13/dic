exorcise: /ˈɛksɔːrsaɪz/ /ˈɛksərˌsaɪz/
verb
驱魔，驱除（坏事）
To drive out (an evil spirit) from a person, place, or thing, especially by religious or magical means; to free from evil spirits.  To remove or get rid of something troublesome or undesirable.
The priest exorcised the demons from the possessed man.  神父驱除了附在那人身上的恶魔。
She tried to exorcise the painful memories from her mind. 她试图从脑海中驱除痛苦的记忆。
近义词: expel, banish, purge
反义词: invite, welcome
词性: verb
