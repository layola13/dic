chaplainship: /ˈtʃæplɪnʃɪp/ | n. | 牧师职位；牧师的任期 | The position or term of office of a chaplain. | He accepted the chaplainship at the local hospital. 他接受了当地医院的牧师职位。|  The college advertised a chaplainship.  学院登广告招聘牧师。 | synonyms: chaplaincy, ministry, pastorate | antonyms: laity, congregation | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *chapelainship*, from Old French *chapelain* (chaplain) + *-ship* (suffix denoting office, state, or condition). Ultimately from Late Latin *capellanus* (chaplain), from *capella* (chapel), diminutive of *capa* (cloak, cope).
