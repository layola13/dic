nooklet: /ˈnʊklɪt/
noun
小角落，小隐蔽处
A small nook; a cozy little corner.
She curled up with a book in a sunny nooklet of the library. 她蜷缩在图书馆阳光明媚的小角落里看书。
Synonyms: alcove, niche, corner, cranny
Antonyms: expanse, open area
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From nook + -let (diminutive suffix).
