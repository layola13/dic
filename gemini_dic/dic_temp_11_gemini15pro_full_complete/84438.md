hallucinogen:/həˈluːsɪnədʒən/ /həˈluːsɪnəˌdʒɛn/ | n. | 迷幻剂；致幻剂 |A drug that causes hallucinations, i.e., perceptions of something that is not present.|  The shaman used a powerful hallucinogen to enter a trance state.  萨满使用了一种强效致幻剂进入恍惚状态。

hallucinogen:| n. | 迷幻剂；致幻剂 |A substance that produces hallucinations.| Some mushrooms contain naturally occurring hallucinogens. 一些蘑菇含有天然存在的致幻剂。

hallucinogen:| n. | 迷幻剂；致幻剂 |A psychoactive agent which can cause hallucinations, perceptual anomalies, and other substantial subjective changes in thoughts, emotion, and consciousness.|  The effects of the hallucinogen lasted for several hours. 致幻剂的效果持续了几个小时。


近义词:psychedelic, psychotomimetic | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

词源: Late 19th century: from hallucinate + -gen.
