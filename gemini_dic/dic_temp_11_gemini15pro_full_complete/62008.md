enamels:/ɪˈnæməlz/
n.|珐琅，瓷釉；指甲油|A glossy substance used to coat metal or pottery for protection or decoration, or for teeth.|The artist used vibrant enamels to decorate the vase.|近义词：glaze, lacquer, varnish, paint|反义词:None|词性:名词

enamels:/ɪˈnæməlz/
v.|涂珐琅，上瓷釉|To coat or decorate with enamel.|She enameled the jewelry with intricate designs.|近义词：glaze, lacquer, varnish, paint|反义词:strip, remove|词性:动词

词源：
From Old French *esmal, from Frankish *smalt, from Proto-Germanic *smeltaną (“to smelt”).  Related to melt.  The spelling influenced by Anglo-Norman enamaile, from Vulgar Latin *in- + Medieval Latin amaille.
