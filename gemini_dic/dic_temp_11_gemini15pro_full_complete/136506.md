nonroutine:/ˌnɒnruːˈtiːn/ /ˌnɑːnruːˈtiːn/
adjective
非常规的；非例行的
Not routine; unusual or out of the ordinary.
例句：
Nonroutine problems require creative solutions.
非常规问题需要创造性的解决方案。

The doctor recommended a nonroutine checkup due to her family history.
由于她的家族病史，医生建议进行一次非常规检查。


近义词：unusual, uncommon, atypical, exceptional, irregular
反义词：routine, ordinary, typical, usual, standard
词性:adjective
