ezra:/ˈɛzrə/| n. | 以斯拉 | A masculine given name of Hebrew origin, meaning "helper" or "help". It is derived from the Hebrew name עֶזְרָא (Ezra), which appears in the Bible as the name of a priest and scribe who led the return of the Jewish exiles from Babylon to Jerusalem. | Ezra was a prominent figure in Jewish history. 以斯拉是犹太历史上的杰出人物。|  My son's name is Ezra. 我儿子的名字是以斯拉。 | synonyms: Esdras | antonyms: N/A | noun
