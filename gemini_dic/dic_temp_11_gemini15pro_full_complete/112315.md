lordolatry: /lɔːrˈdɒlətri/ (US) /lɔːˈdɒlətri/ (UK) | n. | 偶像崇拜；对权贵的谄媚 | The excessive admiration or worship of a person as if they were a lord or god.  It can also refer to the worship of power or authority. |  His lordolatry of the CEO was sickening. 他对首席执行官的谄媚令人作呕。| The journalist condemned the lordolatry surrounding the royal family.  这位记者谴责了围绕王室的偶像崇拜。 |  idolatry, hero-worship, sycophancy, fawning, adulation |  disrespect, irreverence | noun
