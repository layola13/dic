overadjustment:/ˌoʊvərəˈdʒʌstmənt/
|n.|过度调整；过度调节|An excessive alteration or correction, often resulting in a worse situation than the original.|The overadjustment of the thermostat led to the room becoming too cold.|近义词: overcompensation, overcorrection|反义词: underadjustment, undercorrection|词性:名词

词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessive") + adjustment (noun from adjust, from Old French ajuster "to arrange, put in order")
