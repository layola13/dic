disloyalist:/dɪsˈlɔɪəlɪst/| n.|不忠者，背叛者|A person who is disloyal to a person, cause, or country. |例句：The disloyalist was expelled from the group.  这个不忠者被逐出了组织。|近义词：traitor, rebel, defector, turncoat|反义词：loyalist, patriot|词性:名词

Etymology: dis- (prefix meaning "not") + loyal + -ist (suffix forming nouns denoting adherents of a doctrine, etc.) From French *desloyal*, from *des-* (prefix meaning "not") + *loyal*.  Ultimately from Latin *lēgālis* "legal".
