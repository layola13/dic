disheartening:/dɪsˈhɑːrtnɪŋ/ /dɪsˈhɑːrtənɪŋ/
adjective
令人沮丧的，使人灰心的
Causing someone to lose determination or confidence; discouraging.
The team's loss was disheartening. 球队的失利令人沮丧。
discouraging, demoralizing, dispiriting
encouraging, heartening, inspiring
