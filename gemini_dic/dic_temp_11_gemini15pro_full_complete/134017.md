nonhousing:/ˌnɒnˈhaʊzɪŋ/
adjective
非住房的
Relating to or involving things other than housing; not related to the provision of accommodation.  This term often refers to expenses, costs, or sectors of the economy that are not directly related to residential housing.
The family's nonhousing expenses, such as food and transportation, were increasing.  这个家庭的非住房支出，例如食品和交通，正在增加。
Nonhousing sectors of the economy, like manufacturing and retail, experienced growth. 非住房经济部门，如制造业和零售业，经历了增长。
synonyms: nonresidential, ex-housing
antonyms: housing, residential
词性: adjective
