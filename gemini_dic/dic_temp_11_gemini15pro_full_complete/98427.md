inspecting:/ɪnˈspɛktɪŋ/
verb
检查，视察
The health inspector was inspecting the kitchen.  卫生检查员正在检查厨房。
present participle of inspect
Inspecting the goods carefully before shipment is crucial.  发货前仔细检查货物至关重要。
synonyms: examining, scrutinizing, surveying, checking
antonyms: neglecting, ignoring
