nonusuriously:/ˌnɒnjuːˈʒʊəriəsli/ | adv. |  不放高利贷地；不剥削地 |  In a manner that does not involve usury (the illegal action or practice of lending money at unreasonably high rates of interest).  | He lent her the money nonusuriously, simply wanting to help her out. 他不放高利贷地借给她钱，只是想帮助她摆脱困境。 |  fairly, justly, equitably | usuriously, exploitatively | adverb
