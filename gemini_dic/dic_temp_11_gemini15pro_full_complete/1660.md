acknowledges:/əkˈnɒlɪdʒɪz/
|v.|承认；确认；答谢|This verb signifies the act of accepting or admitting the truth or existence of something. It can also mean expressing gratitude or appreciation.|
例句：He acknowledges the difficulties ahead. 他承认前面的困难。

例句：She acknowledged his presence with a nod. 她点头示意承认他的存在。

近义词：admits, accepts, recognizes, confirms, grants
反义词：denies, rejects, disputes, ignores
词性：动词
