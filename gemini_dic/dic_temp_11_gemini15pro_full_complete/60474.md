eject:/ɪˈdʒɛkt/| v. |弹出；驱逐；喷射|To force or throw something out, especially from inside something else. |The pilot ejected from the aircraft. 飞行员从飞机中弹射出来。|The bouncer ejected the unruly patron from the bar. 保镖把那个闹事的顾客从酒吧里赶了出去。|近义词：expel, oust, remove, emit, discharge|反义词:inject, insert, admit|verb

ejection:/ɪˈdʒɛkʃən/| n. |弹出；驱逐；喷射|The act of ejecting something.|The ejection of the player from the game was controversial.  这名球员被罚出场外引起了争议。|The volcano's ejection of ash covered the surrounding area. 火山喷出的火山灰覆盖了周围地区。|近义词：expulsion, removal, emission, discharge|反义词:injection, insertion, admission|noun
