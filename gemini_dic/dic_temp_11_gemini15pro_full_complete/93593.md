imbankment: /ɪmˈbæŋkmənt/ | n. | 堤防，路堤 | A wall or bank of earth or stone built to prevent a river flooding an area, or to support a road or railway. |  The railway runs along a high embankment overlooking the valley.  铁路沿着俯瞰山谷的高高的路堤延伸。

The river burst its banks, flooding the town despite the new embankments. 尽管新建了堤坝，这条河还是决堤了，淹没了小镇。


近义词: levee, dike, bank, rampart | 反义词: cutting, ditch | 词性: noun
