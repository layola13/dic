off-street:/ˌɒfˈstriːt/ /ˌɔːfˈstriːt/
adjective
非街道的；路外的；不在街上的
Located or taking place away from public streets.
The hotel offers convenient off-street parking.  这家酒店提供方便的路外停车位。
Residents can use the off-street parking facility. 居民可以使用路外停车设施。
Synonym: private, non-public
Antonym: on-street, public
词性: adjective
