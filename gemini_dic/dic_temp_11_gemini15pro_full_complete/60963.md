elfishly:/ˈɛlfɪʃli/
adverb
自私地，小气地
In a selfish or self-centered manner; with regard only for oneself.
He smiled elfishly as he pocketed the last piece of candy. 他自私地笑着，把最后一块糖放进了口袋。
She elfishly refused to share her toys. 她自私地拒绝分享她的玩具。
近义词:selfishly, egotistically, greedily
反义词:selflessly, generously, altruistically
词性:adverb
