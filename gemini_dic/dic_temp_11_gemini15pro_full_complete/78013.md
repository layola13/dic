gentlest:/ˈdʒɛntləst/| adj.|最温柔的，最温和的|Superlative form of gentle: most gentle.  It means having or showing a mild, kind, or tender temperament or character.  It also describes something that is not harsh, severe, or violent.|She has the gentlest touch with animals. 她对待动物最温柔。|Synonyms:  kindest, tenderest, mildest, calmest, meekest, softest|Antonyms:  harshest, roughest, cruelest, violentest, fiercest|adjective
