in-chorus:/ɪn ˈkɔːrəs/ | adv. |齐声地，合唱地|Performed or spoken simultaneously by a group of people. |The audience laughed in chorus.  观众齐声大笑。| The children answered in chorus. 孩子们齐声回答。| 近义词:together, simultaneously, unanimously| 反义词:individually, separately| 副词
