kelosis:/kɪˈloʊsɪs/
noun
瘢痕形成;结痂;
The formation of a scar or scab.
Kelosis is a natural part of the wound healing process. 瘢痕形成是伤口愈合过程的自然组成部分。
The kelosis on his arm was a reminder of the accident. 他手臂上的疤痕让他想起那次事故。
近义词: scarring, cicatrization
反义词: (No direct antonym for the physical process)
词性: noun
