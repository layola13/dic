ecotopia:/ˌiːkəˈtəʊpiə/ /ˌɛkoʊˈtoʊpiə/| n.|生态乌托邦|An imagined place or state of things in which the environment is ideal or perfect. A society living in ecological harmony.|例句：The author portrays an ecotopia where humans live in balance with nature.  作者描绘了一个人类与自然和谐共生的生态乌托邦。

例句：She dreams of creating an ecotopia powered entirely by renewable energy. 她梦想创建一个完全由可再生能源驱动的生态乌托邦。|近义词：green utopia, eco-paradise|反义词：dystopia|词性: noun
