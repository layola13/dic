hypermarket:/ˈhaɪpəmɑːrkɪt/ (US) /ˈhaɪpəmɑːkɪt/ (UK)| n. |大型超市，特大超市|A very large self-service store selling a wide range of household goods, food, and other products.|Example: We bought all our groceries at the hypermarket. 我们在大卖场买了所有的食品杂货。|Synonym: superstore, megastore|Antonym: convenience store, small shop|Noun
