indirectness:/ˌɪndɪˈrɛktnəs/ | n. |间接;婉转;迂回 | The state or quality of being indirect.  Lack of straightforwardness; roundabout ways of proceeding or expressing oneself. | He achieved his goal through indirectness, hinting at his desires rather than stating them openly. 他通过间接的方式实现了目标，暗示而不是公开说明他的愿望。 | synonyms: circumlocution, obliqueness, evasiveness | antonyms: directness, straightforwardness, frankness | noun
