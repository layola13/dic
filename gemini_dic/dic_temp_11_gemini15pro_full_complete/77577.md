gecko:/ˈɡekoʊ/ (US), /ˈɡɛkəʊ/ (UK)| n. |壁虎|A small lizard with soft skin, short legs, and adhesive toe pads, enabling it to climb on smooth vertical surfaces.|The gecko scurried up the wall. 壁虎飞快地爬上墙。|Synonyms: lizard, reptile|Antonyms: N/A|noun
