gutwort:/ˈɡʌtwɜːrt/| n. | 石莲，脐景天|A common name for several species of succulent plants, particularly those in the genus *Umbilicus*, known for their fleshy leaves and medicinal properties. These plants are often found growing on walls and rocks. |The gutwort grows well in rocky terrain.|Synonym: navelwort, wall pennywort|Antonym: N/A (no direct antonym for a plant name)|noun
