delve:/delv/ | v. | 探究；钻研；深入研究 | To research something thoroughly; to dig deeply into a subject or issue. |  He delved into the history of the region. 他深入研究了该地区的历史。

She delved into her handbag for her keys.  她在手提包里翻找钥匙。| 近义词：explore, investigate, probe, research | 反义词: skim, ignore, neglect | 词性: 动词

delve:/dɛlv/ | n. | (古语) 挖掘；探究 |  (Archaic) The act of digging or researching deeply. | 例句：His delve into the archives yielded new information. 他对档案的深入研究发现了新的信息。 (古语用法，现代英语很少用作名词) | 近义词: exploration, investigation | 反义词: glance, disregard | 词性: 名词 (古语)
