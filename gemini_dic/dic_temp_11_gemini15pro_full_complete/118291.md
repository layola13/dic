mercurial:/mərˈkjʊriəl/ | adj. |反复无常的，易变的；活泼的|Characterized by rapid and unpredictable changeableness of mood.  Derived from the Roman god Mercury, known for his swiftness and unpredictability. |Her mercurial temperament made it difficult to predict her reactions. 她反复无常的性情使人难以预料她的反应。| volatile, capricious, fickle, erratic | stable, steady, constant | adjective

mercurial:/mərˈkjʊriəl/ | n. |含汞的物质；汞制剂|A substance containing mercury.  Derived from the Roman god Mercury, whose name was associated with the element mercury (also called quicksilver). |The doctor prescribed a mercurial ointment for the skin infection. 医生开了一种汞制剂软膏来治疗皮肤感染。| quicksilver, mercury compound | N/A | noun
