inappellability:/ˌɪnəˌpɛləˈbɪləti/
n.
不可上诉性，不可申诉性
The quality or state of being inappellable; not subject to appeal.
The inappellability of the judge's decision caused frustration among the defendants.  法官的判决不可上诉，这令被告感到沮丧。
The contract stipulated the inappellability of any disputes arising from its terms. 合同规定，因其条款引起的任何争议均不可上诉。
近义词: finality, conclusiveness
反义词: appealability
词性: noun
