heterarchy:/ˈhɛtərɑːrki/ | n. | 异质层级，异质统治 | A system of organization where elements are unranked or where they possess the potential to be ranked in a number of different ways. |  Example: The company's heterarchy allows for more flexibility and collaboration than a traditional hierarchy.  公司异质的层级结构比传统的等级结构更具灵活性和协作性。| Example:  Some anthropological studies suggest that early human societies were organized as heterarchies rather than hierarchies. 一些人类学研究表明，早期人类社会是组织成异质层级而不是等级制度的。| Synonyms: network, web, lattice | Antonyms: hierarchy, pecking order | noun
