oversoar:/ˌoʊvərˈsɔːr/| v. |高飞，翱翔|To soar above or beyond something. |His spirit oversoared the limitations of his physical body. 他的精神超越了他肉体的局限。|Synonyms: soar above, fly high|Antonyms: plummet, descend|verb
