coticular:/kjuːˈtɪkjʊlə(r)/ | adj. | 表皮的，角质层的 | Relating to or resembling a cuticle. | The coticular layer of a plant protects it from water loss.  植物的表皮层防止水分流失。 | Example:  The insect's coticular exoskeleton provided protection.  这只昆虫的表皮外骨骼提供了保护。| Synonyms: epidermal, cutaneous | Antonyms: subcutaneous, internal | adjective

coticle:/ˈkjuːtɪk(ə)l/ | n. | 表皮，角质层 | The outermost layer of an organism, or part of an organism, such as a hair, nail, or plant. | Example:  The cuticle of the leaf prevents excessive water loss.  叶子的表皮防止水分过度流失。 | Synonyms: epidermis, integument | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider terms like "inner layer," "core," or "medulla") | noun
