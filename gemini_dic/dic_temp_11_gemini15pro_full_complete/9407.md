antiracing:/ˌæntiˈreɪsɪŋ/
adjective
反赛车的，防止赛车的
Designed to prevent or discourage racing, especially of vehicles.
The track has installed antiracing barriers to improve safety.  赛道安装了防赛车护栏以提高安全性。
Antiracing tires are designed to reduce traction on dry pavement. 防赛车轮胎旨在减少在干燥路面上的牵引力。
Near synonyms:  antiskid, nonskid
Antonyms: racing, speed-enhancing
词性: adjective
