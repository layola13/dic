benign:/bɪˈnaɪn/
adjective
良性的；和蔼的；吉利的
(of a disease) not harmful in effect: in particular, not malignant.
例句：He was relieved when the lump was diagnosed as benign. 他得知肿块被诊断为良性后松了一口气。

例句：A benign smile touched her lips. 她嘴唇上浮现出一丝和蔼的微笑。
近义词：kind, gentle, harmless, favorable
反义词：malignant, harmful, hostile, unfavorable
词性: adjective

benins: /bɪˈnaɪnz/
noun
良性肿瘤 (This is a less common usage, usually the plural is not used medically.  Singular "benign" is used to describe the nature of a tumor.)
Benign growths; non-cancerous tumors.
例句: The doctor assured her that the benins found during the scan were not a cause for concern. 医生向她保证，扫描中发现的良性肿瘤不用担心。(Less Common Usage)

例句: Multiple benins were identified in the patient's liver. 在患者的肝脏中发现了多个良性肿瘤。(Less Common Usage)
近义词: (In the rare context of plural usage) non-malignant tumors, harmless growths
反义词: malignancies, cancers
词性: noun (plural, less common)
