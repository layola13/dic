decipherable:/dɪˈsaɪfərəbl/
adjective
可破译的，可解读的
Capable of being deciphered; understandable.
The code was complex, but eventually decipherable.  这密码很复杂，但最终还是可以破译的。
The ancient inscription was barely decipherable. 这古老的铭文几乎无法解读。
synonyms: understandable, legible, clear, readable, interpretable
antonyms: indecipherable, unreadable, illegible, incomprehensible
