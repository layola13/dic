nondetachabilities:/ˌnɒndɪˌtætʃəˈbɪlɪtiz/
noun
不可分离性；不可拆卸性
The nondetachabilities of the components make the machine difficult to repair.  这些组件的不可分离性使得机器难以修理。
The nondetachabilities of certain types of fixtures are a key feature for safety. 某些类型固定装置的不可拆卸性是安全性的关键特征。
Near Synonyms: inseparabilities,  inseparableness, nonremovability
Antonyms: detachability, separability, removability
词性: noun (plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix): not
detachable (adjective): able to be detached or separated
-ity (suffix): forms nouns indicating a state or quality
-ies (suffix): plural form for nouns ending in -ity
