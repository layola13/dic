foreclose:/fɔːrˈkloʊz/| v. |取消赎回权，排除；预先阻止|To take possession of a mortgaged property when the mortgagor fails to keep up their mortgage payments.  To prevent something from happening.|The bank foreclosed on their house after they missed several mortgage payments. 银行在他们拖欠了几次抵押贷款后取消了他们房屋的赎回权。

The company's decision to invest in new technology effectively foreclosed the possibility of expanding into new markets. 公司决定投资新技术，实际上排除了进军新市场的可能性。|近义词：repossess, seize, prevent, preclude|反义词: allow, permit|词性:动词

foreclosure:/fɔːrˈkloʊʒər/| n. |取消赎回权，丧失赎回权|The action of taking possession of a mortgaged property due to the mortgagor's failure to keep up their mortgage payments.|The foreclosure crisis hit many homeowners hard. 取消赎回权的危机使许多房主遭受重创。

The foreclosure of the factory left many workers unemployed. 工厂的关闭导致许多工人失业。|近义词：repossession, seizure|反义词: redemption|词性:名词
