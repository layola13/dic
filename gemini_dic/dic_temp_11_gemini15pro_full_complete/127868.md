neocolonialisms: /ˌniːoʊkəˈloʊniəlɪzəmz/
|n.|新殖民主义|The use of economic, political, cultural, or other pressures to control or influence other countries, especially former dependencies.|Many developing nations accuse Western corporations of practicing neocolonialism.|近义词: imperialism, domination, hegemony|反义词: independence, self-determination|词性:名词

Etymology:  "neo-" (new) + "colonialism" (from "colonial" + "-ism").  "Colonial" derives from the Latin "colonus" (farmer, settler).
