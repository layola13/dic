must:/məst/ |aux. v. |必须；一定要；很可能；必定|A modal verb expressing obligation, strong probability, or logical deduction. | You must finish your homework before you go out. 你必须在出去玩之前完成作业。|  We must leave now or we'll miss the train. 我们现在必须离开，否则会错过火车。 |  have to, should, ought to | needn't, don't have to | auxiliary verb

must:/məst/ |n. |绝对必要的事物；不可缺少之物|Something that is absolutely essential or indispensable. |  Sunscreen is a must on a beach holiday.  防晒霜是海滩度假必备之物。|  A good dictionary is a must for any language learner. 一本好词典对于任何语言学习者来说都是必不可少的。|  essential, necessity, requirement |  luxury, nonessential | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mosten*, *moten* (“must”), from Old English *mōtan* (“to be allowed to, have permission to, be able to, may, must”), from Proto-Germanic *mōtaną* (“to have permission to, be allowed to, may, must”), from Proto-Indo-European *méh₁- (“to be able, may”). Cognate with German *müssen* (“must”), Dutch *moeten* (“must”), Gothic *𐌼𐍉𐍄𐌰𐌽* (motan, “to be allowed to”).

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I must go to the bank today. 我今天必须去银行。
He must study harder if he wants to pass the exam. 如果他想通过考试，他必须更加努力学习。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I must be dreaming! 我一定是在做梦！
He must be working late tonight. 他今晚肯定要工作到很晚。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
You must have heard the news by now. 你现在一定已经听到这个消息了。
They must have left already. 他们一定已经离开了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
She must have been working on this project for months. 她一定已经在这个项目上工作好几个月了。
He must have been waiting for a long time. 他一定等了很长时间。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I must have left my keys at home. 我一定是把钥匙忘在家里了。 (Expressing probability in the past)
We must go. 我们必须走了。（过去时表示义务，但这种用法现在不常见，通常用had to代替）

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):

They must have been playing games all afternoon. 他们整个下午一定都在玩游戏。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

He must have seen the movie before. 他以前一定看过这部电影。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
We must leave early tomorrow. 我们明天必须早点出发。 (Expressing obligation)

虚拟语气:
If I must go, I will. 如果我必须去，我会去的。
If he must leave, let him leave. 如果他必须离开，就让他离开。

疑问句:

否定句:
You mustn't be late. 你一定不能迟到。
