crucifying:/ˈkruːsɪfaɪɪŋ/| adj.|折磨人的，痛苦的，使人极为不快的|Causing intense physical or mental suffering; extremely unpleasant or difficult.|The heat was crucifying.  酷热难耐。|近义词:excruciating, agonizing, torturous|反义词:pleasant, comfortable|adjective

crucifying:/ˈkruːsɪfaɪɪŋ/| v.|把…钉在十字架上处死；折磨；严厉批评|1. Put (someone) to death by nailing or binding them to a cross. 2. Cause (someone) to suffer greatly. 3. Criticize (someone) severely and unrelentingly.|The rebels crucified the captured soldiers. 叛军将俘虏的士兵钉死在十字架上。|近义词:execute, torture, torment|反义词:praise, commend|verb
