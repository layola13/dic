banksian:/ˈbæŋksiən/
adjective
班克西亚的; 纪念班克斯的; 属于班克斯的
Relating to or commemorating Sir Joseph Banks, an English botanist and naturalist who accompanied Captain Cook on his first voyage of discovery.  It is frequently used in botanical contexts to describe species named after him.
The garden features a magnificent collection of banksian roses.  花园里有一个宏伟的班克西亚玫瑰收藏。
Banksian roses are known for their repeat flowering. 班克西亚玫瑰以其重复开花而闻名。
Synonyms:  None (highly specific)
Antonyms:  None (highly specific)
词性: 形容词
