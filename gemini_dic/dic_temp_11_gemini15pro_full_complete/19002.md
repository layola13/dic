bestrew:/bɪˈstruː/
verb
散布，撒满
To scatter or cover (a surface) with things:
The floor was bestrewn with papers. 地板上撒满了文件。
Fallen leaves bestrewed the path. 落叶撒满了小路。

近义词:scatter, sprinkle, strew, litter
反义词:gather, collect, clear

词性:v.

词源: From Middle English *bestrewen*, from Old English *bestreowian*, from *be-* + *streowian* (“to strew”).  The prefix *be-* adds a sense of thoroughness or completeness to the action.
