contentional: /kənˈtɛnʃənəl/
adjective
争论的，引起争论的
Relating to or involving contention; causing or likely to cause an argument; controversial.
The judge's decision in the contentious case sparked widespread protests.  法官在这个充满争议的案件中的判决引发了广泛的抗议。
The ownership of the islands remains a contentious issue between the two countries.  这些岛屿的所有权仍然是两国之间一个有争议的问题。
Synonyms: disputed, debated, controversial, arguable
Antonyms: undisputed, uncontroversial, agreed, harmonious
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From Late Latin *contentionalis, from Latin contentio (“a straining, exertion, struggle, contest”), from contendere (“to strive, struggle, contend”).
