baalist: /ˈbeɪlɪst/
noun
保释人；保释代理人
A person who provides bail for someone accused of a crime.
例句：The baalist posted the required amount to secure the defendant's release. 保释人缴纳了所需的金额以确保被告获释。

例句：The defendant hired a baalist to get out of jail. 被告雇佣了一名保释人以摆脱牢狱之灾。

近义词：bondsman, surety
反义词: N/A
词性: noun

词源:  From bail (n.) + -ist. Ultimately from Old French baillier "to control, to guard, to deliver."
