词源：From Urdu hindū, from Persian hindū, from Sanskrit sindhu (“the Indus River”), used to refer to the land and people beyond the Indus. Over time, the term became associated with the dominant religion of the region, Hinduism.  The spelling "hindoo" reflects an older, anglicized form of the word.
