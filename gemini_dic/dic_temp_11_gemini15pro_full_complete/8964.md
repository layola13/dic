antihypertension:/ˌæntiˌhaɪpərˈtenʃən/| n. |抗高血压|A medication or other therapy used to treat hypertension (high blood pressure).|例句：The doctor prescribed an antihypertension medication to lower her blood pressure. 医生给她开了抗高血压药来降低血压。

例句：Regular exercise can be a natural form of antihypertension. 定期锻炼可以是一种天然的抗高血压方式。|近义词：antihypertensive|反义词:None|词性:noun

词源:  "anti-" (Greek origin, meaning "against") + "hyper-" (Greek origin, meaning "over, excessive") + "tension" (Latin origin, meaning "stretching, pressure").
