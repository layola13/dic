damming:/ˈdæmɪŋ/ | adj. |筑坝的；该死的；非常糟糕的；极坏的|  The act of building a dam or the state of being dammed. Can also be used figuratively to describe something extremely negative or bad.| The damming of the river created a large reservoir.  筑坝造成了一个大型水库。|Synonyms: condemning, damnable, cursed, disastrous | Antonyms:  beneficial, advantageous, constructive | adjective

damming:/ˈdæmɪŋ/ | n. | 筑坝；咒骂| The act of building a dam.  Can also rarely refer to the act of condemning or cursing.|  The damming of the river caused significant ecological changes.  这条河的筑坝造成了重大的生态变化。|Synonyms: construction, blocking | Antonyms: removal, opening | noun
