give-backs:/ˈɡɪvˌbæks/
noun
让步，妥协，退让
Concessions or sacrifices made by employees, usually in wages, benefits, or work rules, often to help an employer remain competitive or avoid layoffs.
The union agreed to give-backs in order to save jobs. 工会同意做出让步以保住工作。

give-backs:/ˈɡɪvˌbæks/
noun
(已获得的) 返还物，归还物
Something that is returned, especially benefits or privileges that had been granted previously.
The company demanded give-backs of previously negotiated pay raises. 公司要求退还先前协商好的加薪。

近义词:concessions, sacrifices, compromises
反义词:gains, increases, advancements
词性:noun
