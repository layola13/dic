overrewarding:/ˌoʊvərɪˈwɔːrdɪŋ/
adjective
过度奖励的，回报过高的
giving too much reward; more reward than is deserved or appropriate.
The job is overrewarding considering how little work is involved.  这项工作考虑到涉及的工作量很少，回报过高。
He felt overrewarded for his contribution. 他觉得自己的贡献得到了过多的回报。
近义词: excessive, exorbitant, lavish, extravagant
反义词: underrewarding, insufficient, inadequate
词性: adjective

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively," "too much") + "rewarding" (adjective from the verb "reward")
