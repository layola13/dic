divvy:/ˈdɪvi/| v. n.|分摊，分配，份额|To divide or share something, especially money or profits.|We divvied up the cost of the trip between us. 我们分摊了旅行费用。|近义词：share, distribute, split, allocate|反义词: hoard, keep, monopolize|verb, noun


divvy:/ˈdɪvi/| n.|份额|A share or portion of something.|Everyone got their fair divvy of the prize money. 每个人都得到了他们应得的奖金份额。|近义词：share, portion, allotment|反义词: whole, total|noun
