colting: /ˈkoʊltɪŋ/
n.
the act of giving birth to a foal (a young horse)
分娩；产驹
The mare is due for colting any day now. 这匹母马随时都可能分娩。
近义词: foaling, parturition
反义词: None

词性: 名词
