ethyne:/ˈɛθaɪn/ | n. |乙炔|A colorless, flammable gas, C₂H₂, used in welding and organic synthesis, and formerly used as an anesthetic and in lighting.|例句：Ethyne is the simplest alkyne. 乙炔是最简单的炔烃。

例句：Ethyne is highly reactive due to its triple bond. 由于其三键，乙炔具有很高的反应活性。|近义词：acetylene|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
