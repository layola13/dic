inthrone:/ɪnˈθroʊn/
verb
登基；使即位； enthrone
To place (a monarch) ceremonially on a throne. To install as if on a throne; exalt.
例句：The new king was enthroned in a lavish ceremony.  新国王在盛大的仪式中登基。

例句：They enthroned their new leader with much fanfare. 他们大张旗鼓地拥立了他们的新领导人。

近义词：crown, invest, install
反义词：dethrone, depose
词性：verb
