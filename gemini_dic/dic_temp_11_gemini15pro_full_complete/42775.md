counts:/kaʊnts/| v. n.|计数，计算；重要；认为；有价值|Counts can be used as both a noun and a verb, referring to the act of counting or the result of counting. It can also imply importance or significance.|She counts the money carefully. 她仔细地数钱。|近义词：calculate, enumerate, tally, matter, signify|反义词:disregard, ignore|词性:verb, noun

Etymology: From Old French *conter, from Latin computāre (“to compute”).
