autosuggestion:/ˌɔːtəsəˈdʒɛstʃən/ | n. | 自我暗示；心理暗示 | The process by which a person induces a changed state of consciousness or behaviour in themselves, typically by repeating a verbal formula or focusing on a particular thought. |  She used autosuggestion to overcome her fear of flying. 她用自我暗示来克服对飞行的恐惧。 | synonyms: self-hypnosis, mind control, positive thinking | antonyms: doubt, skepticism | noun

autosuggestion:/ˌɔːtəsəˈdʒɛstʃən/ | n. | 自动建议 | A function in software or online search engines that automatically suggests words or phrases as you type. | The autosuggestion feature helped me find the right keywords quickly. 自动建议功能帮助我快速找到合适的关键词。 | synonyms: autocomplete, predictive text | antonyms: manual input | noun
