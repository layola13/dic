encourager:/ɪnˈkʌrɪdʒər/ | n. |鼓励者，支持者|Someone who inspires others with courage, spirit, or hope.  A person who gives support and confidence to someone. |Her biggest encourager was her grandmother. 她最大的鼓励者是她的祖母。| synonyms: supporter, promoter, booster, advocate | antonyms: critic, detractor, opponent, discourager | noun
