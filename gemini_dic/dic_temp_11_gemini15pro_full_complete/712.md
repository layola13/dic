absentee:/ˈæbˌsɛnˈtiː/ /æbˌsɛnˈtiː/
n.|缺席者；缺勤者|A person who is not present at a place or event where they are expected to be.|The absentee ballot count is expected to be high this year. 今年缺席投票预计会很高。|non-attendee, truant|attendee, present|noun
absentee:/ˈæbˌsɛnˈtiː/ /æbˌsɛnˈtiː/
adj.|缺席的；缺勤的|Not present at a place or event where one is expected to be.|He's an absentee landlord who lives in another country. 他是一个住在另一个国家的缺席房东。|missing, away|present, attending|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French, from absent, from Latin absens, absent- ‘being away’, present participle of abesse, from ab- ‘away’ + esse ‘be’.
