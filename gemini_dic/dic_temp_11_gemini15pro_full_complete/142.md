abarticulation:/ˌeɪbɑːrtɪkjʊˈleɪʃən/| n.|脱节，关节脱位|Dislocation of a joint; the state of being abarticular.  The abarticulation of the shoulder caused intense pain. 肩关节脱位引起剧烈疼痛.|近义词:dislocation, luxation|反义词:articulation|词性:名词

词源:
From a- (not, without) + articulation (joint connection).  Ultimately from Latin *articulatus*, past participle of *articulare* "to pronounce distinctly," from *articulus* "small connecting part, joint," diminutive of *artus* "joint, limb."
