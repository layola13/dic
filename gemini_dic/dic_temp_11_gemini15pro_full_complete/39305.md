conifer:/ˈkɑːnɪfər/ (US), /ˈkɒnɪfə(r)/ (UK)| n.|针叶树|An evergreen tree that bears cones and needle-like or scale-like leaves.  Most conifers are cone-bearing seed plants with vascular tissue; these are woody plants, mostly trees, but a few are shrubs.|例句：Pine, fir, and spruce are all conifers. 松树、杉树和云杉都是针叶树。

例句：The mountains were covered in dense conifer forests.  群山被茂密的针叶林覆盖。|近义词：evergreen, softwood|反义词：deciduous tree|词性：noun
