fibrinoplastin:/ˌfaɪbrɪnoʊˈplæstɪn/ | n. | 血纤维蛋白原 | A former name for fibrinogen, a soluble plasma glycoprotein that is converted by thrombin into fibrin during blood clot formation. |  Fibrinoplastin plays a crucial role in blood coagulation. 血纤维蛋白原在血液凝固中起着至关重要的作用。| Example:  Fibrinoplastin deficiency can lead to bleeding disorders. 血纤维蛋白缺乏会导致出血性疾病。 | Synonym: fibrinogen | Antonym: N/A | noun
