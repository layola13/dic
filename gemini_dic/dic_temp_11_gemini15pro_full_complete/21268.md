bloodhound:/ˈblʌdhaʊnd/ | n. | 嗅觉灵敏的猎犬；侦探，警探 | A breed of dog known for its exceptional sense of smell, often used for tracking.  Figuratively, it can also refer to a detective or investigator. | The police used bloodhounds to track the escaped convict. 警方用猎犬追踪逃犯。 | The detective was a real bloodhound, relentlessly pursuing every lead.  这位侦探是个真正的猎犬，不懈地追寻每一条线索。 | 近义词: tracker dog, sleuth, investigator | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "amateur" in the figurative sense) | 词性: 名词
