inconcocted:/ˌɪnkənˈkɒktɪd/
adjective
未捏造的；未炮制的；天然的；真实的
not concocted; not made up or fabricated; natural; genuine
The jury believed her story because it seemed inconcocted and spontaneous.  陪审团相信了她的故事，因为它看起来很自然，并非编造。
His reaction was immediate and inconcocted. 他的反应是立即的，没有丝毫的虚假。
Synonyms: genuine, natural, unfeigned, spontaneous, authentic, uncontrived, artless
Antonyms: concocted, fabricated, contrived, artificial, feigned, invented, made-up
词性: 形容词
