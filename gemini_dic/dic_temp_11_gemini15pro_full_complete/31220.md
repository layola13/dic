channelling: /ˈtʃæn(ə)lɪŋ/ | n. v.|引导，传递；沟通（与神灵或灵魂）|The act of serving as a conduit for something non-physical, such as spiritual energy or inspiration.  The artist claimed to be channelling the spirit of Picasso.  这位艺术家声称在引导毕加索的精神。|例句：She believes she is channelling messages from her spirit guide. 她相信她在传递来自她精神导师的信息。
