alterableness:/ˈɔːltərəbəlnəs/ /ˈɑːltərəbəlnəs/
noun
可变性，易变性
The quality of being alterable; changeability.
The alterableness of the weather makes it difficult to plan a picnic.  天气变化无常，很难计划野餐。
Synonyms: mutability, variability, changeability, fluidity
Antonyms: immutability, constancy, permanence, stability
