carry-all-before-one: /ˌkæri ɔːl bɪˈfɔːr wʌn/ | adj. | 势不可挡的，非常成功的，所向披靡的 | Overwhelmingly successful; dominant, triumphant.  Describes someone or something that is so successful that it defeats all opposition. |  Her new product carried all before it, becoming a bestseller in just a few weeks. 她的新产品势如破竹，几周内就成了畅销书。 | Synonyms: triumphant, victorious, dominant, invincible, unstoppable | Antonyms: unsuccessful, failing, losing, defeated | adjective

carry-all-before-one: /ˌkæri ɔːl bɪˈfɔːr wʌn/ | n. |  (旧) 一种大手提包，一种大袋子 | (archaic) A large handbag or bag. | She packed all her belongings in a carry-all-before-one and set off on her journey. 她把所有 belongings 打包进一个大手提包，然后开始了她的旅程。 | Synonyms: tote bag, holdall, duffel bag | Antonyms: N/A | noun
