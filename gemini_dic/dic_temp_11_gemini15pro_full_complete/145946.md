overdiscreetnesses: /ˌoʊvər dɪˈskriːtnəsɪz/
n.
过度谨慎；过分小心；谨小慎微
Excessive cautiousness or reserve in speech or behavior.
His overdiscreetnesses in handling the situation led to further complications.  他在处理情况时的谨小慎微导致了进一步的复杂化。

Her overdiscreetnesses made her seem unapproachable. 她过分的谨慎让她看起来难以接近。

近义词:  cautiousness, reserve, prudence, discretion, circumspection
反义词:  indiscretion, recklessness, impulsiveness, boldness
词性: noun (plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
over- (prefix) + discreetness
"over-"  from Old English "ofer" meaning "above, beyond, across"
"discreetness" from  "discreet"  from Old French "discret" meaning "prudent, wise," ultimately from Latin "discretus" "separated, distinguished," past participle of "discernere" "to separate, distinguish."
