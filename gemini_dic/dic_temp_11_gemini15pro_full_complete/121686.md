misportrayal:/ˌmɪspɔrˈtreɪəl/| n. |歪曲；误传；失真|An act or instance of portraying inaccurately or misleadingly. |The media's misportrayal of the event caused widespread confusion. 媒体对事件的歪曲报道造成了广泛的混乱。|Synonyms: misrepresentation, distortion, falsification, perversion |Antonyms: accurate portrayal, faithful representation| noun
