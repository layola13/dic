mitral: /ˈmaɪtrəl/ | adj. | 二尖瓣的；僧帽形的 | Relating to the mitral valve of the heart, which lies between the left atrium and the left ventricle. | The patient was diagnosed with mitral valve prolapse.  病人被诊断出患有二尖瓣脱垂。 |  The doctor listened carefully to the mitral valve sounds. 医生仔细地听了二尖瓣的声音。 | synonyms: bicuspid | antonyms: (No direct antonym for an anatomical term) | adjective

Etymology->
From Late Latin *mitralis, from mitra (“mitre, headband”), from Ancient Greek μίτρα (mítra, “headband, turban”). So called because the valve resembles a bishop's mitre.
