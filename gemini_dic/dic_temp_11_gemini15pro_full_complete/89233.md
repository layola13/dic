homier:/ˈhoʊmiər/ (US) /ˈhəʊmiər/ (UK)| adj.|更像家一样的，更舒适的，更亲切的|Feeling or looking more like home; cozy and comfortable; suggesting family life.  It implies a sense of warmth, comfort, and familiarity.|例句：Her new apartment is much homier than her old one. 她的新公寓比旧公寓更有家的感觉。

例句：The fireplace made the living room feel homier. 壁炉让客厅感觉更温馨。|近义词：cozier, more comfortable, more inviting, warmer, friendlier|反义词：less inviting, colder, less comfortable, unwelcoming, impersonal|词性:形容词
