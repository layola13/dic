overengineers:/ˌoʊvərˌɛndʒɪˈnɪrz/ | v. | 过度设计；过度工程化 | To design or build something in an excessively complex or elaborate way, often beyond what is necessary or practical. |  The team overengineered the product, adding features that no one really needed.  团队过度设计了产品，添加了一些没人真正需要的功能。| overdesigns, overcomplicates | underengineers, simplifies | verb
overengineer:/ˌoʊvərˌɛndʒɪˈnɪr/ | n. | 过度设计者；过度工程化的人 | A person who overengineers things. | He's a classic overengineer, always making things more complicated than they need to be. 他是一个典型的过度设计者，总是把事情搞得比实际需要的复杂。 | overdesigner | simplifier | noun
