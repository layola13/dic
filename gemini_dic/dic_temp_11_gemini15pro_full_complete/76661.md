galvanically:/ɡælˈvænɪkli/| adv.| 电流地，以电流方式，通过电镀| relating to or involving electric currents, especially when produced by chemical action; in a way that resembles the action of an electric shock on the nerves; suddenly and dramatically. | The two metals were joined galvanically.  这两种金属以电流方式连接在一起。| The news galvanically propelled him into action. 这消息像电流一样促使他采取行动。| synonym: electrically, electrolytically| antonym: chemically (in a non-galvanic way) | adverb

galvanize:/ˈɡælvənaɪz/| vt.|  电镀，刺激，激励| shock or excite (someone) into taking action. coat (iron or steel) with a protective layer of zinc.|  The news galvanized him into action. 这条消息促使他立即行动起来。| The steel is galvanized to prevent rust. 钢铁经过镀锌以防生锈。| synonym: stimulate, excite, arouse, stir, prod, jolt, shock, electrify, energize | antonym: demotivate, discourage, depress | verb
