by-law:/ˈbaɪlɔː/ (US) /ˈbaɪlɔː/ (UK)
n.
规章制度；内部章程
A rule made by a local authority or by an organization such as a club, which has the force of law.
The club’s by-laws state that no alcohol is allowed on the premises.  俱乐部的规章制度规定，场所内禁止饮酒。
regulation, ordinance, rule
law
