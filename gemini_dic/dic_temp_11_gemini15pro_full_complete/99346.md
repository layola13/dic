interdefinability:/ˌɪntərˌdɪˌfaɪnəˈbɪləti/| n.|相互可定义性|The property of two or more concepts or terms being definable in terms of each other.|The interdefinability of mass and energy is a cornerstone of modern physics. 质量和能量的相互可定义性是现代物理学的基石。|synonym: interchangeability, equivalence|antonym: independence, distinction|noun
