laymen:/ˈleɪmən/| n. |俗人，非神职人员|A person who is not a member of the clergy.|The church was filled with both clergymen and laymen. 教堂里挤满了神职人员和平信徒。|synonyms: nonclerical, secular|antonyms: cleric, clergyman|noun
