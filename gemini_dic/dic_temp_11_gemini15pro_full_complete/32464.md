china-blue:/ˈtʃaɪnə ˈbluː/| adj.|中国蓝，一种鲜艳的蓝色|A vivid shade of blue, similar to Prussian blue.  It derives its name from its association with Chinese porcelain. |The vase was a beautiful china-blue.  那个花瓶是美丽的中国蓝。|近义词：Prussian blue, azure, sapphire|反义词：(No direct antonym for a color, could use contrasting colors like yellow, orange)|词性:形容词
