externals:/ɪkˈstɜːrnlz/ | n. | 外部事物；外部因素；外在因素；（尤指房屋的）外部，外观 |  Relating to the outside or outer part; things that are external or outside. | The externals of the building were impressive, but the inside was disappointing. 这座建筑的外观令人印象深刻，但内部却令人失望。 | synonyms: exterior, outside, outward, surface | antonyms: internal, inside, inner, intrinsic | noun

externals:/ɪkˈstɜːrnlz/ | adj. | 外部的；外来的；外面的；表面的 |  Relating to the outside or outer part.  (Less common as an adjective) |  External factors influenced the outcome of the experiment. 外部因素影响了实验结果。  | synonyms: exterior, outside, outward, surface | antonyms: internal, inside, inner, intrinsic | adjective
