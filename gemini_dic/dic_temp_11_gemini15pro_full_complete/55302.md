dissolution:/ˌdɪsəˈluːʃən/
n.
解散, 溶解, 终止, 瓦解
the act of formally ending a parliament, marriage, business, etc. / the process of dissolving something in a liquid / the ending of something
例句：The dissolution of the company left many people unemployed. 公司的解散让很多人失业。

例句：The dissolution of sugar in water is a simple scientific process. 糖在水中溶解是一个简单的科学过程。

近义词：termination, disbandment, disintegration, dissolving, breakup
反义词：formation, establishment, union, solidification
