culvertailed: /ˈkʌlvərˌteɪld/ | adj. | 鸠尾榫接的；紧密连接的；环环相扣的 | Joined or fitted together by means of a culvertail joint, characterized by a flaring tenon that fits into a corresponding mortise, ensuring a strong and interlocking connection.  Also used figuratively to describe things that are closely integrated or interlocked. | The drawers of the antique dresser were culvertailed, a sign of quality craftsmanship.  古董梳妆台的抽屉是鸠尾榫接的，这标志着精湛的工艺。 | The different sections of the report were carefully culvertailed to present a cohesive argument.  报告的不同部分经过精心衔接，形成了一个连贯的论点。 | 近义词: dovetailed, interlocked, integrated | 反义词: disconnected, separate, detached | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From "culver," an obsolete word for dove or pigeon, and "tail," referring to the resemblance of the joint to a dove's tail.
