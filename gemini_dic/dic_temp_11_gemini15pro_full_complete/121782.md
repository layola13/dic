misreckon:/ˌmɪsrɛkən/
verb
估算错误，算错
to calculate or estimate incorrectly
例句：
He misreckoned the cost of the project and ended up going way over budget.  他错误地估算了项目的成本，结果大大超出了预算。

The store manager misreckoned the demand for the new product and ordered too few units.  商店经理错误估计了新产品的需求，订购的数量太少了。

近义词：miscalculate, underestimate, overestimate
反义词：calculate correctly, estimate accurately
词性：v.
