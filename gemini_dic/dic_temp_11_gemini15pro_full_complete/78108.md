geological:/ˌdʒiːəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 地质的；地质学的 | Relating to the study of the earth, its structure, and its history. | The geological survey revealed rich mineral deposits. 地质调查显示了丰富的矿藏。 | geophysical | - | adjective
