outrunner:/ˈaʊtrʌnər/| n.|外转子电机|An electric motor where the outer shell rotates around the inner core, rather than the other way around as in an inrunner motor.  It's commonly used in model aircraft, drones, and electric vehicles due to its high torque and efficiency at low speeds.|The drone uses an outrunner motor for better performance. 这架无人机使用外转子电机以获得更好的性能。|Synonyms: external rotor motor|Antonyms: inrunner motor|noun
