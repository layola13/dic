foolproof:/ˈfuːlˌpruːf/| adj.|防呆的，万无一失的|Designed so that it cannot fail or be misused even by someone who is foolish or inexperienced.|This new recipe is foolproof - even I can't mess it up. 这个新菜谱万无一失，连我都不会搞砸。|近义词：infallible, fail-safe, reliable|反义词:faulty, unreliable|词性:adjective
