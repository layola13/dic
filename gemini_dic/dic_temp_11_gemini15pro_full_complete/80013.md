go-places:/ˈɡoʊ ˌpleɪsəz/| idiom | likely to be successful |  Likely to become famous or successful. |  She's got a real talent – she's definitely going places. 她很有天赋——她肯定会成功的。 | 近义词：succeed, prosper, thrive, flourish | 反义词: fail, stagnate | idiom

go-places:/ˈɡoʊ ˌpleɪsəz/| idiom | 注定要成功 |  注定要成名或成功。 |  He's a bright young man and I'm sure he'll go places. 他是个聪明的小伙子，我相信他一定会成功的。 | 近义词：be destined for success, be on the road to success | 反义词: be doomed to failure | idiom
