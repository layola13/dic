opaque:/oʊˈpeɪk/| adj.|不透明的；晦涩难懂的|Not able to be seen through; not transparent. Also, hard to understand; unclear.|例句：The windows were opaque with steam. 窗户上满是蒸汽，变得不透明了。

例句：His explanation was opaque and confusing. 他的解释晦涩难懂，令人困惑。|近义词：nontransparent, cloudy, obscure, unclear|反义词：transparent, clear, translucent|词性:形容词

opacous:/oʊˈpeɪkəs/| adj.(罕用，建议使用opaque)|不透明的；晦涩难懂的|Not able to be seen through; not transparent. Also, hard to understand; unclear.|例句：The curtains were thick and opacous, blocking out all light. 窗帘很厚且不透光，挡住了所有的光线。

例句：The legal jargon made the document opacous and difficult to interpret. 法律术语使得这份文件晦涩难懂，难以理解。|近义词：nontransparent, cloudy, obscure, unclear|反义词：transparent, clear, translucent|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *opacus* "shaded, dark," from *ob* "in front of, against" + *a root related to Greek *skepein* "to cover, look at."
