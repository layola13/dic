moro:/ˈmɔːroʊ/ (US), /ˈmɔːrəʊ/ (UK)| n. | 摩尔人；一种菲律宾炖菜 | A Muslim of mixed Berber and Arab descent, especially one from North Africa; A rich stew of meat or fish, often including vegetables and spices, originating in the Philippines. |  例句：The moro pirates raided coastal towns. 摩尔海盗袭击了沿海城镇。

例句：She cooked a delicious moro for dinner. 她晚餐做了一道美味的摩尔炖菜。 | 近义词：Moor (for the person), adobo (for the dish - similar Filipino stew) | 反义词: Christian (for the person - historically),  None (for the dish) | 词性: 名词
