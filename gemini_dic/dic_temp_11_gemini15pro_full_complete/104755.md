kawn:/kɔːn/| n.| 欺骗; 诡计; 骗局| An act of deceiving someone; a trick.| He was the victim of an elaborate kawn. 他是一个精心策划的骗局的受害者。| 近义词: trick, hoax, deception, fraud, swindle | 反义词: honesty, truthfulness | 词性: 名词
词源: From Middle English *cawn, probably from Anglo-Norman *caune, perhaps from Old North French *calenge (“calumny, slander”), from Late Latin calumnia (“false accusation, calumny”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kel- (“to cut, divide”).  More at calumny.
