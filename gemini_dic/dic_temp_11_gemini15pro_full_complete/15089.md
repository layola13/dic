baccare:/ˈbækəriː/
interj.
呸！；去你的！；胡说！
An exclamation of impatience, contempt, or disbelief.  Used to express strong disagreement or dismissal.
Baccare! I don't believe a word of it. 呸！我一个字都不信。
近义词:Nonsense!, Rubbish!, Balderdash!, Fiddlesticks!
反义词:(No direct antonym, but consider words expressing agreement like "Indeed!", "Absolutely!", "Certainly!")
词性:Interjection
