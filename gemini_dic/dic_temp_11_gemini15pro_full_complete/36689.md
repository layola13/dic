collimate: /ˈkɒlɪmeɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːləˌmeɪt/ (US) | v. | 校准；使平行；准直 | to make rays of light or particles parallel | The telescope was collimated to ensure sharp images. 望远镜经过校准以确保图像清晰。|  The beam of light was collimated using a lens.  一束光线用透镜准直。 | synonyms: align, adjust, focus | antonyms: scatter, disperse | verb
