kilometre:/ˈkɪləˌmiːtər/ (US) /ˈkɪləˌmiːtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |公里；千米|A unit of length equal to 1,000 meters.|The marathon is a race of over 42 kilometres.|近义词: kilometer (US spelling)|反义词: (No direct opposite, consider units like millimetre, centimetre, metre)
词性:noun

kilometres:/ˈkɪləˌmiːtərz/ (US) /ˈkɪləˌmiːtəz/ (UK)| n. |公里；千米（复数）|The plural form of kilometre, a unit of length equal to 1,000 meters.|The town is located several kilometres from here.|近义词: kilometers (US spelling)|反义词: (No direct opposite, consider units like millimetres, centimetres, metres)
词性:noun


词源: From French *kilomètre*, from *kilo-* (thousand) + *mètre* (meter).  Ultimately from Greek *khilioi* (thousand) and *metron* (measure).
