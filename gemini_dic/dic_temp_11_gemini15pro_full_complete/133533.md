nonfighters:/ˌnɒnˈfaɪtərz/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˈfaɪtərz/
n.
非战斗人员，非战斗员
People who are not involved in fighting, such as civilians, medical personnel, or chaplains.
The nonfighters were evacuated from the war zone.  非战斗人员从战区撤离。
The Red Cross works to protect nonfighters during conflicts.  红十字会在冲突期间致力于保护非战斗人员。
