harquebus:/ˈhɑːrkəbʌs/| n.|火绳枪|An early type of portable firearm with a long barrel, supported on a tripod or a forked rest when fired.  It was a precursor to the musket.|The harquebus was a revolutionary weapon in its time. 火绳枪在当时是一种革命性的武器。|The soldier fired his harquebus at the enemy. 士兵用火绳枪向敌人射击。|Synonyms: arquebus, handgun, firearm|Antonyms: bow and arrow, sword|noun
