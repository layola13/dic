delightedly:/dɪˈlaɪtɪdli/
| adv. |高兴地，愉快地|In a manner expressing great pleasure or satisfaction.  Showing delight.|She clapped her hands delightedly when she saw the puppy. 她看到小狗高兴地拍手。|近义词：happily, joyfully, gladly, merrily|反义词：sadly, unhappily, gloomily|词性:副词
词源：From the adjective "delighted" + the adverbial suffix "-ly." "Delighted" comes from the verb "delight," which has roots in Old French "deleiter" and ultimately Latin "delectare," meaning "to allure, charm, delight."
