increasableness:/ɪnˌkriːsəbəlnəs/
n.
可增加性; 增大的可能性
The quality of being increasable; capability of being increased.
The increasableness of the country's wealth is dependent on its exports.  这个国家的财富增长潜力取决于其出口。
Synonyms: expandability, extensibility, scalability
Antonyms:  inflexibility, rigidity, unchangeability
词性: noun
