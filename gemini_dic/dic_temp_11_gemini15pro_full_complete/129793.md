non-forest:/ˈnɒnˈfɒrɪst/ | adj. n. | 非森林的，非森林地区 |  Relating to or denoting land that is not covered with forest.  Also, can refer to the land itself. |  Example: Non-forest land is often used for agriculture. 非森林土地通常用于农业。 | Synonym:  unforested, treeless, open, cleared | Antonym: forested, wooded, sylvan |  adjective/noun

non-forest:/ˈnɒnˈfɒrɪst/ | adj. n. | 非森林的，非森林地区 |  Areas not covered by forest, including grasslands, deserts, urban areas, etc. | Example: The study compared biodiversity in forest and non-forest habitats.  这项研究比较了森林和非森林生境的生物多样性。 | Synonym:  non-woodland, unwooded | Antonym: forest, woodland, woods | adjective/noun
