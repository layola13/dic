maturation:/ˌmætʃəˈreɪʃn/| n.|成熟，成熟期|The maturation of the fruit takes several weeks. 水果的成熟需要几个星期。|例句：The project is now entering its final maturation phase.  该项目现在正进入最后的成熟阶段。

例句：Emotional maturation is a key part of adolescence. 情绪成熟是青春期的关键部分。

近义词：development, growth, ripening, evolution|反义词:immaturity, retardation|词性:noun
