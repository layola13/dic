blaubok:/ˈblaʊbɒk/| n. |蓝羚|An extinct species of antelope, formerly found in South Africa. It was bluish-grey in colour and had large, curved horns.|例句：The blaubok was hunted to extinction in the 19th century.  蓝羚在19世纪被猎杀至灭绝。

例句：Museum specimens are all that remain of the blaubok.  博物馆标本是蓝羚仅存的遗物。|近义词：antelope (广义上的羚羊)|反义词: N/A (已灭绝，无反义词)|词性:noun
