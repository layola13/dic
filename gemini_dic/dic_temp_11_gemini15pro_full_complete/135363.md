nonperceptivities:/ˌnɒnpərˌsɛptɪˈvɪtiz/
n.
无知觉；缺乏洞察力
lack of perceptivity; inability to perceive or understand things clearly.
His nonperceptivities about social cues often led to awkward situations. 他对社交暗示的无知觉常常导致尴尬的局面。
Near Antonyms:  perceptiveness, insight, acuity, awareness, discernment
Antonyms: perceptivity, awareness

词性: noun (名词)
