nonviviparity:/ˌnɒnˌvɪvɪˈpærɪti/
n.
非胎生
The state or condition of not being viviparous, i.e., not giving birth to live young.
Most insects exhibit nonviviparity, laying eggs instead of giving birth to live offspring. 大多数昆虫都表现出非胎生性，产卵而不是产下活的后代。
Snakes exhibit a variety of reproductive modes, including oviparity and viviparity, but not nonviviparity, which is simply the absence of viviparity. 蛇类表现出多种繁殖方式，包括卵生和胎生，但不是非胎生，这仅仅是缺少胎生的意思。
