monotonic:/ˌmɒnəˈtɒnɪk/ /ˌmɑːnəˈtɑːnɪk/| adj.|单调的，单一的|Characterized by a single, unchanging tone or direction.  Constantly increasing or constantly decreasing, but not both.|The monotonic sound of the dripping faucet drove me crazy.|近义词：unvarying, unchanging, constant, tedious, dull|反义词：varied, diverse, fluctuating, dynamic|adjective
