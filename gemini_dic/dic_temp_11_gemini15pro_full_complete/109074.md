legated:/ˈlɛɡeɪtɪd/
verb
遗赠；委托；交付
given by will after somebody’s death; given or entrusted to somebody
例句：He has legated his entire estate to his daughter. 他已将全部财产遗赠给女儿。

例句：The task of writing the report was legated to me.  撰写报告的任务交给了我。

近义词：bequeathed, willed, entrusted, assigned
反义词：inherited, received
