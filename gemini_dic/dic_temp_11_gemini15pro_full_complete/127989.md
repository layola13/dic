nepenthes: /nɪˈpɛnθiːz/
n.
猪笼草，忘忧草
A tropical pitcher plant, the leaves of which form a pitcher that attracts and traps insects.
Nepenthes attract insects with sweet nectar, then trap and digest them. 猪笼草用甜甜的花蜜吸引昆虫，然后将其捕获并消化。
The nepenthes is a fascinating example of carnivorous plants. 猪笼草是食肉植物的一个迷人例子。
