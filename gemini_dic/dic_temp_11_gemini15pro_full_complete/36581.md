collate: /kəˈleɪt/ (US) /ˈkɒleɪt/ (UK) | v. | 校对，整理，核对 | To gather and arrange (pages of a book, a manuscript, or the like) in proper sequence. | The clerk collated the documents before the meeting.  职员在会议前整理了文件。| The printer collated and stapled the report. 打印机校对并装订了报告。| synonyms: assemble, organize, gather, arrange | antonyms: disorganize, scatter, disperse | verb

collate: /kəˈleɪt/ (US) /ˈkɒleɪt/ (UK) | adj. | 校对过的，整理过的 | Arranged in proper sequence. |  The collated documents were ready for review. 整理好的文件已准备好供审查。 |  He presented a collated set of data for the analysis. 他提供了一套整理好的数据用于分析。 | synonyms: ordered, arranged, organized | antonyms: disordered, disorganized, unarranged | adjective
