baccalaureates: /ˌbækəˈlɔːriəts/ (US), /bəˌkæləˈrɪərəts/ (UK)| n.| 学士学位（复数）；学士学位授予典礼（复数）|Baccalaureates are awarded to students who complete a four-year undergraduate program.|The university conferred hundreds of baccalaureates this year.|Synonyms: undergraduate degrees, bachelor's degrees|Antonyms: master's degrees, doctoral degrees|noun
