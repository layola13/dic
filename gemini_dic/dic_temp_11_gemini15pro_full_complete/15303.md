backpedaled: /ˌbækˈpɛdəld/ (US), /ˌbækˈpɛdld/ (UK)| v. | 退步，改变立场，收回前言 | To reverse one's previous position or opinion; to retreat from a commitment. | Example 1:  The politician backpedaled on his promise to raise taxes.  这位政客收回了增税的承诺。

Example 2: After realizing his mistake, he quickly backpedaled and apologized.  意识到自己的错误后，他迅速地改变了立场并道歉。 | Synonyms: retreat, withdraw, recant, retract | Antonyms: advance, persist, maintain, uphold | verb
