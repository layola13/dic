aftercare:/ˈɑːftərˌker/| n.|善后护理；后期照顾；后续治疗|The provision of support and treatment following a period of illness, addiction, or trauma.  The support and services provided to someone after they leave an institution such as a hospital, prison, or rehabilitation center.|例句：The hospital provided excellent aftercare for the surgery patients. 这家医院为手术病人提供了优秀的术后护理。

例句：Drug rehabilitation programs often include aftercare to help prevent relapse.  戒毒康复项目通常包括后期护理以帮助防止复发。|近义词：post-treatment care, follow-up care, continuing care|反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps neglect)|词性:noun
