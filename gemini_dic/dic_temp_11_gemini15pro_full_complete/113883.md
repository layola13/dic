madoqua:/məˈdɒkwə/ | n. | 冕羚，犬羚 | A small, slender antelope found in eastern and southern Africa.  These tiny antelopes are known for their large eyes and delicate build. |  Example: The madoqua darted through the tall grass. 冕羚飞快地穿过高高的草丛。

Example:  We saw a herd of madoqua grazing near the watering hole. 我们看到一群冕羚在水坑附近吃草。 | 近义词: dik-dik, suni (other small antelope species) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym due to being a specific animal name.  One could consider larger antelope like eland or kudu as opposites in terms of size.) | 词性: Noun
