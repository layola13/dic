deposit:/dɪˈpɒzɪt/ (UK), /dəˈpɑːzɪt/ (US)| n. vt.| 存款，押金，沉淀物|A sum of money paid into a bank or building society account.| I made a deposit of $500 into my savings account. 我在我的储蓄账户里存了500美元。|synonyms: payment, down payment, installment, security|antonyms: withdrawal, debit|noun

deposit:/dɪˈpɒzɪt/ (UK), /dəˈpɑːzɪt/ (US)| vt.|存放，使沉积|To put or set down; place.|The flood deposited a layer of mud in the streets. 洪水在街道上沉积了一层泥。|synonyms: place, put, lay, leave|antonyms: remove, withdraw|verb
