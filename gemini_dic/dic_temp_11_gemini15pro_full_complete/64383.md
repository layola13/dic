erector:/ɪˈrɛktər/| n.|安装者，勃起肌，竖立器|A person or thing that erects something, especially a structure or building. Also, a muscle that causes a part of the body to become erect.|The erector set was a favorite toy of his childhood.  他童年最喜欢的玩具是安装玩具。 The erector spinae muscles help to maintain upright posture. 竖脊肌有助于保持直立姿势。|近义词：builder, constructor, assembler|反义词:demolisher, destroyer|词性:noun

erect:/ɪˈrɛkt/| v. adj.|竖立，建造，直立的|To build or construct something, especially a building or structure. Also, upright in position or posture.|They erected a statue in his honor.  他们为他竖立了一座雕像。  He stood erect, shoulders back. 他笔直地站着，肩膀向后.|近义词：build, construct, raise, upright|反义词:demolish, destroy, dismantle, horizontal|词性:verb, adjective
