continent:/ˈkɒntɪnənt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntɪnənt/ (US)| n. |大陆，洲|A continent is one of the world's main continuous expanses of land.|Asia is the largest continent. 亚洲是最大的洲。|近义词：mainland |反义词:island |词性:noun

continent:/ˈkɒntɪnənt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntɪnənt/ (US)| adj. |自制的，克制的| Exercising self-restraint, especially sexually.|He is a continent man. 他是一个自制的人。|近义词：abstinent, temperate |反义词:incontinent |词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *continent, from Latin continentem (nominative continens), present participle of continēre "to hold together, contain, enclose, bound, restrain," from com- "together" (see com-) + tenēre "to hold" (from PIE root *ten- "to stretch").
