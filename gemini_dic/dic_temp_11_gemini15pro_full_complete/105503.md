kilobytes:/ˈkɪləˌbaɪts/| n. |千字节|A unit of information equal to 1,000 bytes (or, more accurately, 1,024 bytes).  A kilobyte is often used to measure the size of small files or the capacity of small storage devices. |This image file is only 15 kilobytes.  这个图片文件只有15千字节。

The floppy disk held a maximum of 1.44 megabytes, which is about 1,440 kilobytes.  这张软盘最大容量为1.44兆字节，约为1440千字节。|近义词：KB|反义词: (No direct antonym, but larger units like megabytes, gigabytes, terabytes exist)|词性:noun
