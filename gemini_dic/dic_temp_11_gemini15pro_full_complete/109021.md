leftwardly: /ˈleftwərdli/ | adv. | 向左地 |  Toward the left.  |  The crab scuttled leftwardly across the sand.  螃蟹向左横穿过沙滩。 | leftwards, toward the left | rightwardly, toward the right | adverb

词源:
From "leftward" + "-ly". "Leftward" comes from Old English "lyftweard," meaning "turned toward the left."
