bye-laws:/ˈbaɪlɔːz/
noun
规章制度；内部章程
Rules or laws made by an organization or company to control its own members.
The club’s bye-laws state that no children under the age of twelve are allowed to use the swimming pool.  俱乐部的规章制度规定，12岁以下的儿童不得使用游泳池。
Synonyms: regulations, rules, ordinances
Antonyms: guidelines (can be less formal)
n.
