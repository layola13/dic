forgetters:/fərˈɡetərz/| n. |健忘的人；容易忘记的人|People who tend to forget things. |  例句：The forgetters left their umbrellas on the bus. 健忘的人把他们的雨伞落在公交车上了。

例句：In a world of rapid change, forgetters are at a disadvantage. 在一个快速变化的世界里，健忘的人处于劣势。| 近义词：absent-minded people, scatterbrained people | 反义词： mindful people, people with good memories | 词性: 名词
