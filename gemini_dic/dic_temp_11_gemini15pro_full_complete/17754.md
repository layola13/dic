bedfast:/ˈbedfæst/| adj.|卧床不起的，长期卧床的|Confined to bed by illness or infirmity.|例句：She was bedfast for months after the accident. 事故发生后，她卧床不起好几个月。

例句：His grandfather has been bedfast for over a year. 他的祖父已经卧床不起一年多了。

近义词：bedridden, confined to bed|反义词:ambulatory, mobile|词性:adjective
