burned-up:/bɜːrnd ˈʌp/ | adj. | 恼火的，生气的；烧毁的 | Feeling annoyed or angry; destroyed by fire. |  The driver was burned up about the traffic jam. 司机对交通堵塞感到很恼火。 | The house was burned up in the fire. 房子在大火中被烧毁了。 | 近义词：angry, furious, incinerated | 反义词：calm, pleased, intact | 词性:形容词

burned-up:/bɜːrnd ˈʌp/ | adj. | 精疲力尽的 |  Completely exhausted. | I'm burned up after that marathon. 跑完马拉松后我累得不行了。 |  She was burned up from working so many hours. 她工作了这么长时间，精疲力尽。 | 近义词：exhausted, worn out, spent | 反义词：energetic, refreshed | 词性:形容词
