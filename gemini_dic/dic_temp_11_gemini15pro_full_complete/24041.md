breezes:/ˈbriːzɪz/| n.|微风，轻风|A gentle wind.|A cool breeze swept across the beach. 一阵凉爽的微风掠过海滩。|近义词：winds, zephyrs|反义词:gales, gusts|noun

词源: From Old Spanish *briza "northeast wind," probably from Vulgar Latin *britia, of unknown origin.
