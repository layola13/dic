camphor:/ˈkæmfər/ (US) /ˈkæmfə(r)/ (UK)| n.|樟脑|A white, volatile, crystalline substance with a strong aromatic odor, obtained from the camphor tree and used in medicine and mothballs.|The smell of camphor filled the air. 空气中弥漫着樟脑的味道。|Synonyms: naphthalene, paradichlorobenzene|Antonyms: None|noun

camphor:/ˈkæmfər/ (US) /ˈkæmfə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|樟脑的|Made of or containing camphor.|Camphor oil is used in aromatherapy. 樟脑油用于芳香疗法。|Synonyms: camphoraceous|Antonyms: None|adjective
