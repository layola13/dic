engineeringly:/ˌɛndʒɪˈnɪrɪŋli/
adv.
工程地；从工程角度；像工程师一样
In a manner characteristic of engineering or engineers.
The bridge was engineeringly sound. 这座桥的工程结构很稳固。

近义词: technically, scientifically
反义词:  (No clear antonym exists, perhaps "unskillfully" or "amateurishly" in context)
词性: adverb

词源: From "engineering" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Engineering" itself comes from the Old French "engin" (skill, ingenuity, war machine), ultimately from Latin "ingenium" (inborn quality, talent).
