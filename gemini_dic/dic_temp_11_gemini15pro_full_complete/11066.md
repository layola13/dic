archaeological:/ˌɑːrkiəˈlɒdʒɪkl/ (US) /ˌɑːrkiəˈlɑːdʒɪkl/| adj.|考古学的；考古学上的|Relating to the study of human history and prehistory through the excavation of sites and the analysis of artifacts and other physical remains.|例句：Archaeological evidence suggests the city was founded in the 10th century. 考古证据表明这座城市建于10世纪。

例句：The archaeological dig unearthed a treasure trove of ancient pottery. 考古挖掘出土了大量古代陶器。

近义词：historical, ancient, prehistoric|反义词:modern, contemporary|词性:adjective
