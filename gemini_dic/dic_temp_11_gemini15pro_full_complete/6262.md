amole: /əˈmoʊli/ (US), /əˈməʊleɪ/ (UK)| n.| 阿莫尔肥皂;龙舌兰;一种清洁剂|A soap or detergent made from the roots of certain agave plants, or the plant itself.  It's used for washing clothes and bodies, and sometimes as a fish poison.|The women used amole to wash their hair.|近义词:soap, detergent, yucca|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *amole*, from Nahuatl *āmoli*.
