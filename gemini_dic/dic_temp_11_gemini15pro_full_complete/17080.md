batiste:/bæˈtiːst/ /ˈbætɪst/| n.|巴蒂斯特；一种细棉布|A fine, plain-weave cotton cloth, typically used for handkerchiefs and lingerie.|She used a delicate batiste to make the baby's christening gown. 她用一块精致的巴蒂斯特布料为婴儿做洗礼袍。|The curtains were made of a sheer batiste that filtered the sunlight beautifully. 窗帘是用一种薄薄的巴蒂斯特布料制成的，它能很好地过滤阳光。|近义词：cambric, lawn, muslin|反义词:canvas, denim|noun
