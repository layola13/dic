overintensified: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈtɛnsɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 过度强化; 过度加强 |  Made excessive or overly intense. It describes something that has been intensified beyond a normal or appropriate level. | The overintensified colors in the photograph made it look unnatural. 照片中过度强化的颜色使其看起来不自然。| The actor's overintensified emotions seemed melodramatic. 演员过度强化的情绪显得有些夸张。 | 近义词: exaggerated, amplified, heightened, overstated, overdone | 反义词: understated, subdued, muted, dampened | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive, too much") + "intensify" (verb meaning "to make or become more intense") + "-ed" (suffix forming past participle and adjectives)
