hypernormality:/ˌhaɪpərˌnɔːrˈmælɪti/ | n. | 超常态，极度正常 |  A state or condition exceeding or surpassing what is considered normal, often to an excessive or exaggerated degree.  It can refer to a situation where outwardly everything appears normal, even perfect, but underlying this facade are serious problems or a sense of unease. It can also refer to an individual's behavior that is excessively conforming to social norms. | The pristine lawns and perfectly matching houses of the suburb gave a sense of hypernormality that masked the residents' anxieties. 这个郊区原始的草坪和完全匹配的房屋给人一种超常态的感觉，掩盖了居民的焦虑。

The CEO's hypernormality in the face of the company's impending collapse was unsettling. 首席执行官在公司即将倒闭面前的极度正常令人不安。 | synonyms:  over-conformity,  exaggerated normalcy, artificial normalcy,  superficial normalcy | antonyms: abnormality, deviance, eccentricity, nonconformity | noun
