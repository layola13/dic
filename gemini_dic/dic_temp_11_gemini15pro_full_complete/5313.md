allow for:/əˈlaʊ fɔːr/ | phrasal verb | 考虑到，顾及，为…留出余地 | to consider something when making a plan or calculation | We need to allow for extra time in case of traffic delays. 我们需要预留额外的时间以防交通延误。| consider, factor in, take into account | disregard, ignore | verb phrase

allow for:/əˈlaʊ fɔːr/ | phrasal verb | 考虑到，顾及，为…留出余地 | to consider the possibility of something happening | The architect allowed for the building to expand in the future.  建筑师考虑到了未来建筑的扩建。| anticipate, foresee, provide for | neglect, overlook | verb phrase


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *alowien for*, equivalent to *allow* + *for*. *Allow* comes from Old French *alouer*, meaning "to approve, praise, commend, allow," from Latin *allaudare*, meaning "to praise." *For* comes from Old English *for*, a preposition with various meanings related to cause, purpose, and direction.
