opiating:/ˈoʊpieɪtɪŋ/
verb
使麻醉；使沉迷
acting like or having the effect of an opiate; causing dullness or inaction.
The medication was opiating her, making her drowsy and unresponsive.  这种药物使她麻醉，让她昏昏欲睡，没有反应。
opiating:/ˈoʊpieɪtɪŋ/
adjective
麻醉的；使人沉迷的
causing a state resembling that produced by opium or an opiate.
The opiating effects of the drug wore off quickly. 这种药物的麻醉效果很快就消失了。


近义词:narcotizing, sedating, stupefying
反义词:stimulating, invigorating
词性: verb, adjective
