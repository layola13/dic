ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "to board," which comes from the Old English "bord," meaning "plank" or "table."  The meaning evolved to include the act of going onto a ship via a plank and later expanded to other forms of transportation. For the "meals" meaning, it refers to the provision of meals at a shared "board" or table.
