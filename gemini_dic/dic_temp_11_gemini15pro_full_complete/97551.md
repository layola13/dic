ingrave:/ɪnˈɡreɪv/ | v. | 雕刻；铭刻 | To carve or cut (words, designs, etc.) into a hard surface such as wood, stone, or metal. |  The jeweler will ingrave our initials inside the rings. 珠宝商会在戒指内侧刻上我们名字的首字母。


The inscription was ingraved on the tombstone. 墓碑上刻着碑文。


| 近义词: carve, etch, inscribe | 反义词: erase, efface | 词性: 动词
