orthodoxical: /ˌɔːrθəˈdɒksɪkəl/ | adj. | 正统的，传统的 | Conforming to established doctrine, especially in religion; conventional. |  Her views on education are quite orthodoxical. 她的教育观点相当传统。| The design of the building was surprisingly orthodoxical. 这栋楼的设计出奇地传统。| 近义词: conventional, traditional, customary, established, accepted | 反义词: unorthodox, unconventional, heterodox, heretical, radical | 词性: 形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin orthodoxus + -ical.  Late Latin *orthodoxus from Greek orthódoxos ‘right-believing,’ from orthós ‘right, straight’ + dóxa ‘opinion, belief.’
