icons: /ˈaɪkɒnz/ (UK) /ˈaɪkɑːnz/ (US)| n. |图标；偶像；象征|A graphic symbol on a computer display screen that represents an app, a file, or some other object or function.  A person or thing regarded as a representative symbol of something. |例句：The icons on the desktop are easy to click. 桌面上图标很容易点击。
