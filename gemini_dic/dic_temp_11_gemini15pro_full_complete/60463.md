either: /ˈiːðər/ (US) /ˈaɪðər/ (UK)| determiner, pronoun, conjunction|两者之中任一的；两者之中每一个的；也（用于否定句）| Used to refer to one or the other of two people or things; used to indicate similarity or link with a statement just made.|例句：You can park on either side of the street. 你可以把车停在街道的任何一边。|近义词：each, any, both (in some contexts)|反义词：neither|词性: determiner, pronoun, conjunction

either: /ˈiːðər/ (US) /ˈaɪðər/ (UK)| adverb|也（用于否定句）|Also (used in negative sentences).|例句：I don't like it either. 我也不喜欢。|近义词：too, also (in affirmative sentences)|反义词：neither|词性: adverb
