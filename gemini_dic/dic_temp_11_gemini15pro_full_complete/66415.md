executor:/ˈɛksɪˌkjutər/ (US), /ɪɡˈzɛkjʊtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|执行者，遗嘱执行人|A person or institution appointed by a testator to carry out the terms of their will.|The executor of the will was responsible for distributing the assets.|Synonyms: administrator, trustee, enforcer|Antonyms: beneficiary, heir|noun

executor:/ˈɛksɪˌkjutər/ (US), /ɪɡˈzɛkjʊtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|执行者，实行者|A person who carries out a plan, order, or course of action.|The executors of the project ensured it was completed on time.|Synonyms: implementer, performer, operator|Antonyms: planner, designer|noun
