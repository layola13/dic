catalyst: /ˈkætəlɪst/| n. |催化剂；促进因素|A substance that increases the rate of a chemical reaction without itself undergoing any permanent chemical change. Also, a person or thing that precipitates an event.|例句：The enzyme acts as a catalyst in the digestion of food. 这种酶在食物消化过程中起催化剂的作用。

例句：His arrest proved to be the catalyst for the riots. 他的被捕证明是骚乱的导火索。|近义词：stimulus, impetus, trigger|反义词:inhibitor, deterrent|词性:noun
