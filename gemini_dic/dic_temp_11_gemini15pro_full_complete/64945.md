esteeming:/ɪˈstiːmɪŋ/
|v.|尊敬；尊重；认为；估价|present participle of esteem|They are esteeming him highly for his contributions. 他们非常尊重他为所做的贡献。|近义词:respecting, valuing, appreciating, admiring|反义词:disrespecting, scorning, despising|verb

词源:
From Middle English *estemen*, borrowed from Old French *estimer* (“to estimate, appraise, value, esteem”), from Latin *aestimāre* (“to estimate, value, rate, assess”), from *aes*, *aeris* (“copper, bronze, money”) (whence the notion of appraisal, assessment) + *-timāre* (frequentative suffix related to the verb *timēre* (“to fear, be afraid of”)).
