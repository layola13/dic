eveners:/ˈiːvənərz/| n. |平衡器，使相等的人或物|Something that makes things even or equal.  They used eveners on the horse-drawn plow to distribute the weight evenly.|The team of horses pulled the plow more efficiently thanks to the eveners.|近义词：equalizers, levelers|反义词: imbalances, differentiators|词性: 名词

Etymology: From "even" (adjective) + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting agents or instruments).  The word "even" comes from Old English "efen," meaning "level," "equal," "flat."
