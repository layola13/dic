innerness:/ˈɪnərnəs/
n.
内心，内在，精神世界
The quality or state of being inner or inward; intimacy with oneself.
She cultivated a sense of innerness through meditation. 她通过冥想培养了一种内在的感觉。
近义词：interiority, inwardness, soulfulness
反义词：externality, superficiality
词性：名词


Etymology:
From inner + -ness.  "Inner" traces back to Old English "innera," comparative of "in," meaning "in, within."  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, indicating state or quality.
