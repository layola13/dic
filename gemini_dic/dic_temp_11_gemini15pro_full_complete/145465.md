overclaims: /ˌoʊvərˈkleɪmz/
verb
过分要求；夸大其词；索赔过多
to state that something is bigger, better or more important than it really is, especially in order to impress people.
The company overclaimed the effectiveness of its product. 这家公司夸大了其产品的效果。

近义词: exaggerate, overstate, inflate, oversell
反义词: understate, downplay, minimize

verb
(保险) 超额索赔
to claim more money from an insurance company than you are entitled to.
He was accused of overclaiming on his insurance. 他被指控在保险上索赔过多。

近义词: defraud, swindle
反义词: underclaim


词性: verb (动词)
