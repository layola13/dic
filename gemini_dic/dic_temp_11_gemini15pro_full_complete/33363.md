chun:/tʃʌn/ | n. | 块，大块 | A thick piece or chunk of something. | He cut the cheese into large chuns. 他把奶酪切成大块。 |  近义词：chunk, lump, hunk, piece | 反义词: crumb, sliver | 词性:名词

词源: "Chun" likely originates from imitative or descriptive roots, possibly related to words suggesting a short, thick shape or sound.  Its more precise etymology is uncertain.
