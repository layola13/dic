macrame: /ˈmækrəmeɪ/ (US), /ˈmækrəˌmeɪ/ (UK)| n.| 壁挂;花结;绳结工艺|The art of knotting cord or string in patterns to make decorative articles, such as wall hangings.|She made a beautiful macrame wall hanging for her living room. 她为她的客厅做了一个漂亮的壁挂。|近义词：knotting, lacework|反义词: - |Noun
