dispersedness:/dɪˈspɜːrsdnəs/ /dɪˈspɝːsdnəs/
n.
分散性，分散度
The state or quality of being dispersed or scattered.
The dispersedness of the seeds ensured the survival of the species. 种子的分散性确保了物种的生存。
The researcher measured the dispersedness of the particles in the solution. 研究人员测量了溶液中颗粒的分散度。
近义词：dispersion, scattering, distribution, dissemination
反义词：concentration, aggregation, clumping
词性：名词

Etymology:
From disperse + -ed + -ness.  "Disperse" comes from Latin *dispersus, past participle of dispergere "to scatter," from dis- "apart, asunder" + spargere "to strew, scatter."
