bandies:/ˈbændiz/ | n. | (bandy的复数形式，罕用) 弯曲的双腿 |  (plural of bandy) legs that curve outward at the knees |  He had bandies, which made him walk a bit awkwardly. 他罗圈腿，走路有点别扭。 | synonyms: bowed legs, crooked legs | antonyms: straight legs | noun
