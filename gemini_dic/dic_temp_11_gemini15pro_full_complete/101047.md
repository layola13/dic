inveiglement:/ɪnˈveɪɡlmənt/ |n.|引诱，诱惑，怂恿|The act of persuading someone to do something by means of deception or flattery.|His inveiglement into the scheme cost him his fortune. 他被诱骗参与这个计划，赔光了家产。|Synonyms: enticement, seduction, allurement, cajolery|Antonyms: dissuasion, deterrence|noun

inveigle:/ɪnˈveɪɡl/ |v.|引诱，诱惑，怂恿|To persuade someone to do something by means of deception or flattery.|She inveigled him into buying her a diamond necklace. 她引诱他给她买了一条钻石项链。|Synonyms: entice, seduce, allure, cajole|Antonyms: dissuade, deter|verb
