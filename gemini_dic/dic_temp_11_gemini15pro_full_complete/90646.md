humanizing:/ˈhjuːmənaɪzɪŋ/| adj.|使更人性化的，赋予人性的|Making something more humane or giving it human qualities.|例句：The company is trying to humanize its image by focusing on the stories of its employees.  该公司正试图通过关注员工的故事来使其形象更加人性化。

例句：Humanizing the workplace can lead to increased productivity and employee satisfaction.  使工作场所更人性化可以提高生产力和员工满意度。|近义词：personalizing, individualizing, warming, softening|反义词：dehumanizing, impersonalizing|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From humanize + -ing.  Humanize comes from Late Latin humanus  "human" + -ize.  Ultimately from Latin homo "man, human being".
