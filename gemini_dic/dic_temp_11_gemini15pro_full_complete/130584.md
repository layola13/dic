nonacuity:/ˌnɒnəˈkjuːɪti/
noun
非敏锐，迟钝
Lack of sharpness or acuteness, especially of vision or perception.
His nonacuity made him miss the subtle clues in the painting. 他的迟钝使他错过了画中微妙的线索。
Near Antonyms: acuity, sharpness, keenness
Antonyms: blindness, dullness
