half-boiling:/ˌhɑːf ˈbɔɪlɪŋ/
adjective
半沸腾的；半煮的
partially boiling; not fully boiling; simmering.
The chef instructed to keep the sauce half-boiling for a richer flavor. 厨师指示要保持酱汁半沸腾以获得更浓郁的味道。
The water was half-boiling, ready for the pasta. 水半开着，可以下意大利面了。
near synonyms: simmering, gently boiling
antonym: fully boiling, rapidly boiling
word type: adjective
