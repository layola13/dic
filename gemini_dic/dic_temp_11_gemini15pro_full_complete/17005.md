bat-in:/ˈbætɪn/ | n. |击球得分; 打点| A run batted in (RBI) in baseball, scored when a batter hits the ball in such a way that a base runner is able to advance and score a run, except when the batter hits into a double play or reaches base on an error. |  The cleanup hitter had three bat-ins in the game.  那场比赛中，第四棒打者有三个打点。 | synonyms: RBI, run batted in | antonyms: N/A | noun

bat-in:/ˈbætɪn/ | adj. |击球得分的 | Relating to or characterized by a run batted in. | He had a bat-in single in the bottom of the ninth. 他在第九局下半场击出一支安打得分。 | synonyms: RBI, run batted in | antonyms: N/A | adjective
