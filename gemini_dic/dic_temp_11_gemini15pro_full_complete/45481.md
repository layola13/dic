curbstone:/ˈkɜːrbstoʊn/ (US) /ˈkɜːbstəʊn/ (UK)| n. |路缘石，界石|A row of stones bordering a street or sidewalk, separating it from the roadway.|例句：The car scraped against the curbstone. 汽车擦到了路缘石。

例句：She tripped over a loose curbstone. 她被一块松动的路缘石绊倒了。

近义词：kerbstone (UK), curb, edging|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps roadway/street) |词性:noun
