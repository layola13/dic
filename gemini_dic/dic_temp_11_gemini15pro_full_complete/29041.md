cartouche:/kɑːrˈtuːʃ/| n.|涡卷形装饰;  铭牌;  弹药筒;|An oval or oblong figure, as in ancient Egyptian hieroglyphic inscriptions, enclosing a sovereign's name, or a scroll-like ornament or frame.|The pharaoh's name was written inside a cartouche. 法老的名字写在涡卷形装饰里。|synonyms: ornament, decoration, emblem, insignia, badge|antonyms: plainness, simplicity|noun

cartouche:/kɑːrˈtuːʃ/| n.| (纸)盒;  (尤指旧时士兵用的)子弹盒;|A cartridge, especially one for a firearm.|The soldiers carried their cartouches filled with ammunition. 士兵们带着装满弹药的子弹盒。|synonyms: cartridge, ammunition box, clip|antonyms: N/A|noun
