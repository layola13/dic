aesthete:/ˈɛsθiːt/ /ˈisθiːt/ | n. | 审美家；唯美主义者 | A person who has or affects to have a special appreciation of art and beauty. |  He’s a self-proclaimed aesthete with a passion for opera. 他自称是一位热爱歌剧的审美家。

He filled his home with artworks, reflecting his aesthete sensibilities. 他把家里摆满了艺术品，反映了他审美家的情趣。

| 近义词:connoisseur, art lover, dilettante | 反义词:philistine, barbarian | 词性:noun
