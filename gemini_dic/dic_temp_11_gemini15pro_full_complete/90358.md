hovercrafts:/ˈhʌvəkrɑːfts/ (US), /ˈhɒvəkrɑːfts/ (UK)| n. |气垫船（复数）|Plural form of hovercraft, referring to multiple vehicles that travel just above the surface of water or land, supported by a cushion of air.|The company operates a fleet of hovercrafts between the islands. 这家公司在岛屿之间运营着一支气垫船队。|synonyms: air-cushion vehicles, ACVs|antonyms: N/A|noun
