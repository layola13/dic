opulent:/ˈɒpjʊlənt/ (British), /ˈɑːpjələnt/ (American)| adj.| 奢华的，豪华的，富丽堂皇的|Characterized by rich abundance or luxuriousness; lavish.  Displaying or suggesting great wealth and luxuriousness.|例句：The opulent hotel lobby impressed all the guests. 富丽堂皇的酒店大堂给所有客人都留下了深刻的印象。

例句：She lived an opulent lifestyle, filled with designer clothes and exotic vacations. 她过着奢华的生活，充满了名牌服装和异国度假。|近义词：luxurious, lavish, sumptuous, affluent, wealthy|反义词：austere, simple, modest, poor, impoverished|词性: adjective
