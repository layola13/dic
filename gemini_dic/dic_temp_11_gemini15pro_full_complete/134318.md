noninstitutionalized:/ˌnɒnɪnstɪˈtjuːʃənəlaɪzd/
adjective
非机构化的；未住院的；未收容的
Not placed in an institution such as a hospital or prison.
The survey focused on the noninstitutionalized population aged 65 and over.  这项调查的重点是65岁及以上的非机构化人口。
The study compared the health outcomes of institutionalized and noninstitutionalized elderly.  这项研究比较了机构化和非机构化老年人的健康结果。
近义词:community-dwelling, independent-living
反义词:institutionalized
词性:adjective
