dissolvabilities:/dɪˌzɒlvəˈbɪlɪtiz/ | n. | 可溶性(复数) | The quality or state of being dissolvable; the capacity to be dissolved.  It refers to the extent to which a substance can be dissolved in a particular solvent. |  The dissolvabilities of different salts vary greatly. 不同盐类的溶解度差异很大。| solubility, dissolvability | insolubility, indissolubility | noun
