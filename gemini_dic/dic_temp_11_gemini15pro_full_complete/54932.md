dispositive:/dɪˈspɒzɪtɪv/ /dɪˈspɑːzɪtɪv/| adj.| 决定性的，最终的；处置的，配置的|Having or showing the power or authority to decide or settle something.|The judge's ruling was dispositive in the case. 法官的裁决对该案具有决定性。|近义词：conclusive, decisive, definitive, determining|反义词:inconclusive, indecisive|词性:形容词

dispositive:/dɪˈspɒzɪtɪv/ /dɪˈspɑːzɪtɪv/| n.| 处置权，配置权；决定性因素|The power or authority to decide or settle something; a determining factor.|The dispositive factor in her decision was the cost. 她做出决定的决定性因素是成本。|近义词：deciding factor, determinant|反义词: N/A|词性:名词


词源: Late Middle English: from Old French dispositif, -ive, from late Latin dispositivus, from disponere ‘arrange’ (see dispose).
