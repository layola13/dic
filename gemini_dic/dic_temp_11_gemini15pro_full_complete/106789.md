labialism:/ˈleɪbiəlɪzəm/ | n. |唇音化；唇化|The articulation of a speech sound by rounding and protruding the lips.|The pronunciation of the vowel /u/ involves a high degree of labialism.  元音 /u/ 的发音高度唇化。|近义词:rounding|反义词:unrounding|词性:noun

labialize:/ˈleɪbiəlaɪz/ | v. |使唇音化；使唇化|To articulate (a speech sound) by rounding and protruding the lips.|The vowel /i/ can be labialized to produce the sound /y/. 元音 /i/ 可以唇化发成 /y/ 音。|近义词:round|反义词:unround|词性:verb

labialized:/ˈleɪbiəlaɪzd/ | adj. |唇音化的；唇化的|Articulated with the lips rounded and protruded.|The French language has many labialized vowels. 法语有很多唇化元音。|近义词:rounded|反义词:unrounded|词性:adjective
