expectednesses:/ɪkˌspɛktɪdnɪsɪz/
|n.|预期；预料；期待|The state or quality of being expected.  The degree to which something is anticipated.|The expectednesses of his arrival were high, given the importance of the meeting. 考虑到会议的重要性，人们对他的到来充满了期待。|近义词: anticipations, expectations, probabilities|反义词: unexpectedness, surprise|词性:名词

词源: From "expected" (past participle of "expect") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality).  Ultimately from Latin "expectare" (to look out for, await).
