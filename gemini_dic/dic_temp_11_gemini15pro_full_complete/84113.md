half-liberal:/ˌhæf ˈlɪbərəl/ | adj. | 半自由主义的；不彻底的自由主义者 | Someone who is half-liberal is only liberal in some of their views or actions, not completely or consistently. | He has a half-liberal attitude towards immigration, supporting it in principle but worrying about its economic impact. 他对移民持半自由主义态度，原则上支持移民，但又担心其经济影响。 |  The party's half-liberal stance on social issues alienated both its core supporters and potential new voters. 该党在社会问题上的半自由主义立场疏远了其核心支持者和潜在的新选民。 | 近义词: moderately liberal, partially liberal | 反义词: conservative, illiberal | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "half-" (Old English healf) + "liberal" (from Latin liberalis, meaning "of freedom")
