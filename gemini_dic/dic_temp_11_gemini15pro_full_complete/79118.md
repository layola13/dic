gladeye:/ˈɡleɪdaɪ/| n.|林中空地；林间空地|An open space in a wood or forest.|The gladeye was dappled with sunlight, creating a magical atmosphere. 林间空地阳光斑驳，营造出一种神奇的氛围。|Synonyms: clearing, glade, opening|Antonyms: thicket, forest, woods|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "glade" + "eye,"  "glade" meaning an open space in a wood, and "eye" metaphorically referring to something resembling an eye, especially in being a relatively open space within a larger, more enclosed area.
