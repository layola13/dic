apocrisiary: /əˈpɒkrɪsɪəri/ /əˈpɑːkrɪsɪɛri/ | n. | 教廷使节；特使；答复者 | An ambassador of the Pope; a legate.  An official in charge of giving answers or replies. | The apocrisiary delivered the Pope's message to the king. 教廷使节将教皇的信传达给了国王。|  The emperor's apocrisiary responded to the ambassador's queries. 皇帝的特使回答了大使的疑问。 | synonyms: legate, envoy, emissary, delegate, representative, agent | antonyms: recipient, receiver | noun
