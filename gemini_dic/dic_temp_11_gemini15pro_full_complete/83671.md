hafting:/ˈhæftɪŋ/| n.|装柄，装柄法|The act or process of providing a tool or weapon with a haft; the way in which a tool or weapon is hafted. |The hafting of the axe head was done with great care.  斧头的装柄做得非常仔细。|近义词：mounting, attaching, fixing|反义词：detaching, removing|noun
