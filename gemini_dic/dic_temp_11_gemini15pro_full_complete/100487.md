intervening:/ˌɪntərˈviːnɪŋ/
adjective
干预的，介于其间的，发生于中间的
coming or occurring between two times or events; intervening years  
例句：The intervening years have seen many changes. 这几年发生了很多变化。

例句：During the intervening period, the situation worsened considerably. 在此期间，情况恶化了很多。

近义词：intermediate, intermediary, interim, in-between
反义词：continuous, uninterrupted
词性：adjective
