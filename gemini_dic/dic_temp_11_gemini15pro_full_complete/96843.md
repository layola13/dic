inevitably: /ɪnˈevɪtəbli/ | adv. |不可避免地，必然地 | in a way that cannot be avoided |例句：Inevitably, the conversation turned to politics.  对话不可避免地转向了政治。|例句：She knew that, inevitably, she would have to face him again. 她知道自己最终不可避免地要再次面对他。|近义词：necessarily, certainly, unavoidably |反义词: avoidably, possibly, preventably |词性: 副词

词源: From Late Latin *inevitabilis, from in- "not" + evitabilis "avoidable," from evitare "to avoid."
