nihilism:/ˈnaɪɪlɪzəm/ (US), /ˈnɪɪlɪzəm/ (UK)| n.|虚无主义|The rejection of all religious and moral principles, often in the belief that life is meaningless.|Many young people feel a sense of nihilism after experiencing trauma.|近义词：pessimism, cynicism, skepticism|反义词:optimism, faith, belief|名词
