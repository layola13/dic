fleecier:/ˈfliːsiər/ | adj. | 更羊毛似的，更柔软的，更容易欺骗的 |Comparative form of "fleecy": covered with fleece; resembling fleece; soft and woolly.  Also figuratively, meaning more easily swindled or cheated. | The new blanket is fleecier than the old one.  新毯子比旧的更柔软。 The salesman targeted fleecier customers, hoping to make a quick profit.  销售员瞄准更容易受骗的顾客，希望能快速获利。 | 近义词: fluffier, softer, woollier (literal); gullible, naive (figurative) | 反义词: coarser, rougher (literal); astute, discerning (figurative) | 词性: 形容词

词源: From "fleece" (羊毛), from Old English *flys, from Proto-Germanic *flūsą (“fleece”).  The "-ier" suffix is a comparative adjective ending.
