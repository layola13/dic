epithelioid: /ˌɛpɪˈθiːliɔɪd/
| adj. |  上皮样的，类上皮的 | Resembling or having the characteristics of epithelium.  Epithelioid cells are cells that resemble epithelial cells in morphology, often seen in granulomas. | The pathologist identified epithelioid cells in the biopsy, suggesting a granulomatous reaction. 病理学家在活检中发现了类上皮细胞，提示肉芽肿反应。|  epithelial, epithelial-like | mesenchymal | adjective
