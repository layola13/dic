gweilo:/ˈɡwaɪloʊ/ (often considered offensive)
noun
鬼佬 (Cantonese slang; derogatory)
A Cantonese slang term for a white person, often considered offensive due to its historical association with colonialism and racial discrimination.  It literally translates to "ghost man."
He called him a "gweilo" which is a very offensive term. 他叫他“鬼佬”，这是一个非常冒犯的词。
The tour guide warned us against using the word "gweilo" as it could be perceived as disrespectful. 导游警告我们不要使用“鬼佬”这个词，因为它可能被认为是不尊重的。
Synonym:foreigner (neutral), expat (neutral)
Antonym:local (neutral)
词性:noun
