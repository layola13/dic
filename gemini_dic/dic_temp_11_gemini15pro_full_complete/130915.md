nonascendency:/ˌnɒnəˈsɛndənsi/ | n. | 非优势；非支配地位 | Lack of dominance or controlling influence; the state of not being in a position of power or authority. |  The nonascendency of the moderate voice in the party led to its radicalization.  温和的声音在党内非优势地位导致了其激进化。| synonyms: subordination, subjection, impotence, weakness | antonyms: dominance, supremacy, ascendancy, control | noun
