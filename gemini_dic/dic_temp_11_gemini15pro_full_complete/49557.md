ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *demener ("to conduct, manage, behave"), from mener ("to lead"), from Latin minārī ("to threaten, drive").  The original sense was of "management" or "administration," evolving to "conduct" and finally to the current sense of "outward behavior."
