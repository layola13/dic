indigogen:/ɪnˈdɪɡədʒɛn/ |n.|靛蓝原|A colorless, water-soluble, organic compound that is the precursor of indigo dye. It is converted to indigo by oxidation.|Indigo is produced from indigogen through a process of oxidation. 靛蓝是通过靛蓝原氧化而成的。|Synonyms: indican, indigo white|Antonyms: indigo (in its oxidized, blue form)|noun

indigogen:/ɪnˈdɪɡədʒɛn/ |n.|靛蓝原|A chemical substance that is transformed into indigo dye upon oxidation.|The leaves of certain plants contain indigogen, which can be extracted and processed to create the blue dye.  某些植物的叶子含有靛蓝原，可以提取和加工以制造蓝色染料。|Synonyms: leucoindigo, indigo precursor|Antonyms: indigo (in its oxidized, blue form)|noun
