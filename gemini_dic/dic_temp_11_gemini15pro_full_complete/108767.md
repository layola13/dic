leaseback:/ˈliːsˌbæk/| n.|售后回租；租回|A financial transaction in which one sells an asset and leases it back for the long term; therefore, one continues to be able to use the asset but no longer owns it.|Example 1: The company sold its headquarters in a leaseback agreement to raise capital.  这家公司通过售后回租协议出售了总部以筹集资金。

Example 2:  A leaseback can be a good option for businesses needing immediate cash flow. 售后回租对于需要立即获得现金流的企业来说可能是一个不错的选择。|Synonyms: sale-and-leaseback|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
