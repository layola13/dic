madams:/ˈmædəmz/ (US), /ˈmædəmz/ (UK)| n. |女士们；太太们|Plural form of madam, a polite term of address for a woman. Used especially in formal contexts.|例句：Good evening, madams and gentlemen. 晚上好，女士们，先生们。

例句：Madams, please take your seats. 女士们，请就座。


近义词：ladies, mesdames|反义词: sirs, gentlemen|词性:名词
