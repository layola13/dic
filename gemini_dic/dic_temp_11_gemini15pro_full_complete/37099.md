combings:/ˈkoʊmɪŋz/
n.
梳下来的头发; 梳理物;
The material removed from wool, cotton, or other fiber by combing.
例句：She swept up the combings from the floor. 她把地板上梳下来的头发扫干净了。

例句：The combings from the sheep's wool were used to stuff pillows.  羊毛梳理物被用来填充枕头。
近义词：leavings,  residue,  scraps
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "uncombed fiber/hair")
词性: 名词 (plural noun)


combing:/ˈkoʊmɪŋ/
n.
梳理;  彻底搜查
The act or process of combing.
例句：The combing of the beach yielded several interesting shells.  在海滩上彻底搜寻，找到了几个有趣的贝壳。

例句：Regular combing helps to keep your hair tangle-free. 定期梳头有助于防止头发打结。
近义词：searching,  scouring, grooming
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "tangling")
词性: 名词
