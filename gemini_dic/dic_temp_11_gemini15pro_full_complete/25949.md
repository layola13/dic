burqa's:/ˈbɜːrkəz/
noun
罩袍（复数形式，通常指不止一件罩袍）
A loose, usually black robe worn by some Muslim women that covers the body and face.  The possessive form indicates ownership or association with one or more burqas.
The burqa's intricate embroidery was stunning.  这件罩袍的精美刺绣令人惊叹。
veil, niqab, hijab
(No direct antonym as it's a garment.  Absence of a burqa might be considered an antonym in a specific context.)
