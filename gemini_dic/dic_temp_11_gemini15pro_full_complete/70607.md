filmier:/ˈfɪlmiər/| adj.|更薄的，更像薄膜的|Covered with or resembling a thin film; more filmy.  Relatively thin and transparent, like a film. |The air grew filmier and the light faded. 空气变得更加朦胧，光线也暗淡下来。|The old photographs were filmier than the new ones. 老照片比新照片更薄。|近义词：thinner, gauzier, flimsier, sheerer|反义词: thicker, denser, opaquer|词性:形容词
