limpidities:/lɪmˈpɪdɪtiz/
n.
清澈度，透明度
The limpidities of the water allowed us to see the colorful fish swimming below.
水的清澈度让我们能够看到下面游动的五彩缤纷的鱼。
The limpidities of her prose style make her work a pleasure to read.  她散文风格的清晰明了使得她的作品读起来赏心悦目。

Synonyms: clarity, transparency, pellucidity, lucidity
Antonyms: murkiness, cloudiness, opacity, turbidity
noun


Etymology:
From French limpidité, from limpide (“limpid”), from Latin limpidus, equivalent to limp(a) (“water”) + -idus (adjective-forming suffix).
