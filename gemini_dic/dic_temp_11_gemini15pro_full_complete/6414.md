amphitheatral:/ˌæmfɪθiˈætrəl/
adjective
圆形的，圆形剧场的
Resembling or characteristic of an amphitheater; arranged in a semicircular or circular form rising in tiers.
The seating arrangement was amphitheatral, allowing everyone a good view of the stage. 座位的安排是圆形剧场的，让每个人都能清楚地看到舞台。
The vineyard sloped in an amphitheatral manner. 葡萄园以圆形剧场的方式倾斜。
近义词:circular, semicircular, tiered
反义词:flat, level


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek "amphitheatron" (ἀμφιθέατρον), from "amphi-" (ἀμφί-) meaning "around, on both sides" + "theatron" (θέατρον) meaning "a place for viewing, theater."
