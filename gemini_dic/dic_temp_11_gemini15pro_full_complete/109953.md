liberalizes:/ˈlɪbərəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使自由化，放鬆管制
to make something less strict or controlled
The government has liberalized its trade policies. 政府已经放宽了贸易政策。
Synonyms:  deregulate, loosen, relax, ease
Antonyms: restrict, tighten, control, regulate
词性: 动词
