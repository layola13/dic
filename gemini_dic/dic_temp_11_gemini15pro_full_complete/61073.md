ellipsograph:/ɪˈlɪpsəɡræf/ |n.|椭圆规|An instrument for drawing ellipses.|The engineer used an ellipsograph to create the oval shape for the bridge design. 工程师使用椭圆规为桥梁设计创建椭圆形状。|近义词:trammel, compass (for some uses)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From French *ellipsographe*, from ellipse + -graphe.  Ultimately from Greek *elleipsis* (ellipse) + *graphein* (to write).
