hindgut:/ˈhaɪndɡʌt/| n. |后肠|The posterior part of the alimentary canal, comprising the distal part of the transverse colon, the descending colon, sigmoid colon, rectum, and anal canal.  It develops from the posterior part of the primitive gut.|The hindgut of the grasshopper contains specialized bacteria. 蚱蜢的后肠包含专门的细菌。|近义词:rectum, large intestine|反义词:foregut|noun
