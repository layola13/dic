immunosuppression:/ˌɪmjuːnoʊsəˈprɛʃən/ | n. | 免疫抑制 |The reduction or suppression of the body's immune response.|  The patient was on immunosuppression following the organ transplant.  病人器官移植后正在接受免疫抑制治疗。| synonyms: immunodepression,  immunosuppressive therapy | antonyms: immunostimulation, immunoenhancement | noun


immunosuppressive:/ˌɪmjuːnoʊsəˈprɛsɪv/ | adj. | 免疫抑制的 | Relating to or causing immunosuppression; tending to suppress the body's immune response. |  Immunosuppressive drugs are used to prevent organ rejection. 免疫抑制药物用于防止器官排斥。 | synonyms: immunodepressive | antonyms: immunostimulatory, immunoenhancing | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
immuno- + suppression

immuno-: from Latin *immunis "exempt, free, not paying a share," from in- "not" (see in- (1)) + munis "performing services, bound, obligated," from PIE root *mei- (1) "to change, go, move."

suppression: from Latin suppress-, past participle stem of supprimere "press down, keep back, repress," from sub "down, under" (see sub-) + premere "to press" (see press (v.1)).
