carburizing:/ˈkɑːrbəˌraɪzɪŋ/ /ˈkɑːrbjʊˌraɪzɪŋ/
noun|渗碳, 碳化|The process of increasing the carbon content of the surface of steel by heating it in a carbon-rich environment.|例句：Carburizing is a common heat treatment for gears. 渗碳是齿轮常用的热处理方法。|近义词：case hardening, cementation|反义词：decarburization|n.

carburize:/ˈkɑːrbəˌraɪz/ /ˈkɑːrbjʊˌraɪz/
verb|渗碳, 碳化|To increase the carbon content of the surface of steel by heating it in a carbon-rich environment.|例句：The gears were carburized to improve their wear resistance.  这些齿轮经过渗碳处理以提高其耐磨性。|近义词：case harden, cement|反义词：decarburize|v.
