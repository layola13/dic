bushmen:/ˈbʊʃmən/ | n. | 布须曼人；旧时对非洲南部土著居民的称呼（现被认为带有贬义） | A member of any of several indigenous hunter-gatherer peoples of Southern Africa.  This term is now considered outdated and often offensive, and the preferred term is San. |  The bushmen lived off the land, hunting and gathering. 布须曼人以狩猎和采集为生。|  The documentary showcased the traditional lifestyle of the bushmen.  这部纪录片展示了布须曼人的传统生活方式。 |  Synonyms: San people, San, Khoisan | Antonyms: None (as it refers to a specific group of people) | Noun
