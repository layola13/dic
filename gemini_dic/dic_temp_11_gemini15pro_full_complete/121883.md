miss-a-cue:/ˌmɪs ə ˈkjuː/
miss-a-cue|n.|失误，犯规；错过提示|A miss-a-cue is a mistake or blunder, especially in a performance or social situation.  It can also refer to missing a cue or signal. It originates from the theatrical term "cue," meaning a signal for an actor to speak or perform an action.  "Miss" indicates failure to follow the cue.|The actor's miss-a-cue ruined the entire scene.|Synonyms: blunder, gaffe, slip-up, mistake, error|Antonyms: success, triumph, perfection|noun
