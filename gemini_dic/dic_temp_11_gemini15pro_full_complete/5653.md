altitudinous:/ˌæltɪˈtjuːdɪnəs/
adj.
高的，极高的，高处的
situated at a high altitude; lofty.
例句：The climbers struggled to breathe in the altitudinous air. 攀登者在高海拔的空气中呼吸困难。

例句：The altitudinous peaks of the Himalayas are a breathtaking sight. 喜马拉雅山高耸的山峰令人叹为观止。
近义词：high, lofty, towering, elevated
反义词：low, base
词性：形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *altitudinōsus, from Latin altitūdō (“height”), from altus (“high, deep”).  Surface analysis is altitude + -inous.
