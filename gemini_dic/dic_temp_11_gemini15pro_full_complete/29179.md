casher:/ˈkæʃər/| n.|收银员，出纳员|A person who collects payment for goods or services in a shop, store, etc.|例句：The casher scanned my groceries quickly.  收银员很快地扫描了我的食品杂货。

例句：The casher gave me the wrong change. 收银员找错钱了。|近义词：teller, clerk, cashier|反义词:customer, client|词性:noun
