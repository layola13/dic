affectingly:/əˈfɛktɪŋli/
adverb
感人地，动人地
in a way that produces strong feelings of sadness and sympathy
She spoke affectingly of her experiences in the war. 她动情地讲述了她在战争中的经历。

近义词:movingly, touchingly, poignantly, emotionally
反义词:unfeelingly, coldly, indifferently
词性:adverb

词源: From affect + -ing + -ly.  "Affect" ultimately comes from Latin "afficere" (to influence, act on).
