inobedient:/ˌɪnəˈbiːdiənt/| adj.|不服从的，不顺从的|Not obedient; refusing to obey rules or follow instructions.  Derived from "in-" (not) + "obedient" (obeying).  The root of "obedient" comes from the Latin "oboedire," meaning "to obey," from "ob-" (towards) + "audire" (to hear).|The inobedient child refused to go to bed.  不听话的孩子拒绝上床睡觉。

The inobedient dog ran away from its owner.  不听话的狗从主人身边跑开了。|近义词:disobedient, unruly, defiant, rebellious, wayward|反义词:obedient, compliant, docile, submissive, tractable|词性:形容词
