apparatchiki: /ˌæpərˈɑːtʃɪki/ /ˌæpərˈætʃɪki/ | n. |  党政机关工作人员；（尤指共产主义国家或政党的）官员；（贬义）官僚 | An apparatchik is an official in a large political organization, especially in a communist party.  The term often carries negative connotations, suggesting blind loyalty, rigidity, and adherence to bureaucratic procedures. |  The apparatchiki were resistant to any reforms. 这些官员抵制任何改革。 | synonym: bureaucrat, official, functionary | antonym:  dissident, rebel, nonconformist | noun
