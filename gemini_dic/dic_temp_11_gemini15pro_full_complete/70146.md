fiancees:/fiˈɑːnˈseɪz/
n.
未婚妻们 (复数)
A woman to whom a man is engaged to be married.  (A man's fiancée is his betrothed.)
例句：
He introduced his fiancees to his parents.  他把他的未婚妻们介绍给他的父母。

例句：
All his fiancees were at the party. 他所有的未婚妻都参加了派对。


近义词：brides-to-be, betrothed (singular), engaged women
反义词：wives, ex-fiancees
词性：Noun (Plural)
