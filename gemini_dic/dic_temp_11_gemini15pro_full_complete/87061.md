heptahedra:/ˌhɛptəˈhiːdrə/
noun
七面体
A solid figure with seven faces.
A heptahedron can have various shapes, unlike a cube which is always regular.  七面体可以有各种形状，不像立方体总是规则的。
The artist created a sculpture based on a complex heptahedron.  这位艺术家创作了一个基于复杂七面体的雕塑。
