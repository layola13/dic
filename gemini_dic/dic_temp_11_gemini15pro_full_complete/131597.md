noncompetitors:/ˌnɒnkəmˈpɛtɪtəz/ /ˌnɑːnkəmˈpɛtɪtərz/
n.
非竞争者，非竞争对手
Individuals or businesses that do not compete with each other in a particular market.
The small bookstore thrived because there were no other noncompetitors in the area.  这家小书店生意兴隆，因为该地区没有其他非竞争对手。
Local businesses often collaborate with noncompetitors to promote the overall economic health of the community.  本地企业经常与非竞争对手合作，以促进社区的整体经济健康。
Synonyms: non-rivals
Antonyms: competitors, rivals
noun
