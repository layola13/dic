OKR:/ˌoʊkeɪˈɑːr/ | n. | 目标与关键成果; Objectives and Key Results | A goal-setting framework that helps organizations define measurable goals and track their progress.  | Example: Our team's OKRs for this quarter focus on increasing user engagement. 我们团队本季度的OKR重点是提高用户参与度。 | Example:  The company uses OKRs to align everyone on common goals. 公司使用OKR使每个人都与共同目标保持一致。 | 近义词: goals, objectives, targets | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
