baromacrometer:/ˌbærəʊməˈkrɒmɪtər/| n.|气压高度计|An instrument that measures atmospheric pressure and altitude.|The pilot checked the baromacrometer to determine the plane's altitude. 飞行员查看了气压高度计以确定飞机的高度。|Synonyms: altimeter, pressure altimeter|Antonyms: N/A|noun


Etymology:

From baro- (Greek baros, "weight") + -meter (Greek metron, "measure")
