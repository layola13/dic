despatcher:/dɪˈspætʃər/| n.|调度员，派遣员|A person who sends out vehicles, goods, etc., to various destinations.|The dispatcher directed the ambulance to the scene of the accident. 调度员指示救护车前往事故现场。|近义词：sender, agent, coordinator|反义词:receiver, recipient|noun

despatch:/dɪˈspætʃ/| n. vt.|派遣，发送；急件|The act of sending something or someone to a destination; a message sent quickly.|The despatch of the troops was delayed by bad weather. 部队的派遣因恶劣天气而延误。|近义词：send, transmit, dispatch|反义词:receive, withhold|noun, verb
