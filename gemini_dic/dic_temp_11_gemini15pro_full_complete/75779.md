fum:/fʌm/| n.|烟，气，愤怒|A pungent smoke, gas, or vapor.  Also, a state of anger or irritation.|The room was filled with the acrid fum of burning plastic.  房间里充满了燃烧塑料的刺鼻烟雾。|近义词：smoke, fumes, vapor, gas, anger, rage|反义词:calm, peace|词性:名词

词源: From Latin *fūmus* (“smoke, steam, vapor”).
