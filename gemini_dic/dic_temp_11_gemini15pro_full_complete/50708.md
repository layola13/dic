dermoptera:/dɜːˈmɒptərə/
n.
皮翼目动物
An order of gliding mammals, also known as colugos, native to Southeast Asia.  They are characterized by a large, furred membrane called a patagium that extends between their limbs, allowing them to glide long distances.
The dermoptera, or colugos, are fascinating creatures. 皮翼目动物，或者说鼯猴，是迷人的生物。
The Sunda colugo is one of the two species within the order Dermoptera.  巽他鼯猴是皮翼目两种动物之一。
近义词:colugos
反义词:None
词性:noun

Etymology:
From Greek derma "skin" + pteron "wing"
