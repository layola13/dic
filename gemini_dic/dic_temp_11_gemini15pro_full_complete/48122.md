decoctible:/dɪˈkɒktɪbl/ | adj. | 可煎煮的，可熬出的 |  Able to be boiled down or extracted by boiling.  | The herbs are decoctible in water to make a medicinal tea.  这些草药可以在水中煎煮成药茶。 |  extractable, infusible | non-extractable | adjective

decoctible:/dɪˈkɒktɪbl/ | n. | 煎煮液 |  A liquid obtained by boiling down or extracting something by boiling. | The decoctible from the bark was used as a dye.  树皮的煎煮液被用作染料。 | extract, infusion |  | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *decoctibilis, from Latin decoctus (past participle of decoquere “to boil down”), from de- “down” + coquere “to cook, boil.”
