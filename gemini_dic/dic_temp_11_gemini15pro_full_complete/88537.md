hit-the-ceiling:/hɪt ðə ˈsiːlɪŋ/| idiom|勃然大怒，暴跳如雷|To become extremely angry and express it forcefully.|例句：When he saw the dent in his new car, he hit the ceiling. 当他看到他的新车上的凹痕时，他勃然大怒。

例句：She hit the ceiling when she found out her son had failed his exams. 当她发现她儿子考试不及格时，她大发雷霆。|近义词：go through the roof, blow one's top, fly off the handle, lose one's temper|反义词：stay calm, remain composed|词性 idiom
