heads-up:/ˌhɛdzˈʌp/ | n. | 预先通知；提醒；警告 | a warning or piece of advice given beforehand | Just wanted to give you a heads-up that the meeting has been moved to 3 pm.  只想提前通知你，会议改到下午3点了。| synonyms: warning, alert, notification | antonyms: surprise, ambush | noun

heads-up:/ˌhɛdzˈʌp/ | adj. | 警惕的；留神的 | alert and aware of what is happening | He's a heads-up guy and always knows what's going on. 他是一个很警觉的人，总是知道发生了什么事。| synonyms: alert, attentive, vigilant | antonyms: inattentive, oblivious | adjective
