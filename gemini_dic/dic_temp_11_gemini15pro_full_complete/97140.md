infinite:/ˈɪnfɪnət/| adj.|无限的，无穷的|Limitless or endless in space, extent, or size; impossible to measure or calculate.|The universe seems infinite.|近义词：boundless, endless, limitless, immeasurable, vast, immense|反义词：finite, limited, bounded|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *infinitus*, from *in-* (not) + *finitus* (limited, bounded), past participle of *finire* (to limit, bound, finish).
