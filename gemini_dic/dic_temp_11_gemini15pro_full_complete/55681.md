dittoes:/ˈdɪtoʊz/ | n. | 同上；同上符号；重复；复制品 | A mark (") used to indicate that something written above should be repeated.  Also used informally to express agreement. | He wrote "10 lbs. potatoes, 10 lbs. onions, ditto carrots". 他写道“10磅土豆，10磅洋葱，同样10磅胡萝卜”。 | synonyms: the same, likewise, similarly | antonyms: different, unlike, dissimilar | noun

dittoes:/ˈdɪtoʊz/ | v. | 重复；复制 | To repeat or copy something. | "You're wrong," she said, and he dittoed her. “你错了，”她说，他也重复了这句话。| synonyms: repeat, copy, duplicate | antonyms: originate, create, invent | verb
