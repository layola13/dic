inky:/ˈɪŋki/| adj.|墨水的；墨黑的；沾满墨水的|Covered or stained with ink; black or very dark blue like ink.|The child had inky fingers after drawing with felt-tip pens. 孩子用毡尖笔画画后，手指沾满了墨水。|Synonyms: inky-black, black, dark, sooty|Antonyms: clean, unstained, light, pale|adjective

Etymology:
From Middle English *enki, equivalent to ink +‎ -y.
