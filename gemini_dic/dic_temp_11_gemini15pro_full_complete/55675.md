dittander:/ˈdɪtəndər/ | n. | 野辣根，胡椒草 | A Eurasian plant (Lepidium latifolium) of the mustard family, having clusters of small white flowers and broadly lance-shaped leaves. It is sometimes used as a potherb. | The dittander grew wild along the riverbank. 野辣根沿着河岸野生生长。|  The forager identified the plant as dittander.  觅食者确认这种植物是野辣根。 |Synonyms: pepperwort, peppergrass | Antonyms: N/A | noun
