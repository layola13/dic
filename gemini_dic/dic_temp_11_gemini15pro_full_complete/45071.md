cubicles:/ˈkjuːbɪkəlz/| n.|小隔间，小房间|A small partitioned-off area of a room, for example one containing a desk in an office.|例句：I work in one of those tiny cubicles. 我在那些小隔间中的一个工作。|例句：She decorated her cubicle with pictures of her family. 她用家人的照片装饰了她的隔间。|近义词：compartments, booths, stalls|反义词:open space, large room|词性:noun

cubicle:/ˈkjuːbɪkəl/| n.|小隔间，小房间|A small partitioned-off area of a room, for example one containing a desk in an office.|Etymology: From Latin *cubiculum*, diminutive of *cubum* "cube."|
