niceties:/ˈnaɪsɪtiz/| n.|细节；礼节；微妙之处|a fine point or detail of something, especially of etiquette or polite behavior|例句：We needn't stand on niceties. 我们不必拘泥于繁文缛节。|近义词：courtesies, formalities, subtleties|反义词:rudeness, impoliteness|noun

niceties:/ˈnaɪsɪtiz/| n.|美好的事物；精致的事物|pleasant or refined things|例句：She appreciates the niceties of life, like fine wine and good food. 她欣赏生活中美好的事物，比如美酒和美食。|近义词：amenities, luxuries, refinements|反义词:necessities, essentials|noun
