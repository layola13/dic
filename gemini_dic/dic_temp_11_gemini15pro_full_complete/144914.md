over-sweetness's:/ˈoʊvər-ˈswiːtnəsɪz/
noun
过分的甜味；甜度过高
Excessive sweetness; a state of being too sweet.
The over-sweetness's of the dessert made it difficult to finish. 这道甜点甜度太高，很难吃完。
近义词：excessive sweetness, cloying sweetness
反义词：tartness, acidity
词性：名词

词源:
"over-" (Old English *ofer-, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above, beyond") + "sweet" (Old English swēte "pleasing to the senses, mild, gentle," from Proto-Germanic *swoti-) + "-ness" (Old English -nes(s), from Proto-Germanic *-inassu-, suffix forming abstract nouns from adjectives)
