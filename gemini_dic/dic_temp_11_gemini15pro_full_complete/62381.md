endocardiac:/ˌɛndəʊˈkɑːdiæk/ | adj. | 心内膜的 |  Relating to the endocardium, the thin, smooth membrane which lines the inside of the chambers of the heart and forms the surface of the valves. |  Endocardiac biopsies are sometimes necessary to diagnose certain heart conditions. 心内膜活检有时是诊断某些心脏疾病所必需的。 |  intracardiac |  epicardiac | adjective
endocardium:/ˌɛndəʊˈkɑːrdiəm/ | n. | 心内膜 | The thin, smooth membrane which lines the inside of the chambers of the heart and forms the surface of the valves. |  Inflammation of the endocardium can lead to serious complications. 心内膜发炎会导致严重的并发症。 | inner lining of the heart | myocardium, epicardium | noun
