orthoceratite:/ˌɔːrθəˈsɛrəˌtaɪt/
n.|直角石|An extinct marine mollusk of the cephalopod class, characterized by a long, straight, conical shell.|The museum displayed a large collection of orthoceratite fossils.  博物馆展示了大量的直角石化石。|Synonym:  Nautiloid, cephalopod|Antonym: None|Noun
