miserably:/ˈmɪzərəbli/| adv.|悲惨地，可怜地|In a miserable manner; wretchedly.  Experiencing or causing great distress or unhappiness.|例句：He failed miserably in his attempt to climb the mountain. 他爬山的尝试悲惨地失败了。|近义词：wretchedly, woefully, pathetically, deplorably|反义词：happily, joyfully, blissfully|词性:副词
词源: From Middle French *miserablement*, from Late Latin *miserabiliter*, from Latin *miserabilis* ("pitiable, wretched").
