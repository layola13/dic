gelatinousness:/dʒɪˌlætɪnəsnəs/| n.|胶状；胶粘性；粘性|The gelatinousness of the substance made it difficult to handle.  这种物质的胶状使其难以处理。|例句：

The soup had an unpleasant gelatinousness. 这汤有一种令人不快的胶状。
The jellyfish exhibited a remarkable gelatinousness.  这水母呈现出明显的胶状。


|近义词：jellylike, viscous, gummy, sticky|反义词: firm, solid, hard|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From gelatinous + -ness.  "gelatinous" comes from Late Latin "gelatinosus,"  from Latin "gelata" ("jelly") + "-inus" (suffix forming adjectives). "gelata" is the past participle of "gelare" (to freeze). "-ness" is an Old English suffix forming nouns of quality or state.
