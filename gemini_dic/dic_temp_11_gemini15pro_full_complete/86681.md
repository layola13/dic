hellions:/ˈhɛliənz/| n.|小淘气，小坏蛋，捣蛋鬼|A mischievous or unruly person, especially a child.|The hellions were throwing rocks at the neighbor's windows. 这些小淘气朝邻居家的窗户扔石头。|近义词：rascals, scamps, imps, brats|反义词: angels, cherubs|noun


Etymology:
From Middle English *hellion, from Anglo-Norman *helion, ultimately from Old French hellequin, possibly related to the Germanic word *hari "army."  Originally referred to a boisterous, disorderly person, often a soldier. The sense shifted over time to primarily refer to mischievous children.
