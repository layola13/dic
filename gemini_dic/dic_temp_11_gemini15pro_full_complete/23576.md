braise:/breɪz/| v.| 焖; 燉 |To cook (meat or vegetables) by browning it in fat and then simmering it, covered, in a small amount of liquid.| I braised the beef in red wine for three hours. 我用红酒炖了三个小时的牛肉。|例句：She braised the short ribs in red wine and herbs. 她用红酒和香草焖了牛肋排。

例句：The chef braised the lamb shanks until they were falling off the bone.  厨师将羊小腿炖至骨肉分离。|近义词：stew, simmer, pot-roast|反义词：fry, roast, grill|词性:动词

braise:/breɪz/| n.| 焖; 燉 |The act of braising, or food cooked by braising.| The braise was delicious and tender.  这道焖菜美味又嫩。|例句：The pork braise was the highlight of the meal.  这道焖猪肉是这顿饭的亮点。

例句：The braise was infused with the flavors of garlic and rosemary.  这道焖菜融入了大蒜和迷迭香的味道。|近义词：stew|反义词：N/A|词性:名词
