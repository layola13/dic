aquaplanes:/ˈɑːkwəpleɪnz/ (US), /ˈækwəpleɪnz/ (UK)| v. |水上滑行，滑水|To move uncontrollably on a thin layer of water between the tires and the road surface, causing the vehicle to skid.|The car aquaplaned on the wet road and spun out of control. 汽车在湿滑的路面上打滑失控。|近义词：hydroplane, skid|反义词：grip, maintain traction|词性:动词

aquaplaning:/ˈɑːkwəpleɪnɪŋ/ (US), /ˈækwəpleɪnɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |水上滑行，滑水|The action of aquaplaning.|Heavy rain caused aquaplaning on the highway. 大雨导致高速公路上出现滑水现象。|近义词：hydroplaning|反义词：traction|词性:名词

aquaplaner:/ˈɑːkwəpleɪnər/ (US), /ˈækwəpleɪnər/ (UK)| n. |滑水者，滑水板|A person who aquaplanes or a board used for aquaplaning. (This usage is less common than the verb and gerund forms related to vehicle skidding.) |The aquaplaner skimmed across the lake. 滑水者掠过湖面。  (This example refers to a person aquaplaning, a less common usage.)|近义词：hydroplaner|反义词：N/A|词性:名词
