outswum:/ˌaʊtˈswʌm/
verb
游得比...远；在游泳中胜过
outdid in swimming; surpassed in swimming
He outswum all his competitors in the race. 他在比赛中游过了所有竞争对手。
近义词: outpaced, surpassed, defeated
反义词: lost to, defeated by
词性: 过去分词 (past participle)

词源:  "outswum" is the past participle of the verb "outswim."  "Out-" is a prefix meaning "to surpass or exceed," and "swim" is the verb meaning to propel oneself through water using the limbs.
