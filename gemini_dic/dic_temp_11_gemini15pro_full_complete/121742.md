mispropose:/ˌmɪsprəˈpoʊz/| v. |错误地提议；提出不当的建议|To propose something inappropriate or unsuitable. |He misproposed a solution that would have actually worsened the problem. 他错误地提出了一个实际上会使问题恶化的解决方案。

He misproposed marriage to a woman who was already engaged. 他向一个已经订婚的女人求婚了，真是不合适。|近义词：misjudge, miscalculate, blunder|反义词：propose correctly, suggest appropriately|词性:动词
