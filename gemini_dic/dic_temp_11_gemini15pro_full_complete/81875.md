grievance: /ˈɡriːvəns/ (US) /ˈɡriːvəns/ (UK)| n. |委屈，抱怨，不平|a real or imagined wrong or other cause for complaint or protest|Example: He had a grievance against his employer. 他对他的雇主怀有不满。

Example:  She filed a formal grievance with the human resources department. 她向人力资源部门提交了一份正式的申诉。|近义词：complaint, injustice, wrong, beef, gripe|反义词:satisfaction, contentment|词性: Noun
