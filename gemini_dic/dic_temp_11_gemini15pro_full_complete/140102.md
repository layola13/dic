off-limits:/ˌɒf ˈlɪmɪts/ /ˌɔːf ˈlɪmɪts/| adj.|禁止入内的，禁止使用的|Not allowed to be entered or used; forbidden.|例句：The area beyond the fence is off-limits to the public.  围栏之外的区域禁止公众进入。|例句：Certain topics are off-limits for discussion at the dinner table.  某些话题在餐桌上禁止讨论。|近义词：forbidden, restricted, prohibited, out of bounds|反义词：permitted, allowed, accessible|词性:形容词

词源:  "off" (away from) + "limits" (boundaries).  The term originally referred to areas that were geographically restricted, perhaps by military or other authorities.  Over time, the meaning broadened to encompass anything that is forbidden or prohibited.
