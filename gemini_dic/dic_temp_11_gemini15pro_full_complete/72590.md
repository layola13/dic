fly-beam:/ˈflaɪ biːm/ | n. | 飞梁，飞桁 | A horizontal beam projecting from a wall or other structure to support a load. | The fly-beam of the old barn creaked under the weight of the hay. 老谷仓的飞梁在干草的重压下嘎吱作响。| The fly-beam supported the upper story of the building. 飞梁支撑着建筑物的上层。 | 近义词: cantilever, girder, beam | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun

Etymology:  'fly' (from Old English *flēogan, related to Old Norse fljúga "to fly") + 'beam' (from Old English bēam, meaning "tree, beam")
