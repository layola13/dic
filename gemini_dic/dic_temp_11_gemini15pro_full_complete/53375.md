disaggregation:/dɪsˌæɡrɪˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 分解；分类；分散 | The process of separating something into its constituent parts. |  Data disaggregation allows for a more nuanced understanding of trends. 数据分解可以让我们对趋势有更细致的理解。

The disaggregation of the company into smaller units increased efficiency.  将公司分解成更小的单位提高了效率。 | 近义词：breakdown, separation, decomposition, analysis | 反义词：aggregation, combination, synthesis | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From dis- (expressing reversal or absence) + aggregation (from Latin aggregare, "to add to a flock").
