overapt:/ˌoʊvərˈæpt/| adj.| 过于合适的，过于恰当的| Excessively apt or suitable; too pertinent.| Her overapt remark made everyone uncomfortable. 她过于恰当的评论让每个人都感到不舒服。| synonyms: fitting, suitable, appropriate, pertinent | antonyms: inappropriate, unsuitable, irrelevant | adjective
