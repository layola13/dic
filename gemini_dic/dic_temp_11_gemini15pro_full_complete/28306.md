cara:/ˈkɑːrə/ (US), /ˈkærə/ (UK)| n.| 亲爱的，朋友（爱尔兰英语、苏格兰英语）; 方式，方法（西班牙语）|  A term of endearment, similar to "dear" or "friend", primarily used in Irish and Scottish English. In Spanish, it means "face" or "way". |例句：Cara, would you like a cup of tea?  亲爱的，你想喝杯茶吗？|近义词：(Irish/Scottish) dear, friend, love; (Spanish) face, way, manner, method|反义词: (Spanish) enemy|词性:noun

cara:/ˈkɑːrə/ (US), /ˈkærə/ (UK)| n.| 面容，脸（意大利语，罗马尼亚语，葡萄牙语）| Face or appearance. Used in Italian, Romanian and Portuguese. |例句：Ha una bella cara. (Italian)  她有一张漂亮的脸蛋。|近义词: face, visage, countenance|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
