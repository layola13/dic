anti-cigarette:/ˌæntiˈsɪɡəret/ | adj. | 反吸烟的，反对吸烟的 | Opposed to the smoking of cigarettes. |  The organization launched an anti-cigarette campaign aimed at teenagers.  这个组织发起了一项针对青少年的反吸烟运动。 |  Anti-smoking, non-smoking | Pro-cigarette, pro-smoking | adjective
anti-cigarette:/ˌæntiˈsɪɡəret/ | n. | 反吸烟者，戒烟产品 | A person who opposes smoking or a product designed to help people quit smoking. (Less common usage as a noun) |  He's a staunch anti-cigarette and advocates for smoke-free public spaces. 他是一位坚定的反吸烟人士，并倡导无烟公共场所。| Anti-smoker | Smoker | noun
