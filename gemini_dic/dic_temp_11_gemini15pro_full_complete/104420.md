kaffiyehs:/kəˈfiːəz/ | n. | 方格头巾，阿拉伯头巾 | A traditional Middle Eastern headdress consisting of a square of cloth folded diagonally and held in place by an agal (a black cord). It is often worn by men in Arab countries. |  Example 1: He wore a red and white kaffiyeh. 他戴着一条红白相间的方格头巾。

Example 2: Kaffiyehs are a common sight in the Middle East. 在中东，方格头巾很常见。 | Synonyms: shemagh, kufiya, ghutra | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
