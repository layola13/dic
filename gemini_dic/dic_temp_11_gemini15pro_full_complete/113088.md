lunatically:/ˈluːnətɪkli/| adv.|疯狂地，精神错乱地|In a manner resembling or suggestive of lunacy; insanely.|He was laughing lunatically. 他在疯狂地大笑。|近义词：insanely, madly, wildly|反义词：sanely, rationally|词性: 副词

词源: From lunatic + -ally.  "Lunatic" itself comes from the Late Latin "lunaticus," meaning "moonstruck," from "luna" (moon).  The belief was that changes in the moon caused intermittent insanity.
