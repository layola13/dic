bypath:/ˈbaɪˌpæθ/ | n. | 小路，僻径，旁路 | A less-traveled or secluded path, often one that branches off from a main road. |  We took a bypath through the woods to avoid the traffic. 我们抄小路穿过树林以避开交通拥堵。| Synonyms: side road, back road, shortcut, detour, track | Antonyms: highway, main road, thoroughfare | noun

bypath:/ˈbaɪˌpæθ/ | n. |  不寻常的方式；间接的途径 | An unusual or indirect way of achieving something. | He achieved success by a rather unusual bypath. 他通过一条相当不寻常的途径取得了成功。 | Synonyms: indirect route, unconventional method, detour | Antonyms: direct route, conventional method, straight path | noun
