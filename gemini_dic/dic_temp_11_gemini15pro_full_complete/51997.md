diagnostic:/ˌdaɪəɡˈnɒstɪk/ /ˌdaɪəɡˈnɑːstɪk/
adjective|诊断的，用于诊断的|Relating to or used in diagnosis. |The doctor ordered some diagnostic tests. 医生要求做一些诊断性检查。|近义词: analytical,  determinative,  distinctive,  exploratory,  identifying,  indicative,  probative,  symptomatic|反义词: therapeutic, remedial|词性:adjective

diagnostic:/ˌdaɪəɡˈnɒstɪk/ /ˌdaɪəɡˈnɑːstɪk/
noun|诊断|A process of identifying a disease or problem by considering its signs and symptoms. |The doctor made a preliminary diagnostic. 医生做了一个初步诊断。|近义词: analysis,  examination,  investigation,  test|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *diagnostique*, from Ancient Greek διαγνωστικός (diagnōstikós, “able to distinguish”), from διαγιγνώσκειν (diagignṓskein, “to distinguish, discern”), from διά (diá, “through, across”) + γιγνώσκειν (gignṓskein, “to learn”).
