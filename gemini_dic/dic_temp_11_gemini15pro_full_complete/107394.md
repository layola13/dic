laminal:/ˈlæmɪnəl/| adj.|层状的，薄片状的|arranged in thin layers or plates|例句：Laminar flow is smooth and streamlined. 层流是平滑且流线型的。|例句：The artist created a laminar sculpture using thin sheets of metal.  这位艺术家用薄金属片创作了一个层状雕塑。|近义词：layered, stratified, sheetlike|反义词: turbulent, non-layered|词性: 形容词
词源: From Latin *lamina* "thin plate, leaf, layer"
