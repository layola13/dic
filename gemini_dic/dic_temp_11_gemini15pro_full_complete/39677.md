conserves:/kənˈsɜːrvz/
| v.|保存；保护；节省|To keep and protect something from damage, change, or waste. |例句：The park conserves several endangered species.  这个公园保护着几个濒危物种。

例句：She conserves her energy by taking the bus. 她乘公共汽车以节省体力。|近义词：preserves, protects, saves, safeguards|反义词：wastes, squanders, depletes|词性:动词

conserves:/ˈkɒnsɜːvz/
| n.|蜜饯；果酱|A sweet food made from fruit preserved in sugar. |例句：She enjoys strawberry conserves on her toast. 她喜欢在吐司上涂草莓酱。

例句：The shop sells a variety of homemade conserves. 这家商店出售各种自制果酱。|近义词：jams, preserves, marmalades|反义词：N/A|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French conserver, from Latin conservare  "to keep, preserve, maintain," from com- "together" (see com-) + servare "to keep, guard" (see observe).
