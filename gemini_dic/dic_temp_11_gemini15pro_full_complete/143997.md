outraze: /ˌaʊtˈreɪz/ | vt. | 超越，胜过，使黯然失色 | To surpass or excel; to eclipse or outshine. |  His talent outrazed all his contemporaries. 他的才华胜过所有同龄人。

| Example: The beauty of the sunset outrazed any painting.  日落的美丽胜过任何画作。

| Synonym: surpass, excel, outshine, eclipse, transcend | Antonym:  be inferior to, pale in comparison to | Verb
