jazziest:/ˈdʒæziɪst/ | adj. | 最具爵士乐风格的，最花哨的，最活泼的 |  Superlative form of jazzy: most jazzy. Having the most characteristics of jazz music, especially its syncopated rhythms and improvisational quality. Can also mean flashy, lively, or exciting. |  That was the jazziest party I've ever been to! 那是我参加过的最热闹的派对！ | 近义词：liveliest, most vibrant, flashiest, most stylish | 反义词：dullest, most boring, plainest, simplest | 词性: 形容词
