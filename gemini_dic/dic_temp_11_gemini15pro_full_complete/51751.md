devon:/ˈdɛvən/| n. |德文郡；德文牛；泥盆纪|A county in southwest England, known for its beautiful coastline and moorland. Also refers to a breed of cattle originating from there, and the Devonian period in geological time.|例句：We spent our holiday in Devon. 我们在德文郡度假。
