diffusing:/dɪˈfjuːzɪŋ/| v. |扩散，传播，散布|The scent of flowers was diffusing through the garden. 花香弥漫在整个花园里。|例句：The company is diffusing its products throughout the country.  该公司正在全国推广其产品。

例句：The teacher was diffusing knowledge among her students. 老师在学生中传播知识。

|近义词：spreading, disseminating, dispersing|反义词：concentrating, gathering, collecting|词性：动词 (present participle of diffuse)


diffuse:/dɪˈfjuːs/| adj. |弥漫的，分散的，冗长的|The report was diffuse and difficult to understand.  这份报告冗长且难以理解。|例句：The diffuse light made it hard to see.  弥漫的光线使人看不清。

例句：His writing style is often diffuse and lacking focus. 他的写作风格经常是散漫的，缺乏重点。


|近义词：spread out, scattered, rambling|反义词：concise, focused, concentrated|词性：形容词


词源：From Latin diffusus, past participle of diffundere "to pour out, spread," from dis- "out" + fundere "to pour."
