bankrolls:/ˈbæŋkroʊlz/| n.|赌本，资金，资助|A bankroll is a sum of money used for gambling or to fund a business venture.|The high roller lost his entire bankroll in one night.  那个豪赌客一夜之间输光了所有的赌本。|Near synonyms: stake, funds, financing|Opposite: debt|noun

bankroll:/ˈbæŋkroʊl/| vt.|为…提供资金，资助|To bankroll means to provide financial backing for a project or person.|A wealthy investor bankrolled the new tech startup. 一位富有的投资者资助了这家新的科技初创公司。|Near synonyms: finance, fund, subsidize|Opposite: defund|verb
