delimitation:/dɪˌlɪmɪˈteɪʃən/| n.|界限，限定；划界|The act of setting or marking the boundaries or limits of something.  It refers to the process of fixing the limits or extent of something.|例句：The delimitation of the national park will help protect endangered species.  国家公园的划界将有助于保护濒危物种。

The delimitation of responsibilities between the two departments needs to be clarified. 两个部门之间职责的划分需要明确。|近义词：boundary, limit, demarcation, definition|反义词：limitlessness, expansion, indefiniteness|词性: 名词 (Noun)


词源:  From Late Latin *dēlimitātiō, from Latin dēlimitāre  "to mark out the boundaries of," from dē- "out, completely" + limitāre "to bound, limit," from līmis "boundary, limit."
