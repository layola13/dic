deriver:/dɪˈraɪvər/| n.|导出器，推导器，衍生者|A deriver is a person or thing that derives something from something else.  A deriver could be a program that derives new data from existing data, or a person who derives conclusions from evidence.|The deriver extracted useful information from the raw data.  这个导出器从原始数据中提取了有用的信息。|近义词：extractor, originator, deducer|反义词: originator (sometimes)|n.
