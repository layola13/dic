hydrothermal:/ˌhaɪdrəˈθɜːrməl/ | adj. |热液的；热水作用的|Of, relating to, or denoting hot water, especially water heated by the Earth's internal heat. |例句：Hydrothermal vents are fissures on the seafloor from which geothermally heated water issues. 热液喷口是海底的裂缝，地热加热的水从中喷出。

例句：Hydrothermal alteration of rocks can create valuable mineral deposits. 岩石的热液蚀变可以形成有价值的矿床。|近义词：geothermal|反义词：-|词性：形容词
