nazism:/ˈnɑːtsɪzəm/ /ˈnætˌsɪzəm/| n.|纳粹主义|The political principles of the National Socialist German Workers' Party. It involved totalitarian control of the state, belief in racial superiority, and aggressive expansionism.|Hitler's rise to power marked the beginning of Nazism in Germany. 希特勒上台标志着纳粹主义在德国的开始。

The Nuremberg trials exposed the horrors of Nazism.  纽伦堡审判揭露了纳粹主义的恐怖。|近义词：fascism, totalitarianism|反义词:democracy, liberalism|词性:noun

Etymology: From German *Nazismus*, derived from *Nazi*, a shortened form of *Nationalsozialist*  (National Socialist).
