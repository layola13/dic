hand-over-fist:/ˌhænd əʊvə ˈfɪst/| adv.|迅速地；大量地；轻易地|rapidly and in large quantities; easily|例句：The business was making money hand over fist. 这家公司生意兴隆，赚了很多钱。

例句：She's been raking in the cash hand over fist. 她一直在快速地赚钱。|近义词：rapidly, quickly, swiftly, easily, effortlessly, in large quantities, by leaps and bounds, like hotcakes|反义词：slowly, gradually, little by little, with difficulty|词性：副词
