myopathic:/ˌmaɪəˈpæθɪk/ |adj.|肌病的|Relating to or affected by myopathy, a disease of muscle tissue.  Characterized by muscle weakness or dysfunction.|  His myopathic condition made it difficult for him to climb stairs. 他的肌病使他爬楼梯很困难。| Myopathic weakness can be caused by various factors. 肌病性虚弱可由多种因素引起。|  synonymous: muscular, dystrophic| antonymous: healthy, robust|adjective
