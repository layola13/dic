electrolyze:/ɪˈlɛktrəˌlaɪz/ /ɪˈlɛktroʊˌlaɪz/| vt.|电解|To decompose a substance by an electric current.  The process of causing chemical changes by passing an electric current through a substance.|We electrolyze water to produce hydrogen and oxygen. 我们电解水以产生氢气和氧气.|Synonyms: decompose, separate, break down|Antonyms: synthesize, combine, unite|Verb
