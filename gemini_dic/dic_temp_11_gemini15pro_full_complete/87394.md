herrenhaus:/ˈhɛʁənˌhaʊs/ (German)| n.|庄园；贵族宅邸；府邸|A large country house or mansion, typically associated with the landed gentry or aristocracy.|The family has owned the herrenhaus for generations.  这个家族拥有这座庄园已有好几代了。|The count hosted a lavish ball at his herrenhaus. 伯爵在他的府邸举办了一场奢华的舞会。|Synonyms: mansion, manor house, country estate, villa|Antonyms: hovel, shack|noun
