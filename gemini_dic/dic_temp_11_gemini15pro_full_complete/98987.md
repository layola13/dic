inter-university:/ˌɪntərˌjuːnɪˈvɜːrsəti/
adjective
大学间的；校际的
Existing or occurring between universities.
The inter-university sports competition was a great success. 校际运动竞赛取得了巨大的成功。
Inter-university collaborations are becoming increasingly common.  大学间的合作越来越普遍。
Synonyms: intercollegiate
Antonyms: intra-university, within-university
词性: adjective
