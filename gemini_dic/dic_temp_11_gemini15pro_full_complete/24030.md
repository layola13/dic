breeds:/briːdz/
| n. |品种；种类；类型|A group of animals or plants having similar characteristics that distinguish them from other groups within the same species.|例句：There are many breeds of dogs, each with its own unique characteristics.  有很多不同品种的狗，每个品种都有自己独特的特征。|近义词：types, kinds, varieties, strains|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

breeds:/briːdz/
| v. |繁殖；饲养；养育；产生；导致| (of animals) To produce offspring, typically in a controlled or selective manner. To cause something to happen or develop.|例句：The farmer breeds prize-winning cattle. 这位农民饲养获奖的牛。|近义词：reproduce, raise, rear, generate, cause|反义词:extinguish, prevent|词性:verb
