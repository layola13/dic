ataxic:/əˈtæksɪk/ | adj. | 共济失调的，运动失调的 | Characterized by ataxia, a lack of muscle coordination resulting in abnormal or unsteady gait. |  The patient exhibited ataxic movements after the stroke.  病人中风后表现出共济失调的运动。| Synonyms: incoordinated, unsteady, clumsy | Antonyms: coordinated, steady, controlled | adjective

ataxia:/əˈtæksiə/ | n. | 共济失调 | A neurological sign consisting of lack of voluntary coordination of muscle movements that includes gait abnormality. | The ataxia made it difficult for her to walk. 共济失调使她行走困难。| Synonyms: incoordination, unsteadiness | Antonyms: coordination, steadiness | noun
