nonegocentric:/ˌnɒnˌiːɡoʊˈsɛntrɪk/| adj.|非自我中心的，不以自我为中心的|Not egocentric; considering things from the perspective of others; not self-centered.|She took a nonegocentric approach to the problem, considering the needs of everyone involved. 她以非自我中心的方式处理这个问题，考虑到了所有相关人员的需求。|例句：His nonegocentric worldview allowed him to empathize with people from different cultures.  他非自我中心的世界观使他能与来自不同文化的人产生共鸣。|近义词：altruistic, selfless, considerate, empathetic|反义词：egocentric, self-centered, selfish, narcissistic|词性:形容词
