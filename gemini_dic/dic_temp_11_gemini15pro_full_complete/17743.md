bedeviled:/bɪˈdɛvəld/
verb
使痛苦，折磨；困扰
tormented, plagued, harassed, troubled.  Experiencing continual or repeated trouble.
The project was bedeviled by technical problems.  这个项目受到了技术问题的困扰。
The team was bedeviled by injuries all season. 这个团队整个赛季都受到伤病困扰。
harass, plague, torment, trouble
aid, assist, help
v.
