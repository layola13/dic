fast-tracked:/ˈfæstˌtrækt/ | adj. | 快速通道的，快速处理的 | Accelerated, put on a faster schedule than usual. | The project was fast-tracked due to its importance.  这个项目由于其重要性而被快速推进。| 近义词: expedited, accelerated, rushed | 反义词: delayed, slowed, hindered | 词性:形容词

fast-tracked:/ˈfæstˌtrækt/ | v. |  快速处理，快速推进 | To accelerate the progress of something. | The company fast-tracked the development of the new product. 公司快速推进了新产品的开发。| 近义词: expedite, accelerate, rush | 反义词: delay, slow, hinder | 词性:动词

词源:  "Fast-track" is a compound word formed from "fast" and "track."  "Fast" meaning quick or rapid, and "track" referring to a course or path. The term originally referred to a specific accelerated educational program.
