flak:/flæk/
n.|高射炮火，猛烈批评|Anti-aircraft fire, strong criticism.
|The airplane encountered heavy flak over enemy territory. 飞机在敌方领土上遭遇猛烈的高射炮火。
|The mayor's new policy drew heavy flak from the public. 市长的新政策受到了公众的猛烈批评。
|近义词: criticism, fire, barrage|反义词: praise, approval
词性:名词


flak:/flæk/
n.|高射炮|Anti-aircraft gun.
|The flak guns opened fire on the approaching bombers. 高射炮向逼近的轰炸机开火。
|They used flak to defend the city. 他们用高射炮保卫城市。
|近义词: anti-aircraft gun, AA gun|反义词: N/A
词性:名词


词源:  Shortened from German *Fliegerabwehrkanone*, literally “aviator-defense cannon.”
