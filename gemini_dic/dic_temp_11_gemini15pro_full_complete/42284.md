counter-extend:/ˈkaʊntərɪkˈstend/ |v. |反向延伸，对抗性延伸|To extend in the opposite direction or in opposition to something else. |The two armies counter-extended their lines, each trying to outflank the other. 两军反向延伸防线，都想包抄对方。

The company counter-extended its competitor's marketing campaign with a more aggressive strategy.  这家公司用更积极的策略对抗竞争对手的营销活动。| 近义词: oppose, counteract, resist | 反义词: extend, support, agree |词性: 动词
