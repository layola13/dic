falcons:/ˈfɔːlkənz/ | n. |猎鹰（复数）|A bird of prey with long pointed wings and a notched beak, known for its swift flight and keen eyesight.  It is often trained for hunting.|The falcons soared high above the mountains. 猎鹰在群山上空翱翔。|Synonyms: hawks, eagles, raptors |Antonyms: prey |noun
