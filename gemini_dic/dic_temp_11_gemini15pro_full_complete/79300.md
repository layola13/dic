glaze:/ɡleɪz/| n. v.|釉，光滑面；(目光)变得呆滞无神|A thin, glassy coating applied to pottery or food.  To cover with a glaze; to become glassy or dull.|The potter glazed the vase.  陶工给花瓶上了釉。|近义词：gloss, varnish, enamel|反义词:dull, matte|n. v.
glazes:/ˈɡleɪzɪz/| n. v.|(glaze的第三人称单数)|Glazes are used to protect and decorate pottery.  He glazes the donuts with a sweet icing.  釉料用来保护和装饰陶器。 他在甜甜圈上涂了一层甜糖霜。|近义词：glosses, varnishes, enamels|反义词:dulls, mattes|n. v.
