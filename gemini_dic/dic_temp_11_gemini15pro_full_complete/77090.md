garrisons:/ˈɡærɪsənz/ | n. | 卫戍部队，驻军；守备队 | A body of troops stationed in a fort or town to defend it. | The general inspected the garrisons stationed along the border. 将军视察了驻扎在边境的卫戍部队。| synonyms: troops, forces, soldiers | antonyms: civilians | noun

garrisons:/ˈɡærɪsənz/ | v. |  驻防；守卫；(在…)驻扎 | To station troops in (a fort or town) to defend it. |  They garrisoned the fort with a hundred soldiers. 他们在堡垒里驻扎了一百名士兵。| synonyms: station, deploy, post | antonyms: withdraw, evacuate | verb
