overblames:/ˌoʊvərˈbleɪmz/ | v. | 过分责备，过度指责 | To blame someone excessively or unfairly for something. |  He overblames his employees for minor mistakes. 他因为员工的小错误而过度责备他们。| Synonyms: overcriticize, overcondemn | Antonyms: excuse, forgive | verb

overblaming:/ˌoʊvərˈbleɪmɪŋ/ | n. | 过度责备 | The act of blaming someone excessively. | Constant overblaming can damage relationships.  持续的过度责备会损害人际关系。 | Synonyms: overcriticizing, overcondemning | Antonyms: excusing, forgiving | noun
