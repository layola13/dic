dampen:/ˈdæmpən/ (US), /ˈdæmpən/ (UK)| v. |使潮湿；抑制；减弱；使沮丧|To make slightly wet. To make less strong or intense.|例句：The rain dampened the ground. 雨水使地面潮湿了。|近义词：moisten, wet, stifle, suppress, curb, diminish|反义词:dry, encourage, intensify|verb

dampen:/ˈdæmpən/ (US), /ˈdæmpən/ (UK)| adj. |潮湿的|Slightly wet.|例句：The dampened cloth felt cold against my skin. 潮湿的布料贴在我的皮肤上感觉很冷。|近义词：moist, humid|反义词:dry|adjective
