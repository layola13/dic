autocross:/ˈɔːtəʊkrɒs/ (US) /ˈɔːtəkrɒs/ (UK)
|n.|汽车越野赛（单数）|A timed competition in which drivers navigate a defined course on a paved or unpaved surface.  This usually involves cones marking the course and penalties for hitting them.|She won her first autocross last weekend. 她上周末赢得了她的第一次汽车越野赛。|近义词：slalom racing, gymkhana|反义词:|名词

词源:  "autocross" is a portmanteau of "automobile" and "cross-country."  While it doesn't involve traditional cross-country terrain, it retains the element of navigating a course.
