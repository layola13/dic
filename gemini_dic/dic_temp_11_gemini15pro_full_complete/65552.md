euthanizes:/ˈjuːθəˌnaɪzɪz/
verb
对...实施安乐死，使安乐死
to kill (a person or animal) painlessly, especially to relieve suffering from an incurable illness.
The vet euthanized the dog to end its suffering.  兽医对这只狗实施了安乐死以结束它的痛苦。
Synonyms: put down, put to sleep
Antonyms: resuscitate, revive
词性: 动词
