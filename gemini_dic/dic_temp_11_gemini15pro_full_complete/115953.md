marriedly:/ˈmærɪdli/| adv.|婚姻地，像已婚者那样|In a manner befitting a married person; as a married person would.|She spoke marriedly about the joys and challenges of raising children. 她像个已婚妇女一样谈论养育孩子的快乐和挑战。|近义词: conjugally, matrimonially |反义词: singlely, unmarriedly|词性:副词
词源: From "married" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Married" comes from Old English "gemarried," past participle of "merrian" (to marry), from Proto-Germanic "*marjan," meaning "to provide with a husband or wife."
