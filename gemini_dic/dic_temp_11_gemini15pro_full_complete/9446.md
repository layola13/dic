antireductionism:/ˌæntiɹɪˈdʌkʃənɪzəm/| n. |反还原论|The philosophical position that a complex system is not simply the sum of its parts and cannot be fully understood by studying its individual components.  It emphasizes the emergent properties of the whole.|例句：Antireductionism argues that consciousness cannot be explained solely by examining brain chemistry. 反还原论认为，意识不能仅仅通过检查大脑化学来解释。

Antireductionism in sociology emphasizes the importance of social structures and institutions. 社会学中的反还原论强调社会结构和制度的重要性。|近义词：holism, organicism|反义词：reductionism|词性:noun
