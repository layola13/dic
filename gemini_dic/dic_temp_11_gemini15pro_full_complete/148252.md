overslavishness:/ˌoʊvərˈsleɪvɪʃnəs/
n.
过度奴性，过度顺从
Excessive subservience or devotion; a tendency to be too compliant or submissive.
His overslavishness to the boss made his colleagues uncomfortable.  他对老板的过度顺从让同事们感到不舒服。
Near Synonyms: servility, subservience, submissiveness, obsequiousness, compliance, docility
Antonyms: independence, assertiveness, rebelliousness, defiance
词性: noun
