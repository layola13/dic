half-petrified:/ˌhæf ˈpetrɪfaɪd/
adj.
半石化的；吓呆的
partly petrified; extremely frightened or stunned.
She stood there, half-petrified, unable to move or speak. 她站在那里，吓呆了，一动也不能动，一句话也说不出来。
近义词:half-scared, terrified, stunned, paralyzed
反义词:calm, composed, relaxed
词性:形容词

half:/hæf/
n. adj. adv.
一半，半个；
One of two equal parts of something.
Cut the apple in half. 把苹果切成两半。
近义词:partially, partly
反义词:wholly, fully
词性:名词，形容词，副词

petrified:/ˈpetrɪfaɪd/
adj.
石化的；吓呆的
So frightened that one is unable to move; terrified.
He was petrified with fear. 他吓得魂飞魄散。
近义词:terrified, horrified, scared stiff
反义词:unafraid, fearless, brave
词性:形容词

词源：
"half-" simply means one of two equal parts.
"petrified" comes from the Latin "petrificare," meaning "to turn into stone," from "petra" (rock) and "facere" (to make).  Figuratively, it means to be so frightened as to be unable to move.
