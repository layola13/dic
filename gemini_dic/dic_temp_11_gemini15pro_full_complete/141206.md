oop:/uːp/ |interj.|哎哟；哎呀|An exclamation of surprise or mild apology.|Oops, I spilled my coffee. 哎哟，我把咖啡洒了。|Synonym: uh-oh, whoops|Antonym: (No direct antonym for an exclamation)|interjection

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What does he do for a living? = 他以什么为生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner tonight? = 她今晚晚餐做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has he done to deserve this award? = 他做了什么才配得上这个奖？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has she been studying at university? = 她在大学里一直在学什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last weekend? = 你上周末做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 聚会上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting?  = 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you moved here? = 你搬到这里之前做了什么？
What had they eaten before the main course? =  他们在主菜之前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called? = 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been working on before she quit her job? = 她辞职前一直在做什么？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will happen if we don't act now? = 如果我们现在不采取行动会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next year? = 明年这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们在下次会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the age of 30? = 到30岁时你将取得什么成就？
What will have changed by the end of the century? = 到本世纪末会发生什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习了什么专业多久了？

虚拟语气:
If I knew what he wanted, I would get it for him. = 如果我知道他想要什么，我会帮他弄到。
I wish I knew what the answer was. = 我希望我知道答案是什么。

被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the summer? = 你暑假的计划是什么？

否定句:
I don't know what to expect. = 我不知道会发生什么。
They didn't say what they were doing. = 他们没说他们在做什么。
