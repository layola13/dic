gibberishes:/ˈɡɪbərɪʃɪz/| n.|胡言乱语，乱七八糟的话|Unintelligible or nonsensical talk or writing.|He was talking such gibberish that I couldn't understand a word.|Synonyms: nonsense, babble, jargon, drivel|Antonyms: sense, meaning, clarity|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From earlier *gibberish, perhaps a variant of jabber, imitative of rapid, incomprehensible talk.
