manure:/məˈnjʊər/ /məˈnʊr/| n. vt.|肥料，粪肥；施肥|Animal dung used for fertilizing land.  动物粪便用作土地肥料。|The farmer spread manure on his fields. 农民在他的田里施肥。|Synonyms: fertilizer, compost, dung|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "barrenness" referring to the resulting state of unfertilized land)|noun
manure:/məˈnjʊər/ /məˈnʊr/| vt.|给…施肥|To apply manure to.  给…施肥。|He manured the field before planting the corn. 他在播种玉米之前给田地施肥。|Synonyms: fertilize, enrich, topdress|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "deplete" in the sense of removing nutrients)|verb
