obsolete:/ˈɑːbsəliːt/ (US), /ˈɒbsəliːt/ (UK)| adj.|过时的，废弃的，淘汰的|No longer produced or used; out of date.|Example: Typewriters have become obsolete in the digital age. 打字机在数字时代已经过时了。|Example: Many old laws are now obsolete. 许多旧法律现在已经过时了。|近义词：outdated, archaic, antiquated, old-fashioned, extinct|反义词：modern, current, contemporary, new, up-to-date|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *obsoletus*, past participle of *obsolescere* "to fall into disuse," from *ob* "toward" + *solescere* "to grow accustomed," from *solere* "to be accustomed."
