midgut:/ˈmɪdɡʌt/
n.|中肠|The central part of the alimentary canal, especially in invertebrates and lower vertebrates, between the foregut and hindgut.|The midgut of the insect is responsible for most of the digestion and absorption.|Synonym: mesenteron|Antonym: N/A|noun
