lowkey:/ˈloʊki/| adv. adj.|低调地，低调的|Lowkey describes something that is quiet, restrained, or subtle. It can refer to a person's behavior, an event, or an atmosphere.  It originated as slang, derived from the hip-hop community, but is now used more widely. |例句：She had a lowkey birthday celebration with just a few close friends. 她只和几个亲密的朋友低调地庆祝了生日。


例句：He's been keeping a lowkey profile since the scandal.  自从丑闻之后，他一直保持低调。


近义词：subtle, understated, quiet, discreet, chill|反义词:loud, flamboyant, ostentatious, public, high-key|词性:adverb, adjective
