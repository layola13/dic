acronyms:/ˈækrənɪmz/ | n. | 首字母缩略词；缩写词 | An acronym is a word formed from the first letter of each word in a series of words. |  Example:  NATO is an acronym for North Atlantic Treaty Organization. 北大西洋公约组织的缩写是北约。

Example:  LASER is an acronym for Light Amplification by Stimulated Emission of Radiation. 激光的缩写是“受激辐射光放大”。

| synonyms: abbreviations, initialisms, contractions | antonyms:  expansions, full forms | noun
