lasering:/ˈleɪzərɪŋ/| n. v.|激光加工，激光照射|The process of using a laser to cut, engrave, or otherwise modify a material.  The act of directing a laser beam at something.|例句：Lasering is a precise method for cutting metal. 激光切割是一种精确的金属切割方法。

例句：The doctor is lasering away the birthmark. 医生正在用激光去除胎记。|近义词：laser treatment, laser surgery|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider traditional methods like sawing, drilling, etc. depending on context)|词性: noun/verb
