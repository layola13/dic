insecution: /ˌɪnsɪˈkjuːʃən/
n.
迫害；烦扰
The constant insecution from the paparazzi drove the actress to seek refuge in a remote location.
狗仔队不断的烦扰迫使这位女演员到一个偏远的地方寻求庇护。
The insecution of religious minorities has been a persistent problem in the region.
对少数宗教群体的迫害一直是该地区的一个持续存在的问题。
近义词: persecution, harassment, hounding, badgering
反义词: protection, support, assistance

词性: noun


词源:  Late Middle English: from Latin insecutio(n-), from the verb insequi ‘follow closely’, from in- ‘on, towards’ + sequi ‘follow’.
