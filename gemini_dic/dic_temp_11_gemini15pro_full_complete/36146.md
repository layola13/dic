coexistence:/ˌkoʊɪɡˈzɪstəns/ | n. |共存，共处|The state or fact of existing at the same time or in the same place.|例句：The two countries have a long history of peaceful coexistence. 这两个国家有着和平共处的悠久历史。|例句：The park promotes the coexistence of humans and wildlife. 这个公园促进了人类和野生动物的共存。|近义词：cohabitation, living together, compatibility|反义词：conflict, antagonism|词性:名词

coexist:/ˌkoʊɪɡˈzɪst/ | v. |共存，共处|To exist at the same time or in the same place.|例句：Different religious groups have coexisted peacefully in the region for centuries.  几个世纪以来，不同的宗教团体在该地区和平共处。|例句：Can different species coexist in the same ecosystem? 不同的物种能在同一个生态系统中共存吗？|近义词：live together, cohabit|反义词：clash, conflict|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From co- + existence. Co- means "together," "jointly," or "mutually." Existence comes from the Latin word "existentia," meaning "being," "existence," or "reality."  Therefore, coexistence literally means "existing together."
