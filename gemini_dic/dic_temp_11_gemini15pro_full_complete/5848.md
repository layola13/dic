amazements:/əˈmeɪzmənts/
n.
惊奇，惊叹，令人惊奇的事物
A feeling of great surprise or wonder.  The state of being amazed; astonishment. Something that causes amazement.
The magician's tricks filled the children with amazement. 魔术师的戏法让孩子们充满了惊奇。
The sheer size of the universe fills me with amazement. 宇宙的浩瀚让我惊叹不已。
Synonyms: wonders, marvels, miracles, astonishment, awe
Antonyms: apathy, indifference, boredom
Noun
