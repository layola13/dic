hemipteran:/ˌhɛmɪˈptərən/| n. |半翅目昆虫|An insect of the order Hemiptera, such as a bug, aphid, or cicada, typically having two pairs of wings, the front pair being partly thickened.  |例句：The stink bug is a common hemipteran.  椿象是一种常见的半翅目昆虫。

例句：Aphids are hemipterans that can be detrimental to crops.  蚜虫是半翅目昆虫，可能对农作物有害。|近义词：bug, insect|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Ancient Greek ἡμι- (hēmi-, “half”) + πτερόν (pterón, “wing”)
