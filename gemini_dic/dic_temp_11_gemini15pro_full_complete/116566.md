matiness:/ˈmeɪtinɪs/
noun
成熟度, 老练
the quality of being mature or seasoned.
Her matiness and experience made her the perfect candidate for the job. 她的成熟和经验使她成为这份工作的理想人选。
maturity, sophistication, worldliness
immaturity, naivete, inexperience
n.


matiness's:/ˈmeɪtinɪsɪz/
noun
成熟度的, 老练的 (matiness的所有格形式,罕用)
possessive form of matiness, rarely used.
