funnelling:/ˈfʌnəlɪŋ/ | n. | 漏斗状物; 汇集;  |The process of narrowing down or concentrating something, often into a smaller area or group.  This can refer to physical objects being channeled, or abstract concepts like information or resources. | The police were funnelling the crowd through a narrow exit. 警察正引导人群通过一个狭窄的出口。

The website is funnelling traffic to its main sales page.  这个网站正在将流量引导到其主要的销售页面。| 近义词: channeling, concentrating, focusing, directing | 反义词: dispersing, scattering, spreading | 词性: 名词 (derived from the verb "funnel")

funnel:/ˈfʌnl/ | n. v. | 漏斗; 汇集; 用漏斗过滤;  |A cone-shaped utensil with a tube at the apex for conducting liquid or other substances through a small opening, or to guide something through a narrow passage. As a verb, it means to guide or channel something through a narrow opening or into a particular place. | Use a funnel to pour the liquid into the bottle. 用漏斗把液体倒进瓶子里。

They are funnelling all their resources into the new project. 他们正把所有资源都投入到新项目中。| 近义词: channel, direct, guide, concentrate (v.); cone, hopper (n.) | 反义词: disperse, scatter, spread (v.) | 词性: 名词, 动词
