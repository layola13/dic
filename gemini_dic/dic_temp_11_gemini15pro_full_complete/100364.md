interstitial:/ˌɪntərˈstɪʃəl/ | adj. |  间质的，间隙的 |  Situated within the interstices or gaps of a tissue or structure. |  Interstitial fluid fills the spaces between cells. 间质液充满了细胞间的空隙。 | The interstitial advertising appeared between levels of a game.  插页式广告出现在游戏的不同关卡之间。 |  Synonyms: intervening, in-between, intermediary | Antonyms: continuous, solid | adjective


interstitium:/ˌɪntərˈstɪʃiəm/ | n. | 间隙，空隙 | A small space or gap between things, especially in a tissue or structure. |  The interstitium of the lung is a complex network of connective tissue. 肺的间质是一个复杂的结缔组织网络。 |  The researchers discovered a new organ in the interstitium. 研究人员在间质中发现了一个新的器官。 | Synonyms: gap, space, interstice, lacuna | Antonyms:  continuum, whole | noun
