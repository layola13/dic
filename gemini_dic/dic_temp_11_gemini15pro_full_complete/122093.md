mission:/ˈmɪʃən/ |n.| 任务，使命；代表团；布道 |A task or important duty that someone is given to do; a group of people sent to a foreign country to represent their government, a religious organization, etc.; an attempt to spread a particular religion or belief system among a group of people.| The astronaut's mission was to collect rock samples from the moon. 宇航员的任务是从月球上收集岩石样本。|synonyms: assignment, task, duty, delegation, objective, quest| antonyms: vacation, idleness, leisure, retreat| noun

mission:/ˈmɪʃən/ |n.| 使命感 |A strong feeling of duty and purpose.| She felt a strong sense of mission to help the poor. 她感到一种强烈的使命感去帮助穷人。 |synonyms: calling, purpose, vocation| antonyms: aimlessness, apathy| noun
