overinhibiting:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈhɪbɪtɪŋ/
| adj. | 过度抑制的 | Inhibiting something to an excessive degree; suppressing natural impulses or behaviors too much. |  He was so overinhibiting that he rarely spoke up in meetings, even when he had good ideas. 他过度抑制自己，即使有好主意也很少在会议上发言。| 近义词: repressive, restrictive, suppressive | 反义词: expressive, uninhibited, impulsive | 词性: 形容词

overinhibiting:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈhɪbɪtɪŋ/
| v. | 过度抑制 | The act of overinhibiting; excessively suppressing natural impulses or behaviors.  | She was overinhibiting her child's natural curiosity. 她过度抑制了孩子天生的好奇心。 | 近义词: suppressing, repressing, restricting | 反义词: encouraging, liberating, indulging | 词性: 动词 (现在分词)


词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "inhibit" (from Latin "inhibere" meaning "to restrain, hold back") + "-ing" (present participle suffix)
