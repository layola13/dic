gutturine:/ˈɡʌtərɪn/ |adj.|喉音的；似喉音的；由喉音发出的|Relating to or produced in the throat; guttural.|The guttural sounds of the language were difficult to pronounce. 这种语言的喉音很难发音。|Synonyms: guttural, throaty|Antonyms: clear, melodious|adjective

gutturine:/ˈɡʌtərɪn/ |n.|雨水管；檐槽；排水沟|A gutter; a channel for carrying off rainwater.|The gutters were overflowing with rainwater. 排水沟里的雨水溢出来了。|Synonyms: gutter, channel, trough|Antonyms: ridge, peak|noun
