nonaffinities:/ˌnɒnəˈfɪnɪtiz/| n. |缺乏亲和力；不相似；不相容性|Lack of affinities; absence of similarities or compatibility.  The nonaffinities between the two groups led to constant conflict. 这两个群体之间的不相似导致了持续的冲突。|Near Synonyms: dissimilarity, incompatibility, divergence, disparity, incongruity |Antonyms: affinity, similarity, compatibility, resemblance, concordance|noun
