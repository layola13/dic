ike:/aɪk/ | n. | 名字；喜欢的人（或物）| A name, or someone or something that is liked. |  My friend's name is Ike. 我的朋友名叫艾克。

Ike:/aɪk/ | n. | 艾森豪威尔（Dwight D. Eisenhower 的昵称）| Nickname for Dwight D. Eisenhower. | Ike was a popular president. 艾克是一位受欢迎的总统。

ike:/aɪk/ | v. | 喜欢（like的非正式说法）| Informal form of "like". | I ike going to the movies. 我喜欢去看电影。
