co-wrote:/ˌkoʊˈroʊt/| v.|共同撰写，合著|A past tense form of "co-write," meaning to write something together with another person or people.|Example:  She co-wrote the screenplay with her husband. 她和她丈夫共同创作了剧本。|Example: They co-wrote a hit song that topped the charts. 他们合写了一首热门歌曲，荣登排行榜榜首。|近义词：co-authored, collaborated on|反义词:sole-authored|词性:动词
