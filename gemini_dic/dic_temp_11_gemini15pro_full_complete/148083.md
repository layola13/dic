oversceptical: /ˈəʊvəˈskɛptɪkəl/ (UK) /ˈoʊvərˈskɛptɪkəl/ (US)| adj. | 过于怀疑的，过度怀疑的 | Excessively or unduly skeptical; inclined to doubt or question everything.  Characterized by an excessive tendency to disbelieve or doubt claims or statements. | He was oversceptical about the new policy, refusing to believe it could be effective. 他对新政策过度怀疑，不相信它会有效。 |  She's so oversceptical that she doesn't even trust expert opinions. 她太过于怀疑了，甚至不相信专家的意见。 | cynical, distrustful, doubtful, incredulous |  trusting, credulous, gullible | adjective
