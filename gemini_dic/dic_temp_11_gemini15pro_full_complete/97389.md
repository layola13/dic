infraspinatus: |n.|冈下肌|A large triangular muscle which occupies the chief part of the infraspinatous fossa; it is covered by the trapezius and deltoid, and assists in rotating the arm outward.|The infraspinatus muscle is one of the four rotator cuff muscles and is essential for shoulder stability.|近义词: rotator cuff muscle|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific muscle)|词性:n.
infraspinate: /ˌɪnfrəˈspaɪneɪt/
infraspinate: /ˌɪnfrəˈspaɪnɑːt/
infraspinate: |adj.|冈下的；位于肩胛冈之下的|Situated beneath the spine of the scapula.|The infraspinate fossa is located on the posterior aspect of the scapula.|近义词: subscapular|反义词: supraspinate|词性:adj.
