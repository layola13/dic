enseel:/ɪnˈsiːl/ | v. | 封闭，密封 | To enclose or seal securely, especially with or as if with a seal. |  The documents were ensealed in an envelope. 文件被密封在一个信封里。|  The king ensealed the decree with his royal signet. 国王用他的皇家印章密封了法令。| synonyms: seal, close, secure, fasten | antonyms: open, unseal, reveal | verb
