overclimb:/ˌoʊvərˈklaɪm/| v.|过度攀爬；爬得过高|To climb beyond a safe or reasonable limit; to climb too high.|The inexperienced climber overclimbed and fell. 那位缺乏经验的登山者爬得过高，摔了下来。|近义词：ascend too high, climb excessively|反义词: descend, rappel|verb
