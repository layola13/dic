crystallinity:/ˌkrɪstəˈlɪnɪti/
n.
结晶度，晶体度
The degree to which a substance is crystalline, or made up of crystals.  A measure of the extent of crystalline regions in a material.
The crystallinity of the polymer affects its strength and flexibility.  这种聚合物的结晶度会影响其强度和柔韧性。

The sample showed high crystallinity. 样本显示出高结晶度。
近义词:crystallization, crystalline structure
反义词:amorphousness
词性:noun
