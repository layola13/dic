forswearing:/fɔːrˈswɛərɪŋ/
verb
放弃，发誓放弃，作伪证
The act of renouncing or rejecting something under oath.
He forswore all worldly possessions upon entering the monastery. 他进入修道院后放弃了所有的世俗财产。
Synonyms: renouncing, repudiating, abjuring
Antonyms: embracing, accepting, affirming
词性: 动名词 (gerund)
