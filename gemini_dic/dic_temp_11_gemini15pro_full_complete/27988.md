canvassed:/ˈkænvəst/ | v. | 拉选票；征求意见；详细讨论；劝说 | to try to persuade people to vote for someone or something; to ask people about their opinions; to discuss something thoroughly; to solicit support |  The politicians canvassed the neighborhood for votes. 政治家们在社区里拉选票。 | Synonyms: solicit, poll, survey, campaign, examine, discuss | Antonyms: ignore, neglect | verb

canvassed:/ˈkænvəst/ | adj. |  (rarely used)  having been canvassed | examined, surveyed, polled | The canvassed area showed strong support for the proposal.  被调查的区域显示出对该提案的强烈支持。 | Synonyms: surveyed, polled, examined | Antonyms: unexamined, unsurveyed | adjective
