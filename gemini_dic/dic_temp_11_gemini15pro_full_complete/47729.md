decagon:/ˈdekəɡɒn/ (UK) /ˈdekəɡɑːn/ (US)| n. |十边形；十角形|A plane figure with ten straight sides and angles.|例句：A regular decagon has ten equal sides and angles. 一个正十边形有十条等长边和十个相等的角。

例句：The architect incorporated a decagon into the building's design.  建筑师将一个十边形融入到建筑设计中。|近义词：polygon|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps circle or nonagon for contrast)|词性:noun
