hylaeosaurus:/ˌhaɪliːoʊˈsɔːrəs/
n.
林龙（一种已灭绝的恐龙）
A genus of ankylosaurian dinosaur that lived in the Early Cretaceous period.  It is known for its large, bony plates and spines along its back.
Hylaeosaurus was one of the first dinosaurs to be formally described. 林龙是最早被正式描述的恐龙之一。
Hylaeosaurus fossils have been found in England. 林龙化石已在英国被发现。
Synonym: Polacanthus, Ankylosaur
