PHRASE->
earmark for:  指定…的用途  The funds were earmarked for disaster relief. 这些资金被指定用于救灾。
earmark funds: 拨款  The government will earmark funds for education. 政府将拨款用于教育。
be earmarked for: 被指定用于…  This money is earmarked for the new hospital. 这笔钱被指定用于新建医院。
an earmark of: …的特征  A dry wit is an earmark of his writing style.  干巴巴的机智是他的写作风格的一个特征。
earmark something as: 将某物指定为…  The committee earmarked the proposal as a priority. 委员会将该提案指定为优先事项。
