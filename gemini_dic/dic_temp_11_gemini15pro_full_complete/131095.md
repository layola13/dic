nonbiased:/ˌnɒnˈbaɪəst/ | adj. | 无偏见的，公正的 | Showing no prejudice for or against something; impartial. |  The judge delivered a nonbiased verdict. 法官做出了公正的判决。| synonyms: impartial, unbiased, objective, neutral, fair, unprejudiced | antonyms: biased, prejudiced, partial, unfair, subjective | adjective

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not" or "opposite of."  From Latin *non*.
biased (adjective):  Showing inclination or prejudice for or against someone or something.  From Old French *biais* "slant, oblique."
