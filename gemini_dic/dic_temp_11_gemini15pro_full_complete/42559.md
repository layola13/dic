counterordering:/ˌkaʊntərˈɔːrdərɪŋ/ | n. | 反指令，对抗性指令 | The act of giving an order that contradicts a previous order. | The general's counterordering caused confusion among the troops. 将军的反指令使部队陷入了混乱。| 近义词: countermand, revocation | 反义词: order, instruction | noun
