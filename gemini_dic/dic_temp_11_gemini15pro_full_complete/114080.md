magnifiable:/ˈmæɡnɪfaɪəbl/
adjective
可放大的, 可扩大的
capable of being magnified
The image on the screen is magnifiable.  屏幕上的图像可以放大。
The fine print was barely magnifiable with a standard lens. 用标准镜头几乎无法放大这 мелкие 印刷品。
近义词:enlargeable, expandable, amplifiable
反义词: unmagnifiable, reducible
词性: adjective

词源:
From late Middle English: from late Latin *magnificabilis, from magnificare ‘make great’ (see magnify) + -able.
