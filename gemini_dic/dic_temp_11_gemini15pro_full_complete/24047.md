breeziness:/ˈbriːzinəs/| n.|轻快，活泼，微风习习|A quality of lightheartedness and cheerfulness, often associated with a gentle wind.|The breeziness of her personality made her popular. 她性格的轻快活泼让她很受欢迎。|近义词：lightheartedness, airiness, cheerfulness, levity|反义词：seriousness, solemnity, gravity|词性:noun

词源: From breezy + -ness.  Breezy itself comes from breeze, which has origins in words referring to a north wind.
