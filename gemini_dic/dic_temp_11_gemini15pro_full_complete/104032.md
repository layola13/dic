juke:/dʒuːk/| n. v. |酒吧;  juke joint(黑人小酒馆); (橄榄球等)假动作; |A juke is a roadside establishment that provides food, drinks, and music, typically catering to African American patrons in the rural South. As a verb, it means to make a deceptive move, especially in sports, to evade an opponent.|

例句：
He owns a juke joint down by the crossroads. 他在十字路口附近有一家小酒馆。

He juked the defender and ran for a touchdown. 他用假动作晃过了防守队员，跑了个触地得分。


近义词：(n.) bar, club, roadhouse, honky-tonk (v.) dodge, feint, fake, sidestep|
反义词: (v.) face, confront|
词性: 名词, 动词|

jukebox:/ˈdʒuːkbɒks/| n. |自动点唱机|A jukebox is a coin-operated machine that plays selected music recordings.|

The old jukebox in the corner played classic rock and roll tunes.  角落里的老式自动点唱机播放着经典的摇滚乐曲。
