monodelphia:/ˌmɒnəˈdɛlfiə/ | n. |单孔目动物|A subclass of mammals characterized by having a single urogenital opening, encompassing marsupials and placental mammals.  | Monodelphia includes a wide variety of animals, from kangaroos to humans. 单孔目动物包括从袋鼠到人类的各种动物。|  Synonym: Eutheria (often used interchangeably, though technically slightly different) | Antonym:  Prototheria (egg-laying mammals)| noun
