glassier:/ˈɡlæsiər/ /ˈɡlæsiər/ | adj. | 更光滑的；更像玻璃的；更冷淡的 | more smooth, shiny, and like glass; more cold and unfriendly in manner | The lake was glassier than usual this morning. 今早湖面比平时更平静光滑。|  The CEO's glassier stare made the employee nervous. 首席执行官冰冷的眼神让员工感到紧张。 | synonyms: smoother, shinier, icier, colder | antonyms: rougher, duller, warmer | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
