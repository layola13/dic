foolhardy:/ˈfuːlhɑːrdi/ /ˈfulˌhɑrdi/| adj.|鲁莽的，蛮干的|Unwisely bold or rash; reckless. Showing a lack of good sense and too much courage, especially when this leads to danger.|例句：It would be foolhardy to try to climb the mountain in such bad weather.  在如此恶劣的天气下尝试爬山是鲁莽的。

例句：His foolhardy actions nearly cost him his life. 他鲁莽的行为几乎让他丧命。|近义词：reckless, rash, impetuous, daredevil, audacious|反义词：cautious, prudent, sensible, wise, circumspect|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *folhardi, from fol "foolish" + hardi "bold, brave".
