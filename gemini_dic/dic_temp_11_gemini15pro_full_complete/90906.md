huntress:/ˈhʌntrɪs/| n.|女猎人|A woman who hunts animals for food or sport.|例句：The skilled huntress tracked the deer through the forest.  熟练的女猎人在森林里追踪鹿。

例句：Artemis is often depicted as a huntress in Greek mythology.  在希腊神话中，阿尔忒弥斯常被描绘成一个女猎人。|近义词：hunter, huntswoman|反义词:prey|词性:noun
