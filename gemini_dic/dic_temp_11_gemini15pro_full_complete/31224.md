chanson:/ʃɑːnˈsɒn/ | n. | 歌曲，歌曲 | A French song, typically a lyrical ballad. |  She sang a beautiful chanson. 她唱了一首优美的法国歌曲。 | song, ballad, tune | anthem, hymn | noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "chanson"  meaning "song," from Late Latin "cantionem" (nominative cantio), from Latin "canere" meaning "to sing."
