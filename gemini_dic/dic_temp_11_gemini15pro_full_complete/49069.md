dehusk:/diːˈhʌsk/
v.
去除外壳；剥壳
To remove the husk or outer covering from something, especially grain or seeds.
The machine dehusks the rice efficiently.  这台机器可以高效地去除稻壳。
Farmers dehusk the corn before storing it.  农民在储存玉米之前会剥去玉米壳。
近义词:shell, husk, peel
反义词:cover, encase
词性:动词

dehusker:/diːˈhʌskər/
n.
去壳机；剥壳机
A machine used to remove husks or outer coverings, especially from grain.
The dehusker is an essential piece of equipment for rice processing.  去壳机是稻米加工的重要设备。
A new dehusker can significantly increase productivity.  一台新的去壳机可以显著提高生产力。
近义词:sheller, husker, peeler
反义词:N/A
词性:名词

dehusking:/diːˈhʌskɪŋ/
n.
去壳；剥壳
The process of removing husks or outer coverings.
Dehusking is the first step in processing many grains.  去壳是许多谷物加工的第一步。
The dehusking process can be done manually or by machine.  去壳过程可以手动完成，也可以用机器完成。
近义词:shelling, husking, peeling
反义词:N/A
词性:名词
