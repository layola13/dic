nutty:/ˈnʌti/| adj.|坚果味的；古怪的，疯狂的|Containing or resembling nuts; slightly crazy or eccentric.|The cake had a rich, nutty flavor.  这个蛋糕有着浓郁的坚果香味。

nutty:/ˈnʌti/| n.|疯子，怪人|(informal) A crazy or eccentric person.|He's a bit of a nutty, but harmless. 他有点疯疯的，但无害。|近义词：crackpot, loony, eccentric|反义词：N/A|词性:名词
