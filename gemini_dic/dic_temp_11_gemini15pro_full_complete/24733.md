bronzy:/ˈbrɒnzi/ (UK) /ˈbrɑːnzi/ (US)| adj.|古铜色的，青铜色的|Having the color of bronze; tanned.|例句：She had a bronzy tan from her vacation in Hawaii. 她从夏威夷度假回来，晒成了古铜色。

例句：The statue had a bronzy sheen. 这座雕像有着青铜色的光泽。|近义词：bronze, copper-colored, tan, tawny|反义词：pale, white|词性:adjective
