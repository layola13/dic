factorization:/ˌfæktərɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 因式分解，分解 | The process of breaking down a number or expression into smaller parts (factors) that, when multiplied together, produce the original number or expression. | The factorization of 12 is 2 x 2 x 3.  12的因式分解是2 x 2 x 3。 | factoring, decomposition | combination, synthesis | noun
