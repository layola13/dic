cantopop: /ˈkæntɒpɒp/ (UK) /ˈkæntɑːpɑːp/ (US)| n. | 粤语流行音乐 |A genre of popular music originating from Hong Kong, sung in Cantonese. It incorporates elements of Western pop music with traditional Chinese melodies and lyrics often reflecting Hong Kong's unique cultural identity.| Cantopop has been a dominant force in the Asian music scene for decades.  粤语流行音乐几十年来一直是亚洲乐坛的主导力量。

Cantopop stars often hold concerts in major cities around the world. 粤语流行歌星经常在世界各大城市举办演唱会。| 近义词：HK-pop | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider genres like Mandopop or K-pop as contrasting examples) | 词性: noun
