fabricius:/fəˈbrɪʃəs/| n.|法布里丘斯，法布里奇奥（姓氏）|A Roman surname, most famously associated with Hieronymus Fabricius (Girolamo Fabrici d'Acquapendente), a pioneering anatomist and surgeon.  He is considered the "Father of Embryology".|例句：Fabricius was a renowned anatomist. 法布里丘斯是一位著名的解剖学家。

例句：The bursa of Fabricius is named after him. 法氏囊以他的名字命名。|近义词: (As a surname, it has no direct synonyms.  For the anatomical structure, one might use "cloacal bursa" in some contexts.)|反义词:  N/A|词性:Noun
