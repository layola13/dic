awfulness:/ˈɔːflnəs/| n.|糟糕，恶劣，可怕|The awfulness of the situation was overwhelming.  这种情况的糟糕程度令人难以承受。|The awfulness of the food made him sick.  食物的糟糕程度让他感到恶心。|近义词：terribleness, dreadfulness, horror, vileness|反义词：wonderfulness, pleasantness, beauty|词性:noun

词源: From awful + -ness.  Awful comes from awe (meaning "fear and respect") + -ful.  The original meaning was "inspiring awe," but it later evolved to mean "extremely bad" or "terrible."
