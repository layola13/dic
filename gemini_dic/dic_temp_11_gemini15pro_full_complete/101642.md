irrefrangibility:/ˌɪrɪfræn(d)ʒɪˈbɪləti/
n.
不可折射性
The quality of not being refracted.
The irrefrangibility of X-rays makes them useful in medical imaging. X射线的不可折射性使它们在医学成像中很有用。

The irrefrangibility of gamma rays allows them to penetrate dense materials.  伽马射线的不可折射性使它们能够穿透致密材料。

Synonym:  non-refractivity
Antonym: refractivity
noun
