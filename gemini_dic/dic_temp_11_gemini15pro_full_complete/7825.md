antagonized:/ænˈtæɡənaɪzd/
verb
使对抗；引起…的敌意；激怒
to make someone feel hostile or angry towards you
His aggressive manner antagonized many colleagues.  他咄咄逼人的态度激怒了许多同事。
antagonize, provoke, irritate, alienate
placate, appease, conciliate
verb
