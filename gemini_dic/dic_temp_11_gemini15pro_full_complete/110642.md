limu:/ˈlɪmuː/
n.
海藻, 特指夏威夷的一种可食用海藻
A type of edible seaweed, specifically referring to a variety found in Hawaii.
例句：Limu is a traditional Hawaiian food.  海藻是夏威夷的传统食物。

例句：She gathered limu from the tide pools. 她从潮汐池里采集海藻。

近义词：seaweed, algae
反义词:  (无明确的反义词)
词性: 名词
