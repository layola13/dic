oorial:/ˈɔːriəl/ | n. |盘羊, 乌里羊| A wild sheep (Ovis vignei) native to Central Asia, having reddish-brown fur and slightly curved horns. |  The oorial is known for its agility in mountainous terrain. 盘羊以其在山区的敏捷性而闻名。|  The oorial's coat provides excellent camouflage. 盘羊的皮毛提供了极好的伪装。 | synonyms: wild sheep, sha, urial | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for an animal species, could consider domesticated sheep) | noun
