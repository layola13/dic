anglophobia:/ˌæŋɡləˈfoʊbiə/
noun
仇英情绪，恐英症
An extreme dislike or fear of England, its people, or its culture.
Many postcolonial writers explore themes of anglophobia in their work. 许多后殖民作家在他们的作品中探讨了仇英情绪的主题。
anglophobe
xenophobia
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Anglo- (English) + -phobia (fear).  The prefix "Anglo-" comes from the Latin "Anglus," referring to the Angles, one of the Germanic peoples who settled in Britain.  The suffix "-phobia" comes from the Greek "phobos," meaning "fear" or "panic."
