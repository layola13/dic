hydrostatical:/ˌhaɪdrəˈstætɪkl/ | adj. | 流体静力学的; 静水力学的 | relating to the branch of physics that deals with liquids at rest and the forces they exert; relating to or involving hydrostatics | The hydrostatical pressure at the bottom of the tank is immense. 水箱底部的流体静力压力巨大。 |  Hydrostatic, fluid static | N/A | adjective

hydrostatics:/ˌhaɪdrəˈstætɪks/ | n. | 流体静力学; 静水力学 | the branch of physics that deals with liquids at rest and the forces they exert |  He studied hydrostatics in his physics class. 他在物理课上学习了流体静力学。| fluid mechanics, fluid statics | N/A | noun
