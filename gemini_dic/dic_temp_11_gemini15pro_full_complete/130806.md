nonanticipations:/nɒnænˌtɪsɪˈpeɪʃənz/
|n.| 未预期，未预料；非预期事件 |The model incorporates nonanticipations, meaning it doesn't use future information to predict the past.  该模型包含了非预期性，这意味着它不使用未来信息来预测过去。| The theory of nonanticipations is crucial in control systems. 非预期理论在控制系统中至关重要。 |foresight, expectations|unforeseen circumstances, surprises|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
anticipation (noun): from Late Latin anticipatio, from anticipare "to anticipate," from ante "before" + capere "to take"
