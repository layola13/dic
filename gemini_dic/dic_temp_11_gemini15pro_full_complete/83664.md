haf: /hæf/ | v. (auxiliary) | 有 (用于构成现在完成时)；吃；进行；遭受 | used as an auxiliary verb with the past participle to form the present perfect tense. Also means to eat, experience, or suffer. | Example: I have finished my work. 我已经完成了我的工作。

| Example: They have been to Paris. 他们去过巴黎。 | Synonym: possess, own (for "have" as a main verb) | Antonym: lack (for "have" as a main verb) | Verb (auxiliary and main)

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I have a car. = 我有一辆车。
She has a book. = 她有一本书。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am having lunch. = 我正在吃午餐。
He is having a good time. = 他玩得很开心。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have finished my homework. = 我已经完成了我的作业。
They have traveled the world. = 他们环游了世界。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been studying English for three years. = 我一直在学习英语三年了。
She has been working on this project for months. = 她几个月来一直在做这个项目。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I had a bicycle when I was young. = 我小时候有一辆自行车。
She had a cold last week. = 她上周感冒了。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was having dinner when he called. = 他打电话时我正在吃晚饭。
They were having a party last night. = 他们昨晚在举行派对。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had finished my work before he arrived. = 在他到达之前，我已经完成了我的工作。
She had already left when I got there. = 当我到达那里时，她已经离开了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been working for two hours when she came. = 当她来的时候，我已经工作了两个小时。
They had been playing football for an hour before it started to rain. = 在开始下雨之前，他们已经踢了一个小时的足球。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will have a party next week. = 我下周要举行一个派对。
She will have a new car soon. = 她很快就会有一辆新车。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be having dinner with my family tomorrow. = 我明天将和我的家人一起吃晚饭。
She will be having a meeting at 3 pm. = 她下午3点将举行会议。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I will have finished my work by next week. = 我将在下周之前完成我的工作。
She will have graduated by next year. = 她明年将毕业。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next year, I will have been living here for five years. = 到明年，我将在这里住了五年。
By the time she finishes, she will have been working on this project for a year. = 到她完成时，她将在这个项目上工作一年了。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would have more hobbies. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会有更多的爱好。
I wish I had known about this earlier. = 我希望我早点知道这件事。

被动语态 (主要针对"have"作为主要动词的用法):
A good time was had by all. = 大家都玩得很开心。
The car was had by my father. = 这辆车是我父亲的。

疑问句:
Have you finished your homework? = 你完成作业了吗？
Did you have a good time? = 你玩得开心吗？

否定句:
I don't have any money. = 我没有钱。
I didn't have time to go to the party. = 我没有时间去参加聚会。
