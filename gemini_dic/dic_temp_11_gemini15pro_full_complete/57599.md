draper:/ˈdreɪpər/ (US), /ˈdreɪpə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 布商，窗帘商，服装商|A person who sells cloth, curtains, or clothing, especially formerly.|The draper measured the fabric for the new curtains.  布商为新窗帘量了布料。|Synonyms: cloth merchant, textile dealer|Antonyms: customer, buyer|noun

drape:/dreɪp/| vt. & vi.| 遮盖，披上，悬挂|To cover or hang loosely or elegantly.|She draped a shawl over her shoulders. 她把披肩披在肩上。 The flag was draped over the coffin. 旗帜覆盖在棺材上。|Synonyms: cover, hang, adorn|Antonyms: uncover, expose|verb
