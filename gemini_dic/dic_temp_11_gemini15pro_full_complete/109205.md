leh:/leɪ/ /lɛ/ | interj. | 唉; 哦; (表示轻微的惊讶、不耐烦等) | An exclamation expressing mild surprise, annoyance, etc. | Example: Leh, I can't believe it! 唉，我真不敢相信！ | Synonym: oh, ah | Antonym:  (Interjections don't typically have antonyms) | interjection

leh:/leɪ/ /lɛ/ | n. | (非正式)谎言 | (Informal) A lie.  | Example: That's a load of leh. 那是一堆谎言。 | Synonym: lie, fib, falsehood | Antonym: truth | noun
