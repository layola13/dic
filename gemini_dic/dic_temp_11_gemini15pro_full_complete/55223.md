dissheathe:/dɪsˈʃiːð/
verb
拔出（尤指剑）
to draw or pull out (something, especially a sword) from a sheath or covering.
The knight dissheathed his sword and prepared for battle. 骑士拔出剑，准备战斗。
synonyms: unsheathe, draw, uncover
antonyms: sheathe, encase, cover
v.
