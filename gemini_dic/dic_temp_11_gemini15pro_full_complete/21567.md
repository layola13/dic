bluest:/ˈbluːɪst/| adj.|最蓝的|Superlative form of blue; having the deepest shade of blue.|The bluest sky I've ever seen was in the Maldives. 我见过的最蓝的天空是在马尔代夫。|近义词：deepest blue, most azure, dark blue|反义词：lightest blue, palest blue|词性:形容词

词源: From Old English *blǣwest, superlative of blǣw ("blue").  The "-est" ending is a common superlative suffix in English.
