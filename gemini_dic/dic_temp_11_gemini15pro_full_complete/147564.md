overperch:/ˌōvərˈpɜːrtʃ/ | v. |  飞越 perch 之上; 越过栖木; | The bird overperched its usual branch and landed on a higher one. 这只鸟飞过了它通常栖息的树枝，落在了一个更高的树枝上。 | 近义词: fly over, surpass, exceed | 反义词: descend, fall below | 词性: 动词

overperch:/ˈōvərˌpɜːrtʃ/ | n. | 高枝; 高位 | The eagle surveyed its domain from its overperch on the cliff face.  鹰从悬崖峭壁上的高处俯瞰它的领地。 | 近义词: high perch, vantage point, high position | 反义词: low perch, low position | 词性: 名词
