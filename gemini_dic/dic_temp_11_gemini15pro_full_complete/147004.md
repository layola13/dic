overlaid:/ˌoʊvərˈleɪd/ | adj. | 覆盖的，重叠的 | Covered or decorated with a layer of something else. | The map was overlaid with a grid. 地图上覆盖着网格。 | The cake was overlaid with a layer of icing. 蛋糕上覆盖着一层糖霜。 | 近义词: covered, coated, superimposed | 反义词: uncovered, bare | 词性:形容词
overlaid:/ˌoʊvərˈleɪd/ | v. | 覆盖，重叠 | Past participle of overlay. To cover or decorate something with a layer of something else. | The image was overlaid with text. 图像上覆盖着文字。 |  She overlaid the fabric with lace. 她在布料上覆盖了蕾丝。 | 近义词: cover, coat, superimpose | 反义词: uncover, expose | 词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *oferlēde, past participle of oferlǣdan (“to lead over, cover”), equivalent to over +‎ lay.
