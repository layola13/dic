multicoated:/ˌmʌltiˈkoʊtɪd/
adjective
多层镀膜的
Having multiple layers of optical coatings, typically on a lens or mirror, to reduce reflections and improve light transmission.
This multicoated lens reduces glare and improves image quality.  这个多层镀膜的镜头减少了眩光，提高了图像质量。
The astronomer used a multicoated mirror in his telescope. 天文学家在他的望远镜中使用了一个多层镀膜的反射镜。
Synonyms: coated, layered
Antonyms: uncoated, single-coated
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From multi- + coated.  Multi- is a combining form meaning "many," "multiple," deriving from Latin multus "much, many." Coated comes from the verb "to coat," meaning to cover with a layer of something.  Ultimately from Latin "cotta" meaning "a tunic."
