ladle:/ˈleɪdl/| n. v. |长柄勺; 舀取 |A large long-handled spoon with a cup-shaped bowl, used for serving soup, stew, or sauce. To serve or transfer (liquid) with a ladle.|She ladled the soup into bowls. 她用长柄勺把汤舀进碗里. He ladled out a generous helping of stew. 他盛了一大勺炖菜. |近义词：spoon, dipper, scoop |反义词: (没有直接的反义词) |词性: 名词, 动词
Etymology: Old English *hladan "to load, heap up," from Proto-Germanic *hlaþaną "to load," from PIE root *klad- "to load." The noun ladle is derived from the verb ladle.
