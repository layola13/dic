nontyrannically:/ˌnɒntɪˈrænɪkli/
adverb
非专制地；不暴虐地
In a way that is not tyrannical; without tyranny.
The new leader promised to govern nontyrannically, respecting the rights of all citizens. 新领导人承诺将以非专制的方式进行统治，尊重所有公民的权利。
The committee operated nontyrannically, allowing all members to express their opinions freely. 委员会以非专制的方式运作，允许所有成员自由表达意见。
近义词:democratically, liberally, fairly
反义词:tyrannically, despotically, oppressively
词性:副词

词源:
non- (prefix): not; opposite of  非，不，相反的
tyrannical (adjective):  exercising power in a cruel or arbitrary way  专横的；暴虐的
-ly (suffix):  forms adverbs  构成副词
