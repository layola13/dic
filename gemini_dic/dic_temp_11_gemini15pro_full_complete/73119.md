for-that-matter:/fɔːr ðæt ˈmætər/| idiom | 就此而言；而且；更何况 | used to add a comment about something else that is also true, especially when you are disagreeing with someone or saying something negative |  I don't like it, and, for that matter, neither does he. 我不喜欢它，就此而言，他也不喜欢。 | incidentally, by the way, come to think of it, as a matter of fact |  N/A | idiom
