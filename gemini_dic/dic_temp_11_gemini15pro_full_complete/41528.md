coronate:/ˈkɒrəneɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɔːrəneɪt/ (US)| v. adj.|加冕，冠以…|To crown; to invest with regal dignity and power.  As an adjective, crowned.|

例句：
The archbishop will coronate the new king next week.
大主教下周将为新国王加冕。

例句：
The coronate king addressed his people.
加冕的国王向他的人民发表了讲话。


近义词：crown, enthrone, invest|
反义词:dethrone|
词性:verb, adjective|


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *coronatus*, past participle of *coronare* ("to crown"), from *corona* ("crown, garland").
