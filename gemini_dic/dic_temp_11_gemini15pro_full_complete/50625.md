derelict:/ˈdɛrəlɪkt/ |  n. | 遗弃物，流浪者 | a person without a home, job, or property; something that is abandoned |  The streets were filled with derelicts. 街上满是流浪汉。 | near synonyms: vagrant, bum, tramp | near antonyms: resident, citizen, inhabitant | noun
