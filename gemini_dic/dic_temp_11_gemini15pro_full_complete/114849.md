mammiferous:/məˈmɪfərəs/
adjective
哺乳动物的
Bearing or characterized by mammary glands; of or relating to the class Mammalia.
The platypus, while odd, is a mammiferous creature, as it nourishes its young with milk.  鸭嘴兽虽然很奇怪，但它是一种哺乳动物，因为它用乳汁哺育幼崽。
近义词: mammalian
反义词: (None -  there isn't a direct opposite for "mammiferous")
词性: adjective
