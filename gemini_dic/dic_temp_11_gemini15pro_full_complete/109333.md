lengthways:/ˈleŋθweɪz/ /ˈlɛŋθweɪz/
adjective|纵向地， lengthwise|The carpet is laid lengthways across the room.  地毯是纵向铺在房间里的。|
adverb|纵向地， lengthwise|Cut the cucumber lengthways.  把黄瓜纵向切开。|
近义词: lengthwise, longitudinally
反义词: crosswise, widthwise
词性: adjective, adverb
词源: From length + -ways, by analogy with sideways.
