harmonisable:/ˈhɑːmənaɪzəbl/ | adj. | 可协调的，可统一的 | Able to be harmonized or brought into agreement or consistency.  |  The regulations are harmonisable across the European Union.  这些法规在整个欧盟范围内是可以协调统一的。|  Example:  The different legal systems proved harmonisable. 不同的法律制度证明是可以协调的。 | Synonyms: compatible, consistent, reconcilable, adaptable | Antonyms: incompatible, inconsistent, irreconcilable, inflexible | adjective
