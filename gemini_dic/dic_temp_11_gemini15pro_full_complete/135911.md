nonpublication:/ˌnɑːnpʌblɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 未发表，不出版 | The nonpublication of his research findings was a major setback.  他的研究结果未发表是一个重大挫折。|  The agreement stipulated the nonpublication of certain sensitive information. 该协议规定某些敏感信息不得发表。 | 近义词：suppression, withholding | 反义词: publication, release | 词性: 名词

词源: non- + publication.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "publication" refers to the act of making something publicly known, especially by printing.
