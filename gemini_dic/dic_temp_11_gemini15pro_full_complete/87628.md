hew:/hjuː/ /huː/
v.
砍，劈，凿
to chop or cut with an axe, pick, or other tool

The sculptor hewed the marble into a beautiful statue.
雕塑家把大理石凿成了一座美丽的雕像。

He hewed a path through the dense jungle.
他在茂密的丛林中开辟了一条道路。

近义词:chop, hack, cleave, hack
反义词:construct, build, assemble

词性:动词
