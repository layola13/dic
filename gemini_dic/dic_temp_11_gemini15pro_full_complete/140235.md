offings:/ˈɒfərɪŋz/
noun
产品；祭品；供品
something that is offered, especially as a gift or contribution; something offered as a religious sacrifice
The company's latest offerings include a new range of smartphones. 这家公司最新的产品包括一系列新的智能手机。
They made an offering of fruits and flowers at the temple. 他们在寺庙里供奉了水果和鲜花。
Synonyms: gifts, contributions, donations, sacrifices, oblations
Antonyms: requests, demands
