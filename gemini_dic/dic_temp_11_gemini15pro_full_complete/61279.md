embale:/ɪmˈbeɪl/ | v. | 打包，装入包内 | to pack or wrap (goods) tightly for storage or shipping | The workers embaled the cotton in burlap sacks. 工人们把棉花打包成麻袋。 | 近义词: pack, wrap, bale | 反义词: unpack, unwrap | 词性:动词

embale:/ɪmˈbeɪl/ | v. | (古用法) 裹尸 | to wrap (a corpse) in cloths for burial |  The ancient Egyptians embalmed their pharaohs. 古埃及人用布包裹他们的法老。| 近义词: shroud | 反义词: exhume | 词性:动词
