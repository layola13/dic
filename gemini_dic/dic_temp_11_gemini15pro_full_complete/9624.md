antistatist:/ˈæntiˌsteɪtɪst/
adjective
反国家的；反对国家主义的
Opposed to state intervention or control, especially over economic and social life.
He's a staunch antistatist who believes in minimal government intervention. 他是一位坚定的反国家主义者，信奉最小限度的政府干预。
近义词:libertarian, individualist, anarchist
反义词:statist, collectivist, authoritarian
词性:adjective
