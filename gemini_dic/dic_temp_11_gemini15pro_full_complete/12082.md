artiness:/ˈɑːrtinɪs/
noun
做作;附庸风雅;装腔作势
The affectation of artistic or refined tastes, especially in an exaggerated or superficial way.
Her artiness was evident in her bohemian clothing and constant talk of obscure films. 她穿着波西米亚风格的衣服，不停地谈论一些晦涩的电影，显得很做作。
pretentiousness, affectation, artificiality
sincerity, naturalness, genuineness
