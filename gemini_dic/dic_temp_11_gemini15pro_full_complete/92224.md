hyperthyroidic:/ˌhaɪpərˈθaɪrɔɪdɪk/
adjective
甲状腺功能亢进的
Relating to or affected with hyperthyroidism; excessively active thyroid gland.
She was diagnosed as hyperthyroidic and prescribed medication. 她被诊断为甲状腺功能亢进，并开了药。
The hyperthyroidic cat was noticeably more active than usual. 这只甲状腺机能亢进的猫比平时明显活跃。
近义词: overactive thyroid, thyrotoxic
反义词: hypothyroid, hypothyroidic
词性: adjective
词源: hyper- (over, excessive) + thyroid (thyroid gland) + -ic (adjective forming suffix)
