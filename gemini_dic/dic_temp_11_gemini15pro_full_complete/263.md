abductees:/ˌæbdʌkˈtiːz/
n.
被绑架者，被劫持者
People who have been abducted, often referring to those claimed to have been taken by aliens.
The abductees reported similar experiences. 这些被绑架者报告了类似的经历。
近义词:captives, hostages
反义词:rescuers, liberators
词性: 名词

abductees:/ˌæbdʌkˈtiːz/
词源:  From the verb "abduct," from Latin "abducere" (ab- "away" + ducere "to lead"). The suffix "-ee" denotes someone who receives or undergoes the action of the verb.
