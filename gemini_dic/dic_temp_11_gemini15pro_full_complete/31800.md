checkmate:/ˈtʃɛkmeɪt/ | n. vt. | 将死；彻底失败；挫败 | A situation in chess where one player's king is under immediate attack (in "check") and there is no way to remove the threat. It also means to defeat or frustrate someone completely. |  例句：The white queen moved to D8, checkmate! 白皇后移到D8，将死！

例句：His opponent finally checkmated him in the last round. 他的对手最终在最后一轮将他将死。 | 近义词：defeat, conquer, vanquish, stump | 反义词: save, rescue | 词性: 名词, 动词

Etymology: From Old French "eschec mat" (checkmate), from Arabic "shah mat" (the king is dead).
