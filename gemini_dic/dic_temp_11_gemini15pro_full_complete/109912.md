libations:/ləˈbeɪʃənz/ | n. | 祭奠的奠酒，饮料（尤指酒） | An act of pouring a liquid as an offering to a god or spirit or in memory of those who have died; (formal) drinks, especially alcoholic ones. |  The priest made libations to the gods. 祭司向神灵献上了奠酒。| synonyms: offerings, drinks, beverages, alcohol | antonyms: abstinence, sobriety | noun

libation:/ləˈbeɪʃənz/ | n. |  (literary or humorous) a drink; the pouring out of a drink as an offering to a god | She poured a libation of wine onto the ground. 她把一杯酒倒在地上作为祭品。| synonyms: drink, beverage, toast, offering | antonyms: abstinence, teetotalism | noun
