micro-environmental:/ˌmaɪkroʊɪnˌvaɪrənˈmentl/ | adj. | 微环境的 | Relating to the immediate local environment surrounding something, especially a living organism or a specific business. |  The micro-environmental factors affecting a plant's growth include soil pH, light, and water availability.  该植物生长的微环境因素包括土壤pH值、光照和水分供应。|  近义词: local, immediate, surrounding | 反义词: macro-environmental, global | 词性: adjective

Etymology:

* micro-: from Greek *mikrós* meaning "small".
* environment: from Old French *environer* (to encircle, surround) and ultimately from Latin *in-* + *volvere* (to roll).
