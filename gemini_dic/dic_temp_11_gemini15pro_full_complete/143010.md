outbent:/ˌaʊtˈbɛnt/
adjective
向外弯曲的
Curved or bent outwards.
The outbent branches of the old willow tree drooped towards the water.  古老的柳树向外弯曲的树枝垂向水面。
近义词:arched, curved, bowed
反义词:inbent, incurved
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From "out-" (meaning "outward") + "bent" (past participle of "bend").  由"out-"（意为"向外"）+ "bent"（"bend"的过去分词）组成。
