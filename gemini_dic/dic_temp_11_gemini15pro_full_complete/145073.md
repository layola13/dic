overanalyzes:/ˌoʊvərˈænəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
过度分析
To analyze (something) in too much detail, often to the point of confusion or inaction.
She overanalyzes every decision, which often leads to her missing opportunities. 她过度分析每一个决定，这常常导致她错失良机。
He overanalyzes his relationships and drives his partners away. 他过度分析他的感情关系，把伴侣都赶跑了。
overthink, overinterpret, dissect
underanalyze, skim
v.
