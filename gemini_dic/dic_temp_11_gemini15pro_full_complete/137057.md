nonstop:/ˌnɒnˈstɒp/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˈstɑːp/| adj. adv.|不停的，不断的；直达的|without stopping; continuously|The flight from New York to London is nonstop. 从纽约到伦敦的航班是直达的。|They worked nonstop for 12 hours to finish the project. 他们连续工作了12个小时才完成这个项目。|近义词：continuous, incessant, uninterrupted|反义词: intermittent, occasional|词性:形容词, 副词
词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + stop
