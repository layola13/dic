excel:/ɪkˈsɛl/ /ɛkˈsɛl/ | v. | 擅长；胜过；优于 | To be exceptionally good at or proficient in an activity or subject. | She excels at playing the piano. 她擅长弹钢琴。

excel:/ɪkˈsɛl/ /ɛkˈsɛl/ | v. | 胜过；超过 | To surpass or be superior to others in some quality or performance. | He excelled all his classmates in mathematics. 他的数学成绩超过了所有同学。


近义词: surpass, outdo, exceed, transcend, eclipse
反义词: fail, fall short, lag behind
词性: verb
