doomsayers:/ˈduːmseɪərz/| n.|末日预言者；灾难预言者|People who predict disaster or doom.  Those who constantly warn of impending catastrophe.|The doomsayers predicted the end of the world in 2012. 末日预言者预言2012年是世界末日。

The doomsayers were wrong about the market crash. 末日预言者对市场崩盘的预测是错误的。|近义词：pessimists, alarmists|反义词:optimists|词性:名词
