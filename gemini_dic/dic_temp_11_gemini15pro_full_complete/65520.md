eurypteroid:/jʊˈrɪptərɔɪd/ | adj. n. |广翅鲎目动物的，广翅鲎目动物|Relating to or denoting an extinct group of arthropods, the Eurypterida, which were aquatic and resembled scorpions.  Also, a member of this group. |Example 1: Eurypteroid fossils are found in Paleozoic rocks. 广翅鲎化石发现于古生代岩石中。

Example 2:  The eurypteroid, with its large pincers, was a formidable predator.  这只拥有大钳子的广翅鲎是一个可怕的捕食者。|近义词：sea scorpion (common name) |反义词: None (extinct group)|词性: adjective, noun
