disquisitory:/dɪˈskwɪzɪtɔːri/ /dɪˈskwɪzətɔːri/
adjective
爱议论的；好钻研的；爱讨论的
Inclined to discussion or investigation; analytical.
His disquisitory mind enjoyed exploring complex philosophical problems. 他那爱钻研的头脑喜欢探索复杂的哲学问题。

近义词:analytical, inquisitive, investigative, studious, inquiring, searching, probing
反义词:uninquiring, incurious, indifferent, apathetic


词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English (in the sense ‘formal, argumentative’): from Latin disquisitorius, from disquirere ‘seek diligently’, from dis- (expressing intensive force) + quaerere ‘seek’.
