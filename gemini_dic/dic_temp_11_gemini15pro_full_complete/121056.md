misconnected:/ˌmɪskəˈnɛktɪd/
adjective
连接错误的，接错线的
incorrectly connected; wrongly joined.
The phone lines were misconnected, and I ended up talking to a stranger.  电话线接错了，结果我和一个陌生人聊了起来。
The plumber misconnected the pipes, causing a flood in the bathroom. 水管工接错了管道，导致浴室淹水。

近义词:miswired, wrongly connected, improperly connected
反义词:connected, properly connected
词性:adjective

词源:
mis- (prefix):  meaning "wrongly" or "badly".  From Old English "mis-"
connect (verb): meaning "to join or fasten together". From Latin "connectere," from "com-" (together) + "nectere" (to bind).
-ed (suffix): forms the past participle of verbs, often used to create adjectives.
