arching:/ˈɑːrtʃɪŋ/ | adj. | 拱形的，弯曲的 | curved or bent like an arch |  The cat stretched, its back arching.  猫伸了个懒腰，背拱了起来。| The bridge has a beautiful arching design. 这座桥有一个美丽的拱形设计。 |  curved, bowed, rounded | straight, flat | adjective

Etymology->

From Middle English *archinge, *arringe, from Old French archier, from Latin arcāre (“to bend like a bow”), from arcus (“a bow, arch”).
