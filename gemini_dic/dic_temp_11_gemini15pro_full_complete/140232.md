officiousness:/əˈfɪʃəsnəs/
|n.|爱管闲事；多事； officiousness |The officiousness of the hotel staff was irritating.  酒店员工的多管闲事令人恼火。|近义词：intrusiveness, interference, meddlesomeness|反义词：reserve, aloofness, detachment|词性:名词

officious:/əˈfɪʃəs/
|adj.|爱管闲事的；多事的|Characterized by excessive eagerness in offering unwanted services or advice. |He's an officious little man and widely disliked in the office. 他是个爱管闲事的小个子男人，在办公室里很不受欢迎。|近义词：interfering, meddlesome, intrusive|反义词：reserved, aloof, detached|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *officiosus "obliging, dutiful," from Latin officium "duty, service" (see office (n.)).  The negative connotation developed in English by the late 16th century.
