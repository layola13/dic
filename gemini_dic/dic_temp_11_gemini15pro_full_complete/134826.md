nonmiraculous:/ˌnɒnˌmɪˈrækjʊləs/ | /ˌnɑːnˌmɪˈrækjələs/ | adj. | 非奇迹的；平常的；不足为奇的 | Not miraculous; ordinary or not surprising. |  The doctor explained that her recovery was nonmiraculous, simply the result of good care and her body's natural healing processes. 医生解释说，她的康复并非奇迹，只是良好护理和她身体自然愈合过程的结果。| Example: His success was the result of hard work, not a nonmiraculous event. 他的成功是努力工作的结果，而不是一个奇迹般的事件。 | Synonyms: ordinary, commonplace, mundane, natural, unexceptional | Antonyms: miraculous, extraordinary, supernatural, phenomenal | Word type: adjective
