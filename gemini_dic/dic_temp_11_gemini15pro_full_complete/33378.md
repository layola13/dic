chunkies:/ˈtʃʌŋkiz/| n.|大块，矮胖的人（尤指儿童）, 厚块|A term of endearment for plump children or people.  Often used playfully or affectionately.|例句：Look at those cute little chunkies playing in the sandbox! 瞧那些在沙坑里玩耍的可爱的小胖墩！

例句：She called her baby "my little chunky monkey." 她叫她的宝宝“我的小胖猴子”。


近义词：chubsters, plumpies, toddlers (if referring to young children)|反义词:skinny, thin|词性:noun (plural)


chunk:/tʃʌŋk/| n.|大块，厚块|A substantial amount or piece of something.|例句：A chunk of ice fell from the glacier. 一大块冰从冰川上掉了下来。

例句：He gave me a large chunk of cheese. 他给了我一大块奶酪。


近义词：piece, lump, hunk, block|反义词:sliver, crumb|词性:noun
