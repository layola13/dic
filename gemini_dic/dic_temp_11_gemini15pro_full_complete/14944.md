azurite:/ˈæʒəˌraɪt/ | n. | 蓝铜矿 | A bright blue mineral consisting of a basic carbonate of copper, often found together with malachite. |  Azurite is often used as a pigment in paints. 蓝铜矿常被用作颜料。

Azurite and malachite are often found together. 蓝铜矿和孔雀石经常一起被发现。 | 近义词: chessylite (同义词), blue bice (颜料名称) | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可以考虑与其共生的绿色孔雀石malachite) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *azur* (azure, lapis lazuli), from Arabic *lāzaward* (lapis lazuli), ultimately from Persian *lāžaward* (lapis lazuli). The ending *-ite* indicates a mineral.
