accumulation:/əˌkjuːmjəˈleɪʃn/ | n. |积累，积聚，堆积|The process of gradually gathering or acquiring something, or the result of this process.  The accumulation of wealth/snow/evidence|例句：The accumulation of snow caused the roof to collapse. 积雪导致屋顶坍塌。


例句：The accumulation of wealth takes time and effort.  积累财富需要时间和努力。


近义词：collection, gathering, amassing, accretion, buildup|反义词:dissipation, reduction, depletion|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin accumulationem, accusative singular of accumulatio, from Latin accumulare "to heap up," from ad "to" + cumulus "heap."
