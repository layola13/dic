overfunded:/ˌoʊvərˈfʌndɪd/ | adj. | 资金过剩的，过度注资的 | Having received more funding than is needed or can be effectively used. | The project was overfunded, so they had money left over to invest elsewhere. 这个项目资金过剩，所以他们有多余的钱可以投资到其他地方。| Example: The charity was overfunded after their successful fundraising campaign.  这个慈善机构在成功的募款活动后资金过剩。| Synonyms: overcapitalized, excessively funded | Antonyms: underfunded, undercapitalized | Adjective

overfunded:/ˌoʊvərˈfʌndɪd/ | adj. | 资金过剩的，过度注资的 |  Having more money than is needed. |  The overfunded program was able to expand its services. 这个资金过剩的项目得以扩展其服务。| Example: The overfunded school district could afford to hire more teachers. 这个资金充裕的学区能够负担得起雇佣更多老师的费用。| Synonyms: well-funded, richly endowed | Antonyms: underfunded, impoverished | Adjective
