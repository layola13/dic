deadeningly:/ˈdɛdnɪŋli/| adv.|沉闷地，麻木地，令人沮丧地|Making something less lively or intense; in a way that reduces feeling or responsiveness.|The silence in the room was deadeningly oppressive.  房间里的寂静令人窒息。|近义词：numbingly, dully, monotonously, oppressively|反义词: excitingly, stimulatingly, vibrantly|词性:Adverb

Etymology:
From "deadening" + "-ly". "Deadening" comes from the verb "deaden," which in turn derives from "dead."  The suffix "-ly" is used to form adverbs.
