jocundness:/ˈdʒɒkəndnəs/ | n. | 欢乐，愉快，高兴 | The state of being lighthearted and cheerful; merriment. | Her jocundness was infectious, spreading smiles throughout the room. 她的快乐很有感染力，让整个房间的人都笑了。|  The children's jocundness filled the house with laughter. 孩子们的欢笑声充满了整个房子。 | 近义词：cheerfulness, mirth, gaiety, merriment | 反义词：gloom, sadness, melancholy | 词性: noun

词源:
From Middle English *jocundnes, equivalent to jocund +‎ -ness.  Jocund itself comes from Old French jocond, from Latin iucundus (“pleasant, agreeable”), from iucundē (“pleasantly”), perhaps related to iuvare (“to help, delight”).
