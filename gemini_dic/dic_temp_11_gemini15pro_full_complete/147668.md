overpositiveness:/ˌoʊvərˈpɑːzɪtɪvnəs/| n.|过度积极；过分乐观|An excessive degree of positivity or optimism, often unrealistic or unfounded.|His overpositiveness about the project seemed naive given the numerous challenges.|近义词：overoptimism, excessive positivity|反义词：negativity, pessimism|词性:名词

Etymology:
"Over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "positive" (adjective meaning "characterized by or displaying certainty, acceptance, or affirmation") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives).
