discos:/ˈdɪskəʊz/ (UK), /ˈdɪskoʊz/ (US)| n.|迪斯科舞会；迪斯科音乐|Plural form of disco.  A disco is a social gathering for dancing, especially to recorded popular music, or a place where such gatherings are held. It can also refer to the music itself, typically characterized by a strong, regular rhythm and electronic instruments.|We went to a disco last night. 我们昨晚去了迪斯科舞厅。|The 70s were the heyday of disco music. 70年代是迪斯科音乐的鼎盛时期。|Synonyms: nightclub, dance club, party|Antonyms: silence, quiet|Noun
