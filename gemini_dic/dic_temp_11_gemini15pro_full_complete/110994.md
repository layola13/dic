liquidness:/ˈlɪkwɪdnəs/| n.| 流动性；液态；现金支付能力 |The liquidness of the market allows for quick buying and selling of assets.  这个市场的流动性允许快速买卖资产。|近义词：liquidity, fluidity, solvency |反义词: illiquidity, solidity, insolvency | noun

Etymology: From liquid + -ness.  "liquid" comes from Late Middle English: from late Latin liquidus ‘flowing, clear’, from Latin liqueo ‘I melt, become liquid’, from the base of liquere ‘be fluid’.
