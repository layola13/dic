misbehave: /ˌmɪsbɪˈheɪv/
verb
行为不端；举止失礼
to act badly or improperly
The children misbehaved in the classroom. 孩子们在教室里行为不端。
He misbehaved at the party and was asked to leave. 他在聚会上举止失当，被要求离开。
synonyms: act up, misconduct oneself
antonyms: behave, conduct oneself well
词性：动词
