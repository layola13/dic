electrolysis:/ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒlɪsɪs/ /ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑːlɪsɪs/| n. |电解|The process of decomposing a chemical compound into its elements by passing a direct electric current through it.|Electrolysis is used to produce aluminum from bauxite.  电解用于从铝土矿中提取铝。|synonyms: decomposition, breakdown|antonyms: synthesis, combination|noun
