jobbing:/ˈdʒɒbɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈdʒɑːbɪŋ/ (US)| adj. n.|零工的，做零活的；零活，散工|Doing individual pieces of work, especially small or temporary ones.|

例句：
He makes a living by doing jobbing gardening work.
他靠做零星的园艺工作谋生。

例句：
She picked up some jobbing work while she was looking for a permanent position.
她在寻找固定职位的同时，接了一些零工。


近义词：freelance, casual, temporary|
反义词：permanent, full-time|
