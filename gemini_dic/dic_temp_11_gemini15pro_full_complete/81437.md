graticule: /ˈɡrætɪkjuːl/ | n. | 标线, 格网, 十字线 | A network of fine lines constituting the coordinates of a map or other geographical representation.  A grid or network of lines, especially on a map, chart, or other visual representation, used for reference and measurement. | The map's graticule shows lines of latitude and longitude.  地图上的标线显示经纬线。 | synonyms: grid, mesh, network, reticle | antonyms: blank, clear | noun

graticule: /ˈɡrætɪkjuːl/ | n. | 刻度盘, 标尺 |  A graduated scale on an optical instrument. | The graticule in the telescope helped to pinpoint the star's location. 望远镜中的刻度盘帮助精确定位了恒星的位置。| synonyms: scale, calibration | antonyms: N/A | noun
