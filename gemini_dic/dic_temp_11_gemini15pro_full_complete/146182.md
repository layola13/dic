overexcites:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈsaɪts/| v. |过度兴奋，使…过度兴奋|To stimulate or excite someone excessively, causing them to become agitated or overly enthusiastic.| The loud music and flashing lights overexcited the children.  喧闹的音乐和闪烁的灯光让孩子们过度兴奋。| The sugar overexcited the children, making them hyperactive. 糖让孩子们过度兴奋，使他们异常活跃。|近义词：overstimulate, agitate, inflame|反义词：calm, soothe, pacify|词性:动词

词源: over- + excite.  "Over-"  作为前缀表示 "过度"，"过分"，"超过"。 "Excite" 来自拉丁语 "excitare," 意思是 "唤醒," "激起."
