cadge:/kædʒ/
verb
乞讨，索取
to ask for or obtain something from someone, often by taking advantage of their generosity or by persuading them that you need it
He's always cadging cigarettes off his friends. 他总是向朋友讨要香烟。
近义词：beg, sponge, scrounge
反义词：give, provide, donate
词性：v.

cadge:/kædʒ/
noun
乞讨，索取（行为）
the act of cadging
He makes a cadge of a few dollars whenever he can. 他一有机会就乞讨几美元。
近义词：begging, sponging, scrounging
反义词：giving, providing, donating
词性：n.


ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain; perhaps related to earlier cadge 'carry burdens,' 'peddle,' of unknown origin.
