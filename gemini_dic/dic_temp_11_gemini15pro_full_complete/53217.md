directing:/dɪˈrɛktɪŋ/
verb|指导，导演，指示|the activity of telling people what to do or how to do something, especially in a film, play, or show|The film was finished last year but they’re still directing the music.|近义词：managing, leading, guiding, overseeing, supervising, coordinating, conducting, orchestrating, handling |反义词：following, obeying|v.
directing:/dɪˈrɛktɪŋ/
adjective|指挥的，管理的|controlling or guiding something|He has a very directing personality.|近义词：controlling, managing, leading, guiding|反义词：submissive, passive|adj.
