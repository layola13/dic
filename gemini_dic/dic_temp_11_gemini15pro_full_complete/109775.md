levitating:/ˈlɛvɪteɪtɪŋ/

adjective|悬浮的;飘浮的|suspended in the air, as if by magic|The levitating train uses magnets.  磁悬浮列车使用磁铁。|floating, hovering, drifting|grounded, landed
