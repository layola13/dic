holostomatous:/ˌhɒləˈstɒmətəs/ /ˌhoʊləˈstɑːmətəs/| adj.|全口式的，全缘的|Having a mouth that is not interrupted by a beak, as in certain insect larvae.  Characterized by having an undivided mouth opening; specifically (Zool.), having the mouth formed for sucking, as in certain dipterous larvae.|The holostomatous larva has a simple, sucking mouthpart.|近义词：entire, complete|反义词：merostomous, schistostomatous|词性:形容词
