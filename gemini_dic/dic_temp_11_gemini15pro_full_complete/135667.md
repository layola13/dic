nonprehensile:/ˌnɒnpriˈhɛnsaɪl/ | adj. | 非抓握性的；不能抓握的 | Not adapted for seizing or grasping, especially by wrapping around. |  The horse's lips are nonprehensile, unlike an elephant's trunk.  马的嘴唇不像大象的鼻子那样具有抓握能力。| The panda's front paws are nonprehensile, making it difficult for them to climb certain trees. 熊猫的前爪不能抓握，这使得它们很难爬上某些树。 | 近义词: unable to grasp, non-grasping | 反义词: prehensile, grasping | 词性: adjective
