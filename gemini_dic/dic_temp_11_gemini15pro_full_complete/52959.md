dinghy: /ˈdɪŋi/ (US), /ˈdɪŋɡi/ (UK) | n. | 小艇，橡皮艇 | A small open boat, often carried or towed by a larger vessel. |例句：
The family explored the coastline in their little dinghy.  这家人乘坐他们的小艇探索了海岸线。

They rowed ashore in a dinghy. 他们乘小艇划到岸边。

|近义词：rowboat, inflatable boat, tender |反义词: ship, yacht |词性: noun
