ascendible:/əˈsɛndəbl/
adjective
可攀登的，可上升的
capable of being ascended; climbable
例句：The mountain is ascendible, but it's a challenging climb. 这座山可以攀登，但这是一个具有挑战性的攀登。

例句：The stairs were steep but ascendible. 楼梯很陡，但可以爬上去。

近义词：climbable, scalable, mountable, surmountable
反义词：inascendible, unclimbable, impassable
词性：adjective


词源：From ascend (verb) + -ible (suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being").  Ascend comes from Latin *ascendere*, from ad- ("to, towards") + scandere ("to climb").
