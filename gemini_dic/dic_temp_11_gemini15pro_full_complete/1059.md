accentually:/ækˈsɛntʃuəli/
adverb
重音地；着重地
With emphasis on particular syllables or words; rhythmically.
She spoke accentually, emphasizing certain words for dramatic effect. 她讲话时注重重音，强调某些词语以达到戏剧化的效果。
Near Synonyms: rhythmically, emphatically, metrically, prosodically
Antonyms: monotonously, dully, flatly
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From accent + -ually (suffix forming adverbs).  "Accent" ultimately derives from Latin accentus, meaning "tone" or "song added to speech."
