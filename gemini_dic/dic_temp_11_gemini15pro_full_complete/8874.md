antifoam:/ˈæntiˌfəʊm/ (UK) /ˈæntiˌfoʊm/ (US)| n.|消泡剂，抗泡剂|A substance that reduces or prevents the formation of foam.|The paint contains an antifoam to prevent bubbling.  油漆中含有消泡剂以防止起泡。|
Antifoam agents are essential in many industrial processes.  消泡剂在许多工业过程中至关重要。|
Synonyms: defoamer, foam inhibitor|Antonyms: foaming agent, surfactant|noun
