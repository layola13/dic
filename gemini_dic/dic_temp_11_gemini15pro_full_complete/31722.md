cheap:/tʃiːp/| adj.|便宜的，低劣的|low in price; of poor quality|例句：The dress was cheap but stylish. 这件连衣裙很便宜但很时尚。|近义词：inexpensive, affordable, bargain, economical|反义词：expensive, costly, dear, pricey|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old English *cēap, from Proto-Germanic *kaupō (“bargain, business, trade”). Cognate with Dutch koop (“bargain”), German Kauf (“purchase”). Originally meant "bargain," and later acquired the sense of "low in price."
