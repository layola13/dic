farrago:/fəˈrɑːɡoʊ/ (US) /fəˈreɪɡəʊ/ (UK)| n. | 混杂，杂烩；大杂烩 | A confused mixture; a medley. | The movie was a farrago of clichés and predictable plot twists. 这部电影是陈词滥调和可预测的情节转折的大杂烩。| synonyms: mixture, medley, hodgepodge, jumble, mishmash, potpourri, gallimaufry | antonyms: order, uniformity, consistency | noun

farrago:/fəˈrɑːɡoʊ/ (US) /fəˈreɪɡəʊ/ (UK)| n. | 混乱，紊乱 | A state of confusion or disorder. | The political landscape was a farrago of conflicting ideologies. 政治格局是一片相互冲突的意识形态的混乱。| synonyms: chaos, disorder, confusion, muddle | antonyms: order, organization, harmony | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *farragō* "mixed fodder," from *farr-* "spelt" (a type of grain).
