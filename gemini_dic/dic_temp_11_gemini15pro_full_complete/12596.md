assail:/əˈseɪl/ /əˈseɪl/| vt.|攻击，袭击，抨击|To attack violently, either physically or verbally; to trouble or afflict. |The enemy troops assailed our fortress at dawn. 敌军在黎明时分袭击了我们的要塞。

The critics assailed the new movie with harsh reviews.  评论家们对这部新电影进行了严厉的抨击。|近义词：attack, assault, criticize, lambaste|反义词：defend, protect, praise, commend|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *asaillir*, from Latin *assilire* "to leap at, jump upon," from *ad* "to, at" + *salire* "to leap."
