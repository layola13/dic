daft:/dɑːft/
adj.
愚蠢的，傻的
Silly, foolish, or stupid.
例句：That was a daft thing to do. 那是一件愚蠢的事情。
例句：It was a pretty daft idea in the first place. 这主意一开始就相当愚蠢。

近义词：silly, foolish, stupid, absurd, inane
反义词：sensible, wise, intelligent, clever

词性: 形容词
