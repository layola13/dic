metage:/ˈmɛtɪdʒ/| n.|计量；度量；衡量（尤指谷物）；计量费|The metage of grain was carefully monitored. 谷物的计量受到仔细监控。|Synonyms: measurement, gauging, mensuration|Antonyms: estimation (rough estimate), guess|noun

Etymology: From Old French *metage*, from *meter* "to measure". Ultimately from Latin *metiri*.
