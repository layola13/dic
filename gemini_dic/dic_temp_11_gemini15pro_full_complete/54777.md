dispense:/dɪˈspɛns/ | v. | 分配，分发；提供；免除；配（药）| to distribute or provide something; to administer something, especially medication; to exempt or release someone from a duty or obligation | The machine dispenses snacks and drinks.  这台机器分发零食和饮料。| synonyms: distribute, allocate, administer, provide, furnish, issue, dole out | antonyms: withhold, retain, collect, gather | verb

dispense with:/dɪˈspɛns wɪð/ | v. | 免除；摒弃 | to do without; to forgo |  Let's dispense with the formalities.  让我们免去客套。| synonyms: forgo, do without, omit, eliminate, waive | antonyms:  include, retain, keep, require | verb
