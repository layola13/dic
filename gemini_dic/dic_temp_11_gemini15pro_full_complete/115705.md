marginated:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
边缘化的；记在页边的；有边的
Having a margin; set or treated as marginal; relegated to the fringes of society or a group.
The marginated community felt excluded from mainstream society.  边缘化的社区感到被排除在主流社会之外。
The handwritten notes were marginated in the textbook.  手写的笔记记在课本的页边。
marginalized, peripheral, excluded, sidelined
central, included, mainstream
词性: 形容词
