overbrags: /ˌoʊvərˈbræɡz/ | v. | 过分吹嘘，夸大其词 | To boast or brag excessively, often to an irritating degree. | He constantly overbrags about his accomplishments, making it difficult to take him seriously. 他总是过分吹嘘自己的成就，让人很难认真对待他。|  She overbrags about her children's achievements, which annoys the other parents. 她总是夸大其词地吹嘘她孩子的成就，这让其他家长很恼火。 | 近义词: boasts, brags, shows off | 反义词: downplays, understates | 词性: 动词

词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "brag" (verb meaning "to boast").  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "brag," indicating excessive boasting.
