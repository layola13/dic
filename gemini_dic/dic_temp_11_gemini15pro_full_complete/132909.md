nonelicited: /ˌnɒnɪˈlɪsɪtɪd/ /ˌnɑːnɪˈlɪsɪtɪd/
adjective
非诱发的；非引出的；自发的
Not elicited by an external stimulus or prompting.  Spontaneous or unprompted.
Her nonelicited laughter filled the room. 她自发的笑声充满了整个房间。
The child's nonelicited confession surprised everyone.  孩子未经诱导的坦白令所有人惊讶。
synonym: spontaneous, unprompted, voluntary, unforced
antonym: elicited, prompted, induced, evoked
词性: adjective
