leopards: /ˈlepərdz/ (US) /ˈlepədz/ (UK)| n. |豹子|A large, spotted cat found in Africa and Asia, known for its agility and camouflage.|例句：Leopards are skilled hunters. 豹子是熟练的猎手。

例句：The leopards stalked their prey through the tall grass. 豹子在高高的草丛中跟踪猎物。


近义词：panther, jaguar |反义词: None|词性: noun
