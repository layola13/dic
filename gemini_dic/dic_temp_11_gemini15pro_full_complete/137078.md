nonstructurally:/ˌnɒnˈstrʌktʃərəli/ | adv. | 非结构地 |  In a way that does not affect the structure or framework of something.  Not related to the load-bearing elements of a building or other structure. | The building was nonstructurally damaged by the earthquake.  地震对建筑物造成了非结构性破坏。 |  The wind caused nonstructural damage to the roof. 风对屋顶造成了非结构性损伤。 | 近义词: superficially, cosmetically | 反义词: structurally | 词性: 副词
