heresies:/ˈherəsiz/| n.|异端邪说；异教；信奉邪说|An opinion or belief that is the opposite of or against what is the official or popular opinion, or an action that shows such opinions or beliefs.|Many people were burned at the stake for heresy. 很多人因为信奉异端邪说而被火刑处死。|近义词：blasphemy, dissent, heterodoxy, unbelief|反义词：orthodoxy, belief|词性:名词

词源:
From Old French heresie, from Latin haeresis, from Ancient Greek αἵρεσις (haíresis, “choice, sect, school of thought”), from αἱρέομαι (hairéomai, “I choose, prefer”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂eyr- (“to grasp, choose”).
