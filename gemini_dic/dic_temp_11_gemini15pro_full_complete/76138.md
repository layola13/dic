fusarole: /ˈfjuːzəˌroʊl/ | n. | (考古学)纺轮; | A weighted disc or ball attached to a spindle to increase its momentum in spinning, often found in archaeological contexts. | Archaeologists discovered several clay fusaroles at the dig site. 考古学家在挖掘现场发现了几个陶土纺轮。| Synonym: spindle whorl | Antonym: N/A | Noun
