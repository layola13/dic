hieroglyphical:/ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪkəl/ | adj. | 象形文字的；难以辨认的 | Relating to hieroglyphs; obscure and difficult to decipher. |  The ancient Egyptians used a hieroglyphical writing system. 古埃及人使用象形文字书写系统。|  The professor's handwriting was so hieroglyphical that no one could read it.  教授的笔迹潦草得像象形文字，谁也看不懂。 | synonymous:  symbolic, cryptic, indecipherable | antonymous: clear, legible | adjective

hieroglyphic:/ˈhaɪərəˌɡlɪfɪk/ | n. | 象形文字 | A picture or symbol used in hieroglyphic writing. | The Rosetta Stone helped scholars decipher Egyptian hieroglyphics. 罗塞塔石碑帮助学者们破译了埃及象形文字。|  He studied the hieroglyphics on the tomb walls. 他研究了墓墙上的象形文字。|  synonymous: symbol, ideogram | antonymous: letter, alphabet | noun
