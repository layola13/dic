declaimer:/dɪˈkleɪmər/
n.|朗诵者，宣称者，演讲者|A person who recites a speech, poem, etc., especially in a dramatic or rhetorical manner.  A person who makes a formal statement renouncing or disavowing something.|The young declaimer won first prize in the speech competition.  这位年轻的朗诵者在演讲比赛中获得了一等奖。|speaker, orator, reciter|denier, repudiator|noun

declaim:/dɪˈkleɪm/
v.|（尤指慷慨激昂地）演说，朗诵，宣称|To speak or recite something in a dramatic or rhetorical manner. To make a formal statement renouncing or disavowing something.|He declaimed against the injustices of the system. 他慷慨激昂地抨击该制度的不公正。|speak, recite, pronounce|whisper, mutter|verb
