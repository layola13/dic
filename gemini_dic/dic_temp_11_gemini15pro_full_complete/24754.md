brookdale:/ˈbrʊkdeɪl/| n. |溪谷，溪谷住宅区|A valley with a brook running through it; often used in place names for residential areas.|We moved to a quiet brookdale on the outskirts of town. 我们搬到了城郊一个安静的溪谷住宅区。|The Brookdale retirement community offers a variety of amenities.  布鲁克戴尔退休社区提供各种便利设施。|近义词：valley, glen, dale|反义词:mountain, hill|词性:名词

Etymology:
From brook (n.) + dale (n.).  "Brook" comes from Old English "brōc," meaning "stream."  "Dale" comes from Old English "dæl," meaning "valley."
