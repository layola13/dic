grandier:/ˈɡrændiər/ | adj. | 更宏伟的，更堂皇的 | comparative adjective of "grand," meaning more magnificent or imposing | The new cathedral is grandier than the old one. 新的大教堂比旧的更宏伟。| synonyms: more magnificent, more imposing, grander, more stately, more majestic | antonyms: less grand, simpler, humbler, plainer, smaller | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *grandior, *graundior, from Old French grandior, grandior, comparative of grand (“large, great”), from Latin grandis (“large, great”).
