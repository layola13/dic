overfloridly:/ˌoʊvərˈflɒrɪdli/| adv.| 过分华丽地，过于修饰地| excessively ornate or flowery in style; too elaborate or embellished |  His prose was overfloridly descriptive, making the simple story seem convoluted.  他过于华丽的描述使得这个简单的故事显得错综复杂。

The politician's overfloridly patriotic speech felt insincere. 这位政客过于华丽的爱国演讲让人感觉不真诚。 | 近义词：ornately, elaborately, excessively, flamboyantly | 反义词：simply, plainly, subtly, understatedly | 词性: 副词
