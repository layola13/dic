flushing:/ˈflʌʃɪŋ/| n. adj.|脸红，冲洗；潮红；|The act of becoming red in the face, typically as a result of strong emotions such as embarrassment, anger, or excitement. Also, the act of cleaning something by causing water to pass through it.|She felt a flushing in her cheeks when he complimented her. 当他赞美她时，她感到脸颊一阵发热。|Near synonyms: reddening, blushing, rosiness|Near antonyms: paleness, blanching|noun

flushing:/ˈflʌʃɪŋ/| n. adj.|冲洗，清除|The act of cleaning something by causing a liquid, typically water, to pass through it.|The flushing of the toilet is important for hygiene.  冲洗厕所对卫生很重要。|Near synonyms: rinsing, cleansing, purging|Near antonyms: blocking, clogging|noun
