navigation-weirs:/ˌnævɪˈɡeɪʃən wɪəz/| n.|航行堰, 导航堰|A structure built across a river to regulate the flow of water and improve conditions for navigation.  These weirs often create a deeper channel suitable for boats.|The navigation-weirs ensured safe passage for barges along the canal.|Synonyms: navigation dams, lock and dam|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps natural riverbed)|noun
