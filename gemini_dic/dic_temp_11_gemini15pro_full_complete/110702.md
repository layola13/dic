linearly:/ˈlɪniərli/| adv.|线性地，成直线地|In a linear manner; along a straight line.  Progressing uniformly through time.|The graph shows the data increasing linearly.|近义词：straightly, directly, proportionally|反义词:nonlinearly, exponentially|词性:副词
Etymology: From Latin *linearis* "of or pertaining to a line," from *linea* "line, string."
