in-line-with:/ɪn ˈlaɪn wɪð/ | prep. |与...一致，符合|Being in line with something means being in agreement or conformity with it. in-line:/ˌɪnˈlaɪn/ | adj. |在线的，联机的|Functioning or available while connected to a central system or network.|The in-line editing feature saves time. 在线编辑功能节省时间。|synonyms: online, connected|antonyms: offline, disconnected|adjective
