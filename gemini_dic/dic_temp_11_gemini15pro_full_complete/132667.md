nondisparate:/ˌnɒndɪˈspærət/ | adj. |无差异的，平等的 | Not disparate; not marked by differences; similar or equal. |例句：The company strives to create a nondisparate work environment for all employees. 公司努力为所有员工创造一个无差异的工作环境。

例句：The study showed nondisparate outcomes for both groups. 研究表明两组的结果没有差异。|近义词：equal, equivalent, uniform, similar, alike |反义词: disparate, different, unequal, dissimilar |词性:形容词


Etymology:
non- + disparate.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "disparate" comes from the Latin word "disparatus," meaning "unprepared" or "unequal."
