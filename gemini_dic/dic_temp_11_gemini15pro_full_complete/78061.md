geocentric:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈsɛntrɪk/ | adj. |地心的，以地球为中心的| Relating to, or measured from, the Earth's center; having or representing the Earth as a center. |例句：The ancient Greeks held a geocentric view of the universe. 古希腊人持有地心说的宇宙观。|例句：Geocentric coordinates are used to pinpoint locations on Earth. 地心坐标用于精确定位地球上的位置。| 近义词: earth-centered | 反义词: heliocentric |词性:形容词
