disagreeance:/ˌdɪsəˈɡriːəns/
n.
不一致，分歧
Lack of agreement or harmony; discord.
There was considerable disagreeance among the committee members regarding the proposal.  委员会成员对该提案存在相当大的分歧。
近义词:disagreement, discord, dissension, variance
反义词:agreement, concord, harmony, accord
词性:名词


词源:
From disagree + -ance.  Ultimately from the Latin dis- (not) + aggregare (to flock together).
