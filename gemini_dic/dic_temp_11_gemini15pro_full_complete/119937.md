milksop:/ˈmɪlksɒp/ (UK) /ˈmɪlksɑːp/ (US)| n.|懦夫，胆小鬼，没有男子气概的人|A person, especially a man, who is indecisive and lacks courage. |He was too much of a milksop to stand up to the bullies. 他太懦弱了，不敢反抗那些恶霸。|近义词:coward, wimp, weakling|反义词:hero, brave, courageous|词性:名词

Etymology:
From milk + sop.  "Sop" originally referred to a piece of bread soaked in milk or other liquid.  The image evoked is of someone who is soft or weak, like bread soaked in milk.
