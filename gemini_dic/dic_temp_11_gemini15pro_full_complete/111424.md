lizard-like:/ˈlɪzədˌlaɪk/| adj.|像蜥蜴的，蜥蜴似的|Resembling a lizard in appearance, movement, or behavior.|例句：The gecko has lizard-like skin. 壁虎有像蜥蜴一样的皮肤。

例句：The creature moved with a lizard-like flick of its tongue.  这个生物以像蜥蜴一样快速地弹动舌头的方式移动。|近义词：reptilian, saurian|反义词:mammalian|词性:形容词
