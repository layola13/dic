horsewood:/ˈhɔːrsˌwʊd/| n. |马蹄树; 木瓜红树|A type of tree, specifically *Cornus florida*, also known as flowering dogwood. It's characterized by its distinctive bracts (often mistaken for petals) that surround the small, inconspicuous flowers. |The horsewood in our yard is in full bloom. 我们院子里的马蹄树盛开了。| The horsewood's bracts are a beautiful shade of pink. 马蹄树的苞片是美丽的粉红色。 |Synonyms: flowering dogwood, Cornus florida| Antonyms: N/A | Noun
