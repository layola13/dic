glorying:/ˈɡlɔːriɪŋ/ | v. | 欣喜；得意；自豪 | rejoicing or taking great pride in something |  He was glorying in his victory. 他沉浸在胜利的喜悦中。 | synonyms: reveling, boasting, exulting | antonyms: lamenting, grieving, mourning | verb

glorying:/ˈɡlɔːriɪŋ/ | adj. (rare) |  荣耀的; 辉煌的 | Expressing or radiating glory. |  The glorying sun rose above the horizon. 辉煌的太阳从地平线上升起。 | synonyms: glorious, radiant, splendid | antonyms: dull, lackluster, dim | adjective
