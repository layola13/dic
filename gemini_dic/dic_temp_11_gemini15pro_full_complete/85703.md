haversian:/həˈvɜːrʒən/
adjective
哈弗氏的；哈弗斯氏的
Relating to or denoting the canals in bone tissue that contain blood vessels and nerves.
The haversian canals are a key component of bone structure.  哈弗斯管是骨骼结构的关键组成部分。
Osteocytes are connected to each other and the haversian canal through canaliculi. 骨细胞通过骨小管相互连接，并与哈弗斯管相连。
近义词: bone, osseous
反义词:  (No direct antonym for an anatomical term)
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Clopton Havers (1657–1702), English physician + -ian.
