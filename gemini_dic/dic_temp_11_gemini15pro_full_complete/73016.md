footbrakes: /ˈfʊtbreɪks/ | n. | 脚刹; 脚制动器 | A brake operated by a pedal, usually in a car or other vehicle. | The driver slammed on the footbrakes to avoid hitting the deer. 司机猛踩脚刹，避免撞到鹿。| The car's footbrakes failed, causing a serious accident. 汽车的脚刹失灵，导致了一起严重事故。 | 近义词：brakes | 反义词：accelerator | 词性: noun
