consociational:/kənˌsoʊʃiˈeɪʃənəl/| adj.|联合制的，协商性的|Relating to a power-sharing arrangement in a politically divided society, typically involving guaranteed group representation.|例句：The country adopted a consociational democracy to ensure the peaceful coexistence of different ethnic groups. 这个国家采取了联合民主制以确保不同族群的和平共处。

例句：Consociational arrangements can help prevent civil conflict in deeply divided societies. 协商性安排有助于防止严重分裂的社会发生内战。|近义词：power-sharing, consensual, cooperative|反义词：majoritarian, adversarial, divisive|词性：形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *consociatio*, from *consociare* "to associate, unite," from *con-* "together" + *sociare* "to join, associate," from *socius* "companion."
