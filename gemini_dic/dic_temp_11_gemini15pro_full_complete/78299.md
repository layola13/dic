germless:/ˈdʒɜːrmləs/| adj.|无菌的，无病菌的|Free from germs; sterile.|The operating room must be kept germless. 手术室必须保持无菌状态。|近义词：sterile, aseptic, antiseptic|反义词：germy, contaminated, infected|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From germ + -less.  "Germ" in this context originally referred to a sprout or bud, later expanding to mean the origin of something, including disease.  The suffix "-less" indicates a lack or absence of something.
