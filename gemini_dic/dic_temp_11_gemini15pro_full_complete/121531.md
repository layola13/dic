mismanager:/ˌmɪsˈmænɪdʒər/| n.|管理不善者，经营不善者|A person who manages something badly or incompetently. |The company's downfall was largely due to the decisions of the mismanager.  这家公司的倒闭很大程度上是由于管理不善者的决策造成的。|Synonyms: incompetent manager, bad manager, ineffective manager|Antonyms: good manager, competent manager, effective manager|noun

mismanage:/ˌmɪsˈmænɪdʒ/| vt.|管理不善，处理不当|To manage something badly or incompetently. |He mismanaged the project and it ended up costing the company a fortune. 他对这个项目管理不善，最终使公司损失惨重。|Synonyms: mishandle, misdirect, bungle|Antonyms: manage well, handle effectively, direct properly|verb
