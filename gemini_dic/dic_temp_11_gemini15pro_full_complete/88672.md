hoatzin:/ˈhwɑːtsɪn/ /ˈwɑːtsɪn/| n. |麝雉|A South American bird (Opisthocomus hoazin) noted for the clawed wings of its young.|例句：The hoatzin is a unique bird found in the Amazon basin. 麝雉是一种在亚马逊河流域发现的独特鸟类。

例句：The young hoatzin uses its claws to climb trees. 幼年麝雉用爪子爬树。|近义词：stinkbird (due to its odor)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

etymology: From Carib *huácita.
