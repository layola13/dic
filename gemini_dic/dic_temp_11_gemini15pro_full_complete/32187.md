chiastic:/kaɪˈæstɪk/| adj.|交错的，交错配列法的|Relating to or arranged in the form of a chiasmus; a rhetorical or literary figure in which words, grammatical constructions, or concepts are repeated in reverse order, in the same or a modified form.  This creates a "mirrored" effect.|The poet employed a chiastic structure, mirroring the first half of the poem in the second.  诗人采用了交错配列结构，将诗的前半部分镜像映射到后半部分。|Synonyms:  inverted, mirrored, symmetrical|Antonyms:  linear, sequential|adjective
