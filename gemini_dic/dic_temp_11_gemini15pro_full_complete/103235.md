jettisoning:/ˈdʒɛtɪsənɪŋ/ | n. | 抛弃，丢弃 | The act of discarding or casting off something, especially that which is unwanted or burdensome.  | The jettisoning of excess baggage saved the hot air balloon. 热气球抛弃了多余的行李才得以幸免。 | 近义词: discarding, dumping, abandoning | 反义词: keeping, retaining, preserving | 词性: 名词 (Gerund/Verbal Noun) derived from the verb "jettison"

jettison:/ˈdʒɛtɪsən/ | vt. | 抛弃，丢弃 | To throw or drop (something) from an aircraft or ship. |  The crew jettisoned the cargo to lighten the plane. 机组人员抛弃了货物以减轻飞机的重量。 | 近义词: discard, dump, abandon | 反义词: keep, retain, preserve | 词性: 动词


词源: From Old French "getaison" meaning "act of throwing," from "geter" meaning "to throw," ultimately from Latin "jactare" meaning "to throw frequently."
