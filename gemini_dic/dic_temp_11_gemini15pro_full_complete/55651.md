dithery:/ˈdɪðəri/ (US), /ˈdɪðəri/ (UK)| adj.|紧张不安的，犹豫不决的|Nervous and uncertain; unable to make decisions.|She was dithery about accepting the job offer. 她对是否接受这份工作犹豫不决。|例句：He was dithery and indecisive about which course to choose. 他对选择哪门课程感到紧张不安，犹豫不决。|近义词：hesitant, indecisive, uncertain, wavering, vacillating|反义词： decisive, resolute, confident|词性:形容词
