dimensionalities:/ˌdɪmɛnʃəˈnælɪtiz/| n. |维度（复数）|The quality of having multiple dimensions; the range of dimensions.  It refers to the number of coordinates needed to specify a point within a given space.|Researchers are exploring the dimensionalities of virtual reality experiences.|Synonyms: dimensions, measures, extents|Antonyms: limitation, confinement|noun
