dog: /dɒɡ/ (UK) /dɔːɡ/ (US) | n. | 狗 | A domesticated carnivorous mammal that typically has a long snout, an acute sense of smell, non-retractable claws, and a barking, howling, or whining voice. |  My dog loves to play fetch. 我的狗喜欢玩取物游戏。

|  She adopted a dog from the shelter. 她从收容所领养了一只狗。 | 近义词: canine, hound, pup, puppy | 反义词: cat (as a common contrasting pet) | noun
