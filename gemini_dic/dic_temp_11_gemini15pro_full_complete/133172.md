nonevadingly:/nɒnɪˈveɪdɪŋli/
adv.
直截了当地，毫不回避地
directly and openly, without evasion

He answered all the questions nonevadingly.
他直截了当地回答了所有问题。

She spoke nonevadingly about the sensitive issue.
她毫不回避地谈论了这个敏感的问题。

Synonym:
directly, frankly, candidly, straightforwardly, openly

Antonym:
evasively, indirectly, subtly


Etymology:
nonevadingly comes from "nonevading" + "-ly."  "Nonevading" is formed from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the verb "evade" (meaning "to avoid or escape").  The suffix "-ly" turns it into an adverb.

词性: adverb (副词)
