overelaborated: /ˌoʊvərɪˈlæbəreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过于精心制作的；过于复杂的； 过于详尽的 |  Having been elaborated or explained in excessive detail; too complex or detailed. | The plot of the movie was overelaborated and confusing. 这部电影的情节过于复杂，令人困惑。 | overdone, overwrought, convoluted | simple, concise, straightforward | adjective


overelaborate: /ˌoʊvərɪˈlæbəreɪt/ | vt. | 过于详尽地阐述；过度修饰 | To elaborate or explain something in excessive detail; to make something too complex or ornate. | He overelaborated his point, losing the audience's interest. 他把自己的观点解释得过于详尽，失去了听众的兴趣。 | overdo, overwork, embellish | simplify, streamline, condense | verb
