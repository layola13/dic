humoring:/ˈhjuːmərɪŋ/| v.|迁就; 迎合; 顺应|indulging or going along with someone's mood or whims, even if you don't agree with them. |例句：I was just humoring him because I didn't want to argue. 我只是在迁就他，因为我不想争吵。

例句：She humored her grandfather by listening to his long stories. 她耐心地听着祖父的长篇大论，以顺应他的心意。|近义词：indulging, appeasing, accommodating, obliging|反义词：opposing, contradicting, defying|词性:动词
