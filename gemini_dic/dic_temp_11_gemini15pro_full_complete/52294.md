dichroous:/daɪˈkruːəs/
adjective
二色的；双色的
Exhibiting two colors, especially two complementary colors when viewed by transmitted and reflected light.
The mineral alexandrite is famously dichroous, appearing green in daylight and red under incandescent light.
近义词:dichromatic, two-colored, bicolored
反义词:monochromatic
词性:adjective

dichroous:/daɪˈkruːəs/
adjective
二向色的
Of or relating to dichroism. (Dichroism: the property of some crystals and solutions of absorbing light differently depending on the direction of polarization.)
Certain polarizing filters are dichroous, allowing only light vibrating in a particular plane to pass through.
近义词:anisotropic (related to dichroism)
反义词:isotropic (related to lack of dichroism)
词性:adjective
