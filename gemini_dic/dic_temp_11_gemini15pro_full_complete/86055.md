heaped:/hiːpt/ | adj. | 堆满的；堆积的 | piled up; filled to overflowing |  The table was heaped with food. 桌子上堆满了食物。

The vendor gave us a heaped measure of strawberries.  摊贩给了我们满满一盒草莓。


Near synonyms: piled, stacked, laden, overflowing

Antonyms:  scanty, meager, sparse

词性: adjective
