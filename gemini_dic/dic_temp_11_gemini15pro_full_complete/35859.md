cockshead:/ˈkɒkshed/ (UK) /ˈkɑːkshed/ (US)| n.|鸡冠花，傻瓜，轻浮的人|A plant with a cluster of tiny red, pink, or yellow flowers at the top of its stem, resembling a rooster's comb.  Also, a foolish or frivolous person (offensive).|例句：The cockshead in the garden was a vibrant red. 花园里的鸡冠花鲜红夺目。

例句：He acted like a cockshead, making jokes at the wrong time. 他表现得像个傻瓜，在不恰当的时候开玩笑。|近义词：fool, idiot, nitwit (for person); celosia, woolflower (for plant)|反义词:genius, sage (for person)|词性:noun
