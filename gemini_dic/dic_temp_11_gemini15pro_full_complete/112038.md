long-windedness:/ˌlɔːŋˈwɪndɪdnəs/| n.|冗长，啰嗦|The long-windedness of his speech bored the audience. 他的演讲冗长乏味，使听众感到厌烦。|Long-windedness is a common problem in academic writing. 冗长是学术写作中的一个常见问题。|近义词：verbosity, prolixity, wordiness|反义词:conciseness, brevity|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From long-winded + -ness.  Long-winded (adj.) is from "long" + "wind" (breath), implying speaking for a long time without pausing for breath.
