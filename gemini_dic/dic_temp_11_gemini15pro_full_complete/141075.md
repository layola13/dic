onomatopoeia: /ˌɒnəˌmætəˈpiːə/ (UK) /ˌɑːnəˌmætəˈpiːə/ (US)| n. |拟声词|The formation or use of words that imitate the sounds associated with the objects or actions they refer to.|The word "buzz" is an onomatopoeia for the sound a bee makes.|近义词：sound imitation, echoism|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin onomatopoeia, from Greek onomatopoiía, from onomatopoieîn 'to make or invent a name or word', from ónoma, ónomat- 'name' + -poieîn 'make'.
