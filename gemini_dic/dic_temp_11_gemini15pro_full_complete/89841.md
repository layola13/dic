hornlessness:/ˈhɔːnləsnəs/| n.|无角；没有角的状态|The hornlessness of some cattle breeds is a result of selective breeding. 一些牛品种的无角状态是选择性育种的结果。|例句：Hornlessness in cattle is often a desirable trait. 牛的无角性通常是一个理想的特征。 Hornlessness can be a genetic advantage in confined spaces.  在密闭空间中，无角是一种遗传优势。|近义词：polled (specifically for cattle/animals with horns)|反义词：horned|词性: noun
