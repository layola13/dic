junctional:/ˈdʒʌŋkʃənəl/
adjective
连接的；交界处的
relating to a junction or junctions; formed at a junction.
The junctional rhythm of the heart is abnormal. 心脏的交界性节律是异常的。
Near the junctional zone of two tissues. 靠近两个组织的连接区域
Synonyms: connecting, joining, linking
Antonyms: separating, dividing, disconnecting
词性: adjective
