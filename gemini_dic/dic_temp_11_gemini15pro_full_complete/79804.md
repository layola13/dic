glycocholate: /ˌɡlaɪkoʊˈkoʊleɪt/ | n. | 甘氨胆酸盐 | A salt of glycocholic acid, a bile acid formed by the conjugation of cholic acid with glycine.  It aids in the digestion and absorption of fats. | Bile salts such as glycocholate emulsify fats in the small intestine. 胆汁盐如甘氨胆酸盐在小肠中乳化脂肪。|  Glycocholate helps the body absorb vitamins A, D, E, and K. 甘氨胆酸盐帮助身体吸收维生素A、D、E和K。 | synonym: bile salt | antonym: None | noun
