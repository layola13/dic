一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does he do for a living? 他以什么为生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? 她正在做什么晚餐？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has he done to deserve this award? 他做了什么值得获得这个奖？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has she been studying at university? 她一直在大学里学什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? 她对这部电影说了些什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? 我来之前你做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? 她生病前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the accident? 事故发生前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major?  她转专业前一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will she wear to the party? 她会穿什么去参加聚会？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be studying next semester? 她下学期会学什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):

What will she have learned by the time she graduates? 到她毕业的时候，她将学到什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):

What will she have been studying by the time she gets her PhD? 到她获得博士学位时，她将已经学习什么多久了？


虚拟语气:
If I knew what to say, I would tell you. 如果我知道该说什么，我会告诉你。
I wish I knew what she was thinking. 我希望我知道她在想什么。

被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

疑问句:
What time is the meeting? 会议是什么时候？
What are your plans for the weekend? 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what you mean. 她不明白你的意思。
