crudes:/kruːdz/
n.
原油；天然的物质；粗制品
Unrefined petroleum or other natural resources.  Materials or substances in their natural, unprocessed state.
例句：The crudes are transported by pipeline. 原油通过管道运输。

例句：The factory processes various crudes into finished products.  这家工厂将各种原材料加工成成品。
近义词：raw materials, unrefined materials, unprocessed materials
反义词：refined products, finished goods
词性:名词

crude:/kruːd/
adj.
粗糙的；天然的；粗鲁的；未加工的
In a natural or raw state; not yet processed or refined.  Rude or offensive.
例句：The furniture was crude but functional. 这件家具做工粗糙，但很实用。

例句：He made a crude joke that offended everyone. 他讲了个粗俗的笑话，冒犯了所有人。
近义词：rough, unrefined, raw, vulgar, coarse
反义词：refined, sophisticated, polished, polite
词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *crudus, from PIE *kreu- "raw, bloody."
