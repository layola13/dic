browsed:/braʊzd/ | v. | 浏览，随意翻阅 | to look through or examine something casually or randomly | I browsed the shelves of the bookstore. 我浏览了书店的书架。|  skimmed, glanced, scanned | studied, scrutinized | verb

browsed:/braʊzd/ | adj. | （古董、书籍等）略有破损的，翻旧了的 | slightly damaged from use or handling, typically of antiques or books | a browsed copy of a first edition 一本略有破损的初版书籍 | worn, used, secondhand | new, pristine | adjective
