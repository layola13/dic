managers:/ˈmænɪdʒərz/| n.|经理，管理人员|People who direct and control an organization or group of staff.|The managers decided to implement a new marketing strategy. 经理们决定实施新的营销策略。|近义词：directors, supervisors, executives, administrators|反义词:subordinates, employees|noun

词源: From Old French *manageor, from maneggiare "to handle," especially "to handle a horse," from mano "hand"
