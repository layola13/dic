dispossessors:/dɪspəˈzɛsərz/
n.
剥夺者；驱逐者
People who dispossess others of their land or property.
The dispossessors forced the farmers off their land. 剥夺者强迫农民离开他们的土地。
The dispossessors claimed ownership of the ancestral lands. 这些强占者声称拥有祖传土地的所有权。
synonyms: usurpers, expropriators, evictors
antonyms: benefactors, donors, grantors
noun
