non-smoking: /ˌnɒn ˈsməʊkɪŋ/ (UK) /ˌnɑːn ˈsmoʊkɪŋ/ (US)
non-smoking: adj.
non-smoking: 禁烟的; 非吸烟的
non-smoking:  Not allowing or characterized by the smoking of tobacco.  It describes places where smoking is prohibited or people who do not smoke.
non-smoking: This is a non-smoking restaurant.  这是一家禁烟餐厅。
non-smoking:  She booked a non-smoking room. 她预订了一间无烟房间。
non-smoking: smoke-free, non-smoker
non-smoking: smoking
non-smoking: adjective
