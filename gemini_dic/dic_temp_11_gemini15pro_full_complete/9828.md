anything-goes:/ˈɛniˌθɪŋ ˈɡoʊz/ | adj. |  百无禁忌的；任何事情都允许的；无拘无束的 |  A situation where there are no rules or restrictions on behavior.  |  It was an anything-goes party, people were dressed in all sorts of crazy costumes.  这是一个百无禁忌的派对，人们穿着各种疯狂的服装。| synonyms: unrestrained, unrestricted, permissive, free-for-all, wide open | antonyms: restricted, controlled, regulated, conservative, formal | adjective

anything-goes:/ˈɛniˌθɪŋ ˈɡoʊz/ | n. |  放任自流；为所欲为 |  A state or atmosphere where anything is permissible. |  The club had an atmosphere of anything-goes, which attracted a diverse crowd. 这家俱乐部有一种为所欲为的氛围，吸引了形形色色的人群。| synonyms: lawlessness, anarchy, chaos, free-for-all | antonyms: order, control, regulation, discipline | noun
