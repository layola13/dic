blurriness:/ˈblɜːrɪnəs/ /ˈblɜːrinɪs/
n.
模糊； 模糊不清； 模糊度
the quality of being blurry or unclear.
The blurriness of the photo made it difficult to identify the subject. 照片的模糊使得很难辨认主体。
近义词：fuzziness, indistinctness, haziness
反义词：sharpness, clarity, distinctness
词性：名词
