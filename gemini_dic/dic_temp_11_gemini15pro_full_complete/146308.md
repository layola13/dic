overfeasting: /ˌoʊvərˈfiːstɪŋ/ | n. | 过度饮食，暴饮暴食 | The act of eating too much, especially to the point of feeling uncomfortable. | After the holidays, many people regret their overfeasting. 假期过后，许多人都后悔自己暴饮暴食了。| Synonyms: gluttony, overeating, gorging | Antonyms: dieting, fasting | noun

overfeasting: /ˌoʊvərˈfiːstɪŋ/ | v. |  暴饮暴食 |  The act of eating excessively. |  He overfeasted during the Christmas holidays and felt sick afterwards.  他在圣诞假期期间暴饮暴食，之后感到恶心。 | Synonyms: gorge, overindulge | Antonyms: abstain, fast | verb
