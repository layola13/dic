landaulet:/ˌlændɔːˈlɛt/ | n. | Landaulet | A car with a convertible top over the rear passenger compartment but a fixed roof over the driver's seat. | The queen arrived in a stately landaulet. 女王乘坐一辆豪华的landaulet抵达。| 近义词: convertible, limousine | 反义词: sedan, coupe | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *landaulet*, diminutive of *landau*, from German *Landau* (a town in Rhineland-Palatinate, Germany, known for carriage manufacturing).
