imagined:/ɪˈmædʒɪnd/ | v. | 想象，设想 |  formed a picture in the mind of something that is not present to the senses or that has not happened; supposed; guessed |  She imagined herself winning the lottery. 她想象着自己中了彩票。| Synonyms: visualized, envisioned, conceived, fantasized, supposed | Antonyms: perceived, observed, witnessed | Verb
