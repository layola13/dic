homegirls:/ˈhoʊmˌɡɜːrlz/ /ˈhəʊmˌɡɜːlz/| n.|闺蜜，女性好友（尤指非裔美国女性或拉丁裔女性之间）|A term of endearment and solidarity among close female friends, often used within African American or Latina communities.  It signifies a deep bond and shared experiences, similar to "sister" or "best friend."|例句：My homegirls and I went shopping yesterday. 我和我的闺蜜们昨天去购物了。

例句：She always has her homegirls' backs. 她总是支持她的闺蜜们。


近义词：best friends, close friends, besties, sisters|反义词:enemies, rivals|词性:名词
