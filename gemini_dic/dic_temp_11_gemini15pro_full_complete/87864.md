hiders:/ˈhaɪdərz/| n.|隐藏者；躲藏者|People who hide themselves or something else.  Those who conceal themselves or things from view.|The hiders were eventually found in the attic. 隐藏者最终在阁楼被发现。|Synonyms: concealers,  shirkers, lurkers|Antonyms: seekers, finders|noun
