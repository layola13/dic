immobilizations: /ɪˌmōbəlɪˈzeɪʃənz/
| n. | 固定；限制；不动产|The act of immobilizing something or someone, or the state of being immobilized.  The conversion of liquid assets into fixed assets; funds invested in capital assets. | The doctor recommended immobilization of the broken arm. 医生建议固定骨折的手臂。|  The company's investments in new equipment increased its immobilizations. 公司对新设备的投资增加了其固定资产。 |近义词：restrictions, constraints, fixed assets, capital assets|反义词：mobilization, movement, activity|词性: 名词
