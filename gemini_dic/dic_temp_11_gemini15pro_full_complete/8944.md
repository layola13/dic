antihistoricalness:/ˌæntiˌhɪˈstɔːrɪkəlnəs/
|n.| 反历史性，非历史性
|The quality or state of being antihistorical; opposition to or disregard of history.|
|His antihistoricalness led him to believe that the past was irrelevant.| 他反历史的观念使他认为过去无关紧要。
|antihistoricism, ahistoricity|
|historicalness|
|noun|


antihistorical:/ˌæntiˌhɪˈstɔːrɪkəl/
|adj.| 反历史的，非历史的
|Opposed to or disregarding history; not conforming to history.|
|Her antihistorical interpretation of the text ignored crucial contextual factors.| 她对文本的反历史解读忽略了关键的语境因素。
|ahistorical, unhistorical|
|historical|
|adjective|
