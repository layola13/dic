compositors:/kəmˈpɒzɪtəz/ | n. |排字工人，作曲家|A person who arranges type for printing or sets music type; a person who composes music. | The compositors worked diligently to set the type for the newspaper. 排字工人努力地为报纸排版。|  The compositors skillfully arranged the musical notes on the page.  作曲家熟练地将音符排列在页面上。 | synonyms: typesetter, typographer (for printing); composer, musician (for music) | antonyms: N/A | noun
