disputed:/dɪˈspjuːtɪd/ /dɪˈspjutɪd/ | adj. |有争议的， disputed |Being the subject of disagreement or argument; contested. |  The ownership of the island is disputed.  这个岛屿的所有权是有争议的。| Synonyms: contested, challenged, debated, controverted, argued, questioned | Antonyms: undisputed, unchallenged, agreed upon, acknowledged | adjective

disputed:/dɪˈspjuːtɪd/ /dɪˈspjutɪd/ | v. | 争论，质疑 | Argue about (something); discuss heatedly.  | They disputed the election results. 他们对选举结果提出了质疑。 | Synonyms: argue, debate, challenge, contest, question, oppose | Antonyms: agree, accept, concede, acquiesce | verb

Etymology: From Late Middle English *disputen*, from Old French *disputer*, from Latin *disputāre* (“to discuss, examine, argue”), from *dis-* (“apart, asunder”) + *putāre* (“to think, reckon, consider, suppose”).
