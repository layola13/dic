houseboy:/ˈhaʊsbɔɪ/| n. |男仆，家仆（尤指男性）|A male servant who performs domestic duties, especially in a non-Western country. This term is now considered offensive due to its colonial connotations.|He employed a houseboy to clean and cook. 他雇了一个男仆做清洁和烹饪。|servant, domestic, valet|maid, housekeeper|noun
