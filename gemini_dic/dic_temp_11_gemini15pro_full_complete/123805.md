mopingly:/ˈmoʊpɪŋli/| adv.|没精打采地，闷闷不乐地|In a way that shows you are unhappy and depressed.  She walked mopingly through the halls after failing the exam.|近义词：sadly, glumly, dejectedly, despondently|反义词：cheerfully, happily, joyfully|词性:副词

Etymology:
From mope (verb) + -ing (present participle suffix) + -ly (adverb suffix).  "Mope" originates from the Middle Dutch word "moppen" meaning "to sulk, be silent".
