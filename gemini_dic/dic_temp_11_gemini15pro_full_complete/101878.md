ischiocapsular: /ˌɪskiəʊˈkæpsjʊlər/
adjective
坐骨囊的；与坐骨和关节囊有关的
Relating to the ischium and the capsule of the hip joint.
The ischiocapsular ligament provides stability to the hip joint. 坐骨囊韧带为髋关节提供稳定性。
Near Antonyms:  extracapsular (囊外的)
Etymology: ischio- (from Greek ischion, meaning "hip") + capsular (from Latin capsula, meaning "small box, case")
