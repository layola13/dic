homonym:/ˈhɒmənɪm/ (UK) /ˈhɑːmənɪm/ (US)| n.|同形同音异义词|A word that is spelled and pronounced the same as another word but has a different meaning.|The words "right" (correct) and "right" (opposite of left) are homonyms.|近义词：homophone, homograph|反义词：(No direct antonym)|noun
