calisthenics:/ˌkælɪsˈθɛnɪks/| n. |健美操，徒手体操|Calisthenics are exercises done using only your body weight, often rhythmically.|She practices calisthenics every morning to stay fit. 她每天早上都做健美操来保持健康。|He uses calisthenics to build strength and flexibility. 他用健美操来增强力量和柔韧性。|近义词：exercises, workout, bodyweight training|反义词:inactivity, rest|名词
