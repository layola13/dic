adjutator:/ˈæʤəteɪtər/| n.|助手，协助者，副官|Someone who helps or assists another person, especially in a difficult or demanding task.  A person who acts as an assistant or helper.  Sometimes implies a subordinate role.|例句：The general's adjutator handled all the logistical details. 将军的副官处理了所有的后勤细节。

例句：She acted as an adjutator to the CEO, managing his schedule and correspondence. 她担任首席执行官的助手，管理他的日程安排和信件往来。|近义词：assistant, helper, aide, supporter|反义词:opponent, adversary|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *adiutator, from Latin adiūtāre "to help," from ad- "to" + iuvāre "to help."
