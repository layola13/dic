bcc'ed:/ˌbiː siː ˈsiːd/| v.|抄送；密送|A shortened form of "blind carbon copied," meaning to send a copy of an email to someone without the primary recipient knowing.| I bcc'ed John on the email to keep him in the loop. 我把这封邮件密送给约翰，让他随时了解情况。|Synonyms: blind copied|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "directly addressed" or "cc'ed")|verb
