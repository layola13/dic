back-out:/ˌbæk ˈaʊt/ | phrasal verb | 退出，撤出，取消（承诺或协议）| To withdraw from a commitment, promise, or agreement. |  I had to back out of the deal when I realized I couldn't afford it.  我意识到我负担不起时，我不得不退出这笔交易。

The band backed out of the concert at the last minute. 乐队在最后一刻取消了音乐会。 | 近义词：withdraw, renege, retreat | 反义词：commit, follow through | verb phrase

back-out:/ˈbækˌaʊt/ | noun | 倒车，退出（尤指车辆）| The act of reversing a vehicle or withdrawing from a difficult situation. | The car's back-out was blocked by another vehicle. 这辆车的倒车被另一辆车挡住了。

There was no easy back-out from the agreement. 该协议没有简单的退出方法。| 近义词：reversal, withdrawal, retreat | 反义词：entrance, entry | noun
