headroom:/ˈhɛdruːm/ (US), /ˈhɛdruːm/ (UK)| n. |净空；头部空间；余量|The space between the top of a person's head and the ceiling of a car, room, etc. Also, the amount of available capacity, power, etc., beyond what is currently being used.|例句：There isn't much headroom in the back of that car. 那辆车的后座空间不太大。

例句：We need to build some financial headroom into the budget. 我们需要在预算中留出一些财务余地。|近义词：clearance, leeway, margin|反义词: confinement, restriction |词性:noun
