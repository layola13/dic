overreflectively: /ˌoʊvərɪˈflɛktɪvli/
| adv. | 过度反思地 |  In a manner characterized by excessive reflection or introspection; thinking too much about oneself and one's actions. | He spent the weekend overreflectively analyzing every social interaction he'd had that week. 他周末都在过度反思自己那一周的每一次社交互动。|  introspectively, ruminatively, excessively thoughtfully | impulsively, thoughtlessly | adverb
