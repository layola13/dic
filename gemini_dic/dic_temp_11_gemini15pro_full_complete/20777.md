blanketing:/ˈblæŋkɪtɪŋ/
adjective
覆盖的，像毯子一样的
covering something completely like a blanket
The heavy snow blanketing the ground made for a picturesque scene. 厚厚的积雪覆盖着地面，构成了一幅美丽的景象。
近义词:covering, enveloping, shrouding
反义词:exposing, uncovering, revealing
词性:adjective

blanketing:/ˈblæŋkɪtɪŋ/
noun
覆盖物
a covering that resembles a blanket
A blanketing of snow covered the entire landscape.  一层积雪覆盖了整个大地。
近义词:covering, layer
反义词:exposure
词性:noun


blanketing:/ˈblæŋkɪtɪŋ/
verb
覆盖，像毯子一样覆盖
to cover something completely like a blanket
Fog was blanketing the city. 雾笼罩着整个城市。
近义词:covering, enveloping, shrouding
反义词:exposing, uncovering, revealing
词性:verb

词源: From the noun "blanket" + "-ing" suffix,  ultimately from Old North French "blankete."
