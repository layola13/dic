glisteringly:/ˈɡlɪstərɪŋli/| adv.|闪闪发光地，闪烁地|Shining brightly with many small flashes of light. |The snow lay glisteringly on the rooftops. 雪闪闪发光地躺在屋顶上。|Near synonyms: sparklingly, glitteringly, brightly, shiningly|Near antonyms: dully, dimly|Adverb

Etymology->
From *glistering* + *-ly*. *Glistering* is the present participle of *glister*, from Old English *glisian* "to shine, glitter," related to *glæs* "glass."  The *-ly* suffix is a common adverbial ending, deriving from Old English *-līce*.
