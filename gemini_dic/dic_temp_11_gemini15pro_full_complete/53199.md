diptote:/ˈdɪptoʊt/ | n. | 双变格词 | A noun having only two case forms. |  The word "diptote" itself is not a diptote in English, but it describes nouns with only two cases in languages like Greek. |  Ancient Greek had several diptotes.  古希腊语有几个双变格词。| Near Synonyms: dual-case noun (less common) | Antonyms: triptote, tetraptote | noun
