nun:/nʌn/
n.
修女
a member of a religious community of women, especially a Roman Catholic one, living under vows of poverty, chastity, and obedience.
The nun devoted her life to prayer and service.  这位修女一生致力于祈祷和服务。
近义词:sister
反义词:monk
