dedicatorial:/ˌdedɪkəˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
奉献的；献辞的
Of, relating to, or in the nature of a dedication.
This book contains a dedicatorial preface to his parents. 这本书包含一篇献给父母的序言。
近义词: dedicatory
反义词: None
词性: adjective


dedicatorial:/ˌdedɪkəˈtɔːriəl/
noun
题献辞；献词
A dedicatory inscription or speech.
The dedicatorial at the beginning of the book moved many readers. 书开头的献词感动了许多读者。
近义词: dedication, inscription
反义词: None
词性: noun
