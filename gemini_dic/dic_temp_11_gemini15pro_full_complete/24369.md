bring-to-light:/ˌbrɪŋ tə ˈlaɪt/ | phrasal verb | 揭露；发现；公开；阐明 | To reveal something previously unknown or hidden. |  The investigation brought to light a series of financial irregularities. 调查揭露了一系列财务违规行为。

The documents brought to light the truth about the government's involvement. 这些文件揭露了政府参与的真相。

| 近义词: reveal, uncover, expose, disclose | 反义词: conceal, hide, obscure | 词性: 动词短语
