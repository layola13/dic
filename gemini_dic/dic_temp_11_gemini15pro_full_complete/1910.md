acting:/ˈæktɪŋ/| n. adj.|表演；演技；代理；行为；假装|the art or occupation of performing in plays, movies, or television productions|例句：He's been acting since he was a child. 他从小就一直在演戏。|例句：Her acting in the play was superb. 她在剧中的表演非常出色。|近义词：performance, portrayal, impersonation|反义词：inaction, reality|词性：名词；形容词
acting:/ˈæktɪŋ/| adj.|代理的；临时的；执行任务的|performing the functions of another person or thing temporarily|例句：The acting president will address the nation tonight. 代理总统今晚将向全国发表讲话。|例句：She is the acting manager while her boss is on vacation.  在她老板休假期间，她是代理经理。|近义词：temporary, interim, substitute|反义词：permanent, regular|词性：形容词
