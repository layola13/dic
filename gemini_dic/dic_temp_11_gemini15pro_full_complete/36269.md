cohabitate:/koʊˈhæbɪteɪt/
verb
同居
To live together in the same dwelling place, especially as an unmarried couple.
My boyfriend and I have been cohabitating for two years. 我和我的男朋友同居两年了。
They decided to cohabitate before getting married. 他们决定在结婚前同居。
近义词:live together, cohabit
反义词:live apart, separate
词性:v.

cohabit:/koʊˈhæbɪt/
verb
同居
To live together in the same dwelling place, especially as an unmarried couple.
My sister and her partner cohabit in a small apartment. 我姐姐和她的伴侣同居在一个小公寓里。
Many young couples choose to cohabit these days. 现在很多年轻夫妇选择同居。
近义词:live together, cohabitate
反义词:live apart, separate
词性:v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cohabitare, from Latin co- (together) + habitare (to dwell).
