ambuscade:/ˈæmbʊskeɪd/| n. v.|埋伏，伏击|An ambush; a concealed position from which to attack by surprise.  A trap; a snare.|The soldiers lay in ambuscade, waiting for the enemy to pass. 士兵们埋伏起来，等待敌人经过。|verb: to lie in wait for and attack from a concealed position. The highwaymen ambuscaded the stagecoach. 公路强盗伏击了驿站马车。|近义词：ambush, trap, snare|反义词：openness, exposure|noun, verb
