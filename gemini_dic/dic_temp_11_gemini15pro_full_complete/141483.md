opinionist:/əˈpɪnjənɪst/ | n. | 持有某种意见的人；评论员；专栏作家 |  A person who holds or expresses strong opinions, often in a dogmatic or biased way.  | Example: He's known as a political opinionist, never shying away from controversy. 他以政治评论员而闻名，从不回避争议。

Example: The opinionist wrote a scathing column about the new policy.  这位评论员写了一篇严厉批评新政策的专栏文章。| 近义词: commentator, pundit, columnist, advocate | 反义词:  moderate, impartial | 词性: noun
