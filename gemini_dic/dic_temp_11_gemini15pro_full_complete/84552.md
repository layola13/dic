hammerable:/ˈhæmərəbl/
adj.
可锤打的，可锻造的
able to be hammered into a shape.
The metal was soft and hammerable.  这种金属很软，可以锻造。
近义词:malleable, ductile, pliable
反义词:brittle, inflexible, rigid
词性:形容词

词源: From "hammer" (n.) + "-able" (suffix).  "Hammer" comes from Old English "hamer," from Proto-Germanic "*hamaraz," from Proto-Indo-European "*kamer-."  "-able" comes from Old French "-able," ultimately from Latin "-abilis."
