clamorer:/ˈklæmərər/ | n. | 喧嚣者，大声呼喊者 |  A person who makes a loud and persistent demand or complaint.  | The clamorers outside the city hall demanded the mayor's resignation. 市政厅外的喧嚣者要求市长辞职。| synonyms: protester, shouter, objector | antonyms: supporter, conformist | noun


clamor:/ˈklæmər/ | n. vi. | 喧嚣，吵闹；大声要求 | A loud and confused noise, especially that of people shouting vehemently. To shout loudly and insistently. |  The clamor of the crowd grew louder as the team scored.  人群的欢呼声随着球队的得分越来越大。 | synonyms: uproar, din, hubbub, outcry | antonyms: silence, quiet, stillness | noun, intransitive verb
