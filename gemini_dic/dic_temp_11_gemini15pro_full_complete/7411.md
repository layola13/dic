animator:/ˈænɪmeɪtər/| n.|动画师，动画制作人|A person who creates animated movies or cartoons.  |例句：The animator spent hours perfecting the character's movements.  动画师花了几个小时来完善角色的动作。

例句：Disney is looking for talented animators to join their team. 迪士尼正在寻找有才华的动画师加入他们的团队。|近义词：cartoonist, artist, designer|反义词:|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From animate (v.) + -or.  Animate comes from the Latin word "animare," meaning "to give life to."
