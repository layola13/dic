catacomb: /ˈkætəˌkʊm/ (US), /ˈkætəˌkuːm/ (UK)| n. |地下墓穴，陵墓|An underground cemetery consisting of a subterranean gallery with recesses for tombs, as constructed by the ancient Romans.|例句：The catacombs of Rome are a fascinating glimpse into ancient burial practices. 罗马的地下墓穴为了解古代丧葬习俗提供了一个迷人的视角。

例句：We explored the dark and eerie catacombs beneath the city. 我们探索了城市下方黑暗而阴森的地下墓穴。|近义词：crypt, vault, mausoleum|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "surface cemetery" or "above-ground burial")|词性: noun
