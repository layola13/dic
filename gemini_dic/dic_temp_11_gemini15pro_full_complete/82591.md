guardianage:/ˈɡɑːrdiənɪdʒ/ |n.|监护期，监护权|The period of time during which a person is under the guardianship of another.  The legal right or duty of a guardian to care for a person or their property, especially a minor or someone incapable of managing their own affairs. |The orphan's guardianage lasted until he turned eighteen.  这个孤儿的监护期持续到他十八岁。|Synonyms: guardianship, custodianship, wardship|Antonyms: independence, autonomy|noun
