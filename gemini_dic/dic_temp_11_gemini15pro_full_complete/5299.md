allotropicity:/əˌlɒtrəˈpɪsɪti/ |n.|同素异形性|The property of some chemical elements to exist in two or more different forms, in the same physical state, known as allotropes.|Diamond and graphite exhibit the allotropicity of carbon. 钻石和石墨展现了碳的同素异形性。| 近义词: allotropy | 反义词: N/A |词性: noun
