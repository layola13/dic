capering:/ˈkeɪpərɪŋ/ | adj. | 欢跃的；嬉戏的 |  Leaping or skipping about playfully. | The lambs were capering in the field. 羊羔在田野里欢快地跳跃。| 近义词: frolicking, gamboling, prancing, skipping | 反义词:  sluggish, still, motionless | 词性: 形容词

caper:/ˈkeɪpər/ | n. | 跳跃；嬉戏 | An act of leaping or skipping about playfully. |  The children's capers in the park entertained the onlookers. 孩子们在公园里的嬉戏娱乐了旁观者。| 近义词: frolic, gambol, prance, skip | 反义词:  inactivity, stillness | 词性: 名词

caper:/ˈkeɪpər/ | v. | 跳跃；嬉戏 | To leap or skip about playfully. |  The dancers capered across the stage. 舞者在舞台上跳跃。| 近义词: frolic, gambol, prance, skip | 反义词:  trudge, plod | 词性: 动词

词源: From the Middle French *capre*, "goat leap", from the Italian *capriola*, ultimately from Latin *caper*, "goat."
