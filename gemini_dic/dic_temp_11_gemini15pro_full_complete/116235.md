masquerade:/ˌmæskəˈreɪd/ | n. vi.| 化装舞会；伪装；掩饰 | A festive gathering of people wearing masks and other disguises.  A false show or pretense. |  例句：The annual masquerade ball was the highlight of the social season. 年度化装舞会是社交季的亮点。| 近义词：disguise, pretense, charade, facade | 反义词：reality, truth, honesty | 词性:n.

masquerade:/ˌmæskəˈreɪd/ | vi.| 伪装；掩饰 | To put on a false appearance; to disguise oneself. | 例句：He masqueraded as a police officer to gain access to the building. 他伪装成警察进入大楼。 | 近义词：pose, impersonate, feign | 反义词：reveal, expose | 词性:vi.

ETYMOLOGY->
From French "mascarade" (masked dance, masked ball), from Italian "mascherata" (masked entertainment), from "maschera" (mask), from Arabic "maskhara" (buffoonery, mockery).
