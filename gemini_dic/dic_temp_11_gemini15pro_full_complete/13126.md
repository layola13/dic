astute:/əˈstuːt/ | adj. | 精明的，敏锐的，狡猾的 | Having or showing an ability to accurately assess situations or people and turn this to one's advantage. |  He was an astute businessman who knew how to make a deal. 他是一位精明的商人，知道如何做成交易。| Synonyms: sharp, shrewd, clever, intelligent, perceptive, insightful | Antonyms: naive, gullible, foolish, unintelligent, obtuse | adjective

astutest:/əˈstuːtɪst/ | adj. | 最精明的，最敏锐的，最狡猾的 | Superlative form of "astute," meaning having or showing the greatest ability to accurately assess situations or people and turn this to one's advantage. | She was the astutest lawyer in the firm, always able to find loopholes in contracts. 她是公司里最精明的律师，总能找到合同中的漏洞。| Synonyms: sharpest, shrewdest, cleverest, most intelligent, most perceptive, most insightful | Antonyms: most naive, most gullible, most foolish, least intelligent, most obtuse | adjective
