inter-strain:/ˈɪntərstreɪn/
adjective
菌株间的；品系间的
Occurring between or involving different strains (of a microorganism, plant, or animal).
Example: Inter-strain variation in virulence was observed.  观察到不同菌株间毒力的差异。
Example: The researchers compared the inter-strain genetic diversity of the bacteria. 研究人员比较了这种细菌的菌株间遗传多样性。

近义词: between strains
反义词: intra-strain
