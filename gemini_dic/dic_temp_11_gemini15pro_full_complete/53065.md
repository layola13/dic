diorism:/ˈdaɪərɪzəm/
n.
界限; 区分; 分割; 特别是思想或信仰上的区分
The act or result of marking out boundaries or limits; distinction or division, especially of thought or belief.
The diorism between church and state is important. 教会和国家的区分很重要。
近义词：distinction, differentiation, separation, demarcation
反义词：unity, integration, fusion, confluence
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *διορίζειν (di-orizein, “to distinguish, define”), from *διορισμός (di-orismos, “definition, distinction”).  Ultimately from *ὅρος (horos, “boundary, limit”).
