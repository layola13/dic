madrona:/məˈdroʊnjə/ (US), /məˈdrəʊnə/ (UK)| n.|草莓树；太平洋熊果|A madrone is a broadleaf evergreen tree of the genus Arbutus, native to western North America.|We saw a beautiful madrone tree with its smooth, reddish bark. 我们看到了一棵美丽的草莓树，树皮光滑泛红。|The madrone berries are edible but not very palatable. 草莓树的果实可食用，但不太好吃。|Synonyms: Arbutus, Pacific madrone|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
