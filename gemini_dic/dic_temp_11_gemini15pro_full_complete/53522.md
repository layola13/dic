disastrously:/dɪˈzæstrəsli/
adverb
灾难性地，极糟地
in a way that causes or involves disaster.
The team played disastrously in the first half.  该队上半场表现得非常糟糕。
The project ended disastrously, costing the company millions. 这个项目以灾难告终，使公司损失了数百万美元。
近义词:catastrophically, ruinously, calamitously
反义词:successfully, fortunately, beneficially
词性:adverb

disaster:/dɪˈzæstər/
noun
灾难，灾祸
a sudden event, such as an accident or a natural catastrophe, that causes great damage or loss of life.
The earthquake was a major disaster.  这场地震是一场大灾难。
The oil spill was an environmental disaster. 石油泄漏是一场环境灾难。
近义词:calamity, catastrophe, tragedy
反义词:triumph, success, blessing
词性:noun


词源: From disaster + -ous + -ly.  Ultimately from the Italian disastro, from dis- "bad" + astro "star."
