auspiciously:/ɔːˈspɪʃəsli/ | adv. | 吉祥地，幸运地 |  In a way that suggests a positive and successful future.  Derived from the Latin word "auspicium," meaning "divination by birds." | The business venture began auspiciously with a large initial investment. 这项商业冒险以一大笔初始投资吉利地开始了。|  favorably, fortunately, propitiously | inauspiciously, unfortunately, unfavorably | adverb
