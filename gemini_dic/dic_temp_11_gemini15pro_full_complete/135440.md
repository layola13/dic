nonpersonifications:/nɑːnˌpɜːrsɑːnɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 非人格化，不赋予人格|The poem relies heavily on nonpersonifications of nature, describing the wind and rain without giving them human qualities. 这首诗大量依赖于对自然的非人格化，描述了风和雨，但没有赋予它们人类的品质。| Example:  The scientist's explanation of the weather patterns avoided any nonpersonifications, sticking to purely meteorological terms.  这位科学家对天气模式的解释避免了任何非人格化，坚持使用纯粹的气象学术语。| Synonym: objectification, depersonalization | Antonym: personification, anthropomorphism | noun
