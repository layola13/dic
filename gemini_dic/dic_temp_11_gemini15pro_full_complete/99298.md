intercosmic:/ˌɪntərˈkɒzmɪk/| adj.|宇宙间的，星际间的|Existing or occurring between universes or cosmoses.|例句：Intercosmic travel remains a staple of science fiction. 宇宙间的旅行仍然是科幻小说的主要内容。|例句：The theory proposes the existence of intercosmic bridges. 该理论提出了宇宙间桥梁的存在。|近义词：interstellar, intergalactic|反义词：intracosmic (if such a word existed, meaning "within a single cosmos")|词性:形容词
词源: inter- (between) + cosmic (relating to the cosmos)
