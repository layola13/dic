exhibiters:/ɪɡˈzɪbɪtərz/
n.
参展商，展出者
People or organizations that display their products or work at an exhibition.
Many exhibitors at the trade show were showcasing their latest innovations. 许多参展商在贸易展览会上展示了他们的最新创新。
The exhibitors were busy setting up their booths before the show opened. 展览开始前，参展商们正忙着布置他们的展位。
synonyms:displayers, presenters, vendors
antonyms:visitors, attendees
noun
