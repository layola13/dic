antihumanisms:/ˌæntiˈhjuːmənɪzəmz/
|n.|反人道主义，反人类主义|Philosophies or beliefs that reject or devalue fundamental aspects of humanism, such as the importance of reason, ethics, and individual agency.  These philosophies may emphasize the limitations or negative aspects of human nature, or prioritize other values over human concerns.|Some critics see certain aspects of postmodernism as promoting antihumanisms by deconstructing the idea of a unified self. 一些评论家认为后现代主义的某些方面通过解构统一自我的概念来宣扬反人道主义。|The rise of totalitarian regimes in the 20th century exemplified the dangers of antihumanisms. 20世纪极权主义政权的兴起体现了反人道主义的危险性。|近义词:misanthropy, inhumanity|反义词:humanism, philanthropy|词性:noun
