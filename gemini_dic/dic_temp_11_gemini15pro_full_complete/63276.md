enthralled:/ɪnˈθrɔːld/ | adj. | 着迷的，被迷住的 | Filled with wonder and delight; captivated. |  The children were enthralled by the magician's tricks. 孩子们被魔术师的戏法迷住了。

The audience was enthralled by her performance. 观众被她的表演迷住了。 | 近义词: captivated, fascinated, charmed, mesmerized | 反义词: bored, uninterested, apathetic, indifferent | 词性: adjective

词源: From en- + thrall (to enslave).  Originally meant "to enslave," later evolved to mean "to hold spellbound."
