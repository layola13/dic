bred:/brɛd/
adjective
培育的，饲养的
produced by breeding; raised in a particular environment.
例句：These horses are bred for racing. 这些马是为赛马而培育的。
例句：He was born and bred in the city. 他是在城里出生和长大的。
近义词：raised, cultivated, nurtured
反义词：wild, feral
词性：adjective


词源：
From Old English brēdan "to nourish, cherish, keep warm, hatch, produce young," from Proto-Germanic *brodjan "to roast, warm," from PIE root *bhreu- "to boil, bubble, effervesce, burn." The meaning "produce (offspring)" emerged in Middle English.
