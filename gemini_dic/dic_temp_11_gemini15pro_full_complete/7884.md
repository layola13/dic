antedating:/ˌæntiˈdeɪtɪŋ/
verb
早于；先于
preceding in time; existing or occurring before.
The church antedates the Norman Conquest. 这座教堂早于诺曼征服。
synonyms: precede, predate, preexist
antonyms: postdate, follow, succeed
v.

antedating:/ˌæntiˈdeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
早于的；先于的
Existing or occurring at an earlier date.
An antedating signature on the contract. 合同上的签字日期早于实际日期。
synonyms: earlier, prior, former
antonyms: later, subsequent, posterior
adj.
