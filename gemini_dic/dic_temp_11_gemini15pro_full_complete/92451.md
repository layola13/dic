hypopituitary:/ˌhaɪpəʊpɪˈtjuːɪtəri/
adjective|垂体功能减退的；垂体机能低下的|Relating to or characterized by decreased activity of the pituitary gland.|Hypopituitary patients often experience fatigue and weight changes. 垂体功能减退的病人经常感到疲劳和体重变化。|近义词: hypopituitarism |反义词: hyperpituitary
noun|垂体功能减退症|Deficient activity of the pituitary gland.|Hypopituitarism can be caused by tumors. 垂体功能减退症可能是由肿瘤引起的。|近义词: hypopituitarism |反义词: hyperpituitarism
word type|adjective, noun
