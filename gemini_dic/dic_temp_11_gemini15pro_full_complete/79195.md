Simple Present:
I glance at my phone every few minutes. 我每隔几分钟就看一下手机。
She glances out the window as the train pulls away. 火车开动时，她望向窗外。

Present Continuous:
I am glancing through a magazine while I wait. 我正在等的时候翻看杂志。
He is glancing at his notes before the presentation. 他在演讲前浏览他的笔记。

Present Perfect:
I have glanced at the report, but I haven't read it thoroughly. 我看过报告了，但还没有仔细阅读。
She has glanced over the contract and signed it. 她匆匆看过合同并签了字。

Present Perfect Continuous:
I have been glancing at my watch all morning, waiting for the meeting to start. 我整个上午一直在看手表，等着会议开始。
They have been glancing nervously at the clock.  他们一直紧张地瞥着时钟。

Simple Past:
I glanced at the clock and realized I was late. 我看了一眼时钟，意识到我迟到了。
She glanced back at the house as she walked away. 她走开时回头看了一眼房子。

Past Continuous:
I was glancing through a book when the phone rang.  电话响的时候，我正在翻阅一本书。
He was glancing at the scenery as he drove. 他开车时不时地看看风景。

Past Perfect:
I had glanced at the email earlier, but I hadn't had time to reply. 我之前看过那封邮件了，但是没时间回复。
She had glanced at the map before setting off on her journey.  她在出发旅行前看了一眼地图。

Past Perfect Continuous:
I had been glancing at the news headlines all day, waiting for an update. 我整天都在看新闻标题，等着更新。
They had been glancing anxiously at the door, waiting for the doctor. 他们一直焦急地望着门口，等待着医生。
