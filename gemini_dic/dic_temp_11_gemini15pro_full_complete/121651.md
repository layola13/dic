mispersuade:/ˌmɪspərˈsweɪd/| vt.|劝阻，使打消念头，误导|To persuade someone not to do something, or to believe something that is not true.|He tried to mispersuade her from leaving her job. 他试图劝阻她不要离职。

The advertisement mispersuaded many people into buying the faulty product.  这则广告误导了许多人去购买有缺陷的产品。|近义词：dissuade, deceive, mislead|反义词：persuade, convince|词性:动词
