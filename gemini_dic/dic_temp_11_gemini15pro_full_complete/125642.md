mummer:/ˈmʌmər/| n.| 哑剧演员；乔装打扮的人；(尤指圣诞节期间挨户表演的) 化装演员|A person who wears a mask or costume, typically for a performance or festival, especially one who acts in a traditional masked mime or a Christmas play.|The mummers paraded through the streets, their costumes bright and cheerful. 化装演员们在街上游行，他们的服装鲜艳而欢快。|Synonyms: actor, performer, masquerader, guiser|Antonyms: audience, spectator|noun
