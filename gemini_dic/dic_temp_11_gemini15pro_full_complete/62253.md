encumbering:/ɪnˈkʌmbərɪŋ/
|adj.|妨碍的，阻碍的；累赘的|Making something difficult to do or deal with. Restricting freedom of action.  Burdensome.|例句：The heavy backpack was encumbering the hiker's progress.  沉重的背包妨碍了徒步旅行者的前进。|例句：Encumbering regulations stifle innovation and economic growth. 繁琐的规章制度扼杀了创新和经济增长。|近义词：hindering, impeding, obstructing, burdening|反义词：facilitating, aiding, assisting|词性: adjective

encumber:/ɪnˈkʌmbər/
|vt.|妨碍，阻碍；拖累|To restrict or burden someone or something, making movement or activity difficult.|例句：He was encumbered by debts. 他被债务拖累。|例句：The company is encumbered with outdated technology.  这家公司被过时的技术所拖累。|近义词：hinder, impede, obstruct, burden|反义词：facilitate, aid, assist|词性: verb

词源:
From Old French *encombrer, from combre "obstacle, barrier," from Latin cumulus "heap, pile."  Originally meant "to block up, obstruct with a heap."
