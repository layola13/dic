deforms:/dɪˈfɔːrmz/
verb
使变形；使残缺；使畸形
to spoil the shape or appearance of something; to make something ugly
The intense heat deformed the plastic.  高温使塑料变形了。
近义词:distort, warp, contort, misshape
反义词:reform, reshape, fix
词性:v.

deformed:/dɪˈfɔːrmd/
adjective
畸形的；变形的
not having the normal or natural shape or form; disfigured
a deformed hand  一只畸形的手
近义词:misshapen, distorted, malformed
反义词:perfect, normal, symmetrical
词性:adj.

deformation:/ˌdiːfɔːrˈmeɪʃn/
noun
变形；畸形
the action or process of deforming or being deformed
The accident caused severe deformation of the car. 事故导致汽车严重变形。
近义词:distortion, contortion, warp
反义词:reformation, reshaping
词性:n.
