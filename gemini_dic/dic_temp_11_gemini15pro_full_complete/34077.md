civilises:/ˈsɪvɪlaɪzɪz/
verb
教化，使文明
to bring out of a savage, uneducated, or unrefined state, or to introduce order and organization into
例句：Education civilises people. 教育使人文明。

例句：The Romans civilised much of Europe. 罗马人教化了欧洲的大部分地区。
近义词：educates, refines, cultivates, enlightens
反义词：barbarises, brutalises
词性：v.
