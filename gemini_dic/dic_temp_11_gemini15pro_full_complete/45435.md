curable:/ˈkjʊərəbl/| adj.|可治愈的，医得好的|Able to be cured or healed.|The doctor said the cancer was curable in its early stages. 医生说这种癌症在早期是可以治愈的。|近义词：treatable, remediable, healable|反义词：incurable, terminal, irremediable|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *curable*, from Old French *curable*, from Latin *cūrābilis*, from *cūrō* ("I care for, attend to, heal").
