banzai:/bɑːnˈzaɪ/ | int. n. | 万岁；日本人的欢呼声 | A Japanese patriotic cry or cheer, equivalent to "hurrah!" or "long live." |  例句：The soldiers shouted "banzai!" as they charged. 士兵们冲锋时高呼“万岁！”

例句：The crowd erupted in cries of "banzai!". 人群爆发出一阵阵“万岁！”的呼喊。

近义词：hurrah, cheers | 反义词: N/A | 词性: interjection, noun

词源: From Japanese 万歳 (banzai), literally meaning "ten thousand years" (of life).
