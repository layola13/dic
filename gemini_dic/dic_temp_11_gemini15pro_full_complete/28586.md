carefreenesses:/ˈkɛərˌfriːnɪsɪz/
n.
无忧无虑；轻松愉快
the state of being carefree; lightheartedness.
Her carefree days of childhood were over. 她童年无忧无虑的日子结束了。
近义词：lightheartedness, insouciance, nonchalance
反义词：anxiety, worry, care, concern
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

词源：
From carefree + -ness.  Carefree itself comes from care (Old English *caru) + free (Old English freo).
