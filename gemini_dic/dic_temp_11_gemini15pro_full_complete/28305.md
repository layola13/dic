car-pools: /ˈkɑːrˌpuːlz/ (US), /ˈkɑːˌpuːlz/ (UK)| n. |拼车；共乘；汽车合用| An arrangement where several people travel together in one car, especially to and from work, to save on fuel costs. |例句：We formed a car-pool to reduce our commuting costs. 我们组成了一个拼车小组以减少通勤费用。|近义词：ride-sharing, car sharing, lift sharing|反义词: driving alone, solo driving| noun

car-pool: /ˈkɑːrˌpuːl/ (US), /ˈkɑːˌpuːl/ (UK)| v. |拼车；共乘；合用汽车| To travel together in one car, especially to and from work, to save on fuel costs. |例句：They car-pool to work every day to save money on gas. 他们每天拼车上班以节省汽油钱。|近义词：ride-share, share a ride|反义词: drive alone| verb
