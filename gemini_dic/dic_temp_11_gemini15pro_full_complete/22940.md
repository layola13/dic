bottlefuls:/ˈbɒtlfʊlz/ (British), /ˈbɑːtlfʊlz/ (American)
noun
满满几瓶；数瓶之量
The quantity contained in a specified number of bottles.
She drank three bottlefuls of water after her run.  她跑步后喝了三瓶水。
The chemist prepared several bottlefuls of the solution. 药剂师配制了几瓶溶液。

Synonyms: bottles, containers, jars (when referring to containers),  loads, lots (when referring to quantity)
Antonyms: drops, dribbles (referring to small quantities)
词性: 名词
