brachyura:/bræˈkɪʊərə/
noun
短尾次目；短尾类
An infraorder of decapod crustaceans, characterized by a short abdomen folded beneath the thorax.  They are commonly known as crabs.
The Brachyura exhibit a wide range of adaptations to different environments, from rocky intertidal zones to deep-sea hydrothermal vents. 短尾类对从岩石潮间带到深海热液喷口的不同环境表现出广泛的适应性。
Crabs, lobsters, and shrimps are all decapod crustaceans, but only crabs belong to the infraorder Brachyura. 蟹、龙虾和虾都是十足目甲壳类动物，但只有蟹属于短尾次目。
