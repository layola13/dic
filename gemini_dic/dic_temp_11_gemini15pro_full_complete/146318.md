overfees:/ˈoʊvərˌfiːz/ |n. |超额费用，额外费用| An amount charged in excess of a standard or reasonable fee. | Example 1: The overfees on my phone bill were outrageous. 我的电话账单上的额外费用高得离谱。

Example 2:  They protested the overfees charged by the airline for baggage. 他们抗议航空公司收取的行李超额费用。 |Synonyms: surcharges, extra charges, additional fees| Antonyms: discounts, rebates, reductions| noun
