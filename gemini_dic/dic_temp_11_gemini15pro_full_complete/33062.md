chowed down:/tʃaʊd daʊn/
verb
狼吞虎咽地吃；大吃特吃
to eat a large amount of food quickly and enthusiastically
例句：We chowed down on pizza after the game.  赛后我们狼吞虎咽地吃了披萨。

例句：They chowed down on burgers and fries. 他们大口大口地吃着汉堡和薯条。

近义词：gobble, devour, wolf down, pig out
反义词：nibble, pick at
词性：动词短语 (phrasal verb)
