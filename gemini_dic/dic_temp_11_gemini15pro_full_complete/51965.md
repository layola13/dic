diacope:/daɪˈækəpiː/ /daɪˈɑːkəpiː/
noun
间隔反复法，重复法
A rhetorical device or figure of speech involving the repetition of a word or phrase with one or more intervening words.  It derives from the Greek word "diakopē," meaning "cut in two" or "interrupted."
例句：
"Free at last, free at last, thank God almighty, free at last." - Martin Luther King Jr.
终于自由了，终于自由了，感谢全能的上帝，终于自由了。

例句：
"Bond. James Bond."
邦德。詹姆斯·邦德。

近义词：repetition, epizeuxis, anadiplosis
反义词:N/A
词性:noun
