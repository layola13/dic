effaceable:/ɪˈfeɪsəbl/
adjective
可抹去的，可消除的
able to be effaced; erasable.
The inscription on the tombstone was barely effaceable, despite years of weathering. 墓碑上的铭文尽管经受了多年的风吹日晒，但几乎没有被抹掉。
近义词:erasable, deletable, removable, obliterable
反义词:permanent, indelible, lasting
词性:adjective

efface:/ɪˈfeɪs/
verb
抹去，消除
erase (a mark or surface feature).
Time had effaced almost all signs of the struggle. 时间几乎抹去了所有斗争的痕迹。
近义词:erase, delete, remove, obliterate
反义词:preserve, maintain, retain
词性:verb

词源:
From French effacer, from e- (variant of ex- “out”) + face “face” (from Latin facies).
