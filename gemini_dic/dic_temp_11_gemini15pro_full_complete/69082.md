faunas:/ˈfɔːnəz/
noun
动物群
The animals of a particular region, habitat, or geological period.
The Galapagos Islands are famed for their unique faunas. 加拉帕戈斯群岛以其独特的动物群而闻名。
synonyms:  animal life, wildlife, animals, creatures, beasts
antonyms: flora
