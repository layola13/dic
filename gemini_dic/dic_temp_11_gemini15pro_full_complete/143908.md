outprayed:/ˌaʊtˈpreɪd/
verb
胜过祈祷
To have prayed longer or more fervently than someone else.
He outprayed his rival in the monastery. 他在修道院里比他的对手祈祷得更久更虔诚。
surpass in prayer, outdo in prayer
be outprayed by
