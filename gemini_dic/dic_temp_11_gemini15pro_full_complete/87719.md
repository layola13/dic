hexdecyl:/ˌheksˈdesɪl/ |adj. |十六烷基的；十六烷的|Relating to or containing hexadecane, a saturated hydrocarbon with 16 carbon atoms. |Hexadecyl chains are common in lipids and surfactants. 十六烷基链常见于脂类和表面活性剂中。|近义词: cetyl (more common)|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective

hexdecyl:/ˌheksˈdesɪl/ |n. |十六烷基|A univalent radical derived from hexadecane. |Hexadecyl alcohol is a fatty alcohol used in cosmetics. 十六烷醇是一种用于化妆品的脂肪醇。|近义词: cetyl (more common)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
