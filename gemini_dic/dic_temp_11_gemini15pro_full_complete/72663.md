foalfoot:/ˈfəʊlfʊt/ (US) /ˈfoʊlfʊt/| n.|款冬; 蹄叶|A perennial plant of the genus *Tussilago*, especially *T. farfara*, with yellow flowers resembling dandelions and large, hoof-shaped leaves appearing later.  It has been traditionally used in herbal medicine.|The foalfoot is blooming early this year. 今年款冬开花很早。|Synonym: coltsfoot|Antonym: N/A|noun

foalfoot:/ˈfəʊlfʊt/ (US) /ˈfoʊlfʊt/| adj.|款冬的; 蹄叶状的|Resembling or relating to the foalfoot plant.|The foalfoot leaves are distinctly hoof-shaped. 款冬的叶子明显呈蹄形。|Synonym: coltsfoot|Antonym: N/A|adjective
