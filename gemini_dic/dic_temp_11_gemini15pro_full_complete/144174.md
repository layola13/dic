outshrieked: /ˌaʊtˈʃriːkt/
verb
胜过…的喊叫声，盖过…的喊叫声；比…叫得更响
outdo or surpass in shrieking.
She outshrieked the other fans at the concert. 她在音乐会上比其他歌迷叫得更响亮。
Synonyms: outyelled, outscreamed
Antonyms: whispered, murmured
词性: 动词
