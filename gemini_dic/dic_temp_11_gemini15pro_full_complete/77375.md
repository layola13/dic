gaunt:/ɡɔːnt/| adj.|憔悴的，瘦削的，荒凉的|Lean and haggard, often due to suffering, hunger, or age; also, desolate and bleak.|例句：The refugees arrived looking gaunt and exhausted. 难民们到达时看起来憔悴不堪，精疲力竭。

例句：The gaunt landscape of the desert stretched before them. 荒凉的沙漠景象展现在他们面前。|近义词：thin, emaciated, haggard, bleak, desolate|反义词：plump, robust, lush, fertile|词性:形容词
