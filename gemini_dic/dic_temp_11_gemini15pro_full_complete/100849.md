introspecting:/ˌɪntrəˈspɛktɪŋ/
adjective|内省的，反省的|examining one's own thoughts and feelings|She spent a quiet afternoon introspecting on her life choices. 她花了一个安静的下午反思她的人生选择。|近义词：reflective, contemplative, meditative|反义词：unreflective, shallow|词性: adjective
introspect:/ˌɪntrəˈspɛkt/
verb|内省，反省|to examine one's own thoughts and feelings|He introspected on his reasons for wanting the job. 他反省了自己想要这份工作的原因。|近义词：reflect, contemplate, meditate|反义词：ignore, disregard|词性: verb

Etymology:
From Latin introspicere "to look within," from intro- "inward" + specere "to look."
