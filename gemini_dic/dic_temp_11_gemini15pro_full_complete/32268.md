chide:/tʃaɪd/| v. |责骂，斥责|To scold or rebuke someone gently or mildly, often with a sense of disappointment or concern.|例句：She chided him for his carelessness. 她责备他粗心大意。

例句：The teacher chided the students for talking during the exam. 老师斥责学生们在考试期间讲话。

近义词：scold, rebuke, reprimand, reproach|反义词:praise, commend|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *cīdan, from Proto-Germanic *kīdaną (“to complain, quarrel, contend, accuse”). Cognate with Old Frisian chīda (“to quarrel, accuse, complain”), Middle Dutch kijden (“to contend, quarrel, scold”), Old High German chīdan (“to contend, quarrel, scold”), German keifen (“to scold, quarrel”).
