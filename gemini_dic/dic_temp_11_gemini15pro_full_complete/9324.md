antipopery:/ˌæntiˈpəʊpəri/
n.
反对天主教；反天主教主义
Opposition to the Catholic Church, especially its political power.
The Reformation fueled widespread antipopery in England. 宗教改革在英国激起了广泛的反天主教情绪。
anti-Catholicism,  opposition to Catholicism
Catholicism, pro-Catholicism
noun
