fragranced:/ˈfreɪɡrənst/ | adj. |有香味的，芳香的 | Having a pleasant smell; perfumed. | The room was fragranced with lilies. 房间里弥漫着百合花的香味。 |  The fragranced candles filled the air with a soothing aroma. 香薰蜡烛让空气中充满了令人舒缓的香味。 | 近义词: aromatic, perfumed, scented | 反义词: unscented, odorless | 词性:形容词

词源:  From the verb "fragrance" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Fragrance" comes from Old French "fragrans" meaning "fragrant, sweet smelling," ultimately from Latin "fragrāre" "to emit a sweet smell," from "fragrāns," present participle of "frāgrāre," related to "frāgum" "strawberry."
