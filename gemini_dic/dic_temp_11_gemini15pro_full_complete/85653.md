haussas:/ˈhaʊsəz/ | n. | 豪萨人 |  People who belong to the Hausa ethnic group, primarily found in northern Nigeria and Niger.  They are one of the largest ethnic groups in Africa and are known for their rich culture, including a distinct language (also called Hausa), vibrant traditions, and a history of trade and scholarship. | The Haussas have a rich oral tradition. 豪萨人有丰富的口头传统。 |  synonyms: Hausa people | antonyms: N/A | noun
