myops:/ˈmaɪɒps/ (UK), /ˈmaɪɑːps/ (US)| n.|近视患者|A person who is nearsighted; a person affected with myopia.|Example 1: The myops squinted to see the distant sign.  近视患者眯着眼睛看远处的标志。

Example 2:  The optometrist prescribed glasses for the myops.  验光师给近视患者开了眼镜。|Synonyms: nearsighted person, shortsighted person|Antonyms: hyperope, farsighted person|noun
