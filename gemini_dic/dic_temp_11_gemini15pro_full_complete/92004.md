hyperoestrogenism:/ˌhaɪpərˈiːstrədʒənɪzəm/
noun
雌激素过多症
A condition characterized by excessive levels of estrogen in the body.
Hyperestrogenism can cause a variety of symptoms, including irregular periods, breast tenderness, and mood swings.  雌激素过多症会导致各种症状，包括月经不调、乳房胀痛和情绪波动。
The doctor suspected hyperestrogenism based on her symptoms. 医生根据她的症状怀疑是雌激素过多症。
Synonym: estrogen excess
Antonym: hypoestrogenism
