americanising:/əˈmɛrɪkənaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使美国化
The process of making something more American in character or style.
Many British companies are americanising their operations. 许多英国公司正在将其业务美国化。
The restaurant chain is gradually americanising its menu. 这家连锁餐厅正在逐步将其菜单美国化。
