gavelock:/ˈɡævəlɒk/ (UK) /ˈɡævəlɑːk/ (US)| n.|投石带，投石器，标枪|A gavelock is an ancient weapon, a kind of lever for throwing stones. It can also refer to a javelin or spear.|The warriors used gavelocks to defend their village.|近义词：sling, catapult, javelin, spear|反义词: shield (somewhat loosely, representing defense rather than offense)|noun
