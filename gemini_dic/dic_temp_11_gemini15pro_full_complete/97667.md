inhibitor:/ɪnˈhɪbɪtər/| n.|抑制剂，抑制物|A substance that slows down or prevents a particular chemical reaction or other process.|The inhibitor prevented the growth of the bacteria. 该抑制剂阻止了细菌的生长。|synonyms: suppressant, blocker, repressor|antonyms: activator, catalyst, stimulant|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *inhibitor, from Latin inhibitus, past participle of inhibere "to restrain, check," from in- "in" + habere "to hold."
