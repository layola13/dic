leafage: /ˈliːfɪdʒ/
n.
叶子; 簇叶
Mass of leaves; foliage.
The dense leafage of the jungle canopy blocked out the sun. 茂密的丛林树冠遮住了阳光。

The autumn leafage was a riot of color. 秋天的叶子色彩缤纷。
foliage, leaves
bare branches
