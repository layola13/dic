jigger's:/ˈdʒɪɡərz/ | n. | 吉格酒杯（尤指容量为1.5盎司的）; 量酒器;  少量（尤指烈酒） |  A jigger is a small measuring cup used for pouring alcoholic drinks, typically holding 1.5 ounces. It can also refer to a small amount of liquor. | He poured a jigger of whiskey into the glass. 他往杯子里倒了一吉格威士忌。 | 近义词: shot glass, measure | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps large amount) | 词性: 名词
