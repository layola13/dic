impalpable:/ɪmˈpælpəbl/ | adj. | 难以察觉的，无法感知的，难以理解的 | Not easily understood or grasped; intangible. impalpable:/ɪmˈpælpəbl/ | adj. |  难以捉摸的 | Difficult to grasp or define. | His motives remained impalpable, even to his closest friends. 他的动机仍然难以捉摸，即使对他最亲密的朋友也是如此。| The artist tried to capture the impalpable beauty of the sunset. 艺术家试图捕捉日落时难以捉摸的美丽。| synonyms: elusive, intangible, subtle | antonyms: concrete, definite | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *impalpabilis, from Latin in- "not" + palpabilis "that can be touched," from palpare "to touch gently."
