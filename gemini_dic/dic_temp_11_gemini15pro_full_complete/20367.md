bister:/ˈbɪstər/| adj. n.|黄褐色的；暗棕色的； bistre颜料；|A dull, yellowish-brown pigment made from wood soot.  一种由木烟灰制成的暗黄褐色颜料。|The artist used bister to create a sepia-toned drawing. 艺术家用bister颜料创作了一幅棕褐色的画。|近义词：brown, sepia, umber|反义词：bright, vibrant|词性:形容词, 名词

Etymology: From Old French bistre, from Latin *bistorius, possibly of Germanic origin.
