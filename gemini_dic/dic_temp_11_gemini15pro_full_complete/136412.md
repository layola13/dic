nonretentively:/ˌnɒn rɪˈtɛntɪvli/
adverb
非持久地，不保留地
in a way that does not retain something.
The information was processed nonretentively, leaving no trace in the system.  该信息被非持久地处理，系统中没有留下任何痕迹。
He listened nonretentively, forgetting the details almost immediately. 他心不在焉地听着，几乎立刻就忘记了细节。
近义词: transiently, temporarily, fleetingly
反义词: permanently, retentively
