disadvance:/ˌdɪsədˈvæns/ | v. | 阻碍，使处于不利地位；减少, 降低 | To hinder or put at a disadvantage; to lessen or reduce. | The new regulations will disadvance smaller businesses. 新规定将使小型企业处于不利地位。 | The constant rain disadvanced the construction project.  持续的降雨延误了建设项目。 | 近义词: hinder, impede, obstruct, hamper, set back, detract from | 反义词: advance, promote, further, aid, assist | 词性: 动词

Etymology:
From dis- (expressing negation, reversal) + advance.  The "dis-" prefix signifies the opposite of "advance."
