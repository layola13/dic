excerpting:/ɪkˈsɜːrptɪŋ/ | v. | 摘录，节选 |  The author was accused of excerpting large sections of another writer's work without permission.  这位作者被指控未经许可摘录了另一位作家作品的大部分内容。 | Example:  She is excerpting the most important information from the report. 她正在从报告中摘录最重要的信息。 | Synonyms: extracting, quoting, citing, abstracting | Antonyms:  including, incorporating, expanding | verb

excerpt:/ˈeksɜːrpt/ | n. | 摘录，节选 | An excerpt from the book was published in the magazine. 这本书的节选发表在杂志上。 | Example: The excerpt from the poem was particularly moving. 这首诗的摘录特别感人。 | Synonyms: extract, quotation, citation, passage | Antonyms: whole, entirety | noun
