Dharavi: /dɑːˈrɑːviː/
n.
达拉维
A large slum in Mumbai, India, known for its informal economy and dense population.
Dharavi is one of the largest slums in Asia.  达拉维是亚洲最大的贫民窟之一。
Dharavi has become a hub for small-scale industries. 达拉维已成为小型工业中心。
近义词: slum, shantytown
反义词: affluent neighborhood, upscale district
词性: 名词
