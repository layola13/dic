FDIC:/ˌɛf.diː.aɪˈsiː/
n.
Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (美国)联邦存款保险公司
The FDIC insures deposits in member banks up to $250,000 per depositor, per insured bank, for each account ownership category.
联邦存款保险公司为每个账户所有权类别中每个储户在每个受保银行的存款提供高达 25 万美元的保险。
近义词:deposit insurance, banking regulator
反义词: (无直接反义词)
词性: 名词

Etymology:  The FDIC was created in 1933 by the Banking Act of 1933, enacted during the Great Depression.  "FDIC" is an initialism for Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation.
