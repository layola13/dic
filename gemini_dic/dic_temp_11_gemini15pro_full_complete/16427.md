bare-down:/ˈbɛər daʊn/ | phrasal verb |  向下推，向下压 |  To push or press downwards. (This sense is less common than the "exert effort" meaning.) |  The doctor told her to bare down during the examination. 医生告诉她在检查过程中要向下用力。 | synonyms:  press down, push down | antonyms: lift, raise | verb
