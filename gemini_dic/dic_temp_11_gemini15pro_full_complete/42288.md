counter-gobony:/ˈkaʊntər ɡɒbəni/ | adj. | 相反的，逆向的（纹章学用语，指颜色相反的棋盘格图案） | In heraldry, counter-gobony describes a pattern of alternating squares of contrasting tinctures, where the arrangement is the reverse of what would be expected.  It's essentially a "reversed" gobony pattern. | Example: The shield was counter-gobony argent and azure. 盾牌是银色和天蓝色相间的反棋盘格图案。 | Example:  The counter-gobony field made the coat of arms quite distinctive. 反棋盘格的底色使徽章非常独特。 | Synonym:  (No exact synonym, but related) inverted, reversed, transposed,  contrary | Antonym:  gobony | word type: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "counter-" (meaning against, opposite) + "gobony" (from Old French "gobonné," referring to a checkered pattern like a game board).  Ultimately, "gobony" likely derives from "gob," a piece in a board game.
