metastasis: /məˈtæstəsɪs/ (US), /mɪˈtæstəsɪs/ (UK)| n. |转移，扩散（尤指癌细胞）|The spread of a disease-producing agency (such as cancer cells or bacteria) from the initial or primary site of disease to another part of the body.|The cancer has metastasized to her lungs. 癌症已经转移到她的肺部了。|Synonyms: spread, dissemination, diffusion|Antonyms: localization, remission|Noun

metastatic: /ˌmɛtəˈstætɪk/ (US), /ˌmɛtəˈstætɪk/ (UK)| adj. |转移的，扩散的（尤指癌细胞）|Relating to or characterized by metastasis.|Metastatic cancer is a serious concern. 转移性癌症是一个严重的问题。|Synonyms: spreading, disseminated, diffused|Antonyms: localized, contained|Adjective
