draughtboards:/ˈdrɑːftbɔːrdz/ /ˈdræftbɔːrdz/
noun
西洋跳棋盘
A draughtboard is a square board divided into 64 dark and light squares of alternating colors, used for playing draughts (checkers).
The two players sat opposite each other, the draughtboard between them.  两个棋手面对面坐着，棋盘摆在他们之间。

近义词: checkerboards
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "game board" as a broader category with an opposing sense)
词性: Noun
