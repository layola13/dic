kava:/ˈkɑːvə/| n.|卡瓦酒，卡瓦根|A South Pacific beverage made from the powdered root of the kava plant, known for its relaxing and sedative effects.|例句：He enjoyed a cup of kava after a long day.  他辛苦了一天后享用了一杯卡瓦酒。

例句：Kava ceremonies are an important part of Pacific Islander culture. 卡瓦仪式是太平洋岛民文化的重要组成部分。|近义词：'awa (Hawaiian), yaqona (Fijian)|反义词: stimulant|noun
