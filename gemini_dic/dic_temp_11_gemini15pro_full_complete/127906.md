neolocal:/ˈniːoʊˌloʊkəl/ | adj. |新居的，从夫居的|Relating to a custom in which a married couple resides separately from both the husband's and the wife's natal household.|例句：They chose a neolocal residence, setting up their home in a new city. 他们选择了从夫居的住所，在一个新的城市安家落户。

例句：Neolocal residence is common in modern Western societies.  在新居制在现代西方社会很普遍。|近义词：none (The concept is specific)|反义词：matrilocal, patrilocal|词性:adjective
