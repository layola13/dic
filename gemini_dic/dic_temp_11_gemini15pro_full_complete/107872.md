largesses:/lɑːrˈdʒesɪz/
n.
慷慨赠予；施舍；赠品
acts of giving money and gifts generously
The king distributed largesses to the poor. 国王向穷人分发赠品。

近义词: generosity, liberality, munificence, bounty, donations, gifts, alms
反义词: stinginess, miserliness, parsimony, meanness


词性: 名词(plural noun)
词源: From Old French largesse, from large (wide, generous). Ultimately from Latin largus (abundant).
