interstimulation:/ˌɪntərˌstɪmjʊˈleɪʃən/
noun
互相刺激，相互刺激
The act or process of stimulating each other.
Interstimulation between neurons is essential for learning and memory. 神经元之间的相互刺激对于学习和记忆至关重要。
The interstimulation of the group members led to a very productive brainstorming session.  小组成员之间的相互刺激带来了一场非常富有成效的头脑风暴会议。
mutual stimulation, reciprocal stimulation
inhibition, suppression
n.
