curvature:/ˈkɜːrvətʃər/
|n.|曲率，弯曲，曲度|The road follows the curvature of the coastline. 这条路沿着海岸线的弧度延伸。|The curvature of space-time is a key concept in Einstein's theory of relativity. 时空的曲率是爱因斯坦相对论中的一个关键概念。|近义词: bend, curve, bow|反义词: straightness, flatness|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *curvatura, from Latin curvatus, past participle of curvare (“to bend, curve”), from curvus (“bent, curved”).
