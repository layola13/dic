murmurous:/ˈmɜːrmərəs/ (US), /ˈmɜːmərəs/ (UK)| adj.|低语的，喃喃的；潺潺的|Characterized by a low, indistinct, continuous sound, like that of a murmur; suggestive of a murmur.|The murmurous sound of the stream lulled me to sleep.  潺潺的溪流声使我昏昏欲睡。|近义词：whispering, rustling, babbling, gurgling|反义词：loud, boisterous, silent|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *murmurosus, from Latin murmur (murmur) + -osus (suffix forming adjectives meaning "full of," "abounding in," "given to," "like").
