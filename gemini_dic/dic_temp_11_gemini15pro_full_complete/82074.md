grize:/ɡraɪz/| v.|变灰白；(使)恐惧；(使)害怕；恐惧；害怕|To turn gray or become gray, especially with age or fear; to feel fear or dread.|Her hair began to grize at the temples. 她的头发开始在鬓角变灰白。|近义词：gray, age, fear, dread|反义词：darken, rejuvenate, embolden|词性：动词

词源: From Middle English *grisen, from Old French gris “gray,” from Frankish *grīs, from Proto-Germanic *grīsa-. Cognate with Dutch grijs “gray,” German greis “old man,” Old Norse grár “gray.”
