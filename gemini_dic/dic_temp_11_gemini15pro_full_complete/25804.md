burgeon:/ˈbɜːrdʒən/| v. |萌芽，迅速发展|To begin to grow or increase rapidly; flourish.|例句：The city’s population burgeoned in the 19th century. 这个城市的人口在19世纪迅速增长。

例句：New industries burgeoned after the war. 战后新兴产业蓬勃发展。|近义词：flourish, thrive, prosper, bloom, expand, mushroom|反义词：decline, dwindle, shrink, contract, diminish|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *burjonen, from Old French burjoner, from burjon (“bud, sprout”), of uncertain Germanic origin, possibly from Frankish *burjan (“to protect, raise, lift”) or a blend with Old French bourgeon (“bud”).
