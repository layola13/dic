disenslave:/ˌdɪsɪnˈsleɪv/
verb
解放，使摆脱奴役
To free from slavery or bondage; liberate.
The organization works to disenslave people from human trafficking.  这个组织致力于将人们从人口贩运中解救出来。
The prisoners of war were finally disenslaved and allowed to return home. 战俘最终被释放并被允许回家。
近义词:liberate, free, emancipate, release
反义词:enslave, subjugate, oppress
词性:verb
