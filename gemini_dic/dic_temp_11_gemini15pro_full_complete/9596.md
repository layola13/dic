antisophists:/ˌæntiˈsɒfɪsts/ | n. | 反智者 |  Those who opposed or criticized the Sophists, a group of ancient Greek teachers of rhetoric and philosophy. |  Plato's dialogues often feature antisophists arguing against the Sophists' relativistic views.  柏拉图的对话录中经常出现反智者反对智者相对主义观点的情节。| Synonyms: opponent of sophistry, critic of sophism | Antonyms: sophist, sophist advocate | noun

antisophist:/ˌæntiˈsɒfɪst/ | adj. | 反智者的，反对智术的 |  Characterized by opposition to the methods or teachings of the Sophists. | His antisophist stance led him to develop a more rigorous approach to philosophy. 他的反智主义立场促使他发展出一种更严谨的哲学方法。 | Synonyms: anti-sophistic | Antonyms: sophistic | adjective
