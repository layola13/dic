misrecollection:/ˌmɪsrɛkəˈlɛkʃən/
n.
错误的记忆；记错；误忆
An inaccurate or faulty memory of something.
She had a misrecollection of the event and thought it happened on a Tuesday. 她对这件事记错了，以为是星期二发生的。

I apologize for the misrecollection in my previous statement. 我为我之前声明中的错误记忆道歉。

近义词:false memory, paramnesia, inaccurate memory
反义词:accurate memory, correct recollection

词性:noun
