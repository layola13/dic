freeholder:/ˈfriːhoʊldər/| n.|自由地所有人；永久业主|A freeholder is someone who owns a freehold property, meaning they own the land and any buildings on it outright, rather than just leasing it.|例句：The freeholder is responsible for maintaining the structure of the building.  永久业主负责维护建筑物的结构。

例句：As a freeholder, she had complete control over the property. 作为永久业主，她完全控制着该房产。|近义词：owner, proprietor|反义词:leaseholder, tenant|词性:noun

freehold:/ˈfriːhoʊld/| n. adj.|永久产权；永久业权的|Freehold refers to the absolute ownership of land and any buildings on it, without any time limit.|例句：They purchased the property freehold. 他们购买了该房产的永久产权。

例句：Freehold property is generally more expensive than leasehold. 永久产权的房产通常比租赁产权的更贵。|近义词：fee simple|反义词:leasehold|词性:noun, adjective
