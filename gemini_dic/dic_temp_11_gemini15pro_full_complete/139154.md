obfuscate: /ˈɒbfəskeɪt/ (UK) /ˈɑːbfəskeɪt/ (US) | v. |  使模糊；使混乱；使困惑 | to make something less clear and harder to understand, especially intentionally | The company's financial statements were obfuscated by a series of complex transactions. 该公司的一系列复杂交易使其财务报表变得模糊不清。

The government was accused of obfuscating the truth about the war. 政府被指责掩盖了战争的真相。


近义词: confuse, obscure, bewilder, mystify, cloud, complicate
反义词: clarify, elucidate, explain, illuminate, simplify
词性: verb
