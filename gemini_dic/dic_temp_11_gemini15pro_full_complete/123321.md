monographs:/ˈmɒnəɡrɑːfs/ /ˈmɑːnəɡræfs/
n.
专题著作；专论
A detailed written study of a single specialized subject or an aspect of it.
例句：He is the author of several monographs on early American history. 他是几本早期美国历史专著的作者。

例句：The library has a vast collection of monographs on various scientific topics.  这个图书馆收藏了大量关于各种科学主题的专题著作。

近义词：treatise, dissertation, study, paper, essay
反义词:  None (Monographs are a specific type of writing; there's no direct opposite.)
词性: Noun
