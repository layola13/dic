hoffmann:/ˈhɒfmən/ (UK), /ˈhɑːfmən/ (US)| n. |霍夫曼|A German surname, also occasionally a given name.  It derives from the occupation of a courtier or steward (literally "court man").  It is also associated with E.T.A.
