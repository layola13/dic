forger:/ˈfɔːrdʒər/ (US), /ˈfɔːdʒə(r)/ (UK)| n.|伪造者；赝造者|A person who makes a copy of something in order to deceive.|The forger was eventually caught and sentenced to prison.  伪造者最终被抓获并判处监禁。|synonyms: counterfeiter, falsifier, imitator|antonyms: authenticator, verifier|noun

forgery:/ˈfɔːrdʒəri/ (US), /ˈfɔːdʒəri/ (UK)| n.|伪造；伪造品|The action of forging or producing a copy of a document, signature, banknote, or work of art with the intent to deceive.|The painting was discovered to be a forgery. 这幅画被发现是赝品。|synonyms: counterfeit, fake, imitation|antonyms: original, genuine|noun
