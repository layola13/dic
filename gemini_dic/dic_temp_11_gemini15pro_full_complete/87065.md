heptameter:/hɛpˈtæmɪtər/
|n.|七步格，七音步诗行|A line of verse consisting of seven metrical feet.|Shakespeare occasionally used heptameter in his plays. 莎士比亚偶尔在他的戏剧中使用七步格。|The poem was written in iambic heptameter. 这首诗是用抑扬格七步格写成的。|hexameter, octameter|pentameter, tetrameter|noun
