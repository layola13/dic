consolement:/kənˈsoʊlmənt/ | n. |安慰，慰藉|The act of consoling or the state of being consoled.  The ceremony brought her some consolement after the loss of her father. |例句：She found consolement in the support of her friends.  她在朋友们的支持中找到了慰藉。

例句：His words offered little consolement. 他的话语几乎没有带来任何安慰。| 近义词：comfort, solace, consolation | 反义词：distress, grief, sorrow |词性:名词


词源: From Middle English *consolement, from Old French consulement, from consoler "to console," from Late Latin consolari "to console, comfort, cheer," from com- "together" (see com-) + solari "to comfort, soothe," from sol "sun" (see Sol).
