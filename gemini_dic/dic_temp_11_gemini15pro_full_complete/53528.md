disaugment:/dɪsɔːɡˈment/ | v. | 减少，缩小，削弱 | To diminish or reduce in size, extent, or degree. |  The new policy will disaugment the company's profits. 新政策会减少公司的利润。| The constant criticism disaugmented her confidence.  不断的批评削弱了她的自信心。 | 近义词: decrease, diminish, reduce, lessen, weaken | 反义词: augment, increase, enhance, strengthen | 词性: 动词

Etymology:
From dis- (expressing negation, reversal) + augment (to increase).  The prefix "dis-" comes from Latin, meaning "apart," "asunder," "away," expressing negation, reversal, or intensity. "Augment" comes from the Latin "augmentare," meaning "to increase."
