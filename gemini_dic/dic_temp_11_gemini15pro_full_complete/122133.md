mistransport:/ˌmɪstrænsˈpɔːrt/ | n. | 误运，运输错误 | The mistransport of goods caused significant delays and increased costs. 货物误运导致严重延误和成本增加。| The package was mistransported to the wrong address. 包裹被误运到错误的地址。 | 近义词: misrouting, misdirection, misexpedition | 反义词: correct shipment, proper delivery | 词性: 名词

词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning "wrong" or "bad") + "transport" (verb meaning "to carry from one place to another").  The prefix "mis-" comes from Old French "mes-" from a Germanic source related to Old English "mis-". "Transport" comes from Old French "transporter" from Latin "transportare" meaning "to carry across".
