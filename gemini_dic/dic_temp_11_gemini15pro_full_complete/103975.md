juger:/ʒyʒe/ (French)| v. |判断；评价；审判；认为|To form an opinion or judgment about; to consider; to deem; to try in a court of law.|

The jury judged the defendant guilty.  陪审团判定被告有罪。

The coach judged her performance to be excellent. 教练认为她的表现很出色。


近义词: assess, evaluate, consider, deem, estimate, rate, appraise, adjudicate, try
反义词: abstain, forbear
词性: verb
