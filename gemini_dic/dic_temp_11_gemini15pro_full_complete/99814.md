interminable:/ɪnˈtɜːrmɪnəbl/ (US), /ɪnˈtɜːmɪnəbl/ (UK)| adj.| 无休止的，没完没了的|Seemingly endless; tediously long.| The meeting was interminable and unproductive.  会议没完没了，而且没有成果。|The journey seemed interminable. 这趟旅程似乎永无止境。|近义词：endless, ceaseless, incessant, perpetual|反义词：short, brief, finite, concise|词性:形容词

詞源:
Late Middle English: from Old French, or from medieval Latin interminabilis, from in- ‘not’ + terminabilis (from terminare ‘to limit’).
