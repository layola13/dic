antievolutionary:/ˌæntiˌɛvəˈluːʃəˌnɛri/
adjective
反进化的，反对进化论的
Opposing or contrary to the theory of evolution.
The Discovery Institute promotes antievolutionary ideas through intelligent design.  探索研究所通过智能设计来推广反进化论的观点。
Creationism is an antievolutionary belief system.  神创论是一种反进化论的信仰体系。
synonyms: anti-Darwinian, creationist
antonyms: evolutionary, Darwinian
