genealogical:/ˌdʒiːniəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
家谱的；宗谱的；系谱的
Relating to the study or tracing of lines of family descent.
She began researching her genealogical history. 她开始研究她的家族史。
The library has a vast collection of genealogical records.  图书馆收藏了大量的家谱记录。
近义词：ancestral, familial
反义词：N/A
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin genealogicus, from Greek genealogikos, from genealogia "genealogy," from genea "race, generation" + -logia "-logy."
