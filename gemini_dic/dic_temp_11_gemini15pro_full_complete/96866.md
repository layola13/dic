inexhaustibilities:/ˌɪnɪɡˈzɔːstɪbɪlɪtiz/ | n. | 取之不尽; 无穷无尽 | The quality of being inexhaustible; inability to be exhausted or used up.  |  The artist seemed to possess an inexhaustibilities of creative ideas. 这位艺术家似乎拥有无穷无尽的创意。 | synonyms:  boundlessness, endlessness, limitlessness | antonyms: finiteness, limitations, scarcity | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + exhaustible (able to be exhausted) + -ity (noun suffix indicating state or quality) + -es (plural suffix).  Exhaustible ultimately derives from the Latin exhaurire (to drain out).
