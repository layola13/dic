cheesesteaks:/ˈtʃiːzsteɪks/
n.
芝士牛排
A sandwich consisting of thinly sliced steak, melted cheese, and often onions and peppers, served on a long roll.
例句：I ordered two cheesesteaks with extra onions. 我点了两个加洋葱的芝士牛排。

例句：Philadelphia is famous for its cheesesteaks. 费城以其芝士牛排而闻名。

近义词：steak sandwich, Philly cheesesteak
反义词: (No direct antonym for a food item)
词性:noun
