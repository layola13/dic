intermarine:/ˌɪntərˈməˈriːn/| adj.| 海间的，航行于海间的|Existing or carried on between seas or oceans.  Relating to or connecting different seas.|例句：Intermarine trade has been crucial for centuries. 海间贸易几个世纪以来都至关重要。

例句：The intermarine cable connects several islands. 海底电缆连接了几个岛屿。|近义词：maritime, oceanic|反义词：landlocked, terrestrial|词性:adjective

词源:
From inter- + marine.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," and "marine" refers to the sea.
