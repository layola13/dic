dictative:/ˈdɪktətɪv/| adj.|独裁的，专断的|Inclined to dictate; overbearing; imperious.  Given to commanding others. |例句：The manager's dictative style alienated many employees.  经理的独裁作风疏远了许多员工。|例句：Her dictative personality made it difficult to work with her. 她专横的性格使人很难与她共事。|近义词：authoritarian, autocratic, bossy, domineering, imperious, peremptory|反义词：democratic, collaborative, cooperative|词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *dictativus, from Latin dictātus, past participle of dictāre  "to dictate, say often, prescribe."
