outsleep:/ˌaʊtˈsliːp/
verb
比...睡得久; 睡得超过
to sleep for longer than someone else
例句：I always outsleep my husband. 我总是比我丈夫睡得久。

例句：She can outsleep anyone I know. 她比我认识的任何人都能睡得久。

近义词：sleep longer than
反义词：wake up before

词性：v.
