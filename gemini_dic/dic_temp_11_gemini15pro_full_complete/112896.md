ludic:/ˈluːdɪk/| adj.|嬉戏的，玩耍的，游戏的|Relating to or characterized by play; playful.  Derived from the Latin word "ludus" meaning "game" or "play."|The children's ludic activities kept them entertained for hours. 孩子们的嬉戏活动让他们玩了几个小时。|例句：The designer created a ludic space filled with bright colors and interactive elements.  设计师创造了一个充满鲜艳色彩和互动元素的趣味空间。

例句：Her ludic approach to teaching made learning fun for the students. 她寓教于乐的教学方法使学生觉得学习很有趣。|近义词：playful, sportive, frolicsome, merry|反义词：serious, solemn, grave|词性:adjective
