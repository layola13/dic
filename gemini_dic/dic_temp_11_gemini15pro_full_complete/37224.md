comfinesses:/ˈkʌmfinəsɪz/
|n.|舒适；惬意；安逸|The state of being comfortable and cozy.|She sank into the comfinesses of the plush armchair. 她沉浸在舒适的豪华扶手椅中。|近义词: comforts, coziness, snugness|反义词: discomfort, unease, hardship|词性:名词

词源:
From comfy (adjective) + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  Comfy is a colloquial shortening of comfortable, which comes from the Old French confortable, ultimately from the Latin confortare "to strengthen much."
