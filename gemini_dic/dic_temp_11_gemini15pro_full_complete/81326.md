grapheme:/ˈɡræfiːm/ | n. | 字素 | A grapheme is the smallest unit of a writing system of any given language.  It is a letter or a number of letters that represent a sound (phoneme) in a word. |  The grapheme "ph" makes an /f/ sound. 字素“ph”发/f/音。| synonyms: character, letter, symbol | antonyms: phoneme (arguably) | noun

grapheme:/ˈɡræfiːm/ | n. | 字形 | A grapheme is a written symbol that represents a phoneme. | The grapheme 'b' represents the phoneme /b/. 字形“b”代表音素/b/。| synonyms: letter, character, sign | antonyms: phoneme | noun
