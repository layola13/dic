laminiplantar:/ˌlæmɪnɪˈplæntər/
adjective
跖板的，蹄底的
Relating to the lamina and the plantar surface (sole) of the foot, or the corresponding surface in animals.  This term often refers to the area between the hoof wall and the sole of a horse's hoof.
The laminiplantar junction is a crucial area for hoof health. 蹄底结合处是蹄健康的关键区域。
Laminitis can severely affect the laminiplantar region of the hoof.  蹄叶炎会严重影响蹄的跖板区域。
Near Antonyms: dorsal (referring to the top surface)
词性: adjective
