cull:/kʌl/
verb
挑选，剔除，宰杀
select from a large quantity; obtain from a variety of sources.  Reduce the size of (a herd) by selective slaughter.
例句：
The farmer culled the weakest lambs from the flock.  农民从羊群中剔除了最弱的小羊。

The editor culled several paragraphs from the original text.  编辑从原文中删去了几段。

近义词：select, choose, pick, glean, gather, reap, harvest, weed out, eliminate, remove
反义词：reject, discard, include, add

词性: verb

cull:/kʌl/
noun
剔除；挑选
The act of culling.
例句：
The annual cull of deer helps to control the population.  每年对鹿的捕杀有助于控制鹿的数量。

The cull of the herd resulted in the removal of several sick animals.  对牛群的剔除导致了几只病畜的清除。

近义词：selection, choice, picking
反义词：rejection, inclusion


词源：
From Latin colligere "to collect," from com- "together" + legere "to gather, choose, read."  Originally meaning "to gather," the sense shifted to "select" and later to "select for killing (to reduce numbers)."
