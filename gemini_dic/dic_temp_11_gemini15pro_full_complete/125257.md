multidwelling:/ˌmʌltiˈdwɛlɪŋ/ | adj. n.|多户住宅，多单元住宅|Designed for or occupied by more than one family.  A building with multiple separate housing units. |例句：The city council approved the construction of a new multidwelling complex. 市议会批准了新建一座多户住宅综合体。

|例句：Finding parking near her multidwelling building was always a challenge. 在她的多单元住宅楼附近找停车位总是一项挑战。|近义词：apartment building, condominium, block of flats|反义词:single-family home, detached house|词性:adjective, noun


Etymology: multi- (from Latin multus, "much, many") + dwelling (from Old English dwellan, "to delay, hinder, deceive, lead astray, go astray, err, linger, stay, dwell").
