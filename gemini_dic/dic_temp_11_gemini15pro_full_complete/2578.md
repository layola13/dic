admonisher:/ədˈmɒnɪʃər/ | n. | 劝诫者；告诫者；警告者 |  A person who admonishes; one who gives a warning or reprimand. | The teacher acted as an admonisher to the unruly students. 老师对不守规矩的学生进行了劝诫。| synonyms: reprover, rebuker, critic, censor | antonyms: praiser, flatterer | noun

admonish:/ədˈmɒnɪʃ/ | v. | 劝诫；告诫；警告 | To warn or reprimand someone firmly. |  She admonished him for his carelessness. 她告诫他要细心。| synonyms: reprove, rebuke, reprimand, chide | antonyms: praise, commend, laud | verb
