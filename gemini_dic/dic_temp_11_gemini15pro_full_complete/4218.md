airings:/ˈeərɪŋz/
n.
播出;播放;公开发表
the act of broadcasting a television or radio programme or of making something known publicly.
The television series had its first airing in 1992.  这部电视剧于1992年首播。
The documentary received several airings on public television. 这部纪录片在公共电视台播出过几次。
broadcasts, showings, presentations
suppressions, concealments
noun
