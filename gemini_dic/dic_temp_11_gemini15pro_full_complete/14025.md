auscultation:/ˌɔːskəlˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 听诊 | The act of listening to sounds within the body, typically with a stethoscope, for diagnostic purposes. | The doctor performed an auscultation of the lungs. 医生进行了肺部听诊。 | examination, listening |  - | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *auscultāre*, frequentative of *auscultare* "to listen to," from *auris* "ear".
