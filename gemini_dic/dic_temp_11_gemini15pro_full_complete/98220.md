insecurer:/ˌɪnsɪˈkjʊrər/ | adj. | 更不安全的，更没自信的 | Comparative form of insecure: more insecure.  Feeling less confident and less safe. |  She felt even insecurer after the presentation went badly.  她演讲失败后更加没有自信了。|  more uncertain, more anxious, less confident | more secure, more confident | adjective
