gastroenteritis:/ˌɡæstroʊˌɛntəˈraɪtɪs/
n.
肠胃炎
Inflammation of the stomach and intestines, typically resulting from bacterial toxins or viral infection and causing vomiting and diarrhea.
She was diagnosed with gastroenteritis after experiencing severe stomach cramps and vomiting. 她经历了严重的胃痉挛和呕吐后被诊断出患有肠胃炎。
The cruise ship was quarantined due to an outbreak of gastroenteritis. 由于肠胃炎爆发，游轮被隔离。
近义词: stomach flu, belly flu
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "digestive health")

gastritis:/ɡæˈstraɪtɪs/
n.
胃炎
Inflammation of the lining of the stomach.
Chronic gastritis can lead to ulcers. 慢性胃炎会导致溃疡。
He suffered from gastritis due to excessive alcohol consumption.  由于过量饮酒，他患上了胃炎。


enteritis:/ˌɛntəˈraɪtɪs/
n.
肠炎
Inflammation of the intestine, especially the small intestine.
Bacterial enteritis can be treated with antibiotics. 细菌性肠炎可以用抗生素治疗。
Symptoms of enteritis include abdominal pain and diarrhea. 肠炎的症状包括腹痛和腹泻。

gastro- : /ˈɡæstroʊ-/
prefix.
胃的，与胃有关的
Relating to the stomach.
Gastroscopy is a procedure to examine the inside of the stomach. 胃镜检查是一种检查胃内部的程序。
Gastrointestinal problems can be very uncomfortable. 胃肠道问题会非常不舒服。
