bdelloidea:/bɛˌlɔɪˈdiːə/ | n. |蛭形轮虫类| A class of rotifers characterized by their ability to reproduce asexually through parthenogenesis and their ability to survive extreme desiccation. | Bdelloidea are microscopic animals found in freshwater habitats. 蛭形轮虫类是发现于淡水栖息地的微型动物。| synonyms: rotifer, philodina | antonyms: N/A | noun

Etymology:  From Greek *βδέλλα (bdélla, “leech”) + -oidea.  Relates to their leech-like movement.
