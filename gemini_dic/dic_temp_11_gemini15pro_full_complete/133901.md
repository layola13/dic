nonguidances: /nɒnˈɡaɪdənsɪz/ | n. | 无指导，缺乏指导 |  Lack of guidance or direction.  This word is formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to the noun "guidance." | The students suffered from nonguidances in their research project, leading to a disorganized outcome.  学生们在研究项目中缺乏指导，导致结果杂乱无章。| Near synonyms: lack of direction, lack of support, neglect,  | Antonyms: guidance, direction, support, mentorship | noun
