ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *erst*, from Old English *ǣrest* (“first, at first, firstly, soonest, most readily”), from Proto-Germanic *airistaz* (“first”), superlative of *air* (“early”). Cognate with Old Frisian *ērest*, *ērst* (“first”), Old Saxon *ērist* (“first”), Old High German *ērist* (“first”), Gothic *𐌰𐌹𐍂𐌹𐍃𐍄𐍃* (airists, “first”). More at ere.
