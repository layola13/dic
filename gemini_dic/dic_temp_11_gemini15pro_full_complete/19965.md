bioethanol:/ˌbaɪoʊˈeθənɒl/ | n. | 生物乙醇 | An alcohol fuel made by fermenting the sugar components of plant materials. It is mainly used as a biofuel additive for petrol. | Bioethanol is considered a renewable energy source. 生物乙醇被认为是一种可再生能源。| Bioethanol can be produced from corn or sugarcane. 生物乙醇可以由玉米或甘蔗生产。 | synonyms: biofuel, ethyl alcohol | antonyms: fossil fuel | noun
