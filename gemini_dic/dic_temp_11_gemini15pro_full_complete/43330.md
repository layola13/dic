cramp:/kræmp/| n. v.|痉挛，抽筋；限制，束缚|A sudden, painful involuntary contraction of a muscle.  / To restrict or confine.|例句：I got a cramp in my leg while swimming. 我游泳时腿抽筋了。 /  The small apartment cramped his style. 小公寓限制了他的风格。|近义词：spasm, restriction, confine|反义词:relaxation, freedom|名词,动词

cramp:/kræmp/| adj.|狭窄的，局促的|Uncomfortably small or restricted.|例句：The room was cramped and stuffy. 房间又狭小又闷热。|近义词：confined, restricted, tight|反义词:spacious, roomy|形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *cramp or *crump, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch kramp and German Krampf.  Ultimately from a base meaning 'to bend, curve.'
