dingle:/ˈdɪŋɡəl/| n.|山谷，峡谷；树林中的僻静处|A deep wooded valley or dell.|We hiked through the dingle, enjoying the shade of the trees. 我们徒步穿过峡谷，享受着树荫。|The secluded dingle was a perfect spot for a picnic. 这个僻静的峡谷是野餐的完美地点。|近义词：valley, dell, glen, ravine|反义词:mountain, peak|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *dingle, from a variant of Old English *denu "valley," of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to Old English dyn "noise, din," referring to the sound of a stream in a valley.
