noncoerciveness:/nɒnˌkəʊˈɜːsɪvnəs/
n.
非强制性；非胁迫性
The quality of not being coercive; the absence of coercion.  The principle of noncoerciveness is central to democratic processes.
例句：The agreement emphasized the noncoerciveness of the partnership.  该协议强调了合作关系的非强制性。

例句：Noncoerciveness in negotiations is crucial for building trust.  谈判中的非强制性对于建立信任至关重要。

近义词：voluntariness, freedom, autonomy, liberty
反义词：coercion, compulsion, force, pressure
词性: noun

词源:  From non- + coerciveness, from coerce + -ive + -ness.  Coerce ultimately comes from Latin "co-," meaning "together," and "arcere," meaning "to restrain."  Thus, coercion implies restraining someone's will.  Non- simply negates this meaning.
