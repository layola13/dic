anthologically:/ˌænθəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ | adv. | 选集式地，汇编式地 | In a manner characteristic of or resembling an anthology; in a way that suggests a collection of choice extracts or representative pieces. | The author anthologically presented the best poems of the Romantic era.  作者以选集的方式呈现了浪漫主义时期最好的诗歌。 | Example: The professor anthologically compiled the key writings on the subject. 教授将关于这个主题的关键著作汇编成册。| Synonyms: comprehensively, representatively, selectively | Antonyms: partially, incompletely |  Part of Speech: Adverb

anthological:/ˌænθəˈlɒdʒɪk(ə)l/ | adj. | 选集的，汇编的 |  Of, relating to, or resembling an anthology. | The book is an anthological collection of essays on modern art. 这本书是关于现代艺术论文的选集。 | Example: His anthological approach to the topic provided a broad overview. 他对这个主题的选集式研究提供了一个广泛的概述。 | Synonyms: compiled, collected, curated | Antonyms: original, singular, individual | Part of Speech: Adjective
