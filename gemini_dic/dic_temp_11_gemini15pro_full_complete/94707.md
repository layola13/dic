impromptuary: /ɪmˈprɒmptʃʊəri/ /ɪmˈprɑːmptʃʊəri/
adjective
即兴的，无准备的
Done without being planned, organized, or rehearsed.
She delivered an impromptuary speech at the wedding.  她在婚礼上发表了即兴演讲。
近义词: impromptu, extemporaneous, off-the-cuff, improvised
反义词: planned, prepared, rehearsed
词性: adjective
