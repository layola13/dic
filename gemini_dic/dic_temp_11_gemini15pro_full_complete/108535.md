lazaruses:/ˈlæzərəsəz/| n. |拉撒路们；死而复生的人；绝处逢生的人|People who have recovered from a serious illness or near-death experience, often in a surprising or unexpected way.  Referencing the biblical Lazarus raised from the dead by Jesus.|Lazarus-like recoveries from debilitating diseases are rare but inspiring.  拉撒路式的从衰弱性疾病中康复是罕见的，但鼓舞人心。|Synonyms: survivors, returnees|Antonyms: deceased, dead|noun
