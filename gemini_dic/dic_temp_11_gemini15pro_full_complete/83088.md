gusher:/ˈɡʌʃər/|n.|喷油井；涌出的人（或事物）；|A gusher is an oil well from which oil flows profusely without being pumped.  A gusher can also refer to something that gushes, like a person who talks enthusiastically or a wound that bleeds heavily.|The new oil well proved to be a gusher, bringing in millions of barrels a day. 这口新油井证明是一口喷油井，每天产出数百万桶石油。

The child, overcome with emotion, became a gusher of tears.  这个孩子情绪激动，泪如泉涌。|近义词：spout, jet, outpouring, torrent|反义词:trickle, drip|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From gush + -er.  Gush itself comes from the Middle Low German guschen, "to pour, gush."
