Etymology:
From over- + articulate.  "Over-"  is a prefix indicating "too much" or "excessive." "Articulate" comes from Latin "articulatus," past participle of "articulare"  meaning "to utter distinctly," from "articulus" meaning "joint, small part," diminutive of "artus" meaning "joint."
