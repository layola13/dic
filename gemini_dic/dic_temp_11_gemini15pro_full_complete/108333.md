lavalike:/ˈlɑːvəlaɪk/| adj.|像熔岩的，熔岩状的|Resembling lava in appearance, consistency, or movement.|The lavalike flow of the mudslide engulfed the village. 泥石流像熔岩一样吞没了村庄。|近义词：molten, igneous, volcanic|反义词：solid, hard, cold|词性:形容词
词源:lava + -like.  "lava" from Italian, from Latin "labes" (a falling or sinking). "-like" is an Old English suffix meaning "having the form or characteristics of".
