frowny:/ˈfraʊni/| adj.| 皱眉的，不高兴的 | Showing displeasure or sadness by frowning. |  She gave me a frowny face when I told her the bad news. 她当我告诉她这个坏消息时，给了我一个皱眉的表情。

She had a frowny expression after the argument. 争吵过后，她一脸不高兴。| 近义词：scowling, glum, sullen, grumpy | 反义词：smiling, cheerful, happy | 词性: adjective

词源:  From frown + -y.  The suffix "-y" is used to form adjectives from nouns, often indicating a characteristic or quality.  In this case, it indicates the quality of having or displaying frowns.
