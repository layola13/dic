notch:/nɒtʃ/ (British) /nɑːtʃ/ (American)| n. vt.|等级；档次；缺口；刻痕；赢得；获得|A mark or indentation, especially one cut into a surface. To score or achieve something.|

例句：
He made a notch on the stick for every day he spent on the island.
他在棍子上为他在岛上度过的每一天刻了一个凹痕。

He notched up another victory in the tournament.
他在锦标赛中再次获胜。


近义词：degree, level, grade, mark, score, indentation, cut, nick, gain, achieve, win
反义词: loss, failure
词性: noun, verb
