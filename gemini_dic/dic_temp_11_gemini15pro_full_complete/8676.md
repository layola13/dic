anticonventionalist:/ˌæntiˌkənˈvɛnʃənəlɪst/
| n. adj.| 反传统主义者，反传统的 | Someone who opposes or rejects conventional beliefs, customs, or practices.  One who challenges established norms and traditions. |  He's always been a bit of an anticonventionalist, preferring to forge his own path. 他一直有点反传统，喜欢走自己的路。|  As an anticonventionalist artist, she shunned traditional galleries and displayed her work in public spaces. 作为一名反传统艺术家，她避开了传统的画廊，在公共场所展示她的作品。 | nonconformist, iconoclast, maverick, rebel, dissident |  conventionalist, traditionalist | noun, adjective
