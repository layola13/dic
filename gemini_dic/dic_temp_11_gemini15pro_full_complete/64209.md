equidiurnal:/ˌiːkwɪˈdaɪərnəl/ | adj. |昼夜平分的，等昼夜的|Characterized by equal periods of day and night.  Relating to the equinox.|例句：The equidiurnal tides are highest and lowest during the spring and autumn equinoxes. 春分和秋分时，昼夜平分潮汐的涨落幅度最大。|例句：The planet's equidiurnal rotation makes for a temperate climate.  这个星球昼夜平分的自转形成了温和的气候。|近义词：equinoctial|反义词:|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *aequidiurnalis, from Latin aequus (“equal”) + diurnus (“daily, of a day”), from dies (“day”).
