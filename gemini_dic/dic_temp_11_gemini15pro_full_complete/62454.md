endorser:/ɪnˈdɔːrsər/ (US), /ɪnˈdɔːsə(r)/ (UK)| n.|背书人；代言人；签署者|A person who publicly approves or supports someone or something.|Nike signed Michael Jordan as an endorser for their shoes. 耐克签下迈克尔·乔丹作为其鞋子的代言人。|Example: The celebrity endorser appeared in commercials for the new product.  这位名人代言人出现在了新产品的广告中。|Synonyms: supporter, advocate, promoter, sponsor|Antonyms: critic, opponent, detractor|noun
