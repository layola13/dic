goad:/ɡoʊd/| n. vt.|刺激；激励；尖棒；|Something that encourages, urges, or drives someone to do something; a pointed stick used to prod animals.|The constant criticism from his boss was a goad to work harder.  他老板不断的批评促使他更加努力工作。| He goaded the bull with a stick. 他用棍子刺激公牛。|synonyms: spur, prod, urge, incite, provoke|antonyms: deter, discourage, dissuade|noun, verb
