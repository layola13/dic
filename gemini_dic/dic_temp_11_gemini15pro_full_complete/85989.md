headstand:/ˈhedˌstænd/| n.|倒立|An acrobatic feat in which one balances on the head, usually with the help of the hands.|She can do a headstand for over a minute. 她可以倒立超过一分钟。|Synonyms: handstand (when balancing on hands), inversion|Antonyms: standing (general opposite), upright position|noun
