becquerel:/ˈbɛkərɛl/ |n.|贝克勒尔|The becquerel (Bq) is the SI derived unit of radioactivity.  It is defined as the activity of a quantity of radioactive material in which one nucleus decays per second.|The banana has a radioactivity of about 15 becquerels.  香蕉的放射性约为15贝克勒尔。|近义词: (unit of radioactivity)  no direct synonym, but related units include curie, rutherford, disintegrations per second |反义词: None (it's a unit of measurement)|词性:noun

becquerel:/ˈbɛkərɛl/ |n.|贝克勒尔|A unit of radioactivity, corresponding to one nuclear disintegration per second.|The sample emitted 100 becquerels of radiation. 样本发射了100贝克勒尔的辐射。|近义词: (unit of radioactivity) no direct synonym, but related units include curie, rutherford, disintegrations per second|反义词: None (it's a unit of measurement)|词性:noun
