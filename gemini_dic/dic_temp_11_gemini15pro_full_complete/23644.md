brander:/ˈbrændər/| n.|烙印者，打烙印的人；污蔑者|Someone who brands something, especially cattle or a product.  A person who gives someone or something a bad name. |The brander marked the cattle with the ranch's symbol.  烙印者用牧场的标志标记了牛。 He was a brander of reputations, spreading rumors about anyone he disliked. 他是一个污蔑名誉的人，散布关于任何他不喜欢的人的谣言。|近义词：marker, labeler, slanderer, defamer|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
