intranets:/ˈɪntrənɛts/ | n. |内联网| A private network accessible only to an organization's staff.|  Our company uses the intranet to share documents and information internally. 我们公司使用内联网在内部共享文档和信息。|Example: The company intranet provides access to employee handbooks and policy documents. 公司内联网提供访问员工手册和政策文件的权限。|Synonyms: private network, internal network |Antonyms: internet, extranet | noun
