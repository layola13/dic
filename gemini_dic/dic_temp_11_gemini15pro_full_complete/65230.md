etonian: /iːˈtəʊniən/ | n. adj. | 伊顿公学的学生; 伊顿公学的; | A male student or former student of Eton College. Also used as an adjective relating to Eton College. |  He's an Old Etonian. 他是伊顿公学的老毕业生。

The Etonian accent is quite distinctive. 伊顿公学的口音很独特。


近义词: alumnus (for former student),  upper-class, privileged (for adjective, in some contexts)
反义词: N/A
词性: noun, adjective
词源: From Eton + -ian.  Eton is the name of the town where Eton College is located. The suffix "-ian" denotes belonging to or associated with.
