deluded:/dɪˈluːdɪd/
adjective
受骗的，妄想的
believing something that is not true
例句：He's deluded if he thinks he's going to win. 他要是认为他会赢，那就是妄想。

例句：The deluded old man believed he was a king.  这个妄想的老人相信自己是个国王。

近义词：misguided, deceived, fooled, mistaken
反义词：sane, rational, clear-headed
词性：adjective
