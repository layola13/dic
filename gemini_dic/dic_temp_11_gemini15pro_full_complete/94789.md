impugnant:/ɪmˈpʌɡnənt/ |adj.|令人反感的；引起反对的；不名誉的|Calling into question the integrity or validity of something; offensive or objectionable.|Her impugnant remarks about his character led to a heated argument. 她对他性格的攻击性言论引发了激烈的争论。|Synonyms: objectionable, offensive, exceptionable, reprehensible, censurable|Antonyms: commendable, laudable, praiseworthy|adjective

impugnant:/ɪmˈpʌɡnənt/ |adj.|令人反感的；引起反对的；不名誉的|Attacking or criticizing as false or questionable.|The defense lawyer impugned the witness's testimony. 辩护律师质疑证人的证词。|Synonyms: challenge, question, dispute, contradict, gainsay|Antonyms: support, uphold, endorse, corroborate|adjective
