depressurizes:/diːˈprɛʃəˌraɪzɪz/
verb
使减压；卸压
To release pressure from something, especially from a sealed container or pressurized cabin.
The pilot depressurized the cabin before landing.  飞行员在着陆前降低了机舱压力。
The spacecraft depressurizes before re-entry.  宇宙飞船在重返大气层前会减压。
synonyms: release pressure, vent, decompress
antonyms: pressurize, inflate
词性: 动词
