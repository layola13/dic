Example 2:  They did away with the death penalty years ago. 他们多年前就废除了死刑。 | 近义词: abolish, eliminate, eradicate, get rid of, dispose of, kill, murder | 反义词: establish, introduce, create, keep, retain | 词性: 动词短语
