blether:/ˈblɛðər/ (UK), /ˈblɛðər/ (US)| v. n.|闲聊，胡扯；废话|To talk at length, especially about unimportant matters.  Can also refer to the talk itself.|例句：They spent the afternoon blethering about nothing in particular. 他们一下午都在闲聊些无关紧要的事。

例句：He was blethering on about his new car for ages. 他喋喋不休地谈论着他的新车，好长时间。|近义词：chatter, prattle, gab, ramble|反义词：be silent, be quiet|词性: verb, noun

blether:/ˈblɛðər/ (UK), /ˈblɛðər/ (US)| n.|闲聊，胡扯；废话|Meaningless talk or nonsense.|例句：His speech was just a load of blether. 他的演讲只是一堆废话。

例句：Don't listen to his blether; he doesn't know what he's talking about.  别听他的胡扯，他都不知道自己在说什么。|近义词：nonsense, rubbish, gibberish|反义词：sense, truth|词性: noun
