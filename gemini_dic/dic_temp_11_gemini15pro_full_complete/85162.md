hardwire:/ˈhɑːrdwaɪər/ (US) /ˈhɑːdwaɪə(r)/ (UK)| v. n. adj.|硬连线，硬接线；（本能地）使…根深蒂固|To connect something directly to a computer or other electronic device by wire instead of using a wireless connection; to make something a permanent and unchangeable part of a system or device, especially by directly connecting parts electronically; genetically or innately determined.|

例句：
The keyboard is hardwired to the computer.  键盘直接连接到电脑上。

The program is hardwired into the system.  这个程序被硬编码到系统中。

近义词：integrate, embed, inbuilt, built-in|
反义词：disconnect, unplug, software|
词性：verb, noun, adjective


hardwired:/ˈhɑːrdwaɪər/ (US) /ˈhɑːdwaɪə(r)/ (UK)| n.|硬连线，硬件连接|A physical connection made using wires.|

例句：
The hardwiring in this building is outdated. 这栋楼里的线路已经过时了。

The system relies on a complex hardwiring system.  该系统依赖于复杂的硬件连接系统。

近义词：wiring, connection|
反义词：wireless connection, software|
词性：noun


hardwired:/ˈhɑːrdwaɪər/ (US) /ˈhɑːdwaɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|硬连线的；（本能地）根深蒂固的|Genetically or innately determined; permanently connected by wires.|

例句：
Humans are hardwired to seek connection.  人类天生就渴望与他人建立联系。

The system has a hardwired security feature. 该系统具有硬连线的安全功能。

近义词：innate, inherent, built-in|
反义词：learned, acquired, flexible|
词性：adjective


词源：From "hard" + "wire," referring to the physical connection of wires in electronic devices.  由"hard"和"wire"组成，指的是电子设备中电线的物理连接。
