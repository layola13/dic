carbonizers:/ˈkɑːrbənaɪzərz/
n.
碳化器，炭化装置
A device or facility used to carbonize organic materials.
The factory uses large carbonizers to produce charcoal. 这家工厂使用大型碳化器生产木炭。

carbonizers:/ˈkɑːrbənaɪzərz/
n.
碳化剂
A substance used to carbonize something.
Certain chemicals can act as carbonizers in specific industrial processes. 某些化学物质可以在特定的工业过程中充当碳化剂。


Synonyms:
chargers, carbonizing units, converters (depending on context)
Antonyms:
(No direct antonyms.  One could consider terms like "purifiers" or "decarbonizers" in specific contexts where carbonization is being reversed or prevented.)
