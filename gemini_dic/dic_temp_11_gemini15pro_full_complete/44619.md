crowdsourcing:/ˈkraʊdsɔːrsɪŋ/
n.|众包|The practice of obtaining needed services, ideas, or content by soliciting contributions from a large group of people, especially from an online community.|The company used crowdsourcing to develop its new logo.  这家公司使用众包来设计它的新标志。|open innovation, collaborative sourcing|traditional sourcing|noun
crowdsource:/ˈkraʊdsɔːrs/
v.|众包|To obtain (information or input into a particular task or project) by enlisting the services of a number of people, either paid or unpaid, typically via the Internet.|The website crowdsourced translations from its users. 该网站从其用户那里众包翻译。|outsource, contract out|insource|verb
