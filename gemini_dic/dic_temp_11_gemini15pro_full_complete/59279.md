eagle-eyed:/ˈiːɡl aɪd/| adj.|目光敏锐的，眼尖的|Having very good eyesight and able to notice small details.  Derived from the keen eyesight attributed to eagles.|例句：The eagle-eyed editor spotted the tiny error in the manuscript. 眼尖的编辑发现了手稿中的小错误。

例句：An eagle-eyed shopper noticed the mispriced item. 一位眼尖的顾客注意到了标价错误的商品。

近义词：sharp-eyed, observant, perceptive, vigilant|反义词:unobservant, inattentive|词性:形容词
