deists:/ˈdiːɪsts/ | n. | 自然神论者 | People who believe in the existence of a supreme being, specifically of a creator who does not intervene in the universe.  They believe that God created the universe and then left it to run according to natural laws. | Many of the Founding Fathers of the United States were deists. 美国的许多开国元勋都是自然神论者。|  The deists believed in reason and observation as the primary sources of religious knowledge. 自然神论者相信理性和观察是宗教知识的主要来源。 | synonyms: freethinkers, rationalists | antonyms: theists, atheists | noun
