entreatingly:/ɪnˈtriːtɪŋli/ | adv. | 恳求地，祈求地 | In a way that shows you are asking someone to do something in a sincere and emotional way.  |  "Please, don't go," she said entreatingly.  “求你了，别走，”她恳求道。 | 近义词: pleadingly, beseechingly, imploringly | 反义词: demandingly, imperiously | 词性: 副词

entreat:/ɪnˈtriːt/ | v. | 恳求，请求 | To ask someone earnestly or anxiously to do something. |  He entreated her to stay. 他恳求她留下。 | 近义词: beg, implore, beseech | 反义词: demand, command | 词性: 动词
