brigaded:/brɪˈɡeɪdɪd/
verb|被组成旅的，被编成队的|formed into a brigade or treated as a brigade.  Organized for a particular task, especially political activism online.|The comments section was brigaded by angry users.|Synonyms: organized, mobilized, assembled|Antonyms: dispersed, scattered|v.
