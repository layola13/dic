mammer:/ˈmæmər/| vi.|支支吾吾，结结巴巴地说；犹豫，踌躇|To speak hesitantly and indistinctly; to be hesitant or uncertain in action or speech. | He mammered an apology, unable to meet her eyes. 他结结巴巴地道歉，不敢看她的眼睛。|Synonyms: stammer, stutter, hesitate, falter|Antonyms: articulate, pronounce, declare, assert|Verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mameren*, frequentative of *mamen* "to mumble," probably of imitative origin.
