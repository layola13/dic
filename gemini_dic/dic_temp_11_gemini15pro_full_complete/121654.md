misphrased:/ˌmɪsˈfreɪzd/
adjective|表达错误的，措辞不当的|expressed inaccurately or awkwardly|Her question was misphrased, leading to confusion. 她的问题措辞不当，导致了混乱。|synonyms: poorly worded, inapt, awkward, clumsy|antonyms: well-phrased, articulate, eloquent
word origin:  "mis-" (wrongly) + "phrase" (to express in words) The prefix "mis-" is of Old English and Germanic origin, meaning "wrongly" or "badly." "Phrase" comes from the Late Latin "phrasis" and the Greek "phrasis," meaning "diction," "style," or "expression."
