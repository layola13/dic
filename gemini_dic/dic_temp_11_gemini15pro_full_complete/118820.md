metanauplius:/ˌmɛtəˈnɔːpliəs/
noun
后无节幼体
The second free-swimming larval stage of many crustaceans, following the nauplius.
The metanauplius stage is characterized by the addition of abdominal segments.
近义词:  nauplius larva
反义词:  adult
