grimier:/ˈɡraɪmiər/ (US), /ˈɡrɪmiə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更脏的，更油腻的|Comparative form of grimy: more grimy.  Covered with or characterized by grime; dirtier.|例句：The kitchen floor was grimier than usual after the party.  派对结束后，厨房的地板比平时更脏了。|例句：His hands were grimier after working on the car.  修完车后，他的手更脏了。|近义词：dirtier, filthier, grubbier|反义词：cleaner, purer, spotless|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From grime + -ier, comparative suffix.  Grime itself comes from Middle English grym, meaning "dirt, filth,"  possibly related to Old Norse grima "mask, visor" (perhaps referring to something that obscures).
