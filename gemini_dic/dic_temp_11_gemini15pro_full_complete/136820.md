nonsibilancies: /ˌnɒnsɪˈbɪlənsiz/ /ˌnɑːnsɪˈbɪlənsiz/
noun
非咝擦音
Sounds that are not sibilants.  Non-sibilants are all the other consonants and vowels.
The word "plot" contains only nonsibilancies.  这个单词“plot”只包含非咝擦音。
近义词: non-sibilants
反义词: sibilants
词性: n.
