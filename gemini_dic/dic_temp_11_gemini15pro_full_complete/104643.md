karate: /kəˈrɑːti/ /kəˈræti/ | n. | 空手道 | A Japanese martial art whose physical aspects involve powerful striking techniques, such as punching, kicking, knee strikes, elbow strikes and open hand techniques such as knife-hands, spear-hands, and palm-heel strikes. |  He has a black belt in karate. 他是空手道黑带。

| 近义词: martial arts, self-defense | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps pacifism) | 词性: noun


karate: /kəˈrɑːti/ /kəˈræti/ | n. | 空手道 |  A system of unarmed combat, similar to judo but emphasizing striking rather than grappling. | She uses karate for self-defense. 她用空手道进行自卫。
