intertouch:/ˌɪntərˈtʌtʃ/ | v. |互相接触，互相影响| To have a mutual influence or connection; to interact and affect each other. | The two cultures intertouch and enrich each other. 这两种文化相互影响，互相丰富。|  The company's departments intertouch on several projects. 该公司的各个部门在几个项目上互相合作。 | 近义词：interact, interrelate, connect, influence | 反义词：isolate, separate | 词性: 动词

词源:  "inter-" (between) + "touch" (to come into contact with).  The prefix "inter-" indicates a reciprocal action between entities.
