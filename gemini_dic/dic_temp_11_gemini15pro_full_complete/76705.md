gambet:/ˈɡæmbɪt/ | n. | 赌注；策略；开局 | An opening move in chess in which a piece, usually a pawn, is sacrificed to gain an advantage in position.  A remark made in order to start a conversation or as a way of attracting someone's attention.  Something risked in order to achieve an advantage. | 例句1：His gambit of resigning from the board forced the company to reconsider its strategy. 他辞去董事会成员的策略迫使公司重新考虑其战略。 例句2：She tried a conversational gambit, asking him about his work. 她尝试了一个对话策略，询问他的工作。 | 近义词：ploy, tactic, maneuver, stratagem, sacrifice, risk | 反义词：safety, security | 词性：名词
