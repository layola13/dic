microfracture:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌfræktʃər/| n.|微骨折|A surgical technique in which tiny holes are drilled into the bone near a damaged joint to stimulate the growth of new cartilage.|The athlete underwent microfracture surgery to repair his damaged knee.  这位运动员接受了微骨折手术来修复他受损的膝盖。|Synonyms: subchondral drilling, cartilage regeneration procedure|Antonyms: N/A|noun
