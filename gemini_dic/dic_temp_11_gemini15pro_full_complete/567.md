abortiveness:/əˈbɔːrtɪvnəs/
n.
流产，失败，夭折
The state or quality of being abortive; failure to achieve a desired end.
The abortiveness of the peace talks led to renewed conflict. 和谈的失败导致了冲突的重新爆发。
近义词:failure, futility, fruitlessness
反义词:success, effectiveness, productivity
词性:名词

词源:
From abortive + -ness.  Abortive comes from the Latin *abortivus*, from abortus, past participle of aboriri "to miscarry," from ab- "away, from" + oriri "to arise, be born."
