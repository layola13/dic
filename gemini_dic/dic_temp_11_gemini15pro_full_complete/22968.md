bottomry: /ˈbɒtəmrɪ/ (UK) /ˈbɑːtəmri/ (US)
| n. | 海底抵押借款；船舶抵押借款 | A loan secured by a ship or its cargo, repayable only if the voyage is completed successfully. |  The ship's captain resorted to bottomry to finance repairs in a foreign port. 船长依靠海底抵押借款在外国港口进行维修。| synonym: maritime loan, respondentia (when the cargo is the security) | antonym:  N/A (a direct antonym is difficult to define, perhaps unsecured loan) | noun
