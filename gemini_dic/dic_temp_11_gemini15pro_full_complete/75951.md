funiculars:/fjuːˈnɪkjələrz/
|n.|缆索铁路；缆车|A cable railway, especially one on a mountainside, in which ascending and descending cars are counterbalanced.|We took the funicular up the steep hillside. 我们乘坐缆车上了陡峭的山坡。|近义词：cable car, incline railway|反义词:|noun


funicular:/fjuːˈnɪkjələr/
|adj.|缆索的；用缆索拉动的|Of or relating to a funicular railway.|The funicular system is very efficient. 缆索铁路系统非常高效。|近义词：cable-operated|反义词:|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *funiculus* (diminutive of *funis* "rope, cord") + -ar.
