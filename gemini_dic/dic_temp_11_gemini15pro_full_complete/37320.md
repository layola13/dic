commemoration:/kəˌmɛməˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 纪念，庆祝 |The act of remembering a person or event, especially with a ceremony. |  A special service was held in commemoration of the victims of the disaster. 为纪念灾难的受害者举行了一场特别的追悼会。| remembrance, celebration, observance | oblivion, forgetting | noun


commemorate:/kəˈmɛməˌreɪt/ | v. | 纪念，庆祝 | To remember officially and publicly a person or event. | They commemorated the fiftieth anniversary of the end of the war.  他们庆祝了战争结束五十周年。| celebrate, observe, honor | ignore, forget, neglect | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin commemoratio, from Latin commemorare "call to mind, recount, relate," from com- "together" + memorare "to bring to remembrance," from memor "mindful."
