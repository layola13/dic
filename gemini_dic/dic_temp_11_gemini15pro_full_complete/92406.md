hypogastric:/ˌhaɪpəˈɡæstrɪk/
adjective
下腹的；腹下部的
Relating to the lower central region of the abdomen.
The patient complained of hypogastric pain. 病人抱怨下腹部疼痛。

The hypogastric artery supplies blood to the pelvic organs. 腹下动脉为盆腔器官供血。

近义词:lower abdominal, infraumbilical
反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider cephalic or cranial for opposite body region)
词性:adjective
