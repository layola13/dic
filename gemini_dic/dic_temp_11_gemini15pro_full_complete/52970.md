dingos:/ˈdɪŋɡoʊz/ (US), /ˈdɪŋɡəʊz/ (UK)| n.|澳洲野狗|Wild dogs native to Australia, known for their sandy-yellow coat and social pack behavior. They are considered a unique species, separate from domestic dogs.|Example 1: Dingos are often seen hunting kangaroos in packs. 澳洲野狗经常成群猎捕袋鼠。

Example 2: The dingo's howl is a haunting sound in the Australian outback. 澳洲野狗的嚎叫在澳大利亚内陆是一种令人难忘的声音。|近义词：wild dog, feral dog|反义词:domestic dog|noun
