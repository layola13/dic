bonnie:/ˈbɒni/ (UK), /ˈbɑːni/ (US)| adj.|美丽的，漂亮的；健康的，健壮的|Scottish, originally meaning 'pretty', 'attractive', 'healthy-looking'. From the French word "bon" meaning "good". It's often used to describe women, children, or landscapes.|例句：She had a bonnie wee lass. 她有一个漂亮的女儿。

例句：The bonnie hills of Scotland. 苏格兰美丽的山丘。|近义词：beautiful, pretty, attractive, lovely, fair, handsome|反义词：ugly, unattractive, plain, homely|词性:形容词
