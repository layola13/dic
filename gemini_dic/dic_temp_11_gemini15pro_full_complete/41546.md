coronium:/kəˈroʊniəm/ | n. | 冠冕元素（曾经被认为是太阳日冕中的一种元素）| A hypothetical element, once believed to exist in the sun's corona, responsible for certain spectral lines. These lines were later found to originate from highly ionized iron (Fe XIV). |  Astronomers once believed coronium was a new element. 天文学家曾认为冠冕元素是一种新元素。

| Synonym: none (as it doesn't exist)| Antonym: none (as it doesn't exist) | noun
