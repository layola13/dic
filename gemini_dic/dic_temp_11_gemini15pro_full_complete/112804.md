luce:/luːs/ /lʊs/ | n. | 光；亮；光泽；灯；(尤指窗户的)窗格；(尤指鱼的)晶状体 | A source of light, especially a window or pane of glass in a window or the transparent part of the eye of a fish. | The luce streamed through the stained glass, illuminating the dust motes dancing in the air.  光透过彩色玻璃照射进来，照亮了空中飞舞的尘埃。| Synonym: light, gleam, glow, pane, window | Antonym: darkness, shadow, obscurity | noun


luce:/luːs/ /lʊs/ | n. |  梭子鱼；狗鱼 | A pike (Esox lucius). | The fisherman landed a huge luce, its teeth glinting in the sun. 渔夫钓到一条巨大的梭子鱼，它的牙齿在阳光下闪闪发光。| Synonym: pike | Antonym: N/A | noun
