budgie:/ˈbʌdʒi/ | n. | 虎皮鹦鹉 | A small, colorful Australian parakeet (Melopsittacus undulatus) often kept as a pet.  It's derived from the aboriginal word “budgerigar,” meaning “good food” or “good bird”. | I taught my budgie to say "hello." 我教我的虎皮鹦鹉说“你好”。 | synonym: parakeet,  | antonym: (No direct antonym for a specific bird species) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Kamilaroi/Gamilaraay *budgery, meaning "good", and *gah, meaning "cockatoo". Shortened to budgerigar, then further shortened to budgie.
