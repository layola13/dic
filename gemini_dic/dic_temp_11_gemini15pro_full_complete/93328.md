ill-treated:/ˌɪlˈtriːtɪd/
adjective
受虐待的；被苛待的
Treated badly or cruelly.
The ill-treated child was finally rescued by social services.  这个受虐待的孩子最终被社会服务机构救助了。
The rescue center is full of ill-treated animals.  救助中心满是受虐待的动物。
近义词:abused, mistreated, maltreated
反义词:well-treated, cherished
词性:adjective
