antifundamentalisms:/ˌæntiˌfʌndəˈmɛntəlɪzəmz/
n.
反基要主义，反原教旨主义
Opposition to religious fundamentalism, which is a belief in the strict, literal interpretation of scripture.  It often encompasses a range of social and political perspectives that challenge fundamentalist interpretations of religion and their influence on public life.
The rise of antifundamentalisms in the region is a reaction against strict religious interpretations.  该地区反基要主义的兴起是对严格宗教解释的反应。
Secular humanism is sometimes seen as a form of antifundamentalism. 世俗人文主义有时被视为一种反基要主义的形式。
近义词:secularism, liberalism, modernism
反义词:fundamentalism, orthodoxy, traditionalism
词性:名词
