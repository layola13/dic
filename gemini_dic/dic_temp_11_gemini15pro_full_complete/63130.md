ensnare:/ɪnˈsneər/ | v. | 诱捕，使陷入圈套 | To catch or trap something, often by trickery or deceit. |  The spider ensnared a fly in its web. 蜘蛛用网捕获了一只苍蝇。

The con man ensnared several elderly people in his elaborate scheme. 骗子用他精心设计的诡计诱捕了几位老人。

| 近义词：trap, catch, entangle, snare | 反义词：release, free, liberate | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From en- (meaning "in" or "into") + snare (a trap for catching birds or animals).  Ultimately from Old English snar.
