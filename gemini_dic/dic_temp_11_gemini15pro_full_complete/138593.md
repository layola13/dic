nubigenous:/njuːˈbɪdʒənəs/ | adj. | 云生的，由云产生的 |  Cloud-born; produced by clouds.  Specifically refers to the formation of precipitation within clouds. |  The nubigenous nature of hailstorms fascinates meteorologists.  云生的冰雹的性质让气象学家着迷。 | 近义词: nephrogenic | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider "terrestrial" or "surface-formed") | 词性: 形容词
