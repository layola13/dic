mass-product:/ˈmæs ˈprɒdʌkt/ (US) /ˈmæs ˈprɒdʌkt/ (UK)| n.| 大量生产的产品，批量生产的产品 |A product manufactured in large quantities, often using standardized designs and assembly-line techniques. |  The factory churns out mass-produced plastic toys.  这家工厂大量生产塑料玩具。 | synonyms: mass-produced goods, manufactured goods, factory-made goods | antonyms: handcrafted goods, bespoke products, custom-made products | noun

mass-produce:/ˌmæs prəˈdjuːs/ (US) /ˌmæs prəˈdʒuːs/ (UK)| v.| 大量生产，批量生产 | To manufacture (a product) in large quantities, often using standardized designs and assembly-line techniques. | The company mass-produces inexpensive clothing. 这家公司大量生产廉价服装。 | synonyms: manufacture in bulk, produce in large quantities, churn out | antonyms: handcraft, custom-make | verb
