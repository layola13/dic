localizable:/ˌloʊkəˈlaɪzəbl/| adj.|可本地化的，可局部化的|Able to be adapted to local conditions or translated into another language.|The software is localizable into multiple languages.  这款软件可以本地化成多种语言。|The error messages are fully localizable.  这些错误信息可以完全本地化。|近义词:translatable, adaptable|反义词:non-localizable, untranslatable|词性:adjective
localise:/ˈloʊkəˌlaɪz/| v.|本地化，局部化|To adapt something to a particular place or market, or to confine something to a particular area.|We need to localize our marketing campaign for the Asian market. 我们需要针对亚洲市场将我们的营销活动本地化。|The infection was localized to a small area. 感染被局限在一个小区域。|近义词:adapt, confine, restrict|反义词:globalize, spread|词性:verb
localization:/ˌloʊkələˈzeɪʃn/| n.|本地化，局部化|The process of adapting something to a particular place or market, or the state of being confined to a particular area.|The localization of the software took several months.  该软件的本地化工作花了几个月的时间。|The localization of the pain helped the doctor make a diagnosis.  疼痛的局部化帮助医生做出了诊断。|近义词:adaptation, confinement, restriction|反义词:globalization, spreading|词性:noun

词源: From the verb "localize," which comes from the French word "localiser" and ultimately from the Latin word "locus" meaning "place."
