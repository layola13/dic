nettier:/ˈnɛtiər/
adjective
更整洁的，更整齐的，更好的
comparative form of 'netty': more netty; neater, tidier, better.
Her handwriting is nettier than mine. 她的字迹比我的整洁。
Synonyms: neater, tidier, more organized
Antonyms: messier, dirtier, more disorganized
词性: 形容词
