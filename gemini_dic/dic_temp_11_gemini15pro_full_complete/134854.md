nonmodernnesses:/ˌnɒnˌmɒdərnɪsnɪsɪz/ /ˌnɑːnˈmɑːdərnɪsnɪsɪz/
noun
非现代性（通常指复数形式）
The state or quality of not being modern; a collection of qualities or characteristics that are not considered modern.
The museum's collection highlights the nonmodernnesses of pre-industrial societies.  这家博物馆的藏品突出了前工业化社会的非现代性。
The artist intentionally incorporated nonmodernnesses into their work, rejecting contemporary trends.  这位艺术家有意地在他们的作品中融入了非现代性，拒绝当代潮流。
Synonyms: archaisms, traditionalism, antiquities
Antonyms: modernities, contemporary trends, innovations
