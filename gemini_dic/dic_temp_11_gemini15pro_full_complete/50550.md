depute:/dɪˈpjuːt/ / /dɪˈpjut/ | vt. n.| 委托；代理人；副手 | To appoint someone as a deputy or representative; a person appointed to act as a substitute for another. |  The manager deputed his assistant to attend the meeting. 经理委托他的助手参加会议。| The president deputed her to sign the treaty. 总统委托她签署条约。 | Synonyms: delegate, commission, authorize, assign, appoint, entrust | Antonyms: retain, keep, withhold | verb
