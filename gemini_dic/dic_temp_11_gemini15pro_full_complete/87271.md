hermaphrodism:/hɜːrˈmæfrədɪzəm/ (US) /hɜːˈmæfrədɪzəm/ (UK)| n. |雌雄同体，两性畸形|The condition of having both male and female reproductive organs.|Hermaphrodism is rare in mammals. 哺乳动物中雌雄同体现象很少见。|Synonyms: intersex, bisexuality (in older usage)|Antonyms: unisexuality, gonochorism|noun


hermaphrodite:/hɜːrˈmæfrədaɪt/ (US) /hɜːˈmæfrədaɪt/ (UK)| n. adj. |雌雄同体者，两性体|An organism that has both male and female reproductive organs.|The earthworm is a hermaphrodite. 蚯蚓是雌雄同体的。|Synonyms: intersex individual, bisexual (in older usage)|Antonyms: unisexual organism, gonochoristic individual|noun, adjective
