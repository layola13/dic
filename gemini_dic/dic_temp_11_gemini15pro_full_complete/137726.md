nonvagrancy:/ˌnɒnˈveɪɡrənsi/
noun
非流浪状态，定居状态
The state of not being vagrant; not wandering from place to place without a home or regular employment.
His nonvagrancy was proven by his lease agreement and utility bills. 他的租房协议和水电费账单证明了他并非流浪汉。
non-vagrancy, settled status, residence,  domicile
vagrancy, homelessness, wandering
