-ism:/ɪzəm/
noun
主义; 主张; 学说
A suffix forming nouns denoting action or practice, state or condition, principles, doctrines, a usage or characteristic, etc.
例句：
Marxism is a social, political, and economic theory originated by Karl Marx.
马克思主义是由卡尔·马克思创立的一种社会、政治和经济理论。

Capitalism is an economic system based on private ownership of the means of production and their operation for profit.
资本主义是一种建立在生产资料私有制及其以营利为目的的经济制度。

近义词：doctrine, philosophy, ideology, belief
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，取决于具体的-ism)
