hothouse:/ˈhɑːthaʊs/ /ˈhɒthaʊs/| n. adj.|温室，温床；（促使事物快速发展的）环境|A heated building, usually made of glass, for growing plants.|例句：The orchids thrived in the hothouse. 兰花在温室里茁壮成长。

例句：They grow tomatoes in hothouses. 他们在温室里种植西红柿。


|近义词：greenhouse, conservatory|反义词:cold frame|词性:名词/形容词


hothouse:/ˈhɑːthaʊs/ /ˈhɒthaʊs/| adj.|温室的，（气氛）紧张的，（感情）过分热烈的|Relating to or characteristic of a hothouse; artificially stimulating or intense.|例句：The hothouse atmosphere of the office was stifling. 办公室里紧张的气氛令人窒息。

例句：He grew up in the hothouse world of competitive tennis. 他在竞争激烈的网球温室世界中长大。

|近义词：forced, intense, heated|反义词:natural, relaxed|词性:形容词
