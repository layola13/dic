outreigned:/ˌaʊtˈreɪnd/
verb
统治比…更久；比…在位时间长
ruled longer than; reigned for a longer period than
Queen Victoria outreigned all previous British monarchs. 维多利亚女王比之前所有英国君主的统治时间都长。
outlasted, survived
predeceased
