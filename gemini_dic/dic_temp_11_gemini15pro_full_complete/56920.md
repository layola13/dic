dotehead: /ˈdoʊthed/ | n. | 痴情汉；溺爱者；糊涂蛋 | A person who is excessively or foolishly fond of someone; a foolish or infatuated person. | He's a complete dotehead over his new girlfriend. 他对他的新女友完全是一个痴情汉。 | 近义词：fool, idiot, simpleton, sucker | 反义词：cynic, skeptic | 词性：名词

词源:  From *dote* (to be excessively fond) + *head*.
