disputes:/dɪˈspjuːts/
n.|争议，纠纷|A disagreement or argument about something important.|The two countries have been in a long-running dispute over territory. 这两个国家长期以来一直在领土问题上存在争议。|
近义词:arguments, disagreements, quarrels, conflicts|反义词:agreements, accords, harmonies|词性:名词

disputes:/dɪˈspjuːts/
v.|质疑，否认；争论，辩论|To argue about something; to say that something is not true or correct.|He disputed the judge's decision. 他对法官的裁决提出了质疑。|
近义词:challenge, question, contest, debate|反义词:accept, concede, admit|词性:动词
