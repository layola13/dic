heraldry:/ˈhɛrəldri/| n.|纹章学；纹章；家徽|The study of coats of arms and other heraldic bearings. It also refers to the practice of devising, granting, and blazoning arms, and to the system of hereditary symbols of rank or family.|The college boasts a rich history of heraldry, with its own unique coat of arms.  这所学院以其丰富的纹章历史和独特的盾徽而自豪。|Synonyms: blazonry, armory|Antonyms: N/A|noun
