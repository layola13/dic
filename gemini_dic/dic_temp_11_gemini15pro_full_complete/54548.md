dislink:/dɪsˈlɪŋk/
verb
断开连接；拆开
to disconnect or separate something from something else
The company decided to dislink the two departments.  该公司决定将这两个部门分开。
The new policy will dislink pay from performance.  新政策将把工资与绩效脱钩。
近义词: disconnect, detach, separate, unlink
反义词: connect, link, join, attach
词性:v.
