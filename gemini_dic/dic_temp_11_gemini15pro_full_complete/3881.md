agree:/əˈɡriː/ | vi. vt.| 同意，赞成；一致；适合|To have the same opinion or feeling as someone else; to say that you will do something that someone else wants or suggests.|We agreed to meet on Sunday. 我们同意星期天见面。|Synonyms: concur, consent, assent|Antonyms: disagree, dissent, object|verb

agree:/əˈɡriː/ | vi.| 一致；相符；适合|To be the same as something else, or to match it.|Your story doesn't agree with hers. 你的说法与她的说法不一致。|Synonyms: correspond, conform, coincide|Antonyms: differ, conflict, clash|verb

agree:/əˈɡriː/ | vi.| (气候、食物)适合|To be suitable for someone's health or digestion.|Spicy food doesn't agree with me. 辛辣食物不适合我。|Synonyms: suit, be good for|Antonyms: disagree with, upset|verb
