insalubriously: /ˌɪnsəˈlubriəsli/ | adv. | 不健康地；有害地 | in a way that is not healthy, especially because of bad air or unhealthy living conditions |  Living insalubriously can lead to various health problems.  不健康的生活方式会导致各种健康问题。 | The miners worked insalubriously in the dark, damp tunnels. 矿工们在黑暗潮湿的隧道里从事着有害健康的工作。 | 近义词: unhealthily, harmfully, detrimentally | 反义词: healthily, wholesomely | 词性: 副词

词源: From Latin *insalubris* "unhealthy" (from *in-* "not" + *salubris* "healthy") + *-ō* (adverbial suffix).
