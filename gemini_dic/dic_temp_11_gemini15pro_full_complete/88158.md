hillier:/ˈhɪliər/| adj.|更丘陵的，坡度更大的|Having more hills; more steep.|The road became hillier as we drove further into the countryside.  我们越往乡下开，路就越陡峭。|近义词：steeper, more mountainous|反义词: flatter, less hilly|词性:形容词

Etymology: From hill + -ier (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *hulniz.
