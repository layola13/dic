narrowed:/ˈnæroud/| v. adj.|变窄的，缩小的|Past participle of narrow.  Made less wide or broad; reduced in scope or extent. |The road narrowed as it climbed the mountain. 道路随着上山而变窄了。|近义词：restricted, limited, reduced, constricted|反义词: widened, broadened, expanded|词性:动词(过去分词), 形容词


Etymology:
From Old English *nearwe, from Proto-Germanic *narwaz.  Related to Old Norse nær "near, close."  The original sense was "close, near," later developing the sense of "limited in space."
