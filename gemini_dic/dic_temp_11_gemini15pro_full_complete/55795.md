diverticulum:/ˌdaɪvərˈtɪkjʊləm/ /ˌdaɪvərˈtɪkjʊləm/ | n. |憩室|A small pouch, or sac, that bulges outward through a weak spot in the wall of an organ, such as the colon.|He was diagnosed with diverticulitis, an inflammation of the diverticula in his colon.|近义词: pouch, sac |反义詞: None |词性: Noun

diverticula:/ˌdaɪvərˈtɪkjʊlə/ /ˌdaɪvərˈtɪkjʊlə/ | n. |憩室（复数）|The plural form of diverticulum.|She has multiple diverticula in her large intestine.|近义词: pouches, sacs |反义詞: None |词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dīverticulum*, from *dēvertere* "to turn aside," from *dē-* "away" + *vertere* "to turn."
