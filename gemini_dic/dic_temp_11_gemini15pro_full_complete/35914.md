cocreating:/ˌkəʊkriˈeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 共同创造 | The act of creating something together with others. | Example: We are cocreating a new product with our customers. 我们正在与客户共同创造新产品。| Example:  They were cocreating a performance piece that blended music and dance. 他们正在共同创作一个融合了音乐和舞蹈的表演作品。 | Synonyms: collaborating, co-producing, jointly creating | Antonyms: independently creating, solo creation | verb

cocreation:/ˌkəʊkriˈeɪʃn/ | n. | 共同创造 | The process of jointly creating something. | Example: The project was a successful example of cocreation between the company and its users. 这个项目是公司与其用户成功共同创造的典范。| Example:  Cocreation is becoming increasingly important in the development of new products and services. 共同创造在新产品和服务的开发中变得越来越重要。 | Synonyms: collaboration, co-production, joint creation | Antonyms: independent creation, solo creation | noun
