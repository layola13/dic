headquarters:/ˈhedˌkwɔːrtərz/ (US), /ˈhedˈkwɔːtəz/ (UK)| n. |总部；司令部；指挥中心|The main administrative office or center of operations for an organization. |The company's headquarters is located in New York City.| synonyms: head office, main office, center of operations, command center | antonyms: branch, satellite office | noun

headquarters:/ˈhedˌkwɔːrtərz/ (US), /ˈhedˈkwɔːtəz/ (UK)| n. | (用作单数或复数) 总部人员；司令部人员|The personnel stationed at a headquarters. |Headquarters has decided to launch a new marketing campaign. | synonyms: staff, personnel, command | antonyms: field officers, field workers | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the early 19th century.  Formed from *head* + *quarter(s)*, originally referring to lodging assigned to a soldier or officer.  Later broadened to mean the location from which a military unit or organization is commanded.
