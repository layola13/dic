foley:/ˈfoʊli/ (US) /ˈfəʊli/ (UK)| n.|拟音，音效|The reproduction of everyday sound effects that are added to films, videos, and other media in post-production.  These reproduced sounds can be anything from the rustling of clothing and footsteps to squeaky doors and breaking glass.|The foley artist spent hours creating the perfect sound of crunching snow.  拟音师花了几个小时来制作完美的嘎吱嘎吱的雪地声。|synonyms: sound effects, audio effects|antonyms: silence, quiet|noun

foley:/ˈfoʊli/ (US) /ˈfəʊli/ (UK)| adj.|拟音的|Relating to or used in the creation of foley sound effects.|The foley stage was equipped with a variety of surfaces and props.  拟音舞台配备了各种各样的表面和道具。|synonyms: sound effect, audio effect|antonyms: N/A|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
Named after Jack Foley (1891-1967), a Hollywood sound-effects artist who developed many of the techniques still used today.
