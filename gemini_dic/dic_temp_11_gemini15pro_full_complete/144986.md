overacting: /ˌoʊvərˈæktɪŋ/ | n. | 过度表演，夸张的表演 | The act of performing in an exaggerated or excessively dramatic manner. |  The actor's overacting ruined the scene.  这个演员的过度表演毁了这场戏。 | 近义词：exaggerating, hamming it up | 反义词：underacting, subtlety | 词性: noun

overact: /ˌoʊvərˈækt/ | v. |  过度表演，表演得过火 | To perform in an exaggerated or excessively dramatic manner. | He tends to overact when he's nervous. 他紧张的时候容易表演过火。 | 近义词：exaggerate, ham it up | 反义词：underact | 词性: verb
