cuneiformist:/ˌkjuːniˈfɔːrmɪst/ | n. |楔形文字学家| A scholar specializing in the study of cuneiform writing. | Example: The cuneiformist deciphered the ancient tablet.  这位楔形文字学家破译了这块古代石板。| Example:  The cuneiformist spent years studying the intricacies of Akkadian script. 这位楔形文字学家花了数年时间研究阿卡德文字的复杂性。 | 近义词: Assyriologist, epigrapher | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) |词性: noun

cuneiform:/ˈkjuːnɪfɔːrm/ | adj. n. |楔形的; 楔形文字| Relating to or denoting the wedge-shaped characters used in the ancient writing systems of Mesopotamia, Persia, and Ugarit. Also, the writing system itself. | Example: Cuneiform script was used to record a variety of languages. 楔形文字被用来记录各种语言。| Example: The museum displayed a collection of cuneiform tablets.  博物馆展出了一批楔形文字泥板。| 近义词: wedge-shaped | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) |词性: adjective, noun
