catapetalous: /ˌkætəˈpɛtələs/
adjective
下瓣的；花瓣下垂的
Having petals which are directed downwards.
The rare orchid was identified as a catapetalous species due to its drooping petals.  这种稀有的兰花因其下垂的花瓣被鉴定为下瓣的物种。
The botanist sketched the catapetalous flower, noting the unique downward curve of its petals. 植物学家画了这朵下瓣的花的草图，并注明了其花瓣独特的向下曲线。
近义词:  pendulous, drooping
反义词:  erect, upright
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From cata- (Greek, 'down') + petal (from Greek 'petalon', a leaf).
