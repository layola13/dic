minging:/ˈmaɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 糟糕的; 令人讨厌的; 极差的 | Extremely unpleasant, bad, or of poor quality.  Origin: British slang, likely originating in the early 21st century.  It's thought to be related to the unpleasantness associated with mining. | That movie was absolutely minging. 那部电影真是烂透了。 | The food at that restaurant was minging. 那家餐馆的食物难吃极了。 | 近义词: awful, terrible, dreadful, horrible, disgusting | 反义词: excellent, wonderful, fantastic, great | 词性: 形容词
