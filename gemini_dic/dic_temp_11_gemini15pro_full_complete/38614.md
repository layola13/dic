concretion:/kənˈkriːʃən/ | n. | 凝结; 凝结物; 结石 | a hard solid mass formed by the local accumulation of matter, especially within the body or within a mass of earth or rock. | Kidney stones are concretions of mineral salts. 肾结石是矿物盐的凝结物。|  solidification, coagulation, petrifaction | dissolution, liquefaction | noun

concretion:/kənˈkriːʃən/ | n. |  具体化; 具体物 | the act or process of becoming concrete; a concrete thing |  The concretion of his ideas into a solid plan took several months.  他的想法具体化为一个坚实的计划花了几个月的时间。 | materialization, embodiment, realization | abstraction | noun
