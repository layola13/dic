antimonopolists:/ˌæntiɪməˈnɒpəlɪsts/ | n. | 反垄断者 |  People who advocate for or enforce laws and regulations designed to prevent monopolies and promote competition in the market. |  The antimonopolists argued that the merger would stifle competition. 反垄断者认为，这次合并会扼杀竞争。| synonyms: trust busters, competitors, regulators | antonyms: monopolists, oligopolists | Noun
