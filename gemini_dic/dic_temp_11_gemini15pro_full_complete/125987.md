muskiness:/ˈmʌskɪnɪs/| n. |麝香味；麝香气|A smell or flavor resembling musk.|The room was filled with a cloying muskiness.|近义词：musky odor, musky scent|反义词: fragrance, sweetness|词性: noun
musky:/ˈmʌski/| adj. |麝香的；有麝香气味的|Having a smell or flavor resembling musk.|The old books had a musky smell.|近义词：aromatic, fragrant|反义词: odorless, unscented|词性: adjective
