disinheritances:/ˌdɪsɪnˈherɪtənsɪz/
n.
剥夺继承权；取消继承资格
The act of preventing someone from inheriting property or money.
His disinheritance left him with nothing. 他的继承权被剥夺，一无所有。

近义词:exclusion from inheritance, deprivation of inheritance
反义词:inheritance, bequeathal
词性:名词(plural)
