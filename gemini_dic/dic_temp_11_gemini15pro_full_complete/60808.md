electropainting:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈpeɪntɪŋ/
n.|电泳涂装，电泳涂漆|A process of painting in which the object to be painted is immersed in a bath of paint and an electric current is passed through the bath, causing the paint to adhere to the object.|The car bodies are coated using electropainting.| 近义词: electrodeposition, electrophoretic painting|反义词: N/A|词性: 名词

electropaint:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈpeɪnt/
v.|电泳涂漆|To coat (a metal object) with paint by electrodeposition.|The chassis parts are electropainted to prevent rust.|近义词: electrodeposit, electrophoretically paint|反义词: N/A|词性: 动词

Etymology:
electro- + painting
electro-: from Greek *ēlektron "amber" (electric charge being originally produced by rubbing amber)
painting: from Old French peint, past participle of peindre "to paint," from Latin pingere "to paint"
