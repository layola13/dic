clericals: /ˈklɛrɪkəlz/
noun
神职人员；牧师；教士；办公室人员；白领
office workers, especially those involved in general office duties such as typing, filing, and answering phones.  Often used in the plural to refer to a group of such workers. Also can refer to members of the clergy.
The company is looking to hire more clericals to handle the increased workload. 公司正在寻找更多的办公室人员来处理增加的工作量。
The clericals in the church prepared for the upcoming service. 教堂里的神职人员为即将到来的仪式做准备。
synonyms: office workers, administrative staff, support staff, clergy, priests, ministers
antonyms: executives, managers, laity (opposite of clergy)
