chitty:/ˈtʃɪti/| n.|欠条，便条，(尤指)非正式借据|A written acknowledgment of a debt, especially an informal one. |例句：He gave me a chitty for the loan. 他给了我一张借条作为贷款凭证。

例句：The chitty stated he owed £50.  欠条上写明他欠了50英镑。|近义词：note, IOU, receipt|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "payment" as it signifies the debt is settled)|词性:noun
