facsimile: /fækˈsɪməli/ | n. | 复制品，摹本；传真 | An exact copy or reproduction of something, such as a document, painting, or signature.  Also, an image of a document transmitted electronically. |  This book contains a facsimile of the original manuscript. 这本书包含了原始手稿的复制品。 | synonyms: copy, replica, duplicate, reproduction, imitation | antonyms: original | noun

facsimile: /fækˈsɪməli/ | n. | 传真（文件） | An image of a document transmitted electronically. | She sent a facsimile of the contract to the lawyer. 她把合同的传真件发给了律师。 | synonyms: fax | antonyms: original | noun
