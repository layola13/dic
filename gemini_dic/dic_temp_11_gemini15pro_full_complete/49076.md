dehydrators:/diːˈhaɪdreɪtərz/
n.
脱水器，干燥器
A machine that removes water from food to preserve it.
例句：She uses a food dehydrator to make dried fruit snacks. 她用食物脱水器制作果干零食。

例句：The dehydrators are essential for preserving the harvest. 这些脱水器对于保存收成至关重要。

近义词：dryer, desiccant
反义词：humidifier
词性：名词
