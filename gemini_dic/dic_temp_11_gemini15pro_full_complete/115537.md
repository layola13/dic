maplelike:/ˈmeɪpəlˌlaɪk/| adj.|像枫树的，枫叶似的|Resembling a maple tree or its leaves in shape, form, or color.|例句：The leaves of the sweetgum tree are maplelike.  枫香树的叶子像枫叶。

例句：The pattern on the fabric was maplelike.  织物上的图案像枫叶。|近义词：maple-shaped, maple-leaved|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective


词源:
maple + like.  "maple" comes from Old English *mapultrēow, from mapul (maple) + trēow (tree). "like" comes from Old English gelic (similar).
