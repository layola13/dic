constrictor:/kənˈstrɪktər/ | n. | 蟒蛇；收缩肌；压缩物 | A snake that kills its prey by squeezing it tightly.  A muscle that constricts a part of the body. Something that constricts or compresses. | The anaconda is a powerful constrictor. 水蟒是一种强大的蟒蛇。 | The constrictor squeezed its prey until it suffocated.  蟒蛇紧紧地缠绕猎物直到它窒息。| synonyms: boa, python, serpent,  compressor, sphincter | antonyms: dilator, relaxant | noun
