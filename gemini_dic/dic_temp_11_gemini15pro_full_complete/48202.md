deconcentration:/ˌdiːkɒnsənˈtreɪʃən/ |n.|分散；权力下放；分权|The government initiated a policy of deconcentration, transferring some responsibilities from the capital to regional offices. 政府启动了一项分权政策，将一些职责从首都转移到地区办事处。|The deconcentration of industrial activity away from the major cities has been a key policy objective. 将工业活动从主要城市分散出去一直是一项重要的政策目标。|近义词：decentralization, devolution|反义词：centralization|noun
