holdout:/ˈhoʊldaʊt/| n.|坚持；抵抗；拒绝者；反对者|A person, group, or thing that refuses to yield or comply; a last resort.|The last holdout against the new development project finally agreed to sell their land.  最后一个反对新开发项目的钉子户最终同意出售他们的土地。|近义词：resister, objector, dissenter,refusal|反义词:conformist, compromiser|noun

hold out:/hoʊld ˈaʊt/| v.|维持；抵抗；坚持；伸出；提出|To resist or endure; to continue to be sufficient; to offer; to present.|They held out against the enemy for several weeks. 他们抵抗敌人长达数周。 |近义词：resist, endure, persevere, withstand, offer, extend|反义词:surrender, yield, give in|verb
