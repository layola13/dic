apyrexia:/ˌeɪpaɪˈrɛksiə/ /ˌeɪpaɪˈrɛkʃə/
noun
无热；退热
Absence of fever; normal body temperature.
The patient's apyrexia indicated the infection was subsiding. 病人的退烧表明感染正在消退。
The apyrexia lasted for three days.  退烧持续了三天。
近义词:afebrile, feverlessness
反义词:fever, pyrexia
词性:noun

Etymology:  From Greek *a* "without" + *pyrexia* "fever".  *Pyrexia* comes from *pyr* "fire" + *-exia* "condition".
