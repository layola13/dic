bullpen:/ˈbʊlˌpɛn/| n.| 牛棚，候补投手区，临时拘留室| A place where relief pitchers warm up before entering a baseball game; also, a small, temporary holding cell.| The manager called a pitcher from the bullpen.  经理从牛棚叫来一名投手。 The police put the suspect in the bullpen while they processed the paperwork. 警察在处理文件的时候把嫌疑人关进了临时拘留室。| synonyms: relief area, holding cell, lockup | antonyms: playing field, courtroom | noun

bullpen:/ˈbʊlˌpɛn/| n.|  （一群人）聚集地，（尤指）智囊团 | A group of people, especially a group of advisors or experts.| The governor's bullpen of advisors worked late into the night. 州长的智囊团工作到深夜。| synonyms: think tank, brain trust, group, team | antonyms: individual, loner | noun
