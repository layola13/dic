astrometeorology:/ˌæstroʊˌmiːtiərˈɒlədʒi/ | n. | 天文气象学 | The study of the supposed influence of celestial bodies on the weather.  It attempts to find correlations between astronomical phenomena and meteorological conditions. |  Astrometeorology was popular in ancient times but is now considered a pseudoscience.  天文气象学在古代很流行，但现在被认为是伪科学。 |  Some farmers still consult astrometeorological calendars for planting. 一些农民仍然参考天文气象日历来种植。| synonyms:  astro-meteorology,  | antonyms:  meteorology | noun
