mouchoir:/muˈʃwɑːr/ (UK), /ˌmuːˈʃwɑːr/ (US)| n. |手帕，手绢|A small square of fabric used for wiping one's nose or face.|She dabbed her eyes with a delicate lace mouchoir. 她用一块精致的蕾丝手帕擦了擦眼睛。|近义词：handkerchief, tissue, wiper|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From French mouchoir, from moucher "to blow the nose," from Vulgar Latin *muccāre, from Latin mucus "mucus."
