deselected:/ˌdiːsəˈlɛktɪd/
adjective|未被选中的，落选的|Not chosen or selected for a position, team, etc.|The deselected candidate expressed his disappointment. 落选的候选人表达了他的失望。|Synonyms: rejected, unchosen, eliminated|Antonyms: selected, chosen, elected|词性:形容词

deselected:/ˌdiːsəˈlɛktɪd/
verb|取消选择，使落选|To reverse the selection of something or someone; to remove from a selected group.|The committee deselected him from the team due to his poor performance. 委员会由于他表现不佳而将他从团队中除名。|Synonyms: remove, drop, reject|Antonyms: select, choose, elect|词性:动词
