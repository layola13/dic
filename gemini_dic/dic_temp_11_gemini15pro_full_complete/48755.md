definitive:/dɪˈfɪnətɪv/| adj.|最终的，决定性的，权威性的，确定的|Serving as a final settlement or decision; conclusive; authoritative and complete.|This is the definitive biography of the artist. 这是关于这位艺术家的权威传记。|近义词：conclusive, final, ultimate, decisive, authoritative|反义词：tentative, provisional, inconclusive, indefinite|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *definitivus, from Latin definitus (past participle of definire ‘to limit, define’), from de- ‘completely’ + finire ‘to finish, limit’, from finis ‘end, boundary’.
