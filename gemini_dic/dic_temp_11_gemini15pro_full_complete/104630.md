karaism:/ˈkɛərəɪzəm/| n. |卡拉派犹太教|A Jewish religious movement of the Middle Ages that rejected the authority of the Oral Torah and emphasized the sole authority of the written Torah.|Karaism is a small but enduring branch of Judaism. 卡拉派犹太教是一个规模虽小但持久的犹太教分支。|The Karaites adhere strictly to the written Torah. 卡拉派教徒严格遵守成文律法。|近义词:Sadduceeism|反义词:Rabbinic Judaism|词性:noun
