airiest:/ˈɛəriɪst/ | adj. | 最通风的，最轻盈的，最空灵的 | Lightest and most delicate; most ethereal. |  The airiest of fabrics draped her slender frame.  最轻盈的面料覆盖着她纤细的身材。 | Synonyms: light, delicate, ethereal, airy, breezy, gossamer, flimsy | Antonyms: heavy, dense, substantial, thick, stifling | adjective

Etymology: From airy + -est. Airy from air + -y.  Ultimately from Latin aer "air".
