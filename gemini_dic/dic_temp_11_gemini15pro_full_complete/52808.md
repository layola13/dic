dilly-dally:/ˈdɪli ˈdæli/| v. | 磨蹭，闲荡，浪费时间 | To waste time; to dawdle. |  例句：Stop dilly-dallying and get ready for school! 别磨蹭了，准备上学！|近义词：dawdle, procrastinate, loiter, linger|反义词:hurry, hasten|词性:动词
dilly-dally:/ˈdɪli ˈdæli/| n. | 磨蹭，闲荡 | The act of wasting time. | 例句：There's no time for dilly-dallying, we need to leave now. 没时间磨蹭了，我们现在就得走。|近义词：procrastination, delay|反义词:haste, speed|词性:名词
