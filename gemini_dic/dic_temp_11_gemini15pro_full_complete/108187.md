latticelike: /ˈlætɪslaɪk/
adjective
格子状的；网格状的
Resembling a lattice; having the appearance of a latticework.  A structure of crossed strips or bars forming a network of open spaces.

The virus creates a latticelike structure within the cell.
这种病毒在细胞内形成了一个格子状的结构。

The frost formed a delicate, latticelike pattern on the window.
霜在窗户上形成了精致的格子状图案。

近义词: latticed, reticulated, meshlike, netlike, weblike
反义词: solid, continuous


词性: adjective

词源: lattice (from Old French lattis, from latte 'lath') + -like (suffix meaning "resembling" or "having the characteristics of")
