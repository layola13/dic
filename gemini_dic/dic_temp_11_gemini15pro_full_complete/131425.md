nonclinical:/ˌnɒnˈklɪnɪkəl/ | adj. | 非临床的；非临诊的 | Not directly involved in the treatment of patients; relating to research or other activities that do not involve treating patients. |  Example:  Nonclinical testing is crucial for drug development.  非临床试验对于药物开发至关重要。

Example: He works in a nonclinical role at the hospital, focusing on administration. 他在医院从事非临床工作，专注于行政管理。 | Synonyms: laboratory, research, theoretical, preclinical | Antonyms: clinical, practical | adjective
