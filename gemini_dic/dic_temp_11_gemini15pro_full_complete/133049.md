nonepically:/nɒnˈɛpɪkəli/
adv.
非史诗般地，不壮丽地
Not in an epic or grand manner; lacking the qualities of an epic.
The story was told nonepically, focusing on the mundane details of daily life.  这个故事讲得并不壮丽，而是专注于日常生活的平凡细节。
The battle, though important, was described nonepically. 虽然这场战斗很重要，但描述得并不壮丽。
Synonyms: ordinarily, plainly, simply, unheroically
Antonyms: epically, grandly, heroically, magnificently
词性: 副词
