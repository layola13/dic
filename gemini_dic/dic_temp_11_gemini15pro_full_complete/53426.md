disappearer:/ˌdɪsəˈpɪrər/
|n.|消失者；失踪者|A person or thing that disappears.|The magician made the rabbit a disappearer. 魔术师让兔子消失了。|近义词:vanisher, evanescer|反义词:arriver, appearer|词性:名词
Etymology: From dis- (not) + appear (to become visible) + -er (one who does).
