ignoring:/ɪɡˈnɔːrɪŋ/
verb
忽视，无视
deliberately not paying attention to something or someone
例句：He was ignoring her advice. 他不理会她的劝告。

例句：She was ignoring the ringing phone. 她不理会电话铃声。

近义词：disregarding, overlooking, neglecting
反义词：heeding, acknowledging, noticing
词性：present participle of ignore

ignore:/ɪɡˈnɔːr/
verb
忽视，无视
to refuse to take notice of or acknowledge; disregard intentionally
例句：He ignored the warning signs. 他忽视了警告标志。

例句：She ignored his rude remarks. 她无视了他粗鲁的言论。

近义词：disregard, overlook, neglect
反义词：heed, acknowledge, notice
词性：verb
