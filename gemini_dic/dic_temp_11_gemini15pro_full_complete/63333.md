entitling:/ɪnˈtaɪtlɪŋ/
|v.|给予…权利；给予…资格|Giving someone the right to have or do something.|例句：The document entitles you to free medical care.  这份文件赋予你免费医疗的权利。|近义词：authorize, empower, qualify|反义词：disqualify, prohibit|verb

entitling:/ɪnˈtaɪtlɪŋ/
|adj.|给予资格的；有资格的|Giving the right to have or do something.|例句：An entitling certificate. 一份授权证书。|近义词：qualifying, authorizing|反义词：disqualifying, prohibiting|adjective
