colostomies: /kəˈlɒstəmiz/ (UK) /kəˈlɑːstəmiz/ (US)
|n.|人工肛门（复数）|The surgical creation of an opening from the colon to the surface of the abdomen to allow for the passage of stool.  This is typically done when the normal passage of stool is blocked or compromised.|
例句：
After his surgery, he had to adjust to living with colostomies.
手术后，他不得不适应带着人工肛门生活。

例句：
The nurse provided detailed instructions on caring for colostomies.
护士提供了关于护理人工肛门的详细说明。


|近义词:|ostomy, artificial anus|
|反义词:|N/A|
|词性:|noun|
