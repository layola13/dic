bladder:/ˈblædər/ (US), /ˈblædə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 膀胱；囊状物 | A membranous sac in humans and other animals, especially one acting as a receptacle for a fluid or gas. | My bladder is full, I need to go to the restroom. 我的膀胱满了，我需要去洗手间。| The fish's swim bladder helps it control its buoyancy. 鱼的鳔帮助它控制浮力。| 近义词：sac, vesicle | 反义词: N/A | noun
