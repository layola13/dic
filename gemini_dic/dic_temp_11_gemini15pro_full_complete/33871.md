circumscriptly:/sərkəmˈskrɪptli/ | adv. | 谨慎地；在限定范围内；简洁地 | In a precisely limited or defined manner; concisely.  | He answered circumspectly, revealing little about his plans. 他回答得很谨慎，几乎没有透露他的计划。 | The lawyer circumscriptly outlined the terms of the agreement. 律师简洁地概述了协议条款。 | 近义词: cautiously, carefully, prudently, concisely, succinctly | 反义词: carelessly, recklessly, verbosely, elaborately | 词性: 副词

词源:
From Latin *circumscriptē* (adverb), from *circumscriptus* (past participle of *circumscrībō*), from *circum* (“around”) + *scrībō* (“to write, draw”).
