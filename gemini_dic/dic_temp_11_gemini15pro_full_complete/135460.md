nonperverse:/ˌnɒnpərˈvɜːs/
adjective
非反常的，正常的
Not deviating from what is considered normal or expected; not perverse.
The committee made a nonperverse decision, opting for the most qualified candidate. 委员会做出了一个正常的决定，选择了最有资格的候选人。
Synonyms: normal, standard, regular, typical, conventional, usual, ordinary, natural
Antonyms: perverse, abnormal, irregular, atypical, unconventional, unusual, unnatural
词性: 形容词
