outweep:/ˌaʊtˈwiːp/
verb
胜过…哭泣，比…哭得厉害
to weep more or longer than (someone or something)
She outwept all the other mourners at the funeral. 她在葬礼上比其他所有哀悼者都哭得厉害。
近义词: outcry, surpass in weeping
反义词: laugh, rejoice
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
