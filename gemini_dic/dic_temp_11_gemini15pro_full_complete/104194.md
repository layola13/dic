junkyard:/ˈdʒʌŋkjɑːrd/ | n. |垃圾场；废品堆积场|A place where junk, such as old cars and machinery, is collected, stored, and sometimes dismantled or sold.|例句：We found some spare parts for the car at the junkyard. 我们在垃圾场找到了一些汽车的备件。

|例句：The artist transformed materials from the junkyard into sculptures.  这位艺术家把垃圾场的材料变成了雕塑。|近义词：scrapyard, junkyard, wrecking yard|反义词:  recycling center |词性:noun
