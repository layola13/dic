country-wide:/ˈkʌntriˌwaɪd/ | adj. | 全国范围的，遍及全国的 | Extending throughout a whole country. |  The government implemented a country-wide ban on smoking in public places. 政府在全国范围内实施了公共场所禁烟令。

The company launched a country-wide advertising campaign. 该公司发起了一场全国性的广告宣传活动。

| 近义词: nationwide, national | 反义词: local, regional | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
"country" (from Old French "cuntrée," ultimately from Latin "contra," meaning "opposite" or "against," hence "territory lying opposite" or "land spread out before one") + "-wide" (a suffix meaning "extending throughout," from Old English "wid," meaning "broad" or "wide").
