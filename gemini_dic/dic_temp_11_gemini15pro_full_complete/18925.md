bespatters:/bɪˈspætərz/
verb
溅污，泼溅
splashes with dirty liquid, or covers with spots of something
The passing car bespattered her with mud.  过往的汽车溅了她一身泥。
近义词: splash, spatter, spray, douse
反义词: clean, wipe
