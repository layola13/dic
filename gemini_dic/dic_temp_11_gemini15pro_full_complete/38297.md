concaving:/kɒnˈkeɪvɪŋ/ | v. | 使...凹陷; 凹陷 | curving inward; making something curve inward | The potter was concaving the clay to form a bowl. 陶工正在使黏土凹陷以形成一个碗。| The intense heat was concaving the metal sheet.  强烈的热量使金属板凹陷。| 近义词: hollowing, excavating, digging | 反义词: convexing, bulging, protruding | 词性: 动词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *concavāre, present active infinitive of *concavō, from Latin concavus (“hollow”). Surface analysis is con- +‎ cavus (“hollow”).
