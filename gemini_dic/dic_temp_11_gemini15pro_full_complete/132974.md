nonenclosure:/nɒnɪnˈkləʊʒə(r)/ | n. | 非圈地；未圈地；开放 | The practice or policy of not enclosing land, especially common land. |  The transition to nonenclosure contributed to a more communal way of life.  向非圈地制过渡促成了一种更公共的生活方式。| synonyms: open access, common land, unenclosed land | antonyms: enclosure, private property | noun

nonenclosure:/nɒnɪnˈkləʊʒə(r)/ | n. |  不封闭；不限制 | The state of not being enclosed or confined. | The nonenclosure of the internet allows for free flow of information. 互联网的不封闭性允许信息的自由流动。 | synonyms: openness, freedom,  unrestrictedness | antonyms: enclosure, confinement, restriction | noun
