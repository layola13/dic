gamey:/ˈɡeɪmi/| adj.|有野味的；有异味的；不正当的|Having the strong flavor or odor of game, especially when slightly tainted; suggestive of dishonesty or trickery.|The venison was a bit gamey, but still edible. 鹿肉有点野味，但还可以吃。|近义词：tangy, pungent, fishy, shady, dubious|反义词：bland, fresh, honest|词性:形容词

词源: From game + -y.  The original meaning referred to the distinct flavor of wild game.  The figurative sense of "dubious" or "shady" developed later.
