overassertive:/ˌoʊvərəˈsɜːrtɪv/| adj.| 过分自信的，过分武断的 | Excessively assertive; too confident and aggressive in asserting one's opinions or desires.  Characterized by excessive or aggressive self-assurance. | She was so overassertive in the meeting that she alienated her colleagues.  她在会议上过于自信，疏远了她的同事。| Synonyms: pushy, aggressive, domineering, overbearing, cocky | Antonyms: timid, shy, reserved, unassuming, meek | adjective
