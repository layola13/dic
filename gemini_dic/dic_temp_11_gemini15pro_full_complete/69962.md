festival: /ˈfɛstɪvəl/ (US) /ˈfɛstɪvəl/ (UK)| n. |节日; 盛会; 音乐节;|A period of celebration, typically with religious or cultural significance, often with special events, entertainment, or feasts. |例句：The town held a festival to celebrate the harvest.  小镇举行节日庆祝丰收。

例句：We went to a music festival last summer. 我们去年夏天去了一个音乐节。

近义词：celebration, holiday, feast, carnival, fete |反义词: workday|词性:noun
