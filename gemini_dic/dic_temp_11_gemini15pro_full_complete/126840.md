naphthide:/ˈnæfθaɪd/
n.
萘基化物，萘的负离子
A compound formed by replacing one or more hydrogen atoms in naphthalene with a metal.
Sodium naphthide is a strong reducing agent. 萘钠是一种强还原剂。

近义词:  naphthyl anion (more technical)
反义词: N/A (no direct antonym for a chemical compound)
词性: noun
