apostasy:/əˈpɒstəsi/ /əˈpɑːstəsi/| n. |叛教，变节，背弃|The act of abandoning a religious or political belief or principle.|His apostasy shocked the entire community. 他的叛教震惊了整个社区。|Synonyms: desertion, defection, renunciation, abandonment|Antonyms: loyalty, fidelity, adherence|noun

apostasy:/əˈpɒstəsi/ /əˈpɑːstəsi/| n. |放弃，背弃(信仰、事业或原则)|The act of giving up a previous loyalty or commitment.|Her apostasy from vegetarianism was short-lived. 她放弃素食主义的时间很短。|Synonyms: renunciation, abandonment, rejection|Antonyms: commitment, adherence, loyalty|noun
