abrader:/əˈbreɪdər/ | n. | 磨损物，磨料 | Something that abrades or causes abrasion; an abrasive. | The rough surface of the rock acted as an abrader, smoothing the sharp edges of the metal. 这块岩石粗糙的表面起到了磨料的作用，磨平了金属的锋利边缘。 | Synonyms: abrasive, grinder, polisher | Antonyms: smoother, lubricant | noun

abrasive:/əˈbreɪsɪv/ | adj. | 研磨的，粗糙的; （人）生硬粗暴的 | (1) tending to abrade; (2) (of a person or manner) showing little concern for the feelings of others; harsh. | (1) The abrasive surface of the sandpaper smoothed the wood. 砂纸粗糙的表面使木头光滑。(2) His abrasive personality made it difficult to work with him. 他粗暴的个性使人很难与他共事。 | Synonyms: grinding, rough, harsh, grating, rasping | Antonyms: smooth, soft, gentle, polished | adjective
