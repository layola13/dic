overbroadens:/ˌoʊvərˈbrɔːdənz/
|v.|过度扩展，扩展过宽|To expand or generalize something too much, making it less precise or accurate.|The law overbroadens the definition of hate speech and could stifle legitimate criticism.  这项法律过度扩展了仇恨言论的定义，可能会扼杀合理的批评。|extends excessively, generalizes too much|narrows, restricts|verb
overbroadening:/ˌoʊvərˈbrɔːdənɪŋ/
|n.|过度扩展，扩展过宽|The act of expanding or generalizing something too much.|The overbroadening of the law is a concern for civil liberties groups. 该法律的过度扩展是公民自由团体关注的问题。|excessive extension, overgeneralization|narrowing, restriction|noun
