microdotted:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌdɑːtɪd/
adjective
微點的，微點印刷的
Covered with tiny dots, usually for security purposes.  These dots are often so small they are difficult to see with the naked eye.
The passport was microdotted to prevent forgery.  这本护照采用了微点印刷技术以防止伪造。
Synonyms: microperforated, micromarked
Antonyms: unmarked, plain
词性: 形容词
