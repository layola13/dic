dauntlesses:/ˈdɔːntləsnəsɪz/
noun
无畏；勇敢
The state or quality of being dauntless; fearlessness.
Their dauntlesses in the face of danger was admirable.  他们在危险面前的无畏令人钦佩。
近义词: courage, bravery, fearlessness, intrepidity, boldness
反义词: fear, timidity, cowardice, apprehension
词性: noun (plural)


dauntless:/ˈdɔːntləs/
adjective
无畏的；勇敢的
Showing fearlessness and determination.
The dauntless firefighter rushed into the burning building.  这位勇敢的消防员冲进了燃烧的建筑物。
近义词: courageous, brave, fearless, intrepid, bold
反义词: fearful, timid, cowardly, apprehensive
词性: adjective

词源:
From Middle English *dauntles*, from daunten + -less. Equivalent to daunt + -less.
