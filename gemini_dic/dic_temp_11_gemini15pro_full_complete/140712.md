omnifarious:/ˌɒmnɪˈfeəriəs/| adj.|各式各样的，包罗万象的|Of all kinds, varieties, or forms.  Encompassing or including everything.|例句：The museum boasts an omnifarious collection of artifacts from around the world.  这家博物馆拥有来自世界各地的各种各样的文物。

例句：Her omnifarious talents include singing, dancing, and playing multiple instruments. 她的多才多艺包括唱歌、跳舞和演奏多种乐器。|近义词：versatile, diverse, multifaceted, multifarious|反义词：limited, restricted, homogeneous|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *omnifarius*  (all kinds), from *omni-* (all) + *-farius* (relating to kinds).  The *-farius* element is related to Latin *fas* (divine law, right), suggesting a connection to the idea of all the divinely ordained kinds or forms of things.
