blendon: /ˈblendɒn/ (UK) /ˈblendɑːn/ (US) | n. | 布伦顿（地名，通常指英国牛津郡的一个村庄）| Blendon is a village and civil parish about 5 miles (8 km) southeast of Woodstock, Oxfordshire, England. |  Blendon is known for its picturesque countryside. 布伦顿以其风景如画的乡村而闻名。|  We visited Blendon last summer. 我们去年夏天去了布伦顿。 | 近义词: village, hamlet | 反义词: city, metropolis | 词性: 名词

词源:  Blendon likely derives from Old English, possibly meaning "hill where flax is grown."  However, definitive etymological information is scarce.
