lapillation:/ˌlæpɪˈleɪʃən/
n.
火山砾的坠落; 火山爆发降落火山砾
The process or instance of falling of lapilli (rock fragments ejected from a volcano).
During the eruption, lapillation was heavy, covering the surrounding area in a thick layer of ash and rock fragments.  在火山爆发期间，火山砾的坠落很严重，在周围地区覆盖了一层厚厚的火山灰和岩石碎片。
Heavy lapillation caused significant damage to the roofs of buildings near the volcano.  大量的火山砾坠落对火山附近的建筑物屋顶造成了严重破坏。
近义词:tephra fall, volcanic fallout
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider volcanic quiescence - 火山静止)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *lapillatio*, from lapillus (“little stone”) + -atio (suffix forming nouns of action).
