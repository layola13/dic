overcritical: /ˌoʊvərˈkrɪtɪkəl/ | adj. | 过于挑剔的，吹毛求疵的 | Inclined to find fault too readily; too critical. |  She's so overcritical of her own work that she never feels satisfied. 她对自己的工作过于挑剔，以至于从未感到满意。| Example: He was so overcritical of her cooking that she refused to cook for him again. 他对她的厨艺太挑剔了，以至于她拒绝再为他做饭。 | Synonyms: hypercritical, fault-finding, captious, censorious | Antonyms: uncritical, accepting, tolerant, lenient | adjective
