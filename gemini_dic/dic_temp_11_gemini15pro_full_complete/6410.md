amphiscii: /æmˈfɪskiˌaɪ/
n.|两回归线之间居民|People who live between the tropics of Cancer and Capricorn, where the sun is directly overhead twice a year, causing their shadows to fall sometimes to the north and sometimes to the south.|The ancient Greeks referred to the amphiscii as those dwelling in the torrid zone.|近义词: tropic-dwellers|反义词: None|词性: 名词

Etymology: From Greek 'amphiskios', from 'amphi' (on both sides) + 'skia' (shadow).
