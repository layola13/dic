crockery:/ˈkrɒkəri/ (UK) /ˈkrɑːkəri/ (US)| n. | 陶器，瓦器 |Dishes and other tableware, typically made of earthenware or china. | She carefully arranged the crockery on the table for the dinner party. 她仔细地为晚宴摆放餐桌上的陶器。| The old crockery was chipped and stained.  旧陶器有缺口和污渍。| synonyms: tableware, dishes, china, earthenware, pottery | antonyms: cutlery (餐具 -  a partial antonym as it refers to a different type of tableware) | noun
