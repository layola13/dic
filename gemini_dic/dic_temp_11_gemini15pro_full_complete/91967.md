hypermorphic:/ˌhaɪpərˈmɔːrfɪk/
adjective
超形态的
Having a phenotype more pronounced than the wild-type phenotype, caused by an allele that acts with greater effect than the wild-type allele.
The hypermorphic mutation resulted in an overexpression of the protein.  这个超形态突变导致蛋白质的过度表达。
hypermorphic mutations may cause disease by producing an excess of gene function. 超形态突变可能通过产生过多的基因功能而导致疾病。
Near synonyms:  overexpressed, upregulated
Antonyms: hypomorphic, amorphic
词性: adjective
