glaive:/ɡleɪv/| n.|长柄刀，戟|A glaive is a type of polearm weapon consisting of a single-edged blade attached to the end of a long pole.|The knight wielded his glaive with deadly skill. 骑士熟练地挥舞着他的长柄刀。|近义词：spear, halberd, pike|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑shield 盾牌作为防御武器的对立面)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *glaive, from Latin gladius (“sword”).
