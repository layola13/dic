abridged:/əˈbrɪdʒd/
adjective
删节的; 节略的
shortened, especially of a text
The abridged version of the novel is easier to read. 这部小说的删节版更容易阅读。
synonyms: shortened, condensed, abbreviated, compressed, reduced
antonyms: expanded, unabridged, complete, full, lengthened
词性:形容词


abridge:/əˈbrɪdʒ/
verb
删节; 节略
to shorten (a book, movie, speech, or other text) without losing the sense
The editor abridged the manuscript for publication. 编辑删节了原稿以便出版。
synonyms: shorten, condense, abbreviate, compress, reduce
antonyms: expand, lengthen, extend, amplify
词性:动词
