boars:/bɔːrz/
n.
野猪（复数）
Wild pigs, especially male ones.
例句：The boars roamed freely in the forest. 野猪在森林里自由漫步。

例句：Hunters tracked the boars through the snow. 猎人追踪野猪穿过雪地。


近义词：hogs, swine, wild pigs
反义词：(No direct antonym for an animal, could use "domestic pigs" for contrast)
词性：Noun

词源：Old English *bar, from Proto-Germanic *bairaz, from Proto-Indo-European *bher- (brown, bright).  Related to Old High German ber (bear), Old Norse bjǫrn (bear), Latin ferus (wild). Initially, the word referred to both wild pigs and bears, but later specialized to wild pigs.
