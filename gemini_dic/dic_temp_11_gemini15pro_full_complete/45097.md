cuckold:/ˈkʌk(ə)ld/| n. v.|戴绿帽者，使戴绿帽|A man whose wife has committed adultery.|例句：He was a cuckold, mocked by his peers. 他是个戴绿帽子的人，被同龄人嘲笑。

例句：The rumor spread that he had been cuckolded. 有传言说他被戴了绿帽子。|近义词：cheated on, betrayed|反义词: faithful, loyal|词性:noun, verb

cuckold:/ˈkʌk(ə)ld/| v.|使戴绿帽|To make a cuckold of (a man).|例句：He cuckolded his best friend. 他给他最好的朋友戴了绿帽子。

例句：She was accused of cuckolding her husband. 她被指控给丈夫戴绿帽子。|近义词：cheat on, betray|反义词: be faithful to, be loyal to|词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French cucuault, from cucu "cuckoo," referring to the cuckoo's habit of laying its eggs in other birds' nests.  The cuckoo's behavior was seen as analogous to a wife's infidelity.
