leecher:/ˈliːtʃər/| n.|水蛭; 寄生虫; 吸血鬼; (尤指从网上)非法下载者|A person who takes advantage of others or gets something for free without giving anything back, especially someone who downloads copyrighted material illegally from the internet. |例句：He's such a leecher, always downloading movies illegally. 他真是个吸血鬼，总是非法下载电影。

例句：The forum is full of leechers who never contribute anything. 这个论坛里到处都是只下载不上传的寄生虫。|近义词：freeloader, parasite, sponge|反义词：contributor, sharer|词性:noun
