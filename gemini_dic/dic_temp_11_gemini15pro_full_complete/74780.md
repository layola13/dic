freezy:/ˈfriːzi/| adj.|寒冷的，冰冻的|Extremely cold; likely to freeze.  Characterized by freezing temperatures.|It was a freezy night, so we stayed indoors. 那是一个寒冷的夜晚，所以我们待在室内。|近义词：freezing, icy, frigid, frosty|反义词:warm, hot, balmy|词性:形容词
词源: From freeze + -y (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Old French *freisier (to freeze), from Frankish *friusan.
