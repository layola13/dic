词源：From Middle English *what, *hwat, from Old English hwæt (“what”), from Proto-Germanic *hwat (“what”), from Proto-Indo-European *kʷód (“what”). Cognate with Scots what (“what”), West Frisian wat (“what”), Dutch wat (“what”), Low German wat (“what”), German was (“what”), Danish hvad (“what”), Swedish vad (“what”), Icelandic hvað (“what”), Gothic 𐍈𐍀 (“what”). Related also to Latin quod (“what, which, that”).
