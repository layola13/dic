cytology:/saɪˈtɑːlədʒi/
n.
细胞学
The study of cells, their structure, function, and pathology.
Cytology plays a crucial role in diagnosing cancer. 细胞学在诊断癌症中起着至关重要的作用。
Cytological analysis confirmed the presence of malignant cells. 细胞学分析证实了恶性细胞的存在。
近义词:cell biology
反义词:histology (组织学, 研究组织而非单个细胞)
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From  cyto- (cell) + -logy (study of).  "Cyto-" comes from the Greek "kytos" meaning "hollow vessel" or "cell." "-logy" comes from the Greek "logos" meaning "word," "reason," or "study."
