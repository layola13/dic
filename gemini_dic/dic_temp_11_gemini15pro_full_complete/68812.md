fasciculated:/fəˈsɪkjʊleɪtɪd/| adj.|簇生的，束状的|Arranged in bundles or clusters; fascicular.|The fasciculated stems of the plant give it a unique appearance. 这种植物簇生的茎秆赋予它独特的外观。|近义词：bundled, clustered, fascicular|反义词:separate, individual|adjective


fasciculate:/ˈfæsɪkjʊleɪt/| v.|使成束，使簇生|To form into bundles or clusters; fasciculate.|The nerves fasciculate to form a larger nerve trunk.  神经束聚在一起形成更大的神经干。|近义词：bundle, cluster|反义词:separate, disperse|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin fasciculus (small bundle, diminutive of fascis - bundle),  + -ate (verb suffix or adjective suffix).
