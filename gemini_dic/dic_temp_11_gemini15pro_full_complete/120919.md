miscataloging:/ˌmɪskætəlɒɡɪŋ/ /ˌmɪskætəlɔːɡɪŋ/
n.
错误分类，编目错误
The act of incorrectly classifying or categorizing something, especially in a library or archive.
The miscataloging of the ancient artifact led to years of confusion among researchers.  古代文物的错误分类导致研究人员多年的困惑。
misclassifying, misfiling, mislabeling
correct cataloging, accurate classification
noun
