discongruity:/ˌdɪskənˈɡruːɪti/ | n. |不一致，不协调，不相称|lack of congruity or agreement; incompatibility; inconsistency|The discongruity between his words and his actions was obvious. 他的言行不一显而易见。|The discongruity between the building's modern design and its historical surroundings was jarring. 这栋建筑的现代设计与其历史环境格格不入，显得十分刺眼。|近义词：incongruity, disparity, discrepancy, inconsistency, clash, conflict |反义词：congruity, harmony, agreement, consistency, conformity|noun
