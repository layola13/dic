biologies:/baɪˈɒlədʒiz/
n.
生物学（复数）
The study of living organisms, divided into many specialized fields that cover their morphology, physiology, anatomy, behavior, origin, and distribution.
Many universities offer degrees in a range of biologies. 许多大学提供各种生物学学位。
The institute carries out research in several different biologies. 该研究所开展几项不同生物学领域的研究。
近义词：biological sciences
反义词：（无直接反义词）
