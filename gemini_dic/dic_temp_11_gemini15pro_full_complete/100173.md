interregionally:/ˌɪntərˈriːdʒənəli/
adverb
跨区域地，区域间地
Occurring between or involving different regions.
例句：The goods are traded interregionally. 这些货物在区域间交易。

例句：Interregionally connected power grids enhance energy security. 跨区域连接的电网增强了能源安全。

近义词：between regions, across regions
反义词：intraregionally, within a region
词性: adverb
