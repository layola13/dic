counterstating:/ˌkaʊntərˈsteɪtɪŋ/
|v.|反驳，提出相反的意见|Presenting an opposing argument or viewpoint; contradicting a statement.|例句：He was counterstating the claims made by the prosecution. 他正在反驳控方的说法。|近义词：contradicting, opposing, refuting, rebutting, denying, gainsaying|反义词：confirming, corroborating, supporting, affirming|词性:动词

counterstatement:/ˌkaʊntərˈsteɪtmənt/
|n.|反驳，相反的陈述|A statement made in opposition to another; a contradiction.|例句：Her counterstatement surprised everyone in the room. 她的反驳让房间里的每个人都感到惊讶。|近义词：rebuttal, contradiction, denial, refutation|反义词：confirmation, corroboration, support, affirmation|词性:名词
