mosha:/ˈmoʊʃə/ (US), /ˈmɒʃə/ (UK)| n.|摩沙，mosh，撞人舞|A style of dancing in which people jump up and down and often collide with each other, usually performed to rock music.|The mosh pit was a swirling vortex of energy. 摩沙舞池是一个旋转的能量漩涡。|The band started playing and the crowd went wild, moshing in the center of the floor. 乐队开始演奏，人群疯狂起来，在地板中央跳起了mosh。| 近义词: slam dancing, pogo|反义词: waltz, slow dance|词性:名词

词源:  Possibly from Jamaican English *mosh*, to mash; possibly short for mosh pit.
