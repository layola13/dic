glazier:/ˈɡleɪʒər/| n.|玻璃工，装玻璃的人|A person whose job is fitting glass into windows and doors.|The glazier came to repair the broken window. 玻璃工来修理破了的窗户。|Synonyms: glass fitter, window fitter||noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *glasier, from glas (glass) + -ier (suffix indicating occupation). Ultimately from Latin glacies (ice).
