absolving:/əbˈzɒlvɪŋ/
verb
赦免，免除
granting remission of sin or excusing from a duty or obligation.
The priest absolved him of his sins.  神父赦免了他的罪过。
synonyms: exonerating, acquitting, clearing, vindicating, pardoning, forgiving
antonyms: condemning, convicting, incriminating, blaming
词性: 动词
