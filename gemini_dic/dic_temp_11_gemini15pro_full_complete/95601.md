incommode:/ˌɪnkəˈmoʊd/| vt.|打扰，妨碍，使不便|To cause inconvenience or trouble to someone; to disturb or bother.|The loud construction outside my window incommoded my work. 窗外嘈杂的施工干扰了我的工作。|近义词：disturb, bother, trouble, inconvenience|反义词:accommodate, assist, help|动词

incommode:/ˌɪnkəˈmoʊd/| n.|不便，麻烦 (罕用)|Inconvenience or trouble (archaic usage).|The incommode caused by the road closure was significant. 道路封闭造成的不便很大。|近义词：inconvenience, trouble, difficulty|反义词:convenience, ease, comfort|名词
