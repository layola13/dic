collaboratively:/kəˈlæbərətɪvli/
adverb
合作地，协作地
in a way that involves working together with other people
The project was completed collaboratively by a team of scientists.  这个项目是由一组科学家合作完成的。
We worked collaboratively on the presentation. 我们合作完成了这次演讲。
近义词: jointly, together, cooperatively
反义词: independently, separately, alone
词性: adverb

词源:
From collaborate + -ive + -ly.  Collaborate comes from Latin collaborare "to labor together," from com- "together" + laborare "to labor."
