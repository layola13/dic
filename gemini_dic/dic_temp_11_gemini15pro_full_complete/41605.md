corpuscle:/ˈkɔːrpʌsəl/
|n.|微粒，粒子|Blood corpuscles are essential for carrying oxygen. 血细胞对于携带氧气至关重要。|
|英文解析: A minute particle, especially a blood cell.|
|例句：Red blood corpuscles transport oxygen throughout the body. 红细胞将氧气输送到全身。|
|例句：White blood corpuscles are part of the immune system. 白细胞是免疫系统的一部分。|
|近义词：particle, granule|
|反义词：wave|
|词性: noun


词源: From Late Latin corpusculum "little body," diminutive of corpus "body."
