overpractice: /ˌōvərˈpræktɪs/
noun
过度练习
Excessive practice of a skill or activity, often leading to diminished performance or injury.
Athletes can suffer from overpractice, leading to burnout and injuries.  运动员可能因过度训练而遭受倦怠和受伤。
Overpractice can hinder your progress on the piano.  过度练习会阻碍你在钢琴上的进步。
近义词: overtraining, overexertion
反义词: underpractice
词性: noun


overpractice: /ˌōvərˈpræktɪs/
verb
过度练习
To practice a skill or activity excessively, often to the point of detriment.
He overpracticed his golf swing and developed tendonitis. 他过度练习高尔夫挥杆，结果得了肌腱炎。
Be careful not to overpractice the same piece of music. 注意不要过度练习同一首曲子。
近义词: overtrain, overdo
反义词: underpractice
词性: verb
