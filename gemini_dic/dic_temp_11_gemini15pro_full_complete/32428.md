chimb:/tʃɪm/ | n. | 壁炉架；壁炉架的侧面 | The chimb of the fireplace was ornately carved. 壁炉架的侧面雕刻精美。 | A nontechnical term for the jamb, especially of a fireplace.  | The cat slept on the chimb, enjoying the warmth. 猫睡在壁炉架边上，享受着温暖。 | 近义词:jamb | 反义词: 无 | 词性:名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *chimbe, from Old English *cimbe (“edge, border”), from Proto-Germanic *kimbijǭ (“edge, border, rim”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰembh- (“to bite, chew”). Cognate with Dutch kim (“horizon, edge”), German Kimme (“notch, sight”).
