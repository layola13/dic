doggies:/ˈdɒɡiz/ (UK), /ˈdɔːɡiz/ (US)| n. |小狗（复数），狗狗（复数，非正式）|An informal and affectionate term for dogs, often used when talking to children or expressing fondness.  It is the plural form of "doggy."|例句：Look at all the cute doggies! 看看所有可爱的小狗！

例句：The doggies are playing in the park.  小狗们正在公园里玩耍。

近义词：pups, puppies, dogs|反义词: (No direct antonym for a diminutive plural noun.  One could consider "cats" as an antonym to "dogs", but not specifically for "doggies")|词性:noun
