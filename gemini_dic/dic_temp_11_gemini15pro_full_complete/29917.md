cavalcade:/ˌkævəlˈkeɪd/| n.|骑兵队，行列，一连串|A procession of people riding on horses, or a series of related things. |The president's inauguration included a cavalcade of floats and marching bands. 总统的就职典礼包括一连串的花车和游行乐队。

The museum showcased a cavalcade of antique cars. 博物馆展示了一系列老爷车。

|近义词：procession, parade, array, series, succession|反义词: None|词性:noun
