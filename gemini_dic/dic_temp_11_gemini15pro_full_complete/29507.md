catastaltic:/ˌkætəˈstɔːltɪk/
adjective
催泻的；泻下的；导泻的
Relating to, or producing,瀉下; purgative.  Promoting evacuation of the bowels.
例句：The doctor prescribed a catastaltic medication to relieve the patient's constipation. 医生开了泻药来缓解病人的便秘。

例句：The catastaltic effect of the herb was well known in traditional medicine.  这种草药的泻药作用在传统医学中是众所周知的。

近义词：purgative, laxative, cathartic
反义词：constipating, astringent
词性：adjective
词源：From Greek *katástalt(ikós)*  from *katástaltēs* “one who makes descend, throws down,” from *katabállein* "to throw down," from *kata-* "down" + *bállein* "to throw".
