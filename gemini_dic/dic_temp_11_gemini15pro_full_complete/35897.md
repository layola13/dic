cocooning:/kəˈkuːnɪŋ/
n.
茧居；居家休闲；自我封闭
The practice of spending leisure time at home, often indulging in comfortable and relaxing activities.
例句：Cocooning has become a popular trend, with people choosing to stay in and enjoy their homes rather than go out.  茧居已成为一种流行趋势，人们选择待在家里享受而不是外出。

例句：She enjoyed cocooning on the weekend, reading books and watching movies. 她喜欢周末宅在家里，看书看电影。

近义词：nesting, hibernating, withdrawing
反义词：socializing, going out, exploring
词性：名词

Etymology: From the noun "cocoon," referring to the protective casing created by an insect larva, extended metaphorically to describe the act of staying home and creating a comfortable, safe space.  词源：来自名词“cocoon”（茧），指的是昆虫幼虫创造的保护壳，隐喻地扩展到描述待在家里并创造一个舒适、安全的空间的行为。
