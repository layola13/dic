cayenne pepper:/ˌkeɪˈɛn ˈpɛpər/ | n. |辣椒，卡宴辣椒|A hot red pepper made from the dried fruits of *Capsicum frutescens*, used as a spice.|I like to add a pinch of cayenne pepper to my chili. 我喜欢在我的辣椒里加一点辣椒粉。|Synonyms: chili pepper, red pepper|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific pepper, but perhaps "mild pepper")|noun

cayenne:/keɪˈɛn/ | adj. |红辣椒色的; 辣椒味的 |Of or relating to cayenne pepper or its flavor.|Cayenne pepper adds a nice kick to any dish. 红辣椒给任何菜肴都增添了美味。|Synonyms:  spicy, hot, fiery|Antonyms: mild, bland|adjective
