at-anchor:/æt ˈæŋkər/| adj.| 抛锚的，停泊的|A ship at anchor is a ship that has dropped its anchor and is held in place by it. | The ship lay at anchor in the bay. 这艘船停泊在海湾里。|近义词：anchored, moored|反义词: underway, sailing|形容词

at-anchor:/æt ˈæŋkər/| n.| 抛锚状态|The condition of being at anchor. | The yacht was at anchor just offshore. 游艇就在近海抛锚。|近义词：anchorage|反义词: underway, sailing|名词

词源:  From the preposition "at" + "anchor" (noun), ultimately from Old English "ancor" and Old Norse "akkeri."
