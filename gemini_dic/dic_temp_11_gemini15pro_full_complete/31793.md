checkless:/ˈtʃɛklɪs/
adjective
无支票的；不用支票的
Not involving or requiring the use of checks.
A checkless society is one where financial transactions are conducted electronically.  一个无支票社会是指金融交易以电子方式进行的社会。
The company is moving towards a checkless payroll system.  该公司正在转向无支票的工资系统。
Near Synonyms: electronic, digital, cashless
Opposite: check-based
词性: 形容词
