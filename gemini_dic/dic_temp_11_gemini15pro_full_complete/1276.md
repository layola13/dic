accoster: /əˈkɒstə(r)/ (US) /əˈkɔːstər/ (UK)| vt.| 搭讪；上前和…说话；（尤指乞丐）上前索要；（船）靠（岸）|To approach and speak to someone, often aggressively or in a way that is not welcome, especially in order to ask for something (money, directions, etc.) or to make sexual advances. Also, (of a ship) to come alongside (another ship or a wharf).|

例句：
A beggar accosted me in the street.
一个乞丐在街上上前向我乞讨。

He was accosted by a group of young men asking for money.
一群年轻人上前向他索要钱财。

近义词：approach, address, solicit, buttonhole, hail|
反义词：avoid, shun, evade|
词性：verb|
