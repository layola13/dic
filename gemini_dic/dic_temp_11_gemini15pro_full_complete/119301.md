microalgae:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈældʒiː/| n. |微藻|Microscopic algae, typically found in freshwater and marine systems, often unicellular or forming simple colonies.  They are a diverse group of photosynthetic organisms.|Microalgae are a promising source of biofuel. 微藻是很有前途的生物燃料来源。

Microalgae blooms can sometimes be harmful to marine life. 微藻爆发有时会对海洋生物有害。|近义词：phytoplankton|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps macroalgae for larger algae) |词性:noun
