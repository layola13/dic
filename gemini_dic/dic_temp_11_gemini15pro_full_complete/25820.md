burghership:/ˈbɜːrɡərʃɪp/
n.
市民身份；公民权；市籍
The right of citizenship in a burgh, or the freedom of a burgh.  The state of being a burgess or freeman of a burgh.
He was granted burghership after living in the town for ten years.  他在镇上住了十年后被授予了市民身份。
The family held burghership in the town for generations.  这个家族几代人都在这个镇上拥有市籍。
citizenship, freedom,  franchise
alienage, disenfranchisement
noun
