bused:/bʌst/
| v. |用公共汽车运送；用校车接送|to transport people by bus, especially to school|The children are bused to school every day. 孩子们每天都乘公共汽车上学。|近义词：transported, conveyed|反义词：walked, drove|动词

bused:/bʌst/
| adj. |乘公共汽车的；被公共汽车运送的|transported by bus|We took a bused tour of the city. 我们乘坐公共汽车游览了这座城市。|近义词：transported by bus|反义词：driven, walked|形容词


词源:  From the verb "bus," which is a shortened form of "omnibus," a Latin word meaning "for all."
