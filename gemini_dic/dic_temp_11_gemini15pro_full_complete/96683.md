indys:/ˈɪndiz/
noun
独立电影; 独立影片; 独立音乐人; 独立音乐家
Independent films or musicians (plural). Shortened form of "independents".
Example: The festival showcased a diverse range of indys.  这个电影节展示了各种各样的独立电影。
Example: Many indys struggle to find funding for their projects. 许多独立音乐人难以找到项目资金。
Synonym: independent artists, independent filmmakers
Antonym: mainstream artists, mainstream filmmakers
n.
