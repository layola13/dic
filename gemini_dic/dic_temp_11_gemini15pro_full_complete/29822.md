cauliflower: /ˈkɔːliflaʊər/ (US) /ˈkɒlɪˌflaʊə(r)/ (UK)| n. |花椰菜；菜花|A vegetable with a large, white head of tightly packed flower buds.|例句：I'm roasting a cauliflower for dinner tonight. 今晚我烤花椰菜做晚餐。|例句：Cauliflower is a good source of vitamin C.  花椰菜是维生素C的良好来源。|近义词：broccoli (somewhat), cabbage (loosely) |反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps leafy greens as a contrasting vegetable type) |词性: noun
