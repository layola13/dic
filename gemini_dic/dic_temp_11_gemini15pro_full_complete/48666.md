defensives:/dɪˈfɛnsɪvz/| n.|防御措施，防御姿态|actions or words that are intended to protect someone or something from criticism or attack|The company adopted a number of defensives to protect itself from a hostile takeover.  这家公司采取了一系列防御措施，以保护自己免受恶意收购。|
defensives:/dɪˈfɛnsɪvz/| n.|守势，防守|the position or attitude of defending against attack|The team was on the defensives for most of the game. 这支球队在比赛的大部分时间里都处于守势。|
defensives:/dɪˈfɛnsɪvz/| adj.|防御性的，防守的|intended or used to protect someone or something from attack|Defensive measures were taken to protect the city from attack.  采取了防御措施来保护城市免受攻击。|
Etymology: From Late Middle English *defensif, from Old French defensif, from Late Latin dēfēnsīvus, from Latin dēfēnsus (past participle of dēfendere ‘to ward off’), from dē- ‘down, away’ + fendere ‘to strike’.|
Synonym: protective, shielding, guarding|
Antonym: offensive, aggressive, attacking|
Word type: noun, adjective
