anathemize:/əˈnæθəˌmaɪz/| vt.|诅咒，咒骂，强烈谴责|To pronounce an anathema upon; curse. To denounce or condemn someone or something vehemently.|The church anathemized the heretics. 教会诅咒了异教徒。|Synonyms: curse, condemn, denounce, excommunicate, ostracize|Antonyms: bless, praise, commend, extol|Verb

anathemize:/əˈnæθəˌmaɪz/| n.|诅咒，咒骂|A formal curse by a pope or a council of the Church, excommunicating a person or denouncing a doctrine.|The king issued an anathemize against his enemies. 国王对他的敌人发出了诅咒。|Synonyms: curse, condemnation, denunciation, excommunication|Antonyms: blessing, praise, commendation|Noun
