icono:/ˈiːkɒnəʊ/ (UK), /ˈaɪkɑːnoʊ/ (US)| n. |图标；象征；偶像|A small picture or symbol on a computer screen that represents a program, file, or other item.  A person or thing regarded as a representative symbol of something.|例句：Click on the printer icon to start printing. 点击打印机图标开始打印。|例句：He became a cultural icon. 他成为了一个文化偶像。|近义词：symbol, emblem, image, idol|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
