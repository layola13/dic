discourtesy's:/dɪsˈkɜːrtəsiːz/
n.
无礼，失礼
lack of courtesy; rudeness.
His discourtesy's shocked everyone present. 他的无礼震惊了在场的所有人。
近义词：rudeness, impoliteness, disrespect
反义词：courtesy, politeness, respect
词性：名词 (Noun),  Specifically, it's a possessive form of a noun.


Etymology:
From Old French descortesie, from des- (expressing negation) + cortesie "courtesy".  Ultimately from Latin cohors, meaning "enclosed yard, company, cohort".
