frailnesses:/ˈfreɪlnəsɪz/
n.
弱点，脆弱
the state of being weak and easily damaged
Her frailnesses became more apparent as she grew older.  随着年龄的增长，她的虚弱变得更加明显。
synonyms: weaknesses, vulnerabilities, infirmities, defects, shortcomings
antonyms: strengths, robustness, resilience
noun

frailty:/ˈfreɪlti/
n.
脆弱，虚弱
the condition of being weak and delicate
Old age brings with it many frailties. 老年带来了许多虚弱。
synonyms: weakness, fragility, vulnerability, infirmity
antonyms: strength, robustness, resilience
noun
