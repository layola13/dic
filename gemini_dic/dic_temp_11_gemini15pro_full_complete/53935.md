discourtesy:/dɪsˈkɜːrtəsi/
n.
失礼，无礼
Lack of courtesy; rudeness.
His discourtesy to the guests was shocking. 他对客人们的无礼令人震惊。

近义词:rudeness, impoliteness, incivility, disrespect
反义词:courtesy, politeness, respect


词性:noun


词源:Late Middle English: from Old French descourtoisie, from descourtois ‘discourteous’.
