deconsecrated:/ˌdiːkɒnˈsɪkreɪtɪd/
adjective
已撤销祝圣的；非神圣的
Having been deprived of its sacred character.
The deconsecrated church was converted into apartments.  这栋已撤销祝圣的教堂被改建成了公寓。
The artifacts were removed from the deconsecrated temple.  这些文物是从已非神圣的寺庙中移走的。
Synonyms: secularized, unconsecrated, profane
Antonyms: consecrated, sacred, holy
