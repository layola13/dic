chrysography: /krɪˈsɒɡrəfi/ | n. | 金字书法；用金墨水书写 | the art of writing in letters of gold; a manuscript written in gold |  The ancient manuscript, a beautiful example of chrysography, shimmered in the dim light. 这份古老的手稿是用金字书写的美丽范例，在昏暗的光线下闪闪发光。 | 近义词: gold writing | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, perhaps plain handwriting) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *khrusographia*, from *khrusos* (gold) + *graphein* (to write).
