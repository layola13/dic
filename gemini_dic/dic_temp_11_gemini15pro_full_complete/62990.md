enomoty: /ɪˈnɒməti/
n.
百人队，百人组
A subdivision of an ancient Spartan army, consisting of about 100 men.
Each Spartan mora was subdivided into four lochoi, each of which was further subdivided into two enomotiai. Each enomoty comprised about 25-36 men.
The enomoty was a crucial tactical unit in the Spartan army.

近义词: company, platoon, unit
