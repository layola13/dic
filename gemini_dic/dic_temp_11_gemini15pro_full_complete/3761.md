agitable:/ˈædʒɪtəbl/ | adj. | 易激动的，易怒的，易 agitated 的 | Easily excited or irritated; prone to agitation. | The dog became agitable when the doorbell rang. 这只狗一听到门铃响就变得焦躁不安。|  The agitable child had trouble sitting still.  这个好动不安的孩子很难安静地坐着。 | synonyms: excitable, irritable, restless, nervous, fidgety | antonyms: calm, tranquil, serene, placid, composed | adjective
