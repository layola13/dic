o'clock:/əˈklɒk/ (UK) /əˈklɑːk/ (US)| adv.|点，…点钟|Contraction of "of the clock". Used to indicate the time of day when a clock strikes the hour.|It's two o'clock. 现在是两点钟。|近义词: sharp (when used to emphasize the exact hour)|反义词: N/A|词性:副词

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
The clock strikes twelve o'clock. 时钟敲响了十二点。
The train leaves at six o'clock every morning. 火车每天早上六点出发。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
It's approaching five o'clock. 快到五点了。
The clock is striking six o'clock as we speak. 我们说话的时候，时钟正在敲响六点。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
It has just turned ten o'clock. 刚到十点。
The clock has struck one o'clock already. 时钟已经敲响一点了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
The clock has been chiming every hour o'clock. 时钟每到整点就一直在报时。
We have been waiting since eight o'clock. 我们从八点就一直在等。


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
The meeting started at nine o'clock. 会议九点开始。
The alarm went off at seven o'clock. 闹钟七点响了。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
It was getting close to midnight o'clock. 快到午夜十二点了。
At six o'clock, I was cooking dinner. 六点的时候，我正在做饭。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
By ten o'clock, the party had already finished. 到十点时，派对已经结束了。
He had left by five o'clock. 他五点前就离开了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
They had been working since eight o'clock that morning. 他们从那天早上八点就一直在工作。
She had been waiting for him since two o'clock. 她从两点起就一直在等他。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
The movie will start at seven o'clock. 电影七点开始。
I will be there by three o'clock. 我三点前会到那里。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
At eight o'clock tomorrow, I will be flying to London. 明天八点，我将飞往伦敦。
This time next week, we will be relaxing on the beach at twelve o'clock. 下周这个时候，我们将在十二点在沙滩上放松。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By five o'clock, I will have finished my work. 到五点，我将完成我的工作。
By six o'clock, the train will have already left. 到六点，火车就已经开走了。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By nine o'clock, I will have been working for eight hours. 到九点，我将已经工作了八个小时。
By ten o'clock, she will have been studying for three hours. 到十点，她将已经学习了三个小时。
