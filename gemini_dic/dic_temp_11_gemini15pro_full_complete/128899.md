nimbostratus:/ˌnɪmboʊˈstrɑːtəs/ | n. | 雨层云 | A dark, gray cloud layer, often continuous, that is thick enough to completely obscure the sun or moon and from which precipitation falls.  It is the principal cloud type associated with continuously falling precipitation. |  The nimbostratus blocked out the sun all day. 雨层云整天遮住了太阳。| Synonym: rain cloud | Antonym: clear sky | noun
