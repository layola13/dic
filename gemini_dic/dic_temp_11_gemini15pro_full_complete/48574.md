defeasance:/dɪˈfiːzəns/
n.|解除义务；废止；失效；作废|The act of rendering something null and void, typically a legal obligation or debt, usually by fulfilling the conditions of the obligation.|The defeasance clause in the mortgage allowed him to prepay the loan without penalty.  抵押贷款中的解除条款允许他提前偿还贷款而不用支付罚金。|cancellation, annulment, revocation, invalidation|establishment, enforcement|noun


defeasance:/dɪˈfiːzəns/
n.|（尤指债务的）清偿，偿付|The payment of funds, typically to a trustee, to offset the obligation of future payment.|The company used a defeasance strategy to remove the debt from its balance sheet.  该公司采用了一种解除策略，将债务从其资产负债表中移除。|repayment, discharge, settlement|debt, liability|noun
