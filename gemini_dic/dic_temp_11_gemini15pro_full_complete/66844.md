expatriations: /ˌɛksˌpeɪtriˈeɪʃənz/ | n. | 移居国外；侨居；放逐 | The expatriations of dissidents have increased dramatically in recent years.  近年来，持不同政见者的移居国外现象急剧增加。 |  The company offered generous packages for the expatriations of its employees to the new overseas branch.  公司为员工移居到新的海外分支机构提供了优厚的待遇。 | 近义词: emigration, exile, diaspora | 反义词: repatriation, return, immigration | 词性: 名词

词源: From Late Latin expatriationem, accusative of expatriatio (“banishment”), from past participle stem of expatriare (“to banish, exile”), from ex- (“out of, from”) + patria (“native land, fatherland”).
