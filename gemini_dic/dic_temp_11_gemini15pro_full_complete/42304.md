counter-offer:/ˈkaʊntər ˈɔfər/ | n. | 还价，反要约 |  A counter-offer is an offer made in response to a previous offer. It signifies a rejection of the original offer and the proposal of a new, alternative offer. |  They made a counter-offer on the house. 他们在房子上提出了还价。| synonyms: return offer, counterproposal | antonyms: acceptance, agreement | noun

counter-offer:/ˈkaʊntər ˈɔfər/ | v. | 还价，反要约 | To make a counter-offer is to respond to an offer with a different offer. | The seller counter-offered with a slightly higher price. 卖方还了一个稍高的价格。 | synonyms: counterpropose, make a return offer | antonyms: accept, agree | verb
