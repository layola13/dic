canning:/ˈkænɪŋ/| n.|罐头制造，装罐|The process of preserving food by sealing it in airtight containers, typically by heating. |The canning industry is a major employer in this region. 罐头工业是这个地区的主要雇主。|近义词：preserving, bottling|反义词：spoiling, decaying|词性:noun
can:/kæn/ /kən/ |aux. v. n.|能，可以；罐头|1.  To be able to; have the ability or power to. 2. A sealed metal container for food or drink.|1.  She can speak three languages. 她会说三种语言。 2.  I opened a can of soup. 我打开了一罐汤。|近义词：(aux. v.) be able to, may; (n.) tin|反义词：(aux. v.) cannot, can't|词性: auxiliary verb, noun
