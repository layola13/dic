emphyteutic:/ˌɛmfɪˈtjuːtɪk/| adj.| 永佃的，长期租赁的|Relating to or being a long-term lease of property, typically for agricultural use, where the tenant has significant rights and responsibilities similar to ownership.|The farmer held the land under an emphyteutic lease, allowing him to cultivate it for generations.|Synonyms: leasehold, long-term lease|Antonyms: freehold, outright ownership|Adjective
