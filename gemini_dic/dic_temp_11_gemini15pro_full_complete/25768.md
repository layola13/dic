burdener's:/ˈbɜːrdənərz/
n.
负担者，施加负担者
The burdener's actions caused hardship for many.
负担者的行为给很多人带来了困难。
English explanation: Possessive form of "burdener," referring to something belonging to or associated with a person who imposes a burden.  Indicates ownership or origin.
The burdener's heavy suitcase caused him to trip.
负担者沉重的行李箱让他绊倒了。
近义词: imposer's, oppressor's
反义词: helper's, supporter's
词性: noun (possessive)
