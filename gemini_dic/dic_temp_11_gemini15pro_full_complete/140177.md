offeree:/ˌɒfəˈriː/ (US) /ˌɔːfəˈriː/ (UK)
n.
受要约人，被发价人
The person or entity to whom an offer is made.
The offeree has the power to accept the offer and create a binding contract.  受要约人有权接受要约并创建一个具有约束力的合同。
The offeree rejected the offer because the price was too high. 受要约人拒绝了报价，因为价格太高。
近义词:recipient, receiver
反义词:offeror
词性:noun
