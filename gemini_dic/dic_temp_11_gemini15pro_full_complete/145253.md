overblithe:/ˌoʊvərˈblaɪð/| adj.|过于快乐的，轻率的，漫不经心的|Excessively cheerful or lighthearted; lacking appropriate seriousness or concern.  Derived from "over" (excessive) + "blithe" (happy, carefree).|He was overblithe about the risks involved, which ultimately led to disaster. 他对所涉及的风险过于轻率，最终导致了灾难。|Synonyms: frivolous, careless, heedless, nonchalant|Antonyms: serious, cautious, prudent, anxious|adjective
