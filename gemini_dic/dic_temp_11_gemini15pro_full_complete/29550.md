catches:/ˈkætʃɪz/
| n. v. |捕捉，接住，赶上；捕获物；隐患，陷阱|The cat catches mice. 猫捉老鼠。|近义词：grasps, seizes, apprehends, snares|反义词:misses, releases, loses|词性:名词复数;动词第三人称单数

Etymology: From Middle English cacchen, from Anglo-Norman cachier, from Vulgar Latin *captiāre, frequentative of Latin capere (“to take, seize”).
