anker: /ˈæŋkər/ | n. | 锚 | A heavy object attached to a ship by a cable or chain and used to moor the ship to the seabed; something that provides stability or security. | The ship dropped anchor in the bay.  这艘船在海湾抛锚了。 | The family is my anchor. 家庭是我的精神支柱。| synonym: mainstay, support, | antonym: instability, insecurity | noun

anker: /ˈæŋkər/ | v. | 抛锚；使固定 | To moor (a ship) to the seabed with an anchor; to secure firmly. |  We anchored the boat in the harbor. 我们把船停泊在港口。| The news anchor anchored the evening news.  新闻主播主持了晚间新闻。| synonym: moor, secure, fasten | antonym: release, unfasten, detach | verb
