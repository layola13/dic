luxuriety: /lʌɡˈʒʊəriəti/ /lʌɡˈʒʊəriɪti/
n.
奢华，奢侈，豪华
A state of great comfort and extravagant living.

例句：
She lived a life of incredible luxuriety, surrounded by fine art and expensive furnishings. 她过着极其奢华的生活，周围都是精美的艺术品和昂贵的家具。

He indulged in every luxuriety imaginable, from private jets to gourmet meals. 他沉溺于各种可以想象的奢华享受，从私人飞机到美食盛宴。

近义词：luxury, opulence, extravagance, lavishness
反义词：austerity, poverty, simplicity, frugality
词性: noun
