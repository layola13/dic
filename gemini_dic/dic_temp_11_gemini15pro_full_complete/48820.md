defocalization:/diːfoʊkəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 散焦，去焦，失焦 | The act or process of making something less focused or distinct.  It can refer to a literal blurring of an image, or a metaphorical shift in attention or purpose. | The photographer experimented with defocalization to create a dreamlike effect. 摄影师尝试散焦来营造梦幻般的效果。| The defocalization of the company's mission led to confusion among employees. 公司使命的失焦导致员工感到困惑。 |  blurring, diffusion, softening | focusing, sharpening | noun
