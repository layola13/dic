conjubilant: /kənˈdʒuːbɪlənt/ | adj. | 欣喜若狂的；欢欣鼓舞的 | Feeling or expressing great joy, especially due to a success. |  The team was conjubilant after their victory. 球队在获胜后欣喜若狂。| 近义词：elated, jubilant, triumphant, exultant | 反义词：dejected, despondent, miserable | 词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from Latin conjubilant- ‘shouting together for joy’, from the verb conjubilare, from con- ‘together’ + jubilare (see jubilant).
