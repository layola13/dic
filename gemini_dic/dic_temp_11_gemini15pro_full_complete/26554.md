cabirian:/kəˈbɪəriən/

cabirian:/kəˈbɪəriən/
noun|卡比里信徒|A worshipper of the Cabeiri.|The cabirian participated in the secret rites.|近义词:initiate, mystic|反义词:skeptic|词性:noun
