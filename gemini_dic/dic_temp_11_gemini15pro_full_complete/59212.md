dyspraxic: /dɪsˈpræksɪk/ | adj. | 失用症的，运动障碍的 |  Relating to or affected by dyspraxia, a developmental coordination disorder affecting physical coordination.  It impacts fine and/or gross motor skills and can also affect speech. |  My son is dyspraxic and struggles with writing. 我的儿子患有运动障碍，写字有困难。|  He has dyspraxic tendencies, making it difficult for him to tie his shoelaces. 他有运动障碍倾向，系鞋带对他来说很困难。|Synonyms: clumsy, awkward, uncoordinated | Antonyms: coordinated, dexterous, agile | adjective
