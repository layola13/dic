lets:/lɛts/| v.| 让我们 (用于提出建议或邀请) |This is a contraction of "let us".  It signifies a suggestion or invitation to do something together. |Lets go to the cinema. 我们去看电影吧。|Lets have dinner together tonight. 今晚我们一起吃晚饭吧。| allow, permit |forbid, prohibit| verb
