atticism:/ˈætɪsɪzəm/| n.|雅典主义；雅典式表达；简洁优雅的表达|An expression characteristic of Athenian Greek, especially a refined or elegant idiom.|Her prose is marked by a concise atticism. 她的散文以简洁的雅典主义风格著称。|Synonyms: elegance, refinement, conciseness, purity|Antonyms: vulgarity, crudeness, prolixity|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *atticisme*, from Late Latin *Atticismus*, from Greek *Attikismos*, from *Attikos* "Attic," from *Attike* "Attica."
