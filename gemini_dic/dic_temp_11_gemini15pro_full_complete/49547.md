dematerialize:/ˌdiːməˈtɪəriəlaɪz/ /ˌdiːməˈtɪriəlaɪz/
verb
消失，非物质化
To cease to exist in material form; disappear.  To convert assets into cash.
The magician made the rabbit dematerialize.  魔术师让兔子消失了。
Investors are looking to dematerialize their assets during the economic downturn.  在经济低迷时期，投资者正在寻求将他们的资产变现。
Synonyms: vanish, disappear, evaporate, dissolve
Antonyms: materialize, appear, emerge
词性: 动词
