cyprinoid:/ˈsaɪprɪnɔɪd/| n.|鲤科鱼|A fish of the carp family (Cyprinidae).|例句：The lake was teeming with cyprinoids.  湖里充满了鲤科鱼。|例句：Many cyprinoids are popular aquarium fish. 许多鲤科鱼是受欢迎的观赏鱼。|近义词：carp, minnow, goldfish (depending on the specific species)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-cyprinid fish)|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From  "cyprin-" + "-oid". "Cyprin-" comes from Latin "cyprinus" meaning "carp," ultimately from Greek "kyprînos." "-oid" is a suffix meaning "resembling" or "having the form of."
