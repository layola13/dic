grimace: /ɡrɪˈmeɪs/ (US) /ɡrɪməs/ (UK)| n.| 鬼脸 | A twisted facial expression indicating pain, disgust, or disapproval. |  A grimace of pain crossed his face.  他的脸上掠过痛苦的表情。 | synonyms: frown, scowl | antonyms: smile, grin | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *grimasse* (attested 1549), from *grimasser*, from *grimache* "ugly face," perhaps of Germanic origin.
