eternalist:/ɪˈtɜːrnəlɪst/ | n. |永恒论者|A person who believes in eternalism, the philosophical view that all points in time exist equally.  | An eternalist might argue that the past, present, and future are all equally real. 永恒论者可能会争辩说，过去、现在和未来都是同等真实的。| Example 1: The eternalist argued that time is not linear. 这位永恒论者认为时间不是线性的。

Example 2:  As an eternalist, she believed that her deceased grandmother was still existing in a different part of spacetime.  作为一名永恒论者，她相信她已故的祖母仍然存在于时空的不同部分。 |Synonyms:perennialist, immutabilist|Antonyms:presentist|noun
