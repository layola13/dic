outsulking:/ˌaʊtˈsʌlkɪŋ/
verb
比...更闷闷不乐；生闷气胜过
Surpassing someone in sulking; being more sullen or morose than another.
He was outsulking even his teenage daughter after the argument.  在争吵之后，他甚至比他十几岁的女儿还要闷闷不乐。
outsulking everyone in the room after his team lost. 在他的球队输球后，他比房间里的每个人都更加闷闷不乐。
近义词: outdoing, surpassing, exceeding
反义词: cheering, comforting, consoling
词性: verb (present participle)

Etymology:
"Out-" (prefix meaning surpassing or exceeding) + "sulk" (verb meaning to be silently sullen or bad-tempered)
