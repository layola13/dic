dramedies:/ˈdræmɪdiz/
noun
悲喜剧
A blend of drama and comedy, referring to works that incorporate both serious and humorous elements.
The show is a dramedy that explores the complexities of family relationships. 这部剧是一部悲喜剧，探讨了家庭关系的复杂性。
Synonyms: tragicomedies
Antonyms: pure dramas, pure comedies
