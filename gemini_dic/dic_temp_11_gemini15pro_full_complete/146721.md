overhonest:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːnɪst/ | adj. | 过于诚实的，坦率得近乎粗鲁的 | Excessively honest, to the point of being blunt or insensitive. | He was overhonest in his criticism, which offended some people. 他批评过于直率，冒犯了一些人。|  She gave an overhonest assessment of her performance, highlighting all her flaws. 她对自己表现的评估过于坦诚，突出了所有缺点。| 近义词: blunt, frank, candid, forthright | 反义词: dishonest, deceitful, tactful, diplomatic | 词性: 形容词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "honest" (adjective)
