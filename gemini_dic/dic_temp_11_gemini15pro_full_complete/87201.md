hereby:/ˌhɪrˈbaɪ/| adv.|特此;以此;|By means of this; as a result of this. Used in formal or official language to indicate that something is being done by the act of saying or writing it.|I hereby declare this meeting open. 我特此宣布本次会议开始。|I hereby certify that the above information is true and correct.  我特此证明以上信息真实无误。|近义词：thus, therefore, formally|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 informally)|词性:副词

I hereby grant you permission to access the files. 我特此授予您访问这些文件的权限。
The company hereby agrees to the terms and conditions. 公司特此同意这些条款和条件。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am hereby informing you of the changes. 我正在特此通知您这些变化。(不太常用)
We are hereby notifying you of the upcoming deadline. 我们正在特此通知您即将到来的截止日期。(不太常用)

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have hereby fulfilled my obligations. 我特此履行了我的义务。
We have hereby completed the transaction. 我们特此完成了交易。
