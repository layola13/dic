froise:/frɔɪˈzeɪ/
noun
冰沙葡萄酒（一种用冷冻的玫瑰葡萄酒制成的冰沙状酒精饮料）
A frozen rosé wine slushy.  A blended cocktail made with frozen rosé wine.
例句：The froise was the perfect refreshing drink on a hot summer day.  冰沙葡萄酒是炎炎夏日里完美的清凉饮品。

例句：She ordered a froise with extra strawberries. 她点了一杯加草莓的冰沙葡萄酒。
近义词：frosé, frozen rosé, rosé slushy
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "hot toddy" as a contrasting hot alcoholic beverage)
词性:n.
