coupledom: /ˈkʌpəldəm/
n.
the state of being a couple; the time when two people are a couple
the state of being in a romantic relationship.

例句：
They enjoyed the bliss of coupledom during their honeymoon.
他们在蜜月期间享受着二人世界的幸福。

例句：
After years of single life, she finally embraced coupledom.
在多年的单身生活之后，她终于接受了二人世界。

近义词：relationship, partnership, togetherness
反义词：singlehood, being single

词性: noun

词源:  A blend of "couple" and "-dom" (a suffix meaning "state or condition of being").
