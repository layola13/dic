lammas:/ˈlæməs/| n.|收获节，圣母升天节|A Christian festival celebrated on August 1st, formerly a pagan harvest festival.|例句：Lammas is a time of thanksgiving for the first fruits of the harvest. 收获节是感谢第一次收获的时刻。

例句：The Lammas fair was a major event in the medieval calendar.  收获节交易会在中世纪的日历中是一件大事。|近义词：Lughnasadh, harvest festival|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hlaf-mæsse "loaf-mass," from hlaf "loaf" + mæsse "mass."  It refers to the custom of bringing a loaf of bread made from the first harvest grain to church for blessing.
