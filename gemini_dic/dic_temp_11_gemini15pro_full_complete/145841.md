overdemand:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈmænd/ | n. | 过度需求，超额需求 | A situation where the demand for something exceeds the available supply. |  The overdemand for tickets to the concert led to inflated prices on the secondary market.  演唱会的门票过度需求导致二级市场价格 inflated. | synonyms: excess demand, oversupply | antonyms: underdemand, undersupply | noun
