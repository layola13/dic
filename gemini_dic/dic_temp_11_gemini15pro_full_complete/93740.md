immaterially:/ˌɪməˈtɪəriəli/
adv.
无关紧要地；非实质性地
In a way that is not important or relevant.
The judge ruled that the evidence was immaterial to the case. 法官裁定该证据与案件无关紧要。
The slight differences in price are immaterial. 价格上的细微差别无关紧要。
Synonyms: insignificantly, trivially, unimportantly, negligibly
Antonyms: materially, significantly, substantially, importantly
词性: 副词
