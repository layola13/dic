fogbound:/ˈfɒɡbaʊnd/ (UK) /ˈfɑːɡbaʊnd/ (US)| adj.|被雾困住的；因雾无法航行的|Unable to travel or operate due to thick fog.  Enveloped or surrounded by fog.|The airport was fogbound for several hours. 机场因浓雾关闭了几个小时。|Synonyms: fog-locked, fogged in, socked in|Antonyms: clear, fog-free|adjective
