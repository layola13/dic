overlearnedly:/ˌoʊvərˈlɜːrndli/ | adv. | 过度学习地，机械地 |  In a way that demonstrates excessive learning or practice, often to the point of automaticity and inflexibility. | He recited the poem overlearnedly, without any emotion. 他机械地背诵了这首诗，没有任何感情。 | 近义词: mechanically, rotely, rigidly, automatically | 反义词: creatively, flexibly, spontaneously | 词性: 副词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "learn" + "-ed" (past participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverb suffix)
