mistermed: /ˈmɪstərˌmɛd/
n.
mister + med (a shortened form of "medicine" or "medical")
先生+药物/医疗（medicine 或 medical 的缩写）
A colloquial term for a doctor or someone who provides medical advice or treatment. 对医生或提供医疗建议或治疗的人的口语称呼。
He went to see the mistermed about his back pain. 他去看医生治疗背痛。
近义词：doctor, physician, medic
反义词: patient
词性: 名词
