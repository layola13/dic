cholinergic:/ˌkoʊlɪˈnɜːrdʒɪk/| adj. n.|胆碱能的；胆碱能物质|Relating to or denoting nerve cells in which acetylcholine acts as a neurotransmitter.|例句：Cholinergic neurons are found in both the central and peripheral nervous systems. 胆碱能神经元存在于中枢神经系统和周围神经系统中。

例句：Alzheimer's disease is associated with a loss of cholinergic neurons. 阿尔茨海默病与胆碱能神经元的丧失有关。|近义词:acetylcholine-mediated|反义词: (no direct antonym, perhaps consider "anticholinergic")|词性: adjective, noun
