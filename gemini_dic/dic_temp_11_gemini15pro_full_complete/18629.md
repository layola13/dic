benumb:/bɪˈnʌm/| v. |使麻木；使失去知觉；使迟钝|to make numb; to deprive of sensation; to stun|The cold wind benumbed my fingers. 寒风冻僵了我的手指。|The shock of the news benumbed her.  这突如其来的消息把她惊呆了。|近义词：numb, deaden, paralyze, stupefy|反义词：stimulate, enliven, invigorate|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Middle English *benomen, from Old English benumen (“taken away, seized, deprived of”), past participle of beniman (“to deprive, take away”), from be- + niman (“to take”). Cognate with German benehmen (“to take away, deprive of, seize”).  The figurative sense of "stunned, emotionally paralyzed" developed later.
