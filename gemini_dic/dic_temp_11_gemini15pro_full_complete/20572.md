blackbirding:/ˈblækˌbɜːrdɪŋ/
n.
诱骗或绑架（尤指太平洋岛民）作劳工；非法招募劳工
The practice of kidnapping people to work as forced laborers, especially islanders from the Pacific.
Many Pacific Islanders were victims of blackbirding in the 19th century. 许多太平洋岛民在19世纪是黑奴贸易的受害者。

The ship's captain was accused of blackbirding.  这艘船的船长被指控非法招募劳工。

近义词:kidnapping, enslavement, forced labor, trafficking
反义词:liberation, freedom
