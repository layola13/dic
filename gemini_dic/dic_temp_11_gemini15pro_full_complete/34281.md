clarendon:/ˈklærəndən/ | n. | 克拉伦登, 一种字体, 第四代克拉伦登伯爵 (Edward Hyde, Earl of Clarendon) |A bold, thick typeface.  Named after Edward Hyde, 1st Earl of Clarendon, who was Lord Chancellor of England. | The document was printed in Clarendon font.  这份文件是用克拉伦登字体印刷的。 |近义词: boldface,  Egyptian | 反义词: lightface | 词性: noun

clarendon:/ˈklærəndən/ | adj. |  克拉伦登式的 | Relating to the Earl of Clarendon or his policies. | His Clarendon leanings were evident in his speeches. 他的演讲中明显带有克拉伦登伯爵的政治倾向。|近义词: authoritarian, conservative (in a 17th-century English context)| 反义词: liberal, Whig (in a 17th-century English context)| 词性: adjective
