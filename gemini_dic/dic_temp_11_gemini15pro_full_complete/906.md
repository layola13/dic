abused: /əˈbjuːzd/ /əˈbjuːst/ | v. | 滥用；虐待 | To treat (a person or animal) with cruelty or violence, especially regularly or repeatedly. To use (something) to bad effect or for a bad purpose; misuse. |  He abused his position as manager by giving jobs to his friends. 他滥用经理的职权，把工作给了他的朋友们。 | misuse, mistreat, maltreat | cherish, protect | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *abusen, from Old French abuser "to deceive, misuse," from Latin abuti "to misuse, abuse," from ab- "away, from" + uti "to use."
