creationisms: /kriˈeɪʃənɪzəmz/ | n. | 创造论，神创论 |  The belief that the universe and life originated from specific acts of divine creation, as in the biblical account, rather than by natural processes such as evolution. | Creationisms are still taught in some schools.  一些学校仍在教授神创论。 | 近义词: intelligent design | 反义词: evolution, Darwinism | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From creation + -ism, denoting a doctrine or system.  Ultimately from Latin *creātiō*, from *creāre* "to create".
