oleander:/ˌoʊliˈændər/| n. |夹竹桃|An evergreen shrub or small tree of the dogbane family, cultivated in warm regions for its showy fragrant flowers. All parts of the plant are poisonous.|The oleander bushes were in full bloom, their pink flowers vibrant against the deep green foliage. 夹竹桃灌木盛开着，粉红色的花朵在深绿色的叶子衬托下显得格外鲜艳。|近义词:rosebay (esp. for Nerium oleander) |反义词: N/A|noun
