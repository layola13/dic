disassent:/ˌdɪsəˈsɛnt/| n. vi.|不同意，异议|Disagreement with a proposal or idea.  The judge expressed his disassent from the majority opinion.|例句：Two members dissented from the majority decision.  两名成员对多数人的决定表示异议。

例句：Her disassent was clear from her facial expression. 从她的面部表情可以清楚地看出她不同意。|近义词：disagreement, dissent, objection, opposition|反义词：agreement, assent, consent, concurrence|词性:名词, 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *desassentir, from des- (expressing negation) + assentir (“to assent”). Equivalent to dis- +‎ assent.
