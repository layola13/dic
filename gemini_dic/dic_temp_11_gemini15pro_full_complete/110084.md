lichenologist: /laɪkəˈnɒlədʒɪst/ /ˈlaɪkənɑːlədʒɪst/
noun
地衣学家
A scientist who studies lichens.
Example: The lichenologist discovered a new species of lichen in the rainforest.  这位地衣学家在雨林中发现了一种新的地衣。

Example: The lichenologist presented her research on the effects of air pollution on lichens.  这位地衣学家介绍了她关于空气污染对地衣影响的研究。
Synonym:  lichen expert
Antonym: N/A (No direct antonym for a profession)
词性: noun
