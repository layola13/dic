blanked:/blæŋkt/ | adj. | 空白的，茫然的 | Empty, without any marks or writing; showing a lack of understanding or expression. |  例句：The student handed in a blanked exam paper.  学生交了一张空白的试卷。| 近义词：empty, blank, vacant, expressionless | 反义词：full, complete, expressive | 词性：形容词

blanked:/blæŋkt/ | v. | 掩盖，遮蔽 (通常指情绪或想法) | To cover or hide something, usually emotions or thoughts.  Derived from "blank" meaning empty or void, implying a covering over or suppression of something. From Proto-Germanic *blanga- "shining, white". | 例句：He blanked his disappointment with a forced smile. 他强颜欢笑以掩饰失望。 | 近义词：conceal, suppress, mask, hide | 反义词：reveal, express, show | 词性：动词
