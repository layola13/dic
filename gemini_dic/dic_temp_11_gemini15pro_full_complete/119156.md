mewling:/ˈmjuːlɪŋ/ | adj. | 呜咽的，发出微弱哭声的 | Making a feeble, high-pitched crying sound, like a kitten. |  The mewling kittens huddled together for warmth.  嗷嗷待哺的小猫挤在一起取暖。 | synonyms: whimpering, whining, crying, wailing | antonyms: silent, quiet | adjective


mewling:/ˈmjuːlɪŋ/ | n. | 呜咽声，微弱的哭声 | The act of making a feeble, high-pitched crying sound. | The mewling of the baby kept the parents awake all night. 婴儿的呜咽声让父母整夜无法入睡。 | synonyms: whimper, whine, cry, wail | antonyms: silence, quiet | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *meulen, from Old French miauler, meuler “to mew,” from Late Latin miulare, of imitative origin.
