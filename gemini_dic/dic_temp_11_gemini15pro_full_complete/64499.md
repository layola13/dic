erodibility:/ɪˌroʊdəˈbɪləti/
noun
可蚀性，侵蚀性
The quality of being susceptible to erosion.  The ease with which earth materials are removed by natural forces.
The erodibility of the soil was a major factor in the landslide.  土壤的可蚀性是滑坡的主要因素。
近义词:vulnerability, susceptibility
反义词:resistance, stability
词性:n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From erode + -ible + -ity.  From Latin erodere "to gnaw away," from ex- "out" + rodere "to gnaw."
