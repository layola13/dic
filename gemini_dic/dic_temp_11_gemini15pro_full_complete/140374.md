oilery:/ˈɔɪləri/| adj.|油腻的；油滑的；奉承的|Resembling or suggesting oil; marked by an offensively ingratiating manner; unpleasantly smooth or slick.|His oilery compliments made her uncomfortable. 他油腔滑调的恭维话让她感到不舒服。|近义词：greasy, unctuous, slick, smooth|反义词：dry, rough, sincere, genuine|词性:形容词
Etymology: From oil + -ery, a suffix forming nouns and adjectives denoting a place or state characterized by the element in the main word.  Ultimately from Latin *oleum*, meaning "oil."
