familiarizes:/fəˈmɪliəˌraɪzɪz/
verb
使熟悉，使了解
to make someone familiar with something; to accustom someone to something
The training program familiarizes new employees with the company's procedures.  这个培训项目让新员工熟悉公司的流程。
The museum's interactive exhibits familiarize children with science.  博物馆的互动展览让孩子们了解科学。
acquaint, introduce
alienate, estrange
v.
