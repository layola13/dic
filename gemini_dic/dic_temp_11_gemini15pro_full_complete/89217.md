homewards:/ˈhoʊmwərdz/ (US), /ˈhəʊmwədz/ (UK)| adv.|回家，朝着家的方向|Towards home; in the direction of home.|She started walking homewards. 她开始朝家的方向走去。|He glanced homewards, a wistful look in his eyes. 他朝家的方向瞥了一眼，眼神里流露出一丝渴望。|近义词：toward home, back home|反义词：away from home|词性: 副词

词源: From Old English *hāmweardes, from hām "home" + -weardes, adverbial genitive of -weard "toward".  The suffix is related to the comparative degree of adjectives, hence the sense of "in the direction of."
