nothing-but:/ˈnʌθɪŋ bʌt/| phrase|只有，只不过，仅仅|Used to emphasize that something is only one thing and nothing else.|He's nothing but a liar. 他只不过是个骗子。|Synonyms: only, merely, solely, just|Antonyms: everything but, more than|Phrase

nothing-but:/ˈnʌθɪŋ bʌt/| adj.|纯粹的，十足的|Used to emphasize the extent or degree of something, usually negative.|It was nothing but a waste of time. 这纯粹是浪费时间。|Synonyms: pure, sheer, utter, complete|Antonyms: partial, incomplete|Adjective
