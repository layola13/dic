benignity:/bɪˈnɪɡnɪti/ | n. |仁慈；善意；和蔼| Kindness, gentleness, or goodwill.  A disposition to be kind and gracious. |  She accepted his apology with benignity. 她宽厚地接受了他的道歉。| Synonyms: kindness, compassion, generosity, benevolence, goodwill | Antonyms: malevolence, cruelty, hostility, malice, unkindness | noun

benignity:/bɪˈnɪɡnɪti/ | n. |良性；温和|  The quality of being mild and not harmful. (Mostly used in a medical context to describe tumors or conditions). | The doctor assured her that the tumor was benignity. 医生向她保证肿瘤是良性的。| Synonyms: mildness, harmlessness, innocuousness | Antonyms: malignancy, virulence, severity | noun
