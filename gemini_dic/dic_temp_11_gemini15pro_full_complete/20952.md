bleep: /bliːp/ | n. v. | 哔声，发出哔声 | A short, high-pitched electronic sound. |  The radar emitted a bleep.  雷达发出哔哔声。 | 近义词：beep, chirp | 反义词: silence | 词性:名词，动词
bleep: /bliːp/ | v. | 消音，审查删除（不雅内容） | To remove (offensive language) from a broadcast. | They bleeped out the swear words. 他们把脏话消音了。| 近义词：censor | 反义词: broadcast | 词性:动词
bleep: /bliːp/ | n. | 电子信号，嘟嘟声 | A short electronic signal, especially one used as a locator. |  The device emits a bleep to signal its location.  这个装置发出嘟嘟声来表示它的位置。| 近义词：signal, beep | 反义词: - | 词性:名词

词源:  Likely imitative of the sound.  可能是模仿声音的。
