flowerily:/ˈflaʊərɪli/| adv.|华丽地，绚丽地，词藻华丽地|In a flowery, ornate, or elaborate manner, often excessively so; using many decorative words or phrases.|She wrote flowerily about the beauty of the sunset. 她用华丽的辞藻描写了日落的美丽。|近义词：ornately, elaborately, fancifully, rhetorically|反义词：simply, plainly, concisely|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From flower + -y + -ly.  Ultimately from Latin *flor-, from flos "flower."
