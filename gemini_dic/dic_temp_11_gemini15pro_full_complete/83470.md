hachure:/ˈhæʃər/ (US), /ˈhætʃʊə(r)/ (UK)| n. |影线；剖面线；短平行线|A series of short, parallel lines used in drawing and engraving to indicate shading or texture, or to represent different materials in a cross section.|The architect used hachures to indicate the slope of the land in the drawing. 建筑师在图纸中用影线表示土地的坡度。|近义词：cross-hatching, shading, hatching|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "blank" or "unshaded")|词性:noun

hachure:/ˈhæʃər/ (US), /ˈhætʃʊə(r)/ (UK)| v. |用影线画；用剖面线表示|To shade or mark with hachures.|He hachured the area on the map to represent forest. 他用影线标示地图上的森林区域。|近义词：shade, cross-hatch, hatch|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "erase" or "clear")|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *hachure*, from *hacher* "to chop," from Frankish *hatjan*, from Proto-Germanic *hatjaną* "to hack, hew."  Ultimately related to English *hatchet*.
