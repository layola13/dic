gelatinous:/dʒɪˈlætɪnəs/ | adj. | 胶状的；凝胶状的 | Having a jelly-like consistency; viscous. |  The jellyfish had a gelatinous body. 水母有一个胶状的身体。|  The soup had cooled and become gelatinous. 汤冷却后变成了胶状。| 近义词：jelly-like, viscous, gummy, slimy | 反义词： watery, thin, runny | 词性: 形容词
gelatin:/ˈdʒɛlətɪn/ | n. | 明胶，凝胶 | A translucent, colorless, brittle (when dry), flavorless foodstuff, derived from collagen obtained from various animal by-products. | She made a delicious gelatin dessert. 她做了一个美味的明胶甜点。 | He added gelatin to thicken the sauce. 他加了明胶来使酱汁变稠。 | 近义词：jelly, agar-agar | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "liquid" in some contexts) | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Latin *gelatinosus, from Latin gelatus (“frozen,” past participle of gelare, “to freeze”), + -osus (suffix forming adjectives meaning "full of, abounding in"). The sense shift from "frozen" to "jelly-like" likely occurred due to the similar textures.
