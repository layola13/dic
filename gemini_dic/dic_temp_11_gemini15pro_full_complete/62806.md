enhanced:/ɪnˈhɑːnst/ | /ɪnˈhænst/ | adj. | 增强的，提高的 |  Increased or improved in quality, value, or extent. |  例句：The enhanced security measures made the building much safer. 增强的安全措施使大楼更加安全。

例句：The software has enhanced graphics capabilities. 该软件具有增强的图形功能。


近义词：improved, better, superior, augmented, upgraded | 反义词：reduced, diminished, weakened, impaired | 词性：形容词

词源: From the Old French word "enhauncer," meaning "to raise," ultimately from the Latin "in" (in) and "altiare" (to make high).
