grille:/ɡrɪl/| n.|格栅，烤架，铁栅栏|A grating or screen of metal bars or wires, often used as a barrier or covering.|The car’s grille was damaged in the accident. 汽车的格栅在事故中损坏了。|synonyms: grating, grid, mesh, screen|antonyms: opening, gap|noun

grille:/ɡrɪl/| v.|烧烤，拷问|To cook food over direct heat, typically on a metal grill.  Also, to question intensely.|We grilled steaks for dinner. 我们晚餐烤了牛排。
The police grilled the suspect for hours. 警方对嫌疑人进行了数小时的审讯。|synonyms: barbecue, broil, grill, question, interrogate|antonyms: freeze, chill (for cooking);  exonerate, release (for questioning)|verb
