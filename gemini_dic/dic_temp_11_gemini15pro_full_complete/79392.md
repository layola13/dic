gliding:/ˈɡlaɪdɪŋ/| n. adj. v.|滑翔，滑行，滑行似的|smooth and effortless movement, as if sliding through air or water.  The act of moving smoothly and effortlessly.|The eagle was gliding effortlessly on the wind currents.  老鹰毫不费力地在气流中滑翔。|近义词：soaring, drifting, coasting|反义词:stalling, plummeting, descending|词性:noun, adjective, verb (present participle of glide)


gliding:/ˈɡlaɪdɪŋ/| adj.|滑行似的|smooth and effortless in movement.|She has a gliding walk. 她走路很轻盈。|近义词：flowing, smooth, effortless|反义词:jerky, awkward, clumsy|词性:adjective


gliding:/ˈɡlaɪdɪŋ/| v.|滑翔，滑行|(present participle of glide)  To move smoothly and effortlessly.|The skater was gliding across the ice.  滑冰者在冰面上滑行。|近义词：drifting, coasting, soaring|反义词:stalling, plummeting, descending|词性:verb (present participle)


词源:From Middle English *gliden, from Old English glīdan (“to glide, slip, slide”), from Proto-Germanic *glīdaną (“to glide, slide”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰleyd- (“to slip, slide”).
