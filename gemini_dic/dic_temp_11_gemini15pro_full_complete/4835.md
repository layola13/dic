alibis:/ˈælɪbaɪz/| n.|不在场证明；托辞；借口|A claim or piece of evidence that one was elsewhere when an act, typically a criminal one, is alleged to have taken place.|He had a perfect alibi for the night of the murder. 他在谋杀案发生的当晚有完美的不在场证明。|近义词：excuse, pretext, defense|反义词：proof, evidence|noun
