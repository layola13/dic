inflammable:/ɪnˈflæməbl/| adj.|易燃的，可燃的|Easily set on fire; flammable.  The word "inflammable" can be confusing because the prefix "in-" often means "not." However, in this case, "inflammable" means the *same* as "flammable." It derives from the Latin "inflammare," meaning "to set on fire."|This material is highly inflammable, so keep it away from open flames. 这种材料高度易燃，请远离明火。|近义词：flammable, combustible|反义词：nonflammable, fireproof|词性:形容词

inflammable:/ɪnˈflæməbl/| adj.|（指人）易激动的，易怒的|Easily roused to anger or excitement. This meaning is less common than the "easily set on fire" meaning.|His inflammable temper often got him into trouble. 他易怒的脾气经常给他带来麻烦。|近义词：irascible, choleric, excitable|反义词：calm, placid, even-tempered|词性:形容词
