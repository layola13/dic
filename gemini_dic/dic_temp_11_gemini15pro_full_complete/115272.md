manitou: /ˈmænɪtuː/ (US), /ˈmænɪtuː/ (UK)| n.| 神灵；超自然力量；守护神|A spirit, a supernatural power, or a guardian spirit, especially among some Native American peoples.|The tribe believed the manitou resided in the ancient cedar tree. 部落相信神灵居住在古老的雪松树里。|Example 2: He felt the power of the manitou guiding him through the forest. 他感觉到神灵的力量指引他穿过森林。|Synonyms: spirit, deity, god, guardian angel|Antonyms: demon, devil|Noun
