gerain:/dʒəˈreɪn/| n.|天竺葵|A flowering plant of the genus Geranium, which includes both hardy species and tender varieties often grown as houseplants or bedding plants.  They are known for their colorful flowers and often fragrant foliage.|The geraniums on the balcony were a vibrant red.  阳台上的天竺葵鲜红夺目。|近义词：cranesbill, pelargonium (often used interchangeably)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

词源: From French *géranium*, from Latin *geranium*, from Ancient Greek *γεράνιον (geránion)*, diminutive of *γέρανος (géranos)* meaning "crane," because the fruit resembles a crane's bill.
