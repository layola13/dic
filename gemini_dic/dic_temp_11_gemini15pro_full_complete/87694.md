hexametrist:/hɛkˈsæmɪtrɪst/ | n. | 六音步诗人；六步格诗人 | A writer of hexameter verse. |  Homer was a renowned hexametrist. 荷马是一位著名的六音步诗人。

Milton also employed hexameters in his epic poem.  弥尔顿在他的史诗中也使用了六音步格。 |  poet, versifier |  prosaist | noun

Etymology: From Greek *hexametros* (six-measure) + -ist.  *Hexa-* means six, and *-meter* refers to a metrical unit in poetry.
