de-Americanizations: /diːəˈmɛrɪkənaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 去美国化；非美国化 | The process of removing American influence or characteristics. |  The historian documented the de-Americanizations that occurred in the Philippines after independence.  这位历史学家记录了菲律宾独立后发生的去美国化现象。|  de-Westernization, localization | Americanization | noun

Etymology:  "de-" (prefix meaning "removal" or "reversal") + "Americanization" (the process of becoming Americanized)
