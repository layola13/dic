disciplinarity:/ˌdɪsəplɪˈnærɪti/
n.|学科性，学科化|The state of being divided into or organized by disciplines.  A system of knowledge specialties. |The conference explored the growing disciplinarity in academic research.|近义词： specialization, compartmentalization|反义词: interdisciplinarity, multidisciplinarity|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *disciplinarité*, from disciplinaire (disciplinary) + -ité (-ity). Ultimately from Latin disciplina (instruction, knowledge).
