nonlubricious:/ˌnɒnlubˈrɪʃəs/ /ˌnɑːnlubˈrɪʃəs/| adj.|不光滑的，不润滑的，粗糙的|Not slippery or smooth; lacking lubrication.  Relating to a surface or substance that does not readily slide or glide. Often used figuratively to describe something that is awkward, clumsy, or lacking in social grace.|The nonlubricious surface of the sandpaper made it ideal for smoothing rough edges. 这张砂纸不光滑的表面使它非常适合打磨粗糙的边缘。| The speaker's nonlubricious delivery made it difficult for the audience to follow his train of thought. 演讲者笨拙的表达方式让观众很难跟上他的思路。|近义词：rough, coarse, abrasive, friction-inducing, unsmooth|反义词: smooth, slippery, lubricated, slick|词性:形容词
