invulnerable:/ɪnˈvʌlnərəbl/| adj.|刀枪不入的，不会受伤害的；无懈可击的|Not able to be wounded or harmed; immune to attack. |例句：He believed himself invulnerable to criticism. 他认为自己不受批评的影响。

|例句：The system is invulnerable to attack. 这个系统不受攻击的影响。

|近义词：impregnable, invincible, unassailable|反义词：vulnerable, susceptible, weak|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *invulnerabilis, from in- (“not”) + vulnerabilis (“wounding”). Equivalent to in- + vulnerable.
