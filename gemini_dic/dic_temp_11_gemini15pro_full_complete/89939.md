horseknop:/ˈhɔːrsˌnɒp/ (US) /ˈhɔːsˌnɒp/ (UK)| n. |马扣；马结；系紧的结|A knot used to tie a horse, typically a quick-release knot. It is also sometimes used to refer to any secure knot. |例句：He tied the rope with a horseknop to secure the horse to the post. 他用马扣把绳子系在柱子上，把马拴牢了。

例句：The sailor quickly tied a horseknop to secure the sails. 水手迅速打了个马结来固定帆。|近义词：hitch, knot, bend|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
