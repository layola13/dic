compar:/kəmˈpɑːr/|/kəmˈpɛər/| v. |比较，对照|To examine the similarities and differences between two or more things.|例句：Compare the two products and choose the better one. 比较两种产品，选择更好的一个。|例句：She compared her handwriting with her mother's. 她把自己的笔迹和她母亲的笔迹作了比较。|近义词：contrast, juxtapose, liken, equate|反义词:differentiate, distinguish|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *comparien*, from Old French *comparer*, from Latin *comparāre* (“to couple, match, pair, compare”), from *com-* + *parāre* (“to prepare, make ready”).
