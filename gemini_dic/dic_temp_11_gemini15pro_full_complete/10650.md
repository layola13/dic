appropriativeness:/əˌprəʊprieɪtɪvnɪs/| n. |适宜性，恰当性；合宜性|The quality of being appropriate or suitable for a particular person, purpose, or situation.  The appropriateness of his remarks was questioned. |例句：The appropriateness of her attire for the funeral was questioned.  她的服装是否适合葬礼受到了质疑。

例句：He demonstrated a lack of appropriateness in his behavior at the formal dinner. 在正式晚宴上，他的行为表现出缺乏分寸感。|近义词：suitability, fitness, aptness, propriety|反义词: inappropriateness, unsuitability, impropriety|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From appropriate (adjective) + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  Appropriate comes from the Latin appropriare "to make one's own," from ad- "to" + proprius "one's own."
