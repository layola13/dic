disputation:/ˌdɪspjuˈteɪʃn/
n.
辩论，争论
a formal debate or argument on a subject.
The two philosophers engaged in a lively disputation about the nature of reality.  两位哲学家就现实的本质进行了激烈的辩论。
debate, argument, controversy, contention
agreement, accord, harmony
noun
