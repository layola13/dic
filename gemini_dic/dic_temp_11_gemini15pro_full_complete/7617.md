annuals:/ˈænjuəlz/
n.
一年生植物
Plants that complete their life cycle within one year.
Many gardeners plant annuals for a burst of color in their gardens. 许多园丁种植一年生植物，为他们的花园增添一抹色彩。
Bright annuals like petunias and marigolds filled the window boxes.  鲜艳的一年生植物，如矮牵牛和万寿菊，装满了窗台上的花盆。
近义词:yearly plants
反义词:perennials, biennials
词性:名词
