fifi:/ˈfiːfiː/| n.|菲菲（女子名）,时髦女子,漂亮女孩|A woman's name, often suggesting stylishness or prettiness.  It can also be used as a term of endearment for a small, stylish dog.|例句：My aunt's name is Fifi and she always dresses impeccably. 我阿姨的名字叫菲菲，她总是穿着无可挑剔。

例句：We call our little poodle Fifi because she's so chic.  我们叫我们的小贵宾犬菲菲，因为它很时髦。|近义词：stylish woman, fashionable girl, chic lady|反义词:plain jane, frump|词性:noun
