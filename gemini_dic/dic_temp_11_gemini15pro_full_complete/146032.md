overdress: /ˌoʊvərˈdrɛs/ | v. n. | 过度穿着；穿着过于正式；盛装 | To dress too elaborately or formally for an occasion; clothes that are too elaborate or formal for an occasion. | She overdressed for the picnic in a silk dress and heels. 她穿着丝绸连衣裙和高跟鞋去野餐，穿得太正式了。|  近义词: dress up, over attire | 反义词: underdress, dress down | 词性: 动词, 名词

overdress: /ˌoʊvərˈdrɛs/ | n. | 盛装；过于正式的服装 | Clothes that are too elaborate or formal for an occasion. | The overdress she chose for the party was a bit much. 她为派对选择的盛装有点过分了。| 近义词: finery, formal wear | 反义词: casual wear | 词性: 名词

词源:  From over- + dress.  "Over-" acts as a prefix indicating "too much" or "excessive."
