outstation:/ˌaʊtˈsteɪʃən/ | n. | 分站；外站；地方办事处 | A station or office located away from a central or main location, typically in a remote or rural area. |  The company has several outstations in remote parts of the country.  这家公司在该国偏远地区设有几个分站。 | synonyms: branch office, field office, satellite office | antonyms: headquarters, main office | noun

outstation:/ˌaʊtˈsteɪʃən/ | adj. | 外地的；偏远的 | Located away from a central or main location; relating to an outstation. | He was assigned to an outstation post in the mountains. 他被派往山区的一个外站工作。 | synonyms: remote, outlying, peripheral | antonyms: central, main, metropolitan | adjective
