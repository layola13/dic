make the rounds:/meɪk ðə raʊndz/
verb phrase
四处走动；巡视；流传
to visit many people or places, often as part of a job or routine; to be passed from one person to another
The doctor made the rounds of the hospital wards. 医生巡视了医院的病房。
The rumor quickly made the rounds. 谣言迅速传播开来。
synonyms: circulate, spread, go around
antonyms: stay put, remain, localize
