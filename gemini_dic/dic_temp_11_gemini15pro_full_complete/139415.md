observingly:/əbˈzɜːrvɪŋli/
adverb|观察地；注意地|In a way that involves watching carefully. |She watched him observingly, trying to gauge his reaction. 她观察着他，试图揣摩他的反应。|近义词：watchfully, attentively, carefully|反义词：inattentively, carelessly|词性: 副词
词源: From observe (v.) + -ing (suffix forming present participles and verbal nouns) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Latin observare "to watch, heed."
