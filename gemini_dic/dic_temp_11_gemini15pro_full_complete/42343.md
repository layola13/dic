counteractions:/ˌkaʊntərˈækʃənz/
|n.|对抗措施，反作用，抵消|Actions taken to oppose or counteract something else. |The government took counteractions to mitigate the effects of the recession. 政府采取了应对措施来减轻经济衰退的影响。|The doctor prescribed medication as a counteraction to the allergic reaction. 医生开了药来对抗过敏反应。| synonyms: remedies, antidotes, measures, steps| antonyms: actions, inactions |noun


counteraction:/ˌkaʊntərˈækʃən/
|n.|对抗措施，反作用，抵消|The act or process of counteracting something. |The counteraction of the poison was swift.  解毒剂的作用很快。 | Eating a healthy diet is a good counteraction to the effects of stress. 健康饮食是对抗压力影响的好方法。 |synonyms: response, reaction, resistance| antonyms: support, aid, assistance |noun
