acrimoniously:/ˌækrɪˈmoʊniəsli/
adverb
尖刻地，激烈地
in a bitter, angry, or resentful way
例句：The divorce proceedings were conducted acrimoniously. 离婚诉讼进行得非常激烈。

例句：They argued acrimoniously about the terms of the contract.  他们就合同条款进行了激烈的争论。

近义词：bitterly, angrily, resentfully, caustically, spitefully
反义词：amicably, civilly, pleasantly, cordially
词性：adverb

词源：From Latin *acrimonia* ("sharpness, pungency of taste or feeling"), from *acer* ("sharp").
