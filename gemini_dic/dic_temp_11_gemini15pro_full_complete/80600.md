gooier:/ˈɡuːɪər/| adj.|更粘的，更胶状的|More gooey; stickier and more viscous.|The cake was even gooier than the last one.  这个蛋糕比上一个更粘稠。|近义词：stickier, gummier, more viscous|反义词：less gooey, less sticky, drier|词性:形容词
词源: From gooey + -er (comparative suffix). Ultimately from goo.  由 gooey 加上比较级后缀 -er 构成。最终来源于 goo。
