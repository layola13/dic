intramural:/ˌɪntrəˈmjʊərəl/ | adj. | 校内的，内部的 | Existing or occurring within the bounds of an institution, especially a school or university. | Example: The university has a vibrant intramural sports program. 大学有一个充满活力的校内体育项目。| Example: Intramural competitions foster a sense of community among students. 校内比赛培养了学生之间的社区意识。| Synonyms: internal, domestic, inner | Antonyms: external, outside, intercollegiate | 词性: 形容词
