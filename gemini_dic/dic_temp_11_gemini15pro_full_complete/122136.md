mistreated:/ˌmɪsˈtriːtɪd/
verb | 虐待，不公平对待 | to treat someone badly, cruelly, or unfairly |  The animals were cruelly mistreated by their owner. 这些动物被它们的主人残酷虐待。| abused, ill-treated, maltreated | cherished, cared for
adjective | 受到虐待的，被不公平对待的 | having been treated badly, cruelly, or unfairly |  She felt mistreated and undervalued. 她感到自己被虐待和低估了。| abused, ill-treated, maltreated | cherished, cared for

ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- + treated, past participle of treat.  The prefix "mis-" means "badly" or "wrongly".
