fissipalmate:/ˌfɪsɪˈpælmɪt/
adjective
半蹼的；蹼裂的
Having the toes or fingers webbed only part of their length, as in the feet of many shorebirds.
例句：The semipalmated plover has fissipalmate feet. 半蹼鸻的脚是半蹼的。

例句：The northern jacana's fissipalmate feet allow it to walk on floating vegetation.  水雉的半蹼脚使它能够在漂浮的植被上行走。

近义词: semipalmate, totipalmate
反义词: (no direct antonym, but consider) non-webbed, separate

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fissus* (cleft, divided) + *palmatus* (palmate, having the shape of a hand).  *Fissus* is the past participle of *findere* (to cleave, to split).  *Palmatus* is derived from *palma* (palm of the hand).
