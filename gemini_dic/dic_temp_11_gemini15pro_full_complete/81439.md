gratification:/ˌɡrætɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/| n.|满足；满意；喜悦|The feeling of pleasure or satisfaction when you have done something you wanted to do. |She felt a deep sense of gratification at having completed the marathon. 她完成马拉松后感到深深的满足感。|Example:  He derived great gratification from helping others. 他从帮助别人中获得了极大的满足感。|Synonyms: satisfaction, pleasure, fulfillment, contentment|Antonyms: dissatisfaction, displeasure, discontent|noun
