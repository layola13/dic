exultingly:/ɪɡˈzʌltɪŋli/
adverb
欣喜若狂地，得意洋洋地
in a way that shows great happiness or triumph
She smiled exultingly as she crossed the finish line. 她冲过终点线时欣喜若狂地笑了。
Near Synonyms: triumphantly, jubilantly, joyfully
Near Antonyms: dejectedly, despondently, sadly
词性: 副词
