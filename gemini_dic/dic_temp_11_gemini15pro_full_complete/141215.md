oophytic:/ˌoʊəˈfɪtɪk/
adjective
卵生的；卵生植物的
Producing eggs that hatch outside the body; relating to a plant that grows on another plant, but not as a parasite.
Oophytic fungi release their spores into the environment. 卵生真菌将孢子释放到环境中。
Many orchids are oophytic, growing on trees for support. 许多兰花是附生的，生长在树上以获得支撑。
近义词: oviparous (for animals), epiphytic (for plants)
反义词: viviparous (for animals)
词性: adjective
