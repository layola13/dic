johnsonese:/ˌdʒɒnˈsʌnˌiːz/ |n.|约翰逊式的英语；冗长晦涩的文风|A style of writing characterized by long, complex sentences and obscure vocabulary, as exemplified by the writings of Samuel Johnson.|His writing was full of johnsonese, making it difficult for the average reader to understand. 他的写作充满了约翰逊式的英语，让普通读者难以理解。|近义词:sesquipedalianism, bombast, verbosity|反义词:conciseness, brevity, simplicity|词性:名词
