armless:/ˈɑːrmləs/| adj.|无臂的，没有扶手的|Lacking arms; having no arms.  Also, lacking armrests (as in a chair).|The armless man typed with his feet.  这位无臂男子用脚打字。|The armless chair was uncomfortable to sit in for long periods. 这把没有扶手的椅子坐久了不舒服。|近义词：limbless, handless (for people); without armrests (for furniture)|反义词：armed, having arms|词性:形容词
