disclaimer:/dɪˈskleɪmər/ | n. | 免责声明；放弃权利声明 | A statement that denies legal responsibility. | The website included a disclaimer about the accuracy of its content. 该网站包含了关于其内容准确性的免责声明。 | Example:  Before the show began, a disclaimer warned viewers about the graphic content. 节目开始前，一个免责声明警告观众注意其中的图像内容。

Example: The advertisement had a small disclaimer at the bottom about potential side effects. 广告底部有一个小免责声明，说明了潜在的副作用。


Synonym: waiver, release, denial | Antonym: acceptance, admission | Noun
