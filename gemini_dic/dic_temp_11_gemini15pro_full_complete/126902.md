narcosis:/nɑːrˈkoʊsɪs/ /nɑːˈkəʊsɪs/| n. |麻醉；昏迷|A state of stupor, unconsciousness, or insensibility caused by drugs or other agents.|The patient slipped into narcosis after the anesthesia was administered. 病人在麻醉药注射后陷入了昏迷状态。|Synonyms: stupor, coma, anesthesia|Antonyms: alertness, consciousness|noun
