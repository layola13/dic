governmental:/ˌɡʌvərˈmentl/ | adj. |政府的；与政府有关的 | Relating to government or the process of governing. | The governmental agency is responsible for regulating the industry. 政府机构负责监管该行业。|  近义词: public, official, state | 反义词: private, nongovernmental | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: from Old French governemental, from governement (see government).
