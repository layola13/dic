hogsty:/ˈhɒɡstaɪ/ (UK), /ˈhɑːɡstaɪ/ (US)| n. |猪圈；肮脏的地方|A pen or enclosure where pigs are kept; a filthy or messy place.|The farm had a large hogsty full of pigs.  农场里有一个装满猪的大猪圈。

The teenager's bedroom was a complete hogsty.  这青少年的卧室简直就是个猪窝。|近义词：pigpen, pigsty, sty|反义词: clean place, tidy place|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From hog + sty.  Hog, meaning "pig," comes from Old English *hogg. Sty, meaning "pen for pigs," comes from Old English stige, meaning "climb, ascent, ladder."  The word "sty" itself also refers to any dirty or messy place.
