llamas:/ˈlɑːməz/ | n. |美洲驼|A domesticated pack animal of the camel family, found in the Andes, that is used as a beast of burden and whose wool is used for textiles. It is closely related to the alpaca.| We saw a herd of llamas grazing on the hillside. 我们看到一群美洲驼在山坡上吃草。|近义词：alpaca, vicuña|反义词: None|noun


llamas:/ˈlɑːməz/ | n. |喇嘛(复数)|The plural form of "llama," referring to the Buddhist spiritual leader of Tibet.|The Dalai Lama is one of the most respected llamas in the world. 达赖喇嘛是世界上最受尊敬的喇嘛之一。|近义词：monk, guru|反义词: None|noun
