omnisciently:/ɑːmˈnɪʃəntli/| adv.| 全知地，无所不知地 | In a way that suggests complete or unlimited knowledge. | The narrator, omnisciently, revealed the character's innermost thoughts. 叙述者以全知的方式揭示了人物内心深处的想法。|  Synonym: all-knowing, all-seeing | Antonym: unknowingly, ignorantly |词性: 副词

omniscient:/ɑːmˈnɪʃənt/| adj.| 全知的，无所不知的 | Having complete or unlimited knowledge, awareness, or understanding; perceiving all things. |  An omniscient God. 全知全能的上帝 | Synonym: all-knowing, all-seeing | Antonym: ignorant, unknowing |词性: 形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin omniscient- ‘all-knowing’, from the verb omniscire, from omni- ‘all’ + scire ‘to know’.
