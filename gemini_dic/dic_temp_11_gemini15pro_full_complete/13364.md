atmology: /ætˈmɒlədʒi/
n.
atmology (非标准词汇)
The study of atms or the ATM industry.  This word is not a standard English word and likely a neologism.
Example: He's developing a new atmology program at the university, focusing on the security aspects of ATM networks. 他正在大学里开发一个新的 ATM 学科项目，重点关注 ATM 网络的安全性。
Example:  Her dissertation in atmology explored the evolution of ATM technology from simple cash dispensers to sophisticated banking terminals. 她的 ATM 学科论文探讨了 ATM 技术从简单的现金分配器到复杂的银行终端的演变。
Near Synonyms:  ATM industry studies,  ATM technology research (these are descriptive phrases, not single words)
Antonyms: None. (Because this is not a standard word, it doesn't have established antonyms.)
Etymology:  Likely a blend of "ATM" (Automated Teller Machine) and "-logy" (study of).
