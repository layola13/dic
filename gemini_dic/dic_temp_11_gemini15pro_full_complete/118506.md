mesoamerican: /ˌmɛzoʊəˈmɛrɪkən/ | n. | 中美洲人 | A native or inhabitant of Mesoamerica. | Many mesoamericans cultivated maize as their primary crop. 许多中美洲人以玉米为主要作物。|  The mesoamericans developed complex systems of writing.  中美洲人发展了复杂的文字系统。 | Near synonyms: Central American | Antonym: None | noun
