jackrabbited:/ˈdʒækˌræbɪtɪd/
verb
像野兔一样快速移动; 快速而不稳定的移动;  (发动机) 突然加速;
To move or operate in a quick, jerky, or erratic manner, often like a jackrabbit.  (Of an engine) to accelerate suddenly and excessively.
The car jackrabbited forward when he slammed the gas pedal. 汽车在他猛踩油门时向前窜了一下。
近义词:  lurch, jerk, bolt, surge, jump
反义词:  crawl, creep, slow, decelerate
词性: verb


jackrabbited:/ˈdʒækˌræbɪtɪd/
adjective
(发动机) 容易突然加速的; 快速而不稳定的;
(Of an engine) prone to sudden acceleration; quick and erratic.
The engine was jackrabbited and difficult to control.  发动机容易突然加速，难以控制。
近义词: jerky, erratic, jumpy, unstable
反义词: smooth, steady, stable, controlled
词性: adjective


词源:  From "jackrabbit," a type of hare known for its quick, jerky movements, + "-ed," forming a verb or adjective.
