counterrallies:/ˌkaʊntərˈræliz/ | n. |反集会，对抗性集会 |A demonstration or organized gathering in opposition to another. |例句：Counterrallies were held in response to the controversial speaker.  为了回应有争议的演讲者，举行了反集会。

例句：The counterrallies attracted a larger crowd than the original protest. 反集会吸引了比最初抗议更多的人群。

|近义词：counterdemonstrations, counterprotests |反义词: rallies, demonstrations |词性: noun
