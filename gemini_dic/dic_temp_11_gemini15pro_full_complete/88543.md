hit-the-roof:/hɪt ðə ruːf/
verb phrase
勃然大怒，暴跳如雷
to become extremely angry
例句：When Dad found out I'd crashed his car, he hit the roof. 当爸爸发现我撞了他的车时，他勃然大怒。

例句：My mom hit the roof when she saw my report card.  我妈妈看到我的成绩单时大发雷霆。

近义词：go ballistic, blow one's top, lose one's temper, fly off the handle, go ape
反义词：stay calm, remain composed
词性：动词短语
