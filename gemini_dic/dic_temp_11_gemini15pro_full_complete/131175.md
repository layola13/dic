nonbreedings:/ˌnɒnˈbriːdɪŋz/
adjective
非繁殖的
Not involved in breeding; relating to a period when animals do not breed.
The nonbreeding season for these birds is during the winter months. 这些鸟类的非繁殖季节在冬季。
non-breeding, nonreproductive
breeding, reproductive
