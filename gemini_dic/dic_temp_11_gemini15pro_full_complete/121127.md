misdeemed:/ˌmɪsˈdiːmd/
verb
判断错误；误判
incorrectly judged or regarded
He misdeemed her silence as consent. 他误以为她的沉默就是同意。
近义词:misjudged, misinterpreted, mistaken
反义词:understood, correctly judged
