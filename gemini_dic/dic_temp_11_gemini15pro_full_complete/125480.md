multipresence:/ˌmʌltiˈprɛzəns/| n.|多重存在，多地存在|The concept of multipresence refers to the ability of a person to be present in multiple locations, either physically or virtually, at the same time. 多重存在的概念指的是一个人能够同时在多个地点出现的能力，无论是物理上还是虚拟上。|With virtual reality technology, multipresence in online gaming is becoming increasingly sophisticated. 借助虚拟现实技术，网络游戏中的多重存在变得越来越复杂。|The CEO utilized holographic projection to achieve multipresence at multiple conferences simultaneously. 首席执行官利用全息投影技术同时出席了多个会议。|synonyms: ubiquity, omnipresence, telepresence|antonyms: absence, singularity|noun
