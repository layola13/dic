cardamine:/ˈkɑːrdəmaɪn/| n. |碎米荠|A genus of plants in the mustard family (Brassicaceae), typically having small white or pink flowers and often found in damp habitats. They are also known as bittercress or cuckoo flower.|The stream banks were carpeted with cardamine.|近义词：bittercress, cuckoo flower|反义词: N/A|noun
