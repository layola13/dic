calamite:/ˈkæləˌmaɪt/| n.|磁铁矿，灾祸，不幸|A mineral that is magnetic; lodestone. A misfortune or disaster.|例句：The calamite attracted the iron filings. 磁铁矿吸引了铁屑。


例句：The flood was a calamite for the local farmers. 洪水对当地农民来说是一场灾难。|近义词：magnet, lodestone, disaster, catastrophe, tragedy|反义词:blessing, boon, fortune|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *calamite*, from Latin *calamitas* ("misfortune, damage, loss"), related to *calamus* ("reed, stalk"). The mineral sense arose from the magnetic properties of some iron ores.
