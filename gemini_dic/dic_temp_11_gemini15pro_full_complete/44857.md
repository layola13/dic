crusty:/ˈkrʌsti/| adj.|硬皮的，易怒的，暴躁的|Having a hard or crisp crust; bad-tempered and irritable.|The bread had a delicious, crusty exterior. 这面包有着美味的硬皮。  He gave me a crusty reply. 他粗暴地回答了我。|近义词：crispy, brittle, grumpy, surly|反义词:soft, pliable, amiable, friendly|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *crusted, from Old English crust, from Latin crusta (“rind, shell, bark”)
