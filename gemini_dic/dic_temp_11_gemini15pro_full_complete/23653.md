brandishers:/ˈbrændɪʃərz/ | n. |挥舞者；炫耀者 | People who brandish something, typically a weapon or something they want to show off. | The brandishers of swords stood ready for battle.  挥舞着剑的人准备战斗。| 近义词：wielders, flaunters | 反义词：concealers, hiders | noun

brandish:/ˈbrændɪʃ/ | vt. |挥舞；炫耀 | To wave or flourish (something, especially a weapon) as a threat or in anger or excitement. To display or show off something ostentatiously. | He brandished a knife at them. 他挥舞着刀子威胁他们。  She proudly brandished her new diploma. 她自豪地炫耀着她的新文凭。| 近义词：wave, flourish, wield, flaunt | 反义词：conceal, hide | verb
