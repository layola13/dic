kublai:/ˈkuːblaɪ/| n.|忽必烈，元世祖|Kublai Khan, grandson of Genghis Khan, was the fifth Khagan of the Mongol Empire and the founder of the Yuan dynasty in China. 忽必烈，成吉思汗的孙子，是蒙古帝国的第五位大汗，也是中国元朝的建立者。|例句：Kublai Khan established his capital in Dadu, modern-day Beijing. 忽必烈在大都（今北京）建立了他的首都。

|近义词：Emperor, ruler, monarch|反义词: subject, commoner|词性:noun
