escharine:/ˈɛskərɪn/| adj.|痂的；焦痂的；结痂的|Relating to or characterized by an eschar; produced by a burn or corrosive application.|The escharine tissue was removed surgically.  焦痂组织通过手术切除了。|Synonyms:  scabbed, crusted, charred|Antonyms:  healthy, uninjured|adjective

eschar:/ˈɛskɑːr/| n.|焦痂；痂|A dry, dark scab or falling away of dead skin, typically caused by a burn, or by the bite of a mite or spider, or by anthrax infection.|The burn left a thick eschar. 烧伤留下了一层厚厚的焦痂。|Synonyms: scab, crust|Antonyms: healthy skin|noun
