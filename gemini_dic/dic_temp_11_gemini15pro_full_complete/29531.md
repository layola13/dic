catboat: /ˈkætˌboʊt/ | n. | 帆船，单桅帆船 | A sailboat with a single mast set well forward, carrying a mainsail only. |  He sailed his catboat across the bay. 他驾驶他的单桅帆船横渡海湾。|  The catboat was ideal for navigating the shallow waters. 这艘单桅帆船非常适合在浅水中航行。 | 近义词: sailboat, dinghy | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可考虑大型船只) yacht, ship | 词性: noun

词源:  From cat + boat.  "Cat" in this context likely refers to the boat's stable, cat-like motion on the water.
