meerkat:/ˈmɪərˌkæt/ | n. |狐獴，沼狸|A small, slender mongoose-like mammal native to southern Africa that lives in colonies and often stands upright on its hind legs.|例句：A group of meerkats stood on their hind legs, scanning the horizon. 一群狐獴用后腿站立，扫视着地平线。

例句：The meerkat is known for its distinctive upright posture. 狐獴以其独特的直立姿势而闻名。|近义词：mongoose|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
