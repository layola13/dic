nps:/ɛn piː ɛs/| abbreviation|净推荐值 (Net Promoter Score)|A management tool that can be used to gauge the loyalty of a firm's customer relationships.|例句：Our company's NPS has increased by 10 points this year. 今年我们公司的净推荐值提高了10分。|例句：The NPS helps us understand customer satisfaction. 净推荐值帮助我们了解客户满意度。|近义词：customer satisfaction score, customer loyalty metric|反义词: (No direct antonym, but a low NPS suggests customer dissatisfaction)|n.
