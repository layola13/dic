iniquitous:/ɪˈnɪkwɪtəs/
adjective
邪恶的；不公正的
Characterized by gross injustice, wickedness, or immorality.
The iniquitous system of apartheid was finally dismantled.  这个邪恶的种族隔离制度最终被废除了。
The judge denounced the iniquitous crime. 法官谴责了这起罪恶的罪行。

近义词: wicked, evil, unjust, immoral, nefarious, sinful
反义词: righteous, just, moral, virtuous, ethical
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *iniquitas, from Latin iniquus (“unequal, unjust, unfair”), from in- (“not”) + aequus (“equal, level, fair”).
