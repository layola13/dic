drink-driving:/ˈdrɪŋk ˌdraɪvɪŋ/ | n. | 醉酒驾驶 | The act of driving a vehicle after consuming alcohol above the legal limit.  | He was arrested for drink-driving. 他因酒后驾车被捕。 |  Driving under the influence (DUI), drunk driving |  Sober driving | noun

Etymology:  Compound of "drink" (from Old English *drincan*) and "driving" (present participle of "drive," from Old Norse *drīfa*).
