bivouac: /ˈbɪvuæk/ (US), /ˈbɪvuæk/ (UK)| n. v.|露营；临时宿营|A temporary camp without tents or cover, used especially by soldiers or mountaineers.|例句：The soldiers made bivouac for the night. 士兵们露营过夜。

例句：They bivouacked in the open air. 他们在户外露营。|近义词：camp, encampment, shelter|反义词: house, hotel|词性:名词, 动词
