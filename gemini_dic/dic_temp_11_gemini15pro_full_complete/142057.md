organza:/ɔrˈɡænzə/ | n. |薄纱，柯根纱 |A thin, stiff, transparent fabric of silk or a synthetic fiber. |例句：She wore a beautiful organza dress to the wedding. 她穿着一件漂亮的柯根纱礼服参加婚礼。

例句：The organza curtains allowed the light to filter through softly. 薄纱窗帘让光线柔和地透进来。
