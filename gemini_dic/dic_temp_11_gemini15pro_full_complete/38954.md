confidingness:/kənˈfaɪdɪŋnəs/| n.|坦率，信任，信赖|The quality of being willing to confide; trustfulness.|She spoke with a confidingness that made me feel comfortable sharing my secrets. 她说话很坦率，让我觉得可以放心地分享我的秘密。|近义词：trust, confidence, openness, frankness|反义词：secrecy, reticence, distrust, suspicion|词性:名词

词源: From confiding + -ness.  Confiding itself comes from the verb "confide," which originates from the Latin word "confidere," meaning "to trust entirely."
