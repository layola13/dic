graveling:/ˈɡrævəlɪŋ/ | adj. | 铺满砾石的；使人烦恼的；令人尴尬的 | Covered with gravel; causing annoyance or embarrassment; unnerving. |  The road was long and graveling. 这条路又长又满是碎石。| 近义词: stony, rocky, embarrassing, awkward, disconcerting | 反义词: smooth, paved, comforting, reassuring | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From the verb "gravel" + "-ing" suffix. "Gravel" comes from Old French "gravele," diminutive of "grave," meaning "coarse sand, gravel."
