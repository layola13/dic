enfranchising:/ɪnˈfræntʃaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 给予…公民权；赋予…权力；解放 | Granting the rights of citizenship, especially the right to vote; empowering; liberating. | The Nineteenth Amendment enfranchising women was a landmark moment in American history.  美国宪法第十九修正案赋予妇女选举权是美国历史上的一个里程碑式的时刻。

The company's new policies are enfranchising employees by giving them more autonomy. 公司的新政策通过赋予员工更多的自主权来赋予他们权力。 | 近义词: empowering, liberating, authorizing | 反义词: disenfranchising, restricting, suppressing | 词性: 动词

词源: From Old French *enfraunchir, from en- + franc ("free").  来自古法语*enfraunchir，由en- + franc（“自由”）组成。
