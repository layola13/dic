enrolling:/ɪnˈroʊlɪŋ/
verb
注册；登记；招收
The act of registering or signing up for something, typically a course, program, or service.  The process of officially joining a school, organization, or group.
She is enrolling in a photography class. 她正在报名参加摄影课。
He is enrolling his son in a new school. 他正在为他儿子注册一所新学校。
synonyms: registering, signing up, joining, admitting
antonyms: withdrawing, unregistering, leaving, quitting
