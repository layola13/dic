misjudgment:/ˌmɪsˈdʒʌdʒmənt/
n.
误判，判断错误
An act of judging wrongly or inaccurately.
His misjudgment of the distance caused the accident. 他对距离的误判导致了事故。
近义词：miscalculation, error, mistake
反义词：accurate judgment, correct assessment
词性：名词
