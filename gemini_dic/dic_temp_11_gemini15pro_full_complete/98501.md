instantaneity:/ˌɪnstənteɪˈniːɪti/ | n. |瞬时性，即时性|The quality or state of being instantaneous; occurrence at a particular instant.  The photograph captured the instantaneity of the explosion.|例句：The instantaneity of the message delivery impressed him. 信息传递的即时性给他留下了深刻的印象。

例句：She was struck by the instantaneity of the event. 她被事件的瞬时性震惊了。|近义词：immediacy, instantaneousness, suddenness|反义词:gradualness, slowness|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *instantaneitas, from instantaneus (see instantaneous) + -ity.
