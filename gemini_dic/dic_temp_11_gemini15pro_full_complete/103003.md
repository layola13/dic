jeffersonite:/ˈdʒɛfərsənˌaɪt/
n.
硅灰石，锰辉石
A manganese-rich variety of pyroxene, a mineral typically found in metamorphic rocks.
Jeffersonite is a relatively rare mineral found in certain metamorphic deposits. 硅灰石是一种相对稀有的矿物，存在于某些变质矿床中。
The geologist identified the sample as jeffersonite due to its distinct chemical composition.  地质学家根据其独特的化学成分将样品鉴定为硅灰石。
Synonyms: pyroxene, clinopyroxene
Antonyms: None (as it's a specific mineral, it doesn't have a direct opposite)
