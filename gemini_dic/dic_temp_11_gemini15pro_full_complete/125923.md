mush's:/mʌʃɪz/
noun
糊状物,软块,浓粥(的)
belonging to or associated with mush; a possessive form of mush.  This refers to something that belongs to or originates from a mushy substance.
The mush's texture was unpleasant.  这糊状物的质地令人不快。
The dog lapped up the mush's remains from the bowl. 狗把碗里剩下的糊状物舔干净了。
近义词: pulp's, paste's
反义词: solid's, firm's
词性: Possessive noun


Etymology:
From "mush," likely originating from the Middle English "mos," meaning marsh or bog, referencing the soft, wet ground. The possessive form is created by adding 's.
