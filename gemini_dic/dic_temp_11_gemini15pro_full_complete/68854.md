fashionista:/fæʃəˈnistə/ | n. |时尚达人；穿着入时的人| A person who is highly interested in fashion; a stylish and trendsetting individual. | She's a real fashionista, always wearing the latest designs. 她是个真正的时尚达人，总是穿着最新的设计。|  He's such a fashionista; he always knows what's in style. 他真是个时尚达人，总是知道什么是流行的。| 近义词: trendsetter, style icon | 反义词: frump | 词性: noun
