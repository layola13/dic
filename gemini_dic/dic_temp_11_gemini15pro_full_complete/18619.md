benthamism:/ˈbɛnθəmɪzəm/
n.
功利主义（尤指边沁的功利主义）
The ethical theory of Jeremy Bentham, holding that actions are right in proportion as they tend to promote happiness, wrong as they tend to produce the reverse of happiness.  Happiness is identified with pleasure and the absence of pain, and the value of actions is assessed in terms of their consequences.
Benthamism is a philosophy that emphasizes maximizing happiness and well-being for the greatest number of people. 边沁主义是一种强调为最大多数人谋求最大幸福的哲学。
His political philosophy was rooted in Benthamism. 他的政治哲学根植于边沁主义。

近义词:utilitarianism, hedonism
反义词:deontology, asceticism
词性:noun
