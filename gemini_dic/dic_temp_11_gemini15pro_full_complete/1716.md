acoustics: /əˈkuːstɪks/ | n. | 声学；音响效果 | the properties or qualities of a room or building that determine how sound is transmitted in it | The acoustics in the concert hall are excellent. 音乐厅的音响效果非常好。 | synonyms: sound, audio | antonyms: none | noun

acoustics: /əˈkuːstɪks/ | adj. | 声学的 | relating to sound or the sense of hearing |  This is an acoustics problem. 这是一个声学问题。 | synonyms: sonic, auditory | antonyms: none | adjective
