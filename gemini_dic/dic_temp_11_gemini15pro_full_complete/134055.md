nonidyllic:/ˌnɒnɪˈdɪlɪk/ | adj. | 不田园诗般的，不理想化的 | Not idyllic; not peaceful, happy, or charming.  | The nonidyllic reality of farming involves long hours and hard physical labor.  不田园诗般的农业现实包括长时间的辛苦体力劳动。| The cramped, dirty apartment was a nonidyllic setting for a romantic getaway.  狭小肮脏的公寓不是浪漫之旅的理想场所。| 近义词:unpleasant, harsh, realistic, gritty | 反义词:idyllic, peaceful, charming, picturesque | 词性:形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  From Latin *non "not".
idyllic: From Late Latin idyllium, from Greek eidyllion "short poem, picture," diminutive of eidos "form, shape."  The sense evolution moved from "short poem describing rustic life" to "charmingly simple and carefree."
