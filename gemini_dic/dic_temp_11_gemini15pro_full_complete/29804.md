caudex:/ˈkɔːdɛks/| n. |茎基; 根茎；块茎|The thickened, persistent base of a herbaceous perennial's stem, which often serves as a storage organ.|The caudex of this plant is quite large, indicating its age.  这种植物的茎基相当大，表明它的年龄。|Synonyms: rootstock, rhizome|Antonyms: stem tip, apex|noun

caudex:/ˈkɔːdɛks/| n. |(尤指棕榈类植物的)树干|The trunk of a tree, especially a palm.|The caudex of the palm tree was scarred from old leaf bases. 这棵棕榈树的树干上布满了旧叶基的疤痕。|Synonyms: trunk, stem|Antonyms: branch, root|noun
