gassing-up:/ˈɡæsɪŋ ˌʌp/| n./phrasal verb |加油；吹嘘；兴奋；鼓动|The act of filling a vehicle's fuel tank, or figuratively, boasting or becoming excited about something.|例句：We stopped for gassing-up before starting our road trip. 我们在开始公路旅行前停下来加油。

|例句：He was gassing-up about his new car all evening. 他整晚都在吹嘘他的新车。|近义词：refueling, filling up, bragging, boasting, hyping up|反义词:deflating, downplaying|词性:noun/phrasal verb

gassing:/ˈɡæsɪŋ/| v. |用毒气攻击；加油；空谈；吹嘘|The act of attacking with gas, or filling a vehicle with gas, or talking idly or boastfully.|例句：The troops were gassed during the war. 军队在战争期间遭到毒气攻击。
