gumps:/ɡʌmps/ | n. | 精神不振；忧郁；闷闷不乐 | A state of low spirits; depression; sulks. |  He's been in the gumps ever since he lost his job.  他自从失业以来一直闷闷不乐。 | 近义词：blues, doldrums, dumps, gloom, melancholy | 反义词：cheerfulness, happiness, joy | 词性: 名词

gump:/ɡʌmp/ | n. | 傻瓜；笨蛋 | A foolish or stupid person. | Don't be such a gump!  别这么傻！| 近义词：fool, idiot, dolt, simpleton | 反义词：genius, intellectual | 词性: 名词
