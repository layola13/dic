intercapillary:/ˌɪntərˈkæpɪˌlɛri/
adjective
毛细血管间的
Located between capillaries.  Relating to the spaces between capillaries.
The intercapillary space is filled with interstitial fluid. 毛细血管间的空间充满了组织液。
Drugs can diffuse through the intercapillary spaces. 药物可以通过毛细血管间的空间扩散。
近义词:  intervascular
反义词:  N/A
词性: adjective

词源:
From inter- + capillary.  "Inter-" is a Latin prefix meaning "between," and "capillary" refers to the tiny blood vessels.
