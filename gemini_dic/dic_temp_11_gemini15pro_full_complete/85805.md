hazardous:/ˈhæzərdəs/ (US) /ˈhæzədəs/ (UK)| adj.|危险的；有害的|Involving risk or danger, especially to somebody's health or safety.|例句：The chemicals are classified as hazardous waste. 这些化学品被归类为危险废物。|例句：Working in a coal mine is a hazardous occupation. 在煤矿工作是一项危险的职业。|近义词：dangerous, risky, perilous, precarious|反义词：safe, secure|词性:形容词
