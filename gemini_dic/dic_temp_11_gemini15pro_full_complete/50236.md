dependencies /dɪˈpɛndənsiz/ | n. | 依赖性，依赖关系 | The project has several external dependencies.  这个项目有几个外部依赖项。 | Example: The success of the project has dependencies on several key factors.  这个项目的成功依赖于几个关键因素。| Synonyms: reliance, requirement, prerequisite | Antonyms: independence, autonomy | noun

dependency /dɪˈpɛndənsi/ | n. | 依赖，依赖关系| The child's dependency on his parents is natural. 孩子对父母的依赖是自然的。| Example:  Her dependency on painkillers became a serious problem. 她对止痛药的依赖成了一个严重问题。 | Synonyms: reliance, addiction, subjection | Antonyms: independence, self-reliance, freedom | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dependentia, from Latin dependēns (present participle of dependēre, "to hang down from, hang upon, depend upon"), from de- ("down from, away from") + pendēre ("to hang, be suspended").
