antimetathesis:/ˌæntaɪməˈtæθɪsɪs/ | n. |逆转修辞，交错配列法 |  A rhetorical device in which words are repeated in transposed order; an inverted repetition of words or phrases (AB-BA). |  "Ask not what your country can do for you; ask what you can do for your country." is a famous example of antimetathesis.  | 近义词: chiasmus, inversion, an inversion  | 反义词: N/A | noun
