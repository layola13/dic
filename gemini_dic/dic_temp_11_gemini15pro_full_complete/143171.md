outclassing: /ˌaʊtˈklɑːsɪŋ/
verb
超越；远胜于
Surpassing or being clearly superior to in quality, skill, or performance.
The new software is outclassing all the competitors.  这个新软件远胜于所有竞争对手。
Our team was completely outclassed by the opposition.  我们的团队完全被对手超越了。
Synonyms: surpassing, excelling, outshining, outperforming, outstripping, trouncing
Antonyms: underperforming, being inferior to, lagging behind
