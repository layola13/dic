badder:/ˈbædər/ | adj. | 更坏的，更差的 | Comparative form of "bad," used informally.  Means worse or more unpleasant than something else.  Considered non-standard in formal writing.  | He's a badder dude than I thought. 他比我想象的更坏。|  worse, meaner, tougher | better, nicer, kinder | adjective
