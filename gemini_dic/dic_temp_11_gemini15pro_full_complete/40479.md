contributively:/kənˈtrɪbjʊtɪvli/
adverb
贡献地；促成地
in a way that contributes to something; by contributing
例句：She contributed contributively to the project's success. 她对项目的成功做出了贡献。

例句：He worked contributively with his colleagues to achieve the common goal.  他与同事们共同努力，实现了共同的目标。

近义词：jointly, collectively, cooperatively
反义词：individually, separately, independently
词性：adverb

词源：From "contributive" + "-ly". "Contributive" comes from "contribute" (from Latin "contribuere," meaning "to grant, bestow, contribute"), formed from "con-" (with, together) and "tribuere" (to assign, bestow).
