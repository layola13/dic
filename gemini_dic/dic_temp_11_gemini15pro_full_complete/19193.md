bewitchingly:/bɪˈwɪtʃɪŋli/
adverb
迷人地；令人着迷地
in a way that is very attractive and difficult to resist
Her bewitchingly beautiful smile captivated everyone in the room. 她那迷人美丽的笑容使房间里的每个人都着迷。
近义词:charmingly, alluringly, enchantingly, captivatingly
反义词:repulsively, unattractively, displeasingly
词性:adverb

bewitch:/bɪˈwɪtʃ/
verb
使着迷；施魔法于
to cast a spell on; to enchant and fascinate
The magician bewitched the audience with his incredible illusions. 魔术师以其令人难以置信的幻觉使观众着迷。
近义词:enchant, fascinate, captivate, charm
反义词:repel, disgust, disenchant
词性:verb

witch:/wɪtʃ/
noun
女巫；巫婆
a woman thought to have magic powers, especially evil ones
The old witch lived in a creepy cottage in the woods. 老巫婆住在森林里一个令人毛骨悚然的茅屋里。
近义词:sorceress, enchantress
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "ordinary woman")
词性:noun

词源: From Old English *wicce (feminine); *wicca (masculine), of uncertain origin.  Related to German "Wicht" (creature). The "-ingly" suffix transforms the adjective "bewitching" into an adverb.
