counter-passion:/ˈkaʊntərˌpæʃən/| n. |对抗的激情，反作用的热情，抑制的激情|A feeling or emotion that opposes another, often used to control or suppress the original passion.  It's a passion employed to counteract another passion. | He developed a counter-passion for exercise to combat his counter-passion for sweets. 他培养了对运动的热情来对抗他对甜食的热情。

She used her counter-passion for work as a way to suppress her grief. 她用对工作的热情来抑制自己的悲伤。 |近义词：counter-impulse, opposing desire, antidote |反义词: passion, desire, urge |词性:noun
