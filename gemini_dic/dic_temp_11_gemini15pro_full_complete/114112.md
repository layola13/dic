Also, a large-caliber bullet or a large-sized version of something. |He ordered a magnum of champagne to celebrate. 他点了一大瓶香槟来庆祝。|Synonyms: large bottle, double bottle (for wine), large-caliber (for bullets)|Antonyms: small bottle, standard bottle, small-caliber (for bullets)|noun
