oligarchy:/ˈɒlɪɡɑːki/ (US) /ˈɒlɪɡɑːʃi/ (UK)| n. |寡头政治；寡头统治集团|A small group of people having control of a country, organization, or institution.|The country was ruled by an oligarchy of wealthy families. 这个国家由富裕家族组成的寡头统治。

oligarchy:| n. |寡头统治集团；寡头政治|A government or control system where power is held by a small group of people.|The oligarchy controlled all aspects of the citizens' lives.  这个寡头统治集团控制着公民生活的方方面面。

oligarchy:| n. |寡头统治的国家；由少数人统治的社会|A country or society governed by an oligarchy.|The small island nation was essentially an oligarchy, ruled by a council of elders.  这个小岛国本质上是一个寡头政治国家，由长老委员会统治。

oligarchy:| n. |少数统治集团|A small, elite, and powerful group that controls something. |The industry is dominated by an oligarchy of powerful corporations.  这个行业由一个强大的企业寡头集团所控制。


近义词: autocracy, dictatorship, tyranny|
反义词: democracy, republic|
词性: noun|

词源:
From Ancient Greek ὀλιγαρχία (oligarkhía), from ὀλίγος (olígos, “few”) + ἄρχω (árkhō, “to rule, command”).
