congratulators:/kənˈɡrætʃʊleɪtərz/
n.
祝贺者，恭贺者
People who offer congratulations.  Those expressing praise or approval for an achievement.
例句：The congratulators gathered around the winning team. 祝贺者聚集在获胜队伍周围。

例句：The congratulators showered her with praise. 祝贺者对她赞不绝口。

近义词：well-wishers, admirers, celebrants
反义词：critics, detractors
词性：noun (plural)


congratulate:/kənˈɡrætʃʊleɪt/
v.
祝贺，恭贺
To express pleasure to someone at their success or good fortune.
例句：I congratulated him on his promotion. 我祝贺他升职了。

例句：She congratulated her friend on getting engaged. 她祝贺她的朋友订婚了。

近义词：commend, praise, applaud
反义词：criticize, condemn
词性：verb


congratulatory:/kənˈɡrætʃʊlətɔːri/
adj.
祝贺的，恭贺的
Expressing congratulations.
例句：He sent a congratulatory message to the new parents. 他给新父母发了一条祝贺短信。

例句：She gave a congratulatory speech at the graduation ceremony. 她在毕业典礼上致了祝贺词。

近义词：complimentary, laudatory, praising
反义词：critical, disapproving
词性：adjective
