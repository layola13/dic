cricketlike:/ˈkrɪkɪtˌlaɪk/
adjective
像蟋蟀的;蟋蟀似的
Resembling or suggestive of a cricket, especially in sound or appearance.
The insect produced a cricketlike chirping sound.  这种昆虫发出像蟋蟀一样的鸣叫声。
The dry, rattling sound was almost cricketlike.  这种干燥的、嘎嘎作响的声音几乎像蟋蟀的声音。
近义词:chirping, grasshopper-like
反义词: (No clear antonym)
词性:adjective

词源: cricket + like.  "Cricket" comes from Old North French "criquet," imitative of the insect's sound.  "Like" comes from Old English "lic" meaning "having the same form or appearance as."
