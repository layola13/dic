flyby:/ˈflaɪbaɪ/| n.|飞越，掠过，近距离飞越|A flyby is a flight past a point, especially a close approach to a planet or moon by a spacecraft to gather scientific data.|The spacecraft made a flyby of Jupiter.  这艘宇宙飞船飞越了木星。|近义词：flight, pass|反义词: landing|noun
