anaphorical:/ˌænəˈfɒrɪkəl/ (US) /ˌænəˈfɔːrɪkl/ (UK)| adj.|回指的，前指的|Referring back to something that has already been mentioned.|Example:  "The dog chased its tail; *it* seemed endlessly amused."  The pronoun "it" is anaphorical, referring back to "the dog."|Example: In the sentence "John said he was tired", "he" is an anaphorical pronoun, referring back to "John".|近义词：referential, reflexive|反义词：cataphoric|词性:adjective
