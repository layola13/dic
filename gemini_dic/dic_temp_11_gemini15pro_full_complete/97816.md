inkfish:/ˈɪŋkfɪʃ/| n.|墨鱼，乌贼|Cephalopod mollusks known for their ability to squirt ink, including cuttlefish, squid, and octopuses.  They are marine animals characterized by their internal shell, called a cuttlebone (in cuttlefish), or a pen (in squid), or no shell at all (in most octopuses).|The inkfish darted away, leaving a cloud of ink behind. 墨鱼飞快地游走了，留下了一团墨汁。|Synonyms: cephalopod, cuttlefish, squid, octopus|Antonyms: None|Noun
