fusty:/ˈfʌsti/ | adj. | 霉臭的；守旧的；过时的 | Smelling stale, damp, or stuffy; old-fashioned and out of date. | The library was filled with fusty old books.  图书馆里满是霉味儿的旧书。|  The museum felt fusty and unwelcoming.  这家博物馆让人感觉死气沉沉，不友好。| 近义词: musty, stale, antiquated, old-fashioned | 反义词: fresh, modern, contemporary | 词性: adjective
Etymology: From Middle English *fouste, from Old French fust "tasting of the cask," from fuste "cask," from Late Latin fustis "wood." The sense development went from "tasting of the cask" to "stale" to "smelling stuffy" and finally to "old-fashioned."
