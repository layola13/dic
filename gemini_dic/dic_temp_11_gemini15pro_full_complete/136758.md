nonseparability:/ˌnɒnˌsɛpərəˈbɪləti/ |n.|不可分性，不可分割性|The quality or condition of being nonseparable; inability to be separated.  It refers to the interconnectedness of elements within a system, such that they cannot be considered in isolation.|The nonseparability of mind and body is a central tenet of some philosophies.|近义词：inseparability, indivisibility, unity, wholeness|反义词：separability, divisibility, disunity|noun
