overmalapert: /ˌoʊvərˈmæləpɜːrt/ | adj. | 过分鲁莽的，过分 impertinent 的 | Excessively bold or impudent; exceeding the bounds of propriety or good manners.  Showing disrespectful and cocky boldness. | He was punished for his overmalapert behavior towards the teacher. 他因对老师过分鲁莽的行为而受到惩罚。| The overmalapert child interrupted the adults constantly.  这个过分鲁莽的孩子不停地打断大人说话。 | 近义词: impudent, insolent, brazen, audacious, impertinent | 反义词: respectful, polite, courteous, deferential, humble | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From "over-" (excessively) + "malapert" (impudent). "Malapert" comes from Old French "mal apert" (ill-behaved), from "mal" (badly) + "apert" (open, evident).
