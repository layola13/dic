flocculus:/ˈflɒkjʊləs/ (US) /ˈflɑːkjələs/| n. |小片，絮片；小叶；绒毛|A small lobe, flake, or tuft, especially of a cloud or a precipitate.|The flocculus in the cerebellum plays a vital role in balance and eye movements. 小脑中的绒球小结在平衡和眼球运动中起着至关重要的作用。|Example: The chemist observed the flocculus forming in the solution. 化学家观察到溶液中形成了絮状物。|Synonyms: flake, tuft, clump, cluster|Antonyms: solution, dispersion|noun
