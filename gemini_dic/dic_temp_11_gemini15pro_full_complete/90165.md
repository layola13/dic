hotkey:/ˈhɑːtkiː/ (US), /ˈhɒtkiː/ (UK)| n.|快捷键；热键|A key or combination of keys that provides a shortcut to a particular function within a computer program.|Example: I use a hotkey to copy and paste text quickly. 我使用快捷键来快速复制和粘贴文本。|Example:  She programmed a hotkey to open her email program. 她设置了一个快捷键来打开她的电子邮件程序。|近义词：shortcut, keyboard shortcut|反义词: (No direct antonym) |noun
