dump-on:/dʌmp ɑːn/
|phrasal verb|
|严厉批评；斥责；对……发泄|
|To criticize someone harshly, often unfairly.|
|The boss dumped on me for the team's failure, even though it wasn't my fault.|老板把团队的失败归咎于我，即使那不是我的错。|

|My coworker always dumps on me when she's stressed.|我的同事压力大的时候总是对我发泄。|
|criticize, berate, lambaste, castigate|
|praise, commend, compliment|
|verb phrase|
