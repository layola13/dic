mamachi:/məˈmɑːtʃiː/| n.|  小镇; 街区;  (尤指) 下町 (日本城市中平民居住的传统街区)|A mamachi is a traditional working-class neighborhood in a Japanese city, often characterized by small shops, narrow streets, and a strong sense of community. |  The mamachi was bustling with activity as people prepared for the festival.  这个下町随着人们准备节日而熙熙攘攘。|Synonyms: district, neighborhood, quarter,  shitamachi (下町 - Japanese)|Antonyms:  uptown, city center|noun

mamachi:/məˈmɑːtʃiː/| adj.|  小镇的; 街区的; 下町的 |  Describing something related to or characteristic of a mamachi. | The mamachi atmosphere was warm and inviting. 下町的氛围温暖而诱人。|Synonyms:  local, communal, traditional,  shitamachi (下町 - Japanese)|Antonyms:  modern, cosmopolitan, upscale|adjective
