fogging:/ˈfɒɡɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈfɑːɡɪŋ/ (US)| n.|喷雾，雾化；(照片)模糊；(昆虫学)烟熏(法)|The result of spraying a fine mist of liquid.  Also, the blurred area of a photograph.|例句：The fogging on the lens made it difficult to see. 镜头上的雾气使人难以看清。

例句：The fogging in the photograph ruined the shot. 照片中的模糊破坏了这张照片。|近义词：mist, haze, blur|反义词: clarity, sharpness|词性: noun


词源: From the verb "to fog," meaning "to cover with fog," which comes from the Old English "fog" meaning "cloud, mist."
