miscreating:/ˌmɪskrɪˈeɪtɪŋ/
verb
错误地创造；歪曲；败坏
Creating something badly or in a distorted way; spoiling something.
He felt he was miscreating his characters, turning them into caricatures. 他觉得他错误地塑造了他的角色，把他们变成了漫画人物。
近义词: distorting, perverting, corrupting, deforming
反义词: creating, perfecting, improving
词性: Verb
