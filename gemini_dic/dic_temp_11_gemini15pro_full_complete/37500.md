commixion:/kəˈmɪksɪən/ | n. | 混合，结合，混杂 | A mixture or blending of different things. | The commixion of different cultures in the city created a vibrant atmosphere. 城市中不同文化的融合创造了充满活力的氛围。|  The commixion of herbs and spices resulted in a unique flavor.  草药和香料的混合产生了一种独特的风味。 | 近义词: mixture, blend, amalgamation, fusion | 反义词: separation, division | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Latin *commixtiōn-, from commixtiō,  from commixtus (past participle of commiscēre ‘to mix together’), from com- ‘together’ + miscēre ‘to mix’.
