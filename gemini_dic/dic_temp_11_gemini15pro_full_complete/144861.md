over-practiced:/ˌoʊvərˈpræktɪst/| adj.|过度练习的，练习过度的|Having practiced something excessively, to the point where performance or skill may be negatively affected.|Her piano piece sounded mechanical and over-practiced. 她的钢琴曲听起来机械且练习过度。|The athlete's over-practiced routine lacked spontaneity.  这位运动员过度练习的套路缺乏自发性。|近义词：over-rehearsed, over-trained, overworked|反义词:under-practiced, under-rehearsed|词性:形容词
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "practiced" (past participle of the verb "practice," from Middle English *practisen*, from Old French *practiser*, from Late Latin *practicare*).
