cow:/kaʊ/
noun|奶牛；母牛|A fully grown female animal of a domesticated breed of ox, kept to produce milk or beef.|The farmer milked the cow every morning. 农民每天早上给奶牛挤奶。|Synonyms: bovine, heifer (young female cow)|Antonyms: bull (male cow)|n.
