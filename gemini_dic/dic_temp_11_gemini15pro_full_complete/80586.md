goofy:/ˈɡuːfi/| adj.|愚蠢的，傻的，笨拙的|Silly or foolish, often in a playful or amusing way.  Originating from the name of Disney's cartoon character, Goofy, who embodies these traits.|He made a goofy face at the camera. 他对着镜头做了个鬼脸。|近义词：silly, foolish, clumsy, awkward, absurd|反义词：serious, sensible, intelligent, smart|词性:adjective

goof:/ɡuːf/| n.  vi. vt.|傻瓜，错误；犯错误，弄糟|A silly or foolish person; a blunder or mistake; to make a blunder, to mess something up.|He made a real goof by forgetting her birthday. 他把她的生日忘了，真是个大傻瓜。|近义词：blunder, mistake, error, slip-up; fool, idiot|反义词：success, accomplishment; genius|词性:noun, verb
