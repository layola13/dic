hyponasty:/ˌhaɪpəˈnæsti/ /ˌhɪpəˈnæsti/| n. |下偏生长，下弯生长|Upward bending of a plant part (such as a leaf) caused by greater growth on the lower side.|The hyponasty of the leaves was evident after the plant was watered.  浇水后，叶子的下偏生长很明显。|The hyponasty of the dandelion flower stalk allows the seed head to be pressed close to the ground. 蒲公英花茎的下弯生长使种子头能够紧贴地面。|近义词：epinasty (sometimes)|反义词：epinasty|词性:noun

Etymology:  From Greek *hupo-* (under) + *nastos* (pressed close).
