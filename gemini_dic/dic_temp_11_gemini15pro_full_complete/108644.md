leaf-through:/liːf θruː/
verb
快速翻阅，浏览
to turn the pages of a book, magazine, etc. quickly, looking at some of them but not reading everything
She leafed through the magazine while waiting for her appointment. 她一边等预约，一边翻阅杂志。
skim, browse, glance over, flip through
pore over, scrutinize, read carefully
