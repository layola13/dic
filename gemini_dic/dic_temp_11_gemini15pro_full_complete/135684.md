nonprescriptive:/ˌnɑːnprɪˈskrɪptɪv/ | adj. | 非规定性的，描述性的 | Not establishing or laying down rules or prescriptions; descriptive. | This dictionary takes a nonprescriptive approach to language, simply recording how words are used rather than dictating how they should be used. 这本词典采用非规定性的语言方法，只是记录单词的使用方式，而不是规定它们应该如何使用。

The teacher's nonprescriptive teaching style encouraged students to explore their own ideas. 这位老师非规定性的教学风格鼓励学生探索自己的想法。 | 近义词：descriptive, explanatory, observational | 反义词：prescriptive, normative | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + prescriptive (giving directions or rules).  "Prescriptive" comes from the Latin "praescribere" (to write beforehand, prescribe).
