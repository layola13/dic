hindutva:/ˈhɪndʊtvə/| n.|印度教特性；印度教至上主义|An ideology seeking to establish the hegemony of Hindus and Hindu culture in India.  It promotes Hindu cultural, national, and religious identity.|India's current political landscape is heavily influenced by Hindutva. 印度当前的政治格局深受印度教至上主义的影响。|
The rise of Hindutva has caused concern among religious minorities. 印度教至上主义的兴起引起了少数族裔的担忧。|近义词：Hindu nationalism, Hindu supremacy|反义词：secularism, pluralism|词性:noun
