glutinosities:/ˌɡluːtɪˈnɒsɪtiz/
n.
粘性；含谷蛋白
the quality of being glutinous; stickiness.  The bread dough had a high degree of glutinosity.
The glutinosities of the various starches differ considerably. 各种淀粉的粘性差异很大。
近义词: stickiness, viscosity, adhesiveness
反义词: dryness, brittleness
词性: noun (plural)

Etymology:  From glutinous + -ity.  Glutinous comes from Late Latin *glūtinōsus, from Latin glūten, glūtinis (“glue”).
