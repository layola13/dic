overcomable:/ˌoʊvərˈkʌməbl/
adjective
可克服的，可战胜的
able to be overcome.
例句：The challenges are overcomable with hard work and determination.  努力和决心可以克服这些挑战。

例句：With enough support, her shyness is overcomable. 有了足够的支持，她的害羞是可以克服的。

近义词：surmountable, conquerable, vincible, defeatable
反义词：insurmountable, unconquerable, invincible, unbeatable
词性：adjective
