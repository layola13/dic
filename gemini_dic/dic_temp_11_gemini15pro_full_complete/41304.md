corduroy:/ˈkɔːrdərɔɪ/ (US), /ˈkɔːdʒərɔɪ/ (UK)| n. adj.|灯芯绒；灯芯绒裤子|A thick cotton fabric with velvety ribs. Also used to describe garments made from this fabric. |例句：She wore a pair of brown corduroy trousers. 她穿了一条棕色灯芯绒裤子。

例句：The sofa was covered in a soft, green corduroy. 沙发上覆盖着柔软的绿色灯芯绒。

近义词：velveteen, moleskin|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a fabric type)|词性: noun, adjective
