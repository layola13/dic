interdenominationalism:/ˌɪntərˌdɪˌnɑːmɪˈneɪʃənəlɪzəm/ | n. |跨教派的主义;不分宗派的合作;各教派间的联合 |The principle or practice of cooperation between different Christian denominations. | Example: The interdenominationalism of the local charity brought together various churches for a common cause. 当地慈善机构的不分宗派合作将不同的教会聚集在一起，为了一个共同的事业。| Example:  Interdenominationalism can foster a sense of unity among diverse Christian communities.  不分宗派的合作可以在不同的基督教社区中培养团结感。 |Synonym: ecumenism, interfaith cooperation | Antonym: sectarianism, denominationalism | noun
