allotted:/əˈlɒtɪd/
Verb|分配的，指定的|Given as a share or portion. |例句：They were allotted a small plot of land. 他们被分配了一小块土地。|近义词：assigned, allocated, apportioned, granted|反义词：withheld, denied
词性:Verb

allot:/əˈlɒt/
Verb|分配，指定|Give or apportion (something) to someone as a share or task.|例句：The teacher allotted each student a different task. 老师给每个学生分配了不同的任务。|近义词：assign, allocate, apportion, distribute|反义词：withhold, deny
词性:Verb
