flagstones:/ˈflæɡˌstoʊnz/
n.
石板，铺路石
Flat stones, typically rectangular or square, used for paving.
The patio was paved with large flagstones.  露台是用大块石板铺成的。

近义词: paving stones, paving slabs, flag paving
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider materials like gravel, asphalt, concrete)
词性: noun


flagstone:/ˈflæɡˌstoʊn/
n.
石板，铺路石
A flat stone, typically rectangular or square, used for paving.
The path was made of flagstone.  这条小路是用石板铺成的。

近义词: paving stone, paving slab
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider materials like gravel, asphalt, concrete)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "flag" (a flat slab of stone) + "stone."  "Flag" in this sense is likely related to Old Norse "flaga" (slab of stone).
