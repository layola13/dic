loben:/ˈloʊbən/ (US), /ˈləʊbən/ (UK)| vt.| 赞美，赞扬，称赞|To praise someone or something highly.|例句：They lobened her for her courage. 他们称赞她的勇气。

例句：The critics lobened the play's innovative staging.  评论家们赞扬了该剧创新的舞台布置。

近义词：praise, commend, extol, laud|反义词：criticize, condemn, denounce|词性：动词

词源: From Middle High German *lōben, from Old High German lobōn, from Proto-Germanic *laubōną (“to praise, allow”), from Proto-Indo-European *lewbʰ- (“to care, desire, love”). Cognate with German loben, Dutch loven, Swedish lova.
