foreorder:/ˌfɔːrˈɔːrdər/ | n. | 预购，预订 | An order placed for goods or services that are not yet available. |  I placed a foreorder for the new video game months in advance. 我提前几个月预购了这款新电子游戏。

| Example: The limited edition watch was available for foreorder only. 这款限量版手表只接受预订。 | Synonym: pre-order, advance order | Antonym: backorder | Noun
