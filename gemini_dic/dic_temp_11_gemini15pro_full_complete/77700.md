geminid: /ˈdʒɛmɪnɪd/
n.
双子座流星雨
A meteor shower that occurs in December, appearing to radiate from the constellation Gemini.
The Geminids are considered to be one of the best and most reliable meteor showers of the year. 双子座流星雨被认为是一年中最好最可靠的流星雨之一。

The Geminid meteor shower peaked last night. 昨晚双子座流星雨达到高峰。

近义词: meteor shower 流星雨
