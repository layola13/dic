indocility:/ˌɪndɒˈsɪləti/ | n. |不服从，不顺从，难驯服 |Lack of docility; resistance to instruction or discipline. |The indocility of the wild horses made them difficult to train. 野马的难驯服性使它们难以训练。| 近义词: disobedience, unruliness, rebelliousness, intransigence, defiance | 反义词: docility, obedience, compliance, submissiveness | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *indocilitas*, from in- "not" + docilitas "docility," from docilis "teachable," from docēre "to teach."
