decarbonised:/diːˈkɑːrbənaɪzd/
adj.
脱碳的
Having had carbon removed, or relating to the process of removing carbon.
The government is committed to a decarbonised economy by 2050. 政府致力于到2050年实现经济脱碳。
The report outlines a pathway to a decarbonised power sector. 该报告概述了电力行业脱碳的途径。

近义词:carbon-neutral, low-carbon
反义词:carbon-intensive

词性:adjective

词源:de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reduce") + carbon + -ised (suffix forming adjectives)
