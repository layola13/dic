outscreamed:/ˌaʊtˈskriːmd/
verb
大声喊叫，盖过…的喊叫声
to shout louder than someone else
例句：She outscreamed the other fans at the concert. 她在音乐会上比其他歌迷的喊声都大。

例句：He outscreamed his opponent in the debate.  在辩论中，他的喊声盖过了对手。

近义词：outyelled, outshouted
反义词：whispered, murmured
词性：verb
