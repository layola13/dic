overjudiciously: /ˌoʊvərˌdʒuːˈdɪʃəsli/
| adv. | 过于审慎地，过于明智地 | Characterized by excessive or undue judiciousness; too careful or cautious in judgment. | He overjudiciously applied the rules, resulting in a rigid and inflexible system. 他过于审慎地应用规则，导致系统僵化死板。| The committee overjudiciously reviewed the proposal, delaying the project unnecessarily. 委员会过于审慎地审查了提案，不必要地拖延了项目。 | 近义词: excessively cautiously, too prudently, overcarefully | 反义词: rashly, carelessly, imprudently | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "judiciously" (from "judicious," from Latin "iudiciosus," meaning "prudent, sensible," from "iudicium," meaning "judgment").
