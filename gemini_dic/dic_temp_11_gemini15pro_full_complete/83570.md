haemapophysis:/ˌhiːməˈpɒfɪsɪs/
noun
血骨刺，脉弓
A ventral process of a caudal vertebra of a fish, to which the caudal artery and vein are attached.  It is an extension of a haemal arch.
The haemapophyses of the fish were clearly visible in the x-ray. 这条鱼的血骨刺在X光片中清晰可见。
Synonyms: haemal spine, haemal arch
Antonyms: neural spine, neural arch
