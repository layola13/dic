outlaboring:/ˌaʊtˈleɪbərɪŋ/
verb
胜过工作；比...更努力工作
working harder or longer than someone else.
The team outlabored their opponents and secured a victory.  这个团队比他们的对手更加努力，并获得了胜利。
He outlabored everyone on the project, putting in extra hours to ensure its success. 他在这个项目上比任何人都更努力，投入额外的时间来确保它的成功。
近义词: outworking, surpassing, excelling
反义词: underperforming, slacking
