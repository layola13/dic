burschenschaft:/ˈbʊərʃənʃaft/| n. |大学生联谊会，学生兄弟会|A student fraternity in German-speaking countries, traditionally characterized by dueling and nationalist sentiment.|例句：He joined a Burschenschaft while studying at university. 他在大学学习期间加入了一个大学生联谊会。

例句：The Burschenschaft held a traditional fencing match.  这个大学生联谊会举行了一场传统的击剑比赛。

近义词：fraternity, brotherhood, association|反义词: sorority |词性: noun
