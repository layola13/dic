melanagogue:/ˈmelənəɡɒɡ/ (UK) /ˈmelənəɡɑːɡ/ (US)| n. |促黑素生成剂；使人悲伤的事物|A melanagogue is a substance that promotes the formation of melanin in the skin.  It can also refer to something that induces sadness or melancholy.|The doctor prescribed a melanagogue to help darken the patient's vitiligo patches.|近义词:melanin stimulant, depressant (figurative)|反义词:melanin inhibitor (literal), mood elevator (figurative)|词性:noun
