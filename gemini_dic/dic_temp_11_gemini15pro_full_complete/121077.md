miscook:/ˌmɪsˈkʊk/ | v. | 煮得不好；烹调得不好 | To cook (food) improperly; to fail to cook something properly. |  I miscooked the steak and it was tough. 我把牛排煮老了，很硬。

The chicken was miscooked and still pink inside.  鸡肉没煮熟，里面还是粉红色的。| 近义词: undercook, burn, scorch | 反义词: cook perfectly, overcook | 词性: 动词
