blastodermic:/ˌblæstəˈdɜːrmɪk/
adjective
胚盘的
Relating to or derived from the blastoderm, the layer of cells that forms the embryo in early development.
The blastodermic vesicle is a crucial stage in embryonic development. 胚盘囊泡是胚胎发育的关键阶段。
The researchers studied the blastodermic stage of chicken embryos. 研究人员研究了鸡胚胎的胚盘期。
近义词: embryonic, germinal
反义词: None
