assumingness:/əˈsjuːmɪŋnɪs/
n.
傲慢；自以为是；自作主张
The quality or state of being assuming; arrogance; presumption.
His assumingness irritated everyone in the room. 他傲慢的态度激怒了房间里的每个人。
近义词：arrogance, presumption, audacity, boldness, conceitedness, haughtiness, pride, superciliousness
反义词：humility, modesty, diffidence
词性：名词
