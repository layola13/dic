joist:/dʒɔɪst/| n. |托梁，搁栅|A horizontal structural member that supports a floor or ceiling.|The joists in the old house were rotting. 老房子的托梁已经腐烂了。|Synonyms: beam, girder, support|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "foundation" in a broad sense)|noun

joists:/dʒɔɪsts/| n. |托梁，搁栅（复数）|Horizontal structural members that support a floor or ceiling.|The builder installed new steel joists. 建筑工人安装了新的钢托梁。|Synonyms: beams, girders, supports|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *giste "a lying place, bed," from Latin iacēre "to lie." The sense evolution seems to have been from "beam supporting a bed" to "beam supporting a floor."
