bruises:/ˈbruːzɪz/ (US) /ˈbruːzɪz/ (UK)| n.| 瘀伤，擦伤|An injury appearing as an area of discolored skin on the body, caused by a blow or impact rupturing underlying blood vessels.|She had a few bruises on her legs from falling. 她的腿上因摔倒而有一些瘀伤。|Synonyms: contusions, discolorations, marks, lesions|Antonyms: heals, cures|noun

bruise:/bruːz/ (US) /bruːz/ (UK)| v.|  使…出现瘀伤；擦伤；碰伤 (vt.)|To injure without breaking the skin; to cause a bruise to form.|He bruised his knee when he fell. 他摔倒时擦伤了膝盖。|Synonyms: contuse, injure, mark|Antonyms: heal, cure|verb
