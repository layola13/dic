no-balls:/ˈnəʊ bɔːlz/| n.| 无效投球（板球）；胆怯；废话 |An illegal delivery in cricket where the bowler's front foot lands beyond the popping crease.  Figuratively, it can also mean a lack of courage or nonsense.| The umpire called a no-ball. 裁判判罚无效投球。| 近义词：illegal delivery (cricket), cowardice, rubbish| 反义词: legal delivery (cricket), bravery, sense| 词性: 名词

no-balls:/ˈnəʊ bɔːlz/| n.| 废话；胡说；胡扯 |Informal term used to express disbelief or disagreement, similar to "nonsense" or "rubbish".| He's talking no-balls! 他在胡说八道！| 近义词：nonsense, rubbish, baloney| 反义词: truth, fact| 词性: 名词
