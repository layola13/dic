episternal:/ˌɛpɪˈstɜːrnl/ | adj. | 胸骨上的，位于胸骨上的 | Relating to the episternum (also known as the manubrium or presternum), the upper part of the sternum (breastbone). | The episternal notch is a visible indentation at the top of the sternum. 胸骨上切迹是胸骨顶部可见的凹痕。|  The doctor palpated the patient's episternal region. 医生触诊了病人的胸骨上区域。 |  suprasternal, presternal |  xiphisternal (referring to the lower part of the sternum) | adjective
