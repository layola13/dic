orthoclase: /ˈɔːrθəˌkleɪs/ /ˈɔːrθoʊˌkleɪs/
noun
正长石
A common feldspar mineral, KAlSi3O8, occurring in white, pink, or other light-colored, glassy crystals. It is used in the manufacture of porcelain and glass.
Orthoclase is a common constituent of granite. 正长石是花岗岩的常见成分。
Orthoclase can be found in various igneous rocks.  正长石可以在各种火成岩中找到。
synonym: feldspar, potash feldspar
antonym:  (No direct antonym for a specific mineral)
