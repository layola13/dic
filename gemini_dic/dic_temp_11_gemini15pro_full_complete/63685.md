epencephalic:/ˌepɛnˈsɛfəlɪk/ |adj.|后脑的，有关后脑的|Relating to the hindbrain, the posterior part of the brain that includes the cerebellum, pons, and medulla oblongata.|The epencephalic structures play a crucial role in coordinating movement and regulating vital functions.|近义词:hindbrain, metencephalic|反义词:forebrain, prosencephalic|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἐπί (epí, “upon, on”) + ἐν (en, “in”) + κεφαλή (kephalḗ, “head”).
