aery:/ˈɛəri/ (US), /ˈeəri/ (UK)| adj. n.|空中的，高耸的；鹰巢|Relating to the air or the sky; high in the air; the nest of a bird of prey, especially an eagle.|The eagle soared high above its aery on the cliff face.  这只鹰在悬崖峭壁上的巢穴上方翱翔。|The castle stood on an aery peak, overlooking the valley. 这座城堡矗立在一个高耸的山峰上，俯瞰着山谷。|近义词：aerial, lofty, high, elevated, nest, eyrie|反义词：grounded, low, terrestrial|词性: adjective, noun


Etymology:  From Middle English *eirye*, from Old French *aire*, from Latin *agrārius* (“pertaining to fields”), from *ager* (“field”). The sense shifted from "field" to "nest in an open field" to "nest of a bird of prey, especially an eagle" and then to the general sense of "high in the air".
