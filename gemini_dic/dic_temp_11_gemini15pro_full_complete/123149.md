monitress: /ˈmɒnɪtrɪs/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnɪtrɪs/ (US)| n. |女班长；女监督员|A female monitor, especially one responsible for supervising students or other individuals.|例句：The monitress kept a close eye on the children during the field trip. 女班长在实地考察期间密切关注着孩子们。

例句：The school employed a strict monitress to maintain order in the study hall. 学校聘请了一位严格的女监督员来维持自习室的秩序。

近义词：supervisor, overseer, chaperone, guardian|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps student or subordinate)|词性:noun
