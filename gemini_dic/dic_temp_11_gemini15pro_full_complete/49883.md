demulsion:/diːˈmʌlʃən/ | n. |破乳，解乳|The process of separating an emulsion into its component liquids.|The demulsion of the oil-water mixture was achieved by adding a chemical demulsifier.| 近义词：separation, breaking|反义词：emulsification|noun


demulsify:/diːˈmʌlsɪfaɪ/ | v. |破乳，使解乳|To separate an emulsion into its component liquids.|The chemical demulsified the oil-water mixture effectively.|近义词：separate, break|反义词：emulsify|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- + emulsion. "Emulsion" comes from the Latin word "emulgere," meaning "to milk out." The prefix "de-" signifies removal or separation.
