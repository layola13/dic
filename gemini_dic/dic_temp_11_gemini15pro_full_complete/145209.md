overbark:/ˌoʊvərˈbɑːrk/ | v. | 过度吠叫；吠叫盖过 | To bark excessively or to bark so loudly as to drown out other sounds. | The neighbor's dog overbarked, making it difficult to hear our conversation.  邻居家的狗叫个不停，让我们很难听到彼此的谈话。 | The small dog overbarked the larger one, surprising everyone. 小狗的叫声盖过了大狗，让每个人都感到惊讶。 | 近义词: outbark | 反义词: whimper | 词性: 动词
