monopsychism:/ˈmɒnəʊˌsaɪkɪzəm/ /ˈmɑːnoʊˌsaɪkɪzəm/| n. | 一元论，单一心灵论 | The doctrine that all minds are ultimately a single mind.  The theory that there is only one mind, or that all individual minds are manifestations of a single universal mind. | Some philosophers believe in monopsychism, arguing that individual consciousness is an illusion. 一些哲学家相信一元论，认为个体意识是一种幻觉。| Monopsychism suggests that we are all connected on a fundamental level of consciousness. 一元论表明我们在意识的基本层面上都是相互联系的。 |  idealism, nondualism | dualism, pluralism | noun
