overbalancing:/ˌoʊvərˈbælənsɪŋ/ | v.  n. | 失去平衡；过分补偿；过重 | Losing or causing something to lose balance due to excess weight or force on one side; compensating excessively. |

例句：
His sudden movement caused him to overbalance and fall.
他突然的动作导致他失去平衡摔倒了。

The weight of the packages overbalanced the bike, sending it crashing to the ground.
包裹的重量使自行车失去平衡，摔倒在地上。

Her overbalancing generosity made people suspicious of her motives.
她过分的慷慨让人怀疑她的动机。

The government's overbalancing focus on economic growth neglected social issues.
政府过分关注经济增长而忽视了社会问题。


近义词：
(v.) topple, upset, capsize, destabilize, outweigh
(n.) instability, top-heaviness

反义词：
(v.) stabilize, balance, steady
(n.) stability, equilibrium

词性: verb, noun
