bagginesses:/ˈbæɡɪnəsəz/
noun
松弛，袋状
the state of being baggy or having multiple bags.
Her jeans had noticeable bagginesses at the knees. 她的牛仔裤膝盖处明显松弛。
bagginess, looseness, slackness
tightness, firmness
n.
