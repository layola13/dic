fileable:/ˈfaɪləbəl/| adj.|可归档的，可提交的|Suitable for being filed or submitted.|Example: The document is fileable under "Contracts."  这份文件可以归档在“合同”之下。|Example:  All expense reports are fileable electronically. 所有费用报告都可以电子提交。|近义词：recordable, submittable|反义词: unrecordable, nonsubmittable|词性:形容词
file:/faɪl/| n. v.|文件；归档；提交|A collection of information stored electronically or physically; to store or submit (a document) in an organized manner. |词源：From Old French *filer "to string things on a thread".|词性:名词，动词
