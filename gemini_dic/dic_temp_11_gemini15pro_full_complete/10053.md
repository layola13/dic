apneumatic:/ˌeɪpnuːˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 无气的，非充气的 |  Not containing or operated by air or gas.  Derived from Greek *a-* "not" + *pneuma* "breath, air."  Refers to systems or devices that function without compressed air. |  The apneumatic tires on the lunar rover allowed it to navigate the rocky terrain. 月球车上的无气轮胎使其能够在岩石地形上航行。|  Solid, non-pneumatic | Pneumatic, air-filled | adjective
