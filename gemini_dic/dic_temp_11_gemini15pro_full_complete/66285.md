excommunicator:/ˌɛks kəˈmjuːnɪˌkeɪtər/
n.|革除教籍者，逐出教会者|A person who excommunicates someone from a church or religious community.|The Pope acted as excommunicator, banishing the king from the Catholic Church.|Synonyms:  anathemiser, anathematizer|Antonyms:  absolver, pardoner
词性: noun


excommunicate:/ˌɛks kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪt/
vt.|把…逐出教会，开除…的教籍|To officially exclude someone from participation in the sacraments and services of a Christian Church.|The church excommunicated him for heresy.|Synonyms:  anathematize, banish, cast out, oust|Antonyms:  absolve, pardon, reinstate
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *excommunicatus*, past participle of excommunicare "to exclude from communion," from ex- "out of" + communicare "to share, communicate," from communis "common."
