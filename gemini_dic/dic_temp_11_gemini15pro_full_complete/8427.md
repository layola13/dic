antiacademic:/ˌæntiˌækəˈdɛmɪk/
adjective
反对学术的；非学院派的；不重视学术的
Opposed to or indifferent to the principles or practices of academics; not scholarly.
His antiacademic attitude made him unpopular with the professors. 他不重视学术的态度让他不受教授们的欢迎。
近义词:unscholarly, nonacademic, intellectual
反义词:academic, scholarly, intellectual
