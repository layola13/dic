dedicates:/ˈdedɪkeɪts/
verb
奉献，献身；致力于；题献
devotes or sets apart for a special purpose.
He dedicates his life to helping the poor. 他一生致力于帮助穷人。
The author dedicated the book to his parents.  作者将这本书献给他的父母。
devotes, commits, assigns
neglects, abandons
词性: 动词
