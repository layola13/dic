emblematical: adj.
emblematical: 象征性的；标志性的
emblematical: Serving as a symbol of something; representative.
emblematical: The dove is emblematical of peace.  鸽子是和平的象征。

emblematical: The flag is emblematical of the nation. 这面旗帜是国家的象征。
emblematical: symbolic, representative, illustrative
emblematical: literal, actual
emblematical: adjective
