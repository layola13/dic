nonconnective:/ˌnɑːnkəˈnɛktɪv/
adjective
非连接的；不连接的
Not connecting or tending to connect.
The two issues are nonconnective and should be treated separately. 这两个问题互不关联，应该分开处理。
The nonconnective tissue did not hold the bones together properly. 非连接组织没有将骨骼连接好。
近义词: unconnected, separate, discrete, distinct
反义词: connected, related, linked, associated
词性: adjective

词源:
From non- + connective.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "connective" refers to something that connects.
