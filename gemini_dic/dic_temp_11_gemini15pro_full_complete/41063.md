coportion:/koʊˈpɔːrʃən/| n.|共同部分，共有部分，共同拥有|A coportion is a portion or share held or owned in common with another or others. It refers to joint ownership or possession.|The two companies have a coportion in the new venture, sharing both the profits and the risks.|近义词：joint ownership, shared possession, co-ownership, common ownership|反义词：sole ownership, individual ownership|词性:noun

Etymology:
From co- (prefix meaning "together" or "jointly") + portion (from Old French porcion and Latin portio, meaning "share" or "part").
