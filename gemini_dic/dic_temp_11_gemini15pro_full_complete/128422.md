newfie:/ˈnjuːfi/ | n. |纽芬兰犬; 纽芬兰人 |A large dog with a thick coat, originally bred in Newfoundland, Canada. Also (informal) a person from Newfoundland. |例句：My newfie loves swimming in the lake. 我的纽芬兰犬喜欢在湖里游泳。

例句：He's a newfie, born and bred. 他是土生土长的纽芬兰人。|近义词：(for dog) Newfoundland dog, (for person) Newfoundlander |反义词: None |词性:noun
