explicable:/ɪkˈsplɪkəbl/ /ɛkˈsplɪkəbl/
adjective
可解释的；可说明的
capable of being explained

近义词:understandable, comprehensible, explainable
反义词:inexplicable, incomprehensible, mysterious
词性:adjective
