deriving:/dɪˈraɪvɪŋ/ | v. |  获得，源于，衍生 | Obtaining something from a source or origin; tracing back to a root. |例句：He derives great pleasure from reading. 他从阅读中获得极大的乐趣。

例句：The word "derive" derives from Latin. “derive”这个词源于拉丁语。

近义词：obtaining, gaining, acquiring, stemming, originating | 反义词：losing, forfeiting, relinquishing | 词性：动词(Verb)
