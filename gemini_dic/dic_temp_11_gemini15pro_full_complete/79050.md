givebacks:/ˈɡɪvbæks/| n.|让步，妥协；（尤指工人在工资或福利方面的）返还，退让|Something given back, especially wages or benefits previously gained, as by labor in exchange for other concessions.|The union agreed to givebacks in order to save jobs. 工会同意让步以保住工作岗位。|Synonyms: concessions, compromises, sacrifices|Antonyms: gains, increases, advancements|noun

givebacks:/ˈɡɪvbæks/| n.|（尤指公司为感谢顾客支持而提供的）优惠，赠品；回馈|Something given back to customers, especially as a thank you for their support. |The company offered givebacks to its loyal customers. 公司向忠实客户提供回馈。|Synonyms: rewards, bonuses, perks|Antonyms: penalties, deductions|noun
