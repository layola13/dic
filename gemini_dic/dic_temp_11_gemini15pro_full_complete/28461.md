carburising:/ˈkɑːrbəˌraɪzɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːrbəˌraɪzɪŋ/ (US)| n.|渗碳|The process of increasing the carbon content of the surface of steel by heating it in a carbonaceous atmosphere.|The gears were carburised to improve their wear resistance. 齿轮经过渗碳处理以提高其耐磨性。|近义词：carbonizing, case hardening|反义词: decarburising|noun

carburize:/ˈkɑːrbəˌraɪz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːrbəˌraɪz/ (US)| v.|渗碳|To increase the carbon content of the surface of steel by heating it in a carbonaceous atmosphere.|The manufacturer carburizes the steel components for added durability. 制造商对钢制部件进行渗碳处理以增加耐用性。|近义词：carbonize, case harden|反义词: decarburize|verb
