dethroning:/diːˈθroʊnɪŋ/
verb
废黜；取代；
The act of removing a monarch from their throne, or replacing something in a position of importance or dominance.
The dethroning of the king led to widespread unrest. 国王的废黜导致了广泛的动乱。

The new technology is dethroning the old ways of doing things. 新技术正在取代旧的做事方法。

近义词: ousting, removing, deposing, supplanting, displacing
反义词: crowning, installing, enthroning
词性: Verb (present participle of dethrone)

dethrone:/diːˈθroʊn/
verb
废黜，罢免
To remove a king or queen from power; to remove someone or something from a position of authority or dominance.
The rebels dethroned the king and established a republic.  叛军废黜了国王，建立了共和国。

The champion was dethroned by the young challenger.  冠军被年轻的挑战者击败了。

近义词: oust, remove, depose, supplant, displace
反义词: crown, install, enthrone
词性: Verb
