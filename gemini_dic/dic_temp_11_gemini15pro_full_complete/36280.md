coheaded:/ˌkoʊˈhɛdɪd/
adj.
共同领导的，共同负责的
having two or more heads, or two or more people sharing leadership or responsibility
The coheaded department faced challenges in decision-making due to differing opinions.  共同领导的部门由于意见分歧，在决策方面面临挑战。
The research project was coheaded by two prominent scientists. 这个研究项目由两位杰出的科学家共同领导。
jointly led, jointly headed
solely led, independently led
