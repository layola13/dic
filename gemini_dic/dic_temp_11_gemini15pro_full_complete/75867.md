functionally:/ˈfʌŋkʃənəli/| adv.|功能上地，实际上地|In terms of its use or purpose; in a way that relates to how something works or operates.  It describes something in practical terms rather than its appearance or ideal form.|The house is small but functionally designed.  这房子虽小，但设计实用。|Synonyms: practically, effectively, operatively, in effect, in practice, essentially, virtually|Antonyms:  dysfunctionally, impractically, ineffectively|adverb

functionally:/ˈfʌŋkʃənəli/| adj.|功能性的|Designed to be practical and useful rather than attractive.|The furniture was functional but not very stylish.  这些家具很实用，但不太时尚。|Synonyms: practical, useful, utilitarian, pragmatic|Antonyms: decorative, ornamental, impractical, dysfunctional|adjective
