interruptedness:/ˌɪntəˈrʌptɪdnɪs/| n. |中断，打断，不连续性|The frequent interruptedness of the meeting made it difficult to reach a decision. 会议频繁的中断使得很难做出决定。|例句：The interruptedness of her sleep left her feeling groggy.  她睡眠被打断，让她感到昏昏沉沉。

The constant interruptedness of the internet connection made it impossible to stream the movie.  网络连接不断的断断续续使得无法在线观看电影。|近义词：disruption, discontinuity, fragmentation|反义词:continuity, flow, uninterruptedness|词性:名词


Etymology: From interrupt + -ed + -ness.  "Interrupt" comes from Latin "interrumpere" (to break between, interrupt), from "inter-" (between) + "rumpere" (to break).
