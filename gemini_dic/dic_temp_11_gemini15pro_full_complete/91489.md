hymenopteral:/ˌhaɪmɪˈnɒptərəl/ | adj. | 膜翅目的 | Relating to or characteristic of insects of the order Hymenoptera, which includes bees, wasps, ants, and sawflies. |  The hymenopteral venom caused a severe allergic reaction. 膜翅目昆虫的毒液引起了严重的过敏反应。| Synonyms:  apian (specifically relating to bees), vespine (specifically relating to wasps), formicine (specifically relating to ants) | Antonyms: N/A | adjective

hymenopteran:/ˌhaɪmɪˈnɒptərən/ | n. | 膜翅目昆虫 | An insect of the order Hymenoptera, such as a bee, wasp, ant, or sawfly. | The hymenopteran flew from flower to flower, collecting pollen.  这只膜翅目昆虫在花丛中飞舞，采集花粉。| Synonyms:  bee, wasp, ant, sawfly (more specific terms depending on the insect) | Antonyms: N/A | noun
