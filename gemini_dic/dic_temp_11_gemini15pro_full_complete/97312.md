informant:/ɪnˈfɔːrmənt/ (US) /ɪnˈfɔːmənt/ (UK)| n.|线人；告密者；提供信息者|A person who gives information to someone, especially to the police or a journalist.|The journalist refused to reveal the identity of his informant. 记者拒绝透露线人的身份。|synonyms: source, informer, tipster, whistleblower|antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider: suspect, accused, defendant)|noun

informant:/ɪnˈfɔːrmənt/ (US) /ɪnˈfɔːmənt/ (UK)| n.|被调查者；被访问者(尤指在语言学或人类学研究中)|A person who gives information to a researcher, especially in linguistics or anthropology.|The linguist interviewed several native speakers as informants for her study on dialect variation.  语言学家采访了几位母语人士作为她研究方言变异的被调查者。|synonyms: respondent, subject, participant|antonyms: (No direct antonym)|noun
