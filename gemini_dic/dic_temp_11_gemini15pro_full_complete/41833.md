corven:/ˈkɔːrvən/| adj.|乌鸦般的，黑色的，乌鸦的|Corven is an archaic poetic word relating to ravens or crows, often used to describe something black or raven-like.  It derives from the Old English "corfen" or "cræfen," related to "crawa" (crow).|Her corven hair framed her pale face. 她乌鸦般的黑发衬托着她苍白的脸。|近义词：raven, black, ebony, dark|反义词:light, white, bright|词性:形容词
