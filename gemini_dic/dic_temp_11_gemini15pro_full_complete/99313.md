intercrop: /ˈɪntərˌkrɒp/ (UK) /ˈɪntərˌkrɑːp/ (US)| n. v.|间作，套种|The practice of growing two or more crops in proximity, typically in the same field, at the same time.|Example:  The farmer decided to intercrop corn and beans to maximize land use.  这位农民决定玉米和大豆间作以最大限度地利用土地。

Example: They intercropped the cabbages with lettuces. 他们在卷心菜地里套种了莴苣。 |近义词：companion plant, mixed cropping|反义词: monoculture|词性: 名词, 动词
