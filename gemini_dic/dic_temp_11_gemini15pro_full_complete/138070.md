normalities:/nɔːrˈmælɪtiz/
n.|正常状态，常态|The return to normality after the pandemic was gradual.  疫情后的恢复正常是一个渐进的过程。|normality is the state of being normal. |例句：Life is slowly returning to normality after the lockdown.  封锁之后，生活正在慢慢恢复正常。

例句：The doctor said my blood test results were within the range of normality. 医生说我的血液检测结果在正常范围内。|近义词：standards, norms, regularities, routines, usualness|反义词：abnormalities, irregularities, deviations, exceptions, oddities|词性:名词

词源: From the Late Latin *normalitas*,  derived from Latin *normalis* "made according to a carpenter's square," hence "right," "regular."
