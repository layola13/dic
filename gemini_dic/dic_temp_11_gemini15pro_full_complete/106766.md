labadist:/ˈlæbədɪst/| n. | 拉巴第教徒 | A member of a strict Protestant sect founded in France in the 17th century by Jean de Labadie, characterized by ascetic communal living. |  The Labadists established a colony in Maryland in the late 17th century.  拉巴第教徒在17世纪晚期在马里兰州建立了一个殖民地。| Synonyms: pietist, ascetic | Antonyms: hedonist, libertine | noun

labadist:/ˈlæbədɪst/| adj. | 拉巴第教派的 | Relating to the Labadists or their beliefs. | Their Labadist community practiced communal ownership of property. 他们的拉巴第社区实行财产共有制。| Synonyms: pietistic, ascetic | Antonyms: hedonist, libertine | adjective
