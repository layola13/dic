certainest:/ˈsɜːrtnɪst/ | adj. | 最确定的，最可靠的 |  Superlative form of "certain," meaning most assured, most definite, or most reliable. |  例句：This is the certainest way to achieve your goals. 这是实现你目标最可靠的方法。| 近义词：surest, most definite, most reliable | 反义词：least certain, most doubtful, least reliable | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *certeinest, superlative of certain, from Old French certain, from Latin certus.
