artsiness's:/ˈɑːrtinəsnɪsɪz/
noun
做作;附庸风雅
the quality of being too deliberately artistic, especially in a way that seems artificial or affected.
The film's artsiness put me off. 这部电影的做作让我反感。
近义词:pretentiousness, affectation
反义词:genuineness, sincerity
词性:名词

Etymology:
artsiness (n.) 1925, from artsy + -ness.  Artsy (adj.) "pretentiously or affectedly artistic," 1926, from art + -sy (1).
