humanitarianisms:/hjuːˌmænɪˈtɛəriənɪzəmz/
n.
人道主义（复数）
Doctrines or principles of humanitarianism; systems of humanitarian belief.  The various ways humanitarian principles are understood and practiced.
The conference explored different humanitarianisms and their impact on aid delivery.  这次会议探讨了不同的人道主义及其对援助提供的影响。
Humanitarian efforts often reflect competing humanitarianisms, each with its own priorities. 人道主义的努力通常反映出相互竞争的人道主义，每一种都有其自身的优先事项。

近义词: humanitarian principles, humanitarian doctrines, humanitarian philosophies
反义词: inhumanity, cruelty, barbarity
词性: noun (plural)


词源: From humanitarian + -ism (suffix forming nouns of action, practice, or system).  Ultimately from Latin *humanitas, from humanus "human."
