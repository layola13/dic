numbness:/ˈnʌmnəs/| n.|麻木，麻痹|A loss of sensation or feeling in a part of your body.|My foot went numb after sitting cross-legged for too long. 我的脚盘腿坐得太久麻了。|Synonyms: insensitivity, deadness, paralysis|Antonyms: sensitivity, feeling, responsiveness|noun
