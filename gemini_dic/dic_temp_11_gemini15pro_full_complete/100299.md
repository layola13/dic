intershaded:/ˌɪntərˈʃeɪdɪd/ | adj. |相互遮蔽的，互相遮阴的 |Marked by shadows cast by overlapping or intervening objects; shaded mutually. |The trees were so dense that their branches intershaded, creating a cool, dim forest floor. 树木茂密，枝叶互相遮蔽，形成了凉爽阴暗的森林地面。| Near synonyms: overshadowed, shaded, canopy-covered | Antonyms: sunlit, exposed, bright | Word Origin: From inter- + shaded, past participle of shade.
