inexcusable:/ˌɪnɪkˈskjuːzəbl/
adjective
不可原谅的；无法辩解的
not justifiable; unforgivable
His behavior was completely inexcusable. 他的行为完全不可原谅。

It is inexcusable to be late for a meeting without notifying anyone.  开会不通知任何人就迟到是不可原谅的。

近义词:unforgivable, unjustifiable, indefensible
反义词:excusable, justifiable, understandable
词性:adjective
