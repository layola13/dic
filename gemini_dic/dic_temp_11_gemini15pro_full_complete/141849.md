orchestian: /ɔːrˈkɛstiən/ | n. | 管弦乐演奏者，舞蹈演员 | An orchestral musician or dancer. | The orchestians took their bows after a rousing performance.  管弦乐演奏者们在激动人心的演出后鞠躬致意。 | synonyms: musician, instrumentalist, dancer, performer | antonyms: audience, spectator | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *orchestien*, from Latin *orchestra* (see orchestra). Ultimately from Greek *orkhēstra*, the space in front of the stage in ancient Greek theatres where the chorus danced.
