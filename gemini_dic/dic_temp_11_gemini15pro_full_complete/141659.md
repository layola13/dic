optimum:/ˈɒptɪməm/ (British) /ˈɑːptɪməm/ (American)| adj. n.|最佳的，最优的；最佳条件，最适宜条件|Most favorable or desirable; best.|例句：The optimum temperature for growing tomatoes is about 75 degrees Fahrenheit.  种植西红柿的最佳温度约为 75 华氏度。|近义词：best, ideal, perfect, optimal|反义词：worst, poorest, least favorable|词性:形容词

optimum:/ˈɒptɪməm/ (British) /ˈɑːptɪməm/ (American)| n.|最佳条件，最适宜条件|The most favorable condition or level for growth, reproduction, or success.|例句：We need to find the optimum balance between cost and quality. 我们需要找到成本和质量之间的最佳平衡点。|近义词：ideal, peak, apex|反义词：nadir, worst|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *optimus* ("best"), superlative of *bonus* ("good").
