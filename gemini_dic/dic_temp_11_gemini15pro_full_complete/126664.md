nakuru:/nɑˈkʊruː/
n.
纳库鲁（肯尼亚城市）
A city in Kenya, located northwest of Nairobi. It is the capital of Nakuru County and the fourth largest urban center in the country.
Nakuru is famous for its stunning Lake Nakuru National Park. 纳库鲁因其令人惊叹的纳库鲁湖国家公园而闻名。
Lake Nakuru is located near Nakuru town. 纳库鲁湖位于纳库鲁镇附近。
近义词:city, town
反义词:(无)
词性:名词
