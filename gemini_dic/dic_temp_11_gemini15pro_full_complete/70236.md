fico:/ˈfiːkoʊ/ (US) /ˈfaɪkəʊ/ (UK)| n. | FICO分数; 信用评分 | FICO score, a credit score created by the Fair Isaac Corporation. It is used by lenders to assess credit risk. | Example:  My FICO score dropped after I missed a credit card payment. 我的FICO分数在我错过信用卡还款后下降了。

Example: A good FICO score is essential for getting a mortgage.  良好的FICO分数对于获得抵押贷款至关重要。

|Synonym: credit score, credit rating|Antonym: N/A|Noun|
