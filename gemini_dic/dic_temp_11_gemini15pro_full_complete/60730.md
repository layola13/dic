ETYMOLOGY->
From electro- + cardio- + -gram.  Electro- comes from Greek ēlektron, meaning "amber" (referring to static electricity). Cardio- comes from Greek kardia, meaning "heart." -gram comes from Greek gramma, meaning "something written or drawn."
