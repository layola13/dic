buds:/bʌdz/
n.|芽, 花蕾, 蓓蕾; 夥伴, 哥们儿|A bud is a small swelling on a plant stem from which a shoot, leaf, or flower develops.  It can also refer to a close friend.|The tree is covered in buds, promising a beautiful spring. 这棵树上都是花蕾，预示着美丽的春天。|  They've been buds since high school.  他们从高中起就是好哥们儿了。 |synonyms: sprout, shoot, blossom, mate, pal|antonyms: flower, bloom|noun

buds:/bʌdz/
v. (budded, budding)|发芽, 萌芽; 开始发展|To bud means to produce or develop buds.  It can also mean to begin to develop or show promise.|The roses are budding early this year. 今年玫瑰发芽得早。| She's a budding artist with a lot of potential. 她是一位很有潜力的新兴艺术家。|synonyms: sprout, germinate, develop|antonyms: wither, die|verb
