diamantiferous:/ˌdaɪəˈmænˈtɪfərəs/
adjective
含钻石的；产钻石的
Containing or yielding diamonds.
The diamantiferous soil of the region made it a prime location for mining.  该地区的含钻石土壤使其成为主要的矿区。
The miners were excited to discover a diamantiferous kimberlite pipe.  矿工们兴奋地发现了一个含钻石的金伯利岩管。
diamond-bearing, diamond-rich
barren, unproductive
