cave:/keɪv/| n. |洞穴，窑洞|A large underground chamber, typically of natural origin, often found in hillsides or cliffs.|The explorers discovered a hidden cave filled with ancient paintings. 探险家们发现了一个隐藏的洞穴，里面充满了古老的壁画。|近义词：cavern, grotto, den|反义词:peak, summit|名词
cave:/keɪv/| vi. |塌陷，屈服|To collapse or give way.|The roof of the mine caved in, trapping several miners. 矿井的顶部塌陷，困住了几名矿工。|近义词：collapse, crumble, yield|反义词:resist, withstand|动词
