brogued: /broʊɡd/ | adj. | 带有布洛克鞋风格的；雕花的 | Having decorative perforations and serrations along the seams, characteristic of brogues (a type of shoe). | Her brogued boots added a touch of country chic to her outfit. 她那双布洛克靴为她的服装增添了一丝乡村时尚感。| The craftsman meticulously carved the brogued pattern onto the leather. 工匠一丝不苟地在皮革上雕刻布洛克图案。| 近义词: perforated, serrated, wingtip (for shoes) | 反义词: plain, smooth | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From the noun "brogue," originally referring to a rough or heavy shoe, especially one of rawhide worn by the native Irish and Scottish.  The term later came to describe the distinctive punched and serrated detailing on these shoes, and eventually, any shoe with similar decoration.
