garrisoning:/ˈɡærɪsənɪŋ/
verb
驻守，驻扎
The act of providing soldiers to defend a place.
The army is garrisoning the town.  军队正在驻守这个城镇。
garrison, manning, defending, fortifying
abandoning, withdrawing

词性：v.

英文解析:Present participle of *garrison*.  The action of stationing troops in a fort or fortified place.

例句：
They were garrisoning the fort against the enemy. 他们正在要塞驻守，抵御敌人。

The troops are garrisoning the border. 部队正在驻扎边境。



近义词：
stationing, deploying, posting, quartering

反义词：
evacuating, withdrawing
