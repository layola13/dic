outhiring:/ˈaʊtˌhaɪərɪŋ/
n.
外包; 外部聘用
The practice of contracting with a third-party provider to perform services or tasks that were previously handled internally by a company.
Many companies are now considering outhiring their IT services to save costs. 许多公司现在正在考虑将他们的IT服务外包以节省成本。
outsourcing, subcontracting
insourcing, in-house
noun
