incipient:/ɪnˈsɪpiənt/| adj.|初期的，刚开始的|Beginning to exist or appear; in an initial stage.|例句：The project is still in its incipient stages. 这个项目还处于初期阶段。

例句：We noticed the incipient signs of a recovery in the economy. 我们注意到经济复苏的初期迹象。|近义词：beginning, nascent, developing, embryonic, initial|反义词：advanced, developed, mature, established, late|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *incipientem (nominative incipiens), present participle of incipere "to begin," from in- "in, on" + capere "to take, seize."
