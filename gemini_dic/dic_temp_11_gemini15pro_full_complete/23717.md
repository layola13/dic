brassies:/ˈbræsiz/| n.|球道木杆; 黄铜制品(通常复数); |A type of golf club, typically numbered 2, 3, or 4, designed for long shots from the fairway.  Also, items made of brass, often used in the plural. |He used his 2-brassie to reach the green in two. 他用2号球道木杆两杆就上了果岭。

The antique store had a collection of beautiful brasses. 这家古董店收藏了一系列漂亮的黄铜制品。|近义词:woods (高尔夫球杆), brass items (黄铜制品)|反义词:irons (铁杆, 高尔夫)|名词
