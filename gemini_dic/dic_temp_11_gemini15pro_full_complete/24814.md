brout:/braʊt/ | n. | 菜汤，肉汤 | A thin soup or broth. |  The brout was flavorful and warming. 这道菜汤味道鲜美，暖人心脾。| Example:  She made a hearty brout with vegetables and chicken. 她用蔬菜和鸡肉做了一道丰盛的肉汤。 | Synonyms: broth, soup, consommé | Antonyms: solid food, thick stew | noun

Etymology: From Old French *brot, from Late Latin *brodum, from Germanic *brod- "broth."  |
