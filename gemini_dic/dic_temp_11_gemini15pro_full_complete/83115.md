gustier:/ˈɡʌstɪər/ | adj. | 更阵风的，风力更大的 | Characterized by stronger or more frequent gusts of wind. | The weather became gustier as the storm approached.  暴风雨来临时，天气变得更加阵风。|  The sailboat struggled in the gustier conditions. 帆船在阵风加剧的情况下艰难航行。|  windier, blusterier | calmer, less windy | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From gust + -ier (comparative adjective suffix).  Ultimately from Old Norse *gustr.
