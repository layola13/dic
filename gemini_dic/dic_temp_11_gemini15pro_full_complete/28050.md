capelin:/ˈkeɪplɪn/| n. |毛鳞鱼|A small fish of the smelt family, found in large shoals in the North Atlantic and North Pacific oceans and commercially important as a food fish. |Capelin are a crucial food source for many seabirds and marine mammals. 毛鳞鱼是许多海鸟和海洋哺乳动物的重要食物来源。|Capelin are often processed into fish meal. 毛鳞鱼经常被加工成鱼粉。|近义词：smelt, forage fish|反义词:|名词
