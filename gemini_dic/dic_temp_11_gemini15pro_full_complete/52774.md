dilatory:/ˈdɪlətɔːri/ (US), /ˈdɪlətri/ (UK)| adj.|拖延的，迟缓的|Slow to act; inclined to delay or postpone.|例句：The dilatory tactics of the defense lawyer prolonged the trial. 辩护律师的拖延战术延长了审判时间。

例句：His dilatory response to the invitation angered the host. 他对邀请迟迟不作回复激怒了主人。|近义词：slow, tardy, procrastinating, sluggish, lagging|反义词：prompt, swift, expeditious, efficient|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *dilatorius*, from *dilator* "delayer, procrastinator," from *dilatus*, past participle of *differre* "to postpone, defer," from *dis-* "apart, aside" + *ferre* "to carry, bear."
