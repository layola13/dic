cacophonous: /kəˈkɒfənəs/ /kəˈkɑːfənəs/
adjective
刺耳的，不和谐的
Involving or producing a harsh, discordant mixture of sounds.
The cacophonous sound of the construction site made it difficult to concentrate.
工地的刺耳声音使人难以集中注意力。
The orchestra warmed up with a cacophonous blend of sounds.
管弦乐队用刺耳的声音混合进行热身。
近义词:discordant, dissonant, jarring, raucous, strident, harsh, grating
反义词:euphonious, harmonious, melodious, pleasant, sweet
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Greek *kakophōnos*  "ill-sounding," from *kakos* "bad" + *phōnē* "sound, voice".
