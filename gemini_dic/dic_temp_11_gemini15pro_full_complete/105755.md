kirar:/ˈkɪrɑːr/
n.
a lyre-like plucked string instrument
基拉尔琴，一种类似里拉琴的弹拨弦乐器
The kirar is a popular instrument in Eritrea and Ethiopia. 基拉尔琴在厄立特里亚和埃塞俄比亚很流行。
The musician played a beautiful melody on the kirar.  这位音乐家在基拉尔琴上演奏了一段优美的旋律。
synonyms: lyre, krar
antonyms: N/A
noun


Etymology:  From Tigrinya ክራር (krar), from Ge'ez ክራር (kärār). Ultimately from Ancient Greek κίθαρις (kítharis, “lyre”).
