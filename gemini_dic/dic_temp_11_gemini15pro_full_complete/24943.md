bruschetta:/bruːˈskɛtə/ /bruːˈʃɛtə/
n.
烤面包片，意式烤面包
An Italian appetizer consisting of toasted bread rubbed with garlic and topped with olive oil and tomatoes.
We started with an order of bruschetta. 我们先点了一份烤面包。
近义词:crostini, antipasto
反义词:(None -  It's a food item, not a concept with an opposite)
词性:noun
