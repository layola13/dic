initializes:/ɪˈnɪʃəlaɪzɪz/
| v. |初始化，设定初值|Sets a program or system to its starting state or configures variables with their initial values.|The program initializes the screen to a blue background. 程序将屏幕初始化为蓝色背景。|Synonyms: sets up, prepares, boots, configures|Antonyms: resets, terminates, closes|Verb


initializes:/ɪˈnɪʃəlaɪzɪz/
| v. |初始化，设定初值|Assigns initial values to variables or data structures.|The function initializes an array with zeros. 该函数用零初始化一个数组。|Synonyms: starts, begins, instantiates|Antonyms: ends, finishes, destroys|Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From initialize + -s (third-person singular present tense form).  Initialize itself comes from initial + -ize. Initial derives from the Latin word "initialis," meaning "of or pertaining to a beginning."
