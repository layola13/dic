anesthetizing:/əˈnɛsθəˌtaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |麻醉；使麻木|The process of inducing a loss of sensation, especially to pain, in a person or animal, especially so as to allow surgery to be performed.|The surgeon is anesthetizing the patient before the operation. 外科医生正在手术前麻醉病人。|近义词：numbing, desensitizing|反义词:stimulating, awakening|词性:动词

anesthetic:/ˌænəsˈθɛtɪk/ | n. adj.|麻醉剂；麻醉的|A substance that induces insensitivity to pain.|The dentist used a local anesthetic before pulling the tooth. 牙医在拔牙前使用了局部麻醉剂。|近义词:narcotic, opiate|反义词:stimulant|词性:名词,形容词
