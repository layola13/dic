chillout:/ˈtʃɪlaʊt/| n. vi. adj.|放松，冷静，休闲|A period of relaxation or inactivity.|After a stressful week, I just want to chillout on the beach. 在压力很大的一周后，我只想在沙滩上放松一下。|近义词：relax, unwind, de-stress, hang out|反义词:stress out, tense up|词性:名词,动词,形容词
Etymology: A blend of "chill" and "out," both informal terms related to relaxation. "Chill" meaning to become or cause to become calm, and "out" implying a state of detachment or freedom from stress.
