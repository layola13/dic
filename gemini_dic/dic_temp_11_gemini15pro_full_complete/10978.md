arbitrationists:/ˌɑːrbɪˈtreɪʃənɪsts/
n.
仲裁员，裁决人
A person who settles a dispute or disagreement between two parties.
The arbitrationists listened carefully to both sides of the argument before making their decision. 仲裁员在作出决定之前仔细听取了双方的论点。
The arbitrationists' ruling was binding on both companies. 仲裁员的裁决对两家公司都有约束力。
synonyms: arbitrator, arbiter, mediator, judge, umpire
antonyms: disputant, litigant
noun
