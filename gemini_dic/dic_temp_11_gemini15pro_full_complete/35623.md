coated:/ˈkoʊtɪd/| adj.|涂层的，覆盖的|Covered with a layer of something.|The table was coated with dust. 桌子上覆盖着一层灰尘.|近义词：covered, layered, plated|反义词:bare, uncoated|词性:形容词

词源: From the verb "coat," which comes from Old French "cote" (tunic, coat), ultimately from Late Latin "cotta" (a kind of tunic).
