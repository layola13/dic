overaptly:/ˌoʊvərˈæptli/| adv.|过于贴切地，过分恰当地| excessively aptly; too fittingly or suitably.  It describes something that is appropriate to an excessive degree, often to the point of seeming forced or unnatural.|He overaptly described the situation as a "comedy of errors," when in reality it was quite tragic.|近义词：excessively aptly, too fittingly, inappropriately aptly|反义词:inaptly, unsuitably, inappropriately|词性:副词

Etymology:
"Over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubar "over, above") + "aptly" (from "apt" - Latin aptus "fit, suitable" + "-ly" - Old English -lice, forming adverbs).
