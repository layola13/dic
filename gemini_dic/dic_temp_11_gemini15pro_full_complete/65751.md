evermore:/ˈɛvərˌmɔːr/ (US), /ˈevəˌmɔː/ (UK)| adv.|永远，总是|Always; forever. It implies a continuous state from the present into the future.|She vowed to love him evermore. 她发誓永远爱他。|近义词：forever, always, perpetually, eternally|反义词：never|词性:副词

evermore:/ˈɛvərˌmɔːr/ (US), /ˈevəˌmɔː/ (UK)| n.|永远，永恒|A poetic term for eternity or a state of lasting forever.|His heart ached for the evermore. 他的心渴望永恒。|近义词：eternity, perpetuity, forever|反义词：temporality|词性:名词
