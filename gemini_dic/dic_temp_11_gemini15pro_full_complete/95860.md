廉洁性；不腐败性；不能腐败或腐烂
The incorruptibleness of the judge was known throughout the land.  这位法官的廉洁全国闻名。
He admired her incorruptibleness and integrity. 他钦佩她的廉洁正直。
近义词: integrity, probity, honesty, uprightness, righteousness
反义词: corruptibility, dishonesty, venality, depravity
