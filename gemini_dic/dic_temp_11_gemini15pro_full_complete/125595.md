multiuse: /ˈmʌltiˌjuːs/
| adj. | 多用途的，多功能的 | Designed or suitable for more than one use. | This is a multiuse building, housing offices, shops, and apartments.  这是一栋多用途建筑，设有办公室、商店和公寓。| This multiuse tool can be used as a screwdriver, a knife, and a saw.  这个多用途工具可以用作螺丝刀、刀和锯。 | 近义词: multipurpose, versatile | 反义词: single-purpose, specialized | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "multi-" (many) + "use" (n. & v.)
