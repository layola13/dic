overcultivates:/ˌōvərˈkʌltɪveɪts/| v. |过度耕作，过度种植|To cultivate (land) excessively, leading to depletion of soil nutrients and reduced fertility.|The farmer overcultivated his fields, resulting in poor yields the following year. 农民过度耕种了他的田地，导致来年产量低下。|Example 2: Overcultivating the land can lead to erosion and desertification. 过度耕种土地会导致水土流失和荒漠化。|近义词：overfarm, overwork|反义词: undercultivate|词性:动词
