outyells:/ˌaʊtˈjɛl/
verb
大声喊叫，盖过…的声音
to shout louder than someone else
The coach outyelled the noisy crowd, trying to get his players' attention. 教练的声音盖过了嘈杂的人群，试图引起球员们的注意。

The street vendor outyelled his competitors, advertising his wares. 街头小贩的声音盖过了他的竞争对手，大声宣传他的商品。

shout louder than, drown out, overshout
be outyelled by, be drowned out by
