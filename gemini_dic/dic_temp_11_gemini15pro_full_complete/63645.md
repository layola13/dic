eosphorite:/ˈiːəsfəˌraɪt/ | n. | 水磷锰矿 | A hydrous manganese iron phosphate mineral, typically pink to reddish-brown in color.  It is often found in granite pegmatites. | Eosphorite is a relatively rare mineral sought after by collectors. 水磷锰矿是一种相对稀有的矿物，受到收藏家的追捧。| Eosphorite crystals can exhibit a beautiful vitreous luster. 水磷锰矿晶体可以呈现美丽的玻璃光泽。 |  Synonyms: Childrenite (similar composition), Triplite | Antonyms: N/A (Minerals don't typically have antonyms) | noun
