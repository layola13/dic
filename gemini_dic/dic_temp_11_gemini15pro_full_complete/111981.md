londonderry:/ˈlʌndənderi/
noun
伦敦德里
A city in Northern Ireland, officially called Derry since 1984, but still often referred to as Londonderry.
The siege of Londonderry took place in 1689.  1689 年发生了伦敦德里围城战。
Londonderry is known for its well-preserved city walls. 伦敦德里以其保存完好的城墙而闻名。
synonyms: Derry,  Doire (Irish name)
antonyms: (No direct antonym, could use a different city name, e.g., Belfast)
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From London + Derry.  "Derry" itself comes from the Irish word "Doire" meaning oak grove. The "London" prefix was added in the 17th century after the city was granted to the London guilds by King James I.
