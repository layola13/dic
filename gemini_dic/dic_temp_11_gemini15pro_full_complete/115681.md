margarize: /ˈmɑːrdʒəˌraɪz/ | v. | 人造黄油化; 用人造黄油涂抹;使…类似人造黄油; | To cause to resemble margarine, especially in consistency or texture. To treat or adulterate with or as if with margarine.  | The baker margarized the bread instead of using butter.  面包师用人造黄油而不是黄油涂抹面包。| 近义词: adulterate, debase | 反义词: purify, refine | 词性: 动词

margarize: /ˈmɑːrdʒəˌraɪz/ | v. |降低质量，掺假（尤指食品）; | To lower the quality of something, especially food, by adding a cheaper substance. |  The company was accused of margarizing their premium coffee by blending it with cheaper beans.  该公司被指控通过将优质咖啡与廉价咖啡豆混合来降低其质量。| 近义词: adulterate, degrade, cheapen | 反义词: enhance, improve, enrich | 词性: 动词
