goethite:/ˈɡoʊθaɪt/| n. |针铁矿|A common iron oxyhydroxide mineral, FeO(OH), occurring in radiating fibrous, colloform, or earthy masses, ranging in color from yellowish brown to dark brown to black. It is an important ore of iron.|Goethite is a common constituent of lateritic soils. 针铁矿是砖红壤土的常见成分。|Goethite forms as a weathering product of other iron-rich minerals. 针铁矿是其他富含铁的矿物的风化产物。|近义词:limonite (褐铁矿 - a general term for hydrated iron oxides)|反义词:N/A (No direct antonym for a specific mineral)|词性:noun
