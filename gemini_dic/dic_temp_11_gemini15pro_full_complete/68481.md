familistical:/ˌfæmɪˈlɪstɪkəl/
adj.
家庭至上的；家族主义的
Prioritizing family relationships and loyalty above all other social obligations, often to the detriment of individual autonomy or broader societal concerns.
This attitude is deeply familistical, prioritizing family needs over individual aspirations.  这种态度非常家庭至上，将家庭需求置于个人愿望之上。
Their familistical values led them to support each other, even when it meant bending the rules.  他们的家庭主义价值观使他们相互支持，即使这意味着违反规则。
近义词：familial, clannish, tribal
反义词：individualistic, independent
词性：adjective
词源：family + -istical (suffix forming adjectives)
