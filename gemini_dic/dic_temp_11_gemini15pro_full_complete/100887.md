intubate:/ˈɪntjʊˌbeɪt/ (US), /ˈɪntʃʊˌbeɪt/ (UK)| v. tr. |插管|To insert a tube into (a person's trachea, for example) to maintain an open airway.|The paramedic quickly intubated the patient to ensure he could breathe.  急救人员迅速给病人插管以确保他能呼吸。|Synonyms: insert a tube, ventilate|Antonyms: extubate|Verb
