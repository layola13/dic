manurially:/məˈnjʊəriəli/
ad.|用肥料地；以粪肥的方式|applied, spread, or derived from manure; relating to the use of manure as fertilizer. |The field was manurially enriched. 这块田地用肥料施肥得到了改良。|Synonyms:  with manure, by using manure|Antonyms: chemically, synthetically
