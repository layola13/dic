heartburn:/ˈhɑːrtbɜːrn/| n.|胃灼热，烧心|A burning sensation in the chest, caused by acid refluxing from the stomach into the esophagus.|I ate too much spicy food and now I have terrible heartburn. 我吃了太多辛辣的食物，现在胃灼热得厉害。|Synonyms: acid indigestion, pyrosis, dyspepsia|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but relief could be considered an opposite state)|Noun

heartburning:/ˈhɑːrtˌbɜːrnɪŋ/| n. | 嫉妒；怨恨|A feeling of resentment or jealousy.|He felt a heartburning when he saw his rival succeed.  看到对手成功，他感到一阵嫉妒。|Synonyms: envy, resentment, jealousy, bitterness|Antonyms: goodwill, benevolence, admiration|Noun
