conferrable:/kənˈfɜːrəbl/
adj.
可授予的，可赋予的
capable of being conferred or bestowed.
The honorary degree is conferrable on anyone who has made a significant contribution to the field.  这个荣誉学位可以授予任何对该领域做出重大贡献的人。
conferrable upon individuals who meet specific criteria. 授予符合特定标准的个人。
Synonyms: bestowable, grantable, awardable
Antonyms: non-conferrable, unbestowable, withheld
