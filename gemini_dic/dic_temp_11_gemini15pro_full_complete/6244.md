amobiography:/ˌeɪmoʊbaɪˈɑːɡrəfi/
n.
自传；个人传记
A biography written by the subject of the book.
例句：He is working on his amobiography, hoping to finish it before his next birthday. 他正在写自传，希望能在下个生日前完成。

例句：Her amobiography provides a candid look at her struggles and triumphs. 她的自传坦率地展现了她的奋斗和胜利。

近义词：autobiography, memoir
反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, potentially biography if contrasting who writes the work)
词性: noun
