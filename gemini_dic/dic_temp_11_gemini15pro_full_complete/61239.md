emanant:/ˈemənənt/| adj. n.|流出的，发出; 流出物|Flowing out from a source; something that issues or originates from a source.|例句：A strange energy seemed emanant from the ancient artifact.  一股奇怪的能量似乎从古代神器中散发出来。

例句：Love is an emanant quality of the divine. 爱是神圣的一种流露特质。|近义词：issuing, emerging, radiating, emanating, proceeding|反义词：absorbed, incoming, entering|词性: adjective, noun

词源: From Latin emanantem, present participle of emanare "to flow out," from ex- "out" + manare "to flow."
