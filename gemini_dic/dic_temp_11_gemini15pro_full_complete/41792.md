corset:/ˈkɔːrsɪt/ /ˈkɔːrˌsɛt/| n.|紧身胸衣|A close-fitting stiffened undergarment worn to support and shape the waist and abdomen, typically laced at the back and historically worn by women.|She wore a corset under her dress.|近义词:bodice, girdle, stays|反义词:loose clothing|noun

corset:/ˈkɔːrsɪt/ /ˈkɔːrˌsɛt/| vt.|用紧身胸衣束紧|To dress or bind (a person or part of the body) in a corset.|She corseted her waist to achieve a smaller figure.|近义词:constrict, cinch, tighten|反义词:loosen, unlace|verb
