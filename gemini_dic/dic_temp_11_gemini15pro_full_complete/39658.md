conservativenesses:/kənˈsɜːrvətɪvnɪsɪz/
| n. |保守性，保守主义|The state of being conservative; adherence to traditional values and practices.  The quality of favoring traditional values and opposing change or innovation. |Her conservativenesses in financial matters were legendary.  她在财务方面的保守性是出了名的。|The family's conservativenesses extended to their social life as well. 这个家庭的保守性也延伸到了他们的社会生活中。|synonyms: cautiousness, prudence, traditionalism|antonyms: radicalism, progressivism, liberalism|noun
