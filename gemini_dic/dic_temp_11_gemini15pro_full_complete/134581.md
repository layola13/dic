nonlosers:/nɒnˈluːzəz/ | n. | 非失败者；幸存者 | People who haven't lost, often in a competitive context.  It can sometimes have a slightly ironic or negative connotation, implying that while they haven't technically lost, they haven't necessarily won or achieved much either. |  The nonlosers in the competition advanced to the next round. 比赛中未被淘汰的选手晋级到下一轮。 | Near synonyms: survivors, remaining contestants | Antonyms: losers, defeated | Noun
