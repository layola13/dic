leucous: /ˈluːkəs/ | adj. | 雪白的，纯白的 |  Characterized by a whitish or pale coloration; lacking normal pigmentation.  Derived from the Greek "leukos" meaning "white". | The leucous petals of the flower stood out against the dark foliage.  这朵花雪白的花瓣在深色的叶子上格外显眼。| Synonyms:  albino, white, pale, blanched | Antonyms: dark, pigmented, colorful | adjective
