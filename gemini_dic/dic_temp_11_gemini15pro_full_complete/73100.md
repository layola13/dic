foppery:/ˈfɒpəri/ (US) /ˈfɑːpəri/ (UK)| n. |愚蠢的行为；虚荣；矫揉造作|Foppery refers to silly or affected behavior, often characterized by excessive concern with fashion and appearance.  It can also refer to the clothes or ornaments of a fop.|He dismissed their elaborate clothes and manners as mere foppery. 他认为他们精致的服装和举止不过是装腔作势。|近义词：vanity, affectation, pretension, dandyism|反义词：modesty, humility, naturalness|词性:noun

词源:
Late Middle English: from French *fopperie, from fop ‘fool’.
