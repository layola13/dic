conversationed:/ˌkɒnvəˈseɪʃənd/
verb
已交谈的 (This word is not standard English and is rarely used.  "Conversed" is the generally accepted past participle form.)
(Non-standard usage) Having engaged in a conversation.
Example: They had *conversationed* for hours about the issue.  (Non-standard) 他们就这个问题谈了几个小时。(非标准用法)
近义词: talked, chatted, discussed
反义词:  (No direct antonym for the act of having conversed)
词性: verb (non-standard)


conversed:/kənˈvɜːrst/
verb
交谈过
Past participle of "converse."  Indicates having engaged in a conversation.
Example:  We conversed about our plans for the summer. 我们谈论了我们的暑期计划。
Example: Having conversed with the witness, the detective left the scene. 与证人交谈后，侦探离开了现场。
近义词: talked, chatted, discussed
反义词: (No direct antonym for the act of having conversed)
词性: verb (past participle)
