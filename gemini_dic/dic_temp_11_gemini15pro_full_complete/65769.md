everyday:/ˈevriˌdei/ | adj. | 每日的；日常的；平凡的 | Happening or used every day; ordinary. | I wear my everyday clothes to work. 我穿日常衣服上班。 |  近义词: daily, routine, ordinary, usual | 反义词: unusual, special, extraordinary, occasional | 词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *everi day*, from Old English *ǣfre ǣlc dæg* ("every single day").
