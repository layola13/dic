discolored:/dɪsˈkʌlərd/
adjective
变色的，褪色的
Having lost its original color, stained, or faded.
The old photographs were discolored with age.  这些老照片因年代久远而褪色了。
The flood left a discolored stain on the wall. 洪水在墙上留下了一个褪色的污渍。
stained, faded, bleached, tinged
bright, vibrant, colorful
adjective
