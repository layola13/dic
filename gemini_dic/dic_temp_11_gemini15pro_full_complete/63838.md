epigeous:/ˌɛpɪˈdʒiːəs/
adjective
地上生的；表生的
Growing on or close to the ground; occurring above ground.
The epigeous cotyledons of the seedling expand, turn green and photosynthesize for a short time.
该幼苗的表生子叶展开，变绿并进行短时间的光合作用。

Epigeous fungi produce their spores above ground.
地上真菌在地面上产生孢子。

近义词:aboveground, terrestrial
反义词:hypogeous, subterranean
词性:adjective
