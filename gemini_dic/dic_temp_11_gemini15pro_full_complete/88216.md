hindered:/ˈhɪndərd/| adj.|受阻的，受妨碍的|slowed, delayed, or prevented from making progress.  Limited in ability or hindered by something.|The heavy rain hindered our progress. 大雨阻碍了我们的前进。|近义词：hampered, obstructed, impeded, restricted|反义词：aided, assisted, facilitated, helped|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *hindren*, from Old English *hindrian* "to prevent, keep back, restrain," from Proto-Germanic *hindrōjaną* (from *hindar* "behind"), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱéndʰro-* "center, heart."  The sense evolution appears to be from "put behind" to "hold back."
