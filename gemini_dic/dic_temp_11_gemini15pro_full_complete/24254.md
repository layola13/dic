briefing's: /ˈbriːfɪŋz/
noun
简报会(的)；情况介绍会(的)
Possessive form of "briefing," indicating ownership or association.  It refers to something that belongs to or is related to a briefing.
The briefing's main points were summarized in a handout.  简报会的要点总结在一份讲义里。
The general reviewed the briefing's key findings. 将军回顾了简报的关键发现。
Synonyms: summary's, report's, rundown's
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a possessive form; consider antonyms of "briefing" itself like "misinformation," "confusion")
词性: noun (possessive)
