airless:/ˈeərlɪs/| adj.|无空气的，不通风的|Lacking air; without air.  It describes a place or environment that is stuffy, stifling, or suffocating due to the absence of fresh air.|The airless room made it difficult to breathe. 这个不通风的房间让人难以呼吸。|Synonyms: stuffy, stifling, suffocating, close, unventilated|Antonyms: airy, ventilated, breezy|Etymology:  From air + -less (suffix meaning "without").|Word Type: Adjective
