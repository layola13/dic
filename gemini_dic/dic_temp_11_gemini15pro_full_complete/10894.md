araeostyle:/ˌærɪˈɒstaɪl/
adjective
稀柱式的，柱间距宽的
Having wide spacing between columns, typically four or more times the diameter of the columns.
The temple was built in the araeostyle manner, giving it a spacious and airy feel. 这座寺庙以稀柱式建造，使其具有宽敞通风的感觉。

The architect chose an araeostyle design for the colonnade, creating a sense of grandeur.  这位建筑师为柱廊选择了稀柱式设计，营造了一种宏伟的感觉。

synonym: wide-spaced, widely spaced
antonym: pycnostyle, systyle
词性: adjective
