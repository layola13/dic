crooningly:/ˈkruːnɪŋli/
adverb|轻哼地，柔声地|In a soft, gentle singing voice, often used to describe a sentimental or romantic style of singing.|He crooningly sang a lullaby to his baby daughter. 他柔声地给他的宝贝女儿唱了一首摇篮曲。|近义词：softly, gently, tenderly, soothingly|反义词：loudly, harshly, raucously
词性: 副词

词源: From croon + -ing + -ly.  "Croon" comes from Middle Dutch "kronen" meaning "to groan or lament," which later evolved to mean "to hum or sing softly."
