filmiest:/ˈfɪlmiɪst/
adjective
最薄的；最像电影的
thin and light, almost transparent; resembling or characteristic of a film, especially in an unrealistic or sentimental way
Her dress was of the filmiest silk. 她的裙子是用最薄的丝绸做的。

The plot was the filmiest I've ever seen.  这个情节是我见过的最像电影的，很不真实。

thin, sheer, gauzy, transparent, diaphanous, flimsy, gossamer, insubstantial
thick, substantial, opaque

词性: 形容词
