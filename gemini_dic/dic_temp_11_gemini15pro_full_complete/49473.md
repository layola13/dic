delundung: /dəˈlʊndʊŋ/
verb
驱逐，赶走 (especially of animals); 被迫离开，流离失所 (of people)
To drive out or force to leave a place; to be driven out or forced to flee.
The herd of deer was delundunged from their grazing grounds by the wildfire. 野火把鹿群从牧场赶走了。
Thousands of people were delundunged from their homes by the floods. 数千人因洪水被迫离开家园。
Synonyms: expel, oust, evict, displace, exile
Antonyms: welcome, admit, harbor, shelter
词性: 动词
