clapperboard:/ˈklæpəbɔːrd/ (US) /ˈklæpəbɔːd/ (UK)| n. | 拍板；场记板 |A board used in filmmaking that displays information about a scene and is clapped at the beginning of each take to synchronize sound and picture. |The clapperboard snapped shut, signaling the start of the scene.  场记板合上了，标志着场景的开始。| synonym: clapper, slate | antonym: N/A | noun

clapperboards:/ˈklæpəbɔːrdz/ (US) /ˈklæpəbɔːdz/ (UK)| n. | 拍板（复数）| Multiple clapperboards used on a film set. |The film crew had several clapperboards on hand in case one broke. 剧组准备了几个拍板，以防一个坏了。 | synonym: clappers, slates | antonym: N/A | noun
