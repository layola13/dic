delivers:/dɪˈlɪvərz/
verb
递送;发表;履行;实现;接生
brings and hands over to the intended recipient or destination; utters or presents orally; carries out or fulfills a promise or request; achieves or produces a desired result; assists in childbirth.
The courier delivers packages every day. 快递员每天都送包裹。
The CEO delivers a speech at the annual conference. 首席执行官在年会上发表演讲。
The company delivered on its promise of higher profits. 公司实现了更高的利润承诺。
The doctor delivered a healthy baby boy. 医生接生了一个健康的男婴。
近义词: distributes, presents, fulfills, executes, achieves, births
反义词: withholds, cancels, fails, neglects
