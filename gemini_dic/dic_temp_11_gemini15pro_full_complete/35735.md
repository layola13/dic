cochaired:/ˌkoʊˈtʃɛrd/| v.| 共同主持，联合主持 |  acted as cochair, along with another person, in presiding over a meeting or event.  |  Example:  She cochaired the conference with her colleague. 她和她的同事共同主持了这次会议。 | Example: They cochaired the fundraising committee. 他们共同主持了筹款委员会。| Synonyms: co-presided, jointly chaired | Antonyms:  solely chaired, independently chaired | verb (past tense)
