forecasted:/ˈfɔːrkæstɪd/| v. |预测的，预报的|Past participle of "forecast," meaning predicted or projected.  It describes something that has been the subject of a prediction.|The forecasted rain never arrived. 预报的雨从未到来。|Synonym: predicted, projected, anticipated|Antonym: unexpected, unforeseen|verb

forecast:/ˈfɔːrkæst/| n. v. |预测，预报|An estimate or prediction of a future event or trend.|The weather forecast calls for sunshine tomorrow. 天气预报说明天是晴天。|Synonym: prediction, projection, prognosis|Antonym: hindsight, retrospect|noun, verb
