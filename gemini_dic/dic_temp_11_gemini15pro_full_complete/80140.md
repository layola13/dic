god-awful:/ˈɡɒd ˈɔːfʊl/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːd ˈɔːfəl/ (US)| adj.|糟糕透顶的；极糟的|Extremely unpleasant or bad; terrible.|The food at the hotel was god-awful.  这家酒店的食物糟透了。|The traffic was god-awful this morning. 今早的交通拥堵不堪。|Synonyms: terrible, awful, dreadful, atrocious, abysmal|Antonyms: wonderful, excellent, fantastic, great|adjective
