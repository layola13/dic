magian:/ˈmeɪdʒɪən/ | adj. | 魔法的；魔术的 | Relating to magi or magic. | The magian rites were shrouded in secrecy.  魔法仪式笼罩在神秘之中。 | synonyms: magical, sorcerous | antonyms: mundane, ordinary | adjective
