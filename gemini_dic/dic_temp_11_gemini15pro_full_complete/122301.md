mitsui:/mɪˈtsuːi/ | n. | 三井 | A large Japanese business conglomerate (zaibatsu).  Mitsui is one of the most prominent keiretsu in Japan, with interests in finance, real estate, retail, and many other industries. |  Mitsui has a long history dating back to the 17th century. 三井有着悠久的历史，可以追溯到17世纪。 |  The Mitsui group includes companies such as Mitsui & Co. and Sumitomo Mitsui Banking Corporation. 三井集团包括三井物产和三井住友银行等公司。 | synonyms: conglomerate, zaibatsu, keiretsu | antonyms: N/A | noun
