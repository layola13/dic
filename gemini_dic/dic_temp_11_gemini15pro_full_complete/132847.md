noneffetely:/ˌnɒnɪˈfɛktli/
adverb
并非娇柔做作地; 强有力地; 果断地
in a manner that is not effete; strongly; decisively.
He gripped the hammer noneffetely and began to work. 他强有力地抓住锤子，开始工作。
Synonym: vigorously, robustly, powerfully, strongly, firmly, resolutely, decisively
Antonym: effetely, weakly, feebly, delicately
词性: 副词
