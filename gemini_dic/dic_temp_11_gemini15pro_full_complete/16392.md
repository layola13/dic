barbizon:/ˈbɑːrbɪzən/ | n. | 巴比松; 巴比松画派 |A village in France, southeast of Paris, and the surrounding area, a popular resort for landscape painters in the mid-19th century. The Barbizon school of painters emphasized portraying nature realistically.|  Example:  The Barbizon painters were drawn to the rustic beauty of the countryside. 巴比松派的画家被乡村的质朴之美所吸引。

Example:  Her painting style was clearly influenced by the Barbizon school. 她的绘画风格明显受到巴比松画派的影响。 | Near synonyms:  rustic, pastoral, idyllic | Antonym: urban | Noun
