heart-ache:/ˈhɑːrteɪk/ (US) /ˈhɑːteɪk/ (UK)| n.| 心痛，悲痛|A feeling of deep sadness or emotional pain, typically caused by the loss of someone or something loved.|例句：She suffered terrible heartache after her husband's death. 她丈夫去世后，她遭受了巨大的悲痛。

例句：The news of his betrayal caused her immense heartache. 他背叛的消息让她心如刀绞。


近义词：grief, sorrow, anguish, heartbreak|反义词：joy, happiness|词性：名词
