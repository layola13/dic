evangelicism:/ɪˌvænˈdʒɛlɪsɪzəm/ | n. | 福音主义，传福音 | The spreading of the Christian gospel by public preaching or personal witness. |  Evangelicism is a core value of many Protestant denominations. 福音主义是许多新教教派的核心理念。

Evangelicism played a significant role in the social reforms of the 19th century. 福音主义在19世纪的社会改革中发挥了重要作用。 | 近义词：preaching, proselytizing, missionary work | 反义词：secularism, atheism | 词性：noun
