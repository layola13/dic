encephalopathies:/ɛnˌsɛfəˈlɒpəθiz/| n. |脑病|A broad term for any brain disease that alters brain function or structure.|The doctor diagnosed him with hepatic encephalopathy due to his liver failure. 医生诊断他由于肝衰竭而患有肝性脑病。|
Example: Various encephalopathies can result from viral infections. 各种脑病可能是由病毒感染引起的。

Example:  The child suffered from encephalopathies caused by a genetic defect.  这个孩子患有由基因缺陷引起的脑病。|Synonym: brain diseases, cerebral disorders|Antonym: brain health|noun
