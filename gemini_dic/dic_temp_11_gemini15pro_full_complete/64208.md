equidistantly: /ˌiːkwɪˈdɪstəntli/
adverb
等距地
at equal distances.

The trees were planted equidistantly along the road.
这些树沿着路等距种植。

The points are marked equidistantly on the line.
这些点在直线上等距标记。


近义词: evenly spaced, uniformly
反义词: unevenly, irregularly
词性: adverb

词源:
From equi- (equal) + distant + -ly (adverbial suffix).  The root "distant" comes from Latin "distans," present participle of "distare" (to stand apart), which is composed of "dis-" (apart) and "stare" (to stand).
