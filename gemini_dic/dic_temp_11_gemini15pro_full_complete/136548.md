nonsaccharinity:/ˌnɒnˌsækəˈrɪnɪti/
n.
无糖性；非糖性
The nonsaccharinity of stevia makes it a popular alternative to sugar. 甜菊糖的无糖性使其成为糖的流行替代品。
The nonsaccharinity of the substance was confirmed by laboratory tests.  实验室测试证实了该物质的无糖性。
近义词: sugarlessness, absence of sugar
反义词: sweetness, saccharinity
词性: noun

Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + saccharinity (noun meaning "sweetness" derived from Latin saccharum "sugar")
