nonplasticity:/ˌnɒnplæˈstɪsɪti/
n.|非塑性，无塑性|The nonplasticity of the material made it unsuitable for molding.  这种材料的非塑性使其不适合模塑。|
nonplasticity|n.|lack of plasticity; inability to be molded or shaped|Due to the nonplasticity of the clay, it crumbled when we tried to shape it. 由于粘土缺乏可塑性，当我们试图塑造它时，它碎了。|
nonplasticity|n.|The inability of a substance to be molded or deformed permanently.|The nonplasticity of the metal made it difficult to bend.  这种金属的非塑性使其难以弯曲。|近义词:inflexibility, rigidity|反义词:plasticity, malleability|
