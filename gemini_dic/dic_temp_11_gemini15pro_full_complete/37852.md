competitiveness:/kəmˈpɛtɪtɪvnəs/
n.|竞争力；竞争性|The ability of a business, a country, or a person to compete successfully against rivals. |The company's competitiveness has improved significantly in recent years.  这家公司的竞争力近年来显著提高。|Synonyms: ability, capability, competence, rivalry |Antonyms: inability, incompetence, weakness
