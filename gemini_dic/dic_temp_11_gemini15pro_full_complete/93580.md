iman:/ˈiːmɑːn/ (US), /ˈiːmæn/ (UK)| n.|信仰，信念，信条|Faith or belief, especially in a religious context.  It is often used in Islam to refer to faith in God and his prophets.|She has a strong iman. 她有坚定的信仰。|He questioned his iman after the tragedy.  这场悲剧之后他开始质疑自己的信仰。|近义词：faith, belief, conviction, creed|反义词：disbelief, doubt, skepticism|词性:名词

词源:
From Arabic إيمان (ʾīmān), meaning "faith" or "belief".

注意事项:
While iman can be generally translated as "faith" or "belief," it carries a specific connotation within Islamic contexts.  It refers to the complete submission and trust in God and his messengers.
