decarbonises:/diːˈkɑːrbənaɪzɪz/
verb
脱碳，降低碳排放
Reduces or removes carbon dioxide emissions.
The company decarbonises its operations by switching to renewable energy sources.  该公司通过改用可再生能源来降低其运营的碳排放。
The government is implementing policies to decarbonise the transportation sector. 政府正在实施政策以减少交通运输部门的碳排放。
Synonyms: decarbonize, neutralize, mitigate
Antonyms: pollute, contaminate
