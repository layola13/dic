awake:/əˈweɪk/ /əˈweɪk/| adj. v. |醒着的，唤醒 |alert and responsive; not asleep; to stop sleeping or to cause someone to stop sleeping|
She lay awake, staring at the ceiling. 她醒着躺在那儿，盯着天花板。
He awoke to the sound of birds singing. 他在鸟鸣声中醒来。

|近义词：alert, conscious, arouse, awaken |反义词: asleep, unconscious |词性:形容词，动词|


awake:/əˈweɪk/ /əˈweɪk/| v. |唤醒，觉醒，意识到 |to stop sleeping or to cause someone to stop sleeping; to become aware of something|
The storm awoke me in the middle of the night.  暴风雨半夜把我吵醒了。
The movement has awoken people to the dangers of pollution. 这场运动使人们意识到了污染的危害。

|近义词：arouse, awaken, rouse |反义词: lull, soothe |词性:动词|

PHRASE->
awake to something: 意识到某事
He finally awoke to the danger he was in. 他终于意识到了自己所处的危险。
fully awake: 完全清醒
I wasn't fully awake when I answered the door. 我开门的时候还没有完全清醒。
stay awake: 保持清醒
I drank coffee to stay awake during the long drive. 我喝咖啡是为了在长途驾驶中保持清醒。
wide awake: 十分清醒
I was wide awake after the nightmare. 做完噩梦后我完全清醒了。
awaken someone to something: 使某人意识到某事
The documentary awakened me to the plight of refugees. 这部纪录片让我意识到了难民的困境。
