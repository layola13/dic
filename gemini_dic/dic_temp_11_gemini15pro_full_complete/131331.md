noncharacter:/nɒnˈkærəktər/ /nɑːnˈkærəktər/| n.|非人物; 非典型人物; 不合格的人; 符号; |A person who lacks the qualities that are considered typical of or essential to a particular role or profession, or someone who is regarded as unpleasant or dishonest.|He was denounced as a noncharacter by his political opponents.|近义词:rogue, scoundrel, miscreant|反义词:paragon, exemplar|词性:名词

词源:
non- (prefix) + character (noun).  The prefix "non-" means "not" or "the opposite of".  Therefore, "noncharacter" literally means "not a character" or "lacking the qualities of a character".
