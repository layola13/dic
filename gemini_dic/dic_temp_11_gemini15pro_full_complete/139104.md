obdurately:/ˈɒbdjʊrətli/ /ˈɑːbdərətli/| adv.|顽固地，执拗地|In a stubbornly persistent manner; unyieldingly.  Showing a determined refusal to change one's mind or course of action.|He obdurately refused to apologize. 他顽固地拒绝道歉。|例句：The child obdurately refused to eat his vegetables.  孩子执拗地不肯吃蔬菜。

例句：Despite all evidence to the contrary, she obdurately insisted she was right.  尽管所有证据都相反，但她仍然固执地坚持自己是正确的。|近义词：stubbornly, persistently, resolutely, inflexibly|反义词：yieldingly, compliantly, submissively|词性:副词
