consolers:/ˈkɒnsələrz/ (British), /ˈkɑːnsələrz/ (American)| n. |安慰者，慰问者|People who offer comfort or solace to someone who is grieving or distressed. |The consolers offered their condolences to the grieving family. 安慰者向悲痛的家庭表示哀悼。|
The nurses acted as consolers to the patients undergoing difficult treatments. 护士们充当了正在接受艰难治疗的病人的安慰者。|近义词：comforters, sympathizers, supporters |反义词: tormentors, bullies| noun
