miserere:/ˌmɪzəˈreɪri/ /ˌmɪzəˈrɛəri/| n.|忏悔诗;求主怜悯|A prayer, psalm, or hymn of supplication, especially Psalm 51 in the Vulgate, beginning Miserere mei, Deus ("Have mercy upon me, O God").|The choir sang a beautiful miserere. 合唱团唱了一首优美的忏悔诗。|近义词：penitence, supplication, lament|反义词:celebration, rejoicing|词性:noun

词源:
From Latin *miserere* "have mercy," the imperative singular of *miserērī,* present passive infinitive of *misereō,* "I pity."
