fish-up:/ˈfɪʃ ʌp/ | phrasal verb | 捞出；搜寻出；找出 | To retrieve something from a place or container, often with some effort or difficulty.  It can also mean to discover hidden information. |  He fished up an old photo from the bottom of the box. 他从盒子底部翻出一张旧照片。 | dredge up, retrieve, unearth, discover | conceal, hide | verb

fish-up:/ˈfɪʃ ʌp/ | n. | (非正式) 捕捞到的东西；搜寻出的东西 | Something that has been retrieved, especially information. | The fish-up revealed some surprising details about the company's finances.  这次调查揭露了该公司财务方面一些令人惊讶的细节。 | discovery, findings | - | noun
