afforested:/əˈfɔːrɪstɪd/
adjective
已造林的；植树造林的
covered with trees by planting; planted with trees
例句：The once barren hills were now afforested with pine trees.  曾经荒芜的山丘现在种满了松树。

例句：The government has ambitious plans to afforested large areas of wasteland. 政府制定了雄心勃勃的计划，要在荒地上大面积植树造林。
synonym:  planted, wooded, forested
antonym: deforested, cleared, barren
词性: adjective
