aficionados:/əˌfɪʃjəˈnɑːdoʊz/ (US) /əˌfɪʃjəˈnɑːdəʊz/ (UK)| n. |爱好者；狂热者|A person who is very knowledgeable and enthusiastic about an activity, subject, or pastime.|例句：He is an aficionado of fine wines. 他是美酒的爱好者。

例句：The restaurant is popular with aficionados of Italian cuisine. 这家餐厅很受意大利菜爱好者的欢迎。

近义词：enthusiast, devotee, fan, admirer, connoisseur|反义词：critic, detractor|词性：名词
