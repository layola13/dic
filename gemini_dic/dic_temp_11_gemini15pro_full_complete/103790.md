joust:/dʒaʊst/ | n. vi. | 骑马长矛比武；竞争；争论 | A combat between two mounted knights using lances.  A competition or struggle for supremacy. |  The knights prepared for the joust. 骑士们准备好了马上长矛比武。 He jousts with his rivals for control of the company. 他与对手争夺公司的控制权。 | 近义词：tilt, tournament, contest, compete, clash, struggle | 反义词：yield, surrender | 词性：名词，动词

词源：From Old French "joster" (to tilt, joust), from Vulgar Latin "*juxtare" (to approach, join), from Latin "iuxta" (near, beside).
