over-breed: /ˌoʊvərˈbriːd/
verb
过度繁殖，过量育种
To breed animals (especially domesticated ones) excessively, often leading to undesirable traits or health problems.
The farmer over-bred his prize-winning bull, resulting in weaker offspring.  农民过度繁殖了他的获奖公牛，导致后代虚弱。
Purebred dogs are often over-bred, leading to genetic defects.  纯种狗经常被过度繁殖，导致基因缺陷。
近义词: overproduce, propagate excessively
反义词: under-breed
词性: verb

over-bred: /ˌoʊvərˈbrɛd/
adjective
过度繁殖的，过量育种的
(of animals) Bred excessively, often leading to undesirable traits or health problems.
The over-bred puppies were prone to illness.  过度繁殖的小狗容易生病。
The shelter was full of over-bred and abandoned animals.  收容所里到处都是过度繁殖和被遗弃的动物。
近义词: overproduced
反义词: under-bred
词性: adjective
