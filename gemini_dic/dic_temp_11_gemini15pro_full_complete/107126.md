ladyfish:/ˈleɪdɪfɪʃ/| n. | 北梭鱼，香蕉鱼 |A slender, silvery game fish (Elops saurus) found in warm coastal waters, known for its acrobatic leaps when hooked.  It is also called tenpounder. |例句：The ladyfish put up a good fight, jumping out of the water several times.  北梭鱼奋力挣扎，跳出水面好几次。

例句：We caught several ladyfish while fishing near the pier. 我们在码头附近钓鱼时钓到了几条北梭鱼。|近义词：tenpounder, bonefish, skipjack |反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific fish species) |词性:noun
