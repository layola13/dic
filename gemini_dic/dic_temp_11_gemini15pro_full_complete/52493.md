diets:/ˈdaɪəts/| n.| 饮食；节食 |A way of eating in which you only eat certain foods, usually in order to lose weight or improve your health. |She's always trying new diets. 她总是尝试新的饮食方法。|  synonyms: regimen, dietary plan | antonyms: gluttony, overeating | noun


diets:/ˈdaɪəts/| v. | 节食|To restrict oneself to small amounts or special kinds of food in order to lose weight.|He's been dieting for months. 他已经节食好几个月了。 | synonyms: reduce, slim | antonyms: gorge, overindulge | verb
