chummed:/tʃʌmd/
|v.|交朋友；做朋友；撒鱼饵吸引鱼；|To have spent time with someone as a friend, or to have thrown chum (bait) into the water to attract fish.|We chummed up while working on the project together. 我们在共同进行项目时成为了朋友。|The fisherman chummed the waters to attract sharks. 渔夫在水中撒鱼饵来吸引鲨鱼。|近义词：befriended, palled around (for spending time with as a friend); baited (for attracting fish)|反义词:estranged|词性:动词

chum:/tʃʌm/
|n.|朋友；伙伴；鱼饵|A close friend, or bait consisting of fish parts thrown into the water to attract fish.|He's been my chum since childhood. 他从小就是我的好朋友。|The sharks were attracted by the chum. 鲨鱼被鱼饵吸引了。|近义词：friend, pal, buddy (for person); bait (for fish food)|反义词:enemy, foe|词性:名词
