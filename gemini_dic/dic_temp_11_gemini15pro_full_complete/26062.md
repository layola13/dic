bushless:/ˈbʊʃləs/
adjective
无灌木的；没有灌木丛的
Lacking bushes; free from bushes.
The bushless plains stretched as far as the eye could see.  广阔无垠的平原上看不到灌木丛。
The fire left the hillside bushless and barren.  大火过后，山坡上寸草不生，灌木全无。
synonyms:  unbushed, clear, open, barren
antonyms:  bushy, overgrown, shrubby
adjective
