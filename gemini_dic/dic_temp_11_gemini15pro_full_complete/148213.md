overshrank:/ˌoʊvərˈʃræŋk/
verb
过度缩水
shrunk or shrunken excessively, usually by washing.
The wool sweater overshrank in the hot wash.  这件羊毛衫在热水洗涤中过度缩水了。
The curtains overshrank after I washed them.  窗帘在我洗过之后过度缩水了。
近义词: shrunken, overwashed
反义词: stretched, expanded
词性: verb (past participle of overshrink)

overshrink:/ˌoʊvərˈʃrɪŋk/
verb
使过度缩水
to shrink excessively, usually by washing.
I overshrank my favorite shirt. 我把我最喜欢的衬衫洗缩水了。
Be careful not to overshrink the delicate fabric.  小心不要把这块精致的布料洗缩水了。
近义词: shrink, contract
反义词: stretch, expand
词性: verb
