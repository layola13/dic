buffer:/ˈbʌfər/| n. vt.|缓冲物，缓冲器；起缓冲作用的人或物|A buffer is something that reduces the impact of a shock or collision.|The trees acted as a buffer against the strong winds. 树木起到了缓冲强风的作用。|近义词：cushion, shield, safeguard|反义词:aggravator, intensifier|noun

buffer:/ˈbʌfər/| vt.|缓冲，减轻；保护|To buffer something means to protect it from harm or damage.|The government introduced measures to buffer the economy against the recession. 政府采取措施以缓冲经济衰退的影响。|近义词：protect, shield, cushion|反义词:expose, worsen|verb
