interaxial:/ˌɪntərˈæksiəl/
adjective
轴间的
Located between axes, especially of an optical system or a crystal.
The interaxial distance between the two lenses is crucial for optimal performance. 两块透镜之间的轴间距对于最佳性能至关重要。
The interaxial angle of the crystal determines its refractive properties. 晶体的轴间角决定了它的折射特性。
Synonyms: interaxis
Antonyms: coaxial
词性: adjective
