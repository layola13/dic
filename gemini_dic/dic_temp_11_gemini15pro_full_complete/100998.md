invalidities:/ˌɪnvəˈlɪdɪtiz/
n.
无效; 无效性; 无效条款; 病弱
The contract was declared void because of several invalidities.  该合同因一些无效条款而被宣布无效。
The lawyer pointed out the invalidities in the document. 律师指出了文件中无效的地方。
Synonyms: nullity, voidness, fallacy, falsehood, illegality
Antonyms: validity, legality, soundness, truth
词性: 名词
