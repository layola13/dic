incommiscible:/ˌɪnkəˈmɪsəbəl/ | adj. | 不互溶的，不能混合的 | Not capable of being mixed together; liquids that do not form a homogeneous mixture when added together. | Oil and water are incommiscible liquids. 油和水是不互溶的液体。| immiscible, non-miscible | miscible, soluble | adjective

incommiscible:/ˌɪnkəˈmɪsəbəl/ | adj. | 不相容的，格格不入的 |  (Figuratively) Used to describe things that are so different they cannot exist together harmoniously. | Their personalities are incommiscible; they constantly clash. 他们的性格不相容，总是冲突。 | incompatible, conflicting, antagonistic | compatible, harmonious | adjective
