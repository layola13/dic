becalm:/bɪˈkɑːm/
verb
使平静；使停滞
To make calm or still; to deprive of wind.
The sudden absence of wind becalmed the sailboat.  突如其来的无风状态使帆船停滞不前。
The ship was becalmed in the doldrums. 船在赤道无风带一动不动。
Synonyms: soothe, quiet, pacify, still
Antonyms: agitate, excite, rouse, stir
