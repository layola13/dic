conjecture:/kənˈdʒɛktʃər/ | n. | 推测，猜想 | An opinion or conclusion formed on the basis of incomplete information. | Many conjectures about the future of artificial intelligence have been proposed.  人们对人工智能的未来提出了许多猜想。| synonyms: hypothesis, speculation, guess, supposition, theory | antonyms: fact, proof, certainty, knowledge | noun
conjecture:/kənˈdʒɛktʃər/ | v. | 推测，猜想 | Form an opinion or supposition about (something) on the basis of incomplete information. |  Scientists conjectured that the universe began with a big bang. 科学家们推测宇宙始于一次大爆炸。 | synonyms: hypothesize, speculate, surmise, guess, suppose, theorize | antonyms: prove, know, ascertain, confirm | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *conjectura, from Latin conjicere "to throw together," from com- "together" + jacere "to throw."
