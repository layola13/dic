chomping:/ˈtʃɒmpɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈtʃɑːmpɪŋ/ (US)| v. |大声咀嚼，用力咬|Chewing or biting on something noisily and vigorously. |The horse was chomping on its hay. 马正在大声咀嚼干草。|The child was chomping on his apple. 孩子在大口啃苹果。|近义词：munching, gnawing, chewing vigorously|反义词：nibbling, sipping|词性:动词

chomping:/ˈtʃɒmpɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈtʃɑːmpɪŋ/ (US)| adj. |（非正式）渴望的，急切的|Informal; Eager or impatient. |He was chomping at the bit to get started. 他急切地想要开始。|She was chomping at the bit to tell everyone the good news. 她迫不及待地想告诉大家这个好消息。|近义词：eager, keen, impatient|反义词：apathetic, indifferent|词性:形容词
