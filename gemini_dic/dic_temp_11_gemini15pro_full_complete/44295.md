crookedly:/ˈkrʊkɪdli/ | adv. | 弯曲地，歪斜地，不正当地 | in a crooked manner; not straight or straightforward; dishonestly | The picture hung crookedly on the wall.  这幅画歪挂在墙上。 | The politician acted crookedly and was eventually arrested.  这个政客行为不端，最终被捕。 | synonym: askew, awry, dishonestly, deviously | antonym: straight, directly, honestly | 词性: 副词

crooked:/ˈkrʊkɪd/ | adj. | 弯曲的，歪斜的，不诚实的 | not straight; dishonest or illegal |  The path was crooked and difficult to follow. 这条小路弯弯曲曲的，很难走。 | He got involved in some crooked business deals. 他卷入了一些不正当的商业交易。 | synonym: bent, twisted, dishonest, corrupt | antonym: straight, honest, upright | 词性: 形容词
