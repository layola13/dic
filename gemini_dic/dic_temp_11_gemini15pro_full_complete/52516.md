differentiation:/ˌdɪfəˌrenʃiˈeɪʃən/| n.|区分，分化，变异|the process of becoming different or developing in different ways|例句：Cell differentiation is essential for the development of multicellular organisms. 细胞分化对于多细胞生物的发育至关重要。

例句：The differentiation of tasks allows for greater efficiency in the workplace.  工作任务的区分可以提高工作场所的效率。|近义词：distinction, separation, specialization|反义词：integration, similarity, uniformity|词性:noun
