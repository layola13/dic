mollifying:/ˈmɒlɪfaɪɪŋ/ /ˈmɑːlɪfaɪɪŋ/
adjective
抚慰的；平息的；使缓和的
Reducing anger or anxiety; appeasing.
例句：She spoke in a mollifying tone, hoping to calm him down. 她用抚慰的语气说话，希望能让他平静下来。

例句：The government made a mollifying gesture to the protesters. 政府向抗议者做出了安抚的姿态。

近义词：soothing, calming, appeasing, pacifying, conciliatory
反义词：agitating, provoking, irritating, exacerbating
词性：adjective
