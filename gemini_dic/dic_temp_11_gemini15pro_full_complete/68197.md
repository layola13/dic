fair-mindedness:/ˌfeərˈmaɪndɪdnəs/ | n. | 公正;公平;不偏不倚 | The quality of being open to and willing to consider different ideas or viewpoints, even those that contradict one's own beliefs; impartiality. |  The judge was known for his fair-mindedness in all his cases.  法官以在所有案件中都秉公执法而闻名。|  impartiality, objectivity, neutrality, open-mindedness, justness | bias, prejudice, partiality, unfairness | noun

fair-minded:/ˌfeərˈmaɪndɪd/ | adj. | 公正的;公平的;不偏不倚的 | Having or showing a disposition to deal with perceived injustices reasonably and equitably. | She was a fair-minded teacher, giving all her students equal opportunities.  她是一位公正的老师，给所有学生平等的机会。 | impartial, objective, neutral, unbiased, just | biased, prejudiced, partial, unfair | adjective
