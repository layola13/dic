half-pleased:/ˌhɑːfˈpliːzd/ | adj. | 半高兴的，略微满意的 |  Partly pleased; not fully satisfied.  A mixture of pleasure and dissatisfaction. |  He was half-pleased with the outcome, wishing he'd done better. 他对结果半高兴，希望自己能做得更好。|  She gave a half-pleased smile. 她露出一个略带满意之情的微笑。|  partially pleased, slightly satisfied, moderately pleased |  displeased, dissatisfied, unhappy | adjective
