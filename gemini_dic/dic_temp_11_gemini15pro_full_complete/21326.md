bloody-mindedly:/ˌblʌdiˈmaɪndɪdli/
| adv. |故意刁难地，故意作对地|In a deliberately awkward or contrary way; perversely.|He bloody-mindedly refused to cooperate. 他故意刁难地拒绝合作。|Example 1: She bloody-mindedly ignored all my advice. 她故意不理会我所有的建议。

Example 2: He bloody-mindedly left the door open, even though I asked him to close it. 即使我让他关门，他还是故意把门开着。|近义词：awkwardly, perversely, contrary, stubbornly, obstinately|反义词：cooperatively, willingly, agreeably, obligingly|词性：副词
