electroluminescent:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˌluːmɪˈnɛsənt/
adjective|电致发光的|Emitting light as a result of the passage of an electric current.|The electroluminescent display on my watch is easy to read in the dark. 我的手表上的电致发光显示屏在黑暗中很容易阅读。|The electroluminescent panels lit up the room with a soft glow. 电致发光板使房间发出柔和的光芒。|近义词:luminescent, phosphorescent|反义词:non-luminescent|词性:adjective

electroluminescence:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˌluːmɪˈnɛsəns/
noun|电致发光|The emission of light from a substance caused by the passage of an electric current through it.|The electroluminescence of the material increased with the voltage.  这种材料的电致发光随着电压的增加而增强。|Researchers are exploring new materials with improved electroluminescence. 研究人员正在探索具有改进电致发光的新材料。|近义词:luminescence, phosphorescence|反义词:non-luminescence|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From electro- + luminescent.  "Electro-" refers to electricity, and "luminescent" refers to emitting light.
