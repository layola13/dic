esker:/ˈɛskər/| n.|蛇形丘，冰河砂礫堤|A long, winding ridge of stratified sand and gravel deposited by a meltwater stream flowing within or beneath a glacier.|The esker snaked across the landscape for miles. 这条蛇形丘蜿蜒穿过地貌达数英里。|Synonyms: ridge, mound, embankment|Antonyms: valley, depression|noun
