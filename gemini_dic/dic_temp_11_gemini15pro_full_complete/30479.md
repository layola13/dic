centrism: /ˈsentrɪzəm/ | n. |中间主义，中间路线|The political doctrine of avoiding extremes and adhering to a moderate course. |  Centrism is often criticized for being unprincipled. 中间主义常因缺乏原则而受到批评。

Centrism can be a difficult position to maintain in a polarized political climate. 在两极分化的政治气候下，中间立场可能难以维持。| 近义词: moderation, neutrality | 反义词: extremism, radicalism | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From French *centrisme*, from centre (“center”) + -isme (“-ism”). Ultimately from Latin *centrum* (“center”).
