eudaemonism:/ˌjuːdɪˈmiːnɪzəm/ | n. | 幸福主义; 伦理幸福说 |  A system of ethics that bases moral obligations on the pursuit of happiness. |  Aristotle believed that eudaemonism, the pursuit of flourishing, was the ultimate goal of human life.  亚里士多德认为，幸福主义，即追求繁荣，是人类生活的最终目标。 | synonyms: happiness, well-being, flourishing, contentment | antonyms: unhappiness, misery, suffering | noun
