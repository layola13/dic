overs:/ˈəʊvəz/ (UK) /ˈoʊvərz/ (US)| n.| 局; 结束; 额外的东西|A period of play in which a team bowls six balls in cricket.  In other sports, it can refer to a period of time or a set number of actions.|例句：The match went into extra overs.  比赛进入了加时赛。|例句：They scored 20 runs in that over. 他们在那一个回合中得了20分。|近义词：innings, rounds (in some contexts)|反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:noun
