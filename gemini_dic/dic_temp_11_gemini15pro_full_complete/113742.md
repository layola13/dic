macrograph:/ˈmækɹəɡræf/ | n. |宏观照片；放大照片 | A photograph of an object taken at low magnification, typically showing its overall structure or appearance.  A photograph or image that shows something magnified. | Example 1: The macrograph revealed the internal structure of the metal. 这张宏观照片揭示了金属的内部结构。

Example 2: The geologist used a macrograph to study the rock formation. 地质学家使用宏观照片来研究岩层。 | 近义词: photomicrograph (at higher magnification), photograph, image | 反义词: micrograph | 词性: noun
