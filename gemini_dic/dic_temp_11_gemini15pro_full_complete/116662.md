matting:/ˈmæ.tɪŋ/ (US), /ˈmæt.ɪŋ/ (UK)| n.| 垫子；衬垫；无光泽的表面；（图片的）卡纸；（头发的）缠结|A piece of coarse material used as a floor covering, for protection, or for packaging. Also refers to a dull or matte finish, or the act of making something matte.  It can also refer to tangled hair.|The photographer used matting to frame the picture. 摄影师用卡纸给照片做框。|近义词:pad, cushion, lining, mat, dullness,  tangle|反义词:gloss, shine, luster, disentangle|noun

matting:/ˈmæ.tɪŋ/ (US), /ˈmæt.ɪŋ/ (UK)| v.| (使)缠结；(使)无光泽|The act of becoming tangled (especially hair) or making a surface matte or dull.|The cat's fur was matting from neglect.  猫的毛因为疏于照顾而缠结在一起。|近义词:tangling, dulling|反义词:shining, glossing, disentangling|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "matte," which comes from the Old French word "mat," meaning dull or unpolished.  Ultimately derived from the Latin "mattus," meaning "moistened," as matte surfaces were often achieved by dampening or moistening.
