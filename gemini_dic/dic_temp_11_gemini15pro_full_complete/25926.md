burnisher:/ˈbɜːrnɪʃər/ | n. | 磨光器，抛光机，磨光工具 | A tool or substance used for burnishing. |  He used a burnisher to polish the silver. 他用磨光器擦亮了银器。

| The jeweler used a burnisher to smooth the metal. 珠宝商用磨光工具磨平了金属。| Synonyms: polisher, buffer, smoother | Antonyms: roughener, scratcher | Noun



burnish: /ˈbɜːrnɪʃ/ | v. | 磨光，抛光；使光亮；改善，改进 | To polish (something, especially metal) by rubbing. To improve or refine. | She burnished the brass until it gleamed. 她把黄铜擦得闪闪发光。

| He tried to burnish his image after the scandal. 他试图在丑闻之后改善自己的形象。 | Synonyms: polish, buff, shine, refine, improve | Antonyms: tarnish, dull, roughen, worsen | Verb
