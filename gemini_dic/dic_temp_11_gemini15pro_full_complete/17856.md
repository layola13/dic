beefeater:/ˈbiːfiːtə(r)/
noun
侍卫；牛肉佬；伦敦塔卫士
A Yeoman Warder of the Tower of London, ceremonially responsible for guarding the Tower and its prisoners, and popularly regarded as a typically English figure.  Historically, they were also responsible for the monarch's safety.  The origin of the name is uncertain, possibly referring to a ration of beef given to these guards.
The beefeaters at the Tower of London are a popular tourist attraction.  伦敦塔的侍卫是受游客欢迎的景点。

The beefeater showed us around the Tower.  那个侍卫带我们参观了伦敦塔。

Synonyms: Yeoman Warder, guard
Antonyms: prisoner, captive
