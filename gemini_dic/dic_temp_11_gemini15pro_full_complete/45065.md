cubical:/ˈkjuːbɪkl/ | adj. | 立方的，立方形的 | Shaped like a cube; having six equal square sides. |  The office is filled with small, cubical workstations. 办公室里摆满了小的，立方体的工作站。| synonymous: cube-shaped, cubic | antonymous: spherical, round | adjective

cubical:/ˈkjuːbɪkl/ | n. | 小隔间 | A small partitioned-off area of a room, especially one used as an office. |  She works in a cubical in a large open-plan office. 她在一个大型开放式办公室的小隔间里工作。 | synonymous: workstation, booth, compartment | antonymous: open space, large room | noun
