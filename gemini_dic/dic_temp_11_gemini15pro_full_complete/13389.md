atom:/ˈætəm/| n.|原子|The basic unit of a chemical element.|例句：Every atom has a nucleus. 每个原子都有一个原子核。

例句：Atoms are the building blocks of matter. 原子是物质的基本组成部分。|近义词：particle, element|反义词：molecule (in the sense that a molecule is composed of multiple atoms)|词性: noun
