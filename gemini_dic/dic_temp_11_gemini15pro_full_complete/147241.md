overmellow:/ˌoʊvərˈmɛloʊ/ | adj. | 过熟的，过度成熟的；过于多愁善感的；过于柔情的 | Excessively mellow; too ripe, soft, or sweet; overly sentimental or emotional. | The peaches were overmellow and bruised.  桃子熟过头了，而且还有淤伤。 | The music was overmellow and made me feel sleepy.  音乐过于柔情，让我感到困倦。 | 近义词：overripe, mushy, sentimental, maudlin | 反义词：unripe, firm, tart, unsentimental | 词性：形容词

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer-, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "over, above, beyond") + "mellow" (from Middle English melowe, from Old English melu, mearu, meaning "soft, tender, ripe")
