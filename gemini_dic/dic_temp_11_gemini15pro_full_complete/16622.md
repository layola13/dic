baronetize: /ˈbærəˌnaɪz/  /ˈbærənˌaɪz/
| vt.|授予男爵爵位；使成为男爵|To elevate to the rank of baron; to make a baron.|The king decided to baronetize his loyal knight. 国王决定授予他忠诚的骑士男爵爵位。|He was baronetized for his services to the crown. 他因对王室的贡献而被封为男爵。|ennoble, elevate|degrade, demote|Verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From baron + -ize.  Baron comes from Old French *baron (modern French baron),  from Late Latin baro, probably of Frankish origin.  The suffix -ize comes from Greek -ίζειν (-izein), used to form verbs.
