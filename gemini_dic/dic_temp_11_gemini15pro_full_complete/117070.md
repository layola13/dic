me-tooisms:/ˈmiː tuːɪzəmz/
noun
我也是主义；性骚扰指控；
Instances of accusing someone of sexual harassment or assault, often in the context of a wider social movement or trend.  This term sometimes carries a negative connotation, implying that the accusations are frivolous or bandwagoning.
Many me-tooisms emerged after the initial accusations against Harvey Weinstein.  许多“我也是”的指控在最初对哈维·韦恩斯坦的指控之后出现。
近义词:sexual harassment allegations, accusations of misconduct
反义词:defense, denial
词性:n
