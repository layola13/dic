outtricked:/ˌaʊtˈtrɪkt/
verb
智胜，瞒过，比...更狡猾
defeat or deceive (someone) by being more cunning or clever.
He outtricked his opponents by feigning injury. 他假装受伤，智胜了对手。
Synonyms: outsmart, outwit, outmaneuver, outfox, dupe, deceive, trick
Antonyms: be outwitted, be outsmarted, be fooled
词性: 动词
