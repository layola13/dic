oilcan:/ˈɔɪlkæn/ | n. |油壶，油罐|A small metal container with a spout, used for holding and pouring oil. |例句：He used an oilcan to lubricate the hinges. 他用油壶给铰链上油。

例句：The oilcan leaked, leaving a greasy stain on the workbench. 油壶漏了，在工作台上留下油渍。|近义词：oil container, oiler|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
