flies:/flaɪz/
n.|苍蝇（复数）|Multiple insects of the order Diptera, known for their buzzing sound and often associated with unsanitary conditions.|The flies were buzzing around the rotten fruit.|近义词：insects|反义词:(无)|词性:名词

flies:/flaɪz/
v.|飞；飞逝；逃跑；飘扬（fly的第三人称单数形式）|Present tense third-person singular form of "fly", meaning to move through the air using wings, or to travel by aircraft.|Time flies when you're having fun.|近义词：soars, flits, speeds, escapes, streams|反义词:falls, crawls, remains, stays|词性:动词
