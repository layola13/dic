limelighting: /ˈlaɪmˌlaɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 聚光灯照明；公众关注；引人注目 | The act of focusing a spotlight on someone or something, bringing them to public attention. |  Her sudden limelighting in the media made her uncomfortable. 她突然受到媒体的关注，让她感到不舒服。 |  The charity event received a lot of limelighting.  这个慈善活动受到了很多关注。 | synonyms: spotlighting, highlighting, publicizing, featuring | antonyms: obscuring, hiding, concealing | noun
