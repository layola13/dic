heedfulness:/ˈhiːdfəlnəs/ | n. | 注意力；留心；谨慎 | The state of being attentive or mindful; paying careful attention. | She demonstrated great heedfulness in her approach to the delicate situation. 她处理微妙局势时表现出极大的谨慎。| synonyms: attentiveness, mindfulness, alertness, vigilance, caution, care | antonyms: inattentiveness, negligence, carelessness, recklessness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From heed (v.) + -ful + -ness.  "Heed" comes from Old English *hēdan "to observe, attend to," of Germanic origin. "-ful" is a suffix meaning "full of" and "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives.
