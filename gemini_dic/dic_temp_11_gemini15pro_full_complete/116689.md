maturement:/məˈtjʊrmɑ̃/ (French pronunciation)| adv.| 成熟地，审慎地|  This word is primarily used in French and translates to "maturely" or "carefully" in English. It describes actions or decisions made with thoughtful consideration and prudence.|  Il a agi maturement dans cette situation difficile. (He acted maturely in this difficult situation.) | 近义词: prudemment (carefully), sagement (wisely), raisonnablement (reasonably)| 反义词: imprudemment (recklessly),  follement (foolishly) |词性: 副词
