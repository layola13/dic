lack: /læk/ | n. v. | 缺乏，缺少；缺点，弱点 | absence or deficiency of something needed or desired; a failing or shortcoming |  The project failed due to a lack of funding.  该项目因缺乏资金而失败。| synonyms: shortage, deficiency, absence, dearth | antonyms: abundance, surplus, excess | noun

lack: /læk/ | v. |  缺乏，缺少 | be without or deficient in | They lacked the resources to complete the project. 他们缺乏完成项目的资源。 | synonyms: want, need, be short of, be deficient in | antonyms: have, possess, abound in | verb
