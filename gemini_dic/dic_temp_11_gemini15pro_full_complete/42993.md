coving:/ˈkoʊvɪŋ/ (US), /ˈkəʊvɪŋ/ (UK)| n.| 弧形裝飾, 穹窿, 凹面|A curved molding, usually concave, covering the angle formed by a wall and ceiling.|The ornate coving in the dining room added a touch of elegance. 餐厅里华丽的弧形装饰增添了一丝优雅。|Synonyms: cornice, molding, trim|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps "flat surface")|noun

coving:/ˈkoʊvɪŋ/ (US), /ˈkəʊvɪŋ/ (UK)| n.| 弧形覆盖物|Something that covers in a curved shape, like the underside of a roof overhang.|The coving of the porch protected the front door from the rain. 门廊的弧形覆盖物保护前门免受雨淋。|Synonyms: overhang, canopy, awning|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|noun
