myrrhine:/ˈmɪraɪn/
adjective
没药的; 香的
Made of or pertaining to myrrh; fragrant.
The myrrhine scent filled the air.  空气中弥漫着没药的香味。
近义词:fragrant, aromatic, perfumed
反义词:fetid, foul, putrid
词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *myrrhinus, from Latin myrrha (“myrrh”) + -inus.
