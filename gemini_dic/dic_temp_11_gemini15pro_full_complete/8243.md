anti-incumbent:/ˌænti-ɪnˈkʌmbənt/| adj.| 反现任者的，反对现任者的| Opposed to the current holder of an office or position. |  The anti-incumbent sentiment is strong this election.  这次选举中反现任者的情绪很强烈。| Example:  The challenger ran an anti-incumbent campaign, focusing on the current mayor's failures. 挑战者发起了一场反现任市长的竞选活动，重点关注现任市长的失败。|Synonyms:  anti-establishment, insurgent| Antonyms: pro-incumbent, establishment| Adjective
