greeters:/ˈɡriːtərz/| n.|迎宾员，接待员|People who greet others, often at the entrance of a place, to welcome them.|The greeters at the restaurant were very friendly. 餐厅的迎宾员非常友好。|近义词：receptionists, welcomers, hosts|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑  farewellers 送别者)|noun
