obstupefactive: /ˌɒbstjuːˈpiːfæktɪv/ /ˌɑːbstuːˈpiːfæktɪv/
adjective
使人目瞪口呆的，令人惊愕的
Causing astonishment; stupefying.
The magician's obstupefactive tricks left the audience speechless. 魔术师令人惊愕的技巧让观众哑口无言。
The news of the lottery win had an obstupefactive effect on her.  彩票中奖的消息让她目瞪口呆。

synonyms: astonishing, amazing, stunning, bewildering, astounding, shocking
antonyms:  unremarkable, mundane, ordinary, expected

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin obstupēfactīvus (“stupefying”), from obstupēfactus, perfect passive participle of obstupēfaciō (“I stupefy, amaze”), from ob- (“against, towards”) + stupēfaciō (“I stupefy”), from stupēfactus, perfect passive participle of stupēfiō (“I am stunned”), from stupēō (“I am stunned”).
