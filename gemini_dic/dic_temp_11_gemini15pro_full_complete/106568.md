kryolite:/ˈkraɪəˌlaɪt/ | n. |冰晶石|A mineral, sodium aluminum fluoride (Na3AlF6), used as a solvent in the electrolytic production of aluminum.|Aluminum is produced by electrolysis of alumina dissolved in molten cryolite.  铝是通过电解溶解在熔融冰晶石中的氧化铝生产的。|Cryolite lowers the melting point of alumina, making the process more energy-efficient. 冰晶石降低了氧化铝的熔点，使该工艺更加节能。|近义词: (in function) aluminum fluoride, sodium hexafluoroaluminate |反义词: None|词性: noun
