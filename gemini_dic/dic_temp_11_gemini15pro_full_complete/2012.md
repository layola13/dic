actualness:/ˈæktʃuəlnəs/
n.
现实性；真实性；现实
The state or quality of being actual; reality.
The actualness of the threat became clear.  这个威胁的真实性变得清晰起来。
近义词:reality, actuality, factuality
反义词:unreality, fiction, falsehood
词性:名词

Etymology: From Late Latin actualitas, from actualis (“acting, effective, real”), from actus (“act, deed”), from agere (“to do, drive, lead, act”).
