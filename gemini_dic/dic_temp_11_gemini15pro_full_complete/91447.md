hygrometric:/ˌhaɪɡrəˈmɛtrɪk/
adjective
测湿的；吸湿的
Relating to hygrometry; measuring the humidity of the air or other gases.
This hygrometric sensor measures the moisture content in the air.  这个湿度传感器测量空气中的水分含量。
The museum carefully monitors hygrometric conditions to preserve the artifacts.  博物馆仔细监测湿度条件以保存文物。
synonyms: hygroscopic, moisture-sensitive
antonyms: non-hygroscopic, waterproof
词性: adjective
