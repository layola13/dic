brusqueest:/brʌskɪst/ | adj. | 最唐突的，最粗鲁的 |  Superlative form of "brusque," meaning abruptly and rudely short in speech or manner. It describes someone who is extremely abrupt and dismissive in their interactions with others.  |  His brusqueest reply offended everyone in the room. 他最唐突的回答冒犯了房间里的每一个人。

Her brusqueest comment was, "Get out!" 她最粗鲁的一句话是：“滚出去！” | 近义词：curtest, rudest, abruptest, bluntest | 反义词：politest, kindest, most courteous, most considerate | 词性: 形容词
