gamboled:/ˈɡæmbəld/ (US), /ˈɡæmbɒld/ (UK)| v.| 嬉戏玩闹; 雀跃; |To run or jump about playfully.|The lambs gamboled in the meadow. 羊羔在草地上嬉戏。|近义词：frolicked, pranced, cavorted, romped|反义词：trudged, plodded|verb

词源:
From Old French *gamboler, frequentative of gambiller “to jump, leap,” of uncertain origin.
