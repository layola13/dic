light-up:/ˈlaɪt ʌp/ | adj.  | 点亮的；发光的；照亮的 |  Something that is light-up is decorated with small lights that can be switched on. | The children loved their light-up sneakers. 孩子们喜欢他们的发光运动鞋。|  illuminated, bright | dark, dim | adjective

light-up:/ˈlaɪt ʌp/ | v. | 点亮；照亮；（使）容光焕发 | To illuminate or brighten something, or to cause someone's face to become more animated and expressive, often due to happiness or excitement. |  Her face lit up when she saw her friend. 看到朋友时，她的脸上露出了喜悦的光芒。 | brighten, illuminate, shine | darken, dim | verb
