interweave:/ˌɪntərˈwiːv/ | v. |交织，穿插，编织|blend or cause to blend together; intermingle. |例句：The author skillfully interweaves historical facts with fictional narrative.  作者巧妙地将史实与虚构的叙事交织在一起。

例句：Threads of different colors were interwoven to create a beautiful tapestry.  不同颜色的线被交织在一起，创造出一幅美丽的挂毯。|近义词：intertwine, interlace, blend, mingle|反义词：separate, disentangle|词性:动词
