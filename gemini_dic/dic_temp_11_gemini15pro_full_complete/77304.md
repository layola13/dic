gateman:/ˈɡeɪtmən/ | n. | 门卫，看门人 | A person who attends a gate, especially one at a railroad crossing or a factory entrance. | The gateman opened the gate for the truck to enter. 门卫打开大门让卡车进入。| The gateman checked everyone's ID before allowing them to pass. 门卫检查了每个人的身份证才让他们通过。 |  Synonyms: doorkeeper, porter, guard | Antonyms:  None | noun
