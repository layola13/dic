acrobat:/ˈækrəˌbæt/ | n. |杂技演员，特技演员|A skilled performer of gymnastic feats.|例句：The acrobat performed a daring backflip.  杂技演员表演了一个大胆的后空翻。|例句：She trained as an acrobat for many years. 她训练成为一名杂技演员很多年了。|近义词：gymnast, stuntman, performer|反义词:amateur, beginner|词性:noun

acrobatic:/ˌækrəˈbætɪk/ | adj. |杂技的，特技的|Relating to or characteristic of acrobatics; involving or requiring agility and skill.|例句：The acrobatic display was breathtaking. 杂技表演令人叹为观止。|例句：He made an acrobatic leap over the fence.  他做了一个杂技般的跳跃翻过篱笆。|近义词：agile, nimble, gymnastic|反义词:clumsy, awkward|词性:adjective
