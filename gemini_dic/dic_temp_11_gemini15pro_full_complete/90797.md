humpbacked:/ˈhʌmpbækt/| adj.|驼背的；弓形的|Having a hump on the back; hunchbacked.|The humpbacked whale is known for its acrobatic breaches.|近义词：hunchbacked, kyphotic|反义词:straight-backed|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From hump + backed.  "Hump" originates from  Middle Low German or Middle Dutch *hump, possibly imitative. "Backed" comes from the Old English "-bæced," meaning "having a back of a specified kind."
