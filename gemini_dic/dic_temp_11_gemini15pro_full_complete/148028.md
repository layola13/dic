overromanticize: /ˌoʊvərroʊˈmæntɪsaɪz/ | v. | 过度浪漫化；过分理想化 | To interpret or view something in an excessively romantic or idealized way, often ignoring negative or realistic aspects. |  She tends to overromanticize her relationships, imagining them as perfect fairy tales. 她倾向于过度浪漫化她的恋爱关系，把它们想象成完美的童话故事。

|  He overromanticizes the past, remembering only the good times and forgetting the struggles. 他过度美化过去，只记得美好的时光，忘记了曾经的挣扎。 | 近义词：idealize, glamorize, sentimentalize | 反义词：be realistic, be practical | 词性：动词
