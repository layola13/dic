monostich: /ˈmɒnəˌstɪk/ | n. | 单行诗 | A poem consisting of a single line. |  Example: "The brevity of life, a fleeting sigh."  生命短暂，一声叹息。| Synonym: single line, one-liner | Antonym: couplet, quatrain | noun

Etymology: From Greek monos "single" + stichos "line of verse"
