biltong:/ˈbɪltɒŋ/ (UK), /ˈbɪltɔːŋ/ (US)| n. |牛肉干，风干肉|Air-dried cured meat, especially beef, originally from Southern Africa.|例句：He enjoyed some biltong as a snack. 他吃了一些牛肉干作为零食。

例句：Biltong is a popular snack in South Africa. 牛肉干在南非是一种很受欢迎的零食。|近义词：jerky, dried meat|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "fresh meat")|词性: noun

Etymology: From Afrikaans *biltong*, from Dutch *bil* ("buttock") + *tong* ("strip, tongue").
