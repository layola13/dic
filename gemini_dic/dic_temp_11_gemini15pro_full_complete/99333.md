intercuts:/ˌɪntərˈkʌts/ | v. | 插入镜头；穿插；交切 | To insert (shots from other scenes, flashbacks, etc.) into the narrative of a film. | The director intercuts scenes of the present with flashbacks to the protagonist's childhood. 导演将现在的场景与主角童年的闪回镜头交切。 | The film intercuts between the two main characters' stories.  这部电影在两个主角的故事之间来回切换。 | inserts, intersperses | separates | verb
intercut:/ˈɪntərˌkʌt/ | n. | 插入镜头；穿插；交切 | An insertion of shots from other scenes, flashbacks, etc., into the narrative of a film. | The intercut to the flashback provided crucial context. 插入闪回镜头的交切提供了重要的背景信息。 | The frequent intercuts added a sense of urgency to the film. 频繁的交切给影片增添了一种紧迫感。 | insertion, interpolation | omission | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- + cut.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," and "cut" refers to the act of cutting film.
