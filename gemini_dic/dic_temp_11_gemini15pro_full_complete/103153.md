ETYMOLOGY->

From the name of the island of Jersey.  The island was known for its knitting industry, hence the association with knitted fabric and garments.  The use of "jersey" to refer to a sports uniform likely stems from the early adoption of such garments by athletes.  The breed of cattle also originates from the island.
