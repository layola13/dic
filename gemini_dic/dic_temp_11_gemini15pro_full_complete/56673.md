doodlers:/ˈduːdlərz/| n. |涂鸦者，爱涂鸦的人|People who doodle, often absentmindedly.  Those who make casual drawings or sketches, especially while preoccupied with something else.|The conference was long and boring, so the doodlers in the audience had filled their notepads with abstract designs. 会议漫长而无聊，所以观众席上的涂鸦者在笔记本上画满了抽象图案。|The doodlers were lost in their own worlds, sketching on napkins and the backs of envelopes.  涂鸦者沉浸在自己的世界里，在餐巾纸和信封背面涂鸦。|近义词：scribblers, sketchers|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) non-artists, serious note-takers|词性:Noun, plural
