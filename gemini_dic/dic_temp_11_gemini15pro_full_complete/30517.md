cepaceous:/sɪˈpeɪʃəs/
adjective
似洋葱的；有葱蒜味的
Resembling an onion or garlic; having the odor or taste of onion or garlic.
The cepaceous aroma filled the kitchen as she sautéed the onions and garlic.  厨房里充满了洋葱和大蒜炒熟后的刺鼻香味。
synonyms: alliaceous, oniony, garlicky
antonyms: bland, flavorless
词性: adjective
