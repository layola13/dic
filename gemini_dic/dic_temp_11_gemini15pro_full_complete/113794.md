macruroid:/məˈkruːrɔɪd/| adj. n.|长尾的；鼠尾鱼|Relating to or resembling a macruran, especially a macrourid fish, characterized by a long, tapering tail.|例句：The macruroid fish swam gracefully through the deep sea. 这条鼠尾鱼优雅地在深海中游动。

例句：Scientists discovered a new species of macruroid in the Atlantic Ocean. 科学家在大西洋发现了一种新的鼠尾鱼。|近义词：long-tailed, rattail|反义词:short-tailed|词性:adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From  *macr- + -ur- + -oid*.  *macr-* from Greek *makrós* (long). *-ur-* from Greek *oura* (tail). *-oid*  meaning resembling or having the form of.
