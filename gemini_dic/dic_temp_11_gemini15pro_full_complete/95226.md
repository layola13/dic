inbreeding:/ˈɪnbrɪːdɪŋ/
n.|近亲繁殖；同系交配|The practice of breeding from closely related people or animals, especially over many generations.|Excessive inbreeding led to a decline in the herd's health. 过度近亲繁殖导致牛群健康状况下降。|
近义词:interbreeding, close breeding, linebreeding|
反义词:outbreeding, crossbreeding|
词性:noun


inbred:/ˈɪnbrɛd/
adj.|近亲繁殖的；天生的；根深蒂固的|Produced by inbreeding; innate or ingrained.|The royal family suffered from many inbred diseases.  王室患有许多遗传性疾病。 |Her shyness was inbred and difficult to overcome. 她的害羞是天生的，很难克服。|
近义词:congenital, inherent, innate|
反义词:acquired, learned|
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- + breed.  The word "breed" comes from Old English "brēdan," meaning "to cherish, nourish, keep warm, hatch, produce."  The prefix "in-" in this context signifies "within" or "among."
