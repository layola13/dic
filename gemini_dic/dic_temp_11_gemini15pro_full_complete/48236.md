decontaminating:/ˌdiːkənˈtæmɪneɪtɪŋ/
Verb, present participle
净化；清除污染；消毒
The act of removing contaminants from something or someone.
The workers are decontaminating the site after the chemical spill.  工人们正在清理化学品泄漏后的现场。
They were decontaminating the equipment used in the experiment. 他们在对实验中使用的设备进行消毒。
Synonyms:  sterilizing, sanitizing, purifying, cleansing
Antonyms: contaminating, polluting, infecting
Verb


decontaminate:/ˌdiːkənˈtæmɪneɪt/
Verb
净化；清除污染；消毒
To remove contaminants from something or someone.
The hospital staff decontaminated the operating room. 医院工作人员对手术室进行了消毒。
They need to decontaminate the area before anyone can enter.  他们需要先对该区域进行净化，然后才能允许任何人进入。
Synonyms: sterilize, sanitize, purify, cleanse
Antonyms: contaminate, pollute, infect
Verb


decontamination:/ˌdiːkənˌtæmɪˈneɪʃn/
Noun
净化；清除污染；消毒
The process of removing contaminants.
The decontamination of the area took several hours.  该区域的净化工作花了几个小时。
Thorough decontamination is crucial after a biohazard incident. 生物危害事件发生后，彻底清除污染至关重要。
Synonyms: sterilization, sanitization, purification, cleansing
Antonyms: contamination, pollution


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (meaning "remove" or "reverse") + contaminate (from Latin contaminare, meaning "to pollute").
