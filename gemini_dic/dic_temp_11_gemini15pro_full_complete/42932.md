covenantal:/ˌkʌvəˈnæntl/
adjective
契约的; 圣约的
Relating to or based on a covenant (a formal agreement or promise, especially one made between God and his people).
The covenantal relationship between God and Abraham is a central theme in Judaism.  上帝与亚伯拉罕之间的圣约关系是犹太教的中心主题。
The church service emphasized the covenantal nature of marriage.  教堂的仪式强调了婚姻的契约性质。
近义词:contractual, pledged, promised
反义词:non-binding, informal
词性:adjective
