例句：He was at liberty to choose his own hours. 他可以自由选择自己的工作时间。|近义词：free, unemployed, available|反义词：employed, confined, imprisoned|词性:形容词

词源：From the French phrase "à liberté," meaning "at liberty."  源自法语短语 "à liberté"，意思是“自由”。
