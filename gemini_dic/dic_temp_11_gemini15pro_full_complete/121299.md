misfits:/ˈmɪsfɪts/| n.|不适应环境的人，不称职的人，不合群的人|People who are different and don't fit in with other people.  A group of people who are not suited to a situation or who are not accepted by other people.|例句：The island became a refuge for social misfits. 这个岛成了社会边缘人的避难所。

|例句：A group of misfits formed a close-knit community. 一群不合群的人组成了一个紧密的社区。|近义词：outcasts, nonconformists, rebels, eccentrics|反义词:conformists, insiders|词性:名词
