hospitalizations:/ˌhɒspɪtəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ (US) /ˌhɒspɪtəlɪˈzeɪʃənz/ (UK)
noun
住院人数，住院治疗
The act of admitting someone to a hospital for treatment.  The number of people admitted to hospitals.
The hospitalizations due to the flu increased dramatically this winter. 今年冬天因流感住院的人数急剧增加。
synonyms: admissions, inpatient treatments
antonyms: discharges, releases
词性: 名词
