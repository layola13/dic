oversimplenesses:/ˌoʊvərˈsɪmplɪfɪkəsɪz/
|n.|过度简化；过分简单化|The act or fact of making something seem simpler than it actually is, often omitting important details or nuances.|His explanation of the complex political situation suffered from several oversimplenesses.|近义词：simplifications, distortions, misrepresentations|反义词：complexities, nuances, subtleties|词性:名词

oversimpleness:/ˌoʊvərˈsɪmplɪfɪkəsnəs/
|n.|过度简化；过分简单化|The quality or state of being oversimple.|The oversimpleness of the argument made it unconvincing.|近义词：simplicity, naiveté|反义词：complexity, sophistication|词性:名词

词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessive") + simple + -ness (suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality)
