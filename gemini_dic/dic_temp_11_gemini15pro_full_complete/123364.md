monomachist:/ˈmɒnəmækɪst/ (US) /ˈmɒnəmækɪst/ (UK)| n. |好斗者；决斗狂|A person who engages in or enjoys a duel or fight, especially to the death.|He was a known monomachist, always looking for a challenge. 他是个有名的决斗狂，总是在寻找挑战。|近义词：duelist, fighter, combatant|反义词：pacifist|词性: 名词

Etymology: From Greek "monomachia" (single combat), from "monos" (single) + "mache" (battle).
