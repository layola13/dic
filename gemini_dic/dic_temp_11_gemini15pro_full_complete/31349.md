characterizable:/ˈkærɪktəˌraɪzəbl/| adj.|可描述的，可刻画的|Able to be described or characterized; having distinguishable characteristics.|The behavior of the chemical compound is easily characterizable under high pressure.  这种化合物的行为在高压下很容易刻画。|The new species of bird is characterizable by its distinctive song.  这种新的鸟类可以用其独特的歌声来描述。|Synonyms: describable, definable, portrayable|Antonyms: indefinable, indescribable, vague|adjective
