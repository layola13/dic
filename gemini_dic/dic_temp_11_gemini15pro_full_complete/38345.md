conceiving:/kənˈsiːvɪŋ/| v. (present participle)|构想，怀孕，设想，认为|Forming an idea, becoming pregnant, imagining, believing.|She is conceiving of a new business plan. 她正在构思一个新的商业计划。|Synonyms: imagining, visualizing, formulating, ideating, gestating, expecting (pregnancy)|Antonyms: misinterpreting, misunderstanding, ignoring, neglecting|verb

Etymology->
From Old French *conceveir*, from Latin *concipere* ("to take in, conceive, become pregnant"), from *com-* ("together") + *capere* ("to take").
