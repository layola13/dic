overcontentedness's:/ˌoʊvərˌkənˈtɛntɪdnɪsɪz/
noun
过度满足的状态
The state of being excessively content or satisfied, often to the point of complacency.
His overcontentedness's led him to ignore the warning signs. 他的过度满足使他忽略了警告信号。
overcontentedness's is a rare word, and often "complacency" or "self-satisfaction" are used instead.  "Overcontentedness" itself is already infrequent.
Near Synonyms: complacency, self-satisfaction, smugness
Antonyms: dissatisfaction, discontent, restlessness
