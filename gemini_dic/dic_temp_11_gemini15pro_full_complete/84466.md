haloscope:/ˈheɪləskoʊp/| n.|晕轮镜，晕轮望远镜|A device used to search for hypothetical particles called axions, which are candidates for dark matter. It utilizes a strong magnetic field to convert axions into detectable photons.|Scientists are using a haloscope to search for dark matter. 科学家们正在使用晕轮望远镜来寻找暗物质。|近义词:axion detector|反义词: N/A|noun
