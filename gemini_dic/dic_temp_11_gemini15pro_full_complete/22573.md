boothose: /ˈbuːθoʊz/ (US), /ˈbuːθəʊz/ (UK)| n.| 靴裤；长筒袜；高筒袜|Long socks or stockings that reach to the thighs or higher, often worn with boots.|She wore a pair of brown boothose under her riding boots. 她穿着骑马靴，里面穿着一双棕色的靴裤。|Synonyms: thigh-highs, over-the-knee socks|Antonyms: ankle socks, low-cut socks|noun
