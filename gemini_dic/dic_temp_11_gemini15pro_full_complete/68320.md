fall: /fɔːl/ (British) /fɑːl/ (American)| n. vi. vt. adj.|落下，跌倒；秋季；下降；瀑布|A descent by force of gravity, as of water.  A dropping or tumbling from an erect posture. Autumn. A decrease in size, number, rate, or level.|

例句：
The leaves fall from the trees in autumn.
秋天的树叶从树上落下。

The child fell and scraped his knee.
孩子摔倒了，擦破了膝盖。


近义词：drop, descend, decline, autumn, tumble|
反义词:rise, ascend, increase, spring, summer|
词性: noun, verb, adjective
