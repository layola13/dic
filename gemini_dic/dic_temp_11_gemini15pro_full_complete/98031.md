inorganic:/ˌɪnɔːrˈɡænɪk/ | adj. |无机的，非生物的|Not consisting of or deriving from living matter.|例句：Rocks and minerals are inorganic substances. 岩石和矿物质是无机物。|例句：Inorganic chemistry is the study of the properties and reactions of inorganic compounds. 无机化学是研究无机化合物的性质和反应的学科。|近义词：non-organic, non-living, abiotic|反义词：organic, living, biotic|词性:形容词

词源:
Late 16th century: from in- ‘not’ + organic + -al.
