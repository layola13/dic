coercive:/koʊˈɜːrsɪv/| adj.|强制的，胁迫的|Relating to or using force or threats.|The government used coercive measures to quell the protests. 政府采取强制措施平息抗议。|近义词：compulsory, mandatory, forced, authoritarian|反义词：persuasive, voluntary, optional|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *coercitivus*, from Latin coercere "to restrain, confine," from co- "together" + arcere "to enclose, confine."
