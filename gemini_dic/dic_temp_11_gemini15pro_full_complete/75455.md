fruitlike:/ˈfruːtlaɪk/
adjective
像水果的；果味的
Resembling or characteristic of fruit, especially in taste or smell.
The candy had a fruitlike flavor.  这种糖果有水果的味道。
The aroma was distinctly fruitlike.  这香味明显像水果。
近义词:fruity, fruit-flavored
反义词:bland, savory
词性:adjective
