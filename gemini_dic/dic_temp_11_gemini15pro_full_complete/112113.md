longshoreman: /ˈlɔːŋˈʃɔːrmən/ (US), /ˈlɒŋˈʃɔːmən/ (UK)| n. | 码头工人，港口装卸工人 | A worker who loads and unloads ships in a port. |  The longshoremen were busy unloading cargo from the ship. 码头工人正忙着从船上卸货。|  The longshoremen went on strike for better wages. 码头工人举行罢工要求提高工资。| 近义词: docker, stevedore | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

Etymology:
From "long shore" + "man".  "Long shore" referred to the waterfront or docks.
