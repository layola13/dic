overdraft: /ˈəʊvədrɑːft/ (UK) /ˈoʊvərdræft/ (US)| n. |透支；透支额度| An amount of money that a customer with a bank account is temporarily allowed to owe to the bank, or the agreement that allows this.|  I had to pay charges on my overdraft. 我不得不支付透支费用。

My account is $500 in overdraft. 我的账户透支了500美元。

|近义词：deficit, shortfall|反义词:credit, surplus|词性:名词

词源:  From *over- + *draft.  "over-" 表示超过，"draft" 原意为草稿、汇票，引申为提取。  因此，"overdraft" 指的是提取超过账户余额的款项。
