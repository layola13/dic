ciliate:/ˈsɪlieɪt/ (US), /ˈsɪlɪeɪt/ (UK)| n. adj.|纤毛虫，有纤毛的|A single-celled animal that has tiny hairs called cilia, used for movement and feeding.  Covered in cilia.|例句：Paramecium is a common ciliate found in freshwater. 草履虫是一种常见的淡水纤毛虫。|近义词：ciliophoran|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially flagellate for contrasting movement mechanism)|n.
ciliate:/ˈsɪlieɪt/ (US), /ˈsɪlɪeɪt/ (UK)| adj.|有纤毛的|Covered in cilia.|例句：The ciliate epithelium lines the respiratory tract. 纤毛上皮排列在呼吸道。|近义词：ciliated|反义词: (No direct antonym)|adj.
