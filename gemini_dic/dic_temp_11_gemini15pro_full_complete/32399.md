chillily:/ˈtʃɪlɪli/ | adv. | 冷淡地，冷漠地 | In a chilly manner; coldly. | She looked at him chillily and walked away. 她冷冷地看了他一眼，然后走开了。| He responded chillily to her greeting. 他对她的问候回应很冷淡。 | coldly, icily, frostily | warmly, kindly | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From chilly + -ly.  Chilly itself comes from chill, from Old English *ciele (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *kaliz, from Proto-Indo-European *gel- (cold).  The -ly suffix is a common adverbial ending, deriving from Old English -līce.
