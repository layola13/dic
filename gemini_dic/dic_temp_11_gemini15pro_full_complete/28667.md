carinated:/ˈkærɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
龙骨状的；有脊的；具棱的
Having a keel-like ridge or projection; keeled.
The carinated hull of the ship allowed it to slice through the waves.  这艘船的龙骨状船体使其能够劈波斩浪。
The bird has a distinctly carinated sternum.  这只鸟的胸骨明显呈龙骨状。
ridged, keeled, angular
smooth, rounded
adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *carinatus*, past participle of *carinare* ("to keel, to provide with a keel"), from *carina* ("keel").
