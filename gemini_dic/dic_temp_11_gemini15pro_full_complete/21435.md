blowfly:/ˈbloʊflaɪ/| n.|绿头苍蝇，丽蝇|A fly, typically metallic blue or green, that lays its eggs in decaying meat or flesh.|The butcher shop had to deal with a blowfly infestation. 肉店不得不处理绿头苍蝇的侵扰。|近义词：bluebottle, carrion fly|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From blow (referring to the eggs being "blown" into the flesh) + fly.
