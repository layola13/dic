homoptera:/hɒˈmɒptərə/
n.
同翅目昆虫
An order of insects (now generally considered a suborder of Hemiptera) that includes aphids, cicadas, and scale insects, characterized by piercing and sucking mouthparts.
Homoptera such as aphids can be serious agricultural pests. 同翅目昆虫例如蚜虫会成为严重的农业害虫。
近义词:Hemiptera (半翅目)
反义词:None (没有明确的反义词，因为它是生物分类)
词性:noun


Etymology:
From Ancient Greek ὁμόπτερος (homópteros, “having similar wings”), from ὁμός (homós, “same”) + πτερόν (pterón, “wing”).
