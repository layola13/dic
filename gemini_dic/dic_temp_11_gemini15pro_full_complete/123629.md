monticulous:/mɒnˈtɪkjʊləs/ /mɑːnˈtɪkjʊləs/| adj.|一丝不苟的，过分注意细节的|Excessively concerned with insignificant details; scrupulous about trifles.|She is so monticulous about her garden that she spends hours weeding every single day. 她对她的花园一丝不苟，每天花几个小时除草。|
He's monticulous about his appearance, always perfectly groomed. 他对自己的仪表一丝不苟，总是打扮得无可挑剔。|近义词：meticulous, scrupulous, fastidious, punctilious|反义词：careless, sloppy, negligent|词性：adjective|词源：From Latin *monticulus* ("small hill, mound"), diminutive of *mons* ("mountain").  The semantic shift likely arose from the image of carefully examining every small mound or bump.|
