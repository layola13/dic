Overnet 曾一度流行用于分享大型文件。|Example:  Due to its decentralized nature, Overnet was difficult to shut down. 由于其去中心化的特性，Overnet 很难被关闭。|近义词: P2P network, file-sharing network|反义词: centralized network|词性:noun
