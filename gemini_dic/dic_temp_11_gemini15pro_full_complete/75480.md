frustrating:/ˈfrʌstreɪtɪŋ/ /frʌˈstreɪtɪŋ/
adj.
令人沮丧的，令人懊恼的
Causing feelings of frustration; discouraging.
It's frustrating to have to wait so long.  等待这么长时间真是令人沮丧。
近义词：annoying, exasperating, irritating
反义词：satisfying, gratifying, pleasing
词性: 形容词

词源:
From the verb "frustrate," which comes from the Latin "frustrāre" (to deceive, disappoint, make vain).
