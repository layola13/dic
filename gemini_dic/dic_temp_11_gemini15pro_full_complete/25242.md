buffability:/ˌbʌfəˈbɪləti/
n.
可增益性，可强化性
The ability of a character or item in a game to be improved or enhanced through buffs.
This new character has incredible buffability, making her a top choice for endgame content.  这个新角色拥有令人难以置信的可增益性，使她成为最终游戏内容的首选。
The item's buffability is limited, so choose your upgrades wisely.  该物品的可增益性有限，因此请明智地选择升级。
