divi:/ˈdiːvaɪ/| n. |股息；红利； 被除数|A sum of money paid regularly (typically annually) by a company to its shareholders out of its profits (or reserves).  The number being divided in a division problem. |例句：The company declared a divi of $2 per share. 公司宣布每股派息2美元。

例句：In the equation 10 / 2 = 5, 10 is the divi. 在等式 10 / 2 = 5 中，10是被除数。|近义词：dividend, payout (for financial meaning), numerator (for mathematical meaning)|反义词: divisor (for mathematical meaning)|词性:noun
