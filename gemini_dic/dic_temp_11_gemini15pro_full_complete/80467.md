goniometer:/ˌɡoʊniˈɒmɪtər/| n. |测角器|An instrument for measuring angles, especially one used to measure the angles of crystals or the range of motion of a joint.|The physical therapist used a goniometer to measure the patient's knee flexion.|Synonym: protractor|Antonym: N/A|noun
