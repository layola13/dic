neocolonialism:/ˌnɪoʊkəˈloʊniəlɪzəm/| n. |新殖民主义|The use of economic, political, cultural, or other pressures to control or influence other countries, especially former dependencies.|例句：Many developing nations accuse multinational corporations of neocolonialism. 许多发展中国家指责跨国公司进行新殖民主义。

例句：The country's economic policies were seen as a form of neocolonialism.  这个国家的经济政策被视为一种新殖民主义的形式。|近义词：imperialism, hegemony|反义词: independence, self-determination|词性:noun
