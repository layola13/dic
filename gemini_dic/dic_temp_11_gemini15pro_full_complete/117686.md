meitnerium:/ˌmaɪtˈnɪəriəm/ | n. |钅麦|A synthetic radioactive element with atomic number 109. It is named after Lise Meitner, an Austrian physicist. | Meitnerium is a highly unstable element and decays rapidly. 钅麦是一种高度不稳定的元素，会迅速衰变。| Meitnerium has no stable isotopes. 钅麦没有稳定的同位素。| Synonyms: element 109 (Mt) | Antonyms: None | noun
