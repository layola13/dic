hanyang: /ˈhɑːn.jɑːŋ/ | n. | 汉阳 | Hanyang is a district of Wuhan, a major city in central China. It is situated on the west bank of the Han River, opposite Wuchang and Hankou. | Hanyang is known for its historical significance. 汉阳以其历史意义而闻名。| Hanyang played a crucial role in the 1911 Revolution. 汉阳在1911年辛亥革命中扮演了关键角色。| synonym: Hankou, Wuchang (as parts of Wuhan) | antonym: N/A | noun
