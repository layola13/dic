hanseatic:/ˌhænsiˈætɪk/ | adj. | 汉萨同盟的；汉萨城市的 | relating to the Hanseatic League, a medieval trade association of North German and Baltic cities. |  The Hanseatic League played a crucial role in the economic development of Northern Europe. 汉萨同盟在北欧的经济发展中扮演了至关重要的角色。 |  Lübeck was a powerful Hanseatic city. 吕贝克是一个强大的汉萨城市。 |近义词: league, confederation, alliance | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Middle Low German *hanse, meaning "group, association," and -atic, a suffix forming adjectives.
