diecious:/daɪˈiːʃəs/
adjective
雌雄异株的
Having male and female reproductive organs in separate plants or animals.
Holly trees are dioecious, meaning that a male tree must be planted near a female tree for the female tree to produce berries. 冬青树是雌雄异株的，这意味着必须在雌树附近种植雄树才能使雌树结出浆果。
Most species of willow are dioecious. 大多数柳树种类是雌雄异株的。
dioecious, unisexual
monoecious, hermaphroditic
