boycott: /ˈbɔɪkɒt/ (UK) /ˈbɔɪkɑːt/ (US)
| n. v. |抵制，联合抵制|An act of voluntarily abstaining from using, buying, or dealing with a person, organization, or country as an expression of protest, usually for political or social reasons. |Many consumers are boycotting products tested on animals. 许多消费者抵制用动物做实验的产品。|
| | |The union called on its members to boycott the company's products. 工会号召其成员抵制该公司的产品。|
| |近义词：ban, embargo, prohibit, ostracize|反义词：patronize, support, endorse|词性: 名词, 动词
