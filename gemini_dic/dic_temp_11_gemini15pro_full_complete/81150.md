grand's:/ɡrænz/
n.
(grandmother's) 祖母的；(grandfather's) 祖父的；外祖母的；外祖父的
Belonging to or associated with one's grandparent.
That's my grand's house. 那是我的祖母/祖父的房子。
近义词:grandmother's, grandfather's
反义词: (No direct antonym for a possessive)
词性:名词所有格
