doveling:/ˈdʌvlɪŋ/| n.|雏鸽，幼鸽；鸽子般温柔的人|A young dove; a person regarded as gentle and innocent like a dove. |例句：The doveling huddled in the nest. 雏鸽蜷缩在巢里。
