aquatic:/əˈkwætɪk/ | adj. |水生的，水栖的，水中的|Living or growing in, happening in, or connected with water.|例句：Aquatic plants thrive in this pond. 这些水生植物在这个池塘里生长茂盛。|例句：Aquatic sports are popular in the summer. 水上运动在夏天很受欢迎。|近义词：marine, water, oceanic|反义词：terrestrial|词性:形容词
aquatic:/əˈkwætɪk/ | n. |水生动物，水生植物|An animal or plant that lives in or grows in water.|例句：Many aquatics are sensitive to pollution. 许多水生动植物对污染很敏感。|例句：The aquarium is home to a variety of colorful aquatics.  这个水族馆里有各种各样色彩鲜艳的水生动植物。|近义词：water creature, water plant|反义词：land animal, land plant|词性:名词
