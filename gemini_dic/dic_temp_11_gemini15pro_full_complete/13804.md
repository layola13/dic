audiovisual:/ˌɔːdiəʊˈvɪʒuəl/ | adj. |视听的| Relating to both hearing and sight. | The conference featured several audiovisual presentations.  这次会议以几个视听演示为特色。| synonyms: multimedia, audio-visual | antonyms: none | adjective
