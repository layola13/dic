engloom:/ɪnˈɡluːm/
verb
使忧郁，使沮丧
to fill with gloom; to cast a shadow over; to darken
The news of her death engloomed the entire family. 她去世的消息使全家人陷入了悲痛。
近义词:depress, sadden, deject, overshadow
反义词:brighten, cheer, encourage
词性:v.

engloom:/ɪnˈɡluːm/
noun
忧郁，沮丧 (rarely used)
a state of gloom or depression
An air of engloom settled over the town. 一种忧郁的气氛笼罩着整个城镇。
近义词:gloom, depression, melancholy
反义词:joy, happiness, cheerfulness
词性:n.
