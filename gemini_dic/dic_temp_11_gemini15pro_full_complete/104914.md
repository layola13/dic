keirin:/ˈkeɪrɪn/| n.|競輪賽, 场地自行车赛|A form of cycle racing originating in Japan, in which track cyclists sprint for the finish after a motorized pace setter gradually increases speed and then pulls off the track.|The keirin is an exciting and strategic track cycling event.|Synonym: track cycling, cycle racing|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps road cycling as a contrasting sport)|noun
