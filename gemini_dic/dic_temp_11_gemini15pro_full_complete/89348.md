homologon:/həˈmɒləɡɒn/ /hoʊˈmɑːləɡɑːn/
n.
同源物，对应物
Something that corresponds to something else in a different context, especially a similar organ, part, or gene in a different species.
The human arm and a bat's wing are homologons. 人的手臂和蝙蝠的翅膀是同源物。
analogue, counterpart, equivalent
antonym: (No direct antonym, but could use "non-homologous" in a scientific context)
noun
