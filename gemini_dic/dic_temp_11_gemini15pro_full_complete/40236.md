contiguate:/kənˈtɪɡjueɪt/
| v. adj.|使连接，邻接的，相邻的|To make things contiguous; to bring into contact; to join together, especially side by side. |The two properties are contiguate, sharing a common boundary. 这两处房产相邻，共享一条边界。|Near, next to, side by side|Separate, apart|verb, adjective
contiguity:/ˌkɒntɪˈɡjuːɪti/
| n.|邻接，接触|The state of being contiguous; touching or bordering upon. |The contiguity of the two countries has led to much cultural exchange.  这两个国家的毗邻导致了许多文化交流。|Nearness, proximity, adjacency|Distance, separation|noun
contiguous:/kənˈtɪɡjuəs/
| adj.|邻接的，接触的|Sharing a common border; touching. |The United States and Canada are contiguous countries. 美国和加拿大是相邻的国家。|Adjacent, adjoining, abutting|Discontiguous, separate, distant|adjective
