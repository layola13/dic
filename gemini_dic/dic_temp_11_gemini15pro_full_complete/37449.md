commiserator:/kəˈmɪzəreɪtər/| n. |同情者，慰问者|Someone who expresses sympathy or sorrow to another person. |She found a commiserator in her best friend after losing her job.  她在失去工作后，从她最好的朋友那里找到了慰问。|Synonyms: sympathizer, condoler|Antonyms: antagonist, critic|noun

Etymology->
From Latin *commiserari* "to pity," from *com-* "with" + *miserari* "to pity," from *miser* "wretched."
