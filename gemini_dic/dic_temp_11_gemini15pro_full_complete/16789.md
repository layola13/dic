bascara: /bɑːˈskɑːrə/
n.
Bhaskara, Bhaskaracharya (人名)
Bhaskara or Bhaskaracharya (c. 1114–1185) was an Indian mathematician and astronomer.
He wrote Siddhanta Shiromani in 1150 which consists of Lilavati (arithmetic), Bijaganita (algebra), Grahaganita (mathematics of planets), and Goladhyaya (spherics).
例句：Bhaskara made significant contributions to algebra and trigonometry. 巴斯卡拉对代数和三角学做出了重大贡献。

例句：Bhaskara's work on indeterminate equations was groundbreaking. 巴斯卡拉关于不定方程的工作是开创性的。

近义词: mathematician, astronomer

词性: 名词
