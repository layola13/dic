interiority:/ɪnˌtɪəriˈɔːrəti/ | n. | 内在性；内心世界；主观性 | The quality of having an inner life; subjective experience. | Her novel explores the interiority of its characters with remarkable depth. 她的小说以非凡的深度探索了人物的内心世界。 |  Example: The artist's work is known for its intense interiority, revealing the hidden emotions and thoughts of her subjects. 这位艺术家的作品以其强烈的内在性而闻名，揭示了她作品中人物隐藏的情感和思想。 | synonyms: subjectivity, inwardness, inner life, introspection | antonyms: exteriority, outwardness | noun
