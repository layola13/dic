believed:/bɪˈliːvd/
verb
相信的，认为的
past tense and past participle of believe  是believe的过去式和过去分词
It was believed that the earth was flat. 人们曾经认为地球是平的。
近义词:accepted, trusted, thought
反义词:doubted, disbelieved
词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English bileven, from Old English belēfan (“believe, trust, have faith in”), from be- (“by, about, with”) + lēof (“dear, beloved, agreeable”).  The original sense was "to hold dear, value."
