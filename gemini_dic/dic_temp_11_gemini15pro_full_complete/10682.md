approximation:/əˌprɒksɪˈmeɪʃn/ (US) /əˌprɒksɪˈmeɪʃən/ (UK)| n.|近似值，近似， приближение|A value or quantity that is nearly but not exactly correct.  A result that is not exact but close enough to be useful.|例句：The calculation is only an approximation. 这个计算只是一个近似值。|近义词：estimate, guess, rough calculation|反义词：precise value, exact measurement|词性:名词

approximate:/əˈprɒksɪmeɪt/ (US) /əˈprɒksɪmət/ (UK)| adj. v.|近似的， приближенный;  接近，приближаться |Close to the actual value but not completely accurate; To come close to a particular number or standard.|例句：The approximate cost is $100.  大概的费用是100美元。|近义词：near, close, similar|反义词：exact, precise|词性:形容词, 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin approximatus, past participle of approximare "to approach," from Latin ad- "to" + proximare "to come near," from proximus "nearest."
