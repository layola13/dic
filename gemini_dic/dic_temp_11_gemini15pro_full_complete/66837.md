expatiators:/ɪkˈspeɪʃieɪtərz/
|n.|详述者，详细解释者|People who expatiate on a subject explain it at length or in detail.|The expatiators on the new law spent hours clarifying its intricacies.|近义词：elaborators, explainers, commentators|反义词：summarizers, abridgers|noun
