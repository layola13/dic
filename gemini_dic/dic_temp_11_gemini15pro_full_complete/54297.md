disgustingly:/dɪsˈɡʌstɪŋli/
adverb
令人作呕地，极其讨厌地
in a way that causes disgust.
The bathroom was disgustingly dirty. 浴室脏得令人作呕。
近义词:revoltingly, nauseatingly, sickeningly, repellently
反义词:pleasingly, delightfully, attractively
词性:副词

disgust:/dɪsˈɡʌst/
noun
厌恶，反感
a feeling of strong disapproval and dislike.
She expressed her disgust at the violence. 她对暴力表示厌恶。
近义词:revulsion, aversion, repugnance, nausea
反义词:admiration, liking, pleasure
词性:名词

disgust:/dɪsˈɡʌst/
verb
使厌恶，使反感
cause (someone) to feel revulsion or strong disapproval.
The smell of the rotten meat disgusted her. 腐肉的气味让她感到恶心。
近义词:sicken, repel, revolt, nauseate
反义词:please, delight, attract
词性:动词


词源:
From Middle French *desgouster, from des- (expressing reversal) + gouster (to taste).  Ultimately from Latin gustus (“taste”).
