acidulous:/əˈsɪdʒʊləs/| adj.|酸的，酸味的；尖刻的，刻薄的|Slightly sour or sharp in taste or manner.  Characterized by a subtly harsh or critical tone.|The acidulous wine paired well with the rich dessert. 这款微酸的葡萄酒与浓郁的甜点很相配。  The critic's acidulous remarks offended the playwright.  评论家尖刻的言辞冒犯了剧作家。|近义词：tart, sour, sharp, biting, acrid, mordant|反义词：sweet, bland, mild, amiable|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *acidulus* (slightly sour), diminutive of *acidus* (sour, acid), related to *acere* (to be sour).
