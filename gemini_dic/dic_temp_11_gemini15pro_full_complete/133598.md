nonflirtatiousnesses:/ˌnɒnflɜːrˈteɪʃəsnɪsɪz/
n.
缺乏轻浮；不调情
The quality or state of being nonflirtatious.  A general lack of flirtatious behavior.
Her nonflirtatiousnesses made her seem aloof. 她不爱调情，让人觉得她很冷漠。
近义词: seriousness, reserve, aloofness
反义词: flirtatiousness, coquetry
词性: noun


Etymology:
non- (prefix) + flirtatious + -ness (suffix) + -es (plural suffix)

"non-"  Latin origin, meaning "not."  Indicates negation or absence.
"flirtatious"  From the verb "flirt," likely of imitative origin, suggesting a quick, light movement.
"-ness"  Old English suffix forming nouns from adjectives, denoting a state or quality.
"-es"  Plural suffix.
