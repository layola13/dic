merozoite:/ˌmɛrəˈzoʊaɪt/| n. |裂殖子， мерозоит (俄语)|A stage in the life cycle of the Plasmodium parasite, responsible for malaria. It is formed within the liver cells or red blood cells of the host and is released to infect other red blood cells.|The merozoites are released into the bloodstream and infect new red blood cells. 裂殖子被释放到血液中并感染新的红细胞。|The symptoms of malaria appear when the merozoites burst out of the red blood cells. 当裂殖子从红细胞中爆发出来时，疟疾的症状就会出现。| 近义词:schizont (裂殖体, technically the stage preceding merozoite release),  |反义词: None (No direct antonym for a biological stage)|词性:noun
