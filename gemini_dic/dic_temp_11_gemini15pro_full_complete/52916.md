dimple:/ˈdɪmpl/| n.|酒窝；小凹陷|A small natural indentation in the flesh, especially in the cheek or chin.|She has a dimple in her left cheek. 她左脸颊上有一个酒窝。|Synonyms: indentation, hollow, depression|Antonyms: bulge, bump, protrusion|noun


dimpled:/ˈdɪmpld/| adj.|有酒窝的；有凹陷的|Having dimples.|Her dimpled smile charmed everyone. 她带酒窝的笑容迷住了所有人。|Synonyms: indented, hollowed|Antonyms: smooth, flat|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English dimpel, from Old English dympel (“dent, impression”), from Proto-Germanic *dumpilō (“pit, hole”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰumb- (“deep, hollow”).
