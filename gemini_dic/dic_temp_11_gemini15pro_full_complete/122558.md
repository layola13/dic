modem:/ˈmoʊdəm/ (US) /ˈməʊdɛm/ (UK)| n. |调制解调器|A combined modulator and demodulator that converts digital data into analog signals for transmission over a telephone line, and back again for reception.|I need a new modem for faster internet access. 我需要一个新的调制解调器来提高网速。|Synonyms: modulator-demodulator|Antonyms: None|noun
