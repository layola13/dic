bellwether:/ˈbelˌweðər/ (US), /ˈbelˌweðə/ (UK)| n.|领头羊；风向标；前导|A bellwether is a leading indicator of future trends.  A bellwether is a sheep or goat that leads the flock, often wearing a bell around its neck.|The company's performance is considered a bellwether for the entire industry. 这家公司的业绩被视为整个行业的晴雨表。

The rise in housing prices is a bellwether of economic recovery.  房价上涨是经济复苏的先兆。|Synonyms: indicator, barometer, guide, leader, pioneer|Antonyms: follower, laggard|Noun
