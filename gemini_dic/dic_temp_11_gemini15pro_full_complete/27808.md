cannonier:/ˌkænəˈnɪər/| n.|炮手，炮兵|A person who fires or manages a cannon.  A soldier in the artillery.|例句：The cannonier aimed carefully before firing.  炮手在开火前仔细瞄准。

例句：The experienced cannonier knew how to adjust the cannon for different ranges.  经验丰富的炮手知道如何调整大炮以适应不同的射程。|近义词：artilleryman, gunner|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps civilian)|词性:noun
