guarantor:/ˌɡærənˈtɔːr/ | n. |担保人；保证人|A person, organization, or thing that guarantees something.|My father was guarantor for my student loan. 我父亲是我的学生贷款的担保人。|Synonyms: surety, sponsor, backer|Antonyms:  debtor,  borrower|noun
