crank-in:/ˈkræŋkɪn/
noun
开机；开拍
The commencement of filming or video production.
The movie had its crank-in last week in Hollywood. 这部电影上周在好莱坞开拍了。
近义词: commencement of filming, start of production
反义词: wrap, wrap-up
