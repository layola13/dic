jawlike:/ˈdʒɔːlaɪk/
adjective
像颚的；钳状的
Resembling a jaw or jaws in form or action.
The Venus flytrap closed its jawlike leaves around the insect.  捕蝇草用它像颚一样的叶子把昆虫包了起来。
The machine clamped down with a jawlike grip.  机器以钳状的抓力向下夹紧。
Near Antonyms: smooth, rounded
Synonyms:  jaw-shaped, pincer-like
词性: adjective
