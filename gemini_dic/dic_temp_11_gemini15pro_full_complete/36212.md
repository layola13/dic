cogitatingly:/ˈkɒdʒɪteɪtɪŋli/ /ˈkɑːdʒɪteɪtɪŋli/
adverb
思考地，沉思地
in a manner characterized by deep, thoughtful consideration.
She stared cogitatingly out the window, pondering the implications of his words. 她若有所思地凝视着窗外，思考着他话中的含义。

He tapped his pen cogitatingly against the desk. 他若有所思地用笔轻敲着桌子。

近义词: thoughtfully, pensively, meditatively, reflectively
反义词: impulsively, rashly, thoughtlessly

词性: adverb

词源: From the Latin verb "cogitare," meaning "to think," combined with the suffix "-ingly" to form an adverb.
