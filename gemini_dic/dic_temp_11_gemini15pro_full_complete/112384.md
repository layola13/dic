loss-leader:/ˈlɒs ˌliːdə(r)/ /ˈlɔːs ˌlidər/ | n. | 亏损领导者，特价商品 | A product sold at a loss to attract customers. | Supermarkets often use loss-leaders, like cheap milk or bread, to lure shoppers into their stores. 超市经常使用特价商品，例如便宜的牛奶或面包，来吸引顾客进店。

The store advertised a loss-leader of a popular video game to draw in holiday shoppers.  这家商店宣传了一款热门电子游戏的特价商品，以吸引假日购物者。


| Synonym:  bargain, special offer, doorbuster, promotional item | Antonym:  premium product, luxury item | Noun |
