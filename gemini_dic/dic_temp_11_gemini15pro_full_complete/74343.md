fragmented:/ˈfræɡˌmɛntɪd/ | adj. |碎片化的，片段的|Broken into small parts or pieces; disconnected and lacking unity.|The once unified empire became fragmented after the war. 曾经统一的帝国在战后变得四分五裂。|The report was fragmented and difficult to understand. 这份报告支离破碎，难以理解。|近义词：disjointed, fractured, broken, divided, scattered, piecemeal|反义词：unified, whole, integrated, coherent|词性:形容词

fragmented:/ˈfræɡˌmɛntɪd/ | v. |分裂，破碎|To break or cause to break into fragments.|The explosion fragmented the rock into small pieces. 爆炸使岩石碎裂成小块。|The political party fragmented into several smaller factions. 该政党分裂成几个小派别。|近义词：shatter, splinter, disintegrate, break apart|反义词：unite, combine, integrate, consolidate|词性:动词
