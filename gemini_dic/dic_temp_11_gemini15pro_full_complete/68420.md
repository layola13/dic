falsifications:/ˌfɔːlsɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/
|n.|伪造；篡改；歪曲|The act of making something false; something that has been falsified.  The report was full of falsifications and distortions.|例句：The falsifications of the historical record were exposed by the new evidence. 新证据揭露了对历史记录的伪造。

例句：Several falsifications were found in his academic credentials. 他的学历证书中发现了几处伪造。

|近义词：forgeries, counterfeits, distortions, misrepresentations, fabrications|反义词：verifications, confirmations, authentications|词性:noun
