gnome:/noʊm/| n.|侏儒，地精|A mythical creature of folklore, typically depicted as a small, bearded humanoid who lives underground and is associated with earth and mining.|例句：The garden gnome stood guard by the tulips. 花园侏儒在郁金香旁站岗。|近义词：dwarf, goblin|反义词:giant|noun
