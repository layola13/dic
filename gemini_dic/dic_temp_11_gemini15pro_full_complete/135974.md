nonradiance:/nɑːnˈreɪdiəns/
n.|无辐射，不发光|The state or quality of not emitting radiation, especially light or heat.|The stealth technology relies on the nonradiance of the aircraft to avoid detection.  隐形技术依靠飞机的无辐射来避免被探测。|Near synonyms: non-emission, darkness, opacity (in some contexts)|Antonyms: radiance, luminescence, emission|Noun
