magnus:/ˈmæɡnəs/| adj. n.|伟大的，大的；大人物，大师|From Latin magnus "great, large," of uncertain origin.  Used as a given name or epithet signifying greatness.  As an adjective, it describes something of considerable size, importance, or distinction. As a noun, it refers to a person of great importance or skill. |例句：Magnus Carlsen is a chess magnus. 马格努斯·卡尔森是一位国际象棋大师。
