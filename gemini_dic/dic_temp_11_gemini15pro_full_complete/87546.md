heterology:/ˌhɛtərˈɒlədʒi/ | n. |异种性；异源性；异质性|Difference or dissimilarity of structure or origin.|例句：The heterology of the transplanted tissue caused rejection.  移植组织的异源性导致排斥反应。

例句：The study focused on the heterology between the two species.  这项研究集中于两个物种之间的异质性。|近义词：dissimilarity, difference, divergence, variation, heterogeneity|反义词：homology, similarity, resemblance, likeness|词性：名词

Etymology: From Ancient Greek ἕτερος (héteros, “other, different”) + -logy.
