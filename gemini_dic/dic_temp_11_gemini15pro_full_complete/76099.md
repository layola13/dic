furrow:/ˈfɜːroʊ/ (US) /ˈfʌroʊ/ (UK)| n. v.|犁沟，皱纹|A long, narrow trench made in the ground by a plow, especially for planting seeds; a deep wrinkle in the skin, especially on the forehead.|The farmer plowed furrows in the field. 农民在地里犁沟。/  His brow was furrowed with worry. 他眉头紧锁，满是担忧。|近义词：groove, wrinkle, trench, channel|反义词:ridge, smooth|词性:名词,动词

furrow:/ˈfɜːroʊ/ (US) /ˈfʌroʊ/ (UK)| v.|犁地，使起皱纹|To make a furrow in; to wrinkle.|He furrowed his brow in concentration. 他皱着眉头，聚精会神。/ The ship furrowed the waves. 船犁开波浪。|近义词：crease, wrinkle, corrugate|反义词:smooth|词性:动词
