befriend:/bɪˈfrɛnd/
verb
与…交朋友；友好对待
To act as a friend to (someone), especially when they are in need of help or support.
He befriended me when I first arrived in the city and didn't know anyone. 他在我刚到这个城市，谁也不认识的时候，主动和我交了朋友。

She befriended the lonely old woman next door. 她和隔壁孤独的老太太交了朋友。

synonym:pal up with, make friends with, fraternize with
antonym:alienate, shun, ostracize
词性:v.
