attend:/əˈtɛnd/ | v. | 参加，出席；照顾，护理；伴随 | to be present at (an event, meeting, etc.); to take care of; to accompany |  I will attend the meeting tomorrow. 我明天会参加会议。| synonyms: participate in, be present at, go to, take care of, look after | antonyms: miss, skip, neglect, ignore | verb

attend:/əˈtɛnd/ | v. | 注意，倾听 | to pay attention to | Please attend to what I'm saying. 请注意我说的话。 | synonyms: listen to, heed, pay attention to | antonyms: ignore, disregard, neglect | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *atendre, from Latin attendere ("to stretch toward, direct one's attention to, apply oneself to"), from ad- ("to, toward") + tendere ("to stretch").
