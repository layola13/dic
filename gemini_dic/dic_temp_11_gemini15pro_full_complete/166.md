abater:/əˈbeɪtər/| n.| 减轻者，缓和者| someone or something that abates something (makes something less strong or severe). |The noise abater helped reduce the sound of the construction.  噪音减轻器帮助减少了施工的声音。|近义词：mitigator, reducer, moderator, alleviator|反义词: aggravator, intensifier| noun
