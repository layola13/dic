hortatory:/ˈhɔːrtətɔːri/
adjective
劝告的，激励的
tending or aiming to exhort; encouraging.
The coach gave a hortatory speech to his team before the final game. 教练在决赛前对他的球队发表了鼓舞人心的讲话。
hortative, encouraging, inspiring, exhortative, urging
dissuading, discouraging, deterrent, inhibiting, repressive
adjective
