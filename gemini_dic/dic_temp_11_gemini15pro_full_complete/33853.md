circumposition:/ˌsɜːrkəm.pəˈzɪʃ.ən/ | n. |介词短语；环介词；包围词|A type of adposition that consists of two parts which appear on both sides of the complement. It's like a two-part preposition that surrounds its object.|例句：The German phrase "von ... aus" (from ... out of) is a circumposition. 德语短语“von ... aus”（从...出发）是一个介词短语。

例句：In some languages, circumpositions are more common than prepositions. 在某些语言中，介词短语比介词更常见。|近义词：adposition|反义词:|词性:noun
