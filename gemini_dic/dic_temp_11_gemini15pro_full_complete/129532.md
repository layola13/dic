non-belligerency: /ˌnɒn bɛˈlɪdʒərənsi/ (British) /ˌnɑːn bɛˈlɪdʒərənsi/ (American)| n. |不交战；非交战状态|The state of not being at war with another country, even though one might be at war with other countries.  A nation or state maintaining such a status is termed a non-belligerent.|Switzerland maintained a policy of non-belligerency throughout World War II. 瑞士在整个二战期间保持了不交战政策。

|例句：During the conflict, several nations declared non-belligerency, offering humanitarian aid but not taking sides. 在冲突期间，几个国家宣布不交战，提供人道主义援助，但不采取任何一方的立场。

|例句：The small island nation declared non-belligerency, hoping to avoid being drawn into the escalating war.  这个小岛国宣布不交战，希望避免卷入不断升级的战争。

|近义词：neutrality, non-participation|反义词：belligerency, hostility|词性：noun
