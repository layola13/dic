mucor:/ˈmjuːkɔːr/ (US), /ˈmjuːkə(r)/ (UK)| n.|毛霉菌，毛霉属|A genus of fungi in the family Mucoraceae, characterized by having tall sporangiophores that terminate in a globose sporangium containing numerous spores. Mucor species are commonly found in soil, decaying organic matter, and food.|Mucor is a common type of mold found on bread. 毛霉是一种常见的在面包上发现的霉菌。|Synonym: mold, mildew|Antonym: None|Noun
