noncohesive:/ˌnɒnkəʊˈhiːsɪv/, /ˌnɑːnkəʊˈhiːsɪv/
adjective
不粘着的，不团结的，不凝聚的
lacking cohesiveness; not sticking together
The sand on the beach was dry and noncohesive, making it difficult to build a sandcastle.  海滩上的沙子又干又不粘，很难堆沙堡。
The group was noncohesive, with members constantly arguing and disagreeing.  这个团体缺乏凝聚力，成员之间不断争吵和意见不合。
Synonyms:incoherent, disunited, disconnected, loose
Antonyms:cohesive, united, connected, coherent
词性:adjective
