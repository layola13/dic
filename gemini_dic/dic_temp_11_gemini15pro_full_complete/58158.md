droughty:/ˈdraʊti/ | adj. | 干旱的，口渴的 | Affected by or suffering from drought; thirsty. | The droughty land yielded a poor harvest.  干旱的土地收成不好。|  The farmer felt droughty after working in the sun all day.  在太阳底下工作了一整天后，农民感到口渴。 | 近义词: arid, parched, dry, thirsty | 反义词:  wet, damp, humid, moist | 词性: adjective

词源:  From drought + -y.  来自名词 drought（干旱）加上形容词后缀 -y.
