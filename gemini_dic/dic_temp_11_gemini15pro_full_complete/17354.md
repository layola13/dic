beacon:/ˈbiːkən/| n.|灯塔，信号灯，烽火，指路明灯|A fire or light set up in a high or prominent position as a warning, signal, or celebration. |The lighthouse serves as a beacon for ships at sea.  灯塔为海上航行的船只指引方向。|synonyms: signal, warning, guide, lighthouse|antonyms: obscurity, darkness|noun

beacon:/ˈbiːkən/| v.|指引，照亮|To guide or warn with a beacon. |The flashing lights beaconed us to safety. 闪烁的灯光指引我们到达安全的地方。|synonyms: guide, direct, signal|antonyms: mislead, confuse|verb
