insourcing:/ˈɪnˌsɔːrsɪŋ/
n.
内包；内部采购；内部解决
The practice of using an organization's own personnel or other resources to accomplish a task that was previously outsourced.
Many companies are now choosing insourcing to reduce costs and maintain greater control over their operations. 许多公司现在选择内包以降低成本并保持对其运营的更大控制。
Insourcing allows companies to leverage their internal expertise and resources. 内包允许公司利用其内部专业知识和资源。
近义词:onshoring, internalization
反义词:outsourcing, offshoring
词性:名词

词源: "in-" (meaning inside) + "sourcing" (from "source," meaning to obtain from a particular place)  A blend of "in" and "outsourcing".  The term gained popularity in the late 20th and early 21st centuries as a response to the trend of outsourcing.
