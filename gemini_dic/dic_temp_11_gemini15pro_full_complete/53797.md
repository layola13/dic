disconcert:/ˌdɪskənˈsɜːrt/ /ˌdɪskənˈsɝːt/ | adj. (obsolete, rare) | 沮丧的，不安的 | Feeling or showing embarrassment or shame. | He felt disconcerted by his failure. 他因失败而感到沮丧。(罕见用法) | Synonyms: abashed, ashamed, embarrassed | Antonyms: confident, composed | adjective (rarely used)
