endomorphy:/ˈɛndəˌmɔːrfi/
n.
内胚层体型，肥胖体型
Relating to or characteristic of a heavy or rounded body type.  A predominance of soft roundness throughout the various regions of the body; mesomorphy with an excess of fat.
Example:  His endomorphy made him prone to weight gain. 他的内胚层体型使他容易增重。
Example:  Researchers studied the link between endomorphy and certain health risks. 研究人员研究了内胚层体型与某些健康风险之间的联系。
Synonym:  roundness, plumpness, stoutness
Antonym:  ectomorphy, mesomorphy
