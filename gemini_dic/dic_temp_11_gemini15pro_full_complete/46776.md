dangerousnesses:/ˈdeɪndʒərəsnəsɪz/
noun
危险性；危险程度
The dangerousnesses of the chemicals used in the experiment were carefully assessed.  实验中使用的化学品的危险性得到了仔细评估。
The report highlighted the dangerousnesses of the current political climate.  报告强调了当前政治气候的危险性。
Near synonyms:  perils, hazards, risks, threats
Antonyms: safeties, securities
词性: 名词
