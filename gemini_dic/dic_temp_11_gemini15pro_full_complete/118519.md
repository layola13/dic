mesocoelia:/ˌmɛsəˈsiːliə/ | n. | 中腔；中脑腔 | The cavity within the midbrain, also known as the cerebral aqueduct or aqueduct of Sylvius. It connects the third and fourth ventricles of the brain and allows cerebrospinal fluid to flow between them. |  Blockage of the mesocoelia can lead to hydrocephalus. 中腔阻塞会导致脑积水。| synonym: cerebral aqueduct, aqueduct of Sylvius | antonym: N/A | noun
