beerhouse:/ˈbɪəhaʊs/ | n. |啤酒屋；小酒店| A place where beer is sold and drunk; a pub. |例句：The old beerhouse was a popular gathering spot for the locals.  这家老啤酒屋是当地人喜爱的聚集地。

例句：We stopped at a quaint beerhouse for a refreshing pint. 我们在一家古色古香的啤酒屋停下来喝了一品脱清爽的啤酒。|近义词：pub, tavern, bar, inn |反义词: None (No direct opposite for a place serving beer) |词性: noun

Etymology: From 'beer' + 'house'.  'Beer' comes from Old English 'bēor', of Germanic origin. 'House' comes from Old English 'hūs', also of Germanic origin.
