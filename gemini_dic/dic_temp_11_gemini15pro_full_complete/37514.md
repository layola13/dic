commodore:/ˈkɒmədɔːr/ (US) /ˈkɑːmədɔːr/| n.|海军准将；船队司令；（游艇俱乐部等的）会长|A naval officer of high rank, but below rear admiral.  Also, the title of the senior captain in a fleet of merchant ships, or the president of a yacht club.|The commodore led the fleet into battle.|近义词：captain, admiral, commander|反义词:subordinate, crewman|词性:noun

commodores:/ˈkɒmədɔːrz/ (US) /ˈkɑːmədɔːrz/| n.|海军准将们；船队司令们；（游艇俱乐部等的）会长们|Plural form of commodore.|The commodores gathered for the annual meeting.|近义词：captains, admirals, commanders|反义词:subordinates, crewmen|词性:noun
