anti-platonist:/ˈæntiˈpleɪtənɪst/ | n. adj. | 反柏拉图主义者，反柏拉图主义的 | Someone who opposes or rejects the philosophical theories of Plato, especially his theory of Forms. |  Example: The anti-platonist argued against the existence of an abstract realm of perfect Forms.  反柏拉图主义者反对存在一个完美的抽象形式领域。| Example:  His anti-platonist stance led him to focus on empirical observations rather than abstract reasoning. 他的反柏拉图主义立场使他专注于经验观察而不是抽象推理。 | 近义词: nominalist, empiricist | 反义词: Platonist | 词性: noun, adjective
