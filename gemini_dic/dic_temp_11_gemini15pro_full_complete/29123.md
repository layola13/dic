casco:/ˈkæskoʊ/ (US), /ˈkɑːskoʊ/ (UK)| n.|头盔，安全帽；(尤指船的)船体；(水果等的)外壳，外皮|A protective helmet, especially one worn by cyclists, motorcyclists, or construction workers.  Also refers to the hull of a ship or the outer covering of a fruit.|例句：He always wears a casco when he rides his motorcycle. 他骑摩托车时总是戴着头盔。

例句：The ship's casco was damaged in the storm.  船体在暴风雨中受损了。|近义词：helmet, shell, hull|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a physical object like a helmet or hull)|词性: Noun


casco:/ˈkɑːsko/ (Spanish)| n.|cash; funds; ready money (Spain)|Money, especially in the form of cash. Primarily used in Spanish-speaking regions.|例句：Necesito más casco para comprar comida. (Spanish) I need more cash to buy food. 我需要更多现金来买食物。

例句：No tengo suficiente casco para pagar la cuenta. (Spanish) I don't have enough cash to pay the bill.我没有足够的现金支付账单。|近义词：(Spanish) dinero, efectivo|反义词: None (No direct opposite for cash)|词性: Noun (Spanish)
