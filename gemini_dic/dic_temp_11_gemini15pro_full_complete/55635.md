ditchwater:/ˈdɪtʃwɔːtər/ | n. | 污水，脏水；(比喻)毫无价值的东西 |  Stagnant, dirty water from a ditch; something of very low quality or interest. | The coffee tasted like ditchwater. 这咖啡尝起来像污水。| 近义词: stagnant water, slop, mire | 反义词: pure water, clean water | noun

ditchwater:/ˈdɪtʃwɔːtər/ | adj. | (比喻)毫无价值的，乏味的 |  (Figurative) Dull, uninteresting, or of very poor quality. |  The play was ditchwater. 这部戏很无聊。| 近义词: dull, uninteresting, boring, insipid | 反义词: interesting, exciting, stimulating | adjective
