extillation:/ˌɛkstɪˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 蒸馏; 提炼; 精华 | The process of purifying a liquid by successive evaporation and condensation.  The extillation of essential oils from plants. | 例句：The extillation of whiskey involves several steps. 威士忌的蒸馏过程包括几个步骤。

例句：The perfume was an extillation of rare flowers. 这款香水是从珍稀花卉中提取的精华。 | 近义词：distillation, refinement, purification | 反义词：contamination, pollution | 词性：名词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin extillatio(n-), from the verb extillare ‘drip out’, from ex- ‘out of’ + stillare (see still).
