evangel:/ɪˈvændʒəl/ | n. | 福音，佳音，福音书 |  Good news, especially about Christianity.  It also refers to one of the four books of the New Testament that tell the story of Jesus Christ's life. | The preacher delivered a powerful evangel about redemption. 传教士发表了一篇关于救赎的有力的福音。| synonyms: gospel, good news, glad tidings, message | antonyms: bad news, ill tidings | noun

evangelical:/ˌiːvænˈdʒɛlɪkəl/ | adj. | 福音的，福音派的，热衷于传播福音的 | Of or relating to the Christian gospel, especially the Protestant churches that emphasize the importance of personal conversion and faith.  Also describes someone who is zealous in spreading the gospel. |  She comes from a very evangelical family. 她来自一个非常虔诚的福音派家庭。| synonyms: gospel, pietistic, devout, fervent | antonyms: irreligious, secular, unbelieving | adjective
