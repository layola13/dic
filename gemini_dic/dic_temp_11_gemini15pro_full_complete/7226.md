anglicising: /ˈæŋɡlɪsaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使英国化；使英语化 | The process of making something more English in form or character.  | They are anglicising their names to fit in better. 他们正在使他们的名字英国化，以便更好地融入。 |  Many foreign words have been anglicised over time. 许多外来词随着时间的推移已经被英语化了。 | 近义词: Anglifying, adapting, assimilating | 反义词: Nativizing, localizing | 词性: 动词 (Gerund or present participle)

Etymology: From anglicise + -ing. Anglicise comes from  "Anglic" + "-ise." "Anglic" refers to England or English.  "-ise" is a suffix used to form verbs meaning "to make or become."
