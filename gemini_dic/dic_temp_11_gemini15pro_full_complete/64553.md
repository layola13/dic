erratum:/ɪˈrɑːtəm/ (plural: errata /ɪˈrɑːtə/)
|n.|勘误；正误表；错误|An error or mistake, especially one in a printed work, corrected in a list of errors. |The book contains several errata. 这本书包含几个错误。|Synonyms: correction, amendment, revision|Antonyms: mistake, error, flaw
|noun|勘误；正误表；错误|A list of errors and their corrections appended to a book or other publication. |The errata were printed on a separate sheet. 正误表单独印在一张纸上。|Synonyms: corrigenda, list of corrections|Antonyms: N/A
