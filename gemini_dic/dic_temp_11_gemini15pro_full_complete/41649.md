correctory:/kəˈrɛktəri/ | adj. | 纠正的，改正的 | Intended to correct or rectify something. |  The teacher provided correctory feedback on the student's essay. 老师对学生的作文提供了纠正性的反馈。|  corrective, remedial, emendatory | incorrect, faulty | adjective

correctory:/kəˈrɛktəri/ | n. | 矫正机构，感化院 | An institution designed to correct or reform behavior, especially juvenile delinquency. |  He was sent to a correctory after committing a series of petty crimes.  他在犯了一系列轻微罪行后被送进了感化院。 | reformatory, correctional facility, penitentiary |  None | noun
