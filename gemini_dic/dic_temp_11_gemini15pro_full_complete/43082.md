cowriter:/ˈkoʊˌraɪtər/| n.|合著者，共同撰稿人|A cowriter is someone who writes something with another person or people.|例句：She and her husband are cowriters on the new play. 她和她的丈夫是这部新剧的合著者。

例句：The song had two cowriters. 这首歌有两个共同的作者。

|近义词：collaborator, joint author|反义词:ghostwriter|词性:noun
