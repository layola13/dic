laagering: /ˈlɑːɡərɪŋ/ /ˈleɪɡərɪŋ/
n.
窖藏，陈酿
The process of conditioning beer, wine, or other alcoholic beverages by storing them in a cool environment for a period of time.
The brewery uses a unique laagering process for their dark beers.  这家啤酒厂对其黑啤采用独特的窖藏工艺。
After fermentation, the beer undergoes a long laagering period. 发酵后，啤酒要经过长时间的窖藏。
maturation, aging, conditioning

noun
