overfixed:/ˌoʊvərˈfɪkst/| adj.|过度固定的，过度专注的|Excessively or rigidly fixated, often on a single idea, behavior, or object. This term implies an unhealthy level of attachment or preoccupation.|
例句：His overfixed gaze on the clock made it clear he was anxious about the meeting. 他过度专注地盯着时钟，表明他对会议感到焦虑。

例句：The therapist believed the patient's overfixed personality contributed to their inability to adapt to change. 治疗师认为，病人过度固定的性格导致他们无法适应变化。|近义词：obsessive, fixated, preoccupied|反义词：flexible, adaptable, open-minded|词性:adjective
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "fixed" (past participle of "fix," meaning "to fasten or attach").
