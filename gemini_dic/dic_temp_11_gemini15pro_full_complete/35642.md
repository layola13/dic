coauthors:/ˈkoʊˌɔːθərz/ | n. | 合著者，共同作者 |  People who write something together, such as a book or article. | Example 1: The coauthors worked tirelessly to finish the manuscript. 合著者们不知疲倦地工作以完成手稿。

Example 2:  The book has two coauthors, a historian and a journalist. 这本书有两个合著者，一位历史学家和一位记者。 | Synonyms: collaborators, joint authors | Antonyms:  sole author | noun
