multicyclic:/ˌmʌltiˈsaɪklɪk/ | adj. | 多周期的，多循环的 |  Having or involving more than one cycle; occurring repeatedly in cycles. |  The engine is multicyclic, meaning it has several cylinders firing in sequence.  这个发动机是多循环的，意味着它有几个气缸按顺序点火。|  polycyclic, multiphase |  monocyclic, single-cycle | adjective
