dismally:/ˈdɪzməli/| adv.|凄凉地，阴沉地，忧郁地|In a gloomy or depressing manner; miserably.|The team played dismally, losing by a large margin.  球队表现糟糕，惨败。|近义词：gloomily, drearily, miserably, depressingly|反义词：cheerfully, brightly, happily|词性:副词
词源: From dismal + -ly.  Dismal comes from the Old French *dismal, from Latin dies mali "evil days," plural of dies malus.
