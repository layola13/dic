autotransplantation: /ˌɔːtoʊˌtrænsplænˈteɪʃən/ | n. |自体移植| The surgical transfer of tissue or an organ from one part of the body to another in the same individual. |  Autotransplantation of teeth is a viable option in certain cases. 在某些情况下，牙齿自体移植是一个可行的选择。| Example: The surgeon performed an autotransplantation of the patient's skin.  外科医生对病人的皮肤进行了自体移植。 |Synonyms: autograft | Antonyms: allotransplantation, xenotransplantation | noun
