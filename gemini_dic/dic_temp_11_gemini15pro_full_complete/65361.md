eulogizers:/ˈjuːləˌdʒaɪzərz/
n.
颂扬者，赞美者
People who deliver eulogies, praising or commemorating someone, often at a funeral.
The eulogizers spoke movingly about the deceased's contributions to the community.  颂扬者动情地讲述了死者对社区的贡献。
The eulogizers shared heartfelt stories about their friend.  颂扬者分享了他们朋友的感人故事。
speakers, praisers, commenders
critics, detractors
noun
