eider:/ˈaɪdər/| n.|绒鸭; 绒鸭绒;|A large sea duck that lives in northern regions and is known for its soft down feathers, which are used for filling quilts and pillows.|The eider duck is known for its incredibly soft down. 绒鸭以其极其柔软的绒毛而闻名。| The eider's down is highly prized for its insulating properties. 绒鸭绒因其隔热性能而备受推崇。 |近义词:duck, sea duck|反义词:|noun
