fantasticalness:/fænˈtæstɪkəlnəs/ | n. | 奇异；奇妙；空想性 | The sheer fantasticalness of the story captivated the children.  这个故事的奇异性深深吸引了孩子们。 | The fantasticalness of his claims made them hard to believe. 他说法之离奇令人难以置信。 | 近义词：whimsicality, fancifulness, extravagance | 反义词：realism, practicality, ordinariness | 词性: noun | 词源: From fantastical + -ness. Fantastical from fantasy + -al. Fantasy from Old French fantasie, from Late Latin phantasia, from Greek phantasia "appearance, imagination," from phantazein "to make visible."
