intine:/ˈɪntaɪn/| n. |内壁；花粉内膜|The intine is the inner layer of the pollen grain wall, lying below the exine.|The pollen tube emerges through a pore in the intine. 花粉管从内壁的一个孔中长出。|近义词:endosporium (though not a perfect synonym, it refers to a similar inner layer in other spores)|反义词:exine|词性:noun
