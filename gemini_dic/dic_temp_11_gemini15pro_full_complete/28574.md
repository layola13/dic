careening:/kəˈriːnɪŋ/
verb
倾斜，横冲直撞
moving swiftly and in an uncontrolled way, especially changing direction suddenly
The car careened down the hill, narrowly missing a tree. 汽车沿着山坡横冲直撞，差点撞到一棵树。
近义词：swerving, veering, lurching, rocking, tilting
反义词：steady, stable
词性：verb (不及物动词, 通常和介词down, along, across等搭配使用)

careen:/kəˈriːn/
verb
(使)倾斜，(使)横冲直撞
(cause a boat or ship) to lean over on one side; (of a boat or ship) lean over on one side
The ship careened violently in the storm. 船在暴风雨中剧烈地倾斜。
近义词：tilt, list, lean, tip, heel
反义词：right, straighten
词性：verb (及物动词和不及物动词)
