misthrive:/mɪsˈθraɪv/
verb
未能茁壮成长；发育不良；衰败
To fail to thrive or prosper; to grow poorly or decline.
The plants misthrived in the poor soil. 这些植物在贫瘠的土壤中生长不良。
近义词:decline, decay, wither, languish
反义词:thrive, flourish, prosper
词性:v.

Etymology:
From mis- (not, badly) + thrive (to prosper, flourish).  The "mis-" prefix is of Old English origin, while "thrive" comes from Old Norse *þrīfa, meaning "to grasp, seize, grip."
