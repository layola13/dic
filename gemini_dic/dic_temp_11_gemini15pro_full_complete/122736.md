moire:/ˈmwɑːr; mwɑːˈreɪ/| n.|云纹绸; 波纹丝; 光波纹|A shimmering pattern produced by two overlapping grids of lines, or a similar effect produced by two overlapping patterns of regular shapes.|The dress had a subtle moire pattern. 这件连衣裙有淡淡的波纹图案。|Synonyms: shimmer, ripple, watermarked|Antonyms: plain, smooth|noun

moire:/ˈmwɑːr; mwɑːˈreɪ/| adj.|有波纹的; 波纹绸的|Having a wavy or rippled appearance, like moire silk.|The moire effect on the fabric was quite striking.  布料上的波纹效果非常引人注目。|Synonyms: rippled, wavy, chatoyant|Antonyms: smooth, flat|adjective
