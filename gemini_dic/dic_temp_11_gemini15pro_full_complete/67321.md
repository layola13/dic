extemporary:/ɪkˈstɛmpəˌrɛri/ /ɛkˈstɛmpəˌrɛri/
adjective
即兴的；临时的；无准备的
Done, spoken, performed, etc., without preparation or with little or no preparation or practice.
The principal gave an extemporary speech at the assembly. 校长在集会上发表了即兴演讲。
Synonyms: impromptu, improvised, off-the-cuff, ad-lib, unrehearsed
Antonyms: prepared, planned, rehearsed
词性: adjective
