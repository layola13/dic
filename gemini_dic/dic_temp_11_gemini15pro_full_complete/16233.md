banquetter:/ˈbæŋkwɪtər/ | n. | 宴会参加者，赴宴者 | A person who attends a banquet. |  The banquetters enjoyed the lavish meal and lively music. 赴宴者们享受着丰盛的菜肴和欢快的音乐。|  The hotel prepared a special menu for the banquetters.  酒店为宴会嘉宾准备了特别的菜单。 | synonyms: diner, guest, attendee, participant | antonyms: host, caterer | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *banqueter, from Anglo-Norman banqueter, from Old French banqueter (“to feast, banquet”), from banc (“bench, table”). Ultimately from Frankish *bank (“bench”), from Proto-Germanic *bankiz (“bench, bank”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰengʰ- (“to dig, raise a mound”).
