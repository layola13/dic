de-stressing:/diːˈstrɛsɪŋ/
| adj. | 减压的，缓解压力的 | The act of reducing or relieving stress.  | Taking a warm bath is a de-stressing ritual for me.  泡个热水澡是我的减压仪式。|近义词：relaxing, calming, soothing |反义词: stressing, straining |词性:形容词
de-stress:/diːˈstrɛs/
| v. | 减压，缓解压力 | To reduce or relieve stress. | I need to find a way to de-stress after a long day at work. 我需要找到一种方法来缓解一天漫长工作后的压力。|近义词：relax, unwind, chill out |反义词: stress, tense |词性:动词

词源:  "de-" (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + "stress" (noun)
