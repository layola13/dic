moonery:/ˈmuːnəri/
n.
愚蠢的行为；荒唐事；傻事
A foolish act, idea, or thing; nonsense.
He dismissed the whole affair as a moonery. 他把整件事斥为胡闹。
近义词：foolishness, absurdity, silliness, nonsense
反义词：wisdom, sense, rationality
词性：名词

Etymology:
From moon + -ery.  Relating to the supposed influence of the moon on mental states, leading to foolish or eccentric behavior.
