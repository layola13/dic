alamites:/ˈæləˌmaɪts/
n.
芦木；芦木化石
An extinct genus of tree-like plants related to horsetails and club mosses, common in the Carboniferous period.  They are characterized by their segmented, jointed stems and whorled leaves.
The fossilized alamite trunks were massive, indicating the plant's large size. 芦木化石的树干非常巨大，表明这种植物体型庞大。

The coal deposits of the Carboniferous period are rich in alamite fossils. 石炭纪的煤矿沉积物中富含芦木化石。
近义词: Calamites (often used interchangeably),  fossil plants
反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's a specific extinct plant genus.  One could consider "extant plants" as a broad contrasting concept.)
词性: noun
