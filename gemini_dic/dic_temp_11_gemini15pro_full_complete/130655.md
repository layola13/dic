nonadvantageously:/ˌnɒnˌædvənˈteɪdʒəsli/
adverb
不利地，没有优势地
In a way that does not give an advantage; in a disadvantageous manner.
The deal was structured nonadvantageously for the smaller company. 这笔交易对小公司来说是不利的。
The location of the store was nonadvantageously positioned.  这家商店的位置很不利。
Synonym: disadvantageously, unfavorably, poorly, badly
Antonym: advantageously, favorably, beneficially, well
词性: 副词
