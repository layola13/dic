gangway:/ˈɡæŋweɪ/| n.|通道，舷梯，（剧院等场所的）座位间的走道|A raised platform or passageway, often used for boarding or leaving a ship or aircraft; a passageway between rows of seats.|例句：The passengers boarded the ship via the gangway. 乘客通过舷梯登上了船。

例句：Excuse me, could you let me through the gangway?  不好意思，可以让一下让我过吗？（在剧院等场所）|近义词：aisle, passage, corridor, walkway|反义词:barrier, obstruction|词性:名词

gangway:/ˈɡæŋweɪ/| int.|让开！借光！|Used as an exclamation to request people to move out of the way.|例句：Gangway! The ambulance is coming! 让开！救护车来了！

例句：Gangway! I need to get through. 借光！我得过去。|近义词：excuse me, move aside, make way|反义词:stay put, block|词性:感叹词
