abash:/əˈbæʃ/|v.|使窘迫，使羞愧|To make someone feel embarrassed, ashamed, or uncomfortable.|He was abashed by her frank comments about his appearance. 他被她对他外貌的坦率评价弄得很尴尬。|Synonyms: embarrass, humiliate, shame, mortify|Antonyms: embolden, reassure, encourage|verb

abash:/əˈbæʃ/|adj.(obsolete, rare)|窘迫的，羞愧的|Feeling embarrassed, ashamed, or uncomfortable.  (This adjectival usage is now quite rare.)|He offered an abashed apology. 他窘迫地道歉。(古旧用法)|Synonyms: embarrassed, ashamed, humiliated|Antonyms: unembarrassed, confident, bold|adjective
