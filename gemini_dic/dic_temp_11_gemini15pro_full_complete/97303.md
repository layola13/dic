infomercial: /ˈɪnfəˈmɜːrʃl/ (US), /ˌɪnfəˈmɜːʃl/ (UK)| n. |电视购物广告，资讯广告|A television program that is an extended advertisement, often including testimonials and demonstrations.| Example: I saw an infomercial for a new type of blender last night. 昨晚我看到一个新型搅拌机的电视购物广告。

Example: That infomercial for the wrinkle cream went on forever.  那个除皱霜的电视购物广告播了很久。


|近义词：commercial, advertisement, advert|反义词: N/A |词性: noun


Etymology:  Blend of information + commercial.
