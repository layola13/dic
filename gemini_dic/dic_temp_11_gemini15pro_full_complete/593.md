above-the-fold: /əˈbʌv ðə foʊld/
| adj.| 首屏的，位于折叠线以上的 |  Refers to the portion of a webpage that is visible without scrolling. Originates from the practice of folding newspapers in half, where the top half was considered the most important. | The website's above-the-fold content needs to be engaging to capture the user's attention.  网站的首屏内容需要引人入胜才能抓住用户的注意力。| synonyms: visible, prominent | antonyms: below-the-fold, hidden | adjective
