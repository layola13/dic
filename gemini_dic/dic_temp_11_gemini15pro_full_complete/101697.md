irreparability:/ɪˌrɛpərəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可挽回性，无法弥补 | The state of being impossible to repair or make right.  |The earthquake caused irreparable damage to the city. 地震对这座城市造成了不可挽回的损失。|Synonyms:  incurableness, irremediableness, beyond repair | Antonyms: repairability, fixability | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *irreparablite, from Late Latin irreparabilitās (“irreparability”), from Latin irreparābilis (“irreparable”) + -itās (“-ity”).  irreparābilis is from in- (“not”) + reparābilis (“repairable”), from reparō (“repair, restore”).
