fixed-wing:/ˈfɪkstˌwɪŋ/| adj.|固定翼的|An adjective describing aircraft with wings that are fixed in place and do not move, generating lift through forward motion.|例句：Fixed-wing aircraft are more fuel-efficient than helicopters for long distances. 固定翼飞机比直升机更省油，适合长途飞行。

例句：The airport caters primarily to fixed-wing aircraft, not helicopters. 该机场主要服务于固定翼飞机，而不是直升机。|近义词：conventional aircraft, airplane|反义词：rotary-wing, helicopter|词性:形容词
