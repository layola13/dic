endear:/ɪnˈdɪr/ /ɛnˈdɪr/ | vt. | 使受喜爱；使受钟爱 | To cause to be loved or liked. | Her kindness endeared her to everyone. 她的善良使她受到大家的喜爱。

The puppy's playful antics endeared it to the children. 小狗顽皮的滑稽动作使孩子们非常喜欢它。

近义词：charm, captivate, attract | 反义词：alienate, repel | 词性：动词
