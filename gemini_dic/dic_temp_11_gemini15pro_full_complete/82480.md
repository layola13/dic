grungers:/ˈɡrʌndʒərz/
n.
颓废摇滚乐迷;垃圾摇滚乐迷
Fans of grunge music, a subgenre of alternative rock popular in the early 1990s.
Grungers were known for their flannel shirts and ripped jeans.  颓废摇滚乐迷以法兰绒衬衫和破牛仔裤而闻名。

The grungers gathered at the music festival.  颓废摇滚乐迷聚集在音乐节上。

近义词:alternative rockers, grunge fans
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "pop fans" or fans of contrasting music genres)
词性:noun
