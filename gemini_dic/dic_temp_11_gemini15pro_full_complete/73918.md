forswears:/fɔːrˈswɛrz/
|v.|发誓放弃，正式放弃；伪证|
|agrees to give up or do without (something); formally renounces or disavows (something); commits perjury.|
|He forswears all violence. 他发誓放弃一切暴力。|
|renounce, repudiate, abandon, reject|
|affirm, uphold, embrace, adopt|
|verb|
