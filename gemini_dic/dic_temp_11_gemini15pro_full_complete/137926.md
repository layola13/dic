nonvolubilities:/ˌnɒnvɒljuːˈbɪlətiz/
n.
沉默寡言，不健谈
lack of fluency or readiness of speech; reticence.
His nonvolubility made it difficult to get to know him. 他的沉默寡言让人很难了解他。
Near synonyms: reticence, reserve, taciturnity, uncommunicativeness
Antonyms: volubility, loquacity, talkativeness, garrulity
noun
