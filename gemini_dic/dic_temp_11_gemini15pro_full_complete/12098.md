artistically:/ɑːrˈtɪstɪkli/
adverb
艺术地；富有艺术性地
In a way that relates to art or artists, in a skillful and attractive way.
She danced artistically. 她舞姿优美。
Near Synonyms: creatively, imaginatively, skillfully
Near Antonyms: artlessly, clumsily, crudely
词性: 副词
