misreferenced:/ˌmɪsˈrɛfərənst/
adjective
错误引用的，引用不当的
incorrectly referenced; cited inaccurately.
The misreferenced data led to incorrect conclusions in the research paper.  错误引用的数据导致研究论文的结论不正确。
The lawyer pointed out the misreferenced case in his opponent's argument.  律师指出了对手论证中引用不当的案例。
Synonyms: miscited, incorrectly cited
Antonyms: correctly referenced, accurately cited
词性: adjective
