hornpipe:/ˈhɔːrnpaɪp/ (US) /ˈhɔːnpaɪp/ (UK)| n.|角笛舞; 角笛舞曲|A lively dance, traditionally popular with sailors, or the music for this dance. It is characterized by a quick tempo and often involves stepping, hopping, and swinging movements.|The sailors danced a hornpipe on the deck. 水手们在甲板上跳起了角笛舞。|
The band played a lively hornpipe. 乐队演奏了一首活泼的角笛舞曲。|Synonyms: jig, reel|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider slow, mournful music/dance)|noun
