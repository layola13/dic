durables:/ˈdjʊərəblz/| n.|耐用品|Consumer durables are goods that are expected to last for three years or more. 消费耐用品是指预期可以使用三年或三年以上的商品。|例句：The demand for durables such as cars and washing machines has fallen. 对汽车和洗衣机等耐用品的需求下降了。

例句：Sales of consumer durables have been steadily increasing. 消费耐用品的销售额一直在稳步增长。|近义词：long-lasting goods, hard goods|反义词：consumables, non-durables|词性:noun (plural)
