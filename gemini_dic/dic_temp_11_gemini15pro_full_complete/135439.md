nonpersonification:/ˌnɒnpɜːˌsɒnɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 非人格化，不拟人化 | The absence of personification; the avoidance of ascribing human qualities to inanimate objects, animals, or abstract concepts. |  The scientist insisted on nonpersonification in his description of animal behavior.  这位科学家坚持在他对动物行为的描述中不拟人化。| synonyms: depersonalization, objectification | antonyms: personification, anthropomorphism | noun
