brack:/bræk/ | n. | 缺陷，裂缝；次品；废物；劣质煤 | a flaw, crack, or imperfection; something broken or damaged; inferior coal; refuse or waste | The wall had a noticeable brack.  墙上有一个明显的裂缝。 | synonyms: flaw, defect, crack, imperfection, fault, blemish, damage | antonyms: perfection, soundness | noun

brackish:/ˈbrækɪʃ/ | adj. | 微咸的；有点咸的；令人不快的 | slightly salty; having a salty taste, especially from containing a mixture of seawater and fresh water; unpleasant or distasteful | The brackish water of the estuary wasn't suitable for drinking.  河口的微咸水不适合饮用。| synonyms: salty, briny, saline | antonyms: fresh, sweet | adjective
