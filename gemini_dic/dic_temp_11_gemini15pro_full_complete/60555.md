elapsing:/ɪˈlæpsɪŋ/
verb
流逝，过去
(of time) pass or go by.
Three years elapsed before he returned home. 三年过去了，他才回家。
synonyms: passing, going by, slipping away, gliding by
antonyms: halting, stopping, standing still
词性：动词现在分词
