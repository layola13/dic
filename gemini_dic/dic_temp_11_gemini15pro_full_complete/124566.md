mourn:/mɔːrn/ (UK), /mɔːrn/ (US)| v. |哀悼，悲伤，忧伤|To feel or express deep sorrow or regret for someone or something lost, especially someone who has died.|She mourned the loss of her beloved pet. 她哀悼她心爱宠物的离去。

The nation mourned the death of its leader. 全国人民哀悼领袖的逝世。

|近义词：grieve, lament, sorrow, bewail|反义词：rejoice, celebrate|词性:动词
