ETYMOLOGY->
From archaic + -ize.  "Archaic" comes from the Greek "arkhaikos," meaning "ancient" or "primitive," from "arkhē," meaning "beginning."  The suffix "-ize" is used to form verbs meaning "to make or become like" something.
