overcharging:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃɑːrdʒɪŋ/
n.|过度收费，过量充电|The act of charging too much for goods or services.|例句：Customers complained about overcharging on their phone bills. 顾客抱怨电话账单收费过高。|近义词：profiteering, gouging|反义词: undercharging, discounting
v.|对…过度收费，过量充电|To charge someone too much for goods or services. |例句：The taxi driver was accused of overcharging tourists.  出租车司机被指控对游客过度收费。|近义词：exploit, fleece|反义词: undercharge, discount
词性: 名词/动词 (gerund or present participle)
