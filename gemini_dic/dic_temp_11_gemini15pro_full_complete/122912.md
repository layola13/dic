molters:/ˈmoʊltərz/| n. |蜕皮动物；换毛动物|Animals that are currently molting (shedding their outer covering, such as skin, feathers, or fur).|The molters were kept in a separate enclosure to avoid disturbing the other animals. 蜕皮的动物被关在单独的围栏里，以免打扰其他动物。|The snake is a molter in the summer.  这条蛇在夏天蜕皮。| 近义词: shedders | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun


molt:/moʊlt/| v. n. |蜕皮；换毛| (v.) To shed periodically part or all of a coat or an outer covering, such as feathers, skin, or hair; (n.) the act or process of molting; something shed in molting.|The bird molted its feathers in the spring.  这只鸟在春天换毛。|The snake's molt was found near its basking rock. 蛇蜕下来的皮在它晒太阳的岩石附近被发现。| 近义词: shed, exuviate | 反义词: None | 词性: Verb, Noun

词源:  From Old English *m(e)oltian, from Proto-Germanic *meltaną "to let go, melt," from Proto-Indo-European *meldʰ- “to render liquid, let flow, melt.”
