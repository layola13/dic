electrons:/ɪˈlektrɒnz/ (UK) /ɪˈlektrɑːnz/ (US)| n. |电子|A stable subatomic particle with a charge of negative electricity, found in all atoms and acting as the primary carrier of electricity in solids.|The flow of electrons through a wire creates an electric current. 电流是电子在导线中的流动。|Electrons orbit the nucleus of an atom. 电子绕原子核旋转。|近义词:  (In some contexts) charged particles|反义词: protons, positrons (opposite charge)|词性: noun
