gastrology: /ɡæsˈtrɒlədʒi/ | n. | 占星术；星象学 | A pseudoscience that claims to divine information about human affairs and terrestrial events by studying the movements and relative positions of celestial objects.  It is considered by the scientific community to be a pseudoscience. |  She believes in gastrology and reads her horoscope every day. 她相信占星术，每天都看星象。|  Near synonyms: astrology, astromancy | Antonyms: astronomy, science | noun
