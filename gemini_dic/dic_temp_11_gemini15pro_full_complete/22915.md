bothrenchyma:/ˈboʊθrɛŋkɪmə/| n. |管状组织，管胞组织|A specialized parenchyma tissue with long tubular cells that form a system of interconnected channels, especially for conducting water in some mosses.|The bothrenchyma of the Sphagnum moss is highly efficient at absorbing and retaining water.|Synonym: water-conducting tissue|Antonym: N/A|noun
