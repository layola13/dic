| adj. | 肛门滞留的，过分拘谨的，一丝不苟的 |  Characterized by excessive neatness, orderliness, and attention to detail, often to the point of being obsessive or compulsive.  Stems from Freudian psychoanalysis, referring to the anal stage of development, where control over bodily functions becomes a source of pleasure or frustration.
