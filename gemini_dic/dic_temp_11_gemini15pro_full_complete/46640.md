damnedest:/ˈdæmdɪst/| adj.|最奇特的；最特别的；最要命的；最该死的|Used to emphasize how surprising, strange, or annoying something is.  It's an intensifier derived from "damned."  While it can carry a negative connotation, it's often used playfully or humorously.|He pulled the damnedest stunt I've ever seen. 他耍了我见过的最奇怪的把戏。|Synonyms: strangest, oddest, weirdest, darnedest (more polite)|Antonyms: ordinary, usual, normal|Adjective
