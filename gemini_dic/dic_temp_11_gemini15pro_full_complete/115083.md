maneuver:/məˈnuːvər/ (US), /məˈnuːvə/ (UK)| n. v.|策略，调遣，操控；演习，调动|A movement or series of moves requiring skill and care.|The driver maneuvered the car into a tight parking space. 司机巧妙地把车停在一个狭窄的停车位上。|synonyms: tactic, strategy, operation, ploy, scheme|antonyms: inaction, stillness, inactivity|noun, verb


maneuvers:/məˈnuːvərz/ (US), /məˈnuːvəz/ (UK)| n. |策略，调遣，操控；演习，调动 (plural form)|A series of movements or actions, especially in military or naval contexts, designed to achieve a specific objective.|The army conducted military maneuvers in the desert. 军队在沙漠中进行了军事演习。|synonyms: tactics, strategies, operations, exercises|antonyms: inaction, stillness, inactivity|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *manœuvrer*, from Old French *manovrer* "to work by hand," from *manouvre* "manual labor," from Latin *manu operare* "to work by hand."
