adorably:/əˈdɔːrəbli/ | adv. |可爱地，讨人喜欢地|In a way that inspires great affection; charmingly.| The kitten played adorably with the ball of yarn. 小猫和毛线球玩得可爱极了。| 近义词：charmingly, sweetly, cutely | 反义词：obnoxiously, repulsively | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From adorable + -ly. Adorable comes from the French *adorable*, from adorer "to adore" + -able.  Ultimately from Latin *adorare* "to worship."
