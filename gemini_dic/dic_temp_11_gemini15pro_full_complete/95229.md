inbuilt:/ˈɪnbɪlt/| adj.|内置的，嵌入的，固有的|Built-in, integrated, inherent.  Something that is inbuilt is an integral part of something and cannot be easily removed.|This camera has an inbuilt microphone. 这款相机有内置麦克风。|Synonyms: integrated, built-in, inherent, intrinsic|Antonyms: external, removable, add-on|adjective
