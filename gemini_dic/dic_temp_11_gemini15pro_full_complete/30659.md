cerograph:/ˈsɛrəɡræf/ /ˈsɪərəɡrɑːf/
n.
蜡纸记录器; 蜡纸; 蜡笔记录
An instrument for recording variations of thickness, as of wire, on a coated cylinder by means of a stylus which removes the coating in proportion to the thickness variations. Also, a record made by such an instrument; a cerogram.
The engineer used a cerograph to measure the minute variations in the wire's thickness. 工程师使用蜡纸记录器来测量导线厚度的细微变化。

近义词: recording instrument, measuring device
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "manual gauge" as a contrasting method)
词性: noun
