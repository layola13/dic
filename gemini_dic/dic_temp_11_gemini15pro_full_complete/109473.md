leporous:/ˈlɛprəs/| adj.|麻风的；患麻风病的；与麻风病有关的|Affected with, relating to, or resembling leprosy.  Characterized by moral corruption or decay.|例句：The leporous beggar sat by the roadside. 患麻风病的乞丐坐在路边。

例句：The report exposed the leporous corruption within the organization.  这份报告揭露了该组织内部腐败不堪的状况。|近义词：leprous, diseased, infected, contaminated, tainted|反义词：healthy, clean, pure, unblemished|词性:adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French lepreus, from late Latin leprosus, from Latin lepra ‘leprosy’.
