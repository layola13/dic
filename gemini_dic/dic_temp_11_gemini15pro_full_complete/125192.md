multicellularity:/ˌmʌltɪˌsɛljʊˈlærɪti/ | n. | 多细胞性 | The state of being composed of multiple cells, as opposed to being unicellular. | Multicellularity evolved independently in several lineages of organisms. 多细胞性在几个生物谱系中独立进化。|  The development of multicellularity was a major step in evolution. 多细胞性的发展是进化中的一个重要步骤。 | 近义词:  pluricellularity | 反义词: unicellularity | 词性: noun

Etymology: multi- (many) + cell + -ularity (state of being)  来自拉丁语 "multi-"（多）+ "cellula"（细胞）+ "-arity"（状态）
