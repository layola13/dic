overheated: /ˌoʊvərˈhiːtɪd/ | adj. | 过热的；过劳的 | having become too hot | The engine overheated and stopped working.  引擎过热并停止工作。| synonyms: overheated, superheated, burning, scorching | antonyms: cool, cold | adjective

overheat: /ˌoʊvərˈhiːt/ | v. |  (使)过热；(使)过劳 | to cause something to become too hot |  Don't let the engine overheat. 不要让引擎过热。  | synonyms: burn up, scorch, overheat | antonyms: cool down, chill | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + heat.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "too much" or "excessively". "Heat" refers to high temperature.  The combination creates the meaning of excessive heat.
