orantas:/ɔːˈrɑːntæs/ | n. | 祈祷者，恳求者 | A person who prays or supplicates, especially with outstretched arms. | The orantas on the mosaic depicted a woman with upraised arms.  马赛克上的祈祷者描绘了一个举起双臂的女人。| Synonyms: supplicant, petitioner, pleader | Antonyms: opponent, adversary | noun

orantas:/ɔːˈrɑːntæs/ | n. | 祈祷姿势 | The posture of praying or supplicating, typically with outstretched arms. | The orantas posture is a common motif in Byzantine art. 祈祷姿势是拜占庭艺术中常见的主题。 | Synonyms: prayer position, supplication posture | Antonyms: None | noun
