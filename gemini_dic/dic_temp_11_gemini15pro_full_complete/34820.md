clinching:/ˈklɪntʃɪŋ/| adj.| 决定性的；确定的；关键性的；(of an argument, evidence, etc.) serving to settle something conclusively;|The clinching evidence came to light just before the trial.|关键性的证据在审判前才曝光。|synonyms: decisive, conclusive, definitive, confirming, corroborative, validating|antonyms: inconclusive, indecisive|adjective

clinching:/ˈklɪntʃɪŋ/| n.| 敲弯钉尖；(in boxing) the act of holding an opponent tightly so as to prevent them from hitting one;|The clinching of the nails secured the frame.|敲弯钉尖固定住了框架。|synonyms: securing, fastening, fixing|antonyms: loosening, unfastening|noun
