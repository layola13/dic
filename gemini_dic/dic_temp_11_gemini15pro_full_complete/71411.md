flailed:/fleɪld/
verb
挥动；乱打；挣扎；打；拍打
(especially of arms and legs) wave or swing wildly.
The drowning man flailed his arms in the water. 溺水的人在水中挥舞着手臂。
thrashed, flogged, lashed out
controlled, precise
