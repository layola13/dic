outlying:/ˈaʊtˌlaɪɪŋ/| adj.|边远的，偏僻的；远离中心的|Situated far from the center or main body; remote.|The outlying areas of the city are less developed. 城市的偏远地区不太发达.|Synonyms: remote, distant, peripheral, outlying, far-flung|Antonyms: central, inner, nearby|adjective

outlying:/ˈaʊtˌlaɪɪŋ/| adj.|异常的，不同寻常的|Deviating from the norm or average.|The outlying data points suggest an error in the experiment.  异常的数据点表明实验中存在错误.|Synonyms: anomalous, atypical, exceptional, aberrant, deviant|Antonyms: typical, normal, usual, average|adjective
