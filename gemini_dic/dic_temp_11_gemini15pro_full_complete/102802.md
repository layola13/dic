/ˈdʒɑːrɡən/ | n. | 行话，术语 | special words or expressions that are used by a particular profession or group and are difficult for others to understand. |
例句：Medical jargon can be confusing for patients.  医学术语可能会让病人感到困惑。

例句：The lawyer explained the legal jargon to his client. 律师向他的客户解释了法律术语。

近义词：terminology, argot, cant, slang | 反义词：standard language, plain English | 词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *jargoun "twittering, chattering" (12c., Modern French jargon), of imitative origin.
