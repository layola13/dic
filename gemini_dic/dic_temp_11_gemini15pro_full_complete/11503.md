aridity:/əˈrɪdəti/| n.|干旱，干燥|The aridity of the desert makes it difficult for plants to grow. 沙漠的干旱使得植物难以生长。|例句：The prolonged aridity caused a severe drought. 长时间的干旱导致了严重的旱灾。

例句：The aridity of the region necessitates careful water management.  该地区的干旱需要谨慎的水资源管理。

近义词：dryness, drought, barrenness|反义词:humidity, moisture, wetness|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ariditas, from Latin aridus (“dry, parched”), from arēre (“to be dry”).
