alliance: /əˈlaɪəns/ | n. |联盟，同盟，联姻|A union or association formed for mutual benefit, especially between countries or organizations. |  The two countries formed an alliance to defend each other against attack.  这两个国家结成联盟，互相防御攻击。| synonyms: coalition, league, pact, union | antonyms: rivalry, enmity, conflict | noun

alliance: /əˈlaɪəns/ | n. |联系，姻亲关系|A close relationship between families, groups, or individuals. |  His marriage created an alliance between the two families. 他的婚姻使两个家族结成了姻亲关系。 | synonyms: connection, relationship, kinship | antonyms: separation, division | noun
