nightingale:/ˈnaɪtɪŋɡeɪl/ | n. | 夜莺 | A small migratory thrushlike Old World songbird (Luscinia megarhynchos) noted for its rich melodious song, especially at night. |  The nightingale's song filled the night air. 夜莺的歌声充满了夜空。 | synonyms: songbird, thrush | antonyms: crow, raven | noun
