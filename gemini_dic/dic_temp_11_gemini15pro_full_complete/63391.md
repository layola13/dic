entothorax:/ˌɛntoʊˈθɔːræks/ | n. | 内胸；内胸骨 | The entothorax is a plate-like structure within the thorax of some arthropods, particularly insects. |  The insect's entothorax provided structural support for its leg muscles.  昆虫的内胸为它的腿部肌肉提供了结构支撑。 | 近义词:apodeme (in some contexts) | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
