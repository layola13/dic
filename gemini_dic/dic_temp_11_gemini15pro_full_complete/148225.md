oversilently:/ˌoʊvərˈsaɪləntli/ | adv. | 过于安静地，过分沉默地 |  In a manner that is excessively quiet or silent.  Characterized by an unusual or unnatural degree of silence. | She moved oversilently through the room, trying not to disturb the sleeping baby. 她过于安静地穿过房间，尽量不打扰熟睡的婴儿。| The meeting proceeded oversilently, a sign of the tension in the room. 会议进行得异常安静，表明了房间里的紧张气氛。 | 近义词:  too quietly, excessively silently | 反义词:  loudly, noisily | 词性: 副词

词源:  "oversilently" is formed by combining the prefix "over-" (meaning "excessively" or "too much") with the adjective "silent" and the adverbial suffix "-ly."
