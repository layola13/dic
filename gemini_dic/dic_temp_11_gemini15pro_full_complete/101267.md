involucellum:/ɪnvɒljʊˈsɛlʌm/| n. |小总苞；复总苞片|A secondary involucre; a whorl of bracts subtending a partial umbel (a group of flowers) in plants of the Apiaceae (Umbelliferae) or similar families.|The tiny flowers of wild carrot are arranged in umbels surrounded by an involucre of bracts, with each umbellet having its own involucellum.|近义词：bracteole|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, as it's a specific botanical term)|词性:noun
