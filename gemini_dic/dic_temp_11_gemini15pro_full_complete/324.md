abevacuation: /əˌbeɪvæˈkjuːeɪʃən/ | n. |撤离，疏散（尤指肠道）| The process of emptying or clearing out, especially the bowels.  This is a medical term, often used in the context of preparing for surgery or other medical procedures. | The doctor ordered an abevacuation before the colonoscopy. 医生在结肠镜检查前要求进行肠道疏散。|  The patient underwent an abevacuation procedure the day before the surgery. 病人在手术前一天进行了肠道疏散程序。 |  近义词: evacuation, purging, bowel cleansing | 反义词: impaction, constipation | 词性: noun


Etymology:  Derived from the Latin prefix "ab-" (meaning "away from" or "off") + "evacuation" (from Latin "evacuare," meaning "to empty out").
