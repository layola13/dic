antirenter:/ˈæntiˈrɛntər/ | n. | 反租佃者；反对租佃制的人 |  A person opposed to paying rent, especially in the context of historical land tenancy disputes. |  The antirenters of the 19th century fought against unfair lease terms.  19世纪的反租佃者反对不公平的租赁条款。 | near synonyms: tenant activist, agrarian reformer | antonym: landlord, lessor | noun
