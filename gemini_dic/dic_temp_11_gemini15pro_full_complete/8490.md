antibillboard:/ˌæntiˈbɪlbɔːrd/
n.|反广告牌；反对广告牌者|A person or thing that opposes billboards or their proliferation.  A sign or message designed to counter the message of a billboard.|The group staged an antibillboard protest outside city hall.  这个团体在市政厅外举行了反广告牌抗议活动。|Synonyms: billboard opponent, anti-advertising activist|Antonyms: billboard supporter, pro-advertising activist|noun
antibillboard:/ˌæntiˈbɪlbɔːrd/
adj.|反对广告牌的|Opposed to billboards. |He had strong antibillboard views. 他强烈反对广告牌。|Synonyms:  anti-advertising|Antonyms: pro-billboard, pro-advertising|adjective
