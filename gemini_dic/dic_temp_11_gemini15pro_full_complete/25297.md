bugged:/bʌɡd/
adjective|被窃听的；烦扰的；有缺陷的|
equipped with a concealed microphone or other electronic device for the purpose of eavesdropping; annoyed, irritated, or bothered; containing or affected by bugs (errors in computer software)|
例句：My phone is bugged. 我的手机被窃听了。

例句：I'm bugged by all the noise. 我被所有的噪音烦扰着。

近义词：annoyed, irritated, bothered, bugged, infested|
反义词：calm, pleased, content, delighted|
词性：adjective

bugged:/bʌɡd/
verb|窃听；烦扰；纠缠|past tense and past participle of "bug", meaning to annoy, irritate, bother, or pester someone; also to install a listening device in a place|
例句：They bugged his office. 他们窃听了他的办公室。

例句：She bugged me to take her to the movies. 她缠着我带她去看电影。

近义词：annoy, irritate, bother, pester, harass, wiretap|
反义词：soothe, please, delight, comfort|
词性：verb
