Habsburg: /ˈhæpsbɜːrɡ/ | n. | 哈布斯堡王朝；哈布斯堡家族成员 | A member of the royal German family that formerly ruled Austria, Spain, and the Holy Roman Empire. |  The Habsburgs ruled over vast territories for centuries. 哈布斯堡王朝统治了广阔的领土数个世纪。| synonym:  Austrian dynasty, Spanish dynasty | antonym: N/A | noun

Etymology: From German *Habsburg*, from the name of their original castle, Habichtsburg ("Hawk's Castle") in Switzerland.
