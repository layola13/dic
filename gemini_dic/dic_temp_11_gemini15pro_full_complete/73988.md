fortress:/ˈfɔːrtrəs/ (US) /ˈfɔːtrɪs/ (UK)| n. |堡垒；要塞；防御工事|A fortified place, especially a large military stronghold. |例句：The old castle stands as a silent fortress on the hill.  古老的城堡像一座沉默的堡垒矗立在山上。

例句：The city was once a mighty fortress, impenetrable to enemies. 这座城市曾经是一座强大的要塞，敌人无法攻破。|近义词：stronghold, citadel, bastion, fortification|反义词:vulnerability, weakness|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *forteresce* (Modern French *forteresse*), from *fort* ("strong") + *-esse* (a suffix forming feminine nouns). Ultimately from Latin *fortis* ("strong").
