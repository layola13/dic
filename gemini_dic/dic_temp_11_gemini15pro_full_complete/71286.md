fixedness:/ˈfɪkstnɪs/| n.|固定性，固执，僵化|The state of being fixed or unchanging; inflexibility.|His fixedness on the idea prevented him from considering other options. 他对这个想法的固执让他无法考虑其他选择。|rigidity, inflexibility, stubbornness|adaptability, flexibility|noun
Etymology->
From "fixed" + "-ness". "Fixed" comes from the Latin "fixus," the past participle of "figere" (to fix, fasten). The suffix "-ness" is used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
