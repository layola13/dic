ballasted:/ˈbæləstɪd/| adj.|加压舱的，装有压舱物的，使稳定|Loaded with ballast for stability.  Provided with ballast to improve stability, performance, or handling.|The ship was ballasted with seawater. 这艘船用海水压舱。|近义词：stabilized, weighted, balanced|反义词：unstable, top-heavy|词性:形容词
Etymology: From ballast (n.) + -ed.  Ballast originally referred to sand or gravel carried in a ship's hold to provide stability. It comes from the Middle Low German word *ballast, perhaps from bal "bad" + last "load" (originally referring to something worthless used as cargo).
