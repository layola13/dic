nondepletion:/ˌnɒndɪˈpliːʃən/ | n. | 非消耗，非损耗，不枯竭 | The nondepletion of natural resources is crucial for sustainable development.  |  The sanctuary ensures the nondepletion of the endangered species. | 近义词: conservation, preservation, maintenance | 反义词: depletion, exhaustion, consumption | 词性: 名词

词源: non- (prefix) + depletion

non-:  from Latin *non, from ne "not" + oinom "one."  Meaning "not, not a, no."

depletion: from Late Latin depletionem (nominative depletio) "an emptying," noun of action from past-participle stem of deplere "to empty out," from de- "completely" (see de-) + plere "to fill"
