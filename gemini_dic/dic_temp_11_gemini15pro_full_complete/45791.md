词源：From Middle English *cussen, *kissen, from Old Norse kiosa (“to choose, test, examine”), from Proto-Germanic *keusaną (“to choose, test, taste”).  Originally meant "to curse" in the sense of invoking evil upon someone. Later broadened to mean any kind of profane or abusive language.
