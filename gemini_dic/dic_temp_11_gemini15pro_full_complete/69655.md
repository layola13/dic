fence-mendings:/fɛnsˈmɛndɪŋz/| n.|修补栅栏; 弥合分歧；和解|The act of repairing fences, both literally and figuratively.  It can refer to improving relationships after a disagreement.|After their argument, they engaged in some fence-mendings to restore their friendship.  他们争吵之后，努力修补关系以重归于好。|近义词：reconciliation, rapprochement, appeasement|反义词：estrangement, alienation|noun
