multigrid:/ˈmʌltɪɡrɪd/| adj. n.|多重网格的，多重网格法|A method for solving differential equations that uses a hierarchy of grids with different resolutions.  It involves transferring solutions between these grids to accelerate convergence. |The multigrid method is highly effective for solving large systems of equations.|近义词:multilevel, hierarchical|反义词:single-grid|词性:形容词, 名词

Etymology: "multi-" (from Latin "multus" meaning "many, much") + "grid" (referring to a network or mesh)
