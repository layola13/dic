boasters:/ˈboʊstərz/| n. |自夸者，吹牛者|People who brag or boast excessively about their accomplishments or possessions.  Those who habitually exaggerate their achievements.|The room was full of boasters, each trying to outdo the other with tales of their success. 房间里全是吹牛的人，每个人都试图用他们成功的故事胜过别人。|synonyms: braggarts, blowhards, braggers, show-offs|antonyms: humble people, modest individuals|noun
boast:/boʊst/| v. |自夸，吹嘘|To talk with excessive pride and self-satisfaction about one's achievements, possessions, or abilities.|He boasted about his new car. 他吹嘘他的新车。|synonyms: brag, crow, swagger, show off|antonyms: downplay, minimize, deprecate|verb
boastful:/ˈboʊstfəl/| adj. |爱吹嘘的，自夸的|Given to or characterized by boasting; bragging.|He had a boastful manner that irritated many people.  他有一种爱吹嘘的态度，让很多人恼火。|synonyms: bragging, swaggering, vainglorious, conceited|antonyms: humble, modest, unassuming|adjective
