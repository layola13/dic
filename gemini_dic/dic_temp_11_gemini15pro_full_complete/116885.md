maybird:/ˈmeɪbɜːrd/| n.|五月鸟，欧洲的一种鸣禽，学名whimbrel (Numenius phaeopus)|A migratory wading bird, the whimbrel, known for its distinctive call, often heard in May.|例句：We heard the maybird's distinctive call as we walked along the coast. 我们沿着海岸走的时候听到了五月鸟独特的叫声。

例句：The maybird is a common sight in coastal areas during migration. 五月鸟在迁徙期间是沿海地区的常见景象。|近义词：whimbrel, curlew|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
