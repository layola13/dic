gaudless:/ˈɡɔːdləs/
adjective
无欢乐的，沉闷的，不快乐的
Lacking gaudiness or ostentation; without showiness or extravagance; also, lacking joy or pleasure; dull.
The gaudless room was sparsely furnished and dimly lit.  这间朴素的房间陈设简陋，灯光昏暗。
The funeral was a gaudless affair, reflecting the family's somber mood. 葬礼办得很朴素，反映了家人的悲痛心情。
近义词:drab, dull, somber, bleak, austere
反义词:gaudy, showy, flamboyant, cheerful, joyful
词性:adjective

词源:
From gaud + -less.  Gaud comes from Middle English gaude, from Old North French gaude, of Germanic origin.
