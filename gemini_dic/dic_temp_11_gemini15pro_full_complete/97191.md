inflamed:/ɪnˈfleɪmd/| adj.|发炎的，红肿的；激动的|Red, swollen, and painful, especially as a result of infection or injury. Also, (of a person or their feelings) roused to anger or another strong emotion.|例句：The wound became inflamed and infected. 伤口发炎感染了。|近义词：irritated, swollen, aggravated, angered, enraged|反义词：soothed, calmed, healthy|词性:形容词

inflamed:/ɪnˈfleɪmd/| adj.|激动的，激愤的|roused to anger or another strong emotion|例句：His inflamed rhetoric only served to worsen the situation.  他激烈的言辞只会让情况更糟。|近义词：angered, enraged, furious, indignant|反义词：calm, peaceful, tranquil|词性:形容词
