hour:/aʊər/ (US), /ˈaʊə(r)/ (UK)| n.|小时|A period of time equal to 60 minutes; one twenty-fourth of a day.|例句：The meeting lasted for two hours. 会议持续了两个小时。

例句：We worked for eight hours straight. 我们连续工作了八个小时。

近义词： sixty minutes |反义词: minute, second |词性: noun
