buddiest:/ˈbʌdiɪst/
adjective
最亲密的，最友好的
(superlative of) buddy: a close friend.  Implies a strong sense of camaraderie and affection.
He's my buddiest mate. 他是我最铁的哥们。

近义词:closest, dearest, thickest
反义词:distant, aloof, unfriendly
词性:adjective
