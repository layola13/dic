overrefined:/ˌoʊvərɪˈfaɪnd/| adj.|过度精炼的；矫揉造作的；过分精致的|Having been refined to an excessive degree, often resulting in a loss of desirable qualities or a sense of artificiality.|The wine was so overrefined that it lacked any real character. 这款酒过于精炼，以至于缺乏真正的特色。|The dialogue felt overrefined and unnatural.  这段对话感觉矫揉造作，很不自然。|近义词：overdone, pretentious, artificial, elaborate|反义词：natural, simple, unrefined, crude|词性:形容词

词源：
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "refined" (past participle of "refine")
