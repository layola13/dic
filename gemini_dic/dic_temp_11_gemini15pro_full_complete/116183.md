maser:/ˈmeɪzər/| n.|微波激射器；量子放大器|A maser is a device that produces coherent electromagnetic waves through amplification by stimulated emission.  It's similar to a laser, but operates at microwave frequencies.|Scientists are exploring the use of masers in deep space communication. 科学家正在探索微波激射器在深空通信中的应用。

The observatory uses a highly sensitive maser amplifier.  该天文台使用高灵敏度的微波激射器放大器。|近义词:laser (in principle)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
