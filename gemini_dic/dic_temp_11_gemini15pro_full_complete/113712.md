macro-scale:/ˈmækroʊ skeɪl/ | adj. |宏观的，大规模的 | Relating to or involving large units or elements; large-scale. |  The macro-scale effects of climate change are becoming increasingly apparent. 气候变化的宏观影响正变得越来越明显。

| The study examined the problem from a macro-scale perspective, considering global economic trends. 这项研究从宏观角度考察了这个问题，考虑了全球经济趋势。 | 近义词: large-scale, global, overall, wide-ranging | 反义词: micro-scale, small-scale, localized | 词性: adjective

macro-scale:/ˈmækroʊ skeɪl/ | n. |宏观规模 | The large scale or scope of something. |  The project operates at a macro-scale, involving multiple countries and organizations.  该项目在宏观层面上运作，涉及多个国家和组织。

|  We need to consider the macro-scale implications of this decision. 我们需要考虑这个决定的宏观影响。 | 近义词: large scale, broad scope, wide range | 反义词: micro-scale, small scale, limited scope | 词性: noun


词源:  "macro-" comes from the Greek "makrós" meaning "long" or "large". "Scale" comes from the Old Norse "skal" meaning "bowl" or "scale of a balance."
