dtype:/ˈdeɪtaɪp/ | n. | 数据类型 | Data type refers to the kind of values a variable can hold in programming.  It determines the operations that can be performed on the data and how the data is stored in memory. |  Example: The dtype of this variable is 'integer'.  这个变量的数据类型是“整数”。 | Synonym: type, kind, class | Antonym: N/A | Noun
