lump-together:/lʌmp təˈɡeðər/
|phrasal verb|
|集中在一起；合并；归并|
|To treat or consider different things together as a single unit.|
|They lumped together all the expenses for the month. 他们把这个月的所有开销都汇总在一起。|

|The company decided to lump together all its smaller branches. 公司决定将所有较小的分支机构合并。|

|combine, group, merge, consolidate, amalgamate|
|separate, divide, distinguish, differentiate|
|phrasal verb|
