loophole:/ˈluːphəʊl/ (UK) /ˈluːphoʊl/ (US)| n. |漏洞，空子|An ambiguity or inadequacy in the law or a set of rules.|The company exploited a loophole in environmental regulations.  这家公司利用了环境法规中的一个漏洞。|Synonyms: gap, opening, flaw, crack, escape clause|Antonyms: closure, seal, block, restriction|noun
