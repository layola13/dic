impower:/ɪmˈpaʊər/| vt.|授权，使能够|To give someone the authority or power to do something.  To enable or permit someone to do something.|例句：The new law impowers the police to search any vehicle. 新法律授权警察搜查任何车辆。

例句：Education impowers people to make better choices. 教育使人们能够做出更好的选择。|近义词：authorize, enable, empower|反义词: restrict, prohibit, prevent|词性:动词
