assiduousnesses:/ˌæsɪˈdjuːəsnəsɪz/
n.
勤奋，刻苦，专心致志
The quality of being assiduous; constant and careful attention.
Her assiduousnesses in her studies resulted in excellent grades. 她学习的勤奋使她取得了优异的成绩。
Synonym: diligence, perseverance, industry
Antonym: laziness, negligence, apathy
noun
