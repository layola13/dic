charbon:/ʃɑːrˈbɒn/ (UK), /ʃɑːrˈboʊn/ (US)| n.|木炭; 炭; 煤|A black or dark-brown combustible carbonaceous material formed by the partial combustion or destructive distillation of wood or other organic matter.|例句：The artist used charcoal to sketch the portrait. 艺术家用木炭画了肖像。

例句：They burned charcoal to keep warm. 他们烧木炭取暖。|近义词：coal, carbon, ember|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "fuelwood" as a source before charring)|词性:noun
