nonaccessories:/nɒnəkˈsɛsəriz/ | n. | 非配件；非附件 | Items that are not considered accessories.  These are essential components or parts, as opposed to optional add-ons. | The engine is a nonaccessory; it's essential for the car to function.  发动机是非配件；它是汽车运转必不可少的。

The frame of the bicycle is a nonaccessory; you can't ride without it. 自行车车架是非配件；没有它你就无法骑行。 | near synonyms: essentials, necessities, main components | near antonyms: accessories, add-ons, options | noun
