fissural:/ˈfɪʃərəl/
adjective
裂缝的；裂隙的
Relating to, formed in, or occurring in a fissure or fissures.
A fissural eruption occurred along the volcano's flank. 火山的侧面出现了裂隙喷发。
The dentist identified a fissural cavity in the patient's molar. 牙医在病人的臼齿上发现了一个裂缝状的龋洞。
近义词: cleft, cracked, fractured
反义词: seamless, whole, intact
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *fissūrālis, from fissūra (“fissure, cleft”) + -ālis (adjective suffix).  The Latin fissūra is from findō ("to cleave, split").
