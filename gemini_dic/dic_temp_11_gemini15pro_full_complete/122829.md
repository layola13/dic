moleskin: /ˈmoʊlskɪn/ (American) /ˈməʊlskɪn/ (British)
n.|鼹鼠皮；仿鼹鼠皮的厚棉布|A heavy, durable cotton fabric with a soft, suede-like nap, used for clothing, book covers, etc., originally made to resemble moleskin. |She bought a moleskin notebook to jot down her thoughts. 她买了一个鼹鼠皮笔记本记下她的想法。|Synonyms: suede, corduroy, velveteen|Antonyms: silk, satin|noun

moleskin: /ˈmoʊlskɪn/ (American) /ˈməʊlskɪn/ (British)
adj.|仿鼹鼠皮的|Made of or resembling moleskin. |He wore a moleskin jacket on the chilly evening.  在寒冷的夜晚，他穿了一件仿鼹鼠皮的夹克。|Synonyms: suede-like, velvety|Antonyms: smooth, silky|adjective
