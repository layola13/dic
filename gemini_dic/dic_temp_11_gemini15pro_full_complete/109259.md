lemman:/ˈlɛmən/| n.|引理，辅助定理|A subsidiary proposition assumed to be valid and used to demonstrate a principal proposition.  A lemma is a proven statement used as a stepping-stone toward the proof of a larger result.|Example: The proof of the main theorem relies on several lemmas.  这个主要定理的证明依赖于几个引理。|Example: This lemma is crucial for establishing the convergence of the series. 这个引理对于建立级数的收敛性至关重要。|近义词：theorem, proposition, corollary|反义词: (No direct antonym exists for lemma)|词性:noun
