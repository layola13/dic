crochetings: /kroʊˈʃeɪɪŋz/ | n. |钩编物，钩针编织品|The process of creating fabric by interlocking loops of yarn, thread, or other material using a crochet hook.  It also refers to the items made using this technique.| She proudly displayed her crochetings at the craft fair. 她自豪地在工艺品展览会上展示了她的钩针编织作品。| 近义词：crochet work, knitting | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun (usually plural)

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *crocheter*, meaning "to hook," derived from *crochet* "small hook," a diminutive of *croc* "hook." Ultimately from Old Norse *krókr*.
