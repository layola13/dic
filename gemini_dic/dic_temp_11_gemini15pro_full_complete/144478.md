outthreaten:/ˌaʊtˈθrɛtn/ | v. | 比...更具威胁性；构成更大的威胁 | to pose a greater threat than; to threaten more effectively than |  The new virus outthreatens all previous strains.  这种新病毒比所有以前的毒株都更具威胁性。| Example: The rising floodwaters outthreatened the levees, forcing a mandatory evacuation.  上涨的洪水对堤坝构成了更大的威胁，迫使强制疏散。 | Synonyms: surpass, eclipse, overshadow, outdo, exceed | Antonyms: protect, defend, safeguard| verb
