mis-registers:/ˌmɪsˈrɛdʒɪstərz/ | v. | 错误地登记，对位不准，套印不准 | To register incorrectly or inaccurately; to fail to align properly. | The printing press mis-registered the colors, resulting in a blurry image. 印刷机颜色套印不准，导致图像模糊。| The camera mis-registered the image, causing it to appear distorted. 相机图像对位不准，导致图像出现失真。 | synonyms: misalign, misplace, err | antonyms: align, register correctly | verb

mis-register:/ˌmɪsˈrɛdʒɪstər/ | n. |  错误登记，对位不准，套印不准 | An instance of incorrect registration or alignment. | The mis-register caused the image to be unusable. 对位不准导致图像无法使用。 | There was a slight mis-register in the printing, making the text difficult to read. 印刷中出现了轻微的套印不准，使得文本难以阅读。| synonyms: misalignment, error | antonyms: alignment, correct registration | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"mis-" (Old English, prefix meaning "wrongly, badly, amiss") + "register" (from Old French "registre," ultimately from Latin "regerere" - to carry back, record).  The prefix "mis-" indicates an error or fault in the action of registering.
