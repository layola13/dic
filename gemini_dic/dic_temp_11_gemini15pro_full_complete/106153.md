knobbliest:/ˈnɒb.li.ɪst/| adj.|多节的，疙瘩最多的|Having the most or largest knobs; most knobbly.|The knobbliest tree in the forest was home to a family of owls. 森林里疙瘩最多的树是猫头鹰的家。|近义词：gnarled, knotty, lumpy|反义词:smooth, even|词性:形容词
词源: From knob + -y + -est.  "Knob" comes from Middle Low German knobbe, meaning a bump or protuberance.
