buffoonism:/bəˈfuːnɪzəm/
n.
滑稽可笑的行为；丑行
The behavior of a buffoon; ludicrous or foolish behavior.
His buffoonism embarrassed his family. 他的滑稽行为让他的家人感到尴尬。

近义词:clowning, silliness, foolery, tomfoolery, absurdity
反义词:seriousness, solemnity, dignity, gravity
词性:noun

词源:From French *buffonisme*, from Italian *buffonismo*, from *buffone* "jester, clown," of uncertain origin, perhaps from *buffa* "a puff of wind, a comic interlude."
