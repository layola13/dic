buoy:/bɔɪ/ /buːi/| n. vt.|浮标，救生圈；使振作，鼓励|A floating object anchored in water to mark a location or a hazard, or to support a mooring.  Also, to keep afloat or uplift in spirits.|The bright red buoy marked the entrance to the harbor.  鲜红色的浮标标志着港口的入口。|近义词：float, lifebuoy, marker, encourage, uplift|反义词:sink, depress|n.

buoy:/bɔɪ/ /buːi/| vt.|使振作，鼓励|To keep afloat or uplift in spirits.|The good news buoyed their hopes.  好消息使他们振作起来。|近义词：encourage, uplift, hearten, inspire|反义词:depress, discourage, dishearten|vt.
