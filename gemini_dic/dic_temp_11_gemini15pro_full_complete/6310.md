amortize: /ˈæmərtaɪz/ (US) /əˈmɔːtaɪz/ (UK)| v. |摊销，分期偿还| To gradually reduce or write off the cost of an asset over a period of time.  |The company is amortizing the cost of new equipment over five years.  这家公司正在将新设备的成本在五年内摊销。

|  We amortized the loan over ten years. 我们在十年内分期偿还了贷款。


|近义词：depreciate, write off|反义词: appreciate|词性: 动词
