erectnesses:/ɪˈrɛktnɪsɪz/
n.
直立, 竖立; 挺拔
The state of being erect or upright.  The quality of being rigid or firm.
The erectnesses of the soldiers' postures impressed the visiting general. 士兵们笔挺的姿势给来访的将军留下了深刻的印象。
The scaffolding's erectnesses was crucial for the safety of the workers. 脚手架的竖直对于工人的安全至关重要。
近义词: uprightness, verticality, rigidity, firmness
反义词: flaccidity, limpness
