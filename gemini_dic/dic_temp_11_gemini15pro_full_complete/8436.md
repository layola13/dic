antiaggressively:/ˌæntiəˈɡrɛsɪvli/
| adv. |反侵略地；非侵略性地；温和地|Acting in a way that avoids or opposes aggression; not aggressive. | He responded antiaggressively to the provocation, choosing to walk away rather than escalate the situation. 他对挑衅的反应是非侵略性的，选择走开而不是使局势升级。| synonym: peacefully, passively, nonaggressively, nonviolently, conciliatorily | antonym: aggressively, belligerently, hostilely |词性: 副词
