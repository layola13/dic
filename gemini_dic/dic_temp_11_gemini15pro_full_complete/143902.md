outpracticing:/ˌaʊtˈpræktɪsɪŋ/
verb
通过更多的练习胜过
surpassing someone in skill or performance by practicing more.
例句：She outpracticed her rivals and won the competition. 她比对手练习得更多，赢得了比赛。
例句：He outpracticed everyone in the orchestra and earned the first chair. 他比乐队里的每个人都练习得更多，赢得了首席的位置。
近义词：outperforming, exceeding, surpassing
反义词：underperforming, lagging behind
词性：v.
