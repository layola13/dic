aldermanships: /ˈɔːldərmənʃɪps/
n.
市議員的職位; 市議員的任期
The position or term of office of an alderman.
The mayor's controversial policies led to several aldermanships being challenged in the next election.  市长备受争议的政策导致下届选举中几个市议员席位受到挑战。
Several aldermanships are up for grabs in the upcoming local elections.  即将举行的地方选举中，有几个市议员席位待价而沽。
近义词: aldermanries, council memberships
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider perhaps "mayoralty" as a contrasting position)
词性: noun
