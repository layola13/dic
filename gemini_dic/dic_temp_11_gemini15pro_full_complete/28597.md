careless: /ˈkɛrləs/ | adj. |粗心的，漫不经心的 | Not giving sufficient attention or thought to avoiding harm or errors. |  She made a careless mistake and lost the game. 她犯了一个粗心的错误，输掉了比赛。|  negligent, thoughtless, inattentive, reckless | careful, cautious, attentive, meticulous | adjective

Etymology: From *care* (Old English *caru*, from Proto-Germanic *karō*) + *-less* (Old English *-lēas*, meaning "without").
