extractive:/ɪkˈstræktɪv/| adj.|提取的，萃取的|Relating to or involving extraction, especially the extraction of raw materials from the earth.|例句：Extractive industries, such as mining and oil drilling, can have a significant environmental impact. 采掘业，如采矿和石油钻探，会对环境产生重大影响。|例句：The company uses an extractive process to obtain the essential oils from plants.  这家公司使用萃取工艺从植物中提取精油。|近义词：extracted, mining, quarrying|反义词:synthetic, manufactured|词性:adjective

extractive:/ɪkˈstræktɪv/| n.|提取物，萃取物|A substance extracted from something else.|例句：The extractive from the plant has medicinal properties.  这种植物的提取物具有药用价值。|例句：Vanilla extractive is a common ingredient in baking. 香草提取物是烘焙中常见的成分。|近义词：extract, concentrate, essence|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
