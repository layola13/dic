doomsaying:/ˈduːmseɪɪŋ/| n.| 末日预言，灾难预言 |The constant doomsaying about the economy is depressing.  不断地对经济做出末日预言令人沮丧。|The media's doomsaying about the virus caused widespread panic. 媒体对病毒的灾难预言造成了 widespread 恐慌。|近义词：pessimism, foreboding, prophecy of doom|反义词：optimism, hopefulness|词性：名词

doomsaying:/ˈduːmseɪɪŋ/| adj.| 预言灾难的，悲观的 |His doomsaying pronouncements irritated many. 他预言灾难的言论激怒了很多人。|Her doomsaying attitude made it difficult to plan for the future. 她悲观的态度使得难以规划未来。|近义词：pessimistic, fatalistic|反义词：optimistic, hopeful|词性：形容词

词源: From "doom" (fate, destiny, especially a bad one) + "saying" (the act of speaking or uttering words).  "Doom" comes from Old English "dōm" (judgment, decree).
