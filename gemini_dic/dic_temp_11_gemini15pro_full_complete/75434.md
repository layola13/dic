fruitery:/ˈfruːtəri/| n. |水果店；果树园；水果|A shop that sells fruit; a place where fruit trees are grown; fruit collectively. |例句：She bought a basket of ripe peaches at the local fruitery.  她在当地的水果店买了一篮子熟桃子。

例句：The old fruitery was filled with the sweet scent of ripening apples.  旧果树园里充满了成熟苹果的甜香味。|近义词：greengrocer, fruit stand, orchard |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
