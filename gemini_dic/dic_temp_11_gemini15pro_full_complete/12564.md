aspirement:/əˈspaɪərmənt/ | n. |渴望，抱负，志向 |A strong desire to achieve something high or great; an ambition. |例句：Her aspiration is to become a doctor. 她渴望成为一名医生。

例句：He has high aspirations for his children. 他对他的孩子们寄予厚望。

近义词：ambition, goal, desire, dream, objective |反义词：apathy, indifference, lack of ambition |词性：名词
