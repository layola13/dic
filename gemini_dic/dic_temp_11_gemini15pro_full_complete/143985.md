outranking:/ˌaʊtˈræŋkɪŋ/
verb
超过，级别高于
Surpassing in rank or importance.
Our team is outranking all the others in the league this year. 今年我们队在联赛中超过了所有其他队伍。
The new software is outranking its competitors in terms of speed and efficiency.  这款新软件在速度和效率方面超过了竞争对手。
近义词: surpassing, exceeding, outstripping, bettering
反义词: lagging behind, trailing, being surpassed
词性: verb
