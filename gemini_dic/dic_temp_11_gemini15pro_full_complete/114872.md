mammonistic:/ˌmæməˈnɪstɪk/
adjective
拜金的；贪婪的
Characterized by a devotion to material wealth and possessions, especially excessive devotion.
He lived a mammonistic lifestyle, always chasing the next big paycheck. 他过着拜金的生活，总是追逐着下一份巨额薪水。
The mammonistic businessman cared little for the environment, only for profit.  这位拜金的商人对环境漠不关心，只在乎利润。
近义词: greedy, avaricious, materialistic, mercenary
反义词: altruistic, charitable, generous, philanthropic
词性: adjective

词源:
From Mammon, the Aramaic word for wealth, personified in the New Testament as a false god, representing riches and avarice.
