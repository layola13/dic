hackbolt:/ˈhækboʊlt/| n. | 安全螺栓，防盗螺栓 | A type of bolt designed to be difficult or impossible to remove without specialized tools, often used to deter theft or vandalism. | The bike rack was secured with hackbolts to prevent theft. 自行车架用防盗螺栓固定以防被盗。| synonyms: security bolt, tamper-proof bolt, anti-theft bolt | antonyms: standard bolt | noun
