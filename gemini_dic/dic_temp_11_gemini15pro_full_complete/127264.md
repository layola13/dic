navaratri:/nʌvəˈrɑːtri/ | n. |九夜节（印度教节日）| A Hindu festival extending over nine nights, celebrated differently in various parts of India but always in honor of a form of the goddess Durga. |  Example 1:  We celebrate Navaratri with fasting and prayer. 我们通过斋戒和祈祷来庆祝九夜节。

Example 2:  The vibrant dances during Navaratri are a sight to behold. 九夜节期间充满活力的舞蹈是一道亮丽的风景线。 | 近义词: Durga Puja (in some regions) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
