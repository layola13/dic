misadjustments:/ˌmɪsəˈdʒʌstmənts/
| n. |失调，错位，误调|A failure to adjust correctly or a state of being poorly adjusted.  A condition in which something is not properly adjusted.|
The therapist helped the patient address their emotional misadjustments.  这位治疗师帮助病人解决了他们的情绪失调。
Economic misadjustments led to the financial crisis. 经济失调导致了金融危机。|
近义词:maladjustments, imbalances, discrepancies, disharmonies|
反义词:adjustments, harmonies, balances|
词性:名词
