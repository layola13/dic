emulsification's:/ɪˌmʌlsɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 乳化作用 | The process of dispersing one liquid in another immiscible liquid, creating a stable mixture. | The emulsification's of fats by bile salts is crucial for digestion. 胆汁盐对脂肪的乳化作用对消化至关重要。 | 近义词: homogenization, blending | 反义词: separation, demulsification | noun

词源：From emulsify + -ation.  Emulsify comes from French émulsionner, from Latin emulgere "to milk out," from ex- "out" + mulgere "to milk."
