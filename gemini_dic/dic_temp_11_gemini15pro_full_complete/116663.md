mattings:/ˈmætɪŋz/
n.
席子；垫子；衬垫；无光泽的表面
coarse, woven or plaited fabric used for floor coverings, mats, etc. or as a protective covering.
The floor was covered with straw mattings. 地板上铺着草席。
近义词:mats, rugs, carpets, pads, coverings
反义词:gloss, shine

matting:/ˈmætɪŋ/
n.
席子；垫子；衬垫；无光泽的表面
coarse, woven or plaited fabric used for floor coverings, mats, etc. or as a protective covering.
The bird used bits of matting to build its nest. 鸟用了一些垫子来筑巢。
近义词:mat, rug, carpet, pad, covering
反义词:gloss, shine
