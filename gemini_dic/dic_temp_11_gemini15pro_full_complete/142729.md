otolaryngologies:/ˌoʊtəˌlærɪŋˈɡɒlədʒiz/ | n. | 耳鼻喉科学 | The study and treatment of diseases of the ear, nose, and throat. | He specialized in otolaryngologies and was known for his skill in treating complex ear infections. 他专攻耳鼻喉科，并以其治疗复杂耳部感染的技巧而闻名。 | 近义词: ENT (Ear, Nose, and Throat), otorhinolaryngology | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun
词源:  From oto- + laryng- + -ology + -es (plural).  "Oto-" comes from Greek "ous," meaning "ear." "Laryng-" comes from Greek "larynx," meaning "larynx" or "voice box." "-ology" signifies the study of a subject. "-es" marks the plural form.
