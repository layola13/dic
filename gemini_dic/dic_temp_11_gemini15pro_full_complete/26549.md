cabinetwork: /ˈkæbɪnɪtwɜːrk/ (US) /ˈkæbɪnɪtwɜːk/ (UK)
n.|细木工制品；橱柜制造；细木工|The detailed cabinetwork in the library was impressive. 图书馆里精细的细木工制品令人印象深刻。|English Explanation:  The craft of making furniture, especially cabinets and other fine woodwork, or the furniture itself.|例句：The antique desk was a beautiful piece of cabinetwork. 这张古董桌子是一件漂亮的细木工制品。|近义词：joinery, woodwork, carpentry|反义词: N/A|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From cabinet + work.  "Cabinet" ultimately derives from the Old North French *cabinet, diminutive of cabane "cabin, hut". "Work" comes from the Old English weorc, meaning "work, action, deed."
