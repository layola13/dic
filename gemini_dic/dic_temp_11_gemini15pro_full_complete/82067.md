grittiest: /ˈɡrɪtiɪst/
adjective
最坚毅的；最有砂砾感的；最粗糙的
Superlative adjective of gritty: having or showing courage and resolve.  Also, containing or resembling grit; coarse and rough.
The grittiest boxer I've ever seen kept fighting even with a broken rib.  我见过的最有毅力的拳击手即使肋骨断了也继续战斗。

The grittiest part of town was riddled with crime.  这个城镇最粗糙的地区充满了犯罪。

近义词: toughest, most resolute, most determined, roughest, coarsest
反义词: weakest, most cowardly, smoothest, finest
词性: adjective
词源: From *grit* (n.) + *-y* (adj. suffix) + *-est* (superlative suffix). *Grit* comes from Old English *grytt*, meaning "sand, gravel."
