noncovetously: /ˌnɒnkʌvɪtəsli/
adverb
非贪婪地，不贪图地
Without covetousness; in a way that does not involve longing for something that belongs to someone else.
He gave noncovetously to the charity, seeking no recognition for his generosity. 他慷慨地向慈善机构捐款，不求任何回报。
She admired the beautiful dress noncovetously, appreciating its beauty without wanting it for herself.  她欣赏着那条漂亮的裙子，并不贪图拥有它。
近义词: generously, unselfishly, altruistically
反义词: covetously, greedily, selfishly
词性: adverb

词源:
From non- + covetous + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Covetous" comes from Old French "coveitos," ultimately from Latin "cupidus," meaning "desirous."  "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.
