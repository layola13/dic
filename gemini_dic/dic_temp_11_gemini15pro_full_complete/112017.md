long-livedness:/ˌlɔːŋˈlɪvdnəs/ /ˌlɑːŋˈlɪvdnəs/| n.|长寿，寿命长|The study explores the factors contributing to the long-livedness of certain species.  这项研究探索了某些物种长寿的因素。|例句：The long-livedness of giant tortoises is well documented. 巨型陆龟的长寿是有据可查的。

The secret to their long-livedness might lie in their diet. 他们长寿的秘诀可能在于他们的饮食。|近义词：longevity, durability|反义词：short life, brevity|词性:noun

Etymology:  From long-lived + -ness.  "long-lived" dates back to the late 14th century, meaning "having a long life." "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, denoting a state, quality, or condition.
