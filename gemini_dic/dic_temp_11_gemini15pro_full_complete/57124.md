dove:/dʌv/ | n. | 鸽子；和平的象征 | A bird with a stout body and short legs, known for its soft cooing.  It is often a symbol of peace. |  A white dove flew overhead.  一只白鸽从头顶飞过。| synonyms: pigeon, peace symbol | antonyms: hawk, war symbol | noun


dove:/doʊv/ | v. (past tense of dive) | 跳水；俯冲 |  Past tense of "dive," meaning to plunge headfirst into water or to descend steeply and rapidly. | He dove into the pool. 他跳进了游泳池。 | synonyms: plunged, dived | antonyms: surfaced, ascended | verb
