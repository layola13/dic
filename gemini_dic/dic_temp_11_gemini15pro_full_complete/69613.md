feminate:/ˈfɛmɪˌneɪt/ | v. | 使女性化；使柔弱 | To make feminine; to make effeminate. | He felt the culture was feminating the boys. 他觉得这种文化使男孩们女性化了。|  They tried to feminate him by dressing him in girl's clothing. 他们试图通过给他穿上女孩的衣服来让他女性化。| 近义词: emasculate, weaken, soften | 反义词: masculinize, strengthen, harden |词性: 动词

feminate:/ˈfɛmɪnɪt/ | adj. | 女性化的；柔弱的 | Having feminine qualities considered undesirable in a man. | He had a feminate voice and manner. 他的声音和举止都很女性化。| He was criticized for his feminate appearance. 他因女性化的外表而受到批评。 | 近义词: effeminate, womanly, unmanly | 反义词: masculine, manly, virile | 词性: 形容词
