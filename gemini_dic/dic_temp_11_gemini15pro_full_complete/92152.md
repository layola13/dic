hypersensuously:/ˌhaɪpərˈsɛnsjuəsli/
adjective
过度敏感地，感觉过分敏锐地
in a hypersensuous manner; with excessive sensitivity.
Her skin reacted hypersensuously to the slightest touch. 她的皮肤对最轻微的触碰都过度敏感。
Near Antonyms: insensitively, indifferently
Far Antonyms: numbly, apathetically
词性: adverb
