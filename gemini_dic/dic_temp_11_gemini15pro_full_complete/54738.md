disparately:/dɪˈspærətli/| adv.|不同地，悬殊地|In a way that is markedly different; widely.|The twins, though identical, were disparately different in their personalities. 这对双胞胎虽然长得很像，但性格却截然不同。|近义词：differently, distinctly, unequally, variably|反义词：similarly, uniformly, homogeneously|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *disparatus, from Latin dis- + paratus (“prepared, ready”).  Influenced by disparate. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *peh₂- (“to protect, feed”).
