checkout:/ˈtʃekaʊt/ (US) /ˈtʃekaʊt/ (UK)| n.  v.|结账；退房；检查；(图书馆)借书|The act of paying for goods or services purchased, especially at a store; the place where this is done.  The act of leaving a hotel or other accommodation; the time by which one must leave.  The act of borrowing something, especially a book, from a library.  An examination or inspection.|

例句：
Please proceed to the checkout.  请前往收银台结账。

The hotel checkout time is 11 a.m.  酒店退房时间是上午11点。

I have three books at the checkout. 我借了三本书。

近义词：payment, departure, exit, inspection, verification
反义词: check-in, arrival

词性: 名词, 动词
