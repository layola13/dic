hexanoic:/ˌhɛkˈsænoʊɪk/ | adj. | 己酸的，正己酸的 | Relating to or derived from hexane, especially denoting a saturated fatty acid with six carbon atoms. | Hexanoic acid is a carboxylic acid derived from hexane. 己酸是一种从己烷衍生的羧酸。|  Hexanoic acid is also known as caproic acid. 己酸也称为羊酸。| synonyms: caproic | antonyms: N/A | adjective
