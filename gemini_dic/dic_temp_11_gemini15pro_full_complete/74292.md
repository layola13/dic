fraction:/ˈfrækʃ(ə)n/| n.|分数，小部分，片段|A numerical quantity that is not a whole number (e.g. 1/2, 0.5). A small or tiny part, amount, or proportion of something.|例句：Only a small fraction of the population voted. 只有很小一部分人投票了。|例句：She sleeps for a fraction of the day. 她只睡了一小部分时间。|近义词：portion, segment, part, bit, piece|反义词：whole, entirety|noun
