disfavorable:/dɪsˈfeɪvərəbl/ | adj. | 不利的，不适宜的 | Not favorable; tending to discourage or oppose; adverse. | The disfavorable weather conditions led to the cancellation of the outdoor concert. 不利的天气条件导致了户外音乐会的取消。| The disfavorable economic climate made it difficult for small businesses to thrive. 不利的经济环境使小型企业难以发展。 | 近义词: unfavorable, adverse, disadvantageous, inauspicious, unpropitious | 反义词: favorable, advantageous, propitious, auspicious | 词性: 形容词
词源: dis- (not) + favorable (from Latin favorabilis, from favor "favor")
