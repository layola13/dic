macle:/ˈmækəl/| n.|孪晶；双晶；菱形斑|A twin crystal, especially of diamond.  A lozenge-shaped spot, especially on the skin.|The diamond showed a distinct macle. 这颗钻石显示出明显的孪晶。|Synonyms: twin, double, geminate|Antonyms: single, individual|noun

macle:/ˈmækəl/| n.|一种苏格兰黄油曲奇饼|A type of rich shortbread, usually cut into a diamond shape.|We enjoyed some macle with our tea. 我们享用了一些菱形黄油曲奇饼配茶。|Synonyms: shortbread, biscuit, cookie|Antonyms: N/A|noun
