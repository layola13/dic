cucurbit:/ˈkjuːkərˌbɪt/ /ˈkʌkərˌbɪt/| n.|葫芦科植物；葫芦|A plant of the gourd family, which includes melons, pumpkins, cucumbers, etc.|Pumpkins and cucumbers are both cucurbits. 南瓜和黄瓜都是葫芦科植物。|synonyms: gourd, melon, squash|antonyms: None|noun

cucurbitaceous:/ˌkjuːkərˌbɪˈteɪʃəs/ /ˌkʌkərˌbɪˈteɪʃəs/| adj.|葫芦科的|Belonging to the gourd family (Cucurbitaceae).|The garden is full of cucurbitaceous plants. 花园里种满了葫芦科植物。|synonyms: gourd-like|antonyms: None|adjective
