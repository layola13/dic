forecastingly:/fɔːrˈkæstɪŋli/
adverb
预测地，预报地
In a way that involves or relates to forecasting.
The economist forecastingly spoke about the future of the market.  经济学家预测性地谈论了市场的未来。
The report forecastingly analyzed the potential impact of the new policy. 报告预测性地分析了新政策的潜在影响。
Synonym: predictively, prophetically
Antonym:  retrospectively, historically
词性: 副词
