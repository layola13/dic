culiciform:/ˌkjuːˈlɪsɪfɔːrm/ | adj. |蚊形的，像蚊子的|Resembling or related to mosquitoes.  Having a slender body and long legs, like a mosquito.|The culiciform larvae were collected from the stagnant water.  从死水中收集到蚊子幼虫。|Synonyms: mosquito-like, gnat-like |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "robust" or "stocky" in terms of body shape)|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *culex* (gnat, mosquito) + *-formis* (having the form of). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱu̯el-* (to turn, to roll).
