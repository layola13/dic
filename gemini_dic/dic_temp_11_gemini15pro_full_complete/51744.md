devolve:/dɪˈvɒlv/ (British), /dɪˈvɑːlv/ (American)| v. | 转移（权力、责任等）；退化；衰落 | to pass (power, duties, etc.) to a less centralized authority; to degenerate or deteriorate | The responsibilities will devolve to the local authorities. 这些责任将移交给地方当局。| synonyms: delegate, transfer, pass down | antonyms: centralize, consolidate | verb
