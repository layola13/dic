episodial: /ˌɛpɪˈsɑːdɪkəl/ | adj. | 插曲式的；片段的；（尤指疾病）间歇性的；分段的 | Occurring in episodes or installments; loosely connected; (of a disease) recurring at intervals. |  The pain she experienced was episodic, coming and going without warning. 她经历的疼痛是间歇性的，来去无踪。| synonyms: intermittent, sporadic, occasional, periodic | antonyms: continuous, constant, persistent, chronic | adjective

episodial: /ˌɛpɪˈsɑːdɪkəl/ | adj. | （叙事）按事件发生的先后顺序记叙的 | (of a narrative or memory) divided into or consisting of separate and loosely connected episodes. | The novel has an episodic structure, jumping between different characters and timelines. 这部小说的结构是分段式的，在不同的角色和时间线之间跳跃。| synonyms: segmented, disjointed, serial | antonyms: continuous, unified, cohesive | adjective
