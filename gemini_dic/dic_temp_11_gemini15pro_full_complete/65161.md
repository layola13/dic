ethnics:/ˈeθnɪks/
noun
伦理，道德规范; 民族(ethnic的复数)
A system of moral principles, or the principles of a particular group.  Relating to a population subgroup (within a larger or dominant national or cultural group) with a common national or cultural tradition.
例句：Medical ethics prevent doctors from taking bribes. 医学伦理阻止医生受贿。

例句：He studies the ethics of the legal profession.  他研究法律行业的道德规范。

近义词：morals, values, principles, morality
反义词：immorality, wrongdoing

词性: noun
