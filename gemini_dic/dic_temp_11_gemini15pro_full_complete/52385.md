didactic:/daɪˈdæktɪk/
adjective
教诲的，说教的
Intended to teach, particularly in having moral instruction as an ulterior motive.
例句：The poem is not just entertaining, but also didactic. 这首诗不仅有趣，而且具有教诲意义。

例句：His didactic approach to teaching sometimes alienated his students. 他说教式的教学方法有时会疏远他的学生。

近义词：instructive, educational, pedagogic, moralistic, preachy
反义词：uninformative, entertaining,  non-instructive
词性: adjective
