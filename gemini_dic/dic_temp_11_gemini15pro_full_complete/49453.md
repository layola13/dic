delthyris:/dɛlˈθɪərɪs/
n.|戴尔西里斯贝，小嘴贝|A genus of brachiopods, common in the Paleozoic era, characterized by a large, flat shell with a prominent beak and a distinctive internal structure.|The delthyris fossil was found in Devonian rocks.  戴尔西里斯贝化石是在泥盆纪岩石中发现的。|近义词:brachiopod|反义词:None|词性:名词
Etymology: From Greek *δέλθυς* (delthys, “womb, uterus”) + *-iris* (a common suffix in taxonomic names). The name refers to the shape of the shell. 词源：来自希腊语 *δέλθυς*（delthys，“子宫，子宫”）+ *-iris*（分类名称中的常见后缀）。该名称指的是壳的形状。
