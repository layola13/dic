anti-ecclesiastic:/ˌæntiɪˌkliːziˈæstɪk/
adjective
反教会的；反对教会的
opposed to the church or the clergy; secular.
The writer's anti-ecclesiastic views were evident in his satirical portrayal of the clergy.  这位作家的反教会观点在他对神职人员的讽刺描绘中显而易见。
The government's anti-ecclesiastic policies led to the confiscation of church property. 政府的反教会政策导致了教会财产的没收。
近义词: anticlerical, secular, irreligious
反义词: pro-church, religious, devout
词性: adjective
