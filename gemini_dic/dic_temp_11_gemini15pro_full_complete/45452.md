curates:/ˈkjʊreɪts/ (US), /ˈkjʊəreɪts/ (UK)| n. v.|策展人（名词），策划（动词）|A curator is someone who manages a museum or other collection, or who organizes an exhibition. As a verb, to curate means to select, organize, and present content, often online.|
The museum curator gave a tour of the new exhibit.  博物馆策展人带领参观了新的展览。
She curates a popular blog about sustainable living. 她策划了一个关于可持续生活的热门博客。|近义词: organizer, compiler, selector, manager (n.), organize, compile, select, manage (v.)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun, verb
