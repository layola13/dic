digestifs:/diːʒɛˈstiːfs/ | n. | 餐后酒，消化酒 | An alcoholic drink, such as brandy or liqueur, taken after a meal, supposedly to aid digestion. |  We were offered coffee and digestifs after dinner.  我们饭后被提供了咖啡和餐后酒。 | Synonyms: after-dinner drinks, liqueurs | Antonyms: aperitifs | noun


digestif:/diːʒɛˈstiːf/ | adj. | 消化的，助消化的 | Aiding or promoting digestion. |  A digestif walk after a heavy meal can be beneficial. 饭后散步有助于消化。 | Synonyms: digestive | Antonyms: indigestible | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *digestif*, from *digérer* "to digest," from Latin *digerere* "to separate, distribute, digest," from *dis-* "apart, away" + *gerere* "to carry, bear, manage."
