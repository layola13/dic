overrate:/ˌoʊvərˈreɪt/| vt.|高估，过高评价|To estimate the value or importance of something or someone too highly.|He tends to overrate his own abilities. 他往往高估自己的能力。|近义词：overestimate, overvalue|反义词: underestimate, undervalue|词性:动词
overrate:/ˌəʊvəˈreɪt/| vt.|高估，过高评价|To estimate the value or importance of something or someone too highly.|The critics overrated her performance in the play.  评论家过高评价了她在剧中的表演。|近义词：overestimate, overvalue|反义词: underestimate, undervalue|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + rate.  "Over-" signifies "too much" or "excessively," while "rate" refers to assigning a value or level of importance.
