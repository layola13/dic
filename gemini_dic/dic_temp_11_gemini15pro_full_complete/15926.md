ballyhoo:/ˈbælihuː/
ballyhoo: n.|喧嚣，大肆宣传|A noisy, attention-getting demonstration or talk. |The circus came to town with a lot of ballyhoo. 马戏团带着大量的宣传来到镇上。|
ballyhoo: v.|大肆宣传，吹嘘|To publicize or praise something excessively. |The company ballyhooed its new product. 公司大肆宣传它的新产品。|
ballyhoo:近义词|hype, promotion, publicity, advertising|
ballyhoo:反义词|downplay, understate|
ballyhoo:词性|名词，动词|
