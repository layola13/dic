overdocumented:/ˌōvərˈdɑːkjəˌmɛntɪd/
adjective
记录过多的，过度记载的
Having far too much documentation; excessively documented.  This often implies that the documentation is cumbersome, unnecessary, or even counterproductive.
The process was so overdocumented that it became difficult to find the essential information. 这个过程记录得太多了，以至于很难找到重要的信息。
Near Synonyms: excessively documented, over-recorded, overly documented
Antonyms: underdocumented, sparsely documented
词性: adjective
