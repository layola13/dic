batching:/ˈbætʃɪŋ/
n.
批量处理，批处理
The act or process of grouping similar items or tasks together for processing or handling as a single unit.
Batching emails saves time and increases efficiency. 批量处理电子邮件可以节省时间并提高效率。
grouping, processing, handling
individual processing

noun
