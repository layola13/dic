overcleanly:/ˌoʊvərˈkliːnli/
| adv. | 过于干净地，洁癖地 | Excessively clean, often to an unhealthy or obsessive degree. | She scrubbed the floor overcleanly, removing even the protective sealant. 她过度清洁地擦洗地板，甚至去除了保护密封剂。| She washes her hands overcleanly, they are always red and chapped. 她过度清洁地洗手，手总是又红又干裂。 | 近义词: excessively clean, too clean | 反义词: dirtily, uncleanly | 词性: 副词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "cleanly" (adverb form of "clean")
