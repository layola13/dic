ornamentalize: /ˌɔːrnəˈmɛntəlaɪz/ | v. | 装饰；美化 | To make something ornamental; to decorate. | They ornamentalized the plain vase with intricate carvings. 他们在朴素的花瓶上雕刻了复杂的图案。 | The company ornamentalized the simple design with elaborate gold leaf.  公司用精致的金箔装饰了简单的设计。 | 近义词: decorate, adorn, embellish, beautify | 反义词: simplify, strip, bare | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From ornament + -al + -ize.  "Ornament" comes from the Latin "ornamentum" meaning "equipment, adornment."  "-al" forms adjectives from nouns, and "-ize" creates verbs meaning "to make or become."
