offbeat:/ˈɒfbiːt/ (UK), /ˈɔːfbiːt/ (US)| adj. n.|不寻常的，另类的；弱拍|Unconventional, unusual, or eccentric; a beat in music that is not emphasized or accented.|
The cafe had a quirky, offbeat atmosphere. 这家咖啡馆有一种古怪的，另类的氛围。
He's got a rather offbeat sense of humour. 他有一种相当不寻常的幽默感。

She prefers offbeat movies to mainstream ones. 她喜欢另类电影胜过主流电影。
The musician played a complex rhythm with several offbeats. 音乐家演奏了一个包含几个弱拍的复杂节奏。|近义词：unconventional, quirky, eccentric, unusual, irregular|反义词：conventional, ordinary, mainstream, regular|词性:形容词, 名词

offbeats:/ˈɒfbiːts/ (UK), /ˈɔːfbiːts/ (US)| n.|弱拍|The unaccented beats in a musical measure.|
The drummer emphasized the offbeats to create a syncopated rhythm. 鼓手强调弱拍以创造切分音节奏。
The offbeats in this piece are particularly noticeable. 这部作品中的弱拍特别引人注目。|近义词：weak beats|反义词：strong beats, downbeats|词性:名词


词源:  "offbeat"源于音乐术语，指不重读的节拍。后来引申为形容词，表示“不寻常的，另类的”。
