cheddary: /ˈtʃɛdəri/ | adj. | 干酪味的，像切达干酪的 | Having a flavor or texture resembling cheddar cheese. |  The biscuits had a distinctly cheddary aroma.  这些饼干有一种明显的切达干酪香味。 |  The soup had a rich, cheddary taste. 这汤有一种浓郁的切达干酪味。 | 近义词: cheesy | 反义词: bland | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Cheddar (a village in Somerset, England, where Cheddar cheese originated) + -y (suffix forming adjectives).
