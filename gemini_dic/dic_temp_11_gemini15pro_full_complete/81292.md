granular:/ˈɡrænjʊlər/| adj.|颗粒状的；粒状的；细粒的；详细的；具体的|Consisting of granules; resembling granules; detailed and specific.|例句：The beach had fine, granular sand.  海滩上有细细的沙粒。

例句：The report provided a granular analysis of the data.  这份报告对数据进行了详细的分析。|近义词：detailed, specific, particular, fine, powdery|反义词：coarse, general, vague|词性:形容词

granular:/ˈɡrænjʊlər/| n.|颗粒|A small compact particle of a substance.|例句：Granular sugar is coarser than caster sugar.  砂糖比细砂糖颗粒粗。

例句：The soil was composed of fine granulars. 土壤是由细小的颗粒组成的。|近义词：grain, particle, granule|反义词：lump, chunk, mass|词性:名词
