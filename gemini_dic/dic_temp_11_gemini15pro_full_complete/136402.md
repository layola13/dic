nonretailers:/ˌnɒnˈriːteɪlərz/ | n. |非零售商| Businesses or individuals that do not sell goods directly to consumers.  They may be wholesalers, manufacturers, or service providers. | Example: Nonretailers like factories and farms are crucial to the supply chain.  工厂和农场等非零售商对供应链至关重要。| Example:  Many nonretailers are now exploring online platforms to reach a wider audience. 许多非零售商现在正在探索在线平台以接触更广泛的受众。|Synonym:  non-retail businesses, wholesalers, suppliers, manufacturers|Antonym: retailers|Noun
