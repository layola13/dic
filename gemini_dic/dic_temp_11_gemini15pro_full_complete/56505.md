domestications:/dəˌmɛstɪˈkeɪʃənz/
n.|驯化，驯养|The process of taming an animal and keeping it as a pet or on a farm.|例句：The domestication of the dog began thousands of years ago.  狗的驯化始于几千年前。|近义词：taming, training|反义词：wilding, feralization|词性:名词

domestication:/dəˌmɛstɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.|驯化，教化|The process of adapting wild plants and animals for human use.|例句：The domestication of wheat was crucial to the development of agriculture. 小麦的驯化对农业的发展至关重要。|近义词：cultivation, breeding|反义词： neglect|词性:名词
