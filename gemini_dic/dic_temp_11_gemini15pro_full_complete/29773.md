cattiness:/ˈkætɪnɪs/
n.
恶意; 刻薄; 恶毒
spiteful or mean remarks, especially made by women about other women
例句：Her cattiness about her rival's dress was evident. 她对竞争对手的衣服的刻薄显而易见。

例句：The article was full of cattiness and gossip. 这篇文章充满了恶意和流言蜚语。

近义词：spitefulness, malice, meanness, bitchiness
反义词：kindness, generosity, niceness


词性: 名词 (noun)

词源:  From "catty" + "-ness". "Catty" itself likely originates from the perceived spiteful or sly nature of cats.
