arbitrative:/ˈɑːrbɪtreɪtɪv/
adjective
仲裁的; 调停的
Characterized by or relating to arbitration; involving the settlement of a dispute by a third party.
The union and management agreed to enter into arbitrative proceedings to resolve their differences. 工会和管理层同意进入仲裁程序来解决他们的分歧。
The contract included an arbitrative clause to handle any future disagreements. 合同中包含一个仲裁条款来处理未来的任何分歧。
近义词: mediative, conciliatory, judicial
反义词: litigious, contentious
词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *arbitrativus, from arbitrat(us), past participle of arbitrari (“to judge, examine, witness”), from arbiter (“judge, umpire, witness”) + -ive.
