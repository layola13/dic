arabesque:/ˌærəˈbɛsk/ | n. |蔓藤花纹，阿拉伯式花饰，（芭蕾舞）阿拉贝斯克舞姿 |An elaborate, ornate design of intertwined floral or geometric figures, typically of Islamic or Moorish origin. Also, a position in ballet in which one leg is extended backward and upward, and the arms are held in various harmonious positions.| The dancer held a perfect arabesque. 舞蹈演员摆出了一个完美的阿拉贝斯克舞姿。| synonyms: ornamentation, decoration, embellishment, flourish, adornment | antonyms: plainness, simplicity | noun

arabesque:/ˌærəˈbɛsk/ | adj. |蔓藤花纹的，阿拉伯式花饰的 | Characterized by intricate interwoven patterns. | The wallpaper had an arabesque design.  墙纸上有蔓藤花纹图案。| synonyms: ornate, decorative, elaborate | antonyms: simple, plain | adjective
