gurdwara: /ˈɡʊərdwɑːrə/ (UK), /ˈɡʊrdwɑːrə/ (US) | n. | 锡克教谒师所；锡克教寺庙 | A place of worship for Sikhs.  It is open to all, regardless of religion, caste, gender, or ethnicity. |  例句：The gurdwara provides free meals to everyone.  锡克教谒师所向所有人提供免费餐食。

例句：We went to the gurdwara to listen to the kirtan. 我们去锡克教谒师所聆听基尔坦圣歌。

近义词：temple, shrine, Sikh temple | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Punjabi ਗੁਰਦੁਆਰਾ (gurdwārā), from  ਗੁਰੂ (gurū, “guru”) + ਦੁਆਰਾ (dwārā, “door, gateway”). Literally "guru's door".
