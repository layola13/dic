funhouse:/ˈfʌnˌhaʊs/ | n. |游乐宫，哈哈镜宫|A building at a fairground or amusement park containing distorting mirrors and other devices designed to amuse. | We laughed ourselves silly in the funhouse at the carnival. 我们在嘉年华的哈哈镜宫里笑得傻乎乎的。| The kids loved the funhouse with its crazy mirrors and slides. 孩子们喜欢那个有哈哈镜和滑梯的游乐宫。|Synonyms: amusement arcade, amusement park, carnival, fairground | Antonyms: library, museum | Noun
