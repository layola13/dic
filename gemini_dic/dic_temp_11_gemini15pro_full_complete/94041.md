imparter:/ɪmˈpɑːrtər/ | n. | 传授者，赋予者 |  Someone or something that imparts knowledge, qualities, or characteristics. | The teacher was a skilled imparter of knowledge.  这位老师是一位知识渊博的传授者。| synonyms: teacher, instructor, communicator, conveyor, giver, bestower | antonyms: receiver, student, learner | noun

impart:/ɪmˈpɑːrt/ | vt. |  传授，赋予，告知 | To communicate information or knowledge; to give or bestow a quality or characteristic. |  She imparted her wisdom to her students. 她把她的智慧传授给她的学生。 | synonyms: communicate, convey, transmit, relate, disclose, reveal, bestow, give, grant | antonyms: withhold, conceal, suppress | verb
