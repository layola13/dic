atrous: /ˈeɪtrəs/ | adj. |多孔的，空洞的；扩张的，膨胀的 | Characterized by holes or cavities; enlarged or dilated, especially in reference to a convolutional filter in deep learning that has spaces inserted between its values to increase its receptive field without increasing the number of parameters. |  Atrous convolution is used in deep learning to expand the receptive field.  多孔卷积用于深度学习中扩大感受野。

Atrous algorithm is effective in image segmentation. 多孔算法在图像分割中很有效。 | 近义词: dilated, porous, expanded | 反义词: contracted, compressed, dense | 词性: 形容词

词源: From French "à trous" meaning "with holes".  来自法语“à trous”，意思是“带孔的”。
