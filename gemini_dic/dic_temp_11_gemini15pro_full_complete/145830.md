overdeliberatenesses:/ˌoʊvərdiˌlɪbərɪtnəsnəsɪz/ |n.| 过度深思熟虑，过分斟酌 | The overdeliberatenesses of his actions frustrated everyone involved. 他行动的过度深思熟虑让所有参与的人都感到沮丧。|  Excessive deliberation, overthinking, overcautiousness | His overdeliberatenesses led to missed opportunities. 他的过度深思熟虑导致错失良机。| 近义词: overthinking, overcautiousness, hesitancy | 反义词: impulsiveness, decisiveness, spontaneity | 词性: noun

词源:  "over" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "deliberate" (adjective meaning "done consciously and intentionally") + "ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives) + "es" (plural suffix).  Ultimately from Latin "deliberare" meaning "to weigh in mind, consider."
