nonconventional:/ˌnɑːn kənˈvɛnʃənəl/ /ˌnɒn kənˈvɛnʃənəl/| adj.|非传统的，非惯例的|Not conforming to accepted, traditional, or conventional standards, methods, or practices.|例句：She has a nonconventional approach to teaching. 她有一种非传统的教学方法。|近义词：unconventional, unorthodox, atypical, unusual, innovative|反义词：conventional, traditional, orthodox, typical, usual|词性:形容词

nonconventional:/ˌnɑːn kənˈvɛnʃənəl/ /ˌnɒn kənˈvɛnʃənəl/| n.|非传统的事物|Something that is not conforming to accepted, traditional, or conventional standards.|例句：Her art is considered a nonconventional in the art world. 她的艺术在艺术界被认为是非传统的作品。|近义词：unconventional, unorthodox, atypical, unusual, innovative|反义词：conventional, traditional, orthodox, typical, usual|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + conventional (following accepted standards).  "Conventional" comes from the Latin "conventio" meaning "agreement, meeting."
