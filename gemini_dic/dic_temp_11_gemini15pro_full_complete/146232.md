overexposes:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspoʊzɪz/
verb
过度曝光；过分暴露
to expose (something, such as film) for too long to light
例句：The photographer overexposed the film, resulting in a washed-out picture. 摄影师过度曝光了胶片，导致照片褪色。

例句：He overexposed his children to the media, putting their privacy at risk.  他过度曝光自己的孩子，将他们的隐私置于危险之中。
近义词：overdo, saturate
反义词：underexpose
词性：v.
