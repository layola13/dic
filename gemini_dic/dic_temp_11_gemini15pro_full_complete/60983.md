elicits:/ɪˈlɪsɪts/| v. |引出，探出，诱出|To evoke or draw out a response, answer, or fact from someone in reaction to one's own actions or questions.|The comedian's jokes elicited laughter from the audience.  喜剧演员的笑话引起了观众的笑声。|synonyms: evoke, draw out, obtain, extract, bring forth, prompt|antonyms: suppress, repress, stifle|verb
