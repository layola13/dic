aedile: /ˈiːdaɪl/ (US), /ˈiːdaɪl/ (UK)| n. | 市政官；营造官 | An ancient Roman magistrate responsible for public buildings, markets, games, and the public order. |  The aedile oversaw the maintenance of the city's aqueducts.  营造官负责维护城市的沟渠。

The aediles organized the annual Lupercalia festival. 市政官组织一年一度的卢珀卡利亚节。 |  Synonyms: magistrate, official, commissioner | Antonyms: civilian, commoner | Noun
