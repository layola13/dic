candlebomb:/ˈkændlbɒm/ (US) /ˈkændlbɒm/ (UK)| n.|蜡烛炸弹，燃烧瓶|A makeshift incendiary device typically consisting of a bottle filled with flammable liquid and a wick, often a piece of cloth, ignited before being thrown.|Protesters hurled candlebombs at the police line. 抗议者向警察防线投掷燃烧瓶。|近义词：Molotov cocktail, petrol bomb, firebomb|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "extinguisher")|noun
