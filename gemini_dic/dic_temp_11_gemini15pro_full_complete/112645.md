lowball:/ˈloʊbɔːl/| v. n.|低估；低价出售；低球|To make a very low offer for something, especially in a negotiation or sale; an offer that is much lower than what is considered reasonable or fair.|He lowballed me with his initial offer. 他一开始就给我报了个很低的价。|近义词:underestimate, underbid, undervalue|反义词:overestimate, overbid, overvalue|词性:动词, 名词

lowballs:/ˈloʊbɔːlz/| v. (third-person singular simple present)|低估；低价出售；低球（第三人称单数）|The third-person singular simple present form of "lowball".|He lowballs his clients to maximize his profit. 他低估客户以最大化自己的利润。|近义词:underestimates, underbids, undervalues|反义词:overestimates, overbids, overvalues|词性:动词
