Modocs: /ˈmoʊdɑːks/ | n. | 摩多克人 | A Native American tribe indigenous to the Modoc Plateau, a region spanning parts of northern California, southern Oregon, and northwestern Nevada.  |  The Modocs fought fiercely to protect their ancestral lands. 摩多克人为了保护他们的祖先的土地而进行了激烈的战斗。 | synonyms: Klamath, Native Americans | antonyms: None | noun

Modoc: /ˈmoʊdɑːk/ | adj. | 摩多克的；摩多克语的 | Relating to the Modoc people or their language. | The Modoc language is nearly extinct. 摩多克语几乎灭绝了。 | synonyms: Klamath (sometimes used interchangeably, though distinct) | antonyms: None | adjective
