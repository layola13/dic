nonallegations:/ˌnɒnˌæləˈɡeɪʃənz/
noun
非指控，未被证实的指控
The absence of allegations; statements that do not accuse someone of wrongdoing.  This word is generally used to describe a situation where accusations have been explicitly denied or refuted, or where there is an emphasis on the lack of any formal accusation.
The lawyer stated that the claims were mere nonallegations and lacked any supporting evidence. 律师表示，这些说法仅仅是未经证实的指控，缺乏任何支持证据。

The company issued a press release addressing the nonallegations, clarifying their position on the matter. 公司发布了一份新闻稿，回应了这些未经证实的指控，澄清了他们在此事上的立场。

Synonym:  non-accusations, denials, refutations
Antonym: allegations, accusations, charges
词性: 名词
