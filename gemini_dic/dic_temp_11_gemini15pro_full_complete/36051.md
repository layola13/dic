codriver:/ˈkəʊˌdraɪvər/ (UK) /ˈkoʊˌdraɪvər/ (US)| n.|副驾驶，领航员|A person who sits beside the driver of a vehicle and helps with navigation, especially in rallies or other motorsports.|Example: The codriver reads the pace notes to the driver during the rally stage.  在拉力赛阶段，领航员向驾驶员读速度笔记。|Example: My codriver helped me navigate the unfamiliar roads. 我的副驾驶员帮助我在不熟悉的道路上导航。|Synonyms: navigator, co-pilot|Antonyms: driver, pilot|noun
