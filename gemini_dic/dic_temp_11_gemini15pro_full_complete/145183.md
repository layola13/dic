overattenuating:/ˌoʊvərəˈtenjueɪtɪŋ/
adjective
过度衰减的
Reducing the strength or intensity of something, typically a signal or a sound, to an excessive degree.  The process of lessening or diminishing something too much.
The engineer realized he was overattenuating the signal, causing a loss of data. 工程师意识到他过度衰减了信号，导致数据丢失。
The thick curtains were overattenuating the sunlight, making the room too dark. 厚厚的窗帘过度减弱了阳光，使房间太暗。
近义词:overdamping, excessively reducing, weakening too much
反义词:amplifying, boosting, strengthening
词性:adjective
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "attenuate" (verb meaning "to reduce in force, effect, or value")
