hobbits:/ˈhɒbɪts/ (UK) /ˈhɑːbɪts/ (US)| n. |霍比特人|A fictional race of small human-like creatures in J.R.R. Tolkien's fantasy world, particularly known from *The Hobbit* and *The Lord of the Rings*. They are characterized by their love of food, comfort, and peace. |The hobbits lived in comfortable holes in the Shire. 霍比特人住在夏尔的舒适洞穴里。|Synonyms: halflings, dwarves (loosely)|Antonyms: giants, orcs|Noun
