chromatology:/ˌkroʊməˈtɒlədʒi/ | n. | 色彩学，色谱法 | The study of color and its properties, including its measurement, perception, and application.  It also refers to a technique for separating mixtures into their components based on their different affinities for a stationary and a mobile phase. |  Chromatology plays a crucial role in the textile industry. 色彩学在纺织工业中起着至关重要的作用。

Chromatology is used to identify the components of a dye. 色谱法用于鉴定染料的成分。 | 近义词：color science, colorimetry | 反义词： N/A | 词性：noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *chromatologie*, from *chromato-* (from Greek *khrōma*, *khrōmatos* "color") + *-logie* (from Greek *-logia* "study of").
