fawcett:/ˈfɔːsɪt/ | n. | 福西特（姓氏） | A surname.  Derived from the Old French word "faucon," meaning "falcon." Originally a nickname for a falconer or someone who resembled a falcon. |  Example: Farrah Fawcett was a famous American actress.  法拉·福西特是一位著名的美国女演员。| Synonym: (No direct synonym for a surname) | Antonym: (No antonym for a surname) | noun
