barging:/ˈbɑːrdʒɪŋ/ | n. |闯入， barge 的动名词形式|The act of entering somewhere rudely or forcefully, like a barge going through a narrow canal. | Example: His barging into the meeting disrupted the discussion. 他闯入会议打断了讨论。| Synonyms: interrupting, intruding, bursting in | Antonyms: exiting, leaving, withdrawing | noun

barging:/ˈbɑːrdʒɪŋ/ | v. | 闯入， barge 的现在分词形式|Moving forcefully or clumsily, like a barge. | Example: He was barging through the crowd, trying to get to the front. 他在人群中横冲直撞，试图走到前面。| Synonyms: shoving, pushing, jostling | Antonyms: yielding, retreating, backing off | verb
