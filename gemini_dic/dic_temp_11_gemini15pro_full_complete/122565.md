moderated:/ˈmɒdəreɪtɪd/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdəreɪtɪd/ (US)| adj. | 缓和的，适度的，温和的 | Made or become less extreme, intense, or violent. | The chairman moderated the discussion. 主席主持了讨论。|  The storm has moderated.  暴风雨已经减弱了。 | 近义词: tempered, restrained, controlled, mild, gentle | 反义词: extreme, intense, immoderate, excessive | 词性:形容词

moderated:/ˈmɒdəreɪtɪd/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdəreɪtɪd/ (US)| v. | 主持，调节，使缓和 | Act as a moderator (in a discussion, meeting, etc.).  Make or become less extreme, intense, or violent. |  He moderated the debate between the two candidates. 他主持了两位候选人之间的辩论。 | The heat moderated as the sun went down. 随着太阳下山，炎热的天气有所缓和。 | 近义词: chair, mediate, control, regulate, lessen | 反义词: intensify, exacerbate, aggravate | 词性:动词


词源: From Latin moderatus, past participle of moderari ‘regulate, restrain’, from modus ‘measure, limit’.
