banefully:/ˈbeɪnfəli/| adv.|有害地，恶毒地|In a harmful or destructive manner; ruinously; perniciously.|Her words, spoken banefully, cut him to the core. 她恶毒的话语深深地刺痛了他。|Synonyms: destructively, harmfully, maliciously, perniciously, ruinously|Antonyms: beneficially, helpfully, kindly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From bane (harm, ruin) + -ful (full of) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *banja- (wound, sore).
