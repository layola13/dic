apostatical:/ˌæpəˈstætɪk(ə)l/ /əˌpɑːstəˈtætɪkl/
adjective
叛教的；背教的；脱党的
Relating to or characteristic of an apostate; abandoning a religious belief or cause.
例句：His apostatical views led to his excommunication from the church. 他的叛教观点导致他被逐出教会。

例句：The apostatical writer was shunned by his former colleagues.  这位背教的作家被他以前的同事们避 shunned。


近义词：heretical, traitorous, disloyal, renegade, faithless
反义词：faithful, loyal, orthodox, devout
词性: adjective
