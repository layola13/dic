debonairnesses:/ˌdɛbəˈnɛrnɪsɪz/
noun
优雅气质；温文尔雅
The quality of being debonair; urbanity and sophistication.
He exuded an air of debonairness that charmed everyone in the room. 他散发出的优雅气质迷住了房间里的每一个人。
Synonyms: sophistication, suavity, urbanity, charm, polish, grace, elegance
Antonyms: awkwardness, clumsiness, gaucheness, boorishness
