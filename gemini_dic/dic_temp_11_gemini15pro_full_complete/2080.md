adact:/əˈdækt/ | v. | 适应，改编 | To modify or alter something to make it suitable for a new purpose or situation; to adapt.  This word is archaic and rarely used. |  He adacted the play for a modern audience. 他将这部戏剧改编为适合现代观众的版本. | 近义词: adapt, adjust, modify, alter | 反义词: maintain, preserve | 词性: 动词

词源: From Late Latin *adactare, from Latin ad- + actare “to do frequently,” frequentative of agere “to do, drive, lead.”
