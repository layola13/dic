hyperproductions:/ˈhaɪpərprəˈdʌkʃənz/| n. |过度生产；超量生产|The act or process of producing an excessive amount of something, often beyond what is needed or sustainable.  It can refer to various contexts, from manufacturing and agriculture to information and cultural products.| The hyperproductions of cheap consumer goods have led to environmental problems. 过度生产廉价消费品导致了环境问题。

The internet facilitates the hyperproductions of information, making it difficult to filter what's valuable. 互联网促进了信息的过度生产，使得过滤有价值的信息变得困难。|近义词：overproduction, surplus, glut|反义词: underproduction, scarcity, shortage|词性:名词
