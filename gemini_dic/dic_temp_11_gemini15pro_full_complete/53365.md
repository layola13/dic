disaffiliates:/dɪsəˈfɪlieɪts/
verb
脱离关系，断绝联系
to officially end a connection with a group or an organization
The union disaffiliated itself from the national federation. 工会脱离了国家联盟。
dissociates, separates, withdraws, breaks away from
joins, unites, affiliates, associates with
