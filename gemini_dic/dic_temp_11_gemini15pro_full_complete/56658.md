dont:/doʊnt/ | contraction | 不要 | Short form of "do not", expressing negative command or prohibition. | Don't touch the hot stove. 不要碰热炉子。|  Don't be late for the meeting. 不要开会迟到。 | 近义词: mustn't, shouldn't | 反义词: do | contraction

词源: Contraction of "do not". "Do" comes from Old English "dōn", and "not" from Old English "nawiht" (meaning "nothing").
