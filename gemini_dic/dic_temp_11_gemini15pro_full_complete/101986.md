isochor:/ˈaɪsəkɔːr/ (US), /ˈaɪsəkɔː(r)/ (UK)| n. |等容线; 等容过程|A line on a pressure-volume diagram representing a change that takes place at constant volume.  A thermodynamic process during which the volume remains constant.|The reaction was carried out in an isochoric reactor.  该反应在等容反应器中进行。|近义词:isometric, isovolumetric|反义词:isobaric|词性:noun

isochoric:/ˌaɪsəˈkɔːrɪk/ (US), /ˌaɪsəˈkɒrɪk/ (UK)| adj. |等容的|Taking place at constant volume.|Isochoric processes are common in closed systems. 等容过程在封闭系统中很常见。|近义词:isometric, isovolumetric|反义词:isobaric|词性:adjective
