compromiser:/ˈkɒmprəmaɪzə(r) (UK), /ˈkɑːmprəmaɪzər (US)| n.|妥协者，折衷者|A person who makes or is willing to make compromises.|He is a compromiser, always seeking the middle ground. 他是一个妥协者，总是寻求中间立场。|The compromisers in the group managed to avoid a serious argument.  团队中的妥协者成功避免了一场激烈的争论。|synonyms: mediator, negotiator, conciliator|antonyms: hardliner, zealot|noun
