doziness:/ˈdoʊzinəs/| n. |困倦，昏昏欲睡|A state of being sleepy and lethargic.|例句：The doziness caused by the medication made it difficult to concentrate. 药物引起的困倦使人难以集中注意力。|近义词：sleepiness, drowsiness, lethargy|反义词：alertness, wakefulness|词性:名词
Etymology: From doze + -ness.  Doze comes from Middle English dosen, from Old Norse *dusa.
