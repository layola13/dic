ideogeny:/ˌaɪdiˈɒdʒəni/ /ˌaɪdiˈɑːdʒəni/
n.|观念起源; 思想起源|The study of the origin and development of ideas.  The ideogeny of this philosophical concept can be traced back to ancient Greece.|例句：The ideogeny of Darwin's theory of evolution is complex and fascinating. 达尔文进化论的思想起源复杂而引人入胜。

例句：Tracing the ideogeny of a scientific discovery can reveal much about the historical and cultural context in which it emerged. 追溯一项科学发现的思想起源可以揭示很多关于其出现的历史和文化背景的信息。
|近义词：genesis, origin, development, evolution|反义词: termination, conclusion|词性: noun
