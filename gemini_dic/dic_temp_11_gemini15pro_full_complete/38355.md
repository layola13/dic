concentrate: /ˈkɒnsəntreɪt/ (UK)  /ˈkɑːnsəntreɪt/ (US) | v. | 集中（注意力），全神贯注 | To direct one's attention or efforts towards a particular activity, subject, or problem. |  I need to concentrate on finishing this report. 我需要集中精力完成这份报告。| focus, attend, apply oneself | distract, neglect | verb


Etymology: From Late Latin *concentrātus*, past participle of *concentrāre* "to join in a center," from *com-* "together" + *centrum* "center."
