doorbell:/ˈdɔːrˌbel/ (US) /ˈdɔːˌbel/ (UK)| n.|门铃|A push-button at an outer door that gives a ringing signal when pressed, used to alert the occupants of a visitor's arrival.|Someone rang the doorbell. 有人按了门铃。|Synonyms: chime, buzzer, bell|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "knocker" as an alternative method of announcing arrival)|noun
