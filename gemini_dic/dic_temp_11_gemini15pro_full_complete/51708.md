devisee:/ˌdɛvɪˈziː/ /ˌdɛvɪˈziː/
n.
受遗赠不动产的人
a person to whom real estate is left by will
Example: The devisee was surprised to inherit the sprawling estate.  受遗赠者惊讶地继承了广阔的房产。

Example:  The will clearly named John as the devisee of the property. 遗嘱清楚地指定约翰为该房产的受赠人。

近义词:heir, beneficiary, inheritor
反义词:testator, grantor
词性:noun
