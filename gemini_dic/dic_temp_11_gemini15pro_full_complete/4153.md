airbases:/ˈɛərˌbeɪsɪz/| n. |空军基地|A location where military aircraft are stationed.|The country has several airbases strategically located near the border.  这个国家在靠近边境的地方战略性地部署了几个空军基地。|Example:  Enemy fighters attacked the airbases at dawn. 敌方战斗机在黎明时分袭击了空军基地。|Synonyms: airfields, military airfields, aerodromes|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider: civilian airports)|noun
