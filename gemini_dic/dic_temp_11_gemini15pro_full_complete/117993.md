memorialize:/məˈmɔːriəlaɪz/ /məˈmɔːriəˌlaɪz/
verb
纪念；将…永垂不朽
To preserve the memory of someone or something, as by a monument or ceremony.
They memorialized the soldiers who died in the war with a monument. 他们为在战争中牺牲的士兵建造了一座纪念碑。
The library was memorialized with a plaque dedicated to its founder.  图书馆立了一块牌匾以纪念其创始人。
commemorate, celebrate, remember
forget, ignore
词性: 动词
