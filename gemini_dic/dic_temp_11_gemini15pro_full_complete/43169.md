cpm:/ˌsiːpiːˈem/ /ˌsiːpiːˈɛm/| n. | 每千次展示成本 (Cost Per Mille/Thousand) | Cost Per Mille (sometimes referred to as Cost Per Thousand) is a marketing term used to denote the price of 1,000 advertisement impressions on one webpage.  | The CPM for advertising on this website is $2.  这个网站的广告每千次展示成本为 2 美元。 | synonyms: cost per thousand (CPT) | antonyms: N/A | noun

cpm:/ˌsiːpiːˈem/ /ˌsiːpiːˈɛm/| abbreviation | 每千次展示成本 (Cost Per Mille/Thousand) | Abbreviation for Cost Per Mille. |  We're seeing a high CPM on our latest campaign. 我们最新的活动每千次展示成本很高。| synonyms: CPT | antonyms: N/A | abbreviation
