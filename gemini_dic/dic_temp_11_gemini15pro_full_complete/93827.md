imminence:/ˈɪmɪnəns/| n.|即将发生，迫近|The imminence of war caused widespread panic. 战争即将爆发，引起了 widespread 恐慌。|例句：The imminence of the storm was evident in the darkening sky.  暴风雨即将来临，从 darkening 的天空中可以明显看出。

例句：With the imminence of the deadline, everyone started working overtime.  随着截止日期的临近，每个人都开始加班。|近义词：nearness, proximity, approach|反义词：remoteness, distance|词性:名词

imminent:/ˈɪmɪnənt/| adj.|即将发生的，迫在眉睫的|The imminent danger forced them to evacuate. 迫在眉睫的危险迫使他们撤离。|例句：They were warned of the imminent arrival of the hurricane. 他们被警告飓风即将到来。

例句：The company faced imminent bankruptcy.  这家公司面临着即将破产的局面。|近义词：impending, approaching, forthcoming|反义词：distant, remote, future|词性:形容词
