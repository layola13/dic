moose's:/muːsiz/| n.|驼鹿的|The possessive form of "moose," indicating ownership or belonging.|例句：The moose's antlers were enormous. 驼鹿的鹿角巨大无比。

例句：We admired the moose's majestic presence. 我们钦佩驼鹿的雄伟姿态。

近义词:elk's (specifically for elk, a related species)|反义词:(None -  possession is a relational concept, not an antonym)|词性:Possessive noun
