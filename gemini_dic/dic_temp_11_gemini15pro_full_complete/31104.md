championships:/ˈtʃæmpiənʃɪps/
n.
锦标赛；冠军称号；冠军地位
A competition to determine the best competitor or team in a particular sport or other activity.  The status of being a champion.
例句：He has won numerous championships throughout his career. 他在整个职业生涯中赢得了无数次冠军。

例句：The World Championships are held every four years. 世界锦标赛每四年举办一次。

近义词：competition, contest, tournament, title
反义词：defeat, loss
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From champion + -ship.  "Champion" comes from the Late Latin word "campio," meaning "fighter, gladiator."  "-ship" is a suffix used to form abstract nouns denoting state, condition, or quality.
