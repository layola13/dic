frayed:/freɪd/
adjective
磨损的，磨破的
worn or tattered at the edges, typically through constant rubbing.
The frayed edges of the rug showed its age.  地毯磨损的边缘显示出它的年代。
The cuffs of his jacket were frayed. 他夹克的袖口磨损了。
近义词: ragged, worn, tattered, threadbare
反义词: new, pristine, intact, undamaged
词性: adjective

frayed:/freɪd/
adjective
(of a person's nerves or temper) strained to the point of breaking.
Her frayed nerves finally gave way. 她紧绷的神经终于崩溃了。
He was clearly getting frayed by the constant interruptions.  不断的打断显然让他很烦躁。
近义词: strained, stressed, tense, on edge
反义词: calm, relaxed, composed, serene
词性: adjective
