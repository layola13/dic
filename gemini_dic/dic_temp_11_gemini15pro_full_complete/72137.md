floodlight:/ˈflʌdˌlaɪt/ | n. |泛光灯；探照灯|A large, powerful lamp that produces a wide beam of light, typically used to illuminate outdoor areas. |The stadium was bathed in the glare of the floodlights. 体育场沐浴在泛光灯的强光下。

floodlight:/ˈflʌdˌlaɪt/ | vt. |用泛光灯照亮|To illuminate with a floodlight. |They floodlit the garden for the party.  他们用泛光灯照亮了花园，为派对做准备。


Etymology: flood + light.  "Flood" in this context refers to a large outpouring or abundance of something, in this case, light.

Synonyms: searchlight, spotlight, work light

Antonyms:  dim light,  shadow
