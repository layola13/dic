dalit:/ˈdɑːlɪt/ | n. adj. |贱民，达利特| A member of the lowest caste in Hinduism, traditionally considered to be untouchable.  Formerly known as "untouchables".  The term "Dalit" means "broken/scattered" in Sanskrit and reflects the social exclusion they faced. |  例句：The dalit community faces significant discrimination.  贱民社区面临严重的歧视。

例句：He is a dalit activist fighting for equal rights. 他是一位为平等权利而奋斗的贱民活动家。|近义词：outcast, pariah, untouchable |反义词:Brahmin (highest caste) |词性: noun, adjective
