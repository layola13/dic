amending:/əˈmɛndɪŋ/
verb
修改，修订
making changes to a document, law, agreement, etc. in order to improve it
The committee is amending the club's constitution. 委员会正在修改俱乐部的章程。
synonyms: revising, altering, modifying, correcting, improving
antonyms: preserving, maintaining, keeping

amending:/əˈmɛndɪŋ/
adjective
修正的，修订的
relating to or making an amendment
The amending bill was passed by the Senate.  修正案得到了参议院的通过。
synonyms: corrective, revisory, alterative
antonyms: original, unchanged


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *amenden*, from Anglo-Norman *amender*, from Old French *amender* (“to correct, improve, mend”), from Latin *emendare* (“to free from faults, correct”), from *ex-* (“out of”) + *menda* (“fault”).
