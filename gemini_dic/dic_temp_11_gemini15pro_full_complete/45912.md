cutose: /ˈkjuːtoʊs/ | n. | 角质素；壳质 | A complex carbohydrate, similar to cellulose, forming the outer layer of various invertebrate animals, especially arthropods.  | The cutose of the insect's exoskeleton provided protection.  昆虫外骨骼的角质素提供了保护。 | 近义词: chitin | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French cutose, from Latin cutis (“skin”).
