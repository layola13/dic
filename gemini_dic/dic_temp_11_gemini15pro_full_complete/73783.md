formalized:/ˈfɔːməlaɪzd/ | adj. |正式化的，形式化的|Made formal or official; expressed in a systematic or structured way.  |例句：The agreement was formalized with a signed contract.  该协议以签署合同的方式正式化。

|例句：They formalized their business partnership. 他们正式确立了业务伙伴关系。|近义词：official, codified, systematized, structured|反义词：informal, unofficial, unstructured|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "formalize" + "-ed." "Formalize" comes from "formal" (from Old French "formal," ultimately from Latin "formalis") + "-ize."
