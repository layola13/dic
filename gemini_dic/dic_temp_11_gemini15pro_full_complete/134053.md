nonidiomaticalness:/ˌnɒnˌɪdɪəˌmætɪkəlˈnɪs/ | n. | 非惯用性；不符合语言习惯 | The nonidiomaticalness of his writing made it difficult for native speakers to understand.  他的写作不符合语言习惯，使得以英语为母语的人难以理解。 |  例句：The student's essay was marked down for its nonidiomaticalness.  这个学生的文章因为不符合语言习惯而被扣分。 | 近义词：unidiomatic, awkwardness, unnaturalness | 反义词：idiomaticalness, naturalness, fluency | 词性：名词

词源：non- (prefix meaning "not") + idiomatical + -ness (suffix forming nouns of quality or state)
