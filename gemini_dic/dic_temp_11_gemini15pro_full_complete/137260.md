nonswimming:/ˌnɑːnˈswɪmɪŋ/ /ˌnɒnˈswɪmɪŋ/
adjective
不游泳的；不会游泳的
not able to swim; not involving swimming
例句：Nonswimming children should always wear life jackets.  不会游泳的孩子应该一直穿着救生衣。

例句：The hotel has both swimming and nonswimming pools.  这家酒店有游泳池和非游泳池。

近义词：non-natatory
反义词：swimming
