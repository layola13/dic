horriblenesses:/ˈhɔːrɪbəl.nɪs.ɪz/
noun
恐怖；可怕；令人厌恶的事物
The quality or state of being horrible; a horrible thing or act.
The horriblenesses of war are difficult to comprehend.  战争的恐怖难以理解。
Near Synonyms: atrocities, horrors, terrors, abominations
Antonyms: beauties, wonders, delights
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From horrible + -ness + -es (plural suffix)

Horrible: From Middle French horrible, from Latin horribilis (“causing horror, dreadful, frightful, terrible”), from horrēre (“to bristle, shudder, tremble with fear”).

-ness:  A native English suffix forming nouns from adjectives, meaning "the quality or state of being (adjective)".

-es: English plural suffix.
