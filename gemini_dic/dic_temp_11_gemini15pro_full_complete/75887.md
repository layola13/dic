fundamentalist:/ˌfʌndəˈmentəlɪst/| n. adj.|原教旨主义者；基要主义的|A person who believes in the strict, literal interpretation of scripture in a religion.|例句：He's a religious fundamentalist who opposes abortion. 他是一个反对堕胎的宗教原教旨主义者。|例句：She's known for her fundamentalist views on marriage. 她因其对婚姻的原教旨主义观点而闻名。|近义词：dogmatist, orthodox, traditionalist|反义词：liberal, modernist, reformer|词性: 名词, 形容词

fundamentalist:/ˌfʌndəˈmentəlɪst/| adj.|基要主义的|Relating to or adhering to fundamentalism.|例句：The rise of fundamentalist movements is a global phenomenon. 原教旨主义运动的兴起是一个全球现象。|例句：They hold a fundamentalist interpretation of the Bible. 他们对圣经持有原教旨主义的解释。|近义词：strict, literal, orthodox|反义词：liberal, modernist, flexible|词性: 形容词
