backups:/ˈbækʌps/| n.|备份，备用物|A copy of data or software that is made in case the original is lost or damaged.  |Example 1:  Regular backups are essential to protect your data. 定期备份对于保护你的数据至关重要。

Example 2:  He keeps backups of all his important files on an external hard drive. 他把所有重要文件的备份都保存在一个外部硬盘上。|近义词：copies, reserves, duplicates|反义词:original|词性:名词
