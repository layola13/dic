fruitless:/ˈfruːtləs/| adj.|无结果的，徒劳的，无效的|Producing no fruit or other desired result; unsuccessful.|例句：After many fruitless attempts, they finally gave up. 经过多次徒劳的尝试，他们最终放弃了。|例句：The search for the missing child proved fruitless.  寻找失踪孩子的努力被证明是徒劳的。|近义词：unproductive, futile, vain, unsuccessful|反义词：productive, successful, fruitful|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From fruit + -less.  The suffix "-less" means "without" or "lacking."  Thus, "fruitless" literally means "without fruit." The original meaning referred to literal fruit, but the meaning expanded to encompass any lack of desired results.
