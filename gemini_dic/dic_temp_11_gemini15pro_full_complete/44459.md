cross-training:/ˈkrɔːsˌtreɪnɪŋ/| n.|交叉训练|The act of engaging in two or more different forms of exercise or training in order to improve overall fitness and reduce the risk of injury. It also refers to training in more than one skill to increase versatility and adaptability.|Example 1: Cross-training helps prevent overuse injuries from repetitive movements. 交叉训练有助于防止重复动作造成的过度使用损伤。

Example 2:  She incorporated swimming and cycling into her running routine as cross-training. 她将游泳和骑自行车作为交叉训练纳入跑步计划中。|近义词：varied training, multidisciplinary training|反义词: specialization|词性:noun


cross-train:/ˈkrɔːsˈtreɪn/| v.|交叉训练|To engage in two or more different forms of exercise or training in order to improve overall fitness and reduce the risk of injury. It can also mean to train someone in another skill or area of expertise.|Example 1:  I cross-train by alternating between running and swimming. 我通过跑步和游泳交替进行交叉训练。

Example 2: The company is cross-training its employees to handle multiple roles. 公司正在对员工进行交叉培训，以便他们能够胜任多个角色。|近义词：vary training, diversify training|反义词: specialize|词性:verb
