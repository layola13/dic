fogbow:/ˈfɒɡbəʊ/ (UK) /ˈfɑːɡboʊ/ (US)| n.|雾虹|A fogbow is a pale white rainbow that appears in fog rather than rain. It is formed by the same process as a rainbow, but the water droplets in fog are much smaller than raindrops, so the colors are less distinct and appear white.|We saw a beautiful fogbow on the mountain this morning. 我们今天早上在山上看到了一道美丽的雾虹。|近义词：white rainbow, cloudbow|反义词: rainbow|词性:noun
