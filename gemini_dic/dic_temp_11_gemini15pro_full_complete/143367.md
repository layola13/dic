outfawn:/ˌaʊtˈfɔːn/
verb
谄媚; 拍马屁; 巴结; 超过…的谄媚
To surpass or exceed in flattery or fawning.
He outfawned all the other courtiers to gain the king's favor. 他比其他所有朝臣都更会拍马屁，以获得国王的宠幸。
近义词:outflatter, brown-nose
反义词:criticize, disparage
词性:v.
