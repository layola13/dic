cenotaph: /ˈsɛnəˌtɑf/ | n. |衣冠冢；纪念碑（尤指为战争死难者而建，但并无遗体）| A monument erected in honor of a person or group of people whose remains are elsewhere. |  A cenotaph was erected in the town square to commemorate the fallen soldiers. 在城镇广场上竖立了一座衣冠冢，以纪念阵亡的士兵。 | synonyms: monument, memorial, shrine | antonyms: grave, tomb | noun
