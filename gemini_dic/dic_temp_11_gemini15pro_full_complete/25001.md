brutalizes:/ˈbruːtəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使残暴；残酷对待；兽性化
to treat someone or something in a brutal or cruel way
The dictator brutalized his people.  独裁者残酷对待他的人民。
The war brutalized the soldiers. 战争使士兵们变得残暴。

Synonyms: mistreats, abuses, maltreats, ill-treats, batters, assaults, violates
Antonyms: protects, cherishes, nurtures, cares for
词性: 动词
