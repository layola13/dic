hobbyist:/ˈhɒbiɪst/ (UK) /ˈhɑːbiɪst/ (US)| n.|业余爱好者；沉溺于某种癖好的人|A person who engages in a particular activity as a pastime rather than a profession.|She's a keen hobbyist photographer. 她是一位热衷摄影的业余爱好者。|enthusiast, amateur, devotee, buff|professional, expert|noun
etymology->From hobby + -ist. Hobby is from hobin, meaning "small horse, pony; toy horse."  The sense extension from "toy horse" to "favorite pastime" is unclear, but may have evolved through the image of a child's rocking horse.
