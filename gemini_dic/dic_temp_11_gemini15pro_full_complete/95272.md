incapacitate:/ˌɪnkəˈpæsɪteɪt/| vt.|使无能力，使丧失能力|To deprive of strength or ability; to disable.  To make legally incapable or ineligible.|The injury incapacitated him for several weeks.  这伤使他几周都不能动弹。|Synonyms: disable, cripple, paralyze, debilitate|Antonyms: enable, empower, strengthen|verb

incapacity:/ˌɪnkəˈpæsɪti/| n.|无能力，无力，丧失能力|Lack of physical or mental power; inability or disqualification.|His incapacity to make decisions led to the project's failure. 他无力做决定导致了项目的失败。|Synonyms: inability, impotence, disability|Antonyms: ability, capability, competence|noun
