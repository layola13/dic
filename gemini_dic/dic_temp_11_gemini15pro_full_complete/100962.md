inurn:/ɪnˈɜːrn/| vt.|安葬（骨灰）；将…放入瓮中|To place (ashes) in an urn, especially after cremation; to store in or as if in an urn.|例句：His ashes were inurned in the family mausoleum. 他的骨灰被安放在家族陵墓中。

例句：The museum inurned the ancient artifacts for safekeeping.  博物馆将古代文物放入瓮中保存。|近义词：inter, bury, entomb|反义词:exhume|词性:动词
