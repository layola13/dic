allenarly:/ˈælənərli/| adv.| only, solely, exclusively| solely; exclusively.  It emphasizes the restrictive nature of "only."|She allenarly focused on her studies, ignoring all distractions. 她只专注于学习，忽略了所有干扰。|近义词：only, solely, exclusively, merely|反义词: wholly, completely, entirely|词性:副词

词源:
Scottish, from allanerlie, from all + anerly 'only.'
