nondiminishing:/ˌnɒndɪˈmɪnɪʃɪŋ/
adjective
不减少的，不减弱的
Not becoming smaller or weaker; remaining constant in size, strength, or amount.
The nondiminishing enthusiasm of the fans kept the team motivated.  球迷们不减的热情激励着球队。
The medication provided nondiminishing pain relief for several hours.  这种药物能持续数小时缓解疼痛。

近义词:constant, unwavering, sustained, persistent, continuous
反义词:diminishing, decreasing, declining, waning, fading
词性:adjective


词源:
"Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "diminishing" (present participle of "diminish," from Old French "diminuer," from Latin "diminuere," from "de-" (down) + "minuere" (to lessen)).
