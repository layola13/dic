definer:/dɪˈfaɪnər/| n.|定义者; 确定者|A person or thing that defines something; that states or describes exactly the nature, scope, or meaning of something.|The dictionary is a definer of words.  这本字典是词汇的定义者。|Synonyms: interpreter, explainer, expounder|Antonyms: confuser, obscurer|noun
