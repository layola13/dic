deglazing:/diːˈɡleɪzɪŋ/ | n. | 煺釉;  去釉;  溶解锅底结块 | The process of adding liquid to a hot pan to loosen and dissolve browned food particles, creating a flavorful base for a sauce. | After searing the steak, I deglazed the pan with red wine. 在煎完牛排后，我用红酒给锅煺釉。| synonyms: fond deglaçage | antonyms: N/A | noun

deglaze:/diːˈɡleɪz/ | v. |  煺釉; 去釉; 溶解锅底结块 | To loosen and dissolve browned food particles from a pan by adding liquid, usually wine or stock. |  She deglazed the pan with chicken broth to make a gravy. 她用鸡汤给锅煺釉来做肉汁。 | synonyms: fond deglaçage | antonyms: N/A | verb
