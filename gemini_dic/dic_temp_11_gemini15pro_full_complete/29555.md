catchiness:/ˈkætʃɪnəs/| n.|朗朗上口；吸引力；感染力|The catchiness of the tune made it an instant hit. 这首曲子朗朗上口，一炮而红。|近义词：memorability, appeal, infectiousness|反义词：forgettable, dullness|词性:名词

Etymology: From catchy + -ness.  "Catchy" originates from "catch,"  meaning to seize or grasp, evolving to also describe something that readily attracts attention. The suffix "-ness" transforms the adjective "catchy" into the noun "catchiness," denoting the quality of being catchy.
