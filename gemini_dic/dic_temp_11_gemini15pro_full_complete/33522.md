cichoraceous:/ˌsɪkəˈreɪʃəs/
adjective
菊科的；像菊科植物的
Resembling plants of the chicory family (Cichoriaceae, now Asteraceae), typically having milky sap and flower heads composed of numerous small florets.
The dandelion, a common cichoraceous weed, is known for its bright yellow flowers. 蒲公英是一种常见的菊科杂草，以其鲜艳的黄色花朵而闻名。

The garden was filled with cichoraceous plants, their delicate blue flowers swaying in the breeze. 花园里满是菊科植物，它们精致的蓝色花朵在微风中摇曳。
synonym:chicory-like
antonym: (No clear single antonym, possibly non-cichoraceous)
词性: adjective
