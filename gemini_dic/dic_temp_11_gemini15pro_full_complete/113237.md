lusterless:/ˈlʌstər.ləs/ | adj. | 无光泽的；暗淡的 | Lacking luster; dull. | The old painting appeared lusterless and faded. 这幅旧画显得暗淡无光。| 近义词：dull, mat, matte, lackluster, dim | 反义词：lustrous, shiny, glossy, bright | 词性:形容词

词源: 
From luster + -less.  "Luster" comes from Old French "lustre" meaning "brightness, shine," ultimately from Latin "lustrare" meaning "to brighten, purify."  The suffix "-less" is an Old English suffix meaning "without."
