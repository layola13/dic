例句：He adjusted the equalizer to increase the bassness of the sound. 他调整均衡器以增加声音的低音。|近义词：depth, richness, resonance, profundity|反义词：trebleness, shrillness|词性:名词

词源: From "bass" (referring to low frequencies in sound) + "-ness" (a suffix forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition).
