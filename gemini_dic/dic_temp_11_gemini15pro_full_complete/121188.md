misdivision:/ˌmɪsdɪˈvɪʒən/
n.
错误除法，误分
an incorrect division, especially of a word at the end of a line of text
The misdivision of "happen" as "hap-pen" looks odd.  把“happen”错误地分成“hap-pen”看起来很奇怪。
misspelling, error
correct division
