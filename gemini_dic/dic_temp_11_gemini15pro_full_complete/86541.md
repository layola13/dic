hele: /ˈheɪlə/ | n. | 光环，晕，(夏威夷)家，房子 |A circle of light appearing to surround the sun or moon, caused by refraction through ice crystals in the atmosphere. Also used to refer to a home or house in Hawaiian. |例句：The sun had a faint hele around it. 太阳周围有一个微弱的光环。|例句：Welcome to my hele. 欢迎来到我的家。|近义词：halo, aura, (for house) home, house |反义词: (no direct antonym, perhaps) darkness |词性: 名词


词源: From Hawaiian *hale, meaning "house, home". Related to Proto-Polynesian *fale.
