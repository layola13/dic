coupure:/kuːˈpʊr/ (French pronunciation)| n.| 优惠券；切；切口；（尤指政府强制的）削减|A deduction or cut, especially one imposed by a government; a coupon.  It comes directly from French, where it has the same meaning.  Ultimately derived from the Latin word "couper," meaning "to cut."|The government announced a coupure in public spending. 政府宣布削减公共支出。

The magazine offered a coupure for a free trial subscription. 这本杂志提供了一张免费试用订阅的优惠券。|近义词：cut, reduction, decrease, voucher, coupon|反义词:increase, rise, addition|词性:noun
