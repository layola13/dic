fusteric: /ˈfʌstərɪk/ | adj. | 慌乱的，不安的 | Feeling or showing great agitation or不安; frenetic. |  The fusteric pace of modern life can be overwhelming. 现代生活的慌乱节奏可能会让人难以承受。

The dog became fusteric when separated from its owner.  这只狗与主人分开后变得慌乱不安。| 近义词: agitated, frenetic, frantic, distraught | 反义词: calm, composed, serene | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  Likely a blend of "fuss" and "hysteric," mimicking the sounds and sense of agitated activity.  No definitive etymological source exists; it's considered colloquial or dialectal.
