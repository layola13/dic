dive-bombed:/ˈdaɪv bɒmd/ (UK) /ˈdaɪv bɑːmd/ (US)| v.|俯冲轰炸，快速下降袭击|To attack something by flying steeply downwards towards it and then releasing bombs or firing guns before quickly climbing again.|The planes dive-bombed the enemy troops. 飞机俯冲轰炸了敌军。|近义词：attack, strafe, bombard|反义词: ascend, climb|词性: 动词

dive-bombed:/ˈdaɪv bɒmd/ (UK) /ˈdaɪv bɑːmd/ (US)| adj.|被俯冲轰炸的|Having been attacked by dive-bombing.|The dive-bombed city was in ruins. 被俯冲轰炸的城市已成废墟。|近义词：attacked, bombed|反义词: undefended|词性: 形容词
