advocating:/ˈædvəkeɪtɪŋ/
verb
提倡，主张，拥护
the act of publicly supporting or suggesting an idea, development, or way of doing something
She is advocating for stricter gun control laws. 她正在倡导更严格的枪支管制法律。
synonyms: supporting, promoting, recommending, championing
antonyms: opposing, criticizing, denouncing
v.
