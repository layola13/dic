egomaniacal: /ˌiːɡoʊˌmeɪniˈækəl/ | adj. | 极端自恋的，自负的 |  Characterized by excessive egotism or self-importance.  | He has an egomaniacal belief in his own genius. 他极度自负地相信自己是天才。 |  egotistic, narcissistic, self-centered, conceited | humble, modest, self-effacing | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From ego + maniac + -al.  "Ego" comes from Latin, meaning "I". "Maniac" comes from Greek "maniakos" meaning "mad, insane".  The suffix "-al" forms adjectives meaning "relating to".  Therefore, "egomaniacal" literally means relating to someone who is insane about themselves.
