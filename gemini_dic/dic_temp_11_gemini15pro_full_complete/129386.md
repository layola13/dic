/neɪm/ | n. |名字，名称，名声，名誉|A word or set of words by which a person, animal, place, or thing is known or referred to. |例句：What's your name? 你叫什么名字？

My name is John. 我的名字是约翰。|近义词：title, appellation, designation, moniker|反义词：anonymity, nameless|词性: noun
