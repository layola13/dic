cartouche:/kɑːrˈtuːʃ/ | n. |  涡卷形装饰;  铭牌;  子弹带;  (古埃及象形文字的)椭圆形圈 | An oval or oblong figure, as in Egyptian hieroglyphics, enclosing characters that represent the name of a sovereign or deity.  A decorative frame or cartouche surrounding a design, inscription, or painting. A cartridge belt. | The cartouche on the tomb identified the pharaoh. 墓穴上的铭牌标明了法老的身份。 | synonyms: medallion, tablet, insignia, ornament, decoration | antonyms: plainness, simplicity | noun

cartouch:/kɑːrˈtuːʃ/ | n. |  (尤指旧时士兵佩戴的)子弹带 | A bandoleer or cartridge belt. |  The soldier's cartouch held fifty rounds.  那个士兵的子弹带装着五十发子弹。| synonyms: cartridge belt, bandoleer, bandolier, ammunition belt | antonyms: N/A | noun
