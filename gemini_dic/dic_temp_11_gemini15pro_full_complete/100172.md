interregional:/ˌɪntərˈriːdʒənl/| adj.|区域间的，跨区域的|Occurring between regions; involving or connecting two or more regions.|例句：Interregional trade is crucial for economic growth. 区域间贸易对经济增长至关重要。

例句：The interregional highway system facilitates transportation. 跨区域高速公路系统促进了交通运输。|近义词：international, multiregional|反义词：local, regional|词性:形容词
Etymology:  "inter-" (between) + "regional" (relating to a region).  "Inter-" comes from Latin, meaning "between, among." "Regional" comes from Late Latin "regionalis,"  from Latin "regio" meaning "direction, district, region."
