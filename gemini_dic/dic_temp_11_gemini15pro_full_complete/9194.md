antiodontalgic:/ˌæntiˌoʊdɑːnˈtældʒɪk/
noun|止牙痛药|A remedy for toothache.|She took an antiodontalgic for her throbbing tooth. 她服用了一种止痛药来治疗她抽痛的牙齿。|Synonyms: odontalgic analgesic, toothache remedy|Antonyms: pain intensifier|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- (against) + odont- (tooth) + -algic (pain).  The prefix "anti-" signifies opposition or counteraction. "Odont-" comes from the Greek "odous," meaning tooth. "-algic" relates to pain, derived from the Greek "algos."
