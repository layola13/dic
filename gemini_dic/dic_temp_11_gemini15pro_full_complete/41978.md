cost-accountants:/ˈkɔːst əˈkaʊntənts/ |n.| 成本会计师 | Professionals who track and analyze a company's costs associated with production or services. They use this information to help management make informed business decisions. | The cost-accountants prepared a detailed report on the manufacturing expenses. 成本会计师准备了一份详细的制造成本报告。|  The cost-accountants identified areas where the company could reduce spending. 成本会计师确定了公司可以减少开支的领域。 |Synonyms: management accountants, cost analysts| Antonyms: N/A (no direct opposite for a profession)| noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"cost" (from Old French "cost" meaning "price, value") + "accountant" (from Old French "acontant" meaning "accountable")
