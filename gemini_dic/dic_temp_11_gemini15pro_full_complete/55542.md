distributive:/dɪˈstrɪbjətɪv/| adj.|分配的，分发的；[数] 分配的| relating to or denoting the distribution of resources or goods. | The government implemented distributive policies to aid low-income families. 政府实施了分配政策来帮助低收入家庭。|近义词：allocative, apportioning |反义词: collective, cumulative |词性: adjective

distributives:/dɪˈstrɪbjətɪvz/| n.|分配词| words indicating shared portions among a group. |  Each, every, either, and neither are examples of distributives.  each, every, either 和 neither 都是分配词的例子。|近义词：partitives |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *distributif, *distributyfe, from Late Latin distributivus, from Latin distribut- ‘distributed’, from the verb distribuere, from dis- ‘apart’ + tribuere ‘assign, allot’.
