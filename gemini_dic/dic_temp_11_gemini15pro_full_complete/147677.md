overpowerful:/ˌoʊvərˈpaʊərfl/
adj.
过分强大的，过于强势的
Having excessive power; too strong or powerful.
The overpowerful central government stifled local initiatives.  过于强势的中央政府扼杀了地方的积极性。

The overpowerful king ruled with an iron fist.  这个权力过大的国王实行铁腕统治。

近义词:dominant, overwhelming, overpowering, preponderant
反义词:weak, powerless, feeble
词性:adjective

词源: over- + powerful
