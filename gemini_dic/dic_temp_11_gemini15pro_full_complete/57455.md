draffish:/ˈdræfɪʃ/
adjective
渣滓的；难吃的；无味的
Tasting like or resembling draff; worthless or flavorless.
The beer tasted stale and draffish. 这啤酒尝起来又陈又难喝。
近义词:stale, insipid, tasteless, vapid
反义词:flavorful, delicious, savory, tasty
词性:adjective

draffish:/ˈdræfɪʃ/
adjective
粗糙的；粗俗的；没有教养的
Lacking refinement; coarse or vulgar.
He made a draffish remark that offended everyone. 他说了一句粗俗的话，冒犯了所有人。
近义词:coarse, vulgar, uncouth, crude
反义词:refined, polished, cultivated, sophisticated
词性:adjective
