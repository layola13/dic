boggard:/ˈbɒɡərd/ (UK) /ˈbɑːɡərd/ (US)| n.| 鬼怪，妖怪；吓人的东西；稻草人|A boggart is a mischievous household spirit in English folklore. It is shape-shifting and often takes the form of the thing a person fears most.|例句：The children were scared of the boggart in the attic. 孩子们害怕阁楼里的妖怪。

|例句：She thought she saw a boggart lurking in the shadows. 她认为她看到一个妖怪潜伏在阴影中。|近义词：goblin, hobgoblin, bogeyman, specter, phantom|反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps) protector, guardian|词性: noun
