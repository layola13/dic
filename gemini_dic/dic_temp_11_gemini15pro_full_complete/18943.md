besprinkling:/bɪˈsprɪŋklɪŋ/
|n.|洒，撒；点缀|The act of scattering small drops or particles of something onto a surface.|The besprinkling of sugar on the cake added a touch of sweetness. 在蛋糕上撒糖增加了一丝甜味。|近义词：sprinkling, scattering, dusting|反义词：drenching, soaking|词性：名词

besprinkling:/bɪˈsprɪŋklɪŋ/
|v.|洒，撒；点缀|(present participle of besprinkle) To scatter small drops or particles of something onto a surface.|She was besprinkling the flowers with water. 她正在给花洒水。|近义词：sprinkling, scattering, dusting|反义词：drenching, soaking|词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + sprinkle, from Middle English sprinklen, of uncertain origin.
