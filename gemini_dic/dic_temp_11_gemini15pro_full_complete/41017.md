copartnership:/koʊˈpɑːrtnərʃɪp/
n.
合作；合伙；合伙企业
A business undertaking jointly owned and operated by two or more persons under a legal agreement.
Example 1: They formed a copartnership to develop the new software. 他们成立了一个合伙企业来开发新的软件。

Example 2: The copartnership dissolved after a year due to disagreements.  由于分歧，该合伙企业一年后解散了。
近义词: partnership, alliance, collaboration
反义词: sole proprietorship
