argas:/ˈɑːrɡəs/ | n. | soft ticks | Soft ticks, a genus of ticks in the family Argasidae. They are parasites of birds, mammals, and reptiles. |  The argas tick is a known vector for several diseases.  软蜱是几种疾病的已知媒介。 | synonyms: soft tick,  argasid tick | antonyms: hard tick | noun

Etymology: From Modern Latin *Argas*, derived from Persian *argās* (ارگس), meaning "tick".
