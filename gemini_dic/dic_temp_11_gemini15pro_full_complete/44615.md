crowding:/ˈkraʊdɪŋ/| n.|拥挤，聚集|The crowding at the concert made it difficult to move.  演唱会上人太多，很难移动。|例句：The crowding in the subway during rush hour is unbearable.  高峰时段地铁里的拥挤令人难以忍受。|近义词：congestion, thronging, packing|反义词: sparseness, emptiness|noun
crowding:/ˈkraʊdɪŋ/| adj.|拥挤的|The crowding streets were filled with vendors. 拥挤的街道上挤满了小贩。|例句：The crowding bus made it difficult to breathe. 拥挤的公交车让人难以呼吸。|近义词：packed, congested, crammed|反义词: empty, spacious|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From crowd (v.) + -ing.  Ultimately from Old English *crudan "to press, urge."
