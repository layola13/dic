cokernut:/ˈkoʊkərˌnʌt/| n. |椰子|A large, hard-shelled fruit of a tropical palm tree, containing white flesh and a milky liquid.|例句：I love the taste of fresh cokernut. 我喜欢新鲜椰子的味道。|例句：We drank the milk from the cokernut. 我们喝了椰子的汁。|近义词：coconut|反义词: None|词性: noun
Etymology: From obsolete *cokernut, alteration (influenced by cock (n.1)) of Middle English cokenut, from Old Spanish cogombre (later coco), from Latin cucumis (“cucumber”), perhaps referring to the nut's shape. The form coco is a borrowing from Portuguese and Spanish.
