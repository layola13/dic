freeing:/ˈfriːɪŋ/
adjective
释放的，使人自由的
The freeing of the hostages was a cause for celebration.  人质的获释是一个值得庆祝的理由。
It was a freeing experience to finally tell the truth.  最终说出真相是一种解脱的经历。
synonyms: liberating, releasing, emancipating
antonyms: confining, restricting, imprisoning
词性: 形容词


freeing:/ˈfriːɪŋ/
verb
释放，使自由
They are freeing the prisoners. 他们正在释放囚犯。
The new law is freeing up the market. 新法律正在开放市场。
synonyms: liberating, releasing, emancipating
antonyms: imprisoning, confining, restricting
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English "freo" (free) + "-ing" (present participle suffix).  The word "free" traces back to Proto-Germanic "*frijaz" and ultimately to Proto-Indo-European "*prijos" (dear, beloved, own). The "-ing" suffix denotes an action or process.
