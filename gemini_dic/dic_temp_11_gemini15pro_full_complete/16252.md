bantering: /ˈbæntərɪŋ/ | adj. |  开玩笑的，打趣的 | Characterized by good-humored teasing or playful exchange of remarks. |  They spent the afternoon in bantering conversation. 他们一下午都在说笑打趣。| 近义词: teasing, playful, joking, lighthearted | 反义词: serious, somber, earnest | 词性:形容词

banter: /ˈbæntər/ | n. vi. vt. |  玩笑，打趣；说笑，开玩笑 |  n. The exchange of playful remarks.  vi. vt. To exchange playful remarks. |  He enjoyed exchanging banter with his colleagues. 他喜欢和同事们互相开玩笑。  They bantered back and forth about their weekend plans.  他们就周末计划互相打趣。 | 近义词: teasing, joking, raillery, badinage | 反义词: seriousness, solemnity | 词性:名词，动词

词源:  From French *banter*, perhaps from Old French *banter* "to brag, boast," of unknown origin.
