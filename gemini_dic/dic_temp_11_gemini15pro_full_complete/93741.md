immaterialness:/ˌɪməˈtɪəriəlnəs/ | n. | 非物质性；无关紧要；无足轻重 | The immaterialness of the soul is a topic of philosophical debate. 灵魂的非物质性是哲学辩论的一个主题。|  The immaterialness of his comments made them easy to ignore.  他的评论无关紧要，所以很容易被忽略。| synonyms: insignificance, irrelevance, unimportance | antonyms:  materiality, importance, relevance, significance | noun
