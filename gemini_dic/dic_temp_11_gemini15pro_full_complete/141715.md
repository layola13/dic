oracularly:/ɔːˈrækjʊlərli/ /ɔˈrækjələli/
adverb
神谕般地；玄妙地；故弄玄虚地
in a way that suggests that someone knows more than ordinary people, in a way that seems wise and important, but often is not
例句：He pronounced oracularly on the future of the industry. 他神谕般地宣称了该行业的未来。

例句：She spoke oracularly about the coming changes. 她故弄玄虚地谈论着即将到来的变化。

近义词：prophetically, enigmatically, mysteriously, cryptically
反义词：clearly, plainly, directly, straightforwardly
词性：adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From oracle + -ly.  "Oracle" comes from the Latin "oraculum," meaning "divine announcement" or "place where divine pronouncements are made," ultimately from "orare," meaning "to speak" or "to pray."
