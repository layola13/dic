enneahedron:/ˌɛniəˈhɛdrən/ | n. |九面体|A solid figure with nine plane faces.|A nonagon is a two-dimensional enneahedron.  一个九边形是一个二维的九面体。|The enneahedron is a complex shape to study.  九面体是一个复杂的研究形状。|polyhedron | - | noun
etymology: From Ancient Greek ἐννέα (ennéa, “nine”) + ἕδρα (hédra, “seat, base, face”).
