coli:/ˈkoʊlaɪ/| n.|大肠杆菌|A type of bacteria that commonly lives in the intestines of humans and animals.  It can sometimes cause illness.|Example:  E. coli contamination can cause food poisoning. 大肠杆菌污染会导致食物中毒。|Example:  Scientists often use E. coli in genetic research. 科学家经常在大肠杆菌中进行基因研究。|近义词:bacteria|反义词:None|Noun
