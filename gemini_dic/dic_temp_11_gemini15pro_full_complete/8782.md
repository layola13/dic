antieducation:/ˌæntiˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃən/| n.|反教育，反教育化|The process of acquiring information or attitudes that are harmful, misleading, or contrary to formal education.  It often involves the spread of misinformation, propaganda, or biased viewpoints that hinder critical thinking and understanding.  It can also refer to experiences or environments that actively discourage learning or intellectual growth.|His constant exposure to conspiracy theories online led to a kind of antieducation, making him distrustful of established scientific facts.|近义词：miseducation, disinformation, indoctrination|反义词:education, enlightenment, edification|词性:名词
