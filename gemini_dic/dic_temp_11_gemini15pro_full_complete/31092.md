champiest:/ˈtʃæmpiɪst/
adjective
最具冠军相的，最有冠军风范的
Most like a champion; having the most qualities of a champion.
He had the champiest grin after winning the race.  他赢得比赛后露出了最有冠军风范的笑容。
近义词: most triumphant, most victorious
反义词: losing, defeated
词性: adjective
词源: champion + -iest (superlative suffix)
