nonextensile:/ˌnɒnɪkˈstɛnsaɪl/
adjective
非广延的，非可扩展的
Not capable of being extended or stretched; not extensive.  Relating to systems where the entropy is not an extensive quantity (i.e., not proportional to the system size).
This behavior is observed in nonextensive systems like self-gravitating systems.  这种行为在像自引力系统这样的非广延系统中可以观察到。
The researchers studied the nonextensile properties of the plasma. 研究人员研究了等离子体的非广延特性。
Near synonyms: inextensible, non-stretchable
Antonyms: extensive, extensible, stretchable
词性: adjective
