nonfisherman:/ˌnɒnˈfɪʃəmən/ /ˌnɑːnˈfɪʃərmən/| n. |非钓鱼者；不钓鱼的人|A person who does not fish.| He's a nonfisherman, so he doesn't understand the thrill of the catch. 他不是钓鱼的人，所以他不理解钓鱼的乐趣。| 近义词: non-angler | 反义词: fisherman, angler | 词性: noun


Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
fisherman: from Old English fiscere "fisher" + mann "man"
