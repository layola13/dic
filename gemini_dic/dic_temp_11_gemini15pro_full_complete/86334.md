hecki:/ˈhɛki/| interj.|见鬼，该死|An intensifier used to express surprise, frustration, or anger.  It's a milder form of "hell."|Hecki, I forgot my keys! 见鬼，我忘了带钥匙了！|近义词：heck, darn, shoot|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider words conveying calmness like "great," "wonderful")|感叹词

What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What does he do for a living? = 他以什么为生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you cooking for dinner? = 你晚饭在做什么？
What is she working on right now? = 她现在在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has he accomplished this year? = 他今年完成了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
