manesheet:/ˈmeɪnʃiːt/
noun
马匹名册；马匹登记表
A list or register of horses, especially those belonging to a particular stable or owner.
The stable owner consulted the manesheet to check the lineage of the new foal. 马厩主人查阅了马匹名册，以检查新小马驹的血统。
synonyms: horse register, studbook, equine register
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms exist.  One could consider terms like "deregistered horses" or "unlisted horses" as conceptually opposite.)
词性: 名词
