over-readiness:/ˈoʊvərˈrɛdinɪs/| n. |过度准备；过分 готовность|A state of being excessively prepared or too eager to act.  It implies a level of preparedness that is beyond what is necessary or appropriate, often leading to premature action or a heightened sense of anticipation that can be detrimental. |His over-readiness to please his boss sometimes led him to make mistakes. 他过分渴望取悦老板，有时反而导致他犯错。

The team's over-readiness for the game resulted in a nervous and disjointed performance. 球队对比赛的过度准备导致了紧张和不连贯的表现。|近义词：over-eagerness, over-zealousness, impatience|反义词:hesitation, reluctance, unpreparedness|词性:名词
