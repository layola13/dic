misallotments:/ˌmɪsæˈlɒtmənts/ | n. | 错误分配，分配不当 | An act of allotting wrongly or improperly.  The result of such an act. |  Due to several misallotments of funds, the project ran over budget. 由于资金分配的几次错误，该项目超出了预算。| The misallotments of resources led to significant delays. 资源分配不当导致了严重的延误。 | synonyms: misallocations, misdistributions, misappropriations | antonyms: proper allocations, correct distributions | noun
