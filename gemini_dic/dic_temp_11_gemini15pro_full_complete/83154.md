gutter:/ˈɡʌtər/| n.|排水沟；槽；贫民区|A channel at the edge of a road or roof for carrying away rainwater.  道路或屋顶边缘用于排水的沟渠。|The gutters were overflowing with rainwater. 排水沟里的雨水溢出来了。|synonyms: channel, drain, trough|antonyms: ridge, peak|noun


gutter:/ˈɡʌtər/| v.|流出成槽；形成沟壑|To flow in streams or gutters.  流成一股股或形成沟壑。|The candle guttered and died. 蜡烛忽明忽暗地燃着, 然后熄灭了。|synonyms: stream, flow, trickle|antonyms: stagnate, dry up|verb
