frontest:/ˈfrʌntɪst/| adj. (non-standard)|最前面的，最前方的|This word is not standard English and is considered incorrect. It is likely a hypercorrection or a childish form of "frontmost" or "foremost". It attempts to express the superlative degree of "front" but does so incorrectly.  The standard forms are "frontmost" or "foremost."|"He tried to get the frontest seat in the classroom." (non-standard usage) |近义词：frontmost, foremost, leading, first|反义词: hindmost, last, rearmost|词性:adjective
