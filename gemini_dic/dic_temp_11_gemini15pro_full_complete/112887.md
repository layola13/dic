luddite:/ˈlʌdaɪt/| n.| 勒德分子；反对技术进步的人|A person opposed to new technology or ways of working, especially one who believes that technological advances will lead to unemployment.|The factory owner accused the protesting workers of being Luddites, unwilling to adapt to the new machinery.|Synonyms: reactionary, conservative, opponent of progress|Antonyms: progressive, innovator, modernist|noun

luddite:/ˈlʌdaɪt/| adj.| 反对技术进步的|Opposed to technological change.|His Luddite attitude prevented him from embracing the benefits of computers.|Synonyms: anti-technology, technophobic|Antonyms: pro-technology, technophilic|adjective
