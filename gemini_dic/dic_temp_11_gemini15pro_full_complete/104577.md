kangaroolike:/ˌkæŋɡəˈruːlaɪk/
adjective
像袋鼠的，袋鼠似的
Resembling or characteristic of a kangaroo, especially in having long powerful hind legs and a large tail.
The small rodent had a kangaroolike hop.  这只小啮齿动物像袋鼠一样跳跃。
The animal moved with a kangaroolike gait.  这只动物以袋鼠般的步态移动。
Synonym:marsupial-like
Antonym:N/A
词性:adjective
