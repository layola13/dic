flirtatious:/flɜːrˈteɪʃəs/ | adj. | 轻浮的，调情的，卖弄风情的 | Behaving in such a way as to suggest a playful sexual attraction to someone. | She gave him a flirtatious glance. 她轻佻地看了他一眼。 |  He has a flirtatious manner. 他举止轻浮。 | 近义词: coquettish, playful, teasing, seductive | 反义词: serious, reserved, modest | 词性: adjective

flirtatiously:/flɜːrˈteɪʃəsli/ | adv. | 轻浮地，调情地，卖弄风情地 | In a flirtatious manner. | She smiled flirtatiously at him. 她轻浮地对他笑了笑。 | She danced flirtatiously with several men. 她和几个男人调情地跳舞。 | 近义词: coquettishly, playfully, teasingly | 反义词: seriously, reservedly, modestly | 词性: adverb

flirtatiousness:/flɜːrˈteɪʃəsnəs/ | n. | 轻浮，调情，卖弄风情 | The quality or state of being flirtatious. | Her flirtatiousness was obvious to everyone. 她的轻浮对每个人来说都很明显。 | He was annoyed by her flirtatiousness. 他对她的轻浮感到恼火。 | 近义词: coquetry, playfulness, teasing | 反义词: seriousness, reserve, modesty | 词性: noun


词源: From flirt + -atious.  Ultimately from Old Norse *flyrta "to flutter".
