boneshaw:/ˈboʊnˌʃɔː/
n.
骨锯，骨刀
A surgical saw used for cutting bone.
The surgeon used a boneshaw to remove the damaged portion of the bone.  外科医生用骨锯切除了骨头的受损部分。

The boneshaw was sterilized before the operation. 骨锯在手术前进行了消毒。
synonym: bone saw, osteotome
antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps bone healer/mender as a conceptual opposite)
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From 'bone' + 'saw'.  'Bone' refers to the material the tool is used on, and 'saw' refers to the type of tool it is.
