hemispheroid:/ˌhemɪˈsfɪrɔɪd/| n. |半球体，半球形|A solid resembling a hemisphere.  A three-dimensional shape that is half of a spheroid, created by cutting a spheroid along a plane containing two of its axes.|The Earth is not a perfect sphere, but rather an oblate hemispheroid.|Synonym: hemisphere|Antonym: sphere|noun
