literalness:/ˈlɪtərəlnəs/| n.|字面意思；拘泥字面；缺乏想象力|The literalness of her interpretation left no room for nuance.|她对字面意思的拘泥使得解释没有细微差别。|synonyms: exactness, precision, accuracy|antonyms: figurativeness, abstractness, symbolism|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From literal + -ness.  "literal" comes from Late Latin *litteralis "of a letter," from Latin littera/litera "letter of the alphabet."
