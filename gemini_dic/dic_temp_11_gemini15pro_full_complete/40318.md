twisted or folded back on itself, often multiple times.
The contortuplicate branches of the old oak tree created a haunting silhouette against the twilight sky.  古老橡树盘绕的树枝在暮色映衬下，形成一个令人难忘的轮廓。

近义词: convoluted, intertwined, entangled
反义词: straight, unfurled, simple
词性: adjective
词源:  From Latin *contortuplicatus, past participle of *contortuplicare, from con- 'together' + tortus 'twisted' (past participle of torquēre) + -plicare 'to fold' (related to plicāre).
