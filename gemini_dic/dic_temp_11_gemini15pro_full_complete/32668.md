chivalric: /ʃɪˈvælrɪk/ /ʃɪˈvæl.rɪk/ | adj. | 骑士的；骑士精神的；侠义的 | Relating to or characteristic of knights or knighthood; marked by honor, courtesy, and valor. |  He displayed a chivalric attitude by opening the door for her. 他为她开门展现了骑士风度。

The chivalric code emphasized bravery and loyalty. 骑士准则强调勇敢和忠诚。 | 近义词: knightly, gallant, courteous, honorable | 反义词: cowardly, rude, dishonorable | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Old French *chivalerique, from chevaler (“knight”), from Late Latin caballarius (“horseman”), from Latin caballus (“horse”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱéh₂wlos (“horse”).
