invested: /ɪnˈvɛstɪd/ | v. | 投资；投入（时间、精力等）；授予；赋予 |  put (money) into financial schemes, shares, property, or a commercial venture with the expectation of achieving a profit. Also means to devote (one's time, effort, or energy) to a particular undertaking with the expectation of a worthwhile result. |  He invested a lot of money in the stock market.  他投资了很多钱在股市。 | synonyms:  funded, financed, staked, endowed, contributed, granted, bestowed | antonyms: divested, withdrew, liquidated | verb

invested: /ɪnˈvɛstɪd/ | adj. | 充满的；被赋予的 |  Having a personal stake or involvement in something. | She was deeply invested in the success of the project. 她对这个项目的成功投入了很大的精力。 | synonyms:  involved, engaged, committed, dedicated | antonyms: detached, indifferent, apathetic | adjective
