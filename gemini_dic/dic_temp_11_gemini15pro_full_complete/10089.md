apodal:/ˈeɪpədəl/| adj.|无足的，无肢的|Lacking feet or legs; limbless. |The apodal lizard slithered through the grass. 这只无足蜥蜴在草丛中滑行。|Snakes and some lizards are apodal. 蛇和一些蜥蜴是无足的。|近义词：legless, limbless, footless|反义词：legged, limbed, footed|词性: adjective
Etymology: From Greek *ἀ- (a-, “without”) + *πούς (poús, “foot”).
