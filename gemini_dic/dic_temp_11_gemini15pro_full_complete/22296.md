bongos:/ˈbɒŋɡəʊz/ (UK) /ˈbɑːŋɡoʊz/ (US)| n. |邦戈鼓|A pair of small drums played with the fingers, usually one slightly larger than the other, originating in Cuba.|He played a lively rhythm on the bongos.|Synonyms: conga drums (similar but larger), hand drums|Antonyms: None (lack of a direct opposite for a specific instrument)|noun

bongos:/ˈbɒŋɡəʊz/ (UK) /ˈbɑːŋɡoʊz/ (US)| n. |邦戈鼓(复数)|Referring to a single set of bongos (two drums). While "bongo" can technically refer to a single drum, it's almost always used in the plural to refer to the pair.|She bought a new set of bongos.|Synonyms:  a pair of bongos|Antonyms: None|noun
