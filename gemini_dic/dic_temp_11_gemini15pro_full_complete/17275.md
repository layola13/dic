baysnet:/ˈbeɪznɛt/ | n. |刺刀| A bayonet is a knife, sword, or spike-shaped weapon designed to attach to the muzzle of a rifle, musket or similar firearm, used in hand-to-hand combat. | The soldier fixed his bayonet to his rifle. 士兵把刺刀装到步枪上。|  synonyms: blade, dagger, knife, sword | antonyms: sheath, scabbard | noun
