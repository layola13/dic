harpooners:/hɑːrˈpuːnərz/
n.
捕鲸人; 用鱼叉捕鱼的人
People who hunt whales or other large marine animals with harpoons.
The harpooners were ready to strike the whale. 捕鲸人准备好要击中鲸鱼了。
The skilled harpooners quickly secured their catch.  熟练的捕鲸人迅速捕获了猎物。

近义词:whalers, fishermen
反义词:conservationists
