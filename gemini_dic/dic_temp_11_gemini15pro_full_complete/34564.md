clearage:/ˈklɪərɪdʒ/| n. |清除；清理；净空；净空高度|The process of clearing something, especially land or a forested area.  The clearage of the land for farming. |例句：The clearage of the forest for agriculture has had a devastating impact on the local ecosystem.  为了农业而清理森林对当地生态系统造成了毁灭性的影响。

例句：The bridge has a clearage of 16 feet. 这座桥的净空高度为16英尺。|近义词：clearing, clearance|反义词: accumulation, accretion|词性:名词
