have-a-finger-in-the-pie:/ˌhæv ə ˈfɪŋɡər ɪn ðə ˈpaɪ/| idiom|插手；干预；参与|To be involved in something, often in a meddling or interfering way.|例句：He seems to have a finger in every pie in this town. 他似乎插手了这个镇上的每一件事。|近义词：be involved in, meddle in, interfere in|反义词:stay out of, keep out of| idiom


have-a-finger-in-every-pie:/ˌhæv ə ˈfɪŋɡər ɪn ˈevri ˈpaɪ/| idiom|事事参与；多管闲事|To be involved in a large number of activities or enterprises.|例句：She has a finger in every pie, from the school board to the local charity. 她参与了各种活动，从学校董事会到当地慈善机构都有她的身影。|近义词：be involved in everything, be a busybody|反义词:mind one's own business| idiom
