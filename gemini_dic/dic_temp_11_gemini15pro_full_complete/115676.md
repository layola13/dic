margaritic:/ˌmɑːrɡəˈrɪtɪk/
adjective
珍珠似的；与珍珠相关的
Resembling or relating to pearls; pearly.
The oyster produced a margaritic gem.  这只牡蛎产出了一颗珍珠般美丽的宝石。
The inside of the shell had a margaritic sheen. 贝壳的内部有着珍珠般的光泽。
近义词:pearly, nacreous
反义词:dull, matte
词性:adjective
