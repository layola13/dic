equiformity:/ˌiːkwɪˈfɔːrməti/ | n. | 一致性；相同性；等形 | The state or quality of being equiform; uniformity of form or structure. | The equiformity of the two triangles suggests they are congruent. 这两个三角形的等形性表明它们是全等的。 |  The equiformity of the snowflakes was remarkable. 这些雪花的相同性非常引人注目。 | synonyms: uniformity, similarity, resemblance, likeness, sameness, congruence | antonyms: difference, dissimilarity, variation, diversity | noun

equiform:/ˈiːkwɪfɔːrm/ | adj. | 等形的; 相同的 | Having the same form or shape. | The two crystals are equiform. 这两个晶体是等形的。 | The architect designed equiform buildings for the housing project.  建筑师为该住房项目设计了相同的建筑物。 | synonyms: similar, identical, uniform, alike, congruent | antonyms: different, dissimilar, diverse, varied | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *aequiformis* (equal in form), from *aequus* (equal) + *forma* (form).
