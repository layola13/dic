nonuniquenesses:/ˌnɒnjuːˈniːknəsɪz/
|n.|非唯一性；非独特性|The nonuniquenesses of the solutions pose a challenge for the engineers. 这些解决方案的非唯一性给工程师们带来了挑战。|non-uniquenesses is the plural form of nonuniqueness, meaning the lack of uniqueness; the state of not being the only one of its kind.  It refers to situations where there are multiple valid solutions, outcomes, or instances of something.|The nonuniquenesses of the experimental results made it difficult to draw a definitive conclusion. 实验结果的非唯一性使得很难得出明确的结论。|
|近义词:multiplicity, plurality, variety|反义词:uniqueness, singularity|词性:名词
