monazite:/ˈmɒnəzaɪt/ (US) /ˈmɒnəzaɪt/ (UK)| n. |独居石|A reddish-brown phosphate mineral containing cerium, lanthanum, and thorium, found in igneous and metamorphic rock and used as a source of thorium.  It is a rare earth phosphate mineral containing the light rare earth elements (LREE) cerium, lanthanum, neodymium, praseodymium, and samarium, and the heavy rare earth elements (HREE) gadolinium, dysprosium, erbium, and ytterbium, often with thorium and sometimes uranium.|Monazite is an important source of thorium. 独居石是钍的重要来源。

Monazite sands are often found on beaches. 独居石砂通常在海滩上发现。|近义词: (No direct synonym, but related minerals include) allanite, bastnäsite|反义词: None|词性: noun
