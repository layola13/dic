impasse:/ˈɪmpæs/ | n. |僵局，死胡同 |A situation in which no progress is possible, especially because of disagreement; a deadlock.|The negotiations reached an impasse, and neither side was willing to compromise. 谈判陷入了僵局，双方都不愿意妥协。| synonyms: deadlock, stalemate, standstill, gridlock | antonyms: breakthrough, agreement, solution | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *impasse*, from *im*- (not) + *passe* (passage).  Ultimately from Latin *passus* (step, pace).
