jilted:/ˈdʒɪltɪd/| adj.|被抛弃的，被拒绝的，失恋的|Left abruptly and cruelly by a romantic partner, especially shortly before the expected marriage.|She felt jilted and humiliated when he broke off their engagement just weeks before the wedding. 她感到被抛弃和羞辱，因为他在婚礼前几周解除了婚约。|近义词：abandoned, deserted, rejected, spurned, dumped|反义词：accepted, chosen|词性:形容词

词源:
From jilt (v.), perhaps related to dialectal jillet "flirtatious girl," of uncertain origin.
