Not blending together; incongruous. | The nonharmonious combination of colors clashed.  这些不和谐的色彩组合在一起很不协调。 | The meeting was marked by nonharmonious debate. 会议的特点是争论不休。 | synonyms: discordant, dissonant, inharmonious, clashing, conflicting, jarring, cacophonous | antonyms: harmonious, consonant, melodious, tuneful, agreeable | adjective
