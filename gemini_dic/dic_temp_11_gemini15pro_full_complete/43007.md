cowardly:/ˈkaʊərdli/| adj.|懦弱的，胆小的|Lacking courage; timid.|He made a cowardly retreat from the battle. 他从战场上胆怯地撤退了。|近义词：timid, fearful, pusillanimous|反义词：brave, courageous, valiant|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *cowardly*, equivalent to *coward* + *-ly*.  *Coward* comes from Old French *coart*, from Vulgar Latin *coda*, *cauda* (“tail”), from Latin *cauda* (“tail”).  The original sense was of an animal with its tail between its legs, hence timid.
