finchbacked:/ˈfɪntʃˌbækt/
adjective
雀背的； having a back like a finch, typically streaked or patterned in brown and other colours.
The finchbacked flycatcher is a small, active bird.  雀背鹟是一种小型而活跃的鸟。
The ornithologist identified the bird as a finchbacked pipit. 鸟类学家将这只鸟鉴定为雀背鹨。
Near Synonyms: streaked, patterned, mottled, speckled
Antonyms: plain, unpatterned
Word Origin: finch + backed.  'Finch' refers to a family of small passerine birds, and 'backed' indicates having a back of a specific kind.
