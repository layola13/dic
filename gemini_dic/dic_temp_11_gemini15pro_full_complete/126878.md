napster:/ˈnæpstər/ | n. | 纳普斯特（一个早期的点对点文件共享服务，主要用于共享MP3音乐文件） | Napster was a pioneering peer-to-peer file sharing service that facilitated the sharing of MP3 music files over the internet. It was highly controversial due to copyright infringement issues. |  Napster was shut down due to legal pressure from the music industry. 纳普斯特由于受到音乐行业的法律压力而被关闭。

Napster changed the way people consumed music. 纳普斯特改变了人们消费音乐的方式。| 近义词: peer-to-peer file sharing service, music sharing platform | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider) legitimate music streaming service | 词性: noun
