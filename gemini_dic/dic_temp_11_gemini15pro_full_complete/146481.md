overfurnished:/ˌoʊvərˈfɜːrnɪʃt/
adjective
家具过多的；过于装饰的
Having too much furniture; excessively furnished.
The room was dark and overfurnished, making it feel cramped. 房间里又黑又挤，摆满了家具，让人感觉很局促。
The Victorian house was overfurnished and cluttered. 这栋维多利亚时代的房子家具过多，显得杂乱。
近义词:cluttered, crammed
反义词:sparsely furnished, minimalist
词性:adjective
