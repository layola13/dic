contemn:/kənˈtɛm/| vt.|鄙视，蔑视，轻视|To treat or regard with contempt; scorn.|He contemned their hypocrisy. 他鄙视他们的虚伪。|近义词：despise, scorn, disdain, spurn|反义词：admire, respect, esteem, venerate|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin contemnere, from com- (together) + temnere (to slight).
