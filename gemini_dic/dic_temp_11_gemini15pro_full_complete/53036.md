dioecious:/daɪˈiːʃəs/ | adj. | 雌雄异株的 | Having male and female reproductive organs in separate individuals. | Holly trees are dioecious, meaning you need a male and a female plant to get berries. 冬青树是雌雄异株的，这意味着你需要一株雄株和一株雌株才能得到浆果。| The ginkgo tree is a dioecious species. 银杏树是雌雄异株的树种。 | monoecious, hermaphroditic | N/A | adjective
