abietine:/ˈæbiəˌtaɪn/
noun
松香脂，枞树脂
A resinous substance derived from fir trees.
Abietine is used in the production of varnishes. 松香脂用于制造清漆。
Synonyms: rosin, resin
Antonyms: None
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *abiētīnus (“of fir, fir-like”), from abiēs (“fir tree”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ebʰ- (“fir, aspen”).
