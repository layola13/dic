interstitial's:/ˌɪntərˈstɪʃəlz/ | n. |间隙的；组织间隙的；|Relating to or situated in the interstices or gaps between things, especially between the cells of a tissue.|The interstitial's fluid helps to transport nutrients and remove waste products.|近义词：intervening, intermediate |反义词:connected, continuous|词性:名词所有格 (通常情况下interstitial本身是形容词，这里加了's表示"间质的...")


interstitial:/ˌɪntərˈstɪʃəl/ | adj. |间隙的；组织间隙的；|Situated within the interstices or spaces of a tissue or structure. |Interstitial fluid bathes the cells of the body.|近义词：intervening, intermediate |反义词:connected, continuous|词性:形容词


词源: From Late Latin interstitialis, from Latin interstitium "interval," from inter- "between" + sistere "to stand."
