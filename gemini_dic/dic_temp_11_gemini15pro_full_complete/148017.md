overripenesses:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪpənɪsɪz/
|n.| 过熟，过度成熟 |The overripenesses of the bananas made them mushy. 香蕉过熟使它们变得糊状。|
|英文解析: The state of being excessively ripe; extreme ripeness.|
|例句：The overripenesses of the fruit attracted swarms of fruit flies. 水果的过度成熟吸引了成群的果蝇。|
|例句：The overripenesses of the tomatoes made them unsuitable for salads. 西红柿的过熟使它们不适合做沙拉。|
|近义词：overripeness, overmaturity|
|反义词：unripeness, immaturity|
|词性：名词|



词源:

From over- + ripeness.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," and "ripeness" refers to the state of being ripe.
