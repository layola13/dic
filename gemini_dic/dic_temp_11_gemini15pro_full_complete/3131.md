aesthesis:/ɛsˈθiːsɪs/ /esˈθiːsɪs/
n.
感觉；审美感；感性
The ability to perceive sensations.  The appreciation of beauty, especially in art.
Her paintings demonstrate a keen aesthesis. 她的画作展现出敏锐的审美感。
近义词：perception, sensibility, taste, appreciation
反义词：insensitivity, numbness
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek αἴσθησις (aísthēsis, “sensation, perception, feeling”), from αἰσθάνομαι (aisthánomai, “I perceive, feel”).
