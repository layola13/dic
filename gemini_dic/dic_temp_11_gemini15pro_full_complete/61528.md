ETYMOLOGY->
From embryo + -logist.  "Embryo" comes from the Greek "ἔμβρυον" (embruon) meaning "something that grows within,"  from "ἐν" (en) "in" + "βρύειν" (bruein) "to swell, be full." "-logist" comes from the Greek "-λόγος" (-logos) meaning "one who studies" or "one who speaks (about a subject)."
