half-squadrons:/ˌhɑːf ˈskwɒdrənz/ | n. | 半中队，半支飞行中队 | A group of soldiers or airmen smaller than a full squadron, typically half the size. |  The general deployed two half-squadrons to secure the flanks. 将军部署了两支半中队来保护侧翼。 |  The airbase housed several half-squadrons of fighter jets.  这个空军基地驻扎着几个战斗机半中队。 | detachments, units | full squadrons, whole squadrons | noun
