ephemeral: /ɪˈfɛmərəl/ | adj. | 短暂的，朝生暮死的 | Lasting for a very short time. |  The beauty of the cherry blossoms is ephemeral. 樱花的美丽是短暂的。

The joy was ephemeral, soon replaced by sadness.  快乐是短暂的，很快就被悲伤取代了。

Synonyms: transient, fleeting, short-lived, momentary

Antonyms: enduring, lasting, permanent, perpetual

词性: adjective
