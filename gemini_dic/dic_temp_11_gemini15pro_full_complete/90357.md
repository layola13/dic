hovercraft:/ˈhʌvəkrɑːft/ (US), /ˈhɒvəkrɑːft/ (UK)| n.|气垫船|A vehicle that travels just above the surface of water or land, supported by a cushion of air forced downwards by fans.|The hovercraft crossed the English Channel in just under an hour. 气垫船在不到一小时的时间内穿越了英吉利海峡。|Synonym: air-cushion vehicle|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps boat, ship, car depending on context)|noun
