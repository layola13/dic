dulcifluous: /dʊlˈsɪfəlʊəs/ /dʌlˈsɪfluəs/
adjective
甜美的；流畅的；悦耳的
Sounding sweet and smooth; flowing sweetly or smoothly.
Her dulcifluous voice charmed the audience. 她甜美的嗓音迷住了观众。

The dulcifluous tones of the cello filled the concert hall.  大提琴流畅的音调充满了音乐厅。


Synonyms: melodious, euphonious, harmonious, sweet-sounding, silver-toned, mellifluous
Antonyms: harsh, grating, strident, discordant, cacophonous
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin dulcis (sweet) + fluere (to flow).
