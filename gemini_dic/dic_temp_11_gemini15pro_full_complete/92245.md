hypertrophous:/haɪˈpɜːrtfəs/ | adj. | 肥大的，过度生长的 | Enlarged; excessively developed.  Characterized by hypertrophy (increase in size of an organ or tissue due to enlargement of its component cells). |  The bodybuilder's hypertrophous biceps were the result of years of dedicated training. 健美运动员肥大的二头肌是多年刻苦训练的结果。

Her heart was hypertrophous due to a long-standing medical condition. 由于长期患病，她的心脏肥大。 | 近义词: enlarged, overgrown, swollen | 反义词: atrophied, shrunken, underdeveloped | 词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *ὑπέρτροφος (hupértrophos) "over-nourished,"  from *ὑπέρ (hupér, "over, beyond") + *τροφή (trophḗ, "nourishment").
