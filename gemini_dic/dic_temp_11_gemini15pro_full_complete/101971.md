isobilateral:/ˌaɪsoʊbaɪˈlætərəl/ | adj. | 等面的，两侧对称的 | Having two similar sides; symmetrical on two sides. | Example 1: The isobilateral leaf has nearly identical upper and lower surfaces.  这片等面叶的上下表面几乎相同。

Example 2:  Isobilateral leaves are common in certain plant families. 等面叶在某些植物科中很常见。 | Near Synonyms: symmetrical, bilateral, equifacial | Antonyms: asymmetrical, unilateral, dorsiventral | Word type: Adjective

Etymology:
iso- (Greek: equal) + bi- (Latin: two) + lateral (Latin: latus, lateris, meaning "side")
