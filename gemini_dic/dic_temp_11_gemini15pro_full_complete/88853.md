hoi:/hɔɪ/ | int. | 嗨，你好 | An informal greeting, similar to "hi" or "hey".  It's common in some dialects, particularly South African English. | Hoi, how's it going? 嗨，最近怎么样？ | 近义词: hi, hey, hello | 反义词: goodbye, farewell | interjection
