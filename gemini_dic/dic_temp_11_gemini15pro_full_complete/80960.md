gracelessly:/ˈɡreɪsləsli/| adv.|笨拙地，粗鲁地，不优雅地|Without grace; in a clumsy, awkward, or insensitive manner.  It describes actions performed without elegance or refinement, often implying a lack of social skills or consideration for others.|She gracelessly stumbled over the rug, spilling her drink. 她笨拙地被地毯绊倒，洒了饮料。|Synonyms: awkwardly, clumsily, inelegantly, ungracefully|Antonyms: gracefully, elegantly, smoothly, deftly|词性:副词
