dropt:/drɒpt/ |v. (past tense of "drop")|掉落，落下；放弃；停止；下降|The temperature dropt sharply overnight. 温度一夜之间骤降。|近义词:fell, declined, sunk, plummeted, descended|反义词:rose, climbed, ascended, soared|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *droppen, from Old English *droppian, *dryppan (“to drop, drip”), of uncertain origin. Cognate with Old Norse *dryppa (“to drip”), Middle Dutch droppen, drippen (“to drop, drip”), Old High German tropfon (“to drop, drip”), German tropfen (“to drip”).
