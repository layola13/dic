deep-frozen:/ˌdiːpˈfroʊzn/ (US) /ˌdiːpˈfrəʊzn/ (UK)| adj.|深度冷冻的，速冻的|Preserved by freezing at a very low temperature.|例句：I always buy deep-frozen vegetables in winter. 我冬天总是买速冻蔬菜。|例句：The deep-frozen meal was quick and easy to prepare.  速冻食品做起来又快又方便。|近义词：frozen, quick-frozen|反义词：fresh, thawed|词性:形容词

Etymology:
"deep-" + "frozen"
"deep-" intensifying prefix, from Old English *deop
"frozen" past participle of "freeze," from Old English *freosan
