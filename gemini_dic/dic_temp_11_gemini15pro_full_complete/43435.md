crappy:/ˈkræpi/| adj.|糟糕的，劣质的|Something of poor quality or condition; unpleasant. |例句：The movie was really crappy. 这部电影真的很烂。

例句：I had a crappy day at work. 我今天工作过得很糟糕。

近义词：lousy, awful, terrible, rubbish (UK), poor, shoddy|反义词:excellent, great, wonderful, fantastic|词性:形容词

crappy:/ˈkræpi/| adj.|令人沮丧的，令人不快的|Causing annoyance or frustration. |例句：I'm in a crappy mood today. 我今天心情很差。

例句：He gave me some crappy excuse for being late. 他给了我一个蹩脚的迟到借口。

近义词：unpleasant, annoying, irritating|反义词:pleasant, enjoyable|词性:形容词


词源:  From *crap* (excrement) + *-y* (adjective-forming suffix).  Ultimately related to Dutch *krappen* (to pluck, tear off), referring to plucking or picking off worthless parts of plants.
