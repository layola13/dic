misconfident:/ˌmɪsˈkɒnfɪdənt/ | adj. |缺乏自信的；自信心不足的|Having or showing a lack of confidence; timid.|She gave a misconfident smile. 她露出一个缺乏自信的微笑。|近义词: insecure, hesitant, timid, diffident, self-doubting|反义词: confident, self-assured, assertive|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From mis- (expressing wrongness or lack) + confident (from Latin confīdere, from con- ‘with’ + fīdere ‘to trust’).
