obfuscation: /ˌɒbfʌsˈkeɪʃən/ /ˌɑːbfʌsˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 混淆，模糊，困惑 | The deliberate act of making something unclear, obscure, or unintelligible. |  The politician's speech was filled with obfuscation, making it difficult to understand his true stance.  政客的演讲充满了混淆，让人难以理解他的真实立场。
