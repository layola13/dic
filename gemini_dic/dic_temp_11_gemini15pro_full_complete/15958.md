balsam: /ˈbɔːlsəm/ (US) /ˈbɔːlsəm/ (UK) | n. adj. |香脂，香膏；凤仙花；安慰物|A fragrant, resinous substance obtained from certain trees, used in perfumes and medicines. Also, a tree that produces such a substance, or any of various similar plants, including the garden balsam.  Can also refer to something soothing or restorative. |The air was filled with the balsam of pine needles. 空气中弥漫着松针的香味。|Synonyms: balm, resin, fragrance, perfume, ointment, lotion, solace, comfort |Antonyms: irritant, stimulant, discomfort |noun
