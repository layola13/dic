noninterfering: /ˌnɒnɪntəˈfɪərɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnɪntərˈfɪrɪŋ/ | adj. | 非干扰的，互不干扰的 | Not interfering or causing interference; independent.  Not hindering or obstructing another. | The system uses a noninterfering monitoring technique that allows for continuous observation without altering the behavior of the observed subjects.  该系统使用一种非干扰的监控技术，可以在不改变被观察对象行为的情况下进行持续观察。 | Examples: The two processes are noninterfering, meaning they can operate concurrently without affecting each other. 这两个进程互不干扰，这意味着它们可以并发运行而不相互影响.  The sensors are designed to be noninterfering, allowing them to collect data passively without disturbing the environment. 这些传感器设计成非干扰式的，可以被动地收集数据而不干扰环境. | Synonyms: independent, autonomous, separate, discrete, nonobstructive, unobtrusive | Antonyms: interfering, obstructive, hindering, disruptive | adjective
