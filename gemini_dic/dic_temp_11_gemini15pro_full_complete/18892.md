beslave:/bɪˈsleɪv/
verb
奴役，使沦为奴隶
To make someone a slave; to enslave.
He was beslaved by his addiction to gambling. 他被赌瘾奴役了。
The conquerors beslaved the indigenous population. 征服者奴役了土著居民。

近义词: enslave, subjugate, oppress, subdue
反义词: liberate, free, emancipate
词性: verb
