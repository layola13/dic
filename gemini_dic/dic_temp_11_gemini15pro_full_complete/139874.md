oculists:/ˈɒkjəlɪsts/ /ˈɑːkjəlɪsts/
noun
眼科医生
A doctor specializing in the treatment of diseases and defects of the eye.
Example:  I went to see an oculist because I was having trouble with my vision. 我去看眼科医生，因为我的视力出了问题。

Example:  The oculist prescribed new glasses for me. 眼科医生给我开了新眼镜。

近义词: ophthalmologist, eye doctor
反义词: None
词性: noun
