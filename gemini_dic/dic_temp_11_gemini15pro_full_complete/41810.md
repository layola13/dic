corticiform:/kɔːrtɪsɪfɔːrm/
adjective
皮质的，树皮状的
Resembling bark; bark-like.  Having the appearance of cerebral cortex.
The lichen had a corticiform texture, rough and resembling tree bark.  这种地衣具有树皮状的质地，粗糙且类似于树皮。
The biologist identified the corticiform layer of the organism under the microscope. 生物学家在显微镜下识别出该生物体的皮质层。
近义词: bark-like, cortical
反义词: smooth, non-cortical
词性: adjective
