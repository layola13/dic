japhetite: 钛铁矿，钛酸亚铁
japhetite: A mineral consisting of iron titanate, FeTiO3, occurring in igneous and metamorphic rocks. It is an important source of titanium.
japhetite: Japhetite is a common accessory mineral in many mafic and ultramafic igneous rocks.  钛铁矿是许多基性和超基性火成岩中的常见副矿物。
