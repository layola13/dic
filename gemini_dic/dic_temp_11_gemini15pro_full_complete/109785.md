levity: /ˈlevəti/ | n. | 轻浮；轻率；不严肃 |  A lack of seriousness, especially about things that should be treated with respect; frivolity. |  His attempt to inject some levity into the proceedings fell flat. 他试图给会议增添一些轻松气氛，但失败了。|  The funeral was marked by surprising levity. 葬礼上出人意料地出现了轻浮的举动。| synonyms: lightheartedness, frivolity, flippancy, cheerfulness, gaiety | antonyms: seriousness, gravity, solemnity, sobriety | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French *leveté, from Latin levitas, from levis ‘light’.
