cetene:/ˈsiːtiːn/| n.|十六烯; 鲸蜡烯|A long-chain hydrocarbon, specifically a straight-chain alkene with 16 carbon atoms and one double bond. It is a colorless liquid at room temperature.|Cetene is often used as a standard in determining the cetane number of diesel fuels.|近义词:hexadecene|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific chemical compound)|noun
