dilettante:/ˌdɪləˈtænt/ | n. | 业余爱好者；浅尝辄止的人 | A person who cultivates an area of interest, such as the arts, without real commitment or knowledge. | He's a bit of a dilettante as far as wine is concerned – he knows the basics, but not much beyond that. 他对葡萄酒只是一知半解——只了解一些皮毛，仅此而已。 |  近义词：amateur, dabbler, superficial | 反义词：expert, professional, connoisseur | 词性: noun

dilettante:/ˌdɪləˈtænt/ | adj. | 业余的；浅尝辄止的 |  Showing superficial interest; amateurish. |  His dilettante approach to painting resulted in some rather unimpressive artwork. 他对绘画的业余态度导致了一些相当不起眼的作品。 | 近义词：amateurish, superficial, dabbled  | 反义词：professional, expert, masterful | 词性: adjective

词源: From Italian *dilettante*, present participle of *dilettare* "to delight," from Latin *delectare*.
