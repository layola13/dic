debarring:/dɪˈbɑːrɪŋ/
verb
禁止, 阻止, 排除
present participle of debar (to officially prevent someone from doing something, or from going somewhere)
例句：
The regulation is debarring him from entering the competition. 这项规定禁止他参加比赛。

He was debarred from holding public office. 他被禁止担任公职。

近义词:  prohibiting, preventing, excluding, precluding, barring
反义词:  permitting, allowing, admitting, including
词性:  Verb (present participle)


debar:/dɪˈbɑːr/
verb
禁止, 阻止, 排除
to officially prevent someone from doing something, or from going somewhere
例句：
He was debarred from holding public office. 他被禁止担任公职。

The university debarred him from taking exams for three years. 该大学禁止他三年内参加考试。

近义词: prohibit, prevent, exclude, preclude, bar
反义词: permit, allow, admit, include
词性: Verb


词源:
From Old French *desbarer, from de- + barrer “to bar,” from barre “bar.”
