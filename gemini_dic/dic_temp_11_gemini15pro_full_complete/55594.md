disuniter:/dɪsjuːˈnaɪtər/ | n. |分裂者，不团结的人|A person or thing that causes disunity or discord.  Someone who sows discord or breaks up a group. |The politician's divisive rhetoric made him a disuniter within the party. 这位政客的分裂言论使他成为党内的分裂者。|例句：His actions served as a disuniter, driving a wedge between the two groups. 他的行为起到了分裂的作用，在两组人之间造成了隔阂。|近义词：divider, schismatic, separatist |反义词:unifier, conciliator| noun
