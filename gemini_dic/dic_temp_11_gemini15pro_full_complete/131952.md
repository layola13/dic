noncontributories:/ˌnɒnkənˈtrɪbjʊtɔːriz/
| n. | 非缴费会员；不缴费者；无贡献者 |  People who are not required to or do not make contributions, especially to a pension or insurance scheme.  |  The noncontributories will still receive basic healthcare coverage. 非缴费会员仍将享受基本医疗保险。 | Synonyms: non-members, non-participants | Antonyms: contributors, members | Noun

noncontributory:/ˌnɒnkənˈtrɪbjʊtəri/
| adj. | 非缴费的；无贡献的 | Not requiring or involving a contribution, especially to a pension or insurance scheme. | The company offers a noncontributory pension plan.  该公司提供非缴费型养老金计划。 | Synonyms: free, gratis, complimentary | Antonyms: contributory, paid | Adjective
