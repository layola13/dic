frouzy: /ˈfraʊzi/ (US), /ˈfraʊzi/ (UK) | adj. | 邋遢的；闷浊的；有霉味的 | Having a stale, musty smell; untidy and dirty-looking.  | The room was frouzy and smelled of stale cigarette smoke. 这个房间很邋遢，一股霉味，还有着残留的香烟味。 |  The frouzy old man hadn't bathed in weeks.  那个邋遢的老头好几个星期没洗澡了。| 近义词: musty, stale, stuffy, frowzy, unkempt, slovenly | 反义词: fresh, clean, tidy, neat | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  From earlier *frowsy, of uncertain origin, perhaps related to Low German *frusen "to mold".
