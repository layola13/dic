abode-by:/əˈboʊd baɪ/| phrasal verb | 遵守，信守|To comply with, adhere to, or act in accordance with (a rule, decision, agreement, etc.)|The company must abide by the terms of the contract.  公司必须遵守合同条款。

|近义词：comply with, adhere to, observe, follow, uphold|反义词：disobey, violate, breach, ignore, defy|词性:短语动词
