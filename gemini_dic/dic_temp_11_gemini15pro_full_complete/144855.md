over-plowing:/ˌōvərˈplaʊɪŋ/ | n. |过度耕作| Excessive plowing of farmland, which depletes soil nutrients and increases erosion. | Over-plowing led to the Dust Bowl in the 1930s. 过度耕作导致了20世纪30年代的沙尘暴。| 近义词: over-cultivation, over-farming | 反义词: sustainable agriculture, conservation tillage |词性:名词

over-plowing:/ˌōvərˈplaʊɪŋ/ | v. |过度耕作| The act of plowing farmland excessively, leading to soil degradation.  | Farmers over-plowed the fields, resulting in infertile land.  农民过度耕作田地，导致土地贫瘠。| 近义词: over-till, over-work | 反义词: under-cultivate |词性:动词
