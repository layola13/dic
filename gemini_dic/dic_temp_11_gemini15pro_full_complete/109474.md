lepra:/ˈlɛprə/| n.|麻风病|A chronic infectious disease caused by Mycobacterium leprae, affecting the skin, peripheral nerves, mucosa of the upper respiratory tract, eyes, and testes.|Lepra is a serious public health concern in some parts of the world. 麻风病在世界某些地区是一个严重的公共卫生问题。|Synonyms: Hansen's disease|Antonyms: health|noun
