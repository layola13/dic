boastful: /ˈboʊstfəl/ (US) /ˈbəʊstfʊl/ (UK)| adj. | 吹嘘的，自夸的 | Given to boasting; bragging; prone to exaggerating one's accomplishments or possessions. | He had a boastful manner that annoyed many people. 他傲慢自大的态度惹恼了很多人。|  Example: She was boastful about her new car. 她吹嘘她的新车。| Synonyms: bragging, swaggering, vainglorious, conceited | Antonyms: modest, humble, unassuming | adjective

boastive: /ˈboʊstɪv/ (US) /ˈbəʊstɪv/ (UK)| adj. | 吹嘘的，自夸的 (与boastful同义，但较不常用) |  Inclined to boast; boastful.  | His boastive claims about his wealth were met with skepticism.  他吹嘘自己财富的言论遭到了怀疑。 | Example: His boastive tone irritated his colleagues. 他自夸的语气激怒了他的同事们。| Synonyms:  bragging, boastful, vaunting | Antonyms: modest, humble, reserved | adjective
