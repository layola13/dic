outgushing:/ˌaʊtˈɡʌʃɪŋ/
adjective
涌出的；喷涌而出的；奔放的
flowing out rapidly and forcefully; expressing feelings in an unrestrained way
Her outgushing personality made her popular. 她奔放的性格使她很受欢迎。
Synonyms: effusive, gushing, overflowing, exuberant
Antonyms: reserved, restrained, inhibited
词性: 形容词


outgushing:/ˌaʊtˈɡʌʃɪŋ/
noun
涌出；喷涌
a sudden rapid flow
The outgushing of lava destroyed the village.  熔岩的喷涌摧毁了村庄。
Synonyms: eruption, outburst, surge
Antonyms: trickle, drip
词性: 名词
