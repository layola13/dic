coevality:/ˌkoʊɪˈveɪləti/ | n. |同时代；同时存在；同龄 |The state or quality of being coeval; existence during the same period of time. |The coevality of the two writers is striking, given their different backgrounds.  这两个作家的同时代性令人惊讶，因为他们的背景不同。| near synonyms: contemporaneity, simultaneousness, synchronicity | antonyms:  non-contemporaneity, asynchrony | noun

coeval:/ˌkoʊˈiːvəl/ | adj. |同时代的；同时存在的；同龄的 |Of the same age, date, or duration; contemporary. |The poet was coeval with the dramatist. 这位诗人和剧作家是同时代的。| near synonyms: contemporary, contemporaneous, synchronous, concurrent | antonyms: non-contemporary, asynchronous | adjective
