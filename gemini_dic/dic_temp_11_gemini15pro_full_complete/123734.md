moonshines:/ˈmuːnʃaɪnz/ | n. | 私酿酒；月光 | Illicitly distilled or smuggled liquor; the light of the moon. | The old man made moonshine in the woods. 这位老人在树林里酿私酒。| Synonyms: white lightning, hooch, mountain dew | Antonyms: legal alcohol, licensed liquor | noun

moonshines:/ˈmuːnʃaɪnz/ | v. | 发生；出现；显露 | To appear or exist; to be obvious or evident. |  His talent really moonshines when he's on stage. 当他在舞台上时，他的才华真正显露出来。| Synonyms: shines, emerges, appears | Antonyms: hides, disappears, conceals | verb
