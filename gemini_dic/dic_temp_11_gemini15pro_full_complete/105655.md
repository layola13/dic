kinglessness:/ˈkɪŋləsnəs/| n.|无国王状态；无君主状态；群龙无首|The state of being without a king; lack of a king or monarch.  The kinglessness of the country led to chaos.|例句：The prolonged kinglessness created a power vacuum.  长期无君主状态造成了权力真空。

例句：The kinglessness of the realm invited foreign invasion.  王国的无君主状态招致了外敌入侵。|近义词：anarchy, disorder, lawlessness, chaos|反义词：monarchy, kingship, sovereignty|词性:名词
