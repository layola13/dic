fresnel:/freɪˈnɛl/ | adj. n. | 菲涅耳，菲涅耳透镜的 | relating to or denoting Augustin-Jean Fresnel or his work, especially in optics.  Also, a type of lens. |  Example: The lighthouse used a Fresnel lens to amplify the light. 灯塔使用菲涅耳透镜来增强光线。

fresnel:/ˈfrɛznəl/ |  n. | 菲涅耳 |  A unit of frequency equal to one terahertz (10^12 hertz). | Example: The frequency of the signal was measured in fresnels.  该信号的频率以菲涅耳为单位测量。
