delirium:/dɪˈlɪriəm/
noun
谵妄；神志不清；极度兴奋
A state of mental confusion and excitement characterized by disorientation, hallucinations, and incoherence.
The high fever induced delirium in the patient. 高烧使病人谵妄。
He experienced delirium after the accident. 事故发生后，他出现了谵妄。
近义词:confusion, madness, frenzy
反义词:lucidity, clarity, sanity
词性:noun

词源: From Latin dēlīrium "going off the furrow," from dē- "off" + līra "furrow, ridge between furrows."
