anachronism: /əˈnækrənɪzəm/ | n. |时代错误，年代误植；不合时代的人或物 | Something that is mistakenly placed in a time where it does not belong in a story, movie, etc., or something that seems to belong to the past and does not fit in the present. | The movie is full of anachronisms, like characters using cell phones in ancient Rome.  这部电影充满了时代错误，比如古罗马人物使用手机。|  The sword is an anachronism in modern warfare. 这把剑在现代战争中显得格格不入。 |Synonyms: misplacement, misdating, incongruity | Antonyms: contemporaneity, timeliness | noun
