mayanist:/ˈmaɪənɪst/ | n. | 玛雅学家；玛雅文明研究者 | A specialist in Mayan culture, history, or language.  |  Example: The mayanist dedicated her life to deciphering ancient glyphs.  这位玛雅学家毕生致力于破译古代象形文字。

Example:  The mayanist gave a fascinating lecture on Mayan astronomy.  这位玛雅学家做了一个关于玛雅天文学的精彩讲座。| 近义词: archaeologist, scholar, researcher | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Maya + -ist.  "Maya" refers to the civilization of Mesoamerica. "-ist" is a suffix indicating a person who studies or specializes in a particular field.
