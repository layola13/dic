inturbidate: /ɪnˈtɜːrbɪdeɪt/ | v. | 使浑浊；使混乱；扰乱 | To make turbid; to make muddy or cloudy; to disturb or confuse. | The chemicals inturbidated the water, making it unsafe to drink. 化学物质使水浑浊，使其无法饮用。 | The scandal inturbidated the political landscape. 这桩丑闻扰乱了政治格局。 | 近义词: muddy, cloud, confuse, obscure, obfuscate | 反义词: clarify, clear, purify | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From Late Latin *inturbidatus, past participle of *inturbidare, from Latin in- + turbidus (“turbid”).
