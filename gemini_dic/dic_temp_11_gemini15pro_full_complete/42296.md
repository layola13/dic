counter-intuitive:/ˌkaʊntər ɪnˈtuːɪtɪv/ /ˌkaʊntər ɪnˈtuːɪtɪv/ | adj. | 反直觉的 | Contrary to what one would intuitively expect.  Not readily understandable by intuition. | It seems counter-intuitive to shut down factories during a recession, but it might be necessary.  在经济衰退期间关闭工厂似乎违反直觉，但可能是必要的。

It's counter-intuitive to think that increasing prices will boost sales, but sometimes it works.  认为提高价格会促进销售是违反直觉的，但有时确实有效。 | 近义词: illogical, unreasonable, unexpected | 反义词: intuitive, logical, obvious | 词性: adjective
