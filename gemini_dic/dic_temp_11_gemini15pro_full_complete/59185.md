dyslysin:/dɪsˈlaɪsɪn/
n.
消化不良素;  (肠内)难溶树脂
A resinous substance formed in the intestine by the decomposition of bile.
Dyslysin is a product of bacterial transformation of bile acids in the colon.  消化不良素是大肠中胆汁酸经细菌转化而成的产物。
Dyslysin contributes to the color of feces. 消化不良素导致粪便的颜色.
近义词: coprostanol, stercobilin
反义词: None
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek δυσ- (dus-, “bad, difficult”) + λύσις (lúsis, “loosening, releasing, dissolving”).
