cagiest:/ˈkeɪdʒiɪst/
adjective
最谨慎的，最守口如瓶的
Most cautious, reluctant to speak frankly, secretive.
He gave a cagey answer to the reporter's question. 他对记者的提问给出了一个谨慎的回答。
近义词:guarded, wary, noncommittal, reticent, secretive
反义词:frank, open, candid, forthcoming
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From "cagey" + "-est" (superlative suffix). "Cagey" likely originated in the early 20th century, possibly from British slang, with uncertain origins.  One theory suggests a connection to the idea of being "caged" and therefore guarded, but this is not definitively proven.
