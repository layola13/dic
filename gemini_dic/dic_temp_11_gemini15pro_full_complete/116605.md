patrimoine: /patʀimwan/ (French pronunciation)| n.|遗产，财产，文化遗产|Something inherited from one's ancestors or the past, especially cultural property. It can refer to tangible assets like buildings and works of art, or intangible aspects like traditions and language.|例句：The city's rich patrimoine attracts tourists from all over the world.  这座城市丰富的文化遗产吸引了来自世界各地的游客。

例句：Protecting our architectural patrimoine is crucial for future generations. 保护我们的建筑遗产对子孙后代至关重要。|近义词：heritage, legacy, inheritance|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
