carotene:/ˈkærətiːn/ /ˈkɛrəˌtin/ | n. |胡萝卜素|An orange or red plant pigment found in carrots and many other fruits and vegetables. It is converted to vitamin A in the body.|例句：Carrots are a good source of beta-carotene. 胡萝卜是β-胡萝卜素的良好来源。

例句：Beta-carotene is an antioxidant that can help protect against certain diseases. β-胡萝卜素是一种抗氧化剂，可以帮助预防某些疾病。


|近义词：pigment, chromophore|反义词: None|词性:noun
