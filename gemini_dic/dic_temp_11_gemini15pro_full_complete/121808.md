misreflects:/ˌmɪsrɪˈflekts/
verb
错误地反映；歪曲；曲解
gives a false or inaccurate impression of something.
The media misreflects the views of the general public. 媒体歪曲了公众的观点。
misrepresents, distorts, falsifies
reflects accurately, portrays truthfully
