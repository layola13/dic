harmattan:/hɑːrˈmætæn/ (US), /hɑːˈmætən/ (UK)| n.|哈马丹风（西非的一种干燥的东北风）|A dry, dusty wind that blows from the Sahara Desert across West Africa during the winter months.  It is caused by the difference in pressure between the high-pressure area over the Sahara and the low-pressure area over the Gulf of Guinea.|The harmattan caused visibility problems for drivers. 哈马丹风导致驾驶员能见度降低。

The harmattan dries out the skin and lips. 哈马丹风使皮肤和嘴唇干燥。|近义词：trade wind, northeast wind|反义词:southwest monsoon|词性:名词
