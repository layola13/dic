amanuensis:/əˌmænjuˈɛnsis/ | n. | 抄写员，笔录员，秘书 | A person employed to write what another dictates or to copy what has been written. |  Example: The author dictated his novel to his amanuensis.  这位作家向他的抄写员口述他的小说。| Example:  She worked as an amanuensis for a famous lawyer. 她为一位著名律师担任抄写员。| Synonym: scribe, secretary, copyist, clerk | Antonym: author, dictator, speaker | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *amanuensis*, from *a manu* ("by hand") + *-ensis* (suffix forming adjectives and nouns denoting place, origin, or material).
