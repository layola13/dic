co-referential:/ˌkoʊˌrɛfəˈrɛnʃəl/| adj.|共同指代的；指称相同的|Referring to the same thing or person.|例句： "He" and "John" are co-referential in the sentence "John said he was tired."  “他”和“约翰”在“约翰说他累了”这句话中是指称相同的。|例句：The pronouns "it" and "the book" are co-referential if they both refer to the same physical book. 如果代词“它”和“这本书”都指的是同一本实体书，那么它们是共同指代的。|近义词：coreferent, synonymous, equivalent|反义词: unrelated, distinct|词性:adjective
