overbrutal: /ˌoʊvərˈbruːtl/ | adj. | 过于残忍的，极度野蛮的 | Excessively brutal; extremely savage or cruel. | The dictator's overbrutal regime was finally overthrown by the people.  独裁者的残暴统治最终被人民推翻了。| The overbrutal treatment of prisoners sparked international outrage. 对囚犯过度残暴的待遇引发了国际社会的愤怒。 | 近义词: savage, barbaric, ferocious, vicious, ruthless, inhumane | 反义词: humane, compassionate, merciful, gentle, kind | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "brutal" (from Old French "brutal," meaning "like a brute, irrational, sensual," ultimately from Latin "brutus," meaning "dull, senseless, irrational").
