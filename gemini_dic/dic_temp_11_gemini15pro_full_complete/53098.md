diphthongised: /dɪfˈθɒŋɡaɪzd/ (British) /dɪfˈθɔŋɡaɪzd/ (American) | adj. | 双元音化的 |  Having been changed into a diphthong; characterized by diphthongs.  A diphthong is a sound formed by the combination of two vowels in a single syllable, in which the sound begins as one vowel and moves towards another (as in coin, loud, and side ). | The vowel in "house" has been diphthongised in some dialects.  “house”中的元音在某些方言中已经双元音化了。 | diphthongized | monophthongized | adjective
