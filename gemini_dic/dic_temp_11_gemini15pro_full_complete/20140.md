bipeds:/ˈbaɪpɛdz/| n. |两足动物|Animals that use two legs for walking.|Humans are bipeds. 人类是两足动物。|Birds use their wings as forelimbs, but most are bipeds when on the ground. 鸟类用翅膀作为前肢，但大多数鸟类在地面上是两足行走的。|近义词：two-footed animals|反义词:quadrupeds|词性:noun
