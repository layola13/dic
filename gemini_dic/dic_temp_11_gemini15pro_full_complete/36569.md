collar:/ˈkɑːlər/ (US), /ˈkɒlə(r)/ (UK)| n. v. |衣领；颈圈；抓捕；逮捕|A band of material around the neck of a shirt, dress, coat, etc., or a separate piece of material worn around the neck.  As a verb, it means to seize someone or something.|

例句：
The dog's collar was too tight.
狗的项圈太紧了。

He turned up his collar against the cold wind.
他竖起衣领抵御寒风。


The police collared the suspect as he tried to escape.
警察在嫌疑犯试图逃跑时将其抓获。

She collared him in the hallway to ask about the meeting.
她在走廊里抓住他问会议的事情。


近义词：neckband (n.), seize, apprehend, arrest (v.)|
反义词: release, free (v.)|
词性: noun, verb|
