firmish:/ˈfɜːrmɪʃ/| adj.|相当坚固的，较硬的|Moderately firm; somewhat firm.  Relatively solid or resistant to pressure.|The avocados were firmish, perfect for guacamole.|近义词：solid, stiff, hardish|反义词:soft, mushy|词性:形容词
Etymology: firm + -ish (suffix indicating a moderate degree of the quality described by the base word).  "Firm" comes from Old French "ferme" and ultimately from Latin "firmus" meaning "strong, steadfast."
