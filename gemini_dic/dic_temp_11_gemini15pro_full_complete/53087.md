diphtheria:/dɪfˈθɪəriə/ | n. | 白喉 | an acute infectious disease caused by the bacteria Corynebacterium diphtheriae, affecting the mucous membranes of the throat and upper respiratory tract. It is characterized by the formation of a tough grayish membrane in the throat that can obstruct breathing. |  Diphtheria is a serious disease that can be prevented by vaccination.  白喉是一种可以通过接种疫苗预防的严重疾病。|  Diphtheria outbreaks still occur in some parts of the world.  在世界的一些地方仍然爆发白喉。| synonym: none (it's a specific disease) | antonym: none (it's a disease, not a state of health) | noun


diphtherias:/dɪfˈθɪəriəz/ | n. | 白喉（复数，较少使用）| Plural form of diphtheria, referring to multiple instances or types of the disease.  | Diphtherias of different severities were observed. 观察到不同严重程度的白喉病例。(This usage is less common; typically, "diphtheria" is used even when referring to multiple cases).  | synonym: none (specific disease, plural form rarely used) | antonym: none (disease, not a state of health, plural form rarely used) | noun
