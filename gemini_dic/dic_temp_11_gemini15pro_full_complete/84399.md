hallmarking:/ˈhɔːlmɑːkɪŋ/
n.
标记，认证，品质保证
the official marking of gold, silver, and platinum articles to guarantee their purity
例句：The hallmarking of silver has been practiced for centuries.  银的标记认证已经实行了几个世纪。

例句：Hallmarking ensures the quality of precious metals. 标记认证确保了贵金属的质量。

近义词：certification, authentication, assaying, stamping
反义词：counterfeiting, forgery
词性: noun


hallmark:/ˈhɔːlmɑːk/
n.
标志，特征，品质证明
a mark stamped on articles of gold, silver, and platinum by the British assay offices, as a mark of purity
a distinguishing characteristic, trait, or feature
例句：The lion is a hallmark of British jewelry. 狮子是英国珠宝的标志。

例句：Attention to detail is a hallmark of a good craftsman. 对细节的关注是优秀工匠的标志。

近义词：characteristic, feature, trademark, emblem, symbol
反义词：n/a
词性: noun


hallmark:/ˈhɔːlmɑːk/
vt.
给…打上标记，认证
to stamp with a hallmark
to characterize or distinguish
例句：The jeweler hallmarked the gold ring.  珠宝商给金戒指打上了标记。

例句：His work is hallmarked by its originality. 他的作品以其独创性而著称。

近义词：certify, authenticate, stamp, mark
反义词：n/a
词性: verb

词源：From Middle English *halmarke, from Old English *heallmerke ("hall-mark"), equivalent to hall +‎ mark.  Referencing the Goldsmiths' Hall in London where the assaying and marking took place.
