cellularly:/ˈsɛljʊlərli/
adverb|细胞地，在细胞水平上|At a cellular level; with respect to the cells of an organism.  Relating to or happening within a cell or cells.|The drug acts cellularly to inhibit viral replication.|近义词：intracellularly|反义词: extracellularly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From "cellular" + "-ly".  "Cellular" comes from the Late Latin "cellulāris", from "cellula" (small room, cell), diminutive of "cella" (storeroom, chamber).
