comparatively:/kəmˈpærətɪvli/| adv.|相对地，比较地|In a way that relates to a comparison; by comparison. |例句：Comparatively speaking, this car is inexpensive. 相对而言，这辆车很便宜。|例句：The exam was comparatively easy. 考试相对容易。|近义词：relatively, rather, somewhat, fairly|反义词：absolutely, totally, completely|词性:副词
词源: From Late Latin *comparativus, from Latin comparativus (“of comparison”), from comparare (“to compare”).
