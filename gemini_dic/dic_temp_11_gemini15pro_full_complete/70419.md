fighter-bomber:/ˈfaɪtər ˈbɑːmər/ | n. |战斗轰炸机|A military aircraft designed to function as both a fighter and a bomber, capable of engaging in air-to-air combat and attacking ground targets. | The F-105 Thunderchief was a powerful fighter-bomber used extensively during the Vietnam War.  F-105雷公式战斗轰炸机在越南战争期间被广泛使用。| synonym: attack aircraft, strike fighter | antonym: bomber (purely focused on bombing), fighter (purely focused on air combat) | noun
