deglutitory:/dɪˈɡlʊtɪtɔːri/

The deglutitory muscles contract to move the food down the esophagus. 吞咽肌收缩以将食物向下移动到食道。
The patient's deglutitory reflex was impaired after the stroke. 中风后，病人的吞咽反射受损。
