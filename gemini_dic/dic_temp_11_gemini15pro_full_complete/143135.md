outcasts:/ˈaʊtˌkæsts/| n.|被抛弃者，被排斥者，流浪者|A person who has been rejected by society or a social group.|The outcasts lived on the fringes of society, shunned by everyone. 这些被排斥的人生活在社会的边缘，被所有人回避。|近义词：pariahs, exiles, rejects|反义词:insiders, elites|词性:noun

outcast:/ˈaʊtˌkæst/| adj.|被抛弃的，被排斥的|Rejected and excluded from society or a social group.|He felt outcast and alone after losing his job and his family.  失去工作和家庭后，他感到被抛弃和孤独。|近义词：rejected, exiled, ostracized|反义词:accepted, included|词性:adjective
