gleaning:/ˈɡliːnɪŋ/
n.|拾穗，收集(少量信息)， gleaning |The gleaning of the grapes is finished. 葡萄的采摘工作已经完成。|
近义词:gathering, collecting, harvesting
反义词:sowing, planting
词性:名词

glean:/ɡliːn/
v.|拾(落穗)，收集(少量信息)， glean|She gleaned her information from various sources. 她从各种来源收集信息。|
近义词:gather, collect, obtain
反义词:scatter, disperse
词性:动词


词源: From Old French *glener, from Frankish *klinōn, from Proto-Germanic *klinōną (“to stick, adhere”). Cognate with Middle Dutch clenen (“to stick, adhere, glean, pick up”), German Kleie (“bran”).
