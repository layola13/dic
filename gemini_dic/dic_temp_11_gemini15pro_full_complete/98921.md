intentionality:/ɪnˌtɛnʃəˈnælɪti/| n. |意向性，目的性|The quality of mental states (e.g., thoughts, beliefs, desires, hopes) that consists in their being directed toward some object or state of affairs.|Her action demonstrated clear intentionality. 她的行为表现出明显的意向性。|近义词：purposefulness, purposiveness, design, aim|反义词：aimlessness, randomness|noun
