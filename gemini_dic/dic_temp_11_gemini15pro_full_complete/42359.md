counterargument:/ˈkaʊntərˌɑːrɡjʊmənt/ | n. | 反驳；抗辩；驳论 |  A statement or argument that goes against another statement or argument. |  He presented a compelling counterargument to the proposal. 他对这项提议提出了令人信服的反驳。 | Synonyms: rebuttal, refutation, objection | Antonyms: argument, support, confirmation | noun

counterargument:/ˈkaʊntərˌɑːrɡjʊmənt/ | n. | 反驳；抗辩；驳论 | A reason or set of reasons put forward to oppose an idea or theory developed in another argument. | Her counterargument was well-researched and persuasive. 她的反驳经过充分研究，很有说服力。 | Synonyms: rejoinder, reply, response | Antonyms: agreement, acceptance, concession | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- + argument.  Counter- is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposite."  Argument comes from Old French argument, from Latin argumentum, meaning "evidence, proof, reason."
