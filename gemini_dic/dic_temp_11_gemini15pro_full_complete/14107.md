authenticatable:/ɔːˈθɛntɪkeɪtəbl/
adjective
可验证的，可认证的
Able to be authenticated; verifiable.
The system requires all users to be authenticatable through a two-factor process.  该系统要求所有用户都必须通过双因素认证。
Users must be authenticatable via biometric scans. 用户必须通过生物特征扫描进行身份验证。
近义词:verifiable, confirmable
反义词:unverifiable, unconfirmable
词性:adjective

词源: From authenticate + -able.  Authenticate comes from Late Latin authenticāre "to give authority to," from Latin authenticus "genuine, trustworthy" (from auctor "author") + -āre (infinitive suffix).
