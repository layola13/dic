inquirers:/ɪnˈkwaɪrərz/
n.
询问者；打听者
People who ask for information.
Many inquirers called the customer service line after the product recall.  产品召回后，许多人打电话到客服热线询问。
The museum welcomes inquirers of all ages.  博物馆欢迎所有年龄段的询问者。
Synonyms: questioners, investigators, seekers
Antonyms: respondents, answerers
noun
