get-out:/ˈɡɛtˌaʊt/ | adj. (informal) |  令人惊讶的; 非常棒的 | Exceptionally good or impressive. | That's a get-out dress you're wearing! 你穿的裙子真漂亮！| 近义词：amazing, fantastic, wonderful | 反义词：terrible, awful | 词性: 形容词
