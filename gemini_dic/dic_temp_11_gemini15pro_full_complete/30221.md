cellars:/ˈselərz/
noun
酒窖；地下室
Underground rooms, often used for storing wine or other goods.
The wine is aging in the cellars.  这葡萄酒正在酒窖里陈酿。
synonyms: basements, vaults
antonyms: attics, lofts
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "celier" (storeroom), from Late Latin "cellarium" (storehouse), from Latin "cella" (small room, chamber).
