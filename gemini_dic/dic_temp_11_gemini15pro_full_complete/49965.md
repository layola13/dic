denier:/dɪˈnaɪər/ | n. | 否认者; 拒不承认者; 拒付者；旦尼尔(丝袜等的纤度单位) | a person who denies something, especially someone who refuses to admit the truth of a claim or accusation; a unit of weight by which the fineness of silk, rayon, or nylon yarn is measured |  例句：He is a denier of climate change. 他是一个气候变化的否认者。 | 例句：These tights are 15 denier.  这条紧身裤是15旦尼尔。| 近义词：skeptic, doubter, rejecter, nonbeliever (for person);  | 反义词：believer, supporter, advocate (for person) | 词性: noun


denier:/dəˈnjeɪ/ | n. |旧法国货币 | An old French coin. | 例句: The denier was a common currency in medieval France.  旦尼尔是中世纪法国的通用货币。| 例句: He paid for the bread with a few deniers. 他用几个旦尼尔买了面包。 | 近义词: coin, currency | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific coin) | 词性: noun
