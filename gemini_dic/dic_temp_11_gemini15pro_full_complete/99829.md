intermise:/ˌɪntərˈmaɪz/ | v. |插入；干预；调解|To insert or introduce between; to intervene or mediate.|He intermised himself in the argument between his two friends. 他介入了他两个朋友之间的争论。|近义词：intervene, mediate, interfere, interpose|反义词：ignore, disregard|词性：动词

intermise:/ˌɪntərˈmaɪz/ | n. |插入；干预；调解|An insertion or introduction between; an intervention or mediation.|His intermise prevented a fight. 他的干预阻止了一场打斗。|近义词：intervention, mediation, interference, interposition|反义词：inaction, negligence|词性：名词
