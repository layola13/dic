interscience:/ˈɪntərˌsaɪəns/| adj. n.|交叉科学，跨学科的；各学科间的；边缘科学|Relating to or involving more than one branch of science.|例句：Interscience research is becoming increasingly important. 交叉科学研究正变得越来越重要。|例句：The interscience approach led to a breakthrough in the field. 跨学科的方法带来了该领域的突破。|近义词：interdisciplinary, multidisciplinary, cross-disciplinary|反义词: specialized, disciplinary|词性：形容词，名词

Etymology:
From inter- + science.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between," "among," or "reciprocal."  "Science" comes from the Latin "scientia," meaning "knowledge."
