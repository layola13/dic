cardialgia:/kɑːrdiˈældʒə/ | n. | 心痛，胃灼热 | A burning sensation in the chest, typically caused by acid reflux. |  He experienced severe cardialgia after eating a spicy meal. 他在吃完一顿辛辣的饭后感到严重的胃灼热。 | synonyms: heartburn, pyrosis, acid indigestion | antonyms: comfort, relief | noun

Etymology: From Greek *kardia* (heart) + *algos* (pain).
