apparailling: /əˈpærəlɪŋ/
adjective
穿着衣服的；打扮的
The act of dressing or clothing someone.  The process of providing someone with clothes or attire.
例句：She was apparailling the children in their Sunday best. 她正在给孩子们穿上他们最好的周日服装。

例句：The apparailling of the actors for the play took several hours. 为这出戏的演员们穿衣服花了好几个小时。

近义词：dressing, clothing, attiring, garbing
反义词：undressing, disrobing
词性：adjective (present participle of apparel, used as an adjective)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *apparaillen, from Old French apareillier, from a- (from Latin ad 'to') + pareiller 'to make equal, to prepare', from Latin par 'equal'.
