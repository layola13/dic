backdated:/ˌbækˈdeɪtɪd/
adjective
回溯的；追溯既往的
Given an earlier date than the actual one.
例句：The agreement was backdated to January 1st.  协议被回溯到1月1日。

例句：He backdated the check to avoid a late payment penalty. 他将支票的日期提前以避免滞纳金。
synonym: retroactive, antedated
antonym: postdated
词性: adjective
