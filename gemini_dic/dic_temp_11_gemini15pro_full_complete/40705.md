converter:/kənˈvɜːrtər/ (US) /kənˈvɜːtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |转换器，转化器|A device or machine that converts something from one form to another. |例句：A catalytic converter reduces harmful emissions from a car's exhaust.  催化转化器减少汽车尾气中的有害排放物。

例句：He uses a currency converter to calculate exchange rates. 他使用货币转换器来计算汇率。|近义词：transformer, adapter, transcoder|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
