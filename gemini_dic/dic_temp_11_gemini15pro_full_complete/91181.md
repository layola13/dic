hydras:/ˈhaɪdrəz/ | n. |九头蛇（希腊神话）；水螅（动物）|A many-headed serpent in Greek mythology that was slain by Heracles; A freshwater polyp with a tubular body and tentacles around the mouth.|例句：The myth of the Lernaean Hydra is a popular tale of Heracles's strength.  勒拿湖九头蛇的神话是赫拉克勒斯力量的流行故事。

例句：Hydras are simple animals that can regenerate lost body parts. 水螅是简单的动物，可以再生失去的身体部位。|近义词：monster, serpent (mythological); polyp (zoological) |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
