microgrammes: /ˈmaɪkrəʊɡræmz/ (UK) /ˈmaɪkrəɡræmz/ (US)

例句：The sample contained only a few microgrammes of the radioactive substance. 样本中只含有几微克放射性物质。|近义词：mcg, µg |反义词: kilogrammes, milligrammes |词性: noun
