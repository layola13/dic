gloppiest: /ˈɡlɒpiɪst/ | adj. | 最粘稠的；最糊状的 | Superlative form of 'gloppy', meaning extremely thick, sticky, or viscous.  | The gloppiest batter made the thickest pancakes.  这最粘稠的面糊做出了最厚的煎饼。 |  The gloppiest mud clung to my boots.  最粘稠的泥巴粘在我的靴子上。 | 近义词: stickiest, gooiest, thickest | 反义词: thinnest, runniest | 词性: 形容词

词源: From 'glop', of imitative origin, suggesting the sound of something thick and sticky being dropped or poured.
