anthroponomy:/ˌænθrəˈpɒnəmi/
n.
人类命名学;人名学
The study of human names, their origins, meanings, and distribution.
Anthroponomy helps us understand the cultural and historical significance of names.  人类命名学帮助我们理解名字的文化和历史意义。
This book delves into the fascinating world of anthroponomy. 这本书深入研究了引人入胜的人名学世界。
近义词：onomastics, name study
反义词：N/A
词性：noun

Etymology:  From Greek *anthrōponumia*, from *ánthrōpos* "human being" + *-ōnumia* "giving of names,"  related to *ónoma* "name."
