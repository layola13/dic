counterblast:/ˈkaʊntərbæst/ |ˈkaʊntərbɑːst/ | n. | 反驳；抨击；猛烈攻击 | a forceful reply or attack, especially a verbal one |  The politician issued a counterblast against the accusations of corruption.  这位政治家对腐败指控进行了猛烈反驳。 | Synonyms: retort, rebuttal, riposte, rejoinder | Antonyms: agreement, acceptance, concession | noun

counterblast:/ˈkaʊntərbæst/ |ˈkaʊntərbɑːst/ | n. | (旧)逆风；迎面吹来的强风 | (archaic) a strong gust of wind blowing in the opposite direction | The sailors struggled against the counterblast as they tried to round the cape.  水手们在绕过海角时，奋力对抗迎面吹来的强风。 | Synonyms: headwind, gale | Antonyms: tailwind | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- + blast. Originally denoting a contrary wind, or a current of air blown back by something.  Figuratively, a refutation or retort.
