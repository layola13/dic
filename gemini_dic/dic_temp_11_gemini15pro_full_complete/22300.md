bonhomie:/ˌbɒnəˈmiː/ |n.|友好；和睦；亲切|Good-natured, easy friendliness or conviviality.|例句：There was a lot of bonhomie at the party. 聚会上充满了友好和睦的气氛。|例句：His bonhomie made him popular with his colleagues. 他的亲切使他很受同事欢迎。|近义词：affability, amiability, geniality, cordiality|反义词：hostility, animosity, coldness|词性：名词

Etymology:
From French *bonhomie*, from *bonhomme* "good fellow," from *bon* "good" + *homme* "man."
