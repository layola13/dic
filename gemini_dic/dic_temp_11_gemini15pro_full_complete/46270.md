czarina:/zɑːˈriːnə/
n.
女沙皇，皇后
The wife or widow of a czar; an empress of Russia.
Catherine the Great was a powerful czarina.  叶卡捷琳娜大帝是一位强大的女沙皇。
The czarina issued a decree. 女沙皇颁布了一项法令。

近义词:empress, queen
反义词:(None -  opposite would be a male monarch like czar/emperor)
词性:noun
