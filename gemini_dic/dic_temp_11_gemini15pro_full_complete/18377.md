bellybound:/ˈbeliˌbaʊnd/| adj.|腹部受束缚的，怀孕的|Bound or constricted around the belly; pregnant.  It also can refer to a feeling of tightness or discomfort in the belly.|She felt bellybound and uncomfortable in the later stages of her pregnancy. 她怀孕后期感觉腹部受束缚，很不舒服。|近义词：pregnant, expecting, gravid, large with child |反义词:nonpregnant, not pregnant|adjective
