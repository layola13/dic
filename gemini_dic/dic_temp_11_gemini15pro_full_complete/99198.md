interclasped:/ˌɪntərˈklɑːspt/ /ˌɪntərˈklæs(p)t/
adjective
互相紧握的，扣在一起的
Two figures stood in the doorway, their hands interclasped.  门口站着两个人，他们的手紧紧握在一起。
例句：Their fingers were tightly interclasped. 他们的手指紧紧地扣在一起。
例句：The lovers sat on the bench, their hands interclasped. 恋人们坐在长椅上，手紧紧相握。
近义词：interlocked, intertwined, clasped
反义词：unlinked, separated
词性：adjective

interclasp:/ˌɪntərˈklɑːsp/ /ˌɪntərˈklæs(p)t/
verb
互相紧握，扣在一起
They interclasped their fingers and walked down the aisle. 他们十指紧扣地沿着过道走去。
例句：The vines interclasped around the old oak tree. 藤蔓缠绕着古老的橡树。
例句：He interclasped his hands behind his back. 他双手在背后紧握。
近义词：interlock, intertwine, clasp
反义词：unlink, separate
词性：verb
