miscatalogs:/ˌmɪsˈkætəlɒɡz/ | v. | 错误分类 | To categorize incorrectly or place in the wrong category. | The librarian miscataloged several books, putting them in the fiction section when they belonged in history. 图书管理员错误地分类了几本书，把它们放在了小说区，而它们属于历史区。|  The intern miscatalogs the inventory items regularly, causing confusion in the warehouse.  实习生经常错误地对库存物品进行分类，导致仓库混乱。 | misclassifies, misfiles | correctly catalogs, properly classifies | Verb
