jointly:/ˈdʒɔɪntli/ | adv. |共同地，联合地|Together; in conjunction with another or others. |The project was jointly funded by the government and several private companies. 政府和几家私营公司共同资助了这个项目。|例句：The report was jointly prepared by two committees. 这份报告是由两个委员会共同撰写的。|近义词：together, collectively, combinedly, concertedly|反义词：separately, individually, independently|词性:副词

Etymology:
From Middle English *jointly*, derived from *joint* (meaning "united, combined") + *-ly* (adverbial suffix).  The word *joint* itself ultimately comes from Latin *iunctus*, the past participle of *iungere* ("to join").
