disequilibrium:/ˌdɪsɪkwɪˈlɪbriəm/ /ˌdɪsˌiːkwɪˈlɪbriəm/
n.
不平衡，失衡
a lack of balance or stability; a state of imbalance or instability.
例句：The sudden drop in demand created a disequilibrium in the market.  需求的骤降造成了市场的不平衡。

例句：The economy is in a state of disequilibrium. 经济处于失衡状态。

近义词：imbalance, instability, asymmetry, disproportion
反义词：equilibrium, balance, stability, symmetry
词性：名词
