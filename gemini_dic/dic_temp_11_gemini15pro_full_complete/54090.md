disembay:/ˌdɪsɪmˈbeɪ/
verb
使（船）出港；使（船）驶出海湾
to leave a bay; to cause (a ship) to leave a bay
The ship disembayed at dawn. 这艘船在黎明时驶出了海湾。
近义词：depart, sail, leave port
反义词：embark, enter port, dock
词性：v.


disembay:/ˌdɪsɪmˈbeɪ/
noun
出港；驶出海湾（的行为）
The act of leaving a bay.
The disembay was delayed due to the storm. 由于暴风雨，出港被推迟了。
近义词：departure, sailing
反义词：arrival, docking
词性：n. (rarely used)
