help-out:/ˌhɛlp ˈaʊt/| phrasal verb |帮助，援助，帮忙|To give assistance to someone, especially in a difficult situation.  It often implies a temporary or specific need for help.|例句：I helped out my neighbor with her groceries when she sprained her ankle.  我帮我的邻居买杂货，因为她扭伤了脚踝。

例句：Can you help me out with this math problem?  你能帮我解决这道数学题吗？|近义词：assist, aid, support, lend a hand|反义词:hinder, obstruct, impede|词性:动词短语
