begged:/beɡd/| v. |乞求，恳求，请求|To ask earnestly or humbly for something; to implore.|She begged him for forgiveness. 她恳求他原谅她。|The children begged to stay up later. 孩子们恳求晚点睡觉。|近义词：pleaded, implored, beseeched|反义词：demanded, insisted|词性:动词

词源:
From Middle English *beggen*, from Old North French *beguer*, from Old French *beguine* (“Beguine nun”), from Middle Dutch *beggaert* (“beggar”).  Ultimately of uncertain origin, possibly related to Proto-Germanic *bagōną* (“to fight, quarrel”).
