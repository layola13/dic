cacochymy:/ˌkækəˈkɪmi/
n.
身心失调；坏脾气
A state of general ill health and bad temper.
His increasing cacochymy was evident in his constant complaints and irritability. 他日益身心失调的状况从他不断的抱怨和易怒中可见。
近义词：malaise, dysphoria, ill humor
反义词：well-being, euphoria, good humor
词性: 名词

cacochymy:/ˌkækəˈkɪmi/
n.
不良体液混合；体液不调
An imbalance of the humors (in ancient and medieval physiology).
Medieval doctors believed cacochymy was the cause of many illnesses. 中世纪的医生认为体液不调是许多疾病的根源。
近义词：imbalance, dyscrasia
反义词：balance, equilibrium
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
