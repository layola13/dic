cajuputene: /ˌkædʒʊˈpjuːtiːn/ | n. |白千层烯|A bicyclic sesquiterpene hydrocarbon found in the essential oil of cajuput. It is a colorless liquid with a camphoraceous odor.|Cajuputene is one of the components that gives cajuput oil its characteristic aroma. 白千层烯是赋予白千层油其特有香气的成分之一。|  Cajuputene has been studied for its potential anti-inflammatory properties.  白千层烯的潜在抗炎特性已被研究。|Synonym:  sesquiterpene hydrocarbon | Antonym: N/A | Noun
