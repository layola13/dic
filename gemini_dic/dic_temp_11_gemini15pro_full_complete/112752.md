一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? = 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she cooked for dinner? = 她晚饭做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last weekend? = 你上周末做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? = 她对这部电影说了些什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? = 我看到她的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? = 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? = 她生病前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after you graduate? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们在下次会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will she have finished by the time I arrive? = 到我到达的时候她将完成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? = 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will they have been studying by the end of the semester? = 到学期末他们将已经学习了什么？
