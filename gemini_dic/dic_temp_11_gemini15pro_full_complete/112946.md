lugubrious:/luːˈɡuːbriəs/| adj.|悲哀的，忧郁的|Mournful, dismal, or gloomy, especially in an affected, exaggerated, or unrelieved manner.|例句：The lugubrious expression on his face told us the news wasn't good. 他脸上悲哀的表情告诉我们消息不好。|例句：The funeral was a lugubrious affair, with everyone dressed in black and weeping openly. 葬礼是一场悲哀的事件，每个人都穿着黑衣公开哭泣。|近义词：mournful, gloomy, melancholy, somber, doleful|反义词：cheerful, happy, joyful, merry, bright|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *lūgubris* "mournful, pertaining to mourning," from *lūgēre* "to mourn."
