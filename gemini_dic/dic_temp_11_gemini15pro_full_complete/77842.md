generics:/ˈdʒɛnərɪks/ | n. | 泛型，通用 |Relating to or descriptive of an entire group or class; not specific.|  Java uses generics to allow type parameters to be passed to classes, interfaces, and methods. | Java 使用泛型允许将类型参数传递给类、接口和方法。| synonyms: general, universal, comprehensive | antonyms: specific, particular | noun

generics:/dʒəˈnɛrɪks/ | adj. | 非商标的，通用的（药物） |(of a product, especially a drug) not protected by trademark and therefore cheaper than the equivalent branded product.|  She buys generic drugs to save money. | 她购买非商标药物以省钱。 | synonyms: nonproprietary, unbranded | antonyms: brand-name, proprietary | adjective
