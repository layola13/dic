electrometallurgic:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˌmɛtəlˈɜːrdʒɪk/
adjective
电冶金的
Relating to electrometallurgy, the process of producing metals using electrolysis.
Electrometallurgic techniques are crucial for the production of high-purity aluminum. 电冶金技术对于生产高纯度铝至关重要。
Modern electrometallurgic processes are highly efficient. 现代电冶金工艺效率很高。
近义词:electrolytic
反义词: (None -  no direct antonym for a process-related adjective)
词性:adjective
