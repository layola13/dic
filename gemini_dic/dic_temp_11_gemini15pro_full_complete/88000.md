high-risk:/ˈhaɪ ˈrɪsk/| adj.|高风险的，高危的|Involving a high level of risk or danger.|例句：This is a high-risk investment. 这是一项高风险的投资。|近义词：dangerous, perilous, hazardous|反义词:low-risk, safe, secure|词性:形容词
