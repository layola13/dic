mordant:/ˈmɔːrdənt/ (US), /ˈmɔːd(ə)nt/ (UK)| n. adj.|媒染剂，腐蚀剂；尖刻的，辛辣的|A substance, typically an inorganic oxide, that combines with a dye or stain to fix it in a material.  Figuratively, it can also refer to a sharp and biting wit or style. |The artist used a mordant to ensure the vibrancy of the colors in the tapestry. 艺术家用媒染剂来确保织锦中色彩的鲜艳。|  The comedian's mordant humor offended some members of the audience.  这位喜剧演员尖刻的幽默冒犯了部分观众。| 近义词：(n.) fixative, dye-fixer, etchant, (adj.) caustic, acrid, biting, trenchant, sarcastic, sardonic|反义词：(adj.) bland, mild, gentle, kind|词性:名词, 形容词

mordants:/ˈmɔːrdənts/ (US), /ˈmɔːd(ə)nts/ (UK)| n. |媒染剂|Plural form of mordant.  Refers to multiple substances used to fix dyes or stains. |Different mordants are required for different types of dyes. 不同的染料需要不同的媒染剂。|The weaver experimented with various mordants to achieve unique color effects.  织工尝试了各种媒染剂，以达到独特的色彩效果。|近义词：fixatives, dye-fixers|反义词：None|词性:名词
