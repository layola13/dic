connectedly:/kəˈnɛktɪdli/
adv.
连接地，连贯地
in a connected manner; coherently.
The chapters of the book are connectedly written, flowing smoothly from one to the next. 这本书的章节写得很连贯，从一章到下一章都很流畅。
近义词: coherently, consecutively, continuously
反义词: disjointedly, incoherently, separately
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From connect + -ed + -ly.  Connect comes from the Latin "connectere," meaning "to bind together."
