over-clever:/ˌoʊvərˈklɛvər/| adj.|过于聪明，自作聪明|Too clever; excessively ingenious, often implying a lack of wisdom or practicality.|He came up with an over-clever plan that ultimately backfired. 他想出了一个过于精明的计划，最终却适得其反。|例句：Her over-clever remarks alienated her colleagues. 她自作聪明的言论疏远了她的同事。|近义词：too clever, smart-alecky, cunning, shrewd|反义词：naive, simple, artless|词性:形容词
