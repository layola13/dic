oversecured:/ˌoʊvər səˈkjʊərd/
adjective
过度担保的；过分保护的
protected or secured to an excessive or unnecessary degree.
The loan was oversecured, meaning the collateral was worth significantly more than the loan amount. 这笔贷款是过度担保的，意味着抵押品的价值远远超过贷款金额。
The child was oversecured, never allowed to play outside unsupervised.  这个孩子被过度保护，从不允许在无人监督的情况下在外面玩耍。
近义词:overprotected, excessively secured
反义词:undersecured, vulnerable
词性:adjective

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "secured" (past participle of "secure")
