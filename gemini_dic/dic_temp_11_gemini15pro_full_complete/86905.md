hemitropy:/hɪˈmɪtrəpi/ |n.|半面像；双晶；孪晶|A crystal twinned in such a way that one half has been rotated 180 degrees with respect to the other, usually about an axis perpendicular to a plane of symmetry.  Two parts of a crystal have different orientations, appearing as mirror images.|The hemitropy of the crystal made it difficult to analyze its structure. 这块晶体的孪晶结构使其难以分析。|Synonyms: twinning, twin crystal|Antonyms: single crystal|noun

hemitropic:/ˌhɛmɪˈtrɒpɪk/ |adj.|半面像的；双晶的；孪晶的|Exhibiting hemitropy; twinned. Of or relating to hemitropy.|The hemitropic mineral displayed a unique optical effect.  这种孪晶矿物展现出独特的视觉效果。|Synonyms: twinned|Antonyms: untwinned, single|adjective
