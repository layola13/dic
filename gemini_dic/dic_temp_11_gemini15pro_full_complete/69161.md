fawnest:/ˈfɔːnɪst/
adjective
最谄媚的; 最奉承的
superlative adjective of fawn: showing affection or seeking favor by servile demeanor
例句：He gave the fawningest smile I've ever seen. 他露出了我见过的最谄媚的笑容。

例句：She had the fawningest manner towards anyone in authority. 她对任何当权者都表现得极其奉承。

近义词：obsequious, servile, sycophantic
反义词：aloof, disdainful, haughty
词性：adjective

词源：From fawn (v.) + -est.  "Fawn" itself comes from Old English *fægnian, from Proto-Germanic *faginōną, meaning "to rejoice."  The sense shifted from expressing joy to showing servile affection.
