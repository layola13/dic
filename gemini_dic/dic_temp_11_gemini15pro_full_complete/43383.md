crank:/kræŋk/| n. vt. vi.|曲柄；古怪的人；脾气坏的人；启动；转动曲柄；（使）弯曲|A crank is a part of a machine that is shaped like a handle and that you turn in order to operate the machine.  A crank is also an unpleasant and easily annoyed person. As a verb, to crank something means to start or operate it by turning a crank, or to cause something to bend.|例句：He turned the crank to start the engine. 他转动曲柄启动了引擎。|近义词：handle, lever, eccentric, grouch, curmudgeon|反义词:agreeable person|词性:noun, verb
