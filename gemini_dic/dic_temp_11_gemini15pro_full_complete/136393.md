nonresurrection:/ˌnɑːnˌrɛzəˈrɛkʃən/
n.
没有复活，无法复活
The concept of nonresurrection is central to some philosophical viewpoints.  没有复活的概念是一些哲学观点的核心。
The vampire's curse condemned him to a state of nonresurrection.  吸血鬼的诅咒使他永远无法复活。

Etymology:
From non- + resurrection.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not," and resurrection comes from the Latin resurrectio, meaning "rising again." 由non- + resurrection组成。Non-是一个前缀，意思是“不”，resurrection来自拉丁语resurrectio，意思是“再次升起”。
Synonym:
non-revival,  death, finality
Antonym:
resurrection, revival, rebirth
词性: 名词
