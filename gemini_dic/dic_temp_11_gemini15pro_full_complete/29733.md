catmint:/ˈkætˌmɪnt/ | n. | 猫薄荷 | A plant (Nepeta cataria) of the mint family, which has a pungent odor that is attractive to cats. |  The gardener planted catmint to attract the neighborhood felines. 园丁种植了猫薄荷来吸引附近的猫咪。

My cat goes crazy for catmint. 我的猫对猫薄荷很着迷。 | 近义词：catnip | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
