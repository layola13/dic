antiregulatory:/ˌæntiˈrɛɡjʊlətɔːri/
adjective
反监管的；反对监管的
Opposing or resisting regulation, especially government regulation of business or industry.
The lobbyists pushed for antiregulatory legislation that would benefit large corporations.  游说者推动有利于大公司的反监管立法。
The new administration adopted a decidedly antiregulatory stance. 新政府采取了明确的反监管立场。
Synonyms:deregulatory, laissez-faire, noninterventionist
Antonyms:regulatory, interventionist
adjective
