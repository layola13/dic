dyad:/ˈdaɪæd/| n.| 二分体，二元体，二人组|A group of two people or things; a pair.|The mother-daughter dyad is central to the novel's plot. 母女二元关系是这部小说的核心情节。|Synonyms: pair, duo, couple|Antonyms:  monad, triad|noun

dyad:/ˈdaɪæd/| adj.| 二重的，二元的|Relating to a dyad; consisting of two elements.|The dyad symmetry of the molecule is readily apparent.  该分子的二重对称性显而易见。|Synonyms: dual, binary|Antonyms: single, unary|adjective
