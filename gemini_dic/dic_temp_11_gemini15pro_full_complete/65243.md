etudes:/ˈeɪtjuːdz/ (US), /ˈeɪtjuːdz/ (UK)| n.|练习曲；习作|A short musical composition, typically for one instrument, designed to provide practice in a particular technical skill.|Chopin's etudes are notoriously difficult. 肖邦的练习曲是出了名的难。|She composed several etudes for the piano. 她创作了几首钢琴练习曲。|近义词：exercises, studies, pieces|反义词:performance, concert|noun
