epiphyte:/ˈɛpɪˌfaɪt/ | n. | 附生植物 | A plant that grows on another plant, especially one that is not parasitic, deriving its moisture and nutrients from the air and rain and sometimes from debris accumulating around it. |  Many orchids are epiphytes. 很多兰花都是附生植物。|  The bromeliad is an epiphyte that thrives in the rainforest canopy.  凤梨科植物是一种生长在雨林树冠层的附生植物。 |近义词: air plant | 反义词: terrestrial plant | 词性: noun

epiphytic:/ˌɛpɪˈfɪtɪk/ | adj. | 附生的 | Relating to or characteristic of an epiphyte. | The epiphytic ferns covered the branches of the ancient trees.  附生的蕨类植物覆盖了古树的枝干。|  The researchers studied the epiphytic community in the cloud forest. 研究人员研究了云雾林中的附生群落。 | 近义词: aerophytic | 反义词: terrestrial | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *epi- (upon, on) + *phyton (plant)
