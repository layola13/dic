dollop:/ˈdɒləp/ (UK), /ˈdɑːləp/ (US)| n.|一小团，一小块，（尤指食物的）一勺，一团|A small shapeless mass or blob of something, especially soft food.|She put a dollop of cream on the pie.|近义词：blob, lump, spoonful, dab, glob|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "smear" if contrasting a concentrated amount vs. a thin spread) |noun
