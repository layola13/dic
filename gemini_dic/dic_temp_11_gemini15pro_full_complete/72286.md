flotsam and jetsam:/ˈflɒtsəm ən ˈdʒetsəm/| n.|海上漂浮物，废料，社会渣滓|Refers to the wreckage of a ship or its cargo found floating on the sea (flotsam) or washed ashore (jetsam), often used figuratively to describe people or things regarded as useless or undesirable.|The beach was covered with flotsam and jetsam after the storm.  暴风雨过后，海滩上到处都是漂浮物和垃圾。 The city's flotsam and jetsam gathered in the park. 城市的流浪汉和无业游民聚集在公园里。|近义词：debris, wreckage, refuse, riffraff|反义词:treasure, valuables|词性:noun
