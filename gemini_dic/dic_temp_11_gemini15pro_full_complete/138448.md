novae:/ˈnoʊviː/
n.
新星（复数）
Plural form of nova. A nova is a star that suddenly increases greatly in brightness and then slowly returns to its original state over a few months.
Example: Astronomers observed several novae in that galaxy last year. 去年天文学家在那个星系观察到了几颗新星。
Synonym: supernovae (for much brighter explosions)
Antonym: (No direct antonym for a sudden increase in brightness)
noun
