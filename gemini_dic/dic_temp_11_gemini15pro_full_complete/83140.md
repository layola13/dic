guts:/ɡʌts/| n. |勇气，胆量；内脏；本质，核心；直觉，本能|The courage to do something difficult or unpleasant.  The internal organs, especially the intestines. The essential parts of something. A strong belief or feeling.|He didn't have the guts to tell her the truth. 他没有勇气告诉她真相。

The surgeon examined the patient's guts.  外科医生检查了病人的内脏。

The guts of the problem is a lack of communication. 问题的核心是缺乏沟通。

My guts tell me something is wrong. 我的直觉告诉我有些不对劲。|近义词：courage, bravery, nerve, intestines, entrails, essence, core, instinct, intuition|反义词:cowardice, fear, timidity|词性:名词
