out-peopling:/ˌaʊtˈpiːpəlɪŋ/
|n.|人口超过；人口增长超过|The out-peopling of the countryside led to overcrowding in the cities.|乡村人口超过城市，导致城市过度拥挤。|近义词: overpopulation, population explosion|反义词: depopulation, population decline|词性:名词

Etymology:
"out-" (prefix) + "people" (verb) + "-ing" (suffix)
"out-" denotes exceeding or surpassing.
"people" as a verb means to populate or inhabit.
"-ing" creates a noun from the verb, indicating the process or action.
