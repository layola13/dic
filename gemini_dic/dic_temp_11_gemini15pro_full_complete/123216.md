monoclinic:/ˌmɒnəˈklɪnɪk/ | adj. |单斜的|Relating to a crystal system characterized by three unequal axes, two of which intersect at an oblique angle while the third is perpendicular to the plane of the other two.  A crystal system in which the axes are unequal but at right angles except for one. |Gypsum is a common example of a monoclinic mineral. 石膏是单斜晶系矿物的一个常见例子。

The monoclinic crystal structure of this material gives it unique optical properties. 这种材料的单斜晶体结构赋予其独特的光学特性。|近义词:oblique  |反义词:isometric, orthorhombic, tetragonal, trigonal, hexagonal, cubic (These are other crystal systems) |词性:adjective
