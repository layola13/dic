hydrosulphate:/ˌhaɪdrəˈsʌlfeɪt/ /ˌhaɪdroʊˈsʌlfeɪt/ | n. | 硫酸氢盐 | A salt containing the anion HSO4-.  It is formed when sulphuric acid loses one of its hydrogen ions. | Potassium hydrosulphate is used in winemaking. 硫酸氢钾用于葡萄酒酿造。 | synonym: bisulphate | antonym:  (No direct antonym exists for a specific chemical compound like hydrosulphate. One could consider its conjugate base, sulphate (SO4^2-), as a related counter-ion.) | noun
