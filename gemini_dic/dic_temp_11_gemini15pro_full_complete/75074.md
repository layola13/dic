frigidity:/frɪˈdʒɪdɪti/
n.
冷漠, 冷淡; (尤指女性的) 性冷淡
lack of warmth or feeling; inability to experience sexual pleasure, especially in a woman.
She displayed a marked frigidity towards her husband. 她对她的丈夫表现出明显的冷淡。
Her frigidity made intimacy impossible. 她的性冷淡使得亲密关系无法建立。
近义词: coldness, indifference, apathy, unresponsiveness, frigidity (for sexual coldness)
反义词: warmth, passion, ardor, responsiveness

frigidity:/frɪˈdʒɪdɪti/
n.
严寒, 寒冷
extreme cold
The frigidity of the air made it difficult to breathe. 寒冷的空气使呼吸困难。
The expedition was hampered by the frigidity of the arctic climate.  极地气候的严寒阻碍了考察的进行。
近义词: coldness, iciness, chilliness
反义词: warmth, heat
