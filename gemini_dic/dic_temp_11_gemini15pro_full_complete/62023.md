enarmed:/ɪˈnɑːrmd/ | adj. | 着迷的，被迷住的 | Filled with love or admiration; captivated. | She was completely enamored with the handsome stranger. 她完全被这位英俊的陌生人迷住了。 |  近义词: captivated, charmed, fascinated, infatuated | 反义词: disenchanted, disillusioned, repulsed | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Old French *enarmer, from en- + arme (“weapon”).  The original sense was "to furnish with arms," but it evolved to mean "to charm, captivate," as if with a love potion.
