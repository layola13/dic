millstones:/ˈmɪlstəʊnz/ (UK) /ˈmɪlˌstoʊnz/ (US)| n. |磨石；沉重的负担|Large, heavy circular stones used for grinding grain.  Figuratively, it refers to a heavy burden or responsibility.|The farmer used millstones to grind the wheat into flour. 农民用磨石将小麦磨成面粉。|They felt the debt was a millstone around their necks. 他们觉得债务是压在他们身上的沉重负担。|Synonyms: burden, encumbrance, weight, load|Antonyms: advantage, asset, benefit, boon|noun
