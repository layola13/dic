common era:/ˈkɑːmən ˈɛrə/ (US), /ˈkɒmən ˈɪərə/ (UK)| n.|公元|The period of time since the traditional date of the birth of Jesus Christ, used as an alternative to AD.|例句：The library has books dating back to the first century of the common era. 图书馆里有可以追溯到公元一世纪的书籍。

例句：Many important scientific discoveries were made in the common era. 许多重要的科学发现都是在公元时代完成的。|近义词：AD (Anno Domini), Christian Era|反义词：BCE (Before Common Era)|词性:名词
