manicheisms:/ˈmænɪkiːɪzəmz/
n.
摩尼教思想；二元论；绝对的善恶观
A system of religious or philosophical dualism, especially regarding the opposition of good and evil.
Manicheisms often see the world in black and white, with no shades of gray.  摩尼教思想经常将世界视为非黑即白，没有灰色地带。
The politician's speech was full of manicheisms, portraying his opponents as purely evil.  这位政客的演讲充满了二元论，将他对手描绘成纯粹的邪恶。
Synonyms: dualism, absolutism
Antonyms: relativism, pluralism
