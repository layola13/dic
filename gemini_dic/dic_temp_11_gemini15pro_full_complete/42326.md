counter-step:/ˈkaʊntərˌstɛp/ | n. |反步骤，应对措施，对抗策略|An action taken to oppose or counteract another action.|The government's counter-step to the rising inflation was to increase interest rates. 政府应对通货膨胀上升的对策是提高利率。| The general ordered a counter-step to the enemy's advance. 将军下令反击敌人的进攻。|Synonyms: countermeasure, countermove, reaction, response |Antonyms:  initiative, action | noun


counter-step:/ˈkaʊntərˌstɛp/ | v. |采取反步骤，反击，对抗|To take action to oppose or counteract something.|The company counter-stepped by launching a new product.  该公司通过推出新产品来应对。|He counter-stepped his opponent's move with a swift maneuver. 他以快速的策略反击对手的行动。|Synonyms: counteract, countermove, oppose |Antonyms:  support, aid | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- + step.  "Counter-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposite." "Step" refers to an action or movement.
