messianic:/mɛsiˈænɪk/| adj.|救世主的；弥赛亚的；像救世主的|Relating to a messiah or resembling a messiah, especially in terms of promising or offering salvation or deliverance.|He had a messianic vision for the future of the company. 他对公司的未来抱有救世主般的愿景。|The leader delivered a messianic speech, inspiring hope in his followers. 这位领导人发表了一场充满救世主意味的演讲，激发了他追随者的希望。|近义词：redemptive, salvific|反义词：damning, destructive|词性:形容词

messianic:/mɛsiˈænɪk/| n.|救世主信徒|A believer in a messiah.|He considered himself a messianic, waiting for the promised savior. 他认为自己是一个救世主信徒，等待着承诺的救世主。|Many messianics gathered for the annual pilgrimage. 许多救世主信徒聚集在一起进行年度朝圣。|近义词：millenarian, chiliast|反义词: (无明确的反义词，可以考虑用atheist 无神论者，或者表示其他宗教信仰的词)|词性:名词
