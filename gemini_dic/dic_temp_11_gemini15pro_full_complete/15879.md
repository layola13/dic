ballium:/ˈbæliəm/| n.|城堡外庭，外壁|An outer wall or court of a castle, especially one forming an additional line of defense outside the main walls. |The ballium provided a crucial defensive position for the castle's archers. 城堡外庭为城堡的弓箭手提供了一个关键的防御阵地。|The attacking forces breached the ballium after a fierce siege. 攻击部队在猛烈的围攻后突破了外壁。|Synonyms: outer wall, outer court, bailey|Antonyms: inner wall, keep|noun
