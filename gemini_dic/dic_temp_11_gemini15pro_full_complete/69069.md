faultless:/ˈfɔːltləs/| adj.|完美的，无瑕的，无可挑剔的|Without any faults or imperfections; perfect.|Her performance was faultless, earning her a standing ovation. 她的表演完美无瑕，赢得了全场起立鼓掌。|Synonyms: impeccable, flawless, perfect, unblemished|Antonyms: flawed, imperfect, defective|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From fault + -less.  "Fault" comes from Old French "faulte," ultimately from Latin "fallere" (to deceive, fail).  "-less" is a native English suffix meaning "without."
