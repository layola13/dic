misappropriation:/ˌmɪsəˌproʊpriˈeɪʃn/| n. | 盗用，挪用，滥用 |The dishonest or illegal use of someone else's money or property for your own use. |  He was accused of misappropriation of company funds. 他被指控挪用公司资金。| misuse, embezzlement, defalcation | proper use, allocation | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (wrongly) + appropriation (the action of taking something for one's own use, typically without the owner's permission). Appropriation comes from the Late Latin appropriatio, from appropriare (to make one's own), from ad- (to) + proprius (one's own).
