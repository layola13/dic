nicotine:/ˈnɪkətiːn/ /ˈnɪkətɪn/ | n. |尼古丁|A poisonous, addictive alkaloid found in tobacco and responsible for many of the effects of smoking. |例句：Nicotine is a highly addictive substance. 尼古丁是一种高度成瘾的物质。

例句：The nicotine in cigarettes can cause serious health problems.  香烟中的尼古丁会导致严重的健康问题。

近义词： stimulant, addictive | 反义词: None (尼古丁没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用戒烟药物或疗法等概念表达相反的意思)| 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *nicotine*, from *Nicotiana* (the genus of tobacco plants), named after Jean Nicot, French ambassador to Portugal, who introduced tobacco to France in 1560.
