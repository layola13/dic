all-of-a-sudden:/ɔːl əv ə ˈsʌdn/| adv.|突然地，出乎意料地|Suddenly, without warning.  It describes something happening quickly and unexpectedly.|例句：All of a sudden, it started to rain. 突然，开始下雨了。|例句：The dog barked all of a sudden, startling me. 狗突然叫了起来，吓了我一跳。|近义词：suddenly, unexpectedly, abruptly, out of the blue|反义词:gradually, slowly, expectedly|词性:副词
