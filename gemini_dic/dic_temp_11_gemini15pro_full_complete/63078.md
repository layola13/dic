ensanguine:/ɪnˈsæŋɡwɪn/
verb
使沾血；血染
To cover or stain with blood.
The battlefield was ensanguined with the blood of fallen soldiers.  战场上沾满了阵亡士兵的鲜血。
The executioner's axe was ensanguined after the beheading.  刽子手的斧头在斩首后沾满了鲜血。
近义词: bloody, gore, stain
反义词: clean, unstained
词性: verb

ensanguine:/ɪnˈsæŋɡwɪn/
adjective
血染的; 血红色的
Covered or stained with blood; blood-red.
His ensanguined shirt was a gruesome sight.  他那件血染的衬衫看起来很可怕。
The ensanguined sword dripped onto the floor.  血淋淋的剑滴落到地板上。
近义词: bloody, gory, crimson
反义词: clean, unstained
词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *insanguināre, from Latin in- + sanguis (“blood”).
