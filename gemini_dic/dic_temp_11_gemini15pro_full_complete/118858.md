metaplasia:/ˌmɛtəˈpleɪʒə/ | n. | 化生 | The reversible replacement of one differentiated cell type with another mature differentiated cell type.  It's an adaptive response to stress or injury, where a less sensitive cell type replaces a more vulnerable one. |  Cigarette smoking can cause metaplasia in the lining of the airways. 吸烟会导致呼吸道内壁的化生。

Metaplasia of the esophageal lining can occur due to chronic acid reflux.  由于慢性胃酸反流，食道内壁可能发生化生。 | 近义词: transformation, conversion | 反义词: differentiation, hyperplasia | 词性: noun
