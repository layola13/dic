ligement:/ˈlɪɡəm(ə)nt/
adjective
韧带的；连接的
relating to or of the nature of a ligament; connecting.
The ligament damage required surgery.  韧带损伤需要手术。
The two bones are connected by a ligament. 这两块骨头由韧带连接。
近义词: connective, binding
反义词: separating, disconnecting
词性: adjective


词源:
From Old French ligament, from Latin ligamentum (“band, tie, string”), from ligare (“to bind, tie”).
