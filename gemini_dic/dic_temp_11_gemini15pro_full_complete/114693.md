malingering:/məˈlɪŋɡərɪŋ/
n.|装病，诈病|The act of pretending to be ill or injured in order to avoid work, duty, or responsibility.|He was accused of malingering to get out of military service. 他被指控装病以逃避兵役。
malinger:/məˈlɪŋɡər/
v.|装病，诈病|To pretend to be ill or injured in order to avoid work, duty, or responsibility.|Some employees malinger to avoid having to work overtime. 一些员工装病逃避加班。


近义词:feigning, faking, simulating, sham, shirking
反义词:diligence, industriousness
词性:n.  (malingering); v. (malinger)

词源:From French *malingre* "sickly, ailing," of uncertain origin, perhaps related to Old French *malingrer* "to feign sickness."
