ministrant:/ˈmɪnɪstrənt/| n. adj.|侍者，服务员；辅助的|A person who ministers or serves, especially in a religious context; assisting or subservient.|例句：The ministrant assisted the priest during the mass. 侍者在弥撒期间协助神父。|近义词：attendant, server, assistant, helper, aide|反义词：master, leader, chief|n.

ministrant:/ˈmɪnɪstrənt/| adj.|辅助的，服务的|Assisting or serving in a subordinate capacity.|例句：The ministrant angels guided the lost travelers. 服务的天使指引迷路的旅行者。|近义词：auxiliary, subservient, supplementary, ancillary|反义词：primary, main, principal|adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ministrant-, present participle stem of ministrare "to serve," from Latin minister "servant, attendant."
