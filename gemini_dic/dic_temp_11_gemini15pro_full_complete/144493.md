outthwack:/ˌaʊtˈθwæk/
verb
彻底击败，痛打
to defeat or beat someone thoroughly.
He outthwacked his opponent in the final round. 他在最后一轮彻底击败了对手。
The champion outthwacked the challenger. 冠军痛打了挑战者。

近义词:beat, thrash, trounce, vanquish
反义词:lose to, be defeated by
词性:verb
