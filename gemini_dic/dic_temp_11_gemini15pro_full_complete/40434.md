contras:/ˈkɒntrɑːz/| n.| 对照;副本;配对物 |A thing that is the opposite of something else. |例句：This study provides a useful contras to previous work on the subject.  这项研究为先前关于该主题的研究提供了有益的对照。

|例句：Light and darkness are often cited as contras. 光与暗常被引用为对照。|近义词：opposite, antithesis, counterpart |反义词: similarity, likeness |词性: 名词
