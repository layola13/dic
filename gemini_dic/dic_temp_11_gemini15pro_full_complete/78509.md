ghee:/ɡiː/| n.| 酥油，印度澄清黄油|A type of clarified butter made by simmering butter and removing the milk solids. It is commonly used in South Asian cooking.|She used ghee to fry the spices. 她用酥油炒香料。|Synonyms: clarified butter|Antonyms: N/A|noun
