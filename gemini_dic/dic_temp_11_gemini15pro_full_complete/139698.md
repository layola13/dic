oceanically:/ˌoʊʃiˈænɪkli/| adv.|海洋地，大洋般地|In a manner relating to the ocean; extensively, vastly.|The sound of the waves resonated oceanically through the hall.  海浪的声音在大厅里回荡，如同海洋般浩瀚。|近义词：vastly, immensely, boundlessly|反义词: narrowly, locally|词性:副词

词源:
From ocean + -ic + -ally.  Ocean comes from Latin Oceanus, the great river or sea surrounding the earth.  The suffix -ic forms adjectives, and -ally forms adverbs.
