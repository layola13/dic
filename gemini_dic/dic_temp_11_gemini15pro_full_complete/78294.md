germination:/ˌdʒɜːrmɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 发芽，萌芽 |The process by which a seed or spore begins to grow and put out shoots after a period of dormancy. | The warm spring weather encouraged the germination of seeds in the garden. 春天的温暖天气促进了花园里种子的发芽。| synonyms: sprouting, budding, emergence | antonyms: dormancy, inactivity | noun

germinate:/ˈdʒɜːrmɪneɪt/ | v. | 发芽，萌芽 |To begin to grow and put out shoots after a period of dormancy. | The seeds germinated quickly in the warm, moist soil. 种子在温暖潮湿的土壤中迅速发芽。| synonyms: sprout, bud, emerge | antonyms: wither, die | verb
