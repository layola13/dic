dodson:/ˈdɒdsən/ (UK) /ˈdɑːdsən/ (US)| n.|鹡鸰科鸟类（一种小鸟）|A small songbird of the Motacillidae family, typically with a long tail and slender body. They are known for their characteristic bobbing or wagging movements.|例句：I saw a dodson flitting about in the garden. 我看到一只鹡鸰在花园里飞来飞去。

例句：The dodson perched on the fence post, singing its cheerful song.  鹡鸰栖息在篱笆柱上，唱着欢快的歌。
