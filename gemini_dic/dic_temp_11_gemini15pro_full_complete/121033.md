misconceived:/ˌmɪskənˈsiːvd/
adjective
误解的，错误的
based on a wrong or inaccurate understanding or belief.
例句：The project was misconceived from the start.  这个项目从一开始就被误解了。

例句：His approach to the problem was completely misconceived. 他解决这个问题的方法完全是错误的。

近义词：misunderstood, mistaken, erroneous, fallacious
反义词：accurate, correct, valid, sound
词性：adjective
