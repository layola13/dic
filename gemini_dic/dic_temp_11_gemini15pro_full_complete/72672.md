foamier:/ˈfoʊmiər/ | adj. | 更多泡沫的；更起泡沫的 | More foamy; producing more foam. | The beer was foamier than usual.  这啤酒比平时泡沫更多。|  The soap created a foamier lather. 这肥皂产生了更多泡沫的泡沫。| 近义词：frothier, bubblier | 反义词：flat, still | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  From foam + -ier (comparative adjective suffix).  "Foam" comes from Old English "fam," related to Dutch "vuim" and German "Feim," all probably from a Proto-Germanic root meaning "foam, froth."
