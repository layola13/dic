orthotone:/ˈɔːrθəˌtoʊn/| n. |正音；标准音|A word with the primary or principle stress on the penultimate syllable.  A word accented on the last syllable. |Example 1:  In classical Greek, orthotone was used to describe words with the accent on the last syllable.  在古典希腊语中，orthotone用来描述最后一个音节带重音的单词。

Example 2:  The word "orthotone" itself is not an orthotone in English pronunciation.  “orthotone”这个词本身在英语发音中并不是一个orthotone。|近义词：tonic accent|反义词:barytone|词性:noun
