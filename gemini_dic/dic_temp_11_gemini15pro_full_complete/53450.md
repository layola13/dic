disappropriation:/dɪsəˌproʊpriˈeɪʃən/
n.|非法挪用，侵吞，盗用|The disappropriation of company funds is a serious crime. 公司资金的挪用是严重的犯罪行为。|例句：The investigation revealed a disappropriation of funds by the former CEO. 调查显示前首席执行官挪用了资金。|近义词：misappropriation, embezzlement, theft, pilferage|反义词:allocation, appropriation, distribution|词性:名词

disappropriation:/dɪsəˌproʊpriˈeɪʃən/
n.|剥夺，没收|The government's disappropriation of land caused widespread unrest. 政府征用土地引起了广泛的社会动荡。|例句：The disappropriation of their ancestral lands left the indigenous community with little recourse.  他们祖先的土地被剥夺，土著社区几乎没有追索权。|近义词：confiscation, seizure, expropriation|反义词: restitution, return|词性:名词
