flow's:/floʊz/| n.|流；流动；流畅；连贯；流量|The flow's direction changed suddenly. 水流的方向突然改变了。|近义词：current, stream, flux|反义词:blockage, stagnation|词性:名词
(Note: "flow's" itself isn't a word. It's the possessive form of "flow," acting like an adjective modifying a following noun.  Therefore providing examples for all tenses using "flow's" is not grammatically correct. The examples below will utilize "flow" in various forms and tenses.)
