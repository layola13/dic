ink-kneed:/ɪŋkˈniːd/
adjective
胆怯的，懦弱的
Having weak or feeble knees; cowardly or timid.
He was too ink-kneed to stand up to the bully. 他太胆小了，不敢反抗那个恶霸。
近义词: cowardly, timid, spineless, faint-hearted
反义词: brave, courageous, bold, daring
词性: adjective
词源:  From "ink" (meaning weak, as in ink-horn terms) + "kneed" (referring to weak or trembling knees, a traditional sign of fear).
