massy:/ˈmæsi/| adj.|巨大的，厚重的，笨重的|Large, heavy, and solid.|例句：The castle walls were massy and imposing. 城堡的墙壁厚重而雄伟。|例句：She wore a massy gold necklace. 她戴着一条厚重的金项链。|近义词：massive, bulky, heavy, weighty|反义词：light, slight, airy|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *massi, from Old French massif (“massive”), from masse (“mass”), from Latin massa (“lump, kneaded dough, mass”).
