misrouted:/ˌmɪsˈruːtɪd/
adjective
中文翻译：路由错误的，传送错误的
英文解析：Sent to the wrong destination or along the wrong route, typically of data or goods.
例句：The misrouted package finally arrived a week late. 路由错误的包裹最终晚了一周才到达。

例句：The misrouted email ended up in the spam folder. 路由错误的电子邮件最终进入了垃圾邮件文件夹。

近义词：misdirected, wrongly routed, lost
反义词：correctly routed, delivered
词性：adjective
