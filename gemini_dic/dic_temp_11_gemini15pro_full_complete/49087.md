deictical:/daɪˈɪktɪkəl/| adj.|指示的，指示性的|Relating to or denoting a word or expression whose meaning is dependent on the context in which it is used, such as 'here', 'you', 'me', 'now', 'then'.|Example: The word "this" is a deictical expression because its meaning depends on what is being referred to at the time of utterance.  这个词“这个”是一个指示性表达，因为它的含义取决于说话时所指的对象。

Example:  Pronouns like "I" and "you" are deictical, shifting meaning depending on who is speaking and who is being addressed. 像“我”和“你”这样的代词是指示性的，其含义会根据说话者和被称呼者而改变。|近义词：indexical, demonstrative|反义词:None|词性:adjective
