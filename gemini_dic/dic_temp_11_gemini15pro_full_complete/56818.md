dorky:/ˈdɔːrki/ (US), /ˈdɔːki/ (UK)| adj.|愚蠢的，笨拙的，呆呆的|Socially awkward or inept; unfashionable or out of style.  Describes someone who is considered silly, clumsy, or uncool, often in a charming or endearing way.  It can also refer to something that is considered unfashionable or outdated.|He wore dorky glasses and a pocket protector. 他戴着呆呆的眼镜和一个口袋保护套。|Synonyms: nerdy, geeky, awkward, clumsy, uncool, foolish|Antonyms: cool, stylish, fashionable, suave, sophisticated|adjective

dork:/dɔːrk/ (US), /dɔːk/ (UK)| n.|呆子，笨蛋|A silly, awkward, or foolish person.|He was such a dork in high school. 他高中时真是个呆子。|Synonyms: nerd, geek, goofball, klutz|Antonyms:  cool person, trendsetter|noun
