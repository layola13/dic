empanel:/ɪmˈpænəl/ | v. | 选入陪审团；组成(陪审团、委员会等) | To enroll or list (someone) on a panel, especially a jury. | The judge empaneled a jury of twelve people. 法官选出了一个由十二人组成的陪审团。| The commission was empaneled to investigate the allegations of corruption.  委员会被组成以调查腐败指控。 | synonyms: enlist, enroll, select, constitute | antonyms: discharge, dismiss | verb
