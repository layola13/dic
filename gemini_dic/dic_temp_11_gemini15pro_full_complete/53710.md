disclose:/dɪˈskloʊz/ (US) /dɪˈskləʊz/ (UK)| v. |公开；透露；揭露|To make something known publicly, or to show something that was hidden.|The company disclosed its financial results for the first quarter.  该公司公布了第一季度的财务业绩。

The report disclosed that the company had been losing money for years. 该报告披露该公司多年来一直亏损。|近义词：reveal, expose, divulge, unveil|反义词：conceal, hide, suppress|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *desclore, from des- "un-, out" + clore "to close".  Literally, "to unclose".

PHRASE->

fully disclose (完全披露): The company fully disclosed all relevant information. 公司完全披露了所有相关信息。

be disclosed (被披露): The information was disclosed to the public.  该信息被公之于众。
