mockle:/ˈmɒkəl/ (Scottish English)
adjective
大量的，许多的
a large amount of; much.
例句：They had a mockle of trouble getting the car started.  他们费了好大劲才把车发动起来。

例句：There was a mockle of food at the wedding feast. 婚礼宴席上有大量的食物。

近义词：much, a lot of, plenty of, a great deal of, heaps of (informal), loads of (informal)
反义词：little, few
词性：adjective
