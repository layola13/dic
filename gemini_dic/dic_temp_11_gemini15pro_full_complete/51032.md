desmidian:/dɛzˈmɪdiən/
adjective|鼓藻类的，鼓藻目植物的|Relating to or belonging to the Desmidiaceae, a family of single-celled freshwater green algae characterized by their symmetrical, often elaborately ornamented cells.|Desmidian algae are common in acidic bogs. 鼓藻类常见于酸性沼泽。|Synonyms: desmid, placoderm|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, could potentially list other types of algae, like diatoms)|adjective

desmidian:/dɛzˈmɪdiən/
noun|鼓藻|A single-celled freshwater green alga belonging to the family Desmidiaceae.|The pond water teemed with desmids and other microscopic life.  池塘水里充满了鼓藻和其他微生物。|Synonyms: desmid, placoderm|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, could potentially list other types of algae, like diatoms)|noun
