mistreading:/ˌmɪsˈriːdɪŋ/
n.
误读；错误理解
An act of reading something incorrectly or misunderstanding its meaning.
She apologized for her mistreading of the situation. 她为误读了情况而道歉。
misinterpretation, misreading, misunderstanding
correct interpretation, accurate reading, proper understanding
noun
