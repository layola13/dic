horsetail:/ˈhɔːrsteɪl/ | n. | 马尾，问荆 |A plant with a hollow, jointed stem and scale-like leaves, belonging to the genus Equisetum. Horsetails are ancient plants, dating back to prehistoric times. |  The horsetail grew along the riverbank. 马尾沿着河岸生长。

The silica in horsetails makes them abrasive; they were traditionally used for scouring pots and pans.  问荆中的二氧化硅使它们具有研磨性；传统上它们被用来擦洗锅碗瓢盆。| 近义词： Equisetum (technical/scientific name) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a plant) | 词性: noun


horsetail:/ˈhɔːrsteɪl/ | adj. | 马尾状的 | Resembling the tail of a horse; long and bushy. |  She wore her hair in a horsetail braid. 她把头发扎成马尾辫。

The comet had a long, horsetail-like plume. 这颗彗星有一条长长的、像马尾一样的尾巴。| 近义词： ponytail (for hair),  plume-like, bushy | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a descriptive adjective) | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *horsstaert, from hors "horse" + steort "tail."  The name refers to the resemblance of the plant's branching stems to a horse's tail.
