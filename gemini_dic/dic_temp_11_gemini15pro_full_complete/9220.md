antipacifist:/ˌæntiˈpæsɪfɪst/
noun|反和平主义者|Someone who opposes pacifism (the belief that war and violence are unjustifiable).|The antipacifist argued that war was sometimes necessary for self-defense.|近义词:warmonger, hawk, jingoist|反义词:pacifist, dove|n.

antipacifist:/ˌæntiˈpæsɪfɪst/
adjective|反和平主义的|Opposed to pacifism; advocating war or violence.|The antipacifist rhetoric stirred up strong emotions in the crowd.|近义词:hawkish, belligerent, war-mongering|反义词:pacifistic, dovish|adj.
