lienteric:/ˌlaɪənˈtɛrɪk/| adj.| 消化不良的，食物未消化的|Relating to or resembling lientery; characterized by the passage of undigested food in the feces.|The doctor diagnosed the patient with a lienteric condition after observing undigested food in their stool.|近义词:undigested, unassimilated|反义词:digested, assimilated|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Greek *leion* (smooth) + *enteron* (intestine)  + -ic.  The original meaning referred to smooth intestines, believed to be the cause of this condition.
