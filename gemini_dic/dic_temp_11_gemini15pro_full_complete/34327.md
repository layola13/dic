clary:/ˈklɛəri/ | n. | 香紫苏，快乐鼠尾草 | A biennial herb (Salvia sclarea) in the mint family, native to the Mediterranean region, having showy pink, white, or purplish flowers and yielding an essential oil used in perfumery. | The clary sage in my garden is blooming beautifully. 我花园里的香紫苏开得很漂亮。| synonyms: clary sage, clear-eye | antonyms: N/A | noun

clary:/ˈklɛəri/ | adj. | (古语，罕用)  明亮的，清晰的 |  (Archaic) Clear; bright. |  The clary light of dawn illuminated the valley.  黎明明亮的光芒照亮了山谷。 | synonyms: bright, clear, luminous | antonyms: dim, dark, obscure | adjective
