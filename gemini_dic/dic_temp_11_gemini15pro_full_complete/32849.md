choleriform:/ˈkɒləˌrɪfɔːrm/| adj.|类似霍乱的，霍乱样的|Resembling cholera; of the nature of cholera.|The patient presented with choleriform symptoms, including severe diarrhea and vomiting.|近义词：choleraic|反义词: healthy|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From  cholera + -iform. Cholera from Greek *kholéra*  meaning “bile,” and -iform from Latin -formis, meaning "having the form of."
