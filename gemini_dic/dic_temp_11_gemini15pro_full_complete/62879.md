enlarger:/ɪnˈlɑːrdʒər/ | n. |放大器；扩大器；增大器|A device used to make something bigger, especially a photographic image. |He used an enlarger to make a poster-sized print from his negative. 他用放大器把他的底片放大成海报大小的图片。|Synonyms: magnifier, amplifier, expander |Antonyms: reducer, shrinker, minimizer | noun

enlarge:/ɪnˈlɑːrdʒ/ | v. |扩大；放大；增大|To make something bigger. |She wants to enlarge the photograph. 她想把照片放大。|Synonyms: expand, increase, augment |Antonyms: reduce, shrink, decrease | verb
