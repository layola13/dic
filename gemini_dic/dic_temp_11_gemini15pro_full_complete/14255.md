autoecious:/ɔːˈtiːʃəs/
| adj. | 自主寄生;同株异体寄生的 | (of rust fungi) Completing its life cycle on only one host. |  Rust fungi that are autoecious complete their life cycle on a single host species.  这种锈菌是同株异体寄生的，在单一寄主物种上完成其生命周期。| Example: The cedar-apple rust is autoecious, completing its life cycle on both apple and cedar trees.  雪松苹果锈菌是同株异体寄生的，在苹果树和雪松树上完成其生命周期。| synonym: monoecious | antonym: heteroecious | adjective
