hearty-hale:/ˈhɑːrti heɪl/
adjective
健壮的，矍铄的
Strong and healthy; robust.
He was a hearty-hale old man, still working in his garden at ninety. 他是一位健壮矍铄的老人，九十岁了还在他的花园里劳作。
近义词：robust, vigorous, hale and hearty, healthy
反义词：frail, feeble, weak, sickly

词源:
Hearty: From Middle English *herti, from Old English *heorte, from Proto-Germanic *hairton.
Hale: From Middle English hail, hal, from Old Norse heill (“healthy, whole”), from Proto-Germanic *hailaz.  Hearty-hale is a combination of these two synonymous adjectives, creating a more emphatic expression of health and vigor.
