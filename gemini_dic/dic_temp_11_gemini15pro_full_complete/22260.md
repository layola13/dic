boneblack: /ˈboʊnˌblæk/ | n. | 骨黑，动物炭黑 | A black pigment made by charring bones.  It is used as a pigment, a decolorizer, and a filtering agent. |  The artist used boneblack to create a deep, rich shadow in the painting. 艺术家用骨黑在画中创造了深邃而丰富的阴影。

Boneblack is commonly used in sugar refining. 骨黑常用于糖的精炼。 | 近义词: animal charcoal, ivory black | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
