autokinetic:/ˌɔːtəʊkɪˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
自动运动的
Relating to or denoting illusory movement of a single stationary light viewed in darkness.  Perceived motion of a stationary point of light in a dark room.
例句：The autokinetic effect can make a stationary light appear to move.  自动运动效应可以使静止的光看起来像在移动。

例句：Pilots flying at night sometimes experience the autokinetic phenomenon.  夜间飞行的飞行员有时会经历自动运动现象。
近义词：apparent motion, illusory motion, self-motion
反义词：actual motion, real motion
词性：adjective

Etymology:  From Greek *autokinētos* "self-moving," from *autos* "self" + *kinētos* "moved," from *kinein* "to move."
