outbawl:/ˌaʊtˈbɔːl/
verb
大声哭喊，胜过哭喊
to cry louder than someone else
The baby outbawled his older sister.  这个婴儿的哭声比他姐姐的还大。

近义词: outshout, outcry, yell, scream
反义词: whisper, murmur
