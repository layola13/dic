honcho:/ˈhɒntʃəʊ/ (UK) /ˈhɑːntʃoʊ/ (US)| n.|头头，老板，负责人|A person in charge or in a position of authority, often in a casual or informal context.  Originally from Japanese 判事 (hanji) meaning "judge".|The marketing honcho decided to launch a new campaign. 市场部的老大决定启动一个新的活动。|近义词：boss, chief, leader, manager|反义词: subordinate, employee|名词

honchos:/ˈhɒntʃəʊz/ (UK) /ˈhɑːntʃoʊz/ (US)| n.|头头们，老板们，负责人|Plural form of honcho.  |The honchos at the company are planning a merger. 公司的高层们正在计划合并。|近义词：bosses, chiefs, leaders, managers|反义词: subordinates, employees|名词
