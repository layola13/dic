mellific:/məˈlɪfɪk/| adj.|甜蜜的；产生蜂蜜的；流畅的|Making or producing honey; sweet as honey.  Figuratively, flowing smoothly and sweetly (of language, style, etc.).|Her mellific voice soothed the crying child. 她甜美的声音抚慰了哭闹的孩子。|近义词：sweet, honeyed, dulcet|反义词：bitter, acrid|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *mellificus*, from *mel* (honey) + *-ficus* (making, producing).  The *-ficus* suffix is related to *facere* (to make, to do).
