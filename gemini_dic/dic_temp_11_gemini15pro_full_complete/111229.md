lithotriptic:/ˌlɪθəˈtrɪptɪk/| adj. n.|碎石的，溶石的；碎石剂|Relating to, or causing lithotripsy; a medicine or agent for dissolving stones, especially urinary calculi.  |This new lithotriptic drug has shown promising results in dissolving kidney stones. 这种新的碎石药物在溶解肾结石方面显示出良好的效果。|The doctor prescribed a lithotriptic to break down the kidney stones. 医生开了一种碎石药来分解肾结石。|近义词：antilithic|反义词: (No direct antonym, but possibly something promoting stone formation)|词性: adjective, noun
lithotripsy:/ˈlɪθəˌtrɪpsi/| n.|碎石术|The process of breaking up stones in the kidney, bladder, or gallbladder using shock waves, lasers, or other means. |He underwent lithotripsy to treat his kidney stones. 他接受了碎石术来治疗肾结石。|Lithotripsy is a non-invasive procedure for removing stones. 碎石术是一种无创去除结石的方法。|近义词: (No direct synonym, but procedures like ESWL (Extracorporeal Shock Wave Lithotripsy) are related)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性: noun
