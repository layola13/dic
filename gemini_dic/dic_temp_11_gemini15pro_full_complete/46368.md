dadaistically:/ˌdɑːdɑːˈɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 达达主义地 | In a manner characterized by the principles of Dadaism, an avant-garde art movement that rejected logic and reason in favor of chaos and irrationality. |  The artist threw paint at the canvas dadaistically, embracing the randomness of the result. 艺术家达达主义地将颜料扔到画布上，拥抱结果的随机性。| synonym:  absurdly, irrationally, nonsensically | antonym: rationally, logically, conventionally | Part of Speech: Adverb

dadaistically:/ˌdɑːdɑːˈɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 达达主义地 |  He dressed dadaistically, with mismatched socks and a coat worn inside out. 他穿着达达主义风格的衣服，袜子不搭配，外套穿反了。 | synonym: bizarrely, eccentrically | antonym: traditionally, conventionally | Part of Speech: Adverb
