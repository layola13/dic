non-Europeans:/nɒnˌjʊərəˈpiːənz/| n.|非欧洲人|People who are not from Europe.  Relating to people who do not originate from Europe.|例句：The museum exhibit showcases art from non-Europeans.  博物馆的展览展示了来自非欧洲人的艺术。|例句：Non-Europeans have made significant contributions to global culture. 非欧洲人对全球文化做出了重大贡献。|近义词：non-Westerners|反义词：Europeans|词性:名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin "non" meaning "not"
European: from Europe, ultimately from Greek "Eurṓpē"
