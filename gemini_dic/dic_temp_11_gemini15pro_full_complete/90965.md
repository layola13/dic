hurricane: /ˈhʌrɪkən/ (US) /ˈhʌrɪkeɪn/ (UK)| n. |飓风，暴风|A violent, tropical, cyclonic storm of the western North Atlantic, having wind speeds of or in excess of 74 miles per hour (64 knots or 119 kilometers per hour).|Hurricane Katrina devastated New Orleans. 卡特里娜飓风摧毁了新奥尔良。|近义词：cyclone, typhoon, storm|反义词:calm, breeze|词性:名词

hurricane: /ˈhʌrɪkən/ (US) /ˈhʌrɪkeɪn/ (UK)| n. | something resembling a hurricane in force, intensity, or speed|The hurricane of criticism swept over the politician.  批评的飓风席卷了这位政治家。|近义词：torrent, barrage, deluge|反义词:trickle, dribble|词性:名词
