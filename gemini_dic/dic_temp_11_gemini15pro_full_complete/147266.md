overmilked:/ˌoʊvərˈmɪlkt/ | adj. | 过度榨取的，过度开发的 | Exploited or used excessively, like a cow that's been milked too much, often metaphorically referring to ideas, trends, or resources. | The overmilked concept of vampires in teen fiction has become tiresome.  青少年小说中过度使用的吸血鬼概念已经变得令人厌倦。| Near Synonyms: overused, exhausted, depleted, overworked | Antonyms:  underutilized, fresh, novel | adjective

overmilked:/ˌoʊvərˈmɪlkt/ | v. | 过度榨取，过度开发 | To exploit or use something excessively. | The studio overmilked the superhero franchise until audiences lost interest.  这家电影公司过度开发了超级英雄系列电影，直到观众失去了兴趣。| Near Synonyms: overexploit, overuse | Antonyms: underuse, conserve | verb
