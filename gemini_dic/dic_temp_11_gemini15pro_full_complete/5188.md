allhallown:/ɔːlˈhæloʊn/ | n. |万圣节前夜；诸圣日前夕|All Hallown is the evening before All Saints Day. 万圣节前夜是诸圣日前夕。|例句：All Hallown is a time for spooky costumes and trick-or-treating. 万圣节前夜是穿奇装异服和“不给糖就捣蛋”的时候。

例句：We celebrated All Hallown with a bonfire and scary stories. 我们用篝火和恐怖故事庆祝万圣节前夜。
|近义词：Halloween, All Hallows' Eve |反义词: None|词性:名词
