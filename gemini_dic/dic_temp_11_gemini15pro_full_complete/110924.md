liposomal:/ˌlaɪpəˈsoʊməl/ /ˌlɪpəˈsəʊməl/
adjective
脂质体的； liposome的形容词形式
Relating to or consisting of liposomes.
Liposomal drug delivery can improve the efficacy of certain medications. 脂质体药物递送可以提高某些药物的疗效。
Liposomal amphotericin B is used to treat fungal infections.  脂质体两性霉素B用于治疗真菌感染。
Synonyms: lipid-based, vesicle-encapsulated
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider "free form" when referring to drug delivery)
词性: adjective
