monotheistic:/ˌmɒnəʊθiːˈɪstɪk/ /ˌmɑːnəθiːˈɪstɪk/
adjective
一神论的
Relating to or characterized by the belief that there is only one God.
Judaism, Christianity, and Islam are monotheistic religions. 犹太教、基督教和伊斯兰教都是一神教。
monolatrous, henotheistic
polytheistic, pantheistic
