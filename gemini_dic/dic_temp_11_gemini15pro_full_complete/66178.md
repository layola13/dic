exchangeable:/ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒəbl/ | adj. | 可交换的，可兑换的 | Able to be exchanged, especially for something of equal value. |  例句：The tokens are exchangeable for merchandise.  这些代币可以兑换商品。

例句：These shares are exchangeable for cash. 这些股票可以兑换现金。


近义词：interchangeable, tradable, replaceable | 反义词：non-exchangeable, irreplaceable | 词性: adjective
