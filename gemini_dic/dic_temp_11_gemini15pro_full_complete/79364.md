glenoidal:/ɡlɪˈnɔɪdəl/
adjective
盂的，关节窝的，浅窝的
Relating to or resembling a glenoid cavity, which is a shallow depression, especially the one on the scapula that articulates with the head of the humerus.
The glenoidal labrum deepens the glenoid cavity of the shoulder.  盂唇加深了肩胛骨的关节盂腔。
The glenoidal fossa articulates with the condyle of the mandible. 下颌髁与关节窝相连。
近义词: articular, fossa
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider 'projection' or 'process' as conceptually opposite)
词性: adjective

词源: From Modern Latin *glenoidālis, from Ancient Greek γλήνη (glḗnē, “socket, eyeball”) + -oid.
