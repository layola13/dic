havala:/həˈvɑːlə/ | n. | 哈瓦拉, 非正式的价值转移系统 | An informal value transfer system based on trust and extensive networks, often used in the Middle East, South Asia, and Africa, where money is transferred through agents without physical movement of cash.  It operates outside of traditional banking systems. | Example 1:  The businessman used hawala to quickly transfer funds to his family overseas.  这位商人使用哈瓦拉系统快速地将资金转移到他海外的家人那里。

Example 2:  Hawala is often preferred for its speed and lower fees compared to traditional banks. 与传统银行相比，哈瓦拉通常因其速度快、费用低而受到青睐。 | Synonym:  underground banking, informal remittance system | Antonym:  formal banking system | noun
