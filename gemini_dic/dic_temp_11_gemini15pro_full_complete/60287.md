eggheads:/ˈeɡˌhɛdz/| n.|书呆子，知识分子|A person who is very intelligent and interested in academic subjects, sometimes seen as lacking social skills.|例句：The eggheads at the university are working on a new project. 大学里的那些书呆子正在做一个新项目。|例句：He's a bit of an egghead, always with his nose in a book. 他有点书呆子气，总是埋头读书。|近义词：intellectual, scholar, brain, nerd|反义词:dummy, idiot|名词
