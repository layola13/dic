excising:/ɪkˈsaɪzɪŋ/
verb
切除，删除
The surgeon is excising the tumor.  外科医生正在切除肿瘤。
Removing, cutting out
The editor is excising the unnecessary parts of the article. 编辑正在删除文章中不必要的部分。
近义词:removing, deleting, extracting
反义词:inserting, adding, implanting
词性:v.
