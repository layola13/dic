isopathy:/aɪˈsɒpəθi/
n.
顺势疗法,同类疗法
A system of medicine based on the principle that diseases can be cured by substances that produce similar symptoms in healthy people.
Isopathy is a controversial alternative medicine practice. 顺势疗法是一种有争议的替代医学疗法。
He tried isopathy to treat his allergies. 他尝试用顺势疗法治疗他的过敏症。
近义词:homeopathy
反义词:allopathy
词性:noun
