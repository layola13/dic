mis-adapted:/ˌmɪsəˈdæptɪd/
adjective
不适应的，适应不良的
not properly adapted; poorly suited to a particular purpose or environment
The polar bear was mis-adapted to the warm climate. 这只北极熊不适应温暖的气候。
近义词: maladapted, ill-adapted, unsuited, unfit
反义词: adapted, suited, fit, well-adapted
词性: adjective
