kerry:/ˈkɛri/ | n. vt.|搬运；运送；携带；赢得；获得；说服；支持；（比赛）领先|To transport or carry something from one place to another; To win or achieve something; To persuade or gain the support of someone.|He kerried the luggage to the car. 他把行李搬到车上。|近义词：carry, transport, convey, win, secure, persuade|反义词：lose, fail, abandon|词性:名词,动词
词源: From Middle English *kerien, from Old North French carier, from Vulgar Latin *carricare, from Late Latin carrus (“car, cart”).
