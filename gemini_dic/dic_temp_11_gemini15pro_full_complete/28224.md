capsicums:/ˈkæpsɪkəmz/
noun
辣椒（复数）
Capsicums are a genus of flowering plants in the nightshade family Solanaceae, native to the Americas.  The fruit of Capsicum plants are commonly used as vegetables or spices.
I grow a variety of capsicums in my garden. 我在我的花园里种植各种辣椒。
synonyms: peppers, chilies
antonyms: None (Capsicums are a type of plant; there isn't a direct opposite.)
n.


capsicum:/ˈkæpsɪkəm/
noun
辣椒（单数）
Capsicum refers to a plant of the genus Capsicum, or its fruit.
This recipe calls for a green capsicum. 这个食谱需要一个青椒。
synonyms: pepper, chili
antonyms: None
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From New Latin *capsicum, from Latin capsa (“box, case”), because of the shape of the fruit.
