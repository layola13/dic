mouldily:/ˈmoʊldɪli/ /ˈməʊldɪli/| adv.|发霉地；陈腐地|Covered with or smelling of mould; stale.  Characterized by mould or mildew; musty.|The bread was stored improperly and became mouldily quickly.  这面包储存不当，很快就发霉了。|近义词：musty, fusty, mildewy|反义词：fresh, clean|词性:副词

Etymology: From mould + -y + -ly.  "Mould" comes from Old English *mold, from Proto-Germanic *muldaz, meaning "dust, loose earth."
