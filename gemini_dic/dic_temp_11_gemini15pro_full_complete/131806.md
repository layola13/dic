nonconsensually: /ˌnɒnkənˈsɛnʃʊəli/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈsɛnʃʊəli/
adverb
未经同意地，非自愿地
Without consent; in a way that is not agreed to by all involved parties.
The video was taken nonconsensually. 这段视频未经同意拍摄。
He touched her nonconsensually. 他未经她同意就碰了她。
Synonyms: forcibly, against one's will, without permission
Antonyms: consensually, voluntarily, with permission
词性: 副词
