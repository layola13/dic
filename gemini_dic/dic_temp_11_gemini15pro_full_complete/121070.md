misconstrued: /ˌmɪskənˈstruːd/ | v. (past participle)| 误解，曲解 | To interpret (something, such as a word or action) wrongly. |  His silence was misconstrued as arrogance. 他的沉默被误解为傲慢。|  The reporter misconstrued the politician's statement. 记者曲解了政治家的声明。| Synonyms: misinterpret, misunderstand, misread | Antonyms: understand, comprehend, grasp | Verb
