daikon:/ˈdaɪkɒn/ (UK) /ˈdaɪkɑːn/ (US)|n.|萝卜，大根|A large, mild-flavored, white East Asian radish.  It is often used in salads and as a garnish.|Example 1:  I added sliced daikon to my salad for some extra crunch. 我在沙拉里加了萝卜片，使其更加爽脆。

Example 2: Daikon is often served pickled in Japanese cuisine.  日本料理中常供应腌制的大根。|Synonym: white radish, Japanese radish, Chinese radish, mooli|Antonym: N/A (No direct antonym for a specific vegetable)|noun
