holothurioidea:/ˌhɒləˌθjʊəriˈɔɪdiə/ | n. | 海参纲 |A class of echinoderms, commonly known as sea cucumbers, characterized by elongated bodies and leathery skin.| Sea cucumbers, members of the class Holothurioidea, are found on the ocean floor. 海参，属于海参纲，生活在海底。| 近义词: sea cucumber (common name) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a taxonomic classification) | 词性: noun

holothurian:/ˌhɒləˈθjʊəriən/ | n. adj. | 海参；海参纲的 | Noun: A sea cucumber; Adjective: Relating to or characteristic of sea cucumbers.| The holothurian expelled its sticky cuvierian tubules as a defense mechanism.  这只海参排出粘性的居维氏管作为防御机制。 |  The holothurian's diet consists mainly of detritus. 这只海参的食物主要由碎屑组成。 | 近义词: sea cucumber | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun, adjective


词源: From Greek *holothourion*  (probably related to *holothurion* meaning "water polyp," perhaps from its shape),  Latinized as *holothurium*.
