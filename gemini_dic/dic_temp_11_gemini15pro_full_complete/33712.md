circadian:/sɜːrˈkeɪdiən/ | adj. |生理节奏的，昼夜节律的 | Relating to or showing a biological process that repeats every 24 hours. |  The circadian rhythm regulates sleep-wake cycles. 生理节奏调节睡眠-觉醒周期。|  The study focuses on circadian variations in blood pressure. 这项研究的重点是血压的昼夜变化。|synonyms: diurnal, daily | antonyms: arrhythmic, non-circadian | adjective
