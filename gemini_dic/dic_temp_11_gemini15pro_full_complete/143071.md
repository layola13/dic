outbragged: /ˌaʊtˈbræɡd/
verb
吹嘘胜过；在吹牛方面超过
outdo someone in bragging; boast more extensively or convincingly than another
He outbragged all his friends about his new car. 他对他的新车吹嘘得比所有朋友都厉害。
近义词: outboasted, outsmarted, surpassed
反义词: understated, downplayed
词性: verb (动词 - 过去式和过去分词)
