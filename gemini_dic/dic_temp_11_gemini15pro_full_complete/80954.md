gracefuller:/ˈɡreɪsfələr/| adj.|更优雅的，更优美的|Comparative form of graceful.  Having or showing grace in a greater degree.|Her movements became gracefuller as she practiced ballet.  随着她练习芭蕾舞，她的动作变得更加优雅。|Synonyms: more elegant, more refined, more poised|Antonyms: clumsier, more awkward, less graceful|adjective
