laurasian: /lɔːˈreɪʒən/ | adj. n. | 劳亚大陆的；劳亚大陆人 | Relating to or denoting Laurasia, a vast continental area believed to have existed in the Mesozoic Era, comprising the present-day continents of North America, Europe, and Asia north of the Himalayas. Also, a person, animal, or plant inhabiting or originating in Laurasia. |  The laurasian flora is diverse and includes many species found in both North America and Eurasia. 劳亚大陆的植物种类繁多，包括许多在北美和欧亚大陆都有发现的物种。 | The paleontologist specialized in laurasian dinosaurs. 这位古生物学家专门研究劳亚大陆的恐龙。 | 近义词:  Northern Hemisphere, Holarctic (for biogeography) | 反义词: Gondwanan | 词性: adjective, noun
