futuritial: /ˌfjuːtʃəˈrɪʃəl/ | adj. | 未来主义的，未来派的 | Relating to or characteristic of futurism, an early 20th-century artistic and social movement that emphasized and glorified themes associated with contemporary concepts of the future, including speed, technology, youth, violence, and objects such as the car, the airplane, and the industrial city.  |  The architect's designs were strikingly futuritial, incorporating sleek lines and metallic finishes.  这位建筑师的设计惊人地具有未来主义色彩，融合了流畅的线条和金属质感。

The futuritial fashion show featured garments made of unconventional materials and bold, geometric shapes. 这场未来主义时装秀展示了由非传统材料和大胆的几何形状制成的服装。 | 近义词: futuristic, avant-garde, modernistic | 反义词: traditional, antiquated, old-fashioned | 词性: 形容词
