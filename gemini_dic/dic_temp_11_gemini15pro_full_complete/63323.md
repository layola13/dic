entries:/ˈentriːz/| n.|条目；登记；参赛作品；进入；入口|A record of something in a book, list, etc.; the act of going into a place.|例句：The entries in his diary revealed his innermost thoughts. 他的日记记录了他内心深处的想法。

例句：The competition received over 500 entries. 这场比赛收到了500多份参赛作品。|近义词：items, records, listings, entrances, access|反义词:exits, departures|词性:名词
