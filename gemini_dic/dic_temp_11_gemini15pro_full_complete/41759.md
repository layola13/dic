corrupter:/kəˈrʌptər/| n. |腐蚀者；败坏者；堕落者|Someone who corrupts or makes something worse. |The corrupter of youth was banished from the city.  这个败坏青少年的家伙被驱逐出城了。|近义词：debaser, perverter, demoralizer|反义词: reformer, purifier, improver |noun
Etymology: From Late Middle English *corruptour, from Anglo-Norman *corruptur, from Latin corruptor (“one who corrupts, briber”), agent noun from corrumpō (“I corrupt, bribe”). Equivalent to corrupt +‎ -er.
