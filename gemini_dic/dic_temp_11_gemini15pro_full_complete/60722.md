electro-osmosis:/ɪˌlɛktroʊ ɒzˈmoʊsɪs/ |n.| 电渗 |The movement of a liquid through a porous material (such as a membrane) due to an applied electric field.|Electro-osmosis is used to separate proteins in a laboratory setting.| 近义词: electroendosmosis | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps reverse electro-osmosis, but this isn't standard) | 词性: noun
