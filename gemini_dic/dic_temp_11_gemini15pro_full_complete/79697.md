glowworm:/ˈɡloʊwɜːrm/ (US) /ˈɡləʊwɜːm/ (UK)| n.|萤火虫|A beetle whose larva or wingless female emits light.|The children were fascinated by the glowworms in the garden. 孩子们被花园里的萤火虫迷住了。|firefly, lightning bug|darkling|noun
Etymology->
From Middle English *glowworm, equivalent to glow +‎ worm. Cognate with Scots glowworm (“glow-worm”), West Frisian gloeiwjirn (“glow-worm”), Dutch gloeiworm (“glow-worm”), German Glühwürmchen (“glow-worm”), Swedish lysmask (“glow-worm”).
