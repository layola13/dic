armies:/ˈɑːrmiz/
noun
军队，大军
A large organized body of armed personnel trained for war especially on land.
The two armies clashed on the open field. 两军在旷野上交锋。
synonyms: troops, forces, legions, hosts
antonyms: civilians, individuals
n.
