norther:/ˈnɔːrðər/ | n. | 北风；寒流 | A cold north wind, especially a strong, cold wind from the north that brings a sudden drop in temperature. |  The sudden norther chilled us to the bone. 突如其来的北风让我们感到刺骨的寒冷。|  The norther blew fiercely, bringing snow and ice. 北风猛烈地吹着，带来了冰雪。 | synonym: north wind, cold snap, cold front | antonym: south wind | noun
