brokered:/ˈbroʊkərd/ | adj. | 经纪的，促成的 | Arranged or negotiated by a broker or intermediary. |  The two warring nations finally reached a brokered peace agreement.  两个交战国最终达成了经由斡旋的和平协议。| Synonym: negotiated, mediated, arranged | Antonym: unmediated, direct | Adjective

brokered:/ˈbroʊkərd/ | v. |  (broker 的过去式和过去分词) 斡旋，调停，作为经纪人 | Acted as a broker or intermediary in a transaction or negotiation. | He brokered a deal between the two companies. 他促成了两家公司之间的交易。| Synonym: negotiate, mediate, arrange | Antonym: obstruct, hinder | Verb
