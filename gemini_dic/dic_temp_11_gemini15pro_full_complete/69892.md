fertiliser:/ˈfɜːrtəlaɪzər/ (US) /ˈfɜːtɪlaɪzər/ (UK)| n.|肥料|A substance added to soil or land to increase its fertility.|The farmer used a chemical fertiliser on his crops. 农民在庄稼上使用了化肥。|近义词：manure, compost, plant food|反义词: N/A|名词

fertilize:/ˈfɜːrtəlaɪz/ (US) /ˈfɜːtɪlaɪz/ (UK)| vt.|施肥；使肥沃；使受精|To add fertiliser to; to make fertile; to cause an egg or seed to develop a new individual.|He fertilized the lawn every spring. 他每年春天都给草坪施肥。|近义词：enrich, nourish, inseminate|反义词: sterilize|动词
