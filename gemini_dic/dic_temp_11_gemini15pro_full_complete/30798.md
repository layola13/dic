cetology:/siːˈtɒlədʒi/
n.
鲸类学
The scientific study of whales, dolphins, and porpoises.
Example: He is pursuing a degree in cetology. 他正在攻读鲸类学学位。

Example: Cetology helps us understand these magnificent creatures and their vital role in the marine ecosystem. 鲸类学帮助我们了解这些壮丽的生物及其在海洋生态系统中的重要作用。

synonym:whale science, marine mammalogy
antonym: N/A
