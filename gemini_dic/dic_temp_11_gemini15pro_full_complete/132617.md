nondisadvantaged:/ˌnɒndɪsədˈvɑːntɪdʒd/
adjective
非弱势的；没有处于不利地位的
Not suffering from a disadvantage or deprivation; privileged.
例句：Nondisadvantaged students often have access to better resources.  非弱势学生通常能够获得更好的资源。

例句：The nondisadvantaged children in the study showed higher academic achievement.  研究中非弱势儿童表现出更高的学业成就。

近义词：privileged, advantaged, affluent
反义词：disadvantaged, underprivileged, deprived
词性：adjective
