concomitant: /kənˈkɒmɪtənt/ /kənˈkɑːmɪtənt/
concomitant: n.|伴随物，伴随现象|Something that accompanies or exists concurrently with something else.|The fever was a concomitant of the infection. 发烧是感染的伴随症状。|synonyms: accompaniment, attendant, corollary,  |antonyms: unrelated, separate
concomitant: adj.|伴随的，同时发生的|Naturally accompanying or associated.|The rise in unemployment has been concomitant with a decline in consumer spending. 失业率的上升伴随着消费者支出的下降。|synonyms: accompanying, concurrent, attendant,  |antonyms: unrelated, separate, independent
concomitants: /kənˈkɒmɪtənts/ /kənˈkɑːmɪtənts/
