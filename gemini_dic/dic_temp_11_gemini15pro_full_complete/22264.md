bonehead:/ˈboʊnˌhɛd/| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜|A stupid person. |例句：He made a real bonehead move by investing all his money in that failing company. 他把所有的钱都投资在那家倒闭的公司里，真是愚蠢透顶。
