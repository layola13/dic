cogitator:/ˈkɒdʒɪteɪtə(r)/| n. |思考者，沉思者|A person who thinks deeply and carefully about something.  一个对某事进行深入思考的人。|The ancient philosophers were profound cogitators. 古代哲学家是深刻的思考者。|近义词：thinker, philosopher, intellectual, ponderer|反义词：ignoramus, fool|词性:Noun
