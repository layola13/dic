obliging:/əˈblaɪdʒɪŋ/
adjective
乐于助人的；恳切的；愿意帮忙的
willing to do favors; helpful
例句：The staff were very obliging and helpful.  工作人员非常乐于助人。

例句：He was obliging enough to show us the way. 他很好心地给我们指路。

近义词：helpful, accommodating, kind, considerate, amiable
反义词：unhelpful, disobliging, rude

词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English *obliging, present participle of obliʒen (“to oblige”), from Old French obliger, from Latin obligāre (“to bind, tie up, fasten, pledge, put under obligation”), from ob- (“towards”) + ligāre (“to bind”).
