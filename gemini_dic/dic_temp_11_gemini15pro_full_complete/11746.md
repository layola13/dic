aromatize:/əˈroʊmətaɪz/ |v. |使芳香；加香料于|To give something a pleasant smell, often by adding spices or other fragrant substances.|例句：She aromatized the room with lavender oil. 她用薰衣草油使房间芳香。

例句：The tea is aromatized with bergamot. 这茶用佛手柑调味。

近义词：perfume, scent, fragrance|反义词：deodorize|词性：动词
