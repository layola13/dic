nonmutative:/nɒnˈmjuːtətɪv/ | adj. | 非交换的 | Not commutative; not obeying the commutative law. In mathematics, an operation is nonmutative if changing the order of the operands changes the result.  |Matrix multiplication is nonmutative. 矩阵乘法是非交换的。|  The cross product of two vectors is a nonmutative operation. 两个向量的叉积是非交换运算。| noncommutative, non-commutative | commutative | adjective
