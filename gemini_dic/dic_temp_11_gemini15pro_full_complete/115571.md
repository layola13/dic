maramani:/ˌmɑːrəˈmɑːni/| n.| 马拉马尼（一种非洲南部津巴布韦的传统乐器）|A maramani is a traditional plucked musical instrument from Zimbabwe in southern Africa. It's a type of mbira, or thumb piano, made from a wooden board or box with metal keys attached.  |例句：The maramani player's thumbs danced across the metal keys, creating a hypnotic melody. 马拉马尼演奏者的拇指在金属琴键上跳动，创造出一种催眠般的旋律。

例句：The maramani music filled the air with a rhythmic, vibrant sound. 马拉马尼的音乐充满了节奏感和活力。|近义词：mbira, thumb piano, kalimba|反义词: None (It's a specific instrument, not a concept with an opposite)|词性:noun
