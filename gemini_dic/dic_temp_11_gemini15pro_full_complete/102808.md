jargoner:/ˈdʒɑːrɡənər/ | n. |行话使用者；惯用术语者|A person who uses jargon, especially excessively or unnecessarily. | He's such a jargoner, I can never understand him in meetings. 他真是个行话大王，我在会议上从来都听不懂他在说什么。| 近义词： specialist, technician, expert | 反义词: layman, novice | noun

jargon:/ˈdʒɑːrɡən/ | n. |行话；术语；黑话|Special words or expressions that are used by a particular profession or group and are difficult for others to understand. | The contract was full of legal jargon. 合同里充满了法律术语。| 近义词： terminology, cant, slang | 反义词: standard language, plain language | noun
