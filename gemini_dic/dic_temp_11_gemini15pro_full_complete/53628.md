discerner:/dɪˈsɜːrnər/| n.|辨别者；识别者|A discerner is someone who has or shows the ability to recognize and understand the difference between things.|She is a discerner of quality in art. 她是一位艺术品质的鉴赏家。|近义词：judge,connoisseur, expert,critic|反义词：fool, simpleton|noun

discern:/dɪˈsɜːn/| v.|辨别；识别；看出；察觉|To discern something means to see, recognize, or understand it, especially something that is not obvious.|I could discern a figure in the darkness. 我能在黑暗中辨认出一个身影。|近义词：perceive, detect, distinguish, recognize|反义词：overlook, miss, ignore|verb
