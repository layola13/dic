demising:/dɪˈmaɪzɪŋ/
n.| 租赁期满，死亡，转让|The demise of the lease meant they had to move out.  租赁期满意味着他们必须搬出去。|
adj.|即将死亡的，即将结束的|The demising empire was clinging to its last vestiges of power.  这个即将灭亡的帝国紧紧抓住它最后的权力残余。|
v.|(正式或法律)转让，遗赠(尤指财产)，死亡|He demised his estate to his nephew. 他把遗产转让给了他的侄子。|
近义词:transferring, conveying, bequeathing, expiring, dying|
反义词:acquiring, inheriting, living, commencing|
词性:noun, adjective, verb (present participle of demise)


词源: From Old French *demis, past participle of metre "to place, put, send," from Latin mittere "to send, throw." The original sense was legal, "to convey or transfer (property)."
