nutmegged:/ˈnʌtˌmɛɡd/ | v. | 穿裆球，被穿裆球 | To pass (a ball) through an opponent's legs (in soccer, hockey, etc.) |  He nutmegged the defender and scored a goal. 他穿了防守队员的裆，进了一球。 | 近义词: meg, nutmeg | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: 动词

nutmegged:/ˈnʌtˌmɛɡd/ | adj. | 被穿裆球的 | Having had a ball passed through one's legs (in soccer, hockey, etc.) | The nutmegged defender was embarrassed. 被穿裆的防守队员很尴尬。 | 近义词: megged | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From nutmeg (the spice), possibly because the action of putting the ball through someone's legs was jokingly compared to putting a nutmeg in their pocket without them noticing.
