aggeneration:/əˌɡrɛɡˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. |聚集，聚合，集合|The process of gathering into a mass or sum or whole.|The aggeneration of data from multiple sources can provide valuable insights. 从多个来源的数据聚合可以提供有价值的见解。|近义词：accumulation, aggregation, collection, gathering|反义词：dispersion, scattering|词性:名词

Etymology: From Latin *aggregatio*, from *aggregare* "to add to," from *ad-* "to" + *gregare* "to flock," from *grex* "flock."
