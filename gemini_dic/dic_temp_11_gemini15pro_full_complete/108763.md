leary:/ˈlɪəri/| adj.|谨慎的，怀疑的，不信任的|Reluctant to trust or confide, based on suspicion or caution.|例句：I'm leary of investing in that company. 我对投资那家公司持谨慎态度。|例句：She was leary of his promises. 她对他的承诺表示怀疑。|近义词：suspicious, wary, cautious, distrustful|反义词：trusting, gullible, naive|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From the Irish *learaidh* meaning "watchful, alert," from *leir-* meaning "watch, look."
