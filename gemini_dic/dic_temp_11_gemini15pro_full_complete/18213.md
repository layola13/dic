beleaguerer:/bɪˈliːɡərər/| n. |围攻者，困扰者|A person or thing that besieges or harasses someone or something.|The beleaguerer finally breached the city walls. 围攻者最终攻破了城墙。|The constant noise was a beleaguerer to her concentration.  持续不断的噪音困扰着她的注意力。|近义词：besieger, attacker, harasser, annoyer|反义词：defender, protector|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From  beleaguer + -er.  Beleaguer comes from the Dutch  belegeren  "to besiege," from  be- "around" +  leger  "camp."
