overassessed: /ˌoʊvərəˈsɛst/
adjective
估价过高的，过度评估的
Having been assessed at too high a value.
The property was overassessed, leading to higher property taxes. 这处房产被估价过高，导致了更高的房产税。
The tax assessor admitted they had overassessed several properties in the neighborhood. 税务评估员承认他们对附近几处房产的估价过高。
近义词: overvalued, overestimated
反义词: undervalued, underestimated
词性: adjective
