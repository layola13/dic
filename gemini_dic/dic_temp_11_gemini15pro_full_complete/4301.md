aisleless:/ˈaɪl.ləs/
adjective
无通道的; 没有走道的
Having no aisles.  A large, open space without designated passageways.
The warehouse was aisleless, making it difficult to navigate with a forklift.  这个仓库没有通道，使得用叉车很难通行。
The aisleless airplane cabin felt spacious and modern.  没有通道的飞机机舱感觉宽敞而现代化。
近义词: open-plan, unobstructed
反义词: aisled
