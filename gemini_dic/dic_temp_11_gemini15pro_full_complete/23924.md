breakroom:/ˈbreɪkruːm/ /ˈbreɪkˌruːm/| n. |休息室，茶水间|A room in a workplace where people can relax, eat, and socialize. |例句：The employees gathered in the breakroom for lunch.  员工们聚集在休息室吃午饭。

例句：There's a coffee machine and a microwave in the breakroom. 休息室里有咖啡机和微波炉。|近义词：staff room, lounge, common room|反义词:workplace, office (in a general sense)|词性:noun
