anisospore:/ˌænɪsɒˈspɔːr/ /ˌænɪsoʊˈspɔːr/| n. |异形孢子|A spore of two or more kinds of the same species that differ in size and/or shape, as in some ferns.|The fern produces anisospores, resulting in separate male and female gametophytes.|Synonym: heterospore|Antonym: isospore|noun
