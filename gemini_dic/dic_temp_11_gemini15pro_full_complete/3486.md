aftergrass:/ˈæftərɡrɑːs/ /ˈɑːftərɡræs/
n.
再生草；割后再生草；二茬草
The grass that grows after the first cutting in a season.
The farmer let his cows graze on the lush aftergrass. 农民让他的牛吃茂盛的再生草。

近义词: aftermath, second growth, regrowth
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "first cutting" conceptually)
词性: noun
