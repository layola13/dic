frilled:/frɪld/
adj.|有褶边的，有饰边的|Having or adorned with a frill or frills.|The frilled lizard puffed out its impressive neck frill.  褶伞蜥鼓起了它那令人印象深刻的颈褶。|Example: She wore a frilled blouse. 她穿了一件有褶边的衬衫。|Synonyms: ruffled, pleated, gathered, trimmed|Antonyms: plain, simple, unadorned|adjective


frill:/frɪl/
n.|褶边，饰边；不必要的装饰|A strip of gathered or pleated material used as a decoration or trimming.|The dress had a delicate frill around the neckline. 这件连衣裙的领口有一圈精致的褶边。|Example:  He considered the extra buttons a frivolous frill. 他认为多余的纽扣是轻浮的装饰。|Synonyms: ruffle, flounce, furbelow, ornament, embellishment|Antonyms: plainness, simplicity|noun
v.|给…加褶边|To decorate with a frill or frills.|She frilled the edges of the tablecloth. 她给桌布的边缘加了褶边。|Example: The curtains were frilled with lace. 窗帘用蕾丝装饰了褶边。|Synonyms: ruffle, gather, pleat, adorn|Antonyms: simplify, strip|verb
