arthrozoic:/ˌɑːrθrəˈzoʊɪk/
adjective
关节动物的；由节肢动物传播的
Relating to or transmitted by arthropods.  Specifically, relating to diseases transmitted by arthropods.
Arthropods, such as mosquitoes, can be vectors for arthrozoic diseases like malaria.  蚊子等节肢动物可以成为疟疾等关节动物传播疾病的媒介。
近义词:vector-borne
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-arthrozoic")
词性:adjective


Etymology:
From Greek *ἄρθρον* (arthron, “joint”) + *ζῷον* (zōion, “animal”).
