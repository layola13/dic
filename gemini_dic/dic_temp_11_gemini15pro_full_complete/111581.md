lobotomising:/ˌloʊbəˈtɒmaɪzɪŋ/
verb
脑叶切除术
performing a lobotomy; cutting into a lobe of the brain, usually the frontal lobe.
The doctor was lobotomising the patient to treat their severe mental illness.  医生正在对病人进行脑叶切除术以治疗他们的严重精神疾病。
He spoke out against the practice of lobotomising patients. 他公开反对对病人进行脑叶切除术的做法。
近义词:  operating, performing surgery
反义词: healing, curing
词性: verb (present participle)

近义词: operate on, perform surgery on

lobotomy:/ləˈbɒtəmi/
noun
脑叶切除术
a surgical operation involving incision into the prefrontal lobe of the brain, formerly used to treat mental illness.
The patient underwent a lobotomy. 病人接受了脑叶切除术。
Lobotomies were a common treatment for mental illness in the mid-20th century.  脑叶切除术是20世纪中期治疗精神疾病的常见方法。
近义词: psychosurgery
反义词: therapy, medication (more general antonyms as there's no direct opposite)
词性: noun

词源：From Greek lobos 'lobe' + -tomia 'cutting'.
