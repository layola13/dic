blowpipe:/ˈbləʊpaɪp/ (UK) /ˈbloʊpaɪp/ (US)| n.|吹管; 吹箭筒|A tube through which air is blown to direct a flame onto something, used for soldering, glassblowing, etc., or a tube used for shooting darts by blowing.|The glassblower used a blowpipe to create the delicate vase. 玻璃吹制工用吹管制作了精致的花瓶。|Synonyms: blow tube, blowgun|Antonyms: N/A|noun

blowpipe:/ˈbləʊpaɪp/ (UK) /ˈbloʊpaɪp/ (US)| n.|吹箭|A dart shot from a blowpipe; the action of shooting such a dart.|He hunted monkeys with a blowpipe and poisoned darts. 他用吹箭和毒镖猎杀猴子。|Synonyms: dart, blowgun dart|Antonyms: N/A|noun
