Etymology:  "lovey-dovey" is a reduplication of "dove," referencing the bird often seen as a symbol of love and peace. The "-y" suffix adds a sense of sweetness and affection. The added "-ness" turns the adjective into a noun, referring to the state or quality of being lovey-dovey.
