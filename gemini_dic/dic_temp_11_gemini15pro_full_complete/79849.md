glyptotheca:/ˌɡlɪptəˈθiːkə/| n.|雕塑博物馆，雕刻收藏馆|A museum or gallery for the exhibition of sculpture, especially ancient sculpture.  |例句：The Glyptothek in Munich houses a remarkable collection of Greek and Roman sculptures.  慕尼黑的雕刻博物馆收藏了大量的希腊和罗马雕塑。

例句：She visited the glyptotheca to study the ancient carvings. 她参观了雕刻收藏馆，研究古代雕刻。|近义词：museum, gallery|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *glyptós* (carved, engraved) + *thēkē* (a case, repository).
