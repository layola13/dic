chlorinating:/ˈklɔːrɪneɪtɪŋ/
verb
氯化，加氯于
The process of adding chlorine to something, typically water, to purify or disinfect it.
They are chlorinating the pool to kill bacteria.  他们正在给游泳池加氯以杀死细菌。
The water company is chlorinating the water supply.  自来水公司正在对供水进行氯化处理。
disinfecting, purifying
dechlorinating
v.
