alchemistry:/ˈælkɪmi/| n.|炼金术; 炼丹术|A medieval chemical science and speculative philosophy aiming to achieve the transmutation of base metals into gold, the discovery of a universal cure for disease, and the preparation of an elixir of longevity.|例句：The study of alchemy was popular in the Middle Ages.  炼金术研究在中世纪很流行。

例句：Many alchemists tried to turn lead into gold. 许多炼金术士试图将铅变成金。|近义词：chemistry, magic, sorcery|反义词: science|词性:noun
