griffinisms:/ˈɡrɪfɪnɪzəmz/| n. |狮鹫语；怪诞的言辞；奇特的表达方式|Eccentric or unusual words or expressions, often coined or used idiosyncratically, particularly associated with the British comedian and writer, Eddie Izzard.| Eddie Izzard's stand-up routines are full of griffinisms, like referring to a toaster as a "bread-heating device." 埃迪·伊扎德的单口相声充满了怪诞的言辞，比如把烤面包机称为“面包加热装置”。| synonyms: neologisms, idiosyncrasies, quirks, peculiarities| antonyms: commonplaces, clichés, standard expressions| noun
