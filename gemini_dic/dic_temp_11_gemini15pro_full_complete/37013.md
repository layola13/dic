column:/ˈkɒləm/ (UK) /ˈkɑːləm/ (US)| n.|柱，列，专栏|A vertical structure supporting part of a building or standing alone as a monument.  A vertical arrangement of items printed or written on a page.|The ancient temple had massive marble columns.  这座古老的寺庙有大理石柱。|She writes a weekly column for the newspaper. 她每周为报纸写一个专栏。|近义词：pillar, post, support, article|反义词:row|词性:名词
