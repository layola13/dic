lepidoganoid: /ˌlɛpɪdoʊˈɡænɔɪd/
adjective
鳞状硬骨的；披覆硬鳞的
Covered with thick, bony, rhomboid scales, characteristic of certain fossil fishes and ganoid fishes.  Characterized by scales that are both bony and ganoid (i.e. shiny, enamel-like).
The lepidoganoid scales of the ancient fish shimmered in the museum light.  博物馆灯光下，这条古鱼的鳞状硬骨鳞片闪闪发光。
近义词: ganoid, bony
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider soft-scaled, cycloid, ctenoid depending on context)
词性 adjective
