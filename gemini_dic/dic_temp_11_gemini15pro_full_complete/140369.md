oilclothed:/ˈɔɪlklɔːðd/
adjective
涂油布的；覆盖着油布的
Covered or protected with oilcloth.
The table was oilclothed in red and white checks.  桌子铺着红白格子的油布。
The floor was oilclothed for protection. 地板上铺了油布以作保护。
近义词:covered, protected
反义词:uncovered, exposed
词性:adjective

词源: oil + cloth + -ed.  "Oil" 指的是油, "cloth" 指的是布, "-ed" 是一个形容词后缀，表示“被…覆盖的”。
