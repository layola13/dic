inhalant:/ɪnˈheɪlənt/ | n. | 吸入剂；可吸入之物 | A substance that is inhaled, often for its intoxicating effects. |  Some teenagers experiment with inhalants like glue or paint thinner. 一些青少年尝试吸入胶水或油漆稀释剂之类的吸入剂。

The dangers of inhalant abuse are often underestimated.  吸入剂滥用的危险性经常被低估。 | 近义词:  aerosol,  volatile substance | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "exhalant" in a physiological context but not common usage) | 词性: noun
