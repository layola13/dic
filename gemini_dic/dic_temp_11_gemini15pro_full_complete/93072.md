idolizes:/ˈaɪdəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
崇拜；把…当作偶像
regards with blind adoration
She idolizes her older brother. 她崇拜她的哥哥。
Synonyms:  worships, reveres, adores, venerates
Antonyms: despises, scorns, loathes
词性: 动词(verb)

Etymology:
From idol + -ize.  "Idol" comes from the Old French "idole," which in turn comes from the Latin "idolum," meaning "image, phantom, or idol."  The suffix "-ize" forms verbs meaning "to make or cause to be," or "to treat as."
