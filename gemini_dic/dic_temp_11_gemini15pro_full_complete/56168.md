dodecahedra:/ˌdoʊdəkəˈhiːdrə/ /ˌdɔːdəkəˈhiːdrə/| n. |十二面体|A dodecahedron is a three-dimensional geometric shape with twelve flat faces, each of which is a pentagon. |The Greeks were fascinated by the dodecahedron.  希腊人对十二面体很着迷。

The museum displayed a beautiful dodecahedra made of glass.  博物馆展出了一个漂亮的玻璃制成的十二面体。|近义词:polyhedron|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider simpler shapes like "cube" or "tetrahedron")|词性:noun
