ditzy:/ˈdɪtsi/| adj.|愚蠢的，轻佻的，傻乎乎的|Scatterbrained and silly; given to lightheadedness.|例句：She's a bit ditzy and often forgets things. 她有点傻乎乎的，经常忘事。|例句：He has a ditzy girlfriend who is always losing her keys. 他有个傻乎乎的女朋友，总是丢钥匙。|近义词：scatterbrained, flighty, silly, dizzy, foolish, airheaded|反义词：sensible, intelligent, smart, serious|词性:形容词

Etymology:  From "dizzy," referring to a state of mental confusion or lightheadedness.
