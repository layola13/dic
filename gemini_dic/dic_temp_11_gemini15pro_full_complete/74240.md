fox's:/ˈfɒksɪz/ (UK) /ˈfɑːksɪz/ (US)|n. (possessive form)|狐狸的|Indicates possession by a fox. It's the possessive form of "fox."|The fox's den was hidden under a tree root. 狐狸的窝藏在一棵树根下。

The fox's cunning allowed it to escape the hunters. 狐狸的狡猾让它逃脱了猎人的追捕。


|近义词: (No direct synonym for a possessive, but contextually: belonging to the fox)|反义词: N/A|词性: Possessive pronoun
