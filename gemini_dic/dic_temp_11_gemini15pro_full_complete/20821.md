blastemal:/blæˈstiːməl/| adj.|胚基的，芽基的|Relating to or of the nature of a blastema.  A blastema is a mass of cells capable of growth and regeneration into organs or body parts.|The blastemal cells were crucial for the regeneration of the lizard's tail.  蜥蜴尾巴的再生过程中，胚基细胞至关重要。|近义词：regenerative, embryonic|反义词：differentiated, mature|词性:形容词

blastema:/blæˈstiːmə/| n.|胚基，芽基|A mass of undifferentiated cells capable of growth and differentiation, as in the regeneration of tissues or organs.|The blastema formed at the site of the amputation. 在截肢部位形成了胚基。|近义词：germ, primordium|反义词：/|词性:名词
