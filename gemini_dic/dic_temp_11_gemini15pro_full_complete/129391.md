nomenclaturally:/ˌnoʊmənˈkleɪtʃərəli/
|adv.|
|命名法上地；术语学地|
|Relating to or according to the system of nomenclature.  It refers to something being done in a way that adheres to the rules and conventions of naming, especially in scientific fields.|
|The species was nomenclaturally challenged, having been renamed several times over the years.|该物种在命名方面遇到了挑战，多年来被多次重新命名。

|The two plants are nomenclaturally distinct, although they appear very similar.|这两种植物在命名上是不同的，尽管它们看起来很相似。
