beamer:/ˈbiːmər/| n.|投影仪；投影机|A beamer is a device that projects images, typically from a computer, onto a screen or wall. |例句：The professor used a beamer to show slides during the lecture. 教授用投影仪在讲课时展示幻灯片。

例句：The beamer wasn't working, so we had to improvise. 投影仪坏了，所以我们不得不临时想办法。|近义词：projector, data projector|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
