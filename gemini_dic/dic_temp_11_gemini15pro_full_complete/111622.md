localising:/ˈloʊkəlaɪzɪŋ/ /ˈləʊkəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
本地化；局限；定位
Adapting something to a specific locale or region, making it suitable for local conditions and culture.  Also, restricting something to a particular area.
The company is localising its software for the Chinese market.  公司正在将其软件本地化以适应中国市场。
The infection was localised to a small area. 感染被局限在一个小区域。
localize, adapting, restricting, confining
globalizing, spreading, dispersing
v.
