goset: /ˈɡoʊsɛt/ | n. | 女紧身胸衣;胸衣;紧身衣 | A close-fitting bodice or outer garment, especially one worn by women in the 16th and 17th centuries.  It extends from the neck to the waist and often has decorative features like lacing or embroidery. |  The queen wore a richly embroidered goset. 女王穿着一件绣有精美刺绣的紧身胸衣。 | 近义词: bodice, corset, stays | 反义词:  (No direct antonym.  Perhaps "loose garment") | 词性: noun
