lacinula:/ləˈsɪnjʊlə/ | n. | 小裂片; 小舌片 | A small, slender lobe or flap, especially in lichens or fungi.  A minute subdivision of a thallus, particularly in lichens and fungi. | The lacinula of the lichen was examined under a microscope.  地衣的小裂片在显微镜下被检查。| The lacinula helped the fungus absorb nutrients.  小舌片帮助真菌吸收养分。 | synonyms: lobe, flap, segment, division | antonyms: whole, complete | noun
