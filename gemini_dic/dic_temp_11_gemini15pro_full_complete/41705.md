corroborate:/kəˈrɑːbəreɪt/ | v. | 证实，确证 | to confirm or give support to (a statement, theory, or finding) | The witness corroborated the victim's account of the robbery. 目击者证实了受害人对抢劫案的描述。 |  The physical evidence corroborates his story. 物理证据证实了他的说法。 | synonyms: confirm, verify, substantiate, validate | antonyms: contradict, refute, deny | verb
