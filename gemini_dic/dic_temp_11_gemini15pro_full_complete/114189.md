Maybach: /ˈmaɪbɑːk/ (US), /ˈmaɪbæk/ (UK)| n.|迈巴赫|A German brand of luxury automobiles, originally independent and later a part of Mercedes-Benz. Maybach: /ˈmaɪbɑːk/ (US), /ˈmaɪbæk/ (UK)| n.|威廉·迈巴赫|Wilhelm Maybach (1846-1929), a German automotive designer and industrialist.
