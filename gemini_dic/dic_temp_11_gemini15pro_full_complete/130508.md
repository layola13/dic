nonaccommodable:/ˌnɒnəkɒməˈdeɪtəbl/
adjective
不可调和的；不迁就的；难相处的
Not willing to compromise or make concessions; inflexible.  Unable to be adjusted or adapted to suit different needs or circumstances.
The nonaccommodable stance of both parties made a resolution impossible.  双方的强硬立场使得解决问题成为不可能。
noncompromising, inflexible, rigid, unyielding
accommodating, flexible, adaptable, yielding
