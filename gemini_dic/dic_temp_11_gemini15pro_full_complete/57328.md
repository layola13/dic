downturn:/ˈdaʊntɜːrn/| n.|衰退，下降; 经济低迷时期|A downturn in the economy is causing job losses. 经济衰退导致失业。|近义词：decline, slump, recession|反义词:upturn, boom, recovery|词性:名词
Etymology:  From "down" + "turn".  "Down" indicating a decrease or negative direction, and "turn" signifying a change or shift.
