misusers:/ˌmɪsˈjuːzərz/
n.
滥用者，误用者
People who misuse something, using it incorrectly or for a wrong purpose.
Many online platforms struggle with misusers who spread misinformation. 许多在线平台都在与散布虚假信息的滥用者作斗争。
The misusers of the software were banned from the platform.  滥用该软件的用户被禁止使用该平台。
abusers, exploiters
proper users, responsible users
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "misuse" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Misuse" itself comes from "mis-" (wrongly) + "use."  The root "use" ultimately comes from Latin "usus."
