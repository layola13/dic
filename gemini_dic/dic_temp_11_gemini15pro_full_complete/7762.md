anoura:/əˈnɔʊrə/
noun
无尾类动物；无尾目
Tailless amphibians, a group that includes frogs and toads.
Anoura are found on every continent except Antarctica. 无尾类动物存在于除南极洲以外的每个大陆。
Anoura such as frogs play an important role in the ecosystem. 像青蛙这样的无尾类动物在生态系统中扮演着重要的角色。
Synonym: Salientia
Antonym: Urodela (tailed amphibians)
词性: noun
