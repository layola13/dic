epigraphs: /ˈɛpɪɡræfs/ | n. |题词; 碑文; 格言 | A short quotation or saying at the beginning of a book or chapter, intended to suggest its theme. | The epigraph to the novel was a line from Shakespeare. 这部小说的题词引自莎士比亚的一句话。 | synonyms: quotation, inscription, motto, adage | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "afterword," "postscript") | noun
