butterbird:/ˈbʌtərˌbɜːrd/ | n. | 黄鹂 | A North American oriole, especially the Baltimore oriole (Icterus galbula), noted for its bright orange and black plumage. | The butterbird flitted through the branches, a flash of orange against the green leaves.  黄鹂在树枝间飞舞，在绿叶的映衬下像一抹橙色的闪光。| Example:  We saw a butterbird building its nest high in the elm tree. 我们看到一只黄鹂在榆树上筑巢。| Synonym: oriole, Baltimore oriole | Antonym: N/A | Noun
