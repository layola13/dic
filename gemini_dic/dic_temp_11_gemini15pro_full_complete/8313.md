anti-pacifism:/ˌæntiˈpæsɪfɪzəm/ | n. | 反和平主义 | Opposition to pacifism; the belief that war and violence are sometimes necessary or even desirable. |  Many world leaders embraced anti-pacifism during World War II.  二战期间，许多世界领导人都拥护反和平主义。| synonyms: militarism, bellicosity, hawkishness | antonyms: pacifism, peacefulness, nonviolence | noun

anti-pacifism:/ˌæntiˈpæsɪfɪzəm/ | adj. | 反和平主义的 | Characterized by opposition to pacifism. |  He held anti-pacifist views, believing that a strong military was essential.  他持有反和平主义的观点，认为强大的军队至关重要。 | synonyms: militaristic, bellicose, hawkish | antonyms: pacifistic, peaceful, nonviolent | adjective
