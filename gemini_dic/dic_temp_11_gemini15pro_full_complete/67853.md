fab:/fæb/| adj.| 极好的，绝妙的|Shortened form of "fabulous."  Meaning excellent, wonderful, or marvelous.| That new restaurant is fab!  那家新餐馆棒极了！|Synonyms: amazing, brilliant, fantastic, superb, wonderful|Antonyms: awful, terrible, dreadful|Adjective

fab:/fæb/| n.| 制作，制造|Shortened form of "fabrication." The act of manufacturing or constructing something.| The fab of the new computer chips is complex. 新型计算机芯片的制造很复杂。|Synonyms: manufacture, production, construction|Antonyms: destruction, demolition|Noun
