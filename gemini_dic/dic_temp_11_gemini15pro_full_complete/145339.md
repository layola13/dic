overbudget:/ˌoʊvərˈbʌdʒɪt/ | adj. | 超预算的 | Exceeding the planned or allocated budget. | The project ran overbudget due to unexpected delays.  这个项目由于意外的延误而超出了预算。|近义词：overspent, exceeding the budget |反义词: under budget, within budget |词性: adjective

overbudget:/ˌoʊvərˈbʌdʒɪt/ | adv. | 超出预算地 | In a manner that exceeds the allocated budget. | The department finished the year overbudget.  该部门以超出预算的方式结束了这一年。|近义词：excessively, beyond the budget |反义词: frugally, within budget |词性: adverb
