dressing-down:/ˈdrɛsɪŋ daʊn/
noun
斥责，训斥
A severe scolding or reprimand.
例句：He received a dressing-down from his boss for being late. 他因为迟到被老板训斥了一顿。

例句：She gave her son a dressing-down for his messy room. 她因为儿子房间乱七八糟而训斥了他。

近义词：reprimand, scolding, rebuke, lecture
反义词：praise, commendation
