flinting:/ˈflɪntɪŋ/| n. | 打火石,燧石击火,燧石碎片|The act of striking or chipping flint, or the process of working with flint. It can also refer to the fragments produced by this process.|The archaeologist found evidence of flinting near the ancient hearth. 考古学家在古代壁炉附近发现了燧石击火的痕迹。|近义词: striking, chipping, knapping|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "smoothing" in the context of stonework)|n.
