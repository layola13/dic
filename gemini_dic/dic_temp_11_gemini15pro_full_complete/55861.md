diviningly:/dɪˈvaɪnɪŋli/
adv.
神圣地，超自然地，像预言家一样地
in a way that seems to involve supernatural powers or prophetic insight.
She smiled diviningly, as if she knew the secrets of the universe. 她神圣地微笑着，仿佛洞悉了宇宙的奥秘。
近义词：prophetically, mystically, supernaturally
反义词：ordinarily, normally, naturally
词性: 副词
