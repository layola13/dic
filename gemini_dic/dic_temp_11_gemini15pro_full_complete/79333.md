glebous:/ˈɡliːbəs/ | adj. | 球状的，圆形的；（尤指月亮）凸圆的 | Resembling a globe or sphere, especially the full moon.  |  The glebous moon hung low in the sky. 凸圆的月亮低垂在天空中。 | Synonyms: globular, spherical, round, rotund | Antonyms: flat, concave | adjective

glebous:/ˈɡliːbəs/ | adj. | （植物）块状的，块茎状的 |  (of a plant) having a rounded or swollen form. | The glebous roots of the plant stored nutrients.  这种植物的块状根储存养分。 | Synonyms: bulbous, tuberous, swollen | Antonyms: thin, slender | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *glebosus, from gleba (“clod, lump of earth”).
