overdenunciation:/ˌoʊvərˌdiːnʌnsiˈeɪʃən/
n.
过度清晰的发音；过分强调发音
The act of pronouncing words too precisely or emphatically, often to the point of artificiality.
Her overdenunciation of every syllable made her sound like a robot. 她对每个音节都过度清晰的发音，让她听起来像个机器人。
hyperarticulation, overpronunciation
underarticulation, mumbling
noun
