gusting: /ˈɡʌstɪŋ/ | adj. | 阵风的；刮大风的 | Characterized by sudden, strong bursts of wind. | The gusting wind made it difficult to walk. 阵风使行走困难。|  The flags were flapping wildly in the gusting wind. 旗帜在阵阵狂风中剧烈飘动。 | 近义词: blustery, windy, squally | 反义词: calm, still | 词性: 形容词

词源: From gust + -ing.  "Gust" comes from Old Norse *gustr, meaning "blast of wind."
