casked:/kɑːskt/ | adj. | 装入桶中的，桶装的 | Stored or aged in a cask (a large barrel-like container, especially one for holding alcoholic drinks). | The distillery produces a range of casked single malt whiskies. 这家酿酒厂生产一系列桶装单一麦芽威士忌。| 近义词: barreled | 反义词: bottled | 词性:形容词

casked:/kɑːskt/ | v. | ( cask 的过去式和过去分词 ) 装入桶中 | To put or store in a cask. | The rum was casked and left to age for ten years. 朗姆酒被装入桶中陈酿十年。 | 近义词: barreled | 反义词: bottled | 词性:动词
