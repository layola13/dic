kettin:/ˈkɛtɪn/ | n. | 毛毡,粗呢,毛毯 | A coarse kind of twilled woolen cloth. |  He wore a thick kettin coat to protect him from the cold. 他穿着一件厚厚的粗呢大衣御寒。|  The kettin blanket kept them warm through the night. 毛毯让他们整晚都很暖和。 | 近义词: felt, tweed, blanket | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps silk or linen as contrasting materials) | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *ketten, likely of Scandinavian origin, related to Old Norse *köttr ("mat, rug, cover").
