carbonize:/ˈkɑːrbənaɪz/ | v. | 碳化，炭化 | To convert or be converted into carbon or a carbon-like substance, typically by heating or burning. |  The intense heat carbonized the wood, leaving only charcoal.  高温将木头碳化，只剩下木炭。| synonyms: char, scorch, burn | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the process of carbonization) | verb

carbonize:/ˈkɑːrbənaɪz/ | adj. | 碳化的 | Converted into carbon or a carbon-like substance. | The carbonized remains of the tree were brittle and black.  这棵树碳化的残骸又脆又黑。| synonyms: charred, scorched, burnt | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the state of being carbonized) | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *carboniser*, from carbone (“carbon”) + -iser (“-ize”).
