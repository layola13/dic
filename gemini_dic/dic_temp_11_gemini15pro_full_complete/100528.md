intervisitations:/ˌɪntərˌvɪzɪˈteɪʃənz/
noun
互相访问；互访
The program encourages intervisitations between schools to share best practices.  这个项目鼓励学校之间互相访问，以分享最佳实践。
Intervisitations among different departments fostered collaboration and knowledge sharing. 不同部门之间的互访促进了合作和知识共享。
Synonyms: exchanges, visits, reciprocal visits
Antonyms: isolation, seclusion
