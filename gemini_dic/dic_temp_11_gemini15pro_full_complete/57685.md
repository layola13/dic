drawliest:/ˈdrɔːliɪst/
adjective
最慢的；最拖沓的
slowest; most drawling
His drawliest Southern accent came out when he was tired.  他疲倦的时候，他那最慢吞吞的南方口音就出来了。
近义词: slowest, most sluggish, most protracted
反义词: fastest, quickest, most rapid
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From drawl + -iest (superlative suffix).  Drawl comes from  probably imitative.
