leviathan:/ləˈvaɪəθən/| n.|巨兽；庞然大物；强大的机构或组织|A sea monster mentioned in the Bible; something huge and powerful.|例句：The company is a leviathan in the tech industry. 这家公司是科技行业的巨头。|例句：The government is a leviathan that controls many aspects of our lives. 政府是一个控制我们生活诸多方面的庞然大物。|近义词：monster, behemoth, giant|反义词:dwarf, pygmy|词性:noun
词源:From Late Latin *Leviathan*, from Hebrew *liwyāṯān* "a large sea animal," perhaps from a root meaning "to twist, coil."
