muffetee: /ˌmʌfəˈtiː/
n.
暖手筒; 連指手套
A small tube-shaped covering, open at both ends, worn over the wrists and forearms for warmth.
She wore a muffetee to keep her hands warm in the cold weather.  她戴着暖手筒在寒冷的天气里保暖。
He slipped his hands into the soft wool muffetees. 他把手伸进柔软的羊毛暖手筒里。
Synonyms: wristlet, hand warmer
Antonyms: glove, mitten
