brown-bagging:/ˈbrɑʊnˌbæɡɪŋ/ | n. |自带午餐；自带酒水|The practice of taking one's own lunch, typically in a brown paper bag, to work or school. |例句：Brown-bagging is a good way to save money. 自带午餐是省钱的好方法。|例句：Many employees are brown-bagging it these days due to the rising cost of eating out. 由于外出就餐成本上升，现在很多员工都自带午餐。| 近义词：packing a lunch, bringing your own lunch | 反义词: eating out, dining in |词性:noun


brown-bag:/ˈbraʊn ˌbæɡ/ | n. |牛皮纸袋|A bag made of brown paper, typically used for carrying lunch. |例句：He packed his sandwich in a brown bag. 他把三明治装在一个牛皮纸袋里。|例句：She brought her lunch in a brown bag. 她自带午餐，用一个牛皮纸袋装着。|近义词：paper bag | 反义词: (no direct antonym) |词性:noun


brown-bag:/ˈbraʊn ˌbæɡ/ | v. |自带（午餐或酒水）|To bring one's own lunch or alcoholic beverages, typically in a brown paper bag, to work, school, or an event. |例句：I'm brown-bagging it today to save some money. 我今天自带午餐是为了省钱。|例句：They brown-bagged their own beer to the concert. 他们自带啤酒去听音乐会。|近义词：pack a lunch, BYOB (Bring Your Own Beverage), BYOL (Bring Your Own Lunch) | 反义词: (no direct antonym) |词性:verb
