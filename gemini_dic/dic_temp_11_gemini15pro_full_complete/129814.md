non-Greeks:/ˌnɒnˈɡriːks/ | n. | 非希腊人 | People who are not of Greek descent or nationality.  Those who do not belong to the Greek culture or civilization. |  Many non-Greeks settled in ancient Rome. 许多非希腊人定居在古罗马。  The non-Greeks were often referred to as barbarians by the ancient Greeks. 古希腊人通常将非希腊人称为野蛮人。 | synonyms: foreigners, aliens, non-Hellenes | antonyms: Greeks, Hellenes | noun
