expelling:/ɪkˈspɛlɪŋ/
verb
驱逐；排出；开除
force something or someone to leave a place.
The school is expelling him for persistent truancy. 学校因为他持续旷课要开除他。
synonyms: ejecting, ousting, removing, dismissing, discharging
antonyms: admitting, accepting, welcoming
