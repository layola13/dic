cornerstone:/ˈkɔːrnərˌstoʊn/ (US) /ˈkɔːnəstəʊn/ (UK)| n.|基石，奠基石；基础，根本|An important quality or feature on which a particular thing depends or is based. |例句：Trust is the cornerstone of any good relationship. 信任是任何良好关系的基石。

例句：A strong education is the cornerstone of a successful future.  良好的教育是未来成功的基石。|近义词：foundation, basis, bedrock, underpinning|反义词：apex, peak|词性:名词
