gansa:/ˈɡɑːnsə/ | n. | 灰雁；鹅（尤指非洲灰雁）| A large waterfowl, especially the greylag goose (Anser anser), which is the ancestor of many domestic breeds.  It can also refer to other species of geese, particularly in Africa. |  例句：The gansa flew overhead in a V-formation. 灰雁以V字队形飞过头顶。

例句：In some parts of Africa, the gansa is considered a sacred bird. 在非洲的一些地区，鹅被认为是神鸟。 | 近义词：goose, wild goose | 反义词: N/A | 词性：noun
