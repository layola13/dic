mensurable:/ˈmenʃərəbl/| adj.|可测量的，可计量的|Able to be measured. |例句：The results of the experiment are mensurable.  实验的结果是可测量的。|例句：Is happiness a mensurable quality? 幸福是一种可测量的品质吗？|近义词：measurable, quantifiable|反义词:immeasurable, unquantifiable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *mensurabilis, from Latin mensurare "to measure," from mensura "measure."
