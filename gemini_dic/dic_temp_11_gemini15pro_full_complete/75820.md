fumify:/ˈfjuːmɪfaɪ/| v. |熏蒸，烟熏|To expose to smoke or fumes, especially for disinfection or to destroy pests.|The greenhouse was fumified to eliminate insects. 温室被熏蒸以消灭昆虫。|fumigate, disinfect|aerate|verb
Etymology-> From Latin *fūmigō*  (from *fūmus* "smoke"), equivalent to *fūm(us)* + *-igō* (suffix forming verbs). The English word is a back-formation from *fumigation*.
