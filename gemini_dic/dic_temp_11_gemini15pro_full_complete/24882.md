brucellosis:/ˌbruːsəˈloʊsɪs/ | n. | 布鲁氏菌病 | An infectious bacterial disease that affects humans and some animals, causing fever, sweating, and weakness.  It is typically contracted through contact with infected animals or contaminated animal products. |  Brucellosis can be a debilitating disease if left untreated. 布鲁氏菌病如果不及时治疗会使人衰弱。

Brucellosis is a significant health concern in many developing countries. 布鲁氏菌病在许多发展中国家是一个严重的健康问题。 | 近义词: undulant fever, Malta fever | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以用health健康 来表示相对的概念) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Sir David Bruce (1855-1931), British bacteriologist who isolated the causative bacterium + -osis.
