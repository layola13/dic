mansion:/ˈmænʃən/| n. |豪宅，大厦|A large and impressive house. |例句：The movie star lived in a luxurious mansion overlooking the ocean.  这位电影明星住在一座可以俯瞰大海的豪华别墅里。

例句：The old mansion had been in the family for generations. 这座古老的豪宅世代相传。

近义词：estate, villa, palace, manor|反义词:hut, shack, cabin|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *mansion (modern French maison) "dwelling, house, home," from Latin mansiō, mansiōnem "a staying, dwelling, abode," from mansus, past participle of manēre "to stay, remain."
