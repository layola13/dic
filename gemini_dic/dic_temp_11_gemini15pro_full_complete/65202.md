ethylidene:/eθˈɪlɪdiːn/ | n. |亚乙基|A bivalent radical, CH3CH=, derived from ethane by removal of two hydrogen atoms from the same carbon atom.  It forms the basis for a series of organic compounds. |The ethylidene group is found in certain types of polymers. 亚乙基基团存在于某些类型的聚合物中。| Ethylidene chloride is a colorless liquid. 亚乙基氯是一种无色液体。| vinyl, vinylene| N/A | noun
