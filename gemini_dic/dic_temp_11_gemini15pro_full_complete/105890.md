kittening:/ˈkɪtənɪŋ/
n.
产仔; (猫)生小猫
The act of a cat giving birth to kittens.
The queen is kittening in the spare bedroom.  女王正在空房间里生小猫。
近义词：birthing, giving birth
反义词：None (No direct opposite for the birthing process of cats)
词性:名词


kittening:/ˈkɪtənɪŋ/
v.
(猫)生小猫
The process of a cat giving birth to kittens.
Our cat is kittening tonight. 我们的猫今晚要生小猫了。
近义词：giving birth, having kittens
反义词：None (No direct opposite for the birthing process of cats)
词性:动词


词源: From kitten + -ing, derived from the Middle English word *kitoun*, meaning "kitten."
