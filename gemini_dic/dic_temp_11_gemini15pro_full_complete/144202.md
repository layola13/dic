outsighs: /ˈaʊtsaɪz/
noun
叹息，叹气
A long, deep audible breath expressing sadness, relief, tiredness, or similar.
She let out a long, weary outsigh. 她发出一声长长的、疲惫的叹息。
synonyms: sigh, groan, moan
antonyms:  cheer, laughter
n.
