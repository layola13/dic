frizz:/frɪz/| n. v.|卷曲，毛躁；使卷曲，使毛躁|A state of being tightly curled, often in small, unruly coils, especially of hair.  (Noun) To form tight curls, often in small, unruly coils, especially of hair. (Verb)|Her hair had a tendency to frizz in humid weather. 她的头发在潮湿的天气里容易毛躁。|近义词：curl, kink, fuzz, crinkle|反义词：straighten, smooth|词性:名词，动词

frizzy:/ˈfrɪzi/| adj.|卷曲的，毛躁的|Characterized by tight curls, often in small, unruly coils, especially of hair.|She had naturally frizzy hair. 她天生头发卷曲。|近义词：curly, kinky, fuzzy, crinkled|反义词：straight, smooth|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French friser "to curl," from Frankish *frisan, from Proto-Germanic *frisōną "to curl, twist," from PIE *bʰers- "to bristle."
