antechamber:/ˈæntiˌtʃeɪmbər/ (US), /ˈæntiˌtʃeɪmbə(r)/ (UK)
n.
前厅，接待室
A smaller room leading to a main room; a waiting room.
The guests waited in the antechamber before being ushered into the main hall. 客人们在大厅前的前厅等候，然后被领进大厅。
synonyms: waiting room, lobby, foyer, vestibule
antonyms: main hall, main room, principal chamber
noun
