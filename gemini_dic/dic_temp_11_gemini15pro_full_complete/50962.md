designless:/dɪˈzaɪnləs/
adjective
无设计的；缺乏设计的；朴素的
Lacking design or ornament; plain.
The room was designless and functional, furnished with only the bare necessities.  这个房间没有设计，只有实用功能，只配备了最基本的必需品。
The building's designless facade was uninspiring. 这座建筑毫无设计的正面毫无吸引力。

近义词:plain, simple, unadorned, undecorated
反义词:designed, ornate, elaborate, decorated
词性:adjective
