oversmokes:/ˌoʊvərˈsmoʊks/
verb
过度吸烟；烟熏过度
To smoke excessively or to expose something to too much smoke.
He oversmoked the salmon and it tasted bitter. 他把鲑鱼烟熏过度了，味道很苦。
