betacismus:/ˌbeɪtəˈsɪzməs/ /ˌbiːtəˈsɪzməs/
n.
betacism;  mispronunciation of the letter 'b'
A speech impediment involving the excessive use or misuse of the sound /b/.  It can manifest as substituting /b/ for other sounds, or difficulty pronouncing /b/ correctly.
He had a slight betacismus, sometimes pronouncing 'v' as 'b'. 他有点口齿不清，有时会把“v”发成“b”。
近义词: lisp, speech impediment
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "clear speech" or "perfect articulation")
词性: noun
