calculary: /ˈkælkjʊləri/
noun
计算器; 计算者; 算法书
a person or thing that calculates.  A book of mathematical tables.  A counting device, especially a mechanical or electronic one.
The ancient astronomer used a calculary to predict the movements of the planets. 古代天文学家用计算器来预测行星的运动。
The shopkeeper used a calculary to add up the customer's purchases.  店主用计算器把顾客的购物款加起来。
synonyms: calculator, computer, reckoner
antonyms:  None (The concept doesn't have a direct opposite)
