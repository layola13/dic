opposer:/əˈpəʊzə(r)/| n.|反对者，对手|Someone who opposes something or someone. |例句：He was a staunch opposer of the new policy. 他是新政策的坚决反对者。

例句：She had many opposers in the company. 她在公司里有很多反对者。

近义词：opponent, adversary, antagonist, foe, rival, enemy|反义词:supporter, proponent, advocate|词性:noun
