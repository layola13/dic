non-Saxons:/nɒnˈsæksənz/ |n.|非撒克逊人|People who are not Saxons, referring to those who were not members of the Germanic tribes that settled in parts of Britain in the early Middle Ages. |The non-Saxons in Britain included the native Britons, as well as later arrivals such as the Vikings.| 近义词:non-Anglo-Saxons, Celts (sometimes)|反义词:Saxons, Anglo-Saxons|词性:名词
