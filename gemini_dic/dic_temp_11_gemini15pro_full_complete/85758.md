ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hawsere, *hauwsere, from Old North French haucier, haussier (“halser, hawser”), from Frankish *hausir, from Proto-Germanic *hauzijaną (“to pull, haul”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)keudh- (“to set in motion, chase, push, run”). Cognate with Dutch houwer (“cable”), German Hausen (“to hoist, pull”), Danish haase (“to hoist”).
