intersubstitution:/ˌɪntərˌsʌbstɪˈtjuːʃən/ | n. |相互取代，互相替换|The intersubstitution of different elements in a crystal structure can affect its properties.  晶体结构中不同元素的相互取代会影响其性质。|例句：The intersubstitution of aluminum and silicon in the mineral is common.  这种矿物中铝和硅的相互取代很常见。

intersubstitution:/ˌɪntərˌsʌbstɪˈtjuːʃən/ | n. |相互取代，互相替换|Intersubstitution of players is a key strategy in team sports.  队员之间的相互替换是团队运动中的关键策略。|例句：The intersubstitution of roles within the team allowed for greater flexibility.  团队内部角色的相互替换实现了更大的灵活性。|近义词：replacement, exchange, substitution |反义词: fixation, permanence |词性: noun
