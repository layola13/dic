explosive:/ɪkˈsploʊsɪv/ /ɪkˈspləʊsɪv/| adj. n.|爆炸的；爆炸物；炸药|Relating to or involving an explosion; a substance that causes an explosion.|The explosive device was detonated remotely.  遥控引爆了爆炸装置。|近义词：volatile, flammable, eruptive|反义词：stable, inert|词性:形容词, 名词

explosive:/ɪkˈsploʊsɪv/ /ɪkˈspləʊsɪv/| n.|爆炸物；炸药|A substance that causes an explosion.|The miners used explosives to blast through the rock. 矿工用炸药炸穿岩石。|近义词：dynamite, bomb, TNT|反义词：dampener, suppressant|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *explosīvus, from Latin explōdere (to explode) + -īvus (forming adjectives).  The Latin explōdere is from ex- (out) + plaudere (to clap, applaud).
