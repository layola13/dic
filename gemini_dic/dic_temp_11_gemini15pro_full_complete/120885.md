misbuild:/ˌmɪsˈbɪld/
|n. |错误构建，构建错误|A misbuild is an instance of something being built incorrectly, resulting in a flawed or defective product.|The software misbuild caused the program to crash.  这个软件构建错误导致程序崩溃。|Near synonyms: defect, flaw, fault, error, bug|Antonyms:  correct build, proper construction|Noun
misbuild:/ˌmɪsˈbɪld/
|v. |错误地构建，构建错误|To construct something incorrectly, resulting in a flawed or defective product.|They misbuilt the bridge, and it collapsed. 他们错误地建造了桥梁，导致它倒塌了。|Near synonyms:  misconstruct, bungle, botch|Antonyms: construct correctly, build properly|Verb
