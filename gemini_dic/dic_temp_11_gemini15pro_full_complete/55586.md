disuniform:/ˌdɪsˈjuːnɪfɔːrm/
adj.
不统一的，不一致的
Not uniform; lacking uniformity; exhibiting variation or diversity.
The quality of the products was disuniform, ranging from excellent to substandard.  这些产品的质量参差不齐，从优秀到不合格都有。
The disuniform distribution of resources created economic disparity. 资源分配不均导致了经济差距。
近义词: diverse, varied, heterogeneous, inconsistent, irregular
反义词: uniform, consistent, homogeneous, regular
词性: 形容词
