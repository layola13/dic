interlabelling:/ˌɪntərˈleɪbəlɪŋ/ | n. |相互标记，互相标记 |The process of assigning labels to multiple items or entities in a way that reflects their relationships or connections.  This often involves cross-referencing or linking the labels.  |例句：Interlabelling of the different components allowed for easy tracking of the assembly process. 不同组件的相互标记使得组装过程的跟踪变得容易。

例句：The interlabelling of the data sets helped reveal correlations between the two studies. 数据集的相互标记有助于揭示两项研究之间的相关性。|近义词：cross-referencing, linking, connecting, associating |反义词: isolating, separating |词性:noun
