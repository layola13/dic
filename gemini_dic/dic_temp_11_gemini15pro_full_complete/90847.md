hung-over: /ˌhʌŋˈoʊvər/ (US) /ˌhʌŋˈəʊvə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|宿醉的，感到不适的|Feeling unwell as a result of excessive drinking of alcohol on the previous day.|例句：I woke up feeling hung-over after last night's party. 昨晚聚会后，我醒来感到宿醉。|近义词：ill, unwell, seedy, rough|反义词:sober, fresh|adjective

hung-over: /ˌhʌŋˈoʊvər/ (US) /ˌhʌŋˈəʊvə(r)/ (UK)| n.|宿醉|The unpleasant physical effects following excessive consumption of alcohol.|例句：I have a terrible hung-over. 我宿醉得很厉害。|近义词：hangover (more common as a noun)|反义词: sobriety|noun
