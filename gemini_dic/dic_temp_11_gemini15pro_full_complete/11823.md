arrear:/əˈrɪr/ | n. | 拖欠，欠款；待办事项 | money that is owed and should have been paid earlier.  A state of being behind in the discharge of obligations, as for payments. |  The company is in arrears with its rent.  这家公司拖欠房租。 | synonyms: debt, default, overdue, unpaid balance | antonyms: prepayment, advance, credit | noun

arrear:/əˈrɪr/ | adj. | 拖欠的 |  being in arrears; overdue | His payments are two months in arrear. 他的付款拖欠了两个月。 | synonyms: overdue, outstanding, unpaid, delinquent | antonyms: current, paid-up, up-to-date | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French arere ("behind, backwards"), from Latin ad ("to") + retro ("backwards")
