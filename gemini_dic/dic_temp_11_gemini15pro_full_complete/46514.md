daisies:/ˈdeɪziz/| n. |雏菊|A small European plant with white, pink, or yellow flowers, or the flower itself.  It is a common wildflower and is also cultivated in gardens.  From Old English dægeseage "day's eye," because its petals open at dawn and close at dusk.|She picked a bunch of daisies from the field. 她从田野里摘了一束雏菊。|近义词：flower, blossom|反义词:weed|词性:noun
