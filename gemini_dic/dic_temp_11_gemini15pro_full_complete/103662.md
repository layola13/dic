jollification: /ˌdʒɒlɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ (US) /ˌdʒɒlɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ (UK)
n.
欢庆；庆祝活动
the act of making merry; festivity.
例句：The jollification after the team's victory lasted all night.  球队获胜后的庆祝活动持续了整晚。

例句：The annual village fete is a great jollification.  一年一度的乡村游乐会是一个盛大的庆祝活动。

近义词：celebration, festivity, merrymaking, revelry
反义词：mourning, grief, sorrow
词性：名词
