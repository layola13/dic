cartwheel:/ˈkɑːrtwiːl/| n. vi.|车轮; 侧手翻;|A sideways handspring, in which the body rotates sideways with the legs and arms extended.  A gymnastic movement in which the body is supported by the hands in succession, the legs passing over in a circle or rotating motion. |She did a perfect cartwheel on the grass. 她在草地上做了一个完美的侧手翻。|Synonyms: handspring, roundoff|Antonyms: N/A|noun
