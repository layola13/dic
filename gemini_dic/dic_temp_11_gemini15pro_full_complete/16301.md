baraca:/bəˈrɑːkə/ | n. | 祝福，赐福；棚屋，简陋的住所 | A blessing; a hut, a simple dwelling.  From Hebrew ברכה (bərakhah). | The missionary built a baraca to live in while he worked with the local people.  传教士在与当地人一起工作时建造了一个棚屋居住。| 近义词：blessing, benediction, hut, shack | 反义词：curse, malediction, mansion | 词性: 名词
