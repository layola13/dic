impact:/ˈɪmpækt/ (US) /ˈɪmpækt/ (UK)| n. vt.|影响，冲击，撞击|A strong effect or influence.|The meteorite's impact created a huge crater. 陨石的撞击形成了一个巨大的陨石坑。|近义词：effect, influence, collision|反义词：insignificance, unimportance|noun
impact:/ˈɪmpækt/ (US) /ˈɪmpækt/ (UK)| vt.|影响，冲击|To have a strong effect on someone or something.|The new policy will impact small businesses. 新政策将影响小型企业。|近义词：affect, influence|反义词：ignore, neglect |verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *impactus*, past participle of *impingere* "to push, drive, or strike against", from *in-* "into, in, on, upon" + *pangere* "to fix, fasten, insert."
