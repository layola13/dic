circumstancing:/ˈsɜːrkəmˌstænsɪŋ/ /ˈsɝːkəmˌstænsɪŋ/
|n.|用环境或细节修饰；环境描写；（对情况的）详细描述|The act of describing or explaining the circumstances surrounding something.  The detailed description or reporting of the facts of a case, situation, or event.|例句：The circumstancing of the event helped the jury understand the defendant's actions.  对事件环境的描述帮助陪审团理解了被告的行为。

例句：The author's skillful circumstancing brought the story to life. 作者娴熟的环境描写使故事栩栩如生。
|近义词：contextualizing, detailing, elaborating, explaining, describing|反义词：summarizing, generalizing, simplifying|词性:名词
