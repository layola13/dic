gemmy:/ˈdʒɛmi/| adj.|宝石般的，闪闪发光的，晶莹剔透的|Resembling a gem, especially in brilliance or color.  Having the nature of a gem; bright, sparkling.|The gemmy facets of the diamond caught the light. 钻石闪闪发光的刻面捕捉到了光线。|近义词：sparkling, glittering, brilliant, lustrous|反义词:dull, opaque|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From gem + -y.  "gem" comes from Old French "geme" and ultimately from Latin "gemma" meaning "bud, precious stone." The suffix "-y" forms adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "full of."
