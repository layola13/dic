boer:/ˈbuːr/ (US) /bɔː(r)/ (UK)| n. |农夫，农民（尤指南非的荷兰裔农民）|A South African of Dutch or Huguenot descent, especially a farmer.|例句：The boer worked hard on his land. 这位布尔人辛勤地在他的土地上劳作。

例句：Many boers migrated north to escape British rule. 许多布尔人向北迁移以逃避英国统治。


近义词：farmer, peasant, settler|反义词:city dweller|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Afrikaans *boer*, from Middle Dutch *buur*, from Old Dutch *būr*, meaning "neighbor," "dweller," "peasant," related to Old English *būr* "dwelling, chamber," and German *Bauer* "farmer." The meaning shifted from "peasant" to specifically refer to the Dutch-descended farmers in South Africa.
