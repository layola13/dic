gumption:/ˈɡʌmpʃən/| n.|进取心，勇气，魄力|Initiative and resourcefulness; courage and guts.|She had the gumption to start her own business. 她有魄力自己创业。|近义词:initiative, resourcefulness, drive, spirit, pluck, spunk, mettle|反义词:apathy, indifference, lethargy|Noun


gumption:/ˈɡʌmpʃən/| n.|精明，机智|Shrewdness; common sense.|He didn't have the gumption to realize he was being cheated. 他不够精明，没有意识到自己被骗了。|近义词:astuteness, shrewdness, wit, savvy|反义词:naiveté, foolishness|Noun
