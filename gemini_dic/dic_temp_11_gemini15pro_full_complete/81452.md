gratingly:/ˈɡreɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 刺耳地；令人不快地；恼人地 |  Derived from "grate" (v.) - to make a harsh, rasping sound, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *kratanon. The "-ly" suffix transforms the verb into an adverb. | The rusty hinges grated gratingly as she opened the door. 生锈的铰链在她打开门时发出刺耳的摩擦声。|

gratingly:/ˈɡreɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 刺耳地；令人不快地；恼人地 | 近义词: harshly, raspingly, stridently, jarringly | 反义词: smoothly, pleasantly, melodiously | He gratingly announced his refusal to cooperate. 他粗声粗气地宣布拒绝合作。|
