nonartistic:/ˌnɒnɑːrˈtɪstɪk/
adjective
非艺术的；缺乏艺术性的
Not artistic; lacking artistic qualities.
She has a very nonartistic approach to problem-solving, preferring logic and data over creativity. 她解决问题的方法很不艺术，喜欢逻辑和数据而不是创造力。
His nonartistic nature made him uncomfortable in the gallery surrounded by abstract paintings.  他缺乏艺术气质，身处画廊，周围都是抽象画，让他很不自在。
synonyms: unartistic, inartistic, uncreative
antonyms: artistic, creative, imaginative
词性: 形容词
