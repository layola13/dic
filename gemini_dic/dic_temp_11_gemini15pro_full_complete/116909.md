mayonnaise:/ˌmeɪəˈneɪz/ | n. |蛋黄酱；美乃滋 |A thick, creamy dressing made from oil, egg yolks, and vinegar or lemon juice. |例句：I always put mayonnaise on my sandwiches. 我总是在我的三明治上放蛋黄酱。

例句：She made a delicious potato salad with homemade mayonnaise. 她用自制的蛋黄酱做了一份美味的土豆沙拉。

近义词：aioli |反义词: (没有直接的反义词) |词性:noun
