hassidism:/ˈhæsɪdɪzəm/ /ˈhɑːsɪdɪzəm/
n.|哈西德主义|A Jewish religious movement characterized by its emphasis on mystical experience, joy, and the leadership of charismatic rabbis.|Example: Hassidism originated in Eastern Europe in the 18th century. 哈西德主义起源于18世纪的东欧。

Example:  Many Hassidic communities maintain a distinct way of life. 许多哈西德社区保持着独特的生活方式。
|近义词:Hasidic Judaism|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially opposing religious or philosophical movements depending on the context) |词性:noun
