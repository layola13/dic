carrageen: /ˈkærəɡiːn/ | n. | 角叉菜；角叉菜胶 | A type of red seaweed (Chondrus crispus) that grows along the coasts of the North Atlantic, from which carrageenan is extracted. | Carrageen is commonly used in traditional Irish desserts. 角叉菜通常用于传统的爱尔兰甜点。| synonym: Irish moss, carragheen | antonym: N/A | noun

carrageenan: /ˌkærəˈɡiːnən/ | n. | 角叉菜胶 | A water-soluble gum obtained from red seaweeds, used as a thickening and stabilizing agent in food products and other applications. | Carrageenan is added to ice cream to improve its texture. 角叉菜胶被添加到冰淇淋中以改善其质地。| synonym: Irish moss gelose | antonym: N/A | noun
