miseries:/ˈmɪzəriz/| n. |痛苦，苦难，不幸|A state of suffering and distress.|She described her childhood as a time of great miseries. 她形容她的童年是一段充满苦难的时期。|例句：The war brought untold miseries to the people. 战争给人民带来了无尽的苦难。

例句：He told me all his miseries. 他向我倾诉了他所有的不幸。|近义词：afflictions, hardships, misfortunes, troubles, woes|反义词：joys, comforts, blessings|词性:名词(plural)

词源: From Middle English *miseries*, from Old French *miseries*, plural of *miserie*, from Latin *miseria* ("wretchedness, misery, poverty, misfortune"), from *miser* ("wretched, unfortunate").
