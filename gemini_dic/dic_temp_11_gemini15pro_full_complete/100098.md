interposit:/ˌɪntərˈpɒzɪt/
verb
插入，介入，置于…之间
to place between; to insert between other things
The government interposited itself between the warring factions. 政府介入交战派别之间。
intervene, intercede, mediate
-
verb
词源：From Latin interpositus, past participle of interponere (“to interpose”).
