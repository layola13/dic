formalizes:/ˈfɔːməlaɪzɪz/
verb
使正式化；正式确定；使成定式
makes something official or sets it out in a definite form
The agreement formalizes the relationship between the two companies. 该协议正式确定了两家公司之间的关系。
formalize:/ˈfɔːməlaɪz/
verb
使正式化；正式确定；使成定式
gives something a definite structure or shape, often making it more rigid or standardized.  It can refer to making something official or giving it a formal expression.
The new regulations formalized the procedures for applying for a visa. 新规定正式确定了申请签证的程序。
synonyms: codify, systematize, regularize, standardize, institutionalize
antonyms: informalize, loosen, relax
词性:v.
