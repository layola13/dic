herring:/ˈherɪŋ/| n.|鲱鱼；鲱鱼肉|A slender, silvery food fish found in large schools in the North Atlantic and North Pacific.  It is commercially important as a food source.|例句：The fishermen brought in a large haul of herring. 渔民们捕获了大量的鲱鱼。

例句：Herring is often smoked or pickled. 鲱鱼通常是熏制或腌制的。|近义词：fish, sardine, sprat|反义词: (无直接反义词)|noun
