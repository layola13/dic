lichgate: /ˈlɪtʃɡeɪt/ | n. | 停灵门，灵棚 | A roofed gateway to a churchyard, traditionally where the coffin rested before burial. |  The coffin was placed under the lichgate while the vicar prepared for the service. 灵柩被放置在停灵门下，牧师则在准备仪式。

The mourners gathered under the lichgate before entering the church. 哀悼者在进入教堂前聚集在停灵门下。

| 近义词: lychgate | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
