bribe:/braɪb/| n. vt.|贿赂；贿赂物|Something offered or given to someone in a position of trust to persuade them to act dishonestly.|He was accused of accepting bribes from a construction company. 他被指控接受一家建筑公司的贿赂。|The official was offered a bribe to ignore the safety violations.  这位官员被贿赂以忽略安全违规行为。|近义词：inducement, payoff, kickback|反义词：reward (for meritorious service)|n. 名词, vt. 动词
