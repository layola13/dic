nutters:/ˈnʌtəz/
n.
疯子；怪人；狂热爱好者
(British informal) A crazy or eccentric person; someone who is excessively enthusiastic about something.
Those nutters are at it again, protesting outside the town hall. 那些疯子又在市政厅外抗议了。
Synonyms: lunatics, madmen, crackpots, fanatics, enthusiasts
Antonyms: sane people, rational individuals
