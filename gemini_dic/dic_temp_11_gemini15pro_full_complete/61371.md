embitterer:/ɪmˈbɪtərər/
|n.|使人痛苦的人或事物|Something or someone that causes bitterness or resentment.|The constant criticism from her boss was the embitterer of her work life.|近义词: spoiler, bane, irritant|反义词: comforter, soother|Noun

embitter:/ɪmˈbɪtər/
|vt.|使痛苦；使怨恨|To cause someone to feel bitter or resentful.|The loss of his job embittered him towards his former employer.|近义词: sour, poison, alienate|反义词: sweeten, cheer, reconcile|Verb
