chastity-belts:/ˈtʃæstɪti bɛlts/| n.|贞操带|A locked garment or device designed to prevent sexual intercourse.|In medieval times, some husbands allegedly used chastity belts on their wives while they were away. 在中世纪，据说有些丈夫会在外出时给妻子戴上贞操带。|Synonyms: chastity device,  virginity belt|Antonyms: None (The concept itself opposes freedom, so a direct antonym is difficult.)|Noun
