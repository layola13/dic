gretto:/ˈɡrɛtoʊ/ | adj. | 粗糙的，沙哑的，刺耳的 |  Rough or harsh-sounding; grating.  Relating to or denoting a musical style characterized by the deliberate distortion of musical intervals and the use of sliding notes.  |  The singer's voice was gretto after the concert.  演唱会后，歌手的声音变得沙哑了。| Synonyms: raspy, hoarse, gravelly, grating, harsh | Antonyms: smooth, mellow, clear, melodious | adjective


gretto:/ˈɡrɛtoʊ/ | n. | （音乐）滑音，滑奏 | A sliding or portamento note in music, especially in blues or jazz. | The guitarist added a gretto to the blues riff. 吉他手在布鲁斯重复段中加入了滑音。 | Synonyms: slide, glissando, portamento | Antonyms: staccato | noun
