inveigher:/ɪnˈveɪər/
n.|抨击者；责骂者|A person who inveighs; one who uses harsh or abusive language.
|The inveigher continued his rant against the government's policies.|这位抨击者继续怒斥政府的政策。
|近义词：critic, denouncer, attacker, assailant|反义词：supporter, defender, advocate|词性:名词
