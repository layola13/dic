overapplauded:/ˌoʊvərəˈplɔːdɪd/
adjective
过度鼓掌的；被过分赞赏的
Having received excessive applause.  Excessively applauded.
The overapplauded performance felt somewhat undeserved. 这场被过度鼓掌的表演感觉有些不值得。
The play, though overapplauded, wasn't particularly memorable.  这部戏虽然被过度鼓掌，但并不是特别令人难忘。
Synonyms: overpraised, over-rated
Antonyms: underrated, underappreciated
词性: adjective
