duumvir: /djuːˈʌmvər/ | n. | 双执政官，二人执政 | One of two officials jointly holding the same public office in ancient Rome. |  Two duumvirs were appointed to oversee the construction of the temple. 两位双执政官被任命监督寺庙的建造。|  The duumvirs held considerable power in their local jurisdiction.  两位双执政官在其地方管辖区拥有相当大的权力。| Synonyms: co-ruler, magistrate | Antonyms: subordinate, underling | noun
