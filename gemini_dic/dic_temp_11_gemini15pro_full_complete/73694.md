forgiver:/fərˈɡɪvər/
n.|原谅者，宽恕者|One who pardons or excuses a fault or offense.|She is a forgiver, not a forgetter. 她是一个会原谅的人，但不会忘记。|近义词：pardoner, excuser|反义词：avenger, punisher|词性: 名词
Etymology: From forgive + -er, from Old English forgiefan "to give, grant; remit (a debt), pardon (an offense)," from for- "completely" + giefan "to give."
