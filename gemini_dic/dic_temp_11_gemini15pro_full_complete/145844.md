overdemandingly:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈmændɪŋli/ /ˌəʊvə dɪˈmɑːndɪŋli/| adv.|过分苛求地，要求过高地|In a manner that makes excessive or unreasonable demands.  It describes an action performed with excessive demands.|She pushed her students overdemandingly, expecting perfection on every assignment. 她对学生的要求过高，期望每次作业都完美无缺。|Synonym: excessively, unreasonably, exacting,  |Antonym: leniently, moderately, reasonably|adverb
