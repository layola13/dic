gossypium:/ɡɒˈsɪpiəm/ | n. |棉属|A genus of shrubs and small trees in the mallow family, Malvaceae, native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Old and New Worlds.  It is the source of commercial cotton.|Cotton is derived from the species Gossypium. 棉花来源于棉属植物。|The gossypium hirsutum is the most widely planted species.  陆地棉是最广泛种植的品种。|synonyms: cotton plant |antonyms: N/A |noun
