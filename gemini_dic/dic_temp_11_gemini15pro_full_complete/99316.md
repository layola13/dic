intercrust:/ˈɪntərkrʌst/ | adj. | 地壳间的，地壳内部的 | Situated within the Earth's crust, between layers of crust. | The intercrust seismic activity is still poorly understood. 地壳内部的地震活动仍然知之甚少。| Example 2: Intercrust magma movements can cause surface uplift. 地壳内部岩浆运动会导致地表隆起。 | 近义词: intracrustal | 反义词: surface, external | 词性: adjective

词源: "inter-" (Latin, meaning "between") + "crust" (from Latin "crusta," meaning "shell, rind").  The prefix "inter-" indicates location or action between layers of the crust.
