overreach:/ˌoʊvərˈriːtʃ/ |v. |过度延伸；不自量力；做过头；|The company overreached itself by expanding too quickly.  这家公司扩张过快，不自量力。|近义词: overextend, exceed, surpass |反义词: underestimate, restrain |verb

overreach:/ˌoʊvərˈriːtʃ/ |n. |过度扩张；过分行为|The government's overreach of power led to protests. 政府的权力过度扩张引发了抗议。|近义词: excess, overextension |反义词: restraint, moderation |noun
