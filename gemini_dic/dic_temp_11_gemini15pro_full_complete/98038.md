inosculate: /ɪˈnɒskjʊleɪt/ /ɪˈnɑːskjəleɪt/
verb
吻合；接合；连结
(of veins, arteries, or nerves) open into each other; blend.
The capillaries inosculate with each other to form a network. 毛细血管相互吻合形成网络。
近义词: join, unite, merge, fuse, coalesce, anastomose
反义词: separate, divide, detach
词性: v.

inosculate: /ɪˈnɒskjʊleɪt/ /ɪˈnɑːskjəleɪt/
noun
吻合；接合；连结
the point of union, or anastomosis.
The inosculation of the two blood vessels ensured proper circulation.  两个血管的吻合确保了正常的血液循环。
近义词: junction, union, merger, fusion, coalescence, anastomosis
反义词: separation, division, detachment
词性: n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *inosculatus*, past participle of *inosculare* (“to kiss, join by a kiss”), from *in-* (“in, on”) + *osculum* (“little mouth, kiss”).
