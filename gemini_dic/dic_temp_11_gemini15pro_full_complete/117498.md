medusian:/mɪˈdjuːziən/ /mɪˈduːʒən/
adjective
美杜莎似的；麻木的；呆滞的
Relating to or resembling Medusa, especially in being snake-like or petrifying;  causing or characterized by a stunned or paralyzed state.
She gave him a Medusian stare that made him freeze in his tracks. 她给了他一个美杜莎般凝视，使他呆立在原地。
近义词:gorgon-like, petrifying, stunning, paralyzing
反义词:invigorating, enlivening
词性:adjective

medusian:/mɪˈdjuːziən/ /mɪˈduːʒən/
noun
美杜莎；水母
A jellyfish; Medusa, a figure in Greek mythology.
The diver encountered several medusans during his exploration. 潜水员在探险过程中遇到了几只水母。
近义词:jellyfish, Medusa
反义词:None
词性:noun
