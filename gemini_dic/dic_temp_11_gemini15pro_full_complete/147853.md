overrank:/ˌoʊvərˈræŋk/ | vt. | 超越等级，过度晋级 | To give someone a higher rank or position than they deserve or are qualified for. | He was overranked and struggled to keep up with the demands of the position. 他被过度晋升，难以跟上职位要求。|  The young officer was quickly overranked and found himself in charge of men much more experienced than he was.  这名年轻军官很快就被过度提拔，发现自己指挥着比他经验丰富得多的人。| 近义词：overpromote, elevate unduly | 反义词：demote, downgrade | 词性: 动词

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English "ofer," meaning "above, beyond") + "rank" (Old French "ranc," from Frankish "hring," meaning "circle, ring, row")
