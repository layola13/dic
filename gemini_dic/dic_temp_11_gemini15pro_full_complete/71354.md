flacker:/ˈflækər/ | n. | 闪烁，摇曳 | An unsteady flame or light. | The flacker of the campfire was mesmerizing. 篝火的闪烁令人着迷。| flicker, wavering | steady light, constant glow | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *flakeren, frequentative of *flakken, from Old Norse *flaka (“to flap, flutter”), from Proto-Germanic *flakwōną (“to fly, flutter”). Cognate with Danish flagre (“to flutter”), Swedish flagra (“to wave, flutter”).
