histrionics:/ˌhɪstriˈɑːnɪks/ | n. |装腔作势；做作；夸张的言行|Exaggerated or theatrical behavior designed for effect.|例句：Her histrionics after the breakup were embarrassing to watch. 她分手后的装腔作势让人尴尬不已。

例句：He launched into a fit of histrionics, complete with tears and shouting. 他开始装腔作势，又是哭又是喊。|近义词：dramatics, theatrics, melodrama, affectation|反义词：understatement, sincerity, genuineness|词性:名词

histrionic:/ˌhɪstriˈɑːnɪk/ | adj. |做作的；装腔作势的|Overly theatrical or melodramatic in character or style.|例句：She gave a histrionic performance, weeping and wailing on the stage. 她在舞台上哭天喊地，表演得十分做作。

例句：His histrionic reaction to the news was over the top. 他对这消息的夸张反应太过火了。|近义词：dramatic, theatrical, melodramatic, affected|反义词：understated, sincere, genuine|词性:形容词
