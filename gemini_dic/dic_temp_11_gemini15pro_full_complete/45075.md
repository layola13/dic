cubiform:/ˈkjuːbɪfɔːrm/
adjective
立方形的；立方体形的
Shaped like a cube; cubic.
例句：The crystals were small and cubiform. 这些晶体小而呈立方体形。

例句：The building is cubiform in design.  这座建筑设计成立方体形状。


近义词：cubic, cube-shaped
反义词：amorphous, shapeless
词性：adjective


词源：From Latin cubus (“cube”) + -iform (“-shaped”).
